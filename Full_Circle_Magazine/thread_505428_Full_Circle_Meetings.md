### Post by mrmonday on 2007-07-20
**[SIZE="3"]Meetings[/SIZE]**

If you would like to talk to one of our contributors, or have any suggestions for the magazine, please come to one of our meetings, to discuss them. 

The meetings are held on [#fullcirclemagazine]("irc://chat.freenode.net/fullcirclemagazine") on the Freenode IRC network, which you can get to using an IRC client such as XChat, or using our [WebIRC page]("http://irc.fullcirclemagazine.org/"), at [http://irc.fullcirclemagazine.org/](http://irc.fullcirclemagazine.org/). Feel free to visit at any time to discuss the magazine :).

**Contributors meetings:** Every Saturday at 16:00 UTC

**General, all welcome meeting:** Held monthly at 17:00 UTC.

The next general meeting will be held on **Saturday 8th December 17:00 UTC**. 

You can find the agenda for the next general meeting at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuMagazine/GeneralMeetingAgenda](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuMagazine/GeneralMeetingAgenda), feel free to add things to it.

I will update this post with the latest information as required.
