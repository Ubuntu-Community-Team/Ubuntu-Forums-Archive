### Post by ronniet on 2009-06-05
It was decided waaaaaay back (about a year ago) that most people preferred the landscape format as it fills the screen and requires no scrolling up/down.

You can still print the magazine in landscape format, just check that your page settings are set to landscape before printing from your PDF viewer.
