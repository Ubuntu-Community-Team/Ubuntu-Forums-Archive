### Post by sgosnell on 2011-01-02
Most e-readers should accept .pdf files without issue.  I've converted some .pdf files to .epub for my nook, and find the .pdf is better, especially if there are non-western fonts involved.  The .pdf will display everything fine, but the conversion tries to replicate the text with its own fonts, which doesn't always work well.
