### Post by mrmonday on 2007-07-19
We have looked at using landscape before, but unfortunately it would be to hard to implement. When we originally designed the magazine, we decided on portrait, and changing now would mean yet another redesign... It is a lot of effort for our editor, Ronnie, to change the template again, and it wouldn't be great for consistency in the magazine. If we change design again in the future, we will look into a landscape layout.
