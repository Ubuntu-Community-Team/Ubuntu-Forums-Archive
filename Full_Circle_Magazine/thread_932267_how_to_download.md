Maybe your FF is trying to display the PDF itself rather than saving it? Or is trying to send the PDF to an application that's not there/broken? Try going in to your Firefox **Preferences** and click the **Applications** tab, in there is a list of what Firefox will do with particular files. For example: in my FF3, all SWF files go to 'Shockwave Flash'. Check your PDF entry, mine says 'Always Ask' so it will always ask me what to do with any PDF file I click on in the web.
