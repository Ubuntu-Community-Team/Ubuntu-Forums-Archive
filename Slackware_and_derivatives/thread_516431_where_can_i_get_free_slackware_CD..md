### Post by Steven_B on 2007-08-03
You can [download the CD images]("http://www.slackware.org/getslack/"), and burn them yourself.  I don't think Slackware has an equivalent to Ubuntu's Shipit.
If you're new to Linux, Slackware may not be the best place to start.  I would suggest taking a look at the most popular options here: [http://distrowatch.com/dwres.php?resource=major]("http://distrowatch.com/dwres.php?resource=major")
