### Post by johnc4510 on 2007-05-16
If you live in Arizona and would like to help us advocate and spread the use of Ubuntu and Open source, please follow the directions below.

To sign up, please register at the following web sites:

Launchpad Arizona Loco Team: [https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-arizona/]("https://launchpad.net/%7Eubuntu-arizona/")

Ubuntu-us-az mail list: [https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-az](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-az)

Once you have done that, you can stop by the irc channel, #ubuntu-us-az and we will get you set up on the members list. You will then have access to your own wiki page to set up and personalize. We can then chat more about yourself, any projects you might be interested in, or ideas you might have to help us spread Ubuntu and Open Source.

If you are not familiar with the IRC Channels or chat clients, please refer to the page linked below for more information.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/XChatHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/XChatHowto)

If you are still having problems, please send an email to any of the addresses below with any questions that you have and someone will be glad to assist you.

[indent][email]questions@azloco.com[/email]
[email]toddc@azloco.com[/email]
[email]majb@azloco.com[/email][/indent]

The Ubuntu-Arizona Loco Team thanks you for you interest and we look forward to getting to know you better!
