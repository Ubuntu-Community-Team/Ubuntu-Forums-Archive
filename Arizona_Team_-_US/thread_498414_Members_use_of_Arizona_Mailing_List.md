Hi folks

I just thought I would let everyone know that our mailing list is for all members to use. It is 

another way to inform the other members of ideas you have for our LoCo. Anyone who is 

registered for the ubuntu-us-az mail list can use it to send bulk mail to the other registered            

members of our team. To use it, type up your idea in your email client, just like you were 

sending a regular email. Then instead of sending it to an individual, you send it to the list 

then, from the list it is automatically send to everyone who is registered. The mailing list 

address is:

[email]ubuntu-us-az@lists.ubuntu.com[/email]   

I hope everyone will consider using the mail list from time to time.

Thanks
