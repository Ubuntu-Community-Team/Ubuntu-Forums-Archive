### Post by IanMcewen on 2007-04-29
This is the thread for logo submissions up for voting on May 6. Please **do not** discuss icons here; do that in IRC or in other threads. Please keep [Ubuntu Trademark Policy]("http://www.ubuntu.com/aboutus/trademarkpolicy") in mind, but also, if there is a somewhat non-conforming logo that is well-liked, it may be accepted.

Please provide a visual for your image, as well as an attachment that is the image itself. SVG format images are most desirable for final use, as they are scalable; however, thumbnails should be in another format for posting, and SVG only in attachments. As well, other formats are OK, although they will probably be converted to SVG for 'production' use if finally voted in.

Thank you, good luck, and let the submissions begin.
