Next: The FOSSED conference at Gallaudet in August...  There hasn't been as much interest as anticipated, partially because we've held off on advertising, waiting for a more finalized schedule.  It looks like we work with the schedule we have, til something better comes along.  That schedule is at:

 [http://dc.ubuntu-us.org/~kjcole/OldSite/events/2007/fossed-dc.php](http://dc.ubuntu-us.org/~kjcole/OldSite/events/2007/fossed-dc.php)

(Note at the top of the page, the link to the spreadsheet version.) The registration form is at:

 [http://fossed.net/mod/resource/view.php?id=88](http://fossed.net/mod/resource/view.php?id=88)

Even if you cannot attend, consider advertising to those who might be interested.  More info available on [http://fossed.net/](http://fossed.net/) and I think I'll throw one of the e-mails I've sent out advertising
the conference onto the dc.ubuntu-us.org site.

Last: If we have interesting tidbits, we should consider contributing to the Ubuntu Weekly Newsletter (UWN) at:

 [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuWeeklyNewsletter/](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuWeeklyNewsletter/)

Til next time...
