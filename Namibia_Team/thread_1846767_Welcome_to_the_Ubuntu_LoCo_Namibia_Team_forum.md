### Post by gjensen on 2011-09-19
Another important resource has just been opened up here, as you can see. Welcome on board everybody!

This forum will be much more usefull for longer discussions than the post/reply options you have in our Facebook group ([https://www.facebook.com/groups/ubuntu.na/](https://www.facebook.com/groups/ubuntu.na/) for those that haven't yet seen it), so you are invited to link to threads and posts on this forum for any more "in-depth" discussion.

Our subdomain for the forum is not yet active, but that's been worked on as I type. As soon as that's done, the forum will of course also be listed as one of the resources in our LoCo page at [http://loco.ubuntu.com/teams/ubuntu-na](http://loco.ubuntu.com/teams/ubuntu-na) in the LoCo team directory.

I'm hopeful that our mailing list will also be activated soon, so that all channels of communication are indeed available.

While you can register here with a seperate account, I think the most practical solution for all LoCo Namibia team members ist to register on Launchpad first ([https://launchpad.net/](https://launchpad.net/)) and then use the login option with the Launchpad ID and have that linked to your account here.

Other than that, not much to add on a Monday night, will have a beer now to celebrate our new forum here 8-) - hope you enjoy your evening too, and that we'll be having some lively discussions and Q&A sessions here soon!

A special invitation to anyone else from Namibia coming accross this forum - please feel free to join us! "The more, the merrier" certainly applies to a LoCo too :)
