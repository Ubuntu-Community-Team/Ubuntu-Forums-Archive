### Post by bepcyc on 2005-10-05
xneur is a tool for automatic keyboard layout switching like windoze Punto Switcher and others
it works with Russian language right now only and have a status of beta (version is only 0.0.3) but I think it is very useful for users who have switched from windoze to linux

for example, all of my friends who have switched to ubuntu are very angry that they do not have such a tool in linux ;))))

homepage: [http://xneur.cidnet.crew.org.ru]("http://xneur.cidnet.crew.org.ru") (Russian)
download link: [http://cidnet.crew.org.ru/frs/download.php/12/xneur-0.0.3.tar.bz2]("http://cidnet.crew.org.ru/frs/download.php/12/xneur-0.0.3.tar.bz2")
