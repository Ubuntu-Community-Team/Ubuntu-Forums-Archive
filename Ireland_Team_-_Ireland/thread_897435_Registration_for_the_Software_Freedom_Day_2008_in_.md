### Post by Mean-Machine on 2008-08-22
Hello all,

The event will start at 11am on Saturday, September 20th. The location is [COLOR="Red"]**The Digital Exchange, Crane St., Digital Hub, Dublin 8**[/COLOR].

Please use the comments at [ubuntu-ie.org]("http://www.ubuntu-ie.org/node/14") to register your attendance and also if you have any suggestions or thoughts.

If you have any questions, please feel free to e-mail me (byMeanMachine [at] googlemail [dot] com), Laura (laura [at] lczajkowski [dot] com) or ubuntu-ie [mailing list]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-ie")

**What can you expect?**

Info stalls
Ubuntu demo machines
Talks by: Irish Free Software Organisation, Google
Elevate Learning taking part as a supporter and sponsor of SFD 2008 in Dublin
...and more

Complete schedule to be published very soon!
