### Post by odonohuem on 2012-10-10
Hi ATAQ,
I guess by now you've realised our forums are quiet. Apologies for that.  While I am not living in the southwest I do know some of the groups there that are interested in Ubuntu.  Members of UL Comp Soc and the Milklabs (Makerspace) are involved in the Ubuntu Ireland LoCo. The guy behind Openpoint.ie is also into open source in general.  If you are interested in getting more active in Ubuntu Ireland and making better contact with people I'd suggest using the mailing list.  It is also quiet but not as quiet as the forums!  Details of our mailing list can be found at:

[https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-ie](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-ie)

The IRC channel is also slightly more active.  It can experience periods of inactivity but in general a response will be forthcoming so keep logged in.

You can connect to the ubuntu-ie IRC channel by pointing your client to the Freenode network and joining the #ubuntu-ie channel.

Kind Regards,

Mike O'Donohue
