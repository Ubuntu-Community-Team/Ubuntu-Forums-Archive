### Post by airurando on 2013-03-03
Howdy,
The Irish LoCo does have meet ups from time to time for different reasons.  The LoCo has been quiet in recent months and, as I am sure you have surmised the forums are not active at all!.  Most activity is localised on the mailing list ([https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-ie](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-ie)), the LoCo Team Portal page ([http://loco.ubuntu.com/teams/ubuntu-ie](http://loco.ubuntu.com/teams/ubuntu-ie)), where most of the events are announced, and on irc freenode channel #ubuntu-ie.  The Loco also uses identi.ca, twitter, google+ and facebook (ubuntuie or Ubuntu Ireland should help you find the accounts there).  I'd encourage you to join the group on the launchpad page as bertie wisely suggested.  I'd also encourage you to subscribe to the mailing list, say hi in irc and keep an eye on the loco team portal for news of events.

Hope to hear from you!

Mike
