### Post by winh8r on 2012-03-06
Open the Ubuntu Software Centre
click on Edit
Software sources

Select the "download from" drop down menu and click the button that says 

"select best server"

 This will check through the available servers and choose the one that is best in terms of connection speed and availability for you (it could be anywhere) I have used the server for the Czech Republic, Sweden and France even though I am in the UK.

Just make sure you click on "select server" before closing the window


Hope this helps you.
