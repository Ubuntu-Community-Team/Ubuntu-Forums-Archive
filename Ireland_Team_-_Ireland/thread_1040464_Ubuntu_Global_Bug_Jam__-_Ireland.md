### Post by Mean-Machine on 2009-01-15
The next Ubuntu Global Bug Jam will take place on the weekend of the
20th to 22nd February. 

LoCos are a huge part of this and we, ubuntu-ie are looking forward to
participating in this worldwide event.

This will be an opportunity for Ubuntu Ireland to get together, meet new
people, triage and fix bugs. 

Anyone is welcome to join in. There will be a presentation on Bug Triage
and Bugs in general on the day, so you don't have to worry about your
level of experience.

All you have to do is show up, participate and have fun!

The venue has yet to be confirmed.
 
We'll keep you posted.

Useful resources:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/GlobalBugJam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/GlobalBugJam)
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/5-A-Day](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/5-A-Day)
