### Post by stream303 on 2008-12-14
For playing ogg files on the mac itself, I really like VLC, which you can get in Intel, PowerPC, or universal binaries:

[http://www.videolan.org/vlc/](http://www.videolan.org/vlc/)

AND, if you like to listen to ogg streams on the mac, or on the ipod, try FStream found at your favorite mac repository or even on the itunes store.  it also does mp3.

Keep on rocking with ogg!
