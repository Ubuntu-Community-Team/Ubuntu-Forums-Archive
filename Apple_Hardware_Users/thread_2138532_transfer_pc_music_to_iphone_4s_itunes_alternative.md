### Post by Kopkins on 2013-04-24
Since iOS 5 syncing has become more difficult. Currently I don't think there is any way for Ubuntu apps to sync to iphone 4. [http://www.gtkpod.org/wiki/Libgpod](http://www.gtkpod.org/wiki/Libgpod)

However, you can try running itunes from a virtual machine if you have that option.

 If you didn't totally do away with your other OS you can dual boot to sync from windows or mac. You could create a music partition and have it automatically mounted at /home/music at each boot so you don't have your music in a weird spot, then in windows/mac you can use that same partition to sync your music from to your iphone. 

Good luck,

Kopkins
