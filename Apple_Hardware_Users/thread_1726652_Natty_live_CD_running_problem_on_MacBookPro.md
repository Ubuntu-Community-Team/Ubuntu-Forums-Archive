### Post by handy on 2011-04-25
If you download a Ubuntu LiveCD .iso & burn it like this:

[http://hints.macworld.com/article.php?story=20060619181010389](http://hints.macworld.com/article.php?story=20060619181010389) 

Then with the CD in the machine, hold the "c" key down when starting the machine, it should boot-up & give you the choice of installing Ubuntu or booting to the LiveCD Ubuntu desktop so you can check out how it runs on your machine.

I hope that answers your question. :)
