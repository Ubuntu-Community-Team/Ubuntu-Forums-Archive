### Post by packetmangler on 2009-12-08
I know that in at least 10.6 this works. Hopefully it works in 10.4 as well.

Open up Disk Utility in OS X and click on the hard drive.  In the right pane see if you have the Partition tab.  

Click it and then type in some number that you want the new size to be in the field below. 

Click the box just to the left beneath Volume Scheme and it should change to include a drag tab.

Click Apply and your changes will be applied.
