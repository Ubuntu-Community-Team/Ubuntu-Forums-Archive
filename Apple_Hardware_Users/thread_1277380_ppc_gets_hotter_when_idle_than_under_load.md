### Post by the_squircle on 2009-09-29
The PPC chips are very power- and heat-inefficient. The fans will speed up when your system is under high load, and make it cooler. But when your system is under low load, the PPC chips are consuming almost as much power as they would at full utilization, and it gets hot (as the fans don't come on because the processor isn't being used too too much).
