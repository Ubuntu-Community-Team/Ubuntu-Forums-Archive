### Post by stream303 on 2008-06-18
My G5 iMac has limited power management, and one thing I always wanted was a way to shut it down based upon **user-inactivity**, rather than a preset amount like some quickie fixed-time shutdown command in a terminal.

Thanks to rpontes, he provided the basis of a script that I modified to take into account virtual terminals and gnome terminals up and running as well.

If you are interested, check out my reply to that thread:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=530973](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=530973)

I link to it here because many PPC users may have the same problem, and searches for shutdown scripts are usually based on preset limits, and not user-inactivity.  His script blew me away.  Scratch off another annoyance from the Linux-PPC list!
