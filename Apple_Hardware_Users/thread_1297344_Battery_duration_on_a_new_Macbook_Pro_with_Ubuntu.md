No not for Ubuntu (or other linux distros)

Part of the problem is that on MacBooks Pros equipped with dual GPUs there's no way to switch to the lower powered 9400m in ubuntu.  You're stuck with the battery sucking 9600m.

Other power saving features in Ubuntu are less successful then in OSX for reasons I do not understand.  The bottom line is my expectation of battery life is much lower in Ubuntu then OSX.
