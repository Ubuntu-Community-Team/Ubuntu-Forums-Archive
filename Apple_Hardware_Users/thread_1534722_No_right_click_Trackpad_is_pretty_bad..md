### Post by alexmurray on 2010-07-20
What model is this?

Try clicking with 2 fingers on the trackpad for a right click.

Also try installing 'pommed' to get keyboard backlight and screen backlight controls working.

For the other issues try looking at the Wiki page for your model: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBook](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBook) - see the links on the right hand side of the page.
