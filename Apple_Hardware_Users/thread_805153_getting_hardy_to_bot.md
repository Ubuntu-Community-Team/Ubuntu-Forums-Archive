### Post by stream303 on 2008-05-24
Were you trying to dual-boot Feisty 7.04 and Hardy 8.04, or are you just trying to install Hardy?

If you just want to install Hardy over the top of Feisty, you could just tell guided partitioning to "use the whole disk" and it will wipe out the existing partitions and do all the repartitioning for you.  Of course this wipes out everything first!

If Hardy were to prove just too much, you could also reinstall Feisty the same way.
