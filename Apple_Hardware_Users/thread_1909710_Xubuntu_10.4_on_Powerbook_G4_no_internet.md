### Post by rsavage on 2012-01-16
As others have said, ethernet connection should just work.  Sometimes you do have to make an adjustment though.  Have a look at the first fix on this page: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lubuntu/DocumentationHelp/MinimalInstall#Unmanaged_Wired_Network](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lubuntu/DocumentationHelp/MinimalInstall#Unmanaged_Wired_Network)
 
You will have to replace "leafpad" with "mousepad" for xubuntu in the commands they use.
 
For wifi/wireless have a look at the [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCFAQ](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCFAQ)
 
Let us know if it works.
