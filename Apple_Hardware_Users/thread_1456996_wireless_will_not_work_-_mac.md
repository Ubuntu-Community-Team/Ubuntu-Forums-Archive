### Post by tom4everitt on 2010-04-18
The driver should be available under (you have to be connected to internet when you do this):

System->Administration->Hardware Drivers

Its pretty much just to click on it and choose activate (and when you're there, go ahead and activate the graphics driver as well, it will bring you some nice desktop effects).
