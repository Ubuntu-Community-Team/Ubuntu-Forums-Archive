### Post by cascade9 on 2010-09-21
> **lukeiamyourfather said:**
> The processor is a G3. How much performance can you possibly get from it with the extra cooling?

About 0.00001%. Unless you want to overclock (which I dont know if the G3s will even do). 

That is performance though, you can sure increase the cooling or drop the fan speeds for the same amount of cooling as 'stock' if you want to play with foam, cardboard, tin, tape and zipties. I tend to do it to most of my machines, I really, really, REALLY hate fan noise. 

BTW, I dont bother with going to 100% load to test that. Idle temps drop when you've got it 'right'. Not as much as load temps do though.
