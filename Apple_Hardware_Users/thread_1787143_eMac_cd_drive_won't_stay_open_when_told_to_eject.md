### Post by holastickboy on 2011-06-23
I had a similar problem to you linuxftw3.  However, I found that it was not Linux that was doing it (it was happening to me when I was using OSX 10.4).  It actually turned out to be that the drive behind the flap was contacting the door a little too heavily when coming out, so when it had reached its full open position, the first thing it did was withdraw back into the mac.  It was an annoying problem that confused the hell out of me.  I opened the flap manually, and made sure that it was aligning to the eMac perfectly when it was closed, and voila! it's back to normal!  If you are still having problems, it might be the actual tray of the drive not being aligned properly.
