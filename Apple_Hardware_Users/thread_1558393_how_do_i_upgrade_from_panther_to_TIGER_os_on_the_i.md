### Post by GregBrannon on 2010-08-22
This isn't an Ubuntu/Linux question, but I'll play until we get shut down.

Here's a guide that talks to the general procedure for updating a Mac OS:

[http://kb.iu.edu/data/akav.html]("http://kb.iu.edu/data/akav.html")

As for which disks you need, there are 2 general categories: ones made specifically for a certain computer model, and others made to update any Mac.  The ones made to update any Mac have a distinctive picture of an 'X' on the disk.  The disks specific to a certain hardware configuration but not the hardware you have will NOT work.  You shouldn't need the original Panther disk, but I haven't done that many upgrades, so maybe there's a reason you would that I haven't come across.
