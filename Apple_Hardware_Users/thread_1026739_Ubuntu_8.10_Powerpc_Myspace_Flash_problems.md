> I didn't realize how difficult it would be to have a PPC based Linux compared to an Intel based Linux when it comes to simple things like viewing all forms of flash.

Unfortunately that's the problem with a proprietary-standard.  (that's a misnomer in my book. :) )

Flash and 3D aren't ppc-Linux's stroing points, but that isn't a fault of the developers - nobody from Cupertino or Adobe is handing out the hardware docs or source.  I'm amazed that Apple didn't lock out anything but OSX on ppc to begin with.

About the best you can do is try to tweak your video card settings in xorg.conf aided by the faqs and wikis.  Have you looked into these for any additional tips:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCKnownIssues](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCKnownIssues)
and
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCFAQ](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCFAQ)
