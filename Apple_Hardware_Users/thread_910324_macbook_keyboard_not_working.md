### Post by oricordeau on 2008-09-04
Hi,
I had exactly the same problem on a Macbook pro 4th generation (Penryn). Here is a solution:
1) plug your external keyboard
2) press the "verr num" key so that verr num is deactivated

Then it should work (even if you unplug the external keyboard). I know this sounds weird but it just works on my laptop.

Cheers,

Olivier
