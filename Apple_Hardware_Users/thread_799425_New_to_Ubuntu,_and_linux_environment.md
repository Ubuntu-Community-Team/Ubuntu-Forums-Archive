### Post by DirtDawg on 2008-05-19
Your A-1 problem, I would say, is using a version of Ubuntu(Dapper) that's almost 2 years old! Hardy was released only a few weeks ago and will probably treat you lots better. You can find it here: [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/releases/hardy/release/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/releases/hardy/release/)
