### Post by Lars Noodén on 2012-10-20
Does it have a DVD/CD drive?   If so, that is one way to go.  You boot from the optical drive by holding down C as the machine starts up.  

If not, use Ubuntu on a USB stick.  Holding Alt on startup should give you the option of booting from the USB stick.
