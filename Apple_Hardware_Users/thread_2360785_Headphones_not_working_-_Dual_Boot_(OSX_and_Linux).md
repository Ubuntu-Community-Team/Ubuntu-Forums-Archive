### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-05-08
Check the volume settings for headphones in pulseaudio-volume-control, normally got to from the volume icon in the panel, **Sound Settings**.

Headphones and loudspeakers are listed separately in the **Port:** box when you click on it and can be set one full volume, the other muted.
