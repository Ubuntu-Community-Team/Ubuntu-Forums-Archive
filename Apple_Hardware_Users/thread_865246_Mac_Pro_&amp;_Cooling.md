### Post by blunt.dualboot on 2008-07-21
Hi,

I am using Macbook Pro 4.1 (Penryn) 15 inches. Dualbooting with OS X and Ubuntu 8.04. 

When running Ubuntu Temperature is slightly higher than OS X. My HDD temperature is marked at 47 C after 4 hrs of using Ubuntu. Location is Singapore(tropical) and room is not air conditioned. Its goes down to 44 C in air conditioned room.

This [[COLOR="DarkRed"]Macbook Pro Wiki[/COLOR]]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBookPro") tells you how to set up CPU and HDD temperature monitor. Scroll to section 13 "Temperature and Fan speed"

There are lots of people who are concerned about heat when running Ubuntu on Macbook Pro. I am one of them. So far I am living with 44 C when running ubuntu. 

Please let me know if you managed to find other useful tricks or facts.
