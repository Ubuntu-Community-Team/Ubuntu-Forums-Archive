### Post by nzjethro on 2011-07-16
This is indeed possible. If you have antivirus software installed on Linux (such as avast!), and have your Mac partition mounted, you should be able to select your Mac drive to be scanned.

[This guide,]("http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/14434/scan-a-windows-pc-for-viruses-from-a-ubuntu-live-cd/") although it's about Windows, will show you how to use avast! to scan a partition other than Linux /.
