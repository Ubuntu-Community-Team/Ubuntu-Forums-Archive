### Post by justsomedude on 2008-10-09
These tags are iTunes specific, I don't know of a Linux program that can interpret them.

I recommend using additional tags such as artist, album and track number. This way, you can sort your tv shows with Banshee or Songbird for example.

You could use something like:

Artist: The Love Boat

Album: Season 1

Track: 12

for example. This won't affect sorting in iTunes. I recommend using the newest Banshee: [http://banshee-project.org/](http://banshee-project.org/)
