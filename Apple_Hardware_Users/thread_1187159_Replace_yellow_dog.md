### Post by MikeTheC on 2009-06-14
I'm not really familiar with getting Ubuntu (current versions) for PPC. I know they're out there, but where... couldn't say.

I *do* know that you can easily get Debian Lenny for PPC, no sweat. I'm not honestly sure what benefit Ubuntu is, per se, on older PPC-based Macs at this point anyhow. Besides, supporting stuff like sound and especially video (other than just plain-jane 2D) is a PIA on PPC.

FWIW, I run a G4 Mac mini with Lenny as a file and web server. It works fantastically in that role.
