### Post by rsavage on 2011-07-17
Won't MintPPC have the same problem or am I missing something?
 
Here are some instructions for reconfiguring a xorg file [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Lubuntu/Documentation/FAQ#Screen resolution is wrong, no matter what I do!!](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Lubuntu/Documentation/FAQ#Screen resolution is wrong, no matter what I do!!) 
 
They are from lubuntu so you'll want some slightly different commands for normal ubuntu, but they give the general idea. Substitute in "sudo service gdm stop" and "sudo service gdm start".
 
EDIT: I've just noticed they don't give you the command to edit the xorg file.  Type "sudo nano /etc/X11/xorg.conf".
