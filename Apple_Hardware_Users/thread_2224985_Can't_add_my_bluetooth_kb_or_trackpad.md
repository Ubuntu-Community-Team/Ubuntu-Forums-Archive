I have an iMac so have configured it to dual boot. I have installed Ubuntu 14.04 for Mac and used a USB keyboard and mouse during the installation.  I now want to add my Apple bluetooth Keyboard & Trackpad.

I click on the Bluetooth icon top right of the screen; select 'Setup new device'; I press the button on my bluetooth device to make it discoverable. ### Post by daj_uk on 2014-05-19
Good news guys & gals (well for me anyway!)

I solved it myself -- check me :p

For anyone who may stumble across this in the future....

Hold down the power button on the device (k/b or trackpad) for a few seconds until it powers off.  
Now press AND hold the power button.  It will appear in the list within a few seconds.  KEEP holding the power button and finish pairing the device.
Release the power button
(For the keyboard I released the power button to type in the pin and it was fine at that point too)
