### Post by cmost on 2009-05-25
Well, if you're considering a Macbook Air with an Intel video chip, then you should be very concerned about the video performance regressions in Ubuntu 9.04 (and probably to linger in 9.10 as well.)  There are workarounds, however, if you're going to go to that trouble you may as well just grab a cheap netbook and try Ubuntu on that.
