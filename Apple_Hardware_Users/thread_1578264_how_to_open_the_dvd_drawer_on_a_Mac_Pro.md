### Post by r2rX on 2010-09-20
In the Nautilus File Browser, select your DVD drive, right-click and select 'Eject'....that's the only thing I can think of. :p

I think there's also a method where, when you restart your Mac, you can press the Eject button and it should pop out.

Unfortunately, the Mace drives are not as solid as they should be. I've encountered reports where people need to slap their drive, or shut down and leave the machine for a while, before the disc comes out.....the Mac drives are picky that way.
