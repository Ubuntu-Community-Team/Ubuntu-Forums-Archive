### Post by fbugnon on 2009-07-27
You have to use the 3rd level key, also known as SUPER (if I'm not wrong), which on Mac is the left Alt.

For me (MacBook Pro 1,1), the SUPER key was defined at the right command (apple key), have you tried this?

Or you can go to System > Preferences > Keyboard > Layout and click on the Layout options button and choose a 3rd level key at your choice.

Hope that helps you.
