### Post by LinuxFan999 on 2011-12-18
Google Earth no longer supports PowerPC (6.0 and later). Linux on PowerPC was never supported. You can still use an older version (before 6.0) of Google Earth on a PowerPC-based Mac if you have OSX installed.
 
Look here for more information: [http://support.google.com/earth/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=40901](http://support.google.com/earth/bin/answer.py?hl=en&answer=40901)

### Post by rsavage on 2011-12-27
Canhoto, I'm not entirely sure what the difference is between google earth and google maps, but you can definitely use google maps without flash. From the main google maps website you can do everything except streetview. If you want streetview then I think you have to go via an external site - e.g. [http://code.google.com/apis/maps/documentation/javascript/examples/geocoding-simple.html](http://code.google.com/apis/maps/documentation/javascript/examples/geocoding-simple.html) .
 
EDIT: Just to add [http://www.bing.com/maps/](http://www.bing.com/maps/) works without flash too.
