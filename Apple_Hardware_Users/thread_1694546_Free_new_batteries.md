### Post by rsavage on 2011-02-24
Hi all,

Just in case anybody doesn't know about the iBook and PowerBook battery exchange programmes that Apple are running I thought I'd provide the link:

[http://www.apple.com/support/exchange_repair/](http://www.apple.com/support/exchange_repair/)

If your battery serial number is in one of the bad ranges then Apple will still provide a replacement for free.  Thought this may be especially useful for people who've just bought a secondhand machine.

### Post by rsavage on 2011-03-05
Hey, thanks for the positive feedback.  It's good to know it was of some use.  I think this is the only thread that I've had a reply to one of my posts!

I heard about the programme on some random forum thread from over a year ago and thought I'd just pass it on.  I was just lucky I came across it!

I was afraid that my second post may have made it sound a bit of a hassle and that would have put people off checking.  Although it took me two phone calls, I have to say the service I received from Apple on and following my second call was first class.  I was very impressed.  Especially considering I had bought the iBook secondhand and really Apple didn't owe me anything.  I received the battery within a week.  Here in the UK, I didn't even have to walk to the post box.  They sent a courier to pick up my old battery!
