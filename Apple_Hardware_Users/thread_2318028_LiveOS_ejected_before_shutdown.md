### Post by buzzingrobot on 2016-03-22
Sounds like you booted into Ubuntu's live mode (the "Try Ubuntu" option).

Does "switched off the operating system..." mean you clicked on "Shutdown" in Ubuntu?

If so, it's not shutting down because it's looking for the routines to do that on the USB, and it's not there.  

Power down and you should boot back into OS X.
