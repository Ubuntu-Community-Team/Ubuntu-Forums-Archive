### Post by mixmastamyk on 2020-07-20
Slow boot, or slow to start booting?

To cut off 30 seconds pre-boot, "bless" the partition from a macOS boot disk/disc.

```
sudo bless –device /dev/disk0s3 –setboot –legacy –verbose
```

[https://apple.stackexchange.com/questions/30607/is-blessing-the-ubuntu-partition-reversible-in-os-x](https://apple.stackexchange.com/questions/30607/is-blessing-the-ubuntu-partition-reversible-in-os-x)
