### Post by ZoiaGuyver on 2009-11-15
Well i only had a quick look at that app. Only thing came to mind that would seem a little like it is Gnome-Do and GTG (Get things gnome).

I personally use Gnome-Do and it's a bit of a mix of erm.... Butler/Mac OS X Dock (with Docky enabled) and Mac's finder. But when tied with GTG it looks similar to Thing's and has most of the same abilitys.

Gnome-Do: [http://do.davebsd.com/](http://do.davebsd.com/)

Get things gnome: [http://gtg.fritalk.com/](http://gtg.fritalk.com/)

Those two between them seem to cover most (if not all) of what "Things" can do.
