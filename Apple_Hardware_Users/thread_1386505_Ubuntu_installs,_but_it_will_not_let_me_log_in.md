### Post by linuxopjemac on 2010-01-22
I was wrong, your iBook does not have a PRAM battery, like the PowerBooks. Your rechargeable battery takes care of that. Probably your battery was completely empty, or broken. You can try to reset the PRAM after charging with option command "p" and "r". See the following post:
[http://www.tek-tips.com/viewthread.cfm?qid=1329865&page=1](http://www.tek-tips.com/viewthread.cfm?qid=1329865&page=1)
