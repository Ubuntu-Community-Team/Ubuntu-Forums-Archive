### Post by joninkrakow on 2008-06-29
Unless something has drastically changed, there is no official Flash from Adobe for PPC for Linux. :-( To get flash playback, and not very well, at that, you need to use gnash. For me, gnash did not work before 8.04. However, the version of gnash that's included in the repos for 8.04 works well enough to mostly replace Adobe Flash. At least for Youtube, and other flash players I've tried on the tech blogs I visit. In any case, this one's not the fault of the Ubuntu team, but Adobe.
