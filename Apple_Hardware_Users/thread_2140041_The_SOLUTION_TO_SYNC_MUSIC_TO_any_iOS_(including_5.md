### Post by blahchici on 2013-04-28
This is not a perfect solution as it does not use the default Apple music player but it is a workaround to get your music library on any iOS Version while avoiding iTunes.                                                       1) Get an app called "EZMP3" (I am not affiliated with this, I just think it is useful)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   2) Open the app and hit "File Transfer"                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       3) Switch "Start Transfer" to on                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      4) Enter the WiFi URL (or 3G URL if wifi not available) into your computer's web browser                                                                                                                                                                                                                     5) Click "Select Files"                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           6) Select your Music Library (This will take a while after you press ok)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          7) Wait                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       8) Enjoy!!   Again, this is not perfect as it's not in iTunes but finally I can get my music library into my iPhone 4S without Windows!! Hopefully this was useful
