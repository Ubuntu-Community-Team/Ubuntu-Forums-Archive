### Post by cyberdork33 on 2009-01-25
when you have one finger on the touchpad and you move a second, it shouldn't do anything...

if you move two-fingers together, then you should get scrolling (if option is enabled)

if you tap two fingers together, you should get a right-click.

Some drivers will allow allow one finger on the pad and clicking the button to emulate a right-click.
