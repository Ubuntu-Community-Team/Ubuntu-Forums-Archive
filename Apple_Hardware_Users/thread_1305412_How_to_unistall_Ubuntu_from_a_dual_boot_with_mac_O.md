### Post by tuwe on 2009-11-05
This might be a little late, but:

The OS X installer offers a possibility to reformat/partition the hard drive. As soon as the top panel appears (usually after the first or second `next` click during the install process) go to Utilities->Disk Utility. There you can erase and/or modify all your partitions.

Make sure you have a backup of all your data though, as it will be overwritten during the installation, obviously.
