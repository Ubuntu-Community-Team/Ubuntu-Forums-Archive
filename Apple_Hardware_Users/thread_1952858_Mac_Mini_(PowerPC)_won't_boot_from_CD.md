### Post by coffeecat on 2012-04-05
@Nibaini, you posted to a year-old thread about booting from an Intel Mac Mini. I have split your post and linuxopjemac's reply into its own thread and amended the title for you.

+1 to what linuxopjemac says. Also - you have only 256RAM. This is insufficient for gnome-based Ubuntu and gnome-based Fedora. (The last PPC version of Fedora was F12 anyway I believe, which is now obsolete.) You need either to find a PPC version of Linux with a lighter weight desktop, or upgrade your RAM. Upgrading RAM in a Mac Mini is a fairly scary experience, but is possible - I upgraded my Intel Mac Mini myself.

Good luck! :)
