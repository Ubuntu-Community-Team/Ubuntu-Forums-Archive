> **-Shuji- said:**
> I would like to know how to transfer/sync my iPhone 4 contacts with Evolution.

Problem Solved.
1. Download Funambol app on iPhone.
2. Upload iPhone contacts to Funambol.
3. Install syncevolution-evolution and genesis-sync on 11.04
4. Run Genesis and sync to Funambol account.
5. iPhone contacts now on Evolution
