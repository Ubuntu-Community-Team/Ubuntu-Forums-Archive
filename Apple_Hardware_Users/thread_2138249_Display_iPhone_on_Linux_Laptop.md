### Post by dannyboy79 on 2013-04-25
i use display recorder (it's in cydia so obviously you have to have a jailbroken device), you can start display recorders webserver and then access that a webbrowser on your ubuntu machine and it shows exactly what is on your iphone screen. very cool app! it also allows recording what's on your iphone screen as well straight to the device but i like recording on my ubuntu machine because then i can control audacity to record my commentary as I am doing a commentary/tutorial.
