### Post by ipina on 2010-01-24
I found a solution to connect the new apple wireless keyboard (the slim one of 2009, november) and thougth that might help someone with the same problem. I'm assuming the keyboard was working fine in OS X (10.6.2) and you have a wired keyboard.
First, remove the batteries from the keyboard and let them 'sleep' for about 10 to 15 minutes. Go to Startup Applications and uncheck Bluetooth Manager. In Synaptic choose to apply Blueman and be sure this application is among the ones checked in Startup Applications. Reboot and run Blueman. The keyboard should appear (along with the wireless mouse). Bond it and once more reboot. That worked for me!
