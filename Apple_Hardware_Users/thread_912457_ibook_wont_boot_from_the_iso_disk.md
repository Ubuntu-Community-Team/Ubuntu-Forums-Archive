### Post by mkvnmtr on 2008-09-06
You say it boots and won't read, tell us where it stops. With my Ibook when it tried to load the kernel it just went black. I had to press tab when it first began to boot. A list of options comes up. If I remember the one I had to type in was linux video=ofonly no splash. Type this in and you will see it at the bottom of the page at the prompt. Then press enter and see if that will bring up Ubuntu.
