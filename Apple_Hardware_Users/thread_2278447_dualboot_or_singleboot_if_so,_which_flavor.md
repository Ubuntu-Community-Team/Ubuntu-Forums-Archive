### Post by este.el.paz on 2015-05-16
Personal opinion, based on a few years of playing with linux . . . stick with dual-boot . . . always handy to have OSX around for an apple.  Best all-around solution is Parallels or VMFusionware . . . and run linux from either one of those.

What "flavor" is entirely subjective to machine and your personal taste, which runs best, looks best to you, does what you want, etc.  Right now on my '09 MBPro I have triple boot, 10.6.8 as default, 10.9.5, and LM 17.1 MATE . . . .

I like MATE, runs well on my MBPro . . . but I would stick with Trusty 14.04.2 as the base for now . . . .
