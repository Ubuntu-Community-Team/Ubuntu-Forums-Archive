### Post by hollowtd on 2010-01-24
I had the same problem and tried to fix this by removing the double MM (mute on) when you open Alsa in the Terminal. I could not for the life of me get this to remove however. So, I went into the software center and found Alsamixer and downloaded it. Then, I could uncheck the mute button and my head phone jack finally started working.
