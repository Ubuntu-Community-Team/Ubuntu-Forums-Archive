When you go to System>>Preferences>>keyboard, and click on the Layout tab, what model of keyboard is displayed? You can click on that, and a list of keyboards drops down, perhaps what you need to do is to select a different model, especially if the model displayed is something like pc 105, for example. 

To clarify ( I hope) a bit, click on the model; there will be a two-sectioned panel that appears; the top one is for vendors, the bottom for models, as I recall. I'd select Apple as the vendor, then take a look at the models presented, and see if there was one that was a better description of your keyboard, and select same, try it out, etc. HTH.
