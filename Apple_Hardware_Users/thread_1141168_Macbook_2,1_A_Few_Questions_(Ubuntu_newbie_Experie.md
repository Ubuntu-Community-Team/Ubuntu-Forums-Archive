### Post by N_Nick on 2009-04-28
Hello and welcome to the Ubuntu family. (I assume from your post you are new to Ubuntu)

My guess would be a wiki page for the 2,1 Macbook hasnt been writen yet, dont worry about that.

I too had problems getting the touchpad to work on my 4,1 Macbook Pro (still have some) but its all down to tweaking your .fdi file. What i would suggest is using the touchpad information from another wiki page like for example the 3,1 Macbook Jaunty wiki  or even the 2,1 Interpid wiki and build from there.  

Here's the links to the wiki pages:

```
https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBook3-1/Jaunty
https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBook2-1/Intrepid
```

You can get more information on the various options available by typing ```
man synaptics
``` in your terminal.

Good luck ;)
