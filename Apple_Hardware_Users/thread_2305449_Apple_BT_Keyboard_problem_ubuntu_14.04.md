Just to mention that if you have a problem to get your apple keyboard bluetooth to work

[[11.04] Apple BT Keyboard thinks its a numpad]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1752177")
Press twice Fn F6 on the BT keyboard and then you got evything turned on. All keys available. Do not bother going to all your config file changing layouts and so on....
