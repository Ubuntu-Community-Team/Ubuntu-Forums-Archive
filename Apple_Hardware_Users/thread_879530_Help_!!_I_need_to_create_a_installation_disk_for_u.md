### Post by kahlil88 on 2008-08-04

### Post by tiresia on 2008-08-04
You can burn it with the Apple Disk Utility. Don't mount the .iso image you downloaded.

---

### Post by stream303 on 2008-08-04
Tiresia's right.  Just use Apple's disk utility.  Drag the downloaded pc iso file into the left-hand pane of disk utility.  Highlight it, and then click on burn.

Remember to burn at a low enough speed for your pc's cdrom to handle if the drive itself is old/slow.
