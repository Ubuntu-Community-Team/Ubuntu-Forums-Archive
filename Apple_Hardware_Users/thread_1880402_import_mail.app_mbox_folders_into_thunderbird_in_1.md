### Post by cgl72 on 2011-11-14
Just an update

I got the import to work. You have to export your mailboxes from mail.app, by choosing 

Mailbox -> Export Mailbox and choosing the mbox format. 

I then used the thuderbird add-in to import.

The only problem I have found is that the subdirectory structure is not conserved, and all folders have a number instead of the original title. I am very surprised there is no way around this...

If anyone has an idea, please let me know..
