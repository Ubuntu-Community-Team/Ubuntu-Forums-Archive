### Post by avtolle on 2009-11-20
First, there is no Mint port for PPC. I wish there was. 

You need to download the Ubuntu ppc iso (I recommend the alternate install) from a mirror as detailed in  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=427714](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=427714). Once downloaded, check the md5sum and burn it at a slow rate to a CD-R. Then, use contol-C to boot. Choose to install, secect the entire hard drive upon which to install. The installer will create the needed partitions. 

Good luck; I'll not be on line for a fw days, but the above will get you started.
