### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-11-08
You might be able to re-polish the surface of the disk you bought and use that.

Try using a cream metal polish which will be very gently abrasive, but may be enough to give you a fully readable disk, depending on how badly scratched it is.  Rub from the centre outwards to the edge, not around the disk as you would have done with vinyl LP disks.

Good luck; let us know if it does any good.
