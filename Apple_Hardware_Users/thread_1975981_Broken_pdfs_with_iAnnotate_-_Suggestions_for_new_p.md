### Post by zerubbabel on 2012-09-16
PDF-Xchange is a very nice and free pdf viewer for Windows that allows annotating pdfs, and it runs fine under Wine. The "Pro" version ($37.50) does more extensive pdf editing, and also runs fine under Wine. It's an actively developed product with frequent and free updates. Their web site is [http://www.tracker-software.com/](http://www.tracker-software.com/)
