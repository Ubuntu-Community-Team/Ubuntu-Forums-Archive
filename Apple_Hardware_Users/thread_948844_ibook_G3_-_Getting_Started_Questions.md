### Post by David Starling on 2008-10-16
I use Ubuntu 8.04 with my iBook dual USB model. I got it here: [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/releases/hardy/release/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/releases/hardy/release/)

It works with my airport card but I'm having a hard time getting the WPA or WPA2 security to work. 

I'm having trouble with the power management stuff. For instance, when I shut the top, the computer doesn't go to sleep. And I can't get the LCD backlight to turn off or even adjust the brightness at all. 

I haven't checked to see if flash works. Everything does seem to run faster than on OS X, though.

Best of luck!
