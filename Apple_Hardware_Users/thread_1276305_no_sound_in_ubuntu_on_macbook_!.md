### Post by WindPower on 2009-09-28
What is the exact model of your MacBook? (You can find out by running "sudo dmidecode -s system-product-name" in a terminal).
Once you know the model you have, go to this page:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBook](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBook)
And look at the specific instructions for your MacBook model.
