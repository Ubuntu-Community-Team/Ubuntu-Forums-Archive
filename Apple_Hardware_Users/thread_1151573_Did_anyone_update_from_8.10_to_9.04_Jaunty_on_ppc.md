### Post by mkvnmtr on 2009-05-07
On my G4 Ibook I was unable to upgrade and had to reinstall 8.10. Then I tried to upgrade from the alt disk. It did not want to do that either. After worrying about how I was going to get 9.04 I put the live disk in and it booted and installed with no problem. I did have to use the option "live-nosplash-powerpc" to get it to boot but that was like the 8.04 disk. Since I use a home partition it was only a short time until I had my apps installed and stuff I don't like uninstalled. Things seem to be working fine and it is just like my 8.10 install.
