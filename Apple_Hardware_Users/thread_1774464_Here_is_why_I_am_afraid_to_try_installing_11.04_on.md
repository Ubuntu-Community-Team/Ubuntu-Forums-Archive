### Post by JohnAtilano on 2011-06-05
I had no problems installing Natty on my MBP 5,1.  Follow the [instructions ]("http://johnatilano.com/dual-boot-mac-os-x-and-linux-mint-9-on-a-macb")on my blog.  You shouldn't have any problems.  Note that the tutorial was written to install Linux Mint 9.  The process is basically the same.  Ensure you create your partitions BEFORE you begin the install and ensure you install the boot loader to your Linux partition.

As always, having at least one backup is highly recommended.
