Hi all,

I have a apple iMac 21.5" (Late 2009)
And i'm not completly new to linux/ubuntu/gnu but i couldn't discover why the sound from my internal speakers didn't work. 
So i used my headset the whole time till i found the following solution (Wich kay work with other sound problens)

1. Open Terminal (Applications > Asseccoires > Terminal)
2. Type: "alsamixer"
3. You will see some green: "00" wich means: 
Open if you see a   "mm"
   that channel is muted so to unmute it you must hit the: "m"
4. You also see some sounds bars they all must be full in order to get some sound.
