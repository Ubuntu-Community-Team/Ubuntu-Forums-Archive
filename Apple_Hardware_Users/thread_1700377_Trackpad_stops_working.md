### Post by rg4w on 2011-03-05
This may be a long shot, but I wonder if it may be related to the pressure of your palms on the handrest putting pressure on the trackpad.  If you moved your hand to interact with the network controls, that might explain the seeming relationship between the two.

There may be other causes as well, so it may be helpful to try different hand positions when it happens to see if that's it.

In my case I had that issue for a while, and I found a tool in the Ubuntu Software Center that included palm detection; a little tweaking with that and things got much smoother here.
