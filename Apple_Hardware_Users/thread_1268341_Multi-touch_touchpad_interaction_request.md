Hi there,

Having been using Jaunty on my macbook pro, I've been really happy with it, and have been especially impressed that the multi-touch trackpad is working.

I dual boot between OSX and Jaunty, and noticed that the trackpad is missing an interaction that users in OSX enjoys that isn't currently supported in Jaunty. If you click with 1 finger, then try to scroll with another finger, OSX treat that as single-click->drag, while in jaunty that becomes a right click(2-finger down) the moment the second finger is on the pad.

I understand this requires separate blob-tracking for each blob the trackpad senses on the trackpad, and I'm just wondering is anyone out there attempting to add this to the driver?
