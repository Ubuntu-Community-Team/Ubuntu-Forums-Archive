### Post by jackdaniels123123 on 2012-08-27
I was having the same problem also. I was able to get it running but going into xserver and choosing twin view, which makes it one monitor, with my external as the primary. I then saved the configuration so that it always recognizes my monitor whenever its connected. You do have to log out and then back in for it to take effect. The only thing Im having trouble with is to get video to play full screen on my external. For some reason, it still recognizes my laptop as the primary and goes to full screen there. Hope this helps!
