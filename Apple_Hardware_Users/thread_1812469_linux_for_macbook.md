### Post by scorp123 on 2011-07-26

> Is Linux applicable to mac books?

I am running Ubuntu 10.10 on my MacBook Pro (MacBook Pro 5,4) and it's working pretty well. Multi-touch support could be a slight bit better (it's not perfect and the number of available gestures is limited when compared to OS X) but OK ... other than that my MacBook Pro is running very smoothly and I am very happy. Performance is better than with Mac OS X, there is far less bloat.

Until a few days ago I had Kubuntu 11.04 with KDE on this MacBook Pro but it seems the kernel in the 11.04 versions of Ubuntu and its variants such as Kubuntu has some issues and stability problems. I experienced several system freezes and other strange issues. So ... no Ubuntu 11.04 for me. 

But the previous release Ubuntu 10.10 which will be supported until April next year is running tip top.

Just follow the Wiki to get all the needed drivers and software bits and you should be OK. 

Wiki for MacBook Pro's:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBookPro](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBookPro)

Wiki for MacBooks:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBook](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBook)
