Macs are very fickle when it comes to booting off of external usb sticks and/or disks. During the last two years or so, I have tried a couple of different approaches on different apple hardware. I've never had any luck with LiveUSB Creator, and very little luck with unetbootin. The one guide that had been most successful for me is this one [http://www.devslashzero.com/node/160]("http://www.devslashzero.com/node/160"), but even that didn't work for all my hardware. So you may have to try several things and be prepared to be somewhat stubborn...
