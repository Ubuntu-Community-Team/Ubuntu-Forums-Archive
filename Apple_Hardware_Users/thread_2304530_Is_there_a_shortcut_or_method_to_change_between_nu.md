### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-11-27
Under the Keyboard Preferences-->Layout-->Apple there are 9 different keyboards.  Test each one and take notes as to what keys work and what don't.

I have a similar, metal-and-plastic, bluetooth keyboard that I use with my Nokia800 and a few other devices.  I don't recall having problems with it.
