### Post by mkvnmtr on 2008-08-01
You can probably get Ubuntu 8.04.1 on your machine if you have enough ram. Several of us with Ibook G4s can't get past the splash screen. When the Disk first comes before it begins to boot press tab. You will see many options. The one some of us have used with success is live-nosplash-powerpc=ofonly. If that does not work maybe something else will. You have to press tab early when it says sometheng like welcome to the live 8.04.1 disk. If you wait  to long it won't work. When you type it in you should see it on the bottom of the page. When you finish press enter. Good luck
