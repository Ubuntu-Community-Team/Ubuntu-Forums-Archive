### Post by perpetualcacophany on 2009-03-14
As far as I know, Apple computers can't boot from USB, so you will have to use a live CD. When you restart the computer with the LiveCD in the drive press and hold "c" during the boot process (before the Apple logo appears). It should work from there just fine.
