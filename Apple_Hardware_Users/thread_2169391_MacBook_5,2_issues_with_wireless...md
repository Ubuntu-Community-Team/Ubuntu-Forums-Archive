### Post by Austin_Constable on 2013-08-27
Go to System Settings/Software & Updates. Check the checkbox at the bottom of the window that is for drivers from the disk. Then go the the additional drivers tab. There should be an airport driver that says it is not being used. Select use and apply changes. Should work.
