### Post by stlu on 2012-11-19
Hi,

I'm trying Lubuntu 12.10 on an iMac G3 600MHz SE, and I had sound on the first go, but later the sound stopped working.

After trying the solution posted here: 
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCFAQ#Why_do_I_have_no_sound.3F](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCFAQ#Why_do_I_have_no_sound.3F)

I realized that I was down the wrong path.  The problem was I had muted the sound just before the problem started, and now I see that the problem is the GUI Mute button on the LXDE desktop: I have filed a bug report.

Read the details here:
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/lubuntu-meta/+bug/1080790](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/lubuntu-meta/+bug/1080790)

Basically, **don't mute the volume** using the GUI checkbox for Lubuntu on an iMac, or you won't get audio back when you uncheck it.
