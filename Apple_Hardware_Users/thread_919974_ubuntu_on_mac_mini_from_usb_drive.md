### Post by hajk on 2008-09-15
It is not possible to boot a "legacy OS" (like Windows or GNU/Linux) from an external USB or FW drive, at least not on a MacIntel computer. For your other question check out the sticky FAQ at the top of this forum section, then you be the judge whether it is worth the hassle or not.
