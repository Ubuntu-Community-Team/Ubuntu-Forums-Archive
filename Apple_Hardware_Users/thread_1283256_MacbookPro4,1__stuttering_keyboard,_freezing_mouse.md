When you say:

> **produnis said:**
> (by the way: It does not matter if I plug in an external usb-mouse or usb-keyboard).

does this mean the external keyboard/mouse doesn't work either?  Might be a stupid question, but i found on my mbp4,1 that i had an almost identical problem to you, but it was due to the mac hardware.  

There's a ribbon cable that comes from the keyboard/mouse logic that is pinched internally, and pressure on the battery can make the connection fail on and off.  I had to get it repaired by apple.

On the other hand, if it doesn't work with external keyboard and mouse, then it probably isn't the hardware, and is software based.  Hope this helped.  Good luck figuring it out.
