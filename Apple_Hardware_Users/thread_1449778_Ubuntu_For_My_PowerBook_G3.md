### Post by B_Free on 2010-04-08
Make sure you have at least 256 RAM. If Karmic doesn't work for you, try 
[http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/releases/6.06.1/](http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/releases/6.06.1/)
The PPC version, Live CD. Burn the iso image to the disk. Insert the CD in the machine, hold down the C key as if you are installing a Mac OS. You will be presented a screen of words. At one point it will say boot_
Just press return. It will take awhile to boot.After it does, play with the OS. If you like it, there is an installer on the desktop. Press install. Answer the questions asked, but just let it partition the disc on its own. Installing should take awhile. Maybe an hour and a half. If it seems to hang, be patient. Hang around, because it will ask questions. There's more. I am sure you'll be back.
