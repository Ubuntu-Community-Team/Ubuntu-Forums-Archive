### Post by sviola on 2011-02-26
Hello,

I can answer a two of your questions...

There is a program in Ubuntu called [OpenOffice.org]("http://www.openoffice.org") that can do basically anything MS Office can. It is compatible with Microsoft Office formats, so you can view powerpoints and Word documents with few compatibility issues. There are also other programs available if it does not satisfy your needs, but OpenOffice.org comes installed on Ubuntu by default.

Something you should know before wiping your disk is that many people suggest to keep an installation of Mac OS X for installing firmware updates (which may be required to be installed in order for your computer to function properly). I would also suggest you take a look at the page on [installing Ubuntu on a Mac]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MactelSupportTeam/AppleIntelInstallation#Single-Boot:%20Ubuntu%20Only") on the Ubuntu wiki first.

I hope that helps. :)
