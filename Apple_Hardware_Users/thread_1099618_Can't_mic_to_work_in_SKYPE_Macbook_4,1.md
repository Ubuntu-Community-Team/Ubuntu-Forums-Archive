### Post by BuffaloBill on 2009-09-30
Hey guys,

I know I'm a bit late for the topic, but here's a link to a thread in the Skype forums where someone gives some 'tweak' solution:

[http://forum.skype.com/index.php?showtopic=98576](http://forum.skype.com/index.php?showtopic=98576)

What the guy did:
1. open volume control
2. go to edit/preferences and check all the possible options and then close preferences
3. go to 'recording' tab and click on the microphone to disable it
4. go to options tab and choose 'front mic' instead of 'microphone'
5. close volume control
6. open volume control again
7. go to 'recording' tab and click on the microphone to enable it again
8. go to options tab and choose back 'microphone' instead of 'front mic'
9. close volume control

Needed a little fiddling, but it works!
