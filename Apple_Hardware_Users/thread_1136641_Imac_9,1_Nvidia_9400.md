### Post by MikaelHolber on 2009-04-25
Just wanted to give imac-owners a heads up if you have the "Cheap" 9400 card.I hade problems with nvidia 180.44 with AWN and compiz/firefox. After upgrading to the PPA-version fixed it all!

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/jaunty/+source/nvidia-graphics-drivers-180/+bug/364965](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/jaunty/+source/nvidia-graphics-drivers-180/+bug/364965)

Follow the instructions to add the the ubuntu-x to your repos and update the 180-driver. 

Now if only Micah get the patch for the internal speaker-problem we are all set! 

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/alsa-driver/+bug/346170](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/alsa-driver/+bug/346170)
