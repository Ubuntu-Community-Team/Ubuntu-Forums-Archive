### Post by jimv on 2009-06-14
Step 1:  Turn capslock off.

Step 2:  When installing Ubuntu, select your thumbdrive as the disk to install to.

Step 3:  On the final installation screen (before you click Install), click Advanced and install the boot loader onto the thumbdrive.

Now you have bootable Ubuntu thumbdrive.
