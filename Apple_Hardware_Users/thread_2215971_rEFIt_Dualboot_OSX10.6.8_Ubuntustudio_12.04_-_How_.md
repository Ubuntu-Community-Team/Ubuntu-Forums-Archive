@matjoe:

Interesting that "you couldn't find anything on this topic" . . . as I have addressed it here many times, as have others.  The short answer is that the general wisdom suggests ****not**** wiping OSX, among other reasons as you state rEFIt lives there or just in front . . . and for purposes of firmware stuff OSX is the default system.  I have a triple boot system on my '10 MBPro w/two partitions for OSX and one w/linux . . . in actual fact it takes a fair amount of tweaking to get linux to run the computer as well as OSX . . . e.g., the heat/fan control area.  It's fun to have the option of using either system, but I, and numerous others here have suggested to those numerous others to keep OSX--perhaps you can shrink the area it uses to give more space to linux.
