### Post by cyberdork33 on 2008-10-22
responding to your duplicate post...

You do not need to mess with Beryl (or XGL for that matter). Compiz Fusion is the same thing as beryl just the newer version.

Usually when you enable compositing (desktop effects) and you get a white screen, that is an indication that your 3D acceleration is not working properly to support compositing. You need to make sure that you have done what you are supposed to for 3D acceleration with your card. Since you have a PPC mac, you cannot use the binary drivers from nvidia (or ati for that matter).
