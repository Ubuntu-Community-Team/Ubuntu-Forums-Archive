### Post by shawnhcorey on 2010-05-16
I have recently installed Xubuntu on my PowerBook G4 Titanium with no problem.  However, I used a [USB drive]("http://mac.linux.be/phpBB3/viewtopic.php?f=10&t=85") to do so.  If you do feel comfortable working "under the hood", then this method is not for you.  :)

You could try the Alternative CD image.  The only difference between it and the Desktop CD is that it used the text-based installer, rather than the GUI one.

The Desktop CD will not fit on a standard CD.  Buy an oversize one of 800 MB or larger.

Keep your burn speed down to 4 or less, especially if you CD drive is older (are they all?)
