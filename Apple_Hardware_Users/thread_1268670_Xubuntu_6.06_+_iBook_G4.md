### Post by MikeTheC on 2009-09-17
Can't speak to wireless, but the Flash issue is fairly common and actually widely discussed here.

Adobe does not produce a PPC version of their Flash plug-in for anything except Mac OS X.

Your only option is to use Gnash, which is a <100% functional open-source re-implementation of Flash.

The only thing I might say about your wireless situation is (and others here who are more knowledgeable, please feel free to correct me on this) you would need a PPC-compatible Linux driver for it. I honestly don't know if the stock-standard driver will work for you or not.
