### Post by ZeroLinux on 2010-05-02
If you're going to use external sound through HDMI (I think it is an extreme and even may not work) - use Digital Stereo HDMI, but I think it will not work and will be useless. I have not iMac 27 that allows this.

Your iMac has only 2 speakers, therefore my choice is an "Analog Stereo" - the simplest way.

If it is possible to use "Analog Surround 4.0" you can use it. But it will be converted to "Analog Stereo" by a mixer.
