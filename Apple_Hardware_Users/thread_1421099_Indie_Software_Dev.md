### Post by alexmurray on 2010-03-03
Depends if its any good - if the app offers something that isn't already available to Linux users people may buy it, but the free software culture is generally against paying for software, as so much of what they already use is free and so people just expect to not pay for software.

There isn't really an app marketplace for Linux anyway, so it would have to be something really special, unique and useful for people to pay for it.
