### Post by Zookalicious on 2010-11-24
Unless you went into the phone and started erasing or tampering with things then no it shouldn't. I plug my iPhone into ubuntu to listen to music all the time and I even swap music files between Ubuntu and my iPhone with no problems. iTunes still sees it just fine on my mac partition and syncs normally. 
you might have to perform a factory restore by setting your iPhone to DFU mode and then plugging it into iTunes. (Don't worry, it will restore afterwards from your most recent backup).
