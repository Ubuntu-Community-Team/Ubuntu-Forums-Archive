### Post by joshdudeha on 2009-04-05
Normally on a CD drive, there is a tiny little hole next to it.
Get a straightened out pin or paper clip and stick it in the hole, the cd will come out regardless of if it is on or not. 
Hope this helped :)

### Post by guja_nebeska on 2009-04-12
Yes, that's the best solution which works. Mouse button on touch pad.

If anyone is interested, there's one more bizzar solution for macbooks. When rebooting (for example) after some installation (of some other distro let's say, since Ubuntu auto ejects CD after installation) when rebooting, take your macbook, turn side where's cd drive (right side in my case) towards ground and hold Mac eject button, right from F12.

And CD ejects. :-D
