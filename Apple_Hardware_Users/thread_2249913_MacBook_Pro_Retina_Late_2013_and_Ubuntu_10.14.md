### Post by este.el.paz on 2014-10-25
Do you mean 14.10?  Or you are installing an old version of Ubuntu, i.e., 10.14???  I'm assuming you mean 14.10 . . . probably you should do your install again . . . but, did md5sum number check out OK?  And, is Ubuntu going in ***after*** OSX on the GParted table??

And, another thing I found on the REFInd thing is, sometimes it takes a few shutdowns and reboots to get it to "figure out" what is going on . . . .  Point being, there could be any number of problems, if recovery mode doesn't find your fix, check the md5sum number, and try the install again.  Then when it's finished, shut the computer down and cold boot . . . see what happens.  Possibly shut down again several times.
