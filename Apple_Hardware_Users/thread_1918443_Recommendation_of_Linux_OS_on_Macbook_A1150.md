### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-02-01
The most lightweight Ubuntu distro is [Lubuntu]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lubuntu"). It's a pretty solid implementation, and I have always found it to work well.

There are other non-Ubuntu lightweight distros such as [Crunchbang]("http://crunchbanglinux.org/") and [Bodhi]("http://www.bodhilinux.com/"), but I've not tried them. For the ultimate in lightweight, you could try [Puppy]("http://puppylinux.org/"), which is *extremely* basic.

EDIT: If you download Lubuntu, you can burn it a CD (or a USB flash disk) and test it without installing. That will give you an idea of whether it will work on your machine.
