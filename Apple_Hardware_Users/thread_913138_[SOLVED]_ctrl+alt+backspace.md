### Post by cyberdork33 on 2008-09-07
> **JECHO said:**
> what is the equivilent kestroke on a macbook pro? (there is no backspace button and ctrl+alt+delete doesnt work)

The backward delete key is the same thing as backspace. The only thing different about it is the way Apple labels it. forward delete (which is just 'delete' to everyone else) when used with CTRL+ALT brings up the system monitor which is what the other poster thought you were talking about.
