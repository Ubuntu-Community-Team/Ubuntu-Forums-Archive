### Post by timsorr on 2016-07-01
Hopefully this helps anyone else with this problem.  I found a work around.  I hooked up an external cd drive through usb.  I didn't think this would work since the interface is still usb, not ide or sata, but it did.  I burned a cd using disk utility that refit was able to see and load.  Cheers

### Post by CDR Services on 2016-07-09
I just went a few rounds with a Mac Mini 2007 and it is a royal pain! I tried everything I could find from a Linux USB Loader, Unetbootin, rEFIt, and they either didn't work or "were not supported in this version of OSX" 10.6.8.  I ended up just removing the Hard Drive and putting it in my samsung laptop and installing 16.04 like normal (erasing the Mac OS)! I put it back in the Mac and after 30 seconds or so of white screen it booted and worked fine! All the hardware was supported including the upgraded Apple Wireless n card and after the updates were done it cut the white screen down to 5 or 6 seconds and now plays 720p video like butter!
