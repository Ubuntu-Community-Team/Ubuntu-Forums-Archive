### Post by Fox11 on 2010-01-10
Actually, because the metal is Aluminum, it should help to keep it cooler :P. Macbook Pros are notorious for getting hot- but this is what happens when tons of high-end hardware is stuffed into a thin case with little breathability! It really shouldn't be used on your lap- you'll practically burn yourself! This why they call it a "notebook" and not a "laptop" lol.

I've never had it overheat or anything, but I wouldn't suggest running it with the lid closed (gotta keep some airflow going). You can download programs to alter the fan speed, but I'm not sure it would really make that much of a difference (it would just make the MBP noisier). You get used to it, and as long as you're not running a gazillion things at once, 90% of the case stays fairly cool to the touch- that one corner, though, does get hot!
