### Post by Derrick Zuk on 2009-11-02
I had the same problem on my MBPro. Is the driver installed and activated in System > Administration > Hardware Drivers? Mine was not listed in Hardware Drivers, so I had to put the Ubuntu CD back in and make it a software source (in System > Administration > Software Sources) so that it would show up in Hardware Drivers to install. Then I rebooted and it worked perfectly.

I'm not one of the pros around here, but that's what I had to do for mine, so maybe it'll work for you.

Hope this helps.
