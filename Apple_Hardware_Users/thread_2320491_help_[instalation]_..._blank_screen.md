### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-04-14
Forget 15.04. No longer supported. Try 14.04 LTS or 16.04 LTS. 16.04 is not officially released for about a week, but if you update/upgrade it regularly you'll get to the final release when it gets here. LTS releases have fives years support. 15.10 is supported until the middle of this year (interim releases are supported for nine months).

You should also get the Sony up to date. Unsupported, end-of-life releases receive no updates, and that includes security. The more obsolete they get, the more vulnerable.
