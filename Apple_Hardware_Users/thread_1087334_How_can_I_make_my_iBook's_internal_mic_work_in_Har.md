### Post by buntuLo on 2009-03-05
i'm on a different hardware (MBP5.1) and kubuntu version (intrepid), but it might be the same for you: in kmix i enabled 'mic' 'capture' 'digital' and highered their levels, and set the first 'input source' to 'front mic'. if any of these items is missing in the mixer, you can add it from kmix\settings\configure_channels.
