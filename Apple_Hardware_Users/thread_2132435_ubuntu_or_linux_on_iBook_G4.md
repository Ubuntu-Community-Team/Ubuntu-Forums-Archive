Try [Lubuntu 12.10 PPC]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Lubuntu/GetLubuntu#Mac_systems"). Burn a bootable CD of it, insert the CD, and hold down "c" while it boots up. You should get a boot menu, from which you can boot from the CD. The installer will step you through it from there.
