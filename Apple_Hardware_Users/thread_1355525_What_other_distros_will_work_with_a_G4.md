DonI'd recommend if you can to try again the 9.04, maybe even the 9.10 tho I had problems mounting drives after installing 9.10. My second choice would be Debian 5.0.3 for PPC.

You could also try:
Fedora 12 for PPC:
[http://fedoraproject.org/en/get-fedora-ppc](http://fedoraproject.org/en/get-fedora-ppc) (I think it's running 2.6.31.6 kernel) -- this may be a bit too much for your system tho you can scale it down, trim the services, I guess

openSUSE 11.1 for PPC:
[http://download.opensuse.org/distribution/11.1/iso/](http://download.opensuse.org/distribution/11.1/iso/) (look for the *openSUSE-11.1-DVD-ppc.iso* 3.8 GB; I think it has 2.6.27.7 kernel) -- know that the 11.1 is the last *officially* supported PPC version so the future upgrades are questionable at best ... in fact, the last release of openSUSE (11.2) is void of the PPC version
