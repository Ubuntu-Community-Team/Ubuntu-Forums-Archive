### Post by inphektion on 2009-04-02
I'm not a mac fan.  In fact i've been quite the opposite.  However once I started looking for a new laptop and knew I wanted a 17" screen I quickly narrowed my options to the dell precision 17" monster, the HP workstation class one, and the new apple 17" mbp.
