### Post by ssam on 2008-05-06
the ubuntu version numbers are based on dates. 6.10 means 2006 october, so it is old an no longer supported. 8.04 (2008 april) has just come out. unless you have a stong reason for using 6.10, i suggets you try again with a new release.

the airport card should work fine. new releases have networkmanager (though avoid 7.10 as network manager was broken for some people). this makes setting up wireless pretty easy. you just click on the network icon in the top right of the screen, and choose a network.
