### Post by stream303 on 2009-04-12
NeoOffice is a good choice for PPC OSX, since the latest OpenOffice releases aren't really made for PPC lately.

Even if you can run OpenOffice with the latest for Intel, you may prefer how NeoOffice develops theirs, so check them both out.

The good part is that OpenOffice is available for most PPC Linux distros.

Other options include running lighter-weight apps such as Abiword and Gnumeric.  Of course KDE has their own built-in office setups.

The key here is to try and find a suite or programs compatible among OS's so you can leverage your knowledge no matter what system you are using.

(market speak for interoperability)
