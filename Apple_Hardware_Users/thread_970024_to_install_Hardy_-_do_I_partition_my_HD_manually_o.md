### Post by mkvnmtr on 2008-11-04
With youe Mac install disk use Disk Utility to form a partition for your Mac os. You can do this before or after you install the Mac os, The partition you wish to use for Ubuntu you can leave as free space. You don't have to form partitions for boot or swap. Then when you go to install choose the instalition method to use the largest free space.. The installer will form the partitions you need and should leave your Mac partitions alone. You should bach up every thing you need to save on your Mac partition before you start.
