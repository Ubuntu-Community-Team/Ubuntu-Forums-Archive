### Post by Helios747 on 2010-06-26
If you got a torrented copy of OS X, there is no guaranty that it will work. I'm not here to question the legitimacy of your disc though.

What you can try to do is at bootup, hold down the 'alt' key, it should show a "windows" hard drive (ubuntu), and you will hear the DVD drive spin up, after a little bit of the DVD drive reading the disc, it should pop up a new icon saying "Mac OS X Install Disc" or something similar. Choose that one.
