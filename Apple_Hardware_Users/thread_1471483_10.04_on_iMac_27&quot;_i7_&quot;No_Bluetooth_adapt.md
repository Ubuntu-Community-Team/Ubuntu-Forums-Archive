### Post by chillyperson23 on 2010-07-31
Same problem. It didn't work in 9.10 either.  I just bought a separate bluetooth adapter.  =\ Still annoying though.


I suspect its the way the smc handles the bluetooth adapter.  If no compatible bluetooth driver is available, it hides it from the OS being booted, and just shows it as an HID input device. Thats why bluetooth doesnt work in windows, but the mouse and keyboard work, until you install the driver for it.  And thats why if OS X sees another bluetooth adapter attached, when it disables the internal one, the mouse and keyboard work except for the special functions.
