### Post by marquez.eduardo on 2011-07-27
Hi there,

The startup tone and the light gray screen are good signs that the Mac has passed their own hardware tests, the next step is the load of the kernel and the kext, etc...

The startup tone volume are directly settled with the last system volume registered, and can be modified only by making a reset over the ram "PRAM" or reseting the parameters.

For example if an electric issue has occurred when your iMac was shut down with or without a no-break connected to an electric supply, maybe your iMac automatically reset their own parameters and as a result you're gonna hear a startup tone at full volume.

I would start worrying if the startup sound and the gray screen doesn't show up, so don't worry your iMac it's fine.
