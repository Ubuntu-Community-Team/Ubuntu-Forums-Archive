### Post by gnomeza on 2008-11-16
Updated my build of [Mozilla Lightning]("http://markferry.net/dl/lightning-0.9.linux-ppc.xpi") (thunderbird calendar plugin) for Linux PPC to 0.9 this afternoon - it's here: 
  [http://markferry.net/tech/2008/11/16/lightning-09-for-linux-ppc/](http://markferry.net/tech/2008/11/16/lightning-09-for-linux-ppc/)


Also included is [gdata-provider 0.5]("http://markferry.net/dl/gdata-provider-0.5.linux-ppc.xpi") which is required for Google calendar sync to work.

Usual caveats apply: this isn't an official build - so I can't guarantee it won't eat your data or kill your dog.

But it works for me on Gutsy PPC doing bi-directional syncing between Thunderbird 2.0.0.17 and Google Calendar.
