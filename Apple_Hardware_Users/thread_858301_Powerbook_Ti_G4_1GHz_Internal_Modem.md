### Post by ssam on 2008-07-15
i am pretty sure its a software modem [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Softmodem](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Softmodem) (basically a sound card that needs a special driver).

there has not been much progress on linux drivers for softmodems. most people who technical enough to write a driver have broadband conections. most linux users either have broadband or would by a hardware modem.

when i needed to use dial up on my powerbook i used a usb-to-serial adapter and a serial modem.
