### Post by P4man on 2010-10-31
Probably means that either your network card doesnt support WPA2 (considering the age of a g3 that seems very plausible) or the driver doesnt support it. Id suggest changing the access point to WEP

### Post by linuxopjemac on 2010-10-31
P4man is correct. The original airport card (not the Extreme) can't do WPA. Only WEP works. Even that seems to be a challenge as I see many people trying to get a secured WEP connection. It will probably give a "bad password" problem. Good luck.
