### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-09-10
Early powermacs had a 650MB CD limitation.  So if your distro is 700MB and you burned it on a 700MB disk, your CD player may not read it completely so your installation may be incomplete.

Also, the onboard batteries die, and the PMU needs the battery to function properly.  It is soldered on the board and not easily replaceable, as most Mac products are designed to be thrown away when new and shiny comes out.

So, replace the battery first and try again.  Let us know how difficult it is.
