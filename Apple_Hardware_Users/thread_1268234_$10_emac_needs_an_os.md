### Post by tgalati4 on 2009-09-19
Older macs can only read 650 MB CD-R's.  Stick a 700 MB disk with a 680 MB ubuntu image and you will have problems.  You need to find an older 650 MB CD-R (which are now near impossible to find) and burn the alternate (384 MB) PPC installer, or stick with Panther/Tiger.

### Post by marshag63 on 2009-09-19
It could not hurt to make sure you clear our PRAM before booting - I do this 3 times before I press Control + C to boot from CD-ROM.

To clear PRAM:

Hold down Command, Option, P and R keys at the same time - hold it down until you hear it chime at least three times, then press Control + C.

Also, how are you burning you linux install CD?
