### Post by ernsteiswuerfel on 2017-04-07
Yep, your partition layout is a mess. :grin:

As far as I can see you only have two big Apple Partitions where your OSX could be, /dev/sda11 (131.2G) and /dev/sdb10 (232.8G). So [COLOR=#000000]*macosx=/dev/sdb10 *[/COLOR]is fine for the second one. But don't forget that editing /etc/yaboot.conf is not enough, you have to write the partition information back to disk with **sudo ybin -v**.
