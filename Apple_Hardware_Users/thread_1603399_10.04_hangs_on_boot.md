### Post by linuxopjemac on 2010-11-03
I had a similar thing today. I booted and I saw a black screen with a blinking cursor. Only a hard power off worked. I presumed it had to do with Grub2  not being stable. I went back to Grub legacy and now I am able to boot again.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Uninstalling%20GRUB%202](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Uninstalling%20GRUB%202)

BTW you have to chroot into your system from a liveCD to perform this operation
