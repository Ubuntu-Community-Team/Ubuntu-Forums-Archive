### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-06-25
I am afraid there is not much that anyone here can do to help you.  The repositories for 5.10 have not been active for some time and wioll only be available with edits to the /etc/apt/sources.list file to point to the end of life repos, [http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/releases/breezy/](http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/releases/breezy/) but even then there will be no security updates of any kind.

I have no idea personally about mac software or hardware and nor have I any knowledge of other Linux distros that might work on that machine but I would advise you not to go online with such an old unsupported version of Ubuntu should you get it working.
