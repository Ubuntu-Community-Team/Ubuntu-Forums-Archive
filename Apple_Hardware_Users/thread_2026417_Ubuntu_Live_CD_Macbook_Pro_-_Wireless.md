### Post by 2blue on 2012-07-15
I think it is possible to make what they call a frugal install of ubuntu. A savefile is made on the harddrive and you boot in CD, but trough the savefile you have storage and saved setting like any other os. There is no change to your main system other than a folder stored on the hd. I personally have never tried it with ubuntu, but other distros like puppy linux are based on this (there is even a macpuppy or two).
