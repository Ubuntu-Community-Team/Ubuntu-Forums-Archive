### Post by Earl_Maroon on 2010-08-22
For music production on a laptop you would need an external sound card as no internal would suffice. Other than that, all you need specifically is a fast enough processor and enough memory. Whether the computer is a Mac or a generic has nothing to do with it. Macs are lovely machines, but they are overpriced for what you get. The only advantage a Mac has for music production that I know of is the capability to run Logic Pro. If you want to get a Mac that is a valid decision, but if Logic Pro isn't important to you reevaluate your decision.
