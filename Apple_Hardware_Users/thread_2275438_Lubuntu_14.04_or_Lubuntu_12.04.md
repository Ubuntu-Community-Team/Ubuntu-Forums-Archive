### Post by PaulW2U on 2015-05-03
> **caesar753 said:**
> but Ubuntu 12.04 should be a Long Term Support till April 2017 ([https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases)), why should Lubuntu not be supported any more?

Some of the flavours decide on shorter support periods for their LTS releases because their development teams are very small. Xubuntu is another flavour that decided not to support their 12.04 release for five years.

You'll still get updates for any packages shared with Ubuntu but any Lubuntu specific packages will not be updated.
