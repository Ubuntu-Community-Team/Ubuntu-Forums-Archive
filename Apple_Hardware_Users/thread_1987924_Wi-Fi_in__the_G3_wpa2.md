### Post by theos911 on 2012-05-27
Powerbook G3s with internal airport do not support wpa2 under any OS. They can be convinced to do WPA1 though. Look into your router settings and try setting your router to WPA1 only. (Your network will also need to be wireless b or b/g. N will not work and g only will not work.)
