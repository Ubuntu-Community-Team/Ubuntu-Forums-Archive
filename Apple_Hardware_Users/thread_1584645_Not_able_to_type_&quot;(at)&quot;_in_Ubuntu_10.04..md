### Post by linuxopjemac on 2010-09-29
Did you choose the right layout in System -> Preferences -> Keyboard -> Layouts

I for example have a Dutch keyboard. I chose Netherlands Macintosh as keyboard and in Layout Options "Key to choose 3rd level" I have "Left Alt" I can then type &#8364; with "Left-Alt-2".

Hope this helps.
