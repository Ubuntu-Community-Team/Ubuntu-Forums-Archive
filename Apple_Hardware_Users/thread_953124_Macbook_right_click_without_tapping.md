### Post by kosumi68 on 2008-10-20
Clarification: Two-finger clicking out-of-the-box works for multi-touch enabled trackpads beginning with the *next* major release of the xserver-xorg-input-synaptics package. In Intrepid, it currently only works for the BCM5974-based trackpads found in MacBook Pro 4, the MacBook Air, and the new unibody MacBook 5. For setting up older MacBooks, please refer to the sticky wiki pages at the top of the page.
