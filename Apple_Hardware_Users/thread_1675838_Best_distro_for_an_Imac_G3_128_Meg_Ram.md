### Post by linuxopjemac on 2011-01-26
You can find information about hardware in everymac.com. You will find your iMac model there. I guess if you have the same kind of hard disk, that you can just replace it. On everymac.com you will also find info how to open it up:
[http://www.everymac.com/systems/apple/imac/faq/imac-g3-hard-drive-upgrade-replacement-instructions.html](http://www.everymac.com/systems/apple/imac/faq/imac-g3-hard-drive-upgrade-replacement-instructions.html) iFixit also gives info:
[http://www.ifixit.com/Browse/iMac_G3](http://www.ifixit.com/Browse/iMac_G3)

Instead of installing from CD, I would go for USB. You will find information how to install from USB in my installation instructions for MintPPC 9.2.

Good luck.
