### Post by Haneef Mubarak on 2011-08-15
After burning the ISO to the disc using Disk Utility, eject the Disc, wait a few seconds, and replace the disc back into your Mac. Close all open applications. Next, go to System Preferences --> System --> Startup Disk. If you've done everything right so far, you should see your Ubuntu LiveCD next to your "Macintosh HD". Select it and then click restart. Upon restart, your mac should boot up to the LiveCD and you should be good to go!
