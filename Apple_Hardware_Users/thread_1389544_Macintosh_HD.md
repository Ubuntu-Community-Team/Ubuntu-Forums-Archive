P. S.: I figured out the Macintosh HD part:
1.  Download [a good Macintosh HD icon]("http://www.elettra.trieste.it/IT/uploads/MacHD.png").
2.  Make a new launcher.
3.  Change its icon to the one you've just downloaded.
4.  Name it "Macintosh HD" and give it an apt description, like, "Browse all local and remote disks and folders accessible from this computer."
4.  The launcher's command is: 'nautilus --no-desktop computer:'
