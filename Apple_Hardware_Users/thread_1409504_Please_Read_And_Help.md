### Post by Ken147 on 2010-02-18
**Step 1:** Go to Application>Utilities>Disk Utility 

**Step 2:** select the disk that you want to repair

**Step 3:** go to the First aid tab near the top (don't ask why...apple calls the tabs) 

**Step 4:** Click "Verify Disk" (you have to do that before           you can repair the disk)

**Step 5:** after its done verifying it should tell you in red lettering that it needs to be repaired, so then click on "Repair Disk".
