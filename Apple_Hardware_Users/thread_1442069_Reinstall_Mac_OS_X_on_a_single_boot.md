### Post by Nandux on 2010-03-29
If you want to reinstall OSX on your machine as a single boot you will need a DVD of OSX, don't forget that it will format your computer and as such you should backup your data. If you just have a .dmg image of your OSX disk, you might want to give this tool a try to convert if from a .dmg to a .iso.

Dmg2Img - [http://vu1tur.eu.org/tools/](http://vu1tur.eu.org/tools/)
