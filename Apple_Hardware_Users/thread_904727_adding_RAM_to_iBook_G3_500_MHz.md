### Post by jimv on 2008-08-29
Those iBooks use older SD-RAM, which is much different than that stick of DDR2 RAM that you have.

If you wanted to put more RAM in, you could do two sticks of PC-100:
[http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16820159402](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16820159402)

You could probably find some on eBay for really cheap.  You'd want PC-100 (speed) SO-DIMMS (physical size).
