ChristosIf you are talking about they internal iSight as opposed to the external, firewire isights, then no, there are no such controls. The internal iSights have a fixed focus and no brightness controls. Brightness can be simulated by adjusting the brightness of the image with software, much like you would in GIMP or photoshop. These kinds of controls would have to be built into the software you are using though (like cheese and skype).
