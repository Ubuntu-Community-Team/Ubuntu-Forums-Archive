### Post by epicoder on 2009-09-09
I don't really know all that much, but you can reduce the chance of damage to your system when it crashes by holding SysRq(Alt + Print Screen) and typing in this order REISUB. Nothing will appear to happen until you hit B, at which point your system will reboot. If you need a mnemonic, try Raising Elephants Is So Utterly Boring.
