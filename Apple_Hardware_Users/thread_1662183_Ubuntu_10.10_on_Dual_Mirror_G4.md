### Post by shawnhcorey on 2011-01-07
The Desktop CD does not fit onto a standard CD.  Use the Alternative CD instead.  The only difference between the two is that the Desktop uses the GUI installer, and the Alternative uses the text-based one.  All the other software is the same.

You can also buy oversized CDs.  They come in 800MB and 900MB sizes.

Also, you can create and then boot from a USB drive:
