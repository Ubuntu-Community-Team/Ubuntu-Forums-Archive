### Post by dondiego2 on 2011-01-12
I have a 5th gen 60 GB iPod.  It's not new but it works perfectly fine in Ubuntu.  I use Rhythmbox and it recognizes it and I can copy music to and from it easily.  I also use gPodder to subscribe to podcasts and it syncs the podcasts to my iPod no problem.  I would think a newer iPod would work the same way.  You can't hurt it by just plugging it in and seeing if Ubuntu will recognize it.  I've even had Rockbox on mine and it could play ogg files and flac files but I ditched it just because I like the Apple interface better.
