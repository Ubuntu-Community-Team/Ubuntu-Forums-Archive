### Post by danish-bronco on 2020-08-27
Is your USB stick formatted as GPT or MBR? If it's MBR, on this generation of Mac, there's a good chance there's no support because they can't boot into "Legacy" mode anymore. You should try to copy Ubuntu on a GPT-formatted USB stick.
