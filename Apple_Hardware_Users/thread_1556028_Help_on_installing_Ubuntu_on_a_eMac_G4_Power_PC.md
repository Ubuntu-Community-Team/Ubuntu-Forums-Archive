### Post by shawnhcorey on 2010-08-22
Download the ISO image and burn it to a DVD.  Reboot the machine and hold down the "C" key after you hear the starting chime.  The DVD should run and after several minutes, you'll see the desktop.  On it, there should be an icon called Install.
