### Post by Brightbelt on 2008-05-25
Hi,
 I have noticed that there is some concern in this forum for iMacs and Macbook pros (or macbooks, I can't recall) in that they *might* run a little hotter on Ubuntu than OS X. 
It's being debated anyways... Some of us (myself included) have concluded that there IS a difference but that it is very slight and most probably entirely negligible. Don't hold me to that however. 

But I'm not certain this goes the same for a Mac Pro Tower; it's just an issue you might explore for your own piece of mind. Do a search in this forum like, "Mac Pro runs hot" and see what you find. 

I will share this: I've done a dual boot with Ubuntu on my iMac which is my personal desktop computer.  I do also have root access to a Mac Pro for professional use, and  I intentionally have refrained from putting Ubuntu on it. And when you come down to it, this decision is simply a "better safe than sorry" decision. 

But then, I'm not a Linux expert either, nor a coder. So I'd have a lot harder of a time recovering from serious problems if they came up.

 Good Luck, whatever you decide.
