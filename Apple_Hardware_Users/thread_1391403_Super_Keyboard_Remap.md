### Post by ghostborg on 2010-01-26
Not sure, sorry in advance, but check in System=>Preferences=>Keyboard.
Keyboard Preferences has a tab "Layouts" and on that page is a button called Layout Options. Click on the arrows to expand options. Thought Alt/Win key behavior might have something to help you.

I have a slim usb Apple keyboard hooked up and under layouts my Keyboard Model is Apple and in the Selected Layout box it is USA Macintosh.
