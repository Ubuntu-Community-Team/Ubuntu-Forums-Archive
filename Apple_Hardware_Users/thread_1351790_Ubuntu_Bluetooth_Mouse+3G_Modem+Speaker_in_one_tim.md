### Post by fbugnon on 2009-12-11
Hi,

I don't have the same exact model (**MacBook 1,1**) but if my experience can help...

With 9.10, for the first time, bluetooth works as one should expect, i.e., out of the box, without any need to do additional configuration / work-around.

MOUSE + KEYBOARD
I use apple bluetooth mouse and keyboard (old white-model) and all I had to do was delete their configuration under OSX first and then configure them in Ubuntu. 
(for some reason they will not accept to be configured under Ubuntu if they are already configured under OSX.  But once you have them paired with Ubuntu you can pair them in OSX and they will work on both, no problems)

3G MODEM
I don't have one, but I tested two different models sold in the Brazilian market once.  Both worked on Ubuntu out of the box (and, as a matter of fact, only one with OSX).

SPEAKER
Never tried.
