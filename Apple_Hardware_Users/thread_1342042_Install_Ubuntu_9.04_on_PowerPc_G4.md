### Post by svtguy88 on 2009-12-02
Sounds like a corrupted burn or image.  Try re-downloading the image, and burn it at the lowest speed possible.  

Does the disc show up at all when you hold opt on boot?  I had a problem where the image would show up as a boot option (I had the image restored on to a partition and was going to boot from that, rather than a burned disc), but would refuse to boot to the image when you select it.

I ended up having to burn two discs.  The first one failed (burned at a high speed), and the second one worked flawlessly.
