### Post by CJN on 2009-11-18
I can't speak to most of those problems as I triple boot Win7, snow leopard, karmic (not in that order) BUT I can confirm that the restricted drivers are NOT INSTALLED when you install from live cd but they are available when you boot from live cd (Ironically because they require a restart to take effect and you can't restart a live cd whilst keeping changes to the OS or settings) What you need to do is add the MACTEL repositories to your list and get a key therefore. I don't have the specifics memorized but a quick search should turn up plenty of resources.
