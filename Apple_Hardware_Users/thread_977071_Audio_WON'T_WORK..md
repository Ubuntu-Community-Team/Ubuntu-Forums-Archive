### Post by rakan21 on 2008-11-30
upgrade to 8.10. Almost all of the drivers are working. Including audio and wifi and so on. 
And to make your volume a bit more louder. Right click on the volume icon. Open Volume control. Check Master, PCM, Front (which is basically like the most important of all), and surround. Put everything up to full. You'll probably notice that its louder then OS X, so be careful. 

If nothing works try this. [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBookPro#Sound%20/%20Audio%20&%20Microphone](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBookPro#Sound%20/%20Audio%20&%20Microphone)
