### Post by a.ross.cohen on 2010-12-17
No matter what OS you run on a USB hard drive it will run slower than it should. You're best bet is to fix your internal hardware. If it was just the drive you can probably put a new one in, they're about 100 dollars for a descent sized WD SATA black drive. Unless part of your logic board blew out causing the hard drive issue you should be fine.
