### Post by moviemaniac on 2008-11-04
As far as I know (only read about it) you can remove OSX completely but it's at least not recommended as you can only get firmware-updates for your machine via the update-function in OSX. You can resize the OSX-partition to a few gigabytes, though, so you won't lose much space.
The single touch touchpad will not be an issue AFAIK, but there will be more nowledgeable persons with you shortly.
