### Post by warreno on 2009-08-16
VB's documentation seems a little out of date. I just got Jaunty running in VB on my Mini (OSX.5.8) and it was quirky and required a few restarts. I think the thing to remember to do is to click ONCE on your Ubuntu virtual machine in the VB app (with the virtual machine shut down), get to "Devices", and unmount/eject the CD image.

I did mine from an .iso, though, so your mileage may vary.
