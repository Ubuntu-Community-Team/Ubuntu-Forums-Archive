### Post by kevin160 on 2017-11-29
Yep, definitely use rEFInd.  If you have further issues, please be specific about which model Mac you have, as the Apple EFI boot can differ slightly.  And be careful about following advice for non-mac UEFI setups, as much of that does not apply to Macs.
