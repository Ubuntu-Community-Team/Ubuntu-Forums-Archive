### Post by nevius on 2011-06-26
Check out the help page for a MacBook Pro 8.1 here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBookPro8-1/Natty](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBookPro8-1/Natty)  MacBooks are good if you want to use / dual-boot OS-X. If you are just going to use Linux you may want to consider a PC laptop with comparable hardware to the MacBook Pro. In that case there is no real advantage to the apple hardware over a similarly priced PC laptop.  BTW according to the community documentation, the current generation of MacBook Pros only get 3.5-4.5 hours of battery life in Ubuntu. This means it is about on par with a similarly priced Elitebook or Thinkpad.
