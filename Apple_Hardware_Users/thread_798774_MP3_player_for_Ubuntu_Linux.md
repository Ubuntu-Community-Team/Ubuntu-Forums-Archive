### Post by damis648 on 2008-05-18
Actually, all you have to do is to enable disk use via itunes in windows and then in ubuntu run your music managment program (ie Rhythmbox, Amarok, GTKpod, or Songbird... I use rhythmbox as it is very itunes-like in behaviour) and plug you ipod in. In the "Source" list of rhythmbox you will see your ipod. Just drag and drop music into it.
