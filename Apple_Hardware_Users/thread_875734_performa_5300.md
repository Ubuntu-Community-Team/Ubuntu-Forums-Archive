### Post by tiresia on 2008-07-31
I installed Ubuntu Hardy on a PowerPC 7500 and it works, slow, but it works. I think you can install Ubuntu on your machine. I would try with an older version, maybe Edgy ([https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCDownloads](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCDownloads)). 
I guess, you know that Apple OldWorld PC cannot start from a CD. You need a bootloader, I suggest BootX. Here you can download it and you can find informations how you can use it: [http://www.penguinppc.org/bootloaders/bootx/](http://www.penguinppc.org/bootloaders/bootx/) 
Here is another useful link:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/OldWorldMacs](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/OldWorldMacs)

Are you sure, that you have 512 Mb RAM? According MacTracker Maximum RAM is 64 MB!!!
