### Post by cyberdork33 on 2008-11-10
Please start by checking the "Before You Post" link in my signature. It will allow you to determine your iMac version number and direct you to some documentation.

I would stick with Intrepid.

Unfortunately, there may be some issues with your wireless mouse (you will definitely want to get a hold of a wired mouse at least temporarily to use before you wireless mouse is setup).

Stretching your OSX partition back over the space used by Ubuntu is a bit of an issue since there are not any FOSS tools that can "grow" HFS+ partitions. You should be able to accomplish this with DiskUtility though.
