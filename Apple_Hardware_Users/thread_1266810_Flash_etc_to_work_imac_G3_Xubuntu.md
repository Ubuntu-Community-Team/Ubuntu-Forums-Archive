### Post by sweetleaf on 2009-09-15
Congratulations on your installation!

I'm afraid I can't offer you any good options for flash.

Adobe Flash is not available for PPC. For an open-source alternative, you could try gnash, with mozilla-plugin-gnash.

However, [it doesn't look like]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/search?keywords=gnash") gnash is supported on Dapper (6.06). To get gnash at all, you would need to upgrade to Hardy (8.04), and to get the latest version, you would need to upgrade to Jaunty (9.04).

Even with Jaunty, I was disappointed with the performance of gnash on my G3. In order to play youtube videos, I had to download the source code and recompile it with special options. There's an old, open bug [here]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/liboil/+bug/133495/").
