### Post by raronson on 2009-07-15
You already know this, and I know you're probably looking for another answer, but most mysterious post-install issues can be fixed by getting a new iso, burning it to new media, and starting over.  Every install (regardless of OS/architecture) has the potential to bork itself.  You may save time and have a usable machine sooner.  If not, you know it's not the disk image or media.  RAM, maybe?
