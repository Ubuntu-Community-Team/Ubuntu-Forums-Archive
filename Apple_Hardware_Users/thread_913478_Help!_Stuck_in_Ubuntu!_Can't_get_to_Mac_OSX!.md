### Post by Jarrkemaar on 2008-09-08
What I think the problem is is that OS X has set your computer to look solely to the CD drive for it's boot device. When you turn your computer on, hold the alt key. A list of drives should appear. Simply choose the OS X hard disk icon, then change your startup disk back to your main hard disk.

Hope this helps!

P.S. From now on, when booting live cds, just hold alt instead of changing the boot device.
