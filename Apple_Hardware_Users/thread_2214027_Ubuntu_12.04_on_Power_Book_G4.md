### Post by Joe_Johnston on 2014-03-30
I just helped my Uncle get Ubuntu 12.04 on his iBook G3 yesterday and we used [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/12.04/release/ubuntu-12.04-desktop-powerpc.iso](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/12.04/release/ubuntu-12.04-desktop-powerpc.iso) and burned it to a CD. You then just reboot and hold down C key and it should boot to Ubuntu and then follow the installation steps. 

He has 640mb RAM which helps with performance but I would only use one program at a time once it is up as we had issues when he had too many apps open.
