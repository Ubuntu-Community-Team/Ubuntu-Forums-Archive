### Post by mfox on 2008-08-09
You can get program menus at the top of your display with Gnome (Ubuntu) or KDE (Kubuntu), but it comes that way in Ubuntu.  You can even customize it that way in Xubuntu (XFCE).  Rhythmbox, which is part of the default installation of Ubuntu, is very iTunes-like and it comes with a plug-in that will give you the cover art.  It's my favourite music box (there are many on the Ubuntu repositories).  I haven't had any sound problems on my G4 PowerBook Al.
