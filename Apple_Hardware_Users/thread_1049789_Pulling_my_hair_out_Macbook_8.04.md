### Post by cyberdork33 on 2009-01-24
First, you should try to use Intrepid. It has the best support for your machine.

Second, please be sure to determine the proper version of your Mac. You can check the "Before you Post" link in my signature for information on that. Be sure to state the version of your Mac when you  request help so that we know what you have on your hands.

The only time I get a spinup / spin down behavior like that on my Mac is when I have a badly burned disc. Make sure to verify the download and disc before installing, and when burning, choose to burn at the slowest speed you can to increase compatibility.
