### Post by stream303 on 2008-11-24
Are you holding down the "C" key long enough after powerup for it to boot?  Are you burning the CD-R as an iso image, and not just copying it over?  Are you burning it at a slow enough speed for your cdrom to handle? - especially important if burning on a different box...  1X or 2X is not unheard of especially with older macs, or even newer ones that are getting weak, dusty, etc.

These are some of the most common issues.
