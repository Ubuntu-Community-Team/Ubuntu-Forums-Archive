### Post by braingram on 2010-07-03
The instructions at [http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop/get-ubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop/get-ubuntu/download)

state:

hdiutil convert-format UDRW -o ~/path/to/target.img ~/path/to/ubuntu.iso

however there should be a space between convert and -format: 

hdiutil convert -format UDRW -o ~/path/to/target.img ~/path/to/ubuntu.iso
