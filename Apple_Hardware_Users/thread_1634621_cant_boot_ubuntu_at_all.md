### Post by breimer273 on 2010-12-01
When you first turn the computer on press and hold the "option" key. After a little bit you will see the hard drive icon that will be labeled as "Macintosh HD" or something like that. Then wait until you see the CD icon appear. Most likely it will be labeled "Windows". Select the cd icon and the computer will then boot to the cd.
