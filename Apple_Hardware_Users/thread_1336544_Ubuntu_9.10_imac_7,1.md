The Hardware Drivers program only shows drivers that don't come preinstalled, and drivers where there are different alternatives.

What method are you trying to use to connect to the internet? Ethernet, 3G, wireless network, dial-up?

Try manually inputting the IP address, gateway, subnet mask and DNS details into the Network Manager applet (towards the right of the top panel, right-click using the F12 key, choose Edit Connections and go to the IPv4 Settings tab for your connection).
