### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-12-24
You can experiment with gnash, lightspark and adobe flashplugin by using the **Advanced Mode** of [Flash-Aid](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/). However, lightspark is only available for installation on Oneiric.

Another add-on I develop that is very popular among PowerPC users is [FlashVideoReplacer](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flashvideoreplacer/). It allows to watch YouTube videos with a different plugin, like gecko-mediaplayer or even launch them through an external player.
