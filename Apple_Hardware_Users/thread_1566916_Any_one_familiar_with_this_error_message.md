### Post by llawwehttam on 2010-09-03
I have seen this once before.

Sorry to be the bearer of bad news but it usually occurs when the computer's power supply is failing so the motherboard turns off USB devices to stop damage to them.
EDIT: Sometimes it can be fixed by enableing USB LEGACY MODE in the BIOS.
