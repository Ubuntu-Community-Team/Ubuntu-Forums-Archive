### Post by Ububurns on 2008-05-02
Although it is outdated, most of what you want is here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCFAQ]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PowerPCFAQ")

So: you will be able to get DVD playback to work.
(Also you are now able to install Sun's version of java, not just IBM's!)

Only **gnash** and **swfdec** are available for flash playback. If they can't play it, *give up* on trying to make it work, basically.  Thankfully flash video can usually be downloaded with the help of a Firefox plugin and viewed in vlc.
