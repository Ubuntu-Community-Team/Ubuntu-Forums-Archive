### Post by jmas13 on 2011-10-18
Hey, I have a MacBook 5.1 as well, but I switched to 11.10 from OSX. I'm not having the booting problem, but I had the same issue with the distorted screen on wake-up. I went into additional drivers and found that the graphics driver in use was not the most up to date version or even the recommended version. After switching the driver I haven't had the same problem waking my computer. That being said I've only tried twice. I hope this helps.
