### Post by cyberdork33 on 2009-04-13
> **cheapchump said:**
> I just installed 8.10 on my macbook 2.1. When I try to establish a connection with my bluetooth mighty mouse, it asks me to enter the pin code. How on earth do I enter a pin code on a mouse? What am I doing wrong?
your mouse probably has a preset pin code to connect to it (usually 0000 or 1111 or 1234). You enter this on your keyboard and this triggers the mouse to "allow" a connection.
