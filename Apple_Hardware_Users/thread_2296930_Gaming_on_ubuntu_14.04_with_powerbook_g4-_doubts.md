### Post by Bucky Ball on 2015-09-30
Welcome. If it runs a little slow with Ubuntu, try Xubuntu or Lubuntu. They are lighter and should make an old thing like that whiz just nicely. 

I know this is off-topic, but thought I'd throw it in. Less resources used by the OS means more resources for gaming. :)

Just reread your post. Less than 1Gb of RAM? Amazing you have Ubuntu running at all. [Lubuntu]("http://lubuntu.net/") is definitely worth exploring. It is an official Ubuntu flavour, supported by Canonical and these forums. :)

Ubuntu may run on your machine currently but I don't know what kind of user experience you will get doing anything beyond fiddling on the desktop (try web browsing with a few tabs open while working on a Libreoffice document, for instance). See how far you can push. Not far I imagine. It is unrealistic to think you are going to get much of a worthwhile gaming experience without at least more RAM, and perhaps not even then. You can only experiment and see how far you get. 

Good luck. :)

PS: [Here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Lubuntu/GetLubuntu/LTS#PowerPC_systems") is a link to the PPC (Power PC) download of Lubuntu 14.04 LTS for G4 machines.
