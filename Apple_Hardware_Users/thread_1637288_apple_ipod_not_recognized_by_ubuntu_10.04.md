### Post by jarviser on 2010-12-04
> **tusu said:**
> No, it is ipod 30 gb..i do not need any password when i use it in windows...

You need Rhythmbox open and ipod should show up about half way down the list just above Playlists (e.g. "Peters").

To get my 30GB Video Ipod working I went into Itunes (for the last time) Reset the ipod completely and set it for manual file management.  Don't know if that was necessary but I did it anyway.
Then in Rhythmbox I just dragged music into the ipod name using the mouse. 

The bonus is you can plug the ipod into another Ubuntu PC and drag the music back OUT into Rhythmbox and thence into the Music folder - can be very handy!.

I have not been able to manage pictures or videos however.
