### Post by stream303 on 2009-04-12
This is true - you can't run exe's, and on the ppc architecture, you aren't going to be running wine.  Don't count on good flash or 3D video support.  These are problems external to Linux.

If you do run in a multi-os environment, my suggestion is to stick to FOSS software that runs on the three major systems such as

Mozilla Firefox, Thunderbird, Sunbird, VLC etc.

As a mac user, you already have the basic bash shell and somewhat similar userland.  For Windows, you may want to investigate cygwin or other porting environments.

This way, you can leverage your knowledge among all three environments.
