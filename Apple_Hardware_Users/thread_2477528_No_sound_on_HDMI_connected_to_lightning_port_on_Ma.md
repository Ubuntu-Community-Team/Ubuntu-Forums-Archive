I probably can't do much to help. But, its possible as I had it happen to get video but no sound from HDMI when the cable has a wiggle issue. You can try wiggling the cable very lightly at both ends and see if the audio shows up.

There are other possibilities though such as audio passthrough setting, incompatible output setting, or whatever so its not exactly easy to diagnose without hands on.
