### Post by tomas.teijeiro on 2008-11-02
I've installed Intrepid Ibex amd64 version on a Macbook 2,1, and I have some keyboard troubles that seemed not well documented.

The first problem that I had was that the num-lock light doesn't light up when active. However, in the live-CD mode it works perfectly. What could be the reason?


The other and most important Trouble is with F11 and F12 keys. I've configured console-setup selecting pc105 key layout and right-apple as alt-gr. When I press F11 or F12 keys, they are recognized as right-click mouse event. This is very annoying, and I would like to know how could I configure these aspects. Oh, and in the Live-CD mode, it works perfectly.

Thanks for the replies, and congratulations for the amazing work

### Post by cyberdork33 on 2008-11-02
the F11 and F12 being right-click might be controlled by the mouseemu package.

---

### Post by tomas.teijeiro on 2008-11-02
Perfect! I've removed mouseemu and the num-lock light works! Of course, F11 now is F11 and F12 now is F12.

Thank you very much!
