### Post by frankbonatelli on 2011-06-19
Well I know its late in the game and maybe you have moved on in this but..................
I have the 2011 air with 128g ssd 4g ram 1.4 ghz coreduo
Ubuntu 11.04 runs smoothly on this device and I get 5.5hrs battery life while surfing unplugged!!

Two issues I had was the Nvidia drivers are not installed by default and so at first boot the screen goes a little haywire. Fidel around with the mouse till you find wifi connection manager and connect to the net. Run the proprietary drivers app and activate the nvidia driver. Rebooted and this was now a nice unity desktop.
Also note that I used the official ALTERNATIVE iso the 64bit version. Used an external dvd drive and it I was done.
