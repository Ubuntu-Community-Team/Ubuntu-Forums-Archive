### Post by HermanAB on 2016-02-09
Well, Mac OSX is based on BSD, all share a lot with Linux.  For example, the CUPS program is maintained by Apple and used by everyone.

So, you should not expect to see a spectacular difference between a Mac running OSX and Mac running PC-BSD or Linux.  At most the difference is like the difference between English and American - about 3 dB.
