### Post by marlls1989 on 2009-06-04
I try to mimic the same conf of OSX, disabling tapping, as you have the button to click.
But still the trackpad behaves oddly, even odder then a pc laptop in windows.

I bet it would that a major rewrite in the synaptic driver to please all mac users out there.

And the 1 second lock up after typing can be very annoying, I do not recommend that.

But I would recommend to disable tapping and dragging completely, setting the two fingers scroll on both horizontally and vertically and setting palm detection on, as the mac trackpad have such function.

Also Typing with your hand resting on the palm rest besides the trackpad helps a lot.
