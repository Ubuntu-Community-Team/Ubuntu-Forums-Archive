### Post by Adam's AI on 2010-04-13
I think burned discs should work as long as you have the right version  discs (I think osx discs that come with a particular model are made to  only work on that particular hardware).

Do you know someone that has a mac and leopard (or snow leopard) discs?
I once temporarily lost my disc when I needed to reformat, so with a friend, I booted up my laptop in  transfer mode (hold down the t button; not sure if you can still do  that now) to make it act like an external hd and connected it via firewire to his comp.

Next, we ran his disc  which on his computer but changed the installation target so it  could perform the installation on my laptop. Worked great (though I  found my own discs the next day :P and my friend laughed at me for wasting his afternoon)

If you do this, just be careful to  change the drive you wish to install on so you don't accidentally wipe  your friend's mac.
