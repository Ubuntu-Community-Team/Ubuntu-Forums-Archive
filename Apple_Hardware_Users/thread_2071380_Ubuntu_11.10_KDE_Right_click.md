### Post by valroadie on 2012-10-15
I have decided to just post in here my fix for the right click issue on my macbook 2,1. 
I HAVE moved to xfce, but this tool is specific for KDE. Though it works for either one and I assume it works for GNOME as well.

To make this easy and simple, open a terminal type/copy/paste
```
sudo apt-get update && sudo apt-get install kde-config-touchpad
```

Let that run through and it should show up under your accessories menu.
No need for a reboot or log out, you can customized a good deal of things, like scrolling and double finger/triple finger tapping. 
I hope this helps someone in the near future! 
Thanks for looking.
