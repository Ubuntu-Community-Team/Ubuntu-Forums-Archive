### Post by shawnhcorey on 2011-01-09
Open System->Administration->Synaptic Package Manager and search for appmenu-gtk.  If it's not there, you have to compile your own.

While you're at it, download the compiler and man pages.  Their package names are:

[LIST]
[*]build-essential
[*]manpages-dev
[/LIST]
