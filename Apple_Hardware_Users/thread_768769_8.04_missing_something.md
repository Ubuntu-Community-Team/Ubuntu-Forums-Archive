[EDIT]
OKAY i found it, i think.  I went to System->Preferences->Keyboard->Mouse Keys tab then clicked "control mouse with keyboard" and my xmodmap file started working.  
> 
keycode 116 = Pointer_Button2
keycode 108 = Pointer_Button3


I just enabled it to run automatically eachtime i log into X with System->Sessions and make the command

> xmodmap ~/.xmodmap

run.
