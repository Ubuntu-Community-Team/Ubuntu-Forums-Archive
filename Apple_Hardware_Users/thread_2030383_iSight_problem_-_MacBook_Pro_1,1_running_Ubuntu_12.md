### Post by HD Junkie on 2012-08-26
I have a MacBook Pro 2,2 and just did a fresh install of Ubuntu 12.04. Everything was working for me "out of the box"..... until I tried to use the webcam. I was able to correct the issue following the directions in [This Thread]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MactelSupportTeam/AppleiSight"). I found out that older webcams such as ours need the mac firmware for Ubuntu to recognize them. The hyperlinked thread gives step-by-step directions on extracting the firmware from the Mac OSX HD or install CD. I hope this helps!
