### Post by cyberdork33 on 2008-07-24
There are triple boot guides all over in the forums. It is generally the same for all Macs. The Macbook Pro wiki page has a lot of detail:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBookPro](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBookPro)

The Mac Minis are pretty compatible. I think that most everything will work out of the box. You may have some troubles with the WiFi, but that should be solved with an a newer version of madwifi.
