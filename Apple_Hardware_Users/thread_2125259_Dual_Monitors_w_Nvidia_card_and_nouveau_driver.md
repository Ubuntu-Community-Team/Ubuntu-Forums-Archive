### Post by br0c0l1 on 2013-05-27
Well, this went unresolved.... However, I did purchased a flashed nVidia 7800 GTX card (512 MB) and installed that into my G5 PowerMac.  I was able to easily set up dual monitor support using Arandr using the instructions on this page: [http://www.lubuntutips.com/2012/05/dual-monitors-in-lubuntu.html](http://www.lubuntutips.com/2012/05/dual-monitors-in-lubuntu.html)

After first installing the card, the two monitors appeared on top of each other in Arandr and as a result were in clone mode.  I am definitely enjoying the dual monitor set up. :)

Thought it wouldn't hurt to share with others who may have ran into the same issue.  Sorry I don't have an actual resolution to the problem above. Hope to see more people use Linux on PowerPC!
