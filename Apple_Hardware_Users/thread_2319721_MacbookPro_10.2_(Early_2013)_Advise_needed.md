### Post by Vinodh Moodley on 2016-04-07
Three finger drag is not supported by Unity. I think it can be added using Touchegg to Ubuntu Gnome.

I used Ubuntu on a 2011 MacBook Pro and a 2013 MacBook Air and battery life was nowhere near as good as OS X. TLP can help in theory but I have no idea how to configure it properly without breaking something. 

Depending on what VM software you use, USB devices can be plugged in to the VM without issue.
