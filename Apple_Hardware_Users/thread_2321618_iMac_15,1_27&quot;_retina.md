I've recently installed Ubuntu 14.04 on a new iMac (I think 16,1). I'm not certain of your question. I'm considering writing a guide to install on the iMac running El Capitan to include a few things that may have been missed in the posts that exist, or ways to deal with things that I struggled with but eventually fixed.
