### Post by B_Free on 2011-04-25
You've started up in Ubuntu. Put the DVD in and restart, at the prompt, type c and return. Or after turning on the power (with the DVD in the drive), hold the option key down. When presented with the choice of the hard drive or the CD, select the CD and press return.
