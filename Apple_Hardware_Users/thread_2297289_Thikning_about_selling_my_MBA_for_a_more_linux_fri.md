### Post by baudrillard on 2015-10-04
As much as I love linux, I am not sure it is worth the cost you will be paying in depreciation and hardware quality. Apple hardware is always reliable and the OS is still pretty good, not to mention fairly close to its Unix roots.

This is obviously a very subjective question, but I honestly would only ever make this decision if money was not close to being a 'problem,' in any sense of the word. And if that was the truth, then I would rather be buying a Lenovo X1 Carbon (or something similar), which is basically an alternative to a Macbook Pro/Top tier MBA.

Unless you have software needs that can be only met (or best perform) on a linux system. Then I completely understand the decision.
