### Post by mkvnmtr on 2009-03-14
You can look at the top of this mac forum to find a link to download a powerpc version of the live disk. You should probably have 512Mb of ram. My Ibook came with 256Mb. I installed 1Gb more and have s system that is plenty fast. You probably won't like having just 256Mb even on Xubuntu. You can burn the live disk in disk utility on mac osx.
When you get a disk burned come back to the forum if you have any trouble booting it. I think the latest you can get on Xubuntu is 8.04.
