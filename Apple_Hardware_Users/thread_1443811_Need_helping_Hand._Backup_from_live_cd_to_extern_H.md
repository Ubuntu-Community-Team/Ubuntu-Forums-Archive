### Post by piraira on 2010-03-31
Solution:

You have to turn off the Journaling of your external HD. Theres maybe a way to do this directly out of Ubutunu itself. I did it on a mac with the Disk-Utility (or by Terminal). Just press the alt key and the File-Menu to select the Journaling deactivate function.
