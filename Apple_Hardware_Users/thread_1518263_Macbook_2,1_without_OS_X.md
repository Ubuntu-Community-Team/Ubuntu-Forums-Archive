### Post by Helios747 on 2010-06-26
hold down the 'alt' key when you turn on your computer.


You should see a hard drive pop up that says "windows", when that pops up, let go of the 'alt' key, hold down the control key so the arrow pointing up turns into an arrow going in a circle, and click on it.

that "Windows" hard drive is actually ubuntu. doing the steps above will tell your macbook to always boot up from that partition.
