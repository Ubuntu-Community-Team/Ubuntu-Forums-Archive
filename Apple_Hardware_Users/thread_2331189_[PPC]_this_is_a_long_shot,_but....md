### Post by este.el.paz on 2016-07-20
@dr:

I have Lu 16.04 PPC running on my iBook G4.  You of course can do a "dual-boot" install, but in motorola you have to wipe the HD to partition the drive.  There are re-sellers or on eBay are OSX install disks; 10.5 is the last upgrade for PPC--of course you have to make sure that any disk you buy is labeled "PPC" and it also has to match your type of machine, so a "grey" install disk is for a specific machine, i.e., laptop, desktop . . . and one doesn't work on the other.

However, any "black" install disk for PPC will work on any machine.  I got a hold of the 10.5 installer, but, because of dual booting on what is in my case a 40 GB HD, the 10.4 installed system is much smaller . . . so I stayed with that on the iBook.
