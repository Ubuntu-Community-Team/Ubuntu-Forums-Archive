### Post by zircon_34 on 2014-03-01
I have a dual boot on macbook air 5,1 with Xubuntu 13.10 and just want to share a positive experience for battery life.

I have just installed laptop-mode-tools (1.63-2ubuntu1) and powertop in synaptic, the battery indicator (in both the panel and powertop) went up from <4 to >5 hours battery time! I am running now wifi, chrome, several terminal session, ubuntu software center, synaptic, and am reading pdfs at a bit more than 50% brightness. 

The <4 hours before install, actually was around 2h of real battery life. Whereas with the new tool installed, I have been working almost an hour now and the battery indicator is still showing above 5h in powertop! I dont know how accurate the reading is though, but will post again with more detail.seems the battery is draining much more slowly:cool:

I probably missed it but is there a fix for the accuracy of the battery reading (the panel battery does not always display the same than powertop~20-30min difference)?
