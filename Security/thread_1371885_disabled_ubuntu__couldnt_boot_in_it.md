### Post by cariboo on 2010-01-04
If your Ubuntu installation is on a separate partition have a look [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=224351"), this may work if you have a wubi install too.

The one way to protect your self from viruses and trojans, is to stop downloading things from or going to dodgy web sites. I have XP on one of my office computers, I don't have an anti-virus or antispyware programs running, I even have the default firewall turned off. Its been running that way for about 6 months, and I haven't had any problems. I do use Firefox or Chrome instead of IE to surf the internet with noscript enabled in Firefox. This will stop 99% of the problems encountered. I also have my router setup with no ports forwarded.
