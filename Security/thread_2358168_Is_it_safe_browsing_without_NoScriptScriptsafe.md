### Post by vasa1 on 2017-04-11
[According to the developer]("https://github.com/gorhill/uMatrix/wiki/Changes-from-HTTP-Switchboard"), uMatrix is the successor to HTTP-Switchboard: > uMatrix and uBlock are both spin-off of HTTP Switchboard ("HTTPSB"). They both improve significantly on HTTPSB. Essentially, HTTPSB is the fancy prototype, proof of concept to test many ideas. uMatrix and uBlock are the final products.

uMatrix inherited the task of matrix-based filtering, while uBlock inherited the task of pattern-based filtering.
