### Post by Tim Sharitt on 2009-02-06
As I understand it, the Ubuntu developers try to get critical security updates released as soon as possible. However, the process of testing the updates and packaging them can take days, or a week or two depending on the scope of the update. 

Ubuntu only provides incremental security updates, and does not upgrade the browser to a new version (i.e. the Ubuntu version may go from 3.0.5 to 3.0.6, but will not upgrade to 3.1).
