### Post by mdebusk on 2009-02-04
> **richardboots said:**
> I am looking for a software that will clean my PC. evidences and improve its performance?

While full-disk encryption is the best choice, it doesn't help you now. It takes time to back up, wipe, reinstall, and restore.

My suggestions are these:

[LIST]
[*]Make sure your computer has an account for each user, so as to never share a profile;
[*]Create and mount a ramdisk, and point your browser cache to it. When you shut down, all tracks disappear;
[*]Install Truecrypt and learn to use it for sensitive files;
[*]Work toward full-disk encryption, or at least an encrypted /home directory.
[/LIST]
