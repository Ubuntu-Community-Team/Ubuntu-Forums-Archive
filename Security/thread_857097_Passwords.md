### Post by lisati on 2008-07-12
Before you consider doing away with passwords, have a good think about the security of your computer: it's only advisable if you are the only person who is ever going to use your machine, and you are reasonably confident in your ability not to make mistakes (or correct problems if they arise). As frustrating as it can be at times, passwords are there for your protection.

You can set your computer to automatically login. Some may advise to have the auto-login go to a non-administrative account, in the interests of helping protect you against muck-ups.

As for doing away with passwords completely, you might encounter a reluctance to share exactly how it can be achieved through this forum.
