### Post by Sarmacid on 2009-10-19
What happens with this kind of malware is that it asks you to download a codec in WMP, but since you're not using Windows you should be safe. The codecs used in ubuntu are usually available from trustworthy sources and would need your administrative password to work.

Even though the attack could be made to work on linux, it is very unlikely someone will try to do it since it's much easier just to throw out a few of those movies and let them spread in windows machines.
