### Post by Sef on 2008-10-18
1) Locked. Duplicate Post.

2) > I have a friend who swears that the cause is that I either got hacked or got a virus. Unlikely to both.  There really aren't any viruses for GNU/Linux operating systems, but rootkits can be installed if you are not careful.  Hackers, today, prefer to make your computer part of a botnet or use it for downloading that is illegal.  That last is true especially for wireless systems.  Crooks can download stuff like child pornography over open wireless systems, which can make it untraceable.  Read about this [police raid]("http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2007/02/10/AR2007021001457.html").
