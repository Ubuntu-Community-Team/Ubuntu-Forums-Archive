### Post by HermanAB on 2011-11-09
Howdy,

Dansguardian and Squidguard and both based on Squid-Cache.  These are simply rule sets for the Squid proxy server.

You may find that using a DNS filter such as OpenDNS is less of a hassle than maintaining your own rule sets.
