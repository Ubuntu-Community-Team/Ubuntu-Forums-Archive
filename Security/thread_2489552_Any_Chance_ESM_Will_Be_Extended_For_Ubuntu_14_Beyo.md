### Post by CharlesA on 2023-08-02
14.04 goes end of life in 2024 per the release schedule:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases)

ESR goes up to 10 years, but it appears 5 of those are because of LTS support. 14.04 was released in April 2014. End of regular support occurred in April 2019. I doubt it will be expended past the end of life date that is already set.

[https://ubuntu.com/security/esm](https://ubuntu.com/security/esm)

If you really want to know, you can always contact them: [https://ubuntu.com/support/contact-us?product=contextual-footer-landscape](https://ubuntu.com/support/contact-us?product=contextual-footer-landscape)
