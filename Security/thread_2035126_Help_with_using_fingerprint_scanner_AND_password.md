### Post by Ms. Daisy on 2012-07-31
Go low-tech first, although no one seems to like hearing that.  
 
Control physical access to your computer first (using locks, physical barriers, a big mean dog, etc). If someone has physical access to the laptop they could circumvent both the fingerprint reader & the password.
