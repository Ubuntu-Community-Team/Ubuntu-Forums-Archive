### Post by insane_alien on 2010-05-01
pop in a live cd, try to get to your home folder by simply navigating to it.

if you can, something has gone horribly wrong

if you can't then its all good under the hood.

chances are close enough to 100%(i know of no cases where it has failed to implement this) that everything is working fine that it isn't really worth bothering about
