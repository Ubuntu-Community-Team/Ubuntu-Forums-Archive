### Post by CodeHeroes::CodeHero on 2011-11-23
1. Why are the files suspicious? Where are they from?
2. The message  "The application launcher "avahi-ui-utils_bvnc.desktop" has not been  marked as trusted. If you do not know the source of this file, launching  it may be unsafe." is normal in ubuntu and will show up, if you try to start an executable or a launcher that hasn't been marked as executable. You can make it executable through right-click on the file->properties->permission tab->tick the make executable checkbox.

But don't excute them if you don't know what these files are or where do you get them from.
