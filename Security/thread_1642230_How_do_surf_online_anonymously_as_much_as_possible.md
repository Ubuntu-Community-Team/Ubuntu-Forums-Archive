### Post by OpSecShellshock on 2010-12-10
Making use of a VPN and/or proxy is fine, but you should also remember to disable advertisements, web scripts, cookies, remove Flash, and don't log in to anything (as it is impossible/contradictory to try to be anonymous and authenticate to a service at the same time).

### Post by TheFr00n on 2010-12-10
There is no way to be completely anonymous. But having said that, try this.

1. Purchase a server in a country that does not give a ****. Somewhere in Russia or the Ukraine, or maybe a Caribbean island with no extradition treaty. Pay in advance using a disposable gift card ie not registered in your name.

2. Connect your new server to TOR.

3. Set up a VPN to that server.

4. Connect to the VPN via your neighbour's WIFI, without their knowledge.

You should be safe, except against someone who really really really wants to find you.

But really, just install NoScript and BetterPrivacy in Firefox, and surf your porn in Private Browsing mode, like everyone else. It's much cheaper.

You see, there is an alternative side of the discussion: law enforcement might only notice your traffic *because it is encrypted*.
