### Post by rookcifer on 2011-01-03
It depends on what the encryption is being used for.  Is it for e-mail?  Files?  Whole Disk?

For e-mail, GPG is the standard.  It comes with Ubuntu.

For individual files, you can also use GPG.

For partitions or entire disks, you will need to use dm-crypt/LUKS.
