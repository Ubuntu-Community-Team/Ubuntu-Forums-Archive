I have natty 11.04 ,firefox browser and a AT&T 2 Wire modem/router. So..when I check connection info while using my wireless connection under security it says wpa/wpa2. But when I check with wired connection under security it says none.Is there something I need to do to secure wired connection or am I alright? Thanks.

---

### Post by hyper2hottie on 2011-10-04
You don't need a security protocol for wired internet because you are plugged directly into a router.  With wireless you have a security protocol because you communicate with the router through radio waves. WPA/WPA2 are just encryption that prevents people from seeing what you are doing on the internet.  They prevent people from reading what you send to the router and what the router sends to you.
