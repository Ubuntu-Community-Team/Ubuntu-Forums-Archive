### Post by The Cog on 2010-12-24
Or do you mean that it automatically logs in as you when it boots up? If so, that's not in users and groups at all, it is configured as auto-log-in and that option needs disabling. Here's a link:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AutoLogin](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AutoLogin)
