If the email address you use for Facebook is visible on your public profile then it could be obtained that way. Same goes for any of the other services. Any of them could have had their site's user information scraped for email addresses as well.

If you don't engage with the instant messages and really really don't click on any links that might be contained in them, you should be reasonably safe.
