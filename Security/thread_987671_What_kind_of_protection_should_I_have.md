### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2008-11-19
Although you are better off with Unbuntu, nothing can protect you from social engineering except yourself.

The "problem" is not necessarily Limewire, it is what and where you are downloading. Pirated software / music / games is a perfect venue for social engineering, so I am more concerned that you change your habits and not just your OS.
