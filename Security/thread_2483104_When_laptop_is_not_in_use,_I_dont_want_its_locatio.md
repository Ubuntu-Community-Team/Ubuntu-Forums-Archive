### Post by naufrsb on 2023-03-20
When your laptop is connected to the internet, anyone can see your IP address and most likely determine your general location. Also if your laptop has GPS capabilities, it may also be possible for someone to determine its location (via GPS) if they have access to your device. Additionally, if your laptop is connected to a Wi-Fi network, it may be possible for someone to triangulate its location based on the strength of nearby Wi-Fi signals. Even in ariplane mode your laptop can be transmitting data (without you knowing)... 


So... the best way to ensure your laptop's location cannot be tracked is to keep it powered off and in a secure location when not in use. :)
