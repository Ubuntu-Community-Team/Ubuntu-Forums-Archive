### Post by Chayak on 2014-05-19
No, you can't.  I did malware research for years and have about 4TB worth of malware samples sitting on my ZFS array.  Malware is simply a piece of software.  It has to be executed by the system to work.  It's perfectly safe copying files even on a windows system.  Your previous windows system got infected because of autorun executing the malware.
