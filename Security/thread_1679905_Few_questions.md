### Post by rookcifer on 2011-02-02
OP, there's no keyloggers or viruses to worry about.  The only thing you need to do to stop trojans is not to install anything outside of the repositories.

As for encryption, what are you looking to do specifically?  Ubuntu comes with GPG already, which means you already have what you need for e-mail encryption and the encryption of individual files.  If you need to encrypt the entire disk, you will need to reinstall and do so with the alternate install CD.
