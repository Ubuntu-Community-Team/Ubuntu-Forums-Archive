### Post by afrowildo on 2010-08-25
I recently had to reinstall Ubuntu after a minor pebkac issue and in doing so I lost my pgp key, as I had forgotten to back it up. Is there any way to recover it from Ubuntu keyserver or am I going to have to create a new one?

---

### Post by FuturePilot on 2010-08-25
You can't recover your private key since that isn't stored on the keyserver. If you don't have any backups of it somewhere you're pretty much out of luck. You'll have to create a new key.
