### Post by uRock on 2010-09-03
As of now, there aren't any known Linux viruses in the wild, but there are loads of malwares** waiting to be installed. Neither one are detected by the AV clients. 

It is good to have AV for scanning files shared with Windows. I haven't tried the one you are speaking of. I have used ClamTK and BitDefender and both were good.
