### Post by ignatius2 on 2015-12-12
Hey there, tor browser is always slow because the packets sent and received from the website you are trying to reach are being "hopped" through many more different routers than just a usual unmasked Internet browsing session.
Think of it like it's an onion layer. Each layer of the onion's skin is a router. To "protect" the core of the onion, there are many onion skin layers covering it. It helps protect your connection, that's why it's slow.
