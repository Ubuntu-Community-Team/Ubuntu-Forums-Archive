### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2011-05-18
It is difficult to accomplish those goals as they have little or nothing to do with your OS, and everything to do with the internet.

The internet is not really designed with privacy in mind, and you have no control over the servers you connect to or the intermediaries, such as your internet provider.

For additional information see:

[http://bodhizazen.net/Tutorials/Privacy](http://bodhizazen.net/Tutorials/Privacy)

### Post by aphatak on 2011-05-18
You could always use a proxy server and block access to sites you do not want accessed.

I have to agree with *bodhi.zazen*, though - this is not a proper OS feature, and ought to be implemented at your Internet gateway.  That way, it will apply to whatever OS the devices inside your local network are running.
