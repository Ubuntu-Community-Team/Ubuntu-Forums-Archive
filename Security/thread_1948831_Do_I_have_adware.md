### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-03-29
You are very unlikely to have adware. I have not come across anything like this on Linux.

Most likely Adblock simply did not block it. (Adblock tries not to block non-intrusive ads, in order to help support websites that make their money through advertising.)

Unless you are pestered with this on different websites, ignore it.
