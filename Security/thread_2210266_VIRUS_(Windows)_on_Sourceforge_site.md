### Post by PondPuppy on 2014-03-11
Yeah. That was the virus link. Frankly, these links to malware are a scourge. The owner of InfraRecorder sold ad space to Google AdWords, and Google re-sold the space to an advertiser. Who used the ad to link to a virus download. Google is not very scrupulous about checking that its clients aren't linking to malware, and obviously InfraRecorder's owners aren't checking either. I've been caught too -- by a link masquerading as a download for GIMP. 

One might say "only download from reputable sites" but in fact those damned bogus links appear on nearly all sites. Not your fault. You been victimized.

Oh, and it looks like the ads on InfraRecorder's page have changed again. This is another nasty tactic -- if you complain to InfraRecorder or Google, I'll bet they "know nothing about it" and "those ads are automatically changed anyway".
