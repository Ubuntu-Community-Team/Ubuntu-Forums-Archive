### Post by him610 on 2015-02-23
Camilia,

I reread you message. I found Weatherblink, an extension, in the Chrome Web Store which I assume is what you have installed. 

To remove it from Chrome, left-click your mouse on the three parallel, horizontal bars at the upper right of your Chrome screen, a picklist menu will appear, near the bottom is an entry, "Settings". Highlight it with your mouse and left-click it, a new page will open. At the upper left of the new page, left-click on "Extensions." Another page will open displaying all of the Chrome Extensions you have installed. Scroll to the bottom of the listing until you locate "Weatherblink", you can disable it by unclicking the Enable box, or you can click on the trash can to remove it completely.

I hope this helps.
