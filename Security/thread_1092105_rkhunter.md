### Post by Tubes6al4v on 2009-03-10
No, Rkhunter is a tool for scanning your computer for already installed root kits. Though, root kits are not all that common. If you suspect that you have one (i.e. your computer is displaying symptoms of such), then it may be a tool to confirm that suspicion.
