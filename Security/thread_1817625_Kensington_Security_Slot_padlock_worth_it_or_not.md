### Post by Dave_L on 2011-08-03
It will prevent someone from quickly picking up the netbook and walking away with it.  It won't stop a determined thief.

It depends on the location, and how long you leave the netbook unattended.  For example, a library, vs. a cafe on the sidewalk of a busy street.
