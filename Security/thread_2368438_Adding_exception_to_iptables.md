### Post by JKyleOKC on 2017-08-11
The first question to ask is whether the request is legitimate, or simply a phishing expedition to gain access to your system. Do you know, or have an account with, Private Internet Access? If you do, can you contact them by phone and verify that they do need for you to poke a hole in your firewall? Such requests, if unsolicited, are one of the more popular ways for the Bad Guys to infiltrate your system and take it over!
