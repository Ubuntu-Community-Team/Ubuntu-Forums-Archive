Security updates are taking forever on my machine.  My updates are coming from us.archive.ubuntu.com.  Is there a faster place to get my updates?  If so how do I change my security update settings?  5500 B/s is killing me.  Nothing else I download goes that slow.

### Post by angryfirelord on 2008-06-23
Yes, that's why there's more than 1 mirror. :) In Synaptic, go to Software Sources, click on the Download From drop down box, choose select other mirror and then click the button "Choose best mirror". Synaptic will then ping all of the mirrors in your region and choose the one with the lowest ping response. Or, you can choose your own mirror if you know there's one nearby.
