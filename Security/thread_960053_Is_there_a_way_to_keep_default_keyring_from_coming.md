### Post by cdavidrun on 2008-10-27
One solution to get rid of key ring prompting for passwords for me was to delete the default key ring and then when keyring came up to ask for a default password hit the enter key to leave the password blank. Keyring then left me alone.

This may be different to your problem in logging on to a wireless network. In trying to solve my problem I came upon this thread which may have your solution
