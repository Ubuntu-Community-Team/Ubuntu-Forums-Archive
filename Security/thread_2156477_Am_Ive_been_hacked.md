### Post by lisati on 2013-06-22
A lot of things can affect your download speed, not just the top speed that your ISP claims to provide. Common reasons for speeds less than what you'd like include busy servers and a busy network between you and the website. The website from which you're downloading something might also have badnwidth limits imposed by their provider.
