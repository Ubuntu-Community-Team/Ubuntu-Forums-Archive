### Post by Kinstonian on 2008-11-30
Key management always poses challenges regardless of the type of encryption used.  You should definitely do what *kevdog* suggested and use a strong passphrase.  If you want more, you could use [steganography](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steganography), and/or keep it on an encrypted TrueCrypt container.
