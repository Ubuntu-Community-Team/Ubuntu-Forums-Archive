### Post by rookcifer on 2011-03-07
1) Download the "standard" .tar.gz file for your CPU architecture (32 or 64 bit).

2) Extract the archive.

3) You should see a file named "truecrypt-7.0a-setup.x64"

4) Open a console and type:

```
./truecrypt-7.0a-setup.*
```

5) A window will pop-up.  Click the "Install Truecrypt" option.  You will have to enter your root password.

6) After it finishes, you will see Truecrypt in "Accessories --> Truecrypt" in your menu.
