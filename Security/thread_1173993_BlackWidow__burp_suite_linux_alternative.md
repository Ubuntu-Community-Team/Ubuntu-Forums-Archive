### Post by The Tronyx on 2009-05-30
W3AF, Paros Proxy, RAT Proxy, ProxyStrike and SpikeProxy.

Burp Proxy is free, at least for the most part.  You need to pay for it for the more "enhanced" features (iirc) but the version you download free of charge is just fine.  I think the biggest limitation with Burp Proxy is the repeater/fuzzer/intruder/whatever it's called limitations but you can make up for that with the free version and a stateless protocol fuzzer.
