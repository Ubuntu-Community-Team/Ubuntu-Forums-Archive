### Post by handy on 2010-04-06
I have an old PIII 7??Mhz 256MB RAM 8GB HDD box running headless.

It has two NICs in it, connects to a modem router that now runs in bridge mode (modem only).

Over a year ago I installed IPCop & then the Copfilter add-on. 

IPCop gives me a firewall, router, proxy & more, & from Copfilter I run some other services including HAVP, Privoxy & ClamAV.

The installation is quick & easy (if you know what you are doing 20 minutes will do it) the browser based interface is excellent, (Copfilter very effectively makes itself a part of this) & I'm very happy with the entire setup.

Here are some links if you are interested in investigating this as a possible solution:

[http://sourceforge.net/apps/trac/ipcop/wiki](http://sourceforge.net/apps/trac/ipcop/wiki)

[http://www.copfilter.org/](http://www.copfilter.org/)

This page will give you much better idea of what I speak:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=6303994&postcount=29](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=6303994&postcount=29)
