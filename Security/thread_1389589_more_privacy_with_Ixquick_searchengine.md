### Post by rookcifer on 2010-07-11
I have used StartPage on and off and while I love it's privacy features, I think it's too slow and I don't like how it limits your search results to a fixed number.

I prefer to use Scroogle's SSL search when possible.  Scroogle has been up and down the last week or so, but it's back up right now (and has been for about 2 days), so maybe everything is worked out with them and Google.

When Scroogle isn't working, I prefer [Duckduckgo]("https://duckduckgo.com/").  It also respects privacy by not recording IP's, is much faster than Startpage, is SSL enabled, and looks a lot like Google.  It also does not limit search results.  It's the 2nd best, imo.

Of course, there is also Yauba, which is nice but it doesn't have SSL which kills the deal for me. Gotta have SSL.
