### Post by tubbygweilo on 2008-06-29
To follow on from hyper_ch, too much heat and faulty memory are often the cause of a machine shutting down unexpectedly. Judging for your location it can be rather warm in that part of the world, any chance of removing the dust from inside of your kit? If the problem still persists then try running the Memtest option from the grub menu as this will show if the memory is still OK.
