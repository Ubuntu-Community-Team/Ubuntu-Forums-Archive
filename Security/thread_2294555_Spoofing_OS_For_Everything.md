Normally, the speeds are ridiculous (relative to residential speeds), anywhere from 60 to 100 mbps on wireless and almost always a solid 100 on ethernet. So of course I threw Ubuntu onto my laptop, and since this software isn't available for Linux, I have internet without spyware. Great. Problem.
