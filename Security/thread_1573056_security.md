### Post by Bachstelze on 2010-09-12
The same common sense measures that apply to other OSes apply here as well: do not open click on links sent by unknown people, do not execute unknown programs, do not give out sensitive information (e.g. credit card number) without double-checking that the site is legitimate, etc.

If you keep that in mind, as a desktop user you probably have little to worry about. If you have special concerns, describe them in more detail so we can suggest a solution.
