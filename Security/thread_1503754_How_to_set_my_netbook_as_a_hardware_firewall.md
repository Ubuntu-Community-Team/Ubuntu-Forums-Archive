### Post by FemmeFatale on 2010-06-07
Honestly, you are not going to find anything that "suits your needs". If you insist on setting up your personal hardware firewall based on Ubuntu, I would recommend you to get an old computer, 2 eth cards and build your own firewall rules based on the iptables software package. It's flexible enough.

There are other easily configurable firewall packages based on open source as well.
