### Post by Double.J on 2012-07-11
Hi all,

Just wanted to put in a little reminder to people to always check what you're clicking on. It seems that this time the back door Trojan writers have been kind enough to include us Linux users. So far the malware has only been found in columbia where Linux usage is higher, and it seems to remain dormant once installed, but obviously once a door is opened...


I'm sure the security guys around here can explain further- it's in the form of a java applet that checks your os then installs the right kit for the job. Just like the attack that caught out mac users last year, you have to click on a required plugin... So just be careful when clicking those links ;)
