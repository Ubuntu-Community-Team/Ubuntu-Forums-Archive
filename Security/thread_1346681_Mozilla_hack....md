### Post by OpSecShellshock on 2009-12-05
This won't require any additional work on your part, as Firefox has this capability built in. The first time you log in to a site, there will be a bar that appears at the top asking if you want to save the password for the site. Just click the yes button.

### Post by BkkBonanza on 2010-01-06
If Firefox isn't asking to save passwords you can re-enable it in 

Edit, Preferences, Security, Remember Passwords for sites.

I would suggest adding exceptions for important things like banking, and paypal. Keep in mind that once it does remember passowrds then anyone happening to sit down at your computer will have access to the site.
