### Post by papibe on 2014-12-11
Hi kccv42.

Try this:
[LIST]
[*]open 'Security & Privacy'.
[*]go to the 'Security' tab.
[*]on the row that says 'Require my password when:', press 'Password Settings'.
[*]unlock the options on the new window by pressing 'Unlock' on the upper right.
[*]you'll be prompt for you password. Enter it.
[*]select your user from the list on the left, and turn off 'Automatic Login' on the right.
[/LIST]
Hope it helps. Let us know how it goes.
Regards.
