### Post by cariboo on 2009-06-29
A email archive is where all you old emails are stored. There are many ways to archive email. Personally, I use Thunderbird, and I just use rsync to backup ~/.mozilla-thunderbird every day. In that particular directory, I have email going back to 2002, I also have other email archives, from email programs that I didn't bother converting to a format that Thunderbird could read.
