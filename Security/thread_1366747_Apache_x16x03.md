### Post by __p1n__ on 2009-12-28
The error indicates an SSL establishment failure.  Most likely causes include but are not limited to an incorrect SSL configuration on your server and/or recon probes from a scanner (e.g. openvas, w3af ,etc.)  If your site is not configured to support HTTPS then the latter is more likely.
