### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-01-11
Bear in mind that rechargeable batteries are best used as they are intended to be, ie not permanently attached to mains power source.

If that is the most practical way to use the laptop you should make sure that you unplug it about once a week, if possible, and run the battery down to a low charge level.

That way, unless things have changed, the life of your battery will be longer and it will hold charge much better.
