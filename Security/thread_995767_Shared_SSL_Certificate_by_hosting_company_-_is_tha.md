### Post by cdenley on 2008-11-28
> **gibbs.hilton said:**
> Why should I get an SSL Certificate when my hosting provider has a shared one? Where can I obtain a private SSL certificate for my website?

Do you own your own domain name, or do you use a subdomain from your provider? If you want to use [https://www.mydomain.com/](https://www.mydomain.com/), then you need a private SSL certificate. If you want to use [https://myuser.myprovider.com/](https://myuser.myprovider.com/) or [https://myprovider.com/myuser](https://myprovider.com/myuser), then the shared SSL certificate would work fine.

I usually buy SSL certificates from godaddy. It's cheap.
