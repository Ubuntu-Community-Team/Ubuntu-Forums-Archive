### Post by OpSecShellshock on 2010-04-27
I don't think it's a redirection as such. Some users on the forums have added their Skype IDs to their profiles, and have a little icon to display whether they're currently logged in there or not. The same exists for people's Yahoo and AIM accounts. I think what you're seeing are requests for the little icons and user IDs for those forum members who have made themselves available for chat and have chosen to show it on the left side of their comments.
