### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2009-08-29
There is a lot of hype surrounding the root account.

Enabling the root account is supported when necessary (and yes, there are times when it is necessary, although rare).

The problems with enabling the root account are outlined on that blog.

The wiki team feels it is important to document information, even if it's use is discouraged.

The bottom line, educate people on how to use their systems and most of the debate evaporates. Tell them you can not do something is not a good strategy.
