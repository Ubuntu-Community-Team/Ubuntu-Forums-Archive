### Post by handy on 2011-05-29
Why do you want to "allow for session" anyway? I have Firefox ask my permission for every cookie, say NO automatically, & have it remember what I said & apply what I said to all cookies from that site.

I use IMDB all the time, but don't accept any cookies from them & their site works fine.

I also run Firefox add-ons: Addblock +, Beef Taco, BetterPrivacy & NoScript. All of which help improve my browsing experience & make me harder to track.

On top of them I use the Greasemonkey add-on running the googlePrivacy script which does wonders re. Google, as I use "Scroogle SSL" for my searching.

There are other edits that you can make to your Firefox about:config that make you even harder to track, if you are interested have a look here:

[http://spiralinear.org/forum/viewtopic.php?f=17&t=243](http://spiralinear.org/forum/viewtopic.php?f=17&t=243)
