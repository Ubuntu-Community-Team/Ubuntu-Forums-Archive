### Post by cariboo on 2009-06-21
If your neighbour has access to your router, and they have changed the WPA passphrase, they don't need your MAC address. I would do as Tews suggested and reset the router. After you have done so change the default access password to a strong password, change the ssid and keep it hidden, use a long passphrase for WPA2.
