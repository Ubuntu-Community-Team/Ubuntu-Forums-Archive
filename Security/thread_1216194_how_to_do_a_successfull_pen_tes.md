### Post by The Tronyx on 2009-07-19
I hope this doesn't come across as rude, but I think if you are asking that question, it is very likely that you are not prepared to perform a successful pen test.

There a lot of factors that come into play such as consent from the managers of the network/machines in question and consent from the highest authority at the institution to be audited.  If the test is going to be done remotely, to do everything 100% by the book, you will also need consent from your ISP and possibly any transient carriers depending on the scope of the test.
