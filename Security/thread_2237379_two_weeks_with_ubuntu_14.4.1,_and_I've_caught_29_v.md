What makes you think you "caught" viruses?  What you can say is that your AV software identified 29 potential threats.

Packers *can *be used by those who create malware, but their presence does not necessarily indicate that they are from malware.  Packers can also be used with legitimate products.

If those packers *are *associated with malware, the likelihood that they could deliver a working Windows payload on Linux is small.  But you could be a carrier and infect the machines of you Windows-using friends via email attachments and the like.

Whatever they may be, this is a good demonstration of why most people here will recommend agains just installing things randomly from the internet.
