### Post by dargaud on 2011-05-10
What you want is probably a VPN. You can't change your IP as it's determined by your network provider. But a VPN allows you to connect to a remote provider as if you were physically there. There are plenty of free and commercial VPNs also I don't know how well they work on Linux.
