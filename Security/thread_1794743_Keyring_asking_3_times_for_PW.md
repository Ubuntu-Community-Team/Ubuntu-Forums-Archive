### Post by Cathhsmom on 2011-07-01
Here is a way to rid of all of the password request upon booting if that is what you want:  Go to your home folder and hit cntrl h; this will show the hidden folders.  Then click gnome2 folder, and them click keyrings folder.  Delete all keyrings.  No more passwords upon booting up!
