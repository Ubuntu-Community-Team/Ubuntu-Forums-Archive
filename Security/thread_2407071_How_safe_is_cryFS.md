### Post by TheFu on 2018-11-29
Early adopters do a great service for everyone else who don't want to experience data loss.  I didn't switch to EXT4 until it was mainstream over 5 years.  I still won't use brtfs, but that is more about having better alternatives available, IMHO. 

 And definitely have automatic, daily, backups.  If you don't, stick with well-tested file system tools.  But really, have automatic, daily, backups. Always good advice for anyone using computers for important work.
