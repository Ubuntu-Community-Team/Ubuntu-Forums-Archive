Take a deep breath, sit back, and think for a minute. 

You went to network places, and saw another icon. Since you are in network places, this would be another device that is on your network. 

What other devices are usually on a home network and may be on your home network? Router? Another system? Some media device (UPNP server maybe)? 

Do you have wireless? It is secured?


There isn't a need to freak out and jump to the "I was hacked!" conclusion
