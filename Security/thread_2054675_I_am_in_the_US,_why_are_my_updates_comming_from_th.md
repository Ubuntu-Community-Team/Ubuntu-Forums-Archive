### Post by lisati on 2012-09-07
Sometimes the geographically closest server provides a slower connection than a more distant one, particularly when the nearest one is busy.

Most of the releases of Ubuntu I've used have had an option to choose a new server from within update manager, and occasionally I've been surprised by its choice of which one to use.
