### Post by tahu363 on 2010-08-25
I currently run 10.04 on my Dell Precision 6500. That being said, others may not be able to reproduce, and/or experience this issue due to the high grade of my hardware. I thought I'd bring it up anyhow.

I often run VMware workstation with a Windows 7/ Windows XP guest, and as one can probably imagine, things can become sluggish on both the host and guest, if very many programs are being run/ multiple HDD intensive operations are present. I have noticed, over the course of perhaps a month, that I may leave my desk for a period exceeding 10 minutes, and return, without having my screen-saver active. (this is peculiar because it is set to activate after a period of 10 minutes of inactivity). Once back, I can manipulate windows/type/do other Linux stuff for about 2 seconds. after that, the screen-saver suddenly flashes, then I am prompted for my password, as if the screen-saver HAD been active and i had moved the mouse or typed on the keyboard. Personally, I have no immediate issues with this possible flaw, as my laptop rarely leaves my desk, but I feel compelled to bring it to SOMEONES attention, as I shudder to think of a situation where a machine running a high load (which seems to be the trigger in my case) may be compromised if certain people know what to type/click in that 2-second window.
