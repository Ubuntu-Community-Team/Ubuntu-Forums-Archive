### Post by deadflowr on 2013-02-22
You'll need to look up at the global menu.
It's up at the left side of the top panel of your screen.
It is usually hidden until the mouse pointer moves over it.

Or if on 12.04 or later, use the HUD function (alt) and type scan a.Then in the list the shows up 'a device' will appear hit enter and the related screen will show up.
