### Post by noway2 on 2010-07-22
Do they always come from the same IP?  If so, ban them with IP tables and forget about it.

If you really want, you can get the log information, do a whois lookup on their address to get the contact information for their ISP and file a complaint.  Something may or may not be done in response, but you will most likely never know.

If you plan on running a server, you will need to get used to this sort of crap.  It is just par for the coarse.  After a while, it won't bother you as much and won't feel as much like the violation that it is.  

Keep learning about how to secure your server and keep aware of what is happening with it.  Remember that security is an active task, not an application you can install and forget about.
