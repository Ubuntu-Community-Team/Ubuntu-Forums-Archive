### Post by BkkBonanza on 2009-11-30
Those popup messages are generally a scam to make you login or buy some product (especially if you're on Linux and see a WinXP popup, ha ha, priceless). I doubt you have any problem unless you foolishly did what they said and logged into something and entered a password. If you did that then immediately go and change your password on whatever site you accessed. 

These sites are mostly phishing sites looking either to sell you garbage or grab sensitive info. Somehow they actually manage to make millions of dollars/year (according to the BBC anyway).
