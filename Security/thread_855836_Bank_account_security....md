### Post by tamoneya on 2008-07-10
I access my bank account through ubuntu.  There is no difference when it comes to accessing bank accounts between Windows and ubuntu.  If anything ubuntu is safer.  Always remember to take good security measures.

-watch out for phishing (make sure its actually your banks site)
-dont leave your passwords lying around
-remember to log out
