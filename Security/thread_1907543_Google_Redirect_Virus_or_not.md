### Post by Ryan Dwyer on 2012-01-12
It sounds to me like your hotel's DNS server got compromised. In that case there's nothing wrong with your computer.

If it happens again, try setting your DNS servers to 8.8.8.8 and/or 8.8.4.4. Those are Google DNS servers.
