### Post by arvevans on 2010-12-15
Easier method might be to use a foreign non-censored email host that supports SSH security protocol.  Then on your PC use an email handler that also supports SSH security.  This way your in-transit email content would be encrypted and not easily decoded by casual monitoring stations.
