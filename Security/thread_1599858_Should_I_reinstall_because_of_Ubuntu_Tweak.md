### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-10-18
You are safe.
Ubuntu Tweak is a piece of software that lets you customize Ubuntu and add third party repositories by means of a user friendly GUI. It's reasonably safe, unless used on a mission critical production machine, where stability is of utmost importance.
If all you have is a home computer with regular backups and an Ubuntu live cd ready for the rescue, I think Ubuntu Tweak is an acceptable level of danger.
Those are sensible warnings, but their grimness and gravity should be taken down a step or two.
