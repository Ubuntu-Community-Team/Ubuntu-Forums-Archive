### Post by strawberry2008 on 2008-05-10
Qoodaa Communications ([http://www.qoodaa.com](http://www.qoodaa.com)), which has been devoted to developing internet transfers service, innovates and develops a new P2P technology, called MNP2P for short. It creates a new epoch of internet transfers acceleration for large files.
It is well known that the main downloading accelerative ways are P2P and CDN. However, the traditional P2P needs multi-users download at the same time to guarantee its downloading speed. 
BT is the most classic example of P2P. But CDN can ensure downloading quality under the circumstance that there are a few downloading users and there is enough bandwidth, but it can’t guarantee the effective service at peak hours because of the limits of the bandwidth. Qoodaa’s downloading resource of technology MNP2P doesn’t have the idea of sleeper and topical like web or BT. Even if there is one user downloading, it can also enjoy the same speed as multi-person use under the condition of P2P downloading.
The main feature of Qoodaa MNP2P isn’t this; the greatest advantages of Qoodaa are in the following two aspects: one is transnational transfers, because of adopting the unique MNP2P technology; Qoodaa’s transnational transfer speed is faster several times than the other software. The other greatest feature is in uploading speed, MNP2P unique technology ensures the users upload the utmost speed that is close to the speed of uploading internet, which P2P software doesn’t have.
