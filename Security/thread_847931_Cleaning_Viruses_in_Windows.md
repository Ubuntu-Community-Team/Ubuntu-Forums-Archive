### Post by starcannon on 2008-07-03
Possible and practical, running clam av, avg, avast, and various other commercial av linux editions from a persistent liveUSB allows you to keep updated av, and remove virus's without booting the windows partition that your working on(particularly advantageous).

Anyway, short answer is yes, and there are many tools you can put together for this purpose. Or you can download any of several projects that work towards this purpose.
