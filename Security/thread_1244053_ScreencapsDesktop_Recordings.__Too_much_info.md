### Post by Agent ME on 2009-08-19
All IPs in the form of 192.168.* and 10.10.* (think I'm a bit off on the second one) are local to your local area network. They're meaningless to anyone without access to your network. (They're like room numbers in a hotel; meaningless to anyone who doesn't know what hotel you're talking about or allowed access in.) Same thing with hostnames in most cases.
