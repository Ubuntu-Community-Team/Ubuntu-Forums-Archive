### Post by The Tronyx on 2008-08-14
The home feed (from what I gather) has the same plugins, but they are on a delay.  This means that if a new vulnerability is discovered and added to nessus, those who pay get it first, while those who don't will have to wait a bit.

Anyways, registering the home feed is what most, if not all home users do.  After you register your feed/scanner you can simply update the nessus plugins.
