### Post by scorp123 on 2009-10-10
I've been using Medibuntu since they came into existence. They do a far better job at packaging their stuff than the infamous "Automatix" project (if you don't know it: this was another source for the same packages ... thank God they stopped!). So far I have not experienced breakages due to Medibuntu.

Automatix on the other hand (see my comment above) was infamous and frowned upon because their stuff would regularly break and cripple systems.

So, in absence of any messages that would indicate the contrary, I'd say yes: Medibuntu should be relatively safe and stable. Their repos is one of the first thing I add after installing an Ubuntu desktop system ...
