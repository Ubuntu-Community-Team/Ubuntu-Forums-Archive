### Post by Lars Noodén on 2010-08-26
> **TNT1 said:**
> . What should I be doing?

Steering them to Ubuntu or Fedora would help a lot.  Live CDs and USB Stick distros are helpful for very dogmatized or otherwise stuck individuals.  

Too often people play the role of [enabler](http://www.amazon.com/Enabler-When-Helping-Hurts-Ones/dp/1587360675) and worsen the situation over time.  

Ubuntu has a good utility called [startup disk creator](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick#Creating%20a%20bootable%20USB%20Drive) which is in the repositories.  It is also part of the default for Kubuntu and many live CDs.  That will help you help them get started with safe systems.
