### Post by TheFu on 2018-08-24
No. Wifi is inherently non-secure without a full VPN so that really isn't the issue.  Any RF is an attack vector.

USB is also an attack vector, but very few locations are wiling to disable all USB access, sadly.  I'm included in the people who find USB unwilling to disable USB ports, but I've worked places that hot-glued all externally accessible USB ports.
