### Post by bashiergui on 2014-12-08
Sure. Someone that has physical access to your computer can change your login password. If that's a concern then lock up the room the computer is in.

If your logon password is the same as your encryption passphrase then yah, it would be easy to guess. Make the passphrase something else and complex.
