### Post by BkkBonanza on 2011-06-21
There are several ways that you can appear to be sharing an IP address. 

But my guess in this case is that they are probably just trying to get you to upgrade. All of these download type sites use various tricks to hassle non-paying users into becoming paying users. The most common is forcing you to wait to download. There are some though where it's practically impossible to download anything because they always have some excuse... 

The ones where I consistently find it easy to download are RapidShare, MegaUpload and MediaFire. Many of the others are like pulling teeth and it's better to not waste your time.
