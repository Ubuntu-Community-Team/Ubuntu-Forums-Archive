I'm going to ask some potentially silly questions.

1) Did you at any point in time add a google toolbar?

2) Are you referring to the search bar in the top-right of your firefox window?

If (2) is the case, what you can do is right-click on the space between it and the navigation toolbar and click on "customize". Then drag the google toolbar to the window that just opened.

I think the warnings you read were about a different type of toolbar. Frequently when you install free programs in Windows, they will offer (or sometimes automatically) install a yahoo or google toolbar into your browser. This is different from the default search bar in firefox. I don't think you need to worry about the thing you removed.  It is just a built-in convenience feature and you can switch it (by clicking on the google logo) to use any other search engine you desire (including ixquick).
