Just an F.Y.I:

The Stumbleupon toolbar for Firefox automatically adds 3 cookies with the "allow" status, regardless of whether or not "Accept Cookies" is disabled under the Preferences screen. :mad:

I haven't found a fix yet, so if this is undesirable behaviour your only recourse is to disable the add-on.
