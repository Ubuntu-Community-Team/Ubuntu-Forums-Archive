### Post by Titan8990 on 2008-09-04
The 8600 GT is a huge waste of money. It's cost is relatively high and it's performance is crap. The 8xxx series was actually two generations ago (although the 8800 GT still is not a bad buy).

I couldn't recommend a P35 chipset more, specifically the GA-EP35-DS3L. 

I assume when you say 2.6Ghz core 2 duo you are referring to the E6750. Don't get it. Go with the E8400 or E8500.


Don't forget to buy a **quality** PSU. Antec, Silverstone, Corsair, Seasonic are all excellent choices.

Basically you have chose all last generation hardware which is not practical when this generation's hardware is relatively the same cost.

You fail to mention the brand, speed, and timings of your RAM.

If you are from America use newegg links, from Canada use NCIX links, otherwise I couldn't tell ya what etailer to use.

Edit: you should also note that gaming doesn't go very well in Linux. Especially if you want the eye candy.
