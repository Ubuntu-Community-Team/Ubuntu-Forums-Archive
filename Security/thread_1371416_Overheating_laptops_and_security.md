### Post by doas777 on 2010-01-05
heating just means utilization. whether that utilization is good or bad...

linux sometimes has trouble working with the part of your motherboard that contols power fans and sensor readings. as a result some run too hot.

my laptop runs a good 15C cooler in karmic than in vista, but many others have had opposite results. others reported increased operating temperatures going from hardy to intrepid/jaunty, and I'm not sure if the related issues have been addressed or just quited down.
