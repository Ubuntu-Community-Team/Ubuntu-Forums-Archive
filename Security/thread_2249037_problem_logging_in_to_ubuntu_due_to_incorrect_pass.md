### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-10-19
I would simply insist that the shop put this right for you; you could certainly do so in UK under the **Sale of Goods** act, or you can follow the instructions at [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/resetpassword](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/resetpassword) which will allow you to find the users already setup and to reset a new password for the users you want to use.

The preinstalled Ubuntu was presumably the OEM install, about which I know very little except that it exists; I don't know anything about the user setup after purchase, but I imagine a search will quickly get you the information that may be helpful.
