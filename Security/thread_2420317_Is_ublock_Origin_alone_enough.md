### Post by Rubi1200 on 2019-06-03
I think you will probably get as many opinions on the matter as there are addons.

Basically, it comes down to personal choice and comfort levels I think. What works for you might not work for me and what works for me might not work for the next person.

One addon to consider though is Privacy Badger from the EFF:
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/privacy-badger17/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/privacy-badger17/)

Works well with uBlock Origin and adds an extra layer of security, which is always good.
