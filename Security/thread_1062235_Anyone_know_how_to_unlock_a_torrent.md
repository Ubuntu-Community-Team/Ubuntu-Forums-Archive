Answer is you can't. The torrent file isn't locked. There are many sites which use private trackers and need you to have your ip with them to allow you to download - simple. So you need to go to this site and register, further when you download the torrent, it gives you a record in their database. Its kinda funny and dont work exactly as I say here in most cases. There is a private tracker where I belong to, which needs me to login to their site for an ip address update before deluge will again start to download the file. i.e. my ip is "acknowledged" by tracker to allow me to connect to peers from the tracker.

Some only use the concept of Passkey rather than IP as dynamic IPs can cause problems

A Passkey is a unique string of 32 characters that has been randomly assigned to your account. It helps the tracker identify you as a user.

When you download a .torrent file from this site, the Passkey is automatically added to the announce URL in the .torrent file. Therefore whenever that .torrent is used the tracker will associate it with your account, no matter what IP you are connecting from.
