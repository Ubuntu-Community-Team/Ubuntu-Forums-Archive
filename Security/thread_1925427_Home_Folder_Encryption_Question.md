---

### Post by kevdog on 2012-02-14
Quick Answer: No you do not.

Long Answer: When the volume is mounted at startup, it is decrypted, when its unmounted its encrypted.  It basically prevents anyone else taking out your hard-drive and mounting it on a remote file system to access your files.
