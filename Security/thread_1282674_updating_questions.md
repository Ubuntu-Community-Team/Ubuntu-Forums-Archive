### Post by snowpine on 2009-10-04
Updates are cyclical... in the weeks after a new release (for example, after 9.10 Karmic is released later in the month), you will get lots of updates, then they taper off over time. If a big package like OpenOffice is updated, you might get 100mb or more of updates.

Remember, it is up to you how frequently you update... some people do it every day, or once a week, or once a month... 

If you want the least amount of updates, use the LTS (long term support) release, currently 8.04 Hardy Heron. It is very stable and rarely updated, yet will be fully supported through April 2011.
