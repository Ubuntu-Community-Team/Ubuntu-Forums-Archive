### Post by cariboo on 2010-02-17
The specification say bluetooth transmit/receive has a range of about 10m. From personal experience, I would say more like 10ft.

> 1---Can anyone (without my knowledge)connect to my phone while the bluetooth is active. If yes how to prevent such intrusions ?

In most cases, depending how you have bluetooth setup, they can't connect to your device without permission. To make sure require devices to enter a password before connecting.

> 2-----Can someone (without my knowledge) connect to my PC while the bluetooth device connected to my PC is active. If yes how to prevent them ?

Again, require a password before connection, or better yet turn bluetooth off when not in use.

> 3----Can someone monitor what files are being transfered between my PC & mobile. Again, if yes how to prevent such activities

See above.

For more info on Bluetooth security, have a look at this [document]("http://www.securenetwork.it/bluebag_brochure.pdf").
