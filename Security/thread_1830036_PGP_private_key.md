### Post by tubbygweilo on 2011-08-21
vagelism, IIRC your keys are stored in your .gnupg folder held within you home folder. It is this folder you need to save and keep in a safe place as it contains information of all your keys private and public.

PS It is also advisable to create a revocation certificate for each of your keys and as always keep you copy of .gunpg in a safe p[lace as if sdomeone gains access to thsi information they have your keys and to the rest of the world they are you. 

Physical security of keys and backups of keys ios something to be taken seriously at all times.
