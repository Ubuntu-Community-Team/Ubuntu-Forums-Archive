### Post by cogset on 2015-08-04
> **PsychedelicWonders said:**
> I know Ubuntu comes standard with Firefox, but I'm looking for the most secure & private browser possible.

  Then you are not looking for Firefox... jokes aside, it is indeed a fine browser but these days not so privacy-oriented in its standard trim. 
Read about the suggested addons and install what suits you, general consensus is that Adblock and NoScript are a must, but there are more that can improve privacy at the cost of usability. 

 And then don't be afraid to delve into the  about:config page and disable all those services that do nothing good for your privacy (as said in the post above, geolocation being one of them)... the Mozillazine pages explain almost all those preferences and what they do control, you can always ask on the forums for other info.
