### Post by fugu2 on 2015-01-27
Yeah, IMHO, iptables-save is way more informative the iptables -L for debugging.
Also, when debugging your own firewall using the -j LOG feature without the -m limit maybe more helpful to you until you solve the problem. after you fix it you can add the -m limit back in. I try and test my firewall and sometimes miss whats actually going on because its not showing up in my log because of rate limiting.
