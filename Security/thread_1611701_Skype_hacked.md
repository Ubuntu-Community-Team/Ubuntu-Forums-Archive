### Post by janpol on 2010-11-02
And preferably use a password made by random numbers and letters, don't use words or numbers that are related to you (like your birthday), since this person might have some of your personal information (or maybe he/she is somebody you know). 

As this is an Ubuntu forum I assume you use skype on Ubuntu and only on Ubuntu, but if you use skype on Windows (or use any service with the same password on Windows), it might be a good idea to check your computer for spyware. 

Also, using wifi open hotspots might lead into information leakage, sending your private information out in the air unencrypted is not a good idea.

Good Luck!
