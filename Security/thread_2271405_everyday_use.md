### Post by BBQdave on 2015-03-31
I find Ubuntu 14.04LTS Unity plus Google Chrome handles most of my needs securely. I add the mp3 codec (*ugly* plug-in) for music playback on my notebook. Otherwise Google Chrome handles streaming and music fine on it's own.

As mentioned by Vladlenin5000, some of the security is with you, what you choose to download and the sites you choose to visit. For me, all applications come from Ubuntu trusted repos (the default repos) and the only extra application I add (not from Ubuntu) is Google Chrome. I trust Google's security, so that is a risk I am willing to take.

I would offer, with any OS you choose to run, keep it up to date (patched) and browse trusted sites and download only trusted data :)
