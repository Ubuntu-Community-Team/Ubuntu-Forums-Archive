### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-09-25
Viruses and infections should not be a worry for Linux users. Google "Linux Virus" and you will find some very worthy information.

Ubuntu will never get involved in directly infecting your Windows Network more than an extent they are already exposed to without the presence of Ubuntu on the network. You need proper Anti-virus, Spy-ware, Firewall on all the Windows PCs to protect them.

Ubuntu is secure by default. No open ports until you open specific ones. For firewall with Ubuntu, see this page.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UFW](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UFW)
