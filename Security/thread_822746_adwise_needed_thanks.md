### Post by The Cog on 2008-06-08
If you have really been hacked, you should re-format and reinstall because you can't trust any of the system any more.

However, sys-sent means the computer has sent a connect request to the remote IP address but has not received a response - neither a refusal or an acceptance. It is normally a sign of a network problem rather than a problem on your PC.
