### Post by lisati on 2009-08-04
> **waqar said:**
> So i dont do anything for my system's security? No firewall?

A firewall isn't the same as antivirus: they have different jobs. Antivirus software helps protect you from damage cause by malicious programs that you run yourself, and a firewall helps protect you from someone (or something) trying to get into your computer from somewhere else.

Edit: a couple of links that might be helpful:
