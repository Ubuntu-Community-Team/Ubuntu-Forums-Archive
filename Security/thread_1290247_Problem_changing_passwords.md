### Post by Sarmacid on 2009-10-13
Not completely sure if this will do it, but go to Applications -> Accesories -> Passwords and encryption keys

There, go to the last tab named passwords, right click the passwords option in there(Next to what looks like a folder) and switch to your new password. I would guess this will do it, but as I said, I'm not sure.
