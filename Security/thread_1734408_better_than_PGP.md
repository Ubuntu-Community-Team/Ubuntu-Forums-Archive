### Post by rookcifer on 2011-04-20
> **mynameisnotbob said:**
> Is there any available Ubuntu encryption stronger than a 4096-bit DSA PGP key that is natively supported or can be supported by Evolution?

For asymmetric encryption, no.  4096 bits is all that GPG allows, and it is strong enough for just about any imaginable purpose. The key size is the least of your worries when talking about encryption security.
