### Post by cariboo on 2009-11-22
Depending on how old and how adept your child is, couldn't you just remove the menu entries from the menu that you don't want the child to access? Just right-click Applications and select Edit Menus and remove the check marks for the programs you don't want the child to acces.

I know this is security by obscurity, but as the child gets older and it learns more, there may be no need to block access to anything.
