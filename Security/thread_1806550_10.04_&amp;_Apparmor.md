### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2011-07-18
Apparmor is a great tool, but is waiting to mature in terms of maintained profiles and graphical tools to manage profiles and common options / errors.

Basically you will need to :

1. maintain rules yourself (a bit of a pain I know).

2. File a bug report. First make sure you have apparmor-profiles installed ;)

These shortcomings are one of the main reasons I use Fedora, selinux is much more mature on Fedora then Apparmor on Ubuntu.

Another alternate is to try SUSE, suse has graphical apparmor management tools. They do a decent job ;)
