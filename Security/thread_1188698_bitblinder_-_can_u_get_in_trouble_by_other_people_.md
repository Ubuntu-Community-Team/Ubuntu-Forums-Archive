### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-06-16
> **pluckypigeon said:**
> What happens if you are the last peer? Does your ip address show up on the website instead?

Yes. If your machine is the exit point, then your IP will be registered as the source of the connection request. The major concern of this system is that if someone downloads something really bad like "you know what", then you might get a visit from the Scotland Yard, MI5 or whatever other federal police enforcement you have in England that deals with such serious crimes. 

There are some discussions if you could be held liable for running an exit point or not in this situation, but does it worth the trouble?

I would stay away from this kind of service, since it is most likely that it will attract some shady users.
