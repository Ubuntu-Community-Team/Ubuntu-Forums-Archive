### Post by fabricator4 on 2011-02-09
> **osbonkers said:**
> I have come to know that one can find my passwords of various sites(gmail/fb) through some method in ubuntu. It says ubuntu saves them and one can easily find them. can you please enlighten me on this topic. and how can i prevent it.

It's not so much Ubuntu, but browsers like Firefox and Google Chrome will offer to remember passwords for you.  In many cases it's possible to go into the password management area in these programmes and see your passwords in clear.

To maintain your internet password security do not let any of these programmes save the password for you.  If you've already saved some passwords simply enter the password management area and delete the entry.  In future select "never for this site" and the browser will not offer to remember passwords for this site again.
