Hi,

So i have encrypted home. Home decrypts automatically when signing in. But after some consideration i have came to the conclusion it is a bad security practice. If somebody steals your comp he can chroot it, change password and he got then full access to your home folder.

Correct or am i missing here something?

---

### Post by sudodus on 2015-08-23
'Encrypted home' depends on the password. If you change password, it will not unlock the encryption. So it is important to make a good password in the beginning.

You can also consider 'encrypted disk' or both :-)

But keep good backups, because if the system is damaged or you forget the password, there is no way to read the encrypted data.

### Post by sudodus on 2015-08-23
Yes, and that would be very inconvenient. Notice that the first time you run a system with encrypted home, you are prompted to save a passphrase for the encryption. It is a good idea to save that passphrase somewhere (not in the computer).
