### Post by FuturePilot on 2010-12-13
> **Fuith said:**
> Nope, runs fine, although very slow, doing anything else.

I would suggest getting the CPU temperature. Just because it appears to be running fine doesn't necessarily mean it's not overheating. CPUs will throttle themselves when they reach their max temperature, perhaps causing this slowness you are seeing. The fact that you can reproduce this in two different OSes suggests that it's most likely a hardware problem.
