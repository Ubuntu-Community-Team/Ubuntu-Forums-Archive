### Post by dionysius on 2011-03-02
Your ISP will have a log with the IP addresses you connect to, but depending on your location they may discard such details periodically or are bound to archive them by law. 

The name of your computer is not declared anywhere outside your private network. Your router may or may not keep a log of MAC addresses and the IP addresses requested from attached devices. That depends on the router's internal settings. My router (Netgear thing, supplied by ISP) does not by default log anything, I'd need to activate logging.
