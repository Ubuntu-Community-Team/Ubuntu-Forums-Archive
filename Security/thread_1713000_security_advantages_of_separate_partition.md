So I am curious of something. Suppose I want a subset of files on a usb to be less likely to corrupt or, have any contact with/ acess to, the rest of the files on the usb.

### Post by The Cog on 2011-03-23
Maybe. If you don't mount that partition so often, I suppose the chances of corruption are less, though they are not zero even if you never mount that partition.

Windows (I am told) can only see the first partition on a stick. You might see that as either a good or a bad thing.

You have the option of encrypting the second partition.
