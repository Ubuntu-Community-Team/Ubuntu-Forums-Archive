### Post by lisati on 2010-04-25
Many of the people who frequent this forum are of the opinion that there isn't the same need for antivirus on Ubuntu as with Windows. This is due in part to the way *nix systems are designed: short of you doing something stupid, it is a lot more difficult for malware to hurt your Ubuntu system.

The main reasons I am aware of for considering AV on Ubuntu are if you share files with Windows users and don't want to pass something on accidentally, you are troubleshooting a Windows system, or you are running an email server.
