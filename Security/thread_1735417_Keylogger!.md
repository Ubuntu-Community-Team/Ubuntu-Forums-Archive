From the look of that email, they didn't have your password, they guessed your Insecurity Question.

'Security questions' are the worst thing to happen to computer security in years. If you answer correctly then they're easy for an attacker to guess, if you make up an answer then they're just another password you have to remember.

I suspect far more accounts have been compromised by 'Security Questions' than by stolen passwords. Didn't someone recently take control of a politician's email account that way?
