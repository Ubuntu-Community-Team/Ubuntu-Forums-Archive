### Post by Hungry Man on 2012-10-31
Good question. There's been a ton of media hype about this and not a lot of really hard analysis.

Essentially what you type into your Dash gets sent to Canonical and from there that information is aggregated and given to Amazon.

Now this may seem like a privacy violation but let's remember two critical things:
1) This already happens for Canonical to suggest new applications and music via software center and music center.

2) You very rarely type sensitive information into the Dash.

I think (2) is very important. Ask yourself - what do I type into the Dash? And then ask yourself if you really care that it's sent somewhere.
