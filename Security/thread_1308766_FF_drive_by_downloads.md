Could you be more specific?

From what I understand, you have visited one of those scam sites asking for downloading anti-virus and showing a fake scan report, is that right? If you have and are worried about it, don't be. Those scams target Windows machines.

How do you know a link from Google is poisoned? What tool are you using to determine that? Are you getting those Firefox/Google alerts?

I recommend installing [NoScript](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/722) and [Adblock Plus](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1865). Then relax and enjoy.

For the slow Internet try **Solution** [*[COLOR="Red"]**FOT005**[/COLOR]*] from the Troubleshooting section of the [**[COLOR="DarkRed"]Firefox optimization and troubleshooting thread[/COLOR]**](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1193567&highlight=FOT005).
