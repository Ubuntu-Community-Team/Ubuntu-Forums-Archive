### Post by Azdour on 2012-01-27
Hi,

The two languages may be the issue. I've come across it before. The password on the lock screen does not echo characters (rightly so) but that makes it difficult to see what language you are typing in.

What language is your password? You should be able to change the keyboard language on the lock screen. On Ubuntu 10.04 the change language part is to the right of the password field.
