### Post by bruno9779 on 2010-03-16
Not really, things are quite different from what you are used too in windows.

There is a firewall installed by default, iptables. you may want to download a gui for it from repos, like gufw.

There are no relevant virus threats for Linux at the moment. There haven't been for ages.
It is possible to make linux virus in theory, but there are none in the wild now.

here is a good link that should cover most of your questions

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/security](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/security)
