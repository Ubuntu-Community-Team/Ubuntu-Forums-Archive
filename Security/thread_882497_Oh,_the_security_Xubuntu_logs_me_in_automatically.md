This has happened to me, once, and only once; but I couldn't really use the "desktop"; turned out that it was still locked, and the password dialog was not being shown. If you wave your mouse near about at the center of the screen, it will change into a text cursor ("I"), click there, and type your password to unlock your desktop. You may have to try a couple of times.

Subsequently all was normal for me. No idea where the glitch came from and where it disappeared.

My problem/solution may not be your problem/solution.
