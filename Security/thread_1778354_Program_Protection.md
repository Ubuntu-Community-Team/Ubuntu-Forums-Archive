### Post by Lars Noodén on 2011-06-09
Either you can rely on the customer or you can't.  The main thing is to be clear before you hand over the system about which parts are proprietary.  Have copyright notices in the source prominently displayed.  If want to have annoying notices at startup, then do that, too.  But it all comes down to whether the customer is trustworthy or not.
