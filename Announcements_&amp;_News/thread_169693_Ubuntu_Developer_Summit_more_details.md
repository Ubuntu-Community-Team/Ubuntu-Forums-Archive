### Post by Jane Weideman on 2006-05-03
As per Friday's message. Canonical has announced that the next Ubuntu
Developer Summit will take place from 19 - 23 June outside Paris,
France. 

The primary focus of this event will be for the distro team and others
to gather together to concentrate on spec writing and technical planning
for Ubuntu 6.10 (Edgy Eft). We are requesting that the attendees start
preparing as many specs beforehand as possible. Enabling us to spend
more time discussing, and approving specs, and less time on drafting.


Here are the things you need to know:

 --> What: 	
                        Ubuntu Developer Summit

 --> Where: 	
                        Radisson SAS, Charles de Gaulle, Paris.  
                        The hotel is a 7 minute drive from Charles de
                        Gaulle airport.  Note that The Radisson
                        provide's a courtesy bus for their clients to go
                        to and from the airport. 

 --> When: 	
                        The event will run from Monday 19th to Friday
                        23rd June 2006 (For those staying in the hotel
                        you should arrange to arrive on Sunday 18th June
                        and check out on the morning of Saturday 24th
                        June.)

 --> Who: 	
                        The Ubuntu Distro Members, Sponsored Guests and
                        self-sponsored visitors.

Reminder that there is a limited pool available for sponsorship. 
Anyone is welcome to apply for sponsorship, but we will give
preference to people who are active, visible, and known in the community
and who are from the general geographical area. 

More information on sponsorship linked from the conference page [2]. All
requests for sponsorship must be made no later than 12:00UTC May 5th
2006.

Any specific questions not answered on that page can be directed to
