At that time, Ubuntu Security Noticeswill no longer include information or updated packages for Ubuntu 8.04.The supported upgrade path from Ubuntu 8.04 is via Ubuntu 10.04.Users are encouraged to evaluate and upgrade to our latest 12.04 LTSrelease via 10.04.
