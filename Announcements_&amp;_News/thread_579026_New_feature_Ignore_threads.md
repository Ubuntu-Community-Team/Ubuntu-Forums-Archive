### Post by ubuntu-geek on 2007-10-17
You can now ignore specific threads on the forums. To do this open the thread you wish to ignore, goto "Thread Tools" and select "Ignore This Thread". The thread is now ignored you can go into your User CP and on the left hand menu remove and view your ignore threads.

Enjoy :KS
