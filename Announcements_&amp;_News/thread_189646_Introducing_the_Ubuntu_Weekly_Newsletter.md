### Post by Matt Zimmerman on 2006-06-05
The Ubuntu Documentation Team has begun to produce a weekly newsletter
to help inform the Ubuntu community of current events related to the Ubuntu
family of distributions (including Ubuntu, Kubuntu, Edubuntu and Xubuntu).

The first issue has just been released and may be found here:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuWeeklyNewsletter/Issue1](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuWeeklyNewsletter/Issue1)

Future editions of the Ubuntu Weekly Newsletter will NOT be announced to
this list: it will remain a very low-traffic list for announcements only.
To receive the weekly newsletter by mail, subscribe to the ubuntu-news
mailing list:

[https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-news](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-news)

If you are interested in contributing to the newsletter, or have feedback
for the Documentation Team, send an email to [email]ubuntu-doc (AT) lists (DOT) ubuntu.com[/email] or
use any of the other methods on the Ubuntu Documentation Team Contact
Information Page:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DocumentationTeam/Contact](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DocumentationTeam/Contact)

We hope that you enjoy this resource and that contributions from the Ubuntu
community will make it even more successful.
