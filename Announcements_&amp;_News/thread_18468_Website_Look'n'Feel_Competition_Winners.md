### Post by Jeff Waugh on 2005-03-07
Website Look'n'Feel Competition Winners
=======================================

We received so many fantastic entries for the Ubuntu website look'n'feel
competition...

So many that even after a gruelling selection period, we could not convince
our judges to choose a single winner! Instead, the first prize will be
distributed between the authors of the top three entries, because they have
all inspired the forthcoming new look'n'feel of the Ubuntu website.

Congratulations to Matthew Nuzum, Brad Griffith and Matt Jones, and thanks
very much to every entrant. It was a very hard competition to judge!

See the winning entries at:

  [http://www.ubuntu.com/webcomp2005winners/](http://www.ubuntu.com/webcomp2005winners/)

- Jeff
