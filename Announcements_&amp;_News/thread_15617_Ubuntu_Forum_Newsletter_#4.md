### Post by ubuntu-geek on 2005-02-15
Ubuntuforums.org has topped 8,000 users in just a little over 5 months. \\:D/ 
 
 Some noteable changes to the forums user titles and the new reputation system.
 
 [b]User Titles: 
 [/b]Have now been updated and hopefully are a little more fun then the previous ones. Thanks for everyone on their input.
 
 [b]Reputation System:
 [/b]This allows you to vote on other users threads that you find useful. You will see a green "dot" under your "post count" in the forum threads to denote your reputation based on other users ratings.
 
 Users gain and lose reputation based on how their posts are scored by other forum users. Users with the ability to affect reputation, will either give or take aways points by approving or disapproving with a post's content.
 
 We will consider this system to be in "beta" for now.
