Hi,

Due to necessary physical relocation, there will be some downtime for
Ubuntu & Launchpad services hosted by Canonical in its London Data
Centre.

The work is scheduled to begin at 22:00 UTC on Saturday, 11th
February.  The expected downtime for all services is 90 minutes.
However the maintenance window for the work is 6 hours and there may
be some limited temporary loss of services throughout this period.

Apologies for the inconvenience and the lack of notice, which is the
result of circumstances beyond our control.

The Ubuntu package archive [[http://archive.ubuntu.com/]](http://archive.ubuntu.com/]) will be
relocated for the duration of the maintenance and will be unaffected.

Affected services will include:

 o The Ubuntu website and wiki [[http://www.ubuntu.com](http://www.ubuntu.com) and
                                [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/]](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/])
 o Ubuntu mailing lists [[https://lists.ubuntu.com/]](https://lists.ubuntu.com/])

 o The Launchpad (including Rosetta, Soyuz[1] and Malone)
   [[https://launchpad.net/]](https://launchpad.net/]), wiki and mailing lists
  
 o The ShipIt website [[http://shipit.ubuntu.com/]](http://shipit.ubuntu.com/])

 o The cdimage.ubuntu.com, releases.ubuntu.com and torrent.ubuntu.com
   mirror sites

 o Planet Ubuntu [[http://planet.ubuntu.com/]](http://planet.ubuntu.com/]), The Fridge
   [[http://fridge.ubuntu.com]](http://fridge.ubuntu.com])

 o The Kubuntu & Edubuntu websites and wiki
   [[http://www.kubuntu.org/](http://www.kubuntu.org/), [http://wiki.kubuntu.org/](http://wiki.kubuntu.org/),
    [http:///www.edubuntu.org/](http:///www.edubuntu.org/), [http://wiki.edubuntu.org/]](http://wiki.edubuntu.org/])

 o The Bazaar, bzr and supermirror websites + wiki
   [[http://bazaar.canonical.com](http://bazaar.canonical.com), [http://www.bazaar-ng.org/]](http://www.bazaar-ng.org/])

 o The OpenCD website [[http://www.theopencd.org/]](http://www.theopencd.org/])

- --
