### Post by ubuntu-geek on 2007-04-05
Today we launched a new optional field on the New Post and New Reply screens in the technical support area's of the forum. This field will let you post a link to an already filed bug report at launchpad.net. The url to the bug will show up in your post.

We hope this will help keep a uniformed approach to linking to bugs and provide more visibility to already established bugs.
