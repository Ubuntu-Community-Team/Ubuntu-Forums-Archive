### Post by Seti on 2007-08-27
If you're trying to locate a program, application or utility you've installed, the whereis command is useful. Maybe you installed a program through Synaptic called xmms but can't find in anywhere.```
whereis xmms
```
Sometimes some programs don't create an entry under the menu. In this case you may have to just manually make your own entry there.
