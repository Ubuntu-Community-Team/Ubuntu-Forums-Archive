### Post by tekknokrat on 2008-09-22
from my point of view its easier to setup falcon and with more foresight.
You can easy configure via interactive configuration editor.
You can template the packages webview via commonly known django-templates.
you can have db backend (pgsql), mirroring and extend falcon through plugins.
