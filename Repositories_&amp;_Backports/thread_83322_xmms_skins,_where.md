### Post by public_void on 2005-10-28
I found searching for them on google easier, I found the home page [here]("http://www.xmms.org/skins.php"). Download a .tar.gz and extract to /home/"username"/.xmms/skins/ I kept it in a folder (note: .xmms was hidden).So you get /home/"username"/.xmms/skins/"Skin Name"/. If you go to options->Skin browser in XMMS you should see your skin there.
