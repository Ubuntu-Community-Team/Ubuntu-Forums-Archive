### Post by de_valentin on 2007-05-19
Oops that was quick and easy just found out.

System->Administration->Language Support 

and add the languages you need 

restart evolution

Edit->Preferences->Composer preferences

tab:Spell Checking

et voila there they are all the languages you just installed you can even use more than one at the same time
