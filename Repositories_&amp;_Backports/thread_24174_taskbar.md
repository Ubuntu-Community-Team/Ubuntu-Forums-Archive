### Post by Xian on 2005-04-05
Visually it would probably be better to create a new panel and use that exclusively for listing your open programs. To do this, just right click on an empty space on your current panel and choose "New Panel". Then right click on the new panel and select:

"Add To Panel" > "Window List"

Or you can just add this option to your current panel.
