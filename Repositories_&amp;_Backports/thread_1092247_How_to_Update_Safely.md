If your installation was corrupted because of a power shortage during the update, you'll be exposed to the same risk even if you download the packages and then update later on.
That's because the updater downloads all the packages and then, after download is finished installs them.

What you can do is select single packages to download from the update app. I've never done it myself, but I see you can check/uncheck the updates to download.

So, unless I'm missing your point, that's the only "solution" to your problem.
