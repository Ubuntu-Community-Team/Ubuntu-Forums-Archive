### Post by felipe on 2008-05-26
Hi, just to inform you that I've started backporting again some of the newest packages. This time I'm porting Intrepid packages to Hardy.

This is the third "edition" of this repo, I already have a number of long time testers and packages seem to do very fine so far, with no problem across updates/upgrades. My policy is not to depend from outside sources and not to pack any libraries (ever), only GNOME or GTK applications.

Right now the "pollyrepo" contains just **Firefox 3 RC1**, updated **Epiphany** to use **Xulrunner** and I'm compiling **aMule SVN** 20080525 (that's *yesterday*) righ now :)

If you feel like using it, let me know of any packages you may want me to backport. This is the repo's homepage [http://pollycoke.tuxfamily.org/]("http://pollycoke.tuxfamily.org/") you can find there further instructions.
