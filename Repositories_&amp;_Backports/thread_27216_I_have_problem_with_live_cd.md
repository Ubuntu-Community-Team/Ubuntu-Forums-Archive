### Post by Bob D. on 2005-04-15
OK, a couple of things. Are you positive you downloaded the Live CD ISO? What is the name of the ISO you have?

It has been known to happen that folks grab the wrong image by accident. Just helped a guy yesterday that grabbed the Live CD in error when he wanted the regular ISO.

If it is the Live CD, the other things I can think of would be to check the md5sums of the ISO to see if you got a bad download. Also, you might check to make sure your PC's BIOS is set to boot off the CD drive.
