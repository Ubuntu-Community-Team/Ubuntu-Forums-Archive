Frank-
Although I have no idea whether 5.0 will be in the fiesty repositories, I do know that to be in they have to be final before the software freeze for any edition occurs. Since Listen doesn't have a very large user base, I doubt it gets updated very often (although if it keeps on bounding ahead of Rhythmbox and more users switch, it may be asked to become a standard part of GNOME).

All said, Listen is one of the few apps i actually update by hand. This would annoy me, but I often try to keep in mind that I have to do this every 3-4 months with WinAmp on my work Laptop, so it isn't unreasonable.

Sometime after Feisty comes out, perhaps you should see if they have updated Listen in the repos. If not, maybe you should submit a ticket for the upcoming 7.10 release.
