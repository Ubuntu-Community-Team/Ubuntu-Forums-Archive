### Post by petenz on 2005-02-06
The excellent Ubuntuguide.org has a section on this [here](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#extrarepositories)  which worked just fine for me.

And then eventually you read your way through the guide and find out that you could  have done it in Synaptic by going through Settings(from memory)>Repositories and adding it there. Character building to use the command line though. obviously.... 

And if you are keen on the command line option but want to get an insight into what you are doing, [linuxcommand.org](http://www.linuxcommand.org/) is the best primer I've come across.
