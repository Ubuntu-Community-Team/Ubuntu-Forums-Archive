### Post by anandkumargupta on 2009-11-19
Hi,
 

 When I upgraded from 9.04 to 9.10 then my wireless connection stop working. I delete the previous connection then go to Network Manager(Right upper icon like mobile network) icon then left click &#8594; Connect to hidden network wireless connection &#8594; Select my type (mine is WPA and WPA2 personal) &#8594; Provide the key then connect.
 

 The above process solve my problem. I think it will do yours also those who are not working due to up-gradation.
