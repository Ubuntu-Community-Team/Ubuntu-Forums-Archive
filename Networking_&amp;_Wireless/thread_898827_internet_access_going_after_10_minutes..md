### Post by gjoellee on 2008-08-23
I do only know one reason for this to happen:

your router is under heavy load. You may use something that use a lot of your internet or many computers are connected to your router.

if you want to check for computer connected to your router go here: [http://www.lawrencegoetz.com/programs/ipinfo/](http://www.lawrencegoetz.com/programs/ipinfo/)

copy your IP address and paste it to your location bar (the bar you enter the address for website in), and you should there see which computers that are connected to your router
