### Post by ieee488 on 2009-01-24
What exactly is the hardware that your satellite internet provider is giving you to connect? It's probably a box that has at least one LAN jack.
You'll need a network cable to connect from that LAN jack to the LAN jack on your PC and hope that your network card chipset on your PC is compatible with Linux.
