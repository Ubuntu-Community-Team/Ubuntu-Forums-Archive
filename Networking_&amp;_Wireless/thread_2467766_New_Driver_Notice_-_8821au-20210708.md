### Post by morrownr on 2021-10-07
News: A new version of the Realtek driver for the 8811au and 8821au chipsets for USB WiFi adapters is now available. Testing has shown it to be a much improved driver over earlier versions of the driver. Managed and master modes perform without known issues and performance is good. Monitor mode supports packet injection for those involved in security analysis.

[https://github.com/morrownr/8821au-20210708](https://github.com/morrownr/8821au-20210708)
