I use the samba share instead. With Samba share the folder under Ubuntu will be seen like windows share folder. I use XBMC to play the file. It works very well. To install samba you can both follow the wiki or the Unofficial Ubuntu FAQ. Under Gnome or KDE, it is very easy to define the share directory.
