### Post by spd106 on 2007-04-14
It depends, you don't say what you want to use it for. There's a big difference between a GPS module for a laptop and a handheld GPS tracker that can connect to your PC for uploading/downloading data.

Anything supported by [gpsbabel]("http://www.gpsbabel.org/") will be usable. 

I've not checked but the FSF might endorse a particular brand that play nice with OSS developers.
