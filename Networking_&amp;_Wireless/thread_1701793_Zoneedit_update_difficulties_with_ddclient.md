### Post by Trapper on 2011-03-07
I have resolved this issue. I changed my password at zoneedit and removed the special characters "*" and "&" from it. I did a restart of ddclient and immediately had a successful login and update.

Interesting. I could login to zoneedit via my browser with a password that had special characters but could not do a login process via the updater. Makes me wonder if ddclient is actually what has a problem passing special characters to zoneedit. My bet's on that and I also bet the "&" is the specific difficulty.

Whatever, I will lessen password security because someone still utilizes functions from the dark ages. There a myriads of implementations that properly address special characters and allow their usage in the interest of enhanced security. Someone's dragging their feet.
