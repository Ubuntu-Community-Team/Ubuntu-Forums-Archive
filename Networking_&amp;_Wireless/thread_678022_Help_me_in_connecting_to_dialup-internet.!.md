### Post by Kevbert on 2008-01-25
Assuming that your modem was connected when you installed Ubuntu you could try going to 
System - Administration - Network.  Then select Modem Connection and then click on Properties.
From this a new window will be displayed where you can select the modem, dial-up number etc.
