Hi.

The solution to passkey and passcode problems is to simply
pick a 4 digit number like    1234
then plug that in to both your cell, and ubuntu screens

other people may need to install a bunch of software and mess about in the terminal, but I prefer not to install software when not needed


more detailed steps:
- pop in a blue tooth dongle into your USB port
  ( I used a free one I got with my Nokia cell phone)
- minimize all apps
- in the corner, by the clock you will see the blue icon for bluetooth
- right click and say browse device 
- be sure to use your cell phone keypad to turn on bluetooth on your cell
- your cell phone should be listed on your computer screen
- pump in the numbers 1234 when prompted

ubuntu 8.04 with all system updates
