### Post by cjastram on 2007-11-08
Haynes or USRobotics.

Try to avoid USB modems, as they tend to be softmodems.  External serial modems are pretty much (are they always?) safe.

'Hard' modems are super-standard.  They might have slightly different init strings, but they are the classic serial device.  If it absolutely requires special drivers, it's probably a softmodem.
