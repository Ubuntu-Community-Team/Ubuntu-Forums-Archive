### Post by UberSoldAt666 on 2007-03-24
To start off this isn't a question, but more of a piece of advice if you happen to have this card.

[CENTER][IMG]http://tekgems.com/images/tn/MW302P-unit.jpg[/IMG][/CENTER]

The Chiefmax MW302P is a pretty common eBay Wireless PCI card. My piece of advice is to not use the included drivers, for me I couldn't ever get them to work with ndiswrapper. Instead, download the WMP11 version 4 driver (PCI Wireless card) from Linksys. From there just use the LSIPNDS.INF file in ndiswrapper (I suggest downloading all the necessary packages for ndisgtk, quite a bit easier). 

Hope this helps someone!
