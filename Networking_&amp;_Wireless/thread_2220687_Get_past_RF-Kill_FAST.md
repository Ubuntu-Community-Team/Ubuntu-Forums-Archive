### Post by sp-1070 on 2014-04-29
On many a laptop getting past the rf-kill hard block can be a pain.

You need to search all kinds o things and forums to find a solution that hopefully works, until you find a final solution to this problem i have a temporary one for you.

When you boot up your laptop and it says that your wifi is disabled and your heart sinks to your toe's have no fear !
A solution is here, just put your laptop into sleepmode then hold the fn+wifi key and hold that until your logon screen is there again.
Your wifi should work now.

I have only tested this on my own laptop so please leave a comment if it worked for you !

See ya
