### Post by cilynx on 2006-04-15
Anything with the Prism 802.11b chipset will work out of the box.
Anything with the PrismGT 802.11g chipset will work just about out of the box.

I have a Linksys WPC11 802.11b CardBus card that Just Works
I have an XG-600 802.11b/g miniPCI card that also Just Works

Best of luck to ya...

### Post by cilynx on 2006-04-15
The WPC11 is a Prism card.  Both the orinoco and hostap modules (drivers) support it no questions asked.  Both are available easily in Ubuntu.  Mine Just Worked without asking me any questions.  Personally, I like hostap as I got to know it very well using a Prism PCI card as an Access Point.
