### Post by osx424242 on 2007-10-16
Hey, that is my router, too!
I have not been able to get my wireless working yet, either, but from reading lots of posts, I think that the people who will be able to help you will need this kind of information from you:
- what version of Ubuntu (Feisty (7.04), Gutsy (7.10), other? Are you using straight Ubuntu, or a derivative (e.g. Kubuntu))?
- basic info about your wireless network (WAP vs. WEP vs. no encryption; are you broadcasting your ESSID)?
- the last dozen or so lines of output from 'dmesg'?
- what is your wireless card (e.g. Intel ipw3945abg)?
- the output from 'iwconfig'?
- what steps do you take to connect to the Internet when you boot into Ubuntu (e.g. "Open Firefox" or "left-click on Network Manager, select my network, wait 20 seconds but nothing happens....")?
