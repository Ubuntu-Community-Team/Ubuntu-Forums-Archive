### Post by Alacrity on 2008-05-27
Hi Napster,

Yes, the DWL-G650 is an excellent injection wireless card using the Atheros chip set.

If you are considering the antenna modification, though, you might want to consider a usb wireless device.

The DWL-G650 will probably have a stripline antenna internally which might be confusing for your to modify.  All the usb units have an easy to modify antenna connection which you can remove and replace with an antenna connector.

Good luck,
