### Post by Baelfael on 2007-02-24
Alright, this is simple. It uses someone elses HowTo, and then something I decided to mess around with. And it worked.

(thanks to solar george for linking me to this howto)
Follow this:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=197102](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=197102)

And when that works correctly, go to your Applications menu, and click on "Add/Remove". Search for "radar", and a program called "Wifi-Radar" should appear. Check it for installation, and when it's done, open it up. It should correctly identify your router. Click on "New" at the right, and set up your options. Then go ahead and try to connect!

I'd like to thank: 
compwiz18, for making the linked HOWTO.
solar george and the rest of the guys in all of my Wireless Card help threads.

I think that's it. This HOWTO is directed at Ubuntu-newbies. Hopefully it's easy enough!

Also, use this at your own risk. I don't have the time or experience to offer support.
