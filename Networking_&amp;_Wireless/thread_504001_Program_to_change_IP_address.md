### Post by spd106 on 2007-07-18
It depends on your ISP. Sometimes if you reset your modem (or whatever device you use to create the connection to your ISP) it will be given a different IP address. This assumes that your ISP runs dynamic assignment and that they have another IP address free to give to you. Even then it won't always work.

I had to do this after the rather buggy Eternal Lands client kept getting me banned from the server when I quit the game with Alt+X. Took me a while to figure that out.
