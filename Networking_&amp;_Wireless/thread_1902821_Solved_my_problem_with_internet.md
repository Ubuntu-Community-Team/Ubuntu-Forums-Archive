### Post by wowie on 2011-12-31
this is how I fixed my problem with the intermittent, then completely lost internet connection. I tried everything that was listed on the internet, and here for a resolution to this problem, and nothing helped. I spent days trying to fix this, and was pretty much resigned to having to reload my entire computer. (I dual boot with Windows 7 and Ubuntu 10.04)
My sister helped me and had me fixed in about 5 minutes. She is a Linux wizard, and I'm a newbie, lol
Okay, Go to System, Preferences, then Network Proxy. In the Network Proxy Preferences window, click on the Ignored Hosts tab, then in that window look to see if you have this in the list. You are looking for: *.local 
If you have that in the list, then highlight it, and click "remove" Then close out all windows, and click on your browser and see if you have internet connection. I did immediately, and I also did a cold boot, and everything has worked just fine since. (I did do all updates right away, just in case) 
I just wanted to see if this would help anyone else, and by the way, I have no idea how that got into my local hosts list. 
Good Luck
