### Post by LeoSolaris on 2008-04-17
Well it could be that the ISP is doing something called throttling, meaning they are directly upping and nerfing your speed depending on what site your visiting, or what your doing. In this case, it seems that they are boosting your bandwidth for that game (most likely because the game's manufacturers pay them to do so) and since it is not fine tuned enough to scale traffic speeds, it is most likely going with the highest setting.

Either that or you have a cable modem and your connecting at a slow time. Cable is a shared connection, so when there is more traffic, everyone slows down. DSL on the other hand is a constant connection, it does not fluctuate based on numbers of users.
