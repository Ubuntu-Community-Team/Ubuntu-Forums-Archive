### Post by cye05 on 2011-03-30
2 things you can try 

the first one is just to right click on your little network icon in the right hand corner and un check enable wireless.

our go into system, prefrences  and click on network connections then hit the wireless tab
here you can either delete the wireless network from the list or click edit and uncheck connect automatically.
