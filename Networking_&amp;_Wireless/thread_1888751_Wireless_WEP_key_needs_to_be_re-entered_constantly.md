### Post by Scott Baker on 2011-11-30
I'm running a fresh install 11.04 on a Dell M90 Precision with the Intel Pro/wireless 3945 card. With the most current updates, my machine will work with both wired and wireless connections (for now, anyhow). What's making me totally nuts is that when I use my wireless at home, I have to enter my WEP key EVERY SINGLE TIME!! In the past, with previous versions, I would only have to enter it on the initial set-up (the first time I'd use that connection) then when I'd use that connection in the future, it would be recognized automatically. I know I am probably missing some simple step to correct this, but damn if this isn't driving me out of my mind. HEELLLPPPP!! :confused:

### Post by Scott Baker on 2011-12-07
SOLVED finally. I have been scratching my head regarding this issue. I finally reinstalled 11.04 (it was a fresh install, so no biggie). Once it was installed, I retried the wireless. Got the screen that requested the WEP key. Once I finished entering the WEP key info, I received a second screen that asked me to enter a password for the account (I hadn't gotten this screen on the first install.) Once that was done, the wireless worked exactly as it should. (Thanks to all of you that took a look at this posting.)
