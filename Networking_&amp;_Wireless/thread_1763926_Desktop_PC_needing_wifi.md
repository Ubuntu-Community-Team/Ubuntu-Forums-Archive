### Post by prshah on 2011-05-21
> **slixz85 said:**
> if i get a wifi pci card the signal would be better than a usb wifi device right?

Not really; a PCI wifi card will usually be tucked away in whatever corner you place your tower in. You might get a better / stronger signal with the USB since you can place it better (USB WLAN cards usually come with an extender cable).

A PCI card is usually more hassle to install (since it requires the case to be opened). The only advantage to not using a USB WLAN is that you get to free up a USB port (if you are running low on USB ports). In all other cases, USB should be your preferred choice over a PCI WLAN card.
