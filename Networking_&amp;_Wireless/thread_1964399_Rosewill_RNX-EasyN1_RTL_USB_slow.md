### Post by clconway on 2012-04-23
I have a Rosewill RNX-EasyN1 RTL USB wifi adapter and I'm running 11.10. The adapter worked out-of-the-box with the rt2800usb driver, but lately it's been really slow, i.e., download speeds of 100-500 kbps on a connection that's rated up to 3 Mbps. I had assumed this was due to my Internet connection or the wifi network, but other computers on the same network can reach download speeds of 2.8+ Mbps, so it seems there's something wrong with the computer/adapter/driver.

The adapter uses a RT2870/RT3070 chipset, so I tried downloading both the latest firmware and driver source from [http://www.ralinktech.com](http://www.ralinktech.com). Neither seem to make any difference.
