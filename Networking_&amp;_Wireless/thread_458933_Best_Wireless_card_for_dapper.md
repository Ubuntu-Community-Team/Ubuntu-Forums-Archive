### Post by sbkcu on 2007-06-04
I have Dapper and I use the D-Link WDA-1320, which is for 802.11.g wlan's and has an Atheros chipset.  It "just works" :D.  However on my first try I apparently put it in the wrong PCI slot and it was not detected.  I then pulled out another card (which I think was the 10/100 ethernet card, I'm honestly not sure) and put the D-Link card in, and it worked immediately.  The first PCI card slot I tried may simply be flaky; I have a Dell Dimension 4800 desktop from 2003 and  I've heard their hardware can be unreliable.  Anyway I hope this helps any newbie like me trying to get wireless working with Dapper.
