### Post by justplayin on 2006-11-15
Well, when doing a [search]("http://www.google.de/search?hs=9lc&hl=de&client=firefox-a&rls=com.ubuntu%3Ade%3Aofficial&q=%22USRobotics%22+usb+ndiswrapper&btnG=Suche&meta=lr%3Dlang_en") I find that somebody seems to have it [working]("http://www.linuxquestions.org/questions/showthread.php?t=434871") under [ndiswrapper.]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper")
You can also download a LiveCD of Ubuntu, start it up, plug the usbstick and see if it shows up. Have a look at the chipset if it doesn't [work.]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WiFiHowTo") If you know the wireless chipset, you can search the forums for it and see if anyone got it working under Linux/Ubuntu.
If nothing works you can have a look at the [compatibility list]("http://www.linux-wlan.org/docs/wlan_adapters.html.gz"), and buy a wireless adaptor that works.

But I think you should be able to get it working, some way or the other.
