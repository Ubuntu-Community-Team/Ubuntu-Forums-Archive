### Post by chili555 on 2015-09-05
Welcome to Ubuntu!

The apparently missing driver for your device is called *e1000e*. Please open a terminal Ctrl+Alt+t and let's load the driver:```
sudo modprobe e1000e
```Either your ethernet will spring to life, or an error or other clue will appear at the terminal.

Did it spring to life? If there an ethernet interface listed now, ideally eth0?```
ifconfig
```Does the Network Manager icon now show a connection or at least one available? [https://myn900.files.wordpress.com/2010/08/1.png](https://myn900.files.wordpress.com/2010/08/1.png)

If not, are there any informative messages in the log?```
dmesg | grep -e eth -e e1000e
```
