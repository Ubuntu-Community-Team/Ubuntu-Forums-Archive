### Post by ihristov on 2007-08-17
Yes you can use a PC as a router, however you cannot do what you are suggesting.
You still have only 1 network adapter in your PC. To do routing you need to have at least 2 network interfaces.

However in your case all you need is a hub/switch. Just go to the store and buy one. If you can't shell $30 get it from e-bay or look for some hub/switch/wireless router with intergrated switch that is on sale

If you have a buget of $0, you could possibly install a second network card in the PC and setup routing, but it will be much more complicated and unnecessary.
