### Post by PaulFr on 2007-06-16
Try to run **ping [www.yahoo.com](www.yahoo.com)** or other sites you are interested in. Another possibility is your DNS (a service resolving [www.yahoo.com](www.yahoo.com) to an actual IP address like 209.131.36.158) settings are not okay. Check this with **dig [www.google.com](www.google.com)** or similar. Any error messages?
