### Post by SeijiSensei on 2016-05-11
Do you have a local nameserver that is authoritative for mydomain.tld?  You don't seem to be using it if you do.  Without a local DNS server, you'll always get the public records from Google's servers that you list.

I have a server in my home office that is authoritative for my domain with local addresses for certain hosts and MX.  Out on the Internet I have a pair of public servers that resolve the same names to public addresses.
