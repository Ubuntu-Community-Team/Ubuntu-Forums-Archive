### Post by pwabrahams on 2017-11-25
I had been having problems with my Internet connection frequently dropping out and then recovering automatically.  I think I've found the cause: having both a wired and a wireless connection active at the same time.  I had set it up that way on the reasoning that if one connection is good, two are better.  Not so!  The remedy is just to deactivate one of them, probably the wireless. 

I just discovered that the problem can occur in another way: a Bluetooth connection to a nearby phone!
