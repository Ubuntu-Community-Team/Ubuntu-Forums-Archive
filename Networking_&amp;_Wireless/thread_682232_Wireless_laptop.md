### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-01-30
> **jmdlcar said:**
> I'm going to buy a new laptop dose Ubuntu 7.10 support any internet wireless? If so can someone give me a list and which model dose.

Unfortunately, hardware manufacturers tend to change their components and model line-up frequently.  So it is impossible to know whether 2 apparently identical models will have the same compatibility.

As a general rule - Atheros and Intel wifi cards are good.  Broadcom less good.  Make sure you avoid some of the new budget laptops that seem to have a built-in internal USB (yes - USB!) wifi card.  Mini-PCI cards can all generally be made to work somehow (even Broadcom).

If you have a laptop in mind - repost and ask if anyone has bought it recently.  Or use google - that's often the best solution!
