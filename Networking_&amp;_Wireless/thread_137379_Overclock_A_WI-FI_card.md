### Post by linksysfan01 on 2008-05-31
ok well i know this is an old post you probably have figured it out by now but if not and for others out there that would like to know there are a few ways you can boost your wireless signal. one is to take a high gain antenna usually costs around 40 dollars or so.  wrap the antenna with an ethernet cable about 20 turns or so and tape it so it stays on the antenna then plug the ethernet cable into your ethernet card. then check your connection. another way is to wrap a cellphone the same way and do the same thing now i haven't tried this method yet but i have heard it works pretty well. another way that is proven to work is to take metal bowl cut a hole in the bottom and stick your antenna through making a sort of dish a wifi dish to be exact though this will give you longer range i wouldn't suggest making one too big you may end up violating fcc rules. this is for use for extending your own wireless connection do not use this to connect into other networks.
