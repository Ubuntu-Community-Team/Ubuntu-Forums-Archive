### Post by beermotor on 2011-05-16
> **kmrs75 said:**
> mine didnt work either for a while

What you have to do is plug it into your machine, then look for the USB connection notification in the top status bar on your phone. Pull that down, then tap it. Tell it you want to connect USB Mass Storage. Then you're done and can xfer files just like normal. You don't want to use the Motoblur portal or whatever it is, I think that's PC/Win only.
