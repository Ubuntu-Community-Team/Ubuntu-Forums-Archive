### Post by t0mppa on 2009-06-30
Issues with networking, especially with wireless, have grown fairly common lately. Usually it's just a matter of installing the right drivers for your interface though, for which there are plenty of tutorials around and/or help can be found from this forum.

To answer your question, if you can't connect to Internet from the Live boot, you'll probably end up having to fix networking issues after installing. Mostly that is not a big deal though, as you can just do a Google search or start a thread here and will get just as detailed help as you require.

If you don't feel that you're up for major troubleshooting, you can give a bit better description of your particular issue here and get feedback that'll help you identify the problem you're facing and how big an issue fixing it will be.
