The other (eth1) through a small Planet hub to a couple of PC's running XP. I'm running DHCP on the internal network, and the firewall through Firestarter. The server connects to the internet, no problem there, but I cannot get the PC's to connect to the network (i.e. no internet or computers present).

_dhcpd.conf _
# DHCP configuration generated by Firestarter

	option subnet-mask 255.255.255.0;

	default-lease-time 21600;
	max-lease-time 43200;
