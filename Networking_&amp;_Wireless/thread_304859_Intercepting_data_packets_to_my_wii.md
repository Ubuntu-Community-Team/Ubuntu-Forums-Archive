### Post by ssalman on 2006-11-22
If you want to intercept the data for your own fun... you can link your wii box to your Linux box (which is connected to the internet) and share the internet connection on your Linux box... use [wireshark ]("http://www.wireshark.org/")which will capture the data that passes through the Linux box and analyze it for you :p

now putting the data to some useful use, is a totally different story!:rolleyes:
