### Post by chili555 on 2008-01-22
This should be one of the easiest to get going. It works perfectly and simply for me. Let's take a look at what you have in place now. Please let us see the output of the following terminal commands:```
ifconfig
iwconfig
```Assuming your wireless interface is eth1, let's also see:```
sudo iwlist eth1 scan
```Is there any encryption on your router? WPA? WEP?
