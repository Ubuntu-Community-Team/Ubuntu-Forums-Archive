### Post by az on 2006-09-12
> **Audimage said:**
>  I have also heard that some serial modems are in fact software based and DO require drivers to work. Is this true, and how would I know if the modem I am buying is hardware or software based. Any suggestions for modems to buy are appreciated. (I have a small budget)

The only external modems that are software modems are usb-connected, AFAIK.  All modems that connect through a serial port are hardware modems, since you cannot use a software modem through a serial port - that would not work.

You may be able to pick up an intel, smartlink or pctel software modem and get it to work with Ubuntu by compiling the driver from source.  But it would be far easier to just buy a hardware (serial, external) modem.
