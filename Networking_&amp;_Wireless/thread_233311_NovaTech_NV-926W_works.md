### Post by Dirkomatic on 2006-08-09
I just wanted to let eveyone know...  This is a pretty inexpensive wireless PCI card which works.

I struggled for quite a while getting a Belkin F5D7001 to work and never could get it connected.  I looked on newegg and found the NovaTech Nova Tech NV-926W, shipped for $22.  I plugged it in and Dapper detected it without a problem as an RT2500 card.  I entered my ESSID + WEP key, rebooted and my pc was connected.

I haven't had the opportunity to test range or speed yet.  That will come tomorrow.  I'm just happy I can move my router to where I need it instead of sitting on the desk next to my PC...
