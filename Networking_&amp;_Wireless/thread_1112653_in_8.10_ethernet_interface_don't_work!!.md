### Post by coutts99 on 2009-04-01
If ifconfig reports your MTU as 64 then remove the option 'interface-mtu' in /etc/dhcp3/dhclient.conf

This should fix the problem.

More info here - [http://propellerheadadmin.com/tutori...tu-810-install](http://propellerheadadmin.com/tutori...tu-810-install)

This seems to be cropping up a lot.
