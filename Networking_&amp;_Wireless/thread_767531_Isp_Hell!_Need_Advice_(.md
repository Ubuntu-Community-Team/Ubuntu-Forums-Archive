### Post by tamman2000 on 2008-04-26
Sorry, I don't have any advice on which ISP to use...

But, before you decide that you need the faster rated service, there are some things you should check up on.

1.  Are you getting 10mbps continuously, or does it peak at 10, but spend most of it's time at a lower speed?

2.  What is your upload speed?  It is possible (but I don't think it is likely) that your commands getting to work could be the slowest part...

3.  How are you connecting to the remote machine?  There are several different ways to connect, with large variation in CPU requirements and bandwidth requirements...

Sorry I couldn't be more help, but maybe this will get your post looked at by someone who can be more helpful than I...
