### Post by PaulFr on 2008-01-30
Please see **[http://www.mozilla.com/en-US/firefox/phishing-protection/](http://www.mozilla.com/en-US/firefox/phishing-protection/)**

Go to Edit -> Preferences -> Security -> "Tell me if the site ... forgery"

If you turn this off, I think you will not have the traffic, AND you will not have the protection !!
