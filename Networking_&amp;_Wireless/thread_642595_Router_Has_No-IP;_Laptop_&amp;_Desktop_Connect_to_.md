### Post by lintoon on 2007-12-17
Have you got the cable modem plugged into the correct port on the router.  Plug your cable modem into the port named "Internet" (I think thats what its called on your router).  You have 4 ethernet ports for your computers and one dedicated port for the cable modem.

Turn off your router and cable modem.  Unplug the ethernet cable between the router and modem.  Plug the cable back in and just make sure it has clicked into place nice and firm.  Turn your modem on, wait for the lights to settle down (a couple of minutes) then turn your router back on.   

If that doesn't work try another cable in between the router and cable modem.

Does it work if you  bypass your router by plugging your pc/laptop directly into the cable modem with the ethernet cable?
