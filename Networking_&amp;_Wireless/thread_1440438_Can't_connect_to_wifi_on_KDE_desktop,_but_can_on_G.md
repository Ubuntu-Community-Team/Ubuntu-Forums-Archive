### Post by MickS on 2010-12-12
There is something wrong with knetwork manager, least there was on mine. The fix I found was to remove knetwork manager with synaptic then add nm-applet to my start up scripts in Kde, after a reboot it works fine with Gnome and KDE. There is more info on [http://kubuntuforums.net/](http://kubuntuforums.net/)
