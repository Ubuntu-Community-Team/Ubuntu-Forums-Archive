### Post by jackyboy633 on 2012-08-06
Hi,

Unfortunately, I don't own this model of dongle, so I cannot help you get it working in Ubuntu. If you've tried most things, TBH there may be only a very small chance of getting it working. There are some other options to consider though:

There is a list of compatible dongles on the Ubuntu wiki: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NetworkManager/Hardware/3G](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NetworkManager/Hardware/3G).

If the person you are trying to get back online has a wi-fi connection on his PC or laptop, then Mi-Fi would be an option to consider. What this essentially is is a very low power wireless router, and as long as this person has wi-fi hardware that works under Ubuntu, then he should be covered. Here is a link to T-Mobile Mi-Fi options: [http://www.t-mobile.co.uk/shop/mobile-broadband/wireless-pointer/](http://www.t-mobile.co.uk/shop/mobile-broadband/wireless-pointer/) There is also eBay as well: [http://www.ebay.co.uk/sch/i.html?_sacat=0&_nkw=mifi](http://www.ebay.co.uk/sch/i.html?_sacat=0&_nkw=mifi)

Hope this helps :D
