### Post by Bartender on 2007-11-19
fire-
I'm amazed you got to the internet with dial-up at all from the LiveCD.  I couldn't do it.

I agree with other posters, you've gotta install to a HDD to really test your dial-up connection.

And Firefox will seem awfully slow the first few times you visit web pages.  Come back to them several times so that some of the graphics can be stored to internet temporary files.

Find yourself a dial-up speed test that runs from a server that's not too distant from your location.  I had to hunt around a little bit to find an ISP that hosts a "dialup speed test" that runs from a server right here in Washington State.  That's always a good test.

Another thing that doesn't really make much sense to me but have seen it too often to ignore - older PC's will give you a much slower dial-up internet experience than newer ones.  There's not much data being processed so you'd think it wouldn't matter but it does.
