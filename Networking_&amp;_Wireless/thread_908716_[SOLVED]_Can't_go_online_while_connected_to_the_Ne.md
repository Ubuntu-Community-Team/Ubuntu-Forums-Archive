### Post by HarrisonBP on 2008-09-02
I had a somewhat similar problem, and mine had an easy fix.  You may want to try different DNS server.  Go to openDNS.com to get addresses.  

Then change the DNS addresses - System> Administration> Network (unlock with login pass)> DNS tab.

This may be a long shot, but it is easy, and easy to revert.
