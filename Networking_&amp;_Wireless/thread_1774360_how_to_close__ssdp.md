### Post by Doug S on 2011-06-04
The SSDP (Simple Service Discovery Protocol) packets are most likely from some device on your local network. For example on my system the adsl modem spews these packets as does a linksys wireless router (configured as a switch).
See also: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simple_Service_Discovery_Protocol](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simple_Service_Discovery_Protocol)
It is not an attack.
I do not know a way to turn it off. (on my linksys router I even thought I had found a setting to not spew these packets, but it does anyhow).
