### Post by pricetech on 2010-05-21
I'm not going to be much help since all my printers both at work and home are network printers that I connect to directly, but I suspect it's related to security settings in win7.  I've only dabbled with win7 so I don't know my way around it like I do XP, but check your firewall and your network settings.  It seems a recall a lot of "paranoia" on the part of win7 when it comes to sharing across the network.

You might also double check and make sure that your Ubuntu box can see itself as a windows share.  In other words, make sure you can see it and connect to its shares as if those shares were on another box.  It might just be a client thing on the Ubuntu box, though I doubt it.
