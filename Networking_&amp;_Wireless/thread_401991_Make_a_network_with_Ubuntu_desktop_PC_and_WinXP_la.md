I have two good links for you

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InternetConnectionSharing](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InternetConnectionSharing) in the wiki
and 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=111972](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=111972) in the forum.

The main downside is that you must have the desktop running while the laptop wants to connect. While it's cheaper initially, in the long term a switch will save you electricity costs as they use significantly less power, it's also far more convenient and you won't need a crossover cable.
