### Post by tturrisi on 2007-06-05
FYI, just because the wireless device is 54 Mb/sec (80211g) or 108 Mb/sec (80211n) does not mean that those are the throughput rates you will get!  The max you can expect from g wifi is about 34 Mb/sec, and a similar ratio applies to 80211n devices.  This is due to overhead.  See:
[http://www.hp.com/united-states/wireless/hsm_download.html](http://www.hp.com/united-states/wireless/hsm_download.html)

Now...what card are you using?  And what driver for that card?
