### Post by kevdog on 2008-01-01
First of all you are not going to brick your router with a firmware upgrade.  I flashed with Linksys router with the ddwrt firmware and regularly upgrade the firmware as new releases come out.  Even if you brick your router its possible to recover it in most cases.

The most important question is what is contained in the new firmware.  If it fixes security holes, then it upgrade is worth while (or adds wpa for example).  If the new features are cosmetic or features you are not interested in, then dont upgrade.

Just use some common sense and spend some time reading about the new firmware.  Dont just make a judgement based on "it might brick my router -- Im scared -- I wont do it!"
