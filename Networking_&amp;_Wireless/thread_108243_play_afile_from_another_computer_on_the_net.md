### Post by FLeiXiuS on 2005-12-25
You may want to open a terminal, type xmms.

This'll bring up the XMMS media player.

Next, I'd click on ADD at the lower left hand corner and select the file.

Be-aware, I believe you have to have the location where the file is stored mounted if it's over the network.
