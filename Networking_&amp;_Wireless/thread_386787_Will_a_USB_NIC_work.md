### Post by gradedcheese on 2007-03-17
Yes!  I use Linksys USB NICs (same chipset was used in an older Netgear one) for testing some stuff.  They are supported by the asix driver, which comes with the kernel. 

The Linksys version is [USB200M]("http://www.linksys.com/servlet/Satellite?c=L_Product_C2&childpagename=US%2FLayout&cid=1115416834080&pagename=Linksys%2FCommon%2FVisitorWrapper&lid=3408040941B03")
