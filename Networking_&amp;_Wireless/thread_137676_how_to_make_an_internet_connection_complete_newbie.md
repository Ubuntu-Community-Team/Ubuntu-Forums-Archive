### Post by ssam on 2006-02-28
with most adsl ethernet modems you should only need to put in the username and password when you set it up. then the modem connect to your isp. the modem also provides a local network which you computer plugs into via ethernet. and dhcp tell your computer that the modem is acting as a bridge to the internet.

you usually configure the modem by going to 192.168.0.1 or 192.168.1.1 in you webbrowser (firefox)
