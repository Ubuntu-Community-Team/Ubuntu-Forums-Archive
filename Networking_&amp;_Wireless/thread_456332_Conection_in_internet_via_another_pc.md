### Post by spd106 on 2007-05-28
There are three main ways to achieve this. Two of them are very similar and are covered on this wiki page [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InternetConnectionSharing](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InternetConnectionSharing), I suggest you go with installing firestarter first, if that doesn't work then try the other way. There is also a thread on this forum with a few scripts that could help you out [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=111972](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=111972)

The third way involves bridging the wireless and wired interfaces and is rather more complex to set-up and manage. I don't recommend this route unless you really have to.
