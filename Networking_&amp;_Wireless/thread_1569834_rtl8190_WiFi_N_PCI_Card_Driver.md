### Post by barnaclekarl on 2010-09-07
Seeing the frustration many seem to experience with WiFi drivers (including myself), I thought it important to post the results of my effort. I tried all of the publicly available drivers I could find. Nothing worked. A simple email to Realtek support via their website fixed the problem. I sent the Version, kernel, and card information. They promptly replied with the latest driver, which installed and worked flawlessly.
[http://www.realtek.com.tw/contact/contentView.aspx?Langid=1&PNid=1&PFid=4&Level=2](http://www.realtek.com.tw/contact/contentView.aspx?Langid=1&PNid=1&PFid=4&Level=2)
Thanks Realtek.

Configuration:
Wireless N PCIe 8190 Card from Realtek
Ubuntu 10.04 on a big ole Dell Desktop

Cheers,
Karl
