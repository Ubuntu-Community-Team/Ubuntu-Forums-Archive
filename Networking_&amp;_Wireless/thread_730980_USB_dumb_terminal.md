### Post by Royall on 2008-03-21
Last night before I went to bed, I decided to make my computer hibernate. When I got up this morning and turned my computer back on, it appeared to boot normally but I wasn't getting anything on my monitor. I used my wife's laptop to SSH in and everything looked fine, so ordered it to reboot and it came back up like normal.

Occasional issues like that make me think it would be convenient to have a small dumb terminal tucked away somewhere.
This device would be like a laptop, but much lighter in terms of hardware. Maybe it would have a dot matrix LCD and a cheap keyboard and be powered by AA batteries. It would plug in to the USB port and present me with a login prompt. Doesn't that sound handy? Does such a thing exist?
