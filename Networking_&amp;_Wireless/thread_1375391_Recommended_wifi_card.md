### Post by H3LlIoN on 2010-01-08
I bought an Intel 4965AGN off of Ebay.  It was $11.99 with free shipping, and it has wireless N dual band support.  Fully functional right out of the box with Karmic.  only concern is some issues with accessing N via secure connection, but haven't really taken time to explore further, as non-secure is non-issue.  Card is mini-pci though.  Have to crack case to install, but awesome b/c you don't have cumbersome card hanging out of computer.  Also, direct replacement for earlier intel cards.  (I was replacing a 3965)  

There are some newer intel cards out there, but I can't personally speak to them.
