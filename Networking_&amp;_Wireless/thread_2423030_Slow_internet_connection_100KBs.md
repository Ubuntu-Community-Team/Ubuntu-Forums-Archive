### Post by sdsurfer on 2019-09-03
I know this is late, but just addressing this issue myself. The first question is: what is the speed of your Ethernet card?

I have a slightly older home built with 100mbps Ethernet, we hardwire everything so when we go onto a 350 mbps Internet service lo and behold, obviously I can't get more than 100 mbps.

I know this seems Captain Obvious but I really hadn't given it much thought until I really looked at it and did a <facepalm>. :-D Solution in my case is a $12 1000 mbps Ethernet card and disable the onboard Ethernet.
