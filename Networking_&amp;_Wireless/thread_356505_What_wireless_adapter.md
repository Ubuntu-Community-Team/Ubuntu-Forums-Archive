### Post by drummer on 2007-02-09
I have a D-Link DWL-G510 (PCI, Atheros chipset) works pretty darn well, just have to install the restricted kernel modules package. Do your research and make sure you get a card with a well supported chipset, that's the important part. Atheros and Prism are good, there are others but I don't know them off the top of my head.
