### Post by atomicben on 2011-05-18
Don't look at the brand names you gotta find out what chipset is in them.

Broadcom, Atheros, Ralink and Realtek are chipsets that work quite well Plug n' Play with linux using ***native*** drivers (meaning NO NDISWRAPPER!). 

Since you are talking about a USB plug n play wifi device; the Alfa AWUS036H comes highly recommended (especially by the folks using aircrack).  It has a full watt (1000mw) of transmit power which is many times the power of most wifi devices.
