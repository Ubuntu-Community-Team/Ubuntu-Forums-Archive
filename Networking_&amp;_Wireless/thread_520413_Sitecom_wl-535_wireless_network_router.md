### Post by sweetcancer on 2007-08-08
problem solved, i dragged the computer to my room, connected it to the internet via my internet cable, downloaded the windows drivers for the wl-535 device from sitecoms web site and installed them through ndiswrapper. After that the connection still wasn't working so i installed the wireless assistant and connected to internet with that and now it works perfectly.
