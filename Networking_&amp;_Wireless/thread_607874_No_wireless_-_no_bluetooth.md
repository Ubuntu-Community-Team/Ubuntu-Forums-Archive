### Post by steveneddy on 2007-11-09
This morning while working at my desk, I suddenly lost not only the wireless connection to our LAN, but I noticed that the Bluetooth mouse and the Bluetooth manager both went away at the same time.

I searched for everything, doing **lsusb** and** lspci** to no avail.

I was about to take my laptop apart when, while the laptop was upside down, I noticed that the wireless switch was turned off. 

I booted back up, flipped the switch and presto! everything works wonders.

I should remember to tell myself what I tell everyone else, check the most simple solution first.

Let this be a lesson to me.
