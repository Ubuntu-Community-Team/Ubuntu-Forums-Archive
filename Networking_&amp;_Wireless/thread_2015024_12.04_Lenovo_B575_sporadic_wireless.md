### Post by nkorth on 2012-07-02
Okay, this is really weird. I have a Lenovo B575 with a Ralink RT3090 internal wireless card. Wireless actually worked for a little while on the live cd, but now that I've installed it doesn't work at all. It claims that the wireless switch is off (rfkill always says it's hard-blocked, networkmanager only sometimes gives a reason for "wireless off") Of course, the switch is on... I have found many posts about this wireless card, most of which end in inexplicable sudden solutions or fixes which don't work for me. I have already blacklisted acer_wmi, which is the most common recommendation, but it did nothing. I tried some ways of "resetting" rfkill and networkmanager, which also did nothing. Additionally, I tried the "nohwcrypt" setting, which did nothing.
