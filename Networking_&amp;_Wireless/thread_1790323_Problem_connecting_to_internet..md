### Post by JDWwayt on 2011-06-24
Update: Problem solved, There was a problem with the ethernet port on the back of my computer.  I plugged in a USB to Ethernet adapter and now the Internet works just fine. =P 

I would just delete the thread but maybe it will help someone who has the same problem.
