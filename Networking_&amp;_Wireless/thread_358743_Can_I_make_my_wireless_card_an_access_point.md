### Post by capdog on 2007-02-21
This is possible, but the hotel would get suspicious if there was a LAN cable going out my room and across the corridor to my friend's room! :)

We sorted it out - bought a USB wireless adapter for his computer, and enabled internet connection sharing in windows. Then my laptop connects Ad-Hoc to the USB wireless, and his onboard wireless connects to the internet!
