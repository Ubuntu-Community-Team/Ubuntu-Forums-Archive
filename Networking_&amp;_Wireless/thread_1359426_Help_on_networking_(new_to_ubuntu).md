### Post by Puck7 on 2009-12-19
[Here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=250203") is how you can change the name of your computer. [This]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=1310955&postcount=3") may also help.

To make your computer visible on the network, you need to install samba. Here is a guide to install samba. Make sure all of the computers on your network are in the same workgroup. Ubuntu's default workgroup is "WORKGROUP". Also [this post]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=8525356&postcount=2") can help you too.

All of the commands in the linked posts are to be used in the terminal.
