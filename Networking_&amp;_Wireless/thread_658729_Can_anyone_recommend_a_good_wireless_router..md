### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-01-05
Have to say, my view is get whichever is cheapest!  I have owned Toshiba, Linksys and Dynamode wifi routers (ADSL 4-port wifi), and they have all been pretty much the same.  Any new router should have firewall, DHCP, WPA2, port-forwarding, 802.11b/g etc.  I can't think what else you need.  I would recommend getting one with 4 ethernet ports (in case things change - and there is no cost difference).
