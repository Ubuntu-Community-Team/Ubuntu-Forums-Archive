### Post by t0mppa on 2009-10-27
> **MyCherries said:**
> Is there a way to connect my desktop that has the live cd (soon probably installed side by side with windows) to my laptop, and receive internet, via the laptop's WiFi, from just a ethernet cable?

Sure there is, but you need to do some configuring on your laptop first. The easiest way would probably be setting up bridging and the more elegant, time consuming & educating way would be to configure some iptable (firewall) rules. Never done either one personally, since there hasn't been a situation that required them, but Google suggests a lot of easy looking tutorials for it, like [this one]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Router") for instance.
