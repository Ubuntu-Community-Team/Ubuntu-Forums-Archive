### Post by chunchengch on 2008-04-01
MoBlockGUI is a small frontend program of MoBlock, it is created by Mr. Paul Bourke and you can find the MoBlock-GUI.tar.gz archive on [http://www.gnomefiles.org/app.php/MoBlockGUI](http://www.gnomefiles.org/app.php/MoBlockGUI) .

I covert MoBlock-GUI.tar.gz to a deb package, so it will be more convenient to use in Ubuntu/Debian system, after installation, you can find MoBlockGUI in Application > Internet, hope you enjoy it!
