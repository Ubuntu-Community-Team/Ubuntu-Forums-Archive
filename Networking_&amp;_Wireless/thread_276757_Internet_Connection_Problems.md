### Post by Pengwyn44 on 2006-10-13
I have tried a "cure" listed by one post and it works! For those using Ubunto 6.06 and connecting to the Internet via broadband and a router/modem, Ubunto does not connect. The answer is to start Firefox and type in the URL box the words "about:config" and click on GO. A listing comes up which is incomprehensible to most people. Simply tab down the list until you find the line "network:dns.disableIPv6". Highlight the line and press the righthand button on your mouse, then click on "toggle". This will change the entry from FALSE to TRUE. Close Firefox and re-start it and enter any URL address,click on GO and you will be on the Internet.
Before doing the above I checked my router set up to ensure that it was Connected.:
