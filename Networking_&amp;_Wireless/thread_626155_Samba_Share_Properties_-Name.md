### Post by jetsam on 2007-11-28
It's the name of the share that will appear in the network places browser in xp.  Any short, possibly descriptive name is fine.  The convention seems to be one word, no whitespace, and lowercase:

music
videos
taxes
accounting

Whatever is useful to you and helps you identify it while browsing the network.
