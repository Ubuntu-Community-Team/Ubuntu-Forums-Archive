### Post by JeQhdMD on 2014-11-12
See this thread . . . read closely.   Especially post #4 for the code to copy and paste into the Terminal, and then the last post to make it permanent.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2194306](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2194306)

To bring up the Terminal . . . . ctrl+alt+t.        To paste text into a terminal, you can do a ctrl-shift+v or "edit>paste" from the menu.   Be sure to copy the code versus just typing.

Remember, Google search is your friend (even better than the forum search tool).    

Another easy way for laptop or desktop wifi is to get a known "plug & play" usb wifi adapter.   

TP-Link TL-722N or Panda Ultra wifi a/g/n.   Both should work great and give you two wireless connections to choose from (after getting your installed device setup)
