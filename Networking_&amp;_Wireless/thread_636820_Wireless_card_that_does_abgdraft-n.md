### Post by Junglizer on 2007-12-10
I would recommend an Atheros chip based card, as these work fluidly with all forms of *nix (though Open's ath driver can be a *******). Find an atheros based draft-n card that is dual-band, as 802.11n is an overlay and improvement of existing PHYs (well, more then that or it would just be TGn). I think you may find it a bit challenging to find a good dual-band draft-n card. Though I've not looked myself. 

What do you need a dual-draft-n card for exactly? 
If you are looking for the range, as well as both channels I would recommend a higher power dual-channel card. If you don't mind spending a bit of money (around $130) I highly recommend the [Ubiquiti SRC]("http://www.ubnt.com/products_src.php4") 300mW with MMCX external ports. Works 100% with Madwifi-ng drivers as near as I can tell, I have one of these cards and they are simply amazing.
