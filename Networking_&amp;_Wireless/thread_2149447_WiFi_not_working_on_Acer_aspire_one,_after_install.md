### Post by GregA on 2013-05-28
Many other people have said they also have difficulty with linux wifi on your model of Acer netbook - - but the good news is you can get a very inexpensive "usb wireless adapter", that plugs into a USB 2 port, and will give you instant wireless connection in any of the Ubuntu OS's, after version 12.04 (precise pangolin).

Here are two models you can get from Amazon:   ***Panda Ultra Wifi (b/g/n) 150Mbps Wireless-N 2.4GHz USB Adapter*** or ***Airlink AWLL5099 mini-usb adapter***

They are both "Plug and Play" - - just plug in the usb port, and either one will scan and see the local wireless networks.   

The first time you connect, you have to set up your wireless connection by adding your wireless network name , and then add your password.  Click the box that says "connect automatically", and you should be in business.   

The cost for each is between $10.00 and $12.00, but you may also find this model at a European retailer closer to you for lower shipping rates.  

Good Luck!
