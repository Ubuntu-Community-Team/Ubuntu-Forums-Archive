### Post by alowe on 2008-04-16
It should just work, as long as you do not use any encryption or security (such as WPA).

These adapters don't seem to work with any security (other then mac address filtering).  I have been using one on and off for a couple of years, and found this to be the case, just installed 8.04 and found I could not connect to my access point unless I turned off all security - I will not try to re-enable security, as I am not that worried and I am doing mac-address filtering.
