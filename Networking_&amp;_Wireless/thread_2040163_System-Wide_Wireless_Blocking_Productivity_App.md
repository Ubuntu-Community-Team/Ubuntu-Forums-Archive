As far as I know, for Ubuntu users, there are 2 softwares quite similar which can do that : 

- Selfcontrol : [http://svn.jklmnop.net/projects/SelfControl.html](http://svn.jklmnop.net/projects/SelfControl.html)
- Selfrestraint : [http://parker.kuivi.la/projects/selfrestraint](http://parker.kuivi.la/projects/selfrestraint)

They work fine.

Improvements can be made however I think, using a whitelist system (everything blocked except a list of websites, such as gmail and such) and proposing a schedule for blocking the internet (block from 9am to 12 am and to 2 pm to 6 pm for example).

But these are open softwares, the guys don't make money with it and I am not sure they have free time to improve their softwares, which works already well.
