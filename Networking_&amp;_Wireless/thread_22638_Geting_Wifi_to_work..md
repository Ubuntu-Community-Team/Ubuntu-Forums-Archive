### Post by Strider on 2005-03-28
If the system detects the wireless card, go to System->Administration->Networking.

You should see a list of networking devices - Wireless, LAN, and any others you may have.  Most likely the wireless is grayed out and not active.  You then go to Properties, configure the WLAN settings and then save/apply.  Once at the initial screen listing the adapters, select the Wireless connection and click on Activate.

Hope that helps.
