### Post by irrdev on 2008-09-05
phpBB is definitely the best choice. I'd say that it may be even more popular than vBulletin. You should check out both phpBB2 and phpBB3, as both are fully supported and offer different strengths. phpBB2 is very fast, with hundreds of available mods and themes. phpBB3 is relatively new, and sacrifices speed for a fancier interface. Unless you have a good webserver, I find that phpBB3 can be overkill, so definitely compare the two versions carefully before making a choice.

Also remember this: you can easily upgrade from phpBB2 to phpBB3, but not the other way around! At the same time, phpBB3 doesn't have nearly as many community-made mods and themes yet, as it is relatively new. If you are unsure, I would recommend going with phpBB2 first, with the option of upgrading to phpBB3 later. :wink:
