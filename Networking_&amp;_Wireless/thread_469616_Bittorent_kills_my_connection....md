### Post by Gausian on 2007-06-10
usb dongles are bad for bt.  for some reason when the # of connections gets really high they just stop working.  ive seen this problem may times with them.

one solution that seems to work is to limit the # of connections to a relatively low value, like say 50.  if it still dies, keep lowering until you can keep a decent connection.
