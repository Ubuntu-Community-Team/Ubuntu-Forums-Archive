### Post by Hobgoblin on 2009-01-26
Relatively simple way - tell him to cut down his usage or pay for an unlimited internet package for both of you to use.  If he doesn't like that than cut off his internet access.

For software to do what you want it would have to be able to intercept his internet traffic.  Which would mean running it on his PC or getting his internet traffic to run via your PC.  Either way would probably be far too easy for him to get around.

In short it falls into the "would be tricky" category.
