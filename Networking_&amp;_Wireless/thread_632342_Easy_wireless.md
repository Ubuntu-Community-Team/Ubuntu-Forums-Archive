### Post by dmackeyd on 2007-12-05
Based on posts here, we are seeing more wireless device support. Most discussion involves drivers. An easy solution is NOT to need drivers at all. 
My initial problems was a way to connect a RePlayTV DVR to either a phone jack (toll call here) or the network. Neither connections were in the TV room and  cannot drivers be installed to the DVR, but an IP address can be assigned to the LAN port.
The D-Link DWL-G730AP (about $50) provided the solution. It does not need drivers and is setup through a browser just as is a router. A small device, connect to LAN port; power supplied either through power cord or USB port (both cords supplied).
Works great with any OS.
