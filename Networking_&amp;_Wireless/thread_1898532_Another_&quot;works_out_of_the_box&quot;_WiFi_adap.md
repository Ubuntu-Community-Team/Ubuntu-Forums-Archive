Mods, you may move this as you see fit.  I'm posting in networking & wireless because one of the most common questions asked seems to be "What wireless adapter can I get that will just work?"  I've advocated two, TrendNet TEW-649UB (rtl8192SU chipset) and NetGear WNA1100 (Atheros 9271(?) chipset.  I just received and can add another 'just works' adapter. **DLink DWA-131**.  It appears to be a close relative of the TrendNet-TEW639UB and the same size, it protrudes 2-2.5 cm. from the USB socket.  It's plug & play Ubuntu 11.04 and later.  Earlier versions require a firmware download.  Speed is N150.
