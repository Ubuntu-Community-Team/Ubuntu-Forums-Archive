### Post by reptilezone2002 on 2012-11-01
first u need a router or a switch, 2 network interfaces (2 Lan cards or 1 LAN card 1 USB 3G mobile broadband modem)or a cross over patch cable(if u r connecting PC to PC directly)

1 Find NETWORK CONNECTIONS from unity or gnome classic System > Preferences > Network Connections Or just click ur network icon on the task bar & select edit connections 
[IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=226511&stc=1&d=1351762597[/IMG]

2 From that select wired connection or Wireless Interface (if u r sharing wireless connection) & edit
3 Select IPv4 Settings 
4 under METHOD Select Shared to Other Computers then Click Save 
[IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=226512&stc=1&d=1351762597[/IMG]
[IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=226513&stc=1&d=1351762597[/IMG]
