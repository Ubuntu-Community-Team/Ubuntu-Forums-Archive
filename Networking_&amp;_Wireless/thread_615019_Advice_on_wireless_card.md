### Post by rustybronco on 2007-11-17
Your best bet is to find what cards are available to you, then find out the chipset each uses and research it on this board.

example.  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=3749763&postcount=5](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=3749763&postcount=5)
lspci will give you the chipset of a wireless card in your system.
also you can do an internet search for the wireless device+chipset

also check here... [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=370108](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=370108)
and here [http://ubuntuhcl.org/pub/categories.php](http://ubuntuhcl.org/pub/categories.php)

the reason I say use a pci card is because there appears to be more trouble getting some usb wireles devices working.
