### Post by ian-weisser on 2013-10-05
Efficiency depends upon your evaluation criteria.

For example: If either method results in a faster connection than your upstream ISP connection speed, then both are equally efficient.

Instead of efficiency, I recommend the method you are most comfortable with.
If you are renting your space, I recommend the method that is easiest to repair.
