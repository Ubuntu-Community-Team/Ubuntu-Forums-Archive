### Post by Steve1961 on 2007-08-31
It's not all bad, at least you do have an interface called ath0, which suggests that your wireless card has been recognised.  Why not try setting it manually without encryption to check that it works.  Go into system>admin>network, highlight the wireless connection, select properties and configure the system to automatically connect via dhcp.  Make sure you uncheck the enable roaming mode option.  Also disable encryption for the time being on your router.
