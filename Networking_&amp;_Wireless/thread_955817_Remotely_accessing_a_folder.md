### Post by camoanimal on 2008-10-22
First, are you implying that she lives outside of your house?  If this is the case then you are in for a world of trouble trying to setup a remote folder to another computer across the internet.  Plus, this is somewhat risky as it can compromise the files being shared if not setup properly.  The best thing to do is to share you files over a file sharing site, or try to find a site that hosts FTP folders.

Hope this helps
