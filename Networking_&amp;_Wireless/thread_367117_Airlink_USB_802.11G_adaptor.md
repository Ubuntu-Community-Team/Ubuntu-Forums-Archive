### Post by Edache on 2007-02-21
Hello I just wanted to let everyone know that Ive gotten the above usb wireless adaptor to work. Once you get the adaptor setup in Systems-Network- and it shows there and you enable it, download and install  wlassistant. Go to Terminal and type in sudo wlassistant and wait for the pop up window to open, select your wireless network and put in your WEP if needed and watch it connect. Now the light on the Airlink adaptor will not come on, but once wlassistant shows a connection just open your browser up and your good to go. The only draw back to this is that you have to use wlassistant everytime you boot back into the system. Well I hopes this helps someone using the airlink101 USB adaptor.
