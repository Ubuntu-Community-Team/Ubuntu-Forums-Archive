### Post by dca on 2008-03-14
Your computer should be set for dynamic when installing router, in fact do that first and shutdown computer just for posterity.  Next connect router to broadband modem or whatever.  If you haven't done any serious config to it, it may be smart to perform complete reset on it by holding the reset button in w/ paper clip for thirty or so seconds during and after plugging router into power source.  Then power on PC and enter IP address of router to config...
