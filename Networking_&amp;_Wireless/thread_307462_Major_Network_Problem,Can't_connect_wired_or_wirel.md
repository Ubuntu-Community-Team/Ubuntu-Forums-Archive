### Post by NetworkGuy on 2006-11-26
Try shutting down your router, modem and all your equipment.  Leave them off for a minute or two

Start the modem back up.  Wait for it to finish booting up (all the blinking lights stop blinking) :)

Then start the router back up.  Wait for it to finish booting.  Start up your PC (wired).  Make sure it gets an IP address from the router. ```
ifconfig
```  Make sure you can surf the net.

Try your PC wireless now.  Same thing as above.  Make sure you can surf the net.

Try your XBox now.

Hopefully everything is working now..


If not, check your router to make sure it got an IP address from your ISP.  I had a bad modem once that made it look like the router was the problem.
