### Post by 77midget on 2008-12-30
QoS is Quality of Service, which is a method of prioritizing network traffic. Sounds like yours is prioritizing you out!

Shouldn't be a big deal for a home network. This is more important in a small biz or home office where you abso-tutely need to have specific apps prioritized.
