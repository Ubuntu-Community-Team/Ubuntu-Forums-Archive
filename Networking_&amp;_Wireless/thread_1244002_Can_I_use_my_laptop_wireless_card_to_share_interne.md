### Post by mess110 on 2009-08-19
Yes it can be done but additional hardware is needed. the xbox 360 needs some wireless adapter from microsoft and as far as I know its quite expensive.

[http://www.xbox.com/en-US/hardware/x/xbox360wirelessnetadapter/default.htm](http://www.xbox.com/en-US/hardware/x/xbox360wirelessnetadapter/default.htm)
according to this its 99 USD

the wlan card from your laptop WILL make the whole thing work with some minor adjustments but (depending on your Internet connection to some extent) it will probably be a bit too slow.
