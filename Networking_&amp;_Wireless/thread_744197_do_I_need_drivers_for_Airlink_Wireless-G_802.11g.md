### Post by heyho on 2008-04-03
If you find out the chipset in your wireless card, it will be easier to work out what the problem is.

Open up a terminal, and type "lspci", then rummage through the output to find your wireless card - it should give you the chipset used in your card.

Edit:  Surprise - looks like it's a Broadcom.
