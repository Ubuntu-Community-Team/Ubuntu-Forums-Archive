### Post by lisati on 2008-06-12
You might want to set up a dedicated folder somewhere and enable sharing for the whole folder. For folders on your desktop, you can right-click on the folder icon, and then click on "Share this folder" - this should also work with folders you access through the "Places" menu.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=202605](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=202605) has a tutorial on setting up "samba", one way of managing the behind-the-scenes stuff that enables sharing.
