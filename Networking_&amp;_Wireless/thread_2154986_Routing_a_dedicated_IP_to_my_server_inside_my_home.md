### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-06-16
If you want multiple fixed IP's then you need to change to an ISP that will provide them for you.

You will have to pay for a business class service and probably have to justify to them why you need the IP addresses, there is a shortage of IPv4 addresses still available and they don't just hand out chunks of the address space to anyone who asks.
