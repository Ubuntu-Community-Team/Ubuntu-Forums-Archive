### Post by kitsune7 on 2010-12-03
Because of the way routers work using two phones lines would not, as a matter of fact, speed things up. In fact, it may even slow it down depending on how smart your router is.

I don't believe routers support a protocol to split the data being sent along two separate lines in order to speed things up. If the data was sent along two lines, only one line would be processed and the other thrown out.

If you google orange livebox manual you will find some links, one of which will let you download a manual as a PDF . I have just done it. If one of those telephone sockets has a white rectangle above then that is for the Talk service (whatever that is). Otherwise the connection to the telephone line is the same as my broadband modem.
