### Post by gnimsh on 2010-10-10
I have this router.  First log in to 192.168.1.1  The default login is all that is needed unless you have changed it. Something like admin and default or admin and pass or defauld and pass.  It's available online, I don't remember exactly.

It's a pain because it comes preconfigured with autosetup so you can just plug it in and connect (theoretically) however because it has a password that you can't figure out without the CD it is useless. 

Once you log in to the admin you can turn off the auto settings and configure it yourself manually.  This is what I did and it worked perfectly.  Just plug the computer directly into the router first. Make sure you've also plugged the computer into a computer port on the router and NOT to the modem in port.
