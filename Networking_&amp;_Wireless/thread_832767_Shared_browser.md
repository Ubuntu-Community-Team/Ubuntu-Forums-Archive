### Post by maddog39 on 2008-06-18
I have never heard of such functionality. However I have seen (Windows only) systems before which allow webmasters to follow their users around realtime as they browse their website. Maybe what you want to do is go into System > Preferences > Remote Desktop and enabling others to see what you are doing. But you MUST setup a port forward on your router for port 4900 (i think) to your computers LAN IP address for it to work. Then get your internet IP (via something like ipchicken.com) and give that to whoever you want to watch you browse the web. That other person also needs a VNC client to watch. This basically allows you to show whomever you want what your doing on your computer. So it doesnt necissarily pertain JUST to web browsing, but whatever is going on, on your screen.
