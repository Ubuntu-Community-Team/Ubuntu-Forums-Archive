### Post by cobelloy on 2006-10-05
Hi there

I have wireless networking at my place, and all the components were dirt cheap off ebay. I have a netgear router that broadcasts wireless and also has standard ethernet connectors also. I have a netgear WG311v3 pci card which works flawlessly with ndiswrapper in my pc and a netgear WG511v3 pcmcia in my laptop which is also flawless with ndiswrapper. This setup works with all the linuxes I have tried, ubuntu, pclinxos, puppylinux are the main ones I use. and although the setup is a little more complicated with windows obviously they are designed to work with it - so once installed there also work just fine.

I think the two cards and the router all together including postage cost in the vicinity of AU$60 off ebay, pretty cheap if you ask me.
