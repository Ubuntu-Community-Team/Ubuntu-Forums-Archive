There are many types of [**Data Synchronizations**]("http://www.onlinesynchronization.com/db_synchronization.html"), you can Synchronize your data for PDA applications, or mobile applications, or you can convert your one type of Database into another types of Database format. Now days Mobile users also using Internet from Mobile Devices. Here we are talking about when you convert one database format into another database format is known as Data Synchronization. Mobile devices also doing same procedure when you connect to your computer or laptop.

What is Data Synchronization?
What we are talking here means convert your Database into XML format and again convert XML format into your Database format.

Why Data Synchronization?
Generally regular Database takes huge space in your Data Storage. Data Synchronization can help you to synchronize your data into small data file. Companies using various software for various applications because companies having various locations for Distributions or regional offices etc. These locations having different different softwares connected to Head Office. Various Branch Automation Software Solutions helping companies to connect other softwares through Data Synconization.

    * Synchronize Data into XML
    * Fast Transfer of Small XML on web server.
    * Your another office can download XML format and synchronize into your Database of off line software.
    * Make web applications to check your important reports from any location.

Benefits of Data Synchronization

    * Database size become small so that Data files can be transfer (Upload or Download) on your own web server very fast.
    * [Synchronized Data]("http://www.onlinesynchronization.com/db_synchronization.html") (XML format) can useful to make web applications to check your sales, stocks reports from your off line softwares database.
