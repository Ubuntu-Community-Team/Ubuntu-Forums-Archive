### Post by Mr. C. on 2007-03-17
You're confusing dial up modem with your broadband ADSL modem.  Two different creatures.

See here for an explanation of "winmodem" vs. "linmodem"

"http://walbran.org/sean/linux/linmodem-howto-2.html#ss2.1

Dial-up modems either have hardware controllers, or closed, proprietary software-based controllers.  Linmodem is just a name for software that allows a winmodem to function under Linux.

MrC
