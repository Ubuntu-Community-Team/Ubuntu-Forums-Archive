### Post by nhasian on 2008-08-22
i dont think you have to do anything special with ushare.  just make sure that its listening on the appropriate network port ie wlan0 if your using a wireless network adaptor.  Are both wireless adaptors set up to use Ad Hoc or are you using infrastructure mode with a router?  make sure you have good signal strength and the channel your router is set to is different than your neighbors :)

a bridge is only necessary when your connecting multiple network segments.
