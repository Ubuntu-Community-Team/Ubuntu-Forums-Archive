I just installed Ubuntu on my Dad's Gateway Laptop.  Seeing as how my Dad does not like passwords, I have it set to auto login.  However when the system tries to auto connect to his WiFi network it asks him for the keyring password.

This is not the password to the WiFi network. Its the password for the keyring.  It will connect to the WiFi network just fine once you type the password but the goal is for him not have to type a password.

I have a Dell laptop and it auto connects to my WiFi w/o asking for a password.  The only difference between mine and his that mine is not set to auto log in.

### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-07-11
Go to Applications/accesories/passwords and encriptions, click on the password tab and the right click on the the password shown in the box.  Type in your password and leave the other two blank.  Save the hit the change button ane exit.  You will get a message telling you what will happen.  This should take care of your problem.
