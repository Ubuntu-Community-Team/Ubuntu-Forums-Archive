### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2011-12-30
Network manager is the little thing with two arrows (up and down) if you are not connected. Click on it, and select your network. You need to know your WEP key, usually. Alternatively, you can look in "network connections" under the menu (probably "system").

Ideally, you should get French everywhere. But applications are developed in English, so I'm afraid it's not perfect. You have checked the language settings?
