So if I understand correctly the bars show up, and if you left click on them you get a drop down of available networks, and if you click on your network it doesn't hookup. Does it ask you for another keyring which is usually your login password, if you have set this key. You can hit deny in this section and get a signal usually. 

If you right click on the bars and choose new network or something like that you may be able to set up the roam to recognize the wep, and try the deny default keyring first.
