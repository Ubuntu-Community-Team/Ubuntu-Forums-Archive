### Post by drmagoo on 2005-02-15
this card uses the infamous ACX111 chipset from Texas Instruments, a company that needs to be encouraged to support linux ... perhaps with "offers that can't be refused." Until they do support us, you can go to [www.houseofcraig.net/acx100_howto.php](www.houseofcraig.net/acx100_howto.php)  and follow craig's guide and you will be online shortly. Presently WEP and WPA are not implemented but they are coming "Real Soon." Craig's How To is easy to follow and just works. Enjoy. (This entire install is totally open source, reverse engineered by some brilliant folks, with no assistance from TI. It is a great work, not a kludge.)
