### Post by NetworkGuy on 2006-09-01
Usually a cable modem can only be used by one computer.  If you need to connect both computers to the Internet at the same time, you will probably have to invest into a router/firewall.   The router/firewall plugs into your cable modem and your PC's plug into router/firewall.

A router/firewall, while it may look like a hub is actually a router that allows multiple computers to share your broadband Internet connection correctly.
