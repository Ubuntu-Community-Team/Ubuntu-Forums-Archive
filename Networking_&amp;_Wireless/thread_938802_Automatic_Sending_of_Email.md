### Post by secondstage on 2008-12-15
I'm back to fix my own problem, so here it is.

If said email account is with Gmail, then go to Settings > Forwarding and POP/IMAP. Now under Forwarding, choose the destination email, and what you want google to do with the email(s). To make a filter, just click on link to do so. 

Google will not edit said emails, but this is great when trying to switch email accounts over.
