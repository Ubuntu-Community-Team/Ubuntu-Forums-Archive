### Post by guiverc on 2023-10-08
You've posted this is the Lubuntu *flavors* section of the forum, and further haven't given any release details.

The [evolution MUA]("https://packages.ubuntu.com/mantic/evolution") is an app from the GNOME project, and isn't used by Lubuntu.

Lubuntu uses the LXQt desktop, thus [did come]("https://discourse.lubuntu.me/t/dropping-trojita-k3b-fcitx-from-lubuntu-jammy-22-04-seed/3044") with a Qt5 email client ([https://packages.ubuntu.com/mantic/trojita](https://packages.ubuntu.com/mantic/trojita)) as it shares resources with the desktop itself.

I'm not aware of any changes that would impact your system, except for *specific *ones that relate to release details you didn't provide; the obvious is are you applying all security fixes (ie. *are certificates on your system being updated?*)
