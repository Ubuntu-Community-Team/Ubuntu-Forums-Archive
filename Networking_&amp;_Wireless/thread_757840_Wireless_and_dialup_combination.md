### Post by sKAApGIF on 2008-04-17
Your question is a little bit vague.  But it seems like you want to share your internet connection so that your ipod touch can use the internet?  And it seems like your computer is the one making the dialup connection?

In that case you would share your internet with your wireless router.  So that you wireless router connects to the internet.  And then share your wireless router's internet with your ipod.

Look [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=713874") for how to share your dialup with your router.  This you would do on your computer.

Now the only possible problem is configuring your router.  This depends a lot on your router model and make.  If your router has a WAN port you can connect that with your computer's ethernet port.  Most routers will automatically share the internet from their wan port with any clients connected to it.  But you will have to test it.

Hope this helps.
