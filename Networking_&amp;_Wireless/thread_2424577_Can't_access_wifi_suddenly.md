### Post by sqeelurgle on 2019-08-10
I am running Mythbuntu 14.04 (I know, I know, it's old, but it has always worked without any problems, so no need to upgrade).  I have recently had to change my Wifi due to a change in the internet connection to the house.  I have set up a mesh network now.  Anyway, the main thing is that the SSID changed.  I have gone in Network Connections and put the new SSID in (everything else stayed the same, even the password) but now the Myth box can't log on to the network, it just comes up with a notification that says wireless connections are available and open network connections to set it up.  Any ideas?

---

### Post by sqeelurgle on 2019-08-10
Found it!  Went into startup and disabled the Indicator Application.  The network manager then reappeared at the top of the panel, and I could click on it, scan for wifi and put in the password.  Done
