### Post by Gallardo Spyder on 2008-11-10
Over the past month or so I have been plagued (as others) with slow page loading on two of my boxes (both quad cores 64 bit).  One has ubuntu 8.10 and the other with 8.04.

This has been frustrating as I had no issues with either just a few months ago.

Now with both boxes being bad - I went down the long arduous trail of thinking it had something to do with my Cable Model, Cable itself or Router.

My ping speeds are incredible, my speed tests just fine - and even bypassing my router - no issues.

I then read 300 threads on possible causes, to no avail.  So I started down another path - the browers themselves.  I tried Opera, Firefox, Swiftfox, etc.

As I started to dig and tweak a little - I started to notice better performance with non flash sites (of course that may make sense overall).

After endless tweaking of both 32bit and 64bit versions of all of these different browsers  - It got better, but still frustrating 4 - 10 seconds to load, often times having to refresh, etc.

So I downloaded a new one Swiftweasel 3.0.1 (there is both a 32 and 64bit).

Let me tell you I installed it - and problem resolved!  I am getting the old performance I was use to.  I am getting the page load of even the heaviest Flash sites in 1 - 2 seconds.  (and it is based off of Firefox) - but something they do must handle flash is so much quicker than the other browsers I was trying.

If you are having the issue of slow performance - I would give Swiftweasel a try - it is on sourceforge (or do a search - I downloaded the 3.0.1 version).  And all the extensions and add-ons are the same as firefox - so no lost functionality.

GSpyder
