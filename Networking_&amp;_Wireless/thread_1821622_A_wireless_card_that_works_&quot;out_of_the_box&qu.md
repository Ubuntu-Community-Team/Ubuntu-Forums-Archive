[B]Always useful to pass on information on hardware that works:)

Just installed a Linksys WMP600N PCI wireless card. Restarted the PC and my wifi network already showed up by clicking on the network icon. One click, entered passphrase and tada, it worked.

Obviously has full support in the kernel on Ubuntu 11.04 and no need to fiddle around with NDiswrapper or anything. The card itself is PCI, 802.11N and dual band (2.4 and 5GHz).[/B]
