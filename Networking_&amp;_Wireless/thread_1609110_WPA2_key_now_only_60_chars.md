### Post by bigfootnmd on 2010-10-30
The WPA2 key that you are using with your laptop must match the key on your router.  Check the key on your router.  One technique that I find useful is to keep my key in a text file on my netbook.
That way if I ever have to renter the key on the WIFI connection I can just copy and paste.

The maximum WPA2 key length is 64 Characters.  So, you really do need to check the key on your router.
