### Post by qamelian on 2013-12-04
If you click on the network icon in the upper right corner of your screen, you should see the Starbucks wifi connection listed. You need to select it there, then what ever URL you enter in the address bar of the browser will redirect you to the Starbucks page that allows you to accept their terms & conditions.
