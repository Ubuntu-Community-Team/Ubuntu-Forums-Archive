### Post by SILLAT on 2009-11-11
Just Bought one of these Encore ENUWI-G2 USB Wireless adapter for an old Dell Laptop and it was plug and play on Ubuntu Jaunty 9.04.
At first first i thought it wasn't working but when i run *lsusb* in the terminal then i noticed it was actually picked up by Ubuntu, so i decided to the check network manager to view my connections it was there i saw my wireless, the signals & routers that i wanted to connect to. i just clicked on the connection entered my key and i was online :p
Geez .. it was that easy !!! :)
[COLOR="Red"]NO NDISWRAPPER[/COLOR] needed, at least not for me :)
