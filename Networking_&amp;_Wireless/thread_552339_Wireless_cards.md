### Post by kevdog on 2007-09-16
To my knowledge the optimizations that certain brands cards/routers possess are more driver dependent.  I dont think unless there is a specific linux wireless driver made for the device that the card can take advantage of these optimizations.  Hence it defaults to the standard g format.  (I dont think pre-N works right now since the standardization has not been ratified).  So bottom line, is that I think any g wireless card would work just as well with any g wireless router independent of brand.
