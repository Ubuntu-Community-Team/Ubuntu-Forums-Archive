### Post by #&amp;thj^% on 2023-04-12
These can be a real pain to pin down.
All wireless devices in the home that operate on a 2.4GHz or 5GHz wireless frequency are operating on the same frequency as IP security cameras, meaning that specific causes of wireless interference can be very hard to pin down.

A great number of wireless devices operating at the same time in a small space will cause interference as well.
Any chance of adding an extender?
