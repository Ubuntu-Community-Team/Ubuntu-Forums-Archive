### Post by dmitriyp13 on 2009-04-02
My understaning of typical wireless cards and their antennae is that they have no directionality, i.e. they receive the signal equally well in all directions.  This is the bad news as being able to tell where an access point is in relation to the computer is not possible.

The good news is that making a directional antenna is quite easy and there are a bunch of articles on the matter.  Here's one such guide [http://www.turnpoint.net/wireless/cantennahowto.html](http://www.turnpoint.net/wireless/cantennahowto.html) and a quick google search will bring up a lot more.
