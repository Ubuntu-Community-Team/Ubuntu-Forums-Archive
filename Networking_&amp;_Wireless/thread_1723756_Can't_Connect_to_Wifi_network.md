### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-04-07
Here is a link to the trouble shooting guide

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessTroubleShootingGuide](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessTroubleShootingGuide)

As you do not say whether your connection is wired or wireless, I am assuming wireless.

What exactly are you clicking on? Is it a left click on the network manager icon and then a click on either a wired connection or a wireless connection? Or, is it a right click and you are clicking Enable Networking or Enable wireless?

If Enable networking or Enable wireless have ticks against them then, clicking on them will disable your connection. It is like you have switched off your networking adapters. That will get you a "You are now disconnected" message.
