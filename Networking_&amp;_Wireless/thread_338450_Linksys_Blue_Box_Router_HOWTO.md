### Post by nrwilk on 2007-01-14
I just found this excellent howto for running and administrating Linksys routers under Linux.  The howto covers many models, including the wrt54g.  It is written by ESR, but he no longer maintains it (it's orphaned at the moment).  Due to not being maintained at the time it may be a little older, but I still found much of it useful, and a lot of it still applies.

From the HOWTO itself:
> Linksys makes a line of cheap, ubiquitous router/firewall boxes (models BEFSR41 and up, including the WRT54G) well-suited for use on a home DSL connection and popular among Linux hackers. This HOWTO gives hints and tips for managing Linksys routers from a Linux system, including the firmware upgrade procedure.

[http://en.tldp.org/HOWTO/Linksys-Blue-Box-Router-HOWTO/index.html](http://en.tldp.org/HOWTO/Linksys-Blue-Box-Router-HOWTO/index.html)

enjoy!
