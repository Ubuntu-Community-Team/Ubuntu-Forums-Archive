### Post by jimrz on 2008-02-06
> **DOH said:**
> I really don't like the wireless card that came with my laptop. Does anyone have any suggestions for a new one?:)

If you are talking about a pcmia card, I have had excellent results on sevaral different laptops using Netgear WG511T (the "T" is important) cards. It uses the Ahteros 5212 chipset and works "out of the box" with ubuntu. The only thing to look out for is that the firmware that the card ships with does not support WPA encryption, so if you need that you will have to upgrade the firmware yourself. The upgrade is available for download from the Netgear site and is easy to install.
