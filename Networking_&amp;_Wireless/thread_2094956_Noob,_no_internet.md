### Post by Cheesemill on 2012-12-15
You should try using a different version of Kubuntu.

9.10 reached end-of-life 18 months ago and as such doesn't receive any support or updates anymore (there have been no security updates since April last year).

Newer versions include support for more hardware so you may find that by installing 12.04 or 12.10 your wireless just works with no additional steps needed.
