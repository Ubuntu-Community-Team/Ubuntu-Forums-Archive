### Post by Tripp523 on 2009-02-26
The Rosewill RNX-G300LX Wireless Card works very well with 8.10; all you have to do is install the card and boot the computer.  I am now working on a solution for 8.04 (hardy) as it shows available networks, but it will not connect.

I am about 100 feet from my access point.  The signal is around 72%, and the speed shows 54Mb/s with Intrepid Ibex.  

I have not tried it with MS-Windows Vista; however, I read that there are some problems there.  One reviewer of this product at newegg states that he/she is using the vista driver for the D-link DWL-G510 Rev. C with great success as both products use the RT61 chipset. 

The price is right; I paid $15.00 with free shipping at newegg.
