### Post by rosegarden78 on 2008-01-25
It's my understanding you type 192.168.1.1 from a browser.  Then you login with admin and password blank.  Linksys will probably say change the base of your router to 192.168.3.1 to prevent conflicts with modem address being the same.  It's all there in the router config and website.  Might I say the tech support and 800 number for Linksys is most useful.
