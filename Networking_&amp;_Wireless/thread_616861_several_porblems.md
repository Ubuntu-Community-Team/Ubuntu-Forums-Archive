I had a similar issue a while back. I checked the upload date on the different distros and choose the most recently updated (I had an old one). Also check the Synaptic Package Manager and verify that all the drivers you need are loaded.

You may want to post the model number and the ethernet card manufacturer in your post since others may have had the same issue.
