### Post by davidonlaptop on 2009-01-30
Hi!

I guess you mean Ubuntu 8.10 (2008), not 6.10 (2006)...

By default on Ubuntu, the network icon appears on the top right corner. By clicking on it (left or right button can't remember), you should see a list of wifi network. Choose your network, and enter password.

If the network icon is not in the tray, right click on an empty space in the bar and choose "add item", then "network manager".

By the way, if you don't have special software requiring 64bit, I recommend you use 32bit.
