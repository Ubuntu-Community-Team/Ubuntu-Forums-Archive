### Post by Dalej on 2008-05-05
I originally installed with my card in.   I got the orinoco driver and was able to get info from the router -- essid and channel -- but it didn't scan "Resource temporarily unavailable".

I read something on this forum that caused me to try a reinstall with the card out.  I did so and when I plugged it back in I got the hostap driver instead of orinoco.  With hostap I was able to scan and find the router but that's all.  The card was on wlan0 instead of eth0.
The Network Manager was no help but after reading another post I clicked on the network icon at the top of the screen, selected my network and I'm online.
