### Post by bmartin on 2007-05-15
There's a [sticky post for enabling encryption at the top of this forum]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=318539"). Try selecting "Manual Configuration" from the network configuration icon in the top-right (assuming you're using Gnome; I have little experience with KDE and couldn't help you there) and typing in the name of your SSID (wireless network ID) after encryption is disabled.
