### Post by jm38712 on 2013-02-17
I thought I would post a successful install in case anyone is looking to purchase a USB Wifi Adapter in the future.

I bought a D-Link DWA-130 Rev E (or version E as it says on the stick) from Best Buy in Canada.  I am using Ubuntu 12.10 with the latest updates.  I plugged in the stick and to my surprise it worked flawlessy out of the box.

Total setup time was about 3 minutes including going to get a coffee otherwise would have been about 30 seconds.  :D
