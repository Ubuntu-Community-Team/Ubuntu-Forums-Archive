### Post by jbrown96 on 2009-04-15
Do you mean geographical map? There's no good way to do that. The only thing a normal wireless card can measure is signal strength, and this is a very poor indicator of distance (not to mention completely useless for determining direction), especially since Wifi is primarily used indoors. There are just way too many factors that affect signal strength.
