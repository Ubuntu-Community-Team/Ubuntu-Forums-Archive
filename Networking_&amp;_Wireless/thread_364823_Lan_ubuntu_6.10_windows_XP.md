### Post by Reedbeta on 2007-02-18
The easiest way would probably be to buy a wireless router.  You can hook it up between your cable modem and your desktop, plugging the desktop into the router's Ethernet port and using the wireless for access from the laptop.  These kind of routers can be bought at most computer stores for around 30-40 USD.

You might also be able to set up the desktop's wireless card to broadcast an adhoc network, and set up some routing table so you your laptop can get to the Internet through it, but I'm afraid I don't know how to do that.
