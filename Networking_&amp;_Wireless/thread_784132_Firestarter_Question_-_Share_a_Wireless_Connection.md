### Post by Ayuthia on 2008-05-09
I would think that under preferences->network settings you would have your wireless set for Internet connection (wlan0 or eth1) and Local Network Connection should be your wired (eth0).  You should also have Internet Connection Sharing clicked.

Your router should have DHCP enabled also.
