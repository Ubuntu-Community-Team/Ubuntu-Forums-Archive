### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-01-17
Right click on the **network manager icon** inn the panel and **Edit Connections.**  Highlight the connection you use in the wireless tab and again choose Edit.  In the dialog that appears, make sure the tick boxes top and bottom left for **Connect automatically** and **Make available to all users** are both selected.
