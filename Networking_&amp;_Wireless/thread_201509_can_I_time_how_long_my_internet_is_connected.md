### Post by Dr. Nick on 2006-06-22
not sure of a utility that will do that for you, But I have a few tips I learned from teh crummy cable internet here.

If you lose the internet just unplug the modem a minute and plug it in again. Do this to the router to if you have one.

Also check the splitters in your setup, If I had 2 splitters infornt of my modem it would work very poorly, rewiring to only have it behind 1 splitter has helped alot
