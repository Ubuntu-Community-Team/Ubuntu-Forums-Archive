### Post by sonofusion82 on 2008-08-11
there are several alternatives.
1. get a USB Ethernet adapter for you PC and do a network bridge or a proxy on the PC.
2. similar to 1 but use a USB wifi adapter and setup as ad-hoc networking mode. your IT/security guys may not like this but wireless better than cable rite?
3. perhaps getting those cheap 4-wire to 2x 4-wire RJ-45 Y splitter. but it has limitations and only works at 10Mbps Half-duplex mode.
