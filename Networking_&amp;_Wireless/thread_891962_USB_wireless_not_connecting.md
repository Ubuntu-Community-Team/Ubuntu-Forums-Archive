### Post by madelacruz on 2010-01-17
I recently had this problem with my desktop. It turned out that the adapter was in an area with a weak signal.  The solution was to move the adapter to an area with a stronger signal. This can be accomplished by moving the computer, or by connecting the adapter to a long usb connector cable and moving the wifi adapter to where it can capture a signal. (BTW, in my situation, the wireless source is external - not from within the house.)
