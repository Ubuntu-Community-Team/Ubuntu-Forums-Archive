### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2007-02-06
Assuming that the nvidia kernel module is loaded, (run "lsmod | grep nvidia" to see if it is), your glx entry in /etc/X11/xorg.conf is wrong.

> Load "GLX"
It should be:

Load "glx"

Linux is case sensitive.
