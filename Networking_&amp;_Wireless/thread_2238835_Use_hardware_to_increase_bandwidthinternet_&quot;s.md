### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-08-10
You can [bond]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuBonding") 2 ethernet cards on your computer to effectively double your bandwidth, but to get real performance improvements, you are at the mercy of your Internet Service Provider.  Although I have not tried it, you could conceptually use two different ISP's (say DSL from the phone company and a cable modem from your cable provider) and try bonding those connections to get better throughput.  

Move to a part of the world that has gigabit connections.
