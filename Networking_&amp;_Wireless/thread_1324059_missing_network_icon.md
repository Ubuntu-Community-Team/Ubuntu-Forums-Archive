### Post by t0mppa on 2009-11-12
> **juntjoo said:**
> what is the little program in the upper right hand corner that allows you to connect to a network called and how do you recover it if it somehow disappears?

It's called Network Manager and if you only closed it, you can press Alt + F2, type in *nm-applet --sm-disable* and click Run. If you closed the whole Notification Area from the top panel, you can right click the panel and choose Add to Panel... and pick Notification Area from the list.

> **juntjoo said:**
> either i'm a big time newbie or this is a common problem for most new users of ubuntu, but i looked high and low and couldn't find this program.

It's not that easy to find, if you don't know what you're looking for, but I think most users just don't close it, so it remains up there easily available.

> **juntjoo said:**
> and again, why is "do not subscribe" the default selection in thread (pages?)? who starts a thread and doesn't want to subscribe to it.  again, am i just dumb with this stuff?  sorry, computers can be really frustrating.
