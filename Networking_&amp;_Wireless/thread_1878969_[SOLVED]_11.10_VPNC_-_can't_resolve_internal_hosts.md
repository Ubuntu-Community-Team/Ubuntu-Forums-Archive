Seems the VPNC client does not have a dependency on resolvconf which appears to be required to allow my connecting client to resolve IPs in my companies internal network.

I'm guessing this was the case in earlier releases or resolvconf was installed by default or something else.

Anyways just posting in case it helps some other 11.10 fresh install.
