### Post by kostkon on 2007-08-10
Just follow [this guide]("http://www.linux-usb.org/SpeedTouch/ubuntu/") and you will be able to make to work. Your modem will work just fine in Windows. 

In the guide, every time that you will need to select between *pre Dapper* and *Dapper* versions of a command, always go with the one for Dapper.

Try it and if you have any issue don't hesitate to ask for help here.

EDIT:

Indeed a specific firmware will be flashed to the modem every time you will boot to Ubuntu. But this will not affect the modem's operation in Windows. See [this answer]("http://www.linux-usb.org/SpeedTouch/faq/index.html#q2") from the FAQ of the site that hosts the guide.
