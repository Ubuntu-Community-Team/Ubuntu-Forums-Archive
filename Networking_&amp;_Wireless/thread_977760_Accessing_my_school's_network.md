### Post by kosmiciatakuja on 2008-11-10
As for "cracking", try aircrack-ng, it's in the repos. Although remember that if you crack it with this tool, you have to do it for demonstration purposes and notify the admin about how easy it is to break into his security. Don't use it for malicious activity!
