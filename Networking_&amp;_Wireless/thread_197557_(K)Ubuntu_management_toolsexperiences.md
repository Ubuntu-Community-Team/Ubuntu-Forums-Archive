### Post by MarinaraOfDeath on 2006-06-15
I was having a heck of a time getting wireless to be stable. I was near unto removing and just going all Windows until my thesis is done. However, as a last ditch I stripped out everything wireless management except wlassistant (the command labeled *Wireless Assistant* in K Launcher --> Internet). Then I installed wifi radar, which has been rock solid in four environments over several reboots so far. I highly recommend trying it _instead_ of Network Manager or the "built-in" management tools if you're having trouble but you know things work otherwise (correct driver, open AP, etc). 

What about all y'all experiences with various network managers?
