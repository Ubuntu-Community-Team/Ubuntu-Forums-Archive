### Post by houldsworth1 on 2010-11-05
Why are you using a USB adapter?  Doesn't the acer aspire 5742 come with a built in networking card?

I would suggest trying ndiswrapper.  Try this:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/8.04/internet/C/ndiswrapper.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/8.04/internet/C/ndiswrapper.html)

I did that to access the windows driver for my Gateway Netbook and it worked instantly.
