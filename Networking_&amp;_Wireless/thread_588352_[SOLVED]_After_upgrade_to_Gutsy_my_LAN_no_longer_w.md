### Post by MacDuff on 2007-10-23
Problem solved!

I have been setting up a small local network at the same time as I have been learning Ubutu/Linux and in my over enthusiasm for Ubuntu have been trying to do too many things at once.  Running as it were before I could walk properly.

I set up Firestarter on the server just before I did the upgrade to 7.10 and had not yet configured it.  After the long procedure of upgrading to 7.10 it slipped my mind that Firestarter was running.

I now have to learn how to use Firestarter, (or disable it until I have more time to study it) before I can continue.

Sorry to have wasted forum space on this rather stupid error on my part but, if nothing else, it does emphasize the need to take things one step at a time when learning a new system.  I shall try to not forget that bit of wisdom.
