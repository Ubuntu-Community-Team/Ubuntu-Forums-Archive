### Post by Ashish Asolkar on 2010-04-21
connect ur phone to the pc by using data cable.
then go to system> preferences> network connections 
new window will open .
go to the <mobile broadband> option 
click on <+Add>
<new mobile broadband connection> window will open
click on forword.
select ur service provider and connect to the internet .
have a nice day
