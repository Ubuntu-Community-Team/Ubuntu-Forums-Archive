### Post by DuckHook on 2016-09-12
The "Connect to Server" functionality was merged into Nautilus (the default file manager) in 14.04. You should be able to find it at the bottom of the left hand bar under "Networks".

It may not be able to connect to Blackberries without additional configuration or protocol drivers but since I do not own a Blackberry, I don't know what protocol it uses.
