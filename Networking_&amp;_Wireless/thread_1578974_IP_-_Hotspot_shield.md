### Post by lb4406 on 2010-09-21
And Tor and Privoxy install will just anonymise your IP address. It does work for region specific sites however it can be very slow, the other downside is that the end IP you end up presenting to the web could be from anywhere in the Tor network so you might end up with a German IP which still won't help you view US sites like Hulu

You actually want a VPN service, which is a tunnel between your computer and one based somewhere else (i.e. in your case in america - all your internet access then goes down this tunnel and it appears to the rest of the internet you are browsing from the computer at the end of the tunnel if that makes sense) Unfortunately these tend to be "Pay" services.  I just did a quick search and found one for UKP 4.25 a month here:  [http://vpnuk.net/](http://vpnuk.net/) (I have no connection with the site but thats the kind of service it appears you want)
