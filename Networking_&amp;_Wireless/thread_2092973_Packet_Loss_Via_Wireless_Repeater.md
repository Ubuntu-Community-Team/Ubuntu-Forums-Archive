### Post by EmmEight on 2012-12-09
I may be a dreamer, 

But if it where me, I would grab a drill (blunt object works as well) and pop a hole in the wall.. Be sure to grab a golf-ball sized paint sample

Run down to the local home supply warehouse and grab the following:


[LIST]
[*]A length of cat5e cable
[*]A couple of RJ-45 mod plugs and a crimper
[*]A small tub of spackle for repairing the hole (when you move out)
[*]And of course a *sample* sized container of paint (matched)
[*]Maybe a paint brush and putty knife as well
[/LIST]

If you have a patch cable long enough go ahead and nix (no pun intended) the cable/plug/crimper

You could now have your own network with a wired connection!

Even if you don't repair the hole in the wall, you should do a cost analysis of which saves you more:


[LIST]
[*]Splitting the internet bill with your mate
[*]or Paying the repair fee for the hole in the wall
[/LIST]
Setting up a wireless router as an access point is easy.):P
