### Post by Greydog on 2006-10-03
Probably not the best wireless card but, I purchased a PCMCIA card for my old Latitude that was referenced in another thread.

I got it from 3B Tech ([www.3btech.net](www.3btech.net)) Item code: NICL-WG-CM-GWA-EO8
802.11g for $18.54 shipped.

I had been trying to get a D-link working and decided I wasn't getting anywhere so, for that kind of money, I thought, "What the hey?"

Delivered in three business days, poked it in and, in a few minutes, I was connected through my D-link router and was a happy guy. The sensitivity (if that's the word I want) is not as good as the D-link that I was using under Windows, but I am able to connect from my patio and that was my goal.

If you dont mind a product called "Chiefmax" hangin' out of your laptop, I'd give it a try.

Good luck,

Greydog
