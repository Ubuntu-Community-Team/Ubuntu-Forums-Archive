### Post by hk42 on 2021-03-24
Normally the dongle and wireless keyboard/mouse are paired out of the box.
The protocol used is proprietary and has nothing to do with WIFI or Bluetooth.
AFAIK it is unidirectional .
Such keyboards and mouse are not detected by Bluetooth software.
