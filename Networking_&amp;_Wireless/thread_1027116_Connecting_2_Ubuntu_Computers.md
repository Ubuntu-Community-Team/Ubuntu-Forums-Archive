### Post by TestDummy! on 2009-01-01
**Firestarter** has an option in its setup to configure a computer for sharing its internet connection, if its directly connected to a modem. Although, you normally can't do it if you don't have two Ethernet interfaces, I did have it going with USB between the modem and sharing computer, and Ethernet between the first and second computer. Then again, I just use a router with a switch, so I don't have to worry about setting Internet sharing on a given computer.
