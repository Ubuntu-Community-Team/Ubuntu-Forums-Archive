### Post by crip720 on 2020-04-28
Sites streaming/downloading videos will probably download as fast as  needed, not what you can handle.  Sites for network speed testing will  push to max.  Google download speed testing and try to use servers close  to you to test your speed.  Speedtest.net and fast.com are only two of  many.  These test should be done often at different times to get an idea  of your speed and what your ISP is really allowing.  Should stop all  downloading before and during test(maybe a minute or less).  Not all  single tests be accurate, but average of many tests should give decent  speed.  Should also do Mb/s to Mb/s or kbps to kbps, mixing them without converting can give wrong assumptions.  If proper tests seem too low, we can figure out other problems.
