### Post by grahammechanical on 2010-11-10
I can tell you this, the red exclamation mark is an indication that you are not connected to the router/modem either with wireless or ethernet. That is the way it is supposed to work, anyway. 

May I suggest that first you rule out the modem not working correctly. Is the power supply stable or intermittent? Do the modem indicator lights show that it is connected. 

I have five LEDs showing power, ethernet, wireless, broadband and internet. As I am connected by ethernet, then the ethernet light is green. As are the broadband and internet lights. This tells me that the broadband connection from the modem to the ISP is established and that the modem is giving me access to the internet right now.
