### Post by Bucky Ball on 2014-03-27
Wired and wireless connection worked fine side by side. The device is designed for this, which is why it has options for both. I have three devices connected via ethernet cable and another three connected wirelessly using a D-Link router. Never been a problem and it has been about five years now.

A wired connection will always be slightly faster (or sometimes radically faster, depending on the quality of the wireless signal you are getting to your computer). Consequently, if the device is close enough I will always opt for wired. I always do Ubuntu installs and upgrades using a wired connection. More reliable.
