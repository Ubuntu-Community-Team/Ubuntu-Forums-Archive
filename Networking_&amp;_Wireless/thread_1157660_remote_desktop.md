### Post by puppywhacker on 2009-05-12
there are a few possibilities, you can when you login to ubuntu decide to login to another machine (using xdmcp) or you can login with ssh and use Xforwarding to open Xapplications. e.g. xclock then you get only a remote window. or you use any vncserver and vnc client to open remote desktops.
