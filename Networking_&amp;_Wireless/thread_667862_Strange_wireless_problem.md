### Post by pieisgood4589 on 2008-01-14
This is almost exactly what happened to me. First, restart. That always helps. :) Then, make sure the password is ABSOLUTELY correct, this is what fixed my problem. Make it CASE SENSITIVE, which Windows doesn't need. Then, right click on the panel with the internet icon, click on add to panel, and then scroll down to "Network Monitor" and add it to the panel.
