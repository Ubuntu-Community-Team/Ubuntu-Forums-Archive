### Post by lixy on 2007-08-18
I seem to recall that with 6.06, that particular card was working out of the box.

If you're serious about Ubuntu, go for a regular install. That Wubi thing will only waste your time. It's a great project, but it still got a long way to go.

Get yourself a fresh Feisty disk, reboot your machine and it'll get you into LiveCD mode. At that point, I'm quite certain your card will work. Then you can go on installing Ubuntu. It's nearly all a point and click process. There's nothing complicated about it. Messing around with Wuby will only frustrate you at this point. So, buy yourself a blank CD and get that burner working. 

The community is of course always here if you have any problem. Your title would have been excellent if you had dropped the "Total noob" bit. It's superfluous and a little disruptive.

Best of luck,
