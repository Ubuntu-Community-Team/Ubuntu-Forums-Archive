### Post by gypsy98101 on 2010-05-27
You're putting in the wrong username.  It should just be the 'alias' part, or just the part before the '@' symbol.  So if your email address is [email]alias@us.army.mil[/email], then just put in 'alias' for your username.  
I was having the same problem and this fixed it.
