### Post by Perkins on 2012-02-21
The network NIC is usually the wired port.  Options to enable or disable the wireless card may or may not actually be in the BIOS, but they're usually clear about which is which.  Some laptops have a physical switch.  Some have a softkey on the keyboard.  Worst case, the wireless card is usually under an access panel, so you don't have to disassemble the entire machine to remove it.  (power off, unplug, and remove the battery first.)
