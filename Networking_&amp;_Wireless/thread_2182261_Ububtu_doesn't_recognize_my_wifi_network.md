### Post by VinDSL on 2013-10-21
Might be a longshot, but...

I had a similar problem with my Latitude D620 -- saw my neighbors wifi routers, but not mine.

Also, when it did occasionally find my router, it *thought* my router was hidden.

Changing the channel in my router from channel 11 => channel 1 or channel 5 (and locking it in, e.g. no auto channel select) cured the problem.

---

### Post by Bill_Paro on 2013-10-21
So I just got into my modem settings and changed the channel from 13 to 11.
