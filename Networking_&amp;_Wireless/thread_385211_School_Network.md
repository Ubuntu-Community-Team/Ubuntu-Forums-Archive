### Post by Mr. C. on 2007-03-17
I'm not sure there is a "normal" way to access the internet.  There are many methods and mechanisms.

It sounds like your school provides a wireless network and that your Windows wireless configuration or authorization dialog appears, where you enter your password.  Configured correctly, you probably can enter it once and configure your Windows box to retain the password.

It may be that your school provides something called a "captive portal", whereby you connect to the network, but cannot browse anywhere but a login site, until you actually login.  From the few details you've provided, I cannot know which your school requires.

You can do the same with Ubuntu wireless.
