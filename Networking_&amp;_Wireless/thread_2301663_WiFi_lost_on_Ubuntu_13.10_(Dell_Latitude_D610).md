### Post by Bucky Ball on 2015-11-04
> **hattpa said:**
> ... install a newer version ;)

Don't think about it. 13.10 is ancient. No repos and very little support, if you can find any at all. Things change and most forget how the older releases ticked, and that is old. Your best bet is to trawl through posts from 13.10 era. 

14.04 LTS is the current long-term support release, supported until 2019 and upgradeable directly to the next LTS release, 16.04 LTS in April next year. 15.10 is the newest interim release, supported until July next year.

Find the current Ubuntu ISOs [here]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download/deskto"). Good luck.

PS: 13.10 hasn't received security updates for months so it might be a good thing you can't get online! There's probably vulnerabilities to find if someone were to look. :)
