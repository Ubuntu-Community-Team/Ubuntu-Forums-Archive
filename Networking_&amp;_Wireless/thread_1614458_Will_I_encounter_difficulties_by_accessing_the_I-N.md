You're speaking of using a USB extension cable to extend it, OR one of those set of boxes that transmit the signal wirelessly or via ethernet cable?

The former, honestly, I don't personally think USB is so affected by cable length as some people claim, and 10-12 meters is no problem.  Though I haven't tried it, and I could be entirely wrong about that.  :P  USB extension cables are cheap and there's no harm if it doesn't work.

As for the range extenders, I can't say.  It depends on the performance level you're seeking.  1-2Mbps, I'd say sure.  Higher than that probably gets iffy.
