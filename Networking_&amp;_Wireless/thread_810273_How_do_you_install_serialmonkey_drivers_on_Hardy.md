### Post by nicedude on 2008-05-28
You would need to blacklist the original wifi driver you are using now and compile a new one from the serialmonkey driver package. Compiling the driver isn't to hard at all so just get the driver and then read the "readme" & "install" text files that the driver pack will contain. 

Hope you get it figured out, I don't use that chipset or I could help more but I found installing the driver for my Atheros chipset a great learning experience.
