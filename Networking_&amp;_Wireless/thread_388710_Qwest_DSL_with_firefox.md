Ok, 
Hopefully this helps allot of people who find themselves lost like I was when trying to connect to the Internet using Qwest DSL and Firefox. Please keep in mind that this post assumes that you have your NIC or wireless device working properly. 

Open Firefox. 

In the URL type   "about:config" without the quotes. 

scroll to the line named, network.dns.disableIPv6

change the value to TRUE. 

Restart Firefox and Presto! You should now be enjoying the wonderful world of the Web! 

Hope this helps anyone out! 

cheers!
