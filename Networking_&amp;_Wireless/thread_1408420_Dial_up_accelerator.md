### Post by pricetech on 2010-02-16
"Dial Up Accelerators" is a misnomer.  ISPs cache frequently used sites on their servers so they can serve them up quicker.  You simply can't force more data across a dialup connection than it is able to accommodate.

Therefore, no software should be needed to use your ISPs "accelerator".

The software they give you for winders has some other function.
