### Post by superprash2003 on 2008-05-21
sure.. have you connected them via a LAN cable already? if so, then go to system->administration->network and set ips for both pcs.probably 10.0.0.1 and 10.0.0.2 .. and try pinging 10.0.0.1 from 10.0.0.2 and vice-versa . if the ping is successfull you have a network!!
