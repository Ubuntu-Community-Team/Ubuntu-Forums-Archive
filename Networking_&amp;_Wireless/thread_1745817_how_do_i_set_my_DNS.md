### Post by Mosabama on 2011-05-01
> **balkrish999 said:**
> how do I change the settings for DNS to 8.8.8.8 and 8.8.4.4

??

can you tell me step-by-step as I am a begginer for linux

right click on the network icon on the notification bar > edit connections
select your network > edit > IP4 Settings tab.

from the drop down window choose "Automatic (DHCP) addresses only"
in DNS write 8.8.8.8. then save and reconnect to the network
