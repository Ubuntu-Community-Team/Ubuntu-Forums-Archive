### Post by Kevbert on 2008-09-15
Welcome to Ubuntu.
Plug in the wireless USB adapter,  Now go to Applications - Accessories - Terminal and enter
```
lsusb
```
Highlight all the displayed text with the mouse and press Ctrl-Shift-C to copy it and open a new post and press Ctrl-V to paste the output here.
