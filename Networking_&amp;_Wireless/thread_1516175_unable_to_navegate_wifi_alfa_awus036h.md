### Post by pitisjol on 2010-06-25
hi there, well i am not sure how long it will last but it seems i managed to get this working.
1) i have downloaded the driver for the AWUS036H (chipset realtek 8187L) from realtek page
2) installed it 
3) re-started the so
4) turnoff internal wifi card
5)start navegating
i wish this also works for people that have the same wifi

bueno pues solo comentarles que no se por cuanto tiempo pero ahora la wifi funciona.
1) baje el driver para linux de AWUS036H (chipset realtek 8187L) desde la pagina de realtek
2) lo instale
3) reinicie el so
4) apague la targeta interna del la laptop
