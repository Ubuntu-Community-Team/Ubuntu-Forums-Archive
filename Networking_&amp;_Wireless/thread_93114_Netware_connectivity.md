### Post by nyle on 2006-03-13
Novell is working on a Netware Client for SuSe and Novell Desktop. The last beta I tried was over my head to get functional but I could have easily missed something and it's under active development. :cool: 

Also, Novell supports NFS and CIFS both of which Linux can attach to. Take a look at the Native File Access pack or if you can upgrade to 6.5 they are built in. In any case there are multiple ways for Linux to access Novell servers. :-D 

I for one would love to see a Ubuntu Novell Client. Maybe someone in the Open Source community can port Novell's to function on Ubuntu? That would be amazing. \\:D/
