### Post by _duncan_ on 2008-07-21
Another thing to consider is your parent's dial-up service provider.

Most providers are OS-neutral. However there are a few that require users to dial-in using proprietary software. These proprietary software are mostly windows-based with no linux equivalent.

-------------

+1 on the external serial modem. I have yet to encounter an external serial modem I couldn't use with ubuntu. The hardest part will probably come from setting the dialer program to connect to the ISP. The 2nd link on my sig contains a wealth of info on the different dialer programs available.
