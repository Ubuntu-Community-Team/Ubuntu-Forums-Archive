### Post by lornt78 on 2012-07-18
I've figured this thing out. For anyone else having trouble like I was, it's no too difficult. Got it working by going into the system setting, network, and turning mobile broadband on. I was connected to the internet via wireless, and needed to turn wireless off. Restarting the computer also helped. Everything else just worked automatically - no special settings or passwords required. (Don't know what the hell all this other stuff on the forums etc is about!!)
