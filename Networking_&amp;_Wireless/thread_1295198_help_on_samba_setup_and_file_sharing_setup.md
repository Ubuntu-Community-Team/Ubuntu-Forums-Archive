### Post by ghostborg on 2009-10-19
Open Places=>Home then in Nautilus right click on a folder that you want to share like Public and click on Properties. Click on Share tab and select Share this folder, at some point a window should pop-up and ask you to install the necessary services. I checked Allow others and Guest access also.
Reboot after services are installed.:guitar:
