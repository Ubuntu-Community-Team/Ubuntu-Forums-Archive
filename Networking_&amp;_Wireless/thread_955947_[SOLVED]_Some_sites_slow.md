### Post by camoanimal on 2008-10-22
I have had the "Upgrade your browser" error for a while, and the best thing to do is just click the link near the bottom to continue to your inbox.  Since it is "M$" Live Mail now I think it doesn't recognize Linux systems, and therefore can not fully utilize the features setup for Windows or Mac.  The alternative is to install Mozilla Thunderbird email client.  There is an addon that allows you to use a Hotmail account with it.  

As for you speed issue, my friend has had a similar problem recently.  There are some wireless cards which the drivers are not completely functional.  The main problem being speed.  However, if it is only on certain sites then I am not sure.  This usually just implies a slow connection to the site's server, which could be caused by anything from high activity on that server, to bad wireless connection.

Hope this helps some  :)
