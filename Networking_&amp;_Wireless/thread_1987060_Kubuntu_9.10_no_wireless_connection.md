### Post by TBABill on 2012-05-25
Firstly, 9.10 has long since become unsupported so the repos are no longer active. I believe you would have to re-point your sources to the archive repos. In my mind, no reason at all to do so when newer and improved versions of Kubuntu are available. I'd recommend either 10.04 LTS (1 more year of support) or 12.04 LTS (supported till April 2017).

Once you can install a newer version, if you can plug into your router you should be able to update and activate wireless drivers.
