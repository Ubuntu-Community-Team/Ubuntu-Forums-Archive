### Post by killabee44 on 2012-07-01
Guys,

On a new 12.04 install, I'm constantly getting an error by the network icon that says: "wired network disconnected". 

This will go on for a few minutes, then stop for a minute, then continue. 

Meanwhile, my internet connection works fine and at the right speed. 

I think this is because my PC has two ethernet ports. I am using one, but the other is not being used. Im thinking it must be the source of the error. 

I have tried switching to my other ethernet port, but it does not work at all, even after reboot (so that must the the culprit?). 




This PC is connected to the web through a comcast cable modem and a router. 

Your help is appreciated...

Update..

I went into Network Connections and noticed that the default connection was set to the ethernet port that was not being used. I changed it to the one in use and the notifications stopped.

Who would have thought.. such an easy fix.
