### Post by gradedcheese on 2007-02-14
Sure, but if it's just laptop<---cable--->desktop, you need the cable to be a crossover.  Either that, or two cables and a hub.  If you think about it, this makes sense: transmit on one PC needs to go to receive on the other, and vice versa.

After making the correct physical connection, simply set each PC's IP address such that they're on the same subnet.  Then you're all set.
