Everytime I connect to a VPN, I automatically lose connectivity to my google account (gmail, gchat, g-reader, etc), and have to restart a new instance of chrome in order to re-access my account. All other websites work fine, except for google. Logging out and signing back into my google account in the same instance of chrome doesn't work. It has to be a totally new session, but it works without fail. Can't figure out for the life of me what could be doing this.

### Post by jayshomebrew on 2011-12-21
edit your VPN connection, goto IPv4 Settings tab, select the routes button.
Select 'use this connection only for resources on its network'.
