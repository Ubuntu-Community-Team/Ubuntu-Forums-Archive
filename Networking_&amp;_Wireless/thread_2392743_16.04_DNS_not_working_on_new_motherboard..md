First the Driver/Module - 
   The onboard chip  Qualcomm Atheros QCA8171 Gigabit Ethernet uses the alx driver
   The PCI card chip  Realtek RTL8169 PCI Gigabit Ethernet Controller uses the r8169 version 2.3LK-NAPI
