### Post by nyinge on 2007-03-02
When you look at the output of "iwconfig wlan0", you'll see that
```
             Link Quality:0  Signal level:0  Noise level:0  
```

You have to wait awhile to get numbers other than zeros.  Move closer to your wireless router and see if that helps.  Also, check if there is any encryption or MAC address filtering setup in your router.  For the purpose of testing, clear those stuff for now.
