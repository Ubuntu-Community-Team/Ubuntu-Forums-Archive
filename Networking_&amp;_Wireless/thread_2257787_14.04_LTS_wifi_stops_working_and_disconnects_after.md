### Post by r.stiltskin on 2014-12-23
I found this thread [http://askubuntu.com/questions/457341/wifi-drops-and-wont-reconnect-until-reboot-14-04-with-rtl8723be]("http://askubuntu.com/questions/457341/wifi-drops-and-wont-reconnect-until-reboot-14-04-with-rtl8723be") which may help you.  A few of the comments there mention an option fwlps=0 for your rtl8723be module.

I think you can just add that to your command
sudo modprobe rtl8723be fwlps=0

or if that doesn't work try the command mentioned in that thread:
echo "options rtl8723be fwlps=0" | sudo tee /etc/modprobe.d/rtl8723be.conf
