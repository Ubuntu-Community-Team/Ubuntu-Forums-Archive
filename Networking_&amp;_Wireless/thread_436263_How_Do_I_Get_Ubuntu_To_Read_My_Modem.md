### Post by 67GTA on 2007-05-07
Ubuntu doesn't support dial-up modems out of the box. You will have to find out what modem you have and get the driver first. On the panel go to SYSTEM>HELP&SUPPORT>INTERNET>CONNECTING TO THE INTERNET>DIAL-UP. You will find the info for all of this.

### Post by _duncan_ on 2007-05-07
you probably have what is known as a winmodem. A software modem designed with windows OS in mind. It is possible to use these with linux, but the process is not easy.
