### Post by whistl034 on 2018-07-08
Ubuntu Linux does not normally consume more bandwidth than any other OS. If anything, I'd say it consumes less, because it contains less bloatware. It entirely depends on what applications you are running.

 Perhaps your systems are just automatically downloading updates? That should be a temporary thing though, maybe 5-15 minutes, depending on your available bandwidth.

I'd also question where you downloaded the Ubuntu installation DVD from. You should ONLY ever download Ubuntu from Ubuntu.com. If you got it from anywhere else, there is a possibility that it may have been modified to include malware, which could easily explain your bandwidth issue.
