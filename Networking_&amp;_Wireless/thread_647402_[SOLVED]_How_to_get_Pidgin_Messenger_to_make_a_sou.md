### Post by berliita on 2007-12-23
Thanks, Sef and vishzilla.

It turns out, i needed to check the "Message received begins conversation" option on the "Sounds" tab of the "Preferences" dialog, and additionally to either choose the "Console beep" sound method, or else to choose the "Automatic" or "ALSA" sound methods AND assign appropriate sound files to the various sound events.
