### Post by Brandon.Viking on 2007-09-18
I have had no real issues with mapping drives and terminal services ... works just dandy.
It depends on what you want to access.

You certainly don't need your credentials to be on the network. Everything will just work. Only thing is that if your proxies are authenticated and don't use NTLM then you might have difficulty surfing the net. I have Linux VM working just fine but our Internet is transparently proxied.

Hope that helps.
