### Post by mdbarton on 2008-01-22
> **oakwoodevents said:**
> Not conversant with servage, but cannot see how and where i connect with cuteftp to download the files and how i edit them. i have only ever uploaded files. finding it very confusing

CuteFTP is a windows program - you don't need it on ubuntu.  Just click on the Places menu, 'Connect to Server' and  put in the server name (ftp.servage.net) and your servage username  - everything else can be left blank.  Click on Connect, and off you go.  You get prompted for the password.  You then get a nautilus window, where you can edit files, etc as if they were local.
