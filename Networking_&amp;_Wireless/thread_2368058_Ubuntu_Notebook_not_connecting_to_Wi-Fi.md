### Post by Autodave on 2017-08-07
Can we have some specs on the notebook and also what version of ?buntu you are using?

One thing you can try in the meantime: and don't laugh, I have seen many laptops, notebooks fixed this way:

  	 	 	 	   1. Power down and disconnect power cord.
2. Unplug all peripherals.
3. Remove battery.
4. Wait 10 minutes.
5. Reinstall battery.
6. Connect power cord.
7. Boot computer and test to see if condition is gone.
8. If good so far, now you can reconnect peripherals.
