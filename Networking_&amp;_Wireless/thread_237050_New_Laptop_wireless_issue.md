### Post by blackened on 2006-08-16
Ubuntu gives superuser privileges to the first user account created (during install). You were asked for a password when you fired up Network Settings, and this gave you superuser privileges.

If by Network Setting form you mean network-admin, then it only displays what is recognized by the system as far as I know.

There are many howtos and solutions to common wireless problems here in the forums. Search for wireless problems. ndiswrapper may also help you.
