I have a Toshiba a300d laptop with Intel iwl4965 driver. It is working with speeds upto 500kB/sec download in Vista, but in Ubuntu it worked only with a speed around 30KB/sec(download speed).

The problem occuring was due to the faulty driver. Now I put driver NETw4x64 downloaded from Toshiba website, it works same as in Vista.
You can download the driver from the link
[http://support1.toshiba-tro.de/tools/updates/intel-wlan/wlan-intel-3495-4965-v111116-xp-2k.zip](http://support1.toshiba-tro.de/tools/updates/intel-wlan/wlan-intel-3495-4965-v111116-xp-2k.zip)
