### Post by namaste on 2007-01-17
Add the Network Monitor applet to the top panel. It makes it easy to see if the wireless is working correctly and the siginal strength.

In the top panel, right click > + Add to Panel... > System & Hardware / click on: Network Monitor > [+Add] > [Close]
