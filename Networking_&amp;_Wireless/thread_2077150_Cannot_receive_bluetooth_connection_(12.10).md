### Post by slmpika on 2012-10-27
I am trying to send files from my phone(LG 420G) to my computer (ubuntu 12.10). When I try to send a file from my phone, nothing happens on my computer and my phone displays "Disconnected". When I try to Browse Files from the Bluetooth Settings, the connection slider moves to ON. However, sometimes Nautilus will open and close quickly, and the slider will change to OFF. If this does not happen, I can start Nautilus and see LG-420G under Network, but when I click it, Nautilus closes and the connection changes to OFF.

 If I try to manually connect from my phone, I see the message "Fail to connect to ubuntu-0. Retry?" and the slider (in Ubuntu bluetooth settings) moves to ON, then OFF quickly. I paired the devices without a problem, and I have no problem sending files to my phone, it is just the other way around that I have trouble.

My bluetooth adapter is Kinivo BTD-300 for reference.

Is there some configuration setting that I might need to tweak. Or do I need to install additional packages? Any help I can get would be appreciated.
