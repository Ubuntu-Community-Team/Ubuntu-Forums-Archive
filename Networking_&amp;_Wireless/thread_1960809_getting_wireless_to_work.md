### Post by TBABill on 2012-04-18
When you said Ubuntu cannot find the wireless card, do you mean it can't see any connections or that you ran a terminal command to list your devices and it did not identify the device? There is a stark difference between those two scenarios.
 
If you haven't yet, can you open a terminal and type in "lspci" which is LSPCI in lowercase. It will list your PCI devices. In that list is your wireless device if Ubuntu sees it. If you are able to copy that section down somehow or save it in a text file, then paste or upload here, we may be able to assist. Otherwise we have no idea what device you have or what driver needs to be activated or adjusted.
