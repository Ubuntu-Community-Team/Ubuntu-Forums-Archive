### Post by CharlesA on 2009-10-16
If by "full speed" you mean the max transfer rate of your connection, then you are probably using most of your bandwidth when downloading the file.

That means that any other downloads will be slower. Not as slow as you say, but I know I've had a bit of "lag" accessing net resources when I'm downloading updates. Should still be usable, but a bit slow.
