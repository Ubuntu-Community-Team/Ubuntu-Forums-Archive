This question doesn't have anything to do with with Ubuntu, but I was hoping a wise networking person would be able to help me out.  Here's the story:

It's possible this salesman has done the same to other local businesses, and I would like to inform them of their mistake.  To do this, I need a way to search websites hosted on a certain domain server.  I've found numerous sites that will do it for a fee, but have yet to find another way.  Can anyone help?
