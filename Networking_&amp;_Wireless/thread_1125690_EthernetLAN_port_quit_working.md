### Post by Bucky Ball on 2009-04-14
Something similar just happened to me. I was using an ethernet card I'd added and internet just stopped, plugged the cable into the one on the motherboard and it fixed the problem! I haven't tried swapping them back to see if that might have fixed the other one, you never know! I figure the card might have died and the port on the board seems to work.

Best way is to take the card out and put it in another machine to rule out the hardware first. If you are using a separate card like I was, you might want to try the LAN socket on the motherboard, most have one in-built nowadays and should be on the back panel (or sometimes even the front).

Do you remember exactly what you were doing on your machine when this happened?
