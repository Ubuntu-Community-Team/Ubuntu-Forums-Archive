A receiver?  For the wireless network?  Or do you simply want to extend the wired network?  Usually you need an uplink from one switch to another when tiering ethernet.. but try just plugging from one port to the other and see what happens.

As far as extending the wireless.. not unless your Access Point supports authentication rollover.. whcih is a feature in higher priced access points such as the Cisco.
