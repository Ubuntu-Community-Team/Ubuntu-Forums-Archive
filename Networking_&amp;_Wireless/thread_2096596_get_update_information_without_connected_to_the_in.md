### Post by ibjsb4 on 2013-03-07
At some point you were connected to the internet, thats the only way to get updates downloaded.  If you wish to stop this, go to your software sources>updates and uncheck it.  Then you will not receive any updates and will have to manually update.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu#Updates_Tab](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Repositories/Ubuntu#Updates_Tab)
