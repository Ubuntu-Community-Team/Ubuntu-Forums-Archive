### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-11-10
Click on the network connection icon (Network Manager) in the upper right corner and choose "Edit Connections" from the menu.

From there, you can navigate to the list of wireless networks saved and just delete the one you were trying to join.  Click OK, close all of that, then try connecting from scratch and enter the correct password.
