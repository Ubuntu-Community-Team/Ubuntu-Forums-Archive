### Post by DaveLG on 2008-08-14
I just wanted to let people know that what is said about the Airlink AWLH4130 PCI WiFi card at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCardsAirlink101](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCardsAirlink101) is true.

The card worked fine with my Ubuntu 8.04 and Atom 1.6Ghz system. Easy ser up and the restricted drivers worked fine. It was a 5 minute set-up.I got my card at Fry's for $12.95 so you can't beat the price.

Surprisingly the wired LAN on the ATOM Intel MB does not work but i  rarely need the wired connection so will investigate that later.
