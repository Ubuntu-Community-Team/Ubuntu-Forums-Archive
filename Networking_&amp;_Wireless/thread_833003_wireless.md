### Post by aj25bd10 on 2008-06-18
First, is your wireless internet functioning? There is a network icon in the system tray that you can left click on to see if there are networks available. If there are, you just click the one you want and input any type of password as needed. If no networks appear, right click on that same icon and make sure that "Enable Networking" and "Enable Wireless" are both checked. If they are but no networks appear in a spot where you know networks should be available, your wireless card is having issues. I recommend [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=830090&highlight=wireless+fix](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=830090&highlight=wireless+fix) in this case as many users have resolve their issues with it.
