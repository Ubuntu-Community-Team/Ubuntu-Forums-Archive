### Post by Paper Pusher on 2013-02-09
I am running ubuntu 12.04 LTS.
 My file browser is Nautilus 3.4.2.
 My router is a netgear n600.
 I have a windows share NAS drive Dlink 221.
 My router shows my NAS drive at location 192.168.1.111.

     When I click on "browse network" in Nautilus it does not show my NAS drive. I can manually add with the "add server" command, however it does not automatically display by itself as it used to.

 Anyone have any ideas on what could be occurring?

---

### Post by sudodus on 2013-02-09
I don't know why it stopped working like before. Maybe because of an updated package. But I think you can get it working again in Nautilus.

When you have connected to the NAS drive again, you can 'Add Bookmark' and if you wish rename it to something that is easy to identify. You should have the bookmark in the left pane of Nautilus.

After next reboot, you should be able to connect to the NAS drive again by clicking on the bookmark in Nautilus.
