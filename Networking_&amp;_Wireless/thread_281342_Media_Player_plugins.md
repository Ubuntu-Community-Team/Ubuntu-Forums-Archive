### Post by hanzomon4 on 2006-10-20
You have to install a plugin from the repos. I use the mplayerplug-in. but their are other options...
1.totem-gstreamer-firefox-plugin
2. totem-xine-firefox-plugin
3. totem-mozilla
4.vlc-plugin(?name)

I'm on edgy so the names may be a bit different, also your best bet would be to use the v.3.25 of the mplayerplug-in. 

To get v3.25 you either have to compile it yourself or I could upload the files and you manually place them in the /usr/lib/firefox/plugin dir
