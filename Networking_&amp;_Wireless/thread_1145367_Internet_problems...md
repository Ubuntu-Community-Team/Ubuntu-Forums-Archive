### Post by chili555 on 2009-05-03
> you said we could make this permanent?Sure. Open a terminal and do::```
sudo gedit /etc/rc.local
```Substitute kate or vim or your favorite text editor if you don't have gedit. Add a separate, new line just above 'Exit 0':```
ifconfig eth0 mtu 1492
```Proofread twice, save and close. Now the command will be run by the system automatically on boot.
