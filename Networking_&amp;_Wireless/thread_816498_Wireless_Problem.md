### Post by Prospero2006 on 2008-06-02
You are going to have to read up on NDISWRAPPER

Basically, the windows drivers come as .exe files that can
be unzipped.

Within those zip files is a specific 'driver' file. 

You'll probably have to invoke ndiswrapper with that file 
as an argument. 

It's complicated, but I've brought maybe 10 different types of wireless cards online in the last 2 years.
