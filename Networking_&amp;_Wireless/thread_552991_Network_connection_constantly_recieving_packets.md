### Post by Brandon.Viking on 2007-09-17
This may or may not be bad.... 
Wilreless networks send out beacon signals all the time which  you may be seeing as incoming. I find tools like Wireshark, TCPDump or IPTraf will show what is coming in on an interface and the net can tell you if they are bad or normal.
If your at all unsure unplug from the internet then just run the wireless see if your wireless interface is still receiving all those packets. Simple tests like that may also shed some light on what is going on.

Hope that helps.
