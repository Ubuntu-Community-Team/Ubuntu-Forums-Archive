### Post by djchandler on 2016-09-24
Your laptop is probably suspending when you close your lid. Go to System Settings, then select the Power icon.

Make sure it says "Do nothing" next to the label "When the lid is closed" and it says "Don't suspend" in the column right under the label "When plugged in."
