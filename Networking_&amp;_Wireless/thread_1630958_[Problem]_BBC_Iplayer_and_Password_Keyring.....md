### Post by wet-willy on 2010-11-26
What I would try first, is to reinstall keyring associated packages. If you open up System/Administration/Synaptic, click on "Installed", type "keyring" in the Quick Search field on the top right, and right click, select reinstall what shows up in the list except for network-manager-gnome, and vinagre.
Then hopefully after a reboot if necessary, you'll be set.
