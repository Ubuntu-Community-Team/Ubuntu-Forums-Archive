You are right to be careful.  When I had AT&T dsl, it came with a 2wire Model 2701 which worked quite well. But later it failed. While AT&T was sending a free replacement, I went on a search for a compatible unit. I found out that most aren't. I did find an inexpensive model at FRY's which was a Motorola unit which worked ok until the replacement arrived. It has two identifiers on it--one says Style MSTATEA and the number 2210-02-1002. I don't know which was the part number. Good Luck.
