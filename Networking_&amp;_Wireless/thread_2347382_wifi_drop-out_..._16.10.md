### Post by jeremy31 on 2016-12-24
See if disabling the firewall helps as it seems to be blocking mDNS traffic

You may also want to disable wifi power management with
```
sudo sed -i 's/wifi.powersave = 3/wifi.powersave = 2/' /etc/NetworkManager/conf.d/default-wifi-powersave-on.conf
```

Reboot
