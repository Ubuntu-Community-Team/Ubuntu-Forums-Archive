### Post by dzark on 2009-03-08
after a lot of googling, reading ancient old debian manuals in plain text (which my firefox displays as green on black for extra charm) and more reboots than id hoped that server would ever have to do......


My solution was to change the auto line at the top of /etc/network/interfaces to

```
auto lo eth0 eth1
```

and then my static eth1 code... something to do with the UDEV device renaming needing the both of them in there.. </legacy anger>
