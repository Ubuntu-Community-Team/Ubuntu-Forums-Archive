### Post by samigina on 2012-04-20
Its physically impossible to use the same wifi card to receive and to share at the same time. You can share your wifi connection through your ethernet one, or your ethernet through your wifi.

The software you quoted creates an ad-hoc network to share a LAN (ethernet conection) or a 3g.

With kubuntu its [pretty easy]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Adhoc") (same way that ubuntu)

Just create a new adhoc network, and in IPV settings, chose "Share".
