### Post by atomkarinca on 2007-11-08
right-click on the panel > hit "add to panel" > click "application launcher" on the top > at the bottom of the list, choose administration -> network. that oughtta do it.

### Post by ubukool on 2007-11-08
if the process for the network manager applet has simply stopped running for some reason, you can hit alt-F2 and type "nm-applet" without the quotes.  The applet should then re-appear :)
