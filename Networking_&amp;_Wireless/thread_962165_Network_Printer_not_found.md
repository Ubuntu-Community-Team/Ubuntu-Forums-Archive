I tried sharing a printer over samba a while back, gave me nothing but grief. In the end, I just used (k)ubuntus own settings to get it going. Assuming both systems are using kubuntu...

Have a read of this: [http://www.kubuntu.org/docs/kquickguide/C/ch02s05.html](http://www.kubuntu.org/docs/kquickguide/C/ch02s05.html) . On the computer connected to the printer, start the print server, share the printer etc. Then go through the add printer process on the second system.

I'd be able to give you more specific instructions if I was more familiar with KDE but I'm afraid you'll have to rely on your own intuition on this one, or failing that, another reply :)
