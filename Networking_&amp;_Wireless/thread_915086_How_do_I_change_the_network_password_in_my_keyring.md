### Post by cha0s on 2008-12-25
Hey, as te poster after you mentioned, go to "Passwords and Encryption Keys" (under accessories in xubuntu), then Edit->Preferences. You should be on a tab called "Password Keyrings". Click on the key named "login", and then click the "Change Unlock Password" button.
