### Post by TheFu on 2013-08-13
It looks like you are doing the right stuff. Do the cards get seen at boot?  what does **dmesg** show?  Any "unknown hardware?"

The two Intel PR0/1000 cards should definitely be known.  Could the PCIe slots be broken? Did you reseat each card?

The ... /70-persistent-net.rules is automatically managed. If there's only 1 entry, then only 1 NIC is seen.

### Post by Takahashi_NZ on 2013-08-16
Turns out it was a motherboard issue.

The mother board has 4 pci-e slots, two 16x and two 1x.

I knew one of the 16x was actually a 4x, but it turns out if you want to use it as a 4x then the two 1x are disabled.

Forcing the 4x to run at 1x allowed the two cards to be detected.

Thanks for the help anyway.
