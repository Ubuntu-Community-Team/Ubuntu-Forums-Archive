### Post by branx503 on 2007-12-23
Haven't used azureus in quite some time, and even then it was in xp.

My general experience with any torrent downloading is being bottlenecked with uploading. Try setting your upload threshold to something pretty small (a few k, for example) and see if that helps. I've always started a torrent and walked away, it's always been unbearable web browsing with any torrent going. High transfer rates (300k, etc) generally mean alot of people seeding, and hence less burden on *you* for sharing. Slower speed means less seeders and hence you have to hold up a good portion.

Also keep in mind, people don't like explicit leeching.
