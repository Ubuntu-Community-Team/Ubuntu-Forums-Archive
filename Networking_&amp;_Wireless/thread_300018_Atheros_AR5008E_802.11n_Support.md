### Post by patl on 2007-02-24
The native Linux driver for Atheros chipsets is maintained by the Madwifi project.  They have a ticket tracking this issue:

[http://madwifi.org/ticket/1001](http://madwifi.org/ticket/1001)

The executive summary is "don't hold your breath".

ndiswrapper is probably your best bet for now.
