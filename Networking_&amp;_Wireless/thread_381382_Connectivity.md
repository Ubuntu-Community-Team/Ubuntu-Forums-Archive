### Post by jml on 2007-03-10
I assume you mean you have installed Ubuntu 6.10 not 4.1.  If that is the case, since you installed without a net connection, you will need to configure and activate the card manually.  Open up your network utility by clicking on:   Desktop---->Administration---->Networking.  Highlight your network card.  Click on Properties, and fill in the appropriate information.  Then close that window.  Make sure your network card is still highlighted and click on activate.  This step may take a bit of time.  If you were successful, you should see a message that the card was activated.  Close all of the windows and you should be good to go.  Post back here with your results.
