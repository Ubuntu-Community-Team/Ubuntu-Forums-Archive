### Post by an.echte.trilingue on 2006-11-08
If the router is set up properly this should not make any difference.

To find open ports on the internet side of the router, go to grc.com and do one of his port scans.  For open ports on your side of the router, use nmap.  Of course, that probably won't help you any.  If all else fails, you can route everything over port 80.

Me thinks you should just ask the owner of the router unless you are stealing somebody's internet wifi?
