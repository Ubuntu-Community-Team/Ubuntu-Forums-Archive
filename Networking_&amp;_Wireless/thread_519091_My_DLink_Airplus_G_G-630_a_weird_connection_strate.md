### Post by RHorse on 2007-08-06
When I use the wireless app to connect to the pcmcia card (ath0), the app tells me, &Connection Failed& But is I wait half a minute it connects anyway.  Another thing: If it doesn't connect. which it doesn't on occassion, I take out, wait a few moments then put the card back in, and then it connects alright, having to wait as described above.  This second procedure works 90% of the time.  BTW, my internal Intel 2100 doesn't work though it is detected and does transmit router data. Now, I am using WEP &always& so your mileage may be better or worse depending on what (if any) encryption ur using.

One other thing.  Sometimes it disconnects by itself, usually 5 min after the first time I connect after reboot.  If I take out and replace the card, waiting a few moments for the computer to recognize the card, I can reconnect and the connection usually stays on (even for days at a time).
