### Post by Kevbert on 2008-06-13
Try an external [antenna]("http://www.amazon.co.uk/WiFi-Wireless-Network-WLAN-Antenna/dp/B000PHCZBA/ref=sr_1_2?ie=UTF8&s=electronics&qid=1213359602&sr=8-2") for the router/modem and/or wireless card.  Do you have any obstructions in the way ? (eg walls). Try line of sight, increasing the height of the antenna .  Do you have other wireless devices working at 2.4GHz nearby ? (eg cordless phones, microwave ovens).  Try moving them out of the way.  Do you see other wireless networks ?  These could be interfering with the signal (change the channel of the router).
