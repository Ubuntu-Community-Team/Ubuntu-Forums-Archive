### Post by linuxhippy on 2011-01-27
figured it out...google changed their search settings page months ago and I didnt't notice till now.  Their page will let you choose show 100 items per search, but it doesn't save these preferences if you also have (this seemed to be enabled by default on all my pcs that have ubuntu and mythbuntu on them) enable google instant checked.

So, do not enable the google instant feature in search settings and then choose 100 results per page.
