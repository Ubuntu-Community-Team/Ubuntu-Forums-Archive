### Post by jonobr on 2012-02-17
Quick solution for me was 
[URL="https://support.vonage.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/2690/~/vonage-extensions%E2%84%A2-overview"]
Using vonage extensions [/URL]
If you need vonage to receive a call then that may be an issue.

However, 

What you could do is get a small and cheap 4 port switch,
[Like this]("http://www.ebay.com/itm/NETGEAR-5port-10-100Mbps-Fast-Ethernet-Switch-Model-FS105-v2-/270912220178?pt=COMP_EN_Hubs&hash=item3f13a06c12") and run a cable from your wireless 4 port you buy to the new switch.

It frees you up to look at the 4 port models which drives the price down,.
