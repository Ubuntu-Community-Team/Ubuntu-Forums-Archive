### Post by sanderj on 2009-12-19
Apparantly your modem supports UPnP IGD ([http://laureldsl.net/pdf/D-Link%202640B%20UserManual.pdf](http://laureldsl.net/pdf/D-Link%202640B%20UserManual.pdf) ,  page 55), so you can enable it in your modem and in Vuze, and Vuze will take care of port forwarding.
