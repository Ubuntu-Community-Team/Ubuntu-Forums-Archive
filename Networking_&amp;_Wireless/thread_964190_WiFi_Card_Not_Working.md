### Post by The Minder on 2008-10-30
Problem Solved.

It seems that in my original enthusiasm to disable the wifi card I removed the restricted drivers for the card.   Naturally enough, without a driver the damn thing wasn't active.   I downloaded the restricted drivers for my Ubuntu build, rebooted and voila!
