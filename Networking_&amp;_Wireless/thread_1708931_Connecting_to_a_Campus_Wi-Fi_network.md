### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-03-17
I have noticed one little thing. It may be the wrong thing.

The encryption that you have set in the Ubuntu Wireless Network Authentication Required dialog box is WPA & WPA2 Enterprise. I compare this with the instructions in the windows XP PDF document  and I see in the box labeled Network Authentication WPA2.

Try changing the Ubuntu setting to WPA & WPA2 Personal. I am guessing that Enterprise and Personal are different forms of encryption and your setting may not match the set up of the campus network.

I hope this is the answer.

Regards.
