### Post by jedimattk on 2009-10-09
I downloaded Jaadu VNC, a remote desktop app for my iPhone, the other day. Excitedly, I used Ubuntu's built-in remote desktop utility to turn on "Allow others to view your desktop" and "Allow other users to control your desktop." I logged into Jaadu, found my computer, and connected without a hitch.

There's only one problem with my remote desktop setup thus far. I can move the cursor around, click, type, and do everything from my phone just as if I was sitting at my computer - but the display on my phone does not update. I can see it working on my computer if I'm here staring at the monitor while I work, but to see the changes reflected on my phone, I have to disconnect and reconnect in Jaadu! This is so time-consuming as to make the app almost unusable.

Does anyone else use Jaadu and know how to fix this? I'm not very experienced with remote desktop as it is and have no idea what to do from here. Thanks!
