### Post by VeeDubb on 2010-07-02
In 10 years of using linux, I have never seen a wired network card that failed to work out of the box.

If you had trouble with your old network card, it's more likely that it was defective or dieing than incompatible.  Either way, it's MUCH harder to find a network card that doesn't work than it is to find one that does.

Oh, and unless I'm very much mistaken, the chipsets you're referring to are wireless network card chipsets, which is a completely separate topic.
