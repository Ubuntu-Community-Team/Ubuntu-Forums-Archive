### Post by PurplePenguin on 2006-12-04
I'm also far from a networking expert, but my problems in the past have been due to firewalls.  I'd have to go into ZoneAlarm and explicitly tell it that 192.168.0.whatever is a trusted machine.  Not sure if this is a problem with the built-in XP firewall (I always used ZoneAlarm). :)

Ping is very helpful, too, in figuring out connection problems.  Hold tight and I'm sure a networking master will come and help you out sooner or later today... although it's a bit late at night right now in North America, it looks like our Australian friends are covering for us. :D

That said, samba is a necessity for communicating with Windows networks, so make sure it's installed as the above poster mentioned!
