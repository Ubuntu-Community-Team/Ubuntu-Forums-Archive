### Post by reubeni on 2008-03-06
Well here is a story got a atheros ar5006xs card on ebay for my laptop, downloaded the driver for winxp and tried it in XP no matter what i did it just would not work so booted into gutsy and low and behold it would not run under madwifi,restricted or ndiswrapper.
Booted back to XP and loaded a intel driver for a 2915abg ver 11.5.0.0 and would you believe it the atheros fired up and worked first shot faultlessly now i was a bit confused.
Well booted back to gutsy and loaded the intel driver with ndiswrapper and it conected straight away dont know how long it will work in this form but for now it works.
Just goes to show how many flaky drivers that are out there:confused:
