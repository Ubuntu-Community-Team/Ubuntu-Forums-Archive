I have a Lenovo U450p Ideapad. I just upgraded to 12.04 from 10.04. The wireless worked in 10.04. It gets the SSID, but never prompts for the password (WPA2) and in the gui it doesn't show the SSID as the selected "network name".

### Post by kchang on 2012-08-25
**UPDATE**
Turns out that the upgrade re-installed network-manager which screwed up wicd. Uninstalling network-manager fixed the problem.
