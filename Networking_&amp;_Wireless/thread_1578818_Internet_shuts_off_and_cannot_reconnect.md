### Post by grahammechanical on 2010-09-21
Please remember that I do not know what I am talking about but could it be that the power management settings in the BIOS are shutting the computer down? In what way are you unable to connect to the internet? Do you see the network manager icon? Two arrows pointing in opposite directions. When you left click, what do you see? Have you tried accessing the modem/router using a web browser? You put in the browser an address something like, [http://192.168.1.254](http://192.168.1.254)

If you can open a page in the modem's set-up program it could mean the failure to connect to the internet is not due to the OS but to something else. Perhaps the browser.

I do not have the answer to your problem but asking these questions might help you to solve the problem and the answers might help someone else give you the answers you need.
