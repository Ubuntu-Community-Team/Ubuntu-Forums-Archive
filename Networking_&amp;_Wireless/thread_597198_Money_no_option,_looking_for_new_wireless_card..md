### Post by SactoBob on 2007-10-30
Easy - call the nice folks at netgate.com (they support open source) and get on Engenius Wireless Bridge.  I have one of these units, and the power, range, and signal quality is incredible.  Plus, by using a bridge, you bypass all the problems with compatibility, i.e., no drivers, no worries about updates etc. [http://www.netgate.com/product_info.php?cPath=32&products_id=361](http://www.netgate.com/product_info.php?cPath=32&products_id=361)

This unit supplies only a single ethernet output.  If you want four wired outputs, try the Buffalo Ethernet Converter.  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=387871](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=387871)
