### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-11-08
There is a router setting that tells your router how to broadcast. "g" or "b" or "n" or some combination of those.  There is also an auto setting on some routers.  Karmic only worked for me when the router was set to broadcast on "b and g" not on the Auto setting,
