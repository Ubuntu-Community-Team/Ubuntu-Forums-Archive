### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-12-20
Make sure that the network you want to connect to is selected as "Connect automatically" and "Connect all users" in the wifi setup dialogue for that SSID and not for the one it connects to at the moment.

### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-12-20
Click on the network icon in the panel and go to Edit.
Now click on the Wireless connection you want to use (you will have to be connected to the SSID for it to show I think, but I'm on a cable ethernet connection at the moment and can't remember) Choose the IPv4 tab and there you should then see those two check-boxes.  My naming of the check-boxes may be incorrect but it will still be obvious.

Having set the one you want as suggested you may then need to temporarily connect to the "wrong" one and repeat the operation, but uncheck the two boxes for that SSID.
