### Post by praseodym on 2011-11-11
In 11.04 the N-mode was shut off per default because of problems, but normally it could have been enabled by:

```
echo "options iwlagn 11n_disable=0" | sudo tee /etc/modprobe.d/iwlagn.conf
```
and it worked normally then.

In 11.10 the N-mode is "on" per default and now those problems occur very often. It can be disabled by "=1" in the above command. Reboot after that.
