### Post by coffeecat on 2009-04-06
> **pointyblufish said:**
> I ran a cable from my router to my computer instead. It's probably the best long term solution,

If you don't want an ethernet cable trailing around, an even better long term solution might be ethernet over mains. [Here's a useful link](http://www.homeplug.org/kshowcase/view). They're not particularly cheap but they're reliable and easy to use. Just plug in and go. A wired connection from computer to router is better in many ways than wireless anyway, so if you're going to spend some money perhaps ethernet over mains would be a better investment than a wireless USB device.

One advantage of homeplugs is that you can just add further devices to your network one at a time, plug them in anywhere in the house, and the router sorts out the IP addressing.
