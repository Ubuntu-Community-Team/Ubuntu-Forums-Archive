### Post by Eiríkr on 2008-04-10
"Connection refused" sounds a bit like a firewall problem.  Try the following:
```
ping -c 4 192.168.1.117
smclient -NL //192.168.1.117
```
The first checks for any basic connectivity at all, and the second should list what shares are available on your .117 machine, if Samba is working properly.  Post the results here, and we can go from there.  Please also post the [global] section of your conf file.

Cheers,

Eiríkr
