### Post by mark bower on 2010-03-03
From my experience and one other post, I believe just about any dongle will work out of the box.  The dongles that I use (from 8.10 to 9.10) are the Hawking HBTC2, Cirago BTA-6210 and a cheapie "Vakoss" from Fry's Electronics for around $6.  The HBTC2 is Class 1 with 100m range (presumably).  Definitely you want to stick with a BT dongle that is supported with onboard native drivers.
