### Post by funman on 2009-05-15
Ahhh, I wasn't aware that UFW dealt with ipv6 as well.

In case anyoen else stumbles across this issue, edit /etc/detaulf/ufw and change the line "ipv6=no" to "ipv6=yes", then disable and enable ufw again, this enables ipv6 and makes ufw's settings count for both ipv4 and 6 (ufw allows pings and all that by default, but not on ipv6 unless this is enabled)
