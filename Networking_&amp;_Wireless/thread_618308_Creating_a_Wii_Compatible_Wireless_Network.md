### Post by MariusSilverwolf on 2007-11-20
Easiest way is to buy a wireless router -- NetGear, LinkSys, etc -- connect it to the desktop, access the web-based setup for the router, and have it clone the MAC address of the desktop.  When you plug in the router, it should connect same as your desktop did, and you can configure the wireless portion (with security, of course) to allow the laptop and Wii access.

It is possible, using a separate network card, to bridge two network devices within XP or Vista and hook the second connection up to a Wireless Access Point, but those aren't much cheaper than a router, and when you throw in the cost of another NIC and the time to install it, you lose out.
