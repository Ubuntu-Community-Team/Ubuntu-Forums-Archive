Hi,

After many hours of trying to get my Netgear Wireless USB stick to connect to my (Netgear) router I tried this:

```
sudo iwconfig wlan0 rate 5.5M
```

and it worked :)

I was so happy I decided to register and tell you this, in the hope that it will help :)
