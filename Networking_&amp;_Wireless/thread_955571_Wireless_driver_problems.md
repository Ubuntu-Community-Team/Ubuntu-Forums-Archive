### Post by sabinedoll on 2008-10-22
Hi, 
I am also a newbie, who just went through some problems with wireless on another brand of laptop. I feel for you!
After a simple request to google search i found this very nice web-site that may help you: [http://linuxwireless.org/en/users/Drivers/b43](http://linuxwireless.org/en/users/Drivers/b43)

If the drivers are installed and things still do not work, give this a try, it worked for me: Unistall network manager and install WICD, found here (with detailed instructions): [http://wicd.sourceforge.net/download.php](http://wicd.sourceforge.net/download.php)

Good luck! And maybe you get someone with more knowledge answering your question, hopefully.
