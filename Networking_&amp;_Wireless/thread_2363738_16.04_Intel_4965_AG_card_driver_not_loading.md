As is the case in many, many problems, the fault is explained in the log *dmesg*. You do, indeed, have some IRQ issues. First, I suggest that you make certain that the BIOS is fully updated. Second, I'd reset the BIOS to defaults. In almost every case, the super-duper go-fast changes that we make in the BIOS are actually worse than defaults.

After doing these steps, reboot and check again:```
dmesg | grep iwl
