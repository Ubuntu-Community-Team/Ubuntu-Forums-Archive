Hi there,

Sharing your internet connection (on any OS) involves teaching your machine to perform NAT. The easy way to do it with Linux involves adding a couple of rules to an iptables firewall. Try taking a look around the forum, or maybe [http://www.google.com/search?q=linux+nat+howto](http://www.google.com/search?q=linux+nat+howto).
