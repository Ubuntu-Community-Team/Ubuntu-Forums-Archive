### Post by Hadaka on 2015-12-02
Hi,the repeater..repeats what it sees so if it is not configured correctly
and sees nothing, that is exactly what it will repeat,however it needs to
be seen so it will broadcast it's ssid, which simply means its broadcast
ability is functioning ,it doesnt mean it sees the internet or your router.
leave the repeater on and unplug the power from your router to verify this.
I have 2 different types of repeaters so by trial and error have learned a couple
things along the way. please let us know the name and model of your repeater
so we may help you.
