### Post by netztier on 2008-01-06
> **cancaseiro said:**
> Oh and of course it should be easy to make port forwarding and other such rules.

These are functions of a firewall/router and not of a switch.

There are devices that can unite both sets of functions in a single box (e.g Linksys RV016) However, I suggest keeping them separate - debugging can become a lot easier.
