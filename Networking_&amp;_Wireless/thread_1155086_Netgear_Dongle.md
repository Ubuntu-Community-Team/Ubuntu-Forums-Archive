### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-05-10
I had two WG111 and the best I could do was to get intermittent service. There is a lot on this forum on this subject and you can try to configure this product and after time you may get it to work.  I gave up.

The Linksys WUSB54GC works out of the box and I have had good luck with it. However, my computer that uses the usb adaptor is a long way from the router and I get a weak signal. I found a youtube vid on the subject of the Edimax EW-7318USG (Newegg approx $25). This adaptor has an external antenna and is great for weak signals and longer range. It works great.
