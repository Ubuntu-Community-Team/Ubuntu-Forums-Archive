### Post by cwsnyder on 2012-12-19
Normally, you have 1, and only 1, wireless network card, because interference between the transmitters will block your transmissions, anyway.

One wired and one wireless could work, or two wired, but unless one can be in the 2.4GHz band only, and the other in the 5GHz band only, it is unlikely to work.

Why do you want to use two when you only need one for both purposes, anyway?
