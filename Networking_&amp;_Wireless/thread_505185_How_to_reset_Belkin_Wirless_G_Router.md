### Post by Rebeca on 2007-07-20
You just need to get a magnifying glass and look for the reset button your router has. You will need something pointy (like a pen or an untangled clip or an infected needle) and press and hold the reset button for a few minutes. Then, it will be set to the defaults (which you can look up in your router's manual). :mrgreen:
