Have you checked out this link:

It talks about an issue with the wireless cards on some dv6000 series laptops.  If your laptop falls into this category, you might be able to get it fixed through HP.

I had a problem with my wireless card and I have a dv6338se.  I was able to contact HP and told them that my laptop could not find my wireless card.  They asked me a few questions and then told me that they will send a box over to have the laptop shipped.  They ended up replacing my motherboard at no charge.  They were pretty quick about it too.  Of course, I was chicken and reinstalled Vista back on my laptop just so I would not get any hassle from them.
