### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-10-17
If you go into your Network admin panel, you can modify your dial-up setup.  And no, your dial-up setup and Ethernet setup will not interfere with one another.  One uses your Ethernet (Cable or DSL most likely) and dial-up uses your telephone line.
