### Post by t4thfavor on 2007-06-29
AOL sucks on Linux. I don't even think the current version can be made to run. even through wine. 
I know there was a few people who created a dial up client for AOL/Linux. I would do a google search on  +AOL +Linux . If you had a real dial up provider you wouldnt have this problem, but since AOL deems it nessicary to use some non-standard protocol you will be hard pressed to get it to work.

Also you may have a hard time getting a soft/winmodem to work with linux, but thats a different story altogether.

Alt: you could use the XP pro box as the router and then connect to it via a proxy, or through nat. Its a bit different to share an AOL connection through nat, but it can be done.
