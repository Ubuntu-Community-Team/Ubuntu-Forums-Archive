### Post by tuxonapogostick on 2011-06-14
Turns out I didn't search carefully within the old threads as the solution of the problem has already posted many times. See the thread [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10773296#post10773296 ]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10773296#post10773296")
So, one basically blaclists lots of rt drivers except rt2860sta, and restarts. Then wireless is working like a charm.
