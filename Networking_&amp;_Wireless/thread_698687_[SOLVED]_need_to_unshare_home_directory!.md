### Post by odinfromvalhalla on 2008-02-16
Go to Places -> Home Folder

your home folder will pop up in Nautilus. Press Up arrow. this will get Nautilus in /home folder

now just right click on your user's home and select Share Folder. In the window that pops up select Do Not Share and press ok. 

That should do the trick.
