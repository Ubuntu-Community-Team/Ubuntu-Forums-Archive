### Post by sbrbot on 2010-07-20
EDUP is just a chinese OEM name. It's important which wireless chip is used inside it. Try to find which chip is inside (Windows can help here) and after that try to find a driver for that chip. For example my EDUP wifi USB dongle is recognized by Windows as "Realtek RTL8188SU Wireless LAN 802.11n USB 2.0 Network Adapter" and it works with Realtek 8712 dirver (8712u.ko). I found my driver on Relatek ([www.realtek.com.tw](www.realtek.com.tw)) official website.
