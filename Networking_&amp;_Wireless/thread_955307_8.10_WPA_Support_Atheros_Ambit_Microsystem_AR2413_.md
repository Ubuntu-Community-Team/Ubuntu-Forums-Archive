### Post by rtmhal on 2008-11-17
I'm running a Lenovo Thinkpad T41 with an Atheros wireless card. I had 8.06 installed and did an upgrade to 8.10. After the upgrade I could not connect to my wireless network with WPA. I did some research and found an entry in the 8.10 release notes [http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/810#Atheros%20ath5k%20wireless%20driver%20not%20enabled%20by%20default](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/810#Atheros%20ath5k%20wireless%20driver%20not%20enabled%20by%20default)

I verified my network card with the command:
lspci

Next I ran this command:
  sudo apt-get install linux-backports-modules-intrepid-generic

I rebooted and then connected to the wireless network without a problem.
