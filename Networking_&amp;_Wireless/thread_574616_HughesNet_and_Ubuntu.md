### Post by bytor4232 on 2008-01-19
From what I understand, the HughesNet equipment is a lot like a cable modem.  The feed comes in and out through a satellite dish connected to a modem.  Your PC should just DHCP the IP address, or better yet, hook up a wireless router like the ones Linksys makes, and you'll be even more set.
