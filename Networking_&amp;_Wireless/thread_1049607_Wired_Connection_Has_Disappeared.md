Amazingly, my wired connection came back last night.  After four days of troubleshooting by booting into Windows 2000, a Hardy LiveCD, an Intrepid LiveCD, and the Intrepid installation on my hard drive, my wired connection started working again.

I don't know if that is related to my problem, but it seems to be the only thing that I did differently before my wired connection came back.

I have no idea why my wired connection stopped working, or why it came back.  I'm just going to hope that it doesn't stop working again in the future.
