I would buy a linksys router.  Maybe wireless depending on existing cable runs, you can move the PC anywhere within range of the WiFi signal.  You will need some wireless cards, of course.

Set up the router, which SHOULD connect without issue to your Broadband.  Plug in your PC LAN cables and you should be able to get internet on all systems.  

If you opted for wireless, be sure to enable Encryption on your WiFI or anyone within range will be able to use and abuse your network and all systems on it.  Even with a wireless router, you can still use wired connections.......still use encryption to limit connections to only known hosts.

I have this setup and it works great with 2 wireless and one wired computer.......at one point, I had 5 computers online and still no issues.
