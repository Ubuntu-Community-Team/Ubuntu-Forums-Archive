### Post by epee on 2007-05-14
I configured the printer on my XP Pro system 'shared' and then connected to the share using my FF 7.04.

Any of my Linux apps can print to this printer - no need for a printing app.

P.S. Also check out [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=2651466#post2651466"). Basically, it tells you to turn off 'bi-directional support' for your printer in Windows.
