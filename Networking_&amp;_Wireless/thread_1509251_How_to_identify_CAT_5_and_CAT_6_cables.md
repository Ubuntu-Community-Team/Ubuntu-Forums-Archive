### Post by HermanAB on 2010-06-14

### Post by ThatGuy5 on 2010-08-24
> **getyourkarthick said:**
> Can you explain me the cabling color codes..?
 
The color codes for wiring are exactly the same for both [Cat 5 Cable]("http://www.computercablestore.com/c_cat5e_bulk_cable.aspx") and Cat 6 Cable.
 
There is a wiring diagram [here]("http://www.computercablestore.com/CAT5e_CAT6_Wiring_Diagram.htm") . .  For straight through cables, you would wire both ends the same . . for a crossover cable . . wire one end 568-A and the other 568-B.
