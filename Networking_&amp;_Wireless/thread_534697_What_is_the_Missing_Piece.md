### Post by jml on 2007-08-25
The card you have "should work out of the box" with Ubuntu.   Do not configure your card with the network utility (System--->Administration--->Network)  Rather configure it with the Network Manager Applet.  Its the small icon in the upper right corner of your screen next to the speaker icon.  Right click on this icon and make sure wireless networking is checked.  Then left click on the icon and you should see the wireless network you want to connect to.  Click on the radio button next to your network, you may be asked to supply a password and then an encryption key.  Once you do that, you should be good to go.
