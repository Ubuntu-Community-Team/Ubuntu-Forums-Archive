I decided to give kubuntu netbook 10.04 a shot after running ubuntu netbook for the last week or so.  Just for fun.

I installed kubuntu last night and now I can't connect.  I put in my ssid, credentials in the wireless security section, and it will not connect. ### Post by bdws1975 on 2010-09-19
got it figured out.  I was selecting 128bit WEP and in reality my WEP is 64 bit.  So, when I was putting in my password to 128bit it was connecting as 64 and changing my passphrase.
