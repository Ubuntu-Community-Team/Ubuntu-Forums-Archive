### Post by Synflux on 2007-07-06
Hi,

If you're buying 100metres of cable then it won't be straight-through or crossover, you decide that by how you terminate the cable once you've cut it, the cable itself is the same for both.

As to how you should terminate it, most switches and routers these days will auto-detect which you have plugged in and adjust themselves accordingly but it'd still be worth checking the documentation. 

I hope that answers your question :D
