### Post by HereInOz on 2007-03-25
This is not an answer, but just to let you know that many of the Rockwell and Connexant internal HCF modem drivers will possibly work with that modem, if you come across anything using those names.  They all used a pretty similar chip.

I have found those modems notoriously unreliable, even with Windows, using the correct Windows drivers.  Perhaps it is an age thing - they are all getting a bit old now.
