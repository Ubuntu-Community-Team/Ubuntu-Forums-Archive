### Post by Tank Jr on 2010-01-31
Left click on the Network icon in the panel and click on VPN Connections>>Name of the connection you want to edit.
 
Choose your connection name and click on edit.
On the dialog that opens look at the bottom of the box, above the apply button.

Hope that helps.:)
 I am using Karmic.
