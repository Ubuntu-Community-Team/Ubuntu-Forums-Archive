You can use this tutorial as pointed out which is basically a patched madwifi solution, or as an alternative use the ath5k driver.  Both work.  The ath5k driver is completely open source, the madwifi hal layer within madwifi is not open source.  Its good to know more than one method for a solution in case one fails.  I support both solutions however am trying to educate all Atheros 242x users, or Atheros 5007eg users there is more than just one option available for your problem.
