### Post by JeQhdMD on 2014-11-30
The TP Link TL-WN722N uses an open source driver . . . in effect, there is no driver to install as the module is already included in the kernel.   In other words it's plug and play.   Amazon and other computer online retailers carry this model.    Just plug it in, and it automatically searches for wifi and displays the results when you click on the network icon in the top-right panel.
