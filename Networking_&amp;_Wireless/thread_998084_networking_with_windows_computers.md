For Windows-Ubuntu sharing, you will probably need [Samba.]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SettingUpSamba") 
 [Here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=202605") is another good How-To.  Realize that CIFS has replaced SMBFS - [this]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=288534") How-To explains how to mount CIFS shares.
