### Post by rosegarden78 on 2008-01-21
You can invisibly run network manager and/or load it into any window manager (Blackbox, Fluxbox, XFCE, KDE, GNOME) by simply typing

nm-applet

at the terminal.  This will activate your default wireless in Blackbox with Nautilus and Kicker.
