### Post by jkndrkn on 2005-05-24
I use this wireless card. By following the ndiswrappperhowto I now reliably connect to my wireless network.

[http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/SetupNdiswrapperHowto](http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/SetupNdiswrapperHowto)

You will need the driver file for your wireless adapter. It will be a file terminating in .inf buried somewhere in the bundle of files found in the driver installation package offered by the linksys official site or on your installation cd.

Let me know if you run into any problems.
