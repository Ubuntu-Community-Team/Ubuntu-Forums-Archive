im trying to connect my xbox 360 to the internet through my pc using an ethernet cable... my pc uses wifi... in windows xp i would usually just go to network connections, select the ethernet and wifi connection, and choose bridge connection and it would bridge the connect and i would be able to connect to the internet with my xbox.  however im trying to find out how to do that in ubuntu, (which i heard is very possible and easy) but i cant find that info.  can anyone help?

### Post by bigdee973 on 2009-11-28
nevermind i solved it.   all i had to do was go to network connections.   click "Auto eth0" in the "wired" tab. click "edit" go to "IPV4 settings" tab and choose "shared to other computers" in the "method" pull down menu.  then choose "available to all users" in the bottom left and then apply.  then back in the network connections main menu i choosed the "wireless" tab clicked the "auto (whatever my routers name is)" and clicked "edit" and then chose "available to all users" in the bottom
