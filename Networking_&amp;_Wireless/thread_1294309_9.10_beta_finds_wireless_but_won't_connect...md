### Post by zaphod777 on 2009-11-17
I had this same problem and installing the backports fixed my issue

```
sudo apt-get install linux-backports-modules-karmic
```

but it seams to be a problem with aht5k driver being blacklisted

or you can also try this:

1. open /etc/modprobe.d & look for "blacklist-ath_pci.conf"
2. if it is there--open the file as root & put a # in front of "blacklist ath_pci"
3. reboot & check

can't take credit for the fix though.
