### Post by queenorych on 2006-06-23
I would take the battery out of the laptop.  It sound probable that your wifi card fried.  When it frys it could create an open short, which will also help fry other components.  Does the wifi board smell of burnt carbon?  I got a mini-pci rt2500 board from newegg for $15 ($20 shipped).  I'd replace it, and move along.  All you can do with a damaged board is damage other boards.  Even if you get it working, it still may have issues

Note that your board just died.  To my knowledge there is virtually nothing you can do to fry computer components, and unless you stuck a coin in your laptop, or took it for a swim, the hardware should be fine.  NOTE I said virtually! :-)
