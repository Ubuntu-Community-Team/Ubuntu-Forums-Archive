### Post by Kraut~salat on 2009-07-21
quite a generic failure report.

- is your Wifi hardware enabled (slider switch, function keys). There ususally is a LED lit when it's active

- do you have a working driver/module? Is there a valid Wifi device in Linux? What does "iwconfig" say?

- Is your hardware capabale of unterstanding the protocol? Maybe it's 108mbit/s network, but your hardware can only to 54mbit/s.

I had the problem once that my think pad could not see a network, but my Eee PC could, because it has a newer wifi chipset.
