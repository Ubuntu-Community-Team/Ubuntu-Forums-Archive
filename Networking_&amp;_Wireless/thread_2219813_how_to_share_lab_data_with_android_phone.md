### Post by ccrs8 on 2014-04-25
This is all off the top of my head, but I know Google is helpful here (as always).  In Network Manager, in the wired connection settings (IP4 settings, I think), you can select to share the connection.  Then in the wireless settings you can select to make an ad-hoc network to connect to.  Then with your phone, connect to that ad-hoc network and you should have the internet on your phone.

I have only done this the other way around (sharing a wireless internet connection through a wire to a Ruku).
