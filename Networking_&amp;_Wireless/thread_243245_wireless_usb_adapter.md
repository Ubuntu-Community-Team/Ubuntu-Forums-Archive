### Post by secdroid on 2006-08-26
Need more info.  SMC is not, as a rule, well-supported on Linux.

[http://linux-wless.passys.nl/query_part.php?brandname=SMC]("http://linux-wless.passys.nl/query_part.php?brandname=SMC")

Get USB ID and all info on labels.  Use with search engine and on forums.

FWIW, I've been trying to find a USB WIFI for myself and it isn't that easy.  It is often easier to work backwards from what chip is well-supported in Linux, to which models have that chip, and then go buy.

Not as big a problem for other form factors than USB.

[http://linux-wless.passys.nl/query_hostif.php?hostif=USB]("http://linux-wless.passys.nl/query_hostif.php?hostif=USB")
