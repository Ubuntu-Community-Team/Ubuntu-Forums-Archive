I have a computer with a PCI wireless card with a Realtek RTL8180 chipset. I had problems in the past getting it to work in Ubuntu, but I just found a site with an open source driver that seems to work very well. 

[http://rtl-wifi.sourceforge.net/wiki/Main_Page](http://rtl-wifi.sourceforge.net/wiki/Main_Page)

If you have a RTL8180, RTL8185, RTL8187, or another Realtek wireless card, give this a try.
