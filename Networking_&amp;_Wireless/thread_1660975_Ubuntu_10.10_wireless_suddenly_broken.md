### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-01-06
If your wired connection works then we can assume that the router/modem is connecting to the ISP and the ISP is allowing access to the Internet.

What information do you see when your left and right click the Network Manager icon?

Do you see a tick mark against the lines Enable Networking and Enable wireless (right click). If not then you need to Enable both of those lines by selecting and clicking.

Regards.
