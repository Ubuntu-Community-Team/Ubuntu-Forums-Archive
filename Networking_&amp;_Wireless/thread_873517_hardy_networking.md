### Post by ModelM on 2008-07-29
If you're going to have a cable running from box to box with no switch or hub between, a regular cable won't work. You'll need a "crossover cable". There are also crossover adapters which will turn a straight cable into a crossover.

After that's sorted, you'll have your choice of NFS, Samba, or other methods. I'm sure lots of folks will jump in here with preferences & instructions.
