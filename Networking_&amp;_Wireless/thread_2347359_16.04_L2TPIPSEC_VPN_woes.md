### Post by strungoutfan78 on 2017-01-04
Well, I finally got this working.  The instructions I followed are here:
[http://amcositsupport.blogspot.com/2016/11/installing-l2tp-ipsec-client-on-ubuntu.html]("http://amcositsupport.blogspot.com/2016/11/installing-l2tp-ipsec-client-on-ubuntu.html")

I did this twice before it started working.  I kept getting an error about "The VPN service failed to start" or some such nonsense.  The fix for that was installing strongswan-plugin-openssl.  After that it was perfect!

Hope this helps somebody else.
