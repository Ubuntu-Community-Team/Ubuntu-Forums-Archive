### Post by sp0nge on 2009-03-21
Not only is it an option, I highly recommend it! 

I have a Linksys WRT54G which has the functionality of much more expensive hardware and I love it. There are some parts that could brick the device, so be careful and read the install guidance thoroughly as you do this. I was lucky enough to have 2 of them and decided to experiment with one and I haven't looked back!  

Hopefully, [this link]("http://wiki.openwrt.org/OpenWrtDocs/Hardware") will give you some good information. While this is only one version, I think there are several available- never hurts to play with them but that does increase the chances for error. Be safe and Happy hacking!
