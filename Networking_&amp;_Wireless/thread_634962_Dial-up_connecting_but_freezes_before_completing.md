### Post by rm_mn on 2007-12-10
No, the line about a malformed input line is in mine also and it connects fine.You may not have the proper DNS setup in your Networking.  Click System>Networking and click once to highlight your wired connection.  Click on the DNS tab and make sure you have the proper address in there for the DNS your ISP uses.

Now click on System>Networking tools.  Choose ping and enter the IP numbers of your ISP.  You should get a reply.  If not, you have a different problem.
