### Post by puffy1980 on 2007-02-02
Anyone know how I can change my nameserver? I can edit it in Gedit, but once I reboot it returns. Its set to 192.168.1.1 which is my modem via a wireless router, and it needs to be 203.8.183.1 so I can access all the goodies on Add/Remove. Its just a pain having to change it everytime I log on

---

### Post by kingmonkey on 2007-02-02
Its changing everytime you reboot because your router is giving it the dns by dhcp. If you change to static setup it *should* not reset after rebooting.

You can also put 203.8.183.1 into your DNS setting of your router which would also get round the problem.
