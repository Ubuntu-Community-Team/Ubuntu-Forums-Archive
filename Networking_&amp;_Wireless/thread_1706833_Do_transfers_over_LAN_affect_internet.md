### Post by doas777 on 2011-03-14
yes, but the question is will you notice?

in the simple analogy, my network is gigabit throughout but my Internet is only 22mb. so as long as I have at least 22mb of bandwidth available, then my internet can still go as fast as it can without impact. 

the reality of the situation is much harder to pin down. there are some key services that enable internet browsing (DNS for instance) that are less about bandwidth and more about query latency. if you have fast internet but slow dns servers on your isp, then in many cases, bringing up a page will seem slow, even though you have plenty of bandwidth to devote to the task.
