### Post by warren3 on 2015-07-12
Hi.

Same here.  I'm using the 7260 as well and this week its taking about 10 minutes to connect to a network and even when I connect internet speeds randomly drop off a cliff.  This seems to be a recurring theme with the 7260 and Ubuntu because its happened to me on a number of occasions.  Ubuntu really needs to sort out proper support for the 7260 because its a huge selling network card. Things seem to regress with this card i.e. an update can totally break it.

Also, Intel seems to have a number of problems with this card on the Windows side going by the number of angry customers posting on the Intel forums.

If I was you I would look around for an alternative to this card.  Today I have replaced the 7260 with a Azurewave RTL8723AE that originally came with the Brix I am using with Ubuntu.  Everything now works and its super fast.  You just simply replace the card then reconnect to your network.  You could pick up one of these off ebay for about £5 second hand.

Or you could persevere with 7260.  I'm not I am selling mine ASAP.

If you want help I would read the sticky in this forum and post the results here and hopefully someone more experienced than me and try and get your 7260 working again.

Good luck!
