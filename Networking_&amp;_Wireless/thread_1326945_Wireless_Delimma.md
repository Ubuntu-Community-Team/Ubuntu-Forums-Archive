### Post by Fir3chi3f on 2009-11-14
Usually I have found that older hardware has amazing support under just about every distro out there. Windows eventually loses support for that and as other things change within an installation of windows older (and sometimes newer) hardware tends to not always work right.

I wouldn't necessarily say that this proves either better. I can say that what works under linux usually REALLY works.  On your windows side I would suggest updating your drivers for the card. 

As you use windows and linux (not just ubuntu) you will be able to blow him out of the water with your knowledge.
