### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-05-06
I'm going to assume that you have wireless at both home and office.  What model of wireless modem is used at work?  What model of wirelss modem is used at home?  What is the price difference between the two?

For example:

Office, Cisco router, $3000
Home, Netgear router, $80

For quicker connection at home, spend $3000.

You can speed up the connection a little by setting up a static IP address on your notebook so that your router doesn't have to spend the time to assign an IP address.
