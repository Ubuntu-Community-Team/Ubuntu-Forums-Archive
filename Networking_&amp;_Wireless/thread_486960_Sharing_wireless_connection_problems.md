### Post by t4thfavor on 2007-06-28
eth0 should be up and connected to something. Thats what it means by not ready.

Is eth0 the LAN side or the WAN side?

If its on the WAN side you should check to see if you are getting an IP address from you ISP.
If its on the LAN side you need to give it an IP so that other pc's on your LAN can talk to it.

There is plenty of doc on the linux as a nat router subject. Take a few minutes to read it over, then try again.
