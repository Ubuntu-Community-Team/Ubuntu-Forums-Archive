### Post by TheFu on 2016-04-19
Can you just shutdown networking, unload the driver, reload it and then reconnect?  Shouldn't be any need to restart the system.  Rebooting is NOT usually needed on Unix systems.

My chromebook has a 7260 wifi card too.  Never drops connections, except with Netgear wifi routers.  I wasn't the only person impacted - 10 other people would get cut off too.  The netgear issue was happening before I got this chromebook.  The simple solution was to disable wifi on the router and connect an external wifi AP. Since doing that, zero drops for me or anyone else.
