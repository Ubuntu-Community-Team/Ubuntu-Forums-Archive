### Post by moyazes on 2007-11-27
I guess the best place to start is with a broadband connection and a router/gateway. As you are a beginner I would probably say it would be best if you got a router/gateway that came with inbuilt wireless and has a 4-port hub. I personally would say the Linksys range is ideal for beginners.

I am not sure how you want to connect you fedora and ubuntu pc.laptops to a LAN but if you have a 4 port hub and want to use a wired connection then you will need 2 straight through cables or a wireless device for each.

I would also say google and the ubuntu support site will help. A good place for linux info I have found is [http://www.linuxhomenetworking.com/](http://www.linuxhomenetworking.com/)

Hope this helps.
