### Post by YourArtDirector on 2011-01-07
The Luxul Wireless extenders work well. I have one in my house. Before I go to much farther though I need to tell you I am the graphic designer for Luxul.

First the one your link goes to is not as flexible as this one: 

Luxul Wireless Pro-WAV&#8482; Kits - Pro-WAV PWK-1 Board Mounted Range Extender Kit [http://bit.ly/ga6oqs](http://bit.ly/ga6oqs)

This one is easier to aim, and that's important because the antenna is directional, you aim it where you want Wi-Fi like a flashlight instead of putting it in the middle of the area like a normal AP (Access Point). The other thing that is happening is it's taking your output power and boosting up. The normal AP puts out around 100mW, the Luxul system puts out about 1000mW.

There's some technical stuff it does to make it all work better but I don't want sound like a sales person. So here's my experience with it. I have my AP sitting in the middle of my house on the second floor. I got good cover in the middle of my house but it was slow throughput at the ends of my house and nothing in my backyard. I added the kit in the link and now I have strong signal all over the house and and all the way to the back wall outside. I bet I would have connection well into the neighbors yard. It's fast too, I just wish I had a faster internet connection now.
