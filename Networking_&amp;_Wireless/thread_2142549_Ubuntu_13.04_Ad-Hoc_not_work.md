### Post by sbnwl on 2013-05-06
Which Android version the phone has? Android version above 2.3 don't support (connect to) ad-hoc networks. They support WPA Enterprise 2 networks.

Is Ubuntu 13.04 machine is not creating Hotspot? Have you turned the Wireless 'ON' before clicking on 'Use as hotspot' button in 'Network settings window'.
