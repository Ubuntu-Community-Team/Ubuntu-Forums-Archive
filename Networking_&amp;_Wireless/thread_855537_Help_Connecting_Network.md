### Post by racie on 2008-07-10
No, he means on Ubuntu.  On Windows, you can check it by right-clicking "Computer" or "My Computer" and clicking "Properties."  Or at least, that's how I do it on Vista.  On Ubuntu, your wireless card may not always be identified correctly.  For example, on Ubuntu, my wireless card was detected as an Atheros AR242, but it is actually an Atheros AR5007.  Windows will have the correct card because WiFi works on it by default.
