### Post by blazercist on 2007-05-17
The answer to your question is yes and no.

It really depends on your understanding of the word "safe".

If you are worried about your safety from viruses and malware/spyware, then yes ubuntu is safer because there are less viruses/bug spyware/malware written for firefox and other browsers under linux then there are for internet explorer.  Also even if you manage to catch one of these pesky buggers during your surfing the damage is limited by Linux's very secure permissions scheme.

On the other hand if you are worried about your personal information being intercepted as you send/receive it then the answer is no, it is the same as XP or any other operating system.  DON'T get  confused here, all I'm saying is that once you've sent information out anywhere on the internet it is no longer "safe" at least in my sense of the word.
