### Post by bkratz on 2010-02-05
> **SofaSmarties said:**
> but you have to have wireless to get the wired connection to work.



Actually you could use another computer and transfer the information necessary to yours with a usb key. But, why not see if we can get the wireless working. 

I thought I saw somewhere that the wireless for this usually worked out of the box, so first check for a switch or keystroke combination to turn it on or off.  Next see if it is on in the system bios (see the initial boot message on the method of entering the bios, it usually says something like press F12 or delete or something during boot).

third, if still "no joy" go to the terminal ( Applications>>Accessories>>Terminal) and type in
lspci
(that is LSPCI in lowercase) and either decode the output or copy/paste it back here, I'm sure someone will know about your card specifically.
