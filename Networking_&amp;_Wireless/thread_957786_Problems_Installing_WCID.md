### Post by iwc5893 on 2008-10-24
I'm assuming you mean WICD...

Take a look at this page, and follow the instructions for your distribution.

[http://wicd.sourceforge.net/download.php](http://wicd.sourceforge.net/download.php)

One thing I noticed in the instructions though, is that they list the wrong version in their instructions.

```
deb http://apt.wicd.net hardy extras
``` should read ```
deb http://apt.wicd.net version extras
``` where version is the distribution you're using in all lower case letters.
