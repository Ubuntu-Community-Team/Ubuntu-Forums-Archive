### Post by Iowan on 2009-05-24
Some modems want to "lock onto" the MAC address of their "client", and must be reset (power cycle) if a different MAC is connected.  If that MAC is your router, then no problem.  Do you currently have multiple "fixed" (ie. static) addresses, or multiple accounts? :confused: 
[Here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Internet/ConnectionSharing") is a help page for connection sharing.
