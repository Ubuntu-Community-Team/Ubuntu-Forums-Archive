### Post by gordintoronto on 2016-02-24
The answer is yes, but the "how" depends on what version of Ubuntu you are using. In Xubuntu 15.10, my Ethernet connection appears as an icon at the top-right of the screen. I can also enable the Wi-Fi from there, and have done so. The command: ifconfig
shows that both wired and wireless connections are active, and my computer actually has two IP addresses.
