### Post by st1ck3m_tux on 2008-12-21
I have a Samsung Epix SGH-i907 and would like to share how to tether with it.  Ubuntu has made it very easy!

1. Plug it into your computer, (running Ubuntu 8.10), with the USB cable that came with your Samsung Epix.
 
2. Turn on Internet Sharing on the Epix

3. Wait for Network Manager (version 0.7.0) to detect 

4. Start surfing the Internet

The Samsung Epix is from AT&T.  It works very well, but for some reason the battery will only last 1 - 2 hours.  It says that it is getting a charge from the USB port, but after a while it will shut off.  When I unplug it from the computer and try to turn it will say that the battery is low.  I have to plug it in and charge it before I can tether with it again. Not a big deal though.  Have fun!
