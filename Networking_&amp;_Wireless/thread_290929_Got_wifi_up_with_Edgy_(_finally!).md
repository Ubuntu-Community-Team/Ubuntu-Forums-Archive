I rescued an old laptop (compaq presario 700z) from the clutches of the evil Gates empire last week and I finally got my Netgear pcmcia card to work. (AR5212 802.11abg NIC). I upgraded the kernel to 2.6.18.1 and then got on madwifi.org and got down the latest source from them, compiled and installed it ( which worked well btw ) and then followed their instructions for configuring wpa on both ends. Worked like a champ, once I read all of the instructions.
