### Post by SeijiSensei on 2013-06-13
If I understand your question, do you mean the VPN connection is slower than your Internet connection without the VPN?  That's not surprising.  Your connection will be only as fast as your share of the VPN server's connection to the Internet.  If there are a lot of people sharing the server, it could easily be considerably slower than your native connection speed, or the VPN provider may be using bandwidth limits on each connection.
