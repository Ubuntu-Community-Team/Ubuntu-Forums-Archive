I made this discovery at the weekend whilst trying to get my phone to work as a modem for my ubuntu laptop.

There is an option in the settings - usb - usb internet menu. if you choose turn on, when you connect the phone via usb and choose phone mode, the pc should connect to the internet.

This works with ubuntu 7.10 and 8.04
