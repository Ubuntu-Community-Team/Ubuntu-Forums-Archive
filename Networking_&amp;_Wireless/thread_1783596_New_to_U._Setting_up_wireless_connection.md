### Post by ratcheer on 2011-06-16
Click on the network indicator at the top right of your screen. If it thinks you are connected, the indicator should look like an up arrow and a down arrow. In the menu that drops down, click Connection Information. That should tell you for sure about your connection.

If you do have a wireless connection but it is not working, click Edit Connections from the same menu. This brings up a Network Connections dialog. Click the wireless tab. Then select your wireless connection (often something like wlan0) and then the Edit button. Maybe you need to change something such as the SSID or your wireless security info to get it to work. Based on your wireless router setup, only you would know what to change.
