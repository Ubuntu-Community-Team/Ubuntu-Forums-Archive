### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-07-20
I suppose you could look up the wireless card model in Windows and post it here. It would help us help you.
Generally speaking, many wireless drivers are license protected are or restricted in one way or another, which prevents them from working out of the box in Ubuntu. If possible, plug in an ethernet cable to get a wired connection, then open System->Admin->Hardware-Drivers. If available, the utility will download and install the driver for your card.
