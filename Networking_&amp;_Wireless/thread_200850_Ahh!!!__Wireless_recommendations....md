### Post by tehwa on 2006-06-20
I use a Netgear WG311T (pci). They selll for about $65 AU, or about $50 US.

Sometimes, it is setup automatically during install, if not, it's a matter of connecting via ethernet after install and installing 'linux-restricted-modules-{uname -r}' which gives me sweet sweet wireless love instantly.

Very good Ubuntu experience with this card.

I'm using an Orinoco Silver 802.11b PCMCIA (part number 012938/G) card on a Dell laptop, and I didn't have to do anything other than insert the card. Ubuntu picked it up, loaded the drivers, started the services and found my router.

It looks like they go for ~$30 on eBay, just do a web search for that p/n or "orinoco silver" and you'll find several.
