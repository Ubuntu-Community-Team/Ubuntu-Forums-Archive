### Post by zephyrus54 on 2006-12-03
The problem seems to have been with firefox...I replaced firefox with swiftfox(a linux-optimized version of firefox), and my browsing speeds have dramatically increased.  I had some installation problems with swiftfox initially, as it kept crashing whenever I tried to browse, but upon complete removal of firefox through synaptic and installation of swiftfox and its plugins through automatix 2 everything seems to be in working order.  My browser settings and bookmarks even remained the same (I suppose some firefox folders weren't totally removed).
