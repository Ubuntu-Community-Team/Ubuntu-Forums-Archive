### Post by robertc36 on 2007-08-07
I have the DG834gt and it works great no cut off problems which i had a lot in windows.
Can keep mine on for weeks and no problems.
I do not use the wireless function as the speeds are always slower than being hard wired.
I have freinds who have the same router and use wireless.
It took them a while to get wireless adaptors working.
Its the wireless adaptor that will give you problems not the router itself.
It is always a good idea to find out which routers your ISP supports.
