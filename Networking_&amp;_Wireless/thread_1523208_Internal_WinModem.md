### Post by Bernie Gallagher on 2010-07-03
Ach! Even a n00b like me knows you can't use a Winmodem with Linux. With a Winmodem, the processing normally performed by the modem's firmware is performed by the CPU as a Windoze process, hence the name. Even with Windoze, you don't want a Winmodem because they gobble up your CPU.
