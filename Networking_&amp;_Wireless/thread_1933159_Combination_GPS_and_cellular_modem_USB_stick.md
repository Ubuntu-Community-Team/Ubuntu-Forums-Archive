I'm considering doing some remote data collection, and
it would be really nice to have a single USB device that
provided both a cellular modem and a GPS receiver.

I suspect devices like this will emulate two separate tty's
over usb (like [http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Qualcomm_Gobi_2000](http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Qualcomm_Gobi_2000) ).

Does anybody know of any that work with Ubuntu?

One candidate is the USB760, which goes for $70 or so.
Lots of people have reported that the USB760 cellular modem
works well with Ubuntu; separately, there are many reports that
it has a GPS unit, but I haven't seen any reports of people using
its GPS with Ubuntu.
