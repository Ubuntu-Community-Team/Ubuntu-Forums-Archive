### Post by x64Jimbo on 2006-10-19
Lacking an IP from your ISP and lacking an IP from your router are two very different things. It seems that you are having trouble getting your wireless network configured, but that has nothing to do with your ISP. Go into your router configuration page, and make sure that your connection options match those that you are using in your wifi card.
