### Post by MaindotC on 2010-09-17
An access point (what you call a router) may require a reboot - it is a computing device just like the machine on which you are running Ubuntu, albeit a much more stripped down version.  If you encounter future errors you may want to consult your manufacturer's website for a firmware upgrade of the access point.  Hopefully you won't experience any further problems and you can continue enjoying the benefits of using Ubuntu.
