### Post by elcasey on 2007-09-04
If the new MacBooks still use the Airport Extreme (like my G4 iBook does), there are loads of tutorials on how to get it working. Don't expect too much (WPA connectivity, etc) out of it once you get it working. And you'll need to plug a patch cable into your MacBook in order to download the necessary files, drivers, etc. in order to get the Airport card working.
