### Post by chili555 on 2011-09-19
As you have found out, Windows drivers (and, incidentally, viruses and malware) don't work in Linux and vice versa. Let's find out what your device is, exactly, and find a driver for it. Please open Applications > Accessories > Terminal and run, with the device inserted, and post:```
lsusb
```Thanks.
