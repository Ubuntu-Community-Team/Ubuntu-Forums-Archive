### Post by pricetech on 2009-06-01
You'll see "connected computers" even after they disconnect.  That's not abnormal.  Your router simply "remembers" the computers that connected to it, asked it for an IP, requested routing to the Internet, etc.

As far as another wireless in the area, just scan for additional networks on your computer.  If you see any, especially with a strong signal, there might be some interference causing your speed to drop.
