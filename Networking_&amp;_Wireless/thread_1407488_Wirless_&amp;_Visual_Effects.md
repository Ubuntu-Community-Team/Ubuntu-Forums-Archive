### Post by gordintoronto on 2010-02-15
According to this web page, the wireless on that computer should "just work":
[http://www.linlap.com/wiki/hp+pavilion+dv4-1100](http://www.linlap.com/wiki/hp+pavilion+dv4-1100) 

At the top-right of your screen, next to the volume control icon, there should be a network manager icon.  If you right-click on it, select edit connections, wireless, add, then you need your router's ID, encryption type and password.

If you don't have the network manager icon, you might open Accessories/Terminal, enter the command lspci, and look for the exact brand and model of your wireless adapter.  Then you can pump that into Google along with the word "linux".
