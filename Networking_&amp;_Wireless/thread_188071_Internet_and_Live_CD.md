Try turning off ipv6 through firefox:

In the firefox address field:

**about:config**

Then in the **Filter:** field:

**ipv6**

Then double click on the one & only line left visible in the big window to change it's boolean value to **true**.

That will very likely do it for you, it does it for me... :KS
