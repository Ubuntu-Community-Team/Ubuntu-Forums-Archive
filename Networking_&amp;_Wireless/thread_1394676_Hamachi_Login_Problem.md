### Post by geofurtado on 2010-01-30
After installing UFW, Moblock and Squid proxy server, I was unable to logon to Hamachi.  Moblock was blocking the Hamachi IP addresses for the logon procedure [24.xxx.xxx.xxx|.

To determine the addresses that are being blocked, you need to bring up Mobloquer.  You can observe the blocked addresses [24.xxx.xxx.xxx] as you attempt to logon from a terminal screen.

Hope this helps someone....
