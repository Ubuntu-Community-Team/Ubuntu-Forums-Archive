### Post by sgosnell on 2009-12-04
Your download speed is likely a factor determined by your ISP, and has nothing to do with your OS.  A slow internet connection can't be sped up by hardware on the consumer end.  You might be able to upgrade to a faster connection if you talk to your ISP.  I know that ATT DSL is very slow with the basic plan, but you can double the speed for $5/month.
