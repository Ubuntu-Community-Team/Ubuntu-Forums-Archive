### Post by greenstar on 2005-09-24
Click on System menu/ Administration/ Networking

Select the modem tab, then select Modem connection,

then select Properties and fill in the appropriate info,

click OK,

then back at the Connections tab, select Modem connections and then click Activate and OK.

Then try dialing up
