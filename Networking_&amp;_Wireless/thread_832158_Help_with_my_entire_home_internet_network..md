### Post by lisati on 2008-06-17
I used to share my desktop's internet connection with my laptop through a wifi device connected to the desktop, and found that my laptop's ability to connect depended on how busy the desktop was. My solution was to buy a separate router that connects to my desktop by cable, to my laptop by wifi, and direct to the ADSL modem/router by cable, thus freeing the desktop for other duties.
