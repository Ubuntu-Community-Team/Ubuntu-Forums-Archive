### Post by 47_MasoN_47 on 2008-06-04
Signal strength effects the speed and bandwidth of your connection.  I've used them with 10% connectivity before and it generally works decent if you are just browsing the Internet.  Anything above 40% is fine for downloading from the Internet IMO.  If you are transferring large files across your home network then I'd say get a wireless N router or one with high range.
