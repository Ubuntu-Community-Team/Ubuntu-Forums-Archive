### Post by spd106 on 2007-07-10
You might want to look at the OpenWRT project for lots of information about getting GNU/Linux to run as an AP. They have made a great deal of progress over the last few years and hack on much of the same code as the wireless-dev tree developers.
[http://openwrt.org/](http://openwrt.org/)

Also have a browse through the Linux Wireless Wiki, that's were the main focus of future development is taking place.
[http://linuxwireless.org/](http://linuxwireless.org/)

Don't forget to check out their mailing lists too.
