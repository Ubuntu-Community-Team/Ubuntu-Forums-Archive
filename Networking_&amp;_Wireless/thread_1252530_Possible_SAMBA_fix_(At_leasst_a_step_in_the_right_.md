So I've been trying to get over my samba woes by endlessly searching the internets only to come up misdirected and empty handed.  In my vain attempt to be a jack of all trades I may have stumbled across something to assist you all in your quest for easy file sharing from a Windows XP "server".

Enable port 445 on your "server's" firewall to accept incoming connections on you local network.  That's it.

No configuring of config files and no work arounds or searches involving dead or "solved" threads that do less than help you but more importantly frustrate you instead.  Please post and let everyone know if this works for you.
