Greetings!

In a dual-boot Windows Vista/Ubuntu 8.10 setup with a Realtek internal usb wireless card, Windows will occasionally forget that the card even exists.  This tends to happen when the user restarts Ubuntu rather than shutting it down.  The short term fix is to boot into ubuntu and shut it down properly do _**not**_ restart it.  Upon a fresh boot, Windows Vista will recognize the card as usual.

Hopefully this helps some of my fellow sufferers in the world of Gateway dual-booting :D
