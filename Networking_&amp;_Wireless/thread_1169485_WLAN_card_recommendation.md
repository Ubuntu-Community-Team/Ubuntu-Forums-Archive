### Post by sedawk on 2009-05-25
Does the box have USB?

USB sticks are easier to use and with an USB 2.0 "extension cord" of 3 m
you have more flexibility of finding a place with better link quality.

It should also be much easier to find compatible devices.
E.g. check some online dealer with customer reviews and look
for a linux customer. Many people might be happy to give you one
of their old sticks they used with their old laptop or ...

### Post by steeleyuk on 2009-05-25
Edimax EW7128G. Uses a Ralink chipset and uses the rt1pci driver which is open source. Supports WPA and WPA2.
