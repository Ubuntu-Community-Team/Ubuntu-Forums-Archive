### Post by rattlehead on 2010-07-30
I don't want any of you to think I jumped the gun... I spent about 2 hours trying to solve this myself before posting for help. But after posting, I fixed it in a really unusual way. Maybe it will fix other people problems, too? 

I noticed that the problem concerned both the wireless and wired connections, and the problem persisted, even when running from the Ubuntu liveCD.

With my laptop off, I went into my router control panel, and manually removed the laptop's entry from the client list. Rebooted the laptop, and it connected fine the first time.

I have absolutely no idea what that has to do with anything or why it helped, but it absolutely did.
