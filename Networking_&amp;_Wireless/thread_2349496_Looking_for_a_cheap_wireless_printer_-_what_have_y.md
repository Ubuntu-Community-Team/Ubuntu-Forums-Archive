### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-01-15
HP are usually easy to setup using wifi, are very well supported, and I have used them for many years now.
In fact, it is a long time since I have had any problem with an HP printer or MFP.
You can find out about printer models and their Linux support at [http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html)

Make sure hplip is installed, then also add hplip-gui and you will find a terrific setup and management GUI application that sits in the system-tray till you need to use it.
