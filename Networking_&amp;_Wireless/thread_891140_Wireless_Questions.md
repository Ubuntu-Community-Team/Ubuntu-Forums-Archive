### Post by Crafty Kisses on 2008-08-15
First, look and see if those wireless cards are supported > [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported)

Secondly they are not hard at all to install, and if your card is supported it's really easy, but if you have a unsupported card in Linux it can be a pain to setup (software/driver) wise.

Installing it in your computer is very simple, hopefully this has helped.
