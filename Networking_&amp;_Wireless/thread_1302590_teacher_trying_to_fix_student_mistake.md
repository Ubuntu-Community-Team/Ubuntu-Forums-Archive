### Post by i.r.id10t on 2009-10-27
Most laptops have a hardware switch to turn the radio(s) on/off - look for a blue key/fn key with a tower/antenna on it, or a switch on the bottom or front or back of the laptop case.  Here's a page that mentions it (towards the bottom) - [http://www.insidehw.com/Reviews/Notebooks/Lenovo-IdeaPad-S10e.html](http://www.insidehw.com/Reviews/Notebooks/Lenovo-IdeaPad-S10e.html)
