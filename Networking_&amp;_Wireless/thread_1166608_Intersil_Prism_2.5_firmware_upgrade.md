I Just wanted to post this. I spent several hours on the intertubes trying to get my wife's HP pavilion working. The solution was to flash the firmware from 1.49 to 1.82 using this procedure:
[http://linux.junsun.net/intersil-prism/](http://linux.junsun.net/intersil-prism/)

The only reason I'm reiterating this is that some posts I saw mentioned compiling hostap with download support. However, the version supplied with Jaunty has all of the tools needed (you may have to download hostap-util too). 

Simply grab the firmware files:
[http://linux.junsun.net/intersil-prism/firmware/](http://linux.junsun.net/intersil-prism/firmware/)

and follow the linux instructions at the bottom of Jun Sun's howto. 

Hope this helps!
