### Post by ABCC on 2007-04-23
I remember looking in to a similar set up a few years ago but never really got out of the starting blocks. The arrival of the Adblock plugin for firefox and the accompanying filterset updater have made my life and setup much easier:

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1136](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1136)
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/10](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/10)

Another setup i have used is privoxy, which is available in from repos. I found it a bit trickier to set up though.

Both of these options have the benefit of being maintenance free after setting them up, as opposed to copying swathes of data into /etc/hosts on a regular basis to keep abreast of ad-developments. I dont want to discourage you from using edexter, but i reckon there are more user friendly ways of solving the ad problem.

Regards,

ABCC
