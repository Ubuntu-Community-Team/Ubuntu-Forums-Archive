### Post by jonallport on 2007-05-16
If they're sitting behind you they can see everything you're doing - scary or what?

Seriously, it all depends on the network configuration, i.e. encryption on the WiFi network, gateway/proxy configuration.

At one end of the scale, if the network is well encrypted and the admins don't care too much about logging then there's not much that will be recorded of your nefarious activities.

At the other end, if the WiFi is open, the routers have full logging enabled and there is a caching proxy on all of the important ports then pretty much anything you send/receive/think is visible to anyone with a signal and is recorded verbatim for the benefit of the admins - panic!
