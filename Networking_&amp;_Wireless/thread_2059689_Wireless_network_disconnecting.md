### Post by ShadowVegan on 2012-09-18
Probably a weak connection. I tether my phone to my laptop for Internet (high speed isn't available here) and I have the same issue, always disconnecting, sometimes automatically reconnecting right away. In my case I have good signal strength on my phone but I think my cord I use to tether is faulty, but I assume a weak signal could do the same thing. If you are connected directly to a modem try using a new Ethernet cable. If you are using a wireless network, make sure your router is placed a few feet away from your modem, and not tucked away in a corner, and facing away from walls, and have your computer relatively close to the router (not the opposite part of the house).
