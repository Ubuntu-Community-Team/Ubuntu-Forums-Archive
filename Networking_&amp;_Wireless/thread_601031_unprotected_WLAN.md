### Post by lyndaj70 on 2007-11-03
Hook an ethernet cable to your router and open a browser.  In general the ip addy is 192.168.1.1.  You will have to know the username or password.... If you haven't changed them then they will be the default ones.... You can google the make and model to find those :)

There will be a window where you can see who is on your system.

If you are running without security, then you are basically inviting people to use your internet.  Security is not that hard to install, just use WEP encryption and write down the hex number.  You type it in once on the systems you allow online and you're through.  

There is a legal precedent for folks getting arrested for using unencrypted wifi without permission, but IMHO if you leave the gate open, you have no right to complain if someone cuts across your yard.  Just close the gate and lock it.

Take care!
