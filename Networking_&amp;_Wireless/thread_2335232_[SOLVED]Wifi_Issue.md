```

```
solves my problem.

### Post by kate.t on 2016-08-27
I'm a noob who had a really annoying problem with wi-fi constantly going out, and I found a website with methodical, step-by-step instructions for what to try if you have wireless problems.

[https://sites.google.com/site/easylinuxtipsproject/internet](https://sites.google.com/site/easylinuxtipsproject/internet)

On the left-hand side of the page are solutions for when you can't get any wireless connection at all, and on the right-hand side is a list of things to try if you have intermittent problems, which was my situation. I tried the first two suggestions on the right side of the page, and everything's fine now. 

Perhaps this will be helpful for you, too, or for someone else who checks out this thread.

Good luck!
