### Post by Scott Baker on 2011-11-29
Howdy all. I've seen these same issues with Verizons FIOS and Time-Warners high speed internet set-ups. Some hinkey behavior of two or more comps on the same router. Make sure that you "INSTALL" UBUNTU from a wired connection, and that you check "install updates" box as you're preparing to install. 24 hours after the initial install, recheck for updates through the update manager. It's AMAZING how many updates need to be done on that second pass. That will normally correct the connection issues.
