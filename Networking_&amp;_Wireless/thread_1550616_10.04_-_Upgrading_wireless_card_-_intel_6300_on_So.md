### Post by Pdavid on 2010-08-11
Solved it by

1. Removing the linux-backports-modules-wireless-lucid-generic package and associated (e.g. linux-backports-modules-wireless-2.6.32-24-generic)

2. Editing /etc/udev/rules.d/70-persistent-net.rules and commenting out the entry for the old card:

```
# PCI device 0x168c:0x002b (ath9k)
#SUBSYSTEM=="net", ACTION=="add", DRIVERS=="?*", ATTR{address}=="2c:81:58:f5:6b:52", ATTR{dev_id}=="0x0", ATTR{type}=="1", KERNEL=="wlan*", NAME="wlan0"

```

and change the NAME field of the new one from wlan1 to wlan0

The new card now works as intended; much faster connection speeds. I suppose the thread should be tagged as solved now?
