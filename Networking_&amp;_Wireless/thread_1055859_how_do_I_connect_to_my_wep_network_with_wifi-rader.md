### Post by Another Monkey on 2009-01-31
Right click on NetworkManager (should be a little network icon in the upper right corner); edit; add a new wireless entry, giving it your various credentials.

Or you can left click and select the network or even connect to a wireless not doing SSID broadcast.  If NetworkManager needs a passkey, it should ask for one.

NB: WEP is not secure (just ask TK Maxx :P), you might want to consider moving to WPA.
