### Post by 1roxtar on 2010-06-22
I just installed a DYNEX DX-WGDTC wireless G card tonight on an older Dell Optiplex GX150 running Lubuntu 10.04.  I couldn't get my computer to detect the card until after a bit of help from Google, I found out it uses Broadcom drivers.  

Using a wired connection, I opened up Synaptic and typed "broadcom" in the search box and checked the box beside "bcmwl-modaliases" and clicked Apply.  

I did a reboot and after a few seconds, my hardware was detected.  It showed me that it required me activating the Broadcom drivers, so I clicked Activate.  

One more reboot and I picked up a wireless signal.  :guitar:
