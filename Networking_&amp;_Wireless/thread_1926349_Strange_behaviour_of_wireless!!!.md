### Post by corrado33 on 2012-02-17
When you turn the router on in the morning, is everything else reset as well?  If you had any ports forwarded are those still there?  Are any of YOUR settings on the router when you turn it on in the morning?  
 
Not that this would fix it, but your router probably has a "backup" feature that allows you to download a file with the settings on your router.  This would make getting the wireless working quicker as you'd just have to load the file and restart the router.
