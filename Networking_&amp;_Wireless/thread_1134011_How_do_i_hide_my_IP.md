### Post by lukjad on 2009-04-23
Well, there are a few ways of hiding your IP address. Basically, you can use a proxy, basically connecting to another computer and use it to browse the Internet and relay the information to you. There are various websites that will do this for you. There is also TOR that is popular. The thing to be wary of is that even if you use these proxies, you have to trust them not to look at what you are doing, or to give it to someone else.
