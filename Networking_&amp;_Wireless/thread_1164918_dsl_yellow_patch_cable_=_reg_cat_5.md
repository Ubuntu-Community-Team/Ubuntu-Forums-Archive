### Post by lisati on 2009-05-20
The colour doesn't really mean much beyond what the manufacturer and/or supplier decides. 
If you're lucky the router or similar device will auto-detect what kind of cable you're using, which will make the distinction of cossover/direct largely academic.

I have assorted cables in my collection, one yellow, three blue (two of which I bought new for $2 each), two dark blue and one black - my routers are comfortable using them interchangably without complaining.
