### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-12-16
Try adding **kubuntu-desktop** with aptitude which will pull in several more programs and configuration options than plain kde and may stop the beeping.  Use aptitude, not apt-get or synaptic, as if you decide you don't like it you can more easily remove it.  It may be that your wireless adapter is causing the beeps if it can't pick up the network, but I'm not at all certain as I am wired, not wireless.
