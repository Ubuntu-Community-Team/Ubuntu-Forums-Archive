### Post by makitso on 2024-02-22
I assume your using a wifi connection right?  
Most connection issues are caused by a poor or weak wifi connection.  
Are you using the 2.4 or 5GHz channel?
Is there channel contension going on, lots of neighbors on 2.4?
Get an Android  wifi signal app, if 2.4GHz is below -50dBm the connection may drop.
As a test, switch to 5GHZ channel and move the laptop close to your Wi-Fi router and see if the problem exists.
