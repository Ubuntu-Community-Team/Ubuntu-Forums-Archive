### Post by matthewboh on 2007-06-15
Can you get to these sites on another PC on the same network?  If so, you have a problem on your Ubuntu machine.  If you can't - you might have some kind of blocking in place for your firewall.

If you can get to the sites on another computer, check your DNS servers.   You find that under System | Administration | Network and then select the DNS tab.  The servers should come from your ISP - check with them to get the IP addresses.
