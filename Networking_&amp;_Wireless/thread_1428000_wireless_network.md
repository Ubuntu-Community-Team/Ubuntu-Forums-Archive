### Post by Naggobot on 2010-03-12
If you mean a wireless network where individual computers work as access points while participating to the network then to my best knowledge such network is called ad-hoc. 

see

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wireless_ad_hoc_network](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wireless_ad_hoc_network)

to check that this is what you want and then you can find a quide on how to set it up from 

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Adhoc](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Adhoc)

Good luck with your endeavors!  I would though recommend that if it is even remotely in your budget then a wireless router will accomplish the same and your wireless connection is available for other computers even if the computer with Internet access is shut down + router is pretty much plug and play. This is of course my personal and very subjective opinion.
