### Post by MaindotC on 2010-08-31
Ok, well it sounds like either the network card is not supported, suffered some damage, or the driver may not be compiled/configured properly.  Please follow the [Ubuntu Wireless Troubleshooting Guide](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessTroubleShootingGuide) and tell us at what point you are having difficulty in the troubleshooting.  You'll probably want to start at the "Connection Established, Problems Persist" section.  Also check the [Ubuntu Supported Wireless Cards](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported) list and see if your card has not yet been tested with Ubuntu.  If not there are several cost-effective options.
