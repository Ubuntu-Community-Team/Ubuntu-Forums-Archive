### Post by kinghippy on 2007-11-16
Hey, I just wanted to share a success story. I have had so much trouble trying to get my wireless card to work. I ended up giving up on the linksys and the Netgear cards, but finally had success with a Verizon EV-DO card, which is better than wireless, since it's available nationwide. Anywhere I go, my laptop works now. I had a little trouble activating the card (I had to activate it with a windows computer) but once the card was activated it was a pretty simple matter of just changing the settings in the KPPP program. There were some instructions that involved the terminal, but as a relative newb, I thought the KPPP method was a lot easier, and only took 2 minutes to configure. So if anyone else is at their wits end when it comes to wireless, maybe give the verizon card a try. I'm using a USB720 card, but they also have one that goes through a pci slot
