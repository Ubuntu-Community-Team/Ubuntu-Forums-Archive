### Post by elcasey on 2007-05-19
"Nothing happens" is really not enough information to troubleshoot the problem. ;)

But, depending on the chipset of your network card, Ubuntu probably doesn't have drivers for it installed by default. If you found a howto, you should follow it and see if that fixes the problem. If not, further troubleshooting is necessary.

Driver installs are rarely a simple affair in GNU/Linux, especially for new users. But simply being unable to connect to your network coupled with that Linksys card leads to me have a strong suspicion that you need to install the drivers for your card (just like you would have to do in Windows).
