### Post by Jouke74 on 2007-05-01
I asked myself this question when my old Asus Wl-138g with Marvell chip set was starting to give me problems when upgrading to Feisty. So i started checking Internet.... 

I came to many conclusions:
1. NO wireless card is a sure bet because manufacturers of cards use different chip sets on the same brand of cards. 

2. You can better look at which chip set is on the wireless card. 

3. The wifi card which is an ideal choice for the current Ubuntu release or any distro realease my not be the best choice for the next release

I hope that answers your question.
