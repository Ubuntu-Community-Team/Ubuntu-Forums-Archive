### Post by the-saint on 2014-02-24
Issue solved...

I had one hunch that the connecting to the wireless during the usb install may be a culprit, so this time I did not connect to the internet while doing the re-install and had it install only with the USB. 
Once installed I was able to connect to the wifi internet with no issues
