### Post by grahammechanical on 2010-11-08
Is the wireless connection set to connect automatically? Right click the network icon. Select Edit connections. Select your wireless connection and put a tick against Connect automatically.

Remember that this will work when you reboot. Make sure that wireless is switched on by any keyboard key press that may be required before the Operating system loads otherwise it will not notice that you have wireless features.

Regards.
