### Post by praseodym on 2011-09-05
Hi,

there are 4 (in words: four) networks with the same ESSID listed. Take the MAC-adress of the one you "really" want to connect to and put this address into the network-manager applet in the field "BSSID". Example:

```
Cell 01 - Address: [COLOR="Red"]00:24:6C:C4:7F:20[/COLOR]
ESSID:"tuftswireless"
```
I recommend changing the encryption mode from "none" to WPA2-AES in your router interface if possible.

Edith says: Otherwise the network-manager wants to connect to any of these networks, which causes breakdowns.
