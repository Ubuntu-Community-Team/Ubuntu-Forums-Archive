### Post by ivanovnegro on 2011-05-13
You have to assure that your machine can boot from CD/DVD, you can set this in the BIOS.
Restart machine and then click ESC or F2 or what corresponds on your machine to the BIOS and there you can change the boot order but attention, the BIOS is an essential thing of your system, dont play with things if you are not sure, only change carefully the boot options.

### Post by krapp on 2011-05-13
Do a Google search for how to boot from CD/DVD with your computer model. Same instructions will apply to all CDs/DVDs.

It's usually just a question of hitting a key like ESC when the screen first comes on (mine says "ASUS") and selecting with your arrow keys to boot from your optical drive.
