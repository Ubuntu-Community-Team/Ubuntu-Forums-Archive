### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-10-27
FYI, "dual band" isn't the same thing as "Wireless N."  Many "Wireless N" routers are only single-band, 2.4GHZ (but still faster than G).  "Dual band" adds a second 5GHZ radio in addition to the 2.4GHZ radio.
