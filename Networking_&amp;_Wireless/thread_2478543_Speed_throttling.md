### Post by TheFu on 2022-08-30
Of course they do.
All ISPs do.
Total bandwidth is a shared resource, so they must. It is not infinite.

Servers also throttle, to be fair to as many client connections as possible.  For a long time, servers limited the speed they'd send data to 1 Mbps.  For huge downloads, they often limit it to 500 Kbps, but it depends on the service.
