Update:
I purchased 2 Trendnet TBW105UB USB Bluetooth adapters.  Just plain works both here and at home under Edubuntu Lucid.

I installed bluetooth from Synaptic here and both bluetooth and blueman at home.  Don't see anything that makes me like one over the other, they just have a different interface from one another.
