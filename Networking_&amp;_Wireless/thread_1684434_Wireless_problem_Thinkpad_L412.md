### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-02-09
If you can see wireless networks under the line Available when you left click the icon, then your wireless adapter is working. Is your wireless network on that list? What happens when you select it? Network manager should try to make a connection and then ask you to authenticate the connection. Does it do this?

If a wireless network in the list has a padlock sign against it, then this network will require a password to connect to it. If you do not have the password then you will not be able to connect.

Regards.
