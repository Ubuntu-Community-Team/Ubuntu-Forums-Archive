Hmm, I have never used the NetGear anything before. You say that Xubuntu reconizes it though?

Perhaps you need to change some of the settings in how it authenticates or something. Because it is finding it, just not connecting to the internet, you could go and change stuff one bit at a time. Since it is a USB, it might need to change the number in the mobil broadband part. Does it have a # followed by a number? My Virgin Mobilebroadband2go is of course not able to link up to the net if that number is gone or changed. (777 for these devices I believe, your's will be different.)

Go to network connections, go to your device, click edit, (remember not to change anything and not remember how to set it back in case.) hit the tab that says mobile broadband, and you will see something that says username and password, keep those blank, right above it, it should say number. Do you have a number or no?
