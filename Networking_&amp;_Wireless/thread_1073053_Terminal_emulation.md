### Post by cariboo on 2009-02-18
You seem to have a few questions about Linux, I would suggest you either get a free liveCD from [here]("http://shipit.ubuntu.com/"), or download an iso from [here]("http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download"), and give it a spin without affecting your current operating system.

Once you have booted from the liveCD go to System-->Administration-->Synaptic Package Manager, and check out the over 30,000 packages that are available, for free. You should find more than you really need.
