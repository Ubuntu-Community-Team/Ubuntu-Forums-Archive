### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-10-20
I'm not sure, but 8.10 should be using the b43 driver for Broadcom cards.  What is the output of:
```

lspci -nn
lshw -C Network
uname -m
dmesg | grep -e wlan -e b43 -e bcm -e ndis
```

Also, did you upgrade from 8.04, or is this a fresh install?  If you upgraded from 8.04, there's a good chance that the upgrade may have broken your wireless, depending on how it was initially set up.  It's probably not too hard to fix, however.
