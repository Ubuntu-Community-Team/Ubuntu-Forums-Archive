Well, that would depend on the wireless that your are talking about.

You are probably talking about Wifi, as in you would need a wireless router to connect to.  You can also connect at any free wireless hotspot.  There are some hotspots that charge (or perhaps free with purchase).  There is also[ tmobile wireless hotspots]("http://hotspot.t-mobile.com/") (like in starbucks).

The other thing that you could be talking about (but I doubt it) is Wireless WAN.  Some laptops have a wireless WAN card built in.  In order to use this you would need a service agreement with the wireless WAN provider (sprint, cingular, etc)
