### Post by Hulkiedulkie on 2011-10-17
If you want to share your 3G (USB reciever?) internet connection you will not only need to establish a connection between your devices, but also do NAT (network address translation). DHCP will also be handy. Basically you need to convert your machine into a router. 
 
Personally I'd use the package "hostapd" to convert your computer into a wireless accespoint, and then use "firestarter" to do routing and dhcp. No doubt you'll run into issues, but I know from personal experience that it's possible and once it works it's great.
