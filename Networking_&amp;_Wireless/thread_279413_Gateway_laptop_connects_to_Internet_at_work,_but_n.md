### Post by rmb3302 on 2006-10-17
At home, my SimplyMepis desktop, but not my Ubuntu laptop, connects to the Internet.  However, my laptop *will* connect to the Internet from work.  The ISP is the same for home and work.  Both machines are by Gateway.  This problem is not unique to Ubuntu.  It also happened when I installed Kubuntu, Desktop BSD, BLAG, and Easys on my laptop.

I'd certainly appreciate any enlightenment on what causes this and suggestions to fix or work around it.

---

### Post by rmb3302 on 2006-10-22
Resolved.  I shut down the DSL modem at home, connected the cable to the laptop, restarted the modem, then powered up the laptop.  Once the computer was up and running, I had access to the Internet.
