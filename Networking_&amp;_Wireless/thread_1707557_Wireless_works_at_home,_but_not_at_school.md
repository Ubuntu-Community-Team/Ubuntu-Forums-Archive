### Post by sanderd17 on 2011-03-16
I'm a university student, and in the student residence we have a wired network.

All went well with my old laptop with ubuntu 10.10 (until he got stolen). My new laptop connected to the network but couldn't load anything.

I went to the IT helpdesk and the answer they gave me was that my network card was "too advanced" for their network. And the only sollution they could give is to write other drivers for it (to disable some features)
 or to place a router and go wireless. I did the second, bought a router and placed it here, now everything works well.

Maybe your problem is something like that. If it you you could search other drivers, go wired, or buy an external network card.

I can't help you with any of those, it's just clear that the problem is not your computer but their network.
