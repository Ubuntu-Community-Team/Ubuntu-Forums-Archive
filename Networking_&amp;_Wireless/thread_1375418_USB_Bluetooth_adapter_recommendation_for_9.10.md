### Post by mark bower on 2010-01-09
Big goof on my part.  The Belkin F5D7050 is a USB wireless adapter, not bluetooth.  

For bluetooth dongle I successfully use Hawking HBTC2 and Cirago BTA-6210 devices.  I think there is a good possibility that a cheap dongle will work just fine. However, I had trouble with PCI wireless cards so went to the USB BelKin device to talk to my router.

mark
