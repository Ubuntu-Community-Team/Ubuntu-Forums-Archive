### Post by brett- on 2009-09-06
I usually don't just vomit comments like this, but I think it needs to be said -

If you have QWEST DSL service and are using the provided Actiontec modem you are not achieving optimal WAN or LAN performance.

GET A REAL DSL MODEM - Actiontec is trash.  After replacing it with a D-Link the speed increase is 25% WAN and 200% LAN (wireless), no exaggeration.  Also ... simple requests through a web browser are 300% faster.  I base this on three painful years of Actiontec and two months with a real modem.

There, I said it.  I had to.  I feel I owe it to you to pass this experience along.  I know it has been said.

I would not be surprised if the Actiontec modems supplied by QWEST are purposely gimped.
