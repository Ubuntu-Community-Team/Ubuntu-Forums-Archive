### Post by kerry_s on 2007-07-04
if you truly want sure fire, get a wireless ethernet bridge. you just plug in the ethernet wire, use your browser to connect to it, set the settings and never worry about it again, you can jump from distro to distro it don't matter the bridge does the wireless. systems just thinks it's a wired connection. i have been using USrobotics ethernet gaming bridge for 4+ years and have only had to reset it up after a couple of power outages, which is simply connecting to it and clicking the apply because the settings are still there, i can even unplug it move it where i want plug it back in and it's still set up. you can even connect a router to it and use 1 ethernet bridge for several computers. can't go wrong.
