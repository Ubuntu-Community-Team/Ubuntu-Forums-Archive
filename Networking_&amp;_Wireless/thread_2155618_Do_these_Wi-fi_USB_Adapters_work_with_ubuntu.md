Just noticed this thread, so sorry if info is too late, but there are two very compatible usb wireless adapters on my "recommend list" . . . . the TP-Link 722, and the Panda UltraWifi mini adapter.  Of the two, the TP-Link comes with a multi-direction antenna, and just seems a high quality device (even better is the TP-Link 822 with dual antenna).   TP-Link and Panda devices are plug & play - - no drivers needing to be installed.
