### Post by ratcheer on 2011-12-28
You can wire as many phones as you like to the voice-out of the splitter, in parallel. If there was no DSL in your house and you had no DSL splitter, that is the way your phones would be wired - all to the one incoming phone line.
 
But, if you have a burning need to do so, you should be able to buy a little phone terminal strip at some place such as Radio Shack. Then, you would have the voice out from the splitter going to the input side of the strip, and have a separate pair of terminals for each phone in your house. But, electrically, it is the same as wiring all the phones to the voice out side of the splitter.
 
Note that you can have one and only one DSL modem connected to the DSL out side.
