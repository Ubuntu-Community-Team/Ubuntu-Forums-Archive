### Post by GaryRixon on 2010-08-19
On your windoze pc put all of the files you would like to transfer to your linux pc in one folder. Now right click on the folder, go to the sharing tab and check enable sharing. Now go to your linux pc, click Places, go down to network, find your windoze pc and copy the desired files/folders to a destination of your choice.

This method has always worked for me, Hope this helps
