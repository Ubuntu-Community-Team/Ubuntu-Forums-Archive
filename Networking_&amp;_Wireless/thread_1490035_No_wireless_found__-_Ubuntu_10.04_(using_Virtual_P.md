### Post by droadtrip on 2010-05-21
This has happened to me half the time I jump in Ubuntu using various new PC's in my house. 1st, hardwire the ethernet cable from your PC to your Modem. Then go to hardware in the System menu and have it check for proprietary drivers. Approve any if they come up. this should kick into gear your wireless finding the network. Unplug the ethernet to allow this. Hope this helps.
