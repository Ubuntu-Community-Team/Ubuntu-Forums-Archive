### Post by dogshoe21 on 2008-10-07
Not sure if posting good news is acceptable, but I'm happy to report that my machine is working perfectly with the installation of the latest beta of Ubuntu 8.10. I have an old compaq presario 2100 or 2190 depending on where you look on the machine. My video card and my older linksys wireless pcmcia card were always causing me problems with previous versions:

 Radeon IGP 330M/340M/350M

RTL8180L 802.11b MAC
Realtek Semiconductor Co., Ltd.

anyone else that has this older hardware, the current beta version APPEARS to have fixed my previous issues where the video card wasn't recognized and where my wireless card was not being recognized. I was very bummed when 7.10 came out because my wireless card stopped working when I upgraded from 7.04. So when 8.04 came out I had problems with my video card AND my wireless card as well. On my first try I was able to connect to my wireless and get my video card to work properly with the live CD and now that I have it installed on the hard drive, its still working. Good luck and hope you have the same success!! Good job Ubuntu team!
