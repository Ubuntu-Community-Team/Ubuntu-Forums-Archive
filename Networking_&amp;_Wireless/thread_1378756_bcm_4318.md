### Post by efflandt on 2010-01-12
If you have a temporary wired connection you could connect to, go to System > Administration > Hardware Drivers and activate Broadcom 43 wireless driver.

Or if you do not have a network connection, you could insert your Ubuntu CD, open Synaptic and check the CD to add it to the repositories and install b43-fwcutter package.

That worked for an older Compaq laptop with BCM4318 that looks like the same as yours [AirForce One 54g].
