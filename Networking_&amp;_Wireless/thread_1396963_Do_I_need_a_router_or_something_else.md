### Post by JackRock on 2010-02-02
If you have your own internet account, you will need something to turn the incoming signal into a wireless radio broadcast.  This will necessitate either a router AND a wireless access point, OR (cheaper) a wireless router.

If the complex has free wi-fi that they offer, then you need to contact your complex management to determine the settings to allow your PC to connect.
