### Post by wapitiscat on 2005-11-15
Give a nOOb a hammer and he goes looking for a nail ... Here's my one comment to any problem similiar to this. If you have a network manager running, it may be scanning for new networks even while you're connected. For me, this caused my connection to drop (3Com 3CRWE154A72 w/ ndiswrapper). If you have something like this installed (NetworkManager, WiFi radar, etc), try uninstalling it , rebooting and then test your connection. This was a problem for me that I spent way too much time on.
