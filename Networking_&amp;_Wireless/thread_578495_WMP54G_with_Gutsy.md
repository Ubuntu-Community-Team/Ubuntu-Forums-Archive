### Post by JustinAlf on 2007-10-17
I just downloaded the release candidate for Gutsy, which shouldn't be too different from the actual release.  Anyways, I would like to thank anyone who worked on the project, because my Wmp54g with ra61 chipset now works with WPA2 Personal!!!!

There was however, some problems.  I could not connect to my wireless connection without a password.  I also could not connect when roaming mode was on.

To get this to work, I had to manually configure my wireless network card to connect to my network, and manually put in the WPA2 Personal password.  But don't get the wrong idea, it works and works bueatifully.

I've had somewhat of a difficult time with my wireless card and connecting since Dapper.  Thanks to all who have made Gutsy the best Linux Distro to date.
