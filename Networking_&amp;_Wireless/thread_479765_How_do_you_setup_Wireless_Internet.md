### Post by reckless2k2 on 2007-06-20
you need highspeed internet, cable/dsl modem, and wireless router. if you have highspeed internet now, you have modem. you would just need to get a wireless router. a router allows you to take a internet connection and split it between several computers. if the laptop does not have a wireless card, you would have to get one to use wireless. a wireless router has at least 4 ports on the back that you can hook up all your computers wired as well.
