### Post by DugieHowsa on 2007-06-25
Anything from 3com, intel or broadcom shoud work just fine.  If you are looking for cheap, you could always try ebay or craig's list.  I saw some on there in the $5 range.  You are better off buying a network adapter from a well known company.  That way, you are ensured that they have developed linux drivers for the card.  Going with a lesser known company doesn't guarantee that you will get linux drivers.
