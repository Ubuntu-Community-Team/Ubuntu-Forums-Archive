### Post by FrodoB on 2006-11-05
Find out what sort of chipset you really have in the device:

[http://linux-wless.passys.nl/](http://linux-wless.passys.nl/)

Just giving a manufacturer and model does not get you to the driver that you need.  Wireless LAN resources for Linux can also help:
