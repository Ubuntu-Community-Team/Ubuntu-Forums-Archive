### Post by pastalavista on 2009-09-25
If you have to use WEP, it's best to use a hexkey password. You need to change the router settings and make the password 10 characters (hexadecimal) from 1-9 & a-f. I don't know why, it's just a limitation Ubuntu seems to have.. at least the network-manager application. You might have better luck installing wicd.


edit: make the hexkey digits 0-9 & a-f
