### Post by grakhul on 2005-03-06
Try the AT&T 6700G Wireless PCMCIA adapter, it worked flawlessly for me.  I took it out of the box.  Jammed it in my laptop. Started my laptop. Went into the network config and clicked "Add".  Typed in the essid of my wireless network. Clicked on OK.  Went back into the network config and highlighted the wireless adapter.  Made sure the the Hostname and Domain were cool.  Clicked ok again.  Rebooted my Wireless modem and voila.  The WLAN card was picked up by the router and away we go!!.

Side Note: enabling security does NOT work!  If you enable wireless security on your router, your card will never synch with the router.  Whether it's WEP or any other type of standard.
