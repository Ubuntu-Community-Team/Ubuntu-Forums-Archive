### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2012-04-23
Sounds like packet loss. Some years ago I had lots of trouble with an ISP (who will remain unnamed) where many of the packets were lost consistently and it acted as you're describing. This may or may not be something that you have control over. In my case it was an issue with the equipment on the ISP end.

Have you tried directly connecting to the modem? That will help you figure out if the issue is on your network or on the network of the ISP. If you find out things work fine when connected directly to the modem then you can begin troubleshooting locally like the Vonage adapter and network bridge on your Linux machine.
