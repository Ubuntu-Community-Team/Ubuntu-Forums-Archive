### Post by t0mppa on 2009-07-09
Notification area is the whole area where you have the icons for clock, volume, wireless, bluetooth, etc. and some programs like put their icons there too, when they're active.

If you simply removed the wireless icon, it won't get back by re-adding the notification area, as you've found out, but rather by re-running the applet behind it. So, press Alt + F2, type in "nm-applet" without the quotes and click run, to get the icon back.
