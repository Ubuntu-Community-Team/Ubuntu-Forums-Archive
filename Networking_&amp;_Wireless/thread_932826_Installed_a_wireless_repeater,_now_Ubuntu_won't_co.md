### Post by Bezmotivnik on 2008-12-15
The AP431W is factory-defective in repeater mode.  This is a known issue.  There's a firmware update that is *supposed* to fix the problem, but does not.  I wasted a **year** trying to get this POS to work in repeater mode before Airlink101 admitted that it was a design problem that caused incessant power cycling.  I'm astounded it worked under Windows.
