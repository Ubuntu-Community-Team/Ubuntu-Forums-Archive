### Post by tfabian88 on 2009-08-15
Hello all,

I've recently installed a Trendnet TEW423PI wireless PCI adapter into my PC.  So I fired up my computer, and it froze before booting into Gnome(Ubuntu).  I took the PCI card out, this time no problem.  Since then I've uninstalled Ubuntu, and I tried reinstalling with the card installed.  The progress bar reaches 100%, then it displays a black screen and freezes.  I've read a couple of threads regarding the TEW423PI, and I'm aware that it functions with ndiswrapper.  Unfortunately I'm unable to get past the "black screen"...  

Any help would be appreciated.

SOLVED!!!!  I had to take the card out, install Ubuntu, pre-install the ndiswrapper drivers, then stick the card back in and presto!
