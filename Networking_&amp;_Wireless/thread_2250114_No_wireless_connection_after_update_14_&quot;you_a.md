### Post by Steven_Williams on 2014-10-27
You probably want to be using the b43 driver instead of wl. [http://wireless.kernel.org/en/users/Drivers/b43](http://wireless.kernel.org/en/users/Drivers/b43) This page explains how you can use the b43 driver. The firmware-b43-installer should take care of what is mentioned on the page for you. Being new to Ubuntu myself, I don't know if it will automatically blacklist the old driver or not.
