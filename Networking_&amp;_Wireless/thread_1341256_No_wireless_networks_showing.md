### Post by dimeotane on 2010-01-31
You've likely already figured this out... but for anyone reading this thread here's what worked for me.

On my Dell XPS M1210 I had no wireless detected on boot.  Then I switched the wireless card off and then on again and it prompted me to load the proprietary drivers.   Other folks needed to try the Fn+F2 keys (or whatever combination your notebook uses).
