### Post by gradedcheese on 2007-02-10
A piece of advice:  you can copy and paste your terminal output into this forum instead of taking screenshots.  To copy on Linux, simply select a block of text with your mouse.  To paste, move the mouse to where you want to paste and press the middle button (usually scroll wheel).  If you have two buttons, press them simultaneously.  Failing that, Control+Alt+C in the terminal will copy the selected text.
