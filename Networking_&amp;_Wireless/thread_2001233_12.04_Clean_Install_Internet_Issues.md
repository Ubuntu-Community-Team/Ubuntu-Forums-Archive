### Post by chili555 on 2012-06-12
The mixed reference is to mixed mode WPA and WPA2; in most cases, pure WPA2 mode works better than mixed. 

You'd need to log in to the administration pages of the router and look around for wireless settings. Many have power settings, for instance from 1 to 10; my old AT&T modem/router came defaulted to 8. I reset it to 10.

Most devices default to channel 6. Many devices do better dodging microwaves, dimmer switches, cordless phones, et al on some other channel, such as 1 or 11.  After making and saving your changes, no other action is required on your computers.
