### Post by jetsam on 2008-06-15
I don't think there's any practical way to do this.  

Clustered computing and distributed computing are both very technical and mostly the preserve of research scientists.  

I think [open mosix]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/openmosix/") was the closest thing to an end user version of this, but I had the impression that even that was pretty complicated, and the project is no longer active.  

Most clusters aren't casual collections of old machines, but rather arrays of inexpensive systems planned specifically for the purpose.

**Added:**  Oh.  Right.  It's open source!  Someone's picking up where Open Mosix left off at the [LinuxPMI project]("http://linuxpmi.org/trac/").  It looks like they're just revving up, though, and they're still in Alpha.
