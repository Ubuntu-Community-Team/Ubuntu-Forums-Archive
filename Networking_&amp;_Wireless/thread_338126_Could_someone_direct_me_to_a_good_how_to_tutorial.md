### Post by Floppyjoe on 2007-01-14
I would buy a wireless router and hook your Ubuntu machine into the router with the ethernet cable.  Connect the router to the modem with ethernet cable. Then configure the router to connect to your modem with the needed info that your ISP has provided. The other two computers connect to the router wirelessly by DHCP or static IP.  You could have all three computers hooked up wirelessly to the router but you probably would need one plugged into the router for the initial setup.
