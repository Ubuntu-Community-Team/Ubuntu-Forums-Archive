1st link: Broadcom card, bad idea

2nd link: no sense trying all of them

Deactivate the N-mode of the driver and the queue:

```
echo "options iwlwifi 11n_disable=1 wd_disable=1" | sudo tee /etc/modprobe.d/iwlwifi.conf
```
reboot and change the encryption mode of the router to pure WPA2-AES (CCMP) and to a fixed channel
