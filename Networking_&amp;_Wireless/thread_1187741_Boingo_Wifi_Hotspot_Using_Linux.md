### Post by nythacker on 2009-06-15
Alright... looks like I'm gonna have to answer my own question.

I called up the Boingo Customer Support line and they told me that I didn't have to download any client software if I was using Linux. They just told me that I just have to connect to the Boingo network and open up my browser and the Boingo welcome screen should come up asking you for your account name and password.

I just thought first that I should download some kind of Boingo software client so I could get in their network as they had software clients for Windows, Mac, and smartphones - no Linux client.

Anyways, hope this helps others who are on the same boat as I am.

Cheers!
