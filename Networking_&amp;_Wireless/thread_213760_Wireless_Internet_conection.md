### Post by jml on 2006-07-11
A bit more information would be helpful.  Do you have an active internet connection?  That is, can you access the internet with another computer?  Can you access any other web sites with your Ubuntu box?

As for e-mail.  When you signed up with your ISP you should have been given a username and password.  You should also get the name of your ISP's incoming and outgoing mail servers from your ISP.  The first time that you run your e-mail client you will be asked to supply that information.  Once that is done, you should be able to send and recieve your e-mail.
