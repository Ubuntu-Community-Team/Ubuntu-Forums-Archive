### Post by Ayuthia on 2009-09-01
> **1080p said:**
> I have succesfully installed B43 Wireless driver & I am using it already. How do i backup B43 firmware ?. Also how do I install the B43 firmware again if I reinstall ubuntu ?

The firmware is stored in /lib/firmware/b43 and /lib/firmware/b43legacy.  All you should need to do is copy those folders into your reinstalled version of Ubuntu and that should be it.
