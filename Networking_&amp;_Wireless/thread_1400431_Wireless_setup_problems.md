### Post by R2-Duck2 on 2010-02-06
ghorton619,
 
Had the same problem a few days ago...have a Linksys wireless connection; said it was connected, but had no Internet. Tried everything I could think of, and then decided to do the following:
 
Shut down the laptop
Removed power cord from the router
Removed power cord from the modem
Waited a minute, or so...
 
Powered up the modem, waited until the lights were blinking
Powered up the router, also waited until the lights were blinking
 
Started the laptop
 
Had an internet connection.
 
If you give it a whirl, you have nothing to lose...
