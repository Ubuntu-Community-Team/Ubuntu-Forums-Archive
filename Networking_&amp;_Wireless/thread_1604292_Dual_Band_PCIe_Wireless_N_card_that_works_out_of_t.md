Not that it helps as far as buying a card for a desktop, my laptop with "Intel Corporation Centrino Advanced-N + WiMAX 6250 (rev 57)" works at full speed (300) at both bands in 10.10. Still testing my other machines. By default N is disabled, but can be enabled per this thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1592846](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1592846). Writing with 5ghz and auto bandwith on Linksys e2000 with ddwrt. Laptop is HP dm4-1165dx.
