### Post by malachi1990 on 2008-08-30
Chances are you need to check your wireless settings.  Usually, ubuntu won't let you go online unless you're using wpa settings on your wireless.

The easiest thing to do is to get the wifi-radar application and change your settings to match the ones the radar shows you.  You can change them using the network tool in the administration bar.
