### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-09-06
So you feel that Ubuntu slows down the network on a fresh install but then eventually speeds it up after an update? Not possible I believe. Never happened to me at least. What might have happened to you a couple of times is that the update servers might have been getting hammered therefore decreasing the speed. Try changing the download server the next time you choose to update your newly installed system. Also try with some other website or downloading some other type of stuff to see, what you are feeling, actually happens!

Go to System >>> Administration >>> Software Sources, then goto "Download from " select "Other" and click the "Choose the best Server" button.
