### Post by ben@kietzman.org on 2009-12-28
I have only had success routing mic input through the bluetooth headset and routing output through the computer speakers.

You will need to right click on your sound icon and select "Sound Preferences".  On the "Input" you should be able to choose your bluetooth device.

From Ekiga make sure your 2 audio output devices are set to your speakers and make sure your input device is set to default.

It would make sense to set your audio output devices to default so they route through the bluetooth.  However, that setup has never worked for me.  Until that feature is fixed I get input from the bluetooth and send output to the speakers.
