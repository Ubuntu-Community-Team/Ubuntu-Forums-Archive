### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2015-04-03
Hello and welcome.

Mediatek's MT7630 is relatively new and supported in Linux via a proprietary driver for kernels 3.13 and 3.14 only, as of today (!!!). Fortunately you have one of those kernels in your Ubuntu 14.04. Here's the instructions in the Linux Mint forum (also work for Ubuntu 14.04 upon which Mint 17 is based): [http://community.linuxmint.com/tutorial/view/1796](http://community.linuxmint.com/tutorial/view/1796)
Carefully read the introduction and instructions and make sure you download the [tweaked] "here" and not the [original] "driver".
