### Post by K3JAE on 2009-01-03
Is this network a local network or pulling from another source? 

Wireless networks not within reasonable distance from your computer(s) can be unstable based on a plethoria of things. Interference, weather etc. all play a part here.

Also, it is possible if the source you are pulling from, again assuming wireless, may be disconnected at times by the owner. 

Basically speaking, if you are pulling from a wireless source, nothing is guaranteed, and most likely will be unstable,  especially if the originating source is not very close to you. :rolleyes:
