### Post by mark bower on 2010-05-23
take a look at:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported)

for cards for which there are native linux drivers, and why get any other card, i suggest the following:

for USB 8.10, 9.04 and 9.10 the Belkin F5D7050 is a reasonably sure bet with many reports of success, including my own on PC and laptop.

for PCI, i would highly recommend the Netgear WPN311v1 - i bot 3 of them for my 3 PC's on 9.10.

i believe the first link you provided, the one with colors, is not kept up to date, just a guess.  when you get a card to work out of the box, come back and post for others to see.
