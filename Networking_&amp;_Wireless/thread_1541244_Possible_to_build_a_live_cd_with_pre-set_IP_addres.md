### Post by chili555 on 2010-07-29
As far as I know, it will work with any distro. The idea is to mount the ISO, make changes, add and delete packages and pack it back up as an ISO. Remastersys is just a more convenient way to do so. Of course, you are limited to the size of a CD.

I have never used remastersys, but I have remastered a Fedora CD back in the day. I found it addictive. Once I found out what could be done, I wanted to do even more. CDs are cheap!
