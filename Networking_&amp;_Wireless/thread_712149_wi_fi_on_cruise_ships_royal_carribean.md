### Post by jflaker on 2008-03-02
The prices are astronomical for even a few minutes online.  It is great if you can do things offline then have everything ready to go once you establish an internet connection.  

As long as you get time and not Kb charges, you can blast everything all at once then drop the connection.

The other option is to inquire about a GSM network phone that can be used as a modem and when you get a signal (which may be spotty at best while at sea) you can use the cell phone to upload your stuff.

Any which way you look at it, it will cost you way too much to stay connected at sea.  And, your laptop may grow legs....you may just want to leave it at home.
