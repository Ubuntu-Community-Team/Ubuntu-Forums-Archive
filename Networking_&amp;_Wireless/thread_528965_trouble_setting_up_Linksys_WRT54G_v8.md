### Post by Scunizi on 2007-08-18
You should not have to use the setup CD that came with the router.  You can access most Linksys routers, regardless of firmware ie. linux or other, by opening your browser and typing 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.0.1.  This will bring up the GUI built into the router for configuration.  From there just find what you need and make the changes.
