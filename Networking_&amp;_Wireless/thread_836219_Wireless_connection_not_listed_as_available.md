### Post by caljohnsmith on 2008-06-26
Just a thought, but are you sure your router's "broadcast ESSID" is set to on/enable? If not you obviously won't see it listed in available networks on your computer, but will still be able to connect if you manually connect w/ the ESSID. Are you able to see other networks in the area?
