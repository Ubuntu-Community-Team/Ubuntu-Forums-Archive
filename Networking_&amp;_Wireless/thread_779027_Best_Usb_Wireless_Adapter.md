### Post by Metalrat on 2008-05-05
I just bought an Edimax EW7318USG, which is based on the new RaLink USB chipset. 
Works great out of the box and has a removeable antenna (you can upgrade this if required).

If you are UK based, you can source it from [www.linuxemporium.co.uk](www.linuxemporium.co.uk) (they include additional driver disc, but you shouldn't need it). 
Alternatively you could look elsewhere, very probably getting it cheaper but without the specialist retailer knowledge.
