---

### Post by P4man on 2010-10-20
That functionality is included out of the box. Make the share on windows, and in ubuntu go to places > network and browse to the share. If prompted for login and password, use your windows account login and password.

Once that works, you can streamline the process by creating a bookmark for it, or a mountpoint and have rhythmbox or whatever music app you use, include that in its library.
