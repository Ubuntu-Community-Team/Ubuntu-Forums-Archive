This thread is for all the n00bs like me out there.

and learned that a computer science degree and a few choice open certifications would now come in handy in figuring out how to make your RTL-#$#@ work. Or some other sad story. For example,

USA Linksys WUSB54GC works on Gutsy 7.10 64 after downloading one script, extracting it, and then opening the terminal, changing to the directory of the download, and running a simple command that launches the script by Hendrick.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=516649](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=516649)

Seriously, I don't think it gets any easier for a n00b. And not having wireless internet is a show stopper for the grand majority of the public. Not that Linksys is necessarily the best, as I was about to purchase a DLink WNA-2330 because I read it was a sweet PCI card until I realized my freaking Toshiba laptop had an expresscard INSTEAD of PCMCIA (Not recommended).
