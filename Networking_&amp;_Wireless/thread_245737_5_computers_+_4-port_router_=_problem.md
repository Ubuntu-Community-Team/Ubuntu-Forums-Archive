### Post by tribaal on 2006-08-28
The easiest way is probably to plug a hub on the cable that currently goes from the router to your room (plug the hub on the end that's in your room, of course). From there, you should be fine with two cheap RJ45 cables. :)

So to answer your question: yes, you can plug in a HUB there :)

Hope this helps.
