### Post by Compyx on 2007-04-28
The icon you're talking about is probably the network manager, to get a network monitor (the flashing thing), click somewhere on  the empty space of your top bar and select "+ Add to Panel..".
In the window that pops up, scroll down to "System & Hardware" and select "Network Monitor" and click on "Add".
You can then right-click on the new icon that appears and click "properties" to select the network interface it monitors under "connection".
