### Post by lovelinuxfreedom on 2011-07-02
If the issue was caused by a upgrade bug, the following may not help. Then u guys have to wait the engineers to solve it. If it is personal setting that caused it, then it may help u.
Click "System--preferences--network connection". If your wireless device didn't work, then click "wireless", choose a connection, click "edit" , you can change the ipv4/6 setting. Once u change it,  try to link to the net. If it still doesn't work, change to another method. :p
