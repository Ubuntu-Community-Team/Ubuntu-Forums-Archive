### Post by Peter09 on 2009-05-13
I am hazy on this as well but my best guess would be

arp-spoofing is where you pretend to provide address resolutions to the network - and give the ones you want.


arp-poisoning is where you insert into a valid server an incorrect address (over-writing a valid one) this is then propagated through the community of arp servers.
