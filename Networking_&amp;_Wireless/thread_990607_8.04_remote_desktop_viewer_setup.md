### Post by spd106 on 2008-11-25
You need a vnc server such as vnc4server, tightvncserver or vino

The last one is quite well integrated with GNOME, but that shouldn't be a problem for Xubuntu, it just reduces the point of having a lightweight desktop.

It's well worth having a read through this wiki page:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/VNC](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/VNC)

Good luck
