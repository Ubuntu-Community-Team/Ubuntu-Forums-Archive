### Post by driftertx on 2009-11-14
Many factors affect your transfer speeds, harddrive speed, bus speed, nic speed, and your router. Just because a router says you get connected at 1Gbit does not mean you'll get 1Gbit worth of throughput. With that said, you'll find that because Ubuntu doesn't have the overhead of an operating system like Windows that and old server works just fine as a server. You may see faster speeds and for 10-15bux it's worth trying.
