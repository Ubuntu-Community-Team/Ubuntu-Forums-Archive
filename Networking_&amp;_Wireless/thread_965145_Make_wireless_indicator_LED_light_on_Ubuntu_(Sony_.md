This solution made my wireless LED start working on Ubuntu. I don't understand the mechanics behind it be further investigation by someone more proficient might use it to find a fix for LED problems.

Solution: Open the bluetooth preferences and click on any of the radio butttons (Hidden|always visible|temporarily visible), the LED light comes on. 

It will continue to come on for subsequent computer restarts. Very simple, but it work :)
