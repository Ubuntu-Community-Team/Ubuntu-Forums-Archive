There are 3 versions of the DWL520G:

DWL-G520  - Atheros chipset (works w/ madwifi in linux-restricted-modules package) 
DWL-G520+ ACX111 chipset (pita to setup)
DWL-G520+A v.C1 Ralink chipset (experimental drivers available but should work)
