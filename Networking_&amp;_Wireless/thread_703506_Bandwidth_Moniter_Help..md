### Post by lawentzel on 2008-02-21
Typically they will do that to discourage people from setting up Bearshare or other P2P networking.  Your System Monitor keeps track of your download total per instance.  So, if you leave your computer on all the time, just reboot it at the beginning of the month, and the total download would be running total for the whole month.  If you are shutting off your computer nightly, this isn't a very good solution.
