### Post by LinuxLovesMJ on 2008-05-01
If you have a broadcom card, I would suggest using bcm43xx instead of b43. b43 only provides 1mb speeds and seems to lose connection a lot. bcm43xx is still stable and provides 24mb speeds. Ndsiwrapper is conflicting at times with hardy.

Bottom line: Trust me on this, use bcm43xx instead of b43 or ndiswrapper!
