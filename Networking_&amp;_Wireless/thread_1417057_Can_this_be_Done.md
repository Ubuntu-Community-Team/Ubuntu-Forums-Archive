### Post by spikyjt on 2010-02-28
Whilst I'm always up for a custom solution, I wonder why you feel the need to host it at your home? Are there large amounts of data involved? (unlikely if you are using a smartphone which will have limited storage). Why not use an already available service like Dropbox? There are already iPhone apps for this and probably other services. You can sync with your home computer too if you wish. Then you benefit from their high upload bandwidth, which you won't have from your home server. If you need lots of data, you could sign up for their paid services or rent a dedicated server, which will probably cost you less than the electricity to keep your home server running 24/7. Just my 2¢.
