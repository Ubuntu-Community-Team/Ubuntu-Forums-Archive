### Post by kyphi on 2007-08-08
You do not need to remove any drivers from your Ubuntu installation.

Routers and modem/routers are configured within the router.  When you get your modem/router your ISP will provide you with the configuration details.  To find out how to configure, go to the URL of the maker where it will tell you what to type into the address bar of your browser to activate the configure interface.  This will happen from router to internet directly.  If you buy a router it will come with an installation manual.

As for which one to buy - rely on the recommendations of your ISP.  Your ISP might even configure it for you.
