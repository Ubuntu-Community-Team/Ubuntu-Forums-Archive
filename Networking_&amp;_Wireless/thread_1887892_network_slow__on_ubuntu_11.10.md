### Post by praseodym on 2011-11-28
Remove the line "auto eth1" via:
```
gksu gedit /etc/network/interfaces
```
remove your wired profile in "wired" and "DSL" (if any) and reboot. You may also want to try to shut off the auto-negotiation via:

```
sudo ethtool -s eth1 speed 100 autoneg off
sudo /etc/init.d/networking restart
```
