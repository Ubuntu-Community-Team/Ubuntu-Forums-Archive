### Post by stuckagain on 2010-10-16
If it is the soft modem you are referring to, it is better to ignore it and rely on the hardware supplied by the carrier. Internal soft modem runs completely oblivious to you and shows up in no dialog or GUI (in linux). It is the domain of the command-line champ. Diagnostic support for it is almost nil.
