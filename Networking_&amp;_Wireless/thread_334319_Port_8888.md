### Post by s4050058 on 2007-02-16
Warmest greetings,

In your case sun-answerbook is likely not to be running on your system - it is an online manual program.  NMAP merely reports the common programs using that port.  You can use amap from [www.thc.org](www.thc.org) which may give more info, or you can use netstat -anptu and look for what is using 8888 on your system. On mine it was tinyproxy, other people have had xinetd and others still media streaming.

Hope that helps
