### Post by bravebear on 2008-10-07
Configuration: Dual boot Ubuntustudio 8.04 and Windows XP on Toshiba Satellite R25 Tablet with a Intel PRO/Wireless 3945ABG wireless card.

1. After installing ubuntustudio 8.04, my  WiFi card was detected but could not connect. Then I found out I could not get into Windows partition from Ubuntustudio because Windows XP had locked it when it went into hibernate. Rebooted into Windows, shut down Windows all the way and voila, ubuntustudio goes online. Seems that Windows locks the WiFi card somehow on hibernate. So don't hibernate Windows to switch to Ubuntu!

2. WiFi disconnected every few minutes. Still don't understand why, but switching to wicd ([http://wicd.sourceforge.net/]("http://wicd.sourceforge.net/")) to manage networks fixed it so far.

 
Just sharing my little experience so far. Just got into Ubuntu (sick and tired of Windows! Or, in the famous words of Freddie Mercury: "I want to be free!").
By the way, my Toshiba Satellite R25 is a Tablet and I got the tablet screen to work pretty nice so far without problems.
