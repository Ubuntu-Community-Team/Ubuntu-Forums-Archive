Karmic Koala, I have a built-in ethernet card listed as eth0.  It is configured (via gnome network-manager) as auto DHCP.  When I click on the icon in the panel, it lists Wired Network Interface 1 and "Available".  If I click on "Available" it connects up to the network properly, but it won't automatically connect.
