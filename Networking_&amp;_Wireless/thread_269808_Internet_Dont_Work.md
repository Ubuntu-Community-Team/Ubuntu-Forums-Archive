### Post by handy on 2006-10-04
Disable ipv6 in firefox:

Enter **about:config** in the address field.

Enter **ipv6** in the new **Filter:** field.

Double click on the one & only line displayed in the view window, this will change the boolean value to **true**.

Restart FF & Bob's your uncle...
