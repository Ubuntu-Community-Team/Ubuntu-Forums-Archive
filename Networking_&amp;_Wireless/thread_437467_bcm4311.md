### Post by spiritofreason on 2007-05-15
The 1390 works with bcm43xx, but it's very slow right now.  Some patches were introduced into 2.6.21, and it looks like they haven't been incorporated into Ubuntu's kernel for Feisty.  Depending on the rest of the system, ndiswrapper may work.  I happened to have interrupt issues with ndiswrapper and it would cause the driver to disable the interrupt and the card (some conflicts between the nvidia binary driver and ndiswrapper--no good way to really figure out why).

Hopefully, the patches will be backported, but at least the 1390 should work just fine in Gutsy when it gets released.

Updated:
The new kernel incorporates bcm43xx from 2.6.21.  While the throughput still isn't awesome, I'm getting 1.7 MiB/s through my LAN (good enough for most web activity).  After you download the firmware, the TrueMobile 1390 should just work. ^_^
