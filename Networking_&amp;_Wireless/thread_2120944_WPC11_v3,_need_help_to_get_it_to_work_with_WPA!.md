### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2013-03-04
Sorry for the late answer.  I was doing research and discovered probably exactly what you did.  I finally went here:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCardsLinksys](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCardsLinksys)

When you go to WPC 11, it is supposed to work out of the box which is obviously not your case.  Until I read that it was last updated in 2005, I did not realize it was so old.  Your best bet is probably to buy a new one.    
Before you do that, do one check.  Remove the security from your network and see if it will connect to an open network.  If it does re-establish your security and try to connect again.  If it works so much the better.
