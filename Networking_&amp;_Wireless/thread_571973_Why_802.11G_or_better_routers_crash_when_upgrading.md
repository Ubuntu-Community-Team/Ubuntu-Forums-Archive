### Post by sageb1 on 2007-10-09
Sustained traffic at 56MB/s overheats the router because it is a box with minimal cooling i.e. air-cooled but box openings are barely able to allow maximumcooling.

Solution is to fix router at 11MB/s or if it is possible fix it for the MAC of the wireless enable computer.

An alternative is to downgrade wireless LAN adapter to 802.11B 11MB/s speed for upgrades and to upgrade speed.

What is needed is a fix for update-manager script to switch from G or N speed to B speed when upgrading.

5-6 hours of 56MB/s will overheat a router.

5-6 hours of 11MB/s will not.
