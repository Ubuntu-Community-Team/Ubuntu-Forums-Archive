### Post by The Cog on 2015-10-28
This type of ICMP packet can be generated by iptables (and presumably other firewalls) when rejecting packets.
Look here for --reject-with: [http://linux.die.net/man/8/iptables](http://linux.die.net/man/8/iptables)
