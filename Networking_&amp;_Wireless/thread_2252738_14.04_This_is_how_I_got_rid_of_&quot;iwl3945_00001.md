### Post by jjaakkol on 2014-11-14
I do not know if this is best place to share my experience but here we go:

Symptoms:


1. Firefox+ adobe flashplayer
2. Watching Youtube 
3. IBM T60p with Lubuntu 14.04

Pretty often (ones in ten minute) WLAN connection broken when watching Youtube videos and in syslogs following:

```
iwl3945 0000:10:00.0: Queue 0 stuck for 2000 ms
iwl3945 0000:10:00.0: On demand firmware reload
```

Finally after several workaround tries I simply disabled flashplugin from Firefox and messages from syslog were gone. Firefox is using HTML5 instead. 

Yesterday I made test just be sure and enabled again flashplugin in Firefox and error messages/WLAN problem returned.

After disabling flashplugin things were ok again.
