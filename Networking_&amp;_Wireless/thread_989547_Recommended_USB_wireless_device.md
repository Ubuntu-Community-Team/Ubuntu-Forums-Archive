### Post by rbc on 2008-11-21
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported)

I have a Linksys WUSB54GC, which was recognized "out of the box". It drops connection about once a month now (used to be more frequent). But a simple "sudo /etc/init.d/networking restart" and I'm good to go for another month. Only WEP though. I think it was $40 USD at amazon.com
