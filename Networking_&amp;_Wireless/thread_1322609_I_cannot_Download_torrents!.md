I found a solution when you wan to download torrents and you are on wifi! You need to download the torrent file from the page, right click save as, rename it in to .txt from .torrent (---> blahblahblah_blahblah.torrent ---> blahblahblah_blahblah.txt ), and then drag n drop in your torrent download client. Unfortunately your speed wont worn on maximum, but it will work.
