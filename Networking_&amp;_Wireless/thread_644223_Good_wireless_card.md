### Post by Rhubarb on 2007-12-19
I did some research a few months ago and bought this:
[http://www.netgear.com/Products/Adapters/GWirelessAdapters/WG111.aspx](http://www.netgear.com/Products/Adapters/GWirelessAdapters/WG111.aspx)
Netgear WG111 usb wireless G adapter.
It's very important you get V2 (vesion 2 of the WG111) as this is the one that contains a good compatible chipset in it that works perfectly in ubuntu.

Though, please note that I tried it in ubuntu 7.04, in 7.10 it might work better as:
I couldn't get WPA / WPA2 working on it (perhaps I needed to install wpasupplicant?)
WEP and no encryption works fine.
