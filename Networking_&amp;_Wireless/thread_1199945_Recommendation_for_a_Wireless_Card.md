### Post by dnairb on 2009-06-29
If you can find one, Belkin USB wireless adapter (FD7050) version 400x works out-of-the-box. Be sure to get version 4 though, as versions 3 and 5 are problematic.

The version number is printed on a small sticker, usually on the bottom of the box. You may need to search through a lot of boxes/retailers to find one.
