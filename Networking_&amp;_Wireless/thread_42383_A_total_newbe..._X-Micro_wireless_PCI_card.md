### Post by antwerx on 2005-06-17
Hello - I would start with this [Hardware Compatibility List](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport) . Look for your current card in the supported PCI wireless cards.

If your card is not supported then look for supported cards with as many 'yes' answers in the last columns. Meaning they work out of the box with out tweaking which is best for newbies for sure.

Hope this helps.  :)
