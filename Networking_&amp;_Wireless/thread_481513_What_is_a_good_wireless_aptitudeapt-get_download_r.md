### Post by dbott67 on 2007-06-22
Downloads are generally rated in BYTES per second, where as bandwidth is generally referred to in BITS per second.

So, you may have an 8 megabit-per-second connection, but the true download speed would be reduced by a factor of 8, plus overhead, plus a whole slew of factors 'out on the net'.  A 760 KBps download is pretty fast (just a shade over 6 megabits-per-second).

A good site to test is [http://www.speedtest.net/](http://www.speedtest.net/).
