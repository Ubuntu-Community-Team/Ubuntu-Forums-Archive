### Post by skimwpi on 2012-12-11
I recently moved, have comcast, and run their router/modem. I experienced something similar where it looked like the internet connection would break every few minutes on my laptop. I found that disabling the amazon search results and turning off the recordings helped significantly. I can't say for sure why but I suspect bandwidth might be the problem. Check out [http://askubuntu.com/questions/192269/how-can-i-remove-amazon-search-results-from-the-dash]("http://askubuntu.com/questions/192269/how-can-i-remove-amazon-search-results-from-the-dash") to disable the amazon search results.
