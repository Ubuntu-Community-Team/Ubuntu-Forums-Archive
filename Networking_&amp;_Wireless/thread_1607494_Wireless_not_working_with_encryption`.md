### Post by NertSkull on 2010-10-27
I've had a lot of trouble with wireless and encryption.

What I have settled on for one of my machines, is to uninstall network manager (network-manager and network-manager-gnome).

Then I installed wicd (its in the repositories).

It seemed to work great for me.  Not everyone likes it, but it works for me, so I'm happy.

Maybe it will work for you.

Otherwise you may want to give more info.  I.e. are you using WEP, WPA2, Wireless card, etc.
