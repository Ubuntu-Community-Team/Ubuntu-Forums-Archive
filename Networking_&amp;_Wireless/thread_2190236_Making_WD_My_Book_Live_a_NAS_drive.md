### Post by mikewhatever on 2013-11-29
You could install and configure Samba, and then share (make that device accessible over the network) it, but that's not always straight forward, especially for a new user. Amahi, on the other hand, is advertised to provide the same functionality in a simple and easy way. You don't have to use it, but I don't actually see why not. So, tough choice, ...you'll have to decide what to use first.
