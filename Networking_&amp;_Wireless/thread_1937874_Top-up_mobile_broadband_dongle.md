This is from Vodafone Australia:
[http://vodafone.custhelp.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/3273/~/recharging-prepaid-mobile-broadband]("http://vodafone.custhelp.com/app/answers/detail/a_id/3273/%7E/recharging-prepaid-mobile-broadband")

At the bottom, **Where can I recharge Prepaid Mobile Broadband?**

You may be able to go to a prepaid webpage to recharge with a voucher - 
[https://selfserve.myvodafone.com.au/myBill/rechargeRedirect.jsp](https://selfserve.myvodafone.com.au/myBill/rechargeRedirect.jsp)
you don't even have to be logged into your Account.   
If you completely ran out of credit you could recharge this way using someone else's computer or at an internet cafe 
- you wouldn't need your dongle plugged in at all.  Just enter your mobile broadband number and voucher information.

Your mobile phone sim "fix" would work as well.
