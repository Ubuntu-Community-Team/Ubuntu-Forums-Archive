### Post by kerry_s on 2008-07-10
i think what i did was put> **ifconfig wlan0 up &**

in /etc/rc.local. i can't remember, i could never get a dependable connection and moved the system to win2k. sorry

but if those commands work for you, you can put them in /etc/rc.local:

[B]sudo editor /etc/rc.local

pccardctl eject &
pccardctl insert &
[/B]
the "&" on the end is so it continues to boot if something go's wrong.
