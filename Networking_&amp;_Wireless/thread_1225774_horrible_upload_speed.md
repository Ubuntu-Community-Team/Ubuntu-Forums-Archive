Yeah, that's where I'd start.  As you stated above, you called to get your price down, and they upped your speed.  They probably trained your modem lower, as the school of thought in the industry is that no one notices upload, and that it does not need to be fast, so long as the download is good.

Check with your other OSes, and preferably other computers, and then call Wide Open West and tell them what is going on and explain your troubleshooting to them and tell them about the previous conversation and that this is not isolated to one computer.  If it's not immediately apparent to them, they'll usually want to blame it on something on your side.

Good luck.  Post back if you are still having problems.
