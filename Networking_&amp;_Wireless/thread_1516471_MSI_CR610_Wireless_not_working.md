### Post by ovidiuteaca on 2011-04-15
I have a MSI CR610 laptop with RaLink rt3090 wifi
 
I went to [https://launchpad.net/~markus-tisoft/+archive/rt3090](https://launchpad.net/~markus-tisoft/+archive/rt3090)
I read the instructions in this PPA''Adding to your system''and I have applied as follows:
I opened Terminal and gave the command: sudo add-apt-repository ppa:markus-tisoft/rt3090
I have introduced user password
I gave the command: sudo apt-get update
I have introduced user password
I closed Terminal
I opened Synaptic
I searched dkms
I checked for installation: rt3090-dkms
After installation I rebooted the laptop and then I clicked the button on the wireless laptop (it's in the top right of the keyboard, just below the display) and the green light lit sign that your wireless is active.
