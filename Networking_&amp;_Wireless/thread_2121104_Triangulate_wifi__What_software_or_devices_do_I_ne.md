Hi, I don't think I have the exact answer to what you want, but something similar. I installed DD-WRT firmware on my router recently (it's a great open-source firmware that's much better than the stock by the way with extra options, etc...) and It is something called Wiviz (I'm not sure of the exact name). It's hard to pinpoint exactly where the networks are and the utility isn't so great but it'll give you an idea. I don't know about its accuracy though.

EDIT: it's called Wiviz, you might want to see if you can find it to install it on a PC...
