### Post by Tyche on 2006-07-09
Go to System - Administration - Networking (it will ask for your password).
Click on the DNS tab and add your DNS numbers (if you do not have them handy, you can get them from your ISP)

I have had to manually enter them every time I re-boot in Dapper Drake.  This did not used to be a problem with previous Ubuntu releases, and I don't know what the problem is.  But I am seeing an increasing number of people with the same problem.
