### Post by dannyboy79 on 2007-07-24
torrent programs will actually work with any port, but no one will actually be able to connect to you unless you use the port you forwarded so you'll get horrible download speeds. Like 1 to 5 kb/s at best. You'll want to always use the same ports you forward on your hardware firewall/router which is between your machine and the internet.
