### Post by QUANTUM ROBO on 2009-10-14
Hi, I also encountered lag spikes occurring approximately once per minute between my wireless client module and router. My wireless device connects through network-manager-gnome, so I disabled my wireless connection through that interface (right-click, uncheck "enable wireless") and re-connected through the command line. My connection is now stable. 

I would give credit to the user who suggested that network-manager routinely scans for available networks, but I previously closed that post and would have difficulty finding it again.
