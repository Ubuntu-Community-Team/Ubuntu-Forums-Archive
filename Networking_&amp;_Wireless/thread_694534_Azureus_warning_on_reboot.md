### Post by cozmicharlie on 2008-02-12
It is because you are not exiting Azureus when you shut down.  But, since you want to just shut down without exiting you can just ignore the message (click "hide" in the message) or go to the menu>tools>options>interface>alerts and there is a box that says "suppress alerts", check it and save.  That should do it.
