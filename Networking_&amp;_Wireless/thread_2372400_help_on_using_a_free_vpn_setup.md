### Post by SeijiSensei on 2017-09-25
Why on earth would you trust a free VPN provider?  How do you know the provider isn't decrypting all your traffic on the server end and selling some of your information to its clients?  In order to have security, you must know that both ends of the connection are secure.  I don't see anyway to verify that when it comes to random free VPN providers.

In the world of open source, the old adage "you get what you pay for" doesn't always apply.  When it comes to commercial services looking to make a profit, I'd trust the adage.
