### Post by mayahustle on 2009-03-28
I ended up rolling back to 8.04, and the card worked.  When you click on hardware drivers in Intrepid, and enable b43-fwcutter, it never gives you the option to fetch and extract firmware.  In 8.04 when you do the same thing it gives you the option.  I'm pretty sure that's what was wrong.  Wish I had known that before rolling back, there's probably some command line way to tell b43 to fetch and extract the firmware... I just didn't realize it at the time.
