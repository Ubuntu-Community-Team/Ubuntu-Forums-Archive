### Post by benmarvin on 2008-04-16
Most of your download speed has to do with the upload speed on the other end. I have about the same quoted speeds as you. And I've had downloads range from 5 k/sec withh 100 seeders to 900 k/sec with 4 seeders.
If you've got your ports open on the router and your download speeds set to unlimited, you've done all you can.
