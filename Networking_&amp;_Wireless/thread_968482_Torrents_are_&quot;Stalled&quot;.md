### Post by pete25r on 2008-12-16
How do you connect to the internet? 
Check to see what ports Ktorrent and other torrent apps are using and make sure those are the ports forwarded. 
In Ktorrent 3.0.1 you can turn on or enable Upnp which will tell your router that you need those ports forwarded, that is if your router supports Upnp.
Some ISP's modems have built in routers. You might have to check their for port forwarding as well.
