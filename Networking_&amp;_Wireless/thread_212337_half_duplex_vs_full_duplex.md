### Post by mips on 2006-07-10
Just get full-duplex although it's not always exactly full-duplex at all speeds. The down is usually faster than the up pipe.

Half duplex works on the same principle as a walkie talkie to simplify things. Only one person can talk at any one. With half duplex you can either send or receive data but not both at the same time.

With full duplex you can send and receive data at the same time.

If you get a Hayes compatible HARDWARE modem with a standard SERIAL port it should work fine.
