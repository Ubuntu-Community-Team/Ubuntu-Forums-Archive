### Post by w_1_n_d on 2009-08-02
Hello, that tells you the signal strength of your wireless network. If your not sure if your wireless card is working, just do this. connect to your wireless network. Then get the ip of  one of the devices on your network and go to the terminal and type ping then the ipadress of the device or computer you are pinging. So it would look like this ping 198.135.21.45. Then if you get a reply that means your wireless card is working fine, if u get anythng else besides that then you have something blocking the connection. I hope this helps.:KS:KS:KS:KS
