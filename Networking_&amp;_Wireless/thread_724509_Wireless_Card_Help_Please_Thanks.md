Unfortunately, that is predominantly about PCI (i.e. desktop) wifi cards.

@fatalflight:
Compaq (HP) laptops sometimes have a BIOS restriction on internal mini-PCI cards that will work with them.

If that is not the case, then I would recommend this: [http://efficientpc.co.uk/components/intel3945abg/](http://efficientpc.co.uk/components/intel3945abg/) (just get it from a local supplier).

Otherwise, Atheros cards are generally available cheap on ebay (amongst other places) - my brother got one recently.  Just check if it is compatible here: [http://madwifi.org/wiki/Compatibility/Atheros](http://madwifi.org/wiki/Compatibility/Atheros)
