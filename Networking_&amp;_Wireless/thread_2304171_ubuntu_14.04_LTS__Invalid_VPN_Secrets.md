Hello, I am not sure if this will help you, as I am not sure of your VPN provider or subscription details. I recently found the same error code ****** failed to connect: Invalid VPN Secrets. I use hide.me VPN, so through their support link I reported my problem. What the representative told me was " Our FREE Subscribers are not allowed to use OpenVPN. That VPN Protocol is reserved for PLUS and PREMIUM members." The representative then gave me trial access to the PREMIUM, and the connection established fine. After confirmation, the representative informed me I could use PPTP Protocol for the free version. I am not sure if this will help or not, however I have connected successfully. Hopefully something similar for you
