### Post by morrownr on 2021-08-19
For those that might be looking to purchase a USB WiFi adapter, I maintain a site that collects information to help you make more informed decisions. I started the site - [https://github.com/morrownr/USB-WiFi](https://github.com/morrownr/USB-WiFi) - last year in an effort to help Linux users. USB WiFi adapters are very handy because they are portable but your happiness with USB WiFi adapters depends on many factors so jumping over to amazon.com to buy the hot, cheap adapter of the day is likely to lead to disappointment. What are the factors that Linux users should be looking for? Good to excellent quality and a chipset that is supported with in-kernel drivers. A lot of information is provided at the main site but below is a short document that is provided at the site. The Short List is a list of 16 adapters that are known to be good quality or better and are plug and play (no driver installation is required.) Enjoy.
