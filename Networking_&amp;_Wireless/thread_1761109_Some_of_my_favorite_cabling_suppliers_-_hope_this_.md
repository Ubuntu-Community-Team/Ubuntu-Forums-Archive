### Post by kobee4mvp on 2011-05-17
Hi, I stumbled on this forum and thought I would share my experience with my favorite suppliers. Hope you enjoy and please don't spam my thread with BS because this is only my opinion.

For Cat5e/cat6 cables cables I like to go to [www.cablestogo.com]("http://www.cabletogo.com"). I go with them even though they are higher priced because their products are UL ROHS and ETL certified. Can you find cheaper suppliers? SURE YOU CAN however, chances are they are selling Copper Clad Aluminum instead of regular bare copper like they are supposed to. 

For Fiber Optic Cables, patch panels and Audio Video equipment I like to go with [www.magnusdeal.com]("http://www.magnusdeal.com"). They are very well priced and always give me discount coupons from their newsletter. They are also UL ROHS and ETL certified and never had a problem using them.

For SVGA Cables I go to [www.cablesunlimited.com]("http://www.cablesunlimited.com"). A little on the higher end but their SVGA cables are class 2 rated and always performed well when I used them. 

Hope this helps you with your cabling!
