### Post by ssuehr on 2008-02-01
Hello,

The amount of bandwidth needed will depend entirely on the traffic being sent through the pipe.  Usually companies tend to give better SLAs for a T1 and, hopefully, they don't oversell them like they do consumer products such as DSL and cable modems.  Therefore, the 1.54mbps of a T1 is almost always faster than the 1.5 of an aDSL connection.

If budget isn't an issue I'd personally get a fractional DS3; then I can ramp up the speed without having to pay the telco connection fees all over again.  But it's impossible to tell what bandwidth you'll need without knowing the traffic pattern.
