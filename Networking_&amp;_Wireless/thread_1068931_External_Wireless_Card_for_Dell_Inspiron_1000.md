FYI: I went with the Linksys WUSB54GC Compact Wireless-G USB Adapter.  Works out of the box.  The supported wireless card list was extremely useful.
Two issues: reception is weak at times and it does not automatically connect to my hidden wireless network.   Hidden networks seems like a problem others have experienced:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=923024&highlight=hidden+wireless+networks](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=923024&highlight=hidden+wireless+networks)
