### Post by tbfr on 2007-12-06
I wouldn't worry too much about the security of your computer, but still it might be a good idea to install a tool to manage your firewall, I use firestarter for this purpose. This should already prevent  someone from seeing files on your computer that you (maybe accidentally) share on the network.

The biggest problem is the data you submit on the wireless connection. If you don't use secure connections when surfing the web or fetching emails, your transmitted data can easily be seen by others (among this usernames and passwords). So only login to websites via https:// and only use an email client if your mailserver supports SSL. And use different passwords for your computer, your email and for example this forum.
