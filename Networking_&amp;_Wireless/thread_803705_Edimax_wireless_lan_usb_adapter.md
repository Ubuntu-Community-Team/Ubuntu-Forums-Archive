### Post by garyedwardjohnston on 2008-05-22
Total noob I see.  That's okay everyone is a noob at some point.

Firstly, the drivers you have are probably not for Linux.  Very few components come with Linux drivers.

Secondly, Linux itself has difficulty with USB 2.0 (high speed) USB devices.  A simple fix for this is to disable USB 2.0 (high speed) from your BIOS.

Good Luck!

PS I hope you know how to change setting in BIOS.
