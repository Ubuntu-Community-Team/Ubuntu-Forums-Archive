### Post by wormser on 2007-06-20
The manual for your router is [here]("http://www.edimax.com.tw/download/manual/AR-7064Sg+_M.zip").

Basically you need to log into your router.  User your browser and put in 192.168.1.1 (192.168.0.1 sometimes).  It depends on your network and should be your gateway's address.  Then log in.  The manual should have the default user name and password in it.  Next find the port forwarding section and open the needed ports.
