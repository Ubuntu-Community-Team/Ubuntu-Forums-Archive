Actually you should change your router encryption to WPA2-AES or WPA2 only and ```
echo "options iwlwifi 11n_disable=8" | sudo tee -a /etc/modprobe.d/iwlwifi.conf
```

Turning power management off for the wifi card wouldn't hurt either
```
sudo iwconfig wlan0 power off
```

I wouldn't worry about the linked post as that was a case where iwlwifi did not yet support the specific wireless card, your issue is different from that
