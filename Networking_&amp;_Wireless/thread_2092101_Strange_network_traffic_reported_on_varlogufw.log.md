### Post by Michele S on 2012-12-07
I found that all those connections are to port 51413 UPD.

michele@raspberrypi /var/log $ sudo fuser -n udp 51413
51413/udp:            1896

1896 is the transmission-deamon, so all the connection are from that deamon!
