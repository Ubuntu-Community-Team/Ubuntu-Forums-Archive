### Post by chili555 on 2012-05-04
> Is it enough to add dns-nameservers xxx.xxx.xxx.xxx to /etc/interfaces (will try now)?Yes, indeed.```
auto lo
iface lo inet loopback

auto eth0
iface eth0 inet static
address 192.168.1.200
netmask 255.255.255.0
gateway 192.168.1.254
dns-nameservers 8.8.8.8 192.168.1.254 some.other.back.up
```
