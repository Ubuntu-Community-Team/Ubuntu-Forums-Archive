### Post by jalleballe on 2018-04-08
I was happily working along a couple of hours ago, when suddenly my connection dropped and the wifi menu showed no available networks. I rebooted and saw "No Wi-Fi Adapter Found" under Wi-Fi Settings. After much googling, updating of kernels and hair-pulling I have not managed to resolve this on my own. Please help?

Wireless script output: [URL="http://paste.ubuntu.com/p/9TSgcMVr2f/"]http://paste.ubuntu.com/p/9TSgcMVr2f/
[/URL]

EDIT: Fixed it! For some reason I had the wrong firmware. From dmesg:

```
iwlwifi 0000:03:00.0: Direct firmware load for iwlwifi-7260-17.ucode failed with error -2
[    6.141133] iwlwifi 0000:03:00.0: no suitable firmware found!
[    6.141173] iwlwifi 0000:03:00.0: iwlwifi-7260-17 is required
```

The .ucode-file present was named iwlwifi-7260-13. Weird. Just downloaded the right one and viola! Wifi is back.
