### Post by rob150588 on 2010-10-24
Hi Mkaveli.

I had the same sort of problem with my wireless here. Turned out it was a problem with the wireless node, not the system.

It would work fine for about 10-15 mins, then the connection would drop out. The networks SSID was still visible, but the passphrase would not register and it would keep on asking.

As a quick fix I just restarted the wireless and it was fine for a while (say a day or two). Then it would happen again. I ended up replacing the wireless node and it's not happened since.

That may not help you, but worked for me.
