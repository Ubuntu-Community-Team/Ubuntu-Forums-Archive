### Post by jwenting on 2006-01-07
Reconnected my laptop to the home network (what there is of it) today for the first time in 2 weeks and (despite no changes to software anywhere) it failed to work.

After over an hour puzzling over software settings that might have reset themselves (you never know with autoupdate options and things like that) I noticed that all the connection lights on the switch were out (all connection lights on the NICs were on).
Before hopping off to the computer store for a new switch I decided to try switching the switch off and on again and lo and behold all network connections came back to life immediately.

Might be a sign to get a new switch just in case ;)

so always check the least obvious thing first when the network fails :KS
