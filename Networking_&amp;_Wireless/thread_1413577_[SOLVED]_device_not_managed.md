### Post by JohnE1 on 2010-02-22
I tried all the suggestions I could find here, at launchpad, and all over the net.

In the process of following the steps of restarting the gnome-network-manager and restarting networking, I discovered that ddclient was being run.

Using Synaptic Package Manager, I uninstalled ddclient and restarted the machine.

The network management in GNOME is now functioning properly!!
