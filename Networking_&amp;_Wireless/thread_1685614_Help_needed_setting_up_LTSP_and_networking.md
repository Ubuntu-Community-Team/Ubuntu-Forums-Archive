### Post by jkinney23 on 2011-02-17
hiya
i see your post is a little old and i am not an expert...i'm struggling with my own ltsp setup :)  however i should be able to answer your questions...

for cabling you can use straight through cables if you have a hub or switch (recommend switch)...crossover cable is ONLY needed when connecting one machine directly to the other.

your hub/switch should be at least 100 Mbps for your client to function satisfactorily.  a typical router will also work assuming you are comfortable configuring and turning off it's dhcp feature.

hope that helps...when i've gotten my own setup running i will point you towards the links that got things working for me if you are still having trouble :)
good luck!
