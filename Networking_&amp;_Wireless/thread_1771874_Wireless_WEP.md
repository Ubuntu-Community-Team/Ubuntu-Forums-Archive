### Post by MunkyJunky on 2011-06-02
For general security, it's highly advisable to switch to WPA encryption if all of your devices support it - WEP is very easy to crack. It may be a minor inconvenience to reconfigure multiple devices, but the additional security for your network is (in my opinion, at least) DEFINITELY worth it.
