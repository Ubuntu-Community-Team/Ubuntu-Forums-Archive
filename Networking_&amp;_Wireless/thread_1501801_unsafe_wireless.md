### Post by Ichido on 2010-06-04
What Version of Ubuntu are you using?
Do you have a home wireless Router?
What is the web site address gives you the "Tunnel" statement?

Some simple answers are:

Your PC running Ubuntu is safe from Virus, Trojans, Worms, Key-loggers.

Anytime your sending info over a Wireless Connection, the info can be "Intercepted" and read, unless you use "Encryption" like WPA or WPA2.

On your Home Network, you should use MAC Filtering in addition to Encryption.

MAC Address Filtering (**M**edia **A**ccess **C**ontrol and Not Macintosh/Apple) is where you list in your Wireless Router, which MAC Address' are allowed to use your network.

Encryption is where your info is Encrypted so anyone 'picking-up' your Transmissions should not be able to read what you are sending or receiving.
