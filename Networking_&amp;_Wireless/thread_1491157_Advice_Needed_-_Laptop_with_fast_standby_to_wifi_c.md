For the majority of my development work I use a workstation.  So I'd like the new laptop to be a secondary dev machine with frequent/quick casual usage (web & email).  For the most part, I keep it on the kitchen bar/counter. It would be great to improve the speed of the network startup in my current Latitude, but as it'll be replaced soon anyways, I'm more interested in what other current hardware that is out there that people have had good experiences with in terms of network/wifi startup/connection after coming back from Standby/Suspend (and perhaps Hibernate mode as well).
