### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-05-07
I do not have Ubuntu Studio 14.04 installed but I do have Xubuntu 14.04 instead. I think they have the same User Interface. 

a) There should be an Network Manager icon in the top panel among the group of icons on the right. If you have a working wired connection it will look like two arrows going in opposite directions. If you do not have any connection (wired or wireless) it will look like an upside down cone. Click on it. You should see a list of wireless access points that are in range. Your router should be in the list. Now it is up to you.

b) Click on the mouse icon at the top left and select Settings Manager. Then select Network Connections. Then click Add. A dialog will open and it will have a panel that is a drop-down menu. WiFi should be in the list. Again, it is now up to you.
