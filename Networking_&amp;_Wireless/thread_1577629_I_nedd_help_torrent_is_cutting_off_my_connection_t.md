### Post by Vaphell on 2010-09-19
Unrestricted torrent can overwhelm crappy routers so they are unable to handle all the traffic generated and hang (router reset often helps and some limit on the total number of connection is desired in such a case), your upload bandwidth can be totally saturated thus all other requests are crowded out (internet works in request/response way, without the request going out first, there will be no response ever -> upload speed should be limited to let's say 80% of max to leave some outbound bandwidth for other activities) or maybe your ISP simply cuts you off when detecting torrent traffic.

### Post by SeijiSensei on 2010-09-19
Oftentimes the problem is with the *number of connections* the torrent client establishes, not so much the bandwidth.  I suggest limiting the number of connections in your client; I've found a number like 60 works better with wireless routers than the defaults which are often like 200.
