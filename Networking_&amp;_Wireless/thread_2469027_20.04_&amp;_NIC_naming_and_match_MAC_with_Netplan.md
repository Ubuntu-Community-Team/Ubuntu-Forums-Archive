### Post by TheFu on 2021-11-17
netplan replaced ifupdown which is why there isn't any /etc/network/interfaces anymore.
Network-manager is for desktops, not servers.

I'm not sure what the issue is.  Use netplan files to control network settings. I wouldn't even change the names, since the new naming convention is what you'll find in the real world.
