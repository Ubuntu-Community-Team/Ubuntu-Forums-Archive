### Post by SeijiSensei on 2010-10-28
Unless I'm badly mistaken you need to install equipment at the telco's central office to provide DSL connectivity.  It's not a point-to-point service like a phone call.

The best you could do would be a modem-to-modem link over dialup.  

You might be able to get [ISDN]("http://www.dslreports.com/forum/r23747804-ISDN-Still-around-Can-I-get-it") lines, but I'm doubtful there's much support for them these days.

If he's in the US, maybe he should talk to [Speakeasy]("http://www.speakeasy.net/service/broadband/dsl/").  They might be able to work out a deal with his telco that he can't get on his own.
