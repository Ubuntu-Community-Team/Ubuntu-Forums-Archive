### Post by gordintoronto on 2015-03-01
If you can follow these instructions, you should be OK: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper)

Generally, it's a good idea to Google "[device name] linux" before buying hardware. Many USB Wi-Fi adapters work as soon as you plug them in and reboot. Printer, webcams and Wi-Fi adapters have varying levels of support.

Often, it's useful to mention what version of Ubuntu you are using.
