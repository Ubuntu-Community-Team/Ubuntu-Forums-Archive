### Post by funkadoobius on 2008-11-08
I FIXED IT

after a great deal screwing around with sysctl, i found an un-activated option that dynamicly adjusts the TCP RCVBUFs. after that option was turned on and all of my firewall interfaces were bounced i was able to access the internet. 

net.ipv4.tcp_moderate_rcvbuf=1

w00t

im still not 100% sure why this was preventing me from connecting, but it was the only thing i changed in that round of testing and i noticed immediate results.
