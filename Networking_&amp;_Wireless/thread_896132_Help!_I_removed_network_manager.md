### Post by picpak on 2008-08-20
Do you have a laptop or extra computer sitting around? What I did when this happened to me was download network-manager (and its dependencies) from [http://packages.ubuntu.com](http://packages.ubuntu.com) onto a flashdrive, go back to your computer, plug it in and install them from there.

Alternatively, you could boot from the Live CD, download the files onto your flashdrive (or mount your hard drive and put them there), reboot, and reinstall them from there.

Hope this helps!
