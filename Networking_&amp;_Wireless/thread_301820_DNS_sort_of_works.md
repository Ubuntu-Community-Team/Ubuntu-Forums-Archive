### Post by rickyjones on 2006-11-17
What kind of router is it? I ask, because most residential routers that I work with will /seem/ like the DNS server, but I'm pretty sure they just forward the packets onward.

Do you know what your ISP's DNS servers are? If so, try those. If not, try the OpenDNS servers as a test.

* 208.67.222.222
* 208.67.220.220

I've used those in some instances and they work quite well.
