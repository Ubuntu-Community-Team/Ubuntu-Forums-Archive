### Post by Captain Colonoscopy on 2007-06-14
You more than likely have a broadcom wireless chipset in thtat HP laptop that is causing the problems. Here is a link with some info: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCardsBroadcom](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCardsBroadcom)

You can try the bcmfwcutter, it seems to work off an on for me. Always works at my house with WEP security, sometimes works with WPA at the office.
