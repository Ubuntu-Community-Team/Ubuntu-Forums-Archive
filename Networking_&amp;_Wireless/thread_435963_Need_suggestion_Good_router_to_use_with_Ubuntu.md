### Post by GolfGeek on 2007-05-07
I have been using both netgear and linksys with absolutely no problems. The netgear is wireless MIMO with 4 additional ports and the linksys is wired only. Sorry I can't give you a model #. I might make one suggestion: if you're on the fence, get the wireless with 4 ports. You can always disable the wireless aspect but it can be handy in the future.
