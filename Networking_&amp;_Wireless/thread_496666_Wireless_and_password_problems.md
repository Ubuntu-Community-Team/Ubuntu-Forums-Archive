### Post by Carleton91 on 2007-07-09
The password you're entering might not be the right one.  It might not sound like a lot of help, but I've had issues where I typed in my router password rather than the network password.  

What helped me figure out was to open a browser on a computer that is hooked up to the network and type in Mozilla Firefox, 172.16.0.1 since that is the default router ip address.  Then you can check what kind of encrpytion it is and what the password is.  Again, I recognize that this may be completely useless to you, but maybe it will help.
