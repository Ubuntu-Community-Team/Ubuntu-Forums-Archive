### Post by militantduck on 2011-08-12
Alfa AWUS036H [b/g USB]
Ubiquiti SRC [a/b/g Cardbus]
Ubiquiti SRX [a/b/g ExpressCard]
Airpcap series [USB]

as suggested by aircrack team, these are the best to buy.

I have used a netgear WNA1100 atheros based card, although the drivers do require patching, it does work well.
