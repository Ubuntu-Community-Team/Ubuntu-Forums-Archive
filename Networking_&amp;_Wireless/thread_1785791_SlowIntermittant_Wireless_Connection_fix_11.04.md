### Post by userfaulty on 2011-06-18
Search for Users and Groups

Open the Users and Groups component->click your account name->advanced settings->Priviledges

make sure "Connect to wireless and ethernet networks" is checkmarked.

by default mine was not.

after checkmarking and closing, my internet seems a lot faster and it feels like it did in windows.

I also checkmarked "use audio devices" and "use video devices".

Could this be causing wireless network drops when the adapter is reregistering for dhcp, causing dvds to fail to load, etc.?

I am still somewhat new to all this but I can already tell a huge difference in my internet speed and just thought I would pass it on.
