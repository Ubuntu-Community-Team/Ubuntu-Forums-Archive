### Post by Brandon Williams on 2010-09-06
Are you getting some indication that your computer is not making optimum use of your DSL connection? If so, what kind of problems are you seeing?

I ask because I know that the default TCP/IP configuration in Linux is actually quite good. Unless you're seeing an obvious problem, it's unlikely that you'll be able to generate a noticeable improvement by tweaking any the TCP/IP conifg parameters. I'm not saying it's impossible, but it's probably not worth the effort unless you are sure that your system is not making full use of the available bandwidth.
