### Post by dmizer on 2009-04-14
> **kehon said:**
> i want to share files through ftp in nautilus i cannot share through ftp or access ftp folders and i would like to also set up a ftp server and choose certain files to share is that possible without me having to copy those files to a certain folder to share

As with any share, you cannot share individual files; you must share a folder. This is true with Windows, Mac, and Linux.

Do you want to share files over the Internet, or do you want to share files only on your LAN? Sharing files over the Internet is significantly more dangerous and requires more effort to configure your FTP server so that your files and your computer stay safe.

For configuring an FTP server that's fairly safe to use over the internet, please see the 3rd link in my sig. For configuring an FTP server to share files across your LAN only, see the 5th link in my sig.
