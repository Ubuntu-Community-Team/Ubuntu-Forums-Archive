### Post by ian-weisser on 2013-12-15
Do you mean Firefox bookmarks synchronized across several machines? (Sync)
It it easy to set up. Try Firefox --> Tools --> Preferences --> Sync

Or do you mean Firefox bookmarks that dynamically change in addition to user-created bookmarks? (Dyanmic bookmarks)
Synchronizing those across machines is more difficult.
