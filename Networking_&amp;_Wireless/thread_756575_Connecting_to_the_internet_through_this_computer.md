First of all, what type of your Internet connection you are using? 

**Case 1. Ethernet LAN in Campus**                   or                      **Case 2. Dial-up/Modem/ADSL**

I believe you are using the ADSL since you do mentioned about the router. There is no ways to connect your Linux box to the Internet since the Linux box itself do not have the capability of routing the traffic.

If your are in [COLOR="Blue"]Case 1[/COLOR]; you will able to connect your PS3 to the Internet through the Linux box.

If you are in [COLOR="Blue"]Case 2[/COLOR]; I afraid you not able to do so, because at last to connect via you Linux box, you definitely need a router as a gateway.
