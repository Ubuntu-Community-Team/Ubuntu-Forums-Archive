### Post by howefield on 2011-07-05
Perhaps I missed the second part of your question, it isn't accessing the drive you are having a problem with, it is creating a launcher icon for it ?

If that's the case, try right clicking on the desktop and select "Create Launcher"

In the name field type MyBookWorld (or whatever you want)
In the command field type nautilus smb://mybookworld/public
In the Comment field type NAS (or whatever you want)

You can change the path in the command field to suit your needs, all my stuff is in the public folder. 

Click on the "spring" icon to change to suit.

Drag your new icon on to the launcher.
