### Post by mvario on 2010-08-01
Ad-hoc mode is when two clients communicate with each other without an access point.

Stock access points will not communicate with each other.  You need hardware capable of bridge mode, client mode or WDS.  It is possible that installing third-party firmware on one or more of your access points/routers may give you some of those capabilities but I couldn't tell you for sure.  You'll have to research available firmware replacement options for each of your devices.

On the other hand you can get a USB wireless NIC for less than $10.
