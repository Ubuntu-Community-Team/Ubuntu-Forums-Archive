### Post by tlongren on 2009-10-30
Hello,

It seems that when I have two active ethernet devices, I can't reach the internet. Read on for my setup.

eht0 is my internet connected device. eth1 is my lan. In network-manager, I set eth1 to manual. gave it an ip of 192.168.1.1, bcast of 255.255.255.0, gateway of 192.168.1.1 and used my own dns servers.

Once I enable eth1 by clicking it in network-manager, I can no longer reach the internet. If I disable eth1, I can hit the internet all day long.

To get other pc's on my network access to the internet I had to set eth1 to DHCP in network-manager. Then went into a terminal and did "sudo ifconfig eth1 192.168.1.1".

Is this a routing issue maybe? Something to do with network-manager making a gateway for this device a requirement?

Has anyone else had these problems?

Oh, and this is on a fresh install of 9.10.
