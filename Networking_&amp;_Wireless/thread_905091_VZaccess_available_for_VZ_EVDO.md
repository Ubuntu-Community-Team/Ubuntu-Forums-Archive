### Post by bliffle on 2008-08-30
I found a way to use my ubuntu systems on the EVDO network: I installed a Cradlepoint CTR500 wireless router with the USB727 pluged into the USB port of the CTR500. Now, any ubuntu (or XP or even Mac) 802.11 WiFi can connect to the CTR500, which is in turn connected to the EVDO network.

So that will work when I'm at home. But I'd like to be able to grab the USB727 when I'm going out and about, and not drag the CTR500 along and find a 110VAC supply. The USB727 is just a USB stick powered off the laptop.
