### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-03-09
What you want is something called "network sharing".  There are a few ways to do it.  You will have to experiment to see what works:  [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Internet/ConnectionSharing](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Internet/ConnectionSharing)

For better performance and less interference, run a "clothesline" Cat6 line to the shed.  Bury it if possible, otherwise just string it so it is out of the way and keep the squirrels from chewing it.
