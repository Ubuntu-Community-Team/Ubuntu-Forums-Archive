### Post by jimbren on 2007-03-02
Take a look at Synaptic and you'll find a slew of software available. You can get more by [enabling the Universe and Multiverse repositories]("https://help.ubuntu.com/6.10/ubuntu/desktopguide/C/extra-repositories.html"), and there are other 3rd party repositories out there that have other software.  

I use Kubuntu, so I can't tell you exactly how to get to Synaptic from Ubuntu, but I'm sure you'll find it.  

The [Ubuntu documentation team ]("https://help.ubuntu.com")has some excellent guides for getting started--check 'em out.
