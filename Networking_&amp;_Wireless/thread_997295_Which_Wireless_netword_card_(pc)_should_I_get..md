### Post by cmnorton on 2008-11-30
You'll get a pile of answers on this. My suggestion is to look at your wireless router brand. Do you like it? I like my Linksys, and it's fairly old by equipment standards. It dates back 3 1/2 years. 

Right now, I use the wireless card in my Thinkpad T61p; it is built in. However, I have a Linksys card, which, being newer than the router, is backwards compatible with the router, but also supports additional network protocols.

My other system, a Windows XP, uses a USB Linksys wireless device that came with the router and modem on a special equipment deal from Verizon.
