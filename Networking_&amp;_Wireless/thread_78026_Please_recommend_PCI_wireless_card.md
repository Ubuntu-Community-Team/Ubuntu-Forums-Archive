### Post by breakthestate on 2005-10-17
[quote=daahli]I was wondering whether somebody here could please recommend a wireless PCI card that is readily available and will work straight out of the box.[/quote] 

[Please see the wiki entry for this.]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCards?highlight=%28wireless%29") But read carefully! Many times a new version of a card will have an entirely different chipset. If nobody at the store knows what version it is, or they do not seem sure, do not take their word for it. Either ask to open up the card and look at it, or go somewhere else so you know what you are getting. I recommend a Mom and Pop's store - They will most likely know the version and chipset. Plus they sell less cards and are more likely to have an earlier version that has been tested and has a wiki entry. ;)
