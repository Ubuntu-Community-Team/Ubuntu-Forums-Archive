### Post by tlk2drew on 2013-12-07
Got Ubunutu 13.04 running and like a lot of other people, my wifi speeds dropped in a big way.  I have the Intel n6300 in my laptop.  I tried just about everything but what seemed to get it was my actual router.  Under QoS, I disabled WMM.  I tried that not because of Ubuntu, but someone had told me that the WMM was causing slowing issues with his IPAD (or some device he uses like one).

Anyways, give that a go and hope it helps.  ;)
