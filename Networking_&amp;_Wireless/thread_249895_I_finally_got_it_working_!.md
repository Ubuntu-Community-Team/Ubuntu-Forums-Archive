### Post by pruyss on 2006-09-03
I had some weird problems with the Broadcom 43xx based wireless interface in my new Dell Latitude D420.
I got it working relatively quick using ndiswrapper with my access point at work.  But at home, with my U.S.Robotics 8054 access point, it would not associate, even though this AP worked just fine with all my other wireless stuff.
I was almost gonna buy a new access point when I discovered that I could download new firmware for the AP from the [www.usr.com](www.usr.com) website.  I was going to discard the AP anyway, so it wouldn't hurt trying.
Guess what - everything works like a charm now !

BTW : The installation report of my D420 can be found at [http://users.ugent.be/~pruyss/D420/](http://users.ugent.be/~pruyss/D420/)
