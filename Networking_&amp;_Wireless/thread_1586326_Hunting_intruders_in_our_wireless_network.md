### Post by arvevans on 2010-10-01
Log into your WiFi hub/router and find the "Connections" screen.  This will show you the IP addresses of those who are using your system.

Or

Use an IP capture program, like EtherApe, to monitor traffic on your Wireless LAN.  This will let you see who is using the system and you can even snoop to see what they are doing.  You will need SUDO or root access to monitor their traffic.
