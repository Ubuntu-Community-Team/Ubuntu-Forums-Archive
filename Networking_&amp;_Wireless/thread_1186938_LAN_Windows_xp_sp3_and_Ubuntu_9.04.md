### Post by Stolea on 2009-06-14
If you want to be able to network 2 computers and have access to the internet you have to either have a broadband router that has 1 internet port and at least 2 lan ports, or have a small hub. The router is the better option as it usually incorporates a firewall and allows assigned ip's for your computer.
To setup a Ubuntu-Windoze network look here [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=202605]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=202605").
