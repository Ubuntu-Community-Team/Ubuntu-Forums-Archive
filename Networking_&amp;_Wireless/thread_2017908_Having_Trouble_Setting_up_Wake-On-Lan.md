### Post by Alevins5 on 2012-07-05
Oops. Well I double checked my power settings after I posted that and it turns out that my Bios had two suspend modes. After some research I found that one was a deeper sleep mode and one was "softer" sleep mode. Selecting the softer mode allows for remote wake from suspend mode. I have yet to try from a complete shutdown. I am not sure that would work, though.
