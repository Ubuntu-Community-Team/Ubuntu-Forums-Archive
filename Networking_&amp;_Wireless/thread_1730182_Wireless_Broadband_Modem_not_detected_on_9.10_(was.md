### Post by gmuhammad on 2011-04-15
Accidently I got my problem solved. After creating a **"Mobile Broadband Connection"** manually from **System-Preferences->Network Connections**, 2 or 3 times I restarted my computer and mount an NTFS partition. After that I checked on Top Bar and I found that the connection I created was there and I try to connect which was successful.
