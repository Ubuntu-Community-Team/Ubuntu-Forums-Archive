### Post by OrthoResident on 2013-01-22
Old thread was 4 years old so I started this today

to connect to NYU wireless:

Security: WPA2
Authentification: PEAP
Anonymous Identity: leave it blank
Certificate:  in etc/ssl the correct certificate to chose is "AddTrust_External_Root.pem"
PEAP version: automatic
inner authentication: MSCHAPv2
username: [I]your NYU net ID
[/I]password: [I]your password

[/I]Hope this helps some people out
(ubuntu 12.04)
