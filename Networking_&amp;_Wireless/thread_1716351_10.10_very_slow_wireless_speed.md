```
### Post by jawilljr on 2011-03-28
I had the same symptoms with my Ralink rt3070 usb dongle...very high ping times with lots of packet loss across the WLAN. What I did to fix the problem was to uninstall network-manager and replace it with wicd.

I also turned off power management for the USB wireless device with this command

```
sudo iwconfig wlan0 power off
```Now my ping times across the LAN are consistently below 1ms and my download speeds are back to normal.

Jerry
```
