### Post by spd106 on 2007-05-08
When your PC is in sleep mode it can't really do much of anything. You can sometimes set the NIC to stay on and listen for wake on lan packets, but that's about it.

If your PC supports suspend or hibernate then that's about the best your going to get in terms of fast re-connection as your apps will resume straight back to where they were.
