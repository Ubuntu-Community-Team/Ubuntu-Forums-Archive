### Post by Project PWNED on 2010-01-14
TP-Link TL-WN321G. $9 on Amazon.com and it works out of the box with Ubuntu 9.10/Backtrack 4. I get really great signals from it and it's so simple looking. Most people would mistake it for a USB thumb drive if the red light didn't flash. (Its my avatar and the end has a cap when its not in use)

Vendors will not have "Linux drivers" on their site, you get the support for Linux in the kernel and operating system itself.

[http://linux-wless.passys.nl/](http://linux-wless.passys.nl/) is a good site also to look at for Linux wireless card support, but look elsewhere online to find other sites to find support.
