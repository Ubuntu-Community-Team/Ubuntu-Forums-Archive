Total bonehead here...I didn't realize that Firestarter was the reason I could not access secure sites.  I remembered that I turned it on the other day, and since then I couldn't access any sites where I had to enter a password.  I just turned it off, and now everything is fine...I can enter my Yahoo and MSN Messenger chat through GAIM and all is good.  

Hey, aside from being a total Linux newbie nobody said I was actually smart.  

I am, however, going to have to learn more about Firestarter though, cos obviously it's a good idea to have it on.
