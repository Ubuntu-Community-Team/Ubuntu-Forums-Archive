### Post by varunendra on 2013-10-24
Is it a GSM/3G dongle?

Did the configuration wizard run to configure it? Or you manually configured it?

Have you made sure the dial no., the plan and accordingly the APN, and the selected Network Type are correct?

The last time I used a Reliance dongle (in 2008-9), it also needed ID-Password to be supplied, both of which were same as the mobile (or currently the SIM) no. Have you confirmed if yours needs that and is saved correctly?

And above all, do you even have the dongle detected by Network Manager? Does it even show it and try to connect?

**PS:**
As a side note, Reliance and Tata Docomo are two services that I personally like to stay away from. Too crappy wherever I've tried them and are doubtlessly something I shouldn't say on this forum. Be aware that a 2G/3G connection success rate and stability greatly depends upon the quality of service from the ISPs end. That alone is more than often a reason for such problems you are having.
