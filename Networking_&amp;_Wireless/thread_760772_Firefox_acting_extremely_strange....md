### Post by djronh on 2008-04-20
Go to system-administration-synaptic package manager and open using your password.
Click on search towards top right corner. Enter firefox. You will see a list of packages related to firefox. Right click on firefox and select the option you want. Click apply on main menu bar. A box will appear with a green tick in it, do not click that until you have viewed the changes to be made, some packages if removed also remove your desktop!!! BAD!!! This will be shown in the details by loads of removals not just one or two.

### Post by djronh on 2008-04-20
I have forgot to mention that removing the hidden .mozilla folder in you home folder gets rid of all you personal details..history,bookmarks ect. In firefox you can export your bookmarks and import them back into a fresh firefox. Viewing hidden files is achieved from the view menu of nautilus and selecting show hidden files, then go to your home folder.

Also you need flash plugin installed, ipv6 disabled and I would recommend using java5 not 6. At this point you will have the best browser in the world! 
OK so its a pain..but worth it!
