### Post by lose_the_grimm on 2009-02-05
I found the solution.

My ISP (Brighthouse) was handing out a MTU of 576.

I basically used the info from here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/archive/index.php/t-996396.html](http://ubuntuforums.org/archive/index.php/t-996396.html)
