Laptop running Xubuntu 16.04 64-bit.

My bluetooth headset pairs and connects to my computer, but I don't get any audio through the headset. The headset is Bluetooth 3.0 with these compatible profiles:
A2DP (Advanced Audio Distribution Profile)
AVRCP (Audio Video Remote Control Profile)
HFP (Hands-free Profile)
HSP (Headset Profile)

I tried connecting the headset as Audio Sink and as Headset. In both cases, the headset is listed in the Output Devices tab of Volume Control (pavucontrol), but cannot switch the audio to it. Clicking the check box 'set as fallback' shows the box is checked and redirects the volume control buttons on the laptop to control the headset's volume (can see this in Volume Control, and the volume control buttons no longer control the laptop speaker volume), but does nothing to redirect audio from laptop speakers to headset.

Any suggestions on how to diagnose this?
