> **snowpine said:**
> Broadcom 4312 is easy to get working.

1. Connect to internet using wired ethernet
2. Go to the System menu and choose Administration, Hardware Drivers
3. Click to activate the Broadcom STA driver
4. Reboot and enjoy!

(this assumes you're using the latest Ubuntu 9.10)

It could be a key on the keyboard... eg. you have to press the "fn" button and one of the F-keys simultaneously to turn on wireless. Usually the letters on the fn button are a certain color and some icons on the F-keys like F2 are too. One of those probably turns wireless on or off.
