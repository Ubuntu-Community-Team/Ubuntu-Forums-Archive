### Post by Soulman on 2004-12-22
I highly recommend the cisco aironet 350.  They are selling loads of them on ebay.  This card works out of the box on linux and is the best supported wireless card that I have come across.  Hope this helps.  Other people have also enjoyed success with Dlink 650's or even cards based on the orinoco chipset.  Goodluck!  The forums here give a great deal of information on various cards and the problems that many of them have.  The cisco is the way to go though as far as the cards that I have messed with.
