### Post by papibe on 2013-06-17
Hi caleb12134.

A few thoughts:

You don't have to manage your own DNS records so people can access your servers. GoDaddy or other companies can handle them for you.

In case you "really" need to do it, you have to first register your DNS servers, then wait the a grace period, and then you can point your records to them (details [here]("http://support.godaddy.com/help/article/668/registering-your-own-nameservershosts")).

Hope it helps. Let us know how it goes.
