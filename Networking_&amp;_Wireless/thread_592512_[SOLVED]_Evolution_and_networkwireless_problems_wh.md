### Post by dimbulb1024 on 2007-10-26
After a fresh install of 7.10 I was having issues with being unable to connect with wired or wireless after resuming from suspend. I was restarting my laptop to get the network working again.
I found if I set Evolution to offline prior to suspending that problem has gone away for now. (I have been doing this the past two days.)
