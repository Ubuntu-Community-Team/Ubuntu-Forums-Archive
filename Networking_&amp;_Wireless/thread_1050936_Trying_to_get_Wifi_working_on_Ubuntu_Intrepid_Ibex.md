I assume this is a laptop, as you look for hotspots. What I recommend is for you to locate a wired connection, it will by far be the easiest way of doing this. Try an internet cafe. If you cannot, hook up to your friend's laptop with a ethernet cable and have him set it up so that his wireless connect is shared.

In order to find what wireless card you have, head to Applications>Accessories>Terminal and enter in
```
lspci
```
and post the output back here.
