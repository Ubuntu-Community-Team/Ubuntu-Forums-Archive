### Post by jeremy31 on 2018-11-12
I would set the router to a channel no higher than 11 as I have yet to see a Broadcom wifi card using Linux be able to use channels 12-14
I would guess that the router is on auto channel and that is why it worked in the past

### Post by kaktux on 2018-11-12
well - now you have seen one as i have been setting my channel to 13 for years - no autochannel ;) - and as i said it worked with other installations on that netbook before (meaning Maui Linux and just some days ago before installing Lubuntu - Bodhi Linux 5).
