### Post by steveneddy on 2010-05-03
In your phone go to the Bluetooth settings.

There will be a button or setting saying something like "make discoverable" or something to that effect.

The phone will say "Here I am" in bluetooth talk for about one minute.

It is during this time that you run the hcitool scan again in terminal.

Once the PC finds the phone, look at the phone and it will ask you if it is OK to connect to your PC.

Hope that helps.
