### Post by tamoneya on 2008-08-12
comcast does sell boxed sets which include a modem and everything.  You dont even need to call comcast I think.  You can pick them up at best buy or somewhere similar.  Basically you put a splitter in the incoming cable on the cable box.  Then one goes to the TV and the other to the modem.  Last time I checked though the first time you connect to the modem you need a windows (Mac may work as well) PC.  This is because the comcast software that allows you to configure the modem for their network and set it up with your account is windows only (maybe mac as well).  After that though you can use ubuntu permanently.  You may also want to have your latest comcast TV bill on hand as it will have account information on it that is necessary to configure the modem.
