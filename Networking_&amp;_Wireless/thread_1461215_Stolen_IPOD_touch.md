### Post by gallaau on 2010-04-23
Sure. I assume you ipod was configured to use your wireless AP at your house also. 
There are several wireless sniffers that you can download.
I don't have a touch but I assume when it was stolen the wireless was turned on and it is actively seeking a network or your home access point.
Unfortunately you will have to get very close to it and you will be very conspicuous walking around there yard with a laptop.
If you are really going to get into this you will need a directional antenna to DF "directional finding" this device.

Also you might want to call tech support for apple and itunes and let them know it was stolen. They will certainly be able to help if someone tries to register it. You should also have the SN of the device in your itunes app on your computer.
