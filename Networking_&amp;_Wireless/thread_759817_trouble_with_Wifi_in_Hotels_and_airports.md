### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-04-19
What kind of "erratic results" do you get?  A lot of the services make you authenticate, but usually it's done in a web browser, so you shouldn't really have any more trouble using Linux than you would on another operating system.  Airports and hotels also tend to be filled with bogus/private access points too, so it could be that what you think is an open wireless network is really a filtered one (it could be filtered by MAC address or something and it would not have a key, but would still refuse your connection), or that they're ad-hoc networks being broadcast by people who don't realize that their computer is doing that, but which won't really let you connect to the Internet.
