### Post by varunendra on 2014-08-17
Those virtualbox modules only mean that the OP may have virtualbox installed, not that Ubuntu is running INSIDE virtualbox. The presence of modules like "dell_wmi" suggests it is a physical machine, not a virtual one.

But the presence of brcmsmac without any detected PCI wireless adapters, and "wl" in blacklist also suggests that the system has been modified (following some guides/posts/tutorials elsewhere) before starting this thread (or at least before posting the first outputs).

Reinstalling would reset the configuration, but absence of a pci wireless card makes me doubtful about the success of a clean install, unless the wifi adapter is actually on a USB bus.

I suggest posting the wireless_script report to give us a wider picture of the setup : [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=13024222](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=13024222)
