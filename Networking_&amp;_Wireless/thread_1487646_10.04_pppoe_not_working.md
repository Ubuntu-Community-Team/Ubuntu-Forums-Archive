### Post by tacoee on 2010-05-21
ok, it works for me.. just do as below

1. edit /etc/network/interfaces as below

auto lo
iface lo inet loopback

2.sudo /etc/init.d/networking restart

3. sudo pppoeconf
enter your user and password, it is ok.
