### Post by peabody on 2007-10-01
What kind of statistics are you looking for?  The Network Monitor applet shows total amount of sent and received data.  Right click your panel, select "Add to Panel..." and pick the network monitor, then right click the applet, select preferences, and pick the interface to monitor.
