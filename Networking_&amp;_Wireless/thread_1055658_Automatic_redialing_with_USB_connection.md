### Post by PatchesTheCaveman on 2011-01-11
Right-click on your network icon.  Choose *Edit Connections...*.  Switch to the *Mobile Broadband* tab.  Click on your connection and click *Edit*.  Check the box that says *Connect automatically*.  Click *Apply* and *Close*.

It should now stay connected until you explicitly disconnect it.  Please note that it will now connect automatically when you plug your phone/USB dongle in and also if it's plugged in when you login.
