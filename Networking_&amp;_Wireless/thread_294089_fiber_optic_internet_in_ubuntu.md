### Post by tribaal on 2006-11-06
Well what will probably happend is that you'll have some sort of "modem" that will interface between your fiber optics line and your PC, most probably via WiFi or RJ45 (network cable). 

If it's network cable, your ubuntu will work just fine with it, completely out of the box. As far as wifi goes, the usual potential issues (drivers etc...) still apply.

Hope this helps :)
