### Post by pdc on 2010-12-18
now, most or all 3g sticks will work on linux; most immediately, the rest with a little work;

if the operator will let you plug the stick in at his shop; into your computer running linux;

wait two minutes; LEFT-CLICK on network manager; (left most of icons at top right);

any prompt to configure new mobile broadband connection?

if not, if you open a terminal type

> lsusb

and save or write down what you see: eg 

> 19d2:2000

or 

> 12d1:1446

and tell us here so we can help you

let us know how you get along

There are no known Tunisian networks listed in the helper in Ubuntu but if you ask the provider for the apn settings; they will know that
