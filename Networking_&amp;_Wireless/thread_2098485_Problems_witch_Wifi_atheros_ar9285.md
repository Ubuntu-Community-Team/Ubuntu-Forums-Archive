### Post by iris-n on 2013-01-02
I had the same problem as you. I think the issue is just that the ath9k driver is a piece of ****, and nobody is capable of getting it to work properly. My solution: use the Windows XP drivers via ndiswrapper. It's not pretty, but now my connection is fast and stable.

To get the drivers: [http://www.atheros.cz/](http://www.atheros.cz/) (unofficial site, use at your own risk)

Guide to use ndiswrapper: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper)

Good luck.
