### Post by JA2 on 2008-02-02
I'm in the same situation as you with accessing my work email. I read somewhere in the Evolution notes that only Exchange 2000 and up are supported due to a mail connector feature that only became available with Exchange 2000.

My workaround has been to use OWA (Outlook Web Access) via browser. Not the same experience, but it works.  Alternately, if your mail administrators can allow POP3 access to your mailbox, then that may be your workaround.
