### Post by Yhetti on 2008-01-30
The DNS resolution on your router appears to be broken, maybe?

wes@skeletor:~$ host takas.it
takas.it has address 62.80.127.185
takas.it mail is handled by 10 takas.it.

Or, lets try something else..  As root, edit
/etc/nsswitch.conf
and you'll see a line like:

hosts:          files mdns4_minimal [NOTFOUND=return] dns mdns4

Remove the "mdns4_minimal [NOTFOUND=return]" and "mdns4", save it, and then try again.  Sometimes when DNS spazzes that will fix it.
