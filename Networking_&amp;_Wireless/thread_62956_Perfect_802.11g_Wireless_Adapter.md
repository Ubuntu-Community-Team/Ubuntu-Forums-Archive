### Post by 12quality on 2005-09-06
I want to share the excellent experience I have had with my wireless adapter.

The Zonet ZEW1601 is great for Ubuntu (or any other linux)! Why?

[list=1]
[*]It is cheap.  I bought my for less than $10 on [newegg](http://newegg.com) (includes shipping and after a mail-in rebate)
[*]It is based on the Railink RT 2500 chipset.  This means that there is an [open-source project](http://rt2x00.serialmonkey.com/wiki/index.php/Main_Page) creating drivers for your card.  It works now and will continue to work in the future.  No NDIS wrapper neccessary.
[*]It works (great speed, no problems).
[/list] 

For in-depth setup instructions, see [the Ubuntu RT2500 wiki entry](https://wiki.ubuntu.com//Rt2500WirelessCardsHowTo/) for instructions
