ok folks,

i know the broadcom cards are sort of a stain on ubuntu to newer users, myself included.
i just got it to work after a long time of pulling my hair out.

hopefully this post makes at least one persons life easier.

this will not show you how to connect to your own wireless network, as that deals with routers, but these days its pretty simple, this is just to get the broadcom 4318 working.

i immediatley made this list as soon as wireless was working.
and the best part, NO TERMINAL!!!!! :guitar::guitar::guitar:


BROADCOM HARDY INSTALL FIX

1 UPGRADED TO HARDY. 

2. ENABLE RESTRICTED DRIVERS, THIS IS AN ICON AFTER HARDY IS FINISHED INSTALLING, ON THE CONTROL PANEL.

3. SYSTEM>ADMIN>HARDWARE DRIVERS

4. CLICK THE CHECK BOX ON B43 DRIVER.

5. FOLLOW THE PROMPTS. IT WILL SAY SOMTHING ABOUT FETCHING AND INSTALLING FIRMWARE

6. CLICK YES.

7. enjoy your wireless
