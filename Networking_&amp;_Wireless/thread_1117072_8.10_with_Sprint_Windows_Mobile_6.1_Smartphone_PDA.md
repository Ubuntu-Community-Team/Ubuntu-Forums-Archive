### Post by mailforbiz on 2009-04-15
Just FYI..tethering works just fine with Intrepid and Treo 755p. The connection seemed a bit slow compared to PDAnet and Vista so I did a speed test with both. Verdict: I get almost identical speeds with both Ubuntu and Vista - about 650 kbps which is awsome. Upload speed with Intrepid was slightly more at around 110 kbps vs 90 kbps for Vista. I used speedtest.net but didn't repeat the test a few times to see if the results varied.

PM me if you have problems and I can maybe help. But you will need the USB Modem shareware - as of now, I know no way around it.
