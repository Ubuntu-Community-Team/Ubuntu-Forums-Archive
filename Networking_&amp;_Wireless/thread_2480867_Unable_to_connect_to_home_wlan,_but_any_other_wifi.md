### Post by vilhohh on 2022-11-13
Hello, I managed to fix this problem. For my last effort I decided to open the machine and suprisingly, the problem was with the wifi card. The second antenna wire didn't connect to the wifi car properly. I duct taped it into it's place. I know, it's probably really stupid idea, but it works. I can now connect it to my home wifi.
