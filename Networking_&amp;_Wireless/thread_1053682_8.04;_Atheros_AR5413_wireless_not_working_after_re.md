### Post by djSeverin on 2009-01-28
Just posting this in case someone else runs across this little problem...

After the update this morning, my Atheros based wireless card decided it no longer felt like working.

"lspci" would show that the card was plugged in; but a "lshw -C network" would show that the card had not been claimed by a driver.

My best guess is that the recent update of "initscripts" overwrite the file that I had used to modprobe the "ath_pci" driver on startup.

Once I "modprobe ath_pci", the card was working quite nicely again.

I then added the line "ath_pci" to the /etc/modules file to ensure that the module would be loaded on boot.
