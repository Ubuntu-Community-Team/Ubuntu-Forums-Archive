Hi!

I have weird problems with my internet connection.

I have a Lenovo T400 with Ubuntu 8.10. My router is airport express (firmware version 6.3).

The WLAN card cannot detect the apple airport router, although it detects a load of networks from my neighbourhood. At least, it worked two or three times and when it worked, it did really good: very fast and even the printer worked out of the box! But only after many restarts of either the router and my laptop. But I couldn't figure out a rule.

I checked my router and it is definetely not a hidden network.
I suppose this is something in the configuration of the airport express router, since my WLAN card detects so many other networks.

Does anyone have any hints about how to configure it for Ubuntu?

Thanks a lot!
mjertin

---

### Post by mjertin on 2009-03-15
So, I think I've got the solution:

Changing airport express from "automatic channels" to a static channel made my network appear.

Cheers
