[http://computer.howstuffworks.com/router1.htm](http://computer.howstuffworks.com/router1.htm) 
Lots of adds, but it has pretty good coverage of how networking in general works, in laymans terms.

You will probably first want to work on just making sure the computers have connectivity-- that is, can each computer on your network communicate on the network.

Next, if you want your home network to be able to use the Internet, you will want to make sure that all your home computers are set up for that.

Finally, you will want to learn how to actually share files between your computers over your new network.  The beginner's guide details this part for you (Look at the Networking topic and the "How to Share Folders the Easy Way" subtopic): [http://www.ubuntuguide.org/](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/) 
If you have questions about specific points, feel free to ask!
