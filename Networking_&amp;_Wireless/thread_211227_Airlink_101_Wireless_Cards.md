### Post by tturrisi on 2006-07-21
Airport cards are made by Apple.

Older airport cards had a different chipset (orinoco) and work well but newer ones have a broadcom chipset and if you browse this forum you'll see how troublesome broadcom (bcm43xx) chipsets are.  Some airport cards have an atheros chipset and also work well.  Stay away from broadcom chipsets, they can be difficult at best.
