### Post by headvampyre@hotmail.co.uk on 2011-03-05
This is easy if your on Gnome :D Connect your phone to yuor computer via the USB cable supplied, make sure its set to "PC Suite". Now goto the network icon at the top of your screen, right click and then Edit connections. Now go to the Mobile Broadband tab, click on add, select your device, just keep pressing next and your done :)
