I recently spent a week with 2 different Bluetooth 4.0 mice

[LIST]
[*]Microsoft Arc Touch SE
[*]Lenovo N700
[/LIST]

On ubuntu 14.04 BOTH mice will Pair ok but they won't connect and in the Ubuntu Bluetooth manager they  both show as unkown type

Doing alot of google searching it appears this may all be due to the fact that Ubuntu (and Debian) do not yet have BlueZ v 5.x ... which from what I could find out seems to support "low energy" Bluetooth 4.0 mice.

I just wanted to post this message to possibly help others understand whats going on and that we will just have to wait for BlueZ v5 -- or if you are adventureous you can compile the source & install it yourself.
