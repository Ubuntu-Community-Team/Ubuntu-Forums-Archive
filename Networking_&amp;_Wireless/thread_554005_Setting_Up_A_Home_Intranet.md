### Post by stalker145 on 2007-09-18
> **eric5279 said:**
> How do I go about setting up an Intranet for my home network?

The very first thing that you should do to get an intranet/network set up is to have a router. From there, you can point all of your computers to the router (or have them automatically negotiate with DHCP which is a standard install). After that you need to figure out what you'd like your server for. You can have a file server, a mail server, a web server, a print server, and countless others... even a multi-purpose server.

Once you figure out what you'd like to do with your network and have your router up and all your computers can "see" each other, we can point you int he right direction.
