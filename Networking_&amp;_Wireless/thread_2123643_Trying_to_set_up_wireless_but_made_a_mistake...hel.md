### Post by ing.tani on 2013-03-08
Open terminal (Ctrl+Alt+Del)
Type **nm-connection-editor** and press enter
Go to wireless tab
Click on the wireless name you want to delete and press the Delete... button
Press Delete again on the window popping up
You are done.
