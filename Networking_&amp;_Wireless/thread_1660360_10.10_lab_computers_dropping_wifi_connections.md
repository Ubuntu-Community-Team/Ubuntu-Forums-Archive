### Post by PatchesTheCaveman on 2011-01-05
Hi john0011010!  The built-in Linux drivers for your Trednet wireless USB adapters are known to have some problems.  You will probably have better luck with the official Linux drivers from Ralink, the company that manufactures the chips inside those adapters.  Your adapters use the Ralink RT2870 chipset, for future reference.

For instructions on how to download and install these drivers, see this thread:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=960642](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=960642)

AussieAub:  you have different hardware so you likely have a different problem.  If you write a new post with your specific hardware information someone can try and help you too.
