### Post by issih on 2008-10-27
Essentially a wireless router does most of what you want, but I'm not aware of any that do it all.

Wireless Adsl modem routers do adsl, wireless and ethernet bit (although it is usually a switch not a hub, but that is irrelevant right now)

What are often called Wireless cable routers on the other hand are actually usually just an ethernet router with wireless built in, the cable modem is typically a separate box with ethernet out that you plug into the wan ethernet port of the route.

Most people have either wireless or adsl not both you see :)
