### Post by Amente on 2010-11-21
Your wireless is not working because the driver for the wireless adapter is not installed.Some drivers are not installed by default in ubuntu (they are called proprietary drivers) but you can install them manually.If you can connect your computer to the Internet using your LAN Ethernet adapter then you can do this  
 
Go to:- System-->Administration-->Hardware Drivers

 It will automatically search proprietry drivers for your device from the Internet and help you install it.
