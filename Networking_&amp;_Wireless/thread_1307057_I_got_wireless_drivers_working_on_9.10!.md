### Post by ninjatiger422 on 2009-10-30
Hey all,

Like many others I had a problem with my wifi drivers. I have a Dell Inspiron 1525 (the same one Dell sells Ubuntu on). It has Broadcom drivers. In the past few releases, my wifi card was automatically detected. This time it was not and I had to figure out a way to make it work.

The solution was simple. Just go System > Administration > Hardware Drivers. It will list one or two drivers you can use. Pick one and activate it.

When I activated, it required the drivers to be downloaded. This is annoying because it required me to physically plug in a network cable. If you don't have a wired connection, I'm not sure how you'll do it.

Hope this helps someone out there!
