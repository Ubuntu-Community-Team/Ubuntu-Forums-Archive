### Post by NHRA_Fan on 2007-08-27
I was running a dual boot between XP and Ubuntu 6.10 with no problems.  After upgrading to 7.04 none of my applications will access the internet.  I can ping my router and outside to the internet.  When I boot into recovery mode everything can access the internet and seems to work fine except I get the error "failed to initialize HAL."   I have looked around and can't resolve the issue.  I am hoping there is something to be learned in this.  Any suggestions on where to start?

---

### Post by NHRA_Fan on 2007-09-07
I found a resolution.  At one time I had Firestarter installed. When I looked under the Applications menu it was not listed.  I went into the package manager, uninstalled it, and now I have access to the internet.
