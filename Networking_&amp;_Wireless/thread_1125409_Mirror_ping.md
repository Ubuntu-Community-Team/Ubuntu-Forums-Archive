### Post by djrelay on 2009-04-14
Depending on the features of your router, you can check your router's security logs for any incoming PING requests as they might list the originating IP address. 

Better still, if you're simply interested in blocking, is to turn on "WAN Ping Blocking" or "Drop ICMP requests," again, a feature of most modern routers.
