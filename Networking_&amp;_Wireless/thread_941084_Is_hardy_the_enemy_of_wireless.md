### Post by inmanc on 2008-10-07
I just found the answer on another thread here and now have a chance to pay back the kindness.

Right click the panel at the top of the screen.
Highlight/click-on the 'Notification Area' choice on the dropdown menu.  Click the 'add' button and close the menu box.
Restart your computer.

There should then be a small screen-icon in the center of the panel at the top of the screen.  Click on that and the dropdown menu should show all the wireless signals available.  click the redio button for the one you normally use and you should be in business!!! 

Good Luck.
