### Post by VK7AY on 2012-10-16
2 weeks ago my HP Pavilion with Win7 and Ubuntu 12.04 refused to connect to Ubuntu, wired connection OK. connection to Win 7 OK.

Today highly frustrated decided to inspect the PC Bios, and found that the Network Adapter setting was DISABLED, changed to ENABLED rebooted and Hey presto.

Well worth a try on your machine.
