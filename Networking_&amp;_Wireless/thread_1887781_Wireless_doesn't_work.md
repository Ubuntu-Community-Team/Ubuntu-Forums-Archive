### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-11-29
First of all, a useful thread title would help you get better responses ... something like "wireless doesn't work".

Second, you should find out the make and model wireless chip in your PC. To do that, open a terminal and enter "lspci".  You'll see a line in that output referencing your wireless chip.

Once you have that info, then go over to the networking or hardware forums, and do a search based on that information.  You might be able to find threads where folks have already solved the problem.
