### Post by danrulz98 on 2008-06-05
I'm really new to Linux and I don't really know much about it. I was having issues with getting my Linksys wireless adapter to work on my laptop. After digging around through the forums I found a few solutions. After getting things even more messed up, I reinstalled Ubuntu and tried again. I checked out the New to Ubuntu thread in ABT and the thread about wireless not working. I almost kicked myself when I figured out what the problem was. 
In Windows: Open the Start menu and right click on My Computer
Select Properties
Click the Hardware tab and open Device Manager
Double click on the icon for your wireless card
Click the Power Management tab
Uncheck any check boxes and hit OK
Restart into Ubuntu. 
After installing the proprietary drivers, the card works fine.
