### Post by PatrickVogeli on 2009-06-12
My parents use Voipbuster in Windows. You have to buy credit, but some countries are free to call. After 4 months of free calling, those free countries get the minimum rate and you start paying. Once your credit has expired, you pay again and the 4 months of free calls. Only for landlines, though.

In ubuntu I once setup ekiga to work with a voipbuster account.
