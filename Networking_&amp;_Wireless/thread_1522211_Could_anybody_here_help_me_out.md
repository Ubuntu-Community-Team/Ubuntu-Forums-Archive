### Post by tgalati4 on 2010-07-02
Cat-5 has to be the easiest cable to pull through walls and hide.  It's a mechanical/electrical marvel that has served us well.  I presume the wireless USB dongle has a linux driver.  They are OK, but they tend to break.  The USB connector is not that robust.  Plus, if you use them in a desktop, they tend to be placed poorly.  You're better using a USB extension so you can get a clear view of the dongle, instead of hidden behind the machine, or in front where it can get smashed.
