### Post by SirGrant on 2010-12-11
Are you talking about tor and vidalia.  Two things.  This guide shows you how to do what you want ([http://www.how-to-hide-ip.info/2009/08/08/how-to-use-an-ip-from-a-specific-country-while-running-tor/](http://www.how-to-hide-ip.info/2009/08/08/how-to-use-an-ip-from-a-specific-country-while-running-tor/)).  However, doing so decreases the anonymity you get by using the tor network.  

You could also specify that you want to use the english versions of certain websites.  So you could visit ([http://www.google.com/webhp?hl=en](http://www.google.com/webhp?hl=en)) to specify the english version instead of just google.com which auto detects your location.
