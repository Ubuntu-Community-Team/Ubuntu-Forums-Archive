### Post by williammanda on 2011-07-25
Update:
I found out at the last minute that the TEW 687GA adapter was 2.4Ghz and not 5Ghz. Based on that I decided not to go the Trendnet route, since there is too much traffic on the 2.4Ghz band. The next solution is Ruckus Wireless.

[http://www.ruckuswireless.com/](http://www.ruckuswireless.com/)

They have a unique approach to wireless which is suppose to be the best. I need this since I use Mythtv. I have ordered two parts:

MediaFlex 7811 multimedia access point
MediaFlex 7111 multimedia Wi-Fi adapter

The two devices will be connected between the existing NIC on the desktop computer and the router. The devices will not be seen by ubuntu, it will think the ethernet cable is still there.

This where I ordered the parts from:

[http://www.geminicomputersinc.com/901-7811-us01.html](http://www.geminicomputersinc.com/901-7811-us01.html)

They are the cheapest that I found. I also got the number of a Ruckus regional engineer that will assist me if the need arises. Once I test the initial parts and they work as expected then I will add 3 more computers.
