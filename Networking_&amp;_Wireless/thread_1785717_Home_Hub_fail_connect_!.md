### Post by elliotbeken on 2011-06-18
Hi all, 

i have had problems connecting to my grandads wireless on his BT home hub (HH2 i believe). i have had a quick look around the net and not really found an answer apart from doing a factory reset !

What seemed to be the problem was the home hub with firmware version 4.7.5.1.83.3.18 (type B) is the DHCP server was failing to give a IP for a reason i dont know. 

To just do a easy fix i just assigned a static IP address on my ubuntu 11.04 laptop. this fixed the problem and i have full connectivity to the net now !

i know this might not be a long term fix for all people but fixed the problem for me while i am staying at my grandads for a few days..
