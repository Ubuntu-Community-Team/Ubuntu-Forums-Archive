### Post by Steve603z on 2010-07-10
when you click on the icon are you able to see the wireless access points around you?  you might also need to active the wireless card in your laptop, for example, my ThinkPad you can turn the wireless card on and off by pressing Fn + F5.

Also, go to System > Administration > Hardware Drivers to check if your wireless card's drivers are activated

you can try opening a command terminal and typing the command "iwconfig" to see if your wireless card is even recognized by the system
