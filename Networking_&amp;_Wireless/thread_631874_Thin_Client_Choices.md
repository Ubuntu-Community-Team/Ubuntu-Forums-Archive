### Post by jonallport on 2007-12-05
If you're looking for clients to PXE boot then almost any x86-type client will do.  If you want a true thin client for X11 then I'd have to say that Wyse, Eizo, HP etc cater mostly for the MS/Citrix market with RDP and ICA.

I know Axel thin clients have VNC and it's also available for Wyse terminals based on CE, XPe or Linux.  I'm not aware of any X11-native terminals, I'm just trying to think what we used to have at Uni in the Solaris custer - nope, gone!

I've googled X-terminal and come up blank apart from the ([http://207.253.4.241/xterminals/](http://207.253.4.241/xterminals/)) - look useful.
