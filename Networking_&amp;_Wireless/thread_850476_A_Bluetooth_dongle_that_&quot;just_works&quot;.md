### Post by martinquested on 2008-07-05
I suspect Bluetooth dongles are less problematic than other hardware, but just for the record, the BlueNext Micro Bluetooth USB Adaptor works out-of-the box with Kubuntu 8.04 Hardy, using the kdebluetooth package.  I daresay it works with other packages on other desktop environments too.

The dongle itself is a tiny thing, less than 2cm long, so it barely protrudes out of the USB socket at all.  It is available on Amazon for not much money.

So, if you're researching what hardware to buy, this one comes highly recommended!
