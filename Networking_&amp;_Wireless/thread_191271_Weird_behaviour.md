### Post by reyfer on 2006-06-07
Thought I may share this with you. I installed Kubuntu Dapper from the Beta 2 cd, and after the official release, did a dist-upgrade. Everything went fine, except that there was no network! I searched Kubuntu Forum, and also this forum, but none of the solutions applied apparently. Thing is, on System Settings - Network Settings, I couldn't get DHCP working, so I tried putting the IP manually, and it worked. So on Adept, I looked and, voilá! the dhcp package was NOT INSTALLED during the upgrade. After I installed it, everything is running smoothly. Maybe someone else is having the same problem, so check if the dhcp package is installed before doing anything else. Thanks Ubuntu and Kubuntu, I'm Micro$hit free at last
