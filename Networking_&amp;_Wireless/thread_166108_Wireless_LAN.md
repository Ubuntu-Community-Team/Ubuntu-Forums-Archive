### Post by steve0921 on 2006-04-25
How you get wireless to work depends heavily on the chipset in the wireless card. I don't know what the chipset is for the TrueMobile card, but since it didn't work out of the box, I'm going to assume that its not particularly linux-friendly.

A general workaround for such cards is to use ndiswrapper. There is a howto for setting it up in ubuntu [here]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=31926").

Hope that helps.
