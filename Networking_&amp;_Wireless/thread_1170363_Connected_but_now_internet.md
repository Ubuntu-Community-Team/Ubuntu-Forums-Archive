### Post by peakshysteria on 2009-05-28
Install [**Wubi**]("http://wubi-installer.org/"). To test Ubuntu before setting up a real dual-boot. It's a bit slower than a real install but it will give you a representative impression of Ubuntu. When you have tried it and you feel comfortable to make an ordinary dual-boot or a clean install of UbuntuJaunty Jackalope(9.04) (single boot) as some of us have done you can just go to add/remove programs in windows to remove Wubi as you do with ordinary programs in windows.

And sometimes one just have to figure things out to try them. It's not always things work out of the box. Sometimes they do and sometimes they don't. No matter what OS you run. That's some of the fun of trying out something new if you ask me. Learn by doing. And above all; [Linux is Not Windows]("http://linux.oneandoneis2.org/LNW.htm").
