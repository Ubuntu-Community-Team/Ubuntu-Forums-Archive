### Post by lisati on 2009-03-31
You might want to investigate if there's a conflict between the channel your home network uses and that of a neighbouring wireless netwrok. I was getting intermittent disconnections which were solved when changing the channel used by one of my routers.

It may be that you're on the same wifi channel as other nearby networks. You could try changing to a different channel (you can set this by going into the router set-up). Another problem could be signal strength/quality is too low or interference from other electronic devices (such as cordless phones) working at similar 2.4GHz frequencies.
