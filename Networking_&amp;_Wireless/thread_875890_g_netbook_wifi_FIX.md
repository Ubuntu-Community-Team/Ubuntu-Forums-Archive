### Post by intrepid7 on 2008-07-31
Just purchased a sylvania g netbook.  I was having alot of connectivity issues.  No help online or through digital gagdets.  Well, I have stumbled across the fix.  The problems I was having: 1) WiFi showed it was connected(by icon on panel) but would load 1-2 webpages then stall and become extremely slow.  2) Also, by right clicking on the icon all ip's were still listed(nothing dropped). 3)When wifi was misbehaving the signal strength icon would fluctuate constantly.

Here is the fix:  You MUST disable the wifi card before reboot, shutdown and hibernation.  To do this press Fn + F2 you will see the indicator light turn off.  Then reboot the machine, now the wifi works great and the signal monitor is at a constant level.  

This has been working for 2 days, you do not have to disable for screenlocking.

Good luck hope it works!:)
