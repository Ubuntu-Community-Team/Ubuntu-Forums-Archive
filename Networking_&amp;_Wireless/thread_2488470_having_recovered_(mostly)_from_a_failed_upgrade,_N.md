### Post by ian-weisser on 2023-07-05
A fresh install would help in most circumstances...but not all (like hardware failure).

Use the "Try Ubuntu" environment to test ALL your hardware and connectivity. Stream a movie. Take a webcam picture and print it. Test everything you can think of.
Also use the 'Try Ubuntu" environment to recover your data onto some other media.

Once you are satisfied that your hardware is working properly, then do the install.
