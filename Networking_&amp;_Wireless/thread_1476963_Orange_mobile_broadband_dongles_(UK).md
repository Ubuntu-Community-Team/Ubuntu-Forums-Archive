### Post by pasti on 2010-05-08
Hello ELD

I've used mobile dongles in the past, I think any with the huewei chipset work with ubuntu, but  didn't like the contracts and costs with these things, but there is a cheaper option in the uk get yourself a 3 skype phone, I use the ZTE F102 one, they're about £20 from a high street chemist, put £10 top up on there at the same time, and 3 will give you 150mb of free internet access which lasts for three months, as well as free texts etc, but the really handy thing is you can connect them via a standard usb cable (supplied), or bluetooth to a laptop or netbook, and connect to the internet via the network manager, just choose the mobile broadband option, and follow the steps there, it's worked for me fine for more than a year, ubuntu 9.04, 9,10 & now in 10.04, plus you get a really cheap phone, which isn't really going to break the bank if it gets pinched;)
