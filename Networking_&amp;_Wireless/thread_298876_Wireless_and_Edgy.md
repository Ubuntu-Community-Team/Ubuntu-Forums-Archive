### Post by CCBalla10 on 2006-11-13
[COLOR="Red"]Hey guys, i recently upgraded to edgy and couldn't for the life of me figure out how to get my wireless card on my HP dv1000 laptop to work.  I tried configuring my wireless and nothing worked.  It wouldn't even scan for wireless networks.  So i got brave one day and did a fresh install of edgy.  Downloaded the drivers and installed Ndiswrapper 1.8 and installed the drivers.  My wireless light came on and decided to try and connect without going to system > admin > networking and configuring my wireless.  Instead i just went to Add/Remove programs and installed Wifi-radar, and IT WORKED!!!!! wifi-radar also scans for wireless networks, and i can create profiles for each network!  Also after this when i go to system > admin > Networking...it says network interface is not configured! go figure, but that is how i got my wireless problem fixed, and i'm loving EDGY!!!! :D 

P.S. I'm posting this with my wireless internet :D[/COLOR]
