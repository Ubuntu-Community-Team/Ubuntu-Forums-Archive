### Post by chili555 on 2017-07-29
You need updated firmware:> [    4.951534] iwlwifi 0000:02:00.0: no suitable firmware found!
[    4.951563] iwlwifi 0000:02:00.0: minimum version required: iwlwifi-8000C-22
[    4.951592] iwlwifi 0000:02:00.0: maximum version supported: iwlwifi-8000C-30With a temporary working internet connection, open a terminal and do:```
wget http://mirrors.kernel.org/ubuntu/pool/main/l/linux-firmware/linux-firmware_1.167_all.deb
sudo dpkg -i linux*.deb
```Reboot.
