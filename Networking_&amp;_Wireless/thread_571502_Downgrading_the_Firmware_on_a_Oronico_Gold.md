Hi!  This is my second or third post, and I seem to be seeing a lot of questions regarding the Dell 1150 Truemobile WiFi card, or other questions about Oronico Gold based cards (with the Prism 2 Chipset)

I have the same card (the Dell) and I was happily using kismet to scour the airwaves for open hotspots . . . well I thought "Heck, why don't I flash the firmware and see if anything improves."  Well I did, and it didn't.  In fact my card became "broken" I could no longer enter the much sought after MONITOR MODE, which means that I can no longer "sniff" for open WLANS. (BTW the broken monitoring mode is for ALL cards above the version 6 of the firmware)

Well, considering that I used a "hacked" firmware without much research, I guess I got what I deserved. 

ANYWAY . . . I was not going to give up that easily.  So I "hacked" my own version of the firmware installer to downgrade the card back into 6.16. This installer will work with the Dell Truemobile 1150 Card but  . . . 

!!! DO NOT USE IT ON ANYTHING ELSE IT HAS NOT BEEN TESTED !!! 

Post or PM me if you need some help downgrading your Oronico Gold Based Cards to 6.16. . . I will not be responsible for you bricking your cards, but I can help you if you don't mind a little risk.  This should work on all affected (effected[?]) Oronico Gold Cards not just the Truemobile 1150.

I have attached the firmware installer for the Truemobile 1150 (sorry, .exe file) and wish you the best of luck.

If you do not trust me, or my funny looking firmware, please find a trusted source for the 6.16 firmware (file name: wsu10616.exe) and run a diff to compare the two.

If I have spent too much time on this and there was a simpler way, I would really like to hear about it.  I'd love to compare notes.
