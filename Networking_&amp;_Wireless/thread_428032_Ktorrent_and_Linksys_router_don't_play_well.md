### Post by neouser99 on 2007-04-30
you might want to try playing with the number of concurrent uploads/downloads that it is allowed, instead of just overall rate limiting. also, controlling/turning off upnp could help relieve some of the stress on the router.

i do recall vaguely that some of the linksys had trouble keeping track of a huge amount of tcp connections, on top of all the upnp that could possibly be going on.
