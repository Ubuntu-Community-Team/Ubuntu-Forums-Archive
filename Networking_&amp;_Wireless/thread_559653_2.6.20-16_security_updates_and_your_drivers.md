### Post by noob12 on 2007-09-25
PITA notice for a lot of wireless and gigabit users.

With the 2.6.20-16 security updates today, any custom in-place driver replacements you had done will be lost.  

For example, in particular, you will revert back to old ndiswrapper 1.38 if you had installed (or used one of the neat HOWTO installer scripts to install) a more recent version in-place.

I find I have to go back and replace a couple of mine.  What we seem to need at minimum is a cleaner way to do custom overrides than replacing modules in place.  (That and more up-to-date versions shipped in Ubuntu!)

Happy rebuilding.
