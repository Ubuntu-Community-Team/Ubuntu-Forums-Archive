### Post by lisati on 2007-09-20
I don't know if this will help everyone, but System->Administration->Software sources has an option to choose where to download updates from. On Ubuntu 7.04 (FF), if you click on the "Download from" button and then "Other" you will be given a "select best server" option. I've found that the geographically closest isn't always the fastest....My system defaulted to "New Zealand" (where I live), and the "select best server" usually chooses somewhere in Australia.
