### Post by Hashiru on 2010-11-05
Hi, first of all the problem could be on two sides. Your laptop or your modem.

Is the dhcp enabled on your modem or do you use static addresses?
Do you use encryption and what kind of encryption?

And how did you set your network settings?

Maybe you can provide a screenshot of the settings you use.
Right click wireless -> edit settings ->wireless tab -> select the network you want to connect with -> edit -> wireless security -> press print screen
Upload the screenshot.

That will help a lot to solve it.

Oh, make sure you check the "Connect automatically" box at the top of that screen.
