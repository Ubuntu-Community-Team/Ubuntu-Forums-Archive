### Post by Jake Sweeney on 2013-02-04
If it is terms of the hardware, there might be keyboard shortcuts that turn on the wireless card. Hold the "Fn" key and try to look for the corresponding icon on your "F" keys.

In terms of Ubuntu: The network applet, located on the top panel of the desktop, can sometimes disable the use of networking. To enable networking again select "Enable Networking" on the network applet.

I hope it helps :KS
