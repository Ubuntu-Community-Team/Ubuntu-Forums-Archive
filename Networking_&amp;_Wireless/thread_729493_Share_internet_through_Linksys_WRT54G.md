### Post by kevdog on 2008-03-20
Install dd-wrt on the second wrt54g, and then turn it into a wirless bridge, or bridge repeater.  This works very well to repeat the signal from the first router to expand the signal.  I have on main router, a second repeater-wireless-bridge, and a third repeater-wireless-bridge that repeats the signal from the second router. 

With the chaining of multiple repeaters, I read somewhere that someone was able to keep extending the wireless signal over a mile!
