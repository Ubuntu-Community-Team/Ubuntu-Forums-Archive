### Post by techtalk123 on 2007-07-02
Hi, I just want to let the forum know that TechnoCo has announced its embedded Asterisk™ PBX appliance, "Vdex", running a technoCo modified, embedded version of popular Asterisk™ PBX open software version 1.4. 
 
Vdex is a hardware telephony platform (appliance) integrating multiple microprocessors and multiple digital signalling processors with numerous industry-standard, hardware-based voice codecs and LAN and telecom line interfaces. Vdex is an appliance: no disk drive, no monitor/keyboard and no noisy power supply.
