### Post by aimwin on 2010-09-21
Dell N4010 and other brands with UBUNTU 10.04 

Wireless need propriety drivers and 
will not function with out them being installed

And LAN port (wired RJ45 port) is not working too.

So what is the easy way to update these drivers.

Borrow someone else USB wireless adapter,
that will work instantly with Ubuntu version you just installed.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCardsNetgear#USB](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCardsNetgear#USB)

Then you can update on line via USB wireless adapters.
I use WG111v2 to connect to dell 4010 to update other wireless drivers.
