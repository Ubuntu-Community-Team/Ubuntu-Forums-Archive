Hi,

     Just wanted to share this. I couldn't get any help here so I looked elsewhere.
My computer wasn't able to get on line because my LAN wouldn't work. I'm dual booting
xp-pro and ubuntu feisty. The trick to getting my LAN to work is:

Go into device manager in xp-pro click on ( Network Adapter )  then right click on
the Realtek network adapter,click on properties, Click on Advanced tab,
select wake on LAN , click on enable click OK And that is it.

After that ubuntu will go online

My machine is:  Ga-G33M-DS2R  Bios flashed to F5a, E6320 processor, 80gig Seagate Sata2
one Gig of Corsair DDR2 800, Home made water cooler with Swiftech Apogee water block
and swifttech pump. Radiator from a Pontiac ( Heater Core ) and the water resovoir is
made from PVC pipe bought at the hardware store along with 1/2 " ID clear PVC tubing.
  
Hope this helps someone
