### Post by Ambimom on 2006-12-31
[www.Portforwarding.com](www.Portforwarding.com) provides complete instructions on how to forward ports.  

The same ports need to be forwarded in either linux or windows bittorrent clients so if you are "connectable" in windows, you should be in linux, provided that you are using the same ports. 

To download torrents, you can use either Opera browser (which is downloadable from Synaptic) Azureus, or Bit Comet.  Typically, each of these clients select a random port.  All you'll need to do is open that port in your router by following the instructions for your router on portforwarding.com.  

Once you've forwarded your ports, you'll probably have to logoff, unplug your router,  unplug your modem.  Re-plug your router and your modem, reboot.  You should be connectable.
