### Post by scbingham on 2013-07-04
Go into edit connections > wireless, Click on your neighbour's ssid, edit & then uncheck connect automatically.

Click Delete
Click Delete again when it asks you..."are you sure?":(

after you delete
but if you dont click on him..he should not attempt to
