### Post by Charly85551 on 2010-04-10
Hi Sugoi48,
I think you are missing some components. Your Macbook Pro is having a WiFi card installed but can not operate it as a WiFi access point for your iPod. You need your modem-router to be either a wireless router or you need a separate WiFi-access point attached to it. As soon as you have a WiFi-network (make sure you have WPA2 as your encryption security) you can use it also for you MAC to get connected via WiFi. Apple's Airport Express will do the job as well as many other boxes like the ones from D-Link or Linksys. Ask for access point with WPA2 security.
