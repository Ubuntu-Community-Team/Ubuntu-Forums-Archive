### Post by duggo03 on 2007-10-31
Hi,

I am running Ubuntu 7.04 with a wireless dlink 510 PCI card and found that the card was recognized straight away but like others it would not connect to my network. There was a trick to it. 

I do not have any encryption or a password on my network, as I live out of town, but found that I needed to put in a password to connect to the network in the cards properties. I put in the password that I use to configure my wireless router and now the connection works perfectly.

Hope this helps.
