You have RTL8111/8168/8411 PCI Express Gigabit Ethernet Controller, Kernel driver in use: r8169 .  you may need to try the kernel driver r8168.

    Hard blocked: no

##### lsmod #############################

acer_wmi               20480  0
sparse_keymap          16384  1 acer_wmi
ath10k_pci             45056  0
ath10k_core           335872  1 ath10k_pci

cfg80211              581632  3 mac80211,ath,ath10k_core
wmi                    16384  1 acer_wmi
video                  40960  2 acer_wmi,i915


```the kernel module "acer_wmi" is loaded... you might need to turn it off / blacklist it. Something like: sudo tee /etc/modprobe.d/blacklist-acer-wmi.conf <<< "blacklist acer_wmi"

and then you'll have to reboot. if that didn't fix it, Then undo (remove the file /etc/modprobe.d/blacklist-acer-wmi.conf) and.... reboot your computer again.  ##

Also, I strongly suggest you change your routers security to pure WPA2 with AES (CCMP). No WEP, no WPA/WPA2 mixed mode, and no TKIP. and select a fixed channel from 1,6,11. Reboot the router after saving the change.
