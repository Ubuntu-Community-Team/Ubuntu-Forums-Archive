Did you click Enable this connection and then click the shield icon to the left of where it says click to prevent changes? Did you reboot? Settings should lock in. 

The network monitor is useful for confirming that you have a connection. The left click on the icon also shows useful information like signal strength. The Configure button is useless. When I click it I get a "The interface does not exist" message even though I am connected through that interface.

I do not know of any way to scan for networks. I took the setting from my standard Unbuntu and used them to configure the connection in Ubuntu Studio.
