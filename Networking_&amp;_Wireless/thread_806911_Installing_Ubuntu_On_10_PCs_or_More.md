### Post by dasunst3r on 2008-05-25
If this is 10 identical computers, you would want to clone the disks (someone fill us in on how to do this, please).  But if you have to do individual installations, your home directory actually contains all your preferences, and hence all the customizations you made.

The user preferences are hidden and start with a dot.  In Nautilus, you can hit Ctrl+H to show them.  Then, you can copy them (and the files you used to perform your customizations) to a USB drive.
