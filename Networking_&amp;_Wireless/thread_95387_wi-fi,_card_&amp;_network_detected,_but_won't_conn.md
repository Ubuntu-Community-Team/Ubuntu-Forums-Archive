### Post by myke on 2005-11-26
Damn.   I worked on it all morning.  Then finally decided to ask for help here.  Then 30 seconds after I posted I figured out the problem.  I have to deactivate my ethernet connection or else it interferes with the wifi connection.  Plain and simple.  It picked up the signal right after I did that.  Now, I just need to figure out how to get it to automatically stay that way unless I change it.  During restart, it goes back and I have to deactivate all over again.   Even so, it's much much better than not working at all.

For those not getting their network cards recognized ... I will say this ... this particular linksys one (wps54gs) did get picked up quickly.
