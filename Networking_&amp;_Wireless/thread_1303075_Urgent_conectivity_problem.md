### Post by sgosnell on 2009-10-27
The OS of the computers doesn't matter at all.  Two computers on one network is a very light load - today I had 12 connected to mine, and the performance was still reasonable.  You may have a faulty router, they do go bad like any electronic device can.  If your laptop works fine on other networks, and the Windows computer is also having wireless problems, I would suspect the wireless radio in the router.
