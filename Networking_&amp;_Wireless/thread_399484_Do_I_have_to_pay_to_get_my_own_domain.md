### Post by H3g3m0n on 2007-04-02
If you want a top level domain name, then yes you need to pay.

However if you don't mind a sub level domain then there are free dns hosts around.

The difference between them is [www.mydomain.com](www.mydomain.com) and mydomain.majordomain.com

[http://freedns.afraid.org/](http://freedns.afraid.org/) is a great sublevel domain host, including Linux scripts for automatically updating your domain if you have a changing ip address, and a huge number of domain names to choose from (Although %80 are pron related or just rubbish)

So you can get something like [http://yourname.firmbuttstone.com/](http://yourname.firmbuttstone.com/) or [http://yourname.chickenkiller.com/](http://yourname.chickenkiller.com/) (use those are both available on afraid's freedns) but not [http://www.yorname.com/](http://www.yorname.com/) without paying.
