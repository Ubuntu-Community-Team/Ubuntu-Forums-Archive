### Post by Tyche on 2008-10-16
To whom it may concern  :lolflag:

If you are running a router and have a problem connecting to the network with a new machine (as I did) check to see if you enabled mac filtering.

Yep.  You guessed it.  3 1/2 days of trying to connect to the internet with a brand new Dell Mini 9, and I finally thought to check if I'd enabled mac filtering.  And once I realized I had, it took all of 5 minutes to make the changes and connect.

I'm sure there are other simple solutions out there that people are forgetting.  This one was MY stupid moment.  I can't even claim Alzheimer's as an excuse.  Just my own stupidity of not checking all the possibilities earlier.
