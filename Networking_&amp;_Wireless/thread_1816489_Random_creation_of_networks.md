### Post by lmarmisa on 2011-08-18
This problem could be due to the password keyring.

Open **System -> Preferences -> Passwords and Encryption Keys -> Passwords**. You will see an item named **Passwords: login**. Select it and right click on **Change Password**. Define a new blank password.

Check if your problem is solved after this change.
