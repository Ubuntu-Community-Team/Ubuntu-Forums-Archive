### Post by eggdeng on 2007-01-22
Sounds familiar. I had to give up on a usb wireless adapter after about a month of doing my head in with ndiswrapper and the rest & concluding that either wireless or usb or a combination of both is just buggy in Edgy. 
So instead of going for another adapter, I got hold of a wireless access point, which can be configured as bridge, repeater or client. You just connect it to the ethernet card with a cable, configure it as client and you're picking up your wireless connection via your ethernet card. It's a neat solution and not much more expensive than a usb key, the only problem being that you need somewhere to plug it in.
For the record, mine is an ecom EW54AP.
