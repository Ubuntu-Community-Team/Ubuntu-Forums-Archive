### Post by jhetrick62 on 2008-02-19
You can't choose a public ip address unless you have more than one static ip address assigned to you and available from your isp.  In other words, if your provider assigned you 2 STATIC ip addresses, then one could be your router and serve your network, and one could be assigned to your server directly.  You would need a simple switch or hub to split your incoming connection and then configure them both for the proper IP addresses.
