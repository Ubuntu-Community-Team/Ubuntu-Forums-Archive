### Post by Laurel Raven on 2007-06-27
My first recommendation is that you reset your DSL modem...those will often only connect to the first device that they are attached to until they are reset or power cycled.  I recommend turning off the laptop and powering off the DSL modem, then leaving both off for 15 seconds...then power on the modem, let it cycle through all of its startups, then boot the laptop.

This MIGHT work.  I've seen it work quite a few times.  If you've already tried that, then I don't know what else to recommend.
