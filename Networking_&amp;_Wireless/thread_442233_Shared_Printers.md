The best place to look for Linux compatible printers is in the open printing database, [http://www.linux-foundation.org/en/OpenPrinting](http://www.linux-foundation.org/en/OpenPrinting)

I prefer using printers with an ethernet connection as they don't depend on any particular OS and are configurable through a webpage interface. These tend to be more expensive though.
