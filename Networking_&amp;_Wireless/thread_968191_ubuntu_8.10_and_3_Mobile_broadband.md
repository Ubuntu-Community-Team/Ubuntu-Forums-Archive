### Post by Stuart McCulloch on 2008-11-02
I have just installed Ubuntu 8.10 and was a  bit worried that it would not detect my 3 Mobile Broadband (UK) PCMCIA HSDPA card. Plugged the card in - detected straight away.  Although would not connect, to solve that just changed the APN from the default 3internet to internet.three.  Connected straight away.  So much easier than with windows - Thank you for a great new version.:)
