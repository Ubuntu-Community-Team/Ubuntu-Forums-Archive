### Post by smaring on 2009-05-31
Well, I finally got it to work!  :D

I found an updated ICS application, a version 2 apparently, at [http://keithelder.net/blog/archive/2008/01/18/ATampT-Blackjack-II-Windows-Mobile-Phone-Review.aspx](http://keithelder.net/blog/archive/2008/01/18/ATampT-Blackjack-II-Windows-Mobile-Phone-Review.aspx)

However, I'm not really certain if that is what fixed my problem.  I noted that there were two different Network connections "AT&T IMS", which was the default, and "Media Net".  I had thought that "Media Net" was just for multimedia text messaging, but apparently I was wrong.  I tried "Media Net" and it WORKED!  I then found a reference to that notion: [http://forums.wireless.att.com/cng/board/message?board.id=samsung&message.id=57457](http://forums.wireless.att.com/cng/board/message?board.id=samsung&message.id=57457)

I should also point out that as soon as my ICS connected I got my rndis0 device:

