### Post by tehwa on 2005-09-02
I've had some luck with Netgear. I have a WGT624 Router with a WG311T NIC.

The first time I set up Ubuntu, setup didn't recognise the WG311T and I had a hell of a time getting ndiswrapper to run it. But after I scrapped my initial install and reinstalled the system, it detected it, and somehow it worked straight up.

I paid approx US $180.00, and didn't shop around at all, so you could get this combo for much less.
