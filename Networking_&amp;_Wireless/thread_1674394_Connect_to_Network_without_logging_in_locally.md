### Post by Superlinkx on 2011-01-23
Hi everyone. I want to be able to login to my desktop over ssh without having to physically login. The only problem is that the desktop will only connect to the network after logging in at the machine. It is connected to both wireless and wired, and I usually ssh over the wired ip.

Basically, I need it to connect to the network during startup, so I can reboot it remotely, and then still have access after it finishes rebooting.

Thanks for your help.

EDIT: I solved the issue. I just had to enable wired connection for all users through the network settings.
