*Thread moved to **Networking & Wireless**.*

First of all, since Ubuntu 11.10 is about to reach its [End Of Life]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases") in April this year, you should consider upgrading to a newer version. I would recommend Ubuntu 12.04 (64 bit if supported).

With newer kernel and drivers, you may get your wireless (& other stuff) working out-of-box. If not, troubleshooting would be easier and more reliable.

However, if you wish to try that later and troubleshoot the current one for now, please follow the "Wireless Script" link in my signature and post back its result here.

As for the multimedia button issue, I think you should ask the question in a separate thread. It is not a good idea to mix multiple problems in a single thread :)
