### Post by Bucky Ball on 2008-11-24
In System->Administration->Network, unlock, choose the wireless connection then 'Properties'.
Make sure 'Configuration' is set to 'Automatic Configuration (DHCP)',
'enable roaming' is off (for now),
you have the correct ESSID and security details,

Reboot and try again. You can check your details with the setup on your Windoze install.
