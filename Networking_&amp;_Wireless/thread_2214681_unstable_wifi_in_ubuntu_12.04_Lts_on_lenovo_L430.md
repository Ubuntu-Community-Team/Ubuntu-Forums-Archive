### Post by ian-weisser on 2014-04-03
1. Since your wi-fi works, I would look at the characteristics of those non-working networks. Distance, interference, movement, antenna orientation, etct.

2. Ethernet hardware can fail. Check both sides of the connection with a known-good partner. Cables go bad, too. And some older hardware does not auto-sense the type (crossover/normal) connection - you may merely need a crossover cable or adapter.
