### Post by kyphi on 2007-08-02
Why don't you install Firestarter from the repositories?  Go to Add/Remove Programs and look under Internet.

Apart from a firewall, there are a few other security devices that you may find useful.  Firefox has several plugins available for download, one of which is NoScript.

The biggest security threat comes from emails.  I use an email programme that lets me read and delete emails while still on the server before downloading.

On your internal box, install fwbuilder and configure your fw.
