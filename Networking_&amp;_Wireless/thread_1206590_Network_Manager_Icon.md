### Post by t0mppa on 2009-07-07
If you just closed the Network Manager icon, then try pressing Alt + F2, typing in "nm-applet" without the quotes and clicking run.

If you removed the whole Notification Area, right-click on the menu panel and choose Add to Panel... and then choose Notification Area and click Add.
