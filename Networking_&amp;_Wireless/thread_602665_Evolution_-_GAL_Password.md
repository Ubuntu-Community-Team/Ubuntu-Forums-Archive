### Post by Jason Green on 2007-11-04
Hi, I would like to disable evolution from asking me for a Global Address List password for my exchange account is this possible. Every time I click on new message, I get a prompt which reads, "Enter password for Global Address List (user [email]jason@mcgill.ca[/email])" I've tried all the settings given by my school for the GAL field, and they don't work, or they slow things down considerably. I don't really need the GAL and can live with out. Any help is appreciated. Jason.

### Post by gjarboni on 2007-11-08
**This post could be related to an Ubuntu bug filed at**: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/evolution-exchange/+bug/131616](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/evolution-exchange/+bug/131616) [br].[/br] ---------------------------- [br].[/br] [br].[/br]
				I disabled this prompt by going to Edit, Preferences in Evolution then clicking the Autocomplete icon. Under my Exchange email address, I unchecked "Global Address List". Give it a shot and see if it works for you. I'm attaching a screen shot of the window I'm talking about (with my email address blacked out).
