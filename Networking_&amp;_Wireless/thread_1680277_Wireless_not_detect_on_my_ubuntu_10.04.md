Thinks to check:
First: Do you have a check mark in your network setting for “Enable Network”, Enable Wireless”, “Enable Notification” . (right click on the network icon in the taskbar at the top of your desktop).
Second: right click  on the network icon and select Edit Connections. Select the Wireless tab. Select your connection and click on the edit button. This will open another Dialog box and make sure there is a check mark in the “Connect automatically” at the top of the Dialog box.
