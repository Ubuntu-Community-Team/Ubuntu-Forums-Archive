### Post by Paerez on 2008-04-15
In some situations where I have to transfer a very large file, I will forward bittorrents ports to my router and make my own torrent file. Then I send that torrent to my friend and he gets it from me that way.

It's a bit of a hassle on my end, but easy for him (if he knows BT). Also it resumes, which is nice for big files.

Transmission has a "Create a torrent" option.
