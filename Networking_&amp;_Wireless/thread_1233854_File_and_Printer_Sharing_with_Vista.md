### Post by dmizer on 2009-08-07
I know that there are lots of posts on this forum regarding file sharing, but for most people it's quite simple. Just right click on the folder you want to share and click "sharing options" and make sure that all three boxes are checked. Just don't try to share your entire home folder, and it should work fine.

As for printing, just click System > Administration > Printing to share your printer.
