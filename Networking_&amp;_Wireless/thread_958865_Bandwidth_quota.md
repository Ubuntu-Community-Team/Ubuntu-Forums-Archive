### Post by svguy on 2008-10-26
What kind of router do you have?
You might consider changing the firmware on the router to something like dd-wrt so give you more options for control.

I setup a wireless network at work on a wrt54g v8 with dd-wrt and made it so my machine takes priority over everything else. But you can kind of create 'groups' for priority.
Might be worth checking out.
