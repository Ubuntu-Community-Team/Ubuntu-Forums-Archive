### Post by logan_huff on 2007-03-19
After trying to install my Linksys WPC54gver.3 wireless adapter on to my laptop, using everything from ndiswrapper to installing the firmware to handing my comp over to a local linux-savvy computer technician, I realized the linksys card wouldnt work (easily on my ubuntu system. I broke down and bought the D-Link WNA-2330 rangebooster wireless-G from bestbuy. I reinstalled Ubuntu 6.10 with the card in its proper slot. it WORKED STRAIGHT OUT OF THE BOX, connecting me to all the wireless hotspots and to my home router. This is the way to go! forget your Linksys and fork over the 50 bucks or so and save yourself both time and headache. :)
