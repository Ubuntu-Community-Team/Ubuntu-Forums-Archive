### Post by daxdog on 2009-01-12
Every device on your network must have a unique IP address.  The first three octets (groups of numbers) should be the same, but the final octet must be different.  Everything should be 192.168.0.x.  Just change x for each device. 1=router, 2=computer, 3=printer, etc.
