### Post by jonobr on 2011-11-17
Hello


For whatever reason your DNS is not responding to your query.

You send a request to 192.168.1.1 and its not responding.

Given the 8.8.8.8 works, the only things I can think of are

1- you have the wrrong IP address for DNS lookup, are you sure its 1.1. and not something else like 192.168.0.1 or 192.168.1.254 ?

2- The DNS at 192.168.1.1 is not working correctly and is not resolving IP addresses.

3- DNS is working on 192.168.1.1 but for some reason is not responding to DNS addresses to your ip addresses.

4- Your network doesnt have a DNS element which means you will have to use 8.8.8.8

thats all I can think of right now.
