### Post by esodin on 2010-05-28
For those of you considering an Android phone as a modem:

I have just installed Android 2.2 on my Nexus One and USB tethering works right out of the box on Ubuntu 10.4. Plug in the USB cable; turn on tethering on the N1 and the "Auto USB0" turns up in the network list.

You can also set up the N1 as a wifi hub that connects via 3G to the internet and shares this connection through a wifi access point. The N1 wifi point did not show up automatically in the list but easy to connect through "Connect to hidden wireless network".

What a joy!:)

Edit: My hardware is Dell studio xps 16
