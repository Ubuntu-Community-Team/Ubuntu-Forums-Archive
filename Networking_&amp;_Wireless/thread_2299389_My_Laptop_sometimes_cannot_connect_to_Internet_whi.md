### Post by ian-weisser on 2015-10-18
Which problem? Your symptom could have a myriad of possible causes.

For example,
Perhaps access point needs to be power-cycled.
Perhaps too many nearby networks on conflicting channels.
Perhaps you're too far from your access point, or sitting in an EM shadow.
Perhaps you have a flaky ISP connection.
Perhaps your roommate playing a joke.
