### Post by jml on 2008-01-28
The fact that you can connect wirelessly at your parent's house tells me that your hardware and software configurations are OK.  I suspect that the problem may be with your neighbors wireless access point.  If you are accessing it with your neighbor's permission, you may want to ask if any recent changes were made to the hardware.  Your inability to access it suggests that either encryption has been added, or possibly, the wireless access point has been locked down to specific hardware MAC adresses.

If you do not have your neighbor's permission to access the network, you may be in violation of "anti-cybersqautting" laws that are on the books in some countries.
