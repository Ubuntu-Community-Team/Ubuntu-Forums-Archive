### Post by ciryatan on 2008-08-17
Hiya,

first of all. If you are not very good at linux this might be overkill, no offense :-)
With aircrack you can get very frustrated since its no exact science, and normally you need to patch your wlan driver.
And it does not even work if you dont have a wlan card for which the monitoring/injection/etc works.

But if you want to try it i suggest you check out the Tutorials at the aircrack site.
There are instructions how to patch the most common wlan drivers.

And the newbie guide is also quite nice:
[http://www.aircrack-ng.org/doku.php?id=newbie_guide](http://www.aircrack-ng.org/doku.php?id=newbie_guide)

Getting started with aircrack:
[http://www.aircrack-ng.org/doku.php?id=getting_started](http://www.aircrack-ng.org/doku.php?id=getting_started)
