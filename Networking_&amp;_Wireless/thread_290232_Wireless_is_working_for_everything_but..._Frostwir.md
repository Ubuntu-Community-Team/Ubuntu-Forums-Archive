### Post by trubblemaker on 2006-11-01
I'm going to guess frostwire needs ports routed to work, and you've routed those ports to your lan ip address. And that is different from you wireless ip address. I don't know of another way to fix it other than to change the port routing on your router to point to your wireless ip address.  That basically means everytime you switch form wireless to lan and back you need to change the routing.  Maybe someone knows another way but that's the only way I know.
