### Post by dmizer on 2007-01-20
i'd suggest avoiding usb if at all possible.  for several reasons.  first of all, wireless usb cards are less than "well supported" in linux.  second of all, it's simply not a very good i/o for network data and even windows frequently has difficulty with it.  if i recall correctly, i believe usb i/o is slower than pcmcia, and certainly slower than a pci slot.

either way though, here is where you can find information about cards that work well in ubuntu (dapper or otherwise) here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported)
