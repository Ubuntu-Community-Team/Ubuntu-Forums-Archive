### Post by trash on 2007-11-06
A silly little thing but good to know.

I installed ndiswrapper after trying the restricted drivers, they both failed and after about 1/2 hour of searching and being frustrated I remembered that the wireless led had never gone on. This can happen if in windows the machine was shutdown while the wireless was off.

Simply boot into windows and enable wireless, make sure the LED is on before rebooting back into linux.... hopefully you'll be as lucky as me and have working wireless:) as i did with ndiswrapper  on Gutsy.
