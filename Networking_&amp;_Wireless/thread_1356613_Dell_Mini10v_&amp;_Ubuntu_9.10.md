### Post by snowpine on 2009-12-16
This worked for me on my Dell Mini 9 (which I believe is similar hardware).

Connect to internet by wired ethernet.
Go to the System menu and choose Administration, Hardware Drivers.
Click to activate the Broadcom STA driver.

Hope that helps!
