### Post by chili555 on 2012-03-09
It appears you have the right drivers and no conflicting drivers loaded; so far, so good.

There is a newer firmware for your device. Please download the attached file to your desktop. Right-click it and select *Extract Here*. Now we back up your existing firmware:```
sudo mv /lib/firmware/rt2870.bin /lib/firmware/rt2870.bak
```Now we copy over the new firmware:```
sudo cp Desktop/rt2870.bin /lib/firmware/
```Now we unload the driver and reload with the new firmware:```
sudo modprobe -rv rt2800usb
sudo modprobe rt2800usb
```Any improvements?
