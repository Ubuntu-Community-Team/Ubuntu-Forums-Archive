### Post by slakkie on 2010-01-09
Networking in general:

ifconfig
route
netstat
ip
ifup
ifdown
tcpdump/snoop


Wireless:
iwlist
iwconfig

Look up each manpage and it will point you to other documents and commands, but these should get you started.
