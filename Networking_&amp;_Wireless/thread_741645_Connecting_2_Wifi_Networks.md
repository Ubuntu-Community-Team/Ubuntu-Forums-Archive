### Post by kaginken on 2008-03-31
two ideas come to mind.  VPN would be a good way, if you wanted to go via the internet.

If, however, you're close enuf to have them talk to each other, you can make a homemade signal booster out of a pringles can, some aluminum foil and other household items.  Then you can hook that up to your wireless router and 'beam' the signal very far, ie right into your house (from home) and/or vice versa.

You can buy these signal boosters, but at over $100, you might as well make your own.  There's lots of how-to's on the net on this.

Hope that helps!  :guitar:
