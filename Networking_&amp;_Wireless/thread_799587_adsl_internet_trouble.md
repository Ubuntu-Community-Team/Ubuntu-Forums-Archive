### Post by nicedude on 2008-05-28
I used to have the old Stingray looking speedtouch as well :-) . Heres a piece of advice that you should use no matter what hispeed internet you use. Buy a router and I will list 4 of the main reasons why you should do so.

1. For people using ADSL connections it can be set to handle your PPPOE login for you which then allows you to connect via wired ethernet or wireless to your ISP without having to do anything different than cable modem users to access the Internet. Ubuntu becomes plug and play :-)

2. Both netgear & linksys models will come with "statefull packet inspection" which is a built in hardware firewall and will help be the first layer of security for your home network.

3. It will allow you to connect more than one PC to your internet connection at one time. Usually home models have 4 ports plus wireless access for more machines or devices as well. 

4. Low Cost - You get all those features and more for very little money. A netgear or linksys model with wifi support can be had for $40-50US for a home model via newegg.com.

So please consider obtaining a router for your home network so you can have a better & more secure Internet setup than just the ISP's modem will provide. Please feel free to PM me if you need help choosing one or setting up one you buy and I will try to help you personally .
