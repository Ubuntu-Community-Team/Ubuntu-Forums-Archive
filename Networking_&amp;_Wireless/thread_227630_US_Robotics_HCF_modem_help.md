HCF is usually a Conexant modem.  Conexant do not offer free linux modem drivers.  You probably have to pay 15 bucks for the priviledge of using their modem in linux.  You will probably have to pay another 15 bucks in a year or so to update the driver, too.

It's probably easier to just buy another modem - one that works with linux, supported by a company that cares about software freedom.
