Other than that, I can say the following: you are running the wl proprietary driver, and from what I'm seeing, it is only capable of running a/b/g annexes of the WiFi standard, although your card is also capable of the "n" annex. From the scan results, all of the scanned networks are capable of at least 54Mbit/s, which is the "g" annex. I don't know if your card doesn't list speeds higher than that because of the driver, or because none of the access points in range support higher speeds. It's a longshot, but check your router if it is configured to work only in "n" mode. Might be that you can connect to your phone because it is using 802.11g, and your router is using 802.11n only.

Also, you can compare the network scan results using the usb thumbdrive and the actual install, see if something is amiss, or if the drivers don't match.
