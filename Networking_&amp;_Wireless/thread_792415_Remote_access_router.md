### Post by Monicker on 2008-05-12
Most of them should have that ability.  It should be somewhere in your configuration options to turn it on.  Poke around through all of the menus.

Of course, if you do this, I would suggest that you make sure the router up to date on its firmware, and has a very strong password.  There have been flaws in the past which allow people to bypass authentication on several of the linksys routers.
