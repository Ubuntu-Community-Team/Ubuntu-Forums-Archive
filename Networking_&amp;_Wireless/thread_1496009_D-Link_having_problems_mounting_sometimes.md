### Post by JayPeeJay on 2010-05-28
My external wireless is having problems mounting some of the time.  If I plug it into one of my USB ports, my computer just completely freezes.  If I plug it into the other one it either does nothing, or it will mount and show all the available connections, but when I try to connect to my home connection (with full signal strength) it just plain doesn't work, and the wireless option disappears.  My connection works when I plug my computer directly to my router with an ethernet cable.

When I have it plugged in and boot it I get the "can not enumerate" error for the usb port.

I have had trouble mounting from my usb ports with other things, but my midisport works just fine from both ports.

Is it possible that the thing is just broken or is something else going on here?

---

### Post by JayPeeJay on 2010-05-29
Okay, problem solved.  It is my physical USB ports, they are loose.  Any device I plug in their wiggles around and mounts then unmounts, in the case of the external wifi, causing a crash.  Figured it out when I saw my ipod keep disappearing then reappearing on my desktop.  Hmmm this sux.
