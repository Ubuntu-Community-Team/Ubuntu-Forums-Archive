### Post by ardvark on 2005-03-29
I had been having slow internet problems since loading warty. I tried all of the fixes and only one seemed to help, but that fix killed my sound and floppy. I was discussing this at work and they suggested using a different DNS other than my own ISP's (comcast). This really solved the problem. It appears that Comcast, and perhaps other ISP's, are not responding to IPV6 messages in any way. This causes a 30 second time-out. I'd call Comcast about this, but they state that they only support IE and Windoz...  I have a 4 port Linksys router so I was able to go static between it and my PC's so I could lock in the other DNS servers..
