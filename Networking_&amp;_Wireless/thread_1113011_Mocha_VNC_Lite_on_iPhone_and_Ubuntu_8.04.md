### Post by Sable683 on 2009-05-14
All you need to do is open the mocha VNC and tap on menu and edit connections enter your ip address for your linux box and click connect. I am using Ubuntu 9.04. 

The only issue I am having is that when I connect, my screen on the i-phone does not update. Anyways, didn't mean to hijack your thread, but the setup should be pretty straight forward.
