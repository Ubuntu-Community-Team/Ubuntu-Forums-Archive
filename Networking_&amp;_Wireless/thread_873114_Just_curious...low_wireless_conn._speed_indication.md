### Post by caljohnsmith on 2008-07-28
If you've seen it reach 54 Mb/s, then there could be many reasons why your connection slows to a few Mb/s. Even if your hardware is working perfectly, it could be simply because you have a weak signal (connection) with your router. When you do "iwconfig", what is the "link quality"?
