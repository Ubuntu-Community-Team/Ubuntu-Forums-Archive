### Post by djf_jeff on 2006-12-06
I buy a Trendet TBW-101UB adapter. It work fine with my setup, I think any bluetooth dongle will work fine. I use it for my mouse and my cell phone.

For your need, check carefully that your cell phone is well supported under Linux and that you can actually do anything with the cell phone (some company block certain bluetooth functionality like file transfer).

For the headset, I search for this a couple years ago and don't find anything, maybe things have change since then.
