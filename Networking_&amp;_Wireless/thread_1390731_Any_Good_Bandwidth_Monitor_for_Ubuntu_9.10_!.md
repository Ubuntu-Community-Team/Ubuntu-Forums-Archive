### Post by pirateghost on 2010-01-26
i use BMON for realtime bandwidth monitoring, jnettop for monitoring realtime connections, and vnstat to keep a database for hour/day/week/month/year.  if you are running a webserver on the same machine you can actually put up a php frontend to connect to your vnstat database.  otherwise, these are all just CLI apps
