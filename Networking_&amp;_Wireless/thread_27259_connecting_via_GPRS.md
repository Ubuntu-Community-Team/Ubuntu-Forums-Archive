### Post by accuser on 2005-04-15
Provided your GPRS device (mobile phone, etc.) can identify itself as a modem, you should have no problems. I am using a Sony Ericsson K700i for GPRS access via Bluetooth - all you need is bluez and ppp. The hardest part if figuring out which number to dial for GPRS activation since this varies by phone and service provider.
