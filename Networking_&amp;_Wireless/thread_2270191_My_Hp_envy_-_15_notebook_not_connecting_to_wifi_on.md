### Post by jeremy31 on 2015-03-21
I would change the wifi router to channel 11 as your current channel is crowded and if that doesn't help ```
echo "options iwlwifi 11n_disable=8" | sudo tee -a /etc/modprobe.d/iwlwifi.conf
``` Reboot
