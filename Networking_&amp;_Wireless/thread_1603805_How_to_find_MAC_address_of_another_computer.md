### Post by zarqoon on 2010-10-23
If your router has a dhcp server it is possible that it keeps a client list. I do not know your model but a lot of routers do. Check you routers configuration menu for a dhcp client list. In some routers i've also seen lists of previously connected clients in the mac filter settings.
