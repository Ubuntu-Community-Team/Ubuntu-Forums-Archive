### Post by Don DeGregori on 2010-09-28
Just want to say the Intel 5300 Ultimate N PCI-mini wireless card works perfectly with 10.04. Thought I would have all kinds of problems, but just lucky I guess. Maybe it's the motherboard, also an Intel. The MB is mini-ITX, 6.7 x 6.7 inches, D510MO. It has a mini-PCI spot right on the MB. Dropped in the tiny card, connected 3 antennas, fired up computer, and connected in seconds to the Net. No terminal commands needed. WOW!

Now I need to know how to kill power to the card in software? It does get rather warm, so if I use wired LAN, no sense heating up the wireless. Anyone know how to do this?
