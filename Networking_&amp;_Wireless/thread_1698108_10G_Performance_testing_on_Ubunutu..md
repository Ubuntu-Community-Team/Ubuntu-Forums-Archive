### Post by Insanemal on 2012-09-11
Use bigger packets.

Your issue is that you can saturate a 10GE adaptor with heaps of tiny packets and not actually use up the full bandwidth because your stuck dealing with things like Interframe gap and other timing related things. 

This 'issue' is part of the reason that some people still use lots of bonded 1G instead of 10G.
