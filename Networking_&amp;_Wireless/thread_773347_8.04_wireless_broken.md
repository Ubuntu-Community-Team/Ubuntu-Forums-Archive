### Post by rstuart on 2008-04-29
This problem was caused by a faulty wireless router. When i disabled dhcdbd and NetworkManager then tried to connect to the route via command line, dhclient was reporting "bogus hlen". When i changed the host-name line of my dhclient.conf from "<hostname>" to something more friendly like "xyz", this fixed the problem.

I was first alerted that the problem might be with the router when i also couldn't connect to it via ethernet either.

For the record, the router is a Linksys WRT300N.
