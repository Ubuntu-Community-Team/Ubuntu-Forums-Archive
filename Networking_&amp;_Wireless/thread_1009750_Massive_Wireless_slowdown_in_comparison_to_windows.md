### Post by Woodgar on 2008-12-13
I've been experiencing dreadful wireless speeds with Ibex for ages now, with downloads being around the level I would expect with a dial-up connection. 

Anyway, I've just been looking for a solution and found 

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=982384](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=982384)

which has solved all my problems and my download is now back to around 5Mbs where it should be.

Note that I don't have the same hardware as the person in the other thread, but the solution (disabling ipv6) worked for me so I'd suggest giving it a go. After all, it's easy to reverse the change if it has no effect on your machine.

Good luck!
