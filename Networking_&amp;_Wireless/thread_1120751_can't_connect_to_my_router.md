### Post by lisati on 2009-04-11
Sometimes it takes a little while for wireless stations to register that an access point has gone "off air" and has been replaced by one of the same name.
If the new name shows up when you click on the network icon on the top panel, and all you have changed is the name, you should be able to connect.
