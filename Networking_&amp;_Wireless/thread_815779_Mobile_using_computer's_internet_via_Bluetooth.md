### Post by gradedcheese on 2008-06-02
Actually that is possible, I've done something similar with the OpenMoko phone (establish a bluetooth link to a laptop and then route traffic, giving the phone Internet access).  Their Wiki describes the process (you can ignore the OpenMoko-specific steps) here: [http://wiki.openmoko.org/wiki/Manually_using_Bluetooth](http://wiki.openmoko.org/wiki/Manually_using_Bluetooth) however you'll have far less flexibility on your phone, so I can't promise that you'll have everything you need to make it work.
