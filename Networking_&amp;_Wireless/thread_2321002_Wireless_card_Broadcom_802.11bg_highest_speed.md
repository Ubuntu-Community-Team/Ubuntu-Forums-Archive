### Post by him610 on 2016-04-19
It is entirely possible the age of your laptop components are limiting your download speed. If you are the interested in *possibly* increasing the speed of your network traffic, you have some options: 
A. Replace your internal wireless network card - may need to disassemble laptop, or it may only require opening an access panel, relatively inexpensive.
B. Install an external usb wireless network adapter - easiest to do, relatively inexpensive
C. Replace the laptop with a newer model - most expensive

I upgraded all of my wireless 802.11b/g devices to 802.11b/g/n a few years ago to take advantage of higher speeds from my ISP. Now I think about the latest A/C devices.;)
