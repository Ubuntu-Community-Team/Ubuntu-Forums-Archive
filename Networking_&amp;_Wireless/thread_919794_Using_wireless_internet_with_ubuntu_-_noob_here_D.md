### Post by Jagged on 2008-09-14
click on system then administration then network. you should see you wireless card (it will just say wireless connection) click on the unlock button enter you login password and the click on the wireless then click properties. you may find your wireless network in the drop down box, if not just type in the name. then enter your passphrase for you wireless network, go down and select dhcp for configuration under connection settings. hit ok then close out and you should be good to go
