### Post by Tsen on 2007-06-22
If it's a laptop you want PCMCIA or USB.  I'd say go with the PCMCIA, it's typically similarly priced and doesn't hog valuable USB slots.  Plus it's a lot less likely to get disconnected.   Other than that, just find one in your price range with good reviews, check via google to see how the Linux support is (find one that's FULLY supported in Linux--don't buy one requiring ndiswrapper unless absolutely necessary).
