### Post by mikedivine on 2020-06-25
I was installing Lubuntu 20.04 LTS onto an HP Stream Notebook 13 and I couldn't get the Broadcom Network Adapter to function.  

I found under Preferences -> Additional Drivers that it saw the drivers I needed and it wanted to download them.  Unfortunately it was giving the error that it couldn't proceed without an internet connection.  This Notebook does not have an ethernet port so I tried a USB Wifi Adapter but had trouble getting the correct drivers to get it to work as well.  I tried to download drivers from my other laptop onto a USB Flash Drive and install them manually but they kept failing with "needing additional dependencies" and those dependencies needed more dependencies.  So it seemed as it would be a never ending trail.  I'm guessing there is a better way but I am not well versed on how to do that.  

I also saw online some people were able to connect by tethering their cell phones.  So I finally tried plugging my Iphone into the Notebook and turning on my hot spot and Voila! It connected immediately letting me download the Broadcom Drivers.  Then I proceeded to install Lubuntu.

Please feel free to add anything that could help with people that the cell phone tethering doesn't work for.
