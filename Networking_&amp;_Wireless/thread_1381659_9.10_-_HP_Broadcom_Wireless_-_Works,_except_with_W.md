This driver has gotten the job done, except in one rather painful matter: I cannot connect to networks that are WEP encrypted. (Yes, I have quintuple-checked to ensure I gave it the right password). Furthermore, I doubt it's a matter of "range", as my laptop is literally inches from the router. Also, other not-my-computer Ubuntu laptops have had no trouble connecting to my network. 

I've given Ndiswrapper a go and have successfully installed it, but experienced the same behavior: no-go on WEP networks.
