I was recently faced with the problem of not being able to connect to the internet. Im using a proxy'd connection. I just thought i'll share how i solved my problem.

To solve it for your whole computer, you can go to System Settings > Network Proxy. Change from "direction connection" to "Automatic proxy configuration" and then enter your autoconfig. URL.

otherwise for a single browser, like firefox perhaps... go to "Preferences" and then go to the "Advanced" main Tab, and then go to the "Network" sub-Tab. From there onwards its evident...

If you feel like you can add other ways or wana correct me, feel free!:)
