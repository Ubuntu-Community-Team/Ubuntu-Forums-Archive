### Post by stickus on 2007-11-22
After much head banging (](*,) not :guitar:), i have decided to give up and get a new card.  For $40 canadian, I can get a D-Link WDA-1320 PCI card that *should* work out of the box. I should have looked into this before I decided to get a cheapo AirLink brand card, but that was my option at the time.  In the end I would recommend staying away from brands that you have never heard of before (like AirLink) and stay with the reliable brands that you can trust (D-Link, Linksys and others).
