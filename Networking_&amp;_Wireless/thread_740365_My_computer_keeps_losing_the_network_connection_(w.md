### Post by pseudo-random on 2008-03-30
You could try running several things. Either run Wireshark wifi to look for erroneous packets or at least get a feel for how much 802.11 traffic is in your area.
Try de-conflicting channels with your neighbours. Having a channel 1 apart isn't enough due to the nature of the spread spectrum modulation. It needs to be at least 2 channels apart.
Good luck.
