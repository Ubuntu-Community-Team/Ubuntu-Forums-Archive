### Post by binarypill on 2009-03-26
thank you ubuntu developers for fixing one major gripe i had with ubuntu, the wifi iwl3945 problem.

for so long, i've been one of the few who had been suffering the iwl3945 wifi problem. the problem was that the wifi switch would not turn on the wifi after the wifi had been turned off. as an interim solution, i used the terminal to do a "sudo rmmod" and "sudo modprobe" to get the wifi working again.

after the latest kernel update to linux-image-2.6.27-14-generic (i heard the problem had something to do with the kernel so i'm thinking the kernel update did it), the wifi switch works great now!

once again i am quite impressed at how much my insistence on staying with ubuntu has paid off so far.

...now if only ubuntu could auto-magically repair my dying batteries...
