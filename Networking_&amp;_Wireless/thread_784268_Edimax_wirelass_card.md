### Post by Moriartie on 2008-05-06
Over the weekend I installed 8.04 onto a friends laptop. We managed to get the wireless network to work with WPA encryption with relative ease, the problem is, whenever ubuntu is restarted, the connection stops working. But rather than twiddling with the software to fix it, it is simply necessary to remove the PCMCIA card and then reinsert it. The wireless then instantly works.

Whilst I cannot remember the exact model of the card in question (I'll ask the friend), it does seem like the wikipage for edimax cards ( [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCardsEdimax](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCardsEdimax) ) doesn't mention this issue.
(There are Linux drivers for the card available on the website, but we didn't install them).
