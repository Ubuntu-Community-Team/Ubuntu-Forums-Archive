### Post by Crisisoverdrive on 2008-06-11
I spent about 2 weeks trying to get my DWA 645 to work. I followed many many how-tos and what nots. I managed to get my card installed but it would not connect to my router. i could see it just not access it. Well anyway i finally got it to work. 

As I had been advised I downloaded the driver from D-Link and installed the inf. file that came with it. THAT WAS MY PROBLEM!!!! I decided to uninstall it and rather I used the Atheros Driver for my card (atheros chipset 5416).
I plugged in my card and instant success!!!!! in fact this post is being written over my wireless network...YAY and about time!!!

Well i just thought that I would put this suggestion out there considering that i could not find a good solution over the internet.
