### Post by northd_tech on 2011-05-01
PlayOnLinux (a WINE frontend) and WINE are fairly complex "issues" in and of themselves.  First, we should probably make certain that your network connection(s) either are or ARE NOT working.

Try viewing some webpages that you never visit (and consequently should not be in your browser cache).   I'm just guessing here but try these:

[http://www.usc.edu](http://www.usc.edu)

[http://www.yale.edu](http://www.yale.edu)

[http://www.brown.edu](http://www.brown.edu)

If the browser just says "loading..." forever when you try those university websites then you likely have a problem in your Networking/Wireless.
