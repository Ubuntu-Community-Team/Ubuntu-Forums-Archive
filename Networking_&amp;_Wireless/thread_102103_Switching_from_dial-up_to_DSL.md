### Post by bigaltx on 2005-12-11
I recently switched from dial-up to DSL, using a Westell 6100 USB modem. I would have preferred to use a NIC but I don't have an open PCI slot. My question is how do I get Ubuntu to regocnize this modem. When I open the networking window, all it shows is my old dial-up modem.

---

### Post by bigaltx on 2005-12-12
:cool: Problem solved. Found that my motherboard had an onboard NIC that was disabled in the BIOS. Enabled the NIC, switched to ethernet cable, and now have a working network connection.
