### Post by crantok on 2012-04-08
Oh. My. Goodness. Well, I feel pretty stupid. There *is* another button to enable/disable wireless.

In case anyone else with an X61s has trouble finding it: The button is on the bottom of the front edge of the case. This button is hidden if the laptop is mounted on an X6 docking station. Note: This button also stops any external USB wireless adapters from working too.
