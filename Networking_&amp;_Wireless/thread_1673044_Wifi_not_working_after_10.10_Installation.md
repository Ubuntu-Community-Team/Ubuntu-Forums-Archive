### Post by sevenenemy on 2011-01-21
for mine it is alt+f11 so its different for most makes/models but if you look in your top panel there should be a button for additional drivers, it will not install firmware for your wireless on its own if it is proprietary so you need to manually do it. when the additional drivers box opens after you click it select the one it has chosen for itself, usually the sta driver and that should do the trick.
