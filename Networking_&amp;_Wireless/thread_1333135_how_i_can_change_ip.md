### Post by .nedberg on 2009-11-21
Nothing wrong there

192.168.1.64 is your internal IP on your network
91.144.37.8 is your external IP, from your ISP

If you want to use remote desktop internally, use internal IP. If it is from an external machine, use external IP (you will probably have to open up some ports in your router).
