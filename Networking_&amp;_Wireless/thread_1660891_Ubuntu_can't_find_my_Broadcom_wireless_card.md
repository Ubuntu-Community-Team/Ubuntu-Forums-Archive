### Post by PatchesTheCaveman on 2011-01-06
Plug into a wired Ethernet Internet connection and go to System > Administration > Additional Drivers on your top panel.  Select the Broadcom STA wireless driver and click Install.  It's that easy!

If you have trouble, refer to this thread:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1604868](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1604868)
