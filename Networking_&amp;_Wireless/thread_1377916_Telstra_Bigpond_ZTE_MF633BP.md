### Post by paul_h on 2010-02-09
Geoff,

I received my new Telstra Bigpond ZTE MF633BP 7.2 USB Modem yesterday and got up and running in Ubuntu 9.10 quite quickly. My settings were the default *99#, Username is your full email address (*****@bigpond.com), password, the APN is telstra.bigpond. But I also had to put in the DNS servers 139.130.4.4, 203.50.2.71 in the IPv4 settings.

I installed it in Windows XP, but only after a 30 discussion with the Bigpond help desk. However I did the Ubuntu installation with no significant problem on my own entirely. 

Give it a go. It works really well.

Paul.
