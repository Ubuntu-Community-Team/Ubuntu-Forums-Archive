### Post by P4man on 2010-10-03
Bandwidth needed to output raw SD/HD is extremely high. The only devices I know that claim to do this wirelessly are UWB (ultra Wide Band). Be aware that these things only have a usable range of a few meters and usually a maximum of 720p. There are very few such products, and I have no idea of their support under linux.  Here is one:
[http://www.wisair.com/products/wireless-usb-solutions/wireless-usb-av-adapter/](http://www.wisair.com/products/wireless-usb-solutions/wireless-usb-av-adapter/)
