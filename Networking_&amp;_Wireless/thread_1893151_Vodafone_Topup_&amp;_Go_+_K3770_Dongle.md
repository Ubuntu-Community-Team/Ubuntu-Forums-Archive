### Post by mightybuddha on 2011-12-09
Hi Guys,

Took me a while to find out what the problem I was having with this.  

My parents bought a K3770 dongle with Vodafone's (UK) Topup & Go package.

They have a Dell Mini 9 that has a built in 3G modem so we didn't need to use the Dongle and put the sim card straight into the sim slot which I know works with Ubuntu.

But we couldn't connect.

Ubuntu (and pretty much every website on the net!) say the APN is **ppbundle.internet ** (it used to be **pp.internet** but changed about a year ago for newer models)

Well it looks like it has changed again.

For anyone havign trouble connecting using Vodafone's Topup & Go package, the new APN is **SMART**.  

Username and password is still **web**

Once I changed that it connected no problem :)

That is all :) Enjoy!
