### Post by Kerubu on 2010-08-12
Not entirely sure of the symptoms so just a few questions:

Are you losing connection to your own wireless base station? OR

Are you having trouble getting access to websites e.g. slow.

At times when you've lost your connection, try viewing your base stations home page (where you set all the settings). For example my base station is 192.168.0.1 so I just type that into a browser. If you can reliable get this page then it possible it be your internet provider.

It's possible that you're getting intereference problems on your wi-fi channel so changing the broadcast channel might help. You'd need to consult your base station's manual for info on how to do this
