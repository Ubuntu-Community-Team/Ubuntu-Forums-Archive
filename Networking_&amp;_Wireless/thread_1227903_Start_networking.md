### Post by mdmarmer on 2009-07-31
Networking will make it easier for you to connect (and share files among) the various computers, printers and other devices you may have.  If you don't know anything about networking you probably don't need it -- just connect a printer to your computer and you have a simple working setup.  If you want to share files you can use a flash drive to do that.
