### Post by MsMeteorrock on 2013-04-04
No, I would not think that the end of life for a ubuntu build would shut down the internet on your computer. It would be wise to upgrade though to a newer build for your ubuntu though. Check your router,  and are you on wireless or plugged into your broadband internet? Lots of times if you cycle the power on your router, by turning the power off on it and powering the router back on, it will help reset it.

Also if you are using a wireless dongle, try to unplug it and replug it in, over time the connections will corrode some, same with if your computer is plugged directly into your router, unplug all of your connections and replug them in to seat them into your ports better. So do those physical steps first and see if that helps. Usually to troubleshoot problems you will want to start with the simplest steps first and work from there. If that does not help you can then assume its a software problem on your computer.
