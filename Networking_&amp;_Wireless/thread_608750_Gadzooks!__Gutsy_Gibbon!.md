### Post by sulligogs on 2007-11-10
Hello Everyone,

Just want to report on my experience of Gutsy Gibbon over the past few days since I did a first fresh install.

IT'S MAGIC!

Not a single crash.  The wireless works immediately.  And the new visual appearance is oozing with charm, though not on full, just on medium setting for me please!

The main benefit for me was the wireless networking.  I've got an EDIMAX EW-7128g wireless network card that would just hang with network manager.  I had to resort to using RutilT, which I posted a guide to on using in [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=554089&highlight=rutilt](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=554089&highlight=rutilt)

Now, from the go, everything works and I hope and pray that nothing gets broken in subsequent releases.

Keep up the good work Ubuntu.  This is a marvelous  alternative to Windows.  How do I go about donating?  I'll do my bit :)

So, for once, not a rant and rave, but an all round WOW.

Over and Out!
