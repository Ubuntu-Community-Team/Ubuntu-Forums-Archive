### Post by TheFu on 2013-08-01
Is the printer shared over the network?

In the sort term, the quick answer is to print to PDF on the remote machine, then transfer the files locally and print them.  Depending on your answers to the questions, remote printing may not be possible at all ... or it might be very easy.
