To be honest, I dont know if this will work, but you can easily undo without problems by deleting from interfaces file, instead assign static *** adjust to fit your network*** but main objective is the netmask, then you will have to either restart networking or reboot whatever. give it a try.

```

address 192.168.x.x
netmask 255.255.0.0
network 192.168.0.0
broadcast 192.168.255.255
