### Post by 3ruce on 2011-02-27
1st time Ubuntu here (Maverick), had a great weekend playing with it. 

No scare stories, despite what I've heard from others (although I did manage to screw up the dual boot option by trying really hard to hibernate, when the install CD clearly said it was disabled!!!)

Anyway, having got basically everything going (apart from an old webcam...), I remembered that I'd completely forgotten about ActiveSync and how my Win6.5 phone doubles as a nice wireless modem. Cue lots of worrying thoughts about dongles and so on...

Having connected the USB cable, I was very impressed to see the laptop share the connection first time (I did an IP address check to confirm it was the 3G connection that was in use).

Fantastic :-)
