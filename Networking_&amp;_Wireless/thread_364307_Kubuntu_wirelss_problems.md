### Post by AusIV4 on 2007-02-18
I have a similar problem when I use wlassistant to connect to certain networks. My campus network requires that I login with a VPN client before getting internet access, and wlassistant seems to think it's not connected if it can't get through to the internet. wlassistant tells me it isn't connected, but the VPN can get through and then I'm online. If your network doesn't have immediate internet acces, wlassistant may say it has a problem, even if it connnects you.

I've found that knetworkmanager works better, as it will automatically connect to preferred networks, and doesn't give me errors. I'd recommend trying it and seeing if it works.
