### Post by brokenbynubs on 2010-03-07
I've just recently bought a gigabit router, I love it.  I frequently transfer huge files across my wired network and the 12Mbp/s max transfer rates just weren't cutting it anymore.  Upgraded to gigabit and I'm getting 40-50Mbp/s... huge difference.

I haven't noticed much difference on my wireless network, feels pretty much the same.
