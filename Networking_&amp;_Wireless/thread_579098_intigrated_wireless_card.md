### Post by jbaerbock on 2007-10-18
I really don't know off the top of my head but best way to check is to run a liveCD and see. Running the liveCD does not influence your hardrive permanantly. Therefore you can test ubuntu on your laptop and see if everything works before you decide to install it.

Note that some cards will work with slight tweaking though so even if it does not work OOTB in Ubuntu don't give up. Also the new version which should be out tommorow had better wireless support so you may want to download 7.10 (Gutsy) at that point and try.
