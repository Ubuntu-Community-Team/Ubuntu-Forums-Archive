### Post by The Cog on 2008-12-17
Actually, you only need 2 pairs for an ethernet crossover. Pins 1 and 2 should connect to pins 3 and 6 at the far end using a twisted pair. Similarly, 3 and 6 connect to 1 and 2.

You absolutely need the right crimp tool, so for just a few cables it's cheaper to just by the cables.
