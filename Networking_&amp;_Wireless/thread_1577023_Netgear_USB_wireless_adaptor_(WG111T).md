### Post by rusivi on 2010-10-04
Thank you for posting. This is a known issue that requests you mark yourself "affected by this" via Ubuntu bug:
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/madwifi/+bug/147203](https://bugs.launchpad.net/madwifi/+bug/147203)

In the meantime to that bug being squashed, you may find success via ndiswrapper:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Device/WG111T](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Device/WG111T)
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper)

Hope that helps and feel free to ask followup questions about this in this thread.
