### Post by Lambert on 2007-11-06
Pump was written by redhat and I don't believe it's actively developed anymore. Packages are still around in respositories etc.. but I believe most use ISC's dhcp3 software now.

There have been threads in the past where some of the nodhcpoffer problems were solved when dhcp3 was uninstalled and pump installed. 

dhcp4 b2 was recently released so hopefully there are some improvements coming that will solve some of the odd nodhcpoffer and other dhcp related problems that are seen.
