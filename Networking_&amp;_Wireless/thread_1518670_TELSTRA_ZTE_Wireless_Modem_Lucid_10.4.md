Settings in Network Connections:

I had non-recognition of ZTE Next G Telstra wireless modem:
(after working, it stopped working)
The man who sold it to me in Telstra shop happens to run lucid lynx at home.
Took it back to him. He fixed my settings. If you're on bigpond, good.
If you're not, maybe still good
Crucial points: 
#1!! When asked to choose between plans in the set-up, tick 'My plan is not listed, or, None of the above, etc
and enter APN as telstra.bigpond.
Edit it, and ensure that under IPV4, method is "Automatic(PPP)"
and under PPP Settings tab,under Compression, untick all but TCP header line.
And Tick Available all users.
