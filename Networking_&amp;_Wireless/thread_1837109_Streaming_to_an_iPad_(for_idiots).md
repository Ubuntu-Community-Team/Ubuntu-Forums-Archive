### Post by kerry_s on 2011-09-01
right click on the folder you want to share in ubuntu, an select share.

on your ipad search the app store for "remote file viewer" by tiod(free) & install that.
in tiod click "my pc" & "scan", i'm sure you can figure it out from there.

also oplayer(free) can connect to samba & stream video.
