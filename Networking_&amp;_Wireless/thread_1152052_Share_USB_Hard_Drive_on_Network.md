### Post by jeremy1138 on 2009-05-07
Ok I figured this out on my own.  In order to get Ubuntu to allow you to share a folder that you do not "own" such as an external drive that you have mounted you have to edit your /etc/samba/smb.conf file and add the following in the [global] section:

```

usershare owner only = false

```

and that's it.  Sounds easy enough eh?  Oh and one more thing, you can't get the option to share the drive by clicking on the mounted drive icon on your desktop.  You need to open it up in nautilus and then go up one level so you can click on the folder there to get the option to share the disc.  Hope this helps anyone else who might be looking for a way to do this.
