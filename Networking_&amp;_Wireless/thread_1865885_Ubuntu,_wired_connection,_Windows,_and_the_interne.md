### Post by pa3fat on 2011-10-20
Open NetworkManager (in 11.04) it is in the topbar. up and down arrows (11.10 don't know).

Check that you have entered DHCP ONLY in IPV4 mode.

Set your settings for DHCP mode.

Verify that both check boxes in the screen are also marked to ON (X).

Then check if your resolved.conf gets nameserver n.n.n.n.
If not disconnect Auth0 or maybe even better, just reboot the PC.

It seems to be a bug in NetworkManager that it is not populating resolv.conf very well.

It worked for me after last weeks daily upgrades on 11.04.

### Post by pa3fat on 2011-10-23
In your router you can define IP reservations. Probably somewhere in the LAN area.
Depends on your router firmware/brand.

Once defined it will be OS independent as it recognizes the MAC address of your network card and will re-assign same IP over and over.
Note that after assigning this fixed IP you might need to reboot the router and PC  to really get it working.
