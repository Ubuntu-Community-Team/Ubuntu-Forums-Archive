### Post by jnorthr on 2008-11-24
do you have any security features activated in your router ? Try to turn off all security till you get the network up, you can turn it on later when you know it all works.

firefox may need to be told about your network manually. Try Edit>Preferences>Advanced>Settings to see what's being used now.
