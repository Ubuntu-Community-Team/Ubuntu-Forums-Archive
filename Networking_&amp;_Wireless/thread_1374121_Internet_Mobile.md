### Post by efflandt on 2010-01-07
You may get a better answer if you say what type of device and mobile provider.  For Verizon USB720 all I had to do was select country and mobile provider in Network Connections and it simply worked.  I do not have any PIN or login.

Some devices apparently are initially seen as usb mass storage, since they include autorun software for some OSs, and you may need to umount or eject that.  There is a wiki with info on may devices at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NetworkManager/Hardware/3G](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NetworkManager/Hardware/3G)
