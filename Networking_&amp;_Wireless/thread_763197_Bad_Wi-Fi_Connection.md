### Post by kevdog on 2008-04-22
With the madwifi driver you can boost the transmission power.  This may be possible with ra based cards, however I believe with other wireless chipsets this feature will not work.  A better solution -- flash your router (if you can) with the dd-wrt firmware.  You could then tinker with the setttings and boost the transmission power of the router if you wanted. (Increase the transmission power 6x).
