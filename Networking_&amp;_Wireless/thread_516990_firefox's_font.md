### Post by sfarber53 on 2007-08-04
Go into the "Options" for Firefox, click the "Content" tab and look down to "Fonts & Colors."  You can change the font & size right there without any problem.

### Post by onering on 2007-08-04
In Firefox you can also change the font size by holding down the CTRL key and moving the mouse wheel Up/Down.  To get it back to "normal size", hit CTRL-0  (That is CTRL and the Number 0).
