In respect to the hardware you need, it depends on the premises. Is there going to be all light surveillance do you need night vision as well?

Look out for these features in the cameras:
-nightvision
-good quality image capturing
-weatherproof(if for outside)
-rain drops view protection
-strong encasing, just in case someone throws a brick towards your camears(if for outside)
-the system needs to detect movement and upload those frames on the internet(ftp or something similar) just in case the thief takes your hard drive as well

Where I live, such a system, for home use costs about £500 and with £25 subscription per month for operator and some other stuff.

I did some research about this a few weeks ago and found something to suit my needs in the mentioned url.
