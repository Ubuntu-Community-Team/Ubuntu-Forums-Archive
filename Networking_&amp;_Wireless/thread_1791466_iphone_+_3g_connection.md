### Post by Dangertux on 2011-06-26
If you are referring to sharing the connection of the laptop with the iPhone via wireless this should be able to help you.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Internet/ConnectionSharing](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Internet/ConnectionSharing)

If you are referring to sharing the iPhone's 3g connection with the laptop you will want to see if your provider offers a tethering plan, if they do not you can tether your phone to your laptop yourself. However, you will need to research that on your own as I do not advocate jailbreaking iPhones which this will require.  Not because I am all about profiteering companies charging way too much for something that should be free or close to it, but because installing 3rd party firmware and or basebands on your phone is a security risk.
