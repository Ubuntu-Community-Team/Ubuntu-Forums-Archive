### Post by fmu96 on 2012-03-04
System, Admin, printing.  Right click on the cpl-310 or cpl-315 and click properties in the popup menu.   Click Change at the end of the line that starts "Make and Model" from the Samsung CPL-310 to the Samsung CPL-315.
