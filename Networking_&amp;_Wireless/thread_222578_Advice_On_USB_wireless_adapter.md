### Post by wislon on 2006-07-25
I have a Dlink GW-120 rev C. and it worked perfectly with ndiswrapper. It's not 'straight out of the box', but it was a snap to follow the step by step instructions, grab the windoze drivers off the CD that came with it and set it up. I am running it with wpa_supplicant for the encryption until I come up with a better solution.

Hint: 'lsusb' and [http://ndiswrapper.sourceforge.net](http://ndiswrapper.sourceforge.net) are your friends :)

HTH!
