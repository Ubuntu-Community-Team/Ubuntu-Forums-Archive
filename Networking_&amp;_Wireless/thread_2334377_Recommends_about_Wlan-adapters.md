### Post by gordintoronto on 2016-08-19
My TP-LINK TL-WN722N worked right out of the box.

Part of the problem is that manufacturers will change the chip without changing the model name, so two adapters which appear identical might work differently.

See this community help:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported)
