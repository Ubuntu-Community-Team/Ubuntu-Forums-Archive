### Post by mattgyver83 on 2009-05-08
Dont know if this quite hits home but, blueproximity is a package that can be used to do specific things when a bluetooth device (your phone) is within a specified distance.  As long as you have bluetooth enabled  you can pretty much setup whatever you want once the phone is within a desired range.  when it goes out of range it doesnt do anything.
