### Post by bouncingwilf on 2011-01-12
Yes that dongle should work with no problems. When you insert the dongle, it will show up as a mass media device, Ignore this and "Eject " the device by right clicking on it but leave it inserted in the computer. Wait a few seconds then click on the wireless icon on the top taskbar and just set it up. The defaults usually work fine.
