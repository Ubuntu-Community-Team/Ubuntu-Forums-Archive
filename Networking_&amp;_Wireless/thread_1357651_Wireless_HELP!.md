### Post by eli006 on 2009-12-17
In the upper right hand corner of your screen, just to the left of your volume control is your network connection tab. Simply click on that and it will show you a list of the available wireless networks. Choose your network and enter your security password (if necessary) and the computer should do the rest. Good luck.
