### Post by spic0m on 2009-01-10
Hi,

You need a base card (ex. TDM400 from Digium) and some add-on modules to it depending what you want to do. FXO modules are for connecting your line to the pc and FXS modules for connecting phones to pc. You can add both or more modules in the same base card or find a card allready having the numbers you want. If you google a bit you'll find more than enough solutions. :)

### Post by kencoe on 2009-02-05
A little assistance on picking the TDM card (most vendors don't help much).
The name tells you the modules on a TDM400 series card.

Digium names their card series (tdm) Office ports (for lines, first digit), Station ports (for phones, second digit), and revision. 

Example, a TDM13b is a second (b) revision of the TDM400 with one (1) FXO and three (3) FXS modules.

These cards are decent, but not cheap.
