### Post by K.Mandla on 2007-01-26
I think the reason the Orinoco Gold card is cheaper is because it's an 11b card, and technically the transfer rates on the 54g card will be faster.

That being said, I get the same *actual* transfer rates on my Linksys WPC11 (a b card with the Prism2 chipset) as I did on a Linksys WMP54G (a g card with a RaLink RT61 chipset), so if it were me and I was choosing ... I'd go with the cheaper of the two.

And to answer your question, yes, Orinoco cards are generally worry-free, for what I've seen.
