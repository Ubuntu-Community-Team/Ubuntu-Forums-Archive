it's probably not possible for a single source signal to get to 500 meters..

IEEE 802.11n has only about 70 meters of range...


Edit:

It might be more economic to just get a long RJ-11 cable for the wireless adapter..

### Post by Kevbert on 2008-12-02
Your best bet would be using a few wireless repeaters for that distance as even with line of sight you will still get some interference as microwave ovens, some cordless phones (eg DECT) use the same frequencies.  Just try googling wireless repeater.  A better way would be to use ethernet cable instead though again you may need to boost the signal due to cable losses.
