### Post by artistxe on 2009-09-08
Have you tried adding the NetworkManager applet to your panel ?
Right click on an empty part of the panel and choose the option "Add new item".
Add the system tray applet (NetworkManager is considered to be a notification tool since it lets you know the status of your connection) . Right clicking on this icon will let you edit your connection.
