### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-01-31
I think that two things are happening. They are related but not necessarily connected.

1) Networking and Wireless is not being enable every time you boot.

2) You have put the wrong password into Network manager. It is designed to remember the password for ease of use. So, it will remember wrong passwords as well as the correct one.

Have you put in your login password or are you entering the wireless key or passphrase? It is easy to make this mistake. I have made it myself.
