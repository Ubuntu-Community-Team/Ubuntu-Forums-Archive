### Post by GFBurke on 2006-07-11
Any NTFS drive is fine, as long as that drive is plugged into a windows system and is shared out.  My OSX, XP and ubuntu systems (wifi and wired) can all read/write to the NTFS drive that is shared out on my main XP machine.

As a stan-alone NAS however, it would have to be FAT32 pretty much.  Or you could build your own out of [www.freenas.org](www.freenas.org)
