### Post by chili555 on 2006-06-19
I have used a Linksys WET11 wireless ethernet bridge trouble-free for many years. They go for $30-40 on Ebay. The only drawback to the WET11 is it supports WEP only, not WPA. Linksys also makes a WET54G and version 3 supports WPA.

My desktop sees it as a wired ethernet connection with no problems.
