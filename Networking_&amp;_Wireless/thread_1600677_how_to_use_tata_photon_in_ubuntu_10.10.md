### Post by schauhan on 2011-02-10
A bit late for a reply but this might help others.

How to connect Tata Photon+ USB modem to internet.

Follow instructions in the booklet that came with modem. I threw away booklet ;). Found instructions here - last link at bottom of page.

[http://www.tataphoton.com/tata-photon-whiz-service.aspx](http://www.tataphoton.com/tata-photon-whiz-service.aspx)

Some posts mention that the modem is auto-detected in Ubuntu 10.10. It was not detected on my laptop. The device gets detected as modem if you go into file manager and 'eject' the storage device.

Then follow instructions above.
