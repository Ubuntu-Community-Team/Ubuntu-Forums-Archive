### Post by sidlinux on 2006-07-26
I'd really doubt if any of the new cards will work with dapper drake at this time.  I even have trouble with the 802.11g cards getting it to work using ndiswrapper and most of the time they freeze up my machine.  Thus, I would hold off on the 802.11n-draft (since they're still in draft status) until ndiswrapper comes up with a later version of the software that works with the 802.11n-draft cards.  Regarding the Belkin cards such as the 54g usb, it works if you use ndiswraper, but it freezes up my machine after a while.

I recommend you go to the ubuntu site and check their database to see which wireless drivers are supported.  I recommend you find one that runs right out of the box, that means it should work even with the live cd version of ubuntu 6.06.

Sidney
