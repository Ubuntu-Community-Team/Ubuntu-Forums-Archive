### Post by Iowan on 2009-09-27
I suppose time will tell, but I'm guessing that it will not. Sunlight may deteriorate the insulation, though. You are probably aware that CAT III cable is [designed to reliably carry data up to 10 Mbit/s]("http://www.actcabling.com/category-3-cable.htm"). Even if the access point and your ethernet card can handle 100 Mbit/s, you will need to convince them to back down to 10 Mbit/s (or use 100baseT4).
