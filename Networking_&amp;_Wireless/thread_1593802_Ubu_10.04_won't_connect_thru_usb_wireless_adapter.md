### Post by RealGomer on 2010-10-16
YAY!!!!! I installed the backport module and my wireless now works!!! The must be a bug in the 10.04 kernel or something. I posted this problem on Launchpad where another person had recommended loading the backport module. 
IF your having problems with the RT2870 wireless driver, install the backport module mentioned there. It worked for me.
