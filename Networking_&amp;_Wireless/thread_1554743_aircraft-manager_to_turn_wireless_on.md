### Post by Brandon Williams on 2010-08-18
aircraft-manager is for use on dell mini netbooks only. I'm not aware of any other machine types on which it works. It's not a terribly well written program (the version you installed is from my launchpad repository, but I only repackaged it ... I didn't write it), which is why it hangs on your system, rather than notifying you that it won't work.

You'll want to search for an enable/disable method that is specific to Atheros wireless cards and/or your notebook model.
