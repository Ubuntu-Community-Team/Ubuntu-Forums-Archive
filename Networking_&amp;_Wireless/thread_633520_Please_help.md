### Post by Original Brownster on 2007-12-06
Hi,
Generally speaking the netgear ones are fine, my friend has one with no problems. This model is for ADSL connections not for people on 'cable' like ntl, virgin etc.
As to working with your switch there should be no problem, as they are connected via bog standard ethernet. you plug the lan side into your switch 'uplink' socket and bingo.

Just make sure you know how your existing router is configured, ie is it configured as a dhcp server for your lan, username and passwords if it's adsl, stuff like that.
