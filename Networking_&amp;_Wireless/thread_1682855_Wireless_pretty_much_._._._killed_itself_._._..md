### Post by firebird_1979 on 2011-02-06
You might have been better off re-installing Ubuntu.

You can get lots of issues if you put a hard drive from 1 computer into another, unless they are of the same make/chipset etc.

Personally, I would put that hard drive back in the 1st computer, make a backup of your home folder (if you had any files on there), then put the drive in your 2nd computer, wipe everything and re-install from scratch.

Ubuntu will then detect all your hardware properly and configure it.

You can try to fix the situation as it is right now, but I don't know how you would fix your wireless, as both computers obviously use a different chip-set.

Sorry I'm not of more help...
