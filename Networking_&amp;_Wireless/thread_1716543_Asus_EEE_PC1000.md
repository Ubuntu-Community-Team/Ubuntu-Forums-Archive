### Post by patryk77 on 2011-03-31
Hey,

to make sure it is indeed Ubuntu you are running, first click on System, and you should see About Gnome and About Ubuntu.

Clicking "About Ubuntu" will tell you which version you have installed.

If it IS indeed Ubuntu, you can right click on the Network icon in the top-right, by the clock, and choose "Edit connections". You should be able to define or edit your connection in there, under the wireless tab.
