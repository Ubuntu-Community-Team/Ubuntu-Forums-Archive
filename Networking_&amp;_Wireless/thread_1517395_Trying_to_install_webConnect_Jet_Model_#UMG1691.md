Hopefully things worked out, but in case they did not:

I just purchased this unit and its accompanying service yesterday.  I popped it into my Cradlepointe CTR-350 router, expecting it to work like my previous Sierra Wireless 881U/AT&T.  No connection.  I plugged it into a USB port on the computer and made a connection through Network Manager and it worked like a charm.  Plugged it back in the router and it worked beautifully afterward.

One thing that I did notice is that the next day the unit would only connect to EDGE/2G and not 3G.  I am not sure if the webconnect program (which I never used) sends statistics/tracking information to T-Mobile or not, but I am almost positive that they sucked the speed away.  After talking with the billing people and their "tech support", the 3G speeds magically came back.

In summary, plug it in, ignore the webconnect icon that pops up, make a new GSM connection with Network Manager, follow its prompts and voila.  Any device issues try here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1443834&highlight=webconnect+jet](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1443834&highlight=webconnect+jet)
