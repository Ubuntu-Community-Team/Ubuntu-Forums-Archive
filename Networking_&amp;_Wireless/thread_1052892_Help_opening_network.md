### Post by Sam Lars on 2009-02-01
How are you connected to the internet?  If you're going through a wireless router, you would want to forward ports.  If you're plugging into a router, you'll also want to forward ports.  For help on this, see [portforward.com]("http://portforward.com").
If you're plugging directly into your modem, then you don't need to worry about that.
