### Post by brad1138 on 2009-02-20
I figured out something that may work. The last 3 reboots it has come back to my router. I was connected to my router, then I cleared all routers from the "edit wireless networks" area. It then asked again for my password, and then reconnected. Ever since it has connected directly to my router after booting. Don't know how long it will last, but so far so good.
