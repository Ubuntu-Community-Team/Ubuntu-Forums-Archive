### Post by esodin on 2010-05-28
I uncertain what your issues are but here are a few things you could try:
1. Yes, connect with a cable and run update. 
2. I take you can see the network icon in the upper taskbar: Right click on it and see if "Enable Wireless" is checked:
2a: If it is not check it and see if it works.
2b: If you are not able to check it (if it is grayed out) you have probably turned off wireless using a hardware key.  I am guessing you have a laptop.  Sometimes the hardware button for WL is among/by the F keys at the top of your keyboard; other times it is at the front of the laptop (towards you).

I have had trouble (on some laptops) turning the WL back on (physically) if this is the issue you could boot into Windows; check that it works and then boot back into Ubuntu.

If non of this works come back and post some more info about your hardware (brand and model of laptop)- it is easier to help you then.

Good luck!
