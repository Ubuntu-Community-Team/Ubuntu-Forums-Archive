### Post by jimbren on 2006-09-25
There's an embedded linux project that you might want to consider called [OpenWrt.]("www.openwrt.org")  It installs on a number of different wireless routers and allows you to do bandwidth throttling, vLANs to segregate your networks if you want, quotas, etc.  

Check it out--I think it might suit your needs.
