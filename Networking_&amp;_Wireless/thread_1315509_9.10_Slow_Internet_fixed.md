### Post by abulluck on 2009-11-05
I finally fixed my slow internet since my 9.10 install.

My symptoms were spotty internet performance on both wired and wireless.  Pages would take for ever to load and would load in spurts.

The solution (for me) was to disable ipv6 in grub.  Once I restarted, internet is now back to what it was before.

Hope this helps others beating their heads on their desks.
