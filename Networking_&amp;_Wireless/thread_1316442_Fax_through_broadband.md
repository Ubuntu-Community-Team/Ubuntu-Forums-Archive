### Post by efflandt on 2009-11-06
You cannot fax directly through broadband or cable unless you have VOIP and the VOIP provider lets you do that.  I have done dial up networking on cable TV phone service before that person had cable internet.

To fax through broadband you would need an account somewhere like efax.com where sending/receiving faxes is done through e-mail and they handle the landline end of it.  Or you could possibly do that tethered (usb or Bluetooth) through a cell phone or pda **with a data plan**.
