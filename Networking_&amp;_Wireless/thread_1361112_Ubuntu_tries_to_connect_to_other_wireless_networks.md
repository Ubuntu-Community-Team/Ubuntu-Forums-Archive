### Post by bhishan on 2009-12-21
When I turn on Ubuntu, it tries to connect to other wireless networks from my neighborhood. How can I make it to connect to my own wireless network as I turn on my computer?

---

### Post by hugmenot on 2009-12-21
Remove the unwanted connections using the connection editor (it&#8217;s in the right-click menu). NetworkManager won&#8217;t connect to random networks unless you specifically click on them.

---

### Post by sgosnell on 2009-12-21
Right-click on the network icon in the panel, select Edit Connections, click on the Wireless tab, select your network, and click on Edit on the right.  At the top left of the dialog that comes up, make sure the box for 'Connect automatically' is checked.
