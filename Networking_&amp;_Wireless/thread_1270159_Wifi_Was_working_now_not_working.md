### Post by Metaljaz on 2009-09-19
Try right clicking on your network icon and make sure enable networking and enable wireless are checked. Then left click on the same icon and make sure you have your connection selected.
You may also want to check to see if you have turned off your wireless by accident. I can turn mine off with the F2 key. Some others have a switch on the side or front.
