### Post by bobred on 2007-11-06
I figured out how t do it.

Connect to the Livebox with the ethernet cable. 

With the cable still connected click on the network icon and select the Livebox WiFi, enter the WPA key and it should connect.

Then disconnect the ethernet cable and you're away!
