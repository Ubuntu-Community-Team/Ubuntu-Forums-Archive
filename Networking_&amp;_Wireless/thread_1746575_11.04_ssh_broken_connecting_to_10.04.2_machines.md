### Post by bagpussnz on 2011-05-01
Hi,
I have just upgraded 2 machines to ubuntu 11.04. One was an upgrade, the other a new install.

On both machines, I cannot ssh to any machines running Ubuntu 10.04.2

I can ssh to machines running 10.04.1, 10.10, etc. and all those machines can connect to the 10.04.2 machines.

I get: Read from socket failed: Connection reset by peer

To workaround the problem, I have copied the /usr/bin/ssh on a 10.10 machine to the 11.04 machines.

Can anyway confirm this please and offer ideas.

### Post by christis on 2011-05-03
I had the same problem and followed your workaround. I found the older version of openssh-client @ [http://packages.ubuntu.com/maverick/i386/openssh-client/download](http://packages.ubuntu.com/maverick/i386/openssh-client/download) and replaced ssh in /usr/bin with the one in the archive.

The servers I connect to are running on RHEL 5.
