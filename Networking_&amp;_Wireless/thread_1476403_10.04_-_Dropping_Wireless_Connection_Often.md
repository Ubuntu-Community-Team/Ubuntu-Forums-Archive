### Post by jschille on 2010-05-07
EDIT: SOLVED
DO NOT INSTALL BACKPORTS! That was my problem.
```

sudo apt-get remove --purge linux-backports-modules-*
```
FIXED THE PROBLEM after removing and installing the drivers and restarting.
