### Post by Elaztic on 2009-10-19
What you are asking for is no problem.
All you need is a router. On your side (LAN) your router will give you as many internal IP addresses as you like (up to 65000) but on the outside (WAN) you have just one IP address.
In other words many computers in your LAN can share just one external (WAN) IP address.

This is really easy to set up and works across platform and vendors.
