### Post by grahammechanical on 2010-12-08
The red exclamation mark indicates that there is not a connection to the router/modem. This will appear whether the connection is through wireless or ethernet cable. Right click on the icon and remove the tick mark against Enable Networking by clicking on Enable Networking. Then, click on Enable Networking to Enable it again. This will have the affect of switching on networking whether it be wired or wireless.
