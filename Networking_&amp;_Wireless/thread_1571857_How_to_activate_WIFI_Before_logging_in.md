### Post by roy.nico on 2010-09-10
Hello Everybody

I have 2 PC : Big and Small. On both, there is Ubuntu Lucid. On Big, there is a WLAN PCI card from D-Link, and on Small an USB-Stick USB from TP-Link.

* On Big, the WLAN connection is performed during the boot, and is active even without opening a session (i.e. if one still on the login screnn of GDM)
* On Small, the WLAN connection is activated only AFTER opening a session.

My question : How can i do on Small to have the same behaviour as on Big ?
Thanks in advance

nico

---

### Post by skkeeper on 2010-09-10
In the Gnome Network Settings you can Tick a box that says "Available to all users", after entering the root password the network should connect on GDM. Make sure you tick "Connect automatically" too.
