### Post by rxlx on 2021-01-29
So after tailing the logs awhile, i noticed a lot of ipv6 addresses popping up, despite the fact i had it disabled in my service providers configuration, as well as my google router.

after enabling ipv6 in both locations, i can now access the internet and LAN. Not sure what to make of it but there you go.
