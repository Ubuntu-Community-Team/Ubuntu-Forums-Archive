### Post by NertSkull on 2010-10-01
Well I don't know what you mean by he is not at station now.

But if you want to share a folder you could just turn on SSH.  And then your brother could SSH in to that folder (i.e. use putty or something) and have access to it.

You could also move the contents to "the cloud" like ubuntu one or something so he could access them.

there are lots of ways to share folders depending on your need.  Samba, SSH, Internet-storage, VPN, free-nx, etc.  Any of those can potentially help you share a folder to varying degrees.
