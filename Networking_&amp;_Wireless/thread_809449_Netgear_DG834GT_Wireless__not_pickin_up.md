### Post by Kevbert on 2008-05-27
On the back of the router there's a small hole where the reset button is.  Providing you know the default settings (user id, password) push and hold the switch in for at least 10 seconds.  The router will reset to it's default settings.  You can now change these (preferably by using a network cable to your PC) opening a browser and entering [http://192.168.0.1](http://192.168.0.1)  The default settings for username is admin and password is password (for me at least).  No you can change any of your settings.
Hope this helps...
