### Post by rmcellig on 2014-04-29
I just got the Bell FIBE service from my ISP and was wondering how I can access my USB drive (ntfs) that I have attached to the router.

I'm using Xubuntu 14.04. As a note I have to literallly put in //mynetwork and not an ip address.

Here are the instructions for accessing from a Mac computer.

For Mac Users:
1. Connect a USB drive to the Connection Hub.
2. On your Mac, click on «Go» and then «Connect to Server»
3. Type smb://mynetwork under «Server Address:».
4. Click on Connect.
5. You will be asked for your Mac password. Please enter the password and click Connect.
6. A window will appear with the contents of your USB drive.
7. Until the USB storage device is ejected from the Connection Hub, you will be able to locate the USB storage device under «Computer».
8. To safely disconnect a USB storage device, you must first click the «Eject» button to protect the data.
