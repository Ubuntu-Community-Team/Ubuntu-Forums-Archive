### Post by changingstate on 2010-01-05
If you could find out what network devices you've got in the mac, it'd probably greatly expand the help people are able to offer. You can do this by typing 'lshw -c network' into a terminal. The text the command dumps out will tell everyone what wireless card is in the mac and thus how to go about getting a driver for you.
