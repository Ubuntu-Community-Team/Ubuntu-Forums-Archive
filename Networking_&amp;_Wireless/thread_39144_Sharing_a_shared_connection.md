Recently I buyed a router and installed it without a problem. The router shares a cable connection to two computers. Both PCs have static ip assigned by the router so my PC has ip 192.168.1.199 and the other PC has ip 192.168.1.198.

Now in my room I have an xbox and I want it to get to the Internet as well for online gaming. I could simply hook it up to the router but it's too far away from it and I'd to set up a cable from the router to the xbox, so it's out of the question. My PC has two nics so I though of using a crossover cable which I already have and somehow share the connection the router gives me.

I already setup a dhcp server and the xbox is getting dynamic IP but I dont know where to go from there. Tried with firestarter but with no luck. 

Any advice would be greatly appreciated.
