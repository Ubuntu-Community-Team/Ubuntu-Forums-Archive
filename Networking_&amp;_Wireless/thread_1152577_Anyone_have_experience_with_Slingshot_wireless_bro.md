From what I have read, Customer Service is virtually nonexistent.  Good luck getting someone to answer the toll free number or e-mails.  

I manged to get a CSR ONCE after seemingly eons of busy signal.
I asked if I could use other USB modems, but was told no.
I wonder if it would be possible to use a 3G/4G modem and assume the one they sell is 3G only but it is said to work on Verizon and Sprint towers.

It's a "virtual" carrier and they only resell the service but there's no credit check and no contract.  Pilot truck stops is one point of sale retailer. $250 gets you an Ovation MC727 USB Modem and 2 months of service @ $60 per month.  Supposedly 5Gb/Month @ $59.95 is the only data plan still offered, despite the lower plans shown on the website.

Thier website, [slingshot.com]("http://slingshot.com"), is a bit lackluster, with some links that point to "coming soon" pages and you find better info on forums but you can activate an E.S.N. there.

Like I wrote above, I wonder if I could activate another modem, like the Franklin U300 3G/4G usb modem.
