i tried to solve my problem during days..

later on, i found that, 
disabling "wless switch/button on the notebook" in bios solved the problem.
a setting in bios enables switch on/off function. but linux cannot get on the wless adapter.
to avoid this, disable that switch on bios, so that wless is always on...

that helped me... i used hp compaq nc8430 notebook...
