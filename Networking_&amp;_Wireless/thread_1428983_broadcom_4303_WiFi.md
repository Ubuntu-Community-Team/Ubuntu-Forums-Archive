### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-03-13
There's a guide for 9.10 for Broadcom including the one you have here:
[The Definitive 9.10 Broadcom Solution Guide]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1368699")

The AMD in the versions is more or less a misnomer in that it represents any system with a 64 bit processor. There is a brainstorm idea on the topic and I for one hope that they change the versions to prevent just this type of confusion.

So if that is the only reason you went with 8.04 I suggest you make the move to 9.10.

I personally don't own an exclusively B card but whether or not to upgrade it to a faster card is your call.

In any event you have the link to the guide to try and get your card working although it is directed to the 9.10 version of ubuntu.

Good luck
