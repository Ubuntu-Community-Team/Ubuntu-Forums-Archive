From a Newegg review: "you will need the rtl8192ce driver from the Realtek website if it is not built in to your kernel." Use lsmod to see if rtl8192ce is being used.

Do you have the N42 or the N45? The Newegg reviews for the N42 gave the unit a very low rating, and the N45 was just a little better. Reviews from Linux users were all over the map.
