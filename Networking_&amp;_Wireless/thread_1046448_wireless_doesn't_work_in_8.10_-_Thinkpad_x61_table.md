### Post by utnubuuser on 2009-01-21
I've been using Intrepid Ibex on an X31, (which crapped out a couple days ago), and it ran it perfectly, - better than the original XP.

I've seen lots of posts about difficulties with Intrepid Ibex.  I think everyone tends to forget that Intrepid is actually considered to be in the Debian "experimental/sid" category.

In my experience, it ran better than Hardy Heron, but something I've noticed about Ubuntu, (and other distros), is that performance varies from hardware to hardware.
Feisty Fawn ran very well on the X31, but Gutsy Gibbon was sluggish (though on other hardware Gutsy was prefered).  Hardy Heron started out poorly, but improved within a month or two. Intrepid - perfect.

Good luck with the configuration.
