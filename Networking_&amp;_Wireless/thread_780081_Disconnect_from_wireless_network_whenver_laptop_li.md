Any workarounds so that the network remains connected even when the laptop lid is closed?

### Post by d_chall on 2009-07-28
If you go into power management and change the "when laptop lid is closed" to "do nothing", that should get around the problem for you.  The monitor may stay lit up when it's closed, but often you can change the bios settings to shut it off completely when closed, which is far better than just a blank screen anyway and doesn't cause the network problems.
