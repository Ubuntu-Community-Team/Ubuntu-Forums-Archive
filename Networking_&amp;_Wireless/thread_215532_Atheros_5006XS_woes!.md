Now with the new atheros I get one bar 16-19% and 48mbps. It's driving me crazy!

This was one of my pulling points for me completely switching to Ubuntu for every day usage, and just keeping windows for gaming. But now, in windows with the Atheros, I get 108mbps and 80% connection compared to the dismal 18% in ubuntu. What gives? Any suggestions?
