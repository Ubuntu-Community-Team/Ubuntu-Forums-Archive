### Post by mjwood0 on 2005-08-08
Just a suggestion...

Most DSL modems have a USB interface and an ethernet interface.  I stopped using the USB interface and switched over to the ethernet interface.

I have a DSL modem plugged into the Linksys wireless router.  From there, all my machines either plug into the router via cable or use the wireless.  Much easier than sharing the DSL connection!

Just a thought...
