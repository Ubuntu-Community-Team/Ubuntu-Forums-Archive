### Post by baza41 on 2007-02-17
OK Livebox users, I expect you've noticed that 'liveprinter' option, well here's how to get it working. Use cups, [http://localhost:631](http://localhost:631) then put in these setting, changing it for your printer.

Description: liveprinter
Location:
Make and Model: HP DeskJet 3740 Foomatic/hpijs (recommended)

Device URI: socket://192.168.1.1:9100
