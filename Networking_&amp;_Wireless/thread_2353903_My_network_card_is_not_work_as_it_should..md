### Post by r.stiltskin on 2017-02-26
I haven't had this problem, but a quick search shows that many people have had trouble with that Realtek RTL8821AE.  If you want to experiment you can try building and installing the rtlwifi_new driver described [here]("https://medium.com/@elmaxx/rtl8821ae-wifi-drivers-in-ubuntu-16-04-4c1286524afa#.lwecdvdmn").  But it would probably be easier to buy an inexpensive usb wifi adapter.  I was just looking at a couple of recent threads about adapters that work well with Linux:
