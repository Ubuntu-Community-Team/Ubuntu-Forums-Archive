### Post by czaarek99 on 2015-06-07
Well, to begin with I have tried Kubuntu before trying to Ubuntu Gnome. That didn't work at all and had many problems with the touchpad and working really bad. It also had extreme scaling problems for the UHD model.
So my recommendation is to pick Gnome over any distro because it handles scaling perfectly.

To fix the keyboard problems with repeating keystrokes just update the bios to A4.

But I still can't get the wifi to work properly even if I install the right drivers. Right now I am using a small usb dongle and the wifi is very fast. So obviously this is a driver problem.

If you are dualbooting with windows you can fix audio with the information from this bug report:
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/1413446](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/1413446)

The most frustrating thing though, is the the first comment on this site:
[https://major.io/2015/02/03/linux-support-dell-xps-13-9343-2015-model/](https://major.io/2015/02/03/linux-support-dell-xps-13-9343-2015-model/)
There is a guy reporting he got wifi to work out of the box... I am guessing they just ship them with a random wireless card?
