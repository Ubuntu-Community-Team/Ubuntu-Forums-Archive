### Post by endersshadow on 2007-05-28
I would assume the answer is yes to your question.

However, I must warn you, I got a WRT300N and all I had was problems.  Connectivity issues up the wazoo...the router kept resetting itself, dropping connections, and blocking all traffic.  Spent about an hour with it, took it back to the store and got the [Belkin F5D7231-4](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16833314008) and have not yet regretted that decision.  My advice is to stay far, far away from the WRT300N.
