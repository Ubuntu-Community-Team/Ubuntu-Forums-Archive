### Post by tturrisi on 2009-04-19
The linksys wrt54g v5 is their junk model.  It has known issues because it has reduced RAM and reduced chip size.  Definitely disable router logging.  That said, it can be made to work well using dd-wrt mini but don't install it unless you do the necessary research and be prepared to get a new router in the event it gets bricked.

Are you connecting by wire or wifi?  You MUST MUST connect by wire to setup the router, then endable the router wifi and security.  After it has been setup, then & only then connect by wifi.  The wifi is OFF by default.
