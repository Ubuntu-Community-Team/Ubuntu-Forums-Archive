### Post by Lambert on 2005-11-29
A card with an atheros chipset works well.

[http://linux-wless.passys.nl/query_chipset.php?chipset=Atheros&zoek=chipset](http://linux-wless.passys.nl/query_chipset.php?chipset=Atheros&zoek=chipset)

Stay away from broadcom.

If you find a card you can look it up [here]("http://linux-wless.passys.nl/") to see how well it's supported in linux.


For a connection manager there are 3 apps

IN the repository is network-manager and wifiradar. (wifi radar has not been that highly recommended by some)

There is also a project called gtkwifi. The tab "projects" at the top of the page will get you a link to it.
