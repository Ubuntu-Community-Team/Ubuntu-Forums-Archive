### Post by bobbob1016 on 2009-09-25
Just got Uverse myself.  Portforwarding on it is a little tricky, I've done it on a bunch of routers, but this was tricky since they want to do more of an automatic route, I had the IP already, but it wanted to go by name.

Anyways, I digress:
[http://portforward.com/english/routers/port_forwarding/2wire/3800HGV-B/3800HGV-Bindex.htm](http://portforward.com/english/routers/port_forwarding/2wire/3800HGV-B/3800HGV-Bindex.htm)
Select the Torrent client you are going to use, if it isn't there, follow the basic steps, and look for "port" in your client.  Then simply enable the forwarding, and you speeds should go up.  You should also follow torrent etiqute, as in [http://infinite-source.de/az/az-calc.html](http://infinite-source.de/az/az-calc.html)

Your upload speed in kbps, mine is 1.5mbps so I used 1500kbps.  Also, it'll say put 0 for max download which sounds good, but could be bad in practice, I think I did 11mbps for my 12mbps connection.
