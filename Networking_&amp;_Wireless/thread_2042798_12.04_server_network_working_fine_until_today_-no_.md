### Post by chili555 on 2012-08-15
The usual suspect is the ethernet driver. Check:```
sudo lshw -C network
```Is a driver shown? Post the result and we'll help.

EDIT: Mrs. Chili says I am taking her to lunch! I'd search for your device and find out what the driver is. Did it load?```
lsmod | grep <somedriver>
```Did it just fail to connect? Try again:```
sudo ifdown eth0 && sudo ifup eth0
```Are there any interesting messages?```
dmesg | grep -e eth0 -e <somedriver>
