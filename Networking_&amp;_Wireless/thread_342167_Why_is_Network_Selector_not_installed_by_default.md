### Post by spd106 on 2007-01-19
Assuming you mean Network Manager, try [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NetworkMagic](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NetworkMagic)

As I understand it there were a few problems with some wifi cards and the lack of static address management. So wasn't a full replacement for network-admin, especially as most home users don't have WPA. I think NM is due to be included soon, though it isn't in the daily builds yet. Chances are it won't hit default install until NM hits v0.7 or at least is stable enough.
