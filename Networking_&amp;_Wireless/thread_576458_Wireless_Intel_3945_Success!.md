Guys,

After two weeks of struggling with my dell inspiron 640m, ive finally figured out my wireless problems. I've been jumping from distro to distro trying to find one which works successfully with my darn Intel 3945 wireless chipset. i've been using linux over the last few years on and off, but as a mcsa sometimes its hard to move away from the dark side.

To all those users with the 3945 Chipset, who successfully connect to their wpa networks the first time but with limited success after, upgrade your access point firmware.
It was something i never considered, as it always seemed like a driver/software problem.Im a qualified network engineer and have never seen this before.

Ive been trying opensuse 10.2, 10.3, pclinuxos 2007, ubuntu 7.04/7.10, kubuntu with knetworkmanager, 
Im now using Ubuntu 7.10rc, and cant be any happier.

Any questions, ill be more than happy to help

Ill let you know if i have any more troubles tomorrow, god i hope not.
