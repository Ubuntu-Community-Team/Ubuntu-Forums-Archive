### Post by Petro Dawg on 2012-08-31
You may have already checked this but make sure the batteries are good, and try to use a USB port closest to the mouse.  Also, some surfaces do not work well with a laser mouse, try it on a mouse pad, or piece of paper to be sure its not the surface.

I tend to only run into issues like what you describe when my batteries are dying.
