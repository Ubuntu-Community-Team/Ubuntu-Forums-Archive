Maybe you could try running the ubuntu box for 12 + hours with the vista box shut off and see if the disconnect happens. Otherwise you could check if changing the 802.11 channels helps and also try T-shooting with the encryption (e.g. try with it in WEP or even disabled). As well as finding a live cd that works well with your hardware and really just try to eliminate the coincidences. Then report back with more info, and I will try to help.
