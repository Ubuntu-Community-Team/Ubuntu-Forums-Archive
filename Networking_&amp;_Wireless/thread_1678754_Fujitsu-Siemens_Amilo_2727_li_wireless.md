This is the second Amilo Ive had to hard hack the wireless so I thought I would post a link on how to do it.

The notebook boots with wireless turned OFF (US air travel regulation I believe) and the softkeys fn+f1 to turn it on do NOT work in ubuntu.

This can be cured easily and very simply using a screwdriver and small piece of masking tape to cover the kill signal pin.
