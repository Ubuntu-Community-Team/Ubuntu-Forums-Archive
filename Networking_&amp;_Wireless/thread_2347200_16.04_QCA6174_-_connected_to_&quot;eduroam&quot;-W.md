### Post by niklas-komani on 2017-07-04
This might be a little late for you but this gets found by Google so..

I had exactly the same problem but under Arch Linux, I found that a combination of the newest firmware for QCA6174 (>= 4.4.1) from [https://github.com/kvalo/ath10k-firmware](https://github.com/kvalo/ath10k-firmware) and installing [http://pptpclient.sourceforge.net/](http://pptpclient.sourceforge.net/) (there is an Arch Linux package for this called pptpclient, no idea about Ubuntu) makes Eduroam work.
