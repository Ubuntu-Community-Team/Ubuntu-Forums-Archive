### Post by DancingSun on 2005-08-29
Ok...so sounds like you might have 2 GPUs?  One on a graphics card and another integrated on the motherboard?

Can you hear any beeps during startup? Usually upon post, the computer will beep once.  Is the HDD activity LED blinking at all?

If you answered no to both of these questions, congratulations!  You know have an excuse to buy a new computer!

My previous computer behaved similarly.  Whenever I did a full shutdown (not restart), then power on the system again, it would likely "die" on me.  I had to slap and shake the case a bit, and if I was lucky it would boot up again.  I could not determine the root cause of the problem, as I found out that my motherboard became defective and could not reliably operate with more than 1 stick of memory installed and that the power supply was also faulty.

I have since built a new computer.
