### Post by Iowan on 2010-02-21
A site like [no-ip.com]("http://www.no-ip.com/") or [dyndns.com]("https://www.dyndns.com/account/entrance/") should work. You will also need to set up port-forwarding on your router, and decide how you intend to connect (SSH, etc).
