### Post by binbash on 2009-03-17
Ok.I have 3 notebooks.1 has connected to the modem with ethernet.Left click to wireless icon at 1, create a new wireless network, make it wep, put password etc.Then others can connect it :D that is so easy.HOWEVER at some distros (fedora 10) and maybe at ubuntu you may need to edit that connection (at pc 1, ONLY at PC 1) and change IPV4 method to automatic (DHCP Adresses only).
