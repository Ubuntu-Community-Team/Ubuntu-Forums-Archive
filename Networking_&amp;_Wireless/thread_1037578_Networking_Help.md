### Post by Iowan on 2009-01-14
Besides the CLI (Command Line Interface) from a terminal, my version of Ubuntu  also has **ping** available under System>Administration>Network Tools>Ping. Under XP (on my version), it's under Start>Run>Cmd - then it's like Ubuntu's CLI. You'll need to know the IP address of the machine you want to **ping** - in case ping-by-name doesn't work.
