### Post by Retro_Trinity on 2015-10-26

Under 15.04, it would function reasonably well on the 2.4 GHz band, but it would constantly time out when connecting at 5 GHz.  Now under 15.10, it will not detect the 5 GHz network at all, and will constantly time out on 2.4 GHz instead.  Here is the result of the wireless-info script.  Note that it appears to be using the wrong kernel driver! That is, 8821ae instead of 8812ae.

The rtl8812ae.conf file is an attempt at fixing the problem that did not work.
