### Post by Bucky Ball on 2010-06-13
Hi all,

I worked on and off for months on this problem. Internet dropping out when it felt like it, generally when it is idle for awhile (sometimes two minutes, sometimes thirty). 

My solution? I installed wicd and that was that. Problem solved. Not a drop out in 48 hours and connection rock solid at 92%.

If you are using Network Manager and having these drop out problems, I strongly advise trying wicd, or for atheros perhaps try madwifi, and see if that fixes your problem. I used the instructions here from 'Installing Wicd in Ubuntu':

[http://wicd.sourceforge.net/download.php](http://wicd.sourceforge.net/download.php)

Using Ubuntu 8.04 LTS with an Intel Pro/Wireless abg card but this should work for all. Just make the changes advised. Good luck.
