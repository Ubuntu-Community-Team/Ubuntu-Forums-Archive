I have a Broadcom 4312 wireless chip. I have use the STA drivers that Ubuntu recommends. I can connect quite easily just by clicking on the icon, so this is more than a niggle than a problem.

My network isn't protected as we live in the middle of nowhere (quite literally) and network theft isn't a problem. ### Post by schmutztaucher on 2010-01-05
System > Preferences > Network Connections > Wireless tab > Click your SSID > Edit > click the Connect automatically box
