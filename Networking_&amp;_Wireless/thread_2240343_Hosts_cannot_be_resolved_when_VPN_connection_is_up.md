### Post by grzechol on 2014-08-20
I eventually found a solution at [http://askubuntu.com/questions/320921/having-dns-issues-when-connected-to-a-vpn-in-ubuntu-13-04](http://askubuntu.com/questions/320921/having-dns-issues-when-connected-to-a-vpn-in-ubuntu-13-04)

I've commented out line "dns=dnsmasq" in /etc/NetworkManager/NetworkManager.conf - that solved my problem.
