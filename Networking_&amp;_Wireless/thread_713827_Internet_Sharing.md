### Post by sillyxone on 2008-03-03
I would suggest buying a USB wireless (look like USB thumb drive), that would free the laptop from being the relaying service. Those wireless dongle is just about $5-10, but they offer flexibility.

If the desktop is at a place where the signal is to weak to reach, you can try a long USB cable so that you can place the USB wireless dongle where reception is good.

of course, if you are setting up an Ubuntu box to relay wireless from a far-away point, just ignore me :-)
