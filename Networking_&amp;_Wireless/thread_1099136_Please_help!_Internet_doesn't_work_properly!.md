### Post by WA_Garrett on 2009-03-17
I had a similar problem on my mac book not too long ago. I was using 8.04 and I compiled the ATH9K drivers for my wireless card. It didn't work, I could detect wireless networks, but I couldn't join them, everything on the OS X side worked. Finally I got rid of the ATH9K drivers and I just used MadWiFi and that works fine. 

I don't know if you had to compile drivers on you computer or not for wireless like I did, but you might consider looking around and seeing if there is something else you can compile for your wireless card.
