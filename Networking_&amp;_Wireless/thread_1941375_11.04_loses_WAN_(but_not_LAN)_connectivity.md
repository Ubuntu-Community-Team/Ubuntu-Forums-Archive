ping [www.apple.com](www.apple.com)    --(from Mac OS system on the LAN) produces normal output
ping 192.168.168.103 --(from 11.04; IP=Mac OS computer on the LAN) produces normal output
my usual way of communicating with the 11.04 system, ssh from Mac OS, continues to operate normally
11.04 Chromium Browser just spins the mouse pointer when attempting to contact [www.google.com](www.google.com)

Ideas or suggestions for diagnosing?
