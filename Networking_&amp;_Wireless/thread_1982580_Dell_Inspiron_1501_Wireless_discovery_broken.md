### Post by wildmanne39 on 2012-05-27
Hi, welcome to the forum! it looks like you are missing the firmware for your wireless.

Please open a terminal ctrl+alt+t copy and paste the following commands into it one line at a time, then paste the results here:

```
cat /etc/lsb-release; uname -a
lspci -nnk | grep -iA2 net
iwconfig
rfkill list all
lsmod
```
by clicking on new reply and click # and paste the information between the brackets.
Thank you
