I'm no expert on dial up im afraid :( however 'no carrier' means no dial tone ie your modem or the isp you dialled has disconnected.

Can you confirm that the dial up settings you have (phone number, account, username and password) are correct, have you used them before in Windows for example?

run 'pon -d' in another terminal.
