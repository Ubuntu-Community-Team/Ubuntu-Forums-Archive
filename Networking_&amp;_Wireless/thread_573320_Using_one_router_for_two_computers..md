### Post by Xenaco on 2007-10-11
I would either get a new router with more ports or a switch to connect to the existing router. It's just a matter of price.  Any RJ45 patch cable will work.  CAT5 or CAT5e will work fine for 10/100 Mps ethernet.  CAT6 and CAT6a is for 1000 Mps network connections but is backward compatible with slower connections.
