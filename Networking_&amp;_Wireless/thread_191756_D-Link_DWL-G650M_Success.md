### Post by mbobak on 2006-06-07
Ok, I just picked up a D-Link DWL-G650M.  It's working via ndiswrapper, nearly out of the box.

I installed ndiswrapper and ndis-gtk.  Then, I ran ndis-gtk, inserted the D-Link CD that came with the card, and loaded the driver.  No problem, it loaded just fine.

I configured the card with iwconfig to set the essid and then ifconfig to set IP, netmask, etc, and finally set a default route, and voila!, I'm surfing wirelessly.

So, for the record, this card works great w/ ndiswrapper.
