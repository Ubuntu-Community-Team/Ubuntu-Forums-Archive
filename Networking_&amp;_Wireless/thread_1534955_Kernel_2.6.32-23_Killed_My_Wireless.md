### Post by Linux_Zoo on 2010-07-20
A quick note for other frustrated Ubuntu users out there. I have a D-Link DWA-510 PCI wireless card (RT61) that just stopped working. 

After wasting hours searching posts and asking for help, I've determined that there's a problem in the latest Kernel to be rolled out.

I have now installed StartUp-Manager, and used it to boot into the old 2.6.31-22 kernel still on the machine and, hey presto, wireless is working again.

Let me know if this helps you out as well. It seems like the simplest fix by far.
