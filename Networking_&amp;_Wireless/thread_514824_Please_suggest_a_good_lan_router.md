### Post by buzzmandt on 2007-08-01
I have a linksys 4port wireless router, Can't remember the price, purchased at wal-mart.  For the most part there is nothing to do but hook it up, set the computers to auto-dns and you're done.  I've not had to configure it at all, it was ready to work out of the box.
On it I have 2 dual boot, with xp, and linux (works from the start with ubuntu, redhat, pclinuxos, opensuse, mandriva...these are the ones I have run, it's worked on all of them), also have hp laptop w/xp and a nintendo Wii, the wireless set up auto on the Wii, and often have a xbox360 wired, no problems
