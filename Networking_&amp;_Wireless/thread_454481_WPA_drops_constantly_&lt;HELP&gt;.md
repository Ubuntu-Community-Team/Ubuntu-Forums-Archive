### Post by ugm6hr on 2007-05-26
Mine did that too.  I searched everywhere, and discovered it was something to do with my Atheros5005G wireless card and Network Manager.  What is not clear is which is at fault.  Options were either try something else instead of Network Manager or use WEP.  I went for the latter (MAC & WEP 128bit).  I know it's not ideal, but my network speeds are higher than with WPA, and no dropped signals.
