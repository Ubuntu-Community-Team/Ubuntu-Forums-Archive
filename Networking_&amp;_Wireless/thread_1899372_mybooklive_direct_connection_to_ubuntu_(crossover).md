### Post by retrodans on 2011-12-23
So, 5 minutes after posting, I try some random things, and now it works.  All posts I had previously read said to use the 'local sharing' option, so I changed this to 'link-local only', and now I am in, and accessing my files like a pro.

Hope this helps others trying to solve similar issues.  I do love it when I beat technology, gives me a false illusion that I am cleverer.
