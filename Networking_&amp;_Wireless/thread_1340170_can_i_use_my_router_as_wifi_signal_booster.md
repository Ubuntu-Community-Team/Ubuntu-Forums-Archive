### Post by oldsoundguy on 2009-11-28
If you mean to access another router WITH YOUR router, the answer is NO.
There ARE repeaters available, but you have to have access to the primary Wi-Fi router in order to be able to program the repeater.

Using a Cantenna can help boost your signal strength,  but it is an "iffy" proposition at best.

Each iteration of 802.11 got stronger in signal strength.  B was stronger than A .. G was stronger than B and N is now stronger than G . Range was also increased with each improvement. (the N really did not increase range that much, but strength DID improve (because of an improved signal to noise ratio) as well as speed.)
