### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-04-25
When it gets to the "Enter key" box, click on the drop-down box and try the WEP 128bit passphrase instead.

I have found in the past that to use your password instead of the long alphanumeric encrypted version, you must use that 128 bit passphrase.  I am, of course, assuming you entered your more easily remembered password you chose in the box and not the long encrypted alphanumeric that I spoke of.
