### Post by mpokrywka on 2007-11-01
Wireless G should be enough for streaming all media EXCEPT maybe HD video. But see [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HD_DVD#HD_DVD_.2F_Blu-ray_disc_comparison](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HD_DVD#HD_DVD_.2F_Blu-ray_disc_comparison)
There is some info about video bandwidth (AVC or VC-1 at 13 Mbit/s + max 6mbit for audio) - seems that about 20mbit is enough for one client. Of course maximum bitrate (48mbit for blueray) is hard to archieve on Wifi-G, so you have to calculate your usage, and if you have some HD content then you could recompress it for lower bitrate.
