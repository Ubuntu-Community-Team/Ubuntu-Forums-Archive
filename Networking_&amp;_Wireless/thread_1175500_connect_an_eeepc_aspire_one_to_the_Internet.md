### Post by dandnsmith on 2009-06-02
I understand the problem, having thought of these options myself.

If you can find a serial modem on an SD card, or connecting via USB, you could do it. However the only usb connected modems I've seen are ADSL modems, and if you get a serial modem and try to connect it via a USB/serial connector you're likely to find it won't work (based on experience gleaned from friends who've tried to use serial devices via a converter).

Good luck in your search.
