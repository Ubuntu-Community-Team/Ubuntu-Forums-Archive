### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-10-10
Did you close Ktorrent? 

Try to close the port in your router before closing it. You can do that through the UPnP tab in the bottom of the interface. Click the "Undo Forward" button to close the port. This will prevent other peers from contacting  your machine.

I would also advise to reduce the upload speed, since it can clog your network.
