[http://packages.ubuntu.com/](http://packages.ubuntu.com/)
lists all of the releases, such as intrepid and jaunty.
There is also a search box for names or contents.

For example, in your case, typing "gnome ppp" gives one package in Jaunty and selecting that gives the list of dependencies (18 of them).  You can then check each one of the dependencies and get those that you don't have.

At the bottom is the link to the file and the size, 82.1kB for i386.  Select that and then you choose the mirror and the download begins quickly.
