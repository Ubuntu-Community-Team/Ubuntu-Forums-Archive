My problem: My internet download speeds are agonizingly slow. In every application, from Amarok to Firefox, my download speed is at a crawl. ### Post by gradedcheese on 2007-03-07
Usually turning off IPv6 is a good place to start.  You can turn it off in Firefox and also globally in Ubuntu.  Search the forums for how and give that a shot.

---

### Post by unquenchablefire on 2007-03-11
Thank you very much. I got rid of IPv6 and it helped a *ton*. I'm not sure if i'm at the same speed as i would be using Windows, but i'm back to DSL speeds, and i'm not going to need to boot into winows as often anymore. It seems a lot of other users have had problems with this too, and turning it off helped a lot. Maybe IPv6 should be removed by default, or there should be a sticky thread?
