### Post by Macchi on 2009-01-17

PS: Unfortunately there have been compatibility issues with wireless acccess points and certain wifi cards in the Network Manager. By the way, this type of problem can be observed in Vista as well, and probably has its origin in the incredibly different ways that a standard can be interpreted and implemented by every different equipment manufacturer. But normally it works fine.
