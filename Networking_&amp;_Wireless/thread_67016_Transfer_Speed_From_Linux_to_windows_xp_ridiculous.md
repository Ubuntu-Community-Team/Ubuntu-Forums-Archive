### Post by OwenH on 2005-09-19
Right, I'm new to Linux but have plenty of experience of messing with little domestic networks ....

For a start even running at a networks top wack of 100 megabits a sec a 700 meg file will take about a minute to transfer.

Modems/adsl (ie via msn messenger) runs alot slower. 
mine is a 1 megabit a sec so that would be a increase of x100 for file transfer times.

so PC -> PC over a network - quick
    PC->PC over the internet - alot slower.


if the speed has slowed down between the 2 PCs on the network your network card might be running at 10 mbits/sec rather then 100, in which case it might be a driver or config change needed?

the speed will also depend on exactly how you are transfering the files.

Good Luck!
