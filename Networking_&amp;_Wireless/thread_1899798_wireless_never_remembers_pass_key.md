### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-12-24
Try this.

[LIST=1]
[*]Click on the network icon in your panel (or open the *Network Connections* application).
[*]Select the *Wireless* tab.
[*]Select your wireless entry and press *Edit*.
[*]Select the *Wireless Security* tab.
[*]Enter your password.
[*]If appropriate, check the box *Available to all users*.
[*]Save; Close.
[/LIST]
Log out and in again to check whether or not it worked.
