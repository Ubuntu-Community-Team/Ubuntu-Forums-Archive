### Post by Donshyoku on 2006-06-21
Not to insult you, but just to be sure...

I had the same problem with WEP in my house.  I typed in ASCII (that is letters) and it did not work.  The same network typed in Hex (CAPITAL letters and numbers) worked for me.

It is easy to make the mistake of typing in the password in letters when selecting Hex.  Sign into your router, look at the security page, and enter the Hex characters instead of the written password you set.

I am not sure if this is a bug or not, but it worked for me...
