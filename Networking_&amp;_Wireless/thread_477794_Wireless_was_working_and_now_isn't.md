### Post by Antonio44 on 2007-06-18
Ok this happened to me as well and the following solution fixed it for me. I hope that it does the same for you.


It was really simple. When you go into system-> administration-> Network you will see your connections. 

Now I was told that you cannot have a wired and wireless running at the same time. 

So check to see if next to the wired connection there is a check. If there is uncheck it.

Then uncheck and check the wireless and it should give you that little loading bar that says configuring

just to be on the safe side reboot

Then for me that was that...

I hope that this may have helped if not I hope that someone out there can help you.
