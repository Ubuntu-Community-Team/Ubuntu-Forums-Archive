### Post by inobe on 2010-01-22
plug your lan to the routers wan port, you will get a limited or no connectivity warning, ignore this......

enable ics in windows, make sure your free lan is selected for use with ics, 

once the setup is complete the limited connectivity warning will go away.


the downside is network and printer sharing isn't possible.
