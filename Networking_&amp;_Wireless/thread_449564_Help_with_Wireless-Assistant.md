### Post by wastedfluid on 2007-05-20
For anyone who is having the same problem - It's something about 128bit encryption.  For my setup, it would not accept a 128bit encryption.  I downloaded KNetworkManager, and switched my encryption to 64-bit on the router - and it worked fine.
