### Post by SeijiSensei on 2010-09-18
KDE supports URLs of the form smb://server/share.  So in Dolphin, just activate the address bar by clicking in the location bar about the folder display and type the appropriate URL.  You'll be prompted for your Windows username and password if one is required.
