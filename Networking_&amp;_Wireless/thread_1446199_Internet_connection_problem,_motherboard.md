Found the motherboard software at Emachine under BIOS. Downloaded it to flashdrive, unpacked in Windows. Then In the Run dialog box, typed: C:\Cabs\D00221-001-002\Setup.exe. Click OK.

Now under networks I see LAN driver and it is working.
