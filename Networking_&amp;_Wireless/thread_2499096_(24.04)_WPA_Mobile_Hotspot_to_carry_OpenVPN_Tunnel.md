### Post by currentshaft on 2024-07-12
The ICMP messages are basically saying, port 30001 on 10.42.1.38 is not available - since the device is listening on 192.168.153.11.

Since the device cannot connect to the VPN itself, I think you basically have the following options:
- share the VPN connection over the wireless network, or
- forward the unreachable ports through the wireless gateway

The latter is much easier and I would try that first. Something like:

```

iptables -t nat -A PREROUTING -p udp --dport 20000 -j DNAT --to-destination 192.168.153.11:20000

iptables -A FORWARD -p udp -d 192.168.153.11 --dport 20000 -j ACCEPT

```
