Hi

I'm running Ubuntu v10 on my Toshiba Satellite Pro A400 laptop. Two windows XP SP3 laptops also in the household. All networked via D-Link DSL-2640R wireless router/AP. Pretty standard stuff and all working fine.

Now, I'd like to add a NAS box. My **strict** requirements that I do not wish to compromise on are viz:

1. Must have torrent client built in.
2. Must be able to power-down/power-save when not in use (not in use defined as: Not downloading torrent files and not being accessed by any of the windows/ubuntu clients. Access/polling by the router does not count as a client access - i.e. I do not want the router to keep the power up on the NAS)
3. Must have web interface
4. Must be cheap/budget but reliable.
5. 1TB capacity or greater

Ideally, but not essential, features would be:

a. wireless
b. include disks(s)

Not bothered at all about:

IDE or SATA
Appearance (will be under a stair cupboard)
Noise (as above)
Heat (as above)

thats about it.

I'd very much appreciate links to products please that you either have reviewed or used. No speculative or theoretical views please - thank you - just direct links together with your assessment of the product as you have used it and found it to be.
