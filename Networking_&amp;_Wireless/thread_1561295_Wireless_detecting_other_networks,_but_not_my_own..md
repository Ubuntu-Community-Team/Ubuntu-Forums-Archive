### Post by spjackson on 2010-08-26
Some wireless routers can be configured not to broadcast the SSID, which could explain why you cannot see it. Some only permit new connections when you perform some action on the router to permit them. For example, there may be a button to press on the router to permit new connections for the next few minutes. (This is something I have forgotten about several times.)

Of course, the problem may have nothing to do with this.
