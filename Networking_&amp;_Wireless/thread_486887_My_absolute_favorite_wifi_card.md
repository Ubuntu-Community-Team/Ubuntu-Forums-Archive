I plugged it into my Linux box just to screw around (not thinking it would work). and low and behold the lights came up, and i got the wireless networks detected box. This card was working, and under Linux, and for $5... AMAZING.

Turns out it had the prismGT FullMAC chipset which is supposed to be very good. Also turns out that it has a 200mw radio which is "Very Nice".
