### Post by tonyps on 2009-08-15
Afternoon,

Spend the $39.99 and buy a Netgear USB 2.0 Wireless G adapter, Model WG111v3.!! After spending the past two days and part of the evenings attempting get a Linksys dual band USB adapter to work, reading and learning much, much more than I really wanted to at this point, about modules, builds, versions....I took it back this morning.
The store had one of the NetGear WG111v3 left. According to the notes and reviews in the hardware list on this site, this one is recommended.
It worked, first time, no hassles, no working with modules or builds...plug it in, enter your security info (WEP for me) and it goes...
best of luck!
