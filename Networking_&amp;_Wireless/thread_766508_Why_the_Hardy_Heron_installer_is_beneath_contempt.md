### Post by GeneralSunTzu on 2008-04-25
After fighting with the wireless connection, working perfectly on my MacBook Pro with GG, thanks to madwifi (not the latest), here is why the HH installer is pathetic:

1. its limited intelligence prevents it from realising that there are proprietary drivers (in my case ATI) being used; screen and xorg configuration promptly screwed up as a result;
2. ditto for the screen resolution; even assuming that not everybody can be as smart as the Knoppix installer, able to understand the HW characteristic of every single box on the planet, the HH installer should be able to extract the resolution from the current configuration;
3. finally, seldom I have seen in my professional career a piece of software worse maintained than the network manager applet, which I would scrap outright and replace with wicd; said NM managed to thoroughly screw up my wireless configuration, without, again, considering what to do with it.

To those who, like me, thought HH would have been a major improvement, remains, alas, little hope...
