### Post by darkMarko on 2011-03-24
Hi houshdaran.
I must admit that i dont know much about the fine settings for a internet connection but here is how i solved my problems.
First of do you use a USB modem or a ethernet one? If your modem supports a ethernet connection use it and not USB.USB tends to be..a problem.Ethernet just works.Second when installing a new Ubuntu ALWAYS leave your internet turned on and plugged into your computer.Ubuntu needs to configure it while installing.The third option would be to check your settings.Is eth0 selected or is lo selected when u left click the 2 blinking computers near the clock? If you are using a ethernet connection make sure eth0 is selected.Also under System> Administration> Network make sure that wired connection is set to Automatic Configuration(DHCP).
I hope this helps.
