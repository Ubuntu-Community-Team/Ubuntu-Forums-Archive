### Post by deadflowr on 2013-10-24
Are you downloading it from the software center or somewhere else?

If you are fetching it from Ubuntu, then maybe try switching servers.

Open the dash(the top icon in the left side panel)
Type or search for "Software and Updates".
Open it and look for the part that says Download from.
Next to it is a dropdown bar, click on it and choose other.
In other click on the button next to, or under(me forgets) find best server.
This will run tests to see which server is best for where you are.
It may take a minute.
When it's done, click ok and then see if that makes a difference.
