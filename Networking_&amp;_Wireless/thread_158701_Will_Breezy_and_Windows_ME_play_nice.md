### Post by LoclynGrey on 2006-04-11
U should be able to connect a 10/100 switch to your router and then both pcs to that switch. Providing the router is set to access the web you should be away.

Hate to say it but Win ME and networking never seem to work. But connecting s above may give your dads ME machine internet connectvity but not access to other machines on the network. (Win ME was never really good for anything...)
From each machine you may be able to ping the other machine using the command line and also ping the router.
