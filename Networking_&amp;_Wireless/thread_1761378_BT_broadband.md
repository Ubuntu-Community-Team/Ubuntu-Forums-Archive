### Post by wilbyforce on 2011-05-18
In short, BT screwed up the firmware for the home hub so Linux won't connect wirelessly. There are various workarounds detailed on here and on the BT forum, none of which worked for me so I complained to BT. They called me last night and assured me that they were working on a fix for the firmware that will be available "very soon". We shall see.
