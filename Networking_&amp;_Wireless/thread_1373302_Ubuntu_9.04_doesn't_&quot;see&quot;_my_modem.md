### Post by pdc on 2010-01-07
plug in your modem to your computer 

type 

> lsusb

into a terminal and 


1) copy and paste the results back here

(the top line of the terminal has an EDIT menu, and you can find a copy command there)



2) your modem should be described by two sets of numbers:

Vendor ID and Product ID;

if you enter those into google;

such as 

> ubuntu 12d1:1003

you should get some suggestions to follow up on, yourself

(the above Vendor ID and Product ID is for a Huawei E220 modem
