### Post by papangul on 2009-11-06
I don't think anyone will answer you because this is a tricky issue. For example I have a linksys card that works out of the box in ubuntu, but the latest version(4.2) of the card refuses to do so. 
And if a card works in the current version even then there is no gaurantee that it will work with the next release of ubuntu. 

Out of the three you have mentioned the tplink one appears to be the (relatively speaking) best. But I take no responsibility in the case you buy it and it doesn't work!

You might want to take a look here(if you don't have already seen it): [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported)
