### Post by Bucky Ball on 2008-09-13
I set this up just tonight with a Windoze box. Maybe of some help. Imagine you would set up the Mac as per normal for sharing.

[http://jngalloway.wordpress.com/2008/06/08/easy-file-sharing-in-ubuntu-no-editing-text-files/](http://jngalloway.wordpress.com/2008/06/08/easy-file-sharing-in-ubuntu-no-editing-text-files/)

Rather than sharing a drive, you create a share folder and plop the files you want to share in there. The folder appears on both machines and you're away. Hopefully! Took a bit of tweaking but no biggie.
