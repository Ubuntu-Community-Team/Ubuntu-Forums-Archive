### Post by lawentzel on 2008-02-15
The information you provided.  Is that coming from your computer or the printer?  Your printer should have it's own IP address.  Typically you can get that from the menu settings on most networked printers.  I would start there.  If your printer isn't showing an IP then it isn't being assigned one by your router.  Which means, one of a couple of things.  The cable could be bad.  The printer's connection bad.  The router's port could be bad.  Or the printer isn't asking for an IP address.  Hope this helps you narrow were the problem lies.
