### Post by sitedesign on 2006-08-07
I use Intel gigabit NIC's in our servers or Broadcom (TG3 or BG driver) Work great in Ubuntu and deliver the full gigabit speed.

Steer clear of realtek as I have tried several which seem OK but fail to deliver the speed.

I have also seen complaints about the speeds with SMC cards

NForce chipsets are well supported as well and people have reported to get good speeds with them.

Hope that helps.
