### Post by p_quarles on 2007-06-23
Assuming that your laptop is running NTFS, you'll need to install ntfs-config.

To see the laptop's disk from your desktop, you should be able to enter the LAN IP address of the laptop into your file manager. This may require setting up Samba, though.
