### Post by qalimas on 2007-03-15
I was recently shopping for a wireless card for my notebook.  I have an HP Pavilion ze4800, and it has a Broadcom 4306 in it, which I have NEVER been able to get working right.

I went to Office Depot, and got my hands on a Netgear for $50.  It works out of the box, as in when I boot with the Edgy LIVE CD all I have to do is click Konqueror and boom internet works, I didn't even have to chose an access point =)

The model is WG511T.

I highly recommend this card! =D
