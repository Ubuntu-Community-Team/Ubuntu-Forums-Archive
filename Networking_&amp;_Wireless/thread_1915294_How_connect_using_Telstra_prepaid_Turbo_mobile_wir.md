### Post by blackdalek on 2012-01-26
Apparently the way to fix it is to NOT choose "any" under the "type:" drop down selection. Instead choose "3G (UMTS/HSPA)".
Had to disable then enable "mobile wireless" several times from the Network Manager applet, then it would connect to Telstra NextG network no problems.

After disconnecting, looked like it would not connect again.
Then tried disabling/enabling mobile wireless a few times and suddenly it worked again.
