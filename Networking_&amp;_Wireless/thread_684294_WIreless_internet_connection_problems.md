### Post by OldLinuxGuy on 2008-01-31
Are you on a desktop or laptop and some details about the network card would help.

From my own experience I would suggest using a cable if possible at first that will verify that all the rest of the networking hardware and software is working.  For the most part if you use DHCP and you should it will configure itself.

Then Google the card for all it's worth get a specific as possible as some of the cards have different hardware for the same model.  More than likely someone has figured it out for the card already and you just follow their instructions.  Also check out NDIS Wrapper it may be needed.
