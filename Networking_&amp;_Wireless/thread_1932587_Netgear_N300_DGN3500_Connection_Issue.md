Do you mean that you set WEP after having no security? Or that you downgraded from WPA to WEP?

Please be aware that WEP stops only the most casual broadband theft, as it can be cracked in a couple of minutes. If you can use WPA, or even better WPA2, with a complex passphrase, please do so.
