### Post by cblah001 on 2010-05-03
Problem solved way easier than I thought. Connect computer to internet via ethernet cable. Once connected go to System>Administration>Hardware Drivers>Activate (and download) The Drivers listed (mine were Broadcom wireless drivers)>CONNECTED!

Thanks for all of your help Me.

I hope this helps any other newbs to Ubuntu that may have been experiencing the same problem as me.
