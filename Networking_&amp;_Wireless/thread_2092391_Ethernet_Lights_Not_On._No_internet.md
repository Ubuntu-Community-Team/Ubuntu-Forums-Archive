### Post by Cheesehead on 2012-12-07
First, check for a hardware problem. Cables can be pinched or gnawed, hardware can die.

Does the ethernet cable work between a different router port and a different machine?
Does the router port work with a different cable and differetn machine?
Does the computer port work with a different cable?

Once you have successfully proven a good router port and good cable, then it's time to look for less-common solutions.
