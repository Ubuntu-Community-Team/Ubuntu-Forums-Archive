### Post by RDV on 2008-03-15
I have an eeePC (701 4G). No model of eeePC comes with an installed internal dial-up modem. There is a modem jack with a rubber insert on the left side of the device beside the Ethernet  connector (RJ-45 jack). Your friend may be mistaking the RJ-45 jack for the dial-up modem jack.

As to an external dial-up modem I cannot help.
