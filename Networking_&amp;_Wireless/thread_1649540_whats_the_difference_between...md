### Post by Boondoklife on 2010-12-20
adsl = Asymmetric Digital Subscriber Line; This means that the upload and download speeds are different.

With that said, almost all residential connections are of the Asymmetric type.

If by ethernet modem you mean a cable connection, then the difference would be that ADSL is normally a PPPoE connection while cable tends to offer a connection that is like a LAN. DSL uses PPPoE (Point to Point Protocol over Ethernet), this sends encapsulated data over the ethernet connection.

Hope that is what you were looking for.
