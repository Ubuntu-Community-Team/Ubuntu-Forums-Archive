### Post by MattBD on 2008-05-25
I've Googled it and apparently it works OK in Linux. If it doesn't work out of the box, you could try the Vodafone Mobile Connect card driver for Linux, available at [https://forge.vodafonebetavine.net/frs/?group_id=12](https://forge.vodafonebetavine.net/frs/?group_id=12) which I believe works OK for a wide range of devices, and isn't limited to supporting Vodafone. You'll probably need to do a Google search or two to get the settings for the APN, username and password, and you'll need another way to get online in order to resolve the dependencies, but if you have to use that you should be OK.
