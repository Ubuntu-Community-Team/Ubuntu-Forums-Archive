Hi. I've had a very similar problem when I installed Ubuntu 7.04 and trying to connect with a WLAN. I'm sort-of connected now, and I'm sure it won't take long to get it working properly. Have a look at my thread and maybe you might find something there to help you, or talk to dmfield who has been helping me.

My thread: [**Problem connecting to Internet**]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=497287")

Since then, I have had to try and make sure it is detecting the network and check that the interfaces are setup properly.

I wish you the best of luck getting your connection working.
