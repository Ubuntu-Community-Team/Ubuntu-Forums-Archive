### Post by williejones on 2009-11-18
I am using Ndiswrapper on both 9.10 and testing Lucid 10.04.  I load Ndiswrapper from my disk by using Synaptic Package Manager, enabling CDRom, searching for Ndiswrapper.

Mark the 3 packages the search finds, apply , install.  Then go to System, Administration, Windows wireless Drivers to add the .inf driver.

Works for me with Netgear Wn111v2 wireless USB adapter.
