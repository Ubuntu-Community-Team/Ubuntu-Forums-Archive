### Post by scbingham on 2013-08-30
Hi David

I would suggest shorting out the switch by soldering a wire across the contacts. Unfortunately that means your wifi will be permanently on, reducing battery power but at least it will get you working again. Maybe even soldering on a couple of wires to another miniature switch?
