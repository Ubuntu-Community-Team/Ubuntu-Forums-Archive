### Post by Xog on 2010-01-21
I don't understand what your problem is. Are you asking where to put the IP, Gateway, mask, etc?

On the top right of the screen, right click on the Network Manager icon and click Edit Connections.
Click on the Wireless tab.
Click Edit.
Click on the IPv4 Settings
Change the Method to Manual.
Click Add.

Then double-click in each field and enter the information.
