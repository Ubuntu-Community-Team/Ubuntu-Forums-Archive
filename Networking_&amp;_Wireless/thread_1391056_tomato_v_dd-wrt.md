### Post by Lars Noodén on 2010-01-27
> **gridd said:**
> hi I want to put tomato or dd-wrt on a Linksys 54GL behind a vigor 100  

You'll probably get the most useful answer about the distros tomato and dd-wrt asking on one of their user forums like the Broadband/DSL Reports Forums, LinksysInfo Forums, or OpenLinksys Forums.  While some at the Ubuntu forum might be into those, too, the focus is on Ubuntu and that is too big to fit into a DrayTek Vigor 100 ADSL modem.


As far as the shell goes, one reason for 'fear' is that one vendor without the shell has spent a lot of time and resources disparaging it as the 'command line'.  You might have an easier time of it call it the shell and almost certainly will have an easier time learning if it is approached as scripting or programming.
