For simply sharing the same internet connection with a bunch of PC's it would look like this.

MODEM - ROUTER - SWITCH - PC1, PC2, PC3.

You can still have PCs connected to your routers built in switch/hub too.

To allow all your PC's to share the same connection it is important that the Router is connected directly to the modem.
