### Post by Jonked on 2008-08-31
Phew. Have fixed it (not easy, over the phone with someone who's not great with computers). 

It turned out to be actually very simple - for some reason the updates and reboot had caused roaming mode to turn off. I now recall that setting the essid etc manually doesn't work for his card, but it had reverted to the manual settings. 

Annoying that installing updates should do this sort of thing - best way of scaring off the novice user.
