The "Public Switched Telephone Network" is not connected to the internet.  The internet (IP networks) are different from circut switched (PSTN) networks.  They can (and do) use the same physical wiring to home or business (last mile), but that is all.

Typically PSTN handsets are analog while VOIP (internet phone) are digital.
