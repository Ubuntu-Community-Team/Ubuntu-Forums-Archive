I've successfully set up my shared printer, however in trying to access my shared drive, my wife's Windows Vista computer keeps saying she does not have rights to it.  The shared drive is an NTFS external hard drive, I shared the /media/Storage mount point and from my Ubuntu laptop I can access the share just fine and modify files and whatever.  From her machine, I cannot.  I have the share settings set wide open and there's no firewall in place on the server. I've checked the option for "Guest Access", made sure that her password was the same on her windows client and the Ubuntu server just to be safe, and everything, and I can't get her into the shared drive.  She can print to the printer just fine though.
