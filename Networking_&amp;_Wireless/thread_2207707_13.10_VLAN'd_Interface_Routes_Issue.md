### Post by Rsxhawk on 2014-02-27
To carry multi-vlan traffic to one host, its switch port (on the HP switch) must be configured as a trunk and each vlan must be allowed on that port.  I'm not sure how you even got that "kind of" working to begin with, but I would start there for sure.
