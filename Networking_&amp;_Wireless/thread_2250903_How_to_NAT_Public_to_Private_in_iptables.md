### Post by Doug S on 2014-11-05
Without the entire context of both your iptables and what you are trying to do, it is hard to know what to suggest. Based on my current understanding, here is my suggestion:```
iptables -t nat -A PREROUTING -i eth1 --dport 1625 -j DNAT --to 10.64.1.45:1625
iptables -t nat -A POSTROUTING -o eth1 -j SNAT --to 203.13.xx.xx

iptables -A FORWARD -i eth1 -o eth0 -m state --state RELATED,ESTABLISHED -j ACCEPT
iptables -A FORWARD -i eth1 -o eth0 --dport 1625 -d 10.64.1.45 -m state --state NEW -j ACCEPT
iptables -A FORWARD -i eth0 -o eth1 -j ACCEPT
```
