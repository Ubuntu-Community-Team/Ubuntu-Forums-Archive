### Post by sunayshah on 2010-01-05
The following just worked for me on 9.10 Netbook Remix:

1.) Go to Accessories
2.) Click "Passwords and Encryption Keys"
3.) Right Click on "Passwords: default"
4.) Select "Change Password"
5.) Enter your current password into "Old password" and leave the rest blank
6.) Hit Change
7.) Proceed with the Unsafe Storage option - I'm not really sure what this means but I just went with it.

You should be good to go.
