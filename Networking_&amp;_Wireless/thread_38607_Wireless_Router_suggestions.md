### Post by SNo0py on 2005-06-01
I have two Linksys WRT54G and I'm really happy with them - the original Firmware runs and runs and runs.... no problem at all!

btw, because someone mentioned the WRT54GS above - don't by the "S"-version unless you also have a "S"-enabled card from Linksys - otherwise the router will just work with 54MBit and not with the "Speed-Update-Thing"...
