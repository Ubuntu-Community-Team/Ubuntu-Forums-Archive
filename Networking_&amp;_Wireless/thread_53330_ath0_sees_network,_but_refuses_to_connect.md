I was on the internet just yesterday when all of a sudden I was kicked off at the same time my two little bro's were. My card sees the network, but I can't conect to it. EDIT: I'm back, and I seem to have figured out the problem. The card I was using was a D-Link DWL-G520 (the old 2003 version with the blue sticker on it, not the green) and apperently it doesn't really like simple WPA encryption key's (in our case, hardman5) so we set up the router so that it's using a regular 128-bit WEP key instead so now we are not only more secure, but I am actually able to post this without getting a little sloppy. I should have named the card earlyer too. We went out and bought two brand new cards to test it, and I even reformatted my hard drive just to humor my dad before we actually figured this out.
