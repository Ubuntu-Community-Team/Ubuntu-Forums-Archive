### Post by zaivala on 2011-07-05
I have this card. It has worked with 8.10, 9.04, and 9.10 with a single, solid light. From 10.04 on, it runs at super (108 Mbps) speed, with both lights flashing together slowly -- but 10.04 and later do not run well on my old machine, as it only has 512 MB RAM maximum and a 1.0 GHz Athlon CPU.

I also note that Puppy and its variants (such as MacPup 5.11 and 5.20) operate quite well (but of course with fewer available programs), with both lights flashing together slowly.

Most of the online documentation I have found did not correctly say what the various LED statuses are. LED1 solid means connected, 56 Mbps; LED1 & LED2 blinking slowly means connected, 108 Mbps. If LED1 is blinking, you do not have a connection.
