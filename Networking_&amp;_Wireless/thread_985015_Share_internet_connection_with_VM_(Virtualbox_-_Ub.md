### Post by wadelewis4 on 2008-11-19
In virtualbox, before you click "start" to load the winXP vm, click "Settings". Click on the "Network" label in the left pane and configure the type of connection you want in the right pane. It may be set at "Not Attached". 

Hope that helps.
