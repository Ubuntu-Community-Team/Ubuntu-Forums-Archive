### Post by cipherboy_loc on 2011-01-07
> **whoisatlas said:**
> Is it physically possible to have two or more wireless cards/ adapters, and use the combination of the two along with two or more connected networks to enhance internet speed?


Is it physically possible to shove two wireless adapters and two wired ethernet adapters in the same box? Sure. But is it possible for them to work together... Maybe. If you wanted to wget the latest Ubuntu LiveDVD with one adapter and use another for web browsing, etc, you might be able to, but if you are on the same network, you might not see that much of a difference. 

Now, if you got four separate ethernet packages, set up a wireless router on two of them, and directly connected the other two to your box, you would see a increase in performance. How much? Depends on the bandwidth of:

1) Wireless card to Router.
2) Router to outside world.


Now ask, is it worth it. ;) For some who have a Intel i7 processor, 32GB of RAM, etc, with a couple of servers running, yes it is. Others, like me, it is not.
