Hi there,

dmesg, lspci and iwconfig are all commands to be entered in the Terminal. The output of these commands will be displayed in the terminal and supply you with relevant information to your computers hardware. Please start by posting the output of these files and someone will be able to better assist you. 

I do not believe you need to re-install your OS, however it may be possible your wireless network card is not supported or linux drivers for your wireless card did not come packages with the Ubuntu distro you are using. Please post as many details as you can about your problem such as:

-wireless card model
-Distro version(distribution version)
