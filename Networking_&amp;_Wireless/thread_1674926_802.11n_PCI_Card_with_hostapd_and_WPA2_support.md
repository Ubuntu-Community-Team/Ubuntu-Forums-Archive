### Post by cancrusher on 2011-02-07
I went with a Mini PCI-E to PCI-E adapter and picked up a cheap AR9280 laptop card.  Just got the card from Hong Kong today, popped it in the adapter and it's working great with ath9k from debian kernel 2.6.32-5 and hostapd 0.6.10.  The antenna leads on the adapter were a bit tricky to get on the card and have the metal shield fit over it.  Picked everything up off ebay for about $20.
