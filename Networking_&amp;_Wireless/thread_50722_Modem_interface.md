### Post by az on 2005-07-21
Conexant modems are not supported by linux with a free and open driver.  The company wants you to pay again.  The driver is available for 15 USD.  Google for it.  There is a free version but that only does 14.4KPS.

You probably can get a pcmcia modem on ebay that works with linux for less than that.
