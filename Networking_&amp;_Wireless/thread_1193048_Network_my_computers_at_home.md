Provided you've got samba installed and running, and it should be so by default, you just change the box's workgroup name to that of the windows boxes, make shares with the share tool in Nautilus, restart Samba and browse to the other computers in Nautilus. Very windows-like.

Oh and turn off the Linux firewall pro tem for diagnostic purposes (if you installed one).
