### Post by jumpslu7 on 2007-11-17
To answer my own question..

editing /etc/udev/rules.d/70-persistent-net.rules to set eth2 and 3 to eth0 and 1, then removing the dodgy entries for eth0 and 1, followed by a reboot solved the issue.
