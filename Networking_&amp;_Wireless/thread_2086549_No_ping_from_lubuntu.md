If you want to analyse this problem, I'd start by creating a LiveCD or LiveUSB for Lubuntu 12.04 and see if your computer behaves the same way.

If this runs OK, then its something to do with your install, or maybe additional packages or modifications that you have made after install.

If it doesn't run OK, I'd try connecting your laptop direct to your Ubuntu computer using a network cross-over cable. This will check for anything odd on your network/router.

Finally I'd suggest you read my notes here: [http://captainbodgit.blogspot.co.uk/2012/10/networking-with-lubuntu-win7-and.html](http://captainbodgit.blogspot.co.uk/2012/10/networking-with-lubuntu-win7-and.html)
...as Lubuntu needs additional packages for full networking.
