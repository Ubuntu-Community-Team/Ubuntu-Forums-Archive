### Post by chewearn on 2010-02-04
Yes, changing channel would change the frequency.  But not as much as you might think.

Channel 1 is about 2.41GHz, going up to channel 11 at about 2.46GHz.  They are still in 2.4GHz range.

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:2.4_GHz_Wi-Fi_channels_(802.11b,g_WLAN).svg]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:2.4_GHz_Wi-Fi_channels_%28802.11b,g_WLAN%29.svg")
