### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-05-10
Easy enough. :)

Open Synaptic (System > Administration > Synaptic) and from there go to **Edit > Mark Packages by Task...**
From there, check LAMP server. Click OK, then Mark, then Apply to get a shiny new LAMP server in minutes. :)
