### Post by icheyne on 2009-01-28
To share the printer in Ubuntu, try going to: [http://localhost:631](http://localhost:631) and setting up printer sharing there.

In Ubuntu 8.04, it's easy to change the keyboard layout:

[LIST=1]
[*]Click System > Preferences > Keyboard.
[*]Click the "Layouts" tab.
[*]Click the "Add..." button.
[*]Select the appropriate country from the "Layouts" drop-down list and click "Add".
[*]Click "Close".
[/LIST]
