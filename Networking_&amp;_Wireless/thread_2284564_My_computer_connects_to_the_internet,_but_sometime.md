### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-06-30
Some routers have a log file that you can examine to see what the errors look like from the router's end.  The long ping times could be a hardware problem or wireless interference.  

How many wireless networks do you see when you click on your wireless icon?  If you have an Android phone, install WIFI Analyzer and look at the wireless channels that are being used.  You might need to change the channel of your router to one less busy.  If it is set to "Auto" channel, you might turn that off and set a specific channel.

If you are playing a game that relies on a continuous internet connection, you might consider getting a long ethernet cable and connecting directly.  Wireless is too intermittent for game use.
