### Post by fredsnet on 2008-01-28
LTSP cLients use their own ram and video card or onboard video. They use the servers programs and files and data storage.  Thats why your client doesn't need a hard drive.  

Reminder - many video cards also use system ram - so some of your clients depending on how heavy your graphics usage may need a liitle more ram than the recommended min.
