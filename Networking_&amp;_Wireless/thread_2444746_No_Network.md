### Post by grahammechanical on 2020-06-03
I think that the question mark symbol indicates that Ubuntu is connected to the router but the router is not connected to the Internet Service Provider.

Assuming Ubuntu 18.04, open System Settings. Open WiFi dialog and you should see a list of visible networks including your own one. If the WiFi adapter is connected to the router and the router is connected to the ISP then your WiFi network will have a check mark against it.

For ethernet connection open the Network dialog.  That will tell if your ethernet adapter is connected to the internet. The cable will either be listed as plugged in or unplugged. It may also be switched off in Ubuntu.
