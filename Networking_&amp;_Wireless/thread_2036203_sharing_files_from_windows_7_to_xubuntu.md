Unfortunately, Xubuntu (Xfce) doesn't have that functionality with its default file browser. To connect from Windows to a Xubuntu share, have a look at [these]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=11384263&postcount=3") instructions to get you started.

To connect to a Windows share from Xubuntu, have a look a the gigolo application.
