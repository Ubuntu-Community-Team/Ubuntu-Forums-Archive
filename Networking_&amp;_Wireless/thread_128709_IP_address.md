Hi,

if you are connecting to the internet directly with your PC, you can use the command "ifconfig" to see all your active network devices and their IP addresses.

### Post by heimo on 2006-02-12
Alternative method: Add network monitor to panel, click on it and select Support tab. There's your current ip.

To add Network monitor, right click on panel (the area where buttons and menus are located), choose "Add to Panel...", under "System & Hardware" double click on "Network Monitor".
