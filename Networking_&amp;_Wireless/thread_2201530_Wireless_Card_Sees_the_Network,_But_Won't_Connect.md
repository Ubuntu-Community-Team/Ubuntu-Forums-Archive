### Post by Quantos on 2014-01-24
I replaced the old wireless NIC in my pc with a DLink DWA-G520.  At first the card wasn't recognized, so I edited my blacklist.conf file and remarked out the prism54 line.  Now the card is recognized fine, and it sees my wireless network, but it won't connect to it.  I have checked that both are using WPA-Personal and that the encryption key is valid, but it still won't connect.


<edit> I solved it, the antenae wasn't making true contact, I reseated it and it's working fine now.  </edit>
