### Post by dmaris on 2009-01-21
I have been working for several months to get Ubuntu 8.04 connected to my wired network. I never got it working on Ubuntu and I tried another distribution.  I then discovered with this new distribution that the network PCI card on eth0 was returning an error, Device or resource busy.  this was caused by the BIOS settings for the HP Pavilion, 6630 in my case. I rebooted, F1 to get into Setup and changed some of the 'Advance Settings" as follows: Installed O/S: [Other] from Windows, Large Disk Access: [Other] and Legacy USB Support [Disabled].  The network card works and I also have my wireless adapator working.

I had a post on this last November 2008 and I wanted to pass this information to others.  I cannot find those post. Thanks for everyone's help and I hope this is useful
