### Post by rogblake on 2008-02-16
If you are using "internet connection sharing" on your Windows system, that computer is operating as a router. You would just connect its ethernet port to a hub or switch, and connect your other computers to the same hub/switch. You could use the 4-port switch built into your router for this, just don't connect anything to the WAN/internet port.
