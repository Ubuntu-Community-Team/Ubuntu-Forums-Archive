### Post by CaptSaltyJack on 2012-04-26
I have a System76 laptop with a Realtek RTL8188CE wifi card in it. I was getting really slow network downstream rates (under 1Mbps), and it occurred to me to change my router's channel from 8 to 11. This fixed it and I'm now back to 12Mbps+ speeds.

Why would this happen?

---

### Post by chili555 on 2012-04-26
In my experience, it's because there is interference on your selected channel; in your case 8, from microwaves, light dimmers, cordless telephones and all manner of RF devices even if they're your neighbors. In many cases changing to a different channel fixes speed or disconnect problems, no matter the chipset manufacturer.
