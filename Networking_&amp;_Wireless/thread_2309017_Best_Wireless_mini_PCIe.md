### Post by Jerry_Gorland on 2016-01-08
Personally I bought an HP Stream 11 a while back with a custom HP M.2 Wifi card (30x22mm).  It has a Realtek RTL8723BE on it.  The laptop has only one antenna so things didn't work out until the cable was moved to the other position.  Others have had similar results.  Check out:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2304607&page=6](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2304607&page=6).  With the build of the new driver, seems like a solid little box now.  So, a card with an RTL8723BE may be an option.  Seems that Realtek have engineers who try to work with the Linux community.  However, if your BIOS whitelists cards, then it may not work at all from the get go.  You just can get your Intel to work?
