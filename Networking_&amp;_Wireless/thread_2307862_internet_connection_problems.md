### Post by Hadaka on 2016-01-25
hi, open a terminal ctrl/alt/t then copy and past one line at a time.
```
echo "nameserver 127.0.0.1" | sudo tee /etc/resolvconf/resolv.conf.d/original
echo "nameserver 127.0.0.1" | sudo tee /etc/resolvconf/resolv.conf.d/tail
echo "nameserver 127.0.0.1" | sudo tee -a /etc/resolvconf/resolv.conf.d/head
echo -e "nameserver 127.0.0.1\n8.8.8.8\n8.8.4.4" | sudo tee /etc/resolvconf/resolv.conf.d/base
```
boot,test wireless
thanks.
