### Post by drjonze on 2008-06-19
I have an Atheros 5005g and it works just fine. It was a little tricky to get going at first. I had to connect by wire, remove the security on my router. I then connected by wireless and turned the security back on. I was able to re-connect and it been working flawless ever since.

I'm using an Acer Aspire 9300 with and AMD64 processor. I have Ubuntu 8.04 (64 bit).
