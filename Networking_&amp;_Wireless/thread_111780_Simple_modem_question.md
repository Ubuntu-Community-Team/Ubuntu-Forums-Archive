hi ryy705,

I haven't heard of any serial COM port external hard drive modems that have problems with Ubuntu. I use a SwannSmart Turbo serial 56k.
The only types of modems that tend to have problems with Ubuntu are internal winmodems, as it can be difficult to find drivers to make them work. But external serial modems, no probs.
