It sounds a bit like you don't understand the concept. You run software on your machine (in this case openvpn) to encrypt your communications, which are sent to a server in another country usually, decrypted there, and passed on to the internet. The pages coming back reverse the process. So, your ISP or anyone in an internet cafe or on any kind of open wireless hotspot cannot monitor what porn you are downloading.  :)
