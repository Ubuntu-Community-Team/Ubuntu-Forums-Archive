### Post by cariboo on 2008-09-09
The easiest way is to on your Ubuntu computer go to System-->Preferences-->Remote Desktop and set the options you want and enable it. Then on your windows computer download and install one of the vnc variants, RealVnc or TightVnc. IF you want to do it the other way around you will have to install a vncserver on windows.
