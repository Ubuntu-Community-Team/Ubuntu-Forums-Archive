### Post by kevdog on 2007-09-19
Someone gave me this laptop today -- a travelmate 220 -- not that I was excited about that since I really think its a POS IMO.  It has had this PCMCIA cisco wireless card -- never seen one of those in best buy.  Did a fresh install of Ubuntu Feisty since windows 2K took like 20 minutes to startup. 

Anyway to my surprise -- this was the exciting part for me -- was that the wireless card worked "out of the box".  Ive been doing this a long time, and I really thought "out of the box" was a fantasy, but wow -- I cant believe it!!!  It has the  Atheros AR5212 802.11abg NIC and uses the madwifi restricted driver - ath_pci.  It is listed as the only Cisco card at madwifi.

Anyway, I guess I surprise myself sometimes -- out of the box does exist in feisty.  In the worst case scenario I guess I could just trash the laptop and keep the wireless card.  

Sorry to bother everyone with my surprise!!!
