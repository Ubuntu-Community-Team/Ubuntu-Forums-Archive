### Post by chele on 2011-06-29
Where I am at the moment we connect via HughesNet. the modem connects to the computer via ethernet, so the OS is irrelevant. Older Hughesnet modems did not connect via ethernet, so may not work. 

10 years ago we connected using one of those modems, I can't remember right now if I got it working with Linux or not ...

Anyway, the service is mediocre. Lot's of congestion during the day, daily download limits that if exceeded cut your connection speed to dial up for 24 hours, and their DNS is flaky at best.
