### Post by Toz on 2011-12-12
Its a program that lets you remotely connect to another computer using ssh, vnc or rdp protocols. Wikipedia is good at explaining these:

- ssh = [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secure_Shell]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secure_Shell")
- vnc = [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vnc]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vnc")
- rdp = [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Remote_Desktop_Protocol]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Remote_Desktop_Protocol")

The vinagre homepage is here: [http://projects.gnome.org/vinagre/]("http://projects.gnome.org/vinagre/")
