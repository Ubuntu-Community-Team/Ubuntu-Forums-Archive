### Post by chili555 on 2010-05-19
Please see: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1435755](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1435755)

I actually believe the correct way is this:```
GRUB_DEFAULT=0
GRUB_HIDDEN_TIMEOUT=0
GRUB_HIDDEN_TIMEOUT_QUIET=true
GRUB_TIMEOUT="10"
GRUB_DISTRIBUTOR=`lsb_release -i -s 2> /dev/null || echo Debian`
GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX_DEFAULT="quiet splash" 
GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX="acpi_enforce_resources=lax"

```Please be very sure to run:```
sudo update-grub
```
