### Post by Paddy Landau on 2013-02-19
I found the solution entirely by accident!

System Settings > Network > Wireless > was turned off.

I don't know why I couldn't turn it on from the usual place.

The little wireless light also works now, albeit not as I expected: blue when idle, amber when in use.

I'll marking the thread as solved.
