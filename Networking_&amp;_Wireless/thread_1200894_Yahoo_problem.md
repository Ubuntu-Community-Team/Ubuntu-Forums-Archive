### Post by drs305 on 2009-06-30
There have been many users lately (including me) that have had yahoo connection problems lately in Pidgin.

Many of us were able to connect after changing one of the settings.
Pidgin: Accounts > Manage Accts > Choose your yahoo account > Advanced

Change "Pager server" to: 
cn.scs.msg.yahoo.com
