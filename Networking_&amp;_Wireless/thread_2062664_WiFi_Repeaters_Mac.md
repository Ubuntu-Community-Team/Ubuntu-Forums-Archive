### Post by windscape on 2012-09-25
Hi,

Wireless repeaters are different than wireless access points and routers. Repeaters will NOT create another wireless network that your devices will change between. It will extend the range of your existing wireless network. The repeater on that page looks pretty nice.

An alternative to getting a repeater might be to move your router to the highest point in your house, that tends to extend the range a little bit. Also make sure that any antennas on the router are pointed upwards and that there is little metal nearby the router, as that can impede a 2.4 GHz WiFi signal.

I hope this helps.
