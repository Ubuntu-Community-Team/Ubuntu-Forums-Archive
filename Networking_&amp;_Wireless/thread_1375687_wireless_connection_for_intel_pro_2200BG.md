Oh, but I think it is. :wink:

The Intel 2200BG wireless chipset should work out of the box with Karmic (9.10). Or at least it does on my old laptop with the 2200BG wireless.

On the top panel, near the right, probably next to the volume control icon, you'll find the network manager applet icon. Click on it. Do you see any wireless networks listed? If yes, then it's working. Can you see yours? If yes, click on it and enter your passkey where prompted.
