### Post by wilee-nilee on 2010-05-08
The noscript addon in FF has a abe (application boundary enforcer) for redirects control. When I try the Blogger.com I get sent there. I also have FF set up to remove all cookies on closure, no 3rd party cookies, this is a history problem to much of it still in FF. A virus is not going to send you to Wot, besides no known viri anyway. Yes your computer could be cracked, but it would take root access.
