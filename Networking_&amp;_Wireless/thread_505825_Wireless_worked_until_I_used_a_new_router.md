I'll be nice....I have used many a wireless card in many a flavor of linux and the drivers are not all created equal.  Meaning some encryption standards are not available, or speed capabilities.  In short try resetting the router to no security, and see what happens.  
if that is what your doing, check to see if the wireless supports multiple speeds and wireless ethernet standards A, B, G...most likely just B and G standards!  Hope this helps:guitar:
