### Post by sdgreen on 2007-08-12
Running Fiesty 7.04 on a IBM Netvista box.

Tried every thing to get wireless working with a number of cards/dongles. No go even with the WDA1320.

So reading the various posts, I note some have wiz bang connects, while other did not. So decided to kill fiesty and start from scratch - Dapper to Edgy to Fiesty reinstall. As soon as I put a fresh install of Dapper on the system, the WDA1320 sprang to life with absolutely no network tweeks. Upgraded to Edgy, again absolutely no problem. Upgraded to Fiesty, again no problem.
No ndiswrapper or anything - the wireless just works.

Then because I like network manger, installed that prog, and zap -WDA1320 died. 

Uninstalled network manager, and zoom the wda 1320 sprang to life again..

[COLOR="Red"]There is something in network manager that breaks the wireless.[/COLOR]

SUCCESS SUCCESS after three months of frustration. :guitar:
