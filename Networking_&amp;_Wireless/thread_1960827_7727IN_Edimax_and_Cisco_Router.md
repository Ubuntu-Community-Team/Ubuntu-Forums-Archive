### Post by chili555 on 2012-04-19
> (even that is not going to happen unless you are close to the router)It looks like your link quality is 44/70, so you are not very close.

802.11G maxes out at 54Mb/s; you have 72.2 so you are certainly in N territory. I suspect your wireless card and the router have negotiated downwards from 300Mb/s based on link quality.

As has been mentioned above, N and even G speeds exceed all but the fastest and most expensive internet services. N is typically only useful if you have a media server in the local network and you are streaming HD video or something similar.
