Sorry no, the A600 won't work at all unless you have Cricket do an ESN change in their switch, in which case the UM185 will cease to function.   

There are a bunch of routers available that support either of those Cricket modems, so you could still share the service if the computers are not too far away from each other.  I believe almost all of the Cradlepoint routers support the new 185C, you can see them at Wirelessnwifi.com.   

Depending on which one you get, you can connect 16 or more users at the same time.  Probably more than your modem will comfortably support. 

HTH
