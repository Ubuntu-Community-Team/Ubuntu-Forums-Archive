### Post by Vorian on 2006-12-11
> **fokas said:**
> I have a linksys WRT300N wireless adapter and I am trying to get it to work on ubuntu 6.10. So far I have been unable to get it to work. Any help? I downloaded the device driver but still does not recognize the adapter on my laptop.

You don't need the driver.  Make sure your router is connected to your computer.  Open a browser and enter 192.168.1.1 in the address line.  The user and password are admin by default.

Use the browser to configure your router, then reset your computer.
