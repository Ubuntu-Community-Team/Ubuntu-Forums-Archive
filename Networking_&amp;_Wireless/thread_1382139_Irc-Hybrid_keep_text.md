### Post by impact on 2010-01-15
I don't think IRC works that way. 

Your users would have to stay connected to the IRC server to see all the chat (they don't really have to be at a single machine, they just have to leave the IRC client running on their "main" or "home" machine and then connect to it).

You can put important information in the MOTD, which is displayed to the user when they connect.
