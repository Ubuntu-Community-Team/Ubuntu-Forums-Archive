### Post by damis648 on 2008-05-18
If you have told firestarter to open the ports, it probably has so the problem is probably your router. If you have a linksys router, type
[http://192.168.1.1](http://192.168.1.1)
in firefox and press enter.
As the username enter "admin" and as the password "admin" again. You are now in your router's configuration and from here find "port forwarding" or port exceptions or something similar and from there you can open the ports.
If you have a netgear router, type
[http://192.168.0.1](http://192.168.0.1)
and enter. As the username enter "admin" and as the password enter "password".  You are now in your router's configuration and from here find "port forwarding" or port exceptions or something similar and from there you can open the ports.

Hope i helped.
