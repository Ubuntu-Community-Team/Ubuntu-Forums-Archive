### Post by az on 2005-02-13
I always get a 100 percent connection with my usb wireless adapter.  My dsl is at 3.0 megs and I guess usb would go up to 11 Megs.  I would still not reccommend it, though.  If you can get a pci card that has full linux support, you will have less trouble.

### Post by emperor on 2005-02-13
Wireless PCI  Card:  get one with a removeable antenna, you may need a 5 or 6db antenna instead of the standard 2db rubber duck! Check the chipsets carefully for the model number and version selected. I prefer cards with Prism based chipsets.
 
 [http://prism54.org/supported_cards.php](http://prism54.org/supported_cards.php)
 
 Are the antenna's on your wireless router replaceable?
