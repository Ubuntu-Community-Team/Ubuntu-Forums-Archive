### Post by barcon on 2012-08-29
This is older, but I just ran into the same thing in 10.04.

Under the FTP menu item on the top menu of gFTP is now Preferences and not Options, as the error message says.  When opening that dialog, I entered /usr/bin/gedit in the Editor box.  When using, I then had an issue with character coding, but selecting Western worked for me once gedit was open.

Hope this helps.
