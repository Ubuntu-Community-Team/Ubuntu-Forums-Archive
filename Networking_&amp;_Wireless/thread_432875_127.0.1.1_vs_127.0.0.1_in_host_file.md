### Post by spd106 on 2007-05-04
Sorry, I don't have any answers, just a little bit of information I have observed.

The loopback address is the entire 127.0.0.0/8 range, so any dependency on 127.0.0.1 specifically sounds like a broken/buggy hack to me. I say this as a networking person rather than a software developer. 

I believe this change was the result of a discussion a while ago in Debian and you may be surprised to hear that it is also present in Edgy.
