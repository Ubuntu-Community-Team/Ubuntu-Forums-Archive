### Post by uncaspi on 2009-12-10
You could log in to the router and find it there. Go to windows and open a browser window and type 192.168.0.1 or 192.168.1.1 in the address field. Usually username to routers are admin and if you haven't changed your password it could be admin or 1234 or leave it blank, depending on your router. If you can't remember the password you could press the reset button. But then you will lost all your settings in the router and it would be reset to default.
