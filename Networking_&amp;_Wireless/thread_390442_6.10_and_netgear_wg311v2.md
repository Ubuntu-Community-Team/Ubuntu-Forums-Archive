### Post by Jose Catre-Vandis on 2007-03-23
Try taking out the Security - e.g. WEP, and see if that makes any difference. if it does then, you will need to configure your WEP properly.  Also, check you dmesg to be sure that the correct driver is loading up for your adapter. if not, you may need to blacklist the one that is loading to allow the r8187 driver to do its stuff
