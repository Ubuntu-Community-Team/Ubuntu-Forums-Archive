### Post by chili555 on 2008-04-28
I suggest unplugging and then detaching the modem. Then log in to the router. If you can, and in most routers I am familiar with, you can, change the address of the router. If it was 192.168.1.1 before, then I'd change it to 192.168.0.1. You will need to reboot the machines behind the router so they grab an IP address on the router's network.
