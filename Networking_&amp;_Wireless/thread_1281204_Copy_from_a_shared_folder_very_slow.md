### Post by issih on 2009-10-03
um you would... theoretical max throughput on wireless (assuming you have medium hardware i.e. 54g) is about 54Mbit/s compared to 100Mbit/s on a similarly middle spec lan (10/100).

So you are looking at at least double the speed, and that is assuming a perfect wireless connection, which never ever happens.You will get far less of your theoretical maximum using a wireless connection than a wired one, so wired will be several times faster.

N.B. all rates quoted are raw data transmission, not TCP/IP throughput which will be much lower.
