### Post by breakthestate on 2005-10-16
To begin, there are plenty of forum posts already covering this subject, but I still want to give advice.  As has been said on this forum before, wireless card companies often sell the "same" wireless card with a different chipset.  You need to pay attention to the version of the card, as it will give you information about what chipset is used.  

Here is a good list:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCards?highlight=%28wireless%29](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCards?highlight=%28wireless%29)

Good luck!
