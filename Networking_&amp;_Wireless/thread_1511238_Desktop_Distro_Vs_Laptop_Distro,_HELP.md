### Post by spotted zebra on 2010-06-16
did you install the drivers for you wireless card?

plug into a LAN connection and make sure you are connected to the internet. now go to system>admin>hardware drivers and let that program run. if you are lucky it will have a driver for your wireless card that you can download and install right there.

unplug the LAN and restart. see if your wireless works now.
