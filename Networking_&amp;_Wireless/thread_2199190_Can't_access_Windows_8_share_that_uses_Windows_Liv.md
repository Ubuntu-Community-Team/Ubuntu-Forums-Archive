*** UPDATE ***

So it just turns out I used the wrong password.

For people with this problem:

Your username for the network share is the first 5 characters of your 'First Name' you signed up with plus a  '_000'  . E.G.

If you are named Michael Jones, then your username (if you use a live email account for your windows 8 login) is Micha_000 and your password is your windows login password.
If for ANY reason this does not work, on your windows machine go to C:/Users/ and you will see a folder that matches your account, this folder name is your 'username'.

Thank you anyway and I hope this helps someone!
