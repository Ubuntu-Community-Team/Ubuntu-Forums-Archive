### Post by SparTacux on 2011-05-14
Get a Voltmeter ( Multimeter ) and test the output of the power supply by putting one lead on the outer metal of the plug and the other lead on the inside of the metal plug ( by plug I mean the metal bit that inserted into your modem ). You should get a Voltage reading according to the details listed on the power supply module. You did check the fuse didn't you ;-)
