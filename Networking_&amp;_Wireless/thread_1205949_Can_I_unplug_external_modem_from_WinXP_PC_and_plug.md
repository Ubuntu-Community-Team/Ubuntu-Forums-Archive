### Post by cariboo on 2009-07-06
Routers are relativly inexpensive, and have the added benefit of having a built in firewall. There are times when you need a second computer to help repair problems in either Windows or Ubuntu, and having a second computer with an internet connection is worth it's weight in gold. Routers are fairly easy to setup. Most of them come with a CD to set it up in Windows. Just follow the instructions, and it will be up and running in less then ten minutes. If you can set up ubuntu, you should have no problems setting up a router.
