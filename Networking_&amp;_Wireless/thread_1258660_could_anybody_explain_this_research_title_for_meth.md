In order to know more about Ad Hoc networkds you should refer to this wiki link
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wireless_ad_hoc_network](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wireless_ad_hoc_network)

But, I can still give you a small example of Ad Hoc network. Many a times me and my friend want to play a computer against each other we both have laptops but not cable to connect then one of the laptop creates its own wireless network and the other joins it giving rise to a small network.. 

This can be done anywhere.. Such type of decentralized wireless networks are called Ad Hoc Networks.
