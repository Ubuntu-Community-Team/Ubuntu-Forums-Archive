Aero,

If you search for wireless related issues on these forums, you will see that many people have problems setting it up initially, but most people get their problems resolved. The forums could help you a little easier if you mention what you have already tried.

1. Have you looked in System->Administration->Networking? Does your wireless appear there, and if so, have you tried configuring it?
2. What make and model of wireless card do you have? Does it show up under Applications->System Tools->System Information?
