### Post by Kirboosy on 2011-01-26
Open up Synaptic (System-->Administration-->Synaptic Package Manager)

In the quick search field type in "ndiswrapper" check the package that matches then hit the Apply button. Accept all the changes and let it go. 

You should have a new menu item under System-->Admin (I can't remember what its called. I think it might just be ndiswrapper)

Also there is no guarentee that this will work for your card. Please look at this [page]("http://sourceforge.net/apps/mediawiki/ndiswrapper/index.php?title=Category:MAYBE")
