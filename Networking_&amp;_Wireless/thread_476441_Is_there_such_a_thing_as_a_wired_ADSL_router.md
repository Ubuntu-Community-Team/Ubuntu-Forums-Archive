### Post by spd106 on 2007-06-18
Yes there are, however they typically cost the same as wireless routers. You will probably struggle to find a wired-only modem/router that is any cheaper than the equivalent with wireless.

Examples include [Linksys]("http://www.linksys.co.uk/acatalog/AG241.html") and [Netgear]("http://www.ebuyer.com/customer/products/index.html?rb=0&action=c2hvd19wcm9kdWN0X3NwZWNpZmljYXRpb25z&product_uid=52754"), who sell ADSL modems with an integrated switch.
