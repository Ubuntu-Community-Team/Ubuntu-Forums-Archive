### Post by GroundNpound on 2009-06-27
Get a cradlepoint CTR-347 mini router.  I use this in series with a wireless-N router at home... my PC, laptop,  and XBOX 360 all share the same internet connection.  All you do is plug it in, and the cradlepoint recognizes it immediately.  If I ever need it away from home, I just take it with my laptop running Ubuntu.
