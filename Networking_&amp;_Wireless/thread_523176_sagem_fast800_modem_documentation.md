### Post by jnorthr on 2007-08-11
FYI - in english, dutch and french are pdf's for the technical specifications for the fast800 and fast840 adsl modems : [http://www.sagem.com/support/site/page_accueil.php?site=1&pays=fr](http://www.sagem.com/support/site/page_accueil.php?site=1&pays=fr) then drill down to adsl homepage then use drop down under adsl modems labelled "selectionnez un produit" then choose your modem and in the pink panel on the left click on 'livrets utilisateur'. Under the lable 'titre' you'll see several pdf documents - the one marked as 'anglais'  is your english version. There is a french language version of a mandrake linux installation at : [http://www.sagem.com/support/site/livret/gu_Linux_fr.pdf](http://www.sagem.com/support/site/livret/gu_Linux_fr.pdf)
