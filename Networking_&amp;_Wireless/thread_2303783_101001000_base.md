### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-11-21
That's about right.  How many other computers are on your LAN?  SAMBA is not known for its speedy performance.  1 Gigabit/sec speeds refer to the burst speed not the sustained speed of the network connection.  On a busy network, expect 1/10 of that speed or 100 Megabits/sec which is 12.5 MegaBytes/sec.  

Try sharing a folder from Windows to Windows and transfer a large file and measure the time with a stopwatch and divide by the file size to get a real performance metric.  Don't rely on the progress bar.
