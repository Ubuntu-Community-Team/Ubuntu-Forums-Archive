### Post by poptones on 2005-07-05
There are several winmodems that are *claimed* to work in linux. I have some friends up and running with a conexant winmodem, but they are stuck in mandrake because I have been unable to get things working on ubuntu with this system.

Don't believe any of them. the conexant system above has real issues when online, the user reports frequent lockups that take the whole system down and this ONLY happens when online (gee, wonder what it could be?)

Get an external serial modem. Beware that many usb modems are actually winmodems, they act just like any other USB WAV device (ie they aren't really modems at all, more like sound cards). 

I strongly suggest avoiding the games and getting a "real" modem. I did this and the upside is, because it is not a cheap piece of crap made to the lowest dollar, it also happens to work much better (on the same phone lines) than any of the winmodems I had when I was using windows.
