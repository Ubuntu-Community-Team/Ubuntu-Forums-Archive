### Post by Cuba71 on 2009-09-18
My recommendation is to use a step by step approach.
First, reconfigure your wireless router and remove the password.
Then, if you see the network it should connect without problems.
After you're succesfull, then define your security and password on your router and test again, sometimes linux wireless drivers don't support WPA/WPE security.
