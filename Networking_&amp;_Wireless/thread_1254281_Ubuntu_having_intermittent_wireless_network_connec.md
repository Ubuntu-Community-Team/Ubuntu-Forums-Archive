### Post by Charly85551 on 2009-09-01
Hi,
I can not really advice you on any of the Linux set up issues but just one additional concern: Quite a few of the early WLAN equipped laptops showed over-heating effects with regular connection drops. Your DELL may be just one of them. There is no easy solution for it other than just switching the module off and monitor if it does better after cooling down for a while.
If it is a continuing problem I would replace it with a WLAN USB stick.
