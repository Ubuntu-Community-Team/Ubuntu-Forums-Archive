### Post by Dark_Stang on 2009-10-28
Your ISP is still going to be your bottleneck. Wired connections are usually 100mbps or 1gbps and most wireless connections are 54mbps or higher (depending on signal type, encryption, distance from AP, etc). However most ISPs only give 10-20mbps download speeds.
