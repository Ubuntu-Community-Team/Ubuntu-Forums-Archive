### Post by ltcolfury on 2007-04-25
I just updated today to 7.04. I had a problem re-connecting to my Wireless Network. (After Restart)

I just figured it out and thought I'd post on how I was able to get working again.

On the "Manual Network Configuration" screen/box, make sure you "name" your connection after your configurations (Home, Office, etc.) and afterwards make sure you click on the the green "Check Mark" on the far right hand side of the Configuration box. After I did that (I didn't see it) it linked right up. :)

Hopefully, I can save some of you some time who are upgrading to 7.04.
