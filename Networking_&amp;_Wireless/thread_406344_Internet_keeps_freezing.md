Because of this part, I tend to think this has nothing to do with your OS/es (just for clarification).

Here's what I would try to start:

1. power-cycle the modem--unplug it for about 10 seconds, plug it back in and let it cycle up, and then turn it off

If the problem persists, then go on to step 2:

2. disconnect your router from your network--in other words, browse the Internet on your desktop directly through the modem without connecting any cables to the router

If the problem still persists, then it's possible that you have issues with your service provider (based on the info you have provided here, which makes no mention of you "changing" anything lately).
