### Post by bacchusmarsh on 2010-02-01
ok it seems to be fixed!
i found info in this thread:
[URL="http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1330641&highlight=dsl-502t&page=2"]http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1330641&highlight=dsl-502t&page=2
[/URL]
which lead me to [this bug report]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/eglibc/+bug/417757") and the following work around:
Just updated to karmic and experienced the same problem (1-3 second delays on dns lookup).
Switching off IPv6 dns support in firefox "fixes" the problem.

Do do that open up Mozilla or Firefox and type in 'about:config' in the address bar
Scroll down to "network.dns.disableIPv6", it's defaulted to a value of false, change it to true.
