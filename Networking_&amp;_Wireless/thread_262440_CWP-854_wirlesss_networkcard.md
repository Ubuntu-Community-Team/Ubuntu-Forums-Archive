### Post by tturrisi on 2006-09-22
The CWP-854 has a Ralink chipset.
Ralink chipset cards use these drivers:
[http://rt2x00.serialmonkey.com/wiki/index.php/Main_Page](http://rt2x00.serialmonkey.com/wiki/index.php/Main_Page)
your card is listed on their hardware page (rt2500 Hardware):
[http://rt2x00.serialmonkey.com/wiki/index.php/Hardware](http://rt2x00.serialmonkey.com/wiki/index.php/Hardware)

If no joy you can likely get it to work w/ ndiswrapper & the Windows drivers:
[http://www.cnetusa.com/newwebsite/techsupport/driverdownload.htm](http://www.cnetusa.com/newwebsite/techsupport/driverdownload.htm)
(do a search here for howto ndiswrapper)
