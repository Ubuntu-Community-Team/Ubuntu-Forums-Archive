### Post by chili555 on 2014-01-15
Is there a question here? Shall we guess that the question is if anyone has experience with the card and can recommend it? Is the question whether it will reliably do N speeds? 

I suggest you Google up 'iwlwifi 11n_disable' and judge for yourself. This card will reliably do B and G speeds but, with most routers, needs to have N disabled. And before you ask about other Intel cards, the 6200 for example, the answer is the same. Some card and router combinations work well at N speeds with *iwlwifi* but most do not.

As to the next question you didn't ask but are about to; if not Intel, then who, I can't really comment. I have experience with Intels. Mine works mostly fine at N speeds with a Linksys EA6200AC router.
