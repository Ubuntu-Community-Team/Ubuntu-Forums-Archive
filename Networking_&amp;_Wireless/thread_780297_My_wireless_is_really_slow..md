### Post by knattlhuber on 2008-08-26
I had the same problem with a RT73-based card under 64-bit Hardy. Worked out-of-the box but was incredibly slow.
After installing [Wicd]("http://wicd.sourceforge.net/") (and thereby uninstalling the Network-Mananger applet), it works fine.
