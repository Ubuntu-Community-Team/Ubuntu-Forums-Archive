### Post by lindsay7 on 2012-03-04
I had on and off luck with the TP usb too and ended up buying a TP-Link card which work much better.  One thing you could take a look at is the channel which the Router is using. You could be competing with other routers around you. Try using a higher channel. You can find this setting in the router set up.
