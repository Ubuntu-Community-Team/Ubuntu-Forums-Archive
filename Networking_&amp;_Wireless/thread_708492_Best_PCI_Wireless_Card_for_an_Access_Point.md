I gather that I can do this with hostAPd and two PCI wireless cards (one to associate with the existing network and one to act as an AP for that network) that would be bridged.

### Post by spd106 on 2008-02-27
You'll have the best success with an Atheros based card, because they have the best, if quirky, support for Linux through Madwifi.org's driver. 

Avoid the newer cards and forget about 802.11n for the moment.

The Atheros cards often have eXtreme G or Super G in the name or on the box. That was an Atheros technology brand.
