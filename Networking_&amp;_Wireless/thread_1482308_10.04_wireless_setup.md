### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-05-13
Here you go.  As you can obviously get onto the net by some means or other, go to [http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/net/ndisgtk](http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/net/ndisgtk) even on a windows machine if necessary and download ndisgkt from there, copy it to your ubuntu machine and double click on it.  That will open the package installer, gdebi, check for dependencies, and you should then get it installed easily.

After that go to **System->Administration->Windows wireless drivers** and you can then navigate to the windows driver.inf file that you must have available on a CD or copied to your hard disk.
