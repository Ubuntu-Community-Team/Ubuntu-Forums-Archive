### Post by mazato on 2009-01-22
Here Orange Luna. 
PCI-X buses run only as fast as the slowest device.

Just to let you know of the variations:
PCI-X - 32 or 64 bit, 5v, or 3.3v.

The 3.3v slot is keyed differently and won't allow a 5v card to fit in
So be careful when buying the network card.
Good luck.
