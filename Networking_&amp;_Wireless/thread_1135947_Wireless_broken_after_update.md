### Post by suprecook on 2009-04-24
It happened to me a few times. The solution is quite easy. Connect you're computer to a router through and Ethernet cable to get and internet connection. Click on Settings-->System-->Hardware Drivers

When it opens up select the wireless driver and click activate. Restart you're system and it should work.
