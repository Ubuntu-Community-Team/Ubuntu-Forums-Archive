### Post by BkkBonanza on 2010-09-04
I think the free Yahoo Mail does not support POP3 and SMTP. I've read that you have to pay for Yahoo Mail Plus for that support.

There is a program called YPops that will proxy/simulate POP3/SMTP for this but I haven't used it. See here for details,

[http://www.ubuntugeek.com/free-pop3-and-smtp-access-to-yahoo-mail-using-ypops-in-ubuntu.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/free-pop3-and-smtp-access-to-yahoo-mail-using-ypops-in-ubuntu.html)

Personally, that's too much hassle. I just use gmail as it does have free POP3/SMTP even with SSL encryption.
