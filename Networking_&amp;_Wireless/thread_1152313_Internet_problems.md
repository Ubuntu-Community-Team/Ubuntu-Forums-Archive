### Post by connorh123 on 2009-05-07
Hey guys, every time I boot my computer after the log-in screen, it always asks for my wireless password in order to get onto the Internet. Is there a way to fix this?
Also, when I'm downloading something, my Internet will occasionally go out for no reason :(. I tried to get into my Network (System/Admin./Network) but it said the file didn't exist. Does anyone know how to fix these problems?

---

### Post by pastalavista on 2009-05-07
That happens, I think, because you added a "keyring" password. Right-click network manager icon in the notifications and select "Edit Connections", click the Wireless tab and delete the connection. Next time you connect and it asks for a keyring password, just click cancel. No keyring - no password. Also click 'edit' and check the box at the bottom that says "connect automatically"
