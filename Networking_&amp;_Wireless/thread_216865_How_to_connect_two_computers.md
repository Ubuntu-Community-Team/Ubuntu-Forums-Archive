### Post by morequarky on 2006-07-16
This seems like a strange question.  You have everything you need, I guess. Do you have any CAT5e ethernet wire?  Does your two computers have ethernet ports?

When the company installed the broadband connection did they give you a modem?

Connect your broadband connection(wire) to the RJ-11 port.
Connect your two computers to any of the 4 LAN ports.
That's it.

You can configure your "modem" by going to 192.168.1.1 after connecting one of the computers to it.

I guess from your list of what you have, you are only missing the wires.

Good luck.
