### Post by t0mppa on 2009-10-14
A lot of Atheros cards are supported "out of the box", ie. the default drivers for them work and you don't have to manually do anything. But really the only way to find out is to try and see what happens, when you plug it in.

You can do a test and see if the default drivers work up to a point (can't test connecting to a network, if none are available of course) even if you don't have a wireless router present at the moment. And if they don't work, then just drop a post here and you'll get some help.
