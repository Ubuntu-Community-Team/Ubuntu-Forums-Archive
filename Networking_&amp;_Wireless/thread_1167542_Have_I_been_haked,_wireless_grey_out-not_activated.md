> where on the pc can i checked if the wireless is (switch (on/off)) ?

If you're using a wireless device that's inside your computer, the position of the switch (if it exists) can be in different places.

On one of my laptops the switch is on the right, just in front of the CD/DVD drive. When switched on, there's a little orange light that glows.
On my other laptop, the switch is on the front, and also has a light that comes on when switched on.
The wireless device I sometimes use with one of my desktops just plugs in to a USB port and doesn't have a switch.
