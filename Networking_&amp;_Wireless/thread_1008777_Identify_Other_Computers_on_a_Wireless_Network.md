### Post by jmoorse on 2008-12-12
There are a number of free OS patterning tools available, NMAP is a good one.  If you scan your LAN segment it will respond with MAC/IP Addresses.  You will be gleaning system information only, not usernames.  Please be responsible with such tools.

If you are concerned about people using your wifi without consent consider using encryption (WPA, WPA2)
