### Post by Eiríkr on 2008-04-01
Having both machines as dual boot means your underlying OS configuration is inherently unstable, which consequently means that your sharing configuration is also inherently unstable.  Decide which machine will be your file server, and then decide if you want that machine to be Windows or Ubuntu.  **Then** we can help you get your sharing configured.  :)
