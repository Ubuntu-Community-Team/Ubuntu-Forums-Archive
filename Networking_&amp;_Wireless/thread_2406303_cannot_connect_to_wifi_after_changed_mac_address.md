### Post by mitesh.agrwl on 2018-11-19
Case 1: In mac address the first six hex character are unique to vendor and also carry important information, if that information is messed during the change in address, there is a chance of no connection!

Case 2: If the environment in which you are changing mac address is controlled - like in workplace with strict security measures - the mac address may have been be blinded to another variable to identify the device to prevent such an action!
