### Post by Ayuthia on 2008-12-11
Have you already loaded the b44 driver after wl?  Also, have you tried connection without encryption and also trying to connect to a site that is a little closer?  The sites that you posted are showing 1/5 signal.

If you have not tried loading the b44 driver after the wl, you can try the following:
```
sudo modprobe -r b44 ssb wl
sudo modprobe wl
sudo modprobe b44
sudo /etc/init.d/networking restart
```
