### Post by aeiah on 2011-02-21
you're streaming from one device on your local network to another, and both are wired. is this correct? if so, then it's nothing to do with:

having a wireless G modem (since G and N refer to wifi speeds, not wired)

having slow internet / old modem (since this video stream is not touching the internet)

someone leeching off your wireless (unless your wifi is open or only WEP encrypted)


the WRT54g is a very good router for its time. you can put ddwrt or openwrt on it, which are based on linux, and are very powerful router operating systems. your friend may have even done this. if you cant get in contact with him to get the login details, you'll have to reset the router if you want to do any changes. if you'd prefer to just spend money, and you won't be using the wireless for streaming, then just get anything. bonus points if its got gigabit ethernet, but if your pc or roku dont then there's no point in even having that.
