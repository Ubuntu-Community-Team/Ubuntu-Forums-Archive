If you trust a site you could simply whitelist it. There is an option to disable plug-ins, but you really don't have to do much after installing it because most options are enabled by default. And as I said, if you trust the site and want to play a flash movie or something you can allow it temporarily or add it to the whitelist. I really don't know how to "hide" plug-ins any other way.
