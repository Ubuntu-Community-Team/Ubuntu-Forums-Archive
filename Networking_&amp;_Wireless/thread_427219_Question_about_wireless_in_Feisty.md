ok, I've solve my problem myself already.
If u r still struggling with the problem above, then maybe u can try this: 
Go to  System>Adminstration>Network;
then right click on the Wireless Network, and choose 'properties', un-tick enable roaming mode.
Then choose the setting and enter password in the appropriate area. Close the window. and u can restart ur PC and u should no longer needed to re-enter ur password for ur wireless network.
