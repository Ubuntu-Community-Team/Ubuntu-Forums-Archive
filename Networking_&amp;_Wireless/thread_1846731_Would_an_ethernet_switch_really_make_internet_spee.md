### Post by tgm4883 on 2011-09-19
Obviously the first image is going to be the most fair, since it's setup the same way for everyone. That said, either isn't going to make much of a difference in internet speed. See below.

His assumption in pic 1 is that the red line is going to be the bottleneck. I seriously doubt this will be the case for the following reasons

1) Unless you bought a super fancy router, the connection from the router to the fibre optic modem? (is it still called a modem if it's fibre optic) is only 100mbit, not 1000mbit. 

2) Unless you have a super fast connection, I doubt your internet connection is pushing 100mbit.
