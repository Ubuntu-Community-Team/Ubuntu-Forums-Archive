### Post by Psychoscorpic on 2011-06-13
You can see the connection type by looking on the modem surface - it'll have an led shining through the plastic when it's connected, and the colour tells which speed it's connected at: deep blue = 3G. Other options are red, sky blue for GPRS and Edge.
