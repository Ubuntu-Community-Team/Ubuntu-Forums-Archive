### Post by dmizer on 2010-10-01
Depending on your wireless card, you should be able to do this via the networking applet in your toolbar.

Some network card drivers in Linux are capable of hosting ad-hoc networks, and some are not. Here's a link with how to configure this via nm-applet (the networking applet in your toolbar). It also has a list of cards that support ad-hoc networking: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Adhoc](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Adhoc)
