### Post by Milo82 on 2007-09-08
I've finally had time to order the new mini pci card and installed it. Worked in Vista, then worked immediately in Ubuntu. Finally I can stop having to log in to Vista constantly. The model I got is the Intel PRO/Wireless 3945ABG. If anyone else happens to have an HP dv6226us with a Broadcom card, its an easy fix. Buy and install a new card.
