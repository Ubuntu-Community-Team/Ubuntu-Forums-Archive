### Post by bkratz on 2010-08-31
> **bakom said:**
> Thank you for your reply. Unfortunately I am not using the STA driver, because it does not support my pci device (BC4135)

Then what driver are you using? According to this page

[http://linuxwireless.org/en/users/Drivers/b43#Known_PCI_devices](http://linuxwireless.org/en/users/Drivers/b43#Known_PCI_devices)
The b43 driver is only partially supported in kernel 2.6.33  (In 10.04 the current kernel is in the 2.6.32 range)

"14e4:4315
partially supported 2.6.33 and later (PIO mode)
BCM4312"

While  [http://www.broadcom.com/docs/linux_sta/README.txt](http://www.broadcom.com/docs/linux_sta/README.txt)
lists it as supported by the STA driver.
Supported devices
" 4312 2.4 Ghz	    0x14e4	0x4315  	Dell 1395"

You might want to look at lsmod in the terminal and make sure it is using what you told it too!
