### Post by dandnsmith on 2011-11-23
What might do the trick is a directional aerial for your end of the wifi connection, to enhance the signal both for send and receive as far as your end is concerned.
I've seen these discussed, so suggest you google to find details, as the precise nature of what you do (parabolic 'mirror' etc) will need to be connected to *your* device.

As far as using the router, you'd need bridging mode as the remote end (which not all routers support) and possibly your end as well, and you might still be up against it as far as strength of signal is concerned - so you need to consider how to make the router more directional for the link and also not stop it working with your PC.

HTH
