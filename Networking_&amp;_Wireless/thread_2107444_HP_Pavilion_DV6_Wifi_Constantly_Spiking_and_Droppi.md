### Post by meteorrock on 2013-01-22
I had that problem too. One crude way is to pull out your wireless dongle and plug it back in. Seems to reset your wifi and gets your wireless dongle working correctly again. 

I got mine in a dock on the desktop here and it acts up once and awhile, losing signal and dropping wifi, when before it would pick up wifi just fine. I just pull it out and plug it back in. I think it somehow gets corroded on the contact points in the dock after a few months.

Also try to clean the contact points with a qtip and some rubbing alcohol inside of the dock and on the dongle itself.
