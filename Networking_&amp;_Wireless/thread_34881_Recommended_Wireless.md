### Post by djpharoah on 2005-05-17
of course, the guys at BB will tell you that the cards will not work with linux, since they dont know better but in reality some cards work beautifully and some just dont.

The key is not actually what card you buy, but what the chipset the card has. Some chipsets have native support within the kernel. 

google 'ndiswrapper list' and that should give u a good idea of the wireless cards that will work and user reviews along with it
