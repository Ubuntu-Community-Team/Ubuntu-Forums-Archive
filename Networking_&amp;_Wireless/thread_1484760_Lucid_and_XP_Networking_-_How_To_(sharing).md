### Post by RamonBianco on 2010-09-16
"Guest" access is pretty simple if you're using Workgroup sharing.

To share a folder on your lucid box, open its parent in Nautilus [SIZE="5"]|[/SIZE] File Browser [SIZE="5"]|[/SIZE] Places, right-click on the folder, and pick either **Sharing Options**, or **Properties** then the **Share** tab.

Check the **Share this _f_older** box, and then the **_G_uest access** box, and you should be able to see the folder from your Windows box.
