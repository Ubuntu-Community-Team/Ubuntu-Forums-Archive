### Post by rhdinah on 2011-03-19
After a little more sleuthing about ... I found the answer to my question given by Mr. Papenpj [[COLOR="Red"]*here*[/COLOR]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=910291").

His suggestion in a nutshell is:

> Applications->Accessories->Passwords and Encryption Keys

Then go to the "Passwords" tab
Then you can right click on the password you want to delete.

Hurrah! That makes Mr. Papenpj a major samba networking guru and me a major networking sleuth ... at least IMHO. :D
