### Post by wrandygast on 2014-08-26
I have an Ubuntu Server (14.4) running PPTP Server. 

I could connect (mac) to the VPN on another wireless network, and reference all my other machines no problem.

Then, I tried to do the same with my wireless S5 connection. That would be the holy grail.  I could not see the other machines on the network AND I could not get any internet.  Sooo, I changed my network DHCP to 192.168.43.x to match my phone's ip (because I can't change the phone). Now I have internet buuut I still can't see my other machines.

I briefly tried connecting the phone to the VPN itself ... terrible results.

Any tips as to the direction I should be going on this ? I haven't googled anything positive on this yet.

### Post by wrandygast on 2014-08-27
I thought I would report back and hopefully save someone some surfing ! It turned out to be a setting on my phone. I had to force the LTE to use ipv4 instead of ipv6.

[solution]("http://blog.cuanticosecurity.com/2014/04/ipv6-apn-means-no-vpn-for-galaxy-s5-on.html")
