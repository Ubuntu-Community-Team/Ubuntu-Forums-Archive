### Post by Tyconic on 2008-12-12
I think by default network manager will automatically reconnect you to your last network. Try setting your wireless to "roaming mode," connecting to your router, and restarting.

Another option is to manually configure your wireless settings, once again via the network manager GUI.  

If you're feeling adventurous (and have only a single network you consistently connect to) you can try the howto given here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=263136](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=263136)

Hope this helps!
