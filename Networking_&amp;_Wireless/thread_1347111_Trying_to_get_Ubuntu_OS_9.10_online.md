### Post by some-what-Gnu-2-networks on 2009-12-05
If you are able to connect to a wired network, go to System-Administration-Hardware drivers. see if any thing is installable. There may be a wireless indicator  in the notification area (top right area of screen). click it and see if there is a "connect to hidden network" option available.
