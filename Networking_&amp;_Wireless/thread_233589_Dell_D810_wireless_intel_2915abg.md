### Post by maniacmusician on 2006-08-10

This means filling in the essid, WEP Key, whether you want static or DHCP, etc. If it doesn't activate or can't get on the internet after that, come back here and I will walk you how to use ndiswrapper to get a windows driver and make it work on linux
