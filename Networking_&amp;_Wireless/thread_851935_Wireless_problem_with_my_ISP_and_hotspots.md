### Post by lisati on 2008-07-07
I just looked at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=5328822&postcount=3](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=5328822&postcount=3)

For Hotspots, if you have wireless networking set to "Roaming" mode, your system will automatically detect what kind of WEP settings to use and ask you for a password only if it's needed.

For your home machine, have a look here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=202834&highlight=setting+wpa](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=202834&highlight=setting+wpa)
