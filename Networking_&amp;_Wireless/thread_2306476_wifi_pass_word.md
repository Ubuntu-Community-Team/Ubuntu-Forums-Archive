### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2015-12-15
No . . . ubuntu does support very long wifi passwords via the standard safe WPA protocol.   IF the far less secure WEP is used instead, then the password length is reduced to either 5 or 13 characters in length. 

This link explains and shows more:   [URL="http://askubuntu.com/questions/304460/wifi-only-accepts-passwords-of-5-or-13-characters"]http://askubuntu.com/questions/304460/wifi-only-accepts-passwords-of-5-or-13-characters

[/URL]
