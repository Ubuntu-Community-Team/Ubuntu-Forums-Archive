### Post by coldraven on 2013-10-14
You probably have a Winmodem. It is a cheap piece of junk that uses software to do all the work instead of a real modem chip.
You can read all about them here: [http://linmodems.org/](http://linmodems.org/)
I never managed to get the Winmodem in my HP Compaq laptop to work in Linux. You may be be better off by buying an external modem.
