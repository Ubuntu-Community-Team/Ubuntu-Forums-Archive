The answer I believe is dns server and this is why.

The key is A. the internet and B. ordinary user.

Web server is what they are accessing and part of the internet. the question says what makes these available for the ordinary user.

DHCP server simplifies things in the big picture as far as connecting all the machines so they interconnect. It's more of a standard created by and for industry professionals. Most ordinary users don't know anything about dhcp, it just works. But this could possible be an answer but I don't believe the best one of the three.

As an ordinary user, trying to remember dotted quad addresses is not easy but remembering [www.pepsi.com](www.pepsi.com) or [www.myfavoriteproduct.com](www.myfavoriteproduct.com) makes the internet simplified and very useful for the ordinary user. They don't deal with servers (except reading what's served) nor do they deal with dhcp settings 90% of the time. But accessing webs through their address happens daily.
