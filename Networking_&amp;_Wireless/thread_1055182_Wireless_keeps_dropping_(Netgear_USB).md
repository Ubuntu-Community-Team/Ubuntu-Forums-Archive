### Post by XS_Caboose on 2009-01-31
I had the same problem with my Netgear USB. Honestly, it wasn't the OS that was the problem in my case, once I got a PCI Wifi card, everything worked great, same when I hooked it up to a wireless bridge. Those Netgear USB adapters are absolute trash. XP, Vista, Ubuntu, I've had nothing but problems.

Note, that was in my case. Who knows, I may have had a bad adapter. But once I switched to another method of connecting, no problems whatsoever. PCI Wifi cards are not that expensive at all, so I would recommend one of those.
