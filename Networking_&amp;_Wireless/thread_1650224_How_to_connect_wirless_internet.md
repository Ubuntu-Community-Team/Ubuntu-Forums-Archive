### Post by rvchari on 2010-12-22
i dont know abt cdma bsnl card. but you can get a clue on how i connect through my gprs via blue tooth (or usb connected, which ever way you can do this)
i use blue tooth to avoid ugly cables dangling out ! first and fore most, when your device is set up, it will prompt you to either manual configure or let you choose from pull down menu regarding ur service provider etc etc.

in my case, once i set up my phone as blue tooth device, it gave me a prompt saying do i wish to connect to internet using DUN ? i approved and configured. my apn is bsnlnet (probably the same for all bsnl users ? !) username and password field is left blank   and phone no is configured on its own. once the set up is over you should get a confirmation that your device is ready for use or something like that.
after that, go to top panel right, clik on the connection manager and you should see your newly configured option. click on that to connect. you should be agle to do it...
