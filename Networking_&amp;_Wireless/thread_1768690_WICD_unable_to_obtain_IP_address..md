### Post by pattelin on 2011-06-08
In the end, I still don't know why it's not working but I have a workaround.

One must not uninstall NM. Instead just disable it from System->Startup preferences.

Run WICD and choose a WIFI that u know works. Once WICD obtains an IP, it is possible to connect to any WIFI.
