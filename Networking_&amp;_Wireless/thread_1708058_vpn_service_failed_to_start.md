### Post by Dis-appointed on 2011-04-29
Might be a bit late, but just read this msg while looking to solve my VPN problems, so thought I'd share my solution.

Here is what worked for me.

In the VPN settings, uncheck the option "[FONT=Verdana]Available to all users"[/FONT][FONT=Verdana][/FONT]
In the "Advanced ..." check the "Use Point-to-Point encryption (MPPE)"

Tested these settings on [http://www.bestfreevpn.com/](http://www.bestfreevpn.com/) and they worked for me :D
