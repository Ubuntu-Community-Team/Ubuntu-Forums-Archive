### Post by spd106 on 2007-06-26
Those pages are the spec docs used by the developers and are not really intended for the end users. The edubuntu wiki pages are a little more up-to-date and slightly more user friendly, [https://wiki.edubuntu.org/EnableLTSP5LocalDevices](https://wiki.edubuntu.org/EnableLTSP5LocalDevices).
