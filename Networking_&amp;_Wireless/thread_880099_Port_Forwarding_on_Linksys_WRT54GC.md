### Post by Halibutt on 2008-09-10
I have the same model and port forwarding works like a charm. There's two things to check: first of all check if the ip address you're using is really 192.168.0.201 (it could be your ubuntu is configured so that it automatically obtains ip and your router provides it with a different one from the list/range). Secondly, you could always try upgrading your router's firmware to 2.0. Hope that helps.
