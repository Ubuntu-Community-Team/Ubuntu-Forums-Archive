### Post by brelic on 2005-09-06
I'm at work at the moment on Windows, so I'm doing this from memory, but if you click on System->Admin->Networking (I believe)... you will get a dialog box with the various network adapters listed.  Highlight the wireless device and click on Properties (or Configure).  The new dialog box that opens up should have a drop down menu at the top in which you can create new profiles/locations.  Then it's just a matter of selecting the appropriate one.
