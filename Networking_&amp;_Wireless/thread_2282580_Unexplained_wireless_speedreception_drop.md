### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-06-15
Try reseating the antenna connections on the wifi card.  You can typically access it from a small door on the underside of the laptop.  Sometimes the wifi card needs reseating itself.  It could be a worn-out antenna, since they route through the hinge.  How many times a week do you open and close the laptop lid?
