### Post by PatchesTheCaveman on 2011-01-05
Go to Applications > Accessories > Terminal on your top panel, and type the following commands in the black box that appears, pressing Enter after each one:
```
lspci -vnn
lshw -C network
```

Copy and paste what appears into a reply to this thread.  This will give us details about your hardware that will identify the exact driver needed.
