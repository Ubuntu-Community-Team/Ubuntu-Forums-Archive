### Post by lisati on 2007-06-30
First of all  you'll need to enable wireless networking. You can do this by clicking on System->Administration->Networking and setting some options from there. If you're likely to be moving around a bit, you might want to consider enabling "Roaming mode" for your wireless network.

Have a look at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs) for more information.
