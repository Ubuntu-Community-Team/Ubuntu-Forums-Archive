### Post by xzero1 on 2007-05-29
Wi-fi is a kind of radio. Think of the ESSID as the station you want to connect to. Usually you connect to a router and if it does not broadcast its ESSID then you normally can't connect to it.Now when you get a connection you are given an ip. If you have a valid ip not say 0.0.0.0 then you ARE connected. There are various settings on both devices that must match for you to get a connection.
