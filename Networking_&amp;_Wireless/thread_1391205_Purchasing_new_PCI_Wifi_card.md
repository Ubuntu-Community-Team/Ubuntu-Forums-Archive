### Post by t4thfavor on 2010-01-26
Ubiquiti, or Atheros are good cards, Find a card with one of these chips, and be happy with it. 

If you want a card that doesn't come in PCI, you can get a pcmcia adaptor for your desktop. I run a senao card (prismGT chipset) on mine which works nice.
