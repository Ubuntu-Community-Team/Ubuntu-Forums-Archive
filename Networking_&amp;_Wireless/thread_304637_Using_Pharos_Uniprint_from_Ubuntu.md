### Post by SendDerek on 2007-06-22
I was just having the exact same problem as you.  My school here in Rexburg, ID uses the Pharos print servers and there wasn't any guide in getting it to work with Linux.  So, I figured it out and created one.  BYU-Idaho excepted it and published it in the wireless printing section of their website.  Here is the direct link:

[http://www.byui.edu/wireless/linuxprint.htm](http://www.byui.edu/wireless/linuxprint.htm)

Maybe this will help you.  The only downside is that you have to create a new user account that is the same as your school's account in order for it to work.  But, that's easy stuff.
