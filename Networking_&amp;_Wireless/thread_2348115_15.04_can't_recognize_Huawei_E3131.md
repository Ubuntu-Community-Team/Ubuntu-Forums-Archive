Welcome to the forums.

15.04 support ended in 2015. First thing you need to do is move to a supported Ubuntu release, probably 16.04 LTS is what you need. We aren't supposed to help with unsupported versions here.

I don't know anything about Huawei hardware. Never touched it or even seen it. Is this a modem?
