### Post by MrCheese on 2006-03-31
This isn't spam - just a bit of advice. I bought a Safecom Ti ACX111 based wifi card last year which I got going, first with Driverloader,then ndiswrapper, then the kernel acx driver. However, I've continued to have performance issues. I started to look for a cheap cardbus card with a native linux driver, and ended up with an Atheros-based card ,which cost me about £16 delivered.

Check item 9705431153 on eBay - if you are still struggling with wifi this may just sort you out.  So, the holy grail of cheap + native driver is achievable! I got mine delivered in 2 days & had it working in 5 minutes.
 
The Safecom is going on eBay  - don't buy it! 8)
