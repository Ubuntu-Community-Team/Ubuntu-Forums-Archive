try making a local network, like, ad hoc. Just connect the two computers with a cat5e cable. It's the standard LAN cable that would connect your network interface with your internet connection. If you don't have one you should be able to pick one up cheap.

I know in windows you could just plug it in and it would autoconfigure the connection. Can't say about linux because frankly I've never tried.

The way to know it works would be to open up your file system explorer and see if you can see the other computer when you're looking at the network tab.
