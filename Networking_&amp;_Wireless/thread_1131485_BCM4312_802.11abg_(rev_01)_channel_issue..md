### Post by definingmoment on 2009-04-21
Solved, for anyone using this card:

go to [http://www.broadcom.com/support/802.11/linux_sta.php](http://www.broadcom.com/support/802.11/linux_sta.php)

download the package for your specific system architecture(32/64), compile and insert module wl, and you can scan/use "a" and "g" (have not tried "b").
