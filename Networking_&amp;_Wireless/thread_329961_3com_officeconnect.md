### Post by scaryant on 2007-01-11
Your 3Com USB WiFi adapter has a ZD1211 chipset, apparently the newer distro of Ubuntu comes packaged with this driver but I had a few problems trying to get it to work. So I re-installed everything from scratch and configured it all manually. It turns out that the instructions are all pretty much [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=288753").

Of course specific instructions really depend on what your WiFi network is setup for (WPA, WPA-PSK, WEP, etc)
