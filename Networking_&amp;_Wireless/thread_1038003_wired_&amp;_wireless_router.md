### Post by superprash2003 on 2009-01-12
well yes.. you could make it a wired/wireles router.. by enabling internet connection sharing in ubuntu .. YOu could connect it to a wired or a wireless router.. which would behave like a wired/wifi hub.. but the main routing part would be done by ubuntu.. if you wish to use the wifi card of the computer as a wifi router , then you could but only with 1 pc at a time.. in adhoc mode.. 
for reference : internet connection sharing in ubuntu [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=91370](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=91370)
