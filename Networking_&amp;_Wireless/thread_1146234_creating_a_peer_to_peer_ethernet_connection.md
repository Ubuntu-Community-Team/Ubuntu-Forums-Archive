### Post by lensman3 on 2009-05-02
Just connect the two computers together with a crossover ethernet cable.  The regular cables won't work!  You need a special crossover cable.

That said, some hardware will automatically detect if the wires need to crossed and will do it for you.  Usually, this is handled in the hub for you.  Try to find a used hub for about $5.00.  Even a 10 Mbit hub that is 15 years old will work and you won't have to make up the special cables.

Crossover cables were used to connect hubs together.
