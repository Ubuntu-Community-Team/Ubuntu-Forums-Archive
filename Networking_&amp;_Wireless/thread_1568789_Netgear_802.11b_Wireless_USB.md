### Post by MaindotC on 2010-09-05
Anything is possible, but it may require some perserverence in getting it working unless Netgear provides a driver for it.  You can view [a list of Netgear cards and adapters](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCardsNetgear) that have been tested with Ubuntu and their respective version.  If you don't see your wireless card on there (USB 802.11b is a little too general) then you can plug it in and follow the [Ubuntu Wireless Troubleshooting Guide](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessTroubleShootingGuide) and let us know if you have a problems.
