### Post by Johnny K on 2007-11-24
I used to be able to connect to my Windows workgroup just fine. I could see the other computers on the workgroup (via *Places => Network*) and share files between them. But ever since I upgraded to Gutsy, I can no longer see the other workgroup computers. When I go to *Places => Network*, a "Windows Network" icon is listed. When I click that icon, no computers are listed.

Has anyone else experienced this problem after upgrading to Gutsy, or is it related to something else?

***edit:*** It turns out that this was caused by my firewall having too high of a setting. Thanks anyway.
