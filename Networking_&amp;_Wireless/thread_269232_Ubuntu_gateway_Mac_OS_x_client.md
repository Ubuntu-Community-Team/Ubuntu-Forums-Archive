### Post by kidders on 2006-10-01
You can only do this if your Linux box's wireless adapter can act as an AP ... I'm sure your manufacturer's website will tell you whether it supports this feature. From there, you'll need to find a suitable HOWTO that mentions your card. (Many WLAN HOWTOs are pretty vendor-specific.)

Of course, the most sensible solution would be to buy another ethernet cable and maybe another network card ... high-speed, easy to do, and dead cheap :-P
