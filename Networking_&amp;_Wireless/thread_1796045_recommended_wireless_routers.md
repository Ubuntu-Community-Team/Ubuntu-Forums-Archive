I am from the UK, all of the good routers are easily available over here and as stated they are OS agnostic.

I love it and it is available on amazon.co.uk,staples,pc world,dixons/currys pretty much everywhere as are its similar counterparts, predecessors and newer models.

Pretty much any reupatble name (netgear, linksys(cisco), dlink, Buffalo etc etc will work.
