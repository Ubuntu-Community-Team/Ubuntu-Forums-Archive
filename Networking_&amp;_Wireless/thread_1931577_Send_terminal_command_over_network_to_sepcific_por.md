### Post by papibe on 2012-02-26
Hi fubofo.

Although in a different approach, I think this may help:

There's an multiplatform application called [synergy]("http://lifehacker.com/254648/how-to-control-multiple-computers-with-a-single-keyboard-and-mouse") that allow you to share the movements of your desktop mouse, and control it from another machine.

There's a couple of Synergy versions on the software center, and there's also an Android version too (check [here]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/synergyandroid/")).

Hope it helps,
