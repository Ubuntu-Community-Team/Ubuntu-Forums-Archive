### Post by jelly on 2008-03-18
Hi, I've just stumbled across this thread - so this response is likely to be a little late!  Anyway..

The speeds advertised by the Wireless equipment manufacturers aren't exactly accurate.  In fact, I consider them a down-right lie.  It's supposed to be some sort of theoretical maximum speed - but in practice you'll never get anywhere near it.

On a 802.11G (54G) wireless network, you should be getting just over 1MB/s transfer speeds, depending on the quality of the connection.  I personally get 1.1MB/s - so you're doing OK.

There is now 802.11N equipment that's available, which is a lot faster - although I've not tried it myself, and don't know what support there is in Linux.

- Jelly.
