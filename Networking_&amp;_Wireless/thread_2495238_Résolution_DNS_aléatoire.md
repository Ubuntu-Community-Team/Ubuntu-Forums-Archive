### Post by TheFu on 2024-02-12
Sometimes parts of the internet have failures. Sometimes those failures are accidental and sometimes "bad actors" are screwing with routing for their own reasons.  Often the issue is with the ISP when it is a wide issue. 

Humans make mistakes.

First, use a "down for me or everyone" web site to see if it is you or the entire world.  If you can't get to that checking website, assume it is you.
