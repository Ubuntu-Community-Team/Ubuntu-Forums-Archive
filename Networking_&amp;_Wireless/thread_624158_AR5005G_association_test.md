The router may be set to hide it's ssid - some people consider it more secure, as you are finding out.  Of course, if you've never gotten it to work before, it may be a dud.  One thing you can try is running iwlist, which will display all the networks in your area, with or without an ssid, which would be a way of determining whether or not the device is broadcasting properly - I'm assuming that your wireless card has worked before...
