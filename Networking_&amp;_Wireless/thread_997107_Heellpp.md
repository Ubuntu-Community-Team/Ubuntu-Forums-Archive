### Post by cmnorton on 2008-11-29
Put a router between them. Get instructions from the manufacturer and your DSL provider.

Between your Linux system and the router, you'll get DHCP and be on the router's local subnet. Using the router's built-in http interface, you'll put the DSL modem into pass-thru mode. 

My guess is that is why you cannot get things to work. 

Especially where this is an older PC, let more modern technology do the heavy lifting (router and DSL modem).
