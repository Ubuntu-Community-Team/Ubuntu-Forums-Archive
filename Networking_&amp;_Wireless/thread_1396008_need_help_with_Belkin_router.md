### Post by underquark on 2010-02-01
Usually it has its own software onboard.  You access it by treating it like a web site.  Connect the router to your computer with a CAT5 cable and use your web browser to go to 192.168.2.1, 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.1.2 or something similar (the address will vary depending upon the manufacture and whether it has been previously changed).
