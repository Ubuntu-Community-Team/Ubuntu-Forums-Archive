### Post by kidders on 2007-12-14
Hi there,

If you can't forward your public IP to your own computer, then there is no way for anyone to initiate a connection with you, I'm afraid. To see what ports are open, try port scanning yourself. There's a variety of utilities (web-based and otherwise) you can use, eg nmap.
