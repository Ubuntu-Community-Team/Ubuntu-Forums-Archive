### Post by varunendra on 2014-10-08
Is the problem not getting the internet connection or not getting a static IP?

In a PPPoE connection, the IP is assigned by the ISP. So if your router is in bridged mode, it means you are directly connected to your ISP and your IP address will be what is assigned by the ISP, you can't control it.

Accordingly, your IP address will be static ONLY if the ISP has put you on a static IP plan. Have you talked to them about this?

It may also help if you let us know what is your goal with a static IP.

Hope I didn't misunderstand anything, please correct me if I did.
