### Post by coolclassic on 2008-01-21
Your problem may be due to window scaling. To correct this problem you need to edit /etc/sysctl.

Put the following into this file.
net.ipv4.tcp_window_scaling = 0 

and run sudo sysctl -p from the shell.

Hope this works
