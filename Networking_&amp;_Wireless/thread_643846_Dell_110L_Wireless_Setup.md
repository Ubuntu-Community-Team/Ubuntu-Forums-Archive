As typical with Dell there's a choice of Wifi nics. So if you have an Intel Wifi card you should be on easy street.

If you have the Dell branded card then you'll be into Broadcom territory, which isn't as nice. There's lot's of guides around the forum and wiki though. I would personally recommend this page in the wiki [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/bcm43xx/](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/bcm43xx/)
