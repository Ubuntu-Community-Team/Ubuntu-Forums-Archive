### Post by superprash2003 on 2008-03-26
what you could do is disable dhcp on all routers except one.so the one router would give the ips to all your pcs and the other routers would just act like hubs or wireless hubs..That way you will get ips of the same subnet etc.. so then all pcs connected to all routers would be in one network
