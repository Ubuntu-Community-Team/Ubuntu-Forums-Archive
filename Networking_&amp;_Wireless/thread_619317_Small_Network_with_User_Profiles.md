### Post by Blutack on 2007-11-21
Hmm...you've got yourself a bit job there!  Network administration is not an easy task, plus if you screw it up free software gets blamed :-)
Hate to say this but the ubuntu documentation for this kind of stuff is generally poor.  Have a look here, you might be better starting with opensuse and then once you are happy with the configs switch to ubuntu.  
[http://en.opensuse.org/Howto_LDAP_userAuth](http://en.opensuse.org/Howto_LDAP_userAuth)
Also, I hope you are aware that kubuntu is slightly less polished than ubuntu - whilst you may prefer it's complexity, your users may not.  There is a reason fedora and suse use gnome as the default enterprise desktop.  Evolution can authenticate against many server types, basically it ain't easy to pick one though.
You've let yourself in for a hell of a job!
Good luck.
