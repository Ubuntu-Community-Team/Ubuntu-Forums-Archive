### Post by ant2ne on 2009-10-29
I'm fond of linksys. Plug and play and reliable.

Having said that: In my experience, netperf on an all G network will result in 6 to 14 Mbs with 10 being the average. I got 14 with a G laptop only feet from the router and no wireless phones or other wifi devices in the area. (I live on a dead end street)

Nothing beats Cat5e (except Cat6)
