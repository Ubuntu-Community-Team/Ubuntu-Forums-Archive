### Post by P4man on 2010-11-06
The speed measured by speedtest is your actual internet connection speed, its not theoretical. But it measures to a nearby node and of course if you download from a server which doesnt have enough bandwidth then nothing will help you.  Try downloading an ubuntu image from a local mirror you will get close to your linespeed.

Oh and dont confuse Mbit/s with KByte/s !
1 Mbit/s  = = 1024 Kbit =  128 KByte/s
(note bits are usually abbreviated with lowercase b and Bytes with uppercase B)
So you are actually maxing out your connection pretty much (there is always overhead)
