### Post by Fire_Chief on 2007-11-09
That probably depends on how much you may or may not want to tinker with the router. If you just want a router that works, either one should be fine.

The WRT54G (v5) has different hardware inside and runs a version of VxWorks as the OS. You can' t modify it much (such as flash with different OS) due to the limited hardware resources.
The WRT54GL has the same hardware as the older WRT54G v4 router and can easily be flashed with several different kinds of Firmware (DD-WRT, OpenWRT, etc.). This allows extended functionality and further customization of the router than is available under the Linksys firmware. BEWARE though, if performed incorrectly, you can kill (brick) your router. 

See [http://www.wrt54g.net/]("http://www.wrt54g.net/") for more info
