### Post by scripters on 2010-05-04
this recently came to my attention, everytime I login I have to authenticate my self to a keyring prompt, but by simply clicking on the "Edit Connections" on the wifi icon, then selecting the "Wireless" tab and selecting your connection and then clicking the "Edit" button I checked the 2 check boxes for: Connect automatically and Available to all users this made me to connect on start up automatically. Im using the NBR release but I believe it should be the same.
