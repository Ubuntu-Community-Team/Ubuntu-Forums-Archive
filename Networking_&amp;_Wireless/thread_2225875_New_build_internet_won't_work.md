### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2014-05-23
Ethernet and Wi-Fi are independent; the former almost always just works, the latter not so much. There are many Wi-Fi chipsets that work OOTB but also many other that don't and some need special "fixes"...
Your onboard Ethernet should work. Again, the same question: Have you connected successfully other computer/device with that same cable in the same router port?

You need to provide information regarding the Wi-Fi if you also need it working? Yours can be one of those that need special care.
