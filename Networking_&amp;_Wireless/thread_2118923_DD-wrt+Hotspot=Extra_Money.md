As far as just sharing your own connection, I suspect Qwest would take a dim view.  Read your service agreement, I'll bet there's something about not sharing your service.  

My other concern would be someone doing something they weren't supposed to do - like trafficking in child porn - on YOUR internet connection with YOUR I.P. stamped on the packets.  If you have a legal arrangement to resell Qwest connectivity, you'd be no more legally liable for misuse of the service than Starbucks or McDonalds or anyone else offering public WiFi.
