### Post by Shay Stephens on 2007-01-10
It could be a temporary local problem.  It could be a driver/hardware problem, it could be an IPv6 problem.  I know for me, I have to turn off IPv6 since nothing I have supports it.  I don't know if this is true in your case or not.  Here are some instructions for turning off IPv6 if you think this applies to you:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=1942241&postcount=6](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=1942241&postcount=6)

Once I have IPv6 disabled, my speed goes back to normal.
