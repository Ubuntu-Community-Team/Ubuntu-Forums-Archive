### Post by veritas366 on 2005-05-13
My profound apologies to everyone!  I had suspected that my install of firestarter was the issue.  Well, I SWEAR I had uninstalled firestarter...but I double checked and it was still there.  I uninstalled and the download speed instantly doubled (after a reboot.)  

Anyway, don't know why Firestarter slowed it down but it's all better now. :-P
