### Post by wojox on 2011-03-09
> **codsniper said:**
> I do have it plugged in. The problem is getting it to connect

Okay so you had it plugged in when you activated the drivers. Now right click on your network manager applet on the top panel and go to wireless. Select edit and make sure connect automatically is checked then save and reboot. Make sure you unplug the ethernet cord before you reboot.
