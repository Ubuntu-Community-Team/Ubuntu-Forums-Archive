### Post by Nordite on 2010-01-23
Finally Success!
I guess changes just take a reallgy long time (over night) in Nautalis.  Here were the steps I took following the information in this link:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1169149](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1169149)

I used the terminal and changed my smb.conf file configuration.
1) changed all the PCs to the same workgroup
2) then right under the workgroup added the name of each PC like "netbios = PC#1, netbios = PC#2, netbios = PC#3,  (substitute your actual PC names where it says PC#whatever)
3) I don't know that this step is necessary but this was just something I did so I am putting it in.  I added my Ubuntu user login name as an Administrator on the PC I was trying to connect to.
4)  I went to my two Windows PCs and made sure they were connecting.  (they were.)
Last night before going to bed I tried one more time to connect and could not.  Today about 24 hours later I could.  Also I went through to STEP 3 and then got to tired and went to bed.
I hope this helps someone!
