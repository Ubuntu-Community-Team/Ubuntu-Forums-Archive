### Post by chili555 on 2010-05-04
> I am hoping to obtain some kind of concrete proof that I can give to my ISP before switching routers.What is your ISP going to do about it? Is it somehow their responsibility that you are running an unencrypted network? Just set up WPA2 and, as has been suggested, MAC filtering. Use a strong password that is unlikely to be in a hackers dictionary, such as 3*Vn@23;F2@. Check the router's logs once in a while and, if things look suspicious, change the password again.

Your neighbor will be foiled probably forever. I don't think you have to explain your endeavors to install proper security to your ISP or your neighbor.
