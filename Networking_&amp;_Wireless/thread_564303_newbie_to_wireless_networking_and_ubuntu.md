### Post by scrooge_74 on 2007-10-01
With Linux it works a little different

if you installed openssh-server you can log into each other and share files on a secure connection and even the desktop

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=499431](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=499431)


you can also use nfs to mount folders in each other.
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=249889](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=249889)

With cups you can share printers easily

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=231630&highlight=cups](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=231630&highlight=cups)

Hope those links give you a couple of ideas
