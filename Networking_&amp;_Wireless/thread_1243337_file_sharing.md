### Post by dmizer on 2009-08-18
> **Rajwinder said:**
> If my partitions in ubuntu are formatted with ext3 or ext4. Can  I share data of  ubuntu pc with other windows machines on network. Or there should be fat partition in ubuntu

It doesn't matter what your Ubuntu drives are formatted as when sharing data across a network. Just right click on the folder you want to share, and select "sharing options". That should take care of everything.

The drive format only matters when both Ubuntu and Windows share the same computer (as with a dual boot system)
