### Post by mips on 2006-01-26
I wish more people would post their ipconfig /all information ;)

On the Gnome panel go: System->Administration->Networking

Select your ethernet interface and ensure it is active/enabled.
Next simply configure it to use DHCP for TCP/IP settings. Its somewhere there but I cannot remember as I now use KDE. That should be it...

Here is a trouble shooting guide to follow: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=87643](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=87643)

If you have hassles come and let us know what the problem is.
