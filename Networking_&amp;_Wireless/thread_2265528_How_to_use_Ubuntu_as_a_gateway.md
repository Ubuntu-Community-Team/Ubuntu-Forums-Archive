### Post by Lars Noodén on 2015-02-16
If you've got a 14.04 desktop machine and want to share the wireless over the wired ethernet port, then you can do it by clicking on the network icon indicator in the panel at the top of the screen near the right.  Then select "Edit Connections..." and "Ethernet->Wired connection 1" and "Edit".  Then select "IPv4 Settings" and under "Method" choose "shared to other computers".  Then just plug in another computer to the ethernet port and it will get network connectivity.
