Incidentally, if you open Software and Updates> Enable the Proprietary Drivers repository> update> open Additional Drivers your nvidia driver should be there waiting to be enabled. You generally don't need to install from a third-party site. If your computer was working and you don't use 3d for games or much else, you don't need the nvidia proprietary driver at all, or shouldn't. The open-source one works fine.
