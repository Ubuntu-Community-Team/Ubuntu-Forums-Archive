### Post by pytheas22 on 2009-04-14
You can download the aircrack packages from [http://packages.ubuntu.com](http://packages.ubuntu.com), then double-click on them to install.  If they refuse to install because of missing dependencies, you will need to go back to [http://packages.ubuntu.com](http://packages.ubuntu.com) and download the dependencies as well.

Of course, a much easier way to do this would be to connect your computer to the Internet and type 'sudo apt-get install aircrack-ng'.

There are also customized live CDs that come with aircrack already installed (and, in most cases, wireless drivers already patched for sniffing and injection); see for example [BackTrack]("http://www.remote-exploit.org/backtrack.html").
