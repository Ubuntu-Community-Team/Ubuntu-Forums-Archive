### Post by Rhubarb on 2010-07-13
While I don't have experience with that particular piece of Telstra gear, I have found that anything with an ethernet jack on it will work with Linux.
The software that comes with routers / gateways / modems are generally for "easy setup / configuration" of the device.
Usually DHCP and an internet browser is all you need to configure one of these devices.

If you call Telstra support they may be able to help you ... just be prepared to be on the phone for a while.  You may have to request to be put through to a technician that actually knows stuff.
