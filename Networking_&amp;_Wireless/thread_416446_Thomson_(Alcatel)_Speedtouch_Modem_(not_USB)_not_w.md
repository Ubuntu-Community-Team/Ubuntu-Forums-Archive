Hi,


Have you got any more information about the problem? Also the information I quote is for the 510E and 510v4 I don't think this works on the 510v6 version.

I suggest resetting the modem and re-configuring it.

I got this from Speedtouch's website.

> 
**Q: I have SpeedTouch 510v4 (or 530v4), and I have forgotten the password. How do I reset the router?**

A:

If you need to access the SpeedTouch web interface, and have forgotten the units security password. It is possible to reset the unit to factory defaults. This will clear the units password AND clear any user specific configuration.

To reset the router, plug in and power up the unit. When the power light flashes green > press the front button. After six seconds the power light will again flash green > press again the front button. All of the lights will now flash and the unit will reboot. The unit is now factory reset, and the web interface can be viewed at [http://10.0.0.138](http://10.0.0.138)


If you then point Firefox at [http://10.0.0.138](http://10.0.0.138) then you should be able to plug in your ISP details. I've had trouble in the past with web configs of modems and routers, you may need to use internet explorer.

Good luck.
