### Post by _graham_ on 2009-01-18
I also have a Samsung D600 and I'm on Hardy Heron.  After spending ages trawling the web for a way to download my photos I eventually found this:

[http://linux-is-sexy.blogspot.com/2006/01/samsung-sgh-e720-with-linux.html](http://linux-is-sexy.blogspot.com/2006/01/samsung-sgh-e720-with-linux.html)
