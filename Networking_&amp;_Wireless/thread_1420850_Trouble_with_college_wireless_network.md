### Post by gordboy on 2010-03-03
do you know if your wireless card is working at all? Have you tried connecting in any other locations (starbucks, library, etc)?

If you're running straight ubuntu 9.10 (gnome), you should have a wireless icon somewhere near the top right corner of your screen. when you click on it, it should show nearby wireless networks you can connect to. if the icon's not there you may have an issue with your setup. I know nothing about the macbook hardware and getting it working with ubuntu, but there's probably some good resources out there if you search this forum and/or google.

I've also found situations where it's difficult to get to the website where you authenticate if the browser doesn't redirect there automatically when you open it. One workaround is (once connected to the wireless) look at the connection info and grab the ip address of the gateway, then type that address into the browser as a url.

good luck
