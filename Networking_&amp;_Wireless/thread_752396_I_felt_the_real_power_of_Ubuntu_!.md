### Post by owoito on 2008-04-11
I would like to say thanks to the whole Ubuntu community , and a special thanks to the developers of the Ubuntu Linux.
Today in the morning was the first time I had an opportunity to really feel the power of Ubuntu Linux online. I had struggled for months to have my Parallels virtual machine connect  wirelessly to the internet  without any success(I have no wired network), and just a few hours ago, I tried VMware, and I thought I was going to have a similar problem, and to my utmost surprise, I was online after setting the network option to NAT mode.That is not the exciting news.
 The exciting news is that I was able to run Hardy Heron, and it ran perfectly okay without any problem whatsoever.I love Ubuntu linux and I would want to say a big thank you again to everyone in this community. I was like a slave to the windows OS, but now I have a choice and I love my new found OS ….Ubuntu!. With time i will completely move from virtualization to a full blown Linux OS without any trace of windows.
My system is Window vista Business 1.67 HZ, and 3GB memory.
