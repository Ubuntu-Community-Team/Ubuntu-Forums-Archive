### Post by Joeb on 2005-06-13
If you are asking if you can connect your laptop to the ethernet port on your desktop and get to the internet through the wireless port the answer is "yes."  You would need to turn on the internet sharing feature of Windows XP and you would need to use a crossover cable instead of a regular ethernet cable.

My question would be "Why?"  Most wireless routers have ethernet ports, so why not just plug in to one of those?
