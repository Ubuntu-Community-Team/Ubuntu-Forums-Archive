### Post by ubuntu1 on 2007-06-30
After using wireless network cards for some years I was becoming fed up with the poor range indoors. I decided to buy some Homeplug network over electrical mains adapters. I opted for the 85mb eConnect turbo models. Within 5 minutes of opening the boxes I had two P.C.s and a IPTV box networked. I'm getting about 65mb connections which are much faster than my wireless cards. If your having wireless problems I can recommend these.
