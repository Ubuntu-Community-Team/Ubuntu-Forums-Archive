### Post by SagaciousKJB on 2018-08-31
I was testing out an older system the other day, it kept crashing on me so I wanted to try an older kernel and loaded my thumbdrive with Xubuntu 12.04.

Wow I forgot how great this version of Ubuntu was.  I really liked the aesthetics, and it always seemed extremely fast.  Things started getting laggy for me on 14.04 but 12.04 was always super quick and snappy.  I also really liked how the log-in screen gave me the ability to choose the desk top envrionment like it use to.

Anyway it was really nice, just felt really good to be on it again and I miss it.  I wish there was a way to use it still and just update everything, but I feel like once I did that it just wouldn't even be 12.04 anymore.  Sadly it turns out the system I was trying to revive has a problem with the motherboard so I can't even play with it on that.

Anyone have any older versions of Ubuntu they miss?
