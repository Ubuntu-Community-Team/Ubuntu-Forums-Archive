### Post by Perfect Storm on 2020-11-23
Greetings,

I have released 4 icon themes for Deepin DE. They are called Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter.
The 4 seasons icon sets are based on WhiteSur, Bloom and Papirus icon set. The 4 Seasons are aimed for Deepin Linux OS and other Linux Distribution that comes with Deepin. elementary OS  is supported.
Gnome and Budgie are available for Spring and Autumn Icon sets

Feel free to request icons for applications you are using. To make thing easier add the name of the icon(s) for requested applications. It will make my life much easier than hunting the name of the different icons, thanks.
