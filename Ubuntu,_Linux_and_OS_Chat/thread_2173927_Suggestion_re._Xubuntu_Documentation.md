I came across [http://docs.xubuntu.org/1304/guide-desktop.html#files-directories](http://docs.xubuntu.org/1304/guide-desktop.html#files-directories) :
> You can add your own shortcuts by simply dragging folders to the shortcut pane. This will allow you to access important folders instantly!
It maybe obvious and that's why it isn't mentioned, but in **subsequent sessions** accessing "bookmarked" folders located in devices that need mounting will be possible only after the device is mounted. Unless the device is mounted, the shortcut/bookmark won't be visible.
