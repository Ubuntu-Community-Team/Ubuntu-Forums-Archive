### Post by QIII on 2019-09-04
From the website:

> Numbers represent search interest relative to the highest point on the chart for the given region and time. 

"Search interest" is much like the numbers on DistroWatch.  They don't mean that people are or are not using Ubuntu -- only that they are or are not searching.

The "interest" could simply mean that Ubuntu has become well enough known that people aren't searching it out.

In short:  The graph of "falling interest" can't be interpreted in any way to conclude that Ubuntu is "going down".

Fun with stats.
