### Post by craig10x on 2013-06-16
Regarding the amazon shopping stuff, you can either:  1) Remove the lens (it's listed as unity-shopping lens in the Software Center)  or 2) Simply turn it off....that's what i do...just go to System Settings, then to the "privacy" section and you will see 2 buttons that are turned ON...just turn them OFF...and you will no longer get those shopping suggestions in the dash search... :)

Having the Amazon  shopping suggestions does help Canonical a bit because that brings in some revenue from Amazon for them...but i prefer to have it off and instead send Ubuntu a donation every now and then, instead...;)

Unity and the dash runs very snappy on my laptop...but i have intel processor and graphics...intels do seem to be very linux friendly...

### Post by Copper Bezel on 2013-06-16
