### Post by ardouronerous on 2019-08-15
Hey guys, just wanted to share what I discovered.

My new second-hand laptop has a two USB3 ports and I used one of the USB3 ports to charge my iPad without installing iPad-charge and it charges pretty fast. Even though the iPad says "Not Charging," it's charging and it's pretty fast.

My old laptop from 2006 only had USB2 ports, so charging my iPad without installing iPad-charge took more than 8 hours.
