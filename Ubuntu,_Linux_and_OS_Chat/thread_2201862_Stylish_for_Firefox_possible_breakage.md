### Post by vasa1 on 2014-01-26
Because of recent changes, users of the Stylish extension in Firefox may find that some of their styles aren't working the way they did.

See [Stylish for Firefox 1.4.1b1]("http://forum.userstyles.org/discussion/39738/stylish-for-firefox-1-4-1b1") and some of the other recent threads.

Some of the issues may be fixed by including
```
/* AGENT_SHEET */ 
``` at the very top of your code.
