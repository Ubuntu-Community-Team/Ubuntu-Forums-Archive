### Post by kagashe on 2015-09-09
There is Realtek Wireless Adopter on my Lenovo G50-45 Laptop which also works as Blue-tooth device. To make the Blue-tooth work on Ubuntu 14.04.3 and 15.04 I had added ppa and installed dkms driver which works and it does not work.

I understood that the driver was included in Linux Kernel 4.2.

I downloaded the Daily/Current ISO of Ubuntu 15.10 Wily and very happy to report that Blue-tooth is working out of the box.

I could send files from the Laptop to the Android device, however, I could not send from Android to the laptop, although, I checked the relevant box in Personal File Sharing.
