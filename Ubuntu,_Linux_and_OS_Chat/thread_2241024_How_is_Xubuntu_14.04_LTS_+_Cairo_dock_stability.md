### Post by travis-falkenberg on 2014-08-26
I've been using Xubuntu/Cairo Dock for many years now.

You can't run Cairo Dock as a session from the logon screen. I simply run Cairo Dock when XFCE loads (Application Autostart in Settings). I auto hide the normal XFCE bar and use Cairo Dock down the left side of the screen.

There is a bug in the XFCE compositor which makes Cairo Dock invisible when style and some other settings for XFCE are changed. Cairo Dock is still running, just invisible. Log out and back in to get Cairo Dock back or else turn XFCE's compositor off and use a different compositor eg. compton.

Works great for me!
