For the official Ubuntu Flavors, it mostly, (but not only this), has to do with the amount of contributors to each project and how long they feel they can support each release. Another factor is how quickly upstream projects evolve, (from whence the desktop code is developed).

For the Official Ubuntu Flavors, start looking here for your answers to length of service for each:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuFlavors](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuFlavors)

Linux Mint is not an Ubuntu Flavor but, a different project that is derived from different distros' in which, Ubuntu is only only one. There were other distros they were a derivative of too. I am thinking of at least Debian. I dunno. Anyways, why not ask your questions about Mint on their forums:
