### Post by exploder on 2013-07-20
I am really enjoying 12.04 x64 on my DV6 laptop! I have grown to appreciate Unity on the laptop's 15.6 inch screen. The thing with this laptop was that is was just new enough that it had hardware problems with most distros but 12.04 has been working just great. I stayed with the 3.2 kernel and even installed the proprietary ATI drivers, everything is holding up great this time around. I added a few ppa repos and am completely satisfied with 12.04, it looks nice, preforms well and I have not had any problems with it at all.

I love rolling releases but I can certainly appreciate the LTS releases. I wanted a good length of support and 12.04 is providing just that. Looking forward to the next LTS release and how it will run. Also, looking forward to seeing the refinements the next LTS will offer.
