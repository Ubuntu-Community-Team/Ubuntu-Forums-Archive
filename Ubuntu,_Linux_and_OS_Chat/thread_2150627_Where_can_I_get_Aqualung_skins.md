### Post by Tamlynmac on 2013-06-01
Based on their [manual]("http://aqualung.factorial.hu/manual/aqualung-doc.html#id83628"):
> *All officially supported skins are shipped with         Aqualung, and installed in the system-wide skin directory         during make install.*

I did notice this statement listed in the manual under 7.1.0 Appearance:
> *You can entirely get rid of all skin settings by selecting         Disable skin support. It allows you to flawlessly         use a GTK or other theme with Aqualung.*

Hope that helps.
