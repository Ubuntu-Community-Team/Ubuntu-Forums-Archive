### Post by ian-weisser on 2023-10-31
The beta is only two weeks because it uses the original (and correct) definition of "beta": A pre-release snapshot of the finished release for the final round of community testing.

You will be able to access the 24.04 release next week at [https://cdimage.ubuntu.com/daily-live/](https://cdimage.ubuntu.com/daily-live/) . That's when synchronizing, integration, and development of 24.04 will begin in earnest.
