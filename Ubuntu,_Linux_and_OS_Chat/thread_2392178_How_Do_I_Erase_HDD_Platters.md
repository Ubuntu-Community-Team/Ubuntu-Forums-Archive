### Post by QIII on 2018-05-17
Hello!

Epoxy and screw a ring-shaped neodymium (rare earth) magnet to the end of a 3/4" hardwood dowel about 8 inches long (for leverage!).  Find a way to hold your platter down on a flat work surface. What works fairly well is a bolt with a large washer passed through a hole through your work surface and tightened snuggly.  Run the magnet around the platter (both sides) in a circular pattern several times, making sure you cover the entire surface.

An inelegant degausser that might cost you $10 or $15US to make with stuff you can find at a hardware store, but effective.  Mind where your fingers are, though!!
