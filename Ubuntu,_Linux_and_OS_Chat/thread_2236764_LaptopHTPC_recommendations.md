### Post by veddox on 2014-07-29
I bought a Lenovo G500s half a year ago, and I'm very pleased with it. Cost me 300&#8364;, and came without a preinstalled OS, so no Windows 8 hassle. It fits your first three points perfectly, if you want to look at the specs, check out [this page]("http://shop.lenovo.com/fi/en/laptops/lenovo/g-series/g500s/#tab-features"). It doesn't have a Trackpoint mouse though, and I don't know how easy it is to switch hard drives on it. I also have absolutely no experience with capturing TV from a computer, so I'm afraid I can't help you there...

About Windows 8: Microsoft does have the power to lock down computers in the way you describe, based on UEFI. However, they have not (yet) done so for normal computers, only for ARM devices.
