### Post by Tar_Ni on 2014-12-27
Firefox Hello. No download or plugin required, all you need to launch it is Firefox 34. No need of any registration, you can share call-back links to anyone using a WebRTC compatible browser (such as Firefox, Chrome, Opera) to start audio/video chat conversations.

[http://venturebeat.com/2014/12/01/firefox-34-arrives-with-firefox-hello-video-chat-revamped-search-and-chromecast-tab-mirroring-from-android/](http://venturebeat.com/2014/12/01/firefox-34-arrives-with-firefox-hello-video-chat-revamped-search-and-chromecast-tab-mirroring-from-android/)

But I do agree with /ADM, most people are using services such as Skype or Google Hangout, therefore you might've a hard time convincing friends and relatives to come over to the little-known service you prefer.. Nonetheless it's worth the try, especially if you have open-minded friends. ;)
