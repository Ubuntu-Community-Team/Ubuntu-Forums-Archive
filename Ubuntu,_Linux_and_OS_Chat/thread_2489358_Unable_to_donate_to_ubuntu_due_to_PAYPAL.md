### Post by coffeecat on 2023-07-27
This is a volunteer end-user forum. We do not solicit donations. I assume you mean that you wish to donate to Ubuntu on the ubuntu.com website. This is not the place to bring up the issue you describe - we have no control over or influence about donations to Ubuntu. I have followed a link trail from [this ubuntu.com page, where PayPal gets in your face](https://ubuntu.com/download/desktop/thank-you#contributions-form), and ended up here: [https://ubuntu.com/contact-us/form?product=generic-contact-us](https://ubuntu.com/contact-us/form?product=generic-contact-us) .

That is the best I could find. If you send a message to Canonical using that form at least someone there should be able to tell you who to contact, if that is not the right place. Maybe another forum member has a better suggestion.

Good luck. I despise PayPal myself. If a retailer insists that I pay with my credit card via PayPal, I simply find an alternative retailer and they lose me as a customer. That's not a solution to you wanting to donate to Ubuntu, but that's just to say you are not alone in wanting to avoid the ubiquitous PayPal.
