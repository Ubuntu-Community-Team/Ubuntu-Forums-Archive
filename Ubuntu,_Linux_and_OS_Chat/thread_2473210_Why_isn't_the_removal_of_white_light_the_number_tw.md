### Post by QIII on 2022-03-28
The developers of the various Desktop Environments (DEs) and Window Managers (WMs) have their own goals and priorities.  They may not have your priority in their list.  You would have to contact them for their reasons.

The beauty in the Linux world is that if you are dead set on having something be the way you think it should be in the graphical environment, you are free to develop your own DE.

Similarly, if you want your sentiment to be impossible to read, use a text color that achieves that goal.  Your choice.  But as this is a moderated forum and we expect posts to use the default colors and fonts, we might not support that.  So, as above: I'd edit it save for its delicious irony.
