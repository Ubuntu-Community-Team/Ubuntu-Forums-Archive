### Post by TheFu on 2020-10-02
About the last 18 months, email notifications from ubuntuforums have seen highly, highly, delayed. Usually a few hours, but sometimes after a few days no notification from some threads arrive.  Initially I thought it was due to my aggressive spam controls, so I whitelisted the Canonical smtp servers. That didn't help with notifications.

Switched to using the "Settings" button to see current subscribed threads with updates around that time.

Because none of the moderators have much control over the system, I never bothered to open a thread. 
There are lots of vbulletin issues. Some are usability and almost certainly tied into the SSO deployment. Most are around subscribed threads. Wish it was easier to unsubscribe from threads from a query results list, for example.  Or I'd love a toggle to automatically unsubscribe to all threads over 4 or 6 months old - or threads marked solved or threads that I didn't start.  If I started a thread, I don't mind having to unsubscribe manually, always.
