### Post by pauljw on 2018-09-11
Each DE is different.  I'm still learning my way around Budgie, but Plank (the dock) shows an indication of which apps are active and when you click on the icon it brings that app into focus.  While in focus if you click its icon it will minimize the app.  If you are using virtual desktops when you click on an active apps icon it will switch to the appropriate desktop.
