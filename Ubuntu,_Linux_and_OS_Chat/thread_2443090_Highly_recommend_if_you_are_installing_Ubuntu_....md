### Post by cwmoser on 2020-05-11
I highly recommend if you are installing Ubuntu that you have this BOOT REPAIR DISK before installing.

[https://sourceforge.net/p/boot-repair-cd/home/Home/](https://sourceforge.net/p/boot-repair-cd/home/Home/)

Its great to have handy anytime when your PC somehow fubars.
Its bailed me out several times over the years and with 20.04 when I was fooling with UEFI.
I had the impression that I could use EFI and gpt booting with my legacy BIOS where the BIOS 
does not have a UEFI selection.

Boot Repair and gparted on the Ubuntu install DVD are two tools I recommend getting familiar with.
