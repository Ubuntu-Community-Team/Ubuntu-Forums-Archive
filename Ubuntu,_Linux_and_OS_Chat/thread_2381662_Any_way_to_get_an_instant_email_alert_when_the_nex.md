### Post by kansasnoob on 2018-01-03
You could subscribe to the ubuntu-announce mailing list:

[https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-announce](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-announce)

Or you could subscribe to the ubuntu-release mailing list:

[https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-release](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-release)

I like ubuntu-release because it includes little bits of info regarding the upcoming releases.
