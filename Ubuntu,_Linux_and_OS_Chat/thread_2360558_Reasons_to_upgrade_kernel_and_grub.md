### Post by lammert-nijhof on 2017-05-07
You should suppress automatic updates in the update tab of the program 'software and updates'. I will do the same, because the same could happen to me. Normally when you have the updates displayed and then decide to update, Ubuntu will ask you whether the fstab or other configuration files should be overwritten. It seems that in case of automatic updates, they just overwrite your settings and that is BAD.
