### Post by QIII on 2014-06-01
Unity is designed for cross platform physical hardware to enable a unified experience on your desktop, laptop, web device, tablet, etc.

That it works best on newer hardware is not itself intended by design, but is a consequence of the goal above.  That may or may not be regrettable.

But it is fortunate that Xubuntu and Lubuntu, for instance, still work exceptionally well on older hardware.

Unity represents a push to the future and that means future hardware.
