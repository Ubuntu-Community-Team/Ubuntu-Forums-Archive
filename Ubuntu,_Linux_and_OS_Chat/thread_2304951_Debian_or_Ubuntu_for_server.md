### Post by deadflowr on 2015-12-01
If you really want to switch over to Full Ubuntu-based, why not simply wait until 16.04 actually gets released.
Then do a little testing with it before you go all-in.

The worse thing would be to switch over to 14.04, have it run great, then upgrade to 16.04 and find breakage or other problems.

Since jessie is well-supported right now, I see no need to rush this.
