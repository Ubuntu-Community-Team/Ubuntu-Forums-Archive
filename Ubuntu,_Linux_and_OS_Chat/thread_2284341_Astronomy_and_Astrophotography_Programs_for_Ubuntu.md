### Post by PaulW2U on 2015-06-28
SkyChart / Cartes du Ciel is a very sophisticated piece of software that I used for a couple for years and is under constant development. It might be too complex for your dad's needs though.

 It's not on the list that night_sky2 posted but you can find it at [http://www.ap-i.net/skychart/en/start](http://www.ap-i.net/skychart/en/start).

There's a lot of program and data to download but there's a page of installation instructions specifically for Ubuntu users.
