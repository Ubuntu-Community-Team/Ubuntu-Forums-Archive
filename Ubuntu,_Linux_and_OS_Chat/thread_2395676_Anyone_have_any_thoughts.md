### Post by oldfred on 2018-07-05
I also believe in clean installs. Then you also are doing a full houseclean.
And it becomes a good test of your backup procedures, so when (not if) your hard drive breaks you know you can easier reinstall & restore from backups.

My first clean install was to a second / (root), so I was able to go back and find some things I may not have included in my backups. I now always install to a new / partition of 25GB and have all data in a separate /mnt/data partition I link to in every install.
