### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2017-07-07
To start off on the right path is important . . . here are a few guidelines (although I disdain tablets, and don't use any),

>   Look for an "All Intel" device (cpu, iris-pro graphics, intel wireless),
>   Look for an OEM that supports Linux well (Dell, Lenovo are at top of heap).   I've heard good reports about the Lenovo Yoga2 in 1's.
>   Look for a distro that run Gnome DE very well (Gnome3) - - which has best support for touch interfaces.
