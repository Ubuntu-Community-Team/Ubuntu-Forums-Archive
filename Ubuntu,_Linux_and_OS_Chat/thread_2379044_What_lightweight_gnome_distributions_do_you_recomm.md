Is it already upgraded to 2GB of RAM?

I recently fixed up some old Dell Latitude 2110 netbooks with 1.83Ghz CPU's and 2GB of RAM and they run standard Ubuntu with the "flashback w/metacity" session quite well, ***until it comes to browsers***.
