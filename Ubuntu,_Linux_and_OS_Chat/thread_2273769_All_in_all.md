### Post by buzzingrobot on 2015-04-17
Hardware compatibility issues in Linux or Windows or OS X are driver issues. It's a fact of life that vendors like AMD and Nvidia devote fewer resources to Linux development, and that development must be carried on in isolation from kernel development due to the closed source policy of the vendors. 

Apple has even more control.

Nothing the Linux community can do will change that until/unless vendors choose to open source their development efforts and work cooperatively. Until then, new components will hit the market without Linux drivers, and vendors will abandon Linux drivers for older components (see AMD).
