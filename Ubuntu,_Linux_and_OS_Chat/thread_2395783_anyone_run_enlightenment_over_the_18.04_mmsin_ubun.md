### Post by Frogs Hair on 2018-07-06
The E17 packages in the repository do not provide the complete Enlightenment experience (missing modules) and the E19 PPA I used was abandoned due to lack of interest. An Enlightenment distro is the best way to go. I liked the speed and options available , but the DE seemed dated. The [Moksha]("http://www.bodhilinux.com/moksha-desktop/") DE is based on E17 and   Enlightenment is up to [E22  ]("https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8bDAWpbeAIc")  .

Edit: There is an E22 PPA for Ubuntu though not for 18.04.
