### Post by zebra2 on 2020-08-11
A question on a web page like "please include your root password" is not a "Ubuntu" virus.  I personally don't know of any virus that affects Ubuntu.  Most of the "Linux" virus codes that have been written that have been able to penetrate the linux kernel over the years have been written by college labs just to prove that it can be done. Modern virus codes are not directed at linux.  So far as the browser vulnerabilities that depend on stupidity are concerned I avoid them by sandboxing my browser and activating UFW. I'm not sure how necessary UFW is on my network but it doesn't cost anything to use it. Because the internet is profit driven it will need you to give up some info, but that is not a "Ubuntu" problem.
