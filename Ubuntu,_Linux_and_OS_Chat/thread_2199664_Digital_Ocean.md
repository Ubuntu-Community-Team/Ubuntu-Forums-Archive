### Post by moisey2 on 2014-01-15
Hi,

I'm one of the cofounders of DigitalOcean. There are a couple of blog posts and reviews regarding people's accounts being locked. In the majority of those cases it is users who are fraudulent using stolen credits cards or causing other issues. Unfortunately we can not reveal how we do our abuse filtering. If such information became public then the bad apples would just figure out how to avoid those filters and it would be a constant game of cat and mouse. When we know that an account is fraudulent we let them know and the account is closed. We have greatly improved our filtering in the past year as well as our response times so for legitimate customers that aren't trying to be abusive this is not an issue.

But if there are any other questions please feel free to email me directly, my first name, Moisey, at our website.

Thanks!
