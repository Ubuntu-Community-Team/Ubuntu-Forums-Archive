### Post by ssam on 2013-10-14
Its a bit hard to find if you are looking for it. If you go to the download page
[http://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/desktop)
and click the download button (does not matter which one), then you get taken to a donation page. You don't have to actually download it.

Also quite a lot of the projects that make the software that makes up ubuntu have their own donation pages. Eg GNOME, KDE, Linux foundation, GNU/FSF, Libreoffice, GIMP etc. Also see the crowdfunding list, for funding some specific open projects [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1946197](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1946197) (the one still open will be at the end of the list)
