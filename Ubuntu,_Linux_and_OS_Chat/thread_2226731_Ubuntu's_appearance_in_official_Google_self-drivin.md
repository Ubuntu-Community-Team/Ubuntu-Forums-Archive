### Post by cariboo on 2014-05-29
It's not really all that surprising, as Google is one of the few operating system agnostic companies out there. Canonical has had a fairly close relationship with Google for years. Google uses a customized version of Ubuntu on their consumer grade hardware search engine farm.

They allow their employees to use whatever operating system that suits that particular person, be it Window, Mac, Linux or whatever OS they want.
