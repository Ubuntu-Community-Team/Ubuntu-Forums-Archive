### Post by guiverc on 2019-12-02
[COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica]The Lubuntu Team is pleased to announce we are running a Focal Fossa wallpaper competition, giving you, our community, the chance to submit, and get your favorite wallpapers included in the Lubuntu 20.04 LTS (Long Term Support) release.

[/FONT][/COLOR][COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica]We will close this thread on February 25th, 2020 and the judging will begin. **No late submissions will be accepted.**[/FONT][/COLOR]
[COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica]
The wallpaper selection committee will select the finalists for the Lubuntu Members to vote on.

For full details, please visit [https://discourse.lubuntu.me/t/focal-fossa-20-04-lts-wallpaper-competition/566](https://discourse.lubuntu.me/t/focal-fossa-20-04-lts-wallpaper-competition/566)

[/FONT][/COLOR]
**Rules**

[COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica]There are a few rules though. Please make sure you are happy to submit your work under these rules before you post it. Posting your images here indicates acceptance of the rules.
[/FONT][/COLOR]
**Ownership**

[COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica]The images you upload must be yours. If you see an image which you know to be misrepresented please flag it and we will take care of it.
[/FONT][/COLOR]
**Size**

[COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica]Please don’t upload the full-size image to discourse. Large images will slow down this thread so please use smaller images here. In due course, we will contact submitters for access to finalist images, which should be at least 2560x1600.
[/FONT][/COLOR]
**Quality**

[COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica]As previously mentioned, the final image size should be at least 2560x1600 pixels. Images much smaller than this won’t scale up well. Equally, images with a lot of compression artifacts won’t look good. Your image won’t be accepted if the quality is low.
[/FONT][/COLOR]
**Watermarking**

[COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica]Personal watermarks should be removed. Acceptable watermarks include the “Lubuntu” name, Lubuntu logo, “Focal Fossa” release name, and “20.04” release version. Any images with other watermarks will not be accepted. Watermarks should be kept small and unobtrusive to the image.
[/FONT][/COLOR]
**License**

[COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica]Your image(s) must be licensed under the CC BY-SA 4.06 or CC BY 4.03 license. If you do not specify a license in your post then we will assume that you are licensing the image under the CC BY-SA 4.0. By entering the competition you are agreeing to these license terms.

[/FONT][/COLOR]
[COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica]There is more information available here:[/FONT][/COLOR]
[COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica][https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuFreeCultureShowcase](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuFreeCultureShowcase)[/FONT][/COLOR]
[COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica]
Get snapping & adding your submissions at [https://discourse.lubuntu.me/t/focal-fossa-20-04-lts-wallpaper-competition/566](https://discourse.lubuntu.me/t/focal-fossa-20-04-lts-wallpaper-competition/566)

[/FONT][/COLOR]
[COLOR=#203243][FONT=Helvetica]*Additional note: If your image is smaller than 2560x1600 and is so great you feel it should be considered, submit it anyway with a note on what resolution can be supplied. We may decide it’s so good we must include it as well.*[/FONT][/COLOR]
