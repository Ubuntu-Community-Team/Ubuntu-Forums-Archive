### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-07-16
At the moment no. You can see the list of currently supported devices here...
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices)

As to whether it is ever supported that's not up to Canonical, it's up to the volunteers who port Ubuntu Touch to other devices so it may or may not happen. I would guess that it's not at the top of anyone's list as it's not a very popular device, most of the effort is going towards porting Touch to smartphones from the top manufacturers.

Most of the developers hang out at [http://www.xda-developers.com/](http://www.xda-developers.com/) so you could try asking there.
