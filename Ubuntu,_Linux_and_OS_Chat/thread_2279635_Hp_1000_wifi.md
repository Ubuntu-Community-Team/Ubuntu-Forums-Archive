### Post by twin798 on 2015-05-25
I just fixed a friends old HP 1000 (Pentium) machine.  The wifi light was orange and would not connect. Nothing worked until I went into the BIOS (F10 on Boot) and reloaded/saved the BIOS defaults.  Rebooted and the Blue wifi light on F12 key went from orange to blue. Wifi works fine now. This old computer is running Linux Lite - Ubuntu 14.04.02 LTS, XFCE4
