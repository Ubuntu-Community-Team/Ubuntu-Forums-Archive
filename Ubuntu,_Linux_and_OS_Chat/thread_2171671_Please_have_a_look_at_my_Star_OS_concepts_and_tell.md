### Post by Werne on 2013-09-01
I agree with Madman, most DEs and OS' tend to concentrate to keyboard/touchscreen and little to no mouse usage. Same goes for Ubuntu's Unity and GNOME Shell, they're designed for mobile devices, with mouse being completely neglected. And that is a big minus for desktop usage, unless you use CLI in which you don't need a mouse, but they you don't need a DE either.

Basically, if you intend it to be used on desktop PCs, then it needs to have a GUI designed for it, meaning you have to get some use out of the mouse too. If you're aiming it at mobile devices (aka, tablets and phones) then I guess it's alright.
