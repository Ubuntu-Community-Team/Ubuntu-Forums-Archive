### Post by Rob_Stevens on 2014-04-05
[IMG]http://deadsynchronicity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/06-FICTIORAMA-STUDIOS-Dead-Synchronicity-Cleaning-Brigades.jpg[/IMG]

You wake up from a coma to find yourself in a refugee camp. You have no memory of who you are. The world has changed dramatically and civilisation as you knew it has collapsed.

You learn of the "Great Wave", a string of global disasters affecting energy and communications, and a new pandemic in which the sick's delirium gives them supernatural abilities, but also accelerates them towards literally dissolving in a horrific death. Worse, you begin to experience inexplicable phenomena yourself...

[IMG]http://www.deadsynchronicity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/04/20-FICTIORAMA-STUDIOS-Dead-Synchronicity-Church.jpg[/IMG]

You must try to escape the camp, find out who you are and what is happening to the world.

[IMG]http://deadsynchronicity.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/17-FICTIORAMA-STUDIOS-Dead-Synchronicity-The-Hunter.jpg[/IMG]
