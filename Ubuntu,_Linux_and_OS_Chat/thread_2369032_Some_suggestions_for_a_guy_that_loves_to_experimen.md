### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2017-08-18
357mag:    after reading your recent posts about "skinning" and Linux desktops, it's clear you are very uninformed and perhaps have been looking at the wrong websites.   Linux DE's (Desktop Environments) are very customizable and several desktops are not even remotely the same re look & feel & operation.

Desktops themes are available for many of the top 15-20 specific DE's . . . some are the traditional mouse-keyboard interface, and others use more of a modern hybrid of mouse-keyboard along with touch-screen design elements.

Google "Customizing the Linux Desktop" and look at Youtube for hundreds of examples (perhaps thousands).

In my view, the three most polished Desktops "out of the box" are Gnome3,  Elementary and KDE Plasma 5 distros.    See distrowatch.com for specifics.
