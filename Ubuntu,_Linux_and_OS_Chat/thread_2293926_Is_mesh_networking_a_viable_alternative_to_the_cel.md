### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-09-08
So that is why my phone is so warm.  Trying to create a mesh network with existing, regulated, communication services will be difficult.  Anyone remember the tethering issues from a few years ago?  You could bypass bandwidth limits by hacking your phone to tether to a laptop and get essentially "free" internet.  Except the phone companies got smart and cut off those tethering methods.

You would need to create a new type of internet using a different topology than what currently exists.  Perhaps infrared transmitter/receivers from one neighbor to the next.  Wireless mesh is problematic because of the heavily-regulated spectrum.  Finding clear bands with distance is difficult.  Maybe something with infrasound--low frequency transmitters and receivers.
