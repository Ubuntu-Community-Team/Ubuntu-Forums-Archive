### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-06-18
I don't think lack of RAM will be your main problem but the relatively slow cpu; Pentium 4 was great when it first appeared but now - - ?

You could also have some difficulty with graphics as in the days that machine was made low powered integrated graphics was the normal way of building computers.

I suggest you try **Lubuntu** first as I suspect it will be the best of the standard *ubuntu family on that hardware, but you can, of course, try them all as live systems to see which you prefer.  Don't get too concerned by the lack of operating speed with a live system; it will always be much slower than an installed OS.
