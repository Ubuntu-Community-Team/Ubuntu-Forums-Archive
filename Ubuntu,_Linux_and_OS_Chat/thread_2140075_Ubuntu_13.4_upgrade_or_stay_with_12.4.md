### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-04-29
You are aware, are you not, that Ubuntu 13.04 only has 9 months support and so does 13.10? So, a decision to upgrade to 13.04 is also a decision to upgrade to 13.10 in six months time and then upgrade to 14.04 six months after that. If you do not want to upgrade twice in the next 12 months then do not upgrade to 13.04.

I guess that you are also aware that the user experience in a Virtual Machine is very much dependent upon the physical hardware of the computer. On my machine I get a better user experience with a Ubuntu LIve Session than I do with a Virtual Machine. And a live session is nothing in comparison to a hard disk install.
