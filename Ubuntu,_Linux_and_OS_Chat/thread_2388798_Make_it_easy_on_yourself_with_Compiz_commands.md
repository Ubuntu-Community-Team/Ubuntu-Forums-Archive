### Post by sandleaf2 on 2018-04-07
Lets say you utilize Cairo Dock and place the computer to sleep (suspend mode), come back a couple of hours after, wake up the computer and Cairo Dock seems a little messed up[.]("https://www.mruniversity.com/users/128549")
Here's a simple way. Open Compiz Config Settings Manager (CCSM) and in the top left Commands under General empower that:


Employing the very best counting as F12, I set at F10, or Control Line 2 controls to kill Cairo Dock, sleep for 5 minutes then restart Cairo Dock (killall cairo-dock & sleep 5 & cairo-dock -o).


Then once you click the Disabled box by Run Control two, that corresponds to what we've already set up, if will request that you place up the keys.
Simultaneously touch controller + change + F10 then press OK.


Then later on if you wish to restart Cairo Dock, simply press Cntl+Shift+F10 and viola.
This is sometimes done such as restarting anything or conkys else in order to simplify a whole lot of things.
