### Post by robin7 on 2014-02-20
"Buggy and weird" depends a lot on hardware, I think, and on the user's familiarity with the desktop.  LXDE and Enlightenment are both "buggy and weird" on my old computer, but Xfce runs flawlessly and intuitively on the same computer (and same user).  The only way to know is to try them out.  It took me a year to pick a favorite (Xfce) after trying out Gnome, KDE, LXDE, Xfce, Openbox, JWM, and Enlightenment.  I still haven't tried them *all*, but chose Xfce for this computer after trying out several others.  Just try them and see how they fly.  Your mileage may vary, as the saying goes.
