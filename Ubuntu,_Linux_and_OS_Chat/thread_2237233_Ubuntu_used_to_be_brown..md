### Post by mcduck on 2014-08-01
I liked the brown, but it can also be a really problematic color as it depends so much on your display. The brown theme looked gorgeous on my desktop with a good monitor, but absolutely horrible on a laptop with a decent monitor but slightly blue-tinted backlight. What might be a nice and warm shade of brown turns into dirty and greyish shade on most TFT panels...

So it wasn't really even just a question if you liked the Ubuntu brown or not, but also if you could even see it looking anything like what the designers probably intended it to look like. Pretty much all other colors are far less difficult to deal with, and the typical blue/grey color scheme will look more or less the same regardless of how good or bad your display is, the blue doesn't really suffer if your backlight is slightly warmer or colder tone. Same goes for Ubuntu's current colors, they look ok even on a bad display (and even better on a good one).
