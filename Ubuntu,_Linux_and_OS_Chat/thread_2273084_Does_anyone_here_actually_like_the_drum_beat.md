### Post by deadflowr on 2015-04-12

Which one?
There are two, usually.
The first when you get to the login screen, that one goes ba boomp.
And the second you get when you actually after typing your password and enter the desktop screen. which sounds like crickets or something.

I like neither, so i usually disable the first one (dconf editor com> canonical  > unity-greeter > play-ready-sound uncheck)
and make my own desktop login sound by adding a startup application (canberra-gtk-play --file=mysound-file.ogg)
