### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2020-07-11
Wow the improvement is unbelievable. Lately I feel that my 20.04 is kind of sluggish, I thought maybe I have too many applications open, maybe the system has been up for too long, or the heat.. but turns out this thing called tracker is eating a lot of cpu and memory. Disabled it and now what an improvement!  The whole thing feels a lot smoother and faster like it was in the beginning! So far I have not experienced any bad effect.
