### Post by ian-weisser on 2015-09-06
Looking over your old thread, seems like you gave up just as you were starting to make progress.

Since hardware compatibility is determined by the kernel (not the Linux distro), and Linux distros all use the same kernel source, it's unlikely another Linux distro will work for you.
Nevertheless, feel free to try. That's what LiveUSB images are for...trying on your hardware.
