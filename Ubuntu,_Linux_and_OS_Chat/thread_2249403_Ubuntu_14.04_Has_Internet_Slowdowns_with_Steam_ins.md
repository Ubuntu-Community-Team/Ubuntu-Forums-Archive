### Post by HeartlessContrapti on 2014-10-21
After installing Steam the other night, I had noticed that YouTube videos were constantly buffering and my non-Steam games had quite a lot of lag. Even after shutting down Steam, the problems persisted, but when I uninstalled Steam the problems went away. From what I can understand, Steam seems to cause these problems, and if you are experiencing them, remove Steam until the issue is resolved.
