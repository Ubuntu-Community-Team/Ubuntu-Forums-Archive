### Post by Mike_Walsh on 2014-08-05
I'll second that about the cleaning. Laptops are notorious for overheating; they have very narrow, often quite convoluted air channels from the CPU to the outside. It doesn't take much to clog them up.

Not like a desktop tower, where you've got lots of space, and room for plenty of vents, and lots of fans to keep the air circulating nicely!

And it's astonishing how many people completely overlook keeping a 'puter clean...and then wonder why it starts to run hot.
