### Post by bravo sierra echo on 2015-04-24
Upgraded to 15.04 today, under KDE.

Generally fairly painless, everything worked out of the box except for sound and that's down to my hardware (for reasons I can't be bothered to explain, this machine has two sound cards and it chose the wrong one - easily remedied).

I love the new look - the desktop and splash screen look fantastic.

Everything seems to work a little better and smoother, which is no mean feat on this ancient hardware which I generally expect to get slower and slower with time.

I miss the screensaver (although that's not a great loss) but I really miss the ability to have a desktop backdrop that changes every so often.  Yes I can just look at my pictures if I want to, but I liked just having them as part of the desktop experience.

What are your thoughts?
