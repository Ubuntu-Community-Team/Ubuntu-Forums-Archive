### Post by papibe on 2013-08-08
Hi physman2.

The slogan:
> Linux: the choice of a GNU generation.
It's a playful phrase based on the pronunciation of the word GNU, which could be (wrongly) pronounce as:
> Linux: the choice of a **NEW** generation.
There's a very entertaining movie about the history of Linux, the GNU project and the roles of Richard Stallman, and Linus Torvalds. It is called "**Revolution OS**". I believe these days it is available on youtube.

Regards.
