### Post by montag dp on 2013-06-01
Yep, and I like it a lot.  It looks nice, it's fast, and I've found that hitting Super and being able to see all your open windows clearly makes it quicker to switch between windows when there are a lot of them open, compared to a panel or dock.  It's been very good for my workflow.

It is also pretty configurable with the tweak tool and available extensions.
