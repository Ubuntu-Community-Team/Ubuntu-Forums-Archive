### Post by TheFu on 2021-03-14
> **fikselo said:**
> Is it legal to copy movies between computers by using scp?

Maybe and maybe not. It depends on your location, the local laws there, and the type of license the movie is under. In general, no. Making any sort of copy is illegal.  Some jurisdictions allow for making 1 backup copy in the same format as the license was purchased.  Whether changing formats is legal is most definitely a local/country law.
