### Post by Mark Phelps on 2015-01-19
In the Windows world, ".exe" files are often used to hide malware because, since they are not text format, they can't easily be read to determine what's inside them.

Any site defaulting to ".exe" files should be avoided at all costs.  You won't be able to run them in Linux, and if you do download then in Windows, you're risking infecting your PC.
