### Post by matt_symes on 2015-12-27
Your ISP must be injecting HTML (or similar) into your HTTP connections, to display the popups.

They cannot do this if you're using HTTPS connections unless they can persuade you to install a certificate your browser can access.

Try installing the browser extension 'HTTPS Everywhere', from the Electronic Frontier Foundation, while you are in the process of changing ISPs.
