Hello!

Some of you I've probably "met" before, but for those of you I haven't, I'm Monica  Ayhens-Madon, the Ubuntu Community Representative and part of the  rebooted Community Team, as well as an Ubuntu member and contributor to Ubuntu MATE. I know I might not be in this part of the  community as much, but I'd like to start spending more time here, which  seems like is something that's needed. I would love to answer any  questions you all might have, listen to  your concerns, and get to know your goals and help you achieve them. And though there will be more regular outreach starting soon, and I'll be starting community office hours in a few weeks, I just wanted to say hello and that I look forward to getting to know more of you, and being a helpful resource and point of contact.

Thanks for having me, and it's a pleasure to be here!

@madhens

Welcome! And thank you for remembering that this forum is part of the Ubuntu Community. A few years ago when Canonical revamped the Ubuntu web site and provided a link to Community it did not include the Ubuntu Forum as part of Community. That was something that some of us felt grieved about. The oversight was rectified.
