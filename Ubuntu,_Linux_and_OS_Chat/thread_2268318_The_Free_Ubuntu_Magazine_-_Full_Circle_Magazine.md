I recently discovered Full Circle Magazine and find it an excellent magazine for everyone that uses Ubuntu - from the most skilled to the beginner.  It's chuck full of news, reviews, & tutorials.  Each edition is a pdf file, so you can view it on any device.  I read it on a tablet.

Check it out, it's free!   [http://www.fullcirclemagazine.org](http://www.fullcirclemagazine.org)
