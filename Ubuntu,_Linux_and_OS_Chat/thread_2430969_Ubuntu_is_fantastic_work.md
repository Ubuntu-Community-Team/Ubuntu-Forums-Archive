### Post by jetsam on 2019-11-10
Welcome to the forums and to the other side, Allan.  If you have any time to kill, you can find plenty of ways to kill said time in the open source community.

We like people who can write and debug compilers here :)

I think maybe the most amazing thing about the distros is the way they address the scale of the library dependency issues...  Yes things sometimes break and bugs do reappear, but sometimes I just wonder at the sheer audacity of making such a complex dependency tree function at all-- and then automating all the tools such that you can reliably version and release the beastie every six months and keep the show on the road...

It just wouldn't be possible at all without the internet, without open source licensing, without the contributions of hundreds of developers and end users over the years...
