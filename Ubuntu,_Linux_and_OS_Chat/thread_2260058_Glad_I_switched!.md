### Post by 77_GCSX on 2015-01-08
I had a small taste of Ubuntu 14.04 back in August of '14 [(See here)]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2240912")

Now I have moved on to our family PC. Nothing could be better!:D

I started the move by searching the web for the Linux version of software I use for video editing and found MORE than enough to satisfy my needs.

Since this install was going on a new hard drive all I had to back up was email accounts and browser settings. After the "initial" install I made a mistake somewhere (I was doing the install after being awake for about 17 hours) I had to reinstall BUT, that was NOT a PITA as it would have been with Windows.

Now, after another dumb mistake [(see here)]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2259907")  I am on my way to a much more stable system.

So far I am VERY pleased with Ubuntu and the abilities of it that I have experienced so far. I know that going from Windows to a Linux system has a learning curve,  but I am MORE than willing and capable to do the learning needed.

(BTW: My video work is just taking cam corder videos, cutting parts out and then making DVD's from the home videos)
