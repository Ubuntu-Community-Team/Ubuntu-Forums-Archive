### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2015-08-01
Amarpreet:   The question you ask is very broad and could have hundreds of different answers.

But - - here is where to start on your learning journey:

>  read about PC technologies on various websites such as these forums and many other sites 
>  watch tutorial videos on youtube and similar sites about technology.   subscribe to the channels and authors of note, such as "Eli the Computer Guy, Jay LaCroix, Matthew Moore, Infinately Galactic, AJ Reissig and many others
>  get a good 2 or 3 linux tech books (for example:  A Practical Guide to Ubuntu Linux)
>  study hard in school - taking much science and math
>  prepare to enroll at a university and specialize in in Computer Sciences (then decide on an area of Computer Science to focus on (such as security, networking, programming, system admin, hardware engineering, etc.)

The road is not easy, but if you're truly interested, and are a hard and persistent worker, you will succeed.
