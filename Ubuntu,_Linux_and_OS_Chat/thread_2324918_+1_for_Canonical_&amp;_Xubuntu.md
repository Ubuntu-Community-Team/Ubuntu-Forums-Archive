### Post by RichardET on 2016-05-17
For well over 10 years I only used SuSE/OpenSuSe;  then in 2013 I made the switch entirely to Ubuntu and I have never looked back.  I try others in VM's, such as Fedora and Leap 42.1, and I keep a standing VM available for OpenBSD, because I really respect their project, but my native OS is always Xubuntu, because with it, everything works, and I get real work done. In my view, Xubuntu is the best desktop OS available.
