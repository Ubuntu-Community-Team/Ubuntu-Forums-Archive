### Post by scoob8000 on 2023-03-23
You could run any flavor of Android, but there may be some hoops to jump thru like unlocking your bootloader.

Just be aware it is very easy to brick your device when flashing a new operating system if you are not careful.  

Spend a lot of time reading up on the XDA forums before deciding on what rom you want to run, and how to flash it.
