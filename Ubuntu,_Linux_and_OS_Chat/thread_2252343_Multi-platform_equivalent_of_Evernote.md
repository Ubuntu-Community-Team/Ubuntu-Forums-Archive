### Post by Tar_Ni on 2015-01-07
> **CybaCowboy said:**
> It doesn't *have* to synchronize with *Evernote* - I'm happy to use Google Drive or something like that - but it *does* need to support *both* operating systems.

There is Evernote web app, which can be accessed on Linux with a web browser, no client needed. Right here: [https://www.evernote.com/Login.action?targetUrl=%2FHome.action](https://www.evernote.com/Login.action?targetUrl=%2FHome.action)

That's exactly what you are looking for, you can log in to your account online on your Ubuntu laptop with say Firefox or Chrome and access your files on the go with the Android app. Your account is synchronized whatever devices you are using, since your files are stored on Evernote's servers.
