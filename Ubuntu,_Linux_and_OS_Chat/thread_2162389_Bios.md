### Post by 3rdalbum on 2013-07-15
The computer's BIOS is embedded in the computer and is not changed by Ubuntu. Enter your BIOS ("Setup" as it is sometimes referred to, when your computer has just been turned on) and choose the USB stick as the boot device.

If the BIOS wont allow you to do that, then your USB stick is not made correctly or the BIOS does not support USB booting. You can live with Ubuntu or buy a CD unit and a Windows XP CD.
