### Post by euclido on 2014-05-09
Aloha from Pahoa, Hawaii! My sister lives in upstate New York. Recently; a few months ago, she purchased a new Toshiba laptop with Windows 8 installed. She hated it more and more for obvious reasons and when her daughter tried to install Windows 7 instead, she ended up wiping the hard drive and making the new laptop useless. My poor sister, who uses her computer for her art and paying bills as well as communication with friends and family has been lost for weeks! She cannot afford to have it fixed. So... I decided to download the ISO of Ubuntu 14.04 to my desktop and burn a copy to send to her. She received it today and her daughter was able to install Ubuntu with no problems. 
My sister, who has never seen let alone used any Linux operating system is so happy! She posted on her Facebook page that "Ubuntu is stellar"!
Thank you Ubuntu! For restoring my sister's computer to perfect working order and giving her access to the world again!!!
