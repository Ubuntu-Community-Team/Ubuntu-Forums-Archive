### Post by d-cosner on 2014-02-09
I have been installing Ubuntu 13.10 for some of my co-workers lately with very good results! After one install and word of mouth recommendations, people have been asking me to install Ubuntu on their computers. I just finished up a Dell computer for a woman I work with in my department. The Dell came with Windows Vista and it was new around 2006 according to the user. The computer was blue screening a lot, so much that I could not really do anything with it.

I installed Ubuntu 13.10, everything worked out of the box and the computer runs fast and smooth. The woman that owns the computer thought that it was time to buy another computer,. The hardware seemed just fine to me though and the machine looks really nice with it's silver front bezel and bright white case. With 13.10 installed the computer seems just as sharp as any expensive Apple product I have ever seen. The Dell is all Intel and I believe it has another good 5 years of life left once 14.04 goes final and is installed on it.

I am amazed at the number of new users that are enjoying the Unity interface! I never imagined people coming up and asking if I would set them up with Ubuntu.
