### Post by exploder on 2023-07-09
Happened to come across this and just thought I would share. Installing the rhythmbox-plugin-alternative-toolbar from the repos makes it look a lot better! Also, if you are using dark mode, the legacy (GTK3} scheme auto switcher extension will make Rhythmbox use dark mode. The end result for me was the app looked a lot less out of place and looked a whole lot better! :D

Edit: Installing the "rounded window corners" extension will put the finishing touch on it.
