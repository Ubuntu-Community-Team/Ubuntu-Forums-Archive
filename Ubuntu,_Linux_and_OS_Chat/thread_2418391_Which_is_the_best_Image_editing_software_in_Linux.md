### Post by slickymaster on 2019-05-09

There's also Photopea, which is a free online clone of the popular Photoshop. It works totally online and has support for .psd, .xd, .sketch, .xfc, raw and also the most common formats like jpg, gif, etc. Although not as powerful as Photoshop, it is possible to make the simplest edits and with the advantage of not having to install anything on the PC.
