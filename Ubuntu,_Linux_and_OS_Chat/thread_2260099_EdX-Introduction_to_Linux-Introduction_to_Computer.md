### Post by Yosef_Karo on 2015-01-09
Just wanted to throw this out there for others who are new to the Linux world and the programming world like myself.

EdX offers excellent, free courses but you can opt-in for a paid, verified certificate as well; the Linux one in this case is a little pricey, but well worth it I am sure.

Just started the Linux one and they will be covering the three Linux families of Debian, Fedora and Suse.  For Debian-Ubuntu 14.04 LTS; For Fedora-CentOS; For Suse-OpenSuse.

The course just started on Jan. 05 but it is self-paced and no time limit to complete it.  Only one test-a final exam which is open book and not timed.


The Python one just started on Jan. 07 and is given by MITx.  I started it last year, barely.  All I can say, it will be more demanding than the Linux one with more tests and assignments and a final deadline.


**Sign up [here]("https://www.edx.org/course") and happy learning.**
