### Post by DuckHook on 2023-03-16
Blame Apple.

The fetish for ultra&#8209;thin ultra&#8209;light laptops started with them. Everybody tried to copy their success. But you can't have ultra&#8209;anything unless you use sheet&#8209;like, flexible, shapeable lithium polymer batteries. These can't be replaceable because they must contort themselves to fit into the guts of a laptop. That's the only way to make the laptop ultra&#8209;thin.

And once the fetish started, there was no going back. Today, due to economies of scale, flexible lithium polymer batteries cost less than the older brick technology. OEMs are not about to spend more for a technology that the general public has been conditioned into thinking as inferior.
