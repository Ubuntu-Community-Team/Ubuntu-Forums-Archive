### Post by SeijiSensei on 2015-04-28

### Post by QIII on 2015-04-28
The situation at the time was that Canonical had decided to no longer keep the sole remaining Kubuntu developer they still had in their direct employ.  That was wildly misinterpreted as Canonical removing support for Kubuntu.  

At the time, Kubuntu was the only official flavor that had a direct Canonical employee.  Kubuntu just became like all the other flavors.

That you are under the impression that Kubuntu is somehow no longer official is a testimony to the persistence and power of misinformation and urban legend.  That's unfortunate and I hope we have helped!
