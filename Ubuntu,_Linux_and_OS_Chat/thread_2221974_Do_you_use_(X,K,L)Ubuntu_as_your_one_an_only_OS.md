### Post by SeijiSensei on 2014-05-05
> **justen_m said:**
> I need something that can adjust the settings for my APC 550 UPS.

Install the [apcupsd]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/apcupsd") package. It can manage your UPS and tell other machines on your network about the device's status.  When the power goes off for an extended period of time, all your machines will shut down gracefully.  You can specify the settings for the UPS in apcupsd.conf.
