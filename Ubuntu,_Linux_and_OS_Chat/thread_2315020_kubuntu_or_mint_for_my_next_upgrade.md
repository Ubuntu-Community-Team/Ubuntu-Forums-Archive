### Post by montag dp on 2016-02-26
It depends on what you're asking.  If you are comparing Mint's KDE spin to Kubuntu, the main difference is that Mint is still on KDE 4 and Kubuntu is on KDE 5.  Personally, I like KDE 5 a lot better, but being newer it also has more bugs.

If your question is about KDE 5 (on Kubuntu) vs. Cinnamon (on Linux Mint), that's a little less straightforward.  Both are very nice desktop environments.  I suggest you keep playing with them both and decide for yourself.
