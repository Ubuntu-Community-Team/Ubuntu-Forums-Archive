### Post by ajgreeny on 2019-05-25
The last version of Edubuntu was 14.04 and as far as I'm aware there is no intention or likelihood of any newer versions being created, so I'm afraid you are out of luck.

I believe, however, that all the packages that were default installs in that flavour are still probably available in the repos so you could try to "roll" your own version or something very similar.
