### Post by Rob Sayer on 2014-08-16
You can find decent reviews at:

[http://distrowatch.com/](http://distrowatch.com/)

Though you have to keep in mind that just because someone writes a blog that does *not* mean they're actually knowledgeable.  Most of the ones linked at distrowatch are good but there are some really bad blogs out there.  Much like forums ... there's actually not that much of a correlation between the # of posts by a user and their level of expertise.

Also, the good ones are written by very knowledgeable linux geeks who don't need a lot of tech support.  So a distro that's fine for them is not so good for noobs who will probably need support.

Which is why I'd install Ubuntu on anything unless it just doesn't have the power for it.  And then it'd be Debian.
