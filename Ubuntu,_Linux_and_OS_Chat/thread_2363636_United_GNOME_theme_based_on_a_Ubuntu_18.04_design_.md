Based on a Ubuntu 18.04 design with some inspiration from Unity 8. Was tested on both GNOME 3.24 and 3.22 using Ubuntu GNOME 17.04 and Fedora. 

The theme should work with 3.20 as well but may have a couple minor issues.

The United Gnome theme is available in several versions, to include a mix of light & dark, dark & darker & light.

The Gtk and Gnome Shell themes are in the same download:
