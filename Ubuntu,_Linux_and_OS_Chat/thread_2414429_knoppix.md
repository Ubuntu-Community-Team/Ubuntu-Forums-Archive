### Post by jdeca57 on 2019-03-10
Well Knoppix and Virtualbox are different approaches (but you can install Knoppix in Virtualbox) In fact, I like knoppix because it allowed a live environment when most distributions didn't provide that and because it has a very open approach to most hardware. If it doesn't work with Knoppix, you are in for finding exotic drivers or compiling things. It's a fantastic distribution to boot and find problems and repair them. But it is meant to be that way, that's the main function. Once a year Knoppix gets an update. The following is coming shortly. In Virtualbox you can test distributions, the kind that do provide upgrades during that year.
