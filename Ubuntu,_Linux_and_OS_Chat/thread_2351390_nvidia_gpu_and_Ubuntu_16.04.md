### Post by Bucky Ball on 2017-02-02
> **pedro_rodriguez2 said:**
> I want to buy a new laptop. Not high end, not expensive, I don't really do much with it, it's basically just a typewriter with internet.

Then why the heck are you going for something with 8Gb of RAM? A HP Stream 11 would do what you want to do and they're under $300AU. 

If you want a typewriter with internet you really don't need that lot, particularly not a discreet GPU. 4Gb is plenty, probably 2Gb is enough, and if you are a touch typist, I'd be more interested in which laptops have a nice keyboard and if the wireless card will work 'out-of-the-box'. 

A typewriter with internet? Lubuntu or Xubuntu on a cheap 2-4Gb machine would do fine.

PS: That machine has an i5. For a typewriter with internet, an i3, or probably lower spec would be just fine. Up to you, but buying this is paying for something you don't need. I retired a typewriter with internet about a year ago. It had a P4 processor with 2Gb of RAM and worked just fine, and did a whole lot more than typing and surfing the net besides. You could probably pick a similar one up from a junk shop for fifty bucks. 

Good luck. ;)

PS: Don't know what site you're looking at, but that machine doesn't have an Nvidia card from factory. It has Intel HD Graphics 4000. It also doesn't have 8Gb RAM, but 4Gb. Whoever is selling this: are they saying it's new, as new, refurbished or second hand? If it's new, something's odd, because those aren't the specs. [See here for full specs]("http://www.samsung.com/us/support/owners/product/ativ-book-5-windows-laptops-np540u4e") and perhaps post a link to the site you are looking at the machine on.
