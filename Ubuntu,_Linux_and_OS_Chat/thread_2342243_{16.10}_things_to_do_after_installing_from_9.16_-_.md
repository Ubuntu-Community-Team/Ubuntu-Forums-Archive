### Post by deadflowr on 2016-11-05
First thing to do when installing Ubuntu for the first time is go to the top right corner of the top panel, where the cog icon is, open it and select Ubuntu Help.
Then read through how Ubuntu works and how to do things in Ubuntu.

Everything you do there after, is to your own tastes and means.

There are thousands upon thousands of program you can install on Ubuntu, and everyone of them has pros and cons.
It is only up to you to determine whether or not a particular program's cons are easy enough for you to live with.
