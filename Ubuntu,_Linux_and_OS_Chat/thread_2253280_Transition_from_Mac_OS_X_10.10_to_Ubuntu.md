### Post by zircon_34 on 2014-11-18
I did transition over several years and actually still use both OS. At work OSX and at home linux for fun. I used few times linux when I needed to do some programming in python, and it was way better for me using linux. I hope to switch over one day soon, but keep being dragged back for work. +macs are soo expensive and durable that it may take few years for me to sell it.

My strategy now is to build a linux box at home:p and enjoy 100% linux, it is great fun! I use my mac at work with linux as a virtual desktop, its soo fast, it does not matter much, and the battery time is great if you use a mba. 

I started to use Gimp as replacement for aperture for working on pictures, it went smooth. 
For itunes I did not really yet find a replacement, would be nice to have a store on linux to buy music or rent movies.
Libre office is ok, but during collaboration with other people I need to use word.... 

I can only recommend to use a virtual desktop, and later perhaps dual boot.
