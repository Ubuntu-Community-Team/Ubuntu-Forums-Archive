### Post by grahammechanical on 2018-01-13
In one sense you are lucky. You seem to have a TV turner card that has Linux support by the manufacturer. I found to my cost that Linux development of TV turner drivers is mostly done by volunteer developers who need tuner cards before they can even start to create a driver. And they have very little money for such things. So, if the manufacturer is only interested in the Windows OS, then it becomes next to impossible to get the TV card working. I certainly gave up trying. It made my brain hurt thinking about it.
