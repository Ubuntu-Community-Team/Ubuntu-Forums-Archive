### Post by 3rdalbum on 2013-08-11
The reason why people were trying to diagnose the slowness is because Ubuntu should not be slow on a netbook with an HDD or a decent SSD.

I use 13.04 on a bog-standard Acer Aspire One. In fact, I use it with home directory encryption. It's no speed demon but it works fine for the kinds of things you'd use a netbook for. Without home directory encryption, of course, it would be faster.

If you installed Ubuntu with full drive encryption, everything will be much too slow to bear. If that's what you did, then no wonder you're having speed issues!

But yeah, even the latest Ubuntu will work fine on a netbook. If you're experiencing bad slowdown, then it's something unusual and we could try to troubleshoot with you.
