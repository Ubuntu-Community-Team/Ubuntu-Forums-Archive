### Post by ajgreeny on 2020-10-25
Yes, you can use evolution in any of the Ubuntu family, though it may look a bit out of place in LxQT and pull in quite a lot of gtk library packages when you install it as it's a gtk application and Lubuntu-20.04, now using LxQT, is not surprisingly a qt based DE.

I did for a while make use of kmail, the email application for KDE as I used that DE a long time ago in the days of KDE-3.5 (I think that's the version I used) and kmail was very good at that time.

As far as I'm aware most of the email applications are capable of being used in business, but I suppose thst depends on what exactly are the specific abilities needed of the application.
