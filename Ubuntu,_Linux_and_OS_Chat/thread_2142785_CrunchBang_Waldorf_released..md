### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-05-06
As the title says CrunchBang Waldorf has now been officially released.

It's been available as a development release for around a year now, but since it's parent OS Debian Wheezy was officially released on Saturday it's now been promoted to stable status and official Waldorf images are now available for download.

#! is always my go to distro for lower end hardware and I even use it on a couple of new machines as well. Thanks go to Corenominal for producing a fantastic distro, I can't even begin to imagine how one man can create such a polished distro, even if he does have Debian to base it on.
