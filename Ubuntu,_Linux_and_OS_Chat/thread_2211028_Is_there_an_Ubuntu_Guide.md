### Post by David_Wright on 2014-03-14
This might not be quite what you are looking for:

[http://linuxcommand.org/tlcl.php](http://linuxcommand.org/tlcl.php)

A downloadable pdf of c500 pages that runs through all the commands.  It sounds very daunting, but is actually surprisingly readable (although I've stalled at about 2/3 through).

What it doesn't do is direct issue/problem solving - a search engine and the forums are best for that by far.

It is good for gleaning a general understanding of what commands there are, what they do and how to combine them.
