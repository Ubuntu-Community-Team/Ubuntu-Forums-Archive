### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2014-01-07
Ubuntu studio ships with XFCE, at least when I installed 12.04 it did.

I installed it on my old music computer (AMD Sempron with only 512mb of RAM).  It was pretty sluggish and limited, but I made a few recordings with 8-10 tracks and a few effects without too much trouble.  Uppling the RAM to 1024mb helped.  (that particular motherboard never really worked quite right, though -- had a lot of crashes).

If you install another flavor of Ubuntu, make sure you switch to the low-latency kernel.  I would just go with Ubuntu studio over those other flavors, though; by the time you configured it like US, it'd be just as heavy.

Other options are Musix, AVLinux, or 64Studio.
