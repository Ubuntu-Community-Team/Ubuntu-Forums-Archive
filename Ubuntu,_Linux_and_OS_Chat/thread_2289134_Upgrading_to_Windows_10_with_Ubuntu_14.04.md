### Post by irv on 2015-08-01
Just to let everyone know things went well this morning.
Windows 10 just came out and I did the upgrade. I was running Ubuntu 14.04 and Windows 8.1 on a Asus i7 laptop and this morning I went ahead and did the upgrade to 10. I have my laptop set to boot into Ubuntu first then Windows. During the upgrade the computer had to boot several times so the only thing I had to do was select Windows from the grub.
It took a little over an hour and everything works as it did before the upgrade. It left my grub alone so Ubuntu boot first as before. I am in Ubuntu right now but plan on checking out 10 to see how I like it.
Now just because I have Windows 10 I don't plan on moving away from Ubuntu. I am one of those users that like the best of both world.
I believe both Windows and Ubuntu are good OS' and I like them both and will use them both. I am not a MS hater. As long as I have a laptop that will run both of them, I will keep on keeping on.
Have a great day, I sure am having one sitting outside on a beautiful day looking out at the Mississippi River while writing this.
