### Post by montag dp on 2016-04-17
You probably could, but you'd have to make a theme.  It's not like some other DEs where you can just adjust it with a slider or a value in a config file.  The themes use vector graphics (SVG files).  Not that I know anything about making them.  Before someone asks, I believe the reason for this approach is that you can make really good looking graphics if you know what you are doing, but you sacrifice some of the easy configurability that you could have with other methods.
