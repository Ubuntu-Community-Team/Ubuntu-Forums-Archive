### Post by Sesshomurai on 2017-12-10
Hi,
  Just sharing my impressions of trying 17.10 after using 16.04 for a long time. Ugh. Not impressed after a quick feel over.
I run mine inside virtualbox and there are a host of separate issues with that (OS doesn't come with make to build guest additions, etc as before). Flat out doesn't work. This is off topic though.

First thought was it doesn't look as crisp and clean as 16.04. Rather "klunky" and chunky.

Here's some advice. "If it's not broke, don't fix it!"

Hopefully, 18.04 will be better and not, yet again, change everything, remove useful things, and just put a search bar in our face and say "Here you go! Great OS!"
