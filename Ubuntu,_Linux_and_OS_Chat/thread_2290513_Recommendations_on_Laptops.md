### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2015-08-15
When the System76 Galago UltraPro first came out . . . there were some component and build issues.   The keyboard and trackpad had design issues.    That's been corrected.    

Pros:   super fast, great non-glare hi res screen, light, good keyboard, very good trackpad, easy setup & maintenance.
Cons:   all plastic unit (no metal at all), very thin screen (top panel) (but that's not been an issue with me at all (just close the lid when carrying.)

Recommended config:   get at least 8 GiB ram (16 better), 120 GiB or better SSD, 500 GiB hdd (7200rpm) (extra ram useful for IT student . . use for VM's)

Solid performance - runs daily between 4 and 12 hours . . . no issues at all.
