### Post by bitsnpcs on 2019-02-24
Hello,

when I first used Linux, I often seen "Ctrl+Alt+t" on websites and forums. I admit I actually used to type the + characters as part of this, in my very first attempts. Then I also tried typing and releasing each key in the sequence.
Later when I wrote a tutorial for other beginners, or answered a post on a forum I added the line below, it makes it more beginner friendly, and teaches the beginner what it  means, it saves the beginner time and frustration, and it saves the  forum poster and tutorial writer a lot more time over the life of the  post/tutorial explaining -

Hold down the Ctrl key and the Alt key at the same time, while they are held down press the t key. (a.k.a Ctrl+Alt+t)
