### Post by d-cosner on 2013-12-29
Have to agree, you can save in .doc format with LibreOffice. My wife downloads movies all the time on the Ubuntu 13.10 computer I set her up with. I am not sure she gets legal downloads but she just watches them and deletes them so I just keep quiet about it. There are some things that are Windows and Mac only but it does not especially bother me any more. I figure that most of the viruses, spyware, adware and so on are Windows only and I will gladly miss out on all that!

I tend to think about all of the good things associated with using Linux. Steam coming to Linux is great, just look at all of the great game titles we are seeing now! Look at all the choice we have as Linux users, we can choose our desktop environment, we have thousands of free applications to choose from and we are free to distribute all of it to our friends and family! Just look at all of the terrific forums we can join! Seriously, I have met some of the finest people on Linux forums and I value the friendships I have made.

There is always things that we are going to be excluded from and I say let them have them. We have so much more to be thankful for than any Windows or Mac user will ever know. Heck, many of the very same applications we enjoy are free for users of those proprietary systems, so the people that write these apps for us are pretty darn generous! My wife gets frustrated about some things being only available for Windows but she has not been a Linux user very long. I have always had a positive attitude, I fell in love with open source on first sight. Just the idea of not having to enter key codes and register my OS was enough to make me switch over.

I can still remember having to call Microsoft because I had changed my hardware too many times. I remember being accused of installing Windows on more than one computer and I do not miss any of it. We have the very best of everything! Live and let live, who cares about proprietary stuff anyway? Enjoy your freedom and all that is freely given to you.
