### Post by coffeecat on 2015-06-06
If you go to the main ubuntu.com website, click on &#8220;Get Ubuntu Desktop&#8221;, then click on the download button for 14.04.2, you are taken to the voluntary PayPal donation page. You can opt to decline any contribution by clicking on the &#8220;Not now, take me to the download&#8221; link. After a brief delay, your browser will prompt you to save the ISO file, upon which the download starts. The PayPal page does **not** reappear. The "Thank you for downloading Ubuntu Desktop" webpage does **not** change during the download. There is no "recycling" of the PayPal request.
