In case any of you visit the [Lubuntu discourse]("https://discourse.lubuntu.me/") or other Lubuntu infrastructure, and find it in *read-only* mode, or *temporarily down*, don't worry about it. We're in the process of ***moving***, and so it won't be at its best for the new few *hours/days*.

If you have any questions, we're still on IRC or can be found using our normal [links]("https://lubuntu.me/links/").  Alternatively you can ask here, and myself or others will attempt to help
