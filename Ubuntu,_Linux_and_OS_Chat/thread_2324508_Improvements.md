### Post by buzzingrobot on 2016-05-14
Here on Unity 16.04, gedit's menu, including undo/redo, are in the global menu bar, by default, as usual.

In Fedora 24 Beta, gedit's menu is via a hamburger icon, while undo/redo is via a right-click on the targeted text.

I don't always agree with Gnome's design choices.  But, there are plenty of old-school designs and tools available for the subset of users that will continue to want them. The vast majority of people are now being exposed to *personal* computing (what they do on devices they buy, rather than what they use at work) via phones and tablets. Their expectations and their demands are being shaped by that experience, not the experience of using DOS or Windows in 1990.  Like it or not, the fallout is going to be profound.
