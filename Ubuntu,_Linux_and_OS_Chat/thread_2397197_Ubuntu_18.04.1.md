### Post by rrnbtter on 2018-07-26
Greetings,
18.04.1 working here just fine with today's proposed updates.

Note: I suggest that you don't enable Proposed unless you are comfortable with a reinstall and have a current verified to work ISO available. It is also helpful if you have package archiving enabled and know how to downgrade a package. FYI

Note: It's not as bad as they make it out. I have been using the dev version with proposed enabled for nine years and I have only reinstalled about thirty-five to forty times in that period.  Not bad I would say!
