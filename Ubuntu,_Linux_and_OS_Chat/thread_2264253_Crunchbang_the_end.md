One can. ;)

I've never been a fan of Crunchbag's visual style:  Too dark and grey for me. I have used it, tho, and it's a well thought out and well-packaged Openbox spin on Debian.  Debian fans seemed to see it as a quick way to get a usable desktop that didn't stray far from the "Debian way".  Newbies seem to approach it as a less frustrating intro to Debian.
