### Post by Bucky Ball on 2015-07-23
*Thread moved to **Ubuntu, Linux and OS Chat**.*

I'm sure people will give you lots of opinions. :)

I just bought a HP Stream 11. Xubuntu 14.04 LTS installed in EFI and 'just worked' and is no problem powerful enough to torrent, type documents (I'm running Libreoffice Writer), email, web and more. The Stream 13 probably has a more powerful processor, same amount of RAM. (I have an [Odroid]("http://www.hardkernel.com/main/main.php") that is probably powerful enough to do that if you have a screen. I use one as a media server hooked up to the TV running Lubuntu and torrent with Transmission.)

If all you want to do with it is what you outline in your post, I suspect the HP 13 would be more than adequate after my experience with the 11. I have no experience with the other machine. If you were using a full-blown Ubuntu, perhaps that as it has 4Gb RAM. Xubuntu won't need more than 2Gb for what you're doing. Lubuntu is lighter again. Good luck. :)

PS: Be aware that the Stream 11 has no ethernet socket and a 32Gb eMMC card, no HDD. Good idea to check on the 13 if those thing are important to you. The wireless worked 'out of the box'. Made it impossible to install the mini.iso though.
