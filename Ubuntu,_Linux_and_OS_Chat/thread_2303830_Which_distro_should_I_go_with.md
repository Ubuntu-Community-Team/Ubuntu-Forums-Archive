Loads of support? Ubuntu.

Your question has been asked literally hundreds of times and is one that, in the end, only you can answer by identifying exactly what it is you want to do with your computer and then finding out what flavour/spin suits the purpose. 

You might start by having a look at the many threads here which ask the same question as you have. Also, check the Kali and Tails sites (and the size and support of their communities). Burn install media and run them from that to 'test drive' without installing. That is generally the best and easiest way to answer 'which distro should I go with?'

Good luck.
