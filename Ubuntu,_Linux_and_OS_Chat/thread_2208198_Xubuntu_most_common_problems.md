### Post by sudodus on 2014-02-28
These problems are common at the Ubuntu Forums, not Xubuntu-specific, but general for linux

1. Problems with ***graphics chips/cards***, where the built in drivers don't work well and or where the proprietary drivers are needed. (Sometimes they work, but not always).

2.  Problems with ***wifi chips/cards***, where the built in drivers don't work  well and or where the proprietary drivers are needed. (Sometimes they  work, but not always).

3. Problems to ***install linux***: to download an iso file and make an install CD/DVD/USB drive and to make the computer boot from it (beginner level).

4. Problems with dual boot of new computers with ***UEFI*** and Windows 8. ***Boot-Repair*** is a useful tool (advanced level).

5. Problems with dual boot of middle-aged computers with MSDOS partition table and ***four primary partitions*** (advanced level).

6. Problems to ***read from and write to external drives*** (beginner level).

*. I suggest that you write something about ***backup***, the advantage of regular backups instead of more or less hopeless rescue actions, when a drive is damaged. Those problems are so serious, that they are too common even if they are not among the most common problems.

*. And I suggest that you write something about security. See for example this link [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BasicSecurity](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BasicSecurity)
