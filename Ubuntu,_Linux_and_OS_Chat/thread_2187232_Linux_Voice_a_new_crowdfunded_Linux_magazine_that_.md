### Post by okachi on 2013-11-11
Hi everyone!

Firstly, apologies if this is the wrong sub-forum to post in. Some of you may remember me from Linux Format magazine (and Linux Answers before that!)

You might have heard that most of the editorial team of Linux Format has recently resigned, as well. I've been working with those who left, and now we're making a new and different kind of Linux magazine, called Linux Voice. It'll be different in three key ways:


[LIST]
[*]We'll give 50% of our profits back to the community: to developers, events, groups that protect our online freedom
[*]We aim to freely license our content (eg under the Creative Commons license) after 9 months
[*]We'll be free of shareholders and boards of directors interfering with the mag
[/LIST]

We have many years of experience in making Linux magazines, but we need funding to get it off the ground. Please join us on [http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/linux-voice/x/5259230](http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/linux-voice/x/5259230) to learn more, and thanks very much for reading!

Cheers,
Mike (a long-time Xubuntu user :-) )
