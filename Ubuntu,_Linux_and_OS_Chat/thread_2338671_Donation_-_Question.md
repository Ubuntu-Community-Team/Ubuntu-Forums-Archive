There's no set amount. It's up to the various Ubuntu projects, teams and flavours to apply for funds as and when they feel appropriate to do so.

I don't have a definitive link for you but Michael Hall, a Canonical employee and Ubuntu Community Council member, releases regular reports to inform us of where the community funds have been allocated. 

[http://mhall119.com/2014/05/community-donations-funding-report/](http://mhall119.com/2014/05/community-donations-funding-report/) is one of his regular reports.

See also [https://community.ubuntu.com/help-information/funding/](https://community.ubuntu.com/help-information/funding/) which states that you must be an [Ubuntu Member]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Membership") to apply for funding.
