### Post by deadflowr on 2015-06-13
super + number (0-9) to launch or move focus to which ever app is listed in that spot.
ctrl+alt+arrow to move around workspaces
ctrl+alt+shift+arrow to move focused windows around workspaces.
alt+F1 and then arrow keys to manually navigate the launcher.
super + w to use scale option, to switch focused apps.
super+ s to switch workspaces.
alt + tab or alt + ~ to invoke app switcher.
alt to invoke the hud.

And super key held down to see the shortcuts I forget...

To be fair though, I use a mouse more than my keyboard.
