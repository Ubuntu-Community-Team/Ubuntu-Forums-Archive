### Post by ckrzen on 2016-11-08
Google Chrome installations in Ubuntu 16.10 do _NOT_ add entries to apt sources.list.d directory for automatic updates.


SEE:  [https://bugs.chromium.org/p/chromium/issues/detail?id=660145](https://bugs.chromium.org/p/chromium/issues/detail?id=660145)

AND:  [https://github.com/keybase/client/commit/7894c1f57f3ca5ecb3b5fc87e4496769103b7fd4](https://github.com/keybase/client/commit/7894c1f57f3ca5ecb3b5fc87e4496769103b7fd4)

for possible *** WORK-AROUNDS *** till fixed by upstream.



Upstream is aware of the issue and working to correct in a near future release:

[https://bugs.chromium.org/p/chromium/issues/detail?id=663069](https://bugs.chromium.org/p/chromium/issues/detail?id=663069)
