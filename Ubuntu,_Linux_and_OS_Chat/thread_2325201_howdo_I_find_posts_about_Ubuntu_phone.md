### Post by coffeecat on 2016-05-20
Well - you posted in the [Ubuntu Phone and Tablet](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=460) sub-forum which is where you'll find support requests for the Ubuntu Phone. But since the tagline section for that sub-forum says:

> Support for Ubuntu smartphones, tablets, and any mobile devices running Ubuntu.

And since this is not a support request, I've moved this to ***Ubuntu, Linux and OS Chat***, one of the discussion sections. I could have moved this to Forum Feedback & Help, but you are more likely to get feedback about Ubuntu phones and tablets here. 

As far as finding out how satisfied people are with the Ubuntu phone, a forum is not really the place to gauge satisfaction for anything accurately. Most threads here are about problems, so the forum as a whole gives a skewed impression as to how reliable Ubuntu and Ubuntu devices are. Users tend not to ask for help when everything is working to their satisfaction! And human nature being what it is, you are more likely to encounter moans and whinges than glowing testimonials in the discussion areas.

Another place you might want to keep an eye on is the [ Mobile Technology Discussions](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=426) sub-forum. Threads about Ubuntu devices are in a minority there, but I do see a recent "we love the Ubuntu Tablet" thread, which slightly counters what I say about moans, whinges and glowing testimonials! :)

For what it is worth, I am very satisfied with my Aquaris Ubuntu phone, mainly because I did not have unrealistic expectations of a phone OS where the range and choice of apps is more limited than with Android or iOS. I certainly find Ubuntu on a phone far less irritating than Android.
