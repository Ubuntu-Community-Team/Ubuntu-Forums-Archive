### Post by vasa1 on 2017-03-11
That's been removed in Google Chrome 57.

> To sum it up:

chrome://plugins is deprecated in Chrome 57.
Only Flash and the PDF Viewer can be controlled via the Chrome Settings.
All other plugins cannot be controlled anymore by the user.
Disable plugins like Flash or Widevine are re-enabled in Chrome 56 after restarts.from [http://www.ghacks.net/2017/01/29/google-removes-plugin-controls-from-chrome/](http://www.ghacks.net/2017/01/29/google-removes-plugin-controls-from-chrome/) with a link to the bug page: [Deprecate chrome://plugins]("https://bugs.chromium.org/p/chromium/issues/detail?id=615738")
