### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-10-01
At the very top of the ubuntu.com opening page is a banner with several links in it. The first link from the left is Ubuntu. The second link from the left is Community. The Community link leads to this site

[http://community.ubuntu.com/](http://community.ubuntu.com/)

Which in turn leads has a Find a Task link which through a Question and Answer session will help us find a task that suits us. One of the sections is Writing & Editing. Open that section up and keep clicking Not a good fit until you come to Translation. Click Tell me more and we get this page:

[http://community.ubuntu.com/contribute/translations/](http://community.ubuntu.com/contribute/translations/)

Regards.
