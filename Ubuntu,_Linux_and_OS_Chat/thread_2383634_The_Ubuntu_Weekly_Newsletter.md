### Post by PaulW2U on 2018-01-27
I'm posting here in an unofficial capacity to advise that unless someone steps up to take more than a passing interest in the newsletter then it will no longer be published.

For personal reasons i stopped contributing to the newsletter towards the end of last year. The few contributors that remained found it difficult to continue. The last issue was published in October 2017. I contributed to over 150 issues and during that time it was always obvious that interest in participating was waning so it as no surprise that publication eventually  ceased. 

The Ubuntu Community Council have taken an interest in restoring the weekly publication of  the newsletter but that will only happen if volunteers are found.

Should anyone want to help in UWN's future then the following links might be helpful: 

[https://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=243](https://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=243)
[https://community.ubuntu.com/t/handover-of-the-ubuntu-weekly-newsletter/3490](https://community.ubuntu.com/t/handover-of-the-ubuntu-weekly-newsletter/3490)
[https://community.ubuntu.com/t/call-for-contributors-to-the-ubuntu-weekly-newsletter/1539](https://community.ubuntu.com/t/call-for-contributors-to-the-ubuntu-weekly-newsletter/1539)
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuWeeklyNewsletter](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuWeeklyNewsletter)
[https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-news-team/](https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-news-team/)

I hope that the publication of UWN continues even though I won't be part of it.
