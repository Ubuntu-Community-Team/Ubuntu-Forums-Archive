### Post by Dennis N on 2017-04-07
I have had it installed since Oct 2015, when I decided to test out a rolling release. That installation works as well now as it ever did. I think there were a couple of small issues (not show stoppers - I don't even recall what they were) that I easily got fixed through their forum. They have a very active forum, with the Manjaro maintainers actively participating. Manjaro usually gets weekly updates, which include upgrades to the installed applications. Manjaro has access to the vast Arch User Repository (AUR) and no PPAs to mess with.
