### Post by russ8 on 2014-06-01
[COLOR=#000000]I tried multiple times to install Google ChromeCast into the Google Chrome browser running on Ubuntu but Ubuntu could not do it. Then I logged into Google on the Ubuntu computer and all my bookmarks were transferred over from my other Windows 7 laptop and.... big surprise the Chromecast broadcast icon appeared in the upper right of Google Chrome and it works! I can now broadcast any web page or video I am looking at on the Ubuntu OS acer laptop onto my Sony flatscreen TV that has the Chromecast hardware device plugged into the HDMI port. (The laptop in question came with windows vista which got corrupted and would not re-install).
