(Puts tinfoil hat on) lol

Do I have anything to hide?

Absolutely not!

I just won't empower those who infring on our privacy. I understand nothing and no OS is absolutely secure, but using ubuntu and deleting accounts from all the major vendors and boycotting their websites is a first step to saying "Your business and privacy practices are unacceptable!"

Sorry, I'm rambling....:D

### Post by coldraven on 2013-06-29
I use these ad-ons for Firefox:
NoScript (you already have it)
Ghostery (gives you lots of info on the trackers that it is blocking when you have to let NoScript "Temporarily Allow")
RefControl (Fakes the site that linked you to the site you're on. You can have fun by creating "strange" links or just leave it in default mode)
AdBlock Plus ( for obvious reasons)

I don't fake the User Agent because I want sites to know that there are Linux users out there.
I only use Chromium for sites that won't work in Firefox and make sure that I delete all history afterwards.

Enjoy!
