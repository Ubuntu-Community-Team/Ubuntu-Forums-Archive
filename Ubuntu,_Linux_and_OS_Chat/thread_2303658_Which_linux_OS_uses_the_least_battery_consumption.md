### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-11-21
> The battery consumption is around the same

The hardware will use whatever power the hardware uses. You may find that the manufacturer has already tried all the hardware tricks he can think of to extend battery life because battery life is an important consideration in making a purchasing decision for many people. And battery type and size will be reflected in the purchase price of the device. 

So, it comes down to user working practices. Or, having a second fully charged battery available to swap in. Or doing your web browsing in a place where there are mains sockets that you can plug into.
