### Post by vasa1 on 2017-03-18
Those of us using google-chrome who prefer to have their web pages with dark backgrounds had to put up with flashes of white when pages were loading or when changing to another tab or opening a new tab. This issue has been around [since 2008]("https://bugs.chromium.org/p/chromium/issues/detail?id=311"). However, the latest google-chrome-unstable version (59.0.3043.0-1), seems to have fixed things so that the background color set by users is used right from the start *in most cases*.

Just mentioning this for users of dark themes with google-chrome or chromium-browser. If we're lucky, the fix may even make into google-chrome-stable 58.
