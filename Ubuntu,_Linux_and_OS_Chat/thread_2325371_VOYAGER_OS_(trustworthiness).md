### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-05-21
They have a smart looking web site but they provide a minimal amount of information.

The fact that the distribution is built on Ubuntu/Xubuntu does not mean that it is officially recognised as being in the Ubuntu family. It is not officially recognised. So, it would not be supported on this forum except in the Other OS Support & Projects>Ubuntu/Debian based section of the forum.

I am not saying that the developers of Voyager cannot be trusted. I do not know if they can or cannot. But not being directly supported on this forum is the reason why it is not mentioned much on this forum. Of course, it could be that the users of voyager have somewhere else to go for support. The web site is not very helpful in the matter of finding support.
