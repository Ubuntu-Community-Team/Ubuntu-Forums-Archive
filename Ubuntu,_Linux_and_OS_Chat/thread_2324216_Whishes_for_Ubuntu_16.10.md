Nothing that you wish for has any importance to me.

I am happy for the developers to use their skills and time improving Ubuntu. I am happy to accept the direction that Ubuntu is going in. I see many benefits to Ubuntu users in the future. 

If developers of Windows applications cannot see any benefit from porting their applications to Linux, then, more fool them. It is not right to ask Linux programmers to port Windows applications to Linux.

I already have root or administrator privileges. I already get a warning if I try to modify any system folders and files when working as a standard user.

And as for your third wish, it sounds like something that could be a dangerous tool for those who do not know how to use it. Based on my experience of trying to give help support on this forum, I have become firmly convinced Ubuntu already gives too much power to the user.

"A little knowledge is a dangerous thing." A misquote from Alexander Pope. "A little learning is a dangerous thing." Too many people do too many things after doing to little research. A quote from me.
