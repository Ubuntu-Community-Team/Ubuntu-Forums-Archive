### Post by Lars Noodén on 2015-06-12
+1 for Lubuntu, specifically 14.04 LTS.  

I have that on an EEE 701 and it runs well enough.  It's the applications that are going to be the hogs and slow things down and there's not much to do about that since they are mostly the same across distros.  The GUI, however, can be made even lighter if you run plain Openbox which is part of Lubuntu.  Or if you like fvwm-crystal (FVWM with modifications).  I added new RAM to mine and that helped a lot.
