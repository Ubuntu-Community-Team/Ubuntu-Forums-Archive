### Post by ajgreeny on 2018-09-07
I'm not sure why this is but if you go to the LO website at [https://www.libreoffice.org/download/download/](https://www.libreoffice.org/download/download/) it appears as if the 6.1 version, though not described as an alpha or beta version, is still at an early stage of development, and is suggested as for use by tech enthusiasts, early adopters and power users; 6.0.6 still appears to be the version for users who need some stability.

Even the pre=releases ppa [https://launchpad.net/~libreoffice/+archive/ubuntu/libreoffice-prereleases?field.series_filter=bionic](https://launchpad.net/~libreoffice/+archive/ubuntu/libreoffice-prereleases?field.series_filter=bionic) has 6.1 for cosmic only, so it seems likely that you will have to wait for a while to get 6.1 in the fresh ppa that you use.
