### Post by bill47 on 2015-02-10
I started out my compter life, such as it is, back in 1984 with CP/M on a Kaypro 2X which came bundled with WordStar and sister MicroPro software plus MBASIC. I was able to get the GOTO, FOR/NEXT, IF/THEN, etc. down pat to the point that I wrote a program to analyse the aerodynamics of a propeller and then design a better mousetrap... er, propeller.
