### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-05-11
What did you boot up? Kubuntu 15.10? The 26 week development cycle of 15.10 has only just begun. Each release of the members of the Ubuntu family is built upon the code of the last release.

15.10 is still very much 15.04. Any future visual differences between releases will depend upon the intentions of the developers. Kubuntu has just made a very visual change to the look of the desktop. I suppose for the Kubuntu developers the next 26 weeks will be mainly a period of refinement.

There was a Kubuntu session at the recent Ubuntu Online summit. That may give indications of what is to come in Kubuntu.
