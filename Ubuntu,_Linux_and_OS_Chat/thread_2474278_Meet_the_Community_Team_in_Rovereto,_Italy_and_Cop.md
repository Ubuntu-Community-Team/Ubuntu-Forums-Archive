Hello all!

The Community Team will be at the [Linux Application Summit ]("https://linuxappsummit.org/")this week in Rovereto, April 29 and 30. If you're local, or not too far away, we'd love to meet you! Besides attending some great talks, and chatting to us at the booth (we'll have stickers!), there's two social events on Saturday - and one is a Jammy Jellyfish release party! That will start at 7:30 PM on Saturday, and go until 9:30 PM, and we'll have cake, beverages, music - all the party musts!

Secondly, we'll also be having a community day and dinner in Copenhagen, Denmark after the product sprint on Saturday, May 7. We'll be doing the Viking Ship Museum in Roskilde around noon CET, and then having dinner in Copenhagen after returning to the city. We have a very handy [form to fill out]("https://forms.gle/a4zC6nrm3epmNTqJ8") where you can say which part you want to attend, and give us more info on your food requirements and preferences.

Let me know if you have any questions, and hope we can see some of you there!
