### Post by GG_Crew on 2013-08-01
Thank you, Ubuntu folks, for the recent "forums are offline and change your password" email.  Specifically, thank you for sending a plain-text email without any hyperlinks or embedded images!

I get lots of phishing emails that masquerade as a "change your password" notice, and they always contain obfuscated "click here to update your password" links instead of clearly presenting the site URL.  The emails also frequently have a linked image with a unique address, so the bulk mailer (or spammer) can tell if the email account is active.

The fact that your email didn't have any of that gobbledygook lent it a lot of credence, and my respect for the Canonical Sysadmins went up a notch.  In an age when everybody seems to want to track everything their users do, it's nice to see a team that just wants to share information (without tracking who sees that information).
