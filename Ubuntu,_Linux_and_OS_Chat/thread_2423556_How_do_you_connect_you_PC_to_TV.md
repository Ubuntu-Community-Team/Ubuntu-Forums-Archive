### Post by mastablasta on 2019-07-26
> **linuxyogi said:**
> 
TV as monitor. I am using VGA to connect the TV.

you plug in the cable to your VGA PC, then you plug the other end to TV. make sure the plugs fit into the hole. VGA goes to VGA, DVI to DVI, DP to DP, and HDMI to HDMI. you might need a VGA to HDMI or VGA to DVI if your TV doesnt have VGA plug. if you have VGA-VGA cable you can also buy an adapter. that's it. if PC monitor is not working you shoul dbe bale to switch to the other monitor (if it's not automatically activated) by using the DE shortcut. in Kubuntu this is winkey+p

once you get picture, you can set the TV as "primary monitor".
