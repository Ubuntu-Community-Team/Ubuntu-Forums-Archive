### Post by obaid on 2010-02-13
Mr angelvert, looks like your ubuntu either falls to single mode or the gdm doesn't start, probably can't fix the screen resolution.

when you are prompted for username and password, enter the username and password and login, while typing the password, it wont appear, because it is shadowed. just type your password and hit enter.

when you loggin, type:
> sudo Xorg -configure
This will auto configure your screen resolution, video card, mouse and keyboard.

if it couldn't find your screen resolution, you will need to edit /etc/X11/xorg.conf
