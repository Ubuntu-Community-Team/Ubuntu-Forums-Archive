***Ubuntu Moblile PC Co-operative. (2 Draft updated after the Canonical's failed Ubuntu Edge Campaign)***

**1. The Objectives.**

1.1 To be run by Ubuntu Community to promote of the new Ubuntu Mobile PC.
1.2 To be leading Mobile PC designer and seller of the new Ubuntu/Android/Linux Mobile PC.

**2. The immediate Activities.**

2.1 Propose new design of Mobile PC
2.2 _*Pilot productions in similar style of Ubuntu Edge campaign*_, but base on communities opinions and demands.

***Minimum fall back of the Products which will be asked for funding in 2.2 will be.***
1. Ubuntu Era 4.5: Ubuntu Edge Clone for Mainboard with 4.5&#8221; Corning Gorilla Glass, 4GB RAM, 16 GB SD, Li-Bat. Minimum fall back will be Ubuntu OS Nuxus 4 $ 400-450 x 20,000 pcs
2. Ubuntu Era 7: Ubuntu Edge Clone for Mainboard with 7&#8221; Corning Gorilla Glass, 4GB RAM, 16 GB SD, Li-Bat. Minimum fall back will be Ubuntu OS Nuxus 7 $ 300-350 x 20,000 pcs
===============
[SIZE=4]The following is "some ideas from friends" please feel free to express your ideas and concepts.[/SIZE]
[I]If community decide to have identical 1st UBUNTU ERA to the failed UBUNTU EDGE
 then it means we would have real UBUNTU EDGE.
I will be one of the happiest member in the coop to support that too.[/I]

**The first proposed Ubuntu Mobile PC called &#8220;Ubuntu ERA&#8221;**
1. Open Service.
2. Open Hardware.
3. Open Source Ubuntu Touch Mobile PC OS.
4. Open Design.
-------
**1. Open Service.**
The Ubuntu ERA will be assembled in such a way that it can be serviced easily by DIY capable user. The Ubuntu ERA will be design as the Desk Top PC, in which you can buy them in kits or as spare parts and assembled the whole Mobile PC your self. 

For example, it would comprise of a screen, a phone chassis frame & back lid, an all in one mainboard or a mainboard with Radio module or power module, a battery. 
Anything went wrong with your mobile, DIY Users can borrow friends Mobile PC and swap spare parts to see which component is not working and do Internet order for spare parts.

Anyone can service them, do not depend on &#8220;Closed Service&#8221; with expensive repair charges.

**2. Open Hardware. Hardware Upgradeable.**
The Ubuntu ERA will have as many options as the demands (with Economy of Scale) identified. Since it is Open Service and components can be picked, changed, upgrade, and enhanced by user as new models become available or as the users ability to pay for higher priced parts. The selection process of parts should consider cloning ideas of the mass productions top brands, so spare parts would be cheap for available everywhere (mainly for the Screen and Batteries). Except the models which are clones of our own Ubuntu Edge models.

For the first proposed Mobile PC 2013 or 2014 models. 

Ubuntu ERA &#8211; 16 GB storage.

Mainboard (or Motherboard), and chipset should be, &#8220;closed to&#8221;, cloned to Ubuntu Edge.
So minimum modification to Ubuntu Edge OS to Ubuntu ERA.
CPU quad core 1.2 GHz (as minimum) two price, mid speed and fastest CPU at the time.
2 GB or 4 GB RAM

**2 types of mainboards ??**

1st model, *Ubuntu Edge Clone* and/or with lower specifications, 4 GB RAM, 16 GB storage, lower grade of cammerras.

2nd model *with Extra features*  more than Ubuntu Edge, as samples below.

Sim card 2 slots

External micro sd card 2 slots (min. capable of 64 GB if not 128 GB each slot)
**REASONS** 1. Cheaper model with 8 or 16 GB internal storage, definitely need more storage
                 2. If something wrong with internal storage, you need 2 SD cards to run unmodified Android OS, or may be for Ubuntu too, to emulate internal and external SD. If you  have only 1 external SD, you have to hack the OS in order to get some programs working, and to do multi partitions on the SD, which for many are too complicated.

Micro USB 2 slots. (1st slot charging, while 2nd slot is for Keyboards or expansion)
FM Radio / TV??  3G/4G may be with Radio module slots.

If possible we will have removable CPU/with CPU socket and RAM expansion 1 slot for upgrading and in case internal RAM Died.

**Screen sizes**,  from 4.5&#8221;, 5', 7&#8221; or 10&#8221; should be able to use Ubuntu Edge mainboard and OS.
Option we hope it will be compatible with Samsung's screen, since cheap spare parts will be available every where.

Since we will try to have 4.5&#8221; model to be Ubuntu Edge Clone, the frame and the main board will be cloned from Ubuntu Edge, Safire Screen hopefully will be available too, it will be option for upgrade as well as standard model options.

Battery would be available in 2 models, 
Ubuntu Edge clone battery, Silicon-anode Li-Ion battery,
as well as may be Samsung S4 or Samsung Note compatible  Li-Ion battery.
Adapter could be possible so we can use at least 2 types of Batteries for the hand set.
And there may be 2 batteries in one mobile PC 7&#8221; to extend the hours.

**3 .Open Source Ubuntu Touch Mobile PC OS.**

Ubuntu touch will be the priority to use in our Mobile PC and will aim for the cloning of the Ubuntu Edge OS. Though being Linux, other Linux Distributions will follow into the Mobile PC later and we have to leave that open for future.

Because there would be many other mainboard features that Ubuntu Edge may not contain. So we may need modifications of the OS. The projects coordinator would need to negotiate with Canonical for the cost of each handset as if we are commercial firm wishing  Canonical's support on the products.
We have to find out if we do these phone on non profit basis, will this issue stand.
Third-party license fees, from http://[www.ubuntu.com/phone/backed-by-canonical]("http://www.ubuntu.com/phone/backed-by-canonical")
"Canonical has agreements in place with third parties and industry organizations to include codecs for common media formats. In addition, we manage access fees for services that provide channel listings or additional information on the content being viewed. Commercial engagements around Ubuntu devices with Canonical cover these fees and related items, such as subscriptions, royalty collection fees and regulatory broadcast fees."

Or it could also be the communities' modified Ubuntu Touch OS which will be left Open source and let each person download those codecs as in Ubuntu OS.

**4. Open Design.**

The Design of the Mobile PC will be open for communities. 
There will be forum and web base project management, so members can vote and finally do fund raising similar style of [http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/ubuntu-edge](http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/ubuntu-edge).

**4.1 Designs for Low Income Family and Education for Kids.**

The Design should also be focusing on the low income family especially in the Third World countries.
The Mobile PC should be designed with long life cycle and low cost.
We could combine free open source OS and mass productions spare parts as the main objectives to lower the cost as much as possible, and the cheapest distribution networks, such as through co-operative connections or NGO connections as well as governments and international organizations.

Emphasis should also be put on to &#8220;Edubuntu&#8221; Mobile PC OS in these low cost Mobile PC to enhance Education for youth in Third World countries. So families' income would be wise spent on one device with multi benefit for their kids.

**4.2 Design to compete with existing mobile OS in the open market.**

This is the best time to launch &#8220;Ubuntu Desktop OS&#8221; to the world, through the Mobile PC device.

Windows lost the mobile battles to Android and IOS.
Windows 8 is trying to launch another war, this time with Desk Top Interface similar to Unity Ubuntu,
that have both mobile device and desktop interface to be the same. 
But you need to buy 2 device or 3 device and of course 2-3 software licenses to use Windows 8.

It is only our Ubuntu Mobile PC is aiming to be &#8220;One Device Do Them All&#8221; for communities,
multi tasks mobile phone, notebook and even Desktop PC, multi devices in one, and free software licenses.

Samsung has successful sales by using strategy many models in every segment of the Markets.
We should follow similar strategy, have lower end and mid range prices to introduce Ubuntu Mobile PC.
And leave the top range to Ubuntu Edge. 

***********
It may be possible to put all the effort to use Ubuntu Touch on Nexus 4 and Nexus 7 as Canonical has started but not finish yet. And help promoting sales using them as the first generation of Ubuntu Mobile PC for the time being until first Ubuntu ERA is born or before the Ubuntu Edge production in 2014 (which I still have hope after the failed campaign it may be revived by Canonical)
Could we agree to buy Nexus 4 and Nexus 7 quad core with &#8220;Ubuntu Edge OS/Android&#8221; installed from the factory?
May be Canonical can help negotiate that for our co-operative to get the first product launch.
And we use these models to start selling for &#8220;Pre Ubuntu ERA Sale Campaign.
As well as to start testing public response to the Ubuntu Touch in the field.

With 10,000 &#8211; 20,000 of orders, $300-400 each, from Ubuntu co-operative may achieved that and would stimulate to other players to really take on Ubuntu Touch OS to their future mobile phone, 
and may possible to raise 3-6 million dollars. 
*********

4.3 Open for Green  Environment.
The objectives of the Open Design should also being GREEN, as mobile industry is polluting our environment to a very great extent, with many millions of phone thrown out each year. 
We should produce long life product cycles. And we should make this GREEN Philosophy stay behind any propose project as much as we can.
,
4.4 Design for particular interest group.
Disability must also be part of the first special Ubuntu Mobile PC, 
for example for the blind, and** voice command orientated OS**.

4.5 Design for Entertainment. 
Special specifications for ***Ubuntu ERA Stream***. With special select best Graphic Display for gaming Mobile PC. Or special sound quality for ***Ubuntu ERA Sound Blaster***. 
Or ***Ubuntu ERA TV*** with built in TV tuner.
[SIZE=3][B]
Open design to communities and let them show the demands they want and help to get those productions for them[/B][/SIZE]
[SIZE=4]Please express your opinions here, or create new thread or do what ever you could,
 to help making community driven Mobile PC Cooperative come true.[/SIZE]
