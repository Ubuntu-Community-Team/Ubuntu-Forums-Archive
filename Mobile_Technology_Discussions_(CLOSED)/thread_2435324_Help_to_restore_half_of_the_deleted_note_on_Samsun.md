### Post by bijayalaxmi1808 on 2020-03-03
Well, it depends on what you want to restore?
Or do you want to mention data recovery as restoring files?

If you have already kept a backup then only you can restore files.
Usually, it is not so easy to recover files from flash storage, unlike Hard Disk Drives.

If you really want to know how to backup and restore, here I have a guide that explains [how to backup data on Android]("https://www.cyanogenmods.org/forums/topic/backup-data-android-device-step-step-guide/").

There is something called a custom recovery in Android such as [TWRP]("https://www.cyanogenmods.org/download-twrp-recovery-android-phone/") which can take a backup of the underlying images such as the system.img, boot.img, vendor.img etc. and restore them later to get the exact snapshot of the current state of the running Android firmware, which is known as [Nandroid Backup in TWRP]("https://www.cyanogenmods.org/forums/topic/take-nandroid-backup-twrp-recovery/").

I hope this will give you an idea or at least you will get an idea on what are you looking for. :)
