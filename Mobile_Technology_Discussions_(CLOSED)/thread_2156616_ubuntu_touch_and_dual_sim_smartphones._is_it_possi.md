### Post by 3rdalbum on 2013-06-25
I don't expect the official Ubuntu Phone software will support dual-sim, at least not at the beginning. However I do expect that the underlying software will be written well enough to support both radios, and a phone manufacturer would be able to write a dual-sim dialler and messaging app easily enough. One might even appear in the Ubuntu Software Center.

Don't expect dual-sim Ubuntu Phones in the first year, but after the platform gains traction they may well appear.
