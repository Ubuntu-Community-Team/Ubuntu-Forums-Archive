### Post by Robertjm on 2013-09-11
I just got a Chromecast today, and have been playing with it by way of my Ubuntu desktop PC, Windows 7 laptop and Android smartphone. Along the way I found out that tab casting from within the Android Chrome app is NOT supported. This means there would be no plug-in or extension to connect with the Chrome app.

Though they say Ubuntu Chromium is not supported, it does work (sounds like you already know that).

To get mine working, I had to modify my IPTABLES, set up a port forwarding on my Router, and then restart my session of Ubuntu.

What's odd is that I CANNOT full screen cast Hulu through my browser tab from within Chromium. However, I can with the Windows laptop, running Chrome.
