### Post by Jordan_Roberts on 2015-11-20
As the man said, when it comes to privacy, "phones are borked". #-oEven the "Blackphone", which purports to be the ultimate in privacy for 700 bucks a pop, is actually still built on 20% Android and allows any Google Play app to be installed on it by the user, which is frankly completely insane. 

In your shoes, I would get a highly portable computer that can run [the Tails OS]("https://tails.boum.org/") , rely on wifi only, and try to make do with that. Cumbersome and not what you asked for, but as good as it currently gets.
