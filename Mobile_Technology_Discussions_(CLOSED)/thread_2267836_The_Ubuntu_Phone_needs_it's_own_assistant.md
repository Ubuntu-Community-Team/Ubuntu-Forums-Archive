### Post by ian-weisser on 2015-03-04
Ubuntu devs were looking at this back in 2013.
See the entire e-mail chain starting at [https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-devel/2013-February/036490.html](https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-devel/2013-February/036490.html)

The basic idea was that speech recognition (sphinx, Julius, or Ted) could be used to control Unity's HUD.
Since the email thread, Sphinx3, PocketSphinx,and Julius have been added to Debian/Ubuntu.
At the moment, HUD seems on the back burner, awaiting further work on Unity convergence.

Great opportunity for a few community members to start assembling the pieces, playing with it, and identifying the hurdles that need to be overcome.
