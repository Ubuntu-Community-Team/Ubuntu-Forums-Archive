### Post by Dragonbite on 2016-07-21
This is a neat idea, and one that I think would be even cooler with an Ubuntu phone.

[Andromium’s $99 Superbook turns your Android phone into a laptop (crowdfunding)]("http://liliputing.com/2016/07/andromiums-99-superbook-turns-android-phone-laptop-crowdfunding.html")

Basically it is a keyboard, mouse and trackpad in an ultrabook / laptop layout but all of the processing comes from your phone you attach with a USB cable.

That would help for those times you want a larger screen and keyboard such as during a meeting, and your (Ubuntu) phone is your primary device! Then you get home and hook your (Ubuntu) phone to your large screen and full-sized keyboard for the rest of your work!  I love it (the idea)!

[IMG]https://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=270261&d=1469130842[/IMG]

Being USB, it may even be able to bypass issues of having to have a bluetooth keyboard and mouse and display adapter.
