> [I]Looking to get a taste of Firefox OS but don&#8217;t have a spare phone to try it out on? You can get a pretty good idea at what part of the experience will be like by installing the latest Firefox Aurora release on your Android smartphone or tablet (or HDMI stick, too). Mozilla has thrown open the doors to the Firefox Marketplace, and enthusiastic testers have already begun installing apps.

You&#8217;ll need to create a Persona account first, and then grab the Firefox Aurora APK from Mozilla&#8217;s servers.
