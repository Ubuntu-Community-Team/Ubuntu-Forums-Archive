### Post by slickymaster on 2016-05-10
> **oygle said:**
> I did have a quick look at that. It doesn't mention contacts at all ??

It does only transfer your sms and mms messages, not contacts. Samsung however has an app based tool to move not only contacts but other data too, such as calendars and messages, as well as pictures and music, [Samsung Smart Switch Mobile]("https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.sec.android.easyMover&hl=en_GB"). All you have to do is install and launch the app on both phones, place them close together and follow the prompts.
