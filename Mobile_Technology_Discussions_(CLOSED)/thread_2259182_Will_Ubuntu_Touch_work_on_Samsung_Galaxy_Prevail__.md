### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-01-02
Ubuntu Touch or Ubuntu for Devices as it is now being called, will work on certain mobile devices where the OEMs have pre-installed it. It is hoped that these devices will be released for retail in the coming year. It will also work on Nexus 4; Nexus 7 2013 and Nexus 10.

Other than that, You will have to experiment yourself. There are some user developed ports of Ubuntu touch and these are at different stages of development. Your device may not be among them and it is all experimental anyway. So, consider "No" as the most accurate answer.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices)
