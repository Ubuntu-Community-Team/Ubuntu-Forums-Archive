### Post by neothethird on 2016-12-02
On [https://www.ubuntu.com/phone/devices](https://www.ubuntu.com/phone/devices) it says:
> new devices will be coming soon
while that could be just a wildcard for "We have currently no idea what we are doing", i think there's still hope.

Also, there are some devices, that can run community ports of ubuntu touch: [https://devices.ubports.com/#/](https://devices.ubports.com/#/)
If you are somewhat technical, you could just flash such a device.

Btw, the people at UBports are doing amazing work and are always looking for help. So if you have some experience in Android porting, you should definitely check that out!
