### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-11-14
Mobile phone and tablet apps. That kind. The usual kind that come out app stores on phones and tablets. At present there are not so many as in the app stores other mobile platforms but a work of work has been done to assist app developers get their apps in the Ubuntu app store.

How to obtain them?

1) Purchase a Ubuntu Phone (fingers crossed, soon to be released by two OEMS) or purchase a Ubuntu tablet (fingers crossed, to be released for retail a little later than the Ubuntu phones). Please see relevant sales publicity or marketing information at the time of release of products on the market.

2) Install Ubuntu for Devices on one of the three Nexus devices used by the developers working on Ubuntu for devices. All still experimental.

[http://developer.ubuntu.com/start/ubuntu-for-devices/devices/](http://developer.ubuntu.com/start/ubuntu-for-devices/devices/)

3) Install Ubuntu Desktop Next on your laptop or desktop machine.

[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ubuntu-desktop-next/daily-live/current/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ubuntu-desktop-next/daily-live/current/)

4) Install Unity 8 onto Ubuntu 14.04 or 14.10.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Unity8Desktop](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Unity8Desktop)
