### Post by ian-weisser on 2017-01-12
The current status is that no handset manufacturers currently have Ubuntu phones in production.
No manufacturers have announced future plans for resume Ubuntu phone production.

Canonical [recently announced]("https://lists.launchpad.net/ubuntu-phone/msg23187.html") that further significant development of Ubuntu Phone OS and features is on hold until converted from Click packaging to Snap packaging.
The announcement was technical, not strategic. 

Some have interpreted this as the quiet, unannounced end of the Ubuntu Phone project(s), others as a mere interregnum while Canonical engineers get phones working with snaps.
