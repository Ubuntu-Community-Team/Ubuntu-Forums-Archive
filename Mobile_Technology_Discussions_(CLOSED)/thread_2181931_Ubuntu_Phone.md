### Post by melchiaros on 2013-10-19
Hello and greetings to all of us.

Unfortunately the crowd funding project Ubuntu Edge has failed to gather 32 Million € for constructing a Ubuntu-Linux based smartphone of the next generation.

Anyway, the community was willing to give nearly 13 Million €, provided by more than 27000 donators.

[http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/ubuntu-edge/x/4894010](http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/ubuntu-edge/x/4894010)
[B]
Actual facts:[/B]Before the next spring/summer there will be no free Ubuntu-Linux-Smartphone, because there will be no Ubuntu Edge.

**However:** The Ubuntu-Phone operating system is already final

[http://www.ubuntu.com/phone](http://www.ubuntu.com/phone)

and working on the Samsung Galaxy Nexus as well as the Google Nexus 4.

**But:** There is no vendor, who provide the phones in a professional way. Installation, final testing, corrections and delivery. Open up the packet and have no trouble.

There is a little crowdfunding campain on Indiegogo that has the goal to provide exactly this and it needs only 2000€ for a free Linux-Phone.

It would be nice of you if you could support this. A link from your website for example. Spread the Word. Or do it directly by money.

Here is the project:

[http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/ubuntu-phone/x/4894010](http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/ubuntu-phone/x/4894010)

Best greetings
