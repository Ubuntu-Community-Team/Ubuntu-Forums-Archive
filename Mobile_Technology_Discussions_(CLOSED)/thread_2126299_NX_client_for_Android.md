### Post by dancho on 2013-03-15
Hello people,

Perhaps bVNC, my open-source VNC app with AutoX functionality, would serve your purposes? AutoX provides the same functionality as NX but uses VNC rather than a compressed X protocol. The back-end consists of x11vnc and Xvfb or Xvnc.

It's available on Google Play and Blackberry World for free and as donation-ware. Search for "bVNC" and you'll find it easily, but here are links to Google Play and BB World:

[http://play.google.com/store/search?q=bvnc](http://play.google.com/store/search?q=bvnc)

[http://appworld.blackberry.com/webstore/search/bvnc/?countrycode=CA](http://appworld.blackberry.com/webstore/search/bvnc/?countrycode=CA)

Here is a link to the instructions for AutoX:

[http://iiordanov.blogspot.ca/2012/10/looking-for-nx-client-for-android-or.html](http://iiordanov.blogspot.ca/2012/10/looking-for-nx-client-for-android-or.html)

and a link to my post about a checf cookbook I wrote to configure a shared server automatically for AutoX (overkill for personal installations, but if you're a fan of chef, why not?)

[http://iiordanov.blogspot.ca/2013/03/chef-cookbook-for-autox-like-nx-with.html](http://iiordanov.blogspot.ca/2013/03/chef-cookbook-for-autox-like-nx-with.html)

I hope you like it!
Iordan
