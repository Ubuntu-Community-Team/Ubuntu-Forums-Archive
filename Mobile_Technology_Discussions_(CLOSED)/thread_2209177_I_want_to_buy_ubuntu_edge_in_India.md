### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-03-04
The Ubuntu Edge project needed funding of $32 million as a minimum to get a production run of about 40,000 units. We needed to pledge to buy a Ubuntu Edge by pledging to pay the price in order to get one. The Ubuntu Edge will not be produced and even if it was being produced only those who pledged and allowed the transfer of the price into the account of the crowd-funding organisation would get one.

So, if the Ubuntu Edge was being manufactured right now for distribution in May or June, you would be too late to buy one. Bookmark this page. It shows where we are now.
