### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-11-16
At the Ubuntu Online Summit there was an opportunity to ask Mark Shuttleworth that very question. For Mark hosted a Question and Answer session. He would have said that decision is to be taken by the OEMs who have agreed to produce a Ubuntu phone.

Even if Mark knew the date he could not tell us because that information is covered by a confidentiality agreement. And who are we on this forum that we should know in advance of the rest of the world. Any company releasing a Ubuntu phone would surely want to give a lot of publicity to the release. They would not want the news leaked out and distorted by rumours.

[http://summit.ubuntu.com/uos-1411/meeting/22395/mark-shuttleworth-qa/](http://summit.ubuntu.com/uos-1411/meeting/22395/mark-shuttleworth-qa/)
