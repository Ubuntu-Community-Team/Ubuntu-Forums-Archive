### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-11-14
There is no such thing as Mobile Ubuntu. Only Ubuntu. At present there are no mobile devices on the market with Ubuntu pre-installed. Unless you are thinking of laptops as a mobile device but I do not think that you are thinking that.

If you wish to experiment then you can examine this wiki page.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices)

Keep in mind that the present 3 Nexus devices used as development reference devices are now being overtaken in specifications by the latest mobile devices on the market. But those 3 Nexus devices are at present the best available but I am waiting until tablets preinstalled with Ubuntu are released on the market by manufacturers. When? I have no idea and I am not going to share in spreading rumours.

Regards.
