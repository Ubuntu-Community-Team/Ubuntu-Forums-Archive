### Post by Don_Stahl on 2014-02-12
Just an aside -- I run Lubuntu-for-PowerPC on an old iBook. Not as a go-to machine, but as a way to keep the unit usable. Might consider that if you want. The install was easy, though it never has detected the onboard wifi. See [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/releases/12.04/release/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/releases/12.04/release/)

On your real questions... I have a dual-boot machine, and to me there's a certain security in having full installs. If one OS gets broken, there's another to go to. But with good thumb-drive OSes so easily available now it's really not an issue. Well, it's not much of an issue anyway. Just my preference.
