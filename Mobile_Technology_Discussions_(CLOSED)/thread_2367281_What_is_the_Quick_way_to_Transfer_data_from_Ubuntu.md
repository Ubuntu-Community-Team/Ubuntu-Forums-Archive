after connecting both devices on same network, open  your "airdroid" app on your phone and select "AirDroid Web" option on it, and see "option 2" it gives you a link like as "http://192.168.42.1:8888", note this link and paste it into any browser (chrome, chromium, firefox, or any other) on your phone or on your laptop.
