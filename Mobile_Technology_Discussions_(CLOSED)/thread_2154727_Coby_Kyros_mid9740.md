### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-06-16
At the moment the only officially supported devices are the Galaxy Nexus and the Nexus 4/7/10.

The community has already ported it to some other devices, you can find a list here...
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/Devices)

If your device isn't on the list (it isn't) then there is currently no way to install Ubuntu Touch on it.

It's very unlikely that Ubuntu Touch will ever be ported to your device as it doesn't meet the minimum hardware requirements.
[http://www.ubuntu.com/tablet/operators-and-oems](http://www.ubuntu.com/tablet/operators-and-oems)
