### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-10-21
Another point to consider is that Ubuntu Touch is also open to community development and the Ubuntu community developers have been very much involved in the development. There is space for community developers to add their apps to Ubuntu Touch. If a developer sees a need for an email client then there is nothing stopping him or her from putting it into the Ubuntu Touch app store and if they developed the app using the Ubuntu SDK and published it using "click" packaging then the approval process will take a matter of hours.
