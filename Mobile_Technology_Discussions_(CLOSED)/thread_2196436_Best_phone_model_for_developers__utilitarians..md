### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-12-31
The state of play for Ubuntu Touch

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/ReleaseNotes](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Touch/ReleaseNotes)

Ubuntu Touch is being developed for the phone and tablet OEM market. The aim is to converge the Ubuntu mobile platform code into the Ubuntu desktop platform code and not the other way around. Do not expect Ubuntu Touch to be Ubuntu on a phone. It is not. It is a phone platform that is being developed into a tablet platform. Plug in a monitor and you may get somewhere. I do not think that desktop apps will work very well on a phone display. That is not the intention of the development work.
