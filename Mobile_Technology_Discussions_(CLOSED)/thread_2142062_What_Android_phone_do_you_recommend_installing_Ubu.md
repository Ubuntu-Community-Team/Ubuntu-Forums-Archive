### Post by psyclechick on 2013-05-04
The Nexus 4 device is something to consider - and there is also an all in one rooting toolkit available for it designed for Linux.

[http://forum.xda-developers.com/showthread.php?t=1848036](http://forum.xda-developers.com/showthread.php?t=1848036)

That toolkit will work on any of the Nexus devices - have tried it with my Nexus 7 tablet and works great :)

Some more reading about rooting with Ubuntu (as ADB and Fastboot are now natively supported too)

[http://forum.xda-developers.com/showthread.php?p=35217628](http://forum.xda-developers.com/showthread.php?p=35217628)

[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wWMlU10ir3I](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wWMlU10ir3I)
