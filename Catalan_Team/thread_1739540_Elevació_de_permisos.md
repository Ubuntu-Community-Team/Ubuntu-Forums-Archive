### Post by arubislander on 2011-04-26
Hi! My written Catalan is dismal and I would not venture to offend by answering in Spanish... So I hope you can understand my English, or else someone may be able to translate.

The elevated permissions key icon you see is the result of performing an operation that needed root privileges, such as installing software or running the disk utilities or administering the network or some such thing. If left alone the elevated permissions would expire on their own and the key icon would disappear. So it's nothing to worry about.

### Post by wgarcia on 2011-04-26
Si és el que penso que és, és simplement un indicador temporal que apareix quan t'autentifiques per fer l'actualització, i per tant tens "superpoders" per una estona, i que desapareix automàticament després d'una certa estona. 

T'avisa que t'has autentificat com a superusuari al gestor d'actualització, i mentre que hi sigui si tornes a obrir el gestor d'actualitzacions no et tornarà a demanar la clau.
