### Post by papapep on 2009-01-12
Hi Ben,

First of all, let me thank you for considering our language in your development.

My answers: 

1.- ºª·$%& 

 º and ª are not necessari in catalan, as we use regular vowels for this issue.
 · The elevated point (don't know how is it called in english) is probably the most important in catalan (of this six, of course).
 $%& this three are as important in catalan as it is supposed to be in other languages. Perhaps % this is the most used of them three, as an aritmethic operand.

2.- It seems you've got almost all of them. I haven't been able to see if there are accents or not in your template. We use both "closed" á, and "open" à accents. Also we use "dièresi" ï.
