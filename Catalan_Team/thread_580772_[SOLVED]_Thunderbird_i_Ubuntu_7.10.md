### Post by Satboy on 2007-10-25
Hola,
 Ja ho he pogut solventar!!
 He editat l'arxiu **profiles.ini** i he canviat l' ordre "start with last profile" de l' 1 a 0, i ara ja se'm obre el **Gestor de perfils**.

 [General]
StartWithLastProfile=**0**

[Profile**0**]
Name=David
IsRelative=1
Path=t15bh3hw.default
Default=1

[Profile1]
Name=Satboy
IsRelative=1
Path=4uhmnbrk.Satboy

 Siau! ;-)
