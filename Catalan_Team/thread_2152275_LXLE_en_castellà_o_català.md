### Post by ronniew on 2013-06-07
this gets people all the time.... the language tool says you must drag and drop the language to the top of the list after install and click apply system wide and reboot.


in other words, install your language (*if you have trouble install a different on first like german then you language.*) , once you install you language find it in the list... it will be greyed out at the bottom probably... Click on it , hold the click and drag the language to  the top of the list.... once there highlight it.  click apply system wide and reboot.
