The Marketing Team needs your help.....

[EDIT NOTE BY JENDA: We do not need **new** material - we are just trying to collect all there is out there. Thanks]

== current needs, summarized ==

Current Marketing Materials of all kinds, preferably only for 6.06,
6.10, and non-release-specific.  Also - we're focusing on the core
distro, *Ubuntu,* rather than giving disproportionate weight to the
sister projects, although materials for these is still welcome.

== types 'o materials: ==

=== physical ===
* Posters
* Flyers
* Brochures
* Buttons
* CD stands/displays
* Stickers, to a lesser extent (we're not looking for negative attention)
* Press Release guides

=== virtual/web ===
* web Buttons
* web Banners

== in what format? ==
* PNG, JPG, SVG, XCF, ODT, etc...  anything *buntu-friendly
* gathered together into tarballs ...until we get a repo up and running
*commercial printer-friendly material is also welcome, but preferably accompanied by source files in free formats... required if under GPL 

if you are interested contact [meatballhat]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=142336"), [jenda]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=17523") or me

Thanks!
