[*]Unity 2D is going away.  If your video card does not handle 3D graphics and acceleration properly, you either need to purchase a new video card, stick to 12.04, or consider switching to a different flavor.
[*]There will be no "Alternate Install" ISOs created for Ubuntu and Xubuntu this round.  This will deprecate one upgrade option for machines with low to no bandwidth connections.
