### Post by robperry on 2007-10-18
Our Dublin, Ohio Church's Global Mission group is considering a pilot project whereby we obtain donated corporate laptops, load Ubuntu and Open Office onto the laptops, and ship them to a school in Nicaragua. 

We are currently supporting young Americans who have gone there to teach english to students and work with abused women and children (go to [http://nicaraguanfootprints.blogspot.com/](http://nicaraguanfootprints.blogspot.com/) to learn more about their efforts) .  This is NOT a missionary effort, but an humanitarian one.  

We have many obstacles to overcome both legal (import restrictions/requirements) as well as technical. 

If any of you would like to assist us is in addressing technical issues as well as configuring donated laptops please send me an email at:
