### Post by Bite_me_Bill on 2010-08-12
Hurricane Labs in Independence, OH will be holding another hack challenge on September 22nd, 2010.

> The Hurricane Labs Hack Challenge is a hacking contest open to the public held in Cleveland, Ohio. IT professionals and enthusiasts of all skill levels are welcome to participate.

You can attend the contest either to hack or to spectate. For spectators, live demonstrations of the hacks will be conducted as they are performed by participants.

Check out [Cleveland Hacks]("http://www.clevelandhacks.com/") for more information.
