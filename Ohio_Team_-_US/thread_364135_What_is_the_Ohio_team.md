### Post by hackle577 on 2007-02-18
To start participating in the Ohio Team, try reading through the descriptions of our different subteams and joining the one which best suits your skills. We have several subteams:

[LIST]
[*][_Communication Team_]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/OhioTeam/CommunicationTeam")
[*][_Web Team_]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/OhioTeam/WebTeam")
[*][_Wiki Team_]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/OhioTeam/WikiTeam")
[*][_Marketing Team_]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/OhioTeam/MarketingTeam")
[*][_Education Team_]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/OhioTeam/EducationTeam")
[*][_New User Team_]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/OhioTeam/NewUserTeam")
[/LIST]
**Concerning CDs:** Right now we are working on becoming an approved LoCo Team, which means, among other great things, that we will get 500 CDs of every new release. If you have some CDs now, or think you can get some, try looking on the Marketing Team page for more details on CD distribution. We are trying to draw up some extra materials we could distribute with our CDs so that new users are not left dangling without support. We mostly distribute CDs to local libraries, coffee shops, small computer stores, etc.

**Concerning events:** We are still working on planning a Fiesty release party for the seperate regions of Ohio (central, NE, NW, NW, and maybe SE). As of now I think that is the only event we are planning for.

Also feel free to join us for weekly team meetings. Meetings are a great way to meet (almost) everyone and see how a LoCo Team works behind the scenes. We meet on IRC (#ubuntu-ohio on irc.freenode.net) every Wednesday at 8pm EST.

Thanks for joining and happy Ubuntu-ing!
