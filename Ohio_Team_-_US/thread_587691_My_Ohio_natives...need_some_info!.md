### Post by Terc on 2007-10-23
Ok, first off, the iPhone is great, I'm still loving it (with 3rd party apps) after my first couple of months.

There are a few things you should be aware of.

You WILL NEED a Windows PC or a Mac to activate and jailbreak your iPhone, which brings up another thing

You WILL HAVE TO jailbreak your iPhone if you hope to sync from Linux apps... once they support it.... IF they decide to support it.

The iPhone will not sync in Linux right now
    There are some ways to get around this, but none of them are acceptable in my opinion.  I currently am syncing by installing ssh on my iPhone and mounting it using sshfs.  This means wireless syncing, but there are many issues.  Album art will be broken, your ip may change, which means each time you mount your phone to sync, you have to find the ip beforehand.  Also, you will need to restart the springboard each time syncing has completed before you can see the new music.  Also, (most disappointing to me) I have not found a way to get Banshee to recognize the iPhone.

I read that the VMWare team is working on fixing usb connectivity between the iPhone/iPod Touch and Windows guests, apparently they have a solution, I do not know if it is available yet.
