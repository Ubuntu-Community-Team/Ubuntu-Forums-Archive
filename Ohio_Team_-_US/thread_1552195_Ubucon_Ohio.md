### Post by Bios Element on 2010-08-13
The [Ubuntu Ohio LoCo]("http://ohio.ubuntu-us.org/") has been asked to run an Ubucon at the upcoming [Ohio LinuxFest]("http://ohiolinux.org/"). For those of you who don't know, the OLF is being held in Columbus, OH at the Greater Columbus Convention Center.

I have volunteered to lead Ubucon alongside Jon Buckley.

I've created a wiki page to help organize things here. [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Ubucon/Ohio](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Ubucon/Ohio)
Also Amber Graner has been kind enough to writeup a todo list which I've copied from google docs and posted here. [http://paste.ubuntu.com/477468/](http://paste.ubuntu.com/477468/)

Anyone intrested in helping out should feel free to contact me and I'll figure out how you can help. There's alot to be done and we need to get started right away if we're going to have things ready.

I can be reached by posting here, on IRC (BiosElement in #ubuntu-us-oh on Freenode) or you can [email]("http://www.google.com/recaptcha/mailhide/d?k=01PTG4p1jTcjXTnPQ1EAocGQ==&c=N5Q7Eo6JaQboSdKrUFyNBaegwk3920GuobvaHDNCdQ8=") me for other forms of contact.

I'll also update this post as needed.

-- William Chambers
