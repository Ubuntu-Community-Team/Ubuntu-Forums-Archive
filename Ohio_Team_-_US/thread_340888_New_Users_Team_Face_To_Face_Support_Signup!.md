### Post by TheIdiotThatIsMe on 2007-01-17
We are proud to start accepting signups for the New Users' Team Face to Face Support project. 

Face to Face Support aims to provide free local support for new or prospect users of Ubuntu, by other local users of Ubuntu. By providing free support, we are enabling others to have peace of mind knowing they have someone they can turn to for help, whether just for troubleshooting or basic how to's (such as installing software).

If you are a part of the NU Team and would like to sign your name up for supporting Ubuntu in your local area, please visit the NU Team wiki and sign your name!

And of course, if you'd like to join the NU Team and help us break new ground in support, check out our NU Team Wiki and and if you'd like to sign up for the NU Team check out the team's Launchpad :) 

NU Team Wiki:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/OhioTeam/NewUserTeam]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/OhioTeam/NewUserTeam")

NU Team Launchpad:
[https://launchpad.net/~oh-new]("https://launchpad.net/~oh-new")
