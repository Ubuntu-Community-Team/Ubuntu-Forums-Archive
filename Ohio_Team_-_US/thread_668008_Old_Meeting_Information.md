### Post by TheIdiotThatIsMe on 2007-12-20
Howdy everyone! Just a quick review on the meeting if anyone missed it.

The first topic of discussion was the team mergers. As voted upon, some of our sub-teams have been merged. The Education and New User teams have been merged into the Education Team; Communication, Wiki, and Web have been merged into Communication; and finally the Marketing and CD have been merged into Marketing. The mergers have taken place on the Wiki and Launchpad.

After the mergers, nominations took place for Ubuntu Ohio Council. The nominations are as follows:

Jacob Peddicord (jacob/jacobmp92)
John (tnseditor)
Anthony Hall (theidiotthatisme)
Michael Price (seisen)
DM (Delvien/DM|}

You may poll for their inclusion on the UOC at:
[https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-us-ohio/+polls]("https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-us-ohio/+polls")

Following nominations, was reporting on the Ohio Ubucon '08 status. We currently have sponsorship with Linux Game Publishing, as well as being in contact with ZaReason following the holidays. Canonical and Google are both beginning to be contacted as well. Everyone is encouraged to seek sponsorship from companies!

We are also currently looking for a location as soon as possible for the event. Right now Rhetoric and TheIdiotThatIsMe are currently seeking out possible places to host the event; However all are strongly encouraged to keep an eye out for places also.

As always,  you can always view meeting minutes on the Wiki for older meetings.

Thank you!

### Post by tnseditor on 2008-01-04

Summary:

      During the meeting the newly elected council-members were announced.

      John (tnseditor) volunteered for the Communication sub-team.

      Michael (seisen) volunteered for the Education sub-team.
