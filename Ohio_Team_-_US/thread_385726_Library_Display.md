### Post by steven8 on 2007-03-16
Well, I have been almost totally inactive in this group since the early going, but I've finally got something on the ball.  I contacted the local library we go to and arranged to have the month of May for a display about Linux, OSS and FSF in the case.  I am going to do some nice stand up boards with the background of linux, oss, fsf and Ubuntu and what it is all about.  I will leave disks of Kubuntu and Ubuntu Dapper with detailed sheets on how they are to be used and links to the community.

I think it will be a nice introduction to things.  The librarian woman certainly does not know about Linux.  She asked me if this was something I was trying to sell.  :)  I said, "Oh, heavens no.  Quite the opposite.  I'm trying educate people to the fact that there are great alternatives out there that don't cost an arm and a leg."

It'll be at the Portage Lake's branch of the Akron-Summit County Public Library.
