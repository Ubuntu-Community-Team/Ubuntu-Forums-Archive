### Post by tnseditor on 2008-04-03
We are planning on having at least two parties for the release of Ubuntu 8.04 Hardy Heron.  One will be at Panera Bread in Easton Center (Columbus) and the other in Lima.  I am still working on a location for the Lima event.  The parties will be on Saturday, April 26 starting at 5:00 p.m.  We are going to try to have live video streaming of both events.  A wiki has been setup at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/OhioTeam/HardyParties](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/OhioTeam/HardyParties).

If you are planning to attend or have your own party, please let us know which party you will be attending and the number of people coming with you.
