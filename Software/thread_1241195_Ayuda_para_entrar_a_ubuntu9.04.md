### Post by quixote on 2009-08-19
My Spanish isn't too good, so I can't really answer your question.  Maybe somebody here who knows the link to the spanish-language forums could provide it?

The error messages you're getting, insofar as I understand them, are very serious.  When you log in as user, you're given no rights, and when you log in to the recovery console, it can't even figure out which terminal it's supposed to be using.  

So -- if I understand correctly (best to wait for someone who speaks Spanish :D) -- the best option might be to do a clean install.  Is that a possibility?

If you have data that you'd like to save, can you boot with a LiveCD, find your hard drive, and see directories on it?  If so, you could copy the files you want to a USB or somewhere else.

I don't know if this will help, but at least it'll get your question back in the queue. :)
