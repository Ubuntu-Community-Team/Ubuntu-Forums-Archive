### Post by GerryB on 2008-03-08
I installed OpenGEU a few days ago on an old computer. It's Gutsy, obviously, but with an impressive window manager called E17. I dont' think you can install E17 on Gutsy to the extent this distro does. Once loaded, it's very fast and I like the fact that you can control almost everything with either a left or right click of the mouse anywhere on the screen. Looks very stable to me. Maybe Canonical could bring these guys in to make E17 one of the great options offered by Ubuntu for those who want a more artistic look, since it relies on the Ubuntu system anyway. I think they've done a great job in the eye candy department. This way, people could log in either with Gnome, KDE, Xfce or E17.
