### Post by pxc on 2007-06-12
I love LinuxMint! It's pretty much everything I wish Ubuntu was already. I'm installing the Cassandra beta on my laptop right now (the stable release is still downloading downstairs, lol).

Someone mentioned that their wireless doesn't work "OOTB," and this is just based off of this LinuxMint LiveCD I'm writing this from: *don't trust NetworkManager!*

This thing comes with NetworkManager and the drivers for my card (acx100--that means the acx module) are loaded correctly. NetworkManager apparently did a successful scan of my neighbors and can even assign an essid... but it can't bind the address. NetworkManager just chokes after a long time when I try to connect.

However, doing
```
iwlist wlan0 scan
```
and seeing my essid (dephawlt)
I was able to do
```
sudo iwconfig wlan0 essid dephawlt
iwconfig wlan0 #to check if it assigned the access point correctly
sudo dhclient wlan0 #assign address to wlan0
```
and successfully get a connection.
