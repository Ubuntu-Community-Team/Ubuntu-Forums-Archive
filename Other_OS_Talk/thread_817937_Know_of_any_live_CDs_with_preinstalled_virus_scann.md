### Post by pelle.k on 2008-06-04
> Seems like someone would have prebuilt something like this though.
I'm sure someone has, but for me that wasn't an alternative. I use a 1GB usb memory stick with SLAX, since it's more or less crafted for Live/USB operation. I just threw the binary packages of avast and fprot on it (some slight modifications necessary), and that was it. Simple, fast, pure.
Naturally, the best (and of course most convenient) method would be to just buy a cheap 2.5" USB HD and install regular ubuntu or whatever. I mean it's so easy, plus you get a lot of space for backing up important files or drive images before you do anything stupid. The only advantage a USB stick has, is it's size.
