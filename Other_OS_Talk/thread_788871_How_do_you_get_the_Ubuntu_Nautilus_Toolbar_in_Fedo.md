I like Fedora, and I'm running it in VirtualBox in Ubuntu.

1. Enabling the Nautilus toolbars in Fedora:

Sid

### Post by miggols99 on 2008-05-10
Simple. Just go to Nautilus and do

Edit >> Preferences

Choose the "Behaviour" tab and tick the "Always open in browser windows" checkbox. Then restart Nautilus. :)
