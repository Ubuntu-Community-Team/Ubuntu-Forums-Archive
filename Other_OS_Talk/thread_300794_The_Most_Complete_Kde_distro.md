### Post by nandasunu on 2006-11-16
I have been using Mandriva for the past week, quite happy with it. I installed the "One" edition. For me it works better than ubuntu/kubuntu because it works with all my hardware, ubuntu doesn't recognise most of my stuff like mouse, usb headset, etc. Mandriva works with it all. It also comes  with 3d drviers, xgl/aiglx & compiz ready installed, you can even use compiz on the live cd. MP3 playback works right away too...

Dowsides are that the package manager is pretty slow, community support sucks, and with compiz running its not as stable (but just with metacity seems very stable & fast).

Oh, and the default theme is super ugly (bright orange), but that can be changed...

They have a one-cd live disc/installer version (Mandriva One) which is worth a go.
