The new release of Crunchbang is available for testing:

[http://crunchbang.org/forums/topic/81001-testing](http://crunchbang.org/forums/topic/81001-testing)

For those who don't know, Crunchbang is an unofficial remix of Ubuntu+Openbox and is noticeably lighter and faster than Xubuntu (in my experience). I recommend it for someone who might be considering Fluxbuntu, but wants something more up to date and user friendly. I've really enjoyed Crunchbang 8.04 and just installed 8.10 on my eee for testing (it's a great netbook distro).
