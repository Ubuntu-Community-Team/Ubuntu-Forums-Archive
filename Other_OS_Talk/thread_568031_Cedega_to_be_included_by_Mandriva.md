### Post by AdamWill on 2007-10-08
Cedega was included in the commercial editions of Mandriva Linux 2007. It wasn't included with 2007 Spring for boring business reasons, but it's back in 2008.

For 2007, anyone who bought the commercial edition, or any Club subscriber, could get a code from us for a few months worth of Cedega updates. And yes, you can use Cedega without a current update subscription, you just...don't get updates. I imagine the arrangement will be the same for 2008.

It was a pretty popular thing with some customers in 2007. I agree it's not really a huge deal - running Windows games inside Linux isn't a *big* interest group - but there were enough people who found it useful that we got quite a few mails from people who were disappointed that it wasn't in 2007 Spring.
