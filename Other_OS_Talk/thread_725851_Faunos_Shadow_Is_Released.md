### Post by kelvin spratt on 2008-03-16
Faunos has released its 3rd release named Shadow based on the current Arch architecture. What is Faunos its a blazingly fast full KDE operating system on a flash drive that runs entirely in ram, without using cut down versions of software open office is their etc.. you get full access to all your partitions read/write,The only draw back is you need decent amounts of ram that's not a problem nowadays with ram so cheap,  On my system with 2 gb of ram first time opening of FFox is 2secs, OOfice 3secs, Gimp 3secs, Its stable and saves all changes back to your flash drive, you do need a little knowledge to set it up as with some graphic cards the drivers need downloading during install, Another feature is the ability to clone your faunos setup  unto another flash drive or hardisc providing you saved it first.
