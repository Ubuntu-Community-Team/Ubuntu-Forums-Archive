### Post by celsofaf on 2007-02-28
Well...

I haven't tried Frugalware yet, even though I have burnt their last RC iso. Since it seems their next version will come up right this March, I'll wait a few weeks more before trying. So, I cannot say about Frugalware.

I haven't read much about Aegean. From the few I have read it seems to be a custimized Arch install but yet a bit standing with the KISS principle.

Underground Desktop is CRAP! It's a custimized Arch install but did not work right on my PC. Its said to make Arch's install easy and to be "ready to use", but it simply did not work right with me. The screen resolution comes flawed, the fonts are crazy. I had much more success installing plain Arch and tweaking it just a little small bit, having no problems to use 3D, problems with resolution or fonts or whatever. Plus, UD's installer is also crap. Yes, UD is still alpha, there's much room for improvement. So let's not judge it yet!

About Arch... Arch is just Arch and I love it!

Won't cast my vote yet before I try Frugalware.
