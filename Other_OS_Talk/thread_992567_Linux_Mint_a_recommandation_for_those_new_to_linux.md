### Post by tgalati4 on 2008-11-25
Linux mintUpdate hides level 4 and 5 updates from the new user.  This lessens the risk of a non-bootable system after an update.  Level 4 and 5 updates would be things like: xorg, dbus, linux kernel, hal, etc.

Applications are mostly rated at "3" and specifically-tested programs and Linux Mint add-ons are rated at "1" or "2".
