### Post by p_quarles on 2007-12-19
I use Conky, which integrates just fine with Fluxbox. Based on the screenshots threads, I'd say that most Flux users do the same. And, it's only as intrusive as you want it to be. 

Other fluxboxish thing: idesk. This allows you to put icons on the desktop. The one that comes with Fluxbox -- fbdesk -- isn't nearly as good. I only have two desktop icons (my /home partition and my server's network share), but I think it adds quite a bit to the overall feel and functionality of the WM.
