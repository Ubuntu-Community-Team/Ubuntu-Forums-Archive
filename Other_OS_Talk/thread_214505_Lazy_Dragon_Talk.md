### Post by cjimmy on 2006-07-17
Hi , My name's cat , I make the LazyDragon live DVD .
Thanks for pointing out the spelling mistake , How stupid of me not to notice. I started LazyDragon about six months ago , I wanted to create a  slackware based installable live dvd to be used for all kinds of media creation .  Just recently a new guy 'TheOFFSet' , who I met on linuxquestions has joined me to help produce it .  So far we've released 3 ISO's , the last being the final release candidate for V.1 . So far , response has been excellent , we still need more people to report on the success of the Xgl/Compiz system with both Ati and Nvidia cards . Nvidia support has shown to very stable , though Ati support hasn't been properly tested . LazyDragon , in essence , is a clean full slackware current system + an awesome range of MIDI/Audio/Video software , 3D+online games , Xgl/Compiz X-server(Testing) , Xorg7.0 is default . Uncompressed it comes to about 6.5 gigs . The latest release has the edition of our new animated bootsplash , alternate SMP kernel ,alternate 1000hz low latency kernel and many package updates and testing additions such as Gnu-HALO .
If you have any questions at all , please dont hesitate to ask
