### Post by earos.dk on 2008-07-08
Try to boot in safe graphic mode. After you are up and running you can enable the restricted graphical driver to your GPU.

If that does not help, then you can try with different Grub boot parameters, for example acpi=force or acpi=off, which depends of your hardware.
