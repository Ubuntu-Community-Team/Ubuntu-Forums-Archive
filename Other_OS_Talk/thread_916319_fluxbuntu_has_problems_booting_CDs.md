### Post by jaqie on 2008-09-10
This is not an issue with the operating system you have, as booting from a CD is handled BEFORE the OS on your hard drive ever has a chance to even come into existence as anything but stored data.  Check your BIOS settings, and make sure it is set to boot from CD first.
