### Post by aletheianalex on 2007-10-01
I thought someone might get a kick out of this.  I dusted off some old MKLinux disks and got it up and running on one of my old powerbooks... a 1400c with 16Meg of RAM and a 133MHz PowerPC 603e processor and a 1G HDD.  I had to do some hacking to get it to install and boot, and it does not run very well, but it was really one of those "do it just to do it" things.  Anyway, it runs an ANCIENT version of KDE, which is hilarious.  Here is a picture of the desktop... sorry about the poor quality, but I could not take a screenshot since I have not gotten the networking to function:

[IMG]http://i188.photobucket.com/albums/z57/aletheianalex/1400kdemklinux.jpg[/IMG]

You can see the navagation bar at the bottom with the buttons for choosing one of four desktops, and the old Mac-like trashcan on the desktop.
