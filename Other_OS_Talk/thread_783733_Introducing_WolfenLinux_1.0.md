### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-05-06
WolfenLinux Enterprises is pleased to announce WolfenLinux 1.0. it is an Ubuntu based distribution which eliminates the need to remove Rhythmbox and OpenOffice Word. They are replaced with Amarok and Abiword. So people dont become disoriented, i have left all the Hardy Heron themes and wallpapers as is! As this is based on Ubuntu Linux, you can also use the Ubuntu Repositories. As i have noticed in the community, there is a crying need for a distro such as this.

Future editions of WolfenLinux will remove such programs as Transmission and F-spot, to be be replaced with apps yet to be determined. I will be concentrating my efforts on retaining features such as language support for most countries, the ability to right click the desktop and create new folder, the ability to have a clock on your taskbar, and many other features that you have grown to love. it is hard work, but feel this community deserves the best.

I do not have a download link yet, but be assured you will see it on Distrowatch. (it will validate this new distro)
