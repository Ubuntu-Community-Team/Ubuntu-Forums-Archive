### Post by DeadSuperHero on 2008-11-17
I personally love the IDEA of gNewSense.

However, I'm waiting for them to re-integrate OpenGL, as SGI finally revised their license to make it fully GPL-compatible.

Also, once the Noveau project supports 3D acceleration and rendering, I'll be sure to switch.

One day, perhaps.

Actually, I really like their Builder. It's a great way to demonstrate radical ideas in an out-of-the-box experience. If it can be ported to different kernels, including HURD: L4/Viengoos, then that is a success in my book.

After all, there are many GPL'ed and GNU-based apps that work as wonderful replacements.

Flash -Gnash
Photoshop -Gimp
Flash (editor) -Synfig
Garage Band -Ardour/Audacity
Maya -Blender
iTunes -Rythmbox, Amarok, Songbird, etc.
ATI -Radeon drivers
Nvidia -Noveau
xCode - emacs, vim, and a bunch of others that I can't think of at the moment.


And then there's the dozens of compilers and code bindings.

So, switching to a system such as this isn't much of a loss at all anymore.
