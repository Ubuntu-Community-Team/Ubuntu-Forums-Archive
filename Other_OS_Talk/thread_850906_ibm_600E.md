### Post by basenvironment on 2008-07-06
Debian! I feel it is easier to create a slim and light debian system than it is a arch system. I think debian is a bit more flexible as well. You should be able to find a power supply on ebay cheap or elsewhere actually - the same (or similar) power supply was used for a long while. The screens did go bad fairly often and the batteries failed as well but they were 'just right' as far as usability and size IMO. I still have my 600e(bad screen)....not to mention my even older 560z which works perfectly...and I am enjoying my new R61e as well. Difficult (but no impossible) to get sound working on the 600e, fyi.
