### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-01-26
It's an interesting idea, but I prefer the Ubuntu Netbook Launcher. The Jolicloud launcher makes the same mistakes as every other non-Ubuntu netbook launcher out there - it doesn't seem to have any provisions for working with files or removable storage! Oh great, I can search the files on my computer... but how on earth do I browse my flash drive?

That's why UNL rocks - you can open programs AND storage devices. And network shares, which is very important for me (I have my netbook plugged into my TV, so I can watch videos streamed from my desktop).

So, good try, and good that people are still thinking about interfaces for netbooks. But it needs a bit more thought.
