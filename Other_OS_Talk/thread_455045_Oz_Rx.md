download the first release now:

What is Oz Rx?

Oz Rx is the spirit of [Ubuntu]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubuntu_%28ideology%29") in action.


> Oz Rx, a customizable Linux OS optimized for the health care field; a solution for a private Dr's Office, medical practice, Hospital, Pharmacy, Optical and Dental practices. Also ideal to be utilized in the teaching environment for the health care field.Please see further release statements here:
