### Post by xarte on 2009-01-25
Never mind, found it: Had to go to System/Preferences/Appearance

Whatever the default themes were in Progex either weren't loaded right or were defaulting to a button-free look. I selected a different one and they are working properly now, buttons there to minimise and I can resize all the apps as needed. Yay !!!!!
