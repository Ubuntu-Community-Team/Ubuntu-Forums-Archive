### Post by RAV TUX on 2007-04-05
I just wanted to announce the new Oz Logo designed by .aku

.aku is a wonderful artist and Oz is proud to debut his awesome work...

Thank you .aku

Edit: very important to note some very important design details incorporated in .aku's design, he has included portions of the "Ornstein-Zernike  Equation"(OZ-equation)
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ornstein-Zernike_equation](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ornstein-Zernike_equation)

Oz is officially named after 2 Dutch Physicist Ornstein & Zernike, in the name of Oz we have memorialized both of these great scientist and what they have contributed to Physics. Here, .aku's design beautifully showcases this great work.
