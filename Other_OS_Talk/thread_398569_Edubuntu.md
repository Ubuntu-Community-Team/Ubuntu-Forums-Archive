### Post by ubuntu27 on 2007-04-01
[Edubuntu]("http://www.edubuntu.org/") is just Ubuntu with [Educational]("http://www.edubuntu.org/") software installed and "think client" ready by default.

I don't know what else could be different. Both [Ubuntu ]("http://www.ubuntu.com/")and [Edubuntu]("http://www.edubuntu.org/") uses the same repositories.
