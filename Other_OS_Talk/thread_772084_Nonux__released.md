Nonux (version 4.4) released

[http://www.nnlinux.com/en/index.html](http://www.nnlinux.com/en/index.html)


Nonux English Edition is the English language variant of the Dutch localized Linux desktop distribution Nonux (version 4.4). 

Nonux is optimized for daily use in a Small Office or Home Office but could also be an interesting alternative for someone who needs a no-nonsense but complete working environment for daily computer tasks. 

Nonux consists technically spoken of two main parts (besides the Linux kernel), parts of the Slackware Linux distribution as the base and dropline GNOME as graphical interface. The fundamental goal of Nonux is to provide simplicity, speed, reliability, security and minimal configuration. The Linux kernel is optimized for desktop use as are the installed software packages.
