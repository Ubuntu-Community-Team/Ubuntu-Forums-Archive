### Post by Redache on 2008-11-18
[PSU Calculator]("http://extreme.outervision.com/PSUEngine")

I tried to fill it in filling in some of the gaps in the info needed and it spat out 249 Watts required to run your system. However, I only put one Hard Disk Drive and didn't add any PCI cards or anything so it may not be accurate.

From what I know of Decent Power Supply brands yours does not seem to be a very good one (I could be completely wrong), and as such it might not output even close to 400 watts brand new and if its a few years old it might have dropped a lot lower.

I'd check the PSU Calculator filling in all of your system components and see what it gives you as a proper PSU calculation.

It does sound like a low power issue, which if it is I'd recommend purchasing a known, good quality PSU like something from Coolermaster, Antec or OCZ etc.
