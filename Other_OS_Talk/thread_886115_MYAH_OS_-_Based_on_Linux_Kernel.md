### Post by Sorivenul on 2008-08-11
I've tried Myah.  It's interesting, but was not worth keeping on my spare box.  From what I've read and experienced, it is essentially Slackware, or at least Slackware compatible.  The nice thing about it, as such, is that it fits on a single LiveCD.  Small software selection compared to many distributions, and as with most Slack-based distributions, dependency resolution was a problem.  The system was very stable, though, as should be expected, and had good response speed.  The release schedule is always a mystery as well, as Jeremiah is the only developer as far as I can tell.  If you have specific questions, ask away, otherwise I hope this helps.
