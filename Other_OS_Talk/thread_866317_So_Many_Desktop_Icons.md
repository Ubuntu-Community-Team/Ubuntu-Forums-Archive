### Post by darrelljon on 2008-09-19
I love lots of desktop icons but have used interfaces without desktop icons. They are called **task-orientated interfaces**. [Mezzo]("http://symphonyos.com") v1 could be installed into Ubuntu. There is also [Kuartet]("http://kuartetdesktop.sourceforge.net/") and [Ubuntu Mobile Edition (UME) Launcher]("http://arstechnica.com/news.ars/post/20080604-hands-on-with-the-ubuntu-netbook-remix.html").
Microsoft even considered using one for [Windows Neptune]("http://www.winsupersite.com/reviews/windowsxp_gold.asp").
