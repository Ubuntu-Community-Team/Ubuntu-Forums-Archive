### Post by Sorivenul on 2009-01-21
Anything at all "non-free" will not run. The gNewSense team is committed to removing even binary blobs from the kernel. All of my hardware works fine with almost every major distribution, but I can't run a pure gNewSense system because I need some of those non-free blobs and modules. Adding them in manually defeats the purpose, so I do not run it.
