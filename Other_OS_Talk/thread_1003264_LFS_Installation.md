### Post by Sorivenul on 2008-12-06
Just an additional note:

I personally suggest building LFS in a virtual environment (VirtualBox, QEMU, VMware, etc.).  This can potentially save a lot of headaches if you backup your images in case your bork your LFS system.  I only have one LFS install that I've done direct to HDD, all my others have been virtual.

You can take my advice or leave it, up to you.  Good luck!
