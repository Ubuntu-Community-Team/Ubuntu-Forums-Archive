### Post by rhebert on 2007-04-23
Hope this helps:

The sentence beginning with "Introduzca sus datos..." translates to "Enter your information to pay with a credit card or login to pay quickly and securely with PayPal"

Pais = country
Nombre = first name (as appears on card)
Apellidos = last name (as appears on card)
Tipo de tarjeta = credit card type
Numero de tarjeta = credit card number
Fecha de vencimiento = expiration date
Direccion de facturacion = payment address
Linea de direccion 1 = Address line 1

Or, on the right, if you have a PayPal account you can enter your email address (correo electronico) and password (contrasena).

That's all I can see.  Hopefully it's enough to get you started, but post again or PM me if you need any more translation.
