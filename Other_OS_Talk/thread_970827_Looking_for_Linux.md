### Post by jml on 2008-11-04
I would suggest Mepis.  A new version is in late Beta testing.  Its based on Debian uses apt-get/Synaptic for package management and it includes all of the proprietary codecs needed for full multimedia support.  It has great hardware support as well.

If you have very modest hardware, I would suggest AntiX.  Its a community supported derivative of Mepis that is designed for older computers with more modest resources.  Here is a link describing it.

[http://www.mepis.org/node/14192](http://www.mepis.org/node/14192)
