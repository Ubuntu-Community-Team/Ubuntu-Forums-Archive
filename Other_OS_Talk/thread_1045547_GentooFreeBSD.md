### Post by Sorivenul on 2009-01-20
I would go with your idea of it as an "interesting proof of concept", but would never actually use it on a production system, especially on a desktop. 

FreeBSD is on the very top of my "list", and I happen to like the installer; Gentoo is almost at the bottom. It's a personal preference, no matter how you look at it. However, combining what is, IMO, a great system, with what is, IMO, a not so great system, is not a good idea. Even something like the [Debian GNU/kFreeBSD]("http://www.debian.org/ports/kfreebsd-gnu/") project seems wrong to me, and I have high opinions of both systems.

Just my two cents.
