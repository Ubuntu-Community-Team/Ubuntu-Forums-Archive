### Post by soapytheclown on 2008-03-17
Stick with emesene, youll want to get the latest svn version as its released with updates nearly every day or so. It is THE closest you can get to windows live messenger, without having windows, its all gtk based so it matches all your themes too. AFAIK the only difference is webcam support is a bit crappy (i dont have one) and voice clips are non existent, personally my transfers on emesene are super fast compared to pidgin.

[http://emesene.org/](http://emesene.org/)

add these to your /etc/apt/sources.list

deb [http://apt.emesene.org/](http://apt.emesene.org/) ./
deb-src [http://apt.emesene.org/](http://apt.emesene.org/) ./
