### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-12-02
To anyone who wants to use Ubuntu 8.04 Alpha 1.

Please be prepared to face bugs because you may need a few cans of bug spray to get them all out;). Here are my problems:-

1) The Nvidia drivers do not get properly installed, you have to reinstall it after every reboot(Though I found a new way, have to give that a whirl).

2) The internet connection does not seem to function in Recovery Mode.

3) You cannot get a terminal using Ctrl+Alt+Fn keys, the screen just flickers and stays the way it is.

But all in all, it is a rather good and nice OS, I can't wait until the release where, hopefully, a new theme would be introduced which would deviate a little from the original Human theme:).
