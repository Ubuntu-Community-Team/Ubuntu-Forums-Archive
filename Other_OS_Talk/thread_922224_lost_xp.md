### Post by jaqie on 2008-09-17
The CD key is what makes it legal to own and run that version of windows on that computer. What you need is to call the maker of the laptop and see how much a new restore CD for that laptop will cost. You could also do something that is in a legal "Grey area" but for you would be completely legal since you have that key... is to download an XP OEM cd ISO (NOT a RESTORE CD! those are proprietary to a single laptop model!) from one of the many bit torrent sites. The key point here is that you have that key, you just need the media. Getting the media is the grey area, but you are within your legal rights to use that CD key to install that version of windows on that computer.
