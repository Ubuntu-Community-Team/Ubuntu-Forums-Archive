### Post by Lord Illidan on 2006-09-11
> **divgradcurl said:**

There are ways to earn a living.

1. Donations
2. Be sponsored by a large corporation like IBM, Google, Novell who will actually pay you to develop free as in beer and in speech software.
3. Work on free software as a hobby...and earn money from another job.

In the case of Ubuntu, Canonical backs it..and earns money from supporting it. The fact that its founder, Mark Shuttleworth is a millionaire helps too ;).

Still, you often find that some free as in beer developers quit because they have to focus more on their job. They have to eat too.
