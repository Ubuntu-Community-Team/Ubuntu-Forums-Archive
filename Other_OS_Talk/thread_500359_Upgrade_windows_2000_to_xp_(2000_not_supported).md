### Post by exploder on 2007-07-13
It is best to do a clean install from the upgrade disk. When you do the install, XP will ask for your previous OS. Just take out the XP disk and put in your W2K disk, XP will verify that you have a qualifying product. Once XP sees W2K it will ask you to put the XP disk back in the drive and continue with the install. 

Microsoft products never upgrade very well, they tend to be real buggy. If you use the above mentioned method you will have the best results.

This will work with XP Home or Pro.
