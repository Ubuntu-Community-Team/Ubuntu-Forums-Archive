### Post by jockepockee on 2008-12-14
Nuxows is a wiki based on the Mediawiki software, and is meant for Unix/Linux users who has to work with the Windows command-line interface.
It's supposed to make it easy to look up Unix/Linux commands' equivalences in Windows.

At the moment the Wiki is pretty undeveloped, but with some help it could evolve into a great resource.
If you think it's a good initiative, you're free to drop a link to Nuxows wherever you believe it could do good to speed up the process.

The URL is [http://nuxows.xkqr.org/](http://nuxows.xkqr.org/)
