### Post by twistedbydesign on 2007-04-25
So I have never really been "anti-windows" really...well...i never really felt like i was.
I actually Stumbled onto Ubuntu a few months ago while I was looking up Vista (and how cool i THOUGHT it was going to be). I did more research and worked up the courage to Dual boot and Now I rarely use windows. I LOVE Ubuntu so much....i was VERY excited for Vista....but after doing more research AND finding Ubuntu..i could care less about it..I am New to linux...so i still struggle with things...but i think it's worth it...because i just enjoy practically everything about it.

The biggest thing that makes me feel strange (actually i guess it's not strange) is that whenever I am on my Windows partition and i see that little update bubble pop up all i think is "great...how are they trying to screw me over and second guess my every move NOW".
But whenever The Update bubble on Ubuntu pops up...I instantly click on it and am anxious to see what new features or gadgets they have and don't have any doubts about what i am getting.
   It's just a strange feeling...Windows is more "user friendly"...which in a way should imply trust...but I don't trust them at all.
I will download anything Ubuntu throws at me without a hitch because i feel like they are genuinely trying to make my life easier...but i HATE downloading windows updates...i dont even bother anymore because i am rarely on windows.
I guess i'm just rambling...i just felt the need to share my thoughts and see what other people thought.
