The General Public License is a copyright license. An Apple EULA is a purchase agreement/sales contract.

In the United States, failure to comply with the terms of a contract is NOT a violation of law, it is breach of contract.  In Germany, Switzerland, and perhaps Denmark, breach of contract can sometimes be in violation of law.
