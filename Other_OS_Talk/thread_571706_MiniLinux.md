### Post by darrelljon on 2007-10-12
Here's some suggestions in order of their size.

Not Linux but worth mentioning is KolibriOS which is a GUI OS under 2Mb.

[SIZE="4"] Under 50Mb[/SIZE]
Distros under 50Mb fit onto a MiniCD.

   (<15Mb) SliTaz
   (<50Mb) DamnSmallLinux - Distro using Knoppix hardware recognition.
   (44Mb) Jinx - with Opera 9, Flash, Textmaker and Planmaker.
   (<50Mb) Austrumi - Austrumi is a business card size bootable live CD Linux distribution based on Slackware.
   (<50Mb) Mitrax - MitraX is live distro based on Slackware
   (<50Mb) Luit

[SIZE="4"] Under 128Mb[/SIZE]

   (<100Mb) NimbleX sub100
   (<100Mb) Puppy Linux
   (84Mb) DamnSmallLinux-Not - distro using Knoppix hardware recognition with Abiword and Gnumeric.
   (<128Mb) DeLi - Desktop Light
   (<128Mb) SLAX Popcorn Edition- A smaller version of SLAX
   (<128Mb) FeatherLinux - Knoppix based distro

[SIZE="4"] Under 256Mb[/SIZE]

   (177Mb) TinyMe - based on pcLinuxOS
   (182Mb) Kurumin 6 Light
   (~200Mb)  Slax - Distro based on Slackware using KDE
   (~200Mb) Runt
   (223Mb) TinyFlux -  based on TinyMe
   (230Mb) Kanotix CpxMini
   (<250Mb) Featherweight- based on Feather Linux
   (<256Mb) Flash Linux- Gentoo-based, initially designed to run off of a 256Mb USB key.

[SIZE="4"] Under 700Mb[/SIZE]
Distros under 700Mb fit onto most CD-Rs.

   (278Mb) AliXe - Slax based
   (410Mb) AntiX - MEPIS based
   (551Mb) Kanotix Lite
   (626Mb) MEPISLite - MEPIS based
   (662Mb) eLive
   (699Mb) SAM - A LiveCD based on PCLinuxOS.
