### Post by exploder on 2008-08-24
AntiX 7.5 has been released. If you are looking for a very light weight desktop environment with a pure Debian base then this might be just what you are looking for.

AntiX offers Fluxbox and IceWM as window managers but has raised the bar considerably in it's use of IceWM. AntiX now has it's own Control Center and the packages are very up to date.The artwork in the 7.5 release is more consistent and the new grub splash now sports the AntiX branding.

My congratulations to the AntiX Team for a very nice release!
