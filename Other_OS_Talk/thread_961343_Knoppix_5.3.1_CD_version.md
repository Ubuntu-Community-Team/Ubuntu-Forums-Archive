### Post by maybeway36 on 2008-10-28
Knoppix does not get updated very often anymore, but it's still one of my favorite distros, in that it packs as many useful apps as it can onto one disk. The official Knoppix 5.3.1 comes only in DVD, but the smart guys who work on the Japanese version have made a smaller CD version:
[http://www.rcis.aist.go.jp/project/knoppix/](http://www.rcis.aist.go.jp/project/knoppix/)
[ftp://ring.aist.go.jp/pub/linux/knoppix/iso/knoppix_v5.3.1CD_20080326-20080520-AC.iso](ftp://ring.aist.go.jp/pub/linux/knoppix/iso/knoppix_v5.3.1CD_20080326-20080520-AC.iso)
I posted instructions on the Knoppix forum for making an English ISO from the Japanese one:
[http://knoppix.net/forum/viewtopic.php?p=117816#117816](http://knoppix.net/forum/viewtopic.php?p=117816#117816)
And amaiman has kindly made a torrent available:
[http://www2.maiman.net/knoppix/](http://www2.maiman.net/knoppix/)
Knoppix is my jack-of-all-trades disc. I can usually count on it having what I need, which is why I like it so much.
