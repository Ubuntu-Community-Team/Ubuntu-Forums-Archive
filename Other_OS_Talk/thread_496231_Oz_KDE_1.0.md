### Post by RAV TUX on 2007-07-08
The CafeLinux.org Development Lab has released the latest Oz Enterprise release:

[Oz KDE 1.0]("http://www.rpath.org/rbuilder/project/oz/release?id=5688"), for those who enjoy a KDE only DE....This comes after the much anticipated debut of [Oz GNOME 1.0]("http://www.rpath.org/rbuilder/project/oz/release?id=5686") yesterday.

The primary difference with [Oz KDE 1.0]("http://www.rpath.org/rbuilder/project/oz/release?id=5688"), is that instead of having both KDE & GNOME like Oz Enterprise, [Oz KDE 1.0]("http://www.rpath.org/rbuilder/project/oz/release?id=5688") only includes KDE.

Like all Oz Enterprise releases I have included Opera and Firefox by default.


Here's the previously released; [Oz Live(KDE)]("http://www.rpath.org/rbuilder/project/oz/release?id=5618") version.

Enjoy.;)
