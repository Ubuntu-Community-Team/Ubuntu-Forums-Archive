Shall I just include the license, or should I also include the source?

The once I plan on including on the disk are: OpenOffice.org 2, The GIMP 2.2, Firefox&Thunderbird 1.5 (maybe also a few add-ons), GAIM 1.5 and Blender 2.42. It depends on the license under which the software is distributed. Under the terms of the GPL, you have to include either the source, or an offer to send the source for up to three years after purchase (and I believe you can foist the cost of shipping and media upon the purchaser). You also have to make it clear that it's under the GPL, and provide a copy of the GPL.
