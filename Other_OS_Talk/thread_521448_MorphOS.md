### Post by Freddy on 2007-08-11
Unfortunately he won't ship outside the UK, but thanks anyway.

You can run MorphOS on the now dead PegasosPPC plattform and it's getting ported to [EFIKA]("https://www.pegasosppc.com/store.php"), unfortunately EFIKA is kind of very low end and it's not suitable for a desktop environment.

Amiga Inc and Hyperion (the developers of Amiga OS4) is in court, both demanding the rights to that OS. As I understand it, if Amiga Inc wins, they will try hard to find a company that can provide the motherboards for a new Amiga One and if Hyperion wins they will port the OS to multiple platforms.

I root for Amiga Inc, cause I don't want a new OS for the inferior x86 platform and I want a OS designed for a optimized hardware, at least if that hardware is a new Amiga =). 

I want a OS not based on *nix.
