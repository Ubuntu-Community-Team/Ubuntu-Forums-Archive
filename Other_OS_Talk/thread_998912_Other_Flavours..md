### Post by Rumor on 2008-12-04
> **skymera said:**

So far on my list:
- Sidux
- Arch
- Gentoo
- PCLOS Gnome

The first three on your list are very much different from the last. PCLOS Gnome will give you their standard set of packages. The others are more or less build-it-yourself distros. Sidux perhaps less so than Arch or Gentoo.

I've used Arch for over two years and doubt very much I'll switch to anything else. I love the control Arch gives you over your system. There are no default programs and no assumptions made as to your preferences. You install only what you want, make it as bleeding edge as you choose, and update it as frequently as seems good to you. I love the "install it once and never again" philosophy. If you are comfortable with the command line and don't mind doing some reading in the excellent wiki, then Arch might be what you're looking for.
