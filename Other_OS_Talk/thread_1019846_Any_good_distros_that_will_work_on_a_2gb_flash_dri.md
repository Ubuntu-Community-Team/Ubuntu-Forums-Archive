### Post by snowpine on 2008-12-23
Puppy and SliTaz are two awesome flash drive distros. They are designed so that everything loads into ram at boot time, which means 1) very fast performance; 2) the flash drive lasts longer.

A CD holds less than a gigabyte, so any distro that fits onto a CD (including Ubuntu) should also fit onto your 2gb flash drive, if you are running it in Live mode.

Two useful tools for this task are the Live USB Creator on the Ubuntu Intrepid Live CD, and Unetbootin (which works with just about any distro).

Good luck!
