### Post by ezsit on 2007-02-19
Other than getting an inferior operating system in Vista, the OEM version is nothing more than the full package without any obligation from Microsoft to support your installation. OEM copies are supposed to be installed by system retailers who will then provide support to their customers. For an individual, if you never require support from Microsoft, there is no difference in the OEM version.
