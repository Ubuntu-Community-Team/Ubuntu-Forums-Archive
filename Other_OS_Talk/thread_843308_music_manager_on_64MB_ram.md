### Post by DJ_Peng on 2008-06-28
I bet MPD would do the trick for you. I discovered it on [Casper Clemens Mierau's blog]("http://www.screenage.de/blog/2008/06/10/my-package-of-the-day-mpd-the-music-player-daemon/") and I love it. He gives step by step instructions on how to install it as well as various options for players that work with it. I usually use either [Relaxx]("http://relaxx.dirk-hoeschen.de/") in my browser or [Sonata]("http://sonata.berlios.de/"), but [Ario]("http://ario-player.sourceforge.net/") is pretty nice, too. I love MPD so much I blogged it (the 5th comment on Casper's post).
