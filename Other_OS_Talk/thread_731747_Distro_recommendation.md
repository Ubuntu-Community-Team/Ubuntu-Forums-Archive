### Post by dptxp on 2008-03-22
Use PuppyLinux or AntiX. Make a SWAP partition of at least 512 MB first, you can use GPARTED.

Both are damn good distros, designed to run well with 128 MB RAM.
Antix is based on MEPIS.

In case of Puppy you can find a lot of derivatives too, Puppy is easy to remaster.

Kanotix needs 192 MB minimum and 256 MB for decent run.
Even sidux will need something similar.

There is one based on PCLINUXOS, Tinyflux.

If you find chaep RAM, upgrade RAM.
