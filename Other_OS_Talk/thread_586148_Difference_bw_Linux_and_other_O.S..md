### Post by rudeboyskunk on 2007-10-22
You could write a 100000000-page book on the differences.  But I'll sum up what I see as the high points:

1) Windows is good with office productivity.  You simply cannot beat Microsoft Office.  I hope so much that OpenOffice will beat it within the next 10 years.  But you can do just about anything with MS Office.

2) OSX is pretty much the default with multimedia editing.  I really hope Ubuntustudio makes strides to overcome them within the next 10 years.

3) Linux (generally speaking) is best at multi-tasking.  You can run several different memory-consuming programs at once and still be fine using Linux.  This is great for gamers, so they won't have to buy computers with 2 gigs of RAM just to play chess.

But the best point of all---Windows and OSX are not free, Linux is!!  (as in free BEER and freeDOM)
