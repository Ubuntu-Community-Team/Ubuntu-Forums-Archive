### Post by MethodOne on 2008-07-24
It's a distro, based on Fedora, [endorsed by the FSF]("http://www.gnu.org/links/links.html#FreeGNULinuxDistributions").  Because of its completely-free nature, the kernel is stripped of binary blobs.  It also doesn't contain non-free software like NVIDIA drivers or Adobe's Flash plugin or instructions on how to install them.   Some of the browser  bookmarks may be political in nature (mostly about social rights), just in case you're offended by that stuff.

### Post by MethodOne on 2008-07-24
You can install Flash and the NVIDIA drivers manually.  Don't try installing the NVIDIA drivers from Livna because those are built for Fedora's kernel, not BLAG's stripped-down one.
