Well, it really depends on what you expect from it. I've seen quite a few people saying that OpenBSD is inferior to Free- or NetBSD because the ports system it much less advanced in it. While the observation is true, the conclusion is wrong. The OpenBSD [FAQ](http://openbsd.org/faq/faq15.html#PkgVsPorts) says that packages are the recommended way for users to install third-party apps, and ports are meant to be used only by advanced users and/or users with very specinic needs.
