### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-12-08
> **Musick Man said:**
> Hi,

I have 2 Mobo's laying here, and I thought that it might be cool if I could make both of them run 1 computer. Is this possible?

Well, with the two motherboards you're likely to have, it's not possible.

However, back in the 1990s Apple released a Macintosh with two motherboards. One was a Mac motherboard, the other was a PC-style motherboard with a 386 processor. The user could switch between the Mac OS and DOS by pressing Option-Tab. Cool idea, except that it cost quite a lot and the PC side was underpowered.
