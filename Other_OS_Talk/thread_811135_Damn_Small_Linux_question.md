### Post by Bungo Pony on 2008-05-28
Pop in your flash drive

Pop in your DSL live CD and boot

When you get to the boot prompt (under the big penguin) type install

You will eventually get an all text menu. There should be an option to install to the USB Pen/Flash drive. Choose that, follow the instructions, and you should be good to go!

Hint: Your flash drive should be called sda
