### Post by K.Mandla on 2008-12-18
I know it's not for everyone, but the new version of Crux has been released.

[http://crux.nu/](http://crux.nu/)

The 2.5 ISO has Xorg 7.4, the 2.6.27.8 kernel and a lot of little side bonuses, like a precompiled version of Openbox 3.4.7.2 on the ISO. 

If you've used Arch, and you're comfortable installing packages manually, or recompiling software with PKGBUILDs, or building your own kernel, Crux might interest you. For as fast as Arch is when compared to Ubuntu, Crux is that much faster than Arch.

If you're interested or need help getting it going, try #crux on Freenode or [the mailing lists]("http://crux.nu/Main/MailingLists"). 

Or you could also ask questions here, and your friendly Ubuntu neighbors will surely help you. ;)
