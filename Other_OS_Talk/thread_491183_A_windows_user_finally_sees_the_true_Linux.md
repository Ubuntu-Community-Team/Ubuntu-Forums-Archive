### Post by m_o_b on 2007-07-03
it's a funny story how people can judge Linux without knowing it or seeing it.

i heard a lot of people talk about Linux and how great it is and they are not going back to use Windows as their first choice. I used to think what's wrong with this people;) i thought of them as sick people. (my mental picture of Linux was the old OS with black and white screens no graphics and what so ever) really that's what i thought about it for really long time. Until, last month where i'm taking an e-commerce class and the teacher told the class about open source programs and OS so i thought to give open office a chance (free what am i going to lose) i was impressed.

Then i went on and look around the web for Linux to see what Linux is all about.....I was shocked (great graphics, 3d, free office suits, GIMP...........etc. "u know better than i do") and the greatest thing was it's FREE.

i still have couple of problems like the sound doesn't work except with headphone and i'm still new to this world......but i'm willing to learn cuz i think it's worth it.

i'm currently reading about the command on linuxcommand.org and i'm getting use to the simple commands.

i went to the teacher and thanked him for let me know about Linux and the open source's world.

and please if someone knows a website that give a good reading for new users to make their life easier with Linux please let me know.:o

i'm currently using Mandriva 2007 and Kubuntu to try both Desktops and see and judge for my self (of course after trying a lot of Livecds).
