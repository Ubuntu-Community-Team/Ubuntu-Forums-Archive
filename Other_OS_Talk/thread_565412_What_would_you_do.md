### Post by mohnkern on 2007-10-02
Part of the answer to this question lies in the question:

"What does he use his pc for?"

It's been my experience that a lot of people use their computer for 2 things:

1. Reading email
2. Surfing the web.

If that's all he's doing, Ubuntu may be a good move to go to eventually.  I'd boot up the live cd, see if he can cope with Firefox and Evolution.  If he can, do the install, if its tough, then consider rebuilding XP.
