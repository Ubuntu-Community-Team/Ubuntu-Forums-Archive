His method is clearly flawed and it is aparent with his choice of words. He said vulnerabilities that MS has '**disclosed**'. It is a known fact that MS keeps a lot of vulnerabilities that are reported to them secret from the public for the sake of PR and to prevent malicious users from finding out about them. This is why all the top security sites that distribute this information are password protected and they screen their members for a need to know. Secunia, iDefense, USCERT just to name a few...

He said he was working with numbers based on what MS has disclosed. MS doesn't always disclose every security hole that is reported because they don't want everyone in the whole world to know about it. This leads me to belive he is not including the numerous ones that are being kept secret right now until MS can fix them.

Keep this in mind...
Who patches MS products?
That's right, MS does!

Who patches open source products?
Everyone in the open source community!

That is why vulnerabilities in open source are found and patched so much quicker. It wasn't the fact that Vista had less, MS just **disclosed** less...
