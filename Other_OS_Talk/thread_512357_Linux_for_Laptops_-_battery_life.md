### Post by smoker on 2007-07-29
if you ran puppy from a usb stick, put a small swap partition on the stick also, then you can disable your hard drive in the bios, or even remove the hard drive completely, this would lengthen battery life i would think. but, i suspect, even with the hard drive enabled, the battery life would be longer anyway, as puppy uses much less resources than most other distros.
