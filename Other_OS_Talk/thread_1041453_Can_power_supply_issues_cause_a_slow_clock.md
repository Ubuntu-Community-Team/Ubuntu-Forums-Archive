### Post by handy on 2009-01-16
You will have to get inside & find the little CMOS battery & replace it.

You will have to figure out how to get the keyboard off, be careful as it will be attached by a ribbon cable which you should be able to keep attached.

Have a [*_Scroogle_*]("http://www.scroogle.org/") for info' on dismantling it if it is not obvious.

An electronics tech' should be able to supply you with a replacement battery for a better price than buying original.
