### Post by Grant A. on 2009-01-18
Are you looking for a dictionary or...?

Ubuntu is from the Native *African* language Zulu.

Also, most Native American languages died out because they are much more complex than the invading languages (English, Spanish, French, etc.), and lack many words for modern items. However, Lakota still has a few thousand speakers.

I wish you luck in your quest in learning this language.

BTW, before you dedicate yourself to a new language and possibly lock your brain to it, you may find learning a language widely spoken in an area near you more useful for the future, unless you plan on moving somewhere totally different in the future.
