### Post by pastalavista on 2009-01-01
It's not so weird. Just click 'manual' and format the way you want. It won't disturb your Vista data if you shrink it's partition. To be extra safe, use [Vista's disk management tool]("http://vistahelp.blogspot.com/2007/02/resize-partitions-with-vista-disk.html") to shrink the partition and leave the freed space unformatted. Works best (quickest) if you defrag Windows first.
