### Post by mips on 2007-10-06
Yes, you are missing something.

It's only available to registered AmigaOne motherboard owners. The motherboard is PPC based and secondly it has a ROM chip on it which is required for the OS to work. The OS comes bundled with the MB, for early owners they shipped linux and they were later entitled to the OS4 upgrade. As mentioned above there are no motherboards available at present and the future looks bleak, they should just port it to a OpenPPC design like PagasosPPC [http://www.pegasosppc.com/products.php](http://www.pegasosppc.com/products.php)
