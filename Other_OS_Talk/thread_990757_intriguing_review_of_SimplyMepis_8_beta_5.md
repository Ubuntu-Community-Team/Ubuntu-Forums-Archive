### Post by 67GTA on 2008-11-23
You have to edit files with almost any distro to use dialup. Linux doesn't support most winmodems out of the box. Mepis includes KPPP by default, so if you have the driver for your modem(if it is supported) then you are halfway there. A lot of distros don't include KPP/Gnomeppp on the CD anymore. If you don't have a working internet connection, then you can't get what you need(catch 22).
