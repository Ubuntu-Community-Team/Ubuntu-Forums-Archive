### Post by laidback on 2007-02-19
Senjai,

I've used both, Mandriva first and now Ubuntu. The thing that got me frustrated with Mandriva was the segregation between paid for use and Open Souce use. If you pay (e.g. Power User Pack) for Mandriva you get a better deal. This is exactly the sort of thing I was trying to get away from (ex XP user) so in the end it got to me. I had no particular complaints about Mandriva, indeed the things I do in Ubuntu I could do in Mandriva, but I feel more at home with Ubuntu and sense that it will meet my needs for some time to come.
The Mandriva Forum was good, I'm getting used to the Ubuntu ones, but I miss that more than anything.
I don't feel like a second class citizen with Ubuntu, long may it continue.

You can also purchase books on Ubuntu from your local store or Amazon, you can't do that for Mandriva specific texts.

I hope you join us.

Kindest regards
