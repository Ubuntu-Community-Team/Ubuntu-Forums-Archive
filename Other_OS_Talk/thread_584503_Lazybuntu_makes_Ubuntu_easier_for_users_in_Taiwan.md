Three steps, optimize your Ubuntu. 






Motivation:
To date, Ubuntu is one of the most friendly distro of Linux. However, some essential parts cannot be integrated into Ubuntu due to restrictions (e.g. patented codecs, non-free packages and drivers). In addition, it is still unpleasant to set Asia language (input methods, language interface......) in Ubuntu. Thus, some geeks in Taiwan have a project called Lazybuntu (GPL). 


Objectives:
1. Easy fine tuning-- few clicks, and ready to use.
2. No excuses from new users.


Lazybuntu homepage: [http://lazybuntu.openfoundry.org/](http://lazybuntu.openfoundry.org/) 
(The English version is under construction)

Developer: PCMan and yurenju
website design: jurenju
Tech support: The Ubuntu community of Taiwan

Lazybuntu is designed for Taiwanese users (for Gutsy), therefore, the current version might not fit other international users. The authors will to release international version soon.
