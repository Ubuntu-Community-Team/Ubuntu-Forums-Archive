### Post by Paerez on 2006-08-02
Right click on your panel in an empty area, click +New Panel. Then you will have an empty panel somewhere on your screen.

Right click that panel and click properties. Then choose "bottom" for orientation. Then make the size nice and big, like 48. Uncheck expand and uncheck autohide and uncheck show hide buttons.

Then click the background tab, choose "Solid Color". Pick white as a color as set the transparency to about half.

Now add the icons you want by going into the gnome menu and dragging them to the bottom. Drag onto the little grey bits on the sides (not onto existing buttons). Then fill it up to your liking.
