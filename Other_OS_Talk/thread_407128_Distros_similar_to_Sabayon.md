Sabayon isn't even close to an install what you want type of system.  In fact, Sabayon dumps a very large amount of apps on your system, and with portage it is very difficult if not impossible to remove them safely.  If you don't want to do a full Gentoo or Arch install, I would try any of Fedora, openSuse, or Debian.  All these allow you to select the packages you want and only those upon install.  The same is true of slackware as well I believe.  There are probably many more I am leaving out, but those are the main players in that department.  FreeBSD would also be a good choice.
