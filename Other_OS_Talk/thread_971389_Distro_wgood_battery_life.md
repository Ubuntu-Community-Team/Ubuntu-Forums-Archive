### Post by fwojciec on 2008-11-05
There isn't really any difference in battery consumption between distros -- the default power settings on most distros are not very well optimized, but they can be tweaked to a great extent in any distro.  My basic recommendation would be to use powertop in order to identify what sucks up battery and then make appropriate configuration changes.  lesswatts.org is also a very good resource for information on how to improve battery life in Linux.
