### Post by OldAdamUser2 on 2012-08-29
Have you tried using alsamixer to adjust audio outputs? Open a terminal window. Type in alsamixer. Then play with the various settings. In particular check the "Speaker" settings.
The "m" key mutes and unmutes. the arrow keys allow you to raise or lower output and to move from one column to another.
