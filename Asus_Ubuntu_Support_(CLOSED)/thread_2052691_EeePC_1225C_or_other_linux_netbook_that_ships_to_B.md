### Post by john stiles on 2012-10-21
Asus 1225C only works with Ubuntu 12.04 in unity 2D. The onchip graphics of the Intel cedar trail cpu is from a separate licensed proprietor and only offers limited linux support. The sticker says "Intel Inside" but this is sadly not true. So Unity 3D does not work and may not ever be supported. Ubuntu 12.10 does not work at all. So beware all Intel Atom 2600 and 2800 chips.

Take a look at the Asus f201e it has a 1.1 GHz Intel Celeron 847 processor and should be okay.
