### Post by momist on 2012-01-28
Just a note to describe my findings so far.  At least one other member here has failed to drive an SSD drive from the SATA connections on this motherboard.  I suspect the problem lies in the lack of support for using the SATA with AHCI interface.  The solution appears to be using a SATA PCI card, you will probably still have a spare slot or two available.  :)

I will update this post when I have more experience/findings.
