### Post by mathematicallysplendid on 2012-06-27
I found the answer. 

Evidently Nvidia Optimus doesn't play nicely with linux based operating systems. [http://askubuntu.com/questions/136079/nvidia-graphics-resolution-problem](http://askubuntu.com/questions/136079/nvidia-graphics-resolution-problem)

Bumblebee is recommended for nvidia cards. 
[http://www.bumblebee-project.org/install.html#Ubuntu](http://www.bumblebee-project.org/install.html#Ubuntu)
