### Post by darthm0e on 2011-12-18
I just got 11.10 runnin with EMGD 1.8 (+fix), but only 2D works. 3D is bad. Also VideoPlayback got only laggy sound. I use an Acer AO 751h.
On first boot you need to feed your kernel with wrong gfx parameters so it boots with VESA. The Poulsbo Driver delivered with 11.10 will otherwise crash X Server. 
For 11.10 their is a new EMGD driver in testphase, Versionnumber is 1.10 -> [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsVideoCardsPoulsbo#EMGD_1.10_.28under_testing.29](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsVideoCardsPoulsbo#EMGD_1.10_.28under_testing.29)
