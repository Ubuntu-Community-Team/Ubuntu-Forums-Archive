### Post by el_koraco on 2011-08-01
The corrupted screen at boot is usual, it's a known issue. There are workarounds, but i suggest you don't attempt them yet, because they can mess your sh*t up bad. As for the game, maybe you can try to tweak things via the Control Center. 

Open the Dash, and type in ATI Catalyst, you'll get two options - Control center, and Control center administrative. Pick the administrative one. 

But you have the driver. if you want to change things up, you can uninstall it via the Additional driver utility, and install one directly from ATI. use at your own discretion. 

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI)
