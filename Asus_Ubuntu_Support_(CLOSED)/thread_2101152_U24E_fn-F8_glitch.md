### Post by Tiler on 2013-01-03
Was experimenting with the new Television, via HDMI last night and noticed the fn-F8 keystroke to switch between LCD and HDMI output doesn't work.  The work-around I found was to use fn-F7 to turn off the laptop's LCD screen.  It switches to the TV and then back when you're done.
