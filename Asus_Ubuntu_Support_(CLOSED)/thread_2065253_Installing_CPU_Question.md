### Post by QIII on 2012-10-01
When properly aligned, the CPU should drop freely into the socket.  If you are concerned, you may tap the edges very lightly with a fingernail.  The locking lever moves the base slightly horizontally to lightly trap the pins.

Don't tap a fingertip on the metallic part of the CPU or the traces of skin oil will reduce the effectiveness of the thermal compound.
