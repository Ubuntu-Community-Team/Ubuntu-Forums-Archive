### Post by diegocaprioli on 2012-08-27
Hello,

The problem after all was not with Ubuntu, but with the notebook itself. I reported the problem to shop where I bought it, and they changed it immediately (I was on warranty time).

It seems that the wifi network card was disconnected from the antenna. If you are on warranty, I recommend that you get in touch with your provider. If not, there are some sites that show how to dismantle this notebook, and where the wifi card and antenna are located, but it doesn't look easy.
