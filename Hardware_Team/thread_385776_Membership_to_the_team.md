### Post by Lord Illidan on 2007-03-16
If you want to join the team, you are free to do so. However, please note that it is not for asking support. So if you want to join the team because you have problems with your hardware, that is not a valid reason. To join the team, you should be a member who wants to **help** other people with their hardware problems.

I don't consider bean count very important as to how skilled a person is, but if you are a very new member, then send me a PM along with your application.

I am interested in having people who:[LIST=1]
[*]Can deal with graphical issues - drivers and stuff like that, and can guide people on such issues.
[*]Can figure out laptops and wifi - very important.
[*]Can help solve desktop issues with miscellaneous hardware.
[*]Can help out with printers and other devices.[/LIST]Obviously, knowing 1 or more of the above is enough...if you know how to solve all 4, then you should be in the team already, what are you waiting for!
