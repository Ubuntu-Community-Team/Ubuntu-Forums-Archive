[https://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=news_item&px=Git-2018-Security-CVE-2018](https://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=news_item&px=Git-2018-Security-CVE-2018)

[https://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=news_item&px=XWayland-Auto-EGL-NVIDIA-Patch](https://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=news_item&px=XWayland-Auto-EGL-NVIDIA-Patch)
