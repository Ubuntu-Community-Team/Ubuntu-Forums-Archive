[https://9to5linux.com/ubuntu-21-04-hirsute-hippo-gets-its-first-kernel-security-patch-update-now](https://9to5linux.com/ubuntu-21-04-hirsute-hippo-gets-its-first-kernel-security-patch-update-now)
