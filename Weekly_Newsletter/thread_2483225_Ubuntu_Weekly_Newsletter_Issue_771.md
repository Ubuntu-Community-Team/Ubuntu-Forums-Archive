***[SIZE="4"]gnome-info-collect: What we learned[/SIZE]***

Allan Day reminds us of the 'research exercise' that ran using the 'gnome-info-collect' tool, which got a "fantastic response" with over 2500 people uploading their data. This post shares what was learnt from those responses. Firstly the users who likely responded and thus have provided their data is outlined noting this may influence the results. The data is presented in various subjects, provided in numerous tables - with descriptions, and is rather detailed in this long report. Finally Vojt&#283;ch Stan&#283;k is thanked for his work on the gnome-info-collect tool, with a note that the final data will not be published in complete form (for privacy reasons).
