***[SIZE="4"]IRC Council resolution: On Recent freenode Events[/SIZE]***

Thomas Ward on behalf of the Ubuntu Community Council informs us of the vote to support the migration of the IRC home of Ubuntu to Libera.chat. Thomas provides a list of the tasks designated to the IRCC, and the Canonical contact, Monica Ayhens-Madon.

[https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-irc/2021-May/001923.html](https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-irc/2021-May/001923.html)

***[SIZE="4"]Updating your launchpad pages[/SIZE]***
