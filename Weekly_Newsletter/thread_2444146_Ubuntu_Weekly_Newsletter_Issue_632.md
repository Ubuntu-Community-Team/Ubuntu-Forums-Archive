[https://9to5linux.com/canonical-outs-linux-kernel-security-updates-for-ubuntu-18-04-lts-and-16-04-lts](https://9to5linux.com/canonical-outs-linux-kernel-security-updates-for-ubuntu-18-04-lts-and-16-04-lts)
