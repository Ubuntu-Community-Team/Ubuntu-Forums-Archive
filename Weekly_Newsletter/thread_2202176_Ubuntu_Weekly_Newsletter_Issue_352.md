**[size="5"]The Planet[/size]**

***[size="3"]David Planella: A quickstart guide to the Ubuntu emulator[/size]***

David Planella gives a quick tutorial on setting up an Ubuntu Touch emulator which will be the primary engineering platform for development of Ubuntu Touch applications. David says that though the emulator itself is still in development it is rapidly reaching maturity and provides the look and feel of an Ubuntu Phone on your Ubuntu system today.

[http://davidplanella.org/ubuntu-emulator-quickstart-guide/](http://davidplanella.org/ubuntu-emulator-quickstart-guide/)

***[size="3"]Riccardo Padovani: Development plans of calc app for Ubuntu Touch[/size]***

Riccardo Padovani explains the importance of the upcoming release of 14.04 Trusty Tahr, the second stable release of Ubuntu Touch and what convergence will mean when it is achieved in the coming months and years, and what that means for the calculator application on both Ubuntu Touch and Trusty Tahr.

[http://blog.rpadovani.com/en/2014/01/plans-for-calc-app/](http://blog.rpadovani.com/en/2014/01/plans-for-calc-app/)

***[size="3"]David Planella: Announcing the first Ubuntu App Dev Schools[/size]***

[http://davidplanella.org/announcing-first-ubuntu-app-dev-schools/](http://davidplanella.org/announcing-first-ubuntu-app-dev-schools/)

***[size="3"]Mathieu Trudel: Call for testing: urfkill / Getting flight mode to rock on Ubuntu and Ubuntu Touch[/size]***
