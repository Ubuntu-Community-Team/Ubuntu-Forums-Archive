### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-03-06
No - the Mini does not have free slots, and good luck getting inside the iMac (it's a solid piece of aluminium!). The Mac Pro would be the only one that would accept the card, and since it's M-audio you'd find drivers for Mac OS for sure.

### Post by handy on 2008-03-07
To get inside an aluminium iMac you have to remove the glass which is held on with really strong magnets.

If you can do that without the correct tool & not break the glass, then you will find that the only things you can change in there are the drive's & possibly the cpu, which would be interesting with regards to the psu & the cooling situation.

You can access the ram without having to remove the glass thankfully. ;-)
