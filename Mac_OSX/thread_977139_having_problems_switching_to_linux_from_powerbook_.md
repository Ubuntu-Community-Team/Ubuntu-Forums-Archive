### Post by Dan_Dranath999 on 2008-11-10
i downloaded Flash Player 10 from adobe, (using 8.06 too) uninstalled the flashplayer-nonfree from synaptic, and then installed the new player:

a.- after extracting the contents from the *.tar.gz, get inside the newly created folder, and you will see a file like "flash-whatever-installer.sh" 

b.- Navigate to that folder using the terminal, and type "sh flash-whatever-installer.sh" and follow the instructions (it will ask you to close down your browser at certain point).

c.- Don't have problems with Flash anymore.
