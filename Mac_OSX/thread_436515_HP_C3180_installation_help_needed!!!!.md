### Post by Spenser_Gilliland on 2007-05-07
If you'd like to install the C3180 in ubuntu linux.  go to System>Administration>Printing wait for it to load.  Then double click on add printer. This will bring up the add printer dialog. Do not choose the printer out of the white box.  Instead use the second option "use another printer by specifing a port". After selecting this printer just click next until its added.
