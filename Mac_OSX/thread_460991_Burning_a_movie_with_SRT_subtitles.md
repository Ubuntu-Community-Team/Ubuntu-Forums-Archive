### Post by pelle.k on 2007-06-13
Why is this thread in Mac OSX forums? This is not OS specific...

You rename the file so it reflects the actual movie name, without the extension of course. (i.e. movie.avi movie.srt) and it'll be loaded by most media players.
you can also have multiple subtitles by naming them like this;
movie.avi movie.en.srt movie.sp.srt

[edit]you do nothing special with 'em, just toss tem im the same directory as the movie itself...[/edit]
