[edit] Ok There is a **long term** way to move **some** of your passwords. So definitely not perfect!

You have to install e.g. [http://lastpass.com](http://lastpass.com) (works on every platform and browser) and every time you visit a website just remember to log out.
Then you have to log in, use autofill from Safari. After you log in lastpass will ask you to save the password and there you go.
It's not a prefect solution but it helps you keep password independent from platform.
Lastpass is not perfect but you can get used to it. Looks way worse than 1password ;-) Oh and the "safe" built in browser is really old school.
That is the cost of keeping your stuff independent ;-)
If I remember correctly some time ago Lastpass had an importer but not anymore. :/ [UPDATE] They do have importer but not for Safari :((( [https://helpdesk.lastpass.com/importing-from-other-password-managers/](https://helpdesk.lastpass.com/importing-from-other-password-managers/) Everything work even on my really old phone HTC One [https://productz.com/en/htc-one](https://productz.com/en/htc-one) so I guess it will work on yours.
