### Post by AlphaMack on 2008-02-26
There are a good number of FOSS apps out there for OS X (native ports).

You can always go the route of either MacPorts or Fink although the packages are typically outdated.  The former requires Xcode (for gcc) and you have to compile all packages from source; the latter uses apt.
