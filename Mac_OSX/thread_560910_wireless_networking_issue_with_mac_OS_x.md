### Post by stream303 on 2008-12-29
I have a netgear WG111v2 usb wireless adapter working well on my G5 iMac with 10.4.11 ppc.

All I had to do was install the driver:
[http://kbserver.netgear.com/release_notes/D103229.asp](http://kbserver.netgear.com/release_notes/D103229.asp)

Although the page says that it is for the G4, I have it working just fine, even with WPA on my G5.  I suspect G3's would work as well, but I can't confirm it.

Just make sure you are using a "v2" model - look at the side of the box in the fine print...
