### Post by aysiu on 2008-03-05
My wife is a hard-core Mac user through and through. There are some things about the Mac, though, that annoy her (the Dock, the boot-up noise, Spotlight).

The other night, she was pissed that some emails in Mail were appearing in a really tiny font, and some were appearing in larger fonts. She looked through all the GUI options to force the message font to be consistent and couldn't find such an option, so she did a Google search for how to force the font to be the same font for all messages.

What she found was a tutorial that asked her to quit Mail, put a command into the terminal, and then restart Mail. She launched up the terminal and asked me if she could copy and paste the command instead of retyping it. I said she definitely could. So she did that, and all the message fonts were consistent.

She was very happy about it. I asked her if she liked the terminal, and she replied, "The terminal is awesome!" There you have it--my wife thinks the terminal is awesome.
