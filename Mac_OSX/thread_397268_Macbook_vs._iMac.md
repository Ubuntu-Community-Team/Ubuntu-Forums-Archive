### Post by Cippy on 2007-03-30
If you're going to be taking a computer to classes, you'll obviously want to go with the MacBook. The iMac, however, would have more HD space on it, which is better for multimedia projects and such. You'll also get a better video card in the iMac.

And just a reminder, make sure you get the education discount.
