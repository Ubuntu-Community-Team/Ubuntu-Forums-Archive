Mac OS X Market Share Nears 8%

[http://www.informationweek.com/news/software/operatingsystems/showArticle.jhtml?articleID=208802203](http://www.informationweek.com/news/software/operatingsystems/showArticle.jhtml?articleID=208802203)


The global market share for Linux jumped in the past 10 months, while Microsoft's Windows lost a few points, says Net Applications.

By Antone Gonsalves
InformationWeek
July 2, 2008 01:30 PM

Apple's share of the global operating system market is climbing steadily, and could top 8% this month, statistics from a Web metrics firm showed Wednesday.


During the same period, Linux has also increased market share, rising to 0.8% of the market in June from 0.47% last August, according to Net Applications. Meanwhile, the global market share ofMicrosoft (NSDQ: MSFT) Windows has declined over the last 10 months. In June, Windows accounted for 90.89% of the market, down from 93.06%.
