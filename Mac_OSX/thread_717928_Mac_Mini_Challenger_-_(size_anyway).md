### Post by handy on 2008-03-07
You can build your own Mini-ITX with one of the VIA boards.  There is a large variety of cases & psu's, many people use them as car computers.  

They have 1 pci slot that can be made into more with an additional card, they play DVD have a large range of input/outputs & various specialty boards too.

I built one to run in a campervan I had a few years ago.  They are quiet & use little power.  I used a notebook drive in mine & 512 Mb ram.  In those days I was still on WinXP.  They run fine on Linux, & people have made Linux based media centers that work with touch screens for automotive use.

Within their limitations they are great little boards.

***_[http://www.via.com.tw/en/initiatives/spearhead/mini-itx/](http://www.via.com.tw/en/initiatives/spearhead/mini-itx/)_***
