### Post by kaziweb on 2012-11-05
The team goal is to introduce Ubuntu locally, help locals and residents to learn and use it personally and professionally, create a community based platform where they can share ideas and experiences as well as help each other to learn. In Time, the team will try to promote Ubuntu in the country.
