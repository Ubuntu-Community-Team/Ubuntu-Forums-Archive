### Post by &amp;)ky#)^ on 2009-03-21
I took my car in for an oil change today at a local Duke of Oil garage.  While paying, I noticed that their computer was running Ubuntu.  I really couldn't believe my eyes that a big business like them would utilize Ubuntu for the majority of their business functions.  I couldn't contain my excitement and I mentioned how cool it was that they use Ubuntu to the nice lady who billed me, but I could tell she had this "whatever, I don't know what you're talking about, I just work here" feeling about it.  I mean, she wasn't rude, she probably just didn't know what I meant.

Anyhow, I think it's reasonable to believe that the rest of their locations also utilize Ubuntu.  I think it would be a nice gesture to send them a postcard or something thanking them for using Ubuntu.  We'd be putting ourselves out there and setting a positive example in the business community.
