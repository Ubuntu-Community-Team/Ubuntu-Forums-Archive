### Post by frrobert on 2009-10-13
South Bend once again has a LUG.  

FREE GEEK Michiana Linux Users Group is a monthly or twice monthly support group for GNU/Linux users. Meetings will include formal presentations and demonstrations and informal interaction. FGMLUG is sponsored by FREE GEEK Michiana, and volunteers from FGM will help organize the group.

FGMLUG meetings are free.

For more information please go to the website 

[http://www.fgmlug.org/](http://www.fgmlug.org/)
