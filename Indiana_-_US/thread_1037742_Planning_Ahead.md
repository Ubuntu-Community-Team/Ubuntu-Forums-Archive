It only filled a portion of the Javits Center - I heard earlier LinuxWorlds had taken over the entire convention center.  I've heard similar stories about the San Francisco LinuxWorlds.

[Ohio LinuxFest]("http://www.ohiolinux.org"), on the other hand, has been steadily growing and is well attended by people from throughout the midwest.  We're working with several LUGs throughout Indiana to help start an Indiana LinuxFest as well.

Maybe Open Source World will again become a vibrant conference and expo but you might see a greater use of resources at Ohio Linux Fest or something like [OSCON]("http://en.oreilly.com/oscon2009").
