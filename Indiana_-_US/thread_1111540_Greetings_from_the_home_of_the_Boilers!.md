Click on the  "Wireless Network Connection" right next to the power icon in the top right corner, select "Connect to Hidden Wireless Network.." Under Network Name type "PAL2.0" without quotes, Under wireless security, select "WPA & WPA2 ENTERPRISE", under Authentication, select "Protected EAP (PEAP)" for user name, type your Purdue ID and under password type your purdue password. This worked for me! Hope it helps!
