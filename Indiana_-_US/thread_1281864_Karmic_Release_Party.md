I was just about to post something about the release party.  Here's what I sent to the mailing list:

The next version of Ubuntu, version 9.10 (Karmic Koala) is being released on Thursday, October 29, 2009. ### Post by schultmc on 2009-10-16
> **Locke_99GS said:**
> I've heard of release parties for years, but I've never been to one.

If you don't mind, could you explain the going-ons at a conventional release party?

The Indiana Ubuntu LoCo team holds two release parties a year in conjunction with the periodic releases of Ubuntu.  Our spring release parties have been held at Fort Harrison State Park in Lawrence (East side of Indy), IN where we have a pitch-in cookout.  Due to the weather, our fall release parties are held indoors at a restaurant.  At either release party, people often bring laptops with the latest Ubuntu installed and sometimes there are copies of the latest release handed out.  We also discuss new features of the release or talk about any problems we're having using Ubuntu.
