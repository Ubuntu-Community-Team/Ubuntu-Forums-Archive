### Post by schultmc on 2007-03-14
The Indiana LoCo team held its first meeting in #ubuntu-indiana on irc.freenode.net on 2007-03-14 at 8:00 PM EDT.  12 team members participated in the meeting.  We decided to hold regular team meetings on the 2nd Monday of each month at 8:00 PM Eastern time.

[IU LinuxFest]("http://www.ussg.iu.edu/linuxfest") is being held at [IUPUI]("http://www.iupui.edu") on Wednesday, April 11, 2007.  [CINLUG]("http://www.cinlug.org") and [BLUG]("http://www.bloomingtonlinux.org") plan on having booths at IU LinuxFest and coordinating on LoCo exhibits.

We're currently soliciting ideas for the Indiana LoCo team logo - if you have any ideas, please share them via the mailing list and/or forum.

If you're interested, a [log]("http://logs.ubuntu-eu.org/freenode/2007/03/15/%23ubuntu-indiana.html") of the meeting is available.  The actual meeting is at the top of the log.

Thanks to everyone who participated in the IndianaLoCo team meeting. We're off to a great start!  See you online (or say hi at IU LinuxFest)! :)
