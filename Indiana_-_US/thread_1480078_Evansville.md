### Post by Corfy on 2010-05-11
Generally speaking, the Indiana Ubuntu LoCo is a mostly online group (although we do try to get together occasionally), so while we are based in Indiana, we don't really separate into different parts of the state.

You might find a Linux Users Group (LUG) in your area, however. While those aren't Ubuntu only, you could still get a lot out of them. Unfortunately, I'm not familiar with the various LUGs in Indiana (outside of the Central Indiana Linux User's Group, of which I am a member, but since it meets in Indianapolis, I don't recommend that one for you).
