### Post by schultmc on 2009-01-11
The Indiana LoCo team is spread out throughout the state, with pockets of members in Evansville, Bloomington, Indy, and Fort Wayne.  We've gotten together in person several times, usually for conferences like [ITEC]("http://www.goitec.com") and [Open Minds]("http://www.k12openminds.org/") or Ubuntu Release parties (We've held parties for Gutsy, Hardy, and Intrepid).

Due to the geographic dispersity, we've had monthly IRC meetings in the past.  We haven't had an IRC meeting in a while but have planned get togethers via the mailing list or here in the forums.

Feel free to organize more in person meetings as well, but there might be more participation for bigger events like conferences or release parties.
