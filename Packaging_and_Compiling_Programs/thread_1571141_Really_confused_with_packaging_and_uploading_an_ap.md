### Post by SevenMachines on 2010-09-10
Deb files are not uploaded, source and packaging are. Many of these files are the result of packaging your source code and running something like debuild -S, most of those files are generated by the build process. 
To upload to revu I'd recommend...
* Your source code packaged obviously!
* Upload it to a ppa, not necessary but its probably a good idea 
* Look through [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MOTU/Packages/REVU](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MOTU/Packages/REVU)
