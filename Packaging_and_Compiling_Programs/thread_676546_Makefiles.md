### Post by stroyan on 2008-01-24
The original documentation is at
[http://www.gnu.org/software/make/manual/make.html](http://www.gnu.org/software/make/manual/make.html)
That gets really dense after a while.  But the beginning is OK.
The simple example in section 2.2 may be all that you need.

Or you could google for "makefile tutorial" and choose from many documents.

Makefiles can be extremely simple, or extremely complex.
It depends on how much you want to do in your project.
A simple program doesn't need a complicated makefile.
