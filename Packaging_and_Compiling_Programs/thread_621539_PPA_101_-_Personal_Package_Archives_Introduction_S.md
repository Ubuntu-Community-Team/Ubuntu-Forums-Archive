### Post by 23meg on 2007-11-23
Matthew Revell sent the following announcement to the [ubuntu-motu mailing list]("http://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-motu"):

> Hello,

PPA 101 session #ubuntu-classroom 15.00 UTC 28 Nov
--------------------------------------------------

We're holding the second of our PPA 101 sessions between 15.00 and
16.00 UTC on Wednesday 28th November in #ubuntu-classroom.

Launchpad developer Celso Providelo (cprov) and your fellow MOTU
Jordan Mantha (Laserjock) will be offering an introduction to PPA and,
if there's time, taking questions.

If there's anything in particular you'd like to see covered in the
session, please drop me a mail.

Cheers!

This is a good opportunity to learn the basics of using Launchpad's [Personal Package Archive]("http://news.launchpad.net/cool-new-stuff/personal-package-archives") service. If you're interested in utilizing PPAs when doing packaging and distribution, for Ubuntu or your own purposes, join the #ubuntu-classroom channel on irc.freenode.net with your IRC client at 15:00 UTC on Wednesday 28th.

More info on PPAs:

[http://www.ubuntu.com/news/launchpad-personal-package-archive](http://www.ubuntu.com/news/launchpad-personal-package-archive)
[https://help.launchpad.net/PPAQuickStart](https://help.launchpad.net/PPAQuickStart)
