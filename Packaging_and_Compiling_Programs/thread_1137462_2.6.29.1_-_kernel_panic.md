### Post by ibuclaw on 2009-04-25
Here's a good reference to kernels on Ubuntu: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=311158](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=311158)

My compile script is:
```
#!/bin/bash
make-kpkg clean
INSTALL_MOD_STRIP=1 CONCURRENCY_LEVEL=3 fakeroot make-kpkg --initrd --append-to-version="-**$1**" kernel_image kernel_headers modules_image
```
Where $1 is the first argument of the script.

I compiled the 2.6.30 kernel myself ... I could share my .config file, as it's taken from jaunty, with the defaults for all other new features. ;)
