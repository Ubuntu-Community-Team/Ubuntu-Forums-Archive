### Post by kostkon on 2010-10-03
In your software sources (select the menu item *System &#8594; Administration &#8594; Software Sources* or in *Synaptic* select *Settings &#8594; Repositories*) deselect the CD-Rom option in the *Ubuntu Software* tab, press Close and Reload.
