### Post by sanderd17 on 2011-03-07
how I work:

* Put a source (like documents or firefox) open on one desktop
* go one desktop to the right and open kile
* build and view the pdf (via kile) and drag it to the desktop right of that

now you can just build with ALT+6 (on my ubuntu) and go to your doc with CTRL+ALT+LEFT/RIGHT. The pdf will be updated automatically, no need to close it.

If you have multiple screens, you can also work with different screens instead of different virtual desktops.
