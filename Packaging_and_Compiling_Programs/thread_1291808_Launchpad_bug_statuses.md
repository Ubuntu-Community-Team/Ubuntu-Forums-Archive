### Post by dinxter on 2009-10-15
see [https://help.launchpad.net/Bugs/Statuses](https://help.launchpad.net/Bugs/Statuses)
Essentially 'Incomplete' means someone with the bug needs to provide more information so that theres enough information to fix it, at which point it is moved to 'triaged', ready for someone to begin work on the cure. 

[EDIT] A bug supervisor makes the decision to when something is 'triaged', generally a developer
