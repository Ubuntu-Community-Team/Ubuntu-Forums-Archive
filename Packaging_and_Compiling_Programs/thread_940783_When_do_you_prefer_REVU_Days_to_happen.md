--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


**What is REVU?**

[REVU](http://revu.ubuntuwire.com) is a website where contributors can upload new packages they've created for Ubuntu Developers to review and comment on them. Once all outstanding issues have been addressed and two Ubuntu Developers are happy with the package, it is upload to the repositories of the current development version of Ubuntu.


**What are REVU Days?**

The so called *REVU Days* are weekly events which span from the moment when somewhere on the world the elected weekday starts until it has ended everywhere. During this day many Ubuntu Developers are likely to give special importance on review packages from [REVU](http://revu.ubuntuwire.com), and Contributors who want to get their stuff reviewed are highly encouraged to be available on #ubuntu-motu (at irc.freenode.net) so that reviewers can easily contact them about any problem.

### Post by nhandler on 2008-10-07
I personally feel that having REVU Days weekly is a mistake. By making them a weekly event, you are making them less special than if they occurred less frequently. This might lead to less participation from Developers. That is why I think that you should make REVU Days every 2/3/4 weeks instead of every week.

As for the day that REVU Days should occur on, my first choice would be Friday. This is because it is right before the weekend. During the week, most people are busy with work. During the weekend, people are busy with their social lives. By placing it on a Friday, you are putting it right between the time for work and the time for socializing. This should enable a large number of Developers to participate. Also, by placing it on a Friday, Developers can stay up late working on reviewing packages, without having to worry about being tired for work/school the next day.

This is just my opinion, but I hope you take it into consideration.

And for anyone else who might be reading this, remember, you don't need to wait for REVU Day to review packages on REVU. You can review packages 24/7 365 days a year.
