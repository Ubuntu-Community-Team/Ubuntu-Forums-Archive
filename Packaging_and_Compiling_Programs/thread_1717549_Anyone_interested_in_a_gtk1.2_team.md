### Post by Ibidem on 2011-03-30
First:  If you don't like gtk1.2, no need to comment.  This is about a PPA, not trying to bring gtk1.2 back to main, and it's not going to affect you.

If you do want to use gtk1.2 (for speed, the applications written for it, or for any other reason), feel free to comment.

However, I mean firstly to ask whether anyone wants to help with a Launchpad team to "foreport" GTK1.2 and such gtk1 applications as xmms, geg, danpei, and so on to Lucid, Maverick, Natty, and future releases of Ubuntu.

I've noticed that several ppas provide gtk1.2 applications, and it seems at least 3 provide the core libraries.  But it isn't practical to max and match, with the oddball versioning.
Additionally, most ppas seem to have a few releases, but then "go dormant".
With a team, there's one archive, and any member can upload a new version of a package.  This means bugs/FTBFS/... get fixed sooner and more packages are available.

So, here's an invitation to set up a team.  I won't set one up until I hear at least one response, and someone else may be more qualified to head a team than I am.  But I am ready to participate.

Ibidem
