### Post by gmargo on 2010-11-22
If you go to the packages page for the kernel (10.10 Maverick in this case):
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/maverick/linux-image-2.6.35-22-generic](http://packages.ubuntu.com/maverick/linux-image-2.6.35-22-generic)

you will see on the right hand side of the page links to the source packages, including the differences file:
[http://archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/pool/main/l/linux/linux_2.6.35-22.35.diff.gz](http://archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/pool/main/l/linux/linux_2.6.35-22.35.diff.gz)
