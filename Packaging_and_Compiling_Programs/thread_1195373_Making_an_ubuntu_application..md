### Post by thomas9459 on 2009-06-26
Wow, I just keep on answering my own questions. When you look at the properties for the launcher, click the springboard icon up in the top-right and move to whatever directory your picture is in and select your picture. Then whenever you look at it from the drop down menu, it will have the picture you selected beside it. Thank you for all you help everybody.
