### Post by Super Jamie on 2008-11-20
No can do guys, Nautilus 2.24 requires GNOME 2.24. Hardy has version 2.22 of each.

Hardy is an LTS, which means it will only receive security and bug fixes now that Intrepid's out, and is extremely unlikely to ever be migrated to Gnome 2.24.


acidrums4, Google around for the problems you have with your hardware an Intrepid, you may find a fix. If not, have a look around Launchpad and contribute to a well-detailed bug report, so the development team can fix Intrepid for you and other users too!

Linux comes together from volunteers. Coders, writers, patchers, as well as testers and bug reporters, which can be an ordinary everyday person just like you or me.
