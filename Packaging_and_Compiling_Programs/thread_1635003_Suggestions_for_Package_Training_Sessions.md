### Post by duanedesign on 2010-12-01
[Ubuntu Packaging Training Sessions]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Packaging/Training") are weekly one-hour sessions in #ubuntu-classroom on freenode that consist of short demonstrations and offer plenty of time to ask all kind of questions. 

If there is an area of Ubuntu packaging that you would like to know more about? You can [request a session]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Packaging/Training/Suggestions") so that it will be covered in future Package Training Sessions.

This thread is an opportunity to suggest and discuss topics. Please let us know what training sessions you would like to see.
