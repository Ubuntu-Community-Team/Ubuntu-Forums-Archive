### Post by SevenMachines on 2010-03-23
if you want to download individual packages, look at
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/](http://packages.ubuntu.com/)
and search for idle for you distribution. you can download debs directly there, but remember, if you do things this way you'll need to make sure you have all idle's dependencies also downloaded or on the installation cd, its a bit of a pain :)

for example, for karmic, see
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/karmic/idle](http://packages.ubuntu.com/karmic/idle)

[EDIT] or rather
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/karmic/idle-python2.6](http://packages.ubuntu.com/karmic/idle-python2.6)
