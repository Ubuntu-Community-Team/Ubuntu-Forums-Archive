### Post by ChuckFrain on 2007-07-29
Our next meeting of the Ubuntu Maryland Loco Team will be Thursday August 16th at our regular location at the [Howard County Central Library]("http://www.hclibrary.org/locations/central.php").

After a very successful inaugural Ubuntu Install Fest on July 19th, we're preparing for our next one on Software Freedom Day September 15th. 

Our main topics for the next meeting will be:

SFD presentations. Who and what do we have arranged so far?

Install Fest. What do we need to correct/prep from our experiences the last time.

Advertising. Where and how?

Feel free to post your ideas.

### Post by rhoderickj on 2007-08-01
Just a reminder to all that the agenda for meetings is open for everyone to contribute to. That doesn't mean, however, that all topics can be discussed in the allotted time. To add an item to ANY meeting agenda, just visit the meeting's wiki page. For example, this meeting is the August 16th meeting, so just go to: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MarylandTeam/Meetings/2007August16]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MarylandTeam/Meetings/2007August16")

If the meeting page doesn't exist yet, you can either create a new page and add your items or you can wait until I put it up. I try to get all meeting pages created at least two weeks prior to the meeting.
