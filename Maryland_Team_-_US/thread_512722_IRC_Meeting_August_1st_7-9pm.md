### Post by rhoderickj on 2007-08-01
> **matthewboh said:**
> Okay - here's a dumb question - how do I register for IRC?

There's no registration necessary. To log on, all you need to do is download an IRC client. My personal favorite is XChat, but others also like irssi and Gaim/Pidgin. Once you've downloaded the client, you initiate an IRC session by going to an IRC server and then choosing a channel. In our case, you'd go to **irc.freenode.net** and our channel is **ubuntu-maryland**.

You can choose a nickname and, if you want to, you can register it so no one else can use it, but registration is not mandatory. For more information on IRC, see [this page]("http://freenode.net/faq.shtml") and [this page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InternetRelayChat") and [this page if you choose to use XChat.]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/XChatHowto")

### Post by ChuckFrain on 2007-08-01
And for our friends who are on still on Windows I like [Chatzilla]("http://www.mozilla.org/projects/rt-messaging/chatzilla/"). Mac and Ubuntu users can also chose Chatzilla if you wish.

Depending upon your browser configuration (FireFox+Chatzilla works for me), once you have an IRC client installed you may be able to click on this link to [#ubuntu-maryland]("irc://irc.freenode.net/#ubuntu-maryland") and be taken directly there.

Remember that if you do not use the above link, once you are logged into the irc.freenode.net server in chat box type in '/join #ubuntu-maryland' without the quotes to find us.
