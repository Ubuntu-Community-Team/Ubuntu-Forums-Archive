### Post by rhoderickj on 2007-07-23
> **phr0ze said:**
> Should it follow the already designed Loco Team logo? This logo can be found on the DIY marketing site.

You mean the banner? That banner can be used, as its format has already been approved, but we aren't limited to it. Most of the other US LoCo teams use the Ubuntu logo (just the circle) overlaid onto the outline of their state, or something to that effect. Others use state branding, such as state flags or state animals. And still some US team don't have a logo. That's also an option. There's no mandate that we must have a logo. Personally, I don't see a team logo as being necessary, but it does a bit of polish to the wiki/web site. 

For examples of team logos, look at the [Kentucky Team]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/KentuckyTeam"), the [Colorado Team]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ColoradoTeam"), the [Pennsylvania Team]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PennsylvaniaTeam"), and the [Ohio Team]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/OhioTeam").
