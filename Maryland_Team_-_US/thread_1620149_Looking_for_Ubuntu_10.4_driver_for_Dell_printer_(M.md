### Post by Benanov on 2010-12-17
A quick google search says this post is #1 hit for "Dell MFP2335dn Linux" which is sort of unfortunate. :(

[http://developer.novell.com/yes/104495.htm](http://developer.novell.com/yes/104495.htm) seems to imply it'll work with SuSE, but SuSE 9 is *old* and that's the "enterprise" version anyway; who knows what that driver actually is?

I will admit I've stayed away from Dell printers for this reason - I'd rather go with HP because HPILP is installed by default & the drivers for most printers are Free Software. (Some require binary firmware and should be avoided.) If Dell techs can't help you, best to reboot into Windows to print (which is *really* annoying) or use another Windows machine as a print server.

I am aware that HP printer supplies are expensive. I found a nice barely-used 1100A laser that serves our needs well.

As for triple boot, I don't do that, so I'm not sure what your options are.
