### Post by ChuckFrain on 2007-11-25
This week the US Teams Project is sponsoring three IRC chats focusing on Reporting, Improving, and Fixing bugs. Each lesson will be about an hour long at 8pm eastern in [#ubuntu-us]("irc://irc.freenode.net/#ubuntu-us") on irc.freenode.net. They will be Monday, Wednesday, and Friday nights.

So for those who are interested in getting started down the bug hunt path, please feel free to attend. For more details, check out the entry on the [Ubuntu-Tutorials]("http://ubuntu-tutorials.com/2007/11/24/us-teams-education-week-reporting-improving-and-fixing-bugs/") site.
