### Post by aboutblank on 2008-02-25
[Mailing list]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-md") - Announcements and alerts
Forum - Discussion on events, etc
[Wiki]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MarylandTeam") - Information on events, meetings, and progress on objectives
IRC - Chatting

Our IRC channel is #ubuntu-us-md on irc.freenode.net. For those who have a default handler in your browser for IRC, you should be able to follow [this link]("irc://irc.freenode.net/#ubuntu-us-md").

New to IRC? Check out [this wiki page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InternetRelayChat") for an introduction and [this tutorial for xchat]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/XChatHowto").
