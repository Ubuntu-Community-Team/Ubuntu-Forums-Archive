### Post by rhoderickj on 2007-07-11
> **phr0ze said:**
> I added my self to the Wiki Member list. However I do not see how you were able to get your Wiki Names to appear as links. Please PM me to tell me what I did wrong.

It'll only autolink the name if it appears to be a WikiName. Your name doesn't conform to the standard name, which is in CamelCase. So, to link your name, you'll have to add the link manually. In your case, you'd make it:

```
[:Phr0ze]
```

...which will link to: wiki.ubuntu.com/Phr0ze
