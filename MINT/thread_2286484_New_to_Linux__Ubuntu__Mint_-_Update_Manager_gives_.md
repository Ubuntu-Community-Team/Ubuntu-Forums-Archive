### Post by Dreamer Fithp Apprentice on 2015-07-14
I concur with all the above, and the Mint forum is pretty good. Nevertheless, a few additional thoughts:
Even if the MInt forum should be your first resort, it is nonetheless true that a heck of a lot of things work pretty much the same in any Debian family distro, so it isn't at all unreasonable to look here or in the Debian forum for answers. To expand on what Deadflowr said a bit you get that error message when you when the package manager can't find the software you told it to fetch. That could be because the repository has moved, because it no longer exists (presumably the case here), or just because your internet connection was briefly interrupted or dramatically slowed.
