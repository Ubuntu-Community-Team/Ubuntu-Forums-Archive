[SIZE=3][FONT=arial][Solved] 

Change grub by "sudo gedit /etc/default/grub"

GRUB_TIMEOUT=-1 #10 to -1
GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX_DEFAULT="quiet splash acpi_osi=Linux" # quiet splash nomodest to quiet splash acpi_osi=Linux
GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX="acpi_backlight=native" # vendor to native

save, update then restart: 
[/FONT][/SIZE][SIZE=3][FONT=arial]sudo update-grub


After restart, the folder is back, and keyboard shortcut is working too. :)[/FONT][/SIZE]
