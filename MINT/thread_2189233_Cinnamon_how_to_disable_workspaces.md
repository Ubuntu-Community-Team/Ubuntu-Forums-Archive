### Post by buzzingrobot on 2013-11-23
By default, Cinnamon uses two workspaces.  If you have disabled the Hot Corner, ctrl-alt-up-arrow  will display them.

Cinnamon does not create workspaces automatically.  You will use only a single workspace as long as you do not deliberately move to another workspace.

Open and minimized windows in a workspace are displayed as small rectangles in the panel if the "Windows List" applet is installed. (Right click on the panel and select "Add to panel...".)  The icon at the far right of the panel that you used is the "Window Quick List" which pops up a list of all open windows in all workspaces.

If you add the "Windows List" applet and it does not appear immediately, log out and back in. You can relocate the applet by turning on Panel Edit Mode (right click on panel) and dragging the applet.
