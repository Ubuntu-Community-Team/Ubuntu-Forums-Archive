### Post by QIII on 2022-02-10
No distribution can provide everything, nor would they necessarily want to.  The fact that a distribution -- in this case apparently the entire deb side -- does not choose to maintain a specific application you want is not "sad".  Purveyors of distributions are under no obligation to provide exactly what you want.  Perhaps what you think is of particular importance to you is not so to others.  I don't think what you are looking for is the latest killer app that no distro should be caught without.  You are quite free to choose a less "sad" distribution.
