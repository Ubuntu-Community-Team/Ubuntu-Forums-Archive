### Post by buzzingrobot on 2016-04-26
Mint has a GUI tool that will display, and offer to install, any available and appropriate proprietary drivers.  I think its in Settings, and I know it's somewhere in the menu.  Probably called "Device Manager" or something similar.

A video driver can take several minutes to download and install.  Avoid canceling out if you start thinking something has gone wrong because it's slow.
