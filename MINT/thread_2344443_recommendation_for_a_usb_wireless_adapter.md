To be clear, Mint and Tails are not Ubuntu. They are Mint and Tails. Not the same beast, although Mint is *based* on Ubuntu, it ain't it. No idea about Tails.

It should also be noted that if a wireless device works fine in Ubuntu, no guarantee the same will be the case in Mint, although it is probable. Then you need to consider Tails. Who knows what works in that but it may bear no relation to what works in one or both of the other two.

You could perhaps try posting on the Mint user forum (and Tails one if they have) as well.
