### Post by gh4mint on 2021-09-23
Mint Kernel **5.4.0.81** works ok. 
Many users are complaining about the update to Mint Kernel **5.4.0.84 & 85** opening the DVD tray on cold boot.
The problem does not happen on warm reboots.

The work around is to fall back to the 5.4.0.81 Kernel, delete the new *.84 or *.85 Kernel and stop updates for those Kernels.
The problem appears to occur on "Desktop" PC,s as newer "Laptops" do not have DVD drives.

Please fix this problem !!!
