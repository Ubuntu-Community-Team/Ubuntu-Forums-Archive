### Post by oldfred on 2014-01-20
Moved to other OS since Mint.

I have seen some UEFI/BIOS settings for older Gigabyte boards. Often some setting in UEFI that does not seem related makes a difference.

Intel drivers often need boot parameters and have many updates recently. Do not know about Mint but newest Ubuntu works better. It often is not just video driver, but kernel & all supporting software.
