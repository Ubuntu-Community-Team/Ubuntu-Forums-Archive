### Post by sudodus on 2021-11-26
Please explain what you have and what you did to get what you have.

'the LM icon in the center' indicates that you have Linux Mint and not Ubuntu. It you have a mixture of the two operating systems Ubuntu and Linux Mint, it might be best to

- backup your personal files that you want to keep
- make a fresh installation of the operating system that you want to run
- copy back your personal files from the backup to the fresh operating system.

Such an operation is likely to be at least as fast and easy as a repair operation, and the result is probably better.
