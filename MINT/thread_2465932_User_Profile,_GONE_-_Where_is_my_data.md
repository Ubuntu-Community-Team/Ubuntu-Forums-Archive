You are not being asked to tag anything "Mint". You are being asked to stop posting Mint questions in the Ubuntu official flavours sections. This is not the Mint forum. This is the Ubuntu forum. Although Mint is based on Ubuntu, it is not an official "flavour" of Ubuntu and the Mint developers have gone their own way leading to significant differences between the various types of Mint and Ubuntu and its official flavours.

Linux Mint has its own forum here: [https://forums.linuxmint.com/](https://forums.linuxmint.com/) . It is an excellent forum and you may be better off with Mint questions there. Although users are welcome to post Mint questions here, they should do so only in the designated Mint sub-forum, in the knowledge that this may not be the best place to get support for Linux Mint. Nor is it the best place to get support for the other 60+ distros based on Ubuntu which are not official flavours of Ubuntu.
