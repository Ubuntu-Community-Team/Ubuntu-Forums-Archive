### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-01-19
I see you are from Canada so the videos should work if you have all the correct hardware and software installed.
A full list of system requirements is available from [http://www.foodnetwork.ca/faq/](http://www.foodnetwork.ca/faq/) and I suspect it may be a problem of either needing a browser that can play DRM content (try google-chrome) or perhaps you have not installed adobe-flashplugin from the canonical-partner repos.

I can not play the videos as I am in UK, but neither do I know if it would be possible were I in Canada as I can not get to the video at all.
