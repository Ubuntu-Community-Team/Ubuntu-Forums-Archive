### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-08-06
I am running Linux Mint MATE 14, so your desktop environment may be different.  In MATE, Alt-F4 is assigned to close the current window and Control-Alt-Del (the Windows 3-finger salute) brings up the logout/suspend/shutdown dialog box.  You can reassign these functions in mintMenu (Preferences==>Keyboard Shortcuts).

You can also assign a direct action such as Alt-F4 could be assigned to *pm-suspend*.
