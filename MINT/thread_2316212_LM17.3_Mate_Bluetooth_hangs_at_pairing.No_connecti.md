### Post by martin154 on 2016-03-07
thanks for all the suggestions [IMG]https://forums.linuxmint.com/images/smilies/icon_surprised.gif[/IMG]

finally got it working, I would like advise of my findings in the hope someone else can benefit.

Seems the problem was blueman not loading the module-bluetooth-discover on startup. i removed blueman, installed the ppa deb [http://ppa.launchpad.net/cschramm/blueman/ubuntu](http://ppa.launchpad.net/cschramm/blueman/ubuntu) trusty main 

then reinstalled blueman. now the module discover starts at startup and all is working.

hope to have helped!
