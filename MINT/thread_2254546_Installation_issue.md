### Post by bapoumba on 2014-12-16
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/search?keywords=lsb-base&searchon=names&suite=all&section=all](http://packages.ubuntu.com/search?keywords=lsb-base&searchon=names&suite=all&section=all)
lsb-base >= 4.1+Debian11ubuntu7 is in Utopic or Vivid (-8). Trusty has the -6 version. This is why you cannot install galileo.

One option would be to upgrade one of your machines to Utopic, provided you are aware this is not a LTS (Long Term Support release). Utopic end of life will be in July 2015. Depends if you want to stay with an LTS or are ready to upgrade on a regular basis.
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LTS](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LTS)

Edit : forgot you were on Mint. Here is the release schedule : [http://www.linuxmint.com/oldreleases.php](http://www.linuxmint.com/oldreleases.php)
