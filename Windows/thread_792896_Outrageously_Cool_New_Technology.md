### Post by Novega on 2008-05-13
My brother showed me this and I was blown away. It looks to be from 2007, so some/most of you may have already seen it.

Because it's actually two separate things, "*Seadragon*" and "*Photosynth*". It's hard for me to really explain it.

**What it basically is: **
Multiple (thousands/millions)photos are linked together in a collection, your photos get tagged by metadata from other peoples photo's. Example: I take a picture of a city's landscape today, it gets added to the collection...my photo will now act as a starting point for me to further explore individual buildings, based on the picture you snapped of a building two weeks ago:) It pretty much creates seamless hyperlinks between images, based on the content inside the images, allowing you to zoom into individual details and aspects of the content of your photos.

**Youtube Demonstration:**
[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s-DqZ8jAmv0](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s-DqZ8jAmv0)

**Photosynth Website:** 
[http://labs.live.com/photosynth/](http://labs.live.com/photosynth/)
*(I haven't tried the demo yet, b/c you must be using XP or Vista...but it still provides the overview:) )*
