### Post by voided3 on 2007-08-11
Me too. Then came this:

[http://www.istartedsomething.com/20061029/royale-noir/](http://www.istartedsomething.com/20061029/royale-noir/)

It's the Royale theme from the Media Center edition, but black. Installs very easily into your C:\windows\resources\themes\ folder and works wonders for appearances if you're sick of blue/silver/olive green and the classic skin. It also includes the blue Royale theme should you want to use it. The best thing is you don't need Window Blinds or anything like that since it's an actual Microsoft theme (read the article for details). Enjoy!
