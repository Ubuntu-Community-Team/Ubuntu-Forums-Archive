### Post by Calash on 2008-10-03
Reload from scratch.

Windows really does not have a clean downgrade path, and even there upgrade option leave junk all over the place causing more problems then it is worth.

For stability your best bet is to backup your data and reload XP, formatting the drive when you do it.
