### Post by erlyrisa on 2007-02-27
There is one of those 'Wizards' for setting up home networking. - There should be a link when you are in our 'Network Devices' or 'Network Folders'  the link (by memory) is something like 'Setup a home network' or 'Setup networking'

-Follow the prompts.

Note: Internet connection sharing maybe another issue, if you require to share your netconnection from a hosting computer.

If you already have a network setup, and all you wish is to add your laptop to your network via WiFI. Then maybe a call for setting up your Gateway computer will be necessary, in which case a little bit about IP addresses and such needs to be learnt.
