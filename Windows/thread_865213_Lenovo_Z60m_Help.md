### Post by abgemacht on 2008-07-21
This sounds as though it could be a hardware, rather than an OS problem.  Take the previous posters advice do back up your data before you do anything else, but be aware that removing windows may not solve this problem.

If you can, I'd check to see if your fans are working properly and if your computer seems to be overheating.  A computer will turn off if it is in danger of damaging the hardware due to overheating.  Try leaving it off for a while to allow enough time to cool down before you try it again.  That way, you should at least be able to back up your data if this is indeed the problem.
