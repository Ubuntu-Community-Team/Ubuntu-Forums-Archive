### Post by Izek on 2008-11-17
Flashing in windows (nor linux) is not recommended, but most computer companies make you do this nowadays (IE: WinPhlash.)

You usually determine if you need to flash the BIOS if your BIOS version is older than the one on the computer company's site.

I recommend flashing via a floppy drive though.
