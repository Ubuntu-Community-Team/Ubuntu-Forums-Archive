### Post by y@w on 2008-11-19
Whether or not you really need to reinstall, if reinstalling in a timely fashion is really a problem then you need to assess your backup/recovery procedure. What happens in the event of a hard drive failure? Will it cripple your business if that single machine failed? Then it's time to change your strategy. You really shouldn't be using a "server" as a workstation for reasons like this.

For the record, disabling the Windows Messenger service doesn't fix the problem. It only suppresses the symptoms.
