### Post by IYY on 2006-12-08
[LIST]
[*]Moving windows around by holding Alt and dragging it with the mouse. Moving the mouse to the tiny title bar just to move a window is silly.

[*]The Mac 'expose' effect that we now have thanks to Beryl.

[*]A quick and easy way to force an application to quit, immediately (xkill, killall, force quit).

[*]A system like apt-get to instantly download and install applications.

[*]A terminal with a real shell, in which you can write scripts and perform complex operations on your system. 

[*]Better organization of menus. Most Windows users are reduced to cluttering their desktop with icons because their menus are too cluttered and require too many clicks (first pick the company's name, which you may not even remember, out of a huge list that fills a screen and a half, then pick the application, and then select 'launch').

[*]An easy way to change themes, and a selection of themes to choose from.
[/LIST]
