I'm a bit confused as to why you'd want to set it up so that you could connect to the internet on your laptop through your desktop computer? 

Why do you not hook your laptop directly into the router and use the internet connection that's coming from the modem?

Sounds to me like you're having problems getting your wireless card to work in Ubuntu, as opposed to getting an internet connection into your computer via a hardwire.

If you want an internet connection on your ubuntu laptop get a ethernet cable and connect your laptop into the router. No need to bridge the connection through your Windows XP computer. 

If you're having wireless card problems, welcome to the ubuntu community. My experience, there will be no one way to get the problem fixed, there will be like 10 different ways.. if you're lucky one of the ways might work...... maybe. 

If you're trying to get wireless to work, ask in the networking community how to get the wireless card for your specific laptop to work. Or run a search for "your laptop name and model" + "wireless card"
