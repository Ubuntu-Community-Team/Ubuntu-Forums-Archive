### Post by QIII on 2015-08-25
Virus/malware scanning and cleaning up startup programs are good things to do as well.  I've helped with this sort of complaint quite often and have always found everything including the kitchen sink running at startup.

Registry cleaners are somewhat effective, but don't just download from some random website.  "We'll clean up your registry (to make room for our own malware)!"
