### Post by raul_ on 2007-11-27

in the middle of my gaming, every 5 minutes the game would minimize, and I would get a horrible 800x600 desktop resolution and a little box saying that I had to restart my computer, and the timer was going down, like my computer was going to explode or something. So i click "thanks, i'll restart later". 

Well I don't know about Windows, but when I say i'll restart later...i mean LATER. NOT NOW.

and 5 minutes later, same story. i was anxious about a button that said "YOU IDIOT, I SAID I'LL RESTART LATER!!!".

oh well, I was in the middle of a LONG mission (in Fable you CAN'T save during missions) and I guess I was pressing so many buttons, that in the middle of a terrible battle, that annoying box came up again, and by accident I restart the computer, losing everything. 

Now just imagine you're working, get out to get a cup of coffee, that thing starts counting, and when you come back YOUR WORK IS FREAKIN GONE!!! you didn't ask, you didn't close, you didn't even save, it's just GONE.

so to all your Windows developers:
