### Post by oldfred on 2014-03-26
If you did not make an image of your Windows and also deleted the vendor recovery partition which replaced giving users DVDs to allow reinstall, you have two options. 
Purchase a full new copy of Windows.
Or some vendors may offer their recovery image for a nominal charge. Some have reported that works, others say vendor refuses to offer help.
One user went back to the store he purchased it from and sales still had same model for sale and was willing to make the DVD/flash drive set from the recovery partition. Recovery set is not a full Windows installer but just an image of pre-installed and configured Windows for your specific system/model.

Your product key is in your UEFI and only works with vendor recovery image for your system. You cannot use that for a new install otherwise.
 Product key now in UEFI/BIOS
