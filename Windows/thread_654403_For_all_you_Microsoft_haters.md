### Post by toupeiro on 2007-12-31
No, not funny really.  Kind of sad really.  I don't see Microsofts place in the console world as a bad thing.  Though I don't own a 360, I did own the original X-box and it was a good system.  I think they were able to bring a lot to console gaming that was lacking, from their PC contributions.  Unfortunately, they also brought with it some bad vulnerabilities. 

I think Microsoft's X-Box live service was something of a milestone for console gaming and a flagship service that I hope one day all other consoles can live up to.  I mean come on, The Dreamcast was offering online play (seganet), and the X-Box was the only system to truly capitalize on that market and evolve it?  In my opinion, what gave (and still gives) the 360 a competitive edge over the fun-factor and price tag of the wii, and the beauty and raw power of the PS3 was its ability to leave both systems in the dirt when it comes to online multi player and online content.

I, for one, hope they resolve the issues soon.  And I hope Nintendo and Sony are taking good notes...
