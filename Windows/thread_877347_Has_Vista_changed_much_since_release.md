### Post by fiddledd on 2008-08-02
If you can get it pre installed, it works just fine. I've used it for months on my Laptop, which isn't high spec, without any problems. It boots < 35 seconds and shuts down < 5 seconds. It's fast and stable, and has been from the start. It does NOT slow down over time, this may have been peoples' experience with XP, but it's not happening to me with Vista. It uses all available memory, but it caches it, similar to Linux, and releases it when needed by applications etc. Total memory it actually uses is about 600mb with all the fancy Aero stuff enabled. I have integrated gfx and Aero runs perfectly OK. I have not had a BSOD (I'm not sure they even exist in Vista), or any lockups, and my Hard Drive is not constantly in use. 

That's just to give you an idea of my experience with Vista. I think people need to accept that if it's pre installed (and you've not been conned into buying a machine that's under spec) many people are more than happy with it, as many are with Linux. I think that people who believe Vista will help others go over to Linux are getting their hopes up a bit too much.:)
