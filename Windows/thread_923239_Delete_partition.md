### Post by smoker on 2008-09-18
you could boot the ubuntu live-cd and use the partitioner (gparted) to delete the partition, and expand an other partition to take up the empty space, or you could download the 'gparted live-cd' from here:
[http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php)
