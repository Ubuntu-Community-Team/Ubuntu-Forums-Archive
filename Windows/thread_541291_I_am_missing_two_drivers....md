### Post by HereInOz on 2007-09-03
Have you installed the motherboard drivers from the CD which came with the motherboard (if you still have the CD)?  If not, then you can perhaps download the mobo drivers from the manufacturer's website.

Most boards these days have integrated everything, and the drivers for those things come on the motherboard CD.

If not, check out what cards are plugged into the PCI slots, and if you can't see them in the device manager, then you will know that it is one of the things which still need installing.

How quickly we forget the heady days of Windows 98, when none of the devices would be installed, and you would have to hunt up drivers for the whole lot.  Now, when two devices are not recognised, it is a Vista problem.  

My bet is that those two things are integrated peripherals which are part of the chipset on the motherboard.   Hopefully that motherboard has Vista drivers available.
