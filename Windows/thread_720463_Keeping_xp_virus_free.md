### Post by Bungo Pony on 2008-03-10
The best way to keep it virus / trojan / spyware free is to NOT connect it to the internet. Failing that, don't use the internet for downloading or browsing, especially with IE. A hardware firewall would probably do you good as well.

I personally hate installing memory-resident garbage to keep out other garbage, so I don't blame you for finding alternatives to keep your system clean.
