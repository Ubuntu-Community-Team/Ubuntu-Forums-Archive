### Post by Bungo Pony on 2009-01-04
> I have tried mixing those 2 with the realtek audio manager but the built in microphone screws up the speaker. I get a loud pitch sound from the computer.

You're getting feedback. Basically, the sound is coming out your speaker, going into your microphone, back out your speaker, into the microphone, going in a continuous loop of sound.

The easy way to fix this is wear headphones or earphones to hear the sound while you're recording.
