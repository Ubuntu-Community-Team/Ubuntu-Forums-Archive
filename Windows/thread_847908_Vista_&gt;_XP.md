Not that I'm an expert, but from personal experience OS crashes and OS issues have a lot to do with your hardware. If your hardware came preinstalled with Vista, XP won't run all that great on it, and mostly you won't find drivers for all your special hardware peripherals that would run on XP, as they were also designed mostly for Vista. Linux sometimes doesn't support certain hardware, and vice versa. You can't generalize - I've heard of all sorts of cases, positive and negative. If you support the Open Source philosophy just experiment until you find the perfect distro that suits your specific hardware.

I am assuming that you did want to wipe out Vista from your computer, and that's why you didn't opt for a dual boot?
And did you make sure if the CD was all right?
