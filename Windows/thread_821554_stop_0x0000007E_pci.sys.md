### Post by fiddledd on 2008-06-07
I'd guess that the drivers in XP service Pack 1 aren't compatible with your motherboard. You may need to install XP Service Pack 2 (download it then use Nlite to slipstream it). Another option is to download the latest drivers for your Motherboard and burn to floppy, then press F6 during setup and load them from floppy. The second option would be easiest.

EDIT:
Just thought you may not have a floppy drive, in which case integrate the latest drivers into service pack 1 using Nlite.
