### Post by darksong on 2007-09-03
I use AVG free or Avast anti virus on my PC. Both are good, AVG has a better UI and is more user friendly. For a firewall (if you have XP) i would use comodo comparable to sygate's old firewall, its free and fully featured, i has a good UI, doesn't bug you about commercial products and is recourse friendly - hopefully it will be vista compatible soon. If you want a vista firewall i would either put up with windows own one or wait for comodo, zonealarm is not worth it in my opinion - i would only ever put it on a PC of which no one has computing experience (which i done for a friend as zone alarm's firewall gives advice on weather to accept or deny)
