### Post by Maheriano on 2009-02-13
I just read the Microsoft licensing agreement last week and it says you're allowed to resell it as long as you destroy all personal copies of it, provide them with all yor hard copies and licenses, as well as ensure they have the same access to the license agreement that you did. For example, you can't resell it after removing the agreement from it somehow. So yes, you can sell.
