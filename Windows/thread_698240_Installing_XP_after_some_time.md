### Post by sethjvm on 2008-02-16
Anti-Virus: Avira Anti-Virus or Avast!  Avira has no mail-scanning, a nag screen, a few more false positives but overall tends to score higher than Avast on AV comparisions.

Firewall: Online Armour is pretty slick and does not fail leak tests according to a few comparisons I have seen.  Good second options are Comodo or Kerio personal firewall.  ZoneAlarm's free firewall has really fallen off.

Software: OpenOffice, Foobar2000, EClean, JKDefrag, Exact Audio Copy, Filezilla client and server, AutoGK, Notepad++, Foxit Reader, Firefox, Opera.

I get by with 1.5GB Ram on a 2 year old compaq desktop.
