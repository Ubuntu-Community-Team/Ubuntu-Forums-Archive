### Post by Boomy on 2007-09-20
I was always one of those guys who never used the terms "M$" and "Windoze". I always thought it was immature, and based on anticorporate feelings. Well I just got a job in IT support, it's my first job in the field of computers. I used to use Windoze at home and always managed to keep it working relatively well. Now in defence of M$, the server/client relationship between MS Server and XP is a good concept. The active directory works well at setting permissions, shared printers, etc. I don't think anything like this exists in Linux, and is probably the main reason for M$ so commonly used in office PC's. But damn is Windoze some bloated, crappy, unstable software. 

I've been using Ubuntu on my Athlon 2500 for almost 2 years now. I really haven't used Windoze at all at home since discovering Ubuntu, and I never realized how FAST and TROUBLE FREE it is until starting this job. Holy ****!! I don't believe how freaking slow 'doze is, even on new machines with 2 gigs of RAM and the latest CPU's, it doesn't compare to my home PC! Applications crash all the time, and often it's a frustrating chore just to do a simple task. I have not had a crash or glitch since installing Feisty. Not one!! Computing Nirvana! Hell, running 2003 Server as a virtual machine on my box is snappier than on some servers I've used!! I've got a whopping 512MB of RAM!!


What the hell is all this antivirus stuff and security updates? Hasn't M$ found a solution to that yet??  Damn, the size of the updates is bigger than than the whole install of XP!! Is it that bad to require all the added bloat? I personally wouldn't use all those updates, but it's a requirement of my job, and I think they really slow the machine down and make it less stable. 

And then there's Vista. Of course it's buggy; it's a new M$ release. Add a psuedo sudo,new gui,  move some things around and call it a brand new OS.....yeah right. At least Ubunut gets better with each release. 

All I can say is at the end of the day, when I come home and use MY pc, it is the fastest, smoothest computer I have used all day. Right now with Feisty, I have the most stable, smooth operating computer I have ever owned or used.

I just had to rant a little because Windoze really is a joke of an OS.
