### Post by Overbyte on 2007-09-11
> **mindtrick said:**
> I couldn't stand Vista more than a week. It was eating up my all ram and giving me compatibility problems even with popular software, games etc. I saw no reason to keep it. All of this for a theme, no good.

Even without Aero the reason it sucks all of your RAM is the Superfetch feature (basically turns your RAM into one big cache). It's supposed to make commonly used programs run faster over time by preloading them into memory...it's nice though. You can turn it off in Control Panel > Admin Tools > Services > Superfetch. Reboot and watch the RAM **not** drop to 0 :)

Compatibility problems: it's because of the new driver model and kernel PatchGuard...supposed to isolate the kernel from nasty stuff. But UNIX has done that for years...and it's a command-line OS ;)
