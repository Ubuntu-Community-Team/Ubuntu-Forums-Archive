### Post by Izek on 2009-01-30
> **diwas said:**
> But i want to surf in chrome.

Brand new browsers and browsers that haven't been in circulation for a long time or don't have as much popularity as Firefox don't get extensions, patches, etc. that allow you to normally remove those things without doing hefty javaScript, CSS, etc. like in Opera. Your best bet is to just bear Firefox and use Adblock or NoScript. I prefer NoScript, as Adblock has issues with displaying certain images, AND it causes issues with deviantART. NoScript, does not. (Exa: NoScript allows you to block individual domains from using JavaScript, Flash, etc. to render stuff. I always have googlesyndication blocked--though it's unblocked by default--so that those annoying ads on DeviantART, etc. don't show.)
