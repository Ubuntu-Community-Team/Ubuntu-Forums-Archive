### Post by mikazo on 2008-07-27
> **Sef said:**
> Check out [TRK]("http://trinityhome.org/Home/index.php?wpid=1&front_id=12").

This seems like what you are looking for... it's a live CD distro, just like Ubuntu, so you won't be installing anything that will risk potential recovery of your Program Files.

From the TRK website:

> -recovery and undeletion of files with utilities and procedures

Edit: You would most likely have to run TRK from a USB stick, unless you can burn another CD with a secondary CD drive while running Ubuntu. I imagine there are tools for Ubuntu for undeleting NTFS files, though I don't know if they would be included on the Ubuntu disc you are currently using.
