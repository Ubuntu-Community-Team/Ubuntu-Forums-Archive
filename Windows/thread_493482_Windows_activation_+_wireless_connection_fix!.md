### Post by osxdude on 2007-07-05
**NOTE:** You have to be in Windows to do this.

Do you experience problems when activating Windows over the Internet? Do you use a wireless connection? Well, here is how to fix it.

DO NOT close Narrator. Press Super+U once logged in. "Narrator" will come up. Click the icon in the corner. Click "About Narrotar..." Click the link to a Microsoft web page. Your default browser will load.

If Firefox comes up, type "C:\" in the address bar. Find a TXT file there and click it. Click "Open File."  Notepad should open. Go to "File > Open..." Now, go to where you want. Once you find a file you want, like the IE excutable,, right-click it and click "Open." Follow the steps for "If IE comes up"

If IE comes up, enter "C:\" into the address bar. On the sidebar, under Places, click "My Computer." In the same place, click "Control Panel" in the same place. If you are in Classic View, go to Category View. Click "Network and Internet Connections." Under "or choose a control panel icon" click "Network Connections." Double click your wireless device, choose a network, enter the wireless network key, click "Connect," and your are connected!

Now, go to the "Activate Windows" window using Alt+Tab and activate!

Yay! All done! (there was waay to many  quotations in there)
