### Post by smoker on 2008-09-07
SP3 makes a restore point before installing, so start in safe mode, then choose the system restore point before the installation. i believe you can also go to add/remove programmes and remove from there. if you don't want it back, disable auto-update, and manually update what you choose whenever you choose.
