### Post by Imsati on 2007-04-27
Change around the borders to make it look Ubuntu-like and you have a nice desktop background there :)

Not sure exactly how (or if) Hidden Files work in the Recycle Bin, but why not give it a shot...

Open up Recycle Bin.
Select TOOLS, then FOLDER OPTIONS.
Click on the VIEW tab, then look for the Folder called 'Hidden files and folders'.
Click the bubble next to 'Show Hidden files' and Apply the changes.

Worth a shot. At the very least it may give you something to Right-click and use Properties to figure out a bit more about.
