### Post by Kernel Sanders on 2008-03-05
I downloaded and installed it.

1) Visually, it's no different from IE7

2) There are no extra customizability options, which means you can't customise your toolbars, and that irritating "EMULATE IE7" is immovable, which means that it's in the way of the home button.

3) It's quite buggy, and some web pages are all over the place, which is odd considering that Firefox passes acid2 and manages to render those same webpages fine?

4) Scrolling is jagged and on some websites keeps returning you to the top of the page randomly.

5) After using this for 5 minutes I find it buggier than Firefox 3 when it was in Alpha

6) Pretty much the only thing they've done is made it Acid2 compliant, which is good, but just brings it in line with the other browsers rather than offering anything unique or groundbreaking.

7) I'm quite disappointed really.

8) Open source FTW!! :p
