I think Microsoft are trying to convey the idea that their figurehead (Gates) is just a normal and average guy, someone with which regular Joe can identify with.

In the past they have been accused of 'Astroturfing', they probably want to build up genuine grassroots support for their products.

The Apple advertising campaign was spread over a number of different commercials with an interlinking theme. This is just Microsoft's variation of the same method.

They have invested much in this endeavour, chump change to Microsoft. But I would think the investors and shareholders would wish to see a return for their money, especially in these perilous times.
