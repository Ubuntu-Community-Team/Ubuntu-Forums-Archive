### Post by oldfred on 2018-01-07
Do not know.

But with my old XP system back in 2009, the video card blew out its capacitors, took out motherboard & I replaced power supply, not knowing for sure if it was good or not.
So new motherboard (but same Intel CPU), new video card & new power supply & same drives.
I rebooted into XP and large error screen popped up. Took me a while to realize there was a smaller re-authorize window behind the larger one. I was able to click on it and it worked.
Not long later I totally retired XP.

Of course Ubuntu just worked. New video card used same video driver, so that was not even an issue.
