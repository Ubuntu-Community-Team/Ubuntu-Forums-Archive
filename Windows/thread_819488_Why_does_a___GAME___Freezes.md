Uh, there are a lot of possible reasons here...

1. Not enough system memory.
2. Video card is not powerful enough.
3. Power supply is not outputting enough power.
4. Video card drivers may be out-of-date.
5. Hard drive could have a few bad sectors on it.

The list could go on...

Use MemTest86 and test the memory. Swap out the video card with something better. Check the rated watts on your PSU and perhaps swap it out for something more powerful. Go to the ati.com website and download updated drivers for your video card. Run Chkdsk on your hard drives and see if anything comes up.

Hope this helps!
