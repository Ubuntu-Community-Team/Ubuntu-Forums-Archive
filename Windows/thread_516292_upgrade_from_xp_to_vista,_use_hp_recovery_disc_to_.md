### Post by cmat on 2007-08-05
This is how most recovery disks I made are used. Make your CD/DVD drive first in the boot priorities. Then the recovery disk will boot up and start the process. Make sure the drives are the same in size, no partitioning since the disk took a snapshot. If the drives are resized the whole process may fail.
