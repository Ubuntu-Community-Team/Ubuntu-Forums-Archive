### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-07-15
for those that do frequent installs of windows, [here]("http://driverpacks.net/DriverPacks/") is driverpacks. just download each driver pack and combine them on cd (will take 2 cd's for this. you could use 1 dvd, but not everyone has a dvd drive) and whenever you need a driver, just pop in the appropriate cd, and update driver through device manager. it has almost every driver imaginable. has saved my @ss a few times out on jobs. especially when they dont have any of the original cd's.
