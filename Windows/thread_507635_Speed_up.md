### Post by smoker on 2007-07-23
disable all your 'startup apps' and it will boot far quicker,

go to start, run, type in 'msconfig' (without quotes), then select the startup tab, and in the box below, untick every box on the left apart from antivirus and firewall apps. save, then reboot. after reboot, another screen will appear, tick the box to get rid of it.

the more boxes that you unticked in msconfig, the quicker your pc will boot,

best of luck

ps - if you have norton, get rid of it for something lighter (and free) like avg free.
