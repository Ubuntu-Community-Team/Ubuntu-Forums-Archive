### Post by Viranh on 2008-10-21
I've had this happen before. The automated answering system responds to some word sort of related to "person." Try yelling "transfer me," "representative," "human," etc. I seem to remember that Microsoft's in particular responds to screaming curse words as well. After this, when the phone rings in India, depending on your luck, you may have to insist on a transfer to their supervisor because some of them can only read from a script and don't really know English. Also, look up a different help number if it really refuses to transfer out. The one I called never offered an option for a person and hung up at the end, but it would transfer when I screamed at it. Barring this, try to find a volume key. They check the key before the OS installs without an internet connection. No hassles. Good luck.
