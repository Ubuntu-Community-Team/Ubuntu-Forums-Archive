### Post by StooJ on 2008-10-15
Like Joeb454 says, even if you DO get a genuine disk I doubt it will work.

Windows serial numbers are version and type-specific. Your serial number will only work with an OEM version of XP, and possibly only a batch that Toshiba have, so borrowing a disk from a friend won't help (genuine or not).

Your best bet is to contact Microsoft about it and tell them you wish to reinstall, but don't have a disk.
