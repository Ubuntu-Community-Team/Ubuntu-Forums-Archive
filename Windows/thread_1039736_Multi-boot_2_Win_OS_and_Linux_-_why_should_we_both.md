### Post by I-75 on 2009-01-17
Regardless of what OS you want to use. I still think that the better way is to use separate hard drives for each OS. 

Less chances of screwing up a installation, no partition problems and no possible future grub issues to deal with. 

With laptops, usually there are three or four screws on the bottom to remove and the hard drive usually just slides out.
