### Post by Tar_Ni on 2014-05-13
The support of Windows 8 ends in October 2015. It will be required to upgrade to Windows 8.1 to keep your PC updated. Users with a Windows 8 license can do so for **free**. Just follow the instructions on the Microsoft website. So basically two years remains before the support of Windows 8 end.

By the way Windows 8.1 is supported until 2023 so once you've upgraded you'll be able to remain on this version for a long time.

Here is an article that explains it: [http://www.bit-tech.net/news/bits/2013/10/14/windows-8-support/1](http://www.bit-tech.net/news/bits/2013/10/14/windows-8-support/1)
