### Post by Midwest-Linux on 2008-01-09
> **hotrod6657 said:**
> how do you like that transformation pack?  It it a trial or a totally free program?  I was looking around and everything says free trial, i just don't want to install it and have it only be "free" for like a month or something lol.

 It looks just like Vista, it is a free program. I do believe, however that the Transformation pack # 8 is a trial/pay interface. The one I downloaded was from Major Geeks was Vista transformation pack # 7, and I also had to download Windows Blinds 5. All it really is just a GUI interface transformation. its still XP, but it looks just like Vista.

They do warn you however that the transformation pack could change some things in your system files and that Windows Updates could revert the transformation pack into a XP interface. Also I had to delete one of the XP updates before I could install it. 

My advice is to do a restore point and back up your files before installing it. My Gateway is a older machine so I didn't mind trying the transformation pack on it, if It didn't go well I could just do a system restore.


Do some Googling before attempting the transformation, I believe there are forums out there for this.
