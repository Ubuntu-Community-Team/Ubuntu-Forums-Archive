### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-04-30
It is completely legal to have more than one copy of Windows on your computer with different serial numbers, and probably okay to use two copies with the same serial number on the same computer. It is illegal to have a single copy of Windows on more than one computer at a time (registered with the same serial number). As far as 64-bit and 32-bit versions, my suggestion would be to read the license; but I'd bet that you aren't allowed to do that.
