### Post by caljohnsmith on 2008-09-13
> **Rytron said:**
> Hi,
I dual boot Xp and Ubuntu.
I use a limited account for internet use. However I was wondering would running Firefox in [Sandboxie]("http://www.filehippo.com/download_sandboxie/") in an XP administrator account rather than in a limited account be just as safe as running firefox in a limited account for internet use?
I think it is just as safe with Sandboxie, probably even safer: with a limited account, Firefox can still save its cache and other files to the HDD. But with Sandboxie, Firefox still saves its cache and other files to your HDD, yet once you shutdown Sandboxie, all those traces are completely wiped clean as though you never ran Firefox in the first place. So even if you run Firefox in Sandboxie under the admin account, your Windows is entirely protected from any possible malicious effects from Firefox.
