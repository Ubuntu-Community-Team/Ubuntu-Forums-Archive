### Post by fiddledd on 2008-07-11
Go to Control Panel and select "Network and Sharing Center" in the sidebar select "Connect to a Network". If that doesn't work select "Set up a Connection or Network". Just follow the prompts on the Wizards. Afterwards make sure your Firewall isn't blocking the new connection. You may also need to delete the old connection that was for your router, as Vista won't know it died.
