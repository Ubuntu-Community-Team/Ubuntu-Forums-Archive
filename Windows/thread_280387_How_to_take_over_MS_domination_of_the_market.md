### Post by meng on 2006-10-19
I'm one of those who believes that the OS landscape will change gradually, and largely independently of any active steps that most of us can take to promote Linux. The danger is that taking certain steps can actually set back "the cause".

e.g. insisting that all your friends install Linux: this runs the (IMO high) risk that someone who takes your advice runs into some problem, minor or not, goes running back to Windows and forever more has a negative opinion of Linux due to a bad personal experience.
