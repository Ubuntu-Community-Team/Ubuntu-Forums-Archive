### Post by Bungo Pony on 2008-06-05
One of the beauties about Win98 is you have the option of changing the name of your Windows directory at install. When I installed Win95, I called the directory "Win95". The same went for Win98. A lot of viruses and trojans will specifically look for C:\WINDOWS. When it's named something else, they can't find it and cannot infect your machine.

That's why I was a bit pissed when they forced C:\WINDOWS as the only place to install Windows. Go figure, I've been infected more times in one year than I was infected running Win95 in 5 years.
