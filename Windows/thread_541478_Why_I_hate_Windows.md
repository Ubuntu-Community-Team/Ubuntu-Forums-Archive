### Post by HereInOz on 2007-09-03
One great little program is Spywareblaster from Javacool software.  It is free software which uses a list of known bad sites - currently around 8000 odd, and configures your browser to ban any download or activeX installs from those sites.

Manual updates of the list of sites are free, or you can get automatic updates for a very modest annual cost.

Because it uses the capabilities of the browser to ban downloads from known bad sites, there is no process running in the background taking up CPU cycles.  It works well, and blocks most of the stuff before you even get it.
