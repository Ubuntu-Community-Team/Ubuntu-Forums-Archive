### Post by falcon61102 on 2008-11-29
To address your question about long time usage, there are a couple things you can do to ensure better performance in the long run.

If the laptop is going to be on 24/7, restart it at least once a day to clear the RAM and cache.  Also, disable any of the many startup programs that come installed that your mom doesn't need to have running.  Vista is enough of a resource hog on its own, all those programs just make it worse.

As previously mentioned, Zonealarm is a great firewall app that will keep out a lot of useless crap.  Highly recommended especially if you disable Windows Defender.

Another thing you may want to do is set it up to automatically defrag the computer at least once a month for just general usage.
