### Post by Gallienus on 2008-09-05
1. Right click on the desktop.
2. Choose 'Properties'.
3. Click on the 'Appearance' tab.
4. Click on 'Advanced' button.
5. From the 'Item' list, choose 'Window', then select the colour you want.

(You can also change the colour of pretty much anything -- menu bars, etc.)
