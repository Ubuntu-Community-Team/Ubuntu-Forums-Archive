### Post by smoker on 2007-08-09
just a suggestion, how is the cooling, are all fans working ok, and plenty air flow around your graphics card? try running with the case open for a while if you think overheating could be a cause. maybe check the card is seated correctly by removing and reinserting. if the card has a separate power connection, double check that also.

also, try running in safemode. if you get no problems in safe mode, i would try uninstalling and reinstalling the graphics drivers again.

you can easily check your monitor by connecting up to another machine.

best of luck
