### Post by jeremyswalker on 2009-10-14
I have not tried the Moblin Remix myself. However, I do know that they are not the same thing. Ubuntu Moblin Remix integrates with the [Moblin]("http://moblin.org/") software stack and interface. Ubuntu Netbook Remix utilizes the [Netbook Remix]("http://www.canonical.com/projects/ubuntu/unr") interface.

### Post by igknighted on 2009-10-19
It's not a matter of better or worse, they are just different.  UNR is just ubuntu/gnome with a new layout, so it is more stable, but Moblin does some nifty things too.  Why not try both?  You can test them from a USB stick without installing anything to your comp.
