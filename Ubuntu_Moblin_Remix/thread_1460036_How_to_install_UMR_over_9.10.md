### Post by JimTaverna on 2010-04-28
Offhand, I'd say shrink the partition and dual-boot, assuming you originally used all 160gb on the drive for 9.10. Given that you only have 100gb of data on the drive, the 60gb should be more than enough to install Moblin.

Use gparted live on a flash drive, then hold your breath. Or you can do what PhilMize said and just buy an external drive and be safe.
