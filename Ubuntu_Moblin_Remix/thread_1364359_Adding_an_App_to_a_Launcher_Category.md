### Post by labinnsw on 2009-12-26
Right click the desktop icon and select properties
Menu
[ATTACH]141259[/ATTACH]

Make a note of the configurations under the "Open With" or "Launcher" tab.
[ATTACH]141260[/ATTACH]

Open the menu editor. (System >> Preferences >> Main)
[ATTACH]141261[/ATTACH]

On the left highlight the category. On the right click "New Item."
[ATTACH]141262[/ATTACH]

Fill in the information from the desktop icon properties.
[ATTACH]141263[/ATTACH]

Clik OK and you're done.
