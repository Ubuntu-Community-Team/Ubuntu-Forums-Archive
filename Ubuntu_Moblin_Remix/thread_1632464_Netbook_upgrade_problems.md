### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-12-01
Welcome to the forums :-)

In addition to above post, you can click on the Ubuntu logo in top left corner of your screen and search for any apps there.

Once the app is open and visible in the launch bar, right click that and add to launcher to keep it permanently.
