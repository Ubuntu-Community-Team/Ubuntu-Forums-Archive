### Post by jthirt on 2009-12-07
You may be experiencing similar issues as described [here]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/netbook-remix-launcher/+bug/368394"). 

I also had trouble with it as I tried it on many PCs ([see related post]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1325096")) and it sounds like not all graphic card drivers support the technique used to implement this menu. 

If your video card is an intel and you are brave enough, you may follow the instructions [here]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/netbook-remix-launcher/+bug/368394/comments/55"). It worked for one of my laptops. 

Otherwise, I use it on a netbook for a few weeks now and I really like it!

Good luck!
