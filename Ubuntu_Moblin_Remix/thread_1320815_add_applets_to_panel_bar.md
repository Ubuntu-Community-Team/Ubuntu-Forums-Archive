### Post by littletinman on 2010-01-11
I had the same problem. This is what i did, 

Right click on the little "handle" for the "notification area" and untick "Lock to panel" then move it over so that you have about one icon worth of space to the right of all the notification icons. Right click on that space and choose "Add to panel".

Does that make sense?
