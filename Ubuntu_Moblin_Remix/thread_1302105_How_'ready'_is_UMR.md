### Post by mikewhatever on 2009-10-26
There has been no reports of special power related optimizations for UNR. You might want to try an lpia port, which should save around 10% of battery life, just be aware that the installer is text based and not as easy to use.
Moblin is a good option, but you don't get the Ubuntu repositories, unless we are talking about Ubuntu Moblin Remix.
