### Post by Kirboosy on 2011-02-17
Sounds like an error that is causing compiz to crash. Do your pretty effects stop working as well?


If you have the CompizConfig Settings Manager (CCSM) installed you can easily look at the settings. 

System-->Preferences-->CompizConfig Settings Manager


If not you can install it via Synaptic (System-->Admin-->Synaptic)


Now once you have the settings on your screen Navigate to 

Effects > Window Decoration. Is the box checked? If it is click on the Window Decration button to open an advanced menu.

Copy and paste whatever is in the "Command" line (could be similar to /usr/bin/compiz-decorator but if its not don't change it...yet)
