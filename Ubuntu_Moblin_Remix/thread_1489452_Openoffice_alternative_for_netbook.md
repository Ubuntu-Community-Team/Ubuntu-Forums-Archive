### Post by Hagar Delest on 2010-05-22
If OOo is slow, try first to [reset your OOo user profile](http://user.services.openoffice.org/en/forum/viewtopic.php?p=58403#p58403). But during the welcome process (if you had 2.x before), don't transfer your personal data, former configuration files might corrupt the new profile.

If no change, try the Sun version: [[Ubuntu] Installing OOo on Debian and Co.](http://user.services.openoffice.org/en/forum/viewtopic.php?f=16&t=68)
