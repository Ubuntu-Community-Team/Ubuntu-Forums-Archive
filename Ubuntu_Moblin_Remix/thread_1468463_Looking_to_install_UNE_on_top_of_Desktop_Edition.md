Actually, you'll have your choice of NE or NE2D, along with Gnome and xterm. Change "sessions" (bottom panel) at login screen or "System>Administration>Login Screen" if you log in automatically.

(Note: Don't see it? Check "System>Preferences>Main Menu")

Whatever you choose becomes the default.
