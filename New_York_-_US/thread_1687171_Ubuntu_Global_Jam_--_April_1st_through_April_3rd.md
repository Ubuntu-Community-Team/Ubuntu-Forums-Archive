### Post by cprofitt on 2011-02-13
I wanted to make you aware of the Ubuntu Global Jam date.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuGlobalJam/](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuGlobalJam/)
[http://loco.ubuntu.com/events/global/567/detail/](http://loco.ubuntu.com/events/global/567/detail/)

A global bug jam is an loosely organized international event that focuses on contributing to the success of Ubuntu and the Ubuntu community. As a team we can work on testing, upgrading, documentation, or answering support questions. This participation can be done solo or in a group. It can be held in a private location or in a public setting.

As a member of the Ubuntu Beginners Team I have a broad familiarity with the processes involved in these areas. If you or a group would like to participate in the Ubuntu Global Jam please contact me and I can assist with the details.
