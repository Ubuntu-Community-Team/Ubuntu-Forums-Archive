### Post by fragmented_user on 2007-04-01
Hey guys, 

As many of you may know Feisty will soon be released (on the 19th of April 2007). In celebration of this event many LoCos from across the globe will be having their own release parties where they will meet to party, have fun, and of course install as many copies of Feisty as they are humanly able too. We too would like to take part in the celebration by holding our own release party, right here in New York! 

Planning for the release party has already begun and we urge you to take part in the planning for this great event. Find out more ways to get involved by signing onto #ubuntu-ny on irc.freenode.net, posting a reply on this thread, or submitting a response to [the mailing list]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-ny"). 

The NYC party will take place on Sunday the 22nd at 3:00 PM at Think Coffee 248 Mercer St. go [here]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NewYorkTeam/Events/FeistyReleaseParty/NYC") for RSVP and more info.

See you at the Party!

-Yosef
