### Post by cprofitt on 2008-11-03
If you live in New York State and would like to help us advocate for the adoption of FOSS solutions, and Ubuntu in particular, please take the steps below:

_Sign up:_

[LIST]
[*]Join the launchpad [NYS Loco Team]("https://launchpad.net/%7Eubuntu-newyork") on Launchpad.net
[*]Subscribe to the [Ubuntu-us-ny mailing list]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-ny")
[/LIST]
Suggested:

[LIST]
[*] Review the [Ubuntu Code of Conduct]("http://www.ubuntu.com/community/conduct") and place a signed copy of the C.O.C on your Launchpad account ([directions]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/GnuPrivacyGuardHowto")).
[/LIST]
 
IRC channel:[INDENT]#ubuntu-us-ny on freenode
(if you are not familiar with IRC please refer to the [XChat how-to]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/XChatHowto") for more information)
[/INDENT]If you need any more assistance or have additional questions please feel free to message me on the forums.

The New York State Loco Team appreciates your interest and looks forward to getting to know you better.
