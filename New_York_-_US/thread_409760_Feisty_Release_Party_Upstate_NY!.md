### Post by Hendrixski on 2007-04-14
[SIZE="4"]I would like to announce the upstate NY release party for Feisty on Saturday April 21[/SIZE]

It shall take place in the city that houses the international headquarters of Kodak and Xerox.  The city with the beautiful High Falls and the one and only Tahous restaurant.  [SIZE="3"]Rochester NY![/SIZE]  

Everyone who lives between Syracuse and Rochester should attend, and:

[LIST]
[*]Meet fellow Ubuntonians
[*]Share your Ubuntu stories
[*]Upgrade your install, or install it on a friends computer
[*]Discuss future possibilities, events and more
[*]have a great time!
[/LIST]

We should hold a meeting no later than Tuesday.  I know that it's short notice, but it's worth the hustle.

Edit:  You need to register for the installfest with LUGOR at [http://www.lugor.org/ifest2007/](http://www.lugor.org/ifest2007/) that is taking place from 9am to 3 pm.  We'll meet at Jitters Café afterwards for trivia (with prizes) and to talk about open source. This should take about an hour or two and we would wrap up at around 5:00.   I may hold a separate Pub Crawl if anyone is interested.
