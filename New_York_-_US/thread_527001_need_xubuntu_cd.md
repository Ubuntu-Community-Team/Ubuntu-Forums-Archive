### Post by Amanda HazLaPaz on 2008-05-31
I was taken with Xubuntu's look/feel, too, and from reading the Absolute Beginner forum I learned that one can go into synaptic, search-->All by "name" using "xubuntu" as the search term.

Mark for installation the appropriate packages, and then on  log-in, you choose the "Xfce" session. This can also be made the default session, so you'll always get Xubuntu/Xfce log in.

Of course, it's also good to have an installation CD.
