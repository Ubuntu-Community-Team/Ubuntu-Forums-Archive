### Post by trippinnik on 2007-06-05
the best one I have found is devede (it's in the repos), sudo aptitude install devede
but I've also used QDvdAuthor, toDisc and Vive!  Devede has a simple interface, just choose the movie(s) you want to burn the DVD, VideoCD, SuperCD or whatever and follow the menus.  You'll get an .iso for DVDs.  QDvDAuthor had a lot more options and was able to make some custom DVD menus, but it didn't always work for me.
