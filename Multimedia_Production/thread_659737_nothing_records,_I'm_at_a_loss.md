### Post by kdwillia on 2008-01-07
Did you enable the capture in the sound properties?

Right Click on the Speaker and Open the Volume control.   Click on Edit and Preferences and see if you have selected Microphone Capture and Capture.   Mine worked with Capture selected.

Before I enabled those, like you I could not record anything in Audacity or even Sound Recorder.
