### Post by jlh68 on 2008-02-22
Good News Ubuntu users.

I ordered the CITRONIC AC-1 USB Audio Capture Device from Great Britain.  It cost me $87 todal including shipping.  The best part was the instructions.  Well the unit worked fine with my laptop and Audacity.  

Now for added good news:  The Citronic instructions were enought for me to use my ADS Instant Music device also with Audacity.  

The key is in the Audacity set up.  You have to pick the input as USB codex and the output as the same.  You have to change the output to ALSA to playback.

Bottom line is both devices work with Linux (Ubuntu 7.10)

I will try to post a How To on the Ubuntu Forums later.:)
