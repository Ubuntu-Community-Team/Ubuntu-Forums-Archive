### Post by markekeller on 2008-03-27
I highly recommend [Kdenlive]("http://www.kdenlive.org/").  It's a very easy to use, yet quite powerful program.  It's for the KDE environment, but it ought to run in GNOME, too.  I like it better than Avidemux and Kino, personally.

And, if you ever feel like using some *real* power, try [Cinelerra]("http://cv.cinelerra.org/").  But it's a little more difficult to use . . .
