can use the file-open command to import them into Kino.

### Post by Jeremy23 on 2007-07-31
That's because AVI is just a container format. It doesn't necessarily contain MPEG video, as is common.

Just as you can open JPG files from your digital camera in Ubuntu without installing restricted codecs, Kino can read the motion JPG files from your AVI file, as they're just JPGs, basically.
