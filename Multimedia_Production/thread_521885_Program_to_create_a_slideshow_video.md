### Post by Tsu Dho Nimh on 2007-08-12
It's really not glamourous, but OpenOffice can bring in the clips and do a timed slideshow ... even exports ot FLASH (SWF).  I've never tried using a soundtrack, but I know you can embed audio clips.

Kino itself can import still pictures as "clips" of whatever length, but it can't edit the soundtrack.  If you have music already, make the pictures separately, load the soundtrack and start bringing in clips. You can adjust the timing, do dissolves, etc. 

Then save as whatever ... FLV or MOV or ????

### Post by stmiller on 2007-08-12
Digikam can create an mpeg slideshow, if you can make your content into individual images.

Then you can put music to the video with Kdenlive or LiVES.
