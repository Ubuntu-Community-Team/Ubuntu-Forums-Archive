### Post by IanW on 2007-07-12
Open K3b, and click on the bar labelled "Further Actions" BELOW the standard burn options (DVD data, DVD image, etc).
This will open a menu of ALL the types of disc K3b can make. DVD Video is 7th down from the top (on my copy, anyway).

NB. You MAY need to re-encode the .mpg file to be DVD-compatible first. For this I recommend Avidemux, which has an "Auto" menu for doing this.
