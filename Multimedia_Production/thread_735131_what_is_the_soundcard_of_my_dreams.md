### Post by robeast on 2008-03-25
The M-Audio Delta 1010LT has audio and MIDI I/O. It's a mere $200 for a 10 ins and 10 outs. Probably the best value you can find. Any other interface that has JUST 2 audio I/O is going to cost that much or more. Check out the second link in my sig for a full list of compatible hardware. If you want MIDI and audio I/O you're looking at $200+, although there are very cheap MIDI I/O options.
