### Post by eye208 on 2008-03-19
According to [this page](http://www.wikihow.com/Work-with-JVC-.MOD-Files) MOD files are really just MPEG-2 streams. Any video editor that handles MPEG-2 should be able to read them. Otherwise you can probably use MEncoder, FFMPEG, Avidemux etc. to convert them.
