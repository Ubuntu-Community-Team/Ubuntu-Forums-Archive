### Post by kayosiii on 2007-05-24
A reasonable work around is to do your text in Inkscape (much more capable IMHO)... Export as a bitmap from Inkscape and import into gimp... You can drag and drop from a file manager into your gimp image window. and it will become a new layer - just make sure you resolution settings match up.

But beyond that the best thing would be to ask the gimp developers themselves.
