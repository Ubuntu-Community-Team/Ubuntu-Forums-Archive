Solved it... For future reference,
Installed avidemux 
Changed the following settings:
Video: Mpeg4
Audio: FAAC
Format: MP4
Found the info [here]("http://linux.vanvalkinburgh.org/2007/05/25/stream-videos-to-xbox-360-with-patched-ushare/")
