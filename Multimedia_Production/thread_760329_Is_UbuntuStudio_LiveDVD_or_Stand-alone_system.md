### Post by logos34 on 2008-04-20
Techinically, you could install the ubuntustudio-desktop pkg and linux-rt kernel, then choose the latter at startup and login to ubuntustudio instead of kde.  I have a mixed system like that, but the opposite (running standard x86_64 kernel on 64-bit ubuntustudio 7.10--because I can't hibernate with -rt kernel and other issues).  Personally I'd reinstall.
