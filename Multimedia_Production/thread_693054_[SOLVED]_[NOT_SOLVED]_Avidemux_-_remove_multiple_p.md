### Post by eye208 on 2008-02-11
The usual workflow with Avidemux is like this:

[LIST=1]
[*]Load the video.
[*]Mark start and end point of the part you want to cut out.
[*]Select "Delete" or "Cut" from the "Edit" menu.
[*]Optionally repeat steps 2 and 3 several times until all the unwanted parts are removed.
[*]Save the video.
[/LIST]
