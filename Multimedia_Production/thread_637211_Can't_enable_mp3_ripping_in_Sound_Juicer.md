Hi there, 

Sorry, I just reread your post and realized that you already did what I suggested.
The only other thing that comes to my mind is the little active checkbox in the preferences for the encoding profiles for sound juicer. That sounds stupid, but this was one of the problems I had when I didn't see the MP3 format in Juicer. Go to Sound Juicer->Edit->Preferences-> Edit Profiles. Chose the MP3 format and hit edit. Make sure that the active checkbox is checked.
