### Post by nalmeth on 2007-11-01
Good ole Coast to Coast, I haven't been able to listen in a long time. Is Art Bell still doing his odd shift?

Anyway, what program streams the show to you in the first place? Totem (Default in firefox)? You'll want to turn up the source volume. If totem-mozilla won't do this, maybe you'll want to change to mplayer-mozilla
