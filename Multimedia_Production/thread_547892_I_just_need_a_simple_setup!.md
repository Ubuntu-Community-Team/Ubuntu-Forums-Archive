### Post by nalmeth on 2007-09-10
Hey staticvoid, I just replied in your other thread about the jack problems

+ 1 for Ardour! If you're purely interested in working with audio, I think there is no better Free Software than Ardour for that. If you want to use VST with it, you have to compile it yourself because of the licensing problems with the Steinberg VST SDK. Here are instructions for that: [http://ardour.org/building_vst_support](http://ardour.org/building_vst_support)

A sample of other useful audio related utilities would be:
Jamin (mastering software)
jack-rack (ladspa plugins interface)
jackeq
patchage
