### Post by Richard Kut on 2007-09-08
First, get streamripper installed so that you can rip music through the streamtuner interface. To do that, open a terminal and type:

> sudo apt-get install streamripper

Then add the URL for sky-fm in stream tuner. My URL for SKY.FM Jazz is:
[URL="http://ware.catv.ext.ru:8136/smoothjazz_1_sky128.mp3"]
http://ware.catv.ext.ru:8136/smoothjazz_1_sky128.mp3[/URL]

I believe that there are other categories of SKY.FM music in the Xiph tab of streamtuner.
