### Post by nalmeth on 2007-06-09
(1) edit .wav files
Audacity
Rezound
Jokosher
gwc (gnome wave cleaner, discovered in other thread. I used rezound to do this, but I like gwc now that I've tried it)

Ardour is a hard-disc recorder, but has many editing features, you can apply realtime plugins, and do a lot of rudimentary editing. It's probably more than you need at this point tho

(2) sequence midi files
Rosegarden
LMMS
Seq24

(3) print readable sheet music (preferably from the midi files) 
I use tuxguitar for this
Rosegarden and LMMS also have this ability, I believe
There are many alternatives, just google

4) mix these .wav and .midi files down into a final product and export them into various formats for listening pleasure. 
Rosegarden
LMMS
