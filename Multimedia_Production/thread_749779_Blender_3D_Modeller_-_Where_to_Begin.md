### Post by cotcot on 2008-04-09
Loads of info in the Blender site. A good entry is the [[COLOR="Red"]manua[/COLOR]l]("http://wiki.blender.org/index.php/Manual").

Video editors : there are quite some editors under linux. Which is the best one ? Depends on your needs and wants and the learning curve you are willing to follow. So there is no 'best editor'. Cinelerra is a professional level editor and in that sense different from the others. Study the (good) manual carefully and you will find it great. Other like kdenlive, kino, avidemux, lives, pitivi, jahshaka, ... are easier with less features and sometimes in early stage of development.

There is the blender VSE (video sequence editor) as well. As you intend to learn blender I can recommend to try it it. Blender improved the video editor a lot in the latest releases. (I am running 2.45 and 2.46rc1). There is a sound issue (bug) in the linux version. Here is a solution :

The solution is to place the variable in /etc/environment
   # sudo nano /etc/environment
   place SDL_AUDIODRIVER=alsa
   restart the computer and launch blender
