### Post by FakeOutdoorsman on 2007-08-29
I believe MEncoder can do this, but it is command-line and might be considered difficult to use.  [Avidemux]("http://fixounet.free.fr/avidemux/") is a frontend / GUI for MEncoder I do believe.  If you want to brave the command-line, then get MEncoder from the [Medibuntu repository]("http://medibuntu.sos-sts.com/repository.php") and try these examples:

[mencoder converts rmvb to avi]("http://apple.sysbio.info/~mjhsieh/archives/000398.html")
[convert rmvb to avi]("http://www.fedoraforum.org/forum/showthread.php?t=104161")
