### Post by theacoustician on 2007-12-29
> **steelmole said:**
> I'm a bit stumped on this.  I kinda need to do it fairly quickly rather than nicely too.

Go to the FX tab.  If you want to create text over a black background, make sure you're creating a clip (option on the middle left of the page).  Remember, most video is approx. 30 fps.  If you want to make titles over video, then select overwrite instead.  On the right hand side of the page under video effect, choose Titler.  Enter your text in there. You can set where the text appears, the font and color, fade in and out, etc.  Good luck.
