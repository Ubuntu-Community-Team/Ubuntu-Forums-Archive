### Post by cb474 on 2007-10-04
I just installed Celtx. How can I make a icon in the applications menu for launching it?

[Edit: Nevermind, answered my own question. Right click on the applications menu in the panel and select "Edit Menus." Fairly self-explanatory form there. With my installation, the entry in the command field should read "/usr/local/celtx/celtx". Icons can be found by clicking on the icon button and browsing to the folder /usr/local/celtx/icons.]
