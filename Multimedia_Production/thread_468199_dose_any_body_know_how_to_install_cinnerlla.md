### Post by Cryophallion on 2007-06-08
Search the forum for Cinelerra, there are a ton of posts on how to install it.

For importing video, and for an imovie-esque experience, use kino.

For more full featured editing, use kdenlive.

Cinelerra in particular can be hard to learn for people new to NLE (non-linear editing), so if you are just looking for simple cuts, stick with Kino.

Search the forums here for video editing, and you'll see a ton of threads on the subject, and the pros and cons of the 6 or 7 most used editing programs.
