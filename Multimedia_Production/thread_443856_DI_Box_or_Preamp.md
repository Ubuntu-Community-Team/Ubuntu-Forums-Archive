### Post by elglas on 2007-05-14
Hello there

as another Delta 1010 owner, and someone who tinkers with recordings on occasion, I'd like to recommend that you do use some sort of preamp. a powered DI box might do the trick, but I think you should be looking into buying a small end mixer for flexibility's sake. This way if you need to record vocals, a multitiude of instruments, or want to just mic your amp for a more natural sound, you wont be that limited by hardware. I started off with a Behringer eurorack mxb1002 and I can say it gets the job done nicely for around $145 CDN
