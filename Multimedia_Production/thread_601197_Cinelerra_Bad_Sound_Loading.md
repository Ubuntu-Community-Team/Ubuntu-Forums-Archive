You have probably posted insufficient information for most people to be able to advice you. I suggest checking the [cinelerra mailing list ]("http://cvs.cinelerra.org/mailinglists.php")archives and see if others have used your specific camcorder and what workflow they are using. If you are using a DVD CAM, then ... check the mailing list.
  If you haven't already tested editing with Cinelerra, then try editing some sample files (not sure which ones, check the Cinelerra mailing list), and ensure that Cinelerra is able to play video and audio on your system.
