### Post by chipants on 2008-02-18
using compiz has given me trouble with apps that use opengl to draw to the screen, especially fullscreen.

the best thing to do at the moment is just turn off desktop effects while running the emulator.

also, some emulators, like zsnes, have support for non-opengl video modes. try picking a video mode that doesn't use opengl and that will probably fix the problem, without having to disable desktop effects.
