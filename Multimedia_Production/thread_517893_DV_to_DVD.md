### Post by rabbit83 on 2007-08-05
Kino can do this. Go to "export", then click the "mpeg"-tab, choose "dvd" and "burn with k3b". This works fine for me. I'm using Kino 1.0.0 from [http://www.getdeb.net]("http://www.getdeb.net")
