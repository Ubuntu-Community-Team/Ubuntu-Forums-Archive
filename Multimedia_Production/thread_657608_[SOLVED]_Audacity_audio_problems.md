### Post by radi0j0hn on 2008-01-03
There is some place on the Audacity menu where you select the output driver. Look at the two drop downs near the top, left. One iis for the output (has a speaker symbol) and the other for the input (has a mic symbol) I suspect it somehow jumped to another driver.
