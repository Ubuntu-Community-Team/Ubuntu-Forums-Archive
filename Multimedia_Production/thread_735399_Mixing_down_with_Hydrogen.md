### Post by robeast on 2008-03-25
No, Ardour will only export the tracks that it "owns". I am not very familiar with Hydrogen, but I assume it has it's own export feature, in which case you could export those tracks and import them into Ardour, then mix and export the entire piece from Ardour. You can also hook Hydrogen's outputs into a stereo track on Ardour and record them as it plays.
