### Post by robeast on 2008-03-26
I had the same problem with my Audigy. I recall that it had something to do with a reversed switch, meaning that the audio needed to be MUTED for it to be NOT MUTED! Right click on your audio volume in your top panel, select Open Volume Control and go to the Switches tab. If that is checked, try un-checking it.
