### Post by Stochastic on 2007-11-22
Well the software I'd recommend is mixxx [http://mixxx.sourceforge.net/](http://mixxx.sourceforge.net/) and their Hardware Compatability page [http://mixxx.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Hardware_Compatibility](http://mixxx.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Hardware_Compatibility) lists a few devices that should do the trick.  I should mention that the FAQ page specifically states that the Herculese DJ console will have excellent support in the upcoming release.

The other route is a bit more flexible and professional in my opinion, and that's to buy a turntable setup and run XWAX [http://www.xwax.co.uk/](http://www.xwax.co.uk/).  That way he can look that much cooler in front of the ladies.  Not to mention that the touch and responsiveness that vinyl offers just can't be matched by a jog wheel.
