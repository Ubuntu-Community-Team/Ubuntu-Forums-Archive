### Post by LCC on 2007-06-08
> **wcbardwell said:**
> I ran into a program called kdenlive ([www.kdenlive.org](www.kdenlive.org)). does anyone have any experience with this program? how is it?

I do and think that it is the best video editor for Linux out there the reasons why would be that it imports most of the known codecs (except flash videos) without having to wait like in LiVEs (with converts first to mjpeg)
Kdenlive is full of bugs but it is the only one so far that incorporated all the good aspects that a video editor must have (in my opinion)

.It supports most used codecs
.multi track
.export to several types of codecs (lots of bugs here)
.lets you add simple but very simple transition

 So I think that once all the bugs are gone it would be the best video editor out there.
