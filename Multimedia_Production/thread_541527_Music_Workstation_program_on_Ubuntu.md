### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-09-03
If you want a real-time kernel, with a collection of music production programs, then give dynebolic a spin.  It's a live CD that you can try and save your session by "nesting" your data on your harddisk.  This has the benefit of not mucking up your current installation with a bunch of music programs that don't run well without a real-time kernel.

dynebolic.org
