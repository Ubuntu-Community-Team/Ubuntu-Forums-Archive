### Post by gorgon on 2008-02-22
I found this quite confusing as well when I first did it (you're not alone:)).

If you want to stream from your pc, directly to the listeners, the 'host' info will be your ip-adress, and your 'port' can be ex 8000. An important point is also to make sure that this port is open for traffic in your router.

together this makes the adress for the listeners: [http://[your-ip]:](http://[your-ip]:)[port]/

If you got a dynamic ip adress (very likely) it would be smart to use a service like dyndns.org. In that way, if your ip adress changes, you can have an alias, a link, which stays the same.

I've tried to find free streaming servers as well, couldn't find any....

Good luck
