### Post by Stochastic on 2008-02-16
Presonus makes really nice sounding cards for the price, and they work great with the freebob/ffado drivers.

Check the listing at [http://www.alsa-project.org](http://www.alsa-project.org) to see cards listed by manufacturer.

Oh, and the xrun issue you're having might be able to be fixed if you increase the buffer size in the settings panel.  But this will increase latency.
