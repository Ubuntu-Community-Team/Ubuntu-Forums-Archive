### Post by chunderbunny on 2007-11-11
Unfortunately ATi's drivers do not yet support the video capture device. AMD are committed to releasing specs for ATi devices so it is possible that this feature will be available within the next few years, but I wouldn't hold my breath if I were you. 

Some standalone video capture cards do work well with Linux, the most popular of which would be Hauppage's WinTV cards.
