### Post by Shay Stephens on 2008-03-05
Go to stopmotions website and get the latest version.  I use it for my stop motion stuff often.
[http://developer.skolelinux.no/info/studentgrupper/2005-hig-stopmotion/index.php](http://developer.skolelinux.no/info/studentgrupper/2005-hig-stopmotion/index.php)

Note, the working folder/path can't have a space in it or you will have trouble.  There is a version in the repository, but it chokes on more than 30 or so files at a time, so get the 0.6.0 version from the website instead.
