### Post by ronnybob on 2007-09-16
I am looking for a program that will edit multiple sound files. Here's what I have, 
I have an audio CD that I ripped in K3B, it had 99 tracks, when I put that in my MP3 it will play with intermitten sound and some times if not often lock up.

What I would like to do is put those files in a editing program and combine all 99 into one file as one and be able to here it on my mp3.

---

### Post by Stochastic on 2007-09-16
audacity is probably one of the more user-intuitive sound editors and it has mp3 capability.  Just import the 99 mp3s to an audacity session and "Export session as mp3"

---

### Post by ronnybob on 2007-09-16
that worked, but the way to do it is to hold ctrl. down while you click on each file and that loads it into Audacity and the rest is history, thanks.
