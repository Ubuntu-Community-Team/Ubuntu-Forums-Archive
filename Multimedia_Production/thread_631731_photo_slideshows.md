### Post by Unterseeboot_234 on 2007-12-05
I like Kdenlive. It has a Slideshow as a Project option. You bring in a whole folder of photos or you have to make several folders. The thing is, it imports stills in gangs. You set the time per photo. Do a little cut and snip, if you like, on a multi-track Timeline with transitions. Export as DV(Raw) if you want to take it into Cinelerra as a video / DVD project with multi-track sound. Otherwise, I've found Kdenlive pretty impressive to convert both multi-track audio and video into whatever formats for what I'm trying to create. Kdenlive is in the Repositories. Cinelerra takes time to install and learn (but it IS powerful).
