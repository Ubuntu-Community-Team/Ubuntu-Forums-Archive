### Post by vanadium on 2007-06-04
Do not use Gnome baker for audio CD's. It does not support DAO burning which is required to burn gapless audio CD's. Serpentine is a dedicated audio CD burning app, and probably, it will do its job very well (yet I have still to see it myself: my experience with Ubuntu is limited). It takes ogg and mp3 files.
