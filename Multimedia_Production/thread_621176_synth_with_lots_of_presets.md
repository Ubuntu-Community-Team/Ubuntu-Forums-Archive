whoops, upon further research I realize I messed up that advice entirely.  according to this old site (an old cache from google) [url]http://www.google.com/search?q=cache:d7BXe9vtCGEJ:stdio.be/aui/studio/firststudio1.html+zynaddsubfx+soundfonts&hl=en&ct=clnk&cd=9&gl=ca&client=firefox-a
