### Post by jeffus_il on 2008-01-27
I suggest that you look for info about subtitles under Xine and not Totem, since I think it is the Xine Engine that does the work. You have to install a special font in a special location for Xine subtitles. If you are looking for other options, MPlayer also does a good job with subtitles, using a different system from Xine. You could try MPlayer and you may be lucky enough to get a more suitable result without doing any tweaking.
