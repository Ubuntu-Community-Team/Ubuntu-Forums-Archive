I have loaded a long video into kdenlive.

---

### Post by cotcot on 2008-01-21
Bring the video to the timeline.
Split the video with the "razor" tool and save the part with different names or select a zone on the timeline to render.
You can render a selected zone using --> file --> export timeline.
