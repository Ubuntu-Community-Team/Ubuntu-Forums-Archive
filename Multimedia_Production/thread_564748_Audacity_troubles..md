### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-10-01
Nothing wrong with what you see.  Click on the little red record button and the track or tracks you are recording will show up with a frequency wave according to levels.
If you want to export as mp3 you will need to do a search for the libmp3lame.so before you can encode to mp3.  In ubuntu there is no libmp3lame.so, but there is a libmp3lame.so.0 in /usr/lib.  Chose that, ignore the error message that comes up chosing yes to continue and all will work OK, or it did for me.
