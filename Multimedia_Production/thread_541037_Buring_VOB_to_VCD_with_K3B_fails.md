### Post by vanadium on 2007-09-02
Are you trying to burn video-DVD compliant VOBs to VCD? This can't work. For VCD, you need to produce a VCD compliant video stream (mpeg 1 for VCD, mpeg 2 for sVCD). VOB is a container format used for video DVD (the video is mpeg 2).
