### Post by netyire on 2007-11-01
Check the file's audio stream, right click -> properties -> audio/video. If the audio codec is anything besides mp2/mp3 or ac3, chances are dvd players can't handle them. Try following the steps here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=320389](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=320389) to recode and burn a working DVD.
