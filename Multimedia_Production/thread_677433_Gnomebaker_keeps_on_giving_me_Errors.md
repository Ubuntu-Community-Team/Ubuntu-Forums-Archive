### Post by disturbed1 on 2008-01-25
Gnomebaker is only a gui to wodim/growisofs/genisoimage, the same as Brasero, and K3b.

The errors you received break down like this.

SK-4h (Sense Key)
HARDWARE ERROR: Indicates that the device server detected a non-recoverable hardware failure
(e.g., controller failure, device failure, or parity error) while performing the command or during a self
test.


ASC-09h
SPINDLE SERVO FAILURE, TRACK FOLLOWING ERROR, TRACKING SERVO FAILURE, and on and on.

ACQ-02h (
CANNOT READ MEDIUM - INCOMPATIBLE FORMAT

Basically it means, the blank disc is of poor quality at the point the errors were encountered, or you attempted to burn more data than there is space on the disc.

Genisoimage has a known bug attempting to write more than 4gigs of data.


Your first error is self explanatory, 
Directories too deep........(7) max is 6
With the program you're using (genisoimage) and the options passed, you can not go beyond 6 directory levels. It's most likely a limitation of the iso level selected.


What's the final size of your of project? And have you tried creating an iso first, then burning that iso?


> 
Flash drive/everything..................


Is this a mounted USB stick? If so, copy the files to the hard disc first.
