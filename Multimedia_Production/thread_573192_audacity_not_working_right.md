### Post by Stochastic on 2007-10-11
There are LOTS - and I mean LOTS! - of different ways to record sound in Ubuntu.  Although Audacity is one of the older "beginner friendly" apps, I personally dislike it as I constantly hear people complaining that it's not working etc.. and I know I've had my fair share of troubles with it too.  A couple that you might want to try include:
-Rezound (my personal favorite editor)
-Ardour (might be a little overkill for a podcast)
-Jokosher (get the version in Gutsy or from their website as the other sources are quite out of date)

There are lots more, all you have to do is do a little research.
