### Post by lapcat66 on 2008-02-11
I used foobar 2000 under windows, and made the transition to Amarok.  Amarok supports media players (ipod shuffle & 4G among others), cover art, lyric searches, wikipedia artist bios, streaming radio, and loads of formats.  The Amarok appearance is not quite as customizable as foobar 2000, and the foobar 2000 tagging/file management abilities are superior.

However, foobar 2000 will run under WINE if you miss it!
