### Post by jnbek on 2008-04-02
I've used the built in Intel AC97 card on my old Celerons, and still am, in fact. With no problem, as for USB sound cards, I've not had any experience with those, but I'm sure you can get good results. The area you may run into problems will be with sync issues with multitracks. (Example: You record one guitar track and go to record a secod guitar track, and you double the first one perfectly, but as you listen to the playback the second guitar gets further and further out of sync.) This behavior is overcome by using a preemptive kernel.
