### Post by Sensenseppl on 2007-09-04
On the left side of your screen, you should have a tool panel. Near the bottom of this panel you should see 2 color-filled rectangles. The upper one of those represents the currently selected foreground-color. If you click in the foreground-rectangle a window pops up and presents you with the color-picker.

Over here, you see the color-picker picking blue (at the tool panel, the foreground-colour rectangle is also blue!):
[http://www.tomrichmond.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2007/03/color-picker-blue.jpg]("http://www.tomrichmond.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2007/03/color-picker-blue.jpg")

I hope this is what you needed.
