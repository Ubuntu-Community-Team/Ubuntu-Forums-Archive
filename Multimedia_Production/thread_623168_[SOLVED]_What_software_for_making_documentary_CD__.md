### Post by indytim on 2007-11-29
I don't believe that what you are looking for has been created in a cohesive application in Linux-land yet.  This is, in fact the sole reason why I need to keep Win2k around.  I use DVD Photo Slideshow to create dvd's of photo albums etc.  I use SlideshowZilla to create flash slideshows for the web.  Both app's can combine text, transitions and background music/voice if needed.  Neither app is free, but they get the job done.
