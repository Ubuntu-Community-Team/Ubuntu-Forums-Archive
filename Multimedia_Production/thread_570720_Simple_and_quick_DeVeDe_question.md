### Post by kelvin spratt on 2007-10-17
Just put both of your clips in the same title it should play them one after the other with a 1 second gap between them hopefully. You can also turn off the menu that way it will just start and play all the files you have added. There is a little button just under the left pane on the right  hand side, you can alter how it plays the menus using the options in properties. Note this is on the latest versions.
