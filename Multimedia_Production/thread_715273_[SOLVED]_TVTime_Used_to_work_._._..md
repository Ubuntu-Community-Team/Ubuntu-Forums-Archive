### Post by theophiles on 2008-05-27
The problem was that the update somehow removed my nvidia driver.

First I went to a terminal and typed

sudo apt-get install nvidia-glx-new

Then I went to
System>Administration>Hardware Drivers

Then I enabled my driver, restarted and all went well.
