### Post by chucky chuckaluck on 2008-03-19
i don't know if there is a quicker way to do this, but when you select the cropping tool, check 'fixed' in the bottom part of the toolbox and 'size' from the options beside it. you can then alter the dimensions you wish just below it. (see attached pic) you can then move the square around to where you like it, then hit 'enter' when you're ready to crop.

### Post by unutbu on 2008-03-19
If you want freedom to crop any sized square, click Fixed, but select 'Aspect Ratio' instead of 'Size'. Make sure the colon separated numbers below that are equal (of course).
