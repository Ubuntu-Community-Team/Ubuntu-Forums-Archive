### Post by Stochastic on 2008-03-31
The simple answer: 
no.

The positive answer: 
check out [http://osflash.org/](http://osflash.org/) those guys have quite a thing going on there.

The 'kinda how I see it' answer:
Nothing out there in the FOSS world comes close to flash, from what I know.  There are attempts to fix this, but since Flash has become such a web standard and is kinda [licensed by Adobe]("http://www.adobe.com/licensing/") FOSS is kinda forced to re-invent the wheel without infringing on copyrights yet still make everything playable in IE7 and Safari - not an easy task.

Keep in mind I'm no expert web dev, just a music guy that does some web stuff on the side, so I may be way off base.

You may also want to check out Open Laslo.
