### Post by paul928 on 2008-04-02
If you open IDJC, select Preferences then Microphone you will find various editable settings that will improve your microphone performance. For example, the hiss that you noticed can be helped by adjusting the De-ess setting. 

I hope that this helps. [http://ubuntuforums.org/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif](http://ubuntuforums.org/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif)

### Post by shawnjones on 2008-04-05
Another option is the Jack Rack. You could route your microphone signal into the Jack Rack and apply an equalizer or other type of filter to eliminate the hum on the line. Also, you should try to isolate where the hum is originating instead of just covering it up.
