### Post by byteshack on 2006-11-27
If you are using NX and Ubuntu, you might have noticed that when you connect remotely to your server you get nasty fonts.  By nasty, I don't mean that the fonts are ugly, they are the correct fonts, but distorted.  Can't see the i and the l.  Periods disappear from view.  And other random issues.

A way to fix that is to set you font to use "Best Contrast" or "Best Shape".

System -> Preferences -> Font

The issue seems to be with "Sub-pixel smoothing" choice.
