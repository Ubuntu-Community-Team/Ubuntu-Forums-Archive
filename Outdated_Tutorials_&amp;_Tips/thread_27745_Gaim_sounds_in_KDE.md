### Post by Wraithmonk on 2005-04-17
I've used this for some time now in KDE with various distributions, & have posted it in other forums. 
Open Gaim > Preferences > Sounds. Use "Command" & in the box type: artsplay %s
Quit Gaim, & restart. You should now have sounds. :)

### Post by -Rick- on 2005-04-17
