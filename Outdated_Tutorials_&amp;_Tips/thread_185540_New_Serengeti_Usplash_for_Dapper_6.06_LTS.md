### Post by j_baer on 2006-06-01
[QUOTE=benplaut]too much text...[/QUOTE]

I know what you mean ... A part of me says keep it simple and another part says make a statement.

Obviously the second part prevalied. Here's some thoughts ...

Product branding is not bad and we live in a world where everything is branded. (e.g. niki shoes). 

*Why not make ubuntu the brand for linux? *

I believe LTS will win big in the business community so let's state it.

My previous versions of this theme played on the symbolism of the sun rising in the sky. I think the ubuntu 
logo plays to that symbolism and says alot about the ubuntu community.

The statement "humanity to others" is too important not to be included.

Last, there's a bit of an allusion with the status bar. When installed you may ask the question,
"*is the bar moving or is a curtain being pulled away revealing the light behind?*"

Cheers ...
