### Post by najames on 2006-04-15
I have 3 AMD64 PCs and a dual Opteron server on a JustCom 4port KVM with sound.  One of them, a Chaintech VNF4 Ultra, would always turn itself on constantly, driving me nuts.  ](*,) 

I checked the BIOS several times, looking for wake on LAN, but I have it  disabled.  I finally realized there is also a wake on mouse, wake on keyboard and disabled them too.  When I was using the mouse/keyboard on another computer it was evidently sensing it through the KVM and turning itself on.  DOH, doh, DOH, doh!

For at least 20 minutes now it has behaved perfectly!!  Ahhhh.
