### Post by yetkin on 2008-03-01
SubSection     "Display"
        Depth       24
	Modes       "2560x1600_60"
EndSubSection

i havent seen a "_60" or something like that before.
 just my modes are  "2560x1600" "800x600" like that..
also  if you want to change your resolution i think it must be seen in xorg.conf
also when you are editing it be sure that you kill the kdm or gdm pid.
it can change that config automatically if its working(/etc/init.d/kdm {start|stop|restart|reload|force-reload}) it usually works on background with bootup even you dont use X.
also finally ...
sudo dpkg-reconfigure -phigh xserver-xorg
