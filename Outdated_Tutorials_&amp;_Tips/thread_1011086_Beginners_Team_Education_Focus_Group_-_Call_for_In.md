### Post by cprofitt on 2008-12-14
Hello Tutorial and Tip Makers

The Beginners Team Education Focus Group is looking for 'instructors' to offer 'courses' to Ubuntu users. These courses could be forum or wiki based only, irc only or use a combination of the two formats. (I am considering developing some content that could be delivered by local Ubuntu LoCo teams as well). Obviously many of the threads here are evidence of the great knowledge you all possess. I would love to invite everyone who has gotten a thread approved here to schedule question and answer sessions for people looking for help on the topics you have covered.

**Hypothetical topics:**

[LIST]
[*]How to install wirless drivers via a wrapper
[*]How to install a proprietary video driver
[*]How to configure UFW
[*]How to add additional repositories
[/LIST]
The goal would be to give more information about the general process than is typically found in forum posts.

Please feel free to ask questions in this thread or contact me directly.
[B]
More information:[/B]
[wiki page]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BeginnersTeam/FocusGroups/Education")
