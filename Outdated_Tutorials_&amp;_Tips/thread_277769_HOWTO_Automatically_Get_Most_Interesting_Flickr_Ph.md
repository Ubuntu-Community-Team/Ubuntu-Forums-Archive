### Post by thesamet on 2006-10-15
Hi All,

Webilder delivers stunning wallpapers to your Linux desktop, directly from Flickr and Webshots. You choose what keywords (tags) to watch for, and photos are automatically downloaded to your computer. Webilder can also change the wallpaper every few minutes.

[IMG]http://www.webilder.org/static/images/screenshot2.thumb.jpg[/IMG]

Webilder has installation packages for Ubuntu Dapper and Edgy available to download.

For more information and installation instructions, visit: [http://www.webilder.org/]("http://www.webilder.org/")

Suggestions and ideas are welcome!
