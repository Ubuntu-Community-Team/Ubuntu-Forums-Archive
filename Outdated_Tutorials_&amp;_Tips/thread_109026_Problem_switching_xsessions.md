### Post by saul on 2005-12-27
Hi, my problem was that i only could open one xsession (gnome or kde) and when i tried to open another one i only got a weird illegible screen :confused: . I have Hoary and Breezy but Hoary works fine. I took a look at /boot/grub/menu.lst and noticed that Hoary had the parameter vga=791 so i copied it to Breezy and problem solved :p .
