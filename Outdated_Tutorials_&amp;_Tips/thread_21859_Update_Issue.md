### Post by pseudonym on 2005-03-25
Have you added the extra repositories to your /etc/apt/sources.list file? You need these so that apt/synaptic points to places where newer versions of things like firefox and firestarter can be downloaded from. If you haven't already, go to [http://www.ubuntuguide.org/](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/) for the necessary steps on how to do this (just look down the list of how-tos until you come to what you want).

You should still be aware of the above tips, but you might find it easier to wait a couple of weeks and download the next release of Ubuntu, which includes newer versions of firefox etc.
