### Post by necromonger on 2010-11-16
change window title bar button layout using gconf-editor.

Press Alt+F2,and type gconf-editor in pop-up &#8220;Run Application&#8221; window,and then &#8220;Configuration Editor&#8221; opened up.Navigate to apps &#8211;> metacity &#8211;> general, double-click the Value of &#8220;button_layout&#8221; in right box and change into :minimize,maximize,close  right click and set as default.
