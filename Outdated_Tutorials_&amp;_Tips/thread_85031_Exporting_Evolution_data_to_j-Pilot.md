### Post by rplantz on 2005-11-01
I want to try j-Pilot but Evolution does not provide good tools for exporting calendar and contact information. (One of the things I dislike about Evolution.)

I have an old Palm Pilot that had nothing important.

First step is to synch the Palm with Evolution.

This step only took a few minutes. Most of the time was spent trying to figure out how to get the system to see the Palm. System->Preferences->Palm OSDevices provides the tools. Help tells how to use 'em.

Then sync it with j-Pilot.
