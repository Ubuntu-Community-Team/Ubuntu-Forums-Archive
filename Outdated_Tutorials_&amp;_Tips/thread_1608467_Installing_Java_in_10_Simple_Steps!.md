**_Installing Java:**_

**Step 1:**
 -Go to ***System*** at the top, go down to ***Administration***, then click on ***Synaptic Package Manager.***

**Step 2:**
-You will be prompted to enter your password, do so then continue on and the ***Synaptic Package Manager*** will open.

**Step 3:**
-In the ***Synaptic Package Manager*** window, click on ***Settings*** at the top, then click on ***Repositories*** on the drop down menu.

**Step 4:**
-In the ***Software Sources*** window click on the ***Other Software*** tab at the top.

**Step 5:**
-Check the boxes next to both of the ***Canonical Partners*** repositories and then close this window.

**Step 6:**
-In the ***Quick Search*** box on the top left, type in ***sun-java6***

**Step 7:**
-Check each box for ***sun-java6-bin***, ***sun-java6-jre***, and ***sun-java6-plugin*** and select ***Mark for Installation*** from the menu that pops up when each box is clicked.

**Step 8:**
-Click the ***Apply*** button at the top and this will finish installing ***Java***

**Step 9:**
-Restart your computer and continue to the [Java Site](http://www.java.com/en/download/installed.jsp?detect=jre&try=1) to take the ***Java Verification Test***.

**Step 10:**
-If the Java site says you have the current version of Java then you're ready to go. If not then try restarting your computer and try this tutorial again.

*This worked perfect for me on the first try and I'm running Ubuntu 10.10*
