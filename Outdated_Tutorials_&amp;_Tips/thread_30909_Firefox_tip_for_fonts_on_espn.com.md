### Post by 12quality on 2005-04-30
This may not be the most crucial technical advice, but it bugged me for weeks. The [ESPN.com](http://espn.com) homepage has a section called ESPNNEWS Headlines, with a list of its RSS headlines.  In Firefox on XP, each item appears on a single page, but in Ubuntu most of the items take up two lines.  This may only be a problem on my monitor at 1024x768, but that is the most common non-LCD resolution.  

The fix is to  change the default Sans-serif font in Firefox.  Go to Edit -> Preferences, click on the General tab and then click on Fonts & Colors.   Under Sans-Serif, I changed mine to FreeSans, but others may work as well.
