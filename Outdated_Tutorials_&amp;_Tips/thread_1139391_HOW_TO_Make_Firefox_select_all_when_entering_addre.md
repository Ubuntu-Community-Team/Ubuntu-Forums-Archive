### Post by MontelEdwards on 2009-04-27
I proposed this idea in Brainstorm and got a nice reply from a developer.

simply type in
 ```
about:config
```and then search
 ```
browser.urlbar.clickSelectsAll
```
right click>toggle

done!
no restart or anything


EDIT: You WILL have to accept a warranty agreement, which as long as you do exactly what i say, and nothing else you'll be fine
