Many of you may have noticed, but this is a HOW-TO thread, meaning you give complete instructions on how to do something. 

[COLOR=Red][B]All Faqs, Howto, Tips & Tricks are screened by a Mod or Admin before being approved.
[/B][/COLOR]
Please do not make threads in this section asking questions about how to do something, ask them in the thread that contains the howto you used. 

Should your question be in regards to something other then one of the  guides in the section then post that question in the appropriate section of the forums.  

Do not request guides in this section. Requests do not decrease the time in which this happens. Since guides are created freely by forum users, they will appear as soon as someone makes one. 

If you find there is a guide from a previous release of Ubuntu which works with the current release, PM a Mod and they shall assist in the migration on the thread. Thank you.
