### Post by lixy on 2006-10-28
Amarok's getting better by the day. Yet, after installing Edgy, Amarok woulon't recognize the Ipod as was the case with Dapper. To fix this, simply go to "configure Amarok" in the settings menu. On the popup, select the media device section, and add device. You can then select Ipod from the scrolling menu, and point Amarok to the correct destination (/media/ipod).
Hope this helps, as gtkpod doesn't seem to be around anymore.
