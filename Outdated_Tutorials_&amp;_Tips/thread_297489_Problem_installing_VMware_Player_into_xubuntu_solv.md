### Post by gregconquest on 2006-11-11
This is just a pointer for people in the same situation as me. I looked around and could find nothing on this problem.

I was installing VMware Player 1.0.2 from Add/Remove Applications in xubuntu 6.10 (situation may be the same in ubuntu, kubuntu, and edubuntu). When I got to the license agreement within the GUI installer, I had to click "ok", but there was no way to click it. It just looked like a screen capture of the license agreement, but I could click anywhere to do anything. <Esc> dropped me to a command-line interface for a moment, but then it went back to the non-clickable GUI. Finally I started hitting function keys, and I got it. After I hit <F12>, or maybe it was a delayed reaction to <F11>, I got a slightly interface that did allow me to select "OK" using the keyboard.

VMware appears to be working OK now :)

Greg Conquest
