### Post by Zarteg on 2006-03-27
The lack of ability to right click and open terminal bugged me for a while.

Recently installing Core 5, I noticed its a new trend in Gnome. Arg!
 
It may have been posted here already, but if so I couldn't find it.

Right Click on blank Desktop to Open Terminal fix worked for me in both Fedora Core 5 and Ununtu 5.10 Breezy Badger.

Breezy:

sudo apt-get install nautilus-open-terminal

Fedora Core 5:

yum install -y nautilus-open-terminal

You may have to restart Gnome

Cheers.
