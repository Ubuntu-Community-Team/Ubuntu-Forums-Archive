### Post by shoverbj on 2007-05-07
Ubuntu has become the new fad OS at Rose-Hulman, but many people haven't figured out how to connect to wireless. Here are some instructions.

Network Manager -> Connect to Other Wireless Network...

Network Name: RHIT-1X
Wireless Security: WPA2 Enterprise
EAP Method: PEAP
Key Type: TKIP
Identity: Kerberos User Name
Passwork Kerberos Password
Anonymous Identity: <LEAVE BLANK>
Client Certificate File: (None)
CA Certificate File: (None)
Private Key File: (None)
Private Key Passwork: <LEAVE BLANK>

Hope this helps out the other Rosies on here (if there are any). I spent many hours trying to figure this out and finally asked someone who knew more than me, and now, I believe I can do everything in Ubuntu and (almost) forget Windows.
