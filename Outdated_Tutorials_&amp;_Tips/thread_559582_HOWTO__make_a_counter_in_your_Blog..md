### Post by MaximB on 2007-09-25
So, you have a blog and you want to know how many visitors you got, what OS and browser they are using and more info ?

1. Go to one of those sites and register :
[http://www.branica.com](http://www.branica.com) or [http://blogcounter.com](http://blogcounter.com) , I'm sure there are more sites so take your pick.

2. After you registered you get a counter code, copy it.

3. Go to your blog and add a page element (via customize) - add HTML/JavaScript

4. Paste your counter code into HTML/JavaScript  

5. SAVE and enjoy the counting !

You can go to your "counting site" and get more info, as which OS and Browsers are used and other info.
