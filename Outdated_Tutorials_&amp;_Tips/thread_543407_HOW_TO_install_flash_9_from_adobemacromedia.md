### Post by antisocialist on 2007-09-05
download flash from the adobe site.
go to **places>computer** once there click the pencil and paper icon so it displays a text entry bar
in that area type "**~/.mozilla/plugins**" when you are there drag the files "**flashplayer.xpt**" and "**libflashplayer.so**" to that folder.
go to the site where you want to run flash and try it.
have fun



NOTE: you can download flash 9 at this link
[http://fpdownload.macromedia.com/get/flashplayer/current/install_flash_player_9_linux.tar.gz](http://fpdownload.macromedia.com/get/flashplayer/current/install_flash_player_9_linux.tar.gz)
open it with archive manager and choose extract.
extract it to desktop and delete the original file
the files i described above will be found in the folder that you just made

another way to do it is to go to [youtube](youtube.com) and click a video and a yellow bar appears. click the button "**install missing plugins**"
press accept (or read it if u want i dont care but if u get in trouble for incorrect use its not my fault)
hit continue
[it downloads and installs]
hit finish
the page will refresh and flash is installed
