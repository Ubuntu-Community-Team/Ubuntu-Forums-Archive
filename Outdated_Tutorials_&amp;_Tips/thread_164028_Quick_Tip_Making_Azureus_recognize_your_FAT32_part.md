### Post by btnheazy03 on 2006-04-22
If you want to save files you acquire from the bittorrent client Azureus onto your FAT32 partition (perhaps to share with Windows),

Tools => Options => Files

And then tick the check box "Enable incremental file creation"

That's it :)
