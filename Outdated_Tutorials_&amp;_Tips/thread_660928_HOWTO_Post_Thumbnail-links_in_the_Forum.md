### Post by dbbolton on 2008-01-07
It seems simple enough, but it also seems to be a recurring problem/question, especially in the "Post your desktop" thread: how to post thumbnails that link to the original, full-sized photo. 

The general format is this:

```

[noparse]
[[img]path to thumbnail[/img]]( path to full sized photo)
[/noparse]

```
Of course, you may not have a thumbnail image or time to make one. Good news: photobucket.com does the work for you.

Check the box under the photo:
[IMG]http://i103.photobucket.com/albums/m128/envyouraudience/photob.png[/IMG]

Then scroll down to the bottom and click "Generate Code":
[IMG]http://i103.photobucket.com/albums/m128/envyouraudience/photob2.png[/IMG]

Then copy the code from the box labeled "IMG clickable thumbnails for message boards":
[IMG]http://i103.photobucket.com/albums/m128/envyouraudience/photob3.png[/IMG]

And that's all there is to it.
