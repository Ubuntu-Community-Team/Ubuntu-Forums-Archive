A "how to"  for network monitor IPTOTAL

Introduction:
It's nice to now your network traffic of your WWW or FTP server.
This is simple open your Terminal.

Code:  sudo apt-get install iptotal

This command will require your password.

In your WWW dir. you will find a new dir. named IPTOTAL.
Start your browser and type [http://your](http://your) ip address/iptotal/tamplate.html and go to Last 24hr and it's shows a graph.

example: [http://www.antarctica-rbak.nl/cgi-bin/iptotal/iptotal.cgi](http://www.antarctica-rbak.nl/cgi-bin/iptotal/iptotal.cgi)
