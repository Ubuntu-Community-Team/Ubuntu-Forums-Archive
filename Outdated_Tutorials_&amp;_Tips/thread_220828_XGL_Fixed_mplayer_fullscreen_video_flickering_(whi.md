### Post by FedeXX on 2006-07-22
First of all, keep children away from my english! :)

Somebody, like me, have problems while playing fullscreen video with XGL.

Well, i've found a stupid fix to see fullscreen video with mplayer without that strange white frames, while running XGL. If somebody has already written this please remove this topic.


Start mplayer, and in preferences/video set "gl2" as video driver. Maybe you'll need to check "enable frame dropping" if there's no audio-video synchonization during playing.

Play the video on Desktop 1. A completely blank window will appear, that's ok. If you switch to fullscreen now, you'll see the video, but with lots and lots of white frames ](*,) 

But, if you grab the white window on Desktop 2 an THEN switch to fullscreen, the video will be played correctly. Don't ask me why. :D

I hope this will help. Bye!
