### Post by Hoag on 2007-03-18
After installing Firefox 2, I noticed that the backspace key no longer worked as my beloved "Back" function, but now worked as Page Up.

Not satisfied with this turn of events, I went on the prowl on the internet, and found out how to change it back.

Thanks go to carthik of [http://ubuntu.wordpress.com](http://ubuntu.wordpress.com)!

Clearly this is all a matter of preference, but I hope this helps anyone who preferred it the other way, and searched here first. ;)


**1) Type "about:config" in the address bar, and press enter.**

[IMG]http://img235.imageshack.us/img235/5613/screenshotrn9.png[/IMG]


**2) Type "browser.backspace_action" in the filter bar. One result should come up.**

[IMG]http://img252.imageshack.us/img252/8692/screenshot1hs8.png[/IMG]

**3) Double click it and change the value to zero.**

[IMG]http://img338.imageshack.us/img338/8476/step3un0.png[/IMG]

**4) Close the configuration window, and enjoy!**

**Note:**
I've only tried this on Ubuntu 6.06 LTS with Firefox 2.0.0.2

Edit: Last picture fixed, and made it a tad more presentable. >_>
