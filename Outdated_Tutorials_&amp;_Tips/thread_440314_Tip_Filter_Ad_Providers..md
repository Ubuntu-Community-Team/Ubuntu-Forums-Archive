I found this tip while surfing around...

[http://forums.debian.net/viewtopic.php?t=14129](http://forums.debian.net/viewtopic.php?t=14129)


Just add these line in your /etc/hosts to stop seeing ads from these providers :)
sudo nano /etc/hosts

127.0.0.1 doubleclick.net
127.0.0.1 ad.doubleclick.net
127.0.0.1 ad.ca.doubleclick.net
127.0.0.1 adremote.timeinc.net
127.0.0.1 adsremote.scripps.net
127.0.0.1 a.as-us.falkag.net
127.0.0.1 interclick.com
127.0.0.1 a1.interclick.com
127.0.0.1 media.fastclick.net
127.0.0.1 network.realmedia.com
