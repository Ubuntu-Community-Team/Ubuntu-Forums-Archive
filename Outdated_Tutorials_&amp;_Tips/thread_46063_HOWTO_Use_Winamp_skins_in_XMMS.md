### Post by mulperi on 2005-07-03
I had a hard time finding a nice skin for XMMS until I found out [here](http://gentoo-wiki.com/HOWTO_From_Winamp_to_XMMS) that you can use any classic Winamp skin in XMMS!

It is really easy:

1) Download a classic skin from winamp to $HOME/.xmms/Skins
2) Enable it in XMMS (alt-s)

ps. this is my [favorite](http://www.winamp.com/skins/details.php?id=81567&download=yes&url=http%3A%2F%2Fdownload.nullsoft.com%2Fcustomize%2Fcomponent%2F2001%2F10%2F11%2FS%2FNucleo_NLog_v102_.wsz)  skin.

//mulperi

[edit: no need to rename skin]
