### Post by shibainucan on 2005-11-16
A simple quick fix improves the look of firfox greatly.

make bitstream vera the default font.  Go to edit>preferences>general>fonts & colors

where it says serif, change to 'Bitsteam Vera Serif'
where it says Sans-serif, change to 'Bitstream Vera Sans'
where it says monospace, change to 'Bitstream Vera Sans Mono'

You will see an improvement.   I know people recommend installing the package "mscorettfonts".  But that package makes extensive changes to your system.  It puts Microsoft font everywhere.  I think the Bitstream fonts are as good as or better than the Microsoft fonts.   See for yourself
