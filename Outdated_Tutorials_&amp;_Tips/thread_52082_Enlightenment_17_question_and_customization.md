### Post by Stormy Eyes on 2005-07-26
For your first question: if you want Nautilus without getting the GNOME desktop, use **nautilus --no-desktop** instead of plain **nautilus**. As for your second question: just about everything you need to know for theming and customization of E17 is at [get-e.org](http://get-e.org/). Look at some of the other HOWTOs on the Ubuntu forums.
