### Post by dabear on 2006-01-14
Something that has really, really annoyed me for quite some time, is the startup and shutdown sounds played by Ubuntu. Here's how to disable 'em 
```

cd /usr/share/sounds
sudo chmod -r startup.wav
sudo chmod -r shutdown.wav

```
