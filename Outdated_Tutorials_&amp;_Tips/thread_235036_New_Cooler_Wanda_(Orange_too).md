### Post by KSDZ on 2006-08-12
According to [Vincent]("http://www.vuntz.net/journal/2006/08/11/394-do-you-feel-the-love") Gnome 2.16 is going to feature a new Wanda: [IMG]http://weblog.ksdz.nl/wp-content/uploads/2006/08/wanda.gif[/IMG]

If you can't wait to have it (like me) here's what you must do:

Download the attached file and save it as /usr/share/gnome/panel/pixmaps/fishanim_new.png
Then add the Fish-applet to your panel if you haven't done yet, click on it with your right mousebutton and open the preferences. Then click the "fishanim.png" button and select our wanda, fishanim_new.png

Be sure to set the number of frames to 8, and the time to your liking (the time in the picture above is 0,3s but 0,6s might be better for your panel)
