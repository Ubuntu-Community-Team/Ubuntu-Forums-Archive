### Post by heimo on 2005-04-15
You can easily disable the screen saver - I think it's in System->Preferences or Settings or something like that. Under 'Screen saver' choose disable or something like that.
 
 
EDIT: BTW, if you really want to use GUI to administer your server, I suggest that you shut down the X when you don't need it. Saves some memory.
