### Post by hk_2999 on 2006-12-02
Do you know you can use yelp(the help viewer) to view your manfiles as a replacement for GNU man?

It is much more easier to read and use there especially for beginners.

To do this you type in the terminal:

```
yelp man:*app*
```

where app is the manpage you want to view.

I've also made a deb that makes this easier to do, and you can download it [here]("http://www.fileden.com/files/2006/12/2/449486/debs/uman_1.0_all.deb")

With this deb installed, you can just view your manpages via yelp by typing in the terminal:

```
uman *app*
```

where app is the manpage you want to view.

much more easier than the above code, and easy to remove and install.
