### Post by SendDerek on 2007-07-08
**Original Purpose**
The original purpose of this print out guide was to stick something on the side of my Mom's computer so that she could have something to easily refer to while learning Linux.

**Why?**
I wanted a small, 5x7 index card sized, reference guide geared towards a Linux newbie.  I tried to find one online, but couldn't find one anywhere, so I created one!  That's where this comes into play...

[IMG]http://img.photobucket.com/albums/v203/send_derek/LinuxHelper-page1-1.png[/IMG]


**Can I Get it?**
Absolutely!  That's why I posted it here to begin with!  I figured it might help some of you out like it did for me.  The download link is at the bottom of this post!

**I Don't Like it.  Can I Change it?**
Please change it to what you'd like!  Just download it and open it with Scribus!  In fact, here's a link to installing Scribus... [clicky!]("http://wiki.scribus.net/index.php/Getting_Scribus_on_Ubuntu/Kubuntu_up_and_running").

**What's the Quality Like?**
I'm not the biggest graphical expert, but I took the vector illustrations of all the logos and used them with Scribus.  When I printed mine out, it was perfect with no "pixelation" at all.

Enjoy!
