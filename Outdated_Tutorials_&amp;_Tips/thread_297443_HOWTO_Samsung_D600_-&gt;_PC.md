### Post by _simon_ on 2006-11-11
Ubuntu can only read images, videos, music etc. from the D600 if it's on a t-flash card. It cannot read from the phone's internal memory.

To transfer data from your phone to PC you need to do the following:

1. Make sure what you want to transfer is on the t-flash card.
2. Plug USB cable into phone & pc.
3. Menu -> Settings -> Phone Settings -> USB settings -> Mass Storage
5. A window should open up on your PC with the folders on your t-flash card - just drag and drop what you want to transfer.

Once finished you need to disconnect the USB, otherwise you will not be able to use the phone.

Once you disconnect the USB, it forgets that you set it as a Mass Storage device for some reason so each time you will need to set it.
