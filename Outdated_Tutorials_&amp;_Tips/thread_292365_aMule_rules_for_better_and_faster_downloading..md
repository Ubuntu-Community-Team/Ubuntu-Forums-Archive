### Post by MaximB on 2006-11-03
after you installed aMule :
sudo apt-get install amule


and read the wiki :
[http://www.amule.org/wiki/index.php/Main_Page](http://www.amule.org/wiki/index.php/Main_Page)
[u][b]
here are several rules for better and faster downloading :[/b][/u] (more will be added as needed).

_**1. You MUST have a CD/DVD burner !**_
the amount of information you will download from aMule will fill any hard disk today.
so you must burn it on a CD/DVD.
and it's good idea to back up the things you downloaded.

_**2. You must give to take.**_
if you want to download at higher speed you must let other people to download from you at reasonable  speed
go to the "preferences">"connections" and set your UPLOAD limit to at least 10 KB/s (10 is the best).
***you MUST set your upload and download limits in "bandwith limits" AND in "line capacities"***
But I do NOT recommend you to add other share folders other then the default which you cannot change (it's your "Temp" folder) as it will slow down your computer.

_**3. Download first and last chunks first.**_
if you download movies you might want to see a preview of what you downloading
to be able to do this you must have already the first few MB downloaded from the start of the file.
go to the "preferences" to the "file" category and select "try to download first and last chunks first".

_**4. Connect to a good server.**_
in the "networks" tab (in aMule) you will have a long list of servers to which you can connect.
choose the server with the more files and users and with the LOWEST PING as possible.
this will let you download more files quicker.
you can set the priority of the server to "high" and it will connect to this server automatically.
make sure you have the options "auto connect at startup" and "reconnect at loss" enabled.
and that you enabled both the "ED2K" and the "kademila" networks. (preferences>connection)

_**5. Know what you downloading.**_
some files have "ITA" "RUS" "FR" and other names...
those are the languages in which the movie will "speak" - so if you don't know French don't download French (or if it's porn it's doesn't matter anyway ;).
BUT many other movies that appear "normal" are in fact in other languages , so I ALWAYS recommend you to "preview" the file first.

_**6. Know how to search and download.**_
in aMule you can search for files (opposite from bit torrent)
the first step for a good search is to make the **search "GLOBAL"**.
this will enable you to search in ALL servers.
when you search for a movie, you know that the file cannot/shouldn't be less then 500 MB.
so in the "search" "extended parameters" you should choose the minimum size for the file. (this will show you much less junk).
after you get the search results (wait for the search to complete, it may take a few minutes) you can see useful information regardless the file.
***file name*** - make sure it will NOT include ITA,RUS,FR and other languages...IF you don't know them.
***size*** - the size should be reasonable , if it's a movie it shouldn't be less then 500 MB.
***type*** - shows you if it's a movie , mp3 archive etc...
***sources*** - this is **VERY** important , if you have zero (0) sources the file is NOT complete and you may never download the full file.
***the higher the sources the faster it will download the file.***

_**7. Download and search on more people **_
there is an option in aMule to get your file from more people
in the preferences>connections you can see at the bottom
"max sources per file" and "connection limits" - make them really high !
about 5000 to 7500 is a good choice.
this is enable you to have more sources per file and increase your download speed.
now there is one more thing to do
in "preferences">"core tweaks" set your "max new connections per 5 seconds" to be 500.
then restart aMule.
this will enable you to....have more connections !

_**8. All beginnings are hard**_
aMule has ranks !
this means that if you didn't yet share enough MB/GB of data , you won't be able to download at high speed.
this also means that once you have installed aMule you won't be able to download at high speed right away.
so after a few days and even a few weeks you will download much faster (depends on the sources too).
and this drives us back to rule number 2 : "you MUST give to take".

_**9. The night is aMules friend !**_
the download speed gets higher with time
as long as you leave your aMule opened the more sources it will find and the faster it will download.
leave your aMule opened at nights ! ,this way you download more files.
and by the way - it is very bad for your computer to shutdown every day.
when you start your computer the amount of power that it consumes at this moment is very unhealthy and dangerous !

_**10. aMule has a character **_
yes , it has !
I always thought that OS's and computers had some kind of a soul.
(this was meanly because when I used to use windows it crashed without a good reason whenever it wanted ;)).
but if you leave aMule open at full screen for 1-20 minutes , you will see that for some unlogical reason your download speed drops drastically.
so every time your aMule is open minimize it to tray icon. - that's the way he likes it ;).
and when you almost competed to download your big file the download speed drops drastically - that happens because when you complete the download - you no longer share the file ! (unless you didn't listen to me and decided to share more folders then your default "Temp" folder ).

[center]_**FAQ.**_[/center]
those are questions that I've asked myself and that weren't already explained at my amule rules.

1. Q : My download speed was high a few minutes ago but now it's dropped drastically , why ?
    A : that is because you had a good source before and now he disconnected , or because you almost finished to    download your file (in that case it drops the speed automatically).


that's it for now - more will be added as needed and/or requested.
post here any other questions you don't have answer to regarding aMule.
