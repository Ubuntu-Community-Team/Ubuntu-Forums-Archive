	 <p>Vinicius Franco do Nascimento <a href="http://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-users/2005-October/054380.html">has announced</a> the launch of <a href="http://planeta.ubuntubrasil.org/">Planet Ubuntu Brazil</a> in Portugese. Many groups of Ubuntu users around the world have websites, forums, and IRC channels, so if you&#8217;re looking for people local to you to spread the love, make sure you check out the <a href="https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LoCoTeamList">list of teams</a> around the world.</p>
<p>If there&#8217;s no team in your area, you can always <a href="https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LoCoTeamHowto">make your own</a>, and join the ever growing Ubuntu Local Team community.</p>
