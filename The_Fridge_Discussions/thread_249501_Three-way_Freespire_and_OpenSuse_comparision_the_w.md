### Post by TheFridge on 2006-09-02
If you guessed that Ubuntu came top in a comparision of three of the leading Desktop distributions, then you&#8217;d be spot on.  Ubuntu founder [Mark Shuttleworth]("http://www.markshuttleworth.com/") gave us a tip about the following shoot-out. [Freespire]("http://freespire.org/") and [OpenSuse]("http://www.opensuse.org/") are pitted against our own Ubuntu and we thought that you&#8217;d like to hear the results as well.
 Online technology site CRN whipped out three sets of CDs for their recent review in an aim to track down [Which Free Linux Desktop is Best?]("http://crn.com/sections/breakingnews/breakingnews.jhtml;jsessionid=BXCPDLXXMEZM0QSNDLSCKHA?articleId=192203540")
 Comparisions were made across the areas of Installation, Setup and Configuration, Support, Feature Set and Usability.  Points being allocated across the five topics, one to three points points awarded in each area.  The results were tallied up with the total scores revealing the overall winner. In the highlighted section below we can see how Ubuntu faired in each speciality:
   **Installation**</p> [LIST] 
[*]Freespire: 3 points 
[*]**Ubuntu**: 2 points 
[*]OpenSuse: 1 point [/LIST] **Setup / Configuration**
 [LIST] 
[*]OpenSuse: 3 points 
[*]**Ubuntu**: 2 points 
[*]Freespire: 1 point [/LIST] **Support**
 [LIST] 
[*]**Ubuntu**: 3 points 
[*]OpenSuse: 2 points 
[*]Freespire: 1 point [/LIST] 
 **Feature Set**</p> [LIST] 
[*]OpenSuse: 3 points 
[*]**Ubuntu**: 2 points 
[*]Freespire: 1 point [/LIST] **Usability**
 [LIST] 
[*]**Ubuntu**: 3 points 
[*]Freespire: 2 points 
[*]OpenSuse: 1 point [/LIST] **Final Rating**
 [LIST] 
[*]**Ubuntu**: 12 points 
[*]OpenSuse: 10 points 
[*]Freespire: 8 points [/LIST] 
 
 Everyone reading this article, users of Ubuntu, Kubuntu, Edubuntu, and/or Xubuntu, give yourself a pat on the back. You all are the reason that Ubuntu keeps doing as well as it does. Without all of you, the tables could have very well been turned in this review, but with your hard work and dedication, Ubuntu keeps trucking along out front and in the lead.
 Head on over to the [CRN article]("http://crn.com/sections/breakingnews/breakingnews.jhtml;jsessionid=BXCPDLXXMEZM0QSNDLSCKHA?articleId=192203540") for the full story.  CRN has pointed out the strengths aswell as some valid criticisms for each of the desktop distibutions.  Our thanks go to everyone who continues to improve Ubuntu, the points selected by CRN will provide an excellent starting point for future work and you too can help by providing feedback and assistance with making Ubuntu.
