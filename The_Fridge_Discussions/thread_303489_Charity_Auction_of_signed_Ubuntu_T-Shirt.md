### Post by TheFridge on 2006-11-20
The [Ubuntu-UK team]("http://wiki.ubuntu.com/UKTeam") are holding a charity auction in aid of [Children in Need]("http://www.bbc.co.uk/pudsey/"). This is an annual charity drive in aid of disadvantaged children around the world.
 The item being auctioned is a Ubuntu t-shirt signed by [Mark Shuttleworth]("http://www.markshuttleworth.com/"), various Canonical staff and members of the Ubuntu-UK team.
 The auction is being held on [the Ubuntu wiki]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UKTeam/CharityWork/ChildrenInNeedTShirt"), and ends on Friday 24 November 2006. This is your unique chance to become the proud owner of a limited edition t-shirt, and all in the name of a good cause: place your bids now!
