### Post by TheFridge on 2007-11-25
The Launchpad team have released Launchpad 1.1.11! There are two big pieces of news this month!
 [LIST] 
[*]Personal Package Archives are now out of beta and available to all Launchpad users and teams. Find out more in the quick-start guide at [help.launchpad.net/PPAQuickStart]("https://help.launchpad.net/PPAQuickStart") 
[*][Launchpad Translations]("https://translations.launchpad.net/") is now much quicker and rock-solid. The Launchpad team say to expect exports and imports in half the time and next to no timeouts. [/LIST] See the [Launchpad News blog for full details]("http://news.launchpad.net/releases/launchpad-1111-released") of the release.
