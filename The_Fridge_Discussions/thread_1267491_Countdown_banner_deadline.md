### Post by TheFridge on 2009-09-15
You can make a highly visible contribution to Ubuntu 9.10 by helping to create the release countdown banner&#8230; but only if you get your submission in soon! By noon UTC on Sept 22nd you need to post your concept to [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Website/KarmicCountdownBanners](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Website/KarmicCountdownBanners) so that a banner can be chosen. You do not have to complete your banner by the 22nd but you do have to submit a design for review. Anyone is welcome to participate. Discussions will take place on the Ubuntu Web Presence team&#8217;s mailing list and chat room at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Website#Mailing%20List](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Website#Mailing%20List)
