### Post by TheFridge on 2006-09-08
**Great Lakesters** (yes, I made that up) and those of you within a stone&#8217;s throw of Lake Erie and Lake Michigan, you are just a few hours away from an upcoming LinuxFest.
 On Saturday, September 30th, at the Greater [Columbus Convention Center]("http://www.columbusconventions.com/info/parking.asp") in Columbus, Ohio, [Ohio LinuxFest]("http://ohiolinux.org") will kick off.
 Jeff Waugh (aka &#8220;[jdub]("https://launchpad.net/people/jdub")&#8221;) and Jorge Castro ([whiprush]("https://launchpad.net/people/jorge/")) will be there as presenters from the Ubuntu Community.  You also get Jon &#8216;maddog&#8217; Hall, Chris DiBona from Google, Michael Johnson representing rpath, Jay Pipes of MySQL and many more.   I would have to say, that you couldn&#8217;t go wrong with this event!
 If that list of people didn&#8217;t excite you, or if your *significant other* is still not wanting to let you go and geek out&#8230; Then you tell them that you know that the [Columbus Zoo]("http://www.colszoo.org/") will have REAL LIVE PENGUINS! That always works.
 Registration is free, and the shows are a blast.  You can find more information at [http://ohiolinux.org](http://ohiolinux.org).
