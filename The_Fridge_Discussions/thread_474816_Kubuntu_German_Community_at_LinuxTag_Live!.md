### Post by TheFridge on 2007-06-15
The German Kubuntu Community is representing Kubuntu at the annual [LinuxTag]("http://www.linuxtag.org/2007/") in Berlin. The booth is shared with the Ubuntu and Edubuntu Community as well as the Linux4Africa project.
 Anybody who cannot visit the LinuxTag, but still would like to feel the atmosphere, has an opportunity to watch them via webcam. A periodically refreshed still image (every 30 secs) can be found at [http://webcam.kubuntu-de.org](http://webcam.kubuntu-de.org). The webcam will be working every day between 07:00 and 16:00 UTC.
