### Post by TheFridge on 2006-08-19
The Magazine poll to [produce a name]("http://fridge.ubuntu.com/node/465") has now finished;  a third of you loved &#8220;Ubuntu World&#8221;, 21% liked the plain name &#8220;Ubuntu Magazine&#8221; and almost one-in-six appreciated &#8220;Ubuntu Full Circle&#8221;.  The magazine team are thankful for your vote and who knows, your name may come out on top.
 For our new poll, [kiko]("https://launchpad.net/people/kiko") from the [Launchpad.net]("http://launchpad.net") team has asked for a hand and some feedback on what font/typeface style should be used for displaying bug reports.
 The system has now been switched to to look plain-text email, so that when you visit a bug report, such as [https://launchpad.net/bugs/1](https://launchpad.net/bugs/1) the text is displayed without any wrapping like you get in a word-processor.  The result may also look like more like other Bugzilla bugtrackers.  
 Changing the style from variable-width to fixed with probably has as many advantages as disadvantages, so you should vote and make your thoughts heard.  You can also contact the team directly over at the [Launchpad feedback]("https://launchpad.net/feedback") page.
 By the way, if you haven&#8217;t seen the famous [Bug #1]("https://launchpad.net/bugs/1") before, be sure to check out *the* report and see if you can lend a hand to help!
