### Post by TheFridge on 2005-12-08

	 <p>Dennis Kaarsemaker once again brings us the <a href="http://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-devel/2005-December/013576.html">latest summary</a> of the Community Council meetings, this one is for the 6th of December. Community volunteers who sign the <a href="http://www.ubuntulinux.org/community/conduct">Code of Conduct</a> are now officially referred to as &#8220;Ubunteros&#8221; instead of &#8220;Ubuntites&#8221;. The Council also approved new members Mauricio Hernandez, Jordan Mantha, Chris Peterman, Kevin Cole, and Steve Kowalik.</p>
<p>The <a href="https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AccessibilityTeam">Accessibility Team</a> has conducted its second meeting, Daniel Holbach has posted <a href="http://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-devel/2005-December/013604.html">minutes</a> of that meeting.</p>
