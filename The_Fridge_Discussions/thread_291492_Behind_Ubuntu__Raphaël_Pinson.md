 [Behind Ubuntu]("http://behindubuntu.org/") has published an [interview]("http://behindubuntu.org/interviews/RaphaelPinson/") with Raphaël Pinson.
 Raphaël is known for his work on [Kubuntu]("http://kubuntu.org"), and more recently on [Ichthux]("http://www.ichthux.com/"), a Linux distribution based on Kubuntu and aimed at Christian users and communities.
 In his interview, we learn how Raphaël&#8217;s Christianity has influenced his involvement in the open source community, how he feels non-English speakers fare and what he gets up to in his spare time.
