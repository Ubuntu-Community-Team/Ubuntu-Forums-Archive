### Post by TheFridge on 2007-01-13
[Matthew East]("https://launchpad.net/~mdke") has [announced the creation of a Screencast Team]("http://www.mdke.org/?p=76"), and they need your help!
 Screencasts are videos which show users how to achieve a specific task in Ubuntu. They can be seriously useful when walking users through a new task and are intended to complement Ubuntu&#8217;s other support resources, such as documentation, forums, mailing lists and irc.
 The project is led by [Alan Pope]("https://launchpad.net/~alanpope/"), who has already done some [great work on screencasts]("http://doc.ubuntu.com/screencasts"). But now the project is expanding, and you can help out! Simply head over to the [team wiki page]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ScreencastTeam/") and soak up the material there. You&#8217;ll then be ready to contribute to the team by requesting or even making new videos!
 [Contact the Documentation Team]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DocumentationTeam/Contact/") with any questions or simply to get involved!
