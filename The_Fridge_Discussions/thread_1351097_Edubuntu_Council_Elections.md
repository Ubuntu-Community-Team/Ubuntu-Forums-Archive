### Post by TheFridge on 2009-12-10
Recently, the current Edubuntu Council [announced]("http://fridge.ubuntu.com/node/1940") the Edubuntu Council nominations and elections. The nomination round has been completed and even though later than originally anticipated, the voting will soon commence.

6 polls have been set up on Launchpad. Each poll has two choices, &#8220;yes&#8221; and &#8220;no&#8221;. The poll starts at 01:00:00 UTC (around 12 hours from now) on 11th December 2009 and runs for 7 days.
