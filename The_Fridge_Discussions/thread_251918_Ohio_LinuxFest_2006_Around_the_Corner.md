### Post by TheFridge on 2006-09-06
Great Lakesters (yes I made that up), those of you within a stone&#8217;s throw of Lake Erie and Lake Michigan, you are just a few hours away from an upcoming LinuxFest.
 On Saturday, September 30th, at the Greater Columbus Convention Center in Columbus, Ohio, [Ohio LinuxFest]("http://ohiolinux.org") will kick off.
 With names like Jeff Waugh (jdub) and Jorge Castro (whiprush) as presenters from the Ubuntu Community, as well as Jon &#8216;maddog&#8217; Hall, Chris DiBona, Michael Johnson, Jay Pipes, and many more, I would have to say you couldn&#8217;t go wrong with this event.
 If these people didn&#8217;t excite you, or your girlfriends might not let you go geek out, let them know that the Columbus Zoo will have REAL LIVE PENGUINS! That always works. Registration is free, and the shows are a blast.
 You can find more information at [http://ohiolinux.org](http://ohiolinux.org).
