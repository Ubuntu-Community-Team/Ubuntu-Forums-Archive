### Post by TheFridge on 2006-09-06
It&#8217;s been a busy three weeks for [Launchpad]("http://launchpad.net")! The latest Launchpad news is available at:
 [LIST] 
[*][https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/launchpad-users/2006-September/000606.html](https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/launchpad-users/2006-September/000606.html) [/LIST] Highlights include:
 [LIST] 
[*]**Bug tracking:** new features include three new filtering options. 
[*]**Translations:** Edgy translations have now opened in Rosetta, based on a billion uploaded templates and translations! 
[*]**Translations:** Rosetta now records the name of the most recent person to update a translated string, making reviews and feedback much easier. 
[*]**Support:** Improved searching and sorting of support tickets, one of the top requested features for the support tracker. 
[*]**Support:** New support requests now go through a guided process, which first tries to locate similar reports&mdash;avoiding duplicates and helping users get their answers faster. 
[*]**Other news:** The official URL for the Blueprint specification tracker has been changed to [http://features.launchpad.net/](http://features.launchpad.net/) [/LIST] Don&#8217;t forget that the launchpad-users mailing list which is shared between developers and users of Launchpad, you can sign-up at:
 [LIST] 
[*][https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/launchpad-users](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/launchpad-users) [/LIST] Come and help the team define *what Launchpad should become!*
