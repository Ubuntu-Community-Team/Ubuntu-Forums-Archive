**Edubuntu at Technology In Education Conference**
The [Colorado Local Community Team]("http://coloco.ubuntu-rocks.org/") manned a Canonical/Ubuntu booth at the [Technology in Education Conference]("http://tiecolorado.org/2007/index.cfm") in Copper Mountain. The general consensus was that the Ubuntu session turned out to be “the cool session” amongst all of the conference attendees for that day, nearly one thousand classroom teachers, administrators and technology                                                                enthusiasts from around Colorado and surrounding states. Edubuntu was                                                        the main focus and was run on an HP server in a thin-client configuration.
