### Post by TheFridge on 2007-05-16
Andrea Veri has announced the creation of the [MOTU]("http://ubuntuforums.org/glossary#term16") torrent team, and they need your help! The torrent team will take care of and develop packages related to the peer-to-peer protocol called BitTorrent, while working closely with upstream. It&#8217;s also the bug report contact for all torrent packages in Launchpad.
 They are currently interested in universe and main sponsorship and are looking for more packagers too, so if you are someone who is interested, ping Andrea Veri (bluekuja) on #ubuntu-motu-torrent on irc.freenode.net, or just apply to join the team in [launchpad]("https://launchpad.net/~motu-torrent"). As is the custom across Ubuntu you can keep an eye on them via [their wiki page]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MOTU/Teams/Torrent").
