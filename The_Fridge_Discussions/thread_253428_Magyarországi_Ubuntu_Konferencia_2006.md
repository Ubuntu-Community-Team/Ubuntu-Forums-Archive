  Our friends in the [Hungarian Ubuntu community]("http://ubuntu.hu/") are hosting the [Hungarian Ubuntu Conference]("http://ubuntu.hu/konf/?q=english") (Magyarországi Ubuntu Konferencia) on 28 October 2006, in [Budapest]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Budapest").
 With a mix of Hungarian and English language speakers, this is a great chance for Hungarian and international Ubuntu fans to get together in one of Europe&#8217;s beautiful old cities.
 Hungarian speakers: Attila Dobi, [Gábor Kelemen]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/GaborKelemen"), Péter Mátó, Gábor
Süveg, Gábor Török.
 English speakers: [Mario ?ani?]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MarioDanic") (Edubuntu Developer), [Ante Karamati?]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/AnteKaramatic")
(Ubuntu Developer, Ubuntu Printing Team), [Jani Monoses]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/JaniMonoses") (Xubuntu Lead
Developer), [Raphaël Pinson]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RaphaelPinson") (Kubuntu Core Developer), [URL="http://phanatic.hu/"]Szilveszter
Farkas[/URL] (Google Summer of Code student).
 Get along to Building &#8216;I&#8217; of the [Budapest University of Technology and Economics]("http://www.bme.hu/en/") on 28 October 2006 for a great day of Ubunting!
 [Ubuntu Hungary website]("http://ubuntu.hu/") ([English conference information]("http://ubuntu.hu/konf/?q=english")).
