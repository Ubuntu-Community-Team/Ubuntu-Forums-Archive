### Post by TheFridge on 2007-12-04
On Thursday, December 13th, the Kubuntu developers are holding their first ever &#8220;Tutorials Day.&#8221; The Tutorials Day will contain talks, tutorials, and a Q&A session about the development of Kubuntu.
 [INDENT]With KDE4 only a month away, now is the time to get involved in becoming a Kubuntu developer. We&#8217;ve set up these IRC sessions below to get you started.
[/INDENT] The sessions begin at 15:00 UTC on IRC (#kubuntu-devel on Freenode) and will consist of six different topics:
 [LIST] 
[*]15:00: **Packaging 101** 
[*]16:00: **Kubuntu Bug Triage** 
[*]16:30: **Branch your svn with bzr** 
[*]17:00: **Get programming with PyKDE4** 
[*]18:00: **Get your work into Kubuntu** 
[*]19:00:  **Kubuntu and KDE Q&A** [/LIST] For a full run-down of the entire session, head on over to Jonathan Riddell&#8217;s blog and read his post, &#8220;[Kubuntu Tutorials Day]("http://www.kdedevelopers.org/node/3125").&#8221;
