It&#8217;s been two years since we introduced the [Regional Membership Boards]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Membership/RegionalBoards") and since then they have done a fantastic job recognising the contributions of new Ubuntu members.

The term of most board members in the [Americas]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Membership/RegionalBoards/Americas"), [Asia/Oceania]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Membership/RegionalBoards/AsiaOceania") and [EMEA]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Membership/RegionalBoards/EMEA") (Europe, Middle East, Africa) ended yesterday and the Community Council asked for nominations.
