It has been a month since Ubuntu Developer Summit Intrepid Ibex was held in Prague, Czech Republic. The track leads have collated all their reports from the UDS discussions and Jorge Castro has made a summary of them, available here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UDS-Intrepid/Report/Summary](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UDS-Intrepid/Report/Summary). This report is a combination of reports and specs:

 
[LIST] 
[*]Reports from UDS: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UDS-Intrepid/Report/](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UDS-Intrepid/Report/) 
[*]The current list of approved blueprints for 8.10: [https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/intrepid](https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/intrepid) 
[/LIST]
 The reports are meant to be a bird&#8217;s eye view of what&#8217;s to come in Ubuntu 8.10. Like all best-laid plans, **these are subject to adjustment and/or changes**, but should be general enough to give you a good idea of what&#8217;s being worked on.
