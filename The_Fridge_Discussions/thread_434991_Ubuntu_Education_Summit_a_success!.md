### Post by TheFridge on 2007-05-06
The [Ubuntu Education Summit]("https://wiki.kubuntu.org/UES-Sevilla") took place this week in Sevilla, Spain, and went smoothly. The Ubuntu Education team heard problems from educators and people working in education from around the world. 
 Problems and situations that people regularly face have been noted and will be taken into account during the developers&#8217; summit, taking place this week.
 The next Ubuntu Education Summit will be held in Boston, Massachusetts, USA, over 25, 26 October 2007. For more information, refer to the [UES-Boston wiki page]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UES-Boston") and the [Launchpad entry]("https://launchpad.net/sprints/ues-boston-2007"). Ideas and suggestions from the education community are most welcome.
