### Post by TheFridge on 2007-01-18
This weekend 20-21 January 2007 has been officially declared a WikiWeekend. This is an initiative similar to the legendary Ubuntu Bug Day, except for documentation!
 It was started by [K.Mandla]("http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=71172") of the Forums to encourage contributions to the [Community Documentation wiki]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community"). Now it&#8217;s going global, and we need your help!
 All you need to do is dedicate some of your time this weekend, however small, to improve the documentation. It&#8217;s one of the easiest ways to give something back to the Ubuntu Community.
 To find out how to get involved, visit [the WikiWeekend page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WikiTeam/WikiWeekend") and [the forum thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=341385").
