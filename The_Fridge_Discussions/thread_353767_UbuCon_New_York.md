### Post by TheFridge on 2007-02-05
On February 16 the brand-new [ Google offices]("http://maps.google.com/maps?f=l&hl=en&q=google&near=New+York,+NY&ie=UTF8&z=15&ll=40.74196,-74.00465&spn=0.015542,0.040169&om=1&iwloc=A") in New York will host UbuCon New York, a 1-day conference for Ubuntu users and developers. This event will be part conference, part installfest, and part user group meeting.  And there&#8217;s an added bonus: you will be served lunch on-site, hopefully from the [famous Google cafeteria]("http://nymag.com/daily/food/2006/11/new_google_cafeteria_crushes_c.html").
 For more details on the conference and instructions on how to register please visit [the UbuCon page]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/TheUbucon") on the Ubuntu Wiki, or the [UbuCon blog]("http://theubucon.blogspot.com/"). And you can also visit the IRC channel #ubucon on irc.freenode.net if you have any questions.
