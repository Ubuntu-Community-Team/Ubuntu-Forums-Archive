### Post by TheFridge on 2008-05-25
Mehdi Hassanpour from the Iranian team, reports that the Iranian Team held a Hardy party yesterday, in Tehran. With more than 50 people in attendance, inspite of looming university examinations, it definitely made a great day for the Team.
 They had the following presentations :
- Ubuntu Installation,
- Kubuntu 3.5 Tour,
- KDE 4 features and Tour,
- Desktop Effects in Ubuntu,
- Ubuntu Overview and Hardy Tour,
- Iranian Team report and future plan.
 The team also distributed K/ED/Ubuntu CD&#8217;s, DVD&#8217;s, stickers and mugs. In future, they plan to hold Install fests to enable more face-to-face talk and free chatting.
 Enjoy some [shots of the event]("http://picasaweb.google.com/saeid.zebardast/Ubuntu804HardyHeronReleaseParty") and read the full story and presentation available [on their website]("http://wiki.ubuntu-ir.org/Meetings/13870302").
