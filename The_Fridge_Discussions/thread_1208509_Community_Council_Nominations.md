### Post by TheFridge on 2009-07-09
In order to get the [Community Council]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CommunityCouncil") from four to eight members again, we are going to have an election in a few weeks. All [Ubuntu members]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Membership") are eligible to vote.

I will announce the details of the election soon. What we want from you now is nominations.

If you know somebody in the Ubuntu community, who

[LIST]
[*]has been an Ubuntu member for a while
[*]is dedicated to the project
[*]is well-respected and known for balanced views and good leadership
[*]has a good overview over various aspects of the project
[*]is organised and has some organisation talent
[/LIST](or you know that this all applies to you), please send an email to me (**daniel.holbach at ubuntu dot com**) with the subject “**[CC Nomination]**” until **July 17th, 12:00 UTC.** (I’ll be on vacation afterwards.) If you can confirm that the person is willing to stand for election, please do so.
