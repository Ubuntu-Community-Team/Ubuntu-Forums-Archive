### Post by TheFridge on 2005-12-02

	 <p>The inimitable Daniel Holbach has posted <a href="http://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-devel/2005-December/013500.html">the latest</a> status report from the <a class="glossary-term" href="glossary#term16"><acronym title="MOTU: The &quot;Masters of the Universe&quot; maintainership team">MOTU</acronym></a> team. Highlights include merge updates from Dapper, a new logo contest, and a look at the <a href="https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MOTUScience">MOTU Science</a> team. The ranks continue to swell with the announcement of four newly minted MOTUs: Benjamin Montgomery, Zak B. Elep (zakame), Loic Pefferkorn (pef), and Benjamin Mako Hill (mako).</p>
<p>In related news, Stephann Hermann has <a href="http://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-motu/2005-November/000008.html">announced</a> the Ubuntu MOTU School, a virtual &#8220;how to get started&#8221; educational program where prospective packagers can learn the intricacies of maintaining the ever growing library of software in <em>Universe</em>. Visit #ubuntu-motu-school on irc.freenode.net for more information.</p>
