### Post by TheFridge on 2010-03-03
Back in January some of the Ubuntu Women members [launched a competition]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-women/2010-January/002406.html") to gather stories about women and girls discovering Ubuntu. The aim of the competition was to counteract the invisibility of women and girls in the community and demonstrate that their path is really no different than any other.

 Entries for this competition closed last week, and the [voting has now opened]("http://elkbuntu.net/ubuntuwomen/") on the 15 wonderful entries received. Everyone can have their say in which are their favourite stories; so please take a moment to read these tales of discovery and cast your votes.

 The Community Manager, Jono Bacon, will announce the most popular story winner and draw a random winner on March 8th, 2010 to celebrate [International Women&#8217;s Day, 2010]("http://internationalwomensday.org").
