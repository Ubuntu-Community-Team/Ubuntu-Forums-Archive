### Post by TheFridge on 2006-06-07
<p>Ubuntu was awarded a PC World 2006 <a href="http://www.ubuntu.com/news/pcworld2006">World Class Award</a>, identifying it as one of PC World&#8217;s <a href="http://www.pcworld.com/reviews/article/0,aid,125706,pg,13,00.asp">100 Best Products</a> of the Year &#8212; all the way up at number 27! Ubuntu was the only Linux distribution listed, but is among quite a number of Open Source and Free Software friends. Hooray!</p>
<blockquote ><p>&#8220;Products that win PC World&#8217;s World Class Awards set the standard for excellence in the IT and consumer electronics industries,&#8221; stated Harry McCracken, editor in chief of PC World. &#8220;From notebook computers and security software to music players and Web sites, the editors reward the most outstanding performers in this annual awards program. Congratulations to Ubuntu.&#8221;</p></blockquote>
