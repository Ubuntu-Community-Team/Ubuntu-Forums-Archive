[[IMG]http://fridge.ubuntu.com/files/ubuntu-tops-security-small.png[/IMG]]("http://fridge.ubuntu.com/files/ubuntu-tops-security-big.png")[SearchSecurity.com has identifed Ubuntu]("http://searchsecurity.techtarget.com/originalContent/0,289142,sid14_gci1202417,00.html") as the quickest major Linux distribution when it comes to fixing security vulnerabilities.
 &#8220;One way to make a non-scientific determination as to how quickly various Linux distributions publish their updates is by searching the [Secunia]("http://www.secunia.com/") database of advisories &#8230; In this instance, Ubuntu and Fedora received the highest scores overall, reflecting their tendency to be among the first responders for many issues.&#8221;
 So, Ubuntu&#8217;s free, easy to use, looks great and it&#8217;s secure.
 Congratulations to Martin Pitt and the rest of the Ubuntu security team!
