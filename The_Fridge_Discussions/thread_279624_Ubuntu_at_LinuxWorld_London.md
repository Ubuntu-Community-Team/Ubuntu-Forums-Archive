### Post by TheFridge on 2006-10-18
[IMG]http://fridge.ubuntu.com/files/header_logo_linux.gif[/IMG][CENTER]Linux World Conference & Expo 2006[/CENTER]

 On the 25th and 26th October, [LinuxWorld London]("http://www.linuxworldexpo.co.uk/") is happening, and it is filled with Ubuntu goodness. Here are some details of what to look forward to:
 Ubuntu will have an official stand at the show - do come along and see us. In addition to this, the excellent [Ubuntu UK]("http://www.ubuntu-uk.org/") LoCo team will have a stand in the .org village to show off the UK Ubuntu community and the work they are doing. A number of Ubuntu UK members will be there, so be sure to visit them too.
 Ubuntu Community Manager, [Jono Bacon]("http://www.jonobacon.org") who has helped organise the booth has also organised an Ubuntu UK meet-up that takes place at the Ubuntu UK stand on **Wed 25th Oct at 2.30pm**. Everyone is welcome to come along to meet the community, get involved or otherwise hang out.
 Jono is also involved in a number of talks and events while at LinuxWorld:
 [LIST] 
[*]Wed 25th Oct 2006 11.30am - 11.50am - Talk about Ubuntu on the IBM stand (free to view). 
[*]Wed 25th Oct 2006 12.30pm - 1.30pm - Panelist on The Great Linux Debate about Virtualisation (free to view). 
[*]Thur 26th Oct 2006 - 11.50am - 12.20pm - Talk on *Cracking Open The Linux Desktop* in the conference (pay to view) 
[*]Thur 26th Oct 2006 - 12.30pm - 1.30pm - Panelist on The Great Linux Debate about the GPL (free to view). [/LIST] Finally, Ubuntu and Canonical have been [nominated for a number of awards]("http://www.linuxawards.co.uk/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=14&Itemid=32") at the [UK Linux & Open Source Awards]("http://www.linuxawards.co.uk/"), and the winners will be announced on the evening of Wed 25th Oct 2006.
