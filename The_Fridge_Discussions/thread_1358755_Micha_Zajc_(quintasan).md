Micha? Zaj?c

 
 **Age:** 16
**Location:**Lubin, Poland
**IRC Nick:**Quintasan or Quintasan|Szel

 **How long have you used Linux and what was your first distro?**
How long? Kind of hard to remember, maybe three years. My first distro was Ubuntu then very soon I switched to Kubuntu and after few months I found myself using Gentoo but I discovered it was a waste of time and I switched back to Kubuntu.

 **How long have you been using Ubuntu?**
I would say I have been using Kubuntu for two years. I was curious how Linux works and after googling and visiting some sites I though Ubuntu would be a good choice but I didn&#8217;t like GNOME and Kubuntu was obvious choice for me back then.

 **When did you get involved with the MOTU team and how?**
At first I used to translate apps but serious contributions started somewhere in the Jaunty cycle. My first &#8220;contribution&#8221; was importing upstream patch to fix issues with Qt 4.5 and KDE 4.1

 **What helped you learn packaging and how Ubuntu teams work?**
Packaging? I have learned it The Hard Way, instead of reading the awesome wiki we have I started meddling with debian/ dir and poked guys on IRC each time when some Strange Error popped out [IMG]http://s.wordpress.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif[/IMG] . Guys on #kubuntu-devel and #ubuntu-motu were very helpful and I managed to grasp the basics of packaging, patching and whole review process within two hours.

 **What&#8217;s your favorite part of working with the MOTU?**
Everything [IMG]http://s.wordpress.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_biggrin.gif[/IMG] . But if I had to choose just one thing it would be when I users drop in to IRC channel and thank developers for their hard work (+100 to motivation [IMG]http://s.wordpress.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif[/IMG] .

 **Any advice for people wanting to help out MOTU?**
Don&#8217;t be shy, join us on #ubuntu-motu and ask how you can help. We don&#8217;t bite and can provide you with assistance if you run into problems on your MOTU adventure [IMG]http://s.wordpress.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_wink.gif[/IMG] 

 **Are you involved with any local Linux/Ubuntu groups?**
Ubuntu Polish LoCo, you can find me on our IRC channel (#ubuntu-pl)

 **What are you going to focus on in Lucid?**
Of course working with Kubuntu Ninjas (sssh we don&#8217;t actually exist [IMG]http://s.wordpress.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_razz.gif[/IMG] ) to bring you new and shiny KDE SC releases. I&#8217;m the new maintainer of Project Neon (nightly builds of KDE SC and Amarok) so I will also concentrate on that. I&#8217;d also like to tidy needs-packaging list a bit so we have a clear list on thing we really need to have in repositories.

 **What do you do in your other spare time?**
So, I learn Japanese by myself since there are no courses here in Lubin or somewhere nearby. I also love skateboarding and watching anime. I&#8217;ve recently started learning C++ so I do it very often too.
