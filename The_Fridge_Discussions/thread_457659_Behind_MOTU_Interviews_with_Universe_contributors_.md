[Behind MOTU]("http://behindmotu.wordpress.com") is a blog by Universe maintainer Jordan Mantha that interviews Universe developers (known as Masters of the Universe) and frequent contributors. The goal is to show the fun and human side to the hard working volunteers that keep the Universe and Multiverse software repositories in good shape.
