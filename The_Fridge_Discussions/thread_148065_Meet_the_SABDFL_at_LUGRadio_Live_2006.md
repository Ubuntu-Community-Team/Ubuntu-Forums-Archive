	 <p>Say it loud, say it lewd! The cheeky pranksters at LUGRadio let us know that the <a class="glossary-term" href="glossary#term8"><acronym title="sabdfl: Mark Shuttleworth, the Self-Appointed Benevolent Dictator For Life">sabdfl</acronym></a> will be making an appearance at <a href="http://www.lugradio.org/live/2006/">LUGRadio Live 2006</a>, which takes place in Wolverhampton, England on the weekend of the 22nd and 23rd July. The sabdfl was at last years event and according to all reports, captivated the audience throughout his inspiring talk.</p>
<p>LUGRadio Live 2006 follows on from last year&#8217;s inaugural community event for Free Software and Open Source fans. This year over 40 speakers, an exhibition and BOF sessions are packed into the event. You&#8217;ll recognise some Ubuntu names among the speakers, such as Scott James Remnant, Matthew Garrett and Jonathan Riddell. Also appearing will be Simon Phipps, Michael Meeks, Sarah Ewan, Christian Schaller, Gervase Markham&#8230; and many more.</p>
