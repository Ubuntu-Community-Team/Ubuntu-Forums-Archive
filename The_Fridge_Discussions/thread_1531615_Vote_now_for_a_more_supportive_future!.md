Evan, seen lurking here in the background, has a favour to ask. [Stackoverflow]("http://stackoverflow.com/") is a website that allows professional and enthusiast developers to ask questions of one another in order to share ideas, solutions and make the life of developers better. It&#8217;s an extremely high quality resource but those clever folks over at Stack Overflow aren&#8217;t content to stop there! 

They have another site they call [Area 51]("http://area51.stackexchange.com"). This is where groups of people can propose custom sites based around Stack Overflow for their own projects. This is where Evan&#8217;s favour comes in as he, like many of us working on Ubuntu, would like to have a really great place to send people with questions about Ubuntu. Not only that but one that&#8217;s already got an army of intelligent helpful people helping one another &#8211; with a healthy collection of Ubuntu users in there too I&#8217;ll wager. 

A stackexchange powered solution would be nothing short of amazing and **YOU** can help!

[INDENT]But how do I help, Iain?

</p>[/INDENT]I&#8217;m thrilled you asked!

1. Visit the [Ubuntu proposal page]("http://area51.stackexchange.com/proposals/7716") on stackexchange.
 
2. Register and vote for this proposal.

Then while you enjoy a celebratory cup of tea and a biscuit think of ways to spread this as far and wide as you can. 

Let&#8217;s tweet **[www.tinyurl.com/stackexchange](www.tinyurl.com/stackexchange)** using **#ubuntu** and put up signs in our offices, and send each other pictures of these signs in our offices!

Tell your brothers, sisters, friends, lovers, mothers, ex-lovers, ex-mothers &#8230; wait that doesn&#8217;t work &#8230; get a tattoo!*

If you can think of any other ways of getting this out there tell us in the comments! Thanks for listening and good luck. We&#8217;ll follow up with progress [IMG]http://s.wordpress.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif[/IMG]  At time of writing we have 76 people committed! 

**Seriously, really don&#8217;t get a tattoo*
