 Personal Package Archives is Launchpad&#8217;s new beta feature that builds and hosts Ubuntu .deb packages in your very own apt repository.
At 15.00 UTC on Thursday 13th September, the Launchpad and [Ubuntu MOTU]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MOTU") teams are jointly hosting **[PPA and Packaging 101]("https://help.launchpad.net/PPA101")**. This IRC session in #launchpad will introduce you to:
[LIST]
[*]the basics of packaging for Ubuntu
[*]solving your dependencies from the relevant Ubuntu section - i.e. the ogre model
[*]version consistency between PPA and Ubuntu&#8217;s primary archive.[/LIST]It&#8217;s also your opportunity to ask questions about PPA and get answers from the Launchpad and [MOTU]("http://ubuntuforums.org/glossary#term16") teams. If you want to see a particular topic covered, add it to the [class&#8217;s agenda]("https://help.launchpad.net/PPA101") on the Launchpad help wiki.
See you there!
