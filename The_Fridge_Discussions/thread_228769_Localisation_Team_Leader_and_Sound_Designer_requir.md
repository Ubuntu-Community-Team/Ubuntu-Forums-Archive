### Post by TheFridge on 2006-08-03
The Ubuntu project has two exciting volunteer opportunities!
 **Localisation/Translation Team Leader**
 If you&#8217;re a whizz with Launchpad&#8217;s Rosetta translation tool, enjoy making sure things get done and have experience with localisation, get yourself to the [next Community Council meeting]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CommunityCouncilAgenda").
 If you&#8217;re the right person for the role, you&#8217;ll be keeping in touch with translation teams and coordinating with the Rosetta and language pack development teams. If you&#8217;re up to the job, step up at the next Community Council meeting.
 **Sound designer**
 The Ubuntu Art Team is looking for a sound designer. The rich log-in and event sounds are as much a part of Ubuntu&#8217;s human-friendly appeal, as its warm colours and easy to use interface.
 If you can create meaning in a snatch of sound, without it becoming irritating, read more in [Troy Sobotka&#8217;s post]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-art/2006-July/002753.html") to the Art Team&#8217;s mailing list.
 As an aside, prog-rock hero Robert Fripp has developed Windows Vista&#8217;s sound environment. Let&#8217;s hope he didn&#8217;t go for a twenty minute drum solo at start-up.
