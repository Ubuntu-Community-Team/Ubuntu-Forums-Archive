### Post by TheFridge on 2006-08-08
One Ubuntu, two Ubuntu, three Ubuntu, four!
 Visit the unofficial [Ubuntu Counter]("http://ubuntucounter.geekosophical.net/") project and you can register details of how many machines you have running Ubuntu, Kubuntu, Edubuntu, Xubuntu and just about any other kind of Buntu. In return, you get your own Ubuntu Counter user-number to proudly display on your home page.
 At the time of writing, 5360 people with 7350 Ubuntu machines have signed up, including our own Mr Shuttleworth.
 Interesting stats include:
 [LIST] 
[*]6 machines registered running Ubuntu 4.10 (Warty Warthog, the first release). 
[*]Average of 1.371 machines per registered user. 
[*]90.68% of registered users running latest stable version. [/LIST] The project initially began as a novelty for proud Ubuntu users, but the statistics gathered are nonetheless facinating.
