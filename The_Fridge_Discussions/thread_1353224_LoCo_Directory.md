### Post by TheFridge on 2009-12-12
After hours of work of lots of nice people, we&#8217;re happy to announce

    [http://loco.ubuntu.com](http://loco.ubuntu.com)

The LoCo Directory has been talked about for a long time now and we&#8217;re happy to release a first functional release, which is 0.1.0. In this first release we simply want to keep team data up to date.

Make sure your LoCo Team is in [http://launchpad.net/~locoteams](http://launchpad.net/~locoteams) and if you are approved in [http://launchpad.net/~locoteams-approved](http://launchpad.net/~locoteams-approved) - then you can, if you are an team admin or team owner of the team, edit your team data and we can slowly replace [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LoCoTeamList](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LoCoTeamList)

There&#8217;s a lot of things we still need to do and lots of ideas kicking around for version 0.2.0, which will let you put up Events data for your LoCo team.

If you want to help out:

[LIST]
[*]Translate it: [https://translations.launchpad.net/loco-directory](https://translations.launchpad.net/loco-directory)
[*]File bugs: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/loco-directory](https://bugs.launchpad.net/loco-directory)Review/push changes: [https://code.launchpad.net/loco-directory](https://code.launchpad.net/loco-directory)
[/LIST]Thanks a lot to everybody who worked on the code, made designs, helped fix stuff, had conversations about it, specced it out, filed bugs and translated it. You are all awesome!

Go and try it out, edit your team data and file bugs. :-)
