### Post by TheFridge on 2006-07-25
LXER.com has published an [interview]("http://lxer.com/module/newswire/view/64805/index.html") with Simon Law, head of Quality Assurance for the Ubuntu Project.
*Specifically for QA, you can join the Ubuntu BugSquad. We’re a tight-knit group of people who are interested in helping with the neverending flow of bugs that come in. You can start by helping to triage new bugs and testing experimental bits of software. Plus, we give out lots of virtual hugs.*
If you’re new, you can join in the “Hug Day” in starting on Wednesday, 19 July, in every timezone. For more information on how to get involved, Jeremie Corbier has a nice [summary]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-devel-announce/2006-July/000162.html") of everything you need to get started.
