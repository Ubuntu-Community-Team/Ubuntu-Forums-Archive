### Post by TheFridge on 2007-05-25
Today Lionel Menchaca, Dell&#8217;s Digital Media Manager, [announced]("http://direct2dell.com/one2one/archive/2007/05/24/15994.aspx") the availability of three different systems with Ubuntu 7.04 installed: the XPS 410n and Dimension E520n desktops as well as the Inspiron E1505n notebook. These systems will be available at the [Dell website]("http://www.dell.com/open") by 4pm CST today along with hardware support for their customers in USA.
 Dell users can opt for the fee-based OS support provided by [Canonical]("http://www.ubuntu.com/support/paid"), including a 30-day Get Started, One-year Basic and one-year Standard or use the Dell community forum for help and also [get the latest updates]("http://linux.dell.com") from the Dell-Linux team.
