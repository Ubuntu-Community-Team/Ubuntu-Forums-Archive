### Post by TheFridge on 2007-05-19
[Ubucon-Boulder]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Ubucon-Boulder") is a free, all-day gathering of Ubuntu-related demos, talks, chats, to show each other what *buntu can do, and Google&#8217;s Boulder office (1433 Pearl) is where all the proposed action is. 
 Jim McMaster, a developer at Google and Leslie Hawthorn, of Google&#8217;s Open Source Program Office in California are the gracious hosts for the venue of this unconference-like Barcamp. Due to space constraints, it will be a small gathering of around 20 people, and registration is first-come, first-served. You can [register here]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Ubucon-Boulder/Register") !
