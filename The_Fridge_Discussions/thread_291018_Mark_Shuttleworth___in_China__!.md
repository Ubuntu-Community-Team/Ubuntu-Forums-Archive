[[IMG]http://fridge.ubuntu.com/files/mark-in-china-small.jpg[/IMG]]("http://fridge.ubuntu.com/files/mark-in-china.jpg")[RIGHT]Join Mark Shuttleworth in Beijing to celebrate Edgy![/RIGHT]

 [Amy Jiang]("http://www.amyjiangsu.com/") of [Ubuntu-China]("http://www.ubuntu.org.cn/") has let us know that [Mark Shuttleworth will be in Beijing]("http://www.amyjiangsu.com/?p=13") for the Ubuntu 6.10 (*codenamed Edgy Eft*) release party.  The key date is 2006-11-02, starting at 19:00.</p> The party, with a chance to meet and hear the SABDFL speak, is being held at the Automation Research lab.  If you&#8217;re wondering how to get there, [excellent directions and a map]("http://www.ubuntu.com.cn/releaseparty") are available for everyone.
 The Ubuntu China team have arranged the whole Beijing release event, even deciding to get Ubuntu 6.10 CDs produced locally based on their own cover-art.  Accompanying the CD-ROMs are a pair of exclusive [T-shirts and sweaters/hoodies]("http://www.amyjiangsu.com/") specific to the release.  Following the codename of Ubuntu 6.10, the designs feature a large eft, or newt, on the back.  Balancing up the front is a smaller motif with the familiar *Linux for Human Beings* slogan.
 Here at Fridge Central we&#8217;re jealously awaiting getting our hands on one of those limited edition garments.  If you&#8217;re are one of those lucky Ubunteros who manage to make your way to the Beijing release party, please send us back a t-shirt!  We&#8217;re certainly looking forward to the photographs afterwards.
