### Post by TheFridge on 2005-11-09

	 <p>While our readers <a href="http://fridge.ubuntu.com/node/115">debate</a> as to what exactly a Dapper Drake <em>is</em>, developers from around the world have gathered in Montreal, Canada at the <a href="https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuBelowZero">Ubuntu Below Zero</a> conference to begin work on the next release.</p>
<p>Can&#8217;t make the trip? Don&#8217;t worry, there&#8217;s an easy way to track the development progress as it unfolds. This <a href="https://launchpad.net/sprints/ubz">specification</a> page on the Launchpad has the list of topics being discussed at the conference. These specifications will completed as the conference continues, and will eventually be the feature goals that will guide the development of Ubuntu 6.04. You can also follow along on the <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/tags/ubz/">photo</a> <a href="http://photos.shermann.blogweb.de/main.php/v/ubz/">feeds</a> from the event.</p>
