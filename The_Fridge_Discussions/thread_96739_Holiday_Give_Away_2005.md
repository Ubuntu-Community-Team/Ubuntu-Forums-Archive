### Post by TheFridge on 2005-11-29

	 <p>To continue a tradition started last year, members of the Ubuntu Community have created a <a href="https://wiki.ubuntu.com/forum/GiveAway">Holiday Give Away</a> wiki page.  The goal of that effort is to try to provide people with Ubuntu CDs in time for the Holidays in December.  Other ideas of things that can be shared within the community are personalized CDs with extra packages not usually found on the official CDs, and unwanted hardware looking for a new home.</p>
<p>azz from the forum explains how he started the project:<br />
<blockquote>Last year, I offered to send out Ubuntu CDs to people who had not ordered through shipit early enough to receive them before the holidays. People answered and I sent some out. This year, I would like to do that again.</p></blockquote>
<p>Visit the page to submit your name and how many CDs you can send to fellow Ubunteros in need.</p>
