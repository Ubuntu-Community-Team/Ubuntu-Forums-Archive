 As most of you who follow the Fridge know, [Ubuntu has been given a face-lift.]("http://ubuntuforums.org/”http://www.markshuttleworth.com/archives/308”") There is a cool new theme with new colors, a lighter feel, and just all around an upbeat tone to all the official Ubuntu related sites. Check out [ubuntu.com]("http://ubuntuforums.org/”ubuntu.com”") and [canonical.com ]("http://ubuntuforums.org/”canonical.com”") to see the new look if you haven’t done so already.

 The news team for the last couple of years ([https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Fridge/Rejuvenate](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Fridge/Rejuvenate)) has been trying to get The Fridge moved from Drupal to Wordpress and that effort is almost complete now. You’ll see a new site, you will be able to comment on each post on the new site rather than only being able to comment on the Forum. You can submit news stories and ideas on the site and more (instructions on how to do so will be sent out in the near future). The new site will also include links and pages about and to the other areas of the community.

 The goal was to have the redirect go live on Sunday 10.10.10 in conjunction with the [release of Ubuntu 10.10]("http://ubuntuforums.org/”https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MaverickReleaseSchedule”"). However, confirmation of this has not happened. However starting on Sunday, October 10th, we will be publishing Ubuntu News on The Fridge via [ubuntu-news.org]("http://ubuntu-news.org/").

 We are sorry if this causes any inconvenience and hope we can make this transition as smooth as possible.
