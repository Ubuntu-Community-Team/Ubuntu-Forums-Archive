### Post by TheFridge on 2006-05-02
<p>Daniel Holbach wants to motivate more of you to participate in <A href="https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-motu/2006-May/000651.html">Ubuntu Hug Day</a>. A &#8220;Hug Day&#8221; is where volunteers join up in IRC, #ubuntu-bugs, and help sort out bug reports from users. You can work in a nice team, make sure the bug reporters&#8217; concerns are heard, gather all the information needed so developers can fix bugs, close useless bugs, find out where the bugs come from, and eventually work together with upstream to make changes happen, and get experience in hacking and fixing bugs.</p>
<p>Time is short, so the team will try to triage as many bugs as possible, but specifically look into the targets outlined at <a href="http://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuBugDay">http://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuBugDay</a> for more information. The Hug Day starts this Wednesday, May 3rd, and will run throughout the day and night, so drop on by and get involved!</p>
