  Got any questions about K/Ubuntu that have been burning inside you, but you have never known who to ask?  Now we&#8217;ve managed to secure a regular slot with [Canonical&#8217;s]("http://www.canonical.com/") Ubuntu Community Manager.  
 Following from the great success of the [Ubuntu Open Week]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuOpenWeek") question sessions, mister [Jono Bacon]("http://www.jonobacon.org/") will now be having a regular date, once a month for **[Community Question Time]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BuildingCommunity/CommunityQuestionTime")**.
 There will be a one-hour time limit, so it&#8217;ll definitely be a case of quick-fire to ensure that as many people get their questions in and their answers out!  To join in the fun you&#8217;ll need to get on to [IRC]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/InternetRelayChat") and join #ubuntu-meeting to watch the conversation develop.
 If you&#8217;re also also wanting to *ask* questions, then here&#8217;s how;  also join #ubuntu-meeting-questions and prefix what you&#8217;re wanting to know with QUESTION.  It should look something like this:
 [LIST] 
[*] QUESTION: Will the next release of Ubuntu come with free chocolate? [/LIST] Look forward to seeing you all there, there will be plenty of room for everybody!
