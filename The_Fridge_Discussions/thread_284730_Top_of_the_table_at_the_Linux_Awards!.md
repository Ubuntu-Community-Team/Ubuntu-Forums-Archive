### Post by TheFridge on 2006-10-26
[Ubuntu]("http://www.ubuntu.com/") has walked away with **Best Distribution** at the [Linux Awards]("http://www.linuxawards.co.uk/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=14&Itemid=32") held in London last night.
 Maggie Meer of UK Linux magazine [Linux User and Developer]("http://www.linuxuser.co.uk/") presided over the show with guest presenter [Perry McCarthy]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Perry_McCarthy")&mdash;formerly of BBC TV car show [Top Gear]("http://www.bbc.co.uk/topgear/")&mdash;who kept the awards rolling.
 Canonical&#8217;s Jane Silber was there to pick up the award, a transparent, and extremely sharp glass trophy with the wording *UBUNTU* engraved into the glassword.  Jane spent the rest of the evening closely guarding her new toy.
 Also at the ceremony [Jon &#8220;maddog&#8221; Hall]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jon_%22maddog%22_Hall") picked up the Lifetime contribution recognition, won last year by Linux kernel developer Alan Cox.   Representing the [four boys]("http://www.lugradio.org/") from Wolverhampton, Jono Bacon took home the prize for *Best Open Source marketing campaign* on behalf of [LUG Radio Live 2006]("http://www.lugradio.org/live/"), narrowly beating Ubuntu&#8217;s [Ship IT]("https://shipit.ubuntu.com/") free CD-ROM scheme.
 Fans will be pleased to know that following a previous incident, Jono&#8217;s famous beard has grown back.
 Reading out the nominations, Perry McCarty had great fun with some of the more interesting project and company names, at one point tongue-twisting himself over *Canonical* (&#8220;Camomica&#8221;).  After the meal and award ceremony, dancing and drinking continued at the [Millennium Gloucester Hotel]("http://www.millenniumhotels.com/MCIL.nsf/LU_HOTELDOC/42$$HotelDescription") until late into the morning.
 The trophy only mentioned *Ubuntu*, but we&#8217;re sure that this includes Edubuntu and Kubuntu aswell.  Pictures will follow as soon as we at El Fridge have managed to wrestle the gong away from Jane&#8217;s firm grip&#8230;  Congratulations all around!
