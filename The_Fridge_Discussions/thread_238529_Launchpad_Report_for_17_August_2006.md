### Post by TheFridge on 2006-08-17
[Christian Robottom Reis]("https://launchpad.net/people/kiko") (aka &#8220;kiko&#8221;) who heads up the *Launchpad* team has released the **Launchpad Report** for August 1st through to August 16th. *Launchpad* is a collection of online services for products in the open source and Free software universe.
 Highlights for this Launchpad Report:
 [LIST] 
[*]Staging Server up and running 
[*]Bug tracking on Malone (*As in Bugsy Malone*) 
[*]Source code imports to native Bazaar format deployed 
[*]Contextual Karma (*How to total up your street cred*) 
[*]Edgy Translations (*Are you speaking your language?*) [/LIST] It may sound like a bunch of gibberish to me or you, but I must say this is really interesting stuff. [Launchpad]("http://launchpad.net/") has already become the backbone of the [Ubuntu]("http://launchpad.net/distros/ubuntu") distribution, providing access to various layers of support. The [Launchpad Report for 2006-08-17]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/launchpad-users/2006-August/000536.html") has the full juicy report.
 The memorable [https://launchpad.net](https://launchpad.net) address is your starting point and you can also join the [Launchpad-Users]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/launchpad-users") mailing list and following up on previous happenings in the [archives]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/launchpad-users").
 Happy Launchpadding!  Have you got **your** account yet?
