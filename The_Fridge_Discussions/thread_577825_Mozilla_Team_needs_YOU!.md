### Post by TheFridge on 2007-10-16
[Alexander Sack]("https://launchpad.net/~asac") and the [Mozilla Team]("https://launchpad.net/~mozillateam") are working on updated Firefox packages for **Dapper**, **Edgy** and **Feisty** releases and they need YOU to test them! To help you help them, a special [QA report page]("https://mozilla.qa.stgraber.org") has been created. This Firefox testing effort is a great way to contribute to Ubuntu and is important for helping Ubuntu to raise the quality of every release. Testing will take just a few minutes:
 Register for a new account [here]("https://qa.stgraber.org/user/register")!
 Click on the distribution and Firefox package you want to test!
 Check tests list and test beta packages out! 
 Fill out the requested form with your report! 
Feel good about contributing to Ubuntu
 Feel free to contact any member of the Mozilla Team in the **#ubuntu-mozillateam** IRC channel on Freenode or on the ubuntu-mozillateam&#8217;s [mailing list]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-mozillateam") if you have additional questions or you are just interested in joining the team.
