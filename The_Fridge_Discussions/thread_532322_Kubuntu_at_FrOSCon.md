### Post by TheFridge on 2007-08-22
[FrOSCon]("http://www.froscon.org"), the Free and Open Source Software Conference 2007, starts in just 3 days (August 25th and the 26th). During this event [KDE]("http://www.kde.org"), [Amarok]("http://amarok.kde.org"), and [Kubuntu Deutschland]("http://www.kubuntu-de.org") will share a room providing talks about Marble, Amarok 2, Kubuntu DE, and KDE 4. So if you are in or around Bonn, Germany, stop by the Department of Computer Science of the University of Applied Sciences Bonn-Rhein-Sieg in Sankt Augustin, Germany.
