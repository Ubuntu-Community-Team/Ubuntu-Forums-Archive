### Post by TheFridge on 2010-05-06
ZaReason has kindly come forth as a sponsor for the [Ubuntu Women World Play Day competition]("http://wiki.ubuntu-women.org/Events/Competitions/WorldPlayDay/")!
 
[[IMG]http://www.geekosophical.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/zareason500px.jpg[/IMG]]("http://www.zareason.com")

 Cathy and Earl of ZaReason have kindly offered their support by donating a [Terra A20 netbook]("http://zareason.com/shop/product.php?productid=16216&cat=250&page=1") and a [gold USB necklace]("http://zareason.com/shop/product.php?productid=16225&cat=255&page=1")

 This means that the competition can now offer** 3 prizes!** Not only that, after contacting Canonical CEO Jane Silber, we can confirm that she would be honoured to choose the second netbook prize!

 The prizes are now as follows:

 **Community Choice**: A [Terra A20 netbook]("http://zareason.com/shop/product.php?productid=16216&cat=250&page=1") with Ubuntu 10.04 LTS

 **Jane&#8217;s Choice**: A [Dell 10n]("http://www.dell.com/us/en/home/notebooks/laptop-inspiron-10/pd.aspx?refid=laptop-inspiron-10&s=dhs&cs=19&~oid=us~en~29~linux_4~~") with Ubuntu Moblin Remix

 **Random Draw**: A prize pack of random goodies from [The Ubuntu Shop]("http://shop.ubuntu.com") and a [gold USB necklace]("http://zareason.com/shop/product.php?productid=16225&cat=255&page=1") from ZaReason

 All winning entries *also* come with a subscription to their choice of either [Ubuntu User Magazine]("http://ubuntu-user.com/") *or* [Linux Pro Magazine]("http://www.linux-pro-magazine.com/").

 Entries closes on May 14 &#8212; which means you (yes, *you*) have barely more than a week left [to submit]("http://wiki.ubuntu-women.org/Events/Competitions/WorldPlayDay/HowToEnter"). What are you waiting for?
