### Post by nanotube on 2008-10-12
well, seeing as how i'm one of the creators of ubuntuzilla, i'm a bit biased :)

i think the main drawback is that you are basically not using the "official" supported repository method, and thus have to take care of updates separately from the rest of the system updates.

ideally, ubuntuzilla would re-package the mozilla builds into .debs, and host own repository, so that the updates would come through the official update-manager. but that's too much trouble for me to be doing. :)

that said, ubuntuzilla does give you its own update notifications...
