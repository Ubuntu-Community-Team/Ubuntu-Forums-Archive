### Post by An Sanct on 2011-09-29
In Thunderbird, in the top menu, select the 2d from right to left (Tools, something like "*des outils*") and in the dropdown, the third (Add-ons). Then select the second tab on the left - languages (a blue flag)

There, find French (française) and disable it ...

Hope, that helps ...
