### Post by Harry33 on 2011-02-04
The new major upgrade 6.14.0 has been released.
This new driver supports KMS EXA / Xv in HD 5000 (Evergreen), HD 6000 (Northern Island) and AMD Fusion (Ontario) hardware.

For HD 6000 and Fusion, kernel 2.6.38 is required.

Release announcement:
[http://lists.freedesktop.org/archives/xorg/2011-February/052420.html](http://lists.freedesktop.org/archives/xorg/2011-February/052420.html)
