### Post by ankspo71 on 2011-04-03
Hi,
Yes, the classic gnome desktop is already installed in Ubuntu 11.04. All you need to do is log out, click on your username in the log in window, then change your desktop choice from Ubuntu to Ubuntu Classic in the session menu at the bottom of the log in screen, then enter your password and log in.
Hope this helps.
