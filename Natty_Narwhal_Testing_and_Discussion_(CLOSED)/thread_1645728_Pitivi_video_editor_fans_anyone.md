### Post by knarf on 2010-12-15
Pitivi... A pitiful - or maybe pythonfull - excuse for a video editor. This love affair with [Python]("http://www.bigcatrescue.org/images/000BigCatPhotos/NotBCRcats/notcats/python.jpg") is seriously bogging down the distribution. Python is a nice language for protoyping but it has serious drawbacks when used as a centerpiece of a desktop distribution: no matter what Python afficionados say it is a hog feasting on your processor and memory.

While this may not be all that noticable on up to date multi{core|gigabyte|gigahertz} hardware it bogs down my humble 1.2 GHz Thinkpad T23 and similar machines. Yes, this notebook is 9 years old. So what? It works fine, it runs most things I throw at it. It runs [kdenlive]("http://kdenlive.org/") just fine, a more capable video editor than Pitivi.

I am more and more tempted to purge python and all its dependencies from my system to see what I end up with. A bare bones box, that is for sure... just about everything depends on it.
