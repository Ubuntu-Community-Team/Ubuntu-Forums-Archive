### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-04-24
Don't click on the Ubuntu logo - just throw the mouse into the top-left corner to show the launcher. Then you can click on the Applications Lense (looks like a magnifying glass with a plus symbol) to access your programs. Alternatively, hit Alt-F2 to show the "Run Application" lense.
