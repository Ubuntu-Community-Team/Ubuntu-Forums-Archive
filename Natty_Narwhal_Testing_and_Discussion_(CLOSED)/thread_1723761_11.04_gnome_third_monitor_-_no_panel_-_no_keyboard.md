### Post by Krytarik on 2011-04-07
You need to specify **all** of your monitors in there. Also, you need to put the device sections like this:
```
Section "Device"
    Identifier     "Device0"
    Driver         "nvidia"
    VendorName     "NVIDIA Corporation"
    BoardName      "GeForce 210"
    BusID          "PCI:1:0:0"
[COLOR=Red][B]    Screen         0
[/B][/COLOR]EndSection

```Basically, it's this way:
1.) You assign the cards to separate xscreens,
2.) assign the monitors to the cards, 
3.) and for the second monitor of the shared card, you specify its position, like this:
```
    Option         "TwinViewOrientation" "RightOf"

```Sorry, that option apparently isn't mentioned by the guide, it's really hard to get good guides for multiple monitor setups.

PS: Initially I wanted to give you also this guide at hand:
[http://en.gentoo-wiki.com/wiki/X.Org/Dual_Monitors](http://en.gentoo-wiki.com/wiki/X.Org/Dual_Monitors)
But unfortunately it still isn't responsive, and because of this I don't know how suitable it is for you. Maybe it becomes available again sometime in the near future.
