### Post by supergirlkara on 2011-04-10
I'm assuming that qdvdauthor is software for creating DVDs to play in home DVD players. May I recommend using DeVeDe? It is much better and it has loads of options. It creates an ISO image that you then right click and select 'Write to Disc' make sure that if your DVD is for a USA DVD player that you choose NTSC If you want it to play correctly. I only say that because it took me a while to figure out. Cheers! And good luck!
