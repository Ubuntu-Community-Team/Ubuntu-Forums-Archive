### Post by screaminj3sus on 2011-04-16
Alt tabbing in compiz is still completely unacceptable IMO. Its been years and nothing has been fixed. There is only thumbnails for unminimized windows and icons for other windows, which is very inconsistent and would be just plain confusing for new users. To make things worse, the icons are incredibly blurry. At the very least we need to do what gnome3 does and just make it icons blurry with nice sharp icons, which I don't think would be too difficult, or find a way to make them all thumbnails. This is just terrible and is a usability nightmare that has been ignored for far too long.
