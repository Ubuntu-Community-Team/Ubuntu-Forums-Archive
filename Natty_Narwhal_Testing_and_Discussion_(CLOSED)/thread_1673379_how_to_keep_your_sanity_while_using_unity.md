### Post by rburkartjo on 2011-01-22
this will work i just did it and have no problems
click on any folder on your desktop and navigate to /usr/share/applications
go to the spm and open-you might get the message you dont have the proper access to make changes and you might have to click on twice. ignore you do.
search for docky and python docky and install. then in the same folder find startup apps and add docky -command is docky. maybe add the trash can and reposition the dock where you want. i put it on the bottom. log out. when you log back in docky should be loaded. again nav to the aforementioned. drag the following to docky: home folder, control panel, application browser, computer. this will give you access to all your previously installed apps. you can also drag whatever else you want.
