### Post by coffeecat on 2011-03-05
@mamamia88, you bring up several issues.

Natty is stable-ish day-to-day but I wouldn't recommend it for regular use, and definitely not for a single driver problem. Although most days it runs OK, every so often there are updates that lead to - ahem - interesting times. :wink: And that is likely to continue for a while yet.

Suggestion:

Don't install 10.04 but try running it in live mode from the latest daily ISO burnt to CD. At least that way you'll see what the wireless is like atm. Dailies here:

[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/daily-live/current/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/daily-live/current/)

As far as your Maverick experience is concerned, it ought to be fixable since it works in Lucid. Have you started a support thread elsewhere?

**EDIT**: just saw your latest post. A problem associated with suspend may not be testable in live mode, of course. Sorry.
