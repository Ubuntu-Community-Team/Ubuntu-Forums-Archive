### Post by Harry33 on 2011-04-07
AMD has released an update to the Catalyst proprietary driver (11.4) called 11.4b.
Note, that this is not the same driver as AMD med specially for Natty and its xserver 1.10.

This newest Catalyst 11.4b for Linux does not have xserver 1.10 support and should not be installed on Natty. Luckily it is not to be found in the official Natty repos either.
