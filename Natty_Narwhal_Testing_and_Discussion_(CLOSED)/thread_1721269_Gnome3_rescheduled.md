This is as close to GNOME3 as Ubuntu currently comes and while the experience is usable it is far from as polished as the liveUSB distros right now.

[https://launchpad.net/~gnome3-team/+archive/gnome3](https://launchpad.net/~gnome3-team/+archive/gnome3)
