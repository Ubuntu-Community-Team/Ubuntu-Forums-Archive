I can't speak for 2-d Unity - no experience of it - but you certainly can move the launcher icons in 3-d Unity. Drag the icon you want to move out onto the desktop (it disappears, rather disconcertingly), then move the mouse pointer to the new position via the desktop and drop it. The other icons will shuffle to give you room if you're doing it correctly.

You can't move the Trash icon or any mounted volumes icons, and the Files & Folders and Applications icons seem to be tethered to that area of the dock. You can swap them around, but not move them.
