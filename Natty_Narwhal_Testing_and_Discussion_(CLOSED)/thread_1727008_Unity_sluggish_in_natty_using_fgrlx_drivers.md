### Post by screaminj3sus on 2011-04-12
If you have "tear free desktop" enabled in catalyst, disable "sync to vblank" in compiz, they seem to conflict. And for some reason sync to vblank was enabled by default in natty's compiz. On my system it was much more responsive after doing this.
