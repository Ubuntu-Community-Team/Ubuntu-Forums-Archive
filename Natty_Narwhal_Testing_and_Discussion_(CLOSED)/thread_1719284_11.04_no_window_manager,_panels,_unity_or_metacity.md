### Post by davepoth on 2011-04-01
If you've got no window manager at all, what I did was hit CTRL-ALT-T to pop up the terminal. I typed "gnome-panel" which brings up Gnome Panel (it'll be a bit messy as some bits of unity are loaded) and you can then click on the power button on the top right and select log out. 

When you go to log in again there's a menu at the bottom of the screen from which you can select Ubuntu Classic.
