### Post by anders_c_ on 2011-03-31
"system > preferences" is now located in the application lens under "themes & tweaks"
"system > administration" is now located in the application lens under "system"

The "system settings" that can be reached from the power button or by opening unity and start typing system settings contains both preferences and administration.

Edit: The application lens is the magnifying glass with the + in it, and then you change section in the top right.
