11.04 is still an Alpha release. It breaks quite often because it is still being developed.

(a) You are planning to test and submit bug reports
(b) You don't rely on the system for anything important
(c) You are able to fix and/or work around problems

Otherwise, you are better waiting until release day on 28th April.
