### Post by Atermoon on 2011-03-29
You make it sound way too complicated.

Just select "Ubuntu Classic" in GDM and Unity is gone. Don't like the global menus? Right click on it, deselect "lock to panel", right click again and remove.

Done. You're now using the classic 2-panel gnome setup.
