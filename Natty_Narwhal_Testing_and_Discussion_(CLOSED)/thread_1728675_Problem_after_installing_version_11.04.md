### Post by Dutch70 on 2011-04-15
Hi and welcome to UF

As idef1x said, install a stable version of Ubuntu or you may be plagued with problems even if you get past this one & then you'll probably have a bad opinion of Ubuntu. 

There is not really much help for 11.04 because very few people even have it installed, and the ones that do, are usually running a stable version as their main OS. I really wouldn't recommend installing 11.04 for several months after it comes out. It will be buggy! 

I recommend installing 10.04 LTS (Long Term Support). Go with 10.10 if you want, but there isn't much difference in the two & 10.04 is supported until April 2013. Support runs out for 10.10 April 2012.
