### Post by exploder on 2011-03-21
Go into "Preferences", "Content", click on the advanced tab, change the value on the "Minimum Font Size" drop down to 13 or 14, take away the tick in "allow pages to chhose their own fonts, instead of my selection above" and click ok.

You can experiment with different font sizes to have them suit your needs. I always go with 13 or 14 but that's just my preference:).
