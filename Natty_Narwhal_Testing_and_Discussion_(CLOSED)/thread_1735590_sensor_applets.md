### Post by WorLord on 2011-04-21
I, too, want this, but have an interesting workaround.  

GKrellM.  Yes, it's old, and hasn't been updated in approximately forever, but its fast, low-resource, bug-free, and can be quite compact - enough to fit in the empty space of Unity's launcher.

If that doesn't work, you can tell GKrellM to treat itself as a panel and put it on the other side of the screen, where windows can't maximize over it.

It's working out pretty well for me, I must say, but since finding out about those indicator-applet monitors, I'm installing and trying some of them out now.  Those look great.
