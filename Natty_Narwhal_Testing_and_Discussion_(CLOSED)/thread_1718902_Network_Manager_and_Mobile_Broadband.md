### Post by bennachie on 2011-04-01
Natty Beta-1 (32-bit) with all post-release updates installed is generally working quite well for me on my Compaq C700 laptop, and is certainly a vast improvement on previous iterations. 

However, I have noticed an odd glitch with the NM applet when using a mobile broadband USB stick. I can connect to the Internet via the stick in the usual way, with NM walking me through the modem setup process, but the NM indicator in the menu bar immediately becomes unresponsive, and remains so even after the USB stick is removed. The only remedy I have found so far is to to log out and back in again.  

I haven't yet been able to check this out thoroughly. I can access my home wireless network without any difficulty and without the indicator freezing. The actual connection via the Vodafone USB stick works fine until you want to disconnect and switch back to some other form of Internet connection. Right now I don't have access to any other mobile broadband services to check whether the behaviour is somehow specific to my circumstances, and would welcome any comments before actually reporting a bug.

I can now confirm, however, that the problem does not arise when running in Classic mode.
