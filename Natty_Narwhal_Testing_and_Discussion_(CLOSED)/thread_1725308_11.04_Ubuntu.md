### Post by r-senior on 2011-04-09
Try rebooting and select Classic Desktop with No Effects from the little menu below where you login. If that works, we'd have to suspect a problem with Compiz/Unity/video drivers.

If that desktop works, the first thing I would try would be to look in System->Administration->Additional Drivers and see if it offers you anything for your video card. Then try Unity again by switching back to Ubuntu on the aforesaid menu at login.
