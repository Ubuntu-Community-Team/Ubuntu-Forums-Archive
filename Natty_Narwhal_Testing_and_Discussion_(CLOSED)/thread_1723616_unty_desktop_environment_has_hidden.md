### Post by mc4man on 2011-04-07
If you made bad changes in compizconfig-settings-manager then open it up and make sure the unity plugin and window decorations are enabled. Set any other changed plugins back to defaults

Alt+F2 > ccsm or navigate to /usr/share/applications and click on compizconfig-settings-manager

Or maybe better - just open home folder > view > show hidden files and delete the .gconf folder
Then do a logout/in or restart, should restore default compiz settings and  plugins, may take 2 logout/ins
(Ctrl+Alt+Sysrq+k to logout -
