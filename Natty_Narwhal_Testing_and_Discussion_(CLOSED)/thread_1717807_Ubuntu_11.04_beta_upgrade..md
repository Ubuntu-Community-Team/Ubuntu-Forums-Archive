### Post by 3Miro on 2011-03-30
There is no beta. There is only an Alpha (beta is coming by the end of the week).

Unity is part of compiz, so your desktop effects are enabled. You can install Compiz-Config-Settings-Manager (CCSM) to get more options on exactly what effects you want and what you don't.

It's been stated many times before on these forums that in theory whatever you install, once fully updated will be the same as waiting and installing the final release. In pratice, it doesn't always work that way and several re-installs may be needed to correct problems that some of the early builds create.

An alpha, beta or release candidate is merely a snapshot of the release at that time.
