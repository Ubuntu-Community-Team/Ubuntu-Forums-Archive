### Post by Framli on 2011-04-01
Two ways :

1) When installing from the Software Center, a sliding bar at the bottom invites you to put the application in the launcher.

2) Press Super, or click the logo, type the name, or the first letters, or the purpose of the application, or even the first letters of the purpose. Or navigate with the mouse, categories are at hand.
