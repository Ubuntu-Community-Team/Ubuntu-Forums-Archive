### Post by coffeecat on 2011-01-02
It very much depends on which video cards you have in the two machines. With the 7-year old machine the answer will probably be no unless you fit a 3d capable card. With the 2-year old laptop the answer is probably yes, but whether you have to 'play around' with drivers depends on the make of video card. The most onerous 'playing around' you are likely to have to do is to open the Additional Drivers utility and enable the appropriate driver with a couple of mouse clicks.

I'm posting from the Unity desktop in 11.04 alpha at the moment  using an ATI Radeon HD4350 card. Since this works well with the default open source driver I didn't have to play around at all.

Post details of your two graphics cards and someone may be able to answer more definitively.
