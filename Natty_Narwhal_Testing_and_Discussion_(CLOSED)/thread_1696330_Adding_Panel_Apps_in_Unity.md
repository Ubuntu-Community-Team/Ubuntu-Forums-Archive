### Post by nilarimogard on 2011-02-27
You cannot do that, the top panel is not a GNOME panel and it won't get support for applets. For a clipboard utiliy, use something that has an appindicator. For instance ClipIt is a Parcellite fork that comes with an appindicator. I for one really like [Pastie]("http://www.webupd8.org/2010/07/pastie-052-stable-with-any-file-type.html") though.
