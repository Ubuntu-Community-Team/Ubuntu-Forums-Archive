Don't worry, the dash as we see it is effectively a stand-in until everything else lands. You can get some sense how it's going via Unity 3's Launchpad page:
[https://launchpad.net/unity/3.0](https://launchpad.net/unity/3.0)
(Targeted bugs there are mostly tasks to complete for each milestone).

Soon enough, that search box will give you global search across all the installed &#8220;Places&#8221; (Files and Applications, and other services can provide more sources via the Places API). The different buttons will open Unity's Applications place showing just Internet apps, for example.

Because the Places stuff hasn't been reimplemented yet, it's just opening /usr/share/applications in Nautilus to maintain some semblance of usefulness.

The graphics are also stand-ins, likely to change as the process goes on.
