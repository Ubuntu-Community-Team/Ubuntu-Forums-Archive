### Post by ubuntu27 on 2011-03-01
You should make a bug report at [Launchpad.net]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/libreoffice") and [Freedesktop Bugzilla]("https://bugs.freedesktop.org/")

First look around and see if it has already been reported, if it has, add yourself among the people who are affected. Click on "This bug affects me" button in launchpad.

If there is no bug report about it, create a new one. :)

**********

More language support in Libreoffice will be added in the future, I am sure.
It is relatively young project. 
Since LibreOffice is based on OpenOffice.org, Perhaps you could import OpenOffice dictionary to Libreoffice.

Go to this site and download the dictionary
[OpenOffice.org dictionaries]("http://extensions.services.openoffice.org/en/dictionaries")
