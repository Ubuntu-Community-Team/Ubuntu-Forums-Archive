### Post by aphatak on 2011-04-19
You could always add the weather report to the Gnome panel - right-click the Gnome panel, click 'add to panel', scroll way down till you see 'Weather report', click it, then click the 'Add' button.  You should see the new weather icon (a kind of depressing-grey rain cloud).  You must then right click the weather icon and set your location and preferences.
