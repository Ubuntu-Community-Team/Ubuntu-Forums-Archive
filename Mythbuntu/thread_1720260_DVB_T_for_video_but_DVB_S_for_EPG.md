### Post by AlextheKiwi on 2011-04-03
Hi all,
I live in NZ where we have freeview on DVB S and DVB T. 

DVB T is high definition but runs an mheg 5 EPG which I can't get to give me a 7 day EPG.

DVB S has an excellent out of the box EPG but is low definition.

If I put a DVB S card in my existing box which already has my DVB T card in it can I use the DVB T for the high def picture but get mythtv to look at the DVB S card for the EPG?

If so any clues as to how to do it and is it an easy task?

Many thanks in advance!

### Post by nickrout on 2011-04-03
Suggest you join the mythtvnz mailing list alex. You can sign up here:

[http://lists.ourshack.com/mailman/listinfo/mythtvnz](http://lists.ourshack.com/mailman/listinfo/mythtvnz)

Basically there are a few ways of doing this, and you don't need to get a DVB-S card, you could simply use tv_grab_nz-py which is an xmltv grabber that gives much better info than EIT over DVB-S anyway.
