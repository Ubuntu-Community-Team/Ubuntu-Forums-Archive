### Post by martin90 on 2014-09-09
I also have a LG TV, but I've set it up to 1280 x 720 (16:9), detected as a 720p signal. If you see a tiny square, press the Quick Menu button and change the Aspect Ratio, it should get back to an acceptable ratio, particularly in Just Scan mode.
