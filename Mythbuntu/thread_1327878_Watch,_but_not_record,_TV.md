### Post by xinix on 2009-11-16
Live TV is cleared out by the backend pretty quickly, especially if it needs the space for a scheduled recording.  I can see how you would be concerned about this if you have low storage space. 

 If you don't want live tv to show up in the recordings list, you can (from the recordings list) hit 'M',  then choose 'Change group view' and un-select live tv.  This will at least keep live tv stuff from cluttering your recordings list.
