### Post by klc5555 on 2008-09-22
I've generally gone with AMD processors for Linux, but I've found over the last couple of years that I've had real problems keeping my Athlon-based systems cool enough to avoid thermal-related slowdowns and lockups. For that reason, I've started switching back to Intel for new constructions, and I haven't experienced any significant thermal problems, even when cramming combined backends/frontends into micro-ATX cases. (As long as I use NVidia cards with fans rather than heat sinks...)

Your mileage may vary, of course.:)
