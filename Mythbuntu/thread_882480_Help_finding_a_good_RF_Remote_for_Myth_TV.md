### Post by madrivereric on 2008-12-25
Another alternative is to buy an RF remote extender that turns pretty much any remote into an RF remote.  The company's web page is here:

[http://www.nextgenerationhomeproducts.com/](http://www.nextgenerationhomeproducts.com/)

but you can buy it from many other stores.  It runs around $50.00.

you replace one of the batteries in the remote with the special rechargeable battery and transmitter and then tape the IR transmitter to the IR receiver.  Note that I had issues getting it to work with the Happauge remote, so I bought a cheap universal remote and it works just fine.

When I got it almost 2 years ago, I was very unsure about it working, but it works quite well and we can control our Myth box from anyplace in the house.
