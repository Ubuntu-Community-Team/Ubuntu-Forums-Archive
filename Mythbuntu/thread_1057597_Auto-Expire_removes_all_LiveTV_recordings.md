### Post by liquidhot on 2009-02-03
From what I understand the LiveTV recording group is set to expire after 24 hours (or whenever the hard drive is nearly full). If you're actively watching a show and you want to save it, hitting the record button usually moves it from the Live TV group as if you had scheduled it to originally. Although if you're watching a show and you decide AFTER the show end time has passed, it will only record the current section and you will have to go back and move it from the Live TV group.
