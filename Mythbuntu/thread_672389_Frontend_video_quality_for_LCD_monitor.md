### Post by mshelton on 2008-01-19
I have Mythbuntu installed on a dedicated system and am using Mythbuntu Frontend to connect via my Ubuntu workstation. 

Everything works fine but the video quality is poor even in full screen mode. I had some interlacing issues but was able to fix those through some of the display settings but the overall picture is still poor. It's very grainy and appears as if you are watching a television though a mesh screen.

The input to my Mythbuntu backend system is an analog cable signal, at first I though this may have been the culprit but I tested it on a television set and the picture was fine. My tuner card is a Hauppauge PVR-150, I'm wondering if I should begin to suspect this as being the cause of the poor picture or if I need to tweak my frontend display settings.

Any advice or suggestions would be appreciated.
