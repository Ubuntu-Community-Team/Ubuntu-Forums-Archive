### Post by Meph1st0 on 2008-07-19
What you oughta do is go in the appearance settings. Utilities/Settings, Setup, Appearance.  I think on the second screen called screen settings, it gives you the option of setting the X and Y axis as well as the GUI width and Height.  You just need to adjust these settings, more specifically the Y axis to cover or reach all the way to the top of your screen, thereby overlapping the toolbar.
