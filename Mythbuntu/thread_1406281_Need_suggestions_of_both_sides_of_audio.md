### Post by ian dobson on 2010-02-14
Hi,

I'm using digital audio on my frontend and the sound is maybe abit better than analog. OK on my system the SPDIF output on my motherboard goes to the SPDIF passthrough on my graphic card, which is connected to my TV over HDMI.

For me the main thing was to reduce the number of cables, but if a TV program has AC3 sound it sounds abit "deeper" than with the analog output I used to us.
