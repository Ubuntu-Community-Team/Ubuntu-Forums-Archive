### Post by ian dobson on 2008-09-13
Hi,

Just start Mythtv-setup, add the new PVR-150 as a new tuner and then add the tuner to your existing video source.

Myth will now automatically use the first free card to record your programs.

Can your settop box handle 2 channels at the same time?
