### Post by volkswagner on 2008-02-27
Go for the remote.  MCE USB remote is the most recommended with great support.  Myth tv is designed to work with a remote.  To recommend a tv card, for analog it is hard to beat the PVR150.  For ATSC, QAM, the K-world 110 is well supported.
