### Post by neutron68 on 2009-11-27
From what I've read, Ubuntu (and other Linux distros) aren't able to play a Blu-Ray disc directly.  

It looks like you'd have to use software to extract the video off the disc and THEN you can play the content.  In my mind, this isn't the same a putting a Blu-Ray disc into a Blu-Ray drive and pressing a Play button.

See:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/BluRayAndHDDVD](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/BluRayAndHDDVD)
