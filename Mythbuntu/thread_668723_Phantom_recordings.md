### Post by ubnewbie2 on 2008-01-15
Are these just the recordings created by watching live TV?  Live TV has to record, to allow pause and rewind etc.  The files created are recording files, just like those scheduled by you. They are, however, set to expire first, so they effectively, do not limit you disk space for scheduled recordings, and there is an option, to not show them in the recorded programs list.

---

### Post by tonyr1988 on 2008-01-15
ubnewbie2 is right - you're probably seeing the list of LiveTV recordings. When selecting what recording to watch, hit the M key and choose "Change Filter" (or similar). Tada.
