### Post by JustinR on 2010-07-16
You could run MythBuntu in a VirtualMachine although you probably won't get the greatest video playback. As nickrout said you can add the package, I believe its called Mythbuntu-Desktop.

Installing Mythbuntu on a separate HDD is unnecessarily complicated. You have one terrabyte of space on the first HDD right? Why not just create a new partition on the first drive and install MythBuntu from there? If you don't like it, just delete the partition! The second drive then doesn't have to be used.
