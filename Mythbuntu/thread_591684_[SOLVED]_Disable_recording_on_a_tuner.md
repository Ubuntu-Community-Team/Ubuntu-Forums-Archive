### Post by napsilan on 2007-10-25
If you wanted to turn it off entirely, you could just remove it from the capture card list in mythtv-setup.  If you only wanted to use it for live TV, you can try adjusting the priority of the tuner to some really low amount.  There could also be some other settings in mythtv-setup that might work for you.
