### Post by lionsnob on 2010-09-30
For future recordings, set the audio codec to AC3, which should play back in the PS3 without alterations.  This doesn't help past recordings though.

Directions to do this are [here]("http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/HDPVR#Bitrate_and_Picture_Controls").
