### Post by jpglab on 2013-11-14
For anyone else who runs into this problem...

It was an ATI/AMD driver issue. I had enough of wrestling with them so I gave in and ended up buying an [ASUS GeForce GT 610]("http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814121646") which works flawlessly with the NVIDIA drivers. There are no more solid colored bars on the bottom (and at sometimes the top) of the video picture.

Hope this helps!
