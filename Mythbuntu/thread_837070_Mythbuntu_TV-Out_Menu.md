### Post by vanhoja on 2008-06-22
Not too sure about having to enable the proprietary driver again after the install script... I'm using an nVidia 6200 series card, and selecting the GeForce 5+ series driver (nvidia-glx-new) at install had it running after the first post-install reboot.

As for the TV-Out question, I believe the options refer to both the ports on your TV *and* the outputs on your graphics card.  More pointedly, how you're going to hook up your computer to the TV: i.e., **out** of the S-Video port on your card and **in** to the S-Video connector on your TV, **out** of the yellow Composite Video port on your card and **in** to the Composite Video port on your TV, etc.  I've got a dongle that came with my nVidia card that has the Component Video outputs (Red, Green, Blue) to hook up to my 5 year old CRT HDTV; I selected "Component" on the TV-Out install screen.
