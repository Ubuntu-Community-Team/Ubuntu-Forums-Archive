### Post by davidryderuk on 2012-07-12
MythTV is useful if you want the computer to record directly from a television tuner. It sounds like you just want to watch videos recorded by other devices though. Have you considered installing XBMC on Windows? 

[http://xbmc.org/](http://xbmc.org/)

It has an application for controlling XBMC from Android:

[https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.xbmc.android.remote](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.xbmc.android.remote)

It has recently included some prelimenary support for Slingbox:

[http://wiki.xbmc.org/index.php?title=Slingbox](http://wiki.xbmc.org/index.php?title=Slingbox)

Your setup is quite different from mine, since I don't have a slingbox device or a windows laptop. Perhaps you could ask around on the XBMC forums?

[http://forum.xbmc.org/](http://forum.xbmc.org/)
