### Post by sanderj on 2011-07-19
It all depends on the chipset(s) used in the USB-TV-tuner. See the long thread on [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=606487&goto=newpost](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=606487&goto=newpost)

You could buy a cheap USB-TV-tuner on dealextreme.com; only 15 US$. That's what I have and now works with Ubuntu 11.04. As you use Ubuntu, you don't need Windows-software for recording etc.

My advice: find a USB-TV-tuner-stick with a normal (='big', about 8 mm) size coax-connector (not the tiny small 2-3 mm connector) so that you can connect normal coax cables.
