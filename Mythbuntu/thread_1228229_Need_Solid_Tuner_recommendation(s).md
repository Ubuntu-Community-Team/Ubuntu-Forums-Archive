I'm using two HDHomeRun boxes in a MythTV setup. I quite often use the PS3 to play back the recordings using the UPnP feature. PS3 has lipsync issues that can usually be fixed by rewinding a few seconds. It also has issues with any spurious data in the file. The PS3 will refuse to play a file, abort playback, or lock up when it comes across a slightly corrupted file. When this happens I have to watch the file on my computer which at most glitches slightly on the corruption.

That being said, the playback quality (de-interlacing & scaling) are much better on the PS3 than on the computer. Also, since I have been unable to get SPDIF pass through working on my computer, the sound is better from the PS3 (stuck with stereo on my computer).
