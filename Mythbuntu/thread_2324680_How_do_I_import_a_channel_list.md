### Post by khPWXxF on 2016-05-15
If you scan for channels you will pick up multiplexes from both transmitters.
If you can identify the rogue multiplexes you can delete or hide all channels for each of those rogues in one fell swoop with 
[https://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Channel_Editor](https://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Channel_Editor)

I am currently working on version two of the editor but v1 should do this very capably.
