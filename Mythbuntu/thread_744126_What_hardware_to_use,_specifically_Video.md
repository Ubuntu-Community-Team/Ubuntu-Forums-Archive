### Post by amphibem on 2008-04-07
To be clear about what previous poster's have said, what is wrong is you are trying to capture digital TV on a analog TV card. Hence the suggestion to get the pcHDTV 5500, which is a digital TV card. 

The other option is to add firewire to your PC and capture the firewire output of your Time Warner box, if it has one. You then need to use an IR blaster to control the Time Warner box. Do some research on that, but you can change channels on a external box within mythbuntu (and hence not be limited to one channel).

I think it would be good for you to have a look around this site, and google mythbuntu. There is heaps on info out there for those wanting a myth box :)
