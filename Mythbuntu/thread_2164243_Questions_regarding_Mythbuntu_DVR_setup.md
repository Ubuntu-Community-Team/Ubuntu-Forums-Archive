The Zotac Zbox ID41 have been great front ends for me. Have one in my home theater and one in my Family room. I pretty exclusively use HDhomerun and HDHomerun prime for tuning and they are good alternatives to USB tuner sticks.

### Post by Steve Goodey on 2013-08-02
I've had limited use of a PCTV Systems DVB-T2 290e nanoStick HD TV Tuner for use in the UK. From the limited testing it worked very well.

Why limited? Well the CPU or motherboard went faulty and so I need to replace those as well as the case and so far I haven't managed to get round to it.

The only down side is having the kit hanging out the back. The DVB-T2 290e seems to have a rather flimsy aerial connector so may be a problem if you move it around a lot.
