### Post by coffeecat on 2016-11-15
Following the [announcement by the Mythbuntu team](http://www.mythbuntu.org/home/news/mythbuntusolongandthanksforallthefish) that the Mythbuntu distribution is to close down, this sub-forum will close to new threads with effect from 1st December 2016. The sub-forum will still be open for posting to existing threads for the time being. 

Ubuntu forums will continue to provide volunteer support for Mythbuntu and for MythTV installed in one of the Ubuntu flavours. If you require such support, please start a new thread in the [Multimedia Software sub-forum](https://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=334). 

This sub-forum will be moved from the home page to become a child forum of *Multimedia Software* on or about 1st January 2017.
