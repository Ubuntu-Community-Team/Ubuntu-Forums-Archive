### Post by Dreamer Fithp Apprentice on 2014-12-30
Not sure what your update problem is, but to save your data, the sensible thing is to copy it to some removable media, preferably twice. Then make a data partition on your hard drive and copy them there. You'll probably need to shrink an existintg partition to do that. Then reinstall, but not on the data partition but to a partition dedicated to just the system. This is a good example of why I do not advocate keeping data in home. Of course you could put home on a seperate partition for some of the same benifit, but that seems more complex to me, not simpler, and it seems silly to keep all those user config files on a seperate partition, but I'm in a minority there. Suit yourself.
