The sensible answer is: if your 8.04 as stable as you claim, then leave it alone. Unless you just have the hobbyist's delight in blowing things up for fun! :) If/When your killer feature is implemented (VDPAU/VA-API hardware decoding), then it will be time to rev up the upgrade engine.

Otherwise, Mythtv 0.21 is Mythtv 0.21 whether it comes to you via Mythbuntu 7.10. 8.04, 8.10, 9.04 Alpha2, KnoppMyth, MythDora, or compiled source code. So you won't see anything different there from your upgrade. Plus, 8.04 is an LTS version, so it will still be supported (likely via backport) after 8.10 and 9.04 are kaputt. 

Cheers! :)
