### Post by richard.e.morton on 2008-09-25
Hi,

I know this is a late reply... I am new to Mythtv and although I have not found a direct equivalent of Sky+ series link there is built in functionality which is at the same time better and worse.

When selecting a program to record there are various recording options, such as:

Record this program once
Record this program once/always on this channel
Record this program once/always on any channel

so there are five options which means you don't have to keep setting each series to record. Once you've found a series you like, the system keeps recording it and if it's good enough you can record all the repeats across all channels as well (like I love the WestWing and continue to watch it)....

I have seen patches for BBC Series Link... but I haven't seen it in the application yet and the patch was a year or so old.

Hope that helps
