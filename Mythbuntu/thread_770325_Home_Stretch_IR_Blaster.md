### Post by superm1 on 2008-04-27
Okay,

So there are typically two jacks on a mceusb2 receiver.  Only one is usually active.

Go into mcc, and for your remote type pick the mceusb2 (like you did), and for the transmitter type make sure you pick the mceu2b option as well (w/ your cable box).  If your cable box isn't listed, then you can pick custom and do some magic on your own.

Restart after doing this to allow all the modules to refresh properly (lirc_pvr150 can complain a lot and have a hard time unloading if the firmware isn't there).

All that will be loaded is lirc_mceusb and lirc_dev.

now you can try to irsend, and hope for more luck :)
