> **MonsterMaxx said:**
> The HD channels are all duplicated in non HD and myth doesn't seem to care if it records in HD or not.

Seems like in it's prior life (this is a clean build starting from scratch) that it gave higher priority to the HD channels.  I assume this is a setting somewhere.  Anyone know where?

On the frontend:
Setup --> TV Settings --> Recording Priorities --> Set Recording Priorities --> HDTV recording prioity --> bump it up.
