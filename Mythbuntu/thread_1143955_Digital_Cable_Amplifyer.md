The problem may not be your amplification, it may be your Haupauge digital tuners. I found my own 1600 to be so digitally deaf that it was not worth using in this capacity, even with amplification. On the other hand, I have no trouble with a 5-way split including 2 pchdtv-5500s, an AverMedia A180, an old PVR 150, and the analog half of the 1600 with no amplification at all.

When I was using a line drop amp, I followed some online recommendations and went with a "Motorola Signal Booster 484095-001-00 Bi-Directional RF Amplifier", which I bought from Amazon. It did not add any noise or artifacts in any of my tuners that I could determine. The 1600 could now lock on to perhaps a third of the stations that the other digital tuners found without amplification.

I notice now that the amp now also comes in a 4-port version, the "Motorola Signal Booster 4-Port BDA-S4 Cable Modem TV HDTV Amplifier". 

Your mileage may vary, of course, but these amps might be worth looking into for you.
