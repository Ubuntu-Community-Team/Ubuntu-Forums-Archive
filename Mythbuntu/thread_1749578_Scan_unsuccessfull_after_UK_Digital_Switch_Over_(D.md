### Post by PhilWig on 2011-06-01
Hi Elfantin,

  yes, I had that same frustrating problem with Llandonna in North Wales and 9.04.  Some programs (eg Dave) did not work.    No great cultural loss, but still irritating.  A fresh install on a spare disk worked though.  I came to the conclusion (rightly or wrongly) that 

1.  some parameters (eg QAM) for the faulty multiplex/frequencies had changed during the switchover and that this needed to be acknowledged by Myth.

2.  When myth does a scan, if it finds a multiplex it already 'knows' then it does not bother checking and updating these parameters in its database - it assumes they have not changed.

It was resolved on my 'live' system by deleting all channels, all inputs and all video sources, setting them up again from fresh and then re-scanning.  All then worked with no loss of recordings.

Hope this helps.
Phil
