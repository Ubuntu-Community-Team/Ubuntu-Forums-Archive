There are Two main option for free TV:

Freesat, which is received by satellite.
Freeview, which is received via aerial.

Both have slightly different channel listings, and freeview doesn't have HD in all areas yet; we can't get freeview HD in Portsmouth until next year, but we use freesat.

Your *HD Ready* TV has a digital receiver built in?  If not, you'll need a set-top box,  ideally before the digital changeover.
