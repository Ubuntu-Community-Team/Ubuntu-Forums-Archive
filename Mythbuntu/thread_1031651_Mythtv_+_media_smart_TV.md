### Post by newlinux on 2009-01-05
Mythtv has a UPnP server. Your TV may or may not find it. If not you could try running a couple of other UPnP servers like fuppes or ushare or twonky (twonky isn't free). 

I have tried mythtv's UPnP server with a couple of different UPnP networked device clients, and some work, some don't. Of course, I have found this to be true with most UPnP server/client combos, unless a client specifically says it supports a certain UPnP backend.
