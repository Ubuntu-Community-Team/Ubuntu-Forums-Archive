### Post by klc5555 on 2011-04-20
> **davidstoll said:**
> I have a few shows recored...let's call it "ABC show".  But I want to rename the channel, not the channel number, but the name of the recorded group from "ABC show" to "XYZ show".

Is that possible?

Not quite sure which of the two separate things you described you're trying to do. 

As far as the backend is concerned, the channel and its recordings haven't changed as long as the four-digit channel code that gets plunked onto the start of a recording's filename hasn't changed. So if you wish to change the channel's name or its callsign, you can easily do either in the Channel Editor in the backend setup. Or from the frontend from livetv by calling up the "edit" menu with the "e" key.

If, however it's the titles of the individual recordings you need to modify, you can easily edit the metadata from the front end from the drop-down menu including the recording parameters that appears when you "move right" on a highlighted recording in the "View Recordings" screen. But of course, this only corrects the title for currently recorded material. Future episodes of this show would continue to be listed under their original title until they were recorded and their metadata edited.

At least that's what I think you were asking about. Apologies if not.
