### Post by newlinux on 2009-02-05
I don't know anyway to automatically jump 3 minutes into show upon pressing play in it for the first time, but you can direct seek.

Pressing "3" and the "i" (info) key will take you directly to the third minute once you are viewing the show.

### Post by crez79 on 2009-02-06
Do you have commercial skip setup? Since it is mostly commercials between shows, this should cut out most. Also you can skip the the end of the next commercial break with a key. I think it is right arrow on the default setup.

Another option is to find out which shows/networks are the problem and adjust those start times individually, since as long as the time on your computer is right, it should be pretty accurate. On the off chance a bit of recording is cut off for me, usually it is from the end, and that is a lot easier to fix.
