### Post by Calash on 2008-03-06
The capture card is what takes your TV signal and turns it into something the computer can work with.  This is usually from a coax cable that comes out of your wall and feeds into the cable box or TV.

Is there a place on your computer where you can connect the Cable TV cord from the wall?  If not, then you will need to buy a card to do this for you.
