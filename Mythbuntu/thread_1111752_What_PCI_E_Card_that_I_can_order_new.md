### Post by utar on 2009-03-31
Check out [http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Main_Page](http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Main_Page) to see which devices are supported.  Just bear in mind that this isn't entirely up to date and some devices that are listed as not working/experimental support do work.

Per [http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_WinTV-HVR-1800](http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_WinTV-HVR-1800) the 1800 does work, so not sure why it wasn't on your system.

You may however struggle to get digital TV working with a desktop aerial unless you live on top of the transmitter.
