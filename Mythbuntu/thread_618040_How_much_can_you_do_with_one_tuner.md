### Post by MrFSL on 2007-11-20
No. With a PVR-150 you cannot record one show and watch another. You can record one show and watch a PRE-RECORDED show, but not a "live" one. Get it?

A PVR-350 comes with a few more inputs. You have 1 input that is RF (coax) and 1 that is s-video. There is also a cheap add on that gives you an additional component input.

### Post by uopjohnson on 2007-12-02
The 350 is only a single tuner.  The 500 is the dual tuner.  I believe they are both equally well supported so it really just comes down to what you are willing to pay an dhow many pci slots you have available. 
If you are building a low end SD system then the 350 is nice because it has on board mpg decoding, however this is less and less important as your system gets more powerful.
