### Post by xinix on 2009-11-05
So you've uninstalled myhtweb and things load as is it were still installed?
Look in the /usr/share/mythtv folder.  It should not have a mythweb folder.  If it does, then the uninstall didn't go through completely.  Remove the folder and reinstall mythweb.  
Mythweb has been tricky for some people.  It took me a while to get it working again.  In the end my issue was related to the mythweb tables in the mythconverg database.  But, I do have it working on an AMD64.
