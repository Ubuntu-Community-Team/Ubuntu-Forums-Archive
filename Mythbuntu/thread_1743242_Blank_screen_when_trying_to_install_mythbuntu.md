### Post by agamotto on 2011-05-01
I have been hearing from some folks that the black screen can be as long as 5-10 minutes as the software is probing your system and configuring your gfx setup.  I know seeing 'nothing' is annoying, but try giving it at least 10min after it appears.  It may not work, but I have been hearing of long blank screen times with various nVidia and AMD cards, so you aren't alone with this.
