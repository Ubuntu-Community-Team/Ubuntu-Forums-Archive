### Post by Old Jimma on 2012-12-25
Folks:

If after upgrading to MythTV 0.26 you find you need a password to access mythfrontend > general > settings, open mythbackend > general and then change the PIN to 0000. 

Your troubles may cease, as mine did! 

:)

If your sound vanishes, after changing the PIN, go into mythfrontend audio settings and fool with the sound type until it works. Setting it as "stereo" for me worked well enough.

:)
