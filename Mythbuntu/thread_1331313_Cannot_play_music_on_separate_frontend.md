### Post by novellahub on 2009-11-19
You need to share your music directory from the backend to the separate frontend machine.  This can be done via NFS or Samba.  See here for more details:

[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Mediashares](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Mediashares)
