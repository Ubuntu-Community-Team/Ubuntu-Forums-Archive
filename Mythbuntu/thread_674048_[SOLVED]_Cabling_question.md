### Post by Chuckels550 on 2008-01-23
The problem was a cabling issue.  On the Soundblaster Audigy X-Gamer the audio out to the TV should be from the Line Out jack and not the Analog/Digital out jack.  I now have audio for LiveTV, TV Recordings and Mythmusic.  I haven't configured MythVideo/MythDVD and MythArchive, yet.
