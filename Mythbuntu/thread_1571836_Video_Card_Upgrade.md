### Post by ian dobson on 2010-09-10
Hi,

If you can find a NVIDIA card that supports VDPAU (thats the NVidia hardware acceleration for videos) then maybe it's worth it. Otherwise:-

1) Save the money
2) Go of meal with wife/girlfriend/boyfriend or what ever
3) Purchase a newer system.

I'm running my frontend on an underclocked e5200 (1.2GHz) and it has no problems playing back HD (1080i) video. The cpu load is no more than about 10-20% as the graphic card (NVidia 9600) does all the work.
