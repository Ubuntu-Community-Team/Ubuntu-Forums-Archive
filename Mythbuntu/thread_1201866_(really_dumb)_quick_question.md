### Post by crez79 on 2009-07-01
In the home folder. The period in the front of the folder name means it is hidden, so that is probably why you can't find it. from terminal use ```
ls -al
``` display all the contents. Also, in nautilus, you can hit ctrl+h to see the hidden contents.
