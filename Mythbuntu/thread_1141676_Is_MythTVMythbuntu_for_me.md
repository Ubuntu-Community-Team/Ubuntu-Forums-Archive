### Post by Caps18 on 2009-04-28
I would use MythVideo (part of MythTV) to play mp4 files stored on the computer.  You or your son can just use the remote to pick the movie poster of the video to watch.

Streaming videos from the web doesn't always work too well, and setting up a torrent sounds like work.  You can probably use handbrake on the mythbuntu computer if you get out of MythTV.

I would recommend trying it, especially if you have a spare computer or hard drive around.
