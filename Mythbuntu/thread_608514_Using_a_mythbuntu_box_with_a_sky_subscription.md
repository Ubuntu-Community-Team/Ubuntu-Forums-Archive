### Post by roseway on 2007-11-10
It's marginally possible, but quite messy. You can get a satellite TV card with a viewing card reader, such as [http://www.twinhan.com/product_satellite_s2_CI.asp](http://www.twinhan.com/product_satellite_s2_CI.asp) but it needs a thing called a CAM (which links the viewing card to the reader) to complete the hardware. CAMs are specific to the service which you're accessing, and Sky haven't issued CAMs for their service. So there's no legitimate way to do what you want.

There's a programmable CAM called DragonCam, which can be programmed to work with Sky, but I understand that you still have to insert the card into a genuine Sky digibox periodically to keep it alive. I gave up on the idea when I was looking at this. :(
