It means that you don't have to mess with sudo in order to reboot your computer :). Since you already have an install, you need to edit the shutdown command manually. Enter "/usr/share/mythtv/myth-halt.sh", or myth-reboot.sh or myth-suspend.sh or myth-hibernate.sh...
