### Post by agamotto on 2008-07-27
If you have Mediacom analog cable, and are trying to find the digital channels that are 'passed through,' set your channel source for OTA, then scan manually for channels using the Cable freq table, and QAM 256.  This seems to give all of the local DTV channels that they carry in my area.
