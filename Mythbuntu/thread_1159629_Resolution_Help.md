### Post by tgm4883 on 2009-05-17
Are you talking about resolution for recording?  If so, you can do this for analog cards, but not digital ones.  You do it in the frontend, under "Utilities/Setup > Setup > TV Settings > Recording Profiles"  You will want to change the Default one and probably live TV (if you use live TV).  You can setup a bunch of different profiles if you want and have different recording schedules use different profiles.  Increasing the resolution will increase the disk space used.
