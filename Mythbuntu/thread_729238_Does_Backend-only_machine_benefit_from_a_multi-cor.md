### Post by majoridiot on 2008-03-20
a dual core would definitely benefit in a multi-tuner recording setup- especially for HD tuners.  it does not
take much power to simply record or stream, but if you will be doing multiple recordings at once, with
commercial flagging, transcoding, streaming to frontends, etc.  the extra power will be a boon.

plus, for the small difference in price, why not future-proof your new build a little?
