### Post by CaptainPatent on 2008-11-12
I didn't look up the specs of your motherboard but how fast is the AMD processor running the configuration and what video card do you have?

Is the problem restricted to watching live tv or are all recordings slow like you're saying? Are you doing HDTV or analog? (you need a much beefier setup for HDTV)

If you have a slow processor / onboard video or are doing HDTV, it may just be that the processing power to decode / save to file / read from file / produce displayable format for video out / display video is too much for the setup you have.

If you have a quicker processor or an (accelerated video card / newer onboard card) though it sounds like you may have a driver problem.
