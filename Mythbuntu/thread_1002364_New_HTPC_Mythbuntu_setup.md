### Post by ahood on 2008-12-06
> **jschodde said:**
> One thing I'm not able to figure out is what software to run to actually schedule record tv shows with. What will you be using? 

Any suggestions...anyone?

Mythtv is the software used to schedule tv recordings. What Mythtv won't have is tv schedule data. Thus, on a fresh install, Mythtv won't know what channels you have or the programs channels provide.

To obtain schedule data, and because you are in the US, sign up for a [**[COLOR="Sienna"]SchedulesDirect[/COLOR]**]("http://www.schedulesdirect.org/") account.  

If you haven't installed Mythtv yet, I recommend reading and a basic understanding configuration Mythtv

[**[COLOR="Sienna"]Mythbuntu Manual[/COLOR]**]("http://www.mythbuntu.org/installation_manual")

[**[COLOR="Sienna"]Mythbuntu Wiki[/COLOR]**]("http://help.ubuntu.com/community/MythTV")

Hope this helps.
