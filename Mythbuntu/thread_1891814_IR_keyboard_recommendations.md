### Post by thatguruguy on 2011-12-06
True, but it can be used for other things on the desktop, as needed.

I have a Logitech wireless keyboard and mouse (which I bought at Walmart for ~$30) that I use with my Mythbox.  I also have a separate remote control, which I use with a homebrew serial IR receiver (made with ~$5 dollars worth of electronics, plus a $5 serial cable.  The remote is an old universal remote I've had for over 10 years).

I tend to use the remote, my kids tend to use the keyboard.  They also play a lot of games on that computer, for which they need the keyboard and mouse.
