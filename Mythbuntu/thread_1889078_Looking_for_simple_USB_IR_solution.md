### Post by mutant_matt on 2011-12-02
If you want an IR USB receiver that can take input from any random remote, then I would too be interested in any help anyone has to offer (eventually, I'd like to be able to use an old TiVo "Peanut" remote to control Myth, as I have 10 years "muscle memory" with it, and it is brilliantly designed!).

However, you might want to try the MCE USB, as it pretty much "just works".  I too have a Revo based (Mythbuntu) 11.10 install, using this, and setting it up was mostly very easy.  It was £15 in Maplin (approx £13/£14 online), and is labelled as the "Windows Vista/Windows 7" MCE USB, which has a mousepad built in, so you can control the mouse outside of Myth on normal apps/the OS, and comes with a USB IR receiver dongle.

I plan to put together a thread at some point, detailing the steps I took to get this fully working, on 11.10, as I have pulled together various tips on various pages to get this working fully (it works in a basic sense, out of the box on 11.10 with no work at all).

HTH!

Matt :)
