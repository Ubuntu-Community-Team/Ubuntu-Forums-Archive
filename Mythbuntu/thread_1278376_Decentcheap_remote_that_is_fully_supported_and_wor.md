### Post by nzsouthernman on 2009-09-30
I've had lots of success with the Microsoft Media Center remotes. Works no problem out of the box. Once you've got the USB receiver you can replace the remote with a Logitech Harmony remote and control everything from one device. You just tell the Harmony software that it's talking to a Microsoft Media center PC and Myth works sweet.

### Post by Lepy on 2009-10-01
Chalk me up as another satisfied customer of the MCE remote and receiver. The setup requires 1.) plug in the usb receiver and 2.) select the proper remote from the myth control center. 

If you happen to have a universal remote, as nzsouthernman said, setting up one up afterwards is really very simple. It may only require another trip to the myth control center.

I'm using an MCE receiver with a Radioshack 15-2116 (a little tweaked in lirc, but it also has a preset in myth control center). It also works perfectly in mythtv as well as boxee and xbmc. I never have to leave the couch! Now if I could only get it to work with the logitech z5500 :confused:
