### Post by dude08 on 2010-01-10
In Mythbuntu 9.10: Applications > Settings > Xfce 4 Settings Manager. Go to "Session and Startup" and then go to the "Application Startup" tab. Scroll down and you'll see the "Add" button. Here you can add any program you want to the startup, you just need to know what the command (or path) is to the executable program to run.
