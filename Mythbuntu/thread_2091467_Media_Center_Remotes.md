### Post by topcat5 on 2012-12-05
I've tried a lot of combinations and the only thing that suits my needs and isn't a pain to setup is a Logitech Harmony 650 remote paired with a USB receiver from flirc.tv. It works really well with mythtv and the advantage of this is you don't need lirc.  

I bought a $15 wireless USB keyboard (on sale) that I use when I need keyboard access.
