Make sure your 10.04 is up to date, then upgrade to 10.10 (sudo update-manager -c).  After that I would enable MythTV-Updates repository as well: [http://www.mythbuntu.org/repos](http://www.mythbuntu.org/repos)  Then you should be running the latest and greatest.  As with all updates, backup your DB first.
