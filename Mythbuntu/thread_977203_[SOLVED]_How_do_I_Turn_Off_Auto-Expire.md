### Post by bergqvistjl on 2008-11-10
in one of the recording/playback settings screens in the frontend (sorry i cant be more specific at the moment, but ill post a screenshot when i get back from college), theres an option to 'Auto-Expire Default' which is checked. Uncheck it. As i said when im sure which settings screen its on, ill post a pic. You might need to manually turn off auto expire for all your previous recordings though. Oh, and auto expire still applies to livetv recordings.
