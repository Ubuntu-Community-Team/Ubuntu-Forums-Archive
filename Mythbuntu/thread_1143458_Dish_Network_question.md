### Post by tgm4883 on 2009-04-30
Yes, the PVR-500 box will work with Mythbuntu, just plug it in and set it up.

You will need 1 DN box for every channel you want to record at the same time (live TV counts as recording).  You cannot bypass the DN box and go straight to the PVR-500 card.
