### Post by newlinux on 2008-12-29
what kind of tuner card are you using? Is CC not available as an option in the menu during TV and recording playback? If not, have you set VBI to an appropriate setting (PAL or NTSC) in mythtv-setup (under general options)?

I have a key on my remote set to toggle VBI captions. You could also do that (or if you are using a keyboard).
