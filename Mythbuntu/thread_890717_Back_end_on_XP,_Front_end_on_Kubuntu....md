### Post by newlinux on 2008-08-15
I don't think mythtv is meant to function as regular UPnP client. GeexBox would probably be better for that. Mythfrontend is primarily meant to function as a client to its backend.

I think this will be very difficult for you to do because there is more to a myth backend than UPnP and mysql.
