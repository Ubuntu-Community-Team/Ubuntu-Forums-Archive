### Post by silverdulcet on 2008-05-13
> Also, when you look at the gallery index, there are options along the side. (see here) Mine are slightly different (such as an option to edit meta data) but the problem is: How do I access that menu? I can't seem to get there from my keyboard.

Not sure about your first question, but to access that menu hit the "M" key on your keyboard. It will highlight the first option on that menu, then you can use arrow keys to select the options you want. To shift back to selecting photos and directories hit the "M" key again. I assume whatever you have designated as your MENU key on your remote (if you have one) will work also.
