### Post by nickrout on 2011-02-20
A ripped dvd is not a 'recording', it is a 'video'

go into mythvideo, hit the menu button and then 'scan for changes'.Your video should appear.

### Post by waterwheel on 2011-02-20
That worked perfectly (on my windows media player remote I hit the windows button and got up a menu I wasn't aware existed, scanned for new videos and there it was.
