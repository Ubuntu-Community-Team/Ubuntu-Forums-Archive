### Post by squaregoldfish on 2009-01-08
There's two parts to mixer settings for sound inputs. The first is the volume, which allows you to hear what's coming in (which is why you can hear the live TV). However, to actually record the sound, there's a toggle switch to enable Capture mode, which allows the incoming sound to be recorded.

If you're using the Gnome mixer application, select your sound device at the top, and then look under the Recording tab (you may need to adjust what the mixer shows using the Preferences button). The little microphone icon beneath the slider toggles the Capture mode.

Hope I explained that well enough.
