### Post by topcat5 on 2013-12-14
I like these cases, but two things.
  
- For that kind of money I'd get one that holds more drives.  You can never have too much storage on a BE. 
- They need to be in the open to cool properly.  i.e. don't put it in a cabinet.   

I ended up with a Lian Lu HTPC case for my combined BE/FE and I stuck a large 140mm fan in it that had been slowed with a resistor.  I can't hear it.
