### Post by laffinet on 2009-07-01
> **luken8r said:**
> 

1) Can I Record multiple channels at once, in HD


That depends on the number (or type if dual card) of tuner cards you put in the system. One tuner card, one channel recording (unless the programs are on the same multiplex).

> **luken8r said:**
> 
2) Can I watch live or recorded shows on more than one TV with the signal sent over my home coax?

You'd want multiple frontends to supply more than one TV. Unless you simply want to feed the second TV the same signal as the first TV. 

> **luken8r said:**
> 
3) Can I manage the DVR shows and recording over the innertubes?

Innertubes ?
> **luken8r said:**
> 
1) Manage and play MP3s and display pictures on primary TV
Cheers
Yes. Myth comes with a plugin called mythmusic for mp3s and mythgallery for pictures. I use both and they work fine.
