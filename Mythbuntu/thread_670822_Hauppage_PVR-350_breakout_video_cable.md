### Post by Chuckels550 on 2008-01-18
Your PVR 350 should have come with a A/V breakout cable with S-Video, composite video and stereo audio out.  The connector for the PVR 350 backpanel resembles an S-Video connector, but has more pins and larger diameter.  You shouldn't require any adapter to use either composite or S-Video cables to connect to your TV.
