### Post by black veils on 2012-09-23
1. right-click the panel, go to *panel preferences*

2. on the *display* tab, uncheck *lock panel*

3. grab the panel by the end and drag it to the bottom. then choose the length etc that you want, and you can recheck the *lock panel* setting if you want.
