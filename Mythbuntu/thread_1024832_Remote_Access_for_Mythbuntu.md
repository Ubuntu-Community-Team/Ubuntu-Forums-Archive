### Post by elj4176 on 2008-12-29
If I understand your question correctly: 

You could use mythweb and ssh to control the mythbuntu backend from outside your lan instead of vnc.
If you are on the same network and you want to watch content from your mythbuntu machine on an XP machine then you can use mythplayer ([http://www.sudu.dk/mythtvplayer/](http://www.sudu.dk/mythtvplayer/)). It even has a live tv function now.
