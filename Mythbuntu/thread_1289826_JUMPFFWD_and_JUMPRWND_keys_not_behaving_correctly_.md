### Post by falcon15500 on 2009-10-12
Now I feel like an idiot. :)

It was the SEEKFFWD and SEEKRWND keys I needed...  Somehow I mapped the wrong keys to the buttons on my remote.  All fixed now.  And in case anyone was wondering, the amount of the seeking is controlled in the "Default Playback Groups" in setup.
