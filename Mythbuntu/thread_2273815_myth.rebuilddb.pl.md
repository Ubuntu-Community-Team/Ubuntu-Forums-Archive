### Post by aelfric55 on 2015-04-17
That script hasn't been maintained since about mythtv 0.24, and may no longer be safely usable since the database's conversion to the use of UTC times beginning with v0.26. The only supported method since that time for importing recordings from other mythtv installations has been to add them to mythtv as Videos, rather than Recordings.
