### Post by grunge09 on 2013-05-10
If you are using analog TV and your TV is capable of receiving channels w/o a set top box I recommend Haupauuge's PVR-500.  Its a PCI Card with DUAL analog tuners and dual mpeg hardware decoders.  I have 2 in my PC with another dual tuner card giving me the ability to record up to 6 recordings at once.  

And the PVR-500's are cheap on eBay.   Get one with the IR USB receiver and a remote if you can/  Very easy to setup on the backend (note you will have to setup both tuners separately for each card that supports dual tuners).
