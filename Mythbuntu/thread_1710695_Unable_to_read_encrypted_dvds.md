I'm sorry you are having issues playing DVD's. It is not a problem with MythTV or Mythbuntu. DVD's are changing the way they are read. It used to be you put the disk in and it read the file. Now The DVD has 30-60 files and the PC has to scan those files until it finds the correct one to play. Unfortunately it doesn't always work correctly.
