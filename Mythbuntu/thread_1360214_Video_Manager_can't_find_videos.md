### Post by JDGlanville on 2009-12-20
Brief synopsis: new install of Mythbuntu 9.10, moved some MPGs to the videos share, and the Video Manager doesn't see the MPGs.

Longer story: I recently tried to upgrade my Mythbuntu 9.04 installation.  Unfortunately, it didn't go 100%, so I took this as an opportunity to do a clean install.

I copied the MPGs from the 9.04's Recording share to another computer.  I then did a complete install of Mythbuntu 9.10 over top of the 9.04 installation.  Finally, I moved the old recordings from the other computer back to Mythbuntu's Videos share (not the Recordings share).

Now that I have the videos back on the PVR, the Video Manager can't find them.  I can still watch the videos, but outside of Mythbuntu through VLC, but that slightly defeats the use of Mythbuntu.

What do I need to do in order to get the Video Manager to recognize the MPGs?

Thanks.

---

### Post by myth_cougar on 2009-12-20
In the video manager, press the M key and select scan for changes.

This should pick up any videos that Mythtv doesn't know about yet.

---

### Post by JDGlanville on 2009-12-20
That did it.  Thanks!
