### Post by twkisner on 2007-11-19
I just upgraded this weekend to Mythbuntu from the Fiesty packages.

First, let me say that upgrading from fiesty to gutsy (I was running a dedicated frontend /backend) breaks the packages to a certain extent.  It doesn't autologon anymore and the plugins that were installed get un-installed.  So I kinda "had" to goto Mythbuntu.

Anyway, it was the most painless it's ever been for me for installing Mythtv.  The most painful stuff that had me pullling out the hair all seems to be automatically done now (xorg config, lirc, the perl mysql database "fixer", and many many others).  Aside from installing the drivers for my tuner cards, I didn't have to do anything manually.  Very nice!

I had picked Ubuntu to install Mythtv initally because the instructions in the Wiki and the package were so easy compared to other distros.  It just keeps getting better and better.
