### Post by benlyboy on 2009-10-10
Thought I would post a short note with my thoughts on the new mythbuntu 9.10

In short I love it.

It is still in bata, and it shows. I have had a couple of lock ups and a few things do not quite work right, but it is early days. and I know these thing will get fixes

The big change you notice is in the look and feel of the GUI much slicker and smarter than the older version. 

I couldn't get the live CD to work. Maybe someone smarter than me can get it to work.    

They have removed the link to the mythbuntu control center in settings. I don't really mind this, but one thing I have always thought would be good is if you could make all changes to myth using only a remote, and not have to hook up a keyboard and mouse. This may not be doable but it would be nice.

Would be interested in hearing others thoughts on 9.10
