### Post by Mad_Professor on 2010-07-22
Wireless B is not good for HD playback.

Wireless G 108Mbps might be a better choice but it's hit or miss with brands. 

You might want to try lowering the bitrate for HD recordings,  Assuming you're using mpeg4 settings.

On the other hand try Wireless N setups or better yet just get cat5 or 6 cable and hook your box to the switch.
