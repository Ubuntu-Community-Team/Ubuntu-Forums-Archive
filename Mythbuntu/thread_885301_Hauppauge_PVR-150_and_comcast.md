### Post by newlinux on 2008-08-10
the pvr-150 is an NTSC tuner. It can tune analog stations transmitted to you (It will pick up the same stations as an old VCR or analog TV tuner). What stations you get analog depend on your location. It's not the same everywhere for comcast (for instance Chicago Comcast no longer transmits any analog stations).

The PVR-150 can also be used to capture from cable boxes.
