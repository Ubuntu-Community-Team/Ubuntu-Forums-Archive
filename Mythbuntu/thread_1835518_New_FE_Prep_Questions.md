### Post by nickrout on 2011-08-29
Basically you need the same version of mythtv and that's it, so at least 10.04, 10.10 and 11.04 will all work because all will give you the same version of myth via mythbuntu repos.

I would however stick with 10.10 because 11.04 is a dog in dog's clothing. Refer to the many "I upgraded to/installed 11.04 and now myth doesn't work" threads. Alternatively 10.04 will give you upgrades (OS and myth) until the next LTS version of *buntu.
