### Post by nasha on 2009-04-15
A point worth noting with the 9400 & nvidia restricted drivers, as i've just experienced the problem.

The problem is, when connecting the 9400 to a display via DVI - HDMI cable, the TV is telling the video card that it supports HDMI audio, so the video card is sending an empty audio stream along with the video, even though it is DVI. Since the TV recognizes audio over the HDMI, it refuses to accept audio from any other port. 

This is easily worked around however (see link below), and the 9400 has proven to be a great card, and i've had no issues what so all with it after that.

Personally i'd go with the 9400 based purely on the fact its a newer series gpu for much the same price.
