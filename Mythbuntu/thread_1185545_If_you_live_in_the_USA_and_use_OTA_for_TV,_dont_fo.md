### Post by LowSky on 2009-06-12
no one posted this so I figued it might be a good thing to post

If you live in the USA and use over-the-air signal (antenna) for TV, dont forget to rescan your channels this weekend on your MYthTV box.

with the move to digital signals, many stations will be broadcasting on new frequencies, you may even recieve more stations.

dont forget that you will lose most of your analog channels by tonight. these means all analog tuners wil no longer work unless you have a cable providor, like Comcast, or a digital converter box.
