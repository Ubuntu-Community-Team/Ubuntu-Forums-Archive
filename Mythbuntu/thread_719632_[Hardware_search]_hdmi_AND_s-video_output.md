### Post by volkswagner on 2008-03-10
Check the sticky:functional hardware...Kameleon25 has the same MOBO as myself (post #68).  I was able to install Mythbuntu 7.10 AMD64 while connected via VGA and s-video.  The ATI graphics is not without issues.  This MOBO had everything I wanted onboard.  Check newegg for reviews.  The hdmi on the back plate.  The TV out is via an expansion plate.  I am using firewire capture, optical audio, and s-video to CRT TV, 2 PCI tuners, all in an Antec Fusion case.  The biggest flaw is the location of the SATA ports.  I was able to shoe horn 2 90deg cables in my case without mods.  I cant post links from my phone.

### Post by Jamikest on 2008-03-10
I just purchased a card with exactly your requirements. 

So far: 720p working, have not made1080i work.  I think this may have more to do with my TV than this card.  Unable to get SPDIF working, so not running sound through HDMI.

Video is smooth, and card is silent.  Price is reasonable.

MSI NX8500GT-MTD256EH GeForce 8500GT 256MB 128-bit GDDR2 PCI Express x16 HDCP Ready Silent Heatsink Video Card - Retail 

About $70.00 from online stores (dont know if its ok to post the retailers name).

Its a low profile card with DVI, HDMI and S-Video.  You will need to order or custom make the low profile plate if you need it.
