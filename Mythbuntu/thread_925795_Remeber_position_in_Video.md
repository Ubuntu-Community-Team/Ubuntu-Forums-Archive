### Post by newlinux on 2008-09-21
There is a setting that is a dropdown on what to do when exiting a recording. By default it is set to "Just Exit" I tis either in general TV settings or playback TV settings.

---

### Post by newlinux on 2008-09-21
It's on setup->TV Settings->Playback screen 2 of 9 (Action on playback exit)

---

### Post by newlinux on 2008-09-21
Of course you can always manually set the bookmark position by pressing the spacebar while watching playback too (this can be configured to toggle a bookmark or always set a bookmark).
