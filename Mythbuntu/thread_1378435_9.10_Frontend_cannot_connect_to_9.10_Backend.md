### Post by SiHa on 2010-01-11
Most likely, you need to run mythtv-setup on the BE/FE, and in the general setup page, put that machine's IP address in the spaces in the relevant boxes (ie, all the ones that have 127.0.0.1 in them).

Unfortunately, there is nothing in the frontend setup to prompt you to do this, and this is compounded by the fact that when you enter the details in the 'Test Connection' page you get a successful result! I spent several hours pulling my hair out after upgrading to a fresh install of 9.10 because I forgot to do this...

HTH
