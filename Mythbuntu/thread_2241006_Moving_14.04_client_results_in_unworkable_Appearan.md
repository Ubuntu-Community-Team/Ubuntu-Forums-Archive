I moved my Mythbuntu 14.04 client from my office to the den.  In the office it was connected to HDMI monitor with DVI cable, in the den it is connected via an HDMI cable.  Previously, in the den the Mythbuntu 12.04 server was using this HDMI Connection.  The HDMI connection goes to a Pioneer VSX-520 HDMI receiver, and then to a Samsung LN55C650 television.

This arrangement worked well before the change.

Now when the Mythbuntu 14.04 client is started the appearance is off, meaning the screen appears as if it were much smaller, see the screen shot. [[img]http://www.zimagez.com/images/zz.png[/img]](http://www.zimagez.com/zimage/screenshot-08232014-093240am.php)

Normal appearance can often be achieved by going to Settings:Appearance, selecting a different option, then reselecting Mythbuntu.  Occasionally this does not work, and logging off, then logging back on before making the change works.

I have no clue how this happened or how to fix it, some help would be appreciated. :confused:
