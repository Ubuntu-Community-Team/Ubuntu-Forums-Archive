### Post by jb5 on 2009-10-04
Long Shot - but on one of my TV's the s-video enabled scart socket can operate in two modes, s-video mode and 'normal' mode. 

If trying to use composite (s-video) and the TV was using the other mode for that socket, the picture would be in black and white. 
Toggle to the other mode and the colour would be recognised.

So it might be worth checking if it is a similar situation with your set-up.
Sorry, don't know your TV model personally so it would be a case of reading the manual.
Good luck.
