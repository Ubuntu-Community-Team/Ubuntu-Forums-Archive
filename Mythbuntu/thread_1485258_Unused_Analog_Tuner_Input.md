### Post by dotnick on 2010-05-16
Hello all,

I currently use MythTv for OTA broadcasting only and it is working very well.  Thank you.  However, nearly all broadcasts are digital now (USA) and I have an unused analog input on my Hauppage 1600 Tv Tuner card.  I would like to hook up my DTV converter box to record a third channel at any given time.  I only get a handful of channels here (FOX, NBC, ABC, CBS, PBS), so leaving the DTV tuner tuned to the same channel always is the option I was hoping to go for, but because the analog tuner would be tuned to channel 3 I don't know how to forward the schedule information to that channel.  Any ideas.

Quick sceneario:

Tuner 1 (Digital is recording) -- ABC
Tuner 2 (Digital is recording) -- NBC
Tuner 3 (Available but confused about when to record)

### Post by volkswagner on 2010-05-17
If I follow you, your DTV converter would need to be controlled by your PC, via IR, Serial, FireWire or other means to tune the correct channel.  

Or you could use just one channel and manually create/edit a new lineup to include the information for the static channel you choose on your DTV box, but set the frequency to channel 3 for your analog input.
