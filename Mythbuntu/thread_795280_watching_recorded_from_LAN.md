### Post by murchball on 2008-05-15
Depending on what operating system you are using, you can either install the frontend on the other computers on your network or install mythweb on the backend and watch your shows on the media player of your choice.

---

### Post by amphibem on 2008-05-15
The other option you have if the other machines are on Windows is to use Mythtv Player ([http://sudu.dk/mythtvplayer/](http://sudu.dk/mythtvplayer/)).

It allows you to browse the recordings on your backend, and can read the commercial skip points. Its not completly stable (it has crashed on me a few times whilst skipping, especially with a show that is still being recorded) but is still a better way than using Mythweb in my opinion.
