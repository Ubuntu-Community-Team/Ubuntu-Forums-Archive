### Post by Lester_Burnham on 2008-07-06
Hi,

As DutchLoki said... I would check what the native resolution of your new TV is and try and send this resolution to the TV.

Are you running the restricted drivers for your video card? What is it, nvidia or ATI?

Some TV's like the Panasonic Plasmas allow you to turn off overscan on the HDMI input, allowing the desktop to fit nicely on the screen.

If you have a nvidia card and are running restricted drivers, you should be able to type "sudo nvidia-settings" into a terminal to change the resolution to 1920x1080 if required.

I try and back peddle to where you started and see if the above helps.

Lester
