Tv appears full-screen by design, since mythtv is primarily designed for HTPC use. But there is a setting in the frontend setup to make the frontend open in a window. Check the user manual on the mythtv wiki at: [http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/User_Manual:Detailed_configuration_Frontend#Appearance](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/User_Manual:Detailed_configuration_Frontend#Appearance)

The onscreen commands (and most others) are handled through keyboard shortcuts. These key bindings are intended to be easily programmable into a remote, and may be reconfigured to suit your needs. The complete list of default bindings is also to be found on the myth wiki at: [http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Keybindings](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Keybindings)

Cheers!
