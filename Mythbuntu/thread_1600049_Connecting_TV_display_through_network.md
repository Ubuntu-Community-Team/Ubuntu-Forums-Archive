### Post by LowSky on 2010-10-18
Hi welcome to the forums.

Mythbuntu (and Ubuntu) is based on Debian, so the learning curve wont be too bad.

What you want to do is not possible. You will need a PC with a VGA, DVI, or HMDI port to get recordings made on the PC to the TV.

Yes ethernet can do some amazing things, but the ethernet port on the back of many newer TV is just a service port, some can access limited online content like Youtube, but what you wish to do is not very simple or possible with the hardware you have.

There is Intel Wireless Display but its only on very new equoiptment and still requires a small set top box for the TV. I'm not even sure it will work with Linux
