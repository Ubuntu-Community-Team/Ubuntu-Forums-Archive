### Post by ttabbal on 2008-10-06
Outside of compiling a custom kernel, is there any way to get UDF 2.5 in Mythbuntu 8.04? I could make my own kernel, but if someone has a faster way, that hopefully won't keep me from updating via apt, it would be nice.

I got the directions from the post here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=718744](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=718744) working. 

I was having trouble as the mentioned package "linux-headers" does not exist. I used "sudo apt-get install linux-headers-2.6.24-19-generic" and got the build running. The new module can mount the discs I had problems with.
