### Post by bance on 2011-08-09

I'm not familiar with harmony remotes, I've used mceusb in my system.

I think that you may be on the right track, going with a harmony (learning remote) though. 

Most people from what I've gathered, tend to go for an mce receiver, however you might think about using a cordless keyboard,

### Post by ZedThou on 2011-08-09
I got a cheap 3rd party MCE remote (doesn't really matter, but in my case it was an Anyware HA-IR01SV) just for the USB receiver, and in the Harmony One configuration added it as a Media Center PC device.

Then just had to tweak the config in ~/.lirc/mythtv to make the remote buttons (using irw, the list of commands in the Logitech setup, and mythweb key editor) act as I wanted. This was the most time-consuming part of the process.
