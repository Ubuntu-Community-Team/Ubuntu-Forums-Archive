Last weekend I built a Mythbuntu 9.04 box to replace my old analog-only MythTV box running FC4 (and MythTV 0.17(!) hacked to handle the new SchedulesDirect listing format.)

Mythbuntu made things incredibly easy - I remember it taking almost  a month to get my old box going.

Thanks to everyone who's contributed their time!
