### Post by klc5555 on 2012-10-24
If the machine is intended for doing a wide range of desktoppy computery stuff, and also TV, then Ubuntu (Xubuntu, Kubuntu, etc.) with mythtv frontend/backend packages is likely the better way to go.

If the machine is intended primarily as an entertainment machine, with only very minimal forays into other areas, then Mythbuntu itself is likely the better choice. All of the stuff you need for an entertainment/DVR machine, but less of the stuff you don't. (When compared to its closest sibling, Xubuntu + mythtv packages)
