### Post by laffinet on 2009-02-19
Or you can leave Mythfrontend running, hit ALT+F2, which opens the "run program" dialog. At the same time your normal taskbar becomes visible which means you can access your menu, shortcuts in the taskbar etc.

BTW Mythbuntu runs xfce by default, not gnome. Not a major thing, just thought I mention it.
