### Post by newlinux on 2008-08-26
the recording profiles control how the video stream is captured. You don't get to change much about how digital streams are captured. What you want to modify is how they are played back in your TV->playback settings. There are two settings in there that deal with stretching an aspect ration. Additionally you can change these settings on the fly from the menu while watching playback. What resolution are you using to playback content?
