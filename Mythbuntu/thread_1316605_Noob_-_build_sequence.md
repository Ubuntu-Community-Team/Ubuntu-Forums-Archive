### Post by SiHa on 2009-11-06
Yes.
The installer will default to resizing the 'resident' OS partition, and install alongside.
But you can elect to use the entire drive for Mythbuntu.

Stick with it, it'll be a steep learning curve, but if you've ever tinkered with Unix / Dos, you'll find the command-line is your friend. I embarked on this process as a Linux noob about 18 months ago. You will find the people on this forum, and the Ubuntu forums in general very helpful.

BTW. I wouldn't necessarily agree with "Install all updates", but I guess that's down to personal preference. Search the forum for "I've just updated and...". 

Cheers,

Simon
