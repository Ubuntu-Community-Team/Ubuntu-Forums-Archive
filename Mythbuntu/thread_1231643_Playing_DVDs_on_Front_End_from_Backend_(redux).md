### Post by issih on 2009-08-04
I believe it is possible using samba to share out the mount point over the network, but I will admit I have never done it:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=829658](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=829658)

search about on samba and dvds.

Be aware though, that you will need a reasonably pokey network connection to stream the data fast enough.
Full dvd rips are generally not happy over my 54g wireless network, so you are liable to need either an n based wireless network, hardwire connections, or powerline gear to get smooth video to play.

Hope that helps
