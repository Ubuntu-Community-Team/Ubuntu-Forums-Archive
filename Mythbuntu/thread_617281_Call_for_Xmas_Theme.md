### Post by toorima on 2008-12-13
You could easily modify one to become a christmas theme, I have modified Iulius and added a christmas background.
make a copy of /usr/share/mythtv/themes/Iulius and name it something
move the new background to /usr/share/mythtv/themes/<new-name>/background.png
edit /usr/share/mythtv/themes/<new-name>/themeinfo.xml change name to what you want to call your theme.
