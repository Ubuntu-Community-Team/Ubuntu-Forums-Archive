### Post by tgm4883 on 2008-07-09
Yes you will need to backup your database (at least the recorded shows table) as well as all your shows.  This is a process that will require a great deal of space, as you need somewhere to store your shows and movies while you reformat.  Ideally, if you have a non-traditional storage location on a separate drive then you can leave those there, wipe the main drive, and restore the db after you reinstall.

On another note, why do you want to wipe the drive?
