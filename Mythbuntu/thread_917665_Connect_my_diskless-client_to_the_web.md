### Post by volkswagner on 2008-09-12
What you are looking to do, add router services to your server.  Search linux as router.

This is for wireless but can give you start.  The main thing is one of your network cards need to have the option of master mode.  This will be connected to the web.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=5774362#post5774362]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=5774362#post5774362")

It may be less work to replace the switch with a router.
