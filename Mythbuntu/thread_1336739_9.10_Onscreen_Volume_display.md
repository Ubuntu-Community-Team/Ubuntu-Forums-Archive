### Post by djroketboy on 2009-11-25
Just incase anyone else has this issue, here is the fix

Turn off the XFCE Volume Daemon in Sessions and Startup.

Source: [http://www.mythtvtalk.com/forum/general/12274-myth-0-22-volume-oddities.html](http://www.mythtvtalk.com/forum/general/12274-myth-0-22-volume-oddities.html)
