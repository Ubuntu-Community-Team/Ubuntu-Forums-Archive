### Post by Lester_Burnham on 2014-11-24
Hi,
I'm using a Zotac ID41 (ION2 VESA mounted) in the bedroom. To turn it on I either WOL from my phone or reach over the 26" TV and turn it on. You can mess with waking from USB using your remote, but it can take some fiddling to get right.

I also use a "smart" power board on that system, using the Zotac as the master, as I don't watch live TV via the antenna. If i do watch live TV, I just use it through mythtv. That way the TV is shut down by the Zotac HTPC.

I have succeeded before with powering on and off using a Zotac mini-ITX ION 1 system. It stopped working after a major upgrade and I haven't bothered fixing it yet :)
