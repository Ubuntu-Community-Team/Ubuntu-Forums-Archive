### Post by delaroyas on 2009-04-25
I have the  Hauppauge 1250  running un Mythbuntu 8.10.

The ATSC tuner worked out of the box, but the analog tuner doesn't work.

As for the 1250's IR reciever in LIRC here is what i've learned from the [mythtv wik]("http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Hauppauge_HVR-1250")i:
"As of LIRC version 0.8.4a the IR chipset used in this cards Infrared (IR) receiver is not 
supported. You will need to purchase or make a separate IR tuner to use it's remote control." 

I also asked Hauppauge why there IR reciever doesn't work under Linux/LIRC, they didn't even bother to answer me.
