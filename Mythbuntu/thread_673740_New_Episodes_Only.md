### Post by superm1 on 2008-01-21
This is going to depend a lot on how much data the network provides about the show.  Personally, i've seen that networks like comedy central don't often have all the data for the daily show.  To get around this, i'll tell it to record only at say 10 PM, weekdays.  I'll still get a few duplicates here and there, but its a lot better than when i tell it to record every episode.

Additionally there is a flag to record only 'new episodes', but this will only work if the network provides adequate data.

The flag for a "new" episode is determined by its "first air" date.  You can look at your recording schedule and customize if this is looked at in the "Look for duplicates in" section.  You can get to this in both mythweb and mythfrontend.
