### Post by TheMiz on 2010-01-29
After much soul searching, I have discovered that NO, lircrc cannot reset itself.

However, I have also determined that your remote will not work without an appropriate hardware.conf file, and I have also determined that I apparently do not know how to setup a functioning symlink (I created it, but apparently lirc says it "can't get exclusive access to events coming from `/dev/input/irremote' interface".

Now I just need to see if I can get DVD ripping to work!
