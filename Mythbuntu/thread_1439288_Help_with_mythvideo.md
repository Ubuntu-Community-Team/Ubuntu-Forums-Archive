### Post by xinix on 2010-03-28
I'm not quite sure how to do what you'd like to do; that is open MythVideo to a specified directory other than the one you set as default.  But, if you have a sub-folder in you default video folder, and disable "flat view" (hit 'M' to see the option to enable/disable this), you can navigate its contents separate from your movies.  The sub-folder would appear listed the same way other movies are shown (as a single entry). Just select it to browse it's contents.  This sub folder could be a symlink to what ever folder you normally keep non movie videos in.  Not ideal, but it is a way to separate things.
