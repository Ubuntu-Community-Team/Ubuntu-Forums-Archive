### Post by movieman on 2009-08-08
An Atom will be fine for SDTV and music: that's about $80 for the motherboard/CPU plus a case, some RAM and maybe a hard drive and/or DVD drive depending on what you plan to do with it.

Of course by the time you've added a monitor to that you probably would do better to buy a cheap netbook and get it all in one, if you can live with the small screen.
