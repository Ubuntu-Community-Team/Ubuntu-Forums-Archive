### Post by ratcheer on 2009-12-27
I am not saying it is the best, but I have been using a Netgear WNDR3300 dual band router for a little less than a year and it has been rock solid. It is supporting a Macbook Pro, and iPod, a PS3, and a PSP and all are connecting, flawlessly.

On the other hand, at the same time I bought a Netgear WN111 USB adapter, and it is crap. When it connects, it drops frequently. Sometimes it won't connect at all. And when it is working, it runs very hot, hot enough that it scares me.
