### Post by uncle hammy on 2008-04-26
I just finished doing an upgrade (as in not a fresh install) from 7.10 to 8.04.  As a Linux idiot, I don't get to participate much in here, other than seeking guidance, so right off the bat, my kudos to everyone involved in the project.  The upgrade essentially went flawlessly, and the new product is simply stellar.

The only "issue" I had was during the upgrade process, when asked to set up my remote, the only options were "none" and "custom".  I had backed up my lirc file, so it was rather easy to go into the control panel, set up my remote, and replace the lirc file.  It would have been nice to have been able to do that during setup (especially the option to save the existing lirc file).

My suggestions to anyone about to go ahead with this...

1.  If you used Envy to install your ATI or NVidia drivers, uninstall the drivers with envy, then remove envy.
2.  Backup xorg.conf and your lirc file

Anyways, again job well done to all who had anything to do with this project!
