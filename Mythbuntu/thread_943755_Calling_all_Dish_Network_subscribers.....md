### Post by anonymousdog on 2008-10-10
It's been about 3 yrs we've had Dish with one dual-LNB antenna and two SD dual-output receivers, 322s, hooked to the four outputs from a DP34.  Works great for four SD TVs, but the second output from each of the 322s can only be controlled with the RF remotes (so no IR channel changing).  The second TV output on each receiver is run over RG58(?) coax to the TV location that can be as distant from the receiver as you like (within the limits of coax's and RF's ability to carry the signals).  The second TV output also has RCA outs for video and stereo audio.  All the initial wiring (not involving the MythTV system) was done by the installer; I'm surprised to learn that they didn't offer you a similar option.

I have no experience with their HD offerings, but it looks like the LNBs are already capable.
