### Post by Flecko on 2010-01-21
I would say a big thumbs-down for Wiimote. Its a fun novelty, but it doesn't work well as an actual remote as the battery will die quite quickly when your wife or one of your friends forgets to turn it off. (It doesn't automatically go to sleep like it does when connected to the Wii.) Plus, if you have a Wii, you'll always accidentally turn it on and off when you're trying to watch TV.

Big thumbs up for Streamzap USB remote. I've had one for years, and it has always worked well.

Best of luck.
