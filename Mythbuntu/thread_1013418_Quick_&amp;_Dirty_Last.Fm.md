### Post by t3chn0b0y on 2008-12-16
I had a hard time finding much pertaining to last.fm plugins so I
thought I would toss together a quick and dirty means of getting
last.fm to work, at least for my personal use, and I stumbled upon
a script called  [mcf]("http://aur.archlinux.org/packages/mpdmc/mpdmc/mcf"). It requires mpc & mpd to run.  I placed mcf in /usr/local/bin.. and then I proceeded to add menu's to mythtv.. lastfm_main,lastfm_artists,lastfm_friends,lastfm_groups,  and lastfm_tags. Like I said it's quick and dirty but it does the trick, but the downside is there isnt a means of skip,love,ban with the mcf.. :(  but it does work.. :D

Does anyone know of any other means of getting last.fm working in Myth? I know there has been some effort in adding shoutcast, and last.fm to mythmusic but I couldn't wait... :lolflag:
