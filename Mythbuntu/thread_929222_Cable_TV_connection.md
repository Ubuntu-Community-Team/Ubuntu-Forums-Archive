### Post by volkswagner on 2008-09-25
I have Time Warner Cable.  I have a pvr 150 connected directly to CATV, no set top box.  I have a pvr350 connected to cable box via s-vid/rca for audio.  I also have the same cable box (SA 3250HD), connected to my mythbuntu box via firewire.

I can capture basic free channels via the firewire port, including HD.  The protected or any/all channels I can use the pvr350 for recording (no HD via s-vid).  The firewire also changes the channel, for firewire capture and when using svid to the pvr350.

If you don't have firewire on your PC, you can use an ir blaster.  Many IR USB devices have one or two IR-out ports for the blasters.
