mATX with 3 PCIs aren't generally made.

Possibilities:

1) Not a new motherboard --a whole new mATX box, run together with your original one as a secondary backend.

2) Get a bigger box --some ATX and e-ATX come with the number of slots and ports you need. Migrate myth and your tuners onto one of these.
