### Post by nasha on 2008-05-13
That MoBo just isnt quite upto spec for Mythbuntu...
Major issues being CPU and Video (would require a card upgrade), and possibly RAM (I would recommend at least 512)
Of course, if you require HD playback, minimum requirements will not work

```
    * 1.0 GHz x86 or x86_64 Processor
    * 192 MB of system memory (RAM)
    * 2 GB of disk space (Frontend Role)
    * 20 GB of disk space (Backend Role)
    * Graphics card capable of 1024x768 resolution
    * Supported TV Tuner Card (Backend Role)
```

There are plenty of other small form factor options that may be more suited to MythTV on the website you linked to. I would look at the Intel boards as you need a CPU wit x86 architecture

I use Dell Optiplex's with a Video card upgrade for most of my front ends. Reasonably small size, and relatively cheap to pick up, with headroom for HDTV
