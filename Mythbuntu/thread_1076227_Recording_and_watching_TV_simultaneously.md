### Post by NautiusMaximus on 2009-02-21
I have a dual tuner TV card (Hauppauge WinTV Nova T 500), and I can record 2 programmes simultaneously. If I am already recording something, I can watch TV.

So far so good.

But if I am watching TV and a recording is scheduled to start, I get a message saying it's about to record, and I have the option only to cancel the recording or to change channel to the channel that's scheduled to record. I can work around this by stopping watching what I was watching, waiting a couple of minutes, and then choosing "Watch TV" again, and then all is OK, but the workaround obviously means that I am interrupted in the programme I was watching.

Is there a better way of dealing with this?

Thanks
