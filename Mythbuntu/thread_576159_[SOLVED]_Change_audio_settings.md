### Post by antoniuk on 2007-10-14
I solved my own problem (gotta love that!)

In the volume control, I selected show switches atthe bottom and turned of (unchecked) "SB Live Analog/Digital output Jack)

To get here either run xfce4-mixer or add volume to the menu bar panel
