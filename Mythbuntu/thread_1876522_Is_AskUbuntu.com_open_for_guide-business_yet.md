### Post by castrojo on 2011-11-06
You can post a how to, just formulate it in the form of a question. So instead of "Guide to Getting a Foozbar 9000 working with LIRC in 9.10" it would be:

"How do I get a Foozbar 9000 working with LIRC?" and then tag it 9.10, mythbuntu, and probably lirc. Then just post the guide as an answer to the question.

EDIT: if you have a newish account the system might make you wait before answering your question, so if you have a bunch you might want to team up with someone and have them ask the question for you so you can answer it right away or the other way around. (I can help you with this, see my profile for contact info)
