You'll note I didn't say increase or decrease.  I'm actually seeing some of both after upgrading to jaunty.

The performance has improved when moving through my selection list of episodes to watch.  Typically it'd slow down as I went from group to group.  For example, I'd go from mythbusters to mlb with no problem, but then going from the daily show to csi:ny, it'd pause for 3-8 seconds.  Now, it flows from group to group flawlessly.

That's great, but the upgrade also seems to have negatively affected my skip speeds (skip 30 seconds, skip to next flag, and skip 10 minutes).  Each skip is progressively worse (2 second delay, 4 second delay, and 6 second delay ... roughly).

Pause/play and exit all seem to work as before.  Has anyone else noticed this or have any thoughts on resolving?  I tend not to upgrade often, and went from 8.04 to 9.04 direct.  Prior to that, I believe I was 7.04 (not positive on that, though).

My box is a low-power odd-ball config (from what I've seen of other configs :)  It's an Everex gPC, with a Hauppauge 500 and 350.  The 350 offers up SD to my TV, and functions as a recorder.  I don't do any HD recording at all.  It's handled just about everything I've thrown at it, with only the occasional hiccup when trying to record three things at once while watching one and doing a lot of skipping/pausing.
