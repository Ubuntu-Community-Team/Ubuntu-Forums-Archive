### Post by newlinux on 2008-02-10
For you first issue:

Utilities/Setup->Setup>TV Settings->Playback has an option you can turn on to include or exclude liveTV programs by defualt in the Recording Groups screen. Also, when you are in the view Recordings screen, you can press "m" to get to the menu and change the Group filter to not display liveTV recordings.

I only playback ripped isos, so I'm not sure about your second issue, but it does seem odd the menu key isn't working. when you press the "i" key do you get regular OSD? Just making sure it is using the internal player for dvds, which it should by default.
