### Post by Caps18 on 2008-12-31
Does the front end come up? 

Go to setup (the bottom option), then utilities/setup (the bottom option) and then Mythbuntu.  Enter your password and the control center (centre) comes up.  Setup the backend.  If you have schedules direct, it should populate the channels I believe.

Exit out of that and it should fill the database if the computer is on-line.

Look through the other control center options, then exit and the frontend comes back.  Go through the setup option menus there, but don't touch anything that you aren't sure about.  Exit out of that and manage recordings and pick some things to record.  Then watch TV and make sure it is working.

There should be some wiki's or other documentation around on-line someplace.
