### Post by danbodoh on 2008-06-13
I used a PVR-350 for 4 years, and it served me well.  But it didn't support features like running at 1.1X with normal sound (the sound became chipmunk-like).  Getting it running was a pain, but would probably be much easier in MythBuntu.

I've converted to a laptop with S-Video out and an PVR-USB2 tuner.  With the PVR-350 TV-out, contrast/gamma worked out of the box and there was no need to deinterlace.  I've had to play with both on my S-Video system, and there are still artifacts.

On the PVR-350, interacting with X outside of mythtv was a pain, because of the low resolution.  With my laptop, I just switch to the LCD display.  I was never able to get dual-head set up on the PVR-350 combined with the internal VGA.

Overall, I think I prefer the S-Video  path.

Dan Bodoh
