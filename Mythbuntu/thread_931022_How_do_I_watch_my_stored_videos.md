### Post by newlinux on 2008-09-26
install the mythvideo plugin (use mythbuntu control centre)

Go into the setup menu in mythfrontend (get to the video setup) and specify where your videos are located on your on your hard drive (or put the videos in /var/lib/mythtv/videos which is the default location)

Go into video manager and do a scan.

There are lots of other sttings you can do but these are the basics.

You can open up the frontend from the applications menu or by typing mythfrontend in a terminal.
