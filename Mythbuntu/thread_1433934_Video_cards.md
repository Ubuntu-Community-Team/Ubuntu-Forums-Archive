### Post by timconradinc on 2010-03-19
I'm using the Hauppauge HD-PVR to record HD video from a cable box and controlling the cable box via firewire.

If you plan on doing HD, VDPAU is likely the way you want to go. And, to do that, you need to find a card that's on [this list]("http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/VDPAU#Supported_Cards").

NVidia has had pretty good Linux support for quite awhile now. I have a 8600GT and am using a DVI->HDMI connector to display video on my TV.
