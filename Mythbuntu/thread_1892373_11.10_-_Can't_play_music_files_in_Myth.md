### Post by J.H. on 2011-12-24
I managed to solve the problem, but I can say that the answer was not intuitive.

I needed to set the audio output to the surround51:CARD=SB,DEV=0 device, set the speakers to Stereo, and uncheck upmix to 5.1.

If I set the speakers to 5.1 or had the upmix checked it would not play anything. 

Now it plays music files, through all of the speakers.
