### Post by volkswagner on 2008-04-08
I recall seeing jitter until adjusting the playback settings.

This should get you started.  I suggest noting your current settings.  At the top you can select from several preformated groups.  Change to a group you are not using.  I selected a group with two sets of settings and deleted one so I knew when I made changes, that they were the settings in use.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=726571]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=726571")

I was not successful in setting separate groups for HD and SD.  I was happy with my final setting for one controlling both sd and hd.
