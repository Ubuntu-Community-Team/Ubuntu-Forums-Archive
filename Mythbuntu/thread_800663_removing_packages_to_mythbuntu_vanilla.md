### Post by gfk on 2008-05-20
I just use synaptic to do this.  You will find this under your system menu.

I tediously scroll through the things installed and just purge things which I think I wouldn't use like bluetooth related stuff.

I read the descriptions, maybe use it's man pages and would google to decide see if I would need or use a package.
If you don't know how to use the man pages just open a terminal and type "man" then the name of the package.
