### Post by agamotto on 2009-12-09
I discovered something this morning whilst futzing around with Mum's Myythbox.  In the mixer control, there is a switch/option for a source called 'beep.'  Activating this switch and turning it up to ~90% has significantly boosted the sound whilst playing recorded programs and other bits.  It seems that you might have to have BEEP, PCM, and FRONT all displayed and turned on to get good sound...


Hope this helps someone who has been dealing with audio issues!
