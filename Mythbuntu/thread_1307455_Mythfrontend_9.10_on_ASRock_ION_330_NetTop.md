### Post by gacmb5 on 2009-11-19
I have created an installation manual for Mythbuntu 9.10 on an ASRock ION 330 pc. The installation is based on the ASRock combined with 3 external USB tuners from Hauppauge and a Silvershield power switch to save energy. MythTV 0.22 runs great on the ION (0.22 has build in vdpau support for the integrated NVidia graphics).

Some parts of the manual maybe usefull for your situation. As you can see most importend parts (graphics, sound, vdpau, mythtv...) work right o.o.t.b. 

Check this page for the installation steps:
[http://asrockion.wiki.zoho.com/mythbuntu-910-asrock-ion-330.html](http://asrockion.wiki.zoho.com/mythbuntu-910-asrock-ion-330.html)
