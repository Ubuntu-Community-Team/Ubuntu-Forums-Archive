### Post by tohc1 on 2007-12-10
I think the "adding char" bit maybe the channel name- my pvr150 (analogue tv tuner) added all the channels in with a name "adding channel" when I did a scan. Try going into the Myth setup >channels and see what their called. In here you can change the name,callsign, number, and give a xmltv tag which links a channel to guide data.

The video being scrambled - is it scrambled when watching TV as well? It could just be an incorrect channel - mine seems to be very sensitive and adds loads of channels when there should be only 5 (I'm in the UK). Again have a look in the setup and get rid of any channels with the wrong settings.

Hope this helps
