These bugs have been reported, but I think the developers are more interested in the new features they are adding right now than fixing bugs. It may be that the stuff they are messing with affects MythWeb, and they don't feel it is worth their while fixing mythweb until they are done making changes to the data base that affects it.

This is the kind of thing you can expect when you install the stuff that the developers are actively working on before it is released.
