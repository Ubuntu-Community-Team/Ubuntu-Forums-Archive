### Post by superm1 on 2007-10-29
I would be apt to lean towards the TV.  Take a look if the TV has a "Game" mode.  It will remove any possible delays from video playback to audio due to filtering and such.  Many newer tvs have a filter or something similar applied at all times otherwise.
