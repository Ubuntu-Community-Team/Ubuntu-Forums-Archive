### Post by bughound2 on 2013-11-18
Running mythfrontend/mythmusic 0.27 on top of Ubuntu 12.04 LTS with XFCE/xubuntu desktop.

If I leave mythmusic playing a playlist that repeats, the screen goes black and the audio stops playing after about 10 minutes or so, as if a screensaver or power-saving feature has kicked in (DPMS perhaps?).

Don't see any knob to disable this feature in any of the setup options for mythfrontend.

What's the easiest way I can stop this from happening (and not have to take a manual step every time I fire up mythfrontend)?

This seems like a bug, not a feature.


Workaround for Ubuntu 12.04/Xubuntu:

[COLOR=#000000]DPMS (Display Power Management Signaling) can be disabled under Settings-->Settings Manager-->Power Manager; uncheck "Monitor power management control"


[/COLOR]
