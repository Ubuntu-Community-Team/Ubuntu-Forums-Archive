### Post by klc5555 on 2008-06-09
Usually when you start getting spontaneous system reboots, the power supply isn't up for the load being put on it. There can be other physical causes too, of course, like bad memory or the like, but power is the first suspect. Live TV, or worse, live TV plus recording on other channels with multiple tuner cards has to be when the usual myth box is using the most juice.
