### Post by LowSky on 2010-10-04
Sounds like a cool product, but your going to run into a few more snags other than USB 3.0.

First I looked at their site, and it doesn't mention Linux support anywhere. Sure many devices talked about in here don't mention Linux in their work with section, but this one doesn't even show up on the MythTV or V4L websites at all


Also you posted a similar question a few months back, and their advice there was pretty sound
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1520007](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1520007)

You would be better off with a Hauppauge HD-PVR. Sure it doesn't have HDMI, but TV is only shown up to 1080i so component is fine. Plus the HD-PVR is fully supported by the mythtv crowd, while that Blackmagic device is not even on their radar. Which is too bad it looks a lot nicer.


Also USB 3.0 is completely supported in Linux, no matter the distro.
