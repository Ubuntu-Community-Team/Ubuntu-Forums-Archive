### Post by SiHa on 2009-12-16
Try changing the wireless channel that the router uses? Most routers have web-based configuration screens, and hopefully, one of them will allow you to change the channel.

If that doesn't work, or isn't an option, does the wifi have an external antenna? What sort of connector does it have if so? Some I've seen have an F-Type screw thread, same as a satellite connector. You could make an extension cable for the antenna and put it somewhere further away from the microwave.
