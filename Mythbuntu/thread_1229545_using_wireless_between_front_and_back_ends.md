### Post by newlinux on 2009-08-03
CDs and SD tv should be fine unless your wireless G connection has horrible quality. HD mpeg-2 will most likely be unwatchable (lots of stuttering) on most wireless G connections, unless it has really good quality, or is extremely buffered.

### Post by archonic on 2009-08-09
I got by with 802.11g with SD streaming, but when I went to HD I had frequent stuttering.  Going to 802.11n@2.4GHz helped but I still encountered stuttering during peak hours, probably when everyone in the neighborhood was home and using their wireless, electronic devices, etc.  I then made the move to 802.11n@5GHz and have been stutter-free since.
