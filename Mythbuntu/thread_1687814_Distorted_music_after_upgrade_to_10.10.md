### Post by jdk82 on 2011-02-14
NOTE: I'm running 10.04 and upgraded to .24 with the Autobuilds repo enabled, all updates applied. If a mod could change the title I'd appreciate it.

Hi all,

After upgrading to 10.10 all of my music is distorted (crackly, especially at higher frequencies, almost like it's clipping). This includes mp3 and flac audio. Live/recorded TV and movies (analogue and digital) are fine. I have a separate FE/BE, music is shared via nfs. My sound card is a C-Media Electronics Inc CM8738 (rev 10), using a coax digital connection to my receiver. If I play the same music through any other media player (amarok, vlc, mplayer, etc) it sounds fine. On a secondary FE (we'll call it FE2) that uses analogue out music sounds fine. I haven't tried analogue on my FE (although I suppose I should) because it kinda defeats my high fidelity goal. The only errors I see in the FE logs are:

[mp3 @ *] max_analyze_duration reached
[mp3 @ *] overread, skip -5 enddists: -2 -2
[mp3 @ *] Header missing

and similar for flac audio (I'll be happy to include full logs if anyone thinks it may be helpful).

The audio device used is ALSA:iec958:CARD=CMI8768,DEV=0, which matches what other player are using. I've been struggling with this for a while now, especially since there are no good errors for me to google (although I have tried googling for relevant problems). If anyone has any suggestions/ideas I would be really grateful, I'm sick of using amarok for audio (as much as the UI is light-years ahead of myth-music it's a pain with a remote).

Josh
