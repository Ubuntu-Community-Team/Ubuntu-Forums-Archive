### Post by never say never on 2010-08-05
Um, I think you are just getting scheduling data from QAM, each channel can choose to post a few hours of information.  This is normally displayed on a digital TV when the channel is changed.  It is not meant to replace the guide data, or give the data for an extended time period.

You need something like:
[http://www.schedulesdirect.org/](http://www.schedulesdirect.org/)

(This is for the U.S.)  There are other services for other areas.

You also need to make sure your channels are properly identified.
