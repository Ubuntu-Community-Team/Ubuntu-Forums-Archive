MythTV doesn't handle weak signal very well.  I had issues where my 6  yr old STB and my TV (all from the same 4way splitter/amplifier) can  deal with it.

I'd suggest trying to improving the signal first.  Move the antenna to a better spot, i.e. get an 75ohm extension cord and move the antenna outside, or take the PC to a friends house, or get a amplified antenna, etc.  I found my MythTV performance improved drastically when I got a better antenna.

Mythbuntu 10.04
1500GB HDD
