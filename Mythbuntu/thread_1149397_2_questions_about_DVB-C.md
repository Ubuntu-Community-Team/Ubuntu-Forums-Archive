### Post by ian dobson on 2009-05-05
Hi,

I'm using 2 DVB-C cards in my backend:-

1) SATELCO EasyWatch HDTV PCI (DVB-C) TV-Karte
2) DVB-C, KNC ONE

Both cards use the SAA7146 chip and work out of the box.

I'm not sure about the CI question, my cable provider doesn't have any interesting encoded channels (and also it's far too expensive). I'd imagine that you'll need a CI per card as the TV signal is passed through the CI and doesn't touch the PCI bus until it's decoded.
