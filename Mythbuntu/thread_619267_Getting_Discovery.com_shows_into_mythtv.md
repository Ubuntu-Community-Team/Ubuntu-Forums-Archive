Discovery.com is using the Move player, just like ABC.com.  No one has got it to work that I know of (from a quick Google search).

Like any video, you could play the desired video on a Windows machine that has a video card with S-Video or Composite outputs.  Connect those analog outputs to a second machine that can capture video and you can make a recording.

If you want to stay all digital, look into Camtasia's Screen Recorder product.

Once captured, you can bring the content over to your Linux box.
