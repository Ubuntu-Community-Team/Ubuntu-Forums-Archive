Actually....David....would you mind sharing how to ZOOM...period?   My display has about 3"'s of black all the way around.

The easiest is to just keep hitting the w button on the keyboard (or assign it to a button on the remote) until you get the right zoom.  The only down side is that these are the most common (standard) zooms and may not be perfect for your situation.

The other way is to go into the menu (during live tv) and go to video and zoom.  You can zoom manually one step at a time (small increments).
