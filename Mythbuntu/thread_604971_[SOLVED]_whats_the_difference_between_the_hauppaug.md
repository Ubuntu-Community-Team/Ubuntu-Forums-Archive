### Post by tgm4883 on 2007-11-06
The PVR-350 has Video out.  Not a mpeg4 encoder.  The possible problem with your blurryness, is that you need to increase the encoding bitrate (IMO, the default is too low)

Go into setup on the frontend and change that under recording profiles.
