### Post by uteck on 2013-09-09
I assume you want to make a combined front and back-end unit?
You will also need a TV tuner of some sort, the type depends on what your cable or satellite setup is.
Video card is not that special, try to find an Nvidia card that is fan-less or very quite.
CPU is not used much by the system except if you do commercail skip or transcoding of recordings.  Myth will not play Blu-ray, so I assume you will MakeMKV to rip them?
