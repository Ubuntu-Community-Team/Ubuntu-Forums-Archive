### Post by tgm4883 on 2010-08-03
In mythbuntu-log-grabber, after submitting the logs you are given the URL to access them directly. It sounds like you are running the build of mythbuntu-log-grabber that shipped with lucid and broke after pastebin changed their API. You will need to enable the -testing PPA in order to get an updated version of mythbuntu-log-grabber. See [http://www.mythbuntu.org/node/341](http://www.mythbuntu.org/node/341)
