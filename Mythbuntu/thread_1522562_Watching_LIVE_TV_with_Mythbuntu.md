He's talking about Set Top Box from AT&T UVERSE and he can only watch one show at a time per capture card or per STB. You need another STB for the second card since you have two cards a PVR150 and HVR950.
Unless you can link us that site you were talking about so we can see what you  are actually trying to achieve.

I guess he is also complaining about time shift when watching live tv, which is what it's normally suppose to do.  Also if you have one tuner and select watch TV while recording, it will interrupt the recording to fulfill the live TV request.


for the second paragraph of his post, about remote contorl.

GOOD NEWS! YOU CAN!
It's call mythweb.

on your mythbox:
You can enable it by going to mythcontrol center under "system" of the main menu that you get when you right click on desktop. Look in plugins for mythweb and enable it. If it's not there try one of the other groups.

Once enabled, on your computer:
Enter the ip of your mythbox in to your web browser "http://exa.mpl.e86/mythweb"
click on remote then click on your mythbox name, hit refresh since its a bit buggy, it will highlight and have fun. I have this setup for my bedroom, I control it with a cheap netbook. It's pretty awesome.
