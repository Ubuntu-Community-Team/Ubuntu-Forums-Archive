### Post by Meph1st0 on 2008-07-17
Just FYI.  It's not as simple as I had described in the first post.  when I moved, it wasn't sufficient enough to simply change my location on the schedules direct website and then run mythfilldatabase.

I had to basically go into the backend setup and remove my channel list, listings and tuner card (may not have needed to do this but did it anyway).  I then readded the tuner card and setup the schedules direct listing and rescan for available channels in the area.  This allowed it to recatalog all of the available channels and program information.
