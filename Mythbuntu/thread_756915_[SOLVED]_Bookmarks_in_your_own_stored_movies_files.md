### Post by kyfehr on 2008-04-16
newlinux is right.

You can use the bookmark feature of the internal player for your videos, but you need to go to the settings for video files and mark the type of extentions (avi, mpeg, etc) to use the internal player, then all the features you expect with your recordings are applied to your videos.
