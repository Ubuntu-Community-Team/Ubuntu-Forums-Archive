---

### Post by blm-ubunet on 2014-05-09
As long as the recordings are present in one of the storage groups mythtv does not care which folder/drive the recordings are on.
You can move them to re-balance.
You can have some of the folders read only as well.

The 2TB drives are good value & seem to be more reliable than 3TB.
