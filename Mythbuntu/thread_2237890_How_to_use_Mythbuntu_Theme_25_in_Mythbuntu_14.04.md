### Post by one30nav on 2014-08-04
Utmost respect for Thomas Mashos and those that continue to work on the new Mythbuntu theme 27, but I still prefer the legacy, Mythbuntu theme version 25.

Perhaps someone could fork the legacy and keep it relevant. It still works in Mythbuntu 14.04 and I refer to a slightly-edited previous post of Thomas Mashos to help:

[http://www.gossamer-threads.com/lists/engine?do=post_view_flat;post=566131;page=2;sb=post_latest_reply;so=ASC;mh=25;list=mythtv](http://www.gossamer-threads.com/lists/engine?do=post_view_flat;post=566131;page=2;sb=post_latest_reply;so=ASC;mh=25;list=mythtv)

Mythtv 0.25 themes are located here: [ftp://ftp.osuosl.org/pub/mythtv/themes/0.25](ftp://ftp.osuosl.org/pub/mythtv/themes/0.25)

1.) Download 'Mythbuntu-25.30_0.25.zip' [FONT=Verdana][SIZE=2][COLOR=black][FONT=Verdana][SIZE=2][COLOR=black]and extract it to your home directory[/COLOR][/SIZE][/FONT][/COLOR][/SIZE][/FONT]
2.) Rename the folder from 'Mythbuntu' to 'Mythbuntu25'
3.) Edit themeinfo.xml in that folder and find the <name> field. Change 'Mythbuntu' to 'Mythbuntu25'
4.) Move the folder to ~/.mythtv/themes/
5.) Go to theme chooser and select Mythbuntu25

Hope this helps and my intent is not to disrespect or stifle creativity.
