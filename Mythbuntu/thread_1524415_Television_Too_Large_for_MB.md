### Post by goffa72 on 2010-07-05
Sounds like an overscan issue, try adjusting the zoom on your TV, my TV has the following options - 16:9, wide zoom, zoom, 4:3, just scan. The scan or just scan setting usually works.

### Post by nickrout on 2010-07-05
goffa is right, but the ability to do that is dependent on TV make and model. What TV is it?

[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Overscan](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Overscan)

If your TV can't be set right, the latest nvidia drivers have an overscan slider in nvidia-settings.
