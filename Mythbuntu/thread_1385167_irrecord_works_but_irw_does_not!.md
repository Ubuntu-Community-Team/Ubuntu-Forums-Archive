### Post by myth1914 on 2010-01-19
Based on the Myth page here: [http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Hauppauge_WinTV_Nova-T_500_PCI#Known_issues](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Hauppauge_WinTV_Nova-T_500_PCI#Known_issues)

Your input listed in /etc/lirc/hardware.conf should be 
"/dev/input/eventX" where X is variable.

Yours seems to have "/dev/input/irremote" currently
