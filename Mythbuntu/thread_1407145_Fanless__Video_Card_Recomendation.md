I'm not sure if audio over HDMI works on this card but I have my pre-order in for one of these:

[http://www.amazon.co.uk/exec/obidos/ASIN/B002VREZJC/ref=ox_ya_oh_product](http://www.amazon.co.uk/exec/obidos/ASIN/B002VREZJC/ref=ox_ya_oh_product)

There's good thread about the chipset and VDPAU here: [http://www.gossamer-threads.com/lists/mythtv/users/403785](http://www.gossamer-threads.com/lists/mythtv/users/403785)
