### Post by LowSky on 2010-11-05
In ignoring all the "confilicting" channles you may have missed the channels you are looking for.

"Conflicting" can mean a few things. Most importantly it means the channel could have multiple options for instance the same frequency one town over is used for another station. Not knowing UK transmission I can't be certain. But I would rerun the  channel grabber and add those conflicting channels and edit by hand if the need is there
