### Post by LowSky on 2010-08-31
schedulesdirect.org

its $20 a year, after the short free trial. it will provide TV guide data for everywhere imaginable. 

All cable companies need to provide the basic channel lineup in QAM but nothing more... expect the normal basics, like NBC, CBS, ABC, and your local channels but nothing beyond that
