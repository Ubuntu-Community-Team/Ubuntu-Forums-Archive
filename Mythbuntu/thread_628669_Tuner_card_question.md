### Post by uopjohnson on 2007-12-02
The 350 has onboard mpeg decoding and video out.  If you are recording any HD or you have sufficient processing power and a decent video card there is no need for the 350.

### Post by twkisner on 2007-12-03
What is your source going to be (OTA, Cable)?

If it's Over the Air, I don't know why anyone would but a brand new expensive tuner card does not have a digital tuner (even if you don't have a HDTV at least you get a better picture/sound).  Also, you will have to buy a new card in a little over a year anyway...

If it's cable, that OK, but depending on the speed of your box you could go for a cheaper solution.  Maybe I'm just cheap...
