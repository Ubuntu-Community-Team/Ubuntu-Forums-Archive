I have Mythbuntu 8.04 with MythTV .21

I am new to linux and I successfully used myth to play a DVD and rip a DVD, but I cannot get the DVD to play in Myth, it does not show up in the Media Library.

If I go to the "Media Library" then "Watch Videos" it says there are no files. However, I ripped the DVD twice, once as an avi and once as an iso. Both files I can find by navigating to var/lib/mythtv/videos.

### Post by tgm4883 on 2008-06-05
You need to go into setup in the frontend then into the video manager.  This will scan for videos.  You must do this to see new movies.  You could also change it to browseable mode.
