### Post by flecki on 2010-05-01
Hi All,

i was running Mythbuntu 9.10 without any problem on my Asus M3N78-EMH HDMI using a spdif optical output.

I had ALSA_default and AlSA[lots of digits] plus the passthrough activated and enjoyed digital sound on my ONKYO.

After the upgrade i can´t get the system to put out any sound via spdif at all. (I think i tried almost every possible setting in mythtv)

What works is ALSA_HDMI but even with 5.1 activated it will only output stereo via HDMI - passthrough produces scratching noises but when deactivated i get at least stereo.

HDMI Sound never worked before which but i´d love to get my old digital spdif 5.1 sound back.

Any suggestions ?

On the dektop sound menue i can chose a lot of analogue sound modes but digital only in stero and HDMI which might be a hint since there is no SPDIF out option in the menues. I chose HDMI for now which gives me sound on the desktop too(all other modes don´t)

Alsamixer shows Spdif interface activated and i also checked for the light(which is there glowing happily but to no avail)



-SOLVED-

Seems it was a problem with my Onkyo Receiver - all SPDIF inputs were down and i had to physically switch the device off(standby wouldn´t work) and on again

Luckily i have another device with spdif attached so i was able to lacate the problem when this one also didn´t produce any sound.

Might have happeded with the update procedure or not - i will never know - good thing is it works again like it did before
