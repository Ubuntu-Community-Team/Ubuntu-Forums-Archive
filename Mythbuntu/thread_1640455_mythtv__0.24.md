Whether common or not, it's a situation that's easily fixed.

> Second... I see that after the upgrade they say you should rescan for the audio. how do you do this?

There's a button in the frontend set up to scan for audio devices in .24.  It's fairly self-explanatory and pretty easy.

> Last of all... Whats the upside and down side of this upgrade?

thanks

I can't speak for everyone, but here are the major pros and cons for me:
CON:
1. It took a while to figure out how to get everything set up, particularly the audio.

PROS:
1. Now that everything has been set up correctly, it has worked really, really well. In particular, the audio is much better on analog recordings than it was in 10.04. 
2. I like the fact that scanning for metadata (screenshots, descriptions and fan art) of my video files now works automagically. Since I have > 500 video files (movies and TV series), having to set up the metadata myself was unwieldy.
