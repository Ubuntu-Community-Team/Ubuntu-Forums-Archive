### Post by Lepy on 2010-04-27
I have an ASRock K10N78 in a fe/be combo that works pretty well with an AMD 240:
[http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16813157159](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16813157159)

It has lots of usb and sata connections and hdmi output (with audio according to manuf's website) as well as vga. The 8200 handles any content from 1080p on down extremely well too (with vdpau). 

I have experienced only two problems with this board. I can't get suspend or hibernate to work even with the latest bios. I haven't really put too much effort into this though. Also, rarely, some usb ports will stop working with a "task blocked for 120 seconds" error in dmesg. A reboot always fixes the problem, but it seems this might be a kernel issue and not hardware related. Finally, the board isn't full sized, so it is kinda flimsy but this shouldn't be an issue if you don't plan on switching components out all the time.
