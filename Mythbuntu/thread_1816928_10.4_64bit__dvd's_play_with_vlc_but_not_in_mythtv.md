### Post by klc5555 on 2011-08-04
Sometimes Mythtv in Mythbuntu 8.04 and following (when Ubuntu  shifted to all-scsi drive labelling) has had trouble finding the dvd drive device if it is set to the default /dev/dvd instead of /dev/sr0. 

If your DVD device is set to /dev/dvd in mythtv's setup, try changing it to /dev/sr0 and see whether it helps.
