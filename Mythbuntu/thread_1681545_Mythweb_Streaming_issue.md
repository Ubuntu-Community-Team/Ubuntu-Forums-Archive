### Post by nhtrader on 2011-02-09
I have to admit I haven't experienced your problem. But I did experience stuttering / slow performance. I had a problem streaming on the local network and the remedy for me was to make a few changes with the wiring.

I have this arrangement:   cable modem -> 4 port wireless router -> 8 port switch -> (wired) PCs

I was able to eliminate the switch and performance improved dramatically. I could now stream HD content without stuttering: cable modem -> 4 port wireless router  -> (wired) PCs

Haven't tried streaming over internet yet.
