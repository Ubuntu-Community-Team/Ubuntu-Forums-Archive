### Post by Hayesio on 2008-12-07
I think your system will run mythbuntu fine - over kill if anything.  I would check the compatibility of you graphics card though (I've had plenty issues in the past with ATI cards). To watch and record tv at the same time you will need either 2 tuners or 1 dual tuner. The Dvico dual digital 4 is a brilliant dual tuner if your willing to pay the money. And it comes with a good remote control. Otherwise check [HTML]http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/DVB-T_Devices[/HTML] for device compatibility.  There shouldn't be any problem running 1080p res as long as you card can do it.
