[http://iguanaworks.net/product1.psp](http://iguanaworks.net/product1.psp)

Obviously, the wired emitters can be moved around more and shoved closer to the IR receiver on any STB I may have.  But, maybe you don't usually need this flexibility, and the wires just get in the way so you're better off just getting LED's fixed on the side of the transceiver?

### Post by Levander on 2008-03-02
Well, I emailed Iguanaworks.  They recommended to get a hybrid one.  E.g., a transceiver with one LED and one jack for a wired emitter.  Then, as an option to buy one wired emitter... It doesn't increase shipping costs to buy a wired emitter...

It sounded weird at first, but I think that's what I'm gonna do.
