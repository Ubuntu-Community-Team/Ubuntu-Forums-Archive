### Post by superm1 on 2007-11-07
Inherently these are all 16:9 resolutions.  What you want to do is override the behavior of particular video formats it sounds like.  So if you have say a 480x480 video, you might want it to stretch.  Does this sound accurate?

If so, go into the appearance settings, and you can modify this behavior on the page that comes after the page for customizing the resolution of myth.
