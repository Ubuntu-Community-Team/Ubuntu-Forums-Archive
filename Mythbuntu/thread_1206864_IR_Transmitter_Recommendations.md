### Post by crez79 on 2009-07-07
Depending on the model cable box you get, you can also use [firewire to change the channel]("http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/FireWire") and even use it as a tuner to record channels that aren't encrypted.  I had this set up for a while and it worked great. 

As far as ir blasters, I had a standard Microsoft media center receiver and blaster for a little while and it wasn't too hard to set up from what I remember. For me, it wasn't 100% reliable and missed changing the channel every once in a while.
