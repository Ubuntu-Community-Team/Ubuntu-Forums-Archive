### Post by volkswagner on 2008-04-03
S-video can't display HD.  PVR150 only captures SD.  What other inputs are avail. on your plasma?  You will need to capture QAM with a capable card, such as k-world 110 or other.  You may investigate firewire capture via a supported cable box.
