### Post by Flecko on 2011-11-07
I know mostly people come here to post about problems, workarounds, or solutions, which I why I'm usually here. But I wanted to post a big thank you to all of the developers of Mythbuntu (and MythTV for that matter) and all the great members of the forum.

Recently, my 2 year old Mybuntu install went kaput as it's hard drive was failing. I hesitantly decided to try the latest version of Mythbuntu (and I only say hesitantly because I had a lot of teething problems with my DVR and 11.04.) To my great suprise, the install went nearly perfectly. I only had to use a few small tweaks to get it up and running. Definitely much easier than any past version of Mythbuntu. And on the rare occasions that the frontend crashes, no more diving into a cabinet to get my wireless keyboard out to restart it...*it just works!*

So to everyone that had a hand in 11.10, I want to say thank you as a long time user!
Cheers,
-Flecko
