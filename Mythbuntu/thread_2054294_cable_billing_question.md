### Post by larsolav on 2012-09-07
If you are going to use this with MythTV, remember that "HDHomeRun PRIME supports streaming access-controlled **_copy-freely channels _**to other DVR applications including MythTV. "
[http://www.silicondust.com/products/hdhomerun/prime/](http://www.silicondust.com/products/hdhomerun/prime/)

I think most of the cable channels that are not over-the air channels will not be "copy-freely channels". So your NBC, CBS, PBS, Fox affiliates cable channels will be copy-freely, but your HBO, ESPN etc will most likely not, and will not work on MythTV. For those channels you will have to use Windows I believe...

Someone, please correct me if I am wrong.
