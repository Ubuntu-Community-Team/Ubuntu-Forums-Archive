### Post by Trollslayer on 2008-07-02
For satellite you need power passing attenuators (to allow the DC power for the LNB through).
Toner Cable ([http://www.tonercable.co.uk](http://www.tonercable.co.uk)) do their own SFAM ones. the FAMs are for cable/tv and would produce the same effect.
If you ring around a few places and specify power passing attenuators for stallite other places should be able to sort you out.
