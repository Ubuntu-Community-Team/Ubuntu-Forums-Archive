### Post by beartard on 2011-07-12
While watching live tv on my Mythbuntu box, if I use the up/down arrows to enter browse mode, all the channels show "1" as their channel number.  Also, the browse bar shows up not (as I would expect) one channel above or below the current channel, but at the beginning or end of my channel list.  Just for fun, I went into the channel editor and it showed normal channels up to 999, at which point everything was showing "1" as the channel.  The guide seems to show the correct information, however.

My computer is a combo frontend/backend using 11.04 with an HD-PVR hooked to a Scientific Atlanta cable box using firewire for channel changes.  Mythbuntu is up-to-date as of today.

Any ideas what could be causing such weird behavior?


Edit: Channels showing up as "1" was actually a theme problem.  The theme I was using (blue abstract) would only show the first digit of the channel number in browse mode.  The annoying problem persists where browse mode begins at channel 2 for up keypress and at the end of my channel list for down keypress rather than +/- one channel from current.
