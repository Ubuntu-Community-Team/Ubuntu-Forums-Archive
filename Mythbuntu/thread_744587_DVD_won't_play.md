### Post by dsbw on 2008-04-04
To elaborate: 
   a) Open Mythbuntu Control Centre
   b) Click on Proprietary Codecs
   c) Click on Enable Medibuntu Propietary Codec Support
   d) Click Apply
   e) Wait for installation to finish
   f) Click on Enable libdvdcss2 (and I'd also do w32codecs and ffmpeg)
   g) Click apply
