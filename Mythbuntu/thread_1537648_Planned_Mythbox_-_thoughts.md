### Post by oldsoundguy on 2010-07-24
Just make sure that not only the tuner card works, but that Ubuntu (Myth) also supports the IR control.  I made the mistake of spending some cash on a card that works just fine, but there are no drivers for the IR!! (one of those duhhhh moments!)

Have fun! .. And remember that setting up a Myth box (mine was to be a PVR) is a bit more work than just setting up a computer!  In other words, a challenge. 
You will have to learn another language (terminology).
