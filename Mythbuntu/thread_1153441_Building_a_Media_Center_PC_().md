### Post by newlinux on 2009-05-08
search around the mythbuntu forum and the hardware thread for ideas on hardware. You probably want to get a VDPAU capable video card or IGP, and mythtv is a good DVR/media center. I use myth and xbmc.

There's a ton of information out there about mythtv. Read up and let us know if you have questions.
