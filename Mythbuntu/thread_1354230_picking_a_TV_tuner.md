Does your HDTV have a QAM tuner? I am also in San Jose, and my tuner gets a bunch of clear QAM channels. You only need a comcast cable box if you wish to get encrypted channels. That would be any HDTV beyond the free over the air channels, or anything beyond extended basic.
This may change in the future, but this is how it is now.
Note that the channel numbers that a clear QAM tuner will get have no relation to the channel numbers comcast claims for the stations. Comcast uses a proprietary channel mapping scheme to encourage users to rent cable boxes.
