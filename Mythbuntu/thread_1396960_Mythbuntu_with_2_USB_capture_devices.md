### Post by Slacker3k on 2010-02-05
You don't mention the type of signal that you are trying to tune, but there are several options.  Hauppauge does make some low profile tuner cards, though I think they only have a single tuner.

I do use two HD-PVRs in a single machine and they work just fine.  The HD-PVRs are recording HD video on a machine that has 4 other internal tuners, so they are sort of a worst case scenario when it comes to bandwidth, so you should be fine with multiple USB tuners of any type.
