### Post by hineas on 2010-09-27

When I try to watch/record anything on that channel the video is full of static and you can barely make out what is going on.  However, the audio is usually very clear.  Channels 62 and 64 work fine.

My first thought was that I had a bad cable or loose connection.  That all checked out fine.  I can watch channel 63 on my TV and it comes in clear, but when I use MythTV it is full of static. Using the Hauppauge software and drivers in Windows, I can watch channel 63 and it works perfectly. I have also tried fine-tuning my channel 63 in Myth, but I have had no success in doing that either.  I was wondering if anybody has another suggestion for me to try?

I am using Mythbuntu 10.04 (installed about 1 month ago)
My tuner card is the Hauppauge HVR-1950 (USB)
I am using the pvrusb2 drivers
I have analog cable-tv.

If you want to know the rest of my hardware or setup, let me know.  If you need any log messages, I can post them as well...  I would love to get this since my favorite show is on channel 63!
