### Post by nickrout on 2012-07-02
> **AlecJ said:**

That would be the safest way, but you'll use horrendous bandwidth and either your ISP or your hotel will charge you appropriately (even assuming you have the bandwidth at all).

You can also access mythweb and use the flash player for recorded programmes, or in 0.25 use the HTTP Live Streaming feature. Both will use much less bandwidth, but will require a bit of CPU power in the backend to do the transcoding.
