### Post by pipedream on 2008-06-24
Does anyone have any experience with storage groups?

I have read the following:
[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Storage_Groups](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Storage_Groups)

However, I am not sure how they truly work and am looking for some guidance.  For example I have my first SATA HD which has mythbuntu installed on it and I also have mounted three other SATAs.  

When I go into the Information center in myth it tells me I only have the storage indicated in the first HD.  In the setup I added the three other HDs to the default storage directory in the backend setup.  It has write access and no errors.  How do I tell mythtv to use the other three HDs?

Thanks!

---

### Post by nasha on 2008-06-24
You need to mount your HDD's to a directory location, then add that directory to the storage group in MythTV Setup.
That is all that is required for storage groups to operate.

Awesome!

It worked.  I think I am going to like Storage Groups.

Also, I've learned that you can move files among your hard drives as long as they are part of the Storage Group MythTV will find them.
