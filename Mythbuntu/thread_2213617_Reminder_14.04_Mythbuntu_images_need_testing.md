The final release for 14.04 happens in a couple weeks, and will require testers to help ensure we have good images! But no need to wait! Grab a daily image and run through the tests now to find bugs and issues.

Go help your favorite flavor of ubuntu by testing today, and respond to the calls for testing in a couple weeks for final verification. Here's to a great trusty release!
