### Post by volkswagner on 2008-11-16
I don't know of any cards with a coax output.  Does your dvd player have additional inputs.  Perhaps you can use it as a "switch".  Have all your inputs go to the DVD player and use the SCART out.

Provided your graphics card has svid out and your dvd player has svid in you could do the following.

Split tv signal to tv card and dvd player.  This would allow you to watch live tv (channel changing would be via the DVD player) without the mythbox.

Then svid out of the vid card to the DVD player.

Of course this all relies on you DVD player being capable.

Or you will need an RF modulator to go from S-vid on your video card to Coax.
