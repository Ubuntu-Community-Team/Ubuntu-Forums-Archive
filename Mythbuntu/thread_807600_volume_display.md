### Post by tgm4883 on 2008-05-26
The OSD is under
Utilities/Setup > Setup > TV Settings > Playback OSD

Since you upgraded, you might want to install some more themes.  Open up mythbuntu-control-centre and you can install from the mythtv themes tab.  Anything ending with OSD is the OSD theme.
