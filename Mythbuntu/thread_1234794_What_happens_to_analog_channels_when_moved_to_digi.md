### Post by agamotto on 2009-08-08
You should be able to plug the digital tvs in and get both the unscrambled digital and analog channels without much trouble.  

My cable provider, Medicom, is moving channels into digital and replacing them on analog with lovely things like more shopping and religious channels.  This would be fine, if the religious channels weren't ALL Christian.  I won't bother opining on the shopping channels.

With this change, Medicom is providing the MAIN digital OTA feeds unscrambled on the analog system, but mysteriously, all of the TV guide info is missing.  I plugged an antenna into my Hauppauge 1800 card, and all of the sub-channels popped up, including the TV guide info.

I still use my pvr-150 card for the analog cable, as the analog port on the 1800 doesn't reliably work yet.

Interstingly, on both digital tellys, I am getting about 40 unscrambled digital channels in the 51-58 range.  These weren't announced, and when flipping around in them, no TV guide info shows up.
