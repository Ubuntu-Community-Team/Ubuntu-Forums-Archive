### Post by klc5555 on 2011-04-19
The myth transcoding daemon became very problematical with storage groups around about mythtv 0.23.something, and it has been "temporarily" removed in 0.24. Probably never to return.

Your best bet is to use one or another 3rd party ripper. handbrake may be a good choice, depending on your specific needs. Vobcopy is also, I think, a recommended possibility.
