### Post by volkswagner on 2008-03-14
My laptop works well for frontend.  54g wireless works well for SD TV/Video, not HD though.

Important to have good graphics support.  Mine is ATI200m which is not able to dish out eye candy.  It works well for SD content.  I don't use it as a backend.

The following tuner is for SD only.  It is supposed to work with linux.  I have seen some have troubles in these forums.  It is in the choices for tuners in myth.

Get it before mar31 for the eighty dollar rebate (or before they run out).  I just got one.  I have not had time to set it up though.

[http://www.newegg.com/product/product.aspx?item=N82E16814144504]("http://www.newegg.com/product/product.aspx?item=N82E16814144504")


For HD there is the HD home run.  I think you will need a wired network connection.  May even support a crossover cable direct to LAN port on laptop.

Running the live CD is a good start to see how well your hardware is supported.

Good luck!
