### Post by msebast on 2008-04-23
I have the Gigabyte 780G GA-MA78GM-S2H. 
See some of my posts in this thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=716451&highlight=780g](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=716451&highlight=780g)

I'm not sure nvidia's driver support is a great reason to go with them.
There seems to be a trend lately of nvidia's drivers having more problems while amd's have been improving. Also AMD/ati has opened the specs of their video cards and has been doing a lot to support and encourage open source drivers. Nvidia continues to ignore Nouveau like they wish it would go away.

There is great potential for really good open source amd/ati drivers to become stable in the next 6-12 months. That was why I chose amd/ati over nvidia. I didn't get Intel because MBs with nice intel IGP chipsets were much more expensive and less featurefull then the board I got.

Read [http://www.phoronix.com](http://www.phoronix.com) for more.
