The PVR350 is a great card.  If you already own it fine.  If you have a powerful pc and intend on using an hdmi out vid card, a pvr150 will be just fine.  The difference is the pvr350 can output directly, not using the video card, via composite or svideo.  If you don't need this option you can save money on the pvr150.
> 
Can mythtv forward information from the capture card to the video card at the same time as I am using the video capture card to record a program? (most likely on another channel)

Not sure what you mean here.  you can capture only one channel at a time.  One per tuner, unless you have a dual tuner.  The pvr150/250/350 are single tuners vs pvr500 dual tuners.  You can record a program while watching a previously recorded program.
