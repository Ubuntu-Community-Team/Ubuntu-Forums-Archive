### Post by oscarsfriend on 2008-04-01
> **max69 said:**
> Mobo
Abit AN-M2HD AMD AM2
[http://www.overclockers.co.uk/showproduct.php?prodid=MB-137-AB](http://www.overclockers.co.uk/showproduct.php?prodid=MB-137-AB)
£58

I have one of these, and it generally works very well under Linux.  However, I don't use hdmi, and I've read reports of people unable to get hdmi audio working under linux with this mobo -- though that was some months ago now.  So you might want to check this out before you buy.  Also, the GPU can get fairly hot with heavy use -- but I have an ATX case, with plenty of room for air to circulate, and a couple of fans.  [EDIT: having said that, looking at the picture on overclockers, it looks like abit have may fitted a better heatsink on the GPU now.]

EDIT: re hdmi audio not working: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux-source-2.6.22/+bug/148097](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux-source-2.6.22/+bug/148097)
