### Post by khPWXxF on 2015-01-12
There is no right answer to this question - it all depends on your sense of adventure vs domestic harmony.
 
A developer might want to update daily, but then they thrive on the instability of the bleeding edge and are capable of fixing it quickly when it goes wrong.
 
Others for whom stability and waf /harmony are very important will treat the box like an appliance.  They will consider that once stable then all updates should be frozen.  They consider that waf and updates don’t go hand in hand.

If (like me) you fall into the latter category then you can disable updates in
Applications > system > software updater > (let it scan but don't update) > Settings > automatically check = never
 
Happy household!

Phil
