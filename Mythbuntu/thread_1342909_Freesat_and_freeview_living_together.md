### Post by Singing Dwarf on 2009-12-01
I have both Freeview and Freesat sitting quite happily on the one be/fe unit (Mythbuntu 9.10) and have previously done the same on a Gentoo installation until the last month or so.

The secret is to ensure that the callsigns and station names for the channels are the same where they appear on both Freeview and Freesat.  I also renumbered the channels too, in order to ensure that the Freesat numbers correspond with the Freeview numbering where they differed.

With the ability to record multiple channels from the same multiplex, I can record 8-streams of TV at the same time from a single Freeview and a single Freesat card - not that there is that much worth recording simultaneously!
