### Post by ian dobson on 2008-08-03
Hi,

What tuners are your PVR 500 using? Usually they have Philips or Samsung, and the philips ones have a better picture quality.

I have 2 DVB-C cards and a PVR-500 in my backend with the cabling going to the first DVB-C card, then from the DVB-C card to the next then from the DVB-C out on the second card to the PVR-500. Note the DVB-C cards have an onboard amp.

If you can try using a shorter or longer cable to strengthen/weaken the signal.

Regards
Ian Dobson

### Post by ian dobson on 2008-08-04
Hi,

1) Can you try with only one card attached to you cable.
2) Can you try using a shorter cable.
3) Can you try using a longer cable.
4) Can you describe your cable wiring in abit more detail (Cable lengths, splitters etc)
 
From what I've experianced PVR-500 are very sensitive to signal strength. If the signal is too strong or too weak you get a really crap picture. Maybe you need to use an external RF amp with 2 outputs that go directly to the PVR's.

Hope this helps abit
Regards
Ian Dobson
