### Post by bcgrown on 2010-01-17
My cable provider has a whole bunch of pay-per-view channels that usually I don't use, but that I still want to appear in the listings so that I can easily switch to them if I do order PPV.

This sometimes causes MythTV to try recording from pay-per-view channels, which obviously won't work since I haven't ordered the show.

I added them all to a channel group called PPV, now how can I make sure Myth doesn't record from that group unless I specifically tell it to?

### Post by myth1914 on 2010-01-19
If you remove them from your programming, you will still be able to tune to them, they just wont show in your on screen guide.  I don't know that it's the solution your looking for, but the only other option I can think of is when setting your recordings to specify "on this channel only" rather than the broad "any time, any channel"
