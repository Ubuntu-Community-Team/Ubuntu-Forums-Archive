### Post by PhilWig on 2013-05-13
Yes.  eg:
80 followed by right arrow will skip forward by 80 minutes.
80 then I will jump to the point 80 mins from start of recording which is what I think you are asking for.
See [http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/MythTV-HOWTO_-_0.26](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/MythTV-HOWTO_-_0.26)
