The latest w_scan is from Nov 2014. I had to build from source.
Not sure I would trust the versions shipped by some distributions to be complete or up to date.

Buy a cheap signal meter.
"femon -H" is very good for monitoring signal level once you have tuner locked on to a multiplex.

The whole UK had been through an extended period of transmitter frequency restacking/shuffling?

Could your tuning expts show the device does not like changing between T & T2 modes?

### Post by blm-ubunet on 2015-11-13
You did say in the first post you had doubts about cable.
I have a patch cable (to be replaced) that cause picture macroblocking when bumped during vacuum cleaning.

DVB-T2 has much higher symbol rate efficiency by having reduced pilot carriers etc. Maybe this makes the T2 signal detection & locking slower or more easily disrupted.

Incorrectly connected RF cables can cause very weird symptoms.
That 4" USB cable could act like a 1/4 wave trap at about 525MHz.

The USB tuner stick design concept looks to be quite a challenge to physical robustness & electrically meet input sensitivity & RFi/EMI & ESD..
