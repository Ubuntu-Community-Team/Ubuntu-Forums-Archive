### Post by laffinet on 2008-11-26
There's a little application called Album Cover Art Downloader.

It's got a few bugs, but works pretty well. I downloaded and tagged all my collection, not everything worked automatically but was still okay.

You can download it [here]("http://www.unrealvoodoo.org/hiteck/projects/albumart/") or [here]("http://www.kde-apps.org/content/show.php/Album+Cover+Art+Downloader?content=10069") plus some documentation too.
