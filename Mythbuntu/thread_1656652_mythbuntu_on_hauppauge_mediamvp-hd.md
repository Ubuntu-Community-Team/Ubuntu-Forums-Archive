The MediaMVP-HD is an embedded product which has a nonstandard processor.  Although it technically runs Linux, the Linux variant is highly customized for the hardware (for example, it fits within 64MB of flash).

That said, it will not run any form of Ubuntu distribution.

Devin Heitmueller
(who also happens to be a Hauppauge software developer working on the MediaMVP-HD, and also happens to be the person who told tech support to tell you the subtitles are not supported).
