### Post by ian dobson on 2008-11-19
Hi,

I picked up a 2Gb IDE flash drive for about 10Euro on ebay and installed the normal mythbuntu frontend (version 8.04) without any problems.

I've now gone over to a 8GB CF card in a SATA to CF adapter (also from ebay) and have a 100% silent frontend.

Before anyone says anything about the life of CF/Flash cards and writes I have a small linux Pentium 1 system running an old kernel that's been running for over a year now. I didn't do anything special for the configuration so the /var/log/log files is written very often.
