### Post by jabeez on 2008-04-22
Kinda hard to say. Does it do anything at all when you turn it on? Beeps? I think most modern motherboards will give some kind of output even with a fried CPU, usually a series of beeps. I would guess it's maybe a bad motherboard or possibly power supply. Try unplugging all drives/cards from the motherboard leaving only the ram and cpu and see what happens.
