### Post by ZedThou on 2009-11-05
I really struggled with this, in the end it was due to the proper frontend setting for maximum channels being unintuitive. If set at 5.1, I cannot get passthrough, but set to Stereo it works, and the receiver reports a 5.1 stream. So the frontend settings that worked for me were 'ALSA:spdif' output device, AC3 and DTS passthrough enabled, PCM mixer, and maximum channels is Stereo.

I haven't messed with the default Mythbuntu ALSA configuration at all, no custom configuration files or anything.
