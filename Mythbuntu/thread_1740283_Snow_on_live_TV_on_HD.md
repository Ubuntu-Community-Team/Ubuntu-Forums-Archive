First check whether it's the recordings or the playbacks. The livetv files for what you watch persist for a day --play a section of one that you noticed was troublesome with an external player. Xine or mplayer or VLC. If the flaws are still there, then it was the recording of livetv that was introducing them, not the playback.

If it's in the recording, do scheduled recordings (i.e., ones that you watch sometime after the show is over) display the same problem? Or does, say, the B/E value that displays in the livetv OSD at the bottom of the screen when you first tune a channel show a low bitrate error like 0-6,000-ish or a high one like, say, 32,000 (at which point there will be artifacting and audio errors). Do you use a drop amp? Do you actually need one (signal could be too strong as well as too weak). How's your cabling going into the machine? ... Lots of stuff to troubleshoot.

If it's in the playback ... well, cabling is always the first suspect here, too. But one thing at a time.
