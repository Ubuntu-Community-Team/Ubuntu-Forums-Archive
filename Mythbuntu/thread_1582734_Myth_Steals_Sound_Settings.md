### Post by LowSky on 2010-09-27
Bug or feature... Well its kinda both.

Mythtv is supposed to act as its own GUI. So it takes over command of the sound settings and anything running on the system that could interfere with the payback of videos.

I do see it as an issue if you run a computer with multiple screens or multiple X-sessions, but I think it is something most users have no complaint about.
