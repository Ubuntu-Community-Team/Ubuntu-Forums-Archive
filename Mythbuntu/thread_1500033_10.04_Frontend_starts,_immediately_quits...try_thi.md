### Post by LarryJ2 on 2010-06-02
If your  10.04 frontend starts (perhaps the blue screen typical of the old style theme shows briefly)  and then the front end immediately dumps to the xfce desktop,  then check this:

If your backend's time zone is set to say "Denver"  and  your frontend time zone is set to "Boise",  you will experience this frontend startup problem.   Despite the fact that  Boise, USA  and   Denver, USA are both in the same time zone.  Mythbuntu doesn't recognise this.

Fix this by setting your FE time zone  to the same identical one as your backend  by using the frontend  desktop  "time and date" menu selection.
