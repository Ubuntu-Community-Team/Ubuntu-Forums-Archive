### Post by newlinux on 2008-02-18
You aren't going to be able to directly tune dishnetwork or direcTV in the U.S. You'll need a set top box, and you can capture the output of it (VCR style). Capturing component is expensive and has other drawbacks (size of uncompressed stream). So you'll be stuck with SDTV and HDTV downconverted to SDTV through composite or S-video. U.S. satellite companies have their content locked down...
