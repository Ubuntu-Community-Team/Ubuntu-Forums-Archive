### Post by 4dognight on 2010-01-18
I recently did a fresh install of mythbuntu 9.10 . I used to run 8.04, and had 2 displays. :0.0 was my desktop, and :0.1 is my LCD TV. I had it set up that Myth would start always on the TV, allowing my desktop available on the first display.

After I switched to 9.10, I kept my same xorg.conf file. Myth still starts up on the second display, but I have just a black desktop on the first.

After fiddling around for hours, I came up with a workaround. 
I exit mythtv. I then right click to get the menu. I go to the settings menu, then panel. I bring it up(on the second display). At this point I click on the first display (in the panel settings window), and then my panel at the top of the first display appears!

I must also mention that I am unable to bring up the menu via a right click in the first display. This is why I have to shut down mythtv on the second display to get at the desktop.

And of course this happens everytime after a reboot.
