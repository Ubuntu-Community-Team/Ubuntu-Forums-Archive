### Post by justanotherhack on 2008-04-28
I built it as a media central. Playing music and videos to my TV as I like, without the need to turn on the workstation (yes, the workstation is not running 24/7 ;) ). Sometimes I let it record shows or movies, but not that often, since they mostly show crap on tv ;) .
