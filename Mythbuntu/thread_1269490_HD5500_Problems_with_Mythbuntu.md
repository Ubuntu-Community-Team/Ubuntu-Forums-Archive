### Post by williammanda on 2009-09-18
Your problem sounds like the problem I have when I first scan for channels, I have comcast and the don't supply any channel info.
After scaning in the channels I open mythtv and select live tv. As soon as a channel comes up I hit S, this will bring up the guide. Next I select a channel from the guide and wait no more than 15 seconds for the channel to come up. If it doesn't I select S and another channel and I write down that channel for deletion. This way you are in more of control while trying to view the nameless channels. Once you get a channel that works, you can use zap2it tv guide to determine what channel it is (move the mouse over the channel icon for the xmltv info), press E and enter the channel info. After you go through the channels and determine good and bad ones, you can open mythtv backend and delete the unwanted channels.
