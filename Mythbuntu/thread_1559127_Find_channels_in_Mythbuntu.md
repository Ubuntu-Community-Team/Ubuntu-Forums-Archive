### Post by xykevinr on 2010-08-23
Freeview (dvb-t) and freesat (dvb-s) both have 7 day channel guides send with the signal. If you' re starting out I'd suggest just using that. It's called EIT. Turn it on - it works. 

You can also get a free guide online from radio times - ie myth regularly gets the guide automatically. This takes more setting up.
