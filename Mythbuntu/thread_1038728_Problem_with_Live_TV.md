---

### Post by tgm4883 on 2009-01-13
Sounds like you are either hitting record, or your filter is set to also show live tv recordings.

---

### Post by kingjos on 2009-01-13
Where do I go to edit the filter?

---

### Post by ahood on 2009-01-14
Try

Home/Main --> Utilities/Setup --> Setup --> TV Settings --> Playback --> Recording Groups (5/9)

Look for the option 'Look for LiveTV recordings...'

I hope this helps.

Al
