### Post by nickrout on 2008-08-18
This forum is not about how to defeat encryption on satellite broadcasts, which is what I assume you are doing with your smartcard reader (also from your reference to pay per view). Please don't ask questions about how to defeat your provider's encryption (if thats what you are asking).

DVB-S cards are well supported in mythtv, if they are supported in linux. [http://linuxtv.org](http://linuxtv.org) has a DVB wiki that has a lot of info about what is supported.

I don't know about your card, but if it is a DVB-S2 card then it may not work as yet. Most of them don't seem to, but as I say linuxtv.org wiki will have more info.
