### Post by matt06 on 2009-12-22
As far as I know, the digital and analog inputs on the card are limited to digital and analog signals respectively.  That being said, I've never personally tried to feed both inputs an analog or digital signal to see what it picks up.

I believe what you are wanting to do would require a "hybrid" digital/analog input on the card that could tune and record either digital or analog signals on the same input.  The HVR-1600 is a "dual tuner" card, but the tuners themselves are not hybrid.  Someone please correct me if I am wrong.
