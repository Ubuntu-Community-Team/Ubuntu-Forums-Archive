### Post by xinix on 2010-04-04
You can change the encoding so that the recording is at a lower bit rate and will fit on the dvd.  After you have added the recording select it in the list and press 'm' and then select 'Change Encoding Profile'.  Unless you go with a really low bit rate you shouldn't see much of a difference.
