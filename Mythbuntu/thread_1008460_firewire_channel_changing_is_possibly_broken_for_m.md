### Post by majoridiot on 2008-12-11
a recent firmware updates to at least some motorola STBs has broken channel changing in certain situations.
if you are suddenly experiencing issues changing channels on your **motorola** STB, try forcing channel
changer 7 (-f 7) and see if you get any love from that.

more info can be found here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=6350515](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=6350515)

note that this issue does *not* affect mythprime *unless you are priming on a 3-digit channel*.
