### Post by LowSky on 2010-08-21
I'm a bit partial to the HVR-2250 it digital picture has never let me down, analog support is finally on its way, even if you really don't need it anymore. But the newer versions still have issues with the included remote not working. I have the older model that had a USB remote so I dont feel that pain. Other than those flaws I think its a great card. 

The DViCO FusionHDTV7 seems to work right out of the box which is great according to MythTV documentation.

I have noticed that most poor reviews on Newegg for TV tuners are from people who think these things will "just work" using the supplied software and older OSes like Win XP. They don't take into consideration things like proper cabling/antennas and the fact that the newer OSes can handle TV tuners much better, and that the included software is always going to be bad.

Either way you go good luck.
