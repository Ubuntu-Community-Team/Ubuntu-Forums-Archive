### Post by OldGaf on 2010-01-09
I accessed mythweb with my smart phone the other day and was happy to see the page format changed to a "lite" format to fit my phone.

The problem was that it "stuck" that way even when I logged on with a normal PC, and there was no menu / option to switch it back. 

I even removed / purged mythweb and reinstalled but it did not fix it.

Finally I found a post on another forum that said to add:
> ?RESET_TMPL=true
to the end of the URL..... problem solved!

There really should be an option on the lite template to change it back to normal.
