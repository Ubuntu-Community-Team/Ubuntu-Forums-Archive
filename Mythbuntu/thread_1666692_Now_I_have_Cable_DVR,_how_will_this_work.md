### Post by thatguruguy on 2011-01-14
It works for me, but YMMV.  Streaming an mpg stream (which is essentially what digital cable is) is a lot less hardware-intensive than decoding an analog stream.  However, the actual ***display*** of the HD stream is much more cpu intensive. Given your setup, you shouldn't have any problem with that, though.
