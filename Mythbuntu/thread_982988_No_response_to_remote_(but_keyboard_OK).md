I have this same issue and it's not due to batteries -- the remote becomes unresponsive during playback of recordings or live TV..

Constantly tapping the buttons sometimes gets it to respond (there seem to be two kinds of lag) -- otherwise if you press pause... maybe about 20-60 seconds later (it varies) it will actually pause... Same goes for BACK (ESC,) FW, or any other key.  I can VNC into my TV and press the key on my remotely connected keyboard for instant response.

This problem has increased in the later lirc/kernel/mythtv 0.21 builds.

Rebooting the machine can sometimes resolve the issue, but not always... or often times it returns.

I've tried giving lirc a -10 nice, but maybe I'm not doing that correctly. 

It just seems like the machine doesn't give any priority to listening to the remote commands, which you would think is the opposite of the desired functionality.
