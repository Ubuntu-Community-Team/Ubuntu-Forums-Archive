### Post by gilson585 on 2010-11-22
I have noticed the same thing and don't entirely believe it's a defect or bug. Some shows are in Dolby Digital 5.1 and are not mixed properly. Idk why but it was just playing out of the left and right but no center, I think it was the broadcasters fault. But I myself haven't seen that too often and only occurs every once in a great while. You should be fine on build 27299 and up. It could be your config and ALSA:default will not do.
When you first arrive at audio settings you should hit "Scan for devices"
Then select AlSA:iec958 as you have done
check DD and DTS boxes if your receiver supports them
Check the upmix to 5.1 box
use upmix quality Best if your PC can handle it "most will"
advanced config is not necessary here yet
play a video/livetv and it should work, if not make sure it's not muted by launching alsamixer from the cmd line
last but not least you can try to use other digital devices by overriding them under adv config
also be sure your reciever's audio mode is setup right as well lol

This has worked fine for me ever since I upgraded to 0.24
I am using optical out from my mainboard to my Sony 5.1 reciever and havent had any issues, I haven't needed to try overriding my device either it just worked.
