### Post by aelfric55 on 2016-01-20
The Mythtv wiki offers a couple of possibilities for this [https://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Sound_Troubleshooting](https://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Sound_Troubleshooting) One fellow over at Gossamer Threads solved a very similar-sounding problem by setting the "Stereo PCM Only" flag in the advanced audio settings: [http://www.gossamer-threads.com/lists/mythtv/users/579614](http://www.gossamer-threads.com/lists/mythtv/users/579614)
