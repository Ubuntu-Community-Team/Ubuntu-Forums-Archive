### Post by newlinux on 2008-12-01
You can find the meaning of each of them by looking at the details. Those are just example profiles that you can use or modify, and of course you can create your own as well. For more specifics on each of the example profiles provided:

[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Playback_profiles#Default_profiles](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Playback_profiles#Default_profiles)

I use a variation of normal and CPU++ on most of my machines. But this all dependent on your hardware, drivers, and what you view.
