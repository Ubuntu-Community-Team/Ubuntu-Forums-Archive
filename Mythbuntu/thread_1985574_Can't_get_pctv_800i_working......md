### Post by klc5555 on 2012-05-23
In addition to getting the driver and firmware loaded in the OS, did you successfully configure the Mythtv backend after the install to add your tuner card to Mythtv, scan for available channels, etc. as per  [http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/User_Manual:Index](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/User_Manual:Index) ?

Also, once you get this thing going, a single 40 gig drive won't get you very far --one hour of SD TV will give you a 1 gig file; one hour of HD TV will give you 6.6 gigs.
