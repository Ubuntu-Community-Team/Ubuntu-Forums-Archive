### Post by larsolav on 2009-12-10
No need to go into videomanager in 0.22.
From [http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/MythVideo](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/MythVideo) >   Scanning for Videos

[B]When you add new videos to your storage directories, you can trigger a scan from any MythVideo view by choosing MENU->Scan For Changes.
[/B]
In versions of MythTV prior to .22, it was necessary to enter the Video Manager screen to initiate a scan. This is no longer the case.
