### Post by -Thomas- on 2009-11-14
Just scroll down on the start page, there's a link saying "Reset template and skin to default". This will redirect you to the standard MythWeb template.  If you want to switch back to the iPhone skin goto the MythWeb settings and choose "Current MythWeb Template" -> "iPod" and "Current MythWeb Skin" -> "iPod-default".
