### Post by nickrout on 2011-06-20
The hdmi audio thing probably needs a asoundrc setting the default audio device to the hdmi device.

---

### Post by rokrep69 on 2011-06-23
Your suggestion worked, thank you.

I created an  ~/.asoundrc like this:


pcm.!default {
type hw
card 0
device 7
}

I now have hdmi sound in mozilla/flash and videos in mythtv.
