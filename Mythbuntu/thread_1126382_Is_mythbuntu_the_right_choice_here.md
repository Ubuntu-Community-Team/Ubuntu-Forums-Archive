I think the problem may be that their VCR is about to be broken: June 12th cometh. A converter box will allow the VCR to still record, but it won't be able to change its own channels any more.

That said, Myth ('buntu or otherwise) is not a Black Box appliance that can just be set up, flicked on, and walked away from for months. And an OTA/no-internet environment strips away most of the software's real power. There must be a not-terribly-expensive commercial DVR out there that will do the simple things required of it, while still operating like a maintenance-free Black Box appliance of the sort that DVD-players and VCRs are.

A myth machine here would likely be very frustrating for user and "tech support" alike. 

Just my $0.02
