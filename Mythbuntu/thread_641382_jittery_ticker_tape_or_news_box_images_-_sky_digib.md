### Post by tohc1 on 2008-01-01
I think I read somewhere that problems with scrolling ticker tapes etc are usually due to scaling issues (i.e. your video resolution compared to the screen resolution) and displaying interlaced signals on a progressive display like a lcd tv. 

Have you tried enabling the deinterlacing in the setups?
