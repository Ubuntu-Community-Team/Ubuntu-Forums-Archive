 In my Opinion right now on the market there is only one board for HTPC setups and that is the Asus P5N7A-VM. It works with dvi, vga, hdmi, spdif, etc.. The chips are a little more pricey but You can easily overclock a 7300 to 3GHz and get 1080p .mkv, .ts playback very well. If you only want 1080i then a 5200 will do as well and now you are in the same price point as the amd stuff and the BONUS is you get a Nvidia 9300 video card with the P5N7A-VM on board. The cpu of choice for XBMC users seems to be the 8400.
