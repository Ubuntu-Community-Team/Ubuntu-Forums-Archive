### Post by SoNick on 2009-01-21
It's "I" using the default setup; most lirc configurations that I've seen map that to the "Info" button on the remote.

### Post by SoNick on 2009-01-21
Going to "Utilities/Setup" and then "Edit Keys" lets you edit the keybindings, but I can't seem to find any entries under it for Info other than the one under the Global setting.
Needless to say, this will affect everything that uses the "Info" key.

If you're looking for how to set a button on your remote to that key, then take a look at [this](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/LIRC#LIRCRC).
