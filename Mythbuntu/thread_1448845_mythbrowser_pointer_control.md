### Post by iamlindoro on 2010-04-07
It's always frustrating when ignorance turns into opinions.  

If you want to emulate mouse actions in mythbrowser, bind the MOUSEUP, MOUSEDOWN, MOUSELEFT, MOUSERIGHT, and MOUSELEFTBUTTON actions to whatever you want.  By default, these are bound to 2, 8, 4, 6, and 5.  You may need to toggle input to the browser first with F1 (TOGGLEINPUT, also bindable).
