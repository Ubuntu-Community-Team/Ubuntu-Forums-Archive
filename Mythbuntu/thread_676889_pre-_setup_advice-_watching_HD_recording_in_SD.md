### Post by larsolav on 2008-01-24
You can watch recorded HD content on a SD TV. No need to transcode or anything. On the frontend conneced to the SD TV just make sure the output resolution to the SD TV is set for SD (this may be the default setting when enabling your video driver for TV out). At least it was when I just recently installed Mythbuntu on a combined frontend/backend box with AirStar-HD5000 tuner cards. I think your video card should take care of the TV out signals.
