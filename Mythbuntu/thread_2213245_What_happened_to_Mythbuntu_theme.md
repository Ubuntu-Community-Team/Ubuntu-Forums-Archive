### Post by nsk7even on 2014-03-25
Hi all,

I really liked the Mythbuntu theme ... until the last two updates.
Since the previous update the beautiful background pattern is gone and was replaced with ugly grey color. Additionally the colored dots are gone which told me recording states. I understand that bright white coloured text means active recording now and grey coloured text means inactive recording - but come on, this is hard to distinguish and therefore costs more time and concentration! I wear glasses and sometimes the sun is shining!!

Now I just updated to Mythbuntu theme 28.4 and things got even worse! The font size is as big as line height now which looks really distorted.

For me the previously beautiful and simple theme is completely broken now. I just am trying other ones, but I would love to see Mythbuntu theme fixed!

Is there any better place where I can get in touch with the theme developers for helping them get the theme fixed?
