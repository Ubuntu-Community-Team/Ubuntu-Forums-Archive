### Post by superm1 on 2008-05-05
Hi,

CD based upgrades can only happen if you have an alternate CD to upgrade from.

Your best bet is to just use update-manager.  If your internet flakes out, all the stuff downloaded is still there, just pick the update process back up.  Everything downloads before anything installs.
