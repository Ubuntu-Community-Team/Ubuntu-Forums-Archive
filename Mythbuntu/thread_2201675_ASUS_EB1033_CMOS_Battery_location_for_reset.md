### Post by one30nav on 2014-01-25
Folks, This little PC makes an excellent Mythtv frontend. Just wanted to share some info that I COULD NOT FIND anywhere: Lord forbid you dork up the BIOS giving you a blank screen, you do not have to send it back to ASUS to reset. The CMOS battery is UNDERNEATH the board. Open the top of the case; removing four screws on the board will allow you to pop open the BACK panel of the case. Remove the CMOS battery for 1-2 minutes. Once re-installed, the PC will bootup with default settings. Share!
