Most anything you record will have this and it usually isn't a big deal since when you play it back on a TV there is overscan (image is bigger than the actual TV screen so it is effectively off the screen), so it is never seen by anyone.  If you are seeing it on a TV then you can adjust for overscan a number of different ways (search).  If you're viewing on a computer or 'windowed' then your only options are to deal with it or, as previously suggested, to crop it off.
