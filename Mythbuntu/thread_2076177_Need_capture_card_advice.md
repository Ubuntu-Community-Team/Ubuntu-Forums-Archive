### Post by dannyboy79 on 2012-10-25
Time Warner Cable suuposedly switched to Digital as well BUT they are still providing the analog cable stations over the coax cable line. In fact, I only pay them for internet service and since they didn't come and install a inline filter, I still get like 99 analog channels. I have a PVR-350 and a PVR-500 and it still works.

So are you sure that your cable company completely removed the analog tv signal from your coax line?

I can't suggest a digital tv tuner with a remote but I know when TWC finally get rid of analog stations, I will be getting a HD Homerun, it's my understanding it can pick up clear digital QAM stations from the line.

As far as a remote, I would look into a USB wireless remote, not an IR one. that way you don't have to point it directly at the reviever to get it to work. Like the old ATI Remote Wonder (+)

Good luck
