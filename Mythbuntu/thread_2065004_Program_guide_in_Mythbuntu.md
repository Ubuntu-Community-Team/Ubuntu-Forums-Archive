### Post by chuckrp on 2012-10-07
Your digital channels include the TV programming in the signals. Analog channels do not. If you are getting your show OTA go thru your backend setup again and make sure you are selecting "bcast" for the programming. It is easy to miss. I had the same problem when I first set up my Mythtv box. It is working fine now. You may have to go thru the setup several times to get it right. It will work with the right settings.
