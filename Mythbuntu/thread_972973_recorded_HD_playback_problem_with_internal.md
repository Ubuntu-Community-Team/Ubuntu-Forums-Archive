### Post by newlinux on 2008-11-06
You have to use the internal player for liveTV and recording playback. Changing the default video player only affects mythvideo. You probably need to change your playback profile settings. What hardware are you using and what are your playback profile settings? Also, it might be useful to see your frontend logs from when you playback recordings.
