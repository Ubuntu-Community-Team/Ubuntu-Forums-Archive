### Post by sisco311 on 2011-09-16
No. It's against the IMDB [terms]("http://www.imdb.com/help/show_article?conditions") of service.

MythTV used to support fetching movie details from IMDB. Now that feature has been removed due legal reasons.

We don't support circumventing IMDB terms and conditions here, so we can't help with that.

Check out themoviedb.org 

It's a free and open movie database. It's completely user driven and it has a powerful API, also used by many popular media centers like Moovida, XBMC, Plex, MythTV and MediaPortal.

### Post by thatguruguy on 2011-09-19
In the Videos menu, press "M" to bring up the menu.  Then select "Scan for Changes".  The rest should be automatic, although it can occasionally take some time.
