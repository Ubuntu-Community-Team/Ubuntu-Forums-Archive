### Post by gfowler on 2008-08-02
> **strick1226 said:**
> OK, an update:

The remote works so long as I don't configure an IR blaster to control my Scientific Atlanta STB.  If I add the IR blaster in the Mythbuntu Control Centre then the remote stops working pretty quickly.

Go into your /etc/lirc/lircd.conf.  You will see one entry for your remote and one for your IR Blaster.  Using the MCC will put the remote first and IR Blaster second.  Reverse them, i.e. put the include for the IR Blaster first and then the include for the remote.  

It should work.

Restart LIRC: sudo /etc/init.d/lirc restart or reboot!
