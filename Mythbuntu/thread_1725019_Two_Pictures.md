### Post by LowSky on 2011-04-10
on the frontend

Utilities/setup > Setup > TV Settings > Playback

On the third page (playback profiles) change the  "Current Video Plackpack profile, until you find one that just works. Note options with VDPAU  in the Title only work for Nvidia cards of the 8xxx series or higher.
