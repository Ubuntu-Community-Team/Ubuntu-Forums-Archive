### Post by ahood on 2008-12-11
I use mythstream, but mainly for video. The videos I download can be deleted by navigating to the 'downloads' tab, which is the last tab off to the right.   I use the right arrow button on the remote or the right arrow key on the keyboard to navigate to the downloads tab. I then highlight one of the downloads in the list and press the 'e' key on the keyboard, which will bring up a small menu that includes an option to delete.

I hope this helps.
