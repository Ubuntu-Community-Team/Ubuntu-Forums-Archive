### Post by williammanda on 2009-12-11
The remote you setup will be for that frontend and it will change the channels on that frontend. Your myth system whether you have one frontend or 30 will operate independently on each frontend (if you have 30 tuners, you will be able to handle 30 frontends with 30 different remotes). Now with that said, you will need enough tuners in the backends to allow for each frontend to view channels at the same time.
