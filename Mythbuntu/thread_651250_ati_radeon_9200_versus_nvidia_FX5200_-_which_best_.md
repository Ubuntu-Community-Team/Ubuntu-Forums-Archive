The nvidia cards are a little easier to configure for tv out and support opengl vsync, whereas the ati cards take a little extra effort.  I'd keep the fx5200 in your primary box, and drop the 9200 in the secondary.  If the 9200 doesn't work out too well for you, move to a new card.
