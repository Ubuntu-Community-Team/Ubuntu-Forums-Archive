### Post by Legace on 2009-05-01
There is not difference with Intel or AMD. Take the one that suits you best..

For a graphics card, nVidia has the best drivers (= less Terminal), but ATi's drivers are catching up little by little.. As of now, I'd suggest a nVidia card.

For your DVB cards, refer to this list: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MythTV_Feisty_hardware_list](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MythTV_Feisty_hardware_list)
