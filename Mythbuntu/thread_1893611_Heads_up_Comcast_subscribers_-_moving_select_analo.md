### Post by klc5555 on 2011-12-10
This is news? For most of us this happened over two years ago.

They should also have given you (at least) a DTA appropriate for whatever your current cable subscription is. Which means that if you wish to continue using your old pvr-150 you can pipe the DTA's output into the card, and configure lirc with a blaster to do the channel changing on the DTA.

A nuisance to be sure, but it reclaims a lot of channel frequency space for Comcast to use for higher-quality digital.
