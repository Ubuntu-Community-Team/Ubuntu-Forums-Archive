### Post by slighted on 2007-12-02
A Webmin install will not harm your mythbuntu box, although an edit to your config files from Webmin may cause ill effects. I would suggest making a backup of you originals before committing any changes. For ssh access I would recommend using [**_putty_**]("http://the.earth.li/~sgtatham/putty/latest/x86/putty.exe"), if your behind an M$ windows box that is.
