### Post by foxbuntu on 2008-04-15
Well everyone, the 8.04 (Hardy Heron) Development Cycle is nearing its end. We have made some great strides in improving the Mythbuntu release and on popular demand, we have provided a new set of artwork and a new overall look. We have also created a new theme for our website (Set to be released 3//16/2008) alongside the release candidate of Mythbuntu 8.04. When release of the website hits and you have seen the screenshots or use the new themes yourself, please leave feedback. We are always looking for constructive feedback to help improve our distro.
