pchdtv500 is a half-height card. 

NTSC enccoding is software-based, but has finally begun to work quite well with kernels above 2.6.26.

NTSC over-the-air may be dead, but the analog half of the 5500 does work perfectly well getting the analog output from a DTA or STB (though I tend to prefer a junker Hauppauge 1600 for that --but it's not half-height). The 5500 will handle the analog channels from the cable companies that still offer them, if that's what you need, as well as whatever clear QAM (usually the locals and a few others) that will be freely avaiable on your cable connection.
