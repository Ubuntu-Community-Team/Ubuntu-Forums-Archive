### Post by nrune on 2009-01-14
I recently downgraded from 64 bit to 32 bit os, mostly to run boxee.  I have to say that I am running one remote frontend and one master backend frontend combination and I have to say that the 32 bit os is much better in terms of load and speed than the 64bit os. according to top I am seeing loads of .32 .38 recording from two sources and live tv on two frontends.  The 64bit os had loads of 1.3 1.5 and more. I am not sure why the big difference, but I won't be going back to the 64bit build. 

Any one else finding themselves more and more in boxee and less and less in mythtv?

Mike
