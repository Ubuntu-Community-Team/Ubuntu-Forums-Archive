### Post by klc5555 on 2009-09-15
The place to start is alsamixer. Your audio was likely autodetected at installation, but the mixer tends to be muted and the volume zeroed by default.

For more serious issues, the place to start reading is here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SoundTroubleshooting](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SoundTroubleshooting)
