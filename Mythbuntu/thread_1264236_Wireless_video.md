### Post by williammanda on 2009-09-11
Ok I decided to do a HD wireless test. I have the following equipment:

Linksys wrt350n 2.4Ghz
Linksys wrt54g 2.4Ghz 
Linksys wmp300n  I used this post for the driver: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=7200515&postcount=2](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=7200515&postcount=2)
C2D 6400 1G ram

I setup the wrt350n as a wireless N only and the wrt54g as a wireless G only (this was based on the dual N band of Linksys here [http://www.linksysbycisco.com/US/en/products/WRT610N](http://www.linksysbycisco.com/US/en/products/WRT610N)). I cascaded the wrt54g onto the wrt350n, the wrt350n is the router. I tested a digital video and it was good so I upped the stakes and tested a HD video and it also was good (HD NFL football game).
I also tried to upload a video while the HD video was playing (using another computer on wireless G) and the HD video had problems. I right clicked on the network icon in the upper panel and I was getting 34Mbs. This explains any problems I may have had during the HD video. Another user expressed that he had great video performance using 802.11n at 5 Ghz. 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=7759404&postcount=5](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=7759404&postcount=5)
At this point I can only tell you what I got with what equipment I had. It seems that more could be had. If you are using wireless, please post your results.
