### Post by tamoneya on 2008-08-19
Looks like a good setup but just so you know I think that with that card you will only be able to record one analog and one digital at a time.  Not two analogs or two digitals.  This isnt a problem as long as you are aware of it and okay with this limitation.  

As for the sound you can get a 3.5 mm(computer) to stereo RCA connector(TV).  I used on in my setup.  It really depends on what your TV has.  If you can tell us your TV model we can let you know the best way.

Other than that the setup looks pretty good.  The only change I would make is I would try to use something better than svideo.  Ideally I would use a HDMI/DVI converter since they are pin for pin almost identical except that they have different connectors and that HDMI can carry sound as well as video.  Again if you tell us the model of your TV we can recommend the best setup.
