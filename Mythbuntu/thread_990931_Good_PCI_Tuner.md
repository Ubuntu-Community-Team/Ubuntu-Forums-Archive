### Post by SiHa on 2008-11-24
I've seen some people report problems, but I've found the Nova-T 500 works for me with minimal tinkering. I'm in the UK too.

The only thing I needed to do was enable the onboard LNA as detailed here:
[http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_WinTV-NOVA-T-500](http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_WinTV-NOVA-T-500).

Apart from that I couldn't get repeat keypresses, but that's only a minor niggle that I overcame by using a homebrew IR receiver.

It's been rock-solid as far as I can tell for the last 3-4 months. I haven't got auto-shutdown working yet, so my backend runs 24/7 and has been up for a max of 20 days without any problems from the card.
