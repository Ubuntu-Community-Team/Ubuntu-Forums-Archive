### Post by Billsputters on 2009-03-25
I am in the process of building a MythTV box using a Hauppauge PVR150 as the video source. Living in Uganda, the only TV we get is via sat care of DSTV. So the only option is to come PAL out from the STB and into the PVR150. No problems.

I've come back to the UK for a short break, and am building all this whilst here. Things in the UK have moved on a bit since I was last here. Freesat is available. Looking at the cards available some have on board MPEG encoders, but they are only linked to the analogue inputs. To me this implies that the Freesat channels DVb-S comes through the box as a MPEG stream from the receiver card. From there it can either be recorded to disc or displayed. Am I right in this or barking up the wrong tree.
