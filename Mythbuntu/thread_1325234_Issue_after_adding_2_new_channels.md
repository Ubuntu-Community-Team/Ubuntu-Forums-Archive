### Post by SiHa on 2009-11-13
Sounds like these two channels are from a different video source than the others? Somewhere in the vast cavern that is the Mythtv setup pages (perhaps TV playback settings?) there is a checkbox called something like 'Browse channels from all sources'.
I installed a DVB-S card for BBC-HD only, and had to select this. Otherwise, Myth will only allow you to browse between channels on the same video source.

In my case I could watch BBC-HD, and only that or I could watch everything but. With this box checked, I can browse through all  channels from LiveTV.
