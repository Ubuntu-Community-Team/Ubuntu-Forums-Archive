### Post by Sonthun on 2008-10-20
Hi.  I've had Mythbuntu take over my desktop after a power failure.  I've been running a regular Ubuntu installation with myth installed.  For some reason after a power failure the Mythbuntu desktop came up and got rid of all of my display settings.  How do I undo this and go back to the traditional Ubuntu desktop?

Thanks.

---

### Post by laga on 2008-10-22
Open the Mythbuntu control centre and deselect Automatic login. You can also disable the artwork there.

---

### Post by Sonthun on 2008-10-25
Thanks for the input.  I just figured out what was going on.  For some reason the xfe desktop became the default.  All I had to do was log out and log back in with the gnome desktop.  I didn't even know I had the xfe desktop installed!
