### Post by Caps18 on 2009-03-31
The way I do it is from the mythTV program, go to setup - utilites until you get to Mythbuntu.  Then the control program window comes up.  Click one of the icons until there is a MythTV back end setup.  Make sure your channels are right in there.  But when you exit, it will run and update mythfilldatabase.  Exit out of the control panel after it updates and you are back in MythTV.  (It sometimes needs a restart at this point.)
