Hi.

I am running Mythbuntu 8.10 AMD64 with all current updates.  I had to install the drivers manually using the instructions found at [http://mythtv.org/pipermail/mythtv-users/2008-August/229796.html](http://mythtv.org/pipermail/mythtv-users/2008-August/229796.html) After that, the tuner started working perfectly. However, my DViCO Fusion HDTV7 Dual Express card works fine until I receive updates.  Every time I get an update for Mythbuntu it breaks the drivers for my tuner.  The tuner seems active but it says "NO SIGNAL" on every station that I know I get.

To remedy the problem, I have to reinstall the driver, reboot the system, redetect the tuners in MythTV backend setup and then the tuner starts working again. It is such a pain that I have disabled updates for my system.
