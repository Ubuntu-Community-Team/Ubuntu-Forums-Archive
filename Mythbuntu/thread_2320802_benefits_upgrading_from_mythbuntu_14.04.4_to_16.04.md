### Post by yonkiman on 2016-04-18
I upgraded because my 14.04 Intel video drivers were a little wonky and I wanted the latest kernel for the latest zfs support (didn't realize 14.04 was going to get all the new goodies you listed).  Also I've hacked (dull machete-style, not stealth ninja-style) around a lot with the system and thought it would be good to start over with a clean setup.  If you've got a stable 14.04 / 0.28 system that you're happy with, I can't think of a compelling reason, particularly if the system's primarily for MythTV.  The upgrade hasn't been trouble-free, either.  Getting the heart of myth 0.28 working and the database restored wasn't too bad, but it's taken me days to get mythweb working correctly (almost there - will post details here soon).  Would be interested if anyone has a more compelling reason for 16.04.
