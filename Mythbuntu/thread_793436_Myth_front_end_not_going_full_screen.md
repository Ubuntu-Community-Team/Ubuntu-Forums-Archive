I figured out the cause.  In an effort to eliminate the Overscan of my TV I Adjusted the settings under:

Utilities/Setup -> Setup -> Screen Setup Wizard

However, when I adjusted it down/right suddenly the menu bar appeared on the top and it readjusted the whole screen farther down (below the menu bar) and to the right.  So basically I have no way to correct overscan :(
