### Post by SiHa on 2010-01-18
If you use the proprietary nVidia drivers, and connect via TV-Out (Composite, S-Video) then there should be an overscan slider in the 
nvidia-settings utility. Otherwise, AFAIK it's not possible.

If your TV has a VGA port, you shouldn't get overscan if you connect via this.
