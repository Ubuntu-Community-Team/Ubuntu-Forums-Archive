### Post by rhpot1991 on 2008-03-18
Here are some links that might help some:
[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Mythfrontend](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Mythfrontend)
[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Mythbackend](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Mythbackend)

Basically you need both.  They can be in separate boxes or run on the same box.  The backend should include tuner(s), storage devices and so on.  While the frontend should include a video card that can display on your TV, a sound card, some sort of IR receiver for your remote, etc.  I have 2 boxes which run both backends and frontends, most people who run them separately do this so that they can hide away a hideous box into a closet or something and have a small little HTPC box sitting by their TV.  You can also do things like have multiple frontends connect to a single backend or have multiple backends which work with each other.  It all depends on what kind of hardware you have and what you want to do.
