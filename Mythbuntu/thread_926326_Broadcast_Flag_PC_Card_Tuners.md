### Post by seeker5528 on 2008-09-21
If you are plugging directly into the cable or getting the OTA broadcasts, I don't think the broadcast flag is an issue.

Not sure what you get if you pull from the cable output of a cable or satellite box.

There are lots of posts in the Myth TV mailing list about using firewire with cable boxes, results seem to be a mixed bag.

If you are looking for something that provides HDMI in to the computer, the available options seem expensive and HDCP seems to be built into all of them, and you end up with uncompressed video, so not so good for recording TV programming.

If you have a cable or satellite box that provides component out the Hauppauge HD PVR seems like a good option, I have not seen anything that would indicate any HDCP kind of stuff is an issue over component video connections, there is potential that the cable or satellite box might output a reduced quality on the component interface. Don't know what the status of the HD PVR driver is, less maturity in the driver could be an issue.

Personally I use an HD Homerun hooked directly to the cable, happy with the HD Homerun, no so thrilled with the quality of the feed from Comcast.
