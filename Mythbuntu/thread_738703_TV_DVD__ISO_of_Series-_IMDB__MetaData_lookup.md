### Post by foxbuntu on 2008-04-02
In the frontend:

Utilities/Setting > Video Manager

-Select the file/folder of the video (file if its just an ISO or Folder if you ripped it to VOB's) 
-Press the Right Arrow or Info Key on your remote/keyboard
-Edit MetaData or Search IMDB/Enter IMDB number
-Myth Will download MetaData
-Enjoy yor fancy new DVD Covers :)
