### Post by JohnLW on 2011-11-10
TV HD video shows vertical lines and occasionally a doubled image. 
Configuration:
- Myth front end V0.24.1-80-g1de0431
- ATI graphics card driver 8.88.7 (proprietary ATI/AMD)
- TV source HDhomerun

What does work:
- identical configuration worked perfectly in 10.04, broke after replacing with 11.10
- can see the image (corrupted) on all the channels, along with audio
- viewing TV channels with a known SD source sometimes displays properly
- piping HDhomerun directly to VLC on the same machine works perfectly
- viewing HD videos on the same machine from a remote file using MythTV works perfectly

So there is no issue with:
- HDhomerun (works fine outside of mythTV)
- internet bandwidth (same as above)
- video driver (same as above)
- video driver with mythTV for videos (videos look fine)

It seems to be something associated with how mythTV handles the TV source being streamed to it from HDhomerun, but I'm at a loss on how to explore that.

I expect that I can dump 11.10 and shift back to 11.04, but would prefer to work it out.

Any thoughts on how to chase this down?

---

### Post by JohnLW on 2011-11-10
Go to Utilities/Setup > Setup > TV Settings > Playback and set profile to "High Quality".
