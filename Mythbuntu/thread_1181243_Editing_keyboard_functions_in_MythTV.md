### Post by Bloodrule on 2009-06-07
How can I change the default jump ahead of 10 mins for the Page Down key when watching recordings in MythTV?  I am familiar with MythWeb and can see how I can reassign the jump ahead to a key of my choosing, but not how I can alter the default of 10 minutes.

---

### Post by crez79 on 2009-06-07
Utlities/setup -> setup -> Tv settings -> Playback groups  There choose your playback group, probably default, and it is in there.

Alternately, you can use mythweb http://<serverip>/mythweb/settings/tv/playgroup and change the jump from 0 which is default, to whatever you want. I am not sure if it is in seconds or minutes, but a little playing will answer that..

Cheers.
