### Post by utar on 2009-04-15
I have personally just added the Western Digital Caviar Green WD10EADS to my Myth box as an additional data drive.  I choose this because it is nice and quiet, compared to some drives that can make a bit of a racket when seeking.

Unless you want to record multiple HD programmes simultaneously the speed of the HDD is basically irrelevant as all drives are plenty fast enough for a media PC.
