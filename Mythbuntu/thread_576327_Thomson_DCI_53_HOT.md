### Post by superm1 on 2007-10-15
When using a cable box, you need to hook it up to the composite or svideo input ports of the tuner card.  When you do so, you don't use the tuner functions of the card, but its always locked on that input instead.

You also need an IR blaster, serial control, or firewire control method for changing channels on the cable box.
