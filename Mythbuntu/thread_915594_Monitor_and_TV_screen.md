### Post by mwcrowley on 2008-09-11
In Mythfrontend there are settings for this, I think it's under 'Settings>Setup>Appearance' and then the second step in under 'Screen Settings'.  The line where is says 'Display on Screen'.  I'm guessing your monitor is screen 0, and your tv is screen 1.   It took me a while to dig this one up, the documentation isn't exactly complete, but for the price you can't beat it.
