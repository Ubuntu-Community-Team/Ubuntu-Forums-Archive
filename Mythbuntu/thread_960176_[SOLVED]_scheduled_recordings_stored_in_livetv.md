### Post by uopjohnson on 2008-10-27
When you schedule a recording there is a field in there to put it in a default group.  I think there is also a place to put a default group on a tuner in mythtv-setup.  I would look for the solution in one of those two places. 
I have no idea how to move a recording from a group.  You might have to go to the DB to do that.  You could also poke around in the context menu that comes up when you select a recording and press the right arrow key.
