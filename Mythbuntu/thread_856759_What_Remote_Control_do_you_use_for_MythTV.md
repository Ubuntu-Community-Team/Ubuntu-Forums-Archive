### Post by DutchLoki on 2008-07-13
Yes the M$ MCE remote is good I use it to, works out of the box and is cheap.But there are also a lot of good  bluetooth or radio frequency (RF) remotes for MythTV. This way you can put your system out of sight. 

In general you can just look for remotes that are compatible with Lirc, than it should work fine with MythTV either.

### Post by nickrout on 2008-07-13
I think that once you find the ones that are compatible, the ergonomics are the key. The more buttons the better, there are a lot of functions in MythTV.

But on the other hand, the buttons have to be big enough and spaced out enough for my eyes. The buttons on my Philips DVD player's remote are impossible to see in a semi-darkened room. 

The MS remote seems about right, I am sure there would be a lot more functionality with more buttons, but I know where all the ones I use are by touch so it works for me.
