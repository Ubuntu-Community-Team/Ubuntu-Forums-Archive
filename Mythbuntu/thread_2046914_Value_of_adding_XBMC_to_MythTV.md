### Post by klc5555 on 2012-08-24
Also I find the unofficial XBMC Hulu addon considerably superior to the annoying Huludesktop, which I've since removed from all my mythtv frontends. I also like the Freecable addon, and use several of the other video and music addons.

In fact, I've found that I like XBMC sufficiently that nowadays I use Mythtv proper only as a DVR and Live TV app. Everything else gets funneled through XBMC.
