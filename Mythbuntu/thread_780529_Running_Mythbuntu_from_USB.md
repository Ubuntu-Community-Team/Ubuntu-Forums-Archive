### Post by superm1 on 2008-05-04
A few options:

1) If you've got a flash drive that has smart wear leveling, you can just install directly to the USB stick

2) If you want to use the combination stick / CD, you can do so for just a frontend.  Settings can be saved to the stick

3) You can copy the contents of the CD to the stick and use "persistent" mode.  Ther eis a bug with this in Hardy though and you will have to re-roll your initramfs to make it work.
