That's intended. MythTV is suppose to be used with a keyboard (or even better, with a remote). There's a setting somewhere that'll enable the mouse cursor, though. 

For general nagivation, just use the arrow keys, tab and return. 'm' and 'i' (menu/information) are also helpful.
