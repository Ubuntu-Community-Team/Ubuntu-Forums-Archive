### Post by hackmeister on 2009-09-02
It all depends on your cable provider. Digital capture cards will let you record channels "in the clear" or un-encrypted without a cable box. Most cable providers are moving to encrypting everything except the standard network channels (NBC, ABC, CBS, PBS, CW, Fox). I subscribe to RCN and went from about 30 open HD channels down to 6 literally overnight. I still use my QAM card to record shows on those open channels but will be moving to a Hauppauge PVR-1212 box as my primary capture device once MythTV 0.22 is released. From the wiki:
[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Hauppauge_HD-PVR](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Hauppauge_HD-PVR)

FYI, QAM cards will also let you record OTA digital signals. You just need a digital antenna hooked up to it.
