### Post by aflores3 on 2010-01-15
drhiii,
I am far from an expert. After all, my box has only been up for about 4 months. 

I have an HD Homerun. I purchased it as my first tuner. It works great for unencrypted HD streams. That means you'll get NBC, ABC, CBS. However, the HD Homerun box will not pick up encrypted HD channels like History HD, ESPN HD, Disney HD. (I'm naming these channels assuming that you are in the U.S. as I have no experience with other countries)

If you are fine with just the 'basic' channels in HD, then I would recommend the HomeRun. If you're looking for ALL HD channels, you might want to take a look at the HD-PVR. Keep in mind that for all HD channels, you'll need your cable companies STB and a means to control it with your backend.

Hope this helps.
