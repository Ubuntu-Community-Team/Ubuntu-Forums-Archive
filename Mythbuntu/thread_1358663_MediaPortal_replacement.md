### Post by nickrout on 2009-12-19
Your machine should do all that as you can enable vdpau on that nvidia card and get hardware decoding. I do 1080p on a P4 2.8 GHz with an nVidia 8400 card.

However if you don't want to record TV, xbmc will be a good choice too (and also does vdpau).

The good thing is you can try either of them from a live cd without disturbing your current setup :)
