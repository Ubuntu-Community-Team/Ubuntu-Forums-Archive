### Post by agamotto on 2008-05-16
From what I have experience so far with DTV, you might just be SOL.   I live in an area with several DTV channels, most coming from one tower about 27mi away, somewhere around Orion/Sherrard.  I have tried several indoor amplified and non-amplified antennas.  None give me a range of better than 30mi with digital.  Some of the same antennas are reaching Iowa City (nearly 55mi) when plugged into an 'old' telly.

  I think it is fair to say that OTA DTV is going to be a crap experience for most if they don't have powerful outdoor antennae.  I also have the HVR-1800, and am now wondering if I will ever find it useful...  I wouldn't mind the OTA if it weren't for the fact that when a digital signal fades/jumps, the sound distortion can make Sigue Sigue Sputnik sound pleasant.
