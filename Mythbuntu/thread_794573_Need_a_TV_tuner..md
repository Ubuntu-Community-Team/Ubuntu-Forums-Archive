### Post by newlinux on 2008-05-16
rcolson: You should specify what type of signals (ATSC or QAM) you are trying to capture. There are a lot of options out there... You should also be aware of the limitations of capturing digital signals (only stations your cable company sends via unencrypted QAM for QAM tuning).

Also, the analog switch is only mandated for OTA broadcasts. Cable companies *can* continue to broadcast analog. Many will. Some won't. Some already stopped broadcasting analog. Most are slowly reducing their analog offerings to make more room for HD stations (Deleting 1 analog station makes room for about 3 digital HD stations).
