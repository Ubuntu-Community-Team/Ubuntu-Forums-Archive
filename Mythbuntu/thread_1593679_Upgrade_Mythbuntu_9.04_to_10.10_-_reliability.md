### Post by ian dobson on 2010-10-12
Hi,

I upgraded my frontend last weekend from 10.04 to 10.10, took about 2hours in total and almost everything worked. The only real problem I has was with the remote control repeating every key press twice. But it didn't take long to solve the problem.

The new NVidia drivers (260.x) appear to have improved the picture quality abit on my HDMI connected TV, as well as handling slightly corrupt streams better.

BUT, as always before doing such an upgrade make sure you have a good backup on the system.
