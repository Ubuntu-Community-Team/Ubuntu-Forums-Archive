### Post by NightStorm on 2010-01-03
I have both an Hauppauge 2250 and a SiliconDust HDHomerun.  If you want a "card", then the Hauppauge works well for me (digital only .. but that is what you want).  Of the two however I like the HDHomerun the best as it is the most flexible, esp given that it is on the network and can be "tapped" and queried by other PCs on that network.

        - Bruce

One other thing in favor of the HDHomerun: it has an IR receiver that works well with MythTV, so there is no fussing around with any additional USB (or other) IR inputs if you want to use a remote with your Mythbox.
