### Post by blackoper on 2010-03-19
When I use a capture card I only use 1 of the coax inputs on it usually. For HD qam capture cards usually one of the coax inputs is for standard def and the other is digital. When I use a pvr-150 type capture card, one of the coax lines is for fm antenna and the other is the cable coax line. I'm guessing this is what you are indicating, but it is hard to tell.

### Post by colinnwn on 2010-03-19
> **phillipcc said:**
> i have the antenna cable that leaves 3 sockets to plug in the cables i remember i had 2 other cables to plug in but only 3 sockets

Do you know what brand and model capture card you have? Do you mean you have 3 antenna sockets coming out of the wall, or 3 sockets on the back of your computer?

Almost all capture cards have only 1 or 2 antenna inputs. So if you have 3 on your computer, it is likely you have 2 different capture cards on your computer. Look really closely around those antenna inputs, one should say TV and the other FM. Ignore the FM input.
