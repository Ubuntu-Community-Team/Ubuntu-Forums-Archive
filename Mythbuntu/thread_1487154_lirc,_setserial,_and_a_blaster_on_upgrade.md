### Post by azmyth on 2010-05-18
Not sure if anyone else noticed this but when I upgraded from 9.10 to 10.04, even though setserial was installed on 9.10 when I did the upgrade setserial didn't get installed. Since you have to set the uart to none with this in order to make the blaster work, this lack of setserial caused both my remote and the blaster to not function. Once I installed setserial, lirc and my remote and blaster worked as planned.
