Eldis,

the transmitter part is easy- you can just buy any learning remote that has the keys you need and teach it from your MCE remote.
You will however need a receiver. You should be able to use an old-fashioned lirc "homebrew" receiver that you can either solder together yourself or buy on the net- google "lirc homebrew". I believe there was a small outfit in Germany that ships internationally.
When I had the homebrew receiver, I needed to do some additional installing - 7.10 did not automatically support it. Don't know how 8.10 handles it.
