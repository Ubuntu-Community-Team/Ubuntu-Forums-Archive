### Post by s13_mills on 2011-10-05
Hi there, not sure if this affects your Freedom stick, but some USB tuner sticks have a receive which will not work properly with a very strong signal, because of the tuned circuit type. If you have an RF amplifier in your system you might want to turn it down. If you don't have an amplifier, but you still can't get it to tune, you can try an attenuator.
