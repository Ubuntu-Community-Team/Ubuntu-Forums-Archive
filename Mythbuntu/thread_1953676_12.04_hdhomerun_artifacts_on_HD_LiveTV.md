### Post by nickrout on 2012-04-06
0.25 on 11.10 or 12.04 is UNRELEASED at this stage. You may have problems. Having said that it should be almost ready as the projected release date of 0.25 is 2 days away.

Have you tried all of the permutations, ie:

1. is there a difference between playback of material recorded on 0.24 and material recorded on 0.25

2. Does the material recorded on 0.25 play back OK on 0.24 frontend? You can usually test a file that is not in the database with mythavtest filename.mpg. Also try other players like mplayer, vlc, xine etc. 

3. Sounds like it could be a reception/stream corruption problem. Are you sure you haven't bumped the antenna connection? Or had a storm bump your antenna? Co-incidences happen.

4. Lastly, the most obvious question of all - what do your logs say?
