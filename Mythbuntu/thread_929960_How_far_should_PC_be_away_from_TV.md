### Post by SiHa on 2008-09-25
Tell someone (politely) they are talking nonsense.

As long as the PC is in a properly shielded metal case you needn't worry. The worst that a poorly shielded PC will do is cause interference to the TV signal, if you're not getting any glitching to a normal TV picture (ie using a tuner other than the Mythbox) then the PC is causing no problem.

You certainly won't hurt the TV, or get static.

### Post by DrMega on 2008-09-25
If either the PC, the TV or the cables connecting them are not properly shielded, then you *might* get some very minor interference. If you do, then your kit is not compliant with the FCC regulations for RF interference (which all kit has to be, otherwise it can't be sold).

In practical terms though, I think it is a load of rubbish that the two could affect each other. My media PC was directly under my TV for ages with no ill effects.
