### Post by LowSky on 2009-12-23
You will have very few issues. ION will support HD video.

I not sure on the Miglia usb tv reciever, never heard of that brand here in the US. More than likely the USB stick has a chipset inside that is from a more well known company like Philips or Conexant. 
I will say that the ATOM chip is low powered and might be an issue if you wish to watch and record at the same time.

You might want to look at a more robust miniITX system. Intel does have miniITX boards that do support more robust chips, including hire end Core 2's. And plenty of companies make media center cases that look amazing like Antec, Silverstone, and Lian-Li.
