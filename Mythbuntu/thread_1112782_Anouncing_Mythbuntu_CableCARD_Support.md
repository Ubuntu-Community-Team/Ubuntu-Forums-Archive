### Post by rhpot1991 on 2009-04-01
The Mythbuntu project is pleased to announce a partnership with 2 major US cable companies beginning today.  For the last year we have been undergoing negotiations with Time Warner & Comcast about supporting Cable Card on Mythbuntu based consumer PCs.  Negotiations are now complete and the engineers have helped to draft a technical preview of support on Mythbuntu.

Please visit [our website]("http://www.mythbuntu.org/cablecard") for more details about how you can help test Cable Card support.
