### Post by johnc4510 on 2007-08-06
The Ubuntu US Teams Project is here to provide resources and assistance to new and established US State LoCo Teams. Our goal is to have an approved US LoCo team in every US State by 2008. Everyone is welcome to join one of our US State LoCo Teams and help make this goal a reality.

A US LoCo team is a group of like minded Ubuntu enthusiasts within a state who are dedicated to the advocacy of Ubuntu.

Below you will find a link to help you determine if there is a US LoCo Team in your state or not. You will be able to link to an established state team by clicking on it's name. There you will find contact information to join the team. 

If you find that there is not team in your state, please viist us in IRC Channel #ubuntu-us, the server is Freenode, and let us know that you are interested in starting a LoCo Team in your state. We look forward to having a chance to get to know you better. 

US LoCo Teams List: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/USTeams/TeamList](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/USTeams/TeamList)
