### Post by Event_Horizon on 2008-04-20
Are you looking for a house or an apartment? Do you want to be on a Wolfline route (so you can take the bus to work) or do you mind driving a few minutes? If you have the money to spare, I would avoid any of the really cheap student housing, as its bound to be plagued with the typical problems that plague lower income housing (noisy, burglaries, littering, etc). My roommate and I are about at the end of our lease, and we are going to move to this place called Centennial Ridge. Its right up the street from a Wolfline stop and has its own shuttle service. Its also about a 10 minute drive from NCState, if you wanted to go that route. 

Here's the site link if you're interested:

[http://hracentennialridge.prospectportal.com/](http://hracentennialridge.prospectportal.com/)
