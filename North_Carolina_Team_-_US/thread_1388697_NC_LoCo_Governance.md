### Post by alinuxfan on 2010-01-23
In the IRC meetings, we have been discussing a method of leadership where the duties are split between multiple people.  The plan is divide the duties and allow individuals to focus their attention on the duties of the position.

The wiki has this link: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NorthCarolinaTeam/council](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NorthCarolinaTeam/council)

It says, "The purpose of this document is to create a fair and equal way for the entire NC LoCo Team to contribute in determining our leadership candidates. This subject will be ongoing and discussed in the Monthly meetings in IRC; please join us to have your voice heard. Nominations will be accepted from any and all parties seeking to participate and give back...."

Feel free to edit the wiki and add your name to any of the duties that you would be willing to organize or at least take a part in.

We all know this is a volunteer organization and the more work we distribute, the more work we can accomplish.  Feel free to join the channel on Freenode and discuss any contributions.
