Hi All,

There will be a meet and greet event sponsored by the North Carolina Ubuntu Local Community Team at Juicy Java in Kernersville, NC (link below) on August 1, 2009 10:00 am until 11:30ish.

Anyone interested in Ubuntu and its various flavors or GNU/Linux in general is welcome. We'd like to gauge interest in participating in bug jams leading up to the Karmic Koala release, promotional events/activities and installfests.

Wifi is available so bring your laptops, netbooks etc. Perhaps we can converse with folks meeting the same time and date in Charlotte.
