### Post by jimv on 2008-11-04
If you have a good amount of RAM and aren't doing anything memory intensive like video editing, you probably aren't ever going to use your SWAP space.

That said, an SSD would probably be a good choice for SWAP, since they excel at random reads and writes.  If you have a laptop with an expresscard slot, they sell little 4-8gig SSD's that will slide into those, and they're meant for Windows Vista ReadyBoost, but would work just as well as swap space.
