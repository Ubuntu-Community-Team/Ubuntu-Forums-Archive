### Post by bobkny on 2009-01-17
I received a fix for this problem from the eeeBuntu forum. First, you must install 8.4.1 or later release. Then you must get into the BIOS by repeatedly pressing the F2 key (I think) during the boot process to interrupt the boot and get into the BIOS control. Once there, you must select the "advanced" menu and enable the webcam by checking it. Not hard -- but kind of tricky.
