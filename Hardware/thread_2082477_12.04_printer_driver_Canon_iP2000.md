### Post by olligobber on 2012-11-09
Had a look at the list of supported Canon printers on ubuntu ([https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsPrintersCanon](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsPrintersCanon))

the iP2000 is mentioned, but test results are 5-7 years outdated. If you want a printer on ubuntu I suggest an HP printer as hplip ([http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html)) is constantly being updated and supports most printers.

I had a Canon printer working on ubuntu until I updated from 10.04. Since then I've got an HP one which isn't working yet, but I haven't spent long trying to fix it.

Good luck!
