By 'reduce the specs for a computer attached to a home network with a server.' do you mean this- 1 desktop, 1 server-  then reduce the specs on the desktop? 

Reducing the size of the HDD will make a minimal difference at best. The only real difference would be 1 or 2 platter HDDs vs the same type HDD with 3, 4 or 5 platter...less energy is required to spin fewer platters. A 2TB WD "Green Power" (or any of the 'green' drives from the other HDD manufacturers) will chew less power than the 7200RPM models, even old, small 7200s.
