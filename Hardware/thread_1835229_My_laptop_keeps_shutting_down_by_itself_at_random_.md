### Post by SiathLinux on 2011-08-29
Couple of 'non-OS' things to check on your HP Laptop - 
1. is it overheating (the fan / venting system can become clogged with dust)...
2. is there a memory failure? (the ram having issues while in use could cause an auto-shut-down).

On the overheat that can be checked while running - there is a blog I found for 'how-to'
[http://www.compdigitec.com/labs/2008/07/18/measure-cpu-temp-in-ubuntu/](http://www.compdigitec.com/labs/2008/07/18/measure-cpu-temp-in-ubuntu/)

I used to have an Acer Aspire that did overheat and auto-shut-down (fyi - it gave no warning - just shut off  as if pulling the cord from a desktop would...)

Hope this helps - but not sure...
