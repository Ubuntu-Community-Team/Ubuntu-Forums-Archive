### Post by CatKiller on 2020-03-19
No experience of Thinkpads at all, but

> **polar-bear-8 said:**
> 18.04.4 LTS...AMD Ryzen 5 3500U 

20.04 will probably work out of the box OK, but 18.04 would need a newer version of Mesa. It's just adding a PPA; there are a few to choose from, depending on your preferred level of newness. 

>    Wireless     REALTEK Wireless 8822BE 2x2 AC, Bluetooth Version 4.2

No experience of that chip, either, but it's the kind of thing you should look for reports about. Some Realtek stuff is fine, other stuff can be a real pain. 

If you have the option of swapping out that slow hard drive for a second SSD, you should.
