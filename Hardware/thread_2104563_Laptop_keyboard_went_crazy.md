### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-01-13
Take the keyboard out and look for any evidence of fluid spills.  Turn it over and bat the backside on a towel--you would be surprised how much stuff gets caught inside a keyboard.  Reseat the connector and monitor the behavior.  What else does the F6 key control?  Any way to turn that function off in BIOS?
