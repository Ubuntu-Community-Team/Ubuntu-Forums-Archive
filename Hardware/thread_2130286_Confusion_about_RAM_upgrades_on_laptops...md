### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-03-30
Both the BIOS and the chipset will be the limiting factors.  The only well to tell is to buy some RAM and test it with *memtest*.  You will get both the speed of the RAM and the reliability.  Your best bet is a matched pair that provides the maximum RAM that you want.  In this case it is probably 2 sticks at 4GB each.  You will probably spend a little more, but find a local source with a return policy.  Or find a local computer fair and bring your laptop so you can test the RAM on site.
