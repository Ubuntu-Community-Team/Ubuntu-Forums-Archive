### Post by doobrie on 2012-01-30
If you go to Power Options, you've the option to either suspend or hibernate when the laptop lid is closed.

Suspend puts the laptop into low power mode which will drain the battery somewhat.

Hibernate puts the laptop to sleep and doesn't use any power at all.
