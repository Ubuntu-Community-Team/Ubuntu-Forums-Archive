### Post by bastian1343 on 2011-11-04
> **r730 said:**
> ...my back lights on my screen don't work anymore can anyone help me please thank you

While it may be moot now, the solution here is in your BIOS settings.

The R530 ships with a lot of battery saving features in the BIOS, some less useful than others. One of these is that by default, the backlight won't work when booting without the AC-adapter connected.
