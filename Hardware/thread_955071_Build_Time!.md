### Post by Afkpuz on 2008-10-21
Should boot up and cruise pretty well.  There might be a few small things that annoy you.

1.) Wireless might not work-I don't know any details here, but know that many people have problems with this.  Check this on google

2.) Mouse buttons might not all work-The mouse will work, but sometimes, multi-button mice don't use all their buttons.  Still, it will point and click

3.) Sound-If you're using onboard sound, some of the cheaper mobos have weak linux drivers and hiss/pop/annoy sometimes.  Check on google


Overall, the comp will work and work well.
