### Post by AltomineUK on 2010-07-05
If you have tried so, you can have a look at the processes running on your PC by typing in 'top' command in your Terminal. It will not reduce the workload on your processor, but it will show you where your resources are going...... It may also help to stop any unnecessary "start-up" programs from starting at boot up.
