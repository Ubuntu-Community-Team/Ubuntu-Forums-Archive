### Post by squenson on 2011-11-06
I would try again the installation. Install the CD and boot the laptop. After Ubuntu is visible, test that everything is going well. Then you can ask Ubuntu to perform an installation on the hard disk. As far as I know, you cannot hibernate a live CD, you have to shutdown the computer or press 5 seconds the power button.

Make sure your laptop is connected to the power as any interruption in the installation process can prevent your laptop from booting.
