### Post by realzippy on 2011-09-03
I edited post #6...

```
gksudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf
```
change Hsync
[COLOR="Red"]55[/COLOR] 
to 
85
Also change:
Option         "metamodes" "CRT: [COLOR="Red"]1360x768[/COLOR] +0+0, DFP: 1920x1080 +[COLOR="Red"]1360[/COLOR]+0"
to
Option         "metamodes" "CRT: 1920x1080 +0+0, DFP: 1920x1080 +1920+0"

restart X or reboot,and you should be fine.
