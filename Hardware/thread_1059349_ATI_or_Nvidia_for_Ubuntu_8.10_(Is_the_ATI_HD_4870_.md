### Post by markbuntu on 2009-02-03
The latest driver from ati, 9.1 and some new workarounds from compiz have fixed the last of the compiz problems. All this without DRI2 which is coming with Jaunty which would have fixed them anyway.

ATI has greatly improved their proprietary drivers and their support for the open source driver writers is far more involved than nvidia. For that reason alone it would be better to choose ati. Before the year is out there will probably be a fully functional open source driver so you will have choices.
