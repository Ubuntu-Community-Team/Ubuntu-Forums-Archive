It's a Fujifilm Finepix A600 as the thread title mentions. So far I've tried installing gphoto2, to apparently no effect. And this particular camera isn't listed in the [supported cameras list]("http://www.gphoto.org/proj/libgphoto2/support.php"), although a few similar ones are:

"Fuji FinePix A330"
"Fuji FinePix A800"
"Fuji FinePix A820"
"Fuji FinePix A920"
Has anyone got any experience with this? or might have any idea how to do it?

### Post by WinterRain on 2010-04-11
Try Gthumb. After camera is attached and turned on, open gthumb and go to file>import photos.
