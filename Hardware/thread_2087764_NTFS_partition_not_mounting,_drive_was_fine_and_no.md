### Post by oldfred on 2012-11-24
What does Disk Utility say about drive. If you click on drive it should show Smart Data on right side. All I really know is green is good or drive is ok. Age of drive does not really matter.

And data that is valuable should be backed up on a regular basis. Drives fail, users make errors, power failures corrupt data. Many things can occur and most are out of our control.

Since it is NTFS, you can try running chkdsk from Windows or a Windows repairCD.
