My LG E300 laptop (with Intel T2390 @ 1.86GHz) is starting to make me sad. Under full load the CPU temp goes up to about 94 degrees Celsius and the system throttles down the CPU because it is CPU too hot. The dmesg output looks like this:

> CPU1: Temperature above threshold, cpu clock throttled

Crap. Why couldn't they make this laptop with better cooling? Or put a CPU in it that wouldn't have to throttle.

From what I read, this problem isn't that uncommon. Let's list our laptops that throttle on use so other people can stay away from them!
