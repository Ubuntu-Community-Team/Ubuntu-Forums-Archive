### Post by steve01832 on 2009-05-13
Hi. I have no experience with that brand of HD but I have a 500GB Maxtor and it works just fine in Hardy. It is easy to format to ext3 also. You can do it using cfdisk or you can format it using gparted. I left mine NTFS because I dual boot with Vista and having NTFS I can move files around with ease. I just upgraded to Jaunty and haven't had any issues with the drive there either. Sorry about using the V word. I don't usually swear on the forums, this one kind of got away.
