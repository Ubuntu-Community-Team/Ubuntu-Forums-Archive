### Post by jasonab on 2009-11-02
I'm not sure about your phone but some phones need to be swiched to mass storage mode before the computer will detect it as a mass storage device. Sometimes a message will appear on the phone when you plug it into a usb port.

If that doesn't work. In terminal run lsusb to check if the device is being recognized. Paste it's output here if you can. running dmesg just after plugging the device in will also show some useful information.
