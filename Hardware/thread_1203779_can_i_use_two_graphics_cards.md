Can i use my onboard vga graphics adapter(Nvidia 7050) for my monitor, but use my Nvidia 8800 gs graphics card at the same time?  The 8800 gs has dual dvi ports but i don't have a cable and there expensive to buy.  Any help would be appreciated, cheers.

### Post by juzzbott on 2009-07-27
Unfortunately no.

You cannot use the onboard VGA port with an PCI-e or APG graphics card. The only option you have is to either get the required cable, or try and hunt down a second graphics card.

You can use a PCI graphics card alongside a PCI-e or APG card, but unfortunately not your onboard port.
