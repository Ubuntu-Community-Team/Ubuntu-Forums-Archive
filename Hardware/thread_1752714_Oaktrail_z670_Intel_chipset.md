### Post by pibach on 2011-08-17
according to benchmarks Z670 will be more or less the same speed  as N270 at probably half the power consumption. So it basically doubles the battery runtime. And allows for fanless devices. Fujitsu Q550 or Motion CL900 are the first Oak Trail devices.
Surprisingly Tegra2 devices seem to offer much higher speed at even lower power consumption - but that might be due to the more ressource aware adroid OS.
