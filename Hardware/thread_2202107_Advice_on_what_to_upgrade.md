### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2014-01-28
If you're a gamer and it's been 6 years, it's probably time to redo the whole system. However, if I was going the upgrade route, then I would keep the CPU and I wouldn't buy a new mobo (unless I could get a Socket 775 mobo for a good price). The money is better served getting another 1GB stick of RAM so you can take advantage of dual channel memory (assuming you have 3 x 1GB sticks) and upgrading the GPU (stay with nvidia).
