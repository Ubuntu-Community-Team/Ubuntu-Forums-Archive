### Post by elemal on 2008-06-07
You can download G15macro from [sourceforge]("http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=172261&package_id=196992") or as I did, dl the debian g15macro .deb file, and install it. Worked fine for me.
Then you can remap keys G1-G18 with macros.
