### Post by DanneStrat on 2011-06-09
Hi.

When I saw your post I googled around a bit and found this:

[http://compreviews.about.com/od/monitors/a/LCDBurnIn.htm](http://compreviews.about.com/od/monitors/a/LCDBurnIn.htm)

Look under the section: "Can it be prevented/corrected?"

LCD screens work in a different manner than the old CRT-monitors in 

displaying the picture on screen as you can read from that article and 

also that a "burnt-in" image on a lcd is not as critical as one on a crt-

monitor. There's a good chance that you may get rid of the 

"ghosting" on your monitor by following the advices on that page.

I would start with running a colorful, rotating screensaver overnight.

Let me know how it goes.
