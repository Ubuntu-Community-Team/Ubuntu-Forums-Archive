### Post by Beacon11 on 2011-07-27
You can't go wrong with anything from System76 ([http://www.system76.com/](http://www.system76.com/)). They design their laptops explicitly for Ubuntu. I wouldn't dare to say they are the most beautiful designs, but they are very capable and will run Ubuntu very well.

Take for example the Bonobo Pro-- 1080p resolution (1920 x 1080) and you can choose which graphics card you want (not integrated). Is that what you had in mind? If 17 inches is too big, at least some of their smaller ones have the same resolution, and I don't believe any of them have integrated graphics (except for the Pangolin on down).
