Personally I have a acer aspire one D250, with the ram maxed to 2 gigs. I don't use the mic so I assume it works, same for the web cam, it shows it in cheese the webcam program. Otherwise every Ubuntu I have installed runs great, as well as archlinux, and a bunch of others in virtualbox. It was a cost of 299$US, so you get what you pay for so to speak. I did have the ethernet port go bad on me within the warranty, but because I had upgraded XP to W7 and had Ubuntu acer would not service it unless it had the original OS. I purchased the OEM XP re-installl discs, but just bought a usb adapter for the cord in the end.
