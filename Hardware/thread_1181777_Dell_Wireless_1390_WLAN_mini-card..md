### Post by Los Frijoles on 2009-06-08
Dell has almost all of their drivers available for download on their site. The file should be called something like Rxxxxxx.exe (x's are numbers). The site is a bit difficult to get around in, but just click on anything that says drivers. Also, the search box will help.

This also is helpful if anyone needs drivers for dell laptops on linux since the .inf files that the executables spit out when ran can be used by ndiswrapper. (I don't think this is an issue for ubuntu though...)
