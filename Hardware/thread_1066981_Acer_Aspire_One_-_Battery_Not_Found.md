### Post by Kira Vakaan on 2011-10-24
Success!!

I updated the BIOS and now for the first time ever, my battery status indicator works on Ubuntu!!  I don't have an Aspire One, but an update worked for my Aspire 5745G.  Be sure to get the correct version for your model.  I dual-boot with Windows 7, and so I was able to update the BIOS from there by downloading the flasher from Acer.
