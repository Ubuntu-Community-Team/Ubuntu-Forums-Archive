### Post by Dark_Stang on 2008-10-21
Take out the ram and all devices hooked up PCI. Turn it on. The motherboard should beep because it doesn't have any memory. If it doesn't you're probably looking at a bad mobo or a bad processor. Either way, just get a new machine. If you've only got a 200 wat PSU, I'm guessing it's a fairly old machine.
