### Post by WinRiddance on 2010-12-11
You might think that I'm crazy but I'm not convinced that you were ever powered off completely. This has happened to me in the past before too and when I tried to restart, thinking that I powered off, I actually only extended the suspend function somehow. So go ahead and try this instead .... hold your power button down for 4 to 5 seconds and see if you can physically hear the system power completely off. Even if it's just a little tick noise, something .... then wait a minute or two and try to power up again. See what happens and report back. I can't imagine that you're having a hardware issue unless it resulted from something that you did with your video cables which would cause display issues.
