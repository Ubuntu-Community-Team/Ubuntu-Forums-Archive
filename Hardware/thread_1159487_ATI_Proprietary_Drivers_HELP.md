### Post by mossman44 on 2009-05-14
My suggestion is to download the drivers for your card here: [http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/fire/Legacy/Pages/fire_linux.aspx?type=2.4.3&product=2.4.3.3.2.3.1&lang=English](http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/fire/Legacy/Pages/fire_linux.aspx?type=2.4.3&product=2.4.3.3.2.3.1&lang=English)
Then, look at the release notes and download the necessary packages. Then, run the installer and the setup wizard should guide you through the process and after a reboot you should be good. I do this with my radeon hd4850 (don't worry the link I provided is to your card) and it works. Not sure if it will work for firegl cards but its worth a shot.

If my suggestion doesn't work, try this link for help:
[http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu)
