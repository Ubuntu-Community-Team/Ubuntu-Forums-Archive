### Post by glenngds2007 on 2008-12-03
I will be running TvTime and the system will lockup kind of.  The mouse in question is a Logitech cordless p/n 831192-d000.  The system never locked up until I changed the mouse.  What happens is that the system will be normally showing say channel 7 and when I try to change channels or move the mouse I discover that the mouse and keyboard does not work but TvTime continues to show the channel.  Any suggestions would be helpful.  I can attach any files from my computer if that would be helpful to find the problem.

---

### Post by glenngds2007 on 2008-12-03
I discovered the problem.  The mouse and keyboard were plugged into a USB hub and apparently the hub was being overloaded.  When I plugged the keyboard and mouse into the USB ports on the computer the lockups stopped.:)
