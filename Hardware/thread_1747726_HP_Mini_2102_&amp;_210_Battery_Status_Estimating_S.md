### Post by VideoRoy on 2011-05-02
I know a bunch of people are having problems on the Mini's with Gnome Power Indicator always showing "Estimating ...." instead of minutes or a percentage.

On Maverick, Andy Eyre's created patch that fixed it but it was broken again in Natty.

Just wanted to mention I ran across Battery-Status here:

[http://live.gnome.org/BatteryStatus]("http://live.gnome.org/BatteryStatus")

that seems to have solved my problem.

The maintainer Ivan, just updated the PPA for Natty and it seems to replace the broken Gnome indicator in Unity quite nicely.

Note: If you go this route make sure to add the correct statements to your startup apps, but that is all covered on the website.

Good luck.
