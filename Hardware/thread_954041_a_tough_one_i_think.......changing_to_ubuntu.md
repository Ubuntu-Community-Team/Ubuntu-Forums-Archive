### Post by tuxxy on 2008-10-20
You use the app **gparted** to edit your partitions, you cannot edit partitions while mounted so you need to run the Live CD and then select gparted (system > admin > partition editor)

Now you can edit your partitions from here so delete your windows and resize the Ubuntu one with the space you made free.

formatting your XP partition shouldnt delete your MBR so GRUB will be fine.
