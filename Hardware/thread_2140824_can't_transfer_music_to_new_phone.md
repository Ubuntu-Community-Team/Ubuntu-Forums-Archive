### Post by evol4g on 2013-04-30
you're new phone more than likely uses MTP (Media Transfer Protocol) which isn't supported by 12.04 i believe.. I'm not sure what the best route is for you if you're not rooted.. However if your phone is rooted you can boot into recovery, mount usb storage, and then transfer any files you'd like.. 

Also a great option is to just use google play music, you can upload 20,000 songs for free and you can stream them from anywhere or download them to your device..
