### Post by prshah on 2008-10-22
> **Xzoky said:**
> I have Ubuntu 8.04 and a Sony PSP ; and when I connect my PSP to my PC using the USB cable, Rhythmbox starts up automatically.
I'd like to turn this auto-start off.

Click System-Preferences-File Management. If you can't find it there, press Alt+F2 and give the command ```
nautilus-file-management-properties
``` Open the tab "Media", and under "Music Player" change from "Open RhythymBox music player" to "Ask what to do" (or other suitable option).
