### Post by jfr1tz on 2009-01-22
dmesg,syslog,messages log etc might provide a clue System->Administration->System logs.

some basic system specs are also useful, ie amd or intel processor, type of graphic card etc. 

Also if you know how have a look at the temperature of the processor; if it's very high it might not be a driver problem but simply the processor overheating(or some other component). Do you have these issues under a different os, for example windows?
