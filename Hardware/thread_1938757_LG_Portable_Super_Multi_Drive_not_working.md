### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-03-14
I don't normally suggest this, but the newest 12.04 Beta will have both a newer kernel version and newer drivers.

So, you should consider downloading and burning the 12.04 Beta to CD, booting from that, choosing Try Ubuntu, and once you get to a desktop (it will take a WHILE), then plugin the external drive and see if it is detected.  Since this is a BETA, some stuff won't work but this will give you a chance to see if a newer kernel and/or newer drivers work better for you.
