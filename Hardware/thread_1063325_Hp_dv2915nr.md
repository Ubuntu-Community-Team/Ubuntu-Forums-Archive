### Post by rrwood on 2009-02-07
I recently picked up an HP DV2915NR laptop, and am fantastically pleased with how well Ubuntu works on this machine!  ALL the hardware works perfectly out of the box with Ubuntu 8.10.  Wireless, audio, webcam, video, and DVD.  Even suspend-to-RAM works perfectly!

So, if anyone is considering this model and is wondering about Ubuntu/Linux compatibility, wonder no more-- this is a great choice.


The only thing I haven't checked is video out via the S-video connector, since I really don't care about it.  Oh-- and the funky IR port (this laptop comes with a little remote control even), since I can't imagine ever really needing that either.
