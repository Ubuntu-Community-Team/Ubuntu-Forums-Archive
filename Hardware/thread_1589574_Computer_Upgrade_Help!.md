### Post by mastablasta on 2010-10-07
i am not sure this kind of maschine is really worth upgrading. waste of money. but if you wish to do it it seems fine. 
 
i would suggest to get more ram. it's more important than graphics card. graphics card is probably not worth upgrading unless you need to play certain games. i used a 16MB card and most things worked fine.
 
also instead of upgrading disk it might be better to buy & add external usb disk. you can use it for data, transfer it easilly to another computer and you can use it later after you get a new maschine.
 
BTW have you tried running LXDE on the maschine (Lubuntu or Mint LXDE). it needs less resources...
 
My advice would be: upgrade the ram if you can do it (check the motherboard specifications) and get a USB disk for your data. if you lack USB ports or if all are occupied you can buy a cheap PCI USB expansion card that will add 4 more USB ports.
 
> P.S Does changing the processor affect the bios and the kernel of the OS? (Ubuntu 10.04 32-bit)
no.
