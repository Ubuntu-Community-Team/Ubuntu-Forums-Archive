### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-06-10
The ATI Mobilty 9000 came out in 2004 -- so it's now 10 years old!  There are no fglrs drivers that will work with this card.

Even the default Radeon open-source drivers might not work well -- considering the age of the card.
