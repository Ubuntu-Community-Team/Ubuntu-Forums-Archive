### Post by avtolle on 2008-06-10
Just because it (the Live CD) worked well on your desktop is no guarantee, as you have learned, that it will do equally well on your "baby laptop".

If you are going to install anyway, I'd suggest a download of the alternate cd iso, burn it to disk and install from there. Many times this allows installation where the Live CD fails, as it uses a text-based installer, rather than the graphic one, which eliminates some of the problems with graphic card compatibility and other things.
