### Post by rg4w on 2012-03-12
This sounds like an issue reported by many others in which the option in Settings to turn off the trackpad when the keyboard is in use has a bug which causes the trackpad to remain turned off.

I believe this has been fixed in 12.04.

For 11.10 you may be able to turn that option off to retain use of the trackpad.

If that doesn't do it please report back and we'll explore other options.
