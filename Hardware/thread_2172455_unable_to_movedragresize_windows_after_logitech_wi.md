### Post by Donny Bahama on 2013-09-05
On Mint 13 Cinnamon, when I connect a Logitech  wireless mouse (M305), all of a sudden, I'm no longer able to drag  windows around via their title bar. Nor am I able to size them. I can  click on Menu and the Mint menu pops up - but the selection highlight  doesn't track the cursor and I'm unable to click on anything. The cursor moves smoothly and is not sluggish or unresponsive - I'm just very limited wrt click/drag operations.

Any suggestions on how to troubleshoot this? Are there some alternate drivers I should look into?

### Post by Jonor on 2013-09-08
If RAM and swap memory were in heavy use and a PS2 mouse was pulled and replaced with a USB mouse while the system was up (a hot swap), the what i believe resulting increased memory demand might produce this kind of problem as various processes get gently pruned back. So firstly a shutdown and mouse cold swap should help in this scenario followed by increasing RAM and swap memory.
