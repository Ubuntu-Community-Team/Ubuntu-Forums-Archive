### Post by elliottj on 2010-11-14
I have that machine (some different specs though).

My experience has been great, with a few quirks, such as:
Wireless is disabled on startup. Bizarre, but it is. You have to right click on it and enable wireless, then you're good to go. This machine has pretty impressive graphics, but suffers greatly on the ubuntu side of things (not for everyday use, but for any kind of gaming.... no way).
Other than that, I would say you shouldn't have too many problems!
