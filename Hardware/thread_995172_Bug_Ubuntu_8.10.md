### Post by jespdj on 2008-11-27
That's a Flash website. Try this: Right-click on the website, you'll see a small pop-up menu with "Settings..." and "About Adobe Flash Player".

Click "Settings...". You'll now see a small window with the question "Allow images.badoo.com to access your camera and microphone?". Set it to "Deny" if you don't want it.

On my computer, it's on Deny by default. I don't know why it allows this by default on your computer.
