### Post by Henrike Corinna on 2009-05-25
Hi,

         A USB TV and Video Capture Device is a device that is capable of transferring an audio and video signal from an electronic device such as a VCR, Television or DVD Player to a Computer. The USB TV and Video Capture Device inputs the video signal into the device by either Composite, S-Video or RF inputs, and audio by Stereo RCA inputs or RF input. A TV-only USB Capture Device will input a cable TV or Satellite signal by RF input, but Composite cables can be used to input a TV signal as well on USB TV and Video Capture Devices that have Composite A/V inputs. The signal from the USB TV and Video Capture Device is output to the computer by a USB 2.0 cable at 400mbps plugged into a USB 2.0 port on the Computer.
