### Post by 2F4U on 2012-11-07
Fan noise not necessarily depends on the load situation, but usually it depends on the temperatures inside the machine. Besides the situation that the BIOS reports the wrong temperatures to the operating system, which happens quite often on Windows laptops, there is also the case that temperatures are raised by the graphics card, and not the CPU.
Your laptop seems to have two graphics cards, and unless you have shutdown one of them, both will be running and both will produce heat.
