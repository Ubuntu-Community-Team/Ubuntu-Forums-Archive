### Post by nick_h on 2008-05-25
What you need to do is look up on a site like [www.crucial.com](www.crucial.com) what type of memory each computer uses.

The Acer Travelmate 2420 needs DDR2 PC2-5300.  Most of the Mac powerbook G4s used the old DDR.  A few of the later models used DDR2 though.

It would be worth checking what you have in the Mac but you are probably out of luck.
