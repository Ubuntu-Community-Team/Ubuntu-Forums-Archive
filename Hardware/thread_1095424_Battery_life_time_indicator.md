### Post by Macchi on 2009-05-02
If the calculations of estimated remaining battery charge (in time or percentage) are wrong then the power manager will shutdown the computer (or eventually hibernate) despite the fact that there is some usable power left in the battery.


I am experiencing similar problems with the battery indicator for a relatively new netbook on Ubuntu 9.04 Jaunty. The parameter measured is battery voltage ad I fear that the algorithm might be too simple. 


Any ideas on more precise estimates on the power manager?
