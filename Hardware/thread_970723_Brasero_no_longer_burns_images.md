### Post by zamajama on 2013-01-26
Burning issues could also be directly attributed to the hardware environment.  Check cables, and check for dust!  I cracked open my chassis and re-seated the SATA cable on the back of my DVDRW drive, and when I traced it to the MOBO input I came across a huge ball of dust fermenting in between the inputs for my SATA drives.  I also tried using a different brand of DVD-R; was using Verbatim, but then popped in one from Staples.  Low and behold, my ISO image burned successfully.
