### Post by starcannon on 2008-06-29
I have a 2g, a couple of 4g's, and an 8g.

If I would have waited until this year I could have gotten the Asus Eee 900 for about the same money as the 8g.

If your considering the 4g, it runs Ubuntu excellently. I did put a 16gb sdhc card in it though. Suspend resume will require one to compile 2.6.25 kernel so that it doesn't improperly unmount the sdhc card during suspend/hibernate.

There are some nice script packs out that install the essentials for you as well as resize everything for the 7" screen (the 900 has nearly 10" screen)

I'd go for the 900 if I were buying today though, a little more money gets you 20gb of ssd and a larger screen, and the case size is nearly identical.

just my 2cp.

P.S.
Lots of info on all the Eee's can be found at [http://www.eeeuser.com](http://www.eeeuser.com)
