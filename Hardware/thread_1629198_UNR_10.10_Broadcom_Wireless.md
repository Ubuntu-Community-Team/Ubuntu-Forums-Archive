### Post by Fafler on 2010-11-23
In my experience, the Broadcom wireless devices doesn't behave very well under Linux. I brought a Intel 5100 wlan adapter from eBay and changed it in my Dell. Got rid of all my wifi problems and 150 mbit instead of 54. I think i paid around $12 including shipping.

Another road to try is the alternative driver. Go to System -> Administration -> Additional drivers and look for it. Or you could try ndiswrapper.
