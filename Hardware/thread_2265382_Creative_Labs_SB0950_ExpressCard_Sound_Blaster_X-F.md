### Post by bcschmerker on 2015-02-15
As far as I am aware, development of the Driver snd-ctxfi for the Creative Technology CA20K1 and CA20K2 chipsets has been abandoned by the Advanced LinUX Sound Architecture Project.  The latest Creative Technology DSP supported by ALSA is the CA10300-IAT (Driver: snd-emu10k1), used as of February 2015 in the SB1570 Audigy FX and SB1550 Audigy RX.
