### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-08-06
As does the HP Deskjet F2180 all-in-one.

See [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsScanners](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsScanners) for all you may need to know.  I accept there are some machines missing from this list, simply because no-one has added them yet, but it's a good start.

If you get an HP machine, you may need to add **hplip** if it's not installed by default, (I can't remember whether it is or not) and if you get an all-in-one, **hplip-gui** is needed for the toolbox.  It may also be needed for a stand-alone scanner, but I am not sure.
