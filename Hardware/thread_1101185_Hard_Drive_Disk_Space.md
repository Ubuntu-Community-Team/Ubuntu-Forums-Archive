### Post by lisati on 2009-03-20
One source of discrepancy between what's on the box and what the OS reports is the way 1 Gb is measured. Strictly speaking, a gigabyte is 1000Mb which in turn is 1000kb etc...... but sometimes it's more convenenient to measure it in terms of 1024 instead of 1000.
