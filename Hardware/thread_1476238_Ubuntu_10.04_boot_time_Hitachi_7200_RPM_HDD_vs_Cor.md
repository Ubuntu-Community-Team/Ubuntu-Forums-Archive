### Post by TDK800 on 2010-05-07
I researched SSDs to upgrade the HDD to and the new Corsair Nova V64 released last month was clearly above competition for its speed/price on hardware comparison sites, so posting here in case anyone's searching for whether it works with Ubuntu and if it is worth buying:

Ubuntu 10.04 shutdown/bootup times with Full Disk Encryption:

HDD Hitachi 7200 RPM:
Power off: 8 seconds
Power On to Firefox open: 69.2 seconds

SSD Corsair Nova V64:
Power off: 6 seconds
Power On to Firefox open: 36.3 seconds

Should You upgrade to SSD?
Faster startup was nice, but I really felt the speed difference in web browsing (must be the instant random access reads of stored cache)... few seconds here and there really adds up if you're visiting hundreds of pages per day.
