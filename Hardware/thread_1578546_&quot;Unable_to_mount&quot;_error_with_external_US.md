### Post by chettyk on 2010-09-26
When I have problems with my external drives not being picked up, I try the following:

1. First, check the cable connecting the external drive to the computer. If you keep the cable connected to the external drive, disconnect and reconnect it. Then connect it once again to the computer.

2. Shut down the computer completely and then restart Ubuntu. Try connecting the external drive now. Believe it or not, this sometimes works; I have no idea why.

If neither of these work, there could be a problem with the drive itself.

Good luck!
