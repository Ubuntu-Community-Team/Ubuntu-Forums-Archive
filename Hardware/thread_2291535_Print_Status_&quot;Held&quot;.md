### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2015-08-21
If running Ubuntu, goto "System Setting>Printers>Job Options (expand window to see all content) & click on _more_ to see the "Held" dialog-box (change "Hold Until" to "don't hold" or similar).  Note my printers are Epson and HP . . your printer config windows and text may be slightly different . . but you should be able to get it sorted out.
