### Post by Autodave on 2020-09-13
Sounds like a possible power supply issue.  The mouse needs the 5V signal from the PC to operate.  When the 5V gets weak, the mouse is usually the first thing to quit working because the laser light doesn't have sufficient voltage to operate.  Try a different mouse and/or different port.  If the bad PC still won't operate the mouse, you can try a powered USB hub: that may work and if it DOES work that way, you have a failing power supply.

I have seen a couple of times, where a HD is pulling to much power and that dropped the 5V line too low.  So, that is a possibility, but I would lean towards a bad power supply.
