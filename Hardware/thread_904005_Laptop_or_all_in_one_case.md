### Post by nicedude on 2008-08-29
If you want a laptop then I would buy a laptop, if you want to build a portable then you could look at a mini itx size motherboard and put it in a pelican case ( plastic but tough enough to drive a truck over like the military stores stuff in )  along with a LCD and hard drive and you could even rig up a sealed battery and power charger system as well. I plan on doing one myself setup for wifi security penetration duty one of these days.

Heres a site on mini ITX where you can find allot of info

[http://www.mini-itx.com/](http://www.mini-itx.com/)

Hope that helps
