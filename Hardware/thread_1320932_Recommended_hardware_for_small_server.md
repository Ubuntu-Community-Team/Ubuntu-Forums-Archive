### Post by tgalati4 on 2009-11-09
For a home server:

Pentium III or better
256 MB RAM or better
40 GB disk or better
100BaseT network card (your internet connection will be the bottleneck)

For a small business, any recent, rack-based server will provide years of service and more power than you will know what to do with.
