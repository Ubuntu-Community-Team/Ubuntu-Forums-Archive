### Post by tgalati4 on 2010-06-06
There are some tutorials on youtube for how to do this repair.  Basically, you take a heat gun and heat the top side of the chip until the solder balls melt and make a decent connection with the motherboard.  Repair is successful in perhaps 50% of the cases.  You need to isolate the GPU with tin foil to protect the other components.  Make youself a tin foil hat while you are at it.  If the repair is successful, then you will be proud to wear the hat.
