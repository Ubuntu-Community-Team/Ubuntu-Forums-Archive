### Post by uberdonkey5 on 2009-01-29
The last two days I have been searching the internet to get my HP 1005 printer working with ubuntu 8.04 (Hardy).

There are several sites thats (correctly) say the drivers in hardy don't work. However the fix of downloading foo2zjs didn't work for me (I tried several different methods).

Eventually I came to this site:

[http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/downloads.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/downloads.html)

that I think is maintained by hewlitt packard, and their automatic installer for the HPLIP worked perfectly... just follow the instructions.

I think this will help with ALL hewlett packard printers.

Incidently, I did have a version of HPLIP ABOVE that which they were saying was going to be downloaded, but only when I used this did it work. Hope this helps other users.
