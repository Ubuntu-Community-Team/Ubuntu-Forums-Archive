### Post by kurja on 2013-04-29
I use a photo developing program called Darktable, it uses the gpus for number-crunching... I only wish I could plug more of them on my motherboard ;) There are other programs out there that do it too, they just see each gpu as a resource, not caring if they have any screens connected to them.
