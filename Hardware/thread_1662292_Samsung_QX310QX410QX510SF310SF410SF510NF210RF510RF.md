### Post by EricDP on 2011-01-07
There is an open bug that many newer Samsung laptops/netbooks don't have properly working touchpads. They are improperly detected as a mouse and only have basic mouse controls. Things like side-scrolling and palm detection don't work, and they keep detecting a left-click if you accidently graze the pad with your hand.

If you're having this problem, please go to the bug, login, and click "this bug affects me" at the top. Right now, the bug doesn't seem to be getting a lot of traction because not many people are "affected" and I suspect it's actually affecting a lot more people who just don't realize it can be fixed.

Please help escalate this bug!

The bug is here:

[https://bugs.edge.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/681904](https://bugs.edge.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/681904)
