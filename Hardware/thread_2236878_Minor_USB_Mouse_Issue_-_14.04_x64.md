### Post by lah-ca on 2014-07-29
Hi,

If my mouse gets bumped or jostled while 14.04 is loading, the mouse drivers do not load and there is no mouse enabled when boot is finished.

This issue is more of a curiosity item than a problem; at the very worst it is only a very minor annoyance.  

Logging in, unplugging the USB connector for the mouse, and plugging it back in resolves the problem immediately.

Just a little weird.

Any ideas other than not bumping the mouse during boot?  :p:p:p

### Post by M._Gruber on 2014-07-29
Can you check if the BIOS of your motherboard has something like 'USB mouse support for DOS' enabled?
That's often the reason for such a problem...
