### Post by Afkpuz on 2008-10-20
Right click on "Applications" and click "Edit Menus". Select Other and check the box "screens and graphics". Then close and click Applications>Other>Screens and Graphics

You'll want to make sure that you can set your resolution to either 720 or 1080 resolution.  

720=1280x720
1080=1920×1080
