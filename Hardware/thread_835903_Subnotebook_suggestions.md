Why not an eeePC? I am not sure how the 701 models do with decoding high-res video, but they definitely output even at high resolutions quite well. Maybe an eeePC 9xx would be good, it has a bit more power under the hood and it can also output to a big screen.

I do not think there are any subnotes with HDMI, but as long as your videos are not ultra-high-res you can use VGA or DVI just fine.
