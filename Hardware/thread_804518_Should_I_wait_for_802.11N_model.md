### Post by Beowulf.1000 on 2008-05-29
REPORT: I bought the better U405-S2830 Toshiba Satellite subnotebook with the 802.11N wifi. Arrived yesterday. Wow, some interesting good and bad. Good: N wifi worked flawless after installing Ubuntu 8.04, as did to my amazement the detection and automagic connection to my new wireless printer, a Brother HL-2170w, very cool. Bad: DVD movie playback essentially useless, so poor, slow, blocky pixelations, I can only figure that has something to do with an poor driver for the Intel graphics chip that is part of the subnotebook. I bought the subnotebook though as a second laptop, primarily to take to the coffee shop for word processing and surfing the web, so lack of videofile playback is not critical; hopefully the next version of Ubuntu will fix this issue.
