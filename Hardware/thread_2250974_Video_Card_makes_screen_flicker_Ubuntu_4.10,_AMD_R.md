### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-11-01
> Also I know the issue is software

You are correct -- the issue is the poor state of Linux drivers for that AMD video card.

Unfortunately, it takes a while for the open-source community to "catch up" when new hardware is released, and in the case of AMD, their support for Linux drivers fell off when AMD bought out ATI.

You could check on the Phoronix forums to see what they suggest; otherwise, it's just a waiting game to see when updated drivers get released.
