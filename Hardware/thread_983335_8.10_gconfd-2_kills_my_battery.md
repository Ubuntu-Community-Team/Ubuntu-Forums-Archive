```
$ ps aux | grep gconfd-2
jarek     5639 29.2  0.2   8040  5004 ?        R    21:53   4:33 /usr/lib/libgconf2-4/gconfd-2
```

Anybody noticed such things?

[EDIT]

For reference, here's launchpad bug report: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gconf/+bug/293535](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gconf/+bug/293535) - removing stale configuration from ~/.config made the gconfd to behave normally.
