### Post by Dthdealer on 2008-10-06
You may need to enable the power saving features from the GNOME toolbar (System-->Preferences-->Power Management). Also try dimming you monitor - the largest power-consumer of an LCD is its backlight which determines how bright it is.
You may also be running frivolous background applications that are using CPU powers, therefore increasing battery consumption.
Finally, have you changed battery or not used the computer for a while? The battery itself may be in the end of its lifecycle if its several years old.
If all else fails, try a BIOS update. It make allow more power-saving features.
