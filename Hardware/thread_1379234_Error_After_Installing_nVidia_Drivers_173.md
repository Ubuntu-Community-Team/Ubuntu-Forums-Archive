### Post by m374nch0714 on 2010-01-12
My problem is solved!!!!
I install the version 96 of nvidia driver through System>Preferences>Hardware Drivers.
I rebooted and got a resolution of 1024x768. I go to NVIDIA X-Server Settings and set it to 1280x1024 and saved the settings to the X configuration file.
Then i installed the NVIDIA 173 through Hardware Drivers again, after rebooting, it was a successful install! No more black screen or Xserver/Xorg Errors!! And my resolution is 1280x1024.. I backed up my Xorg.conf so that if any problems again regarding Xserver, i have a back up..
