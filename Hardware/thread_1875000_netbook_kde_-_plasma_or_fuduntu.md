### Post by mtron on 2011-11-04
plasma-netbook is horrible buggy on oneiric, the plasma widgets crash often and the activity overlay just freezes here...

fuduntu is a good choice. Though keep in mind that fuduntu is fedora based. So you won't get the bounties from the ubuntu ecosystem (huge community, PPAs, ubuntu file sync, ect.) 

On the other hand fuduntu is pre-configured with a very nice theme and has some power saving tweaks enabled by default, so you save yourself some hours of tweaking. 

If you want to stick with ubuntu go with gnome-shell. I use it on my 10" netbook and it rocks :)
