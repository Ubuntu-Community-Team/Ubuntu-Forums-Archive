### Post by Hobbsilla on 2009-11-14
I was curious to know how and if it was possible to restore lost data on an external harddrive one vector at a time rather than all at once. I used photorec to try and recover my data after accidentally formating my harddrive and it was only able to restore about +50% (120GB out of 220GB) of the data that was once on my harddrive. When my harddrive was reformated the only thing that was put on it was about 1GB of things that were needed to create a USB start disc with Ubuntu 9.10. When I ran the process it seemed like it was going to take close to 6 hours but two hours in (half way) it just took off and was done within a matter of minutes which has raised some doubts on the recovery process.
