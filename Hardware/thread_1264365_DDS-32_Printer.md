to use over your lan 
system>administration>printing>new>LPD/LPR host or printer

Host=printer IP address
Queue=postscript

for drivers use
generic postscript or
Hatachi DDP 70 or
Talley ML450


will print and duplex from the printer default source [paper]

for win 7 use the IBM info print 32 [or 40] PS driver
