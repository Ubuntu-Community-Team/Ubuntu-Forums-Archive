### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-08-07
If you want good Linux compatibility, consider buying a laptop with Linux preinstalled. System76 and Zareazon are some of the popular options if you are in North America. HP has its ProBook line available with SuseLinux in some countries. Amazon may also have some available.
