When moving files over a wireless network, the wireless signal would interfere with my Logitech MX Revolution mouse and cause the arrow pointer to stutter.  The mouse would also "reset" itself as my wheel click settings would be lost and I would have to use revoco again.

In my case, changing the channel on my router from 6 to 11 resolved the issue for me.
