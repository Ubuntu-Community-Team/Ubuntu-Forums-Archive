### Post by Fafler on 2010-08-09
You shouldn't experience that problem more than once. There are binary drivers from Nvidia and ATI, which aren't installed by default because of licensing issues (some people don´t want non-free software on their Linux box, just like other people don't want pickles in their hamburgers). All other graphics drivers are open source and already included in your installation.
