### Post by gordintoronto on 2016-03-16
What is the resolution of the TV? If it's a "Full HD" model, that's not a lot of pixels by the time you split it in four.

Most HD TVs have HDMI input, so there are many, many video cards which could be used - including my seven-year-old Nvidia 9400 GT.

I predict that you would be much happier with two 21-inch "1080P" monitors, and, once again, there are many video cards which can drive such a setup - including my seven-year-old Nvidia 9400 GT.
