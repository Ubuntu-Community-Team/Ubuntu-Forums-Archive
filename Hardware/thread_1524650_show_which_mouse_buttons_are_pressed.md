### Post by MountainX on 2010-07-05
In Kubuntu there is a setting under [Mouse > Advanced]("http://grok.lsu.edu/Article.aspx?articleId=5717") for "mouse wheel scrolls by:" X lines (enter a number of lines). Mine was 3 and I changed it to 7 lines. This is how many lines are scrolled for each wheel click.
