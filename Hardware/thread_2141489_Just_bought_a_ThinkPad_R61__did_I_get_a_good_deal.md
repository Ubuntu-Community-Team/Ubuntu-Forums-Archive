Hello :)

Mine has an Atheros "AR58XB63", but as for the machine being picky about wifi cards, have you heard of "Middleton BIOS"? There are restrictions which IBM/Lenovo imposed on certain Thinkpad hardware, which Middleton BIOS completely removes, including the aforementioned wifi blacklist. I've flashed it into mine, and it's working a treat:

[http://forum.notebookreview.com/lenovo/459591-t61-x61-sata-ii-1-5-gb-s-cap-willing-pay-solution-8.html](http://forum.notebookreview.com/lenovo/459591-t61-x61-sata-ii-1-5-gb-s-cap-willing-pay-solution-8.html)
