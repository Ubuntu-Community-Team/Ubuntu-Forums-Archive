### Post by snurfle on 2011-12-20
DsipcalGUI and Argyll do a great job of calibrating my LCD monitor, and a pretty good job of calibrating my CRT.

I don't know for absolute certainty that they will work with an LED monitor, but the LUT settings apply to the video card, not the monitor itself, so I would be terribly surprised if they did not work correctly.
