### Post by Autodave on 2019-03-12
I have seen several computers where the power supply just wasn't able to operate the mouse, but everything else worked.  If you still can find a non-lazer mouse (the ones with the ball in them) you could try that.  IF it works that way, then you power supply is the issue.

Another thing to try is a powered USB hub.  Only plug in the mouse and try.  Again, if that works suspect your power supply.
