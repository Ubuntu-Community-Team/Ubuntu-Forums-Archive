### Post by firecat53 on 2008-06-16
Hey. I've got the dv6058. On both Gutsy and Hardy, I could not get hibernation to work. However, with the nvidia drivers installed (doesn't work with the stock nv driver), suspend works great! Just go into your power management preferences and make sure all the options are set to suspend instead of hibernate. 

Every so often it will wake up with a white screen, but just type your password and hit enter and it will come back!

Good luck!
Scott
