### Post by Fafler on 2010-03-30
The only ones with dedicated graphics are the ones based on the Nvidia ION chipsset. They are MUCH faster than their GMA950 counterpart and are able to decode 720p video, which the normal Atom/GMA950 combo can't do. You should definitely go for one of thoose or wait for the new ION2 platform.

Most netbooks are based on pretty much the same hardware and are well supported by Ubuntu.
