### Post by corrytonapple on 2011-05-14
Here is what he is talking about:
[http://aboutgadgets.net/2009/02/benq-announces-nscreen-i91/](http://aboutgadgets.net/2009/02/benq-announces-nscreen-i91/)
Where it is like a car stereo with the on/off and volume button.  You could try the keyboard shortcuts menu by going to **System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts**.  There, for the volume up key, click the volume up row, then move the volume up using the knob.  Then, do the same thing for volume down.  If it does not work, then maybe you can just adjust the volume by a keyboard shortcut, such as F11 and F12.
