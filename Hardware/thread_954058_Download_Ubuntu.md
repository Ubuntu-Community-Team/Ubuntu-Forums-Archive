### Post by JoeLaX75 on 2008-10-20
I use a program called LeechGet for Windows.  It is a download manager that allows you to resume downloads and splits them up in to separate pieces so that your file downloads faster.  I believe you can find it by searching for it on download.com.

There is also a way that you can have a copy sent to your house for free (no shipping costs or anything).  It is called Shipit.
[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/shipit-faq](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/shipit-faq)
The downside it it takes 6 to 10 weeks.
