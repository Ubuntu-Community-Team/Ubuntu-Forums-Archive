### Post by Will_munns on 2009-04-17
Hacking with scotchtape:

Remove wireless card:

[http://www.flickr.com/photos/will-and-becky/3008846380/](http://www.flickr.com/photos/will-and-becky/3008846380/)

Identify pin 20, and cover with a sliver of tape
[http://www.flickr.com/photos/will-and-becky/3008846626/](http://www.flickr.com/photos/will-and-becky/3008846626/)
[http://www.flickr.com/photos/will-and-becky/3008008211/](http://www.flickr.com/photos/will-and-becky/3008008211/)

Bingo, wireless kill switch disabled for good (or at least, till someone fixes the hot key and the tape can be removed)
