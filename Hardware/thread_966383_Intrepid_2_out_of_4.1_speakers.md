I have an onboard soundcard and 4.1 speakers. I initially had sound coming from only 2 speakers after install. Then I turned "surround" on in the volume control, chose "duplicate front" to be on under switches, and changed the channel mode to 4ch under options tab. They are all under the volume control gadget. If these options are not visible to you by default (mine were hidden) then click on preferences to make them visible first. Good luck.
