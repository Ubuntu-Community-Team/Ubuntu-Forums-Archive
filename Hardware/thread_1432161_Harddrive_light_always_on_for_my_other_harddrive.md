### Post by cascade9 on 2010-03-18
Its more than possible that dropping your laptop will kill the hdd. 

If it is dead, how to get the data back? You will either have to pay somebody to recover the data, or do it yourself. That will involve finding the exactly same HDD, pulling both the new and the old HDDs apart, then swapping the platters from the old HDD to the new HDD. 

Not fun unless you have a good idea what you are doing, have the right tools and have good fine motor skills.
