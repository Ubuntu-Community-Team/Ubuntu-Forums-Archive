I just got a new ThinkPad X1 Carbon 6th generation, and Ubuntu doesn't seem to see my fingerprint reader at all.

I filed a bug about the fact that attempting to enable fingerprint login caused GDM to break ([https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/pam/+bug/1817897](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/pam/+bug/1817897)), but that devolved into a discussion of whether the fingerprint reader in my laptop is supported by Ubuntu in the first place.

Ubuntu doesn't seem to be recognizing the fingerprint reader, but when I tell the BIOS to reset the fingerprint reader it says that it succeeded, which implies that the fingerprint reader is visible to the BIOS, just not for some reason by Ubuntu.

Has anyone else gotten their fingerprint reader to work in a recent ThinkPad X1 Carbon, and if so, did you have to do anything special to make it work?
