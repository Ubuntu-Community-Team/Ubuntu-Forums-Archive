### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-04-09
> **Zettaichi said:**
> ... I want to be able to play windows game without the lag, like wow and upcoming D3...

Whether AMD or Nvidia, you're going to need the restricted drivers installed to get decent frame rates in Windows games.

You should first check the WineHQ site, application compatibility page, to check out the ratings of the games you want to play.

If they are rated below Gold, they're not going to work well -- regardless of the video card you have installed.
