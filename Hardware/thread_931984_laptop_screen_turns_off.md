### Post by chainofcommand02 on 2008-09-27
Sounds like a bad backlight. This usually means that you need to replace the entire LCD panel on your laptop. Had you noticed your display colored a brick red when you first power it on and then warming up to its normal colors? That is sometimes an early warning sign of a CCFL going bad.
