So I was looking at all this Wii remote mouse business. Most of them require using the accelerometers as inputs and tilting back and forth trying to click out of ff because your mom found your pr0n. There are others out there but you still need that ever annoying sensor bar plugged into your Wii. Am i going to bring my Wii with my laptop when I go to the coffee shop showing off to my new geek friend that i can play the wikipedia game from across the room while having the sleek robust and orgasmic feel of a quality nintendo in my hand? No. I could bring two candles and set the mood for proposing with the candelabra. I think that someone out there with one of those fancy Eee's should consider this mod. Frankly, I'm not a chinese 5 year old so I have no chance of typing anything on an Eee so I wouldn't have one to experiment with, would I? This is by no means a step by step, merely a suggestion. Get your hands on some surface mount leds, like these [http://www.optekinc.com/viewparts.aspx?categoryID=24]("http://www.optekinc.com/viewparts.aspx?categoryID=24") make sure the wavelength is around 940nm that's what the Wiimote is tuned for. You'll need about 4? Sap some voltage from the webcam if you haven't already. It's a standard 5v usb and wire 'em in series with some resistors till your equations work out. dremel out slots in your screen plastic and get some fancy dark plastic to put over the whole ordeal. remember two leds on each side. add bt and congratulations you have Eee with built in Wiimote support, now every old person and toddler will want three.
