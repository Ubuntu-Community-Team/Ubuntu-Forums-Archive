### Post by benmoran on 2010-06-30
The fuse won't have any bearing on how much current is getting though. It's simply a fail-safe in case there is a short elsewhere in the circuit. The idea is that the fuse will blow (basically melt) before anything else does. You should use whatever fuse the supply is rated for. If it's too high, it won't serve it's purpose. Too low, and it will blow under normal operation.
