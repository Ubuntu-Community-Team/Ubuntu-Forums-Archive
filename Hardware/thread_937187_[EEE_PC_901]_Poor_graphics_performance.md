### Post by BlackWolf90 on 2008-11-13
Launch Firefox.

Select "Edit" -> "Preferences".

Click the "Privacy" tab.

Uncheck the following:

 - Keep my history for xx days
 - Remember what I enter in forms and the search bar
 - Remember what I've downloaded

With those items unchecked, the browser will no longer temporarily freeze, every time a page loads.
