### Post by Kevbert on 2008-08-17
There are three main types of display cards nowadays in general use (excluding ISA which is virtually obsolete).  These are AGP, PCI and PCIe (express). With both PCIe and AGP there are also bus speeds to take into consideration. 
There should be information on what your motherboard supports in the user handbook.  If not it may be worth googling online for your motherboard details.
With regards to compiz, you want to go for an ATI, nVidia or Intel display card as these are best supported.  Stay clear of Via cards as these are not.
