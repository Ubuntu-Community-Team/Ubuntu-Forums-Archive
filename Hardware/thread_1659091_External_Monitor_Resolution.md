### Post by Crazedpsyc on 2011-01-03
Yes, that is what happened when I used that dell monitor.
Open up System>Administration>Monitors (Actually, it might be under Preferences, I don't have the default menu)
And it should show a yellowish rectangle for each monitor. Your main netbook monitor should be labeled something like "Laptop" or "Netbook". Click on the other one. Make sure "Same image on all monitors" is unchecked, and try changing the resolution to something fit for your external.
