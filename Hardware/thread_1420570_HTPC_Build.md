### Post by DownTown22 on 2010-03-03
I recently (end of last year) built an HTPC as well.

My only recommendation would be to use a dedicated video card, not one that is part of the motherboard.  That way, the video card has dedicated RAM and isn't using your system RAM.

And on that note, I like RAM...I threw in 4GB in mine and it's super snappy.  I've never used A-Data before so I really can't comment on the brand, but my pics are OCZ and GSkill - they haven't let me down yet.

Also, I like having power supplies that are at least 500W, typically I go with 600W.
