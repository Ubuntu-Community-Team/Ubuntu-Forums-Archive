### Post by HalPomeranz on 2008-05-19
The write-ups at [**http://tuxmobil.org/mylaptops.html**](**http://tuxmobil.org/mylaptops.html**) are useful for figuring out who's had the easiest time of getting Linux installed on various vendors' laptops.  I encourage you to pick a laptop that seems to work best for your needs, and then check TuxMobil and see if Linux runs well on it.

That being said, Lenovo/IBM and Dell laptops seem to have good Linux compatibility.  If you buy the "latest and greatest" new hardware from any vendor, expect some things to not work without some manual effort on your part.  Personally, I run Ubuntu on my Lenovo X61s and everything basically worked as soon as I installed the OS.
