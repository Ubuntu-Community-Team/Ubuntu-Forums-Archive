### Post by cascade9 on 2010-08-09
If everything is equal, a desktop would be faster. 3.5'' desktop HDDs are faster than laptop 2.5'' (or smaller) drives, desktops tend to have faster CPUs, faster RAM, etc. 

That said, a laptop with an SSD will boot faster than a desktop with a HDD, but thats not anywhere near equal ;)
