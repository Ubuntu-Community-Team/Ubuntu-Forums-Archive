### Post by Favux on 2010-02-08
Hi akshay.sulakhe,

The "HP tx2 1050au" is one of what we are calling the TX2z series.  It has a N-trig 'digitizer' which provides both digitizer (stylus/pen) function and multi-touch.

The TX2500 has a Wacom digitizer sandwiched with a touch screen.  The touch is single finger touch.

Both have AMD CPU's and ATI video.  They shouldn't have the overheating or WiFi issues of the TX1000's.

The TX1000, the first generation, has only a touch screen.
