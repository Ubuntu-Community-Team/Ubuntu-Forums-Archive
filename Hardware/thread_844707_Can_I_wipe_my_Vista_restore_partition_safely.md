### Post by mtbsoft on 2008-06-30
I recently got an HP laptop for my son which came with Vistugh, it had a mechanism installed which enabled you to create restore disks (using 2 DVDs).  Fortunately, I created the disks before wiping the hard drive and dual booting XP (for his games) and Hardy.  I say fortunately because I couldn't get all the drivers for XP (only Vistugh, b****y HP) and ended up putting Vistugh back on it and persuading the retailer to take it back as their display model (I was lucky enough that they hadn't unpacked their official one).
