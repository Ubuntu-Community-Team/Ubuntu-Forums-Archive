### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2014-01-18
AMD's open source driver is nice for older AMD cards (RadeonHD 6000 and earlier), but it's got some catching up to do on recent generations with the GCN core.
So if it comes down to running binary drivers, nvidia's the easy choice. For the price of a R7 260X, you could get a GTX 650 Ti (and make sure it's a Ti because the vanilla GTX 650 is just a glorified GT640).
