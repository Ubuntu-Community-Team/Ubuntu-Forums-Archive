### Post by Insomnautik on 2008-10-08
When my external hardrive turns on and mounts itself ubuntu opens a window showing me the contents of the drive. how do I disable automatically displaying that window?

---

### Post by Partyboi2 on 2008-10-09
Open up nautilus (places>computer) and go to "edit" then "preferences" select the media tab and untick "browse media when inserted"
