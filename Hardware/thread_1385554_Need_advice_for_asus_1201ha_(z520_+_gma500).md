### Post by BrandonG777 on 2010-03-20
It's the worst possible laptop you can buy to run Ubuntu on. GMA500=PowerVR chip not an Intel chip which means no driver support from Intel. PowerVR has always been infamous for not supporting Linux. They showed off this combo playing Quake @ 1080P in Linux (Mobllin) but has since never updated their drivers. People get it working and then the next kernel update breaks everything again. DO NOT BUY ANYTHING WITH GMA500!!!! Pay the extra money and get the ASUS 1201N with the Nvidia chipset, which has the best Linux support around IMO.
