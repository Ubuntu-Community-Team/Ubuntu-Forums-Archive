### Post by Da CalebMan on 2010-01-29
Hey guys, I have an older Geforce 5600 FX 128MB AGP. For some reason, whenever I do anything graphical or 3D, such as, playing games like: Hedgewars, Nexuiz, or Sauerbraten, I get fuzzy lines almost zigg-zaggy, but a little more curved, on the sides of my screen. That happens about 5-10 mins after doing something graphically resource hungry. If you can manage to still play when the fuzzy lines appear, 5 mins after, the screen goes black for about 10 seconds, and will keep doing so, until it just stays like that.

At first I thought it was an OpenGL issue. Because in Nexuiz, when I untick the box that says, "Use OpenGL 2.0 shaders" The fuzzy lines freeze for a second then start again. But then I wondered if it was a refresh rate issue. I have set it to 60hz, and disabled vsync. Last of all, it may just be overheating. But I'm quite a newbie so I would hardly know if this is an overheating symptom.

Please help!

### Post by cascade9 on 2010-01-29

1- check the temp in nvidia-settings

2- the old 'deskfan pointing into the case with sidepanels off' trick.
