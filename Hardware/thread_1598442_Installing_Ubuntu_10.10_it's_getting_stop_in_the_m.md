### Post by P4man on 2010-10-16
If the drive was actually formatted and then data was written to it, then thats a lost cause. You can try with testdisk:
[http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk](http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk)
(works from a livecd) but I dont give you much chance.

More likely however, if you tried installing ubuntu over an existing ubuntu install and failed, you didnt format anything and all your data should still be there. Just mount the drive from the livecd (probably automatically done in a live session,  just look in places mneu or system > admin > disk utility).
