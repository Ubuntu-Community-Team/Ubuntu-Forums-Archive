### Post by cascade9 on 2011-02-12
Its pretty uncommon to have problems with internal CD/DVD drives. 

Buffalo- I dont really know them (I've only heard of them because of the CSIRO taking them to court), IODATA- never heard of them. LG- has been around for ages. Not the greatest for CD/DVD drives though. LiteON- is huge (they do a lot of parts for other companies). 

BTW, there are only a few DVD drive factories around, a lot of drives are just rebranded. I wouldnt be suprised if those 4 drifferent brands you listed are sourced from just 2 factories.  

If itwas just based on brand, I'd go for LiteON......if I couldnt find a few extra dollars (or yen in this case) to get a pioneer drive, or some other branded drive that is built at least in part by pioneer.
