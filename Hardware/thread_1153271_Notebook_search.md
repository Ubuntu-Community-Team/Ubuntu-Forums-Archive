### Post by raymondh on 2009-05-08
I use an MSI wind.  With 9.04, wi-fi works out of the box.  My MSI has the updated bios which allows me to overclock from stock to +24% ghz .... ok for games... although I had to flash the BIOS to get this.

The Dell mini 12 looks nice and comfortable for touch-typing... however will cost more than your budget.

I suggest you try tthe keyboard, trackpad and the screensize with a couple of websites.  Get the one you're most comfortable with.  All other specs would be similar, if not the same.
