### Post by tcfan on 2009-04-23
I have a Compaq Presario CQ50-215NR and to put it nicely, the trackpad is annoying when I type.  I find that the cursor gets moved and I end up inserting letters into words I've previously typed.  I know in Vista I can disable the trackpad with little effort, but wasn't sure if there was a way to do it in Ubuntu.

---

### Post by Synthros on 2009-04-23
Absolutely.  If you go to System-->Preferences-->Mouse, there should be a tab within called "Touchpad".  Go to that tab and uncheck "Enable Touchpad", and you should be all set.
