To answer the original question, the j110 and j110a are supported, but you must be careful not to get the older models (Deskjet 1000cse or 1000cxi): [http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/deskjet.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/deskjet.html)
