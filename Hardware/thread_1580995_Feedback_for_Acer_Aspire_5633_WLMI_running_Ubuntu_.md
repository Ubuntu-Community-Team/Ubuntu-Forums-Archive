### Post by XenRobot on 2010-09-24
Hi All,

I successfully installed Ubuntu 10.04 from a disc yesterday onto this laptop 
and it works straight out of the box. Excellent Job Ubuntu team - thanks for making the installation so simple :)

**To Test**: I haven't tested the USB ports yet to see if flash devices work

**Problems**: The touchpad mouse froze for about 2 mins as soon as I activated the wireless card, that was a one off and after pressing several random buttons, fixed itself and has worked fine since.

**My Own Installation Method**: 
1. Downloaded ISO image (onto a different PC for space reasons)  and burned onto a DVD
2. Downloaded Active@ KillDisk Free and killed all partitions and wiped data off ACER 5633 WLMi Laptop (I only did this because I didn't need any of the existing data)
3. Rebooted laptop with Ubuntu DVD - followed instructions.

I look forward to playing with Ubuntu. Hope you have similar success.
