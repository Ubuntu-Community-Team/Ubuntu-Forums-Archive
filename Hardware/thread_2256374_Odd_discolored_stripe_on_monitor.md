### Post by papibe on 2014-12-11
Hi bejoe905.

If I have to guess, it may be a problem with the video driver. I wouldn't worry about it since it will be easy to solve after you install and boot into the system.

Another solution would be to select 'Try Ubuntu' instead of directly installing it. Once you are in the desktop, you may try to look for additional video drivers by opening 'Additional Drivers'. If that solves the problem, you may initiate the install from there by clicking on the 'Install' icon on the desktop.

Hope it helps. Let us know how it goes.
