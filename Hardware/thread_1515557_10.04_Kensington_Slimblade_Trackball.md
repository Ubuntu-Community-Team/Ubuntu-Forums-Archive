### Post by rbellamy on 2010-06-22
When trying to remap the buttons on a Kensington Slimblade Trackball so that the top-left button is "Back" and top-right is "Forward", you have to use a /usr/lib/X11/xorg.conf.d snippet.

Hal doesn't work - deprecated in 10.04.
Udev doesn't work - x11_options not supported.

*/usr/lib/X11/xorg.conf.d/10-kensington-slimblade.conf*
```
Section "InputClass"
  Identifier "Kensington Slimblade Trackball"
  MatchProduct "Kensington Kensington Slimblade Trackball"
  Option "ButtonMapping" "1 8 3 4 5 6 7 9 2 10 11 12"
EndSection
```
