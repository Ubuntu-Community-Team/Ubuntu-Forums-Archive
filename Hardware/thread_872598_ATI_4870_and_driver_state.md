### Post by markbuntu on 2008-07-28
The linux driver 8.6 was released with the 4870 card by ATI though many resellers chose not to include it on their installation cd. 

ATI has their own closed beta testing program for linux drivers so by the time they are released they are very stable. They expect their linux drivers to achieve parity with their windows drivers sometime in the next six months. The newest 8.7 driver has the Catalyst AI and crossfire support already.
