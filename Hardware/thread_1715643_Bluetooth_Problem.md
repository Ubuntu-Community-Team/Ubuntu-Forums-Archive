### Post by gpdas on 2011-03-28
Thanks to those who have taken interest on this topic.
However I could solve the problem as follows.
1) Click on Bluetooth Icon on the top panel.
2) Go to Preferences
3) Remove all Devices already exist.
4) Click on Add New Device.
5) Now my phone is detected and I paired them.
The problem is solved.
