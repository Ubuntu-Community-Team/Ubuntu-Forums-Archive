### Post by Gerry07 on 2011-03-12
I spent all day trying to get MYTHTV working with my HVR 900 USB Tuner(DVB-T) and I gave up. I then found Me TV in the "Applications-Ubuntu Software Center-Get Software-Sound and Video" and having uninstalled Mythtv installed ME TV. Within 5 minutes of installing ME TV it had found all available channels both digital and terrestrial with even an EPG to boot and I was watching TV on my PC screen. Excellent piece of software. Thanks to the developer.
