### Post by josephellengar on 2010-06-25
I was robbed!  I bought the 160gb G2 Intel X25-m as soon as it came out.  And as soon I got my new laptop I installed said hard drive.  Since then, I have been amazed at the speed, responsiveness, battery life of the laptop, among other things.  Two days ago, the drive bricked so yesterday I RMA'd it and sent it back to intel and installed the original 7200 rpm drive that came with the laptop.  Performance changed by very little.  Battery life by >5%.  I noticed no change in responsiveness.  Even loading times in games didn't change all that much.  Really, I won't be buying another SSD until they are standard.  So, was this a unique experience or does anybody else share my feelings?
