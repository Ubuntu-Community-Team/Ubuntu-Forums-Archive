### Post by cariboo on 2009-07-04
Just swap the video cards and reboot. If you get a black screen on reboot, restart in recovery mode and at the menu select xfix. Once xfxix is finished type exit at the prompt and select resume. This should get you back to the desktop, where you can go to System-->Administration-->Hardware drivers, and enable the restricted drivers.

BTW in my opinion you would be better off getting an newer Nvidia card, as they just seem to work.
