### Post by cascade9 on 2011-02-14
I'd agree. it should be fine. As long as the fans at the back are sucking air out of the case anyway. 

If you are worried, you can check the HDD temps. I do it with the ever reliable 'finger', if the HDD is hot to touch then its running to hot, if its body temp or lower its fine.  

Looks like there is a fan mount in front of the HDD rack as well, if its running hot just install a fan in there.
