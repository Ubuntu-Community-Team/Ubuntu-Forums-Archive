### Post by NDboyMB on 2012-03-23
It's part of the TinyHPC Cluster Project by Mukaarram Tahir.  You can see it here.

[http://www.mukarramtahir.com/lab.html](http://www.mukarramtahir.com/lab.html)

Basically it is a bunch of motherboards powered by power supplies used as nodes and he uses a master computer as the master node.

You can do the same thing if you find a business that is going out of business and you can buy up like 5-10 desktops cheep in a bundle or lot.

You can also if you can get your hands on them, use the new Raspberry Pi boards which are like $25 each.  Those can be found here.

[http://www.raspberrypi.org/](http://www.raspberrypi.org/)
