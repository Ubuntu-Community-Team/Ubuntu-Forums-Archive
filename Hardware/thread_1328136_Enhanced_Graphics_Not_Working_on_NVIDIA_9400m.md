### Post by bernardfrancois on 2009-11-16
If you go to 'System > Administration > Hardware Drivers', you get a window where you can manage proprietary / third party drivers.

Normally, you should see the nvidia driver in the list. If it's not yet activated, you can click it and then click the 'activate' button.
