### Post by Fafler on 2010-04-03
Probably not. But with RAID1 you won't get any real performance gain with hardware RAID versus software RAID.

I would just go with software RAID and keep it that way. That way, you won't be out of luck when your controller breaks and you need to get your data out some other way.
