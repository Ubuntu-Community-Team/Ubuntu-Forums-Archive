### Post by sigve on 2008-05-03
It has for a long time been hard/impossible to get laptops with ATI cards (at least some of them) to properly suspend to RAM. Mine included. After updating to Hardy Heron it has been possible, but the computer would wake up immediately after suspending, making it just as useless. Yesterday I updated my BIOS firmware to version 1.11c and finally it works!!!!

I know that this BIOS-upgrade is available for several of the Fujitsu-Siemens laptops - so maybe it's worth a try if your computer won't suspend to RAM!

[URL="http://www.fujitsu-siemens.com/support/downloads.html"]
Fujitsu-Siemens driver downloads[/URL]
