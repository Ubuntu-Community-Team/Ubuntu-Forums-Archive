### Post by oldfred on 2020-05-25
I liked pcpart picker. Did not use suggested vendors as I ordered everything from one.
But it saved me from ordering the wrong drive. I was building a SFF system and picked a standard 3.5" drive. It said I needed a laptop type since case was smaller.
[https://pcpartpicker.com/list/](https://pcpartpicker.com/list/)

You can also see others with similar builds and estimates of prices.

I regularly read this site to see comparisions and whats new.
[https://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=phoronix_articles](https://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=phoronix_articles)
And that has a test site where users post results based on their system.
[https://openbenchmarking.org/](https://openbenchmarking.org/)
