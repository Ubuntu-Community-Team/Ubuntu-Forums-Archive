### Post by baytuni on 2008-12-04
solved the problem. It turned out that Turkish Alt-Q was not the one that I look for. I took a close look to the map and I figured out that Turkish Q (with dead keys) was the one. &#305; removed alt+q and put that one. Also &#305; added Turkish as a language. So everything works now.
