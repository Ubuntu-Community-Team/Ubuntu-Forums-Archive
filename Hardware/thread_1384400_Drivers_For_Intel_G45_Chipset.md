### Post by argosreality on 2010-01-18
The intel graphics driver is installed when you install Ubuntu. Its an open driver so no additional steps need to be done to get it up and running

EDIT: Having said that, Intel graphics are never fast and there's been plenty of issues relating to performance regressions with Intel IGP drivers in the past year or so so dont expect to play alot of high end games
