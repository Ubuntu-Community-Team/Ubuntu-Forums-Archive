### Post by reiki on 2008-05-28
For some reason Screens and Resolution is not visible by default in Hardy. If you go into the the System -> Preferences -> Main Menu (the menu manager... I think I have the path right, but you may have to hunt around... it might be in Administration rather than Preferences...) ...

When it opens go to "Other" and tick the box for Screens and Resolution. That will add it to your menu.
