Most of what you want doesn't take that much power. 

1- Very light (unless you like running firefox with 20+ tabs open, and that uses ram far more than CPU)
2- Very light again
3- Depends on the games you want to play
4- This is something that CPU power would really help with.  
5- 'future proof' with computers is impossible. That best you really get is 'upgradeable'. 
6- Again, light (this can depend on what exactly you want to do) 
7- Super-light!  

Try to get a decent case and power supply (not generic junk), monitor, keyboard and mouse. Those bits tend to hang around for several upgrades.

That lot would come to aprox. $750, so you've got a bit left over for monitor, keyboard, mouse and speakers.
