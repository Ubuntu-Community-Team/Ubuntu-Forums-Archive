### Post by TBABill on 2010-10-27
If you can get the MSI for the price you stated it is a much faster processor. The Intel Core i3 is a pretty fast chip. I have the Core i3-330 and I really like it. Having the GPU on the CPU chip seems to be a pretty good answer to the video card/cpu/motherboard issue and it works well with all distros I have tried. Only thing I would recommend is try to make sure your wireless brand and model are well supported, and same for the sound.
