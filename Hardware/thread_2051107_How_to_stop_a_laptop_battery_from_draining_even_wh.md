### Post by Pusalieth on 2012-11-01
This is actually more probable to be the transformer rather than the operating system. Grab a multimeter that you own or go buy one from Radioshack for maybe $20 - $30, and check the amperage/voltage coming from the transformer. If you not to familiar with electronics use the manual with the multimeter, and make sure to use the 10amp setting, and the leads needs to break the positive current lead in series.

Otherwise it shouldn't be the exHDDs hooked up, as most are 10 watts, and more transformers are capable of 70-80 watts.
