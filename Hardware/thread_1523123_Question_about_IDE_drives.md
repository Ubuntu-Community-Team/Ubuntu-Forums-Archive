### Post by Yarui on 2010-07-03
I remember when I first started using SATA drives I couldn't believe how much faster they seemed than IDE drives, but that was mainly for copying files and stuff like that.  I don't remember ever noticing any huge difference in game performance, most of the speed in games comes from data in memory.  If you noticed any major difference in speed it would likely be in load times, since that is when data is being transferred from the hard drive into memory.  When it comes to gameplay, though, I don't think you will have anything to worry about.
