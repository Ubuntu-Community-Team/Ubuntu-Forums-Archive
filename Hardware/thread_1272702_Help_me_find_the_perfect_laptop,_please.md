### Post by swoody on 2009-09-22
HP doesn't say who manufacturers the 802.11 g wireless card on the laptop, so if you were planning on going with one of those, we wouldn't be able to tell what's in it until you can get your hands on it. They also don't give much information on the microphone, or the webcam besides "HP Pavilion Webcam". After a little bit of Googling, it seems that many people are having success with their Pavilion webcams. Don't take this as the end-all-be-all, but I did find more positive search results than I did issues. Again, I can't speak for much on this particular computer, but hopefully you'll get lucky and someone can chime in here who owns the laptop.

If you do find out more info on the specs of the laptop, you may want to try searching in the Linux and Ubuntu Hardware Compatibility Lists. I checked, and there were no reviews so far on this specific laptop.

[http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/](http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/)

[http://www.linux-drivers.org/](http://www.linux-drivers.org/)
