


I also noticed that there are several backups of the xorg.conf, but it looks like they are just the resolution getting upgraded. However, you should check if the xorg was changed for your upgrade:

```
/etc/X11/$ diff -u xorg.conf xorg.conf.dist-upgrade-*
```
Or to check all of the differences:
```
/etc/X11/$ for a in `ls xorg.conf.*`; do diff -u xorg.conf $a ; done
```
