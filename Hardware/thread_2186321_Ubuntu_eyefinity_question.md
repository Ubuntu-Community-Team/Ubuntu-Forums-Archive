### Post by QIII on 2013-11-07
OK.  Sorry about the wait.  Real life, you know.

In CCC, under display manager, select (Multi-Display pick Multi-display desktop with display(s)..."


[ATTACH=CONFIG]247628[/ATTACH]

With three monitors, you'll have to do this twice to get all three split out.

Arrange your monitors as you want them and reboot.

Let me know if you have any problems and we'll work from there.
