### Post by Fafler on 2010-08-28
It depends on your graphics card. With a new Nvidia or ATI card, use the setup utility provided with the driver (i'm actually not 100% sure ATI provides one) otherwise use xrandr. [http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Xorg_RandR_1.2](http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Xorg_RandR_1.2)
