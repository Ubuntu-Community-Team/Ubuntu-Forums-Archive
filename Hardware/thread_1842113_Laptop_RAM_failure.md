### Post by 2F4U on 2011-09-11
It is almost impossible to say whether the RAM was defective from the beginning or not. What I can say from my experience is that RAM is relatively unimpressed by overheating, i.e. some other components would get problems before the RAM chips. So unless you touched the RAM chips with your fingers I would assume that it was defective from the beginning.
