### Post by zero244 on 2009-02-07
Ive been having Hard lockups with Hardy from day one.

No mouse or keyboard input. It took a hard shutdown to get back.
The lockups were random, and never right after bootup.

It could lockup just moving the mouse around. It might run 10 hours or 10 minutes before a lockup.

At first I thought it was video drivers.
Then I thought it might be pulseaudio related.

Some people are having lockups but still have mouse and keyboard access.
I finally cleared the video and pulseaudio out of the equation.

It finally came to mind maybe it was my wireless keyboard and mouse.

So I unplugged the USB connector and used adapters for PS\2

It has been three days and I haven't had a single lockup.

Up in till the time I switched to PS\2 connections I was having a lockup about once or twice a day.

So if this fix holds that should be the last hurdle to get Hardy stable.

So if your having mouse and keyboard lockups you might try going PS\2.
