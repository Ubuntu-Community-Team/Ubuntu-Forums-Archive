### Post by Twizzle on 2008-08-02
I am about ready to setup my home server and want to install it on a RAID system. I have made the decision to use hardware RAID as opposed to software and have been looking at two controller cards.

Before I rush out and buy, I was wondering if any one had any experience with either and / or could offer advice on which is best.

They are:

[Adaptec 1210SA]("http://www.microdirect.co.uk/(3719)Adaptec-1210SA-Serial-ATA-RAIDcontroller-kit.aspx")

and 

[MRI-SATA/4CR]("http://www.microdirect.co.uk/(15174)MRI-4-Port-Serial-ATA-Adaptor4-Channel-RAID.aspx")

As I see it the MRI has the advantage of being four ports so offering more options for any future upgrade. The disadvantage is that it says nothing about working under Linux

The Adaptec specifically claims to work under Linux (but not Ubuntu but I am sure it will) but only has two ports.

There is only about £2 in the price difference too!
