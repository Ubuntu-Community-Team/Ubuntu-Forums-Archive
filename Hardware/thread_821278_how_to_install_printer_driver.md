Hi...

According to linuxprinting.org, your particular printer works with CUPS but the driver (on your CD) is recommended. Please see this page...

[http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Samsung-SCX-4521F](http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Samsung-SCX-4521F)

Plug in your printer and first see if there is a listing for it. Your version of Ubuntu may already have the driver for it included "out of the box." If not...

Copy the driver from the CD to your Desktop and extract it. Does it have a command line installer?
