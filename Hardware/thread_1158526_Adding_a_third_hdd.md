### Post by logos34 on 2009-05-13
two connectors max per cable--master (black), slave (grey), and blue to motherboard.

To add the 80 gb to the mix you'll either have to buy an IDE/ATA Host controller card (plugs into one of the slots), providing an additional 1-2 channels, or else get an IDE-to-USB adapter. (~ $20).  Or else put it in an external usb case.  An ide-to-sata converter is also an option if you have sata support
