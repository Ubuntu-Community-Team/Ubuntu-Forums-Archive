### Post by Ayuthia on 2008-04-25
If I recall correctly, kernels 2.6.24 or greater need to use a version of NDISwrapper greater than 1.50.  I am thinking that might be what is causing your compile problem.  You might try installing ndiswrapper-common and ndiswrapper-utils-1.9 from the live CD.
