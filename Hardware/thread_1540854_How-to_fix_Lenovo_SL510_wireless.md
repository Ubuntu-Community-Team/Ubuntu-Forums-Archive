### Post by WA1DH on 2010-07-28
Bought a new Lenovo SL510 laptop. Everything worked out of the box in 10.04 except for a wireless issue. I was getting 60% signals and disconnects in the same room as my router. If you have the Realtek 8172 chipset on your wireless adapter (check lspci), the problem is solved by installing the latest driver for the 8192SE chipset from the Realtek website.

Just a heads up for anyone searching for a solution to this problem. Drove me crazy for a day, I thought there was something wrong with my laptop.
