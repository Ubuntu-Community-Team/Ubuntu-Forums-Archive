I'm experiencing this on Ubuntu MATE 20.04 currently, but have also seen this on Kubuntu 20.04 & Xubuntu 20.04 on previous installs/from live USB.

Multi-touch functionality works perfectly after a reboot: two-finger & three finger right-click/middle-click, plus two-finger scrolling both vertically and horizontally. I configured this with the built-in Ubuntu MATE Mouse Preferences dialog.

If I suspend the laptop, at least some of this functionality stops working. Most of the time, two/three-finger click only works on the right-hand side of the touchpad. Sometimes two/three-finger click doesn't work at all. Two-finger scroll also sometimes stops working, or works but less reliably. I can reproduce this issue every time I reboot and then suspend/wake.
