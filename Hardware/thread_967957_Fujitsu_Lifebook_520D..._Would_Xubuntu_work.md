### Post by Zill on 2008-11-02
The simple answer is no, it won't run Xubuntu!

Check out [http://www.xubuntu.org/get]("http://www.xubuntu.org/get") and you will see that it is far short of the minimum system requirements.

I suggest you take a look at [DSL]("http://www.damnsmalllinux.org/").

This is an excellent distro that is well suited to low-spec machines.
