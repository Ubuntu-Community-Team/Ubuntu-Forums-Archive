### Post by Paddy Landau on 2010-06-21
I see no one has replied to you yet.

I don't know why your keys don't work; they work perfectly on my Toshiba (a different make, though).

To adjust your brightness without using the Fn keys, you could do this:

[LIST]
[*]Right-click on your panel (your task bar).
[*]Add to Panel...
[*]Select "Brightness Applet" and Add.
[/LIST]
This will put an icon on your task bar that you can click and set your brightness.

You also can set your default brightness to its lowest setting with:

[LIST]
[*]System > Preferences > Power Management > On Mains Power > Set display brightness to... 0%
[/LIST]
There are other settings too that you can play with to reduce screen power.

However, if your battery fails after just a few minutes, then I think that your battery is KO'd.
