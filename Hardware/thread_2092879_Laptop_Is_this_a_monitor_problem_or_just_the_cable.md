### Post by tgalati4 on 2012-12-11
Try to squeeze the plastic bezel just below the purple lines and see if they change.  Try squeezing at the top and bottom.  If the lines do change, then it's possible that your screen is suffering from delamination.  This happens when you open the lid from the corner causing the glass to flex.

A cable problem causes you to lose a color, say red, green, or blue, or the entire screen will black out or flicker.  

Did you spill any soft drinks or coffee lately?
