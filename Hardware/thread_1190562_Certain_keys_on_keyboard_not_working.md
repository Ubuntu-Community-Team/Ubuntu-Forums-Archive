### Post by markmckee6011 on 2009-06-19
It is possible that the ribbon cable for the keyboard isn't in properly, with some laptops you need to take the top case off to replace the hard drive.

Do the same keys work with a live cd?
If they do then you know it's just a configuration issue.
If not I would take the laptop back to the repair shop and get them to check it.

You could also post the make and model of the laptop and I should be able to tell you if the top case had top be removed to replace the hard drive.
