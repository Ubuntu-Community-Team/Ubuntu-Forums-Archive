### Post by Mark Phelps on 2018-08-16
There's a very good chance you will NOT be able to get three displays working, regardless of which laptop you purchase.

I have a desktop with three displays, and I can not get all three to work in Linux, regardless of the distro used.

The third display uses a DisplayLink USB adapter box, and despite their providing Linux drivers, it will not display -- and their site refers to a "generic three-monitor Linux problem".
