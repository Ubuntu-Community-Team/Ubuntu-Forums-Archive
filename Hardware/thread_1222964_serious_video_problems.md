If, as I believe, this laptop has Intel graphics on it then that is your problem.
There is a bug in 9.04 with Intel graphics.
You have three choices as I see it...
1. live with the problem
2. wipe drive & install Ubuntu 8.10 which works well with Intel graphics.
3. Google net & follow the suggestions of down grading Intel drivers.

This is why I, & others, do not install the latest version of Ubuntu.
