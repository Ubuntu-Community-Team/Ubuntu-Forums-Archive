### Post by Sam Lars on 2008-05-27
One possibility is that the system is scaling the processor to a slower speed, which makes it cooler (and more power friendly).  You can add an applet to the panel to monitor it.

---

### Post by EvilGnome on 2008-05-27
thanks! and in case any other noobs want to do this, it's also helpful to give the applet root privileges so that you can choose the processor speed. 

sudo dpkg-reconfigure gnome-applets

and choose yes.
