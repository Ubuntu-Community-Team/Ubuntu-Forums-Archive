I believe that a short circuit happens whenever the front panel is tapped or touched by any means. Odd enough, this issue is not present when I plug in other USB cables or memory sticks. However, I am still very confused. It does happen when just cleaning the front panel with a duster and when plugging in this IPod data cable.

I can't think about any solution, so I posted here in hope that anyone will be able to figure out what the problem is. Is this because my hand is probably loaded with static electricity (less likely)? I am completely at loss.
