### Post by P4man on 2010-08-02
Looks like you have an AGP system. Its gonna be hard to find any AGP card new, but there should be plenty on ebay and the like.

Ill second the recommendation for an nVidia card, and any AGP card should work in that system, the only issue could be that you might need a 6 pins PCI-E connector for a somewhat recent cards and your powersupply may or may not have that (can be solved with an adaptor if you have 2 spare molex connectors).

A highend card doesnt make much sense with a 1.8 GHz sempron, im not sure what software you want to run on it (games? cad? compiz?) but see what you can find on ebay. Something like a 7900 generation card can be had for next to nothing and are still rather good.
