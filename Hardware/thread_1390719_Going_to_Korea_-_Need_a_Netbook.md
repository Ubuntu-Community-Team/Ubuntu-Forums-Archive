### Post by VeeDubb on 2010-05-01
I'm sure you're already in Korea teaching, but do you mind if I ask what you picked?

I'm actually heading to Korea in a few weeks to visit my girlfriend who has been teaching english there since August, and ask her to marry me.

And, I just ordered a 1201n from newegg to take with me.



Also, word to the wise, there are a lot of really great schools to teach english at, and most of the public schools treat their foreign teachers very well.  However, there's a few really rotten private schools.

If you start having lot's of trouble with your boss paying you correctly and on time, you should expect to get stiffed for your last month, and prosibly get fired with 1 month left on your contract so they can avoid the bonus for finishing, and then you're stuck buying your ticket home out of pocket.

The best thing to do is to see if you can find an email address for whoever taught in your post last year.  Once you find a few others, most korean cities have a small but strong community of foreign teachers who keep in touch with each other.
