### Post by slooksterpsv on 2010-10-01
Disconnect all power connections from the computer, remove the cmos battery, remove the power connector from the motherboard, let sit for at least 30 min - then boot. That should remove the password on the cmos.

I don't see a pin where we can reset the password, look up documentation on your motherboard.
