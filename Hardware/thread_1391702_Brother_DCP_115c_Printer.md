Newbus,  You did not give the Forum much info about your system, eg Ubuntu version etc.  Do a search for DCP-115C in Synaptic Package Manager there should be a couple of packages for the printer side of the DCP-115C and you will have to download from the Brother site a couple of files for the scanner.  also there are a few reference for DCP-115C in the Forum, just do a search on DCP-115C.  The following link have a wealth on info on how to set-up Brother DCPs though a lot of the posts are dated but that depends on what version of Ubuntu you have installed.

BoardDWorld post:  HOWTO: Ubuntu All Brother Printer & Scanner Driver Installation for Newbies!
([http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=590793&highlight=dcp](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=590793&highlight=dcp))

Basically the printer side is fairly easy to set-up using what is in Synaptic Package Manager.  The Scanner set-up depends on Ubuntu version you have installed. Have a go in working through ir yourself & if you have any problems the aske the question in the Forum and I am sure you will get the help you need to get you printing & scanning.   TopEnder
PS I have posted a few post over the years on setting-up various Brother DCPs
