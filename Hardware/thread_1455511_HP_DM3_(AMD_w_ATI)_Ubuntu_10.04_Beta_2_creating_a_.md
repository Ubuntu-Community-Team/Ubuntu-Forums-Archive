### Post by mdance86 on 2010-04-16
Nevermind.  I got it fixed. Removed the proprietary ATI drivers and am using the open source drivers.  Much better.  Hotter, quieter fan, and better battery life.  So if anyone is having similar problems with a config similar to mine, remove the ATI drivers.
