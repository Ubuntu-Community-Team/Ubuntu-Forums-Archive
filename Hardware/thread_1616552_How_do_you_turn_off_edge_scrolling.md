### Post by Cypher2 on 2010-11-11
Go to System > Preferences > Mouse (Touchpad Tab) > Scrolling (.)

Disable it..

Voila!

You can also manage settings through synclient

Make sure all synaptics related packages are installed through Synaptic Package Manager first...
