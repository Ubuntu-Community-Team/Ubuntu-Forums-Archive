### Post by bobkil on 2008-05-31
To get the full functionality from your 5180 you will need to download the HPLIP software package from Sourceforge and install it.  The following url will get it and is in English.  All the functions on mine seem to work including the memory card function which my manual said would not work over a network interface.

[http://hplip.sourceforge.net/install/index.html](http://hplip.sourceforge.net/install/index.html)

Good luck;
bob

### Post by tact on 2008-05-31
agree with the poster above...  hplip toolbox may be just what you need.  

I have a HP multifunction printer on a router.  My wife uses XP laptop and me ubuntu.  Often she asks me to scan and print documents for her as it works so much better on my linux box than her xp box.  

hehehe.. its a little cool actually - when I am maybe relaxing and surfing in bed late evening..  she is downstairs in the (home) office with the scanner/printer trying unsuccessfully to get the result she wants.  So she puts the document on the scanner and comes up to the bedroom and from there over wifi I can scan, preview, crop etc..  and initiate the print, even dump a soft copy into her shared folder on her XP laptop, before she gets back downstairs to the office.

cheers.
