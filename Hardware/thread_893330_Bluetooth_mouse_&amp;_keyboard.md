When I power up the computer, the bluetooth interface connects immediately to the devices.
However, just before the Gnome login screen comes up, the connection is lost.
I've found that the fastest way to make the connection again is to switch off the M&K, pull the bluetooth pin out, reconnect it, and switch on the M&K again.

Very clumsy.

Advice? For instance, what programs could I disable or uninstall without risking to have to re-install the complete system? (Which isn't really that bad; after all it's not Windows! :) )
