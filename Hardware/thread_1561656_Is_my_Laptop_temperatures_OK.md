### Post by James2k on 2010-08-26
This isn't a problem with anything to do with Ubuntu or anything but I just wanted to get a second opinion on my laptops temperatures.

I have a Compaq Presario CQ60 laptop, with a 2.10 Ghz single core AMD Sempron Processor and nVidia 8200M graphics card. I take good care of my laptop and air dust my vents regularly. My laptops vent is placed in an underneath position near the left side of the laptop base, so I have the back legs of the laptop raised slightly for better air flow.

My idle temps are:

**CPU**: 34 - 36 C
**GPU:** 51 - 53 C

And when im clicking about on the internet, for example reading blogs and news websites or just checking email

**CPU**: 36 - 40  C
**GPU:** 52 - 55 C

I thought these were quite good temperatures for a laptop, but I've found when I'm watching a video on YouTube, my GPU temperature goes up very quickly and hits the 68 C mark sometimes 70 C.

I know there is obviously going to be an increase in temperature as video content will make the GPU work more, but is the dramatic increase OK? I wasn't sure. My CPU also increases but not as much usually about 45 - 47 C

Thanks
