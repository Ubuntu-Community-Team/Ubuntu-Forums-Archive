### Post by alex2399 on 2009-03-23
The beeps you hear are an error code, they can be (all) short and/or long. You can find out in your motherboard manual how to interpret these beep codes to find the cause of the error. Google around a bit what kind of motherboard is used on your PC, or if you can't find the exact type the brand might be just enough. Look for a manual from that era then from a similar motherboard, codes are listed in there maybe, or even on the manufacturers website/Google.
