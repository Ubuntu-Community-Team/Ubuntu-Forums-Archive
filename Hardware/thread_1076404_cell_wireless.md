### Post by mtbsoft on 2009-02-21
I can't be sure what's available over there but here in Oz it is possible to buy 3G USB modems which simply plug into your computer and use the normal 3G network when in range and switch to the slower GPRS network the rest of the time.  When in a 3G area the speed is quite reasonable but at GPSR speeds it's not too good.

Here is a link to some of our devices so you can know what to look for locally over there: [http://store.three.com.au/mobilebroadband/broadband-6gb-cap?cid=5](http://store.three.com.au/mobilebroadband/broadband-6gb-cap?cid=5) .
