### Post by Eberbachl on 2009-11-30
A beautiful Netbook!

My Samsung n310 works very nicely out of the box with Karmic. Wireless included.

The poster is right about brightness fn keys not working in addition to the wireless on/off fn key. Volume fn keys and sleep work fine though, as does all hardware.

Hopefully the fn key bug is sorted soon, but for now I can live with it. It's such a nice netbook. The screen is drop dead gorgeous, the keyboard is great to type on, with much better feel and key positioning than my MSI Wind u100+, and the body is very nice too.

Battery life isn't amazing with the standard 4 cell 4000mAh battery. I get about 2.5 real-life hours from mine :( I'll probably grab a 6 cell 6600mAh battery from eBay for it.

Performance-wise it's about what you'd expect from the ubiquitous Atom n270 platform.
