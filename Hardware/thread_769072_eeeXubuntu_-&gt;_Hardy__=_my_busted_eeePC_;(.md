Just FYI to other eeePC users - 

I upgraded with the Update Manager over wireless last night. When prompted to override or not override customized files, I always said "no". 

Results:
ucview - paints garbage on the screen and freezes the whole UI if I pick the "wrong" video size.
wireless - completely not available (though the light is on)
Function keys - All the on screen feedback no longer work and the volume keys don't work at all.
Ruby on Rails - webrick runs, but all the pages throw a 500 error.

Before the "upgrade", my eeePC was the most functional Linux laptap I have ever had. This is the first time I have upgraded a Ubuntu distro and experienced this kind of degradation in functionality. 

I'll probably just wipe the whole thing and go back to the Gusty based eeeXubuntu distro and go through all the customization steps again.
