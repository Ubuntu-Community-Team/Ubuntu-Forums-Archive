### Post by kurt18947 on 2013-11-25
Brother driver for your machine:

[http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/download_prn.html#DCP-J132W](http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/download_prn.html#DCP-J132W)

If you're not familiar with the Brother install procedure or the command line, it's kind of confusing because it appears the directions haven't been modified since Ubuntu 8.10 or thereabouts.  Many of the commands appear to be unnecessary on current *buntu releases.  If you're able to download this shell script, it makes printer installation pretty simple in my experience.

[http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/faq_prn.html#f00104](http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/faq_prn.html#f00104).

### Post by unho on 2014-02-12
Kinda worked for me on Ubuntu 10.04:

[LIST]
[*]Going to the printer page: [http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/BSC/public/eu/es/es/model_top/colormfc/dcpj132w_eu.html?reg=eu&c=es&lang=es&prod=dcpj132w_eu](http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/BSC/public/eu/es/es/model_top/colormfc/dcpj132w_eu.html?reg=eu&c=es&lang=es&prod=dcpj132w_eu)
[*]Click on **"Downloads"**   ("Descargas")
[*]Select **"Linux (deb)"**
[*]Select **"English"**
[*]Click on the **"Search"** ("Buscar") button
[*]Download **"Linux-brprinter-installer"** by clicking on **"Download & Information"** ("Descarga y Información")
[*]Follow steps from **"How to Download and Install"** ("Modo de descarga e instalación")
[/LIST]
Seems to print fine. Not sure about color. The scanner seems not to work. I am using USB to connect the printer.
