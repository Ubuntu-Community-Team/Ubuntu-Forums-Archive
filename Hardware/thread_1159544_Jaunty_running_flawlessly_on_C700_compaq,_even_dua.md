### Post by blindside on 2009-05-14
Just here to let anyone interested know that Ubuntu 9.04 Januty jackelope installs and runs perfectly. Wireless works out of the box (it can even packet inject with aircrack-ng suite with STOCK drivers NO PATCHING!) hard drive recognised fine, it displays battery life correctly, sound works.  I am very happy with Ubuntu and if it weren't for a few things I would just delete Vista altogether. 

There are only 2 problems I ran into when testing my system. 

1. I have a button that turns off wifi, that still works, but its proper function is to turn blue (from amber) when connected to a network, when connected it stays amber. This is very minor. 

2. 2D graphics were not rendering properly, I couldn't turn on desktop enhancements and when pressing down in firefox to scroll it would freeze and then go rapidly all the way to the bottom.

 Problem 2 has been remedied with the help of psyke83 on thread   [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1130582](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1130582)    After performing that my laptop is running great. The most surprising thing about the whole process is the fact that with the ath5k drivers for my atheros wifi card supports packet injecting without having to mess with madifi at all. Now all I have to do is find a solution to all the programs I use Vista for, and resist the urge to get Windows 7. 

If anyone has any questions about installing on c700 series of lappys hit me up. Also I am pretty familiar with the aircrack suite so I may be able to help there. I have been browsing this forum for a while and am getting pretty fluent with Ubuntu thanks to all your help. Hopefully I can give what has been given to me
