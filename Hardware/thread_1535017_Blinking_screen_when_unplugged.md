### Post by panopticon on 2010-07-20
This is not really a prominent "fix" per say, but it can help you avoid the flickering issue. 

Just maximize the display brightness (using "FN" + ? or manually through power management) when unplugged, then lower it to your needed level. That should in some cases, on some netbooks/laptops, stop the flickering. 

It helped for me, and I hope it helps for you.
