### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2020-07-01
It's on the CPU Support List, so yes:
[https://www.asus.com/Motherboards/ROG-Strix-X570-I-Gaming/HelpDesk_CPU/](https://www.asus.com/Motherboards/ROG-Strix-X570-I-Gaming/HelpDesk_CPU/)

I think they are indeed using the process to determine "gen". There were no 1000-series APU's. The 2000-series APU's are based on the same Zen (14nm) as the 1000-series CPU's, so they are considered the 1st gen Ryzen APU. 
The 3000-series APU's will be based on "Zen+" (12nm) process, while the rest of 3000 series use the newer "Zen2" (7nm) process.
