### Post by totalhealth on 2009-08-30
You can't use the ENV3 music sync feature, but you can get arround it.  You can only do it if you buy a micro-SD card and use it in the phone (this is true on Windows and Mac also).  After you put in the card do the following.

1. On your phone go to setting and tools -> USB Mode and select "Ask On Plug"
2. Connect the phone to your computer using the USB cable.  When it asks you if you want Music or Modem hit the End key (should go back to the main screen)
3. Now open the main menu again and select settings and tools -> Tools -> USB Mass Storage

Your phone will now connect like any USB drive.  You can move files back and forth between your micro SD card and your computer.  It won't automatically sync everythinig, but you can easily get stuff back and forth.

Hope this helps...
