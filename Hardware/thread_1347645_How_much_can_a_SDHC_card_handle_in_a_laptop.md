### Post by schmolch on 2009-12-06
Dont do it.
I tried this a few years ago when SSDs where much more expensive, today its certainly not worth the trouble any more.
SDHC-Cards are horribly slow and the driver might suck (both happenend to me).
You can get a good 60GB SSD for only $150, spend your money on that. You will also be able to use your SSD for many years while a SDHC-card is a throw-away product.
I have 2 60GB SSDs in my Laptops and use the old 8GB SSD in my Desktop as a system-disk.
