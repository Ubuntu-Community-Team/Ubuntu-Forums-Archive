### Post by NikoC on 2008-07-22
For your partition problems I would download [Gparted livecd]("http://http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php"), which is essentially a bootable linux cd containing a bunch of harddrive tools... a mighty essential for every computer owner out there!

As for the rescue cd's: sony's come preinstalled with windows, don't they? Therefor I wonder if you are going to find any help on a linux forum...
You can always borrow a windows disc from somebody else and download all the drivers for your specific model via sony.com or your local sony website. That's how I did in the past for my sony laptop, before I switched to linux that is...
