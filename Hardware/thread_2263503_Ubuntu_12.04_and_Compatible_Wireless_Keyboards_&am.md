### Post by DZDB on 2015-02-01
For those of you who have Ubuntu 12.04 and are looking for a wireless keyboard and touchpad that works right out of the box, I highly recommend the Logitech K400 Wireless Keyboard and Touchpad. You can purchase one in the price range of $32-$40 at Staples, Best Buy, Radio Shack, etc. I am pretty sure that the Logitech K400 Wireless Keyboard and Touchpad would also work with Ubuntu 14.04 and pretty much all flavors of Ubuntu.

In my family room, I have connected my Ubuntu computer, which contains the Logitech K400 Wireless Keyboard and Touchpad, to my Samsung 41" TV via vga port (i.e. HDMI port would also work).  I don't subscribe to cable TV. So I use the Ubuntu computer connected to my TV to watch YouTube videos, AOL videos, and Watch ESPN.
