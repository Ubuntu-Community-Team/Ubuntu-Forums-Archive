### Post by QIII on 2014-07-17
Hello.

I think you should contact HP to be sure.

The problem is that for a time HP/Compaq used non-standard proprietary pinouts on their PSUs/motherboard headers.  Dell did the same for years.

It would be best to contact HP to confirm that the pinout is the same.

That, or buy a new power supply at the same time.

Edit:  If you get a new power supply, do not skimp and get a cheap, off-brand "silver box".  They rarely conform to the ATX standards electrically and the components are cheap and prone to failure, which could damage your whole system.
