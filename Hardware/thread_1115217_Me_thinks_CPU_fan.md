### Post by squaregoldfish on 2009-04-04
It could also be the PSU fan -  the PSU will store a lot more heat in it than the CPU, which might explain the varying times that the computer survives before switching off. You may be able to check this by feeling for air coming out of it.

However, the only way to know for sure is to open up the case and watch the fans - see which ones are or aren't working.
