### Post by jayothree on 2010-10-21
Although I did not try ubuntu, I was able to test one of these with fedora on a flash drive.  It was so nice (found all hardware, gave no fits), that I bought three!  I have a few  family members that need linux support.  Battery life is good, screen brightness is excellent.  The smaller 14" form factor makes is a very easy notebook to use for novices and professionals alike (some of us just watch youtube and post things on facebook; while at least one of us uses it to develop software for a large corporation).

You will be very pleased with this unit as a linux platform.
