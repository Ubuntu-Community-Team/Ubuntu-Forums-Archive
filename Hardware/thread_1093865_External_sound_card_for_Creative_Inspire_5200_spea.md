### Post by blackdalek on 2009-04-12
The Frisby USB-S-012 external sound card works pretty much out of the box in Ubuntu 8.10 with PulseAudio and ALSA Mixer. Can play regular 5.1 audio and split normal stereo into 5.1.
I haven't tried the line-in but it does show up on the mixer so I assume if it is present on that it should work.
I don't have any SPDIF equipment so I can't really test if that works.
