### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2013-12-23
What is your current card?
What will be using new card for? Gaming? Just video playback?

I would highly recommend steering clear of dual GPU's (SLI/Crossfire) or dual-core AMD GPU's (especially the 4870x2 since it was moved to the legacy Catalyst driver).

Nowadays, you can get an Nvidia GTX 660 for ~$160 with the right rebate. [http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814133470](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814133470)
It's hard to think of a better deal on a Linux gaming card unless you're looking to spend more $$.
