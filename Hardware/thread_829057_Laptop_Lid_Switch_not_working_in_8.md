### Post by issih on 2008-06-14
Hmmn sounds more like you are having issues with getting your pc to suspend at all rather than a problem with the switch.

From what you've described, I'd say the switch is being detected, and the computer is trying to suspend, but something is failing.

Is this a fresh install? I know a lot of people had issues with suspend on the kernel that shipped with 8.04, but there have been a few updates since then. Try running the update manager if you are not up to date.

If that isn't it, have a look here:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UnderstandingSuspend](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UnderstandingSuspend)

have a look at the debugging bit, hope that helps
