### Post by tlhIngan on 2013-01-23
Some older electronics develop trouble from either dust accumulation or loose soldering and only perform normally after they've warmed up.
Try turning your monitor and computer on way ahead of time without disconnecting/reconnecting or turning it on/off a bunch of times like you're doing. My old stereo won't play CDs immediately after I turn it on, it needs to stay on for a few minutes first and then it's fine.
Try that and post back.
