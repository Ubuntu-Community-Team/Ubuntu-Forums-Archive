Yes! By default linux leaves the fan stuff to the BIOS and windows overrules the BIOS settings and does it's own thing with the fan.

Personally I just configure the BIOS to be more quiet, but there's also software for linux to control the fan. You'll have to google that though, or ask someone else (sorry).
