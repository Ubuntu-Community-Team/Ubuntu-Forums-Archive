### Post by Dennis N on 2014-09-07
The last two cameras I have bought were Cannon and I transfer the images by just removing the SD card and using a common SD card reader - built in or plug into USB port. Mine just plugs into the USB. If you want to know, the card reader is a Targus bought at Wal Mart.  No drivers needed to be installed.

### Post by ibjsb4 on 2014-09-07
Wife has a Fujifilm that is not recognized by ubuntu, but is recognized by Shotwell.

The Shotwell package for some reason, has the ability to detect certain cameras when the file manager cannot.  Its in the software center.

[https://wiki.gnome.org/Apps/Shotwell](https://wiki.gnome.org/Apps/Shotwell)
