### Post by dalebert on 2008-06-30
Chucky,
I just got this camera and I was as perplexed as you. Finally I just opened the Synaptic Package Manager and did a search for "webcam" and a number of apps came up. I installed several and don't have much to report on most of them, but I ran "Cheese" and instantly saw my own face on the screen. Looked kind of bright but otherwise a decent resolution and frame rate it seemed for a $17 webcam. I think some tweaking might address the brightness issue. I also installed a camera monitor app which seems to just pop up an icon when you run Cheese which seems to turn on the camera.

Basically this appears almost plug-n-play in Ubuntu once you install some webcam apps.
