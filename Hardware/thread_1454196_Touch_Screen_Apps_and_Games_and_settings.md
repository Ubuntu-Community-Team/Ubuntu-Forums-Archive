### Post by LSenf on 2010-04-16
You might give the new Gnome Shell a try. It's already quite good for touchscreen usage.
In general, I think that KDE is a slightly better option than Gnome 2 for touchscreens.

As for games, the game engine Spring is relatively good for playing with a touchscreen, you can find the installation instructions here:
[http://springrts.com/wiki/Ubuntu_install](http://springrts.com/wiki/Ubuntu_install)
followed by:
[http://springrts.com/wiki/Template:Finalizing_linux_install](http://springrts.com/wiki/Template:Finalizing_linux_install)
You can get mods and games for it on springfiles or similar sites.
