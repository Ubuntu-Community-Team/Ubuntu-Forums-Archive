### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2012-01-26
What makes you think flashing the firmware will fix them? Drives die all from issues unrelated to firmware (motors wear out, laser diodes fail, etc.). If they both stopped working at the same time it might be something else like the power supply, cables, or the controller on the motherboard.

If the drives are the same age, and you've used both of them a lot over the years, the simple fix might just by to purchase a lens-cleaning kit and use that.  This is an optical disk with small fibres sticking up that brush the lens clean.  It's cheaper than buying new drives.
