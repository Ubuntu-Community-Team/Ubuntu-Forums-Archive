### Post by drpeppercan on 2018-09-10
Dave from [Computer Hope]("https://www.computerhope.com/forum/index.php?topic=168855.msg981763#msg981763") advised this:

"*[COLOR=#000000][FONT=Verdana]Remove all cards from the PCI Express slots except for video card does it still happen. If the only thing plugged into PCI Express is the video card then if you have integrated video as an option remove the video card and try running on integrated to see if problem goes away. Or if it lacks integrated video, try a different video card with all other PCI Express cards removed to see if you can get to a healthy state.[/FONT][/COLOR]*"
