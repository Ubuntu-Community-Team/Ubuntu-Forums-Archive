Hey friend -- I bought the i5 5742G and am having some heartache on Ubuntu 10.10 as well. That said ....

* Is there a way to get sensors to work? Temp/FanSpeed etc

They worked out of the box for me. Do you have reason to believe your fan/sensors aren't working? I believe they're controlled by the BIOS.

* The screen doesn't look or feel right, its to bright but lowering brightness
makes it worse. 

I ran Windows 7 for a few months on mine, but ultimately wasn't satisfied. That said, it's just the quality of the LCD. It was just as bad in Windows. I imagine Acer was just being cheap.

* System recognize 2.5Gb of memory instead of the 3Gb installed

It's true that a 32 bit OS can recognize around 3.2 gb of memory, but that stacks with the video memory -- which is why you're missing half a gig. You probably want to install the 64 bit version of 10.10

As for me ---

Have you had any luck with the single internal speaker? Ubuntu thinks it's a left channel, so I have no right channel unless I plug speakers in.
