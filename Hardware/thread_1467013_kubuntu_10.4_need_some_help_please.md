KDE 4 is all about widgets. Even on panel everything are widgets. So if you want to add task bar to the panel you need to:
1) unlock widgets 
2) click on the button which is placed next to digital clock on your panel 
3) click "add widgets"
4) and add "task manager" widget.

That would be all :)
