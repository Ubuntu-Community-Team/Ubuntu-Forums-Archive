### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2013-05-12
> Could I just install the nvidia drivers and use it that way or would I have to just install the intel drivers and use it that way?
Intel drivers are open-source and come preinstalled. Simply installing the nvidia driver will not work. You'll have to use nvidia driver 319.17 (or later) and do some configuration: [http://www.barunisystems.com/index.php/site/page?view=blog](http://www.barunisystems.com/index.php/site/page?view=blog)
