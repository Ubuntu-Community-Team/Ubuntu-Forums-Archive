### Post by felichas on 2009-08-23
lduperval, try this:

Right click on the bluetooth icon --> Brose files on device... --> Select device --> click on your phone --> connect --> browse your phone folders.

If your phone doesn't show up in the list, then: 
Right click on the bluetooth icon --> Setup new device... --> next, next, next. Then go back to the begining.
