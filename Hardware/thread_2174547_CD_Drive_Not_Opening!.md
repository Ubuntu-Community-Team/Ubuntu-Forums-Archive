### Post by QIII on 2013-09-15
Hello!

It sounds like the mechanical latch is not working.

Before trying anything too high tech for the moment, let's see if the low-tech solution will work.

There should be a small hole near the drawer/door.  Unfold a paperclip and *carefully *insert one end into the hole.  Don't jam too hard.  All you are trying to do is trip the latch, not force the drive open.

If it doesn't open let us know.

If it does, try opening and closing it a few times to see if this is just a one-time occurrence.
