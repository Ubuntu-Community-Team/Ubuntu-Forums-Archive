### Post by mjwood0 on 2010-02-15
I know that this is a common problem -- the Realtek RTL8187SE card in the MSI Wind is next to impossible to get working correctly.  I had issues with all of the following:
[LIST]
[*]Slow wireless access point detection
[*]Frequent disconnects
[*]TONS of dropped packets
[/LIST]

In fact, it was so bad, that I found it was dropping more packets than it was receiving.

So, I went ahead and swapped out the card for an Intel 4965AGN.  Less than $12.25 US and I have fully functioning wireless with none of the issues.  

The procedure involves a bit of tinkering, so I wrote it up on my blog.  Hope this helps someone else in the same, frustrating boat.
