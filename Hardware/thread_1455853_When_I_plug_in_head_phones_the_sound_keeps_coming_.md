### Post by Hamchan on 2010-04-16
There used to be something called "headphone switch" in the gui for alsamixer. Not sure if it's still though (I'm not on Ubuntu). 

If you bring up your volume mixer look for something called "switches." You may have to look through preferences menus as I don't think that it was shown by default.

If you find that, hopefully you'll have a headphone switch. Just check that box and you should be good.
