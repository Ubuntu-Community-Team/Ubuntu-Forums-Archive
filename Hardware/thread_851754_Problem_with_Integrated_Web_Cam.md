### Post by scragar on 2008-07-07
pidgin does not currently support video chat at the moment, although the story goes that the fun pidgin guys(the carrier team since it's name change) are working on that. there are other chat programs that support video chat though, including kopete(which is available via the package manager: system>applications>Synaptic Package Manager).

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Webcam](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Webcam) <-- documentation on testing and troubleshooting your webcam, let me know if you get stuck.
