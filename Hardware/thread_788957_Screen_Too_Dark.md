### Post by jongkind on 2008-05-10
I'm not sure whether this works under Wubi, but what you can try to do is in the following order:

[LIST=1]
[*]Right click a panel
[*]Select "add to panel"
[*]Select the "brightness applet"
[*]With this brightness applet you can adjust the brightness of your screen.
[/LIST]

Good luck

### Post by werries on 2008-05-10
another option is going to System->Preferences->Power Management.
this allows changing dim when idle and brightness options for when in battery or AC power.
