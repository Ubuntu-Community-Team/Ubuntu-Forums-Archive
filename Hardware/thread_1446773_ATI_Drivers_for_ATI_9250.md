### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-04-05
If you're currently seeing a graphical desktop, not a text-mode command line, you're already running the only ATI drivers available for your card.

ATI dropped Linux support for your card over a year ago.  The only driver that will work with that card now are the open source drivers -- and they are installed by default upon Ubuntu installation.
