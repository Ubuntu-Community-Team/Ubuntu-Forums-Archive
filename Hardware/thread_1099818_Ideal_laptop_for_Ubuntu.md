### Post by tuxpenguin on 2009-03-18
[This]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam") might help you out. It's the laptop testing team's wiki. You can find the Ubuntu test run on many laptop models. 

As for what laptop to get, that's up to you, since most will run Ubuntu with ease. Since you probably won't be gaming too heavily, video chipset power doesn't matter as much, but get one that will run compiz well [most any modern chipset will]. I would shoot for battery life as the main selling point. I have a Toshiba A305 series. It runs Ubuntu perfectly, however it's probably out of your price range.
