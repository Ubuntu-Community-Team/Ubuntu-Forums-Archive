### Post by stairwayoflight on 2009-01-01
Well my search is over. I have ordered a P43 Neo3-F.

It has an issue with Linux and the sata hard drives showing up but that is fixed with a bios update. I'm not overclocking or anything, so I opted for this chipset and a pair of Crucial Ballistix ddr2 800 2 GiB modules.

It seems there isn't much of a difference in features between the P45 and P43; in the end I won't have more than one graphics card on this board so no worries there.

And it has a parallel port!!!
