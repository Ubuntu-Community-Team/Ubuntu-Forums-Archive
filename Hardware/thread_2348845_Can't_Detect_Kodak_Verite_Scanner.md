### Post by jfblagdengmail.co on 2017-01-07
I have a Kodak Verite 55 XL Plus All-in-One printer. I installed the drivers and I'm able to print, but Ubuntu doesn't detect the scanner part of the Kodak printer, even though I have installed the scanner drivers for it. Is there a solution?

**Update**: I fixed it. I just had to uninstall the scanner driver, reboot, install the scanner driver, and reboot. I just wish proprietary drivers were in the repositories - it might have made it a little easier.
