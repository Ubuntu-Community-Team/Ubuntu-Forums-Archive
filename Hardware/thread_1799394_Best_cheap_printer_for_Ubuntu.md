Anyway, OP, I would look at printers in your area at your budget and choose a HP inkjet.  Try to get one that is listed on the HP website as compatible with Linux.  

Most are but maybe google the printer you're considering and 'Ubuntu.'  

Also, if possible, one in which you can order cheap ink.  Cartridges are expensive for all of the brands but a popular brand that has various places/vendors who sell the cartridges cheaper than your local store is definitely preferable!
