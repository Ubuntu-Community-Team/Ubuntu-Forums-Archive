### Post by nymusicman on 2011-09-13
I'm searching the same thing. Best I can say is buy a laptop made for ubuntu. There are only two companies I can think of that will customize high end laptops strictly for linux (I know there are a lot of linux laptop sellers but the rest are not customized just for linux and a lot of them are not "high end").


[http://www.system76.com](http://www.system76.com)
[http://www.zareason.com](http://www.zareason.com)

Both companies have great support. Zareason is a little more customized if you want a laptop that literally should never leave a desk, but both companies provide what you want.

My opinion is that I'm leaning towards system76, but you judge for yourself.
