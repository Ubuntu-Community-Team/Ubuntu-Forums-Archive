### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-02-18
One other thing that CAN cause a PC to slow down and hardware to stop working is a failing hard drive -- especially on an older PC.

IF the filesystem is corrupting, that can hose up device drivers, causing the related hardware to stop working.
