### Post by gdea73 on 2012-03-15
It's a common bug with Brasero. Not sure what the deal is, but it happens all the time. Search for "GnomeBaker" in Ubuntu Software Center. Its interface isn't quite as nice as that of Brasero, but it's fairly easy to use and far more reliable, in my experience.

edit: and also, in general, slower speeds yield a better-readable CD-ROM as they make a darker imprint upon the disc.
