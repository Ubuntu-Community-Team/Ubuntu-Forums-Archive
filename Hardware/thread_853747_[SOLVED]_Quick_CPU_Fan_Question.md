### Post by Vivaldi Gloria on 2008-07-08
> **Separ said:**
> I am just wondering if any Socket 775 fan will be suitable for an Intel Core2 Quad Q6700. Microdirect got me confused by saying that one of the fans I looked was suitable for any Dual Core/Celeron D/Pentium 4 amongst a few other processors.

Any socket 775 heatsink will be suitable for any socket 775 processor. Of course a bad one may not be enough to cool a quadcore.

But what do you mean by fan? Do you mean the heatsink or the fan attached to a heatsink?

For example a have a thermalright si-120 heatsink for my Q6600 and a 120mm fan attached to si-120. A fan of size 80mm or 140mm cannot be used with si-120. 

So any 775 heatsink can be used with any 775 processor but what size fan is needed depends on the what size fan the heatsink use. There are even fanless heatsinks out there.
