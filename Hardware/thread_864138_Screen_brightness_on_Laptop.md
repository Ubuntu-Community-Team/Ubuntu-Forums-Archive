### Post by phidia on 2008-07-19
Click/open the System>Preferences>Power Management applet from the top panel menu and open the the "On Battery" tab. Paid close attention to the brightness dimming slider it works opposite of how you might think it does.
Added: Sorry I see you're using kubuntu-I think you'll find power management in control center.
