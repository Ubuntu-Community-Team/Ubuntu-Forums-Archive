Update, checked with the IRC channel & got help from a person. Then found the thread [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1971463](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1971463) & followed the instructions to the sudo apt-get install nvidia-current & it worked aside from view being left of center & a lil low. Then did Alt+F2 gksu nvidia-settings & altered resolution to 768X1024 for perfect centering, & potentially more the appropriate settings for personal preferences.

Consider the thread resolved. Muchos Gracias Ubuntu community.
