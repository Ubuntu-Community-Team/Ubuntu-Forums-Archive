### Post by ronocdh on 2011-02-14
There are various VNC server apps available in the Android Market, but they require a rooted device. Pop open that app when you're at home and you can click or type anywhere you need to, on your phone, from the comfort of your computer! I use [droid VNC server]("http://www.appbrain.com/app/droid-vnc-server/org.onaips.vnc") and have been extremely pleased with it.
