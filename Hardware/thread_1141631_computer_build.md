### Post by Random-penguin on 2009-04-28
I can't see any thing there that will cause a problem. My only query is why go for a micro atx form factor when you are self building from scratch. this will make things tighter to install and possibly limit future upgrade potential. And the larger atx form factor will improve air flow around components, meaning less concern for things getting too hot. That is unless you're intent on a small case with a small foot print!!

I have not tried onboard nvidia graphics but I believe it will work out of the box and of course the proprietry driver will be available. But maybe a cheap nvidia card would provide better overhead for the compiz bells and whistles!
