### Post by jeffmartinxyz on 2018-11-24
Thanks for making this post! I could not figure out what drivers I needed to get my USB-C dock to recognize my monitors. The drivers are now available for 18.04.

Install Guide here:
[https://support.displaylink.com/knowledgebase/articles/684649](https://support.displaylink.com/knowledgebase/articles/684649)

Note on Secure boot:
[https://support.displaylink.com/knowledgebase/articles/1181617](https://support.displaylink.com/knowledgebase/articles/1181617)

Make sure you disable secure boot or follow the steps in the article above to sign the kernel module.
