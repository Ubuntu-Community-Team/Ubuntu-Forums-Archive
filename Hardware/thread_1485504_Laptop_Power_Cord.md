### Post by VeeDubb on 2010-05-16
There probably are.

There's nothing magical in a laptop power cord.  Any old power adapter will work provided that it has the same output voltage, amperage, and the same plug.

If you find one with the wrong plug, you can actually cut the right plug off your fried adapter and just splice it in,  Just be sure you get the polarity right.

There are also after-market chargers from companies like targus and brando.
