I thought I might have a bad strip so I plugged directly in to the wall.  That seemed to solve my problems for a while until I closed the door on my makeshift half rack.  The problem re-occurred during minimal use every hour or so.  Now with the system open and the rack open it will shut down when under medium strain.

Overall processor barely gets above 40 which is about what the hard drives get to, and I'm barely at 5% overall usage even when running SC2 in wine.  Finally there is absolutely nothing in any of the log files in /var/log.  I mean no hardware errors no shutdown requests, nothing...It's like someone pulled th eplug out of my machine.  Right after it happens I can touch nearly every component in my box and nothing feels like it's about to melt.

Is my PSU going bad? Or is there something else I'm missing?
