### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-03-02
Are you willing to experiment porting Ubuntu for Devices to your new tablet device? Personally, if I paid good money for a mobile device I would not be prepared to take this risk

> [COLOR=#333333][FONT=Ubuntu]Ubuntu for devices is still under development. Installing an Ubuntu for devices image may make your device unusable. Important features may be missing or broken. New images may introduce new features and may break existing features as development continues. Ubuntu for devices does not yet provide a reliable replacement for your current handset, phone or tablet. [/FONT][/COLOR]

[https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/installing-ubuntu-for-devices/](https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/installing-ubuntu-for-devices/)

We can wait until Ubuntu mobile devices are released for retail or we can buy a device and experiment trying to port Ubuntu for Devices to that device.

[https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/porting-new-device/](https://developer.ubuntu.com/en/start/ubuntu-for-devices/porting-new-device/)

At the end of this thread there is a video from Will cook the Ubuntu Desktop Manager, He shows the progress being made with Ubuntu on a tablet. Convergence in action.
