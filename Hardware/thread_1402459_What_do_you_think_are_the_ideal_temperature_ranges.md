### Post by cascade9 on 2010-02-09
Ideally? 20c, maybe a bit less. I'd say less that that, but some CPUs/motherboards have issues under 0c LOL

Those temps are fine. Its hard to make generalisations about temps, what will cook one GPU might be fine for another. But, generally, as far as your CPU stays under 55-60c (and better yet, under 45c) you have nothing to worry about. I've seen nVidia cards hit over 100c and keep on going (yes, I live in a hot part of a hot country). They are much happier under 60-70c. HDDs- better to keep then under 50-45c or so. Some HDDs have a max temp of 65c or less, they are probably the part that deals the worst with high temps.
