### Post by Dngrsone on 2012-01-24
Laptop heat is almost always a problem; the nature of the beast, as they say.

When is the last time you blew out the vents with compressed air?  In my region (which is basically an irrigated desert), I have to blow out my laptop every three weeks or so.

Make sure you keep the vents clear when using the laptop (in other words, don't place it on a blanket or other soft/squishy), and it may be helpful to get one of those laptop coolers.

Video-intensive applications may make your laptop run hotter.
