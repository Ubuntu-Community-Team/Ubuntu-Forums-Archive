Connect all your power-bars to it, and connect it to the wall socket through a spark-arrester...  Lightning kills electronics ten-times faster than you can say "oops"...

If there's any room in the mouse or keyboard, you might be able to put the remote's cct-brd inside the keyboard or mouse case.. or just leave it on the desk as an accessory...  Take the remote with you and no one would know how to power-up your desk.. especially if you hide the remote's plug-in unit, or made it inaccessible.. or simply locked it in a tamper-proof tin box, with holes for the cables...

Specialty keyboards and mouses could have power-remotes built-in...
