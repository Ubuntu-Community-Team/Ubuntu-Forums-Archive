Hello, I recently purchased the RØDE Wireless GO II. 

This is a microphone which has sound out both via an audio jack (working properly) and via usb (where there is constant loud noise). 

It is recognized, and appears to be recording sound in time with what I say (for example, blowing into the mic raises the volume of the noise). Sometimes it almost sounds like words I say are going through, although the distortion is very high. I am not sure what is the error, but I have tried numerous fixes so far with no success.

The system is: 
B450M DS3H V2
AMD Ryzen 5 PRO 4650G
Ubuntu 22.04 LTS

This microphone is advertised as a "generic usb audio" device and indeed I have tried it on some other systems (windows) and it loads without any drivers. 

Please advise on how to proceed.
