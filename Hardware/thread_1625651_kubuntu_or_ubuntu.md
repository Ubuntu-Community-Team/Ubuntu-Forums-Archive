### Post by snowpine on 2010-11-19
A very frequently-asked question. If you google "kubuntu vs ubuntu" or "gnome vs kde" you will have hours of reading. :)

The short answer is that you should use Ubuntu if you like the Gnome desktop environment, and Kubuntu if you like KDE. If you don't know the difference between Gnome and KDE, burn a Live CD of each and try them for yourself. Both are equally well-suited for your goals of "wine gaming working, listening music, browsing web."
