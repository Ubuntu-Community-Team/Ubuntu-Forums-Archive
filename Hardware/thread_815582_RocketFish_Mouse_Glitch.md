### Post by jhoe on 2008-06-02
You mentioned BTNX, I use BTNX with my Logitech MX Revolution.  When you set your mouse up, the purpose is to only detect one button at a time and set each button up accordingly.  Give BTNX a try, it works great.  My mouse has 12 buttons and I had to set up each button at a time.  It was a bit trying but I have had it set up since 7.04 this way and have had no problems.  You may also have to do some editting with your x11.conf, I will leave that to the experts though as I don't have your mouse to give you good advice in that respect.
