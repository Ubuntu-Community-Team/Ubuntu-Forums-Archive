> **d3drocks said:**

They all seem pretty comparable with the major difference being the processor.  They have gone up a notch on the internals which just makes them a bit faster.  I will add that I upgraded my ram to 2G and swapped the 160G 5400RPM drive for a 200G 7200RPM that I had already.  The extra ram definitely helps. ### Post by gloken on 2009-10-03
One thing I might advise is that you try 8.10 rather than Jaunty. So far it seems that 8.10 supports the video drivers better, it's been a flawless experience, although I did have to enable madwifi drivers rather than the ones that came with it.

Jaunty had graphics drivers issues that caused instability once resolved. Karmic Koala didn't work at all.

I've done a lot of playing with distros in the last few weeks,and my top two recommendations right now would be Kuki or Intrepid.
