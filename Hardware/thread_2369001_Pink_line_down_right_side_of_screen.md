### Post by Autodave on 2017-08-17
If the line was there, I am thinking that you more than likely have a screen problem. Doesn't sound like a software problem or a video card problem.

One thing you can try, and I doubt it will work, but it won't cost a penny:

  	 	 	 	   1. Power down and disconnect power cord.
2. Unplug all peripherals.
3. Remove battery.
4. Wait 10 minutes.
5. Reinstall battery.
6. Connect power cord.
7. Boot computer and test to see if condition is gone.
8. If good so far, now you can reconnect peripherals.
