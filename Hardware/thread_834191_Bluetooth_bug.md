### Post by ravl on 2008-06-19
Last night, I was transfering a file from my laptop to my cellphone through bluetooth. It worked fine, as usual. But I accidentally deleted the pairing on my cellphone, so I re-paired the phone with the laptop.
The dialog asking for the pin appeared, and the devices were paired succesfully. Then the notification popped up, saying that the devices were connected, and with my cellphone's MAC address.
Right then, the entire system locked up. I couldn't switch to different terminals with Ctrl+Alt+F1, or restart the X server with Ctrl+Alt+Backspace. The caps lock light was blinking like once per second, which I found very odd.
I had to shutdown the laptop with the power button.
There seem to be no consequences to this, I think it might have happened because the laptop already had my phone as paired.
