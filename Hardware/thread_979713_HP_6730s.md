### Post by wwwclaes on 2008-11-19
I've bought a similar 6730B, running 64 bit Ubuntu 8.10. Altogether, it's been a relatively smooth ride from my previous Dell Inspiron 6000 running Windows XP.

I appear to have Broadcom wireless...

85:00.0 Ethernet controller: Broadcom Corporation NetLink BCM5787M Gigabit Ethernet PCI Express (rev 02)

...and it has been working out of the box.

I think the support for the Intel 4500MHD graphics card seems a bit rudimentary and extending the display to an external monitor does not work that well (only a mirror of the laptop display and switching back and forth doesn't work). But maybe it is something I've missed. But I believe the S-version has an ATI card.

The FN keys for adjusting the screen brightness don't work either. The sound needed a little tweak to work.
