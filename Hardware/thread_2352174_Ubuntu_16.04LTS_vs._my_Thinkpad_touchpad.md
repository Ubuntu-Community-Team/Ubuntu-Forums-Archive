### Post by Fire_Chief on 2017-02-13
Those three buttons at the top are positioned there for a more natural use of the eraser head mouse pointer in the middle of the keyboard. I started using this (since the thinkpad ones are actually pretty awesome) and just disabled the touchpad altogether. Probably not the answer you were looking for but I promise, once you get used to it, the touchpad will seem very inefficient. The buttons embedded in the touchpad (at the bottom) should get disabled with the touchpad when it is turned off in Ubuntu or in the BIOS.

Cheers!
