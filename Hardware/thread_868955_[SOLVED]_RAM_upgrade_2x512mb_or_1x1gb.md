### Post by phidia on 2008-07-24
There are some memory sellers online that can check your system and tell you the ram type and max amount you can use. I did that and for your system it looks like you are correct 1GB is Max so do what's cheapest. (sometimes the maximum might change but that's only if the motherboard will recognized a larger max& a larger ram stick is released)
