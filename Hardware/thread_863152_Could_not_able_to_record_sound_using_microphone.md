### Post by silkstone on 2008-07-18
Right-click on the volume control icon (top right) and select 'Open Volume Control'. Make sure that the appropriate Microphone input is enabled and the slider is up the top. The 'Mic Boost' sliders need the normal Mic slider enabled and turned up to work.
