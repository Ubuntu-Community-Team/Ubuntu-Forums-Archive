### Post by kat_ams on 2011-06-01
In the file 

sudo vim /usr/Xerox/xpxx/ppd/xr_WorkCentre5222.ppd

Note: the 5222.ppd is for the 5222, 5225, & the 5230 models

change the line 
*cupsFilter: "application/vnd.cups-postscript 0 /Library/Printers/Xerox/filter/XeroxPSFilter"

to
*%*cupsFilter: "application/vnd.cups-postscript 0 /Library/Printers/Xerox/filter/XeroxPSFilter"

You are adding at *% to the front of the line

Then in the Ubuntu Printer manager, just re-add the ppd by clicking on the change button after Make and Model. Or readd the printer. When it asks for the ppd browse to the ppd and choose use new PPD as is.
