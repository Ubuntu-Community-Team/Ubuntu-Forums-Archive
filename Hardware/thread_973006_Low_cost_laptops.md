Best Buy has the MSI Wind for $349.  [http://www.bestbuy.com/site/olspage.jsp?skuId=9078534&type=product&id=1218016541018]("http://www.bestbuy.com/site/olspage.jsp?skuId=9078534&type=product&id=1218016541018")
