### Post by FerretDefunct on 2010-11-22
Running 10.10 as well, on an Eee PC 1005PE and having similar issues. In my case the brightness hotkeys are erratic at best. Moving up and down the scale jumps from brightness levels all over the place, and at certain levels the screen blacks out completely.

UPDATE: Updating the BIOS of my 1005PE to v1202 seems to have done the trick for me. Although I think our problems were a bit different. Now I seem to be in the same boat as you: my brightness levels are normalized (no more sporadic flickering etc.) but it isn't quite as bright as I've found Windows 7 to be. Regardless I'm just happy to have it a viewable level now.
