### Post by pqwoerituytrueiwoq on 2018-07-28
The main thing you need to watch on the motherboard is the lan/wlan and audio chipsets
if you are going to be using the GPU's audio output the audio chipset is less important

If you are planning to overclock pay very close attention to the VRM, the motherboard makers have been doing some sketchy/misleading stuff
