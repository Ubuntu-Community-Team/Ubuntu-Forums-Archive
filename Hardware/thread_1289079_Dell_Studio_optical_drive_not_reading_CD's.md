### Post by cnbiz850 on 2009-10-14
Well, guess there was no interim solution.  I called Dell and they found that it was a hardware problem.  (Can you believe it?  I got it for still less than two weeks including diagnostic time)  So they agreed to send me a replacement machine.  I hope this time it is OK.

### Post by Haventfoundme on 2009-10-14
I had the same issue with my Dell Studio 1737, two days after I received it and installed ubuntu on it through the slot dvd drive, I had to return it to dell because the slot drive didn't function anymore. 
When I got the laptop back I discovered that they downgraded the bios version from A03 to A01. I think that was the problem because no hardware was changed out. 
I hope this helps anyone in the future. FYI the laptop was refurbished of course but everything works and has been stable for the past five months after I got it back for the slot drive repair.:guitar:
