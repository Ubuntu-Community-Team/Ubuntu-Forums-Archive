### Post by myoldhouse on 2008-10-16
Hey Ryo,

According to the messages here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=938500&highlight=motherboard]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=938500&highlight=motherboard")
and here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=935279&highlight=motherboard]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=935279&highlight=motherboard")
you don't need to do anything special to switch from AMD to Intel CPU and a new motherboard. Just hook up your hard drive(s) to your new rig and away you go! All the new hardware should be detected and the correct modules loaded at boot time.
