Is every thing working to your satisfaction?

The same questions would apply in regards to the video display. Do you have a reason for using another driver? With video drivers you can experiment.

I have Nvidia and it has been my experience over the past few months that the Nouveau open source driver has improved considerably. Whereas the Nvidia drivers were buggy causing a broken desktop. I use the open source video driver. It is more than sufficient for my hardware. I cannot comment on what is better for your hardware.
