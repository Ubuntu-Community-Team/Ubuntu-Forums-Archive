### Post by 2F4U on 2012-09-11
Logitech's Linux support is almost non-existent and this is unlikely to change. However, there are quite some configuration guides available for this particular mouse. I don't own this mouse but from what I read it seems as all the hardware buttons are recognize and it is "only" a matter of mapping the buttons to functions. Here are two articles on how to do that:

[http://forums.logitech.com/t5/Mice-and-Pointing-Devices/Guide-for-setup-Performance-MX-mouse-on-Linux-with-KDE/td-p/517167](http://forums.logitech.com/t5/Mice-and-Pointing-Devices/Guide-for-setup-Performance-MX-mouse-on-Linux-with-KDE/td-p/517167)
