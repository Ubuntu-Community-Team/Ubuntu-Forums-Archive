### Post by desertdog on 2010-06-24
This scanner is not supported by sane. 

If you are adventurous, you could try to see if it is a clone of the 4800 or 3840, which are supported by the sm3840 sane backend.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CheckIfScannerIsClone](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CheckIfScannerIsClone)



Edit: Upon further research, your sm 4700 scanner uses the Ali m5615 chip, whereas the sm3840 has a SQ Scan 08 chip. So it is unlikely that this technique will work.
