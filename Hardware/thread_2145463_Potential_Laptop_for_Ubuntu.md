### Post by 2F4U on 2013-05-16
This laptop basically has a CPU which is often used in so-called netbooks, so don't expect high performance from it. I can easily imagine that Ubuntu with Unity is too much for this machine. You should maybe aim for Xubuntu or Lubuntu. The disadvantage of both is that Xubuntu LTS has only three years of support and Lubuntu doesn't have a LTS version at all.
If a used or refurbished machine would also be acceptable, you might get a good deal, for example on a ThinkPad or similar business machines.
