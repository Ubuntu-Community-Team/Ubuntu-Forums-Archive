### Post by fauigerzigerk on 2009-03-28
For me, the year of Linux on the desktop/laptop has finally come. During the past 10 years or so, I tried to install various Linux distributions on my various laptops about once every 6 months. I always reverted back to Windows or Mac because some hardware related stuff didn't work properly, and I couldn't make it work on the weekend I had given myself to do it.

Yesterday, I installed the Ubuntu Jaunty beta on my Dell Latitude E5500 and everything worked out of the box. No tweaking, no exceptions. Wireless, sound, graphics acceleration, power management and CPU scaling, suspend and resume, etc, they all work.

So let me say thanks to all the Linux and Ubuntu folks who made this happen. Thank you for your good work. Keep it up!
