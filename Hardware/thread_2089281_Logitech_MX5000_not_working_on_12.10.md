### Post by tomse on 2013-03-25
For this to work (in Kubuntu) I had to use an other keyboard first.

After logging on, and plugging in the bluetooth adapter, it's a matter of going to Bluetooth in System Settings.

Add device (not the one in the middle) like you would any other bluetooth device.

While it's searching, click on the connect button in the bottom of the keyboard. wait till the keycode box to appear, and add the code in the MX5000 keyboard (don't use the num keyboard for this).

multimedia keys still needs to be configured, but the volume works out of the box.
