My Nvidia GTX260 worked out of the box on Kubuntu.  The issues with Nvidia drivers seem largely when using Gnome and Nvidia and Compiz-KDE doesn't seem to have the issues--though IIRC they can be traced back to a few problem code lines in the Nvidia drivers.
