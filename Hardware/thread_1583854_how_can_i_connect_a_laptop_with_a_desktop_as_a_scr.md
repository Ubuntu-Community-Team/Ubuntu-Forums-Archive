### Post by Dawei87 on 2010-09-28
when you say you are connecting it to a desktop, i assume you mean you are plugging it in directly to the monitor? you can try opening Monitors in the Preferences menu, and hit the detect displays button, if you see two monitors in the diagram just click on the one you want and it should hook up fine. what kind of monitor is it (screen size and resolution)? sometimes i have seen errors trying to do this when you connect to a monitor with an aspect ratio or resolution that isnt supported by the laptop graphics card.
