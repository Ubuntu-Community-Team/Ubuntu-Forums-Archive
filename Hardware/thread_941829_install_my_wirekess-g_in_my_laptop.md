### Post by Kevbert on 2008-10-08
Hi and welcome to Ubuntu.
You need to tell us what wireless chipset your adapter is based upon.
Go to Applications-Accessories-Terminal and enter the following;
```
lspci
```
This will tell us what chipset the wireless adapter is based upon. You can copy the output by selecting the output and pressing Ctrl-Shift-C to copy and Ctrl-V to paste here.
