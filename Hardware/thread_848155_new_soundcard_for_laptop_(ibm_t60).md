### Post by luksol on 2008-07-21
I don't think that you can upgrade your internal sound card. You will have to buy external one. If you have ExpressCard on your laptop you can use X-Fi Xtreme Audio Notebook from Creative. If you dont have ExpressCard you can try an Audigy PCIMCIA sound card (but you might find it difficult to find on the market, I think it is an obsolete now). Otherwise you will have to buy external USB sound card. I like Creative SC, so I would go for something from this manufacturer.
