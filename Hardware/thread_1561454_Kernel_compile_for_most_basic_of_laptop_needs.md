### Post by IcarusR on 2010-08-26
To make good improvements to speed & resources you need to know exactly what hardware you have & are likely to use as well as filesystems.
There are tips in the graphical compile method.
Once done you will need to apply config to any later kernel used.

There are loads of howtos on the net, I suggest you read up before you start.

You could go through the motions of selecting config to see what is available & make some notes, then search for answers to the inevitable questions. Then go back later & actually do it.
