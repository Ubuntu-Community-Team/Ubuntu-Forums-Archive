### Post by rwgale on 2010-07-14
Perhaps...
Enabling accessibility under mouse preferences would compensate for this problem.

1. Enable Accessibility (needs logout/login
2. Set doubleclick timeout to short under the General tab.
3. Accessibility (see attached .jng of the settings I tried.
(basically, using dwell-click to replace the doubleclick)

  I tried it (but with a working mouse), dwelling now acts like a double-click.  To minimize single-clicks becoming double-clicks I set the double-click timeout to short.  I don't know if that will beat your mouse's time, but it might help.  I found the dwell-clicking tolerable after a short feedback conditioning on my part.
