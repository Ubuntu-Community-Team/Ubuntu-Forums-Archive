### Post by _Duhhh on 2012-03-29
I am running 11.10 64-bit on an HP EliteBook 8740w (Intel I7-720QM CPU). I recently had to have a new motherboard installed, and after that, I noticed that only 1 CPU was active, even though the multi-CPU and HT options were selected in BIOS config. I tried turning off multi-CPU and HT, then re-booting, no matter what combination I tried, only 1 CPU was active.

HP was going to replace the mobo again when I tried resetting BIOS to factory defaults, then re-configuring again. This got all 8 cores running (also fixed my wireless mini-PCI card, which also stopped working when the mobo was replaced).

I don't know how many others will find themselves in this situation, but at least they have SOMETHING to find if they search hard enough.
