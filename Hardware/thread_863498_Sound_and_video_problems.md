### Post by phidia on 2008-07-18
To defeat the pnp (plug&play) you need to enter your bios. Different bios have different ways to do that, but it's usually entered by holding down keyboard keys at boot. There's a short guide on that [here]("http://www.techadvice.com/tech/B/BIOS_Enter.htm"). Once you are actually in the bios you will need to navigate to the pnp feature and disable it.
