### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-07-02
As long as there are no problems with the source disc (scratches and such) or your burner, FLAC is lossless. The best way to test for source/burner errors is to listen to the track for pops, skips, etc. If you hear any, try cleaning the disc and perhaps the CD-ROM laser (if it's old or you've put dirty discs in there). Also, make sure the burner is mounted securely and the disc is in the tray properly.
