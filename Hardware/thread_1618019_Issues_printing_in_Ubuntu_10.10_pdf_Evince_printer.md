### Post by JamesR777 on 2010-11-26
I seem to have fixed the problems (as described above) with my Lexmark E460dn laser printer when I was attempting to install a Canon inkjet printer on Ubuntu 10.10.

I installed the [Openprinting Gutenberg]("http://www.openprinting.org/download/printdriver/debian/dists/lsb3.2/contrib/binary-i386/") files and downloaded all the CUPS & development files from the Ubuntu software Centre.
Since installing these pieces of software all the pdfs on the Lexmark laser printer have printing perfectly with no missing pages. The Lexmark prints instantly now instead of having to wait and hope for ages.

Maybe this will help resolve your issues with the Dell laser printer?

- James
