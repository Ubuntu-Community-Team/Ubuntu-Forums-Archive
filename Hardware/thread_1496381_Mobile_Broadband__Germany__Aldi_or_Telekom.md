### Post by totoff on 2010-06-08
hi meread, i have not checked the aldi device but learned from german forums that it should work fine with 10.04.

however, given that the hardware works the more important question is the quality of the network. aldi uses the e-plus network that hasn't got a strong network outside urban areas (i know what i'm talking about as i'm e-plus customer myself).

you should consider vodafone prepaid "web sessions" usb stick (eur 29,90 for the stick) or the telekom prepaid offer if you travel outside major cities. both sticks are reported to work fine in the german forum.

hope that helps.

regards, totoff

edit: just have seen that you are on karmic. you should consider upgrading to 10.04 - it's much more compatible and stable.
