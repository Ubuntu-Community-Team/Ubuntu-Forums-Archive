### Post by Firezap on 2011-05-14
I got it working...I left the video card out and installed Xubuntu, then installed the latest nvidia driver from the software center. I put the GT240 card back in and rebooted. Everything including opengl appears to be working now.:guitar:Soooooooo, I guess this is solved, even though I did it myself lol. I bet some people would not be able to do this, especially if they do not have an integrated video card as backup....I hope this problem will be fixed in the next release. Because I had no problems running 10.04 and 10.10.
