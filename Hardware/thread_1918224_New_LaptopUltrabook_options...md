### Post by hanbo on 2012-01-31
I am in a similar situation. Tried a Macbook Pro 7.1, but had some issues and didn't really like it. 
So, i am looking now at the following devices:
Dell Vostro V131 - A rather light decent 13" with very good linux support (even ubuntu certified) but the display is not that good
Dell Latitude E5420 - 14" but not really light (2.0kg) also ubuntu certified
Acer S3 - super light 13" not sure how good linux support is
HP Folio 13 - Has same issues with backlight
HP 5330m - 13" with nice alu casing, is sold with linux preinstalled - so will probably work well
Asus UX31 - Has several issues
Dell Latitude E6320 and Lenovo Thinkpad T420s too expensive
