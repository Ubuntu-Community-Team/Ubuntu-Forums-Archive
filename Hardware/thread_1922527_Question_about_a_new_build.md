### Post by wolfen69 on 2012-02-09
You might get a slight improvement depending on what you are doing, but for regular everyday computing, you might not notice any improvements. I use a 2nd gen i5, and the biggest bottleneck is probably the hard drive. (can you say SSD?)

Even with virtualbox running, my laptop is still pretty darn fast. But I can't really tell you *not* to get an i7.
