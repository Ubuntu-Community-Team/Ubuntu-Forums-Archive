Thought your question might have been about Ubuntu not seeing 6GBs of ram, it is not.  Ubuntu sees all your ram, but is just using 6.1GBs at this time.  Other times it might use less or more.  Having unneeded ram is good, not having enough is bad.  Depending what you are doing, ram usage will go up and down.
