### Post by Peter Hartman on 2010-12-19
I had alot of difficulty with getting my broadcom wireless ( in a HP ( compaq ) )to work. After trying various solutions offered by a friend who is quite expert at this type of thing, and on this forum including loading the proper driver etc I realized it was a recognition problem during installation so:
[B]I INSTALLED UBUNTU 10.04 WHILE HAVING THE LIVE CD RUNNING AND CONNECTED TO THE INTERNET
[/B]steps:
put in CD
click the TRY option not the load option
click on the drivers icon at upper right -next to wireless icon
load the broadcom driver
connect to internet
click on install UBUNTU icon on desktop while still connected to internet
after installation do the standard restart
click on the wireless icon after being fully rebooted (takes a minute after desktop is up)
connect to the wireless network of your choice
configure to connect automaticly if desired

please post if this works or doesnt for you!!!
enjoy and thanks to all who have helped this process work!!!
