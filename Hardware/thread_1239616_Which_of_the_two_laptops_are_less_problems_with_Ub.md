### Post by phantom3113 on 2009-08-13
Just from those specs, it will be *very* difficult to determine which would be more fit for Ubuntu. Some of the biggest things to look for when choosing a computer that you plan on running Ubuntu on, is Graphics card (Intel, Nvidia, ATI) and Wireless (Realtek, Atheros, etc.) I cannot express enough how important the brand of Graphics card is. Currently, your best bet would be Nvidia (on that front).
