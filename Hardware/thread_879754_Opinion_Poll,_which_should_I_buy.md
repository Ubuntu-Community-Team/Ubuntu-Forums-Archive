### Post by phidia on 2008-08-04
I have a hp pavilion with an nvidia gpu. You know this is one of those trade off things. the intel GMA cards don't perform as well for gaming but if you search the hardware & laptop forum you will see plenty of suspend-resume issues with nvidia cards. That's not to say all laptops with nvidia cards are a problem but some definately are going to be a problem.
If you want the nvidia graphics then from my own experiances and what I've see posted try to get the intel cpu and wireless card. HP does have that configuration in it's Pavilion line and I wish I had known before I purchased  the pavilion I have that intel, for a laptop, is more likely to be the better choice.
