### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-09-25
Remove the battery.  Try booting with just the AC cord.  If you can't boot, then get a new power brick.  If you can boot and get a stable system, then get a new battery.

Your motherboard may need a cleaning.  Which is difficult to perform if you have not taken your laptop apart before.  If you have bulging or leaking capacitors, then it might take a few power cycles to get to a full boot.
