### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-12-14
A more important concern should be to avoid hybrid-graphics PCs -- which I think include the HP DV6x and HP DV7x machines.  These don't work well under Linux due to the lack of support for hybrid graphics.

For more info on hybrid graphics, see the link below:
