### Post by tomsavage on 2009-08-05
This is what worked for me...
Go into your Orange account set-up http:\\192.168.1.1 and enter your user ID and password (normally 'Admin' for both). Then go into 'Security' then 'Perameters'. Then reduce the security level to 'weak'. Then press the WiFi button on the back of your livebox (whatever you do, DO NOT press the 're-boot' button!) and you should be able to log in on your lap-top. Having logged in, you can then reset the secrtity level to 'High' (or whatever) and it should be OK from then on.
I hope this helps!
