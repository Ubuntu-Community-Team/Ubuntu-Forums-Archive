I think you will have no chance to change the graphics chip, so you have to make the best of your i945.

### Post by fernandoc1 on 2010-07-04
Nvidia is the company that best support Linux and many Unix based system, with great drivers.
If you are looking for the best, then NVIDIA is your choice.
If Nvidia weren't the best graphic card it would never be installed on the Macs, XBoxs and Playstation 3.
You can go to Nvidia's web site and see that they supports all platforms that you can choose. From Windows to FreeBSD.
However, the best open source drivers are from Intel.
Intel hardware is better integrated in Ubuntu, but it lacks of performance.
That is my experience, with more than 30 machines, with different hardware, that I had tested Ubuntu and other Linux distributions.
