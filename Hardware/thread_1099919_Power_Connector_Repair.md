### Post by nbo10 on 2009-03-18
Your best would be to google for a guide to disesemble the model you own. General outline would be to remove battery, remove all screws from the bottom. remove, HD, RAM, wireless card, modem, optical drive. remove lcd, keyboard. then you can pop apart the top and bottom of the case.  then remove the MB and access the power jack.

Be warned that it's not a trival task to reassemble the laptop.
