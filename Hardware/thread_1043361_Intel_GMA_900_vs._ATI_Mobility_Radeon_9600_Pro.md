### Post by jespdj on 2009-01-18
The ATI card is much more powerful than the Intel GMA 900. However, ATI (still) does not have really good Linux drivers, so it's possible that you get a problem to make it work fully.

If you want powerful graphics that work well on Linux, then an nVidia graphics card is the best choice.
