### Post by 00b00nt00 on 2009-01-11
1.5 Ghz to 2 Ghz offers only a marginal speed increase of 33% (all other things being equal). However, you'll have to contend with an increase in heat, which may be even greater than the performance you gain by overclocking the laptop. For this reason, I can't recommend overclocking.

A safer way to increase your computer's performance would be to increase the amount of installed RAM.
