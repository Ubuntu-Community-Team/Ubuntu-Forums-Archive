### Post by jdbower on 2010-06-05
I recently got a [Logitech Presenter R800]("http://www.logitech.com/en-us/mice_pointers/presentation_remote/devices/5873") and I thought I'd report that it works perfectly out of the box with both Ubuntu 8.10 and 10.04 (and likely most other flavors) paired with the stock OpenOffice.org flavor.  The USB dongle appears as a standard HID device, with the slide forward/back mapped to PgUp/PgDn, Black Screen mapped to ., and Start Presentation mapped to F5.  The [Presenter R400]("http://www.logitech.com/en-us/mice_pointers/presentation_remote/devices/5993") seems to be the same dongle and should also work.

I'm not sure about this "plug in hardware and it just works."  Where's the downloading drivers, editing esoteric parameters, recompiling the kernel, downloading three different packages manually and hoping things work from the Linux I grew up with? :)
