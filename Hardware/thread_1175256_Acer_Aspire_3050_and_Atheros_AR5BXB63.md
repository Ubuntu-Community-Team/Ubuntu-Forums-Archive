### Post by tommcd on 2009-06-01
Boot up the 9.04 live CD and go to: system > administration > hardware_drivers, and see if the atheros is recognized and gives you the option to enable it. You will have a better chance of getting wifi working on 9.04 than with 7.10. Even if wireless does not work right out of the box, chances are you can get it to work with a little tweaking.
Also, 7.10 is no longer supported. You should at least upgrade to 8.04 LTS. Ubuntu 9.04 is a very solid release though imo, except for the widely documented intel graphics driver problems.
