### Post by Lisiano on 2012-05-15
You mean Android? Droid is a smartphone made by Motorola, not an OS (Which is made by Google). Either way, you can run it side-by-side at the same time (Like a VM but without the performance loss) but usually you can't install it as a separate OS, else I'd do it on my own Android Tablet. Poke around on [XDA-Developers]("forum.xda-developers.com") in the section for your tablet or in [Android Development and Hacking]("http://forum.xda-developers.com/forumdisplay.php?f=564")
