### Post by coffeecat on 2010-11-19
I'm sorry that no one responded to your thread and I'm glad that upgrading to Maverick helped you. One of the reasons you may have had no response is that ATI is still less popular than nvidia for historical reasons, and there are consequently fewer people with experience of your card. What those who give a knee-jerk 'nvidia' response to requests for recommendations for graphics cards don't seem to realise is how much the open-source ATI driver has improved recently. Your experience going from Lucid to Maverick bears this out. You may be interested in this Cafe thread with started in 2009 but which is still very much alive:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1238129](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1238129)

I'm very happy with my ATI card having replaced a much hotter-running nvidia card for it. I shall probably stick with ATI.

Good luck with Maverick.
