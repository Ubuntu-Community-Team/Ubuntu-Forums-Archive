### Post by colgan2 on 2016-12-05
SUCCESS in two ways!!!

best news first.... i went into

Settings hardware Sound ..output mode High Fidelity Playback {A2DP Sink} and both my bluetooth Phillips Headphones work perfectly and then I got my MONIC PSYC bluetooth speaker to work... WITHOUT needing the USB adapter?!! so somethings is working the sound... (is a soundcard just to physically make an analogue signal? )

but if you need a hardwired solution.. the Tecknet USB sound card from amazon turned up today as well and it works fine (£9) delivered... so as far as I'm concerned the Bluetooth solution which I didn't know was available solves the problem... and the Tacknet USB stick is icing on the cake.

so now i have full functionality on the ASUS E200HA. 

woohoo!!

only issue is micro SD card reader doesn't work, but as 64GB mini USB sticks are £12 in Tesco I'll just fill the two USB slots and save myself the trauma of solving that!
