### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2011-04-20
No, do not get a laptop with Nvidia Optimus (the N53). They don't support Optimus on Linux. Get a laptop with SandyBridge graphics for good compiz and video accel support. If you want to do 3D and gaming, get an ATI 4250 laptop and run Catalyst.
