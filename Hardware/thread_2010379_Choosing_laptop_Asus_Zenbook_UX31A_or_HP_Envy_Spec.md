### Post by Thirion on 2012-06-25
Hi,

why do you prefer the HP Spectre?

In my opinion the Zenbook is the perfect Ultrabook/Laptop. Its very thin and light - but the case makes it very robust (i had a Toshiba Sattelite for some days and i always feared it would break ...). The non-glare (i will never buy a glossy dislay again) full-HD display is amazing (according to tests the best display out there - and in my opinion one of the most important things on a laptop). Also the Zenbook hardware is (because of the newer Intel generation) better than the HP hardware.

Currently Ubuntu on the Zenbook works ok, there are some annoying things you have to fix and some things (for me quite unimportant - like FN-keys ...) don't work yet - but im sure they will work soon. I'm also quite sure Ubuntu on the HP works quite well, because the hardware on Ultrabooks is similar and it works on most other Ultrabooks.
