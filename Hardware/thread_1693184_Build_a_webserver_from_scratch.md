### Post by atti84it on 2011-02-22
I think it depends on the question: what do you want to do with it?

if it is for local development, just install a LAMP on your pc.
if it is for small office application you could consider installing apache on a computer that you normally use for normal use. or maybe just buy a power computer from a mall with 8 gigs of RAM and use it as a server.
if you need it as a dedicated server for a big company then you should look for something different..
