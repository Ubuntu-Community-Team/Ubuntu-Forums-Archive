### Post by francofunes on 2010-09-09
I think is better option a Nvidia GPU than ATI. You have more support among Nvidia Propietary driver, or opensource drivers like Noveau or Nv... At least, I've had Nvidia (FX5200, 7050, 7300GT, 9500GT) for a long time, and I didn't remember problems on Ubuntu. Currently I
