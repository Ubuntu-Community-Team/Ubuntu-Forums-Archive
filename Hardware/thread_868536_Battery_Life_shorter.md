### Post by Dark_Stang on 2008-07-24
Ubuntu doesn't know how long your battery will last until you drain it once. Run it on battery power until your laptop shuts off. Just make sure you aren't working on anything important or doing updates around the time you think it might die, otherwise you're going to be fairly angry.
