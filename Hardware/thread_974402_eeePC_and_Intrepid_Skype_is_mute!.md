### Post by pascalm on 2008-11-26
Hi,

I had the same behaviour with an Asus eeePC 900. Actually, the external mic is enabled by default. You may want to use the "Front mic" instead. On my system it is located on the left of the camera.

In the volume control panel (Dble-click on the loudspeaker icon), click the preferences button, tick the "Input source | option" and click OK. Back in the panel, a new tab called "Options" appears, select the front mic as input source.

But the sound is quite bad, I have to use the boost front mic setting which adds sounds from the environment like the fan noise etc... I think I need a headset anyway ;)

HTH.
