### Post by sanderj on 2011-07-21
I have a netbook running Ubuntu: it's a Samsung NC10, and it's great. 

My experience / advice:
[LIST]
[*]The 1GB and the Atom processor work well.
[*]Avoid the GMA 500 GPU (if it's still there): no driver for (recent) Linux
[*]If possible, find a "matt" display; my laptop (not my netbook) has a shining screen, and it's annoying.
[*]If you're buying at a physical shop: boot Ubuntu on it from a USB stick and check everything works: screen, Wifi.
[/LIST]
