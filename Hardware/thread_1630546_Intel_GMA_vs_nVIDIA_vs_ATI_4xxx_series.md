### Post by Fafler on 2010-11-26
Theres no binary driver for the GMA family. You can get a good basic comparison here:
[http://www.notebookcheck.net/Comparison-of-Laptop-Graphics-Cards.130.0.html](http://www.notebookcheck.net/Comparison-of-Laptop-Graphics-Cards.130.0.html)
ANY discrete GPU you can get is bound to be a lot faster than the Intel IGP's.

I have little hands on experience with the HD4xxx series, but in general the Nvidia drivers seem to be better. I'd pay the price and go for a Nvidia GPU.
