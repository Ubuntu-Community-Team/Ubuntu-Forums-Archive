### Post by Shunsuke_01 on 2008-10-09
OK, I have found the solution and I'm just sharing it incase anyone else has this problem. :)

First you go to System > Preferences > Screen Resolution

Then you should see two displays. One will be your external LCD, and the other will be your laptop's LCD. Now you select the laptop and change its resolution from whatever it is to "off".
