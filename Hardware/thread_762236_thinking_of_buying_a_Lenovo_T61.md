### Post by djs_uk on 2008-05-04
I'm running Ubuntu 8.04 on a newly purchased T61.  I liked that the nvidia drivers could be installed with one or two clicks.  I'm still adjusting to the T61 though.  Previously I've had the T41, and its keyboard was much better.  

So far the only problem I've noticed with running Ubuntu on my T61 is that the capslock light is not working under X windows. I.e., if I press capslock, the LED does illuminate (however characters are echoed in uppercase).  This seems to be a problem with X, since the LED works from a pure terminal, and also works at the login prompt.  Very odd. 

Oh, another problem I've had is that xine / totem / vlc have all the colors wrong when playing DVDs.  The video is very blueish.  This problem is intermittent too. Definitely something to do with the software.
