### Post by sudanmas on 2010-10-11
No really issues to speak of at this point.  Some observations:

o Running a Gateway LT3701h NetTop (smaller than laptop, but larger than a standard
netbook), 2gb ram, AMD Athlon 64 processor, 250Gb HD, ATI Radeon X1200 video card

o Previously running 10.04 LT

o Did an inplace upgrade to the desktop version of 10.10 (I didn't see the LT version of listed as an inplace upgrade from within 10.04 LT)

o Post install, laptop hung on reboot.  Cold boot brought up grub.  Selected first kernel listed (the list hasn't come up in subsequent boots so I can't tell you which kernel I used.

o Subsequent boots were pain free (and faster than 10.04)

o Wireless worked on first logon and auto connected much faster than 10.04

o Wireless signal strength is stronger

o Visual effects set to none.  Switching to Normal or Extra works temporarily before video scrambles and becomes unreadable.

o Moovidia didn't launch in 10.04  Launches in 10.10 but video immediately scrambles and becomes unreadable

o Suspend works again (hurray!!)  I don't use hibernate.  Takes too long to come back up.

o Flash support works fine

I only use my nettop to read e-mail, surf the interweb and watch movies so I can't provide many other details

I'm not sure if doing an inplace install helps, but I had problems doing fresh installs in the past.

Rob
