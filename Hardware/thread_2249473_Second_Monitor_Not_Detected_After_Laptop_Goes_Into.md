### Post by pbjoiner on 2014-10-22
I am using a Lenovo T430s with Ubuntu Trusty. I frequently move from the company's office to my home to work. I don't always remember to shut down when I do. I find that the system will not detect the second monitor if I wake the laptop from sleep mode after chaning location. I suspect that the fact that the two external monitors are different manufacturers is a contributing factor.

I found that if I shut down, reboot with the machine undocked, shut down again, and reboot after docking then whatever setting blocks the second monitor is cleared. The system will then detect the second monitor and apply the appropriate settings for that model.

Hope this helps.
