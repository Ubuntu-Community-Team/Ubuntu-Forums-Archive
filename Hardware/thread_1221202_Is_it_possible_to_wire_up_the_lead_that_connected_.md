### Post by B-D_ on 2009-07-24
If you replaced your PSU then you have a live 12V line available all the time in your computer from your PSU.

It's in the big connector [OOOO]... i don't know which pins are the right ones, you should use a voltmeter to find the 0V and 12V pins. If it shows -12V you got the right ones, just in reverse.

Then all you have to do is cut the old power connector off and somehow connect the 0V and 12V wires to appropriate pins in the PSU socket.

(ab)using old Y cable, SATA disk cable PSU connector converter, or some old connector from old fan comes to mind... connect the 2 wires to wires of the connector and plug the connector in the big socket.
