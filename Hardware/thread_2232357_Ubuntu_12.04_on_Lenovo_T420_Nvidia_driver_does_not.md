ral2; Hi !

Hybrid graphics is somewhat of a problem in linux, as Nvidia offers no support. There might exist alternatives:
See:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Config/HybridGraphics](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Config/HybridGraphics)
[http://www.webupd8.org/2013/08/using-nvidia-graphics-drivers-with.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2013/08/using-nvidia-graphics-drivers-with.html)
[https://wiki.debian.org/Bumblebee](https://wiki.debian.org/Bumblebee)
[http://rudrageek.com/linux-now-supports-hybrid-graphics-systems-ubuntu-13-10/](http://rudrageek.com/linux-now-supports-hybrid-graphics-systems-ubuntu-13-10/)

Best I can offer, is try and see what works in your use case.
