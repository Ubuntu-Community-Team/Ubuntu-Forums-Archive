### Post by rubenverweij on 2010-05-16
I don't know if this will work, I just read here on a forum about the previous model e100.
>    1. Backup all of the data from your E100 to your hard drive, including the following
          * /E100.sys
          * /tuner.dat
          * /System/Rating.bin
          * any media/text files you currently have on your E100
   2. Go to System > Advanced and select Format Internal Memory
   3. Once the format is completed connect it to your GNU/Linux box. It should autoload as E100, and you can then drag and drop your backed up files with no data loss.

[http://www.linuxquestions.org/questions/linux-hardware-18/iriver-e100s-internal-memory-doesnt-automount-682289/#post3387917](http://www.linuxquestions.org/questions/linux-hardware-18/iriver-e100s-internal-memory-doesnt-automount-682289/#post3387917)
