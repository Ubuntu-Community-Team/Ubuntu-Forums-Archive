### Post by cjazz on 2008-05-02
The Linux Foundation's Open Printing database's entry for your printer

[http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-x1190](http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-x1190)

says it works perfectly, which I have to say is pretty unusual for Lexmark. If you'll notice, it also says the Lexmark Z600/Z601 driver works great with a model similar to yours. It has a link to instructions for installation for Ubuntu. Unfortunately, that link failed to come up when I clicked on it. I believe there are instructions for downloading and installing the driver if you select the Lexmark Z600 at the (above referenced Open Printing website.
