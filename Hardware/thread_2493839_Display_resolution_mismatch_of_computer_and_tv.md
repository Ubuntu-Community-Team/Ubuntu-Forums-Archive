### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2023-12-31
Funny enough the problem is usually round the other way..
The early flat panels typically default to overscan for any external input.
Typically, there is a setting called "one-to-one pixel mapping" or direct scan or PC computer mode for ONE of the HDMI inputs.

Modern 4K flat panels also have similar input settings (& behave better) but is possible your video card output is the root cause.
What is the video card?
