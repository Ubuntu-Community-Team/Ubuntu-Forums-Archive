### Post by stwstl5926 on 2010-03-27
Alright, so this is a weird one...

Every once in a while, after I reboot my computer, my graphics driver has been deactivated (ATI driver, by the way). Now, it's no big deal to just go and reactivate it, reboot, and move on, but I really shouldn't have to do that, should I? Is this just a result of an update or something like that, or is this something weird that shouldn't be happening?

---

### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-03-30
If you're running the ATI restricted driver, it is locked to the kernel version last installed.  Every time you apply some updates that include a new kernel version, you will have to reactivate the driver.
