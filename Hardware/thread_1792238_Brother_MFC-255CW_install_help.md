### Post by thane1 on 2011-06-28
Brian;
     From the Brother website follow the installation instructions.  The first time I did this, I just installed the printer driver and had no success.  You need to install both the cups and the lpr drivers and then you will probably have your printer up and running.  Get them on the following page: 
 [http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/download_prn.html#MFC-255CW](http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/download_prn.html#MFC-255CW)
