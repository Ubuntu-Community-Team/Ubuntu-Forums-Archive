Hi,

I've got a fresh install of Xubuntu Intrepid on an X61. Ever since I installed, I noticed the screen was dim, and some fonts, especially in Firefox, badly legible (esp something like monospace bright on dark background). 

I made changes to /etc/X11/xorg.conf to bind the caps lock to be the compose key. I had a typo and that triggered X11 booting into failsafe "low resolution" mode. (Otherwise, my xorg.conf is/was unmodified, and I have since taken out that section about the compose key). 

In failsafe mode, suddenly my screen is noticeably brighter, and fonts in Firefox are much more easily legible. I checked and it seems the only difference between xorg.conf and xorg.conf.failsafe is that the "vesa" driver is specifically listed in the device section in xorg.conf.failsafe:
```

Section "Device"
        Identifier      "Configured Video Device"
        Driver          "vesa"
EndSection

```

Problem is, with the vesa driver, Compiz doesn't work. 

I'm now looking for some way to get the brighter and crisper display that I get with the vesa driver with a driver that will let me use Compiz, and am really grateful for any hints.
