### Post by Tharmas on 2008-11-27
The old generation of Sony music players that encrypted files finally works in Linux! I am now able to stop dual booting just to put my music in the player!

The solution is [JSymphonic]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/symphonic").
No more complicated half-solutions that never really worked. Now you just launch the program and import your music to the player.

Now if I could install Rockbox on it, it would be heaven...
