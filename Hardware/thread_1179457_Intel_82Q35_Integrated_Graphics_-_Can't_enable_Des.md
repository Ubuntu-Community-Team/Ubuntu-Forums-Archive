### Post by AirIntake on 2009-06-05
I don't think the open source driver for Intel graphics included with Ubuntu supports 3D effects, and there is no proprietary driver available from Intel, so you won't be able to enable visual effects as they require 3D support.

At least, I haven't found a way on my old desktop that has Intel graphics onboard.
