### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-07-09
You can try tightening the screws in the laptop.  If the laptop case is not properly grounded (because the case or lid is loose), then you can become the grounding path and be subject to voltage (12 to 19 VDC for the power supply, 90VDC for the backlight).  It could also be worn wires in the hinge which allows voltage from the graphics card or backlight to bleed into case.  Power supply jacks get worn and loose, so moving the power supply can change the connection condition.  If the ground connection is weak, then you become the ground--that fuzzy feeling.
