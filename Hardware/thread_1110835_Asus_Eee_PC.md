### Post by pewterbot9 on 2009-03-30
Regarding small window height:

If you have a spare monitor (like, for a desktop system), hook it up to your netbook. Then access "System>Preferences>Screen Resolution", turn "mirror screens" off (to create an extended screen), and drag netbook screen to reside directly underneath the desktop screen (so screens will align vertically instead of horizontally).

Now, when you can't access the bottom of a window (in order to save settings, for example), you need only access it via the top part of your netbook screen.
