### Post by jggenius on 2010-12-23
Wow, this was my first foray into the use of ubuntu.  I was able to get everything completely running on a Compaq Presario V5000 by searching and trying everything in the forums.  It even detected my 50" Samsung TV I hooked up to watch DVDs.  The external devices that work with no issue - Logitech Wireless keyboard and mouse (via USB), Internet tethering via an HTC G2 and USB, Wifi (although that was the biggest pain), several types of USB flash drives, and an external 15" HP monitor.  I am using the b43 standard drivers for the Broadcom 4318 chipset for Wifi for those still having problems.  I had to manually edit /etc/modules to make sure b43 loaded upon reboot.  This forum helped me a bunch: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1650856](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1650856).  Merry Christmas all!
