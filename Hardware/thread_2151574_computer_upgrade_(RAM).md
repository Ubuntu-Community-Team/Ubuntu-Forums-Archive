Going beyond 4 GB makes no sense if you are going to use Windows XP (which is limited to using around 3.7 GB of RAM at max - except for the very buggy x64-version).

Oh and by the way, in terms of computers and ageing, your machine is actually rather old: DDR2 RAM was replaced by DDR3 RAM in 2007 (that's six years ago - an eternity for computers ;))

Nevertheless, if that works for you that is great.
