### Post by The_Cavester on 2009-09-01
Hello Freind
I have one of these tablets and I know what you are up against.
In fact this one that I'm using right now is my second.
My first one had just the antenna and the spec's was misleading in that
you get the idea that your machine has the built-in BT. What is not told to you
is that you need a module to attach to the antenna, this module has a home
under the keyboard.( just about under the F7 or F8 keys)
I found the module part number by digging through the net, bought it, installed it and bingo I have BT. (with the help of "toshset")

But before you to crazy just check and see if you have the antenna first, otherwise
it is a real pain in the *** to put in, the antenna is sandwiched between the LCD
and the bazel edge.
hope this clears up anything for you.
