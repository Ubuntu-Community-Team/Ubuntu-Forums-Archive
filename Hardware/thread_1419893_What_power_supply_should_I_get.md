> **ChodeOfDoom said:**
> 
P.S.
I found a case I really liked with a 450 watt power supply and it was relatively cheap.  Would this work?

I wouldn't recommend getting the case/ps bundle, as the ps's included are usually no-name junk that fails soon after.

Apevia has been a good brand to me, and usually nicely priced.

Oh, and I'd recommend on getting the most wattage you can afford.  

500 is a good starting point, but it depends on whether or not you're going to stick a huge graphics hog card in your machine and how many peripherals you use.

I'd recommend 750 watts at least for your machine if you can get it.
