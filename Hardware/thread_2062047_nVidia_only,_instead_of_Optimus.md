### Post by typhoon_tip on 2012-09-24
No need to search around. Unless you have the option to turn off the integrated card in BIOS, and use discrete only, you are stuck with Bumblebee. So, BIOS is the only solution, but only a handful of laptop has that option (Lenovo ThinkPad being the most complete).
