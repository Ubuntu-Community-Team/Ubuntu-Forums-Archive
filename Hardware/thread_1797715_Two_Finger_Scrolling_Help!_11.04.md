### Post by Alwimo on 2011-07-05
Click on the power button in the top right of the screen, and select "System Settings". On the window that appears, down the bottom, you can select "Mouse". There's a touchpad tab up the top of the window that will appear, with options to set whether two fingered scrolling or edge scrolling works.

Hopefully it was just set to 'disabled', and it isn't a lack of compatibility.
