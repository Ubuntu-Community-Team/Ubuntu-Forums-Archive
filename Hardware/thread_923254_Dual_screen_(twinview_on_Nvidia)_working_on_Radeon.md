### Post by markbuntu on 2008-09-18
ATI released the linux drivers for the 48xx cards at the same time they released the cards. Unfortunately, most resellers declined to include the driver in their packaging. Nonetheless, you can get the driver and install it following the directions here, Method 2:

[http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Hardy_Installation_Guide](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Hardy_Installation_Guide)

The driver in the Hardy repository is not the latest one and does not support the 48xx series cards. The latest driver from ati is 8.9, released today:
