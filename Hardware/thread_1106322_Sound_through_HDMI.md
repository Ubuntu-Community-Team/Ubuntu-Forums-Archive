### Post by Cyberneticbrain on 2009-06-02
Solved!! My great full thanks towards the developers because under Ubuntu 9.04 the sounds through HDMI works perfect. Even at my asus T3 and V3 it works great. Sound now also recognised for the T3M3N8200! Make sure IE958 is selected and select HDA NVIDIA (Alsa mixer).

### Post by Cyberneticbrain on 2009-06-09
Not much to say about the hardware I am using. It are bare bones and you can find the specific details at the AsusTek site.
After installing 9.04 just click on the sound properties and adapt like I wrote that IE958 is selected and select HDA NVIDIA (Alsa mixer) Maybe it helpful to check at the Linux compatibility test results. But like in my case it wasn't working in 8.10 though works now in 9.04. [http://linux-tested.com/products/results/asus_v3-m3n8200.html](http://linux-tested.com/products/results/asus_v3-m3n8200.html)
This is a low cost bare bone, but for me nice to test linux and hdmi on my lcd television. With me the graphics are more stability using the 32 bit version.
The V3 is rather noisy while the T3 is silent.
