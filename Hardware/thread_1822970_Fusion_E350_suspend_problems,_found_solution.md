### Post by Redblade20XX on 2011-08-11
I've got the e-350 in my dm1z with an ati radeon graphics card and suspend works pretty good with the catalyst 11.7 drivers on Kubuntu 11.04. When I had Kubuntu 10.10, the suspend problem was fixed with the 11.6 drivers but didn't work for the 11.04 release. This was due to the catalyst driver not developed for the kernel I had with the 11.04 release. Anyways, the 11.7 release fixed the suspend option in the 11.04 release!! :P
