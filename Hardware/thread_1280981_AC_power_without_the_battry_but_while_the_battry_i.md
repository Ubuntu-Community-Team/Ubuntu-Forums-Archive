Most systems can not operate like this. (From my experience) The current supplied from the ac adapter is not enough to run the system very well. And can result in crashes as the battery is DC and supplies much more current.  My Macbook pro automaticly knocks the system to 1ghz and one slot of ram when the battery is out and computer plugged in. This makes me believe that most laptops now require the extara power form the battery to keep it running properly. Using the battery and power adapter at the same time does not run the battery through a charge cycle although you should follow proper charge, discharged on batteries. I could be completely wrong and please correct me if I am, I could do good with learning something today :)
