### Post by weatherman2 on 2014-10-14
Power issue sounds like a good guess.  Double check all of the power connections especially, if you unplugged anything from the power supply to the motherboard.

Power supplies do go bad - one of the most unreliable parts of a PC.  Could you have coaxed a failing power supply to its ultimate end by your cleaning?  I suppose it's possible.  Not everyone has a spare power supply laying around that they can swap in, I guess, but that's the first thing I'd try.

IF a different power supply has the same problem, then I'd start unplugging things from the board - expansion cards, other drives, etc. - and try to power up the computer with as few things connected as possible.
