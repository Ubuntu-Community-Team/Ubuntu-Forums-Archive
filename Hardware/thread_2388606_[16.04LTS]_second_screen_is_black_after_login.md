### Post by arcueidtoo on 2018-04-05
Hi all,

I'm running Ubuntu 16.04 LTS on a Dell Latitude 5580, with a second HP E231 screen.
After login, the second screen is turned out a short time, then goes black quickly.
In "System Settings > Displays", the second screen is powered on.
I have the same issue when I set the laptop out of sleep.

As workaround I wrote this script to switch off/on the second screen:
```

#!/bin/bash


xrandr --output HDMI-1-1 --off
sleep 1
xrandr --output HDMI-1-1 --auto --right-of eDP-1-1 --output eDP-1-1 --pos 0x250

```

The sleep looks mandatory : if I remove the "sleep 1", I'm not sure to get my second screen back.

With a native 16.04 LTS I don't have this issue. It appears after having installed hwe kernel: 4.13.0-38-generic.

Thanks for your help,
