### Post by TBABill on 2012-04-30
I would doubt you'll find low power consumption and an open source driver combination, at least that's my experience on a laptop with open source vs. ATi driver. I'd say the ATi cards often have great performance when using the proprietary driver, but not the Ubuntu-offered one. Get it straight from AMD and follow the online instructions to install. They work well in the versions I have used. I'd just look for one that also works well with the open source driver so you won't have blank screen issues.
