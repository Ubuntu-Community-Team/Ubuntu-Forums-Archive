### Post by buccaneere on 2009-01-19
A tip:

Before going into the closet, digging up an old CRT and power cord and confirming that the computer DOES work with a CRT, and then plugging your new LED into second computer (to confirm that your new LED is toast), and then confirming THAT by plugging the LED into a 3rd computer, check the power button on the LED to make sure it is turned on.

If it is turned OFF, it probably won't work regardless of what diagnostic tests you do.
