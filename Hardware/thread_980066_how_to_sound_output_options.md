Ok i was looking for 2 days on these forums and on the irc and i couldn't find a solution, i woke up thismorning and figured it out on my own.  lots of help on irc tho, but they were tired i'm guessing (it was 5am)

ok so if your laptop has 3 'headphone' jacks in the front of your laptop like mine and youw ant to switch where the sound comes out this is how i did it.

FYI i'm running ubuntu 8.10 and i'm using a dell inspiron 1525

1) top right corner of the screen (near the date) you have a volume control.  right click on that and select 'open volume control'
2) a window will pop up.  botton right corner will have a preferences button. click it
3) there will be a pretty long list of stuff.  experiment with the diferent options to see what they are set for, while playing music to see if it comes out the correct output/input source.

i was tryinkg to get it to come out my middle 'headphone' port (left was broken and right was input) and i found 'center' in the list and it was muted. as soon as i clicked unmute it worked. it was the best sound ive hearn in a long time (the sound of VICTORY!!!!!)

I hope i've helped someone, as i've been helped.
ty  and good luck.
