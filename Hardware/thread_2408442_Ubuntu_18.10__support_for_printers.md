### Post by Tadaen_Sylvermane on 2018-12-19
Doesn't matter what brand you use really. Inkjets are flat out way more expensive than they need to be. I'd suggest either buying ink on [www.ldproducts.com](www.ldproducts.com) or switching to a  laser printer. You mention black cartridge. If you are like me where 99.9% of your printing is black and white, laser is unbeatable. I came across an old Laserjet Pro 400 by HP. Have zero complaints. The cartridge in it was already half done when I got it, still going strong nearly a year and a half later. Fraction of the price of inkjet cartridges. The only reason I even keep my inkjet around is that it is also a scanner, else I'd have sold / recycled / tossed it awhile ago. Way to expensive to keep up with it. For as little color printing as I do it's probably cheaper for me to put stuff on a flash drive and take it to OfficeMax or Staples and have them do it.
