### Post by attma92 on 2012-06-21
Hey, I just got mine in the mail today! I'm downloading Ubuntu 12.04 as I type this, and I will be sure to get back to you on how well it runs. I will say, first, that you MIGHT want to reconsider not buying this particular laptop. I will give it to Lenovo for making a decent laptop, but there are much more better laptops for about the same price. I don't hate the laptop, but I don't seem to love it either. I'm sure it will grow on me in time. I bout mine for college mainly. I will still be taking my desktop, though. There is no way that I'd be able to use this thing for a long period of time. Reviews that I've read about thi laptop mention that it's really not for gaming, at all. I'm not a huge gamer at all, but I do enjoy playing minecraft quite a bit with my friends. I figured that almost any machine could run that game, but this laptop has a hard time keeping up. Sorry for all of the extra information. I spent days looking for reviews of this particular modle and found close to none, I just figured that you wouldn't mind hearing what I had to say about this laptop.
