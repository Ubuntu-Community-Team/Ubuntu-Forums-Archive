### Post by ryanlutz on 2009-11-28
Just got one of these after Thanksgiving. Installing the printer should be as easy as any other printer. Go to System > Administration > Printing. Then choose new printer. It should find your printer and set it up. Beware that it says CLP-310, though. Look over this [thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1313805") for the solution. It took me a quick search to find this, so it's always a good idea to search for it on the forums to see if someone else had the same issue. :D
