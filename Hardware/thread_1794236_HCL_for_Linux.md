### Post by gordintoronto on 2011-07-01
When I am considering hardware, I look it up on newegg.ca. If it's a mainstream device that's not brand new, it will have lots of reviews. Then I filter the reviews for "linux" and then for "ubuntu."

I recently bought a USB wireless adapter; the reviews pointed out that it was fine with recent kernels, such as in Natty or Mint 11. And it is!
