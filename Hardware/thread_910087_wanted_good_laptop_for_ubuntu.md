### Post by kpkeerthi on 2008-09-04
Any laptop with nvidia/intel graphics card and intel wireless chip will work fine (out-of-the-box) with Ubuntu. 

The Graphics (GPU) and wireless cards are the most problematic with Linux. That doesn't mean that they don't work but a bit hackish to get them working properly because some vendors (Broadcom, its you!) keep the specs proprietory. ATi GPUs are slowly becoming linux-friendly but nvidia & intel are your best bet.

Better yet, post the link to the laptop specs you are planning to buy and we will tell you if you should buy it or not :)
