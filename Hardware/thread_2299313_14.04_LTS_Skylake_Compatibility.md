### Post by shadytv on 2015-10-18
Linux *should* just see the new hardware and reinitialize everything at boot-up (Windows ties the key to the Mobo, that's why you cant do this in Windows). I would still make a back-up and prepare for the need to reinstall (Just in case it's necessary).

This is an area where Linux excels and why it's used in very large data centers. The OS needs to be as modular as the Hardware and Windows does not like major hardware changes.
