### Post by 4ebees on 2009-07-21
Thought I'd post a bit of good news.

I bought two cheapie MS VX500 webcams - no audio - for my kids machines so they could vid conference with relatives overseas and friends here.

Using Hardy 8.04 LTS I plugged 'em in and they work 'out of the box'.

A couple of cheap microphones on stands and off they went.

The rels use Skype (what can you do) so installed it and everything works as it should.

Thought I'd post this in case other people were thinking of what sort of cheap webcams to buy.
