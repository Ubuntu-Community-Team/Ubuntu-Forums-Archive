### Post by PhilMize on 2009-05-27
There's no doubt in my mind that it will run... and since dell ships it with 8.10 that means theres gotta be some proprietary driver support from dell. The only issue i can see you running into is that the graphics card is an intel chipset and currently 9.04 doesnt support some intel chipsets like on my lenovo y510. so that means no cool desktop effects... you would prob be better off just leaving it as is and waiting till 9.10 comes out where there is full support available.

I'm also planning on getting one of these, touchscreen, 2gig ram, bluetooth, everything available pretty much... and I would assume shipped with 8.10 everything works out of the box. But ive never bought a computer with linux pre-installed before so im not 100%.
