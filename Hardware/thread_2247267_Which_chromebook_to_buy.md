### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-10-07
A very recent consumer report on available Chromebooks in UK by Linux Format magazine, which were the following five:-

HP 14-q005sa
Asus C200
Toshiba CB30-102
Acer C720P
Samsung XE503C12

concluded that the HP was the best of those, but also commented on a Dell Chromebook 11 which was unavailable at the time, and suggested that an update of the Acer to 730 might be better.  I have no experience of any of these personally so can't say any more of use.
