### Post by mondoUNC on 2008-11-23
Wound up finally getting the drive formatted, stupid thing overheated and gave me the same problem. At least now I fully understand all the quirks of this drive, and I was able to recover all of my data. Looking into other backup options, but I would highly recommend to anyone to keep away from Maxtor drives, its been nothing but trouble with my laptop. If you do get one (mine was cheap) don't move huge chunks of data all at once and don't leave it on for too long.
