### Post by houstonbofh on 2016-03-04
Regressions like this are not uncommon.  As software "progresses" they add features and this makes it heaver.  As systems have more resources, they want to use them...  And damned be those of you with fewer resources! :)  Have you noticed how slow Firefox has gotten lately, even wihtout a distribution upgrade?

So, the answer may not be what you want to hear...  Work on optimization and disabling features, or roll back to 14.04, or user a lighter weight distribution. (Like xubuntu)
