### Post by Dennis N on 2016-03-11
Take a look at Zotac. The two Zotac barebones systems (add memory and disk drive) that I own are 100% Ubuntu compatable. I suspect they all are (like the Cylons, there are many models). Both of mine are the original ZBOX line - no problems at all with these for me. The oldest is now 6 years old. Now they have also C series and nano series. The specs on my newer one (ZBOX ID18-U) say is is dual-display capable. It has HDMI connection and DVI connection. I don't mount it on a monitor, so can't speak to that.
