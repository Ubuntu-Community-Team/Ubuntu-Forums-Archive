### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-01-11
Well, if the machine runs fine with two sticks in and not four it would be either the RAM or the RAM slots on the board. They do die. 

You say you have swapped them all around. Simple test for the slots. Take the two cards out of the slots where the machine is working and put them in the other two slots. Still working? No?

Put the OTHER two sticks in the slots where the first two were working. Still working? 

I'd be basically starting by testing each stick one at a time in a slot you know works. If all the sticks work, probably one of the slots has died.

New or old RAM? All sticks the same (make but speed mostly)?
