### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-07-17
With integrated graphics, HD movies may barely be acceptable.  But, you'll have to try it and find out how it works.

It looks like the PCI-E (PCI Express) spec on this board is old, only supports up to 1.0a, which means most PCI-E X16 graphics cards you could buy cheaply won't work, since most of them now are 2.0 or higher.
