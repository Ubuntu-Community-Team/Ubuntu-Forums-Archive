### Post by Joe of loath on 2011-05-30
The question is - How much do you want to spend?

I have loads of laptops, all the way from a 1995 HP omnibook (with a 486 cpu and 8mb of RAM), through some Asus L4L's I pulled out of a bin, to my Dell 1545.

The Asus machines are great, 6-8 years old, used every day in a school, and not a scratch on them. They'd easily be less than £100 on ebay, definitely. My Dell 1545 is a couple of years old now, still going strong, and I bought a 9 cell battery for it off ebay for £28, so now I have 5 hours' battery life too. This laptop would probably be £150-200 on ebay.

Both are 100% Linux compatible (Although I paid £10 for an Intel wifi card for the Dell, because it came with a Broadcom one).
