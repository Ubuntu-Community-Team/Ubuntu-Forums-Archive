### Post by dabl on 2009-10-12
Some of these new laptops and netbooks have power-saving firmware on the motherboard that makes them "go to sleep" if there is no keypad input for a certain length of time.  Try touching the mousepad or the space bar when it blinks out and see if it suddenly comes back to life.
