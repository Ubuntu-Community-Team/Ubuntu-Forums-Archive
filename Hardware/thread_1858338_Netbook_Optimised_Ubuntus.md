### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-10-12
The netbook edition was nothing but a small screen tailored interface on top of the regular Ubuntu distro. It wasn't fit or optimized for netbooks in any other way. The last usable netbook edition was 10.04 (10.10 UNE was experimental), and as of 11.04, the UNE was dropped, since the new Unity interface was deemed fit for small and large screens alike.

ARM is a different architecture, and is irrelevant for Intel based netbooks.
