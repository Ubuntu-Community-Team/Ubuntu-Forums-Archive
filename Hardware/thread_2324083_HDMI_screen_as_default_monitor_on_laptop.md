When the laptop starts it shows the bios screen, but after that the screen stays black and it shows 'no signal' after a few seconds. Can the HDMI of my laptop only be used for a second screen? Is it possible to use a standalone screen as default screen to my laptop? What do I have to change?

### Post by dirk-vanda on 2016-05-16
Thanks runrickus,
To have my HDMI work I had to update my NVIDIA driver and I had to upgrade my Xcfe from 4.10 to 4.12 (her is why: [http://docs.xfce.org/xfce/xfce4-settings/display](http://docs.xfce.org/xfce/xfce4-settings/display))
Problem solved :-)
