### Post by CrocoDuck on 2016-09-07
Hi there. Probably [this page]("http://wiki.linuxaudio.org/wiki/hardware_support") contains many useful interfaces for you. I would also have a look at the [ALSA Matrix]("http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Main"). I own a Focusrite 2i4. It works out of the box very well. It is overall good, although I think you can probably get a similar quality for a cheaper price. The very good Linux compatibility is a big pro though. The new generation of this interface is [reported to work]("https://linuxmusicians.com/viewtopic.php?f=6&t=15810").
