### Post by sgosnell on 2010-02-10
I've always had success connecting to HP network printers by just doing the basics.  Open System/Administration/Printing, click on the New button, and tell it to find a network printer.  Wait a few moments, and it should find your printer, then let you select the driver.  If it doesn't show the exact model, I just select whatever it recommends and let it install.  I've never had one not work, at least under Linux.  Windows has been problematic, but with Ubuntu printing just works.

However, the site you linked indicates that the model you have has no network support.  You'll probably have to connect it to a computer on your network via USB and share it using Samba.
