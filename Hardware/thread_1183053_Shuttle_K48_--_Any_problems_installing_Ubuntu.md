### Post by lbaldwin on 2009-11-07
No. None. Nada.  I am typing this from a K48 running Ubuntu 9.10 generic. 

Everything works just fine.  Adobe flash kinda sucks, but this is a Flash problem, not an Ubuntu problem.  May get better over time (i.e. new releases).  System works fine with flash under XP so not a hardware problem.

I have the Celeron 440 (the stock low power green K48) with 1GB and an 80GB HDD.  I do not have the WiFi option but rather am using a bridge through the ethernet port.

Installation was the usual Ubuntu pleasure.  Simple, fast, easy.
