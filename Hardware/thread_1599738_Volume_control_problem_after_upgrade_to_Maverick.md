### Post by horde on 2010-10-18
I upgraded from Karmic to Maverick on my Acer Aspire 8942G and am now having trouble with the dial that controls the sound volume.  With Karmic it worked fine...with Maverick it no longer controls the primary audio channel.

Kmix lists 2 channels: 1) Juniper HDMI Audio [Radeon HD 5700 Series] - the control dial now controls this channel.  2) Internal Audio Analog Stereo - this is the primary channel, but I can't seem to remap the dial control to control it.

I was thinking the solution might be as simple as removing the 1st channel.  I haven't managed to figure out how to do that, though.

Can someone point me in the right direction or offer some other suggestion?  Many thanks in advance.

---

### Post by horde on 2010-10-18
This is ridiculous, but 5 seconds after posting I found the 'Select Master Channel' option in Kmix settings.  That's fixed it and I feel real dumb now ;-)
