### Post by mediumcool on 2010-03-25
Hi there,

I just got an AverMedia AVerTV Volar Black HD A850 usb tv tuner and it worked fine with lucid beta - can't vouch for Karmic but Lucid is not far off even if it doesnt work with karmic.  You will need to install the firmware but this is done through the Hardware Driver tool - I got a notification that new drivers were available.

I got it working with Kaffeine straight away after installing the firmware and restarting.
