### Post by IntuitiveNipple on 2008-08-25
The internal Bluetooth module for the Dell Inspiron 1525 is the "Dell TrueMobile 355 Bluetooth 2.0 + EDR module" It seems to use the Broadcom BCM20x5 chip-set and connects via the internal USB bus. From what I can tell it is supported by the kernel drivers.

There are [several seemingly identical internal units with different Dell part numbers](http://accessories.us.dell.com/sna/category.aspx?k=355&_nks=true&c=us&category_id=5584&cs=19&l=en&s=dhs&x=0&y=0), most around US$20, so it is worth checking with Dell as to the exact part number the laptop you have requires.

There look to be [several available from eBay for around US$20](http://search.ebay.com/truemobile-355).
