### Post by cariboo on 2009-01-17
The destructive recovery, just reformats the partitions and installs windows as it was when you first got the laptop. The restore partition is never touched. Have a look at this [howto]("http://www.pcstats.com/articleview.cfm?articleID=1778") for an overview of partitioning and formatting hard drives. Although this howto is specifically for windows the principals are the same, it is only the tools that are different for Linux.
