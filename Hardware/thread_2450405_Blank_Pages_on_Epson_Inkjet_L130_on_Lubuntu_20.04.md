### Post by ajgreeny on 2020-09-13
The printers that use a tank system seem much more prone to this ink jet drying problem if you print very seldom, as I used to, so I moved to a printer where the jet is part of the cartridge itself.
I accept thie is more expensive but I haven't  had the problem since doing so and if anything print even less than I did in  the past, perhaps only 1 page every two weeks.
