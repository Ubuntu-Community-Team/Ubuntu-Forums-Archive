### Post by mirnander on 2010-12-04
I figured it out on my own, but for anyone else who might run into this problem in the future, here's what I did.

Installed gnome Alsa mixer using synaptic. Opened gnome alsa mixer, there was a tab there for the mic! Turns out it was muted by default.
