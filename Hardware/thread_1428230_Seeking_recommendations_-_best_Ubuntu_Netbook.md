### Post by jan schoubo on 2010-03-13
My wife and I purchased a Samsung NC10 (Pink ! ;)) before our recent trip to Key West and I threw Ubuntu 9.10 on immediately (never even booted Windows 7 ):P)

Everything worked on my first try - video, sound, keypad, almost all the FN keys (*), external DVD drive (pink too ;);)). I also got an extra high capacity battery - it comes with a 6-cell battery that last maybe 6 hours, but the non-original 9-cell, 7800 mAh has more than 9 hours - enough to cross the Atlantic! And this is without dimming display etc.
I also got a 2 Gb RAM module instead of the 1 Gb it came with.

It works perfectly, I used wireless in at least five places in Florida, and the color made it a good conversational piece too :D
I run Eclipse, MySQL server, Thunderbird, OpenOffice and a bunch of other stuff, and the only "problem" is the limited screen - Eclipse encourages you to use larger screens, so you can have 4-6 windows open at the same time. However, I learned to use Alt+Drag and everything works, no UFO's and no unwanted reboots.

I plan to buy a 128 Gb SSD once they come down a bit in price.

- Jan

(*) The top row has a few I have not tried (external monitor) and a couple that does not seem to work (F9-switch off wireless, and F3-Euro !?)
