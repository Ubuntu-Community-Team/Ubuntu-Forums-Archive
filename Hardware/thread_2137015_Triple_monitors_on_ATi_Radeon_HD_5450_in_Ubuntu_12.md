Hi,

Here's the situation.

I have got an ATi Radeon 5450 with VGA, HDMI and DVI ports, but no DisplayPort. Initially (quite naively), I thought as there were three ports I should be able to use three monitors at the same time. So, I found three monitors, two analogue and one digital (DVI), with one analogue cable, one HDMI -> DVI and one DVI -> analogue. Then, everything got connected and (bang!) all the monitors were detected and worked in Ubuntu 12.04.

(Here comes the) But, after a few moments, I noticed intermittent display freezes, which after a while became very annoying. Then I googled and saw some suggestions about fixing freezes to be installing ATi Catalyst Center. Then, done that. Then, now, only two monitors can be used at the same time rather than three!! AMD confirms three monitors set-up only works when one of the monitors supports DisplayPort and the graphic card supports that too.

So here come my questions.

1. What makes Ubuntu proprietary driver work with three monitors but ATi's not? (A thumb up to Ubuntu)
2. What exactly caused the freezes?

I hope at some point a solution can be found and we can use three monitors with no DP.

Any ideas and suggestions are deeply appreciated.
