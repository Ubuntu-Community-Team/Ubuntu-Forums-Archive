### Post by Fafler on 2010-08-09
Both companies offer a set if closed source drivers for their cards. The general census is that Nvidia has better driver support, but i'm not sure if its even true anymore. I only use Nvidia, except for my laptop, which sports a Radeon x1400, a bigger brother to the x1250 in your laptop. One reason to choose Nvidia is that their drivers offer video decoding in hardware. By the way, your current graphics chipset uses the radeon driver and it should be well supported.
