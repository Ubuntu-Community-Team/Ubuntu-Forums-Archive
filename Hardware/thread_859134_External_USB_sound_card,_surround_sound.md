### Post by sgleo87 on 2008-12-04
I think pretty much all usb sound cards work since they use the same alsa usb drivers.

Check the alsa matrix to see which sound cards are supported: [http://alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Main](http://alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Main)
