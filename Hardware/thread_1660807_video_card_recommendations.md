### Post by cascade9 on 2011-01-06
nVidia GT210/GT220. Good support, VDPAU (nvidia hardware video decoding), and not a power hungry heat producing beast. Easy to get the drivers for that card with ubuntu (system-> administration-> hardware drivers) 

BTW, a video card wont help with internet video watching, as flash for linux has no hardware video decoding (its all done by the CPU).
