### Post by henwyn on 2008-09-30
The problem I was having with this board turned out to be one of the two sticks of memory. I had tried it with just one stick in (the one in the number one position) and had the same issues so I thought it was something else. I finally started over completely, took everything out and put it back in and this time installed the other stick by itself and everything worked. The headache from smacking myself in the head has finally faded and I thought I'd let anyone interested in this board know that it works great and I like it a lot.
