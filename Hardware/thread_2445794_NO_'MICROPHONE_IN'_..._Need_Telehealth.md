### Post by CelticWarrior on 2020-06-19
That green socket is an OUT and an OUT only. If your headset is designed for phones/tablets with a 4-rings single plug then it can also work without adapters in an handful of laptops that have the same combo IN/OUT socket like phones/tablets. In any other case you'll need some Y splitter, green goes to green and red/black goes to red/black.

In a nutshell that's why, as expected (by me), the microphone isn't and couldn't possibly be detected.
