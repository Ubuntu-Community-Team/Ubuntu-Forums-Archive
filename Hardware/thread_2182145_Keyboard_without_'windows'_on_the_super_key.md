### Post by Petro Dawg on 2013-10-20
If you have a bit of cash to throw at this problem you have a few options...

You can get a whole custom designed keyboard (the coolest option):  [http://www.wasdkeyboards.com/](http://www.wasdkeyboards.com/)

Or you can get a custom "stickers" made for your keyboard (you'd have to contact the vendor and request what you want, and probably buy in bulk or pay a premium price):  [http://www.smartkeyboardsolutions.com/custom-labels.htm](http://www.smartkeyboardsolutions.com/custom-labels.htm)

Or print your own design on a blank sticker (cheesy, but cheap):  [http://www.4keyboard.com/blank-keyboard-stickers-p-169.html](http://www.4keyboard.com/blank-keyboard-stickers-p-169.html)

Personally, I would probably just use a black magic marker to cover up the windows key and paint a white "SUPER" on the key.
