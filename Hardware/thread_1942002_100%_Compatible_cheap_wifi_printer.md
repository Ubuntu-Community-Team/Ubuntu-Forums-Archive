### Post by kghunt on 2012-03-16
Just thought id let folks know I bought an epson SX235W wifi printer/scanner from asda walmart for 39 pound on a whim and it works perfect on ubuntu 10.04 11.10 and 12.04 not to mention android and ios apps available too.

Drivers are on epsons site. All working over wifi.

My only complaint is that the drivers are split into about 5 debs and have to be installed in a certain order. But once they are installed ubuntu found the printer straight away and was printing and scanning over wifi immediately.
