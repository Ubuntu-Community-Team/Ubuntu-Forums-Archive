### Post by coldraven on 2013-06-22
If the drive door is flat then any standard drive should fit. If the door is slightly too wide or tall you can trim the plastic easily by dragging a sharp blade across it.
I did this on an old Dell with good results. I'm not bothered if the colour does not match.

Edit: You might need to transfer any cradle fittings from the old drive. Easy to do with a small screwdriver.
