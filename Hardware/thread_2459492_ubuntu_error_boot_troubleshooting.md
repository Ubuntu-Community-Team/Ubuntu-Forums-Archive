### Post by TheFu on 2021-03-19
The messages are in the **dmesg** output. Take those copy/paste them into google search. Add "ubuntu" and the exact release - 20.04 or 20.10?  That's the best troubleshooting advice I have.  Many boot complaints are just informational and don't mean you should be worried.  But until you look each up, you'll never actually know.
