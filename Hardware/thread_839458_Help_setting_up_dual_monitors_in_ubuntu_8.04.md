### Post by GepettoBR on 2008-06-24
For ATI, chances are you won't be able to make it work. Only the newest ATI cards work at all with Ubuntu, using the proprietary driver and Catalyst. My Radeon IGP 340M works with full 3D support in dual-screen under Windows, but good luck trying anything of the sort in Ubuntu.
