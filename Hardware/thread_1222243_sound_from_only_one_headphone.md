### Post by dhigger on 2009-07-24
Sound problem and solution follows:

Just recently I found that sound would come out of both laptop speakers but when I put headphones in, I only got sound out of the right headphone. The hardware is fine (dual boot Sony Vaio SZ61MN, works fine in Vista).

I searched these forums and followed a number of suggestions (which worked for others in previous Ubuntu versions; I'm using 9.04), including installing QAMix, changing from ALSA to OSS etc. No joy.

What worked for me (it's bizarre) was simply taking the headphones out and sticking them into the microphone socket, then putting them straight back into the headphone socket. Hey presto, sound out of both headphones. I'm sure the headphones were in properly when only one worked - I was shoving them in and out again and again before trying the microphone socket and back. They're iPod headphones and I use them all the time and have never had any problems.

Very confusing, but I hope this helps someone anyway!
