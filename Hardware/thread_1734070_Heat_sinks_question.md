### Post by georgemc on 2011-04-19
Simply answers to your questions:

No and
No

Every brand and model has different specifications and therefore work differently.

Every processor type does require different mounting hardware, so a heat sink that fits a 462 CPU most likely will not fit a 754 CPU.

You need to at least match your CPU type with the heat sink.

Other requirements to look at are:  form factor, noise level, cost, quality etc.

[B]BTW never remove a heat sink and then run the unit.  You will just burn up your CPU.
[/B]
I suggest you goto your favorite on-line store and check the specifications on the various heat sink setups.

Hope this helps.

George
