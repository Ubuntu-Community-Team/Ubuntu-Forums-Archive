### Post by gameshints on 2008-05-05
I installed Ubuntu 8.04 about a week ago on my Thinkpad X40 without any problems.  Recently, it began to hang when I shutdown or restarted.  The screen would go blank black right after hitting the shutdown or restart button from the menu, the hard drive activity light would become solid, and I would have to hold down the power button to shut down.

I've come across [many]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=610791") [posts]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=780840") [like this]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=591229") with people having the same problems, but the only response involves typing this in the console:

```
sudo shutdown -h now
```

Which, indeed, fixed my problem... for now.  Any ideas why it occurred in the first place?  Is this a known bug?  Is there anything I can do to help solve why this is happening?
