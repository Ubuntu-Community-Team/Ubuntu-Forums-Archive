### Post by Megaptera on 2011-03-11
I'm dual-booting a Dell Inspiron laptop very comfortably, it was about the price range you're looking at. This has 2gb ram & 160gg hard drive though.
It came with a Vista restore partition plus the restore discs so I deleted the restore partition and used that for Ubuntu. I still did the Vista set-up 'cos I'd paid for Vista so why not have both.
Shop around. PC World & Currys are now joined up, but don't overlook Tesco Extra or Tesco Direct as they have some good deals from time to time.
