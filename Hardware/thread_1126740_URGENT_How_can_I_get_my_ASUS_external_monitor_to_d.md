### Post by girishsasikumar on 2009-04-16
Go to 
system -> preferences -> Display

Click on 'Identify Monitor/Display' ......

Now u should see the identification tags on the two monitors .....

u can set the external monitor as a clone of laptop screen or an extension of screen which is really cool ... u can decide where u want the extension to be ... on right/left/top/bottom etc by clicking and dragging the two monitor icons ........

if it does not work ... restart the comp and see whether your external monitor works during grub and splash screen .......
