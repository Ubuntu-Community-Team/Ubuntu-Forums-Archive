Simply I want to share with you on how to solve the no sound problem on SONY VAIO PCG-TR3 laptop just follow these steps as listed below:

1) Run Volume Control application (gnome-volume-control).
2) Click switches tap.
3) Find External Amplifier and turn it OFF.

That's it .....
