### Post by GrayMagiker on 2008-04-28
My recent printer died after 4 years of faithful service.  

As a replacement my boss went out and bought me a HP Deskjet 6940.  I plugged it in, used the old USB cable from my old printer, and added the ink cartridges that came with it.  

By the time I did that, and put in my password to unlock the screen, Ubuntu informed me that my HP Deskjet 6940 was ready to print.  I checked this by printing a simple text file I had.  It printed fine, and also did a self test which seemed to work great.  (The self test could have been cued up from trying to diagnose my dead printer).  

Long story short, it just worked.
