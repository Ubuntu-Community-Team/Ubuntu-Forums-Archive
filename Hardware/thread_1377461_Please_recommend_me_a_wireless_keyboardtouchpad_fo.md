### Post by DragonlordP on 2010-01-16
Success! I'm happy to report that not only does the ACK-340 RF works out of the box on karmic, but it also (so far) seems to me more or less like the ideal HTPC keyboard. It's small - basically, it's just like the keyboard of a 8.9" netbook (lacks a right shift btw). Not great quality, some keys are not that firm but I think it won't have any issues if not abused. It's too soon to report on battery life though.
