Hey guys. I've just upgraded from Ubuntu 10.04 to 10.10a. Everything is fine but, well I must say this is after some tinkering with the video driver, my multi-touch trackpad doesn't work properly as before.

It's supposed to work like this:
2 fingers tapping the pad = middle click
3 fingers = right click
2 fingers moving around on the pad = scroll up/down, left/right

But now scroll function won't work and middle/right click are switched.

Any idea how can I work around this, please? Thanks a lot :)

edit: This has been solved. Install gpointing-device-settings and you'll find settings there.
