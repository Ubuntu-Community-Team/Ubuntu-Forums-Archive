### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-02
There is a checkbox on the Display panel that you have to uncheck to stop mirroring.

What might work is, once you open the Display panel, go to the top and drag the current left-most display outline to the right.  That will make the (currently right-most) panel the new primary panel.

Then, reboot.

See if that then allows you to uncheck the mirroring checkbox.
