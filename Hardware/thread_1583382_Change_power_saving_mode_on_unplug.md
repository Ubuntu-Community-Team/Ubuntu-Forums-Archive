### Post by NightwishFan on 2010-09-27
Powersave is a misnomer. It usually uses more power than ondemand. More power is saved by the CPU using a sleep state than by having a constantly low frequency. You want to do tasks as fast as possible, and than race back to idle. Ondemand is the best choice.
