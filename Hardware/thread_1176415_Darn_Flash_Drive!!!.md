I reccomend the gparted live cd. [http://gparted.sourceforge.net/](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/)
What I would reccomend doing, is deleting the 922 Mb partition, making it into unallocated space. Then expand the partition with all the stuff into said unallocated space. Be warned, you will loose everything on the 922 mb partition. Back up critical data too!
