### Post by phidia on 2008-07-21
What you're describing isn't normal IMO. I have a couple of desktop installs and a laptop. I think the time the system looks for a connection is between 15-30 seconds.
That might vary some depending on the age & speed of the computer, but several minutes is excessive.

BTW unless you turn that off-in services I believe-the system will always look for DHCP at boot whether it was there or not during install.
