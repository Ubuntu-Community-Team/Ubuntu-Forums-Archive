### Post by Fafler on 2009-12-11
Errors like that are related to the USB hardware in the computer or the USB core driver in Linux, not the bluetoothdriver itself.

Try this: 

```
sudo echo -1 >/sys/module/usbcore/parameters/autosuspend
```

In a terminal and retry. Or maybe [http://http://www.mepis.org/node/5860]("http://http//www.mepis.org/node/5860") could help.
