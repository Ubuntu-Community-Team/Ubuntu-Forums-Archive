### Post by madverb on 2009-10-19
Sometime if the RAM isn't recognising at full speed you just need to change the setting in the BIOS to set it at the correct speed.
It is fine to mix RAM. 
You can also mix speeds as long as they are supported by the motherboard.
When you mix speeds it will mean all the RAM will run at the speed of the lowest one installed.
