### Post by BBUCommander on 2011-02-20
I am not sure if the original poster is still around to read this, but have you tried updating your ath9k driver?

Instructions and the driver are provided here:

[http://wireless.kernel.org/en/users/Drivers/ath9k]("http://wireless.kernel.org/en/users/Drivers/ath9k")

I am not entirely familiar with your wireless card, so you may want to run the following command to check the chipset and ensure that ath9k is the appropriate driver:

```
lspci | grep Network
```

If you are still having trouble after updating the driver, post the output of the above command and I will do my best to help.
