### Post by michael-hi on 2020-02-11
Hello Daniell59


The Epson page you have linked to appears to relate to a driver which is not appropriate for your particular scanner. The search page you need is here:

[http://download.ebz.epson.net/dsc/search/01/search/searchModule](http://download.ebz.epson.net/dsc/search/01/search/searchModule)





If you input your product name **perfection v30** and then in the Operation System drop-down box choose **Linux** from the list of options and click on the magnifying glass symbol, you will get to a page where you should choose the third option (version 2.30.4  All-in-one package) and click Download. Then on the Package Download page for "Image Scan! for Linux" you want the second of the four Download options i.e. [deb] Ubuntu etc... 64 bit. On the same page there is a link for instructions on how to install the package.


I don't have an Epson scanner but I wish you luck!
