### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-03-03
I find the Samsung TV's to have better monitor support than most.  They bought (or licensed) the NEC's MultiSync technology so their computer display engines tend to work better with different computers.  Overall, TV's make crappy monitors and monitors make crappy TV's.  So what is your Use Case?  

Many TV's will display "No Signal" when the signal is cut off.  If you are using an HDMI cable, it is complicated by the HDCP handshaking that occurs to allow you to use your TV.  So, some power needs to be on to allow the TV to wake up when it receives a signal.  Many TV's will shut off after an hour (or 10 minutes) of no signal.  Sometimes you can change this in the TV settings.
