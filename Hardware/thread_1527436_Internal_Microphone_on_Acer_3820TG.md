### Post by myles.tonnies on 2010-07-09
HI s7ev1n. 

I would try installing the package for 'PulseAudio Volume Control'. Then turn either the left or right mic all the way down, leaving one channel at about 80% (You may have to unlock or they will both slide together). 

Save your settings and then try your microphone. This worked for me before.

Cheers
