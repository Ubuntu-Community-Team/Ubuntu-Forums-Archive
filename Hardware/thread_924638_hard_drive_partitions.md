### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-09-19
AFAIK, you cannot "merge" partitions into one, you would have to copy the data off of one partition (preferably the second one), delete the partition, then expand/grow the first partition to use the newly freed space.  I recommend it deleting the second partition because it is easier to grow a partition from the end outward, than from the beginning toward the beginning of the drive.
