I have just bought a new graphics card (XFX HD 6450) on Dell U2412M & once again display flicker (graphics signal NOT monitor)

### Post by rugs on 2012-10-19
Found a solution. Just switched Unity from the full fat to the Unity 2D version & all OK now.
Had to do some extra tweaks, ie launchpad tiles scale as this option unavailable in the 2D version :)
