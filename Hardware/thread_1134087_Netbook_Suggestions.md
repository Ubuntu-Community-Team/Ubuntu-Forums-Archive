You won't find an XP netbook with more than 1GB RAM, 16GB SSD or 160GB HD due to MS licensing requirements.

Most netbooks can be upgraded to 2GB RAM and some can even be ordered that way if you specify Linux as the OS.

H/W support is pretty good on my Eee PC 901 (upgraded to 2GB RAM and came with 20GB SSD.) There's a European version (I think) that comes with 3G, the 901GO, but I'm not sure if that's usable/available in the US. (My son had the USB Verizon thing which worked better in his Acer Aspire One than his wife's Mac. The Mac didn't like hot plugging whereas the AAO running Xubuntu handled it with aplomb.)

The Eee has an Ralink WiFi chip and I have no idea if it supports what yoyu want.
