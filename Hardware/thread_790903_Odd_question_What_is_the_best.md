### Post by jamyskis on 2008-05-12
Grützi andreasl98 and welcome to Ubuntu Forums!

Your PC, although not critically old, may be a little underpowered to run Ubuntu. Heck, it's at stretching point when it comes to running XP.

Have you considered Xubuntu? Xubuntu comes with XFCE, which is a lightweight desktop environment. Most of Xubuntu is configured to avoid causing too much strain on your system. It takes some getting used to (it's not as easy to use as GNOME) but, for a point of reference, I got it working usably on a Pentium II 366MHz, 256MB RAM and 20GB hard drive.

Edit: The freezes aren't to do with your PC by the way - freezes in Hardy are a regular occurrence at the moment (although becoming ever fewer with the barrage of patches coming out at the moment)
