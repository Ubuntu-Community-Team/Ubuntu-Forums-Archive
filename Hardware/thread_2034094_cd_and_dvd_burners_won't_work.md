### Post by johnplov on 2012-07-27
I have not installed the restricted extras package. the link you gave me looks promising.
I just solved the immediate problem on my own though. I took the cd burner out of my old , retired Compaq, and put it in the newer machine just to see if the drive I put in was to blame. Not only did it work, but the existing cd player that I was told didn't work is now working. I guess I either had the jumpers set wrong or just a bad drive.  When I changed out the dvd burner that came with the machie, I set the jumper in the same position as the one I was replacing. When I took the cd burner out of the Compaq, I left the jumper alone, even though it was set differently from the two dvd burners.  Maybe both of them were set wrong, or I just got unlucky enough to get two bad drives.  I don't really need a dvd burner, so I may just leave well enough alone, although a player would be nice.
Anyway, I guess I'll mark this solved, but I'll check out the link you gave me. Thanks
