### Post by hansmex on 2009-10-19
I had to search for a bit, but I found a really easy way to install almost any Epson printer-scanner AUTOMATICALLY under Ubuntu 6 or later

First, go to 

   [http://www.avasys.jp/lx-bin2/linux_e/spc/DL1.do](http://www.avasys.jp/lx-bin2/linux_e/spc/DL1.do)

Fill out the questionnaire for scanners, it starts halfway down the page.
Also answer the questions about your system at the bottom of the page.
Click NEXT and you will arrie at a "custom" page for the download you need.

My printer was working properly, but Ubuntu didn't see my scanner. 
I selected the appropriate download. For me (Ubunto 9.04 and Epson RX585) that was 

   DEB 32bit package [libltdl7] (for Ubuntu 8.10 or later)     
   iscan_2.22.0-2.ltdl7_i386.deb

Download the file and it auto-installs. 

Under APPLICATIONS - GRAPHICS you will see a new icon IMAGE SCAN!

Click and scan!! How easy can it get??
