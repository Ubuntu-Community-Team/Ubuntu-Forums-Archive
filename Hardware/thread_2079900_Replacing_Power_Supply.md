### Post by N00b-un-2 on 2012-11-02
Yes and no... No, they do not all adhere to the exact same dimensions, but for what it's worth, if you get an ATX power supply it should work in virtually any ATX case or tower.  The slot in the back of your case where the PSU mates to is a standard size with standard mounting screw holes, but the depth of PSU may vary somewhat.  Subsititute "ATX" with whichever form factor you are using (eg; mATX, ITX, BTX, etc).  I've built dozens of computers and never once ran into an issue with a PSU not fitting into a corresponding case.
