### Post by gordintoronto on 2017-12-30
It's possible to help you if you provide more information. What version of Linux are you using? (Eg. Ubuntu 16.04 64-bit.) What is the video adapter in your computer? Have you installed an "additional driver"? What is the exact model number of the Acer monitor? How is the monitor connected to the computer? (VGA, HDMI, etc.) Simple cable or via some sort of adapter?

If it's an LCD or LED monitor, it has a native resolution, and that's what you should use. Most current monitors are higher than 1280 by 1024.
