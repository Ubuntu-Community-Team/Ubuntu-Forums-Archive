### Post by eddier on 2010-02-02
Is it 2.5" or 3.5" ??

Either should connect via a USB port and be recognized by the OS.

 3.5 inch will probably have a separate power brick or plug in power plug.

 2.5 inch will probably have 2 x USB plugs on one end to connect to the PC,to enable it to draw enough power without the need for an additional power cord. The plug on the longest part of the connecting cable goes to the drive.
