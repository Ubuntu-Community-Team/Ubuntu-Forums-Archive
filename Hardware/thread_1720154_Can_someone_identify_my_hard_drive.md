### Post by dandnsmith on 2011-04-03
With IDE HDDs for (some) laptop systems the IDE connector has more connection pins than the standard, the added pins/sockets being for power and jumpering. The connectors are 50-pin (with 2 pin positions missing, leaving a group of 4 at one end) Unitised ATA.

For such connection there is only one cord.

HTH
