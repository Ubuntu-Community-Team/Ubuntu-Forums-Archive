### Post by weatherman2 on 2014-11-02
What does "HD" mean to you?  If you mean 720p, you will have no problem at all watching videos with the Intel graphics.  My current media center (connected to a 720p TV) has an Ivy Bridge (3rd-gen) Pentium Dual Core with the integrated graphics and it does 720p flawlessly.  I suspect it would also have no trouble with 1080p.

I can't verify Ubuntu compatibility with the H81 chipset or 4th gen graphics.   If you can find in some of the H81-based motherboard reviews on say NewEgg that mention Ubuntu, you might get some idea that way.
