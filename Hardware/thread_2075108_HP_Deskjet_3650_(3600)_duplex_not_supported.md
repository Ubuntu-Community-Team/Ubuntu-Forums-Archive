### Post by alybaby on 2012-11-14
Hi,

i don't know if you already looked into that option to first enable duplexer. you can do that in the printer option under /usr/share/applications there are two printer options, at least for me, in one of them you have a list of printers and it says  ~localhost. choose your printer and double click on it. here you can tick duplexer installed.
