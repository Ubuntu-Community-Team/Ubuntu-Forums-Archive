### Post by Ubuntiac on 2008-08-10
I just ditched my Nvidia 8600GT in utter frustration at Nvidia's handling of the drivers (making KDE4 crawl) and went for a ATI HD4870.

Best move I ever made. My desktop 4.1 flies. I can open multiple tabs in Firefox again and window resizes now look decent again. To paraphrase the KDE guys: "ATI: You Won't Look Back."

Just **make sure that you use a setup guide that uses the .run file to create debs rather than installing on automatic**. This one piece of advice would have saved me about 40 hours of work and will ensure your system upgrades smoothly in future.

There's a great guide at:
[http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Hardy_Installation_Guide#Method_2:_Manual_Method_.28installing_Catalyst_8.7.29](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Hardy_Installation_Guide#Method_2:_Manual_Method_.28installing_Catalyst_8.7.29)

May you love your upgrade as much as I'm loving mine. :popcorn:
