### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2013-05-09
Nvidia works better for video playback (via VDPAU) vs. AMD (via XvBA). The problem is that Nvidia only sells really wimpy cards in low-profile factor. A GT520 or GT610 (same card, different name) is probably the best option with that.

If it was me, I prefer passive cooling for noise, so I would buy this: [http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814121641](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814121641)
I'm not sure if your system is quiet enough to take advantage of passive cooling (especially since it's a power-guzzling P4).

If you can find a low-profile GT430 for sale, that would be a really good option.
