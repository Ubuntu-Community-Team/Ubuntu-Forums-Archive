### Post by Old *ix Geek on 2011-02-06
When I first got my current laptop, an HP, the same thing happened to me ALL the time. I SWORE up and down I wasn't inadvertently touching the touchpad! But you know what? I was. :shock: :oops: Specifically, I was touching the right-most edge of the touchpad, where there's a scrollbar, and that was making the cursor jump to other parts of the screen.

When my daughter was visiting recently she kept doing the same thing--and INSISTING that she wasn't touching the touchpad. I told her she really was, and once she paid more attention it stopped happening.

So, the moral of my story is this: Pay extra special attention to what you're doing and it may end up that you're just having the same 'problem' we did!
