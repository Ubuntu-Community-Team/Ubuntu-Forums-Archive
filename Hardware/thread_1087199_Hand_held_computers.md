### Post by sgosnell on 2009-03-05
Handheld computers of that vintage can't handle a CD drive.  I assume he has some version of PocketPC.  Those almost certainly have an ARM CPU, and the OS is in ROM, which may or may not be flashable.  I would be pessimistic about the possibility, but it might be possible, given the proper flashing tools, whatever they might be.
