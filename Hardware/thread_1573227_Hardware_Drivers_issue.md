### Post by cascade9 on 2010-09-12
IMO as long as everything is working, then you dont "need" proprietary drivers at all, on any system.

About the only thing in that system could use proprietary drivers would be the video card, and in your case there are no proprietary drivers avaible for that card with 10.04 (ATI discontinued support quite a while ago**,** the only drivers avaible are the open source drivers)
