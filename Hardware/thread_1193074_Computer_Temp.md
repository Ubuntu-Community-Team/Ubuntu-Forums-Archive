### Post by markmckee601 on 2009-06-21
The components I would monitor the temperature of would be the CPU, Hard Drives and the GPU if you have a separate graphics card.

Most CPU's can operate at a maximum temperate from 50°C - 70°C (122°F - 158°F).

Hard drives 50°C - 55°C (122°F - 131°F) although some new drives can operate up to 60°C (140°F).

GPU's can operate at about the same temperature as CPU's.

For more specific information about your hardware you can download the appropriate datasheet for your CPU, GPU or hard drive.
