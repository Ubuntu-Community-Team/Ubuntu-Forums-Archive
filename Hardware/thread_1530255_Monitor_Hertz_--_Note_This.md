### Post by volobiz on 2010-07-13
I discovered something today. If you have a monitor where the edges of a line on the screen seem to "vibrate" faintly, then you likely have the monitor hertz set to high. I have an attached 19" flat panel monitor and it was set at 75 hertz. Lines were vibrating only faintly. I had to almost squint to notice, but it will wear your eyes out if you do a lot of graphics work and have to look at it all day. But when I dropped it to 60 hertz, those faint vibrations on lines went away. Evidently, my flat panel display prefers 60hertz instead of 75hertz.
