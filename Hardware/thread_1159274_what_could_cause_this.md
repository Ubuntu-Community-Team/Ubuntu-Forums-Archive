### Post by rivenathos on 2009-05-14
My first thought:  Once the case is open, check the exhaust fan.  Chances are it is dusty and needs cleaning. That can cause the fan blades to become unbalanced, and the vibration can rattle the plastic air deflector in which it is probably mounted.  A can of compressed air works wonders, but be sure to take the case outside due to the dust cloud that might occur.  Hopefully this is just a routine problem. Good luck.
