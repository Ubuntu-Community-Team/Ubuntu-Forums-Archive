### Post by djuke04 on 2009-05-12
Well to update this for whoever might take an interest later, I have everything working just fine.  

The solution turned out to be using the included dongle rather than trying to use bluetooth.  I couldn't make Ubuntu find and connect to the keyboard, so I thought I would just find out how the keyboard worked with the dongle it came with even though everyone said the touchpad didn't work right.  Well it worked perfectly fine, no drivers needed or changes to anything.  Even the little proprietary buttons on the top line work fine, setting Ubuntu to sleep if I want, or opening rhythmbox etc.

Now if I could just get my sound card working again...
