### Post by whitethunder922 on 2008-07-14
Chances are your video card can do the higher resolution, but the 15" monitor probably isn't capable. Doing a simple Google for Flatron L1510S TFT, I come up with a product that says its max resolution is 1024x768. If you're going to get the 22" either way and it might require a video card upgrade, you may as well try the 22"er with the old video card first. Just don't get rid of your old monitor until you have a working configuration.
