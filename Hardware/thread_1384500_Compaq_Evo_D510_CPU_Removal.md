### Post by atomizer on 2010-01-18
I think the cooler is glued to the processor with cooling paste.
The processor has its own clip with wich it is tied to the motherboard (and you can't get there without removing the cooler).

If you lift the green lever, it should be possible to loosen the other end.
Then you can carefully remove the cooler from the processor (take care: I have pulled my processor out once that way and had to straighten the feet to get it back in)

Good luck and be carefull!!
