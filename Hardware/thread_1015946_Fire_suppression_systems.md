### Post by hambone79 on 2008-12-19
From what I have read online, Halon cannot be used on new installations. Also, it is extremely bad for the environment as well as extremely hazardous to people, pets, and computer equipment (when it is exposed to extreme heat).

I still think that CO2 is a viable alternative to the proprietary suppressants. The server rack is going to be in a closet that isn't much bigger than the rack. If you were to release a 80 cubic foot bottle (i.e. scuba tank size) of CO2 into that closet, there is no way a fire could have enough oxygen to burn.
