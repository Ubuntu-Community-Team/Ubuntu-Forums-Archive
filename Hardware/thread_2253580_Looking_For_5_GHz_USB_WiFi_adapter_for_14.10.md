### Post by weatherman2 on 2014-11-20
Is this for a desktop or laptop?

If for a desktop, consider getting a wireless ethernet bridge.  I buy used 5GHZ or dual band routers that are compatible with TomatoUSB firmware  and configure them as wireless  ethernet bridges.  For example, Cisco/Linksys E2000, E2500, E3000 have worked well for me for this purpose.

If you use a wireless ethernet bridge, then as long as your wired ethernet port is supported in Ubuntu, you need no other driver.
