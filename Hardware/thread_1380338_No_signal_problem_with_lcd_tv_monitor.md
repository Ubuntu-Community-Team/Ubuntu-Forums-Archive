### Post by andrewc6l on 2010-01-13
You didn't say, but if your TV is a Samsung, then this is apparently not uncommon. You will need to add a Modeline to the Monitor section of your /etc/X11/xorg.conf file. Here's a list of some:

[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Modeline_Database](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Modeline_Database)

You might also find one by searching for your TV model number + "Modeline". Alternately, I've heard that HDMI doesn't need this - if your TV and video card both do it.
