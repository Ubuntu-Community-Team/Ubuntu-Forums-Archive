### Post by Bucky Ball on 2015-11-24
Welcome. Not that I can see. The main problems that crop up are the wireless and graphics card. Best to check this out *before* building a machine you are going to install Ubuntu on, but ... ;)

Something else you can do to find the possible problems for yourself is, as an example, do a search for your video card and add ubuntu. For instance, search for:

> GeForce GTX 980 Ti Ubuntu

Do the same with your wireless card. You will see that plenty of folk appear to have these working. I have no idea about the compatibility of your motherboard, but generally that is not a problem. Hopefully someone, or your good self via some digging, can confirm one way or the other. Good luck.

> Asus MAXIMUS VIII HERO ATX ubuntu

You can also add the release:

> Asus MAXIMUS VIII HERO ATX ubuntu 14

... for instance, for more fine grained ...

I'd suggest burning Ubuntu install media (USB or disk), boot from that and 'Try Ubuntu'. You can see how it runs from the install media without changing anything on your hard drive or installing.
