### Post by cvnmjs on 2014-02-03
Hello,
I unfortunately bought one of these apparently Win32only monitors. On a Microsoft Answers thread a responder advised to hold down the power button for approximately five (5) secs or till a OSD appears in the bottom right of the screen. Press the on/off button to move the selection along the four (4) icons displayed. Then you should hold the pwr button for a further 5 seconds to activate the option. 
The first is auto-adjust which might solve your problem. 

Cheers.
