### Post by rules on 2011-05-25
Loaded 11.04 the other day and the touch pad still did not want to work. I then fiddled around a bit with unity and found this applet, mentioned on a website, called Touchpad Indicator. I installed it, disabled the touch pad and then re-enabled it and shazam (yes that movie was so lame it has been forever been imprinted on my brain), it started working again :D The bonus of this applet is that it has a setting where it will automatically disable the touchpad as soon as it detects a mouse ... perfect for a lazy idiot like me :D
