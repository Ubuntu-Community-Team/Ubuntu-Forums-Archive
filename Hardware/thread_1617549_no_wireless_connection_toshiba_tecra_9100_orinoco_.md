### Post by sindahir on 2010-11-09
navigate to [B]/lib/firmware
[/B]look for **agere_ap_fw.bin **and [B]agere_sta_fw.bin
[/B]move these two files to a different directory (f.e. your homedrive)
reboot the machine. You should now be able to connect to your wireless network.:P
This works on Ubuntu 10.04 and 10.10
