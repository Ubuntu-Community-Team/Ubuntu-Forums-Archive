### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-05-24
Would you have the ability to boot a Ubuntu live USB flash drive in a new netbook you are about to buy?  Some places will let you do this.  That way you can see for yourself how Ubuntu works on the hardware.

Just explain to the store that your flash drive will not modify the hard drive at all - it will run entirely from the USB drive and when you shut it down, they can boot Windows again.
