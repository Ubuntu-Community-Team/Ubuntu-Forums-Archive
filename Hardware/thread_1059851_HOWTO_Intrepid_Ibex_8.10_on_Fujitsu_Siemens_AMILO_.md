### Post by drizzt on 2009-02-04
This is a mini installation HOWTO to setup Intrepid Ibex on AMILO PA 3553 laptop.

There's a bug opened at [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/301469]("AMILO PA 3553 does not boot with Ubuntu 8.10").

1) Install Ubuntu 8.04 Hardy Heron;
2) Download the last Catalyst driver, at the moment I used *ati-driver-installer-9-1-x86.x86_64.run*;
3) Download my attached xorg.conf;
4) Upgrade to Ubuntu 8.10 Intrepid Ibex;
5) Install the ATI drivers you downloaded;
6) Replace orginal xorg.conf with mine, keyboard settings are useful for italian users, change it for your country.

I'll keep the topic updated.
