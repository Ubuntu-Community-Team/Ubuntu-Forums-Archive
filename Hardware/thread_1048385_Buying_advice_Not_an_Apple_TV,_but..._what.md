### Post by dardack on 2009-01-23
Depends what you are looking for.  I bought an AppleTV awhile ago and hacked it to be a mythtv frontend.  HOwever, now with Nvidia releasing VDPAU to their chipsets (unfortunately not the 7300 series the AppleTV has) i've been debating building a micro HTPC, think through newegg can build for around $200.00 with nvidia 8200 built in, HDMI out, not much bigger than the ATV.

But if you want something to be more of a regular computer, appletv is not what you are looking for.  It is severly underpowered, as it is to get it working smoothly with MythTV you need to do some wierd stuff that I can't remember at this time (like force the system to use less ram).  It will not play mp4's well, avi's encoded at a decent rate seem to be ok.
