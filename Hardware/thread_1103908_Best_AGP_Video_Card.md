### Post by Haitoyou on 2009-03-23
Oh you won't need that much money to find a video card that will be CPU capped by P4 capabilities. The best AGP video card out there, and arguably the final upgrade anyone can get for an AGP motherboard due to the P4 processor, is the ATI Radeon HD 3650 AGP video card. It sells for about 75$ or less. This video card will last you the remainder of your AGP-based motherboard.

There are two typical brands for the 3650 series, Sapphire and HIS. IF YOU GET HIS (I own one) YOU WILL HAVE TO GET YOUR DRIVERS FROM HIS' SUPPORT SITE, NOT ATI'S.
