### Post by jerrrys on 2009-02-23
"USB microphones are not just microphones, they actually have their own built-in sound cards that then communicate with your computer through the USB bus. And, like any sound card, they need drivers to work. Finding one that is supported can be a bit of hit-and-miss, as it's often quite impossible to find out what the actual hardware inside all the various USB microphones and headsets is."
