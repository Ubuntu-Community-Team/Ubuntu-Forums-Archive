Solution for Huawei and other mobile broadband dongles:
( Take in mind that this solution won't make it work stable but it is working with me very nice )

[Tested on Ubuntu 10.04]
You will need windows xp or any OS that your dongle works stable in it.

1- System > Preferences > Network Connections

2- Goto Tab " Mobile Broadband"

3- Click Add and Setup the configurations according to your service provider
( And dont forget to enter the APN )

4- Open Network Connections again & Go to Mobile Broadband then to your new connection to edit it

5- Mark on " connect automatically " and on " Available to all users "

6- Restart your computer and login to the OS that the dongle works stable with it
( for me its windows xp )

7- just connect your dongle and disconnect

8- Restart and login to your Ubuntu and it shall works ;)

Note:
For me it is connecting everytime I login to my Ubuntu, just when I logout or suspend it disconnects, So I had to repeat steps 6&7 again. Then it works fine again :)

I know it may not be a perfect solution, just a simple tricky one :)
