### Post by gregzeng on 2012-06-11
DV7-6C here but with I7 CPU.  Similar problem happens here with all my Linux & Windows operating systems.  Need to do EXACTLY  as you do, at each time.

It matters not whether the cooling fan on noisy (on fast).  60+ temperature is common here, & the cooling fan always lowers the temperature.  If it can't, the notebook will automatically stop.

After each "emergency stop", choose the "repair" option on reboot.  Each (advanced) op system self-corrects as best it can if you do not.  But auto-self-correct is not always enough.
