### Post by gradinaruvasile on 2010-10-10
If they can see you, the webcam works...

The problem, it seems, is the drawing method used by the gmail chat plugin. It only works right with the nvidia or fglrx proprietary drivers (and maybe intel?) as far as i observed - my parents had an ati x1600 and they could not see their webcams image nor the other webcams - i changed the card to nvidia 7300 with the proprietary nvidia drivers and everything is ok.

You should try Chrome, it is their browser, maybe there works (but in that case above it behaved the same with all browsers).
Alternative is using Empathy/Pidgin (the sound/video quality is better with the plugin though).

PS. The other guy above said to upgrade to Ubuntu 10.10, out recently. I would advise against it. Wait 1-2 months before you do it, i can tell from experience that there will be unsolved bugs...
