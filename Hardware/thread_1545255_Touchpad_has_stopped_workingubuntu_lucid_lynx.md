### Post by Noob-untu on 2010-09-11
Just for the record, same happened to me. Lucid Lynx, it worked well after upgrade, but then, computer froze with a program, I forced a restart and the touchpad didn't work anymore.

A USB mouse works, and checking similar problems in the forums, I installed TouchFreeze and this other (how was it) GPointing Device thingy, which makes it work again, although with an unusually slow acceleration and inverting the touchpad switch funcion (so Fn key + F7 enables and disables the touchpad but on screen appears the opposite state).

Anyway, if you or anyone find a solution, I'm very curious about this.

**EDIT: I uninstalled from Synaptic all touchpad related packages (TouchFreeze, Gpointing, Xserver), installed only Xserver again, reboot and it worked.
