### Post by wolfen69 on 2011-06-28
The only thing you may have a problem with is the AMD graphics. I bought a laptop with AMD Radeon 6313 graphics and couldn't get any distro to install. (I returned the laptop) Your card is even newer and probably less supported than the one I tried. For this reason, I stick with nvidia or intel graphics. 

But the latest generation of nvidia graphics (I forget the name) is known to have problems also. I would do some more research before deciding.
