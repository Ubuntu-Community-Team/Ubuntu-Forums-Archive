### Post by Mekik on 2011-07-24
Dear friends,

issue solved. Steps taken:-
1. Activate HDTV connection.
2. Restart ubuntu.
3. Disable HDTV display (screen no 2) and just need to enable the display when needed.
(no need further system restart upon enable/disable HDTV display)
