### Post by tomreid on 2010-03-06
Hi

I didn't get a reply from anyone on this. But I figured it out myself. 

On the HP website when I put in all the different variables, it asked me to individually download a driver, which I did. 

When I put in the same variables into the HP Linux driver website today, it simply saidL

[B]Y[SIZE=4]ou have selected Ubuntu 9.10 using the HP Deskjet f4500 All-in-one Printer Series.

Ubuntu 9.10 supplies HPLIP 3.9.2 and it does support your printer.

As the version of HPLIP supplied with your operating system supports your printer, you may continue to use that version of HPLIP.

You may now optionally download the latest version of HPLIP to get access to new features and bug fixes.[/SIZE][/B]



I just enabled HPLIP in Synaptic and it works fine now.
