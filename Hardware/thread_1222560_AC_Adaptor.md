### Post by warfacegod on 2009-07-25
This is more of a weird issue than anything else. I have a Toshiba Satellite P-25 s607 and recently had to buy a replacement AC adaptor. The old one was putting current into the computer chassis and buzzing and the same buzzing would come from the power transformer on the pole in front of my house. Anyway, that actually isn't the issue here. The new one wasn't staying connected and the laptop would switch to battery. This went on for weeks when I started to notice that it would disconnect about the same time my screen saver kicked in and soon after the screen would shutdown. On a hunch, I told power manager to stop turning off the screen and... eureka!
The laptop has been on for three days and zero disconnects. I would prefer the screen to turn off but I'm more than willing to live with it as is. Hope this will help someone.
