### Post by ian-weisser on 2013-09-17
SimpleScan, included in the default install of Ubuntu, is pretty good for many use cases. It requires SANE-compatible scanners.
Since we don't know your use case, we don't know if it's appropriate for your use.

You may want to check the manufacturer website.
Or just plug one into an Ubuntu system and see if you can scan with it.
