### Post by msimon1960 on 2009-10-14
Go to System->Preferences-Mouse.  There is a Touchpad tab -- disable the touchpad.

I'm using 9.04 with an Asus Aspire 9410 laptop - Synaptics touchpad.

I hope this helps!
