### Post by sparr on 2019-01-18
How are you connecting the monitors? If you have both of them on one dock using a single port on the laptop and they are high resolution monitors then you might be exceeding the total bandwidth for the connection.

As an example, a WD15 USB3.1 dock for a Dell 7490 laptop can support two monitors at 1920x1200 resolution but if you go any higher than that then it can only support one.
