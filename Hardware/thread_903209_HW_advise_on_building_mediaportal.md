### Post by Bucky Ball on 2008-08-28
Or you could invest in a silent cpu cooler without a fan. They aren't that expensive anymore and use heat pipes to drag the heat away. No fan required! :)

This is what I use in my UbuntuStudio box:

[http://www.tigerdirect.ca/applications/SearchTools/item-details.asp?EdpNo=2844210&sku=T925-2034](http://www.tigerdirect.ca/applications/SearchTools/item-details.asp?EdpNo=2844210&sku=T925-2034)

It has a fan but is rated at 21db max - I can hardly hear the thing sitting right next to it. Here's some more info.

[http://www.endpcnoise.com/cgi-bin/e/silentcpu.html](http://www.endpcnoise.com/cgi-bin/e/silentcpu.html)

Your only noise then will be coming from the power supply and that is covered too. Coolermaster make some beauties and there are many others rated very low db (there is even a coupla fanless ones on the market but, for now, restrictively expensive!). I am a muso and my box has to be quiet for recording, etc (plus extraneous noise drives me nutty(er)).

I am a bit of a greenie, so I go for the 85+ green power power supply. Again, within price range and will save on electricity... (which also saves you money) :)

Good luck ...

ps: When you say attic, do you mean a room that is used often and clean? If not, you need to think about the dust factor as good air circulation is what it is all about. Box gets hot, fan speeds up, noise level increases ...

pps: and yes, ASRock ROCK! Although not sure about for your purposes with your new media box. Hopefully someone with a similar setup to what you're thinking is out there and can help.
