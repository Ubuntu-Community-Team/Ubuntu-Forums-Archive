### Post by Bucky Ball on 2014-07-07
The only two real things to worry about are graphics and internet. You don't mention wireless/wired card or what is on the board re. ethernet.

As for graphics, as above. Do some googling and see what you dig up. Name of card + 'Ubuntu'.

Hard drives, RAM, usually motherboard, case, PSU and everything else generally work fine. Research the video, any network devices you're intending to use, and possibly the sound card as well.

As for whether everything will work together: does the CPU fit the motherboard? Does the box fit everything you want to put in it comfortably? Is the PSU 85+ energy rating? Has the board got the slots and plugs for everything you want to connect to it? If yes, then probably.
