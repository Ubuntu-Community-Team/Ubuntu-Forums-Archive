### Post by skymera on 2008-06-10
Well if you want a card just for desktop effects try a 7600, 7800 or 7900 Nvidia.

NOTE: The 8 and 9 series Nvidia cards perform pretty low on Linux due to drivers.

I have an Nvidia 8800GT, fairly cheap now.
It runs effects good (could be better!) i can't complain.
And it runs games in Windows awesome.

I did have a 7900GS, desktop effects, PERFECT!
Games, was good but 8800GT is a MAJOR upgrade in gaming peformance.
I got my 8800 for £137, some are £110 now.

Hope i helped a little.

NOTE2: Avoid ATi i've seen nothing but trouble with them on Linux.
