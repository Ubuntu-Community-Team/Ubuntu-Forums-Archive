### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-12-24
Graphic card - Intel generally work very well as do many nvidia.  Some AMD/ATI seem to be OK but there are probably more queries about them than others.
Wireless, if you need it rather than cable network.  Again Intel is good, but as I don't really use wireless, I'm the wrong person to ask.
CPU is not that important, as Intel and AMD will both work fine and make only a small difference.

### Post by pme 72 on 2013-12-25
When the time comes consider having the machine rebuilt. This fall after almost 8 years my desktop would no longer boot. I took the pc to a local shop that advertized they were linux friendly. The motherboard needed replacing which necessitated upgrading other components too. The machine was ready for pick up that afternoon. Saved me having to shop for a new machine and there is one less chassis in the graveyard.
