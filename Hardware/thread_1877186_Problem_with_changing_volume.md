### Post by little_penguin on 2011-11-07
Try this: click the gear icon on the top right and open "System Settings..." -> Sound. On the first tab, Sound Effects, make sure "Alert volume" is down to zero (or you can mute it if you like). Leave the "Output volume" as it is, that is your playback volume - it does not control notification sounds (alerts).

### Post by little_penguin on 2011-11-08
Try this: while a video is actually playing in the respective player, click the Loudspeaker icon, go to Sound Settings then select the Applications tab. You can correct the sound for that application there (it may have been muted or turned down by mistake).
