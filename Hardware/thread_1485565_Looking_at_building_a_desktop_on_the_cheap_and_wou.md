I assume your in the US. $1000 should build a really nice system.

Video card- nVidia GT220. You could probably save a few $$$ by getting an older 8400GS, but the GT220 is much better at 1080p. 

CPU- something by AMD. Probably a Athlon II X4, or a Pheon II X4. Intel makes goodCPUs, but AMD is faster for your money. 

Motherboard- Gigabyte, AM3 AMD/ATI 770 chipset. You could get a 790FX/890FX, they are very good, and also allow you to 'unlock' the locked cores on dual/triple core AMD CPUs, but they are expensive. 

Monitor- Umm...I know, its a cop-out, but  always reccomend that people actually look at the monitor before they buy it. What one person finds pleasing to the eye can be awful for other people. Id probably look at the samsung, asus, and maybe viewsonic montiors, and avoid AOC, acer and LG. That is purely my opnion though.
