### Post by thinking2loud on 2010-01-02
This is the current bottom line.  If you connect storage, you get anonymous FTP and Samba.  If you connect other USB devices, you get a protocol called SharePort which is proprietary to a company called Silex.  There is not a current Linux support for SharePort.  Short answer:  if you are serious about Ubuntu and need anything other than storage, buy something else.
