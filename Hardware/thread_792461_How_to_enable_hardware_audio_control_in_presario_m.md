### Post by MaindotC on 2008-05-12
Hi, real75.  Not sure how familiar you are with this but I'll ask some kiddy questions.  Have you tried going to System -> Preferences -> Sound and selecting different devices or "Auto" for your options?

You can also right-click the sound icon on your menu bar and select "Open Volume Control".  From there you select "Edit -> Preferences" and you can select different audio devices to enable and disable.
