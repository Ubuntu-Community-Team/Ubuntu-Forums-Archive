### Post by Zenze on 2008-06-19
I had the same problem that you are having, sound at the login but no sound afterwards. To fix it I just went to System > Preferences > Sound and tried each device until I got to one that played sound. Then I set all the devices to that device. After that everything worked fine.

However, if that doesnt work for you I would try this page: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=205449](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=205449)
