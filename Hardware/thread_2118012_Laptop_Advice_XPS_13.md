### Post by SeanBlader on 2013-02-20
Sorry to say I have the prior version XPS13 Developer Edition and it's brilliant. It's like you'd expect a top-tier stunning piece of hardware to work, it's all flawless and outstanding with the sputnik driver updates from Dell. The sound is *SO* surprisingly good from an Ubuntu system, and the multitouch is excellent. I can't even go back to using the retina MBP they gave me at the office because the XPS touchpad is better. And any Linux window manager is better than MacOS. Overall, if you use Linux, the best notebook available for it right now is the XPS, hands down.
