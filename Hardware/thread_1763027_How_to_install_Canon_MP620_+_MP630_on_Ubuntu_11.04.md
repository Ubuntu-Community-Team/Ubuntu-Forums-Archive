### Post by Quadunit404 on 2011-05-19
32-bit: [http://bkintegration.com/2011/05/canon_mp620-630_printer_for_ubuntu_1104/](http://bkintegration.com/2011/05/canon_mp620-630_printer_for_ubuntu_1104/)
64-bit: [http://bkintegration.com/2011/05/canon-mp620-or-mp630-printer-for-ubuntu-11-04-x64/](http://bkintegration.com/2011/05/canon-mp620-or-mp630-printer-for-ubuntu-11-04-x64/)

Select the appropriate URL for your architecture. Remember, i386 = 32-bit and AMD64 = 64-bit. Also, remember that this only works on Ubuntu 11.04. If you use anything below that, you will need to search for directions. Sorry :V

What you need:

- A Canon MP620 or MP630 connected to your local network
- Knowledge of above printer's IP address
- The ability to follow directions
- The development MP620/630 driver (bottom of linked-to pages)

Yes, I have tried the above directions and they worked. You will not screw up your system if you follow the appropriate directions for your architecture. I promise :P
