### Post by Bucky Ball on 2011-09-07
Welcome. Plug in an ethernet cable and get online that way. Get updates. Check System>Administration>Additional Drivers.

The catch 22 is that to get wireless working you sometimes need to have an ethernet connection to load the drivers and firmware for your wireless card as they are not included in Ubuntu 'natively' (for reasons which I'm sure you can research).
