### Post by David D. on 2010-12-14
Do you know what wireless card is in your laptop?  This information will help someone know what assistance to provide.

Regarding the sound input, I assume you mean the built-in microphone.  On my laptop the sound level of input for the microphone was set to "0" by default.  I had to increase the input level for the microphone to work.  Try going to System>Preferences>Sound, select the Input tab, make sure microphone is selected as the connector, and increase the input volume.
