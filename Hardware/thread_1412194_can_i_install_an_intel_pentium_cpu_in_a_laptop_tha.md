### Post by mr clark25 on 2010-02-21
1.make sure you have some thermal compound before lifting the heatsinks on the cpu's.

2.it sounds like your problem of them turning off is that the laptop is overheating.

3.after you buy some thermal compound from best buy (or some other place) i would swap the cpu's. if the laptop turns on and boots, then it works. if you turn it on and nothing happens, the cpu's are incompatible. just switch them back and they should still work.


NOTE:thermal paste it very important. without it, you will have severe overheating issues. every time you remove the heatsink from the cpu, you need to get rid of the old paste and apply new. there are many good guides as to how to do this.
