### Post by gerowen on 2011-02-19
I saw a topic mentioning NVidia and figured I would post my experience so you guys were aware of the issue.  I did a search and couldn't find anything too similar.  My wife's old laptop, which we don't own any more, had an NVidia Geforce 7150m video card.  Upon initial installation of Ubuntu 10.10, the video was "striped" I guess you could say.  It also seemed to be shifted right just a little bit so if I moved my mouse all the way to the right edge, it would appear, distorted, on the left side.  I was able to see good enough to get the proprietary drivers installed and then it worked great, but I just thought I would let you guys know that with a default Ubuntu 10.10 64 bit installation, this is an existing issue.
