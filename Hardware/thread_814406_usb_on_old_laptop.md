### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-05-31
There are sometimes adapters that will covernt one interface to another, but the chances of a USB device working on a parallel or serial port are pretty slim unless you have specific software for that device.  The best option is to buy a PCMCIA USB card, they usually come with 3 USB ports or so.  You just have to make sure it will still fit with the network card taking up the other slot.
