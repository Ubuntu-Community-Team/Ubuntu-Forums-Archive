### Post by damis648 on 2008-08-20
You must jailbreak it to 1.1.4 at this point. There is no option at this point for syncing without a jailbreak. I have tried to sync with my jailbroken 2.0 iPhone, but the new 2.0 firmware performs a checksum on the iTunes synced library so the synced music doesn't show up... I had to go back to 1.1.4 until they come up with a solution for this. If you decide to jailbreak back down to 1.1.4, take a look at this:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPhone](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PortableDevices/iPhone)
