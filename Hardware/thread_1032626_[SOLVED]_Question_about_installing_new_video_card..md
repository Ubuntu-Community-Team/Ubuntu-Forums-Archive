### Post by Smeags on 2009-01-06
I just installed Ubuntu 8.10 a little while ago and installed the nvidia 177.xx driver for my video card.  I recently bought a new video card, however, and will be installing it soon.

My question is, with Ubuntu do I need to uninstall anything (the old video card or drivers) before installing the new card, as needs to be done in Windows?

---

### Post by markbuntu on 2009-01-06
It would be a good idea to completely remove the old driver before installing the new card because configuration for the old card may remain and cause confusion.
If it is a nvidia card you can just reinstall the same driver afterwards.
