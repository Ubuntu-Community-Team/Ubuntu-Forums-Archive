### Post by Penguin Guy on 2010-04-20
I was looking for a fast SSD with about 8GB capacity, but I'm going for a 30GB one instead ([OCZ Vertex Turbo 30GB]("http://www.google.com/products?q=OCZ+Vertex+Turbo+30GB")).

Consideration:
[LIST]
[*]PCI-e and 2.5" SATA are the same considering speed, but thinking about physical space, it's probably best to go for 2.5"
[*]It's hard to find good-value-for-money drives below 30GB
[*]A 60GB drive does not cost twice as much as a 30GB drive
[/LIST]

EDIT: I've decided to go with 2x500GB Western Digital HDDs in RAID0 for now.
