### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2023-12-05

You can still use the original GA kernel in Desktop if you want: [https://ubuntuhandbook.org/index.php/2023/11/install-ga-kernel-5-15-ubuntu-22-04/](https://ubuntuhandbook.org/index.php/2023/11/install-ga-kernel-5-15-ubuntu-22-04/)
