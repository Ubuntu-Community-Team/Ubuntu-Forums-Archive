### Post by Ranko Kohime on 2013-07-06
It all comes down to resolution, screen size makes no difference.  There are for example, screens as small as 10.1" and as large as 15.6 that display 1366x768.  Sure the elements become smaller on the smaller screen, but you can still fit the same amount of work on the screen.

HP lists at least 3 different display options for that 8230, (and Google alludes to there being both a 4x3 and 16x10 version of that laptop) so without knowing which screen you have, it's difficult to say what'll be cramped.
