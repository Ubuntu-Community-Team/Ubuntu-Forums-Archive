### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2015-05-25
Hi and welcome.

Unfortunately I have bad news for you: It isn't compatible.
Please check [http://store.steampowered.com/app/8870/](http://store.steampowered.com/app/8870/) - scroll down and expand the system requirements (for SteamOS+Linux) and you'll find
> *MESA drivers* and *Intel Graphics* are not currently supported.

Your notebook has the minimum system requirements for the same game in Windows.
Yes, it's really sad, but I'm sure you'll find lots of native games at Steam that will run beautifully in your computer, just not this one.
