Fingerprint reader didn't work, and there seems to be a general disinterest in getting it running in Linux, and rightfully so: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=6044690&postcount=9](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=6044690&postcount=9) .
