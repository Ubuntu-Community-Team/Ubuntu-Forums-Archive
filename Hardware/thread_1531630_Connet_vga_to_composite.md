### Post by ieee488 on 2010-07-15
It's not Ubuntu's fault.
You would still have the same problem even with Windows.

**You need to BUY a converter box to convert VGA to composite video. These boxes cost about $90. Silly cables aren't going to do the job unless your laptop uses unused pins of the VGA connector to output the composite video signals. Laptops that have this are rare. You need to verify the documentation for your laptop.**
