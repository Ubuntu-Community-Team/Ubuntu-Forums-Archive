### Post by m-dw on 2014-04-27
Technically the upper limit for a 64bit OS is humongous - 16.8 million terabytes has been quoted.  With 16 Gig, you could probably run for years at a time without needing swap.  Ubuntu will see it all (as will any other 64bit OS I know of.
