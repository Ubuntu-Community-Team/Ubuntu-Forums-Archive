### Post by dragonfly41 on 2017-04-26
Welcome to the "freeze club". You will find variants of freezing symptoms in the forum.

Sometimes .. just to avoid power down button .. you can use Ctrl+Alt+F1 .. followed by Ctrl+Alt+F7 .. to resume operations.
But that does not always work and only avoids power down.
You can use Alt+Prnt Scr .. followed by R .. E .. I .. S .. U .. B instead of hard power down.

System Monitor will show if there is heavy usage of memory.  Video playing is a common culprit since scripts hang.
You can disable browser extensions to see if that improves matters.
Also try disabling hardware acceleration.
But there is no single solution that I have found.
