### Post by rue1341 on 2009-11-07
I have looked at the Sane supported scanners list and your DX8450 is not listed (it goes up to the DX7400) so that may be the problem.
However you could try installing libsane-extras & sane-utils (using Synaptic Package Manager in the System-Administration menu)it has worked for me in ubuntu versions before 9.10. 
I am having problems gettiing my RX520 to work on 9.10 (it worked fine on 9.04) so that may also be a problem. 
There are quite a few threads showing you how to change rules and conf files
