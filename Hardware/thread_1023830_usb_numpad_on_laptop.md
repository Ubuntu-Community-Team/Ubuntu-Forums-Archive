### Post by waldherrvonbirnbaum on 2012-10-02
i got it!
i got an thinkpad usb keyboard and an lenovo numpad seperatly connected via usb.
in ubuntu 12.4 you go to settings -> keyboard layout-> options (right down side)-> find "Miscellaneous compatibility options"-> and here you check "Numerich keypad keys always enter digits (as in Mac OS)".

i hope your numpad is still there
