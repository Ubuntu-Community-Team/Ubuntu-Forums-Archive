### Post by bdoe on 2008-12-21
It sounds like you may have an old, incompatible USB host in your computer. The cheapest, easiest way to address that problem is to pick up a USB2.0 PCI card and use that. Barring that, you may have to revert to using the PS2 ports using inexpensive USB-to-PS2 adapters (most USB keyboards and mice include one).
