### Post by Bucky Ball on 2009-11-09
Plug in an ethernet cable and get your updates. See if you are offered the restricted drivers.

Whilst online, go to System->Administration->Synaptic Package Manager, search for and install:

ubuntu-restricted-extras

System->Administration->Hardware drivers

Anything disabled in there?

Try and give more detail. What release are you running?
