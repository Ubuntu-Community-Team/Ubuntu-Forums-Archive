### Post by blwizard on 2009-09-23
Hello fellows, I just installed Ubuntu Remix on my Samsung N110 netbook and there comes a problem, for which I searched the forum but didn't find an answer.

Basically it's the different maximum brightness in battery and AC modes. When the netbook is on battery, the maximum brightness is way lower than that in power mode, about as half bright, though the brightness bar has reached maximum. I've looked at all the power options I can find on the Gonme desktop but just can't figure out how to make it brighter on battery mode. I think I might have to change certain configuration files or something.

Any help or directions would be greatly appreciated1 Thanks!

---

### Post by blwizard on 2009-09-23
After searching the web for a bit, I finally fixed the problem. Just have to turn off the auto brightness in BIOS settings, and everything goes as expected then. 

The credit actually goes to lain's post on this page:[http://nc10ubuntu.wordpress.com/2009/04/27/ubuntu-netbook-remix-unr-904-is-an-improvement/](http://nc10ubuntu.wordpress.com/2009/04/27/ubuntu-netbook-remix-unr-904-is-an-improvement/)

Hope it helps.
