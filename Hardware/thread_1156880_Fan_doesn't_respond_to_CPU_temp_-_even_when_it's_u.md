### Post by br3nd6n on 2009-05-12
Good news. Although my fan speed still doesn't seem to change with higher CPU temps, my CPU is now running at a much healthier temperature of roughly 30. I went to clean some superficial dust off the fan and realised that the whole cooler unit was loose. The plastic holder for the metal conductor unit (under the fan) had snapped. So I've glued and strung it back together and it seems to have done the trick until I can get that plastic bit replaced.
