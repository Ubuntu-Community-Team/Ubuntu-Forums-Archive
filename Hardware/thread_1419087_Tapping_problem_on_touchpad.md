### Post by six30two on 2010-03-01
I looked around and think I might have a solution.

Assuming our menus are the same:

System > Preferences > Mouse

Under the General Tab (which should be open by default) at the bottom you should see "Double Click" timeout. You might try changing that so the timeout isn't as long? Worth a shot.

Also, under the Touchpad Tab (the last one), make sure the box is checked that says "Enable mouse clicks with the touchpad." Since it works one in awhile, I'm assuming this is already checked.

No idea if that'll help, but I figured it's worth a shot.
