### Post by yelvington on 2010-04-19
You can just plug it in and use it, but you will have all the limitations of a Windows filesystem and none of the advantages of a Linux filesystem. With 1TB of data you'll be much better off (and safer) partioning the drive and creating Linux partitions for Linux data. That way a Windows disk screwup (which is sadly NOT unusual) or a Windows virus won't be able to trash your Linux data.
