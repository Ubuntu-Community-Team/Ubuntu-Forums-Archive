### Post by rCXer on 2009-08-26
Most programs in DOS use soundblaster to produce sound.  Many modern computers ("modern" means 1995 or later :p ) do not come with soundblaster hardware.  Therefore the sound doesn't work.  You should ask your questions over at the [FreeDOS mailing list](http://www.nabble.com/FreeDOS-f902.html) if you haven't already.  You could also use a DOS emulator such as [DOSBox](http://dosbox.com/) with Ubuntu.
