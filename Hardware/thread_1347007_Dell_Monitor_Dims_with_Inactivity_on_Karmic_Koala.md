### Post by Sloppyunderfoot on 2009-12-05
When watching an online video my monitor will dim to black after a few minutes of inactivity from the keyboard.  Striking a key from the keyboard will return the monitor picture to normal.  I am running Ubuntu 9.10 and have not been able to find a setting to change this.  I have changed the power management setting to 1 hour however, this has made no difference.  Any help would be appreciated. Thank you.

### Post by Snosrap68 on 2009-12-05
Systems>Preferences>PowerManagement.....uncheck dim idle option on both AC and Battery power schemes.

### Post by iris-n on 2009-12-06
This is not about power management. It is a bug with gnome-screensaver.

I'd suggest that you just disable the screensaver, System->Preferences->Screensaver, and untick the relevant box. And please, let the Power Manager with some sane settings (i.e., not everything turned off).
