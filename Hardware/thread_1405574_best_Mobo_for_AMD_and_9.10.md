### Post by bruno9779 on 2010-02-12
My setup works great with 9.10.

mobo GIGABYTE GA-MA770T-UD3P

There are 2 very minor glitches on the mobo, but they are ok for me:

1. Only one PCMCIA socket. I don't need two, as I am fine with one graphic card.
2. Hibernate works great. Suspend doesn't.

PS: it is a pretty good mobo and comes cheap because of the one PCMCIA only

PPS: and I can overclock my black edition processor
