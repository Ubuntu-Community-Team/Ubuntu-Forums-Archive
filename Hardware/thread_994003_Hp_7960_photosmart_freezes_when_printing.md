### Post by pdean44 on 2008-11-27
I resolved this problem by totally removig any references to Hp printers from my system. Removed the printer and cold booted. Went to the Hplip site and downloaded the latest driver again installed it and plugged in the printer when asked. That solved the issue. I then looked up sharing your printer in ubuntu and was able to quickly set up my wife's vista lap top so it could print through my linux box. I am a very happy Ubuntu user right now. Not bad for a novice. :)
