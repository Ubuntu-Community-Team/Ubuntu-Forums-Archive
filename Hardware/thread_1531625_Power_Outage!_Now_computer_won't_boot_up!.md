### Post by dino99 on 2010-07-15
you might protect your computer with an ups or secured cord if you often have electrical issues

try to check the bios settings, or remove the motherboard battery a while , then set again the bios settings.

you can check the hardware one by one to know which one is damaged ( in case of): unplug either cd/dvd reader, graphic card, ...)

and call/complain then send the bill to your supplier ( let it use his insurance)
