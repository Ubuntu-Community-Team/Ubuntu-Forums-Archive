### Post by 2F4U on 2012-10-21
If it happens on the BIOS level, then it is a hardware problem, which also explains why it happens all operating systems you have tested. If you are lucky, the connector of the keyboard to the mainboard is just loose (assuming that the built-in laptop keyboard is affected). A temporary solution may be connecting an external keyboard.
Do you still have guarantee on the machine? Then it may be worth thinking about sending it in for repair. Else it depends on what model and make it is if you can get a cheap replacement.
