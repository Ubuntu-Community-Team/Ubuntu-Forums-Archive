### Post by ectospasm on 2009-06-18
The problem I'm having is that when the idle brightness kicks in, the screen gets really dim (which is expected and desired).  However, when I go back to the non-idle state the brightness stays at its idle, dark setting.  I have to manually increase the brightness (via Fn and arrow keys).  This is slightly annoying, because I intend to use this machine in a classroom note-taking environment, and there may be significant pauses between notes.

For the time being I've disabled both "Reduce backlight brightness" and "Dim dispaly when idle" to workaround the problem.  This seems to have negatively impacted battery performance.  This netbook is reported to have a 9.5hr battery life with constant, normal usage, and thus far I've nearly depleated the battery after close to 7hrs.  I know, I know, 7 should be good enough for anybody, but I want to see if I can stretch out the usage on a single charge.

All in all I highly recommend the N110 with UNR, with only a few minor hiccups.
