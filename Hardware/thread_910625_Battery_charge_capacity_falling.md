### Post by Whiffle on 2008-09-04
How deeply do you discharge it before you recharge it?  If you get it pretty much all the way dead before recharging it, you'll make the battery hold less of a charge more quickly than if you keep it topped off.  Its bad for a LiIon battery to fully discharge it.
