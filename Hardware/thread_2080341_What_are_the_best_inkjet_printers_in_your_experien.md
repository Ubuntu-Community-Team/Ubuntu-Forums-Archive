### Post by coldraven on 2012-11-04
Before you buy anything, Google for "<the_printer_model> CISS"
CISS stands for continuous ink supply system. I have six refillable cartridges in my Epson so my ink costs are negligible. 
Don't buy a Lexmark, the build quality is not very good.
