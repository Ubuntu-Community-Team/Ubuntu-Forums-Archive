### Post by requeth on 2010-06-17
Have you tried the hardware function key? On my Gateway it's Function F4 and has two boxes with a line between.

Otherwise go to System: Preferences: Monitor and where it says Monitor Laptop at the upper right side choose On. This will switch the primary display to your laptop screen.
