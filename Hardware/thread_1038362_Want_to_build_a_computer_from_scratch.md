### Post by electrogeist on 2009-01-12
I always liked building my own too, although I am more of an Intel kind of guy.

#1 advice - do not skimp on the power supply!  Get a good quality name-brand unit.  Just earmark $100 in you budget now.

Blu-ray - Still quite expensive and as previously noted, linux support is not so good yet.  Unless you have an immediate need for it why not just get a $25 DVD burner.  In a year blu-ray will prob be 1/4 of the current price and hopefully with better support too.

HD - Everyone has their favorites, but I would stay away from the 1.5TB Seagates right now as they seem to have some reliability problems.
