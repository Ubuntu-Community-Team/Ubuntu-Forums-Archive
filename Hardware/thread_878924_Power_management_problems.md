### Post by cwhill on 2008-08-04
You can use pretty much everything there is and unfortunately you'll never get anywhere near the efficient power usage of Vista because of design. Even using Powertop and trimming out any non-essentials your still only likely to get 75% of the battery life you can attain with Vista. This unfortunately is one of things still needing much work with Linux. One of the few reasons I still keep a dual boot. I prefer to work in Ubuntu but when I'm on battery and need to preserve power I switch to Vista. It nearly doubles my battery life.  :(
