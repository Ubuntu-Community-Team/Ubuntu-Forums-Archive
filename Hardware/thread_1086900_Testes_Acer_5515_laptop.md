### Post by stchman on 2009-03-04
To all my fellow Ubuntu people.  I tested an Acer AS5515 model and all worked very well.

The wireless, sound, correct resolution, etc. all worked.  

Since I was only using the LiveCD I was not able to load the ATI drivers.  The video card is a Radeon Xpress 1200.  I am sure that Envy will install the proper drivers.

I was amazed at the wireless.  It appears that the Acer InviLink wireless is a Broadcom 43xx.  This worked OOB with Hardy 8.04.2.  The wireless connected to my WPA2 access point perfectly.

This laptop is under $400 at many stores.  I see it as a good Ubuntu candidate.
