### Post by ashughes88 on 2010-05-01
I've been trying to figure out the same problem for the past 8 months. It used to work on mine, and then one day it stopped. I just found on another thread that people said when you have an SD card in there's a driver problem and it prevents the laptop from entering suspend. Sure enough...I put an SD card in my tablet about 8 months ago and when I took it out I could suspend again!!

Hope that solves your problem as well. Here's the link to the other thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1332790](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1332790)
