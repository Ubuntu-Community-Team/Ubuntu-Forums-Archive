### Post by ooVoh9em on 2010-01-09
Update:

1) I found that if I use Applications -> Sound & Video -> Pulse Audio Control that I could control the volume for the headset on an application-by-application basis. Meaning; the master volume controls still do not affect the volume of the headset but once I get an application transmitting sound I can force it to use the headset and adjust the volume settings for that specific application and it works. This is not very elegant, but does get the job done in a crude manner.

2) I still cannot get the microphone to work.
