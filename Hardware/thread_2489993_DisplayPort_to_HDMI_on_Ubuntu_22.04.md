### Post by agentl074 on 2023-08-18
Yeah, it looks as though there's really no benefit to using DP to HDMI since the article that got me thinking about HDMI RGB limitations was pretty old -- and, now that I think about it, Ubuntu 20 and above should be fine with HDMI to HDMI. I never had limited RGB issues on Mint 20.3 with a Samsung HDMI monitor... all of the colors were spot on and not washed out or anything. 

That said, there may be a benefit of using DP for higher resolution screens, or when using multiple displays.
