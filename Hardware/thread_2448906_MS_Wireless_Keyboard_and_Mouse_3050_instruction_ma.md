I know nothing about the +/- keys in the keyboard. My guess is Volume keys??
The two additional buttons in the mouse is a different story, they're standard and found and lots of mice. They're used to navigate  back and forth in a web browser, maybe applicable to other software, don't know, never tried.

Also you should be aware the customizable shortcut keys require the Windows only "Mouse and Keyboard Center" app.
In summary, your hardware is designed to work in Windows 7 or newer. Anywhere else it'll have limited functionality or not work at all.
