I cannot find any reference to this so I'll post it.  If you have a Linkskey KVM switch and it plays havoc with your mouse, most likely you are able to reset the mouse by tapping [scrllock][scrllock][end].  

I'm really loving **Ubuntu** after using it for about a week, but going without a mouse wheel is no fun, and trying to find an actual solution - even the name of a product that definitely works, is difficult.  Finding the solution is free, is very nice.  

From the tiny users' manual pdf.  It may work with other kvm switches.  It simply resets the mouse.

Incidentally, thanks to all the great developers here!
