### Post by rewyllys on 2012-07-28
I've had good luck with both Brother MFC machines and Canon PIXMA printers.  Both manufacturers support Linux.

For Brother support of Linux, you can go to:

[http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/index.html]("http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/index.html")

I've been especially pleased with the Brother script for installing drivers in Linux:
[welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/faq_prn.html#f00104]("welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/faq_prn.html#f00104")

For Canon support of Linux, you need to go to a non-USA Canon Website, such as their European site:
[http://www.canon-europe.com/support/software/linux/]("http://www.canon-europe.com/support/software/linux/")

Good luck!
