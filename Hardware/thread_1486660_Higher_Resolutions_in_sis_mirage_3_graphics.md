### Post by wmatthews on 2010-05-19
First thing you have to do is make sure that you have the right graphic drivers in the computer. 

Second is make sure that your xorg.conf is configured correctly.  I had to put in my HorizSync and VertRefresh. I got the information off of the internet restarted GDM.

Best thing to do is post your notebook model on here and see if you get any help with configuring xorg.conf.
