### Post by WinEunuchs2Unix on 2014-11-04
If it keeps cutting on and off every 2 minutes then see this thread at comment #24: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/wpa/+bug/1323089](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/wpa/+bug/1323089)

The rest of the thread has a heated debate on whether the bug fix overlaps another bug on a different thread.  But #24 fixs a common annoyance to everyone and a bug to others.

Additionally see the script "wireless info" written by wildman which will collect technical information on your wi-fi that others can anayalyze.

When it comes to wifi and ubuntu remember that "google is your friend" and you can find many of threads to read.
