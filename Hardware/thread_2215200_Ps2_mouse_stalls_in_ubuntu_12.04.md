### Post by Double.J on 2014-04-05
Hi there!

this is very strange. I've personally not had this issue come up before! Especially as the mouse stays powered the whole time. 
Do you have a dual boot/live image that you can use to verify if it is a hardware or software issue? (Same issue in two different OS- likely hardware)

if you were previously using a ball mouse, is your mouse mat too smooth a surface for the optical scanner (different optical mice have different resolutions)

In the meantime having to hard power off over and over increases the risk of system errors and data loss use ctrl+alt+F1 to get a terminal and then
```
 sudo shutdown -r 0
```
to restart.
