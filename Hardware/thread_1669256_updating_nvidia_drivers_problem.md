### Post by PRC09 on 2011-01-18
With the x-swat enabled it will automatically update whenever there is a new driver.If you install from the nvidia site you will also have to redo the drivers after every new kernel which is fairly often.Other than that I think you will have to wait until they hit the regular repos.I cant remember how long I have used this way( 6 or 8 months) and I have yet to have a problem on anything I have installed on (30+ systems).......
