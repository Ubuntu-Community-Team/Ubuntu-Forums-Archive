### Post by gordintoronto on 2017-05-29
You went from Ubuntu to KDE and the power usage went up? I'm not surprised. An idling 5400 rpm hard drive doesn't use much power.

For example, a Western Digital Scorpio Blue WD1200BEVS SATA Hard Drive draws .85 watts at idle, or .25 on standby.
