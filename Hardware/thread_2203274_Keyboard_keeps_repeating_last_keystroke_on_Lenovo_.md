### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-02-03
Take the machine apart and clean it.  It sounds like you spilled a soft drink.  The keyboard is relatively easy to remove, search around for your model.  I've seen  some suggestions about putting it in the dishwasher, but only on the low temperature cycle, with a small amount of detergent.  Let it dry for 3 days in a sunny window.  It's probably safer to use electrical contact cleaner and spray under the keys to get to the switches.  All it takes is one stuck key switch to cause the entire keyboard to act strange.
