### Post by cprofitt on 2012-08-14
Lenovo has a good record and they allow Optimus to be tamed... 

However, I recently made a purchase and despite being a loyal Lenovo fan over the past decade or so I went with a [System76]("http://www.system76.com") laptop. The core reason for looking at their stuff was supporting a company that actually sells the device with Ubuntu installed. When I did the research though it became apparent they are selling quality machines.

In the end the value I was getting from System76 far exceeded what Lenovo was bringing to the table. (Lenovo = $2589 vs System76 $1352). You can read [my other post]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=12167447&postcount=63") on the subject for a bit more detail on the specific hardware involved.

After placing the order I was amazed at the customer service (example below:)
[IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=222685&stc=1&d=1344950353[/IMG]
Seriously... most companies are all friendly up until they process your credit card... System76 has been friendly before and after.

In the end you have to look for the features you want and then narrow it down by compatibility with the current kernel (and hopefully future kernels).

I really wanted a nice LCD panel (1920x1080 - FHD) and the System76 panel I chose is the 95% gamut version.

I hope this helps.
