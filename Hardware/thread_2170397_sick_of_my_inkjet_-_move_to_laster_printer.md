### Post by kurt18947 on 2013-08-26
I would add Brother lasers as a choice as well.  They emulate HP pretty well apparently, they can run using generic PCL5 printer language.  I have 2 Brother inkjet MFDs and one laser MFD.  Yes, the laser toner does not dry out, the toner cartridge may need to be shaken every few weeks/months because it can cake a little.  Tiger Direct's Sunday flyer had a Lexmark X264dw multi-function on sale for $149, $169 everyday price.  I thought that was a screaming deal until I read some Amazon reviews.  The hardware seems okay but the complaints were that toner was expensive and the network software seemed flaky.  Of course the reviews were running Windows, Lexmark does offer Linux/Ubuntu print & scan drivers.  I have no experience with the machine, only saw the ad.
