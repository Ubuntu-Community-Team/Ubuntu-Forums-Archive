### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2013-08-05
You probably have Optimus/hybrid graphics, and installing proprietary Nvidia drivers breaks the Intel driver. Check lspci
```
lspci | grep VGA
```

If you have an Intel and nvidia GPU, remove the nvidia driver, reboot, and follow: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Bumblebee](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Bumblebee)
