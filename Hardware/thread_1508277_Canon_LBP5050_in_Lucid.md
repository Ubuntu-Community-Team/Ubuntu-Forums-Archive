### Post by dbiswas_007 on 2010-06-14
Canon LBP2900 Laser Printer is working in Lucid (10.04). I hv followed the guide in - [http://www.unixmen.com/linux-distributions/ubuntu/229-installation-canon-lbp2900-on-linux?joscclean=1&comment_id=968](http://www.unixmen.com/linux-distributions/ubuntu/229-installation-canon-lbp2900-on-linux?joscclean=1&comment_id=968)

I hv used the capt 2.0 printer drivers from Canon. After installation there may be 2 entries in System>Administration>Printing . one with Device URI stating with usb:// and another with ccp:// . Make the later one default, it should print.
