### Post by namaku0 on 2009-03-25
Umm, that Matrox card is an old card with old AGP slot and I
believe your onboard GPU much faster than that card.

If you want dual monitor there is PCI-E 16x slot for another
GPU card. There is many dual monitor cards out there for
under US$25 (should be lower than 20 pounds). That means
combined with your onboard GPU you can have 3 output for
monitor.

BTW, your onboard GPU should have dual monitor capabality,
consult the computer manual if there is any possibility
for the second connector through an extension cable from
motherboard.

EDIT: Sorry, but the spec mentioned on the manufacturer
website your computer have GeForce 7050 + nForce 610i and
according to this comparison chart:
[http://www.nvidia.com/object/IO_35712.html](http://www.nvidia.com/object/IO_35712.html)
there is no multi display capabality. Also that means 
the slot I mentioned above shoud be PCI-E 8x (but I am
not sure as the manufactures site says it's a 16x)

I guess buy a new card is the only solution for you.
