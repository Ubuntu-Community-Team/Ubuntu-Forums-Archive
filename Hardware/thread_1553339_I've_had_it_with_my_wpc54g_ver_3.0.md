### Post by skaramanger on 2010-08-19
Hi,

I solved my problem. I did look through lots of posts on this matter and decided to buy a USB adapter. A Gsky 500mW USB dongle even came with and extra 1' long antenna.  Even though my signal strength ocillates between -7dbs and -68dbs, (short antenna in same room as wap) my response time has never been better. It would've worked out of the box except that I had forgot that I'd setup mac access rules on my WAP.  As soon as I whitelisted the dongle, I got connected! Very Kewl.

My .02 cents worth.
