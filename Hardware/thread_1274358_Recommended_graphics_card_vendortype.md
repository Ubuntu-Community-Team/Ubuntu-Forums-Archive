### Post by amingv on 2009-09-24
AFAIK when it comes to graphic cards it works the other way around: Ubuntu does not support them; THEY support Ubuntu.

The major vendors of GPUs (namely ATI and Nvidia) offer proprietary drivers for their cards (which Ubuntu will help you install, takes less than 5 minutes). I prefer Nvidia personally, and I have never had any problems with it.

Intel, on the other hand...

Although your card seems to have restricted drivers for it, some people seem to have had trouble with it:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=603595](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=603595)
Maybe try the suggestions on the first post?
