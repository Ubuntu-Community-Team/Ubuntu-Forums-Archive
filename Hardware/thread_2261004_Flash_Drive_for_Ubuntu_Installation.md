### Post by Mike_Walsh on 2015-01-15
I can't say as I have ever bothered to read benchmarks on data transfer rates for flash drives, although I have done quite a number of flashdrive installs with various 'buntus. I have found, through trial and error (and with total disregard for benchmarks & reviews, since I find them so often to contradict each other!), that the SanDisk Cruzer 'Blade' and 'Fit' flash drives are very good in terms of bootability, and general overall reliability.

Don't expect a flashdrive install to boot up FAST, though.....even USB 3.0 ones; they're still slow compared to a good-quality HDD, and like snails compared to a SSD...

Mike.
