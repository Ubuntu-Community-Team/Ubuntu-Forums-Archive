### Post by Autodave on 2020-07-24
I am more inclined to believe that you may have a power supply issue.  Are these SSDs or spinners?  If SSDs, then your 5V line could be going low: how many other things are in USB ports?  Do you have a powered hub?  You may need one or a beefier PSU.

### Post by Autodave on 2020-07-24
Then disconnect all but one of them and run it like that and see what happens.  If no issues, then connect the second one and try.  Then the third.  

You could possibly have a controller on one of them failing.  You have have other USB devices: keyboard and mouse at least unless this is a laptop.  You could still have a 5V problem.
