### Post by brokenromeo on 2010-08-02
Was at walmart yesterday picking up a vacuum cleaner and saw they had a decent looking Acer Aspire laptop on sale for 298...on a whim I picked one up since I wanted a Ubuntu computer with a larger screen than my current only Linux machine which is a netbook size machine. It is amazing what you can buy these days. Specs are below, and this is a new amd processor which is single core, but with the ATI Radeon HD 4250 and this new processor, it is way faster than any netbook I have used. Most everything seems to work out of the gate, have not yet tried the hdmi output or hibernate, suspend seems to work though. Still trying to determine the best video driver to use...currently have the fglrx proprietary driver from Administration/Hardware Drivers section installed...seem to work well other than the lame low res boot splash screen. I am sure there will be a few issues along the way, but a great machine considering the extreme low cost.
