### Post by pastalavista on 2009-09-19
Maybe you have accidently hit the key combination to turn it off. On my Acer Aspire, the key combo to toggle the touchpad on/off is Fn-F7.

---

### Post by Ampi on 2009-09-19
Sorry!! I got my solution, the Fn+F7 did the trick, apparently you can enable and disable the mouse.
After hours of checking google, I got it! :)
