### Post by oldfred on 2022-08-09
This is now two years old, but useful to review:
Sabrent USB 3.2 enclosure and 2TB NVMe solid-state drive
[https://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=sabrent-rocketq-enclosure&num=1](https://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=sabrent-rocketq-enclosure&num=1)

I upgraded a SATA M.2 SSD to larger NVMe and then put SSD into an adapter with a full install & data. 
I only have USB3 ports but found it to be very fast as expected USB3 to slow it down, but almost as fast as it was internally.
I have found it to run warm, and thought NVMe drives were even warmer, so check that adapter has good heat sink.
