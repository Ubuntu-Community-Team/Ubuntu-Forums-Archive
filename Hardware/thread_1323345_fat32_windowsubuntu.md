Is there a way that jeg can get access to it from Windows

---

### Post by bernardfrancois on 2009-11-11
In windows, if you click on start > run, and then you enter 'compmgmt.msc'.
There you can go to 'disk management'. Normally you should see the partitions on your usb stick there. If you right-click on the fat32 partition you can choose to asign a drive letter.
