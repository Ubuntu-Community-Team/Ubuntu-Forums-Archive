### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-04-28
Ok, get a 1680 1050 modeline for the monitor with cvt.
```
cvt -v 1680 1050 60.0Hz
```
Change the '_' to a '@' and paste it in the Monitor section of your xorg.conf.  Reboot.
