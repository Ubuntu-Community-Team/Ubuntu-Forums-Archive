### Post by IcarusR on 2009-10-14
Having just run smartctl I find my drive has 42 errors in the past.
Not really worried though, as it is quiet & does not thrash.

I do however have a backup of important data on my server.

If you are concerned I would suggest you keep regular backups of any important data. External hard drives are cheap now.

I'm no expert on smartctl, but all your listed errors have identical details. Two are at the same time & three are at the same time. 
I guess there are some sectors that were unreadable.

Check out the SMART wiki [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/S.M.A.R.T.#Known_ATA_S.M.A.R.T._attributes]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/S.M.A.R.T.#Known_ATA_S.M.A.R.T._attributes")
