### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-08-27
Recent Linux kernels have a bug that prevents some processors from slowing down when you're not doing anything -- which results in them drawing nearly full power nearly all of the time.  This typically results in two problems: overheating and rapid battery drain.

If your laptop is overheating as well, you should look into Jupiter to see if that helps in any way:
