### Post by linuxwizard on 2008-10-09
HP printers are very well supported in Linux. If you know which model you are looking at. Look on this list for that model read the notes see if it is supported. [http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=HP](http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=HP)
They are classified in groups how they will work > Perfectly, Mostly, Partially, Unknown. Of course you want to try and find a printer that is classified as Perfectly.
