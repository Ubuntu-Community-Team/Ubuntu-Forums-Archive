### Post by talz13 on 2008-10-04
If that motherboard uses standard micro ATX mounting locations (where the mounting screws go), then you should be able to put it in any ATX type case.  I just ordered a Coolermaster HAF 932 which should be coming monday, and it has 6 spots for hard drives and 5 or 6 spots for 5 1/4" bays as well.  Kind of expensive, though, around $140-$160ish.
