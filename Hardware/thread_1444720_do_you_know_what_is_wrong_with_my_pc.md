### Post by TBABill on 2010-04-01
Weird scratchy noise sounds like something mechanical failing, such as a fan, hard drive, CD drive or something else with moving parts. Without your HDD plugged in you should at least get to the bios screen. If it's beyond that you may be in for a headache because if it was the fan or something in your power supply that failed, along with the PS itself, you could have had damage to the motherboard by either a spike or sharp drop in voltage and/or current. I'd try all the mechanical parts by unplugging one by one, but if that's not it all that's left is MB and its components (RAM, CPU).

Do you have 2 RAM DIMMS? Can you try them 1 at a time too? Could be something simple like that and worth trying to see if you get to the bios screen.

### Post by cascade9 on 2010-04-01
"Scratchy noise" is one way of describing electrical shorting. Which can be very bad for the components hooked up to the supply. 

You could very well have a dead motherboard/CPU/RAM (most likely the motherboard). The light going on is no sign that your board is fine, I've got a few dead ones just sitting around here that the light comes on for.
