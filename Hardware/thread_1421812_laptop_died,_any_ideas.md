### Post by akscotsman on 2010-03-19
It happened again so I'm thinking hardware issue.

I called Sony today and talked with a tech and was given the following information. If you have a fully charged battery and run the computer on AC for long periods of time there becomes a buildup of static on the internal components. When the computer turns on the screen will stay off because of the static charge. You need to remove the battery, plug in the AC power and hold the power button for 20 seconds in order to boot the computer.
To keep this from happening in the future, they suggest that when you have a full charge on the battery to unplug the AC and run off the battery for awhile.

I'll give it a try for a couple of days and see what happens.
