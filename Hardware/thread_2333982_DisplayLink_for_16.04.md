The DisplyLink package worked for me on both 16.04 and 16.10:

- HP Spectre x360, i7-7500U
- IoGear GUH3C44 ViewPro-C USB-C 4-in-1 Video Adapter

Both VGA and HDMI ports work after downloading the .run file from [http://www.displaylink.com/downloads/ubuntu](http://www.displaylink.com/downloads/ubuntu) and following installation instructions here: [http://support.displaylink.com/knowledgebase/articles/615714#ubuntu](http://support.displaylink.com/knowledgebase/articles/615714#ubuntu)
