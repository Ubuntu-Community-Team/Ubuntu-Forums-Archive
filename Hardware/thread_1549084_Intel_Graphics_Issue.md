### Post by snowpine on 2010-08-09
Welcome to the forums!

Let me answer your question with a question: Are you having any video issues, and if so, is there a specific reason why you think it is related to the Intel video drivers?

Ubuntu is designed so that all of the parts work together and are upgraded at once. So, the typical method to get "the latest version of x" would be to use the current release (10.04 Lucid). 9.04 is the April 2009 release, so everything is more than a year out of date. 9.04 was not tested to be stable with anything from 2010, so proceed with caution.

That being said, if you have a strong do-it-yourself ethic and are willing to risk breaking things, here's the best method I know of to get the latest video drivers as well as the necessary Xorg packages to support them: [https://launchpad.net/~xorg-edgers/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~xorg-edgers/+archive/ppa)
