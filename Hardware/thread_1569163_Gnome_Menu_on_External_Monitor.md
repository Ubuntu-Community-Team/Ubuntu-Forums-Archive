I have a netbook and can use an external monitor without problems.  I can also use an external monitor as an extended workspace, e.g. I can make the netbook monitor show the left side of a workspace and the external monitor the right side.

Trouble is, when I have the external monitor showing the right side of the workspace, the external monitor does not show the gnome bars -- neither the top (menus) nor the bottom (windows list.)

Any idea how to get the gnome bars/panels on the external monitor?  Thanks.

---

### Post by realzippy on 2010-09-06
...can`t you rightclick your panel,create a new panel and drag this to the right monitor?

NOTE: You can drag panels only, if they do not have the "expand" property set.
