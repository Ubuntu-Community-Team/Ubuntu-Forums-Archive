Do you use unity? Unity tends to take up a large portion of RAM in systems with less than 2 GB. Your best bet would be to try using the session Ubuntu Classic if unity is lagging down your system. It's so new, it doesn't quite work yet, and with all this focus on unity, some drivers and other components may fall behind.
