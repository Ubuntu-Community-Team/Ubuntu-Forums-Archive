### Post by cbope on 2009-09-20
Most modern NVIDIA GPU cards spin the fan at 100% upon post, to check that the fan is working. Many of them also spin the fan at higher levels but below 100% until after the driver loads. After the driver loads then the fan goes to a normal idle speed which is usually silent.
