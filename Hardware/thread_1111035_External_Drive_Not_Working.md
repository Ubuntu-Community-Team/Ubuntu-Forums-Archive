### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-03-30
Two things come to mind that may be causing the problem.

1. Does this external drive have a power supply to drive it? If so make sure you use it. If not, you may have an issue with not enough power to drive it from one use port. You can get a connector that will use two usb ports to power the drive.

2. Some external drives use some software on the drive and on the computer to make them work. When you did the reformat you may taken them off. Reload the software for the drive if you have it.
