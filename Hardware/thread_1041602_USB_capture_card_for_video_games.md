### Post by electrogeist on 2009-01-18
Not requiring HD capture is a big money saver.

The lag-free requirement is going to burn you.  If your goal is to just record, then I would look into splitting the signal and watching a monitor/tv with live feed.  If your goal is to use the laptop's display to play your games the lag may be an issue.
