### Post by kerry_s on 2008-07-31
[http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&q=reduce+computer+noise&btnG=Google+Search](http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&q=reduce+computer+noise&btnG=Google+Search)

just look through the tips, see what you have lying around that can accomplish the same results.

in my case i used slower, but bigger fans and the padding on the inside door, i also have all wires tie strapped.
the padding is packaging kind that's usually used for shipping, it's a white flat sheet, the small tie straps where like a buck, fans i got from a friend who went to water cooling.
