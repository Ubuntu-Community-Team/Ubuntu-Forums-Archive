### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2015-05-15
The SCX-4623F had some reported issues back in its time, according to a quick Google search. I suggest putting the "used to work fine" in context: Has it worked "plug'n'play" before? Or have you/someone installed Samsung's drivers in previous Ubuntu versions? Any other "teaks" you're aware of?

There's the Samsung Unified Driver you may try: [http://www.samsung.com/us/support/owners/product/SCX-4623F/XAA](http://www.samsung.com/us/support/owners/product/SCX-4623F/XAA)
It should be as simple as download, extract and run *install.sh* in the extracted folder. Issues may arise though and in that case please copy/paste any error messages here. Please also note that some topics back in 2011 advised against installing this driver due to some dependencies or compatibility problems with the then current Ubuntu release. The driver available in the above link is fairly recent (2013) so chances are it works.
