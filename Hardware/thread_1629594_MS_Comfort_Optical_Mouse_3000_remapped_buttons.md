### Post by Splat_NJ on 2010-11-23
For anyone using a Microsoft Comfort Optical Mouse 3000 and would like  to get the side red button on the mouse to be the "back" button this is  the command you need to enter into terminal:
xinput set-button-map "Microsoft Microsoft Optical Mouse with Tilt Wheel" 1 2 3 4 5 6 9 7 8

This will get your side red button to be the "back" button, however this  also makes a tilt right of the wheel a "forward" button. Not a bad  setup, actually, and took me about 30mins to figure out. Oh, I'm running Ubuntu 10.10. Cheers.
