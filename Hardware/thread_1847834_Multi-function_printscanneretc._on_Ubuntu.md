### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-09-21
Certainly some of the canon multifunction machines work well in ubuntu but you may need to download the drivers from the canon website.

For some weird reason it appears that the Australian website has some drivers that are not found elsewhere, or perhaps it is just a search fault that I made at some point in the past.

here is where I found the drivers for a canon mfp 250
[http://support-au.canon.com.au/contents/AU/EN/0100236101.html](http://support-au.canon.com.au/contents/AU/EN/0100236101.html)
Perhaps it will help you find other machine drivers if this one is not right for your machine.
