### Post by Findarato on 2009-10-03
I am not completey sure, but charging a laptop doesn't seem to be able to be related to any software issue. The only thing that COULD happen is that your laptop actually gets charged, but the software doesn't register it. If however your battery simply won't charge, the only explanation for me would be a hardware problem.
