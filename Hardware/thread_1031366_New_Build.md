### Post by nick09 on 2009-01-07
The ASUS M3A79-T Deluxe motherboard looks like a good one and the AMD Phenom 9600 CPU is good also. I would go with the Rosewill TU-155-P mid ATX tower. 

Now for a power supply you will need quite some power. A 600W power supply is what you will need, like the XIGMATEK MC NRP-MC651. For what you want, you need to push the budget a bit here.

The motherboard I suggested does not have a integrated video card but that will not be a problem. ATI is getting better but if you really want to game then grab a nvidia card. I think the MSI N9600GT will do you fine for a graphics card. I would not worry about a sound card as the motherboard already has a good one integrated in it.

For a fan controller I would recommend the sunbeam brand. They have two models that support 20w and 30w in each channel. Either one would easily control a Vantec Tornado fan. A good card reader to take up a 3.5" space would be the Rosewill RCR-IC002.

When you build the computer, don't place the motherboard on a metal object or you could fry a part of the motherboard. Use a anti-static Wrist Strap(clip on a piece of unpainted metal on the case) and a anti-static pad.
