### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2010-04-09
If you go to the HDD manufacturer's website, you should be able to download an iso image that you burn to a disk and then you boot your system from this disk.

It will allow you to run full diagnostics on the suspect HDD.

Of course, you will need access to another PC initially to do the download and to burn the iso image onto a CDROM.
