### Post by glln0v on 2013-04-06
Unless you do high-end gaming, I would recommend the Ivy-Bridge integrated graphics. The open-source drivers are trouble-free. If you wait a few months, you'll be able to buy the Haswell CPU which will have even better graphics.

I'd stay away from the proprietary stuff unless you need the gaming performance.
