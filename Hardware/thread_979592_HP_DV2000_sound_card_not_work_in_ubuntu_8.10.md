### Post by junejune0605 on 2008-11-14
I got this from another thread (can't remember the link)

Basically, right click on your volume control on your taskbar and you will see options such as lock to panel... remove from panel... etc etc...

Click on "Open Volume Control"

There you will see "master" "pcm" "ext mixer"

Un-mute the "pcm" that worked for me.... i was without sound for like 2 weeks, it was driving me crazy...

Hope this helps!!
