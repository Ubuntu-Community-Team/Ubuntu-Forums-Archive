### Post by Aearenda on 2009-04-26
To get your threads, use the 'search' drop-down in the row immediately under the forum name - it's got 'find all your posts' and 'find all your threads' near the bottom.

The Intel video driver should be automatically installed - you only need to do something special if you have ATI or Nvidia hardware, or if you need a very recent version of the Intel driver.
