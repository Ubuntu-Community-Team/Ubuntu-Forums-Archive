### Post by CoNinja on 2009-11-23
If this isn't in the right forum I'm sorry. I know a lot of people had this problem on ubuntu 7-8 and I had it on this release. I switched over to nvidia proprietary drivers and realized I couldn't go over resolutions higher than 1360x768(never checked with generic drivers) My monitor's native resolution is 1280x1024. I tried many sudo commands and attempted to change xorg.conf with no success. 

So, if you are like me and have tried all of these fixes check your VGA/DVI cable. I was using a cheap belkin cable. I switched over to a higher grade one that was also able to pull the vendor ID etc etc from the monitor of which the belkin was not capable. Both cables have same pin count, I don't understand. However, this fixed the issue for me. If you are having troubles and have given up hope, give this a try.
