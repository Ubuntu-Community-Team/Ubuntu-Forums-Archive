I have two monitors hooked up with DVI and the other with DisplayPort.  This monitor has native support for DisplayPort, so I am not using one of those dongles (active or passive). The trouble I am having is that when I turn off the displayport monitor, the graphics card somehow knows that the monitor is turned off, and for whatever reason, changes how the monitors are configured.  So I go from having one big desktop, to having three monitors with cloned images.  Logging out and back in again seems to fix the problem, as does messing with CCC.  I would rather just be able to turn my monitors off and then turn them back on again to see everything exactly where I left things.
