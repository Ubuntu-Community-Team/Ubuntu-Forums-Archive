HDD spin up and down for various reasons.  Perhaps the file system is journalling, or a service is running in the background like a cron job etc.  Could be a thousand reasons, none of them a Virus ;-)

Linux does not suffer from Viruses, or at least there are no current ones in the wild designed for Linux, as for malware in general then it is possible depending on what you do and how you do it.

however all that aside it is likely to be normal operation
