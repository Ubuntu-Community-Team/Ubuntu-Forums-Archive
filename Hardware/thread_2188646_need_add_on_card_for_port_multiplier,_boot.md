### Post by thompsw on 2013-11-22
I decided to take a chance on this controller card:

SYBA SY-PEX40039 PCI-Express 2.0 SATA III (6.0Gb/s) Controller Card

I received it last night from Newegg, took a couple of minutes to install and attach the cables to the external ports.  It's now connected to an Orico 3xhdd (3.5" or 2.5") external "enclosure".  I put enclosure in quotes because the drives are removable.  It all works well.  I've done quite a bit of I/O already on the setup and am now booting from one of the external drives.

Here's the enclosure:

ORICO 6638SUSJ3-BK 3 Bay 3.5 inch USB3.0 & ESATA Hard Drive Docking Station

I haven't done any benchmarks and likely won't get to that unless I notice issues.
