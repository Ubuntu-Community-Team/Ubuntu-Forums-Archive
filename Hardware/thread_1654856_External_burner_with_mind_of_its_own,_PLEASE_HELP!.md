### Post by coffeecat on 2010-12-29
I was suggesting a failing power supply, not a failed one. It would provide power, but at insufficient voltage, so the drive would get warm (and buzz) but not work.

When I said check the rating, I meant look at the data on the alternative power supply and check that this has the same voltage and can deliver a current equal to or greater than the original one. But since you haven't got a spare power supply, this is theoretical.
