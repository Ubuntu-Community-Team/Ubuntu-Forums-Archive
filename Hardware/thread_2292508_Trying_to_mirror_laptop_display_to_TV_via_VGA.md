Kubuntu 15.04 is running the new KDE5, and many people have found it to be [somewhat buggy]("https://www.kubuntuforums.net/showthread.php?68105-Don-t-upgrade-to-15-04-just-yet-like-I-did"). I'm sticking with 14.04 until at least the next LTS release of Kubuntu which happens in April of next year.
