### Post by bertrand.barbier on 2011-03-17
Hi all,

I thought I would share this with everyone else in case one of you finds yourself in a similar bind I got myself into.

After a fresh installation of the Ubuntu 10.10 on my HP Pavilion dv5-1153ei laptop, I could not print any documents in landscape. I would set the orientation to Landscape but it would still come out as Portrait (even the preview showed Landscape orientation).

After a couple of days, the same issue happened but this time with the Landscape orientation to Portrait print.

For over 7days now, I've been very frustrated. To resolve this all I had to do was delete the printer from my printer list (I have only one printer in the list. HP Officejet 6313 All in one series).

I 'reinstalled' the printer (it is actually a network printer, not using cables). Now, after a couple of prints. It would seem the problem has been averted and I can continue with regular office work.

(was starting to have second guesses about Ubuntu and I'm scared to go back to Windows) :)
