### Post by efflandt on 2010-09-11
If disconnecting/reconnecting the cables does nothing, I would try removing and reseating the video card.  Static electricity should not be a problem unless the air is very dry where you are (winter or hot and dry).  Static should not be a problem if mild, hot and humid, or rainy.

Even though video cards are solid state for the most part (other than fans), they sometimes do burn out if made with other overrated electronic components.  Although, I got suckered into a Monster DVI cable once that is so thick and stiff that something was not always making good contact (monitor often would not wake from standby), because the same monitor would work with VGA, and I have had no problems using a slimmer, less expensive, more flexible DVI cable (or DVI to HDMI cable with HDTV).
