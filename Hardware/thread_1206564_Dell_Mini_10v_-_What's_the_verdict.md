### Post by dckrooney on 2009-07-08
I've had mine for about a month now, and as soon as I got it, I wiped 8.10 and did a clean install of 9.04.

Since then I've had no issues of any kind. It works flawlessly.

My next major change will be to add a SSD (OCZ's Vertex), as the only complaints I'd make of the Mini 10v are of the hard drive. It's loud (given that everything else is silent, it's REALLY obvious to me... nitpicking, I know), and loading applications takes longer than I'd like (I'm looking at you Mozilla).

Hopefully the SSD will sort these out. :P
