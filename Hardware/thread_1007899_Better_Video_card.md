### Post by Existentialist on 2008-12-16
Well if you are looking to going with an Nvidia card, you won't find anything else in the $100 range other than the 9800gt.  If you do go this route; the dual slot cooler version of the 9800gt is on sale now for $95.

[http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814130397](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814130397)

You could go with a 9800 GTX+ for around $150, or GTX 260 for about $200.  Here is a link to the frame rates achieved playing Crysis with some different cards.  Going to depend how much the extra FPS are worth to you, and additionally; what resolution you want to play at.  Lower resolutions will be less benefited by SLI, as the CPU becomes a bottleneck.  In these cases, a higher-end single card is often a better choice.

[http://www.guru3d.com/category/vga_charts_crysis/](http://www.guru3d.com/category/vga_charts_crysis/)
