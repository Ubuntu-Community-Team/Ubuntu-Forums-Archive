### Post by sibgatullin on 2009-08-09
Just tried. Ubuntu 9.04, LG KP500 and everything works great (excluding GPRS).
I've plugged phone to USB and have selected "PC Suite" on phone. After few second the NetworkManager wizard appear.
But nobody cares about GPRS.
It looks like there is only one way to "sync" contacts. It's possible to save all phone contacts to one .vcf and then download it to PC. Also you can import .vcf files by launching them from phone's filemanager.
