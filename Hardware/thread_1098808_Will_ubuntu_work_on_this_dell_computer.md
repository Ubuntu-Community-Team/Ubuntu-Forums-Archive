### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2009-03-17
I have many GX260's in my office that I've put Ubuntu on and had no trouble --except that I strongly recommend updating to the latest BIOS (A09) because early BIOS's had bugs that caused low resolutions with the onboard intel graphics.

I've also seen some people report problems with the graphics even with the BIOS upgraded, but I've not experienced this.
