Never mind. I Figgured it out. Catalyst 9.3 can be installed on X.org 7.4 . X.org 7.3 is in 8.04
And last kernel that works is 2.6.29 or 2.6.28. In Ubuntu 8.04 is 2.6.24.
YAY :D maybe now I can get to play games again without going back to windows XD
