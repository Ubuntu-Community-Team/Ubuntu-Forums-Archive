### Post by SoFl W on 2010-09-06
Popped capacitors usually means there is another problem, possibly heat related.  Most of the time on-line you need to buy several of them from an on-line retailer.  I would ask the local Radio-Shack if you have one. 
I once had a Dell monitor that went bad with popped capacitors, turned out it was a common problem with that monitor and found some one on ebay that was selling a package containing three capacitors, a resistor and one other part along with a description on how to fix the exact problem I had with the monitor.
Check ebay, or a local shop like Radio Shack.
