### Post by TheFu on 2020-03-19
No motherboards support hardware RAID.  They only support Fake-RAID.  Learn the difference so you make an informed choice between HW, SW, Fake RAID solutions.

Almost always, Fake-RAID is the worse choice.
HW-raid requires an expensive raid card - usually $250+.
SW-raid is flexible and fast.  Usually the best choice.

Google if you want more details about why those statements are true.
