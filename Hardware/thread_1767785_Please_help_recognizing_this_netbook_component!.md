### Post by fballem on 2011-05-26
I think this is referred to as an LVDS (low voltage differential signalling) cable. You will need to be careful to specify the brand and model of the laptop. There is a wikipedia article here: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Low-voltage_differential_signaling](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Low-voltage_differential_signaling)

If you need it, you may be able to find a service manual for your laptop here: [http://www.laptopmanualsnow.com/](http://www.laptopmanualsnow.com/) Once you have selected your laptop service manual, there are 4 or 5 pages of ads that you have to go through (about 15 seconds each page, then select next underneath the ads), but the manuals are free.

Hope this helps.
