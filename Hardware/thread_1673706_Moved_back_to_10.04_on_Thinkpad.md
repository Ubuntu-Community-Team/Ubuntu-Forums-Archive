### Post by Duncan J Murray on 2011-01-22
After a few months of using 10.10 on my T40 thinkpad with Mobility Radeon 7500 32MB graphics, I moved back to 10.04, initially for compatibility with my printer.

It seems that the graphics are running much better in 10.04 than on 10.10.  I had problems with compiz, which meant that visual effects could not be enabled.  After a lot of googling, I discovered a way to enable it, but with poor (very low frame rates) effects.

The only downside is that I lost the ubuntu font - but having installed it and meddled with the appearances*, I now have the best of both worlds!

Duncan (once-again happy T40/ubuntu owner).

*Ok so I go carried away.  I now have a custom desktop with the humanity icon them, with ambiance for the toolbar and human-clearlooks controls - is nice!
