### Post by jjex22 on 2011-11-22
It can be both - for myself I can set several things about brightness in my BIOS - basically bright, dim, same as last shutdown, or dependant on AC power.

Also 11.10 causes some issues with changing brightness surrounding power saving plans - when booted, check your power settings in the power settings - by default it auto dims on battery power - you can turn this off if you like.

Hope this helps :)
