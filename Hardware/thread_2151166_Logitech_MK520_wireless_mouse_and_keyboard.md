### Post by geomcd1949 on 2013-06-03
Just a note that I hope saves someone from the hours of searching that I went through:

Got a Logitech wireless mouse and keyboard. Ubuntu 12.04 wouldn't recognize either. Searched; tried many things; nothing worked.

Then realized I had the old wireless mouse in range of the computer, and it (the mouse) was still turned On and had batteries in it. Turned the mouse off and removed the batteries. Ubuntu immediately recognized the new keyboard and mouse, and both now work perfectly.
