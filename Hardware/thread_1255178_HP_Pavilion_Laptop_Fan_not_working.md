### Post by sergiom99 on 2009-09-01
I dont have any problems with mine. But, this model is not included in the service release that jerrrys posted, so no problems on that end.

I would first update your BIOS, following the instructions on the service release article, and then try to use a custom DSDT file. It helped a lot in mine, and now it runs cooler and quieter.
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1036051&highlight=dsdt](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1036051&highlight=dsdt)
Its very easy, read the whole thread and check if there's already a fixed DSDT for your model.

Good luck!
