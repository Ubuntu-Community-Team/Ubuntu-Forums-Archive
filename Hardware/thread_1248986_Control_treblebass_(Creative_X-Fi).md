### Post by mr_muddle on 2009-11-11
It may be a little different for you, but I have 9.10 installed and a Creative Audigy, but maybe it's the same? Worth a try.

If you right-click the speaker icon in the task bar and click 'Sound Preferences' -> 'Hardware' tab -> 'Profile' dropdown
From there you can select either analog or digital outputs and choose between stereo/5.1 etc.

For speaker / headphone settings:

'Sound Preferences' -> 'Output' tab -> 'Connector' dropdown
