### Post by joe.turion64x2 on 2008-11-13
If you have the SSD version (8GB) then you will want to get an adapted Ubuntu for it (Ubuntu for portables, I think) because a full version, although it would fit and perhaps detect everything, it would wear the SSD drive too soon (have in mind that SSD drives support less write cycles in any given sector, than a regular hard drive).
