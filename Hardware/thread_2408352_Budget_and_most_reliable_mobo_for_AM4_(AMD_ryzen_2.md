### Post by him610 on 2018-12-17
You might want to consider your total budget you have allocated for your system build. Which might include.... 
APU/CPU
Motherboard (size)
Operating System
Maximum RAM vs Affordable RAM
Graphics Card
Video Memory
Wired 
Wireless
Bluetooth
Webcam
HDD
SSD
Optical Drive
Display
Case (size to fit MB)
Power Supply (sized to load and case size)
Keyboard
Mouse

You can put your model system, as well as, possible alternative systems, in a spreadsheet for comparison purposes. This is actually the fun part of building a new system. 
Another resource you might checkout is [https://pcpartpicker.com/]("https://pcpartpicker.com/")
