### Post by tamoneya on 2008-05-08
building a laptop is very uncommon.  I strongly recommend that you just buy a prebuilt one.  This is because the components are so customized and integrated that it can be very hard to improve upon what is already on the market.  If your reason is just because you dont want to have windows already installed you should take a look at [http://system76.com/index.php?cPath=28](http://system76.com/index.php?cPath=28)
