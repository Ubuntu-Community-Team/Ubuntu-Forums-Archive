### Post by coffeecat on 2008-09-06
The version of Ubuntu on the Dell is not the standard Hardy Heron, but the netbook remix one. Apart from the special user interface I believe it includes certain tweaks to enable faster bootup. Unfortunately, it's not available (yet) as a download for we ordinary mortals. There's a howto [here](https://launchpad.net/netbook-remix) for tweaking vanilla Hardy to make it work like the netbook remix, but it looks like a work in progress. And I wouldn't be surprised if Dell hasn't done their own tweaking to optimise Netbook Remix for their own hardware.

Why not ask Dell if they can sell/give you a copy of their Ubuntu Netbook Remix restore CD? If they are willing/able to, I would imagine that would be the best way of getting Ubuntu onto the machine.

**Edit:** [More information here]("http://www.canonical.com/netbooks") on the Netbook Remix. You can see that their download link is not for an iso, but a link to the launchpad page.
