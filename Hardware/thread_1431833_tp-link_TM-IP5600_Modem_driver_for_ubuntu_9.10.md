### Post by IcarusR on 2010-03-17
There are two sorts of modems, full hardware & software modems (winmodem)
Hardware modems all work under linux using the kernel serial driver.
Software modems are another thing, they require drivers if available

I suggest you read the modem howto & a few other pages

[http://tldp.org/HOWTO/Modem-HOWTO.html]("http://tldp.org/HOWTO/Modem-HOWTO.html")
