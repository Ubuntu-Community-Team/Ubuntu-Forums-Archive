### Post by mc4man on 2020-03-28
For DVD material the difference between usb3 & 2 would be nil as your 'bottleneck' will be the read speed of the drive.
For an external drive you'll get an effective rate of 4-8x which is well within the range of usb2 data transfer in practice.
Internal sata dvd drives can consistently read much faster on desktop hardware, laptop dvd drives are best case only minimally better than good external ones, in many cases the same or even worse..
