### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2012-06-29
I'm pretty sure nvidia only does Optimus graphics with mobile Geforce cards nowadays. If you want a powerful, discrete nvidia card on a laptop, it's Optimus or nothing.

You're better off just getting an Intel Ivybridge (HD4000) GPU. They do video playback acceleration well, and unless you're a real hardcore gamer running stuff through wine, they'll be okay for 3D at laptop resoultions.
