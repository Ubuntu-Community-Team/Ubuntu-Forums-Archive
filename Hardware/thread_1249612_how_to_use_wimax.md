### Post by alfacher on 2009-08-27
Hi [a7med93]("http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=886215"). madWiMAX is a reverse-engineered Linux driver for mobile WiMAX (802.16e) devices based on Samsung CMC-730 chip. These devices are currently supported: 
[LIST]
[*]Samsung SWC-U200
[*]Samsung SWC-E100
[*]Samsung SWM-S10R (built in Samsung NC-10 netbook)
[/LIST]
