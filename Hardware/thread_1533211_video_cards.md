### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-07-17
Yes, go with nvidia, not ati, which in some cases work OK, in others cases are a pain in the whatsit.

Which nvidia will depend not only on your budget, as howefield says, but on which type your motherboard will accept; AGP, pci, pci-express, etc etc.  If it is an old AGP type, your choice will be very limited, unless you chance a used model from ebay or somewhere similar.
