### Post by DizzyTech on 2008-12-11
Yes, this would be much easier on a Mac (or even a PC).  But don't go spending your arm and leg just yet.  I highly recommend installing VirtualBox ([http://www.virtualbox.org](http://www.virtualbox.org)) and installing a virtual machine of Windows XP.  If you don't have an XP disc, I would also recommend obtaining the TinyXP CD from the website dedicated to those who swashbuckle.  Simply install XP in the VM, install VirtualBox's Additions to the VM, install iTunes in the VM, and hook up your iPod.
