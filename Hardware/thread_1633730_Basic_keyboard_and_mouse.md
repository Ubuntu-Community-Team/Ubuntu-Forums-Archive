### Post by lombaardcj on 2010-12-06
My advice:  Try getting hold of a USB hub or USB splitter that is  powered from the USB port, not externally.  This way you can take the option you mentioned and not worry about replacing the mouse or keyboard at later stage.

The splitter I have used looks like a Y with one end going into the PC/Laptop USB Port and the other two ends splitting into two ports.  Just ask around.  Sometimes these splitters are multi purpose including a USB flash disk per splitter.
