### Post by Lokesh123 on 2011-01-16
> **Smipims said:**
> Any tips at all?

Download [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-input-synaptics/+bug/308191/comments/167](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-input-synaptics/+bug/308191/comments/167)

double-click to install. It will

[LIST=1]
[*]avoid this weird hopping of the pointer
[*]enable scrolling with two fingers, even better than under Win7
[*]**disable **normal right-clicking, but you can still right-click by double-clicking with two fingers
[*]disable normal click & drag which requires 2 fingers. It can still be done, though, by single finger click, hold and drag.
[/LIST]
It is not perfect, try Solitaire and you know what I mean. But it is in my view much better than was before. 2 Finger scrolling works perfectly.

The discussion is ongoing, unfortunately those preferring right-click and those, asking for click&drag,  are confusing the discussion.
