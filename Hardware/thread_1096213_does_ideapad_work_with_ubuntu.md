### Post by undoIT on 2009-03-14
I have had no problems with the Intel X4500 and 5100 wireless card on another laptop model, so these components shouldn't give you any trouble. If you don't do any gaming and value battery life, it would be better to go with the integrated graphics card over the Nvidia card. The X4500 is plenty powerful for most things you'd be using it for. Nvidia drivers aren't always that good, especially on newer graphics cards. They really need to open source their drivers like Intel.
