### Post by mgmiller on 2011-05-06
I had purchased a Canon i960 printer shortly before swithcing to Ubuntu a few years back.  I went crazy trying to get it to work.  I finally gave up and bought the turboprint driver, which has worked great.  I even upgraded to version 2 a year or so back.  It's worth it to avoid the hassle and I love the printer.  You can download it and try it out for free, then if you feel you want to you can purchase a key to unlock it.

I looked at their compatibility list and they show support for Canon Pixma MP620.  They seem to imply this is a multifunction device.  This driver is for the printer part only, not the scanner.

Since they support it, they can probably tell you how to get the wireless connection going for it. 

[http://www.turboprint.info/download.html](http://www.turboprint.info/download.html)
