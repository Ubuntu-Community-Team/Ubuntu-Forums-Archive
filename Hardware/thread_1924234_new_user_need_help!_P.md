### Post by madvinegar on 2012-02-12
First of all get internet connection (i.e. through an ethernet cable connected with your router or through wifi) and update all your system packages. To do that go to synaptics package manager (system>administration>synaptics), mark all upgrades and then apply.

After your system has been updated, go to "additional drivers"(system>administration>additional drivers) and see if there is any proprietary driver recomended for your graphics card and enable it.
