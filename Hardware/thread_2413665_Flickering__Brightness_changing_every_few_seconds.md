### Post by TheFu on 2019-02-28
I was seeing something similar on an i5-8250U laptop. It was a loose power cord. It was really picky. If I unplugged the cord and setup the power prefs for battery, it stopped.  The brightness controls on the keyboard helped too.

### Post by Autodave on 2019-02-28
When all else fails:

  	 	 	 	   This may or may not help, but it is the very first thing that I do when I get a weird thing happening on a laptop:

1. Power down and disconnect power cord.
2. Unplug all peripherals.
3. Remove battery.
4. Wait 10 minutes.
5. Reinstall battery.
6. Connect power cord.
7. Boot computer and test to see if condition is gone.
8. If good so far, now you can reconnect peripherals.
