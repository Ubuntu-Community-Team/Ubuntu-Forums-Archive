### Post by recluce on 2009-08-23
Can't help you with the enclosures, except that anything I have thrown at my system so far (No Name el cheapo, Icy Box, Star Tech Dock) has worked without a hitch.

The 1TB WD caviar black works fine on my system.

I am using the WD caviar black 1TB with a "Star Tech" dock, connected to a PC card eSATA controller - and I am very satisfied. Works without a hitch, including features like SMART, that are not available through USB. Also, this setup is roughly three times faster than USB 2.0.
