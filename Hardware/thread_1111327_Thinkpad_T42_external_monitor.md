### Post by kestrel1 on 2009-03-30
There shouldn't be a problem using a 24inch screen. You just need the correct cable. Most monitors still have the older 15 pin D type connection, as does your laptop. Look for the port with the blue inner (most are blue, some are black)

### Post by kestrel1 on 2009-03-30
To run anything in High Def you do need HDMI connections & good quality cables.
You shouldn't have any problem getting the monitor to work, but we are here if you do have problems.
I have used my HP laptop with my 32inch LCD TV & it worked like a charm. Just plug it in & away it went.
If you do not see anything on screen, you may need to press FN & the relevent F key (often F5 or F8) & this should switch between screens. I think if you are connected first though you should be fine. You may also want to check in the BIOS to make sure that both screens will be used on boot or default it to the external.
