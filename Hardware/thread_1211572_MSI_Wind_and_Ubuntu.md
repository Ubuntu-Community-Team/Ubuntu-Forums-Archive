### Post by quixote on 2009-07-14
Just a week ago I bought an MSI Wind U100 for a nephew and set up both Mac OSX on it and Netbook Remix Jaunty.  Well, to be exact, I messed up my dual boot attempt, so I'll have to re-do Remix, but I've used all 3 OSes on it.

XP (which comes with it) runs okay, but I'd say it's the weakest of the lot.  Very nice as a Hackintosh.  Netbook Remix is phenomenal.  Highy recommended.  Obviously, everything worked under XP.  Once I had all the right drivers (kexts, they call them, for some reason) everything, including webcam and bluetooth also worked under OSX.  I think everything worked out of the box under Remix, but I'm not sure whether I tested bluetooth or not.

The computer is wonderfully light.  I found the keyboard very good, although a friend of mine with large hands said it was cramped.  The screen is good, 1024x600, but the rather short vertical means that lots of web pages require scrolling down to get all of the first page.

I'd recommend it highly, and it does work fine with Ubuntu, at least with NetBook Remix.
