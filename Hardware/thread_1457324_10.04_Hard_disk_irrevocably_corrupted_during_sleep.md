### Post by quixote on 2010-04-19
Sounds like what happened is it didn't really suspend, just blanked the screen.  (Suspend can be flaky, as you probably know :( )  The ran the battery down and since power management wasn't working right, it didn't shut down right.  There's the option to skip disk checking.  For one second or so, it says to press ESC or Ctrl-C or something to skip the check.  You only have, literally, about 1 second to do it.  If you tell it to skip the check, will it boot?

If yes, tell it to shutdown.  After a normal shutdown, it may be less persnickety.
