### Post by ModelM on 2011-06-26
About 6 months ago I wanted to putz around with a Linux laptop & bought a refurb Thinkpad. It's been great & if I wanted another laptop that's the first thing I'd look at.

Staples & Tiger Direct sell refurb & off-lease Thinkpads but you never know what they'll have. Mine is a T60 (Core 2 duo, DDR2 ram, etc) & I paid $300 for it. In your price range you could get a newer model.

If you've never dealt with a Thinkpad (especially the T-series) they're a real treat. Wonderful support in Linux, amazing construction, features you can't find elsewhere like the roll-cage, & little treats like the thinklight & docking connector.

There's a whole subculture out there of Thinkpad fans swapping parts, upgrading displays, trading photos (Thinkpad porn) of Thinkpads on the International Space Station (it's the only laptop approved for use up there) and generally having a blast.

I have another new laptop but for just plain fun the refurb T60 has no equal & runs Debian like it was made for it.
