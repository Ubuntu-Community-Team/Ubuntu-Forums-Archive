### Post by imgx64 on 2010-11-29
I have the same problem on my HP dv5 laptop. I couldn't find a solution, but I found a workaround. When menus and buttons freeze, press <ctrl>+<alt>+F1 to switch to tty1 then <ctrl>+<alt>+F7 to get back to the GUI. This unfreezes it most of the time.

Hope that helps. :D
