### Post by dominiquec on 2008-08-09
I'm using an Epson C90.  Depending on where you are in the world, you might know this as the D92.

I've had it converted to continuous ink supply to save on the cartridges.

Apart from having to use a different driver than the one CUPS recommends, it works perfectly for me.

I use it for printing iron-ons for t-shirts.

I did a little [write up on my experiences.]("http://ubuntuliving.blogspot.com/2008/04/epson-stylus-c90-with-ciss-modification_11.html")
