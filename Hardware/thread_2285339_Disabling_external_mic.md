### Post by halfnote52 on 2015-07-05
Well, you MAY be in for an adventure in dis-assembly, depending on how the thing is engineered. Here's why:

It may be that plugging in a mic to the external jack PHYSICALLY (or electronically) alters the circuit, and disables the onboard mic via either the chip or the circuit, or even by pushing a switch out of the way.  Maybe try superglue on the butt-end of a shish-kebab skewer? Let it dry and then pull the whole thing out? Just don't let it glom onto the SIDES of the port, or you're REALLY hosed.  ; )
