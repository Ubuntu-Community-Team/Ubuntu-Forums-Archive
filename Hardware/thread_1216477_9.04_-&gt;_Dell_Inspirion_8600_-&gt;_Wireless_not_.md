### Post by Ayuthia on 2009-07-21
If you currently have a working internet connection in Ubuntu, can you try going into Synaptic and install b43-fwcutter?  It will need to download the firmware to cut the portions it needs to make your wireless card work.  Once that is complete, you will need to restart your computer and hopefully your wireless card will work.  If it doesn't, please post the results of:
```
dmesg|grep b43
```
