### Post by aysiu on 2008-09-24
I have a Sansa Clip, and mine just mounts as removable storage.

In the Clip (before you connect it to your computer), make sure you change USB mode to MSC (not MTP or Autodetect).

### Post by aysiu on 2008-09-24
> **wayneo said:**
> Hi Aysiu,
How do I go about changing the settings as you suggested?
Thanks
Wayne
Turn on your Sansa Clip (while it is not connected to your computer)

Go to the *Settings* menu and click right

Click up twice to go to *USB Mode* and click right

Then click down twice to go to *MSC* and click right

Then plug your Sansa Clip into your computer.
