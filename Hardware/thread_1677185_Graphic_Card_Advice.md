Quadro NVS300? No way. Overpriced, a huge amount. From experience, passive heatsinks in a low-profile cases are a bad combination. Quadros are just normal nividia geforce chips with more features (meant for CAD and digital media workstations) 

MSI R5670 PD1G-OC is (porbably) a full height card. (hard to tell because of the MSI discalimer)-

[http://www.msi.com/product/vga/R5670-PD512.html](http://www.msi.com/product/vga/R5670-PD512.html)

I'd probably get a low profile GT430-
