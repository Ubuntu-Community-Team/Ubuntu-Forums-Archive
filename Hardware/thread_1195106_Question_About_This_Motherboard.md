### Post by Therion on 2009-06-23
> **izizzle said:**
> Also, I need to know how many IDE ports it has, because I have 2 IDE devices: 1 IDE Hard Drive and 1 IDE DVD/CD drive. My question is, will both of these be able to run on the board?
Of course... One channel/connector is for the hard drive/s (thats the white connector at the top, next to the 24-pin main power connector) and the other channel/connector is for the optical drive/s (that's the green connector next to the orange SATA ports me thinks, though it could be the black connector behind the PCI slots, it's hard to tell for sure from the picture.  Pretty sure the black connector is the Floppy Drive connector).  Again, the typical PATA "ribbon" cable will support two devices.
