### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2011-01-22
I'm not really sure what you expect to get out of this question. You're going to get a bunch of answers that probably won't show a clear pattern, and a bunch of people saying, "I only use this kind of drive."

Drive life varies and sometimes you get one on the early side of the curve. 
> I had used this disk for 1 year and 3 months, with typical usage being about 4 powered on hours per day.
FWIW, drives that fail earlier in life tend to be more prone to cold temperature failure. [http://research.google.com/archive/disk_failures.pdf](http://research.google.com/archive/disk_failures.pdf)
