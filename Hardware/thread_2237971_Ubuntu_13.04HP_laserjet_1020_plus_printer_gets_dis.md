### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-08-05
It will be difficult to give you any useful advice about this problem as 13.04 lost support some months ago, so installing any needed packages will be very time consuming and not really worth the bother.

You really should update your OS to a supported version, which now means 14.04, or if you prefer, you could go back to the older but still very good, supported and available 12.04.

However, if I remember correctly, that printer needs HPLIP and an added plugin, the latter of which I had some problems getting at one time, though I did eventually succeed, but I can not now remember what I had to do to get it working; it is too long ago.
