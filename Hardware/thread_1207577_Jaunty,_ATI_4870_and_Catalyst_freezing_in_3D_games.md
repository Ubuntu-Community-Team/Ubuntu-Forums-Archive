### Post by learners permit on 2009-07-20
I dunno zarko that looks like video memory errors or gpu overheating to me. Perhaps try monitoring your vid cards fan speed with your case open to see if the fan is slowing, failing to increase speed with increasing temp  or stopped. You might wanna see if theres a bios update for that vid cards bios as well. I run two 4850's in crossfire with 9.6 drivers with no issues at all on Jaunty. BTW here is a vid card bios database you can check for bios updates for your card [http://www.techpowerup.com/vgabios/](http://www.techpowerup.com/vgabios/) .
