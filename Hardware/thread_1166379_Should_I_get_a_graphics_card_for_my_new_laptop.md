### Post by Dark_Stang on 2009-05-21
ATI drivers are (at this point) the definition of "the suck." I'd recommend nVidia if at all possible. I'd recommend getting the dedicated card if it isn't too much of a price different. I bought my current laptop about 2 years ago. I decided against getting a dedicated card because it was a pretty big step in price back then. I picked up a laptop with an integrated nVidia 7150m and it does surprisingly well for an integrated card. However I have been dissapointed by pretty much anything that reequires GL because it just craps out. So again, I'd say go for the dedicated card.
