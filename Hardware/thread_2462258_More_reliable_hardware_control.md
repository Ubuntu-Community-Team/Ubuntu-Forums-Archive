### Post by CatKiller on 2021-05-17
Just so you know, we aren't Ubuntu developers here. We're just Ubuntu users, like you, trying to help each other.

From your description of the limitations of the volume control widget on the panel, it sounds like you're using Gnome. Their tools are very limited in functionality. They used to be better, but they took a bunch of functionality out. KDE gives you access to a proper configuration tool right from the volume control, and gives the user much more control in general.

Also, PulseAudio will, some time in the future, be replaced by PipeWire. That will do video as well as audio, and should have lower latency than PulseAudio like JACK does.
