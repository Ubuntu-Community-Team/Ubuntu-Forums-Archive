### Post by cascade9 on 2011-01-06
AM3 will NOT support DDR1. Ever. 

Technically, it shouldnt even support DDR2. DDR3 only (well, from what we know anyway, the AM3 datasheet has not been made public AFAIK) though you will find 'AM3' motherobards with DDR2 slots. 

If you cant afford the RAM, dont buy! By the time that you get the money to get RAM, parts prices will probably have fallen further. Provided that the AU$ doesnt drop badly against teh US$ anyway (very unlikely)
