### Post by grahammechanical on 2020-11-22
Is the new monitor working correctly as a standard desktop monitor?

As a general rule, the newest hardware needs the newest version of Linux. Ubuntu 16.04 will reach end of life 30 April 2021. Your employer should be thinking of upgrading to Ubuntu 18.04 which has support until the end of April 2023. Linux developers are continually updating the Linux kernel including hardware drivers. The latest releases of Ubuntu include the latest Linux kernels and the Hardware Enablement Stack (drivers).

You may be in a situation where you need the latest Ubuntu with the latest Linux kernel and hardware enablement stack to get the best results out of that monitor.
