### Post by gordintoronto on 2011-06-22
Six hours on an i5 is a tall order. Do you compute at the beach? Any place which gets cleaned has power outlets. (I once spent three hours writing in O'Hare, while plugged in.)

I'm very happy with my HP G62, but the battery is empty in less than two hours. The biggest hassle during installation was that HP had allocated all four primary partitions, and I wanted to dual-boot. A month after I set it up, I found a program in Linux (sopcast) which removed the need to dual boot...
