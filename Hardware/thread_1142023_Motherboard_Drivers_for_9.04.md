### Post by barisurum on 2009-04-28
> Nvidia is better than ATI as far as drivers are concerned

I suggest reading reviews in the forum about the latest fglrx driver from ati (9.4) if you plan to build a system with newest ati cards (hdxxxx). As far as I've read people are quite happy with it. Also ati is now a step ahead of nvidia concerning open source driver support. All the legacy cards of ati (x1000 series and older) are supported with open source drivers now and ati's open source policy is more promising. The support gets better and better: [Have a look here]("http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=amd_open_jaunty&num=1")
