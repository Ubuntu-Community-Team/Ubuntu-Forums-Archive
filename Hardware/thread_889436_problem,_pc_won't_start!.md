### Post by Submatrix on 2008-08-14
When you put your CPU into your brothers PC, did you set it up in the BIOS? It should do this automatically but sometimes it doesn't and this could be causing it to crash. If this sorts it on your brother's PC we've narrowed the problem down to your mobo.

Your PC beeping when you use your brother's CPU is a beep code telling you that the BIOS (probably) cant initialise the display for whatever reason. It's probably worth looking up the specific beep code you get in your BIOS manual (you can get it off the phoenix website) to see exactly what's wrong.

If you can't make your CPU work on any PC, and no CPU will work in yours, it is indeed possible that both your mobo and CPU are buggered :(
