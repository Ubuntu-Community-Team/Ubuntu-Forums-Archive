### Post by PrimaryMaster on 2010-03-03
Hi,

After I installed 9.10 I figured that the mic wasn't working. Not with the sound recorder, not with Skype either. I thought that I'd have to go thru a lot of stuff configuring files etc. but the solution was much, much easier...

All you have to do is right click on the speaker icon and choose Sound Preferences, go to Input tab and choose Mic 2 from the connector drop-down menu. 

Voila! Mic now works!!!

Leave a message if this helps you and I'll feel happy that I've helped someone in Linux environment for the very first time! :D
