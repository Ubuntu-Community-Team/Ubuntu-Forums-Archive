### Post by cascade9 on 2010-12-21
> **rfbennett said:**
> What graphics cards for my system are supported by Ubuntu?

As far as the only names worth going to these days (ATI + nVidia), pretty much everythign works with the open source driver. 

If you want to use the ATI/nVidia drivers, then all the PCIe nVidia cards work, and ATI cards from HD2XXX onward. 

Unless your a gamer, or looking for the absolute cheapest card you can find (which would probably be a nVidia 8400GS) then the GT210/GT220 would be my choice.
