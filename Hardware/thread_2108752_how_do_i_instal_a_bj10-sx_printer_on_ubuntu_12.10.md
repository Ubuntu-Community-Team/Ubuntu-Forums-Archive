### Post by kovu on 2013-02-18
today i found out how to install my bj 10 sx printer here is the link i used [http://myotragusbalearicus.wordpress.com/2012/01/17/install-a-network-printer-on-ubuntu-11-10-64-bits-via-cups/](http://myotragusbalearicus.wordpress.com/2012/01/17/install-a-network-printer-on-ubuntu-11-10-64-bits-via-cups/) 

here are the steps i took :

1. [http://localhost:631/](http://localhost:631/) 

2. CUPS for Administrators-->[Adding Printers and Classes]("http://localhost:631/admin") 

3. printers--->add printer

4. a dialog box pops up to input your username and password

5. fill out the printer info then add the ppd file 

6. voila installed and ready to go
