### Post by sgosnell on 2009-05-25
The battery life is going to vary with the screen brightness, CPU speed, battery condition, whether the wifi is on, and lots of other things.  I don't think Ubuntu is going to be much different from Xandros, maybe a little better, but it depends on your settings.

I find that I can type faster and easier on my 900 than I can on a full-sized keyboard.  It takes a day or so to get used to the configuration, but after practice I find that I don't have to move my fingers as much nor as far, and that makes for faster typing.  I have somewhat larger than average hands, but I don't find the keyboard to be a problem at all.
