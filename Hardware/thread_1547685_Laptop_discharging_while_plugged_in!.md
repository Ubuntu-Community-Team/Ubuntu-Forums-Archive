### Post by gallifrey on 2010-08-07
It may not be the adapter itself, but the connection within the laptop. 
I had a similar problem, where adjusting the cable would fix it. But it got to a point where I would have to do so constantly. 

Then I bought a universal adapter, which suffered the same problem. It turns out that during an accident I had previously where I tripped over the power cable, and tore it out of the laptop jack, I also damaged the jack connection itself. 

If the jack connection is okay, it may be the a/c converter which connects the jack to the battery.
