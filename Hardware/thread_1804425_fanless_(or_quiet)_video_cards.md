### Post by discolemur on 2011-10-04
In case anyone else is looking for a fanless option, I bought

*Sparkle GeForce 8400GS 512 MB DDR3 PCI with Native HDMI Graphics Card SFPC84GS512U2LP*

earlier this year and had some compatibility issues, so I returned it.  A week or two after the original post, I decided to give

*ASUS GeForce 210 1GB 64-bit DDR3 PCI Express 2.0 x16 Low Profile Ready Video Card, EN210 SILENT/DI/1GD3/V2(LP)*

a try, and it has been working great under Lucid and Natty.
