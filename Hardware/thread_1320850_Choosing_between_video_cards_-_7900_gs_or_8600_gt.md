### Post by Dark_Stang on 2009-11-09
Those would be pretty close, but I'd probably stick with the 8600 since it's newer. In nVidia's model numbers the 2nd number usually indicates how good the card is and the first number indicates how new it is. So a 7900 would have been one of the best cards in the 7000 series. An 8800 would beat the 8600 and the 7900 though, since it is newer. And a 9800 would kill them both.
