### Post by kurt18947 on 2012-03-06
I can't help with the scan direction, but Brother makes large format MFDs  that are Linux friendly and affordable.  I have an MFC-6490CW which works quite well although I needed to do an undocumented tweak to get the scanner to work in 12.04.  Brother will release an 'official' fix once 12.04 is released.   For those interested, my tweak was to add relevant users to the 'lp' group and restart.

The MFC-6490CW is no longer produced but there are successors.  Look down this page a bit more than half way:

[http://www.brother-usa.com/MFC/Color_Inkjet_Multifunction/](http://www.brother-usa.com/MFC/Color_Inkjet_Multifunction/)
