[LIST]

[*] I should be able to play any HD video without jittering

[/LIST]

[LIST=1]

[/LIST]

I recently built a system which includes this video card:
[http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814127412&cm_re=msi_9400_gt-_-14-127-412-_-Product](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814127412&cm_re=msi_9400_gt-_-14-127-412-_-Product)

It's based on the nVidia 9400 GT.  My requirements are the same as yours, and I'm very happy with the card.

I have not tried the HDMI port yet.  The card comes with a cable to take sound from the motherboard (in my case) for the HDMI port, which I have installed, so I expect to get sound with just an HDMI cable.

I have read that some video playback uses the GPU and some (flash in Firefox) does not.  I have not had any imperfect playback, and I watch a lot of videos online, downloaded, or in Miro (RSS aggregator).

Ubuntu does support connecting two video displays.

My computer is based on a Phenom II X2 550.
