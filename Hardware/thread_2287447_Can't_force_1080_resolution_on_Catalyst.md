### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2015-07-19
Do you really need the Catalyst driver? How does the free driver work on that card? In my experience the open AMD driver is on par or even better that Catalyst for most use cases (except for gaming) on many AMD cards and doesn't risk breaking on kernel updates. A lot of work has gone into the open AMD driver but probably not reflected in the stock Ubuntu driver in 14.04 (too old) Check out [https://launchpad.net/~oibaf/+archive/ubuntu/graphics-drivers](https://launchpad.net/~oibaf/+archive/ubuntu/graphics-drivers) for up to date open driver.
