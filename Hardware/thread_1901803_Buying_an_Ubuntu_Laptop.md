### Post by cornelis spronk on 2011-12-29
Do a search on laptop compatibility with linux, and you will get a good idea which might suit you needs and budget.

I have placed newer versions of Ubuntu on 3 different laptops that have developed corrupted Windows.  I have been very pleased with the results.

If you need to get a USB Wifi dongle, make sure it will work.  Again do a compatibility search. Some very inexpensive ones are excellent.  An excellent one is the TP-Link WN727N.

About support--If you get an older laptop, there is so much help available on forums, that manufacturer support may never be needed.
