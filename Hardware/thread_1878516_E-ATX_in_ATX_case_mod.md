Sometimes it's possible.  That case can certainly has enough wiggle room between the expansion slots and PSU, but I'd need to know what motherboard you're talking about to say if there's enough space in front of the drive bays.

Even if there isn't it should take 10 minutes with a dremel or sturdy tin snips to cut some tabs you can bend out of the way.
