### Post by oldfred on 2015-01-30
If you keep 10.04, do not get the 3TB. Gpt partitioning is required for drives over 2TiB. But gpt was not supported back with 10.04. The updates may have added support but I would not rely on that.

       GPT Advantages (older but still valid)  see post#2 by srs5694:
