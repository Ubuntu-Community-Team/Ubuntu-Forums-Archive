### Post by cascade9 on 2011-01-17
10.04 only has nvidia-current at version 195.xx, the GTS 450 needs driver 260.1912 or better. 

Since you manually installed the nvidia drivers, if there is a kernel update you will lose 'x' and have to muck around to get your desktop back. 

As for if you should return it....I'm not sure. If you do and you are a gamer, get a GTX460, its  a much better gaming card. If you dont game much, or only play fairly light games, get a GT220.

### Post by cascade9 on 2011-01-18
A video card isnt going to 'future-proof' your machine. As long as you are comparing the GTS450 to a decent, modern video card its not going to help your photo editing either. 

The only reason why the GTS450 hasnt got support with 10.04 or 10.10 is because the card was released after they were. 11.04 will have support 'out of the box'.
