### Post by kurt18947 on 2013-08-16
Unless it's changed recently,  Dell printers are rebadged Lexmark and Lexmark doesn't seem to have a great reputation for linux support in their SOHO product line.  My preference for printers under linux are HP or Brother because I've had positive experiences with those two.  You might consider going to any manufacturer's web site or use a search engine and look in the support section for linux drivers & support for the model you're considering.  If there's nothing, that may be indicative of their level of linux support.
