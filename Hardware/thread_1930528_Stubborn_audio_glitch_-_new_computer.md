### Post by Rahsennor on 2012-02-23
Hello all. :D

Using my new laptop - Samsung 305 series + Xubuntu 11.10 - for playing music and videos, I encounter a problem. There is an annoying digital-sounding squeal coming from the speakers at all times. Nothing I have tried has made any difference whatsoever, which includes:

[LIST]
[*]Using a different media player: Parole, mplayer, audacity - the sound also persists when there is no audio playing at all.

[*]Changing every volume-control and mute option I can find in xfce4-mixer and pavucontrol: the noise does not change volume with the audio.

[*]Pulseaudio: The noise is not interrupted by pulsesudio -k.

[*]Using a different output: Laptop speakers, headphone socket, USB soundcard and bluetooth A2DP all make the same noise.
[/LIST]

The first three suggest a hardware fault, but the last one seems to rule that out. :confused: The noise is hard to notice in normal operation - but on soft songs, quiet movie scenes, or with software volume turned down for headphones, it becomes a serious PITA. :x Anyone else had a similar problem? Any hints on where to look/what to google?

### Post by Rahsennor on 2012-02-24
Success! :popcorn:

I asked a technician, and he said it might be the power supply. Sure enough, I unplugged the AC adaptor and the noise vanished! The noise was in the power, not the data, so it affected anything *powered* by the laptop. Apparently my bluetooth reciever's powerpack has the same problem. :(

New problem: laptop battery only lasts 40 minutes... #-o
