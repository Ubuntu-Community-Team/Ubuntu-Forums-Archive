One thing that I've noted on my system is that the printer-scanner will sometimes disconnect from the router because out of the blue, for reasons unknown, it tries to use an invalid IP address. To fix this, I generally have to reboot my router three times (really!) and then reboot my printer. Try to reboot your router and then your printer to see what happens.
