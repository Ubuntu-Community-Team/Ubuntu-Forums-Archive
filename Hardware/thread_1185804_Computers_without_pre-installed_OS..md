### Post by Alan Bell on 2009-08-02
glad you like it! I just got fed up of hunting about to find computers I could buy without rewarding Microsoft for providing an operating system I don't want. Fair competitive markets don't work like that. So I figured loads of people would be in the same position as me so a catalogue of suppliers selling naked computers would be a handy thing to have around. Do let me know if you find any other suppliers selling naked computers. Some places will do a naked desktop as a special order, but under the counter deals don't count. It has to be an up front option on the price list.
Laptops are a worse situation than desktops at the moment. If the laptop you want doesn't have a naked option and you still decide to purchase then please do follow the "request a refund" procedure and keep records of what happens, some suppliers will refuse to honour the request which is outrageous. Anything you can do to raise the media profile of the situation (or just the site) would be appreciated.
