### Post by Bucky Ball on 2009-07-18
And what is the latest and greatest version? Be more specific. There are differences.

I suggest you plug in an ethernet hardwire cable and get your updates and you will probably be offered the restricted drivers for your wireless card then.

Also look in System->Administration->Synaptics package manager and search for and install:

ubuntu-restricted-extras
