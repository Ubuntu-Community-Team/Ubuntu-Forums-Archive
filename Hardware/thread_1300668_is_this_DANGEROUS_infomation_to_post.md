### Post by polax on 2009-10-25
Hello Paul,

I would say chances of someone hacking your machine just because he has your MAC address, or your configuration is not very high. My suggestion would be to 

a) Change the access password on your Wifi Access Point (I am sure your service provider would have given you instructions on how this can be done)
b) There are a number of tools that allow you to check whether your WiFi network has been compromised - "Google is your Friend".
c) Unless you have posted your root password (which I seriously doubt you would have), I don't think you need to worry too much about someone hacking into your system.
