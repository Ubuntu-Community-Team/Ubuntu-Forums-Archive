### Post by cascade9 on 2009-11-26
I checked the specs on that board, and it appears to only have PCI and 1xPCI-E slots. That rules out almost all the video cards out there (almost all are PCI-E now). It is possible to 'chop' a 16xPCI-E card down to 1xPCI-E, but thats probably only for those have are willing to risk killing a video card. 

If your interested, I can probably find a link to a thread where people talk about chopping odwn 16xPCI-E card. I wouldn't even suggest it but for the price difference between PCI and PCI-E.

Eg- a nVidia PCI 8400GS 512MB is $115 here, where a PCI-E version of the same card is more like $40.
