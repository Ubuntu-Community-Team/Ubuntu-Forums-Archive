### Post by oldfred on 2011-04-07
Hardware is more difficult to fix in my opinion.

I heard a pop on my system one night but it kept working. Next morning my wife said it would not even start. No power, no lights nothing.

I had a weak motherboard & power supply that I was looking for an excuse to upgrade anyway so I replaced those and still nothing. Put in new video card & it worked like a charm. On further review, I could see that the capacitors on the video card were not quite flat on top but slightly open. I suspect I heard one pop, but it took more than one to pop before system stopped working. 

You might check all the boards for any irregularities. Burnt spots, open capacitors, anything that looks wrong.
