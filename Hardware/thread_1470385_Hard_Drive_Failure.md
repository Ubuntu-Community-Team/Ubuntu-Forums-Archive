### Post by tgalati4 on 2010-05-02
Look for the pdf "200 ways to revive a hard drive"

First put it in a baggie and put it in the freezer for 1 hour.

Take it out and immediately plug it into your USB.  Make some space on another drive to receive files.

If the drive spins up and mounts, work quickly to grab your files.

You probably only have one shot.
