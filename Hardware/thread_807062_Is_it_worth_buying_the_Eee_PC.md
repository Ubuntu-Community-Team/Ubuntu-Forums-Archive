### Post by AlanR8 on 2008-05-30
Have had the 4 gig EeePc since March this year. 512 Meg of RAM and have got Kubuntu installed.

The EeePc is NOT my main laptop but I use it a lot. I watch a lot of content from the BBC I Player service on it and stream video from my home "server" to it. Same with music. I've also used it in a full blown business environment with good results.

One of the things I like about it is its size. Its convenient and light to carry around and the screen quality is excellent, as is the sound quality. One slight niggle, to answer an earlier post in the thread, Kubuntu does not pick up the WiFI by default. Indeed the latest kernel upgrade blew the driver out! Not a problem, and easy to fix.
