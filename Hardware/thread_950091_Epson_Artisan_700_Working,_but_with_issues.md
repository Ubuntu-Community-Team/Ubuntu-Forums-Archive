### Post by SqRt7744 on 2009-11-13
Kellogs, do not use the Stylus Photo RX700 as suggested, but select the Epson Stylus Photo TX700W, which is the same printer as marketed in Australia. For some odd reason it is the only variant listed. According to the openprinting database, PX700W (Europe), TX700W (AU), Artisan 700 (US) or EP-801A (JP) - all same printer.

I also used the RX700 driver initially and had poor colors until I switched to the correct driver.
