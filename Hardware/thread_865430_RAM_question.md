### Post by Dark_Stang on 2008-07-25
For the dv9200. I'm pretty sure it maxes out at 2gb. I've sold many HP laptops and I think the first ones that handled 4gb were the x600 series. Also, DDR2-667 is the best and fastest. You will notice no difference between 3gb and 4gb on a 32bit system.

As for the zv6129ea, I'd recommend scrapping the 2x256 and just doing 2x512's. Motherboards that use DDR (not ddr2) don't care to have their dimms with unmatched pairs of memory. So try to keep the memory matching in size and speed. DDR PC2700 would be fine. And as I said, I'd recommend 2x512's. If you're going to be doing large scale graphic design or CAD I'd suggest 2x1gb's.
