### Post by prshah on 2009-02-17
> **pczafer said:**
> 
after windows screen, a blue screen coming up so quickly, I cant even read than its restarting itself.

If this is Windows XP, press F8 _after_ the BIOS bootup sequence, but _before_ the Windows splash screen. This will bring you to a Windows XP startup menu. Choose the option "Disable automatic restart on failure"; now your computer will stop at the blue screen, and not reboot.
