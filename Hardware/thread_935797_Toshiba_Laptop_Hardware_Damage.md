### Post by lisati on 2008-10-02
I've been using Ubuntu (the "Feisty" flavour, version 7.04) for over a year now on a Toshiba laptop. Here are some links to threads on a couple of issues I've faced and (for the most part) found solutions for:

USB keyboard and mouse not working after a while: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=854684](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=854684)

Phantom audio CD on desktop (more of an annoyance than anything)/strange sounds from within the laptop: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=935552](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=935552)

Trouble with updating AVG free (not a hardware specific issue): [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=889552](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=889552)

I've also noticed that my battery life has shortened somewhat, but I believe that probably has more to running off A/C a lot than specifically using Ubuntu.

Feel free to ask questions.

EDIT: You can set things up for a "dual boot" installation, where you can keep Windows (or whatever other OS you choose) on your system, and choose which OS to run when you boot your system.
