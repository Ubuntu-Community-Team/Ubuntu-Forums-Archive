### Post by serverfarm on 2011-06-05
Transfer speeds will be pretty similar.  From experience I would go Internal if you are always going to be accessing the files, but external if you are going to be transferring fewer, but bigger files (e.g. backups).  

Here is just some facts about the two interfaces:
SATAII: 3.0 Gbps
USB 3.0: 5.0-3.2 Gbps

Most hard drives cannot transfer data that fast, so either works, but what is more important is the quality of the actual drive.
