### Post by speedkreature on 2009-07-04
If you don't need (and don't think you'll need) fax capability, the Zoom 3095 has worked for me.
I've also used the Rosewill RNX-56USB and it works like a charm--Jaunty was ready to roll with this modem a few seconds after I plugged it in and connected a phone line.  In fact, I've deployed a few of the latter at work where needed.  It seems to be a really good sub-$25 piece of hardware.

Theres my two cents.
