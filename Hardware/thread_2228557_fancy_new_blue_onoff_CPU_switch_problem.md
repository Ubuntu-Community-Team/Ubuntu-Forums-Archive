### Post by LastDino on 2014-06-08
On my older CPU box, on/off from hard button on CPU malfunctioned, then I changed that button to ''I'' from keyboard or ''double-left mouse click'' in my BIOS. Is that option not available to you? Of course, I booted by putting tester on pins where ''boot/startup'' pin from mobo to button on box is connected.
That ''I'' was in red colour as it was an i-ball product, quite conveniently.

Not sure what you're looking for other than this.

### Post by HC48 on 2014-06-27
After much research I took the solution of Occam's razor, ie the simplest. I unplugged the CPU, switched off then on via the switch on the back. Plugged in again to a different socket on my adaptor and bingo. The computer powered up first time!
