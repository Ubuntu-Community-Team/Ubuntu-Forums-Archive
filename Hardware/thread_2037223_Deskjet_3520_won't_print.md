### Post by scottbomb on 2012-08-04
I have installed the drivers from HP, added the printer and all. The computer seems to think it's printing and reports no errors but the printer doesn't respond.

* SOLVED * I re-installed the drivers from the HP website. During the installation process, it asks about "Duplex Mode" as an option. I haven't read up on that yet but this time I chose no instead of yes. Now it prints perfectly! 

Scanning works perfectly as well. Couldn't be happier with this printer.
