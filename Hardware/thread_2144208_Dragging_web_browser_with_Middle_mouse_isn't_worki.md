### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-05-11
Open "about:config" in a new tab in Firefox.

Search for middlemouse

You're welcome. Oh, and welcome to the forums.

Understand that some laptops have a strange middle button, like this Acer Extensa.  It's actually a mini D-pad that scrolls up and down and side to side.  You can sometimes fake the middle button by pushing the left and right buttons at the same time.  Not convenient, but a work around.
