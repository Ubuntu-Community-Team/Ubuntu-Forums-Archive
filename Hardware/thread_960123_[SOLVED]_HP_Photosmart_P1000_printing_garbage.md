### Post by exmatelot853 on 2008-10-27
Hi Everyone,

I hope this helps other users who may have similar problems.

I have a fresh install of Ubuntu 8.04 (Hardy Heron) on a HP Slimline S3015uk and a HP Photosmart P1000 printer connected on USB, the printer installed first time and appeared ok as it printed a test page correctly but when printing from Firefox or Openoffice all I got was garbage characters.

When I checked the printer settings in System, Administration, Printing I noticed that the make and model was HP Photosmart P1000 Foomatic/cdj1600.

To change this click on the change button and select the first option which is HP Photosmart P1000 Foomatic/hpijs [en] (recommended), click forward, click use the new PPD as is radio button, click forward and click apply.

Your make and model will now be updated and all should be well.
