### Post by cariboo on 2009-06-06
The answer to both of your questions is yes. Your laptop will run either 32-bit or 64-bit ubuntu, the big question is whether your graphics and wireless devices are supported.

I would suggest downloading the LiveCD from [here]("http://releases.ubuntu.com/9.04/"), or if you have a slow internet connection you can request a cd-rom from [here]("http://shipit.ubuntu.com/").

Try it first before installing.

Note: Shipit only ships the 32-bit version.
