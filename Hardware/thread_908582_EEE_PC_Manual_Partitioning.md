### Post by C.S.Cameron on 2008-09-02
My wife and daughter just move everything downloaded to their EEE to their own FAT32 formatted thumb drives.
I understand you can also keep your home folders on the drive and do this automatically.
I find it handy to have a bit of fat on the thumb drive so I can copy things from a windows computer.
Yes thumb drives seem to die a sudden death sometimes, not like a HDD where a good whack can get a few last spins.
Transcend has some flash recovery tools on their support site:
[http://www.transcendusa.com/Support/DLCenter/index.asp](http://www.transcendusa.com/Support/DLCenter/index.asp)
Did not work for me though with a V10.
