### Post by butter[s] on 2009-03-25
FIXED!!!
I read this from the bugfix's in the ubuntu wiki so .... 
The wiki said that this is a common bug.  The wiki suggests to use 2 USB thumb drives and to plug the first one in (you will get no response) then you plug the second one in to another port and it WILL mount automatically.  
I only have one USB drive so I tried plugging it in to a USB port on my machine... wont mount... so... unplug the drive and plug it in to a DIFFERENT USB PORT and it will mount.
I can't guarantee this will work for everyone but it did for me and I am happy as a pig in .... ;)
