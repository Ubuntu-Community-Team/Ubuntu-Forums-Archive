### Post by 2F4U on 2011-10-15
Maybe the SATA cable is broken or the connector on the motherboard, or even the hard disk. If it recognizes the Windows HDD I would take that out of the computer and connect the Ubuntu HDD instead. Use the same cable and plug it into the same connector that the Windows HDD uses. If it works, then there is something wrong with the cable and/or the motherboard connector.
