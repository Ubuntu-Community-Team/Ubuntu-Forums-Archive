### Post by cascade9 on 2009-12-05
Who ever said 'lots of games only use dualcores' is semi-right. Lots of older games dont, lots of newer games do. See this post I made on a different thread about just this subject-

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=8424769#post8424769](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=8424769#post8424769)

As far as I know, later versions of ubuntu like quads, just fine. There will be programs that dont use multithreaded that well, but even then I would expect quite some deal of performance increase. 2MB cache for the e5200, vs 12MB for the q9550 will make quite a difference.
