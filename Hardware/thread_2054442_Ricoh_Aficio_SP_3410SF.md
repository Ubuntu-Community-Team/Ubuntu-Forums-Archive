### Post by megahelten on 2013-06-12
Hallo,
I have had the same problem with Ricoh Aficio 3410DN.
Before i used the 8100dn driver, but after upgrading to 12.04 (i think) it stopped working.
Now i have found something that worked.
I have installed CUPS gutenprint for gimp ( standard ubuntu repo )
and chosen the generic driver : Generic PCL 6/PCL XL Printer - CUPS+Gutenprint v5.2.9
It might even work without the guten print, but i didnt try this driver until after having installed gutenprint.. 
so wohoo !
