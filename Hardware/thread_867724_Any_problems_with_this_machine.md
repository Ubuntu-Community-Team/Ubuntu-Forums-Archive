### Post by evets25 on 2008-07-23
Generally speaking, you'll never have driver-type problems with the CPU, ram, HDD, dvd, and CD drives. Things you do need to worry about are weird integrated devices on the mobo, wireless, video (decide which has a better driver: ATI or nvidia?), sound card, and "other weird sh*t" (as my friend used to say) like peripheral devices or custom built snes controller-to-com-port converters.
