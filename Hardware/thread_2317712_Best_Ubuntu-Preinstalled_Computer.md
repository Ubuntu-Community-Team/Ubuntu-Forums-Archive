### Post by him610 on 2016-03-18
There is no simple answer as to best Ubuntu-preinstalled computer. Those of us that may have one only have one so there is nothing to compare it with. Not only is there no way to compare, but if one of us may possibly have one then it may be a few years old. The best anyone can do for you is possibly recommend a particular brand. You need to do your own market research, after all, it is your money.

I have been very satisfied with the laptop I purchased from System76 about 2-1/2 years ago. 
Another brand I considered was ZaReason. I believe Dell recently came out with a developer model pre-loaded with Ubuntu.
There maybe other brands that I am unaware of.

Shopping and comparing the specifications of computers is one of the great joys in life.
Good luck, and have fun.
