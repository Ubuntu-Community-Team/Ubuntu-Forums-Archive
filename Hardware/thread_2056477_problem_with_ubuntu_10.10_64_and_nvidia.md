### Post by SeijiSensei on 2012-09-11
Ubuntu 10.10 (Maverick Meerkat) reached its "end-of-life" for support on April 10th of this year.  Trying to use more recent software on 10.10 may be difficult.  I strongly suggest upgrading to [12.04 LTS]("http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/precise/release/") which will be supported until April of 2017.

In general, unless you like to tinker, it's best to avoid any distributions not marked as "LTS" for "long-term support."  The version just before 10.10, 10.04 was also an LTS release; it is still being supported though April of next year.
