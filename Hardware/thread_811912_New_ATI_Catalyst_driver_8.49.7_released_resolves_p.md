### Post by sigve on 2008-05-29
I just wanted to post my positive experiences with the latest proprietary driver for ATI-cards (21.05.2008): [ATI Catalyst driver 8.49.7]("http://ati.amd.com/support/drivers/linux/linux-radeon.html")

On my computer (Fujitsu Siemens Amilo with ATI Radeon Mobility HD 2300) I've had problems with compiz and running dual-head displays ever since upgrading to Hardy. This new driver however solved all the problems!

I can now get all of the compiz effects at full speed and toggle dual head from the Catalyst control center! I had to uninstall xgl to make it work at full speed though.

Phoronix has a [thorough article]("http://www.phoronix.com/vr.php?view=12359") on the new driver with detailed list of the improvements.

Hope it works for those of you ATI-users who've expreienced similar problems with compiz and dual-head displays!

Ubuntu rocks!!!!
