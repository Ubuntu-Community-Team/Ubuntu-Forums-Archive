### Post by weatherman2 on 2016-06-11
It may support 16GB of RAM.  Manufacturer specs are not always correct.  8GB may be the maximum amount of RAM they tested with that machine when it was first released.

My Acer netbook also supposedly supports only 8GB of RAM, but I have been using 16GB in it for years without issue.

I highly doubt you would hurt your laptop by trying it.  At worst, 16GB of RAM may not work at all and you will have to return it for a refund, perhaps for a restocking fee or whatever.

I would probably make sure you have the latest available BIOS installed from Dell - which may mean using Windows to install it.
