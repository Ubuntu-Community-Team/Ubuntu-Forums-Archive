### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2015-06-21
Do you have proprietary nvidia drivers installed? If not, you may want to look here for some helpful tips: [http://nouveau.freedesktop.org/wiki/Optimus/](http://nouveau.freedesktop.org/wiki/Optimus/)
In fact, if you don't need the nvidia card (like for gaming, 3D design, or using the HDMI ouput), you're better off disabling it completely by using bbswitch as the wiki suggests.
