### Post by lswb on 2009-07-23
Is there a problem using the intel wireless you mentioned? My older laptop has an intel ipw2200 mini-pci adapter and it works fine. There are linux drivers for most if not all the intel adapters, though I've seen some posts of problems with certain systems/combinations.

I can't comment on how well the adapter you've linked to works, but it sure looks like there would be a bunch of stuff hanging out of that card slot. Personally I would get an expresscard wifi adapter or a usb adapter. Some of the usb wifi adapters are very inexpensive from ebay, newegg, or amazon. Just do your homework and get one with a well supported chipset.
