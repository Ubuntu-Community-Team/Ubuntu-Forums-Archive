### Post by metalf8801 on 2010-08-05
I most likely don't need to write this but heres a really simple how to creating a panel icon that will turn off your computers display 

Right click on a panel 
click on Add to panel 
select "Custom Application Launcher" then click Add or just double click on "Custom Application Launcher" 
Type: Application in Terminal 
Name: Turnoff Display 
Command: xset dpms force off
Comment: (put whatever you want here I just wrote: turns on the display)

if you want to use the icon below which I believe was made by [Old Pink]("http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=161322")
first save the image by right clicking on it and then click on "Save Image As..." make sure you save it somewhere you will be able to find it 

back in the "Custom Application Launcher" click on the default icon then move to the folder you saved the off.png then select the off.png icon and click open 
then click close and your done
