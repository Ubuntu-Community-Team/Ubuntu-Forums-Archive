### Post by K.J. on 2013-08-20
I would go for the dell xps 13 developer edition. It's a very nice laptop. If you need more ports, you can get a USB 3.0 port replicator.  If you want multiple monitors, unfortunately the HD4000 does not support daisy chaining display port. However, if you are willing to drop $200 on a Matrox DualHead2Go, you can do two monitors with resolutions up to 1920x1200 each on that chip.

Personally, as much as I can't wait to get my hands on that laptop, I'm trying to resist until you can get one with a Haswell chip.
