### Post by clueless on 2008-08-24
I have Logitech Cordless Desktop EX110 and works great with linux. On Ubuntu you can select the specific model from keyboard properties to have support of the extra keys. 
As to why yours stopped working there are 3 probabilities:

1) battery exhausted. And since you changed the batteries, we could exclude this one

2) synchronization not done correctly. To sync my keyboard and mouse I have to do the following procedure:
a) press connect button on base
b) press connect button on mouse
c) wait 20 seconds
d) press connect button again on base
e) press connect button on keyboard

3) the keyboard could have been damaged, or batteries not inserted properly... 

You could try synchronizing it again and check batteries, maybe even try and see if it works on another computer.
