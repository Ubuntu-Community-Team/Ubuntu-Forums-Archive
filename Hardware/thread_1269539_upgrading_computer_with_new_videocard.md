### Post by oldfred on 2009-09-18
I always read Tom's and like their chart at the end that compares performance.
[http://www.tomshardware.com/reviews/best-graphics-card,2404.html](http://www.tomshardware.com/reviews/best-graphics-card,2404.html)
Currently it seems that Nvidia works better. I have only had minor issues converting from a 7600gts to a 9600gt recently.
What size is your power supply? That may limit what you can install. A few years ago my son wanted more games and I put in a new video card and a month or two later the system died. Too much power draw wiped out motherboard and/or video card. But it became a good excuse to build my new dual boot system.
