### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-05-21
Does the manufacturer of that DVB card provide a Linux driver? No? I did not think so and that is the problem. There might not be any drivers. Video capture and DVB card are a special category of getting hardware to work in Linux. This is the site that can help the most. But speaking from my own experience, I would not get my hopes up.

[http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Main_Page](http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Main_Page)

There is a section of this site set aside for Mythbuntu which is a version of Ubuntu with Myth TV built on top of it. Some of the users of that section might have advice although you are not specifically asking about getting Mythbuntu working. It is a related matter.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=301](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=301)
