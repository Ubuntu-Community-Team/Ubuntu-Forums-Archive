### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2014-04-27
If you're getting an i7, it will probably have HD4600 graphics, which should be good for light gaming. The good news is that you don't really pay too much extra for an onboard GPU nowadays since most CPU's have them.

So what I suggest most people do is try the onboard graphics, and if they don't cut it, then throw in an external GPU.
