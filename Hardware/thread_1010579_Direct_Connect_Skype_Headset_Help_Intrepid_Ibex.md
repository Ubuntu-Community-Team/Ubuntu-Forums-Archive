I'm using Kubuntu 8.10 so I'm not I can truly help you with your desktop environment.

What I would do in your case is check the settings.
I know that when you check your Mixer(the audio management system where you can slide volume up and down) something called a "mic" should pop up. 

Sometimes it's also application specific and Teamspeak I believe should have an option to tweak where the microphone input is. 

The best way to check whether your microphone is really working or not is to install the software "audacity" and press record(and speak, ofcourse).
