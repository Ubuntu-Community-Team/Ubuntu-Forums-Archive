### Post by Sam Lars on 2008-05-30
Your laptop probably has a VGA out connector, and that's what you're seeing.  Try just changing the VESA to intel instead of both, leave the one that says "none" alone.
Also, check your /var/log/Xorg.0.log to see what driver you're using.  For instance, mine has:
```
(==) intel(0): Depth 24, (==) framebuffer bpp 32
```
Starting about halfway down, most lines begin with the driver you're using.  See if it says vesa or intel.
If you're using Compiz effects, it isn't vesa.
