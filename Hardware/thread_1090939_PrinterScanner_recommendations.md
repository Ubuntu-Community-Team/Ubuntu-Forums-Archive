### Post by ad_267 on 2009-03-14
I'm surprised you're having problems with a HP, they're usually the best supported out of any brand on Linux. If you don't want an HP then an Epson is probably the next best supported.

I've only had experience with an HP and a Canon printer myself. The drivers for the Canon were nonexistent and I had to make do with drivers for another model that worked only for very basic printing. The HP was much better supported and worked well. I haven't used a scanner but again I've heard the HPs work well.
