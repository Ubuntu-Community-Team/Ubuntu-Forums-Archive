...you have to activate the nvidia driver, sudo dpkg-reconfigure -phigh xserver-xorg

then, sudo nvidia-xconfig

then reboot...now you can adjust your resolution thru the nvidia control setting...system>administration>nvidia x server settings> xserver display configuration

### Post by byStanderone on 2010-05-15
this is my screen section in xorg.conf, perhaps you can add the lines for the resolutions that you need:

Section "Screen"
    Identifier     "Screen0"
    Device         "Device0"
    Monitor        "Monitor0"
    DefaultDepth    24
    Option         "TwinView" "0"
    Option         "TwinViewXineramaInfoOrder" "CRT-0"
    Option         "metamodes" "1360x768 +0+0; 800x600 +0+0; 640x480 +0+0"
    Option         "AddARGBGLXVisuals" "True"
    SubSection     "Display"
        Depth       24
    EndSubSection
