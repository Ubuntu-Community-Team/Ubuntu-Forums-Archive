### Post by avilella on 2010-01-11
Hi Betelgeuse,

As far as I know, there are successful reports of people using the nvidia drivers in 9.10 with a bit of fiddling around, and people is already using 10.04 on it, and it seems to be quite stable.

There are already ~40 people using Linux on this Asus UL30/UL80 laptop series, and the projection of the numbers goes to ~200 Linux users in the next ~10 months. It will then be the most successful ultrathin Linux laptop together with the Sony Vaio Z-series, which is substantially pricier, and the Acer Timeline 3810T series, which seems to work fine apart from the suspend to ram issue.
