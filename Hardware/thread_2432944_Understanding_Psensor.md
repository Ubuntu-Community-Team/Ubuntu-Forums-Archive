### Post by crip720 on 2019-12-11
It is just reading the different sensors available on your computer.  Some sensors might be read two different ways.  Do you have a dual core or four core cpu?  Usually just  have one sensor reading for core 0 or 1.  Sometimes I have found that one sensor reading will be out of wack, like a SSD reading 99. Best to use sensor readings to keep check on temps and only worry if one or more start rising high.
