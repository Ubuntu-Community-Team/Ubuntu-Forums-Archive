### Post by blueyedguy on 2008-11-02
Hey guys,

pretty much a noob here but I was having problems getting my Intel 2200BG card to recognise any Wi-fi at all

Tried lots of troubleshooting steps I could find online but the only step that worked that I foubd was

When booting up your computer press F2 and then that will open the BIOS and there should be a Wireless Setting and my setting for this was disabled, so I set it to last state and saved changes, rebooted computer..

TADAH! it worke

I think the reason the Wi-fi didnt work to begin with is because I have a wi-fi button you need to press on my laptop to activate it but it didnt work when it was pressed but by Pressing F2 and then putting the Wireless to "last state" and saved the changes , that worked :D woop!
