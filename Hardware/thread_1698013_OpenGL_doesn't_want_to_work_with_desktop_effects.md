I should check X configuration. There is no Xorg configuration file, everything is left for kernel and radeon to decide. Checking hardware compatibility is disabled.

I know that OpenGL works on this laptop (glxinfo shows the appropriate hardware, glxgears works and show the animation of turning gears).

I don't know where to look for error messages, as the one being displayed are non-informative.
