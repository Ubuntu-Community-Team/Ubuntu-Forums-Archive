### Post by samchezobonto on 2010-07-27
Hi there,

 My webcam's mic wasn't functioning out of the box. I searched the forum but didn't find an answer.

 After a bit of playing around, I got it to work and would like to share this with others who may have a similar issue.

 I got my webcam mic to finally work by doing the following (specs to follow):

1. Go to "System" -> "Sound"
2. In the "Sound Preferences" dialog box, select the "Input" tab
3. I had 2 options listed in the "Choose a device for sound input:" box (Internal Audio Stereo and 0808 Analog Mono). The first was selected.
4. I selected the 2nd one (which corresponds to my webcam)

Et voilà, my voice was finally heard!

Specs:
- Ubuntu 10.04 running on Dell Inspiron 700m
- Logitech Webcam C500

Cheers!
