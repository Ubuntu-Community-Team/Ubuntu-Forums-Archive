### Post by bvpainter on 2010-12-26
Needing a new all in one printer copier, I consulted various articles and came to the conclusion that  HP models were the best to go with Linux. So I got me a new HP Deskjet 3050. Plugged it in and lo and behold no printer was recognised by my computer. Tried another USB socket and it was, however what I ever I did including downloading various software etc, I could not get it to print let al one scan. After a week of trying I gave up and returned it to the supplier.

This time I decided to try an Epson Stylus SX515W. I downloaded the printer drivers from Avasys as suggested on the Epson site, plugged it in and much to my suprise and delight it automatically installed it self, both for printing and scanning.

The moral of this story seems to be that Epson give the best support for Ubuntu and any other Linux system.:popcorn:
