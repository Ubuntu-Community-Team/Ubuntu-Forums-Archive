### Post by shuamu on 2010-09-18
Mouse clicks are enabled by default on install.

Go to:

System > Mouse > Touchpad 

and deselect "Enable Mouse Clicks with Touchpad."

Easy as that. The "disable mouse while typing" function never really seems to work how you want it to. I say just scrap Touch-Click completely. It's an awfully annoying thing.
