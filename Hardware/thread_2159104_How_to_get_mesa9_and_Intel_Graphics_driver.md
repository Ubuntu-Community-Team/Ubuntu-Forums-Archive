### Post by elpidiovaldez5 on 2013-07-16
Finally I have answered my own question.  Google 'Distrowatch Lubuntu' to find a site that details the versions of hundreds of tracked packages.  In the case of mesa, versions are:
9.1.3(Saucy)    9.1.1(Raring)    9.0(Quantal)     8.0.2(Precise)

So if I upgrade to Lubuntu 13.04 I will automatically get Mesa9.1.1, SiftGPU will work perfectly and I will live happily ever after.
