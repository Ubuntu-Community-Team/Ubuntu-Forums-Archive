### Post by AirIntake on 2009-06-05
CCC probably is underscanning or overscanning (compressing or stretching) the image. Go into CCC and there should be a slider you can adjust. I think it's under HDTV settings or something. I had this happen with my 4850. Are there black bars around the image or is part of the image cut off? That's one way to tell. It also might be the TV trying to compensate for an overscan or underscan when it's not necessary.
