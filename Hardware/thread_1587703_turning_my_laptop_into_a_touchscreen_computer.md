### Post by SaintDanBert on 2010-10-04
Step one involves finding the hardware parts for your touch-screen and digitizer.

Step two involves mounting these parts in ways that make sense for your application. This mechanical engineering seems a very big deal.

If you want to do tablet things using your laptop computer, consider a Wacom Bamboo(tm) or Intuos(tm) device. The Linux-Wacom project offers software for touch and tablet if you find suitable Wacom(tm) hardware.
