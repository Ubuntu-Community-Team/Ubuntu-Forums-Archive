As long as you are not planning to play any intense games, practically any current nvidia card will be trouble-free and cheap.

I was using an 256M nVidia 7300GT (or GS?) for a long time with great satisfaction, until I started playing demanding games such as Mass Effect. 

Note that the 7300GT (GS?) is about the lowest and cheapest you can get. Practically every other graphics card, even with lower model numbers, are faster than this.
