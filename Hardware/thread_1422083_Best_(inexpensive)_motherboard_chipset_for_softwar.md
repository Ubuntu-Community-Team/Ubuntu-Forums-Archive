### Post by cascade9 on 2010-03-05
I'm assuming you are in the US. *the following is opinion, not a 'hard fact'*

You WANT onboard video? Gah. Intel onboard video is pretty lame IMO, and nvidia with intel is prettty rare. That leaves AMD- ATI onboard is something I avoid, and while nVidia onboard video is more common with AMD, it tend to be on the 'entry level' boards. You might not get exactly what you want re. SATA ports. 

I'd consider buying a motherboard with no onbaord video, a cheap GF 8400 which does VDPAU is only about $30 or so.
