### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-03-10
You shouldn't need to remove any ribbons. It's just 2 screws on the plastic cover, one end will lift up when you unscrew the screws, then you slide it in the direction of where the screws are and it will lift right off.

Unscrew the 1 screw that is holding the drive in and using the plastic strip to hold it with one hand and the other hand where the screw was, slide it in the same direction about 1/2" and lift it out.

Unscrew the 4 screws holding the drives case to the drive and slide the drive out, it should be obvious which way.

Reverse the process to install the new drive.

Yours may be slightly different but that's how it is on mine.
