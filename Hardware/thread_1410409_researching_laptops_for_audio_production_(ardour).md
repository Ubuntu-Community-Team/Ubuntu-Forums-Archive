### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2010-02-19
It depends how much you want to do. You will still be able to record at low latency with fairly standard hardware thanks to the rt-kernel and jack. I used a Dell inspiron (1.6ghz duo, 1GB RAM) for a while which was good enough to record and mix on.
If you are using Ardour then I assume you want to record live instruments? If thats the case you will want something with a firewire port as firewire is best for external soundcards. Having said that USB still has some very good options for audio work:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1409828](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1409828)
