### Post by uutnubu on 2009-06-25
I have an HP OfficeJet J6480 that works great the HPLIP driver.  Prints, scans copies and will fax too.  

If you don't like that model look at this site [http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html) to get info on other HP models.
