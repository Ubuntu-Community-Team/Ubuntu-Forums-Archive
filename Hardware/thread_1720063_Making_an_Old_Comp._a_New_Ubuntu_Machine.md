### Post by dandnsmith on 2011-04-03
There is on-board graphics supplied - which I would have expected the Ubuntu to detect and use - and this may well not be fast enough for what you want.
If you plan to add a graphics card, the options available are AGP (4x/8x) or PCI, and I know (as a result of trying to upgrade a system before Xmas) that the opportunities are now more limited (I got mine from ebay).
You have to be careful what sort you get, as some chipsets are not well supported by ubuntu.

I'm not into flight sims, so don't know what to suggest for you.

How much RAM is there? The default 512M isn't nearly enough - better to aim at the max it will take.
