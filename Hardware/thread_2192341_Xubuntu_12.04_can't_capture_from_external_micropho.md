### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-12-07
What setting is there for your mics in pulseaudio volume control? Yopu may have two mics if this is a laptop with an internal mic as well as your external mic, so don't be confused by that possibility.

Click on the volume icon in your panel and go to **Sound settings**, then to the **Input Devices** tab where you may find the level is set too low.
