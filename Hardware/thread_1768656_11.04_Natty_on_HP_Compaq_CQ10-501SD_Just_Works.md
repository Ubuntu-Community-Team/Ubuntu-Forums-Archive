### Post by kc600 on 2011-05-27
A short note for the next person to search the internet for this:

I just installed Natty on a HP Compaq CQ10-501SD. Everything works out of the box. 

Spotify Linux Preview and Guyadeque both work fine as well.

Update 29-05-2011:
- Beware that the keyboard (Hewlett-Packard Mini 110 layout) has no PgUp/PgDown keys.
- Battery indicator always says "Laptop battery (estimating...)". Icon gives a good indication of remaining capacity though.
- Suspend/resume on lid close/open works perfectly.
