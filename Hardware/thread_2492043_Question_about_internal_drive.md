I got this used HP EliteDesk G3 computer & I installed my own SSD in it.  I noticed it didn't have any drives - neither a 2.5" or 3.5" drive in any of the bays inside.
However, it does have a 250 gigabyte drive, that shows up and I can format and use for backups and etc...
I'm just curious where this drive is?  Is it built into the motherboard ???

---

### Post by deadflowr on 2023-10-28
Probably an m.2/pcie drive which would be directly connected to the mother board.
Would look like a memory module but laying flat on the motherboard and secured with a screw.
