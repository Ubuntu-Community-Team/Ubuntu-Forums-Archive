### Post by JC Cheloven on 2011-07-17
It's a matter of taste really. Most important thing is to get one which is [supported in alsa]("http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Main#Vendors").

For example RME Hammerfall have a good reputation of having both excellent sound quality and decent linux suport.
EDIT: But avoid rme hdsp 9632 in particular. There's a nasty bug in the way alsa presents the card to pulseaudio.
