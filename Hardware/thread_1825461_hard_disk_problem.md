### Post by .... on 2011-08-15
A bit of overheating isn't a catastrophe for newer disks (they're more likely to fail from cold operation). However, as disks get older, they show a greater negative correlation between temperature and drive life.

So, dust your disks, give them plenty of room to breathe, and install an intake fan if necessary.
