### Post by sgosnell on 2010-07-19
Any speaker system that works for any computer will work.  The OS makes no difference at all, as long as the hardware will physically connect.  Pretty much any self-powered speakers will do the job, it's just a matter of which will give you the best sound for the money.  Even if they have different plugs, you can use them, given the vast array of adapters available.
