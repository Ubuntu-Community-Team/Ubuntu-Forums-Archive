### Post by gballard on 2008-11-10
I installed 8.10 the day it was released on my Inspiron e1505 and had a terrible experience with it.

**Wireless**

It did not detect the Dell Wireless 1390 out of the box so I had to hook up an ethernet cable and let it do its thing to get the wireless card driver/firmware installed and then once I manually configured the wireless by manually editing /etc/network/interfaces it would not keep the network settings.  I tried starting and stopping the networking but that did not help.  Everytime it lost the settings, I had to reboot the laptop and even then it didn't work all the time.  This was a game killer for me.

**Video**

My laptop has the nVidia GeForce 7300 256MB video card in it and I was never able to get the driver installed for it.  It would alert me that 3rd party drivers were available but it would not install the video driver regardless of how many times I "told" it to.  Another show stopper in my book.

I guess I will just wait for 8.10.1 to see if these problems have been resolved....:(
