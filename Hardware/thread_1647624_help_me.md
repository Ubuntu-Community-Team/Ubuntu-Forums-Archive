### Post by trinitydan on 2010-12-17
Sorry to hear about that.  You probably have a 15 pin VGA output on your laptop.  If you connect it to an external monitor Ubuntu should detect it and you can still access your system to get to important files if you need to while you wait for your new display to come in.  I have an old computer with a broken display hooked up to a flat screen TV with no intention of ever fixing the display.  All it took was a VGA cable.
