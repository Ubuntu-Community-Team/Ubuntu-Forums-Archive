### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-04-28
A Dell CD, one that came with a PC preinstalled witn Win7, is likely only to activate on a Dell PC.  It's likely an OEM version and if so, uses a form of activation tied to the BIOS on the Dell PC -- meaning, you probably won't be able to activate it on another PC.

The version of Win7 you got from your school could be retail, but it could also be a VLK (Volume License Key) version the school acquired under a licensing agreement with MS.  Either way, they will need to provide you with a valid license key -- which will work on any PC.
