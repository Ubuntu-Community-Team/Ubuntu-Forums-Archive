### Post by michaelkahl on 2008-09-27
I just got the Asus Eee 900 yesterday.  I installed Ubuntu Eee which is Ubuntu with the netbook remix.  I must say that so far I'm impressed with it and am happy.  Wireless and Ethernet work.  The only device I haven't tested is the webcam, but from what I hear it works with the 900.  Everything else works. 
I've only had one hitch with the whole setup....and I don't think it's an Ubuntu issue.  Flash 10 beta is installed with Ubuntu Eee.  It works fine with one exception,  Cisco Netacademy.  Once I log in I simply get a white screen.  So I removed 10 beta and reinstalled 9.  Everything works fine for me.  In most cases, this probably isn't an issue and 10 beta will be fine.  With 10 hulu.com was excellent, with 9 it can get a little choppy at full screen.  Again this is a flash issue not Ubuntu.
Here's the link to get ubuntu eee.
[http://www.ubuntu-eee.com/](http://www.ubuntu-eee.com/)
Netbook remix may look similar to easy-mode on Xandros(default eee linux), but it isn't.  You don't loose functionality here.  Just packaged different on the GUI side of things.  You still get access to everything you would with regular Ubuntu.
Good luck and I hope you enjoy.
