### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-10-13
Forget about using ATI restricted drivers with your card.

As has been documented many times in other forums, AMD dropped support for your card and lots of others months ago, ending with Catalyst 9.3.  

There is no newer driver that will work with the Xorg delivered in 9.04 or the newer one in 9.10.

If you search the video subforum, you will find some posts dealing with hacks folks have done to the Xorg to get ATI drivers working again -- but that locks you into an older version that then, can not be upgraded anymore.

After 8.10, except for the hacking mentioned above, those of us with the unsupported cards are relegated to using the open source drivers.  That includes the x1300.
