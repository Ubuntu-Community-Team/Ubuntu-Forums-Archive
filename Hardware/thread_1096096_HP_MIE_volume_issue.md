### Post by tvs82 on 2010-08-12
In case you're still looking for an answer... 

Click on Settings on the top right hand corner.
Click Advanced
Click on Sound
Click on Devices tab

The right hand side has a list of different adjustable items.
Highlight what you want the keyboard volume keys to adjust.
Master is one of the options. Hope that helps.
