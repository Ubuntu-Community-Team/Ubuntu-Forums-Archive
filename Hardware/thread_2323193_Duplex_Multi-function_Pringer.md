HP and Canon are well supported under Ubuntu in my experience, I own a wifi HP multifunction printer and it works flawlessly. I would recommend looking for a HP model based on my experience, you can also check HPLIP library if the models you are considering are supported:

[http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html)

HPLIP also comes with a nice graphical interface that gives you total control over your printer, including aligning print heads and cleaning heads.
