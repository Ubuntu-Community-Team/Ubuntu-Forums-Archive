 Symptoms

 hdmi audio works until the screen resolution is changed. The system defaults to 1280x720. The native tv resolution is supposed to be 1368x768.

 As soon as the resolution is changed, hdmi audio stops working; laptop speaker audio is unaffected.

 
 
 After install (the above symptoms were also present in 16.04lts, 16.10, 17.04 when running from HDD install. From here on troubleshooting was only done with 16.04lts fresh install +conky)

 
 
 Even though the laptop appears to be closed even at the bios level, the system starts in a dual monitor configuration. Xrandr is used to disable the eDP screen (presumably the laptop?)

 ```

 Again, as soon as the resolution is changed, there is no longer any audio out over hdmi.

 If the resolution is changed back to 1280x720 the sound starts to work again.
