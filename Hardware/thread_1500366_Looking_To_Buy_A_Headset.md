I've got a Logitech 960 headset that works perfectly. I just have to open the sound settings and switch the input+output to the headset. I'd count on getting better Linux support from Logitech rather than Microsoft.

Doing a google search for the 250 finds some people that seem to have it working without any problems:
[http://nozell.com/blog/2006/09/13/logitech-usb-headset-ubuntulinux/](http://nozell.com/blog/2006/09/13/logitech-usb-headset-ubuntulinux/)
