### Post by Noah_Kapiolani on 2009-05-22
I build my own external USB hard drives  Price, as you stated, is about the same, and there are no other fancy features like Seagate does on their external USB drives that use part of the space to appear as a DVD-ROM that has fairly useless win programs on it.  Moreover, i have had several problems with Western Digital USB drives where 2 die in about a year of occasional use and zero abuse.  If you go to newegg.com you can read reviews, with a bucket of salt, and then decide.  My humble experience is that Seagate and Western Digital make good internal drives, but most pre-made external USB drives, regardless of brand, in my humble opinion, suck. Remember to BACKUP or your chances of becoming SOL increase exponentially.
