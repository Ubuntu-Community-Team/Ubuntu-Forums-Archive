### Post by gifford on 2018-04-03
If I understand your problem, you have the Brother printer that is connected to the network but your wife can only access it through your computer when it is turned on? It sounds like you are sharing the printer with
your wife through your computer. Did you install the drivers on her computer? If not, that would be the problem. You need to do the installation on your wife's computer just as you did on yours. The printer should be set up as a network printer available to you both.
