### Post by Fire_Chief on 2008-05-31
It may be a volume setting from either the hardware (volume buttons on the laptop), software master volume or software pcm volume. Check all 3 and make sure they are not cranked all the way up. They should be able to be nearly full volume and not have any crackling/popping in the sound.

Cheers!
