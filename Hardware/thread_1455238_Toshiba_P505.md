You might want to be more specific with your model number. Current Toshiba laptops include a six-character code after the first four-character code in the model number. For instance, I've got a Toshiba L505-ES5018. Although models in a particular series (L505, P505, etc.) may be similar, they're not identical, and some of those differences could be important for use under Linux.
