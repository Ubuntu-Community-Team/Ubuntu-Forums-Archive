### Post by corrytonapple on 2011-05-20
I also had that issue with a Toshiba L455 for the first time I plugged in headphones, there was no sound at all.  My solution was to go to **System > Preferences > Sound** and then to the Output tab.  At the bottom of the window next to where it says "Connector" click the drop down menu.  Then, click, well for me, "Analog Headphones".  
Good Luck
