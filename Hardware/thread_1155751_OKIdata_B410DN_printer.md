### Post by growingneeds on 2011-01-02
On Lucid, my B410dn is installed via Appsocket and the driver B4200 is selected. Duplexing works fine. As I live in Singapore, I had to select the page size to A4 as it defaults to US Letter sizing.

In addition, there is a toner cartridge / image drum indicator on printer properties. Very neat.
