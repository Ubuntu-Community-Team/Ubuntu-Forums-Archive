### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-07-27
Your machine is running on Gallium 0.4 llvmpipe. Which Ubuntu uses as a fall back mode. It is getting you to a desktop instead of a black screen.

Try going to Additional Drivers and activating either the full open source video driver or a proprietary video driver.

Regards.
