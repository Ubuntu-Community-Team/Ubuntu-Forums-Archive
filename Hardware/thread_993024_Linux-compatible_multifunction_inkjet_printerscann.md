### Post by Ualpa on 2012-05-25
I'm not a great fan of HP (any more), but I recently bought one for my sister (Xubuntu 10.04) because I already had an old Deskjet at home and a Laserjet at the office (both Ubuntu 10.04) and I've never had problems with them. HPLIP ([http://hplipopensource.com](http://hplipopensource.com)) seems to me a well developed and complete software and I would say that HP printers in general are reasonably Linux-friendly.

This one - Deskjet 3070A - has four cartridges, wireless and "email-to-print" (though not tested myself yet) and no need to turn on the pc if you only need to copy a document. A bit noisy and definitely not a beautiful object (very "plasticky"), but cheap and hassle free (so far). Ah, no card reader, sorry.
