### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-04-09
Keep in mind that your netbook will ALWAYS be slow no matter what OS you run on it.  You might find one distro that's very slightly faster than another, but you're still going to be slower than your desktop.  Netbook processors (e.g. Intel Atom) are designed to be light and energy efficient, at the expense of performance.

I run Google Chrome as my main web browser on all my computers, including on my Acer netbook.  (Which runs Ubuntu 11.04 at the moment.)  You might try Chrome and see if it's any faster than Firefox for you.
