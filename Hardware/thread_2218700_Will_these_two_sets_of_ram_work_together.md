### Post by sffvba[e0rt on 2014-04-21
These are the different timings on your RAM.  If you put mixed ones they will default to the lowest speed (which means if you have very fast and very slow RAM you end up having slow RAM).  In your case there doesn't seem to be a huge different in the timings and I think that the boost of having 4GB RAM outways any loss in speed you might suffer.
