### Post by altoiddealer on 2011-08-27
I'm running Ubuntu 11.04 Natty on an Asus Eee PC 1015dn

I bought a Logitech Cordless Rumblepad 2.

I downloaded the Joystick calibration program "Joystick"

I plugged in my joystick, then in a terminal typed "jscal /dev/input/js0"
Then I typed "jstest /dev/input/js0"

That was it! All the buttons were recognized.  I just had to set up a keymap.xml for XBMC


**My problem is the second controller I just purchased**

I bought another Logitech Cordless Rumblepad 2.

I plugged it in, then in a terminal typed "jscal /dev/input/js1"
Then I typed "jstest /dev/input/js1"

The device itself appears to be recognized, but none of the buttons that I press are doing anything.


Can anyone please let me know how to get my 2nd Logitech Cordless Rumblepad 2 to work?  Thanks!
