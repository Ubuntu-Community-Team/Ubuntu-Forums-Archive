### Post by noelvh on 2009-04-05
I do not have a fix for you but I have a T43 and have been running ether Kubuntu, or Ubuntu for that last 2 years and have never had this happen to me. I would try and set all of you power options to default and see what happens. Another thing is to feel the back of the lid and see if it is really hot, it could be over heating. If so you only need to replace a small board under the lcd. I have seen them on ebay for 5 bucks. The best thing about IBM laptops is you can get part cheap on ebay.

Well good luck.
