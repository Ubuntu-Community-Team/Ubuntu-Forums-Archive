### Post by quixote on 2009-12-04
Just curious: why would you want Ubuntu Netbook Remix in a 64-bit version?  Why not just use the regular version in that case?  UNR is designed for sort-of underpowered machines, so that it has a light footprint, works on small screens, etc.  But a 64-bit machine isn't going to be memory-limited.

To answer your question, no, as far as I know, there's no 64-bit UNR, nor is there any planned, but I don't know very far! :D
