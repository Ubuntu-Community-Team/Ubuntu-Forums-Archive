### Post by oldgeekster on 2009-11-13
With all of the negative press given to Karmic, thought I would toss in a positive discovery.

I have the subject Logitech speaker system for notebook computers.  On Jaunty I tried and tried to get them to work but no matter what I did, (short of compiling my own kernel), the irritating background buzz continued.  I was told what a piece of crap Logitech speakers are, yadda, yadda, and finally put them away but couldn't bring myself to toss them.

Today, on a whim, I plugged them into the Toshiba notebook I had elected to upgrade to version 9.10 and guess what?  They work perfectly!

Guess it was the audio config in the old kernel when it was compiled after all.


Go figure. ;)
