### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-10-12
The battery circuit will disconnect if there is a shorted battery to prevent fire.  This is normal behavior.  I would buy a new battery and your mother should be OK.  It's possible that the power jack is loose--this is a common problem on laptops.  Because of the age of EEE's, I would suspect a dead battery first.

The easiest way to check is to remove the battery completely, plug in the AC cord and boot.  If the machine boots and runs OK without the battery, then you can assume that the power supply and the power jack are OK.  A shorted battery takes too much current to allow the computer to boot.  Not all laptops will allow booting without the battery, so you will have to research that.  I don't have a EEE to test.
