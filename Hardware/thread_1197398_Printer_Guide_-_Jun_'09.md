### Post by Culip on 2009-07-14
In fact, I bought Brother DCP-8085DN.

**Brother DCP-8085DN officially supports Linux**; They distribute its printer driver and its scanner driver. The installation of those drivers were very easy. I'd used an affordable Canon's copier which had poor Linux compatibility, and so I was impressed how Linux friendly Brother DCP-8085DN was.

Now I use **Brother 8085DN under Ubuntu 9.4 Jaunty and it works very well** so far. The **duplex** (two-sided) printing and the **scanner** work perfectly.

Brother 8085DN is **pricey**: $410 (incl. Calif. tax) at PC Nation, but you don't have to worry about the Linux compatibility. If you are a Linux newbie or busy person, I'd recommend this copier.

Also, Brother 8085DN is **large** compared to most copiers for consumers, so consider its size before you buy one.
