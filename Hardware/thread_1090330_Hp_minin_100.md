### Post by Chibone on 2009-03-08
Components can still hold a charge even after removing the CMOS battery. Quick google says waiting longer than 15 minutes might be necessary.

Sometimes changing the amount of memory might do it too.  Pulling out the RAM stick and booting the mini might make it reset, but if not you'll just have to pull both the laptop battery and the RTC battery and wait.

Here's a service manual for the HP Mini 1000 (though you probably don't need it if you already pulled the CMOS battery). As far as i could tell there is no reset button but you should check the manual yourself just in case I missed something.

[http://bizsupport1.austin.hp.com/bc/docs/support/SupportManual/c01658655/c01658655.pdf](http://bizsupport1.austin.hp.com/bc/docs/support/SupportManual/c01658655/c01658655.pdf)
