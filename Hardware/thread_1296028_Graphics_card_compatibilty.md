### Post by cascade9 on 2009-10-20
LOL, from the same city. 

A 9600GT would be fine.  $89-$142 depending on brand, but dont buy an expensive one, you can get a 9800GT for $119 (I would get a $129 gigabyte card). **** 

The 9800GT would give you a bit more performance in games, up to you if its worth the extra few dollars. 

Video card memory is not an issue, your motherboard might take DDR2 sticks but it has no idea what type of memory is on the card. 

PCI-E 2.0 cards should run fine on PCI-E 1 slots.
