### Post by RedRat on 2014-06-13
If you are trying to scan line art into B&W, this may be a "feature". In the days of yore, when you were making pictures of line art for printing or lithography, they used to use "orthochromatic" film which was insensitive to blue. This allowed artists to use blue lines in the art for alignment and other things. I may well be wrong about this. You may have to control the sensitivity for all colors in some way through some proprietary software.
