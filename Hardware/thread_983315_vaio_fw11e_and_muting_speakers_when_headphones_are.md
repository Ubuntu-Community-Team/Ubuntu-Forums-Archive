Maybe it's obvious but i realy spent some time to figure this out. 
The problem was that the speakers still was playing sound when i plugged in my headphones. There is no switch to mute this (only "headphone" switch which turns on/off headphones) so the easiest way to mute speakers and be able to hear sounds in headphones is to mute "Front" volume.
