So here's the story(sorry for my bad english):

I just noticed that my Samsung 535u3c(A4 4355M 4gb RAM) goes really hot, especially while charging. I just found a great small tweak to make my apu stay around 60/63°(no more 78/80°) while charging(and a normal usage). You just have to open the small back cover of your Samsung and remove the silver piece of paper(just a RAM slot cover) so that the big RAM grid is free to take air.
Here's a pic: [http://oi58.tinypic.com/ae5pw6.jpg](http://oi58.tinypic.com/ae5pw6.jpg)

Now my apu is just cool and while charging tha fan is not so fast.

I hope this story could help all of you!
