### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2011-01-13
You do not need to pay for any drivers. By non-free he means proprietary or closed source, as in opposed to [open source]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_source"). Linux is mostly open source but companies like Nvidia and ATI release their own proprietary drivers which allow better functionality as they are designed by the people who made the hardware.

Also, dirtectX is for windows only, Linux and Mac use [OpenGL]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Opengl").
