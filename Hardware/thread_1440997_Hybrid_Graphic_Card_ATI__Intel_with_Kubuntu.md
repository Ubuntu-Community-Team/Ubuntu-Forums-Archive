### Post by b0wter on 2010-04-30
I dont have any experience with Acer Notebooks but I am using a Thinkpad with a hybrid graphics card which should be quite similar.
The bios offers me to select three options: "switchable", "integrated" and "discrete". Switchable can so far only be used under Win Vista and 7 (with the manufactures drivers).

You can use both cards with compiz fusion (haven't tried tried any of the fancy 3D stuff. But basically you can look up what the onboard card is capable of as there is a special intel driver around). The ATI card will however be able to render more effects with better speed but at the cost of battery power.
