Hi there, hope this help but i've had trouble getting my old Laserjet 4 Plus to work on ubuntu now that i've had to convert to USB. Sometimes it would just freeze up and not print anything, next time you turn the pc on it'll start spitting prints out at you! I think i've cracked it though. Turn your printer on first, let it run through its self test and settle down, and THEN boot up your computer. Even switching it on whilst booting seems to cause problems. Hope this helps!
