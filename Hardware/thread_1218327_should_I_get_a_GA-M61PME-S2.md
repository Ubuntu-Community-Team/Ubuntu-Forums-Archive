### Post by joeoshawa on 2010-05-02
I have this board and it has been great. I even have two monitors running using one video card and the onboard video. The only problem i have encountered is that i cannot get the microphone to work (a bug with alsa) some have had some luck others not. The machine is plenty fast and does all i want it to do.  I was running windows 7 and everything configured plug and play no work needed. (switched cause i hate microsoft and ubuntu 10.4 got my girls phone working with it).  My thoughts are good board with plenty of extras good bang for the buck for sure.  Just remember if he wants to get the mic working he will either have to buy a sound card or a usb microphone.  I waisted $10 on a headset i cannot use. all in all i would say not a bad board at all.
