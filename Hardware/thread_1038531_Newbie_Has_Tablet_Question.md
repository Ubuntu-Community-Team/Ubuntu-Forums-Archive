### Post by Favux on 2009-01-14
Hi aced19,

The discussion between Rendrago and myself really isn't that esoteric.  In a little while you'll know as much or more.  You could post on Rendrago's thread.  Maybe he'd have the time/inclination to help.  He is posting those HOW TO's!

He has the wacom digitizer version.  Do you?  Or do you have the touchscreen version?

If you have the digitizer version the stylus seems to be working through HAL.  That may be all you want.  Does the side switch work on the stylus?  If so then you're set.  You need to calibrate the stylus.  I don't know if the linuxwacom calibration tool works with HAL.  Let's find out.

In a terminal (Applications>Accesories>Terminal) type "wacomcpl".  A little program will pop up.  This is the linuxwacom calibration gui.  On the left is stylus.  Click on it and options should appear.  Pick calibration and calibrate.

Good luck.
