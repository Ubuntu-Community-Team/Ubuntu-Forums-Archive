### Post by kessaris on 2008-06-30
Are you able to access the Touchpad control panel in System->Preferences->Touchpad?

If it launches, chances are your touchpad is at least detected as such.

In there you can find the settings for scrolling.  For some reason on my particular machine, vertical scrolling doesn't work, but horizontal works most of the time.  It also has to do with the sensitivity setting I think.

Here is a post you might find interesting too!
