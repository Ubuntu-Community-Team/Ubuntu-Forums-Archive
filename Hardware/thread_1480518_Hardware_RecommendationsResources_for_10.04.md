### Post by cascade9 on 2010-05-16
You shouldnt have any issues with newer AMD hardware under 10.04. Some manufacturers like to use crappy jbmicron RAID controllers with little/no linux support (MSI is bad for it) but aside from that, no major issues. 

The ATI chipsets have been around for a few years now,a dn the new AM3 8XX series chipsets are just newer revisions of the older 7XX chipsets. nVidia chipsets for AMD are pretty much the same thing..but I'd go for an ATI chipset.
