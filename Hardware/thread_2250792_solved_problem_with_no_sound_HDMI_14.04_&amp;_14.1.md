### Post by briancb on 2014-10-31
After update 14.04LTS there was no sound via HDMI and no HDMI shown in system sound.

The problem was that in my Sony AV the HDMI settings were set to AMP + TV Ubuntu was seeing the TV and not handshaking with the Sony. Changed to AMP. rebooted and I had glorious 5.1 DD. 

I also have a Amazon Fire TV this however needs the settings on AMP+TV but after original handshake every thing still worked fine when I changed it back.

I probably will need to do this after every reboot to re-engage handshake.
