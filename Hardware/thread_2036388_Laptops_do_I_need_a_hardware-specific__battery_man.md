### Post by Ubun2to on 2012-08-01
Ubuntu comes with a battery manager built in. Although it's not the most accurate, I'd be shocked if anybody here can name 1 battery manager that accurately predicts the time left in the battery.
So, I don't think you need to worry. But, you should consider replacing the battery.
