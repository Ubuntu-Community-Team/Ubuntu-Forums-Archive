### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2009-08-27
I think that most recent digital cameras will work.  Most of them can be configured either to use "USB Mass Storage" mode or PTP mode.  Both are supported modes of data transfer.

### Post by Goombie on 2009-08-28
I would just buy an SD card reader. They're usually under $20, and work just fine with Ubuntu. I have an ioGear brand reader that was $20 USD, reads SD cards, and works with Ubuntu. Then, it doesn't matter what type of camera you buy. :) Just pop the card into the reader when you want to access/transfer your photos.
