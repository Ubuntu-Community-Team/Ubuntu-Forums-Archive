### Post by foresthill on 2013-03-08
The older HP printers work extremely well in Ubuntu. The newer ones, however, seem to have some serious quality issues and compatibility is limited in some cases, especially the all-in-one models. 

So if possible, I would try to get an older HP LaserJet model, the older the better, provided that it has at least USB support. Would definitely not recommend anything without a USB port, as parallel ports can be quite slow, and newer desktops and notebooks don't even have a parallel interface.
