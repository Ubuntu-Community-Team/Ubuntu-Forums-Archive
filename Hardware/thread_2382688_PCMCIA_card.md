### Post by Autodave on 2018-01-18
Does the card work in Windows?  I have seen many of those cards fail.

One thing you can try: won't cost you anything but a few minutes of your time and I always do this when a laptop starts doing weird things:

   1. Power down and disconnect power cord.
2. Unplug all peripherals.
3. Remove battery.
4. Wait 10 minutes.
5. Reinstall battery.
6. Connect power cord.
7. Boot computer and test to see if condition is gone.
8. If good so far, now you can reconnect peripherals.
