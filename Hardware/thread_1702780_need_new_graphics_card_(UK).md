### Post by mastablasta on 2011-03-10
> **cyberboy109 said:**
> I have both connections 1 agp and 2 pci
 
 
PCIe is not the same as PCI. PCIe is a lot faster. 
 
You probably have AGP port for graphics card. AGP cards are still made but they are a dying breed as PCIe offers much faster transfer rates.
 
you will also likely need to invest into VGA adapter (or not as sometimes they come with the card). Most cards now use HDMI port (or maybe DVI sometimes). 
 
another cheap option (besides the aforementioned nvidia) could be ATI Radeon HD 3xxx series. They are descent card and have proprietary driver's linux support. I only tried live session with opensoruce drivers on Radeon HD 3650 and it all worked out of the box. desktop effects and all. haven't tried gaming. 
 
4xxx series should also work nicely, but i doubt it's worth to invest so much into this unless you really game a lot. and since your much weaker onboard GPU was fine before...
