### Post by fresnofred on 2010-06-23
u can use an external wifi card. either use airlink101 model AWLL 3026 usb wifi device (NOT 3028) or use their pcmcia card awlc4030 or 4130- both are super-g with atheros chip.  they work automatically with ubuntu and most linux distros.  i use these myself with the many linux pcs and laptops i own.  probably can only buy them now off ebay.  guaranteed to work with no drivers needed.
