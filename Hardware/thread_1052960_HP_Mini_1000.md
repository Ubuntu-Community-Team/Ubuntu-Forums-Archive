### Post by leenyx64 on 2009-01-29
I just recently purchased the HP mini 1030nr from Bestbuy and after two days of XP I installed Intrepid Ibex.  Installing it was somewhat troublesome.  I created a live usb and it kept failing to load, but after a couple tries it worked and installed perfectly. There are some subtle performance differences.  Ubuntu works well on this machine with no real problems. The web cam is a bit dark, but it wasn't much better under XP. All and all I love this little guy.  I am just waiting until next payday to buy a 2Gb stick of DDR2 to pop in here.  My little brother was impressed so much by it that he went out and bought the exact same thing and installed 8.10 right out of the box.  I am assuming that you know what you are getting when you go with a netbook (its not a Powerhorse) but for a netbook it's the best combination of perfomance and sexyness that i have seen.

Hope this helps:D

### Post by leenyx64 on 2009-01-29
The only modifications i made to 8.10 was delete the bottom panel and added AWN then used:

 sudo apt-get install ubuntu-restricted-extras

for multimedia.  Thats about it.  I tried to run the remix UI on top of Ubuntu but it wasnt for me.  Make sure you turn your Visual Effects OFF. I didnt and the screen kept glitching. 

Good Luck;)
