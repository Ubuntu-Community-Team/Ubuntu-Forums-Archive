### Post by Autodave on 2018-04-13
Sounds like you need a new mouse.  First, I would borrow one and try it because I have seen a few occassions where a power supply is going bad and literally the mouse doesn't have enough voltage to work properly.

I actually have a machine just like that: it will work with the older style mouse with a roller ball in it, but a laser mouse just doesn't work for very long after booting up. And, I have tried at least 6 different laser mice on it!

In all likelihood, you just need a new mouse.
