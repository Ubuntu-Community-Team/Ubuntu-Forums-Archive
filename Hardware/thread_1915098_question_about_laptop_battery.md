### Post by Dngrsone on 2012-01-26
Tell him the battery cover is bent because the battery overheated from too much charging.

That's mostly BS, of course, but there is a kernel of truth, there-- batteries have only so many charge cycles in them, and leaving a laptop plugged in continuously will shorten the battery life (and laptops are notoriously hot; heat being very detrimental to battery health).
