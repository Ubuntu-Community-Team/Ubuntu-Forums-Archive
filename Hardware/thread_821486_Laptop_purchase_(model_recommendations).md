### Post by thideras on 2008-06-07
I just picked up a Lenovo T61.  Absolutely love this laptop.  It is extremely durable, has the T9300 (45nm dual core) and I get around 4 hours battery life at the desktop, slightly under if I'm browsing the internet.  I paid just over $900 for the laptop itself. :D

If you get one with Suse linux, it is actually cheaper.  I dropped Ubuntu on it and everything works without changing anything.
