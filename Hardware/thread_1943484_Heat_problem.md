### Post by Zorael on 2012-03-20
This could be anything from driver or kernel regressions to Adobe Flash soaring in CPU use to your cat finally having succeeded in glogging your fan intake with hair.

Try hitting Ctrl+Esc; by default, this should open the System Activity window. I'm assuming you're running Kubuntu now, as you tagged your thread with **[color=royalblue][kubuntu][/color]**.

Choose All Processes in the dropdown menu to the right, then click the CPU % column header to sort by processor use. Is there anything there using unduly amounts of CPU time? If it's your browser, try installing a flash blocker.

Is your laptop well ventilated? Try putting some distances underneath it to easen the air intake.

If you're handy and if it doesn't void your warranty, you could open up the laptop and rid any heatsinks of dust and other debris.
