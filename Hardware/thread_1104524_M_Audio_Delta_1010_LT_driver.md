### Post by astonerose on 2009-03-23
Just to let anyone who reads this know i have found the solution (for me anyway) 
It wasnt drivers i needed it just prints an error that looks rather 
driver related. What actualy had happened was another process was using the card.

System > Administration > System Monitor

Processes tab

find pulseaudio and kill that process

Hope this may help someone in the future!!
