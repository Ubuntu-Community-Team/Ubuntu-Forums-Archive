More information on this issue can be found at [http://devphone.org/hardware-32/ubuntu-10-10-usb-hc-takeover-failed/]("http://devphone.org/hardware-32/ubuntu-10-10-usb-hc-takeover-failed%21/")

I started with 2.6.35 and had issues. I went up to 2.6.36 and still had the same issue. I then went down to 2.6.32 and now the USB ports work just fine and dandy. I will do some more playing around another time to see with what Kernel the issue starts at and will follow up here.

I don't really call this fixed but rather a work around for the time being.
