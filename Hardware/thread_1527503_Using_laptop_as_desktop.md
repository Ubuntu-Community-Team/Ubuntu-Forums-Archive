### Post by bobcollard on 2010-07-10
First, it is a little more involved than that.  Drain your battery down to half power and remove it, that is the recommended procedure.  When you get your larger screen set up with the proper Resolution then you can set the Power management to do nothing when the lid is closed in both battery and AC.  I use a Logitech wireless keyboard and mouse with my Dell laptop, with a Samsung SyncMaster 2233 21 1/2" screen.  My laptop is in the drawer of the desk I work on.  Chords all hidden and it is neat and clean.  To add to the cooling I leave the DVD drive open when not in use.  There are other things you can add like an external HD, a USB-2 Hub etc.
