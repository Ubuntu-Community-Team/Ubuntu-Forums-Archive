### Post by Manyette on 2010-07-03
I've recently done the same, and Win 7 comes with a facility to build a "restore the system" on 3 DVD's.  It works just fine, because I had to use it, when the first machine failed, and I had to restore Win 7 to turn it back in for another machine.  So I'd say just make those DVD's, and then blow off Win 7 completely.  Then you won't have to go through the whole reformat or gparted or whatever when you decide to dump Win 7 for sure.
