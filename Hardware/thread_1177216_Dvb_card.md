Some of the supported cards are listed in [MythTV wiki]("http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Category:Video_capture_cards").

The interesting thing is that older DVB-T cards work just fine, you don't necessarily need the newest and latest. Especially since most new DVB-devices are USB. So if you can get an used PCI DVB-T card from ebay, that's ok.

I have a Technotrend and a Hauppauge if I recall, both work just fine, but I built my Mythbuntu box some years ago.
