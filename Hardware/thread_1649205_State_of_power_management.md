Hello all,

A short time ago I upgraded to [laptop-mode version 1.55]("http://samwel.tk/laptop_mode/") on my highly customized Karmic installations of Ubuntu.

I have two laptops:

Dell Inspiron 1440
14" LED
super-drive
Intel P8600 processor
Intel x4500 graphics
Broadcom wireless
49Wh battery

Dell XPS m1330
13" LCD
super-drive
Intel T5250 processor
Intel 3945 wireless
Intel x3100 graphics
86Wh battery

With new laptop mode configured (requires minimal configuration), low-power idle (screen dim, wireless off) is down to 
7.4Wh on the Inspiron 1440
8.6Wh on the XPS m1330

I am writing this as a **thank you** to everyone who has worked on laptop power management features.  These power saving times rival anything that OS X and Windows can offer!
