### Post by khelben1979 on 2011-03-22
You can start with sharing some of your pc hardware by typing **lspci** from a terminal window, such as [xterm]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xterm") for instance.

If it's intel graphics you got, then the graphic driver is inbuilt in the [Linux kernel]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linux_kernel") and you don't need to install anything. Having the [Mesa 3D]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mesa3d") in the system might be a good idea though!
