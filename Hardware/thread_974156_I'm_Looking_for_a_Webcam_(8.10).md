### Post by forkandles on 2008-11-30
Jim,
I am afraid that Plug'n'Play with Windows-designed webcams in Linux is a distant dream, that is if you actually want the thing to work with Skype, PulseAudio in 8.10 etc.

Do not be put off by what you see next. It is MUCH easier to do than your first impression will tell you, so DON'T PANIC!! Make a cup of tea, sit down and gently browse through here.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=990057](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=990057)

As well as the Logitech Quickcam Vision Pro, I have also read good reports of the Logitech Quickcam E3500 working well in Linux.

[http://linux-uvc.berlios.de/](http://linux-uvc.berlios.de/)

Good luck.
