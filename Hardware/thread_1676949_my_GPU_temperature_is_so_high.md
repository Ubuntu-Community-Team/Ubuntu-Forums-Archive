### Post by Filenotfoundx404 on 2011-01-28
Well not all laptops are created equal. some have heat problems others dont.

could be:
-while playing a game or watching HD videos, graphics cards tend to heat up.
-too much dust in yout laptop (if you had it for awhile and never used compressed air to blow it out or open it).
-room temperature (how warm is it where you are?)
-what is your laptop sitting on? if it on a bed or your lap and the it blocks the intake ports, you can have heat problems.
-broken fan

maybe also think about getting a laptop cooling pad (if you dont have 1).
