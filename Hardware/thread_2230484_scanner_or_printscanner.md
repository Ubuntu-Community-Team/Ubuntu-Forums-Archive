### Post by kurt18947 on 2014-06-20
I'd recommend a bit of caution when using lists of compatible devices,  they can be somewhat dated so newer compatible devices may not be on the list. I think H.P. printers and all-in-ones are compatible and pretty easy to install.  I'm partial toward Brother MFDs, I think Brother ink & toner is less expensive than most others.  I have no experience with other manufacturers.  

I do have one caution about scanners.  We have a Canon 8800F which is plug-n-play and works well for photos, paper etc.  What is apparently not supported by SANE is the cover light required for transparencies such as slides and negatives.  We purchased Viewscan for that purpose.  Viewscan does scan slides and negatives nicely.
