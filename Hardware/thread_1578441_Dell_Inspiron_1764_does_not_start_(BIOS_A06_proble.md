### Post by blecha on 2010-09-20
I have the 10.04 ubuntu on my Dell 1764 i3. I experienced with increased frequency the problem to start the machine (no LED,  nothing happens). I had to remove the battery wait some 10 sec, then after re-inserting of the battery the machine would boot … Dell suggested to update the BIOS. I moved from A06 to A11 (I did it under windows 7 – have a double boot) and the problem was solved. I am quite happy with 10.04, except for the integrated webcam which does not work consistently through all kernel releases till 2.6.32-25-44 (see  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=9668268#post9668268](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=9668268#post9668268) ).
