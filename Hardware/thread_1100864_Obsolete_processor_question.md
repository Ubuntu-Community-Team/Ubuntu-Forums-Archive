### Post by nitrogensixteen on 2009-03-19
Check out the power draw on the new processor.
If the new processor utilizes a smaller(nm) process, the efficiency of the new processor might mean that it emits less heat.
Make sure you use new thermal paste(rec. silver) when re-attaching your heatsink.
If this is a desktop, buy a new cooling solution, it's not worth finding out your old one wasn't up to snuff.
