### Post by dmahoon on 2012-11-20
I managed to do a bit of a work around to solve this, I removed the SD card from the phone and inserted it into a card reader.  Then I had to download mt tools and mtp from the Synaptic Package Manager because my card reader was not mounting.
Then I discovered that I had to plug the card reader into the usb port and then restart my computer.  Interestingly, if I unplugged the card reader from the port and plugged it in again it would not mount until I restarted again.
Whatever.
I was able to upload music from Rhythmbox onto my computer very easily once I got the card reader to mount.
