### Post by CrunchyDoodle on 2009-04-15
There are two types of HDD used in laptops, IDE and SATA. They are not interchangeable, but can be used with an adapter. My newer notebooks have SATA HDD and my older ones have IDE. You might consider buying an IDE / SATA USB adapter. I bought one for about $20. It came with a power pack to run the HDD, power and connector adapters and cables. That way I can take any IDE or SATA HDD and connect it to any PC easily.

Bye.    :cool:
