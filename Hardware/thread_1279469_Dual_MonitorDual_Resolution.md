### Post by Littleweseth on 2009-10-01
No, the resolutions do not have to match. I have a 1920x1080 + 1680x1050; in the past I had 1680x1050 + 1024x768.

You are lucky that you have an Nvidia card; the nvidia-settings utility makes dual monitors extremely easy to set up (just select 'Twinview' on the second monitor, and everything else is automagic.)

ATI is substantially more painful. :(
