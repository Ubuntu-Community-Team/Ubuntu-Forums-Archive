### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-03-30
You should be able to just put in the new card and reboot.  I have changed out Nvidia cards on Ubuntu systems and have not had any problems.

I have one computer with the Nvidia 5200 card (PCI card) and it works well with the 173 driver. I did have trouble with this card when I was using a crt monitor. The resolution would want to start at a low setting at start up, but it works fine witha lcd monitor.
