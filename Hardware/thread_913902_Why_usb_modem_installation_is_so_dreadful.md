### Post by ChimpofDOOM on 2008-09-08
Well an easy fix to your solution, buy an Ethernet modem.

Why you might ask...

Ethernet will connect your pc to your modem/router at 100mbps, USB I believe is 12mbps. If your internet speed is less than 12mbps then you will not experience a difference as your not maximizing your usb data transfer rate of 12mbps, However if your speed is more than 12mbps, get yourself ethernet :)

Also it's not to much a limitation by Linux, it's more the limitation by the vendor.

The problem is the industry, because the mass majority of PC users use windows (basically because of unfair trading practises, OFCOM, take note!).  M$ dominates the market, so all these hardware vendors will make drivers for the majority (as to be fair, their customers are 90% more likely to be using a Windoz machine).
