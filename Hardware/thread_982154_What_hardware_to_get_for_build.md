### Post by starcannon on 2008-11-14
Nforce or Intel based motherboards have never let me down.

Nvidia Video cards in my opinion are the best.

Kingston RAM

AVOID: SiS and VIA, while VIA is getting better, its still not quite there yet, especially if your going to be doing the support on the machine ;) I look for VIA to be very linux friendly in the very near future though.

I've not built a quad core system yet, so no advice for that specifically.

I would stick with 32bit Ubuntu for now, 64bit still requires a lot of 32bit softwares for most users, so unless your client specifically requires 64bit keep your support life simpler by using 32bit for now.

Those are my opinions, be sure to get a few more, I have it on good authority (wife) that I am occasionally wrong, though I probably rarely admit it.
