### Post by him610 on 2016-07-18
Hello and welcome to Ubuntu forums.

From the Meercat system specs webpage...
[https://system76.com/desktops/meerkat]("https://system76.com/desktops/meerkat")

CPU - My guess is that the CPU is ***NOT upgradable*** which is why you are given CPU choice of a i3-6100U or i5-6260U when you purchase it. Those motherboards are tiny, and probably do not have a ZIF connector to allow replacing the CPU.

RAM - Most likely* upgradable* unless you max it out (32GB) when you purchase. If you get the 4GB minimum, you can upgrade it later up to 32GB.

HDD to SSD - Minimally, you get a 32GB M.2 SSD. Also, you can get no additional drive, or up to 2GB SSD - you can do this yourself.

Did you consider contacting System76 to seek answers to your questions, or post your questions in the Ubuntu System76 forum?
