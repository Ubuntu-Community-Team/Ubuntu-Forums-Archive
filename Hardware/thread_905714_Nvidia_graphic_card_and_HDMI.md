### Post by thenellt on 2008-08-30
In my experience their drivers work great (I have a desktop 8600 GT). I to haven't tried HDMI but it's supposed to work. I assume that your laptop has DVI out. If so then it's easy, just get a DVI to HDMI adapter, like this [one]("http://www.google.com/products/catalog?q=dvi+to+hdmi+adapter&btnG=Search+Products&show=dd&cid=15275007591010724192#ps-sellers"). Then just get a HDMI cable, 15 to 40 dollars. Or get a DVI to HDMI [cable]("http://www.google.com/products/catalog?q=dvi+to+hdmi+cable&btnG=Search+Products&show=dd&cid=5870584279808507999#ps-sellers"). Should work either way. 
Hope it helps, Taz
