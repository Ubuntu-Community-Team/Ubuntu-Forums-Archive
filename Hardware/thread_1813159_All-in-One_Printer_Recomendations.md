### Post by TBABill on 2011-07-27
I have an HP C309A all in one that works perfectly. It's now updated to a new version that will also allow you to print from an IPod or IPad if you have them and the model is C310A. I think the IPad feature is called Air Print. Maybe not a big deal today, but there are few printers those devices can print to so if they are in your future, maybe something to consider if you may want to print from them.
