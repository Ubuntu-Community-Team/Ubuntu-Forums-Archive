### Post by trev mesh on 2016-05-06
I got this error on reboot after previously connecting my camera via usb. usb would no longer respond to mouse.

 Solution seems to be:

unplug mouse.

plug in camera and make sure it's mounted.

plug in mouse.

unmount camera.

Worked for me. Hope this helps someone else.
