### Post by snowpine on 2010-11-02
Welcome to the forums! I love netbooks; I own two and use them every day for catching up on email, surfing the Forums, playing simple games, etc.

I purchased one of my netbooks (Dell Mini 9) with Ubuntu 8.04 preinstalled. This was roughly two years ago and I paid about $200; this works out to pennies for every day I've used it! The other vendor I know of who pre-installs Ubuntu is System76. (I have no personal experience with this company, just sharing the link.)

If you search these forums, you'll see lots of existing topics where people share their netbook experiences. You might also find this page useful: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/Machines/Netbooks](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/Machines/Netbooks)

ps The "do/should computers with Ubuntu cost less than with Windows?" topic has been discussed to death already, too. The general consensus is that Ubuntu-preinstalled computers are very mich a niche market, and you will not necessarily save any money by going this route. However, you will be helping to develop the Ubuntu market share, and generally speaking, as an item becomes popular, competition get more fierce and prices come down. :)
