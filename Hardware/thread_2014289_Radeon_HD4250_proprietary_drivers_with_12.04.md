### Post by benny215 on 2012-07-01
I recently installed Ubuntu 12.04 on another system and without even really trying the generic drivers I automatically went and installed the proprietary ones.

It was a nightmare. Catalyst Control Center would always crash when I changed my display settings to multiple monitors and a higher resolution  and tried to save them. I had to redo my display settings every time I rebooted. Compiz would constantly crash and my system would always freeze up when I tried moving windows between desktops and whatnot. It was ridiculous.

[LEFT]Well I finally uninstalled ATI's driver and reinstalled the open source and WHAT A DIFFERENCE. No freezing, crashing and compiz is smooth as can be.
[/LEFT]

I just wanted to let anybody that's having this issue know what finally worked for me. I tried a lot of things for get the proprietary drivers to work right but the only thing that helped was reverting back to the open source drivers.
