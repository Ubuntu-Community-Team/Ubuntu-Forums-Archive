### Post by wyliecoyoteuk on 2016-10-10
Might be a firmware issue.
does it work if you shut the computer down fully before changing OS?
wireless diveics often have firmware loaded by the OS at startup.
Sometimes a warm restart leaves the firmware in an unsuitable state, while a cold reboot will start it afresh.
