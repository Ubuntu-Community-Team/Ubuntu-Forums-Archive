### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-03-09
Suggestion is to upgrade to a newer motherboard that takes PCI-E graphics cards.  Your motherboard is several years old, so your AGP graphics card options are very limited.  A new motherboard with onboard video may do better without an extra graphics card than your AGP cards can do now.
