### Post by Favux on 2009-11-19
Hi MisteR2,

I hope Ping (lead dev.) or one of the other dev.s can help.  It may have something to do with the wacom xdrv code where there is a scaling/coordinate issue, at least on 0.8.5-4.  Any time the Xorg input system inits or reinits the device after Xorg is already running the scale can get set on a subdevice rather than the proper device.

You can also add:
```
	<merge key="input.x11_options.CommonDBG" type="string">12</merge>
```
which will get you some more info.
