### Post by him610 on 2024-09-17
++Logitech

I have two inexpensive Logitech keyboards, K270 and K360. I don't know about other brands, but the Logitech keyboards and Logitech mice can be paired together so you only need one Unifying Receiver for paired set. 

I have been using Logitech keyboards and mice for years; never had any problems with them, as long as you replace the batteries periodically and avoid dropping them.  I usually buy them from Walmart.
