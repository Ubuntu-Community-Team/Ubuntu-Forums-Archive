### Post by bigbee79 on 2008-07-27
So, I've been using Hardy for a while, and I love it.  I have one annoying problem that drives me crazy on my laptop.  If I close the lid and reopen it, the screen stays dark.  After fiddling for it for a while, I've noticed that hitting Ctrl+Alt+F7(my brightness down slider) then Ctrl+Alt+F8(my brightness up slider) then Ctrl+Alt+F7(my brightness down slider), the screen will pop back up.  I have no idea why this works, and I just found it when I got upset and started pushing random buttons with no idea that it would lead me to a fix. 

Is there anyway to not have to do this and just have my screen come back on it's own?  Thank you!

---

### Post by bb05 on 2008-07-27
you can do ctrl + alt + backspace which will re-load the system w/out reboot. this has been my quickfix for this error for awhile now. any other tips appreciated.

### Post by bigbee79 on 2008-07-27
