### Post by TenPlus1 on 2008-08-21
Windows drivers will not work for sound cards in Linux, but pop into the System -> Preferences -> Multimedia System Selector and mess around in there to see if your sound works again, switch between ALSA, Pulse and OSS...

Also, double-click on the volume control in the tray and edit the preferences to turn ALL of the switches ON, sometimes front/rear speakers for your sound card may be the problem...
