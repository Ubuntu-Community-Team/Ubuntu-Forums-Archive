### Post by jtappin on 2008-10-28
Firstly: the maximum resolution of the screen isn't 1280x800 it's 1280x768 [unless there are newer models with a higher resolution].

Assuming that's what you want and you currently have 1280x720 which is what the vesa driver will give, you have two choices, both of which can be obtained from the VIA Linux Portal [http://linux.via.com.tw/support/downloadFiles.action]("http://linux.via.com.tw/support/downloadFiles.action")

Make sure you have selected the Ubuntu 8.04 downloads and either download the binary driver and the kernel module sources, or the driver sources.

In either case you the need to follow the instructions embedded in them. 

You may find more useful information on [http://forums.mininoteuser.com/]("http://forums.mininoteuser.com/")
Notably a working xorg.conf from MikeZ, and some explanatory note on the kernel modules from me (unfortunately the search engine on that forum is timing out on me right now so I can't find the specific posts).
