### Post by MeditatingHamster on 2014-03-06
Just for anyone else wanting to know the same, this does work in 13.10, but make sure you plug the mic usb into the PC after you've booted up and logged into the desktop, and unplug it before you shutdown.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2153270](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2153270)

When you do plug this in, give it a few minutes. I found that if I opened audacity too soon after plugging the mic in, I couldn't see it as an option to record from in the Audacity menu. After closing audacity and leaving it a few minutes I could see 8 x Blue Audio 2.0 entries and it doesn't seem to matter which you choose, it just works.
