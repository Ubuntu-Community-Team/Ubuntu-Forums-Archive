### Post by canucked on 2010-12-09
I recently configured a Toshiba Satellite L655-S5115 laptop (purchased at Office Depot I believe) for a friend with Ubuntu 10.10 Maverick 64-bit.  I was able to straighten out the issues with wireless and audio, but I was never able to get the battery recognized.  I summarized this here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1641931](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1641931)

It seems like there are quite a few Toshiba laptops with ACPI issues.  Apart from the battery/ACPI problem, the L655 works fine.  I'm hoping either a future Toshiba BIOS update or linux kernel hack will fix the battery problem in the near future.

Hope this helps!
