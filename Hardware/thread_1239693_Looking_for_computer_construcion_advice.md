### Post by DownTown22 on 2009-08-14
I'll agree with alex.pancescu on the CPU choices, but there's no problem going with the [[COLOR="Blue"]_PhenomII X3_[/COLOR]]("http://www.newegg.ca/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16819103649") either (3 cores).  I have one in my media center and it performs wonderfully.

As for graphics, getting a 9000 series nVidia card will do the trick.  I run an [[COLOR="Blue"]_XFX 9600GT 512MB_[/COLOR]]("http://www.newegg.ca/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16814150397") in my media center and it runs everything with out an lag, etc.

And for power supplies, I always go with at least 500W.  My top brands are Corsair, OCZ and CoolerMaster.  My recent [[COLOR="Blue"]_OCZ power supply_[/COLOR]]("http://www.newegg.ca/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16817341017") is great - it's super quiet and keeps my computer very cool.
