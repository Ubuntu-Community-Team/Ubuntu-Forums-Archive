### Post by papibe on 2012-12-10
Hi sgcharest.

I own a original Kindle Fire, and it's very easy to upload both music and videos.

You connected as a if it were a USB stick, and then copy the files using nautilus (file manager).

Take a look at this youtube video tutorial: [Kindle Fire - Transfering Files to It (PC)]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jWb8xufomDw"). It is for Windows, but it works almost the same way on Ubuntu.

Let us know how it goes.
