### Post by GoldenSun on 2009-07-16
well am2 and am3 are amd socket types.  A am3 will fit into a am2 socket, but with the penalty of losing ddr3 for ddr2.  

The idea is to buy a mobo that says something like "am2".  Then you look for a processor that fits into the am2 socket; or vice-versa.
