### Post by quanumphaze on 2009-04-03
The Thinkpad SL series has good Linux support. Only problems are with the special keys and brightness control, due to the SL series using IdeaPad firmware instead of the normal ThinkPad firmware.

There is an experimental driver [here]("http://github.com/tetromino/lenovo-sl-laptop/tree/master") to fix the buttons.
More at [Linlap wiki]("http://www.linlap.com/wiki/lenovo+thinkpad+sl500") and [ThinkWiki]("http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Category:SL_Series")

I too am thinking about getting the SL500, a little research can save you from bad decisions.

### Post by Barry Carroll on 2009-04-03
