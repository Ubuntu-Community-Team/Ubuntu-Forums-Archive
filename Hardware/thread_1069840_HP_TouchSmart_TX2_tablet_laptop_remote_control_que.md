### Post by Ayuthia on 2009-11-10
You can find the keycodes by using xev.  Once the application is running, you can then press the buttons on the remote.  From what I can tell, all the buttons are working.  Just be careful with the power button.  I can't remember which OS I was testing that button on (I was pushing the buttons in the dark and found the power button) but I found out quickly that the button worked.
