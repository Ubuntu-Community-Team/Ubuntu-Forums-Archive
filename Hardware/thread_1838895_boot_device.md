### Post by racoffey63 on 2011-09-04
drive 0 is your hard drive.  if you're installing ubuntu, you need to make a boot disk or usb drive.  i used unetbootin
[http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/)
read through the instructions first and make a boot disk or usb.  i like the usb.  then you will need to change your boot options in your bios to boot from the disk or usb that you just made.
