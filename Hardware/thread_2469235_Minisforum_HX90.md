### Post by Shibblet on 2021-11-23
Just got a [Minisforum HX90]("https://store.minisforum.com/products/hx90") to replace my well aged Core i7-3770.

I purchased the "Barebones" Version, and added my own drives.  Samsung 980 M.2, Samsung 870 QVO, and Western Digital 2TB, and Crucial Ballistix 3200 (32g).

First thing, was to load up Kubuntu 21.10, and I was immediately impressed by the speed and usability right from the LiveUSB.

I have only been using this thing for about a week, but it runs like a top.  I am currently running Kubuntu 21.10.

Since I am not much of a gamer anymore, the built in Vega 8 Graphics (2100mhz) are not as good as my old GTX780ti... but they work well enough for most of the titles I feel like playing.

I still cannot get [Hardware Decoding]("https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2468995") in the browser, for services like Stadia or GeForce now, but it uses ***significantly*** less CPU cycles to decode the streams, and that keeps me from getting the same amount of lag I was before.

All-in-all, this is a ***fantastic*** little Linux PC.
