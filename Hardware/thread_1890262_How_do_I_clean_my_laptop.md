### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-12-05
Every laptop is different -- no one here is going to be able to tell you step-by-step how to disassemble it to clean it, and them reassemble it.

You need to find the make and model -- and then search the Internet to see if there is a service manual available.  Those generally include such instructions.

Usually, about all you really need to do regularly with a laptop is use one of those cans containing compressed air and spray it into the fan slots to keep the fan blades clean of gunk.
