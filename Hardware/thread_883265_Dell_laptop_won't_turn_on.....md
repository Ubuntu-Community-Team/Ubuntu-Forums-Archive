### Post by starcannon on 2008-08-09
51xx series are notorious overheaters, there's reams of information about the overheating issue on the dell forums (both linux and windows)

With luck the CPU is still fine. I have one of these series, I haven't had your particular issue yet, but some things I'd try first are:

make sure heat sink fins are free of debris and dirt
clear cmos
check for blown capacitors
check thermal compound (known issue on these models)
test or get tested the psu
reseat any and all cards, ram, cpu, vid card, wifi, etc...
try another hdd

those are listed in the order I'd go at it.
The dell forums are excellent as well, be sure to look in the windows forums there as well, if its a hardware issue they can be quite valuable even though your running linux.
