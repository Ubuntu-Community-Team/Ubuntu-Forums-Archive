### Post by Handssolow on 2011-11-09
Anyone else had problems with a SyncMaster 940mw LCD monitor? Mine takes ages (well over a minute) to wake up from hibernation. Internet searches suggest the likely cause is a problem with the power supply board's capacitors.

Normally after shut down I leave the monitor on but for once after reading the reports I switched off the monitor using the remote. I thought I'd never get it back on. Eventually after a several minutes it was back on and it's staying on until I've done the repair.
