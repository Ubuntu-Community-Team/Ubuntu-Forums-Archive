### Post by anewguy on 2011-09-27
If you just want to disable the tap-to-click feature this can be done with one of the touchpad configuration tools, such as synaptiks that you will find in the ubuntu software center.  While I haven't looked at it yet, it may have an option to totally disable your touchpad as well.  Of course you would need an external mouse if you totally disable the touchpad.
