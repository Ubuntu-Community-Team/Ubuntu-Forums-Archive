### Post by stretch427 on 2009-05-13
Well my first assumption would be that you maybe burned it too fast.  Try lowering the speed to like 8x or 4x.  Before you do that though, make sure the file type your burning is readable by whatever machine your wanting to put it on. And another way to test if its your burner is if you burn it again, just write a simple text document, "test" and burn that also. If it shows up its probably a compatibility issue. I don't want to recommend burning though because I have burned through soo many cd's trying to it right once. Try USB transfer. quicker cheaper.
