I am no expert on brand-based performance review, but I can give you an engineer's perspective...

Can you watch online videos? If so what definition?... This will give you a good idea of the video decoding capabilities of your PC for watching online content. As for using your tuner,I think it's a good card that can reduce CPU load (as the good ones preforms MPEG/J-PEG encoding and having an additional onboard processor for things like demodulation etc). Just one question.... are you using it to watch cable or digital or any HD content?

Although I'm no expert on AMD's performance ratings......your processor is not small (65nm SOI is a larger manufacturing process). It has a 2.2 GHz clock speed and supports Dual-Channel SRAMs. But it is a bit short on Cache, might run slightly warm and has an older socket model....all of which might reduce performance. If you want to up your PC's performance add a Dual Channel RAM kit (and if possible, assuming the motherboard supports the socket...change your CPU as well!!!)

The main problem is it might be just lacking that little bit of extra horsepower needed for watching Digital TV or HD.
