### Post by CdK1 on 2009-07-01
Si tienes Nvidia puedes usar  [B]nvidia-settings.

[/B]ó

Haz una copia de tu xorg.conf y edita la sección "Screen" de la siguiente manera:

**Section “Device”**
Identifier      “TARJETA&#8243;
Driver          “nvidia” 

Option         “AllowGLXWithComposite” “False”
Option         “CursorShadow” “on”
 Option      “RenderAccel” “False”
Option          “TwinView”
Option          “TwinViewOrientation” “Clone”
Option          “SecondMonitorHorizSync” “31.5&#8243;
Option          “SecondMonitorVertRefresh” “50-70&#8243;
Option          “MetaModes” “1280,1024,800×600&#8243;
Option          “ConnectedMonitor” “CRT, TV”
Option          “TVStandard” “PAL-NC” 
Option          “TVOutFormat” “COMPOSITE”
**EndSection**
