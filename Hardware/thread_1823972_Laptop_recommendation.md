### Post by Patrickdlg on 2011-08-17
I recently purchased a Gazelle Professional and highly recommend it.  It works right out of the box, and you don't have to worry about hardware not being compatible or lacking drivers.  I ordered mine with an i7-2630QM, 8GB DDR3 at 1333 MHz, and the 120 GB SATA 3.0 SSD.  It is the most powerful/quickest Ubuntu laptop that I have ever used, and I can't imagine that the i5 would have a significantly lower performance for most tasks.  It has a nice chiclet keyboard and the casing is fingerprint resistant and almost sandpaper-like (soft though).  The two-finger scrolling and clicking is a nice touch.  The one downside to this laptop is that it is relatively heavy (6.83 lbs.).

Also, I have only heard good things about their customer service, and my laptop got to me a day before it was supposed to!  Also, it comes with a free, plain, black case.  Not the best case, but it holds the laptop and all of its cables.

I recommend this laptop if you are serious about using Ubuntu as your primary OS.  Hope this review helps.
