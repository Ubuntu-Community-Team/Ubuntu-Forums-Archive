### Post by sandy8925 on 2010-06-20
first you have to pair the phone and laptop.open the bluetooth settings or preferences and check the 'make this computer visible' box. then search for the laptop from the phone and try to pair them. after that you should be able to send files to the phone using the 'send files to device' option.
