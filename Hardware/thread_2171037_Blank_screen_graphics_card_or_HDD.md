### Post by wripley on 2013-08-29
Sounds like a graphics card issue, unless the whole motherboard is going (that is an integrated chip right?)  It could also be an Xconf problem.  If the hard drive was failing, some sort of message would pop up on the screen and there would at least be some startup screens or something.  Does it go straight to black and stay there?
