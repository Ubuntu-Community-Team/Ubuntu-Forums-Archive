### Post by kpatz on 2008-09-03
That sounds more like a dying fan, or a fan with something caught in it than a hard drive.

Hard drives usually make a higher-pitched whine when the bearings go.  Chances are, a hard drive making the sound in your recording would be beyond dead.

Can you see the fan if you flip the laptop over?

And fans do stop when in suspend, at least on laptops.
