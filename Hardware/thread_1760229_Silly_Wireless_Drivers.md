Are you sure about that? [The wikipedia page for the HP Mini 1000]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HP_Mini_1000") seems to think there is an ethernet port, and my HP Mini 110 has one but it's hidden. On mine, there's a plastic cover over the ethernet port. Have a look on the right-hand edge, nearest the screen, next to the two USB ports.
