### Post by roshanjose on 2009-02-10
you have three options....

1) you have a cancelling button on the printer.. when you press it it cancels....I have this feature...dont know whether you have it


2) when you start the printing....you see the printer on the top panel..just click it and cancel the job

3) press Alt+F2 and type gnome-system-monitor and click "Run"  and kill the printing process....usually some cup process if i am not wrong
