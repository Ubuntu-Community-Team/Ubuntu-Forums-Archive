### Post by TheSurak on 2009-07-15
I am using (K)ubuntu 9.04, now. This problem happened again (it happened with 8.04, too).

I noticed that lsmod showed no wl (sta driver module). Also, the only thing that changed recently was an update manager's update. Luckily, this time I saw that a new kernel version was part of the update.

The simple solution is to choose the previous kernel version in the grub menu. You may also wish to edit /boot/grub/menu.lst and change the default choice.

The guys apparently forgot to add wl when compiling the kernel.
