### Post by tschew on 2009-04-29
Webcams are usually accessed using the video 4 linux interface. Many webcams do not work with linux and are not expected to work in the near future due to a lack of cooperation from the hardware vendors. 

On the other hand, there are drivers for numerous webcams available, many with excellent support for the various features. The UVC driver is probably the best of the bunch. I've had great results with a quickcam communicate deluxe which works out of the box. (including skype video conferencing)

Here's a [list]("http://linux-uvc.berlios.de/#devices") of supported devices for the UVC driver.
