### Post by khelben1979 on 2009-07-19
It seems that [SpinRite]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spinrite") is able to fix bad sectors even on a Linux disk. LinuxJournal wrote an article on this in 2004. [Here it is]("http://www.linuxjournal.com/article/7684").

So SpinRite isn't a Linux native application, but it is able to fix the disk.
