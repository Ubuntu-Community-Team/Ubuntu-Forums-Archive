### Post by Dark_Stang on 2008-07-12
In Add/Remove Applications install ATI Catalyst Control Center. Through there he can manage his laptop screen, and other screens he wants to hook up with VGA. Under linux when you want to hook up an extra monitor you normally have to log out and log back in to restart the X windows system. 

I'd recommend he set it up as a seperate monitor rather than mirroring, that way stuff doesn't get stretched out.
