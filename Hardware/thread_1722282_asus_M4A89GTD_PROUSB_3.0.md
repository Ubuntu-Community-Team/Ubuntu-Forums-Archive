### Post by Kir_B on 2011-04-15
According to the [AMD Catalyst™ 11.3 Release Notes]("http://support.amd.com/us/kbarticles/Pages/AMDCatalyst113ReleaseNotes.aspx"), Our onboard graphics (Radeon HD 4290) are supported by [this driver]("http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/linux/Pages/radeon_linux.aspx?type=2.7&product=2.7.4.3.5.3.1&lang=English") (this is the one that I previously mentioned).

We're still waiting for Canonical to upgrade/update the drivers for us,  before we take the leap to the proper proprietary drivers, as that way,  the drivers will be dealt with automatically when updating is required. 

Until then, we're still using the open source drivers, which have been  good enough for compiz and the few games that we play (Warzone2100,  Neverputt, Neverball, and UFO: AI).

I hope that helps you somewhat, but, if your unsure, remember to read the instructions for installing the drivers very carefully, and everything should work out alright.

Best of luck to you, and be sure to post your progress for everyone else's benefit.

Kirby
