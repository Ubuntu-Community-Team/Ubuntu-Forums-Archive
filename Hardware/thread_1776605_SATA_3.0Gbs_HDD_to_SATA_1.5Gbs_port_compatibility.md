### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2011-06-06
The SATA controllers that were infamous for needing the manual jumper setting were older than your chipset (ICH9). Chipsets like nForce2/3, VIA 8237, and ICH 4/5/6 are often culprits for that issue. I can't guarantee you it will work, but I would go for it if I was in your position.
