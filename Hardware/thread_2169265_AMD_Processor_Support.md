### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-08-21
I use an AMD 6-core processor with Ubuntu 12.10 and Mint 15 and it works fine.  As in MS Windows, it uses all the cores.

As to video drivers, AMD has a nasty habit of terminating Linux driver support when their video chipsets are still in cards that are on the shelf.  My PC is only two years old and the video chipset (AMD 4290) was dropped from AMD support last summer.

I've also seen lots of posts in this forum about folks having problems with the newer AMD video cards and with the newer APUs -- but not having either, I can't provide specifics.
