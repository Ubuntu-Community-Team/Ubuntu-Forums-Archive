### Post by yelvington on 2009-12-30
Suggestion:

* Install Google Chrome [http://www.google.com/chrome?platform=linux](http://www.google.com/chrome?platform=linux)
* Add YouTube HTML5-ifier extension [https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/kchoimdlcbapmcdnheaahjcdpdjdpfco](https://chrome.google.com/extensions/detail/kchoimdlcbapmcdnheaahjcdpdjdpfco)

This will replace the Flash player on most YouTube pages with a native HTML5 embedded video element. The Flash player is the primary culprit in stuttering videos on netbooks.
