### Post by monggnom on 2009-11-09
^_^ Finally, I found the way to fix this problem by downgrade "network-manager" to 9.04 jaunty versions. please see detail as bleow

1) download below package from [http://packages.ubuntu.com/](http://packages.ubuntu.com/)  by search in jaunty version
       libnm-glib0_0.7.1~rc4.1.cf199a964-0ubuntu2_i386.deb
       network-manager_0.7.1~rc4.1.cf199a964-0ubuntu2_i386.deb
       network-manager-gnome_0.7.1~rc4.1-0ubuntu2_i386.deb
2) dpkg --install --force-downgrade  libnm-glib0_0.7.1~rc4.1.cf199a964-0ubuntu2_i386.deb network-manager_0.7.1~rc4.1.cf199a964-0ubuntu2_i386.deb network-manager-gnome_0.7.1~rc4.1-0ubuntu2_i386.deb

3) go to synptic, search for above package and "Lock Version" (under "Package" menu) 

4) Enjoy to surve internet by your Sierra Aircard 775 ^_^"
