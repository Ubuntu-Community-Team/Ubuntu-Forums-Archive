### Post by SeijiSensei on 2019-11-26
Sounds like it's treating the TV as a second display. You can fix that in the Display settings.  

Also you might need to change the audio settings to use HDMI rather than the local speakers.  

Do a little browsing in System Settings, and I bet you can fix both these problems.  If there's no obvious solution to the audio problem, install "pavucontrol" which will let you select output channels.
