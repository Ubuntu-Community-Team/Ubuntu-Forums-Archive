### Post by Foxcow on 2010-12-19
I was kind of in the same situation you were in a few months ago but all of the reading I did indicated that clone/re-image to an SSD is not conducive to good performance.  Most of the stuff that I read stated that there was an alignment process that took place during installations.  Being in your situation, I sprang for new installs of everything.


That being said, you can very easily and quickly clone and image your new drive with Clonezilla.  Its free and fast.
