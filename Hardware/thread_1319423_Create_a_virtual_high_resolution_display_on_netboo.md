### Post by Cheltspy on 2009-11-08
Most Netbooks have an external monitor function key which can flip to dual mode. By setting a resolution higher than laptop screen gives you this ability.
 
However, you may need to connect an external monitor to do this and I am not sure Linux drivers will support this.
