### Post by thelugnut on 2009-04-01
I found an answer to my question.

[URL="http://www.land-of-kain.de/docs/sony_vaio_sz61_mnb[/URL]

> Synaptics Touchpad

Something that always annoys me with touchpads is the "one touch means click" behaviour, which encourages weird mouse clicks while typing. But this is curable (thanks to Peter Österlund from Xorg):
Peter is the maintainer for the Synaptics for XFree86/XOrg; his site contains a lot more useful tips.

I opened nautilus (gksudo nautilus) and navigated to /etc/X11/xorg.conf.
I then entered the following:

```
Section "InputDevice"
        Identifier      "Synaptics Touchpad"
        # ...
        # To avoid tap counting as click:
        Option          "MaxTapTime"            "0"

```
    

It worked for me. Thanks a lot to the originator.
