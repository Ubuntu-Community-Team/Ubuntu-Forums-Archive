### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-08-30
LaCie makes some rugged external drives.  Monster products are known to be cheapest bidder, so what it looks like on the outside does not match what is on the inside.  Take any Monster product and pull it apart--cables, headphones, you name it.  This is not the company from the 1980's which did make quality speaker cables, not plastic filler found in today's products.

Read the reviews on Amazon for popular external hard disks.  You will find a better price-to-quality correlation.
