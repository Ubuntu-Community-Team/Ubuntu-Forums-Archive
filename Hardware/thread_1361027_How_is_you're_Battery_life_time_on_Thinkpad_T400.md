### Post by tgalati4 on 2009-12-21
You're lucky to get 3-4 hours.  I only get 2 hours with a 6-cell on a T43p.  It's at 77% capacity, so it's not comparable to a new 9-cell.  Simple math tells me a new 6-cell will be 2.5 hours.  Adding 3 more cells will give me about 3.75 hours.  So I would say that is nominal battery performance.

Post your power usage using the Gnome Power Manager power history.  If you can keep it under 15 watts you are doing well.  My machine usually runs around 17 watts with all the powertop tweaks.

Not sure what windows does, but it tends to give you 10 to 25% better battery life.
