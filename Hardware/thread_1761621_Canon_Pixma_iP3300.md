### Post by FriscoSoxFan on 2011-05-18
I'm a complete newbie, but I spent a couple of hours on this last night and the solution is so simple (and not mentioned anywhere else that I could find) that I wanted to get it on a forum somewhere for others.

There are various instructions out there for configuring an ip3300 printer that involve downloading international RPMs, converting them to DEBs, then installing them. I could not get any of them working on Ubuntu 11.04 and the files mentioned to download on the Canon japan FTP server were no longer there.

Through a bunch of research, I learned that the print head on the ip3300 is the same as on the ip4300. The ip4300 drivers are out-of-the box on 11.04 and work great.

install the printer and select that model number and you are good to go. Easy-Peasy.
