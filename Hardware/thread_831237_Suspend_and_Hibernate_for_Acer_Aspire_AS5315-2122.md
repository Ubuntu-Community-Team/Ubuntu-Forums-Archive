### Post by ozymo on 2008-06-16
I would suggest that a lot of it will have to do with your video card.  It seems that lots of people (including me) have suspend problems if using ATI video cards and the fglrx drivers and desktop effects.

I have successfully in the past (until a kernel upgrade recently unfixed it) resolved the issue on my Gateway MT3705 which uses an ATI Radeon M200 XPRESS card.

A quick search around these forums, and particularly through Google (use goosh - it's more linux friendly), will yield plenty of hours of trial and error.  Basically, it boils down to trying things out until you find something that works, and then sticking with it.  You'll likely get more helpful responses if you post some more hardware specifics, especially the video card.  But do the search first - that's going to be the best place to begin.
