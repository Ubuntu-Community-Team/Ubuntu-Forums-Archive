### Post by snowpine on 2013-02-10
Welcome to the forums!

I recommend that you upgrade your hardware. A good minimum spec is 1ghz processor with 1gb RAM and a decent video card. You can read more here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements)

With your current hardware, the only version of Ubuntu that *might* work (but it will be very slow) is [Lubuntu]("http://lubuntu.net").
