Hi guys just wanted to say I haven't used Ubuntu since dapper drake, but out of the blue decided I'd try out the latest version. I had installed dapper drake on an older laptop, but wanted to try 8.1 out on my newest laptop, so, of course I got all the ndisswrapper stuff ready to enable wifi and such. But wow, this version really rocked. Wifi didn't work right away, but a popup on my "taskbar" showed up saying i just had to unrestrict the drive inorder to use it, so i unrestricted it and... it worked! I didnt have to open up the terminal or anything! Another great thing is the media keys work, if you have a dv6000 or mayb even some other HP builds, you'll know what i mean. the media keys work just like they would in vista! without adding any drivers or anything! all in all this is a great build, and i think im gonna stick with it!
