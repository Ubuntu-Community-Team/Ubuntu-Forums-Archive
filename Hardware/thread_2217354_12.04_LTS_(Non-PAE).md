### Post by mikewhatever on 2014-04-17
From the [Xubuntu 12.04 release notes]("http://xubuntu.org/news/12-04-release/"):
> 
Release notes

    The i386 images use a non-PAE kernel. The non-PAE kernel will not be available in future Xubuntu releases.
...

...and there you have it.
So, to recap, Xubuntu and Lubuntu 12.04 were released with the non-pae kernel, and Ubuntu 12.04 with pae. The repositories for 12.04 contain both kinds of 3.2 kernels, maintained till Apr 2017.
Also, check out [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PAE](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PAE).
