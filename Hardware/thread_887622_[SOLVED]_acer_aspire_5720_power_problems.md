### Post by isparkes on 2008-10-21
It is the problem that the fan is not controlled correctly after resume from hibernate. It's a problem with the BIOS, so:

Update your BIOS to V1.40 or above!

It is a problem that Acer is aware of (they package a driver with Windows that controls the fan - if you reinstall with a clean Windows or Ubuntu, this driver is not installed).

There are a number of other threads about this.
