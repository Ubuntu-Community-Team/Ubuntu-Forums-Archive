### Post by stefangr1 on 2009-02-24
What you could do is build a system like:
- IO-board with energy efficient chipset (like AM780G)
- energy efficient amd X2 (like) 4450e
- 4 GB ram
- 2x sata harddisk of 500GB (there are also some low energy drives like the samsung ecogreen, which uses only 3W)
- consider an energy efficient psu (corsair has some that just came out)

When you keep everything as simple as possible, this would cost only just over 300 euro's, at an idle power consumption of ~40W.
