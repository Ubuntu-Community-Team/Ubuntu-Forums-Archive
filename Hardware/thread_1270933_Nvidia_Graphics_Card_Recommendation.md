### Post by cbope on 2009-09-20
A 9800GT 512MB should fit within your budget quite easily. A 512MB version should be ok for most games, but get a 1GB card if possible, it will be a bit more future-proof.

Even better would be a GTS250, again go for a 1GB model if possible. At least where I live (Finland), the GTS card is only marginally more expensive than the 9800GT (~10%).

If you will be using a 1680x1050 or lower res display, the 512MB models should be fine in most cases with most games. The 1GB models will allow a bit more headroom for future games and allow higher resolutions like 1920 x 1200/1080.
