### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-10-24
There is a major problem here. You don't see anything in the Additional Drivers, because Intel does not provide a working Linux driver for gma500, and, as the time passes and the problem gets older, it's pretty safe to assume that it never will. You can find various explanations and guesses on the web as to why that is the case. Check out the wiki page and the gma500 magathread for info on how to proceed:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsVideoCardsPoulsbo/](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsVideoCardsPoulsbo/)
