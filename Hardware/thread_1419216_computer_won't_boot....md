### Post by oldfred on 2010-03-01
About a year ago I was using the desktop and heard a loud pop but it kept working. Next morning my wife could could not boot it. It seemed to be bricked. I suspected power supply or motherboard but it turned out to be video card (capacitors with slightly open ends). But motherboard would not even power up without good vid card. Since you said server you may not have a separate vid card but newer motherboards will not boot unless everything seems ok.
