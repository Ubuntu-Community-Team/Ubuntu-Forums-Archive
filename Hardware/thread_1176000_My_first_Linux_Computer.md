### Post by Steelmourne on 2009-06-01
For a general pc that can do everything but is slower for video encoding you would be looking at around $1500. For video encoding $1800. 

As for the specs, the mobo would be an GA-EP45-DS3L LGA 775. Supports Core 2 and Core 2 Quad. 2GB of PC-6400 RAM is enough. Graphics card would be a 9500GT. PSU equivalent to 750W for future upgrades. 

If you are not doing any video encoding, then a E8400 cpu is fine. For encoding a Q9400 is enough. Then choose a case and dvd burner and screen. This PC would last for a while with Ubuntu. Hope this helps!
