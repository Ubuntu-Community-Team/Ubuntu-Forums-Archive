I have had some problems with beaglebone black and ubuntu, debian works ok so I'm told.
One thing with the beaglebone is the hardware developer is active on support boards, Gerald is passionate about what he does and involved with support.

MK802 make sure you get the HDMI protection built in as earlier models it was omitted.

you may wish to look at cubieboard / 2 as it has sata- needs enabling
[https://groups.google.com/forum/#!topic/cubieboard/g_xLWzoIfzs](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!topic/cubieboard/g_xLWzoIfzs)

 oxdroid u2  or x2 by hardkernel  quad core, make sure you get eMMC add on board as it improves performance over sd card if running off  flash

really depends what you want to do but android sticks have disappointed me in real world performance mali 400 I personally am not keen on
