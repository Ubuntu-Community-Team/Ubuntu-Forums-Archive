If you're willing to give Canon another chance, I have a Canoscan LiDE 220 that works flawlessly, including surviving Ubuntu updates from 16 something to 18.10.  Added bonus is that they're inexpensive.  Build quality isn't the absolutely best, but mine works well enough.  Plug 'n Play.
