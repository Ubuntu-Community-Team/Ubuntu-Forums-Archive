### Post by Ng Oon-Ee on 2008-12-15
More than a minute seems fine to me. Are you timing from a cold boot (pressing the power button)?

You could always use preload, there's a guide somewhere around these forums to that. It sped up my boot time (cold boot) from over 2 minutes to 1 minute 30 seconds on average. I don't find that very long, just enough time for me to hang my coat up, plug my external keyboard in, and sit down comfortably.

The next version (Jaunty Jackalope) is rumoured to have MUCH faster boot/shutdown, as well. I wouldn't recommend installing the alphas yet though (has it gone to beta?) unless you're prepared for the instability and bug-hunts.
