### Post by DigitalRonin on 2008-11-04
Solved.

Edit /etc/X11/xorg.conf

Find the Screen section - mine looked like this;


> 

Section "Screen"
	Identifier	"Configured Screen Device"
	Device	"Configured Video Device"
	SubSection "Display"
		Virtual	2384 768
	EndSubSection
EndSection




Delete the "Virtual* line and save the file. Then logout to restart xorg. I had my external monitor plugged in, at this point, which is almost certainly necessary.

After xorg restarts, go to the screen resolution applet and (in my case), the correct resolutions appear.

HTH

David
