### Post by Enigma12 on 2014-10-10
At least, according to your bean count, you are, like me, fairly new to Linux. I have a different Lexmark printer that sorta mostly works correctly with my Lubuntu computer, but I have yet to get it fully reliable. When I Googled your printer and added "linux driver" I got a link to a page that will likely discourage you. See the bottom line. 

[http://support.lexmark.com/index?pmv=print&page=content&productCode=&segment=SUPPORT&userlocale=EN_UK&id=RE108](http://support.lexmark.com/index?pmv=print&page=content&productCode=&segment=SUPPORT&userlocale=EN_UK&id=RE108)
