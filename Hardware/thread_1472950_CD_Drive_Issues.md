### Post by dargaud on 2011-06-20
Two observations:
- CD players have lots of moving parts and as such have only a limited lifespan. Replacements are cheap (and now come in SATA). Just spend 10$ instead of wasting 20 hours on the problem.

- I once had all kind of strange problems on a CD player after an upgrade. The large IDE connector had actually come partly unplugged when I'd previously opened the case.
