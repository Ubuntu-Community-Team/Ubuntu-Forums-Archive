### Post by MeURi on 2008-05-27
ASUS does not provide drivers for linux

BUT you can use official drivers for your video card, as to say nVIDIA or Ati (and I always prefer official drivers :-))

Install the correct drivers for your video adapter through synaptic, enable them (you should be prompted whether to enable these or not), and reboot to get them loaded and, hopefully, working

Feel free to ask if you have any problems doing the steps above
