### Post by gsgleason on 2010-05-14
I just wanted to share my experience after upgrading to 10.04 and trying out the nouveau driver instead of the proprietary nvidia driver I'd be using.  I have a thinkpad R61 with nvidia NVS 140M discrete graphics.

After the switch, many things are better.  Graphics initialize a LOT faster, resulting in faster screen wake up after opening the lid and faster session screen after logging out.  The slash screen is sharper and more crisp, web pages scroll smoother.  It's a better overall experience.

The only thing that is less good is full screen hi-res internet vids, but it's certainly the lesser of the evils.

That is all.
