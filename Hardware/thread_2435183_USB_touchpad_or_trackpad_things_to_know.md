### Post by CelticWarrior on 2020-01-17
The vast majority of  touchpads work without any user installed drivers. The rule, as always, is to install a current distro release for new hardware.

Typically the hardware vendor of touchpads isn't disclosed in the laptop's specifications. The trick is to check for Windows drivers at the manufacturers' websites as sometimes - but not always - they show the hardware vendor in the installation file.
