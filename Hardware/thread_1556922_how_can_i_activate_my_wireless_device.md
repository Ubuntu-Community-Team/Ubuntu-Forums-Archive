Hello Friends........

There should be a switch on the front of the laptop, on the edge.  It  should be a swich that is two-directional.  There will be some pictures,  one with a computer, and one with a computer with some waves coming out  from it.  Switch the switch to the side of the computer with the waves,  and you will activate wireless internet.  Follow the on-screen  directions and you will receive the internet.
