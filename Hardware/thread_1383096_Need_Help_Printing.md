### Post by sgosnell on 2010-01-16
Anything is possible, I guess, but I've never seen an HP or Brother that wouldn't print under Ubuntu.  HP can be a little problematic under XP, at least for scanning, but in general printing in Linux is far easier and more dependable than in Windows.  There are a couple or three manufacturers that sell non-standard equipment, like Canon and some others, but Linux will print to any standard printer out of the box, pretty much.
