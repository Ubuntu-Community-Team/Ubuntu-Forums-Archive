### Post by BatteryKing on 2010-07-03
Don't buy this board and don't buy Gigabyte, period.  Here are the reasons why:
1. Friends who bought Gigabyte boards all had them die in 1 years time.
2. Support is a sham.  You send defective board to them, they send it back to you, no repairs done.  Thought you had a 3 year warranty, well not really.

GA-EX58-UD5 specific:
3. Ethernet ports cop out pretty quick and never quite work again.
4. Unstable even at stock speeds.
5. Trouble detecting expansion cards consistently.
6. Trouble detection boot devices consistently.
7. Mine in particular completely failed at detecting the boot device after 1 year and 4 months.

In comparison with my EVGA replacement board with all components moved over from GA-EX58-UD5 board to the EVGA board:
3. Ethernet ports work fine.  No problems.
4. 100% stable at stock speeds.
5. Same expansion cards detect fine 100% of the time.
6. Boot device detects properly every time.
7. Only time will tell, be so far so good.
