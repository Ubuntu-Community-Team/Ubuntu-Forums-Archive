### Post by Kevbert on 2008-10-29
Most bluetooth USB adapters are virtually plug and play so you should not have any problems.  If you can you want to go for one that supports bluetooth version 2.0. I'm using a Belkin F8T003 version 2 and as soon as I connect to the PC bluetooth is enabled.  Your problem is more likely to be the devices that you try to connect via bluetooth such as phones which may not support data transfer to and from the phone (eg HTC Lobster, but all Sony Ericsson phones I've used work fine).
