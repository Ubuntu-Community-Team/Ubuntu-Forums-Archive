### Post by Autodave on 2018-04-19
Be careful!  Dell LOVES to use proprietary hardware!  Proprietary means that it is different from others: made just by Dell. A lot of Dell machines use ZIF (zero insertion force) cabling on their HDs. 

If that is the case on your particular machine, you will have to buy an adapter to adapt from ZIF to SATA.
