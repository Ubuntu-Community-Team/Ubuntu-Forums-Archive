### Post by NFblaze on 2010-07-21
Do this.

Burn an Ubuntu LiveCD.
Take it to whatever shop that has laptops on display. 
Insert CD into the computer.
Reboot.
Test it out.
If anyone gives you any trouble tell them you are thinking of buying a laptop, but that where you work you run Linux and have to make sure it's compatible.

Do not take the word of the employees. As most of them have probrably never even used Linux. 

That being said I have a Samsung R480 and that works perfectly with Ubuntu (except for some function keys). The graphics card is not namebrand and is integrated Intel HD but that was a budgeting issue. Also, the HP Elitebook series run Ubuntu well.
