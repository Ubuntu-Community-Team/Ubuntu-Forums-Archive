### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2023-04-27
PSU's usually have standby power. They stay on to keep the CMOS battery charged and such. You may have a sensor in the mouse going bad. It's no big deal, unless you're trying to conserve every last mW of electricity, in which case, you should unplug it.
