### Post by AshNova on 2013-04-20
I've managed to get the printer working but not the scanner. Anyway here's what I did :

When I installed Steam, I noticed a lot of my 12.04.02 xorg packages had been downgraded. 

So I removed Steam did autoremove and did this :

```

sudo apt-get install xserver-xorg-lts-quantal linux-generic-lts-quantal

```

Besides reverting my packages to their previous state, it also upgraded my kernel version from 3.2 something to 3.5.0-27-generic.

Then I installed the printing driver for the XP-100 from [http://www.openprinting.org/printer/Epson/Epson-XP-100_Series]("http://www.openprinting.org/printer/Epson/Epson-XP-100_Series") and installed CUPS via synaptic.

I think you can also get the linux printing and scanning driver from this website when you search under "XP-100" and "Linux" :
[URL="http://download.ebz.epson.net/dsc/search/01/search/searchModuleFromResult"]
http://download.ebz.epson.net/dsc/search/01/search/searchModuleFromResult[/URL]

However even after I've installed the scanning driver, associated data file and app, I can't get the my laptop to detect the scanner part of the printer as anything other than "Epson unknown".

Will update if I get the scanner to work.
