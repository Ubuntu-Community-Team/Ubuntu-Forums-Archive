### Post by avtolle on 2009-02-13
Since I run ppc Ubuntu, the support is provided by the community, as I understand is the situation with Debian. Intrepid runs great on my G4 Cubes, obviously with a healthy RAM upgrade. 

The OP might want to take a look at Yellow Dog Linux, as it is specifically and "officially" for ppc architecture; openSUSE has a ppc "port" with good support; and, as I recall, there is a Fedora ppc version. I'm with the OP on one thing: these Cubes appear rock solid, and give no indication the hardware is ready to "give it up" anytime soon, as it appears is the case with the G5 box he's posted about. If the OP is interested, he might lurk over at the Apple Users Forum to see what's going on over there with the ppc community.
