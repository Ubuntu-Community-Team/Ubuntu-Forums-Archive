### Post by Greedo on 2008-05-11
I seemed to have fixed my problem (and I feel a little silly after finding out what it was) 

After rebooting into my window OS to print a page I tried printing a page and ran into an issue - but this time there was a status message both on the screen and on the printer, that the Y cartridge was empty. (and if you've ever had one of these brother printers their one possible downfall is that they'll stop printing if a single one of the CMYK cartridges doesn't have enough ink for it's liking) 


SO: 
No rollback was needed, just a simple Y cartridge change and rememebring to stick to the KISS principal

Just thought I'd post my solution in case anyone else happens to run into this problem
