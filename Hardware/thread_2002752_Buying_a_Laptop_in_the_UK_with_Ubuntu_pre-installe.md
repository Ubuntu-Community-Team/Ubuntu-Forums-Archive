### Post by SilentMute on 2012-06-19
I have a few different solutions depending on what you want to do:

First off you can buy a windows pc, purge it of the evil, and then install ubuntu on it, this is the easiest, but also the most expensive option.

Secondly, you can search for people who are selling laptops, often people will sell computers well below price because they have a broken OS that they do not know how to fix. some such sites are craiglist.com (a site that connects users with local sellers) and ebay.com (it's ebay). This is potentially the cheapest option, but it also carries a fair amount of risk as sometimes sellers will lie about issues and/or will not know about them.

Thirdly, if you really want a custom experience without the taint of Windows, you can build your own computer, I personally have built 2 computers to run ubuntu on and it's a breeze, but laptops are a little harder to do. This is the most involved option, but it's the most fun.
