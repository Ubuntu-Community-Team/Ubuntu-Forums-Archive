### Post by rmadla on 2009-11-07
The very last post in this thread solved the problem for me:
[http://support.zenwalk.org/viewtopic.php?f=28&t=24764](http://support.zenwalk.org/viewtopic.php?f=28&t=24764)

Specifically, I used these commands:

sudo chmod 700 /usr/lib/cups/backend/cups-pdf
sudo chmod 700 /usr/lib/cups/backend

... and then all was well.
(I had earlier tried uninstalling and reinstalling cups-pdf, and so whether that step was also required or not, I don't know.)
