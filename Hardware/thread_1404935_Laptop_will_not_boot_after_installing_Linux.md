(Usually you have to hit "DEL" or "F12" during the boot post to get there) If you cannot or don't even see the boot post, your system is dead. You may have an issue with the NVIDIA chip, google for "NVIDIA heat issues".

This would emphasize a possible NVidia chip issue, which often starts with intermittent failures (black screen, lines on the screen, sudden system freezes). Please do yourself a failure and read up on it. If your notebook has one of the faulty chips, there is a good chance that there is a warranty extension for that problem in place - google is your friend here.

Any problem that keeps you from getting into BIOS cannot be OS-related. You do have a hardware problem of some kind.
