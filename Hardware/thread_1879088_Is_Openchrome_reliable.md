### Post by bcschmerker on 2011-11-10
In fact my hybrid Everex®, prior to the three rounds of hardware upgrades that I have performed thereon as of November 2011, was a stock TC2502 ([VIA® pc2500-based system](http://www.via.com.tw/en/initiatives/empowered/pc2500_mainboard/index.jsp):  1.6GHz VIA® C7-D CPU, CN700 chipset, UniChrome GPU) initially running a now-orphaned distro (Kernel 2.6.22-generic) based on XUbuntu® 7.10 Gutsy.  (Everex Systems is now a property of First International Computer, Inc., of Taiwan; Good OS LLC went bankrupt in 2009.)

The LinUX Kernel Organization already supports the Unichrome GPU in all Kernels 2.6 and 3.0; X.org Foundation already has UniChrome servers (package: xserver-xorg-unichrome) in the Ubuntu® Main Repositories for all supported Releases.
