### Post by gallifrey on 2010-05-10
Hi, sorry, I know this is an old thread, but thought I would pipe in with what I hope is useful. Last year, I purchased 3 Seagate 1.5 TB expansion drives, all of which had problems (re: waking up from suspension), and consequently all died clicking noisily. I replaced them with Samsung Story Stations (1 x 1TB and 2 x 1.5 TB), and I have had NO problems whatsoever. I just wish I had opted for them sooner, instead of going the for the cheaper (and disastrous) drives that I initially chose. The Samsung Story Station gets a big thumbs up from me. No problems with the power management, and they have been resilient with the tough regime I put them through. 

 Hope that helps. :D
