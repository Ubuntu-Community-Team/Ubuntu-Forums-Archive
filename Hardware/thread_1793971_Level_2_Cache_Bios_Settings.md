I am running Natty on a Toshiba Dynabook.

In my bios settings under Level 2 cache I can select write-back or write-through. Is one recommended over the other and what exactly does it mean? Does it affect performance?

---

### Post by psusi on 2011-06-30
Write-through means that writes to the cache do not complete until they have also hit main memory, so obviously that is slower than write-back.
