### Post by Diphallia on 2010-01-05
Tried getting a transparent mouse pointer?
Seems like one of the easiest solutions.

Quoted from another forum:
Click System > Preferences > Appearance

Then select your current theme and click the Customize button at the bottom of the window. 

The last tab on the new "Customize Theme" window that pops open is Pointer.

And I suppose you can make your own pointer somehow.
