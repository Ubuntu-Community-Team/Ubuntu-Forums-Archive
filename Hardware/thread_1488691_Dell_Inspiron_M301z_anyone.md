### Post by rx78gp03 on 2010-12-05
> **GerritHiemstra said:**

And being as you have to type function-f2 to type f2, and just f2 for the wifi button, it happens from time to time :( Anyone helping me with this would be greatly appreciated.

Just reverse keyboard function setting in BIOS menu, then it will behave normally as F(n) key :)

> **zool--- said:**
> Is there anyone who can give more detailed reports? How does suspend/hibernate work? How is the overall performance?

I'm using  AMD Athlon II Neo K325 version (dual core 1.3GHz), the overall out-of-box performance under Ubuntu 10.10 is very good, snappy (even with 2GiB of RAM) :D, running compiz at "eyecandy" setting, the performance is still very good. Unfortunately, returning from suspend give me black screen (though the rest is seems normal); and waking from hibernate make beeps and black screen too (then have to force power-down). So no suspend/hibernate for me (set in power management tool), luckily the boot/power-down sequence is fast enough :P
