### Post by p110011 on 2009-04-13
I just wanted to put this up in case any others had this.
I left the cdrom  as the 1st boot-up drive in my bios from when I installed Ubuntu and that caused the light to always be on.

I now just have my hard drive as the only drive bootable and the yellow light blinks normal. That is all.:p
