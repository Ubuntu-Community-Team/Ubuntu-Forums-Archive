### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-10-18
900 Watts is excessive for a single GPU system. The only site I trust to accurately review/rate PSU's is SPCR. See [http://www.silentpcreview.com/article28-page4.html](http://www.silentpcreview.com/article28-page4.html) for real-world examples of how much power systems actually draw and see [http://www.silentpcreview.com/article699-page1.html](http://www.silentpcreview.com/article699-page1.html) for a list of good PSU's.

### Post by swoody on 2008-10-18
Yes, I would agree that the power supply is a bit excessive. On the rig I built I have a AMD X2 4600+, MSI NX8600GT, and a Western Digital 150gb Raptor X. The system is powered by a 300watt psu, and I've never had any issues with power. I would agree with Temujin to use that link he provided to find a better estimate of your power needs.

Also, if you're purchasing anything with a mail-in rebate I would be very hesitant. When I was planning out my setup I read reviews about some companies denying rebates after you send them in. I stayed away from any companies that I read bad reviews about, but still wound up being denied on $80 of rebates for my RAM :(
