### Post by snowpine on 2009-07-05
Ubuntu did not release an lpia version of 8.04; 8.10 was the first. They did, however, develop a special version of 8.04 lpia for the Dell Mini. Personally, I was not impressed with it (I deleted it within 15 minutes of receiving my Mini), but if you search around the 'web I bet you can find a copy for download (it is not legal for me to post a link). Or, if you actually own a Dell Mini, I suspect you can contact Dell directly for a factory restore disk of Ubuntu 8.04 lpia. :)

May I ask why you need lpia? I strongly recommend the "regular" i386 Ubuntu releases over the lpia release (which stinks imho) for Atom-based netbooks.
