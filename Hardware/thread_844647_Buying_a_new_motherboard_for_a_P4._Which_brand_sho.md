### Post by starcannon on 2008-06-29
Stick with brands like Abit, Asus, XFX and you'll be fine, the thing that usually blows (pun intended) on budget boards is the capacitors. In the end you generally get what you pay for. I will say that I have had very good luck with Biostar boards, they tend to be considered a budget board, but for me they have held up very well. My favorite boards are Abit and XFX though.
