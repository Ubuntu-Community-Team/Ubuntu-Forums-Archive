### Post by phidia on 2008-07-08
First type > users in a terminal to see who is on your system.
From [this page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SystemAdministration") check out the approved method of [adding a user]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AddUsersHowto").
I think [this]("http://www.zolved.com/synapse/view_content/28019/How_to_add_an_additional_Super_User_DOSUDO_in_Ubuntu") may be helpful also for your situation.
