### Post by markbuntu on 2008-10-10
Get a AMD quad core but fast dual is better than slow quad for most stuff. A mobo with at least one PCIx16 slot, two if you can afford it, expandable DDR3 ram to 16GB. And all SATA for the drives so 4 or 6 SATA plugs. ATI 48xx graphics card(s), a couple of nice 24inch monitors for native 1080p a 600w p/s, etc, etc....how much do you want to spend???
