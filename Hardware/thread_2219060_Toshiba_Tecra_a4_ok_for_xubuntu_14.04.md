Hello and welcome to the forums.

I'm running Xubuntu 14.04 on a Tecra S3, Pentium M 2.13Mhz, 2GB Ram and a 100GB 7200rpm drive and an nvidia GeForce Go 6600 and its fine. If you've got an SSD in there, you should be fine.
