### Post by blueridgedog on 2009-05-01
Based on my quick search, Nautilus and the ntfs drive do not allow for taking files to trash.  You can create a trash directory on the ntfs formated drive and manually move files there (putting a shortcut on your nautilus bar to make it easy).
