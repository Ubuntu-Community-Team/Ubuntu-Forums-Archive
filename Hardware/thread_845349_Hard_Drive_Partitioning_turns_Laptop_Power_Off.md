### Post by greco8523 on 2008-06-30
It could be an number of problems. The battery ran out? the laptop had power saving and switched itself off. Gparted hanged and the laptop switched off. Whatever the cause I would check your partition if it's still accessible as anything that interrupts a partition edit could damage the partition.
