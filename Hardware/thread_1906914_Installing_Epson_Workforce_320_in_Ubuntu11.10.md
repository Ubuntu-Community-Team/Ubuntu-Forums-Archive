### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-10
I have a couple of Epson printers and installed them both using CUPS.

To access CUPS, open a browser and enter "localhost:631".

Then, go to the Add a printer page -- and when it opens, you should see your printer listed there. IF so, just continue through the pages and add it, including the proper driver.
