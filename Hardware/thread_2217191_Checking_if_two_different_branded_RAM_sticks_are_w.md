### Post by kurt18947 on 2014-04-16
The only difference I'm aware of is rated speed, for example, DDR3-1333 or DDR3-1600.  If mixing pieces with different ratings, both pieces run at the lower speed.  At least that is how it has worked to my knowlege.  I would still try to match the original piece though if possible, I don't know that brand is critical.
