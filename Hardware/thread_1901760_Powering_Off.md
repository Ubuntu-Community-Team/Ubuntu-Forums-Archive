Ever since my last desktop computer, after turning it off (or it had finished putting itself into hibernation) I would wait about 7-10 seconds and turn both the monitor and desktop's plugs off at the wall. ### Post by searchfgold6789 on 2011-12-29
It should be fine.
Most computers, after shutting down completely, will still have residual power running to the motherboard which goes down gradually (over a space of a few seconds). However this power comes from the power supply of the computer, not the wall socket, so if you unplug the computer the power supply will act as it normally would. You won't have to worry about damage to your computer.
