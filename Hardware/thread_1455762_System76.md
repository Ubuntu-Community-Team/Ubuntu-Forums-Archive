### Post by gamerchick02 on 2010-04-16
Yes.

They are slightly more pricey than some other computers, but I think they are a very good value.  I own a Pangolian (version 5; they are on 7 now) and a Starling netbook (version 1; they are on version 2 now).  They work out of the box with Ubuntu (I assume this is why you want one) and other Linux distros.  You won't have to install Ubuntu on the machine and worry about things not working; they are designed for Ubuntu.

Both of the ones I have are awesome computers, and they have a year warranty for repairs standard.

You may want to look over the System76 subforum for more information about System76 and their products.
