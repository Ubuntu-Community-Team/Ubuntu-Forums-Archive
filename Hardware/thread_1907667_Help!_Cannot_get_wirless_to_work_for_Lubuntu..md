### Post by flyingchilli on 2012-01-12
This seems a lot like my problem in that when I installed Kubuntu 11.10 with my USB mouse in one of the ports I lost my wlan0 and my touchpad with complaints that the Symantic driver is not installed.

I have posted a request for help around this issue. I had tried some of the common suggestions with IRQ in the GRUB option menu. 

My solution was to not install the OS with a mouse or other device in a USB port. The installer was able to find a touch pad and a WLAN.

I hope to discover a work around for the USB bus problem soon.
