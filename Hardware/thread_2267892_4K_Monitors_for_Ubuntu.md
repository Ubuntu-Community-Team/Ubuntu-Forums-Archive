### Post by Mark Phelps on 2015-03-05
It's not the monitor that is the real limitation; instead, it's two other considerations:
1) Resolution limits imposed by the video card/chipset
2) Resolutions supported by the video driver.

AMD video products generally offer more flexibility with MS Windows drivers, than they do with Linux drivers.  So, if you have an AMD card/chipset, that could be a problem.
