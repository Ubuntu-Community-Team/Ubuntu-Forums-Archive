### Post by Dark_Stang on 2009-05-14
Have you drained it completely under Ubuntu yet? If not, then Ubuntu doesn't actually know how long your battery will last. Also, make sure your CPU is throttling down correctly and not running at 100% all the time. And it really helps to set Ubuntu's power settings to dim the backlight a little when you're on battery power.
