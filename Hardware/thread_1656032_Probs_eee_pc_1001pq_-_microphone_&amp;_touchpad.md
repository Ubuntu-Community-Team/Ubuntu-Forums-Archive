### Post by Jimmy9pints on 2011-01-28
Sorry nobody got back to you about your problem. That sometimes happens.

Did you ever find a solution? I have the same problem and Googling hasn't given me exactly the results I need. 

[This page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EeePC/Fixes#1001PX") details the same problem with the [1001PX]("http://commercial.asus.com/product/detail/52"), I am not sure how similar they are. There are just so many eeepcs out now, and they all seemingly have different hardware configurations.

I have tried the fix that requires you to install pavucontrol and alter the left mic to 90% and the right to 10%. That had no effect. 

The PX has two bugs about microphone problems filed. 
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/580183](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/580183)
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/alsa-driver/+bug/689258](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/alsa-driver/+bug/689258)
I've searched for bugs specifically relating to the 1001PQ and found nothing. 

Anyway, I hope the 1001PQ and the PX are not that similar because they report there is no fix! "This model still has problems with the internal microphone and there doesn't seem to be any fix available at the moment. This model also features a combined input/output audio jack which also isn't working properly."

Please let us know if you found any fixes!
