### Post by marin123 on 2012-09-01
I would recommend buying a laptop without preinstalled OS and then just install Ubuntu.

Before buying check how does Ubuntu play with different hardware. Example, I would recommend not buying ATI graphics (because I have it). You could also check compatibility with different processors.

From my experience, older hardware works better, so I advise you not to buy bleeding edge hardware.
