### Post by tgeer43 on 2009-08-30
You should be able to enable/disable the touchpad under the 'Touchpad' tab in System->Preferences->Mouse. This may or may not affect the isometric joystick as well. If not, check the BIOS to see if it can be enabled/disabled there.
