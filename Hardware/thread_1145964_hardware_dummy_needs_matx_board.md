### Post by Barry Carroll on 2009-05-02
If you're going with AMD I'd possibly look for a board with integrated ATI graphics as you'd be keeping it 'in the family'.

I have one of these: [http://kakaku.com/item/K0000000565/](http://kakaku.com/item/K0000000565/) which has an integrated Nvidia geforce 9300 and I have no complaints with it whatsoever on jaunty 64 bit.  There are cheaper boards available (~$89) with 9300 chips too and they would be a lot more powerful than a 6150.

A socket AM2/2+ board with radeon 3000 series graphics:
[http://kakaku.com/item/K0000031116/](http://kakaku.com/item/K0000031116/)

My order of preference for the igp would be:  nvidia 9300, radeon 3000 series, intel gma x4500 and I'd be putting the nvidia 6150 firmly at the bottom.
