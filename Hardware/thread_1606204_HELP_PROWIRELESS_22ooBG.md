### Post by coffeecat on 2010-10-26
Which version of Ubuntu are you using? Not that it should make any difference.

The 2200BG chipset has been supported out of the box since about 2006. It should be simple. Find the network manager applet icon in the upper panel towards the right. It should be next to the volume control. Click on it, select your network and type in your WEP/WPA passphrase where prompted.

By the way, iirc the 2200BG chipset doesn't support WPA2, so if your are using that, configure your router to use WPA.

By 'Device Manager' do you mean Network manager'? If so, what are you seeing? Describe your problem precisely.
