### Post by cascade9 on 2010-04-02
Something dead (motherboard, CPU) or your BIOS settings have gone bad. 

You can try clearing the CMOS- there is normally a little jumper to clear it (move it to the 'clear' position leave it for a minute, move it back, start the computer) or just remove the battery on the motherboard for a minute, then replace it, start.
