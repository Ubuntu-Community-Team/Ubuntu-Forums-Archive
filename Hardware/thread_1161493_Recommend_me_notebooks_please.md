### Post by Miguel on 2009-05-16
My experience with a Dell Inspiron 8600 and a Lenovo Thinkpad T400 has been positive. From what I've read, seen and used, you should get a new Centrino 2 laptop (P8400, P8600, T9400, P9500, T9600 and maybe a couple more). These can perform a lot, but are usually extremely efficient.

I'd have a look at either a Dell Latitude E6500 or a Thinkpad T500. None of these, however, have a chance of getting all the hardware to work with Ubuntu 8.04 without compiling/getting bleeding edge packages. HP business notebooks are also pretty cool, although they are quite expensive in Europe.
