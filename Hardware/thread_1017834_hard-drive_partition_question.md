### Post by roelpeeters on 2008-12-21
Hi!
Do not worry. Even without installing Ubuntu you can recover your data from your Windows partition. Download any Ubuntu live cd (8.10 is the latest) and start the computer from cd. In ubuntu you can access the Windows partition and copy the data to a USB key or an external harddisk. After you have made a backup of your data, you can start the installation of Ubuntu - if needed on a separate partition.

Hope this helps,
Roel
