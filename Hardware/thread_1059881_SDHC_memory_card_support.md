### Post by ajmctaggart on 2009-02-04
A couple of things...
1.  Is there a switch on the card adapter?
2.  Are there any pics on the card now?

If there are no pics, reformat the card in the camera, and then try it...

If it's not the switch on the adapter, there are some very comprehensive commands on formatting various flash systems in this thread, about 2/3rds the way down...

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LiveUsbPendrivePersistent]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LiveUsbPendrivePersistent")

This thread specifically talks about PenDriveLinux, but the commands are the same...

And don't worry about the fact that your using an SD Card not a flash drive, once you figure out what your system calls the SD Card, the commands are pretty much the same...

Hopefully though, it's just the switch...
