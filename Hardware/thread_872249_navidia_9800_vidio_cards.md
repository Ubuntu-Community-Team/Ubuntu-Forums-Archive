### Post by SZorg on 2008-07-28
> **hotweiss said:**
> All 9800's will have outputs for two monitors... Take a look at the ATI 4870, it's a better deal in my opinion.


A better deal if you are looking for gaming performance, yes. Price to performance that is a great card. 

To note as well it will have support for dual monitors.



However, these cards are overpowered and overprices if all you are looking to do is run two monitors smoothly. You don't need anything in this pricerange unless you are gaming a lot. Look in to something like a 9600GT or an 8800GT. You should be able to find a card that will run dual monitors smoothly for around $100, and certainly in the $100-$150 range. Just look for a card that has two DVI outputs.
