### Post by zanderman1985 on 2011-08-05
Maybe you could get some inspiration from this guy...

[http://www.tacticalgamer.com/hardware-software-discussion/91613-110cfm-laptop-cooler.html#post709385](http://www.tacticalgamer.com/hardware-software-discussion/91613-110cfm-laptop-cooler.html#post709385)

I made a cooler similiar to this one using the same fan, but instead of PVC tubing I got a metre of 100mm air ducting and 125-100MM PVC reducing socket which I attached to the fan and just jammed into the air ducting. just get a tie strap and fold up the other end under your laptop vent, making a 90 degree right angle and tie the group of rings on the inside of this right angle together so that it holds the fold and doesn't collapse when you put your laptop on it.

my temps plummeted, results vary depending on the game but between 8 and 17C drops - the 17C drop I got from OC'ng it was cooler OC'd than it was running stock after I used the fan.

try it out if you have the time, costs about the same as a conventional cooler as well.
