### Post by An Sanct on 2011-09-15
In my experience, integrated (on-board) graphics are always stripped down to the minimum, they barely handle stuff, that needs 3D acceleration.

If you get that powercolor graphics card, you are talking about, it will help a lot, just don't expect that you can play games with that ;) 

I got a similar card and at 8fps in full-screen 3D ... that just doesn't cut the mustard ;) but it is good enough for Gnome3 or Unity.
