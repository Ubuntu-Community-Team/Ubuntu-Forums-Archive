### Post by Odrodzona-Sarmacja on 2008-06-25
For installing Ubuntu disconnect your computer from internet (I always do so to make the installation in shortest possible time), because if it is connected to internet, then it will be trying to connect to usa (not to your local server) and download from there all new versions of software. So I guess your internet connection is a) slow or b) not very good.
