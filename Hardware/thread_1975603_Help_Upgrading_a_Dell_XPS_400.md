### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-05-07
With graphics cards, the most important element to care about is the chipset - probably either an Nvidia or ATI (AMD) chipset, made by multiple graphics card manufacturers.

This is an old Dell as you probably know.  A Pentium D is a hot, slow dual core CPU, Intel's worst dual core CPU.  The Core 2 Duo that came right after runs much cooler, uses fewer watts, and will probably be faster even at a much lower clock rate.  The point I'm trying to make is: it's not worth spending much money to upgrade this old thing.  Put the $60 toward a new i5 Ivy Bridge (very new) or even a Sandy Bridge (current generation) and use built-in graphics on the CPU and that may be even faster than what you have now.
