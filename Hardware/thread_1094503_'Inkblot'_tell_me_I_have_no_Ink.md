### Post by JBA2337 on 2009-03-12
I could not get Inkblot to work with my printer either, but I solved the problem. Use Synaptic to install hplip, hplip-gui, and hplip-data. These are printer programs written specifically for HP printers. Once installed, you can click on the HPLIP Device Manager icon, and it will show your ink correctly.

JBA
