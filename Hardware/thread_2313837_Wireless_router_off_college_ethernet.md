### Post by ub-newbie on 2016-02-18
To answer the question, "if it could be possible to use a wireless router to connect all my extra systems to the Internet without making them share one ethernet plug", the answer is yes.
You can have a Wi-Fi router get it's Internet from Wi-Fi, and pass it on to wired devices plugged into to LAN ports of the router.
Will all Wi-Fi routers do this, probably not.   Can you do it with a router running DD-WRT, yes.
