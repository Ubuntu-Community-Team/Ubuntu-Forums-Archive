### Post by HankB on 2009-04-29
I just plugged a 21" LCD into my Eee PC 901 via the external VGA connector. The "Display" applet recognized it and let me switch to the external monitor at 1600x1200. Trying to show the same thing on both monitors resulted in less satisfactory results because it seemed to constrain the external monitor to the resolution of the internal monitor.

I'm running Jaunty.
