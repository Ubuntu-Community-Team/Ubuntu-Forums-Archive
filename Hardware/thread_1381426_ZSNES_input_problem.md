### Post by pi/roman on 2010-01-16
Possibly mapping jump to the alt or space and X cannon to the shift button would work without too much problem.  The other keys in that area [a,s,d,z,x,c] usually seem to interpret pretty well on the keyboards I've tried.  There is also an option under misc - key combination to map a group of commands to a single key.
