### Post by raymondh on 2009-05-10
> **gjbob said:**
> How do I determine what hardware I have - or more to the point what hardware Ubuntu 8.04 thinks I have? Right now my box was/is confused about the monitor and graphics card I have.  Is there a list or utility that will tell me what Ubuntu thinks?
```

lspci
```

will list your pci cards

to be specific for the VGA

```
lspci | grep VGA
```

Is that what you were looking for?
