### Post by dabl on 2008-12-01
I don't think there's any danger of messing up your system with the ubuntu-modules package.

I got the information here:

[http://backports.ubuntuforums.com/showthread.php?t=652838](http://backports.ubuntuforums.com/showthread.php?t=652838)

You might want to use Synaptic and make sure the package name is correct for your kernel.

It appears there are two modules for that chip:

ipw3945, and 

iwl3945

You should probably try both.
