I don't know if this is of any use to anyone or if anyone even cares.

I hooked up a Ancient, Seagate ST35IA/X 44MB IDE HDD to my desktop computer as a slave drive. The drive is Circa 1992 with PC-DOS as it's original OS.... 

Not only did it work but was immediately recognized. I decided to disconnect the primary drive and load Ubuntu from disk to see how this drive performed.

 It was fast to write and retrieve files to and from the ancient hard disk. 

As I said I don't think this will be of much use to anyone since I have files larger than this hard disk's size. I just thought it was interesting.
