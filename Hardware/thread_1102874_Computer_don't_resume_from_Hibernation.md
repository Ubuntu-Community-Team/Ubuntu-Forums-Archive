### Post by karantina7 on 2009-03-22
Hello, I am using Ubuntu Intrepid and last night I wanted to close the computer and so I hibernate instead shut down. My problem is that after clicking Hibernate the screen went black as should be but the computer was still on all night. I wake up and it seems the computer is not responding at all  but is still powered on and the HDD light is on. It just don't respond on mouse or keyboard and screen is black. Give me some tips how to make it resume from Hibernation cause I have a PC I can't use now, please.

P.S. I tried to keep the power button so the computer will close, it closed but when I press the button to open the computer I still have a black screen, I can't even see the start screen of the computer.


OK, problem solved from info I found with a little googling. What I did was power off the computer and unplug the power cable until the light of the network card go off, then I connected the power cable, opened the computer and finally it started normally.
