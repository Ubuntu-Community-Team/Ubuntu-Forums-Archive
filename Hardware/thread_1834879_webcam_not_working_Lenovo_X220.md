### Post by saheba on 2012-04-10
To anyone who accidently finds this page while searching for a solution to get the integrated web cam of an x220 tablet workin': 

Please check, whether you have enabled the integrated camera in your BIOS. It took me two hours to find out that it was disabled by default on my x220 tablet.

For some of you (depending on your Ubuntu version) the following may be helpful as well: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/cheese/+bug/838739/comments/16](https://bugs.launchpad.net/cheese/+bug/838739/comments/16)
