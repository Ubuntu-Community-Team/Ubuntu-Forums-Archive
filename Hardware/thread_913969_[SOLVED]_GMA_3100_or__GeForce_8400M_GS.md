### Post by jordanribera on 2008-09-08
i've got a gma 3100 in my laptop and i am able to run compiz without any problems. setting it up was a little bit of an issue though as 7.10 didn't support it without some trickery. i don't know about drivers for the 8400m but it is definately a more powerful card. the gma 3100 will rely a lot on your cpu and system memory, whereas the 8400m will take a load off of it.

again, i don't have any experience with the 8400m, just letting you know that the 3100 is fully capable to run compiz!
