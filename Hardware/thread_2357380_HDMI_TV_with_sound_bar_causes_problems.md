### Post by dvjunk on 2017-04-01
I have a mythbuntu system that I have been running for years (currently upgraded to 16.04/028) using a Samsung 55" TV over hdmi from a NVidia GeForce GT610 video card. The motherboard has a separate DVI and VGA output also.
Ocassionally if the system boots while the TV is off I will get no display on the TV. I assume it is defaulting to the other VGA output. This usually isn't a problem since I just make sure the TV is on and use the front panel power button (short press) to shut the system down and then restart it.

However, I recently purcahsed a Vizio SB3851-D0 soundbar. When I connect the HDMI through the sound bar with the system powered up I get no video on the TV and the output defaults to the VGA output. It seems like just unplugging the HDMI cable from the TV causes it to default to the VGA output. I played around with mirroring both outputs, however whatever I tried it seemed like every time I turned off the TV with the soundbar connected the video to the TV was shut down and I had to re-enable it. I never had this problem shutting of the TV with the HDMI cable directly connected to it.

Is there some way to force the system to always use the hdmi output so I can use the soundbar without losing video to the TV?
