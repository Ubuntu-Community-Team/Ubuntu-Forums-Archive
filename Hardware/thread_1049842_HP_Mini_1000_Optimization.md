### Post by Saghaulor on 2009-01-25
Hello all.

I'm running Ubuntu 8.1 on a HP Mini 1000.

I'm writing this post with the intent that those who have the mini 1000 might like to optimize it as much as possible.

I've been experimenting with various tweaks and I'll post my results as they occur.


I'm not sure if everything works, ie, the webcam and microphone, but everything else seems to work.

I would like to be able to adjust the sensitivity of the touchpad. But that is really not an issue as there is a button featured on the mini 1000 that disables the touchpad entirely.


I should start by saying this however, personally, I am using the HP mini 1000 as an internet kiosk sort of netbook. I have nurses using them in the field purely to go to the web and do their charting through an online electronic medical record. Consequently, I have no use for additional programs or features, other than to make the computer internet accessible, and secure to the end user. 

What I would like is to achieve configuring the mini 1000 to boot as fast as possible and to have firefox open as fast as possible. I would also like shutdowns to be as fast as possible. Furthermore, I would like the battery life to last as long as possible, and through all of this I do not want to have to sacrifice ease of use or security on behalf of the end user.

Since these field nurses are not responsible for keeping the computers up to date, I have disabeld the update notifier as I will be running system updates periodically for them via a control mini 1000, cloning it, then reimaging each one with the master image.

I've been experimenting with things such as shutting down unnecessary services, readahead, boot profiling, and reducing use of swap. So far nothing has seemed to help very much. I may have shaved off a few seconds here and there.

Ideally, a 15 second boot would be nice(including being connected to the internet via my UM175 aircard), followed by a few seconds to boot of firefox and a few second shutdown process.


If anyone has any suggestions, I'm all ears.
