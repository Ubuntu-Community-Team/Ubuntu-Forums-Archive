### Post by Dugger5688 on 2010-03-08
Unfortunately, yes they can. But you would normally notice some funny symptoms before it happened, like spontaneous restarts, kernel panics, fire coming out the back of your case, or screen glitches. Boot into your bios and search through to see if the motherboard is  detecting it in the AGP port is detecting it, and if the motherboard is detecting the AGP port. It's also possible for an AGP or PCI to go dead. Hopefully it's not dead, if it's an AGP card those things are at the point where it's almost too expensive to bother replacing.
