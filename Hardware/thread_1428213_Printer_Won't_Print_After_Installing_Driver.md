### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-03-12
There are drivers available for this printer in the repos, **brother-cups-wrapper-extra** and **brother-lpr-drivers-extra**.  Give them a try if yours came3 from brother's website; they may be more appropriate for the Ubuntu system than those direct from brother, though I suspect you may still need the scanner driver from brother direct.
