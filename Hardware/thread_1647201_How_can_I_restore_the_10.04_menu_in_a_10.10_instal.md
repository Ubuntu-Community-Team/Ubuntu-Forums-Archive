### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-12-17
I suspect you are using Ubuntu 10.10 Netbook Edition and not the Desktop one. And that comes with Unity, a new Desktop Environment under heavy development currently ;-)

If that is Netbook Edition which I hope it is, log out and click your user name. Sessions menu will appear in the bottom of your screen. Select ubuntu-desktop from that and login. That will give you standard Gnome to work with.

Or the large text might also be a problem with your resolution. Isn't it?

If none of those applies, post the screenshot as already mentioned above by *wilee-nilee*.
