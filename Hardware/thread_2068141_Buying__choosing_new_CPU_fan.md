### Post by Noor1101 on 2012-11-02
Just to answer my own question and maybe help others by doing that:

I copied the information that's on the Fan itself (make and model number), and searched for it on ebay. It turned out it was only available in Hongkong and it cost 20 euros + 3 euros shipping costs. 

I was a little nervous about ordering from so far away (first time for me), from someone I don't know,but the customer feedback was a big help (99,7% positive).

I received the fan 2 weeks after placing the order (that means it's traveled about 30km/hour :D) (and paying by (my mom's 8-[) creditcard) and it works great.

So don't pay attention to some of the ridiculous prices that are out there. I'm sure Packard Bell would have liked me to pay 70 euros..but I wouldn't!
