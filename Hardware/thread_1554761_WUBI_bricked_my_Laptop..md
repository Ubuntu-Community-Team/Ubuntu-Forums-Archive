Wow, that sucks!

Don't use Wubi at all but it might be the laptop is just confused about power states.

Try this:


[LIST=1]
[*]Shut the laptop off (hold down that power button)
[*]Unplug the laptop and remove the battery
[*]Look at the machine longingly for 2-5 minutes (the longingly not necessary)
[*]Plug the laptop back in but leave the battery out
[*]Turn it back on
[/LIST]
If this works you can shut it down again and plug the battery back in.

Hope it works!
