If it's only the buttons and not the Touchpad itself then I suspect that it isn't a software issue at all. On the M1330 there are separate connectors for the pad and the buttons and the likely problem is that the button connector has come loose. If you're feeling brave there are disassembly instructions at [http://support.dell.com/support/edocs/systems/xpsm1330/en/sm/palmrest.htm#wp1038494](http://support.dell.com/support/edocs/systems/xpsm1330/en/sm/palmrest.htm#wp1038494). Otherwise take it to your trusted local computer repair person.
