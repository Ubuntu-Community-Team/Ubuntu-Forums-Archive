### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-10-27
Did you buy these used?  It's possible that they are blown.  Plug in a DVD or CD player into the 2nd pair of RCA jacks and play some sound.  If they sound distorted at moderate sound levels, then perhaps the final output stage is blown.  If the sound was also distorted at very low volumes, then perhaps the input stage is blown, but that does not seem to be the case here.

If you read the reviews, quality control seems to be an issue on a small percentage, so I would not be surprised if you have a defective unit.
