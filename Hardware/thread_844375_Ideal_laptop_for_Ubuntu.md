### Post by jualin on 2008-06-29
I have an Acer 4720Z and everything worked by default except the wireless, but installing the Windows XP driver with Ndiswrapper solved the problem. All the Hotkeys worked, including brightness, volume controls, Wireless Switch, Internet and Email Hotkeys. It cost me $299 because it was a floor sample, however i think they sell for $500. The specs are Pentium Dual Core 1.6 Ghz, 1 GB Ram, 160 GB Hard drive, DVD Burner, Built-in Webcam, 14.1 Glossy Display.
