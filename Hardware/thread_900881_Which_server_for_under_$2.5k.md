### Post by chrisdeckard on 2008-08-26
The first thing you need to realize is that a server is just a computer.  Generally it'll have special features like hot swappable hard drives, power supplies, sometimes hot swappable RAM modules and processors.  All those features just drive up the price of a standard computer.

If you really want a Sun, go for a Sun Fire X2250.  They start around $1500.  If you just want to play with distros, compile stuff, and generally just play around, I'd spend less than $1000 and save yourself some money.  Heck, buy regular parts and a rack mount case and you have a "server".  It's just a computer.  :-)
