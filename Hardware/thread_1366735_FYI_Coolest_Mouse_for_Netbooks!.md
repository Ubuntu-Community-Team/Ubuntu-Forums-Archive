I can't use the finger mouse pad on my HP Mini - my fingers just ain't that coordinated. So I went looking for a mouse. I found the absolute coolest mouse I've ever owned and it was just $10 bux at Target. 

It's the Logitech V220 wireless mouse. [Here]("http://www.target.com/b/177-7401655-3540327?node=259113011&ref=tgt_adv_XSHA3667&AFID=googlestr&CPNG=electronicsvf_logitech&LNM=logitech_v220_mouse&LID="). Except that Target had them on sale for $10. If you haven't seen this, it has a tiny USB thumb module which is the wireless element, and the 3 button mouse to go with it. You put the USB Thumb stick in a port and instantly you've got a wireless mouse. And it works on any surface, and it works magnificently and it's comfortable, small, light! 

Maybe these have been around forever, and everyone is now laughing at me for this Gee Whiz moment, that's ok. I dunno, but I think for any netbook user this kind of mouse is a godsend. Nope - I am unrelated to Logitech and have no interest other than enjoying cool things that work well!
