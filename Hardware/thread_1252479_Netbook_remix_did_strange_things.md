Yeap, It's not a problem at all)) But somebody thought that netbook users would be pleased if every window they open goes maximized.
So, to solve this 'problem' find something like 'Applications that run automatically on startup' (sorry, It's Russian localization enabled in Ubuntu on both of my computers, so I don't know the correct English variant =) ) This option is in System - > Preferences (or Settings =D , I don't remember)
Find 'maximus' in the list and remove the tick or even remove it at all. And after reboot your windows will be opened normally.
