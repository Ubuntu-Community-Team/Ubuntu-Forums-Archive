### Post by mathieu-tarral on 2021-03-29
After checking some messages from the [Gitlab]("https://gitlab.freedesktop.org/drm/amd/-/issues/1251") issue, one of the comment mentionned a "**modern standby**"
UEFI setting the needed to be changed.

I checked on my UEFI and configured it from "**Windows10**" to "**Linux**".

The suspend/resume feature is now working !  :popcorn:
kernel: **5.8.0-48-generic**
