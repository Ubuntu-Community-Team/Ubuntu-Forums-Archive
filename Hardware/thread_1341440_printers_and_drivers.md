### Post by coffeecat on 2009-11-29
> **r22d22 said:**
> Is there a site that lists printers fully compatible with Ubuntu 9.04

Not specifically. But have a look here:

[http://www.linuxfoundation.org/collaborate/workgroups/openprinting](http://www.linuxfoundation.org/collaborate/workgroups/openprinting)

Click on the 'Printers' link to look up a specific model. If it's listed as working perfectly, then chances are a recent release such as Jaunty will be OK with it.

If you are wanting to buy a Linux-friendly printer, the first rule of thumb is to avoid Lexmark (you've clearly found that out already) and Canon. Then look up your intended machine in OpenPrinting.
