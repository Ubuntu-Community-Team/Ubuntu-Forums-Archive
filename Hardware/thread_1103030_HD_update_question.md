### Post by oldrocker99 on 2009-03-22
IF you copy your /home directory to another device, all settings will be saved, but you will have to install video and wireless drivers over again, not to mention all the software you had installed. You will have all the Firefox bookmarks retained, all newsgroup (for example) headers saved, etc.
