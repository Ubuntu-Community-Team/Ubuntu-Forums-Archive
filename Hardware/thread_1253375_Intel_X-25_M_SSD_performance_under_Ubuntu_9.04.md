I have installed the X25-M Gen2. in an inexpensive HP laptop, which is currently booting Ubuntu 9.04 and Windows 7 Ultimate.

Average read speeds hover around 210MB/sec, according to HD Tune 3.50, average writes at 72MB/sec. 

To compare, the 160GB Hitachi Travelstar it replaced read at a paltry 45MB/sec. You can expect reads of approximately 150MB/sec (the limit of SATA 1.5 Gbit/s).

In my mind, the hard drive is the weakest link in most systems. A good quality SSD from Intel, Corsair, or OCZ will eliminate this choke point.
