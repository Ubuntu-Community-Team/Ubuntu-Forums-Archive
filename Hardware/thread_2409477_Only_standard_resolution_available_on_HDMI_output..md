### Post by wingarmac on 2019-01-02
Only standard resolution available on HDMI output.
Before the logon I could see that the EDID my Flat screen TV on HDMI output wasn't recognized.
I had found a post where it was explained that the screen doesn't transmit correctly the EDID and that it works beter on HDMI and DVI ports to get this ID.
They made a lot of complicated tests and diagnostics, but I'm new to Linux, and couldn't understand everything of it. So it wasn't a solution for me ...

I just thought that it would be a good try to restart my TV while it's connected with the computer running.
And it was a good guess, the computer re-detected my screen and got the ID like it should !

So If you have the same problem, just try to turn of your flat screen and put it on again, while connected.
