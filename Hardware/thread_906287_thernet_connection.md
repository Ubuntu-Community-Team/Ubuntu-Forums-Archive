### Post by GepettoBR on 2008-08-31
The only concern you need to have is whether the cable is a crossover cable or not. For some (IMO very stupid) reason, ethernet cables can be wired in different ways, and only one of those ways is fit for directly connecting two computers (the others are used for modems, routers and verious other ethernet devices). 

If it is a crossover cable (I'm a bit too lazy to find the pages myself but if you google "ethernet crossover cable" or something to that effect, you'll get tons of pages that tell you how to identify the nature of the cable based on the order of the colored wires on the heads) then all you have to do is plug the cable into both machines, install SSH (if it isn't already installed by default, which I think it is) and connect via Places>"Connect to Server..." to transfer your files.
