### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-03-09
If you need a separate card go for nvidia; they are generally easier to deal with than AMD, though that does depend to some extent on the exact card model.  As a general rule it is best to go for a card that is at least a few months since release as it is more likely the Linux drivers will be available from the Ubuntu Additional Drivers utility.

If you don't need a separate graphic card, Intel are probably the best and easiest to deal with, in my opinion, with excellent open source drivers.
