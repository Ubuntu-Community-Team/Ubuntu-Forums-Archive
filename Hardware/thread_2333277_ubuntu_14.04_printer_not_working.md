### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-08-08
Minimum HPLIP version 3.14.10 is needed for that printer according to [http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/models/other/envy_5660_series.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/models/other/envy_5660_series.html) so you probably need to get the most recent version from hplip direct from [http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/gethplip.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/gethplip.html) as 14.04 has version 3.14.3, too early a version for that printer.

Installation instructions are at [http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/install/install/index.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/install/install/index.html) and it is not as difficult as you may think from that link; just follow the steps and all should be fine.
