### Post by bayonetblaha on 2009-04-27
Please help! I'm tired of reformatting!

Since upgrading to Jaunty, my Inspiron 1520 no longer shows an unlock dialog after resuming from an apparently healthy suspend.  When I lift the lid of my suspended laptop the hard drive wakes up but the screen remains black.

---

### Post by bayonetblaha on 2009-04-27
I just found the solution.

I reenabled Nvidia accelerated graphics driver ver. 180 in the "Hardware Drivers" section of system menu, and everything's back to normal, with good suspend and resume.
