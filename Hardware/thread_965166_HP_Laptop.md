### Post by teaker1s on 2008-10-31
no intrepid can be kubuntu/ubuntu/xubuntu or a mix of all

linux works like this
kernel (brain of system and text input-like dos)
login manager (most popular gdm or kdm)
window manager (metacity,kwin or compiz)
desktop (gnome as standard/kde xfce4 or other)

Standard ubuntu is gdm metacity and gnome

so I have ubuntu with kdm for login and at login choice of either gnome or kde session.
