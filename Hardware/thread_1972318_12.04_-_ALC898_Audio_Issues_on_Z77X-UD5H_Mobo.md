### Post by anmar on 2012-05-07
Hello Draton:

If you have an HDMI screen with audio out (3.5 mm audio plug), you can just plug your speakers into your screen and launch the sound settings to switch the output to HDMI/DisplayPort output. You will get sound this way.

Good luck.

BTW, I filed a bug ([https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/alsa-driver/+bug/996245](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/alsa-driver/+bug/996245)) to track the audio level since it is too low with this setting.
