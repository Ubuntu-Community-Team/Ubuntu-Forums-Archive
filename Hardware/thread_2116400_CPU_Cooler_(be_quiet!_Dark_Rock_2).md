### Post by Subv3rse on 2013-02-15
I use one of these. It's ENORMOUS. Seriously. You've never seen a heatsink / fan so big. Can't advise if it'll fit in that case exactly, but check out [www.bit-tech.net]("http://www.bit-tech.net") for a review on the thing, with images in cases etc.
