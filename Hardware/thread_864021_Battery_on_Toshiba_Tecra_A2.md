### Post by silvanus2005 on 2008-07-19
Go to System-Administration or System-Preferences, you shall find there an option to show you the level of your battery. After selecing that, it will appear an icon in the right corner of your screen similar to battery icons on some mobile phones (like Sony-Ericsson) showing you how many power you still have in battery.
