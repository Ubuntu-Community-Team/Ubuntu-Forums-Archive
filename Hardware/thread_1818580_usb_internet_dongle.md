### Post by bouncingwilf on 2011-08-05
I think you will need to give a bit more info if you want specific help but as a generalization ( and it is only that) In my experience when you insert these dongles into the USB port, they register as a mass storage device owing to the software that's contained on them. You do not need this software and usually you should "Eject" the mass storage device (Rt click - eject) but leaving the device physically attached. Wait a few seconds and then click on the network manager icon on the top panel and follow the setup instructions ( defaults usually work). The ejection process has to be repeated whenever you startup in Lucid/Maverick but Natty seems to remember.
