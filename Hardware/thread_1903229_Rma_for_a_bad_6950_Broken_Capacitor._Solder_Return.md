### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-02
IF you've reassembled it, you have nothing to lose contacting the vendor and see if they will do an RMA for you.  

If they say NO, then you could try returning it to the seller.  

If they won't take it back, then you could try fixing it.

I would try the various solutions in that order.
