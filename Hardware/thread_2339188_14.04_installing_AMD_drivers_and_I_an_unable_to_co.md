### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2016-10-05
> xserver-xorg-core-lts-xenial conflicts with fglrx-core

You are using the kernel (and Xserver) from Ubuntu 16.04. It's too new to work with the old fglrx.

Look into installing amdgpu-pro driver if you want a proprietary driver.
