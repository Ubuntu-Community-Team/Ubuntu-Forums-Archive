### Post by dixie460 on 2009-12-27
It's an IOGear GBU421WM Bluetooth 2.1 USB Micro Adapter. Picked it up at Walmart for about $20 in the section where all the routers are. It's one of the real small ones that's almost unnoticeable and I believe it uses a Broadcom chip. Plugged it in and Karmic picked it up right away as a Bluetooth device. I was able to connect to and browse my Samsung SCH-R430 cell phone without a problem. The phone itself has a few Bluetooth bugs but it does that with any BT connection, Ubuntu or otherwise. Just thought I'd let y'all know.

Regards,
