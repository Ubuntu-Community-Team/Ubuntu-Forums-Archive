### Post by howefield on 2012-10-29
Removing the software isn't likely to damage the battery, however you likely won't get best life / use from the battery if you do.

Go to the Lenovo support website and download the manual for your laptop, then you will know exactly what it does, and whether you want to lose it or not.

[http://support.lenovo.com/en_US/downloads/detail.page?LegacyDocID=MIGR-70602](http://support.lenovo.com/en_US/downloads/detail.page?LegacyDocID=MIGR-70602)
