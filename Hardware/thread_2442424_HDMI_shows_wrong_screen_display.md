### Post by aughey on 2020-05-05
Mine is the older HDMI 40" screen. I havnt made it to 4K yet. Anyhow I connected the HDMI cable and found the resolution setting/ It appeared on the TV. screen. I moved the mouse across and ticked a box marked mirror.It asked if I wanted to go ahead so I said yes. It then mirrored my desktop onto the TV = JOB DONE. It appears this setting only appears when the HDMI cable is being used. Also there was another two settings in puse audio for sound HDMI. I chose the one with HDMI & analogue stereo which I then picked. This allowed sound which up until then was only playing on the computer to now play through the TV as well Thanks guys another happy user of Ubuntu
