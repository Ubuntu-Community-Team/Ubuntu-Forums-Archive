### Post by danbodoh on 2008-06-13
I've gotten two Inspiron 1525 from the refurbished store recently. They come with Win XP, but I wiped the drive and put on Ubuntu.

For the easiest install, get a version with the intel AG wifi card, rather than the dell wifi.  Not sure what the status of the driver is for the intel AGN card that's available.  However, there are some How-To's in the forums to get the Dell Wi-Fi working.

With 8.04, update to the -19 version of linux in the hardy-proposed.  This will keep cpufreq running properly through a suspend/resume.

On the sound, set the volume controls to control the PCM rather than the master control.  The master control is too sensitive.

Other than that, both systems have been working great!

Dan Bodoh
