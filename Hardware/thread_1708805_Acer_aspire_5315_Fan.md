### Post by Matt Barbee on 2011-11-23
I had this problem after getting a new hard drive and using Ubuntu as the only boot, with no Window$ at all. Fortunately, i had the Vista drive. I put the drive back in with Vista, updates the bios with 4.something (its on Acers website), removed the Vista Hard Drive and inserted Ubuntu 10.04 and the new bios fixed the fan issue. This is the simplest way I could come up with with out reinstalling Vista on my new hard drive. Hope this helps.
