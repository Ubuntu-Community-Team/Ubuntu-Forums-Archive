### Post by RaZoR1394 on 2009-01-02
The Lachesis has a lot of cursor jumping/skipping issues with the factory firmware. The 1.64 firmware fixes this for some and for some not but it worked for me. 1.75 has some other fixes.

[http://www.razersupport.com/index.php?_m=downloads&_a=view&parentcategoryid=18&nav=0](http://www.razersupport.com/index.php?_m=downloads&_a=view&parentcategoryid=18&nav=0)

My guess is that many of these issues are because of an incompatible surface or dust in the lens. The lens is placed too close to the surface and easily collects dust. This prevents the laser from behaving correctly. I have had this problem a few times where I have had to clean the lens space. Adding extra pads on the feet helps some.

My personal annoyance with this mouse is that sometimes when I right click on a webpage in Firefox the adblock or the downthemall window pops up instead of just bringing up the standard menu.

There is also a beta 1.91 firmware available at razerblueprints.net

[http://www.razersupport.com/index.php?_m=downloads&_a=viewdownload&downloaditemid=184](http://www.razersupport.com/index.php?_m=downloads&_a=viewdownload&downloaditemid=184)

There is some info here from the Razer President.

[http://razerblueprints.net/index.php/component/option,com_smf/Itemid,99/topic,7394.0/](http://razerblueprints.net/index.php/component/option,com_smf/Itemid,99/topic,7394.0/)
