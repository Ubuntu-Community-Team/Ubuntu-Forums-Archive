Hi John --

I had the same problem.  I did a big upgrade and lost wifi completely.

In going to Administration | Hardware Drivers, I found nothing listed for my Starling Netbook.  I connected the Starling1 to ethernet using my home router, bypassing wifi, and chose Administration | System76 Driver and went to the Restore System tab.  The screen says it won't destroy your data and indeed it did not.  The process only took about two minutes (I have a fairly fast DSL connection) and at the end I had a driver for RealTek RTL8187 Wifi cards Licence: Free showing on my list of dirvers.  Wifi is now working again.:D
