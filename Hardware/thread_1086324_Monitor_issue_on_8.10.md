### Post by nzadLithium on 2009-03-07
This line here:

Modes "1600x1200@60"

is not correct

It should be like:
Option "MetaModes" "1600x1200@60"

Also make sure your monitor and video card both support this high a resolution otherwise weird stuff might happen :D

You may also need to add a subsection inside screen like this:


Section "Screen"
Identifier "Default Screen"
Monitor "Configured Monitor"
Option "MetaModes" "1600x1200@60"
Device "Configured Video Device"
  SubSection "Display"
    Depth 24
    Modes "1600x1200@60"
  EndSubSection
EndSection
