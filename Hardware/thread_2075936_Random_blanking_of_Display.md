### Post by burnsmicro on 2012-10-24
About 3 months ago, my display blinked and blanked and, over time, the situation got worse, to the point of becoming unusable.


Initially, I suspected the on-mother board ATI Radeon graphics chip. So, I added a new  grapcs card: Zotac Nvidia GeForce GTX. The blinky blank problem became  more pronounced and my on-board graphics ceased to provide VGA output.


 I replaced my Dell 17" monitor by my wife's LG monitor which  is recognized by Linux as a Goldstar Company Ltd 23", 1920 x 1080  dispaly and the blinky blank problem was as bad as with the Dell 17"  monitor.
 The problem was:
- not the monitor
- not the graphics hardware
- not Ubuntu, as it seems I am the only person with this problem
 

At a loss as to what to do next, I replaced my VGA cable with my wife's VGA cable. Voila! Blanking vanished.


It appears my VGA cable had worn out.
