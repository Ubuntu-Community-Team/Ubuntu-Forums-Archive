### Post by weatherman2 on 2015-08-18
A friend of mine had the same kind of problem and did use a bluetooth card to add sound to his laptop again.  I'd guess it will work just fine.  There are also USB sound cards but of course bluetooth will work wirelessly.

As this is an older laptop you might try booting a live USB of an older Linux distro, even one that isn't supported anymore.  It's possible some newer kernel broke your sound.  Go back to something like 10.04 (not to install - just to boot it).
