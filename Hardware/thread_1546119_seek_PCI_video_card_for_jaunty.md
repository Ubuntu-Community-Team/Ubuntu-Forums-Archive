### Post by Fafler on 2010-08-05
They do in fact exist, although i think they are hard to come by. What do you use the machine for? And which onboard graphics chip does it have? SIS, by any chance?

If you don't need more than just a minimal 2D desktop at a not to high resolution, any old PCI card will do. Matrox cards are really well supported and you should be able to find a G200 or G450 PCI second hand quite cheap. An older Geforce or Radeon should also be an option. Search your local fleamarkets.

But the 8400 is probably your only chance of getting some decent 3D performance and HD video decoding on hardware. And it's not even that fast.
