### Post by dabl on 2011-04-24
This one is similar in performance to the Megahalems:  [http://www.thermalright.com/new_a_page/product_page/cpu/venomous-x/product_cpu_cooler_venomous-x.html](http://www.thermalright.com/new_a_page/product_page/cpu/venomous-x/product_cpu_cooler_venomous-x.html)

But, you need to check your case width.  The Venomous X is 140MM tall, and I think the Megahalems may be that tall too.  They won't fit in a typical tower case -- you need something like a Corsair 600T.

Also, you'll need excellent airflow through the case.  Use one or two fans in "exhaust" configuration, and at least one in "inlet" configuration.  Usually the PSU has one fan in exhaust, so you should have a second one help to pull the air through the case and over the heatsink.
