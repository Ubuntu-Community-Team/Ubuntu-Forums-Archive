I have 8.10 installed on an Inspiron 9400 and have installed the latest Geforce 7900 proprietary drivers.  When I'm booting the machine, the startup screen (splash screen?) with the Ubuntu logo and the status bar is all scrambled and distorted. Same on the shutdown splash screen.

Any idea what to do on this issue?

---

### Post by ahbart on 2009-04-15
You could try to change the resolution with startupmanager. It is in the repository. Be very careful with that. It could ruin your installation.

---

### Post by rexless on 2009-04-15
That's the key!

I changed the starting resolution to 800x600 and that solved the startup screen problems.  Thanks for that!
