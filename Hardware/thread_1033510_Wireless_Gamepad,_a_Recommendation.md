I would just like to recommend a wireless gamepad controller for Ubuntu since I know some people ask about that from time to time.

I bought a GAMPC-WCONT20 from König Gaming, the pack includes two cordless gamepads and a USB receiver. It's incredibly cheap, bought the whole pack for little more than 200 SEK (about 19 Euros). The controllers have a great range, for me about 5 meters and through a closet door.

Read more about it here:
[http://www.koniggaming.com/pcgaming2.html](http://www.koniggaming.com/pcgaming2.html)

I have been using it for some months now and it works great in emulators like ZSNES and others.
