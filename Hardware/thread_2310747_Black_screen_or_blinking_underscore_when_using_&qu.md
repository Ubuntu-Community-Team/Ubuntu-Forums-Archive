### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2016-01-21
FYI, fake/hacked cards do work in Windows with the Nvidia drivers. 
Those cards are usually made from an inferior chipset hacked to report itself as a newer, higher version albeit with the same internal clock as the true chip.
Thanks to the (sort of) unified driver under Windows it just works... Well no, not really, but because you have video, you _assume_ it is OK... Other issues are to be expected but _if you aren't a gamer you most likely won't notice them at all_.
In Linux they don't work, period.
