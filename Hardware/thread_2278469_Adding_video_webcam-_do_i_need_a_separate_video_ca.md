### Post by weatherman2 on 2015-05-16
No, you do not need a separate video card.  If the onboard graphics work fine for everything else, just plug in your webcam and go.  Sometimes you need to mess with drivers for the webcam and/or microphone, but that has nothing to do with the video card.
