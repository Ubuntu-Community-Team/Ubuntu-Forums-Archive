### Post by Therion on 2008-11-07
Ubuntu should recognize any external USB hard drive you plug in.  If it doesn't, it's not the drives fault; it indicates an issue with your install.  I've used WD and Maxtor external HDD's with my Ubuntu installs without issue.  Currently I'm using a Maxtor OneTouch (500GB) but I feel safe in saying if WD's are in your area at a good price, GET ONE.  Backups are just too important.  Heck if the price is really good, pick one up for me and I'll pay you back.
