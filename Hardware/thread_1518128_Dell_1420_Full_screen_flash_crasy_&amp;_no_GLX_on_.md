I just managed to solve why flash would crash on going full screen after an upgrade to 9.04.

The upgrade must have had an issue because it wouldn't load GLX for Intel graphics no matter what. 

Just remove all the nvidia drivers for x and whatnot and intel will pop right back to where it should be.
