### Post by 2F4U on 2011-07-23
Not sure what you mean. Connect one laptop to two monitors is possible, if you have two connectors available on the laptop or use some kind of docking station. Connect two laptops in parallel to two monitors is not possible, at least not at the same time. However, there are monitor switches available which allow you to have the two laptops connected at the same time and switch from one to the other. But at the same time, you would see the desktop of just one laptop.
