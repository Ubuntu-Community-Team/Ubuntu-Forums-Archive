### Post by samborambo on 2010-06-27
Sounds like a hardware problem. 

Have you got enough power to the hard drive? A single USB port sometimes struggles to supply a 2.5in drive and will not power up a 3.5in drive. 

Try a different USB cable. Yours may be faulty.

It could be the USB interface in the hard drive enclosure. If you can, remove the hard drive from the enclosure and connect it directly inside the computer or put it into another enclosure. If the hard drive is fine, the USB interface is probably at fault.

Sam.
