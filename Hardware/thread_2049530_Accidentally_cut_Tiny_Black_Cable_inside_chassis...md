### Post by lindsay7 on 2012-08-28
The motherboard should have a model number printed on it. Look it up on the web and see if you can get the manual which will show you what everything is. If the wire went to the front of the computer it could be a led light or speaker, or reset button, or start button, could also be a earphone or mic.
