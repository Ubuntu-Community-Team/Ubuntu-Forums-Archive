### Post by Linuxisfast on 2013-09-13
The latest Ubuntu 12.04.3 comes with nvidia optimus support out of the box with nvidia prime, these packages are the ones found in 13.10 Saucy and work quite well with multiple monitor. It also negates the need for Bumblebee as the nvidia driver handles the graphic unloading option directly. This has been a heavensent as multiple monitor and HDMI was a pain with Bumblebee previously. I would have a difficult time with my presentations in meetings and conferences.
