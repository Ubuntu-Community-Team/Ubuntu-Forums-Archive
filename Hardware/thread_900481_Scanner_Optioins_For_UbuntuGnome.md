### Post by eggdeng on 2008-08-25
In general. HP printers and scanners work out of the box. There is a list of compatible scanners at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsScannersHp]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsScannersHp") If you are interested in an All-in-one, the PSC series (I have a 1510) is a good bet.
