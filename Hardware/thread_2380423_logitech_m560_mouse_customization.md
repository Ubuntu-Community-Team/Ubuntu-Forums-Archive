### Post by tokyobadger on 2017-12-18
Have a look at the following [link]("https://blogs.ethz.ch/daesters/2015/10/24/customize-mouse-m560-on-linux/")

I should add that my logitech mx2 anywhere (with unified receiver dongle not bluetooth) just worked out of the box - both wheelmouse modes, middleclick (square button behind the wheel), browse back/forward on the left thumb buttons. Edit: seems this is expected.

Elsewhere I'm reading some higher-end logitech mice store their settings in the device itself. If none of the guides (similar to the above work) you would need to plug the mouse into a windows box and remap there
