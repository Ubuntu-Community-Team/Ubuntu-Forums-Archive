### Post by linux_tech on 2008-12-08
The power supply would be built into the motherboard on laptops.
When cmos batteries go out, you  will usually still see somthing 
appear on the screen often an error. So possibilities are -
bad power supply on motherboard, bad power adapter, corrupted bios or bad lcd. 
With a bad lcd, you should be able to hear hard drive spinning,
but screen may remain black. If lcd is bad then the solution would be to replace the lcd.  If power adapter is bad, then you would need to replace power adapter (Ebay or Amazon often has them cheap). With corrupted bios, you often get an error. But you can try reflashing the bios as well.
