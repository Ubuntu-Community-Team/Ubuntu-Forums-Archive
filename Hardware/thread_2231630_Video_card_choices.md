### Post by ubunt72 on 2014-06-28
I think many video cards out there are dual slots.   Where are you located?   It might be best to find a computer parts site (buy and sell - shopbot.XX where XX is your nearest location) and check available parts and prices?

I bought a Nvidia geforce gtx 750 and although it's not single slot per se, it's quite a short card.   It's low power and low temps.

For nvidia, I'd suggest to look at it and if you absolutely need single slot, try to find a Zotac GT 640 or a gtx 650 (very few are single slot but some are left).   There's also the gtx 740 that is single slot
For amd, maybe a Radeon hd 7750.   For newer, R7 250 or 250X.
