### Post by weatherman2 on 2015-02-15
Can you still buy a (non-server) CPU without video integrated?  Even Intel Celeron CPUs have video integrated into the CPU now.  And even if you have integrated video, you can still buy a motherboard with a PCI-E slot that allows you to add an external video card if you need better video performance.  Integrated video is a lot faster now than it used to be and is good enough for the average person just watching videos or something.
