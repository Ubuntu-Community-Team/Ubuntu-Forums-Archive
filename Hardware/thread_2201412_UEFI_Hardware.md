### Post by oldfred on 2014-01-24
New UEFI systems have CSM/BIOS/Legacy as a boot setting. You may also have to change other settings to get it to work.

But both new install and UEFI install may offer a lot of improvements or your old drivers may not even work with some very new hardware.
