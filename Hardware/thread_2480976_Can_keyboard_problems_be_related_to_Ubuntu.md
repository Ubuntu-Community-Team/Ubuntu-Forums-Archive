### Post by Autodave on 2022-11-15
Sure sounds like a sticking key to me.

Try this:
Power of laptop.  If possible, get a can of compressed air and blow it around all of the keys.  Now turn the laptop upside down and blow a little more air over the keys.  (When blowing the air, keep it as much as possible, blowing across the keys, not straight down on them.  Turn laptop right side up..........quickly and repeatedly press every key 10 times.  Use the compressed air again.  Try it and see what happens.
