### Post by andrewc6l on 2009-03-13
Probably not the Ethernet or USB (unless your set-top box is unusually feature-rich). You can get a video capture card (such as the pcHDTV 5500), connect it to the set-top box's video out, and *probably* use the USB or Ethernet port to tune the set-top box.

If you want to do that, I'd recommend looking into mythbuntu.org.
