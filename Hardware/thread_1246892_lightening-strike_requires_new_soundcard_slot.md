### Post by nss0000 on 2009-08-22
Gents:

Running x64_8.04.1 on AMD5400+/ASUS_M2N kit. UPS+surge protection inline. 

Co-incident with a recent thunderstorm & nearby lightening-strike, I lost ALL computer sound function. NO other system function was lost. Swapping speakers with another system pointed to my SB_16 work.alike sound-card as the problem.

Originally I had bought the sound,card because the ASUS_M2N mobo onboard sound chip never worked. 

Anyrate, I did NOT do the obvious and swap sound.cards, but immediately bought a new $9 work.alike sound-card and replaced using the same slot. Still no sound! Then after reading about such issues I moved the new sound.card to an adjacent slot and BINGO. After a bit of fluxing with ALSA controls system sound returned !
