### Post by CatKiller on 2013-06-19
Benchmarks don't matter. There's no magic score where you have to retire a machine or hand in your nerd card. If your computer feels slow to you doing the things you normally do then upgrade or replace it. If it doesn't, don't :)

If you're looking for a pretext to upgrade because you just want to, then go for it.
