Hey all,

I have an HP DV2000 and it is mostly working without a hitch.

One thing that is bugging me greatly is the fact that the touch sensitive buttons seem to be broken on it because they keep making a beeping noise (probably from the PC speaker perhaps) and the indicator applet shows the volume level changing.  It usually ends up going down slowly until eventually it mutes but it keeps showing up every couple minutes or so.

Very frustrating.  Does anyone know how to turn off support for the buttons since I never use them anyway?

Mylan

---

### Post by Sub101 on 2009-05-31
If you go to System ==> Keyboard Shortcuts you should see the keys labelled next to Volume down/up etc.

Reassign them or press backspace to clear the shortcut.
