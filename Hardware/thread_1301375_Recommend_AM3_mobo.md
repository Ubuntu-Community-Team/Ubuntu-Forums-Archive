The RL888/889 isnt amazing, but its got decent sound. Depending on how its setup, as you can see here-

[http://ixbtlabs.com/articles3/mainboard/roundup-790gx-p3.html](http://ixbtlabs.com/articles3/mainboard/roundup-790gx-p3.html)
