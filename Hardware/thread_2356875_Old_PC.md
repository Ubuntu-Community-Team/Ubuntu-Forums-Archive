### Post by QIII on 2017-03-27
Hello!

Your question is moot.  Lubuntu 12.04 passed its End of Life in October 2013.  Although Ubuntu 12.04 was an LTS (and it loses support in April of this year anyway) Lubuntu 12.04 was not.

So, no.  I would not suggest attempting to install Lubuntu 12.04.  It would certainly fail unless you know what to do.

You could try a currently supported version of Lubuntu (16.04 would be desirable), but your machine may still not be up to the task of the sort of performance you would like.  If it did run, it would do so only barely.
