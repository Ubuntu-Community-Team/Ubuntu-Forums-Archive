A little update sort of thing for you, go to the "Avasys" site and get their Imagescan package, go through the form filling in bit and when you get to the download page scroll right down until you see "Iscan or Imagescan deb" which is a completely seperate app  from the full printing package, once downloaded use gdebi to automatically install it, needs a reboot to work properly. See if that gets you working.

### Post by OliW on 2010-05-20
Image Scan! is actually an amazingly decent simple scanning app.

I especially like its "enable Start button" feature, allowing you to set the scan settings, a base filename and then you just go to the scanner and hit the start button, change the page, hit start, over and over again. Makes scanning in huge documents very speedy. 

I think I got up to 8 or 9 ppm last time I scanned in a big document.
