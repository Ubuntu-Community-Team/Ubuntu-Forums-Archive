### Post by mindpowerz on 2010-05-28
Check to see if the micro switch (the one that triggers suspend/hibernation upon closing the unit) is indeed working.

You may have to do a fresh installation of Windows to be able to test it.

If it really does, then there could be an issue with the Ubuntu power management drivers with your unit.
