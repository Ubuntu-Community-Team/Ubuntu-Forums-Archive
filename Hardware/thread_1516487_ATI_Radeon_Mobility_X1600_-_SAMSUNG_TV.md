### Post by waynefoutz on 2010-06-23
the latest version of catalyst should not work with that card if you are running 9.04 or later. The ATI Radeon Mobility X1*** series of cards have really lousy Linux support. ATI stopped updating drivers for them about 18 months ago, and the open source drivers are just now catching up to where they should be.

My best advice is to install Hardy Heron, 8.04, and the ATI driver that it downloads through System/Administration/Hardware Drivers. That will get that card at it's peak performance, or, use Windows when you connect to the TV, or simply replace the hardware.
