### Post by mörgæs on 2017-01-24
My experience is that more memory is always better. The bandwidth to and from RAM is many times faster than to and from a hard disk so the objective should be to keep as much work as possible away from the disk. 

Try for yourself if you can measure a difference but beware of static electricity if you begin experimenting.
