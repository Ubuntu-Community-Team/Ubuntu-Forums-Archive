### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-05-17
To do this with an AMD card, it must have EyeFinity support (HD 5x series or newer) and it uses the DisplayPort jacks.

However, it's unclear if that support is available in Linux.  All the AMD FAQs say this is coming in a future version, but don't say what version that is.
