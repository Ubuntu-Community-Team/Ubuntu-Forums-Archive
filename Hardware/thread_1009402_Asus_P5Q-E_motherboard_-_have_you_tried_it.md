### Post by Therion on 2008-12-12
I have one installed on my current PC built earlier this year.  

IMO, overall, an outstanding board.  I've had nothing but good experiences with Asus motherboards over the years and several builds.  

Did it work with Intrepid?  Well... I guess I'm going to have to say no; but I can't say that it was and fault of the motherboard.  Hardy runs like a scalded cat on my system but Intrepid refuses to give me a clean install.  The installer consistently fails when creating the Swap partition and kills the install right then and there.

As a work around I've done *updates* from Hardy to Intrepid and had Intrepid up and running "smoothly".  I use the quote marks around "smoothly" because on my system though, to put it bluntly, Intrepid runs badly. Quite badly, really.

What's to blame, Intrepid or the system it's installed on?  Which came first, the chicken or the egg?
