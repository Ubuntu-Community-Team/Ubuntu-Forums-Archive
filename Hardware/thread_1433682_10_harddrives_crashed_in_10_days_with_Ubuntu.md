### Post by jrssystemsnet on 2010-03-19
> **bluelightav said:**
> We are facing at the moment almost a daily hard-disk failure since about 10 days with our Ubuntu computers. Are we alone with this or are other users in a similar situation?FWIW, I haven't personally seen nor heard of anybody else having that sort of issue.  You might want to look to see if anything else is common to your group of computers running Ubuntu - are they on a different power circuit from your other machines?  Do the machines have anything other than Ubuntu in common with one another, but different from the non-Ubuntu machines - hard drives, power supplies, motherboards?

Ubuntu is *highly* unlikely to be "killing hardware".  That's difficult-to-impossible for an OS to do.

(Also for reference, I support somewhere around thirty or so Ubuntu servers and four or five Ubuntu desktops, currently ranging from 8.04 LTS to 9.10, both amd and i386, on a fairly comprehensive array of hardware depending on age of box, particular customer, etc.  No problems here.)
