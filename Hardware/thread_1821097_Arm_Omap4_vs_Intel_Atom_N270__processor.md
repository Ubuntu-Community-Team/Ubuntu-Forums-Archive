### Post by pibach on 2011-09-09
I have a Toshiba AC100 with Tegra 2 chip and can compare Ubuntu @ ARM versus Android versus an Atom N450 based Samsung N150. It seems that AC100 is a good bit faster in almost all benchmarks and day to day usage, in particular it has much faster GPU and video acceleration. OMAP4 might be again a bit faster that tegra 2.

Here is a browser speed comparison video: [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Tk8DTx0Xnfw](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Tk8DTx0Xnfw)

ac100 with Android 2.1 versus N450 Netbook. They are almost same. Note that Android 2.2 is approximately 30% faster that 2.1 and Ubuntu is again 30% faster than that. This is just my first impression - more precise benchmark comparison need to be done of course.
