### Post by snowpine on 2010-03-08
Yes, boot an Ubuntu Live CD or Live USB and choose the "Try Ubuntu with no change to your computer" option. Test the wireless, webcam, video, etc. to make sure your hardware is compatible, before you install.

You can also "dual boot" so you don't need to "trash" your Windows install.
