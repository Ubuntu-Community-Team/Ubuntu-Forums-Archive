### Post by PurposeOfReason on 2009-03-10
First, in an attempt to save you a few bucks, why do you want 7.1? AKA, how big is this room for the media box? If it's not the size of a good theatre you will not notice the difference between 5.1 and 7.1.

EDIT - Looking at that card, I'll end up costing you more money. That sound card is barely better than onboard.

EDIT 2 - What are the speakers? Do you need spdif?
