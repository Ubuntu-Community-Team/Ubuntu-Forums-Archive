### Post by speedygonzales on 2010-12-19
I have an AMD/ATI Radeon HD 5670 graphics board, and after upgrade to 10.10 my monitor enter standby instead of showing the login screen after booting. My monitor is DVI only, so I haven't tried the VGA port yet to see if I get any signal there.

### Post by speedygonzales on 2010-12-19
Solved: Rebooting with only the VGA monitor attached and then installing the latest drivers from AMD got my PC back in business.
