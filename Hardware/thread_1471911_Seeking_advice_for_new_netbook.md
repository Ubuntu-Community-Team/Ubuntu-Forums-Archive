### Post by pytheas22 on 2010-05-04
I've had a great experience with Ubuntu on a Dell Latitude 2100 netbook.  Everything works out-of-the-box, and the hardware seems really solid.  You can also get a pretty good deal if you're willing to buy from Dell's refurbished outlet--I paid around $320 for a machine with 2 gigabytes of memory, SSD, webcam and six-cell battery.

I wrote a review of my netbook at [http://www.workswithu.com/2010/01/07/review-ubuntu-on-the-latitude-2100-netbook/](http://www.workswithu.com/2010/01/07/review-ubuntu-on-the-latitude-2100-netbook/).

Also, in general, most Dell netbooks should work great with Ubuntu, since Dell offers Ubuntu as a pre-installed option.  You could also check out Zareason and System76, which do the same and also have netbook offerings.
