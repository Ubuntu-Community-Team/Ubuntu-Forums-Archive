### Post by Dennis N on 2021-01-17
> If anyone has ever installed ubuntu on such a machine or one similar any advice about installation and its pitfalls would be much appreciated.
I have a Zotac ID18 - (if model number had 'I' its Intel, if 'A' is AMD.) which was bought in 2014. Its the original style Zotac case. You can google the specs, but its UEFI, with Celeron pricessor and integrated graphics.
Currently has Ubuntu MATE and Manjaro XFCE installed in dual boot. I had no problem installing any OS on this machine. In fact, the UEFI is easier to work with than on my newer machines. I had Ubuntu Gnome installed at one time and even that ran OK. 

Important!
Be sure to use an SSD drive - that makes a world of difference.
