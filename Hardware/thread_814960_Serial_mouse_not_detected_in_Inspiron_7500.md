I've installed Xubuntu in an old Dell Inspiron 7500 and it's working great. However, my serial mouse is not being detected.

### Post by plvera on 2008-06-01
I solved the problem. Fashed the bios and now the mouse works fine.

Had I done this before loading Xubuntu, I would have saved several hours.
