### Post by Dark_Stang on 2009-04-04
Is this a laptop or a desktop? If it's a desktop just turn it off, unhook it from power, disconnect the problem drive, and turn it back on. If it's a laptop I'd say just take it out and order a new one. Laptop optical drives are usually accessible by getting under the keyboard. How complicated that is varies from model to model.
