I'm not 100% certain, but I do believe that the spare sectors are hardcoded into the drive's onboard controller, or perhaps as a value in the drive's firmware.

If it is the latter, there may be a way to hack it, but I'm not sure if there would be a 'universal hack,' as each firmware for each drive (and revision of that drive) varies.
