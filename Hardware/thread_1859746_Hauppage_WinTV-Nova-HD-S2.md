### Post by BryanDW on 2011-10-14
I was hopeful that I would make Ubuntu and MythTV as my Media Server but in the end after a great deal of heartache and wasted effort I had to revert back to Windows.

In the end it was the support for Hauppage WinTV-Nova-HD-S2 PCI card that defeated me.  I trawled the net for many suggestions but I was unable to make any of them work.  The result was that I was unable to scan any channels.  Every attempt failed.

A media server without TV is rather an expensive paper weight!

I tried to do this in and outside MythTV.  Both failed.  I tried reseating and swapping the card into a different PCI slot and still no success.

In the end I installed Windows and it all worked.

As much as I would have liked to have moved to Linux it is problems like this that prevent me from doing so.
