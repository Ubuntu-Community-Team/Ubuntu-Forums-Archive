### Post by djsephiroth on 2011-11-11
Lots of fancypants keyboards have blank keycaps, ergo the "Windows" or "Command" key is just another key, or has a blank circular indentation. I recommend them; they're nicer, more reliable, and they'll force you to learn how to touch-type within days. :twisted:

I wouldn't get a keyboard for a logo on a key. However, someone could make keycaps that replace the Windows key with an Ubuntu logo, or a small sticker that achieves the same effect. I smell a business opportunity...
