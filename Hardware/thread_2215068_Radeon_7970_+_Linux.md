### Post by Kris_Spencer on 2014-04-05
I'm not too familiar with Radeon cards (Nvidia user), but I'd probably use the newest version of Ubuntu for that. In fact, Ubuntu 14.04 LTS will be releasing very soon and will probably be the best for you to use as the kernel version is much newer. I'd also recommend installing the proprietary drivers for that card. You can find those under 'Additional Drivers' after install.
