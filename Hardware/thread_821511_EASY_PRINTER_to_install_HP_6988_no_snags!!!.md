### Post by kaibob on 2008-06-07
I purchased one of these a few months back when they first came on the market. The printer was immediately recognized by Gutsy, and print quality is good. At Amazon's cartridge price, the cost per black-and-white page is 3.5 cents. I've gotten the cartridges on sale, which brings the price down to 2 cents per page. They have this printer at a local computer chain store for $90.00, which means you're paying around $40.00 for the printer after deducting for the included print cartridges. 

The only downside I've noticed is that the printer makes a bit of noise and shakes the table when printing in normal mode. I turned on quiet mode which fixes this problem and slows down the printer only marginally.

Anyone looking for a good all-around ink-jet printer should consider the 6988.
