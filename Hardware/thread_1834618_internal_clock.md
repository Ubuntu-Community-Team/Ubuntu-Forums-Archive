### Post by Firezap on 2011-08-27
Interesting and cool ey?
Most computers have a small battery on the motherboard that stores the bios settings and keeps the time. So if you unplug even a desktop computer the time stays correct. Although these batteries can go bad and it will lose it's time and dates but it takes years usually before the battery needs replaced. Usually by then you got a new computer anyway lol.
