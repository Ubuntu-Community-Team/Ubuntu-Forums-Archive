### Post by bcschmerker on 2015-05-05
I HAVE A WINNER:  The Hewlett-Packard® OfficeJet® 7110 Wide Format e-Printer, which has sufficient capabilitites for my requirements.  Uses HP® 932 black and 933 one-color pigment-based cartridges in a thermal printhead, 600x1200dpi native resolution (capable of 4800x1200 color under Microsoft® Windows® 6 and later).

The runner-up is the Hewlett-Packard® OfficeJet® 7612, which includes a scanner large enough for 11"x17" originals.
