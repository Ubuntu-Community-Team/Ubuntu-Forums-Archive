### Post by trundlenut on 2011-02-17
sounds like it is knackered.

Try putting it back in the enlosure and then put the drive on end or on it's side rather than flat and see if that makes a difference.

I had a hard drive in a laptop fail and it was making noises like you describe and I found that when I had it in an enclosure and balanced on it's side it worked long enough for me to copy the important data off.  When it was laid flat on the desk it wouldn't read the hard drive at all.

This is a bit of a long shot though.
