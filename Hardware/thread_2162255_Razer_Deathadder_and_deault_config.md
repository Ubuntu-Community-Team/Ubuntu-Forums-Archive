### Post by feardotcom on 2013-08-07
This worked for me. Very simple. [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1578998&page=3](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1578998&page=3)

Note: If your result for "razercfg -s" is something like mine which is "Mouse:DeathAdder 3500DPI:USB-001:1532-0016-0" the space between deathadder and 3500 needs to be escaped so you would put "Mouse:DeathAdder\ 3500DPI:USB-001:1532-0016-0" otherwise it wont work.
