### Post by teranine2 on 2013-09-06
The prior version, Wacom Bamboo CTH670, works beautifully in Ubuntu 13.04 and XSetWacom 0.22.1.  However, Wacom's newer model of the Creative tablet, now under the Intuos family of Wacom tablets, does not and is unrecognized.  Running "xsetwacom --list" with the newer tablet (CTH680) only shows a serial tablet device plugged in.  The tablet is unresponsive to finger movements or stylus interaction.  But I have verified that the newer tablet does work perfectly on a Macbook Pro.

Hoping the tablet driver developers can get this new release working soon.  Perhaps there is something I can do on my end to speed up the process?
