### Post by sideaway on 2009-09-09
Key thing, for linux buy nVidia :)

I'm never keen on second hand computers, no warranties and too much chance to go wrong, especially laptops, desktops are less of an issue.

With a nVidia video card in hand, you can pretty much pic up any c2d, and it'd make a nice linux box. Enjoy! The older AMDs I found a little sluggish in comparison. AMD made excellent budget chips at the time however, the new phenom II's are on par with the intels now, unless you're wealthy enough to fork out for an i7.
