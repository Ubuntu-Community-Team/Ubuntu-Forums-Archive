### Post by edcrumly on 2011-09-18
I just bought this one: [http://us.toshiba.com/computers/laptops/satellite/L750/L750D-ST4N01](http://us.toshiba.com/computers/laptops/satellite/L750/L750D-ST4N01) from London drugs in Grande Prairie, Alberta, Canada for $512.00 Canadian (tax included) and couldn't be happier. Everything works but the microphone, which I don't use. Works beautifully through my 32" LG LED T.V. at 1080 P. Very nice laptop's, especially running Kingston 64GB SSD which replaced the stock 5400rpm 500GB disk drive. Boots in 15 seconds, runs 11.04 flawlessly. Best of luck with your's!.
