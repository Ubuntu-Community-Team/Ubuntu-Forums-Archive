I have a Fujitsu, too and think its normal - at least my laptop does the same. It turning off so quickly is annoying. Same thing when the power gets low. My older laptop (another brand) would stop shutting down if I reopened the lid or plugged in the power, but Fujitsu keeps shutting down.

On the heat... easy way to know where the CPU is inside your laptop.
Heat underneath should be in a line up  ventilator slits.
If its older and the keyboard gets hot on the top side ... CPU ventilator may need cleaning.
