### Post by vidtek on 2015-01-30
I just bought one of these mini keyboards to go with my htpc, and it's been working well apart from the top-right key which has a padlock engraved on it. There are 3 mode switches next to it, Win/Android/IOS I use it on the win setting.  The power button was being pressed accidentally, when the keyboard slid under a cushion or between two cushions, or when the cat jumped on it-whatever it drove me nuts because it immediately shut down the computer as if with the halt command.  
I scoured the internet for a solution, as no matter what I did this button stubbornly refused to change.  It has a keycode of 116 and I edited all sorts of files to try and stop this behaviour.
Using Ubuntu 14.04.1 LTS trusty with mythtv and xfce.

There is a small icon in the system tray for power and after spending most of the day I thought -it can't be that simple - but there it is, just change the default action of the power button and it disables the keyboard button.  doh- I feel like a real idiot!

Posted this in case someone else has similar probs.
