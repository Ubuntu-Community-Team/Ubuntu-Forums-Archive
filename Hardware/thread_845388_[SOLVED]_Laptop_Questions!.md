### Post by canoe529 on 2008-07-01
Get yourself a good laptop bag, like a Targus or something like that. Something with a lot of padding that your laptop fits snugly into. There are cheap laptop sleeves and bags out there with little or no padding, avoid those. 

I've never heard of a laptop HDD warping if you move it. I've used mine on planes, trains, automobiles, etc... As long as you don't drop it, you should be fine. 

If you will be in a harsh environment where you expect it to get jostled, bumped, dropped, or whatever, than Panasonic makes a line of laptops called "Toughbooks" that you might look into.
