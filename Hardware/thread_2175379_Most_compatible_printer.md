### Post by buzzingrobot on 2013-09-19
Laserjets are HP's.

HP printers often need a proprietary HP driver.  Make life easy by install "hplip-gui" from the repos. It shows up as "HP Toolbox".  Click it, follow the instructions, and it will get that driver if it's needed, set up the printer, and install an HP settings tool.
