### Post by madvinegar on 2012-01-13
I can do that by using thunderbird.

Create a new e-mail and at the recipient's address type:

[the fax number]@fax.[your provider].[the code of your country]

An example would be:

[email]12345678910@fax.ontelecoms.gr[/email]

Whatever you write on the "subject" space and on the main e-mail body would be your first page.
If you put any attachments (pdf attachments) they will be sent as extra pages.

This works for me just fine and I can send faxes over the internet!:D
