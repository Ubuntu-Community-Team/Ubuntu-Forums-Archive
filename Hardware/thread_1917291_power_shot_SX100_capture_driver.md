### Post by squaregoldfish on 2012-01-30
libgphoto2 and gphoto2 (command line front end) can control Canon Powershot cameras - I've used it successfully with my SX110.

I don't know what its official status is now - its main website seems to have disappeared, but it's still in the repositories and the mailing lists appear to be quite active on the project's SourceForge page.

[http://sourceforge.net/projects/gphoto/](http://sourceforge.net/projects/gphoto/)
