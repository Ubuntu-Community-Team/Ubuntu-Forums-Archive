### Post by aloogobi on 2011-10-27
If you choose the power options you can choose what to do when shutting the lid. Unlike windows where the option is "Do Nothing", the Ubuntu option you would want is "Blank Screen".

### Post by teejay17 on 2011-10-27
> **ProntoAnthony said:**

Thanks, but I figured it out. I had to restart the machine for the settings to take effect. 
I had gone to power settings and clicked on "do nothing" when the lid was closed, but the display was still shutting down when I closed the lid. That was until I did a complete reboot. I closed the lid while it was on the BIOS screen and now all is well, I'm seeing this display while on my TV while the lid is closed.
