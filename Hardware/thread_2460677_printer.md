No useful ideas I bet.

- unplug the power & leave it off for some time (*maybe an hour*). I've had this work too, but it was probably so long ago it was on a dot-matrix printer....  the printer forgot all about it's being *tied in knots* and just printed when power was eventually restored (*10 minutes maybe all that's needed*)
