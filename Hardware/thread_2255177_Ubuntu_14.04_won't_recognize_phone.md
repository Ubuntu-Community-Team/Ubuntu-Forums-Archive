### Post by carlwsnyder on 2014-12-02
Your phone does not support connection to another computer.  Options as I see them are: 1) Email the photos to your account.  2) Send them as an MMS message to an account which you have set up which can be accessed by your laptop.  3) Set up Blue-Tooth on your laptop, possibly by a Blue-Tooth dongle, and attempt to transfer the photos over that channel.  4) Samsung lists software on this page to sync the phone, but the software is only guaranteed to work with Windows.[http://www.samsung.com/us/support/owners/product/SGH-A847ZAAATT](http://www.samsung.com/us/support/owners/product/SGH-A847ZAAATT)  You can try running with WINE to see if that will work, or simply give up and hook up to a Windows computer to do your file synchronizing.

Good luck!
