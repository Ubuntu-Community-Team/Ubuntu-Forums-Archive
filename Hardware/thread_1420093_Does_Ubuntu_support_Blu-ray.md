> I am getting a laptop for college within the next year. As a future Computer Science major, I want to get the best laptop for a budget. Currently I have one in mind and that is the Sony Vaio E series. One of the features I'm interested in getting is a read/write Blu-ray drive. 

From Synaptic:

libburn4

library to provide CD/DVD writing functions

libburn is a library for reading, mastering and writing optical discs.
For now this means CD-R, CD-RW, DVD-RAM, DVD+RW, DVD+R, DVD+R/DL, DVD-RW,
DVD-R, BD-R, BD-RE.
Not supported yet are DVD-R/DL.

Ubuntu 9.10 contains a version of Mplayer that is capable of reading decrypted Blu-ray files.
