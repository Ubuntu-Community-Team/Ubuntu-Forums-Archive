### Post by chamm on 2010-10-14
Just ran up against this problem. There's a substantial thread on the problem, with LOTS of different solutions, but for the fix I used, check out post #54 from John Baptist. It's on page 6 of the discussion.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1388164](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1388164)

You won't get right-click working, however, it will enable two-finger right-click, which IMHO, is better than the click pad on the Mini 210. It also enables two-finger scrolling as well as the feature that ignores it when your palms hit the trackpad while typing. It also smooths out the motion of the trackpad.

You CAN get the right-click working by applying the fix on the first page, (post #6) however, you'll lose any scrolling capability, which was a deal-killer for me.
