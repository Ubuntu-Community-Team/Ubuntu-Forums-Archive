### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-11-04
You probably can't get an "enclosure" that reads both types of drives, but you can get an "adapter" that does - I have such an adapter.

The difference between an "enclosure" and an "adapter" is that an enclosure is meant to turn an internal drive into an external drive.  An adapter is designed for short-term use.  Get an adapter if you just want get data off...
