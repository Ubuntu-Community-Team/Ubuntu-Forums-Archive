### Post by rbmorse on 2018-11-15
What interface are you trying to use?  The ethernet or Wi-Fi?  

That's a fairly recent model motherboard. Ubuntu 16.04 predates it by quite a bit. It could be that the distro doesn't have the correct driver for the motherboard's Intel I219-V ethernet chip.  Search may help, or check Intel's support site for a Linux driver for the I219-V.
