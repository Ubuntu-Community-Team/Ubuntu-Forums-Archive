### Post by sdennie on 2008-06-16
It's fairly hard to go wrong with keyboards and mice.  Whenever I buy new hardware for linux, I find the exact thing I want and then google for "name_of_the_thing_I_want linux" and "name_of_the_thing_I_want ubuntu".  If no pages show up saying, "It didn't work", it's usually safe to assume the piece of hardware works fine on linux.

As for the extra buttons on keyboards, for many keyboards Ubuntu will just do the right thing for many of the keys and, you should be able to reprogram the keys however you like (though, it may not be super easy to do this).
