### Post by fishmonger on 2008-06-29
I installed Hardy on my 6910p the other day.  Some generic notes -
1.  Wireless works, make sure the card is powered on under Windows before installing/rebooting, otherwise the cards seems to just stay off.
2.  I have an HP ev2210 EVDO Rev-A card with Sprint service.  It works ok with the "sierra" driver, but I'll likely never use it under Linux so I haven't spent much time on it.  Same comment about making sure the card is on as w/ the Intel wireless.
3.  Suspend/hibernate appear to work correctly with all of the latest updates.
4.  The SD card reader (Ricoh-based) does not work.
5.  Video, etc, works great.  I installed when the laptop was docked, and it auto-configed for the external monitor - that was very easy to fix.
6.  Sound works.
7.  All of the HP-specific hotbuttons that I use regularly work.
8.  Wired LAN works.
9.  All other interfaces appear to work.  SD is the only hold-out right now.
10.  I just wanted to get to ten here.  :)
