### Post by TheFu on 2013-05-10
You didn't say the purpose of the PC, so we really cannot make any recommendations. The CPU seems to be on the low side, so you aren't planning to game much beyond card games and chess, I take it.  Video playback will be fine, that isn't really an issue with any modern APU at all.

I will say that I am NOT a gamer, so any graphics card or built-in graphics that is supported by the OSes involved is just fine for my needs.  I have an AMD-E350 APU from a few yrs ago. It handles 1080p content just fine. It is inside an XBMC box.

When I build a new PC, I never spend over $50 for a GPU card - at least not in the last 10 yrs.
