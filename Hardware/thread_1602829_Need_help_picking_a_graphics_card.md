### Post by cascade9 on 2010-10-22
Probably your best bet would be a PCI nVidia 8400GS. A 2nd choice would be a PCI 6200. I have no idea about computer shops in Guyana, or even importing from the US/etc, so things could be very different, but in the US you can get a PCI 8400GS for about $40. 

There might be slightly cheaper PCI nVidia cards, like FX 5200s, MX4000s and MX400s- unless its a really, really good deal, dont bother. Even then......in most cases, dont bother. The FX5200 isnt a great card, and as for the MX4000 and MX400s- they use the older 96.XX nvidia drivers, and those drivers dont run on 10.10 yet.

*edit- you might want to get a new power supply as well. A 250 is really very small for the P4s, they sucked power. After a few years, power supplies cant supply the amount of power they used to (capacitors age, etc). Running any video card is going to put more strain on your power supply.
