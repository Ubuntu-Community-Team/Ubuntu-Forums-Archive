### Post by TheFu on 2023-06-16
I have an EZCOO 4-port HDMI+USB KVM switch for about 16 months.  It is USB3 and 4K@ 60hz video.  Switching between systems is like unplugging the USB and re-plugging it into the new system, so there's a 2-10 second delay.  Also, my main monitor doesn't support HDMI, so I needed an HDMI-to-DisplayPort conversion cable ($9).

Before, I had a Belkin Cube for VGA + PS2.  Toggled between systems using a double-tap on the *scroll lock* key and were instantaneous.  I miss PS2 connections.

OTOH, have to admit that the VGA video quality was a bit off - some colors were just shown wrong.  That may have been the active HDMI-to-VGA adapter I had to use on my first system that didn't include any VGA out. The newer KVM + HDMI-->DP are clearly higher quality video.  I added a 2x4 4k@60hz matrix HDMI switch to control my second monitor.  I've been waiting for 4k sub-40 inch monitors to become reasonably priced. Considered using a TV, but I understand when they are put into sleep --> power off modes, then pressing the power button is necessary to bring them out. Life is too short for stupid hassles like that, IMHO.
