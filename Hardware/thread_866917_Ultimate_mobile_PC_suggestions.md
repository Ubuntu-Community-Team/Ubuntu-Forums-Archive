Hello folks.

I'm looking to build a low-power PC for a conversion van I'm working on.
I'd like something similar in performance to what I currently have, but need something that consumes far less power.

AMD X2 3800+ 939
2GB DDR1 Corsair 3200 (512MB x 4)
Abit KN8 SLI
Nvidia 8800 GTX 320MB

2) gps, currently have a Pharos GPS-360 i plan to use
3) over the air digital tv where available, using a Hauppauge HVR-1800 (again, shopping for low key, external antenna)
4) concerned about hard drive storage (i need lots of space, will be pushing all my compressed audio/video/data to this) don't want to worry about drives going bad with all the bumps on the road

probably going with a 19" LCD and some basic stereo speakers (gonna miss 32" 1080p and 5.1) but space is a major concern

smaller secondary screen up front for gps, using same hardware in back

propane burner/microwave/icebox/sink
