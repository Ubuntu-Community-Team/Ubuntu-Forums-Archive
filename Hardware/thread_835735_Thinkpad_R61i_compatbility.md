### Post by likuidkewl on 2008-06-20
I have a R61 773219U, upgraded to 2GB of ram and 7 cell battery, other than that stock and Ubuntu 8.04(x64) works like a champ.  One thing not to skimp-out on with Linux and a Thinkpad is the battery, 4 cells were garbage(2hrs on a good day Ubu) in anything other than SLED(3hrs) or XP(3.5hrs).

Using powertop I get around 5 hours on my 7 cell battery.

[www.thinkwiki.org](www.thinkwiki.org) is a good site for info on Linux and Thinkpads. 

If you really want one "designed" for linux look into T61p, select R61's or Ack! smEll.

HTH
