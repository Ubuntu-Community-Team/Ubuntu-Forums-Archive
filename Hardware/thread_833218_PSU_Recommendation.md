### Post by theravenproject on 2008-06-18
I would go with a 750 watt...You not only want to have ample power for what you have now but if you are anything like me and are always plotting/planning on an upgrade/addition-you want to leave room for future improvement as well...And make sure to go with something 80+ certified too!  It'll run cooler and more efficiently-better energy bills!  A 750 w should accomodate any upgrades you may want such as a 2nd GPU- an extra optical drive, another HDD eventually or Larger HDD's and anything else you can think of-I definately wouldn't go lower than 500-600 w minumum....Hope this helps..
