### Post by geekygirl on 2009-04-13
Just a quick question: you still have your recovery partition when you created your dual boot setup? The likely hood that is was overwritten is pretty high as I have seen on my own Vaio TT..lol

Because if you have that could explain why there is no recovery entry there. Had a search in the Program folders? 

You can always try hitting F10 at boot and seeing if it launches into the Vaio recovery console - you can always exit from it, will tell you if its still there!

The other option is to order them from Sony (I *think* they are around the $17 USD mark? - someone could clarify that for you though) or to use your COA (product key) with a copy of XP (make sure its the OEM version though) and download the drivers direct from Sony esupport.
