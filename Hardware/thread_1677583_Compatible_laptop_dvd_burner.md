### Post by IcarusR on 2011-01-29
DVD burners do not require a specific driver to be installed or configured, good quality ones should just work.

I have two slim DVD rw drives in laptops.

Sony DW-Q58A in a compaq Armada M700
Sony ND-7550A in a Tosh Equium A100 2006

Both are old & work well with Ubuntu 10.04

There are currently are loads of the DW-Q58A on ebay uk used from £20 - £30.
