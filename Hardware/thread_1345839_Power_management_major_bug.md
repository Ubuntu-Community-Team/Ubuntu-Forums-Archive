I have an Acer Aspire One D150 and I've installed UNR Karmic. It is a clean install over a previous UNR Jaunty, which used to work fine.

My problem is the following. Whenever I attach or detach the power cable, the netbook goes in standby. After that I can wake it up with the power button as usual. Of course the settings in gnome-power-manager are fine (and, well, there is not the option to go on standby istantly when you toggle battery/power).
