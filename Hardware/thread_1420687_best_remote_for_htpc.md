### Post by DownTown22 on 2010-03-25
When using the iPod Touch with Boxee, you can use the FREE Boxee app from the iTunes store.  There's also apps to use with XBMC as well.  I couldn't tell you off the top of my head if there's any MythTV apps...who knows.

A cool "remote" would also be the Logitech MX Air mouse.  That way you could have all your functions of a mouse without the need of a surface for the mouse to work on.

If you feel the need to do a lot of typing on your HTPC, then yes, a keyboard probably would be handy.  Maybe even a keyboard that has a trackpad built into it....not sure if those are compatible though as I've never tested one out.
