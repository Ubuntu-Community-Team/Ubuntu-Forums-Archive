### Post by Wesley Dawson on 2011-02-09
I would recommend the newest version of ubuntu. It is very user friendly for people looking to get into linux. Hook your computer up to the Internet (Ethernet cord) when you install and it will automatically look up your hardware and find the correct drivers for it. And if there is still drivers that you need (for plug in play mice, keyboards, etc...) if ubuntu doesnt already find it for you... just click on the additional drivers button and it will find everything you need
