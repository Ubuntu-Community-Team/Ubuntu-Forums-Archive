### Post by zepita on 2009-07-02
As far as I know, only the mechanic parts (hdd, fans, cd drive, keyboard, etc) gets beaten up faster than the electronic parts that may last several decades with good use.
You should get a S.M.A.R.T. monitoring tool to retrieve the hdd health level.

The problem you have could be hdd related or maybe temperature from the CPU. In that case you should clean the fans from dirt and replace the thermal paste to assure a good heat transfer to the cooling parts.
I'm assuming you already ran a memory test using any linux cd at boot, since it's a common issue.
