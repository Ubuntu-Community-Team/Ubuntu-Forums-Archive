### Post by pezed on 2010-12-12
Hardware failure 100%.  Start by running memtest for at least 3 passes.  I'd be surprised if you didn't find some errors or see it reboot during testing.  Could also be a power supply that is failing.  If the powersupply isn't a good brand like antec, seasonic, etc, I would be surprised for it to be stable after 3 years.
