I received an HP Probook 4420s as an upgrade at work and I have overcome a few issues to get Linux (hell,even Windows on this pile has its issues) working smoothly except the touchpad. The touchpad has HP's newest design where the scroll area and buttons are essentially the same piece of plastic with certain areas being the left and right mouse "buttons". I've done a bit of research and the only solution I've come up with his disabling the touchpad and using an external mouse. While the touchpad is dreadful to use, it is handier at times then using a mouse on the couch. Anyone have any ideas or solutions to this jumpiness?
