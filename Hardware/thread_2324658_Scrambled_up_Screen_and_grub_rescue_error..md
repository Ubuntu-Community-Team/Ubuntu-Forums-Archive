### Post by oldfred on 2016-05-15
The only way to know for sure then is to move hard drive to another known working system.
But use with bad BIOS/motherboard could corrupt drive. That is main reason we have good backups.

I had video card literately blow up. I heard a pop, but it still seemed to work, so was not sure it was system, but shut down. Next morning my wife could not start system. Turned out most capacitors on video card had ends opened or exploded. Wiped out motherboard. But drive was ok, but I did get new drive also.
