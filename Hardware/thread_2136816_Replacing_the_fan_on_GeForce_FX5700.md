### Post by CatKiller on 2013-04-19
As far as I know, fans are pretty standardised. It should be a case of simply finding one the same size with holes in the same place.

For airflow, you normally want an intake near the bottom and an exhaust near the top so that you get air flowing over everything on its path through the case.
