### Post by DavidFromCanada on 2008-09-23
Okay I've done this on a projector with a touch screen surface in the background and its sweet!

Put the game in windowed mode with your screen's resolution.

Using Alt-Left Click you can freely move the game's window to be able to see the most of the game.

The game is then calibrated.

I would suggest as well starcraft in wine. Calibration works great and the game plays like a champ.
