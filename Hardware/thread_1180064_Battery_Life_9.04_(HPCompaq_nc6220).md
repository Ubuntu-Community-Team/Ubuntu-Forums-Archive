I know that there have been several posts concerning laptop power "problems", but I felt that I needed to add my own story of woe.
 
Unfortunately, it would appear that newer releases of Ubuntu bring worsening battery performance.
 
On my nc6220, a fully charged battery gives:
 
> 120mins life on Windows XP
approx 60 mins life on Ubuntu 8.10
approx 30 mins life on Ubuntu 9.04 (even using powersave)
 
Much to my disappointment, it seems that whilst I can happily use Ubuntu on my Desktop machine - I will have to fall back to a Micro$oft Windows variant for my laptop.:(
 
Any ideas or indication as to when Ubuntu will properly handle battery operation much appreciated.
