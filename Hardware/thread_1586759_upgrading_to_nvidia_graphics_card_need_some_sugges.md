### Post by SeijiSensei on 2010-10-02
Any current NVIDIA card will support "[VDPAU]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/VDPAU")," which enables a video player to offload decoding of files in formats like H.264 to the graphics processor.  I have an older XFX card with a 9500GT that works great with SMPlayer.  Take a look at these: [http://www.newegg.com/Product/ProductList.aspx?Submit=ENE&N=100007709%2050001669&IsNodeId=1&name=XFX](http://www.newegg.com/Product/ProductList.aspx?Submit=ENE&N=100007709%2050001669&IsNodeId=1&name=XFX)

You'll need a DVI<>HDMI cable, but that's all it took to connect it to the HDMI port on my Sony TV.
