### Post by Claus7 on 2020-04-14
Hello,

since comments is what you are seeking then I would say: do you have physical access to those monitors and see directly their performance? Sometimes, just by checking the monitor yourself, might surpass the specs provided. 

Now if you do not, then as far as I can see, the brightness of Dell is superior than that of LG, while both have the same response time: 5ms. If for gaming, I would suggest smaller value, yet your wish is just office usage.

The power consumption of Dell, on the other hand, is greater than that of LG. 
Yet, Dell has higher display area but smaller static contrast ratio. As a result, for movies, more ideal would be LG I suppose.

Just some comments,
Regards!
