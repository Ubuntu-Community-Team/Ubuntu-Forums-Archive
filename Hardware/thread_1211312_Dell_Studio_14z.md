Second, I'm having a problem with the Dell 1515 (atheros) wireless card. I can see networks fine, but it has a lot of trouble connecting/authenticating. It takes 5 or 6 tries before it succeeds. Once it gets logged in, it works fine.

I haven't tried to mess with the drivers at all yet.

I haven't found any other problems and I really like the machine. The display is fantastic!
