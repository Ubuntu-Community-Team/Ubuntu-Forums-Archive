### Post by Aquinus on 2009-06-24
I found it. I have a HannsG 28" display that uses VGA and HDMI and EDID/DDC stopped giving any of my systems it's information. So it rendered HDMI and DVI (Via DVI-to-HDMI) to stop working and display information to stop being sent via VGA. I think it is a glitch with HannsG firmware on the display.

I attempted using it on 4 video cards, Mobility Radeon HD 3650, Radeon x300 SE, Geforce 8600 GTS, and Radeon HD 4850. No luck over any of them. It is the display. I would make sure that DDC is enabled on your display first. For some weird reason HannsG gives you the option to disable DDC, in my case it didn't want to come back.
