### Post by Mark Phelps on 2020-07-22
I can' answer your questions -- and based on what you have provided, no one can!

Screen resolution is not based on the Linux distro version; instead, it is based on the hardware in the laptop, what resolutions that supports, and the availability of Linux drivers for that hardware.

Same with a touch screen -- once again, depends on the availability of Linux drivers for that laptop.

You would need to provide folks here the specific make and model of the laptop and find out everything you can about its specifications.

Once you provide that information, folks here might be able to answer your questions.
