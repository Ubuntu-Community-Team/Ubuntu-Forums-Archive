### Post by mapes12 on 2012-06-28
Google HPLIP, Go to the site and follow the instructions to download the latest driver.

As well as dowloading it you will need to follow the script instructions to install it. It will probably download to your "Download" folder. Drag it to your Desktop then the instructions on the HP site will work perfectly. I did all this only the other day.
