### Post by Aesiir on 2010-09-26
I think this refers to the actual amount of charge your battery can hold. The capacity is at 57% meaning it only holds 57% of what it original could hold. So your battery can be charged fully, but thats not the max amount of power it used to have. You can see that in your screenshot where it says "Enerygy (Design)" the battery is designed to hold 88Wh, but now, after the 2 years, it can only hold 38.8Wh. So eventually, you will have to replace your battery, this is something everyone eventually goes through with all batteries.

I get the same notification, my battery capacity is 8.7%, so my battery, even when charged 100% only stays powered up without the charger for about 20 minutes, depending on what I'm doing... like watching movies, playing games and all. So I'll have to pony up and order a new one sooner or later.
