### Post by garyinspringhill on 2013-09-09
Just for future reference, if serial ports (tty ports) didn't show up when adding your cutter via "add printer" then either you have them off in your computer bios or it has no serial ports at all. Switching to an lpt port was a good idea anyway, no worries about port settings like flow, stop bits, parity etc.
