### Post by gordintoronto on 2016-01-27
If you're shooting 4K, the video files are probably pretty large. 256 GB of storage might become an issue, depending on how much you shoot.

Are you using VLC for playback? Your suggested config is probably overkill for that.

If your "editing" is mostly just trimming the ends, Cinelerra should work nicely on that config. If you have experience with Lightworks, it should also be OK.
