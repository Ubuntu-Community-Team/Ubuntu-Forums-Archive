### Post by AKA the A on 2009-06-29
I personally can confirm that it runs very well with 9.04 jaunty(sitting in front of it now) right "out of the box" & after a few clicks(proprietary driver install) it works as it should...

The only major hickup I see so far is the (complete lack of any) video acceleration for flash video(youtube etc.) & not accelerating normal video when windowed(works fine when fullscreen, even 1080i);
the wifi being incapable of remembering the WPA-personal key is problem even for other distros(had opensuse 11.1)

btw. I'd suggest the ZM processor (I have the ZM-82;) ) & less RAM, 4 gigs is a little overkill, the bigger cache & slightly higher speed will be notable when booting...
