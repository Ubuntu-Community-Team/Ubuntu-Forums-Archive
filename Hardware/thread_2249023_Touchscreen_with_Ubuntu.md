### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-10-19
It depends upon what you expect Ubuntu to do. There is support in Ubuntu for touch gestures. I think a lot depends on applications being able to make use of touch gestures.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Multitouch](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Multitouch)

Do not expect Ubuntu to use this screen like it was a tablet. We will have to wait until the release of Ubuntu 15.10 at the earliest. Most likely, until the release of Ubuntu 16.04, to get that kind of use out of a touch screen.

Regards.
