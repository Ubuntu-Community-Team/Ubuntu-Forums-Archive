### Post by TheFu on 2019-06-18
Any of the recent generation Intel CPUs have a sufficient onboard iGPU for what you are asking. 
The Ryzen2 CPUs with onboard GPUs are even better, but might demand extremely new kernels.  Ryzen3 is coming in a few weeks might be a little buggy for the next 6+ months.  Not all Ryzen CPUs have onboard graphics. Look for a "G" in the name. The 2200g and 2400g are examples.

I would ask, why couldn't you get an itx case that supports a low-profile GPU?
