### Post by sloggerkhan on 2010-09-26
Any digital camera that is standars compliant is fine on 'buntu.

They should all have DCIM folders with pictures in a sub folder.
In fact, I find it extremely odd that a digicam wouldn't work with ubuntu.

If the camera has a non-standard USB interface that doesn't allow it to work as a mass storage device, maybe try reading memory card separately?
