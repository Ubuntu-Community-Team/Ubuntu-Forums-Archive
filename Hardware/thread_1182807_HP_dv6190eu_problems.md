### Post by Yoksha on 2009-06-09
Hi All. This is my first post, be gentle with me!

A couple of months ago I installed Ubuntu 8.10 onto a HP DV6190eu laptop. I have since updated to 9.04 and installed all relevant updates but have recently encountered a couple of problems:

Firstly, I have to restart up to three or four times for the wireless network to be recognised and automatically log on,

Secondly, when I do finally get on to the internet and am using Firefox (latest release) the laptop randomly locks up requiring me to turn off & back on again. The lock up can occur anywhere between minutes of turning on or after a good couple of hours of use. There seems to be no pattern to it.

I also have Windows XP 2005 Media Edition installed on a separate partition and suffer none of these problems when using it which would suggest that it is not a hardware issue.

Has anyone had any similar issues to this or any advice as to where the problem may lie?

Thanks in advance.

### Post by Yoksha on 2009-06-10
Turns out I was wrong about it being a hardware issue. The problem was the internal wireless adapter failing. It just coincidentaly happened to work properly in Windows XP on the two occasions I tested it.
