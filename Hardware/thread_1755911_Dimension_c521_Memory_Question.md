### Post by MDguy on 2011-05-11
The maximum amount of RAM allowed should be a hardware limitation, so using a 64-bit OS shouldn't increase the max amount of RAM.  According to crucial.com ([http://www.crucial.com/store/listparts.aspx?model=Dimension%20C521&Cat=RAM](http://www.crucial.com/store/listparts.aspx?model=Dimension%20C521&Cat=RAM)), you can only have 4 GB of DDR2 memory, with a max of 1 GB in each slot.
