I am trying to set up a cart full of CTL 2go PC e11 netbooks. Thankfully, I have the summer to get them working. I love UNBR so decided to put UNBR 10.04 on them. The first machine I installed on worked fine but seems to see only some of my wifi access points. In particular, it sees my secured (WPA) network but not my public one. Both AP's are identical models with the only difference being the security settings. I tried UNBR 9.10 as well with the same results.
For grins, I put JoliOS 1.2 on and it sees both networks fine. The machines have RealTek network cards and RealTek wifi. Going to CTL's support site leads me to believe UNBR should work. The wired works fine. Any thoughts?
