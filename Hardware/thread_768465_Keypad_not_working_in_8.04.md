### Post by Klement on 2008-04-26
Hi,
after upgrading from 7.10 to 8.04, my keypad stopped working in X/Gnome. It does work when I switch to console. It does work when I'm typing my username/password. After I press enter to confirm the password, Numlock key lights up and the keypad is dead. The only thing thats working is the Enter key on the numpad, and the numlock key which toggles numlock light. Arrow keys, numbers, /, *, -, +, etc. are not working. When I switch to console at this moment, the keys ARE working.
The keyboard is Canyon multimedia keyboard, USB connected and multimedia keys are working..
Can anyone help?

### Post by dynathi on 2008-04-26
I also had the same problem and was trying to figure out what was going on, as the numpad would move my mouse rather than entering numbers o_0 After some googling I found out that I needed to go to System->Preferences->Assistive Technologies->Keyboard Accesibility->Mouse Keys and uncheck "Allow to control the pointer using the keyboard". Maybe this is your problem?

---

### Post by Klement on 2008-04-27
I went to the menu and found out that the option "Allow to control the pointer using the keyboard" is NOT checked, I checked it, and the keypad started moving mouse, unchecked it, and woo-hoo ... the keypad its working!
Thanks ;)
