### Post by winh8r on 2012-03-18
Some motherboards will accept double sided RAM in the primary DIMM slot, but can only use single sided RAM in the other slots, I do not pretend to be an expert on this, I am merely stating what I have learned after experiencing issues with RAM not being recognised when upgrading a machine and having to trawl around looking for answers.

Strangely enough though the machine was an HP desktop!

 I have dug out this link for you to have a look at, the RAM section on this site helped me to learn a lot about it all:

Hopee this is of some help to you.
