### Post by GARoss on 2009-02-09
This worked for me & may for you.
Open *Applications>Sound & Video>Gnome Alsa Mixer*. About midway, below the PCM Cent slider are several check-type on/off switches. Look for one that says Audigy Analog/Didital Output Jack & uncheck it. This fixed mine. It might just say *Analog/Didital Output Jack*, too. My SB is made by Creative Sound & Audigy is their trademark.
HTH
George
