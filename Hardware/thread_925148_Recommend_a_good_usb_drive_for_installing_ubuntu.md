### Post by C.S.Cameron on 2008-09-21
I have bricked two Transcend usb drives and one GXT drive running Ubuntu.
Kingston seems reliable but does not have very fast read /write speeds.
If you have lots of ram read / write speed is not that big a deal for normal operation.
I've also heard that OCZ is quick, expensive and limited to 4G, but I guess you could stick your home folder on a second drive.
