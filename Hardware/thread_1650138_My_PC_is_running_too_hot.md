### Post by davidmohammed on 2010-12-21
35-40 deg inside a house!  From your location, I would suggest turning down the heating controls :-)

no seriously - venting is you key issue I believe - or possible you have turned off acpi control (e.g. via BIOS/grub boot options)?

Have a look inside - do you case fans work?  Full of dust?  Are they obstructed in any way e.g. lots of wires/ribbon cables.

If you are convinced the desktop cooling is OK then I would suggest buy a tower case for 20 "bucks" - also two case fans - so that it can both pull air in and expel air repectively.  Case fans cost a fiver I think.

You should be able to easily move the insides of your desktop to the tower.

You can replace the heat sink for the CPU with a fan version - spend say 30-50 "bucks" on this.
