### Post by solitaire on 2010-01-06
Sounds like a short in the system.

First make sure the PSU is not shorting (i.e. make sure all loose cables from the PSU are tied up away from the sides & the plugs are secure and fitted correctly)

Also remove the items you added recently and see if it still happens. If not then there is a short with one of the devices and power is being transfered to it's case instead of being earthed!!
