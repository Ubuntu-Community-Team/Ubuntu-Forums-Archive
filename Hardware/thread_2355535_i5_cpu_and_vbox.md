### Post by SeijiSensei on 2017-03-13
I have an ASUS desktop with an i5.  The BIOS has a switch for VT-x virtualization.  I suspect it may depend on both the processor and the BIOS, but at least I know that an i5 has the capability to run 64-bit virtual machines.

Update:  Looks like you're all set.  [https://support.lenovo.com/us/en/solutions/ht500006](https://support.lenovo.com/us/en/solutions/ht500006)
