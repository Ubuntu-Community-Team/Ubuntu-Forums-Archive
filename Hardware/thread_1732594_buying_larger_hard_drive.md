### Post by howefield on 2011-04-18
Upgrading your hard drive doesn't require any other parts to be replaced, if it is the usual scenario of replacing with a bigger hard drive.

If it is more than that, eg replacing an IDE drive with a SATA drive, you'll need to know that your computer has the connectors for it.

You may well be able to add the second drive in addition to the original. Handy for backing up, storage ect.
