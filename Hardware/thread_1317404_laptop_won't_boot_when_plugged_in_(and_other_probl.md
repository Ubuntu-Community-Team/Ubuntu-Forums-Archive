The laptop will charge and stay charged as long as it's plugged in, and the plug indicator light on the front will light up, but the power manager never recognizes that the laptop is plugged in (says "Not plugged in" even as it shows the battery charging). And now it has started turning off while I'm watching videos. I can use it for hours with no problem or leave it sitting around open and on all day while I'm at work and it's still on when I get home. It's not overheating, and I have everything in the power management settings set to never turn the computer off without me turning it off. (And unplugging it while I watch the videos, although it seemed just crazy enough to work, didn't help.) So I'm pretty stumped. Help?

"Battery: 0% (fully charged)
AC Adapter: not plugged in" but I was able to watch an entire episode of Supernatural with no problem! (not to say that I do not still have a problem..)
