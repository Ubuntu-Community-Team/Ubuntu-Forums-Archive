### Post by vanadium on 2009-03-23
I have just purchased an HP laserprinter myself. It appears to be seamlessly recognized. However, an attempt to printing doesn't do anything.

Secret is: you need to run "sudo hp-setup". I found this on this forum. This is an unfortunate very counterintuitive issue remaining in nowadays Ubuntu, but after that, it works perfectly. Besides this issue, I guess HP printers are among the best you can have under Linux.
