### Post by sdennie on 2008-07-25
In some countries (many?) it's actually illegal to sell computers without an operating system because it's assumed that a pirated version of Windows will then be installed.  Depending on your reasoning for wanting a machine without an OS and the country where you live, you will probably need to buy a machine that has Linux or FreeDOS pre-installed.  Dell offers both and several other companies offer Linux based machines (System76 for example).
