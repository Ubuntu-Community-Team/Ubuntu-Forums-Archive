### Post by ecookman on 2009-04-05
i currently own a Dell Studio 17

i got a 1.5K build which included

-2.0Ghz processor
-3gig ram
-320 5200rpm SATA HD
-the 1440X900 screen (720p)
-back lit keyboard (it has 2 settings low and high brightness)
-256 ATI 3650 GPU (i can run most new games on high res)
-webcam
-the G/G/N draft card ( on my wireless network i can get up to 130mb/s connection speed)
- don't get the creative sound package..it is just software...not a better sound card...the IDT HD SOUND program that comes with it is just fine 

likes:
fast computer...
runs games flawlessly(crysis on high runs at 20FPS)
good wifi range
screen detail is nice
i just installed ubuntu...and it runs realy well

dislikes 
lol vista


all in all...good laptop... it should power through anything.
