### Post by QIII on 2019-10-02
No, it does not.

Even AMDGPU-PRO configuration is done via the terminal.

By the way, AMDGPU and -PRO are a team.  AMDGPU-PRO runs on top of AMDGPU.  It's an overlay/add-on that provides specific proprietary functionality.  AMDGPU-PRO does not replace AMDGPU.  It enhances it.
