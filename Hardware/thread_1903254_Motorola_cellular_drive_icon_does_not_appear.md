### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-02
Others may have a solution, but most likely, the Cell phone mounts under USB in Windows because special drivers were installed when you first connected it.

That's true of my Android phone -- connecting it to Win7 forced the installation of special drivers.

Those same drivers won't work under Linux and are most likely NOT available.

Unless someone comes along with a solution, you will have to accept that you can't use Linux to mount your device.
