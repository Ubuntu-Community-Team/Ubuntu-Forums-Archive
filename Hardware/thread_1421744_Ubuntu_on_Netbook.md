### Post by snowpine on 2010-03-04
If UNR works, "regular" Ubuntu should work too... they are the same core system under the hood. 

This list might help your shopping: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/Machines/Netbooks](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/Machines/Netbooks)

You will find that vga cards vary from model to model, just like with any other "class" of computers, from well supported (intel gma 945) to unsupported (intel gma 500 "poulsbo").

The easiest option of course is to purchase an Ubuntu-preinstalled system from a vendor like Dell or System 76.
