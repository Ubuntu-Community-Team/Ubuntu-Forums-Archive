### Post by karesz on 2010-10-22
If anyone happens to have trouble with this printer:
Download the actual XP/2000 postscript driver from konica-minolta homepage, extract with fileroller, and use the 25cpmeu.ppd.
Then CUPS will be able to use your printer, in full functionality!
