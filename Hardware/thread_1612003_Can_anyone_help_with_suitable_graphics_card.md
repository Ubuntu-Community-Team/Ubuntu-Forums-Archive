### Post by dabl on 2010-11-02
I'd take a pass on the 8xxx series of Nvidia GPUs, at this point.  Besides the design being 3 or 4 years old, there are scattered reports of driver problems with *buntu 10.10 and 8xxx series cards.  I had a 9600GT before I got my GTX260 - if you can find a 9600GT it shouldn't be too expensive, and will perform well.

I'd go for a second 1GB memory DIMM, along with the new hdd, if it were my decision.  It shouldn't cost nearly what a graphics card costs.
