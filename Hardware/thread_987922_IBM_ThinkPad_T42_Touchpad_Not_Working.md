### Post by rouge haggis landing on 2009-08-16
This question is really old, but I found it while I was searching for an answer to the same question, and so I'll post my findings here for historical purposes:

The touchpad seems to be disabled by default in the Bios. To get it working I had to restart the computer, press the Access IBM key and then (at the prompt) F1 to access the Bios. Then it was a matter of navigating through to the part about the touchpad, and switching it from "Disabled" to "Automatic". After that it worked perfectly, and a Touchpad tab magically appeared in the Mouse control panel.
