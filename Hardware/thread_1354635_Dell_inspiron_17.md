### Post by leg on 2010-01-06
I have bought [this laptop]("http://www1.euro.dell.com/uk/en/home/Laptops/laptop-inspiron-17/pd.aspx?refid=laptop-inspiron-17&s=dhs&cs=ukdhs1") now (£449 one intel graphics and 500gb hdd) and I have 9.10 installed on it. So far very nice and I have had to make the wireless card work which was as simple as following [these instructions]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=8539988&postcount=7"). The next thing I had to do was sort dns from overwriting itself on a reboot by [doing this]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=8607984&postcount=2"). Then I had a nice new laptop that I can access the Internet on. Next I turned on the [medibuntu repositories]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683"), reloaded and went through it and made sure I had dvd playback etc.

I have not got the integrated webcam working yet but these are new to me so I will go away and try to get this going. 

Very nice laptop and comfortable to use. Boots quite fast and is actually quite quiet in use. I am using it on my lap in my front room and I can't hear it over the tv and I can feel no heat from it as I have with other laptops. The screen seems very big to me as I have been using a 10.2" tablet pc but it is really nice to look at and is perfectly useable for my needs. I like this laptop quite a lot.
