### Post by coffeecat on 2009-04-09
> **Graeme2804 said:**
> The HDD is a Maxtor and its powered via USB, it has a light on there which is solid green, which means it should be ready to use.

This may be your problem. It is possible for enough power to get through for the light, even for the OS to know something is there, but not enough for the HDD and therefore not enough for the drive to be mounted. I have a similar problem with a USB-powered external drive on one particular computer. Some machines don't provide enough current through the USB ports, particularly older ones.

Perhaps you've been unlucky with two machines. Are they desktops, laptops or netbooks? Have you access to any other machines you could try? Friends? Relatives? It doesn't matter what OS.
