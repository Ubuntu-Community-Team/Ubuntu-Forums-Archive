### Post by $yl9pAR%t on 2017-03-10
I understand your printer is connected via router, so you do not need any driver if you decide to scan to folder (using samba/SMB) or scan to e-mail. Scan to folder is OS independent. Scan to e-mail is even better, because you can scan without computer on.
