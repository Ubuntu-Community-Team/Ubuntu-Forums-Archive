### Post by rsay on 2010-08-16
Sometimes until a player has been out for a while it won't have proper definitions in HAL and it gets treated as a generic USB flash drive. In the past I have been able to put a .is_audio_player file in the root directory and then banshee recognized the device as an audio player. 

Unfortunately I don't own an iaudio 9, so I don't know what settings to tell you. If you google for .is_audio_player, you can probably find one that you can tweak a little for your player.
