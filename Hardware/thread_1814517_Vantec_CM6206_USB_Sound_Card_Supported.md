Hey there,

I found extremely little information on the topic and manufacturers swore high and low linux in not supported. Google searches also yield vague posts and very (old) technical fixes by Mac users - hence this simple post.

The CM6206 USB chipset is fully supported on Ubuntu 11.04 for 5.1 and 7.1 sound. :popcorn:

Plug it in and you'll immediately have stereo. Click the Speaker top right hand corner, click preferences. Select the Hardware tab. In the drop down select your speaker layout. Done.
