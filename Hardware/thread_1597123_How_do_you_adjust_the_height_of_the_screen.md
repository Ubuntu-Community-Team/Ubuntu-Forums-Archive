### Post by coffeecat on 2010-10-15
> **Rodayo said:**
> A small portion of the screen gets cut off on my netbook's monitor at the bottom. Right now I can't see the bottom of this chrome window.

How do I fix this?

Do you mean that the whole desktop is spilling off the bottom of the monitor, or just an open window? If the latter, press the alt key, then left-click on any part of the window and you will be able to move the window above the upper boundary. Grab the resize area at bottom right to make the window smaller, and then move the window back down again with alt-leftclick.

This was a common problem with the early netbooks such as my EeePC 701, but with the bigger resolutions of later ones it should happen less often. What screen size/resolution do you have?
