### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-05-31
Unless you have another video card in addition to the on-board Intel chip, then the Intel drivers ARE already installed and working -- else -- you would see nothing but a blank, black screen! 

This is a VERY old Intel chipset, for which Intel has not made new drivers since before Vista came out.  What you have running at present is what there is today.
