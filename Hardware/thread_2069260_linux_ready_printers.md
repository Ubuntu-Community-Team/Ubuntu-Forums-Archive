### Post by GeForce 9500GT on 2012-10-10
HP printers will work out-of-the-box. Good support under Linux.
Epson will work also out-of-the-box.
Canon devices suffer from poor support under Linux.
 
I never had any problems with my HP printer (ePhotosmart B110) and i keep the driver HPLIP up-to-date by updating it manually. HPLIP is available in the repositories but mostly this is an older version, few version lower than the latest version available at [hplipopensource]("http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html").
 
My advise: buy a HP printer.
