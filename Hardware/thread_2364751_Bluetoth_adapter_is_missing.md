### Post by efflandt on 2017-06-27
What kind of Bluetooth hardware device do you think you have? The only USB device I see is a company that makes memory card readers and chips. Whatever type of device your Microsoft Sidewinder Pro is appears to disconnect from USB. And as near as I can tell your mini PCIe card for WiFi does not include Bluetooth. So maybe that is an optional feature for your laptop that you do not have, even if there is a key or LED that appears to be for Bluetooth.

There are tiny regular USB Bluetooth dongles available for about $10 US. I use one for my desktop PC.
