### Post by darshana on 2009-11-07
Ok the problem is.. in battery mode your screen will go to sleep mode before it runs the screen saver. 

open System>Preferences>Screen saver 

and click on the Power Management button
now it should have a tab related to batter power. click on that and increase the monitor sleep time.
