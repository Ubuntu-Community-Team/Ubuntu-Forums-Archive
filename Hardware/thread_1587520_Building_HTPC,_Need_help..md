Is everything running in your current setup? If so, I wouldnt worry about getting a new motherboard. You might be able to find something better than a 790i, but it wont be a huge difference. 

Buying a Socket 775 (Core2Duo and earlier Intels) motherboard is going to limit you to using, at best, Core2Quad CPUs...Core2Quads are $300+ now, while the Core2Qauds there are left are good, they arent worth that much money...... 

A 9800GX2 should run in any motherboard with a PCIe x16 slot. The main problem with 9800GX2 (and other dual GPU video cards) is the huge amount of power requirements. 

$250-300 on a motherboard? Wow, thats a lot. You should be able to get away with half that. You could get a AMD Phemon II X4 + motherboard for that amount of money.
