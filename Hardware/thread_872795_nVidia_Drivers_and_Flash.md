### Post by SZorg on 2008-07-28
nvidia refuses to open up their drivers, and says that their proprietary drivers are "good enough." So on one hand we can't be too terribly happy with nvidia because they will not allow US in on their game, but at least they support Linux.

That said, I have had similar experiences. The "nv" drivers work fine (great, even) for everything that doesn't require 3D performance.

As of the moment I, personally, do not have a solution (although I'd love to hear one.) All I can say is that it is likely a tradeoff.

You may try, though, running your system with the nvidia drivers but WITHOUT compiz or 3D effects enabled. See if that has an effect.
