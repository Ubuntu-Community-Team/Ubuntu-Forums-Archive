### Post by arleslie on 2009-01-28
If it was recently a windows computer, make sure the speakers aren't muted or turn down low on the windows side. Many people think that that the sound controls on the linux side should fix this, but I had this problem when I installed Ubuntu on my Toshiba Satellite L35, I got tired of the problem installed windows again, turns out my speakers were muted, went back on to Ubuntu and I had full sound.
