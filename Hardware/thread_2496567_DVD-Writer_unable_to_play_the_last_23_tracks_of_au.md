### Post by Autodave on 2024-04-06
This has had me thinking.  My guess is that the files on the CD are corrupted when written to the CD.  For instance: if there was just a bit or 2 missing from the burned track, I would think that it wold play just fine on anything.  However, when you went to copy that file, the copying is interrupted because of the missing bit or two.  That would lock up the machine.

I would try creating the CD again....use a slow burn speed.  You may also want to run a CD cleaning disc first and a shot of compressed air into the CD drive might not be a bad idea.
