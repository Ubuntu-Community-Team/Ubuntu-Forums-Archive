### Post by coffeecat on 2008-07-19
> **rado3105 said:**
> Please could you help me chose motherboard with integrated graphic card 100% running linux. I dont know if chose AMD or Intel. I just need quiet pc with minimal power consumption, no any speed machine for playing games. Just want motherboard only from ASUS or GIGABYTE. Please help.

Your safest bet would be a motherboard with an Intel chipset with Intel graphics. Just avoid the very latest chipsets to ensure that the drivers will work. If you want to limit yourself to Asus and Gigabyte, find 2 or 3 models with an Intel chipset that are in your price range and google them in [www.google.com/linux]("http://www.google.com/linux") to see if others have had good experience or otherwise. And/or search this forum with the motherboard model - then you'll know whether or not you can expect Ubuntu to work.
