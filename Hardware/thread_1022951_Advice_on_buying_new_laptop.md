### Post by hardyn on 2009-01-08
what metalf said...

pay extra special attention to wifi chipset.  make sure that it will work.

video support in linux goes as follows:
intel
nvidia
ati
...
..

That would be another chipset you want to watch out for.

depending on what you buy, some of the special keys might not work, and some of the extras like card readers do not work (mine might work know... but i haven't had it for so long i just don't really care anymore)
