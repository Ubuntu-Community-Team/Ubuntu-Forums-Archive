### Post by jespdj on 2009-02-23
Looks like your harddisk has died... :(

If even the BIOS doesn't see it, then there's most likely something wrong with the harddisk itself. Open up your computer and check if all the cables are correctly connected to the harddisk. Try connecting the harddisk to another power connector.
