### Post by deadflowr on 2013-11-15
Try this method.
Open the dash/menu of ubuntu and type "software and updates".
Then when that program opens go to the tab marked additional drivers.
select it and let it run.
Then look over the slection to choose from.
Best option is usually "Recommended".
Edit: It might also say proprietary, tested.
The top entry is usually the safe choice.

Look over this for more help
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI)
