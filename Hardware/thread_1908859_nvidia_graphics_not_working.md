Why did you upgrade your ubuntu 10.04LTS to ubuntu 10.10? 10.04 LTS will be updated until April 2013, while 10.10 has passed end of life, so it is not getting any updates (not even security updates). If you have a backup image of your 10.04 LTS system, I suggest that you restore it and wait until about July, when I expect an upgrade to 12.04 LTS will be offered. Or maybe that you make a fresh install. Consider also Xubuntu and Lubuntu, because Ubuntu 12.04 needs much more 'horsepower' than 10.04.
