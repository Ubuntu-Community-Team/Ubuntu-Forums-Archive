### Post by Lirein on 2011-01-29
It is  known that some modern platform for QST in the form factor ITX or Barebone do not cope well with the cooling, even with good fans - they simply  do not exhibit momentum, respectively, the temperature or adjust the  speed is not quite adequate.  Solutions for Windows already exist, but for Linux this decision was not (except open SDK from Intel).  I had realized [the driver]("http://lirein.org.ru/cgi.php/index?topic=Bio.QSTFanControl") to control the fan. Is stable for nearly six months. 
  For those who do not like noise or vice versa  overheating of the processor on the motherboard say Intel DG45FC can  easily compile the driver. The README file describes the steps for configuring the drivers for your specific cooling system.
