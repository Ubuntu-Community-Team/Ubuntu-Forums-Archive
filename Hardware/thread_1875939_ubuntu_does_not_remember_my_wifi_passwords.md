### Post by madvinegar on 2012-01-13
Go to "edit connections", go to the wireless tab and chose your network. Click edit, give your password and on the bottom of the tab tick the box "available to all users".
You will be asked for your password again and as soon as you give it, your connection will switch off and then on again.
Your password has now been stored.
