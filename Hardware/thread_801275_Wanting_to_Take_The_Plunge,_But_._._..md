### Post by barnex on 2008-05-20
Ubuntu can have problems detecting a second monitor if it is connected after the computer has started up (although efforts have been made to improve this in 8.04). So you might want to connect the monitor before you boot. It may however be sufficient to restart only your X-server (ctrl-alt-backspace) to achieve the same result.
I hope this helps, good luck.
