### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2013-05-25
9.01 means it's probably based on Catalyst 13-1.
Precise also has a Catalyst 13-4 experimental driver (fglrx-installer-experimental-12) in the proposed repo. The versioning is very confusing since AMD tends to randomly interchange dots/dashes in the version, and Ubuntu often uses pre-release versions not available to the general public.
This helps me: [http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Category:Releases](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Category:Releases)
