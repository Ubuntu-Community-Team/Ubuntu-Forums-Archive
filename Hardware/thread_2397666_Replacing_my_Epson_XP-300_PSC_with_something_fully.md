### Post by The Cog on 2018-08-01
I bought an HP Envy 5030 recently (cheapest of the Envy range and I think they all run the same software). I entered the wireless key through the LCD panel. It appeared on my Ubuntu printers list without me even clicking add printer. I didn't use the USB lead - with wireless the whole family can use it. And the scanner is driven by the printer's web page - choose size, colour or monochrome, and gif or pdf and click download. I'm very happy with it.
