### Post by Favux on 2012-12-28
Hi Drew U,

I'm assuming your Intuos5 worked in Precise out of the box.

For pressure sensitivity in Gimp.  Edit > Preferences > Input Devices > Configure Extended Input Devices.  In the Device drop down select stylus and in the Mode drop down select Screen.  Do the same for eraser.
