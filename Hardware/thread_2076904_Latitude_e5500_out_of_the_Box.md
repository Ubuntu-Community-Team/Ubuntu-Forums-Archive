### Post by The Cog on 2012-10-27
Yup. I use it at work. No problems at all. Nice laptop.

Caveats:
I use Xubuntu, don't remember if I ever tried Ubuntu on that laptop.
My laptop didn't come with bluetooth so no guarantees there
My laptop only had a DVD reader, not writer so writing is not tested.

Despite those caveats, I don't think you will have any problems.
Try a live CD if you're not sure. Apart from being slow running from CD, they are a good indication of compatibility.
