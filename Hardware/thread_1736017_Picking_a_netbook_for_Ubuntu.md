### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2011-04-23
> **wittgenstein66 said:**
> Aha! But the Mini 10 seems to fit the budget. Any experience with the Mini 10?

Some of the Dell Mini 10 netbooks have a Broadcom wireless card. Unless you want to install the Broadcom driver, I would look for a Mini 10 that has the Intel wireless card. 

I have a Toshiba NB305 that has the same specs and worked out-of-the-box with Ubuntu. The NB305 is in your price range; however, don't substitute any other model for the NB305 without researching it first as Toshiba's are know for not being Linux friendly.
