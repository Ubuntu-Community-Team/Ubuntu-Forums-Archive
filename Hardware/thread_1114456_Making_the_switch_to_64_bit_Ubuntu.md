### Post by Net_Resident on 2009-04-02
It depends on your hardware and your demands. In all likelihood it is a non issue for you. One of the main reasons that people run 64bit instead of 32bit is to utilize in excess of 3.2GB DRAM. I have a system with 4GB DRAM plus video card memory and other assorted physical memory spaces and I still use 32 bit because there is no real pressure for me to do otherwise. Hope that helps.
