### Post by Dennis N on 2015-02-04
> **abduljones said:**
> ... I can't reach the Accept button and further the installation. The box itself won't move or scroll and I can't shrink it...

Some windows are too big for the screen, and don't resize - this may be one of them, with the Accept button below the screen and unreachable. The solution is to drag the window up with the mouse until the button is visible.

While Holding Alt key down, place cursor anywhere inside the window and press and hold left mouse button down. You can then drag the window up with the mouse to see the button.
