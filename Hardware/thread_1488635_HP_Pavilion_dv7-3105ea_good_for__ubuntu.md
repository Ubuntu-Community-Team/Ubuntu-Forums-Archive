### Post by quinne on 2010-05-22
My HP DV7 3165dx is a little different -- AMD Turion II with ATI Radeon grfx.  However, I felt compelled to mention that, since having bought  this machine a couple of weeks ago, I've tried half a dozen Linux OSs, looking for one where the wireless internet would work (and believe me, I jumped through ALOT of hoops in the terminal, trying to make various ones work, to no avail.)  All of them had major issues, except Ubuntu 10.04 64 bit which worked perfectly.  Also,  I've had zero issues with the video card/display, which is refreshing (all of the others had various problems -- some small, some not so small.)  Yes, there are other things that need tweaking, but I imagine most of are used to that with Linux.  Overall, I'm very pleased with Lucid Lynx, and am confident that the remaining bugs will work out with time.

I know the machines aren't exact, but if you're like me, trying to get feedback on net before buying, even models that are 'cousins' are of interest.
