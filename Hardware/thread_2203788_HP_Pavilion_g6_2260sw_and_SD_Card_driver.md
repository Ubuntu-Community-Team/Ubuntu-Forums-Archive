### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-02-05
Sorry to tell you this, but laptops generally require specialized drivers for their builtin memory card readers -- and those drivers are not available for Linux.

You will probable have to resort to buying a USB-based card reader and plugging it into your laptop.
