### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-17
Choice depends on how much you want to spend and how far on the "leading edge" of technology you want to be ...

Then, I had a board short out for no apparent reason.

Switched over to using Gigabyte -- and have been really impressed by their work.

Suggest you go read some reviews of recent boards to see which ones best fit your needs.  A really good review site is:
