### Post by mindmice on 2009-02-14
Xubuntu 8.1 installed without incident on an IBM Thinkpad 1200 (Celeron 500 mhz processor with 32mb base memory and an additional 128mb installed). 60G hard drive was reformated and Win XP Pro installed before Xubuntu was installed so it is a dual boot system.  Win XP is working surprisingly well considering the processor and limited memory. On start up, Xubuntu loads, progress bar works across to the end, then the screen flashes 3 times, the hard drive appears to be working then the OS hangs at that point, and the screen turns grey (not black, it appears to be lit), the hard drive light flashes a few more times then the HD light goes out, and the screen remains grey. During the screen flashes, you see the Xubuntu logo and progress bar clearly but the rest of the video is distorted (off colors, vertical bars and dots of color).  I've Ubuntu installed on a Dell Inspiron 5100 laptop and I observe the same screen effect after Ubuntu loads, just a brief flash, then the OS completes loading.  I'm guessing it is a video conflict?
