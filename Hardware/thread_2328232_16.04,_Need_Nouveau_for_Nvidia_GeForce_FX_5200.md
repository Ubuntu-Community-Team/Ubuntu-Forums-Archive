### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-06-19
The open source video drivers are installed by default. They are deactivated when we install a proprietary video driver and reactivated when we deactivate the proprietary video driver.

That machine has both an Intel video adapter and a Nvidia video adapter. It is my guess that Nouveau will not activate unless the machine is set to use the Nvidia video adapter in the BIOS. If the machine is using the Intel video adapter then an Intel driver will activate and Nouveau will be deactivated.

I am not aware that Nouveau can switch between video adapters. The open source video driver that attempts to switch between video adapters is Bumblebee.

Regards.
