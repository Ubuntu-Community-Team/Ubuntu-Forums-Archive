### Post by camorri on 2010-09-06
My HP Mini came with an Antheros wireless card. If you buy a new one, check before you buy though.

From lspci,

> 02:00.0 Network controller: Atheros Communications Inc. AR9285 Wireless Network Adapter (PCI-Express) (rev 01)

Works out of the box.
