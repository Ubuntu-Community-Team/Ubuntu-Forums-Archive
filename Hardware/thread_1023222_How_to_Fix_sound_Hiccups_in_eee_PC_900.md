### Post by emmerc on 2009-01-22
Hi everyone!!! 

I got my sound solved! 

I enabled the CPU scaling according to [http://ubuntu.wordpress.com/2005/11/04/enabling-cpu-frequency-scaling/](http://ubuntu.wordpress.com/2005/11/04/enabling-cpu-frequency-scaling/) and whenever I wish to play sound or video I put the cpu frequency to 900GHz and it goes flawlessly :p
