### Post by gale401 on 2010-09-24
Using a ethernet connection, Go to System scroll down to Administration, select Hardware Drivers, select Broadcom Driver and wait for it to load - takes about 1 min 

Then once it has loaded disconnect the ethernet wire, search in wireless icon for available connections
the faint orange light will now glow bright orange and your on.
