### Post by VeeDubb on 2010-05-17
Since you just installed, and installing is pretty quick and painless, then yes.  I would recommend you try UNR.  It's not a huge amount lighter, but it's certainly enough lighter to feel the difference.  More importantly, the interface is much better suited to the small screen size of a netbook, which improves the overall feel quite a bit.


As far as making it lighter, there are a ton of how-to's floating around on the topic.  You can make a big difference just by going into preferences and the "Startup Application" program, and disabling anything you don't use.  For instance, there's no reason for the update manager to run on every boot if you're diligent about checking manually once in a while.
