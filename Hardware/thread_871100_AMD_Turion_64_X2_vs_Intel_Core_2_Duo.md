### Post by argosreality on 2008-07-26
While the Core 2 Duo is faster than a similar speed Turion x2 in most cases you also have to look at the chipsets as well. The x1270 is worlds faster than the X3100 and has better multimedia support and capabilities as well. 

However, the intel based stuff is still better supported at this point but the x1250/70 works pretty well with restricted drivers
