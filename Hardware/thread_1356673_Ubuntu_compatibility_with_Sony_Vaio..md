### Post by BigMeanMikeRich on 2010-01-13
I'm posting this from a VAIO VPC-CW14FX right now running Karmic (9.10).  Just about everything works out of the box.  The only exceptions:
[LIST]
[*]Nvidia driver causes black screen on reboot -- search for Sony EDID on the forum for solution
[*]Brightness controls do not work -- neither the Fn keys nor Gnome brightness applet work.
[*]Wifi connection is flaky -- every few minutes it disconnects from my router at home (WEP) and at work (open).  Sometimes it reconnects within 10 seconds, sometimes it fails after about a minute and asks me to either enter WEP key or manually select a router.
[/LIST]
Surprisingly, volume keys, audio and even the built-in card readers and webcam work out of the box.
