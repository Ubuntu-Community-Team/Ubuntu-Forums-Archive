### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-11-24
You can buy those connector pigtails and simply solder them into the 5VDC and 12VDC wires coming out of the power supply.  I would research how many amps at 12VDC that the graphics card requires and compare that to the PSU specifications.  Most servers have dual PSU's, so if yours does, then you would need to investigate how to turn one off (or just physically remove it) so that you would not have a power-switchover without your GPU power patch.

Search the web, I'm sure someone has made similar power supply modifications and posted a youtube video on how to do it.
