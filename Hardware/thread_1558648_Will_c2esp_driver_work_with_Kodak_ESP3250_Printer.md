### Post by meangenegjco on 2011-02-01
I Used the following procedure for loading c2esp driver. 
1.turn off the printer.
2.Download the driver (I obtained it from sourceforge.com
3.go to system>administration>printing and clear the esp3250 from the list.
4.Right click on the download icon. select "open with g-debi installer" from the pull down menu.
5.turn on the printer
6.Follow the device installation procedure in the pop up window installing it as a kodak 5200 series with dithering.
It now works great as a printer but I am still trying to find a driver for the scanner.
