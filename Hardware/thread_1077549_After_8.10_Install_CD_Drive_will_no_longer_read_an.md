### Post by cariboo on 2009-02-22
When a CD/DVD drive stops reading disks. it almost always is a hardware problem. I would suggest that seeing as your laptop is still under warranty that you get the drive replaced. I would also suggest you remove Ubuntu, as some manufacturers us that for an excuse not to honour the warranty.

Back up all your important data before you send your laptop in for repair, as they will more than likely reformat your hard drive first to see if that will fix the problem.
