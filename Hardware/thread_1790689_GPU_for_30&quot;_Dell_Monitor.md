### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2011-06-25
I have a silent GT210, which you can get real cheap now. It works fine for HD video thanks to vdpau and has a max resolution of 2560x1600 so it should be OK.

ATI are OK, but I have had nothing but trouble with the proprietary drivers. If you do go with them, stick with the open source driver.
