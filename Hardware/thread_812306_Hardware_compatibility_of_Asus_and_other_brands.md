### Post by dogscoff on 2008-07-08
My laptop was built by a small company that specialises in linux-friendly builds. It is based on an ASUS S96s if that helps.

If you are in the EU, check out [http://efficientpc.co.uk/laptops/anubis/](http://efficientpc.co.uk/laptops/anubis/)

If you are not, maybe you can buy Ubuntu pre-installed in your on country. I know there are companies in the States who do this, and there must be more elsewhere. 

Although you miss out on the fun of installing your own OS< it's nice to know that the hardware is definitely compatible, and that the machine will just power up into Linux when you take it out of the box.
