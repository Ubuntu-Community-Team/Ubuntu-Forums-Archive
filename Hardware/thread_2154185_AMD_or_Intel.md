Almost four years ago I built my system, based on an AMD Phenom II X2 running at 3.1 GHz (when required). The CPU stays at 40 C unless I play videos or render video in Cinelerra. Chassis airflow plays an important role. One fan blows air in across the hard drive, another exhausts it out the back.

With a 6 GB/s SSD for root, I doubt if a RAMdisk will make much difference -- and it takes time to set it up.
