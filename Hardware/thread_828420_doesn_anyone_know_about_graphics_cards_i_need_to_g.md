As far as I know, [stuck pixels]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stuck_pixel") are a problem with the monitor not the graphics card so in this case you should replace your monitor not your graphics card.

The 7900 GS you have is more than enough for what you mentioned, compiz and even a lot of games but In case you still want to replace it for some reason, I recommend a 8600 GT, it's powerful but not too powerful, it'll be very comfortable running compiz and in case you want to play games it'll run a lot of games a decent speeds, there are 256 MB and 512 MB versions available, some are passive/silent cooled(no fan) so this is probably what you want for minimum noise.
