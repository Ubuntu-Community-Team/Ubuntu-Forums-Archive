### Post by ushimitsudoki on 2009-01-09
I have a couple of bluetooth mice, here's my thoughts on the matter:

1. My laptop has bluetooth built-in, so I don't need a USB dongle or the like. Clean and one less thing to worry about.

2. The "media pc" downstairs. Bluetooth has a better range than most wireless devices, so it fits the layout of that room better.

Bluetooth takes about 3 sec. to connect, I would estimate. Rarely the device is "lost" and you have to re-scan for it - it takes one command from the command line.

Also, sad to say, KDE4 bluetooth support is broken like every other third thing in KDE4.
