### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-04-15
I think what is happening to you is the mouse is shutting off transmission when it has not been moved for a while (to save power) and then not connecting back up properly when you do eventually move it.

I have a wireless keyboard with a trackball that also stops transmitting when left untouched to save power, but once I do hit a key or 'roll the ball' it reconnects to the little USB receiver instantly so I rarely even notice the delay on re-power-up.  I type on it all the time (even now) and it works very well

I think your particular wireless mouse is just not designed well.

Most likely, you will need to select a different model (or use a wired one).
