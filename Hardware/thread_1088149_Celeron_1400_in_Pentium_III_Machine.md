### Post by 00b00nt00 on 2009-03-06
I believe that the Pentium IIIs and PIII based Celerons over 1 GHz are Tualatin based, which means that you need a socket 370 mobo. If you don't know your mobo, do a Google search. Chances are, if it is slot 1, the fastest processors you can 'drop in' will be 1 GHz Coppermine PIIIs.

Are the processors you are looking at in some way modified? I'm pretty sure that Tualatins don't support slot 1 without some hacking (though I could be wrong).
