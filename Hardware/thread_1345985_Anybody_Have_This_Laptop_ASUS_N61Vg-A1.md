### Post by palmira on 2010-05-30
Maybe a very important issue has been neglected in this thread. Doesn't the NVIDIA card in the ASUS N61 include Optimus? That's a new system switching automatically between an Intel card and the powerful NVIDIA card depending on whether the full power is needed or not.

A quick web search shows up that there's currently no support for this technology in Linux. It seems that NVIDIA doesn't even have plans to change this. I also got the impression that nobody succeeded in making at least the NVIDIA card work. Installing the proprietary driver ends up in problems.

So, it seems likely that those new NVIDIA cards in laptops cannot be used in Linux at the moment. Instead, one would have to use the -- probably automatically detected -- weak Intel graphics.
