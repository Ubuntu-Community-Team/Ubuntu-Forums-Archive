### Post by bgerlich on 2009-01-24
1. When posting code put [*code]  [*/code] (without the "*"!) around it, makes your posts easier to read. You can edit your previous post to add them.

2. Try adding this line to "Device" section
```
Device
...
**Driver "intel"**
EndSection

```
of your /etc/X11/xorg.conf

(above [*code] [*/code] ,without "*" of course, at work)

3. Be weary of this bug [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/268615](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/268615). It shouldn't (but could) affect your laptop and is fixed in newest Intel drivers, informing you just in case.

If something goes wrong post your xorg.0.log again
