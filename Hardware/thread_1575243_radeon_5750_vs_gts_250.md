### Post by cascade9 on 2010-09-15
Honestly, I have no idea why some people have issues with ATI and others dont. I guess its some combonation of luck, skill and persistence. 

ATI 5750  vs GTS250? For windows gaming, they swap back and forth depending on what game you are playing- some games are faster with the 5750, some with the GTS250. Example- 

[http://www.hwcompare.com/graphics/compare.php?c=GeForce-GTS-250-1GB-vs-Radeon-HD-5750-1024MB-1GB-](http://www.hwcompare.com/graphics/compare.php?c=GeForce-GTS-250-1GB-vs-Radeon-HD-5750-1024MB-1GB-)

BTW, if your getting a standalone video card, save yourself a few dollars, pass on the M4A88TD-V EVO and get a M4A87TD/USB3 (or a different brand motherboard with the 870 northbridge/850 southbridge. Its cheaper, and does everything the 88TD does (apart from possible core unlocking, but with an Athlon II X4 there are no cores to unlock anyway).
