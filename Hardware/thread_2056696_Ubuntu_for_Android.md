### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-09-13
The main concern expressed in that other article is that, since the phone providers customize the OS builds for their phones, they have basically NO incentive to do the same with an Ubuntu build.  Given that it often takes MONTHS for a new Android OS upgrade to get customized and rolled out by all the major ISPs, it could be a VERY long time before they offer a phone with Ubuntu on it.
