### Post by Joe of loath on 2011-01-22
I have a 1545, which is quite similar.

All you have to worry about with most laptops are the wifi and the graphics. The wifi will be a broadcom chip, they suck, but with a bit of work you can get it working with Linux. Or, for around 15 USD you can upgrade to/buy separately an intel wifi card (I bought a 5100), which will work 'out of the box'.

The graphics, I have no idea. I don't own any modern ATI cards, although for the older ones I have, the open source drivers work fine. The 'hardware drivers' application should install the ATI drivers automatically though, so no need to worry.
