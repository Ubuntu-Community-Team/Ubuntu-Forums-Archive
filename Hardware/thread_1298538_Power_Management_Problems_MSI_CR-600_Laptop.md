### Post by WestCoastSuccess on 2010-01-12
You'll likely need to update your BIOS - I had the same problem and installed BIOS A1683NMS.X0N, which you can download from the MSI site.

See the instructions I posted here:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam/MSICR600#preview](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam/MSICR600#preview)
