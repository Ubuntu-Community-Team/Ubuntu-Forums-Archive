### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2015-11-15
System Settings > Software&Updates > Additional drivers (the rightmost tab).
Give it some moments and it will present you a list of proprietary AMD drivers (code name: fglrx). Choose and apply the recommend one. Reboot and done, you now have the proper drivers for your graphics card.
