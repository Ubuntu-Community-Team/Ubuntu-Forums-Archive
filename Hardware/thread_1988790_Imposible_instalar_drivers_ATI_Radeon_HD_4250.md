### Post by coffeecat on 2012-05-28
Hi.

First thing. The common language of the main parts of the forum is English, so you are less likely to get help using another language. If you need to post in Spanish, then you are welcome to use one of the Spanish-language forums in the [LoCo Teams section]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=183").

I have put your post through Google Translate and although Google Translate is having difficulty with some of the text, it seems that you are having problems with the proprietary AMD driver for your HD4250 card in 12.04, both from the Additional Drivers utility and the one downloaded from AMD's website. I have a straightforward suggestion for you. If the default open-source driver gives you adequate performance, then use that. I get enough 3d acceleration from a number of AMD Radeon cards with the open source driver, quite sufficient to give me a functional Unity desktop in Ubuntu 12.04. I never bother with the proprietary driver.
