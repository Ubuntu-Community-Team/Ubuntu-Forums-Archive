> I just install ubuntu version 10 for the first time on my HP pavilion dv5 but have not been able to make the in-built microphone work. Can somebody help

I had a similar problem with my laptop. When u said ubuntu version 10 i assume its 10.04.

There are two channels to your mic if you go to volume control, left and right. Only when one is muted, the other will work. If you don't find this u can consider installing padevchooser/pavucontrol

One of them controls the 3.5mm jack while the other controls the inbuilt mic. Good luck.
