It is a heat problem.  Discussed in another thread with more than 120 contribution - thread name "Acer Aspire 5315 Shuts Down".

In a nutshell, this laptop (and many other "Acers") control the fan through the special bloatware that the manufacturer adds on. Unfortunately it is only accessible through -- Win Vista (which is pre-installed).  Please read last 20 or so posts in the "Acer Aspire 5315 Shuts Down" thread.
