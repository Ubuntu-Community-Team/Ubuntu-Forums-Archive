### Post by markbuntu on 2008-06-28
I have a Logitech Quickcam Communicate STX and it works OK for me. It seems to have a half-decent frame rate and the color is good in varying light conditions and it has a wide range of focus and the mic works OK. 

It is a webcam and you really cannot expect videocam quality for $20-40 but if you want to use Ekiga or Skype or cheese etc, I will have to say that this one is good enough for that.

I thought about buying a $100 one but then I thought, why bother for a little tiny window on my monitor to talk to people who are looking at me in another little tiny window.

btw, V4l2 does not recognize it but V4l finds it by name.
