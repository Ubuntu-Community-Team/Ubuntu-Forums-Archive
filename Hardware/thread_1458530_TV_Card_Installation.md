### Post by halj32 on 2010-04-20
first open Synaptic Package Manager
then install
linux-firmware-nonfree
make sure youve installed all the video codecs (ffmpeg lib-xine etc)
install Kaffeine or any other media player that has tv card support,
in kaffiene click on television channels
then source, then your card, then scan for channels
pick the ones you want..
hope this helped
