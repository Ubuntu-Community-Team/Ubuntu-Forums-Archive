I'm pretty busy these says to recalibrate & to confirm what exactly could be wrong. Surprisingly it now reports 3hrs [but never gets past the 2hr+ mark and shorts down ABROPTLY] 

In the mean time:

1. Power down [turn off] your laptop.

2. Plug it to the [power supply] mains to charge till the charging light goes off [or change color in your own case (I use hp tx1417)]

3. Power on your laptop, and do nothing than leave it overnight [whenever you can get it idle] till the power discharges TOTALLY. 

4. Connect the laptop back to mains.

This should solve it else, you can consider flaging it as a bug.
