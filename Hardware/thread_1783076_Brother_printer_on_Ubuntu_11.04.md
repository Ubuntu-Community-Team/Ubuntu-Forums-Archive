### Post by frankiethepill on 2011-06-16
I'm not sure what sort of printer your model is, but I had a problem with a Brother multifunction printer under 11.04. It would sometimes work but usually got stuck with the printer itself seemingly starting to print but then just waiting ... and waiting!. The only way I could get a print job done was to uninstall the driver, and reinstall it, when it would work for a few minutes. 
Apparently there was (for my printer) a problem with 11.04 and the driver, and the only solution was to reinstall 10.04 or 10.10- which works just fine. I'm sorry but I haven't tried the scanning functions so can't comment on that.
