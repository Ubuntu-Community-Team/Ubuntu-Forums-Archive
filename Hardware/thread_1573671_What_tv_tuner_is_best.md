### Post by IcarusR on 2010-09-13
Happauge are generally well supported & work well.

I had a Hauppauge WinTV NOVA T USB, it worked well with my house aerial but was useless with the supplied aerial. Tried in several locations using different transmitters. Even tried with an amplified aerial without luck.
Ended up giving up & getting a refund.

Have also used internal pci cards, again Happauge & a kworld. Happauge worked well, kworld not so good. Both in server with house aerial.
