### Post by ianhaycox on 2008-09-29
I'm not familar with the D-Link router, but first off, if you are using a wired ethernet connection then no software should be required.

Once you have connected the Telephone cable and ethernet cables try typing,

[http://192.168.1.1/](http://192.168.1.1/)

into the address bar of Firefox and you should be presented with various configuration pages from the router. Enter the username/password given to you by your ISP and connect to the Internet.

Once the modem has syncronised you should be good to go.
