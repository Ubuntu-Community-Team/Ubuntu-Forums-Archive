### Post by sola on 2017-03-12
In theory, the Win10 edition and the Developer/Ubuntu edition are the exact same hw wise. (which is a first, there were always differences in previous generations)

I have just installed the stock 16.04.2 Ubuntu on the XPS 13 (Core i5, FHD, 8GB, 256GB) that arrived with Win10 Home.

Most of the things seem to work very well (suspend-resume, function keys, backlight control, bluetooth, wifi) but I haven't tested anything in much detail yet. 

Battery life seems to be 6-8 hours with light browsing. This is without much tuning (tlp installed but that is it).
