I had just been messing with CUPS at [http://127.0.0.1:631](http://127.0.0.1:631) because of a previous problem. I don't know if I may have messed something up.

---

### Post by feedmecereal on 2008-08-28
OK, I figured it out. I went into Printers-->Right-clicked and selected Properties-->Connection and changed the Printer Type from "Network Printer" to "Local or Detected Printer."
