### Post by bloggerama on 2009-03-12
Hi there, i have been trying to fix this problem for ages, read every forum, then only managed to work it out from messing around. Try this...
Double click on your audio icon in the top corner, then mute FRONT in the playback menu (if you don't have front then add it by clicking preferences and ticking FRONT). Make sure that though FRONT is muted, it should be on 100%, i.e. take it to the top, and this should mute your speakers whilst keeping the headphones on.

Hope this helps :)
