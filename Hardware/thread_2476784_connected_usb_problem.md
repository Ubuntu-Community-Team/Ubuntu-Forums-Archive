### Post by Autodave on 2022-07-07
USB hubs can fail....and they do fail.  First of all, is this a powered hub?  If not, my guess is that either the power supply in your PC is failing or you have something drawing way too much power that is plugged into your hub.  If this is a powered hub, then I would guess that it is/has failed.

I have quite a collection of failed hubs here.....powered and non-powered.  The one thing that I can tell you is this: the less expensive the hub, the quicker it dies.

If everything works when you plug them right into the PC, then get a new hub.....a powered one.  Having a non-powered one is like taking an extension cord and plugging your refrigerator, air conditioner, TV, and hot tub in and expecting them all to work at the same time.
