### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-02-25
Did you try installing one of the "Additional Drivers?"  This is probably a Broadcom wireless card that doesn't have an open source driver so an "additional" (non-open) driver must be installed after Ubuntu is done.  Search Ubuntu for "additional drivers" and see if one is offered for your wireless card.
