### Post by dabl on 2010-07-14
I have a GTX260, running a Samsung SyncMaster 1100 CRT display, and it is beautiful.  I use the downloaded proprietary 256.35 driver, and the built in Desktop Effects, on Kubuntu 10.04.

On your other question, the amount of memory is not the main consideration on the architecture choice.  If your CPU and chipset are designed to support a 64-bit OS, then my own humble opinion is that would be the best choice.  The "32-bit vs. 64-bit" question is an old debate:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=765428](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=765428)

Be advised that Adobe recently withdrew their 64-bit flash plugin, due to bugginess/security vulnerabilities, and so you'll be required to work around that particular issue (workarounds do exist):  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1491268](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1491268)
