### Post by coffeecat on 2011-10-09
I did a google on that model looking for the specifications. That's about 2006-era. Correct? If I found the right one, it has onboard Intel graphics with the 865 chipset, which will probably be problematic with the latest releases of Ubuntu. Unfortunately, it looks as though the motherboard has only 3 PCI slots, no AGP and definitely no PCIe. Finding a suitable PCI graphics card with a decent GPU is going to be a challenge, but see what others say.

To be sure I've got the right specs, take the side panel off and see how many expansion slots there are near the back towards the base.
