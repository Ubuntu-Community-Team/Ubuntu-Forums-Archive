### Post by Jagoly on 2012-04-18
Yes, and it should work very well at that. Multiple monitors are one of the few things amd's linux drivers do better than nvidia's. A Dvi-vga adapter should work without any hassle. And I'm assuming that this would be for a productivity setup, and the 5450 is very energy effecient as well. It is EXTREMLY slow though, but for office apps it's fine.
