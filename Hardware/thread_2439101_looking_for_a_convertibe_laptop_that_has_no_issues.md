### Post by lovix on 2020-05-13
I have a Lenovo X1 Yoga gen3.  Its got critical issues running the fan properly under Ubuntu.  But if you wont be asking it to do processor heavy tasks, that might work for you.  If you are curious to the extent of the challenges there, I have posted here about it.

I find the keyboard to be quite good, and Ubuntu 20.04 works right from the get-go (no fingerprint reader or mobile broadband, though and it wont run the other versions of Ubuntu I tried).  Stylus and touchscreen, rotation works.  Sturdy hinge stays at any angle except when almost 360 degrees.

In Windows, I have seen pressure sensitivity and I believe palm rejection function fine.

It is a nice machine. But I have got to find something else.  Please post what you decide on!
