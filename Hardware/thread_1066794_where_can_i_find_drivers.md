### Post by Alexis Phoenix on 2009-02-11
Most of the hardware should be supported by the Ubuntu kernel.  If you have a problem with a particular item, please list it here (for example, you may need firmware).  If you have an ATI or NVidia graphics card, you may want to obtain a driver from their web-site.  If you have a modem with a Conexant chip, you will find a driver on the Linuxant web site.
