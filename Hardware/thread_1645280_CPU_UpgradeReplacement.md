### Post by Frogs Hair on 2010-12-14
I had no problem locating your mother board and because it has a Pentium 4 it seems to be a 478 socket. With out the board specifications it's hard know what upgrades are possible. I did find a possible fit which was a Pentium 4 3.2 ghz 478 socket mobile cpu , but again no board specifications. The bios will also figure into the upgrade equation , as you may have to update that as well.
