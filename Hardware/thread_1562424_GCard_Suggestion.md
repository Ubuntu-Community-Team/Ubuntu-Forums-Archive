### Post by cascade9 on 2010-08-28
I dont know if you will find a card to suit that computer for $20-30 unless you go for a 2nd hand card. 

It will need a 1/2 height card, in PCI or AGP. Minimum $40 for a 1/2 height PCI card at newegg (and that card is very, very old). Minimum $60 for a slightly less old AGP card......

If you got a new card, you would probably be best off getting a nVidia 8400GS PCI card, probably about $50-60.
