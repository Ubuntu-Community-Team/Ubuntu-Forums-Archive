### Post by cascade9 on 2010-12-11
I havent had any issues with the nVidia drivers, and if you watch lots of 1080p HD video then nVidia is what I would get (VDPAU is great, ATI XvBA is, well, 'buggy' is being nice). 

A GT220 is slightly slower than the 4650, a GT240 is pretty much the same 3D perfromance. A GRS450 would be faster. 

If your happy with your gaming on the 4650, then a GT240 would be good. If you think you could do with a few more frames, get a GTS450. IMO anyway.
