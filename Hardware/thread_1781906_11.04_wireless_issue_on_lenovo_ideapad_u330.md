### Post by tobiasquinn on 2011-06-14
Hi,
If its anything like my Lenovo (Ideapad b570) then you need to blacklist the acer_wmi module for wireless to work:

You could create the file:

[FONT=Courier New]sudo gedit /etc/modprobe.d/blacklist-acer.conf[/FONT]

Add the line:

[FONT=Courier New]blacklist acer_wmi[/FONT]

Reboot and see if that helps...
