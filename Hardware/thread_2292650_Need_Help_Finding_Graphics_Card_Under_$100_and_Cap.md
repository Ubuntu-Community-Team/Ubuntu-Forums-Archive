### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2015-08-30
Hi and welcome.

A [cheap Nvidia GT630]("http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=9SIA70F31J4758&cm_re=gt630-_-9SIA70F31J4758-_-Product") chipset based card meets and exceeds your requirements.
Although it needs proprietary nvidia drivers for optimal performance, the native open source driver *nouveau *(default driver in Ubuntu) has also good support for this card. The proprietary drivers can also be easily installed: System Settings > Software & Updates > Additional drivers. Select and apply the recommended nvidia driver, apply, reboot, done!
Enjoy.
