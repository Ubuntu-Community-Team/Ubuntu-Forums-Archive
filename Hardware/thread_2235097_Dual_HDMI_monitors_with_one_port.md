### Post by bcschmerker on 2014-07-19
Two monitors on one HDMI port?  Sorry, no can do - High Definition Multimedia Interface can only support one A/V display on a port.  Your Asus® M4A78T-E should also have a DE-15 socket for analog VGA; the internal ATi® Radeon® in the Advanced Micro Devices® 780GX/SB710 chipset can run DVI-D or HDMI, but not both simultaneously, in addition to VGA.
