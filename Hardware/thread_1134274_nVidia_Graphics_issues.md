### Post by tcfan on 2009-04-23
Not sure if I'm the only one but I have a Compaq Presario Q50-215NR notebook with the nVidia Gforce 8200M graphics.  When I use the proprietary nVidia drivers, version 180, I get a complete GUI lockup no matter what I do.  It could take 5 minutes, it could take 30 seconds after logging in.  However, downgrading to version 173 works flawlessly for me.  This is all on the final 9.04 release.

This isn't a topic for help, more of a general statement and observation on my behalf.  Hopefully this can help others with the same graphics card/computer.
