### Post by TeamRocket1233c on 2011-11-08
CPU: Core I7.
Mobo: a good ASUS ATX mobo that will work with the Core I7.
Optical drive: Blu-ray burner.
Hard drive: 1Tb.
Ram: 8Gb.
Case: nice clear tower case.
Sound card: A nice sound card that will support all the major surround sound formats.
Video card: A nice hi-def video card.
OS: Either Fedora 15, Ubuntu 11, Crunchbang 10, Debian 6, or PC-BSD 8.
Monitor: 34" Bravia HDTV.
Speakers: At least a 5.1 ch home theatre sound system.

That enough for 'ya? XD
