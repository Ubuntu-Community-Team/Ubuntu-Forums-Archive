### Post by mlempenau on 2018-10-13
Using 18.04 with all the updates on a desktop.  Asus MB H81M-K about two years old.  When I turn on it beeps once like it should.  Then I see the Asus logo with the ability to go to Bios.  After that there is nothing.  If I try to boot holding down the shift key there is still nothing.  Can't get grub to appear.  I put a bootable 18.04 install disk in the cd and still nothing.  Played with it off and on for over an hour ... suddenly it booted up and worked fine.  I used it for over an hour.  Went to system log but didn't find anything of note.  Finally I shut it off again and I'm back to the no boot problem.  At this point I am thinking it's the motherboard.  Is there something else I could do?

### Post by 23dornot23d on 2018-10-13
It could also be the hard drive - not working properly ............

Have you tried booting from a USB drive with a installation of a OS on it ...........

When you press the <ESCAPE> key at boot do you get a choice of boot options ...... and if so choose to boot from cd / dvd if you have a live cd / dvd

Just a couple of things that might at least tell you if its just the hard drive failing - or if its something else on the motherboard itself then
you will probably still have problems booting from a CD/DvD or a USB stick .......

Best of Luck

---

### Post by mlempenau on 2018-10-13
Thanks for the help.  Great thoughts.  I inserted my USB stick and went into bios to try and boot from it.  When I did this it booted from the HD properly.  It occurred to me that maybe the bios was at fault.  So I checked and found out there was a updated bios.  After updating everything seems to be working.  Forgetting to update bios is just another thing I have to remember to update!
