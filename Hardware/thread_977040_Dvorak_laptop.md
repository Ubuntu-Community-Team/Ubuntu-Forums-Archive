### Post by starcannon on 2008-11-09
Go to:
System->Preferences->Keyboard

Then click on "Layout" Tab:
Then click on "Add"
Then under "Layouts" drop down choose your localization.
Then under "Variants" drop down choose dvorak.

Enjoy
