### Post by rockingrector on 2010-05-31
OK, solved it  myself! Install Adobe Digital Editions using Wine. Install Calibre. Download books to Adobe Digital editions, then click on "Add books" in calibre. Works perfectly. Calibre automatically detects the E-reader, and it's easy to transfer books to it.
