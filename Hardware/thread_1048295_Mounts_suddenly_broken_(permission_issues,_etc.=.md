You know you don't have to mount your /dev/sda5 & /dev/sda6 twice since /dev/sda6 is already mounted at / while /dev/sda5 is your /home.

And since your external harddrive (/dev/sdb1 & /dev/sdb5) is not always plugged in when you boot into Ubuntu, I would either remove those two entries (/dev/sdb1 & /dev/sdb5) or place a # in front of them to comment them out.

Remove the last entry in /etc/fstab also.

```
/dev/sda7                                  /media/sda7    swap         defaults                          0  0
```
