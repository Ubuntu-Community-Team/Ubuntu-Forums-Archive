### Post by TheFu on 2024-07-06
Just another idea, but have you checked that the USB ports are actually connected to the motherboard?  The ones on the back of a desktop don't need a wire connector, since they are directly "on" the motherboard, but those that connect to the front-panel do need a connecting wire from the motherboard.  Unlikely, but perhaps the builder didn't make those connections?
