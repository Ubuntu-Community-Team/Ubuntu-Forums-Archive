### Post by QIII on 2017-03-03
It appears that 512 is the max RAM -- and it is SDRAM.

The disk capacity is likely a non-issue, but its physical size will be.  There were a couple of "experimental" sizes for a while.

The biggest issue you will have is probably the ATI Rage Mobility graphics adapter.  That is old and the only driver that will work with it is the open source Radeon driver.
