### Post by mythsmith on 2012-07-10
It's a long time (at least 6 months) that I have a very annoying problem with my laptop and now I want to share.
1. Start the laptop. Login screen appears. Both mousepad and USB mouse works perfectly.
2. Login. Desktop displays. Both mousepad and USB mouse works perfectly.
3. After few seconds from the desktop loading, the USB mouse starts moving in a strange way. It seems it have some sort of "exponential acceleration": you do a movement, the mouse does not respond for few milliseconds, then immediately accelerates and almost teleport to the other side of the screen.
4. I tried with wireless, optical and mechanical mouses. Nothing counts. Also changing the mouse after the problem started does not change anything.
5. This problem is not constant. It only shows up sometimes. Then, the only solution is to restart the pc and hope it will not be there again. Logging in/out, restarting lightdm, nothing counts!

I've having this issue from Ubuntu 11.10. It is very difficult to track down the reason as it is not constant. But very, very annoying and sometimes embarassing.
"I have linux. See how it's fast!" - "Wow, wonderful" - "Ehm... but the mouse is not working. A moment, I just have to restart 3-4 times untill it is fixed" - "Wow, less wonderful".

Ubuntu 12.04
DELL Latitude E6510 with SSD
