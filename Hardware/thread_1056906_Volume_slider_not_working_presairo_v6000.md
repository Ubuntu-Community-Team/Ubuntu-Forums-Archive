I have a V6000 too (V6560EA).  Everything so far works without additional effort, only DNS configuration needed changing for wireless to work. 

Try checking your keyboard shortcuts to see if its configured correctly. Mine is: 

**XF86AudioRaiseVolume** for Volume Up,
**XF86AudioLowerVolume** for Volume Down 
**XF86AudioMute** for Volume Mute
