### Post by fancypiper on 2010-05-10
Be careful about selecting a replacement. I have heard that you need to use Dell parts when replacing either the motherboard or PSU.  On some models, they reversed a couple of pins on the plugs so that if you use a generic replacement for one it will burn out the other.

### Post by pricetech on 2010-05-10
Use a genuine Dell replacement.  Their power supplies are proprietary.
