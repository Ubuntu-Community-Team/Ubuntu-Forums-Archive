### Post by Xyphoid on 2009-01-22
Hya Devwillie,

I got sound to work by installing "alsamixer".
Then doing

"sudo mousepad /etc/modules.conf"

and adding:

# --- BEGIN: Generated by ALSACONF, do not edit. ---
# --- ALSACONF version 0.9.0 ---
alias char-major-116 snd
alias snd-card-0 snd-es18xx
alias sound-service-0-0 snd-mixer-oss
alias sound-service-0-1 snd-seq-oss
alias sound-service-0-3 snd-pcm-oss
alias sound-service-0-8 snd-seq-oss
alias sound-service-0-12 snd-pcm-oss
options snd major=116 cards_limit=1 device_mode=0666
options snd-es18xx index=0 dma8=1 mpu_irq=-1 irq=5
# --- END: Generated by ALSACONF, do not edit. ---

After that I did

"sudo mousepad /etc/modules"

and added (don't forget the empty line at the end. Don't know if it's needed, but it was there already):

"snd-es18xx"


I have sound now, but when I play a sound with "aplay" it keeps playing in a loop until I kill "aplay". This also happens when I try to watch a YouTube movie (which is already slow. The sound loop makes it even slower).
If anyone has a solution, please tell us. If I find a solution, I'll post it.

PS I still have the PII 365MHz in it. With 192Mb RAM. I installed ubuntu (mini.iso) and XFCE.
