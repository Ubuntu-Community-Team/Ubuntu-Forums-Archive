### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2014-06-20
> since I'm buying this model would default Nvidia drivers for Linux allow for good resolution?
In Optimus configuration, the Nvidia GPU is used for 3D rendering and Intel GPU is used for display, so the Intel driver would control the resolution. Resolution is usually not the issue for Optimus users (things like battery life and HDMI output are more problematic).
