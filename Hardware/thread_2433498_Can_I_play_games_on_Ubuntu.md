### Post by CatKiller on 2019-12-19
You can play games on Linux. [Here are some that are available](https://store.steampowered.com/linux).

Overwatch isn't Linux-native, and its anti-cheat doesn't work with Wine. In fact, for a while, use of Wine made the anti-cheat think that the player was cheating already, which led to some bans. So that one you can't.
