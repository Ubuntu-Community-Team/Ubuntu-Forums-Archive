### Post by Y^2om&amp;#7sgP on 2016-01-27
The small round battery is for holding CMOS settings when the PC is switched off, so you need to replace it.

Also, you should replace the cables you disconnected (if you haven't already donw all of this;))

You will almost certainly now need to go into BIOS and change some of the settings, things like date and time.

Depending on the PC, it 'may' automatically update things like disk drives and memory.

Once done, reboot and try again
