### Post by mikedep333 on 2009-10-29
I'm personally waiting for the creative zii to come out. It will be the consumer version of the zii egg, which sounds very nice and runs android.
[http://www.zii.com/Developer/Landing.aspx](http://www.zii.com/Developer/Landing.aspx)

However, in the meantime and if you have windows/OS X, your best bet is one of the ipod touches. Preferably go for the 32GB model over the 8GB as it has a faster processor.

The PDA market is basically being replaced by these MID (mobile internet device) and PID (personal internet device) devices.

There's also the Archos 5, which is larger, but runs a non-standard version of android (at least for now.)
[http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002OL2PLU](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002OL2PLU)
Make sure you get the android one, not the specialized linux one that won't run any apps. Also, the 32GB model is thinner.

Hope this helps.
