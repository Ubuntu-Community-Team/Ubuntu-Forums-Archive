### Post by jimv on 2008-06-24
ATI and AMD in general have both been sucking lately.  Nvidia is the way to go as far as performance and compatibility.

I use an old Dell as media server hooked to an old TV via S-video...not really any problems other than resolution sucking.  An HDTV with HDMI or even VGA should look great.  HDMI is definitely better though.

HDMI will give you great quality and shouldn't impact your performance...especially if you're running a beefy video editing machine.  Your video card should be able to handle HDMI output easily if it has HDMI output on it.
