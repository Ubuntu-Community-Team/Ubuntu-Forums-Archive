### Post by Joe of loath on 2011-03-13
The amplifiers in the motherboard are only meant for high impedance headphones and amplifier inputs, connecting up many low impedance speakers may damage them.

However, when you only have 500mW of output power, you aren't going to get any more sound by hooking up more speakers. I suggest you look into buying a decent stereo amplifier to hook them up to ;)
