### Post by IcarusR on 2009-10-19
I have a 3ware 8006-2LP with 2 1Gb WD WD10EADS-00M2B0 (green) drives attached in my server running 8.10.

Don't know how compatible 3ware are with your mobo though. But guess should be ok.
