### Post by prshah on 2011-05-21
> **Pacopag said:**
> I know that the i7s are at the top of the game right now, but, would you say that they will be significantly faster than AMD Phenom II with comparable clock speed and same number of cores? 

I'd go for the i7 any day. Not only are they much faster, they also scale much better (eg, turning off cores when not required, to reduce power usage).

However, no matter what you choose, you must pay equal attention to the supporting hardware; there's no sense to choosing a top-of-the-line processor and then sticking it into a mediocre board with cheapo RAM.

Also, I'd suggest going for twin RAM sticks, eg, 4GB x 2, instead of 1 8GB to take advantage of Dual Channel architecture for RAM. It's about 8-10% faster, usually, over single-channel.
