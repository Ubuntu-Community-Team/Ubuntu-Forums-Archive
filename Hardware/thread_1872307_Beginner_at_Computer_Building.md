### Post by hwttdz on 2011-10-30
Do you have any power indicators on on the motherboard?  And not to imply anything, but it never hurts to check that 1) power cord is plugged into the wall, 2) power supply is in "on" state.

There are quite a few cables that if you miss the cpu won't do anything sometimes because there's not a complete circuit, but like the cpu fan, if it's not connected the cpu generally won't even attempt to boot.
