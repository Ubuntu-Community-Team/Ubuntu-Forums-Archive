### Post by SeijiSensei on 2018-05-15
Another option is a SATA/IDE to USB connector.  I have this device from Vantec: [https://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16812232002](https://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16812232002).  Works like a charm and doesn't require mounting the drive in an enclosure.  Makes testing a bunch of drives much easier.  

Newegg has many other such devices, some with prices under $10.  I bought the Vantec because it had over 1,600 mostly-positive reviews.
