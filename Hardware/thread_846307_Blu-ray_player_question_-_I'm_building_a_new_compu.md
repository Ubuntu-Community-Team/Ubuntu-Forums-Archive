### Post by llama320 on 2008-07-01
i'd wait on the blu-ray, because the prices are just going to drop. Invest in a dvd writer now, and just (assuming you're building a desktop) leave a slot open for a future blu-ray drive. That way you'll have two cd drives (good for copying cds/dvds and such) and you don't have to overpay for a blu-ray player now
