### Post by lexfati on 2009-11-27
In Kubuntu 9.10 you don't have to even mess with compiz.  From the Kmenu, select Favorites > System Settings > Desktop.  Desktop Cube is one of the options under the All Effects tab.  Enable that and Desktop Cube animation and you should be ready to go.
