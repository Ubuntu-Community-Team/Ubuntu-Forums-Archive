### Post by |{urse on 2011-12-09
Looks good, From what I'm reading that specific motherboard carries a via hd audio chipset, I've had hit and miss success with via in general + linux. Also that motherboard is an nForce 630a which I just installed 10.04 on yesterday, no problems. Asrock is kind of a meh brand, but you could definitely do worse.

Looks good though, good ecc registered ram and good clean power.
The only thing I would suggest that you change is your hard-disk selection, I've had a lot of dead spinpoints come thru my shop door lately. Stick with wd or seagate, they cost more than normal at the moment due to a massive flood that took out a bunch of hard drive factories but still are worth the investment.
