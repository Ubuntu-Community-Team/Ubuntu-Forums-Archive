### Post by beboylips on 2012-08-25
I think that would not be a good idea since at higher temperatures in condensed environments such as a laptop, parts will start to melt and that would be a disaster.

 To fix such a problem, you would probably need to look at your cooling system (fans, heat-sink) and improve there. Perhaps, get a new laptop fan or a laptop cooler pad (with fans), whichever is cheaper and/or more convenient.
