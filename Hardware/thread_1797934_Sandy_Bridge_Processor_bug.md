### Post by Manyette on 2011-07-05
INFO FOR ALL SANDY BRIDGE PROCESSOR USERS!

I have just purchased a new Acer Laptop, and upon registering the machine I checked the support forum, only to find a notice to purchasers of machines with this processor.  Intel has found an error in some of the chips, which only shows up after some undetermined perios of time. The 5750 laptop which I purchased does not happen to have this error, but obviously, some do.  They furnish a place to enter a serial number where you can enter your serial to see if you have the error.  But they also have a link to the intel site which also describes the problem.  The error has to do with slow degradation of the hard disk controller.  I hope this will help someone out there.
