### Post by bumanie on 2010-03-08
Generally, unless they are a win-modem, most wired modems works out of the box on linux. You would be very unlucky to come across connection issues. It is not the model of modem that is the issue, it's what chipset it has. You could find the chipset of your modem and check that is supported in the linux kernel - it most likely will be - I have never had wired internet issues and wireless has got a whole lot better over the last year.
