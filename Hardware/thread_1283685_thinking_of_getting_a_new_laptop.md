### Post by theZoid on 2009-10-06
what you want for linux optimally is an Nvidia graphics card, and intel wireless card.  I saw the inspiron uses a Intel Integrated Graphics....this works, but I wouldn't stop until I found the above two features.  Intel sound card is recommended also.  Those three pieces of hardware are what you need to concentrate on.  There are databases on the web listed linux compatible hardware....for example ATI graphics 'work', but not nearly well/easy as Nvidia.  You should be able to find something in your price range with those three components.
