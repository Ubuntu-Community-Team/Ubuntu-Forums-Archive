### Post by gianluca.pettinello on 2009-03-03
The problem is that, when you put your laptop onto a surface made of cotton or wool, you prevent the flow of the air which dissipates the heat generated by the laptop. So my suggestion is that you put some sort of spacer between the laptop and the cotton/wool surface as to let the air flow freely (see the openings below your notebook)
