### Post by rsteinmetz70112 on 2021-01-30
I've finally solved this issue. The powered speaker was picking up the radio station. I tried RF chokes on the cables and it stopped the unpowered speaker but the powered speaker kept playing even with chokes. The amplifier must have been picking it up directly. I traded out the speakers again for a different set and it has now stopped.
