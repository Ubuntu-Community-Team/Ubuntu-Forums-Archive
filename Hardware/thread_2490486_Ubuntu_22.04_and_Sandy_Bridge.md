### Post by oldfred on 2023-09-06
With 8GB of RAM and SSD, it should run well.

I use Kubuntu and had to use my old 2006 BIOS only laptop on a trip. It only had 1.5GB of RAM and was functional but slow with Kubuntu. I had a external SSD with 22.04 Kubuntu installed in UEFI boot mode. I added a BIOS boot of external drive on internal drive, and ran Kubuntu from external SSD on old laptop. As long as I only added one larger app and a couple of smaller apps I was ok and lot faster than old 5400 rpm internal drive. When adding more it when to swap and slowed down.
