It may be good to know what you've tried already.
Sometimes after install for some reason sound is muted.
Try installing Pavucontrol & make sure that sound is turned up & unmuted on the playback tab & Output Devices. Also on the last tab you'll see "Configuration", make sure that the right profile is being selected.
