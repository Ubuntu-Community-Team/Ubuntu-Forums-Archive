### Post by coffeecat on 2009-11-12
> **ProbeDrone said:**
> My mouse keeps moving to the sides of the screen at random moments. It does it especially when typing.

> **ProbeDrone said:**
> I also do not use an external mouse but the built in touch pad and track stick.

It's because:

a - you are accidentally brushing the touchpad/stick with part of your hand as you type,

or

b - the touchpad is very sensitive and can pick up hand movements when your hand gets very close without actually touching.

I can't remember the mechanism for the latter - it may be static - but it's  happened to me when I've posted a reply in this forum with this Sony laptop. I find the cursor has jumped and I'm inserting text in the wrong place.

Have a look in System > Preferences > Mouse > Touchpad tab. Try unticking the "Disable touchpad while typing" option.
