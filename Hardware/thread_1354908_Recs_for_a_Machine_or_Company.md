Oooh, that was too easy! :wink:

The Linux Emporium:

[http://www.linuxemporium.co.uk/](http://www.linuxemporium.co.uk/)

LXer. Go to 'show desktop vendors' and look for the UK ones:

[http://lxer.com/module/db/index.php?dbn=14](http://lxer.com/module/db/index.php?dbn=14)

Naked Computers. They specialise in computers with no OS, but list suppliers that pre-install Linux. Interestingly, that includes Ebuyer in the UK, which pleasantly surprised me.

[http://nakedcomputers.org/](http://nakedcomputers.org/)
