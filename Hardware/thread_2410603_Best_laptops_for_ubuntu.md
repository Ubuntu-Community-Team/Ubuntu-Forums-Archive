### Post by mastablasta on 2019-01-18
one option is  - old/used business grade laptop.

second is - any laptop that comes with Ubuntu or linux preloaded (HP has some models SUSE EL, but ubutnu works out of the box on them). in general business versions are often certified for linux (CenOS, SUSE or Ubuntu).

for under 400$ you should be able to get the entry "business laptops". they are usually more rugged, and are often are better built than consumer ones. they often have dock capability of some sort.
