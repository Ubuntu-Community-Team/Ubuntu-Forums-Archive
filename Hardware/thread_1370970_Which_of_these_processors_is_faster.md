### Post by cascade9 on 2010-01-03
Its always more complicated that just cores and clock speed. Eg- 

Core 2 Duo E6700- 2667Mhz, 2 cores. 

Core 2 Duo E8200- 2667Mhz, 2 cores. 

If you just went off clock speed and cores, they are the same. But they arent.... The E6700 has 4MB cache, 1066FSB. The E8200 has 6MB cache, 1333FSB. Its a faster CPU. 

BTW, gimp does use multi processors as long as you have version 2.3.x or higher.
