### Post by Keith_Helms on 2015-04-22
See if there's an ICC color profile for your TV model.  If not, you can create a custom one by using the Argyll software and a colorimeter, such as one of the Spyder models.  The software is in the repository, but the device is a bit pricy.

You didn't mention what you're playing your music on.  I have my PC connected to a receiver and the sound output is fine.  If you don't have one, look into one of those home theater sound systems or maybe a soundbar.  Even a couple of those little speakers designed to attach to laptops through the headphone jack would probably sound much better than the internal speakers.
