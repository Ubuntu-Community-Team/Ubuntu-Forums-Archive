### Post by TenPlus1 on 2009-02-27
I have an external USB2.0 IcyBox with a 350Gb 2.5" hard-drive inside that works perfectly when I connect it to my Compaq Presario m2000 laptop, but for some reason will not work when I connect it to the usb2 port of my desktop computer...

lsusb lists the device on the laptop but shows nothing on the desktop, but... the same port will allow me to use other usb2 devices from flash drives to cameras...  Both systems are running the exact same Ubuntu 8.04 setup

Any ideas ?

---

### Post by TenPlus1 on 2009-02-28
Problem solved, it turns out that the supplied cable with the IcyBox wasn't that great and a shielded usb2.0 to mini-usb cable that I purchased fixed everything and now the drive is recognised on all systems...
