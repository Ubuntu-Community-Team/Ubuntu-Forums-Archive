### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-07-11
The hp pendrives and Android phone will not need much power to spin them (as they are solid state) and allow mounting and use.  Do you need to use a double plugged USB cable, ie one with an second USB plug at the computer end to supply the extra power needed for your WD disk?

I have a USB Intenso disk that works fine on some of my direct installed USB ports, but needs the extra power supply attachment if I use the USB port on my integral card-reader.
