### Post by chee on 2011-09-28
I ran 11.04 on my Inspiron 6400 with the 950gm chipset.  At first I used Unity2d,  worked fine but was  a bit buggy.  Then I switched to regular unity but used compiz configuration and ubuntu-tweak to get rid of most of the lag issues.  Worked decently but after a couple months I decided to go back to 10.10 as it just runs better.

In my books, Unity sucks.  I gave it a whole hearted try and still didn't like it.  But that is regardless of it's issues with the 950gm chipset.  From all my research I have come to the conclusion that Ubuntu drivers for this chipset simply don't address opengl issues.  Which really limits wines capabilities too.  I can't even run SWKOTOR at this point,  which is almost 10 years old and runs perfectly in windows.
