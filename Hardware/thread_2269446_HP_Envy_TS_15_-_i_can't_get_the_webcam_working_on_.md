### Post by gordintoronto on 2015-03-16
If you enter the command: lsusb

it should show several lines of output, one of which is the webcam. Here's mine:
Bus 008 Device 002: ID 0ac8:303b Z-Star Microelectronics Corp. ZC0303 Webcam

The ID is the key. I would Google: 0ac8:303b usb linux
to get the scoop about my webcam.
