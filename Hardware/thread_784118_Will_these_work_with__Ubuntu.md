### Post by webby35 on 2008-05-06
For the Canon LIDE 70, look at the SANE website for supported devices:
[http://www.sane-project.org/sane-mfgs.html](http://www.sane-project.org/sane-mfgs.html)

For the printer, there doesn't seem to be CUPS support for it. However, there is a shareware called Turboprint ([http://www.turboprint.de](http://www.turboprint.de)) which works well with a lot of printers. It does support the PIXMA iP8500. (FYI, I have no link with Turboprint, I just use it for my Canon MP750 at home).
