### Post by coffeecat on 2018-07-31
@q123q, I have a HP Deskjet 2130. 

> **q123q said:**
> I have one, and it works wireless under Win XP.

That would be quite an achievement. It is **not** a wireless printer. 

> **q123q said:**
> Can I install the printer without the USB cable?

Since the only way of connecting the Deskjet 2130 printer to your computer is with a USB cable, the answer is, no.

> **q123q said:**
> HP Deskjet 2130 come without USB cable.

Are you sure? But even if you have lost the cable that came with the printer, the cable used is a standard USB type B to USB type A one, easily purchased from shops that sell such stuff, or you could even use an old cable that came with an older printer. You probably (or a friend) have one lying around.  

Once you do find a suitable USB cable, I can tell you that the printer works out of the box with Ubuntu 16.04, or one of the flavours - no need to install anything extra.
