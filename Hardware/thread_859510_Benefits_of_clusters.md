### Post by tamoneya on 2008-07-14
I dont really know of any benefits to cluster in a home environment.  It is really nice for CPU intensive tasks like rendering videos or CAD drawings.  unless you do things on your computer that involve large CPU loads you probably wont benefit from it. If however you do want to give it a spin take a look at openmosix.  I havent used it myself but ive heard good reviews.  I would love to use it if I get a chance but at the moment I dont have any need for it.
