### Post by fireowl on 2010-07-04

Just follow the directions at: 
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/KernelTeam/MainlineBuilds?action=show&redirect=KernelMainlineBuilds](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/KernelTeam/MainlineBuilds?action=show&redirect=KernelMainlineBuilds)

Basically download the 4 deb files for your cpu (32-bit i386 or 64-bit amd64) and
do a "sudo dpkg -i *.deb"
It will automatically update your grub listing. Reboot and pick new kernel. Easy!
