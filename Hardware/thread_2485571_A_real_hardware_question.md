### Post by #&amp;thj^% on 2023-04-02
Not proven on my end personally and all other things being equal:
```

P=V^2/R

dP = 1.35^2/R1 - 1.2^2/R2
```

Assuming R1 = R2, 1.2V DIMMs use about 20.9% less power.
That was simple right? lol

32GB with 16Gbit modules will consume less power than 32GB with 8Gbit modules.

32GB with 16Gbit modules should consume the same amount of power as 16GB with 8Gbit modules

what your asking really won't amount to much difference either way.
