### Post by silkstone on 2008-08-08
I don't think that the nvidia drivers in the Ubuntu repos support this card, and also Envy doesn't seem to work with it.

You have to download the Linux drivers from the Nvidia website and install them yourself, having first uninstalled all existing Nvidia drivers. It sounds a little complicated but it's probably OK if you take it a step at a time.

The thread below relates mainly to Gutsy, but the procedure is probably the same. Look at Post #15 onwards.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=717978&page=2](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=717978&page=2)

There may be a guide specific to Hardy, but I can't see one.
