### Post by bobcollard on 2010-07-13
Left click the Network Icon in the taskbar to see if your network is found.  If it is, click on the network and you may be asked to enter the pass phrase for the security.  If it is not found you may need to rightclick the icon and open Edit Connections.  Click the tab for Wireless connections and Add the information manually.
