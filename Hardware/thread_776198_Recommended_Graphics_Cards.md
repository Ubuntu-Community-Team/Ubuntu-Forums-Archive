### Post by Wildy75 on 2008-05-01
Hm... Did you try to find some Matrox cards? They'd been used as a graphics designers' cards of choice some years ago, they offer superior picture quality in 2D. But beware of PCI standards mismatch, there could be an issue with 5V and 3V (newer) PCI bus slots on the MB. You won't be able to plug in the wrong voltage card, though.

For further reference, see [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peripheral_Component_Interconnect#Conventional](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peripheral_Component_Interconnect#Conventional)
