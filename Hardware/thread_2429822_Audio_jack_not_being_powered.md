### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2019-10-25
A (three contact) headphone jack only carries ground, left signal and right signal (on a four contact jack for headsets there's one more contact for a microphone) . No voltage for an amplifier. And normally none is needed since headphones are basically just small speakers, the signal should be strong enough to produce sound. 

What exactly do you mean by 'messed up' ? Weak ? Distorted ? Broken up ?

Holger
