### Post by linuxonbute on 2013-08-06
Not sure I can help.
I have just had a Hp G56 laptop fail. 
I know it was the processor because I downloaded a file from HP's site
which tells me what to look for.
When there is something wrong the caps-lock LED flashes.

If it flashes once then pauses for about 3 seconds it's the processor failure.

 2 quick flashes and a 3 second pause a BIOS failure and so on.

I do not know what your system is so I cannot help.
I suggest you look for some service manual and see if it can help.
