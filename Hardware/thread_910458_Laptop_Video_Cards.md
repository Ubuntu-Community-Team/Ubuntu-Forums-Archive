### Post by IntuitiveNipple on 2008-09-04
There is no video *card* in laptops, unless in a docking station. Because of the small size of the laptop all main components are directly mounted on the circuit board, with things such as wireless network and Bluetooth on mini-PCI or mini-USB cards (less than 4 centimetres on a side).

The only option, if the part is physically broken rather than mis-configured, is to replace the main circuit board.
