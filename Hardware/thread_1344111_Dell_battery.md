The Dell Inspiron 6400 laptop which I am typing this on now has previously run 8.04 & 9.04 (it came with Windows XP however Ubuntu 8.04 was installed on first boot!). Since installing 9.10 however the battery will not fully charge.  It charges to 60% (as viewed in the gnome-power-manager panel applet) only with a resultant decrease in the length of time that I can use the laptop when not connected to AC power.

This could be the result of the battery beginning to wear out except that the laptop is only a little over 2 years old, has not had a great deal of use and it would be an odd coincidence if the battery began to fail on the exact day that 9.10 was installed.
