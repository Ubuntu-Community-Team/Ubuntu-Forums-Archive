### Post by jgw on 2015-03-09
I have been setting up a computer that I got for free as the owner said it was just not working and nobody could fix it.  He was right, it was not working but, kinda.  It just didn't behave and the problems were not consistent.  This is the second time I have come across this one.  Once I swapped out a motherboard as I was convinced it was bad.  

The solution turned out to be incredibly simple - the battery on the motherboard was bad.  Just saying - if you are ready to toss a machine, or motherboard, check to make sure the battery is good.  

I just thought I would throw this one up in case somebody else has one of these kinds of problems
