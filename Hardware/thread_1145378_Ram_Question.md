### Post by Eviljim on 2009-05-01
It could still be the PSU, especially if it on its way out and not providing a stable voltage on its rails. If you can check this with another PSU then do so.

Failing that, remove one of the two memory sticks and try again. This will rule out a RAM issue.

Make sure the PC is also free from dust inside, as overheating could be an issue.

Finally ensure all the internal cabling is fully seated in their sockets, including all those little case cables for reboot switch/power switch etc...
