### Post by bankg3 on 2008-08-27
if you are on 8.04, plug in the external monitor and then navigate to system > Preferences > Screen Resolution.  This is a handy little application that make it easy to use an external monitor.  Both of your monitors will be on top of each other by default, so just grab one w/ your mouse and move it to the side and you should see both, then just configure how you want them and you should be good to go
