### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2009-12-26
If that is the DIR-655 Xtreme N Gigabit Router then it should work if the other computers have wireless-n adapters. If they only have wireless-g then you could have problems. It could be almost as expensive to get a wireless bridge/range extender as it would be to upgrade their adapters to wireless-n.

When I had only wireless-g, I had to have a router or range extender/bridge in each floor. With wireless-n I don't.

Edit: One note though, the wireless-n signal strength looks weaker; however, with the N technology it actually takes advantage of multi-path signals.
