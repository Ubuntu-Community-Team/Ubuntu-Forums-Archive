### Post by lanerobe on 2011-11-14
[SIZE=1]I had the same problem with my HP Photosmart B110. Copy'n'pasting the  URI from my (older) ubuntu netbook did the trick - it seems Kubuntu  wasn't finding it correctly.

Correct URI: [/SIZE][SIZE=1]hp:/net/Photosmart_B110_series?zc=HP8A2A9C[/SIZE][SIZE=1] [/SIZE]
