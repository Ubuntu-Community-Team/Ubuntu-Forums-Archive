### Post by sudodus on 2013-07-07
One thing is the pixel size (which decides whether you need glasses or not, when using the full resolution). Another thing is the number of pixels (width x height), how much of a particular page, that can be viewed without scrolling. The difference between 12" and 14" is not that big, but you really notice the difference when going down to the netbooks with 10" (or less). 10" wide-screen feels much smaller than 12" 1024x768. I think a 12" laptop or notebook is quite nice, if you plan to carry it a lot. Weight and total size including power adapter is quite important.
