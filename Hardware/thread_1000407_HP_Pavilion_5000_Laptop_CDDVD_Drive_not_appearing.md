### Post by galenanderson on 2009-04-09
After spending probably an hour trying to fix it with updated drivers from HP based on my computer etc, i had to take the drive out to find the exact model number. I quickly discovered that the the bracket the holds the drive inside was bent and there was no electrical connection between the computer and the drive. I bent the bracket back, and then also used a hammer to reshape the drive and reassembled everything. Now it all works!
