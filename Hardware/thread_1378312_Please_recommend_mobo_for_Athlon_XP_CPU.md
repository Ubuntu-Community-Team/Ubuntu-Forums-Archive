### Post by cascade9 on 2010-01-11
You've got 2 choices- nForce 1 chipsets, or nForce2 chipsets. Either will work.

The nForce 2 chipset is better. I'd recommend an Abit NF7-M, but being old stock, at best, you wont have much choice. Better yet would be a NF7-S with an AGP nVidia card. 

BTW- why intergrated video? AGP video cards are cheap, wont eat some of your main memory and are faster. (except in the instance that you install an older video card, like a TNT2 and compare that the the intergrated GeForce2 (nForce 1) or GeFroce 4MX (nForce 2)
