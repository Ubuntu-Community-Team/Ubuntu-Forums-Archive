### Post by eddier on 2012-03-05
you can do a fresh install without losing your personal files-if you have a seperate /home Partition. 

Use the live cd/dvd to access your home folder,select "view hidden files".

Delete all the dot folders except maybe '.mozilla' BUT dont delete your Document/Music/Video etc folders.

When you re-install DO NOT FORMAT  your /home partition.THis will leave your personal stuff in situ.

If (may the Gods forgive) you do not have a seperate /home partition,Zip up what you need and dump it to a dvd or 2! Then re-install and make sure you have 3 partitions-

8 to 10GB for / (root)
2GB(about) for swap
the rest for /home
