Now...
UT-300R2U has both Ethernet and USB. Always prefer Ethernet. You said "Ethernet cable not working in win vista" but you didn't explain how you came to that conclusion and whether or not you've done some troubleshooting... It can be either an hardware problem (the cable or the port itself) or just lack of (Vista) drivers. Anyway... What really matters is whether or not it works in Ubuntu and, provided it isn't defective, it should work out of the box because all Realtek NICs from that time have native support in the current Linux kernel.
This is what you should be testing and proceed accordingly instead of burdening yourself with layers upon layers of most likely unneeded complexity.
