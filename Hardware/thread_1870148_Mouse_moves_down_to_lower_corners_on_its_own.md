Your mouse and keyboard issues could be related to the hub. Is it self powered? It could be an issue of power to those devices or a more deeper bios issue. My mouse issues have more or less disappeared when I replaced my CMOS battery. My old one was weak and thus BIOS settings were being lost almost at every bootup. A good number of booting and other issues as well were resolved.
