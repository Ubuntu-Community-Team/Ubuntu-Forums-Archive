Hi,

So, I'm running Ubuntu 14.04 on a MacBook Pro Retina 2013 model. Earlier today, my computer froze when I opened it after having suspended it. I had to hold down the power button and force it to shut down. Now, when I've started it again, it does not recognize any devices that I connect to the USB port on its left-hand side. I've tried with a mouse, keyboard and my mobile. However, it does charge my phone when it is connected, but does not react to it. The USB port on the right-hand side works fine, and when I connect my phone to it I get a dialog confirming that it's been connected.

What causes this, and what can I do to fix it?
