### Post by Cheesemill on 2012-07-11
> **sharathpaps said:**
> Is it important that I buy the same brand of DDR3 RAM that my laptop came with?
No.
> I remember reading somewhere that the RAM modules have to be synchronized. Is that true?
No.
If they aren't both rated at the same speed then they will just both run at the speed of the slowest module.

If you are unsure what to buy then either check the laptop manual or enter the model number into the Crucial website.
[http://www.crucial.com/store/advisor.aspx](http://www.crucial.com/store/advisor.aspx)
