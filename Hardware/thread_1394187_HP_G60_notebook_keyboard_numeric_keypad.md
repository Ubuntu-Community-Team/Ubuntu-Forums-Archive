### Post by techiebiker on 2010-01-30
The numeric keypad stopped functioning after login.

It worked while logging in but once on the desktop didn't work any longer.

The information in Eben Fourie's post solved the problem
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1358263&highlight=keyboard+keypad](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1358263&highlight=keyboard+keypad)

<quote>
...switch off "Pointer can be controlled using the keypad" in System  Preferences > Keyboard (Mouse Keys tab) + switch ON numlock.
</quote>

No idea when/how I changed the setting but after going back and disabling it the numeric keypad works again! Thanks Eben!
