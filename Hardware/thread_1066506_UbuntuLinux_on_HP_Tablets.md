### Post by Favux on 2009-02-11
Hi beastrace91  	

Depends on the make and model.  Is it a built in Wacom digitizer, serial or usb?

For instance with TX2000's and TX2500's you can get pretty much everything working including touch.  Both in Hardy and Intrepid.  Actually the TX2500 seems to work a little better with Intrepid's kernel.

But the TX2z has a N-trig digitizer with multi-touch.  This isn't quite fully supported yet, especially multi-touch.
