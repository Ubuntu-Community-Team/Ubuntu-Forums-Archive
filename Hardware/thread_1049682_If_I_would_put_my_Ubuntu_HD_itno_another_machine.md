### Post by nixscripter on 2009-01-24
In short, it depends on what the machines are. Hardware drivers are smart, but could be incompatible, and a lot of configuration stuff (e.g. networking) is machine-specific. It will probably boot if the machines have similar processors (machine architectures are compatible), but a lot of stuff won't work.

What are the two machines specifically?
