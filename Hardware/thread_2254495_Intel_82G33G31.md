### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-11-27
Welcome to Ubuntu and all that...

The "Additional Drivers" tool won't show any drivers for a video card unless that card is activated and in use. If your machine has either an Nvidia or an AMD card, in addition to the Intel graphics, you can probably switch to it in BIOS settings.

Note: AMD no longer provides drivers for older cards, so if you have one of those, the only option listed in "Additional Drivers" may be the open source driver.
