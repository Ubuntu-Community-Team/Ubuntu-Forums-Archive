### Post by texadactyl on 2013-09-06
I have an Intel D2550MUD2 motherboard (NM10 chipset and GMA3650) - a close cousin of your motherboard. In Xubuntu saucy (13.10), kernel module gma500_gfx and the modesetting X driver were autoselected for my installation. This works fairly well for a programmer (me) although it might be sub-optimal for an Ubuntu Studio user. At least, I can use the full display resolution capability (1920x1080x60) of my 22" monitor.

All of us Cedar View users have been orphaned by Intel.  Their software ([http://01.org]("http://01.org")) cannot detect GMA3650 on Linux (maybe on Windows?).

Good luck.
