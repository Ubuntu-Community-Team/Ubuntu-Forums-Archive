**I CONFIRM** that by installing ubuntu 12.04 ( kernel 3.2.0-31 ), and installing the "out-of-the-box" "nvidia-current", solves the problem I reported earlier on ubuntu 11.04.

Moreover, My FN buttons work out-of-the-box with ubuntu 12.04, in comparison to 11.04 that didn't.
