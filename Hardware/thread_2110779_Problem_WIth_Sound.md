### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-01-31
Unless you have one of the older "surround sound" headphones, you don't get 5.1 sound in them; you only get 2-channel sound.

And, if your audio source is 2-channel (as in MP3s), your 5.1 sound is being synthetically generated -- which takes time, and introduces a delay in the process.

So, it's understandable that the two sound outputs would be out of synch.
