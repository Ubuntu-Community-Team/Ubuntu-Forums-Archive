### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-06-28
What you have is known as Switchable Graphics -- which does not work well in Linux.  You should read through the linked materials to get an idea of the problems you will be facing:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1930450&highlight=switchable+graphics]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1930450&highlight=switchable+graphics")

In stark contrast, this tends to work very well in Windows -- but ONLY because the PCs that come preconfigured this way also have the special video drivers preloaded that make this work.  IF you start messing around with the video drivers in Windows, you'll likely end up in the same difficult situation as you would be in Linux.
