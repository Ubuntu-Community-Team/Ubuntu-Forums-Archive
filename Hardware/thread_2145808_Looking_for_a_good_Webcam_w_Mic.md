### Post by papibe on 2013-05-16
Hi markw10.

Logitech webcams are generally well supported on Linux. I have a Pro 9000 (with mic) and it works out the box.

This webcam uses the UVC driver, and I would recommend choosing one that supports it. Here's a [list]("http://www.ideasonboard.org/uvc/") of webcams, and the level of support under the driver.

Just a thought.
Let us know how it goes.
