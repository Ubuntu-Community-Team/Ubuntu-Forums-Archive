### Post by 5Euros on 2010-02-18
Hi, I have successfully (more or less) installed 9.04 UNR on the U100. Wireless card and Webcam works very well on this build. However, Sound did not work.

I upgraded (not clean install) to 9.10 and...Sound works very well, WebCam doesn't work (I subsequently learned that if I'd switched the WebCam off before the upgrade it would still be working). The wireless card is working but quite often it will lose connectivity and a reboot is the only solution that I have found to work. This last bug is very annoying.

One other point. Before you upgrade to 9.10 you will need to do a flash update of the machine BIOS. This is much easier than it sounds - check other threads and the MSI website for details. If you don't do this update then the brightness control on the MSI will do a merry dance from bootup. ;)
