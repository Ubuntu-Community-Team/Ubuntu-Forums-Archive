### Post by dagoth_pie on 2009-03-25
if your using the psx emulator. make sure you have at least 4gb of ram, 6 would be more than i would imagine youd need, that way you have enough space for it to cache the entire dvd image to the ram, the same way as a ps2 would...
as for the rest, I know that asus makes some boards designed to have passive cooling, that way you wont have fans humming, it may be a good idea to replace the fans to get quieter ones, although a media center case should, in theory, have quiet fans,

Thats about all the advice I have, have fun
