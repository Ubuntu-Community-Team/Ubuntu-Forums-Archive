### Post by wyliecoyoteuk on 2012-09-04
There should be a mic control option in pavucontrol's mixer (if not, check the menus, there may be a "select control" option which will allow you to see it). it is probably just muted.
The headphone unit will not show up in device manager, as it is a passive device connected via your sound caard.
