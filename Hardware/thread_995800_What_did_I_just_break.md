### Post by bear54 on 2008-11-28
I think that little tab you are talking about is there just to hold the video card in the slot. Your video card should stay in the slot with out the tab but it might work loose over time. I'm not sure what it would do to the motherboard or video card if it were to work loose when the machine was running most likely you would just lose the video but it might fry the motherboard or video card or both. You should clean the inside of your computer every six months or so when you do it's a good idea to check all the cards inside your PC to make sure they are tight in their slots. If the video card fits tight in the slot it probably will stay in the slot.
