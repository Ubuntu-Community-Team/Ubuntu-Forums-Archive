### Post by Autodave on 2021-10-05
OK.  But that does not tell us whether or not you have lost the 5V signal from the power supply.  My guess is that either you have a failed or failing power supply, or something else on the PC (hard drive, USB hub, printer) is pulling the 5v too low.  IF you are using any USB hubs: powered or nonpowered, disconnect them.  Disconnect EVERY USB peripheral including the receiver for the mouse and try a wired mouse.  You should also try another keyboard.  See what happens and report back!
