1st, Hi. I'm Dan, using Ubuntu for 2 months now on my Acer Aspire 5315 notebook, coming from XP.

2nd, the following are the observations made so far on 9.10:

[LIST]
[*]shorter battery life (about 20 minutes less than before when fully charged), but still 35 minutes more than xp and vista used to be (can't actually remember what it used to be on vista, but it was shorter than xp)
[*]unstable system (sudden shutdown, twice today, plugged in battery full or recharging), both time with firefox and openoffice open, as it is always on my laptop. First time this ever happened since I switched to Ubuntu.
[*]looks worst, especially the top right bar, it's less visible.
[*]some weird resolution issues, from time to time image gets blurry for a few secs.
[/LIST]

I'm sorry, I can't see improvements yet, but I do admit I am still very new at Linux, and they might not be visible to me.
