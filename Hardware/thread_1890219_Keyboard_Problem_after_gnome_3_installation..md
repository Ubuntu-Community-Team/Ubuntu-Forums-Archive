### Post by dandnsmith on 2011-12-04
Sounds as if you may have enabled a facility to assist disabled users (suppresses extra keystrokes as if erroneous).
You probably need the compiz control panel (look under preferences) to sort it out. Please excuse my lack of precision, I'm trying to get away from some of the features of 11.10, and managed to disable my system recently.

---

### Post by channey_mithu on 2011-12-04
Thank you so much for your help. you are right. It is under Universal access. Just search for universal access and under typing there is option of bounce keys. This option was somehow get activated. I just turn it off and everything works perfectly.
You save me from installing whole ubuntu again.
Thanks..!!:biggrin:
