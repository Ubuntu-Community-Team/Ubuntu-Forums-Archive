### Post by underquark on 2010-01-27
Webcam arrived today (as did audio and usb extension cables, all from different vendors).

Stand will sit on a flat surface or fold out to clip to a monitor.
Adjustable for most angles.
Built-in mic (somewhere - can't actually see it)
Separate USB and mic connectors.
Cable length is about 150cm - too long for a laptop and too short for PC sitting under the table (hence the extensions I ordered too).  Would be OK for a desktop so long as it's on the desk.

Picture using cheese seems quite good in low light conditions.
Twiddling the in-line rotary switch turns on six blinding little LED's - fine for illuminating objects about 3m away but way too bright to look at.
Haven't sorted out my sound settings correctly but the mic does work as I can hear sound coming out of the speakers and I can generate feedback if I push the "Front Mic" slider all the way up.  Can't get cheese or xawtv to record sound yet and there is an annoying high-pitched clicky noise when cheese is running but all probably sortable.
