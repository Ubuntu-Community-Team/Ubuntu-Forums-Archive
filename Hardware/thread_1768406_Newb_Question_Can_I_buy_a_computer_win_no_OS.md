### Post by snowpine on 2011-05-26
Believe it or not, it's usually cheaper to buy a computer with Windows pre-installed, through simple economy of scale: Windows is over 90% of the desktop market, so that's where the good deals and best sales can be found.

### Post by Allavona on 2011-05-26
This is going to sound goofy but some countries have laws that say products sold have to work. A new computer with no OS doesn't "work" and therefore violates the law!

There are retailers that sell new computers with Linux installed, as mentioned by roberts3000. Good, because that means all drivers are there and things will work as advertised!
