### Post by JazzPotato on 2012-01-08
1) play around with the files in the directory /etc/acpi but MAKE BACKUPS FIRST. Edit the shell scripts and see what happens (thats how I fixed mine).
2) Type in "additional drivers" in the unity launcher (top left corner) to get your additional drivers window up and ubuntu will suggest your options to you.
3) Terminals can be opened with ctrl+alt+t or going to the launcher (super key, or the icon in top left corner) and typing terminal.
