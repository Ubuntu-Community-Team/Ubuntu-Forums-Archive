### Post by him610 on 2016-02-29
This is just a guess...
> The adapter works perfectly the second I plug it in.

From your description, it appears as if the conversion process inside of the adapter can not convert the signals without overheating. Anytime one converts a signal, ie hdmi to vga, there are increased chances of problems. Have you tried the same exact setup on anything else - like a monitor or a TV? If not, then try it. It is entirely possible, the hdmi_to_vga adapter is defective. The schools do not have the resources to keep up with the rapidly changing technology. I looked up the specs of your laptop; it seems like a good machine.
