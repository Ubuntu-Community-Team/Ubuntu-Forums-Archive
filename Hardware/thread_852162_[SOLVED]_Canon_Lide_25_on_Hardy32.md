### Post by be4truth on 2008-07-07
I have two Hardy 32 bit installations: One upgraded from Gutsy and one a fresh install. Canon Lide 25 scanner worked fine under Gutsy but scans only black pages under Hardy. But not on the upgraded machine.

I copied from the home directory the .sane folder from the Gutsy-upgraded-Hardy to the Hardy fresh install and the scanner started to work on Hardy-fresh-install as well. Rename your .sane directory first to .sane.orig  - just in case:(
Find the folder attached. The archive starts with a dot and will be hidden.

Hope that helps
