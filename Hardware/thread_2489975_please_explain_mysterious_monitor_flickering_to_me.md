### Post by goodstuff9 on 2023-08-16
Two computers, A and B, both running Ubuntu Jammy.  

Monitor 1 and 2 are connected to computer A.  

Via a physical switch, Monitor 1 can also be switched to be connected to computer B.  

I start out with both monitors 1 and 2 connected to computer A.  All is fine.  

When I throw the switch to switch Monitor 1's connection from Computer A to Computer B, Monitor 1 works fine on Computer B.  But, then Monitor 2, which is still connected to Computer A, starts flickering severely.  Why does that happen?  How do I prevent that from happening?

### Post by him610 on 2023-08-17
I don't know if this will do anything one way or another...

Prior to switching Monitor 1 from A to B, turn off Monitor 2.
After you have switched Monitor 1 to B, turn on Monitor 2.
Observe Monitor 2. Is flicker present on Monitor 2?

You could also turn off both monitors before switching from A to B.

You could also install your A-B switch on the Monitor 2 cable.

I don't know if any of the above suggestions will work, but they are worth a try.

As to why it happens, IMOH I believe it is related to common circuits on both sides of the A/B switch to Computer 1.
