### Post by dandnsmith on 2012-03-07
The BIOS is often in a chip on the motherboard of the PC.
Possibilities for updating or restoring depend on what the manufacturer has built in - if there is a possibility, it will often be mentioned in the manual (if not google for the make and model).

If you get anything on the screen when you try to boot, there may be hope - if you see no messages, then it is probably dead.
