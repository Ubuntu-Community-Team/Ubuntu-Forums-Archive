### Post by Scott.57215 on 2010-04-09
If anyone is trying to find out if their HP printer or a HP printer they plan to buy is supported, you can [visit the hplip website]("http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html") and check the Supported Printers list. Keep in mind that if it says the GUI is not an included feature, you can just download hplip-toolbox and it should include one.
