### Post by IcarusR on 2010-07-13
To grow your ubuntu partition download 'gparted live cd' from here...

[http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php]("http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php")

Boot the machine from cd & use gparted to shrink one partition to create free space, then grow your ubuntu partition with that free space.

There are links to howtos on gparted site, suggest you readup first.

Be carefull, it is possible to mess things up, usual caveats don't blame me for any mess ups !!


As for your other questions fraid I can not help.
