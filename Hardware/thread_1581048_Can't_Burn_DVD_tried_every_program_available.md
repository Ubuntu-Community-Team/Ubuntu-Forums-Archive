### Post by IcarusR on 2010-09-24
The laser lense could be dirty. Try cleaning your drive out with an air duster aerosol. Or take it to pieces & clean carefully with cotton bud.

Try different media.

Try burning at a lower speed. I always burn at very low speeds.

Update your drive firmware. You may find info & firmware on this forum [http://forum.rpc1.org/]("http://forum.rpc1.org/")
