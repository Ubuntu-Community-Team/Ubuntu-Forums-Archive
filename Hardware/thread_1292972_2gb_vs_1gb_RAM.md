### Post by nortexoid on 2009-10-16
I always wondered whether I would see a benefit from going from 1gb to 2gb. Most people think upgrading would be a no brainer but my ultraportable only has 1 SODIMM slot, and it was already occupied by a 1GB stick. Anyway, I just swapped it today for a 2GB stick and the difference is quite noticeable. E.g. whenenever I had a moderate amount of programs open, calling up an "expose" (compiz) would stutter. It's always smooth now, no matter how many programs I have open. There's lots of other noticeable improvements.

So anyone wondering whether to go with more than 1gb RAM (a common configuration on netbooks), I highly recommend it.
