### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-05-10
Are you complaining about a proprietary driver? Ubuntu developers can do nothing about those except pass your bug reports upstream to the owner of the driver. This is why some of us delay upgrading to the latest Ubuntu version to allow time for driver developers to catch up. They work at their own pace.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/bcm43xx](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/Driver/bcm43xx)

The answer, of course, is to buy a computer operating system pre-installed. In this way the machine should work as advertised and paid for. Linux is developed by volunteers and we get the distribution without charge. I always keep that in mind. It helps keep my dissatisfaction in perspective.
