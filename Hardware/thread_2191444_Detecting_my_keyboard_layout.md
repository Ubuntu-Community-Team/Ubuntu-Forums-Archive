### Post by roiikkata on 2013-12-02
you mean for the language? or for the keyboard hardware.

for the language it should be in the ubuntu settings menu under "keyboard layout" not "keyboard"

if you are trying to change the hardware selection of the keyboard, it is possible. it pops up with the hardware selection for the keyboard and you select the keyboard manufacturer and such. if thats what you are looking for i suggest you search for it and be careful when using it as you could make your keyboard un-useable temporarily.

otherwise its just in the system settings under "keyboard layout", like you asked.
