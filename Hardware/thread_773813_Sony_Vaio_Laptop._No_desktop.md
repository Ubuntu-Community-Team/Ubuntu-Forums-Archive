### Post by randomAccess on 2008-04-29
Well if you
1. created /home partition on the first place
2. saved files on usual location (desktop, home, etc)

then you can just reinstall the system by specifying which partition is /home and NOT SELECTING it be formated. 

Or you could just login with live cd and browse to your /home partition as root :)
