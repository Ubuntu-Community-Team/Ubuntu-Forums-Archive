### Post by AgentZ86 on 2013-04-21
old thread revival

First go to system settings / Details 

Try to make it do nothing when you plug it in

Don't let it start Rythmbox or Banchee cause it makes them crash or freeze

--Go to Computer and see if you see your device shown if you can click into the file structure then it's mounted if not then try to right click and mount it.
-- Then you can start gMTP and wait a few seconds for it to do whatever it does don't just click Connect give it like 15 secs or so.
-- Then click connect and give it another 15-20 secs to connect.
-- then you should be able to add your stuff
-- You have to disconnect also once your done
-- then go back to computer and make sure it's unmounted


I have no idea why but I sort of have this zen player down to a science and was able to get it working that way.
