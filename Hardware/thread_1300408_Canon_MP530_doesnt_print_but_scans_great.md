### Post by jeffimus on 2009-11-08
Same problem here.  Ubuntu 9.10, Canon MP530, using MP520 driver (there is no MP530 driver): blacks are printed correctly but colours are all over the place, not just displaced but wrongly scaled as well.  The printer worked perfectly under Ubuntu 9.04.

Latest: I fixed it by switching from the MP520 driver to the MP500 driver, as recommended at [http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Canon-Pixma_MP530](http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Canon-Pixma_MP530). Works perfectly now.
