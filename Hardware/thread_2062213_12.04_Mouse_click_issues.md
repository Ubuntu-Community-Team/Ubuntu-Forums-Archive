### Post by Revenant86 on 2012-09-24
I installed 12.04 yesterday, and since then I have had random occasions where mouse clicks are not recognized. I can move the mouse and use the mouse wheel, and the system responds to keyboard commands, but not mouse clicks. Sometimes just one button, sometimes both. Unplugging the mouse and plugging it into a different usb port does not help. I am using a Toshiba Satellite A215 laptop, and have disabled the touchpad on it.
EDIT: Just wanted to let anyone who reads this know I fixed it. I had disabled the touchpad, but had not done anything to the touchpad driver kernel module. Unloading that module fixed the problem.
