You should be able to use the keyboard and mouse no problems (provided they are PS/2 or USB). The DVD-RW should be fine as well. The case, maybe, maybe not. Depends on what case it is. 

Yo'ull be lookign at about $65 for 1TB WD 'Green Power' drives. Dont get one, they are all 'advanced format' drives now, and its a PITA to set them up. A 1TB Caviar Blue is about $70, and the really fast Caviar Black drives about $90. 

4GB DDR3 1333 is about $45. I dont think you'll need it- hi, my name is 'cascade' and I have a tab problem. 30+ tabs open on firefox, deadbeef running, 675MB used. That said, its not really worth dropping to 2GB, thats going to be about $30-35. 

You'll also need a power supply (about $45 for something decent),  motherboard ($40 for junk, up $60+ for anything decent) CPU (about $50 for a the cheapest AMD dualcore, Athlon II X2 240) and a possibly a video card as well, if you dont want to use onboard ATI or nVidia video. 

With a 1TB Caviar blue, 4GB DDR3 1333, decent power supply alone your looking at about $160. Add a  X2 240 and thats $210. So it might just be do-able to get a decent system for under $300.
