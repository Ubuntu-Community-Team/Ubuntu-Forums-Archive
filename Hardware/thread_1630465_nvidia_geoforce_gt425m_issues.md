### Post by Fafler on 2010-11-26
The Nvidia drivers in Ubuntu 10.10 are version 185 and keeps being so. If the GT425m isn't recognised by the driver, and it's a simple matter of mathing PCI id's, it won't work. The latest from nvidia is 260. This doesn't mean is really old, Nvidia just like really high version numbers. Anyway, you can use these instructions to get the latest driver: [http://www.ubuntugeek.com/how-to-install-nvidia-260-19-12-drivers-in-ubuntu-10-1010-04-using-ppa.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/how-to-install-nvidia-260-19-12-drivers-in-ubuntu-10-1010-04-using-ppa.html)
