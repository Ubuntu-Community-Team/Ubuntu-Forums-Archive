### Post by Ubunthree on 2010-01-08
I also use a U100, and have the same problem. This seems to be a common issue on this machine. I don't recall exactly what the cause of it is; it has something to do with improper reading of a keyboard buffer or something. Anyway, an easy workaround which works FOR ME is: After you get logged in, right-click somewhere on the screen to bring up a context menu - it doesn't seem to matter where - and hold the button until the flickering settles down. This only takes a few seconds for me. You can move the cursor off of the menu before releasing it, and you won't end up with a new folder that you have to delete. Hopefully this will get fixed sometime soon, but for now this gets around it without too much trouble.
