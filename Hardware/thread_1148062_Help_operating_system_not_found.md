### Post by mikedep333 on 2009-05-04
It sounds like your hard drive died.

The PXE stuff is info about a network boot. It would only do that if your hard drive died.

Go into the bios configuration for your thinkpad laptop and look if it sees the hard drive. You need to press F2 (or del. or something like that) at the bios menu to do this.
