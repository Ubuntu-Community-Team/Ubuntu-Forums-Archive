### Post by theirishfozz on 2008-10-16
Different bluetooth dongles support different functions. My old dongle didnt support much other than file push and pull but i just got a new one from newegg for $12 that does just about everything. If bluetooth is active in your phone and your dongle cant detect it to pair under any profile services running then check your dongle supports what you want it to do. It would be strange for a dongle not to support file push/pull...
