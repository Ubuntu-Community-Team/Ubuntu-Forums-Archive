### Post by doas777 on 2009-09-27
go with nvidia if you want to do anything advanced like tv out or dual monitors.

ati has come a long way in the last 2 years, but they still have a long way to go. you can get most advanced features to work, using the driver from their site(not the one in the repos) but there is far more wrestling with it. took me 18 hours to get my HD3850 going with dualmonitors, big-desktop, etc. my nvidia rig took me about 30 minutes, with repo drivers.
