### Post by lethalfang on 2014-06-12
Suddenly this morning, my computer doesn't turn on. 
When I unplug and plug in the cable, and hit Power On, the CPU fan may spin for a fraction of a second, and the CD-Drive's indicator light may flicker for 1/10 of a second, and then nothing happens and power is off. 
So from the fans and light flickering, I think the wires are circulating electrons, but something is preventing the computer from powering on. 

Any idea what could be wrong?

### Post by lethalfang on 2014-06-17
Turned out to be a power supply problem. 
I disconnected the power supply from the motherboard, and tried to short the green wire and a black wire on the 12-pin connector, and the power again went on for a fraction of a second and stopped. 
I switched the power supply and everything is working.
