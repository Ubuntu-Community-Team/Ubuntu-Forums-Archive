### Post by QIII on 2013-09-01
Hello and welcome back!

Please try opening the Catalyst Control Center, going to "Display Manager" and selecting your monitor.

In the "Adjustments" tab, make sure you have "Use display for scaling" checked and under "Graphics processor scaling" select "Scale image to full panel size".

Please let us know if that helps.

Cheers.

### Post by emix101 on 2013-09-01
Well, thank you for the help...

The advice helped me...but the problem is not image option. everything checked as per your advice.

It was in the scaling option below the image option. I just slide the bar from underscan to overscan and whoalaaa!!! the miracles happen.....:KS
