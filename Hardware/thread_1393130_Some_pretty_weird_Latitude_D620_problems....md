### Post by wandalalakers on 2010-01-29
I have linux mint kde 8.04 on my D620.
My D620 has the core 2 duo and a broadcom wired and wireless.
I've seen D620s with the core 2 duo and centrino.
I recommend trying 8.04 on your D620.

How to burn an ISO image
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)

After creating your image, you will need to use F12 to boot from the livecd when starting up the laptop or change the bios to boot from cd by default.  Use CD-Rs or DVD-Rs when creating your image.

Ubuntu 8.04.4
[http://releases.ubuntu.com/releases/8.04.4/ubuntu-8.04.4-desktop-i386.iso](http://releases.ubuntu.com/releases/8.04.4/ubuntu-8.04.4-desktop-i386.iso)

I use a D630 at work - i used 8.04, 8.10, 9.04 and now 9.10 (Xubuntu) on it. No problems, everything working.
I dont know what is the difference between D630 and D620, but i have in BIOS TPM security related stuff - turned off everything related to it (computrace stuff also), also i turned off hdd encryption (this gave me problems in 8.04, never turned it back on, maybe its solved).
