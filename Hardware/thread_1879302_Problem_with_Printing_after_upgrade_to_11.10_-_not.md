### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-12-01
I have several different HP printers, some networked, and they all work fine under 11.10.

What worked for me was using CUPS to install the printers, not an HP installer.  It found all the networked printers very quickly -- unlike older versions where you had to manually enter the printer's network address.

You have to experiment with different drivers to see which one works best -- but in general, I have avoided the "simplified" driver versions.
