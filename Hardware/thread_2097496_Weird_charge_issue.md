When i plug the charger in the display bar at the top of the ubuntu screen shows the laptop is charging.

### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-12-23
Restart the computer and enter BIOS Setup.  (Hit F2 just as the Dell logo is coming on.)  You should be able to check the health of the battery in BIOS Setup.  Laptop batteries don't last forever - it's quite possible yours is dying.  You can probably find a replacement battery on eBay or Amazon new for about $30 USD.
