### Post by dabl on 2010-07-06
Linux drivers don't exist for all printers -- sorry.  Here is where you go to look up a printer and learn whether a driver exists:

[http://www.linuxfoundation.org/collaborate/workgroups/openprinting/database/databaseintro](http://www.linuxfoundation.org/collaborate/workgroups/openprinting/database/databaseintro)


For Canons, I have had good luck buying a proprietary Linux driver here:

[http://www.turboprint.info/](http://www.turboprint.info/)

The last one I bought cost about $40 USD.  Unfortunately, I don't see your PIXMA iP1900 listed there, either.
