Both drives work great by themselves, but as soon as I try to burn at the same time, the external SATA/USB drive is slowed to 1x - 1.5x burning until the internal SATA drive completes its burning session. The external will then speed up until the internal drive begins another burning session. The external drive ends up taking over 30 min to complete burning the 4.4GB image, in this time the internal drive has completed 3 discs.

In windows I have no issue making this setup working as intended. 

Anyone else experience this or have a workaround? Thanks.
