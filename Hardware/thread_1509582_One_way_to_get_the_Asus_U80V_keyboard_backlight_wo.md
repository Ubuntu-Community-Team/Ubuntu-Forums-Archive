### Post by potion on 2010-06-14
For anyone who's got an Asus U80V and can't get the keyboard backlight to work properly...

I just hit upon a little workaround. In Preferences>Power Management, I've got it set up to blank the screen when the laptop lid is closed. And I just realized that if I close and reopen the lid, the keyboard backlight comes on if I'm in a dark room. (For me, that doesn't happen as it should if I simply boot up in a dark room.) Just wanted to share in case that helps someone else.
