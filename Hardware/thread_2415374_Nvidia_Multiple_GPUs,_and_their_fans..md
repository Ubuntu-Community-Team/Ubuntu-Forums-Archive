### Post by pqwoerituytrueiwoq on 2019-03-26
I am guessing the hot card is the top card in your system
you could try mounting/rigging a case fan to force air between your 2 cards
you could try using a riser cable to get more space between the cards, you should be able to mount it anywhere you can find in a makeshift manner to since your card does not need access to the video out ports (do not put it against a glass side panel, it will not get enough air)
you could put the top card on a full coverage water block and water cool it

i assume you tried doing this:
```
nvidia-settings -a "[gpu:0]/GPUFanControlState=1" -a "[fan:0]/GPUCurrentFanSpeed=60" \
                -a "[gpu:1]/GPUFanControlState=1" -a  [fan:1]/GPUCurrentFanSpeed=50"
```
command is from this page: [https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/NVIDIA/Tips_and_tricks](https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/NVIDIA/Tips_and_tricks)
