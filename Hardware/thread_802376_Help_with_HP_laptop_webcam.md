I have the same and the webcam is a bit of a problem. i have it working in kopete but only with a redish tinge.

First thing is to check which camera you have (there are 2 possibles: Ricoh or Microdia. Type lsusb in a console will tell you.

Next drivers. Check this post out where I did manage to get my cam working (scroll down to webcam section):
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=512059]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=512059")

If your cam is a Ricoh, then I've seen a post that suggests better drivers are available here (I'm going to give them a go when I get chance):
[http://wiki.mediati.org/R5u870]("http://wiki.mediati.org/R5u870")

Good luck!
