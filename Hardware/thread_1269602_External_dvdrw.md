### Post by TenPlus1 on 2009-09-18
Only certain slimline external DVD-RW's will work without a PSU, but a salvaged standard DVD Writer will need an external case with it's own power adaptor to power the internals...  The 5volts that the USB cable supplies is in no way enough to power the whole unit...
