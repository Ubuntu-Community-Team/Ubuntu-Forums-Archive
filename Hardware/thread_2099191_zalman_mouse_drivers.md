### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-12-29
Mice don't need extra drivers, they all communicate with the computer through the USB HID standard which is supported on Linux.

If your mouse isn't working, it may be that your USB port is faulty or there is something else plugged into your computer that is drawing too much power from the bus.
