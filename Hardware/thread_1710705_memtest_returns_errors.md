### Post by Joe of loath on 2011-03-20
Yes, memtest should come back clean.

Is your system overclocked? That might effect it. Try one stick of RAM at a time, see which one is throwing up errors. Bear in mind you could have a faulty slot on the motherboard instead of dodgy memory.
