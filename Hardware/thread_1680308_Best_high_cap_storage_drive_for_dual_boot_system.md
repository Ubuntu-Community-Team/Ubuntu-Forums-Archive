### Post by cascade9 on 2011-02-06
Newegg reviews arent worth bothering with IMO. 

Hitachi deskstars have virtually northing in common with the old IBM deskstars (and most of them were pretty hardy anyway, I've got a few that are well over 7 years old, still running strong). There was one nasty bunch of deathstars (GXP75 and similar models), but somehow they all got a bad rep.

They really should have changed the name. People who would never buy ECS or even worse 'pc chips' parts have no peroblem with XFX....mainly because they dotn know its the same brand. 

AFAIK you cant get a 'firmware update' for any of the 4k drives in production that will tell software that it really is a 4k sector drive, not 512b. 

From that list I would probably get the WD GP, or the GP AV. Hitachi or samsung, maybe. Seagate? Nope. Thats just my opnion.
