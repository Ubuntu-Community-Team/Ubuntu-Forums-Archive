### Post by cwsnyder on 2012-10-13
You included the webcam as part of the requirement for your computer, not as an accessory.

The 'built-in' microphone would have a limited usefulness beyond a range of about 3 feet.  If you can use a USB webcam, you should DEFINITELY consider using an external microphone.

One part of your specifications points to a laptop or netbook, another part of your specifications points to a nettop or similar.
