### Post by rachel.greenham on 2013-11-02
Probably any currently-sold nvidia card will do you. I've been interchangeably using a geForce 210 and geForce 520  on two monitors with twinview and no problems. (These and many others have a DVI-DL, HDMI and VGA, but you can only use two of the ports at any one time.)

Flipside: I'm thinking of getting an AMD 7750 based card because i want to run *three* monitors, but you're scaring me (and all the amd issues generally seem to scare me) but i suspect given i'm no gamer, i just want a multi-monitor unity desktop, that even the open source radeon driver may actually do me.

So I have no recent AMD/ATI experience, but recent nVidia experience has all been pretty positive; also on an onboard-geForce 610-equipped htpc running xbmc. On 13.10 I just install nvidia-319-updates and reboot, it just works.
