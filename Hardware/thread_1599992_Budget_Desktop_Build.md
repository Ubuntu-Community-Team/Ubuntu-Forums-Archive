### Post by gordintoronto on 2010-10-18
Does your city have a dealer who sells refurbs? I'm in Toronto, and we have three dealers who carry refurbs, beginning at about $150. (But they are crap!) There are two items to be sure of: memory and the video card. Ubuntu is very happy with 512 MB of memory, and an nVidia 9400GT video card works extremely well. The rest affects speed and capacity to hold downloads, but it does not affect whether Ubuntu works.
