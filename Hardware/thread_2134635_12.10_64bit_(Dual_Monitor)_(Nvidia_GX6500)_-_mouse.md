Greetings,

I recently moved my computer, and no longer use a second monitor.  I'm unable to left click with a mouse without the second monitor plugged in.

Monitor 1 (PC port)
Monitor 2 (HDMI).

I dragged in a second monitor from the garage and when I plug it in (while booted) the mouse becomes fully useable.  Unplug the HDMI cable from the second monitor and I lose all click functionality again.  Keyboard remains active and fine regardless of Monitors.

I have tried this with multiple monitors and with multiple mouses.

I have tried removing twin view and cannot get around the (FAIL) low graphics modal dialog, and have no mouse to continue <---  here keyboard in non-responsive.

Has anyone found a way to successfully remove a 2nd monitor from the NVidia config and retain mouse functionality?

### Post by sadler_david on 2013-04-12
**[SOLVED]**

Encase anyone else experiences this bizarre behavior...

Creating a new user account resolved the issue.

I created a second user of myself and migrated everything in my /home folder over to the new user & mouse worked perfectly, and was able to run perfectly with just ONE monitor again.
