### Post by papibe on 2014-05-21
Hi chris236. Welcome to the forum ):P

Do you actually refer to the BIOS or EFI/UEFI?

I'm not familiar with UEFI, but BIOS does not depend on the OSes present. You have to press the proper key immediately after you press the power button. There are 2 techniques: keep it press the whole time until you get to the BIOS, or press and release it very fast as if you were on a game firing a weapon with the key.

If you get to the purple grub menu, it is too late, the system won't detected at that time.

I believe this is a laptop, so as a way to complete disconnect the OS from the hardware, you can remove the hard drive and turn on the machine.

There's no way to access the BIOS from Ubuntu. If you want to reflash it, go to the Acer site and look for a image. Some manufactures provide either an ISO that you can boot into, or a file that is compatible with FreeDOS.

Hope it helps. Let us know how it goes.
Regards.
