### Post by Miljet on 2008-11-21
I ran across on article in Computer Shopper magazine on this exact subject a few months ago. It seems that AGP went through numerous upgrades during its life, with very few of them being compatible. After reading all the explanations, I was like you, very confused. The last of the article suggested making sure that the seller has a very liberal return policy and be sure to check restocking fees. That tipped the scales in favor of upgrading both the motherboard and graphics card in my aging desktop. That way, I could go with PCIE X 16.
