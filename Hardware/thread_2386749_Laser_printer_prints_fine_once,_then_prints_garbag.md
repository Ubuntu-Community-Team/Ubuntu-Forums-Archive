### Post by Enigma12 on 2018-03-10
I had a somewhat similar problem back when I used a Lexmark laser with Lubuntu 14.04. If I printed a number of pages continuosly, all pages printed fine. If I paused the printing, like to print on the other side, then I got the "garbage." The only way I stopped that was to turn off the printer, then turn it right back on for subsequent page(s). The page then printed perfectly. You might try that. 

Since turning the printer off and on was annoying, I purchased an HP laser that has printed perfectly from day 1 on both Lubuntu 14.04 and 16.04. Now that Lexmark is attached to my number 2 computer running Lubuntu 16.04 and prints flawlessly always. I don't know the why of the matter, but my problem went away, so I don't care.
