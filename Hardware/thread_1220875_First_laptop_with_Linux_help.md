### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-07-23
Sounds as if all the software she will need is already installed on a default ubuntu install, so only updates will be needed.  As for drivers, it is impossible to know without an idea of the actual hardware in the machine.  One thing that may be needed is various codecs for DVDs and multimedia playback.  Once you have it I suggest you enable the medibuntu repos and install the w32codecs, libdvdcss2, adobe-flashplugin, gecko-mediaplayer, smplayer or gnome-mplayer or vlc, or even all three. Without those there will be no multimedia, and your mother will probably be very disappointed.
