### Post by sdennie on 2010-05-30
With a RAM upgrade it will probably work fine.  Having said that, even with a RAM upgrade, you may not find it usable as a desktop machine running Ubuntu.  For older hardware like that, you may be better off trying something like Puppy Linux, DSL or possibly Moblin.  It's been a while but, I recall DSL using something like 25M of RAM to come up to a full (but primitive) GUI while Moblin was using something on the order of 60MB to bring up a much more modern GUI.

Either would benefit from having more RAM but, they might work for you.  If the RAM upgrade is very expensive (I imagine finding RAM that old might be), it may be worth it to look at getting a cheap netbook.  If it's not much more expensive than the RAM, it will probably give you much better performance.
