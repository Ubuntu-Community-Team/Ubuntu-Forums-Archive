### Post by nkinar on 2009-04-30
Yes, I also have an HDX-16 laptop computer.  Apparently, sound is not supported yet on this particular hardware, but a patch has been submitted for inclusion in mainline:  [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/332479](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/332479)   You could either attempt to patch the kernel with the information given in the above link, or you could simply wait until the hardware is supported. I'm waiting until the hardware is fully supported, as I know from experience that patching kernels can be quite an interesting exercise.
