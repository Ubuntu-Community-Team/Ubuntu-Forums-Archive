### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-02
Sorry no one replied, but onboard SD slots very often require special drivers -- that are only supplied in Windows.

I've even upgraded Windows PCs to newer versions and THEN not had the SD card slots work anymore.  This is especially true with XP-era PCs.

So, there's most likely no way you're going to get the onboard SD card slot working in Linux.

Similar situation with the DVD drive.  VLC comes with its own codecs, so if it doesn't work, apart from experimenting with installing other codecs, it's not going to work.
