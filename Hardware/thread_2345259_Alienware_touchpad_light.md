### Post by s-chrzs-q on 2017-01-29
I've been unable to get to the bottom of the driver reporting to the system that the touchpad is in use, _however_, there is a solution;

Go into CMOS (or system setup, whatever it's called these days) and at the bottom of the advanced menu you have an option for touchpad backlight, it's probably set to auto if you're reading this, just change it to [ON] and problem goes away.

### Post by swmeyer on 2018-01-26
I had the same issue, with the touchpad light not activating on my Alienware 17 when in Ubuntu. 

Going into the BIOS, going to the advanced settings tab, and changing the touchpad backlight setting from auto to on DID turn the touchpad backlight on. Thank you for this suggestion; it is definitely an improvement!
