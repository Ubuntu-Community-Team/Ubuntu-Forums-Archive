### Post by Funklink on 2011-04-04
Nevermind, I figured I would post it here for anyone else wondering and weren't already aware that HPLIP is a program that has a ton of HP printer drivers and most likely came pre-installed on updated Linux OS otherwise you can go to the HP website and download an updated version from the outside link they provide. 

I think this will work: [http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html)

If not, like I said, check the HP website.

To get the printer set up I went to System->HPLIP Toolbox. Go to device and click setup device. It's pretty easy going from there. You will most likely need to have a USB printer cable to connect to your laptop/desktop computer during the initial part of the setup.
