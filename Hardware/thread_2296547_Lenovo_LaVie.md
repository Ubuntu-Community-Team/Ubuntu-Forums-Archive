### Post by susancragin on 2015-11-22
I have installed both Ubuntu LXDE and Debian LXDE on my Lenovo LaVie Z. They both worked really well, although I'm not a power user. One thing that bothered me was that the video drivers didn't seem to work as well as they might for the touch screen. The LaVie Z has a soft, flimsy touch screen, and it cannot really be written on, the way, say, an iPad can. The pen "skips" and its positioning is imprecise. (A finger works just fine.)
In addition, the refresh rate seems a little slower than it should be, when watching TV online. (Youtube is fine.)
I have been on Lenovo forums to complain about this, however, and it appears that Windows users may have the same problems.
