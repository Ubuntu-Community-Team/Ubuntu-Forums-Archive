### Post by Zorael on 2008-05-05
I can't say I've ever heard of a clear-cut solution (problem is pretty vague), but you're sure it isn't caused by the overclockage?

In any case, I'd take a basic troubleshooting approach to it and remove as many hindrances as possible when installing, and then readding them back one-by-one to see which introduces your stability issues. For you, this'd mean disabling the overclocking, removing extra devices attached (like USB), possibly even non-critical expansion cards (PCI). Then install. Then reconnect.

(For instance, I see [this thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=782993) on the first page here on Hardware & Laptops, so yours just might be related.)
