### Post by TheFu on 2017-01-20
NUCs cost about $130 too much for what they provide, IMHO.  For about $130 and 30 minutes of time, you can build a fairly respectable Intel system using normal-sized parts and a mini-ATX/ITX MB and case.  Is a small size REALLY worth more to you?  Plus really tiny footprint PCs often have overheating problems and use non-standard parts. 

If you need a monitor too - check out a used chromebook and get a Raspberry Pi v3 for your "server development" stuff.
