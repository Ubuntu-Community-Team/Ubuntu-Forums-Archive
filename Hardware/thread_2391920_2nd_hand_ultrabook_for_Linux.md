I have a hand me down toshiba satellite z930 ultra book (guy had windows 8 on it and it was getting very slow, probably got some virus but since he has money he just bought a Mac and gave this to me. :)). Super happy about it, great performance. I had Ubuntu 16.04 and now 18.04 with unity. I think all Toshiba ultrabooks have that.
