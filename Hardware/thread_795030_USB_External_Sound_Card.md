### Post by Zorael on 2008-05-15
I bought a Creative SoundBlaster MP3+ which Just Works. Autodetected upon connecting, Pulse sees it and adds it as a sink, everything's dandy.

The only drawbacks would be that it's made by Creative (:<) and that it *only* has stereo output, so no surround sound. RCA, optical and 3.5mm stereo connections.

Some fast googling turns up with [http://www.practicalpc.co.uk/reviews/hard/sound/creativemps.htm](http://www.practicalpc.co.uk/reviews/hard/sound/creativemps.htm). I just bought it in a random hardware store, on impulse.
