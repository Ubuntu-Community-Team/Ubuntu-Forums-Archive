### Post by lisati on 2008-09-02
This is offered as an opinion, based on my own minimal experience with a laptop that turned out to be slightly under-spec for capturing video via firewire from a tape-based camcorder.

If you're into something like real-time music or video processing, then "the more the merrier" will probably be useful for just about everything (gigahertz, ram, storage capacity)

Another important consideration would also be how quickly your hard drive can write data, without the computer losing its capacity to monitor its input channels.
