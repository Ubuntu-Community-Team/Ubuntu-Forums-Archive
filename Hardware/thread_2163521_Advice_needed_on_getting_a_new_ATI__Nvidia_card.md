It doesn't matter when AMD drops support in the Catalyst driver if the open-source driver is good enough, and with the recent additions of VDPAU support and proper power management, you probably won't need/want Catalyst by the time support for the 6450 ends. I found my 4550 to be an ideal card once the VDPAU acceleration support came out.
