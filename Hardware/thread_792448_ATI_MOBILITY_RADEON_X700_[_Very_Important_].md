### Post by MaindotC on 2008-05-14
What you're looking for is the [Ubuntu Hardware Compatibility List]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport").  Although you may not find your video card listed here, you can also check to see if ATI has released a driver for your video card on the [ATI Support & Driver Downloads]("http://ati.amd.com/support/driver.html") page.  There is usually a generic ATI driver for any Linux distribution.
