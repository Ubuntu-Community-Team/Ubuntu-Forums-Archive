Hello batterybaby, and Welcome to the forums ):P

It's very hard to say about your exact battery, and your situation. Many people who use their laptop frequently, and who charge/discharge their battery once or twice a day can render their battery inoperative in less than a year. This normally results in reduced battery life, but not having it charge altogether may be a different issue. As mentioned above, I would remove the battery, and check that there's no lint/dust/dirt/etc. on the contacts. If they look dirty at all, I would clean them off with compressed air, and a pencil eraser. Let the battery sit for a couple minutes outside of your laptop, and then re-insert it, and try to charge it again. If this still doesn't work, please let us know :)
