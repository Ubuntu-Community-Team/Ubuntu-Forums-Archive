### Post by squaregoldfish on 2011-08-05
It would be a heck of a lot of work, if it's even possible.

Laptop motherboards don't follow any particular standard in terms of layout, so the screw holes for attaching it won't match up to any standard desktop case.

If you get that sorted out, you then need to work out the ports (USB, VGA etc.) - the laptop doesn't have a nice array of them along the back to match a standard case. Instead they're all over the place to match the necessity of having ports on all 4 sides of the laptop case.

As with all these things, it's not impossible but probably more hassle than it's worth.
