### Post by jack frost on 2011-02-16
Just wanted to share this if you have the Asus G72GX or the G1.  I found this from a post on the Asus forums for a fix.  

[http://technology911.com/v/Xw7NYYLhO6U,asus-g71-white-screen-flicker-fix](http://technology911.com/v/Xw7NYYLhO6U,asus-g71-white-screen-flicker-fix)

Besides just pushing down on the connectors, I read where people actually said it was better to unplug and plug them back in again.

I actually pulled them out using needle nose and pushed them back in with a very small screw driver that I use for my glasses. Be careful not to pull the plugs out with too much force due to the delicate wires attached. My flickering has stopped with no issues so far. I did it about 2 weeks ago. I also took out the battery and unplugged the a/c  while I did this so no power connected.  I also did the battery/lcd brightness  recalibration after. That option is in the bios.
