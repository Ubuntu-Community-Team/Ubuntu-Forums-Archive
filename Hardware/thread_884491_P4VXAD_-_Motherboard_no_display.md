### Post by cariboo on 2008-08-09
Make sure your video card is seated properly. Do you get any display at all, like during post. If you don't get anything you may want to try resetting the bios to the defaults. You can do this by setting the jumper close to the battery to the open pin and leave it for a couple of minutes. If you don't have a reset jumper you can always remove the battery for 5 minutes. The other thing you can try is another video card to see if this makes a difference.
