### Post by kc1di on 2014-08-14
Hi Winston_Lim_H and welcome to Ubuntu,

That would depend a lot on the hardware and if the manufacturer provides hardware that is supported by Linux drivers. 
Most are but there are still some hold outs so if your having this machine built, make sure you specify you want it to be 100% compatible with linux. 

Or you could order one from a company that specializes in Ubuntu/linux pre installed. 
Such as [System 76 ]("https://system76.com/laptops/")or[ Zareason.]("http://zareason.com/shop/Laptops/")   
the UEFI if your not dual booting should not be a problem. 
Good luck in your quest :)
