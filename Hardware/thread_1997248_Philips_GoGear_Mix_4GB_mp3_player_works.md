### Post by etrexuser on 2012-06-04
I'd like to report that the very affordable Philips GoGear Mix 4GB mp3 player works with Ubuntu if you use it manually.

I got one for $14.98 at ship to store at the link below.  If you have a folder on your computer with an album name with X amount of mp3's in it, just plug this device in and drag that folder to the devices music folder.  I suppose you could also just drag mp3's to the devices music folder.

It works without having to use any software.

[http://www.walmart.com/ip/Philips-GoGear-Mix-4GB-MP3-Player-SA3MXX04KC-Assorted-Colors/17236796](http://www.walmart.com/ip/Philips-GoGear-Mix-4GB-MP3-Player-SA3MXX04KC-Assorted-Colors/17236796)

Note:  I have not upgraded to Ubuntu 12.4, but it works with my older version of Ubuntu so it should work with 12.4
