You can get one from any shop that sells computer components.

It's not that "today's fans are bigger", the fan sizes are the same standard sizes they have always been, but most cases these days are designed to sue bigger fans. Smaller ones are still used where necessary.

You can get pretty much any size you need, although the most common sizes would be 60mm, 80mm, 90mm and 120mm.

(It's hard to say what size your case uses, as "small as a CPU fan" doesn't really mean anything, as there is no such thing as a CPU fan. It's just a fan, you can bolt it anywhere you like, and it depends on your CPU cooler what size fan it uses, just like it depends on your case what size fan you can fit in it. But most likely you'd be looking for a 80mm fan).

Anyway, why don't you simply *measure* the air hole to see what size fan you need?
