### Post by kahlil88 on 2008-08-04
I'm not sure what advantages each filesystem has over the other, although there's probably a comparison page on Wikipedia. I don't think Ubuntu supports ReiserFS or ext4 out of the box, and I'm not sure what other formats GNU/Linux systems use
