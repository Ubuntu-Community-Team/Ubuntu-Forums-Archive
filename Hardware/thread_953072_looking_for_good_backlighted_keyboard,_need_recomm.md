I assume you're asking for an external, USB keyboard..

It's very hard to beat the Logitech G15, on any level.  Look for the older model, blue-backlit one.  In my opinion, it's the best keyboard ever produced; Crisp keys, blue back-lighting, 3 sets of 18 extra custom-programmable keys, media keys (Play/Pause, Stop, Forward/Back, Volume and Mute), a USB 1.1 hub, and to top it all off, a programmable LCD.

I don't know the status of the LCD compatibility with Ubuntu, as I don't use the G15 on my Linux box, but the community is huge, I'm sure someone can or has got it to do something useful.

Was about $70 USD when I got mine.


If that's not your fancy, take a look at the Razor Lycosa, which is backlit with laptop-style keys.  Several of my friends have it and love it - It doesn't seem to have media keys, though.
