### Post by oldsoundguy on 2010-09-21
installing the NVidia card should simply be a matter of installing the restricted driver, and the choice will be offered to you. (the generic NVida driver from Ubuntu works great in most cases, but 3-D and other effects are limited. I have that and it runs a cinema monitor without a problem.)(not a gamer, do not need all the bells and whistles) 
The wireless card .. really depends on the maker of the chip as to if it will work or not out of the box.  I have had great luck with D-Link and other MAJOR manufacturers and horrible results with Broadcom on installs I have done.
