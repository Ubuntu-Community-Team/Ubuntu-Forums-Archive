### Post by AnotherBrian on 2017-11-16
For those who may endeavour on the course I took here were my results. I picked up an i7 e7270 dirt cheap on ebay that was poorly spec'ed out so I was unsure of what I was getting. 
It had a small m.2 sata dirve and led screen. 

Purchased a touchscreen assembly for fairly cheap. Swapped out the LED and it runs just dandy.  The assembly does come with hinges but does not hinge covers.  The LED hinge covers are just a tad to small but will work but its not a perfect fit.  It seems about impossible to find touchscreen hinge covers on ebay or through other sources so that is a minor negative. 

My efforts to use my msata drive in the e7270 failed.  The m2 msata adapter which comes with a ribbon cable had the best hope of working.  I think I could have gotten it in there (after removing the smart card assembly)  but the battery was being placed under stress.  Also I was concerned about heat dissipation because the msata was up against the battery.  

If ya all have better luck on these issues let me know.
