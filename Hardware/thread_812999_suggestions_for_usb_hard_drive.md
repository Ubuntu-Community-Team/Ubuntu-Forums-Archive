### Post by Eman1955 on 2008-05-30
I just went through this research a month ago.  You have to decide whether you want portable or just external, because some externals are not meant to be "portable".  

I use to just buy Seagate or Maxtor, BTW which are now the same company.  The new Maxtor drives are not getting good reviews so I disregarded them.  Seagates have better reviews. I did a lot of reading of customer reviews on Newegg.com.  I used to dislike immensely Western Digital, but their external USB HD are getting good reviews. I used them (internal drives) for door stops years ago.  I can't believe I did it but I bought 2 - 500 GB (1tB) drives for $215 on Staples.com  Plus they have a good warranty, 3 years.

Everyone has their preferences.  I'm hoping they'll last!
