### Post by him610 on 2022-01-09
Well, my bluetooth adapter was delivered a few days ago. Installed into a USB 2.0 port on the laptop. I use Sony SRS-XB13 wireless speaker as I do not have wireless headset. You should not need to install anything since you have bluez and blueman.
Be advised this is what works for me, your setup may be different since you have microphone with your device.
1.  Turn on your bluetooth device.
2.  Click on the Blueman-applet icon, you should get a dropdown list. At this point, I click on the Devices option that opens another dialog box that may or may not display a list of your bluetooth devices. If not click on the search widget, it will go into search mode and should show all bluetooth devices within range. When you see your device, highlight it, and complete the setup process - you will need to pair and connect to it.


Hope this helps
