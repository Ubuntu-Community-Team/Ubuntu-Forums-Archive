### Post by iponeverything on 2010-03-07
If your not getting to the bios screen, more that likely your MB is toast. Everything else -- memory, cpu, hd's, etc are more that likely fine.

Glance over your MB, if you see a cap that is leaking or misshapen it might be as easy as replacing it.
