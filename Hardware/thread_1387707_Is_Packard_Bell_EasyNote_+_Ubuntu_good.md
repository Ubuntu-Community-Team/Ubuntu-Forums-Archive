### Post by Evoluate on 2010-03-27
Hey!

I have a packard bell easynote tj64 and I'm running karmic koala 9.10 (the amd64 bit version).

I haven't experienced any problems with the graphics card or any of the drivers so far. 

The most annoying and persistent issues are probably the wireless.
I can't exactly tell what wireless card I have but I know it's an atheros with a broadcom chipset. One issue for example is that the signal strength seems to deteriorate with every minute spent online, no matter how close you are to your router. The other thing are the hotkeys that lock the mouspad (so you can type without klicking something by mistake), the hotkeys for volume control etc don't work in the way they should. For example you can lock the mouspad but you cannot unlock it again since the key seems to only have a one way function on ubuntu.

I have spent hours checking the internet for solutions but at this moment in time I see no other solution as to wait until enough people decide to put ubuntu on their Tj64.

Hope that helped somehow,

cheers!
