### Post by tgalati4 on 2009-08-02
I'm currious as to what happened to your current machine?  Was it flexing of the motherboard that killed it?

Batteries only last 2-3 years or 300 cycles whichever occurs first.  If you used battery every day, then you are lucky to get a year out of it.

What is the model of your older Thinkpad?  How do the two compare in quality?

I would recommend a used T43P 15" with flexview screen.  Runs linux well, and has a high-res screen.  If you can find them, they run $300-400 depending on condition.  Avoid T41 and T42--their graphic chips tend to delaminate.

I'm not sure of the quality of the new lenovo's.  Nor would I risk spending a lot of money to find out.
