### Post by agim on 2009-01-08
Dells work really well. You can even get a dell with Ubuntu preinstalled.

You used to be warned to avoid Broadcom wireless cards. Its still a good idea (Dell doesn't use them), but they do provide a driver now for their cards. Its not often updated, so its still best to avoid it.

My sister also had a weird problem with a Toshiba and its wireless card, I don't remember which off of the top of my head.

I use a Compaq laptop, other than the broadcom issue, it works wonderfully.

Good Luck
