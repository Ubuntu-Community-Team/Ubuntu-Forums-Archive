### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-02-16
When I tried Ubuntu 11.10 with Gnome 3, I noticed it had a "create a Hotspot" built in.  I had two WiFi cards plugged in - I assume you'd need two to do it, unless your laptop is wired to a network.  You can buy a little USB WiFi dongle for $6 on eBay - I have a couple and they work pretty well after some configuration.

I haven't played with how to do this in Ubuntu 11.04, though.

But why not just buy a Wireless Router?  They can be had on sale often for under $20 USD.  The biggest reason I can see for needing a portable one is when you travel and want to share WiFi with several people or share files at a business meeting securely, at Starbucks or something...
