### Post by david tutton on 2011-01-11
Having recently bought an Epson Stylus SX425W printer i soon realised that there was no driver available in the CUPs printer database. The Epson product support team were of no help merely stating that they did not support Linux drivers. However I found this url that solved my problem.

[http://www.openprinting.org/drivers/epson-nx420](http://www.openprinting.org/drivers/epson-nx420)

This driver was produced by Seiko who own Epson. 
Others may find drivers for their printer problems here.:D
