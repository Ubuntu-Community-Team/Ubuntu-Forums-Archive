### Post by Grenage on 2010-10-19
Coincidences happen, and it's unlikely that Ubuntu actually killed your PC.  If you can't boot from a live CD, I'd postulate that it's down to hardware failure.  Linux ATI drivers are poor, but they don't generally toast the cards.

You can rule out obscurity by removing the battery and power cable, then holding down the power button for 10 seconds or so.
