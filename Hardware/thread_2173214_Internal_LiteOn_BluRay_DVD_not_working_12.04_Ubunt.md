### Post by carlwsnyder on 2013-09-08
First, K3b is for a Blu-Ray/DVD/CD burner, not a player.  Players can't burn anything, only read them.  <Edit> This device would only be capable of reading Blu-Ray disks, not writing them, although it should be capable of burning/writing to DVDs or CDs.  See [http://us.liteonit.com/us/bd-internal/item/bdinternal/ihes208](http://us.liteonit.com/us/bd-internal/item/bdinternal/ihes208)

Are you certain that the disk is blank and writable? </Edit>

Second, if 12.04 doesn't work, try the latest version of Ubuntu (either 13.04 or Saucy Salamander) before complaining that Blu-Ray isn't supported in the kernel.  12.04 is 17 months old and few Blu-Ray devices were available in April of 2012.  If you want an LTS version, wait for April of 2014.
