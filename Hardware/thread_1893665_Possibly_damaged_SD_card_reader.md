### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-12-12
Quite obviously, Ubuntu has NOTHING to do with a spring working or not working -- that is purely hardware.

If you concern though is that, even when inserted, you can't read the contents of an SD card in Ubuntu, that's more likely a driver problem -- and a common one at that.

I ran into the same driver problem with 11.10, so I am now using a USB-connected SD card reader -- and it works fine.
