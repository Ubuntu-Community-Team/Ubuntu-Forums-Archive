### Post by teeleef on 2010-05-20
This week I bought my 25yr old Son a V13 Netbook from Novatech.  His old Medion Netbook gave up the ghost two weeks ago.  

As a long serving XP user, my Son wanted XP installing on the machine, however as it came without a cd/dvd drive I asked if I could put Ubuntu 10.04 Netbook Remix on it until he bought an external cd reader.

I unwrapped the netbook, inserted the usb stick and installed Ubuntu.  A completely flawless install.  All hardware detected and it ran like a dream.

My Son has used GNU/Linux in the past, as I don't have a Windoze pc at home. He managed to tether his Android phone to the laptop and even did some command line work to get the phone to speak to the netbook.  

All is working well!

Many thanks as always to the Ubuntu developers and staff.  

Greater thanks to Stallman, Torsvald and the community, and finally thanks go to Novatech for a cute netbook free of the Micro$oft tax!


Regards,
