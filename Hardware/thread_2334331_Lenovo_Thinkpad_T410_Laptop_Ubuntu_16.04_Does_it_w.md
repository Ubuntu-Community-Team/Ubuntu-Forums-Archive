Hi,
I have a T410 (25223EG) and previously used 12.04 and 14.04 on it and was happy with that. Today I upgrade to 16.04 and so far I have a couple of issues:

* graphical glitches (can easily be reproduced by switching focus between terminals and hitting a keyboard key after every switch)
* slow performance in general (Google Hangout + one Google Docs document was terribly slow)
* thinkfan complains that Module thinkpad_acpi doesn't seem to support fan_control

So, out of the box, 14.04 works way better according to my old notes, but I wanted to upgrade to get newer browser(s).

Haven't verified these things: laptop keyboard (external works just fine), laptop touchpad, laptop touchpoint (the red rubber thingie), internal video (at least shows up in Google Hangout, external camera works fine), laptop speakers (headset plug works just fine), laptop microphone, bluetooth (shows up just fine though).
Seems to work: wifi, docking port and two external monitors.
