### Post by andrewc6l on 2009-09-14
I've had good luck with the Brother MFC-9840 CDW ([http://reviews.cnet.com/multifunction-devices/brother-mfc-9840cdw/4505-3181_7-32587735.html](http://reviews.cnet.com/multifunction-devices/brother-mfc-9840cdw/4505-3181_7-32587735.html)). It mostly works on Linux (except 2-up pages face the wrong way no matter what the setting), and wasn't too hard to configure. Brother has OK Linux support ([http://solutions.brother.com/linux/en_us/](http://solutions.brother.com/linux/en_us/)).

It's a beast, though - you'll probably want a separate table for it :-/
