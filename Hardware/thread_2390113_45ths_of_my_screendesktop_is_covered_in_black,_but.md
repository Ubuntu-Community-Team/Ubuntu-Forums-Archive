### Post by mörgæs on 2018-04-25
This has happened a lot for Atom processors. Do a fresh install (no upgrades) of 18.04 and the problem should be solved.

### Post by mörgæs on 2018-04-26
I was referring to an upgrade from 16.04 or 17.10 to 18.04. More often than not they have wrecked my systems. 

Downloading and applying updates to 18.04 during the installation is fine.
