### Post by dabl on 2008-07-08
I'd suggest going into your new BIOS and look at the hard drive settings.  If they are SATA drives, look at the "mode" and see if it can be changed.  Mine like to be "AHCPI" but maybe yours would rather be "legacy IDE" or something.
