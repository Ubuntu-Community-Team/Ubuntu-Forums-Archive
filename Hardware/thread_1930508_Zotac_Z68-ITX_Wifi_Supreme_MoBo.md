### Post by fjgaude on 2012-02-27
I looked at that board before buying the ASRock Model 68M-ITX/HT. Z68 chipset, all work even booting from an SSD, except it doesn't come out of suspend, will not resume.

The UEFI BIOS is very fast, cutting boot time by about four seconds. SATA 6G SSD cuts it in half, from about 40 to 20 seconds total.

I would think the Zotac with the same chipset will work as the ASRock does.
