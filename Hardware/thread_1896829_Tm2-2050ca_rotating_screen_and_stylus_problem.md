### Post by thed0ctor on 2012-01-20
I figured out a solution for those having a similar problem with rotating the stylus of a computer.

These link was useful:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=6274392&postcount=1](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=6274392&postcount=1)

It kind of confirms that the stylus isn't being rotated. I found a program, however, that took care of the problem: [https://launchpad.net/magick-rotation](https://launchpad.net/magick-rotation)

Follow the readme in the installation instructions and you'll be set! Hope this helps others
