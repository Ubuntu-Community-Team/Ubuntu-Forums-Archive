### Post by rockey12 on 2009-01-16
Those who are learning the basics of computer repair often assume that all boot issues are due to a hard drive failure.  The truth is, hard drives are probably the most reliable.  That does not mean that they last longer than memory modules or video cards.  They are rarely at fault when a PC is in for repair.  Hard drive failures are generally pretty easy to troubleshoot.  The operating system will include tools to report on the hard drives reliability when it’s assessable.
