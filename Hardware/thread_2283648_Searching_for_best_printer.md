### Post by mattharris4 on 2015-06-24
The Canon Pixma MG2120 and Pixma MG3520 both have drivers in the buntus that you can select from via the printer utility.  Both work fine, the only problem is they don't send ink level info to the computer so you don't have warning of a low ink cartridge.  Both also have a copier and scanner in them, the scanner works using Simple Scan once the printer driver is selected in the print utility.

Here is a link to the Pixma MG3520:  [http://www.officedepot.com/a/products/210107/Canon-PIXMA-MG3520-Wireless-Inkjet-Photo/](http://www.officedepot.com/a/products/210107/Canon-PIXMA-MG3520-Wireless-Inkjet-Photo/) .  The MG2120 no longer appears on the Office Depot website, I bought mine over two years ago so it may no longer be manufactured.
