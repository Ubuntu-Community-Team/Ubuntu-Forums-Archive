### Post by ajgreeny on 2018-11-02
Check that you have **hplip** installed in your system; I also recommend you install **hplip-gui** as it gives you a good GUI for printer management, including installing printers.

I assume your printer is attached by USB as that is the only connection mentioned at the hplip site.
[https://developers.hp.com/hp-linux-imaging-and-printing/supported_devices/index](https://developers.hp.com/hp-linux-imaging-and-printing/supported_devices/index)
