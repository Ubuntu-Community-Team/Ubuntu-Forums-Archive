### Post by sudodus on 2014-07-01
The CPU horsepower and the RAM size are enough for standard Ubuntu, but I would probably select Xubuntu anyway (or even Lubuntu and mplayer2, if you want to play high resolution video (1920x1080 p)).

What graphics and wifi are there (those models are delivered with Intel and AMD graphics, Intel is more likely to work with Ubuntu, but AMD might be better if it works). Have you checked the [Laptop COMPATIBILITY List]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1543006")?
