### Post by jayson.rowe on 2009-03-15
Are you running Linux only or do you dual boot?

For Linux it won't matter - it'll just pick up the new stuff and run w/ it. With Windows, you'll be ok as long as you are dropping that Phenom on the same motherboard - it'll only freak out if you change out the motherboard (and even then only sometimes)...

BTW - just curious why a 9850 rather than one of the new Phenom II chips? They are WAY better - the 810 x4 and the 720 x3 are pretty low cost as well.
