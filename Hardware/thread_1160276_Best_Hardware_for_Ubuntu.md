### Post by dtoronto on 2009-05-15
As far as hardware goes you probably want to be especially careful when selecting sound and video drivers.  Most Intel and AMD chipsets will work fine.  If you plan on using a mainboard with on-board video and sound then you'll probably want to check it out.
