### Post by pointcrossed on 2011-05-27
You probably realized this info by now but this is for any D430 owner that has issues. I am running 11.04 on my D430 I upgraded from 10.10; not a good idea. 10.04 and even 10.10 is a very stable version for the D430 and I am sure 11.04 will stabilize with further updates but its far from ready, functional but not ready for the D430. It is so buggy and not worth it, my cpu fan runs constantly, which it didn't do with 10.04 or 10.10. I have resource problems to the point I have to reboot. 10.04 and 10.10 were fast and had no issues with 1 GB of memory. I am running 2 GB of memory in 11.04 and I can run the browser or an any other application but not both at the same time or it would start to freeze. You cannot roll the version back which would be nice, especially when you find out its not a stable version like other OS out there, which would be a nice feature. Its probably a good idea to hold back for now and stick with 10.10 at best if you are strictly an end user, especially if you want to get some good use out of your D430. Hope this helps and good luck to you
