### Post by M_Mynaardt on 2011-12-16
Hi all reading this!

Got it sorted out…

The thing was the settings took some trial and error (as in, 'mostly error') for the computer that would be receiving the wireless signal.  My receiving computer is now on Lubuntu.  I had to make sure the method was set to "Infrastructure", and the IPv4 was enabled.  When I'd finally messed about with those it worked just fine.
