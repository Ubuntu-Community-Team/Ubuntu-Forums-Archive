### Post by Javich on 2010-06-28
Ubuntu itself doesn't, however the reason why the batteries are lasting less is because the same box may have throttling enabled for power, i.e. when running on batteries, changes the maximum DVD/CD drive speed, lowers the cpu speed, lowers screen brightness, etc. Depending on your ubuntu distro you should find a way to have the same behavior on linux than on windows and make the battery last longer.
