### Post by brus brother on 2013-11-27
Linux Mint 15
HP DC7900
Installed all printer and scanner drivers from Brother website.
Everything seems to work except duplex printing.
The option is greyed out.
Duplex printing works under Windows 7 on the same computer (separate HD) so I know the printer works.
Anyone have this unit (or other Brother printer) enabled for duplex printing?
SOLVED: Not sure why but during initial installation, recommended driver was 7840W. When I changed it to 7860DW cups driver, duplex works as expected.
