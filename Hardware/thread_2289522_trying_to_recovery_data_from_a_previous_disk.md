### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2015-08-04
Hi and welcome.

What wubi did - paste tense because it has been discontinued and *de facto *unsupported for so time now - was never a proper installation. Your whole (old) Ubuntu is but a file inside the Windows partition.
There's no other way than booting that drive directly and do your backups to somewhere else. After that do what you want with the drive.
