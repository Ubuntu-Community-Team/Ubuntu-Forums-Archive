### Post by cascade9 on 2011-01-22
800MHz is waht the Phenom II CPUs throttle down to. It should still go back up to 3.3GHz under heavy load. 

'Unknown processor' processor means that your motherboard doesnt have full  support for the CPU. That should be fixable with a BIOS update.
