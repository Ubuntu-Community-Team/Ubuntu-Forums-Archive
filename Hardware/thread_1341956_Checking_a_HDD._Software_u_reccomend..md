### Post by doas777 on 2009-11-30
any SMART enabled utility.
I use speedfan in windows, and in karmic, there is the new disk utility under System->Administration. 

SMART will give you an overview of the lifetime stats of the device (peaks and thresholds and whatnot) that can be used to rate your drive on both fittness and performance. it won't necessarily tell you about bad blocks though so additional tools may be required.
