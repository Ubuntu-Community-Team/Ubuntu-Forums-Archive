### Post by General_Faliure on 2014-02-09
I used this mainboard for years in my main system with a core 2 duo 8400 (@3.6GHz), 4 Gb ram and several AMD video cards, in dual boot windows and various linux distributions.
Linux (and windows) always worked without issue.
The mainboard works now in my arcade cab, now with a Nvidia 9500 video card with Lubuntu 13.10, a lot of emulators and Wahcade as frontend.
It's still running great, and i expect it to for years to come.
