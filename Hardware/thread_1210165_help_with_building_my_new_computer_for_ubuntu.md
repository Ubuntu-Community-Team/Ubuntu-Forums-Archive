Personally, I got the same case but then in green/black, love it.
You won't see the PSU unless you're really looking at it. So I'd instead get a quality branch PSU instead, like Corsair or Be Quiet! units. They might be a bit more expensive, you get the investment returned because they're efficient, using less power then non-efficient PSUs...

Graphics card, avoid any geforce card that has as second number a 4. It is simply crap. I'd go for [an ATI HD4670](http://www.tigerdirect.com/applications/SearchTools/item-details.asp?EdpNo=4483711&CatId=28) instead. It beats a 9400 by a mile as far as I know... :)

I'd suggest a [Western Digital WD5000AACS](http://www.tigerdirect.com/applications/SearchTools/item-details.asp?EdpNo=4040008&csid=ITD&body=MAIN#detailspecs) instead. 500GB, 16MB cache, ought to be good. In any case, I'd recommend to stick with Western Digital.

### Post by DownTown22 on 2009-07-16
Have you compared prices on Newegg yet?

Typically I find their prices to be much better than TigerDirect's.

So.....

- The case is alright, but I like simpler cases.  I used to have a case with windows, lights etc, but got kind of tired of it after a while.  My latest is a Cooler Master Centurion which has amazing air flow.

- I'd go with a Corsair, Cooler Master or OCZ power supply.  I've had good luck with them all!  Mind you, the Cooler Master is a little louder than the Corsair, but still not that loud.

- Can't go wrong with an Asus motherboard!  But why not a mid-ATX?

- Nice CPU.  I don't really see the need for a quad core...seems rather excessive to me, but hey, that's just me.

- Corsair is an excellent choice for RAM!

- I always go with nVidia for graphics card.  Never tried an MSI before, I like eVGA or XFX personally.

- A lot of people like WD hard drives.  I've only used Seagate and Samsung hard drives in the past and prefer Samungs as they're quieter.

- I don't think there's any issues with the DVD drive...as far as I know, DVD drives are DVD drives.  I usually go with Lite-on.

Hopefully that was some help....
