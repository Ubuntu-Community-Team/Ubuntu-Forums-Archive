### Post by dabl on 2011-03-04
Cool avatar!  :P

I'm not going to study your boot chart -- I can't even read it.  But here are two suggestions:

1. Use S2RAM, instead of shutting down your system.  It wakes up much faster than booting.

2. Buy a good SSD, and install your OS on that.  They boot way faster than hard disk drives.  I have two of them. My desktop with a PCI bus SSD boots to the KDE greeter in 11 seconds.
