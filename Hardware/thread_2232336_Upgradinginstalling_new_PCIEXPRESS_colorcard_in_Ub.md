### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-07-01
Do you mean a video card/adapter? There should not be a problem but if you are using a proprietary video driver then change it for the open source video driver before you make the swap. If you are changing from one video card maker to another video card maker then any proprietary video driver already installed will be incompatible. So, to avoid problems use the open source video driver. We can change card video drivers in System Settings>Software and Updates>Additional Drivers.
