### Post by vandorjw on 2010-07-29
Click "System --> Administration --> Hardware Drivers"

Click on "NVIDIA accelerated graphics driver (version current) [Recommended]
just once.

Then click "Activate"

Wait....

Click OK.. Close

Reboot

Enjoy
