### Post by cascade9 on 2010-03-26
The S3 savage is suppoted. Prety rare card, but it has a package- 

[http://packages.ubuntu.com/karmic/xserver-xorg-video-savage](http://packages.ubuntu.com/karmic/xserver-xorg-video-savage)

Passive cooling is probably not possible. Maybe, if you can underclcock the CPU (not likely with most laptops). You might be able to get a quieter fan, the stock fans tend to be pretty loud. 

Using a CF card (or a USB stick, but not a good idea if yuo only have USB 1) is pretty easy. 

Moving the guts of a laptop into a different case is....well....a right pain, at best.
