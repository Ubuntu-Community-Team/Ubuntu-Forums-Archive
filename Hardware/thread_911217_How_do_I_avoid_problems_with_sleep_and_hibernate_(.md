### Post by imho74 on 2008-09-09
I think that you have bought your laptop already and that my post is too late but better late than never: I have the Aspire 2920 and Ubuntu works like a charm, except suspend. Hibernation works well, only suspend won't.

Suspend works manually with the hibernate package. I have installed it and I suspend the notbook with

```
sudo hibernate-ram
```

and it wakes up without any problem.
