### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-10-28
> Is this the kind of adapter to buy?

Only a fool would say 'yes.' Would you listen to a fool?

Ask yourself these questions:

1) What is the voltage coming out of the USB port and going into the speakers/amplifier?

2) What is the maximum current drawn by the speakers/amplifier?

3) What is the maximum power drawn by the speakers/amplifier?

4) Do these specifications match up with those of the charger?

5) Should I be asking this question in the Absolute Beginners Section or in the Community Cafe section of this forum?
