### Post by searchfgold6789 on 2012-01-23
I can confirm that, yes, you are correct in saying that Lite-On does not know crap about making a DVD burner.
However maybe you can fix your problem. If there's an exact error, that would help. Also, the outputs of the 'mount' command may help. But in general DVD burners tend to work.
Try opening the terminal and typing:```
sudo mount /dev/sr0 /mnt
```and see what happens.
Plextor and Lightscribe are good brands to buy.
