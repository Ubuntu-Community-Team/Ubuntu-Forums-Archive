### Post by krazyd on 2008-11-03
nVidia is currently your only option.

the fglrx drivers don't do composited 3d or video.

the open source radeon / radeonhd drivers do composited video (without flicker etc.) but still don't do composited 3d. They are also a bit slower than fglrx for full-screen 3d gaming etc.

my 6xxx series geforce works well on all your points - except that it isn't very recent ;). AFAIK, the newest geforce cards work as well.
