### Post by craecke on 2012-06-08
I've just had this problem, too. What fixed it for me was to turn off hardware acceleration for those videos. Just right-click on any Youtube video and uncheck the box that enables hardware acceleration, and it should be fine. If you find that the checkbox is unclickable, try logging out and logging back in with Ubuntu 2D. The videos will continue to work in the future when you log in to standard Ubuntu.
