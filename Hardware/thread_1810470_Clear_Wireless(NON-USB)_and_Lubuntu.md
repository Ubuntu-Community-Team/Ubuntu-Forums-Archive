### Post by cavalier911 on 2011-07-26
You set your own WPA password in the hotspots webadmin panel by connecting with your web browser to [http://192.168.0.1](http://192.168.0.1) and entering the admin password provided by clearwire.

Directions Here:
[http://www.clear.com/support/faq/connection-issues/wireless-network/how-do-i-secure-the-wi-fi-network-i-use-on-my-clear-spot-4g-personal-hotspot](http://www.clear.com/support/faq/connection-issues/wireless-network/how-do-i-secure-the-wi-fi-network-i-use-on-my-clear-spot-4g-personal-hotspot)
