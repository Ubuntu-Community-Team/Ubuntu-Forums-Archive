Some info: nvidia-96.43.23 was in 12.04 since the beginning of 2013 (in "proposed" repo), then few weeks after, it went in the standard repo.
I have a resume/thaw issue with it that remains, but I found a guy saying he has his 440 (nv17) video working by setting grub to GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX_DEFAULT="noquiet nosplash"and GRUB_TERMINAL=console (uncomment this one), then sudo update-grub. Btw, the nvidia driver, once installed with synaptic, needs to be activated (control panel/additional drivers)

 This didn't work for me (GeForce4 4200Go nv28) but I hope this all info will help you.
See all in the end of  [https://bugs.launchpad.net/nvidia-drivers-ubuntu/+bug/948053](https://bugs.launchpad.net/nvidia-drivers-ubuntu/+bug/948053)
