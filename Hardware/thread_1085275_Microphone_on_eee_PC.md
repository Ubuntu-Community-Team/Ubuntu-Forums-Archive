### Post by TuortoAm on 2009-12-02
Seems like this is still a problem for some folks, this solution worked for me:
 
I have an asus eee pc 900, with 9.10 installed. Like most people, most everything worked out of the box, except the microphone on the top bezel of the display.
 
In order to get this to work:
 
Go to Sound Preferences (Right click on the speaker icon on the panel)
Go to the <input> tab
Pull down the Connector drop down - you should see "microphone 1" selected, and "microphone 2" available
Choose <microphone 2>
raise the input volume until the <input level> meter shows activity
Close the window
 
I tested this first with the Sound Recorder Application and then with Skype - both worked succesfully.
 
Hope this helps others....
