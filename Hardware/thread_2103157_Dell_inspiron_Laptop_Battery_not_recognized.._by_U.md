### Post by ahallubuntu on 2013-01-09
I have an Inspiron 1545 too (using it now) and have run 10.04 LTS and 12.04 LTS (and booted the others in between)  and have never had a battery not recognized message.

Are you sure the batteries you're installing are actually good?  Where are you getting them?

Do you see a battery in BIOS (F2 key when you restart the computer)?

Are you sure your charger isn't bad and simply can't charge the battery anymore (and each battery is completely dead)?  Dell chargers are notorious for failing in a way that they can still power the laptop but no longer charge the battery.

Could be a hardware problem with the laptop itself.
