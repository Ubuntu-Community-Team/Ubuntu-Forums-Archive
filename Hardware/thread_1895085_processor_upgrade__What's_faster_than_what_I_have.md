### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2011-12-14
All of the processors you could upgrade to are not worth the investment in my opinion. If you decide to upgrade double check that the motherboard BIOS supports the processor chosen, just because the socket is the same doesn't mean it'll work.

A much better option would be to purchase a new motherboard, processor, and memory. That would cost not much more than a new processor for your current system but would be substantially faster.
