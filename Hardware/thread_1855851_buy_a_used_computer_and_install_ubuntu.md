### Post by WasMeHere on 2011-10-07
In Sweden, you can buy used computers from companies, with a guarantee that they work (3-12 months depending on company). These computers are usually professional quality desktops or laptops that have been replaced after 2-4 years. The companies shred the hard disks (wipe them in a certified way), test and sell the computers as is or with a licensed operating system re-installed.

This is often a good choice if you need a cheap but reliable computer (unless you want to play PC-games or play or edit high definition video). An extra bonus with a 2-4 year old computer is that linux has 'almost always' had time enough to make software that can manage the hardware in the computer.

If there is no such company in your country, maybe you can start one ;-)
