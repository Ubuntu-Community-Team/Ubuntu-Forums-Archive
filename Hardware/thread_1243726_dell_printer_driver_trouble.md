### Post by cjazz on 2009-08-18
Before you dive into extracting your download, you might want to try installing the printer via Ubuntu's printer utility under System > Administration > Printing. According to the OpenPrinting database site [http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Dell-1710]("http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Dell-1710"), your printer is a rebranded Lexmark E232 and works fine with the HP Laserjet 5 driver. Hope this helps.
