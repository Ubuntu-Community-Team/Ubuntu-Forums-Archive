### Post by weatherman2 on 2016-06-21
Next time, Ralph, buy a generic Dell power adapter on eBay for about $10 USD.  They will charge the battery as well as power the laptop.  Or buy a used one.  My local thrift store usually has them for about $5 USD.  I've bought probably ten of them over the years (because that price is great when you are refurbishing old laptops) and never had a problem with any of them.

Most Dell laptops between about 2005 and today use a 65W PA-12 or a 90W PA-10  (you can use a 90W PA-10 adapter in place the 65W but not the other way around).  Some Dell laptops can charge the battery faster if the wattage is higher.  But check to see which type your specific laptop needs.
