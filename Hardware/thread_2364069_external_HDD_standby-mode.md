### Post by Autodave on 2017-06-18
How long is "awhile"? A few seconds? Normal. A few minutes? Not normal.

Do you use an external power source for the for this drive? Do you have ANY powered USB devices hooked to computer? Even a powered USB extension will back-feed power through the USB port in the PC.
