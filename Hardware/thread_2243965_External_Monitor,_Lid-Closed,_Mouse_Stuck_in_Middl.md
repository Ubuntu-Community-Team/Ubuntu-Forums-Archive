### Post by SLaCK3r on 2014-09-12
Probably should've experimented more before I posted this lol but I solved it.

To whoever may run across this problem. Ask yourself this: "does my laptop have a touchscreen?"

If your answer is "yes,' then you need to disable it by referring to this thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2209083&p=12945855#post12945855](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2209083&p=12945855#post12945855)
