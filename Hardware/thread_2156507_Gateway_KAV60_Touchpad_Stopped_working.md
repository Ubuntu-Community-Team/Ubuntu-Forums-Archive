### Post by cmb271 on 2013-06-22
after a large amount of troubleshooting I found it was either the f6 or f7 key, I took out the battery, unplugged it, gave it a moment, hooked it back up, booted into ubuntu and repeatedly pressed f6 and f7 and it worked. sorry for taking up ubuntu server space I hope this helps people with the similar problem.
