### Post by BigJules on 2009-05-18
Thought I'd just pass this one on -

If you've got a hard drive retired after an upgrade, why not put it to good use? You can get an external 3.5" IDE to USB 2.0 drive enclosure for peanuts, complete with cabling, metal enclosure and power supply (I got one from PlusCom for < $20)

What this does is turn the HDD into a USB stick, and Jaunty handles it at near native speed as a transparent extension to the fs. I use it to prep a disk image to have as a drop-in standby, or as a truly massive backup external HDD - the metal case is only 4"x 8" x 1", or as an easily portable sucker of data from a newly returned laptop. No end of uses, and as value for money, it can't be beat... :D
