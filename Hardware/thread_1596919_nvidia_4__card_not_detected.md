### Post by macstevejb on 2010-10-19
Nvidia v96.xx legacy driver currently not compatible with xorg 1.9 in Ubuntu 10.10.

Until nvidia come up with an updated driver, users of old nvidia legacy cards are stuck for the timebeing.

See this bug:

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xorg-server/+bug/626974](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xorg-server/+bug/626974)
