How I installed ubuntu netbook remix onto a Lenovo 10e

You will need 
a laptop with a cd burner
a cd burning program
good quality blank cd disk
your Lenovo 10e
a usb cd drive (they are cheap, just go and buy one).

1) Download the netbook remix, you can do this on any computer.
[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download-netbook](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download-netbook)
2) You now have a big ISO file,  take this to the computer with the burner (for example, just move it onto a usb disc using Windows explorer, etc).  Note, moving it onto a USB disc does not give you a bootable USB disc, that is dealt with in other forums.
3) Using your burner program, burn an ISO image onto a CD . The burner menu should give you this exact option somewhere, you go to 'create a data disk' then you should see an option to burn an ISO file, then select your Remix ISO file,  follow the prompts.
4) After burning, take out and label your CD disk, "ubuntu netbook remix".
5) Start your Lenovo, press F2 (I think it was F2) early to get into the BIOS, and use (shift) +/- to change the bootable device to USB CD drive.
6) Plug in your USB CD into the Lenovo, restart with the Ubuntu remix CD, and the fun begins.
7) Once installed we have to deal with the Broadcom wireless transceiver issue, or you will not get wireless.
8) Plug in your wireless router using a network cable connecting directly both the Lenovo ethernet port to a spare port on your router. 
9) Now, you should have a direct internet connection.
10) Go to System, Admin, Hardware drivers, and TWO (2) Broadcom drivers will appear (in time), one says STA and the other interprets these drivers. You require both, so install (activate) one, then the other.
11) Disconnect the ethernet cable. reboot.
12) To then get wireless services coming up in the Network icon I had to press the  button with a green TV and pictorial radio waves (to the right of the OFF/ON switch) on and off a few times, then the Lenovo seemed to get the message and give me a list of available wireless networks. 
13) Key in.
