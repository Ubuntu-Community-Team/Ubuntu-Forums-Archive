### Post by prshah on 2012-06-28
> **Chameleon102 said:**
> it's been on pretty much constantly for about a year with no problems.

there was no signal from the HDMI port.

 short period where the system was beeping,

Given these symptoms (and especially if you notice date/time is not maintained correctly - difficult AFTER booting into the OS since it will automatically correct date time from 'net), I would suggest that you check the CMOS battery.

This is a tiny, coin type battery, usually CR2032 (3v). You can use a standard digital multimeter on 20 VDC setting to check this.

If the battery is OK, or if the system still misbehaves after about 3-4 hours of battery replacement, then your initial assumption may be right.
