### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-11-12
Both are dual core.  Yes, one is an ultra-low power CPU that will also probably improve your laptop battery life a bit and generate a tad less heat.

But the i5-3210M performs about 33% faster on average of benchmarks, according to these sites:

[http://www.cpubenchmark.net/cpu.php?cpu=Intel+Core+i5-3317U+%40+1.70GHz](http://www.cpubenchmark.net/cpu.php?cpu=Intel+Core+i5-3317U+%40+1.70GHz)
[http://www.cpubenchmark.net/cpu.php?cpu=Intel+Core+i5-3210M+%40+2.50GHz](http://www.cpubenchmark.net/cpu.php?cpu=Intel+Core+i5-3210M+%40+2.50GHz)
