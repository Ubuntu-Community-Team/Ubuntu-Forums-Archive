### Post by Scunizi on 2009-06-22
I've been looking to do the same and have settled on a mini-itx system.  The components I'm still trying to decide on.  There are lots of options and it's a small form factor with low power consumption and decent performance to great performance.. I have an Intel core-2-duo E8400 3.0 processor that's looking for a home on a compatable board.  That's a fast processor and the mini-itx has some options for it.  Check out [www.newegg.com](www.newegg.com) and search for mini-itx for motherboard and case options.  Some even come with a processor.  Add your old drive, throw in some ram and hook it to the big screen! :)
