### Post by derekmbarnes on 2010-01-06
Main Menu: System > Administration > Hardware Drivers

Provided your Internet connection is working, you should get a list of any proprietary drivers you need. Just be sure you're getting the right ones; e.g. you may be offered a graphics driver that your card isn't actually compatible with, so double-check before trying to install.
