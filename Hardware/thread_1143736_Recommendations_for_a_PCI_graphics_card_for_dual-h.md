The only upgrade possibilities are 2x PCI slots (no AGP, no PCIe). So, I would like advice - are there any PCI graphics cards that work well under Ubuntu and would let me run two monitors with DRI enabled?

I have found examples such as the GeForce 6200, but cannot obviously see if this would support dual-monitors at high resolutions with direct rendering.

Thanks in advance for your help! Mark
