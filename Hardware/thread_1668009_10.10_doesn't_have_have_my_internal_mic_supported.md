Edit: I installed the aslamixer and I started to play with all the settings and realized that ubuntu had indeed recognized my mic. The problem was in PulseAudio that I had not set the hardware type to "Analog Duplex" and once I did it recognized that I had an input(which was muted as the other websites I found said) and I enabled that to let the audio work perfectly(at least as well as this little mic lets it)
