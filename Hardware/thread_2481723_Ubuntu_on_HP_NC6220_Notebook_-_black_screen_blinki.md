### Post by tea for one on 2022-12-07
Recommended system requirements:
2 GHz dual-core processor or better
4 GB system memory
25 GB of free hard drive space
Internet access is helpful
Either a DVD drive or a USB port for the installer media 

Info found here [https://ubuntu.com/download/desktop](https://ubuntu.com/download/desktop)

Your PC does not meet the minimum requirements, perhaps try a lighter flavour i.e. Lubuntu
