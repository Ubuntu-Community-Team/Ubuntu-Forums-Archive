### Post by shatterblast on 2009-07-09
If you have the option, I would suggest trying to feed it adapters through USB instead.  I understand though that this method can still have complications.  I believe it mainly depends on if the device connects directly to your system or to a USB hub, and also, it can have issues on whether the compliance level happens to run on the 1.1 or 2.0+ level.  In most cases, 2.0 or higher compliance is best, but that's not always true.  Oddly enough, 1.1 compliance can do the job in most of the remaining compatibility issues.
