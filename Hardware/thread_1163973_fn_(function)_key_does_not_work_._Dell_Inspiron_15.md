### Post by lightstream on 2009-05-19
You can change which audio channels are affected by your volume controls by going to System > Preferences > Sound.

At the very bottom of the dialog which appears, in the **Default Mixer Tracks** section, you can select which channels and even the device if necessary. I think it's normally PCM or Master.
