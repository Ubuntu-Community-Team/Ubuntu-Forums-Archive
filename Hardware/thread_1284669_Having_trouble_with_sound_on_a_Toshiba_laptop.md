### Post by Urnso on 2009-10-06
Try typing "alsamixer" at terminal and make sure your hardware is not muted there. I have heard of that happening.

Wireless: go to System>Administration>Hardware drivers. See if it has anything listed in there. You should be able to activate. If you have a standard ethernet cable hookup on there try hooking that up and see if you can connect to get your updates that way. You may need to be connected to get the correct drivers from Ubuntu.

Good luck!
