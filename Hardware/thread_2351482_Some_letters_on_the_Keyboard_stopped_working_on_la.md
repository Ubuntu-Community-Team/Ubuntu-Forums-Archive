### Post by geeksmith on 2017-02-03
Does the keyboard still work on another computer? Switching a functional keyboard between computers should have absolutely no effect on either the computer or the keyboard. That's why they made them detachable :D

If it doesn't work properly on another computer, then you have your answer -- time to buy a new keyboard.

This happened to me on a Dell laptop. You can find a new keyboard on ebay or through Dell and replace the keyboard yourself, or have a Dell technician do it all for you ($$$$$$, unless you're covered by warranty).

In the meantime, you'll have to continue using your wireless keyboard or buy another keyboard that plugs in.

### Post by Bucky Ball on 2017-02-04
As above. Try another keyboard on the machine or try the new keyboard on another machine. If a different keyboard works with your laptop, then you've bought a dud keyboard. Take it to the shop with receipt and swap.
