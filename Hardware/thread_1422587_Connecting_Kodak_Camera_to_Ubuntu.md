### Post by gordintoronto on 2010-03-05
Did you follow this procedure: connect the cable to the computer, connect the cable to the camera, turn on the camera?

If you cancel the pop-up which offers Fspot, does the camera appear on your desktop as if it were an external hard drive?  If so, you can open it and use Nautilus to copy the pictures.

Alternatively, you might need to remove the memory card from the camera and put it in a card reader attached to your computer.  Card readers cost around $10. Don't delete anything or format the card from your computer.
