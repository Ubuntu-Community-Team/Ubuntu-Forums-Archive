### Post by sudodus on 2021-11-26
If you would be satisfied with a refurbished (second-hand) laptop, Dell Precision M4800 is an alternative with 4 USB ports. One of them is an eSATA/USB 2.0 port.

I checked some new Precision models that have two USB type A ports and two USB C ports. You can check models 'between' those to check when they stopped providing four type A ports, and maybe find a model that fits what you need.
