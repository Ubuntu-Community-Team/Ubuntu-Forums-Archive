Hi,  I have the same Laptop and had the same problem, i also used the plug/unplug trick for a little while.  However i found the 'Brightness Aplet' useful.  If you right click on your panel, then 'Add to Panel...' you should find the brightness aplet there.  It doesn't sort the problem but you can then manually change the brightness.
