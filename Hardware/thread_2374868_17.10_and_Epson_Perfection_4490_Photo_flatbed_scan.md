### Post by faberman on 2017-10-26
I forced an iscan install and everything seems to work:

dpkg --ignore-depends=libsane -i iscan_2.30.2-2_amd64.deb
dpkg -i data/iscan-data_1.36.0-1_all.deb  plugins/esci-interpreter-gt-s80_0.2.1-1_amd64.deb plugins/iscan-plugin-esdip_1.0.0-5.ltdl7_amd64.deb

The installed plugin libraries are now at a wrong location, fix it:

cd /usr/lib
mv sane/* /usr/lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/sane/
rm -rf sane/
ln -sf /usr/lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/sane sane


Et voila.
