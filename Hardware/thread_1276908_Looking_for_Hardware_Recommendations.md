### Post by skymera on 2009-09-27
Hello :)

Everything in my PC works fine with Ubuntu 64bit
- ASUS P5N-E SLI
- 4GB XMS2 800MHz
- GTX260 216 55nm
- Q6600

As for my personal suggestions, i highly recommend choosing Nvidia over ATi. I had a HD4870, the drivers were shockingly terrible, playing video used 100% CPU and Compiz jerked around. 

1GB RAM will be enough, my laptop has 768MB and runs fine. Though RAM is cheap, so stick in 2GB or so'.

Wireless? I use the Belkin F5D7000 PCI card, it runs OOTB and has done ever since i've used Ubuntu. My laptop has the Belking F5D7050 USB which also works OOTB.
