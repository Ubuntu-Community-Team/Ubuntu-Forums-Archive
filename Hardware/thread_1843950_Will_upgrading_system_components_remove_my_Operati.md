### Post by diasf on 2011-09-14

However, since the installation wasn't made for that hardware, I would do a fresh install.

### Post by diasf on 2011-09-14
Theoretically, yes. In practice, you might get lucky enough to cross over something your .confs are not ready to do. Like switching an ATI vga for an nvidia one. It will probably work, but on the best case scenario you end up with a ton of orphan software and files.
