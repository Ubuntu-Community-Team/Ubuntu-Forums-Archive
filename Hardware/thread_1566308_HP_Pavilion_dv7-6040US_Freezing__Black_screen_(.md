### Post by Shadow Death on 2010-09-25
Allow me to congratulate you for buying a very nice laptop.  I've spent the last week reading reviews and checking and compairing the specs to other laptops.

All of your specs look accurate except for one.  Let me be the first to inform you that your laptop has 2 video card setups.  The one that you know about is the ATI Mobility Radeon 5650.  This laptop has a less powerfull intergraded card called the ATI Mobility Radeon 4250.  By default the laptop will run the 4250 for power saving modes then switch to the 5650 for gaming.  I believe that Linux I'd getting confused on which card to use.  It's probably switching to the 4250.

This feature is actually designed to run on windows 7.  More than likely the Linux drivers do not support the ability to switch cards.  It seems that it's trying though.
