Ball-bearing fans that run for two minutes after you power down.

> 5) Is the Enermax Noisetaker 370W a good PSU? Is 370W good enough?

As long as you don't have a superfast processor or PCI-Express video card, it's "iffy." It definitely won't do for a modern computer.

### Post by Mr A Mouse on 2008-05-14
If you have a mid-range system, it seems to be a good one, based solely on the specs. Problem is, I don't have any direct experience with the brand: I usually either recycle my power supplies from computers at work that are being discarded, or buy the really cheap power supplies from Wal-Mart or Best Buy (in the US--if you're somewhere else, these stores aren't available). Your graphics card is AGP (as opposed to PCI-Express), so the power requirements aren't as high. it should do the job, provided it lives up to the specs.
