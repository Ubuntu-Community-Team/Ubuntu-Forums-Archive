### Post by jmstern on 2010-03-22
I had built a white box machine - biostar MoBoard- and Version 8.04 was acting wonderderful... Java, comms, sharing with windows.. all good. Except perodically I would get a system shutdown. 

I began by saying.. I will check later, knowing that finding answers for a sticky problem can be a chore.

Finally did get around to looking. Here is the error from the logs. 

Mar 17 22:15:02 Ubuntu-Linux kernel: [ 8251.800147] ACPI: Unable to turn cooling device [f7c48fd8] 'off'

I had been concerned the system was unable to turn fans on, and i would end up with a dead motherboard / proc.. but when i saw 'unable to turn off' i thought *well, that's a plus*


Ran a software update - and Tada! problem has gone away.
