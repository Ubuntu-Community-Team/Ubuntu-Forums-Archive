### Post by iliadav on 2008-08-27
OK,

This problem is not Linux specific. It's a hardware problem. The dv2000 series of HP have an engineering issue which leads to motherboard overheat and damages it. People with Vista reporting exact same issue. The cure is to unplug  the cable, take out battery and let it cool down for couple of hours. Than the laptop boots just fine. HP has a bug confirmation on their support site.

Whoever considers buying an HP laptop should probably change the decision, since it seems their laptops are overwhelmingly getting getting complains from users. The technical support considered extremely poor.
