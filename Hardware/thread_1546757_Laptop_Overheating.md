Hi All,

Recently installed Lucid on an HP 2730p... everything other than some screen functionality and the special buttons worked out of the box as expected.

However, I am noticing the laptop runs so hot... the wifi card often ceases to function and I have to shut it down to cool off for a while before it will connect to a router again.  I imagine part of this could be blamed on poor design... all the heat producing components are all located on the same side... cpu, wifi, battery, hdd... and when docked the ultra slim burner resides under the same side and produces a good deal of heat as well.  This said... I did not observe this behavior in previous releases.  

A few searches have revealed no documentation on the subject to speak of... and I have verified the behavior by downgrading to Karmic, abusing the cpu and physically capturing exhaust heat, then upgrading to Lucid again and doing the same.  (Naturally you can tell I havent figured out yet how to capture temperature from my Core 2 DUO... using lm-sensors yet, just doesnt seem to work.)

Anyone have some advice, suggestions, similar experience?
