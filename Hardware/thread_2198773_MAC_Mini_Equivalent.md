### Post by houstonbofh on 2014-01-11
You best bet is going to be a build your own, or custom white box.  You will need a mini-itx, or a NUC.  I do not know any mini-itx systems with Firewire, so you will need a firewire card.  And NUC is the same thing, but some have thunderbolt, and there are thunderbolt port replicators.  For example;
[http://www.logicsupply.com/computers/processor/intel-core/dc3217by-s/](http://www.logicsupply.com/computers/processor/intel-core/dc3217by-s/)
