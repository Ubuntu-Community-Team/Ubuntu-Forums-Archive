Hi.

I recently installed xubuntu (8.10 amd64) on my laptop a  Zepto Znote 6024W, I also use an external Samsung Syncmaster 2253BW (22") when I am at home. Now the first time I installed it (xubuntu) it had no problem at all scaling up the resolution to 1680x1050 on my external display (connected through a regular VGA port) with help of the xrandr tool (actually grandr but hey). I reinstalled later the same evening since I botched something and now my monitor only goes up to 1440x900. If I try to change it my external monitor "freaks out" shows large parts of the screen black and parts of it also flickers (but not the whole screen). The only way to fix the problem is to restart X11 with CTRL-ALT-Backspace. The chipset I have is an Intel 965GM. 

I also tried my work laptop which runs windows to display the full resolution and it had no problem at all running 1680x1050 so the monitor isn't damaged.

If any more information is needed just tell me!
