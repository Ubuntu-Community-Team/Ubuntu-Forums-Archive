[Bad news](http://www.ollisalonen.com/btnx/)

Maybe this should be posted in the pre-release section of ubuntu forums, but I feel this is a very important issue that could do with attention. The Logitech series of mice are very popular, and having this fully functional and easy to configure support in linux is a huge plus, I have even recommended this series of mice to friends and family who are interested in linux.

I just find it very hard to accept that something as useful as btnx could just dissapear.
