### Post by configt on 2009-08-07
I found the NVIDIA 8400GS to be a real pile of junk.  The drivers installed without issue, which was nice, and compiz worked, dual monitor worked, but even with all the eye candy disabled, full screen (one monitor) video play was HORRIBLE.  It would freeze up and be extremely choppy.  Also doing things like simple slide show was very chunky.  I would stay away from this card unless you just do text based work, but there are cheaper cards for that.
