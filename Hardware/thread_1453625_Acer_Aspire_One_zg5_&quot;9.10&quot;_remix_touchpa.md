I can't see any update that should/could affect the touchpad. It's more likely hardware in some way or other.  I believe that on a T61 Fn F8 is the touchpad toggle - try that. You can also disable the touchpad in the BIOS (when the Thinkpad logo is showing press the blue Thinkvantage key at the top left of the keyboard to go into the BIOS set-up).
