### Post by Autodave on 2024-05-22
Yes....but be careful!!!!!!!!!!!!  Remove the cartridge and turn it upside down.  Wet a paper towel with warm water and squeeze excess water out.  Carefully, place the paper towel on the cartridge and lightly press on it several times.  Now, turn the cartridge right side up and gently place the wet paper towel on the the bottom of the cartridge.  Only do this for a second.  Remove the paper towel and observe what, if any, ink is on it.  Have all 3 colors nice and bright?  Good!  Put back in printer and try printing test page.  If color on paper towel is light, or if 1 or 2 colors are way lighter than the others, repeat the process one more time.  If it doesn't work after the second attempt, you will probably not save that cartridge.
