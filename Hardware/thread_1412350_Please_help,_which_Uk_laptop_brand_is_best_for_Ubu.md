### Post by coffeecat on 2010-02-21
Well - if you want a laptop in the UK that's known to be Ubuntu compatible, you can't do better than go to a specialist supplier in the UK. :) Have a look here:

[http://www.linuxemporium.co.uk/products/laptops/](http://www.linuxemporium.co.uk/products/laptops/)

As far as the Huawei modem is concerned, it'll probably "just work" with Ubuntu 9.10. My Vodafone Huawei dongle (different model) certainly does, and a few weeks ago, I tried a relative's T-Mobile device. I think it was the E160, but I can't be 100% sure, but it worked just fine. I simply plugged it in, unmounted the pseudo-CD that was mounted to the desktop (that may or may not be necessary), clicked on the mobile connection that auto-appeared in Network Manager, completed the simple wizard, and that was that.

Good luck!
