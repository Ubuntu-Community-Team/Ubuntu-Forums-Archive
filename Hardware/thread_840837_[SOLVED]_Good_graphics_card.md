### Post by paulisdead on 2008-06-25
Really anything from nvidia with 2 outputs on it should be great.  Just install the nvidia restricted drivers, then after a reboot install nvidia-settings package to configure your second display.

The other question is, are you planning to play games on it?  If not, you could get by just great on a $50 nvidia card.

If you're looking at gaming, you can get 9600 series cards that fit your budget.  If you're willing to go up to $200, you can find 9800GTXs, which would definitely be worth the cost difference.
