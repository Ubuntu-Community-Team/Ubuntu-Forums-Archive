### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2011-03-27
Puurge all of the nvidia proprietary stuff. Recent versions of the nvidia driver don't  support your card and older ones probably don't have correct kernel/Xserver support. Just use the open-source nouveau driver that comes by default. It should give you full resolution and enough acceleration to run Compiz. Post /var/log/Xorg.0.log if it doesn't
