### Post by aikishugyo on 2016-11-02
I maintain the Canon driver in gutenprint, and as it is I've only just noticed that this printer exists. Canon produces a myriad of models for South and South East Asia, which do not seem to exist in the Japanese market. I usually do checks in March and October for new models in Japan, the US and Europe, and try to check other geographic locations too, but time is very limited.
I've seen that there are "G" series, more MAXIFY series, and the new "T" series printers, as well as the "E" series models at this time.
So while it is still early days, I expect that I will have time to add support in the next 2-3 months.
