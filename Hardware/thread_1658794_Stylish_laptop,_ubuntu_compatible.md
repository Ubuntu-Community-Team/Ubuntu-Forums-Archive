### Post by luddis on 2011-01-04
Hi there. I'm using my envy 14 with Ubuntu 10.10.

My biggest problem with this model has been the touchpad, but after following the advices [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10235323") its working rather well, though it's not perfect - I cannot press the touchpad with one finger and move for example a window with another finger (i.e. as you can do with a classic touchpad with buttons).

As for the screen brightness I have had no problem, it's working well.

I haven't digged much into the graphic details, but my envy 14 doesn't come with switchable graphics as far as I know. And the integrated ATI-graphics is working well I think with the catalyst drivers.

So if you like the model I think the envy 14 works rather well with ubuntu. Personally I like the feel of it, but think it is an inch or so too big. They should have decreased the space around the screen and thus make it a bit smaller. 

Anyway good luck
