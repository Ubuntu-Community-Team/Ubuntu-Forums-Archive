### Post by KatBuntu on 2008-08-04
You have that issue because you installed a different version of the flash player, i don't remeber the name, but with that player you can even download the flash content you're viewing. 
Solution: just uninstall the flash player plug-in from your browser, and install a "normal" flash player like adobe. There's also a plugin from mozilla for firefox in which you can switch the version of flash you want to use.
