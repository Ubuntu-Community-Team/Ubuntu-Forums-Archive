### Post by IcarusR on 2009-12-24
Right click on speaker icon on pannel > open volume control > preferences & make sure 'microphone' is checked.
Then make sure 'microphone' is not muted.

Can also install 'Gnome Alsa Mixer' or alsamixer from terminal. Again make sure microphone is listed & not muted.

What box is this & what is the audio chip ??
