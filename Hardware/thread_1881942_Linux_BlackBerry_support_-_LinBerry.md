### Post by An Sanct on 2011-11-16
I Thought I'd share this...

There is a new Linux based (currently *deb* and *rpm*) Desktop Manager. Its still in the Beta stage and the the homepage and forum is in Spanish (correct me if I am wrong!) but generally it works.

it promises to:
-make backups
-edit the contact list
-install COD files
-tether your BB
-the beta version has a roughly 97% english and german language support.

Now, I personally couldn't make a phone backup (BB 9000 Bold), but that should be because of the limitations it has (weak support for 64bit systems).

Thats it, you can check it out at [http://linberry.webcindario.com/index.html](http://linberry.webcindario.com/index.html)
('Descargar' takes you to the download section)

PS. I already offered my full (translation/coding) support to the project and still got no answer. Its a bummer, that it is not open source, if it where, I would at least do the translations.
