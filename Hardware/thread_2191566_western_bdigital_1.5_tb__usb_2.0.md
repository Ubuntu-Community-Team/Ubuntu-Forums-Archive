### Post by drmrgd on 2013-12-04
Are you asking how to create a partition for the unallocated space on the drive, or are you asking to format and repartition the whole drive?  You might be getting that 'write protected' message because the drive is mounted when you're trying to format it.  You can only do these kinds of tasks on an unmounted medium.  So, unmount the drive first, and then try to partition as you like.  

This is a pretty decent guide to how to partition larger drives:
[http://www.cyberciti.biz/tips/fdisk-unable-to-create-partition-greater-2tb.html](http://www.cyberciti.biz/tips/fdisk-unable-to-create-partition-greater-2tb.html)

You can change the specifics to meet your goals (i.e. format to ext4 instead of ext3 or whatever).

Hope that helps.  Post back if you're still stuck and / or with a little more information as to what you want to do if I'm misunderstanding your goals.
