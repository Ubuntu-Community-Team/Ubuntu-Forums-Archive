### Post by pandan on 2008-07-09
Hi everyone,

I've installed Xubuntu Hardy 8.04 and 8.04.1 on two HP vectra pentium 3 500Mhz machines that have Intel Motherboards.

It works well with 384MB RAM and I rekon is faster than Gutsy.

Even with all updates up to 1/7/08 on the 8.04 installation, the second IDE controller fails to see one of the two drives attached to it.

Looking around the forums, one user has suggested that this is because the motherboard is getting old and not fully supported anymore.  I tried once to switch the jumpers to cable select but this didn't work.

Also being a low end system, I cannot make things run faster by
changing the colour depth to 16bit.  DVD's played fine in Gutsy but not in Hardy anymore.  
And GDM is at too high a resolution that there are lines running down the screen, and I can't change this in Hardy.

It would be good to have this info handy around the download links/ about x/ubuntu pages....

Conclusion...  Back to Gutsy.
