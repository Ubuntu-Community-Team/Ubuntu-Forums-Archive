Hi,

I'm using fancontrol on my desktop and **it works**. Here's my config:

```
INTERVAL=10
FCTEMPS=hwmon0/device/pwm2=hwmon0/device/temp2_input
FCFANS= hwmon0/device/pwm2=hwmon0/device/fan2_input
MINTEMP=hwmon0/device/pwm2=54
MAXTEMP=hwmon0/device/pwm2=60
MINSTART=hwmon0/device/pwm2=90
MINSTOP=hwmon0/device/pwm2=120
```

But when my CPU reaches 54C and below, it turns of the fan completely. I'd like to set its minimal frequency to 1600RPM, because there's very little difference in noise between 1600RPM and 0RPM.

Now it sets 0RPM and after a while it has to work faster, about 1800-2100 RPM which is audible. 1600RPM is my optimum frequency.

Any ideas?
