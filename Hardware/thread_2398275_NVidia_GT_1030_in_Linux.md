### Post by Autodave on 2018-08-09
You should have no problems at all.  After you get the card installed, go to Additional Drivers and install the recommended one. That *should*be all that you need to do. IF you do have any issues, feel free to post here and some one will be sure to help.

I run Nvidia cards in 4 different machines and have no issues whatsoever.
