### Post by Dragyrn1456 on 2011-01-03
Problem solved! if you go to System -> Preferences -> Keyboard, there is a setting to control your mouse with the keypad. i thought it was really cool, until i realized it was the source of my keypad problems. i turned it off, and my keypad was working fine. 

:D :D :D  YAY :D :D :D
