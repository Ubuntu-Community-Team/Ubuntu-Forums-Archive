### Post by SnickerSnack on 2011-12-17
Thanks for the replies.  I didn't keep an eye on this thread after a while, so I didn't seem them bofore buying.

I ended up getting a Gigabyte Radeon HD 6850 OC with 1GB GDDR5 256-bit ram.  I'm very happy with it, and it's performed well under tests with phoronix.  The proprietary ati drivers installed in a few clicks via the 'additional drivers' menu option.

The best advice I can give to someone choosing a video card for linux is this: ati cards have excellent driver support, though I've read that nvidia cards have slightly better 3D support.  If you're primarily interested in 3D games through wine, nvidia is probably a little bit better.  Overall, both are perfectly good choices.
