I just got a Thinkpad X240 and upgraded to Ubuntu 15.10. A couple of problems have arisen with the touchpad.

First, on reboot the touchpad does not work at all (although the TrackPoint center button works fine). To get it to work, I have to do:

```
$ sudo modprobe -r psmouse
$ sudo modprobe psmouse proto=imps
```I have tried to get this to happen at boot automatically by creating /etc/modprobe.d/touchpad.conf with:

```
options psmouse proto=imps
```But this doesn't seem to work, and I still need to manually reload the kernel module. However, this way of fixing the problem may be wrong altogether, since even after running the commands, multitouch functions still don't work (although I can tap for click, I can't do anything like e.g. two-finger scrolling).

Any suggestions you have are more than welcome.
