### Post by Smeags on 2009-03-10
I believe a notebook and laptop are the same thing.  A netbook is basically an "ultra portable" laptop or notebook that use less powerful hardware and are mainly used for Internet browsing purposes on the go.  You can also do other things on netbooks, obviously, but their hardware specs prevent the use of most heavy-duty, memory-hungry or graphic intensive applications.
