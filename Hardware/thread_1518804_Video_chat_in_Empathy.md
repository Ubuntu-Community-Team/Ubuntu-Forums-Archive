### Post by YannBuntu on 2010-07-02
Sorry, no way to do voice/video calls with Yahoo protocol on GNU/Linux.

To do voice/video calls with Windows interlocutors, I recommand :
- Ekiga (which exists also on Windows)
- Empathy (with Telepathy PPA , and ubuntu-restricted package, and of course a Jabber account e.g. gmail adress), that can do voice/video calls with Windows Gtalk interlocuters
- Skype (which exists also on Windows), but this one is not open-source
