### Post by SecretCode on 2009-08-08
If your power source is stable, I don't see a problem. It would be "safer" than leaving the machine fully powered up for days or weeks.

If you have a power failure, standby will drain the battery (if you have one) if die immediately (if you don't) - it still draws power. And if you have a power spike (and no surge protection device), you have the same risk of damage as if it was powered on. 

The safest is to **hibernate **and unplug the power - then you can leave it for years!
