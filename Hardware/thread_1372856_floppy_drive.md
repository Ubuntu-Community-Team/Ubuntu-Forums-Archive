### Post by janyjany2010 on 2010-01-16
The floppy disk drive reads data from and writes data to a small disk. The most common type is the 3.5" drive followed by the completely obsolete 5.25" drive, among other sizes.
Short for floppy disk drive (FDD), a [disk drive]("http://www.webopedia.com/TERM/F/disk_drive.html") that can [read]("http://www.webopedia.com/TERM/F/read.html") and [write]("http://www.webopedia.com/TERM/F/write.html") to [floppy disks]("http://www.webopedia.com/TERM/F/floppy_disk.html").
