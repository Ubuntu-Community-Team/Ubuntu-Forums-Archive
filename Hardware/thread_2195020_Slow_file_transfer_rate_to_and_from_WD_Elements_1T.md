### Post by Bucky Ball on 2013-12-22
10-20Gb will take some time. What machine/model are you using and what are the specs (RAM particularly)?

FYI: 10.04 LTS is no longer supported and hasn't been since April. This means no updates, and most importantly, no security patches or updates. I advise that, once you have this issue sorted (which is a matter of waiting around until the files are transferred by the sounds of it) you upgrade to a supported release. 12.04 LTS will probably suit as it is supported until April 2017 and upgradeable to 14.04 LTS next April which, in turn, is supported for five years until 2019.

PS: You say you have 5.5Gb spare on the internal drive. Not sure how relevant that is as you are transferring the files to the external drive, right? How much room is on that is probably more important.
