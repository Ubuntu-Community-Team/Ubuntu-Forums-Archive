### Post by pi/roman on 2010-02-06
Try the configuration editor: alt/f2 | gconf-editor

look under / apps / gnome-power-manager /, there are good descriptions there for what each key does. Also look at the next one down / apps / gnome-screensaver / there are some keys there that can effect sleep mode.
