### Post by labinnsw on 2009-12-25
When disconnecting from Windows, do you click the "Safely remove USB Device" button and ensure you get the all clear message before disconnecting the stick? Failure to do this has been known to cause the problem you describe.

Are you able to copy the files? If so copy them to another disk, reformat the disk and restore the files.
