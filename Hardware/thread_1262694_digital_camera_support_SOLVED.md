### Post by Duckslammer on 2009-09-10
If you have a digital camera that is not recognized by ubuntu (my Nikon D60, for example), take heart, there is a fix.  

Install libgphoto2 from http:/www.gphoto.org and gtkam (ditto) or f-spot ([http://f-spot.org](http://f-spot.org)).

F-spot will not work without libgphoto2.

Then it will just magically recognize your camera and be able to download the images.
