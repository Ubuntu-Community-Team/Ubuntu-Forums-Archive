### Post by 2F4U on 2013-07-07
The open source ATI drivers by default do not have power management settings and the proprietary ATI drivers do no longer support your card. You can play around with the power management capabilities of the open source driver. The Arch Wiki has a nice article about it:

[https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Ati#Powersaving](https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Ati#Powersaving)
