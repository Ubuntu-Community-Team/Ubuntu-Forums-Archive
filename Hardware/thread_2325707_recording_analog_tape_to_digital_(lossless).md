### Post by Dennis N on 2016-05-24
It is pretty easy to do. You need a working tape player with line out connection. With L and R channels, you need an adapter to change the two RCA connectors over to one mini stereo cable. Then connect the mini stereo cable to the line in connection on your Linux computer. Use Audacity to record this audio input as a digital file in real time. You then edit this file as needed with Audacity, and finally export to a common digital format, such as mp3 or flac (lossless).
