### Post by rjackson1969 on 2010-04-30
Just got 10.04 on my HP 311 mini last night. Was working absolutely great all day today for the most part then out of nowhere tonight I was typing in a forum and my usually sensitive keyboard touch decided that I had to now physically jab each key hard to get any response. This is typing within my Firefox, terminal, unlocking my sleep mode or any other form field that the computer has EXCEPT... when booting up the computer and putting in my password.

Once booted, you have to jam the keys VERY hard to get any kind of a response.

I'm praying that this is a simple I.D.10.T error of some sort. Only on login does the keyboard act appropriately.

Any idea folks?

btw... this didn't come just after any update then happen. It was in the middle of typing. I'm hoping perhaps there is some sort of accidental keystroke combination that I did to set this off perhaps?

---

### Post by rjackson1969 on 2010-05-01
Found it out.

The "visual assistance" button that was added to my after the upgrade... when you click on it, it says "Universal Access Preferences". You select that and it gives you 5 checkboxes of preferences you can choose from. Some how I triggered to check the last box "Press and hold keys to accept them (Slow Keys)". I unchecked this damn thing and it seems to be working again fine.
