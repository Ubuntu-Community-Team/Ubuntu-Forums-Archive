### Post by Vansch76 on 2011-07-14
Hi Penny

I have not had any problems with any wireless mice in Linux
I would buy one that fits your hand well.  I have tried the minis
but they caused my had to cramp after a few hours of use.

I use a regular sized mouse with my Netbook.  its a microsoft moble
mouse 4000.  

I chose it because the wireless adapter only sticks out about a quarter 
of an inch.  the older style adapters that are around 2 inches long were
always getting bumped when i move my netbook around.

that was my deciding factor on buying my current mouse.
