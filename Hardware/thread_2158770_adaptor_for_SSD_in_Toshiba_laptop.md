### Post by ub9876 on 2013-06-30
I ended up using a 5mm plastic shim underneath the SSD and it worked ok. I measured a 6 mm space underneath so I thought 5mm would work.
It has a big hole in the middle for heat dissipate.The pins lined up exactly. If you need to do this make sure you use plastic and it has holes thru out it for heat.
