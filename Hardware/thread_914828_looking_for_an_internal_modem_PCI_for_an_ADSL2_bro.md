### Post by adam_kimber on 2008-09-09
I did some research a little while back as I wanted to build myself a firewall router and did not want a seperate modem, hence requiring an inbuilt one. There are very few PCI ADSL modems out there, many have incompatabilities with XP and Vista, and was wary of getting on incase there was not way of running under linux. I did not find any ADSL2+ modems but there might be some now. 

I got a Linksys external modem router in the end. The bethere forum has many articles on what is a good external router!
