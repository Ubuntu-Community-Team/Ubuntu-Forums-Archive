I am fairly certain this is the queued trim bug.  You should not attempt to trim this drive as it will probably result in data corruption.

Note that 850 Pro with older firmware rev EXM01B6Q does not have this problem due to missing NCQ Send/Receive log and will fall back on sync trim.
