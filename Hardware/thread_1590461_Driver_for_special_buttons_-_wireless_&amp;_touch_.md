### Post by IcarusR on 2010-10-08
In Linux, of which Ubuntu is a variety, the drivers (known as modules) are built into the kernel & loaded at boot.

Type xev in a terminal, then press said keys to see if ubuntu recognises them. Some special keys are not recognised & are difficult to get working.
