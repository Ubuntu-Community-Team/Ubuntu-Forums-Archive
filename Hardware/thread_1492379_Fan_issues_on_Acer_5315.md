### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2010-05-24
Sometimes, a BIOS upgrade will fix fan control issues. [http://support.acer-euro.com/drivers/notebook/as_5315.html](http://support.acer-euro.com/drivers/notebook/as_5315.html)

It looks like you can make a DOS boot CD/USB and use that to flash.
