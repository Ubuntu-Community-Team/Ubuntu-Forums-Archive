### Post by .nedberg on 2008-10-18
> **IsKall said:**
> The thing is when I unplugg the hdmi cable, the sound comes back... very weird right?

Not really. Your TV expects audio and video in the HDMI cable. When it does not get video, it takes audio from a separate input. It is the same with my TV, I can't use HDMI with a separate input for sound. So I use VGA/DVI for video and RCA for audio.

Have a look in your TV manual, I am sure it is mentioned somewhere...
