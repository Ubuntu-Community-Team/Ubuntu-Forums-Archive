### Post by ladr0n on 2008-09-20
Lexmark is horrible about Linux support (and about their Windows and Mac support, for that matter.  I warn everyone, regardless of their OS, not to purchase Lexmark products.)  In Linux specifically, there are some Lexmark printers that will work if you give them enough effort, and some that just plain won't.  
According to [Open Printing]("http://www.openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Lexmark-Z13_lexmark") the printer just won't work.  Then again, it it's free, you can always try.  Good luck.
