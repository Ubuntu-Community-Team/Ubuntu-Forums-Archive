### Post by ne4ve on 2011-09-06
I've also got the new Z-Series and ran into this problem. Probably a little late but may help others.

I fixed this by pressing e when on the grub boot screen (if grub isn't showing hold SHIFT). Replace 'quiet' with 'nomodeset'. Press CTRL+X to continue booting.

This is where I found the info: [http://thedaneshproject.com/posts/ubuntu-11-04-blank-screen-on-boot-solved/](http://thedaneshproject.com/posts/ubuntu-11-04-blank-screen-on-boot-solved/)
