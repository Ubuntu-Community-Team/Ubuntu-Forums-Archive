If you want a usable Ubuntu on the P11z stick with Jaunty because you can install a working Poulsbo driver (for the Intel US15 chipset) so you actually have some 2D/3D acceleration - not just Vesa.
That's not available in Karmic.

Also the wireless driver in Jaunty does work opposed to the one that comes with Karmic.
