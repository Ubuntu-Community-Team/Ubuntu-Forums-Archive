### Post by snowpine on 2010-03-27
> **ManInBlackXIII said:**
> I really like the interface, but it lags  sometimes and like it'll leave screen imprints of a window I just moved  and randomly freeze.

This sounds like a possible graphics driver problem. Try clicking System->Administration->Hardware and checking if there are any drivers available for your graphics card.

The terminal command 'lspci' (without the quotes) will tell you exactly what kind of graphics card you have (among other useful hardware info), if you're not sure. You can search these forums for information specific to your card.

1.6ghz is not that bad, I've installed Linux on far worse. :)
