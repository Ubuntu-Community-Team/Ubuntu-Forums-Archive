### Post by hybenz on 2012-07-09
will this trick work? got it from the net

1.)Go to system settings either on your status bar or search for it.  
2.)locate  hardware options (click it) then plug your  printer into your computer. this will automatically ask you if you want to download drivers for the  printer.
3.) Download the drivers then click on the printer tab (you should still be  in the hardware settings) the computer should now check for the  appropriate drivers and install your printer.  
4.) try to print a test page
