### Post by rrsis2 on 2023-05-06
Hi guys, I bought TP Link TX401  10Gbps.
There is no information about this product within the forum, I have search TX401 and I've got nothing. 

I've got a problem which I thought it was a driver problem. I spent many hours trying to figure it out what the problem was, and I would like to save you that time. 
The CARD is PCI express 3,  and I was installing it in a motherboard with two PCI express lanes, one of them was 3.0, and the other one was 2.0.  That was my mistake.
I've switched the card from 2.0 to 3.0 Lane and works smooth.   Didn't have to install drivers manually. Ubuntu 22 recognize it without manual drivers installation. 

I hope this will help someone in future!
