### Post by indianya on 2012-08-18
Sharing my experience.
My PC has integrated SIS video chip. That worked ok at lower resolution and non standard aspect ratios, however, I was never able to configure 1920 x 1080 (16:9) native resolution to match my HP monitor.
 
I then upgraded my machine with Nvidia geforce 210 chip but ubuntu didn't even boot properly with that. I  kept having even more issues with display.
 
Returned Nvidia and got a card with ATI HD5450 chip and I am very happy with ATI. I did not have to manually configure anything. Ubuntu now runs perfectly on native resolution and aspect ratio. Even windows vista works much faster now with add on ATI graphics chip. 
 
Based on my experience ATI runs best on Ubuntu 12.04.
