### Post by gsparky2004 on 2010-02-22
If you get it to work, please post the results of how on this site.  Not to be a naysayer, but I think you'll find it will be a battle.  The screens on laptops do not have the hardware to accept standard VGA signals.  And the VGA connectors they do have are outputs, not inputs.  The hardware to create the signals for the screens is part of the motherboard, so just pulling the screen off won't work.

Good luck!
