### Post by Toriam on 2009-06-15
I am *so* pleased with my new little laptop I think I've become an unofficial salesperson for the Acer Aspire One. I installed UNR soon after I received it, and surprise surprise it was faster than Windoze, the wifi worked way better, and that awful windoze itch stopped. It need a few tweaks to get the webcam and sound working properly, but it goes with the territory. All I need now is a pinch more RAM and it'll be perfect. One thing that still irks me though, when I try to play a video sometimes the windows start "shuffling" meaning they open and close on their own in rapid succession almost like shuffling a deck of cards. It happens for about 10 seconds then stops on its own. Is this a RAM issue, or software? It isn't a big deal, just an annoyance.
