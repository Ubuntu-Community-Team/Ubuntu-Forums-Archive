### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-06-05
Whilst I agree that Epson cartridges are usually cheap, compared to HP at any rate, I always found my Epson printer head was blocking, causing very poor print quality, and I spent so much time cleaning and unblocking print heads that I gave up on the machine.  That was a Stylus Photo 830U, so some while ago now, and things may have got better in that respect, but I replaced it with an HP Deskjet F2180 all in one, which is faultless, and so far in about three years of use has never blocked.

I think this is all down to the fact that Epson print heads are a permanent part of the hardware, whilst HP and many others are replaced each time with the cartridge.  This could mean that if you do not print very much, which is possibly my problem, Epson heads dry out more easily, and a cartridge change does not make any difference, the heads remaining as they were.
