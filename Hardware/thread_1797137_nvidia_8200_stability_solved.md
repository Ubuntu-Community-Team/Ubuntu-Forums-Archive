### Post by kadil on 2011-07-04
Maybe someone is interested. Since upgrading my Shuttle box to the latest version of Ubuntu it has been very unstable, and flashplayer was the common factor in every crash. Turned off the hw acceleration in /etc/adobe configuration file and it is back to rock solid.

With acceleration on it would intermittently
- log itself out of the X session,
- freeze the whole computer
- scramble the contents of chrome while on the gmail page
- play online video poorly

With acceleration off, 720HD movies on youtube can be problematic but all lower spec movies are fine, and vimeo HD movies are fine.
