### Post by SamsungService on 2011-12-19
Hi Ph1b,

Your machine may require a 'cycle power', this is a kind of reset on your printer. To do this please follow these steps:

Disconnect all the cables connected to the printer and leave it for 24-48 hours to reset memory and then plug it back in again. This should fix the error for you.

Please be advised that by doing this you may delete settings you have set up on the unit.

If this fails to work please contact us on 03307267864, selecting option 4 followed by option 2. This will take you to our Technical Team.
