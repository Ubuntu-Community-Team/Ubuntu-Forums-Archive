### Post by seren6ipity on 2008-12-01
You can view mounted devices and their mount points by adding item "Mount devices" to your panel. 
It can be configured to add specific mount points for particular devices.

Right click on panel -> Add items -> Mount devices 
Right click Mount devices to set property/ command tab and give specifc mount points here.


You can configure Xfce to automatically open the inserted USB driver (or any other storage) for you. Go to settings -> settings manager -> Removable Drives and Media -> Check "Browse Removable media when inserted"
