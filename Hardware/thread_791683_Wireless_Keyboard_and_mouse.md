### Post by PS5000 on 2008-06-23
I recommend the A4 Tech PowerSaver R7 Wireless keyboard/mouse combo.
This device works well in (K)ubuntu Gusty and Hardy.

The Keyboard model is RK-6.
The mouse model is R7-70MD.
The combo uses a single convenient USB stick as the radio antenna, and an extension wire is included in th package.

The keyboard and mouse are compact, but some users may need to get used to the keyboard's laptop-like keys.

The mouse has multiple buttons including a scroll wheel; all are recognized in (K)ubuntu.  I especially like the mouse's power saver feature which automatically shuts off the mouse after inactivity to save batteries.

The keyboard has additional buttons: Power, Back, Forward, Reload, Home, Find, and Mail.  The Back, Forward, and Reload buttons work out of the box in Firefox/Gusty.

I have two PCs on the same desk and I use an A4 Tech PowerSaver R7 Wireless keyboard/mouse combo with each PC.  The mice are within 2 feet of each other, and I do have to reset the channel once in a while due to conflicts/interference.  Fortunately these devices allow you to use multiple channels.
