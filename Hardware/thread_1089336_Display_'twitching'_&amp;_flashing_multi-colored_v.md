### Post by renesisspeed on 2009-03-07
Ok.
So this morning I started it up and my video card fan was making all sorts of noise and barely spinning. I opened up the case and tapped on the fan, It went up to full speed, stopped making noise and has been working fine since... no more goofy lines on the monitor. (Note: the fan was not making noise before) So it looks like I need a new fan on the VC. (Damn thing is only a little over a month old)
