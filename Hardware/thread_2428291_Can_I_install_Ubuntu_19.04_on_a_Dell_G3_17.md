### Post by deadflowr on 2019-10-02
Because support for 19.04 ends at the end of the year.
It only has 9 months support from the day of release.

Ubuntu releases two kinds of releases.
Long term support releases (LTS).
These get 5 years support.
And short term releases (interim releases).
these get only 9 months.
LTS releases are released in even number years in April.
Interim releases are released every 6 months in between those two years.
More here: [https://ubuntu.com/about/release-cycle]("https://ubuntu.com/about/release-cycle")
