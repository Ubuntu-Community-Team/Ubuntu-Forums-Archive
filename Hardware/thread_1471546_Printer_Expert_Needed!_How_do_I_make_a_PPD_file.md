### Post by Ted_Smith on 2010-05-03
Hi. 

Have just installed Ubuntu 10.04, 2.6.32-21-generic kernel. Trying to get my printer to work (that it used to do with earlier versions). It's a USB HP Deskjet 990cxi (about 9 years old). 

Have installed latest version of hplib from [http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/index.html) which supports my printer (according to [http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/models/deskjet/deskjet_990c.html#distros](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/models/deskjet/deskjet_990c.html#distros)). ### Post by Ted_Smith on 2010-05-03
Sods law. Despite spending the last 6 hours on this, I found the answer two minutes after posting the above!! 

Downloaded a PPD file from [http://www.openprinting.org/printer/HP/HP-DeskJet_990C](http://www.openprinting.org/printer/HP/HP-DeskJet_990C) and re-ran hp-setup. I tehn pointed it to the PPD file. I then had to restart cups  

/etc/init.d/cups restart

Then it worked! What a palava.
