as far as i know, the BIOS is never locked on default. Someone (i hope its you...) must have put a password on it. I dont think you can recover the BIOS password, i could be wrong though. The Ultimate way to get rid of it would be flashing your BIOS. This is very dangerous tho, because:

1. if you flash the wrong bios,

2. if the flash gets interrupted,

then you render your motherboard unusable. This should be your FINAL option if nothing else works.
