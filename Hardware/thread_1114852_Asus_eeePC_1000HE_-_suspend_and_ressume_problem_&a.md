Hiya

Anyone out there with a Asus eeePC 1000HE with ubuntu 8.10 and having problems with the backlight not coming on when returning from suspend mode?

The work around is to do the following:

1. wait until all the indicator lights are steady coming back from standby, then press ctrl-alt-f1 to switch to another tty
2. wait a few second and then press ctrl-alt-f7 to switch back to the xwindows session.

This seems to work, but would like to hear from anyone that has got fix.
