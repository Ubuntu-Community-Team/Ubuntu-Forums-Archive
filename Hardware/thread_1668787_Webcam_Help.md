### Post by jonbonjovi on 2011-01-16
Click on the audio applet (top right) and then on "audio preferences", the select the "audio-in" tab (I'm translating on-the-go from italian localisation, try to read that), once in there, check if the volume of incoming audio is unmuted (box unchecked is ok).

If this doesn't work, post a screenshot of:
> alsamixer press "tab" until you see "all" displayed.
