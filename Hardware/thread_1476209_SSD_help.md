### Post by paulisdead on 2010-05-07
If you've tested it in multiple machines than this probably isn't going to work.  Adapters have to be compatible with what you put in them.  For instance, I've got some corsair and sandisk microsd cards that have adapters to SD.  I can't plug a sandisk microsd into a corsair adapter, and vice versa, they just don't work.

Is there some real reason you're doing this?  Like for a tiny, or quiet computer, because performance is not going to be good running from something like that.  Even the really cheap SSDs made to replace hard drives use way faster memory than what you find in those little cards.
