### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-16
The driver CD will likely not help you, as those are nearly always Windows drivers.

To open a terminal, click the Ubuntu circular symbol on the top-left corner of the screen, and when Dash opens (search tool), enter Terminal, and then click the application icon that is shown.
