### Post by jbitkill on 2009-01-18
I have found the answer for Ubuntu-running ECS A950's out there. Go to [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=795525](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=795525) and set the channels to 4. Restart your Linux system, go to Vol. Control, go to "Sis7012 Mixer (ALSA)", go to Edit > Preferences or click Preferences. Tick the surround box. Unmute the surround and knock it up! Sound! To control it, go to "Playback: ALSA PCM on surround40" then adjust the Master!

Happy raving!
