### Post by kyphi on 2008-12-15
The lack of drivers for Canon printers is solely due to Canon refusing to share information on the driver source code.

In view of that, it would be easy to say: Do not buy Canon products.  That, however, does not help those who made their purchase before upgrading to Ubuntu.

Have a look at this URL: [http://www.turboprint.info/printers.html](http://www.turboprint.info/printers.html)

It costs money but the claim is that your printer is supported.

Note: You might consider writing to Canon about their poor support for users of Linux ... perhaps one day they will listen.
