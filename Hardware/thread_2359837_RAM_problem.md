No "swap" is not there because you only have one stick, it is probably there because on your system you didn't either make or enabled "swap"

Anyways, usually on older motherboards with on-board graphics, motherboard will utilize/share some of your RAM memory. And this is part of your hardware reserved memory. You can probably check it out in BIOS if it shows.
