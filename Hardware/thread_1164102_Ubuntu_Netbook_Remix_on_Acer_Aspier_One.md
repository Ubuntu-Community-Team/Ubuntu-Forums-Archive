### Post by zampes on 2009-05-19
Hey there!
In my experience, jerkiness with flv under firefox are due to your decoder more than your hardware, since I have an old desktop running 8.10 with 512mb of ram and everything runs smoothly. I had the same problem as you, and it was the free version flash video decoder that came with ubuntu. After I installed the version from ?Adobe? it worked perfectly, no problems. in a firefox tab type about:plugins to check what flv decoder you're using...

Now, all in all, I always had really bad experiences with Acers and signal quality, that's -one of the reasons why- why I use HP nowadays...
