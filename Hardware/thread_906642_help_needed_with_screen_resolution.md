### Post by IntuitiveNipple on 2008-08-31
To make things as easy for you as possible, before we start trying to solve things with Ubuntu here, I suggest you ask in tuxx's forums since he knows the Matrox issues inside-out and gives a great personal service out of kindness, too.

He'll likely provide you with a working xorg.conf right out of his head!

When you post your question include the results I've asked for above in the article, and attach the latest **/etc/X11/xorg.conf** and **/var/log/xorg.0.log** to the article. You might want to compress them and put them in a single archive file. Here's how:
```

cd ~
cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf .
cp /var/log/Xorg.0.log .
tar -czf MatroxG200-4.4.4-x64.tar.gz xorg.conf Xorg.0.log

```
Attach the archive file **MatroxG200-4.4.4-x64.tar.gz** to the article at tuxx.
