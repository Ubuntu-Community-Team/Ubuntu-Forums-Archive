### Post by ugriffin on 2009-05-05
Hey. Jaunty has this lovely bug that slows down the video card of your Intel chipset. I fixed it on several computers I mantain by regressing the driver to the version in intrepid:

[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/904](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/releasenotes/904)

Your issue is somewhere in there, along with a bunch of fixes...

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ReinhardTartler/X/RevertingIntelDriverTo2.4](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ReinhardTartler/X/RevertingIntelDriverTo2.4)


For instructions of regressing the driver. Read this carefully.
