### Post by lumpymayo on 2008-05-07
I know a lot of people are having problems with getting their screen resolution higher than 800x600 after installing their video cards. I saw this somewhere else and tried it and it worked perfectly for me. Heres what I did:

1)Make sure your video card is installed and enabled in Restricted Drivers (may require reboot)

2)Open a terminal and enter ```
sudo init 1
```


After I did this everything worked fine and I was able to select my desired screen resolution from System>Preferences>Screen Resolution


Hope this helps, and let me know if anyone else has tried this and had success
