### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2011-04-30
> **Linux_junkie said:**
> Now I know why the computer shuts down when I'm using the optical disk because theres too much power being pumped in to the computer.
This doesn't make sense. You could theoretically use a 1200W PSU, and it would still only deliver the needed power. Using a bigger PSU than needed is okay (but becomes inefficient after a certain point). Anyway.. adding an optical drive shouldn't prevent the system from functioning unless:
- there's a hardware problem with the drive or PSU (like a short)
- your PSU is already struggling to feed your system (not likely unless you have dual high-power GPU's).
So my suggestion is to try the drive in another system and see what the deal is (or try another drive in this system).
