### Post by Chris4 on 2010-07-04
Hey guys. I have the X1950 Pro too and experiencing same problem. 

The [answer]("http://forums.ebuyer.com/showthread.php?t=53899") to your question is that unfortunately the X1950 Pro does not have fan control capabilities, no matter which application you use on any operating system. 

For a graphics card to have fan speed control it requires  "3-pin" wires - the X1950 Pro only has 2. :( 

It's frustrating because it's so noisy - when I stop the fan with my finger, the PC is silent, so its just that which is making all the noise. 

You may be able to buy a new fan/heatsink for it with fan speed control capabilities, but it's probably better to just buy a new (quieter!) 3-pin graphics card.

Just a lesson to learn for next time I'm afraid.
