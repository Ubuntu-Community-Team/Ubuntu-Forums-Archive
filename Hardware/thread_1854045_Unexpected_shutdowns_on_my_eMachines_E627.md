### Post by tgalati4 on 2011-10-04
Look for a program called "Log File Viewer" under System Admin.  Open up various log files and take note of any interesting messages related to kernel panic or error that would lead to shut down.  Post them in this thread.  Keep track of the time when the laptop shuts down and look in the log files just before that time and see if you can relate any messages to shutdown.
