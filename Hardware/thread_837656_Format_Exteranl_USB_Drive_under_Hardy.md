### Post by ahbart on 2008-06-23
I can't help you with the command line command. But I personally like to use the gnome (gparted) or qt (qtparted) partition manager for this. Delete the partition and reformat. Be careful that you select the right disk. 
To select go in gparted to the right upper corner. /dev/sdb or /dev/sdc depending of the harddrives in your system.
