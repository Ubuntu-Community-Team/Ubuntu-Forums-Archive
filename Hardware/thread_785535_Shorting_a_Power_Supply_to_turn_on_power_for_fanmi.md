Just thought I would share this with anyone who needs to use a separate power supply for testing. Note that this is a dirty hack and is not recommended for people who want to minimize risks. Please do not hold me or ubuntuforums responsible if this does not work.

I had to power some fans, yet I no longer had a free cable from the power supply. I got another power supply just to realize that the fan on the back did not turn on when plugged in, I also needed to connect some fans to the power supply, these were not working either.

The fix/tip: On the motherboard connector, which looks like this in my case:

[============] 

I used a slightly bent staple to create a link between the green cable (power) and one of the black cables (ground). This worked perfectly and power started coming out of the power supply, powering my fans.

Hope this helps someone out there.
