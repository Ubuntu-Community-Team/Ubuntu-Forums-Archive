### Post by whitebridge on 2010-12-28
Attention Dell X300 owners,

My ageing Dell X300 from 2004 accepted an out-of-the-box install of Ubuntu 10.10, after buggy/incomplete installs with earlier versions. In particular Ubuntu 10.10 successfully installed the Broadcom B43 WIFI driver, the I855 graphics, reading and writing SDHC flash cards, two-finger scrolling of the Synaptics touchpad.

Possibly this happy news has been reported earlier? Anyhow, my X300 with its 640 MB RAM, 60 GB HD, is now ready for a new life as a snappy cloud computer.
