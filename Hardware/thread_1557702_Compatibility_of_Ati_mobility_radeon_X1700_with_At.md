### Post by cascade9 on 2010-08-21
> UNSUPPORTED HARDWARE! 
Support of many Mobility Radeon (like X700) was dropped by AMD after the 9.4 release: see release notes link on [http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/linux/9-4/Pages/radeon_linux.aspx](http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/linux/9-4/Pages/radeon_linux.aspx). The drivers are supposed to be 'available on amd.com' but it's not true for Linux. See [http://support.amd.com/us/kbarticles/Pages/737-28041SupportforATIMobility.aspx](http://support.amd.com/us/kbarticles/Pages/737-28041SupportforATIMobility.aspx). No proprietary driver is available (no 3D...): you're stuck with the free / open source drivers (like radeon). 


[http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Hardware](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Hardware)

I think that makes it pretty clear that there is no current support for the X1700, even though that particular card isnt mentioned on that page.

Following the support.amd.com link to ATIs website, then checking he release notes for the 9.4 drivers will give you a .pdf that shows all the cards supported by that driver (which is pretty old now itself). he 'X' series cards are way out of support.....
