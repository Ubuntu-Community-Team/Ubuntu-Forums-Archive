### Post by mtbsoft on 2009-03-13
First thing you need to do is identify what activity shutting the lid is trying to cause - I would presume either Hibernate or Suspend.

Go to System | Preferences | Power Management

...and on the first tab you will see a drop down list labelled *When laptop lid is closed* - this weill tell you what the laptop is trying to do when you close the lid.  Next I would suggest you try manually performing the same action from the logout menu so that you can watch the screen to see what activity occurs.

Report back with that info. and we can go from there...
