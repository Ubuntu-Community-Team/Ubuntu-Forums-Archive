### Post by justin_c18 on 2009-02-12
I fixed it. I haven't restarted X to see if the fix will be like that. But, I just went into system > preferences > keyboard and re-enabled key repeat. It **was** already checked off, but I unchecked, and re-checked. Now it works. 

And here I thought I was doomed. :)
