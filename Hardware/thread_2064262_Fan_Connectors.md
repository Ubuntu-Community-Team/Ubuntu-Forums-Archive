### Post by deadflowr on 2012-09-28
I'd google molex fan connector.
It'll allow you to connect the fan directly to the PSU. 
The problems when running it like this is the fan is constant and runs at a steady speed, forever. 
When the fan is connected through the motherboard, sensors in the board let the fan know when it needs to speed up or down.
What really might be of importance is whether you want it to be an intake or outake fan. I have a fan set like this, and I personally use the psu-connected fan to constantly draw fresh air into the case. You might have your own ideas.
