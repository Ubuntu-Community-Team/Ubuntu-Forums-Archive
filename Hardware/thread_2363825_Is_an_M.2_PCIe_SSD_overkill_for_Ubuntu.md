### Post by Bucky Ball on 2017-06-15
A PCIe M.2 is super fast and will leave a regular SSD in the weeds. If that is what you're looking for, then it is not overkill. I would put both OSs on the M.2. General rule of thumb is to put the OSs on the fastest drive, whatever that is. In your case, the M.2.

Good luck.
