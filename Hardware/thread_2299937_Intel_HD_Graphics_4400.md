### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-10-22
Intel integrated graphics drivers are installed with the system and there is nothing more for you to do.  What you need for the AMD discrete card will depend on which card it is; have you looked in Additional Drivers where AMD's fglrx driver is to be found for most cards?
