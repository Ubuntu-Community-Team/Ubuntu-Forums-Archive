I have a 15.4 inch laptop, and today I got an extra 17 inch LCD monitor to link to the laptop for a dual-screen setup. [For schoolwork, etc.] Setting it up has been relatively painless, except for one thing. 

Since my laptop's native resolution is 1280x800, and the 17 inch screen's native res is 1280x1024, I'm only using it as extra space. However, I can't seem to get the second screen to it's native res...it's stuck at 1024x768. It's working, so I can't really complain...but it would be nice to have a little more space. 

I can't open the display settings manager with the screen plugged in and compiz enabled...it nearly freezes the computer. Also, Catalyst CC is reporting the second display's maximum res as 1024x768. Any help would be great. Thanks.

Never mind...I got it working. :p If anyone else has the same problem, try unplugging the second monitor, opening the the display settings, and plugging it in again. I was able to adjust the resolutions correctly, and after logging out and in again everything is good. ;)
