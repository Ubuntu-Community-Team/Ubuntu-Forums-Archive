### Post by MonkeyPaw on 2008-12-24
I use ubuntu-eee on my 701 with great results. Wireless is very stable, and it's fairly snappy. You might also have a look at eeebuntu, which was just recently updated to  8.10 and comes in 3 flavors. eeebuntu also has custom scripts for each model of Eee, which typically fixes model-specific issues. No matter which build you decide, all of them have "live CD" functionality, so you can try 'em before you install 'em. 

Also, ubuntu-eee's website has a nice utility for making these ISO's bootable on a USB stick, so be sure to download that. It's called uNetboot-something.
