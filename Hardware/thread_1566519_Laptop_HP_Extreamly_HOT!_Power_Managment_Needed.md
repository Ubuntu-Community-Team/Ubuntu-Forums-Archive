An HP L2005 Turion 64 Laptop with Radon M400 Video is driven totally beyond it's power envelope by Ubuntu 10.04 Gnome.  ACPI installed and on APM installed and on.

The unit now melts off power supply plugs or leaves them burnt.  It is very HOT!

When run with openSuSE 11.1 KDE 3.5 the unit has perfectly acceptable thermal performance and XP has perfectly acceptable thermal performance.  In openSuSE Kpowersave showed the core quite nicely cycling down to 800mhz.

It seems to me there is a laptop package missing.  OpenSuSE had Kpowersave ACPI APM and Laptop tools installed.
