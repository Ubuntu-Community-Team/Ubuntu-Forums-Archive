### Post by Giblet5 on 2009-10-14
I replaced the one in my laptop with the [Intel 4965AGN]("http://www.thinkwiki.org/wiki/Intel_PRO/Wireless_4965AGN_Mini-PCI_Express_Adapter") which I got from TigerDirect for $24.95. It does everything, very very well and even provides semi-useful signal-strength data.

The 4965AGN is supported on pretty much every OS. It has been superseded on most laptops by the i8XXX series, but those are just cheap knock-offs of the 4965AGN card. That's why I replaced mine.
