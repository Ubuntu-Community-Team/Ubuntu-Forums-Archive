### Post by marti972 on 2008-11-30
Is the drive IDE (PATA) or SATA? If it's an IDE you'll need a cheap adapter to hook it up in a desktop. It think they're about $7 delivered. The pins on a 2.5 inch IDE drive just don't fit the IDE cable in a desktop. Once you get a adapter you just install the hard drive into your desktop and do what you want with the files.

If it's a SATA hard drive it may be easy but I've never seen one so not sure. Surely it just hooks up OR you have to get a cheap adapter.

The place I found my adapter was from [http://www.pricewatch.com/hard_removable_drives/adapter.htm]("http://www.pricewatch.com")
