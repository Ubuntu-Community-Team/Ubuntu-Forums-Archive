### Post by irv on 2011-01-31
Things to check:
1. Power Supply.
2. Fan in Power Supply.
3. Fan on CPU. (This will over heat and tune off the computer).
4. Fan(s) in the computer case.
5. Make sure the fan on the CPU didn't become detached.
