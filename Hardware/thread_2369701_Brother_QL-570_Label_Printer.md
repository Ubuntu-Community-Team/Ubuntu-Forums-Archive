### Post by matheist76 on 2017-08-26
I previously had this printer working under a old version of Ubuntu. While it had issues, settings and general no cooperation, it did work. So I plugged it. It almost installed itself. Thought great. We to do a test print and LED turned from green to red. It had been a long time since last used so I thought it may just be the printer but after plugging into my old laptop it worked. So I played with the settings and got it working.

Printer Properties--> Printer Options: set Advance distance to something other than none. Turn off Label Preamble then Apply. Turn it back on then Apply. Should work.
