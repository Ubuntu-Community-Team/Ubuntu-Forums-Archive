### Post by Alberto Leal on 2010-04-25
I've just found the bizarre solution for it:

[INDENT]2.1 If you are in Windows unplug the AC cable and plug it back after you logged into Ubuntu.
2.2 If you are already on Linux after a Windows session and you didn't follow point 1.1estat, Unplug the AC cable, then SHUTDOWN , not restart the computer. Start it again manually and plug the cable back after you logged into Ubuntu [/INDENT]
