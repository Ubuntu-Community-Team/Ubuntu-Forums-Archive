### Post by Whiffle on 2008-09-18
Your battery voltage is a measure of how fully charged the battery is.  In fact, the battery gauge reads the battery voltage to calculate how full a battery is, so some fluctuation is normal.

Yeah it seem like maybe it was left on or left in sleep mode and discharged.

Not sure what to tell you about your screen brightness.

If you'd like to read up more on laptop batterys, check out the articles about lithium ion batteries here: [http://www.batteryuniversity.com/](http://www.batteryuniversity.com/) .  They have lots of great information that will help you know how to keep your battery in good shape and last a long time.  These batteries do not behave like rechargeable batteries of old and definitly need different care to work right.
