### Post by halverson on 2011-08-03
I have 10.04 Lucid Lynx and Windows 7 on a Dell T5500 workstation with two NVIDIA Quadro 600 graphics cards and two Dell LCDs. In Windows I am able to make use of both monitors, however under Ubuntu the second monitor is not recognized. Only one monitor works and the "twinview" option is viewable but not selectable. I tried manually editing xorg.conf without success. Also, I have the latest driver (NVIDIA-Linux-x86_64-280.13.run).

I am now aware of a post which says that the NVIDIA Quadro 600 chipset is not supported in Ubuntu 10.04 LTS:
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/810426](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/810426)

Does anyone know if going to 11.4 will make a difference. Any other solutions?
