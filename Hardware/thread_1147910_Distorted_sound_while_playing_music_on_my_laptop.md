### Post by Dark_Stang on 2009-05-03
Actually, since I work on Windows and Mac machines all day long at work, this doesn't affect me too much. It can very well be related to you reflashing the motherboard. Or it could be background processes sucking down your CPU power or accessing the hard drive. 

How many background processes are running? Check in task manager. A nicely running vista machine should have 60 or less (70 if you're on a toshiba). My Vista desktop has... 57 running. It would be less than that if I didn't have the tablet drivers running on it (but it IS a graphic design machine).

I've also seen this issue caused by...
Sound card drivers
Failing motherboards
Failing hard drives
Failing power supplies (and underpowered systems)
And bad dialup modems (go figure)
