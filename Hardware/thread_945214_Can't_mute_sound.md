### Post by UniversalCitizen on 2008-10-27
Hey, I found the answer to my mute button problem and thought I would pass it on to you.

There were two steps.

First I had to make sure my mute button was mapped correctly.  I did this in System / Preferences / Keyboard Shortcuts.  Clicked on Volume Mute row and then hit the Mute button.

Then I had to change what sounds the button controlled.  I went to System / Preferences / Sound.  Under Default Mixer Tracks on the Devices tab, I had to play around to figure out what Device from the pulldown, and then what choice in the window below.  For my particular setup, I have Realtek ALC850 rev 0 (OSS Mixer) and the Digital-1 choice in the window below, because I use a digital input to my receiver.

Now my mute button successfully turns the sound off and on.

Hope this helps!  -=Drew
