### Post by TheFu on 2023-04-08
You'll need to get out a tape measure for the case.  Sometimes cable management gets in the way, even if, technically, everything can fit.
You'll have better luck asking these questions on an MSI specific forum. Also, do you have any other addon cards in the system?  Sometimes GPUs use a little more than 2 slots of space, so 3 PCIe slots are taken up.

Get out a tape measure.  Measure everything - 

As for SATA ports, could using 90° SATA cables help?  Some options: [https://www.amazon.com/90-degree-sata-cables/s?k=90+degree+sata+cables](https://www.amazon.com/90-degree-sata-cables/s?k=90+degree+sata+cables) to give you ideas.  I didn't look any further. Definitely not a recommendation.
