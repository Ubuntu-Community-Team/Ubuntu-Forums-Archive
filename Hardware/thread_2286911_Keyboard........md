### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-07-16
True, if you have an older motherboard, you may have leaking capacitors which can cause noise which interferes with keyboards.  How old is the computer?  My guess is that the cheapest keyboard uses the least power so is less affected by the noise.  Also check your power supply.  If the 5VDC is not greater than 4.8VDC you will have issues with the keyboard.
