### Post by ird on 2009-10-29
compiz --replace revealed my card to be black listed. So I googled around some and found out how to skip the blacklist check. Everything works okay now, and my UI seems to work better with compiz enabled.

Sorry for the waste of space with this thread. Feel free to delete it, or leave it for others to learn from.

nano ~/.config/compiz/compiz-manager

add the line

SKIP_CHECKS=yes
