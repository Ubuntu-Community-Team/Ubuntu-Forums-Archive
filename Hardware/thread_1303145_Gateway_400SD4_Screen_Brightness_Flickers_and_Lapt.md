### Post by Dark_Stang on 2009-10-28
Sounds like a pretty obvious hardware problem. Could be a loose DC jack, failing AC adapter, or some internal power issues. I'll go over them breifly...

Loose DC Jack. Where the AC adpater plugs into the laptop. Sometimes the connection to the motherboard gets broken or loosened up. It can happen from tripping over the power cord, or just using the laptop so many times that it eventually wears out. This can cause issues like this.

Failing AC adapter. If your AC adapter is starting to go out you might hear random high pitched noises, squeeks, or humms. The AC adapter may randomly not be able to provide enough power to the laptop which can cause issues like this.

Internal power issues. If your motherboard is failing you can have random power issues. Generally it doesn't take long for total failure to happen if this is the case.
