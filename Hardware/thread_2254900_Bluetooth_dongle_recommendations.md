### Post by xmjsilverx on 2014-12-12
Well I didn't get any replies but I did find a list of suggestions.  I went with the IOgear bluetooth 4.0 and it's great.  It was plug and play, no drivers to install and the sound quality is great.  Also for anyone new to this I found out that in order to transfer files to ubuntu you need to go to the dash and search for personal file sharing, and checkmark to receive files via bluetooth.
