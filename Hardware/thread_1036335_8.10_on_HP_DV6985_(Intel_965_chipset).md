### Post by dptulk on 2009-01-10
I found the answer for this elsewhere and thought I would post it here.  

To get the 1280x800 Screen res. I edited xorg.conf and added the following to the screen section:

SubSection "Display"
Modes "1280x800" "1024x768" "800x600"
EndSubSection

The issue with the CPU going nuts?  I, uh, well... I flipped back over to Vista and turned the scheduled virus scan off. What a dufus.
