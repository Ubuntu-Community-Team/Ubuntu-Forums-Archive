### Post by pheonix7117 on 2009-12-04
If you haven't tried it already, the key combination Fn+F11 is the (I believe hardware) toggle switch for wifi and bluetooth. I think the laptop comes with both turned off, when you hit the combination once it turns the wifi only on, hit it a second time and only bluetooth is on, third time both are on, and fourth you're back to off. I'm currently writing this on an MSI Wind u123-003 (which I believe is the same hardware as the 004) on Ubuntu Jaunty netbook remix using my wifi card.
Good luck :)
