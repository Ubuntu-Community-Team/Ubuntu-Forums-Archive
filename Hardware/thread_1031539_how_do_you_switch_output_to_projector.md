### Post by olemarho on 2009-01-08
Hello. 

Connect and power on the projector, and go to System - Preferences - Screen resolution. At first you may see only your standard monitor, but just click once on it and the projector should be displayed. You can now click it and select what resolution you want. In this window it's very possible to show two different resolutions on your two screens, however this has not worked for me, so I turn off my normal screen. This works for me on a laptop thinkpad t40p with ubuntu 8.10.
