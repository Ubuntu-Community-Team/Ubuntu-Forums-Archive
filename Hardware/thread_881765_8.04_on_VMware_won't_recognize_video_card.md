VMWare emulates a PC (and its HW) for the guest operating system. The Guest OS has no access to the HW except for some special stuff. Any HW you "actually" have is of no interest to VMWare. Sorry.

*Getting desktop effects (direct rendering) working in Ubuntu in a VMWare box is something you will have to look up on the VMWare forums.*

When it comes to your "native" installed Ubuntu there is ways to get the ATI working fine. There is loads of guides here on the forum. The search function is good for finding such things.
