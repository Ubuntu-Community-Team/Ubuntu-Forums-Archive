### Post by jwcalla on 2011-03-01
That looks like a SATA drive so you'll need a SATA data cable to connect to the motherboard and a SATA power connector.  If your power supply doesn't have any SATA power connectors you can get a molex 4-pin to SATA power cable adapter.
