### Post by snowpine on 2011-01-18
I took the liberty of Googling your machine's hardware specs:

```
Intel Pentium 120 MHz MMX, 16 MB RAM (32 MB max), 1.6 GB hard disk, 6.1" TFT display
```

There is no chance Ubuntu will work on such low hardware specs according to the [published hardware requirements]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements").

I would recommend checking Craigslist, Freecycle, thrift stores, recycling centers, etc. for free or very low cost computers. I regularly see 1ghz CPU, 512mb RAM systems (the minimum specs for Ubuntu) on Cragislist for under US$100.
