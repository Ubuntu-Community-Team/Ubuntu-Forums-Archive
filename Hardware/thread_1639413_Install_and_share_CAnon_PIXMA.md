### Post by Brian88 on 2010-12-06
First try to plug your printer to the computer while the computer is on, and turn on the printer. Ubuntu will automatically try to install the driver.

If Ubuntu can't found the driver, follow the instructions here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=1380393&postcount=1](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=1380393&postcount=1)

To share the printer, follow the following instructions: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/NetworkPrintingWithUbuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/NetworkPrintingWithUbuntu)
