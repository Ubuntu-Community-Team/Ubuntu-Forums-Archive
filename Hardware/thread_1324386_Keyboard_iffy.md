Was that 'bad sectors' message with Ubuntu 9.10? Before you part with hard-earned cash for a new HD, check around the forums. There are many reports of the Palimpsest Desk Utility that ships with Karmic putting out false positives with some makes/models of hard disc. Apparently, it's a problem with Palimpsest interpreting the SMART data from the HD. Tbh I'm a bit vague about the details, because I've been given a clean bill of health on the WD/Samsung/Hitachi discs that are on the three machines I've installed Karmic to.

Of course, your hard disc may indeed be failing, but Palimpsest is crying wolf all over the place.
