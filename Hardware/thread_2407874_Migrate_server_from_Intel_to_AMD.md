### Post by Autodave on 2018-12-10
Since no one else has jumped in yet, let me give my 2 cents.  I have several HDs here that I installed Ubuntu on at one time or another.  I have never had an issue with just grabbing any one of them and sticking them into a machine and booting it up.

The one thing that you may have to do is to delete a graphics card driver if you are changing to a different card manufacturer, but other than, I don't forsee any problems.
