### Post by Mark Phelps on 2018-05-12
The basic answer to your question is NO -- there is no way to "fool" it into thinking Ubuntu is Windows.

What you would most likely have to do is remove Secure Boot from the tablet -- and I have no idea on how to do that, sorry.
