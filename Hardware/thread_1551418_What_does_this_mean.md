### Post by howefield on 2010-08-12
Probably just means that there was an issue between the router and the exchange. So whilst you probably had a LAN connection, you didn't have WAN. Although the "click" thing that you broke keeps the cable tightly in place, without it, the cable may simply have come a little loose.
