### Post by mrazster on 2008-12-10
I had the same problem with my E500.

1. I downloaded an "Alternate CD" from ubuntu.com.
2. When the boot menu appeared I pressed F6 and in the end of the commandline that appears I typed in
```
nolapic_timer
```
3. Pressed ENTER and went on with the installation...worked perfectly fine.
