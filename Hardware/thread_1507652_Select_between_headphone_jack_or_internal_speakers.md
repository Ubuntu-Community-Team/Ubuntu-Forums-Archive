### Post by Bucky Ball on 2010-06-13
Applications->Sound & Video->Pulse Audio Device Chooser. An icon should appear in your top toolbar. Click it and Volume Manager.

Looking at the playback stream window, get some sound happening and you should see the stream. Right click on it, 'Move Stream ...' , move it to the desired device, headphones or speaker. Tweak around in there and see what you come up with. You can direct skype to headphones, music to speakers, mic to wherever, whatever!

See how you go.
