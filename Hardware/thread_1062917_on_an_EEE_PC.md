### Post by tobydeemer on 2009-02-07
One thing to consider is eeebuntu. It's not EasyPeasy or Ubuntu Eee, which I could not get to install.

eeebuntu is a full Ubuntu desktop installation, but it uses a custom kernel that is optimized for Eee PC hardware support. It should recognize the web cam and other hardware out-of-box.

I know b/c I installed it last night to my wife's Eee 2G Surf (original 700) and all hardware was up and running.

[www.eeebuntu.org](www.eeebuntu.org)
