### Post by davidmohammed on 2010-06-22
since you've got an old intel graphics,

suggest - edit your grub and boot with either

i915.modeset=0

or

i915.modeset=1

also rename /etc/X11/xorg.conf to something else.  You generally dont need an xorg.conf file any more.
