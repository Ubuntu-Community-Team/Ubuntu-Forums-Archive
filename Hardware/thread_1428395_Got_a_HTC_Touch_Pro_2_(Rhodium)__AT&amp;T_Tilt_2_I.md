### Post by Bombenbach on 2010-03-12
Your Touch Pro 2 / Tilt 2 is a sexy peace of hardware with a strong 528MHz ARM CPU, brilliant WVGA touchscreen, nice QWERTY keyboard, GSM/UMTS, WIFI, GPS and Bluetooth. But it runs a proprietary OS from M$. An OS which is doomed because its successor will come soon and won't be compatible with it. An OS you can scarcely find good apps for and even if you do, they would probably cost you a bunch of money. An OS which has more limitations and bugs than features. 
 
Have you ever dreamed of running some other OS instead? An OS that is free and opensource. An OS that can control your device the way you want it and not the manufacturer. An OS that runs thousands of great free apps. An OS you can customize, tweak or hack without any limitations. An OS on your smartphone that is so similar to the OS you run on your desktop: Ubuntu. 
 
** Well, it's up to you to make this dream come true. **
 
We are the guys from xda-developers.com who currently develop an Ubuntu-like Distro for Touch Pro 2 called Rhobuntu. We use an Android kernel from Rhodium Android Port  Project and run a heavily tweaked Zubuntu (Ubuntu for Sharp Zaurus) derivate. It is an ARM system which is binary compatible to Zubuntu repos, which means apt-get install indeed works. So far we can boot  into Linux from WinMo using HaRet (it doesn't hurt WinMo). With the aid of Rhodium Android Port  Project we already can access and use a large part of  the hardware like Keyboard, Touchscreen, SD-cards, WiFi and GSM (currently only 3G/GPRS). There will be probably even more working  hardware pieces to come.  
 
Our problems are lack of people and lack of knowledge. Currently there are only four people actively developping and testing Rhobuntu. We have never developed a distro before and would greatly appreciate any help. We don't even know where to host our builds (~250MB) and use megaupload.

You don't have to be an ARM developer  as we can get necessary modules and kernels from the Android guys. Right now it's more important to cleanup the userland, write proper configuration scripts, get LXDE running, the way we want it, compile binaries and so on.  
 
Join us in developing this great FOSS projet! Make your Touch Pro 2 become the greatest ARM netbook ever!
