### Post by The Cog on 2012-12-31
I just found this:
[http://patrickmylund.com/blog/lowering-gaming-mouse-sensitivity-in-ubuntu-9-10/](http://patrickmylund.com/blog/lowering-gaming-mouse-sensitivity-in-ubuntu-9-10/)
It works for me on 12.10.

I used these commands:
```
xinput --list --short
```
from which I could see that my mouse is named "OM".
Then:
```
xinput --set-prop "OM" "Device Accel Constant Deceleration" 2
```
The artlcle I linked suggested 5 but that made mine rather too slow.
