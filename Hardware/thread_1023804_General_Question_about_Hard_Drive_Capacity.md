Supposing I only use a few gigabytes of my hard drive, will there be any noticeable difference between processor speed between a 60 and 160 GB hard drive?

---

### Post by Therion on 2008-12-28
Processor speed or *processing* speed?  In either case the answer is no.  All other things being equal, and excluding capacity (e.g. 60GB vs 160GB), how quickly your system can access data on the hard drive is determined by things like the drives spindle speed (RPM's), cache size, and read/write speed - also referred to sometimes as it's "bandwidth"; not the drive capacity.
