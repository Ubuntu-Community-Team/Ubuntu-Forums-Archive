I just want to post that I recently bought an HP HP Officejet Pro 8600 Premium e-All-in-One - N911n for our library. I had previously had a brother multifunction laser and after tons of time wasting trying to get it to scan in Ubuntu over a network I gave up.The above HP I plugged in add clicked add a network printer the printer popped up and the driver installed then I clicked add a network printer again and the fax scan driver pooped up and installed. 1st try I could print and scan from all 10 ubuntu machines. Windows 7 machines likewise worked easily. It is relatively cheap and even claims cheaper per page cost than lasers (if using XL cartridges). Also it has a legal sized scanner glass. The brother claimed legal sized scans but only was sheet fed, but the glass was only 8.5X11.Also coupons are available for $20 off.
