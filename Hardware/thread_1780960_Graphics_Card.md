### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2011-06-12
> does that mean the GPU is made by NVIDIA and the rest of the card, the fans heatsinks etc. are made by Asus?
I guess you could say that, but technically, Asus doesn't make all of the components (memory chips, capacitors, etc.). Nvidia has a reference design for their video cards. OEM's can modify the other components to their liking, but some models are exactly the same as the reference with the exception of the PCB color and/or sticker on the cooler.
