### Post by cascade9 on 2011-02-08
It should all work, but if you can get a different motherboard to the ASUS M4A88T-M, do so. 

Its got the older SB710 southbridge, limited to SATAII, the newer SB850 has SATAIII. It also has the horror that is a VIA 1708S sound chip. 

See if you can get a 870/SB850 or 890FX/SB850 chipset motherboard with a different sound chip ;)

### Post by Bucky Ball on 2011-02-08
ALWAYS go for a good quality PSU, 85+ or better and 600,000 hours or better. NOT a silver box. It is the heart of the machine and if a PSU dies, it is generally not pretty and rarely goes alone, if you follow me. ;)

Good luck with it. You might like to check out the Linux computer build guide in my signature.
