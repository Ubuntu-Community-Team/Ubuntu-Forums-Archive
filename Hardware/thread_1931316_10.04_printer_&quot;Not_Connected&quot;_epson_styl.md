### Post by pbhj on 2012-07-02
I had a not connected issue - details of [fixing "may not be connected" problem on Epson CX535WD]("http://alicious.com/epson-install-kubuntu/").

Basically the KDE printer config had kept an IP address that the printer no longer had - altered it, set the printer to fixed IP address and things have been working right since.
