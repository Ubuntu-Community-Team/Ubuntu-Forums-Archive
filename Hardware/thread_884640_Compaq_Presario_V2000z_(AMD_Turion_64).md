### Post by phidia on 2008-08-09
You can use either 32 or 64 bit it's up to you-there are advocates for each. I use 64 bit on my laptop and desktop and think it works fine some browser plugins can require more work in 64 bit. 

Suspend and resume-getting those features to work smoothly can be a little tricky and that trickiness can have a lot to do with video and wireless card drivers.

You could search the multimedia and video as well as the networking forums here for those cards to see how well the broadcom and your specific ATI card work. Added: Although there may be other methods you can download either the 32 or 64 bit driver for your video card from the [ATI site]("http://ati.amd.com/support/driver.html").
