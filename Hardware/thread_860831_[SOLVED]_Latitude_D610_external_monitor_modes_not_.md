### Post by DrJohn999 on 2008-07-16
Redetected displays: 

```
sudo dpkg-reconfigure -phigh xserver-xorg
```

and now the external display works at 1600x1050. However, the horizontal extent of the taskbars only runs to the width of the laptop screen, 1400px.

No idea why, but this is fine for me as is.
