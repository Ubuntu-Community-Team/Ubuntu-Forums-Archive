### Post by stefanst on 2008-12-29
Maybe I can clear up some possible confusion:

DVI-D is digital only. It does not carry the analog signal necessary for connection of a VGA monitor.

DVI-I has both- the digital and the analog signal. So it can be used with a VGA monitor. All that's needed is a VGA adapter.

Your card seems to have DVI-I output, so with two DVI-I to VGA adapters you should be fine.
