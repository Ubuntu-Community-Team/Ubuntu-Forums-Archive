I have a similar problem but it is intermittent and compounded by the fact that my second monitor is constantly reminding me that the best resolution is 1280x1024 but only ever displays 1024x768. The screen size/shift issue you describe comes and goes.  Sometime there is large footprint in each screen where a large percentage is off to the right and sometimes the screen size and display size are the same.  I have learned to open the Workplace Switcher when expecting message responses because they are often off the screen somewhere.  It can be frustrating.  Try opening the screen settings daemon and just click APPLY (don't change anything).  That has reset the display regions for me in the past.  Looks like a bug but how to report it is a mystery.
