### Post by Ayuthia on 2010-02-27
2 finger scrolling is having the ability to scroll a window by using two fingers on the touchpad instead of clicking on the window's scrollbar.

The ability to zoom in/out of a photo is usually done by the pinch gesture instead of the two finger scrolling.
