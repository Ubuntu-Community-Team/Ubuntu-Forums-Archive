### Post by requeth on 2010-06-17
I'm on an NV53 with 10.04 and that doesn't happen to mine. I'd imagine it's a hardware issue but I'd try installing Windows again and see if it acts up still. Clicking is not a good thing, warranties are. :)

As a side note since your new to the NV53, I recommend the proprietary ATI driver that can be found in System: Administration: Hardware Drivers. I've been getting purple screen of death's often and think it's linked to the opensource driver. Each time you upgrade your kernel you'll have to check the drivers, my driver got reset to opensource and I was blaming the new kernel instead of the driver. Whoops!
