Wow. That link is incredibly old and would now be all but useless. A word for the future: Don't bother with threads this old. They may end up causing more harm than good. 

You give no indication of what you are using to try and configure this. My advice would be to get an audio stream going (play some music> open Pulse Audio Volume Control> Output tab) and see which device your output is routed to. Then check in Playback. 

If the level meter for the 5.1 system doesn't show up, make sure it is not down the 'Output' or 'Playback' tab and out of view.
