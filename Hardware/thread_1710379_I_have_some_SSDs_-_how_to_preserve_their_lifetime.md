### Post by francis21 on 2011-03-19
Hey there. I actually have an SSD as well on my current laptop - the OCZ Vertex 2 Drive.

In general, do not try to use defragmentation onto your SSD - not really helpful. Also, I heard that you should avoid using hibernation as much as possible, because you'll keep writing data (that as big as your current RAM; based from what I read from some forums in here), and will reduce the lifespan of the SSD.

These are some of the things that should give you some tips to preserve the lifespan of your SSD. Hope this helps. Cheers.
