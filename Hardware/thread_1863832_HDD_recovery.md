You can always use the the disk utility under System==>Adminstration to view any SMART info the drive can give. But from the clicking noises, I believe that it is a hardware failure so no software fix will work on it. If the data is really important, you might have to find a company that does data recovery for hardware failures.( Maybe one of the reading/writings heads became misaligned or a platter got damaged :confused:) Anyways, post the info from SMART and best of luck.
