### Post by heir4c on 2013-11-09
With following command you get a list of all hardware in your PC/Laptop:
```
sudo lshw > hardwarelist.txt
```
In your home folder you find now a file: hardwarelist.txt
You can also use hardware.html than it is opened by the browser.
