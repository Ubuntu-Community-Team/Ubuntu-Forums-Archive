### Post by clusterpenguin on 2014-05-01
I don't have a water cooled system, but I've done some research on it before. Depending on the kind of GPU (780-ti and what not), manufactures will make water blocks for it. Depending on how much you want to spend, Compression fittings are generally better than barb fittings and bigger tubing is almost always better.
