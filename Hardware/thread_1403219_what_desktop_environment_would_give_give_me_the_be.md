### Post by underquark on 2010-02-11
It's not the desktop that matters but the programs you are running and whether your device can power down the monitor/disk(s), go into sleep mode/hibernate etc.  The actual desktop or OS has minimal impact except for some very special applications.  If battery life is tight, go for few (if any) fancy effects as they use up a bit of power.
