### Post by Barry Carroll on 2009-05-19
That can often happen when you are trying to run ram at lower than specified latencies.  The spec for your ram is supposed to be:

DDR2 1066MHz Non-ECC CL5 5-5-5-15 2.2V

You might have better luck on a more hardware / overclocking oriented forum though.
