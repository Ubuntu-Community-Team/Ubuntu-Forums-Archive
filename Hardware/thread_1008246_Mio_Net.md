### Post by -Cluster- on 2008-12-15
Hey!!!! GREAT NEWS!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

I have done it after lots and lots a testing i found a way round it not the easiest but the best.

All you need is a a special username and password!

When you install Mio Net on a windows machine the first time you sign in a pop-up comes asking you to upgrade. It also at the bottom gives a a username and password (they are very very long)

Then go to network places click on the idxxxxxxxx folder and use the username and password to login using the domain thats already on there. Give a a while its slow the first time but there you are on your harddrive
