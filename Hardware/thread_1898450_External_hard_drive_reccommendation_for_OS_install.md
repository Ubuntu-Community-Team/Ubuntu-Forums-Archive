### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-12-21
I have found a USB-powered drive perfectly acceptable for such matters.

Do ensure, when you install Ubuntu, that you install Grub onto the external drive and not the internal one, otherwise it will not be portable -- and it will mess up your usual boot when your external drive is not plugged in.
