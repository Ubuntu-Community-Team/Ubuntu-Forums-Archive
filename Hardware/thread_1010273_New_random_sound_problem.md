### Post by brandon88tube on 2008-12-13
I think I found a solution to my problem. Here is some information that might help others with the same problem. > **kostkon said:**
> Try to go to sound preferences (*System &#8594; Preferences &#8594; Sound*), select the first tab, and in the *Default Mixer Tracks* section make sure that your sound card is selected in the *Device* drop-down menu and the *Master* volume is selected in the list below.

Hope that helps.

> **jlfallaw said:**
> You may need to just play around with it. For me, I had to select PCM rather than Master- it's the only one that works for me for some reason.
