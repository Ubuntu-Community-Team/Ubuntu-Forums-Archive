### Post by petsomk on 2011-05-08
I own a Dell Studio notebook with an ATI Mobility HD3xxx graphics card, and while it may seem that only frustration is talking here, I suggest you stay away from ATI cards.  My dilemma is quite easy to explain: the closed-source drivers from ATI perform quite badly in the 2D regime (especially with 11.04 and Unity), while the open-source radeon drivers lack the power-management features compared to the official one's, meaning that my notebook permanently overheats a good 10°C (accompanied by the lovely sound of fans at full power).  If I had the choice of buying a new notebook, I'd go for Nvidia or Intel only.  On the other hand, I have no experience with Nvidia in a Linux environment, but can't really imagine that it's worse.
