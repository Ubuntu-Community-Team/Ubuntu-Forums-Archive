### Post by TheFu on 2022-02-16
Backblaze just released their quarterly reports for HDD reliability.  Those contain facts for over 160K real disks.

The warranty is the only thing we can trust from drive makers. Everything else is marketing.  Even within the same plant making the HDDs, there are wildly different reliability numbers. It comes down to the exact model, so picking a vendor for better engineering isn't really the situation. Some models of exactly the same storage and "marketing type" have vastly different reliabilities. The exact model is needed to make informed choices. It has been that way for 15 yrs.
