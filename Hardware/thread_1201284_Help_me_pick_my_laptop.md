### Post by LepeKaname on 2009-07-01
If you want to play 3D games in linux (or compiz), I would not suggest to take the one with ATI video card as ATI recently decided to withdraw the support to several models up to Intrepid. So, if you want to use Jaunty, could use open source drivers (not that bad) or stick always to Intrepid.

About the "Intel Graphics Media Accelerator 4500MHD" card, I have no idea how well is supported in Linux. I suggest to go with a Nvdia one (if possible).

I personally like AMD processors over Intel, but that really doesn't matter so much. 

About the noise, I'm almost sure it can be tunned up to decrease fan speed (or what is the noise source?). 

If I had to choose for one, I would choose the first one.
