### Post by number nine on 2009-01-21
Actually the SFP-83H is not DisplayLink inside. It's some kind of custom development for Samsung. I'm not sure who makes it (I've heard it's a Korean company) but it's not DisplayLink.

Samsung's other "Ubisync" products (full-sized monitors) are DisplayLink though. I think the SFP-83H is the only one that isn't.
