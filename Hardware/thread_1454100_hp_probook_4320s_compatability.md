### Post by siriusnova on 2010-04-20
Hello!

I have the Probook 4320s, mine came with SUSE Linux Enterprise Desktop 11 therefore pretty much everything works if you get the version with the Linux install from HP.

I installed the Ubuntu 10.04 LTS Beta on the laptop and everything "just worked", however I have heard that there are issues with the intel driver in many other distributions currently, mainly involving KMS and lack of mature support which will change in a few months.

Hope this helps!
