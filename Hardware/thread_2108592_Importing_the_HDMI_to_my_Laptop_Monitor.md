### Post by SeijiSensei on 2013-01-25
I doubt it's possible at all.  I've never seen a standard laptop with a video input port.  I also don't see any HDMI to USB converters which makes sense.  HDMI is designed to work with the HDCP copy-protection system.  Converting an HDMI signal into something that could be routed to a USB port would defeat the purpose of HDCP.
