I finally got Ubuntu 8.10 installed, and my Dinovo edge keyboard does not work at all.

I'm new and have no idea how I can get it working.

EDIT:
Go to "System" -> "Preferences" -> "Keyboard"

Choose the "Layouts" tab -> There's a selection list for Keyboard Model. Choose Logitech under Vendor and your keyboard under model. There's as new as diNovo Edge listed 


Then get your receiver and keyboard to sync (re-connect) and it will work!
