### Post by marshmallow1304 on 2009-07-29
> **alimooghashang said:**
> isn't it a demo file one?

Biometrics are not yet really integrated into Linux.  The fprint project is the best available, but it's not done yet.  That's why it's just a demo at this point.

This page has some background info and more complete instructions.
[http://ubuntu-unleashed.com/2008/04/get-your-fingerprint-reader-to-work-in-ubuntu.html](http://ubuntu-unleashed.com/2008/04/get-your-fingerprint-reader-to-work-in-ubuntu.html)

There's also [ThinkFinger]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ThinkFinger"), but I've not tried that one.
