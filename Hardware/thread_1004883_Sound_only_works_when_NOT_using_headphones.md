### Post by Mathiasdm on 2008-12-07
Okay, it would seem I forgot the 'headphone' switch.

For anyone else who might need it: go to the sound icon in the top right of the screen, right-click and select 'Open Volume Control', then to the 'Switches' tab, and select 'headphone'.
