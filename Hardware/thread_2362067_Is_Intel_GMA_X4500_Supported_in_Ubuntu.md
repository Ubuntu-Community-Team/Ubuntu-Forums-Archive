Intel Mobile 945GM/GMS 943/940GML Express Integrated Graphics Controller


Just for grins I installed 16.04 since LTS Long term support.
With 12.04 the Unity GUI was too slow to be useful, but I installed fallback/flashback/Gnome panel which was like the old gnome screens and much lighter weight.
