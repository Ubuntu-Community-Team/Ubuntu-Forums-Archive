### Post by matthew82 on 2015-11-27
There seems to be some support for touch screens and devices running Ubuntu 14.04. A quick google search of "Ubuntu 14.04 Touch Screen" brings up some relative information. You won't get full functionality like you get with Windows 7/8/10 /Android but you will get 1-3 point touch function. Then there's always an option of Ubuntu Touch. Though, that is still in Development. It's worth checking out though.
