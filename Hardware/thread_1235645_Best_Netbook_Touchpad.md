### Post by markbuntu on 2009-08-09
keyboard usability is far more determining. A lot of netbooks have way too small keyboards and screwy key layouts. You can adjust to a touchpad but if your fingers keep colliding while typing or the ctrl key is in the wrong place that is a serious problem.

You should really try to try them out for yourself.
