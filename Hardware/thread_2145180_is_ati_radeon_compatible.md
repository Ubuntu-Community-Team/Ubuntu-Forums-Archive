### Post by QIII on 2013-05-14
Hello and welcome to the forums!

The short answer, I am afraid, is probably no -- unless you use the open source Radeon driver.  And it is probably far too old to game with unless you will tolerate very marginal performance.

ATI's legacy driver available for it isn't going to work with any X Server version after late 2008/early 2009.  Even the HD 2000 to HD 4000 cards won't work with 13.04 without downgrading X Server, changing the kernel and using the most recent legacy driver. 

Try it with the Radeon driver and see if you get good enough performance to make you happy with the game, but don't hold your breath.  ;)

Best wishes!
