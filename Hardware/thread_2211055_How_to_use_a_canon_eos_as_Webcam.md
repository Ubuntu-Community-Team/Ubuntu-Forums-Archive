### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-03-13
Some Canon cameras are supported with [capture]("http://capture.sourceforge.net/").  *gphoto* has some remote capture capabilities as well:  [http://www.gphoto.org/doc/remote/](http://www.gphoto.org/doc/remote/)

I have used *capture* with some old Powershot models, but I don't have experience with any EOS models.  So your search continues.
