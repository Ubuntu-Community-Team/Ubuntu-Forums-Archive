### Post by Flash858 on 2009-07-04
.chk files are windows files that were corrupted, and then found by scandisk. Unless they are text files, they are most likely unrecoverable. If you open them in Wordpad (or Geany, Gedit, Abiword, etc...), you may get a better handle on what they are and if they are important to Windows.

They most likely are not...
