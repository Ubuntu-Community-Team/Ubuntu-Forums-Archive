### Post by sedawk on 2014-07-24
I recommend to check some big online dealer in UK for laptops that supports customer reviews. Then check the cheap bestsellers that are sold without OS and read the latest customer review to see how ubuntu works for them. As models can differ between countries and different hardware is often sold under the same name do not rely on reviews from other countries or that are older than a few weeks.
