### Post by Frogs Hair on 2010-03-07
If you have updated your Nvidia graphics drivers from the download drivers page @ Nvidia .com you may want to roll-back. The the latest Linux 195 drivers have been recalled due to a possible software error in fan speed control that may lead to the
quick demise of your graphics card.

The windows 196.76  has also been affected. See Nividia forums and download drivers
page for details.
