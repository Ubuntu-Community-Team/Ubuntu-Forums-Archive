### Post by bumanie on 2010-03-08
I had that issue with 9.10 with HP f4185 printer. I tried the latest hplip driver from HP with no luck, tried cups debugging with no luck. In the end I uninstalled the printer and then went back in to the printer menu and chose 'new'. After ubuntu searched for a driver and installed it, the printer works fine. Might be worth a try.
