### Post by silkstone on 2008-08-03
Hmmm... Has the 512MB card ever worked before in the Acer?

It's a little odd, but I'm wondering if it's something to do with the block format system used by SD and SDHC cards. The original spec for SD was up to 1GB, and then SDHC came along for higher capacities. Usually the problem is that people try to read SDHC on a card reader that doesn't support it, but here it may be the other way around!

I guess the consolation is that memory cards are very cheap now, so you could replace the 512MB one.

Apart from that, I'm sorry but I really can't come up with an explanation.
