Yeah, the problem is that proprietary drivers by short definition are *closed source* drivers written by the company who produces the hardware. Unfortunately, AMD will not open the driver development to the community, and therefore we are stuck with whatever they feel like giving us.

It is very unfortunate that it has to happen this way, but that is just the way it is. Pretty much your only hope is to just sit and wait for the next release and pray your card is supported :-/
