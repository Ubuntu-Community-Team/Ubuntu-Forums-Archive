### Post by Paul Sodtke on 2011-10-28
I have Ubuntu 10.10 running on a netbook, the Aspire One A110-ZG5. I could not get either the internal mic nor an external mic to work with Skype, even though the internal mic worked fine with Sound Recorder. A search of these forums did not turn up any similar report.

However, the fix prescribed for the Acer Aspire 1810T running Maverick Meerkat found at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/Machines/Netbooks](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/Machines/Netbooks) seems to also work for my hardware. padevchooser solves the problem.

I hope someone else may find this helpful.

Paul
