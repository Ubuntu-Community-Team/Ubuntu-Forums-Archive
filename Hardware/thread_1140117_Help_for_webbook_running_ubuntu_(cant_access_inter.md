### Post by pafarthing on 2009-05-01
Do not panic. I had the same problem - also on T-mobile - and there was a long discussion on the Webbook Blog and Wader launchpad forum.

There has now been an upgrade that fixes the problem - find yourself a wireless (or wired) network you can connect to, download the Wader update that appears (along with anything else), reboot, and you should be all right... though I needed to delete my wifi profile and recreate it as well - very simple.

At worst it will take a couple of reboots, but you will be fine. The new version of Wader has a few extra functions and gives more information about your connection.
