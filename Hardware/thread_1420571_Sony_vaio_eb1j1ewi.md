### Post by galdren on 2010-03-03
i have a vaio cw 1s1e and it does work but will give you some hassle. 

-after activating the nvidia driver the screen went blank. This is because the vaio screen's EDID was not conventional. There is a workaround involving configuring your xorg to look for your screen's custom EDID

-still after finding the screen there is no way to control the nice backlight of the screen

-to be able to find external screens on the VGA or HDMI port, there is further hacking needed -.-

edit:

oh and it's wake up from standby is too sensitive. Sometimes the movement of the bluetooth mouse wakes it up, which is obviously not what you want from a laptop.
