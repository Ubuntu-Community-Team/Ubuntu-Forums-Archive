### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-11-22
Sorry to say this, but 11.10, due primarily to Unity, places a lot more demand on resources than previous Ubuntu versions, causing it to run hotter.

That, coupled with reported bugs in its inability to properly handle CPU scaling, tend to make it run really hot on some PCs.
