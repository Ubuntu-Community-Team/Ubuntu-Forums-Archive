### Post by drjoewebb on 2009-02-16
I have a Dell Mini Inspiron 1210 and had the battery charging problem. Batteries would not charge whether the system was plugged in while running or off.

I spoke with Dell tech support and learned that Airplane Mode (under System - Preferences) TURNS OFF charging capabilities. (Yeah, I know... dumb)

As soon as I disabled airplane mode, charging started immediately.

The person claimed that this was in process of being documented soon.

She also said to turn off updates because some software were putting computers into airplane mode and that I should check now and then if it had accidentally been turned on. She reiterated that updates were not coming from Dell and that Dell would notify by e-mail when updates were available.

I know there are some purists that are not exactly pleased with Dell's customizing the Ubuntu software, but I must say that I am astonished at how well this computer works compared to Windows notebooks I have had for many years.
