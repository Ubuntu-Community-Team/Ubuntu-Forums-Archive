Hi,

Although I don't have the solution to the problem I can give some insight...

The space bar key is more than likely performing two tasks when you press it, first being [Space Bar] and the second being [Down Arrow]. That would explain the fact that the cursor only jumps down when there is text beneath where you want to type.
