Hi, 

I have just moved to Ubuntu and was struggling with various distros and my USB 3G dongle and noticed that many people were having the same issues and I thought I would pass on some advice.

I found 2 ways of doing this without all the hassle of unmounting usb  dongles and using modeswitch.

Most android phones now have the option to turn the phone into a wifi hotspot.

I use a Nexus One and i simply flick the toggle for Wifi Hotspot and my Ubuntu system on MSI Wind u230 automatically connects to my phone over wifi and uses its 3g connection.

PC World and Currys in the UK now sell portable wifi hotspots that act as a dongle but then share the signal over short range wifi. This should solve and Ubuntu dongle issues.
