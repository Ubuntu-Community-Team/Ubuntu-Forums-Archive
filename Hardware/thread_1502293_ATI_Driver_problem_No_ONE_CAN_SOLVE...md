Absolute abandon?  Gimme a break.  Since the first time I logged into here I have either received answers in a very timely fashion or I have searched the threads and found what I needed.

About ALL your graphic problems: have you installed the latest driver from the repositories?  Have you tried ndiswrapper?  Or, let's try something even simpler: try disabling Compiz.  I have a Nvidia graphics card that runs greta except when I have Compiz running and then everything goes haywire: screen locks, slows to a crawl, has weird colors, etc.  Disable Compiz and everything is fine again.  I know that your eye candy will be gone, but you can then use your computer for it was intended for: computing.  Or reading email. Or playing games.  

If the worst thing that happens to me is that I can't make my screens jiggle like Jello, that's OK.  Still beats Windoze hands down.
