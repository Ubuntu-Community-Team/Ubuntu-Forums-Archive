### Post by WinterRain on 2010-04-15
Not sure about system wide settings, but for firefox there is [SmoothWheel]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/357"). I'll post back if I find out anything more.

Edit: I even checked in ubuntu-tweak, and there are no settings for it. And after googling, I see no one has had any success. However, one person fixed it by simply unplugging and replugging the mouse. Worth a try.
