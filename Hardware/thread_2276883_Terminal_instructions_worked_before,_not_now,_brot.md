### Post by Bucky Ball on 2015-05-06
15.10??? Don't you mean 15.04? I presume you mean you did a clean install of 15.** as a net upgrade from 14.04 LTS to 15.04 is not possible as far as I know. You'd need to go 14.04>14.10>15.04>15.10 to get to where you say you are with a net install. 

If you are using 15.10, just wondering why ... :-K That is very much in development and won't be released until October. You've uninstalled 15.10 by the looks, so let's for the moment forget about it and stick to the stable 14.04 LTS.

As for your Brother printer, let's start from the beginning. Install 14.04 LTS, and get that stable. Go to the [Brother site]("http://support.brother.com/g/b/downloadlist.aspx?c=au&lang=en&prod=mfcj475dw_us_as&os=128") and download the Driver Install package. Install it. 

Reboot. Go to Printer> Add Printer. When you get to the section where you pick a driver, choose Brother and scroll through the available drivers. Yours should be there. Install.

PS: I think you can safely remove the precursor of the background in your first post. It may serve to confuse rather than help as you now have a stable 14.04 and printer problems and that's what we're looking at. ;)
