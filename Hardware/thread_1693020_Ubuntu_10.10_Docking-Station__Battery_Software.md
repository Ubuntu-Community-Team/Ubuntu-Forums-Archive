### Post by TechnikAlsace on 2011-02-22
No, as far as I know there is no specific software since the administration of battery charging is a complicated and very individual task of communication between the guard chip in the battery and the notebook's BIOS and ACPI mechanisms.

The best solution (in my eyes) is to take off the battery once charged and working on the docking station without battery. Once per month you should work on battery until it is discharged below 10%, recharge it and take it off until it is really needed.
