### Post by Jesper Ø. Jensen on 2008-08-01
Hi Henriette

I also recently bought two asus eee 900 computers. 

First I tried the ordinary Ubuntu 8.04 desktop i386 installation. But the wireless access did not work.

I therefore quickly decided to try the ubuntu eee from ([http://www.ubuntu-eee.com/index.php5?title=Get_Ubuntu_Eee](http://www.ubuntu-eee.com/index.php5?title=Get_Ubuntu_Eee))

It works very well. Wireless access works, sound works, webcam works (if not, check that it is enabled in bios). I have some troubles with the microphone, but I have not had much time to investigate it yet :)

One thing missing is all the function keys (fn keys - the blue ones on the keyboard - I especially miss the volume up and down functionallity.

Overall the performance is good, I turned desktop effects of to get a better respons when clicking on menu and so.  

Hope this helps

Kind regards
   Jesper
