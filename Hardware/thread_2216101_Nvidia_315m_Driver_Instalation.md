You mean ".run" is the filename extension, like "somefile.run", right? I won't swear to it, but I think you:```
/bin/sh /path/to/filename.run
```At least I'm pretty sure I got one of those that came with those instructions and it worked. It may not be a general principle. I assume that extension is conventional to indicate a script written in the Bourne shell. You could probably confirm this with a few minutes with a search engine.
