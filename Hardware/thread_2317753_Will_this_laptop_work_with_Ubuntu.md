### Post by buzzingrobot on 2016-03-19
Looks good to me, but search specifically on the CPU and Linux. Support for the very latest processors in that Skylake line is a bit sketchy right now. The 6200U has been out for a while, but there's no harm in checking.

I'm running 16.04 on an i5-6260U with Iris 540 graphics.  It's not stable -- very frequent freezes and lockups -- with the kernel shipping with 16.04.
