### Post by damis648 on 2009-01-21
Are you using Hardy or Intrepid?

Hardy: System>Preferences>Bluetooth
Go to the Services tab and check the input service. Select it, and then click the 'add...' button at the bottom. Follow the prompts.

Intrepid: System>Preferences>Bluetooth
Click the plus sign and follow the prompts.
