### Post by TBABill on 2011-01-06
Intel chipsets seem to have great success, but others are natively supported in the kernel as well. Ralink comes to mind, but you will see quite a few posts of users with issues in recent releases. If you find one of interest, if you can find out which chipset it has (like a Broadcom BCM4312, for example), then you can search online and the forums for issues or ease of use.
