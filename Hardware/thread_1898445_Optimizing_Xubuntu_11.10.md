### Post by snowpine on 2011-12-21
The single biggest improvement would be to install Xubuntu on hardware that meets the [Minimum System Requirements]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements"): 

> Minimum system requirements for Xubuntu would fall roughly between Ubuntu Server and Desktop:

    512 MiB of system memory (RAM)
    5 GB of disk space
    Graphics card and monitor capable of 800x600 resolution 

With Lubuntu, you can use computers with even less memory.

Regarding processors, a recent thread on the Ubuntu Forums describes a 500 MHz Xubuntu machine as being "too slow" and discusses alternatives: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1267443](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1267443)

Take a look on Craigslist, Freecycle, relatives/friends who might be getting a new computer for Christmas and looking to get rid of their old one, etc.
