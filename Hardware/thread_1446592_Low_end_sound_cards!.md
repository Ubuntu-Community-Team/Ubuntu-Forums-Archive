### Post by cascade9 on 2010-04-05
Depends on waht you call 'low end'- there are quite a few soundcards with work well with linux, but most of them arent much better than onboard sound. 

I perfer to avoid creative, they have awful drivers in general and some of the lower end creative sound cards can have problems. 

If you in teh US, this is really cheap and should work fine (depeding on what version of ALSA you have- 

[http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16829180005](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16829180005)

If you have a bit more money, I'd go for the ASUS Xonar- 

[http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16829132013](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16829132013)
