Most wifi cards need a proprietary driver to make them work.  Under System/Hardware Drivers you should find one to install using an Ethernet connection to the Internet.  Then you click on the wifi icon in the task bar and find your connection.  You may need to fill in your WEP security code, a password after that and perhaps reboot.
