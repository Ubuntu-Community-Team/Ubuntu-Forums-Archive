### Post by 200mg on 2009-07-03
While transplanting an intel q6600 from another machine into my ubuntu server I accidentally dropped my Asus P5K motherboard onto the floor.  Motherboards tend to act funny after being dropped a few feet onto a wood floor so I ventured into the world looking for a replacement.  I landed on a P5Q Turbo that was on sale for like $140.00.  It seems as if everything has worked right out of the box on this motherboard.  It found my ubuntu partition without any configuration needed.  And the same goes for ethernet, sound, etc...  If you're in the market for a high performance mobo for ubuntu I would give this Socket 775, P45 chipset mobo some serious consideration.

The Asus P5Q Turbo and Ubuntu 9.04 64bit is a winning combo!!!

M: Asus P5Q Turbo
P: Intel Q6600 oc'd to 3.00ghz
R: 8GB G.Skill DDR2 1066 CL5
MD: 250GB Seagate Barricuda
SD: 2x WD 500GB Caviar
