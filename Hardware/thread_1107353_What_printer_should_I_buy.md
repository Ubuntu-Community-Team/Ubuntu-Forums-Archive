### Post by sgosnell on 2009-03-26
Brother provides Linux drivers for their printers, and seem to work pretty well.  I don't know about the cost for toner, etc, because the company pays for that.  Installing the Brother on Jaunty was a piece of cake - click on Add a printer, computer found the networked Brother, I chose the closest match to the model, and it printed.  It takes a couple of downloads to get scanning working, but it's not difficult, and printing works OOTB.
