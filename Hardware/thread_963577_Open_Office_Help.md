### Post by nullspace on 2008-10-30
Congrats on your first ppt.

displaying the presentation on the projector is a question of hardware more than anything. I am going to assume a few things here and you can correct me if I am wrong. 

your going to need a vga cable to connect the external monitor port on your lappy to the projector (that might be provided for you). Once that is hooked up and you have signed in then goto System >> Administration >> Screens and graphics. You should see two screens.Click on screen 2 and then make sure it's set as secondary screen and then you can mirrior your desktop or extend it much like in windows. 

Good luck on the speech.
