### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2013-10-16
You're SOL. The nvidia driver doesn't support OC'ing GT/X 400-series or later. If it's Factory OC'd, then you probably wouldn't have much headroom anyway. The GT640 is great for video, but for gaming... well, you should have gotten a better card.
