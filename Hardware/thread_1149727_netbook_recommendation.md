### Post by HuckleSmothered on 2009-05-05
I'm pretty sure there is NOT a laptop that comes with a solid state drive over 40GB. Good place to start is here:
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comparison_of_subnotebooks](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comparison_of_subnotebooks)
The EeePC 1000 is close to what you might be looking for. 10" screen. 92% full keyboard. Atom processor. Up to 2GB RAM. 40GB SSD (8+32). Xandros standard. Unfortunately, Xandros sucks. Very, very buggy. I considered leaving it on mine until I used it for about five minutes. That'll convince you to put Ubuntu NBR on it.
