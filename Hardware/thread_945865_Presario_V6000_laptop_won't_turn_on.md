### Post by jw_90 on 2008-10-12
Okay, so I was on my laptop last night, and it was just like normal, and I shut the screen so it and myself could go to sleep, then this morning when i got up and opened my laptop again the screen wouldnt come on...thats an easy fix just ctrl-alt-f7+f8, but that still didnt do anything, so I did a hard boot and now it won't start up at all, the led lights come on and it checks the drives, but the screen won't come on at all, and in 15-20 seconds it restarts itself and does the same thing.
I made sure the screen was still plugged in, but I am not sure what else it could be, any ideas would be wonderful

### Post by phidia on 2008-10-12
Sounds like your xserver (/etc/X11/xorg.conf) may have gotten corrupted.

Providing your system specs plus the video driver you're using might help too.

As a first thing to do I would shutdown completely & remove the battery (to reset everything) and after a mintue or two reinstall the battery and try to boot up.

If that doesn't work see if a live cd will get you a desktop.
