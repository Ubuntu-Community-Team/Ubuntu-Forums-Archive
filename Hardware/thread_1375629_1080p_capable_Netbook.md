### Post by firefeather on 2010-01-09
The HP Mini 311 is about $400, has a 1366x768 pixel 11" display with HDMI out. It has an NVIDIA Ion graphics card (one of its main selling points) to make HD graphics run smoothly. So, no, the display isn't 1080p, but it's the best HD in a netbook that I've heard of. I just bought one and I'm happy with it so far!

### Post by mkoehler on 2010-01-09
I've only heard of netbooks that can output 1080p video; I have yet to hear of any netbooks with a resolution of 1920x1080.  The industry standard seems to be 1366x768, which is only capable of displaying 720p video.

Sorry, I don't have any experience with the netbooks themselves, so I don't know which one would be the best bet to play the videos on an external display smoothly.  Good luck!
