### Post by 00b00nt00 on 2009-02-16
To give you an idea about speed, my 800Mhz PIII laptop can just about do the following things:

* Watch flash video on Youtube (though not at high quality setting)
* Play FFMPEG and XVID standard definition video.
* Play DVD video.
* View flash intensive web-sites, but they take several seconds to load.
* Skype

Things it won't do:

* 3D - No compiz
* x264 video. Ha, ha, ha.
* Games

Your computer is likely to struggle with all of the above. Go with Xubuntu. At least then you will still have a typewriter. :)
