### Post by kidders on 2010-03-27
Hi there,

Lots of UPSs work well with Linux. I've used APC ones without any problems for years. Personally, I tend to ignore pretty features ... anything cheap with a sufficient output rating & a USB port, manufactured by someone reputable should do the trick. Just be sure whatever you buy is on the list of supported devices for a well-known UPS manager (eg nut/apcupsd).

I hope that helps.
