### Post by baosheng on 2008-12-14
I just got a wired Apple mighty mouse. Everything works fine. The only problem is that sometimes, the right-click is recognized as left-click and the menu popped up. This is very annoying. I got to click one or two more times if such case happened. 

Do you know what might be the problem? Is it because I pressed the mighty mouse in an incorrect way?

---

### Post by baosheng on 2008-12-14
ok, i guess that I have figured out the problem. It is actually because of my habit of using mice. I should not put two figures on the shell together. According to Apples webpage: [http://www.apple.com/mightymouse/design.html](http://www.apple.com/mightymouse/design.html)

"Capacitive sensors under Mighty Mouse’s seamless top shell detect where your fingers are and predict your clicking intentions, so you don’t need two buttons — just two fingers."

So, I tried again with only one finger on the shell while clicking. It worked. No problem at all. 

Wow, this stuff is so high tech that I even don't know how to use it. Apple should put this in their documents.
