### Post by Lavahead on 2010-02-06
I spoke too soon. After some research, I was able to find the possible fix for Karmic:

Set "ENABLE_LAPTOP_MODE=true" in /etc/default/acpi-support

Also unchecked "Spin down HD" in AC Power Management.
