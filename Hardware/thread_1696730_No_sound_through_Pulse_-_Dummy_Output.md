I wasted SO much time trying to figure this out, and I couldn't find the solution ANYWHERE on Google.

Okay, so if you have a dummy output in your sound preferences, before you go try OSS or sticking with ASLA I suggest you try this.

Go to your sound preferences, click on the Hardware tab, and on the bottom change the profile to something else. Try them all, since some may not work. For each one you try, go back to the output tab and see if you have the device you were looking for. Hopefully, no more Dummy Output!
