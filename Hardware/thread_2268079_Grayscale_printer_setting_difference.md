### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-03-08
Some HP printers (like the 7310) will allow you to print with the black cartridge only.  Others won't work at all if either the color or black cartridge has a fault.  The sure way to tell is to put an empty color cartridge and try different print modes.  You will quickly know what works and what doesn't.
