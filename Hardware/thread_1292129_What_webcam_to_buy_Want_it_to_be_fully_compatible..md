### Post by Fire_Chief on 2009-10-15
Not to overwhelm you but here's a pretty good list of supported cams based on the Linux UVC project.

[http://linux-uvc.berlios.de/#devices]("http://linux-uvc.berlios.de/#devices")

The Linux UVC drivers are built into kernels newer than 2.6.26 so you probably don't need to do anything extra to get the camera working in Ubuntu.

A quick glance at reviews points to the Logitech Quickcam Pro 9000 as the best sub-$100 webcam on the market. Again, check the device list for compatibility.

Hope this helps!
