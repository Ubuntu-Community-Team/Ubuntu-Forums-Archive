### Post by RiBBiT on 2009-04-26
Hi!

I have an RX1500, it is a very impressive mouse, I really enjoy the fast scrolling :). The 3 main buttons and the wheel is working "out-of-the-box", the other two buttons needs to be configured, but I haven't done that yet.
One thing to mention: the middle button is not the scroll wheel, but the small one below it. You can change between regular scrolling and hyperscrolling by pushing the scrollwheel itself. It is a bit confusing for the first time, as usually the scroll is the 3rd button.

UPDATE:

I was wrong, the other two buttons are working as well as horizontal scrolls.
For reconfiguring the buttons check out [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Config/Input](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Config/Input).
