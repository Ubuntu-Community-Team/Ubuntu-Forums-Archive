### Post by Martin.Weinberg on 2016-07-07
An alternative url is here:

[http://www.ubuntu.com/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/download)

Again, pick the variant that you want.  For example, click on "desktop" if you are installing on a laptop.  You will see options and instructions for installing from Linux, Mac OS, and Windows midway down the page.  After selecting an option, your browser will download an ISO image that you may burn to a DVS or copy to a memory stick.  I usually do the memory stick.  After rebooting your computer with the media, you will have the option to try Ubuntu without installing or install, guided by the installer GUI.

Have fun!!
