### Post by TBABill on 2010-12-08
For compatibility most people seem to run into issues with video, wireless and sound (and USB devices that may have no support at all). This machine looks fine. You have Intel everything but I don't see sound listed. Performance aside, Intel hardware is very well supported. I have both nVidia and Intel video graphics on different machines, as well as ATi on another one, and I have no compatibility issues once the drivers are installed.
