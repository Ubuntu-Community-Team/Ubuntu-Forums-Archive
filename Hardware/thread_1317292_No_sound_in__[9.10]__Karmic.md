### Post by Intel91 on 2009-11-06
I am assuming since you installed your GPU driver you indeed updated all Hardware Drivers. If that is the case try going back to System>Administration>Hardware Drivers then look to see if Software Modem is activated. If so, disable it then reboot. Check for sound.
