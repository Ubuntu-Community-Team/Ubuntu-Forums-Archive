### Post by DellX200user on 2008-08-15
This xorg.conf solved almost all my problems with my Dell X200.

I compiled it from reading from other users in the forums and from some other pages I found in the internet.

Right now it's running smoothly and fast.

UBUNTU 8.04.1
Dell X200
Pentium III 933mhz
640 Megas of Ram

I tried to set a higher VideoRam but it did not help at all. It made things worse in my system.

I hope it helps.
