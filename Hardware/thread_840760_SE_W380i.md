### Post by the_shadowfax_rider on 2008-06-25
Connect the phone with the USB cable that came along. It will detect it as a flash drive and then you can drag-n-drop your desired music files to the audio folder in the Phone. It works the same way with my W580i.
 But for this you will have to select the "File Transfer" mode in your phone once the data cable is connected. Once you're done, unmount the drive(phone) from the desktop by right-clicking on it!
Hope this helps.

### Post by mykrob on 2008-06-25
My wife has one of those. I was never able to mount it, so I just bought a Bluetooth dongle from [www.geeks.com](www.geeks.com) for $5.99. Now we can transfer ringtones to my phone, her phone, friend's phones, any phone that is bluetooth enabled.. A worthwhile investment to never pay for ringtones :)
Just use Audacity and make 15-20 second clips from you own MP3's then bluetooth them to the phone.
