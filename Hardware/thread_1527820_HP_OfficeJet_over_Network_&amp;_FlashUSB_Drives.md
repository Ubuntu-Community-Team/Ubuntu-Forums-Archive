### Post by tgalati4 on 2010-07-09
I've got several HP printers.  I think the card readers only work with USB connection.  And you can only do USB or ethernet connection, not both.  If you have a server, you could perhaps use the USB connection and then publish (share) the flash card mount over the network.  With flash cards getting larger, it's a convenient backup method.  Older HP models can't read the larger capacity cards.
