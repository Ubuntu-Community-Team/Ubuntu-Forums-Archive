### Post by cascade9 on 2010-04-21
I'd check in your BIOS and see if there is an 'initialise by PCI/AGP' setting in there. 

That system only has AGP and PCI slots, and AGP cards are getting harder to come by these days....a PCI card might give you more options. 

If you can only use AGP, and you could find one, I think an AGP nVidia 6600GT would be a good choice, but finding one might be hard...
