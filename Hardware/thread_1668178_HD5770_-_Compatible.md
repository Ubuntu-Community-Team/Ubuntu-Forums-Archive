### Post by cascade9 on 2011-01-16
You can get more from AMD for your money then from Intel, so if you want to build a cheap machine then AMD is the way to go. You dont need to run an ATI (AMD now) video card with an AMD CPU however, you can run an nVidia card. 

In general, the AMD/ATI open soruce drivers are better than nVidia, and proprietary drivers better from nVidia. 

AMD 5770s are nice cards, but more for gaming than anythign else. Unless I've missed some development, having a faster video card wont realy help with photo editing, so you would do better to get a lower end card, ATI/AMD or nVidia.  

Probably your best best would be a AMD 870/SB850  chipset motherboard, whatever AMD CPU gives you the most for your money, and a nVidia GT220/210.
