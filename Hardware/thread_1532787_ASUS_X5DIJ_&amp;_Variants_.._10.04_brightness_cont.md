Hello Everybody , lovely to be here :D .

For all those with the Asus X5DIJ & variants that have no control of screen brightness with 10.04 .. you do now \\:D/

after a bit of messing around trying to figure out how to fix it , i saw a piece on upgrading the kernel from 2.6.32 to 2.6.34 , it didn't specifically have anything to do with the asus , but thinking it might provide some better hardware support i gave it a go .. and it worked . So does audio ( haven't checked the headphone issue yet ) , Wi-fi is rock solid too .. 9 hours and it hasn't dropped the connection once .

Just follow this link .. [http://usablesoftware.wordpress.com/2010/05/26/switch-to-a-newer-kernel-in-ubuntu-10-04/](http://usablesoftware.wordpress.com/2010/05/26/switch-to-a-newer-kernel-in-ubuntu-10-04/) ( choose the right versions to install ) and bask in the low light of your display from now on  :)

hope this helped someone
