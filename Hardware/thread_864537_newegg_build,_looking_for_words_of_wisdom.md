First off, make sure EVERYTHING works for Linux. Good way to do this is to take the hardware's name and Google "<Name of hardware> ubuntu" or "<name of hardware" linux. Read what you find.

Avoid ATI video cards, but you knew that anyway. nVidia is indeed the "way to go."

I like AMD processors. They're less expensive than Intels and usually come with more power to the dollar ratio.

With a power supply, RAM, and hard disk: Surplus is GOOD.
