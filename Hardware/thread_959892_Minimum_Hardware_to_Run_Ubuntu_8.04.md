### Post by ardvark71 on 2008-10-27
Hi...

I'm not completely sure what the minimum specs would be for a system to run Ubuntu 8.04 comfortably and quickly, however, I can tell you what didn't work so well.

I had a Pentium III 600 mhz, 384 MB's of memory, 32 GB ATI Radeon 7200 system that lagged with Ubuntu *6.06!* Kubuntu 6.06 ran just fine. I can't tell you the reason, though.

I'm guessing your minimum (for the standard I mentioned above) would be a Pentium 4 or AMD Athlon class (Socket A, 462 pins) system, with at least 512 MB's RAM (more if possible) and preferably a Nvidia graphics card or onboard chipset. :)

Celestia is also a nice program! ;)
