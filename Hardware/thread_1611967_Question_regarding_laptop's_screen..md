### Post by Fire_Chief on 2010-11-02
No, VGA ports on computers are output only. They are not designed to be bi-directional (input/output). The closest you would get to using another laptop's screen to view/use a separate computer's desktop would be to remote into that computer from the laptop (RDP, VNC, XDMCP, etc.).
