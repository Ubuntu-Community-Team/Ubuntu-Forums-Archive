### Post by JoeCarter on 2011-03-01
I also ran into this despite tw68 working for me in the past.
I made the following edits and it now builds (and works) for me.

diff tw68-core.c tw68-core.c.orig 
113c113
< 	videobuf_waiton(&buf->vb, 0, 0);
---
> 	videobuf_waiton(q, &buf->vb, 0, 0);


diff tw68-video.c tw68-video.c.orig
1125c1125
< 			    fh);
---
> 			    fh, &dev->lock);
1131c1131
< 			    fh);
---
> 			    fh, &dev->lock);
