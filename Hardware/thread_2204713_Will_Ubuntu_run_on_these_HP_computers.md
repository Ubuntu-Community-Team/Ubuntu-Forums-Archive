### Post by Bucky Ball on 2014-02-10
Just a point, you have a link to one US model and two links to Australian models. I am in Australia, owned an HP at one point, so be aware that not all models are available everywhere. 

Unless you are in Australia I would check that whatever model you are looking at is readily available where you are. If a model is exclusively made for Australia (or Asia, or the US or UK) it is NOT as easy as just ordering that model and HP will happily accomodate. They will not sell it to you generally. You can only buy the ones for your region for many, and many obvious, reasons. ;)

If you do buy from overseas, by whatever means, be totally certain there is an international warranty on the unit that covers YOUR country. International warranties are sometimes not what they seem.
