### Post by MonkeyPaw on 2012-04-19
Sounds like the graphics were failing. If the graphics were integrated, the board was going bad. If you can say more about the hardware you had, you might just be able to find a replacement board on ebay with similar features for under $100. Generally the CPU is pretty bulletproof unless you've abused it with excess voltage. The repair shop likely doesn't have a suitable motherboard around, so they are selling you a full upgrade (which isn't all bad)

I have swapped motherboards without reinstalling, but it was 2 AMD-based chipsets.
