### Post by weatherman2 on 2016-03-19
Do you mean Suspend (aka "Sleep") where the battery maintains state and the computer should resume instantly or Hibernation, where the computer state is saved to disk, so resume is not instantaneous?

By default, closing the lid should Suspend, not Hibernate.

This is an important distinction, because while Suspend seems pretty reliable for me, Hibernation is notoriously not well supported in Ubuntu and often does not work properly.
