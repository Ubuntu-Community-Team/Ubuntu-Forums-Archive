### Post by victorph on 2011-03-14
Hello, everyone! I tried to install Ubuntu 10.10 on my Sony Vaio CW notebook but after setting the language to "English" and selecting the option "Install Ubuntu" I get a black screen and nothing happens. It seems that the DVD is being read, but I get no response on the screen. Thanks in advance for any help!

Edit: I found the solution [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1471297"); all I had to do was, after selecting the language, press F6 and mark the option "nomodeset".
