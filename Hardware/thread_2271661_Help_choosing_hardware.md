### Post by gordintoronto on 2015-04-01
Editing movies or viewing them?

Sometimes the latest and greatest motherboard will include components which are not yet supported, such as the Ethernet port. Especially if you are thinking of installing 14.04 LTS, which is almost a year old now, this could come up. Same issue arises with video cards.

I look at the reviews on newegg.com, searching for "linux" and "ubuntu".
