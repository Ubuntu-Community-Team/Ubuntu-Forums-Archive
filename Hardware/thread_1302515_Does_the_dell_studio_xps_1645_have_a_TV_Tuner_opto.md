[http://cgi.ebay.ie/Dell-XPS-Avermedia-A306-Mini-Pci-e-TV-Tuner-Card-hybrid-/180529276329?cmd=ViewItem&pt=UK_Computing_Computer_Components_Graphics_Video_TV_Cards_TW&hash=item2a086225a9](http://cgi.ebay.ie/Dell-XPS-Avermedia-A306-Mini-Pci-e-TV-Tuner-Card-hybrid-/180529276329?cmd=ViewItem&pt=UK_Computing_Computer_Components_Graphics_Video_TV_Cards_TW&hash=item2a086225a9)
 
The tuner seems to do DVB-T / Analog and radio. But from what I can see it can only be used in 32bit mode through Windows Media centre. I have bought one myself to have a play and see what can be done. But at £37GBP I couldn't see a reason not to buy it. 
 
Much like you, I couldn't see any references to the card on the Dell site and I can only assume that this must be a regional option that isn't open to Europe. But the card is spec'd to be global coverage, so I can't see a reason why not.
