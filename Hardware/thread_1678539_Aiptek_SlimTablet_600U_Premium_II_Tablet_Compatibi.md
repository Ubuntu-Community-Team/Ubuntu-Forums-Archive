### Post by Favux on 2011-01-30
I believe that is a rebranded Waltop tablet.  If so you can get it working in Maverick with the Wacom drivers.  See the [Waltop HOW TO]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1595648").  You won't be able to use the tablet's hotkeys and right now there may be an issue with both stylus buttons acting the same.  But you do get access to the Wacom pressure sensitivity and other stylus parameters.
