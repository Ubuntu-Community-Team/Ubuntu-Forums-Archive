### Post by akand074 on 2010-12-23
I don't own a BD burner. But it's 1X speed = 36Mb/s = 4.5MB/s. To do the math for you: 

6x Speed
27MB/s
15.43 Minutes for 25 GB
30.86 Minutes for 50 GB

12x Speed (double 6x speed)
54MB/s
7.72 Minutes for 25GB
15.43 Minutes for 50 GB
