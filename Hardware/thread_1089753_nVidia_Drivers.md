### Post by Ng Oon-Ee on 2009-03-08
What they are telling you, and is probably true, is that the problem is not the drivers, its in your xorg.conf file. I just tried an reinstall of 8.04 and realized how bad the usual xorg.conf file generated by nvidia binary is. I'd suggest crafting your own, if you have the time. Otherwise, look for some guides to setting it up. It should at the very least have a Screen, Monitor, and Device section, with the Device specifically using the nvidia driver instead of nv or nvidia-new.
