### Post by Gabix on 2008-10-04
I solved it with the great tools Ubuntu comunity has for us.
This is what I did:

I follwed the steps explained here to remove absolutly all drivers realted with nvidia (tried all just in case):
```

sudo envy --uninstall-all 
sudo dpkg -P envy 

sudo apt-get remove --purge nvidia*
sudo rm /lib/restricted-modules/.nvidia*

sudo nvidia-installer --uninstall

```

Then I installed Envy and used *not* the newest drivers for my card (96.something)
After Envy finished doing its magic I restarted the PC and everthing looked great.
Went to desktop effects, clicked on Normal **AND IT WORKED!!** After that, clicked on Extra and again, everything was perfect.
That´s all. Compiz is working perfectly now, thanks to all for your help.
