Hey all, I have a Thinkpad X31 that i'v been running ubuntu on for a while now.  Since I'v upgraded to 8.10 from 8.04 i'v encountered two problems.  the first is connecting to my college's tkip network, but I fixed that with wicd.  the second is the lack of support for the center scroll button/trackpoint.  The trackpoint mouse works great, as do the left and right buttons, but when the center is depressed I can not scroll with the nub.  In 8.04 I solved this by adding this...

        Option          "EmulateWheel"          "on"
        Option          "EmulateWheelButton"    "2"
        Option 		"YAxisMapping" 		"4 5"
        Option 		"XAxisMapping"	 	"6 7"

in the input section of my xorg.conf file.  I got that from thinkwiki.com  I know that HAL is used now instead of xorg, but is there any way that you think I can get this to work?  You guys have always been a great help, so thanks!
