### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-06-25
It's a 3.5 inch IDE drive.

Both 2.5 and 3.5 inch SATA drives have exactly the same pin layout, this is why you can use a 2.5 inch SATA drive in a 3.5 inch SATA enclosure.
