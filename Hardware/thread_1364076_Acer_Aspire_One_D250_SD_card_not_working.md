### Post by Objekt on 2009-12-25
I got a refurb'd AAO D250 a couple of days ago and put UNR 9.10 on it.  Everything works (as far as I can tell; can't test the wireless since all my neighbors are Grinches)...except the SD card slot.  I put in an SD card that works fine on my other Aspire Ones (D150 models), but nothing happens.  It doesn't pop up on the usual menu, or show up in Gparted.  

Another odd thing I noticed: as long as the SD card is in the slot, the netbook's HDD access light blinks constantly, in a regular pattern, turning on perhaps twice a second.  It is quite different from the sporadic flashing you get from actual HDD access.  As soon as I remove the card, the light stops flashing.

I am able to access the SD card on the D250 by using a USB-to-SD adapter.  It pops right up in the "Volumes" section of the "Files and Folders" area.

Yet another odd thing: An entirely different SD card works straight away.  Just pop it in the slot and up it pops in "Volumes."  No idea why.  FWIW, it's a 1 GB card, whereas the original one referenced above is an 8 GB micro SDHC card.

Do I have a bad card reader - possible, as it's a refurb - or is this a bug in UNR?
