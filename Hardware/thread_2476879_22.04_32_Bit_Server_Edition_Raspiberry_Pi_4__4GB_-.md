The problem now is that I can no longer mount the 18 TB HDD. I have tested this on another Pi, a notebook and a workstation, all say that there is nothing wrong with the hard drive and everything is fine. As I said before the installation, the hard drive was working fine on the Pi. Here are the logs from a clean fsck.ext4.
