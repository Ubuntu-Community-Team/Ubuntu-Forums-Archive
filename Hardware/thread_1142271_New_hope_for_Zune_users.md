### Post by rainwalker on 2009-04-29
I did a fresh install of Jaunty the day it came out, and while configuring it the way I wanted, I discovered that my Zune mounts when I plug it into my computer to charge. It shows up on the desktop, it is recognized as "Microsoft Corp. Zune", the window asking what action should be taken pops up and suggests Rhythmbox (it knows the Zune is a music player), and I can browse the files on the device in Nautilus.
However, I cannot play any of the files (Totem and VLC tell me I don't have access to them). Banshee doesn't see the device unless I plug it in after I've started Banshee, and even then it says that it is ignoring the device because it can only handle one at a time. Also, if I plug it in after starting Banshee and it tries to load it, the Zune no longer shows up on my desktop or anywhere else as mounted. Amarok doesn't see it at all, though that may be because it is Amarok 2 (very lacking in features, and device support may be one of them). 
The "Properties" window also lacks the normal device information for mounted disks.

However, the fact that it shows up at all and I can see the files on it is a huge step forward (I haven't tried moving/deleting/renaming anything on it out of fear that I'll mess something up). 
What would be a good course of action now?
