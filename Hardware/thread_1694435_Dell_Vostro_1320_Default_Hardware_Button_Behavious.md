### Post by Jack Brown on 2012-12-24
simply to leave an updated answer for this thread:

You can use keyboard shortcuts.
in 12.04, type 'keyboard' in the launcher.
in keyboard window, click shortcuts tab
click SOUND and MEDIA on the left panel
click 'Launch Media Player'
press Fn + the play function key on your keyboard.

but you could still achieve coolness without this 8-)
