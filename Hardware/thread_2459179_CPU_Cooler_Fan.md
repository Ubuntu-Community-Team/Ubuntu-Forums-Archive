### Post by QIII on 2021-03-12
The motherboard components get hot.  If you pull air from the mobo side, you'll be pulling warmer air over the heatsink.  If your heat sink is parallel to the board, pull the air from the open space in the case and blow it into the heat sink.  If it is perpendicular blow the air in the direction the air is flowing in the case -- probably front to back.
