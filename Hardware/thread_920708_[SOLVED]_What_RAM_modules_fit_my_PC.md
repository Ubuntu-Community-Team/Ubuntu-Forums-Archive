### Post by issih on 2008-09-15
Whether you can use a higher frequency module will depend on the motherboard you have. As a general rule you can put higher frequency modules in and the motherboard will just run them as fast as it is capable of doing so. This theory doesn't always work out however, and i have seen several reports of people being unable to get laptops to boot using higher frequency modules than recommended for their motherboard.

In short you can be sure 533 modules should work, as for anything else you are taking a bit of a gamble unless you fish out the exact model of your motherboard and take a gander at their supported modules list

Hope that helps
