### Post by paulisdead on 2009-12-07
I've been using a massive Thermaltake Big Typhoon for a few years now, and it's proven to be really effective at cooling, while still being quieter than other things in the system.  It is huge, though, so you need to have the clearance in your case, and in the area on the motherboard surrounding the CPU socket.  The 120mm fan is adjustable, but I run mine at max speed, since I still hear other things in the case over it, even at full speed.

Anything from Zalman tends to be really quiet as well.  That's pretty much what they do.  I've been really happy with the zalman products I've bought over the years.

You might want to mention what sort of case you're putting this into.  Is this for like an HTPC or some sort of compact case, or is it a regular desktop?
