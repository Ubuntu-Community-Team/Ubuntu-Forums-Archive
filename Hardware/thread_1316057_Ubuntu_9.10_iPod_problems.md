Perfectly true, due to the nature of the 'next-gen' devices (read; iPod Touch/iPhone) Rhythmbox can't recognize it as an MP3 player. All other iPod's work fine, but for your Touch you'll either have to [Jailbreak]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dg5FOYgs8Kk") it (What I've done) or use it with another computer. Unfortunately if you want to jailbreak your iPod, you'll need access to a Windows or Mac computer.
