### Post by martintremblay on 2010-04-26
Finally figured this out...

1 - I have been able to link Phone and Ubuntu 9.10 PC by installing and then running  WM5torage on my phone (freeware available here: [http://www.freewareppc.com/communication/wm5torage.shtml](http://www.freewareppc.com/communication/wm5torage.shtml) )
For some reason I had to reboot the phone, but this enabled me to see the storage card on the HTC Touch phone running WM6.1 as a USB storage device so can easily transfer files between the phone and PC 

2 - My Gmail account/email is set up through the phone by simply adding the gmail account via the WM6.1 wizard. You can also do this with Google Sync (its really just using activesync to pull from the google server [http://www.google.com/support/mobile/bin/answer.py?answer=138636&topic=14299](http://www.google.com/support/mobile/bin/answer.py?answer=138636&topic=14299))

3 - My Contacts and Calendar are set up using google sync (same link as above [http://www.google.com/support/mobile/bin/answer.py?answer=138636&topic=14299](http://www.google.com/support/mobile/bin/answer.py?answer=138636&topic=14299))

4 - I still cannot sync google notes with my phone... hopefully google sync gets it together and addresses this issue. for now I live without or email my notes back and forth.
