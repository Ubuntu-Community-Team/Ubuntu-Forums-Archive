### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2016-05-13
You have audio drivers (or else you wouldn't hear sound). Get more info: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Audio/AlsaInfo](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Audio/AlsaInfo)
Some laptop manufacturers are taking standard HDA codecs and tweaking things like speaker placement and God knows what else, and then bundling proprietary sound "enhancement" software that only works on Windows. Hopefully, yours is not one of them.
