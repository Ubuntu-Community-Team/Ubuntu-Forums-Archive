### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-10-01
You don't need to worry if you bought a new Laptop as you can always go for the warranty claim :-)

When does the "click" noise actually come out? When you hit the touchpad? Or when clicking a specific application? Or just randomly?

The noise that is often heard with a bad HDD sound like "Click, Clickkkkk, Clickkkkkk" rather than just "Click".

Check your HDD for bad sectors and if it has got any, return it to the point of purchase.
