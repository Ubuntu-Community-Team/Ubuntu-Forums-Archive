### Post by davidryderuk on 2012-09-17
Hi,
Sometimes an Android phone won't appear on your desktop without first having to be configured. They can support a number of different ways of communicating with your computer. One of the easier methods is to setup the phone to act as a Memory Stick, and copy files using the file manager. 

Steps for configuring phones vary across Manufactures and Android versions. If you haven't already, perhaps you could try Method 2 on Samsung's website?

[http://www.samsung.com/us/article/how-to-transfer-media-to-your-android-phone](http://www.samsung.com/us/article/how-to-transfer-media-to-your-android-phone)
