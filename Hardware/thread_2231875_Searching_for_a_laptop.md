### Post by Ratscallion on 2014-06-29
Honestly nowadays I reckon you could get away with pretty much anything. Sometimes you might be a bit more unlucky and have to find some drivers for compatibility with a completely obscure Wifi card or something but it's probably going to work out of the box. (Personal story: 8.04 I needed to install extra Wifi drivers, come 9.10 it was ready from the get-go).
If it meets (okay, very exceeds) the minimum requirements for Ubuntu and is a fairly well-known brand, it's probably going to work with Ubuntu. If something doesn't work, come here, or try a different Linux OS (Arch, Debian, whatever suits).
