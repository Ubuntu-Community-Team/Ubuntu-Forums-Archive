### Post by QIII on 2014-11-04
Thinkpenguin.com is a great place to find things that will work with Linux and they have a 14 day return policy which I understand is quite liberal -- although I have never needed to  return anything.  Wireless devices are some of the most difficult hardware in the Linux world.  They have firmware driven wireless items specifically made for Linux.  If you want to use them with Windows, the tables are turned:  you have to go out and track down drivers.
