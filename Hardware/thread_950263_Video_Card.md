If I were you I would go with nvidia.  There drivers work better in linux then ATI.  Of course an open source driver is being developed for ATI now that they have released the specs so this could change soon.  ASUS and EVGA are both good companies and Newegg is usually the best place to order parts from.  They always ship on time and they don't give you a hassle if you have to return a defective part.  Search the forums for the particular card before you make your decision to make sure nobody is having serious problems with it (or do a google search with 'your card' + linux).
