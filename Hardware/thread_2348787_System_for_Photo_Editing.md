### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-01-07
You are unlikely to get a lot of help direct from most UK companies when it comes to Linux and Ubuntu support.
However, are you aware of the **pcspecialist Linux forum** at [https://www.pcspecialist.co.uk/forums/forumdisplay.php?32-Linux](https://www.pcspecialist.co.uk/forums/forumdisplay.php?32-Linux) which I found very useful when I was about to purchase my desktop machine from them.  I suggest you register with the forum and ask about the hardware you've chosen; you may get some very useful answers as well from those who have already done this or at least tried it.

I posted my intended hardware list to the forum, copied and printed from their configurator, and got several replies from other users suggesting minor changes in order to avoid potential bottlenecks in performance, which I made.

I purchased it just over three years ago. That machine is still my main computer and has been faultless, apart from a faulty card reader, which they replaced immediately, and even allowed me to open the case and do the replacement myself. From the start I have been running Xubuntu, my preferred OS, 12.04 at the start, followed by 14.04 and now 16.04, all LTS versions, but it also runs any of the other *ubuntu family with no problem in VirtualBox.
