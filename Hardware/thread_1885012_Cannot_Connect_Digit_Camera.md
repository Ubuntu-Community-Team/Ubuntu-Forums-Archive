Just wanted to pass on some insight.  I had trouble connecting my Kodak EasyShare C-195.  Plug in and turn on camera, and it would turn off.  Tried everything.
   Added in the libhd16 library.  Checked camera set-up.  Chose the 'Use other Software' instead of Kodak Easyshare.
  Works now.
