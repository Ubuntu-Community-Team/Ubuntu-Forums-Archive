### Post by herrdeh on 2010-09-03
Hi everybody,

I tried to make my girl happy with trashing Wintendo ME and donating her a brand new linux on her old box - and it ended in a disaster.

xubuntu 7.03 worked like a charm out of the box - so I did a reinstall of 10.04 - which came to hang quite after reboot.

I found that the Trident Cyberblade Ai1 graphics chip needs an entry GRUB_cmdline_LINUX="i915.modeset=0" into /etc/default/grub to make the machine boot at all.

Now the boot process comes to the point where the xserver should start - but it seems to fail.

I found a quite nice HOWTO to get around this:

[http://michaelminn.com/linux/toshiba1800/]("http://michaelminn.com/linux/toshiba1800/")

I tried the modifide sorg.conf, but it didnt work - the machine still hangs with a dark screen after most of the boot process is through.

The DDC thing still seems to be an issue, as this is the last entry in the Xorg.0.log


Any help will be appreciated.

Greetings,

herrdeh

### Post by herrdeh on 2010-09-04
SOLVED.

I did quite a stupid mistake: Copied the xorg.conf file from my Mac - and that contained tabstops.

With removed tabstops everything is fine.

Greetings,

herrdeh
