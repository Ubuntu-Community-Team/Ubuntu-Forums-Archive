### Post by Zeedok on 2009-07-16
Here are the specs of my Mythbuntu (since upgraded to 9.04) HTPC.  I spent a little more than you  ($1800AUD, but ~$300 of that was for an XP licence, don't you hate DRM video!!):

Antec Micro Fusion 350 HTPC Case
Intel Core 2 Duo E8400 CPU 3.0 GHz
Gigabyte GA-EG41M-S2H Mainboard
4GB DDR2 PC6400 (2x2GB) Dual Channel RAM
8X Dual Layer DVD+RW + 20X DVD+-RW + CD-RW Combo Drive
1TB 7200RPM SATA HDD
512MB NVIDIA Geforce 8400GS
Gigabit Ethernet
Hauppauge Nova-T-500 MCE Dual DVBT SD/HD tuner

It is brilliant (after some teething problems)

Hope this helps.

### Post by DownTown22 on 2009-07-16
First of all, nice to see another Canadian on the forums!

Second, for components, I recommend checking out Newegg.  Their prices are usually better than places like TigerDirect or NCIX.

Thirdly, here's my HTPC setup: (I can't remember the exact details of everything though)

Case - Thermaltake Lanbox Lite
MoBo - DFI Lanparty JR with an AMD chipset
CPU - AMD Phenom II X3 710
RAM - 2x2GB G-Skill DDR3
Video - 512MB XFX 9600GT (or maybe 9800GT, can't remember right now)
DVD - Lite-On DVD burner
Power Supply - OCZ 600W

I spent a little more on the MoBo than originally planned, but I was getting rather picky....Total, it was about $900.
[I]
EDIT:  You could probably go with a lower CPU and MoBo and save a 1-2 hundred dollars.[/I]

*EDIT 2:  I also have a 500GB Samsung HDD.*
