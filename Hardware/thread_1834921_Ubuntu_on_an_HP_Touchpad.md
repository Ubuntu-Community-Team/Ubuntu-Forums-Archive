LXDE was simple to install and configure. Here is a screenshot of libreoffice and the touchpad onscreen keyboard (also works with a bluetooth keyboard):

[IMG]http://i72.photobucket.com/albums/i195/texaskev2005/xserver_2011-28-08_094033.png[/IMG]

Ubuntu runs as a card within webOS, so my webOS apps are within easy reach:

[IMG]http://i72.photobucket.com/albums/i195/texaskev2005/xserver_2011-28-08_094143.png[/IMG]

I also have synaptic package manager, gimp, chromium and terminator working well on the Touchpad. Not bad for 99 greenbacks.
