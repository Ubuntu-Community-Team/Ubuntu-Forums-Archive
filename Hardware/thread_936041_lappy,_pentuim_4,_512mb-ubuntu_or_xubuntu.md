Either should run fine on your computer. In my experience, the performance boost of Xubuntu over Ubuntu is over-rated; I'd estimate it to be about 10-20% faster. It comes down to which do you prefer, Gnome or Xfce? You could even install both (using 'sudo aptitude install ubuntu-desktop' or 'sudo aptitude install xubuntu-desktop') and switch back and forth until you decide.

Good luck!
