### Post by b18b on 2016-04-23
This is a fresh install of 16.04 on a new Yoga 900.  The install went fine and everything seems to be working so far except when resuming from wake/suspend, the trackpad is not working.  The only way I can get it to work again is open Mouse & Trackpad settings and turn off, then on the trackpad toggle.

Any suggestions would be awesome.

---

### Post by jpereira on 2016-05-15
Pressing ALT+CTRL+F1 and then ALT+CTRL+F7 gets the trackpad to work again by going to the console and then back to graphical mode. Haven't found a more peermanent solution yet.
