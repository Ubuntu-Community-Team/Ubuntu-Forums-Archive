### Post by nos676 on 2010-07-06
I am running Ubuntu 10.04 x64 in favor of Windows 7 Ultimate X64
my problem is when i update Ubuntu with the update manager; GRUB dies, as in it shows GRUB bash on reboot, and when i try to boot grub is just showing GRUB bash and i can't do anything.

i even disabled the updates for grub-common and grub-pc from update manager and when i do the update and reboot grub is dead..

i am using my new ASUS N61J
MB Ver: N61JQ
N61JQ-JX002V

### Post by nos676 on 2010-07-08
I got it fixed (courtesy of my friend Dan who is a coding genius)
i ran update manager
than when it says reboot now or later i chose later
than i went into terminal and ran sudo grub-mkconfig
that fixed grub after that update as the update broke my grub initially.
so i reboot and voila it works!
