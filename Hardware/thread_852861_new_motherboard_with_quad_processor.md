### Post by tribaal on 2008-07-08
Sweet motherboard :)

As ProbablyX mentioned, there should be no reason why this wouldn't work with linux. Motherboards, like CPUs and harddrives are OS agnostic, they adopt only low level conventions (x86, ARM, Sparc... etc).

So with a motherboard for x86 processors, you're good to go.

:)

Other peripherals like video cards, sound cards, WiFi card need specific drivers to work properly, on the other hand. So while you can buy a motherboard and a chip with your eyes closed, you might want to make sure the rest of the system will support your favorite OS :)

Hope this helps
