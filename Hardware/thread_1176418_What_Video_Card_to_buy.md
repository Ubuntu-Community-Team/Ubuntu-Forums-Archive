### Post by barisurum on 2009-06-02
You'd choose between open source driver support (ati, in development) and good proprietary support (nvidia, robust nowadays). I personally chose the open source path when upgrading from x1650xt to x1950xt, because proprietary support is tricky in that, with every kernel or xserver update proprietary drivers may break but open source drivers will allways be easy to maintain, develop and adapt to new features in different platforms ( ie: wine, virtualisation, network GPU sharing, opencl support etc. ).
