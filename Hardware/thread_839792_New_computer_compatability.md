Just a quick question:I'm curious as to why you would opt for a more powerful GFX card instead of a dual SLI setup. Any particular reason(s)?

---

### Post by tamoneya on 2008-06-26
well things dont scale linearly.  If you take two GFX cards that get 30 FPS on game X you wont get 60 FPS when you SLI them.  So I think it is more cost efficient and energy efficient to have just one good card instead of two bad cards.  Now you may ask why do you go with a low clock speed quad core instead of a high end dual core according to the same logic (I have a quad core if you look at my sig).  The reason is because a GPU is a many cored processor.  It is not uncommon for current GPUs to have 128 cores and when you are at 128 cores the non-linearity has more effect.
