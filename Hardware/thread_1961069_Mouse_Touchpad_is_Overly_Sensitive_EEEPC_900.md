### Post by Irihapeti on 2012-05-02
I have an EeePC 900 and I find that the tap-to-click on the touchpad is way too sensitive. I find it unusable and just disable it altogether.

In ordinary 10.04, which I used until recently, you can do this in the Mouse settings. There's a similar setting in 12.04.

If the mouse buttons don't work, it might be worth getting a cheap USB mouse so that you can go on using it. That's what I use most of the time.
