### Post by yancek on 2017-07-09

Not sure how you are doing the copying but if you are doing it in a terminal, the simplest way to avoid a problem with a directory/file with a space in it's name is to put the entire path in quotes or double quotes.  Even simpler, don't put spaces in directory/file names.  In the output you posted, you have 3 spaces in the filename.

> Photo Ian DeSouza 50kb.jpg

I would not trust using a drive, particularly if it is a spinning drive that has been dropped on a hard surface from any significant height.  Then again, it might last for years.
