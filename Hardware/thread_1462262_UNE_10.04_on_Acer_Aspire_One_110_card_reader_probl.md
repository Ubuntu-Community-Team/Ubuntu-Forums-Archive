### Post by arscariosus on 2010-04-25
Anyone knows how to make the right card reader *hot swappable*? I used to add a simple script copied from a tutorial to */etc/modprobe.d* although I'm not sure if this will work with Lucid Lynx since if I understand it correctly, HAL is removed from 10.04.

Any ideas would be appreciated. Btw, I do not have any problems on the card reader at the left side. Though I use it as a permanent slot for my 16gb sdhc. Thanks.

**UPDATE:** fixed by editing the ```
GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX_DEFAULT=
```line that could be found on */etc/default/grub to *```
GRUB_CMDLINE_LINUX_DEFAULT="pciehp.pciehp_force=1 elevator=noop quiet  splash"
``` Credits go to Subban, thanks dude.
