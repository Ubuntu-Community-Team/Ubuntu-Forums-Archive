### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-03-13
Check to see if the memory stick is in the correct slot and that it is seated all the way down. Is the power cable to the motherboard 20 or 24 pins?  Make sure that is seated too. Most new motherboards have a 4 pin cable and connector on the motherboard that powers the cpu.  Some power supplies have an 8 pin plug at the end of a cable that can be used for an 8 pin connect when needed or you use 4 of the pins to go to the cpu power female connection, it is configured so it can only go in one way.
