### Post by magic8ball on 2011-10-12
Hi,

First off it sounds like a good cleaning might be in order.  Check for dust buildup around the ventilation locations and try vacuuming them out.  Maybe there is a processor cover plate on the bottom that can be removed to give better access.

If that doesn't work, it is possible that a component is going bad and becoming susceptible to overheating.

Good Luck.
