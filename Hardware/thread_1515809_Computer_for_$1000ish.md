### Post by cj.surrusco on 2010-06-24
You can build a more than decent desktop for far less than $1000. 

As far as Intel vs AMD, it depends on what you want. Intel is going to be more expensive, but the performance will probably be greater. However, AMD has very good cost effectiveness with quad core models for less than $150.
