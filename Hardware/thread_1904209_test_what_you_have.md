### Post by 2F4U on 2012-01-04
The most basic tests would probably be memtest to verify that the RAM is properly working. Additionally, there are so called stress test suites, which put different components of your machine under pressure (cpu, hdd, graphics card).

### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-05
If you're looking for an actual test suite, I don't know of any that run under Linux.

For some ideas of what to test, you can Google for various laptop makes and models and will find some online reviews.  Look through the various things they have tried to get an idea regarding what you can test.

Also, hibernation/suspend/sleep is flaky at best with Linux.  If you are having specific problems with that, then post a thread specific to that -- don't start adding problems to the same thread.
