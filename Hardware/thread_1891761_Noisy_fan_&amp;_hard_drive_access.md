### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-12-06
What is "normal"???

But seriously ... there have been lots of reports of overheating with PCs using 11.10, and have not seen the kernel patches for fixing this coming out yet.

So at present, unfortunately, getting overheating with 11.10 is more "normal" than it would be otherwise.

There are utilities that can be used to slow down the fan -- but with a laptop, I would strongly advise AGAINST using them, as since the fan is trying to cool down the CPU, slowing down the fan is only going to cause it to overheat and burn out.

The hard drive access is "normal" too.  Where do you think the PC is writing all of objects that it's downloading from the Internet to render the web pages?
