### Post by TheFu on 2022-11-25
Use a different flash drive.  

Some flash drives aren't compatible with some systems, some ports. Wish I could tell you which are and aren't, but I've seen cheapos work and fail and I've seen multiple well-regarded branded flash devices fail and work too.  No pattern is clear.  Try a different one is my only suggestion.

### Post by daniell59 on 2022-12-13
I finally solved the problem. In the BIOS I disabled the Floppy drive. I had removed it years ago. After that it booted from the Flash Drive.
