### Post by ZaHACKieL on 2009-08-12
I would say you should look for Dell or Toshiba.
I have ATI video card and I don't have any problems with it but, for what I've read, ATI presents more problems with Ubuntu than Nvidia.
What I've had is, sometimes, conflicts between ATI and Compiz but I managed to solve them. Those are conflicts I haven't seen with Nvidia.

Dell network/wifi cards and stuff usually work fine with Ubuntu, at least as far as I've seen.

A lovely and useful thing to have is the backlit keyboard, I love that stuff.

About sound...mm...i've seen toshiba laptops with subwoofer and stuff like that but I don't know how good they are. I prefer to use my laptop with very good headphones like Sennheiser or Bose.

Screen? mmm....I would only buy LED screens, they are great. I like big screens, 17" ...if you like that you can get it in Toshiba and Dell. They even have a 18.X" in toshiba but that's too big for me, I'm fine with 17"

I would also pay for 2 or 3 warranty years, it's worth it.
