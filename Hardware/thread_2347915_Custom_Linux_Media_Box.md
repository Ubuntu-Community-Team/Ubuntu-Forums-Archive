### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-12-29
I use Kodi myself for watching/listening installed in a *buntu, and not sure what you mean by 'Linux Media Box' or 'Android box'. What exactly do you require the media box to do? As for the remote control, Flirc can deal with that for you. 

Have a [look here]("https://flirc.tv/more/flirc-usb").

For media on Ubuntu, [a Raspberry Pi]("https://www.raspberrypi.org/") may cover what you need. They are cheap. I was using an Odroid for awhile too and that worked great but not as much support as the RPi.

Hope that helps in some way.
