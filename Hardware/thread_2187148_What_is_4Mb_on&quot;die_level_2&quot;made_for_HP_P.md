### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2013-11-11
> What is 4Mb on"die level 2"made for? 
Don't worry about it. It's just onboard cache on the CPU to make it faster (so it doesn't have to query RAM as often). The OS doesn't need to do anything special to support CPU cache.

All of that stuff you list is pretty standard fare and will be supported on Saucy or Trusty (and maybe earlier versions).
