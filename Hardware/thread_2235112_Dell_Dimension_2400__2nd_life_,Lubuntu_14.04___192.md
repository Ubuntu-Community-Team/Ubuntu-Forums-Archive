### Post by josef6 on 2014-07-19
The support stop of Window XP made me think ,  hm ...what to do  : 
-  go for new PC with Win8 ... scary !
-  move to Mac ... but I'm not so religious - didn't want to join  the apple church

Then thought why not  try with Linux ... so ended up with Lubuntu ...!  It runs very fast and smooth on my old Dell . Next thing was to go for a 1920 x 1080 monitor .... but so far under  XP the onboard 82845g could never support HD resolution . In some forums I saw people even upgrading their Dimension 2400 with PCI graphics cards  to reach  1920 x 1080.

Hey , but I need to report here      **1920 x 1080  with the original intel 82845g on board graphic works with Lubuntu 14.04 . Right out of the box !** Sure I didn&#8217;t try games or so ...but for surfing  and photo editing it's still very smooth . With my new Dell S2740L monitor  it feels like working at a Thunderbolt monitor or 27" iMAC . Photos look really stunning with  the glossy  S2740L .   Even fonts look sharper to me than on my old 19" Eizo 1280 x1024 .

Really having fun again now with my old Dell  Dimension 2400  and his 2nd life  !   Long live Linux and Lubuntu  ;-) 


Josef

photo browsing with Geeqie  ( very fast ...)
photo editing Gimp ( of course )  , but also my old Photoshop CS2  (with wine) still works fine !
