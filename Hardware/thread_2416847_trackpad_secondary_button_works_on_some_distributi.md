### Post by moontan2 on 2019-04-16
I recently obtained a new travel laptop (Lenovo 11e, i3) to replace a wimpier model (Celeron based).
Upon installing Ubuntu, I noticed that the right trackpad button does not work properly.  It is simply treated as the primary/left button. It does not work with elementaryOS either. 
 It does work properly with Zorin, Deepin and Wind*ws, so it's not a mechanical issue.
I didn't have these issues on the previous laptop.  While I can use a two-finger tap to simulate the secondary button, it's not an ideal solution.

Is there a driver or configuration item that may correct this?

---

### Post by moontan2 on 2019-04-19
I was able to resolve the issue using gnome-tweaks (Keyboard & Mouse -> Mouse Click Emulation -> Area)
