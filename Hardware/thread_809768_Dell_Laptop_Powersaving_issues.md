### Post by Sam Lars on 2008-05-27
You can use the Fn+Arrows to adjust the brightness, and there is also a setting in the Power Preferences to dim the screen when on battery.  I've noticed that after coming back from battery it won't go to what I had it on before, and some applications automatically set their own brightness for some reason, but other than that it works fine.
