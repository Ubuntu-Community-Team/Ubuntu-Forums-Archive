### Post by limpangel on 2008-10-07
Funny thing.
I'm writing this down on my HP 6715s laptop with Ubuntu 8.04.1 installed. :)
All I needed was a Ubuntu 8.04.1 Hardy Heron i386 desktop cd.
Live cd worked (without wireless though). 
Install went without problems.
All you need is a wired internet connection and it will automaticly install the necesary ATI(video) and Broadcom(wireless) Proprietary drivers after the first reboot.
Everithing works great now 3D desktop, wireless (good signal by the way), bluetooth, etc.

I even updated the wiki page related to this. Check it out.
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam/HPCompaq6715s](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam/HPCompaq6715s)

Hopefully in the future more laptops will be as compatible as this one.

Laptop configuration:
AMD Turion X2 TL-62 (2100 MHz)
2GB RAM
ATI Radeon X1250 (128MB shared)
160 GB HDD Fujitsu
DVD-RW LightScribe
Wireless from Broadcom (IEEE 802.11 b/g)
