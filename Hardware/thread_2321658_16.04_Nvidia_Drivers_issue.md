### Post by roler2 on 2016-04-26
Please excuse me if I am mistaken, however I believe the Kernel/Graphics issues started with the Release of 14.04.2, which included a Kernel Update. The original 14.04 Default Kernel 3.13 worked well with many Graphics, such as NVidia and especially Intel. It wasn't until the upgraded Kernel that was a part of the 14.04.2 Upgrade that the Graphics issues started. Since then the Graphics issues have worsened, especially with certain Intel, such as the i1915 Series and other Intel Chipsets. As I have been posting, with 16.04, I have gone from continual Desktop freezes with very poor Font Rendering with the Lubuntu 16.04 Beta to a completely black screen with blinking cursor, no boot data and no fix as yet with the 16.04 final release.
