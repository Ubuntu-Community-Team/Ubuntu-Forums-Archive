### Post by smsmith050 on 2008-06-24
The Tl52 is an exposed die dual core CPU. You can either go with phase change thermal interface or grease. Phase change material is recommended for exposed die processors. 
Attached is a list of suggested phase change materials for exposed die processors. 
There is some concern with grease affecting the reliability of exposed die processors. If you choose grease then Dow Corning TC-5022 or Shin Etsu 7783D are suggested.
