### Post by snetmonster on 2008-07-31
Hi all,

I just wanted to tell u that after 1 year of searching about my problem of no sound in UBUNTU and after trying 10001 operation including kernel recompiling and other complicated stuff i finally unmuted my PC.

I'm so happy then, Well all I did is reflashing the Bios, it is a common bug in my PC series and now they've fixed that problem in the new Bios version.

So if u have a Toshiba P100-233 (PSPA3E) and u don't have sound in UBUNTU just update ur bios to the last version and reload, u'll hear it drumping (I really heard it) 

Nice, after 1 year waiting for this.
