### Post by cascade9 on 2010-01-26
SATA1 is about as fast as the theoretical limit of PCI, and SATA2 i much faster than PCI. If you can get SATA2, for the same cost, you might as well go for it, but I would'nt pay any extra for it. 

As for brands, not much difference there either. Linux support is the only thing you really need.
