### Post by Marc Higgins on 2008-07-18
I have suddenly started to have problems using headphones on a dell inspiron 640m/e1405 laptop running ubuntu 8.04 kernel 2.6.24-19-generic. 

Sounds still works through the laptop speakers when the headphones are unplugged but won't play through the headphone jack when headphones are plugged in. The headphone jack works OK in windows & live distros & as far as I can tell the headphone jack is not mute anywhere. The problem seems to exists regardless of the desktop, ubuntu, xubuntu, icewm. 

Occasionally when I plug the headphones in the sound kind of works but it sounds really dirty, almost as if 1/2 the sound tracks from the recording we missing, often it is the voice track that seems to be missing. This happens intermittently & despite trying I can't recreate this with any reliability. If I remove the plug completely the sound reverts to the laptop speakers & when I plug it back in both the laptop speakers & the headphones are mute.

I normally have a USB voip phone plugged into the machine, but it doesn't seem to make any difference if it is removed.

I would really appreciate some help with this, below is some more background information.
