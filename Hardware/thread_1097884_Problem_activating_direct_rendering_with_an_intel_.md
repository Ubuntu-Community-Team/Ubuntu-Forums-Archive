### Post by ksraju007 on 2010-08-30
I had a similar issue in lucid. I was getting segfault on running glxinfo. I installed 0ad and also billiard-gl games.. both were failing. I went through multiple forums and things.. 

This is my display card information.
$lspci |grep VGA
00:02.0 VGA compatible controller: Intel Corporation 82Q963/Q965 Integrated Graphics Controller (rev 02)

i was getting this error in Xorg.0.log :
(EE) GLX error: Can not get required symbols.

here is what i did to solve the issue...
$sudo apt-get remove --purge xorg-driver-fglrx
$sudo apt-get remove fglrx*
$sudo apt-get remove virtualbox-ose-guest*

reinstalled mesa dri and glx
$sudo apt-get install --reinstall libgl1-mesa-glx libgl1-mesa-dri


then, I restarted the computer. 
$sudo reboot

this worked for me ! :D
