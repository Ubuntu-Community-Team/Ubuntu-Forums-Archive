### Post by kurt18947 on 2020-05-10
If you haven't purchased the WiFi adapter yet, I'd recommend holding off and doing a bit of research first. WiFi can be the biggest challenge in purchasing Ubuntu compatible components. I imagine if you were to search the wireless and networking forum here you'd find what works or at least what doesn't, or is difficult to enable. You could also look on thinkpenguin.com for hardware that has a high likelihood of working on Linux systems. You could also check deviwiki.com, formerly wikidevi which has been useful in the past. I don't know about its current state.
