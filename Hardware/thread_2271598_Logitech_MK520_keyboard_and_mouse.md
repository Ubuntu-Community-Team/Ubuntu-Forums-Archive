### Post by lz1dsb on 2015-03-31
I just wanted to share with the community my pleasant experience with this product and Ubuntu: Logitech MK520 and a wireless mouse M310, both running with a Unifying receiver. 
It works just out of the box, amazing :) 
I also used this nice little application called Solaar, which recognized the receiver and does a great job at managing which wireless devices connect to it. 
Now I can have multiple keyboards and mouses connected to the Unifying receiver - cool :)
