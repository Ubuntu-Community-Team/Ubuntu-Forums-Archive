Yes, it is.  It got its name by being and keeping the distro installation at 50 meg.  They even have a book out about it with some ideas for using it.  I have it installed on an old 450 mhz laptop and it works pretty well there.

I haven't used Puppy so I can't compare the two.  Maybe someone else that has will pitch in.
