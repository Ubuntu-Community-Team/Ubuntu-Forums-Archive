### Post by kyphi on 2009-03-03
You can associate the buttons on your keyboard with Ubuntu functions in "Keyboard Shortcuts" (System, Preferences).

Extra mouse buttons cannot be set as far as I know.

As for the erratic behaviour, try connecting the transmitter to the computer with a shielded cable so that you can move it closer to the position of the keyboard and mouse.  Plugging it into the back of the computer leads to signal transmission problems.
