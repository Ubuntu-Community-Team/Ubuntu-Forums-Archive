### Post by ahallubuntu on 2012-07-08
What matters more than motherboard brand is CHIPSET brand and model.  What is the chipset used on your friend's motherboard?  (At least, what is the motherboard make/model so you can find out the chipset it uses?)  Find out then google for info about how well that chipset is supported in Linux.  The more recent the chipset, the less likely it is to have great support in Linux.
