### Post by carl.greco on 2011-10-14
I'm assuming you are using the kde windowing environment since Synaptiks is a kde app. I have a similar problem with unity in 11.10.  Touchpad worked OK on my HP G60-230US until the screen counter timed out and locked the screen.  I disabled the option to lock the touchpad while typing (in System Settings) - so far touchpad is operational.
