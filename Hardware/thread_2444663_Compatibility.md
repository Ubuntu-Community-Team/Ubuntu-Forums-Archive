### Post by CelticWarrior on 2020-06-02
Welcome.

Yes and no. Your 32-bit CPU is nowadays a major roadblock. The 32-bit architecture is supported (with standard Ubuntu 18.04) until 2023 or only 2021 if using flavors/derivatives like Ubuntu-Budgie 18.04.

In a nutshell, nothing in the Ubuntu "family" has currently good support for 32-bit. Use Debian instead or many other distros, just not Ubuntu or Ubuntu-based.
