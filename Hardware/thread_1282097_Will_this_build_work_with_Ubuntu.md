### Post by bark50 on 2009-10-04
I've only done two Ubuntu builds from scratch (plus periodic hardware upgrades), so I'm no expert.  In general, I've had no problems with RAM, power supplies, DVD drives, or hard drives from any manufacturer.  My current build runs a Phenom tri-core CPU.  It's not the exact one you're considering, but it works fine.  I can't really comment on your motherboard since I've always used MSI.

My biggest hardware problems have been with video cards.  I think an nVidia chipset is the way to go, and that's what you have with your GeForce.  Just be sure after you're up and running to enable the drivers for the card (go to System &#8594; Administration &#8594; Hardware Drivers).
