### Post by pricetech on 2011-06-13
You don't say which version of Ubuntu you are using, but here's what I'd do under Lucid.

System - Administration - Printing
Click the Add button
Click the Plus sign next to Network Printer
Select Find Network Printer
Type in the IP Address of the printer and click Find

Should be pretty much intuitive from there.  If not, let us know where you get stuck.
