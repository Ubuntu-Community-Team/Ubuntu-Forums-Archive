### Post by khelben1979 on 2009-07-08
If you compile your own Linux kernel you have the possibility to optimize it for your processor. This includes support for MMX instructions afaik.

Other than that you can look for another kernel within your [Synaptic Package Manager]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Synaptic_package_manager") to see if they have one of these MMX optimized kernels.
