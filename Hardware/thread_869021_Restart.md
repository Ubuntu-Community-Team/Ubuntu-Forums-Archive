### Post by evets25 on 2008-07-24
instead of alt+sysreq+b, you should use alt+sysreq+reisub. That is, press and hold alt+sysreq, then keep holding them and type (slowly) r e i s u b . Each one of these keys does a different thing that will prepare your computer to shut down a little bit more safely, such as doing an emergency unmount, and syncing filesystems. This key combo ALWAYS works, even on a system that is completely hung and non-responsive.
