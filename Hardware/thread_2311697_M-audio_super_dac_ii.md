### Post by yoshii on 2016-01-29
I took a look at the skimpy "user guide" PDF file from M-Audio.com and it does NOT mention USB Class Compliance but it does mention needing Windows drivers.  My guess is that it isn't compatible with Linux because M-Audio usually mentions USB Class Compliance for their devices such as the KeyStation MIDI Controller series in their PDF manuals.  Devices that are USB Class Compliant don't need drivers and are automatically recognized by Windows, MacOS, and Linux.  

But to make sure, what I would do is contact M-Audio support and ask them.
