### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-05-21
I think, first, you need to make sure the device you connect, the cable and the usb port (in short, all the hardware) functions properly, and if that's the case, move on to the driver part. Checking the driver on malfunctioning hardware is pretty pointless. You'll get errors for sure, or may find that the driver isn't loaded, in case USB gets no electric current.
