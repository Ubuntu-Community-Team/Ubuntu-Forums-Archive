### Post by hjacker on 2009-05-30
Hey!
I've just tried all this stuff and it became useless, because my VAIO for example has ALPS touchpad.
It came up to be extremely easy.
I'm on Jaunty 9.04.
Click System - Settings(Preferences) - Mouse - Touchpad and untick "Enable Touchpad"
Touchpad refuses to work. Tick again and it's back in the game. That's all.
