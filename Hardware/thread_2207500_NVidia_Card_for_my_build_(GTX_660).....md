### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2014-02-23
I haven't seen any specific complaints about the GTX 660, and I generally have recommended it to people on these forums. I can't say I have first-hand experience with it, because I personally use "low end" passively-cooled cards with a focus on good video acceleration capabilites.

To me, you only have a few options:
1. Get the GTX 660.
2. Wait for an equivalent part based on Maxwell architecture. The Maxwell-based GTX 750(Ti) was recently introduced, and its power efficiency is promising, but who knows when Nvidia will roll out more powerful cards based on that architecture.
3. Step down to the 750Ti or up to the 760.
