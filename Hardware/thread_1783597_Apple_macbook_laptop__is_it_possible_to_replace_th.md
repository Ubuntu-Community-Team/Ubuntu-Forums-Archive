### Post by tgalati4 on 2011-06-16
Most laptop batteries are good for 300 charge/discharge cycles or 3 years whichever comes first.  So with your usage pattern, I would think that's about right.

Apple wants you to throw non-working Apple products away and buy new Apple products.  "It's the Apple Way".  Since you received it as a gift, it's the gift that keeps on taking.

I bet your next laptop will have a replaceable battery.  I have an old Mac powerbook and I rotate between two batteries every 3 months and they seem to last longer that way (~5 years now).  

Dropping the laptop can shorten the battery life as well.
