### Post by TheFu on 2021-02-05
> **MibunoOokami said:**
> The answer was another 4.5 hours. Seems excessively long for a brand new HDD, but it is what it is.

Can't just blame the HDD, but the interface and CPU are involved as well.

If it was connected by USB2, it will take much longer than USB3.  If the drive is external and didn't also have extra power connected - i.e. it was only powered by the USB port, then it will always be slow, regardless of the interface. Almost all external, USB, 2.5inch portable HDDs are really, really, really, slow.  Some USB ports don't provide sufficient power, but the HDDs either work or they don't. As a hardware vendor, most decide that if they can possibly work, they will, regardless of speed.
