### Post by WinRiddance on 2010-11-15
Did you check several different websites? Sometimes choppiness is directly related to the type of pages being viewed (video clips in particular), the actual internet access (especially if your access is wifi as opposed to direct cable connection), the amount of open tabs that are working at the same time, and/or the time of day that you're accessing certain multimedia pages that may be viewed by lots of other people at the very same time.

I'm pretty sure that your choppiness is directly related to the internet connection itself ... **somehow**. Close some of your open tabs (if applicable) or try installing chromium web browser from the software center and compare the same pages and their performance. Before doing that you can also try restarting your system, giving it the opportunity to refresh a lot of settings. Good luck.
