My touchpad for no reason locked up and stopped working along with its buttons. This seems to affect lots of people, although I'm sure there are different causes/solutions

I got mine working again by locking the screen (brining up the black screen and password request) and then unlocking it again. Might work for someone else.

I run Ubuntu 10.04 on an old ac*e*r Aspire 3660. Hope to have helped.
