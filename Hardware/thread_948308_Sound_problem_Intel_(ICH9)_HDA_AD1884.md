### Post by da451 on 2009-01-07
Same exact problem here. 

For what its worth, if you go into the Volume Control properties and change the Output device to "ALSA PCM on front: 0 (AD198x Analog) via DMA" you can get audio out of the headset jack on the front of the machine.

Works for me since all I need is audio for headphones anyway since I'm in an office.
