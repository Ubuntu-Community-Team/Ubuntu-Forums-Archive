### Post by Axet120 on 2008-11-08
ok go into control panel. Then go into sounds and audio devices. Followed by selecting the sounds tab on top. Now you want to scroll in the  program events window; til you find select. Is there a speaker icon beside it? If not; you need to appoint a sound for "select". Highlight "select" then choose to look into sound scroll down option, or you can select browse to get an option from media folder. Hope this helps.
