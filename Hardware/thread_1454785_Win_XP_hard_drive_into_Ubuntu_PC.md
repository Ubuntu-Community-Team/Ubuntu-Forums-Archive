### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-04-15
In terms of "restoring" an MS Windows install to a different machine, take a look at Paragon's new Adaptive Restore product.  It (supposedly) allows you to do exactly what you want and automatically deals with the "different drivers" problem.

Acronis has had a similar product for a while, but whereas theirs costs lots of money, the Paragon product costs a lot less.

You will still have to deal with the activation problem, but you already know that.
