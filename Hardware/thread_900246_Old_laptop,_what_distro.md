You have enough RAM, and barely sufficient HDD space; as suggested you can use Xubuntu. You can actually use practically anything (subject to processor speed), but a lightweight and complete distro like Xubuntu will do fine for you. 

DSL is another great distro, but when installed to hard disk, it takes a hell of a lot of time to startup, but it's great once it's running.

My current favorite is puppy. (IceWM window manager).

All are great, but I recommend Xubuntu.
