### Post by cascade9 on 2010-05-17
Yes, you can run different sized/speed RAM sticks. The RAM will run at the lowest speed though, so you might be better off just removing the 2x1GB DDR2 667 sticks and just using 2x2GB DDR2 800/1066.

That will depend on your RAM usage, but in most cases, 4GB is more than enough.
