### Post by cascade9 on 2010-01-26
ATA is 'AT Attachment' (from the old IBM 'AT' series computers). 

PATA is Parallel ATA, using a 40pin and 40/80 wire cable.

SATA is Serial ATA, using 8 wire cable. 

IDE is from the old WDIntegrated Drive Electronics Hdd (called that because the drives electronics are on the drive, not on a controller board).
