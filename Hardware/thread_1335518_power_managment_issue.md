OK, this is not a proper fix, but it will pass as a workaround. Suspend your laptop before you close the lid by pressing  (fn+esc)  wait a second until it powers down. when you open the lid it will spring back to life with a splash screen asking for your password.   obviously this is not helpful if your 3 year old son, your wife or your work buddy comes along and arbitrarily shuts the lid, but i hope it is of some use until we find a better solution.
