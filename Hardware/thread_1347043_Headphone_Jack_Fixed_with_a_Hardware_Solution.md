### Post by Teeth Kicker on 2009-12-05
Forever I grinded on fixing the notorious headphone jack problem with most notebooks and Ubuntu Hardy, Jaunty, and Karmic. I started using Ubuntu with Jaunty and now I'm on Karmic.  I tinkered with getting rid of Pulseaudio and upgrading Alsamixer, but to no avail the headphone jack wouldn't not show as an output source in Alsamixer. So if anyone else can't seem to solve the insanity of getting the headphones working...run down to Radioshack or some similar store and pick up a USB headphone/mic jack, it will show up under Sound --> Preferences --> Output as a USB Audio Analog Device. 
  This was my only frustration with Ubuntu since I converted over from Windows, and now it's fixed! Hope this helps somebody out there, it's a frustrating and time-consuming fix since the solutions seem to vary by your make and model of laptop.
