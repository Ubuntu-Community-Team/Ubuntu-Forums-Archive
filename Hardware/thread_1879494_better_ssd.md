### Post by flloyd on 2011-11-13
I would avoid Corsair SSDs since they do not release a firmware updater for Linux nor Mac. You MUST have Windows installed in order to update your Corsair firmware. With a major bug in all Sandforce-based SSDs (including Corsairs) having just been released weeks ago, I woulf argue that the ability to upgrade SSD firmware is essential.
