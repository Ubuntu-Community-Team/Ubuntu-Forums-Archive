### Post by jimbob on 2010-02-02
Open a terminal window and type in ***alsamixer***.  Make sure the levels on all mic inputs are turned up into the red not muted.

### Post by jimbob on 2010-02-03
Use the left and right arrows to navigate along the bottom and select the function.  Use the up and down arrows to change the sound levels.  Use the letter m button to mute/unmute and when you are finished use esc to close the window.
