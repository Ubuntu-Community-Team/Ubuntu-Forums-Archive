### Post by Simian Man on 2009-10-22
I'm partial to HP, but what's more important than the brand are the actual components.  In particular, you will likely have a good experience going with either Intel or Nvidia for the graphics card.  Getting a supported wireless card is important too.  I know intel cards usually work out of the box, but I'm sure there are other good brands as well.
