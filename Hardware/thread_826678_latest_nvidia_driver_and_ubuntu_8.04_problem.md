### Post by rplancius on 2008-06-12
Your Nvidia troubles sound familiar. I solved mine by installing EnvyNG in Hardy. This will automatically install the correct video drivers in Ubuntu. Watch out, there are two versions available. If you install Hardy there's EnvyNG, on Gutsy there's Envy Legacy.  Details can be found here [http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html](http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html)
