### Post by LostOverThere on 2011-06-04
Hello,

I recently installed LibreOffice 3.4.0 and as soon as I finished installing this, my mouse touchpad on my laptop went dead and wont work at all. Bam, no working mouse. I'm not entirely sure if these things are linked, or just a very big coincidence. The laptop is a new Dell Inspiron. 

Keyboard and everything is working fine, but the mouse just sits on the middle of the screen and wont budge at all.

What can I do to get my mouse working again?

**SOLUTION:** It appears software can sometimes switch the touchpad on and off on some Dell laptops. The solution to this is simply to press the Touchpad button on your Laptop (to the right of the F12 button). If this doesn't work, you may need to hold it done. This should solve the issue.
