### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2014-03-29
> FYI I do not have pulseaudio, I only have whatever it is that was built into ubuntu 13.10.
Ubuntu has pulseaudio built in by default...
```
Pulseaudio:
      Installed - Yes (/usr/bin/pulseaudio)
      Running - Yes
```

> That is NOT my sound card. My assumption is that it is assuming the HDMI port is my audio output device instead of my headphone jacks.
No. Your onboard is recognized too and I'm assuming you're getting sound from the speakers?:
```
00:1b.0 Audio device: Intel Corporation 7 Series/C210 Series Chipset Family High Definition Audio Controller (rev 04)
```

See: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/alsa-driver/+bug/1267675/](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/alsa-driver/+bug/1267675/)
{Specifically, see comment 15 there.)
