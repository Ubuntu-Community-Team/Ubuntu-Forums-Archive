### Post by kurt18947 on 2014-03-23
You won't find a new machine in that price range.  In my area, I find quite a few Thinkpad T61s in your price range on Craigslist.  I think you'd find Thinkpads pretty much plug 'n' play.  The most likely bump would be if the machine has a Broadcom wireless adapter.  That can be usually be easily overcome if you have temporary access to a wired internet connection.  I'm sure there are other good choices but my experience is with a T61 & an X61, both were install and go.
