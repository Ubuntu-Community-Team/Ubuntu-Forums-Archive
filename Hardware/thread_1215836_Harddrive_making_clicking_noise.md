### Post by DaveM59 on 2009-07-17
Clicking or rattling means the hard drive is dying.  Back up all your data NOW.

Sometimes a drive will rattle for months before it finally expires but sometimes it will go in a matter of hours.

I could write a dissertation on how difficult and expensive it can be to recover data from a failed drive, but I hope you won't need that information.

One concrete suggestion.  Don't shut down your computer until you do the backup.  The drive may refuse to spin up when you turn the machine back on.

Good luck.
