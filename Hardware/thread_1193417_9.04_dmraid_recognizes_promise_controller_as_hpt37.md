### Post by moz_21 on 2009-06-24
Ok first of all, all drives (IDE/SATA/SCSI) will be shown as /dev/sdx instead of /dev/hdx. This is just something that has changed due to the unified IDE driver.


Second about the "wrong number of drives" error. Have you switched the drives around? Example, switched drive 2 to drive 1's port and 1 to 2's?

This would cause problems. It seems the 1st drive has the array info on it and if they are switched it gets confused. (I've had it happen).
