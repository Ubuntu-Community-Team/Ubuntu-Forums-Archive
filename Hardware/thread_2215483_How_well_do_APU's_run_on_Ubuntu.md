### Post by mastablasta on 2014-04-07
AMD has better GPU support. but some of the chips might not have good support yet. some of the A serres work very well though.
some early tests: [http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=amd_a10kaveri_linwin&num=1](http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=amd_a10kaveri_linwin&num=1)
I expect AMD to release good drivers for this chip along with 14.04 - tests in link above show that performance is basically as good as in Win8.

Intel has opensource driver and works well. but the graphics chip is (usually) not as good. 

i think you could get away with intel if you have the money. AMD is usually "cheaper".

another option might be a good AMD CPU + lower end GPU (maybe even one of those with no fans and maybe nvidia)
