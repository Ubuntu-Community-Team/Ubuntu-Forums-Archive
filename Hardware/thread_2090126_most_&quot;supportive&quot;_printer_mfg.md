### Post by agillator on 2012-12-01
I have used nothing but HP printers for some years and have had no problems. I have not found or heard of an HP printer that doesn't work with HPLIP. I have found, though, that for my purposes it is best to get and install it from the HP site rather than the repositories. The versions in the repositories seem not to be compiled for scanner support; at least the scanners on the all-in-one printers don't seem to work unless you get HPLIP from HP directly.
