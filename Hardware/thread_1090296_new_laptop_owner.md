### Post by Chibone on 2009-03-08
For powersaving, try installing powertop.

Then run "sudo powertop" when it's installed and follow some of the suggestions (I would suggest ignoring the disable-hal-polling advice as it stops CDs from automounting, which I like).

Battery suggestions:

I top off every night. Once a month I completely drain until the laptop shuts off completely and refill to full to keep it in shape.

When storing batteries for more than two weeks (I have 2 batteries) I drain them to 60% and then unplug them from the laptop.
