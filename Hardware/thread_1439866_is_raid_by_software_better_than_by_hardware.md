### Post by ratcheer on 2010-03-26
Generally, HW RAID is considered to be superior. But, some SW RAID implementations are excellent. Also, if there are bugs in HW RAID, it is much harder to get them fixed.

DISCLAIMER - I am speaking from my experience of working with midrange server systems for many years. I have never worked with RAID on a desktop-type machine.
