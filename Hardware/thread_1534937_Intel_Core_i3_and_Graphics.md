I tried Mint 9, based on Ubuntu 10.04, on my Dell laptop with an i3-330 Intel processor and it had continuous freezes with the 2.6.32 kernel. The disk is good, works great on another machine, machine works fine otherwise, etc. (just so no assumptions are made that it's a disk or hardware problem) My older machine runs great on 10.04 so I'm convinced it's a video issue since switching to PCLinuxOS 2010.7 with kernel 2.6.33-6 works great with it. I believe the correction came in 2.6.33 so if you can get a kernel of the .33 or .34/.35 variety it should work fine. If not, my experience was very poor and intolerable on Ubuntu or Mint, but that should resolve when the newer kernels are available and I'm looking forward to that!
