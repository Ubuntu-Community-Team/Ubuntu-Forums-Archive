### Post by navlig on 2010-05-27
I just installed Ubuntu 10.04  on my Acer 1410 laptop but my Brother HL-2170w printer won't print anything. Printer installation was very easy. If I hit print the printer lights come on, it looks like it's gonna start printing and them the red error light come on. I tried it wireless and USB only, uninstalled, reinstalled and made no difference. The printer is woking fine on my 2 Windows machine with no problem. This is only happening in Ubuntu. Can someone help me please?

---

### Post by navlig on 2010-06-01
Here's what I did to solve this issue:
Updated Printer firmware. ***Old version Printer Firmware v1.09, Network Firmware 1.*06 new version now 1.15 and 1.09**. After the firmware update I installed the printer
just like before and everything is working fine through USB, wired, and wireless. On Brother website nothing is said about linux regarding this firmware I went ahead and aplied the firmware anyway and to my surprise it's working now. I used my Windows machine to apply the firmare.
