### Post by lipin on 2010-11-07
Ohh crap this was the dumbest question I ever made. I just talk with my friend under system->preferences->mouse->touchpad I had selected option: disable touchpad while typing.

Solved in suprisingly easy way. Way to go Ubuntu.
