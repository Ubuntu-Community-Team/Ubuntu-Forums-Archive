### Post by 2F4U on 2011-10-02
Not sure if you can get a cooler laptop. That depends on what components are included. Some laptops are very powerful (CPU, graphics card, etc.), but the heat flow doesn't match this power. There are some things you could check:
- Is there a BIOS upgrade available
- Is dust collecting inside the laptop, which reduces the efficiency of the air flow
- Does something block the air getting out of the machine
- Does it help if you lift the machine a bit from the surface (e.g. laptop stand)
- Can you use proprietary graphics drivers with more advanced power management
