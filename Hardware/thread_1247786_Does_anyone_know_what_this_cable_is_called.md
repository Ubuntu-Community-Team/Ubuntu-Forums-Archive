### Post by aesis05401 on 2009-08-23
Hello, to be a little more specific - that is an FPC cable.  These are etched according to each manufacturer's specifications, and I don't believe there is a market for third party FPC cables.  

If you can identify the part numbers it is possible to obtain replacements through overstock suppliers, but it is usually best to attempt replacement through the OEM instead.
