### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-31
> I want hardware acceleration with open source drivers.

Good Luck with that ...

You might be able to do it with Intel drivers, but you're NOT going to be able to do that with either AMD or Nvidia chipsets.  

Only minimimal acceleration is incorporated into the open-source drivers.  You generally have to go to restricted drivers to get the full features of the video chipset.
