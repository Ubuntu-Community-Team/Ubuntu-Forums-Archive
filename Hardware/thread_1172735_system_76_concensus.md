### Post by tyroeternal on 2009-05-28
From what I have seen and heard about System76 it is a great brand.  All of their units compare well to other vendors in their price range, but their value comes from how well they work with Ubuntu.  For just the hardware you could probably find better deals and usually better looking units, but for suport and peace of mind they are well worth it.

I've got a Dell Mini 12 with Ubuntu preinstalled which, while cheaper, has left me wishing I would have grabbed the Starling.
