### Post by Autodave on 2022-11-04
Sounds like a mechanical problem to me.  Try cleaning it this way:

Power down.  Hold unit upside down and tap (to hopefully knock out any dirt beneath the keys).  Now, turn on its side and tap.  Turn on other side and tap.  Repeat.  With it righted again, start repeatedly tapping on EVERY key multiple times with a little more force than you normally use.  When done with that, repeat the entire process again from the beginning.
