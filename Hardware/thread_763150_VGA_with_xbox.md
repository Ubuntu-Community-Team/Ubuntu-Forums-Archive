### Post by frkuout on 2008-04-22
The VGA port on the side of your laptop is only for output of the video from your video card. To change the output from the laptop most laptops require that you se the alt and F4 key to switcht he display output. There's three modes available, your laptop LCD, the external display, and a dual isplay with your laptop LCD and the external display.

Unfortunately, you cannot use the VGA port on the laptop to display the xbox video output.
