### Post by MrPapersHead on 2009-04-30
I recently upgraded from 8.10 to 9.04. I am still rather new to whole Linux world, but I am catching on and really enjoying what I am seeing. The only hiccup I thought occured from the upgrade was the loss of my Nvidia drivers. After quite a bit of forum searching, I managed to fix that problem. 

I thought everything was in working order until I unplugged my A/C adapter. When I did this I received a system message telling me that it could not determine the battery life left. It shut down about 5 minutes after the message appeared. After subsequent reboots it seems as though my battery is no longer receiving a charge. I decided to pull the battery, reboot, shut down, and put the battery in the hopes it would reset it. No help.

I was not able to find anything searching through the forums. Would someone be able to assist me, or at least point me in the right direction with what is going on? Would be sad if it was an RMA issue, but would rather that than some insane glitch.

Thank you for any and all help.

### Post by MrPapersHead on 2009-05-27
I figured out what it was. Apparently the HP's like to un-synch the battery sometimes. Had to let it completely drain, charge it while off, let it completely drain in a safe mode setting, and then charge it up again. Everything is working great now. Hopefully no more problems here =).
