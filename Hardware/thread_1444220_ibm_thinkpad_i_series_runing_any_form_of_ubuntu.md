### Post by Fafler on 2010-04-01
The I series are pretty crappy compared to other ThinkPads. You should find some more memory. I think all of them use SD RAM, which is very cheap second hand. Note the speed of the current modules and get something equally fast or faster. It's 66, 100 or 133 mhz, depending on CPU and chipsset.

Instead of the older versions you've tried, you should go for Xubuntu 9.10. It is optimized for older machines and uses xfce instead of gnome. It should give you a much better experience than Ubuntu 5.10. If you can't upgrade your RAM, then maybe danm small linux could be for you? [http://www.damnsmalllinux.org/](http://www.damnsmalllinux.org/)
