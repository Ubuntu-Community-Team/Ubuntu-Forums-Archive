### Post by Nylo on 2010-05-10
I recently changed my resolution from 1024x768 to 1360x768.  After a re-boot I got to my logon after which I recieved a black desktop, no curser, nothing.

Here is what I did.

1, Recovery mode. (hold shift key on reboot after IBM logo)
2, Choose basic graphics recovery mode.
3, Re-boot and reset to 1024x768.

Note:  If you hold shift key before the IBM logo it gets you the bios.
