### Post by Dark_Stang on 2009-10-22
Gateway laptops can go either way. Sometimes you can get away with resoldering, sometimes the motherboard is too damaged and needs to be replaced. I'd try a different AC adapter first. Sometimes the DC plug goes bad. But if it's loose, it's probably the DC jack in the laptop. Resoldering would be much cheaper than buying a new laptop/mobo if you're up to it. Or, if it's still under a warranty/service plan by any chance, get it serviced under that.
