### Post by cascade9 on 2009-12-26
If you havent already tried this, go into your BIOS and change the video from 'onboard' (or AGP) to video card (probably 'PCI'). You might need to dig around in the BIOS to find the right settings. 

BTW, I would guess that you could unplug the monitor from the card and plug it into the old onboard video port and you would get your video back.
