### Post by user_of_gnomes on 2017-08-14
You can always ignore Dell. The question is if it will work. You might have some success mixing double and single sided RAM but often the limits set by the manufacturer are pretty much the limits you'll have to work with. The CPU is limited by what the mainboard is capable of.
