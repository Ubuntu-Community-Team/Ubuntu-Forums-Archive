### Post by =E= on 2008-05-03
I fixed it following Ubuntu Guide ([http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Gutsy](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Gutsy))

To those having the same problem, add the directory "compiz" under your .config directory in your home directory, e.g.:

home/darthvader/.config/compiz

Then enter this into a terminal:

echo SKIP_CHECKS="yes" >> $HOME/.config/compiz/compiz-manager

Then reboot and activate your "extra" desktop effects.  Apparently, this video card is blacklisted for use under Compiz.  But it works!  I had to do the same with another laptop to get Compiz to work as well.

HTH
