After 4 good hours I managed to solve DUAL Monitor Setup. Its a little bit tricky but it works for me. And I think it would work for any ATI card.

Install in recovery mode:
1. I installed latest ATI Drivers.
2. Added options manually to Xorg.conf
   -Control Panel sucks mainly. Doesn't work that well. 
3. Xorg.conf:
**BOLD** MEANS IMPORTANT!

mine was 1280*2x1024. (Based on my settings)

**System->Preferences->Screen resolution.**
CLONE SCREENS <-NOT CHECKED!
