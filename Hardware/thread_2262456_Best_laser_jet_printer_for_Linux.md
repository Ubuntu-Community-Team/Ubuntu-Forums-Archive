### Post by Joseph_Tortorello on 2015-01-25
HP is the only company that actually has good, maintained support for linux users as far as I know. Here is a list of the printers that they support: 
[http://www.hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/recommended.html](http://www.hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/recommended.html)

(Hint: all the laserjets are listed together, just use Ctrl+F to get to that part of the page)

Make sure to pick one that has the support level listed as "Full". The list also describes whether it prints in black and white or color, and whether or not it has scan and Fax capabilities, which should help you narrow down your search.

If you were just looking for someone to tell you what to do, I would recommend the HP Laserjet 400-m401n. It's fairly recent, has full support, and goes for $180-190 depending on where you get it.
