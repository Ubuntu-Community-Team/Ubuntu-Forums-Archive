### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-05-28
When my Verizon DVR box crapped out on me, they replaced it (for free) -- but they failed to tell me that ALL the videos I recorded to the attached external drive would be LOST!  I did a LOT of research on this, and discovered that (at least in the U.S.) this is a movie industry requirement on DVR manufacturers -- that you can't migrate the recordings to another device.  I'm not saying every DVR is like this, but this does seem to be a "standard" practice.
