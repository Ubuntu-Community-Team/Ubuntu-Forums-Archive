### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-06-21
> **Greenhorn Geek said:**
> Last time I updated my graphics driver through the terminal, my whole computer went nuts.  If I were to download it from amd.com, would it be different?

No, not really.

There is no ATI fglrx driver that will work with your card and any recent version of Ubuntu.  The older drivers (which would otherwise work) have a conflict with the recent X-servers, so they can't be installed in any Ubuntu newer than 8.10.

So, you're stuck using the open source drivers -- which are installed by default.
