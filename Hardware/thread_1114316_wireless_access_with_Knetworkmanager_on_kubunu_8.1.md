### Post by tospo on 2009-04-02
This is just for the benefit of others who may have the same problem. I installed Kubuntu 8.10 on my Acer Aspire 2930 and I had problems accesing my own home wireless network but I could connect to other networks I tried (e.g. at work). It didn't even help when I disabled access control and all network encryption (a scary 5 minutes....).
When I booted into Windows, I could connect to that network without any problems and of course other machines are also connected to the same network.
In the end the solution for me was simple: I had to change the channel on which my router broadcasts (a Netgear DG834PN) from 13 to 6 and now it all works fine.
