### Post by kelpdip on 2010-08-25
This wireless home laser printer goes on sale fairly often at good prices (~100usd) and is highly rated. Both wired and wireless network printing work just fine on 10.04.1. Took about 5 minutes and I was printing.

[LIST]
[*]Follow printer setup guide for toner/paper
[*]Attach to wired network via ethernet.
[*]Use router to determine dhcp address, or hit the printer button 3 times quickly to print settings page.
[*]Navigate to web setup located at printer IP. User:admin Pass:access
[*]Set up WiFi networking via web interface, then unplug ethernet.
[*]System --> Administration --> Printing
[*]Click Add, then network printers.
[*]BrotherXXXXXX should be on the list as a LPD via DNS device.
[*]Drivers download automatically and you are good to go.
[/LIST]
