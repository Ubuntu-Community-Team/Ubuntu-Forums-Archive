I checked the forums, and while this appears to be a common problem, mine has...a twist.

As short of a summary as I can make it:

I had 10.04 with the ATI catalyst control center and everything worked great (or as great as the CCC is able to work)

I did a wipe-and-install to 12.04.  

During the installation process (booting into the OS from the live CD), both monitors worked fine, I even set up a "real" dual-monitor setup, with different content on each screen. 

However, now that I'm in the actual install, I can't detect the secondary monitor.

I've tried installing both NVIDIA drivers (recommended and updated), I've tried moving xorg.conf out of the way and letting the OS rebuild it, and I've tried buying my resident guru tacos until he fixed it.  None of those worked.

Any other tips?
