Hit Stop-A on the Sun keyboard while it boots, or send a <Break> signal if you're connected via serial console. This brings you to the **[FONT="Courier New"]ok>[/FONT]** prompt, where you issue the **[FONT="Courier New"]boot cdrom[/FONT]** command, and off you go.
