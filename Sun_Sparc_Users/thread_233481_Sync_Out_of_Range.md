### Post by arkham on 2006-08-16
This seems very similar to the problem I had, listed here:

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1386663#post1386663]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1386663#post1386663")

The solution is to add the following line to the xorg.conf Device section:

Option “ReferenceClock” “29.500MHz”

Worked a treat for me......
