### Post by nhaines on 2010-06-10
Ubuntu is a world-class operating system that makes computing easy and simple.  But starting out can be intimidating.  Ubuntu Hour makes it easy to get started.

Your local Ubuntu Hour is a time and place where some Ubuntu users get together to chat for a bit and have some coffee or tea.  They are more than happy to talk to you about what Ubuntu is, answer any questions, provide a free CD so you can try or install it, and help you get things up and running.  They can also share some of the tips and tricks they've used to make their computers more convenient.

You're free to join us at any of the times below.  Say hi and feel free to bring a portable computer if you have it.

[COLOR="Purple"]Current Ubuntu Hour events in California:[/COLOR]

Every Thursday at 6pm
Panera Bread - 23592 Rockfield Blvd., Lake Forest, CA - [http://ur1.ca/0cec5](http://ur1.ca/0cec5)
