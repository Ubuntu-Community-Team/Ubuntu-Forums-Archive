### Post by grantbow on 2008-07-02
Hi toupeiro and archer6,

If you would like to start up a group to meet in person it might be more successful if you created/restarted a Linux user group.  Pulling from a wider audience for participation is a good strategy for success.  The page for the Fresno LUG listed on [http://www.sbay.org/linux-info.html](http://www.sbay.org/linux-info.html) seems to be missing.

To participate in the LoCo there is no need to meet in person on a regular basis.  Join us in #ubuntu-california on freenode.net.  If you aren't familiar with IRC on freenode you will be amazed at all the OSS projects that maintain channels for discussion.  There are also lots and lots of people talking about Ubuntu 24x7 in #ubuntu.
