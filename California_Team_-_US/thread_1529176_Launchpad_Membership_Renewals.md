### Post by Flannel on 2010-07-11
Howdy All,

In our last two IRC meetings, we discussed making our Launchpad group more accurate and useful. To keep its membership list up-to-date, members will now be prompted to renew periodically. When you're due for renewal, you'll get an email from Launchpad; to renew yourself you just need to follow the link in the email within a week.

Everyone's initial renewal period is two months. After that, you'll only have to renew once a year.

You can find out more about the new policy here: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CaliforniaTeam/LaunchpadMembership](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CaliforniaTeam/LaunchpadMembership)

If you have any questions, encounter problems, or forget to renew your membership, just contact a group administrator for help. You can contact me over email, on IRC, or by private message on the forums (my contact info is at _[my launchpad page](https://launchpad.net/~nealbussett)_).
