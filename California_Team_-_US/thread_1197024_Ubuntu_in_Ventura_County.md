**Update**
Hey guys just an update on what is going on.  I have joined the guys from vclug ([www.vclug.org](www.vclug.org)).  They meet every other Saturday at the Fry's cafe in Oxnard and are looking for a more permanent location.  There are anywhere from 5 to 10 people who show there.
