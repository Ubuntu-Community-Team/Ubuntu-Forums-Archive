### Post by Yasumoto on 2010-03-31
1) A LoCo is a LOcal COmmunity (LO CO) team. It is a group of volunteers that love free software, particularly Ubuntu. We advocate and help others use Ubuntu, we attend conferences, user group meetings, and hold other events to spread the word. We also manage to have a lot of fun working together and helping people use technology to do great things :)

2) The best way to get involved is to particpate in the discussion and attend events, take a look at our mailing list or IRC channel and join up!!

Hopefully that answered your first two questions, but I'm sure you have more, so please ask away!! :)
