### Post by Yasumoto on 2009-02-01
Thanks for your interest in the Ubuntu California team!

We are an active group of contributors who love to use and promote Ubuntu and Free Software.

If you'd like to participate, check out our [wiki]("https://www.wiki.ubuntu.com/CaliforniaTeam"), which is our hub for info. Join us on IRC if you'd like to chat, and be sure to sign on to the [mailing list]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-ca"), which is our primary means of communication.



**Wiki**         - [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CaliforniaTeam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CaliforniaTeam)

**Mailing List** - [https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-ca](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-ca)
