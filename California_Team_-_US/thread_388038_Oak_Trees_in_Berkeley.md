I'm not from US, let alone Cal :) But still, I think this is a genuine way to do some environmentalist work. So I thought I can promote it here:

1. University of California wants to cut mature [live oak trees]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Live_oak"), an ecologically significant element of Cal.

2. [These people]("http://www.saveoaks.com/SaveOaks/Main.html") are trying to stop the university before it is too late. 

3. To save them, they did [a nude photo event]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Li_SvF3LWuY") (youtube), which, to me, is brilliant. What else will symbolize our relation to nature better than [nudes hugging these creatures]("http://www.saveoaks.com/SaveOaks/TreeSpirit.html") (link to their website).
