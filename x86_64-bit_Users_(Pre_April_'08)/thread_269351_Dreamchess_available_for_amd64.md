Hi folks,

I've made my first .deb (whoopee) of [Dreamchess]("http://www.dreamchess.org/") for Dapper. You can [ get it here]("http://www.dpb.org.uk/ubuntu/"). There's also a Gnome [desktop launcher here]("http://www.dpb.org.uk/ubuntu/") for the ultra lazy ;)

It's a nice little chess prog with a choice of 3D and 2D boards/pieces. It has it's own built in chess engine or you can use any other XBoard-compatible engine. There's also on-screen move lists using SAN notation, undo functionality, and savegames in PGN format etc.

Please let me know if it works (or not!).

Cheers ... Paul
