### Post by tmeier on 2006-02-16
Spaceball,

I have a TiBook 1Ghz and 512 MB of RAM.  I have a dual boot of OS X 10.4.2 and Ubuntu Breezy Badger.  I also have MOL (Mac On Linux) running.  There are directions on how to install for Tiger as well as Panther on the Wiki.  Go to the Wiki at the top of of the page here and then do a search for Mac On Linux and it will give you the directions.  Make sure you check out towards the bottom of the aritcle for that is where the Tiger Directions are.  MOL works great for me except I haven't got sound working just yet.  Otherwise my apps all work and can access the internet.  I love not having to reboot to get to my OS X side of things it allows me to just stay in Breezy.    The Mac On Linux website isn't all that helpful.  I hope this helps.
