Why installl "32 bit drivers on a 64 bit machine"? Doesn't sound good, if it's possible at all.

Why not use 64-bit drivers?  I think these links are relevant:
[LIST]
[*][http://hplip.sourceforge.net/models/photosmart/photosmart_3200_series.html](http://hplip.sourceforge.net/models/photosmart/photosmart_3200_series.html)
