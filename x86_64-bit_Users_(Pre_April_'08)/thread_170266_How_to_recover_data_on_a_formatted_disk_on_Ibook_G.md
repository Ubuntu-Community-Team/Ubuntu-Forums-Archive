Other than that, [DiskWarrior ]("http://www.alsoft.com/DiskWarrior/") is generally regarded as one of the best utilities for recovering data from damaged/deleted OSX disks.  But don't expect miracles (especially if you're expecting to restore Ubuntu/Linux files too, which I don't think DiskWarrior can do at all-- restoring from your latest backup is going to have a much higher chance of success.
