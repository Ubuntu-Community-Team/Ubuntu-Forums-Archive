### Post by david_2001 on 2007-05-21
If you are using the "Hardware Sensors Monitor" applet just right click over one of the icons, select Preferences from the menu, select the Sensors tab of the dialog, click the triangle opposite "libsensors" to drop down the list and then check/uncheck what you want/don't want (see screenshot).
