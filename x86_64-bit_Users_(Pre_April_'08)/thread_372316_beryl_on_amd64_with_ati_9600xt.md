### Post by Soma1975 on 2007-03-05
Hello,

just try the "xserver-xorg-video-ati" driver with synaptic.
Replace "fglrx" with ati in your xorg.conf.
You also need to enable composite in xorg.conf
And then trie this command:
LD_PRELOAD=/usr/lib/fglrx/libGL.so.1.2.xlibmesa beryl --replace
