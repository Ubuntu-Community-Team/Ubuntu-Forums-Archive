### Post by bobpur on 2006-04-11
I did a dual boot similar to what you want. I used the Ubuntu v5.10 install CD. Pay attention to the Dialogs the CD shows you and you'll have what you want. I had two different harddrives so I caught on easily (One a 60 gb and the other a 40 gb.) When the partitioning block comes up it will show you your harddrive choices. Just enter the one you want to partition and that's where ubuntu will be installed.
