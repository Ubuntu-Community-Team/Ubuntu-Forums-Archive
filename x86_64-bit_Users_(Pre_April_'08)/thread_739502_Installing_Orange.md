Hi,
I'm guessing you're reffering to the USB modem supplied by Orange and not the livebox. Why save yourself the problems of hooking up your usb modem, and invest in a wireless modem/router ? I've seen them advertised in most places for really reasonable prices (UK Argos £30ish) and that would give you the option of using either or both on the internet at the same time.
Failing that, the USB modems are generally made by Thompson and they should have various drivers available on their website.
