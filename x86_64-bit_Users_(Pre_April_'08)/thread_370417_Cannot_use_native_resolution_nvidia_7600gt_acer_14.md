### Post by nodegamra on 2007-09-30
if you have installed the nvidia drivers and still not running at the 1440x900 (native) resolution try to edit the xorg.conf ( CAN'T STRESS ENOUGH TO MAKE A BACK UP BEFORE YOU EDIT THE FILE) and add the resolution manually to the screen section and SubSection display as follows:


Section "Screen"
        Identifier      "Default Screen"
        Device          "Generic Video Card"
        Monitor         "Generic Monitor"
        DefaultDepth    24
        SubSection "Display"
                Depth           1
                [COLOR="Red"]Modes           "1440x900" "1280x800" "1280x768" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"[/COLOR]
        EndSubSection
        SubSection "Display"
                Depth           4
                [COLOR="Red"]Modes           "1440x900" "1280x800" "1280x768" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"[/COLOR]
        EndSubSection
        SubSection "Display"
                Depth           8
                [COLOR="Red"]Modes           "1440x900" "1280x800" "1280x768" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"[/COLOR]
        EndSubSection
        SubSection "Display"
                Depth           15
               [COLOR="Red"] Modes           "1440x900" "1280x800" "1280x768" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"[/COLOR]
        EndSubSection
        SubSection "Display"
                Depth           16
                [COLOR="Red"]Modes           "1440x900" "1280x800" "1280x768" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"[/COLOR]
        EndSubSection
        SubSection "Display"
                Depth           24
                [COLOR="Red"]Modes           "1440x900" "1280x800" "1280x768" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"[/COLOR]
        EndSubSection
EndSection


Hope it works for you as it did for me!!!
