### Post by srunni on 2006-09-14
The best graphics card would be any high end NVIDIA. I'm pretty sure they're all the same as far as driver support goes, as NVIDIA has open sourced all of that stuff. Most audio cards work just fine, so that shouldn't be an issue.

One thing I suggest you do is go to [http://www.system76.com/](http://www.system76.com/) and take a look at what parts they're using for the desktops they build. They build computers for Ubuntu specifically, and you could just copy off of them for a lot of the parts.

### Post by kuja on 2006-09-14
srunni is certainly right, nvidia cards work nice. I would stray away from some of the newer high end creative sound cards though, or at least check that they are supported. You can do so by visiting [http://www.alsa-project.org/alsa-doc/](http://www.alsa-project.org/alsa-doc/)
