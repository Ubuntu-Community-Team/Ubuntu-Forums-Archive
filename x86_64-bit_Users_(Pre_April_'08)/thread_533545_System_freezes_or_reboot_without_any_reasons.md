### Post by bdoe on 2007-08-24
Sounds like either defective memory or an overheating issue.  I'd have the RAM tested (if you have more than one stick, try testing with just one stick at a time installed), and make sure your system's cooling is working properly, that the CPU's heatsink is clean, the fan works properly, and you've applied a thin, even layer of Arctic Silver between the chip and the heatsink.
