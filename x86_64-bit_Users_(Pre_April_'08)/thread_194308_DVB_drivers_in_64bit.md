### Post by RAOF on 2006-06-11
For DVB support, you should check out [www.linuxtv.org](www.linuxtv.org).  The DVB drivers are uniformly open-source, so you won't have any additional problems with Dapper64.  Wireless *may* be a different matter - it depends on your card.
