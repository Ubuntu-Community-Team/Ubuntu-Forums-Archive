### Post by Cappy on 2007-07-14
Close all of your windows. Right click on the bottom where the Window Lists would be if you had open programs. It should bring up a window that says "Window List Preferences" at the top.

Under **Window Grouping**
Click "Always Group Windows".

I read this post and I didn't know but I accidently just found it =P
