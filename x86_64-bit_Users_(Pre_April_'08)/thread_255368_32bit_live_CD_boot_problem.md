### Post by Naikei on 2006-09-11
Hey, after checking the .iso and burning it to CD again. I figured out that I needed to change the "ACPI APIC" setting in the "Power" section of my BIOS to disabled. Quite easy really. Don't know why I didn't think of it before, when I tried SuSe it took me a week to figure that out :(.
But yes! All sorted now, running 32bit as I type!
Thanks for the quick response though! Yeah 64bit will be ok... in the future. For beginners like me anyway!
