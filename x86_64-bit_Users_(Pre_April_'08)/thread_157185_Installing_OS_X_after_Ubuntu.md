### Post by jdp on 2006-04-08
From the OS X installer CD, go into the menu right after it finishes booting and run Disk Utility.  Fromt here you can erase and repartition it however you need.  If you have the space and you think you might want to try and install of Ubuntu later, you might leave 5GB or so of blank space at the end of the drive.  That way if you get the urge to play again you won't have to wipe and reinstall anything.
