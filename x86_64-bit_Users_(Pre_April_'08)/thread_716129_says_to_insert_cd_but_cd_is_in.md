Yes I Have A Suggestion:

In Synaptic Package Manager 

Click The Settings Tab At The Top:

Than Click Repositories

Check All The Boxes In There.

Go To The Third Party Tab And Click "Add Cd-ROM"

Now Insert Your Ubuntu Disc And Add It

------Also - If You Have 2 CD Drives Make Sure Your Putting It In The Correct Drive.
