### Post by Lil_Eagle on 2006-07-17
That is a bit strange.

I don't think it has anything to do with Ubuntu, rather with Google.  If you want the real google.com, just click on the "Go to Google.com" link in far right side of the line under the search box.  It might be in German, so it may be hard for you to read.

If you do that often enough, google will put into the cookie that they send you that you prefer the original google.com and will send you there in the future.  The only reason this happens at all is because Google is trying to be smart and helpful, thinking that you'd prefer another language.  When in reality there's still no way of a computer knowing what a human wants.  Google may beg to differ with me (adsense anyone?).
