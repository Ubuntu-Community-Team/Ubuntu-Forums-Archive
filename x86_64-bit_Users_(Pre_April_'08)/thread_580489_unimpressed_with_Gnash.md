### Post by John.Michael.Kane on 2007-10-18
IMHO being that Gnash is under heavy development. You have two options.

1) use it, and file bug reports that you may find. 
2) Use Flash non free. 

The choice you will most likely have to make is one between being a beta tester for the plugin or using the non free version till Gnash is up to your standards.

Note also your issues with Gnash may be with only that one site. you may want to see how it holds up on other sites.
