### Post by incubus on 2006-11-13
Works here.

I'm running Ubuntu KDE 64bit on a Core 2 Duo with a Gigabyte Motherboard, chipset 945G.  Everything worked flawlessly, out of the box.  Actually, hardware detection was better than in Windows (doesn't surprise me).

SATA, Audio, Video, Speedstep, Network, you name it, they're all working.

Hope that helps.

best,
incubus

PS: I'm running Edgy now, but I had Dapper before with the same results.

PPS: Oh, I remembered one. My motherboard has a Realtek network card and they don't have Wake On Lan support in Linux (even their latest driver doesn't have it).  That's a little annoying.  But it's related to the network card, not to Core 2 Duo or Ubuntu.
