### Post by teripittman on 2005-12-24
I found out something interesting about the resolution problem on G3s, the one where you can't get anything higher than 800 resolution. It seems that the AV personality card affects the resolution you can get. Found this out when my mini tower G3 (with the AV card) died. Nothing had worked to force a higher resolution. The monitor and video card would go to a higher res in OS 9.2 but not under ubuntu. I took the hard drive with ubuntu installed and put it into a different G3. Suddenly, I had the higher res! Then I decided to install the AV card. I was back to just 640 and 800 resolution. Pull the card and I could get higher resolutions again. Don't know what causes this but I can certainly repeat the issue.
