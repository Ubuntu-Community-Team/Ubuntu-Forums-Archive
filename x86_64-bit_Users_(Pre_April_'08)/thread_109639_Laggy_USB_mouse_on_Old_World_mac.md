### Post by 8-bitDesigner on 2005-12-29
Okay, here's a weird one. I've got a Powermac 8500 with a USB card and a KVM'd wireless mouse/keyboard, and a locally connect ADB mouse/keyboard. Now the USB mouse is perfectly responsive in OS9, OS8.6 and WinXP, however, in Gnome, the mouse is laggy as all hell. 

No clue why, and disconnecting the ADB mouse/keyboard combo doesn't seem to have any effect, however, the ADB mouse is infinitely more responsive than the USB one.

Any ideas?

---

### Post by 8-bitDesigner on 2005-12-31
Figured this one out, finally! I have a USB KVM switch from IoGear and the USB receiver on my wireless mouse/keyboard combo only uses one port. Apparently the KVM switch prefers one port over the other, and so when I switched the ports, the mouse started working perfectly.

Fancy that!
