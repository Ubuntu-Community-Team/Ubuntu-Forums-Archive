### Post by RAOF on 2005-11-28
You'd need to ask [nvidia]("www.nvidia.com").  SLI support is done pretty much entirely in their (closed source) graphics drivers, so unless their linux drivers support SLI your second card won't do anything.  Except possibly confuse the X-server ;)
