### Post by ace007 on 2008-02-21
If you are looking to utilize the full potential of your processor, you should download the 64bit version of Ubuntu.  Running Windows 32 Bit is purely your choice and has nothing to do with Ubuntu.  You should also decide which version of Ubuntu you want, Gutsy is pretty sweet but has a few compatibilities issues, mostly with some laptops.  Feisty is the older slightly more stable version.  Mind you there incompatibilities are extremely small.  I suggest you download Gutsy.  

Go to: [http://releases.ubuntu.com/7.10/]("http://releases.ubuntu.com/7.10/") for Ubuntu 7.10 Gutsy Gibbon.  Scroll to the bottom and download the 7.10-desktop-amd64 version in .ISO form or torrent form.

Welcome to Linux!  There forums will solve any questions you have!
