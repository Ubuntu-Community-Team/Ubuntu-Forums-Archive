I have noticed a few messages re cd/dvd drives not being recognised at boot. I also had this problem and found that inserting a cd into the drive and doing a cold boot the drive was recognised. 
Yesterday I installed a new Motherboard and because I have ditched my Ide drives and gone exclusively Sata I changed my cd drive to Master. The cd drive is recognised at boot.
Hope this helps .
