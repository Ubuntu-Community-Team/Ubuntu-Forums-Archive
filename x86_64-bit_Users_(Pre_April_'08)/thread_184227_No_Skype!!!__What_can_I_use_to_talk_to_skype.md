HI!

To download skype for linux:
[http://www.skype.com/download/skype/linux/](http://www.skype.com/download/skype/linux/)

Your looking for the Debian Package. Skype works fine in Linux, it just takes a while to load on my system. I don't think that there may be any other apps to replace skype for skype. See, Skype isn't true VOIP(SIP) compatible, it's almost like peer-to-peer. 

A good true VOIP alternative is Gnome messenger, EKiga or X10.

But skype gets the job done! :)

PS: I don't now how new you are but you might try double clicking on the downloaded file... it's not going to work. Copy the deb file into  your home directory, rename it to skype.deb and in the terminal:

sudo dpkg -i skype.deb
