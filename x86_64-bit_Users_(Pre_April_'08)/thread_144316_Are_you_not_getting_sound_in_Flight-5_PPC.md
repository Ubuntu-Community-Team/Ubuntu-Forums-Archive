Just something to try... took me a while to figure this out myself. If you have a red "X" over the speaker in the top right, go up there and right click it (either F10 or F11, i forget). Go to volume properties and play around with the settings there. I think you might find that the master is disabled by default.

Anyhow, that was my problem. Make sure you're doing a clean Flight-5 install for the sound to work.
