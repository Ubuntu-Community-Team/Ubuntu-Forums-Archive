### Post by Uta on 2006-05-26
I installed from the Dapper 6.0.6 Live CD. I first resized my iBook HD with 'parted' via the terminal, then clicked on the installer, the install was successful. On restart I was presented with the options, 'l' for Linux and 'x' for Mac OS. I first booted in Mac OS to make sure my resizing did not do damage and confirm I still had a working system. It was as I had left it only less the space I allocated to Linux. I then booted into Linux and currently doing the updates. Thanks to all the people who posted on this subject; your postings made this dual boot successful. I am really grateful to the Ubuntu developers who have done a lot of work to include PPC users. Thank you!
