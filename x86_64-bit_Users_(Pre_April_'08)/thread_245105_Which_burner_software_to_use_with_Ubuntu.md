### Post by Garyu on 2006-08-30
The way I see it, you have a bad recorder. Change hardware and everything will be fine. Or, possibly, you've just got bad disks, maybe you bought some cheap crap?

I have tried k3b and gnomebaker and nautilus and a bunch of windows-apps, they all do the same thing, the difference is only in usability and personal preference on design issues of the software. What is written to the disk should be exactly the same data. 

Why don't you borrow some hardware from a friend or a local dealer with 30-day try-before-you-buy or whatever, you should be able to tell if there is something wrong with your drive or if it is the disks.
