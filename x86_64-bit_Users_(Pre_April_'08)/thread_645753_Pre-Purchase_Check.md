### Post by Clancy_s on 2007-12-20
One thing to watch for is the difference between Mobility Radeon and plain Radeon: the Radeon HD 2600 is supported by the fglrx drivers (with all their problems), the *Mobility* Radeon HD 2600 is not.

The open source Radeon DH driver installs fine but at the moment only supports 2d.  See this thread for details:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=3821451](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=3821451)
