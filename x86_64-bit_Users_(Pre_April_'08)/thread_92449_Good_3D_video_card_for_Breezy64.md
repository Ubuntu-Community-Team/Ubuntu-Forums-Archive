### Post by metoo on 2005-11-19
For Linux, NVidia is probably the best at the moment. (Faster OpenGL drivers than ATI but both should be faster then the old Matrox anyway).
All 5xxx or 6xxx should run without problems (7xxx and former models most likely too).

Drivers are available for the kernel you run in package linux-restricted-modules-2.6...
(for ATI too.)

The quality of the analog output is most likely not as good as the Matrox. From what one hears, neither ATI nor NVidia can match this.
This should not be a problem when using a TFT monitor.
