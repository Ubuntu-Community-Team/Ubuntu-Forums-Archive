### Post by John Jason Jordan on 2007-05-28
> **Johnny K said:**
> Most hardware works?

I thought in order for hardware to work it needed a 64 bit Linux driver. Do most manufacturers provide that?
There are 64-bit drivers for just about every mouse ever made and they come standard with Ubuntu amd-64. The reason I can say that is because there are only a handful of different mouse drivers that cover every make and model. 

If you want a recommendation for an optical mouse that will definitely work with Ubuntu amd-64, get a Logitech. I use a little Logitech optical cordless USB mouse specifically designed for notebooks. It's nice because the receiver part is smaller than a thumb drive and just sticks in a USB port, so the mouse has no cord.
