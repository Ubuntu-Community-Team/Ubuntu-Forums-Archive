### Post by RAOF on 2006-11-14
Everything works better on Edgy :p.  More concretely, you shouldn't need XGL on Edgy to get Beryl to work with your x800.

But, as to your actual questions: Yes, XGL/Beryl will work just fine on your system.  The actual install steps are almost independent of your hardware - just about any howto should work, but you'll need to make sure that you use one of the repositories with AMD64 packages in it.  I'd check out the Beryl forums - there are tons of up-to-date howtos on it, and links to the best repositories for the packages.
