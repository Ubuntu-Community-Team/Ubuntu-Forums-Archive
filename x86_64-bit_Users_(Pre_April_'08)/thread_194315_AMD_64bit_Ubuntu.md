### Post by skippy81 on 2006-06-11
I dont think you will notice the speed gain unless you are doing long CPU intesive operations like encoding media files and possibly compiling stuff.  If you were to rip a DVD you might even get it done 10-20% faster on 64bit.  These figures are just my guesses after using 64 and 32 bit dapper, so take them with a pinch of salt.  

The general speed gain is, at a guess, between 2 and 5% i think, so hardly anything to write home about.  On the other hand, if you are willing to put in a bit of effort to make flash etc work, then it certainly wont do any harm to switch to 64bit.
