### Post by wylfing on 2006-05-16
You're going to have to add your own stations to Rhythmbox. Right-click the Radio icon and select New Internet Radio Station. Paste in the URL for the station's stream.

Some other music applications have stations preinstalled, but not Rhythmbox.

There is an app that can do that job for you. It's called "streamtuner" and you can get it  using Synaptic P. Manager  or  in the terminal type  "sudo apt-get install streamtuner  


What does Streamtuner do?  It collects Radio Broadcast and gives you those list in nice comprehensive way.  When you find the broadcst that you wanna listen, just doble click on it and it launches your default player.

More info on their website: [http://www.nongnu.org/streamtuner/](http://www.nongnu.org/streamtuner/)
