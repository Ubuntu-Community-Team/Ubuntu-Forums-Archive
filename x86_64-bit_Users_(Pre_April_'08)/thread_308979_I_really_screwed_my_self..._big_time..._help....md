### Post by RAOF on 2006-11-29
:shock: That's a **lot** of repositories! :shock:

In general, there's no way to revert such a setup.  However, there are many special cases when you can.

The first thing to do is to work out what is not working.  What error messages, if any, do you get?  Try booting into "Recovery Mode".  What error messages, if any, do you get there?

If you want to be **safe**, you should just reinstall.  Any one of those repositories could have introduced a security hole in your system, even though it is unlikely.
