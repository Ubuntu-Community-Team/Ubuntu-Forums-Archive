### Post by riggsd on 2005-11-02
[QUOTE=mariuss]Could point me to that deal?

[http://www.sun.com/desktop/workstation/ultra20/freeopteronworkstation.html](http://www.sun.com/desktop/workstation/ultra20/freeopteronworkstation.html)

Basically, the Ultra 20 is free, you pay for 3 years of hardware and software support from Sun for $29(US)/month (about $350/year). If you don't want the support, the normal Sun store sells this base model for $895, about $150 cheaper. That $150 will upgrade you to the Quadro DVI video card and a Sun keyboard. I will say that I used to work at a University and Sun's support was excellent, I have not dealt with them for several years though.

It looks like the "free" deal is US only.
