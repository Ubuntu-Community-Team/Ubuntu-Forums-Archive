### Post by rsambuca on 2007-10-06
I really don't think that you will find any more "problems" by using 64bit rather than 32bit.  The difference in the available packages is very small (< 1%), so it is unlikely that you will have problems in that regard.  Nowadays, with the scripts available, even the proprietary plugins like java and flash are just one click away, and things should only get easier with Gutsy.

Odds are, this being linux, you will probably run into some sort of issue down the road, but you will get that regardless of which architecture your OS is running.  You may as well utilize your computer to its full potential and go with the 64bit system.
