### Post by Kilz on 2006-09-14
Click ok, click Settings, then repositories. Highlight the ones you  added then click remove.
You cant add wine with Synaptic in 64bit no matter what repository you find on the wine site. The reason you are getting the error is because there is no 64bit repository. Only a 32bit one you cant use.. Visit the link in my signature for the wine howto for amd64 version.
