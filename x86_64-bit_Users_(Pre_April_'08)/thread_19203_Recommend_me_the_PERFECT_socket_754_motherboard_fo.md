### Post by mips on 2005-03-13
If I had a choice now I would get a genuine Intel MB. The are very reliable & Intel actually provides different flavour Linux drivers for their boards. I know you asked for a AMD board.

Go to to the Intel website, see what board you like and then check the support section for available Linux drivers.

I'm very much a AMD man because I support the underdog and like the bang4buck I get out of the AMD cpu.

Have a look at [http://139.95.253.213/SRVS/CGI-BIN/WEBCGI.EXE/,/?St=4,E=0000000000195908778,K=9847,Sxi=9,Remove=CASES,Problem=OBJ(568](http://139.95.253.213/SRVS/CGI-BIN/WEBCGI.EXE/,/?St=4,E=0000000000195908778,K=9847,Sxi=9,Remove=CASES,Problem=OBJ(568))
