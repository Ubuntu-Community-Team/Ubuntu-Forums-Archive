### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-10-13
If you're concerned about taking a pc out of commission, then invest in a second hard disk for one of the machines.  You can partition it with 2 or 4 main partitions and a swap drive and put Gutsy on it.  This way you will always have Dapper to boot to (since it works) and Gutsy that you can try out on the second disk.  After a while, when Gutsy gets patched and everything is working, you can delete Dapper and free up space for either data or an even newer distro (Fedora 9, Opensuse 11, Linspire 3.0).  

I threw in Linspire to see if you were paying attention.
