### Post by 3rdalbum on 2006-03-13
What BoneKracker means is this:

Rather than just typing "live" at the prompt, you should press Tab. That will list all the booting options. At least one of those options should be 64-bit. (It's probably something like "live-powerpc-64")

Type whatever that particular option is and press return. Will it boot when you do that?
