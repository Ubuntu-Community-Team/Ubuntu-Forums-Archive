### Post by Nonno Bassotto on 2006-07-14
I'm not sure about this, but I heard that often when you are not able to hear sound form multiple apps, it is because some applications use ESD (enlightenment sound daemon), while other connect directly to ALSA (Advanced Linux Sound Architecture).
Try searching the forum about ESD, [this topic]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=44753") and [this]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=32063") should be related to your problem.
