### Post by Drain on 2006-03-12
It is possible to get FreeNX working on AMD64.  However, my experience with it has been... strange.  You have to compile some of the parts to make it work, as there aren't any well-tested pre-compiled .deb's for it.  Search the forums for "AMD64 freenx" and you'll get lots of information.  I understand that some of FreeNX's abilities will eventually get incorporated into KDE 4 (check it out here: [http://wiki.kde.org/tiki-index.php?page=NX%2FFreeNX+integration+into+KDE](http://wiki.kde.org/tiki-index.php?page=NX%2FFreeNX+integration+into+KDE)), but wish/hope that it gets incorporated into Dapper as well.
