### Post by kidders on 2007-03-02
Hi there,

The answer is no, I'm afraid. You would need to manually replace and reconfigure almost everything on your system.

Switching to a 32-bit OS on a 64-bit machine will give you a little performance hit though, since your CPU will spend all day emulating non-native behaviour.
