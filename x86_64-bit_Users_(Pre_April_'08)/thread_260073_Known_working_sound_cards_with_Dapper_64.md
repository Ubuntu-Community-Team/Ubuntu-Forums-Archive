### Post by kick6 on 2006-09-18
I don't know about a compatibility list, but I've had good success with any of the sound blaster cards that use the EMU10k1 module.  This would include sblive!, audigy, audigy2, etc.

also cards based one the envy24 chipset seem to work fine.
