It's a known issue that U_6.06x64 and ASUS_M2N onboard-sound do NOT play nice.  I "solved" the problem by disabling OB_sound in BIOS, and installing  a NONAME 32_bit card  from a local JAX computer shop.   

The card is immediately recognized , and reported as:

     CMEDIA PCI CM18738 

Works the 1st time: cost = $32.
