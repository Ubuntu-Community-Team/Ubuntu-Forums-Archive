### Post by erginemr on 2008-01-17
Sorry, but AFAIK, you pretty much don't stand a chance. On account of the nature of the ext3 file system, it is next to impossible to retrieve a file that has been just deleted, let alone a complete formatted hard drive.

There are several tools (most of them shareware) that are asserting to recover lost files in ext3 systems (Google is your friend), but I don't think that they will do any good.
