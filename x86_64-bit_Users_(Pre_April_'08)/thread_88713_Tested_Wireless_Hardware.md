### Post by ushooz on 2005-11-10
Just wanted to make a quick post on a PCMCIA Wireless Network Card that works on my PowerBook 15" 1.33 Ghz machine.

D-Link DWL-G630 

Hardware Version:C2
Firmware Version: 3.0.1

Installed the Atmel-Firmware package via Synaptic.

Rebooted into OSX and added MAC address to Airport Base Station.

Rebooted to Ubuntu. Went to System/Administration/Networking; activated the card and set the SSID.

Once done I can now browse the net via the new wireless card.

Hope this helps anyone looking into adding a wireless card to their Ubuntu/PPC linux setup.
