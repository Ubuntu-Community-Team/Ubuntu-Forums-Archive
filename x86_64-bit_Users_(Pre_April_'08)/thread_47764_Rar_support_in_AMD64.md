### Post by WMCoolmon on 2005-07-09
1) Download [this file](http://www.rarlab.com/rar/rarlinux-3.5.b5.tar.gz)
2) Extract it ("$ tar -xzf rarlinux-3.5.b5.tar.gz")
3) Go into the "rar" dir
4) Use "sudo checkinstall" (recommended) or "sudo make install" to install the package
5) Double-click on any RAR file.

It may also require "unrar" (nonfree) from the Synaptic repo.
