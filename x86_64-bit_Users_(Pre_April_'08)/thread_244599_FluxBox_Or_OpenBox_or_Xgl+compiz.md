Well, as far as I understand is that fluxbox uses 100% open standards and has been build for speed, so all the eye candy of the larger wms is not included in fluxbox. Xgl / Compiz is nothing but eye-candy. Neither has been optimized for a specific processor as far as I'm aware.

Basically you are choosing between the speed of fluxbox and the niceness of xgl / compiz but with your processor and graphics card I don't think speed is going to be one of your problems :P
