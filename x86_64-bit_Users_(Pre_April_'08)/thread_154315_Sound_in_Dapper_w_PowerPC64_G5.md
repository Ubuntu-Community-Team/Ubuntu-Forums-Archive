### Post by plush on 2006-04-02
In order to make the sound work (this applies to powerpc G5 users), right click on the speaker icon on the top right of the screen, choose to open the volume control. Go to edit, preferences, and check everything. Click on switches, and then unclick pc speaker, and click line out. Man, that's it! (This is since the latest update to kernel, 2.6.15-20.30).

This used to not work only a few weeks ago, btw.
