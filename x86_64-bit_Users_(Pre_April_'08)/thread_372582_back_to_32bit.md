It is a complete reinstall, you will lose everything. If you are under the mistaken idea that by installing the 32bit version you wont have to do any setup and its problem free . I refer you to the [aboslute beginners]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=73") forum and the [General help area]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=131"). They are full of posts by 32bit users having problems with things you need to set up and problems with the 32bit version.
The fact is , that there is no such thing as a version of Linux you dont have to do some setup with. If you are having some problem, please post in the 64bit section about the problem, dont give up. The setup required is small, and once its done, its done.
