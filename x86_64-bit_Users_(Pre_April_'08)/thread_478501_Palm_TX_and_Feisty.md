### Post by brookiemonsta on 2007-06-19
capecove,

 **[Evolution]("http://www.gnome.org/projects/evolution/")** is an integrated email, addressbook, calendar, memo & tasks program similar to Outlook on Windoze. The** [Gnome Pilot Applet]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/feisty/gnome/gnome-pilot")** synchronises very nicely between Evolution and my TX on x64 Feisty.

 Evolution is integrated very closely with Gnome if that's the desktop you use, so that the calendar, tasks and appointments show up in a drop down window when you click on the clock in the panel. Nice for being able to quickly check what's coming up soon.

 I had a look at Sunbird a while back. Looks interesting, but not in the same class as Evolution.

 brookiemonsta
