Though I'm a native English speaker, I occasionally find myself needing tildes, accent marks and umlauts for typing in Spanish and German. ### Post by dombleu on 2006-01-20
Under System/preferences/keyboard you can load a hell lot of keyboard agencements that you can switch from a taskbar applet.

Or from Applications/accessories/character table you can copy paste even more weirdo symbols.

Hope it helps!
