### Post by j0eb0b on 2007-05-28
In my experience with both Linux and MS OS systems, unintended automatic restarts usually are hardware problems.  A power supply that is failing or undersized will cause this type of thing as will changing bios setting, used in over clocking.  Insufficient cooling/overheating can also cause automatic restarts. 

Is this an established Dapper machine that just began to fail?  Have there been any recent changes (such as replacement of a  video card) that may render the power supply insufficient?  I am an over clocker and have seen this type of thing under Ubuntu when I push the memory timings/FSB too hard.  I would consider an environmental cause.  Do you know the hardware specs of the machine?
