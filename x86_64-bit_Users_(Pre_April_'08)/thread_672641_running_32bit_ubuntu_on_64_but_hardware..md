### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-01-19
> Can I migrate Ubuntu to the 64bit version without a reinstall? 
No.

> What do i stand to gain by going to 64 bit?
Speed improvements, especially in multimedia apps. Also, the ability to support more than ~3GB RAM. For more details, read the stickied threads at the top of this forum.

Would I take a chance on messing up a working install that I had spent substantial time on just to move to 64-bit? No, I personally would not. However, some people here might feel differently. It's your call though.
