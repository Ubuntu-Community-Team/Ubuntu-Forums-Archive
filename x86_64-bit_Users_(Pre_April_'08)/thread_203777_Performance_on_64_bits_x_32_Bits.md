### Post by RAOF on 2006-06-25
For maximum performance, Ubuntu64.  Multimedia encoding/decoding is one of the big wins for x86-64.  From memory of the last benchmark I saw, encoding with lame was something like 33% faster under x86-64 than IA32, and that was representative of other codec tasks.
