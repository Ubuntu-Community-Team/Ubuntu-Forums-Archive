### Post by YldGuy on 2008-03-31
do u see the volume control on your system task bar.. right click and choose "Open Volume Control".

Inside, you will see a volume control.. depending on the sound engine you have chosen you will see different settings for mic, line in etc etc... 

so first go and select "File" and change the device to "Alsa Mixer"... now you must be able to see different mixers and some tabs too... for me the tabs are named "Playback" "recording" "switches" "options"... yours might be different... in there the "Switches" tab is the one which has the headphones checkbox.. check it... now you must be able to listen via headphones..
