### Post by ssam on 2005-10-13
those function should be bound to the middle click.
in most linux browsers middle click is open in new tab.
in x11 (the linux graphics system) highlight is copy, and middle click is paste.

a single button mouse should just do left click. middle and right are bound to the keyboard buttons F11 and F12.

i am not sure how to fix the bindings, but that might be enough info to get you started.
