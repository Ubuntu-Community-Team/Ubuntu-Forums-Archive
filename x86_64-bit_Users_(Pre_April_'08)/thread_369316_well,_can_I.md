### Post by rsambuca on 2007-02-24
The 64bit version is NOT "quite buggy".  It runs very well.  The only place where it is tougher to set-up and people can have problems is when using proprietory closed-source software like Adobe's Macromedia Flash Player (even then, there are ways to get it to work).

If you are doing multimedia and video editing, I would strongly suggest you try to stay with 64bit, as you will find upwards of 30% performance gain over the 32bit version in these areas.
