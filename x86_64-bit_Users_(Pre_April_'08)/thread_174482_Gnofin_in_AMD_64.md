### Post by maury on 2006-05-11
I have Dapper AMD_64 on a drive and managed to get gnofin installed. In the past I could never do it. You can download it from debian but not through ubuntu. I put it in a folder and clicked on it and got a message that it needed 
gdk-imlib1,so I opened up the sources list using synaptic to un-official maintained repositories and added  gdk-imlib1 . I then clicked on the gnofin package and it installed ok. Thought I would put this out to anyone else that needs it. :)
