### Post by jimjawn on 2006-02-07
Basically, you have 4 main choices:

MPlayer
Xine
VLC
Totem

but all of them are sort of interchangeable.  I'm a fan of the plain old totem player.  Check out [Easy Ubuntu]("http://easyubuntu.freecontrib.org/") and install the multimedia packages.  

Alternatively, you can enable the multiverse and universe repositories in synaptic and install totem-gstreamer.

Personally, I removed tote-gstreamer and installed totem-xine.  I find that it runs the files I want much better, looks the nicest and is the easiest to configure and run out of the box.

Hope that helps.
