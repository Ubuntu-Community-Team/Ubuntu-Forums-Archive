Well, there are two answers here.  If you're very good with Linux and want to try some very alpha code, there is a semi-working driver out there. However, this is difficult to set up at the moment, and will likely require you to update to Dapper Drake (the next release).  Also, keep in mind that features such as Encryption and other security measures are likely not supported yet.  If you're feeling brave, then try the steps detailed in this thread:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=99486]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=99486")

However, if you'd rather not go through the instability and trouble, you can wait it out.  Chances are that this driver will be around for an upcoming release of Ubuntu (maybe even Dapper), or at least more full-featured for individual installing.  Also, USB Wifi dongles aren't that expensive, and seem to do the trick.

Hope that helps!
