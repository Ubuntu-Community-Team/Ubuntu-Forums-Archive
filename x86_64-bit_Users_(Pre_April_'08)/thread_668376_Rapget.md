### Post by Steveway on 2008-01-15
I would advise to use Jdownloader.
Jdownloader not only supports Rapidshare but also a whole lot of more 1-click-hoster.
It can watch your cache for supported links and it'll pop up a little dialog where you can choose the destination. Everything else will be done automatically, Captcha, Waiting Time etc.
It can even Reconnect your Router (If it's supported and you choosed the correct one.)
Even though it uses Java, it is not very bloated. And it can even use your GTK-theme.
[https://www.syncom.org/projects/jdownloader/](https://www.syncom.org/projects/jdownloader/)
You can download it here: [http://jdownloaderinstall.ath.cx/](http://jdownloaderinstall.ath.cx/) (Rapidshare link, pretty ironic.^^)
