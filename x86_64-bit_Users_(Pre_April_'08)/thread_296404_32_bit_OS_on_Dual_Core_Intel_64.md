### Post by dbott67 on 2006-11-09
My understanding is that the 32-bit version of 6.06.1 would be your best bet for stability and reliability.  You wouldn't be taking full advantage of the 64-bit CPU, but you would gain stability and access to more apps & drivers (as 64-bit support in those areas can be limited at times).

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/64-bit#32_vs_64_bit](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/64-bit#32_vs_64_bit)
