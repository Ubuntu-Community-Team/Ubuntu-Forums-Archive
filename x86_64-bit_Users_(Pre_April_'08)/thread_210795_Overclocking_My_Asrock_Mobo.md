Hi All
I've got a question regarding overclocking my amd athlon 3500+ venice.
The motherboard I have is an Asrock 939NF4G-SATA2.

I can boot into windows at 11 * 220 but Ubuntu just hangs after the login window. 
Ubuntu is stable around 11 * 210.

My question is why I can boot into windows but not into Ubuntu with that speed? My mobo obviously can handle it 'cause it seemed stable in xp.

Thanks in advance

---

### Post by JoWilly on 2006-07-07
This means that it "seems" stable in windows, but it actually is not. Running heavy benchmarks like 3d mark will show you this.

Most of the time my systems are stable at higher clocks on linux than on windows, but not always as is your case.

The best for you is to run at 210 on windows and linux.
