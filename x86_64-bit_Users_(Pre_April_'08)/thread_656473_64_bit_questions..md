### Post by ~LoKe on 2008-01-02
AMD just named themselves after their AMD64 instructions.  It'll run on any 64bit processor.

As for the packages...all the packages in the repositories have 32 and 64bit versions.  So, anything official will be available for 64bit.  As for other packages...well...it's not guaranteed that there are 64bit versions, but most 32bit applications should work just the same.
