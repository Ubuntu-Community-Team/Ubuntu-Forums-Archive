### Post by Cappy on 2007-06-14
Thread is here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=425672](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=425672)

[http://home.comcast.net/~ubuntume/nsplugin-wrapper-install-0-1.1.tar.gz](http://home.comcast.net/~ubuntume/nsplugin-wrapper-install-0-1.1.tar.gz)

Extract it on your Desktop, goto the folder it extracted to, double click "nspw install", when prompted click "Run".
