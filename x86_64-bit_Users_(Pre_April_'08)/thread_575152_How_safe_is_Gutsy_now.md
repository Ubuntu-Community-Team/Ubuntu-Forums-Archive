You will get varying views on Gutsy regarding it's current stability level. 

Every members experience will be different some will have no issues what so ever, and you will have those who will have issues. 

You have to judge the OS's readiness for yourself, and decide if you want to deploy it on your production systems.

IMHO you are better off installing it on a separate partition for now, As this will allow you test it, and judge it's stability without loosing the currently install version of Ubuntu you are using.
