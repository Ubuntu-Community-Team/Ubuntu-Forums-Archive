### Post by Ribs on 2006-05-24
Hi,

Don't panic, your question is valid.

To be honest, Dapper isn't out the door yet, so it's very very early to be talking about Edgy... The mixed 32/64-bit enviroment thing which was being spoken about is indeed what you describe; much better 32-bit compatability in the 64-bit enviroment. However, that announcement was only Mr Shuttleworth 'guessing' at what's going to happen. He's basically said the developers have a free run to do whatever they like with edgy, and he is mearly guessing that x y and z will happen.

So for now, look forward to better 32-bit in 64-bit land... but don't count on anything right now...
