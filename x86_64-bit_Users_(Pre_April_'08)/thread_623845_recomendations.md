### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2007-11-26
If Compiz eye-candy and 3D gaming are important to you, then I would advise avoiding newer ATI cards (X1k and RadeonHD 2k series) if possible. The drivers just aren't there yet. Sure, the latest releases support AIGLX, but the performance is still sub-par and the rest of the driver is buggy (screen corruption, memory leaks, etc.)

However, if you can live without Compiz and Windows-equivalent 3D for a few more driver releases, then I'd say ATI graphics would be a good choice.
