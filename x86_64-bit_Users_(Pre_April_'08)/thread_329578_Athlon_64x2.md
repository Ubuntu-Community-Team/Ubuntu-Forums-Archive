### Post by ~LoKe on 2007-01-01
It will work just fine.  There's even a specific kernel for 64 bit processors (x86).  It's labeled AMD, but it also works with 64bit Intel processors (Core 2 Duo).  A common misconception is that the "AMD" refers to AMD processors, but it's more than that.
