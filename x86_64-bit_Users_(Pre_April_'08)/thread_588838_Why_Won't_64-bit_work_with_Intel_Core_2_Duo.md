### Post by dewey on 2007-10-23
Try checking the md5 sum of the iso after downloading it, if that checks out, then burn the disc at 4x speed.

Once the disc is burned, boot the computer using the live cd as normal, and use the option to check the cd for defects, or verify the integrity.  (Can't remember what they called it exactly on 7.10)
