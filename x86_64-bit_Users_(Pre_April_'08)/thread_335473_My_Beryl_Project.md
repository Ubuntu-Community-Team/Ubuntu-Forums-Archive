### Post by Sqwishy on 2007-01-10
When I first saw sun microsystem's '[project looking glass]("http://http://www.sun.com/software/looking_glass/")' i was amazed at the potential and possibility that that had. However the project soon left my interest as i heard about Novell's [compiz]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compiz"). I was very exited about this project and showed some people the movie that Novell had put on their web site, I tried to install it on my own computer, but neither me or my geeklord of a brother could figure out why it wasn't working. Then compiz-quinstorm came out, i had a new computer at the time and when i first tryed it for myself on my own computer my infatuation became obsesion. I didn't show very many people the movie at the time, nor did i tell very many people about it. Mostly I just told my friends... err friend. Then after a while came cgwd. That didn't last long and shortly after... [Beryl]("http://www.beryl-project.org/") when i switched from gentoo back to ubuntu and installed beryl, i was amazed. Not only by it's ability and potential, but how easy it was. That was the day i fell in love 8} I told almost everyone i could, I found that [youtube has several screencast's]("http://http://youtube.com/results?search_query=beryl&search=Search") the demonstrated well a lot of beryl's potential. Still today I've still been telling everyone about this project and showing them screencast's on youtube. Every single one of them have been amazed, with the exception of one person. One particular person i showed it too who had previously HATED linux, was amazed and taken a back by beryl. During the conversation we had i told him about the control, the power, how fun it is and easy and that everything is customizable. And in the very end he gave me the most challenging question, "Yeah but how much does it cost!". :-D I proceeded to tell him about the beauty of linux being free as in freedom. After our conversation he didn't promise to install linux but he did take of his hate label. Anyway, that was just the past story of where my love began.
In the present i'm telling you about my beryl project at school. Given the reactions of people who have seen the beryl movie, i'm betting that showing them the real thing will even draw them in more. So before summer break i started installing ubuntu and beryl on a spare computer at school, i failed numerously. After the break i continued and after the first hour i worked on it, i had got beryl working. I felt so happy inside 8} I had used the nvidia-glx-legacy drivers because the video card was to old. And because i did that i needed to install XGL, eventualy i got beryl working. The next day (yesterday) i fixed the theming and it looks pretty! Here are some of the compy's specs.
Intel Processor 733mhz
256mb ram
some really old Nvidia card with 64mb ram i think?
That old computer runs beryl, and not at 2fps either. It is actually usable, and sqwishy likes it :) 
So this is my project! Setting up beryl on a school computer for people to see and become obsessed with. What do you think?

If anyone post's TLDR i will come to your home and i will cut you!!

Edit: does anyone care or did i write all that for nothing...
