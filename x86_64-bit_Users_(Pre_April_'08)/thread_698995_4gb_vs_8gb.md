### Post by yetanothersteve on 2008-02-18
If you feel you need the RAM and can afford it, get it, I made that decision after trying to run two Virtual machines (one with 2GB allocated and the other with 1GB allocated and both were using all allocated.) And I had very little free memory (out of 4GB) left for the host and no hope of running Rational Application Developer on the host. So, I noticed that another 4GB(2GBX2) of DDR2 800 was only $92.99 US with a $20 rebate and I bought it.

Buying the RAM is a lot cheaper than building another machine.

As a buddy of mine once put it: "I wish I could add RAM to my shoes."
