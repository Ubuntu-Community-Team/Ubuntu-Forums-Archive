### Post by 3rdalbum on 2006-01-16
Start up the Mac OS, then put in the CD. Does it mount? If the drive sounds like it's having trouble (that's a sound that all tray-load iMac owners know!), try burning the image onto an audio CD-R. You know, one of those ones which has "For Music" or "Recordable For Consumer" on the case. They tend to work well in iMacs. I recommend the TDK XA80s.
