### Post by mikeymouse on 2007-04-27
Well I feel proud of myself.
 I have a pentium 930 dual boot with Xandros 4.0 profesional and Windows XP media.
 I wanted Ubuntu Feisty 64 installed but i didn't want to mess up my current settup so.
 I  disconnected my internal drive and replace it with a new sata drive.
 I installed ubuntu64 final on it and then took the drive out replaced the original dual boot drive and all ran fine.
 Next I put the ubuntu64 drive into a Vantec external harddrive enclosure  and connected it to my system.
 All i have to do now is boot from the external and i have Ubuntu 64 up and running..
  Just thought than anyone else with an install problem might get some benefit from my setup.
