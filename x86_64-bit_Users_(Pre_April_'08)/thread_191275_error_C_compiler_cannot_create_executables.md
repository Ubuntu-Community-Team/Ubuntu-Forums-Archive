### Post by Mike Kimmick on 2006-07-24
I too have and amd64 X2 and getting the same error when trying to run configure script for wine.  I have build-essentials installed, so I went looking elsewhere for similar problems.  I remember having this issue even under RedHat under certain situations.  Everything I found kept pointing to glibc-devel not being installed.  After looking through Synaptic for anything related to libc, I discovered I have lib6-devel installed, but not lib6-devel-i386.  Under my circumstances, installing lib6-devel-i386 solved the problem.  Hope this helps the rest of you.
