### Post by RAOF on 2005-10-23
Pressing Ctrl-Alt- and a function key (F1,F2,etc) will change virtual terminals in linux.  One of the terminals will have the installer running, another will have some more detailed output on it, and at least one will have a command prompt for you to play with :)
