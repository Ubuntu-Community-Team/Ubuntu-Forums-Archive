### Post by RAOF on 2006-08-02
Pick your favourite i386 guide, and replace the "xgl.compiz.info" repository  with either "ubuntu.systemadministrator.org" or "ubuntu.moshen.de" (or both).  If you don't want a bunch of unrelated stuff (like, for example, a backport of Edgy's MONO stack), use only the "eyecandy" section of those repositories.

That's pretty much the sole difference between i386 and AMD64 installs at the moment.
