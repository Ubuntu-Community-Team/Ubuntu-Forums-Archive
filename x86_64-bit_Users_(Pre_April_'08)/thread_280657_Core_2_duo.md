### Post by kuja on 2006-10-19
Yes, though whether or not you notice it will depend on what you're doing/what applications you're using. Things like graphics rendering, audio/video encoding, and precision math can see great speed benefits (upwards of 30%). Simple things like browsing, writing a letter, or watching a movie won't see a significant performance difference though. If you don't do anything that would see a real benefit it's probably not worth the full reinstall though, but that's something for you to decide.
