Hi,
  I just want to save you guys' time for any problem of installing/upgrading Flash player 9 for 64bit firefox.  I used Update Manger to upgrade my flash player, but it didn't work somehow.  So, I uninstalled it.  I remembered that the newer version of firefox has ubufox as an addon installed in default, it will download the flash player for you if you don't have one and went to a webpage that requires one. I tried it that way, and it works for the version 9.  

Somehow ubufox did something right that the update manager didn't.

Hope it help.
