### Post by AlphaMack on 2006-03-31
If your hard drive is about to die, your S.M.A.R.T. status should tell you.  You could grab an excellent utility that is both available for Ubuntu and OS X (via Darwin Ports) called smartmontools.  DiskWarrior and Disk Utility can also read your S.M.A.R.T. status but won't give you much details.
