### Post by ssam on 2006-02-11
Sometimes Ubuntu does not configure the sound levels very well by default.

Right click on the volume control in the Gnome panel, and choose Open Volume Control. Now Select Preferences from the Edit menu. You will be shown a list of all the options from your sound card, each with a check box. Enable them all and click ok.

Now adjust the volume sliders and see if that helps
