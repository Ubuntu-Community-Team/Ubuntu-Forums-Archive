### Post by jdp on 2006-03-29
The LiveCD needs at least 128Mb RAM to run.  You can install plenty in a Lombrad for fairly cheap.  It takes low profile PC100 SO-DIMMs.  It should take two 256MB DIMMs to get to 512MB if you want.  At least adding another 64, but even better, a 128 or 256 will make almost anything you do on the machine much better, for OS X or Linux.
