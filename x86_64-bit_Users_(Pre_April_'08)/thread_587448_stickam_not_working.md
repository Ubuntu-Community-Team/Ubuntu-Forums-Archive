### Post by sean32 on 2007-12-12
HI

Stickam is a bit of a pig on ubuntu, especialy with webcam support.
The best way to get it working is to use a tv card with a camcorder or webcam with composite video out. and connect it to the video in socket.
heres how to do it with most cards. you will need to install vlc

to get sound and video in stickam

start vlc
click file, open, open captue device

on video device name add a 0 to the end so it says /dev/video0

on norm select pal  (or ntsc for america)

click advanced options

change channel to 1
click ok
click ok

a picture should now appear on vlc player.

open the stickam website and click go live

wait until it has loaded

(if it wont load click on the right side of window and drag to widen it a bit, then it will appear.)

click allow when it asks to use your webcam

when loaded you will still see no picture, but thats ok

close vlc

close stickam window

click go live again

your picture should now appear

IF PROBLEMS WITH SOUND AS WELL

SOUND

doubleclick speaker icon

click edit, preferences

tick line in capture

close and close again

doubleclick speaker icon again

click switches

click line in capture

close
