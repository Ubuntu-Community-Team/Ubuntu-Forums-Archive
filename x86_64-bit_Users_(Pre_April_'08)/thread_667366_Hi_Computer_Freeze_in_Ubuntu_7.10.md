### Post by NightwishFan on 2008-01-25
Hi. I am a fairly new to Linux as well but it is like a breath of fresh air leaving the Windows world. My advice is, see if you can view the files in the terminal.

Open a terminal and type:

cd /home/YOURUSERNAME/Examples

push enter then type:

ls


It should list the files in the directory, or perhaps give you a reason why it can't.

Remember linux is case sensitive. examples is different from Examples or EXAMPLES and so forth. When you put your username in remember to use the correct case.
