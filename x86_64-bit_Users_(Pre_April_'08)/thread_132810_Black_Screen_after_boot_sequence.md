### Post by 3rdalbum on 2006-02-19
We're going to need more information than this to start to solve your problem. What computer are you using? Is the screen simply blank, or has the monitor actually turned off? Does the Live CD work?

The congo drums sound plays when you reach the login screen, but it can also play when GNOME brings up a message box. When the drums play, try typing your username, press return, type your password, and press return again. After a few seconds you might hear the Ubuntu startup sound (with synthesised strings and a triangle). If this happens, then your problem is not something that GNOME has detected.

Two screens might be a problem for Ubuntu without extra software, but I haven't heard of this before.
