You'll have to change the **monitor** section to suit your display, if you try to run the wrong frequencies through it, you could kill it, Caveat Emptor and all that.

I'm also using a Logitech (3 button/mouse-wheel) Optical mouse (red beam) and a UK 105 key Keyboard, you may want to copy those sections out of your your xorg.conf too.

If you change keyboards, or mice in X it can be really unhappy about it. X is hardwired, not plug and play like windows. You may want to leave out the **DisplaySize** option as I added that, (calculating the values accordingly) to make my fonts properly 96dpi on a 1600x1200 display.
