### Post by engla on 2006-03-04
Do you have AirPort Express? (A newer powerbook?)
If you do, it can't work out-of-the-box as I understand it (for legal reasons). You have to extract the firmware from the Apple driver and install into ubuntu.

I don't have the full details, my APE is not working correctly right now but I'm waiting until dapper to get it straight again.

As I understand it, it will work better in Dapper than in Breezy, but the needed things should already be on the LiveCD (but not the firmware, as above).

Search for bcm43xx (the driver) if you want to know more.
