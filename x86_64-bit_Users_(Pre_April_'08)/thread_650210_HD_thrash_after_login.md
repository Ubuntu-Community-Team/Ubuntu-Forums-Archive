### Post by crjackson on 2007-12-26

Your system is probably indexing the HD files on your system.  I personally turn this off on all my installs, but others love it.  I'm told that once your system is fully indexed, you won't see too much activity from it.  It's there to make for lightening fast search results when looking for someting (a file) on your system.

I'm not a home right now so I can't look at it to tell you how to turn it off.  It SHOULD be one of the following...

system>administration>indexing

or

system>preferences>indexing

Go there, turn it off and see if that helps..
