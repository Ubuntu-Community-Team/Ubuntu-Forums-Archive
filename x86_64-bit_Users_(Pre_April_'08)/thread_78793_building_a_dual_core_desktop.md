### Post by Kuolio on 2005-10-19
Looks good to me, but...

SuperLanboy case, why? Sure it shines and blinks and looks cool, but in my experience it's cramped inside (cant get decent air or watercooling going) and with default fans it's noisy. It also makes lots of resonating sounds. So I'd suggest to change that for something else.. like maybe Antec p180 or if you have the money to blow, Lian-Li v1100, 2100 or similar.

And to get the best kick out of using 64bit system, buy as much memory as you can afford (remember dualchannel), maybe something like 4gigs?
