### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-02-11
Right click on the panel where you want the shortcut.
Click the "custom Application Launcher" button.
For the command field, type: firefox32
Give it whatever name/description you want.
Click the icon, and copy/paste this: /usr/share/pixmaps/firefox.png
Click OK twice.
Fin.
