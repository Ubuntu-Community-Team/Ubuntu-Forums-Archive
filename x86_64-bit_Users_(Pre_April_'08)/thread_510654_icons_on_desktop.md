### Post by NeuralEskimo on 2007-07-26
Are you running KDE or Gnome?

In KDE, 
1) From the K menu, click on "System Settings"
2) Under "Look & Feel" (the 2nd row of icons), click on Desktop
3) In the left-most panel, click on Behavior
4) Under the "General" tab, check the very first check box (labeled "Show icons on desktop")

For Gnome, you need instructions from someone else (I'm a KDE user). Let us know how it turns out.
