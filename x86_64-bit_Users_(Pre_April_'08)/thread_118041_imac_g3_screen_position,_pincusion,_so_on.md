### Post by sulobanks on 2006-01-17
xvidtune can help a little. But it's a bit limited.  You can move the screen horizontally and vertically, but you can't rotate it.  There is a way to adjust this physically on the imac by taking part of it apart and sticking a screwdriver into dangerous parts, but I wouldn't recommend that unless you know exactly what you're doing.  It's beyond me why Apple didn't provide the basic screen adjust functionality on the imac monitors. It seems to be a standard feature on just about every other monitor I've seen.
