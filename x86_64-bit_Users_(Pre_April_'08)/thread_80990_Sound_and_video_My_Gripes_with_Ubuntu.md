### Post by ssam on 2005-10-23
are you running the smp (Symmetric MultiProcessing) kernel?

search for linux-powerpc in synaptic. and install the smp one.
you dont need to uninstall the normal one.

if you open volume control, by right clicking (F12) on the volume control in the gnome menu bar, and go to its prefferences, then tick all the boxes. you will get lots of sliders, you may need to work out the best ballance between them.
