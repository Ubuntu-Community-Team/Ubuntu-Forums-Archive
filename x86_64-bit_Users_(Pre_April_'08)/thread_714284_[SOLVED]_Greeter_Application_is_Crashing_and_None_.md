Upgrades from one version to another are never 100% guaranteed to work. Thats why most people suggest that you backup anything you cant afford to loose.  As I see it you have a few options.
1. Continue to try and find out exactly how to fix this. Since you have tried a few things it may be easier to do one of the following.
2. Do a clean install over the broken install. You will loose everything as it will format the partition first. If you have backups this is the best solution.
3. Install to a new partition. Then mount your old partition and copy over any files you need. Once thats done, delete the old partition to reclaim space.
