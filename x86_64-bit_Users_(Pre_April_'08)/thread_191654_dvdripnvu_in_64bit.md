### Post by harro0209 on 2006-06-08
dvdrip works fine here on AMD64. I installed dvdrip and its dependencies using the Automatix script ([http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=185604]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=185604"))
You have to change the NPTL workaround settings (in the preferences dialog), otherwise dvdrip won't find most of the needed executables.
