### Post by insane_alien on 2007-06-24
there shouldn't be any difference. if the process load is identical it will have the same drain.

If you can, undervolting and under clocking the CPU slightly can squeeze a few minutes out of the battery as it won't get as hot and the fan won't run as often. running off a flash drive can also provide a slightly longer battery life.

disabling as much as possible will also save a bit of energy.
