Which Audigy card do you have?  Some Audigy cards (the Audigy LS) for example, will not set up correctly.  You need to start alsaconf and turn up the 'front analog' slider to truly turn it up on these cards.  (i think this is specific to the ca1016 driver on the audigy cards, but am unsure)

### Post by two00lbwaster on 2007-11-03
I have the same card and had the same problem with it not working with either the OSS or ALSA drivers.  The reason and the solution was to simply to go to right click the volume control in the panel and select Open Volume Control.  Click on the Switches tab and untick Audigy Analog/Digital Output Jack.

You can now use the test buttons to confirm that it works.  Once you have sorted out your ALSA problems.  OSS also works by the way in the mean time.  Change through System>Preferences>Sound of course
