### Post by rsambuca on 2007-10-08
Note that Gutsy is still beta software, so you will have many updates over the next couple of weeks, and expect a few bugs.  If you want the stable release, you will have to wait for a couple of weeks to carry out those instructions.  If you can't wait and wish to experiment with the beta version, then back-up your data and execute the following command in a terminal```
gksudo "update-manager -c -d"
```.
