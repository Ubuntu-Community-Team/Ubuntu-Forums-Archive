### Post by ebonflame on 2005-05-06
The semprons definately are NOT 64 bit processors.  There are actually two varieties of Semprons out there.  One is  based off of the older SocketA Thorobred (sp) cores. 

The other variety is based off of the Socket 754 Athlon64 processors but either do not have the 64 bit part built in or simply have the 64 bit part disabled on die.  They also have a smaller L2 chache, usually 256 or 128 KB depending on the model.  This sounds to be the type you have.  Since they share the same board with their 64 bit siblings, I am not surprised about the driver confusion.
