### Post by linbetwin on 2005-11-17
[quote=vimo]How can I install and use Gnome and KDE together (like many other distro do)?
And how can I choose which of them to use during start-up (i.e. see Mandrake or Suse).[/quote]
 
Install kubuntu-desktop or ubuntu-desktop, depending on which of them you have right now.
 
To choose between them, click on "Sessions" at the login screen and select either GNOME or KDE.
