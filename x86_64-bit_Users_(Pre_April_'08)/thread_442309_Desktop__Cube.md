### Post by cmost on 2007-05-13
I absolutely love the eye candy.  Not because I'm a show-off, mind you, but because some of the effects are actually useful.  The cube, the switcher, the task bar thumbnails, and the Expose feature are just a few.  Beryl and Compix work beautifully if you have a fairly recent nvidia graphics card.  You don't even need to activate AIGLX (and certainly not XGL) if you're using the proprietary nvidia driver.  Simply add a couple of lines to the xorg.conf and you're good to go.  Bless nvidia!
