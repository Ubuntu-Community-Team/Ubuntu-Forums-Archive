### Post by RAOF on 2006-11-26
You might as well compile from source.  The Edgy (6.10) kernel is built with Edgy's toolchain (gcc, libc, etc), and may or may not actually work properly on a Dapper system.  Additionally, there's no (supported/easy) way to get just the kernel from the Edgy repositories.
