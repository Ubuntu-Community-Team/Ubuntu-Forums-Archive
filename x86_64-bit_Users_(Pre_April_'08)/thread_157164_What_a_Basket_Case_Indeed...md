### Post by skylark on 2006-04-08
Try right clicking on where you think it should be added (rightclick on your the taskbar that is). There is an option at the top "add to panel". Select this and scroll down a bit and you should be able to find a trash icon. If it ends up in the wrong spot, right click on it and select move, then you can move it around (if move isn't available, unclick "lock to panel" and try again). Correct me if I misread your post...
