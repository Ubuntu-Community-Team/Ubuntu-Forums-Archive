At this point you may want to at least consider Feisty over Dapper. There isnt that much difference in end of life on the desktop. Dapper will end in June 2009, Feisty in Jan 2009. While Dapper has slightly more life left , Feisty is a better distro with a lot of improvements over Dapper.
We also know that the next long term release will be Gutsy +1, or the release after Gutsy. Upgrading from Release to Release is easy. But there is no way to skip a release. So if you start on Feisty, you can upgrade to Gutsy and then Gutsy +1. If you start on Dapper, you will have 4 upgrades to get to the next long term. 
Feisty is also stable, its 4 months old and runs real nice. The only thing you need to think about is how long to wait after Gutsy is released to allow the update manager to upgrade to Gutsy. I would leave it go a month or so if stability is a main concern.
