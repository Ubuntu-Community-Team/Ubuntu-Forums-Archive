### Post by MaxAmp on 2007-08-10
I have an old HP laserjet 4L printer and a laptop with no parallel port.  I have a dual boot with Ubuntu 7.04 64 bit and Vista 64 bit (for games).

I bought a IOGEAR usb to parallel printer cable (GUC1284B) and I am happy to report that it works out of the box with both operating systems! \\:D/  It costs about $25 dollars with shipping.
