### Post by kuja on 2006-09-07
Well, when you burn the disk, in the initial menu, there should be an option "check disk". Arrow down to it and press enter... If you get crazy errors, or the test won't even run, that usually means it's a bad disk. If it is in fact bad or that doesn't work for you, try reburning at a significantly lower speed... think I did mine at 4x. Good luck to ya.
