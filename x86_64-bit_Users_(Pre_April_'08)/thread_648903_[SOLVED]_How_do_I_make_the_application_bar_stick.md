### Post by Kilz on 2007-12-24
System > Preferences > Sessions  on sessions options tab make sure to save the session. You will also have to remove the bar at the bottom or move it.

### Post by daengbo on 2007-12-24
If you have two bars, it might be easier to make just one panel and make it thicker, with everything placed on that one panel. You can also replace the main meno with a more compact version resembling the "Start" menu from Windows.
