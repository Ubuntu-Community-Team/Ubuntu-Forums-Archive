### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-04-28
Whenever you resolve a thread, (bumped or not), feel free to add this key to your post in the text:

**UAResolved**

This:[LIST=1]
[*]Helps out the Bump search method skip over posts that were marked resolved
[*]Lets you see the UA team's progress by searching for UAResolved.[/LIST]For example:
[quote="The Sample Poster"]Try using Ndiswrapper with your wireless card. Then do ```
sudo /etc/init.d/networking restart
```UAResolved.[/quote]

It is up to you *where* in the thread you post it (such as after a user replies back, or right when you reply).
