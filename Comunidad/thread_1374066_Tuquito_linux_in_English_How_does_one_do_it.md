### Post by alfplayer on 2010-01-13
You can try Alt-F2 to bring the "run application" box. There you can type "xterm", "gnome-terminal", or "konsole" to run a terminal. xterm is a generic terminal, gnome-terminal is Gnome's terminal and konsole is KDE's terminal.

Tuquito and most distributions aren't very different in terms of hardware support, so you may want to give a second try to a more popular distro that defaults to a language you can understand.
