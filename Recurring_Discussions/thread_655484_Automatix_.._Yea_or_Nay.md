### Post by maniacmusician on 2008-01-03
It's true that Automatix did use to break things often for people by installing 3rd party things. The breakage usually happened when people tried to upgrade to the next version, and there was no upgrade candidate for the 3rd party packages.

I never had a breakage because of them, simply because I was careful and knew what I was doing. But I can definitely see that new users wouldn't have been able to avoid those problems like I did.

However, the AX team recently made a post to the forums in good conscience about how they were revamping it to work better and not break things. They're actually supposed to be working with Ubuntu developers right now to make sure that it doesn't cause breakages (I don't know how well that's going, haven't been keeping track). I think the future is looking better for Automatix...hopefully.
