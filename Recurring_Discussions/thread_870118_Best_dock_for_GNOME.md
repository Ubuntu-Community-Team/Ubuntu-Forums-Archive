### Post by tjwoosta on 2008-07-30
> What theme did you use for AWN? And also, how do you add launchers? I have asked like 12 times! What are the "commands" for other programs?!

i used the default theme

then i enabled the 3d look in preferences and unchecked the box that says enable rounded corners


also on my AWN i have launchers as well as stacks 

for the stacks and other applets you need to first need to remove your existing AWN  

then add theese repositories one at a time that you can find here in this link
[https://launchpad.net/~reacocard-awn/+archive]("https://launchpad.net/~reacocard-awn/+archive")

(just go to "system-administration-software sources" then click the "third-party software" tab and click add, then just copy and paste to add them and only do one at a time)

after adding the repositories do this

```
sudo apt-get install avant-window-navigator-bzr awn-core-applets-bzr awn-manager-bzr

```

for applets you can just go to the preferences and click applets then click on an applet and choose activate

for the launchers you can just go to the preferences and click launchers then click add

(to figure out the commands for programs you can simply right click on existing desktop icons then choose properties and click the last tab on the right)
