### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-08-02
The core issue in the case of the notification area was that it was inconsistant. To show the main window, some programs might require a single-click on the notification icon, and some might require a double-click. Qt programs and GTK programs would look different in the notification area, especially their menu.

The Indicator Applet attempts to address these issues and increase consistency between different programs.
