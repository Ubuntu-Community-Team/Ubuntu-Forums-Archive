### Post by Gallahhad on 2010-02-19
It's probably been said already, but here goes anyway.

There is no Linux equivalent to Windows, nor is there a Linux equivalent to Mac. There is not Windows equivalent to Linux, there is not Windows equivalent to Mac. There is no Mac equivalent to Windows, there is no Mac equivalent to Linux.

If you like Windows XP, then use it while you can. It is still fully supported by Microsoft until 2014, so you still have plenty of time to use and enjoy it. If the Windows platform is your preferred or only way of getting your computing done, then seriously consider buying Windows 7.

I can not advise strongly enough:
[LIST=1]
[*]Do not use ReactOS for anything other than a hobby, it is NOT even remotely close to being a useful OS yet.
[*]Do not switch to Linux expecting it to be anything at all like Windows. 
[*]Do download a Virtual Machine for Windows, or get a dumpster or other second hand computer cheap, try Linux there, find out what you can and can not do, find out whether it can meet your needs in a safe environment. Which leads us to the next point.
[*]Do NOT just jump off the deep end and blitz your Windows Partition and go full on Linux. If your giving Linux a serious consideration, then give Linux a fair evaluation, including allowing time to learn and find out if it is the right solution for you. Jumping off the deep end will not give you an objective evaluation, and will almost certainly end in frustration.
[*]If you do end up deciding that Linux will meet your needs, don't throw away your Windows License; Windows is an excellent tool, and while much of what can be done in windows can be done in linux as well, there are times when there just is no reasonable substitute for windows. In other words, don't reach for pliars when what you really need is a screwdriver.
[*]Finally, Operating Systems are tools, they are not or at least should not be cults or other religious types of endeavors. So keep perspective, and keep your options open, the goal is to get work done and enjoy the computer right?
[/LIST]

Gallahhad
