### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-06-12
> **sadaruwan12 said:**
> I found two good looking DVD authoring softwares but I don't which is best, Please help me to decide.

1.DeVeDe
2.DVDStyler

3. ManDVD

But it depends what you want. If you want menus and such, then ManDVD is the best on Linux AFAIK. DeVeDe is good for if you just want video files onto a DVD with as little fuss as possible.

In fact, even Brasero can create video DVDs, but I don't think it can do menus, and you might get more consistent input-format support from DeVeDe.
