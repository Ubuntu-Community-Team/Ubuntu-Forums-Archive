I want to say I'm sorry from the bottom of my heart...

For the longest time, I have snubbed you and said cruel things like, "KDE's just a transitional environment for ex-Windows users..." and "KDE's clunky and slow..."

I have not stayed in touch for a long time...and in such a long time, things change.  Systems change...and desktop environments change.

You have definitely changed.  Gone are the clunky issues with random lockups, bleeding edge software that looked great but ran terribly unless you had exactly the right piece of hardware for it...the random crashes, screaming fits and sleepless nights fighting you appear to be over too...you've...you've matured and that's what I've been waiting for.

Gone are the days of the Windows-ish appearance.  You have grown into a fine looking desktop in your own right.

In short, I...I'm sorry for ignoring you for all these years...you see...Gnome's been taking up all my time, demanding I pay attention to it and well...

I'm tired of Gnome <snip>.  Gnome has...changed...and not for the better, like you.  I guess what I'm trying to say is...I want you back into my life.  

Remember those heady days when we played together on SuSE?  Well, I'm an Ubuntu man through and through, but if you'll have me...I have a Kubuntu CD and you'll be going on my home computer tonight.
