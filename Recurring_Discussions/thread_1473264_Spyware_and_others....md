### Post by Rubi1200 on 2010-05-05
Also very important: although they are confusing at first, learn to read the logfiles in /var/log/[filename]

These commands are also useful in general:

last
lastb
w
tail /var/log/[filename]
sudo netstat -tap (only cups should be running as a listening service unless you use ssh or vnc)
faillog -a

Hope this helps.
