### Post by TBABill on 2010-03-29
I tend to use whatever I need to get the job done. Normally it's with free software, but not always open source, especially on my Windows machines. I also have a multi-license copy of MS Office 2007 for those machines. On my Linux installs I use proprietary drivers when necessary, plus Adobe Flash, Java and other software needed to make my system do what I want it to do.

I'm not big on being pure open. If the stuff is there and does the job, I use it. I don't pay for much other than MS Office, and only because I needed it for my wife for work. PC's usually come with Windows so I have that as well, obviously paid for in the price. I do tend to stick to free antivirus and firewall software.
