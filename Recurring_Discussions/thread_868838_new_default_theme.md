### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2008-07-24
[This theme]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/Intrepid/Kin_Intrepid") for Intrepid is getting a lot of attention right now. I for one am not huge on the GTK part, since I think the darkest it should get is around the tone of 96 white paper. The window theme, however, (powered by Emerald right now) is quite unique and attractive looking. And no, it does not look like Windows Vista. The borders are very minimal, as I like them, yet it has easy to reach resize handles.

There's a Metacity theme in the works, too. Hopefully Metacity's theme system supports some kind of compositing now -- I haven't looked.
