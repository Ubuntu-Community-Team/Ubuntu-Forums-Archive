### Post by beew on 2011-05-04
I don't really care. I have two installations of Maverick, one has left buttons the other has right (I use diffferent emerald themes) My Lucid installation has buttons on the left.

Remember how we told people they were complaining about nothing because they could easily move the buttons either way? That is no longer possible in Unity, yet another customization option bites the dust. Even though I don't care either way some people may and removing options cannot be good.

(The reason why you can no longer move the buttons to the right is that maximized windows now take up the top panel and the buttons are fixed on the left.  I am sure the location of the  buttons are still changeable for unmaximized windows but you wouldn't want to move them to the right unless you want the buttons switching between left and right depending on whether the windows are maximized. So in effect the option of keeping buttons on the right no longer exists)
