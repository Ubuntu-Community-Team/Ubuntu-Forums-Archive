### Post by TNT1 on 2010-09-14
> **Vigh said:**
> What are the key differences between KDE and Gnome display managers?

  One is built on QT, and the other on GTK.  Both are from the mid to late 90's, KDE was traditionally built on non-free sources, whilst Gnome tried to remain true to the free only sources.  I mean free as in open, not free as in not paid for.
