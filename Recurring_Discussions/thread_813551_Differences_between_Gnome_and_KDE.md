### Post by cardinals_fan on 2008-06-01
I find KDE much more powerful, but not on Ubuntu.  Trying to use KDE and Qt apps on (K)Ubuntu was an irritating endeavor in slowness.  IF you want to try out KDE, grab a SLAX live CD from [http://www.slax.org/](http://www.slax.org/).
