### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2011-02-11
As long as you continue to download and install the updates to !0.04 Lucid Lynx, you are good for three years (well 2 years and a few months.)  That is the advantage to a LTS version.  If you are not concerned about the cutting edge goodies and are satisfied to have a very good OS that you can get used to, a LTS version is the way to go.  Just because there are new versions released every six months does not mean that you have to install them.  I use Lucid on my netbook and my laptop and will continue to use it for a while.
