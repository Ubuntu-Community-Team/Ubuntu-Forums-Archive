### Post by RotorRick on 2011-09-19
The movie: Revolution.OS.2001

I'm a relative newcomer to Linux and this whole corner of the computing universe, so much of this documentary was fresh and new to me. I learned about how this whole free and freedom based computing began, what GNU stood for and why, how that came about. I learned about the differences between the standard software dev model compared to open source, the "cathedral vs bazaar" idea.

If you are new to Linux world, I highly recommend it, and if you aren't new, you still may appreciate the history as presented.
