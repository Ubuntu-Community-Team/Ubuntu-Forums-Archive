### Post by CJ Master on 2009-06-01
Protip: Use HAL, not Xorg.conf. Seriously, configuring Xorg completly froze my computer.

...And I wouldn't trust the first mirror under "United States," it sent me a corrupted driver which pacman wouldn't remove, had to reinstall Arch. Unixheads is extremally fast.

And try KDEmod before using KDE. Even if you were planning on not using KDE, I'd give it a shot. MUCH better then Kubuntu. (Imo.)
