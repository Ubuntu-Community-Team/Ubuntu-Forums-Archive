### Post by 3Miro on 2009-04-21
Depends on the apps. For regular use (watch videos + browse Internet + simple documents) I find 64-bit to be a bit smoother. For video editing/scientific software and such 64-bit is MUCH faster.

Unless you have very little RAM or problems with a driver or already have a working 32-bit system and don't feel like changing, go with 64-bit.
