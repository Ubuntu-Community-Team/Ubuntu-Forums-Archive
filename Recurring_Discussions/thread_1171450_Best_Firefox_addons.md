### Post by hyperdude111 on 2009-05-27
I love firefox, its cross platform, open and highly customizable. 
Firefox addons have to be the feature I use the most and there are loads Which is both good and bad.

The "Recommended Addons" Is good but it only has those which have been around for a long time and only a few are of use to me, and there are far too many addons to search through countless pages until you find a good one.

So the point of this thread is to share your favorite addons, so an up to date and more complete list of addons can be found.

_**My List **_
1. [WOT]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3456")
WOT, Web of Trust, warns you about risky websites that try to scam visitors, deliver malware or send spam. Protect your computer against online threats by using WOT as your front-line layer of protection when browsing or searching in unfamiliar territory. WOT's color-coded icons show you ratings for 21 million websites - green to go, yellow for caution and red to stop – helping you avoid the dangerous sites. Surf safer and add WOT to your Firefox now. 

2. [Desktop]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/11781") 
Display thumbnails on new browser tab with full control on their layout. 

3. [Firefox Notify]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/search?q=firefoxnotify&cat=all")
This addon was made for ubuntu users, it replaces the default firefox download notification with the notify-osd in jaunty.

4. [URL="https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon
/11243"]Skipscreen[/URL]
Skips unnecessary pages on sites like Rapidshare, Megaupload, zShare, Mediafire, and more. Try it out, or watch a demo at skipscreen.com

5. [Limitux]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/search?q=megavideo&cat=all")
We all hate it when on megavideo when suddenly we are told that we have reached our daily quota and must wait another day for more. This addon skips limitations imposed by streaming plateforms. Its purpose is watching without restriction videos from MegaVideo, Veoh, MegaUpload, Rapidshare.

What are your favorite Firefox addons?
