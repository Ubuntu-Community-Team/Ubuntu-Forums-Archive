### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2010-08-22
The thing to remember about open source software is that the software itself is usually treated as a means to an end. The end being producing high quality 3D renders, colliding particles or serving web pages. The caretakers of that software are often the same people who are using it to do _something_ that puts food on the table, sometimes in very indirect ways. The software itself isn't doing that; it is a tool.
