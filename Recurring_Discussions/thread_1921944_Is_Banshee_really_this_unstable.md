### Post by screaminj3sus on 2012-02-11
I like banshee, and have no stability problems with it. Its a little slow to open on a cold start, but besides that they've made lots of performance improvements to it lately. It really doesn't use anymore resources, or feel any slower than rhythmbox on my laptop... I have a library with 13k songs and its perfectly responsive, searches fast, and stays around ~60mb of ram and 0-4% cpu when playing music.

Banshee did have serious performance issues in the past but it seems like they've mostly fixed it. If you still run into major issues make sure to report a bug! Behavior OP describes is definitely not "normal" and I've never seen anything like it with banshee.

The amazon mp3 integration is awesome too :) its kind of a pia to get the amazon mp3 downloader working in ubuntu, so its  especially nice to have it integrated with my media player.

I had preferred clementine lately, but banshee is nice too. I think its a good choice as default player.
