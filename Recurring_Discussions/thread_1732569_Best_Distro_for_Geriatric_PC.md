### Post by snowpine on 2011-04-18
No GUI is necessary for a server, and therefore you have a lot more options (including Ubuntu Server).

Here is a nice blog about Linux on low-spec machines:

[http://kmandla.wordpress.com/](http://kmandla.wordpress.com/)

Personally, I would give Debian a try first. It was recently named Server Distribution of the Year by Linux Questions. Arch and Slackware are also very popular.

If Debian is still too "heavy" for your hardware, then you might want to give the "base" flavor of SliTaz a try.
