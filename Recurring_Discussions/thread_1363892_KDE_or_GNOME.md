### Post by hoppipolla on 2009-12-26
Erm, this is a tricky one. People do tend to give Gnome the praise for best speed and stability, but I think Qt4 or something does tend to make KDE quite snappy, I actually found I loaded up Dolphin on KDE faster than Thunar on XFCE, for example.

Overall, I would say the biggest reasons to choose KDE are not stability or speed necessarily but diversity of features and configurability, eye candy, plasma, Qt4, Phonon sound, and the exciting new changes that 4.4 will bring :)
