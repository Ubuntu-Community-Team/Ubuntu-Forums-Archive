### Post by adelphos on 2010-01-03
Well, on the philosophical side of things, there is often a similar ethic behind open source and the sharing of commercial software/music/movies. For instance, Stallman and the FSF strongly oppose the word "piracy", because they view the desire to redistribute and copy *anything* to be a sort of natural right.

Obviously open source != "piracy". But I think it's natural that many free software advocates are sympathetic to the piracy movement. Depends on how you look at it.
