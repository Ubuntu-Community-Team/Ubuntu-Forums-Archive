### Post by dniMretsaM on 2011-10-14
I would say that your using older hardware doesn't have much (if  anything) to do with you not getting support. I run 9-ish year old  hardware and still get answers. However, I would say that there are  fewer people running Lucid than there are Natty/Oneiric. And people like  me who have never used 10.04 and thus have little knowledge of it and  aren't very qualified to help. Whatever the case may be, you should  definitely read the sticky on how to get you questions answered quickly  found [here](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1422475).  Also, try answering your own questions. Search the forums (it sounds  like you already to this, at least to some extent), search the web via  Google or another search engine, look through the [bugs on Launchpad]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu"), read the [documentation]("https://help.ubuntu.com/10.04/index.html"), check the [IRC channel]("http://www.ubuntu.com/support/community/chat"). there are lots of outlets for support, try them and you'll probably find an answer somewhere.
