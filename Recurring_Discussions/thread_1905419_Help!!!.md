### Post by jjex22 on 2012-01-06
Use corse brown caster sugar instead of regular caster - we call it demerera... I think it's called turbinado in America? anyhoo I always mix it thus for the little 'uns;

equal weights (125g or 4 oz) plain flour, sugar & butter, 2 medium beaten eggs and an optional drop of vanilla extract.

Case 'um up,  190C 12 minutes, yum yum yum!


The only thing with the course sugar is the cake mix isn't as yummy for the kids to eat while the cakes are cooking!
