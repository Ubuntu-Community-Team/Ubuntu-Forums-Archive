### Post by icheyne on 2008-09-13
Any USB "Mass Storage Device" (UMS) MP3 player will do. Almost anything apart from a Zune or an iPod will do that these days.

The challenge is finding an MP3 player that will let you upgrade the **firmware** through Linux.

This store lists MP3 players supported by Linux.
