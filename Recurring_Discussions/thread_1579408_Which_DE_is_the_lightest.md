### Post by cj.surrusco on 2010-09-21
Lubuntu is *very* light compared to Gnome. I haven't used Xfce, but lxde (The DE that Lubuntu uses) is supposed to be even lighter. So if you're looking for something that will be the easiest on resources, check out Lubuntu. I have it installed on an old P3 laptop and it only uses about 125-150 mb of RAM at idle.
