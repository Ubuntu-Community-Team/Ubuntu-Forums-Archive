### Post by snowpine on 2009-11-05
Hi Myolbug, I've been an Ubuntu user since 7.10, and I would say this happens with every single release. :)

About 80% of users (that's a guess!) have no problems whatsoever, but the 20% that do, come here on the forums and ask for help (or complain).

If you are considering upgrading, I **always** recommend trying out the Live CD first to identify any major problems or incompatibilities with your hardware. (For example, 9.10 crashes with my graphics card!!) If you are conservative in nature, you should wait at least a month until the most major bugs have been identified and fixed. I actually like to test the latest on my "play" laptop, but stay one release behind (currently using 9.04) on my "work" computer.

Also it is always worth at least considering a fresh reinstall of the new version, rather than upgrading.
