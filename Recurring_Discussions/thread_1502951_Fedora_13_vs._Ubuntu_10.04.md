### Post by snowpine on 2010-06-06
Fedora and Ubuntu are two of the best Linux distributions, you can't really go wrong with either. :) 

Ubuntu 10.04 is a "long term support" (LTS) release, meaning it will be maintained for 3 years. Fedora does not have LTS releases (unless you count Red Hat of course). That is one of the biggest differences as far as I'm concerned.

You can read more here: [http://distrowatch.com/dwres.php?resource=major](http://distrowatch.com/dwres.php?resource=major)
