### Post by dannyboy79 on 2010-06-17
if you're connected to the internet via a modem and you do NOT have a piece of hardware called router/firewall, then you can install firestarter which is a gui front iptables. then you can open up those ports which need to open and leave all others closed. if you have a router/firewall between your computer and the modem, then you don't need any operating system firewall (iptables). just my 2 cents.
