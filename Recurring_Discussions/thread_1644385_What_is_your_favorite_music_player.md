### Post by cascade9 on 2010-12-13
Foobar2000 is my favourite. Very powerfull, support for every common codec (and support for codecs that nobody with any sense cares about, like .tak), the most configurable interface out of any media player I've ever seen, and handles huge libraries without any issue. Ohh, and it will also rip and transcode, not that I ever used it for ripping (EAC or rubyripper all the way) 

What a pity there is no linux port of FB2K, but its never going to happen. I'm using deadbeef on linux when I cant be bothered to install WINE + Foobar.
