### Post by NightwishFan on 2011-01-06
Well I have tried Transmission, Deluge, Ktorrent, Vuze.. They all are pretty good.

Vuze is more of a whole suite of stuff you do not need, but it works well. Ktorrent and Deluge are similar, and if you like uTorrent, you will be right at home. I have found transmission to be the best overall choice. It is simple, very very light (even to utorrent standards), and utilizes my bandwidth better than the others. If I set my limits to 150/150, it usually is always full used. Deluge at least (and probably the others) tend to queue a specific amount of torrents and thus can under utilize available peers if you have many torrents.

Search for "torrent" or p2p in software center to find a bunch. :)
