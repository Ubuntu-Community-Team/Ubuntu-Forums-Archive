### Post by Werne on 2013-05-10
> **ViliX64 said:**
> I want it mostly fast and I would like options and menus on a top bar (like in OSX).

Well, there's GNOME, not the shell but GNOME fallback (or "Classic" as Ubuntu calls it). Fallback comes with a menu on top just like GNOME 2, looks ugly at first but that can be fixed, and it's pretty fast on my PC. You can get it on *buntu 12.04/12.10/13.04/13.10 with "aptitude install gnome-session-fallback". Or you can get [Ubuntu GNOME Remix 12.10]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuGNOME/ReleaseNotes/12.10") which comes with both fallback and shell.
