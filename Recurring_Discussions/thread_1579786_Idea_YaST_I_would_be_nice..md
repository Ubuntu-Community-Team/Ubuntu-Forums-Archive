### Post by BrokenKingpin on 2010-10-01
I would have to agree. Yeah, there is the Gnome Control Center, but this is really just a collection of small utilities, mostly from Gnome itself, and do not cover even half of what YaST covers from what I can remember.

Currently in Ubuntu/Debian to get GUI utilities for things like Samaba server configuration and mounting of local/network drives you have to get them from the repos, and they do not integrate well with the Gnome-Control-Center. YaST has all this in one central application for handling all system settings. YaST is the one thing SuSE has that I wish Ubuntu has.

YaST4Debian sounds cool, but the project is currently not active.
