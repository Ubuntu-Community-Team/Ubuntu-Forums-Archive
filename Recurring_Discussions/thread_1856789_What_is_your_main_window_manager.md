### Post by 3Miro on 2011-10-09
> **krapp said:**
> Well I don't know the ultimate cause of being unable to manipulate my windows and workspaces with the ease Openbox affords, but something's not working right in Unity.

Are you talking about Classic Compiz or Unity. I have some experience with OpenBox (although less then what you have), but I can only think of one feature that OpenBox has that Compiz doesn't: keep maximized windows from covering several pixels around the edges of the screen. This is as far as WM goes.

Unity comes as a replacement of panels and such and while I myself find it a bit lacking, it is now a flaw in the Windows Manager.

PS: I also don't think Compiz would work better with Gnome 3, given how Gnome 2 and Compiz were build on the same standard, while Gnome 3 has as default shell Gnome-shell, which is different standard. For example, one can go from Gnome 2's Metacity to Compiz without logout or even closing any programs, while going from Gnome-shell to Compiz requires at least logout-login.
