### Post by darkstaar on 2010-12-27
> **rdwray said:**
> I took a look at ubuntu and I was extremely disappointed in that the email program was a junior Thunderbird

Evolution definitely isn't Thunderbird and it certainly isn't a junior. Evolution is actually a very full-featured application that is far more complex than stand-alone Thunderbird, perhaps too complex for those who are accustomed to and only require a simpler e-mail client such as Outlook Express or Thunderbird.

Evolution is actually more like Microsoft Outlook.

Personally, I use Evolution to handle the heavy load of my business e-mail and calendaring requirements. I use the lighter Thunderbird for my personal e-mail accounts. They are both excellent applications but the complexity of Evolution does present a slight and possibly unacceptable learning curve to those users who really need nothing more than something to send and receive e-mail with.
