### Post by Bucky Ball on 2012-11-24
*Thread moved to **Recurring Discussions***

Your question is impossible to answer without knowing what you require your software to do. Please make a list of what you do with your computer and post it here. Do you mostly surf the web, check emails, and that's about it? Mostly game? Are you going to need to use Windows software?

Your specs will run any *buntu. Why not try them all and make a decision? You can run them all from the install disk before installing. There is also plenty of information available online regarding all the *buntus. From lightest to 'heaviest' the main four are Lubuntu, Xubuntu, Ubuntu and Kubuntu. There are other flavours you might like to research also. You can also mix and match with desktop environments and apps from different flavours. You can even start from scratch with a minimal install and totally customise your Ubuntu from the bottom up. Something to look at once you are a little more familiar, perhaps.

Good luck and welcome to the forums.
