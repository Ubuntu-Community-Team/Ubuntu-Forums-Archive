### Post by yetiman64 on 2010-11-24
With 2GB of RAM 32bit would suffice, and you wouldn't have to worry about flash and codec issues (w64codecs in particular seem a bit limited i.m.o.). Currently Adobe have 64 bit flash available but it is "pre-release" software and requires manual installation and upgrading, unlike the 32 bit versions available in the repositories.

Although 64 bit will work on your setup, 64 bit comes into its own performance wise when there is more than 4GB of RAM available to it.

My *_opinion_* is to go 32 bit with those hardware specs only because of flash and codec support on 32 bit.
