### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-08-14
Use the "manual partitioning" option. Set the mount point of your old partition to be "/". If you want a completely fresh install, click the "Format?" checkbox. If you want to preserve your /home (assuming it is in the / partition) then don't click the checkbox.
