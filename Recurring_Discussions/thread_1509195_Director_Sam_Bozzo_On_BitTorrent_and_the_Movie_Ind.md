[Director Sam Bozzo On BitTorrent and the Movie Industry](http://torrentfreak.com/director-sam-bozzo-on-bittorrent-and-the-movie-industry-100613/?utm_source=feedburner&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=Feed%3A+Torrentfreak+%28Torrentfreak%29)

> Last month an early cut of the unreleased movie Hackers Wanted found its way to BitTorrent. As the director’s cut of the movie also leaked out last night, TorrentFreak caught up with director Sam Bozzo who through the prism of 15 years in movie making gives his opinion on how leaks, BitTorrent and file-sharing affects the industry.

Although TorrentFreak is a biased community, this article explains some interesting points about how the movie industry operates, why the studios produces so many bad movies and how BitTorrent is really affecting the industry. The author of the article is an independent movie producer/director/editor who doesn't use BitTorrent, so is not just someone saying bs because he wants to download unauthorized content for free.
