### Post by Meskarune on 2009-11-15
Viruses don't affect linux machines as easly as windows because of the permissions on linux files and becuase linux processes are sepperate from the kernel. It is however curtious and recommended to scan your files for viruses so they are not spread to other systems. 

Avira, AVG, Avast and ClamAV can all be used to scan linux for viruses. Search for Virus in synaptic and they will show up. I would suggest scanning any e-mails you have on yoru computer (if you use a mail client like thunderbird) and also scan torrent files and any other suspicious files. Also temp is a good place to scan. 

To be extra secure make sure you are running a firewall. This keeps people from connecting to your computer when you don't want them to.
