### Post by running_rabbit07 on 2009-10-16
Linux Mint is Ubuntu with dressing. They have added some of the things that Ubuntu doesn't come preinstalled with, such as codecs, flash, fonts, and a few other things to include a minty theme. Basically to bring Ubuntu up to par with Mint, you have to add Ubuntu-restricated-extras in Synaptic Package Manager.
