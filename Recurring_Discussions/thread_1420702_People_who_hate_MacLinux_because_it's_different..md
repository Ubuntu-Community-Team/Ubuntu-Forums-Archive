Reason 1 example) My fiance bought a mac book pro and a new LG TV.  Gave her my VGA cable and HDMI cable both could not be connected to the TV and the laptop. :-( She received a cable to convert mini DVI to VGA but she has a mini data port made exclusively by Apple.  Had to buy an adapter to make it function with VGA to add monitor her TV.  This limits my choice in picking technology I have to pick all Apple products or pay for trying to get a better product.  I dont like this.

Reason 2 example) If I were to take the macbook pro apart and add RAM to it even if the RAM is 100 percent compatible I will void the warranty in the future.  I have to buy apple approved and manufactured RAM which costs quite a bit of money.  Also iTunes is the only software that works with iPods and iTouches.  And yes I know you can sync them to linux equivalents but an iTouch is useless without apps (i have issues with the app store but thats another topic) and if you want to buy music from Apple you have to go onto iTunes.  There is no other way.  :-( Again this upsets me.

Reason 3 example)  After looking at the specs on my fiance's macbook pro and my HP workstation I saw a few differences.  She had a dual core 2.66 Ghz per core processor while i had 2.50 Ghz dual core.  She had 1.5 to 2 more hours of battery life then me while i had about 2.5 hours total, this is no big deal cause both laptops are plugged in 95% of the time anyway so battery is no big deal to me.  She has a 320 GB hard drive while I have 160 GB hard drive.  She has a 17 inch screen and I have a 15.4 inch screen.  We both have 4 GB RAM and similar Graphics cards with same dedicated memory.  I have more 1 USB port, a VGA port, a better wifi card, a card reader, an adapter slot for a SSD or wireless card(think apple has there version of this on the macbook pro but not 100% sure and still that means Apple products only again), and finally the 2 things that apple has not embraced fully yet blu-ray read write drive and HDMI output.(i know apple just started HDMI output on macbook pros about a week ago but is taken forever)  Now I paid about half as much for my laptop as she did for hers.  So she paid nearly double for less ports, slightly more speed and memory, and no Blu-Ray compatibility.  Yes her laptop looks nice a pretty but aluminum dents easily so now the dent in the front from a drop of 2 feet makes the front look broken.  I just think that apple over prices all their products.

Dont get me wrong I like some of Apple's products.  I dont mind using the macbook pro when I have to but some things I dont  like and can not be changed hence why I own a HP with ubuntu.  I like setting things up the way I like them not the way Apple or Windows or anyone thinks I should have them.  Its MY computer now I bought it and I should be able to do what I want with it.  Anyway thats my deal with apple.
