### Post by cariboo on 2010-08-15
There is no really safe OS by default, it all depends on the operator. Stay away from warez sites, p2p and any place were you can download pirated software. Be aware of bank web sites, they should use https. Do not forward any joke emails you get. Stay away from random porn sites. Don't let anyone else use your computer using your account, create a guest/friend account with limited privileges. Make sure the firewall and av software that comes with your computer is turned on and up to date. Being a smart computer operator goes a long way towards protecting your system from malware and the bad guys.
