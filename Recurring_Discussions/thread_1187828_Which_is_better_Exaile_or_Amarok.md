### Post by rookcifer on 2009-06-15
> **Gias Kay said:**
> Which is the better one in your opinion, Exaile or Amarok? Vote and post your reasons please :popcorn:

I've used Amarok for a long time now and found 1.4 very stable and feature rich.  It was without a doubt the best music player for Linux.  Now that they have redesigned it for QT4 and KDE4, it has lost some functionality it used to have (no more equalizer for instance).  And it's buggy.  It's constantly failing to read the tags from my collection, so I end up with screwed up playlists.

So I thought I'd try Exaile.  Even though it is not quite where Amarok 1.4x was, it is close and it reads my tags just fine.  

So, based on the state of Amarok 2.x, I will have to say Exaile is the best of the two.
