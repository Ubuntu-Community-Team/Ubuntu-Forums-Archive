### Post by aysiu on 2008-03-09
I know you didn't *intend* for this to be a debate, but if you start comparing the two on these forums, it will end up that way! So I've moved your thread.

In answer to your more technical question, right-click on Applications/Places/System and select *Remove from Panel*. Then right-click on the now-empty space, select *Add to Panel* and add *Main Menu* (instead of *Menu Bar*, which is what you just removed).
