### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-04-09
If you've ever clicked on a link to an Amazon product contained in a blog post or a review, and then ordered the item from Amazon, there's a very good chance you generated a little payment from Amazon back to the owner of the site with the referring link. It's called the Amazon Associates program.  It's been around for years.

What happens with the Amazon search in Unity is much the same. As explained *ad infinitum*, it is dead simple to turn off.
