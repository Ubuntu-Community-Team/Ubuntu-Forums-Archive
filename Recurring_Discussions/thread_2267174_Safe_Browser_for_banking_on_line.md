### Post by Bucky Ball on 2015-03-02
The link I gave is it. Just make that your homepage. ;)

If you click the Menu at the top right of the duckduckgo homepage>Advanced>Change settings>Save profile to the cloud. That way, you can anonymously load your profile and settings into duckduckgo no matter what computer you're working on. 

You can install Adblock Plus and NoScript from Tools>Addons (from memory) or from [here]("https://noscript.net/") and [here]("https://adblockplus.org/").

I also have Tools>Add-ons>Plugins>Shockwave Flash and Iced-Tea (Java) set to 'Ask'.

### Post by cogset on 2015-03-02
Maybe it's just me, but for a browser to be used for online banking ,I wouldn't store anything on the cloud, I would set Flash and Java to "never activate" (unless they are actually needed) or even uninstall them altogether, and set Firefox to not accept third-party cookies (again, unless they are actually needed) and also to clear everything on exit.  Agreed on NoScript and Adblock, I would also check  [RefControl]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-us/firefox/addon/refcontrol") and  [RequestPolicy]("https://requestpolicycontinued.github.io") .

Although, NoScript will make using the browser a bit harder, and the last two will make it even harder, tweaking everything to make the banking sites to work properly could turn out to be quite a challenge ;)
