### Post by youbuntu on 2009-11-19
Just been talking on macrumors to some Macheads (I am one too... but gradually moving away - sick of non-free systems!) and the sheer level of ignorance toward anything *except* Macs and proprietary solutions, is both overwhelming and laughable!.

Some guy took a look at the FSF website, and simply commented:

"Sorry, but that website is a pile of crap" <----- (an ACTUAL pile of crap, between the quotes ;-))

Why is it that we are all SO dependant on being spoon-fed solutions over which we have no apparent control?. Okay, so we're not *ALL* geeks and programmers, but we all have choices, and the majority of us *know* these corporations are greedy and selfish on the whole (and sometimes evil... sometimes, that is), but how many will admit it?.

Ugh - the more I see of this closed-mindedness, the more I want to move further away from the Mac "community" (?) I once blindly defended, no matter what. I can now see that a Mac is *just* a computer, but the software that sits upon it, is better off being liberated and shared.

Mac users have a reputation of belonging to some nose-thumbing "elite club" - I am now starting to see evidence of this snobbery, when I even MENTION free software & GNU/Linux. For the record, I have been 98% Mac for three years now, but am fast becoming fed up with all the gimmicky gadgets and widgets that they bring out, and bleed you dry, for. Macs have great value, and I won't dispute that, but I really am sick of the whole ridiculous snobbery which is afflicted upon anyone NOT using a Mac - makes me sick.
