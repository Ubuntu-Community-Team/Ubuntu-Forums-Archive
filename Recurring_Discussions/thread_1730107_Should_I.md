### Post by Simian Man on 2011-04-15
> **el_koraco said:**
> Does Kwin have screen edges? Ya know, when you roll your mouse to the corner, and expose the desktop, or go to the "expo" view? Didn't seem to find them, Compiz just isn't agreeing with my machine, and the edges are the only advanced Compiz features I use really.

Yep.  Look at System Settings->Workspace Behavior->Screen Edges.  You can set an effect for any edge or corner you like.

You can configure the "Present Windows" effect (expo) specifically under System Settings->Desktop Effects->All Effects->Present Windows.
