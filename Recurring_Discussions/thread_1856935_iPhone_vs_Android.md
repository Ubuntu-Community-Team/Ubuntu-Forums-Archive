### Post by aysiu on 2011-10-09
Android doesn't really have viruses, but occasionally malware does sneak into the Android Market. If the person mentioned in the OP likes to install apps indiscriminately, then, yes, an unjailbroken iPhone is the better choice, since Apple has to approve an app for it to make it into the App Store, whereas Google lets anything into the Market and then boots out malware that's discovered.

That said, it's not really a good idea to just install apps indiscriminately. Even though Apple curates apps before approving them, Apple isn't perfect. It can't hurt to rely on your own good judgment instead of hoping some corporation is going to protect you from malware.
