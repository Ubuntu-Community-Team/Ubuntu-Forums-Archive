### Post by phrostbyte on 2010-05-01
The USA DMCA makes circumventing copy protection illegal except for some extremely limited circumstances. 

In the DeCSS case, a US court ruled that DeCSS is illegal under the DMCA, regardless for what legal purposes it may be used for. The same judge ordered all copies of DeCSS are to be destroyed, any websites hosting it (or even linking to another website hosting it) to be taken down. The response to this court order on the Internet was kind of comical. Some people went as far as tattooing the DeCSS source code on their bodies and doing some pretty silly things to protest the idea. See: [http://decss.zoy.org/](http://decss.zoy.org/) or "Streisand effect".
