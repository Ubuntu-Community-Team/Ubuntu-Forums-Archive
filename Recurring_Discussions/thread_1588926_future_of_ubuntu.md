### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2010-10-05
The idea was to make software-properties-gtk into something more akin to a preferences dialog rather than a standalone application. It is available from Synaptic, Software Centre and Update Manager.

Hopefully it'll be annihilated in the future and replaced with something baked into each respective application (which should get them feeling a lot smoother and remove another ugly case where we use sudo to elevate permissions).

Discussion is right here:
[https://bugs.edge.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/software-properties/+bug/624072](https://bugs.edge.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/software-properties/+bug/624072)

It was linked to from the [changelog for software-properties]("https://edge.launchpad.net/ubuntu/maverick/+source/software-properties/+changelog").
