### Post by dyslexia on 2010-02-20
> **iRock said:**
> What is the most secure commercial operating system aside from Linux and Windows?

Thanx for your opinions.

Trick question.

All of them are the "most secure" for the people who know them best: i.e.  the OEM.

Insight gleaned from Vista: clearly a lot of effort had gone into making a very obtuse system of file permissions;  the more obtuse it is made the more secure the "obfuscators" (in this case Microsoft) become, but for anyone else the much touted layers of "security" actually have the reverse effect, opening scores of loop-holes only the "insiders" would know about.

So for me, I would say the simplest systems are the most secure unless you have Hundreds of Billions in assets you need to protect, in which case you are best off developing your own system.
