While reading about the HTC sues Apple article on the BBC, I happened across [this article](http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/technology-14682700) about a patent troll called Lodsys that goes after app developers for iOS and Android. One of the many shockingly generic and cryptic patents they own is [this one](http://www.google.com/patents?vid=7222078). No, it made no sense to me either? Unable to believe that the company produces literally *no* products or services, and its business consists entirely of coercing developers into licensing its stupid patents, I looked at their website. That was when my mind really boggled:

[http://www.lodsys.com/index.html](http://www.lodsys.com/index.html)

Yes, that's right, their home page is just a simple banging on about how they own patents and how to contact them to licence them.

Here's hoping again that software patents never make it to the EU.
