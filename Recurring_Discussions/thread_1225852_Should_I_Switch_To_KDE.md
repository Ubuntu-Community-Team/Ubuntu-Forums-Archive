### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2009-07-29
It really just depends on what you prefer as long as your computer will run it. Both of my machines are older hand-me-downs. Gnome runs "just okay" on them, and KDE was much slower (I don't know why, it just was).

Openbox by itself with no desktop environment at all ([Crunchbang]("http://crunchbanglinux.org")) was blazing fast but not user-friendly on the "community laptop" that is shared among several people, most of whom need a familiar-looking desktop.
