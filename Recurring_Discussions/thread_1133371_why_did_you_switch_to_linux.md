### Post by Ichtyandr on 2009-04-22
I would break it down to:

- pragmatic reasons: "software rot", fragmentation, unstoppable malware, frequent system crashes, bloated programs and system. so in a while my windoz was falling apart.

- principled reasons: freedom of speech and thought (which I figure underlie open source), looking for innovations (cant use same system forever), to increase computer literacy (windoz kind of protects you from learning), lack of paternalism (sometimes get a feel windoz assumes users are imbeciles not just noobs)
