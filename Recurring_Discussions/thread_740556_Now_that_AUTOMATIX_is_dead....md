### Post by aysiu on 2008-04-01
I really don't understand the fascination with Automatix, even if you assume that it doesn't cause breakage and even if you assume that the processes it automates are still difficult in Ubuntu.

Seriously.

The way I look at it is this...

If you don't mind going through a few basic steps to get things working (yes, I did this even back in Hoary when it was "difficult"), just use vanilla Ubuntu.

If you think those basic steps aren't basic enough and you just want proprietary stuff working without needing to do anything extra, use a version of Ubuntu that has all that stuff preinstalled (Linux Mint, for example).
