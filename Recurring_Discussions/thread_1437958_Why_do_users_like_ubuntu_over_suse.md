### Post by oldsoundguy on 2010-03-24
since 6.10, every Ubuntu build has installed right out of the box on my desktops.  Only changes was adding the restricted to synaptic (and Ubuntu Tweak now does that) and adding the restricted drivers for my NVida cards.

So ease of basic install and the fact that you are up and running in an hour or so has been the factor as far as MY experiences are concerned.

BUT .. I did my research BEFORE I installed and put in cards (such as using a D-Link 520x for wireless) and dumping all my ATI cards in favor of NVidia (that may have changed by now) .. those cards being the ones that usually went trouble free.

Would rather buy one pre loaded such as a System 76 than go through the hassle of all of the proprietary crap that makes up most laptops.
