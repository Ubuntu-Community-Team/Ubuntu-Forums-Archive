### Post by docus on 2009-10-28
1.  Epiphany.  Fast, stable, light on resources, easily customisable, nice Gnome integration.  My favourite browser.

2.  Opera.  Very fast, stable.  Great built in features - I particularly like the way it handles tabs and downloads.  Nice themes.  Not open source though.

3.  Firefox.  Not so fast as Epiphany or Opera, also not so stable (for me).  Really flexible and lots of plug-ins though.

4.  Chome.  Fast and stable, and shaping up very nicely.  However, at the moment something just doesn't 'feel' right about Chrome to me.  I really don't like the way it handles bookmarks; to me it feels counterintuitive and retrograde.  But perhaps I'll get used to it when I give it another go.
