### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-05-17
Since when has "the right-hand side" been the correct side for the window buttons? The Mac OS, back in 1984, established the convention of having the close button on the left side and the other two buttons on the right side. That's the original convention which is still in use today, and two decades of Microsoft Windows doesn't change the fact.

Now, Ubuntu can't keep the maximise and minimise buttons on the right-hand side, because they would interfere with the windicators that are coming in Ubuntu 10.10. So they're all on the left.

It took me less than a week to get used to the new location of the buttons, especially with the Ambiance theme (because the red of the close button stays in your peripheral vision).
