### Post by disabledaccount on 2011-04-06
There is huge difference between locked car and locked software:
Car: Untill You break the seal manufacturer is responsible for any failures/malfunctions/safety concerns. You can get compensation in case when manufacturer is unable to fix Your car on time. You are free to break the seal - then You are becoming responsible. You can break the seal after warranty period without any consequences.

Closed Src Software: software manufacturer takes no responsibility of his product - it can destroy Your data, allow others to spy your choices, preferences, steal Your projects etc - and You have no guaranted time limit for fixing problems, moreover You have no guarantee that malfunctions/bugs will/could ever be solved. You won't get any recompesation for possible losses.

Open Source: You may share ideas and solutions with others, Others may share with You - both sides can learn, both sides can make better software then. It's easy to detect security holes or bugs and fix them. No seal, no guarantee.
Open Source is of same or better quality as Closed Source, because Open Source developers are notoriously stealing patents:
[http://money.cnn.com/magazines/fortu.../28/100033867/]("http://money.cnn.com/magazines/fortune/fortune_archive/2007/05/28/100033867/")
> The Redmond behemoth asserts that one reason **free software is of such  high quality** is that it violates more than 200 of Microsoft's patents.                      :)
It doesn't mean however, that set of features is the same for CSS and OSS.

Open Source doesn't equal to Free. It's free for developers - if You are not developer/programmer/student then You have to pay for support or for implementation of additional features/functions. And in this aspect it's analogous to physical product - if You don't know mechanics/elactronics then You have to pay serviceman for repairs.

edit: there is no conspiracy, but there are agreements ... :)
