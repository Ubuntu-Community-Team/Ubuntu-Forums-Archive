### Post by tjwoosta on 2008-12-27
lets put it this way


vista home premium requires a bare minimum of 1GB ram
 (with only 1GB it is definatly not fast or responsive and you will be swapping hard)
 (with about 2GB you will get fairly smooth operation, but sometimes it will still swap)
 (with 4GB or more swap will dissapear)

ubuntu requires a minimum of about 256mb
 (with about 512MB you will have perfectly smooth operation, but it will sometimes swap)
 (with 1GB you would be able to completely load up all four workspaces with apps, and then some, without ever even touching the swap)
