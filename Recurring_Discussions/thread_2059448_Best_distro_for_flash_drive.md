### Post by Grenage on 2012-09-18
Just make sure you prioritise speed over capacity, it makes one hell of a difference.  I've had an install on a nippy 16GB drive, and on a slower 64GB drive - the latter over the weekend, for an emergency; the 64GB performance was awful.

The max read/write in the same on both, but the performance is not.  A moot point, if you already have the drive, I suppose - use whatever distro you like.  Disable swap.
