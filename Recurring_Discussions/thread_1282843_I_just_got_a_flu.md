### Post by Giant Speck on 2009-10-05
Make sure you stay clean and drink plenty of fluids.

Also, if most of your symptoms are above the neck, such as runny or stuffy nose, then I'd recommend some light exercise, like a brisk walk in the fresh air.  It can help you feel better and can help unclog your sinuses better than sitting on the couch.  It can also rev up your circulation system, which will give you more energy.

However, if your symptoms include body aches, chills, stomach problems and diarrhea, then don't exercise.  Exercising could make these worse.
