Although I will not delude myself in to believing our numbers are very great, I believe the 1-2% statistic to be a bit unfair to invoke in a conversation that is specifically about gaming.  Much like a person exaggerating the mainstream popularity of Linux, I believe gamers to be exaggerating the general appeal of games, to the average computer user.

I'll take that 1-2% general usage statistic for both Linux and Mac vs Windows 90-whatever% ..but only if we acknowledge the fact that probably only 1-2% of Windows users are actually able to be described as "gamers" .. and how many of those gamers are actively and consistently buying new games?
