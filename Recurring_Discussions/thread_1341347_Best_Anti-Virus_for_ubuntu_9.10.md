### Post by Kevbert on 2009-11-30
Unless you're using something like Wine or DosBox, antivirus software will only slow down your system and it's unlikely that you'll ever get a linux virus. Most 'linux' virus checkers only look for Windows viruses anyway. You could try [[COLOR="Red"]BitDefender[/COLOR]]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BitDefender"). Windows viruses are unlikely to cause any problems when running Linux.
