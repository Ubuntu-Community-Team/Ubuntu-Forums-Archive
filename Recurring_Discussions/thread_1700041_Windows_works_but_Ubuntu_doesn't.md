### Post by jwbrase on 2011-03-06
> **Dragonslayer21 said:**
> I loaded Unbutu Netbook Remix on a USB drive and changed the boot sequence to **USB FDD** on my netbook.

Is "USB FDD" exactly what it said on the boot sequence menu in BIOS? If so, that may be your problem, since FDD generally means "floppy drive". It may be that it's looking for a USB floppy drive and not finding one, instead of looking for your flash drive. Are there any other types of USB devices listed on the boot sequence menu in BIOS? Could you say what options it's giving you?

EDIT: Just had a look at the BIOS on my own laptop. Sure enough, it four different entries for different types of USB devices:

USB FDC: This is for floppy drives connected to your computer with USB. It's equivalent to the USB FDD option on your machine.
USB CDROM: This is for CD drives connected with USB.
USB HDD: This is for hard drives connected with USB.
USB KEY: This is for USB Flash drives. If your BIOS shows this option, it's most likely the one you want.

If you ever actually do come back and read this, look for an entry like "USB KEY" or "USB FLASH" in your BIOS (what exactly says will depend on your BIOS), and put that first. If you can't find such an entry, or it doesn't work, try putting *all* of the USB entries at the top of the list and all the non-USB entries at the bottom.

Good luck!
