> **"Why can't someone create an operating system that is impervious to viruses, spyware and other unwanted communication?"***-- Steve Arnold*
  Impossible. And I'll tell you why.
  The reason there isn&#8217;t an operating system  today that's completely virus-free is because there are too many outside  factors that can cause an infection in a computer.
