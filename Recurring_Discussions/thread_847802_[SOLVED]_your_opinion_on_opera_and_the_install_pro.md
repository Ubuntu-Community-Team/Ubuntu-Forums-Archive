### Post by bluepowder7 on 2008-07-02
i LOVE opera much more than firefox.  ha!  install is very easy - the ubuntu guide covers the 2-3 steps you need to take to accomplish it.  i did it via command line, and it was 3 steps.  if you use synaptic, it might be less steps.

why do i love it?  well, it's easy to trim out the excess tabs and toolbars, i LOVE LOVE LOVE the mouse gestures (and catch myself trying to use them on firefox, only to say "damn!  doesn't work here"), and i really like the "wand" which will automatically fill in your username and password combinations on pages where you have 2 or 3 profiles (like, for instance, on GMail i have a personal and work account, and the wand lets me pick one and it automatically enters the user name, password, and clicks OK for me to log me in).  oh, i also like the speed-dial feature that lets you quickly access your favourite 9 web pages (email, weather, forums, photobucket, comics, etc)
