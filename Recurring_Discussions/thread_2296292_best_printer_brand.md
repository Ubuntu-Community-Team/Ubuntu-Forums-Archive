### Post by kurt18947 on 2015-10-13
Brother installs easily and works well as does Samsung. Those are the machines I have experience with. If you need color lasers have an advantage in that they don't have liquid ink to dry out and clog the nozzles due to infrequent use. The downside is that color toner cartridges are not inexpensive and they may not be good for that many pages. As far as cost/color copy it's hard to beat an inkjet using refillable cartridges but again they can't sit unused for days or weeks without the risk of clogged nozzles. I've also found that not all refill inks are equal, some seem more prone to nozzle clogging due to disuse than others.
