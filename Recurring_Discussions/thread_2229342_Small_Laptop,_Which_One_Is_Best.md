### Post by Bucky Ball on 2014-06-13

The one that suits you best is best. The answer to the question is completely subjective. Write a list of what you want to do with the machine and that will give you an idea of what type of machine you will need regarding RAM, CPU, screen size, etc. Good luck.

For what it's worth, I have a Toshiba Satellite Pro L510. Faultless. My wife's Compaq 610 (model from memory) is less happy, but has been tweaked into submission over the years and releases. It is far from a new machine, though. Dual core something with 2Gb of RAM.
