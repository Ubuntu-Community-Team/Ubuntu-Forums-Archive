It benefits them indirectly, since the desktop is now just a plasmoid it means that developers can easily (at least, easy compared to how it was previously) develop their own panel, menus, task manager, system tray to replace the defaults (there were replacement panels for KDE3.5 but they generally didn't integrate as well as these ones can, since they'll by default use your plasma theme).
