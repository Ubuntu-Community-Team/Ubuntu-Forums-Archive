From what I was able to find about your video card, it is way too old. The chipset was first released in 2003 and while you probably have a more recent update, it is still old technology. Recent versions of Ubuntu have grown more demanding in terms of hardware. I have a brand new System76 Sandy Bridge laptop and I have no issues with Unity.
