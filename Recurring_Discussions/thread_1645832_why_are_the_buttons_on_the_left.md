### Post by mcduck on 2010-12-15
> **Grenage said:**
> It was, apparently, to make room for 'windowcators' in the top right.  Not sure why those couldn't have gone in the top left...

In reality, it's just something to garner attention.
Placing windicators on the right side gives more space for them than what you'd have on the left. On right side they can use the available space from not only the window title bar, but from menu bar as well. Of course that wouldn't be possible if the windicators were on the left, since the menu bar actually has menus on the left side.

You might have also noticed that you can now move the window using all the space on the right side, not just from the title bar.

(Of course we don't actually *have* any windicators yet, but making the move more gradual should make it easier for people. If you get used to having window buttons on left now, you won't end accidentally clicking the windicators when trying to close a window...)
