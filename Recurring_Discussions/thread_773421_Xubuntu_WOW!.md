### Post by elmer_42 on 2008-04-28
Ubuntu uses GNOME. Kubuntu uses KDE. Xubuntu uses Xfce. GNOME is the heaviest of the window managers, followed by KDE, and then Xfce. The reason Xubuntu goes faster than Ubuntu is that Xfce is much less resource-intensive than Ubuntu. That's why I ran it on my old 733MHz Box; it was lightweight and worked.
