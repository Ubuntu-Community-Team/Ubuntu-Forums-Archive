### Post by sertse on 2009-04-17
> **Mulenmar said:**

And how did you get evilwm to work, .

evilwm is *really* minimal, it doesn't come with an menu by default but control+alt+enter will open a terminal for you. I usually use xbindkeys to create my own extra keyboard shortcuts to something like gmrun or dmenu, for more convenient ways to open apps. 

The other instructions can be found in man evilwm (it pays to know the useful keyboard shortcuts!); though for starters, left click on window border is move, middle click is resize, right click is lower. 

btw: I'm was more referring to afterstep fvwm etc.., I personally would put awesome first, but that's just from my own experiences.
