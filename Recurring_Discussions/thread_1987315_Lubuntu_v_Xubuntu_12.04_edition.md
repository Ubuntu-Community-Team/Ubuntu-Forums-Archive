### Post by Peripheral Visionary on 2012-05-26
I have tried them both on my 8-year old hand-me-down Dell Dimension (Celeron processor, 512 RAM) and find **no discernible difference **in speed or performance.  Xubu 12.04 is a 3-year LTS edition, Lubu is not. Xubu has the way cool panel with goodies like the weather applet, Lubu doesn't. I suppose it's more "lightweight" by some geeky measuring standard, but it is *applications* that consume resources more than the desktop environment.

Both distros have a nice choice of lightweight applications that offer a great balance between functionality and the miserly use of limited resources.

If Xubu ever outruns this old hardware, Lubu is where I'll look first for a replacement.

In short, both are awesome! But Xubu - for me at least - offers more functionality and configurability without any measurable difference in speed.
