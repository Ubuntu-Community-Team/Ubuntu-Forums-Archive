### Post by tomasrey88 on 2009-08-02
Antivirus is not useless. I run a dual partitioned hard drive with Linux and Windows. The Windows partition is set-up with no internet access. Since Linux is "impervious" (almost) to viruses, then I download stuff with Linux and scan for it with ClamAV. I then open all appications to make sure that they work and are legit. Master Foo reached for a nearby ball of string, and began wrapping it around the novice's feet.
