For example, my wife constantly has to pinch-to-zoom and then unzoom in Safari on her iPhone because *Safari does a terrible job of rendering readable webpages*. Multi-touch on my Android phone is terrible and completely unsmooth. It is also wholly unnecessary. I've never felt the need to zoom in or unzoom on webpages, because the text is formatted to fit my screen.

These touchscreen-based tablets look attractive and fun. But when I think about having to hold them or to buy a stand for them so I don't have to hold them, I realize that a laptop or netbook is just a more appropriate device for me. If I want to hold something, I'll hold my Android phone, which is a lot smaller.
