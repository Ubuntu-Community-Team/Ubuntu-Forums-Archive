### Post by Pasdar on 2009-05-20
> **Twitch6000 said:**
> I use KdenLive with an slightly old version of virtualdub in wine.

I find the two combined work wonders.

Why virtualdub in wine? The equivalent of virtualdub in Linux is AVIDemux and it does a better job than virtualdub. For example, changing things in a divx file on virtualdub will make it go out of synch many times, you then have to put it in synch again, and there are many formats that don't work in virtualdub. In AVIdemux all formats I've tried just open, and it hasn't gone out of synch yet... there are a whole range of filters in it too.
