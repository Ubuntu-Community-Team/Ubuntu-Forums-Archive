Utterly and totally disagree in every single respect.

Technical design by review and committee processes can work, its been proven time and again. Although even then someone with an overarching understanding of how the final product should feel is invaluable.

Artistic design by committee on the other hand is an awful idea. It would produce utter rubbish, because every single person would think that their design was best, and the final result would be a pathetic hobbled compromise.

Where there is no definitively better solution (i.e. all is subjective) for gods sake keep committees out of it.
