### Post by philinux on 2010-05-18
This section is a collection of popular and thus recurring discussions.  We recognize the importance and general interest in these discussions  and prefer to keep similar conversations in one place. This is analogous to other forums categories such as "General Help", but here the  topics are somewhat fluid in that they may change over time as their  popularity waxes and wanes.

Threads are relocated to this section either by request (when a thread  is reported by the community) and/or at the discretion of the staff.

**Prototypical conversations:-**
Gnome vs KDE (vs XFCE).
Unity vs Classic
Browser wars.
Do I need Antivirus/Firewall/Defragmentation  ?
Ubuntu vs Debian vs Fedora.
Linux vs. Windows (vs BSD).
What is the best lightweight distro ?
VMware vs Virtualbox.
32 bit vs 64 bit.
Opensource vs Proprietary/closed source
Mono
etc etc..
