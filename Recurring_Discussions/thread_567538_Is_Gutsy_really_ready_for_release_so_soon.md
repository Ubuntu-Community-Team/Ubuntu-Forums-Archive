### Post by Mazza558 on 2007-10-04
From what I've seen, there are still a few major bugs in Gusty, though I'm pretty impressed by what HAS happened. The thing that worries me is that I was able to use Feisty with no crashes and no obvious bugs on both of my PCs from about 2 months before it was released, compared with Gusty approximately 2 weeks away and having several monumental bugs on both of my installs:

-OOo crashes when attempting to do... anything

-Restricted drivers are incredibly unpredictable (bcm43xx, I'm looking at you), and often don't function

-The keyring manager conflicting with nm-applet and refusing the correct keyring.

Luckily, these bugs have already been filed on the Launchpad, but will there be enough time to fix them?

It seems with me that the Autumn releases are always inferior in terms of compatibility to the spring releases :(

I know it's still beta software, but even so, Feisty was solid as a rock for me, even before beta stage. I guess it's due to the amount of new features coming in to Gusty compared to more on the bugfix side for Feisty.
