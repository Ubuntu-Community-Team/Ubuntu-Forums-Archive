### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2010-02-02
Moved to recurring discussions.

I suggest you try both, why not xubuntu while you are at it ?

sudo apt-get install kubuntu-desktop xubuntu-desktop

After installing all that stuff, lo out, and select what you want to try (KDE, Gnome, XFCE) from the log in screen.

After a while you will settle into the one you like, and the next time you install you will know what version to go with ;)

There are other options as well

[http://xwinman.org/](http://xwinman.org/)

Most are in the Ubuntu repositories. Fluxbox, Openbox, Enlightenment, IceWM are all fairly popular.
