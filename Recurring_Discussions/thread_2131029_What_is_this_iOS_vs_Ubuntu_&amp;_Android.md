### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-03-31
The first thing that you must understand is that we do not at this time have either a Ubuntu phone or a Ubuntu tablet. But we think that these devices may be on sale by the end of the year.

Second, there is available for everyone what is called cloud storage. We can store data on computer servers belonging to companies. Some companies charge for this service. Others may allow us to store a small amount of data free of charge and then charge us if we need to store large amounts of data. 

This means that if I take a photograph on my camera phone I can upload it to one of these on-line storage services which I can then access with my desktop PC or my tablet PC and the photographs taken by the phone are now on my PC or tablet as if by magic.

There is such a storage service called Ubuntu One. I do not have a camera phone or a tablet but I use the free service of Ubuntu One to store 5GB of data as a way of backing up my data.

[https://one.ubuntu.com/](https://one.ubuntu.com/)

Regards.
