### Post by Larkspur on 2011-05-16
> **Linxguy said:**
> Can you recommend another liunx without a sidebar?

You can stick with Unity and get rid of the sidebar if you want.  Saves messing around installing new stuff:

1) Download cssm.

2) In the Unity plugin, set Reveal to Autohide and the Reveal edge to None or Disabled.

Now the only way you can get the sidebar is by pressing Super.
