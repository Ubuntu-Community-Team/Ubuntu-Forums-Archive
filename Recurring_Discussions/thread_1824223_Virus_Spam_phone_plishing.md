A common misconception is that "they got my phone number".

Why would they bother paying someone for a list of valid phone numbers, when they can simply write a 5-minute script that generates a billion possible phone numbers, and then just get their computers to dial numbers off that list? They don't get charged for dialling a number that doesn't exist.

It's a very, very common scam.
