### Post by jpl888 on 2010-02-20
If you want you can install clamav and there are a couple of front ends "clamtk" and "Klamav". However there aren't enough viruses for Linux to really warrant it. Most people use clamav as a way of scanning shares available to windows clients or for scanning internet/email traffic on a Linux box being used as a gateway.

Don't know of any anti-spyware apps either but again not really needed and hopefully won't ever be.
