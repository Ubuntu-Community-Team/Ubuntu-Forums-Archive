### Post by LuisGMarine on 2009-12-06
I thought you would never ask!  It's quite simple.

First make sure that your iPod is disconnected from your computer.  Next open up your terminal window and type in:

```
killall -9 nautilus
```

Then plug in your iPod, wait about one minute, then reboot nautilus.  You can do so by hitting Alt + F2 and typing in "nautilus"

After that, try opening up banshee again, and hopefully your iPod will be recognised.  I know this works for banshee, can anyone else confirm for songbird and Exile?
