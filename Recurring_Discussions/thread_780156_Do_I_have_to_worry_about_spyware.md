### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-05-03
right, there is no real spyware for linux in general though firefox does attract some malicious stuff every so often.
But any stuff that happens to firefox wont go systemwide, it will only effect your local firefox but not the core firefox...
so if anything happens you can just back up your bookmarks or even cookies (if you want to) and then delete the firefox profile folder.
its easy enough to do, the local firefox folder is hidden but its easy enough to unhide it by pressing ctrl+h
