### Post by DeMus on 2010-08-01
Hi,

It's just over 3 months ago that Lucid Lynx was released. One of the major changes we all could have seen was the move of the Min, Max and Close buttons from the right to the left side of the screen. At least in some themes this happened.
It's a subject much spoken about on the forums, especially how to change the position to the original one.

[CENTER][B][U]Who is still using the buttons on the left side of the screens?
In other words who joined Ubuntu with this step and why do you do it?
If not, why did you change it back to the original position?[/U][/B][/CENTER]

Please don't answer here how to change the position, we all know how to do that, if not look at the general help forum.

I changed the position immediately for a couple of reasons:
I am used having them on the right side and that's the place they should be.
I use Google Chrome which did not change the position so I would end up with one program having the buttons on the left side, another having them on the right side. Not practical, is it?

I think the people who make Ubuntu have enough other things to worry about then how to "improve" the Ubuntu looks. I would think Speed, Safety and Stability are much more important. Why not concentrate on that?
