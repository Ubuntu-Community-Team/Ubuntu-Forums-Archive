### Post by crimesaucer on 2008-10-21
Build Firefox 3 from source, optimize it for your processor, remove junk you don't need.


Part of my mozconfig:

```

ac_add_options --disable-accessibility
ac_add_options --disable-debugger-info-modules
ac_add_options --disable-freetypetest
ac_add_options --disable-glibtest
ac_add_options --disable-ipv6
ac_add_options --disable-leaky
ac_add_options --disable-libIDLtest
ac_add_options --disable-logging
ac_add_options --disable-mailnews
ac_add_options --disable-mathml
ac_add_options --disable-mochitest
ac_add_options --disable-parental-controls
ac_add_options --disable-pedantic
ac_add_options --disable-postscript
ac_add_options --disable-printing
ac_add_options --disable-v1-string-abi
ac_add_options --disable-xterm-updates
ac_add_options --disable-zipwriter
#ac_add_options --disable-xinerama
ac_add_options --disable-startup-notification
ac_add_options --disable-safe-browsing
ac_add_options --enable-single-profile
ac_add_options --disable-profilesharing
ac_add_options --enable-system-sqlite
ac_add_options --with-system-mng
ac_add_options --enable-application=browser
mk_add_options MOZ_CO_PROJECT=browser

```

I love my Firefox 3 the way I have it now.... it feels better than Swiftfox or Swiftweasel ever felt for me.
