### Post by aysiu on 2009-04-22
I don't care as much about *Ubuntu* gaining marketshare so much as a GPL'ed operating system gaining marketshare. Most likely, that would be a Linux distro of some kind. Maybe it'll be Android.

But gains in marketshare will bring more hardware support, more consumer awareness of choice, more preinstalled options, and more ported commercial software (which you do *not* have to use if you're a Free software fanatic, just because it is available).

The only downside to more marketshare is more trojans and social engineering targeted at Linux. And antivirus software companies conning people on more operating systems into using placebos to "protect" their computers instead of getting some effective security.
