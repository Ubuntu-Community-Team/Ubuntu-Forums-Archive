### Post by alzie on 2008-11-03
As long as you don't run as root then viral infection is rare in linux in general.  That being said, if you would be more comfortable with an anti-virus installed there is Clam AV in the repositoies and both Avast (Alwil) and AVG both offer free anti-virus programs as deb files, basically download and install.

As long as you install from trusted sites (like the repositories) there should be little chance of getting a virus.

I hope this helps
