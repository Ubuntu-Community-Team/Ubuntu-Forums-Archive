### Post by joshuajtl on 2009-02-23
Hi, I've used many Linux distributions for over a decade now, and Ubuntu on and off for many years now. Although I am very fond of Ubuntu, I find that out of all the distributions I've used (many, many) Ubuntu has the least desirable artwork. I think that default distribution artwork should be something as generally acceptable as possible. In colors alone the orange and brown combination is not very generally common, and therefore not what the common person likes. I think that (if not already done) a simple way to find a good middle ground for Ubuntu artwork would be asking forum users to vote on a few different styles/ color combinations... and go from there. Although superficial, artwork can be a very important factor for new users especially.
