2. XP / Windows 7. 
3. MS-DOS (almost never hangs). But a bit out-dated yet.
4. 2000 (_NOT_ the Millenium Edition, or as called here "Mega Ellende" which is translated as "Mega Trouble"). A good and very stable OS, more hardware-friendly than NT was.

7. Windows 95/98fe. It was nice, but to me NT was better.
8. Windows 98se/Windows ME. Where "ME" stands for "Mega Ellende", "SE" stands for "Super Ellende" which is translated as "Super Trouble".
9. Mac OS. I once needed 3 hours to get something done that would take me only 2 minutes on MS-DOS. Besides, apples are to eat, not to work with.
