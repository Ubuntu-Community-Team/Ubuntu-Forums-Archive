### Post by screaminj3sus on 2011-05-02
I always see news about the latest kde 4.x version. it looks shiny and nice and I want to like it, but every time I end up very quickly going back to gnome:

1. Performance. on my ati card kwins performance is total ****. Its a little better with the latest kde 4.6 but still really bad, whether I am using the oss driver or the latest catalyst (both of which perform acceptably with compiz)

2. Visual Clutter. KDE's UI is nice in some ways, I love dolphin's ui compared to nautilus for example. But I find some of the applications are atrocious UI's (kopete). Also they overuse transparency and blur to such a degree it makes windows aero look Conservative. All the notifications are totally transparent windows, its just a pia. By default there is WAY too many tray icons. Some UI elements are downright confusing, for example when you click the plasma icon to the right side of the taskbar it brings up this transparent, confusing blurred atrocity of a widget manager.

Gnome 2 may be too simple in places, but kde is worse, just clutter eveywhere. Its UI needs a lot of work to make it more streamlined and easy to use. And Kwin needs to sort out its massive performance issues.
