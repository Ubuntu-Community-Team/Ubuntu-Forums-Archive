### Post by montag dp on 2016-03-02
I don't find any of these reasons particularly compelling.  Why do I care about touch screens or convergence?  If I want to use a phone or tablet, I'll use an operating system suited for it. I see no reason why it has to be, or even should be, the same as my desktop environment.  Searching for files and programs with the Dash?  I can do that in any of the major desktop environments.  Plus, in most of the other more "traditional" DEs, that functionality is also part of a menu, which I sometimes find useful even if I don't normally use it to launch apps.  All the other reasons listed just sound like advertisements, not well-reasoned arguments.

But most importantly, why do I need someone to tell me which DE to use?  No one here is whining. They're just finding something that they like and using it.  And they're as free to voice their dislike of any particular interface as you are to voice your appreciation of it.

Normally I wouldn't bother with such a topic, but I feel like when someone is implying that I'm a child for not using Unity, it should be addressed.  Seriously, use what you like and stop worrying about what other people are using.
