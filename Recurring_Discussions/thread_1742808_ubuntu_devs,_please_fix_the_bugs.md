### Post by _outlawed_ on 2011-04-29
Monitor out of range most likely means the settings of your video card are higher than what your monitor can handle.

Try booting into safe mode and lowering the settings (such as screen resolution and frequency).

This issue isn't constrained to Linux, it affects Windows and Mac systems too.

EDIT: Oh and the devs don't look at the forum, they look at the bug tracker on launchpad -> [https://bugs.launchpad.net/](https://bugs.launchpad.net/)
