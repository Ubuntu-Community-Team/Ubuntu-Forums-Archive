### Post by earthpigg on 2009-11-16
take the world of Free Software.

picture a typical 2d graph with two lines and four quadrants.


```
     |
     |
     |
-----+-----
     |
     |
     |
```

call one axis "orderly intelligent development vs evolutionary/chaotic development". 



everything Linux would evolutionary/chaotic development.

everything *BSD would be orderly intelligent development.

the other axis would be "simple to use vs simple design".

Gentoo/Arch/etc are well known in their own quadrant of evolutionary/chaotic development combined with simple design.

Net/Open/FreeBSD are well known in their quadrant of orderly development and simple design.

Ubuntu, etc, are well known in their quadrant of evolutionary/chaotic development and simple to use.

the last quadrant would be PC-BSD -- orderly development, and simple to use.
