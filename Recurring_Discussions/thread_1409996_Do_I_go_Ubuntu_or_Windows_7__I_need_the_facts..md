Anyway- There are two factors that play into getting games working on your Linux box. One, like was mentioned, the program has to work through wine. Thats no big- you just have to check the compatibility on winehq.org

second, you need to make sure that your video card is supported. If you have an ATI, theres a good chance that it isnt. I have a radeon x1270 and ATI dropped support for it 2 months after it was released, so it only fully works in Ubuntu 8.04.
