### Post by varunendra on 2013-07-15
The decision to shorten the support period for interim releases from 18 months to 9 months was taken the same time when it was decided to extend the support for LTS from 3 years to 5 years. The release cycle of every 6 months will be same as before.

A detailed chart : [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LTS](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LTS)
