### Post by directhex on 2009-08-03
The methodology of learning is probably a more important question - i.e. top-down or bottom-up

The language you pick determines how much there is to learn at the top and how much at the bottom - e.g. C involves an awful lot of "bottom" before you understand remotely enough to move to "top", other languages you can get away with knowing less crap (e.g. as you don't need to handle memory manglement by hand)
