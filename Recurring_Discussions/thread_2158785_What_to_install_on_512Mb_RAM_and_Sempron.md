### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-06-30
Lubuntu is the one, if you want to stay within the *ubuntu family.

Be warned that you may have a problem with flash video as the sempron cpu, or at least some of them including my old sempron 2400+, are not sse2 enabled and therefore will not be able to use the latest version of flash, 11.2 or later, including the pepperflash of google-chrome.

You can use flash but it will have to be the previous version (you can get from [https://github.com/downloads/webgapps/flashaid/flashplayer11_1r102_63_linux.i386.tar.gz](https://github.com/downloads/webgapps/flashaid/flashplayer11_1r102_63_linux.i386.tar.gz)) as mentioned in the thread at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1953796](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1953796).
