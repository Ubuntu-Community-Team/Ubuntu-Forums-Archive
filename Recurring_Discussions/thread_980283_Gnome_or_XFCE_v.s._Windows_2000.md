### Post by aysiu on 2008-11-12
I think with those specs, you'll get similar performance in Gnome and KDE. Xfce might seem slightly faster at first. Ultimately, it'll come down to what kinds of applications you run. If you do a more Sylpheed-Claws, Dillo, xTerm, Mousepad kind of setup, it'll be fast no matter what desktop environment you use, but if you do a more Evolution, Firefox, Gnome Terminal, Gedit kind of setup, it'll be slow for sure (and you won't gain much by using KDE and its similar applications).
