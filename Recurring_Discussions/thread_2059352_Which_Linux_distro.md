### Post by snowpine on 2012-09-17
I recommend 12.04 because it is a "Long Term Support" release, designed for stability through April 2017.

You might also consider a stability-oriented distribution such as Debian Stable, CentOS, or Slackware. Ubuntu is based on Debian Unstable and stability is not the no. 1 goal of the distro.
