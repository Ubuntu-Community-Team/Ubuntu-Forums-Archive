The default Human theme in Intrepid, is the Human Murrine theme from Hardy.

The earth tone color pallet of Ubuntu is simply brand identification in the marketplace. When I think of blue, I think of Windows. Gray, I think of a Mac. Earth tones, I think of Ubuntu. The brand has been set, and I doubt that it will change.

As said many times on the forums, if you don't happen to like the default theme, there are many sources to download and install new themes, icon sets, etc. Ubuntu makes it easy to do that through the Appearances tab.
