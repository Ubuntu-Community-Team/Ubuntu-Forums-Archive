### Post by orangesoda22 on 2010-09-29
just thought it would be cool for firefox users to post up which add-ons they use and maybe say which ones they really liked or didnt


i run quite an assortment of add-ons

Ad Block Plus (of course)
ColorZilla (an eyedropper tool like you would find in GIMP but it lets you pick colors out of the browser window. quite useful.)
Ctrl+Tab (dont use it too often but its neat.)
CyberSearch (a handy enhancement to have.)
Download Statusbar (one of my favorites because i love being able to see my downloads' statuses without switching to another window.)
DownThemAll! (a pretty useful mass downloader. especially the dTAoneclick option)
FastestFox (one of my favorite add-ons ever. it does tons of cool stuff.)
Fire Gestures (awesome and efficient)
Flash Got (a mass downloader for flash content)
Flash Video Resource Downloader (download youtube videos etc. etc.)
Lazarus Form Recovery (an awesome add-on that will restore lost forms. ultra time and aggravation saving.)
Open in Browser (lets you open documents in the browser.)
Optimize Google (just what it sounds like)
Panic Button (dont use it that often but its neat to have.)
Personas (change themes quickly and without navigating to a page specifically for that.)
Quick Drag (lets you drag URLs into the address bar to open them
Quick Proxy (proxy. duh.)
Screen Grab (kind of a more specific version of a screen capture. copy or save certain portions of the browser window.)
Search Preview (shows a thumbnail of each website in your search results on google. handy.)
Speed Dial (self explanatory)
StumbleUpon (probably one of my favorite and most used add-ons of all time. it will suck away your life!)
Tab Mix Plus (adds some improvement to tabbed browsing
Tab Scope (shows the contents of a tab when you hover over it)
TinEye Reverse Image Search (allows you to do a tineye seacrh of an image by simply right-clicking
Tiny Menu (makes the whole menu bar fit into one button)
Ubuntu Firefox Modifications (thats a given)

also changed up about:config a bit to make firefox a bit faster.

well there ya go. what are you using?
