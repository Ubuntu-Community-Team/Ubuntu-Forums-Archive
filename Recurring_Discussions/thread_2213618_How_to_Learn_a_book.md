### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-03-27
Repeat key facts orally 6 times.  That will help you remember. The problem with linux books is that there are so many variations of linux and its implementation that the information is often out of date.  A better question to answer first is what is your prefered learning style?  Do you prefer to listen to audio, read books, watch videos, or hands-on exercises?  With linux, there are so many resources for all of those methods, just pick one and run with it.
