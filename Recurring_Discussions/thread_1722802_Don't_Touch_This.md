### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-04-06
Yes and no. Unity is Gnome with a plugin. The Classic Desktop option won't be available, but it will almost certainly be possible to re-enable it.

Ubuntu isn't ditching Gnome. Gnome is, itself, ditching the Classic Desktop, and Ubuntu is using the Unity interface in place of the even less familiar-looking Gnome Shell.
