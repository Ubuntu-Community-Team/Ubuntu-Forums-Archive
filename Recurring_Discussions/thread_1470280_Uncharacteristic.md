### Post by D-Dan on 2010-05-02
Am I the only one who thinks that, despite the improvements, 10.04 is uncharacteristically buggy. Usually, most bugs have been squashed by the time final is released, but I keep stumbling across them (grub2 DEFAULT=saved, emerald crashes, TV card failing to lock channels etc) that simply weren't there in previous releases.

Now the emerald bug I can understand to some extent. I have two machines with differing architecture (though the same base manufacturer architecture). On the newer machine it crashes having applied the theme, and on the older one it just crashes and does nothing, but this may be down to the vast array of gfx hardware to be supported.

I'm much less forgiving of the grub2 problem (enough so that I reverted to grub legacy).

I'm sure I'll hit more problems, but this is enough "in your face" problems to suggest that the quality is lower than previous releases.
