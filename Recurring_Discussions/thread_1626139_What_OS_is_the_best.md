### Post by slooksterpsv on 2010-11-20
That is highly debatable and dependent on the person.

I personally find Linux, in general, better for me than Windows for a few reasons:
1. Virtualization just works better under Linux in my opinion.
2. No viruses or spyware target Linux (yet) like specifically where windows and os x are constantly targeted.
3. No constant rebooting to apply updates (Windows is horrible at that).
4. Cost to setup a server - Windows Server for the basic standard edition is what $400 or that maybe higher? I can get a Ubuntu server set it up how I want for FREE, just the cost of the hardware.

Those are just my thoughts and opinions why Linux works better for me.

### Post by oldsoundguy on 2010-11-20
Whatever works on your computer and does the job and tasks you want it to do.

Best way to decide is to make a list of what you do and what you want to do on your computer and see if your choices line up.
 
Do you need to dual boot or run a VM in order to run programs that that you can not live without that will not run on your OS choice?
(For that problem .. I just run another computer tower through the same keyboard/video/mouse.)

Also evaluate your time.  How much time do you want to spend setting up and/or maintaining?

Those that say "yadawhaterver" OS is the best .. are coming from THEIR usage and viewpoint.
