### Post by The Cog on 2012-11-03
I've got an athlon 3200+ in my PC. I use 32-bit because I play a couple of games that don't have 64-bit versions, otherwise I'd probably use 64-bit.

You may find Ubuntu a little sluggish - I use Xubuntu which is a rather more traditional looking GUI and is not so heavy on resources.

It might be a good idea to try the different (Ubuntu, Kubuntu, Lubuntu, Xubuntu) live CDs to get a feel for which you feel more comfortable with.
