### Post by Giant Speck on 2009-03-20
You shouldn't change your operating system just because there is a chance your computer could become infected with malware.  If you take the correct measures and precautions to securing your system, whether it be Windows, OS X, or Linux, you should nothing to worry about.

Currently, there isn't a single virus designed for Windows that can do damage to Linux-based operating systems.  In fact, the amount of viruses in the wild that can do any damage at all to Linux is very low.

However, Linux users are not immune to damage to their computer caused by social engineering.  For example, copying code from the internet and running it in a terminal without knowing what it does can seriously damage your computer.

The website you mentioned does not offer any information about Linux security, nor does it even mention Linux at all.
