### Post by snowpine on 2010-07-07
Yes, you can sell an Ubuntu CD for as much as someone is willing to pay. Many people are willing to pay a small amount for Linux CDs because they have a slow connection, don't have a CD burner, prefer a professional look to a CD-R drawn on with a sharpie marker, etc. Also some distribution companies give a percentage back to the developers, which is really cool. :)
