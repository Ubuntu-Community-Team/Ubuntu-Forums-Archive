> **zombifier25 said:**
> Still, anyone who is paranoid about Big Brother would not be using it.

If I am not mistaken Chrome and others that send in surfing data send in "anonymous statistical information" that is used mainly to determine content for that IP. Not the person. For example if that IP address views computer related hardware in southern United States, then you may see many Newegg or similar advertising banners and you will not be seeing advertisements for Flower Shops in China.. They dont track who "you" are. Only what the IP address views. So if the information was used badly, only the IPs in that area are viewing. If its that big of a deal. Just unplug your cable modem often and when it comes back up it should get a random new IP address unless yours is set to Static.
