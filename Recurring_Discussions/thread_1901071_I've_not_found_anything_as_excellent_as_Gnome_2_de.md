### Post by QIII on 2011-12-27
GNOME 2 is fading.  That is not the fault of Ubuntu or any other distribution.  The GNOME developers themselves have chosen another path.  MATE is trying to carry on.  From all I hear it looks good but needs to mature.

The moves to different DEs for many distros are driven by the anticipation that tablet pcs are the way of the future.  Touch screens will require a different metaphor. 

The look and feel of the XP desktop gave way to the Vista desktop.  That gave way to 7.  That will give way to 8 -- which is Microsoft's anticipation of the touch screen future of tablets.
