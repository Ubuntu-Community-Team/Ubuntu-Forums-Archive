### Post by hoppipolla on 2009-11-11
I was just thinking about this while... well while grabbing myself a drink from downstairs actually lol (there are a LOT of pcs in the house...)

I was thinking that OK, my Windows using friend who dislikes Linux, he has a better media center than me, and tends to have better connectivity around the house. His PC is compatible with more hardware and has a slightly cleaner looking interface. He also has fewer small technical problems.

However, I can sit here with a four desktop cube and tons of easy to download free software, I can decorate my desktop with flexible plasmoids and enjoy booting into different desktops for different needs. I can squeeze lots from my older hardware, and I can enjoy a high level of security even without anti-virus.

My mac using friend has wicked software and a clean, polished interface, with good security, cheap upgrades and good, affordable software packages.

I guess what I'm saying is... all our OSs have their pros! And as much as people may claim that one OS or another is poor, as we sit here in the '09, they are on a fairly level playing field :)

That was all I wanted to say really!

I hope you are enjoying your evenings! ^_^

Hoppi
