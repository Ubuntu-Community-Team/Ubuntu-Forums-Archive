### Post by olskar on 2008-08-23
I suggest that EOG is replaced with gThumb.

Reasons?

* gThumb supports all the formats EOG supports and more, including animated gif

* gThumb lets you EDIT your images fast. For a lot of changes there is no need to open GIMP. 

Seriously, what changes can you do with EOG? None? Yes, that's right. It doesn't even let you crop or rotate your images! :confused:

Perhaps gThumb is slightly more memory-demanding, I do not know, but perhaps that is a low price to pay for having a imageviewer that lets you make quick changes.

To me, it is a real mystery that EOG is still default with no editng capabilities  what so ever.


If you have other suggestions than gThumb, I'm happy to hear them :)
