### Post by Redrazor39 on 2008-04-12
Hardy-sparkle is a project for rhythmbox to put a coverflow-style viewer in the music app. I think we should integrate this into the nautilus file browser. Don't make it a complete clone of coverflow, but get the good features. Some sort of quick-look implementation that isn't exactly the same, but still cool and possibly better, would be very nice. Here are some pics of the mockups that would probably look the nicest. Just picture this integrated in nautilus (and put the flowing previews at the bottom with the list on top- just to be different and I think it looks nicer)

[IMG]http://people.ubuntu.com/~mmueller/sparkle-mockup-1.png[/IMG]

[IMG]http://people.ubuntu.com/~mmueller/sparkle-mockup-2.png[/IMG]


By the way, the rounded corners are VERY important. They add a sense of elegance and completeness to it. If you don't like rounded corners, that's ok, because I think there should be an option to have rounded corners or not under View or Edit>Preferences.
