I thought it might be a good idea to make a list of radio stations that could be the standard selection for Rhythmbox in Ubuntu.

The criteria that i could come up with for the radio stations  are:


1, Commercial free. 
(Though a small "brought to you by" or "show your support, pledge today" announcement at the beginning or something should be OK i think)

2, Only Ogg Vorbis streams so that Ubuntu can play them out of the box.

3, Not Focused on a specific region, (local news, or talk etc..), for that this would be better, [http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/idea/10591/](http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/idea/10591/).

4, Not religious, discriminating or offensive.
