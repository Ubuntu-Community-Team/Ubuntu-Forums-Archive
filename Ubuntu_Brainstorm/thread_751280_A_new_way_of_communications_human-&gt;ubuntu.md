I´d like to post here the new ideas of Johnny Chung Lee about a new concepts for comunications with computers, that I havent been seen anywere.

It has a few videos for show theese ideas working in a demo scenary.

[http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~johnny/projects/wii/](http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~johnny/projects/wii/)

Theese projects need a great base software to integrate with desktop. Maybe you (ubuntu) people can think that its kernel, or gnome stuff, but I think that popularity of this OS may do more easily usable to common people this great ideas, that I suposse finally will be imposed.

Seems to me, that with this new projects, ubuntu may take advantage over other OS, that allways are been simply "copied" one year delay.
