### Post by amano on 2008-05-09
And light grey bars remind the standard gnome theme. Is it the default what Ubuntu shall look like?

I took the black bars from a Vista theme,thus only that is the reason that it resembles Vista for you.

If the theme is supposed to look like orange on black, dark panels should be the way to go IMO. And remember that this screen shall give you just a basic idea. The fonts and window buttons on the panel would have to be all orange in some way, making it look even less "Vistaish".

And dark panels would look different to the standard GNOME look and only few themes use it yet. Thus it would give Ubuntu the chance to look "different".

In my opinion much thought should be spent on the panels because those are among those things which you see ALL the time and thus are an important part of the theme. I would prefer orange on black for the panels, but could deal with black on orange as well of course. Just don't use the boring standard grey.
