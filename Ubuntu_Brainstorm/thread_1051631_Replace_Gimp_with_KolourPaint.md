### Post by kingleer on 2009-01-26
I think that gimp should be replaced with KolourPaint. 

Gimp, which currently comes with ubuntu, is a tool for more advance users. 

Most people only want to do simple image editing. A very simple program similar to MSPaint in terms of ease of use and ability is needed. 

Kolourpaint is a better image editor for most people. It can do everything MsPaint can do and is as easy to use. 

[http://kolourpaint.sourceforge.net/](http://kolourpaint.sourceforge.net/)

It's also smaller than gimp, which has to be a plus in terms of saving space for the cd .iso.
