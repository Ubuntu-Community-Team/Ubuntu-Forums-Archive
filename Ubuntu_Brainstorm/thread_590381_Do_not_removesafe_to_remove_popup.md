Instead of referencing the device name in the "Writing data to ... do not remove" dialog (when unmounting a flash drive from within GNOME) instead of referencing the device could the name of the filesystem be referenced instead? Personally, I don't care but a novice user may not know the make or model or name of the flash drive but instead know the name of whatever it automounts as on the desktop.

This is probably the lowest priority possible, but still not a bad thing to put into a LTS release.
