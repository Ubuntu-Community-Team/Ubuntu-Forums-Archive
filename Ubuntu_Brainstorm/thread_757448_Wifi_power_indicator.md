### Post by Kralizec on 2008-04-16
Forgive me and educate me if this exists, but a friend of mine recently complained about a feature he had on Vista that he doesn't have on Ubuntu: a notifier of the power state of his wifi card. He says that on Vista if he switched the card on or off, a pop-up notified him of its state and he was irked that Ubuntu didn't have such a thing. I feel that this would be a good thing to include, as very often users accidentally turn off their wifi cards with a switch or a button and fail to notice, immediately chalking their networking problems up to something more serious.
