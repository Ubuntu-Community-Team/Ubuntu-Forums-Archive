### Post by irrdev on 2008-05-28
This is not something that will ever be included into the default Ubuntu distro. Instead, there are many "mac-like" bars which you can download from the repositories and the internet. The most popular one available for Ubuntu is the [Avant Window Navigator]("http://wiki.awn-project.org/index.php?title=Main_Page"). It has 3d, transparent and glossy effects, and is truly worth checking out. ;)
