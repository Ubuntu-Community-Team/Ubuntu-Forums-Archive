### Post by barbedsaber on 2009-01-14
Hello fellow ubuntu ers

As you may be aware, the Ubuntu Brainstorm website ([http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/](http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/)) has recently had a major update. This should make the website easier, and more fun to use. One of the major goals of this update, is to keep the ideas that are actually package requests and bug reports, separate from the ideas that are genuine ideas.
Please note, there are still people working hard on improving the speed of the website, so please consider this a late beta, or a release candidate.

Feel free to check it out and see how it all works, when you have a few minutes.

That's all for now.
