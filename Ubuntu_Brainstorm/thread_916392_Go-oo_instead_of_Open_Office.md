### Post by richard.e.morton on 2009-04-21
You could say it would be better to put go-oo into oo.org. Sun insists on ownership of the code, some developers are unwilling to do this.

Also oo.org is directed by sun, so developments made by the go-oo may for various differences in design goals or priortisation may not want to include the changes.

Its a similar situation to Ubuntu existing alongside Debian.

### Post by directhex on 2009-04-21
In case nobody knew, Ubuntu ALREADY uses the Go-oo patchset. Has done for years. Sun's build system is fundamentally broken, and they won't accept fixes for it, so the ooo-build replacement is where it starts, and other patches from Go-oo are included from there.
