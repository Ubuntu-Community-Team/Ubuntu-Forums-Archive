### Post by Sam on 2008-01-18
Some suggestions about the main menu.

[list=1][*]**Easy menu editing**


[list][*]Allow drag'n'drop of launchers between the menus
[*]Contextual menu on launchers:[list][*]Open (= left click directly on the icon)
[*]Edit (displays the launcher properties as in alacarte)
[*]Hide (to remove, user would use alacarte)[/list]
[/list]



[*]**Contextual menu in Places**

Available items would be:[list][*]Open (= left click directly on the icon)
[*]Properties
[*]Rename (only for bookmarks)
[*]Remove from menu (only for bookmarks)
[*]Eject (only for removable medias (see [this idea](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=596395)))[/list]



[*]**"Run application" menu entry above/below Add/Remove**

(see [this idea](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=593846))
[/list]
