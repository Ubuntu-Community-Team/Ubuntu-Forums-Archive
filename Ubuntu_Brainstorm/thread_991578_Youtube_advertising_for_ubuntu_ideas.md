### Post by jmite on 2008-11-23
I put this on the brainstorm, here:
[http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/idea/15791/](http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/idea/15791/)
 and someone suggested coming to the community.

So, Novell released "I'm a pc, I'm mac, and I'm linux commercials." They were horribly cheesy, especially compared to the actual mac ads which are hilarious at mocking windows.

So, I think Ubuntu should fill in where they failed. Make a series of short, digital, low-budget videos promoting Ubuntu in a catchy, comedic manner. Not cheesy funny, but hilarious funny, in the style of popular Youtube videos.

It's cheap, it's easy, and if the videos are good, they'll spread like wildfire.

I also think that part of the key here is not to try to tell everything about Ubuntu in a 60second video, more so spread the Ubuntu name, and attach Uubuntu in peoples minds to funny videos.

Anyone have ideas for these videos? I'm sure some of you out there are budding comedians and videographers, and I'd love to see Ubuntu have a much greater presence on the internet...

in the end, the reason why so few people use ubuntu compared to windows and mac is so few have heard of it...
