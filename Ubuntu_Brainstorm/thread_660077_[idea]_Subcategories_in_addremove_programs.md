**Unfinished example:**

Internet (top category)
[INDENT]-Peer to peer (subcategory)
-Web browsers
-FTP
-HTML editors
[/INDENT]Games
[INDENT]-Card games
-Strategy
-FPS
[/INDENT]Sound & Video
[INDENT]-Video players
-Music players/organizers (Amarok, Rhythmbox...)
-Codecs
-CD/DVD
[/INDENT]

And even though you can use "search" to find what you are looking for it's not quite as fast, elegant or precise a solution and it doesn't offer the same kind of discoverability for new users.
