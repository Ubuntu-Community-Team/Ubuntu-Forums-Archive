### Post by bikeboy on 2008-03-01
It's already possible in Synaptic (and aptitude think).

System > Admin > Synaptic Package Manager > "New in Repository" on the left hand side, assuming there's something new.

Not saying your idea is bad (I'm indifferent), but letting you know it's possible - although the description isn't quite the same as that found in Add/Remove.
