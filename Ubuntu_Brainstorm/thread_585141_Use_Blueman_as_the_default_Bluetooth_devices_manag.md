### Post by PmDematagoda on 2007-10-21
The current bluetooth applications in Ubuntu are good, but are not centralised which requires users to move around looking for one specific function, they also do not satisfy a good amount of the requirements of people for whom Bluetooth is their lifeline.

Blueman would be a good native bluetooth manager for Ubuntu as it has many good features such as support for BT headphones, connecting to the internet using a phone and even browsing devices(Though this is one feature I couldn't try out as there is no file manager for OBEX locations).
