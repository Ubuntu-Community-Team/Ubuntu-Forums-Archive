### Post by smartboyathome on 2008-05-13
I have used it before, and it is seriously worse than what we haev now. I don't like the fact of how long it takes to get a program open with it. I have to click, click on All Applications if I am not opening a program in my favorites, and then click on the category the program is in. In contrast, with the current menu, I can just click on Applications, hover on teh category I want, and then click the application I want. And to replace favorites, I place my favorite apps on my panel right beside the menus. Also, the documents and settings is also slower than using the places system. With the places menu, I can go to Places, Bookmarks, and click my bookmarked folder, with that I could only get a few folders displayed and then had to scroll, which didn't help much. Finally, the size. It takes a long time to load, especially at first, and when it takes up a large portion of the screen (when clicked on). The applications, places, and system menus only take up a small portion of the screen when clicked on, especially when you customize it to remove the items you don't need.
