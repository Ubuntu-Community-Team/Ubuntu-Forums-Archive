### Post by Sand Lee on 2007-11-21
"Inspired" by [Bug #164126]("http://https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+bug/164126") I decided to make a mockup of a futuristic and helpful visual effects tab.

[IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=50894&stc=1&d=1195633727[/IMG]
Clicking on an option on the left side would play a video in the box. The video would give a tutorial of the features/shortcuts in each section.
[LIST]
[*]None would describe the metacity shortcuts.
[*]Normal would describe the default compiz shortcuts.
[*]Extra would describe the extra shortcuts enabled from normal (like shift-switcher).
[*]Not sure what custom would do yet.
[/LIST]
Clicking preview would play the video in fullscreen mode. In the future (if possible) maybe the video could be made on the spot, using the users current configuration. I'm not sure if I should make a spec for this. I think it's cool, but at the same time, adds little functionality. (This took me forever to make in GIMP!!) What are your opinions?
