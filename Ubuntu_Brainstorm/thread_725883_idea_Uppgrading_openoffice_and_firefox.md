### Post by bikeboy on 2008-03-17
Firefox doesn't even need to be compiled to keep up with the latest. Download the tar.gz from Mozilla's ftp site, extract it to eg. your home directory or /opt and create a shortcut to there.

You miss a few goodies like apt integration and some plugins might cause some initial problems, but overall it's not difficult.

[ftp://ftp.mozilla.org/pub/firefox/releases/](ftp://ftp.mozilla.org/pub/firefox/releases/)
