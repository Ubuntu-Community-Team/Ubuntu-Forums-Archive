### Post by teasum on 2007-12-15
I use gkrellm with the i8k plugin to manage the fan speeds on my Inspiron 8600.  However, I agree that this could be handled better within Ubuntu.  Though it can be dangerous to adjust the settings manually, perhaps a gui interface to control fan speed could be integrated into the power management settings app.  I tried the panel applet for controlling the fans (can't remember its name) but it didn't work for me; gkrellm with the i8k module installed was the only thing that worked. 

More broadly speaking, I agree that better power management should be a priority for future releases.
