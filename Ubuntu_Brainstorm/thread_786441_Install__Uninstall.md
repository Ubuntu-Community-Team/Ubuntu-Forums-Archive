### Post by [h2o] on 2008-05-08
This has been a hot topic before, and the answer is still that it is too hard to do. Probably impossible.

As for seeing installed packages it is possible to do in both "Add/Remove ..." and in Synaptic.

In "Add/Remove.." you just choose "only installed programs" from the dropdown list in the top of the window.
In synaptic you press the "Status" button and then choose "Installed" from the top list.

### Post by vishzilla on 2008-05-08
In synaptic you can filter out programs you'd installed independently not from any repos. All you have to do is to click on Status in the bottom left of Synaptic and select the 'Installed (local or obselete)'
