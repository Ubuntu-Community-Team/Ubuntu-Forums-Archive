### Post by dreamersbrow on 2008-09-24
You can still adjust screensaver settings it just is a lot more inconvenient than it used to be.  Here is a nice tutorial I found explaining how to do it.

[http://www.freesoftwaremagazine.com/columns/customizing_your_screensaver](http://www.freesoftwaremagazine.com/columns/customizing_your_screensaver)

glslideshow is the program (screensaver) that works well with showing photos on the screen.  If you want to know what different settings you can use then open your terminal and type:

```
man glslideshow
```Like I said, more inconvenient but at least it will work.

Hope this helps.
