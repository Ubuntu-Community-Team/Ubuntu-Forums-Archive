### Post by dperfors on 2008-07-23
I think being total anonymous is very very hard to achieve. (if not impossible) The biggest problem is that every byte can be seen by others. The solution is of course encryption, but even that is very difficult. Say I want to send an e-mail to my parents... They don't know how the decrypt an e-mail, so I have to send them that e-mail in plain text... bye bye anomity.

I think you can compare an anonymous system with a secure system... The biggest problem is the user...
