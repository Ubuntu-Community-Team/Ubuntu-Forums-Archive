### Post by sicofante on 2008-08-12
I would like to suggest that II installs a fax printer by default whenever it detects a fax-modem in the system (most laptops have one and many office desktops too). Just like having a PDF printer is very handy, a fax printer is a much needed tool in office environments.

There's simply no friendly procedure for installing a fax printer. (As a matter of fact that's an understatement: installing a fax printer is almost impossible for anyone but the most advanced users.)

I think [GFAX]("http://www.cowlug.org/gfax/") should be installed along and popup proper dialogs asking for the destination fax number and other options when printing to that fax printer. (efax-gtk is simply horrible).
