### Post by jpeddicord on 2008-11-28
So you've posted your idea, got opinions, and refined your concept. What do you do now? Submit it to Brainstorm!

[Ubuntu Brainstorm]("http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/") is a quick and easy way to contribute ideas for future versions of Ubuntu. You can submit an idea you would like to see implemented, and have it voted on by other users. More popular ideas are then brought to the attention of developers for consideration into the current development version of Ubuntu.

[SIZE="4"]Great! How do I send in my idea?[/SIZE]
If you don't have one already, [create a QA account]("http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/user/register"). Brainstorm currently does not use Launchpad accounts.

Then, [search]("http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/advanced_search") to make sure your idea hasn't already been suggested or implemented. If it has, stop here. Add your vote to the idea (click the green up arrow) and/or comment about it.

If you can't find your idea (and you're SURE it is not a bug), you're free to submit a new one:
[http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/submit](http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/submit)

Fill out an appropriate description and a possible solution. Make it good! Take some time to review your idea here and submit what you really think would work. Also, if you discussed your idea here or elsewhere on the forums, be sure to add a link to it under "Ubuntuforums.org thread URL."

Once you submit your idea, you're done! Watch as people vote on your idea and comment. If it is a good idea, it may show up on the front page, or even be considered for implementation.

[SIZE="4"]What else is there?[/SIZE]
If you want to read up on how to use Brainstorm and information about submitting Blueprints (the official way to document an idea) see the Brainstorm wiki page:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Brainstorm](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Brainstorm)

If you have a problem with Brainstorm or don't like the resolution to your idea, feel free to ask a Brainstorm moderator. We're online on **#ubuntu-brainstorm** at irc.freenode.net. You can also contact the [staff]("https://launchpad.net/~brainstorm-moderators") directly on the mailing list: [email]brainstorm-moderators@lists.launchpad.net[/email]. **Please note that the Ubuntu Forums staff does not control Ubuntu Brainstorm**, with the exception of a couple of staff members. The best way to contact the Brainstorm staff is through IRC or the mailing list.

See you there!
