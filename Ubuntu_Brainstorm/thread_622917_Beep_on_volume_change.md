### Post by AusIV4 on 2007-11-25
I've been using Ubuntu pretty much exclusively for about a year now, and I just came to realize something pretty simple that it seems to be missing. There should be some option (configurable, of course), to play a sound when the user changes the volume. A simple bong or siren from the sound menu would work nicely, and it would let the user know roughly how loud anything they play after that will be.

I can't think of many other objects that have volume changes but don't give you some indicator. Windows plays an obnoxious ding, Macs play a little "blip", most phones make some kind of "bleep". Even some stereos make a "tick" sound.

I can also think of plenty of times where this would be obnoxious, so I'd definitely advocate that it should be easily disabled, but in most cases I think it would be a nice feature to have.
