### Post by smartboyathome on 2008-03-16
No, nothing like this exists, and I am glad it doesn't. KDE has gone away from the all-in-one idea of Konqueror to Dolphin. Nautilus would just weigh Firefox down again, and it would be a bad move imo. GNOME's idea is do one thing and do it well, which is what each gnome program strives to do. This would go against it, and thus it doesn't exist, at least not officially.
