### Post by pmn03 on 2007-12-08
Some friends and I put together a website to help us brainstorm new projects to work on, mostly open source. I think this is exactly what you are talking about, a place to suggest, comment, and vote on new ideas.

It is in early development, but check it out:
[http://mindgrapes.org]("http://mindgrapes.org")

I also created a ubuntu specific section:
[http://ubuntu.mindgrapes.org/]("http://ubuntu.mindgrapes.org/")

Feel free to add your ideas here. We just started but the idea is to create an environment where anyone can suggest an idea, and developers will come to get ideas for what to work on.
