Just want to say that removing the accessibility features would be a terrible idea.

The Ubuntu Philosophy is outlined here: [http://www.ubuntu.com/community/ubuntustory/philosophy](http://www.ubuntu.com/community/ubuntustory/philosophy)

You'll notice the third point mentions people with disabilities. Should it be made so that the most able of users are able to use their desktops with no problem and no clutter, or that the least able have to struggle to get the software they need to be productive?
