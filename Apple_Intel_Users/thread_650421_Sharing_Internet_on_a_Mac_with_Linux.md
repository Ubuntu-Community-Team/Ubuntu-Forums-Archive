### Post by elithrar on 2007-12-28
> **Black Mage said:**
> Hey, at home I have a Desktop set up in my room without an wireless card and I have a MacBook Pro running Leopard. Is there a way where I can get my Mac to share internet with the Desktop?

System Preferences > Sharing > Internet > From: AirPort - To Computers Using: Ethernet (tick it). That's it.

> And I do not have a cross-over cable but I do have an ethernet cable and a firewire cable and both computers can  utilize firewire.

The MacBook Pro has an auto-sensing Ethernet port and you won't need a crossover cable.
