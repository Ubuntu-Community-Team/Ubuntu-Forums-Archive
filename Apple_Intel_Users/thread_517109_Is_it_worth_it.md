### Post by russo.mic on 2007-08-08
You know, it's rather inaccurate to say that OS X is a closed source form of Linux.  Actually, it's a closed source GUI on top of an altered version of FreeBSD, which, although it is a Unix like operating system,  as is linux,  isn't the same thing as Linux.  The kernels are quite different.  

Just pointing that out.
