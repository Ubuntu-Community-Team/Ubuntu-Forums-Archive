it sounds like you have something but it just isn't a cube.
If I have interpreted this correctly, you just need to go System>Preferences>Advanced Desktop Effects Settings>General>General Options>Desktop Size (tab) and move the Horizontal Virtual Size to 4 from whatever it was.
That'll give you a cube.
