### Post by cyberdork33 on 2008-01-14
Open the "Energy Saver" preference pane, click on the "Options" tab and make sure that "Wake for Ethernet network administrator access" is checked.

I do not believe that WOL works on a machine that is turned completely off though, it only wakes a machine from a sleep mode... It has to be (standby) powered for the event to trigger a wakeup... Power is a precious commodity on a portable. I may be wrong, but I could not find any other reference (on Mac hardware).
