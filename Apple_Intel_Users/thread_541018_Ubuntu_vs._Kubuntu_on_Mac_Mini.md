Hello,

I was wondering whether other Mac Mini users here are using (Gnome) Ubuntu or (KDE) Kubuntu. 

First I tried Ubuntu, I am using a Intel Core Duo Mac Mini, triple booting into OS X, K/Ubuntu, XP. 

With Gnome  Ubuntu everything was working fine right out of the box, no problems setting up anything I did however run into problem when I tried to run some KDE applications, so I thought why not running Kubuntu instead of mixing Gnome and KDE.

Since I am used to KDE from a few years back, when I was using different distributions, I thought I try Kubuntu, but on the Mac Mini it seems to be so much less polished and fine tuned  ...or something went wrong with my installation. 

All the problems are not that serious, but they add up, whereas there were none in (Gnome) Ubuntu. 

Some examples of problems I had with KDE

Did anybody compare Ubuntu and Kubuntu on the Mac Mini or other Intel Macs? What did you find, and which flavour are you using?

### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-09-02
KDE is a moving target.  You can wait for KDE 4 and check out the new stuff.  Of course there will still be other problems.  I think a lot of people go back to Gnome because of the problems with KDE in Kubuntu.  I know I did.  Sure it's funky to run KDE apps under Gnome in Ubuntu, but somehow they seem more stable, even if bright blue doesn't blend well with brown.

Of course triple-booting a notebook drive inside a hot mac-mini case means that you will be changing distros often.
