This was a huge pain for me, too, for the better part of a whole day. Anyway, what I ended up doing was going into the sound preference panel (via the System menu) and changing there what the volume control affects. This was great because once I set it to "Speakers" (it had been set on "Front" or something), I could use the F4 and F5 keys to adjust the volume! *whew* Good luck!
