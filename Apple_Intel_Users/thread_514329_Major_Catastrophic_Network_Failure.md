### Post by russo.mic on 2007-07-31
The problem here isn't ubuntu or anything it did.  If it won't connect via the Wii or the OS X partition, the problem is obviously with the wireless Router.  go into the Belkin 54g's setup (from the vista computer you have to manually type in it's IP address, consult your manual) and make sure it didn't get reset.  there is an option in that router to turn off wireless, perhaps it got activated?  Just turning it off and on again wouldn't do a thing if the reset button got hit, or if it got reset some other way.  if that doesn't work, call a friend with a laptop, and/or bring your laptop to a starbucks to see if it connects just to prove it's not your computer and/or wii.  if it still doesn't connect, try replacing the router.  

I'd almost bet an eyebrow that the router is the source of what ails you.

Good luck!  and please, post your results.  I'd like to know if I'm right.
