### Post by cyberdork33 on 2007-06-28
915resolution is only for Intel graphics...

you probably need to get fglrx working... vesa will only work at non-widescreen resolutions.

Here are the syncs and modelines for the 20" cinema display:

```
Section "Monitor"
        Identifier      "Apple Cinema Display"
        VendorName      "Apple Inc."
        ModelName       "Apple Cinema Display 20"
        HorizSync       28-90
        VertRefresh     43-72
        DisplaySize     434 270
        Option "DPMS"
        UseModes "Modes0"
End Section

Section "Modes"  
       Identifier "Modes0" 
        ModeLine "1680x1050" 119.00 1680 1728 1760 1840 1050 1053 1059 1080
        Modeline "1280x800" 67.26 1280 1312 1560 1592 800 817 824 841
        Modeline "1024x640" 51.90 1024 1056 1248 1280 640 653 660 673
        Modeline "800x500" 30.98 800 832 944 976 500 510 515 526
EndSection
```
