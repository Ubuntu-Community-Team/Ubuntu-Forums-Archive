### Post by mrsteveman1 on 2008-04-03
The optical should work, the chipset in most of these things is an ALC chip which ubuntu does support, and you should be able to manually select spdif (optical) from the mixer in ubuntu.

I have noticed that sometimes my apple devices turn on optical all the time, like my airport express has its optical out on all the time even if im not using fiber.

### Post by mrsteveman1 on 2008-04-04
You would think that if one Apple box works with a particular system in a certain way, all of them would. The truth is, Macs use a very wide range of hardware depending on the model and revision, the only reason people don't notice is Apple hides the complexity by bundling complete, supported drivers for every piece of hardware with OS X. However, it is still commodity hardware, stuff Linux in general supports because it is used in other machines from other manufacturers. Most audio hardware is well supported at this point.
