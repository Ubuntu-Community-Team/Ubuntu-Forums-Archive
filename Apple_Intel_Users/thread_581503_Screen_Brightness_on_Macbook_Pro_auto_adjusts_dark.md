### Post by Namtabmai on 2007-10-19
Yeah I had a similar problem, but it didn't turn the screen completely dark, just dark enough to be annoying.

Try System->Preferences->Power Management, General Tab

Untick the "Use ambient light to adjust LCD brightness"
