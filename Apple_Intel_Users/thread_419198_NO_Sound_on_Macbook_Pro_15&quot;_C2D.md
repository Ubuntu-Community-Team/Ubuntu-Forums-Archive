I've installed Feisty twice on my MBP 15" C2D and for some reason both times the speaker is muted by default. 

Right click the volume icon next to the clock > Open Volume Control > under the Playback tab, if the little volume icon in the section for the Speaker has a red X next to it, click it and it will turn your speaker on.
