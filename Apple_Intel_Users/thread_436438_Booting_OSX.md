### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-05-08
During power-on, hold the "c" key for 20 to 30 seconds until a selection screen comes up to select your boot device.  It's probably set to the CD Drive and with no disk, it will sit there.  No problem, just switch it to the hard drive.

This is useful for booting off of a firewire (not USB) drive, as many mac owners are aware.  This is also a good way to experiment with Ubuntu on a Mac, get a decent firewire (not USB) drive, partition it and install Ubuntu on it.  That way you leave the internal drive alone.
