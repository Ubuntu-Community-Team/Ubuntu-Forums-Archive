### Post by alephsmith on 2007-10-11
You should use the standard desktop CD. You'll need to use  DIsk Utility in OSX to burn the disc image to a CD.

Once you have the CD, you will need to restart your computer and press the C key to boot from the CD.

You don't mention which computer you use, but you may be better off finding a HOWTO for your particular model and following that.

### Post by cyberdork33 on 2007-10-11
if you are just dual booting (no windows) then just install into the bootcamp partition.
