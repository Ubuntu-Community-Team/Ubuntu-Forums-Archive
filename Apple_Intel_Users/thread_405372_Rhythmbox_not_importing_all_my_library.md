### Post by ronocdh on 2007-04-09
What are the filetypes you're working with here? Given that you use iTunes, you are likely to have at least a handful of AACs. AACs and even MP3s can be handled if you have the right codecs, but tracks purchased from the iTunes Music Store **will not** play in Linux, or pretty much anywhere else for that matter. To circumvent this, you can burn the songs to a CD in iTunes and then rerip them; this will remove the DRM.

That's just a guess as to what's going on, please post with more information.
