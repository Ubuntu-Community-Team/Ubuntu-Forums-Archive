### Post by XpulseezarX on 2007-04-23
Hey, i ran into the same thing today, here's the fix!
Boot into OS X and option click your folder you want to get in from linux. (movies/pictures/whatever).
click "Get info" then expand "ownership and permissions", then expand "details".  under the dropdown menu labeled "others" you can change it to read/write or read, which will let you either view, or view and edit the folders contents from linux!
