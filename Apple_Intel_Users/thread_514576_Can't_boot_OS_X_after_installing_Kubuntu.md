### Post by ronocdh on 2007-08-01
Sounds like perhaps the OS X partition was inadvertently used and thus at least partially overwritten. Maybe you designated it as swap space? Regardless, do boot from the OS X installation disc and see whether you can mount your OS X partition. If it's there and everything appears intact, you should be able to verify and repair, then reboot into it. If it's not there, well... it may be gone.
