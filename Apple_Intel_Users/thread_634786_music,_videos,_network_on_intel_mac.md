### Post by Rog-Mahal on 2007-12-10
Can't help you with the PS3 stuff, but regarding media: there are tons of audio and video players out there for linux and everyone has their favorites. Personally, I use Rhythmbox for audio (comes stock with ubuntu) and VLC for video and DVDs (can be downloaded from the ubuntu repos). The important thing is ultimately not what program you use, but installing the right codecs. Check the community documentation as a start and then go from there if you still have issues. As for picture quality (HD, etc) I can't really help other than say that DVD playback is almost indistinguishable from a commercial DVD playing program, however, there are occasional periods where the picture gets a bit grainy for brief moments, usually if there is a lot of movement on the screen. Hope this gives you a start.
