> **Greywhind said:**
> Not all Intel Macs have the same WiFi card, so the answer may differ - what's yours?
Great answer. Broadcomms have pretty solid support, as they're a very common chipset, but the Atheroses are still works in progress IMO--although, once again, widemos has posted a slick and quick how-to on getting the newest Atheros cards running, even with WEP and WPA.

Run **lspci** and perhaps **sudo update-pciids** to find your exact card if you don't know.
