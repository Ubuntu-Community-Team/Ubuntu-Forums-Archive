### Post by exoren22 on 2008-01-15
Well, the old ATI drivers could not function without xgl installed, which I recognize your distortions as a symptom of. The new drivers are promising because they can run compositing through aiglx, but they are very raw, somewhere between alpha and beta I'd say. I suggest you revert to the old drivers until the new aiglx drivers are refined, and installing xserver-xgl in your most favoritest way (aptitude, ftw!)
