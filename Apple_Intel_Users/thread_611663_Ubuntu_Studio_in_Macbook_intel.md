### Post by cleentrax on 2007-11-13
I've been trying to get [Ubuntu Studio running on my MacBook]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=610375"). Basic install was simple, but as cyberdork33 mentioned, full functionality is not yet possible on the new MacBooks. I'm still working to get sound, wifi, and keyboard/trackpad customization.

If you have the older C2D or the original Core Duo MacBook, then you'll have better luck, as people have worked out solutions, and specific drivers for that gear has worked its way into the kernel.
