### Post by handy on 2008-01-05
I had previously attempted to install Sabayon 3.4f on my current model 24" iMac, but it failed to handle the video card properly.

Today I installed the ***ubuntu-7.10-alternate-amd64.iso***, the process was as smooth as glass, I made a 15Gb / & an 82Gb /Home with a 3 Gb Swap partition for good measure.

When I setup the partitions I set the 15Gb / partition to boot, on the other two this was turned off.

Finished the partitioning & installed, then reboot & choose the Linux Penguin from the rEFIt boot menu & away we go, it was too easy! :-D

I've updated the system on the net & that's about it, I don't think my sound is working, but I think that is covered in the forums here somewhere.

Anyone who is looking to do this should get the [***_rEFIt_***]("http://refit.sourceforge.net/") software & install it.
