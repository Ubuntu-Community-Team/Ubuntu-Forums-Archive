### Post by x64Jimbo on 2007-11-17
Theoretically, any client that follows the rules of its chat protocol should work with any other compliant client. So AIM, iChat, Kopete, and any other AOL Instant Messenger client with webcam support should work with each other. The key word is "should" of course, and I cannot make any guarantees. My best advice is to give it a go. The worst that could happen is that it doesn't work.
