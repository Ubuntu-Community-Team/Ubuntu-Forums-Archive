### Post by ronocdh on 2007-07-30
Holding down the C key is correct. If you wish to always be confronted with a choice, try installing the bootloader [rEFIt]("http://refit.sf.net"). I love it, and find it much prettier than Apple's alt/option one, which is greyscale.
