### Post by oceanelement on 2007-12-17
Yes, there's Fink and MacPorts (and many thanks to the developers), but the ease and volume of packages available through these are nowhere near Ubuntu's Synaptic. From my own experience, this is what I can summarize about Fink and MacPorts.

Fink: Dependable, but limited number of packages available. 

MacPorts: Good number and selection of packages, but the dependencies go astray frequently. 

In addition to both of the packages, the installation time is quite long compared to Synaptic.
