### Post by russell.h on 2007-06-23
In the middle model you get a 256mb GeForce 8600M GT instead of the 128mb. Also note that the LED backlit screen only applies to the 15" models, not the 17".

Between the LED backlighting and some power saving features in Santa Rosa Apple is claiming a fairly large increase in battery life in the 15" model, and a somewhat smaller increase in the 17" (which again has no LED backlighting, yet).
