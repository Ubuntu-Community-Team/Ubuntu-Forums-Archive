### Post by mdmunoz on 2007-03-11
[Handbrake]("http://handbrake.m0k.org/?page_id=8") (also called MediaFork) is free and open source.

Ripping DVDs is somewhat processor-intensive, so doing it in a virtual machine might bog down the system.
