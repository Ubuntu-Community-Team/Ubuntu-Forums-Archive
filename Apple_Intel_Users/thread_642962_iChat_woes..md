### Post by uc50_ic4more on 2007-12-20
kurage -

Put in your installation disk and reboot. While the system is booting, press "C", which will have the Mac boot from the DVD. You'll want to install the system, but make really, really certain that you *uncheck* everything but iChat.

If something is going wrong with iChat, however, it may be better to trash the .pref file. Look in /Users/[you]/Library/Preferences and spotlight in Finder "ichat". Trash anything and everything that has anything to do with iChat, empty the Trash and try starting the program again *WITH THE CAVEAT* that you just trashed all of your account info, and possibly even buddy lists. The IM services I use all store my buddy list in their Orwellian basements somewhere, so as long as I can log in, my buddy list will remain.
