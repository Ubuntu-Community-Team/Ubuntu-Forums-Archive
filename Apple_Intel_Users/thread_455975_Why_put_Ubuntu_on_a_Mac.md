### Post by Greywhind on 2007-05-27
Although I still love the look and feel of Mac, along with many of its features, and use it more frequently than Linux, I installed Ubuntu on my iMac because:

1. I wanted to try Linux and become more familiar with it because I like the idea behind it.
2. I wanted to use some software and games that are only compatible with Linux, not Mac.
3. I needed to compile my game (stressfracture.sourceforge.net) for Linux and Windows as well as for Mac, so installing Linux would let me compile for both of those without having to use Windows.
