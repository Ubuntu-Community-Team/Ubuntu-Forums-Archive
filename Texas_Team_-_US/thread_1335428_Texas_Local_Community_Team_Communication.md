 The state of Texas is very **big** and we include many areas.
You can contact the team [EMAIL="ubuntu-us-tx@lists.ubuntu.com"]here[/EMAIL] by mail. We also have an 
active chat room where you can ask a question or find someone nearby [here ]("http://java.freenode.net//index.php?channel=ubuntu-us-tx").

Many areas have regular meetings and you are welcome to take part either by 
[chat]("http://java.freenode.net//index.php?channel=ubuntu-us-tx") or in person. We welcome your participation.

Our state wiki page can be found [here]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/TexasTeam") where you might find an established 
group to join. You can also find a member nearby by visiting our [Members List. ]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/TexasTeam/Members")

If you team has a wiki please keep it up to date. If you currently have no wiki 
page for you Team please go [here]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HelpOnEditing") it will explain how you create your page/s.
