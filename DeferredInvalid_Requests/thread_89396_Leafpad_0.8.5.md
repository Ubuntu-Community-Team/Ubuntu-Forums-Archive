### Post by jdong on 2005-11-12
For now, we do not want submitted binaries because of accountability, security, and GPL compliancy issues.

Users can help us by making sure their requests meet backports requirements (primarily that it's in Dapper already), making sure others' requests also meet these requirements, and also by verifying that approved backports build by Debian methods (apt-get source, apt-get build-dep, dpkg-buildpackage), and the built package installs and functions properly.
