### Post by thenetduck on 2007-02-06
They usually have a presentation about something related to Ubuntu for instance, the last one was at the SLC Public Library and was about getting a constitution for the Ubuntu-Utah team. The one  before that was about creating .deb files. Most of the information can be found on the IRC #ubuntu-utah , on the google mailing list, or on our website at [www.ubuntu-utah.org](www.ubuntu-utah.org) . 

 So to sum it up, we talk about really geeky computer stuff that is realated to Ubuntu and sometimes other distros. 

 Hope this helps,
