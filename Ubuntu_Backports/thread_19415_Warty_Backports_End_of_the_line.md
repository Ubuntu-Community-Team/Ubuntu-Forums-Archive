### Post by jdong on 2005-03-11
Attention all backports users:

Warty is closed to backports requests as of now. Those requests already made will be evaluated and possibly processed.

Hoary is almost out now, and we still have a mess of packages in Staging. It would seem like the recent backports we've been making are more prone to bugginess -- especially with GAIM and GLib.


Those who are about to complain, remember that stopping development at the Preview Release was one of my original promises, and though I've broken a lot of promises already ("only 10 or so packages, <100MB" [SF.net project application]), I would like to preserve this one.


Thanks to the Ubuntu Community for all your support, and keep your eyes peeled for Hoary Backports!



------
John Dong         Ubuntu Backports Project Leader

Rufius                Ubuntu Backports i386 Developer
