[QUOTE=drfalkor]what about us "x86 people" ? :p[/QUOTE]

Well now x86 people are way ahead of PPC folks. E17 has already been done by a guy Shadoi or something. There's a post about it here [http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/E17_shadoi_breezy]("http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/E17_shadoi_breezy") for x86 folks.

As for PPC people we are far behind x86 development on linux. Only the brave and curious and those who have time to contribute to PPC are able to help out the minority in Linux systems.

I have not yet been able to port or backport for ppc due to some missing libraries which needs to be found or ported to PPC.

I am personally following the developments of E17 and Engage, hopefully I'll get something going for the PPC folks. X86 users have nothing to fear they are quite ahead of everyone in sense. It's time to focus now on PPC it's only fair to help us out even when we're the minority. Apple is going x86 anyway and not a lot of us have money or ability to upgrade to x86 but to use what we have. All in respect I'm here for everyone in this particular need.

I hope this brings some closure and good vibes from both. Cheers.
