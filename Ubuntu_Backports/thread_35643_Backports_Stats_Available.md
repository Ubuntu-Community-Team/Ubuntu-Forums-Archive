Statistics for the Backports master server are now available at [http://backports.ubuntuforums.org/stats]("http://backports.ubuntuforums.org/stats"), with nightly updates.

Here are some numbers to keep in mind. [b]From May 11th to May 19th:

665,000 [/b]downloads by **64,000** users
**250 GB **of backports transferred

**74,000** downloads per day, weighing in at a mean **28 GB** worth of transfers daily.
