### Post by ubuntumaneh on 2005-10-27
It means lots of things. It will depend on the program. Sometime you have changed the default configuration of a program, then by the time of upgrade, you are asked if you want to keep your configuration, or if you want the default. Anyway, usually there will be no harm whatsoever to your system. In doubt, just press <enter>, for the default choice of the installer. One advice is to read and take notes on the question, then ask on some foruns or look at google.
