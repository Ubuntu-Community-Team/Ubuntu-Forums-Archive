### Post by wgrant on 2008-08-23
update-manager retrieves changelogs from [http://changelogs.ubuntu.com/](http://changelogs.ubuntu.com/). That doesn't always update immediately after new packages are published, so you won't always be able to see them there immediately. They will always be available immediately on Launchpad, however.
