I have installed some games on ubuntu via wine, (some of which do not work), so I have uninstalled them, however, the names of the programs are still listed in the applications/wine/programs menu bar, and I don't know how to get rid of them. ### Post by dje on 2008-08-24
right click on the menu bar (Applications  Places  System) on the panel and select 'Edit menus', in the menu editor navigate to the wine menu and either untick or right click and select 'Delete' for the menu entries you wish to remove from the menu
