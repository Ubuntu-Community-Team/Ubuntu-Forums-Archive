### Post by Toffeeapple on 2010-03-14
It depends very much on the game as there are many different ways about which game developers go in making their games.

Generally speaking with commercial games, ones that I run through wine on linux anyway is I to do a full install and then find what is commonly called a nocd crack, google will help you find them, there is one site in particular that has a very large comprehensive collection, I expect I'm not allowed to link to it or mention it by name.. but if you google for enough nocd cracks you will come across it. Sometimes you have to manually copy over a few files to the installation folder from the cd as well or maybe alter a config file.

You 'can' make an image of the disks in question and mount them to virtual drives, but I gave up messing about with that a few years ago as making working copies of protected disks can be difficult if not impossible, sometimes depending of what DVDROM you have as certain models will work and others will not and what software you use and what copy protection is being used.... much easier to just find a nocd crack.
