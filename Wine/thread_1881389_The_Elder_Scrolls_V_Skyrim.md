### Post by cgolson84 on 2011-11-19
I can confirm Skyrim works in Xubuntu 11.10 64 bit.  I am running wine 1.3.32 and installed xact, vcrun2008 and d3dx9_42 via winetricks.

I am running on a Sager np8130 notebook with a core i7-2630QM, Nvidia GTX 560m, 8GB ram.  I can play on a mix of High and Ultra settings at 1920x1080 resolution with absolutely no choppiness.

Edit:  Okay, played a little more and there is definitely a bit of choppiness, but it doesn't make the game unplayable or anything.
