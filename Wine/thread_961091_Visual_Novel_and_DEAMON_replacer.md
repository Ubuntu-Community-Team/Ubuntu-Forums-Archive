### Post by Genji on 2008-10-28
Hi Majeh,

A good place to start when wanting to load a new game on wine is the Wine App Database (which can be found [here]("http://appdb.winehq.org/")).
The database has useful information about compatibility between current game versions and the current wine release, but also information on the level of support available for your game.
Take into account that it is (like most Linux apps) a user based community and that experiences may differ from the ratings shown.
For example, I see that the current version of the Clannad Visual Novel (the voiced version) is rated as "garbage" in wine.
Now, you may not be trying to get the Clannad Visual Novel running, but check the games status on the AppDB to make sure. If it IS the Clannad one, then unless someone else here has it and can help you, I&#314;l have to dig my version out to see what I get.

As for the ISO program:
[Wikipedia is your friend :)]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_ISO_image_software#Linux")

Although I honestly couldn't tell you which one is best. I use PowerISO myself. Works quite well.

Hope this helps you.
