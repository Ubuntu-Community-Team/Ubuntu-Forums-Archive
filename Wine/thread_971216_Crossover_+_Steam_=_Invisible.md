### Post by teqsun on 2008-11-07
Disable compiz-fusion by going to:

System > Preferences > Appearance 

Then tab over to "Visual Effects" and select none.

Apply.

### Post by The_Real_Bacon on 2008-11-07
Open the wine config.

(In crossover it's in the bottle config menu)

Uncheck allow window manager to draw borders.

This got mine to come up. It's still slightly opac, but it shows up. Sometimes it doesn't come up at first. Right click on panel, close window. Go to steam icon in corner and open via store tab. Opening via games tab does not always work. At least in my experience.
