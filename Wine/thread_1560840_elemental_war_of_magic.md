### Post by Treesong on 2010-08-26
Well, I figured out a workaround.

I emptied the Elemental/Fonts directory, then copied arial.ttf to Elemental/Fonts, then removed all files from windows/Fonts. 

Obviously this defaults all fonts to arial, but everything seems to be perfectly readable/playable.
