### Post by donkyhotay on 2012-05-12
This is old news, and to be honest I really think these type of threads belong in recurring discussions but thats just my opinion. Anyways there have been many "official" reports like this for years. If you *really* want to know the story about steam on linux just do a simple search through these forums for ["linux steam porting"](http://ubuntuforums.org/search.php?searchid=86486097&pp=25&page=3) and you'll find stuff all the way back to 2008. I've said it before, gonna say it again, I personally refuse to believe *any* report of steam coming to linux, even from valve themselves, until at least three days after a working download link is available on the steam website.  Be aware even if steam itself gets a linux client that does not mean all those games available on steam will also be ported as well. Personally I couldn't care less if steam came to linux and would much prefer if the companies that distribute games on steam would port their software and make it available on linux natively without steam.
