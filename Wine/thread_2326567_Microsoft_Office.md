### Post by howefield on 2016-06-02
Thread moved to the "*Wine*" forum.

Wine or the commercial Crossover Office will run versions of MS Office.

[https://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?appId=31](https://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?appId=31)

Crossover has a 15 day trial for evaluation, Wine being free and available from the repositories. How well it runs depends on the version of office you want to run.
