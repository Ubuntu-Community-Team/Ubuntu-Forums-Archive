### Post by devildog3543 on 2010-06-04
Ok I've fixed my own problem! and this problem might not be known to many because of its nature. My second computer is set up for someone with a disability and uses the mouse with his left hand, so I set the option under Ubuntu mouse settings to switch the mouse click keys for a left handed person. Soooo....

Out of frustration I offered to let said disabled person use my computer until I manage to figure out why his has the clicky issue. Well I said to him let me set the mouse up for you and as soon as I checked the radio button to use the mouse for left handed person the steer move function of the mouse in WoW locked the camera again!! So I switched it back to normal and bingo everything worked normally!!! Not sure why this does this but it was reproduced on two computers exactly the same.. could be a problem?
