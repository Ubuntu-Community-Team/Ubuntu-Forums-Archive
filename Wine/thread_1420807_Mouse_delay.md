### Post by Exüberance on 2010-03-05
I know this has been a problem in WoW (and I assume it would be the same thing as in Starcraft), and I just recently discovered a rather peculiar solution.

If you have Compiz Fusion, enable cursor effects (the thing that makes rather excessive effects appear around the cursor when you hit whatever key you've bound that feature to), start WoW/Starcraft. Now once the game is loaded, hit the button to enable effects, then turn the effects off again. You're mouse is somehow now magically synced with your normal cursor update rate. Interesting.

I notice that whenever I load a new area in WoW it goes bag to LAG mode, but then I just enable and disable effects again and it goes back to working at normal speed. Awesome. Doesn't really make sense, but awesome nonetheless!
