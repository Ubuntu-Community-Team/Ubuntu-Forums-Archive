### Post by Patriot1776 on 2008-05-03
I just installed The Orange Box and its games (Portal, HL2, HL2: E1, HL2: E2, and TF2), and sound is not working in any of the games.  Also  video's jerky even though I have an nVidia GeForce 7300 vid card with 256 MB of video memory. Any help?

Hardy Heron, using ALSA 1.0.15, Wine 0.9.59.

Pentium D 3.4GHz, 2 GB system RAM.

---

### Post by Patriot1776 on 2008-05-03
Solved.  Found out I have ALSA enabled by default and Wine was set to OSS.  Switched to OSS and now sound is working properly.
