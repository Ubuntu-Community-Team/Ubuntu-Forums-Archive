### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-12-05
BEFORE you take on the onerous task of installing Wine and getting one or more Windows games/apps to work, you could save yourself some grief by going to the WineHQ website in the link and looking up the game/app version you want to use and seeing the rating for it.  Anything not listed has not been tested and you would be on your own to get it working. Anything listed rated less than Gold is going to be a waste of your time because one or more major function's don't work -- otherwise, it would have a Gold or Platinum rating: [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true")
