### Post by beastrace91 on 2009-12-22
Its just the weakness of your graphics card and the overhead of Wine. Games make DirectX calls in different manners, so just because one game with similar quality graphics level runs fine Wine might tank FPS under something else. 

Try playing with a few of the [Direct 3D registry keys](http://wiki.winehq.org/UsefulRegistryKeys) and see if changing any of them help your FPS at all.
