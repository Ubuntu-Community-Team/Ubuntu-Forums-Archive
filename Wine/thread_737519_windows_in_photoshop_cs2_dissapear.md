### Post by Ralphie on 2008-03-28
The tools go behind the main photoshop window when the window manager gets a hold of them (it sometimes does at startup). You can spot this when instead of just the small blue bar on top of the tool pallet, there is also the normal bar for ubuntu.

You can solve this by going to the "Window" tab in photoshop, deselecting the pallet(s) effected, and then reselecing them, in most cases they will come back without the window manager taking them.

If this does not work, resize the main window so that it is in the middle, and your tools are outside on your desktop, this way you will never have the issue.
