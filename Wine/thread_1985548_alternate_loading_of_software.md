### Post by Paqman on 2012-05-23
> **coolwalker said:**
> Is it possible to take the cd's and dump them on a thumb drive and let the entire process work from that?

Yes. You can make an ISO image of a disk, and then mount that image. On Ubuntu you don't need special software to mount an ISO like you do on Windows, you can just click on it.

The problem however might be Wine. I assume that by COD and CODUO you mean the Windows game Call of Duty? Wine can be very hit-and-miss, I tend not to bother for Windows games and just run them under Windows.

### Post by thatguruguy on 2012-05-23
Irrespective of what Paqman wrote, Call of Duty is well-supported by Wine, earning a ["Platinum" rating]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=1346"). Call of Duty 4 also has a [Platinum rating]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=5934").

You may want to direct your questions regarding running programs under Wine to the [Wine sub-forum]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=313") in Gaming & Leisure.  That's where you're likely to get the most help.
