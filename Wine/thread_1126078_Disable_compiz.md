### Post by Mmmbopdowedop on 2009-04-15
I always do;

Alt+F2 "metactity --replace"

Play

Quit

Alt+F2 "compiz --replace"

Do whatever, is simple enough for me. :)

### Post by stumbleUpon on 2009-04-15
Two more ways

(1) Right click on desktop > change desktop background > visual effects > choose None

(2) Look for compiz fusion icon in Applications > System tools menu and add it to the panel. Right click on it to select the  window manager (metacity, or compiz)
