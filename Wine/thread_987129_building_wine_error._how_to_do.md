### Post by Dizzle7677 on 2008-11-20
It's easier to pick up the wine source from the Sourceforge page and extract. The source in the ubu repos I think is v1.00 or v1.01. The default source directory install is to /usr/local/lib , share & bin , the deb package would be /usr/lib , share & bin. There's a difference. You can change the directories with some switches in configure. The instructions are pretty thorough on the wine page.

  I was looking at the config.log a little more closely compared to my own... Check the folder/file permissions for the /home/goksu/wine and wine/lib32  directory. Make sure 'Others' have create and delete and the links are in green when you view them. It looks like a linking error to gcc somewhere.
