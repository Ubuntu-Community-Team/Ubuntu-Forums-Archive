### Post by karth on 2009-05-23
> **mellowd said:**
> Yes you can: [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=2890](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=2890)

It has a platinum rating which means it'll run with no problems at all

This is actually not completely true. By Steam, he implied hl2 which may encounter problems since the jaunty upgrade.

Steam itself will run almost flawlessly except minor annoyances (ie Community panel not being functionnal at all), but several games have become strictly unplayable (crash to desktop) with the upgrade of pulseaudio (which controls sound on the default ubuntu setup).
Fixing and/or working around may require advanced knowledge of Wine's options and setup.

This affects mainly Source engine based games as TF2 and Left4Dead.
