### Post by Mike'sHardLinux on 2007-12-18
I use Cedega and like it, but I don't see Oblivion on their game database, so I wouldn't expect it to work, although it is still possible. If you're planning on dropping money on it, that's something to think about.

I play several games in Cedega and several in Wine and I don't believe one is consistently better than the other. They both have their strengths and weaknesses. A lot of people prefer Wine over Cedega because of the fact that Cedega uses proprietary code that doesn't make its way back into Wine. So that is really more of an ethics issue, I guess.

A few people say that Wine works better, but a quick search on the forums tells me that it can have just as many issues as Cedega, depending on the game(s) you want to run.
