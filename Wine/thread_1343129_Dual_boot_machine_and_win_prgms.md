### Post by beastrace91 on 2009-12-01
The Wine [Applications Database](http://appdb.winehq.org/) contains a list of programs and how well they are known to work under Wine along with tips on getting the most out of said programs.

As for running programs through Wine - in most cases you need to reinstall the program under Ubuntu using Wine (there are some exceptions to this but for the most part it is safest/easiest to just reinstall them under Wine).

Best of luck getting your software running :)
