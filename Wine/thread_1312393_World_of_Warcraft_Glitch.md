### Post by Toffeeapple on 2009-11-03
Have you tried messing with the everything mentioned [here]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=17421")?

I've had stuff like that in games through wine and got rid of it by changing OffscreenRenderingMode to 'something else' in the registry, see the Drect3d section [here]("http://wiki.winehq.org/UsefulRegistryKeys") for options.

If you are not happy messing with the registry in wine I suggest playonlinux as it has a plug-in that will allows you to do this through a GUI.

[Here]("http://www.wowwiki.com/Wine_troubleshooting") there is an issue mentioned that may be your problem if you are using ATI, scrole down it's under ATI-Specific issues.

Good luck, it does work, quite well too : )
