### Post by cogadh on 2008-04-26
Programs can be easily uninstalled from Wine, but they often leave behind old menu entries. You can manually delete them if it does.

One thing you need to remember, programs installed with Wine are not actually installed into the system, they are installed into a "fake Windows" directory in your home folder. In a worst case scenario when a program is being exceptionally difficult to remove, you can just delete the .wine directory (the "fake Windows" directory) from your home folder to remove it.

Being new to Wine, you might want to click on the "Stuff I've learned about Wine" link in my sig, it will give you some basic information to get you started with Wine.
