### Post by nickleboyblue on 2011-02-17
C: should be inside of the .wine folder in your home directory.  It's a hidden folder, so to view it, either open your home directory in nautilus, hit ctrl-h or click on the view menu and find the item called "show hidden files."  Find .wine, open it, and explore a bit. I think the emulated C: drive is actually called dive_c.
