### Post by LordRaiden on 2006-07-05
Wine supports online connections just fine. Just like DiamondX stated, SRO uses gameguard as does a whole lot of popular MMORPGs to stop hacking. Unfortunately, gameguard uses hooks into Windows and even some hardware information to work (Gameguard is actually considered by some security experts to be a major security risk on Windows). Wine developers have stated in the past that they will not attempt to get Gameguard to work on Wine. As annoying as that is, it's Gameguard's fault for being so unsafe and Windows dependent. Some rumours about a linux version keep cropping about now and then, but there is no *clear* sign that Gameguard developers have any concrete plans.

Some people have even got in touch with Gameguard customer service who end up recommending installing Windows to play.
