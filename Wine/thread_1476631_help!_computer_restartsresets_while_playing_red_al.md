### Post by asdfoo on 2010-05-08
> **lohren michael said:**
>  why? how can I avoid that to happen?


Possible reasons:

a) bug in Intel video driver
b) overheating
c) bad ram


Possible solutions:

Upgrade to a newer version of Wine, 1.0.1 is over 1 year old, it's possible the bug will not be triggered in your video drivers.  1.1.44 is the most recent.
