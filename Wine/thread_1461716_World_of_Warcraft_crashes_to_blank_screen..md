### Post by The Thunder Chimp on 2010-04-24
This won't help you solve the problem directly, but it might save you a hard-boot each time your computer freezes. Try pressing Control-Alt-F2, this will open a fullscreen command line interface. From there, sign in and then enter:

```
 kill -9 -1 
```

This will reboot your Ubuntu session without hard-booting your computer.

To leave the command line interface without "soft"-booting your computer, simply press Alt-F7.

Hope this helps!:)
