### Post by wolfyking2 on 2009-01-10
Ok!  I got the patcher to work!  All you have to do is go to the AO homepage > downloads > updates and update it manually if the patcher doesn't work for you.  Or, download the old patcher here [http://sharebee.com/c62cfa8b]("http://sharebee.com/c62cfa8b") and put it in the AO directory (go to the .wine, program file, funcom, anarchy online) then insert the old patcher in there.  HAVE THE AO CLIENT RUNNING WHILE DOING THIS!  then, log in, press the 'patch' button, and then, the patch stuff will come up.  Note, you might have to do this more then one time.

Hope it helped!
