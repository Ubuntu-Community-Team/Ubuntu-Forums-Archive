### Post by mungatsuma on 2012-12-09
If installed under wine, the programmes are available in Dash. Just type Word or Excel in Dash and you will be pointed to MS Office Word and Excel respectively.

IMHO installing it under PlayonLinux is easier and will give you Desktop Icons to launch the programmes from.

Either way when you have it running, you can pin the programme to the launcher for subsequent ease of access.

### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-12-09
You should be aware that running MS Office apps in Wine always results in the the loss of SOME functionality.  

In the case of Excel, the WineHQ website rates it Silver -- meaning that some stuff will work, while other stuff will not.
