### Post by BenAshton24 on 2008-10-16
It seems to have a good rating on appdb [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=3664](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=3664)
> What works

installation: works out of the box, flawless.

start up: flawless.

graphics: flawless.

game play: everything ok except the mouse "escape" bug. read on for the solution.

online gaming: not tested much, but seems to work out of the box.


What does not
nothing..


What was not tested
long time online playing.


Additional Comments
THE MOUSE ESCAPE BUG The problem is cured by a new dinput.dll code. it has not made it into wine release versions, however somebody has provided precompiled dinput.dll files to over come the issue. you can find it at [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=5444](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=5444) in the HOWTO section. 
Hope this helps :D
