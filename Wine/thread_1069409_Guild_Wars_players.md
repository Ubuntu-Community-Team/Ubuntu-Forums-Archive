### Post by handy on 2009-02-22
I like Prophecies best & Nightfall is great also. Like most GW players I do not like Factions at all.

You have to have one of those three to be able to play Eye of the North, which is great also.

Even though I prefer Prophecies I would recommend a new player start with Nightfall, as you can build up a team of great hero's, which can take you through the game if need be.

The hero's can be used in all but a very few instances in the entire game, they are really great value.

So if you can't find anyone who wants to do a quest/mission, you can choose your team to suit the needs, from the hero's you have been accumulating in Nightfall,  there is only a very limited number of hero's available in the other 2 base sections of GW.
