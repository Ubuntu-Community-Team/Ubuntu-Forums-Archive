### Post by opendevlite on 2008-09-12
I finallly fixed it, I think it was the PCM Volume control that made the problem, i set it to maximum, and change the Volume Applet Preferences to Master.

I also use another PC speakers just to test if the speakers where the problem, i think it was a system problem.

Also about Counter-Strike online play it was also fixed.
