### Post by procent on 2008-03-13
Why you use Wine? You can use nQuake and it's working with *.exe 
My cmd line:
```
/media/sda4/nQuake/ezquake-gl.exe -width 800 -height 600 -conwidth 400 -conheight 300 -nodga -nomdga -bpp 32 -mem 64 -democache 32768 +gl_gamma 0.7 -noforcemparms -noforcemspd -noforcemaccel +set s_khz 44 -norjscripts +cfg_load pimp
```
