### Post by Zayfox on 2009-12-26
My fix for this was:
Game Menu
Video Settings
"Windowed Mode"
"Maximized"
Then Apply/Okay and you're good. It's fullscreen but windowed, which doesn't mess your game up when you ALT+TAB.
