I installed Sims 3 in PlayOnLinux for my niece in Linux Mint 11 (the game ran perfectly in full screen). I have since downgraded to Ubuntu 10.04. I use manual partitions so all my settings remain in the home directory. I went to launch Sims 3 in POL and it runs in full screen alright but the GNOME panel is at the bottom.

Can someone help me solve this? At least could someone tell me how to launch it in a window?

### Post by Perfect Storm on 2011-07-17
Make sure you have Compiz Settings Manager installed. Under General Options unselect 'Unredirct Fullscreen Windows'.

Otherwise make sure you run the game at the same resolution as your Desktop.

or


Open Configure Wine ===> Graphics and unselect 'Allow window manager to control the windows'.
