NO.

If you read the WineHQ FAQ, this is covered.  You will potentially corrupt your windows installation.

This is just the same as trying to share two different windows installations, a recipe for disaster.

You need to reinstall your programs in Wine, or (unsupported) copy the files to your Wine C: if they work without being properly installed
