### Post by BigSilly on 2010-06-03
Just to let you know that the new BBC game [Doctor Who - The Adventures: City of the Daleks]("http://www.bbc.co.uk/doctorwho/dw/theadventuregames") seems to run brilliantly on WINE. I'm running the latest WINE and Lucid 64 bit. I had to do a couple of tweaks, such as setting it to emulate a virtual desktop, but it's mostly great and just the same as on my Windows install. There's an issue with the mouse (it seems to stick occasionally), but it's not a game breaker as the WASD or cursor buttons will control wee digital Matt if you wish. I also had a sound issue at one point, but running it from the terminal seemed to end it automatically. :confused:  Still, it's all good, and a nice freebie for us fans who live in the UK. :)
