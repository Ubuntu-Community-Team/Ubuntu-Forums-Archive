### Post by tzedek on 2008-07-06
A lot of people are having problems with battle.net with starcraft.  I think the problem has something to do with the window manager.  on ubuntu 7.10 with wine 1.0 what I have found is: Emulating desktop enviroment allows battle.net to work.  But its windowed, so that was a pain.  I have also found that using gnome safe mode login lets you play battle.net fullscreen.
