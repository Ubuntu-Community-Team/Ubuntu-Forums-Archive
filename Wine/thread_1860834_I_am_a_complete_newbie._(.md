### Post by efikkan on 2011-10-16
Welcome, **Swine Flu**, to the friendly Ubuntu forums!

Ahh, Diablo II, I love that old game, and I've played it far too much. It's known to work very well in GNU/Linux.

First of all, I assume you are trying to run the game in Wine? Which version of Wine do you have? (I you don't know, open a terminal (Ctrl+Alt+T) and enter "wine --version" and press Enter).

There is a utility called [PlayOnLinux]("http://www.playonlinux.com/en/") to help with installation of games, you can find this program in Ubuntu Software Center.

You can also find a lot of useful information regarding the game in Wine's [AppDB]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=74").

Let us know about your progress.

Greetings
