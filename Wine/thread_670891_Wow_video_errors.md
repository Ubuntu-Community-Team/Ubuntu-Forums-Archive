### Post by Dark Hornet on 2008-01-18
When you run WoW, or any game for that matter, I have found that it is best to turn off all Windows Managers--Compiz, AWN, etc.  I know some people have gotten it to work, and so have I, but it was a pain in the butt, and "buggy" at times....hope this helps.

**edit**  I had a ton of problems trying to run everything with my old 9800 pro...I just gave it up and got a nVidia 6800 GT AGP card.  nVidia has MUCH better driver support for Linux...good luck!
