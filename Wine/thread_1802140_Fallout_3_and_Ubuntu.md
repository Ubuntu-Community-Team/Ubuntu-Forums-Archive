### Post by HolgerB on 2011-07-12
> **domoappo said:**
> What's a CD check and why may we not discuss the no-CD patch?
A CD check (or whatever you might call this) is often used to verify that you have the original media in your drive. There are a lot of copy protection schemes. You could check out Wikipedia and the additional links:
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Copy_protection](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Copy_protection)

We may not discuss no-CD patches (at least officially) because in some countries it is illegal to "find ways around copy protections". DCMA for example. 

One might be smart enough to google with the right keywords. 

To my best understanding the results of fallout looked pretty mixed under WINE:
[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=14322](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=14322)
