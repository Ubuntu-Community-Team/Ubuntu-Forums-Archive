### Post by Daminvar on 2009-01-03
Reinstalling wine won't delete the .wine directory.  If you want to do that, you have to press Ctrl+H in your home folder and scroll down to .wine and delete it (or rm -rf .wine)

If it's a hardware issue then removing the .wine and running winecfg won't do anything, but it'll get that option out of the way. =)
