### Post by YokoZar on 2008-10-27
I'm not exactly sure what you're asking.

Wine in Intrepid is the stable version, 1.0.1, unless you deliberately go out of your way to install the beta version (currently 1.1.7).

Your graphics drivers in Intrepid are a different matter; you enable them from the Hardware Drivers panel in System->Administration.
