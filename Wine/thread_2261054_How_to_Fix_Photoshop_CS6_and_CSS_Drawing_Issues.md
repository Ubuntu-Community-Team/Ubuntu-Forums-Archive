### Post by Mike_Walsh on 2015-01-16
Hallo, dannymichel.

Perhaps a silly question; but I take it you are aware that running most of Adobe's Creative Suites under WINE tend to produce mixed results, at best?

Many versions of PhotoShop won't even install, never mind run. Have you investigated the Wine App Database, to see what it has to say about CS6? You can find it here:-

[https://appdb.winehq.org/](https://appdb.winehq.org/)

It would appear that the installer rating for CS6 is rated 'Garbage', so.....I'm surprised you've even managed to get it to install, never mind run.

Had you considered investigating the G.I.M.P? It works much the same as PhotoShop, although the way the menus are arranged, and the way in which layers, masks, etc. are accessed, is somewhat different.
