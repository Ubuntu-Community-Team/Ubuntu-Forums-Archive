### Post by cogadh on 2009-07-01
The "fixme" errors are just developer notes about incomplete or unimplemented functions in Wine. As an end user, there almost nothing you can do to to fix them in most cases (depending on the error, sometimes replacing a DLL with a Windows native one could fix it).
