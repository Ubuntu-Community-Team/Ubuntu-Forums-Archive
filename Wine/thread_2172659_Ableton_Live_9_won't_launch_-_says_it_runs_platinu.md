### Post by Bucky Ball on 2013-09-05
My advice? Just don't bother with Ubuntu for high-end audio software that is intended for Win or Mac. Ableton, Pro-Tools, Reason, Cubase, Sibelius ... the list goes on. I have a dual-boot for this very reason. The Wine path is generally more trouble than its worth and if you're using your computer to run Win/Mac programs in Wine, why? Just use Win or Mac. You don't need a Mac, even to run Pro-tools nowadays. 

There is ample proof of wasted hours with Wine and A/V software on the interwebs.

My 2cents, but having said it, might be something simple (though not sounding good) so good luck anyhow. ;)
