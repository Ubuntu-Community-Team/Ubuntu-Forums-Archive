### Post by hikaricore on 2009-10-19
Nearly all of your applications should be listed in the WINE application database, there's a sticky that covers this.
Many of them will include specific instructions on what you may need to do to get them working.

If you don't mind me asking exactly why would you need [WLM]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=127") or [IE]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=25") on Linux?
There are many native messengers that offer most of the functionality without the bloat.
There are also half a dozen native web browsers including Firefox which are superior to IE in most respects.
