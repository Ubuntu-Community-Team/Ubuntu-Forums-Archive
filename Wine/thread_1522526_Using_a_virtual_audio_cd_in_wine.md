### Post by biubidboy on 2010-07-02
Hello all.
The problem I have is quite obscure. Having recently bought Total Annihilation, I would like to play the game in Wine, with a virtual cd. However, to hear the music in-game, you must have the cd (which is a mixed half-audio and half-data cd) in the D drive of windows. To this end, I have installed cdemu and can successfully mount a virtual version of the mixed cd. However, I cannot make Wine recognize the audio section of the disc. How could I force my D drive to be this audio cd? (In Nautilus, it tells me the audio is located at "cdda://sr1/", if this helps)
Thanks a lot,
Michael.

### Post by biubidboy on 2010-07-02
Ok, I figured it out myself. For the record, I just set the associated data track of the CD as D drive and the music tracks worked out nicely.
