### Post by WienerWuerstel on 2010-11-22
I have a Laptop with the same Graphics Chip and the same "weird Screen" Issue when I exit sometimes. The Solution is pressing *ctrl+alt+F1* at the same Time and after you see the Shell you press *ctrl+alt+F7*.
