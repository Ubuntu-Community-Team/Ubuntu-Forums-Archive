### Post by Meow27 on 2009-06-10
1. a simpler way of removing it is by typing "sudo apt-get remove wine" (in the terminal without quotes)

2. that doesnt completely remove wine, if there are scraps still left in your menu type this in a terminal "alacarte"

3.finally, all of your wine data is located in your .wine directory, go to /home/username (you home folder) and press ctrl+h to see hidden files, search for .wine and delete it

may i note that if you were simply trying to start your fake c drive form scratch, all what you would need to do is follow step three. all of the fake windows files are recopied to the folder when you run another windows program.
