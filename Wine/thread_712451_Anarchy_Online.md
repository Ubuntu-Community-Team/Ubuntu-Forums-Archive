### Post by xinos on 2008-03-25
What I do is:

1. Have a old AnarchyPatcher.exe ready
2. start up client
3. Before hitting the "patch" button, I overwrite the anarchypatcher.exe file in the game folder. infact, everytime the game starts up it seems to restore the most up-to-date patcher, so you have to replace it while the client is running.
4. Hit Patch button. 

Mind that you may have to do this several times, since the patcher will close the client a couple of times. So, have that old anarchypatcher.exe ready.
