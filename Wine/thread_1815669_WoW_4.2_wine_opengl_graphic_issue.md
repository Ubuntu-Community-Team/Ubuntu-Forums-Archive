### Post by dardack on 2011-07-31
So I remembered about same time 4.2 came out, I upgraded to Nvidia's newest drivers 275.21 (July 2011) from 260.19.44 (march 2011). Something in those drivers F'd up graphical stuff.

Now I didn't try each driver in between, just stuck with what I know works. I reinstalled 260.19.44 (uninstalled 275.21) and now guess what, no graphical Glitches.

Not sure if this is blizz or nvidia's problem (leaning towards nvidia).

BTW it's not just OpenGL people, I was reading another thread that a guy posted he had to go back to January drivers to stop graphical texture glitches on Dx11/10 or w/e. 

So something changed from early in the year to July for either the nvidia drivers or WoW. Anyways, happy 260.1944 works with no issues, just hope I don't ever purchase a 500+ nvidia card soon, since only later drivers support that.
