### Post by echo $USER on 2006-08-14
Do many people here play this game?  I got it working rather well in Steam through Wine 9.16 w/ this command

> wine "C:\Program Files\Steam\steam.exe" -applaunch 1200 -opengl -heapsize 512000 +map_background none

The game is not well known, and really underrated.
