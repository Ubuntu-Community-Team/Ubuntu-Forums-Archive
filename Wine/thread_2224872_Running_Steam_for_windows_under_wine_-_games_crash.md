### Post by Wolfsblood on 2014-05-18
I recently built a new system and installed Ubuntu 14.04 LTS and managed to install Steam for Windows under wine to access my steam games that aren't native to Linux. I successfully got some installed, and was able to start both ArmA 2 and ArmA Cold War Conflict. Both games ran through their splash screens, and brought me to their menus. I started new games, and all the beginning graphics ran with little to no problems. However; as soon as I used the keyboard to begin to move around the games, they both stopped working.  I could use the mouse to look around before touching the keyboard, but the keyboard killed both games.  I haven't tried any other of my Steam/Windows games because I didn't want to take the time to download them if they weren't going to work either.

System specs: 8 core AMD FX unlocked (runing stock speed) Asus Crossfire X Formula Z mobo 16 GB ram, nvidia 9800GT nVidia 331.38 stable tested drivers

Anyone else have this problem?

---

### Post by Wolfsblood on 2014-05-25
While looking at other games in the wineHQ appdb, i came across one that mentioned the steam in-game overlay causes some steam games to crash on keyboard input when the overlay is enabled. I disabled the in-game overlay in the steam settings, and the game crash issues went away.
