### Post by zeroblitzt on 2012-08-17
AHA! Right after I posted this, I knew I couldn't give up my search. So a quick change of search terms to "Ubuntu Steam Keyboard", and this is what I come up with:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1325857](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1325857)

Short answer for those who may be experiencing the same issue I had: Wine has issues giving keyboard focus to Steam game windows. So you can force this by Alt+TABbing out of your game and explicitly selecting the game in your dock/taskbar/what-have-you.
