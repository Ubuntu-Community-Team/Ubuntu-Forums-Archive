### Post by brian62 on 2015-04-28
I've had The Sims 4 sitting in my Origin account for a while but I never tried playing it on Ubuntu because I figured it wasn't supported by Wine or Crossover. I purchased a Crossover 14.x license a month or so ago when it was $10 and saw that there was a "bottle" for The Sims 3. Just for fun I went ahead and tried installing The Sims 4 using the Sims 3 "bottle" and sure enough, it worked fine. I had to do the usual tweaks to get Origin to download it but The Sims 4 worked right out of the box.

I made a video making a Sim and playing around with it a little bit. The video is a little choppy but the screen resolution was a bit messed up and I was recording using Simple Screen Recorder which naturally degrades the fps.

But Sims 4 works great on Ubuntu!
