### Post by smitty_the_smith on 2013-08-15
I have installed Diablo II recently with playonlinux and my advice is this:  Go to battle.net and register your game. You will then be able to download a digital version that you can install using playonlinux. Alternatively, if battle.net won't work for you make isos of your diablo II disks and mount all of them at once as separate drives in wine. You may need to install gisomount.
Good luck.

For gmountiso  [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MountIso](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MountIso)
