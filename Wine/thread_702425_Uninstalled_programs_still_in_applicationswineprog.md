### Post by munch3 on 2008-02-20
When I uninstall a program I installed with wine, the shortcut for it stays in the drop down menu applications/wine/programs!How do i remove that?

---

### Post by aashay on 2008-02-20
~/.wine/drive_c/windows/profiles/All Users/Start Menu/Programs
assuming the .wine and c drive directory is in your home folder
Also check out preferences->main menu to delete the menu items
