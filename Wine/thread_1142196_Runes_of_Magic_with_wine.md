### Post by cogadh on 2009-04-29
You need to update Wine to a version greater than 1.1.17 and the game will run right out of the box without any additional DLLs:
[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=16051&iTestingId=39498](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=16051&iTestingId=39498)

In order to get a more current (i.e. the latest) version of Wine, follow the instructions here:
[http://www.winehq.org/download/deb](http://www.winehq.org/download/deb)
