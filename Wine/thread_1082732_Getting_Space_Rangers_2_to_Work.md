### Post by jnewl on 2009-03-01
Starforce doesn't involve online activation. It checks to make sure the CD is in the drive when you play. Problem is, it only works in Windows. IF that's what's causing your problem, you're going to need a no_CD/DVD patch (i.e. crack).
