### Post by namdung on 2008-12-31
Which version of OO r u using? OO2.4 is available with Ubuntu repository OO3 is the latest available which has better interface and other features.
[http://www.howtoforge.com/how-to-install-openoffice-3.0.0-on-ubuntu-8.04](http://www.howtoforge.com/how-to-install-openoffice-3.0.0-on-ubuntu-8.04)
[http://news.softpedia.com/news/How-To-Install-OpenOffice-org-3-0-in-Ubuntu-8-10-96449.shtml](http://news.softpedia.com/news/How-To-Install-OpenOffice-org-3-0-in-Ubuntu-8-10-96449.shtml)

MS Office works fine under wine but outlook doesn't work.

Virtualization is another option but may be not worth the while just for Office applications.

But final recommendation, use OO3. Once u get used to it, u'll never touch MS Office again.

But if u still insist, spend that $20.

EDIT:
Are u aware that OO3 can create, read & write MS Office formats (doc,xls,ppt)? OO3 even opens Office 2008 formats (docx,xlsx,pptx) out of the box.
