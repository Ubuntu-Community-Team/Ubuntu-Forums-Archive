### Post by Lightning Dragon on 2013-01-17
I have never encountered that before. Are you using the stable version of wine, or the beta version? If you are using beta, uninstall and go for the stable version like 1.1.3. Or, if you don't want to uninstall wine because it was a long, tiring mess to install, try PlayOnLinux first.

But tell me, does your *whole* screen turn black, or is it just a window? If it is the whole screen, I have seen people getting it to work by switching to Windowed mode--but I'm unsure how they did that in wine if the screen turns black. But you can try it by adding a launch option to a Steam shortcut: -window or this to the game you are trying to play; -nod3d9ex.

Good luck!
