### Post by Gadren on 2007-12-06
It's been a while since I've used wine, but at the core, what you do is use the terminal to navigate to the game's directory, and do something like:
```
wine GAME.EXE
```

Of course, most games aren't quite that straightforward, so you should look here for info on the specific game:
[http://appdb.winehq.org/](http://appdb.winehq.org/)
