The ForceMouseOveride thing only works about 60% of the time, but my method here, isn't even really a method, its VERY simple.
First off, in Wine configurations make sure the direct x mouse in window is ticked, and the emulate desktop ISNT clicked.

Now obviously, this may or may not work for you. So, all you have to do is go into your game settings, move the mouse speed around a little, and turn mouse smoothing on and off, and then your mouse will be fine.
