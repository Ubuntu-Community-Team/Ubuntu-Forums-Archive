### Post by ackanao on 2009-09-01
I've tested the newest version of Offline Explorer (Offline Explorer Pro 5.6.3090 Service Release 2) with Wine 1.1.28 and it works fine. The only thing that seems not to work is "Browse" option - exactly as described on this page:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=14298]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=14298")

There's a native Linux offline browsing software that you can use such as HTTrack and Pavuk (both available from repos); HTTrack is an excellent tool and I think your best option; Alternatively, there's a Scrapbook and SaveComplete add-ons for Firefox.
