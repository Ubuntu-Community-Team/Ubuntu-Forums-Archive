### Post by cogadh on 2010-05-09
Wine does not fully support all copy protection schemes, so even with a disk in the drive, the game may still ask for the disk. Usually the only way around it is to find a cracked executable that has had the copy protection removed and replace the game's default executable. Unfortunately, cracks are a legal quagmire, so don't ask where or how to get them here, you'll have to figure that one out on your own.
