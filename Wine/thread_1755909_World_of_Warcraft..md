### Post by rickygk on 2011-05-12
I solved it :-), If anyone else has this issue it's quite simple. Since I am running dual monitors I needed to select monitor 1 or monitor 2 on graphic settings. Keeping it on primary was causing it to black out.

### Post by cwwilson721 on 2011-05-12
I get 40-50 avg FPS w/NV9600GSO. You might want to do some tweaks there.

Some possibilities:
[LIST]
[*]Run in opengl mode (Search the forum. In opengl in wine, I get faster FPS than in Windows 7 w/D3D)
[*]Start your settings at 'low' and start moving sliders.
[*]Don't use AA
[*]Water effects, and distance kill FPS too
[*]If you like the 'eye candy', keep what you got. I prefer FPS and pwning the opposition over "Ooh. You got pretty armor!" and getting ganked.
[/LIST]

Just a few things. Find a balance between the eye-candy and FPS.

And as far as FPS goes, try in a BG or 10+ man raid to see what 'pretty' can do to your FPS.
