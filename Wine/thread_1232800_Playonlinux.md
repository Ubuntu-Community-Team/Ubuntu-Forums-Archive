### Post by Nixie Pixel on 2009-08-07
Just do a search in Synaptec for ubuntulooks and install it.

The message pops up because you installed a Gnome theme that wants ubuntulooks, and you don't have it. It will continue to show up when you do various things in the terminal and such until you install ubuntulooks.
