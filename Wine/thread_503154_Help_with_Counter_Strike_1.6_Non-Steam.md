### Post by huangja on 2007-07-20
Make sure you dont have any other processes running that use the sound driver, such as a media player or whatnot. 

I am assuming you are using WINE, try setting the driver to OSS in winecfg. Also, try setting the settings to the lowest possible: emulation, 8 bit, 8000 hz

For the mouse, make sure desktop effects are disabled. Also, go to wincfg and make sure "allow the window manager to control the windows" is checked. Also, try checking "allow DirectX apps to stop the mouse leaving their window"
