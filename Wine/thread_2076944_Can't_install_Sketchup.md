### Post by grey1beard on 2013-01-01
Strange, but I too have now seemed to have solved it !
Having downloaded the latest versions of wine and sketchup I thought it would be plain sailing.
However, having got stuck, I opened Applications/Wine/winetricks, chose the option - "*Install an app*" and selected Sketchup from the list, even though I knew it was installed already.
Winetricks downloaded GoogleSketchUp8WEN.exe (or something like that), and at the end of a normal looking install, there it was.
Amazing !

And it even appears to work !!!
