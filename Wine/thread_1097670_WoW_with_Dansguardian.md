### Post by hikaricore on 2009-03-16
The problem is not just the tracker IP address, but the fact that torrenting connects to a wide range of other users.
I suggest if you absolutely must use a firewall that you download your patches from mirror sites instead of using the downloader.

[http://www.wowwiki.com/Patch_mirrors](http://www.wowwiki.com/Patch_mirrors)

ps.  it's really just easier to turn off the background downloader and just deal with it the download on patch day imho
