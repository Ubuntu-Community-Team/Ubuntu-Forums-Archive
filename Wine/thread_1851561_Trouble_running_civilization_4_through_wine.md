### Post by TacticalApe on 2011-10-04
From what it sounds like, you already have it installed. If you do, open terminal and type "winecfg". In the "drives" tab, add a new drive, and browse and locate the disc you have in the drive. Then it should work, at least that's what I do for all my games that require a disc.
