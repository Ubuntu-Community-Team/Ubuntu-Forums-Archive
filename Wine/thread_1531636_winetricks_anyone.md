### Post by cogadh on 2010-07-15
You can't possibly "clobber your whole Wine installation" with winetricks. You could possibly screw up something in your .wine directory (which is very easily fixed), but Wine itself cannot be harmed by it. Really, the best advice is the oldest advice in the tech world: RTFM.

[http://wiki.winehq.org/winetricks](http://wiki.winehq.org/winetricks)
