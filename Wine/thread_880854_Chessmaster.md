### Post by davidpeace on 2008-08-05
I got Chessmaster, grandmaster edition (latest one) to work under wine. It needs directx9 which can be downloaded via winetricks which you can get from here:

[http://wiki.winehq.org/winetricks](http://wiki.winehq.org/winetricks)

run it with:

sh winetricks

on the commandline.

Scroll down until you see directx9, click and install.
