### Post by cameran on 2009-02-24
hi.  i've used cedega for a while now and i think it is worth the money.  i can run warhammer online without the problems encountered by wine users.  guild wars is identical in cedega to windows for me.  the same with oblivion - i can max it out in cedega (including HDR lighting and pixel shader 3.0) and performance is almost identical to windows.  bioshock works great too.  

the only game i use wine for is LOTRO, everything else runs better in cedega than wine for me and they release monthly updates to the engine to improve performance and enhance compatability.  for a while last year the customer service sucked but they listened to the community and things have been good again.

i know i'm in the minority on this but i've had a good cedega experience so i figure i'd chime in. 

cameran
