### Post by Tweak42 on 2011-10-06
Windowed (Fullscreen) - 1920x1200

Because I use 2 monitors (Twinview), the picture would get distorted using Fullscreen because of the merged desktop (3200x1200).  Also the mouse pointer "sticks" to the main screen in Fullscreen mode, thus requiring an alt-tab to break the pointer out to use on the second screen.  Performance didn't seem to vary in either mode, but I haven't actually compared them in a year or so.

Note: this is on my desktop system, Core2Duo 3Ghz w/Nvidia Geforce GTX460

The main reason would be for task switching easily. In windowed mode, one can easily switch between WoW and other apps open, like Firefox or messengers.

Also, some people do experience better performance in windowed mode over fullscreen. I get much better performance in a windowed virtual desktop than I do actually playing WoW fullscreen.
