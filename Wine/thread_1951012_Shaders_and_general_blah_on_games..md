### Post by lite1979 on 2012-04-05
You'll definitely want to get your hands dirty when it comes to finding the right driver for your machine and as for menus and fonts, I'd check to make sure you have all of the microsoft fonts installed. Tahoma is one that I used to need separately to run Half-Life 1 based-games, but I think that it got popular enough to be included with other packages at this point. Check for playability on winehq.org as well in their AppDB. I find a lot of helpful information there.

When looking for drivers, make sure you know exactly what kind of video card you have, and don't be afraid to try installing a driver from a tarball or script. If you mess up, you should always have an option to revert back to your old driver/kernel module.
