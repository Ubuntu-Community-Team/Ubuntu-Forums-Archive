### Post by SMOKE14 on 2012-10-24
Okay, I don't know how to install the SP's, but I do know how to get it installed. Insert your Office DVD, and navigate to the setup.exe file, and right click, open with WINE. Enter your Product key, install as normal. Then, go to the Dash, type in the name of one of the programs (etc, Word), and select don't install Microsoft Updates, because, in my experience, when you do that, Office freezes, and keeps freezing on startup. Hope I helped :)
