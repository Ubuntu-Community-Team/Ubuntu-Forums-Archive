### Post by The Cosmic Hobo on 2008-06-12
You'll need to know the name of the package you downloaded, but if you open a terminal, the command
sudo apt-get remove
followed by the package name should do the trick. It will ask for your user password; this is perfectly normal. I'm surprised it's not listed in your installed programs, though.
