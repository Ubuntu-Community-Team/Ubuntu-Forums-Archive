### Post by howefield on 2012-02-05
Thread moved to the *"Wine*" forum.

Try opening the dash and typing the name of your Windows application.

To get to the Wine folder, open Nautilus and press Ctrl + H to reveal hidden folders and files., look for one called .wine (or similar)
