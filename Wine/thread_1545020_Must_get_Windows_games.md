### Post by proteusmoteus on 2010-08-08
I like the idea of a short list of fun games. the appdb is just about whether the game works not whether the games are fun or well liked.

My suggestions are some of the better indie games:
Links are to reviews which have at the end of each a direct link to a demo

[Braid]("http://jayisgames.com/archives/2009/05/braid.php") - Manipulate the flow of time to solve puzzles (jayisgames best of 2009)
[Audiosurf]("http://db.tigsource.com/games/audiosurf") - adapts your mp3 collection into a puzzle racer (IGF 2008: Excellence In Audio)
[Everyday Shooter]("http://db.tigsource.com/games/everyday-shooter") - album of musical abstract shoot-em-ups (IGF 2007: Design Innovation, IGF 2007: GameTap Indie Award, IGF 2007: Excellence In Audio)
[Max and the Magic Marker]("http://maxandthemagicmarker.com/") - platformer where everything you draw  becomes a physics based object. see [appdb for demo]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=20747") (IGF 2010 D2D Vision Award)
[Plants vs Zombies]("http://jayisgames.com/archives/2009/05/plants_vs_zombies.php") - Defend your home from zombies with an arsenal of plants (jayisgames best of 2009)
[VVVVVV]("http://jayisgames.com/archives/2010/01/vvvvvv.php") - retro puzzle platformer
[Torchlight]("http://jayisgames.com/archives/2009/11/torchlight.php") - beautiful rogue like action RPG

Freeware indie games:
[spelunky]("http://jayisgames.com/archives/2009/09/spelunky.php") - rogue like platformer (jayisgames best of 2009)
[Knytt Stories]("http://jayisgames.com/archives/2007/08/knytt_stories.php") - Ambient pixel platformer
[Chalk]("http://db.tigsource.com/games/chalk") - Shoot em up via drawing lines
[ben there dan that]("http://jayisgames.com/archives/2008/09/ben_there_dan_that.php") - hilarious old school adventure (jayisgames best of 2008)

I just recently discovered indie games and was delighted with how many worked easily with wine.
