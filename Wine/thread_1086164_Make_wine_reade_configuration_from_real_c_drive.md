### Post by cogadh on 2009-03-03
Doing that is a great way to destroy your Windows operating system, hence why Wine is preventing you from doing just that. It is a safety feature of Wine that is designed to prevent you from making a monumentally stupid mistake. Honestly, even if you could do that, there is absolutely nothing to be gained from it, since pretty much all the configs and DLLs you could need are already in Wine and the ones that aren't are easily added to Wine.
