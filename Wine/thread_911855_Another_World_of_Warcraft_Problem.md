### Post by Eviljim on 2008-09-08
Heres a thought for you.....

Have you tried checking the cooling fan on top of the graphics card?

I have had this before, and the fan was full of dust causing the card to overheat after about 15-20 mins. The card had a thermal protection device which throttles the GPU down to hopefully lower the GPU temperature. This would cause the "stutter" you are experiencing.

When you log out of WoW you are reducing the workload on the GPU and hence it starts to cooldown.

When you log back in, the workload increases hence re-occurs.

Failing that check that the PC itself is free from as much dust as possible. A restricted airflow is not good for air cooled components.
