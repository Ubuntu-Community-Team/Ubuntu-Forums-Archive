### Post by YokoZar on 2008-10-14
Picassa is an application ported with Wine - it actually bundles its own copy of Wine that only it uses.  So upgrading your system Wine to 1.1.5 doesn't affect Picassa.


As for why both Office and Picassa are running faster for you in Ubuntu/Wine than they are in XP -- well, there's really no reason they shouldn't.  The normal reason Wine apps tend to be slower is due to bugs in Wine, but for the Office you're running they've been largely taken care of.

When it's not Wine bugs causing slowness, differences between Linux and Windows itself become important.  So, in your case, Linux was simply faster than your Windows setup.
