### Post by cireland0307 on 2008-09-20
Hey all.

I used to use Ubuntu (I now use Arch), but that's not the point. I still come to these forums because they are pretty darn good. I just wanted to put out a positive comment on how great wine has come along. I'm very impresssed, compared to where I was a year ago (which was basically nil).

Anyway, I just wanted to share my successes with wine with my fellow linux users.

There are some flakey things still, some games work one time, then don't work the next...crazy. Everytime you start fresh, it seems one of your games (or expansion packs) are going to fail. 

However, I've successfully installed on my laptop, Civilization IV (and all xp packs), Galactic Civilizations II (and all xp packs), Warhammer Dawn of War (2nd and 3rd xp only...the 3rd is called Soulstorm), Warhammer Mark of Chaos, Battle of Middle-Earth I and II and Rise of the Witch King, Heroes of Might and Magic V (and all xp packs), Fantasy Wars, and some older games, such as, Heroes III and IV Complete, Warlords IV, Kohan II.

I'm very impressed. These are all games from when I used Windows. I'm still awaiting the day Linux versions of our beloved games are made, but in the meantime, we can play some of these on wine. Well, these are some of my favorites anyway.
