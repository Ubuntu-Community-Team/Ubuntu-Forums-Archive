### Post by Toz on 2012-02-08
Click on the "Configure" button in PlayOnLinux. On the left side of the dialog that opens up, click to select Sim City 3000 and on the right side on the General tab, there should be an "Enable debugging" checkbox. Check it and start the game. This time a terminal window should open up and show debugging information. Have a look there for info as to why its not starting. Feel free to post the contents back here if you want a second set of eyes to have a look at it.
