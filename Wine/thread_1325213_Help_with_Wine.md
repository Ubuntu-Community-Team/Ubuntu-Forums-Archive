### Post by beastrace91 on 2009-11-13
Howdy,

Just to throw out there - Wine will not crash your system, at worst you will mess up your Wine settings and just need to reinstall (just) Wine.

On to your question - for most applications you are going to reinstall them under Wine. When Wine is installed it creates it's own "fake" C:/ drive on your Linux partition (it doesn't point itself to your Windows). While it is possible to run some applications right from Windows - at times this can cause issues and for a beginning user it is simplest to just reinstall the applications under Ubuntu using Wine.

Have any other questions or if any of the above need clarification let me know.
