### Post by schtufbox on 2008-05-07
in winecfg  go to the graphics tab, click the 'Emulate a virtual Desktop' checkbox, then type in the size you'd like the window. I typically use 1280x800 as I have a 1440x900 res on my desktop, this way i can play in a window with a little screen estate free :)
