### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-11-28
Wine is not a Windows emulator that will run just any Windows apps.  To see if yours will work, go to the WineHQ webpage and enter the name of the app.  IF it's not found, or if the rating is lower than Gold, it probably won't work:  [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true")
