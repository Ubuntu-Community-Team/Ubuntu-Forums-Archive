### Post by humphreybc on 2009-03-12
You need to use Wine software emulation. Search the repositories (add/remove programs) for "wine" and install it.

Once it is installed, navigate to your Office 2007 installer (.exe) and right click > open with Wine. Install the program. You can find it under Applications > Wine > Programs.

To check whether other programs will work with Wine, go to WineHQ.org

Word 2007: [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=12811](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=12811)
