### Post by jockyburns on 2011-11-18
Open a terminal (ctrl +alt+t+ then type in     (  sh winetricks corefonts) **without the brackets** ;)

Should download the corefonts from Wine. Might ask for your password, just type it in the box and continue. A small window will open once they've downloaded (looks like a typical Microsoft window and asks you to agree to continue (just like Windoze) Agree and it should then load whatever it does. Hopefully your windows games/applications should now show something readable. ;);)
