### Post by Gothamite on 2009-05-18
Yes, I did manage to get it to work; it turns out that I needed the driver from the ATI website in addition to the xorg driver. To go to the page with the driver download on it click [here. ]("http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/linux/Pages/radeon_linux.aspx?type=2.4.1&product=2.4.1.3.1&lang=English&rev=9.4&ostype=Linux%20x86")
 I highly recommend that you look at the installer instructions because you may have a hard time getting it to work with no idea how to install it (like me), the link for the installer instructions is on that page. (note: this will only work with ATI cards and not nvidia for sure.)

Hope this helps ;).
