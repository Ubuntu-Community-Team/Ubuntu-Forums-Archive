### Post by rocky5051 on 2010-01-23
I've done it several times. To install it you'll need to get mfc42.dll and put that into the wine system32 folder, though, or the product key entry won't work.

Also unless the game is being played on some solid hardware your friend will likely experience bad mouse lag. I've tried to play it on my laptop that would otherwise play it well on Windows 7 to no avail.

If they have an older graphics card (one that uses legacy nvidia or legacy fglrx drivers) they may experience a case where the map models completely disappear.

Any other info you'll need is on the AppDB.
