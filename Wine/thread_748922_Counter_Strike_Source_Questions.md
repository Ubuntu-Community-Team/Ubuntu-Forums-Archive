### Post by schtufbox on 2008-04-09
While I can run it with everything full on my PC, it's slow as a dog :D
Mind you my PC isn't top of the range. I just disable HDR, Low Shadows, and Simple reflections and it runs fine.
I'd imagine with a decent PC you can run it all on full.  I know I will when I replace my PC :D

I read on the winehq appdb that high quality sound doesn't work correctly.  Seems to work for me, though it occasionally slips into 'medium quality' while still showing 'high' in options..I just select mediium, reselct high, hit apply, and it works again.

Other than that no problems at all.
