### Post by cogadh on 2010-07-31
Wine does not support every copy protection scheme out there and most likely never will (it would require licensing the copy protection and possibly incorporating non-open source code into Wine). The only way around the instances where Wine does not support copy protection is to use a "cracked" executable. That subject is taboo here, as the act of creating or using a cracked exe may be illegal in your country, so don't bother asking where to get a crack or how to use it. You are on your own in this, remember: Google is you friend.
