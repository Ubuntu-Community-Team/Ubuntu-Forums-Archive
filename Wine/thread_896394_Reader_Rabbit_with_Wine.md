The Paso Robles Allure de Robles is produced from grapes grown on three of our vineyards on the west side of Paso Robles.   The first is Cristalla Ranch which surrounds the main winery on San Marcos Road. The vines from this vineyard are from an old clone of Syrah that was grafted for Rabbit Ridge and two different clones of Syrah - the Estrella clone and the Tablas Creek clone.   The second vineyard is located on Live Oak Road in the hills on the north side of Highway 46 West.   This vineyard is one of the highest in Paso Robles, located at 1,600 feet elevation.   This vineyard has large amounts of limestone in the soil with an average soil pH of approximately 8.   From the Live Oak Vineyard come three different clones of Syrah; Estrella, Tablas Creek and Entav 877.   These vines are planted in three distinctly different locations within the vineyard and the fruit ripens as much as three weeks apart.
