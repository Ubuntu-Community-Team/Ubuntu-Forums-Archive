### Post by pedro_orange on 2009-07-21
I just open my home folder and hit Ctrl + H (Show hidden), go into the .wine folder, and find the folder which contains the stuff I want to delete. Delete the folder.

Then to remove them from the Applications menu, just open it up, right-click and choose to edit the menu. 

Sorted.
