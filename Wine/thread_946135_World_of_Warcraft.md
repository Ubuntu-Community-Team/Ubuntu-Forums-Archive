### Post by Mad-Halfling on 2008-10-13
I run WoW on WINE and it actually runs better than on windows (*snigger*).  I've got an HP Pavilion laptop - dual-core 1.7Ghz, 2Gb RAM and a GeForce 7400, I play hoarde on Asuzune with ct_mod, threat meter, damage meter.

Here's a sample of my speed comparison on my system (dual booting 8.04 and vista)

Prog load time - linux=5 secs, windows=19 secs
char load time - linus=12 secs, windows=21 secs
FPS (tested in the same area under the same circumstances) - linux=36, windows=29

so as you can see I get a noticeable performance increase.
