### Post by Brian88 on 2010-10-16
Try changing to another theme (like Clearlooks) and see if the problem persist. The default Ubuntu 10.04 and 10.10 uses texture patterns for the taskbar background, soif the background of the icon was not transparent, this behavior would happen.
