### Post by Mornedhel on 2009-07-16
According to the Shub-Internet, BioWare developed a native Linux client for Neverwinter Nights. Apparently it's freely downloadable for anyone who purchased the game. You should look into this instead, as it's likely to give you a better gaming experience.

Otherwise, the latest version of Wine from the Wine repositories is likely to work very well with NWN (1.1.23 is marked Platinum compatibility for the patched version of NWN). Just follow the instructions on the Wine website on how to enable their repository for Ubuntu and update Wine through the package manager.
