### Post by JWACKED on 2012-06-21
I have found the solution to getting Diablo II to work in Linux Ubuntu 12.04

Here's how:

First install Wine, make sure it's the current version
Make sure you have Winetricks, if not, install it.
Next go to Dash Home at the top left of your screen.
Type in when it pops out Wine, it should give you a choice.
Next, when you open up winetricks, go to 'Install a Game'
Make sure your installation disk for Diablo 2 is in your CD/DVD player
Follow the instruction it gives you.
When installation is finished, it will do a video test that will distort your monitor screen, don't be alarmed, just go to log out at the top right of your screen and restart your computer.
When you restart your computer, go back to 'Dash Home', now type Diablo II, and it should work.
I figured out what it does, it makes a C drive in your computer, for Window type programs, it even has internet explorer on the fake C drive.

So that's it
