I play Diablo II under Wine and it works okay except for this one issue. I play in window mode and when I minimize the diablo window the top border of all windows (Firefox etc.) disappear. It's also impossible to unminimize the diablo window. Instead a small square, which seems to be the top left corner of the diablo window, appears. Diablo can't be exited either.

How do I solve this? I read somewhere that Alt + Return would restore the window but that doesn't work.

Edit: I solved it by setting Wine to emulate a virtual desktop. A bit ugly but it works.
