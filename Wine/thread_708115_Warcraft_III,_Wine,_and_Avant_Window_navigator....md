### Post by naz37 on 2008-03-01
i had this exact problem and compiz have a workaround for it . 

In the ComizConfig settings manager, enable the the "Workarounds" plugin and tick the "legacy fullscreen support" in that plugins settings settings. Fullscreen should work for almost all 3d games.
