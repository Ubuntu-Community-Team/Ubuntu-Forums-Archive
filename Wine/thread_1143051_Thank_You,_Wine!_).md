So here's just an lol story for you.

I logged into my bank account under XP and tried to upload a deposit using a scanner. XP found the scanner, installed the drivers, and when I got to the site, it couldn't scan, for whatever reason. So, I rebooted and loaded up Ubuntu.

I then went to the site and again attempted to do the same process, and much to my dismay, the deposit program won't work with Linux. So I'm wondering what to do since mailing it in would take more business days than I have to offer. (Just for a background, I have no local branch where I am located.)

That's when the lightbulb went off in my head. I installed Firefox for Windows under Wine, loaded up my bank's web site, and lo and behold, the deposit was completed. Sometimes you have to do stupid bass-ackwards things just to do something simple.
