### Post by themusicalduck on 2010-09-24
While playing TrackMania, the game will seem to run fine through until I join a multiplayer game. Then it will still work fine for a few seconds, but eventually start locking up quite badly. Or I will just experience very low frame rates through parts of the track. Sometimes the audio goes completely choppy and the video more or less stops and I have to force quit.

It doesn't look like there is an increase in CPU usage when this happens. Strangely, if I play in single player mode, I get no problems at all.

I thought it might be to do with the car models of the other players messing things up. Although, the lockups don't seem to coincide with lots of players being on the screen at once, but more likely when I go on a wall ramp or when things get particularly fast.

I also tried disabling some things related to networking, like peer to peer, but it made no difference.

I have the dx9 dlls in System32. Using a virtual desktop and either OSS or ALSA doesn't make a difference.

I have 10.10 and Wine 1.2.
