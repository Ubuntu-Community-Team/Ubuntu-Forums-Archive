### Post by YokoZar on 2008-11-06
> **mkofal said:**
> I have upgraded a few of my machines from 8.04 to 8.10. My kids play some Windows games on one of them.  Although we don't have compiz enabled on that machine, it appears that the multimedia performance has degraded since the upgrade, almost making them unplayable. Has anybody else experienced this, and have you found a solution?
Some older video cards are no longer supported with proprietary (restricted) drivers in Intrepid.  It's possible you got migrated during the upgrade to the free (slower) drivers.

Double check at System->Administration->Hardware Drivers to make sure.
