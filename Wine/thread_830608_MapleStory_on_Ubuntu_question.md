### Post by cogadh on 2008-06-16
GameGuard runs as a virtual driver, which is why it doesn't work in Wine (Windows drivers do not and never will work in Wine). It has nothing to do with any scripts it may or may not use. WineX is a hideously out of date precursor to Trangaming's Cedega. It can't really run anything anymore. At the moment, VMWare is still the only way anyone has gotten it to work.
