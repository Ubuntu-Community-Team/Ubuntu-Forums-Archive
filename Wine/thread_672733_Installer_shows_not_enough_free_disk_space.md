### Post by IanW on 2008-01-22
How old is Fallout 1?

ISTR that Win95/98 had a HDD size limit of 37Gb. It's possible that the game installer is designed with the same limit, and is getting a false reading of your free space.

You could try reducing the size of your /home's free space by creating/copying several Gb into /home, then starting the installer again.
