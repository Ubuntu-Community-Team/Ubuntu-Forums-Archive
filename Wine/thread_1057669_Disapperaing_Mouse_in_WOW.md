### Post by Ameneon on 2009-02-02
As I wrote in another thread (and again, this isn't my solution, but it worked for me anyway) :

> **Ameneon said:**
> Not my solution, but

```
/console farclip 499
```

in the regular game chat will probably solve the problem as it sounds exactly like the problem I had. The explanation was given in this thread:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1052433](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1052433)

Worked just dandy for me at least :)

Edit: I haven't myself paid much attention to what it does to the draw distance so I'm not gonna say that it's bad or good. For me; it fixed the problem to an acceptable price (bearing in mind that it is a workaround and not a fix). For others; they don't like losing the draw distance and so will choose other workarounds. I don't have the details on that though, nor do I know whether these other workarounds, eh, work.
