### Post by {BzF}~JOKesTER on 2008-03-06
Yeah Purge The Configuration Files Also Delete The Main C: Drive Folder - Heres How:

In The Console Type:

apt-get remove wine --purge

Ok, Now.

Open Your Home Folder - In The "View" Tab At The Top Select "Show Hidden Files" - Now Find The Folder ".wine" And ".wineWAX6XV" And Delete Them.

And Now Reinstall Wine.

Hope This Helps:popcorn::KS

### Post by {BzF}~JOKesTER on 2008-03-06
Also Note That When You Re-install Wine Your "Applications" - "Wine" - "Programs" -----Entrys May Still Be There.

To Remove Those Go To: "System" - "Preferences" -"Main Menu" ------And Select The "Wine" Tab And Delete The Subfolders Of The Programs That Are Not Installed.


Hope This Helps.
