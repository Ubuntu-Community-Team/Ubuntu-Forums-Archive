### Post by MyOwnOwner on 2010-01-02
I know the topic is a bit old...

After I tried to install LoL (League of Legends) on my Ubuntu 9.10 with wine-1.1.32, I searched the internet and came out with the following:

LoL - Forum topic: [Configuring LoL in WINE?]("http://leagueoflegends.com/board/showthread.php?t=5204")
WineHQ: [Main > Games > Strategy Games > Real-Time Tactics > League of Legends]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=10436")

The wine entry is not up-to-date, but it seems like LoL is really not supported yet (the beta version was rated Garbage).

By the way: To find out your wine version number just open a terminal and type: ```
wine --version
```
