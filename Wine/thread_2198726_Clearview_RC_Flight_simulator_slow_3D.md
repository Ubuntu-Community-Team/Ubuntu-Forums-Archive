### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-01-10
You didn't say what version of Clearview you're using,  but the WineHQ webpage has only a Silver rating for the 5x version -- and that means that major functions will not work (otherwise, it would have a higher rating).  When running games, I strongly recommend that folks only use Wine if the game rating is Gold or higher:  [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=5390]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=5390")
