### Post by cogadh on 2008-06-18
From the official announcement:
> The Wine team is proud to announce that Wine 1.0 is now available.
[B]This is the first stable release of Wine after 15 years of development
and beta testing[/B].
1.0 *is* stable. However, stable, in software relase terms, does not mean everything will work with it. It just means that this release will not be changing on a bi-weekly basis as the previous unstable versions did. There will still be unstable development releases (the git source tree), but there won't be another stable release until 1.0.1 in a few months.
