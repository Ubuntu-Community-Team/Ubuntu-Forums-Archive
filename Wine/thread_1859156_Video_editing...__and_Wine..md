### Post by e79 on 2011-10-13
The software itself is only supported by the company who made it, Cyberlink in this case. If they don't have a Linux version, you could still try to install it under [WINE]("http://www.winehq.org/"), but after checking their [AppDB]("http://appdb.winehq.org/"), I haven't found anything related to PowerDirector.

Another option would be to run a Windows 7 installation inside a virtual machine.

Hope this helps.
