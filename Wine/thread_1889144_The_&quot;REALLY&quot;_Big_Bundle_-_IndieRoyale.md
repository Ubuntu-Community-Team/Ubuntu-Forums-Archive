### Post by thatguruguy on 2011-12-01
I looked up the games on Winehq.org.

There are no listings for "Runespell:Overture" or "Really Big Sky."

The results for "Eufloria" are old and somewhat mixed (from Gold to Bronze).

Both "Cthulhu Saves the World" and "Breath of Death VII" have earned Garbage ratings (i.e., they are unplayable on Wine).

If you are looking for a package of games to run on your Linux computer (with or without Wine), you are probably better off sticking with the offerings of the Humble Bundle, rather than this package.
