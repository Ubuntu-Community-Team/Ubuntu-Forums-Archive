### Post by cogadh on 2008-11-19
It is probably due to the fact that you are playing on a laptop that is not capable of natively displaying 640X480 resolution in full screen mode, so the game is instead displayed in a 640X480 section of the screen's default resolution. If that is the case, there is really nothing you can do about it.
