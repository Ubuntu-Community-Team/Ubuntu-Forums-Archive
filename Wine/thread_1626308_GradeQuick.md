### Post by theProf on 2013-02-05
Solution!!!  I just checked the edline website, and in the last week, they've released a browser only version of GradeQuick for use on MACs and Ipads.

I used the ChromeUASpoofer extension and spoofed to Safari.  I loaded up edline, and an option for "My Gradebooks" was available!!  Clicked on it and was given a web interface I could use to manage my grades in linux!

I hope you are all able to get it working as well.
