### Post by Brynster on 2008-06-27
it depends

on my system based around a AMD 6000 x2 and a Nvidia 8800 gts and 3gb of ddr2 (sorry not bragging i just like the way it looks when you type it out) it runs really well and looks good in directx 8.1 mode. (Launch option dxlevel 81) and i get around 200fps in CS:Source and about 150fps in HL2 in 1024 x 768 resolution. However if i move away from directx 8.1 or change resolution either higher or lower it all crumbles to nothing my frame rates drop and the visual effects don't improve significantly except for the water effect, which looks as it would in Windows.

the moral i guess is you can tweak it so far to make it about 95% reproduction of the windows version however your mileage will vary.
