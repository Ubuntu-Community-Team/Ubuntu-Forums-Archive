### Post by khelben1979 on 2011-01-22
I hate to say this, but the problem with your performance is that you have way too old hardware to get the performance with Wine that you want from this. Playing Windows games with Wine gives lower performance than a real Windows system in most cases. With Wine you rather need a fast cpu than a fast graphics card, although you need both.

To increase performance you can start off playing the game on only 1 monitor and then think about if you have any future plans regarding hardware upgrades.

Also, it's important to use the non-free nVidia drivers for maximum performance.
