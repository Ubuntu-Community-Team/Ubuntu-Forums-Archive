I spent a long time looking for a way to install wotlk directly from the dvd with little success. 

But I have a solution that worked for me.

open the wotlk in nautilus. Then open a terminal as root (I used sudo -i)
then simply drag and drop the install.exe icon into the terminal window.

That worked for me.
