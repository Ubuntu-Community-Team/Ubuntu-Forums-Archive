### Post by nikkkko on 2009-10-21
You probably figured this out already, but what I do is right click over the file, choose Properties>Open With and choose Wine.  I do this for SketchUp files and Wine figures out that I need the SketchUp application to open SketchUp files so I would imagine it will work for other file types.
