### Post by Greg T. on 2008-01-07
Have you taken a look at wine-doors? They're claiming (limited) success with Office 2003. [http://www.wine-doors.org/wordpress/](http://www.wine-doors.org/wordpress/)

Unless you're using Crossover Linux ($40), I wouldn't expect Office 2003 to work well at all, and even with Crossover it has problems. If you do manage to get it running pretty good, you'd be doing us all a favor by posting how you did so.

The other option, if you have a copy of Win XP gathering dust, is to install Windows and Office on VirtualBox. This is what I'm doing on my home PC, and it works surprisingly well on those few occasions when I use it.
