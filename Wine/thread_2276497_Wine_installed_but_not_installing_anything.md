### Post by Mark Phelps on 2015-05-05
Hate to tell you this, but you're likely wasting your time with iTunes in Linux.  Even though POL helps install it, once that is done, it's not going to work very well.  Look at the ratings for iTunes on the WineHQ website: [https://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=1347]("https://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=1347")

Any rating less than Gold is essentially wasting your time -- as so much will NOT work as to make it useless.
