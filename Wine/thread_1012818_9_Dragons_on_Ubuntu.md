### Post by cogadh on 2009-02-11
It doesn't matter whether or not it installs, it won't actually work. 9 Dragons uses GameGuard anti-cheat protection, which will likely never work in Wine, since it is a driver-based protection system (Windows drivers don't work in Wine):
[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=4561](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=4561)
