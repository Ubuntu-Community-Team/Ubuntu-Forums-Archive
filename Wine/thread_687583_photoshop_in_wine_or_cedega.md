### Post by handy on 2008-02-04
Have a look at [***_Codeweavers' CrossOver_***]("http://www.codeweavers.com/products/"), it may solve your problems.

Codeweavers are Wine's primary supporter, their forum support is excellent, with a strong developer presence.  Codeweavers are all the things that, unfortunately, Transgaming (Cedega) are not.

There is a [***_trial version of CrossOver_***]("http://www.codeweavers.com/products/download_trial_linux/") available on their site.

Version 7 of CrossOver is due out any time also.
