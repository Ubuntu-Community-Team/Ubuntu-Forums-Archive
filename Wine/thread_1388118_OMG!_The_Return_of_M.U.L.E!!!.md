### Post by oldrocker99 on 2010-01-22
The late Dani Bunten's game, which has sat for 20+ years on Computer Gaming World's all-time best list, has been fully resurrected, (updated) theme music and all!:D

[http://www.planetmule.com](http://www.planetmule.com)

is where to get it. There isn't yet a Debian binary, but cding to the ule directory and a ./mule.sh will get you rolling. Online play is built in, and you can play solo against 3 AIs as well.:eek:

I played many, many games of this sophisticated economic simulation back in the day on my C=64, and have wished for this good a recreation for lo these many years. My wish has been granted.):P
