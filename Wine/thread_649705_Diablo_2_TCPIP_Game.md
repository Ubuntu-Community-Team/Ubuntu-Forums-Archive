---

### Post by Beren Camlost on 2007-12-26
For people to connect to your game, they'll need to know your IP (since you are the host, 127.0.0.1 will be the address you 'connect' to, so don't worry about that). 

To find out your IP, go to [http://whatismyipaddress.com/](http://whatismyipaddress.com/). You will have to give this IP to the players you want to connect to your game, as they will have to manually enter it in their multiplayer settings when they try to join.
