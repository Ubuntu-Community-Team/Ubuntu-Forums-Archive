### Post by bipolaric on 2008-08-28
hello friend, I had the same problem a while ago, here's what you do.......Once you have uninstalled the required program through wine, Right click your applications then Edit Menus, then left click the wine arrow, then click the programs icon, then simply un-check the programs you've uninstalled, then they will be removed from your wine>programs list.

Hope this helps - Mark :)
