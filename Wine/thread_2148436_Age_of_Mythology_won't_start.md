### Post by Rayke on 2013-08-16
I had several problems involving the installation of Age of Mythology on 12.04. Could not get PidGen.dll to be recognized so I copied Age of Mythology from a Windows system into the windows/wine directory. This solved every issue I had, although I realize that you may not have a Windows installation to fall back on.
