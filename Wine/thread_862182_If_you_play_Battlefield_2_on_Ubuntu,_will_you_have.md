### Post by blazercist on 2008-07-17
I don't know about battlefeild, but when I play HL2 based games on ubuntu using wine, my latency is always much lower than it was on windows.  I don't think it yeilds any measurable result in game play but it is definately better latency.  a few ms will generally go unnoticed.  If you have a high speed connection and are joining servers that are relatively close you will generally have 20-30ms ping on windows, I get 5-10ms on linux, 10-20 ms will not be noticable by any human.
