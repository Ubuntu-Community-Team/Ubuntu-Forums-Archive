### Post by David Steele on 2009-03-31
I thought you might like to know.

Couldn't get Spotify to play music even though I'd followed all the setup steps.

The app kept crashing with "internal corruption" messages and I spent ages messing with cache settings and audio drivers.  Nothing worked.

In the end I read the "read this before posting" message, and on its advice, checked out the Spotify section of the Wine Application Database.

Where I read:

"Will work fine as long as you have run Wine Internet Explorer at least once"

I tried it.  Problem solved. Seems you need to download "gecko" patch for Spotify to work.

Another thing I did was upgraded WINE from 1.1.1 to 1.1.18.  I've no idea which of these two things worked but if you try them both it should work out for you too.

If you've not tried Spotify yet, I can't recommend it highly enough. I say the same about Ubuntu.
