### Post by jlg6184 on 2009-10-11
I just installed Morrowind GOTY, and I have virtually zero Wine experience, and I am running great. There are a few issues that arise, such as mp3-playback problems and what not. In my search to get mp3-playback ingame I found a pretty straightforward tutorial on getting Morrowind up and running the best it possibly can. It also has the solution for mp3 playback as well, which is a matter of replacing the winemp3.acm with another mp3 codec, but that is also in the link below.

[http://forums.fedoraforum.org/showthread.php?t=156033](http://forums.fedoraforum.org/showthread.php?t=156033)

It is a complete walk thru, so even if you don't know much about Wine, you should still be capable of installing. I would also recommend gmount-iso (sudo apt-get install gmountiso) incase you have ripped your copy to your hard drive.
