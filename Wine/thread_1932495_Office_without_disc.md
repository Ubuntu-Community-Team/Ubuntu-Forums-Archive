### Post by cbanakis on 2012-02-27
If you have a desktop with a dvd drive, you can easily burn to an iso, with your choice of program.

Then just extract the iso to a folder on your netbook, or mount it.

You may even be able to just open the disk, select all, then copy and paste into a folder of your choosing.

Office 2010 does not work too great in wine though.

I would recommend 2007.  Thats what I ended up using.

Has some minor glitches here and there, but barely noticeable.
