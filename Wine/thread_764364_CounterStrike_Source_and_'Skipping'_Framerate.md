### Post by Brynster on 2008-04-24
I would suggest its down to the directx emulation you are running.

Try adding -dxlevel8.1 to the launch properties for the game in steam.

(open steam and right click the the game and a box will open one of the options is a launch option)

if that doesn't help try also 
-directx 8.0, 7.0 until one runs smoothly.
