### Post by cogadh on 2008-11-03
Go with Wine, or if you feel you have to pay for something, go with CrossOver Games. Stay away from Cedega. They charge a monthly fee, have absolutely terrible customer support, do not contribute back to the Wine project (which is the source for Cedega's functionality) and are steadily falling behind Wine and CrossOver in ability.

BTW - Someone mentioned Bordeaux, that is not actually another product, it is just a GUI font end for Wine, like PlayOnLinux and WineDoors.
