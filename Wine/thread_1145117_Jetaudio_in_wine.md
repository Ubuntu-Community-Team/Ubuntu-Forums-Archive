### Post by nucleuskore on 2009-05-01
Downloaded and installed JAD basic

On first run, you are asked to select the mode of operation/display. After selection and clicking ok nothing happens. You have to double click on the desktop icon again. If you get a clutter of windows, note that the media library windows does not move. This is because you are prompted that a windows media codec needs to be downloaded, Yes/No. I went ahead with it, and I got some error messages which I clicked ok. the program did not crash and the codec downloaded and installed successfully. I then closed jetaudio from the toolbar and had to force quit. Double clicked on the desktop icon again to open it :)
It opened a little slowly, but without a hitch. Loaded files into the library. Clicked on track 2 and it started to play after a brief 3 second freeze. The sound quality is fantastic as ever.

Tested:
Player
Rip
Convert
Burn

Not tested:
Broadcast
Get music
Record
Timer/Alarm
