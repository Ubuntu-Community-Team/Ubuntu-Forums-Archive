[http://www.filesweb.com/easy_unit_converter.html](http://www.filesweb.com/easy_unit_converter.html)

I needed a basic units converter and while Linux offers many, the apps often need a great many dependencies, sometimes 200 mbs worth.

If you're running Wine, Easy Unit Converter v1.21 is only 500K and does the job.  The author has no homepage and the download site struck me as a little sketchy, but no viruses were detected, and I've been using it for a couple of days without problems.

FWIW,
