### Post by elpichi on 2008-02-28
I'm using wine to play wow, windowed, the problem with this is that its really laggy, so when i try to play it full screen, the taskbars, appear on top of the game. ---

### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-02-28
open up winecfg and go to the Graphics tab.  Uncheck 'Allow the window manager to control the windows'.  That should get rid of the panels.
