### Post by Toz on 2012-05-20
From [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=2711]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=2711"):

> Problem:  The installer can't find PidGen.DLL.
Solution:  Copy PidGen.DLL from the installation CD and place it in C:\WINDOWS\SYSTEM32.
