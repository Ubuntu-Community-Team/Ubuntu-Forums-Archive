### Post by Jave_ on 2010-11-14
I just had a problem with the wine configuration window being too big,
as a result of my putting the slider in the Graphics tab for the txt size all the way to the right..
 (I couldn't slide it back, it was too far below what I could see.)
I didn't find any thing on the forum about this, 
but managed to fix the problem any way...

I was still able to get into the "Graphics" tab, 
so I changed the settings to something huge... like 2000X2000...
I made sure the "emulate a virtual desktop" button was checked,& hit enter.
This closed the window...
I opened it again, then I used alt/F7 to move the window, 
and slide the screen resolution back to the left making the txt smaller...
I can't say this will work for any one else, or if it is safe, 
but I'm on a crap comp, and it worked...
It's only a small tid-bit of help, 
but if it gets some one out of a jam..
Then it was worth posting :)

I'm a total beginner, 
so if your going to try this; 
Please wait until there are other comments here about safety before trying it...
If anyone experienced thinks this might be dangerous,or not please say so...
