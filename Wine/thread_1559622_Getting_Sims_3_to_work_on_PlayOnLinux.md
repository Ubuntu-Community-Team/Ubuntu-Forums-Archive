### Post by kw2010 on 2010-08-28
Looking at Wine's AppDB, people there report that the Sims 3's copy protection doesn't work properly in Wine, and that is why it doesn't see the cds. Many posts there mention using game mods that disable the cd check or no-cd cracks. The forum rules here don't allow those, so you're on your own with that.
