### Post by ukbeast on 2014-02-04
there are two things to try. 
1 for full-screen - Open "wine configure", Under "Graphics"  untick "Allow the window manager to control the windows"
2 for Windowed find out the resolution of the game in settings, then tick "Emulate a virtual desktop"
