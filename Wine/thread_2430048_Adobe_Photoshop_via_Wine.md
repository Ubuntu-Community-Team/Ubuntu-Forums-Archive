### Post by zimbuf on 2019-10-27
Once upon a time, Google was paying engineers to prop Photoshop up to Platinum status in WINE.

It looks like you may have luck running it via [WINE]("https://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=17") but over all it looks pretty hit or miss depending on version (CS and Elements seem fine, CC looks like a coin flip).

Over all, you're better off using GIMP or Krita. Krita is actually very similar to Photoshop in functionality and form, while GIMP is similar in functionality. 

If anything, using either of the OSS alternatives sticks it to Adobe for charging digital rent, but you could give Photoshop (depending on the version) a go on WINE and see how far you get.
