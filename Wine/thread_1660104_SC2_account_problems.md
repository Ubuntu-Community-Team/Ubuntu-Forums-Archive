### Post by Psyael on 2011-01-05
Since SC2 is a newer game, I'm going to throw down a general suggestion for the newest build of PlayOnLinux (from their web site, not the Ubuntu repositories) since it has an SC2 installer script. Furthering that, you might also want to try the development build of Wine from winehq.org (again, replacing the stable you installed from Ubuntu) since most of it's newest fixes are game related. I know that Left 4 Dead 2, for instance, pretty much requires the Dev version.


I don't play SC2 or have a battlenet account (at least not yet), but you might want to check their web site and make sure your CD key wasn't nabbed. I know that for WoW there is a minefield of ways to accidentally get your account jacked.
