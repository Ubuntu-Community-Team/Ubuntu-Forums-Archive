### Post by sk8ordie97 on 2008-11-03
its called a no-cd. its basically a launcher for your game that bypasses the cdrom. you can download them [here]("http://gamecopyworld.com")
and then put them into the game folder. it should be under /home/username/.wine/drive_c
.wine is usually hidden so press view at the top of the file browser and select show hidden files
