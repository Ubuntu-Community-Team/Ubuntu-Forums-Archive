### Post by aoanla on 2008-04-01
Assuming that you installed Wine with default settings, open a terminal and type the following:

cd .wine/drive_c/Program\ Files/Steam     (I think that's the location of Steam... you may have to poke around for it)
wine steam.exe

and then launch Team Fortress 2 from the Steam browser.
