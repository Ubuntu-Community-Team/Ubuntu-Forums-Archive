### Post by DanglingPointer on 2013-11-25
I was trying to install it to a RAID5 mounted to /media/ because my whole system was running off a USB stick and there wasn't enough space.

I bought a sata card and installed an SSD and moved my entire system from the USB to the SSD.
After which I then installed DeadSpace by just running the installer and it worked!  Only thing I had to do was turn off decals.  Also it appears that AA doesn't work even if it is enabled in the settings.  Other than that the games works flawlessly so far on 1080p.

I must say that for an old game it is beautifully made and rendered and can stand toe to toe with modern DX11 games now.
