### Post by Naegling23 on 2008-07-03
alright, nevermind, I finally fixed it.  Why is it that you can never solve your problems until after you post for help...oh well.

So, I noticed that there was warcraft3.exe and war3.exe after the patch.  Using war3.exe gave me a memory error.  Removing the movies from the movies folder made the game launch...problem solved!
