### Post by aoanla on 2008-08-14
Which app is it? Older games (in some cases, surprisingly recent "older games" - some from 2005, for example) often don't properly understand widescreen resolutions, and will either ignore them (and do something in 4:3 or 5:4 that approximates what you want), or reject the resolution entirely.

But, we need to know what the games are, first, to see if this is the case...
