### Post by Moustacha on 2007-04-30
CoD2 runs, granted you're using 0.9.35 or 0.9.36. You need to do some registry entries, the UseGLSL and OffscreenRenderingMode. It only runs in DX7 mode atm, and it crashes when you get shell shock. 0.9.34(or it's 0.9.33) with Mirek's patch doesn't encounter this issue.
Single player doesn't work either, with original exe or no-cd cracked (safedisc and sound error, respectively). From reports punk buster doesn't work either.
