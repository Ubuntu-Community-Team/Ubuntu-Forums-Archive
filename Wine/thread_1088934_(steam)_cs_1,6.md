### Post by Technophobia on 2009-03-06
First there is sadly no native Steam Client for Linux. I have no idea why.

1) Go to Applications > Add/Remove Applications

2) Change the Show drop down menu to "All available applications" and then Search for "Wine"

3) Click the check box next to "Wine Windows Emulator" and then hit the "Apply Changes" button in the bottom right corner.

4) Next download the Steam Installer [http://storefront.steampowered.com/download/SteamInstall.msi](http://storefront.steampowered.com/download/SteamInstall.msi)

5) I believe you just have to double click the installer and it will start the install.

You can check the How To on this page if double clicking doesn't work. [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=1554](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=1554)
