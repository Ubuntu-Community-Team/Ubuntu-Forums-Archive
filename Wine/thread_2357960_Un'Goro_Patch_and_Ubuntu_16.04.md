### Post by jonnyjungle on 2017-04-13
okay i got a good answer now:
after the ungoro patch of hs you need to switch the version used by playonlinux to 1.9.18 (tested) or highter, you can do this by
-Install the version(s) you want playonlinux to use: Tools > Manage Wine versions
-Change the version used for a virtual drive: Configure button > (select virtual drive) > General tab > Wine version
hoping that helps someone :)
