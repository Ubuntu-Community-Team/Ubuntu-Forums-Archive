### Post by jacksaff on 2009-04-23
Could be loads of reasons - crap video driver, weak card, fact that wine is NOT windows and in some cases doesn't do a perfect job. 

You might need to specify a direct x version for wine to emulate if the game is using calls for a late version of dx that wine doesn't deal with well yet. Often games in wine are a little slower than on the same computer running windows and if the game is borderline for your video card this is enough to stop it working under wine. 

In your case you say your games are running but slow and your video card is not good enough for most modern 3d games so I'll guess that's your problem.

What games are you trying to run?
