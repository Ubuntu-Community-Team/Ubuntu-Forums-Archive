### Post by Felson on 2008-07-15
Not that I ever expect to see this, but I would love to see a "Scale Mode" display option. So, You can have a wine desktop set to 640x480, and scale it up to 1280x1024 for example. Purpose would be for older games/apps that run at a size smaller then 1280x1024. Instead of going full screen, you could leave them windowed, and maximize them to fit your screen. Then all your other workspaces are available, and there is no fear of a crash screwing with your display. Dosbox does this, but then it is actually an emulator, and has a good deal less to do then Wine.
