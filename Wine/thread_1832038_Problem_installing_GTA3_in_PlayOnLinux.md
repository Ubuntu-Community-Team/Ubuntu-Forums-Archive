### Post by Rytron on 2011-09-02
I installed GTA3 on POL yesterday with little problems.

At game launch after the screen goes funny, hit 'Enter' twice. To prevent the game exit changing my normal desktop resolution, I selected 1360*768*16 instead of 1360*768*32 in the game display options. So you may want to change the 32 to 16 for your resolution (although it may be something to do my my video card or driver).
