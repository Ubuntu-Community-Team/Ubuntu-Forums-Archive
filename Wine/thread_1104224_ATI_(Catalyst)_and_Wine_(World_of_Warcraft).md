Well I have that card and have got it MOSTLY working(though took some doing). What issues I have are:

-sounds are sporadic(shouldn't be ATI related though ;-). Either Wine or sound card issue more like it)
-Item pictures(and buffs/debuffs) have funny habit of changing randomly. Ie image for copper coins is chainmail tunic etc. No biggie but does mean you have to check NAME of item when you are selling to avoid selling wrong stuff.
-Big one: Dungeons, cities, houses. Everywhere small map top of corner goes white. Bang! I can see quests etc but rest is white. No problem in city, can get annoying in cramped tunnels.
-Need to switch off death glow. Without that it goes haywire. Just a minor issue and of course hopefully you'll be spending very little time as dead so you don't have to see this issue anyway ;-)

Apart from those issues it works just fine for me. Not sure how FPS is affected. I have bit of a overkill machine so it works just fine.
