### Post by Asraniel on 2008-09-29
I think you will know as soon as you install it.

Installing half life with our without steam should be easy, and should work with no major problems. You would probably want to use steam, because the steam version of half life is quite well tested in wine, but the old half life version, has perhaps regressions that nobody noticed (that's just a guess).

Smooth as butter, thanks for the vote of confidence.  I followed install instructions from [http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=1554](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=1554)
