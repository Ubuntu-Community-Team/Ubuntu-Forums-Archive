But with Linux I have no lagg at all, not even once, not even in crowded areas.

### Post by hikaricore on 2009-12-01
Turnoff key repeat.  I think it lives in System/Preferences/Keyboard but I'm not using gnome so I can't be certain.

Aside from that numlock (autorun) is always an option.
Personally I need keyrepeat on so I just work around it in such cases.
