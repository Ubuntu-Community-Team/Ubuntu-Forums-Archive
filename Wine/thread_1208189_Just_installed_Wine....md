### Post by LinuxGamer on 2009-07-09
The easiest way to find out is to [go here]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse%20Applications&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true"), and set the filters to Category: Games/MMORPG and Platinum Rating. Should give you a good idea of what you can expect to work well.
