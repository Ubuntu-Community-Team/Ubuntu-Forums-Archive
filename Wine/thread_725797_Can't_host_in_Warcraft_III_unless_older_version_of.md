### Post by FrozenFox on 2008-04-14
I don't have a fix for your problem, but I do have a workaround.

Download Playonlinux and install the older version of wine in it. You can run wc3 with it on an older version of wine while keeping your system's primary wine up to date.

[http://www.playonlinux.com/en/](http://www.playonlinux.com/en/)
