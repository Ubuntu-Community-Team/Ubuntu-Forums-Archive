### Post by cogadh on 2008-06-30
What software is it? Some apps that run perfectly in Wine (i.e. apps that have a Platinum rating on Wine's [AppDB](http://appdb.winehq.org/)) can be completely trusted to work as they do in Windows. Other apps that don't actually have 100% complete functionality (i.e. gold or lower rated) really should not be trusted for mission critical purposes.

### Post by cogadh on 2008-07-03
As long as you keep the data files in a directory that your user has read/write permissions to and is accessable in Wine (like your home folder), then read/writes should be fine. As for the security of the data, well that depends on how secure you keep your system, not on how Wine works.
