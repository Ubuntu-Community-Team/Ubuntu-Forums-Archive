### Post by cogadh on 2009-01-12
If they are actually DOS games, then [DOSBOX]("http://www.dosbox.com/") should work fine and is probably your best option, but you may need to spend some time configuring it (and learning how to use it). If they are games based on the old SCUMM engine, then you can also try [ScummVM]("http://www.scummvm.org/"). If they are the Win 95 or later versions of the games, then try [Wine]("http://www.winehq.org/").

BTW - Your link to the Secret Agent Wikipedia article is missing the last ")".
