### Post by MaxIBoy on 2008-10-18
Find the game, see what it's rated. Platinum games run perfectly, gold games run very well, silver games have minor problems, bronze games have major problems, "garbage" games don't run at all. Also, take a look at the reviews, they might have information on what they needed to do to get the game running.
