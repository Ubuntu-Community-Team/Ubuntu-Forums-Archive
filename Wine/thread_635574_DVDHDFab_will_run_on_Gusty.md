### Post by caratuco on 2007-12-08
I just installed gusty 7.10 and wine from the repositories (.9.46-not the newest) and found that DVDFAB 4.0.1.2 will work if installed under NT 4.0 the old way by placing the .exe in the win32 folder.
Also, the video_ts of the dvd must be open in Dolphin or DVDFAb will not recognize it. Insert the DVD first and open it in dolphin, then open DVDFAb.

For "Superbad" DVDFAB took 10:23 mins(for the main movie only)
DVDShrink took 3:15
K3b burned in 6:26

This was with the free version of DVDFab. 
This new system is much faster than 7.04!
