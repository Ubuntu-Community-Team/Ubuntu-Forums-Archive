### Post by karth on 2007-11-28
As far as I know, you should run your program in a terminal and check for any possible errors (fixme messages usually don't matter for the wine user) while attempting to contact your server.

appdb.winehq.org doesn't output anything for your app, this means that the user is on his or her own. At least, he or she could contribute by filing a review, even if it's only to tell it runs at "Garbage" level (meaning that it's not usable)
