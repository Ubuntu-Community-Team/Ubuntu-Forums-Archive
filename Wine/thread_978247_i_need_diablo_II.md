### Post by Durden on 2008-11-10
Hmm, well. I'm bad with simplifying stuff but I'll give it a quick shot.

Open a terminal and type

sudo apt-get install wine

Once that is done, put your D2 CD in the drive, navigate to the setup.exe (might also be install.exe) And then double click on it like you would in windows.

If this fails for any reason, post here with details as to error messages or anything else relevent.
