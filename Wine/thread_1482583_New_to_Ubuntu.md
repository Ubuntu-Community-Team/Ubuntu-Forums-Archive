### Post by Zintha on 2010-05-14
If your framerates on XP was 100, on Ubuntu it'll be about 80.
50 = 35 etc.

There will be a small decrease in FPS and occasionally an addon (x-perl for example) will be a -little- mis aligned.

My advice:

Install WoW on your XP partition (and duel boot, obviously) and get it all 100% working.
Go to Ubuntu on the other side of your HD and access it through the partition and run your windows installed WoW through wine.

It was 10x more stable than my Wine installed version.
