### Post by cogadh on 2008-12-23
The best gui version of Wine is [CrossOver]("http://www.codeweavers.com/"), but there is also [Bordeaux]("http://bordeauxgroup.com/"), [PlayOnLinux]("http://www.playonlinux.com/en/"), [Q4Wine]("http://api.qt-apps.org/content/show.php/q4wine?content=85751"), [WineBot]("http://winebot.sandbox.cz/tracker") and [WineXS]("http://tsx.nl/index.php?p=winexs"). I'm sure there are others I've forgotten as well.
