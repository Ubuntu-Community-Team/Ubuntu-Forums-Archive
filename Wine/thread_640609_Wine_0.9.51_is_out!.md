What's new in this release:
  - A bunch of WinHelp improvements.
  - Better Japanese font support.
  - A ton of rpcrt4 fixes.
  - Several Alsa capture fixes.
  - **Improved support for screen resolution changes.**
  - Lots of bug fixes.

I think a lot of people are looking for that fix. No DEB package yet, but we should have it in a couple of days.
