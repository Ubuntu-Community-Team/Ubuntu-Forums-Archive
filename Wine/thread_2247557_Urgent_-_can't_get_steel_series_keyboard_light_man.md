### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-10-15
Sorry, but like with Logitech keyboards, the need for specialized hardware drivers relegates the hardware use in Linux to the most basic functions provided by common Linux drivers.

The company that makes your keyboard provides no mention of Linux drivers, so all you're going to get in Linux is the most basic keyboard functions.  You're fortunate it even works at all!
