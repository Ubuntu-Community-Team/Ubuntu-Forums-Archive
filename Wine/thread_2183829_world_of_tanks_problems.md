### Post by Val87 on 2013-11-28
There is a simple solution, this error comes up while downloading the updates. To avoid it, open the World of Tanks launcher, and top right corner there will be a little key icon. Click it and untick the use torrent icon. Download should then proceed with no errors. Could take a little more time as torrent will be disabled, but worked fine for me.
