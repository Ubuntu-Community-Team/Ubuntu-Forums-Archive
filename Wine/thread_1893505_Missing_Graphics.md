### Post by Tweak42 on 2011-12-13
Could try setting wine vertex shader support to none.  

3D support for intel linux drivers is pretty buggy compared to windows side of things.  All the reports of Stalker working under wine were probably using Nvidia or ATI video chips.

You can try searching through this forum for tips from other intel users that managed to get other 3d games working and apply those tips to yours in hopes it will also work.  Be prepared for an uphill battle though as the vast majority of responses when intel video is mentioned is to upgrade.
