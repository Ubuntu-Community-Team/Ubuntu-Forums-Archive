### Post by amauk on 2011-06-10
1. Subscribe to the wine development mailing list
[http://www.winehq.org/mailman/listinfo/wine-devel](http://www.winehq.org/mailman/listinfo/wine-devel)

2. Track the wine git tree
git://source.winehq.org/git/wine.git

3a. Find something that needs fixing
Fix it, and post patches to the devel mailing list

3b. Come up with a new feature / idea for wine
Announce it on the list, and get feedback on it's suitability, interest and/or acceptance within the wine project
Develop your idea, and let the core wine devs know where to pull your tree
