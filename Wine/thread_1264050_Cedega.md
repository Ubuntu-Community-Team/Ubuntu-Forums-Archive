### Post by beastrace91 on 2009-09-18
Minus Links 2003, Madden 08, and Tiger Woods 08 Most all of those games run very solidly under Cedega. If you have the hard drive space to spare for now I would recommend leaving the dual boot until you get all your games setup and working how you want under Ubuntu/Cedega (sometimes it can take some tweaking) and if you get them working well enough for your needs then dump the Windows partition.
