### Post by bcarlowise on 2013-05-09
As a photography buff I have been using Adobe Photoshop Elements for about 8 years. I have a big investment in many plugins for Photoshop, not to mention being comfortable with the workflow. I recently discovered that I can run Photoshop Elements 10 under Wine. Up to now I've been running a Windows 7 VM in VirtualBox but it runs much faster under wine than in the Windows 7 VM. Basically, I just have Wine and Winetricks installed. I then installed the following Windows DLL/Components from winetricks:

atmlib
mfc42
msxml4
vcrun6
vcrun6sp6

irFanview also works with the above combination.

I will be installing and testing Photoshop Elements 11 and Premier Elements 11 shortly and will update with how they work.

Although I have and have used Corel Aftershot Pro which runs natively in Linux, I find I prefer the capabilities and workflow of Photoshop Elements so getting it to work in Wine is huge for me.
