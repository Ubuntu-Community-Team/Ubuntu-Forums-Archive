### Post by BigSilly on 2009-03-21
Game turned up yesterday and I installed it straight away, so I've had a day + on the game. Bit disappointed to find the performance no different to the demo. There are still a couple of niggles with the frame rate here and there, but overall though the game is pretty stunning. Both patches installed just fine (you need both) but seemingly had little or no impact on the overall performance of the game. From the sounds of things, it's more of a FIFA and EA thing rather than a Linux and WINE issue. Many Windows users with similar specs to me have complained of chugging frame rates every so often.

Overall though it works well with WINE. Had to apply the controller fix, that some Linux users have wrongly attributed to WINE on their forums. That too is an EA bug that needs a little user intervention to fix. As a game it's well worth the tenner or so I paid, and if you're an Nvidia/WINE gamer looking for a footie game, you should be okay with this.

Pity PES doesn't work with WINE, but FIFA will do nicely!
