### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-01-16
The overall Wine experience varies greatly by individual Windows app and even the version of that app.  For example, quite a bit of Office 2007 works quite well, whereas Office 2010 hardly works at all.

I personally tried out Wine for several months and quite using it.  Not only did it NOT work on most of the apps I tried, I saw no reason in having to use three-year-old versions just to use Wine.

But, experience varies greatly -- and has some have said, it's not a replacement for MS Windows.
