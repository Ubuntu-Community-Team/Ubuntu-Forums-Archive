### Post by noasad on 2013-07-01
san andreas relies on Directx so get winetricks [http://wiki.winehq.org/winetricks](http://wiki.winehq.org/winetricks)  and when you get winetricks get MSCORE fonts and Directx 9 and after that go to configure wine and put on Emulate virtual desktop and set your resolution and its optional tick the Capture mouse in fullscreen windows then press apply and it should work if it doesnt PM me.
