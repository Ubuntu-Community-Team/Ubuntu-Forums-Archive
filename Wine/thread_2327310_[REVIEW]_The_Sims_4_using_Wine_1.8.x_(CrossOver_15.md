### Post by brian62 on 2016-06-09
So EA released a big patch and a new game pack for the Sims 4 and I thought it'd be a good time to explore how well the Sims 4 runs on Linux. I've done a couple videos on the Sims 4 in the past but I've never done a video where we focus on how the game runs on Linux through Wine or another compatibility layer.

In this video we take a look at how the Sims 4 runs on ElementaryOS (Ubuntu 14.04) Linux using CrossOver Linux 15.0.1 (which uses Wine 1.8).

Here are my system specs:

CPU: AMD A10 7850k
GPU: AMD Radeon R7 360
SSD: Samsung 850 EVO
RAM: Crucial Ballistix Sport XT

Compared to the first Sims 4 gameplay video I did a year ago, the performance improvements are astounding. I recorded this video using the "Very High" graphics settings and got an average of about 30fps.
