### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-07-18
The link below is to the WineHQ page for iTunes:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=1347]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=1347")

As you can see, most of the ratings are either Garbage (won't work, no matter what), or Bronze (barely works, some functions don't).

You can read through the page, looking for the version of iTunes you're using, and if it's there, read through the testing results on the right side of the page.

But in general, iTunes does not work well with Wine.
