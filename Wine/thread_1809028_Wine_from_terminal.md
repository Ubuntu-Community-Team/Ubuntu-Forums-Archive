### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-07-21
There should be a menu entry for all your wine applications, though I can't remember exactly where it is, and I don't have wine on my system any more.

You can copy the command from the launchers there, into your own launcher on the desktop.  I know that will work, as it is what I used to do back when I used wine.  If that is not successful for some reason, try the full pathway in the command rather than the ~/ shortcut for /home/user.
