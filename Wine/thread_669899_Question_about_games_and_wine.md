### Post by hikaricore on 2008-01-16
**Sorry directx installation isn't really beneficial in most situations, and can actually make things worse in others.**

_I advise highly **AGAINST** it unless your application has a specific need for it._
IE: World of Warcraft can make use for the default "directx" libraries that come /w WINE.
WINE actually converts all DirectX functions to OpenGL on the fly from my best understanding.

There is absolutely no way to actually use DirectX in Linux.
