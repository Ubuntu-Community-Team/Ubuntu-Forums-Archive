### Post by aoanla on 2008-09-21
It's not identifying Linux - it's worse, it's identifying "Not Internet Explorer". Wow - I didn't think any web developers were left who were stupid enough to reject on that anymore...

You can get around this (I've just tested this) by using Opera to browse to it, then using Tools->Quick Preferences->Edit Site Preferences
and choosing (under "Network") to "Masquerade as Internet Explorer" as your Browser Identification.

Stupid web developers. Feel free to post a support call about it to them.
