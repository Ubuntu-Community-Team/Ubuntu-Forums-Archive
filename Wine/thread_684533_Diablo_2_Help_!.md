It's been a while, but IIRC the expansion did *not* add any new abilities for the existing character types.  What it did was add two new characters (the Druid and the Assassin), add one additional area (the barbarian highlands), added a lot of new items/weaponry, and of course some new monsters in the new area.

To get to the new area, take your character back to Hell, and talk to Tyrael.  He should provide a portal for you (if you've converted your character, that is).
