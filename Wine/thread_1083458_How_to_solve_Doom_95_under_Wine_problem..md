### Post by yoobuntu on 2009-03-01
When I installed Doom 95 shareware version on my Xubuntu laptop, I was aghast at the fact that the graphics were screwed up. Now, on my Ubuntu desktop, I have discovered the problem. When you start up Doom 95 and get the screen with the game variables to be configured by the user, click "Advanced". Now then, click the "Screen Resolution" tab. Select 640x400 or 640x480 ONLY! If you select the 320x240 or 320x200, the graphics will be very messed up. Upon selecting one of those resolutions, click "OK" and get back to setting up your game.
