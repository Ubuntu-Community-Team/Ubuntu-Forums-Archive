### Post by j_baer on 2008-10-29
The Human Synergy theme is designed for Ubuntu Intrepid or Hardy. Forged to be contemporary in appearance with rounded scrollbars and neutral colors the goal of the theme is to display all "default" applications as designed. Staying with the tradition of Ubuntu many of the widgets use orange as the primary color and the icons are Human. Go to the wiki to view over 30 screen shots and download.

[http://wiki.iffii.com/index.cgi/SynergyGtkTheme](http://wiki.iffii.com/index.cgi/SynergyGtkTheme)

Cheers!
