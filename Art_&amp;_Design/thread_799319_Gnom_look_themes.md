### Post by ryanhaigh on 2008-05-18
ubuntu...> has themes for many parts of the OS: boot splashes, emerald themes, desktop backgrounds as well as many others.

If I am not mistaken the gtk (gtk 2 to answer your original question) theme will change the widgets used in the windows, like the buttons, scrollbars etc. Metacity themes will change the window borders.

Generally all you do is download the theme, open system>preferences>appearance and drag the downloaded theme into that window to install.
