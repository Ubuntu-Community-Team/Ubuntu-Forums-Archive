### Post by snickers295 on 2007-10-22
go into System>Preferences>Appearance  and tinker with that stuff until you make something you like then click the save button. to package it to upload it somewhere go to /home/(Your Username)/.themes and find the folder with the name of the theme you made and right click on it and copy then paste it somewhere and right click on it and click "Create Archive..."
then upload it somewhere so other people can use it.
(hint:go to [http://gnome-look.org ]("http://gnome-look.org")and make a id there and start uploading.)

### Post by Genius314 on 2008-06-17
> **Aeph said:**
> In the second screenshot, the terminal window is translucent. How do you do this?

Go to the terminal. Click on Edit>Current Profile. In the "Effects" tab, check the "Transparent" option and turn down the slider. It looks better if you have compositing (such as Compiz)
