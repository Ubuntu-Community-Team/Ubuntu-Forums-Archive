### Post by ipatfrmww on 2011-03-06
My guess is that the calendar and weather thing are conky. The clock probably is too.
I'm not an expert on conky, but I rknow a little bit.
If you are interested in this sort of thing you should search around for conky. There are quite a few tutorial threads on ubuntu forums if you search for them. There is also a list of basic commands on sourceforge[:  http://conky.sourceforge.net/variables.html]("http://conky.sourceforge.net/variables.html").
You can do quite allot with that.
I find that you need to install some more packages for some of the HDD temp's to work and things like that. (depends on your system)
I suggest finding somebody elses script and having a look at how they work before trying to make your own one. They can get quite complicated.
