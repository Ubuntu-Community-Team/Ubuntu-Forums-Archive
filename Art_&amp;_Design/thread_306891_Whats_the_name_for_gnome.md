### Post by tom56 on 2006-11-27
The best way to explain this is to open up System->Preferences->Themes. Ok, now go to "Theme Details". The "Controls" tab show you the gtk themes, "Window" shows metacity. Messing around a bit shows you what gtk themes and metacity themes change respectively. Both "bits" of theme can be found at gnome-look.org.
