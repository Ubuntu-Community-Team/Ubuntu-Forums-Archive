### Post by Nikola on 2005-03-02
This is pack which contains colour mod of Lila icon theme, plus matching wallpapers, gdm theme, gnome splash and gtk2 theme.
Now, there is one small problem, and that is that on Ubuntu icons get scaled realy small. I don't know why, or if it is Ubuntu related or not, but bvc sent me fix:
> 
In order for the icon theme to work as others, I
modified my own index.theme file, not yours in the
download, and added
DesktopDefault=48
and changed all
Size=128
to
Size=48


So aply that to index.theme in this archive.[]("http://bvc.kernow-webhosting.com/theme/Nikola/")

EDIT:
You can download here.
[http://download.ubuntuforums.org/art/lila-brown/lila-brown.tar.bz2](http://download.ubuntuforums.org/art/lila-brown/lila-brown.tar.bz2)
