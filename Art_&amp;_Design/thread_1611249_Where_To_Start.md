### Post by Paul820 on 2010-11-03
Start by doing some wallpapers, move onto icons and then onto themes. There are a ton of resources on the web on how to do the themes and icon packs. When you have finished what you have done, post them on [http://gnome-look.org/]("http://gnome-look.org/") to share your work.

If that is not what you are after, then you need to be a bit more specific in what you want to do.

You can always ask on here for help if you need it. :)
