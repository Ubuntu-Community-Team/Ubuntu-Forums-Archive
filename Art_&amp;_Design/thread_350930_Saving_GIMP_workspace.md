### Post by maruchan on 2007-02-01
Have you asked specifically how to do that? I'm sure somebody would know (just not me).

The closest I can get: Add a Document History tab to your toolbox, then detach it. That way you have a window with thumbnails of your recent documents.  You can enlarge the thumbnails or display them in a grid. Pretty handy, even if it's not exactly what you want. :)
