### Post by Fzang on 2009-08-11
Most artists draw raw sketches of their art in vectors first so they can freely alter the shapes. Then they transfer the image to raster like Gimp or PS and edit the smaller details. Raster can create image distorts like noise and blur to make the image look more realistic.
