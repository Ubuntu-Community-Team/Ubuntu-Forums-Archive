### Post by Genius314 on 2008-11-29
Go to Image>More Effects, and play around with Gamma and Contrast. I think you have to turn Gamma down and Contrast up. That should work to some extent, but you might get better results in GIMP (something like that in GIMP is pretty simple. You just have to open the image, and go to Colors>Brightness/Contrast).

Hope that helps! :)
