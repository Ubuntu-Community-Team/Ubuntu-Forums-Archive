The main thing that I think defines the theme is the wireframe, so as long as I get that, I'm happy.

> **acelin said:**
> This is just a pinker wavy ripoff of Kith, which in itsled is nothing more than a differen, awful colored theme with Windows Vista min/max/close buttons.

Kin was originally going to be a second version of "Kith", but I decided to name it Kin because it follows a more flashy philosophy (and the names imply they are together), as opposed to Kiths more minimal approach. I want to continue on both themes separately. They might grow further apart, but at this point they still closely resemble one-another by design.

As per the Vista-ish buttons, there's not too much that really makes them look like Vista anymore. The min/max icons are different, they don't touch the top of the window, they do glow but it's an "underglow" and the buttons are not all connected. The X is about the only thing they have in common, but what theme doesn't use an X?
