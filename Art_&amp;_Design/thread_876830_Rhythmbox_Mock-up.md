### Post by tretle on 2008-08-03
[http://bp1.blogger.com/_NncjCndy0C4/SJUKwcHpu0I/AAAAAAAAAX4/YBwWp0iAMs4/s1600-h/Mockup+9.png](http://bp1.blogger.com/_NncjCndy0C4/SJUKwcHpu0I/AAAAAAAAAX4/YBwWp0iAMs4/s1600-h/Mockup+9.png)

Thats one of my mockups I have been creating for the developers. My advice to you if your interested in gnome projects is to send on the mockups to the mailing list.
Try and be more gnome compliant and realistic when creating a mockup for a project. 
If you are interested in usability creating mockups for developers can be a huge benefit as they get to test out how UI's would work and often quench arguments on mailing lists without having to waste time developing something that might not work.
For instance in the after finishing the mockup above I came to the conclusion that the slider for the volume wouldnt be a good change but the drop box for views might be a good idea when macslows sparkle is introduced. 
One of the developers also liked the cell spacing over the album art in the mockup and has integrated it into svn.
