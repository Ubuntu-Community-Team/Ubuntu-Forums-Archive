### Post by Bruce M. on 2007-11-24
I got to thinking.  Why not Flame something other than text?

So I made:
Filter>Render>Nature>Flame>Linear
And then made flames out of them.  That took a while   :)

Added a layer and another Filter>Render>Nature>Flame>Spherical
I added the same colouring to that as the Flames:

      Shadows
      ------------
      +100
      +30
      -30

      Midtones
      ------------
      +100
      +30
      -30

      Highlights
      ------------
      +100
      0
      -100

The results are:
