### Post by alex.rayu on 2009-03-27
Such images are made in vector editors. Like inkscape. Vector editors draw with shapes and geometric figures (roughly speaking). So, lights and shades are geometric figures. Lights are lighter figures, shades are darker figures. They are placed on top of image and it looks like shades or lights. Can't explain simpler - there are tons of tutorials. If you want to learn how to draw, you will find lots of resources to guide you.
