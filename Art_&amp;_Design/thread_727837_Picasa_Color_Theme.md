### Post by lammoxian on 2008-07-25
This was posted some time ago, but I'll tell you what I did to change this.

Rename the .picasa folder (in your home directory) to .wine, you will have to rename your .wine folder to something else in the mean time.

Run winecfg, assuming you have wine installed and change the appearance settings to your liking under the "Desktop Integration" tab. If you have actual .theme files from windows you can load these here too and skin the default windows looking buttons too. Then rename the folder back to .picasa and it should look a little better.

Remember to rename your original .wine folder back to .wine.
