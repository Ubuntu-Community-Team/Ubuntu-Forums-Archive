### Post by theraje on 2010-11-18
If you decide to go Wacom, see if you can't find an Intuos3 on ebay. The newer Intuos4 tablets have "paper-like" textured surfaces that literally *eat* your nibs away.

The models prior to that have smooth surfaces, and are much easier on your nibs. Plus, there's no texture to get worn away.

The Intuos4 models are nice, but if it weren't for the textured surface sheets, it would be nigh on perfect.
