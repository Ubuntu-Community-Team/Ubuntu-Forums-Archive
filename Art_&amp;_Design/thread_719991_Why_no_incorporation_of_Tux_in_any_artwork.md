### Post by geoken on 2008-03-11
As far as logo's go, tux is way to complicated to be integrated into a wide variety of themes. 

I think someone needs to make a really stylized shillouette version of tux that is suitable to be used as a monochrome image and in small sizes. It should also not be cartoonish.  If this happened I think more people might integrate it into their designs. 

A good example would be the Amarok mascot vs. the KDE dragon. The Amarok wolf is used frequently because it's extremely stylized and can fit well with many different looks. Conversely, the KDE dragon is very cartoonish and is rarely used outside of cartoonish themes.
