### Post by Genius314 on 2009-08-29
Try going to Colors>Hue-Saturation. You should be able to change the hue of the colors.
For instance, to change the blue of the sky, make sure you select blue in the color wheel, then slide the Hue slider around until you get a color that you like.

Clouds might be trickier, since they're white, and don't have a hue. You might want to play with the Clolors>Colorize option.

Hope that makes sense.
