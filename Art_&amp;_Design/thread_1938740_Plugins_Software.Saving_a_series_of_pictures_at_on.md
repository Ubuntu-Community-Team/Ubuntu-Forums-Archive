### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-03-10
If all you want to do is resize or rotate the pictures you can do it most easily with a nautilus plugin.  It is called nautilus-image-converter in Ubuntu 10.04, but I think the name has changed in Ubuntu 11.10, so look for a likely named candidate in the software-centre.  You can then right click on one or more images and change their size with a few clicks.
