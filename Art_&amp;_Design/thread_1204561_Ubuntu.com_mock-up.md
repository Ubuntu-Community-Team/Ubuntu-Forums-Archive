### Post by iamthez on 2009-07-05
Ok, so this was just a very rough concept, done for the fun of it, but here we go:

@Technique13: I can indeed recreate the layout with CSS/XHTML. As for the font, you're absolutely right.

@meborc: I did green 'cause I really, really wanted to get away from brown/orange. But it can always be changed. Also, I changed the slogan because most of our potential customers don't give a damn about this thing called "Linux". And why should they? They just wanna check there e-mail already.

@Fzang: The laptop/screenshot were just things I had on hand at the moment that I could use quickly. They can always be changed. That said, AWN simplifies things greatly. It would be great to show a default interface that doesn't leave the user feeling cheated. But not necessarily that one...
