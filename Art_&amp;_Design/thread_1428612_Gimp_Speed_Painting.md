### Post by Minipalmer on 2010-03-13
Thanks!

For this piece I used all different sizes of the standard round and fuzzy Gimp brushes. Most the soft, because I was just doing a lot of skin shading. But for things with harder edges, like the eyes, I would switch to the harder brushes.

I only used two layers for this. The foreground, with the character, and the background. I usually do it this way because I like to do the foreground first.

Edit: I guess I should also add that I use the smudge tool way to much. That's what I used for most of this piece, actually, haha.
