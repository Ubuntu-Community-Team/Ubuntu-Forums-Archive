### Post by Sand &amp; Mercury on 2008-11-23
> **maruchan said:**
> I would start by identifying just who your client is. Once you understand the organization, work at finding a single solution that fits them. Otherwise, the design is simply decorating, and not communicating a message about the organization.
This is good advice; do some homework, maybe even talk to the organization to find out what they're about. Do some brainstorming and come up with the elements that could visually represent their direction. A company logo of theirs is also a good way to get a good motif. A lot of websites will design in a manner that kind of draws from the logo design, whether it be the entire visual style or just a colour palette and/or selection of fonts.
