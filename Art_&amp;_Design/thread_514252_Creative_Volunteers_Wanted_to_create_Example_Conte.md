Want to get involved in helping Ubuntu, but not confident to delve into programming?

If you've got any creative streaks, in any area, take a look at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Examples](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Examples)

Even if you don't feel like making anything today, have a look anyway, because you'll be able to see what others have made, and help select the best entries.
