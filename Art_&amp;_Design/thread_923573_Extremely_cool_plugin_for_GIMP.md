### Post by artir on 2008-09-18
It's called resynthesizer and you can find more about it here:  [http://www.logarithmic.net/pfh/resynthesizer](http://www.logarithmic.net/pfh/resynthesizer)

What's so great about it?
With that plugin you can apply one pic as a texture to another pic, remove parts from an image cloning the background (automatically!) and make tilleable textures.

Click to install:

[apt://gimp-resynthesizer]("apt://gimp-resynthesizer")
