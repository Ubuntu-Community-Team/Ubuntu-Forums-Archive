### Post by lyceum on 2010-04-05
To be realistic, every recording studio program has its good and bad. Once you find something you love, it is hard to find the exact same thing out there. ### Post by kayosiii on 2010-10-05
Because of the nature of Linux -- samples are very rarely bundled with applications as the normal method of distribution is download rather than on physical media, tastes vary widely and usually getting together a high quality library of samples costs quite a bit of money (unless you can get musicians to volunteer).

There is a freepats package in Ubuntu that can be used with a quite a few different programs. There are also some good places to look online for samples.

Lmms off the top of my head is probably the closest to what you are thinking though it is more like fruity loops than Garage Band.
