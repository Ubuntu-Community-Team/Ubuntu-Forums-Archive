### Post by Starlight on 2009-08-08
Thank you! I'm happy that you like it :D

Yes, I made it 99% in Inkscape, and just a final contrast adjusting in Gimp. As for the shadows and highlights, I use the freehand tool to draw lots of small (and sometimes bigger) and very transparent white (or black, for shadows) shapes around the places where the highlights and shadows are supposed to be. :) Inkscape doesn't really like being used that way, though. When I was finishing it, Inkscape kept crashing every few minutes!
