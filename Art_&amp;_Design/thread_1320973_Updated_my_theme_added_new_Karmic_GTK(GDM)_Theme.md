### Post by Exodist on 2009-11-13
Theme has great potential.

Few things I noticed was.
- GtkCombo and GtkComboEntry menu Buttons are larger then the actual Entry box. You may be able to adjust this matching up the xthickness & ythickness of both the entry and button size.
- Second is the sliders, they are very bright and hard to see against the background. Working with their contrast will really help bring them out and make them easier to find for the user.
- Third is the progress bars. Noticed a slight gap between the actual progress bar and the trough they sit in. Optional but you may consider closing the gap just a tad to make them look little cleaner.
- Last I notice the panel buttons [active] you can see the panel bar background gradient through them. You may wish to consider on those taking the translucency out in the center of the active ones to add a touch of depth to give better reference to which is actually active.

Hope this helps :-)

Keep up the good work.

- Exo
