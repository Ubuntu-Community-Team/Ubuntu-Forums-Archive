Thanks everyone for your kind comments! :)

@Dragonbite: Most of them are taken here in Sweden, Falköping where I live. I live beside a very small mountain, photos like the junebug, woods an the ice are taken there. The interior churches and ruined monastery are from around Falköping. The image with the tree and some stones are taken from outside Falköping too.
