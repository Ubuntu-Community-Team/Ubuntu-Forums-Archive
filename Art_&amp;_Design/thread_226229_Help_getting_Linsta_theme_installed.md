### Post by ironfistchamp on 2006-08-01
If you are still interested I got it installed. To use it what I did was download the LiNsta metacity pack. When that is installed (extracted into .themes there should eb four folders) click on your current theme. Then click theme details. From the first list pick LiNsta GTK2, from the second list pick one of the 4 LiNsta metacity themes and finally pick an icon theme. Then click OK or whatever. I then saved it as LiNsta.

Hope it helps.
