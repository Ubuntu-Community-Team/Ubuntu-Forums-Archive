I recently played around with my fonts and ended up using something from the restricted-extras package, I guess. "Comic Sans". While looking for some proper fixed width font I stubled across some pretty interesting 'discussions' about wether or not this is a font to hate or love. And there are actually people who get into it really emotionally (well in the internet posts that is).

Now It is probably true that there are texts one does not use certain fonts for. And it does have this weakness of 0 / O distinction, but I kindof really like it on apps and the desktop. It feels a little less boxy-techy-mechanical.

It's a little like the desktop one uses... or the theme... or the wallpaper... 

A matter of taste.

And please post if you know of a font that is as 'semi-organic' while still readable and maybe lacks the 0O flaw. Thanks.
