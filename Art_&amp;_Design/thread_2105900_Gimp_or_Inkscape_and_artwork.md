### Post by Thee on 2013-01-17
No. You have no obligation to make your artwork free. You can do what ever you like with your creations.

### Post by Warren Hill on 2013-01-17
There are no limitations on Gimp or Inkscape.  What you produce provided its your own original work is yours.

You own the copyright.  You can sell it commercially if you want, or give it away or anything in-between,  Its up to you.

This part of the licence applies to "derivative works" which are other programs that use source from the original software. It doesn't apply to things you produce using the software (otherwise, everything done on Linux would be free...).
