### Post by Montblanc_Kupo on 2009-01-18
I like the concept... 

The things that bug Me:

The ubuntu menu logo in the bubble doesn't seem to fit the theme of the theme... too shiny and bubbly for the subtle theme you have going.

The white window inside is a bit ... stark... maybe toning it down to a tan just a tad to match the theme might be less distracting or abrupt.

The close / minimize / maximize icons are... boring.

Things I like:

Very clean, easy to see what's going on quickly.

I LOVE the little icon at the top of the window that shows what app it is. Slick.. it would be awesome if you could move it to any corner.

The little drop down on the window chooser tabs at the top is cool too. :)

What I'd like to see:

A little more contrast on the sliders so you can see where they are more easily... as well as maybe on the window edges themselves.

A wicked cool icon set to go with it. ;)

Good work.
