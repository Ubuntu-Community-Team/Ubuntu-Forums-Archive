### Post by cookieofdoom on 2007-07-08
YaKuake is a very useful application. You simply hit a user assigned key combination to bring down a terminal window (like in Quake). You can make this terminal whatever size you want and customize it's appearance a bit. It doesn't sit on your desktop, though. It just drops down from the top of your screen. It's the same concept -- having a terminal that's easy to access -- but it's not exactly what you asked for. I rather like it, but I always forget to use it and end up opening a new terminal anyway.

This might be more like what you were looking for, though I never tried to get it working. [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=202249&highlight=terminal+desktop](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=202249&highlight=terminal+desktop)
