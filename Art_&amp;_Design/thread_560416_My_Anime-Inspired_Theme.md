Thanks. It took me a LONG time to find out the right colors and combinations for the theme. As you may have guessed, the winows are colored a purplish-pink to resemble Roger's computer screen as he enters the cockpit. But the Orange-Pink color was the most difficult to attain considering Big O's helmet is glassy. Everything else fell into place.

The final addition I need is a Big O themed login screen saying "Cast in the Name of God, Ye Not Guilty".  Along with the musical piece "Sure Promise" accompanying it.

:)

Plus, the colors compliment each other very well when it comes to typing. The pinkish text contrasts well with the background yet doesn't strain your eyes. I usually don't use pink in themes (I'm a guy, what can I say) but there are combinations where it looks reallly cool.
