### Post by lyceum on 2008-04-28
That is a nice picture. If I may add my 2 cents, open GIMP and use "Color/Hue-Saturation" to increase the saturation level by about 20-30. 20 would be good but anything over 30 may be too much. Then go to "Filters/Enhance/Sharpen" and increase by 25-40. I would not go over 40. This will keep the feel of the picture while enhancing its mood a bit. You can also leave it as it is. I like the colors to jump off the page and grab you just a little more.
