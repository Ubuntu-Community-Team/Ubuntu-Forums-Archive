### Post by digitalis_vulgaris on 2009-02-07
For resizing layer on some size I use this method:
1. Layer > Autocrop Layer // This will shink layer border
2. After this Layer > Scale Layer // Here you can enter desired size

For placing on the right position I use rulers:
1. Open rulers with View > Show Rulers
2. Drag Guides from rulers and place them on desired position. Zoom in and zoom out are handy here...
3. Turn on View > Snap to Guides
4. Move your layer to snap to guides crosssection

Wish you happy gimping ;)
