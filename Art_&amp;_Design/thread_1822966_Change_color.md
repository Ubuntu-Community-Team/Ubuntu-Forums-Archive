### Post by Dry Lips on 2011-08-11
1. Right click with your pointer on the desktop
2. Click &#8220;change desktop background&#8221;
 3. Then click the &#8220;theme&#8221; tab
4. Click "Customise"
5. Click the "pointer" tab
6. Select "DMZ (black)"
7. Close.
