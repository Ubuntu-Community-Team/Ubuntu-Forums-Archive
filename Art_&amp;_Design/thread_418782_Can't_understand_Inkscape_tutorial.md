### Post by QwUo173Hy on 2007-04-22
I've just had an email from the author of the tutorial and the answer lies in this

> > Permutations:
> 
> 1     x =  30  (60px x sin( 30° ))   y =  52  (60px x cos( 30° ))
> 2     x =  30  y = -52
> 3     x = -30  y =  52
> 4     x = -30  y = -52
> 5     x =  52  y =  30
> 6     x =  52  y = -30
> 7     x = -52  y =  30
> 8     x = -52  y = -30
