### Post by Newfoundlander on 2009-11-26
After 15 years of using versions of Adobe PageMaker, I switched over completely to Scribus last year.  Scribus seems to have taken more inspiration from QuarkXPress but has many features of PageMaker/InDesign.  For doing layout in Linux, you can't beat Scribus.

Other applications I use for simplier layouts are [Inkscape]("http://inkscape.org/") and  OpenOffice.org's Writer.
