### Post by smartalecks on 2007-02-18
Adobe Photoshop 7-8 work on Linux under WINE, but it takes a little effort. (search forums for howtos).
Photoshop 9 CS2 also works under WINE, but you need to have a windows computer with cs2 installed. Its quite a bit more complicated, unfortunately. Again, search forums for a howto.

Or you could use the GIMP and GIMPShop, as suggested above, or Pixel. 

I purchased Pixel to support the development. It works pretty well and is easy to install and use. It is _very_ fast (pascal). ATM alot of the stuff is pretty unusable but Beta 7 is to be released very soon and it looks very promising. FYI Pixel Beta 7 will have Wacom tablet support :). If you are going to buy it buy it now for $38.
