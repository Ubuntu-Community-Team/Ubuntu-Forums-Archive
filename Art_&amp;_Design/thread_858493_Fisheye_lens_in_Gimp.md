### Post by Xfcn on 2008-07-13
I don't know if there's an exact filter that can do this but under Filters > Distorts you can play with curve bend.

What I did was created an upper curve to view the results (that's a picture of a statuette of Catwoman), and then while STILL on the "Upper" part, I clicked "Mirror".

If you take a look, you can see my results. Uuuuh... hope that's helpful...
