"Wrong architecture" simply means you downloaded the wrong installer for either your processor or OS install.

If you're using 64-bit linux, you'd prefer an 'x86_64' installer. 32-bit linux requires the 'i386' or 'i486' or 'i586'... You get the idea.

Basically, go back to the downloader form thing and under the "Platform" dropdown, select the OPPOSITE of whatever you picked the first time.
