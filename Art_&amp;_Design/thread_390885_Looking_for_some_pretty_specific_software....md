### Post by Scunizi on 2007-03-22
One of the best looking Mindmap programs that I use is Vym.  It's in the repositories but on Dapper it's an older version.  You can grab the source and compile or I think there is a RPM version you can use alien on and convert it to a deb for install.  Lots of features on Vym like being able to export your map to html/xml, open office presentation and other formats.
