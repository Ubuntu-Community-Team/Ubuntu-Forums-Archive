### Post by Xfcn on 2008-06-29
It's okay, but there's a certain amount of pixelization that has occured on the new image that wasn't present in the previous image that really takes away from the over-all feel. If you look at the finer lines on the guys themselves (especially around the sunbursts and light on their sleeves) you'll see what I'm talking about. This jagged, pixely look doesn't really fit with the smooth flowing lines of the rest of the image. The "daft punk" logo is also kind of blurred and pixelated, when it could stand to be crisp and clear. Other than that, I like where you're going with this and I'd love to see a cleaned up version. I'd probably add it to my rotating wallpaper collection.
