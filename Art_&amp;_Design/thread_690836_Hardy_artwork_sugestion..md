### Post by exploder on 2008-02-07
I know that many Ubuntu user's would like to see improvements in the artwork for Hardy. As I see things, the artwork for Hardy will be very conservative. Why not ask for something obtainable?

Here is a suggestion for a wallpaper. (The one with no logo.)

[http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/ubuntu+glass?content=66701](http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/ubuntu+glass?content=66701)

With a couple of modifications this could be the GDM theme.

[http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Ubuntu-Orange+GDM+Theme?content=68092](http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Ubuntu-Orange+GDM+Theme?content=68092)

The changes to the GDM theme would be to make it with the wallpaper without the logo, replace the Gnome planter with a Ubuntu logo, and change the text from Gnome Desktop to Ubuntu.

I put together the GDM theme and have made another one with the changes I have described.

William Tax did a nice job with the wallpaper. The design has a look that fits with the style of gnome. The colors used look good with the current default Ubuntu theme and icons. The design is clean and not overdone. The wallpaper and modified GDM theme is appropriate for home or business settings. 

The number of downloads demonstrates that the artwork has been well received. I am using William Tax's wallpaper on my Hardy test system along with the modified GDM theme and I think the developer's might consider this conservative approach.

This would solve the brown problem and these two minor changes make a big difference in the appearance. Please post your thoughts, good or bad.
