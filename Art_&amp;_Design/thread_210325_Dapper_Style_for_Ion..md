### Post by phibxr on 2006-07-06
I just submitted a style for Ion inspired by the default theme in Ubuntu 6.06 Dapper Drake LTS. I'm not too much of a fan for orange myself, but at least there is a theme with official Ubuntu colors for Ion now.

A screenshot has been attached.

Link: [http://joshua.haninge.kth.se/~phibxr/look_human.lua](http://joshua.haninge.kth.se/~phibxr/look_human.lua)

Instructions:

1. Get the .lua-file from the URL above.
2. Move it to ~/.ion3
3. ln -s ~/.ion3/look_human.lua ~/.ion3/look.lua
4. Restart Ion.
