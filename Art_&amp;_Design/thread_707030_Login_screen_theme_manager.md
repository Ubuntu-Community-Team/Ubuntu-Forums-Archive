### Post by SpiderGorilla on 2008-02-25
They're very busy, with a lot going on. Now that's not to say they're bad: they're not. They're just very busy and they draw the eye in a lot of different directions, which could cause confusion for people not ready for them.

The second two are by far the best, with the least amount of "clutter". Although the Final Fantasy VII is the only one I'd consider for personal use. The EarthBound one would get my vote if the characters in the background didn't have that drop-shadow effect on them, or were ommited entirely.

All in all, these are great. I really feel that with just a bit more tweaking, these would be great to upload to gnome-look in the very near future.
