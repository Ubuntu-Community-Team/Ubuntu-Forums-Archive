### Post by crimesaucer on 2007-07-02
You can use any .png or .jpg, I haven't ever tried a .svg.... Just add the proper path to your image location, and then choose both "Scale" image, and "Adjust" image.


In ccsm:

Desktop Cube-->--Appearance-->--Cube Caps-->--and press " + Add ", then enter the path to your image in the "Value (String) for Image Files, then press OK.

Then, move it "up" so that it's on top of your other cube cab paths... then check both the "Scale Image", and "Adjust Image" boxes.
