### Post by chipants on 2008-02-19
honestly, i have never seen anybody distribute their own patches for zynaddsubfx. i could be wrong, and would love to be corrected on that.

zynaddsubfx does have built in ability to record what you play. i believe it's as simple as clicking 'record' on the main window. although, i'm not at my computer right now and cannot verify that.

however, i would recommend using an external application such as ardour or audacity to record the output of zynaddsubfx. that way, you can more easily edit and add to your recording. of course, you would need to be using jack to do so. i assume that you have some knowledge of jack. but, if not, see [http://jackaudio.org](http://jackaudio.org). jack is easily the most important piece of audio software in my studio.
