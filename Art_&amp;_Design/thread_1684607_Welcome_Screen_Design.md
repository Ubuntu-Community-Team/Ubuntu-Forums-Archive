### Post by xtremethegreat1 on 2011-02-12
I have tried [Jave]("http://www.jave.de/").

If you're looking forward to creating ascii art from images, I'd suggest [ascii convert]("http://asciiconvert.com/").

If you can program, or have anyone to program it for you, you can go for the libraries [libcaca]("http://sam.zoy.org/projects/libcaca/") or [aalib]("http://aa-project.sourceforge.net/"). I have used both and can vouch for it that they're just cool. The only difference is that where aalib concentrates on plain ascii, libcaca can give you colored ansi output.

Good luck!
