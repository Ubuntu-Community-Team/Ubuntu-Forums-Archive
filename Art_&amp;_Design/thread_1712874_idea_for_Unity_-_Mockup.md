### Post by jarreboum on 2011-03-25
It looks very similar to the Ubuntu Netbook Remix, 10.04 version, only with more ambition (and animation). It's great!

I like it very much, it's much much more clean than the current Unity crap. In your version you can actually guess the organisation and its more easy to find something.

Because I mainly use a netbook I'd be more in favour of popups that take the whole screen (see UNR), it looks nicer than everything crammed at one side, its easier to read and the cursor movement is not that a big deal (small screen). Generally speaking your design could use more space, it would be easier to read.

Also the software center icon should really launch the app, but this is nickpicking.

I'll say it again, it looks great, you should push it forward!
