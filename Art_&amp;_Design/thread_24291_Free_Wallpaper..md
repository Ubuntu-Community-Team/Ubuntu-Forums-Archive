Hi,
Thanks for the reviews its great to get feedback.
Ive been an artist for many years now so you cant hurt my feelings.

I agree with some of you, I am moving away from text an them so they can be used anywhere, I dont think logos have as much of a limiting effect.

You might notice that they are in chronilogical order so the first few are a bit low quality as I was new to Gimp but I moved into 3D and have changed programs now to blender so they will hopefully get better ?

The Egg one was just me mucking about but I used the Ubuntu reds, oranges etc. to give it a Ubuntu theme.
I am using Mepis at the moment but I loved the Ubuntu Human theme and logo so I am still doing Ubuntu stuff and the colour theme is one I made for Mepis KDE but I presume it will work in Kubuntu. 
It is a colour theme and a control bar bitmap in the Human theme colours although I have made slightly richer browns and is easy on the eye, I have made all the whites into a pale beige so it isnt as dazzly on the eyes when your on the PC for many hours like me.
When I have to return to my XP dual boot I get head aches from the bright screens straight away.

Im glad you like them and Ill be back with news on new stuff as it is added, or you can just bookmark my page and pop in now and again to see if there is anything new there.
