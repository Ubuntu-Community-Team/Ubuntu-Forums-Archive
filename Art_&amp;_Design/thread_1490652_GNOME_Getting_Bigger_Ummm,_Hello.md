### Post by ross9885 on 2010-08-09
I'm on a netbook with a maximum resolution of 1024x600 (not 800).
There is empty space around everything; shadows and glowing auras take up even more space.
The icons are too big.
The buttons, tabs and similar widgets are too tall.
The default font is too big (but I changed that to 9pt Trebuchet MS).
There is too much space between items lists.
There is way too much space between items in menus.
I had to remove the window list and the bar at the bottom of the screen.

The result is that there is barely any room left over for me to see my own work. Also, there are a few windows / dialogs that don't fit on the screen. To hit an OK button on a long dialog, I have to guess how many tabs it will take to focus on it, then hit enter and pray. Very scary.

It could probably be solved by capping all padding and spacing system wide to 1px and using 16px icons.
