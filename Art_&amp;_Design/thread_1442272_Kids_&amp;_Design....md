### Post by thekanuk on 2010-03-29
I was messing around making smilies the other day, and did not know my youngest son ((6) years old) was looking over my shoulder. He was using MS paint on an old bot; copying my design... 

What a great job he did! 

I knew he was a bit of an artist, like his Dad. I had no idea how good he was. I set him up in Gimp; left him to his own devices... ...I was amazed with how much he figured out on his own. Dumbfounded in fact! I *WILL *do more help expand his experience... 

Children are more imaginative then adults by far. Sometimes we forget this fact, we forget where we came from.

Place your child in front of the bot, open an art program, and let them go to town...
You maybe surprised with the outcome.
