### Post by lyceum on 2008-03-02
> **Mia_tech said:**
> I'm working with inkscape but I need more fonts, how do I go about installing more fonts?

thanks

Easy answer: Just got to you home folder and add a new folder, and it .fonts (be sure to ad the . ). Put your fonts there, they are added. When you need to go back to add or remove fonts you will not see the folder due to the . in front of the name, so just type .fonts in (...home\user\.fonts)

edit...
the name might need to be .font, I can't remember and I am in Vista right now, sorry. If .fonts doesn't work re-name the folder without the s at the end.
