I'm not sure about your question. Are you asking which program is better, Inkscape or GIMP?


I'm not professional but I am using them for quite sometime already. Inkscape is a program for doing vector art while GIMP is a photomanipulation and digital painting program.  Basically they are quite different. 

I recommend using both in your art work as each has its advantage.

You might want to look up the difference between raster and vector image.
