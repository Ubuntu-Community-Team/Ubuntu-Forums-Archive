### Post by bigsmitty64 on 2010-10-25
DeviantArt and Wallbase are all you need!

**WALLBASE:**
 Although as mentioned before, with Wallbase, you really need to be careful to select "sfw" because there are some very graphic wallpapers. But the upside is, you can choose your desktop size and random search. And it just never ends!

**DEVIANTART:**
Artists heaven! There are so many great artists here its unbelievable. And tons of **tutorials for gimp and photoshop** alike. And **free** to become a member. 

I stopped looking for wallpaper sites altogether once I found these 2 sites.
