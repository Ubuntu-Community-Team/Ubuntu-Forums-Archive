### Post by hsn6 on 2012-07-06
the part of the tablet that is on one of picture used such as keyboard is the extraLED .
ExtraLED is another led that it duty is save the energy and a place for view the unity launcher or play controller or progress bar or keyboard or any extra widget.
for example ,you have low battery and you want to listen to music , at this moment you turn off the main led and control your music with extraled at resault you save the energy .
this extraled can put on the up,down,left or right via connect to main or put on another place and connect via usb .
those 5 rectangle button are the tabs that use for switch between windows . you touch with your finger and press on one of this button with your another finger for connect the window to this button and when we need to it you can switch to this window.
