### Post by mcduck on 2009-11-24
Scribus and Inkscape would both be good tools for that kind of purposes, and they are both also available for Windows and OS X.

Inkscape is a vector graphics editor, but can also handle text to some level, definitely well enough for creating print material with only a few pages (like in menus).

Scribus is a full-featured desktop publishing tool.
