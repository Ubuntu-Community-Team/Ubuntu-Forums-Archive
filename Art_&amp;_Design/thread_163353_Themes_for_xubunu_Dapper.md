Xubuntu developers invite you to help choosing a theme for the upcoming xubuntu dapper.
We have three themes to choose from; each includes a logo, a color scheme, a wallpaper, a gdm greeter and a usplash. 
They can be viewed here:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DapperXubuntuColorScheme](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DapperXubuntuColorScheme)
