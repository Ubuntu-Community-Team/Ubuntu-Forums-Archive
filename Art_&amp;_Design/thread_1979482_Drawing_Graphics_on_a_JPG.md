### Post by kurja on 2012-05-14
Yes "CAD" stands for Computer Assisted/Aided Design/Drafting, I meant to ask what these "cad-like drawings" you spoke of might be. Arrows and numbers it is, then.

Tried libreoffice draw yet? You can import the jpg picture in it, then just add the arrows and whatnots over it, libreoffice has a bunch of different arrow styles that you can use. It also keeps the arrows and texts that you add as separate entities so you can adjust them later.

Just the quick solution that sprang to mind first... There are a million way to do this, if it's a one off thing and doesn't need to be very presentable just draw on picture in gimp or whatever, I can't really give much better advice without knowing exactly what, and for what kind of use, you want to produce.
