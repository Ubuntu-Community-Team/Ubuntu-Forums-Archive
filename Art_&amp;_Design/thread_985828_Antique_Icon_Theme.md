Thanks. I would suggest just practicing a lot with Gimp and Inkscape most of all. Try out every tutorial you come across until you're comfortable with the programs. Then try opening up some icons that you like and see if you can recreate them. For instance, check out this Nepomuk icon that I've attached. Open it up in Inkscape, zoom in and ungroup the elements and move them around. It's a really impressive icon. It should give you an idea of how things are done.
