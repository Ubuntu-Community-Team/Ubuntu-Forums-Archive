### Post by evgeny12 on 2010-12-27
I had the same problem with inkscape 0.48 in ubuntu 10.10.
This problem will happen only if I use mac4lin package.
Therefore I installed macubuntu 10.10 script, that may be downloaded free
at [http://sourceforge.net/projects/macbuntu/](http://sourceforge.net/projects/macbuntu/)
Just download it, extract somewhere and in terminal run ./install.sh (not sh, it will not work correct!)
Now I have everything I had + not buggy inkscape.
P.S. By the way all your previous options in Avant window navigator will be intact.
