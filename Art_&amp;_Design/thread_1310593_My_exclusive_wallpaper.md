### Post by FiveSidedPoly on 2009-11-06
I think it is a good start, quite interesting concept for sure, keep working at different variations.  The open areas are usually where some people place their typical icons on their desktop.  I tend to not use wallpapers that put complex shapes or color combos in those areas because it distracts my eyes.
 
Some suggestions I do have though are to:
 
- the upper and lower parts are somewhat "faded/worn", but the horses' mid section is very crisp.  Seems a little unbalanced imo.  You could possibly add some wear to the mid section, maybe the rear end of the horses.
 
- if you take the first suggestion or if not, I think you could add some irregularity to the outline of the horses, make it a little rougher perhaps.
