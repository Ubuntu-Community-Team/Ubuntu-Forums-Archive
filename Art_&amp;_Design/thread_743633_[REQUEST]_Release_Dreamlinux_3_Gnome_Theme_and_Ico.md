### Post by Zero Prime on 2008-04-12
I have the gtk themes, but I don't know if I would be allowed to release them.  You can do what I did though.  I have DL loaded on to one of my partitions.  The themes are located under use/share/themes.  Just copy and paste to your desk top then compress them and install via the theme manager.

### Post by dldev on 2009-06-03
Just a suggestion:

Asking on Ubuntu forums for Dreamlinux artwork is probably not going to be seen by the Dreamlinux Devs. But anyway.

Andrefelipe is the designer who individually created every Icon and Wallpaper for Dreamlinux, he is also one of the main developers.

As has been suggested, you can download the Dreamlinux iso, run it live and copy the icons and use them.

Mixing and matching to create your personal desktop is one of the beauties of Linux.

Andrefelipe was interviewed on Linux Graphic Users [HERE]("http://linuxgraphicsusers.com/index.php?topic=384.0")
And Dreamlinux Wallpaper designer Ruud Kuin won the Xmas wallpaper competition [HERE]("http://linuxgraphicsusers.com/index.php?topic=457.0")

Maybe one day, an icon install script will be released as the Dreamlinux style seems to be quite popular with other distro users who like the Mac influence.
