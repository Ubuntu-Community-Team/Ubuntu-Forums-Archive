### Post by ~unknown on 2009-12-11
Here's a guide for making some cheap stickers.
1. Print the stickers on normal paper
2. Put some thin contact plastic on the frontside
3. Carefully put some double face tape on the backside (ex. Scotch Double Face)
4. Cut the stickers. You can use scissors.

Remember to use **thin** materials.
