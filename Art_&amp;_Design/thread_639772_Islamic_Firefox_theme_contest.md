### Post by jmehdi on 2007-12-13
We on the Ubuntu Muslim Edition ([www.ubuntume.com]("http://www.ubuntume.com")) team are looking for an Islamic Firefox theme, and we've decided to leave it up to you. That's right, we're holding a contest to let you the user decide how it should look. So get out your virtual paintbrushes and get to work. The deadline is March 1st, 2008. Then the UbuntuME majlis will vote for the winner, whose theme will be included and selected by default in the next release of UbuntuME (8.04) (the other themes could be included if we find them interesting).

Minimum Requirements

    * Your submission must be licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution-Share Alike license.
    * Your submission must include at least a PNG mockup and SVG/PSD sources, and instructions to build the theme jar file.
    * Your submission must be received by 23:59 UTC on March 1st, 2008 
    * Your submission must be sent to administrator _at_ ubuntume.com or it will not be considered


Thank you all in advance for your submissions, and good luck!
