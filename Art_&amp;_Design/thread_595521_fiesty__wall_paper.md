### Post by damianvila on 2007-10-29
If you use the original artwork you'll get better results: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Official]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Official")
I don't know which software you're using, but try importing or copy/pasting.
Also, white fonts work best over orange backgrounds than black fonts.
And try to give some "rhythm" to the logos. Maybe you could put the top logos well defined and go down the wallpaper giving progressive amounts of blur, or alternating logos with different levels of blur, like it's "snowing" Ubuntu logos...
Hope it helps.
