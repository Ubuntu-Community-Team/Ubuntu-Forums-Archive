### Post by InfectedWithDrew on 2009-08-19
I hope you all don't mind my blatant advertising but I've started an autobiographical webcomic called The Journey.

Anyways, lemme sum it up for y'all:

The Journey is an auto-biographical webcomic.  It tells the story of my life as I grow up as an American teenager, and later man, in this big, bad world.  I make it using a camera and a photo editor; most people call it a photo journal or a photo comic.  It truly is different, and maybe even one-of-a-kind, though I credit the style much to [A Softer World]("http://asofterworld.com/") and the concept to [Allan]("http://allancomic.com/") and [Inkdick]("http://ghostcarpress.com/inkdick/").  I update The Journey daily.

But enough blabbering – I've written an About page for a reason.  Ladies and gentlemen, I give you:
