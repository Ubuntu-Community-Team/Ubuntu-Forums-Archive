### Post by BslBryan on 2009-06-14
A lot of people who've liked my artwork have been continuously emailing me, suggesting I send it to Mark Shuttleworth.  Well, so, I did.  

I've asked about joining the artwork team as well, so I may be looking into that.  This is part of what he said:
[QUOTE=Mark Shuttleworth]Hi Bryan,

I've asked some of the guys doing theming at Canonical to take a look and get back to you. In principle we have space on the CD for two community-contributed themes and if yours is the best out there then it should definitely be on the Karmic CD.[/QUOTE]

I've already told most of you who've emailed me, but just to repeat: I am flattered that you like my artwork, and I will continue to expand it and try my best to become more involved in official work, but I've contacted Mark, and now it's out of my hands.  I've done my part, and if Canonical likes it, you'll know about it.  

Thanks everyone for supporting me thus far.  :-)
