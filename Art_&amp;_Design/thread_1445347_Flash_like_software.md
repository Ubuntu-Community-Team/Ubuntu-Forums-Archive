### Post by goseph on 2010-04-02
I think that OP was looking for a substitute for flash studio rather than a substitute for adobe flash player.

For simple animations you can use the GAP plugin for GIMP to make animated GIFs

For more sophisticated animations, you may wish to try:
[http://www.assembla.com/wiki/show/MadSwatter](http://www.assembla.com/wiki/show/MadSwatter)

which will create animated SVGs for you.
