### Post by hyperdude111 on 2009-11-28
All of these "I like the current one better" responses are really not helpful o the OP. 

I do like your Idea but think there are some areas for improvement. The font i think is comic sans ? Well it is not a professional font you would expect for an OS it is too childlike. A font such as "Ariel" or "Trebuchet MS" have a more relaxed smooth feel than Times New Roman but also keep that professional feel. 

The colour scheme is also a bit simple and it seems like most of the work is copied from the ubuntuone site. However I do think that this could make quite a nice theme (even if not to my personal tastes). However this is good work and people just comparing it to a site that has a professional art team working on its look is not a fair comparison. 

I would advise you to look at a website such a kubuntu which is in desperate need of a revamp and try to make a mockup of a new site that fits in with your new ideas so it is easy to see a marked improvement.

Good Luck.
