### Post by xl_cheese on 2008-02-25
Well, not entirely new.  I just took clearlooks, made some alterations to it and renamed it.



I have a handful of ideas up my sleeve I still want to implement.  The pixmap engine allows a lot of freedom and creativity to the themer.  I want to be able do some of that with code and not pictures.

So far the changes are subtle.  Download it and compare the button and button prelights to clearlooks glossy for one example.

get it here:
[http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/xl_cheeselooks+gtk-engine?content=73163](http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/xl_cheeselooks+gtk-engine?content=73163)

A few themes:
[http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/xl_cheeselooks+theme+pack?content=75868](http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/xl_cheeselooks+theme+pack?content=75868)

I posted here to try to round up ideas from others.  **[SIZE="2"]So please critique, criticize, and suggest![/SIZE]**
