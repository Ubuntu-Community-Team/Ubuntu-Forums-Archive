### Post by Aphorism on 2007-05-24
Generally, mixing and matching bits results in a sub optimal approach -- although Lizardking managed to do this quite successfully with OranSun.

There are a few extra 'whole' themes in the repositories:
blubuntu-look
tropic-look
oransun-look
peace-look

If anyone manages to bundle up a full set for a 'look', they should consider trying to get it into the repositories for other users.

I do not know of many currently developed full 'look' attempts.  It takes a heckuva lot of time and effort to provide a fully comprehensive look / feel.  I am trying to get one to a near alpha state, but it unfortunately requires a good deal of time in between other projects.  The sig link below should help you find it.
