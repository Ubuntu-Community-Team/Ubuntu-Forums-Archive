### Post by Logistics64 on 2008-03-27
Ok, I will teach you how to make Paper (sticky) stickers using gLabels. You can install it throught the Add/Remove program. Head to your local office store and pickup Avery 5161 Rectangular Labels.

Step One: Open up gLabels, it will be uder "Office"
Two: Click New and select 'Avery 5161 Labels
Three: Click "Create Image Object and Select the area where you want the Image
Four: Select the Ubuntu Image and Size it too your Liking (see attached files)
Five: Print and Enjoy!
