### Post by Darkstar_444 on 2008-09-01
Hi

Don't think you can add a stroke directly round an imported image, but you can create an rectangle over the image with removed fill (Alpha Opacity to 0) and your stroke paint and style. 

Go to Object/Fill and Stroke to see your stroke and fill options.

If its a raster image you have imported (jpeg etc), Export bitmap to save all your drawing otherwise jpeg is lost.

If your editing a raster image best program i find is the GIMP.

Hope this helps:guitar:
