### Post by objecm on 2010-08-10
There are plenty of monochrome icons for panels - try elementary or faenza. However you'll have a harder time finding icons that are all monochrome, if that's what you want. You might have to take another icon theme and set up a batch edit for all the icons in it.

That will give you lots of icons though.

Sorry I couldn't help more.

Actually, I think there's something called something like icogen you could try. It's designed to recolour icons, but you could just use whites and greys as the new colours.
