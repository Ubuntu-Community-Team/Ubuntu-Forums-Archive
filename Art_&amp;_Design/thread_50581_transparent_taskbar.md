How do i make the tasks listed on my taskbar transparent in KDE (Kubuntu)?

---

### Post by SGC on 2005-07-20
**For a tranparent taskbar:**

Right click on the taskbar and choose:
"Configure panel" -> "Appearance" tab -> "Advanced Options" button

Change the "Tint amount" to the value that you want, "Min" for full transparency.

**For a transparent tasks in the taskbar:**

Download [taskbar v2](http://www.kde-look.org/content/show.php?content=25615) , and install it using this command
```
sudo dpkg -i mtaskbar-0.7_0.7-1_i386.deb
```
After Installing taskbar v2, right click on the taskbar and choose:
Remove from panel -> Applet -> Taskbar

And then , right click on the taskbar again and choose:
Add to panel -> Applet -> Taskbar v2
