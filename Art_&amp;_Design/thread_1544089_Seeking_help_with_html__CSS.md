### Post by Drenriza on 2010-08-03
i did get help whit this on the joomla forum.

Saloution:
What you want to accomplish doesn't require html/css/php knowledge. At least for 1,3, and 4. I cannot advise on how to change the logo until you can tell us that you have the permissions to remove the logo.

1.) The "Polls" module can be disabled under the Extensions>Module Manager menu.
2.) Depending on the TOS under which you downloaded the template, I am not sure if you can remove the logo or not. Please, ask the developer of the template.
3.) The "Advertising" module can be disabled like the above module.
4.) The "News Flash" module can also be disabled like the above.
