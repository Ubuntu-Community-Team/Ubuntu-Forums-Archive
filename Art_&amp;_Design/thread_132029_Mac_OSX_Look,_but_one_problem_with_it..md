### Post by LaSSarD on 2006-02-17
you have to send an .png image over /usr/share/icons/hicolor/48x48/apps/distributor-logo.png

it doesn't need to be 48x48, actually I recommend you to use the same height you use on your panel :)

for a really nice Mac OSX look on gnome, I recommend you to take some inspiration from sonik:
