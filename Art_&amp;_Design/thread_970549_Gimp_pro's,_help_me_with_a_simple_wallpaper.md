### Post by unutbu on 2008-11-06
The steps to making this are as follows:
0) make a new 2560x2048 image
1) make elliptical/rectangular selections to form the logo. Save the selection to channels.
2) Using the selections, redraw the logo
3) Draw a black and white version
4) Blur the black and white version
5) Use Filters>Light and Shadow>Lighting Effects (use the BW version for the bump map)
6) Image>Scale Image to 1280x1024. (This helps hide some artifacts).

The smaller logo made yesterday also used Filters>Map>Bump Map and compositing of layers in 'burn' mode. The BW blurred image was burned on top of the logo.
