### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-01-31
I know it's a fairly common theme, but have you considered [ACYL]("http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Any+Color+You+Like?content=102435")? With the right colors, textures, and gradients selected, it can really evoke whatever century you want. I'm using it for a rather retro theme myself, although with a bit of CRT nostalgia mixed in....

[IMG]http://i146.photobucket.com/albums/r256/Kalimol/Screenshot-6.png[/IMG]

And it's been upgraded since this version with some new filters that allow for a couple of different types of outlines and bevels. Pretty cool, and potentially, you could get the look of little brass buttons with emblems on.

There was actually a recent thread in here on steampunk themes - just search for steampunk.
