### Post by thomasmathiesen on 2005-03-28
Well, I am not very good at it, but here's what I know;

GDM is the logon screen theme. These are all kept in /usr/share/gdm/theme(s?)
GTK is a normal theme (windows border etc.) and they are all kept in /usr/share/themes

The metacity configuration is to be found in the Gnome menu -> System Tools -> Configuration Editor (-> App -> Metacity).
