Hi everybody,

i've been using my Wacom Bamboo tablet to do cartoons and stuff but i'm having troubles with the drawing tools. Currently i am trying to combine the facilities of 3 different programs: Gimp, MyPaint and Inkscape Vector. ### Post by Paddy Landau on 2013-01-06
I don't have any clever ideas, but have you considered adding new brushes to GIMP?

[LIST]
[*]You can [upgrade to GIMP 2.8]("http://www.unixmen.com/gimp-2-8-is-out-ppa-ubuntu/"). I recommend this, especially if you prefer the Single Window Mode.
[/LIST]

[LIST]
[*]You can [create your own custom brushes]("http://www.gimp.org/tutorials/Custom_Brushes/").
[/LIST]

[LIST]
[*]You can [find many new brushes]("https://www.google.com/search?q=gimp+brushes") on the 'net to add to GIMP. Also [search the GIMP site]("https://www.google.com/search?q=gimp+brushes+site%3Agimp.org").
[/LIST]
