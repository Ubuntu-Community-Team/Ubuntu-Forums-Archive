### Post by BradwJensen on 2008-07-28
Have you seen the new features and changes to Banshee-1?

[Here]("http://bradwjensen.deviantart.com/art/Ubuntu-01-92874487") is a screenshot I took of it.  I think Rhythmbox is still a bit too cluttered with all those buttons on the top and could learn and mature a bit more from noticing the features in Banshee-1.

Take for instance the Repeat 1, Repeat all, No-Repeat button in the bottom right.  It compacts all the related functions into one quick popup menu.  Another nice feature is the connection of buttons for Shuffle or No-shuffle with the forward button.

Another thing I like that Banshee-1 now has is that it lets you choose to have the Browser on the left for widescreen monitors, or top for fullscreen monitors (your choice, really)..
[I]
Agian, these are just some ideas that I think Rhythmbox could make use of and mature more from.[/I]
