### Post by jem7v on 2007-01-07
The two places I usually look for GTK2 themes (these set the "Controls" in your System->Preferences->Themes... Theme Details dialogue) are at GNOME Art and GNOME-Look.
[http://art.gnome.org/themes/gtk2/?sort_by=popularity&limit=24&view=list&order=DESC](http://art.gnome.org/themes/gtk2/?sort_by=popularity&limit=24&view=list&order=DESC) lists their most popular ones, including 3-4 convincing Mac themes...
[http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xsortmode=down&page=0](http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xsortmode=down&page=0) lists GNOME-Look's most popular GTK2 themes, many of which are Mac-inspired.
