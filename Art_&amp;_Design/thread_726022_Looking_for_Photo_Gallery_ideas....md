### Post by jimbren on 2008-03-16
Gallery ([http://gallery.menalto.com/](http://gallery.menalto.com/)) is a very robust photo album organizer that does slide shows, as well as many other things.  It is easy to configure and use, and you can enable or disable features on the fly for all of your albums or just particular ones.  

Check it out.  It should be right up your alley.
