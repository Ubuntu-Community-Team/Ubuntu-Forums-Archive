### Post by beesthorpe on 2007-06-26
Well there is a tutorial on Metacity theming at:
[http://developer.gnome.org/doc/tutorials/metacity/metacity-themes.html]("http://developer.gnome.org/doc/tutorials/metacity/metacity-themes.html")
but it seems the way most people get started with it is to take an already existing theme that you like and tinker with it until you break it completely, err, I mean understand how it works.  On the other hand if that sounds a bit scary the Murrine theme engine has a gui configurator  at [http://murrine.netsons.org/?q=node/8]("http://murrine.netsons.org/?q=node/8")
though I haven't tried it myself.
