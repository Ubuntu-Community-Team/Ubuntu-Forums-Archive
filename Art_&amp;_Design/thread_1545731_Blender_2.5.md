### Post by Paul820 on 2010-08-14
> This new set up is going to test me ..... my memory is not so good nowadays and I use far too many 3D systems .... pressing the space bar now seems like a distant past ..... one key press replaced with a combination now .... 

Go into **File**->**User Preferences**, click **Add-Ons** at the top, click on the **3d view** button on the left, click on the little arrow where it says **3D View:Dynamic Spacebar Menu** and click the box on the right. Press save as default. Press your spacebar :D

Here is a couple of links that will help you out:
[http://www.blenderguru.com/whered-that-button-go/]("http://www.blenderguru.com/whered-that-button-go/")
[http://www.blendercookie.com/]("http://www.blendercookie.com/")

You will get used to it, just give it time.

---

### Post by Paul820 on 2010-08-14
@chips24, go to file->user preferences->input and click emulate numpad on the left.

If anyone else can't find things i would suggest looking in the user preferences for it. There is a ton of settings in there that can be set to how you want. If you make a mistake and break something, just go to file->load factory settings to reset everything back. No harm done:P
