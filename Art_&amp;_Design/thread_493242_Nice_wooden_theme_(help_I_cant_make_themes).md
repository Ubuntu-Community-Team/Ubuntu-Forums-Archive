### Post by coffeecat on 2007-07-05
Do you know about [art.gnome.org]("http://art.gnome.org/")? They've got a [number of tutorials]("http://live.gnome.org/GnomeArt/Tutorials") for gnome theming which you might find useful.

I've never found the time to go through them myself but I wish you well with that idea. Polished wood sounds good. That's classy. Perhaps some leather upholstery to go with it? :wink: But it's such a change from all the blue themes and MacOS and Vista ripoffs. Good luck with it.

One last idea. If you haven't come across it yet, get yourself thewidgetfactory. It's in the repositories and listed in Synaptic. You use it to test your widgets. I can't remember whether it sets up a menu entry when it's installed - I rather think it doesn't. If it doesn't, just do 'twf' from a terminal.
