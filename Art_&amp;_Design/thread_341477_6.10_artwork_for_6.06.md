### Post by broy on 2007-02-15
The themes in 6.10 weren't much different from 6.06. The wallpapers are probably available off of images.google.com or gnome-look.org. If there is a theme that you don't have in 6.06 you can probably just get them off another pc running 6.10 and copy them into the themes directory on 6.10.

The themes are located in /usr/share/gdm/themes/ btw.
