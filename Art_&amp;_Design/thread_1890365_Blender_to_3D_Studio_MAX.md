### Post by LBokkers on 2011-12-11
This is my experience from changing from 3ds max to another 3d package:
basic tools (move, scale, rotate): 5-10 minutes
mesh basic tools (apply basic tools on vertices, faces, etc.): 10min - 2 hours.
From there, you need to learn a lot of new things, but you will also recognize a lot of things you used in the old package.
