### Post by cookieofdoom on 2008-06-06
Just don't try to do it all at once. Scan through the Blender noob to pro wiki, pick out interesting projects and do them when you feel like it. Once you've gotten a little more comfortable with the interface, start a project that challenges you. It'll take a few days, and you'll have to look stuff up on the internet but you'll come out of it knowing a lot more than you did before.
