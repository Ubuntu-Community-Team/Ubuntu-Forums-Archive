### Post by Flimm on 2009-03-24
File, Save a copy.
Type the name you want to save it as, ending with .jpg . You can see all the other formats the GIMP supports by expanding Select File Type, just above the Save button. Click Save. If it gives you any warnings, just click Export. Then click Save in the export dialog. It's easy really.

### Post by gjoellee on 2009-03-24
