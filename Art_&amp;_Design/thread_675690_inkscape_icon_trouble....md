### Post by chipants on 2008-02-19
there are a number of things that could be causing your problem.

inkscape itself does not (by default) export to standard .SVG. instead, it uses it's own version of the standard. fortunately, you CAN export to 100% standards-compliant SVG, by selecting that format when saving your work. see if that helps, first.
