### Post by Dry Lips on 2013-02-27
Riiiight, now I see what you mean! :)

Pinta is inspired by paint.net, and in paint.net they actually removed the "clear history" button. So it's perfectly possible that there simply isn't such a feature in Pinta, either. (Correct me if I'm wrong, anyone?)

If it turns out that there isn't such a feature, I'd recommend you to file it as a bug: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/pinta](https://bugs.launchpad.net/pinta)

Pinta *is* supposed to be much simpler than Gimp, but I'd say there ought to be a reasonable balance between simplicity and the amount of features present.
