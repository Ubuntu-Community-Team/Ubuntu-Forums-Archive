### Post by huwshimi on 2007-02-12
HI Michel, as far as I know the are a couple of things that may affect you with the -lowlatency kernel. One of them is that I think some people have had problems with graphics drivers in the past, but unless you notice anything then you're probably fine. The other thing is that I think that if you're running a laptop on batteries then you won't get as long as on a normal kernel.

You might want to check this topic for more talk on the -lowlatency kernel: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=308287]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=308287")
