With all the effort gone into providing a beautiful default theme for ubuntu I think it is ridiculous that dapper doesn't have custom Gaim Smileys. [http://tango-project.org/Tango_Icon_Library](http://tango-project.org/Tango_Icon_Library)
I vote they be made default for Edgy. :-D
