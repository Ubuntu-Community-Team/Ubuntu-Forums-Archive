For those of you who have upgraded OpenOffice.org to Version 2.0, you might be interested in an ubuntu custom splash to replace the default.

Just download the tarball attached, extract it and replace the file **intro.bmp** located in **/usr/lib/openoffice2/program**. You'll need to be root to save in /usr/lib.
