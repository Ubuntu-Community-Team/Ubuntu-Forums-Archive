### Post by -Phi- on 2006-07-18
Imagemagick runs in the command line. To convert your picture from .ico to .png you would go to the folder the picture is in and type something like:

```
convert image.ico image.png
```

More information here: [http://www.imagemagick.org/script/convert.php](http://www.imagemagick.org/script/convert.php)

- Phi
