### Post by Aelfric5578 on 2006-11-06
> **s_harn said:**
> how hard would it be to make it just read Ubuntu instead of Kubuntu?

If you want the name and the logo to change then it's very easy.  It's just a matter of turning layers on and off in the GIMP XCF file that I posted.  If you want to keep the Kubuntu logo and just change the name, it's still doable, it would just mean making the Kubuntu title and logo visible, then erasing the word Kubuntu, then you would make the ubuntu title and logo visible and erasing the ubuntu logo.  Like I said above, I don't have  much time to work on artwork at the moment, but I posted the XCF file so that anyone who wanted to could edit the labels as they saw fit.  So go ahead, download the file and see what you can do with it.  Let me know if you come up with something interesting.

Also just in case you haven't read the whole thread (I know I do that sometimes) I did post a pure Ubuntu label earlier.  The Kubuntu one was only made because someone asked me for it.
