### Post by the.scarecrow on 2009-12-15
Wow, hey Islington you are a genius.

I don't have Adblock installed but I did a search on the Firefox help for adblock that lead straight to the problem. I had accidentally "blocked all images from that domain". 

Goto Edit>preferences>content and you get to "load images automatically" which has an exceptions button. Press that to access a list and sure enough tavmjong.free.fr was the only entry in there and blocked. I just had to delete it to get the images back again.

Thanks a bunch Islington - much better than un-installing/re-installing Firefox which may well have not solved this problem but could have caused other problems as well.
