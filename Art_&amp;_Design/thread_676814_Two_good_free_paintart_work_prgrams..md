### Post by Liam-Gorham on 2008-01-24
I have found two rather good free paint programs which if installed correctly will work with ubuntu. They will need wine to install:

1.) Paint.net quite complex  but very easy to use.
and 2.) Inkscape Quite a few more features for the more advanced art designers.

Paint.net: [www.getpaint.net/redirect/wp/index.html](www.getpaint.net/redirect/wp/index.html)
Inkscape: [www.inkscape.org](www.inkscape.org)
