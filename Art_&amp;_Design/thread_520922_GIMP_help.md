### Post by hikaricore on 2007-08-08
If you rightclick on the titlebar of the window, there is an option that says "always on top" click this and your toolboxes will stay above the image.

Otherwise if you're doing extensive graphic design I suggest using the highest resolution you possibly can, example; I've never gone below 1600x1200 in nearly 10 years on any system I've owned.
