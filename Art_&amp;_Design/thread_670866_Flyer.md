[http://vectormagic.stanford.edu/](http://vectormagic.stanford.edu/)

It's absolutely amazing... I used it when I was designing a box that needed a couple of pictures. Even if your bitmaps are large enough, vectors help keep the file size small. Try it out, anyway, and see if the vectorization is detailed enough for your purposes.
