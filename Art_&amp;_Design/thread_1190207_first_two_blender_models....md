### Post by cubeist on 2009-06-20
Hey Chips, To select multiple vertices at once, use the B key.  One press will give you a box to drag around the vertices you want, press B again and it will switch to a circular selector which you can change the size of using your mouse button scroll wheel and rapidly select multiple vertices, lines, or walls.
