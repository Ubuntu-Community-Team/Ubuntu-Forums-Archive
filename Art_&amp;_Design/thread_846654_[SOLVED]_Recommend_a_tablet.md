### Post by V for Vincent on 2008-07-02
I got myself a small bamboo fun about a week ago. I really like it. If you're having doubts about which size you should get, try them out at the store. For me, the small model definitely cuts it. I've got a 19 inch widescreen monitor, so I'd say that, generally, an A6 will do. I'm pretty glad I didn't go for the medium. I can draw what I want as it stands, the A5 is twice the cost, and I'd have to move my arm more.

### Post by V for Vincent on 2008-07-07
pretty easy, considering that I was prepared for the worst. btw, if you're going for a medium, does that mean you've tried out both models? I would really recommend that, as I'm more than happy with the A6.
