### Post by Geezanansa on 2012-05-23
Have been looking for software capable of producing models from drawings to high standard (2d and 3d).
As the software is not being used daily i do not want to pay 00s of shekkels and found this thread thinking there would be loads of high quality linux apps to choose from but can not find anything like sketchup.  
Have used autocad and other softwares through work but after having a look at and trying sketchup AM truly amazed.
Using sketchup from a landscaping designers point of view it is possible to upload photos of your site(or navigate to using google earth) and create a 3d model on your screen.  Adding to and removing as you desire.  This is an amazing tool.  All very intuitive and complex.  Feels very querky at first though just watch tutorials which will explain some of the things going on that might not be apparent to new user.
There are third party add ons to sketchup that will render models in full hd. There are loads of animation options aswell for making presentations etc.  Endless uses and functions. 

All this if you are willing to agree to the google privacy agreement.:lolflag:

No such thing as free.:lolflag:
