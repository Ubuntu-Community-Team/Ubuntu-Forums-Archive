### Post by MadsRH on 2008-06-29
Please post feedback on this idea to get digital artists interested in submitting suggestions for Ubuntu.
The "banner/poster" graphics are done very fast and not final at all! I would like feedback on the text witch is:

> 
**Design a wallpaper!**
Have your work see by millions of people!
Put your creativity to best use by improving the look of Ubuntu. Help design a background for the next Ubuntu release!

_What is this competition?_
Although this resembles a competition, it’s not. There is no prize you can win, except that your work will be included in software used by millions of people. 

_How can I “win”?_
If the artwork you have submitted get’s selected it will become the default artwork in Ubuntu.
[U]
How do I participate?[/U]
You can go to the [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork) where you will find more information. The direct link to the wallpaper submissions is: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/Intrepid/Desktop_Background_Submissions](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/Intrepid/Desktop_Background_Submissions)

Ubuntu is a community developed, Linux-based operating system. Ubuntu is and always will be free of charge. You do not pay any licensing fees. You can download, use and share Ubuntu with your friends, family, school or business for absolutely nothing.

Open source culture is the creative practice of appropriation and free sharing of found and created content.
