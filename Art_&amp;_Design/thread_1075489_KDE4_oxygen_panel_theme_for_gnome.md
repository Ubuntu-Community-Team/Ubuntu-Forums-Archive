### Post by NickArgyle on 2009-02-20
Panels can be changed regardless of the theme. 

Right click the panel, got to properties-> background.

I just posted a thread including a panel I made here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1075033](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1075033)

Just open the .png and save it somewhere, then set it as the panel background. It is not exactly like the kde one, but it looks good and is a similar dark style. Also increase the panel to 35px with my image. 


As far as themes for gnome go, there is an oxygen (kde4 theme) port for gnome which I am currently using with my panel background. :

[http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/KDE4+Oxygen+new+port+for+GNOME?content=86653](http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/KDE4+Oxygen+new+port+for+GNOME?content=86653)
