### Post by mcduck on 2009-05-06

> Ok that may be it.  Can I zoom in on text but not graphics?  I didn't realise graphics where zoomable.

Most new browser versions support scaling graphics as well as text. Ans also seem to do that by default (possibly because so many web developers seem to find it waste of time / too hard to make the page layout to work even if the text sizes are changed.. :P).

Anyway, just go to View/Zoom and mark the "Zoom Text only"-box to get back the correct (:D) behavior.
