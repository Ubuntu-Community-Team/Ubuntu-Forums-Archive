### Post by orro on 2006-06-17
You may not know this (its not the easiest thing to find with google), but you can actually [download the official logos in a load of formats from the ubuntu website]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Official"). This is what i've been using, but beware that when working with the official logos you must adhere to the [terms of the trademark policy]("http://www.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/TrademarkPolicy/"). 

So check out the [Official Artwork wiki page]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Official"), for the all the vector logos.
