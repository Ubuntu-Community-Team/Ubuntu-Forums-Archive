I have post a thread that ask what is people's layout looks like, and one thing I see is that most people like very limited of thing on their desktop, what is that mean is people like simplicity, as well as I do. 

I think, it will be a good idea to allow all the panel along with their icon (or anything on it) to be transparent. and only glow up or reflect up when your mouse point pointed at it. or even give the option that allow the panel be to mono transparent, what is that mean is that all the colors will be turn into (black and white) and then transparent to a point you can just see it on the desktop but not destroy the view of your pretty wallpaper. 

I understand that it is better to post this on gnome, KDE, or even compiz fusion site. but I want to see how you guy thing, as far as my English is bad, and I don't have any visual image to show. ask me questions so I can make myself clear.
