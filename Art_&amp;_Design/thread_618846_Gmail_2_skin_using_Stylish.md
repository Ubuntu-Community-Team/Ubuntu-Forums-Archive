### Post by crimesaucer on 2007-11-20
I found a pretty cool Gmail skin for the "Stylish" Firefox add-ons.

It's called Better Gmail 2 Skin - Fe mails: [http://userstyles.org/styles/4108](http://userstyles.org/styles/4108)

It's really girly ( hence the name... Fe - mail ),  but the design of the page is really nice. I found it very easy to change the colors and the font (size also). I also changed the gmail logo back to the regular one.

check it out:

1280x800 view: [http://i144.photobucket.com/albums/r161/crimesaucer/Screenshot-88-1.png](http://i144.photobucket.com/albums/r161/crimesaucer/Screenshot-88-1.png)
[IMG]http://i144.photobucket.com/albums/r161/crimesaucer/Screenshot-88-2.png[/IMG]


All I did was copy the "Fe -mail" code, then paste it into gedit. Then using Agave to find the colors of different parts of her skin, I would then "Search and Replace" it with the colors I wanted to change it to.


You could probably make it the exact colors of Ubuntu.
