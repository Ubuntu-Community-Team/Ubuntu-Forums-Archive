### Post by peterbrowne on 2005-10-29
I'm currently doing this to mine, when I have done it I'll post pics and instructions, but I think you'll need:

[LIST]
[*]Casing of your phone
[*]Brown Paint
[*]Yellow Paint
[*]Orange Paint
[*]Red Paint
[/LIST]

Simply remove the casing, Paint the outer side of the casing in Brown, now let it dry.  Print The image [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuArtwork?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=UbuntuLogo.png](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuArtwork?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=UbuntuLogo.png) on a transparancy, now trace it onto the phone using an X-Acto knife to cut through the plastic transparancy and through the paint you applied.  You now have the Ubuntu logo traced onto the phone.  Simply apply the remaining paint using the knife lines as your guide.
