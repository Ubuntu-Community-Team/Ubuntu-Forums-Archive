### Post by Lux Perpetua on 2005-09-05
Don't worry, there are TONS of OS X lookalike Gnome themes. art.gnome.org also has some, including ports of third-party OS X themes and themes inspired by the OS X look. Here are a few:

[http://art.gnome.org/themes/gtk2/615](http://art.gnome.org/themes/gtk2/615) - Aluminum Alloy Volcanic
[http://art.gnome.org/themes/gtk2/571](http://art.gnome.org/themes/gtk2/571) - Glossy P (beautiful)
[http://art.gnome.org/themes/gtk2/567](http://art.gnome.org/themes/gtk2/567) - Milk 2.0 (also beautiful, but has display bugs on progress bars)

You can also find OS X GDM themes there if you want.
