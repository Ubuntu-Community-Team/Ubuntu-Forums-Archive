### Post by rsambuca on 2007-05-15
Hi,

1. Sorry, I have no idea how to restore defaults.  I have changed mine so many times now that I have no idea what the defaults were, either!

2.  To move any window in ubuntu, just move your mouse cursor over the window, then press 'Alt' and hold down the left mouse button while you move the window wherever you want.  This way, you don't have to be able to see the top title bar to move a window.  One of the more handy features.

3.  If you open up the Synaptic Package Manager (System -> Administration -> Synaptic Package Manager), look for gimp-helpbrowser and gimp-help-common and click install, then apply.

Good luck!
