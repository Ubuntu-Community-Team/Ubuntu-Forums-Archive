### Post by Finalfantasykid on 2010-06-13
That is the way it is supposed to look.  If you are doing graphics design you want to see an accurate representation of how the image looks.  If you are editing something which uses a lossy scaling algorithm, you are not actually modifying the correct pixels, but rather interpolated ones.  Photoshop does it this way as well, and is the correct way to do it for this type of program.
