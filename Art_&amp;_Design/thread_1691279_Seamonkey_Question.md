### Post by Dry Lips on 2011-02-19
When you open the Seamonkey browser, select “composer” under “windows”.

...Or you could go to System>Preferences>Main menu. 
Uncheck Seamonkey and Seamonkey Navigator, 
(under Internet) and check Seamonkey Composer 
(under programming), if you just want to use the composer.
