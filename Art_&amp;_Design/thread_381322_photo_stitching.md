### Post by noen on 2007-03-12
Take a look at [AutoPano]("http://autopano.kolor.com/")
> 
It takes a folder and searches it for image files. By analysing the pictures, it attempts to divide them into panorama sets. The output will be the Panotools projects for actually stitching them.

It's free for non commercial work. You'll need to convert the archive:

[autopano_1_02b_linux_binary.tar.gz ]("http://autopano.kolor.com/autopano_1_02b_linux_binary.tar.gz")

Into a deb archive. There are instructions on how to do this.
