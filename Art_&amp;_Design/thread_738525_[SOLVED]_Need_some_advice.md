### Post by ph1 on 2008-03-28
Here's what I would do (just so you can get some ideas, none of these is being forced upon you):

Put the UN either in all caps or in all lowercase to match.  
Center the "main part" of the letters, e.g. the centerline of letters like e, a, or o with the globe, it'll probably look more symmetric.
Soften the letters' outline, or give them a reflection too.

Otherwise, looks fantastic!  Quite impressive, very clean and sophisticated.
