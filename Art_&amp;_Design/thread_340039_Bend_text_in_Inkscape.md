### Post by Madpilot on 2007-01-17
Just for future reference, Inkscape's own tutorials files are excellent - and written in SVG, so you can dissect them to see how things are done! They're at Help->Tutorials inside Inkscape.

Also, there's an awesome Inkscape guide online - [http://tavmjong.free.fr/INKSCAPE/](http://tavmjong.free.fr/INKSCAPE/)

And the status bar in Inkscape is great too - unlike a lot of programs, Inkscape actually gives (usually) *useful* info in it's status bar!
