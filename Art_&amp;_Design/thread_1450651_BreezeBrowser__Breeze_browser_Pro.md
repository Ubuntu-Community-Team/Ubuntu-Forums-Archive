### Post by tgalati4 on 2010-04-09
Do a google search on photography workflow in linux and you will find a few blogs on what works and what doesn't.  It's a moving target.  There seems to be more focus on integrating photography with social networks rather than on improving photography.

When I was on windows (years ago), I used BB with the Canon cameras that I was using at the time.

It's nice, but in linux, my thinking has changed and now things like creating photo CD's and slideshows are more important than tweaking raw images.  I use hugin for photostiching with cheapo powershots and hdr landscapes.

With the move to more open formats for media, proprietary RAW formats are taking a backseat to standardized JPG.  If you need more information, take multiple shots of the same scene and use hdr processing.
