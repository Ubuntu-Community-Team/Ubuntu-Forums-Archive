### Post by gnuman on 2012-05-11
Hi Sableyes,

Nice work!

One think I like to do when I'm adding the finishing touch to a wallpaper is to add the color last.  One really easy way to do this in Gimp:
[LIST=1]
[*]Create a new transparent layer over the image
[*]Choose a foreground color (I choose brown for this)
[*]Fill the new layer with that color (will hide your image)
[*]Change the layer property to "overlay"
[*]Adjust opacity and tint of color layer as desired
[/LIST]
