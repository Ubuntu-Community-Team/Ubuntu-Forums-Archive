### Post by shafin on 2009-10-29
Get the theme on incoming wiki for ubuntu LL. Then you need to contact ubuntu-art mailing list about inclusion of the theme in community themes package. If it becomes quite popular, hopefully in MM it will be included by default. This is what happened to dust/new wave, and is happening to impression etc.
