After I have asked for permisson ( spelled right ? ) I have set up an mypaint forum there we can share art and talk art. I/we are looking for someone who can be forum admin, since I dont fit like an forum admin.

MyPaint is a fast painting program with focus on brushes. It supports graphic tablets and has an easy GUI for those who just want to paint.

Right now, mypaint dont have a layer system or neither undo, but undo is in the subversion, and will come this month! And maybe a layer system is coming in the future - it is alot of work to make a layer system.

Mypaint is an project by Martin Renold, and his site can be found here: [http://people.ee.ethz.ch/~mrenold/mypaint/](http://people.ee.ethz.ch/~mrenold/mypaint/)

Project site here: [https://gna.org/projects/mypaint](https://gna.org/projects/mypaint)
and a mailinglist here: [https://mail.gna.org/public/mypaint-discuss/2007-05/threads.html](https://mail.gna.org/public/mypaint-discuss/2007-05/threads.html)

And the **mypaint forum** can be found here: [http://mypaint.digital-brush.org/](http://mypaint.digital-brush.org/)

I hope you guys wanna use those forums, and have fun, hehe :P
