### Post by uuwatti on 2007-01-26
Blender = hard to learn, but free. Maya has a Linux version, just search forums for installation instructions if you decide to buy it. Maya is also hard to learn. Then there is k-4d, which is also free. And Houdini and Softimage XSI, both of which are proprietary. (XSI is quite cheap however USD 495.)  [Wings3D]("http://www.wings3d.com") is a beginner-orientated modeling tool. (maybe a bit too simple) Facegen equivalent i know nothing about, ask [blenderartists forums]("http://blenderartists.org/forum"). Maybe they know some program.
