### Post by projectreina on 2008-11-05
Hey, is anyone interested in designing a shirt for a celebrity tshirt line? If you're artsy and creative, you should check it out.

Gina Ravera (of NBC's ER and TNT's THE CLOSER) is launching a national VIP Special Edition T-shirt Design Contest hosted by non-profit organization "CommonThreadz" to benefit her non-profit organization "Project Reina."

CommonThreadz and Ravera are looking for a special-edition T-shirt design to celebrate Gina's charity, Project Reina - a health and pro-wellness campaign created to empower young women and girls 13-24, especially those hardest hit in the US by the HIV/AIDS pandemic: African Americans and Latinas. Anyone is invited to submit ideas for a great t-shirt design that reflects the cause of Project Reina.

The winning design will be printed as a SPECIAL EDITION T-SHIRT line and sold online and in select stores. While proceeds of each shirt sold will go directly to Project Reina, CommonThreadz will give a school uniform to an orphan or vulnerable child in Africa for each shirt sold. In addition to having the design printed on the line, the winner of the design competition will also receive many other prizes and recognition.

To submit a design and for details about the t-shirt design contest visit:
[http://www.commonthreadz.org/contest](http://www.commonthreadz.org/contest)

Best of luck to you! And hope to see the winning design in select stores soon!

Project Reina
