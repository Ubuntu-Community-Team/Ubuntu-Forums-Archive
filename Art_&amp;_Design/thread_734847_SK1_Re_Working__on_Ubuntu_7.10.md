### Post by mad4dweb on 2008-03-25
Finally i have SK1_Re working
Install sK1  on Ubuntu 7.10

REMEMBER SK1 WORKS ONLY WITH KDE

Access synaptic and add this packages with their dependencies

tcl8.4-dev
tk8.4-dev
libpng12-dev
libtk-img
libcairo-ocaml-dev
imagemagick
zlib-dev 
libglitz1-dev
libxrender
liblcms1-dev
g++

I recomend you REBOOT and then Enter the sK1_RE directory and do

sudo ./install.sh

Things should work now, it took me 8 hours playing around and reading how to get sK1_RE working on my Ubuntu 7.10, so i hope it helps any one of you.
