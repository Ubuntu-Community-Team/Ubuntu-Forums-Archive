### Post by maruchan on 2007-04-18
Nice job! Overall I like the effect. It's easy to get a quick idea of what your blog is about with a header like this. Two points I would address:

1) Fonts: The letter "n" in "Corner" is looking pretty bad in that font, as if someone sat down on it. The font below it looks like Comic Sans, which in some areas will get you arrested. :) [http://lmnop.blogs.com/lauren/2006/10/americas_most_f.html](http://lmnop.blogs.com/lauren/2006/10/americas_most_f.html)

2) Gradient: The contrast in the background gradient is pretty heavy. Check out this tutorial. [http://9rules.com/blog/2006/08/a-gradient-tutorial/](http://9rules.com/blog/2006/08/a-gradient-tutorial/)

A gradient is not the ideal tool for displaying information hierarchy, so I would suggest making the gradient much more subtle and altering the transparency of the logotype at left in order to make the most important message stand out more.

Thanks for sharing your work - nice job of it.
