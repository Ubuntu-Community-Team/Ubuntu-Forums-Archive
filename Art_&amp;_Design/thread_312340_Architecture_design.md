### Post by Aldrik on 2006-12-07
[Put them together]("http://blenderartists.org/forum/showthread.php?t=78688") and you'll have something quite powerfull (also check the procad blender script). ;)

[SagCAD]("http://sagcad.sourceforge.jp"), [Archimedes]("http://archimedes.incubadora.fapesp.br/portal") & [gCAD3D]("http://www.cadcam.co.at/freiter/gCAD3D_en.htm") are all FOSS (I haven't used em) so give them a try.

Also [CYCAS]("http://www.cycas.de/") is free for non-commercial use.
