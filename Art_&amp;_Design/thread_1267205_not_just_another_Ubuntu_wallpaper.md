### Post by Fzang on 2009-09-15
I'm not trying to sound like an idiot but feel like I must point out a few things which would not be approved by higher standards...

1. The Ubuntu logo looks blurry around the edges, while having a sharp texture in the middle. This makes no sense and only serves to confuse the eye. The edges around the logo should be way sharper.

2. Actually... the whole object looks kind of blurry around the edges :S

3. Those small gears to the right of the logo are WAY too sharp and shiny for the rest of the image and really stand out - but not in a good way. They should share the same colors and elements as the rest of the object.

4. I don't know about you but I don't like the colors. They're not really anything. A mixture of rot green and orange gives... rotten orange? It doesn't really trigger a feeling of anything... besides mismatched colors..
 
All in all: Needs serious work on bevel techniques, color choice and especially sharpness. The whole image is blurred. And it looks like it was made 15 years ago.

/end harsh critique
