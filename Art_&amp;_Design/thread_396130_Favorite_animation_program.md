### Post by gzed on 2007-04-09
K-3D:    [http://www.k3d.org](http://www.k3d.org)

Architecturally much more advanced than blender, supports pluggable-everything from the render engine to the scripting language/shading models/GUI etc. Much closer to Maya than Blender architecturally-speaking. It currently and primarly supports RenderMan engines (RenderMan is an industry 3D standard used in most production movies), but also has plugins for Yafray and PovRay(which I happen to dislike).

It's younger than Blender but it's progressing nicely. Also the K-3D team recently got accepted as a google SOC mentor. Good news! :)
