### Post by darksideforge on 2009-02-24
I'm a total noob at this. I installed Jaunty Beta 4 last night and then decided to download some themes from gnome-look.org and kde-look.org.

I downloaded the Emerald manager and some Emerald themes, created a folder in my documents folder, unzipped the packages into their own folders, transferred them into Emerald....now what?

System>>Preferences>>Emerald Theme Manager brings up the Themer with the 5 themes that I downloaded...but how do I pick one and get it to take effect?

Do I need to be running Kubuntu 9.04(b4) instead of Ubuntu 9.04(b4) ??

---

### Post by smartboyathome on 2009-02-24
You need to make sure you are running Compiz, then run alt+f2 and "emerald --replace".

---

### Post by sambita on 2009-02-24
You have to do what smartboyathome said, also if you want the emerald theme to be there every time you turn your computer you need to add the "emerald --replace" command
to System-Preferences-Sessions. At least thats how i do it.
