### Post by Jay Car on 2010-09-05
You can download sofachrome [here]("http://www.1001fonts.com/font_details.html?font_id=1166") for free.

It's just a Windows true type font. My own experience has been that Inkscape handles true type fonts pretty well. 

[edit] Regarding the "out of date" comment, I have font collections that are nearly 15 years old, they still work fine in all my systems. Some of them seem old fashioned these days, but they still work fine. Fonts are fonts. Just have to install them to use them.
