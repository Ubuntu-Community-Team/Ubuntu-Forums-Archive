### Post by maruchan on 2006-12-19
Manslide is a Qt app but it might do what you want:

[http://kde-apps.org/content/show.php?content=46558](http://kde-apps.org/content/show.php?content=46558)

The idea is, you import photos, assign effects/transitions, add audio, and it makes a video slideshow out of it, saving the video to your project folder. Not bad if you want to upload to a video site like Youtube or just email a video.

Have a look here too: [http://www.google.com/search?q=slideshow+site%3Alinuxlinks.com](http://www.google.com/search?q=slideshow+site%3Alinuxlinks.com)
