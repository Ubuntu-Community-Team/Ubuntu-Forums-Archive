Actually, the contemporary Santa Claus figure, with the red and white outfit was a creation of the Coca-Cola company's advertising back in the first half of the 1900's.  Prior to that he was a sort of woodsman/elf type guy.

The association between the specific shade of red that Coca-Cola uses and used when they created the modern Santa, and the colors in the rather nice wallpaper is probably what triggered you to make the association.

Rather troubling, isn't it, how corporations can almost forever change our culture with their advertising and efforts at establishing logos. colors, images, etc. in our cultural psyche?  All the more reason to ditch microsoft and other corporate giants.
