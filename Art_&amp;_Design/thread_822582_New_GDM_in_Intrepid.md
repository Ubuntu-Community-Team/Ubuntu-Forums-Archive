### Post by Chokkan on 2008-06-10
OK, glad I wasn't the only one. Someone asked the same question that was on my mind.

What's with all the faces sliding around?

Quote:
It&#8217;s me triggering (via keystrokes) removal and adding of list-entries to test the layout-algorithm and implicit animation. For the reason behind this see the spec of the gdm-face-browser at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DesktopTeam/Specs/GdmFaceBrowser](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DesktopTeam/Specs/GdmFaceBrowser) The &#8220;chaotic&#8221; arrangement is just an extreme example to expose the visual artefacts of the missing mipmapping. It has nothing to do with the final behaviour of the face-browser. Besides this is still very early code and much of it is just exercising clutters API.
