### Post by Nevon on 2009-08-09
As a response to [this thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1234984) I created a website where I display 4 different "Made on Ubuntu" badges to be placed on websites made by Ubuntu users. The background on this is that a certain web page builder application in the Mac iLife suite automatically inserts this "Made on a Mac" badge in the footer of every website created with it.

Now I'm interested in hearing your thoughts on the website and the badges. I didn't spend a crazy amount of time on it, and I'm still not sure what I think about some of the elements. But nevertheless, constructive criticism is always good. 

I would also like to hear your ideas for features, or your thoughts on the concept itself.

Anyway, here's the site:
[http://www.blastfromthepast.se/ubuntubadges](http://www.blastfromthepast.se/ubuntubadges)
