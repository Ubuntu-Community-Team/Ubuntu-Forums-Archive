I don't currently have a functioning printer, and I didnt want to put Ubuntu and Kubuntu in plain jane cases. So i hand drew logos on each side on a piece of paper. 

It is a PS2 video game case, what i did was take an 8.5" x 11" piece of paper, trimmed it to 10 3/4" x  7 1/4", and began the project.
I haven't written the text "Ubuntu" or "Kubuntu" at the bottom yet, because i dont want to screw up again lol.

Sorry if this isn't the right forum, i'm new :P

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

First, with a pencil, a traced a circle, then traced a smaller circle inside. Then i erased some space on 3 different corners, and then tied the ends together. Next i outlined it with a black pen, then colored each segment in with colored pencils.
