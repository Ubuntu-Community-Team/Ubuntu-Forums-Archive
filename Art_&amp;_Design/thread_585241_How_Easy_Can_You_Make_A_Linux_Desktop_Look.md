### Post by rzrgenesys187 on 2007-11-07
> **sistoviejo said:**
> how do you change the icon for the gnome menu?

In the terminal type gconf-editor.  apps >> panel >> objects.  Then scroll down through the objects until you find one who's object_type is a menu-object.  In this object, at the bottom check the use_custom_icon checkbox.  Scroll back up and where it says custom_icon, type the location of the icon you want to the right of custom_icon.
