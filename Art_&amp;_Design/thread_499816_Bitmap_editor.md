### Post by skullmunky on 2011-09-01
Sorry to bump this oldish thread, but I'm just so excited about mtPaint that I wanted to mention a few of its features for pixel art:

- reference image tracing: you can load an image to use as a reference, and have it in the background at full resolution while you're editing your low-res image zoomed in.  

- tablet support 

- patterns, smart dithering, and good palette management

- layers and animation

- lots of helpful keyboard shortcuts

loads more, that's just what i've figured out in the first 10 minutes playing with it.
