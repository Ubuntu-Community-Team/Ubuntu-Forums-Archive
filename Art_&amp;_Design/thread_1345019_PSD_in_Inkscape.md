A file can go from Photshop to Illustrator, but only very limited capacity.  Last I checked Photoshop cannot save to svg. The vast majority of tools/filters in Photoshop are unavailable in Illustrator and vice versa.

As said before PS/GIMP are raster editors Illustrator/Inkscape are vector editors.
