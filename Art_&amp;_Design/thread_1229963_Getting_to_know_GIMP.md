### Post by RoselleChris on 2009-08-02
Greetings:

I am new to Ubuntu and am trying my hand at image editing with Gimp.  

I want to remove the Toolbox from the window once I choose a tool.  However, when I try to click on the "X" at the top of the Toolbox, the whole application closes.  Is there a way to minimize the Toolbox?

Thanks,

---

### Post by hansdown on 2009-08-02
Hi RoselleChris.

Try this.

Edit ->Preferences -> Window Management -> Hint for the toolbox -> change
to 'Normal window' -> click OK

You must restart Gimp for the 'minimize' button to appear in the toolbox.
