### Post by K.Mandla on 2007-09-15
Ubuntulite has resurfaced after a long period of dormancy, this time as an Openbox and XDM-based deb package, installable through aptitude.

The project needs a unique look, which would probably included unified wallpaper in a variety of sizes, Openbox themes (both 3.3.1 and 3.4.4; I don't recall if they're interchangeable) and complementary GTK1 and GTK2 themes. Take a look at the project group site, for an idea of the color scheme that's under way.

[http://groups.google.com/group/ubuntulite](http://groups.google.com/group/ubuntulite)

You can contact Shae, the group leader, through that site. I'm sure Shae would be delighted to get as much help as possible. :)
