### Post by eveningsky339 on 2010-07-20
I'd like to hear some thoughts on Canonical's rebranding of the Ubuntu logo.

I understand that a lot of people complained about Karmic's (among others) "poop brown" color scheme.  However, I enjoyed the earthy colors.  I also liked how the Ubuntu symbol was broken into three colors-- it gave the impression of three people holding hands, which I believe is what Ubuntu was originally going for.

With Lucid, the earthy color scheme seems to be gone.  The desktop background didn't mesh with the gnome panels.  This can obviously changed via System > Preferences, but it doesn't serve well for first impressions.  The logo is entirely white and is downlplayed against UBUNTU in large letters.  Can't say I'm a fan of the orange surrounding the logo either.

I found your questions interesting. I would like to hear some explanations to, if I'm not asking to much ;-)... because I also like old logo a lot.
