### Post by gemmahenchmen on 2011-04-04
Hey everyone, I'm the programmer for a new open-source FPS game in development (called [Frozen]("http://frozenproject.org"), click link for more info). We're now at a stage where it would be useful to have community-produced 3d models, concept art, textures, map designs etc. The theme for the game is a post-apocalyptic world where the planet has frozen over, so the settings in the final game will be primarily snowy. At the moment our main level isn't as it is based primarily on placeholder models, but the idea of the ruined buildings is the general feel we're going for. 

Practically any ideas or art will be helpful, but the first thing we need is concept art. We're at an early stage of development art-wise so now is your chance to have a lasting effect on the project.

Our site is frozenproject.org, the game can be downloaded from [http://code.google.com/p/frozenproject/source/browse/trunk/test/Frozen-0.2.0.jar](http://code.google.com/p/frozenproject/source/browse/trunk/test/Frozen-0.2.0.jar). We also have another ubuntu forums thread about this game for more general contributions: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1663683](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1663683)
