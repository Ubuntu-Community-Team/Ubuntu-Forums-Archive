Rather look at Font Squirrel or Fawnt for quality fonts that are freely available. I tend to find fonts from DaFont unrefined at times. Most of the time you end up converting the text to paths and fixing stray nodes and curves. :P

Also it seems like you just traced bitmaps for the font names. I don't remember Comic Sans being that edgy and doesn't Papyrus have more of a broken feel around the edges?
