### Post by eightmillion on 2008-11-09
Try going to Image > Guides > New Guide (by Percent) and adding a horizontal guide and vertical guide at 50%. Then your selections will snap to the guides.

### Post by aaronp on 2008-11-11
You can try using the align tool (next to the move tool)

You choose what you want to align and then from the drop down you choose what you want to align it against (entire image, selected layer etc.)
Then use the 'arrow buttons' to center it, align it top, bottom, left, right etc. etc.

hope this helps
Aaron

### Post by sbarre on 2012-09-22
Hi,

there is another quick way to do do a pure "center item in image":
-select your item in whatever way you like
-type: ctrl-x, ctrl-v, ctrl-x,ctrl-v (yes, twice cut-paste)

=>The first cut-paste will put the item back on the same place, the second one will put it on the center of the image (I'm not sure exactly why but happened to see that accidentally).
