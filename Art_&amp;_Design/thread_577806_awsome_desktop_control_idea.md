### Post by Merk42 on 2007-10-16
While the game itself is an incredible accomplishment, I don't see how that could be used for any sort of UI.

You say rotate the cube of the multiple desktops?  I don't see how rotating a small cube that must be in front of a camera (which most people have on top of their monitors) would be a better idea than the way it's navigable now.

The point of the game was combining real world visual with artificial ones. Something PlayStation's EyeToy has done in the past.  If the cube were used to change desktops, you'd want the cube to take up the full view of the camera, and therefore lose the real world visuals.
