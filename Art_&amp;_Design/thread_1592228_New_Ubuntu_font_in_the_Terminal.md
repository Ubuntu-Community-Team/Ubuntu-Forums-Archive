### Post by clockworkpc on 2010-10-10
> **dwxvi said:**
> Didn't seem like it. But the terminal uses it anyway, and I need to fix it. xD

It's pretty simple to fix:

Go to System > Preferences > Appearance
Then, the Fonts tab (between Background and Visual Effects)
Change the "fixed width font" to **Monospace**.
