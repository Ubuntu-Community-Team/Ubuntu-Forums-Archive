### Post by VitalBodies on 2010-01-14
VitalBodies did a blog post on "Getting MakeHuman Up And Running In Ubuntu" in support of the MakeHuman and the Ubuntu community. MakeHuman allows you to quickly create 3d human that you can render in AQSIS or export to Blender. Also details how to get AQSIS going for MakeHuman. MakeHuman is a bit like Poser or Daz Studio in that you can create a 3d human without being a total 3d pro. 

[http://www.vitalbodies.com/blog/2010/01/09/getting-makehuman-to-run-in-ubuntu/]("http://www.vitalbodies.com/blog/2010/01/09/getting-makehuman-to-run-in-ubuntu/")
