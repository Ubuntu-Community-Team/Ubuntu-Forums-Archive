### Post by 23dornot23d on 2010-02-13
Blender is probably ..... the best ....

But wings3D is good too ...... for doing the quick rough basics .....
when I work in 3D .... I start in Wings 3D rough things out
then transfer to Blender as 3Ds files or Obj files .... both read in ok
and Wings3D I find is a much easier environment to start working in
Blender is very complex and is very powerful ... it can be a bit awkward
to start designing in ..... but its whatever suits you .... have a look at
the videos first ......

It depends on how detailed you want to go with it ....... and what you
mean by landscape design ..... if its for doing gardens .... etc ... 

This is not what you want .....

Just so you know what I mean by landscape design here is a quick
video of one done in Blender ......

[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k1oo0orJXow](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k1oo0orJXow)
