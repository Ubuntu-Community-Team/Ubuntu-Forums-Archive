### Post by alex.rayu on 2008-11-15
A few things which I use in Photoshop all the time and that I absolutely miss in Gimp are:
1. Clipping layers. I can draw a shape in Photoshop and clip other layers on it without actually editing the layers, just with ctrl+G.
2. Shapes and fonts. For some reason, they get rasterized in GIMP, become uneditable.
3. Layer effects. Missing! You can apply an effect to a selected area or layer in GIMP but then you can't edit or disable it!
4. Photoshop Format. It's not supported well enough. PHotoshop is a standard in web design, so if I get a mockup to edit or slice and dice and I open that PSD file in GIMP if can't neither edit nor clice it there. It looks different.

So no, absolutely not. GIMP does not do.
