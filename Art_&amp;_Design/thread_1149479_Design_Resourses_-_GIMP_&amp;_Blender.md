### Post by digitalis_vulgaris on 2009-05-05
On this link you can take some unique graphic design resources for GIMP & Blender. They are completly free for download, use or modifing without any restrictions.

admiror-design-studio-gimp-tempera.zip is animated brush for GIMP with support for graphic tablet.

I especially recommend admiror-design-studio-blender-sin-city.zip, material for Blender which produce renders like Frank Miller ink artwork. Even have nice contour around objects.

As soon as possible I'll add some more resources. Any new content I'll notify in this topic.

Enjoy GIMPing & Blending...
