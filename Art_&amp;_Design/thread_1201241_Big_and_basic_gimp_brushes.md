### Post by cubeist on 2009-07-01
It took me a while to find this in the Gimp...At the bottom of the brush menu, you can either edit and existing brush, or create a new brush.  If you select new brush it will open a dialog with the available size/hardness/angle/feathering options.  The largest you can make a brush in the Gimp is 1000px wide.

For brushes others have made, you can find lots on deviantart.com, just search for gimp brushes.
