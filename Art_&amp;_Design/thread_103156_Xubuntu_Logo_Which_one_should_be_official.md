### Post by Gadren on 2005-12-13
I've seen several logo designs for Xubuntu at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/XubuntuArtwork](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/XubuntuArtwork) , but was one specific one ever chosen to be the official logo?

I was just wondering -- which ones do you all like?  Here are the current logos made:

**First design:**
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/XubuntuArtwork?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=xubuntu_logo.png](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/XubuntuArtwork?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=xubuntu_logo.png)

It also has an animated version...
[http://lehrer.schule.at/goell-pregesbauer//wp-content/xubuntu_logo_suggestion_3.gif](http://lehrer.schule.at/goell-pregesbauer//wp-content/xubuntu_logo_suggestion_3.gif)

While the running mouse is a fun idea, the color scheme is, in my opinion, too close to Kubuntu's, and I think that it should be more distinctive.  Also, at small resolutions, the mouse becomes hard to discern.



**Viper 550's idea:**
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/XubuntuArtwork?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=xubuntulogo2.png](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/XubuntuArtwork?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=xubuntulogo2.png)

The color scheme is more distinctive than the first one, but it seems dull to me.  While the X's are a good idea, they are so small and thin that they disappear at low resolutions, and they just aren't noticeable, I say.  At small sizes, this logo ends up being nothing more than a greyscale version of the original Ubuntu logo.


**AbelChiaro's idea:**
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/XubuntuArtwork?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=xubuntumice.png](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/XubuntuArtwork?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=xubuntumice.png)

I personally don't like the mouse-head implementation, since it seems to lessen from the "humanity" point of *ubuntu, and the mouse face details are too complex and run into each other at small sizes.  It also feels a bit too "cutesy."


This is my favorite:
**Derick's idea:**
[http://penguin.agrid.usm.edu/~deisenhardt/xubuntu_x-circle.png](http://penguin.agrid.usm.edu/~deisenhardt/xubuntu_x-circle.png)

This one has the best features, I say.  It has a distinctive color scheme that seperates it from the other *ubuntus.  It is simple, easy to recognize, connected to the other *ubuntu logos shape-wise, and scalable.

**EDIT:** I thought that I would, as a matter of comparison, show each logo at 1/8th their original size, to see how they look when used in various ways.  In fact, they may even be smaller with some (for example, the Ubuntu "start button" is smaller than the images here):

http://img489.imageshack.us/img489/7498/xubuntulogosmall1bh.png[/img][img]http://img381.imageshack.us/img381/4226/xubuntulogo2small0lj.png[/img][img]http://img381.imageshack.us/img381/2599/xubuntumicesmall0wm.png[/img][img]http://img381.imageshack.us/img381/9513/xubuntuxcirclesmall7ue.png

What are your thoughts?  Which logo is best?
