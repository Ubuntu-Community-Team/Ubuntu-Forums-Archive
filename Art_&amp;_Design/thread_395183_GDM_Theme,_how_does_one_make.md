### Post by adam.tropics on 2007-03-28
Download a couple you like, extract them (they are just .tar.gz archives) and have a look inside. They are not too complicated. I would however suggest you may want to test them out in a nested login window before committing! (Applications-->System Tools-->New login in a nested window)

### Post by graabein on 2007-04-05
I've just uploaded [my first creation]("http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/XfceCarlos?content=55740") to gnome-look.org. I'm so proud. Anxiously awaiting feedback and watching the download counter. 

I wanted a GDM for Xubuntu that didn't say *GNOME desktop* so I took a picture of a cat I was taking care of at the time and wrote up a quick mod of a Novell GDM theme.

Take a look at this guide:
