### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-04-18
Make sure you are in RGB mode. To check go to Image>Mode.

After you have done that go to Colors>Color to Alpha. A window will pop up and just make sure you have the color white selected. Once you click OK, all of the white in the picture will turn transparent.
