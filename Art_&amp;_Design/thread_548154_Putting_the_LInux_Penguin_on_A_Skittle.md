### Post by kayosiii on 2007-09-13
There are lots of ways that you might attempt this...

I would try perhaps in the gimp
making sure the image had an alpha channel (can be transparent)
desaturating the image (colours menu) Inverting if the blacks are in the penguin. Then using colour to alpha on the blacks to remove them from the image....
Hope this helps.
