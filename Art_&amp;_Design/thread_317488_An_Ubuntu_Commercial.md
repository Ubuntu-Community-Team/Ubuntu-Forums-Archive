### Post by Esben Kramer on 2006-12-12
What I would propose, is a very simple thing. A guys walks into the picture. The background is brown (of course), and he starts talking. He very briefly explains about open source, and how a thing like Ubuntu can exist. Probably mentions open standards and other things like that. The camera moves away and above him, very slowly as he talks, and finally it zooms all the way out, and you see him from directly above, standing in the middle of the Ubuntu GUI, and the cursor, which is doing something useful, like sending a message in Gaim or saving a document in a word-processor, and just as he says something like "switch to Ubuntu.", it comes over and closes him with a nice Beryl-effect. Turn the cube slowly and nicely to the top of it, where it says something like 'Tired of people telling you how to use your computer? -Ubuntu'

There are many ways to do it, but it should, at the very least, include a visible and human narrator. It may be best to use a woman.
