### Post by smartboyathome on 2007-12-31
This site has helped me out a lot:
[http://www.gimpusers.com/](http://www.gimpusers.com/)

Also, to make transparent images, make a new image, and instead of pushing ok, select Advanced Options, and click the drop down menu with Background Color and select Transparency. The best file format to save with in GIMP is XCF, as that is the native format and will save everything.
