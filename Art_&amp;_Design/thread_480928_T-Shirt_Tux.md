### Post by red_team316 on 2007-06-21
I made a high resolution version of the tux from the cystal style tux. The guy at [www.tuxntosh.com](www.tuxntosh.com) (great website lol) kind of inspired me to make it as well as looking for a suitable high resolution version that could be printed on a t-shirt. It's so high res that you could probably make a decent banner too if you have a or access to a plotter. Figured I'd post it for others benefit ;)

[www.avery.com](www.avery.com) makes t-shirt transfers, you should be able to find them or similar product at any local store. 

[http://img.photobucket.com/albums/v77/red_team316/linux/Tshirt_Tux.png](http://img.photobucket.com/albums/v77/red_team316/linux/Tshirt_Tux.png)

Enjoy.
