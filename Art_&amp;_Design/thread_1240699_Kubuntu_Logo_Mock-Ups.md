### Post by fatbuttlarry on 2009-08-15
Ok, I've been wanting to do this for a while...

Basically, when I look at the Kubuntu logo, the three "8-tooth" gears on the logo look like small snowflakes to me.

[IMG]http://img219.imageshack.us/img219/3527/kubuntulogo.jpg[/IMG]

Now... When looking at the KDE logo, I feel the gear is more "iconified" and less... well... complicated.  For some reason I've always felt the KDE logo doesn't seem to be portrayed well by simply replacing the ubuntu circles with small gears.

[IMG]http://img219.imageshack.us/img219/8575/kde4logo.png[/IMG]

So, I did some work on the laptop using a live cd and Gimp.  I've attached 9 mock-up logos hoping for feedback.  Click the links below to see each mock-up.

Cheers.
