It is odd that that option is only available buried in the gconf-editor-like global options dialog. You could always file a bug, tracked on [Freedesktop.org]("https://bugs.freedesktop.org/buglist.cgi?quicksearch=libreoffice"). There's a bit of weirdness, though, in that the colors you're selecting there aren't just for Writer, but for the whole suite, so changing them within one would affect all others.

If anyone knows a better way of handling this, I'm curious - after two years of depending on OOo for every professional and academic thing I've done, I'd love to know where to make suggestions about the interface as well. I have rather a long list.
