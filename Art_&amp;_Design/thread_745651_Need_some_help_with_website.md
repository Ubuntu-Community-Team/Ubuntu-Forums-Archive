### Post by st0n3cutt3r on 2008-04-05
I have a couple of ideas for you, although I agree with hyperkill on the suggestion to dump them if you dislike them as much as you do.

However, if you choose to keep them, I would use an image similar to [http://www.msu.edu/~heslipst/contents/ANP440/images/Zafarraya_mandible.jpg](http://www.msu.edu/~heslipst/contents/ANP440/images/Zafarraya_mandible.jpg) for your logo for the mandible site, however I would change the angle of it (probably have to have a custom photo taken or have one created in photoshop) so that you saw it more from the bottom, as if you were holding the bone as seen in the photo, and then rotated it away from you (front up, back down) until the near side was more-or-less straight, and the far side arced downward.

Sorry if that's a bit hard to follow, moving on though...

If you want to get away from the 'cursed' element of 'accursed' I would just break it into 3 words  a-(c)cur-sed  = a [cur]("http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/cur") said, and have your logo a cowardly man with a word bubble....   maybe have the first C have a big hand-painted-looking X across it, or a thick line through it or something.  It would be a little cheesy, and a disappointment to anyone looking for a site about cursed things, but it could be fun too, and would definitely get away from the cursed feel.

*shrug* that's my $0.02.  Hope it helps!
