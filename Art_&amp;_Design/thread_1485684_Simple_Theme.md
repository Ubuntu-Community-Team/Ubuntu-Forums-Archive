### Post by tcelgen on 2010-05-27
There is no easy way to create themes, there is no GUI program for making themes in GNOME (as far as I know). Furthermore themes in GNOME are divided into parts, there is the **metacity theme**, the **gtk theme** and the **icon theme**.

_**Metacity themes**_ are not that hard, download a theme and look at all the files you will find config files and sliced up images.

_**GTK themes**_ are more difficult because they depend on which engine to render the theme is being used. Also depends if you want to modify a GTK theme completely or just to change some colors.

_**Icon themes**_ are easy but require a lot of work, download a complete icon theme take a look at how it is setup. Also keep in my the system links, many icons are linked all over the place which can be confusing.

Here is a pretty decent guide to edit Metacity themes - [http://developer.gnome.org/doc/tutorials/metacity/metacity-themes.html](http://developer.gnome.org/doc/tutorials/metacity/metacity-themes.html)
