Besides the official Canonical contribution of artwork to Karmic there will be community submissions as well. Although not installed by default they will be available via Synaptic as "community-themes".

The entire portfolio may be viewed here.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/Karmic](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/Karmic)

I would encourage you to test some of the themes such as Night Impression.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/Karmic/NightImpression](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/Karmic/NightImpression)

This is Ubuntu with an emphasis on community.

Cheers!
