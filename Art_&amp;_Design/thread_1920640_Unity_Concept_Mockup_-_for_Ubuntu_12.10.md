### Post by nq6 on 2012-02-05
I made a video with a dynamic mockup showing my ideas, which include the reformulation of the home of the dash, two new lenses, one of "social network widgets +" and a configuration of Unity. Besides an improvement suggestion for the lens of music and files and folders. 

On the new home of the dash, in my view the most interesting is having a practical option to access the applications with a few clicks, because we have the categories of applications in a practical way "as the old menu was gnome2"
