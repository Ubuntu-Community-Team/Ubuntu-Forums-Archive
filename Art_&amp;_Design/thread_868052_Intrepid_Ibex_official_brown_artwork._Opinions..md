### Post by Colonel Kilkenny on 2008-07-23
There's this thing called usability which should prevent using dark color schemes. I have nothing against "dark" theme (like Vista has) but there really is a reason why Windows & MacOS aren't as dark as that preview theme for Ibex is. Imho, too dark themes will never work (ok, few geeks here and there might think totally black desktop is cool but their eyes are already doomed).

Imho, art team should focus more on small things like panel (it's too flat atm) and fonts (which have too much contrast and could use some sort of smoothing) instead of trying too hard to change the whole color scheme. There's nothing wrong with light colors. It's the little things which make Windows and MacOS look as "quality package".

edit. Also, I can't understand why Tango icons are apparently out of the question.
