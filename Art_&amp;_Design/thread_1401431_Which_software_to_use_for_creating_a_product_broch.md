There's a bit of a learning curve for the more advanced features (OK, that may be understated ;) ), but it's a great application... one of those where you say "How can this be free?"

### Post by Onesimus on 2010-02-08
I used a combination of OOo Writer and GIMP (see [leaflet-web.pdf]("http://www.bewdleyparish.org.uk/parish/Ribbesford%20leaflet-web.pdf")).  

Things I learnt during the process:
[LIST=1]
[*]Contact the printers early on.
[*]Professional printers may not be able to accommodate transparency in PDF documents (I had to export from OOo Writer to PDF, then import PDF into GIMP and then export to JPEG).
[*]Printers require a 'bleed-margin' around the document (see [here]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bleed_%28printing%29"))
[/LIST]

Hope this helps
