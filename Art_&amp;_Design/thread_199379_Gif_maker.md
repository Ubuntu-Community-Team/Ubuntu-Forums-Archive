### Post by DirtDawg on 2006-06-19
Actually, you can make a multi-layered image in GIMP. Then when you "save as..." a gif file, it gives you the option to save as an animated gif where each layer is a frame!

When I stumbled onto this for the first time and realized how incredibly easy it was, I almost poo'ed myself. I spent countless hours trying to figure out Adobe's ImageReady to no avail.

One of the many small touches that makes GIMP so nice nice nice. :cool:
