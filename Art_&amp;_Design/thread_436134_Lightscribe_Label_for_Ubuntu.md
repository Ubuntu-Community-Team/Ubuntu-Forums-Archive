### Post by McNee on 2007-12-10
If you want to do some of your own CD lables (I'm working on one for edubuntu right now), check out VectorMagic - [http://vectormagic.stanford.edu/](http://vectormagic.stanford.edu/) - I used it to vectorize the edu-logo so I could blow it up for CD size.

Here's what I came up with, you can use it in 4L, but you need to resize it a bit, and shift it. The "arm" on the logo will run off the edge of the CD, no real way around that.

The placement of the version number means it will be easy to erase and re-enter for future use.
