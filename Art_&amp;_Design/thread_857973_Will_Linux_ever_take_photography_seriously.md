### Post by lyceum on 2008-07-16
Check out LightZone

[http://www.lightcrafts.com/linux/index.html](http://www.lightcrafts.com/linux/index.html)

You get the demo for free, then $199 for the real thing. I thought it looked really good. It you want some thing like photoshop, you will need to be ready to shell out the cash. It is twice as much as the photoshop equivalent, but if you want it to run on FOSS you got to pay, that is what shows companies where the market is.

-edit-
and by-the-way- Linux is the kernel, it is not a photoshop program or a movement. Other people have to make the programs work on the kernel. So, Linux takes art programs as seriously as it can, it is the program makers that need to take the Linux kernel seriously. ;)
