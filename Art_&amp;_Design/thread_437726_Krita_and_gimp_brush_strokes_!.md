### Post by drfalkor on 2007-05-09
If you take a look, I've discovered that krita draws brush lines better than gimp ( I think so) with a wacom I 3 tablet.. here is some pictures of that:

Gimp brush strokes: [http://work.digital-brush.org/Gimp_brush.png](http://work.digital-brush.org/Gimp_brush.png)

Krita brush strokes: [http://work.digital-brush.org/Krita_brush.png](http://work.digital-brush.org/Krita_brush.png)

So, if you see the difference, The gimp has a plastic look on the brush strokes, but Krita has more of an "more used to" strokes from Photoshop, and that is good, for me. I feel that Krita is a much better app to create better art than the gimp ( it is like that for me) 

What do you think ? :) 

Oh, and the eraser thing at the back of the pen is working in Krita! :D but not in the gimp !
