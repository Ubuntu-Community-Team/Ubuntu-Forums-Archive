### Post by paullinux on 2006-06-26
The pictures are 120x80 px. The max size of a gnome-panel icon is 48x48px.  So I would have to make the pictures really small.  But I like the size they are having now... Therefore I use this "work-around" manner.

Not using a gnome panel, just icons placing on the desktop would work too, because there are sizeable.  But they have a resolution limit of 128x128px.  Make them any bigger and they lose their quality.  The problem is that icons on the desktop seem to lose their position on the screen sometimes.. 

So I opted for the gnome-panel instead.
