Danish? no I'm Swedish.

I'm thinking of getting a laptop and run some sort of linux on it. It seems there's a sourceforge project for using a wacom tablet on linux. Not sure how well Gimp works with my technique though. I mostly use basic brushes so maybe it'll be okay.


I think it'd be great if Ububtu had a mascot too. It could be used as a 'help wizard' and stuff. Maybe the one I did isn't that suitable, but I didn't want to do something regular and boring, like most mascots are an animal with a colored shirt or cap. I wanted something with slightly unfamiliar (weird) with an ambivalent attitude. The ubunchie might actually eat people... but in a happy, funny and cheerful way. Sort of a bit like Max and Domokun in terms of personality.
