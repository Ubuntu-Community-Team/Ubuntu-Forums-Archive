### Post by bambootree on 2008-11-20
Go to the Page Setup in the Print dialog box: type **100%** in the **Scale** box of the Layout column and then click the Image Settings tab, click the **Ignore Page Margins** check box to choose this option.
Have you ever tried this way before ?
