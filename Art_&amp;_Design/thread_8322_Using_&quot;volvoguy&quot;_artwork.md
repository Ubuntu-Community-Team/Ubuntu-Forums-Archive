### Post by Chibi on 2004-12-16
That is SVG code. It tells a program how to draw a set of vectors. (Like a single frame of a flash animation) Right click one of the pictures and hit 'Save link as...' Or whatever it may be in your respective browser. :P

---

### Post by UJM on 2004-12-16
ok I've got it now,  thanks Chibi 

1.right-click "save link as"
2.set a desktop colour  (desktop prefs)
3.choose add wallpaper (navigate to the saved link) and open it
4. you get the background with a scaled ubuntu logo ... very slick!
