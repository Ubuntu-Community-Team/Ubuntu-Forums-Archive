### Post by hessiess on 2009-09-26
If you want it pixalated, scale it with nearest (`none' in GIMP) instead of bilinear or bicubic. This will reduce the edge bluing and make it look less `amiturish' (scaling up images which are too small is commonly done by inexperienced digital artists, it is imposable to scale up bitmaps without making them blurry, pixalated or introducing some artifact(hq2x etx)).
