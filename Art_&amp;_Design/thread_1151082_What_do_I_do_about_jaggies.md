### Post by hessiess on 2009-05-07
Make sure that anti aliasing is selected in the tool options and use the brush tool not the pencil tool(which doesn't anti alias). Learn the difference between vector and bitmap, simple images like your attachment are well suited to vector graphics, more complex and photographs/photo realistic images are better stored as a high-res bitmap.You could try drawing the image 2+ times bigger, and then scaling it down, notice that the thumbnail of your image isn't aliased.
