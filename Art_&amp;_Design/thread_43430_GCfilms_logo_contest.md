The GCfilms film cataloging project is holding a logo and webdesign contest, thought some of you might be interested. [http://gcfilmslogo.free.fr/](http://gcfilmslogo.free.fr/)

The program, itself, should be in Breezy, soon. Hoary repository:
deb http://personal.telefonica.terra.es/web/infinitodev/ubuntu/ hoary universe

If there's interest, I'll post my work and you all can build off of it, if you'd like.
