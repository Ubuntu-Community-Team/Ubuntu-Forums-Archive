### Post by days_of_ruin on 2009-05-19
Your icon theme must have replacement icons for the notify icons.

The only way I can think of too fix this is to delete the icons with [these]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/NotificationDevelopmentGuidelines#icons") names from the theme.

Actually don't delete them just replace them with the icons from "/usr/share/icons/Human/scalable/status/"
