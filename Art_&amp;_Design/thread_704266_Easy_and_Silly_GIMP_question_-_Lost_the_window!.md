### Post by SpiderGorilla on 2008-02-23
I was going to answer with this stupidly complex response, but this is easier:

This **ASSUMES** you have the Layers, Channels, Paths, Undo | FG/BG, Brushes, Patterns, Gradients window in the list of open windows at the bottom of your screen. I'm also assume a default installation.

Do this:

Right-click on the button on the toolbar that you'd use to bring that window to the fore.
Choose "Maximize".
Click on the "Unmaximize Window" button at the upper-right.

It SHOULD snap right back...

### Post by badmedic on 2008-02-23

### Post by p_quarles on 2008-02-23
You know what? I didn't even realize I was missing the lower half of the dock until you pointed that out. :oops:

I was able to fix it pretty easily, though:
File >> Preferences >> Window Management >> Reset Saved Window Positions to Default Values. 

To avoid having to do this in the future, you may want to also uncheck the box that saves window values on exit.
