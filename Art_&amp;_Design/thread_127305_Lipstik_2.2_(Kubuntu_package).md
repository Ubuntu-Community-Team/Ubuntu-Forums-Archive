### Post by npu on 2006-07-30
You can use the Debian package for Lipstik 2.2.1:

[http://packages.debian.org/unstable/kde/kde-style-lipstik](http://packages.debian.org/unstable/kde/kde-style-lipstik)

Just choose your CPU architecture and (on the next page) download server. Download the file, right click on it and choose *Kubuntu Package Menu > Install Package*. Works fine on Kubuntu 6.06.

miobio: Adjust shading and all other settings in the Lipstik's configuration window. *System Settings > Appearance > Style > Lipstik > Configure*.
