### Post by mocoloco on 2007-09-05
What ATI card do you have?  If you don't know open a terminal and paste in
```
lspci | grep -i ati
```

About Mac emulators, I've never tried any, but there are some "Classic" Mac OS emulators (pre OSX) called basilisk2 and pearpc.  You have to get a ROM image of the os. System 7.5.3 is free to download.  Google is your friend for that.
I don't know of any OSX emulators.
