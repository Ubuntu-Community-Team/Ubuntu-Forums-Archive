### Post by %hMa@?b&lt;C on 2006-02-20
Yesterday, being bored, I decided to paint my computer.  I decided to go with an image of tux, so i printed out three copies of an image of him.  On the first one, I cut out all of the yellow areas, the second, I cut out all of the white areas, and the third, i cut out the whole thing to use as a background.  The case was already black, so i didnt need a black cut out.  I used the one with the yellow cut out as a stencil, which i painted "Instant Gold".  The white one i used as a stencil, and painted "Johnson Outboard White" Then I put the full outline over the painted part and sprayed the rest white, I personally think that it came out well!
