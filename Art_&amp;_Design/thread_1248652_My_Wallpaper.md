### Post by Fzang on 2009-08-24
I don't like it. The light is too concentrated in one point and looks like a graphical artifact or a serious burn-in. It steals focus far too much from the rest of the wallpaper... which is basically a grey background.

Spread out the light more, much more. Having a focus point is fine, but the rest of the wallpaper should never be bland. Also, may I suggest using gaussian blur like crazy? It smooths things out.

I would like to show you some examples, but deviantART is broken atm (at least for me :() but you should look up "blur wallpaper" and check em out.
