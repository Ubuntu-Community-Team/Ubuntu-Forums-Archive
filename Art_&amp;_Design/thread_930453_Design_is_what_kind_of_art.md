### Post by oldsoundguy on 2008-09-26
Design is creativity with a purpose.  The goal or what needs to be done is what comes up first and then the creative process begins on just how to achieve that goal.  It can be aural, visual, or even practical engineering.  It is AN ART but it is NOT ART in the sense that we all interpret.  There is no interpretation of design .. it just IS once it has been created.

IE, I just finished designing a DIGITAL switching system to power a surround sound system with more than one digital computer source.  Took it to a tech (my eyes won't let me do that work any more .. and it took tools that I did not want to invest in for a one shot deal.)
Brought the system home and it worked right out of the bag o crap!
Absolutely NOTHING artistic about it .. but the goal was met with DESIGN since there is nothing on the market like it!
