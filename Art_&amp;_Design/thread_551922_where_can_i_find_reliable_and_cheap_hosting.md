### Post by kadath on 2007-09-16
I use [NearlyFreeSpeech.NET]("https://www.nearlyfreespeech.net/"). You only pay for the storage and traffic you use, instead of a flat monthly rate. The cost is 1 cent per megabyte per month, and $1 per gigabyte of traffic. This can be very cheap if your site is small. You can make it even cheaper by hosting any large media files you want to post by hosting them on Amazon's super-cheap S3 service.

Of course, all this means you need to already be pretty familiar with how to put together and administrate your site. If you're not interested in mucking around with all that, I'd reccomend [A Small Orange]("http://www.asmallorange.com/"). They're not the cheapest, but they're reliable.
