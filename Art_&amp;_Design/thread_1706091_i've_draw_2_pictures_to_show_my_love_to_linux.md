### Post by 23dornot23d on 2011-03-14
Where it says threads merged ........
( you must have had two threads similar and they have brought them into one now )

If you want to do some graphics first of all install [GIMP]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GIMP") ........

GNU Image Manipulation Program 

Then look on You Tube ....... for [GIMP graphics videos]("http://www.google.com/search?client=ubuntu&channel=fs&q=GIMP+graphics++videos&ie=utf-8&oe=utf-8#client=ubuntu&channel=fs&q=GIMP+graphics++videos&oe=utf-8&um=1&ie=UTF-8&tbo=u&tbs=vid:1&source=og&sa=N&hl=en&tab=wv&bav=on.2,or.r_gc.r_pw.&fp=e07fd1ba61039d28")

If you get stuck with anything post a message on here ......

But the main thing is to think of something like colouring in the letters
then to think of a way to do it ....... and then just keep trying things out with it and keep
using the program .

Some people say GIMP is difficult ..... it is only difficult if people do not use it .
( a lot of things become easy once people get used to doing things - like riding a bike or driving a car )

_______________________________

For the Fill in of the letters you just need one tool ......... bucket fill ...... [IMG]http://img846.imageshack.us/img846/1285/bucketfill.jpg[/IMG]

A [paint pot on the left side in GIMP is the fill icon]("https://sites.google.com/site/paintings23dornot23d/") ...... this is a good tool for
filling in areas like the letters in your picture.

Try it in areas like your text ..... then change the colours or use patterns to fill in shapes.
