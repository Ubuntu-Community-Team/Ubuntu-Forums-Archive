### Post by Cope57 on 2009-12-11
**Exodist**,

You do realize that when you use the **Quick Links** > **Networking** > **Pictures & Albums** section of this forum, that you can upload your images to [your own gallery]("http://ubuntuforums.org/album.php?u=888035")?  Or you could visit other forum member [Albums]("http://ubuntuforums.org/album.php") to get inspired to create more works.

Every forum member has a personal gallery, many just have not explored all the tools given for them to use.  The [Search]("http://ubuntuforums.org/search.php") feature being the least used here.  If the search engine was used by every person here, no questions would be repeated.

Nice avatars by the way Exodist, you have found a style and have stuck with it.  If you continue using the same style in all your works, it could almost be a distinguishing aspect to identify the graphics you have created.  Using the same graphics however, without changing, could also make your gimp skills stagnant to a specific mindset.

Feel free to expand your gimp graphics skills, and use [my bookmark collection of gimp tutorials]("http://share.xmarks.com/folder/bookmarks/RVQ2thwaWi").

**Edit**: One more thing Exodist, when adding images to your avatars, you could use your own works.  Create your own tux versions by following [Tutorials]("http://www.crystalxp.net/tuto/en.htm") at CrystalXP.net.  My Tux for example is loosely based on the tutorial for the G1 Tux, but I changed some of the steps slightly so mine would not look exactly like everyone else.
