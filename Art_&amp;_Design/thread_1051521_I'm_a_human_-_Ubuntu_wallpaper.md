I'm a human is a take on Microsofts new advertisement scheme, "I'm a PC", now, Ubuntu users can officially claim, they are human :P I got the idea of Ubuntu users being 'human' because of its slogan 'linux for human beings.' (and ofcoarse because the ubuntu community is, by far, the most friendly of all official and fan OS communities ;)) I contemplated having 'I'm an Ubuntu' but it didn't sound right.

Hope you enjoy it :)
