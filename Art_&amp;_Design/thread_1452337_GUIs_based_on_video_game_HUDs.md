### Post by Greevous on 2010-04-11
I'm sure that I'm not the only person who would love to use an OS that looked, sounded, and felt like the heads-up display from Modern Warfare 2, or inside the Iron Man suit from the film. Even though bloggers complain that modern computers don't do those fancy things because they're distracting, and don't make tiny blips and bleeps for every character that shows up on a screen because it would be annoying, I would thoroughly enjoy that kind of end user experience. 

I wish my hopes would reach the ears of game developers, programmers, graphic artists, and musicians in the Ubuntu community who would have the know-how to create raw sound effects, Compiz plugins, fonts, and whatever else necessary to bring science fiction and modern life together for interested individuals. 

Again, I'm pretty sure that the greatest obstacle is the inefficiency of it all, but imagine the excitement of kids and adults alike if their home computers could imitate the movies that they grow up with and enjoy. I have no doubt that it would be possible, in the hands of the right people.
