### Post by evissecx on 2007-07-28
Thank you!

Ok, I've been testing around with the tips from the tutorial. But I wasn't happy with the result I got. 
The extra channel for bumpmapping with light looked very repetitive on a 1600x1200 image. 
I try to blur the bumpmap cloud to get rid of to much detail, but then, it just didn't get a good visible effect at all :)

Instead, I did change the light, brighter and darker, on some spots of the image, 
and then paint higlights and shadows to get more feeling of a sky with clouds.
