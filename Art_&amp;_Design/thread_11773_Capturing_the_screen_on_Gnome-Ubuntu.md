### Post by Buffalo Soldier on 2005-01-19
Which version of Ubuntu are you using? If it's Warty (4.10) what you can do is click at "Computer" at the taskbar on top of your screen. Then "Take Screenshot" and lastly "Save".

Computer -> Take Screenshot -> Save to file / Save to desktop -> Save
