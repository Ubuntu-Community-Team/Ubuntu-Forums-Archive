### Post by Mongoose on 2007-05-11
I found out about the OS-tan today browsing some Japanese web sites.  The OS-tan are a group of manga style characters that represent various operating systems such as Windows 95.  I found some WIPs of a 'Linux-tan', and reworked it to be 'Ubuntu-tan'.  I didn't like all the DE logos, clutter, and some of the color theme so I changed that too.  Questions, suggestions, or comments?

I'm planning on using this as a website logo as well.  I don't know who made the WIPs I used as a basis either, since the images were third hand.  I made a 'light' and 'dark' version for this image.
