### Post by PrimoTurbo on 2009-11-25
Design wise, it's not bad. Good as a simple wallpaper.

However, it has over saturation and too much brightness in that area where the lines meet. This makes it hard to use, you could make it more subtle.

Color wise I guess it sort of fits Ubuntu, but I don't think the colors are attractive enough. Only thing I can recommend is a slightly warmer hue.

There are some compression artifacts near the light source, not sure if it's the image or the compression you used.

Overall it's not too bad, but perhaps you could apply the following suggestions.
