### Post by aklo on 2010-04-13
Mine works fine, and i can open the my collection of brush perfectly. I'll just tell you what my settings are.

You may want to check the permission of .gimp-2.6 (insert your version here)
If you cannot see this folder, then view->hidden files/folders
The hidden gimp folder is usually at: computername/home

Mine set to 

Owner:Create and delete files
Group:Create and delete files
Other:Create and delete files

Remember to click "Apply permission to enclosed files" 

Double clicking those brush files won't open them with GIMP automatically....you just have to "Open with other application" and select GIMP.
