### Post by stephantom on 2007-11-22
The icons are packaged in the human-icon-theme package.
The sources for this package (as with all other packages too) can be found on Launchpad ([https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/human-icon-theme/0.23](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/human-icon-theme/0.23)). On the bottom of that page you can download a tar.gz (2.5 MiB).
