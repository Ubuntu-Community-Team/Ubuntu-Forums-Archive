### Post by Technique13 on 2009-07-20
putting aside your fantastic graphic art skills for one second, I don't think the poster works. Infact, I think its slightly contradictory to the above poster which I prefer, here's why:

Ubuntu is a linux distro targeted at bringing linux to the non-technical user, your first ad does a great job at suggesting this, however, in the second one you've chosen a portrayal of someone who appears to be a technical user. People will associate this with ubuntu and think; "well, its his cup of tea, but I'm not that technical" I think if you were to use this ad you should do so with a series of ad's that have a number of different people with the highlighted features tailored to their demographic. 

From a marketing perspective, whenever you use people in advertising it sends a message. It's integral that you send the correct message when trying to sell ubuntu; "this is not a highly technical piece of software, its linux for human beings"
