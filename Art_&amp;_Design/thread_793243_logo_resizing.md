### Post by Xfcn on 2008-05-13
A) Are you using level 9 compression on your PNG? Because that's the most compressed you can get a PNG image.

B) Are you scaling up or are you scaling down? Up-scaling always causes pixelation.

C) What program are you using to resize the image?

### Post by days_of_ruin on 2008-05-13
Since your logo has very distinctive lines I converted to svg in inkscape.
(Path>Trace Bitmap).
Then (Path>Break apart) filled in the parts with gradients.
As for your future logos, use inkscape.Or if you need to use gimp,
make it very big:)
