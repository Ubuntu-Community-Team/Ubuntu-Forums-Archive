### Post by Therion on 2009-02-27
Open the Terminal, go to the Edit menu and select Profiles.

Select "Default" and click Edit.

In the Effects tab, click the radio button and slide the bar to the left the adjust the transparency.



Or... To get really fancy:  [http://www.ubuntugeek.com/howto-create-a-transparent-terminal-in-ubuntu-desktop.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/howto-create-a-transparent-terminal-in-ubuntu-desktop.html)
