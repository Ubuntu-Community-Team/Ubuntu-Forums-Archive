### Post by hungrybastard on 2007-06-07
I am a designer and have done a number of carts and can say Zen Cart is by far, the best I have tried. Although it does not have all the bells and whistles of some other carts, what it does have works spectacularly! If you need to skin a cart but doent have the time or knowhow to do it yourself, check out something like monster templates. 

If you are looking to "fit" and existing sites design on top of ZC, you will really need to know what you are doing and have all the appropriate source files.

Good Luck!
