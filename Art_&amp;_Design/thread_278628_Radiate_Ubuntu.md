I would like to take you up on one point though:

Electromagnetic waves... light electricity etc *DO* travel in "rings" - look at a ripple on the surface of a pond, for the prime example.

As an electronic engineer I happen to KNOW this is true - just wanted to make you aware!. If things travelled in "rays" then there would be gaps in the sunlight, and on a clear day, you would see stripes in the sky EVEN THOUGH there was no cloud to obstruct the sun.
