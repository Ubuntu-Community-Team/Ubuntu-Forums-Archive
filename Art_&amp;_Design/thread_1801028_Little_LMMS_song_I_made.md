[www.jamendo.com](www.jamendo.com) is for everybody who want to share their music. There's a lot of amateur stuff there. I'm surprised you've never heard about them before. The music app that comes as default with Ubuntu (Banshee, and before that, Rythmbox) provide streaming music from Jamendo.  Highly recommended!
