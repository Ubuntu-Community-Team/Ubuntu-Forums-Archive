### Post by Hyperkill on 2008-09-03
I wouldn't suggest doing reseller hosting plans, it really sucks.  With so many other people doing it your chances of landing a sale are pretty slim.  Most people that are creating a website/buying hosting already have a company in their mind, such as Go Daddy.  Overall, the only way it's worthwhile is if you know for sure you have a solid way of bringing in customers.  If you're a freelance web designer/programmer this might be a good solution for you.
