These themes are under GPL since I made them from Macslow's zen theme. And, regarding Foo (the bear which is the mascot of KLDP), free to use and modify.

How to use? Extract and copy zen-foo and zen-ubuntu into /usr/share/cairo-clock/themes/, then you can choose them from cairo-clock's "Properties".
