I decided this didn't belong here. So I removed it, instead heres a (very) short guide to customizing Ubuntu (from what I've learned).

Goto Add/Remove and download the following:
Beryl Manager
Gnome Art Manager

Gnome Art Manager allows you to download custom themes, logon screens, and buttons.
Also every aspect of themes is editable in Theme>Customize
Beryl Manager lets you use all the custom graphics everyone is raving about, alot of which isn't in the default feisty install.

I know its very basic, but hopefully it will help someone.
