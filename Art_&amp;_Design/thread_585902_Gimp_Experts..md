### Post by Tux Aubrey on 2007-10-22
Yes.  Its best to use .xcf as your default format for your "master" and merge the layers when you are ready.  You can save the "Final" in any suitable format but keep your "master" with all the layers.  

For a "big" project, consider creating a separate directory for component images and "tests".
