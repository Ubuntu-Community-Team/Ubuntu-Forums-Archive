### Post by Terminus on 2007-05-30
Back on the ubuntu forums after over a year... shame on me! :p

I just wanted to share some awesome CD covers that I made, to label my Feisty CDs. The design is largely based on Programad's terrific work, but solved some minor inconsistancies, not least of which was his design didn't include the disc centre, as my discs are designed to be printed on right up to the hole. I also found his to be too busy, and the white-on-red text was jarring. There were some odd drop shadows and squished-looking fonts too.

Anyways, I prepared my design for you to enjoy. I don't particularly want to submit it to the official queue, so I'll leave it here for you.

Here is >>[Programad's original design]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DIYMarketing?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=feistylabelcd32enus.png")<<

Thanks to "Who" for current logo? Shamelessly ripped from >>[here]("http://www.markshuttleworth.com/archives/63")<<. It was the only source I could find.
