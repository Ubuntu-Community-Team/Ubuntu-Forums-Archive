The wait is over. RawTherapee 3.0 is now released with GPL license. To those who don't know, RT is a free (now in both meanings of the word) RAW converter on a par with the leading commercial applications. Hopefully, this will lead to the software improvements and a greater Linux market penetration, since native deb and rpm packages should follow.
