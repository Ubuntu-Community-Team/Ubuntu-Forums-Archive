### Post by Sepero on 2008-01-25
"Synfig  is a powerful, industrial-strength vector-based 2D animation software package, designed from the ground-up for producing feature-film quality animation with fewer people and resources." (Similar to Flash)

Synfig is available in the Gutsy 7.10 repositories, but one of the developers has shared with me a newer build with several additional features.

Now I'm sharing it with you. Add the following line to the bottom of your file /etc/apt/sources.list

deb [http://dooglus.rincevent.net/synfig/repository/](http://dooglus.rincevent.net/synfig/repository/) ./




After that, you will need to reload your available packages and install/upgrade "synfigstudio". You can do that using the Synaptic Package Manager.
