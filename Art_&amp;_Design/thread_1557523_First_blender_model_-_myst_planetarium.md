### Post by Grafens on 2011-01-29
> **chips24 said:**
> so, i took another try at modeling the planetarium, and it looks alot better than my old one previously on the screen, but ive come across another problem, how do i apply a texture two different parts of the same mesh? is that possible?

To add two materials to single mesh.
Goto > Edit mode > select only the faces you want to apply texture to then hit “p” this will partition that segment from the mesh. Goto > Object mode, apply textures in usual way to that part. Stay in Object mode and select the remainder of the mesh and apply the other textures you want. > render. Now you should have a mesh with two different textures applied.

Good work by the way.
Grafens
