### Post by jem7v on 2007-01-15
A **free** image editor better than the GIMP? I doubt it.

Judging from back in the day when I used PSP, the GIMP is a lot more powerful than it is once you learn your way around the menus and the intricacies of the tools.  PSP seemed pretty gimmicky to me and seemed to rely on filters and wizards too much.  To me it never felt like a program for serious artists.

I'm not going to pretend that The GIMP is the best image editor out there... its brush system is pretty darn weak, and in that regard I'd rather still be using Photoshop 7, but I heard that the next release of the GIMP (whenever that is) is going to have a fairly serious rewrite of the brush tool.  I might be wrong about that, but that's what I heard.
