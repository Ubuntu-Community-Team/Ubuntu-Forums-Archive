### Post by Half-Left on 2007-12-27
If you going to do something like that use Vectors, Inkscape is much better for that. Always make a big version and scale down, never take a bitmap and scale up like you seem to have done.

Another tip is to use GIMP for the background colours and the forground as vectors with the text.
