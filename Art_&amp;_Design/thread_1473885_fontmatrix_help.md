### Post by Newfoundlander on 2010-05-05
Is the font changed in all applications/desktop or just in Fontmatrix?

If the font is changed everywhere, just right-click on an empty area of the desktop and select "change desktop background". The "Appearance Preferences" window will appear and on the Fonts tab you can change all of the system fonts.
