### Post by eye208 on 2008-01-17
No, you can't export as SWF. But you can render movies, upload them to e.g. [http://blip.tv/](http://blip.tv/) and then put them on websites using Blip.tv's embeddable Flash video player.

Synfig Studio was not meant to be a Flash replacement. It's just for making movies.
