### Post by bored2k on 2005-03-29
Applications >  System Tools > Configuration Editor > apps > gnome-session > options > splash_image > change.it [not literally...]

You can also change the Boot Loaders ugly black background, just get grubconf and grub-splashimages [or ones from the net; or create your own] .
