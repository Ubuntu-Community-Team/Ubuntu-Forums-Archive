### Post by Grimhound on 2009-09-28
While messing around with Gnome, I came up with a rather nice theme I thought it would be nice to share. Wasn't sure exactly where to post this, but here goes.

I call it Dark Java.
Controls: Human
Window Border: DarkRoom
Icons: Breathe

Colors:
Windows: #131313/#BCB08B
Input Boxes: #4A4A4A/#BCB08B
Selected Items: #5E4E41/#FFFFFF
Tooltips: #211B12/#BCB08B

The overall goal was to make the style feel like a nice dark roasted blend of awesome. I think it accomplishes that with minimal tweaking. :)
