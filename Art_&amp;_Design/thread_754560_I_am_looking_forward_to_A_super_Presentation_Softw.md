### Post by kiddo on 2008-04-14
Here's what I would do:
[LIST]
[*][Inkscape]("http://inkscape.org") for designing the slides and exporting them as a pdf (or PNGs)
[*][KeyJnote]("http://keyjnote.sf.net") for presenting the slides
[*]virtual desktops to switch back and forth between the presentation and the 3D app (such as [Blender]("http://blender.org"))
[/LIST]

Those are all applications that are available in Ubuntu's repositories :)
