I tried it out and I was really liking it's vector art possibilities.

Two problems were it kept wanting access to things that I felt had sensitive information, like my Google Docs account, for saving work. It also wanted, as something optional, access to Picasa. I was ok with it, but I can imagine other people not wanting to give full access to this third party company. 

The second problem was it crashed! Actually my browser crashed. That probably was the fault of my browser, Chrome 10.0.648.204. But... this is really the first time it's crashed in a few months, as far as I can recall. Maybe longer. 

Very cool stuff is out there. I'm still waiting for a cloud 3d modeler!!!
