### Post by bvc on 2006-06-10
if svg they will need ot go in the scalable dir. For them to show up at smaller sizes, you will need to remove all icons of the same name in smaller dirs. Example, if you still have the volume applet icons in the 24x24 dir and you have a 24+px panel they will be used. For yours to be used you will need to remove the 24px icons.
