### Post by Half-Left on 2008-05-12
I'd just thought I would share with people another part of GIMP showing just what great stuff you can do with it.

If you didn't see my part 1 then go here [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=758678](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=758678).

Part2 is more about the clone tool and showing what great compositions you can do. Here is the original image I used [http://www.sxc.hu/photo/1000500](http://www.sxc.hu/photo/1000500)

So far I have a Tiger, Butterfly and Snake composted onto her face, using the clone tool and hair is cloned to over the snake to give the impression it's underneath. I'm using colour adjustment Curves to make each layer blend in with each other.

I've still got alot to do, including cloning the Tiger leg onto her shoulder better and some background stuff.
