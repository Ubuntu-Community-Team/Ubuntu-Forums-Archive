### Post by OgreProgrammer on 2009-08-14
1.On a new layer, select all. 
2.Shrink the selection. I tried 8 pixels. 
3.Fill it with a grey of your choice, and set the layer attribute to burn. 
4.Reduce the alpha of the layer with the slider. I tried 25% and that looked good. 
5.Remove the selection and Gaussian blur it. I set mine to 25 pixels. 

Play with that a bit, and you should get something passable.

Good luck.
