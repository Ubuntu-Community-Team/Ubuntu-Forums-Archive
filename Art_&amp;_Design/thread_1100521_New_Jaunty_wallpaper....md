Windows 7 hit beta with 17 high-res wallpapers (they've [developed over 30]("http://www.windowsvistamagazine.com/05582469249658242575/give-your-windows-vista-pc-a-windows-7-makeover.html"), they're just region specific. The Ubuntu experience can be defined by the memorable design shipped with the system, or it can be defined by the experience of needing to google for an acceptable wallpaper.
