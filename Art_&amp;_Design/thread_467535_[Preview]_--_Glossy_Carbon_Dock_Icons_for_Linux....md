### Post by Izobalax on 2007-06-07
My first set of dock icons! :grin:

Basically, I got frustrated because I couldn't find the ***exact*** type of icons that I wanted. So I decided to create some. I ***may*** release these in the future, once I have more time. They'd be released under the GPL, so peeps would be free to tweak with them. They're also primarily geared towards Linux users. :)

They are colour-coded for quick-glancing ease of locating. The current abbreviations I have here and there meanings are:

FF - Firefox
OPR - Opera
PGN - Pidgin
AML - aMule
KTOR - KTorrent
TBD - Thunderbird
GMP - GIMP
INK - Inkscape
BND - Blender
BSH - Banshee
VLC - VLC
TOT - Totem
GRP - Grip
TRM - Terminal
COM - Computer
HOM - Home

Currently, blue is for internet programs, green is for graphics, red is for media and yellow is for system.

***Please*** let me know what you think of them. This will help me to make any necessary improvements and gear me towards releasing them. :)
