### Post by Nevon on 2010-03-10
Inkscape would be your best bet when making icons for a Linux desktop. While you will still have to (in the interest of quality) make separate versions for the different sizes ( 16x16, 22x22, 24x24, 32x32, 48x48 ), you will also have a scalable vector version if you make it in inkscape. That way the icon can be infinitely scaled up and still look good (go ahead and try scaling one of the icons on your desktop).
