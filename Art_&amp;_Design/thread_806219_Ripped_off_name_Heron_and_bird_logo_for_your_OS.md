### Post by amingv on 2008-05-24
The Ubuntu naming convention goes like this:

<Adjective> <Animal>

The heron was the animal chosen to represent the letter 'H", and "hardy" was the adjective.
Considering a heron looks like this:
[http://images.google.com/images?hl=en&q=heron&btnG=Search+Images&gbv=2](http://images.google.com/images?hl=en&q=heron&btnG=Search+Images&gbv=2)

So, where's the ripoff?
