### Post by hikaricore on 2006-12-13
Ok let me give you the quick rundown:

**GDM** - *Login Screen Theme*

**GTK 1.x/2.x** - *Controls the color and feel of almost everything you see inside of your application windows.*

**Metacity** - *The boarder around your window including the titlebar and buttons in the title bar, some GTK themes affect the color of these and others don't.*

**Beryl** - *If you don't know what it is yet you don't need it yet.  Basicly the same as metacity with visual impovements and more.*
