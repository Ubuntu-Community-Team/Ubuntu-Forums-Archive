### Post by psyopper on 2007-07-25
If they are Photoshop users, try installing [GIMPShop]("http://plasticbugs.com/index.php?p=241") on one of their systems. It is FAR more intuitive to Photoshop users than GIMP is. It's still The GIMP, the interface was just retouched a little to better reflect Photoshop's menu's and navigation.

I wrote up a quick tutorial  on my blog as an easy lesson to Photoshop users that takes them though the various GIMPShop menus to show the similarities to Photoshop and the abilities of The GIMP. [Take a look at it here]("http://psyopper.wordpress.com/reviews/gimpshop-review-part-i/").
