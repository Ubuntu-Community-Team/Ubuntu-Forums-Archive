### Post by aklo on 2010-05-23
It is about manipulating shapes.

Draw a triangle using the [ Stars and Polygon Tool] Change the corners settings to 3 for a triangle.

Draw the tail using the [Rectangle tool]

I would do 1 for you if i knew what you had in mind since there are different types of arrows...sharp arrow? rounded corners arrow?
