### Post by Newfoundlander on 2010-05-03
It depends on experience but $20-30 per hour is reasonable for good work.  I've been doing graphic design since the early 1990s and I now charge $30/hour although I've been told I should charge more.

She should expect to be asked to provide several variations of the logo and for those variations to be tweaked further until they are happy with a single design.

One of my big selling points is that I use open source software.  Using open source software offers my clients true cross-platform flexibility and the ability to update their own projects without expensive or cumbersome software.

If you'd like to check out my portfolio, take a look at stephenfwinsor.carbonmade.com.  Hope this has helped.

Cheers,

Stephen
