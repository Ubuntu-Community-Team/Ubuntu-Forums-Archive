### Post by BigSilly on 2009-01-01
Just discovered the rather sexy [Conte Lien]("http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php?action=content&content=94554") theme over on Gnome Look. The creator has posted up an alternative [metacity theme]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php?content=94555") as well that I feel goes better with it. I have to say I think it's a stunning Gnome theme that is very simple, but clear and elegant. I'm using it with Mist icons, the [Silvermar login theme]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/silvermar_ubuntu+GDM?content=38813"), and the [Ubuntu Silver splash screen]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/ubuntu+silver?content=17226").

I don't use Compiz, so I wanted a pretty theme that didn't rely on the effects to flesh it out. I really think Conte Lien is one of the nicest themes I've ever tried, and does exactly what I wanted. Recommended.
