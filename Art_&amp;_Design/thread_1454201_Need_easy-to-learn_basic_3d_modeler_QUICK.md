### Post by Gemnoc on 2010-04-26
It may be too late, but you actually can now install and run Google Sketchup pretty easily on Linux, through Wine.

I've done it on three machines: two desktop PCs with Nvidia graphics (one with GeForce 6200GT, another with a GeForce 8600GTS, later replaced with a GF 9800GT), and a mini-notebook with Intel GMA 4500MHD. Surprisingly, the last one shows the least graphics bugs! (said bugs are minor nuisances and don't affect the program's use too much)

A couple years ago, running Sketchup in Wine with ATI cards was almost impossible, but I've seen a lot of reports that it can be done now.

A good source of info: [GoogleSketchup - The Official Wine Wiki]("http://wiki.winehq.org/GoogleSketchup")
