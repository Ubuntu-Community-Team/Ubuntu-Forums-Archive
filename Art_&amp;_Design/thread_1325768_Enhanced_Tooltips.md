### Post by Fzang on 2009-11-14
A "tooltip" like that would be better suited in the Help section bundled with the application. A tooltip is just what it is, a tooltip, not a whole page. 

Tooltips are small helpers for visual representation; if you cannot recognize the icon the tooltip is there to quickly help you out, but if you're looking for a more complex guide on how to do things you'll be reading the Help section.
