### Post by psyopper on 2007-07-29
Post the source of your file if you can, that would be rather helpful.

In your gradient tool ensure that:

Opacity: 100%
Mode: Normal
Gradient: FG to Transparent
Offset: 0.0
Shape: Linear
Repeat: None

Dithering and supersampling are up to you.
