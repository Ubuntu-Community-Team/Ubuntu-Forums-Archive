### Post by CharmyBee on 2009-05-02
In addition, a drawing tablet is very recommended. Real anatomy references are also relevant (Anime is not a excuse for avoiding anatomy, it's only a simplified form of it). Practice on realism and it'll help you do anime better. This is even relevant if you want to do just mecha. "How to draw Manga" books are useless and don't teach you anything.

Yeah I know I didn't suggest a program, but this is strong advice if you intend to draw in this style.
