### Post by endersshadow on 2005-12-17
You can select the white background and drag it a bit, and then press Del to erase it.  If that doesn't work, select the white background (by using the wand select), then go to Select>Invert and then copy what you want, go to File>New and hit Advanced, and select the background as transparent.  Then, just paste your image onto it.  Voila.

Mind posting the original, so that I could give you a screenshotted tutorial?
