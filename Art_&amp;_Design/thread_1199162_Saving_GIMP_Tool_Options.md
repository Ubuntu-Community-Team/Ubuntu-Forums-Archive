### Post by cubeist on 2009-06-30
Hey,
Works fine for me.  How do you close the program... you should use ctrl-q on the keyboard or select file:quit.  Closing windows to quit programs does not always give the program a chance to properly save settings.

Also, ensure that your settings are being written to /home/username/.gimp2.x and not the root /usr/share/gimp/

### Post by lightstream on 2009-07-01
thanks for your input. unfortunately it wasn't the things you suggested but I was encouraged enough to explore further and I found another option, on the Input Devices part of the preferences, where there's also an option to save settings on exit. Once this one was checked then settings were remembered correctly.
