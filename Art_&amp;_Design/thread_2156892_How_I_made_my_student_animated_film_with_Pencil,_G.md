### Post by chitarrino on 2013-06-23
I made a "making of" video to show the long process I went through to make my student film, a 2D animated short with a lot of 3D "tricks".
I used real world pencil and paper for the first part, then scanned in batch with sane/xsane and continued the animation with Pencil 2D.
Inkscape was useful for some parts of the backgrounds, in combination with GIMP (also used for coloring).
Blender was the main tool I used, to composite all the pieces together and to animate the 3D baddie.

The Making of Oranguerrilla: [http://www.turiscandurra.com/2013/06/the-making-of-oranguerrilla/](http://www.turiscandurra.com/2013/06/the-making-of-oranguerrilla/)
