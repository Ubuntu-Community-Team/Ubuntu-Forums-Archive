### Post by SQuark on 2007-06-20
GQview is actually a lot faster than most people think. The problem is that its standard zoom quality is Hyper.
Got to Edit>Preferences and change the thumbnail quality to Tiles or Nearest. Now click on the Image tab and change the zoom quality to Bilinear or Tiles.

I find GQview to be slightly more powerfull than gThumb. Unfortunately, its features are a bit hidden away and often overlooked.
