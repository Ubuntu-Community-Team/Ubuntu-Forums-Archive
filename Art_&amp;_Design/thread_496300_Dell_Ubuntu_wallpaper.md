### Post by Bodizzle on 2007-07-10
You could easily make it yourself if you haven't already found where to download it.  

Create a new item in a paint program, since you are using Ubuntu I would assume you are using GIMP.  

Figure out the resolution of your monitor and use that the set the dimensions of your new image.  Fill the background with something that matches the Dell background you like.  

Then, you can get the Ubuntu logo at[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Official?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=UbuntuLogo.png]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Official?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=UbuntuLogo.png") and add it to your background.

Ubuntu makes all of their logos available at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Official]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Official")
