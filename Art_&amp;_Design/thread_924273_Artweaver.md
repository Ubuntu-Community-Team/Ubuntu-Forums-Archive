### Post by Dragonbite on 2008-09-19
If you are talking about Windows-only Photoshop challengers then I wold like to bring up [[COLOR="Blue"]Paint.NET[/COLOR]]("http://www.getpaint.net/") which has an easy interface of Paint but much more powerful (layers, magic lasso selections, etc.).

Another possible advantage of Paint.NET is that it is written mostly in .NET so the Mono crowd (same people that bring F-Spot, Tomboy and Banshee) have [[COLOR="Blue"]ported Paint.NET[/COLOR]]("http://code.google.com/p/paint-mono/") to Linux though not fully successful but closer than nothing!
