### Post by digitalis_vulgaris on 2008-12-08
Don't be afraid to use a little sharpening. It looks a little blured. Use straight lines and some spherical brushes for industrial objects, there is no need to draw all by a hand.

Add more dramatic lights and shadows. Action illustration need hard lights and shadows for dramatic look. Frank Miller's style is extreme example of action illustrations style.

You have a tallent and potencial, just need some more experience to make first class illustrations. Really nice going work!

Some of mine playing and testing with Gimp:
[http://www.gimptalk.com/forum/artistic-brushes-testings-t35000s75.html](http://www.gimptalk.com/forum/artistic-brushes-testings-t35000s75.html)
