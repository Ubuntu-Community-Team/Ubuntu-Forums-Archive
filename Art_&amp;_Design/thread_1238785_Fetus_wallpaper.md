### Post by cwnc on 2009-08-12
The shape is too abstract for anyone to perceive it as a fetus, and even if they did, they would probably give you the benefit of the doubt and assume it's just an abstract color space.

As far as color spaces go, you're throwing a huge amount of green around without balancing it properly - mathematically, it shouldn't matter, but the human eye is extremely sensitive to shades of green, and having so many highly saturated hues so close together creates a huge amount of visual dissonance.

Using something like this as a desktop is just going to suck the perception of color right out of anything you're working with - which is not something that most people would find complements their workflow.
