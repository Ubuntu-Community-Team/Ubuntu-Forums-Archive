### Post by kayosiii on 2007-10-06
from memory there is a ubuntu deb of domino on kde-look... That's the one to install. Once you have done that - Go To "System Settings"
Choose "Appearance" from the second line of icons labeled "Look & Feel", Choose Style from the list of items on the left edge of the panel.

Below a label saying "Widget Style" you should find a drop down menu and domino should be in that menu. You should see a preview of what domino looks like in the area below. 

I personally use domino and think that it looks quite pleasant

Hope that helps
