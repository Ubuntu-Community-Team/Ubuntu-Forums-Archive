### Post by Nonno Bassotto on 2006-05-29
I think we could share the way we make our desktops look so nice.
The idea is that every post should contain some screenshots of our desktops and the settings we used to achieve this eye-candy. For example one could send information about: control theme, Metacity theme, icons, wallpaper, splash screen (maybe also the background color code), gdm theme, gDesklets used, other apps skins (like bmp, xmms, mplayer, etc.) and whatever else he tweaked. The best thing would be to send a link for each of this elements.

So, let me begin with my current theme.

    both are on

Here's the theme I'm currently using. I've put this together from a bunch of different guides and things I've found on my own over the past year or so. My apologies for not crediting all those guides but I honestly can't remember.

   Anyway, the theme breaks down like this:

Wallpaper - Ballance:
[http://www.zixpk.com/2008/02/balance-wallpaper.html]("http://www.zixpk.com/2008/02/balance-wallpaper.html")

Metacity Theme - The Dark Theme:
[http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/..::The+Dark+Theme::..?content=66005]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/..::The+Dark+Theme::..?content=66005")

Emerald Theme - Mint Kore:
[http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Mint+Kore+for+Emerald?content=78430]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Mint+Kore+for+Emerald?content=78430")

Icons - Dropline NEU!:
[http://art.gnome.org/themes/icon/1100]("http://art.gnome.org/themes/icon/1100")


   With the Dropline NEU! icon set if you use thunderbird it's going to change the Thunderbird icon to an envelope instead of the standard Thunderbird icon. The way to fix this is to point the launcher icon at /usr/share/pixmaps/thunderbird.png instead of where it is pointing by default. If you have the launcher in your toolbar also, you have to change it in both places.
