### Post by Newfoundlander on 2009-06-02
Here are three options that I can think of...

1) visit [http://www.yourfonts.com/](http://www.yourfonts.com/) or [http://www.fontifier.com/](http://www.fontifier.com/) and they will make a font from your writing (you will have to pay for the service)
2) visit [http://www.DaFont.com](http://www.DaFont.com) and check the handwriting section for a font that is close to your own writing
3) install FontForge using Add/Remove Apps and make your own font (more challenging but will cost you nothing)

Cheers,
