### Post by erfahren on 2007-09-03
> **SOULRiDER said:**
> Are you following any tutorials? If so, could you post the links?
Actually, despite my searching, I never found any tutorial specific to making website buttons. I just looked through others regarding making [3D globes](http://www.technomono.com/tutorials/gimp/aqua-ball/index.php) and the like.

For this one all I did was resize the gedit logo I found in my /usr/share/gedit-2 directory, added the accessories-text-editor16.png icon out of /usr/share/icons to it and the check mark, and added a border to give it a raised look.

To get the curved surface (convex) appearance I made a new (transparent) image about 2/3 it's height and did a FG to Transparency Gradient (black - 30% or 40% Opacity - I can't remember) and pasted it over as a new layer. --- took me some trial-and-error to get that part.

I'd like to find a good tutorial to make those glassy looking buttons. (I think you can get a reflective look by adding a semi-transparent white layer.)
