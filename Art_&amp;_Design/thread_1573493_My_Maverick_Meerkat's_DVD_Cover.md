### Post by lcjohnny on 2010-09-12
With the approaching release  of **meerkat**, i needed to update my collection with a cover for the new version, **Ubuntu 10.10**.

[CENTER][IMG]http://leverson.files.wordpress.com/2010/09/meerkatbox.png?w=136&h=180[/IMG]
[/CENTER]

Just as done to the [previous editions]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1508785"), the propose is to have a cover for each issue with its on individual characteristics (as a mascot, colors, screenshots)  but working as a collection to be placed alongside the others on the  shelf.

All versions  created are available through [this link]("http://picasaweb.google.com.br/lcjohnny/Ubuntu") on my album, in **Portuguese**, ** English** and also **without texts** to be freely edited in other languages. All under **creative commons**.
