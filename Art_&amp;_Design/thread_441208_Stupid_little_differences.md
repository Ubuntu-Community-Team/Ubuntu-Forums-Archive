well you ca change the size of desktop icons and menu sizes, and font sizes, go to edit->preferances in nautilus and change default icon size, afaik tango icons are in svg, it depends on your icon theme though, also to change the font size go to system->preferances->font and scale everything down 1 or 2 pixels.
