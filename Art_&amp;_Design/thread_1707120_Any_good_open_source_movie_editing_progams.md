### Post by BcRich on 2011-03-15
Kino, Open Movie Editor and Pitivi are the one's I have installed. of those I use Open Movie editor the most for simple video editing for the main reason that it just simply works. I did try cinelarra once (i think it was called the herione verson, or something strange like that) and it would have been a better Premiere 6 substitute if it worked a little better on my machine (ubuntu 9.04). None of these programs are for compositing though, at the moment the best linux compositor IMHO is Blender which also does video editing. Only thing is that if you haven't used Blender before the learning curve is quite steep.
