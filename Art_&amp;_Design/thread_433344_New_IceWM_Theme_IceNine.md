### Post by Albi on 2007-05-04
This one is actually a modification of previous ones I experimented with.  The taskbar isn't my work, it's from the  Kore-Gnometheme: [http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Kore-Gnome?content=55747](http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Kore-Gnome?content=55747), which is also the GTK theme included in the screenshot.

The window is just Thinblack modified with mac-ish buttons that glow blue when you hover.

Wallpaper is included in theme, but compressed.  Here is a better version:
[http://albi.shootdog.com/pictures/rndr2.png](http://albi.shootdog.com/pictures/rndr2.png)
[[IMG]http://img502.imageshack.us/img502/6815/iceninezt9.th.jpg[/IMG]](http://img502.imageshack.us/my.php?image=iceninezt9.jpg)


To change the background, edit the last line in the Default.theme file to point to the background you want.

Enjoy
