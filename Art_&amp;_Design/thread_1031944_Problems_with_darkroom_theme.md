### Post by Sand &amp; Mercury on 2009-01-06
IIRC, DarkRoom started life as NewHuman, which was a sample dark theme they were using for testing how applications worked with dark themes, during the Intrepid Alphas.

The results were pretty much that there are a lot of problems, and regular ol' Human took place as the default theme again, and NewHuman was rechristened to DarkRoom and thrown on the CD as an extra.

Unfortunately these kinds of problems are just what it's like, it was an afterthought by the developers.
