### Post by PrimoTurbo on 2006-11-17
I would highly suggest a black/white laser printer (I have a very cheap ML-1710 and it's awesome for documents, works with no problems under linux). The quality and the amount of money it costs for a color/cart. based printer is insane, unless you manage to get the cartridges cheap.

If you plan to print out photos, don't you won't save any money. Nearly all places now offer 1 hour digital prints and they are cheaper then regular film photography anyways.

Anyways good luck.
