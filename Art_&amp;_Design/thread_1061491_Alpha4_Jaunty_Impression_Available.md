### Post by j_baer on 2009-02-05
The alpha4 release of the Jaunty GTK Theme Impression is available for download and testing.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/Jaunty/Impression](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/Jaunty/Impression)

Please note this theme is based on the svn136 version of the Murrine engine which will need to be installed in order to test. 

This engine may break other older Murrine themes.

[https://launchpad.net/~suraia/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~suraia/+archive/ppa)

Screen shots available for those who are curious.
