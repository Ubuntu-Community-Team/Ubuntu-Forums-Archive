### Post by RemmyLee on 2008-01-01
As part of a series I am doing about the advantages of free software and the power behind them, I put together a little 3d scene in Blender.

There will be a total of 12 shots that are going to be compiled in to a calendar and sold. All proceeds will go to open source projects centered on the further development of graphical applications in Linux.

This is just an example of the work that I am doing.

[[IMG]http://farm3.static.flickr.com/2180/2153515515_af0db1be3d.jpg[/IMG]]("http://farm3.static.flickr.com/2180/2153515515_7c4fe2a999_o.png")

This was just done as an homage to one of my childhood favorites. An idea of the look I would apply to an autumn month. Using the amber tones and bills falling, symbolic to the leafs falling from trees.

What I am looking for are ideas. Copyrighted works obviously can't be used, so if anyone has something that they would like to see visualized, leave a response.
