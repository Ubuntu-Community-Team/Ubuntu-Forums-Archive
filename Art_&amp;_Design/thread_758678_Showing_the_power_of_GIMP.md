### Post by dmn_clown on 2008-04-22
> **BRAXS69 said:**
> i don't really see why people complain so much about gimp.

Because the gimp is touted as the flag ship product for free software but is not nearly as useful for graphic artists as it _should_ be.  Its great for web based images but for print and high end it is lacking.  We could use it for high end (16-32bpp) images but there was a political decision made within the project years ago that prevented the merging of the film gimp (now Cinepaint) changes back into trunk.  GEGL may rectify this if it makes it into the next stable release.

There are some places that gimp stands out, like its intuitive use of alpha channels and its default interface that works nicely on multi-headed systems, like the mac builds of photoshop.
