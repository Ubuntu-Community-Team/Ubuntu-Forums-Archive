### Post by Siberia on 2008-09-08
After a meeting in #rhythmbox last night there was a good amount of changes made to the design for the new Rhythmbox website. After coming up with a variety of slightly different design layouts I'd like the Ubuntu communities opinion on what they prefer.

Things that have/can change:
[LIST]
[*]Position of the feature navigation i.e. Overview, Devices etc.
[*]Size and alignment of the RSS/Last.fm tab icons.
[*]Colour of the top level navigation, feature navigation and feature description (where the screenshot it) background.
[/LIST]

Please feel free to leave feedback on what you think needs changed.

The outcome of this poll will not determine the aesthetics of the site indefinitely but will greatly help in finding a design a lot would be happy with.

**The original design is design 4.**
