### Post by P_Badger on 2006-11-28
I was a bit of a hard-*** about GIMP in the beginning, but after actually, you know, using it, I've found it to be the best photo manipulation program for Linux. I only use the 2.3 "unstable" branch, though, as it's got the best options. Making a bit of a game doing photoshop tutorials on Gimp, found it's not too far off from PS in usage. I think someone just posted a deb of Gimp 2.3.13 in the forums. Just do a search for "gimp".
