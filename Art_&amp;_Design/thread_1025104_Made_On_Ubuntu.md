### Post by schmindy on 2008-12-30
If you mean this one, I'm no graphic designer (though I do a bit) but here's how I did it.
1. I opened up kolourpaint and created a blank image the size I wanted. 
2.Then I went into edit>paste from file
3. I selected [this]("http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=92315&d=1226482954") image 
4.Once the image is imported do Ctrl>z
5. Now click some random tool from the sidebar
6.Now that the blue lines are gone you can enter text or pictures.
7. For my text I used: Font-Sawsdee Colors: White and #E79B6C  
8 Finally for the rounded edges I uploaded it to picnik and used the round frame tool
