### Post by drfalkor on 2007-05-14
Me and Martin(*Martin is the aurthor of mypaint*) decided to have a mypaint contest. About who can make the best mypaint logo, since mypaint currently have no logo. The winner logo will maybe be the official mypaint logo.

since mypaint is based on gtk2, it is a pluss to have the logo fit in the gtk2 interface, and that it can be used as an icon to, so it kind of blend into everything else. The logo has to be that when people see that logo, they know that its mypaint.

You have to make the logo in a open source app, like the gimp, inkscape, mypaint, krita, cinepaint etc.. since mypaint is an OOS.
Use your ideas, and show us what you can make, everything is welcome, even new ways to make a logo, use everything you've got, so long it is a logo .
