### Post by emilywind on 2010-05-16
I recently bought a Wacom Bamboo Pen as an entry-level tablet because I like to draw/paint but do not have the money to spend on traditional media, nor the space to put it as a student living in a dorm. If I upgrade at some point, it will be to a medium sized Intuos4 (or 5 or whatever one is out at the time I do).

If your girlfriend is a professional artist or you just want to get her a really nice tablet, a medium sized Intuos4 is probably the way to go. If you want to get her a nice entry-level tablet, then the Wacom Bamboo Pen is definitely amazing and reasonable as well. Cheers. :)
