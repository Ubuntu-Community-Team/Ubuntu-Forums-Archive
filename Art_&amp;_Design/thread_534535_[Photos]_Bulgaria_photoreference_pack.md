### Post by Sindwiller on 2007-08-25
Wohoo, at last. It took some time - had problems with the cam and the hosting - but it's up now.

**What's this?**

This is a photopack in .tar.bz2 format with 650MB or 259 high quality JPEG photos made in Bulgaria (Sofia, Varna, Targovishte, Goljamo Drjanovo), taken with a Sony DSC-W50. Here are some examples:

[http://xs218.xs.to/xs218/07346/dsc00555.jpg](http://xs218.xs.to/xs218/07346/dsc00555.jpg)
[http://download.tuxfamily.org/realitygames/dsc00568.jpg](http://download.tuxfamily.org/realitygames/dsc00568.jpg)

**And... what's this for?**

This package is mainly for texture artists and modelers. It contains lots of reference pictures and some inspirational pics.

**Cool, what about licensing?**

Since I'm a fanatic, there'll be three possible licenses. Either the [GPL v2.0 or newer](http://www.gnu.org/licenses/old-licenses/gpl-2.0.html) and/or [CC-by-SA 3.0](http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/).
