```
<div id="header">Site header goes here</div>

<ul>

</div>

<h1>Put your heading in here</h1>

<h2>A sub-heading goes here</h2>
<p>A paragraph goes here. <strong>This is a bold sentence (usually)</strong></p>
</div>

<div id="footer">
footer content goes here
</div>

```

Easy for humans and search engines to read. Some people get fussy with div names, but it's not mandatory.
