1) _[Cheese Webcam Booth]("http://freewaretips.myfreeforum.org/about2197.html")_ for Webcam Capture
2) I went to _[GIF Ninja]("http://gifninja.com/")_ To convert the video to a gif
3) Then used _[GIMP]("http://freewaretips.myfreeforum.org/about478.html")_ for everything else.

For the background I used my tv to capture the video, and all I used is a brush downloaded at _[DeviantART]("http://deviantART.com/")_, and the magic wand tool to change the background to something else, of course I used layers as well.

I will make a tutorial as soon as I can get to it, in the meantime you can view my other gimp tuts _[here]("http://freewaretips.myfreeforum.org/forum27.php")_.
