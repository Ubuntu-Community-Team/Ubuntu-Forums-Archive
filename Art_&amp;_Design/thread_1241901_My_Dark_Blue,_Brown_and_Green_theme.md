I've evolved the themes found in my signature even more.  I'm now using (on my home desktop) a dark blue based theme with brown and green highlights.  It's still easy on the eyes, on the darker side of midtones.

Most of the colors tweaks were done with gnome-color-chooser.  The icons are slightly modded gnome-wise and the boreders are a modded dust.  You can find the gnomecc file [here](http://z.nexuizninjaz.com/linux/themes/bluez.gnomecc).
