### Post by Løvskogen on 2010-05-27
**My thoughts on the Apperance Window**
Four tabs to set the apperance of your environment is too much, and makes it look hard to adjust these settings. I propose a solution with no tabs.

The two selection boxes, pick theme and background image, will place itself over the Apperance window while the user picks a theme or background. While hovering a theme or background, a live preview on the users desktop will be activated, so the user can see the option in instant effect.
