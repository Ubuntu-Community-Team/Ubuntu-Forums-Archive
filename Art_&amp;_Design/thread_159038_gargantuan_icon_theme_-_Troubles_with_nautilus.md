### Post by linuxmad on 2006-04-12
.. I have a FIX...

Go inside the folder of the theme. Get inside the folder 128x128 .. then stock folder.
Look inside, you wil find 2 icons named gtk-go-forward.png and gtk-go-back.png, just create a copy of them and rename them:
gtk-go-forward-ltr.png and  gtk-go-back-ltr.png
...It works like a charm:cool:
