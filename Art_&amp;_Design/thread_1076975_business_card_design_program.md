### Post by bubwitmaingay on 2009-02-22
In MS I was used to **MS Publisher** in desktop designs and so on. Now that I've shifted to Ubuntu (Linux), I found that **OpenOffice.Org Draw** is a good alternative to the Publisher.

I teach desktop publishing and I advice that you use Draw for such a project. Scribus is too unfriendly to be of use to me (at least just me). I hope that helps.

Just remember, you have to do the designing without a wizard. Creativity and exploration are crucial skills here. Thanks.;)
