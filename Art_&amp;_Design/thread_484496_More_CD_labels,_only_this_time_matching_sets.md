Hi, I managed to get the XCF file from both french_photon and Aelfric5578... What I wanted was nice looking labels that covered _All_ of the different versions of Ubuntu, Xubuntu, EDUbuntu, Kubuntu, 32bit, 64bit, live, alternate, CD, DVD, 7.04, 6.06... ect. So I fixed up what was available from the two authors and made changing the text to update it for future versions even easier from the XCF file.

The download is 40MB, which includes _Everything_ totaling 112 PNG images and the XCF file... just to warn you first ;)
