### Post by Marlonsm on 2010-06-21
It's called gradient (not sure about that, my Gimp is in Portuguese, it's called degradê here).
Just select the gradient tool (it's between the bucket and the pencil in mine), choose the colors and drag it from the top to the bottom of the image.

If you want transparency, first right click the layer and add an alpha channel to it, then use the gradient tool options to select a color-to-transparent gradient.
