### Post by Paul820 on 2007-09-18
Why don't you just cut the woman out and put her on a new background, there are some nice clean edges so it should not be difficult. You can use a mask for that or use the path tool in gimp. I would go for the mask though, you can get all the detail of the strands of hair. You can use the path tool around the card though once you get the woman on the new background, convert the path to a selection and fill it with a gradient, then put the windows logo on top. You just pick the colours from the original card, which is white and gray and then apply that gradient. It won't take long at all, the trickiest part is getting a good mask in the first place, all the rest is straight forward.
