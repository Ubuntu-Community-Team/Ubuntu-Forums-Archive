### Post by Toz on 2012-09-22
It should follow the freedesktop naming specification ([http://standards.freedesktop.org/icon-naming-spec/icon-naming-spec-latest.html]("http://standards.freedesktop.org/icon-naming-spec/icon-naming-spec-latest.html")). However, find that its not 100% exact. It is, however, a good starting place that lists the icon structure and names.
