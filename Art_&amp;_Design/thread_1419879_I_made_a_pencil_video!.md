### Post by wildhostile on 2010-03-08
Hi RabbitWho,

I'm happy to see that this software runs quite well. I installed it (windows version with wine) and find it very simple (in the positive sense).
With the version I installed, you can export your work to video (.mov but it doesn't work), swf (that's what you chose) and ... image sequence (that's the choice I advice you). Create a folder and take image sequence as output (.png images if you want to keep the alpha channel) . You will be abble to import them into video editor (Kdenlive, Cinelerra, Openshot, Pitivi, etc..) and mix them with audio. eg. ==> [http://makefx.wordpress.com/2009/01/04/stop-motion-animations-with-cinelerra-the-pesci-example](http://makefx.wordpress.com/2009/01/04/stop-motion-animations-with-cinelerra-the-pesci-example) . 
If you want to keep the dark effect produced when recording with your camera, you can imitate it by adding a gradient effect or by making light vary.
I advice you to give Mypaint a try too. You will need a graphic tablet. I think they will add to the software an animation feature (with onion skin) soonly.
