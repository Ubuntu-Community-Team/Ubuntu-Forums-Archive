### Post by tgm4883 on 2007-05-18
You can check 
[http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=150](http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=150)

Get some GDM Themes (gnome login screen) from there.  (Do not extract the archive)  To install, go into System>Administration>Login Window and click on the local tab.  Then click on add and find the archive that you downloaded.  Then click install at the bottom.  Voila, installed.  That is the installation procedure

You can make your own themes with the right software (someone has to make them, right?).  But I don't make my own so I can't point you in the right direction.
