### Post by mcduck on 2012-06-12
Do it in black & white, then add a new layer on top of that one, fill the new layer with yellow color and then set it's blending mode to "overlay". (or "color", "multiply" or whatever looks right for you. It's usually a good idea to just try different belnding modes to see which one works the best).
