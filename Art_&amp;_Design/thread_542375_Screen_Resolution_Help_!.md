### Post by smithman89 on 2007-09-03
First, make a large resolution wallpaper, then you can scale it down to smaller resolutions.  Usually the largest i see is 1600x1200 which is a 4:3 ratio.  Just remember to fix the image for widescreen aspect ratios of 16:9 and beyond.  Or make a 1600x1200 version, as well as a 1400x1050 version, then just scale those down.
