### Post by benplaut on 2005-11-22
Graphite was too dark, and no metacity theme properly complimented my mod of it, so here are the results... my first themes. 

Metacity:
[http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php?content=31634](http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php?content=31634)
Inspired by/Modded from Calmth and Clearbox.

Userbility goes best with the GTK theme of the same name, but should fit with any theme (I'm currently working to make it match GTK theme, stay tuned!)

Fitt's Law complient - the corner button goes to the exact corner pixel, and the buttons are large and easy to hit.

GTK:
[http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php?content=31632](http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php?content=31632)
*FOR CAIRO ENABLED CLEARLOOKS*

This is a mod of Graphite, made to provide a lighter theme that can still take advantage of Clearlooks-Cairo. It has animated progress bars, and as much eye candy as I could figure out how to make.

Fitt's Law complient - no border between scrollbar and edge of screen.
