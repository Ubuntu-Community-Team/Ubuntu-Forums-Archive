### Post by RemmyLee on 2008-01-25
Inspired by some of the mockups I've been seeing for the next Ubuntu release, I've started working on a theme.

[[IMG]http://farm3.static.flickr.com/2129/2219093030_37df2de176.jpg[/IMG]]("http://farm3.static.flickr.com/2129/2219093030_662f9a785e_o.png")

This is heavily inspired by Gilouche Posh (gtkrc) and Blended (metacity). The icons are nuoveXT.

I have a lot of work to do still. I believe a beveled (with rounded edges), fluid look is what I am going for. It's starting to get that feeling, but this is really my first attempt at themes.
