nah I didnt bother saving the source or anything... even deleted this from the computer, but his is how you can replicate what I did:


download that ubuntu logo banner image  you can find if you google it or on the ubuntu website.
open it with the gimp
add a black layer.
select a small brush and change jitter to 5.0 from the brush menu
change colour to white and begin to paint on the black background.
you can also use different shades of gray

now to add some colours to it you should put jitter back to 0 and select a big brush and put size to 10 or something, you want it big.
Change mode on the brush to Multiply
Pick any pretty colour you like... like red
Now paint on the image and notice how you are only colouring the white.

next put the ubuntu logo as top layter and change mode to Multiply
