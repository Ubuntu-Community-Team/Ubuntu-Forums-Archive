### Post by bashveank on 2009-05-07
Your head element is a bit big.. You could cut that "Bringing updates home" line, and the entire element around it, and then shrink the element bellow that one, and it would look much more clean.

Also, it's unclear what the <> in the upper right does until you actually click it.

Just basic SEO stuff, it's not terribly important, but it'll help: Change your page title to "Kerxy Project - free software and automatic updates for Mac, Windows, and Linux"
Google puts high priority on the site's title, and no one's searching for Kerxy Project, they're searching for free software, or automatic updates, or Mac, Windows, Linux.

Also, it's just personal preference, but I would add a big icon of the program in the upper right corner of the page that would link to the download page. It's a bit more straightforward, and people are more likely to download if they see the big enticing button, than the tab that says "download"

A better font, or at least better font color scheme, might be nice too.
