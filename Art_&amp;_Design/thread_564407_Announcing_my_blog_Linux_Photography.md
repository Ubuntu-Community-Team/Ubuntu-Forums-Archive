### Post by Curtiss on 2007-10-01
I read your blog and you have some excellent information there.

I am a  photographer also just a hobby and have been using PS Elements and Lightroom in windows. I am in the process of moving my work flow over to ubuntu cause I just love it so much. But the biggest headache is monitor calibration.

In windows I used Adobe gamma for my CRT with good results I dont have a hardware calibrator. With ubuntu I have perty much got the same results using gamma test charts and then tweaking the gamma setting in the terminal with the xgamma command.

Gimp 2.4 looks very good been playing with the beta.

Also theres LightZone for linux at [http://www-old.lightcrafts.com/linux/]("http://www-old.lightcrafts.com/linux/")

Curtiss
