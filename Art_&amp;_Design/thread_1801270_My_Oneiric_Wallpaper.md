### Post by Dry Lips on 2011-07-10
> **colkey said:**
> This is the first wallpaper I have made.
Thoughts/Improvements please :)

Well, first of all... If I were you I'd be very careful about using 
the lensflare effect in images. When I for the first time tried out
Photoshop back in 96-97, I thought lensflares were 
really cool. But in reality lensflare is an error that occurs
when taking photographs, and generally is something
photographers try to avoid. 
[https://secure.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/wiki/Lensflare](https://secure.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/wiki/Lensflare)

Secondly: If this is your first wallpaper, I'd say well done!
Don't stop playing around with GIMP; it is the way you
eventually learn to master the program. 

Don't be afraid to experiment and try out new features, 
and in the end I'm sure you'll end up as a GIMP-pro! :cool:
