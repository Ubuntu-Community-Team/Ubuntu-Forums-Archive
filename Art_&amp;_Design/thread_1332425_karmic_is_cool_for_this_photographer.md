### Post by bubblehead74 on 2009-11-20
There are a few native RAW converters for Linux available:
Bibble (proprietary, $90/$160)
LightZone (proprietary, $100)
RawTherapee (proprietary, donationware)
UFRaw (open source, free)
Rawstudio (open source, free)
