### Post by mcduck on 2010-04-17
Hold down the shift key when making new selections (the same applies to any selection tool in Gimp, and actually works in most photo editors).

edit:_ Sorry, I didn't read your post quite as thoroughly as I should have. Holding the Shift of course won't work when making selections from the menu. Instead use the option just under the "Alpha to Selection" in the same menu, called "**Add to selection**".
