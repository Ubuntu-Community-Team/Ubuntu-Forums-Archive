### Post by hobo14 on 2009-05-10
Right click the menu and choose "edit menu".

Select the item you want to change the icon of, click properties, and then click the icon in the properties window.

You will not be able to browse through the GUI here for your .png files; only .svg files show as available.  
You need to manually type the target directory in, then you will see your .png and be able to select it.
