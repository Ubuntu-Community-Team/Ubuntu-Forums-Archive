The check and radio buttons controlled by my gtk 3.0 theme are displaying strangely. As if there is another button to the left, the bottom, and the lower-right diagonal(screenshot attached). ### Post by amadis2009 on 2012-08-30
Well, after trying to figure this out for a few days, I figured it out on my own 20 minutes after I made the first post. Oh well. It came down to resizing and centering the image files of the radio and check buttons. They were too small and off center in relation to the transparent border. Just edited them in GIMP and now they look normal.
