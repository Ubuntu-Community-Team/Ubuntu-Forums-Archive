### Post by robert shearer on 2011-02-22
If you simply want to annotate on screen then there are a few options.

gromit, a stand alone app.   [http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/lucid/man1/gromit.1.html](http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/lucid/man1/gromit.1.html)

or the annotate option under compiz (if you have the CCSmanager installed)

System=>preferences=>CompizConfig settings manager=>Extras=>annotate
