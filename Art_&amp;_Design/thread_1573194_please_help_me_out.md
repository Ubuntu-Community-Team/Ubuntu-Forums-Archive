### Post by howefield on 2010-09-12
> **fibrebiz said:**
> Just wondering, is it possible to further customise those visual effects?

You have more control by installing compizconfig-settings-manager. This will put a menu entry in your System > Preferences > CompizConfig-Settings-Manager and allow you to finely tune each option for each plugin.

There is also a package of extra plugins/effects called compiz-fusion-plugins-extra should you want even more.
