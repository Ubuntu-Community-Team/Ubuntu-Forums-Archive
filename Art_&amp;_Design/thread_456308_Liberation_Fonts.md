No. The purpose of these fonts is to provide free fonts that are metric equivalents of the Microsoft fonts. That way, when these fonts are substituted for Microsoft fonts, your pagination/layout will not change. This makes a huge difference with forms, etc. where font widths are important. Currently if you load a word document in openoffice using Bitstream or other non-ms fonts, line breaks, etc. will change because the fonts are not the same widths.

Captions 5-8.9 point
Regular text 9.0-14.9 point

Display 23+ point

This probably exceeds your needs, but mine are quite specific.
