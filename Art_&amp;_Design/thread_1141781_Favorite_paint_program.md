### Post by BslBryan on 2009-04-28
Well, I highly recommend both KolourPaint and Krita, but GNU Paint is a little difficult to use, and the result is never very good, and Tux Paint is for kids.  

Really, KolourPaint is like a better MSPaint, including cool stuff like being able to make images transparent like in GIMP, but Krita has the same abilities and you have the ability to mix colours if you so choose.  

I believe that really the choice between them is personal preference and ease of use.  :)
