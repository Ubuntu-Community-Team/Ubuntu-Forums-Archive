### Post by IanW on 2007-11-17
Gutsy is the codename for the current release version (7.10).

If this is the version you're using, you can install the Flash player by using Synaptic and looking for "flash-plugin-nonfree" (You may have to enable the Universe & Multiverse repositories in "Software Sources").

You may be able to get Flash itself running under "WINE", check at [this link]("http://www.winehq.org/")
