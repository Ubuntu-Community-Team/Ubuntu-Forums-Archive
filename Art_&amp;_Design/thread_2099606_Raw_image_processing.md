### Post by PhilGil on 2012-12-29
My favorite is [RawTherapee]("http://rawtherapee.com/"). In my opinion, it offers the best combination of sensible defaults and ease of use, while still providing all of the advanced functionality you'd expect from a RAW converter.

The current crowd favorite seems to be [Darktable]("http://www.darktable.org/"), but I've never been able to wrap my head around its unconventional UI.
