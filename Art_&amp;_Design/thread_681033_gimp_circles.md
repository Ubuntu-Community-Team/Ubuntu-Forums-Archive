### Post by hsweet on 2008-01-28
You can stroke a selection (rectangle, ellipse as a circle, free-form) as a path. 

Right click on a selection, pick selection to path and dig around to stroke path.  (Sorry I'm a tiny bit fuzzy).  But that is the basic idea.

Selection->path-> stroke path

Strange, isn't it?
