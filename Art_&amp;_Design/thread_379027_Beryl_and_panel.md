### Post by adam.tropics on 2007-03-08
If you are referring to the panel, then you can change it as a whole with themes, or individual parts with a handy little utility called gnome-color-chooser. You can either get the source and compile from [here.]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php?content=47349") or get a deb package from [here.]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=293342") If you get the deb package, be sure to look for post #24, where he lists the dependencies, as I believe its a checkinstall package, which won't do that automatically for you.
