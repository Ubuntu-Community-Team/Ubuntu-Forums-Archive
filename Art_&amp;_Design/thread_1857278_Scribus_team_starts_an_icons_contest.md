### Post by prokoudine on 2011-10-10
Scribus team is starting a contest for a new icon set to improve visual consistency of the application. [Here]("http://forums.scribus.net/index.php/topic,243.0.html") is the official annopuncement, and it's also where you can submit your stuff. And [here]("http://libregraphicsworld.org/blog/entry/scribus-team-starts-a-icon-set-contest") is some background information you might finds useful. Submissions are accepted till October 31. The person who wins will deliver the rest of the icon set, alone or collaborating with somebody else.
