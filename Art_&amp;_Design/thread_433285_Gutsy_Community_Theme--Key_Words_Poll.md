### Post by nothlit.linux on 2007-05-04
Hey forum dwellers! I'm here to ask some of you to contribute to a poll on a few key words, for a community theme. [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/CommunityTheme](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Artwork/Incoming/CommunityTheme) . We've decided on an interesting way to design a concept. I've produced up with a few words that represent some of the aspects of ubuntu. Its some I've come up with, and some taken from some polling of a few Ubuntu irc channels. Anyways--we have a poll set up for these key words, and we plan to combine the two most popular into a single concept that we will build upon for this release. The poll is here, and should open up relatively soon: [https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-art/+poll/ua-community-theme-poll](https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-art/+poll/ua-community-theme-poll) . It'll close 72 hours after its opened, hopefully this is enough for most of you to catch this and take a vote. This will be a secret poll--nobody will know who has voted what.  

We hope to generate a few palettes from this, which will also be voted upon in the future. Thanks for reading this--and start voting! :)

For the sake of transparency--I'll also post the polls contents here.

For a community theme, we'd like to choose some formative words for the yet untitled project. We hope to design around the words - possibly with a motif as a result - for a central theme. Please be aware that our plan is to forge the the two most voted words into a single concept. After the words are chosen, we hope to vote upon a palette that works to support the motivating words.

     * Choice: The community theme should have choice as a central thematic. (active)
     * Collaboration: The community theme should have collaboration as a central thematic. (active)
     * Contribution: The community theme should have contribution as a central thematic. (active)
     * Creation: The community theme should have creation (non Biblical) as a central thematic. (active)
     * Friendship: The community theme should have friendship as a central thematic. (active)
     * Progress: The community theme should have progress as a central thematic. (active)

### Post by Aphorism on 2007-05-04
I cannot stress this enough -

For those of you that wish to see a healthy and viable Ubuntu art / design community, the time to step up is now.

Creating anything of any quality will take time and commitment to a project.  It also requires everyone to contribute on some level as many hands make for light work.  Unfortunately, with our current state, many hands make for *no* work.

Whether or not you disagree with the ideals is part and parcel of pushing a project like this along.  Further, there are many times in creative projects where you might fundamentally disagree with a flavor or direction but are (for either career reasons or otherwise) forced to continue along them.


Consider this a great chance to get your ideas in early and, hopefully, see an interesting project develop out of it.


Sincerely,
