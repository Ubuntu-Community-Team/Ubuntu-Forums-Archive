### Post by nakedfanatic on 2007-04-13
There's a video tutorial for doing a cartoon penguin in Blender [here]("http://www.blendernation.com/2007/04/13/penguin-modeling-videotutorial/").

However the look of the penguin example you gave would be best achieved in a vector program. (Inkscape or Xara)

Try googling for tutorials on 'glossy buttons' or 'glossy icons'. The general techniques you find will be quite easy to copy in Inkscape or Xara, and just the thing for that 3D look.

Good luck. ;-)
