### Post by smartalecks on 2006-11-28
**Photoshop:**[INDENT]1. [GIMP ]("http://www.gimp.org")
2. [Krita]("http://www.koffice.org/krita/")
3. [Pixel]("http://www.kanzelsberger.com/pixel/?page_id=12") (not free, however, and is still being developed)
[/INDENT]

**Freehand:**[INDENT]1. [Inkscape]("http://www.inkscape.org/")
2. [Xara Xtreme]("http://www.xaraxone.com/")
[/INDENT]

**Dreamweaver:**[INDENT]1. [Aptana]("http://aptana.com/") (under development, but is very usable, and very comparable to Dreamweaver)
2. [Kompozer]("http://kompozer.net/")
3. [Quanta Plus]("http://quanta.kdewebdev.org/")
4. [Bluefish]("http://bluefish.openoffice.nl/index.html")
[/INDENT]

Listed in order that I think are the best replacements (ex. Aptana is the best replacement for Dreamweaver).
If the work you need to do for your office is urgent and you are comfortable with the Windows applications then I suggest just using the Windows applications. Like all applications, it takes time to learn the OSS alternatives and to discover their features.
