Just thought I'd pass on a tip. Coming from windows, I'm spending alot of time with colour mgt in ubuntu, but  one easy thing I thought I'd pass on...

if you do critical colour work in a colou r managed workflow, BUT have a typical ubuntu 'brown' or 'blue' tinged theme, you will see colours as warm or cold. Ubuntu has a really easy wa y of choosing your background and sidebars to be 50% grey (neutral), just dial the colours to #808080 - instant neutral grey to visually judge your colours
