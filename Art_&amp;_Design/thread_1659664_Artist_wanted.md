### Post by JakeFrederix on 2011-01-04
Hey everyone,

I'm in the process of designing a game. A scrabble clone.
Still looking for someone to do a decent job on the tiles.
I tried it myself, and I managed an ordinary tile. It's not as good as I'd like though.

The game will also support 'themes', meaning you can choose from various background/tile combinations.
So this is a good chance to be creative.

Requirements;
26 files, each depicting a scrabble 'tile'
size; 64x64 pixels
preferably 3D, properly shaded
NOT marked to show pointvalue (since this is language-dependent)

1 background (an old wooden desk, a cluttered tabletop, ....)

The game allows the user to rescale the tiles (readability), up to twice and down to half it's normal size.
(This is taken care of by the ImageIO API)

Once it's done, I'm still looking for someone to actually make a .deb out of it. (Haven't found a manual that says how to make a .jar into a .deb)
