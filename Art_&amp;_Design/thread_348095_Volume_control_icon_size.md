### Post by CarpKing on 2007-01-30
To avoid this problem in the future, I'd suggest you look for themes where the volume icon is in scalable vector graphics (.svg) format.  Once you know where that icon (as well as the Rhythmbox icon) is located within the theme's .tar.gz, you can replace those icons in other themes with scalable versions.
