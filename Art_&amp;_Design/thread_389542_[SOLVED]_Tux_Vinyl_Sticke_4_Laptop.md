### Post by hikaricore on 2007-03-21
My guess is that it was custom made.

Check your local area for Sign Shops or Promotional Companies.

I used to work for a place in NE Ohio that did signs and tshirts.
We had a customized application where I could import any image,
trace it, and cut it right to sticker vinyl.

Hell for something that size most places won't charge you more than 5-10$
depending on the color and type of vinyl you pick out.  Reflective is usually
more than what I see in the pic which is plain white.  They're easy to apply
as well.

Hope this is of some help. :)
