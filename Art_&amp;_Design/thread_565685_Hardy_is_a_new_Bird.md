[CENTER]**[SIZE="4"][COLOR="Lime"]Hardy is a new Bird[/COLOR][/SIZE]**[/CENTER]
As of today , October the second, Gutsy Gibbon (7.10) is finished as far as art goes. From here on , we begin work on Hardy Heron (8.04). As posted, Hardy will be a LTS release , and with that comes the task of redefining the look of Ubuntu.

*[CENTER]:lolflag:This is where you come in.:lolflag:[/CENTER]*

 The big guys on top want to change this. Starting with Hardy , we move to [COLOR="Orange"]**Orange**[/COLOR]  and **Black** *(unofficially)* for the default look.
   
 If you want to help us make Hardy look better , please do so. .
Please feel free to join the mailing list at  [https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-art](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-art)
and chat with us on the #ubuntu-artwork channel about this.
