### Post by King_Critter on 2007-11-07
So here's my situation: I have a picture of a person. Lucky for me, the photo has a very dark background, so I simply whip out the "Select regions by colour" tool and then use the eraser to get rid of the  background. The problem is that certain parts of the photo I don't want erased are selected, due to their being dark. So I need a way to git rid of the extra selections I don't want. 

I'm sure there's a way to do this, but I can't find it. Any help?

---

### Post by Paul820 on 2007-11-07
Use the free select tool and zoom in to the part you want to take from the selection, then press and hold the control key and draw around the part you want to keep.
