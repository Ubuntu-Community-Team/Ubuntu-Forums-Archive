### Post by oedipuss on 2008-12-09
It should be one of the standard colors, like fg[SELECTED] , etc.
Changing it to affect just the panel can be a bit more tricky, you'd have to create a style and assign it to the correct class of widgets.
check out this tutorial [http://live.gnome.org/GnomeArt/Tutorials/GtkThemes](http://live.gnome.org/GnomeArt/Tutorials/GtkThemes)
