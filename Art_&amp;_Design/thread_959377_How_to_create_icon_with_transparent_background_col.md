### Post by smartboyathome on 2008-10-26
If you start a new image in GIMP, it will have a white background. To get a transparent background, you can follow these steps:
*Denotes optional

*1) Select background with magic wand tool.
*2) If you did the above, then feather it to your desired amount of pixels.
3) Go to Layers > Transparency > Color to alpha
4) select the background color, and then click OK.
5) You are done. :D
