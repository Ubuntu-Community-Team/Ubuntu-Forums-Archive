### Post by Marlonsm on 2011-03-11
If the top of your head is not in the picture, neither is anything in its height, so you'll need not only to add the top of your head, but also everything in the top of the picture.
I would make a new file, the same size as the original picture, but slightly higher, copy the original picture in it, paste the top of your head. And them use something like the stamp tool (not sure about this name) to fix the rest of the picture, it is, the blank part around the top of your head.
