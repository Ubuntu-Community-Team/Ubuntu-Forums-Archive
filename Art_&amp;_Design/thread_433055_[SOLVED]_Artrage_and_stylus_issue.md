### Post by rax_m on 2008-01-06
In case anyone else was having this issue I just wanted to let you know that I know have it solved. 

Thanks to a user on the Artrage forums, I found that if I compile WINE from source then I do have pressure sensitivity in Artrage thru wine. 
Also all tools seem to work fine now unlike previously. 

BTW I am also using Gutsy now. 

WINE version installed is 0.9.52
