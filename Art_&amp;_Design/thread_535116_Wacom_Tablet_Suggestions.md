### Post by graigsmith on 2007-08-27
even a 4x5 wacom tablet is HIGHLY useful compared to a mouse.  i had a 4x5 tablet, and later upgraded to a 6x8.  the 6x8 is a nice size. honestly it's sensitive enough, you wont miss not having a 9x12.   both the 4x5 and the 6x8 i have were both really great.   the nice thing about the 4x5 is that it's compact.   6x8 seems like the perfect size for general use.  a 9x12 tablet, is HUGE.  and while im sure it's nice. theres not much of a need for it.  even the 4x5 is super sensitive.  i had no problem drawing on either.  one thing though, make sure the drawing surface is clean. if you draw even with a hair on it, you could scratch the surface.  though the surface is replacable, and a scratch doesn't ruin the tablet or anything. it's a good idea to clean it before you use it.

if you have a really really large monitor consider getting a bigger tablet.  because the tablet size maps to the screen.
