### Post by PC_load_letter on 2010-01-08
Right click 3 times to create 3 points. Left click + hold SHIFT to select the 3 points. then hit the angle icon to create ANY angle.

Now, while the 3 points are selected click Measure-->Angle. A box will appear w/ the measure of the angle. Now if you start move one of the points around the angle measurement will change accordingly. Move it around until you get the desired angle. 

If you don't like the angle measurement to show, click on it and CTRL+del to delete it.
