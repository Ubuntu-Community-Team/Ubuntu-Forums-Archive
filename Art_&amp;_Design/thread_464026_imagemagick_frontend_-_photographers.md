I just found out something today, that might be useful for photographers:

imagemagick has a native front-end for manipulating and previewing photographs. 
At the terminal just type in :
display imageFileName

This will allow you to adjust brightness, saturation, sharpness etc 

Idea for those people who don't want to or can't use Lightzone and other tools
