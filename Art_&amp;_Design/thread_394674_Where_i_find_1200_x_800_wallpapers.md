### Post by sethmaclaren on 2008-12-24
Deviantart is a great location to go for wallpapers. Just go to [http://browse.deviantart.com/customization/wallpaper/?order=9](http://browse.deviantart.com/customization/wallpaper/?order=9)

You should be able to find something there. If not, check out InterfaceLift, as mentioned above. 

Or you can probably even find some stuff on Flickr that will fit your screen size nicely. It really all depends on what type of wallpaper you'd like.

### Post by .arean on 2008-12-24
[http://www.customize.org]("http://www.customize.org") is nice as well. A lot of the stuff there can be found on deviantart but there's a lot of stuff that isn't.
