### Post by ARhere on 2007-11-24
I noticed a lack of CD art, (*well, generic english art*) so I took a day of my time to create some, two for the live CD and one for the alt install CD.

They are a little small, 700x700 but I designed them for my Epson R300 which has a CD printer and should work well with any other ink jet with a tray for printing directly on CD&#347;.

I tried hard to reuse only current gnome or Ubuntu art or recreated it myself to keep in the spirit of Open/Free.  If any of the _official_ members of the Ubuntu team see something that is not good, please do not hesitate to let me know and I will fix it.  Also, I used gimp to create these and would be happy to share the layers for templates.
