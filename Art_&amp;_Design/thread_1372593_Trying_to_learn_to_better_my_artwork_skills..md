### Post by Niva on 2010-01-04
That's a loaded question man.  Art is my main hobby I would say though I make money sometimes from it I wouldn't call myself pro.

The best online resources for 2d art is:

[http://conceptart.org](http://conceptart.org)

The best online resource for 3d art and technical information for software packages like photoshop and autodesk products:

[http://forums.cgsociety.org/](http://forums.cgsociety.org/)

If you really want to become a better artist, and by what you said it sounds like you do.  I recommend taking a figure drawing class and some real life drawing classes in your area.  They're usually very cheap and until you do it, you won't know how important.  Learning from books and tutorials is good, but the real life learning with someone who is an experienced artist already (or group of) is better.

Good luck!
