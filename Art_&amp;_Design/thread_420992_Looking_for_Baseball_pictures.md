### Post by Jussi Kukkonen on 2007-04-24
There are quite a lot of images with a Creative Commons license on flickr -- the problem is that their search doesn't include a "using-this-license"-argument. Fortunately many people tag  their images with "cc". So do a search on "baseball cc" in Flickr (and check the licenses, that cc tag could be something else too)

Some links that might help:
[http://www.everystockphoto.com/](http://www.everystockphoto.com/)
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Public_domain_image_resources](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Public_domain_image_resources)
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Free_image_resources](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Free_image_resources)
At least the last one seems to have some sports/baseball links
