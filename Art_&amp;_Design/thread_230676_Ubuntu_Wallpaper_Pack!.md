[SIZE="6"][Ubuntu Wallpaper Team]("http://www.freemediaresources.com/ubuntu/"): Pack 1[/SIZE]

This is a new pack of 14 original wallpapers (5 with modifications) with an Ubuntu theme made by the wallpaper team (only me at the moment).
New packs will be released every few weeks with new wallpapers and updates to old wallpapers.

**If you would like to join and contribute**, create wallpapers and email them with a description to [email]tr4veller@gmail.com[/email]. They will be included in the next pack and you will be credited.
