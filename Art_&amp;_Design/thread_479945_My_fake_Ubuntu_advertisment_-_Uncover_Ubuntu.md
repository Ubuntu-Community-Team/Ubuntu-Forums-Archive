### Post by ScottY_ on 2007-06-21
Thanks guys :)
I suppose it can look like Ubuntu is falling off :o but that would never do.

@ King - basically all i did was take the image from the above link, i did some hue/saturation to it, but checked colour so it change the whole images colour to the same hue. That got me the base sepia effect. 
I then used the Clone Stamp tool, to remove some of the falling/ripped poster and leave more wood exposed, took some time but eventually i got it looking good enough for me at 1am lol. 
Then i got myself a vector of the Ubuntu logo off of wikipedia in .svg i opened that into illustrator and copied it into photoshop, rasterized it after enlarging the vector and placed a white background rectangle behind it.
Hit Ctrl+T>right-click>distort, and moved the edges to the right positions so it covered the existing poster area. I then set the blend mode to overlay, think i duplicated just the logo and merged that onto so it was bolder, can't remember.
I did originally start by using a layer mask, but that was hiding the layer and ended up with a darker wood, so i scrapped that idea and just used a white brush on the normal layer and painted where i saw old paper. It took quite a bit of work to get it to look like that but i'm happy with it, and obviously you are too xD
