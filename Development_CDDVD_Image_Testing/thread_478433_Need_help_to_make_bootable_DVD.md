### Post by ruibernardo on 2007-07-17
Hi

you don't need to make the CD (DVD in this case) bootable. You just need to burn the image on the media the right way and make sure the CD drive is the first boot device on the BIOS of the computer (at least before the hard drive).


To burn the image look this page: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)
