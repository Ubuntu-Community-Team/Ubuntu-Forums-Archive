### Post by Henrik on 2007-03-07
Friday saw the release of Herd 5. This is the last test CD before the [beta release]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/FeistyReleaseSchedule"). We are well past feature freeze and are focusing solely on bug fixing. 

Please [download]("http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/feisty/herd-5/") and do some test installs if you can ([mirror]("http://ftp.acc.umu.se/mirror/cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/feisty/herd-5/")). 

We used the (still ad-hoc) ISO tracker in launchpad for the pre-Herd 5 candidates. For Herd 5 images, please post your general experiences here in the forum and actual bugs in Launchpad. As we approach beta and RC we plan to do something slightly more elegant. I have a mock-up [here]("http://people.ubuntu.com/%7Ehenrik/testing/feisty/beta/ubuntu-schedule.html").
