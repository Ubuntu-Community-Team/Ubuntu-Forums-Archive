### Post by pochu on 2007-01-27
Hi flamingandromeda!

The right place to report the issues you find is [Launchpad]("http://bugs.launchpad.net"). You should go there and report bugs against ubuntu packages (if you don't know the package, do it against [ubuntu]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+filebug") itself).

But you can also post here the problems you've found. What you can do (and what I recommend you) is to report bugs in Launchpad, and then post here the links to the bugs. Doing that you let us to go to the bug reports and to help debugging them, confirming them...

Thanks for your testing, and welcome!
If you have any question, don't hesitate to ask me (you can post here, or send me a PM or an email).

Regards
Pochu
