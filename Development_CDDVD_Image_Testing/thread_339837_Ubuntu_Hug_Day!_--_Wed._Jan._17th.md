### Post by Henrik on 2007-01-16
[Simon writes]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-bugsquad/2007-January/000299.html") that tomorrow is [Ubuntu Hug Day]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuBugDay").

**Hug Day:** *This is a very special Bug Day: on Hug Day, when someone closes a bug, then someone else should hug him/her. Why? This is a very special way for us to tell everyone that we love contributions! And triaging bugs is a really big contribution.*

So if you have an interest in testing you should join other testers and bug triagers in the #ubuntu-bugs IRC channel on irc.freenode.net. There will be lots of experienced folks around who can give advice on testing, bug filing, triaging and fixing(!)
