### Post by ynnhoj on 2008-05-14
you could use conky to write the date and time to your desktop; it doesn't have to be very big.  and you ought to be able to set it up so programs cannot go over top of it.  or you could have a small xclock that's always on top, or [the dali clock](http://www.jwz.org/xdaliclock/) is nifty too.

as for sound control: you can map certain key combinations to *amixer* commands to adjust the volume.  i used to use ctrl+shift+up to turn up the volume, and ctrl+shift+down to turn it down, etc.
