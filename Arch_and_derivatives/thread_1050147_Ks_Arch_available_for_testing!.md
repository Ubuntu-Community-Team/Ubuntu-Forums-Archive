Ks Arch v.0.1 Alpha Build 2 Released!

**What is Ks Arch?**
I have some problems on telling others what it is, but I do at the moment call it a "script library". What you to is install it, then launch it form your menu and Firefox should open a offline website (located in /usr/share/ksarch/) which contains tutorials giving information about something.

Ususally that thing us quite "complicated" or takes time and research to do, therefore you can find (in the tutorial) a command whihc will launch a bash script which again will to all the dirty work for you.

Ks Arch is however an really early development, but I decided to release it so early because then I can get tips form other people and have a chance to fix typos, errors and bugs in my coding.
