### Post by kpkeerthi on 2008-02-28
Another reason why I love Arch...

A couple of days back... I guess, I was trying to link my mobile phone and laptop (of course running Arch) via bluetooth. I noticed that the bluetooth packages in the repo were outdated and I flagged them out-of-date. 

To my surprise I noticed today that they were [prompty upgraded]("http://www.archlinux.org/packages/search/?q=bluetooth") to the most recent versions by the maintainers.

I encourage Arch users to keep a tab on their favorite packages. Vote for them (in AUR) and track the latest versions and flag them (both in main repos & AUR) if they are found out of date. I think the maintainers are doing a great job.

Thank you very much.
