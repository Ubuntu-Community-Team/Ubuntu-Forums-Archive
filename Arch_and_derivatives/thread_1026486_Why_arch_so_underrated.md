So does that amount to more control?  Well, AFAICT you can control one as much as the other.  But less abstraction and automation means there are fewer barriers to gaining direct control.

You could also look in gnome-looks.org for Arch start buttons, or in deviantART..... you can even ask in the Artwork section of the Arch forums.
