### Post by Rumor on 2008-05-17
Several of us who use Arch, either dual-booting or exclusively, came to Arch via Ubuntu. 

Speaking for myself, I was comfortable in the Ubuntu forums. I "knew" people here. From time to time people posted questions about Arch and once in a great while I knew the answer. At some point a few of the threads got merged into the Arch Linux Talk thread that eventually turned into an unmanageable beast. There seemed to be enough interest in Arch to justify its own sub-forum to answer questions about the distro and installation and whatnot. We pitched it to the forum mods and they agreed.

Some folks who are very new to Arch go cautiously,asking a lot of questions. I think they feel "safer" asking them here in a "more newbie friendly" forum. 

I find the Arch forums quite new user friendly and very knowledgeable. I think some folks find them intimidating.
