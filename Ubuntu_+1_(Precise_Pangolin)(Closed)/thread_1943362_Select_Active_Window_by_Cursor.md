### Post by Gregory Lee on 2012-03-19
The application "Advanced Settings" has a setting: Advanced Settings > Windows > Window Focus Mode > {Click, Mouse, Sloppy}.  The "Mouse" setting may be what you're looking for.  (I use "Sloppy".)
