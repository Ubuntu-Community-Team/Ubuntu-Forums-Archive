But is it based on gnome 2 also? If so it will go that way soon too. I googled but couldn't find what shell it's based on.This looks ominous though from xfce wikipedia
"It is based on the GTK+ 2 toolkit (the same as GNOME). It uses the Xfwm window manager, described below. Its configuration is entirely mouse-driven, and the configuration files are hidden from the casual user."

Xfce was initialled based on Xforms but was rewritten to take advantage of the GTK+2 toolkit. It's not really "based" on Gnome 2 as much as they both used the GTK toolkit (hence the similarity in appearance and to some extent, functionality). Xfce is planning to migrate to the new GTK+3 framework after the next (4.10) release. See: [http://wiki.xfce.org/releng/4.10/roadmap]("http://wiki.xfce.org/releng/4.10/roadmap")
