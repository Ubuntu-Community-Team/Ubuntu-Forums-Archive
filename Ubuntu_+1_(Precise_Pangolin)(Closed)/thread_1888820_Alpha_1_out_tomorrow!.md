The fourth is just a cosmetic thing that makes me crazy.

But, all in all, this looks very good to me. Most of the old Unity-2D flaws are fixed so I can fairly easily use Unity-2D or revert to the classic "fallback" session, and Lubuntu looks great other than that one broken icon in the panel.
