I think you have a kind of wrong idea of the way LTS releases are built.

12.10 will be the start of development that leads to the next LTS.

The releases in between the LTS's are nice.  Fun if you want to see what is new.  But their real function is to find the packages that will fit in the LTS and make sure they are stable before getting there.

It does not take a lot of space on your drive to install the testing release.  You can help make sure that the next LTS is a good one by doing so.
