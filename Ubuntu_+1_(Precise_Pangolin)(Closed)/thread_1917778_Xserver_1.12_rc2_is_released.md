### Post by Harry33 on 2012-01-30
This newest xserver rc2 version from xserver 1.12 branch has been released and is available from the Xorg-edgers PPA.
In case you want to try that, remember you also need to upgrade all input and video drivers at the same time.

Also, xserver 1.11.4 has been released.
Anyone trying that should remember that Precise has a special Canonical flavour of the xserver 1.11.

I just installed xserver 1.12.rc2 and it works very well with my nvidia-current_295.09 proprietary driver.
