Nautilus is the file manager that looks like the little folder underneath the Ubuntu Logo on the Unity Launcher.

Skype is a 3rd party program  that will help you talk back and forth with friends over the internet using audio/visual  interfaces and you can even call somebody up!

 You open these program by clicking on the Unity Logo in the Unity Launcher to Open the Unity Dash.. then.. at the bottom of the dash you will see a little logo that looks like a stack of books side by side, click that and then you will see available programs.

You may have to download Skype.
