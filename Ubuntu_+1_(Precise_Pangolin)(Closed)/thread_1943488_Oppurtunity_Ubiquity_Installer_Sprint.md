Here's a great opportunity for some less stress get your feet wet iso testing.

[https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-qa/2012-March/002050.html](https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-qa/2012-March/002050.html)

Feel free to coordinate testing here on the forums as well; in addition to the iso tracker of course. Thanks and happy testing!
