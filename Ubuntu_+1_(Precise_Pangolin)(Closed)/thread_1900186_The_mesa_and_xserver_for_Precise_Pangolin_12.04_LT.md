### Post by Harry33 on 2011-12-25
It was planned at the UDS, that Precise would have xserver 1.11 and mesa 7.12 (= 8.0).
The problem was that xserver 1.11 matured without multitouch support
and that xserver 1.12 will mature too late to be supersafe for LTS.

Now, Canonical seems to have resolved this case by pulling X Input 2.2 (the multitouch environment) from the xserver 1.12 into Ubuntu's xserver 1.11.

See more of this here:
[http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=news_item&px=MTAzMzc](http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=news_item&px=MTAzMzc)
