### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-03-07
The upper case F had me going me for a while. It is very difficult to press super+shift+f but it does not work and is not needed. So, I tried again.

A press with the left fore finger on super holding it down, quickly followed by a shape tab with the right fore finger on the f key (or F as it is seen on the keyboard).

You do not need to hold down the F key and the Dash File lens will appear and remain open as long as the super key is held down.

It is the same with the other lenses super+A (applications), super+M (music) and super+V (Video). You do have to follow the press on the super key very quickly with the A, F, M, and V key presses for this to work.
