### Post by drs305 on 2012-01-31
You can click the Dash HOME button (upper left icon), then type 'gnome-terminal' and press ENTER. Works for other apps as well as long as you know the name to type (such as 'Additional Drivers').

If you use this method, just start typing 'gnome-terminal' until its icon appears first on the screen. Then press ENTER to open it.

I assume the keyboard shortcuts work as well but I'm not on Precise to verify them.
