### Post by mc4man on 2012-04-11
If you have no use for the indicator at all then 
```
sudo apt-get purge indicator-messages
```

I find it useful for email notifications in gmail, particularly with 'unity-mail' installed & configured
(though often after receiving notification will just open TB instead of gmail directly
