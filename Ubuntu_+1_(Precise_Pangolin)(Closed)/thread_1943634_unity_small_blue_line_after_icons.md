### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-03-19
No it is not a bug. I have tested Unity 5.2, 5.4 and 5.6. The test specifically asked to see if the feature worked.

Move your mouse over an icon and the area taken by that ghostly shadow will reveal as a kind of backlight to the icon.

It is a way of showing you which icon the mouse pointer is over. It is especially useful when using the tab and arrow keys to navigate through the listings in the Dash for then we do not have a pointer to tell us what icon we are about to press Enter on.
