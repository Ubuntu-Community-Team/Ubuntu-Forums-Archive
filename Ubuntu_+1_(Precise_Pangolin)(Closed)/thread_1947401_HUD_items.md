### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-03-27
Think of HUD as a kind of activities monitor. It has to learn from us as we use the machine. Not all applications work with the HUD.

I, personally do not find a use for the HUD. I imagine that it might be useful on a tablet PC without a keyboard but an on-screen keyboard instead. Navigating a structured menu must be very difficult on a tablet without a keyboard. I do not know because I do not have a tablet PC. But this is where I see the usefulness of HUD.

The Dash is closer to a search engine.

We should not expect the HUD to do what an application does. It is a way of getting to the application's menu without going through the drop-down menus of the application or using a keyboard shortcut. But first we have to show HUD the way to the application menu that we are looking for.

Regards.
