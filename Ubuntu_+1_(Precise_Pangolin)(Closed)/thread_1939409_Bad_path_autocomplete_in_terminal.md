### Post by Nick Payne on 2012-03-11
With some commands, the autocomplete of path information in terminal doesn't work correctly in 12.04 beta (up to date as of earlier today). For example, if I enter "cd /ho" and press Tab, then autocomplete gives me "cd /home/", which is what should happen, ready for the next part of the path.

However, if I enter "cat /ho" and press Tab, it autocompletes to "cat /home ", with a space after home and no "/", which means I have to hit backspace and then slash before I can continue with the path.
