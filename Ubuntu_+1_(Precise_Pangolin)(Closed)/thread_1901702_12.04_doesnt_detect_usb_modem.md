### Post by cariboo on 2012-01-01
It looks like a problem with usb-modeswitch, as it's seeing the data partition, but not the hardware portion of the device. It may be worth creating a bug report against usb-modeswitch.

Follow the instruction on this [page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ReportingBugs"), have a look at the tips and tricks section for collecting information when you don't have a working installation.
