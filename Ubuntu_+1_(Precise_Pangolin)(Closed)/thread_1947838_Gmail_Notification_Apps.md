### Post by neu5eeCh on 2012-03-27
What I've tried and am trying (on 12.04):

[Gmail Watcher]("http://www.ubuntugeek.com/how-to-install-gmail-watcher-on-ubuntu-11-10-using-ppa.html")  & [here]("http://www.webupd8.org/2011/08/ubuntu-email-notifier-gmailwatcher-gets.html").

The fatal flaw: Doesn't update what E-Mails have been read, but continues piling up notifications until one is forced to click on each E-Mail within the app itself.

[Unity Mail]("http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2011/08/unity-mail-gmail-count-desktop-launcher-ubuntu/")

That fatal flaw: Flaky performance. After each suspend or reboot, one must right click on the Unity Mail icon (on the launcher), choose "change account details" and click "apply" before the app once again provides a "mail count". It also confuses gmail accounts, retaining deleted accounts. For example: If I lift click on the "Unity Mail" icon, FF opens a gMail for an account I have deleted (in the app).

Are there any other Unity Gmail apps you can recommend that "just work"?
