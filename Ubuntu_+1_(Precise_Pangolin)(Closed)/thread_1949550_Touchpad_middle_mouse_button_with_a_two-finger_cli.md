### Post by xyzzyman on 2012-04-11
[http://unix.stackexchange.com/questions/27450/mint-12-middle-click-with-two-finger-tap-on-touchpad](http://unix.stackexchange.com/questions/27450/mint-12-middle-click-with-two-finger-tap-on-touchpad)

Right-click is the default option as even though most touchpads have both left/right buttons, middle-click is rarely used. I use two-finger tap for right click all the time as I don't see the point in moving my hand down to the right-click button, plus on my notebook you can hear it when you click the button and I find that embarrassing if other people have to hear it. I wanted to go punch a woman at the library who kept clicking her left touchpad button instead of just tapping. It was made worse as it was constant because she was playing a flash game on facebook.
