### Post by zosky on 2012-03-24
i've got the same issue in mint12 ("kubuntu oneiric")

i found [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=759389"), where zeneti (?) is giving the same error

the same solution worked for me 
(replace $USER with your userName)

```
su $USER -c 'xhost local:$USER; grub-customizer'
```
