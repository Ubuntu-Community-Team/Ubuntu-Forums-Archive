### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-03-19
It is supposed to retain the last searched for item. You will notice that when you open the Dash and it still has the last searched for item in the panel, that it is highlighted. Just press Backspace and that old search item will disappear and the Home lens will reveal.

This is intended behaviour that has been tested prior to inclusion in 12.04 Beta 1.

Regards.
