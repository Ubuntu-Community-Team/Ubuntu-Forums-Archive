### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-03-02
According the keyboard shortcuts overlay in the Menu Bar section alt+F10 will access the menus of the app indicators but the Windows section says that alt+F10 maximizes the current window.

If you press alt+F10 the silly thing will try to do both tasks and it will succeed in maximizing the window but fail to open the app indicator menus.

Instead you will lose all window response and access to the app indicator menus (including shutdown). The Launcher will still reveal but not the Dash.

This is fixed by the very latest updates. Well, almost fixed. Now, the silly thing can do both things at once. Which I see as a work around and not a fix.

Without these updates the only way out that I could find was a reset or switch off at the computer case and not pressing alt+F10.

So watch out those who are using the beta download iso from before today.
