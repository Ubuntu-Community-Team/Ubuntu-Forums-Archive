### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-01-07
So, what's the problem? I am sorry. I am joking you.

Let me understand clearly. The system is notifying you of a problem but when you click Report - nothing happens?

Or, do you want to know what happens when you click Report?

Check out the software centre to see if something called Apport is installed. This utility detects crashes and when you click Report it generates a report about the crash and your system and it will open a browser window at the Launchpad bug reporting page. There you can check to see if you have the same problem as other people and confirm that this bug is definitely happening or log it as a new bug.

You will need to have a Launchpad account. If you have a Single Sign-on Account you may be able to get access to Launchpad with that.
