I understand why you feel this way (in case someone else uses your computer and gets presented with this material they weren't expecting in their search results). However... other people should not be using your account. They should be using the Guest account, or have an account created for them. Zeitgeist search results are account-specific, so they don't show things done in other people's user accounts.

And yes I know that sometimes people are looking over your shoulder as you're using your computer and might see unsavory search results appearing briefly in the Dash. I just thought I'd get on my soapbox about user accounts :-)

Personally, with Zeitgeist, I'd like it to log files that I've opened via dragging and dropping them onto program windows or Launcher icons. Currently it doesn't. It's annoying - you do a search and get no results - "I'm sure that's what the file is called, and I'm sure I opened it earlier.". It's because you didn't actually double-click it in Nautilus, that it hasn't been logged.
