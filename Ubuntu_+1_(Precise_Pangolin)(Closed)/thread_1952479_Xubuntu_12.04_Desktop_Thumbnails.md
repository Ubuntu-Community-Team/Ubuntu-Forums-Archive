### Post by motorcity909 on 2012-04-04
Hi VTPoet

I've just tried 12.04 beta 2 in a virtual machine and have found the new thumbnail setting.

Settings > Settings Manager > Desktop > Icons tab

And there is a new tick box for 'show thumbnails' and as you can see from the attached screenshot, the thumbnail of a picture of a monkey (just grabbed off Wikipedia for this test!) is indeed showing.

This is something I've missed since moving to Xubuntu so glad to see it's here.
