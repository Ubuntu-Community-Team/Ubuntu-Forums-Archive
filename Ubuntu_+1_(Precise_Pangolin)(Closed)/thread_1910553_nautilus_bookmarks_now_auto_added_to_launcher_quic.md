### Post by mc4man on 2012-01-17
Now all bookmarks & the default locations under 'Computer' will be shown in the Home-folder launcher icon's quicklists. 
They will be shown A-Z; a-z, though that can be slightly manipulated.

At least here the downside is the quicklist entries only open in same nautilus window so to open a location in a new window I have to first open a new window, then use the quicklist.

So from here the previous method of a custom nautilus-home.desktop was superior & more configurable as far as order displayed, what was displayed & using new window 
(but you can't please everybody
