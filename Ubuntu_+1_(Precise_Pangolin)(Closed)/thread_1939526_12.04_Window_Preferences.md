### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-03-11
If you press and hold the super key you will get a keyboard shortcut overlay that will tell what keys are doing what.

If you go to system Settings (power cog menu) and open the Keyboard Layout utility and select options you will get the opportunity to change the Alt key behaviour. That might do what you want.

Regards.
