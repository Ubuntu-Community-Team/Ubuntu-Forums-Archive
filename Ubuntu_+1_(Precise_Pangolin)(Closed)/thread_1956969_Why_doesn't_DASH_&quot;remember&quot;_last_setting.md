### Post by cariboo on 2012-04-11
Use the search function to find the programs you are looking for, you can usually find what you're looking for using the first 3 letters of the app name.

Another thing is are you just searching in the dash home, are you looking in the Applications screen?

[LIST]

[*]Super+v = videos
[/LIST]

Hold down the Super key to see all the keyboard shortcuts.
