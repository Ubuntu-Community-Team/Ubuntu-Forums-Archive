### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-03-09
If you hold down the super key you will get a keyboard shortcuts overlay. You will notice that alt+tab is now used to switch between application windows in the same workplace.

It is not a bug. Just because things are done differently  it does not mean things are not working right.

I have shared in three tests of Unity in the last couple of months. The alt+tab behavior is something that I tested  to be working the way it was supposed to work.

You will find a lot of things different in 12.04 even compared to 11.10. It is all by design.

Please read Mark Shuttleworth's blog

[http://www.markshuttleworth.com/archives/1027](http://www.markshuttleworth.com/archives/1027)

This is the way things are meant to go.
