### Post by effenberg0x0 on 2011-10-27
I'm not sure this should be held here at Ubuntu+1. We know the options PP will ship with for DE.

People that need a lighter desktop, whatever option they choose (gnome, lxde, xfce, etc) and for whatever reason, are at the **General Help** section right now, for sure. They are the ones that, right now, need to learn the options they have to overcome hardware limitations or fulfill their personal expectations and preferences better. It is not something that related to U+1, but to Ubuntu current.[B] Installing any of these options is in no way different on PP or Ubuntu current.
[/B] 
Bringing all the Unity haters together inside U+1 will make it very difficult for us to actually discuss PP default DE issues, and other subjects that refer to PP only. It will also stimulate more newbies to attempt a suicidal update to **pre-alpha/alpha** PP, turning this section into the "fix my ****" one and, again, deviating from the serious testing purposes. It's too soon for standard users/hax0rs to realize they can update to PP.

And I'm confused as to the **fallback** term too. For me, a fallback refer to making udev-fallback-graphics work properly and load the default non-accelerated desktop (ubuntu-2d session) when everything else fails. A DE choice is not a system fallback but a user preference that fits loosely, if at all, with supporting current Ubuntu strategy. 

Just my two cents.

Regards,
Effenberg


**EDIT**
PS: And worse, the term fallback imply auto-recovering from fail. I definitely don't feel good about people considering current Ubuntu default DEs as fails.
