### Post by ubuntu27 on 2012-04-01
I think it looks for covers from amazon.com and other online stores. But, if you have a picture/photo with cover.jpg as the name in the same directory as the music, then it will pickup as the cover.

I like to have high quality cover inside my album directory. :popcorn:
