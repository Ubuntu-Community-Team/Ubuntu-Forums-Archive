### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-03-27
The Dash is not something that is easily customised, especially by someone who has only been using Ubuntu for one month. What will you do when you break the operating system?

What you see in the Dash are things called Scopes and Lenses.

If you want to learn how to make you own scopes and lenses then it would be good to learn to search the web.

Try searching for ubuntu lens scopes and see what you get. This is what I would have to do if I was to find information for you.

By the way. A lot of people complained about the four or five icons that you called the default. This is why they have been removed in 12.04.
