### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-03-31
Is this the partial upgrade message?

It comes and goes depending upon what packages are listed as needing to be downloaded and which ones can actually be downloaded. Do not accept the offer of a partial update/upgrade. It will remove packages in expectation of replacing them but when they do not get replaced because they are not yet ready, you could end up with a broken system.

Just click close and update as normal. I have been doing this since November.

Regards.
