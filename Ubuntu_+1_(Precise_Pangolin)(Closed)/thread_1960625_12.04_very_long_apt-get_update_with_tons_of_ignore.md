### Post by cortman on 2012-04-17
You can't "get rid of the ignored". That means that it checked that particular repository list, and yours is up-to-date. "Get" means that it updated your repo list in /var/lib/apt/ (just from memory I believe that's the location).
Therefore, the ignored repository lists are not the same every update.
