### Post by elmeri on 2011-12-20
Just to give you heads up, I just upgraded my old trusty 10.4 LTS to 12.04.

It is possible thru alt+f2 and by typing update-manager -d or by installing from dvd/usb media and upgrade/clean install.

I tried both and went for clear install, because I had some manual network drive installations and I wanted to make sure that I have clean and working system without any old files. I did safe backup and restore for my files.

I gotta say that everything seems to be working quite well and after I have installed dropbox and minor modifications to unity, I am quite happy :)
