### Post by oldfred on 2012-04-02
Moved to Ubuntu +1

While beta has been pretty stable you never should use a beta as your main install. Best to allocate another 20GB and install to a new / (root) partition so you can test that it works with your system or wait until it is released at the end of April.

 It also has a lot of changes, so depending on which version your are coming from it may be a big change.
