Just 2 let you guys know the USB Personal Hotspot Broke in Alpha 1 when I went 2 Apple IOS 5.1 Beta 2 (which I am NOT supposed 2 be talking about here so I am going 2 provide as little info as possible due 2 my NDA) On my Verizon Wireless IPhone 4, however I finally figured out that by using the Alternate CD and by setting up my 802.11 Device as my Primary Internet Connection @ Setup, then specifying my SSID, WPA as Security Type, and then my password, instead of chosing my Ethernet Connection as Default ISP, I now once again have a fully funtional internet connection with my IPhone 4.
