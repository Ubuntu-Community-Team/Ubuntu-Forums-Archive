### Post by Glenn nl on 2011-11-04
Hello, I decided developers might like a place where all the critisism is stored. :popcorn:
This is **not** my opinion, I gathered them from reviews, blog comments and family.

-Dash
*The application launchers in the dash are not needed. The user probably has Firefox/Banshee in the Launcher for very fast access. (Being fixed for PP, I believe)
*The "recently used" and "applications available for download" are both confusing and distracting
*Applications are hard to directly view, an easily accessible button for viewing everything installed seems to be wanted.

-Launcher
*The Dash button looks out of place compared to other buttons
*It is not very clear to see when an application is opened or not (especially for older people). Maybe have the backlight off by default?

-Panel
*The hidden Global Menu and window buttons are confusing and, obviously, hard to see. Especially for an interface designed for touch devices, this is bad.

-Other
*The overlay scrollbars are hard to spot and it is pretty bad for a touch-screen.
