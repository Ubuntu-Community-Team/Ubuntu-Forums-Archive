### Post by Mathor on 2012-03-25
Though the little HUD bar has been available in ubuntu 12.04 for some time, it would often provide little to no results, and was really almost unusable. With the recent update to Unity 5.8, the HUD both remembers recent activity, and actually has all of the current menu options implemented, as well as the top panel, etc. Before, simply typing "Bookmark" would provide me with no options, and now it provides me with around 12. Before, typing "shut down" would provide me with nothing, now i can shut down by just typing "shut down."
