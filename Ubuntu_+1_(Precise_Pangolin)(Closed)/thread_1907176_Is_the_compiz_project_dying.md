### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-01-10
mmm, I do not know about that but we (precise 12.04) did get an update to Compiz the other day. I have heard that apart from the maintainer there are two other Canonical people working on it.

In my opinion the pressure has come from putting Unity in 11.04. I think I know why it was done but as we found out some of the Compiz effects were not integrated too well with Unity. People wanted their Compiz effects and could easily install CCSM and then found that they lost the desktop. So, I think that there has been a lot of pressure to get it perfect for 12.04.

If things do not work precisely, then the Pangolin may regret being called Precise.

Regards.

P.S. This shows that Compiz is not dead or dying. It may just be the integration into Ubuntu Unity that needs some work.
