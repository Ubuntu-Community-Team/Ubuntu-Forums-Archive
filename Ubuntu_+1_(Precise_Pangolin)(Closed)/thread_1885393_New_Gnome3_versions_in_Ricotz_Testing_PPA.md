### Post by Harry33 on 2011-11-23
You can get the following new point releases of Gnome3 (regarding Gnome-shell) from the Ricotz Gnome-shell testing PPA:

- gnome-shell 3.3.2
- gnome-shell-extensions 3.3.2
- gnome-themes-standard 3.3.2
- gsettings-desktop-schemas 3.3.2
- mutter 3.3.2
- GTK+3.0 3.3.5

Yes they all work fine in Precise. It will take time until these are uploaded into official repos. This is because Gnome-shell is not a priority one to Canonical.
