### Post by kaldor on 2012-03-15
Try using a non-composited desktop. Install GNOME Fallback mode and see if your performance is much better. 

This is one of my annoyances with the new wave of desktop environments. When I used to game on GNOME 2 I would simply disable the visual effects in the Appearance window. On GNOME 3, I need to log out of Unity/Shell and log into Fallback due to performance.
