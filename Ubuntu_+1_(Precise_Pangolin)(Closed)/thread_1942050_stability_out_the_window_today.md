### Post by mikewhatever on 2012-03-17
I love when someone talks about crashes, it is probably the vaguest term ever invented in the history of computing. :)
Think about it, when one says "crashes", what does it tell you? Does the computer reboot or just logs out of session? Does it freeze or does it become unresponsive occasionally? Well, apparently, it's any of the above.
Diagnosing the problem is even more fun, because any of the following can cause crashes: bad RAM, faulty HDD, quirky power supply, flaky connectors, bad drivers, malware, overheating, power spikes, beta testing, file system errors. Phew! Good luck with troubleshooting!
