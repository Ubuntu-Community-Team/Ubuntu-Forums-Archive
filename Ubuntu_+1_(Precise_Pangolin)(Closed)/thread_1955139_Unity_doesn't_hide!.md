### Post by The_Autonomist on 2012-04-09
open up compiz config. 

go to Ubuntu Unity Plugin > Behaviour

change the Hide Launcher settings from Never to Autohide.

when i upgraded to 12.04, my unity launcher settings were also reset and my launcher wouldn't hide. all i had to do was redo my compiz settings...:D
