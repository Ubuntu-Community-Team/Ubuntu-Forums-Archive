### Post by Sorivenul on 2008-11-22
I don't have much experience with BSD systems on flash drives, but many LiveCD options, though a bit dated in some cases, are available.  Testing some of these should allow you to get some idea of what type of BSD you want to install when you get your external.  
[BSDanywhere]("http://bsdanywhere.org/")
[TrueBSD]("http://www.truebsd.org/")
[RoFreeSBIE]("http://www.rofreesbie.org/")
[Quetzal]("http://quetzal.matem.unam.mx/")
[OliveBSD]("http://g.paderni.free.fr/olivebsd/")
[Jibbed]("http://www.jibbed.org/")
[DragonFlyBSD 2.1-DEVEL LiveDVD]("http://distrowatch.com/?newsid=05066")

Also, just my two cents, but I suggest DesktopBSD for HD install for those new to BSD.  There are many threads around here on PC-BSD and DesktopBSD, two more "new user friendly" BSD distributions.

Good luck!
