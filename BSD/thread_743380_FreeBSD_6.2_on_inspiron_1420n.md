### Post by jnbek on 2008-04-02
Well my Dell Inspiron 530 is 100% compatible with FreeBSD 6.2/6.3, so I would imagine your's will be good to go as well. Though I must interject, I did have problems with 6.2 seeing my USB keyboard, but 6.3 saw it no problem, other than that I can't think of any reason you should have issues. Check [http://www.freebsd.org/releases/6.2R/hardware-i386.html]("http://www.freebsd.org/releases/6.2R/hardware-i386.html") for 6.2 and [http://www.freebsd.org/releases/6.3R/hardware-i386.html]("http://www.freebsd.org/releases/6.3R/hardware-i386.html") for 6.3 to verify all your hardware is listed.
