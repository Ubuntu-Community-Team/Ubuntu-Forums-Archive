### Post by Antman on 2008-08-01
Well, as the subject states, I installed PCBSD-7 Alpha3 on my IBM T60 laptop last night… basically so I could play around and see how it's progressing.

Well, I am pleasantly surprised. For being an Alpha, it's holding up pretty well so far.

PCBSD-7 Alpha3 is using KDE4(.1?) by default and so far it's looking and behaving very nice. Codecs were installed my default so I was able to play and watch my divx copy of Batman from my USB Stick.:guitar:

The WPI driver was installed by default so my ipw3945 wireless card worked with WPA encryption.

It was late and I didn't get a chance to test suspend, or my printer/scanner, but I will try that tonight. So far so good.
