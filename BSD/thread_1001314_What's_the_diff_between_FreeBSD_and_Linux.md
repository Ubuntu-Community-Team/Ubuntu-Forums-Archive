### Post by Sorivenul on 2008-12-04
DesktopBSD is great for beginners.  It is based on FreeBSD (my personal favorite).  Note: don't get discouraged by the "package management" system; "pkg_add" is nice, and when you feel comfortable, the ports system will become your friend.  The default KDE is fine, but I agree that one should check out the many other desktop environments and window managers available.  My current favorite is ion2, though others like awesome, aewm, fvwm, and wmii are also worth looking at.  For a nice list of window managers check out [Giles Orr's website]("http://gilesorr.com/wm/table.html").
