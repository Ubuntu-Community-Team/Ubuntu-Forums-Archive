### Post by jwbaker on 2007-05-02
> **ThePimento said:**
> If someone could post the step by step instructions on how to correctly burn a CD, I'd greatly appreciate it.

Are you burning the CD from OSX, or from Linux?  In OSX, open up the Disk Utility and drag the ISO into its window.  Then insert a blank CD-R or CD-RW, click on the ISO in Disk Utility's list, and click the "Burn" icon in Disk Utility.

In Ubuntu, simply right-click on the icon of the ISO and select "Burn to disc".
