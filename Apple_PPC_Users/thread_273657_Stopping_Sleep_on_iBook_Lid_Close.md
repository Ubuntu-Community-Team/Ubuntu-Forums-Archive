### Post by Aspirin on 2006-10-08
there's a hack that allows you to use two displays / keep the computer running with the lid closed, HOWEVER, i would strongly recommend against this:

- the hack voids your warranty
- ibooks vent much of their heat through the keyboard, thus go to sleep while the lid is closed so that they dont overheat.

if you want to run the hack, please be aware of these things - you will void your warranty and run the risk of very seriously damaging your machine. the safer solution - close the lid of the ibook so that it's about 1/2" to 3/4" open. it should be enough that you can tuck it away, but it'll keep running and wont overheat too easily.

i dont know the link to the hack, but i'm sure our friend google will help you with that.
