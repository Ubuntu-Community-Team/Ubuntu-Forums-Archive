### Post by dasmith on 2006-11-15
Been having the same issues.  So far as from what I have found, it is a problem with the battery not charging the capisator that keeps the system time.  Most computers have a battery of some type on the machine that keeps the system time.  With an Ibook, I believe that is different.  

So far from my reasearch, there is a micro-processor in the battery that does this.  If that micro-processor goes bad, you can keep a charge on the battery and power the machine, but the clock does not stay calibrated with the correct time.  I have recently purchased a used battery off of EBay and am having the same issues with this.  Here are a couple of links to other fourm posts with the same issues.
