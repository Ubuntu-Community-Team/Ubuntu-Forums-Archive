### Post by DocMSK on 2006-12-15
Hi,

I am by no means an Ubuntu expert, so you may want to research further a solution to your problem. But for what its worth heres how I stopped the fan on my Acer TM803LMi laptop whizzing away most of the time:

I installed an applet called EmiFreq. Its a CPU Frequency Scaling applet. I can now select various profiles for my CPU such as performance or powersaving. I can also view the temp and see when the fan kicks in. On powersaving the fan rarely spins as the CPU on my laptop is running at only 600Mhz which is fine for web browsing etc.

Hope this helps a little.
