### Post by mannheim on 2006-06-13
Your xorg.conf has a line 
```
	Driver		"ati"

```
I don't know about the ppc, but I believe that (at least on i-386) you want the proprietary ATI driver: go to the wiki to check, but I think you need
```
	Driver		"fglrx"

```
