### Post by 3rdalbum on 2006-06-28
In Gaim, go into the Accounts window (Tools > Accounts) and click on your MSN Messenger account. Then click the Modify button.

Just above your avatar, there is a checkbox saying "New Mail Notifications". Click that to turn it on. Now you'll get notifications for new Hotmail messages, just like in MacMessenger.

What things are you trying to download, and are you sure it's saying "archive type" and not "architecture"? If you want to run pre-compiled programs on your Mac, you must download them for the PowerPC architecture (as Macs use PowerPC chips). I suspect what you're trying to do is download programs for x86 (PC) Linux. They won't work. Either find a .deb file that has been made for PPC, or compile the source code which is almost always cross-platform.

Macromedia don't make Flash for PPC, and they don't provide source code, so you can't install it unfortunately. The same was probably true of Java, but maybe that will change.
