### Post by PaganHippie on 2007-11-25
Sorry, that's not going to work.  The upgrade path currently requires you to go through *each successive version of Ubuntu in sequence* from yours to the current one, and 5.04, 5.10, and 6.10 are no longer supported.  The window of opportunity to upgrade is limited.  One exception to this that I've read of is that 8.04, due out next year, will be another Long Term Support release, as 6.06 is, and that there will be a direct upgrade path from 6.06 to 8.04, skipping over the intermediary releases.

Did you try 6.06 or 7.04?  Both of them *should* work very well on your iMac.  If you did try them, what happened?
