### Post by Twiggy003 on 2008-02-08
i386 is the intel architecture used by most PCs and new Macs, the iBook PPC is PPC and thus i386 binarys, .debs etc won't work.  You need to add PPC Ubuntu sources to your sources.list or get the PPC .deb files.  Sorry to not be of much help, but im sure if you search the web or these forums you can find out more about Ubuntu PPC packages.
