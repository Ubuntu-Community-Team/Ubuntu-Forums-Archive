### Post by samden on 2006-12-07
Don't despair because it seems really old, Macs last for ever. I did all my university work up till 2 1/2 years ago on an Apple LCII (made around 1992), with an 80Mb hard drive and 8Mb of RAM, and a Powerbook 150 of similar vintage. Even old girls like that are fine for typing assignments. Especially if you are getting it going for a school it will still be very useful. Most of the time you just want to type and browse the net, there is really no point in having all new computers if few people are actually needing the fancy new features at any given time. Anyway, old iMacs look cool!

My parents bought a tray loading iMac, 32Mb ram etc just like yours while I was at high school. I used it for all my assignments at school, even doing 3D modelling on it. They still use it as their main computer, and have had no need to buy anything newer, even though my mother is a school teacher and uses it often, and my sister is in the middle of high school and uses it for all her assignments.

Don't despair, get it going if you have time! Will be well worth the effort.
