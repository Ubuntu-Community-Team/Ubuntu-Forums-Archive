### Post by heimo on 2005-04-20
Wow. I'm going to use your post as an perfect example how to write question about Xorg driver / screen related problems. Excellent. Just excellent. That's the way to do it. Thank you.

At this point of day I'm quite exhausted and stressed of all similar posts (some of which ended well and some of which haven't been solved), but I looked through your configuration and to me it looks fine. It should work.

Ok, it doesn't. So something's wrong and your dmesg gave a clue that it may be driver related. Change to vesa and try if that works. Check your  */var/log/Xorg.0.log *and post all errors (EE) and warnings (WW) and all the other stuff you consider helpful.

Check are the modes disgarded - though I don't think that'd be the problem in this case. Try disabling modules - especially glx, dri and vbe. Any changes in behavior? Check what modules are loaded (lsmod).

BusID line looks ok. Memory amount looks ok and should be plenty for that resolution / depth, but anyway, try 16 bits.
