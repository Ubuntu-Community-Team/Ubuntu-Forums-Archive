### Post by Jagot on 2006-07-16
In Terminal:

```
gnome-panel-screenshot --delay=5
```

Change the delay to however many seconds you need to open the menus you want open.  If you don't want the Terminal window to appear in the screenshot then open it on another desktop and it will still work.
