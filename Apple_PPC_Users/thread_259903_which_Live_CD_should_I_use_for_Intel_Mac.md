If you've got an Intel Mac, then the 32-bit x86 is definately the one for you.

It won't work "out-of-the-box" as the Macintels came out part-way through the Ubuntu development process, and some of the Macintels came out after the current Ubuntu version. There are plenty of instructions online about how to do it for your particular machine.
