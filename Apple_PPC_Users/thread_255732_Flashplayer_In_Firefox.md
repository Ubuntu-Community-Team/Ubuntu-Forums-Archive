### Post by stmiller on 2006-09-12
There is no good working flash for ppc Linux. The open source alternatives may load simple flash pages, but there's no support of things Flash 7 and up. (No flash video, youtube, google, etc.)

That flash player installer that is prompting to download is for x86 (intel) computers only. Adobe/Macromedia have never released a ppc linux version of flash.
