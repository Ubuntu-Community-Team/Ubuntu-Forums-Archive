### Post by N8K99 on 2006-02-24
If you are getting an iBook with Airport not Airport Extreme you should be able to connect wirelessly. I use a Ti Powerbook with Kubuntu 5.10, it has an Airport card and am able to connect with it. I have to do a couple things to operate, but it's fine, when I am booting I have to C+c when the yaboot tries to configure the network interface. Then once everything is up and signed into the Desktop, sometimes I have to enter; sudo dhclient eth1 in konsole to get a connection. You will need to insure that you have the preferences for your wireless card set for DHCP and it should all be greasy! :cool: 

enjoy your new deal!\\:D/
