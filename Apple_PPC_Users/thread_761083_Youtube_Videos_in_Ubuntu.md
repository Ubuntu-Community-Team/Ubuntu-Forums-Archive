### Post by deamon_knight on 2008-04-20
As I understand it, there is no official flash player for PPC Linux, Ubuntu or otherwise, unlike say the x86 processor (intel) versions of Linux. It seems that macromedia/adobe aren't going to release one either. All is not lost, there is an open source flash viewer called "Gnash" that is in development. You can download this and it and you may get Youtube and the like running. Other people are reporting success with this, however it has not worked for me. Try it and report back with results!

Good Luck
