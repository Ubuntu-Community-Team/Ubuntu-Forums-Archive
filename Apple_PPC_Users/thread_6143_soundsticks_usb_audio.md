### Post by Zugwrack on 2004-11-25
Sound driver does not utilize full hardware capabilites of newer Powermacs.. I have a dual processor G4 mirrored drive. Install gnome-alsamixer first...You will need to check the PCM settings...and jack the volume to 100%. If PCM is too high it will distort the sound miserably. As an aside this is consistent in Mandrake and Debian PPC Sarge as well. Looks like the drive itself needs some work. Anyway I have been able to get sound out of my speakers(although not sound sticks), but the sound volume is real low. If you have head phones use them...it works much better. I agree that it is nowhere near OSX, but then if you are into *nix systems Darwin/Opendarwin cannot compete.
