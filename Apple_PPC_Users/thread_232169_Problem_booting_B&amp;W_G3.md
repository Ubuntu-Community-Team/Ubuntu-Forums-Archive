### Post by linear on 2006-08-08
Some things to look at:

1. Is this a ShipIt CD or one you burned yourself? If burned yourself, are you sure it was burned as an image?

2. If you boot in Mac OS and insert the CD, is it readable? (If not, the burn was bad. Try reburning at a slow speed - 4X or less.)

3. You may have better success with the alternate install CD.
