### Post by stream303 on 2006-11-13
I don't know who to thank personally, so I thought I'd express it here.

I'm currently running Ubuntu 6.06 on my G4 laptop and 6.10 on my Rev-A G5 iMac.  For the most part, everything is good.  There are a few rough spots, and look forward to further support for other models that the rest of the community may be having trouble with.

Admittedly, we might be a small community that could just go running back to OSX, but having Linux of any flavor on the PPC platform is just the right thing to do. :)

If there are no future advancements, I'd just like to say I'm very grateful for what we've gotten so far!  The entire PPC community (not just Ubuntu - but other projects worldwide) has been hugely supportive and seems to reflect a unique spirit of it's own.  With that, it's more than just PPC hardware support we are dealing with...

I've got a feeling there are a lot of us quite happy with what we have so far - you just don't hear about it so much.. 

Thanks again to *everyone* involved with PPC!
