### Post by linear on 2006-09-19
Isn't recognized? How exactly are you trying to burn it?

First check that you have a good iso. Compare the MD5 checksum vs. that posted at the download site. ("md5sum" is the command in Terminal.)

If it's OK, you need to burn it as an image (use Disk Utility).
