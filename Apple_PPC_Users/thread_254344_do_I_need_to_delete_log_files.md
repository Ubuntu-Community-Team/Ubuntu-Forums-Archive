### Post by bernied on 2006-09-09
Have a look in the /var/log/ directory. This is where the log files are kept.
As far as I know, the operating system will do all the maintenance - older logs get compressed and renamed. They don't take up much space. I only ever look at log files when there is a problem. 

Don't be nervous, everything is fine.
