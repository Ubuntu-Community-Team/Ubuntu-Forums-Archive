### Post by stream303 on 2008-04-14
A lot will depend on how much ram is installed, and if you are willing to put in more if necessary.  I'd check here to view the default specs, but I'll bet most people put in more than the default at some point.

[http://www.everymac.com/systems/apple/powermac_g3/index-powermac-g3.html](http://www.everymac.com/systems/apple/powermac_g3/index-powermac-g3.html)

Even with very low memory, all hope is not lost.  You could install the Feisty *Server* for 64mb, and pop a very lightweight Xorg onto it:

[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/releases/feisty/release/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/ports/releases/feisty/release/)
and
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/LowMemorySystems](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/LowMemorySystems)
Ignore the initial title about being Intel - it applies to ppc as well.

For more memory, you could probably get Xubuntu up and running...
