### Post by ssam on 2007-05-12
that seems like it might be an odd bug. you could search the bug reports at launchpad.net ot file a new report.

it might also be that 'supported' means two different things.
1) that something will work
2) that someone will help you out if there are problems.

the powerpc port of ubuntu is no longer supported (definition 2) by canonical, only by the community. however most of ubuntu and the software in the repositories works just as well on powerpc as x86.

by default middle and right click are mapped to F11 and F12. search the forums if you need to change that.
