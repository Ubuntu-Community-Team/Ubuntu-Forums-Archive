### Post by ssam on 2007-10-01
you could look at laptop mode. install the package laptop-mode and have a play with the config file that it puts in /etc

have you got the cpu to stay in the lowest speed?

can you dim the screen any more. this can save a lot.

then you can look at reducing disk access. stop any unused applications. have lots of ram (to cache the disk). turn off swap.

you could look at DynamicClocks for the graphics card.
