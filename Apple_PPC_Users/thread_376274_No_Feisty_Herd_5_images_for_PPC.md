### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-03-05
> **macijuana said:**
> Well as far as I can tell a herd is a kind of pre-beta release, in this case of Ubuntu 7.04. I'm not sure how the term "herd" was coined though.

The names for alphas are given by the names that you'd use for groups of the animal whose name is the codename of the Ubuntu release.

For instance, for Dapper Drake, the alpha releases were called "Flight 1", "Flight 2" etc; as a group of drakes is known as a flight. Breezy Badger - the alpha releases were "colony" as you have a colony of badgers.

What was the alpha name for Edgy Eft? Whatever it was, that's the name for a group of efts.
