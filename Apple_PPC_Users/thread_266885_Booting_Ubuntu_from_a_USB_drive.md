### Post by didg on 2006-09-28
I haven't but it should be doable.

First try [http://ppcrcd.pld-linux.org/](http://ppcrcd.pld-linux.org/) for double checking that your Mac can actually boot from USB. 

After that read 
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LiveUsbPendrivePersistent](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LiveUsbPendrivePersistent)
and 
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CustomizedInitrd](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CustomizedInitrd)

They are for i386 but with ppcrcd.pld-linux.org usb howto you should be able to make the USB drive bootable.
