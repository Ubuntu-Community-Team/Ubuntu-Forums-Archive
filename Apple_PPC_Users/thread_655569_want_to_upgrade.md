### Post by Enginirical on 2008-01-02
I upgraded and it didnt work. To be honest you are better of, saving your home folder (which has all your important personalised settings) and just doing a fresh Gutsy install, then copy your home folder back and it will look exactly the same as you had it before. You will have to reinstall apps like firefox, torrent client, vlc or whatever you use, but at the end of the day you have a fresh install and the most up to date applications. Any dodgey workarounds you may have done in the past will most likely be solved by now so you should end up with a cleaner more efficient system!

As far a black screen goes, (if its Gutsy) it less likely to be graphics card, but most likely poorly burned disk. I find 90% of the time, downloading it again, and burning slowly whilst leaving the machine alone produces a disk that DOES work. It's happened to me a few times now.
