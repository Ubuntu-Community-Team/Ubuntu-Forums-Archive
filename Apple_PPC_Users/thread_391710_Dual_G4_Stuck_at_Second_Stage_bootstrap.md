### Post by didg on 2007-03-26
Ok, looks mostly good.

in yaboot.conf
replace the line 
device=/pci@f2000000/ADPT,39160@12,1/@0:
with
ofboot=/pci@f2000000/ADPT,39160@12/@0:

and rerun ybin -v (either with -C or in a chroot)

You can double check ofboot.b by mounting /dev/sda2
mount -t hfs /dev/sda2 /mnt/

cat /mnt/ofboot.b

that you don't have ,1 in bootyaboot line and in /mnt/yaboot.conf

Now you box should boot,  at least it should go to the second stage.
