Heya Eddie...

I can't say I know exactly what your specific error message is saying, but I CAN recommend you try installing Ubuntu 5.10 instead of 6.06 since you're using a G3.  I tried to get 6.06 running correctly on my PowerBook G3 for 2 days before I gave up and went with the 5.10 Installer.  And 5.10 went on first try, no questions asked...
