### Post by ppc_guy on 2007-11-02
We all have to start somewhere that is for sure.. And not all that much more for us mac users. You'll want to start by reading the first sticky in this area of the forums. That's what I did, and it has served me well with all of my macs. 
  Dapper would probably be your best bet for now. Following the links from the sticky and download the iso. Buring from OS X is fine if you have any version of it on the lappy, if not roxio or anything else in windows will work too. Just make sure to burn as image/iso.
  When you boot the pb. Hold down the c key. Instead of the familiar grey mac background is should go black and look "dos" like with white text. At the prompt, just press return. It will then boot from cd. After that is pretty easy. Just double click on the install icon on the desktop and viola! It's done.
