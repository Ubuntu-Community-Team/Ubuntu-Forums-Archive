### Post by dhirsch on 2006-04-03
I have the same system (maxed out the ram to 640mb) and it works quite well :KS

_There are a few issues that you may come across:_
*** **suspend to disk doesn't work without a kernel patch (last time I checked anyway). There are excellent guides referenced on the forums, but the process can be a bit daunting to a newbie.
***** there are some issues with sound (workarounds are posted on the forums)
*** **You will have to do without flash animations on websites, as there is no official linux ppc flash plugin. The GNU plugin is coming along nicely though, so there is hope.
