### Post by rondackcpu on 2007-05-20
I have a Blueberry.  The live CD does the same thing to my machine.  It's not that you have a bad copy of the CD.  It's just that the CD does that.  I have successfully installed all three versions of Ubuntu on my Blueberry (one at a time) by using the Alternate CDs.  My Blueberry is a little overwhelmed by Ubuntu.  But you should try it on yours.  You may have a faster CPU than mine and it may be OK.  Because of the sluggish response, I have installed Debian 4.0 on my iMac.  I think it is a little faster than Ubuntu, but that may be just an illusion.

Good luck with the Alternate CD.  Let us know how you progress.
