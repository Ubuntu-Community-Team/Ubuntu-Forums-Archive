### Post by tmatt95 on 2007-05-01
Hiya travnewmatic,
welcome to the forum:). To add the trash can to the toolbar:

1) Right click on the bottom toolbar in an empty space (where there is no window switcher or window tabs etc)

2) select "add to panel..." from the menu.

This will open up a new window. In this window you will see a lot of icons. The Trash can is under the "Desktop and windows" section. Left click on it once and then click on the "add" button at the bottom of the screen. The trash button will now appear at the bottom of the screen. To move it to the right hand side of the screen:

1) Right click on the trash can and select move. Move your mouse to where you want the icon to be and it will follow

Hope this helps,
Matt

### Post by audax321 on 2007-05-01
Also, if you want the Trash bin on the desktop you can do the following:

1. Ubuntu Menu > System Tools > Configuration Editor
2. Navigate to: apps > nautilus > desktop
3. Click the checkbox next to 'trash_icon_visible'

I think they disabled all the desktop icons in Feisty to give the desktop a cleaner look. But this may have reset previous settings when upgrading.
