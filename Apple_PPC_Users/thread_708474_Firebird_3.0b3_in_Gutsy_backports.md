### Post by stream303 on 2008-02-26
I just tested Firebird 3.0b3 on my G5 iMac running Gutsy and it is quite a bit faster than the standard 2.x build.

I just went into System > Administration > Software Sources and checked the Gutsy backports.  After it loaded the new repository files, I found Firebird 3.0 in Synaptic which installed it as a separate program so it didn't wipe out my current Firebird 2.x setup.

I didn't want to play with anything else backports had to offer at this time, so I went back in and UNchecked the backports repository.

Looks good so far.  Hope this backport makes it all the way back to Feisty at least, especially for our low-end machines.
