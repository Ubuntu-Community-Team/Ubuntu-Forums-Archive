### Post by BryannMelvin on 2007-02-18
I use a ibook G3 as my daily personal machine.

I have Kubuntu and Ubuntu desktops installed. installed it's a 500mHZ and runs pretty much OK with 384mb ram

I run 606LTS,
Airport works fine
Xubuntu was marginal...I use it on a mixed network Gnome or KDE seemed better for that.

Over time I have modified things somewhat. I have hplip 1.6.12 installed to use my all in one and photosmart large format printers.

I have changed to Opera static for the browser dumping FF as I can't find a 1.5 port that works and I haven't had time to compile FF.

The only modem driver that exists is one from linuxant from source that I can't get to compile. I am currently in a dialogue with Linuxant to iron that outl


I have had some glitches with sleep. Clock losing it's setting. Work around for that has to keep time on UTC. Clock glitches when waking up.


Main use of the machine is routine ...web use...Plus Digikam for photography.

I prefer Kword over OOorg. much faster. 

I have never tried edgy or feisty as my entire work network for producing art and photography runs on dapper.

I still do most of the heavy graphics work on i86 with crossover. (corel suite 9).

I am starting to use inkscape, scribus, nview and quanta more as I accumulate/convert templates I have from years use of Corel and Serif products.
