However when I press it, the cd tray opens and then immediately shuts. No time to put a cd in, or take one out.

### Post by didg on 2007-03-01
As a workaround if you're running gnome and pbbuttonsd disable eject key in gnome keyboard shortcuts.
pbbuttonsd will do the right think with eject key.
