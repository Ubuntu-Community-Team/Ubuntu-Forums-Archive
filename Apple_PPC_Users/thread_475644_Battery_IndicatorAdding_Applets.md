### Post by tcrroadie on 2007-06-17
If you have a mouse available, you could connect a mouse to your ibook and the right mouse button will work on the mouse.  

If you do not have a mouse available, the F12 key will allow you to right click objects.  Move the mouse pointer to the Gnome Panel and press the F12/Eject key on your iBooks keyboard.
