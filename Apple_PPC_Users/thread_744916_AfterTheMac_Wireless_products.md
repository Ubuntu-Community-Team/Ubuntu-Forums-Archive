### Post by ubuntubrian on 2008-04-04
I have a Power book G3  and though my MacWireless adapter worked fine in Debian it wasn't recognized in any Ubuntu version. I'm running Hardy Beta now and I just got a Linksys adapter and had to download and install a Broadcom (the chipset for the adapter) update. that's all command line so if you're not familiar with terminals then it isn't so easy. Regardless, the firmware update worked and Ubuntu sees the card and I am online writing this. Oddly I cannot connect with ethernet so wireless is it.
good luck cause Ubuntu is the best!
