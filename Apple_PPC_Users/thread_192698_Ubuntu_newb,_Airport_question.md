there are programs that can resize your partitions on your laptop on the fly.  these program work, but are dangerous.  they also all are supposed to cost money, but I'm sure you can find ways around that.... iPartition and VolumeWorks are the good ones.  your best bet is to shrink the osx partition down to all you need for it, and leave the remaining space empty and let the ubuntu installer go for the largest unused chunk.
