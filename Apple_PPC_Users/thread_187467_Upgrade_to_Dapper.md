### Post by BoneKracker on 2006-06-03
I upgraded to the beta, and i recently installed the release.  No problems on either.  It upgrades X to 7 I think.

Other thoughts:
- Are you using the best driver for your video card (you said G4, right?  if you're using ati, try r128)
- Is the horizontal refresh and vertical sync set to the right number for your monitor (in accordance with the manufacturer's actual spec for the monitor)?
- Have you tried configuring it with xorgautoconfig?
