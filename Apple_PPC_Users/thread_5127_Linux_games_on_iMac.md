### Post by slux on 2004-11-17
Since you asked for commercial GNU/Linux games that have PPC versions...

There are some: From [Linux Game Publishing](http://www.linuxgamepublishing.com/) there is Majesty (good RTS & sims combination),   Soul Ride (when it gets released) and possibly some other upcoming titles. From [Loki](http://www.lokigames.com/)  (RIP) Myth 2 and Heroes of Might & Magic 3, Civilization: Call to Power, Railroad Tycoon 2. From [Ilwinter](http://www.illwinter.com/)  there are Dominions 1 & 2 which I hear are excellent strategy games.

Most of them don't need any sort of 3d acceleration of course... The PPC GNU/Linux users don't have a very large offering of games available, partly because of the driver situation (NVidia & ATI only offering proprietary binaries...) all the more reason to support free software. There are however, some *very* good free titles available. :)
