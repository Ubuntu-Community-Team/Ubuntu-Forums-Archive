### Post by johng4 on 2007-09-18
1. Multiple Cores != Faster Processing
Its more effecient processing, not faster.  The speed boost is actually a reflection of effeciency.

2. PowerPC Macs
I suggest getting a PPC Mac for many reasons I've gone over and over again on these forums.  I'll summarize it with this:  PowerPC Computers are the betamax of the personal computer market.  Those who have them don't want to use anything else, those who don't make comments about how over priced they are.  A G3 iMac will run circles around a similarly spec'd PC, and will do so until the day you throw it out, because the odds are good it will never break... at least it won't run itself to death like a PC will.

3. Mactel Macs
Good machines all-in-all, but a different breed of computer.  If anything you can rely on Mac making computers that will last.

I personally want to get a 24" Mactel iMac (the one with the Nvidia card, not the brushed metal design), and I will immediately wipe all traces of OS X off of it.  I like the computers that Apple makes.. I don't like the OS.  Never have, never will.  Its had its fair chance with me, and I find it extremely limiting for what I do.
