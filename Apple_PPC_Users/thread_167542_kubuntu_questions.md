### Post by linuxfanatic1024 on 2006-04-28
Live CD's are a great way to start getting hands-on experience with Linux. Their primary disadvantage, of course, is that you're stuck with whatever is on the CD, at least for now. There's no way they can put everything for Linux on there.

Live CD's take up NO ROOM on your computer unless you save stuff on it yourself. They leave your hard drive untouched unless YOU tell it to touch your hard drive. That's the primary benefit of live CDs.
