 Hi, The sound should work. The problem playing audio CDs is a known problem with recent iBooks & PowerBooks that happens because they don't have the cable between the optical drive and the soundcard, you'll have to play the CDs using the cd player plugin for xmms (don't remember it's name). The brightness should be fixable tweaking pbbuttons. And AFAIK the trackpad is partially unsupported in some PowerBooks.
