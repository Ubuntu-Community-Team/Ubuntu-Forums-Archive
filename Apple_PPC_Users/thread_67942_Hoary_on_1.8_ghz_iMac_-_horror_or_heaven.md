### Post by DirtDawg on 2005-09-21
Man, I use an imac G3 to run Hoary and it works like a friggin' champ. I have some sound issues, but other than that she runs better than ever.
My mac had OS9, but that's gone now. I ran the simplest install and have had no issues with fans, USB ports, or screen/video performance (I have no idea what firewire is). 
Packages can sometimes be tougher to find (no Flash plug-in, for example), but Ubuntu is excellent about providing PPC equivalents for almost everything in their repositories.
So no problems here, and my computer's likely more primative.
