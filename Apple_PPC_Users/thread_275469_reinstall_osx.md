### Post by munchbach on 2006-10-11
Jagged:

Are the discs grey?  If so these are machine specific discs, i.e. if I give you my PowerBook discs for your iBook it will not work.  Your best bet is to get a generic OS X disc that will install on any machine.

If you want to give your acquired discs another shot boot your Mac with the OS CD in the optical drive and hold down the "option" key.  This will list all bootable devices.  Select the CD then click the arrow that will be pointing to the right.  If you get the same error then the CD's you have just won't work.
