### Post by ssam on 2007-02-18
for nvidia cards there currently are the open source 'nv' driver and the closed source 'nvidia'.

the 'nvidia' is only available for x86 linux.

the nv driver works on any linux, but only has 2d acceleration. this is what you'll be running currently.

in the future the '[nouveau]("http://nouveau.freedesktop.org/wiki/")' driver will replace the nv driver. it will have 3d acceleration. it might be 6 months or a year before it is worth using though.
