### Post by xurios on 2006-08-26
As far as I know, all of the ATA formats are backwards-compatible. This means that ATA/133 and ATA/100 drives will work with the ATA/66 controller.

I also think WD drives are junk. I've had them fail more than once. I think you should get one or two of [these](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.asp?Item=N82E16822148094).
