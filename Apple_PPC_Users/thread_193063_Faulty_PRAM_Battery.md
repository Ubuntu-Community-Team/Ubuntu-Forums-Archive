### Post by BoneKracker on 2006-06-20
Well, I'm just thinking maybe the battery had been on the shelf and wasn't fully charged to begin with.  If a battery gets down to less than about 30%, it's not reliable.

If we know what percent charge you started with, then we have a better idea whether or not there's some other kind of problem that's draining the battery.  

You test it with a multimeter.  Unless you're an electronics hobbyist, you probably don't have one, but you may be able to get access to one.  

For example, I just go into my local Radio Shack store and they're usually happy to test a battery for me if they're not busy.  A science teacher at your local school probably has one.  Any good electrician probably has one.

Here's some info about how to properly test a battery:
[http://support.radioshack.com/support_tutorials/batteries/batgd-B08.htm](http://support.radioshack.com/support_tutorials/batteries/batgd-B08.htm)
