I don't see why not - Im dual booting panther and feisty on my powerbook G4, not without some teething problems, but nothing terrible. 
There's a great thread on here with details of how to resize your OS X partition without wiping out your data - this may be useful if you're only upgrading to leopard as opposed to a clean install.

30Gb is plenty - I split my hard disk in 2 when I decided to dual boot, and my linux partition was 25Gb - I still have tons of room on it, and on panther!
