### Post by stmiller on 2007-07-31
Hey check out the sticky at the top of the forum on where to download Feisty for PowerPC. Ubuntu is still making official releases for PowerPC Linux, including official updates. They have no plans to end that. They only dropped paid commercial support for PowerPC.

The automatic update system in 6.10 may be trying to find PowerPC Feisty from an official mirror, but Ubuntu has moved it to their 'ports' section. So just go download that Feisty disc.
