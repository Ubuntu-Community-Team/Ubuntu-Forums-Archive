### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-04-21
Wifi and MP3 playback definately work. Your ability to use Beryl will depend on what graphics chip is in the computer. The good news is, hardware support is continually getting better, even on PPC.

Yes, you can boot without install; using the Desktop CD. Xubuntu makes a Desktop CD for PowerPC. Any wired ethernet connection should work out of the box.
