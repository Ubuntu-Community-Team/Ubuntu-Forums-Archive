 Currently it's impossible to put the apps menu on top of the desktop in Gnome 2.x (the desktop that ubuntu uses). The reason is that GTK+-2.x (the base of gnome 2.x) was built to look and work exclusivelly like M$ Windows. Currently the only desktop that works like the MacOS is KDE 3.2.x or later.
