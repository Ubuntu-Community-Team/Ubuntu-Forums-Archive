### Post by dagnabit dang doohickey on 2006-10-15
Installing Ubuntu on a NuBus Mac is currently not possible. The folks at [this project]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/nubus-pmac/") are working to get the 2.6.x kernel on NuBus. If and when they succeed, things may change.

The only options are either Debian or Yellow Dog. You can get started here:
[http://nubus-pmac.sourceforge.net/]("http://nubus-pmac.sourceforge.net/")

As a complete noob, my first plunge into the world of linux was installing Debian Woody on a PM8150 a little over a year ago. That was one heck of a crash course.
