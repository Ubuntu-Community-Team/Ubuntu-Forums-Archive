### Post by jcrc on 2006-07-22
After searching for a all-in-one that works 100% in linux with a good price, I bought a HP PSC 1510. All is working, printer and scanner, although the scanner quality is not so good.

Because I don't use windows anymore, I just plug everything and even didn't looked at operation instructions, and I had the same problem as you. Every time I switch on - a calibration page.
So I looked at the operation instructions and something was missing:
After the calibration page is printed, you put the calibration page in the scanner (pictures of printer to the right side) and hit the last button (start copy or scanner). This is for ink cartridge alignment.
After this, you don't have a calibration page everytime you switch on.
