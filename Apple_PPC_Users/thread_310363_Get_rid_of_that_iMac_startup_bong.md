### Post by stream303 on 2006-12-01
Ah, no more annoyingly loud startup bong on my Rev-A G5 iMac.

sudo nvsetvol 0

Turned that bong off for good.

man nvsetvol for other options like just changing the volume level of the chime if you happen to like it.
