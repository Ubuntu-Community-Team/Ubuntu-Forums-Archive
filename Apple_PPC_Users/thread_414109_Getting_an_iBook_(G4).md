### Post by ditsch on 2007-04-21
Adobe doesn't support PPC Linux, so no Flash. You could try swfdec or gnash, but these are not the best way and some flash features remain unsupported. 

Beside that, Feisty runs fine on the iBook G4 including Airport Extreme/WPA with bcm43xx. On the hardware side there are no restrictions for me so far. On the software side, everything I expected to work does work.

### Post by stmiller on 2007-04-21
