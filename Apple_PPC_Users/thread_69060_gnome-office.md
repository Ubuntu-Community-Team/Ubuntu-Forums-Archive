### Post by callipeo on 2005-09-25
The answer is in your own post: you can't uninstall gnome-office and keep gnome installed - at least not without breaking something. BTW, you don't really need the "gnome" package: it is a universe meta-package, and you can get the desktop selection for ubuntu installing "ubuntu-desktop". If you really don't want gnome-office installed, I see no problem in removing also the "gnome" package.
