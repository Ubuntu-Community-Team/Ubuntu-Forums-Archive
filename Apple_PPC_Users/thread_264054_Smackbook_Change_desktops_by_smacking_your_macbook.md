### Post by anil_robo on 2006-09-24
I posted an [interesting how to]("http://nixtechnica.blogspot.com/2006/09/how-to-change-desktops-on-macbookpro.html") on my blog. It enables powerbook or later (macbook, macbook pro) models to change desktops by smacking it up! Have fun!

Short summary:

1. Install VirtueDesktops from [this page.]("http://virtuedesktops.info/downloads/")
2. Run it from the applications folder in finder.
3. When it runs, open its preferences by clicking its icon on tray, and then selecting preferences.
4. In the Application tab, click the little checkmark against "Enable Motion Sensor switching".
5. Start slapping your Apple!
