### Post by smbrow14 on 2006-08-23
You can get it via the terminal (type "term" into spotlight).

**cp /System/Library/Extensions/AppleAirPort2.kext/Contents/MacOS/AppleAirPort2 /users/yourusername/Desktop/AppleAirPort2**
