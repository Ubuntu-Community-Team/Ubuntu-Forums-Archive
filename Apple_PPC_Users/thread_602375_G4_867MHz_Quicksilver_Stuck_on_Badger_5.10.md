### Post by ClownyShoes on 2007-11-08
Well, I guess this one is actually solved.
I've got the G4 running Edgy and my G5 running Gutsy.
In the case of the G4 - skipping a kernel update or two was the answer.
The only two kernel versions that I found to work with my Quicksilver 867 are:

2.6.12-9-powerpc    and now
2.6.17-12-powerpc
