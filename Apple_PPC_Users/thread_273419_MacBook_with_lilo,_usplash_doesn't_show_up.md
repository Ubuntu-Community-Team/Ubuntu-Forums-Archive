### Post by hajk on 2006-10-08
You need to tell LILO about the splash screen, and besides you should really clean up your /etc/lilo.conf file:
```

boot=/dev/sda3
default=Ubuntu
map=/boot/map
delay=20
root=/dev/sda3
read-only
compact

image=/vmlinuz
    initrd=/initrd.img
    append="quiet splash lpj=8000000"
    label=Ubuntu

```
The quotes around the label aren't needed; the quotes on the append=... are needed; the lpj=... option prevents a kernel panic on account of timer issues.

<--edit-->
This worked, I haven't had a kernel panic so I didn't do the lpj part, and just to note, its 8 million for 1.83 GHz CPU's, and 10 million for 2.00 GHz CPU's. Found that here: [http://www.xs4all.nl/~hajk/mbook-install.pdf](http://www.xs4all.nl/~hajk/mbook-install.pdf)
```
boot=/dev/sda3
default=Ubuntu

map=/boot/map
delay=10
image=/vmlinuz initrd=/initrd.img
	append="quiet splash"
	root=/dev/sda3
	label=Ubuntu
	read-only

```
