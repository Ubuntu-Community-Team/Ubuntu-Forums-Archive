### Post by avtolle on 2006-08-17
No specific ideas, but here are some things to check: 1) Did you download the latest 6.06.1 versions? 2) Did you burn the isos to CD as images, and at slow speed (4x or lower); 3) Did you use CD-R or CD-RW media? Older CD drives have issues w/CD-RWs, as well as CD-Rs burned at higher speeds;4) Did you check the downloaded isos with md5sum to ensure the download(s) were not corrupted? These are some things which come to mind, given the difficulties others have had with install on older Macs. And, regrettably, on some machines at some times, it just doesn't go, for no apparent logical reason. Best of luck; welcome to the forums.
