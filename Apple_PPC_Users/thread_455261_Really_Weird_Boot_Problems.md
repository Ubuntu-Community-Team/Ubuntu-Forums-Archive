### Post by vincethod on 2008-04-20
Having spent the whole of the weekend trying to get a G4 to boot *any* form of Linux (I have more Live Cds, distros and what-not tahn I care to think!), and each time getting stuck at the flashing question mark folder, only where the video is inverted (on a G3 iMac I also tried I had the same issue, but with a red background) - the machine keeps looping round as if trying to read the cd, then giving me a flashing folder again, I think have the answer - the CD drive is set to Slave. 

Changing to Master allowed me to boot fine, and I'm now happily installing xubuntu !

Such a simple thing. I've added this reply in the hope it saves another user a lost weekend.

Vince

PS: Howdy Ubuntu folks! I'm only after giving Linux a whirl on a spare Mac, so I know little to nothing about it, and I'll no doubt be back with more questions.
