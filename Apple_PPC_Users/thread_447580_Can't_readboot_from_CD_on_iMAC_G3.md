### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-05-18
iMac G3s are well-known for their faulty CD drives - they don't like to read CD-Rs.

There are two types of burnable CDs that they sometimes work with:

CD-RW
and "Music" CD

That is, use a CD that says it's designed "for music".

As for CD-RWs, I've got a stack of Verbatim CD-RWs that I use to burn the betas of my Copland distribution to, for testing on my iMac. Either way, just make sure that you burn the CD at a low speed (8x or below) on the Windows machine, and you should be fine!

### Post by rondackcpu on 2007-05-20
Folks,

Indeed the Imac G3's are allergic to CD-R's.  Before you go buy some new CDs, I suggest you try burning the CD at a lower speed.  I have had good results burning at 4X.  Takes only a few minutes more, but all the difference in the world.
