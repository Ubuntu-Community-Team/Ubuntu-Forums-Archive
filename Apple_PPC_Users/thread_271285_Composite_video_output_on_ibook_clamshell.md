### Post by nuttymac on 2008-06-15
All clamshell imac's have the capability to output to a tv.  You'll need one of these cables though:


[http://store.apple.com/1-800-MY-APPLE/WebObjects/AppleStore.woa/wo/StoreReentry.wo?productLearnMore=M8434G/A](http://store.apple.com/1-800-MY-APPLE/WebObjects/AppleStore.woa/wo/StoreReentry.wo?productLearnMore=M8434G/A)

This is exactly what you need.  Ignore all of the "not compatable" messages.  This is the cheapest one out there that I now works with the clamshell line of computer.
