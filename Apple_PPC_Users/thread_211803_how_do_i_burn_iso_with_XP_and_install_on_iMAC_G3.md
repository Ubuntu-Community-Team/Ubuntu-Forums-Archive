### Post by linear on 2006-07-08
You need to burn the iso as an **image** - I don't know if the built-in XP CD burning will allow you to do this, so you may need something else (one suggestion is given [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto")). It's often recommended that you burn at a slow speed (4x).

On the iMac, hold down the  "C" key after the start-up chime to get it to boot from the CD.
