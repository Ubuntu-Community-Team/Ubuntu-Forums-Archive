### Post by Torrance on 2006-10-11
Ricanelite,

In disk utility, you need to click on the whole disk, and click reformat. Disk utility can't reuse mac os x partitions.

The warning that SMART is failing is very worrying though! This is warning for when your hard drive is about to fail, giving you enough time to get important stuff off it. How long have you had the computer for? It's very unlikely Ubuntu has anything to do with causing a hard drive failure. Alternatively, it may be a false warning, and it may reset itself to OK once the whole disk is formatted again.
