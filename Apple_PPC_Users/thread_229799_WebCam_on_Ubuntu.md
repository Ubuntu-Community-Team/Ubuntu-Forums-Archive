> **LLCJ07 said:**

There are a few webcam uploaders available in the Ubuntu repository... I've used camorama in the past.  Most of them rely on your webcam being video4linux (v4l) compatible though-- probably easiest just to try it and see if yours is.

If you mean your webcam can store photos on board, which you can upload later... then you might want/need to try gtkcam instead.
