### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-01-02
Dapper and Edgy for PowerPC are still supported by Canonical AFAIK. Feisty, though, is not. The update notifier will continue to work as usual.

If you're happy with Dapper on your machine, you're not being affected by bugs that are fixed in Edgy or Edgy's apps, and you don't feel the need to run the latest programs, then stick with Dapper.
