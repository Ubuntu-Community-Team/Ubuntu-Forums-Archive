### Post by pauljwells on 2007-02-04
Do you get the yaboot scren offering the 'safe' boot option? If so, try that and see what errors it gives you (you get verbose reporting). Tell us what it says and we might have a clue what's gone wrong!

If you haven't done much with your system yet a reinstall is probably the easiest bet - but my advice would be to try to learn what's gone wrong and how to fix it first, so you're better equiped when it isn't so easy in the future. You can always reinstall just for peace of mind afterwards.
