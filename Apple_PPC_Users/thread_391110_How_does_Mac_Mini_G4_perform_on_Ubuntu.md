### Post by karstux on 2007-03-23
I'm using Xubuntu on a mac mini with 1.4 GHz, 1GB. IMHO the performance is better than with OS X. But I'm not sure it's a good home theater pc with either OS.

Under OS X I used it to watch DivX videos on my television, a task it performed quite well - but those weren't hi-def videos, nor was the TV output quality too great. I'm guessing you'd be better off buying a used (modchipped) XBox and installing XBMC on it...

So I'd agree with the previous poster - it's a great PC for everyday stuff, but no substitute for a HTPC.
