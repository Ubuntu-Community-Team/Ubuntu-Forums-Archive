### Post by zenlunatic on 2005-05-09
Airport extreme is not supported under linux because the specifications are secret and the manufacturer refuses to cooperate with the linux developers. There is a petition online somewhere that is calling for them to release the specifications.

In the meantime I would suggest using a different PCMCIA wireless card if your system has PCMCIA or alternatively using a usb wireless device that is supported under linux.
