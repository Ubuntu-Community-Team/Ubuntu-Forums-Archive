### Post by Michael Steinberg on 2006-01-08
I won't add to the good advice you're getting advice on the Performa; but you're not going to find linux that'll run on the SE and the Plus. Great machines in their day--the Plus could be maxed out at ONE FULL MEGABITE OF MEMORY! In fact, you could attach a hard drive and run Quark XPress on a Plus, and print PostScript files to a LaserWriter--that was the beginning of desktop publishing.

If you're eager to try out reallly old Mac software on the SE there's plenty around on the web. I used to be able to make 800k floppies (that's what the SE took, I believe) and I have some old systems & software around. Let me know if you'd like to play.
