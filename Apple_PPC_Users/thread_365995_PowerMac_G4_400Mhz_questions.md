### Post by seatea on 2007-02-21
I have a PowerMac G4 400 Mhz Gigabit ethernet, 1 Gb RAM. I waited a long time to run Mac OS X on it out of fear of performance problems. I had used ubuntu for a few months prior to that time. I really didn't have any serious complaints about speed issues with ubuntu, I ran Dapper and Edgy. When I finally upgraded to Mac OS X, I was surprised at how well it performed. Mac OS X is faster than ubuntu in my opinion. I eventually upgraded to an OWC Mercury Extreme G4 1.2 GHz, but while it is faster, I can't say  that the speed increase was anything astonishing. With all the concerns surrounding the discontinuation of support  for Ubuntu PPC, I  am planning on ratcheting back to Dapper since it will have some official support for a few more years. I am starting to question the continuing use of Ubuntu though.
