### Post by Ender710 on 2006-09-21
The volume was too low for me too, but the volume controls that i was looking at were wrong.  Try going to volume control and changing the device from what it is (for me it was HDA Intel) to the other (SigmaTel STAC9221 A1) and then change the volume.  I've got a non-pro macbook and it worked for me.
