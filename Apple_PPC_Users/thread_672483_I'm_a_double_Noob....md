### Post by ssam on 2008-01-20
midle and right click should be mapped to F11 and F12. these are a bit awkward to use. it is possible to remap them to other keys (a bit of searching should find instructions), or plug in any 2/3 button USB mouse and it will work.

for mp3 and such install the ubuntu-restricted-extras package.

there are a few books, [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuBooks](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuBooks) but the wiki is probably a better source of information. a lot of things that required manual set up 1 year ago are automatic now, even the newest books will be writen about 6 months before they are in the shops.

for example most linux books with have lots of information about installing and configuring CUPS to get a printer work. but if you plug a printed into a recent ubuntu, there is a very high chance that it will be automatically configured, you may is some cases need to go into system->administration->printing and press add and ok a few times.
