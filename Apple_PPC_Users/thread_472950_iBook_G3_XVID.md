### Post by VikoZurita on 2007-06-17
Hello, I hope this helps you, I'm able to watch DivX and Xvid on my iBook G3 using Xine, and xine-totem instead of gstreamer.

Change your repositories for the U.S. Repos and the Medibuntu ones.
For the U.S.  Repos go to Source-o-Matic and select United States, Feisty or the one you run and PPC... 
For medibuntu go to medibuntu site.

I'm not using VLC or MPlayer (Totem with Xine) wich is reportedly to work, mine didn't, but using xine it went OK.

At this moment, I can listen to mp3, watch DivX and even Beryl on mi modest G3

iBook G3 600 MHz Dual USB
256 MB Ram
16 MB VRAM

Hope it helped
