### Post by papatrpt89 on 2007-12-30
I think that trying to run it on an ipod is probably not the best of ideas.  Spend a little money, and get yourself a USB zip drive, or even an external HD.

That said, a good resource for running linux off of USB things is [http://www.pendrivelinux.com](http://www.pendrivelinux.com)

Link to Gutsy specific article: [http://www.pendrivelinux.com/2007/09/28/usb-ubuntu-710-gutsy-gibbon-install/](http://www.pendrivelinux.com/2007/09/28/usb-ubuntu-710-gutsy-gibbon-install/)

These may or may not work with an ipod.  There is a good chance of turning your ipod into a brick, though.  Seriously, consider some other type of external storage, if at all possible.

Good luck!
