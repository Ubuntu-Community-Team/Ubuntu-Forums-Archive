### Post by robino on 2006-06-23
I have very good out-of-the-box experience with the driver ZD1211. You can see a list of supported adapters on the [homepage of the driver]("http://zd1211.ath.cx/").

My device (3COM OFFICE CONNECT 3CRUSB10075) is also [working]("http://www.netstumbler.org/showthread.php?t=18159") under OSX.

If it does not work as it should (which is rather an exception), [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=188654") you find instructions. These are fairly easy.

Good luck!
