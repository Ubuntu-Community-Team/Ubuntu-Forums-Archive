### Post by fl1pper on 2005-10-05
No matter which app you use to get sound/video, there are issues in Linux-ppc for the multimedia we take for granted on the Mac.
One thing you have to do is this: In the app you hear no sound, get into the settings (usually Edit>Options, or Edit>settings), and select your audio, and configure it (if you're in Ogle, forget it, use something else)...just set the "card" or main setting to use ALSA, (avoid the defaults), and if given a choice besides 'default' also select either "PCM" or PCM,1". If that doesn't get your audio, go to a different playback app.
Ubuntu is terrific, but the Audio/Video thing just simply blows, when you're coming from an Apple background.
