### Post by 3rdalbum on 2006-11-28
You could have a look at PearPC, but you would need a copy of OS X for PowerPC and access to a PowerPC ROM. I think they are included with OS X as the Newworld Macs have a software ROM, but you'd have to check that. It would also mean violating the OS X license, which I honestly can't recommend.

[Basilisk II]("http://gwenole.beauchesne.info/projects/basilisk2")

and

[SheepShaver]("http://gwenole.beauchesne.info/en/projects/sheepshaver")

(a rather absurd name).

Also these require a copy of the Mac OS and a Macintosh ROM image, of course...
