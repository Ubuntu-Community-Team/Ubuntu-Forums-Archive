### Post by Ptero-4 on 2006-06-21
you can't. The odd errors come from the fact that M$ V$PC detects your linux partitions & kernel and automatically changes the emulated hardware in such way that ubuntu (or any OSS OS in general) have a hell of a hard time to recognize the emulated hardware (if it does at all), your only hope at running linux is either native (ubuntu PPC) or through either Q or the older connectix VPC 6 (which doesn't go insane over a linux install).
