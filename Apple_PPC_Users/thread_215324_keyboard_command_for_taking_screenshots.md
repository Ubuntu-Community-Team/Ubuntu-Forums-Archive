### Post by jayemef on 2006-07-13
Assuming you're a Gnome user, the Print Screen button will take a screen shot of your desktop. If you want just your current window, use Alt+Print Screen.

If this doesn't work for you, you can use the Gimp. You can take a screen shot with File > Aquire or something like that.
