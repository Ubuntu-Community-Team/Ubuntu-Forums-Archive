### Post by eklem on 2008-02-17
Yes and no. You get a computer, but it has limited access to the hardware (only using a small portion of the cpu-power, and a tiny part of the gpu). And when it comes to games it's a definitive no! No ps3-games can be played from within the Linux-installation. You can read more about it at [http://psubuntu.com/forum/](http://psubuntu.com/forum/)
