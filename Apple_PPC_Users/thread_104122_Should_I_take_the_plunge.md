### Post by kairu0 on 2005-12-17
I thought your main question was "Should I take the plunge?" My response to that was "try the LiveCD," because, well, that's the best way to try Kubuntu before "tak[ing] the plunge."

Now, for your other questions. Will you take a performance hit? Probably not. Especially since this is only a second computer to keep you busy on the commute and the bulk of what it seems you are going to do with it is video playback. I'm sure Kubuntu will be able to do this sufficiently enough. (It's not like it's a demanding task for a new computer.)

Are there any drawbacks? Well, you might have some ACPI issues. Watching videos on the commute probably means that you would benefit from hibernation on closing the lid. That is one thing you'll have to research via Ubuntu on iBook pages.

And I'm not sure that I understand your last two questions. You want to sync data between 2 ubuntu machines. How else were you planning to do it other than over your network?
