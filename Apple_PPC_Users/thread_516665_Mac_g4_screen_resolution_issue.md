### Post by tcrroadie on 2007-08-03
Ahh, I see that the incorrect  video driver is being used.  Simply change "fbdev" to "radeon" and you should be golden. 

Make the "Device" section of your xorg.conf file look like this.  

```
Section "Device"
	Identifier	"ATI RADEON 9000"
	Driver		"radeon"
	BusID		"PCI:0:16:0"
	Option		"UseFBDev"		"true"
```

As an added note, you may also want to try disabling the "UseFBDev" option to increase your 3D performance.  

```
Section "Device"
	Identifier	"ATI RADEON 9000"
	Driver		"radeon"
	BusID		"PCI:0:16:0"
	Option		"UseFBDev"		"false"
```
