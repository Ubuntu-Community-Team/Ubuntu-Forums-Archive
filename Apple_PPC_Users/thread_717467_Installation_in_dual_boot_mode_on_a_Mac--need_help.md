### Post by stream303 on 2008-03-07
Ah, a couple quick questions:

Are you downloading the Power-PC version?  What kind of Mac are you using?  Most of us recommend using the "ALTERNATE" PPC image for install, although this means that it won't be a live-cd.

Are you burning it as an iso image, rather than just copying it over?  Sounds like you are doing it right if you are using disk utility - drag the iso image to the left-hand pane of disk-utility, highlight it, and click the burn icon....

Here is a great howto just to cover many bases...
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)

Are you burning the disk at too high a speed for your mac to read at?  Depending on your model of Mac, you may also have to burn at a low speed...
