If you are an Xubuntu ppc user, or a new xfce4 convert like me, be sure to see the news about the changes coming in /etc/apt/sources.list :
[http://www.xubuntu.org/news/hardy/ports](http://www.xubuntu.org/news/hardy/ports)

I just installed it tonight, and had to revert to the old sources, but that is about to change very rapidly, so if your sources no longer work, you'll know why and how to change it.

I think I caught this in mid-stream, and didn't see the note.

In case your mirror doesn't carry the ports, you can grab the latest Xubuntu ppc RC here:
[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/xubuntu/ports/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/xubuntu/ports/)
Take your pic from the "daily" images, which are the non-live installers, or the "daily-live", which are um, the live-cd images. :)

Even though I have plenty of ram, I still prefer the daily non-live alternate images.
