### Post by luciferin on 2007-10-05
You don't need to use emusic remote, and since it's unsupported on Linux I'd recoment disabling it.  Just go into Your Account  » eMusic Remote Settings and hit the Disable eMusic Remote button.  All of your track download links will now download the mp3 files instead of the .bin files.

If you want you can try installing the software under Wine, I don't recomend it however since there's a perfectly suitable solution available already.
