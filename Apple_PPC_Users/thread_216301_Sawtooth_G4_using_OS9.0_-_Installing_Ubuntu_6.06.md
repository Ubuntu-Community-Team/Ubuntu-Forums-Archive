Hi Gareth,
Have you tried booting from the CD you burnt? Forget about trying to mount it in OS 9 as it virtually no native support for Joliet (you'll need [this extension to get support](http://www.tempel.org/joliet)), hence the reason you keep getting nagged to initialise the disk. People here refer to Disk Utility, and although I haven't used OS 9 for a long time, iirc, the disk utility app in 9 is nowhere near as functional as OS X's, so you might want to try something like Toast 5 or [Discribe](http://www.charismac.com/discribe/index.php?option=content&task=view&id=79&Itemid=62), although you did mention that your Mac had no burner which basically makes these suggestions redundant.

Good luck.
