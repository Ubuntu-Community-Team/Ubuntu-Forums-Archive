### Post by BladeMelbourne on 2008-04-19
The only version of Windows NT that ever worked on PowerPC is NT 3.51 - which is 13 years old.

Wikipedia says the following: > Windows NT 3.51 added support for the PowerPC processor in 1995, specifically PReP-compliant systems such as the IBM Power Series desktops/laptops and Motorola PowerStack series; but despite meetings between Michael Spindler and Bill Gates, significantly not on the Power Macintosh.

In my opinion, even NT4 is too old and useless, so I think even if you could get 3.51 running, it would be useless.
