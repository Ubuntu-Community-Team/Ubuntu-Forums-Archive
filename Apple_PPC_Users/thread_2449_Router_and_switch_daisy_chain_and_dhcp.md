### Post by sr.pato on 2004-10-28
What type of router do you have? 

When you log into the router web interface to set it up is dhcp running and set up to supply addresses to more than 1 client?

On your switch, does the original computer get an IP?

Do you have the uplink button pressed?

Is the router lan cable plugged into the #1 slot on the switch?

Does your DLS modem require yo to log in and authenticate with your ISP directly through the modem?

Those are a few quickies I would check for starters, post a little more info on your router and we can go from there if none of these help you.
