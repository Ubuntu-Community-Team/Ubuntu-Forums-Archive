### Post by bennogc on 2005-02-21
Here is a tut how to install Debian on an iBook without the use of the CD-Drive:
[http://people.debian.org/~branden/ibook.html](http://people.debian.org/~branden/ibook.html)
Maybe it helps you to do the same with your iMac and ubuntu...

As you don't even have the possibility to reinstall MacOS from CD you'll probably have to boot the mac you want to use with ubuntu in target-mode connected via FireWire to the other iMac.
You can than repartition the ubuntu-mac's HD and proceed as mentioned on branden's ibook page...
