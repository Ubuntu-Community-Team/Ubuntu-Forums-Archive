### Post by stmiller on 2007-10-09
You can upgrade to Gutsy after installing all Feisty updates, then issuing this command in a terminal:

```
gksudo "update-manager -c -d"
```

But **don't do this** until the final release of Gutsy is out, or you risk breaking your system.

As far as the 'gaming' edition, you'd have to ask the creators of that. They are not affiliated with Ubuntu. But I would say probably not, as there are no 3D drivers for the PS3 in Linux right now. :(
