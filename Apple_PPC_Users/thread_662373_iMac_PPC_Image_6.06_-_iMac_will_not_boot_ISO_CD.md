### Post by VidiotGeek on 2008-01-16
You have a G3 iMac. If it has a tray loading drive, then it's a very early model. Later models of the CRT variety all had slot loading drives. I'd guess it a rev C or rev D [(Apple-History page)]("http://www.apple-history.com/?page=gallery&model=imacrevd&performa=off&sort=date&order=DESC"). 

I know there are a couple of threads around here pertaining to the G3 boot issues that should be able to get you up & running.
