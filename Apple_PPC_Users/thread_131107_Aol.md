### Post by eriefisher on 2006-02-15
I'm assuming your trying to dial into AOL on a phone line. Do you have a external modem or supported internal? If so when you open KPPP click configure>detect your modem>add phone number>ok>enter account name>enter password>connect.

I may have missed a step because I'm going from memory here, I'm using it at this time so I can't reopen it without an error but it's pretty easy. If your using an external modem you should have no problem as long as it's a serial modem. You may have problems with internal or usb modems because "they are not modems".
Some internal modems do have support but it varys. Sometimes it's just not worth it. You can buy External serial modems for as low as $50.

Don't know if this is what you are looking for or not but good luck.
