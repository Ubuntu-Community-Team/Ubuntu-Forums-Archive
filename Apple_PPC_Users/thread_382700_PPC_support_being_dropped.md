### Post by linear on 2007-03-12
This has been discussed in the forum quite a bit, and the decision is documented [here]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/ubuntu-announce/2007-February/000098.html").

To quote (emphasis mine):

[LEFT]"Beginning with Ubuntu 7.04, the PowerPC edition of Ubuntu will be reclassified as unofficial.  *The PowerPC software itself and supporting infrastructure will continue to be available, and supported by a community team*."[/LEFT]
