### Post by GREGMACKENZIE on 2006-10-18
I did exactly that, inserting the dapper cd and nothing happened. If your online via high speed, you can upgrade. You'll need to do a search in the help as there are some command line things you have to do.

As a general note, if you launch synaptic with a live internet connection, and reload the repositories, it should inform you soon thereafter that a new upgrade "dapper" exists and provide you with an opportunity to update your desktop. I didn't because I'm on dialup.

If you can download the iso of the dapper install disk your probably going to want that disk sooner or later. Backup your data first if you use that to install!
