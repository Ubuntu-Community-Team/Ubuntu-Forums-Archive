To bot from a CD on your Mac - hold down the letter "C".
Alternativ, you are supposed to be able to hold down the "Alt"-key and that should show you a boot-handler, so that you can choose where you would want to boot from.
If your CD-rom is an external player, the letter "T" is the one to use.
