### Post by Auria on 2007-10-06
> **derred said:**

A little Wine?(is good for the heart :P)

Good thing i asked, it seems like you want everything that doesn't work on PPC :P

There are only the opensource 3D drivers. They are not as good as the binary drivers you can find on x86 machines so don't expect stable complex 3D. Compiz is in no way garanteed to work because of the said drivers, people have had mixed success with it. SDL games are okay though, Frozen Bubble works very nice, never tried others. but for games as i said those using heavily graphics cards may not. Wine dos not run at all on PPC. Adobe flash is not supported PPC (there is free alternative Gnash but it doesn't support quite a lot of websites - people often say it supports youtube but AFAIK in practice support is still very buggy)
Voila ;) It's always better to know the right information before buying
