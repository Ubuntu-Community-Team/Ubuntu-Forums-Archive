### Post by Ripfox on 2007-11-27
I was having a heck of a time installing 7.10 on my old g3 powerbook, the full live cd dumped me to busybox, while the alternate cd said it could not see my cdrom halfway into the beginning stages of the install. (scanning for cdroms)

Well I went and fetched my usb to ide gadget, hooked up a cd drive to the laptop via usb, and slapped the disk in the laptops cdrom drive to start the install. Then, when it complained about no drom, I just stuck a needle in the cdrom drive of the laptop forcing it open and slapped the disk in the usb drive to hit "try again". Well it worked and it installed fine then. Even detected my old ATI card and enabled the ati driver. Cool!
