### Post by tweaksource on 2009-05-13
I just wanted to post that I am using Line 6 Guitarport to record guitar in Ubuntu Studio 9.04.

With the drivers obtained [COLOR="Blue"][here]("http://www.tanzband-scream.at/line6/")[/COLOR], using the [COLOR="Blue"][supplied instructions]("http://www.tanzband-scream.at/line6/INSTALL")[/COLOR], I compiled the usb driver, fired up Ardour, selected guitarport as the sound device and immediately had sound.

Adding a new track and enabling record I was pleased to find that the latency is almost non-existent and the sound is great!

The latency is actually better than in Winblows on the same machine!

Please check out the [COLOR="Blue"][Sourceforge]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/line6linux")[/COLOR] project.

Also, guitarists add your voice [COLOR="Blue"][here]("http://line6.com/support/?threadID=35622&tstart=0")[/COLOR] and kindly ask Line 6 for Linux support.
