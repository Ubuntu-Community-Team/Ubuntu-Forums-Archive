### Post by sgx on 2011-10-01
[http://www.studio-units.com/](http://www.studio-units.com/)

The author felt this did not fit a market niche, and so made it free,
and continues in another direction, but will continue with this, as well. The interface worked flawlessly in wine, the fx I tested sounded fine. It is a design framework, where you route the fx and parameters in realtime, like a rakarrack with bezier patch cords for everything, so you can invent and save simple or complex presets, of which 11 are provided, along with some excellent docs.

Those born of Sargent Schulz can learn, and those with deep knowledge
will have great potential to create some fx masterpieces. 
33 plugins are included to begin the session. :guitar:
And just in time for the monsoons :)
