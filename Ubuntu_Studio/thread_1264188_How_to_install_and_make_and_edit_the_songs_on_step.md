This is a tutorial on how to install stepmania on ubuntu 9.0.4 and create and edit songs.

TO INSTALL THE GAME (does not come with songs)
1. Download from [http://www.stepmania.com/wiki/Downloads](http://www.stepmania.com/wiki/Downloads) (if your a beginner pick binary version)
2. Extract the file to your destination by right clicking on the zip and click extract.
3. Open your extracted file and right click on the stepmania icon (it says stepmania)
4. go to permissions and click the box that says "allow executing file as a program" (unless already checked)
5. right click on the stepmania icon and then click open.(you will have no songs unless you added them.

HOW TO ADD SONGS
1. download your song pack or songs from a website like "http://www.otakusdream.com/animemix/5thmix.htm" or just from "http://www.stepmania.com/"
2. after downloaded (extract if you have to) then just put them in the songs folder.
WARNING: if you have a song you like and you put it in your songs folder it will be blank or the game won't notice it.

HOW TO MAKE AND EDIT SONGS
1.Just make a .txt file and name it whatever you want EX: "Joseph.txt
2. open it
3.Paste this in your txt file (no spaces like i did)(because it would of been a big sloppy mess) 
#TITLE: INSERT SONG TITLE HERE ;

#SUBTITLE: INSERT SUBTITLE HERE ;

#ARTIST: INSERT SONG ARTIST HERE ;

#TITLETRANSLIT: IF YOUR SONG TITLE IS IN A FOREIGN LANGUAGE, INSERT ENGLISH 
TRANSLATION HERE;

#SUBTITLETRANSLIT: IF YOUR SUBTITLE IS IN A FOREIGN LANGUAGE, INSERT ENGLISH TRANSLATION HERE;

#ARTISTTRANSLIT: IF YOUR SONG ARTIST IS IN A FOREIGN LANGUAGE, INSERT ENGLISH TRANSLATION HERE;

#CREDIT:NAME OF STEPFILE AUTHOR;

#BANNER:INSERT THE FILENAME OF YOUR BANNER;

#BACKGROUND:INSERT THE FILENAME OF THE BACKGROUND IMAGE YOU WANT TO APPEAR AT THE BEGINNING OF THE FILE;

#LYRICSPATH:INSERT THE FILENAME OF THE LYRICS FILE, IF APPLICABLE;

#CDTITLE:INSERT THE FILENAME OF YOUR AUTHOR ICON;

#MUSIC:INSERT THE FILENAME OF YOUR AUDIO FILE;

#OFFSET:You can worry about this later;

#SAMPLESTART:INSERT WHERE YOU WANT TO BEGIN THE SAMPLE MUSIC. YOU CAN ALWAYS CHANGE THIS LATER;

#SAMPLELENGTH:INSERT HOW LONG YOU WANT THE SAMPLE MUSIC TO LAST. A NUMBER ~20 IS USUALLY GOOD;

#SELECTABLE:YES;

#BPMS:0.000=JUST PUT A NUMBER HERE, WORRY ABOUT IT LATER;

#STOPS:;

#BGCHANGES:;

5.after you change that make a folder and name it what ever you want EX:Homeade (and put the txt file in)
6. along with the text file your gonna need the song your bg picture and your screen picture.
rename that .txt file to a .sm EX:"Joseph.sm"
7. put your folder into your songs folder with the other folders with it (there will be other folder if i downloaded song packs)
8.open stepmania ang go to edit songs
9. find your song and start editing
