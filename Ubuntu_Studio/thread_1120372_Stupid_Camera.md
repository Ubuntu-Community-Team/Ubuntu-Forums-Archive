### Post by physwizz on 2009-05-22
Excellent, putting the camera into review mode before connecting it works fine.
1. turn camera on
2. press the "review" button on the back
3. connect the camera to the usb on the computer
4. it then asks you if you want to use F-Spot
5. click yes and download all the pics you want
