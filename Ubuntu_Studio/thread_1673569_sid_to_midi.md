### Post by anthony62490 on 2011-02-01
All right, I found one. 

Turns out, there was a tool called SID2MIDI back in 2002-ish, but it looks like the author lost interest and allowed the [host website]("http://www.geocities.com/SiliconValley/Lakes/5147/sid2midi.html") to expire. 

BUT in 2006, the author (Michael Schwendt) reappeared on [this thread]("http://www.remix64.com/board/viewtopic.php?f=3&t=4304") and provided a new download link for a free version of SID2MIDI. I'm not certain if you can still buy the full version, but there don't appear to be any immediately apparent handicaps in the free version.

I have tried running it in WINE and have run into a few minor problems (not detecting instruments, can't play arpeggios or climbing notes). I'm not sure that these are the fault of SID2MIDI though, since I am running the MIDIs through NoteworthyComposer also.

All in all though, it seems to be a pretty reliable program.
I hope this is what you were looking for.

Direct download link:
[SID2MIDI]("http://home.arcor.de/ms2002sep/bak/SID2MIDIw.exe")
[Old SID2MIDI + Documentation]("http://home.arcor.de/ms2002sep/bak/s2mwr17.zip")
