### Post by jejeman on 2011-06-10
To see flash content you need to have flash player installed. Two ways to do that
1. via synaptic (good option for 32bit)
2. download from site tar.gz and extract libflashplayer.so to ~/.mozilla/plugins/  (better for 64bit)
