Get the Fender Mustang 1 usb amp. It's usb connection is seen
by name in qjackctl. You can instantly feed your software
24 different sounds by turning the main dial. Its a modeling amp
with 12 amps, a nice range of fx, for post and pre,
even bias and sag on some. A linux editor called plug, link
