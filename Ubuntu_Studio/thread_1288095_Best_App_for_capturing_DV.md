### Post by mendozaro on 2009-10-11
Your best option for capturing should be Kino ( [http://www.kinodv.org/](http://www.kinodv.org/) ).

As for editing.. for me atleast there are only two real options worth mentioning: Kdenlive ([http://www.kdenlive.org/](http://www.kdenlive.org/)) and Open Movie Editor ([http://www.openmovieeditor.org/](http://www.openmovieeditor.org/)).

U can find all this programs in the add/remove software section.
