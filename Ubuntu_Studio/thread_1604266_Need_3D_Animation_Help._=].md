### Post by RaumTrug on 2010-10-23
the program you might be looking for is called "blender", it's homepage is [http://www.blender.org]("http://www.blender.org/")

it's in the repos of any current ubuntu, and some working opengl driver/graphics card is somewhat needed to use it.

and searching the web for example for "blender tutorial" you might find "some" tutorials and online-books on using it (plenty!!!, in text and video, but not always the best quality...just read/watch them all & you'll dig it after some time). there's even books/videos for sale, from real pros around.

but be prepared that this is not really trivial/simple. you'll need quite some time (not just weeks, rather months of daily learning for mastership) and motivation (and creativity/phantasy) to learn to use it, until you even have something moving in there...and until you get your wobbly blob moving the way you want you'll have to learn&practice a lot. it's not like you push a button and have rendered to viedeowhat you've just imagined ;)
