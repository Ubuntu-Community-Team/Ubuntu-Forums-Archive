### Post by blazer34i on 2010-11-02
Stay away from ATI AIW's (All-in-Wonders) they are not very well supported and are not very good cards IMHO. I would recommend the Hauppauge line of PVRs. If you want to use a ATI though, use the TV Wonder PCI. 

I have operated a MythTV box for about 6 years now and contributed to the MythDora distro. Although I used MythDora for a few years, I switched to  MythBuntu, which I think has matured and is a more refined distro.  

Your best shot at making it work is with hardware that is supported out of the box. Also make sure you look into a schedules direct account. That would provide the listings for your TV programming. You could always use MythBuntu and then load the other multimedia applications using Apt-get, aptitude, or Synaptic.

[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Tuner_Card#Cards_that_work](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/Tuner_Card#Cards_that_work)

Good luck and have fun!
