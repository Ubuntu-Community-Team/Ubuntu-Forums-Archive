### Post by JimmyVolatile on 2008-10-25
Hi. To add music for the whole presentation in OpenOffice Impress: 
1. Select "View"--> "Task Pane".
2. Expand the "Slide Transition" panel and locate the "Sound" dropdown. Select "Other..." from the dropdown and locate your wav-file (you can use other sound formats as well but not mp3).
3. Finally, check "Loop until next sound"

The music will play until the end of the presentation or until another transition with a different music file is encountered.

Only thing is that if you want to time the music with the slides, you need to bear in mind that the presentation plays at different speeds in OO Impress compared to Powerpoint. 
In impress heavy transitions (like the dissolve-effect) will slow down the presentation during the transition so timings will be off compared to with Powerpoint. But if you're running the presentation in Impress only then you'll be ok anyway. :)
