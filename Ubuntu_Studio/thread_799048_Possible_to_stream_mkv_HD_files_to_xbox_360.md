### Post by matt221984 on 2008-05-19
No, you cant stream MKV to a 360 because MKV isnt one of the xbox 360s supported formats. You basically have two options:

1. transcoding on the fly - your server will convert to a playable format on the fly while you are watching - this takes quite a bit of processing power for MKV files.

2. convert to MP4 - you wont have to convert the video, so you can do this quickly, but the 360 only supports stereo sound in an MP4, so you'll lose the 5.1 that your MKVs probably have.

There are a variety of programs to do this on windows, but I havent attempted to do it on Linux. I hear fuppes is able to do transcoding on the fly.
