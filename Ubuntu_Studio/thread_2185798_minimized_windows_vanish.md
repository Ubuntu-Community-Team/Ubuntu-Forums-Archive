### Post by Toz on 2013-11-04
Hello and welcome to the forums.

I believe that Ubuntu Studio uses Xfce as its desktop environment. Check out some of the preferences in the Windows Button plugin. To do so, right click the panel that the buttons are on (don't click directly on the buttons or you won't see the option) and select Panel->Panel Preferences. In the Panel Preferences dialog, click on the Items tab then double-click on the "Window Buttons" item. Here you will see some options. I would suggest toggling the "Window grouping" behaviours and "Show windows from all workspaces" filtering options to see if that helps.
