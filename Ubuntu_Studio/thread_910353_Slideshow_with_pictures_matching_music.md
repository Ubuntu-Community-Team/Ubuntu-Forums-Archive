### Post by cotcot on 2008-09-06
> **Creative2 said:**
> hey cotcot really ?  but would you show something?
It is too big to upload (HD video), it is better to explain how to do.
It is not difficult.
You can import images in the blender VSE. Take care to select the correct format so that the pictures are not distorted. Import the sound(s) on other track(s). With the g-command you can move and lenghten/shorten the images to match the sound. Render. 
You can put at the same moment effects on the pictures (move, zoom, rotate). 
I am currently making a film (an hour or so length) with a combination of video clips, pictures, sound, text (text on transparent background saved as png) in alpha overlay; still discovering and exercising blender features. IPO curves and keyframes are very useful.
