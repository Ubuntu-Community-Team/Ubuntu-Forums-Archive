I use to transport my Ardour-Projects by simply copying them from my workstations harddisk to a USB-harddisk, from there to my Laptops Hd and vice versa.
Works perfectly OK. 
Up to some 16 tracks or so I can work directly on the USB-disk but most of my stuff is about 30+ tracks wich works smoother on built-in drives.

Of course all project-files, presets etc can be transported that way. No real need for a complete system on a disk.
At the other hand: if the external drive is an SSD and both computers and the disk offer USB3-ports....:P
