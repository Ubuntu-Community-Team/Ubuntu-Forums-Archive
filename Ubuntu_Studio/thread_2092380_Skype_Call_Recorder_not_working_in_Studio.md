### Post by nadalex on 2012-12-07
SOLVED.
I know how... but I don't know why...

I un-installed and re-installed through Terminal.
(Not sure how I installed earlier.)
After re-installing through Terminal, "Skype Call Recorder" now shows up in "Internet" rather than "Accessories." 
(It showed up in "Accessories" in Gnome, but still worked fine.)
And now it seems to work fine.
