### Post by mikethedj4 on 2009-10-14
Depending on which gimp version you have 2.7 may vary, for example I still have gimp 2.6.

Just open up your home folder, hit CTRL+H or hover over the View tab, and check Show Hidden Files.

Now head to your gimp folder now the brushes, fonts, scripts, folders are all there.
