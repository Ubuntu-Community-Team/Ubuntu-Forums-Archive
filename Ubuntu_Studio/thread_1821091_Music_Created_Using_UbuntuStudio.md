### Post by Precipitous on 2011-08-08
For people wanting to check out what others are using UbuntuStudio for: 

The latest album from Occurrences In Rain - "[Influence Of Outside Systems]("http://soundcloud.com/occurrences-in-rain/sets")" (their 4th) was recorded, mixed, etc. primarily on a computer running UbuntuStudio Maverick. Some of the open-source apps used were Audacity, Q-Tractor and Rakarrack. Wine was also used  on a few tracks to allow access to a couple of non-Linux soft synths...

Anyone interested in taking a listen may do so at: [http://soundcloud.com/occurrences-in-rain/sets/influence-of-outside-systems/](http://soundcloud.com/occurrences-in-rain/sets/influence-of-outside-systems/)

Sorry for th attempt at thread jacking - I'll force myself to at least half finish a track and maybe start a new 'show and tell' thread.
@Sylos - Thank you for your interest...  Occurrences In Rain is a collaboration between myself and another musician named Bruno Janiszewski. I live in the US and he lives in Poland, so we do all composing, performing, recording, etc. over the web.

On SoundCloud if you click the link for each piece there are detailed notes describing the instruments and processing techniques used for that piece. For instance, the notes for the first piece "The Freezing Of The Sea Created A Perfect Solitude" read: "Guitar, AudioBulb Sophia, U-he Zebra, field recordings, various effects - made using UbuntuStudio... 
This piece started out as a set of guitar drones and picked chords  which were copied four times. One of the copies was slowed by 50%. One  was slowed by 50% and reversed. Another one was slowed by 25%, reversed,  fed through a 24 second feedback delay, then through a 36 second  feedback delay. Finally one was reversed and raised in pitch by five  semi-tones. These, along with the original, were stacked and arranged.  We then added multiple tracks of shortwave radio static, voices and  marine forecasts recorded by Jobro, Toiletrolltube and 833-45 - made  available through the FreeSound Project. Some of these were reduced in  tempo by 10%-15% and some were left "as is". We finally added two tracks  of AudioBulb Sophia and U-he Zebra background textures."

I hope this is of help...
