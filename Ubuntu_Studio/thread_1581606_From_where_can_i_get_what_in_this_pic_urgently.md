### Post by pivotraze on 2010-09-28
The bottom dock is indeed Cairo Dock. The music player is indeed songbird. The theme would be... I can't say on that one :( But I helped as much as I can.

The newest version of songbird is available from here:
[http://developer.songbirdnest.com/builds/trunk/latest/](http://developer.songbirdnest.com/builds/trunk/latest/)

You will have to build it yourself, sadly. They stopped support for linux. Or you can use a pre-built older version from here:
[http://www.getdeb.net/app/Songbird](http://www.getdeb.net/app/Songbird)

Songbird is not in the repositories sadly.
