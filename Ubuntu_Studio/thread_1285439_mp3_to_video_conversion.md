### Post by Malcy on 2009-10-07
Well, there are a number of ways to do this. Here is one way - first select a video clip of a similar length. Open both the video clip and the mp3 track in a video editor like KDEnlive (in the repositories). Mute the audio on the video track and render the files. 

Rendering the files will create one video file with your mp3 track as the audio. To render, click the render button, choose destination and select web sites then choose the appropriate You Tube resolution.

[http://www.kdenlive.org/](http://www.kdenlive.org/)
