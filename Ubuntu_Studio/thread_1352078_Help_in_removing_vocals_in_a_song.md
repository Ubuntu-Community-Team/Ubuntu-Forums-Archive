### Post by Jurica on 2009-12-11
Hi!
You see removing vocals from a mixed song is a very hard and sometimes impossible job. When it comes to multichannel recording its easy as pushing the delete button. But in a mix the only way to remove the vocals is to "cut it" from a mix. The main problem is when you remove vocals you will remove everything that is in the range of the vocals. If you remove it from the pan or if you remove frequencies, you are going to lose quality of other instruments or like in you case it won't do the job at all. My advice is  to re-record the song form  scratch without vocals, or you can download some karaoke program and play the karaoke version of a song...and the choir can sing along to it :)
Best
JJ
p.s. In your case..main and back vocals are differently panned ..so software probably removes main vocal in the center and leaves back vocals intact cause they are probably panned somewhere else.
