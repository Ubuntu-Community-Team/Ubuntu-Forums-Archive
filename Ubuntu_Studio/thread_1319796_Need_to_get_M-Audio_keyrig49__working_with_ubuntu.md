### Post by AutoStatic on 2009-11-09
Click on the wrench and screwdriver (Settings) icon next to your tracks in either the Song-Editor or the Beat+Bassline Editor.
Then select MIDI, this will show you the MIDI devices that are available on your system. Select your M-Audio Keyrig 49 and off you go.
