### Post by LeeU on 2008-09-18
I recently tried out Ubuntu Studio on Gutsy 7.10. I found that it created major problems with my XORG specs. Next time I booted my system, I had a generic monitor set to 640X400. I immediately removed Ubuntu Studio and all the associated programs. (It also deleted all my settings in RhythmBox) I had to reset the specs several times before I could get it to load properly. I also finally figured out that I had to load [Envy]("http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html") to straighten out the rest of the problems.

I know of another person who had the same problem and said they had heard from others. Be very careful.
