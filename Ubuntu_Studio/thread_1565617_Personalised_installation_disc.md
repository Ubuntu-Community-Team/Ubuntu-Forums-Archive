### Post by sgx on 2010-09-02
Hi, a combo of a separate /home/user partition, ans saving a copy of /etc
and /usr/share, used to overwrite newly installed ones, will preserve most of your customizations.

pclinuxos has a proven remasterme script, requiring a certain amount
of disc space, and medium supporting the size of the remaster. It works.
I have not used a ubuntu equivalent.
