### Post by pmains on 2010-01-29
I am looking to set up a recording studio based on Ubuntu Studio (or, possibly, 64 Studio). My question is what hardware audio interfaces are compatible with Ubuntu Studio? Preferably, the solution would work without having to recompile the kernel, or arcana like that. That sounds like a recipe for introducing serious errors.

My preferred constraints are:
- 4+ XLR inputs
- 4+ 1/4" inputs
- External
  I have been reading that on-board cards have sound-purity issues because they're too close to other components.
- 96kHz Sample Rate
  This is what DigiDesign's 002 has, and it appears to be the standard, but the Lexicon Omega, for instance, is USB 1.1, and so is only 44.1 or 48kHz.
- Hopefully around the $500 range or lower.

Any ideas? I'm looking at the Audiofire 12. Is that a good choice? Are there any close competitors to that?
