### Post by pwaugh on 2008-09-08
If you are using 3DSMax professionally, you'll need to stick with it.  Blender is not even in the same league, which is why Max (not Blender) is being used for CGI in movies, etc.  For professional 3D, Max and Maya are it.  You'll want *nix boxes for post-production and Fx software though.

You can do a lot with Blender if you know it, but it is a toy in comparison, and has a completely different approach/UI.

Good luck
