Hi.

Hydrogen has some extra drumkits that you can download.  Just search in Synaptic for a package called "hydrogen-drumkits". Once installed, you'll have some extra drumkits and demo songs available from Hydrogen.  Check out the Ultra Acoustic Demo and Yamaha Vintage demos; I think those are the best sounding drumkits there.

Another thing I would recommend is that you download a copy of a soundfont bank called "Personal Copy", which you can find at their website [http://www.personalcopy.net/]("http://www.personalcopy.net/").  That GM bank has a lot of useful sounds that you are looking for, like keys, good sounding strings, and even an acoustic drum kit that sounds great.  You can use Qsynth (a graphical front-end to Fluid Synth) to play those soundfonts.

Also would recommend you take a look at ZynAddSubFX; it has some useful synth and keys. I specially like the DX7 patches on it.

Hope this helps you.  Have fun rocking!:guitar:
