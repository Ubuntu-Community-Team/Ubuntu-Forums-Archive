### Post by AutoStatic on 2010-01-09
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=651785](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=651785)
[http://tracker.ardour.org/view.php?id=2466](http://tracker.ardour.org/view.php?id=2466)

It won't work as it should. It's an USB2 device and there are no class compliant USB2 devices. I suggest to get yourself either an USB1 class compliant device (that doesn't need specific Mac/Windows drivers to run) or a Firewire device that works with [FFADO]("http://ffado.org").
