### Post by neu2buntu on 2009-02-06
UPDATE.......  just enable click(metronome) by left click on it (until it lights up),then move your mouse to where it says tempo (the horizontal bars above master and audio tracks) and right click on the red arrow beside the word tempo this will allow you to set the metronome tempo       see screenshot!
