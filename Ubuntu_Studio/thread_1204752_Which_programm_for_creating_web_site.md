### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2009-07-06
> **vickoxy said:**
> Thanks for answers-i need something simple, so i hope i will do it right. Just one more question-after i register domain, how do i upload created web page (i make it offline?).

Your host should supply you with FTP information, use an FTP client to upload it to the server. Aside from that I'd suggest not making a page from scratch. A nice CMS or blog would be better for a simple page so you don't have to code anything or deal with uploading and permissions. An account with Blogger or Wordpress would be ideal for this situation, and you can redirect your domain to another hosting service for a blog or maybe your host already has some blogging software installed. Lots of free and easily editable templates too. Cheers!
