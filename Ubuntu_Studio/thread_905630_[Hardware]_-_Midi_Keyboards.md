### Post by Stochastic on 2008-08-30
do you have a soundcard that has a midi port on it?  If so, then any midi keyboard/controller should work fine with it.  If not, then I'd recommend picking up one of the M-Audio USB adapters.  Personally, I'd stay away from the keyboards that plug directly into your laptop via USB unless you've tried it with your laptop and seen that Jack finds it as a MIDI device ok.
