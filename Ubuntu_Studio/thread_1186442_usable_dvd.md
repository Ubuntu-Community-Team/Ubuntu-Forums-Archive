### Post by Stochastic on 2009-06-13
Devede is probably the best for this.  It will convert your media files to a DVD ISO file.  Once it has created an ISO file for you, then open your favorite burning program (Brasero, GnomeBaker, etc...) and burn the ISO to a blank DVD.  Eject and play in your DVD player.
