### Post by trulan on 2010-08-16
Ffado doesn't have a user forum, they use a mailing list and an IRC channel.  They are pretty helpful, and since the developers have a limited selection of equipment to actually test, I'll bet they'd be glad to have you run a few tests on your 'rare bird'.  The contact info is here:
[http://www.ffado.org/?q=contact](http://www.ffado.org/?q=contact)
