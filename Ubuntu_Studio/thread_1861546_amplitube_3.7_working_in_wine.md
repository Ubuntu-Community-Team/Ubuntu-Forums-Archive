### Post by sgx on 2011-10-15
This new free version works in my wine setup, both stanalone .exe
and as a plugin in Reaper. YMMV Also, the Custom Shop online
purchase/test system worked. And they are giving away a
Vox AC30 amp model for a few days, an amp used by a band from Liverpool.

It nicely compliments rakarrack, for guitar and other fx,
and comes with 23 gear models of amps, mics, stomps, racks.
To test gear, create an account and register Amplitube.
Launch the custom shop when online, and choose what you want to test.
The test period is 48 hours, and each item can be retested every 2 months.
You must be online during testing.

Purchases are done with credits you buy online, with discounts for buying
larger packs of credits.
