### Post by CrocoDuck on 2014-07-24
Hi! Refer to this linked pages:

AlsaMatrix for the support status for non FireWire devices: [http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Main](http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Main)
FFADO for the support status for FireWire devices: [http://www.ffado.org](http://www.ffado.org) ([http://www.ffado.org/?q=devicesupport/list](http://www.ffado.org/?q=devicesupport/list))
Some more tips here: [http://wiki.linuxaudio.org/wiki/start](http://wiki.linuxaudio.org/wiki/start) ([http://wiki.linuxaudio.org/wiki/hardware_support](http://wiki.linuxaudio.org/wiki/hardware_support))

You may find info also on Linux musicians forums: [http://www.linuxmusicians.com](http://www.linuxmusicians.com) .

Hope it helps!
