### Post by ilrudie on 2008-10-29
GIMP is much more that just a more complicated paint.  It is very powerful and capable.  Many user-created scripts exist to fill in gaps or automate complex processes.  No its not an exact clone of photoshop just like GNU/Linux is not an exact clone of Windows or any other OS and its not trying to be.
