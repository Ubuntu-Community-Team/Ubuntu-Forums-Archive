### Post by lakris61 on 2010-11-13
Hi, I can wholeheartedly recommend Beringher. I have a 1202FX myself, and it has been a joy to work with. Even for live performance mixing with my band (song only). As we've stepped up the equipment for our band, I now use my mixer for home recording again, as I did before. It's very practical to just plug in Your guitar, microphones, bass, keyboard and have the mix set correctly and just have it there, no need to switch instruments, cables, or change levels. My domestic drummer is called [Hydrogen]("http://www.hydrogen-music.org/") :)

I've always used Audacity with vanilla Ubuntu or U Studio. Currently W7. I've always used line in, with many different sound cards, never any USB or FireWire mixer or interface. Never seen the need. I guess I'm not an audiophile ;)
