### Post by cotcot on 2009-02-09
I read that your sony has a digital video/audio out and that it supports mpeg2. So it should be easy to connect your cam with usb. The computer will see it as mass storage and you should be able to copy over your files to the hard disk. From there you have different editor that you could check out : kino, kdenlive, lives. Later on if you wich more features you could look at blender VSE or cinelerra. Lumiera is a project to keep an eye on.
