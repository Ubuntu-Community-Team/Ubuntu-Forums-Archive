### Post by raboofje on 2008-11-23
> **le_vainqueur said:**
> I want to buy a keyboard with usb connection to my computer. I would like to make sure whatever I buy is a good value and will work in Ubuntu.

Afaik most keyboards emit 'just plain MIDI' which Ubuntu will generally understand fine.

> I would like some advice as to what features I should look for to get the best value.  I'm planning on spending $100-200 but am flexible depending on the type of product I decide to buy.

One important choice is whether you want the keyboard to have built-in sounds, or if you're fine with just a bare MIDI controller.

Of the latter variety, I've been quite happy with the M-Audio Keystation series. Important features are the number of keys, velocity-sensitiveness and aftertouch. The Keystation has velocity-sensitiveness (you really want that) but not aftertouch (I can live without that fine).
