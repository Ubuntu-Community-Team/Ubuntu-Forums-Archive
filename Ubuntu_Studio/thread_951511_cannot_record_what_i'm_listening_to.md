### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-10-18
You need to double click on the volume icon in your system tray/panel and then go to Edit->Preferences and make sure Mix is set in the possible items to show.  It will then appear in a switches tab when you double click in your volume control icon.  Make sure it is active, not line in or microphone, and you should be good to go.  It may also be worth setting Capture to show in the volume control in order to set the record level.

I must add that this is how I do it in audacity, not sound recorder, which I've never found very good.
