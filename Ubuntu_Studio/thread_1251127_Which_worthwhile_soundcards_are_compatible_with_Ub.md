### Post by sgx on 2009-08-27
An old used or old stock pci M-Audio 24/96 will likely be the easiest to use, best supported in software, and nearly the best sounding, around $40-$80,, midi, digital, and analog i/o. Cards costing double and more may or may not sound better.
