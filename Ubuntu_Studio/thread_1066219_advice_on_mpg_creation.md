### Post by cotcot on 2009-02-12
Yes. Blender VSE is the Video Sequence Editor of blender. Once you open blender you have to push 3 x control arrow right to come into the VSE. From there you can follow this [[COLOR="Red"]tutorial[/COLOR]]("http://wiki.blender.org/index.php/Manual/Using_VSE"). [[COLOR="Red"]This tutorial[/COLOR]]("http://wiki.blender.org/index.php/Manual/VSE_Examples") explains how to import pictures.
The wiki is for blender 2.43 but it is applicable for 2.45 and 2.46 as well.
If the pictures (mixed with the videos) are distorted, you can correct the size with the 'transform' effect.

For kdenlive have a look on the kdenlive website for [[COLOR="Red"]pre-compiled packages[/COLOR]]("http://www.kdenlive.org/user-manual/downloading-and-installing-kdenlive/pre-compiled-packages/ubuntu-packages") or [[COLOR="Red"]compile it using kommander[/COLOR]]("http://www.kdenlive.org/user-manual/downloading-and-installing-kdenlive/builder-wizard").
