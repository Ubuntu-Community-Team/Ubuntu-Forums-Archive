### Post by Cracauer on 2010-04-19
I haven't seen a card with MIDI where the MIDI port wouldn't work. Even your onboard sound might have MIDI, just requiring you to buy the physical plug on a I/O shield.

Myself I use one of those old MIDIsport USB interfaces. No problems ever and no mucking with the computer. Just USB, curse at udev for a while and off you go.
