Hi all,

I'm working on an alternative to soundslides for linux. 
It's called airSlider, it works on Windows, Linux (and Mac soon), it's FREE and you can find it here:

[http://www.kjj.it/airSlider2/](http://www.kjj.it/airSlider2/)


AirSlider is *a** simple*** tool for **creating audio slideshow for Flash **in seconds. It&#8217;s a tool created by **photojournalists** for photojournalists.
 [LEFT]Inspired by the **multimedia documentaries**, AirSlider gives photographers the power to create and distribute their work in a simple way.

VIDEO Support is still VERY experimental. As of now, airSlider is suggested only for use in audio/pictures slideshow.

[/LEFT]
**Features**

 
[LIST]
[*]Supports **pictures, sound **(a single sound file) [B]and videos (video support is experimental)
[/B]
[*]**Fullscreen** presentations
[*]**Crossfade** between slides and videos (with  many different fade transitions)
[*]**Rich-text editor for captions, allowing for links**: turn your soundslide into a multimedia document! (only on [development version]("http://www.kjj.it/airSlider2/?page_id=66"))
[*]Zoomable timeline to cut your slideshow, rearrange slides etc.
[*]Internal Preview
[*]Automatic image **resizing **on exporting
[*]**Immediate resize and export to Flash** in a separate folder (/web), ready to be uploaded to your site
[/LIST]

Anyone trying the software and using Ubuntu - or any other Linux distribution - please write in the forum your "compatibility check" (I only tested the software on Ubuntu 9.04). Thanks.
