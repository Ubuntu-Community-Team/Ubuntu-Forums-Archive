### Post by roffemuffe on 2008-10-31
I don't have much experience with Gimp, but canvas size is the correct option.
Once you get the menu, you get the option to increase the size in all directions, or from a corner or side of the canvas:

e.g.
1. 
***
*x*
***

2.
x**
***
***

3.
***
***
*x*

first one increases from the center and out.
second increases the canvas from the top-left corner, holding that pixel in place.
third one increases from center bottom pixel, holding that pixel in place.
Luckily you can undo the change immediately, if you don't like the change.
