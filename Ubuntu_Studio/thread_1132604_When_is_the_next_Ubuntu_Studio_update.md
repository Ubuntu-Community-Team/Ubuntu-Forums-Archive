### Post by Stochastic on 2009-04-22
9.01 will never happen (just a numbering thing).

Ubuntu Studio releases along with Ubuntu, so you'll see Ubuntu Studio 9.04 on April 23rd.

9 refers to the year of the release, 04 refers to the month of the release.  Ubuntu runs on 6 month release cycles so you'll see the numbers 04 and 10 repeatedly in the release numbering.
