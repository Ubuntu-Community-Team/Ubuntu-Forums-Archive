### Post by sgx on 2012-07-15
Hi, Bodhi Linux 1.4 is a lightweight bloatfree ubuntu based distro,
400 meg or so, add apps as needed. Hexter uses almost nothing to run,
you should be able to open and use many at once, in any linux.

Maybe 'cpu scaling' is hindering your performance, it should be
off when playing or recording instruments.

Do you have the extra sysex files for Hexter?

[http://homepages.abdn.ac.uk/mth192/pages/html/dx7.html](http://homepages.abdn.ac.uk/mth192/pages/html/dx7.html)

scroll down and download deckard.zip

which holds 263 sysex banks, most with 32 sounds, so four
can be loaded in each Hexter. Some sounds are fabulous,
many are wonderful for building complex sounds.

Post your /home/user/.jackdrc textfile, maybe someone
will suggest an adjustment.

Cheers
