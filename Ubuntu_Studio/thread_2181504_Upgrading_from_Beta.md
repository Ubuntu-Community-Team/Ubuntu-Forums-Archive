### Post by mörgæs on 2013-10-18
Hi, welcome to the fora.

Just do your normal updates and you will get to the final version. There's no fundamental difference between 'beta' and 'final' version, they are just different points in time for 13.10.

### Post by cub on 2013-10-18
Running updates with Update Manager or 'sudo apt-get upgrade && sudo apt-get dist-upgrade' will put your system at "release" stage. But yes, it's worthwhile to run the update as some patches was fixed between Beta 2 and final release.
