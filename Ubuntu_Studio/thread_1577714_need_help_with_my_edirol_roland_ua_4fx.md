### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2010-09-19
According to what I can find by googling around and looking at the[ ALSA page]("http://alsa.opensrc.org/index.php/Edirol_UA-4FX"), you have two ways to use this device:
Advance mode - [requires application of a patch]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1043691"), and possibly adds background noise, but works with 96KHz 24bit 
Non advance mode - plug in and record at 44.1KHz 16bit only

If you are happy with non advance mode then I could give you the steps to get started with jack and Ardour. Have you used jack or Ardour before, or is this your first attempt?
