Hey everyone who is stuck with video editing,

Blender 2.5 is nearing release and I just thought I'd encourage people to try out the beta. I couldn't get it working on my 9.04 32-bit install but I got it up and running on Win XP. The interface is quite different but if you go to the icon in the lower left of the panels and turn on Video Sequence Editor and then select sequencer or image preview from the drop down box two places along you can recreate what we had in Blender 2.4*. It looks fantastic:

However, the interface is hard, so I think GNU/Linux could do well with a two-tier strategy where there are simple GNOME (open shot) and KDE (Kdenlive) solutions competing against imovie and windows movie maker and Blender going for the top end (much like basic photo management retouching apps vs the GIMP).
