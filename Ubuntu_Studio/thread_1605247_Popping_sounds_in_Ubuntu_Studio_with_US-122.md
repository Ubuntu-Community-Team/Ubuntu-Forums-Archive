I successfully installed drivers with the help of the post at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/TASCAM_US-122](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/TASCAM_US-122) and was able to play sound through the line out and phones outputs on the device. The problem is, I've noticed now that I'm getting crackling and popping in the sound when audio is playing. The device has never given me issues in the past when set up with either my Mac or on a windows machine. 

After searching around on the webs for a while it looks like Koala had some issues with sound akin to this, but I couldn't find anything similar for newer versions (I installed the latest version - Maverick Meerkat). Furthermore, from what I could gather, it looked like the kind of solution that seemed to fix those issues with that version are simply not applicable to 10.10 (found some post about commenting out a line in a config file, but that line wasn't even there on 10.10). 

Does anyone have any thoughts or suggestions?
