### Post by angelsguitar on 2008-07-19
First of all, you should have fluidsynth installed and at least a soundfont package available.  I would like to recommend Personal Copy, which you can get [here]("http://www.personalcopy.net/").

In MuseScore, tgo to Edit > Preferences and click on the Audio Tab. Then on the SoundFont section, look for your soundfont bank and load it. Click OK.

That should do it.
