### Post by barisurum on 2010-01-02
ESI Juli@ 24/192 (ICE1724) working here lovely with both JACK, pulseaudio and esound in 24 bit/192 khz. Let me remind you that there are problems with pulseaudio though so I use esound for consumer audio. There are no mic inputs just two 1/4 jack inputs and no headphone out but you can fix the mic with an audio buddy from m-audio.
