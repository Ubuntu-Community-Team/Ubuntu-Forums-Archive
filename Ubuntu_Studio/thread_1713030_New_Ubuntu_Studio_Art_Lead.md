Hello All,

The Ubuntu Studio team is proud to announce D ick  MacInnis as Ubuntu Studio's new Art Lead.  We are very excited to have  him as part of the team.

His previous experience maintaining Dream Studio [1], an  audio/video/graphical distribution based on Ubuntu Studio, should prove  beneficial to his new role.  Specifically this includes such things as  modifying menus and customizing themes.

Furthermore, the fact that he is a pretty creative individual shouldn't  hurt either.  You can witness his creativity as evidenced by his music  and videos at his website [2].

Please help me extend a warm welcome to him in his new position.

Scott Lavender
Ubuntu Studio Project Lead
