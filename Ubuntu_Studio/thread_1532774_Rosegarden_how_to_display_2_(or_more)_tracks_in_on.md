### Post by Pablo_F on 2010-07-17
Hi!

Select the two or more midi segments at once (shift + left button). Right click and open in notation editor.

More info: 
[http://rosegardenmusic.com/](http://rosegardenmusic.com/)

Cheers! Pablo
