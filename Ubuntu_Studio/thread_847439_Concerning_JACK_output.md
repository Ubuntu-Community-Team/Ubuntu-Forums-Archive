### Post by Stochastic on 2008-07-02
You should be able to get jack to run on your motherboard's soundcard.  Open: Applications -> Sound & Video -> Audio Production -> JACK Control; this is where you start/stop the jack audio server, and where you make connections to/from other programs and your soundcard (like a super versatile patchbay).  For more info, take a read through this help page: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToQjackCtlConnections](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToQjackCtlConnections)
