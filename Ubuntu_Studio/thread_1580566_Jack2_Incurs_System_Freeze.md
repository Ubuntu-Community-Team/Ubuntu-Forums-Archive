Peter, try the synchronous mode. I found it more stable when I used jack2. In addition, the default mode in jack2 adds a period latency. Just add the -S option to the command (server path field: "/usr/bin/jackd -S" or just "jackd -S").

Hi, OK. I don't know what happens. There seems to be something wrong with your system. If you have the time, it won't hurt reinstalling the whole system, and it is very difficult at least for me to help you through the forum at this point.

 However, if I were you I would stick to lucid by the time being.
