### Post by theacoustician on 2008-04-27
You can dump blu-ray discs to file assuming you have a blu-ray drive in your Ubuntu box

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/BluRayAndHDDVD](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/BluRayAndHDDVD)

However, since DRM is a maniditory feature of Blu-Ray, you can burn it with any FOSS.  I would assume though that if you ripped the file and pointed your PS3 to the share drive, that it would play fine, perhaps faster, than if the disc were in the PS3.
