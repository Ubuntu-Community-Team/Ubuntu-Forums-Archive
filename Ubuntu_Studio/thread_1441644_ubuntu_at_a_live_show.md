### Post by bluesscream on 2010-03-29
was not onstage with ubustu still now. but 4 weeks ago we (dr, b, g, k) had a 1 week workshop improvising and tweeking our sounds and I played all my key sounds midi over a standard laptop with 9.10 64bit ubustu into an edirol fw 101 and then into a traynor k4.
Stable, better latencies then in winxp, mostly used linuxsampler, wine/winasio with free vst stuff i.e mrtramp, a hardware peavy organ module and a dynacord lesley simulator, calf organ, yoshimi, rackarrack as effect processor.... Lot of fun. Our recording got it's limit by a windows PC with Reaper and we didn't manage to remove a 1Hz click. Wished my buddies might have trusted into ardour...;) 
just now i'm trying to get realtime loops under control.
