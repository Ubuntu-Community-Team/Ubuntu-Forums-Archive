### Post by jeebustrain on 2010-12-02
what type of controller and what type of midi interface are you using?

I remember having sticking and timing issues with my midiman 4x4 (usb interface) using an Alesis Fusion as a controller - from what I read, it had something to do w/ the midi implementation on the Fusion not liking the way the midiman handled midi timing over USB. I eventually moved the midi connections to my audio card (Delta 1010) and it's been rock solid ever since.
