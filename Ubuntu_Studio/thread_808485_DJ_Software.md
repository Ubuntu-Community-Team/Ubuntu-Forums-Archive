### Post by Stochastic on 2008-05-27
> **thorgal said:**
> there used to be something called 'sweep' IIRC. Check it out and tell us.

Nope, sweep is an audio editor, not DJ software.  See: [http://www.metadecks.org/software/sweep/](http://www.metadecks.org/software/sweep/)

Mixxx is good for mixing via your computer (I've yet to try their timecode support, though it sounds like you wouldn't be looking for this level of advancement just yet).  XWAX is the other timecoded DJ app around, but it doesn't work without the timecode.

If when you say "scribble songs" you mean scratch them back and forth, then you should take a look at terminatorX: [http://terminatorx.org/](http://terminatorx.org/) and make sure to look at the "turntables" page for some cool ways of getting real turntable action with an old optical mouse.

If when you say "scribble songs" you don't mean scratching, then you'll need to explain a bit more.
