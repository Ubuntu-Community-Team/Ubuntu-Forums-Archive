### Post by raboofje on 2009-09-22
> **elliotn said:**
> i want to record a song. i want only to record the vocal while the instruments are playing but audacity record everything that is playing in my system

Hmm, audacity should be able to do that: 
* set the capture device to mic only
* load the instrumental track into audacity, 
* plug in headphones
* hit 'record' and sing along 

As the mic should only pick up what you sing (and not what's playing though your headphones), you should end up with the instrumental track next to a voice-only recording from the mic. Then, if needed, you can remove/mute the instrumental track.
