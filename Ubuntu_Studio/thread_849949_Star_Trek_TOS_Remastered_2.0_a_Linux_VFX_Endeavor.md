Hello guys, 

I'm Sean Burns, the co-head of Star Trek TOS Remastered 2.0. To bring everyone up to speed, this project seeks to go further in remastering classic Trek than CBS has recently done. I and others have been very displeased with the video game quality of their work and believe we can do better. That being said, this is not intended to be a thread trashing CBS but rather, a thread to discuss the realm of VFX on Linux and when I post it will be in relation to my project. Other who wish to join can please do so by contacting me through email.  This is a serious remaster and aims to be more thorough and surpass what CBS has given us. 

I use the following tools:

Blender
Ardour
GIMP
Cinepaint
Houdini (through work)
need Lumiera badly!
