### Post by Subito Piano on 2010-06-03
Just a general, but enthusiastic "two thumbs up!" for all involved in this Ubuntu Studio project.

I'm familiar with several good distros and have been an avid, loyal user of another rock-solid debian-based one for years without complaint -- but as a teacher utilizing multimedia and a professional musician to boot, I decided to check some distributions geared toward music to install in my new laptop (admittedly, much more powerful than my old desktop).  I made a big partition and slapped on Ubuntu Studio without even making a separate /home partition - just to try it out.

I booted up -- first off, it was gorgeous!  "All that glitters is not gold," for sure -- but this Ubuntu Studio hooked me from the start.  Everything JUST WORKED!  OO Impress with embedded video, music, video editing, musescore...

It's been about three weeks now of straight use and as much as i appreciate the labors of love by other linux distros -- i have no plans of switching back or trying out anything else.  This seems to do everything i need and do it well.  Congrats!  [IMG]http://mepislovers.org/forums/images/smilies/snoozer_likelinux_man.gif[/IMG]
