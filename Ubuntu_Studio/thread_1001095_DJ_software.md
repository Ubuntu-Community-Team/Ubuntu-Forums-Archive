Hmm, this is actually one region that has yet to be well developed on the Linux platform.  You might want to look into these packages: freewheeling, lmms, terminatorx, or freecycle.

Your best bet may just be to run Audiomulch in Wine.  According to the wine appDB it works flawlessly: [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=13100](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=13100)
and > Audiomulch has been designed by its creator, Ross Bencina, to be fully Wine-compatible. My experience with it a couple of years ago was that it was a very well-engineered and stable piece of software regardless of which OS was hosting it.
