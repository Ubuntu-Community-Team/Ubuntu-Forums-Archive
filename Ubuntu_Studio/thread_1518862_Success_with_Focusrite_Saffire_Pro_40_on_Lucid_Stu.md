### Post by mango42 on 2010-06-27
**Yay!** :guitar:

As I seem to have got a reputation as a moanergroaner here (c'mon guys - 6 weeks in the doldrums when nothing seemed to work on anything - tedious or what?) I thought I would just share my present delight with the Studio community. Thanks to diligent and knowledgeable posters here, I ended up at:

[Dependencies/Ubuntu FFADO Trac]("http://subversion.ffado.org/wiki/Dependencies/Ubuntu") where even I could follow the very 'lucid' instructions to compile the latest FFADO & JACK code from source for 10.04

Well, now for the very first time, the Saffire Pro40 is at least *communicating* with Jack. I haven't taken it any further...yet...as I'm just enjoying watching the 'sync' light green and bright (sad git! ;-) - don't yet know why channels 5 to 8 show a steady -3dB signal with nothing connected though - that's for another day.

Thanks indeed to all the developers and equally to those kind people who have taken the trouble to translate dev-speak for us mere mortals :KS
