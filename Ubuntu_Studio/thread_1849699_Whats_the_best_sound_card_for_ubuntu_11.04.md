### Post by sgx on 2011-09-25
Hi, for what you describe, a desktop P4 2-3 ghz, and a used maudio 24/96 pci, may be the best price combo, and best linux compatibility. 

For pcie, you also might find a used or sale-priced e-mu 0404 pcie, but I can't predict it's compatible. A usb 0404 is now supported in alsa, a long thread here, a while back.

[http://www.bhphotovideo.com/c/product/706630-REG/E_MU_24_56206_0404_PCIe_PCIe.html](http://www.bhphotovideo.com/c/product/706630-REG/E_MU_24_56206_0404_PCIe_PCIe.html).

An old digital minidisc recorder might also be a simple go-between,  receiving line input from the X-fi, and sending digital output. Plus a cheap plug adaptor or two.
