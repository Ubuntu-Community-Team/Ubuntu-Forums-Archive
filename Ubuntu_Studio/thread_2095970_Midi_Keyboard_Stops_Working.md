### Post by chechenia007 on 2012-12-18
I figured this out!!! Thanks, if you tried or at least read. Actually it was a happy accident, my laptop was running out of battery and i plugged the charger to the wall electrical output, then i unplugged and plugged my midi keyboard and then it started sending midi messages again. Gee, i'm so happy, now back to my music. Hope this can help somehow someone.
