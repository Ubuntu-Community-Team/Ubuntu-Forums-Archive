XMA files are proprietary audio files from Microsoft.  They use that within their XDK (Xbox Development Kit) and as you may have guessed it's based on the already existing WMA format, it's basically their main method for getting music out of the hardware, but they have it encrypted in a way that makes it (nearly?) impossible to play them once you have extracted them off the game disc, even on Windows.
