### Post by kevmitch on 2008-07-01
I would give mencoder a try. It's command line based, so it has a bit of a learning curve, but it is the most comprehensive encoder that I know of and has pretty good documentation. You might also give the associated mplayer a try. I have yet to come up with a video format that it cannot play provided I have the binary codecs installed ([http://www.mplayerhq.hu/design7/dload.html#binary_codecs](http://www.mplayerhq.hu/design7/dload.html#binary_codecs)). Even without the binary codecs, it's pretty unbeatable. I'm surprised at how little I need them on my amd64 system.

I've never done it myself, but here's apparently how you would create a psp video. 
[http://www.mplayerhq.hu/DOCS/HTML/en/menc-feat-handheld-psp.html](http://www.mplayerhq.hu/DOCS/HTML/en/menc-feat-handheld-psp.html)

Obviously, you should try and pick apart that command line and play around with things yourself.
