### Post by trulan on 2009-10-17
+1 for Ubuntu Studio.   Ardour and Audacity will be installed by default, and Rosegarden is in the repositories.  Also 9.10 is looking very promising, you may want to check it out.

Video work would benefit from that a lot more. (My wife runs Windows on this machine.  That OS loves the extra ram.)
