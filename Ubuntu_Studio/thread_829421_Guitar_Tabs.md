### Post by jem7v on 2008-06-15
Tabopolis.com has about 25,000 tabs, most of which are pretty high quality.  However, they require that you use Power Tab Editor (a free Windows program) to open them... Fortunately, PTE works okaaay in WINE.  You just have to download some extra DLLs sometimes, I think.
[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=2784](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=2784) talks about getting it working in WINE.
