### Post by AlexanderDGreat on 2010-02-11
I found the solution as I've also been learning a bit about the technical side of video editing, conversion, what have you...

TAKE NOTE: It's not important that you get the Aspect Ratio right, what's more important is the Video's Original Resolution.

It's a good thing WINFF - keeps a lossless resolution when converting from one file to another. Aspect Ratio depends more on the player of the video.
