### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2009-04-20
The video codec will have more to do with the results than the specific application. Currently H.264 also known as AVC will provide the best results for a given file size in the lossy codec world. Don't forget to compress the audio as well, uncompressed audio can be very large.
