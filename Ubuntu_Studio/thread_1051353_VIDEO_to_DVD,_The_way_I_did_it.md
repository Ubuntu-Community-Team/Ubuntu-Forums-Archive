### Post by n.brenner on 2009-01-26
OK peoples, after 6 months of experimenting and seeing lots of wringing of hands and gnashing of teeth, lets face it---Ubuntu nor any of its offspring does not have any decent VIDEO CAPTURE except for Mythbuntu which is WAY more than I want.   So Here is My WAY OUT.
 
1. Equipment
  VC-500 Video Capture from Diamond
  DELL Demension 3000 2.8 GZ w/512 mgb mem
  GATEWAY Performance P4-1400 1.4 gz w/400 FSB
  GA7-VAX MB @ 1.2 GZ
  755 AMD-64 MB @ 2.2 GZ
 2. WINDOWS XP
    Ubuntu Ibex
    Ubuntu Jaunty
    Kdenlive0.7 video editor
    DeVeDe
    Brasero
3.  Put Windows XP into the Dell  with 2.8 GZ or anyother machine with that speed. The speed is the clue, nothing slower than that will work.
  With that put the VC-500 Video Capture ($40 anywhere).  DO NOT put anyother programs into that machine.  Do not go on the internet with it
except to upgrade---it will slow down. Capture to SVCD which makes MPEG.

  In the 32 (i386) Machines, put Ubuntu Ibex (DO NOT UPGRADE). Get Kdenlive0.7 + Dependencies at Multimedia Production-page 13 or 14-ALL VARIANTS-wadelewise, follow instructions, then do your upgrade with all the changes.  Download DeVeDe.  Brasero is default.

  On the -64 Machines Put Jaunty, Kdenlive comes with it

RESULT--Capture-Mpeg-Edit-VOB-Iso-Burn.  result with a little practice?

 Quality DVD

HAVE FUN
