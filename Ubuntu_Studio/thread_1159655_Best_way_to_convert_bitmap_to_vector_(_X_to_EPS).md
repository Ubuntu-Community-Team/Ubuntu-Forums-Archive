### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2009-05-15
Are you talking about simple diagrams with a few shapes or are you talking about images that truly take advantage of raster formats like a photograph? Simple diagrams could probably be converted by Inkscape but more complex images like photographs are best left in a raster format since the vector format would be even larger than the raster image in the first place. Cheers!
