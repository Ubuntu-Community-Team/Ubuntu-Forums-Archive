### Post by Dark Hornet on 2009-12-28
I have been using a q6600 quad-core for about 1.5 years now, and it works great!  It handles everything I throw at it with ease.  With that said, if the i7 chips had been out at the time I built my computer, I would have bought one.  The i7's are a faster chip, etc....however both will do anything and everything you need to do with ease, including x64 versions of Ubuntu.  

So, if you have the money, go with the i7 chip...but if not, know that the quad core will do you just fine -- and then some.  (by the way, I think the i7 chips are quad core as well).

Good luck!
