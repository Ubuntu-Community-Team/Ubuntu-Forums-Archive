  Ubuntustudio 16.04 will be officially released in April 2016. As this    will be a Long Term Support version, we are especially anxious to  offer   an excellent customer experience on the desktop. Therefore, the    Ubuntustudio community will host a wallpaper design contest! The  contest   is open to all Digital Artists and Amateurs, most especially    Ubuntustudio enthusiasts. We hope to collect the coolest, most    beautiful, inspiring, innovative and downright slick desktop wallpapers.    Each artist may submit up to 4 pieces. The Ubuntustudio community  will   vote on and select 16 final Ubuntustudio Wallpapers! (See what we  did   there? 16? 4? Clever, eh?) [/SIZE]
