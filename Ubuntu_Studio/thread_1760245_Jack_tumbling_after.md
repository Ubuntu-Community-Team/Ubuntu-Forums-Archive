### Post by Pablo_F on 2011-05-17
Hi!

Well, it seems that the user who is trying to start jackd does not have rtprio and memlock privileges, so you have to (1) make sure that users of the audio group gain these privileges, by running the command:

sudo dpkg-reconfigure -p high jackd2

choose yes, of course. And (2) add the user(s) to the audio group, doing a:

sudo adduser the_user_who_wants_to_use_jack audio
