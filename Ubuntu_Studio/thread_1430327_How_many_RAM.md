### Post by RaumTrug on 2010-03-17
no sweat!

just above the topmost post in a thread of the page you're currently viewing, there's a menu "Thread Tools". there go to "subscribe to this thread", and choose "instant notification by email" from the "notification type" dropbox. click "add" below, and you'll get an email as soon as someone posts to the thread you subscribed.

I think you can only have 10 threads at once up for email notifying.
