### Post by nalmeth on 2008-07-15
This confusion is common, 'jack' also happens to be the name of a command-line cd-ripper.

What you're looking for are the 'jackd' and 'qjackctl' packages. Qjackctl is the GUI for JACK, and should appear in the Sound & Video menu.
