### Post by Pablo_F on 2011-03-26
For the record, as post like this one could confuse other new users in the same way other posts have confused you.

Quoting [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuStudio](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuStudio)

> Wireless connections and network manager are left out of the OS in Ubuntu Studio. **This is intentional as it can interfere with audio processing**. Support can be added from the Install DVD or synaptic. 

If this is a good decision or not is another question but indeed wireless can interfere with audio processing.

The (very easy) solution is pointed out in the link provided in the page above. Once more:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuStudioNetworkSetup](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuStudioNetworkSetup)

Cheers! Pablo
