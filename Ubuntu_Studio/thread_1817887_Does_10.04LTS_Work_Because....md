### Post by sgx on 2011-08-04
Hi, full release system upgrades are a waste of time. A clean install, after live-dvd testing is the best option. Can't expect hundreds of
unrelated coding projects (especially video) to seemlessly meld together in some fantasy world. The effort to achieve such grail, would be better used in
solidifying each release. A lame idea doesn't walk/run better because
it's popular. Make a separate /home partition, so at least you
can keep most configurations intact during new installs, should you want to.
