### Post by Grandma_DOG on 2012-06-07
I've found an odd bug that no one else seems to have reported.

I've been using RecordMyDesktop (RMD) for about a year.  But when I used it to make a video from a slideshow with LibreOffice Impress I got a unusual result.

I made 6 videos from 6 parts of the slide show. In fullscreen mode.  In every case, RecordmyDesktop only recorded EVERY OTHER SLIDE.  Very odd. 

I'd understand if it dropped a frame here or there. But when I"m talking about a slide for 30 seconds and recording 10 fps, there is no chance of dropping all those frames.

So accross all 6 videos, RMD dropped every other slide like clockword.  It shows the last slide with the audio of the missing slide. Then the correct slide and audio, then again the old slide and current audio.

Very very odd. Just wanted to post here for others so they don't think they are going crazy.

RecordMyDesktop 0.3.8
Ubuntu 11.10
 Linux  3.0.0-20-generic-pae
