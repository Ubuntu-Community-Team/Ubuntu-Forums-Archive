### Post by TheropodWatcher on 2009-08-09
Just solved this problem for myself. I'm using a Konica-Minolta DIMAGE Scan Dual IV. Not a currently made model (I think) but not hard to find. It does an excellent job at high resolution and color depth. Check the reviews - it's very well rated. The only software I could find that will drive it under Ubuntu is commercial but excellent and only $40. It's called "VueScan". Find it at [http://www.hamrick.com/](http://www.hamrick.com/) . Works better for me than the Windows software that came with the scanner! Simple to install. (You might have to change some permissions on the directory to use it as other than root - that's about it for complications. Unarc and go otherwise.)
