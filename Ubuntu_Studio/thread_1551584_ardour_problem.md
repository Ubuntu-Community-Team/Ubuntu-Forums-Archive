### Post by Pablo_F on 2010-08-16
Hi, just an idea in case you didn't check. First of all, make sure jack is working with your m-audio. Open jack control and check that the interface is the right one. Start the jack server and then launch ardour.

By the way, if your card is envy24 based, you should use envy24control to adjust hardware levels and mixer (if needed). Check the analog volume tab.
