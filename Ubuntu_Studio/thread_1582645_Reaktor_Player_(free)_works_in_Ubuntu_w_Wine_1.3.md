### Post by PC_load_letter on 2010-09-26
I thought here would be the most useful place for people to try this. Well, [Reaktor]("http://www.native-instruments.com/#/en/products/producer/reaktor-55/")  needs no introduction, it's an amazing piece of software for any electronic musician, and it is not free. Native instruments offers a preset player for 3 of the Reaktor synths totally free, well, you'll need to sign for an account, they send you a serial number that you have to punch in to activate the software. You can install it as a VST plugin, but I haven't tried that yet.

Why bother, well, IT WORKS FLAWLESSLY with Jack + Wine 1.3 + wineasio under Karmic 64 bit, so I'm really happy right now:guitar::guitar::guitar:. And by 'it' I don't mean the VST version, it's the standalone player that installs when you run the installer exe. 

Here is the link to download:

[http://www.native-instruments.com/#/en/products/producer/reaktor-5-player/](http://www.native-instruments.com/#/en/products/producer/reaktor-5-player/)
