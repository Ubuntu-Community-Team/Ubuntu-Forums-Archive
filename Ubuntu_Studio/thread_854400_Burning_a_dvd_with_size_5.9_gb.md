### Post by unoodles on 2008-07-09
Had the same problem. There are two options:
1. Burn it to dual-layer disc. (make sure your drive has the capability)
2. Shrink the image using DVD95, XDvdshrink (not in the repository) or K9Copy (I recommend this one). Just shrink it to a 4.7 GB image and you're done!
