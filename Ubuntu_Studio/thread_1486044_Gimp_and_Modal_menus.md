### Post by cub on 2010-05-18
I believe this is a "feature" GIMP implemented some versions ago. I was very frustrated when it came into play. As far as I know there is no way to remove it, but there was a workaround to assign the middle button on your mouse to minimize windows. So now I middle-click on the tools so it slides up to just be the small menu-row. It's still on top of everything though but the best I could find.

### Post by imag1narynumber on 2010-05-20

Two, the menus can be toggled on and off.  I believe it's alt-tab or something like that.  Maybe control-tab.  Anyhow, it's there somewhere.  I'm not on my Linux box right now.
