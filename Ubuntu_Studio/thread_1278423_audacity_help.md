### Post by jolx on 2009-10-02
This method worked fine for what i needed it for however it isn't perfect and will make the sound more muffled and can create clicks and gaps in the song which can be repaired by zooming in on the part, deleting the silence (in the case of a gap), and using the "Repair" effect.

To make the pitch go down then back up again:
1) Make a selection
2) In the "Effect" menu choose "Change Pitch..." dont worry about most of the options just slide the percentage change slider down to about -2
3) Shorten the selection from both sides
4) Go to the "Effect" menu and choose to repeat change pitch, or just CTRL+R
5) Repeat steps 3 & 4

i attached some screenshots and a tarball containg an example using part of a song by The Mars Volta. the effect sounds like it slows the song down a bit as well but the last pitch change at around 50 sec is using the change speed effect which changes both pitch and speed.
