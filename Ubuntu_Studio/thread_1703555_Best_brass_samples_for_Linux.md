### Post by Breambutt on 2011-03-09
I've run Native Instruments' Kontakt Player 2.x-4.0 with WINE ever since Hardy and haven't managed to crash them even once (or even had hiccups for that matter), but, well, that's just like... my opinion, man.

I also don't have a proper MIDI keyboard since I'm more of a guitar guy and only use my basic model without breath control and frills as a composing tool, recording easy stuff and toying around, but from what I can tell even those seemed to work (if I had the controls/pedals) in Mr. Sax T and Komplete 5 & 6.

In short, DSSI-VST and WINE have always been very stable and not overly demanding with resources, but better try it out extensively before going live. There's a good chance anything you can use through Kontakt Player will be just fine. Others, only Tux knows.
