### Post by roberto.eiberle on 2008-06-15
It is difficult to understand your problem. If you have no way to get good sound with your camera, a captureboard won't improve anything. Firewire transfer is the best quality possible. If you record sound separately you should still import video with firewire, sound with your soundcard and sync it in cinelerra. Aa for capturecards - Avermedia are a good choice supported on Ubuntu, but you would have to use xdtv for capture, kino only works with firewire.
