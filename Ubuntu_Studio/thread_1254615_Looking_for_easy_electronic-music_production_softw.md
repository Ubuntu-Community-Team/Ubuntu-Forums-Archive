I'd say LMMS is one that you should try out.  It may take a tutorial or two before you're 100% comfortable with things, but the learning curve isn't as steep as others.  Jokosher could also be useful if you find lmms to be too difficult.

This is an old tutorial on making a song with Seq24, Zynaddsubfx, and Hydrogen: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToSeq24Introduction](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToSeq24Introduction)
