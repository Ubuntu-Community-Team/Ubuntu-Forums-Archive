### Post by paulmerchant on 2008-09-20
VyNil is part of Steve Harris's wonderful LADSPA plugin collection. I think it's quite good. Simply sudo apt-get swh-plugins and use VyNil with any audio applications that support LADSPA. I think VyNil is usually categorized under the LADSPA distortion plugins.
