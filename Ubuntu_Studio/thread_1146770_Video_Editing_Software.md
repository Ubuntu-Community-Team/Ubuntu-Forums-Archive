### Post by cotcot on 2009-05-03
First of all do not expect to find something similar to vegas. 
What you will find is different. If you want high level look for cinelerra or blender VSE (keyframes and ipo curves). These apps will require some time to learn them. Kdenlive is medium level and easy to learn. It is a good application to start with and to check if you need more. Kino is good, simple, rock stable and well known for capturing video from camcorders.
