Hi Shampyon,

See left lower corner here:

[http://linuxwacom.sourceforge.net/index.php/main]("http://linuxwacom.sourceforge.net/index.php/main")

And also with linuxwacom at least some Waltop's and the N-trig duosense.

This link points to a few more drivers for tablets:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/TabletSetup]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/TabletSetup")

The page has links which will list specific tablet models.

Then there is the "evtouch" driver for touchpads and the "fpit" driver for finepoint tablets.
