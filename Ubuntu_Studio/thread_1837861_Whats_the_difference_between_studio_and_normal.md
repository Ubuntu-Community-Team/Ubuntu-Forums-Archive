### Post by sgx on 2011-09-02
> **sketchme said:**
> I just wondering if this OP is better than standard. I currently run the newest ubuntu. I do alot of photo editing and wonder would studio make it run better.
I would google your editing software title with

kernel rt rendering speed realtime

also, having the best nvidia chipset/video card in your budget, and
a fully optimized nvidia driver, may be important to gain speed and
stability. For large images, using hard-disk buffer settings chosen
for large files, not myriad small files, should help.

I find that rushing to the latest update, when you have a stable
working system, is bad medicine. Install the newest on an extra drive,
or usbstick, and prove that it is going to be better for --> you.
The distro maker does not share your motives and priorities.
