### Post by markbuntu on 2009-11-06
I got a plantronics usb headset that came with a separate usb thingy which was a good thing because the plantronics headphones were crap. I use my sony headphones with it because the headset plugged into the usb thingy instead of being hardwired. It worked OOB with hardy and interpid and jaunty and karmic and debian and suse and mandriva and fedora so no worries there. It was pretty cheap too, maybe $40. The usb dongle was a c-media.

You can probably get a better quality usb soundcard and plug some good headphones into it and you would be good to go.
