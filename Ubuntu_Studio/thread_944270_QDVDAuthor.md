### Post by cotcot on 2008-10-11
I use it from time to time. I see the thumbnail error also. You get this error when you add video with the "Add / organize video" tab. You can add your video in another way. Clicking in the white field under "All" "Video" and "Audio" tabs opens your browser. Go to the folder with your clips. Double click on a clip and it is added without error.

### Post by mateuszbaranski on 2008-11-20
I use it since 0.6 version. At that time it was horribly unstable. But since 1.0 is quite nice. It does a lot of work for me. But at the end I edit the generated XML file for my self to tune the parameters - not everything works fine yet. E.g. I use movies with many sound tracks which is fairly complicated to achive.
