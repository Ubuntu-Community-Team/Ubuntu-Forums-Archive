### Post by Stochastic on 2009-01-15
I'd say this is best to do in post production.  It may be nicer to have pitch-shifting during your acting, so that you can respond to things better, but unfortunately Skype on Linux doesn't work well with Jack, and Jack-rack is my first recommendation for real-time pitch-shifting.  So record it normally, open up a wave editor (audacity for example) and pitch-shift sections of the recording.
