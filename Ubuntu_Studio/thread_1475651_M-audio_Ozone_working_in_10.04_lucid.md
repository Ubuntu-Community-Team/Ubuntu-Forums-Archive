### Post by jeroen0046 on 2010-05-07
Hi Guys, 

I'm so happy I wanted you to know:
My m-audio Ozone has been collecting dust since Dapper, and with every ubuntu release I took it out for a spin to see if it would work.

Today with 10.04 I tried again. After some googling I installed Madfuload, (sudo apt-get install madfuload), and instantly my MIDI was working. (confirmed with LMMS).

After rebooting, USB sound was also recognised and worked!

(I had to disable the internal soundcard of course, which can be done in the sound applet!)

I now have LMMS listening to midi playing a Vanguard VST analog synthesizer thru my ozone USB-Audio. 
The only issue is that I have a little bit of lag.

Anyone else noticed that we can now dust of our ozone's?
