### Post by saint-takeshi on 2008-06-28
LMMS is pretty cool, but have you had a look at the other stuff available? for drums, you can't beat hydrogen (sudo aptitude install hydrogen hydrogen-drumkits)

then export your beats and use lmms or rosegarden to arrange them and add other instruments.

also have a play around with jack-rack and creox, for funky-*** effects. and if you need a decent all-purpose synth for bass, guitar, keyboard and whoooshy noises, use the presets on zynaddsubfx.

*ubuntustudio; i use it to show up my windoze sound-production friends. what do you use yours for?* :P

keep up the good work, and let me hear if you get any tracks done, i'm always on the lookout for what other people are doing in the open-source music production world.
