### Post by barisurum on 2009-05-30
I also have a usb audio device here which works with JACK but doesn't work with pulseaudio. It was working in 8.04 but 9.04 pulse has a problem. A lot of people are having issues with usb audio interfaces with pulseaudio in 9.04. If you want to upgrade to 9.04 its better you know this. You may have a look at this confirmed [bug report]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/pulseaudio/+bug/366708") I made.
