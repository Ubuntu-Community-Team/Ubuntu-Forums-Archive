### Post by justsomedude on 2009-07-15
1. Rip your movie to your disk (rip only, don't convert it). You could use mplayer (Cli) or vobcopy (Cli). If you prefer GUI tools, K9copy (qt) or dvd::rip (gtk) will do the job.

2. Avidemux will then do what you want.

> ...convert it to AVI.

May I ask why? Just curious, avi is an outdated container format and you will take a quality hit due to lossy conversion.
