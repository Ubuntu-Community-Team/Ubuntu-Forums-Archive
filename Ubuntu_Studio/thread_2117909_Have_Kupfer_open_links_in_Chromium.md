### Post by Inoki on 2013-02-19
Hi all,

I thought I'd share my experience with you a bit and this time it's a workaround on how to make Kupfer open links in another browser than Firefox, whilst having installed multiple ones.

This of course applies to XFCE:

- you need to go to /usr/share/applications and make a backup of defaults.list
- once that is done remove all traces of firefox.desktop from it
- substitute with google-chrome.desktop

Links will now open in Chromium / Google Chrome :)
