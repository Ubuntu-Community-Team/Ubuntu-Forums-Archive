### Post by Stochastic on 2008-06-06
Freebob lists the Tascam DM-24 under their working devices, but FFADO (the new version of the freebob driver that is in beta testing) lists the Tascam DM-24 support as experimental.  This probably is a sign that there could be some issues with linux support.  I'd give it a try and see (then report back with your findings).
