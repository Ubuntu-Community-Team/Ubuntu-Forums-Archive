### Post by cotcot on 2010-03-08
Editors :  kdenlive, blender, openshot, pitivi, cinelerra, kino, ... (blender and cinelerra is more for advanced editing; kino is simple and easy, the others are somewhat in between). No recommendation. Best is to select a couple of editors on features and try them to check what _you_ like. There is 'no best' app.

Writing to DVD : devede

I use blender and devede.
