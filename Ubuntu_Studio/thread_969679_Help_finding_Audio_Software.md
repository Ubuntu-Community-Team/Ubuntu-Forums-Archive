### Post by thorgal on 2008-11-04
ardour is not an audio editor. It is much much more! it's a DAW. I would suggest ardour and anything "jack" (jack-audio-connection-kit). It is possibly tricky to set up correctly, this is quite demanding and works best with pro to semi-pro gears. But i you are serious about audio work, that's the way to go.
