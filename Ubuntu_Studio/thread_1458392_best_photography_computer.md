1. better image quality / easier to see details.
2. a more streamlined workflow due to the dual monitors.

Therev are 2 different motherboards/sockets for the intel i7 CPUs. LGA 1156- dual channel RAM (so normally 4 RAM slots, and they should be filled in pairs).
