### Post by sonicosonico on 2008-06-30
Attention all Sound designers, Recordists, Music Producers, Filmmakers, Web/game Developers, Audio Freaks!

We have just launched [www.soungle.com](www.soungle.com), an online library for finding and sharing all kind of free sound effects.

Soungle.com is a site where users can search for sounds. What sort of sounds can you search for at Soungle.com? Users can search for the sounds of melodious birds such as the thrush bird or blue jay. Or users can download sound effects such as door bells and rushing water. Soungle.com is easy to use. First users must type in a key word such as “thunder”. Soungle.com will produce a list of thunder sounds including thunder and rain. Each result will have a brief description along with the frame rate, length for the frame and bit rate. The user can them preview all of the sounds and the sound effects that the user wishes to use they can download. If you are producing music or editing a film Soungle.com can be a very useful source because you can find many different sounds and sound effects. Users can donate sounds by listing their name and web, uploading a WAV file and adding keywords. Find sounds at Soungle.com. 

Soungle.com has a clean and clear interface. The site is simple to use and has a large selection of sounds to choose from.

Visit [www.soungle.com](www.soungle.com)
