### Post by T.J. on 2015-12-11
Hi!  Welcome!

If you are trying to play Blu-ray movies, there are no really good methods on Linux because the Blu-ray Disc Association has yet to license the software player for Linux.  If the disc is not encrypted, playback may be possible, but I do not think you will have much luck. Virtually all commercial studios use AACS encryption on Blu-ray.  The 100% guaranteed legal options would be: 

1. Dual boot Windows 
2. Run Windows in visualization with pass-through on the video card and drive.

There are other methods, but they are likely illegal in your jurisdiction.  Even if they are not, discussing them on the forums can cause problems.  I'm truly sorry I cannot be the bearer of better news.
