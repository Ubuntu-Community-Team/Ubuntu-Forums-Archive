### Post by Stochastic on 2010-03-07
Lucid Lynx will contain 0.9.6-beta1 (provided nothing changes before release time).

There is also Denemo (which was just improved in the repos) and frescobaldi (which is a GUI editor for Lilypond, but is much improved in Lucid).  You may want to try either of these programs.

If you can transfer your music from MuseScore to one of these programs and back you may find a way around the bug you've found.  By the way, have you reported the bug to the developers?
