### Post by Sylos on 2012-01-11
You should be able to get it through the repos by adding the following ppa to your sources list:

deb [http://ppa.launchpad.net/philip5/extra/ubuntu](http://ppa.launchpad.net/philip5/extra/ubuntu) lucid main 

The lucid vocproc package will then be available.
