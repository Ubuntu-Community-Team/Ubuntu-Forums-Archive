Kino is a video editing prgrom and not for special effects.
LiVES is also video editing its special effects are audio and video things like fading and transitions.
KDEnlive is the same as the first two.
I did not look at The Open Movie Editor.
PiTiVi is video editing only.
Blender is a 3D modeling program which supports animation and has many plugins for things like lightsabers and stuff of the sort if you can find them. This is what I choose.
AVIDemux is video editing.
