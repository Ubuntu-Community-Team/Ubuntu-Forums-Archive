### Post by raboofje on 2009-09-17
I just got the UA25EX - unfortunately haven't had the chance to play with it yet :/, so I can't give you hands-on advice just yet.

Some things to consider:

- the UA25EX unfortunately lacks 'clip' LED's
- the Audiobox only has balanced TRS outputs, UA25EX conveniently also sports 2 unbalanced RCA (cinch) outputs. 
- the UA25EX has some extra's like a 'ground lift' switch and a builtin compressor/limiter
- the UA25EX has an 'advanced mode' switch. With 'advanced mode' switched off, it should work out-of-the-box. With it switched on, it has some more capabilities, but you might need a patch - see [http://alsa.opensrc.org/index.php/Edirol_UA-25EX](http://alsa.opensrc.org/index.php/Edirol_UA-25EX) for more information
- I didn't find any information about the presonus on the ALSA pages. Might be because it 'just works(tm)', but who knows...
- the Audiobox is cheaper
