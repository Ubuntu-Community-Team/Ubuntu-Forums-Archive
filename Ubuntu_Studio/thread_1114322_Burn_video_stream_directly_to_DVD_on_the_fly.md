### Post by goog1luv4life on 2009-04-24
I'm doing something similar to you and I have to crank out the videos quickly. The best I've come across is "Instant DVD Recorder" by Pinnacle Systems. Nero 8 Ultra does a good job if 1 client needs more than 1 movie on a single DVD. Nero allows you to capture 1,2, 3 or more videos before you close the disk (Finalize). It even creates a generic menu to select each video. Instant DVD is the Fastest for 1 video per DVD. Hope this helps.
