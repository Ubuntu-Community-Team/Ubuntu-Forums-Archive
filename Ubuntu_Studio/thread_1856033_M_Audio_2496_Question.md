### Post by sgx on 2011-10-07
Hi, I have a revision 2 (older) pci maudio 24/96,
and it is indeed a complete soundcard, for games, movies,
youtube, and high quality recording/playback. I use the
5 pin midi connectors, and audio i/o for everything.
There is a dedicated software mixer, envy24control, sometimes
part of the alsa-tools-gui package. I have yet to use the
digital i/o, but it could come in handy in several scenarios.
I don't notice any sonic quality differences between commercial and
homebrew music, and it has worked in every distro I have tried,
ice_1712 is it's kernel module.
Cheers
