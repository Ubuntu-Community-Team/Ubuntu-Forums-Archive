### Post by alegallo on 2008-07-03
I'm having good results with DVDStyler and ManDVD.

DVDStyler is simple and straightforward, but really powerful. You can also make multiple menus, and define them very precisely.

ManDVD has a more stylish look, and allows you to use a background music for your menu and also to add an introduction video before the menu.

You can find DVDStyler in the akirad repos, and ManDVD on getdeb.
