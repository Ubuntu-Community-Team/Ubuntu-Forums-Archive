### Post by bluesscream on 2010-08-21
nice board :D  As it has firewire ports I'd recommend an external firewire device [http://www.ffado.org/?q=devicesupport/list&page=1](http://www.ffado.org/?q=devicesupport/list&page=1) and a separated usb-midi connector for your controllers.  This should be a reliable and flexible solution for a home studio  as well as for additional use with a firewire armed laptop in mobile sessions.
