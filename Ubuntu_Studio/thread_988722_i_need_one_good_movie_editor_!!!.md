Linux doesn't have a good one - at least not one that is bug free and that offers a time line. I've been down this road many times for many years, and encountered all sorts of problems like crashing/freezing when opening media, rendering video and/or sound at wrong speed, razor tool not cutting where it's clicked on, crashing/freezing when outputting to a particular container/CODEC, and on and on and on. If you have the patience to learn the Blender UI, you may want to look it up.

As for a video NLE app, you might want to keep your eyes on a project called Lumiera at:

[http://lumiera.org/](http://lumiera.org/)

They seem to be wanting a address this issue. Their code does nothing right now, and they're currently doing a logo contest, but in a year's time it may actually work (they seem to be interested in building and testing first before releasing).
