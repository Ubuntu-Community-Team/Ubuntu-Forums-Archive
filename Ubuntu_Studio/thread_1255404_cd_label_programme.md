### Post by Stochastic on 2009-09-02
I'm not aware of any program that has this function.  But I do know that the developer of DiscWrapper [http://discwrapper.sourceforge.net/](http://discwrapper.sourceforge.net/) is always open to feature suggestions and ways he could improve his software.

I do notice that one feature is "Textfiles and K3b projects can be imported as a list" so if you were able to detect the contents of the cd/dvd and output that to a textfile, then it could be easily imported to the cover editor.  I know it's a two-step process, but it's better than nothing.
