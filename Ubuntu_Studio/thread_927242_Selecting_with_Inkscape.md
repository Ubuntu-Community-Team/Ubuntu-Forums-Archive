### Post by paulmerchant on 2008-09-23
In vector art, the shapes are often grouped. You need to ungroup the shapes before you can select the individual shapes. Just highlight your grouped shapes and select Object | Ungroup. You may have to do that more than once since you can group groups of shapes.
