### Post by Stochastic on 2008-07-17
You need to record the video of your visualizations (probably with something like gtkrecordmydesktop or other screen capture app), then take that video into a video editor and sync up the audio you played with it (if you merely use the audio from the screen recorder I believe you'll get too much signal degredation).  Then once you've got them synced up, export, and burn to DVD.
