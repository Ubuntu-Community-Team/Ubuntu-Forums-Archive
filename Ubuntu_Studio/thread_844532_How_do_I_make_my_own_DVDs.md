### Post by alegallo on 2008-06-30
There are lots of threads about this, and many different opinions ;)

I'd like to give you some hints too ;)

At the moment I am using: 
- Avidemux for conversion from AVI to MPEG, 
- Cinelerra (rather: learning to use it) for editing, and it's great!
- DVDstyler or ManDVD for authoring (menus and for making the real DVD structure).

I use DVDStyler if I need multiple and/or complicated menus, ManDVD if I want one single and simple menu, but more "eye candy" (with inital video/animation, music)

I used to import my footage with Kino, which I have installed but not configured yet.
