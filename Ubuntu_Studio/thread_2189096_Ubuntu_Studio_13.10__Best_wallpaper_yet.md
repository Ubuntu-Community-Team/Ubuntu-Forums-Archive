### Post by prime8-digital on 2013-11-20
Having been an avid user of Ubuntu Studio for over 5 years now I have seen the distribution go from strength to strength. I use Ubuntu Studio for creating music and the OS has matured as much as the audio applications that it so successfully aggregates.

I just wanted to take this opportunity however to heap praise on the new eye-candy. The lightdm logon wallpaper is absolutely fantastic. In the past I have even resorted to using a half-broken webkit greeter theme to get a logon look I like, but the latest theme is perfect.

So a big thank you to whoever created it and a big thank you to the maintainers, contributors and users of the distribution.
