### Post by cotcot on 2009-02-26
You will not find exact the same as windows movie maker. 
Windows movie maker is a simple video editor. If you want this you could have a look at kino. It is stable, good, simple and easy to learn. 
Kdenlive has more possibilities than kino and is still fairly easy to learn. Play with these editors a couple of hours to be able to compare it to what you have now. 

More difficult to learn is Blender and Cinelerra. If you spent your time in learning it you will not be disappointed. Both are professional and have good tutotials on line.
