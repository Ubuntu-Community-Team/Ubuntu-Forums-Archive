### Post by DrWho8 on 2013-06-12
I had to do a system restart. Then the Bluetooth speaker was recognized. Then in the Pulseaudio Sound Settings, in the playback tab, I had to click on "built in audio" and then click on the Bluetooth Speaker and it worked like a charm. Thanks for helping. BYW, I did do a search on the forums, but it came up with so many choices, I couldn't figure out what to try. I also did a Google search and there was advice overwrite the basic code and I did not want to try that.
