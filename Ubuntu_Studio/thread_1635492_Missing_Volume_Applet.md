### Post by GrantStoner on 2010-12-01
Did a little more digging and got it figured out. I was trying to add the notification area to the panel, and the volume wasn't there. Apparently in newer versions of Ubuntu the volume applet is grouped with the "Indicator Applet" instead of the "Notification Area". Hopefully this'll help someone else confused as I was. ^_^ Still don't understand why it's not there by default.
