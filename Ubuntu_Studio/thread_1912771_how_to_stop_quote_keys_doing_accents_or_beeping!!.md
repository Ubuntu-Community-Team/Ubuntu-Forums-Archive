### Post by CivilizationII on 2012-02-20
If you are using gnome, disable "bip" in terminal setting.

A more general and definitive way to get rid of the pc speaker is to add " blacklist pcspkr " in blacklist.conf (under /etc).

Another way could be to use a more modern distro, Ubuntu 8.04 in 2012 looks rather conservative, may i add a little advise for 10.04, or maybe 12.04 waiting some months ?
