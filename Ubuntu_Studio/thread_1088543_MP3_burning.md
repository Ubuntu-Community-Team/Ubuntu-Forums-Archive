### Post by mcduck on 2009-03-06
> **machosos said:**
> I burn audio CD's with it too.  But I am looking to burn MP3 CD's.

"MP3 CD" is just a regular data CD with MP3 files in it. Any CD burning program can burn those. Just burn the disk as data CD

(Audio CD would be standard music CD, following the Compact Disk Digital Audio standard, playable on normal CD players. Some burning programs list MP3 CD as a some special kind of Audio CD which is incorrect and in the end often creates more confusion than anything else..)
