### Post by Stochastic on 2008-12-12
Here's a couple I was able to dig up.  I've never used any (other than PD - but that was for audio rather than visuals), so I can't offer much more info than what's on their webpages.

FLxER: [http://www.flxer.net/](http://www.flxer.net/) (runs in flash)
Gephex: [http://www.gephex.org/](http://www.gephex.org/)
Pure Data: [http://puredata.info/](http://puredata.info/) (also take a look at: [http://artefacte.org/pd/](http://artefacte.org/pd/) )
EffecTV: [http://effectv.sourceforge.net/](http://effectv.sourceforge.net/)
FreeJ: [http://freej.dyne.org/](http://freej.dyne.org/)
LiVES: [http://lives.sourceforge.net/](http://lives.sourceforge.net/)
DelVJ: [http://www.delcorp.org/delvj/index.php/Portada](http://www.delcorp.org/delvj/index.php/Portada) (it's in spanish but google does translate it into understandable though broken english)
MøB: [http://mob.bek.no](http://mob.bek.no)
VeeJay: [http://www.veejayhq.net/](http://www.veejayhq.net/)
BitchVJ: [http://www.piksel.no/pwiki/BitchVj](http://www.piksel.no/pwiki/BitchVj)


Hopefully that gets you started.
