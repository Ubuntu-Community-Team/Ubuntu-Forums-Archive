### Post by Stochastic on 2009-02-20
Lilypond can do that.  It's a text-based layout language, so there's a bit of a learning curve, but you can always rely on one of the GUI frontends such as Denemo (though I can't say whether any of the frontends have drum notation capabilities built yet).

Edit: this thread also has some further suggestions: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=921766](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=921766)
