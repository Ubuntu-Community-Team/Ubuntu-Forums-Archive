### Post by Atlantix on 2009-04-24
I am an absolute beginner with Linux, but a professional video editor with Windows. All the editing programs I know achieve this by changing the speed of the clip. You right click on the clip and choose "speed", enter the desired speed: 100% is normal playback, 200% is double speed and so on, -100% is normal speed but played backwards.
 
It should be the exact same or something very similar under Linux as well.
