### Post by Kilz on 2009-11-23
I have a semi solution for anyone running an acer 5536 laptop, dynex webcam, or anyone else with freezing problems.
If your webcam also has a microphone built in, make sure to set the input to the webcam in the sound controls before starting cheese. After I do that cheese records just fine.
