### Post by chewearn on 2008-07-04
From what I can find (google), the Archos 605 doesn't support soft subtitle, aka .srt, .sub, etc.  E.g. here:
[http://www.anythingbutipod.com/forum/showthread.php?t=26447](http://www.anythingbutipod.com/forum/showthread.php?t=26447)

You would probably need to embed the subtitle into the video itself, i.e. hardsub.
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=300249](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=300249)
