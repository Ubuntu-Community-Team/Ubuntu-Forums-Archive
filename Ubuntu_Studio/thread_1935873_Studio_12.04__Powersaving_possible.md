### Post by kayosiii on 2012-03-10
If you are doing anything that requires low latencies (that is you want to listen to what you are recording as you are recording it) then you don't want to have power management enabled.

Unless Ubuntu studio has specifically disabled power management things like CPU throttling should already working.

I can see value in having powermanagement on most of the time but switching it off when you are recording music.
