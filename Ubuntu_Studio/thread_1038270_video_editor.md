### Post by cotcot on 2009-01-14
Do you want a performant one ? Then check Blender VSE and Cinelerra. Take time to learn them. You will be surprised with the possibilities. The more you work with these, the more you will appreciate.  

Not too hard to learn and pretty much features : kdenlive.

Do you want only limited functions ? Then look at Kino, Easy, stable and good.

Others : Avidemux, Lives, Open Movie, Pitivi, Jahshaka.

Google to the websites, look at the features. Select on your needs and try out.

You will find posts telling you that video editing in linux sucks. Please experience a couple of editors before judging. At least I am happy with linux editors (Blender VSE).
