### Post by Stochastic on 2008-09-18
My controller of choice is two turntables, two timecoded vinyls, and an analog mixer, as they can also be used to play Micheal Jackson rekkids on - hah! :)  I like this setup primarily for the realism that's afforded with real vinyl.

In all seriousness though I've heard that the RMX is only supported by the newest version of Mixxx so you should be aware that you might need to compile by hand for the time being (unless of course you have a 32-bit install, then the .deb you can find here: [http://www.mixxx.org/download.php](http://www.mixxx.org/download.php) should work)

But that's all I can say about it as really, I don't use those digital things, just the timecoded vinyls.
