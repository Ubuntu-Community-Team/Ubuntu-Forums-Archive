> **Lauxa said:**

Jep - this is called a 'USB MIDI Interface'. 

I have the 'M-Audio 2x2 Anniversary Edition' which works fine out-of-the-box. Some others, such as the regular 'M-Audio 2x2', require some firmware to be loaded before they work - the procedure is well-known, but it's a bit more of a hassle to set up.
