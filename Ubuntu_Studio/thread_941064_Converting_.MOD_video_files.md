### Post by mstjohn1974 on 2009-11-05
I'm having a Panasonic as well and I am using the mod2avi script from [http://mod2avi.sourceforge.net/](http://mod2avi.sourceforge.net/) it works very well after a little tweak you have to do...it converts mod (mpeg2/ac3) files to avi (xvid/mp3) files.

just follow the steps and then use nano and change:

xvid to libxvid

mp3 to libmp3lame

save it and use it...works great
