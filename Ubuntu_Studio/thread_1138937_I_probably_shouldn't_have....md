### Post by dawiba on 2009-04-26
...but I wanted to post this up as it's my first effort at recording with Ubuntu and I didn't see a suitable thread open already.

I had upgraded to Jaunty on Friday, got the apps and hardware to work yesterday and recorded this today.

It's rough and ready, but it's only just over 40 seconds long, so it won't take much of your time if you have a listen :).

I used;

Mackie XD-2 (originally part of their 'Spike' package)
Dell Latitude D 505
Jack (of course :)),
Ardour for all the audio and effects,
Qtractor to record and play back midi events that were used to trigger sounds in my keyboard,
Audacity to make it a bit louder and to export as mp3 and ogg.

Ardour and Jack both seemed to work perfectly but Qtractor disappeared a couple of times, although I didn't seem to lose any data. Audacity caused no problems either.
