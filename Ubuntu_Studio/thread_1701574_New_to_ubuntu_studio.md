### Post by sgx on 2011-03-07
Hi, devices with envy24 sound chips are good. A lot of mAudio and Terratech cards use those. A pci card is great if you have a desktop.
A motherboard soundchip usually will do for testing linux without a purchase.

I have an mAudio 24/96 pci card, trouble free for many years, sound quality better than any speakers/headphones I'd ever purchase :)

Configuration gui details:

[http://www.64studio.com/quickstart_jack](http://www.64studio.com/quickstart_jack)
