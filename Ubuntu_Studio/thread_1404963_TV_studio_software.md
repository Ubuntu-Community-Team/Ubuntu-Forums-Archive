### Post by redxine on 2010-02-16
I'm working on a solution for my Church. I'll share detailed information once it's running but basically it involves:

- Dual head setup; one for control panel and one for output.
- Flumotion streaming server, and icecast video
- DV and capture card inputs
- Compiz fusion for compositing. (Basically taking advantage of
         desktop effects to handle fading between inputs and video.)


It's a little hacky using compiz but it works really well. Essentially if you can see it on your screen, you can mix it live. You might also want to look at cinelerra. It has live video capability.
