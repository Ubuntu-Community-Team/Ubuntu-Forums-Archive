### Post by JackRock on 2010-02-25
I found that this is often the problem of a corrupted profile.  In your Home directory, type CTRL+H to show hidden folders.  You may have one or more that look like .gimp-2.6 or .gimp2.7.  Delete any that start with ".gimp", and try opening the program.
