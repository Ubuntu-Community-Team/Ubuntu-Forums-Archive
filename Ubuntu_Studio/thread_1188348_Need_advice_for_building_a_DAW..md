### Post by kayosiii on 2009-06-17
AFAIK digidesign is pretty vertical solution so that the only software that works with their hardware is Protools and Protools only works with their hardware or MAudio (light version of protools only). Protools can be hosted on Mac or Windows but not linux.

There is a range of good hardware available that works well on Linux. But you are almost certainly going to be host based rather than using outboard dsp. Luckily your workstation looks plenty fast enough.

Some M-AUDIO gear is supported, also Hammerfall PCI cards work great and Focusrite is very proactive with their firewire devices. I have presonus gear here and it works well.

For more advice I reccomend joining the linux-audio-users mailing list.
