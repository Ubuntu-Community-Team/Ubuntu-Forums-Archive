### Post by su:bhatta on 2013-09-24
Hi escreeps2, 

Puppy Studio is not officially supported any more and is unmaintained([http://puppylinux.org/wikka/PuppyStudio](http://puppylinux.org/wikka/PuppyStudio) & [http://www.linuxmusicians.com/viewtopic.php?f=4&t=9996](http://www.linuxmusicians.com/viewtopic.php?f=4&t=9996)). 

My suggestion is to install Lubuntu and then follow this guide : [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Ubuntu%20Studio%20Upgrade%20from%20Ubuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Ubuntu%20Studio%20Upgrade%20from%20Ubuntu)
That way you are getting a much lighter system. Also, you can add the ubuntustudio-audio & ubuntustudio-audio-plugins first and see how your hardware responds.

Then you can try to add the publishing, image editing & video editing softwares one by one. See at what point the system goes to overload and get a stable system with trial & error.
