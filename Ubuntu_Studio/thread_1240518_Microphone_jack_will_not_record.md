### Post by Bigwurm386 on 2010-01-13
I was having the same problem when trying to record with my mixer to my mic jack. 
what worked for me was going to the sound prefrences and changing the hardware configuration to "internal audio 1 input analog stereo input". then going to input and selecting microphone 2. that should be your jack.

for some reason in analog duplex it wont let me use my jack tho
so i do have to switch back and forth between input and output, to record then listen.

well i hope this helps out good luck
