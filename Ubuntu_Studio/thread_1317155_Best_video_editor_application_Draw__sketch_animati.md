### Post by rylleman on 2009-11-06
Of all the applications you list there's only one animation tool, Blender.The rest is editors and such, not at all suited for your needs.
There are at least two good animation tools that are better for this kind of job, Synfig Studio and Pencil. The first vector animation and the second frame-by-frame drawn animation. It all depends on what you want to do.
