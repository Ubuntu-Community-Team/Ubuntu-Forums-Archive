### Post by ffg1977 on 2009-10-19
I have a T61 and it seams to work very well with Jaunty 64bits. I use it for internet, text edition and VirtualBox to run other OS stuff (basically communications and database). I don't dual boot.
Wi-fi, bluetooth, ethernet, audio and video are doing fine.
Still can't get the Ricoh SD/MMC reader driver yet, but it is not a priority for me.
I think the core temperature is a little higher than when it runned Windows XP, but up to 70°C is not to worry about. It seams that the fan never operates at full speed.
Regards.
