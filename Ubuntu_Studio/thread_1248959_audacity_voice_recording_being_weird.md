### Post by bry_melvin on 2009-08-25
It sounds like something is throwing your latency off. You can find what or you can measure what the latency is  and then compensate for it in the settings of audacity.
I can't remeber the exact setting. but you mark one track and then select up to the other and you will have a number at the bottom of audacity showing you the time difference. Do this in a couple of spots and then set an average.
