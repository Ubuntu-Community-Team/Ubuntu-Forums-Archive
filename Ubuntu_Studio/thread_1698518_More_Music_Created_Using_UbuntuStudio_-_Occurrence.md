### Post by Precipitous on 2011-03-02
In case anyone is interested in seeing (hearing) what users are doing with UbuntuStudio...

The second full length release from Occurrences In Rain is now available at [URL="http://soundcloud.com/occurrences-in-rain/sets/memories-suspended-failing/"]http://soundcloud.com/occurrences-in-rain/sets/memories-suspended-failing/
[/URL] 
Occurrences In Rain is a dark ambient music collaboration begun in 2010  between Polish artist Bruno Janiszewski and myself, American artist Precipitous,  formerly of the band The [Insight Of Blindness]("http://www.last.fm/music/The+Insight+Of+Blindness"). We originally found each other on the music service [Last.fm]("http://last.fm/"),  as fans of each others music, and quickly decided to work together via  the Internet. As of this writing, we have never met  in person.

UbuntuStudio was used to record and manipulate most of the tracks, using a combination of Qtractor and Audacity. Most synth effects were from Rakarrack and more LADSPA plug-ins than I could possibly list....

Enjoy!
