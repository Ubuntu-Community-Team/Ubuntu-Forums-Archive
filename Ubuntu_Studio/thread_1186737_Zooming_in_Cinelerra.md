### Post by cotcot on 2009-06-14
Zooming in cinelerra is done with the [[COLOR="Red"]camera and projector[/COLOR]]("http://cinelerra.org/docs/cinelerra_cv_manual_en.html#SEC130") tool. Click on the red text for a link to the appropriate section of the manual. It needs some attention to understand; the tool is more performant than just a zoom button.

### Post by kayosiii on 2009-06-15
This all depends on what you mean by zoom 

If you want to zoom in and out of the timeline. There is a dropdown list, also you can use mousewheel + CTRL or Shift...

If you want the composition window to take up more space there is a dropdown menu on it.

If you want to actually zoom into - that is have the zoom apparent in your final product... then you want to activate the camera or projector tool and use shift + left mouse drag to zoom in or out. You can enable keyframes for this too and edit those via the timeline.
