### Post by judge jankum on 2009-11-11
For some reason Audacity 1.3.7 on my Ubuntu 9.04 would only record about a half a second....I also got latency errors from time to time...I downloaded this from audacity site, an earlier build of 1.37 but it *says*
Stable 1.2 .. I removed Synaptic version of audacity 1.3.7 and installed audacity_1.3.5-2+lenny1_amd64.deb
and so far it works great....
 My next problem was VERY low record/input level. Even plugged into the mic with the 20db boost..I changed to line in from a 300 watt Peavey head. The pre-amp/tape out was too weak and no way to turn it up.... I switched to a Fender system and ran line-in from a moniter channel with a volume control and "presto" everything works great so far and clear as a bell....
 I've installed 9.04 on 2 other systems and not a single problem with any audio or recording. So it must be this old computer built from spare parts. But this goes to show Ubuntu can be made to work on almost anything...
