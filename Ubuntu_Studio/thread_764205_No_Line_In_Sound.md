### Post by oskar2000 on 2008-04-23
Open the volume control (double-click the speaker cymbol in the gnome-panel)
Choose: Preferences

Add "line select" - or something similar to that.
After that you should have the button in the main window.

At least that's the way it works with most sound cards.
