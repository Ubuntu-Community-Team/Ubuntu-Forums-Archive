### Post by vadania on 2007-08-08
I have Ubuntu 7.04 Feisty Fawn and Nokia 6610i conected through USB data cable CA-42.
When I open xnokii, it tries to connect automatically to my phone. Program window becomes white and unresponsive (no buttons). Xgnokii does something but it takes much too long to do so - even if it's showing an error.

However, wammu v0.17 works perfectly with reading phone's agenda, last calls, SMS and files!
Unfortunately Calendar and Todo list do not work at all:
[I]Error while communicating with phone
This function is not implemented for your phone. If you want help with implementation please contact authors.
[/I]

Both xnokii and wammu are installed from repository.

Maybe Wammu is what you need, maybe not but I suggest you try it!
