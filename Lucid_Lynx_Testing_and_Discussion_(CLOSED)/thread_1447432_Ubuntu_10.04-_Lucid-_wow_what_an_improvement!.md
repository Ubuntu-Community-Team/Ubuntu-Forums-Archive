### Post by saffagirl on 2010-04-05
Wow, guys, after struggling with Hardy for a couple of years I thought I'd bite the bullet and upgrade to Lucid before it's release due to lingering bugs that have never been sorted it out with my machine build).

Anyway, thought I'd share that even with the fresh clean look that Lucid brings, the main things just work:)

I'm on dell vostro 1310, which includes Broadcom 4328 bgn wifi card, realtek sound card, integrated 1.3mp webcam, dual booting with Win 7 Pro x64.

Just to share, the following were never completely sorted in hardy and now just work in Lucid;
Wifi (not dropping and driver just working)
NVidia graphics- correct resolution just works straight off the bat
Sound card- this actually works- and is now no longer quiet
Firefox/ flash- problem .no longer an issue
Integrated webcam - the drivers actually worked, before would never pick it up.

Still to be determined/ not sure if a massive problem
CD \ DVD- dvd playback was an issue with the last few kernels in Hardy
Skype 2.0 not recognising sound


Anyway just thought I'd share- here's to the LTS in a few weeks time..looks fab thusfar, am stoked!. (apart from Skype not @ all tempted to boot into Win 7).:guitar::KS

Cheers,
