### Post by peachcolor on 2010-04-16
got the solution.

launch gconf-editor and make 'UTF-8' the first in '/apps/gedit-2/preferences/encodings/auto_detected'.

but this is painful for me when open files in GB2312 generated by windows. will it be fixed in the final version?
