Issue:
- After installing Lucid b1, b2 or rc under VMware workstation 6.5 or 7.0, VirtualBox or Parallels the GDM greeter screen responds to mouse movement and clicks, but does not accept keyboard input (so you cannot login).

Workaround:
- Click the Accessiblity icon and enable On-Screen Keyboard which will immediately crash and dissapear;
- Reboot (several reboots may be required to get the OSK to actually stay on screen);
- Log in;
- Access System -> Preferences -> Keyboard -> Layouts;
- Delete the existing keyboard entry or entries;
- Add an entry with your keyboard type, and ensure that keyboard model is set (I used Generic 105-key (Intl) PC);
- Click "Apply System-Wide";
- Reboot to confirm this has worked.
