### Post by ndefontenay on 2010-03-31
Guys,

i've just found a solution, it's pretty easy.

[https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gnome-panel/+question/5273](https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gnome-panel/+question/5273)

Right click>Add Applet>Notification Area.

There's 2 applets:
1) Notificatoin Status. It will display your laptop battery, your email/chat status, sound and transmission if you set it so.
2) notification area is for wireless and skype if you got the latter installed.

It seems notification area got removed for some reasons after an update.
