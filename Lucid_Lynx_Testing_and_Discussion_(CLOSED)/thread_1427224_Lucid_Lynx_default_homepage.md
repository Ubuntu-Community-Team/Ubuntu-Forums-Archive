### Post by samh785 on 2010-03-11
Yesterday I installed Lucid Lynx on my laptop. Everything worked fine except for a few little kinks that will surely be ironed out (after all it's still and alpha). I opened Firefox and saw that the default search engine was Yahoo. All of this I expected, but I found it interesting that when I changed the upper search bar to use Google, the home page reflected the change and showed the Google icon and utilized it's search engine instead of Yahoo. I never read anything about this before I saw it for myself, and I just thought it was interesting in case someone else hadn't heard about this either.
