### Post by 23meg on 2010-02-25
In the case of crash reports filed through ubuntu-bug, the Apport retracer will automatically mark your crash report as a duplicate if it is one, and thanks to clue files, Apport can even prevent you from filing a duplicate altogether. So with crashes, you usually don't have to worry.

With non-crash bugs, I don't think there can be a better way than a human reading and comparing descriptions.
