### Post by mpt on 2010-03-22
That’s not correct. The reason Mark asked for that gap is so that a confirmation bubble (*e.g.* showing a change of volume) always appears in the top right corner, regardless of whether a notification bubble is on the screen at the same time. It had nothing to do with window buttons.
