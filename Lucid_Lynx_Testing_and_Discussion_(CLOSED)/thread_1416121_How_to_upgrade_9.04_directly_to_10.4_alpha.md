### Post by dcstar on 2010-02-25
> **Lockheed said:**
> The topic is self-explanatory. How do I go about it without having to go through 9.10 in the process?

There is no such thing as an "upgrade" to Alpha software.

Alpha software by its very definition is development software which will be full of unresolved issues and in no way can be considered an "upgrade" of anything - especially a production system.

As is made clear in the Ubuntu pre-release download pages, you are on your own using any pre-release version and are restricted to posting any issues only in that development forum, and you are not to post any issues in the rest of the support forums which are **only** for released versions.
