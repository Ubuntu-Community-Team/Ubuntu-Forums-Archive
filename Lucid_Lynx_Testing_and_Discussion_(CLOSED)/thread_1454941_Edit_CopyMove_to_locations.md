### Post by Didius Falco on 2010-04-15
What you need is to unlock the power of Nautilus Scripts!! Here are two links that will give you all the info you need:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/NautilusScriptsHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/NautilusScriptsHowto)

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Nautilus_Scripts](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Nautilus_Scripts)

The first link has some external links at the bottom of the page to a couple of sources for scripts you may find useful.

The second has the **copy** and **move to** scripts you are after right now.

Ubuntu Tweaks has a nice gui to manage scripts. You can add the Ubuntu Tweaks repo to your system (so you can install and remove, and get updates to it just like other packages) from here:

[https://launchpad.net/~tualatrix/+archive/ppa]("https://launchpad.net/%7Etualatrix/+archive/ppa")

I hope this helps...
