### Post by amano on 2010-04-29
Neither your chosen facebook name nor your e-mail address will work!

You have to use the account name that facebook is setting up for you automatically. Take that from the URL which Firefox shows in the address bar:

Steps:

1) Log into your facebook accont (using e-mail and password)
2) Klick onto your profile (thus you can see the "Wall", "Info", "Photos" tabs).
3) Have a look at the browser URL
4) If the URL is [http://www.facebook.com/home.php?#!/ABC.xyz](http://www.facebook.com/home.php?#!/ABC.xyz), then ABC.xyz is your username for the facebook chat. Don't forget the "." seperator.
