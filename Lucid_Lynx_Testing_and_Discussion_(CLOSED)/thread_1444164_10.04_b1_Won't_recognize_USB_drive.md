### Post by recluce on 2010-04-28
While I doubt that anybody will ever find or see this after the unfortunate (at the least) merging of the threads, I still wanted to post the eventual solution to my problem.

Symptoms: USB works fine, but all printers remain greyed out ("unavailable"). Starting Virtualbox as root makes printers available.

Solution: go to System > Administration > Users and Groups

Klick on "Manage Groups"

Find the group "lp" (line printers) and add all users that should be able to print from Virtualbox to this group. 

Reboot. 

Your printers should now be available in VB.
