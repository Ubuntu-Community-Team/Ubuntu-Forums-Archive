### Post by Keyper7 on 2010-03-01
> **grubdude said:**
> Since no one is responding on this, how do I submit a report?

Evolution is integrated with Apport, just go to the "Help" menu and "Submit a report" (or something like that, I don't have it English right now).

If it locks before you can do that, then type

```
ubuntu-bug evolution
```

in the terminal and proceed from there. Note that in both cases you need a Launchpad account.
