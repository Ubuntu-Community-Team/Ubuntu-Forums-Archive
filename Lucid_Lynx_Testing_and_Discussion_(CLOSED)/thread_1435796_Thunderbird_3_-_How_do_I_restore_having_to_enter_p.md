### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-03-21
Hi,
Open thunderbird, go to Edit > Preferences, Security, click on the passwords tab, click on the saved passwords button, then remove the appropriate password. 
Hope this helps.

Edit, when you restart thunderbird and it asks for your password, don't check the box that says:
"use password manager to remember this password"
