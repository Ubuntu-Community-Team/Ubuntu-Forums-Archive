### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-03-24
I imagine that screen is the result of a graphics card needing an nvidia driver to run properly.  You can try  to get a desktop by hitting F4 (I think but can't remember, have a look at the first menu offering boot options) and choose low graphics mode.  That may get you a low res desktop, but you should then be able to search out a driver from *System ->Administration ->Hardware Drivers*.
