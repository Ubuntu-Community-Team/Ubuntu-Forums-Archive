### Post by Keyper7 on 2010-03-10
The [OpenShot video editor](http://www.openshotvideo.com/2010/03/version-11-released.html) reached version 1.1 with a lot of nice features added since 1.0. I'd say that 1.0 -> 1.1 was a much greater step than 0.9 -> 1.0.

The best news related to this is that this new version is now officially in Lucid, as confirmed by [lucid-changes](https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/lucid-changes/2010-March/thread.html).

The only thing I'm sad about is that the MLT library version 0.5.0 unfortunately wasn't released in time for Lucid. But that's hardly a showstopper. If everything is okay with the packaging (didn't test it myself yet), I highly recommend OpenShot for those who want simple and easy video editing in Linux.
