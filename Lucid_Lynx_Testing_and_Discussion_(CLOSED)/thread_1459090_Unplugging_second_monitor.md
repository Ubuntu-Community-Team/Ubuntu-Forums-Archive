### Post by Captain Smiley Pants on 2010-04-21
Well, what I've been doing is I have the display settings up in my taskbar. You can do this by going through System -> Preferences -> Monitors. When you are there, under "Panel icons", tick the option that puts the display settings in the task bar. When I've unplugged my monitor, I just go to the Monitor Preferences and Ubuntu recognizes that the display is gone. This also applies when I plug one in, I just go to the preferences and the monitor is recognized. Hope that helps?
