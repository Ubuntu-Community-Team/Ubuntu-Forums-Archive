### Post by The Cog on 2010-04-22
Has anyone else seen this?

Occasionally, my laptop boots and there is no visible user chooser / login dialog box. I have discovered that I can make it appear by pressing Ctrl-Alt-F1 then Ctrl-Alt-F7. It doesn't appear in the middle of the screen, but jammed against the right hand edge of the screen.

Possibly related, a couple of times earlier (beta 1?) I had a window open off-screen to the right. I could see it briefly as it shot across the screen when I switched desktops. I could retrieve it by right-clicking the Move item from the window switcher menu and pressing <-.

Also possibly related, my desktop switcher occasionally starts up with its window buttons the wrong shape, as though my laptop is wide-screen. It jumps back to the right shape as soon as I select System->Preferences->Monitors.

My laptop is a Dell Latitude E5500, and the video card is:
00:02.0 VGA compatible controller: Intel Corporation Mobile 4 Series Chipset Integrated Graphics Controller (rev 07)

Weird.

P.S.
I got the wrong-sized desktop switcher again this morning when I booted. I took a screenshot, and to my surprise it showed another non-existent monitor on the right. Reduced screenshot attached. Again, selecting menu System->Preferences->Monitors corrects the error as the monitors dialog opens. I don't actually have to click anything in the dialog.
