### Post by Jay_Bee on 2010-02-26
Yeah I had similar questions, the key is to follow the same logic given in those two words, for example (you can see) fish (in an) aquarium
so you can see "monkey" in a "zoo"

Kind of a tricky example there but I had a similar question like that:
"Given the pair of words below, which one has the closes relationship to 'stone'"
Words given were stick/float
so if "stick" "floats" the "stone" sinks so "sink" was the answer.
