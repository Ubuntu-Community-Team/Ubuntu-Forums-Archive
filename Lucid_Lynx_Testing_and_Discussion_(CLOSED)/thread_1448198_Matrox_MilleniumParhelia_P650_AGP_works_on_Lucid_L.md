Good news to those of you Matrox graphics card users who have been unable to use Ubuntu for a couple of years, because Ubuntu stopped being compatible with Matrox Parhelia cards some two years ago - good news is that Lucid Lynx works with Matrox Parhelia cards out of the box!

This would be excellent news if the official Matrox Parhelia driver would work also and it might, but at least you can now use Ubuntu even though the driver is not the fastest one.
