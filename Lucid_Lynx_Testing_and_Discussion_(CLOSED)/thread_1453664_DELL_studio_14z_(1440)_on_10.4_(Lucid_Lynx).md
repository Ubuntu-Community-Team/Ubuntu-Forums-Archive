### Post by openB on 2010-04-13
I gave a try to the beta 1 (64 bit) and everything is pretty much okay. At least compared to my other experiences with ubuntu.

I've read that other users had issues with the dell wireless 1515 card. I have the same one and it works out of the box.


**Light Issues:**

The function keys are messed up. To use them you have to press "ALT + Fxx"  instead of "fn + Fxx".  It should be very easy to fix, but I'm not that much into linux.

After installing nvidia drivers, the boot screen resolution is too low. The strange is that before this, in the first boot, the boot screen's resolution was perfect. No big deal anyways.



I hope this is useful for someone. I'll give beta2 a try if I find myself some free time.
