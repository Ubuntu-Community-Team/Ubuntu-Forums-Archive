### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-02-12
Those icons are in the "Notification area", not separate icons.  The simplest way to do what you want is to go to the tiny vertical bar to the left of the Notification area in the bottom panel, where you don't want it, right click, unlock if needed, then right click again and choose Remove from panel.

If you have two Notification areas, one in each panel, only one will show the icons, for some reason, and I suspect that at some time you have chosen to add it to both panels.
