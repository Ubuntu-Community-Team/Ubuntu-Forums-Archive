### Post by 23meg on 2010-04-06
That's how the indicator applet works. Add it back to your panel (right click empty space on the panel, click "Add to Panel", choose "Indicator Applet".

The bug report on this possibly misleading behavior is [bug #519553]("https://bugs.edge.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/indicator-applet/+bug/519553").
