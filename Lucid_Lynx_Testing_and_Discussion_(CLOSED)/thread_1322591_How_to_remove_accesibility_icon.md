Hi, for someone reason I enable the accesibility icon when I test the magnifier on logon menu.

---

### Post by Regenweald on 2009-11-11
system>>preferences>>assistive technologies>>keyboard accessibility>> uncheck 'accessibility features can be toggled with keyboard shortcuts'
