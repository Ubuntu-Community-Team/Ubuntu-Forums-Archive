### Post by Roamer145 on 2010-04-05
I had it working on my old iPod Video. I did have an issue with it at first. I don't know if this will work for you, but I got it working with gtkpod ([http://www.getdeb.net/app.php?name=gtkpod](http://www.getdeb.net/app.php?name=gtkpod) or [http://www.gtkpod.org](http://www.gtkpod.org)).

I'm not positive this will work for you, as I had to reflash my iPod's firmware due to a separate issue (meaning I thought it'd be a bright idea to format the thing. As I found out, don't. It's a pain to fix.) But I'm fairly sure it worked before the manually  reflashed firmware was put back on it. It's worth a shot, at least. ;)

### Post by totoff on 2010-04-09
i got it working. it was easy: rhythmbox > edit menu > plugins > activate the ipod plugin (note that the ipod was recognised even without the plugin but rhythmbox seems to need the plugin to update the ipod database).

hope this works for you too!
