### Post by cariboo on 2010-04-28
I had that error on a motherboard that is no longer with us, not because of the temp problem, but because of a bad bios. After seeing the critical temp warning, I noticed that the usb ports weren't being detected, and it wouldn't detect any SATA drives.

A bios update solved the problem for a couple of months, but eventually peripherals started going MIA again, until one day it stopped booting all together.
