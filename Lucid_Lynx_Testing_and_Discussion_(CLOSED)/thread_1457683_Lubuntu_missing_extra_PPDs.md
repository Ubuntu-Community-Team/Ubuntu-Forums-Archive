Wanted to post this in case someone is looking for a solution.

I have a Samsung ML-1740 laser printer.  My Lucid Ubuntu test machine has the CUPS PPD for this printer but my Lucid Lubuntu machine didn't.  I installed both openprinting-ppds and foomatic-filters packages and the needed PPD was added.  I think of the two packages, the foomatic-filters did it (but probably b/c of one of the dependent packages that came along for the ride).

I hope this helps someone.
