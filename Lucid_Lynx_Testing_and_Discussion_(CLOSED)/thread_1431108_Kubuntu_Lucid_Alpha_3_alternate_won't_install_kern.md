### Post by go2dell on 2010-03-18
The first Beta is due out today, March 18.  I suggest waiting for that to arrive and giving it a try before filing a bug report.  Your problem may have already been fixed.

The release announcement will be on the [ubuntu-devel-announce list]("https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-devel-announce").

FWIW, on encountering similar problems with previous alphas, I've had success installing a command-line only system, then installing the Kubuntu-desktop package from the Net.  When in alpha stages, the packages are such a quick-moving target that things are sometimes fixed before you can even download and burn an installation CD.
