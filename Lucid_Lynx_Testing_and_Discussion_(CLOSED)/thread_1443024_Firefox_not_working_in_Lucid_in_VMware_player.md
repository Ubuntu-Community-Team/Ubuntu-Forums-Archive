In case you are here looking for a solution to a similar problem, after  several tries with VMware Player and wubi, I gave up and completely  cleaned out all that stuff.

On the advice of a kind and helpful forum member, I downloaded and  installed VirtualBox (Sun/Oracle) and it ran without a problem. It was  still a bit cumbersome and time-consuming, but there were no errors and  it guided me through the process of updating and gathering guest  additions without mishap.

Most importantly, Firefox ran properly on the first try.
