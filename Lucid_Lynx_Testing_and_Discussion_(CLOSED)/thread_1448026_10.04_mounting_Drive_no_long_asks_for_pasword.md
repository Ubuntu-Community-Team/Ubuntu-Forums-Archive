### Post by mc4man on 2010-04-06
> how do I revert this?

If you are fully updated then ATM a couple of ways. there are 3 admin auth overrides in 1 file, either edit, or move to a .bak (edit is probably better

```
gksu gedit /var/lib/polkit-1/localauthority/10-vendor.d/com.ubuntu.desktop.pkla
```

change the ResultActive=yes to
 
ResultActive=auth_admin_keep

Or I'd guess you could just go 
```
sudo mv /var/lib/polkit-1/localauthority/10-vendor.d/com.ubuntu.desktop.pkla /var/lib/polkit-1/localauthority/10-vendor.d/com.ubuntu.desktop.pkla.bak
```
