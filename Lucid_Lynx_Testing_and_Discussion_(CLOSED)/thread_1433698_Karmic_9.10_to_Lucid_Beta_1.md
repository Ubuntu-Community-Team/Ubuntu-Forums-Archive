### Post by spyderpride on 2010-03-19
This is something you should easily be able to search out and find out how to do on your own... but I will tell you anyway. 

Hit Alt+F2, type 'update-manager -d' (without the quotes), then you will be on your way to upgrading your Karmic to Lucid Beta 1.  

I recommend running normal updates on your current install before doing this.  Your "restricted software" will probably still be functional after the upgrade, but I'm not entirely sure what you mean by "restricted software" so I could be wrong.

EDIT: I just noticed this is probably not in the right forum. Check out 'Development and Programming --> Lucid Lynx Testing and Discussion' for more info on upgrading to a development snapshot.
