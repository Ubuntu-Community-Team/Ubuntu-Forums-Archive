### Post by 23meg on 2010-04-22
There's no such thing as "the update from Beta 2 to RC". Both Beta 2 and RC are stabilized milestones that reflect the state of the package archive at their release time. So unless you kept your Beta 2 installation stale by not updating, you're not updating from Beta 2 to RC; you're just getting whatever relevant updates last became available on the archive.

There's no way to tell how large that will be, since it depends on your architecture, when you last updated, and what packages you have installed.
