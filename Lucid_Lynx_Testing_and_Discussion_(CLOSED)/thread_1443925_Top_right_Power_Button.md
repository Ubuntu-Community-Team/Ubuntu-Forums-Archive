### Post by Arand on 2010-03-31
As far as I know, the red colour indicates restart required, as in the case of updated kernels and similarly. I'm not sure if there is a distinction between security updates requiring restart and normal.

You might notice that the "restart" entry in the menu changes to "restart required" when it is red.

It's purpose is very diffuse indeed and I hope it improves before final, maybe they could bring back the update notification icon, with informative tooltips, hmm?
