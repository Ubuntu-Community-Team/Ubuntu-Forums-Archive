### Post by joey-elijah on 2010-01-26
Mark Shuttleworth himself has said there will be no new theme for Lucid LTS. He did say that they are working on a new metacity - which may be related to the RGBA in GTK+ and clientside support work going on.

Exact quote from the 'ask mark' q&a on Lucid: -

> New window decorations, some new icons for the panel indicators, some new styling on boot and login, no new gtk theme.

if you dip into the 'RGBA' thread knocking around you can read more about that - i'm not sure what the status or likelihood of it being 'stable' enough in time for Lucid. I hope  it is though!

The "proposed" themes on the artwork wiki are usually proposals for inclusion in Ubuntu and/or the community themes package but rarely to be *the* default - after all that's what the Ubuntu Design team get paid for :p
