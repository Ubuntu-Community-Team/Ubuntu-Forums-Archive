### Post by dmizer on 2010-04-08
Click on "Open with Other application". Then select Gedit from the list displayed and make sure that the "always use this application" option is checked.

---

### Post by IanW on 2010-04-08
Or, click "Properties", go to the "Open With" tab, select "Notepad" and click "Remove".
