### Post by garvinrick4 on 2010-03-09
Unless you know your way around a Live CD for one and use 10.04 as a secondary OS for two then you would not enjoy testing the Alpha's. You just do not know what will happen on a day to day basis. But if you must and can not resist make a Live USB they are quicker. To use as a Live CD.
Read all you can on the file system. Mounting and unmounting drives and getting the internet to work while in chroot is helpful. Google, Google and Google some more when get in a bind. Read forums to learn, not just to fix your problems. Take notes on everything and transfer them to another OS so you do not lose them.  Enjoy, expect problems and report them correctly. If you are quite new to Linux this could be difficult at times, most times.
