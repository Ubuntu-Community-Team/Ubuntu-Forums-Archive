By default, you can't do that in Firefox. But the good news is... there's [NewTabURL add-on]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2221")!
Once you install it, go to Tools > Add-ons.
Select NewTabURL and click Preferences then type [http://www.google.com](http://www.google.com) in the URL field, Save.

Hope that helps
