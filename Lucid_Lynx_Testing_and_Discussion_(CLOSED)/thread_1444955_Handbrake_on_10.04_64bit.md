### Post by ranch hand on 2010-04-06
Please keep in mind when fooling with a pre-release OS that there is no hurry for the devs of non standard apps to keep up with every update in the OS.

It is much easier for them to wait until the OS is near release so that all main libs and so forth are installed or at least available for them to work with.

This is yet another reason why a pre-release OS is not the optimal thing to be using for a production box.
