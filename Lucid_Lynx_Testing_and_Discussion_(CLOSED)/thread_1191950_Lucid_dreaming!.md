On a similar note, I found a way to increase the chances of having a dream and remembering it. After 4-5 hours of sleep, wake up and stay half-awake for several minutes (go to toilet, drink a cup of water)(in my case it's my parents pestering me on the phone to wake up for classes). Then I go to sleep for more ~4 hours. The second time I go to sleep the dreams come easy and I remember them.
