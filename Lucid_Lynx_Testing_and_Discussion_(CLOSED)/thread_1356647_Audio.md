### Post by cariboo on 2009-12-16
Make sure you have the volume turned up in the application you are using to view the media, I had the opposite happen last week where flash videos played correctly with sound, but had no sound using Listen or Rhythmbox. Right-clicking the sound icon and clicking preferences allowed me to change the volume level for both programs.
