### Post by Anduu on 2010-03-27
I have a Fuze and it does work...sometimes.

Usually I have to reconnect it repeatedly before it will mount.Keep an eye on the charge indicator.If the yellow lightning bolt comes on and stays on it will mount.If not disconnect/reconnect.Repeat as necessary.

Also go to Setting>System Settings>USB Mode on the Fuze and be sure it is set to MSC.

One more thing...If it absolutely refuses to mount try restarting Ubuntu.That sometimes helps.
