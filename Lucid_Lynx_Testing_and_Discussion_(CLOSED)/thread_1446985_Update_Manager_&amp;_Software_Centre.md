### Post by cariboo on 2010-04-04
There is nothing wrong, that is normal during the testing phase. Depending on how you have update manager setup, you may not get notification that there are updates. I personally have update manager to notify me daily, but I use synaptic twice daily to update, so update manager doesn't have a chance to pop up.

Files being left behind is also normal, as there may be dependencies that aren't available yet. This is still the first beta, so things are still changing, and probably will right up to the final release. Fortunately from now on most of the updates will be bug fixes.
