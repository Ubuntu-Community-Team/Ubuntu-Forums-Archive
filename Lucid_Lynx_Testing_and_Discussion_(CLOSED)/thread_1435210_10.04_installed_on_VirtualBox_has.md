After much playing around, I pulled the xorg I had in a vm of 9.10 and added the section discussed here, and it all works for me now.  Resize, mouse seemless across windows, etc.  Here's what I wound up with:

Section "Device"
        Identifier      "VirtualBox Video Card"
        Driver          "vboxvideo"
EndSection

        Identifier  "VBoxMouse"
        Driver      "vboxmouse"
        Option      "CorePointer"
EndSection

This in addition to replacing the vboxvideo_drm.c and run the /etc/init.d/vboxadd setup command as well.
