### Post by 23meg on 2010-02-20
This is what Mark says in the video:

> We'll have some new styling which is going to be the starting point of another five year view. We've been Human for the last five years, and now we're going to be light oriented.

Interpreting "some new styling" being "the starting point of another five year view" as an entire new GTK / Metacity theme redesign might be a bit of a stretch; it can be interpreted as only the starting pieces of a new five-year outlook landing in Lucid as well, and the work items on the relevant blueprints for Lucid don't exactly help in defining the progress either. 

Based on past experiences of unrealistic expectations being set due to ambiguity or distorted facts, and creating lots of disillusionment later on, I'm closing this thread until further official and more detailed information confirming "the news" is available, or part of the work that will land in Lucid is revealed in public branches on Launchpad, or is uploaded to the archive.
