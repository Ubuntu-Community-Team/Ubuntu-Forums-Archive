### Post by Arand on 2010-04-24
Previously this feature was disabled since allowing rhythmbox to block shutdown/suspend when battery gets low wasn't the best of ideas.
ref. [https://edge.launchpad.net/bug/78038](https://edge.launchpad.net/bug/78038)

And until there is a more fine-grained control of exactly why the computer wants to shutdown/suspend, I do hope it will remain the default.

It is always possible to re-enable the "power manager" plugin in rhythmbox.
