### Post by Paddy Landau on 2010-04-08
Wubi is excellent for testing, but not for full-scale use.

I would go ahead and create a dual boot. Don't get rid of Windows until you've been a year or so without using it.

Do a full backup first! (I recommend [CloneZilla]("http://clonezilla.org/") to back up your entire drive.) Then uninstall Wubi and install Ubuntu. I would wait until May, though, so that you can install 10.04.
