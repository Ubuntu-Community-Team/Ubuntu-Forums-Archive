### Post by john@ukgillies.com on 2010-04-13
Here is some good news for users of the hp ProBook 5310m.

I have just installed the beta Lucid Lynx Kubuntu on to my new laptop.

The installation went flawlessly!!!!

The machine booted straight into 64 bit Lucid first time. 

Highlights....

* Wireless networking worked out of the box with the only input from me being the WEP key
* Plasma effects were available immediately
* All apps from the CD worked first time
* Installing new fonts and then using these was perfect
* Power options and shutdown options worked immediately
* Samba and NFS are superb giving easy access to Windoze machines and Linux machines on my network.
* Sound, video, flash and java all work with no help from me

The look and feel of Kubuntu on the laptop is superb!!!

What a GREAT job you guys in the Ludid Team have done - WELL DONE.
