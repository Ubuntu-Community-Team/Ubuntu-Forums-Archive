### Post by victor9098 on 2010-03-10
Short answer is no.

As long as you keep updating Lucid you will probably already be using the final version before the release date. Just keep up-to-date.

Some people prefer to do a fresh install anyway, but that is completely up to you as long as your up-to-date version us working as it should.

Hope that helps.
