I'm running Lucid 64bit on a Radeon HD 2600XT with the free drivers. Ever since I enabled Compiz fusion when I play a DVD in VLC (as well as Xine and SMplayer) DVD playback loses a few frames every 5 seconds or so making video playback stuttering. ### Post by moviemaniac on 2010-01-21
Jeez. Two seconds after submitting this thread I thought about something: I've got AWN installed and one of the applets "shiny switcher" always shows a preview window of what's going on on my displays. So I switched previewing off for this plugin and playback has stopped stuttering. Hooray :D
