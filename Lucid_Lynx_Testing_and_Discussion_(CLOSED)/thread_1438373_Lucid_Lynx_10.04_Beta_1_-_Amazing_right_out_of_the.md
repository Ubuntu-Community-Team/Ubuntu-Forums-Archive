### Post by simpleblue on 2010-03-24
I have just bought an HP Pavillion Laptop. Amazing experience right off the install!:popcorn:
 
My WIFI, soundcard, and graphics card worked. No extra packages to install. The boot time reduced from 37 seconds to 27 or less. YouTube videos load amazingly fast in Firefox, from 4 seconds in 9.10 to only 1.7 seconds in 10.04!
 
I like the overall look better too. The black menu looks so much more professional. Also, the buttons on the left side of the menu give it a unique feel. And the ability to add muliple program windows to one window is genious.
 
So far I've only had 2 crashes, which are very small avoidable issues and is to be expected in a beta 1. For what I get I am extremely pleased. It shows that Ubuntu has really been concentrating on the quality of their product. 
 
I just wanted the Linux team (Ubuntu, Debian...) to know that you have a very happy (I was going to say customer, lol) person here. Please know that not everyone is going to sign in to thank you, but they do enjoy Linux. For me, Linux is freedom and I can use my computer knowing that every keystroke was made by people with a positive passion.
 
I'm looking forward to the 10.10 full release... and the 10.40 :P
 
If you think perhaps that my article is a little too positive. It is, and it is much deserved.
