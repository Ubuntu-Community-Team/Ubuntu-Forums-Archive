### Post by blueturtl on 2010-03-12
I've already read several rants pointing out how terrible Lucid is going to be but dang. After trying Alpha3 today I have to say I like it! It's a move a way from the warmth of the human theme, and yes there are some obvious influences from Mac OS X, but to me it still stands out as something different and interesting. Not even the "misplaced" window buttons bother me as much as I thought they might.

The boot time is just phenomenal. I never get to see the boot splash!

Looks to be a really good release if they can iron out the worst bugs before release (we know the ones that stay, tend to linger).
