> **jppr said:**
> never do partial upgrades, never!!

That's not good advice.

If you're using Update Manager as the only tool for updating packages, that will lead to packages that are *meant to be* replaced being held back indefinitely, and will lead to an installation where you have old packages lingering around until the end of the cycle, potentially causing unintended behavior.
