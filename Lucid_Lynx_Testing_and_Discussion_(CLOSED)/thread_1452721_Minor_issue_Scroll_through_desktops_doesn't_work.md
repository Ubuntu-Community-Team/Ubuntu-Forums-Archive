### Post by mc4man on 2010-04-12
Open up compizconfig settings mananger and click on the "Viewpoint Switcher'
Under 'Desktop-based Viewpoint Switching 

Move Next - click on the 'disabled' tab, enable and pick button 5
Move Prev - same but then pick button 4

(or switch 4 and 5 as you like

Obviously you need Desktop cube and Rotate cube enabled
