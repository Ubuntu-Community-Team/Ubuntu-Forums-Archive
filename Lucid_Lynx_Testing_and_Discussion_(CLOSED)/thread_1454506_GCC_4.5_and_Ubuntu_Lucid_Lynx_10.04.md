### Post by Didius Falco on 2010-04-14
As of right now, the package listed for 10.04 is GCC 4.4.3. Down the road, the maintainers might move to 4.5, but only after it's been tested and approved.

Having followed your link, the new version was released today, and hasn't even made it to all the mirrors yet, so I wouldn't count on it being included in the Ubuntu repos for quite a while - probably not until the 10.10 release, at the earliest.

If it has some feature you just have to have now, you should download it from the source.
