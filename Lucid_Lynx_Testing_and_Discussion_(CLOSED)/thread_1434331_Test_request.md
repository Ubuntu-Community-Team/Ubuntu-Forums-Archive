### Post by Gadgetech on 2010-03-29
So far, so good on the U100.
[LIST]
[*]External Monitor: haven't tested
[*]Wireless Internet: Works
[*]Bluetooth: Works (successfully paired to my cell phone)
[*]Sleep (Suspend to RAM): Works, it's darn fast too.
[*]Hibernate (Suspend to Disk): Works, but it's slower than a cold boot.
[*]Screen Flicker: Gone, I had problems in Karmic with my current BIOS, but no problem in Lucid.
[*]Sound: Works
[*]Webcam: Works
[*]USB: Works
[*]Fn+F3 (turn off touch pad): Works
[/LIST]

The only issue I've had is with the power manager. I've seen similar reports around the forums. When you unplug the power cord, the system warns that the battery is critically low and the system will hibernate soon. I've got the 6 cell battery and the warning shows that I only have 4 minutes left when I unplug. Clicking on the battery icon shows that I have over 4 hours of battery left. Clicking the battery icon to view the correct status seems to prevent the system from hibernating.

I don't have overclocking enabled in the BIOS, so I haven't tested the Fn+F11 "Turbo Mode".

Everything seems to be working well except for the small glitch with the power manager.
