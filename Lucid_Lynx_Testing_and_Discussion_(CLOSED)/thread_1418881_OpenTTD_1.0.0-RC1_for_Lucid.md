### Post by nickylee on 2010-03-01
I've packaged up OpenTTD 1.0.0 release candidate 1 on my personal PPA for Lucid Alpha 3.  As a bonus I also packaged up the OpenGFX (graphics), OpenSFX (sound), and OpenMSX (music) releases which allow the game to be run without the files from the original Transport Tycoon Deluxe.  The sound and graphics get picked up and activated on first run but you'll have to go into the in-game options menu to switch on the music.  Also be advised that the music may or may not work depending on how midi is running on your system.  It wasn't working for me until an update for ALSA this morning seemed to fix it.

You'll find the packages at [my PPA]("https://launchpad.net/~sirnickylee/+archive/nickylee").  Enjoy! :D
