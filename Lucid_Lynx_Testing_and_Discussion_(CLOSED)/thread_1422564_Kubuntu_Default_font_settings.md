### Post by kernco on 2010-03-05
Starting with Ubuntu 9.10, I noticed that the fonts in the Ubuntu flavor looked so much better than in Kubuntu.  I thought they had switched to using a different font, but after looking into it found both use Deja Vu.  I finally tracked the difference to the font settings, and after changing them in Kubuntu my fonts now look amazing.  I'm wondering why these aren't the default settings for Kubuntu.  These are the new font settings:
Use Antialiasing
Sub-pixel rendering: RGB
Hinting: Slight
