### Post by Tanvir on 2010-04-10
Hi, I have upgraded to Lucid beta 2. I like it a lot. However, one feature that is getting in the way is the password prompt. Every time I am not using the laptop for 2/3 minutes, the screen gets blacked out, and when I try to resume, I have to enter my password. I have a complex password which is a real pain to enter every time I stroll out for a glass of water. 

So my question: Is there any way to disable the password prompt so that I don't have to enter my password after 2/3 minutes of idle time? 

I'd appreciate answers.

---

### Post by drs305 on 2010-04-10
System, Preferences, Screensaver.

Untick "Lock screen when screensave is active." in the lower panel.

---

### Post by jocko on 2010-04-10
Open up gnome-screensaver-preferences (System-->Settings-->Screensaver) and untick the checkbox next to where it says something like "Lock screen when screensaver is active".
