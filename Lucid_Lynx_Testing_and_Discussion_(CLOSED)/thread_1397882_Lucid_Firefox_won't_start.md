### Post by Andrew Olney on 2010-02-03
I had some problems with firefox not starting/crashing instantly after upgrading to Lucid Alpha2 (firefox 3.6).

This is how I solved it.

First I deleted the .mozilla/firefox folder in my home directory. This may be all you need.

I also deleted files in /usr/lib/mozilla/plugins/

Then I reinstalled firefox. Works fine now.

Obviously this will delete your settings and whatever plugins you had installed (at least some of them)
