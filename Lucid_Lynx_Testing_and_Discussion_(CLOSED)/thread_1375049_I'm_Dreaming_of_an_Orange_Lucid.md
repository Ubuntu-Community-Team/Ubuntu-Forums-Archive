### Post by chessnerd on 2010-01-07
Praying more like it.

I don't like the default dark brown theme from Karmic. Personally, I've always really liked the Hardy orange. I think that looked really cool and professional. In my opinion, the brown from Karmic is just ugly and, sadly, I've seen Lucid screenshots that show it in use.

I am hoping that the developers decide to use orange as the default color for the next LTS, because otherwise there will be three years of people who try Ubuntu and see... brown. 

You may say, "Come on, it's just a theme, you can change it if you want." But the next three years are really important for Ubuntu. It has recently acquired 40-50% of the Linux desktop market and has become both the flagship Linux distro and the introduction-to-Linux distro. So, the default theme of it's major LTS release matters because everyone in the world of Linux and introduced to the world of Linux is going to see it.

Anyone else hoping that they'll boot into Lucid and find it nice and orangy?
