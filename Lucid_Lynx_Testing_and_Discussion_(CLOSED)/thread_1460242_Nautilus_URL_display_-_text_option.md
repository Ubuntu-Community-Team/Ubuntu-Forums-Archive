### Post by Infil on 2010-04-22
To get the field as you would with the little button in 9.10 and earlier, press Ctrl-L. 

The problem with this is that:

1. Pressing Ctrl-L again doesn't go back again
2. If you change directories it *does* go back and you have to press Ctrl-L again.
