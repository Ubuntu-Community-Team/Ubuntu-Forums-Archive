### Post by lemming465 on 2010-03-28
Bear in mind that /sbin/init is upstart, which is event-based.  You can get a noisier boot by removing "quiet" and "splash" from your kernel boot arguments, but a lot of stuff is still only going to show up in /var/log/daemon.log.

With grub2, the "quiet splash" default comes from /etc/default/grub, so edit that, not /boot/grub/grub.cfg.
