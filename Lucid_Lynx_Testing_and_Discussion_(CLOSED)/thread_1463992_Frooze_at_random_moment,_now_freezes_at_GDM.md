### Post by Michaelg14 on 2010-04-27
I don't know what is causing your freezes but I had a similar problem with my system.  Since 9.04 I have had random freezes requiring a hard reset to get going again.  I finally isolated it to the network manager and my Netgear PCI wifi card.  It worked most of the time and could go all day without a problem but sooner or later it would cause a freeze up.  I replaced it with a no-name wifi card for $19.95 and it has worked for three days now with no problems.
