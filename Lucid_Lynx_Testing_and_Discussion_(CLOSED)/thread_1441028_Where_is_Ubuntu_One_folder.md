### Post by Unicast on 2010-04-19
Mine was under Applications-->Internet

The icon in the panel only appears while it's syncing. I had to go back into Applications / Internet to open up ubuntu one, change the preference for when the icon appears (default is while syncing), click close and then log out and log back in again. The cloud icon should appear. Right click on it to open your ubuntu one folder.
