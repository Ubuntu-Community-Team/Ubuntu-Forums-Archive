### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2010-03-06
Today I installed Lucid on my netbook (previously all my experiences with it were through a VM). All very exciting; finally 3D accelerated stuff worked!

First of all, though this is totally unrelated, I order everyone to check out the games. Especially Quadrapassel, but also Nibbles and the Lights one... just play with all of them. And enable sound. Quadrapassel has gone from being kind of mundane and ugly to being the best darn Tetris clone I've seen shipped with an operating system. The Clutter stuff and the new sounds make a tremendous difference.



Okay, so I also noticed there's moblin-session now. which seems to be very unobtrusive compared to before. How exciting!
I installed that, logged out, logged in *with a fresh account* to the Moblin session (via GDM's session chooser). It sits there with only the default background. The Moblin MyZone thingy never appears. I press the Start key and Moblin's panel appears from the top in all its Cluttery glory. *However*, the panel only has the Zones button (to choose between running apps, of which there are none) and a Network button at the top right. If I click Network, a panel appears with the text "Loading panel", but nothing ever happens.

Could just be some temporary growing pains. Has anybody been using this? Have I offended it somehow?


Oh, and yes, moblin-session brought all the other moblin- packages with it, including moblin-panel-myzone.
