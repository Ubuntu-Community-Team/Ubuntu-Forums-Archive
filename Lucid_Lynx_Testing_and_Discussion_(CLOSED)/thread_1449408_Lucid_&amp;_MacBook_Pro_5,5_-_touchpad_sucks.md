### Post by Niels den Otter on 2010-04-08
You can change some mouse / touchpad settings (incl. 2 finger scroll)  with System-Preferences-Mouse settings (right tab).

For more detailed settings, please have a look at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1441300](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1441300)

You can use the xinput command to change settings.
