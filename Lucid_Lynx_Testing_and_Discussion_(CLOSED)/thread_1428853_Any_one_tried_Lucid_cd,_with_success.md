### Post by ratcheer on 2010-03-13
I have very similar problems. Here is how I bypass them.

1) Hold the shift key as grub is starting.
2) Select the entry for Lucid in Recovery Mode
3) In the recovery mode menu, select "drop to root prompt"
4) At the root prompt, type "service gdm start"

This brings up the Lucid desktop in its full glory. It is a pain in the *** to have to do all this, but it works.
