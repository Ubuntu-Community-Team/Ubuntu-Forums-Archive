### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-03-07
Hi,
I don't know if the Karmic Human and Human Clearlooks themes will still be there when Lucid is officially released, but I just wanted to let you know mine are still here.

Also, you can easily save the theme files now and use them in Lucid final if they do get removed. They are located in /usr/share/themes/*** You might want to save the icon set Humanity (especially from Karmic) if you liked the Karmic icon look. the icons are located in /usr/share/icons/***

When it comes to install those themes and icon sets when lucid final is release, you might want to rename them so they don't overwrite or interfere with Lucid's themes.

### Post by psyke83 on 2010-03-07
> **6205 said:**
> Humanity is better...

In a sense, the Human and Humanity GTK themes are identical - the only difference is that Humanity is more up-to-date (and yet to be merged into Human). Karmic's Human GTK shipped with less-than-ideal widget outline highlighting; the fix wasn't accepted in time for release.

Humanity (in the visual-refresh-lucid branch) currently contains this fix, as well as various small tweaks to take advantage of the new Murrine engine options. However, the changes are negligible (it's necessary to ensure it doesn't diverge in look too drastically, as I have no say over the Canonical art team's decision making processes).

See the attached screenshots to see the differences.
