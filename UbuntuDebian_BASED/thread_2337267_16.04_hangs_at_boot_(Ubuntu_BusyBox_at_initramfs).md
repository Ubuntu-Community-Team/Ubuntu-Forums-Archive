### Post by cameralibre on 2016-09-18
I had posted this on the KDE forum as well - it seems that this problem had affected a few others and it appears to be that an apt-get autoremove removed lvm2, which obviously is disastrous for anyone with full disk encryption.

I ended up just installing another distro but others in the thread gave and linked to more info: [https://forum.kde.org/viewtopic.php?f=309&t=136107](https://forum.kde.org/viewtopic.php?f=309&t=136107)
