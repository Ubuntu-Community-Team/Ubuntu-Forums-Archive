### Post by papakanush2 on 2023-03-09
The compressed air canisters for cleaning electronics are cheap and easy to find at office supply stores, amazon, or even walmart. I just blew it through the vents without even taking the case off, and you could some dust come out. It made a big difference on my 10 year old dell latitude, and this was right after I bought it. It was a "refurbished" laptop that I bought on-line from Best Buy. It started getting so hot it would shut down. Never had that problem since. Apparently, refurbishment didn't include cleaning the dust out.

Might be worth a try.
