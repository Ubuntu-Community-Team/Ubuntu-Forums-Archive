### Post by grahammechanical on 2022-05-04
You fail to give information about your hardware and the versions that you upgraded from and to. If you upgraded from UbuntuDDE 20.04 to UbuntuDDE 22.04 then I am not surprised that you had problems. The UbuntuDDE web site does not offer a 22.04 version of UbuntuDDE.

The official Ubuntu developers will not be offering an upgrade path from Ubuntu 20.04 to 22.04 for another six months.

There are official UbuntuDDE support channels. They might be the best place for you to go.

[https://ubuntudde.com/support/](https://ubuntudde.com/support/)

P.S. The problem is nothing to do with Grub. The problem is happening sometime after the Linux kernel has loaded. At some point a display manager should load and then a login screen. You are getting the UbuntuDDE splash screen but not a login screen.
