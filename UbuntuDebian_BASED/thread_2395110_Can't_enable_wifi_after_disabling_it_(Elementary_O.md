after the Installation of the OS, my WiFi connection works perfectly, but when I disable it under "Networks", it's not possible to enable it again. So the blue slider turns on, but nothing happens afterwards. I restarted my Computer but nothing changed.

What can I do? I also tried to reset the network-manager but nothing changed.

---

### Post by paul.petersen on 2018-06-26
I've found the solution by myself:

Go into Terminal and type *nmcli radio wifi on *and hit enterNow it works again
