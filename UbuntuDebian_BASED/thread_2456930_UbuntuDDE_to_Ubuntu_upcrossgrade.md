### Post by QIII on 2021-01-21
UbuntuDDE is an independent distro BASED on Ubuntu.  It is not an official flavor of Ubuntu.  You can't "cross-grade".  You would have to install Ubuntu fresh, but you'd also likely have to do some good backups of important data.
