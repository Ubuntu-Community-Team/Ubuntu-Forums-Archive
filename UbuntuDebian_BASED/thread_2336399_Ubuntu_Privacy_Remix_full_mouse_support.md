### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2016-09-07
EDIT:   UPR is based on the dated Ubuntu 12.04 - - not sure if all security updates applied as this not an official derivative of Ubuntu.    Plus the older kernel is just not going to have all the hardware compatibility updates (re mouse support,etc., etc.)

A full-install of the latest Ubuntu (16.04) on a usb v3 SSD (SanDisk Extreme 500), utilizing full disk encryption is the optimal route for a transportable OS that leaves your Host PC alone.
