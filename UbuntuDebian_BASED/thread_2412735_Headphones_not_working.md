### Post by ajgreeny on 2019-02-16
Elementary-OS probably uses pulseaudio, just like Ubuntu, in which case you will need to open the pulseaudio-volume-control -> **Output Devices** tab and make sure the headphone gain (volume) is up to the level you need; at present they are probably muted.
