### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-09-28
Assuming you can use PPA repos in Voyager try using the ppa at [https://launchpad.net/~mc3man/+archive/ubuntu/trusty-media](https://launchpad.net/~mc3man/+archive/ubuntu/trusty-media) instead of the ppa that you have tried.

It will have VLC-2.2.1 which works fine on my Xubuntu 14.04 which is slightly lower a version than the 2.2.4 that is available from the ppa that you have tried.  I have no idea whether or not it will work for Voyager but it is easy enough to try after disabling your current ppa andf also removing your current VLC version.
