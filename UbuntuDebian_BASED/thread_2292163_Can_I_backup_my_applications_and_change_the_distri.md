### Post by nerdtron on 2015-08-25
Depends on the new distribution you are going to install. You can't simply copy/paste the installed application from one OS to another. 
Most likely you'll need to re-install your applications again. What you can backup is the data you saved on those applications. If you know where your data is saved, you can compress those files, backup and restore them again on your new distribution.
