### Post by TheFu on 2022-08-14
> **straitsfan said:**
> Was just wondering if there are any good antivirus programs out there for elementary OS/Linux lUbuntu,if it's at all necessary.

There's a sticky thread at the top of the Security sub-forum here with a longer answer to this, which still applies.

I've only used AV on Linux when a contract mandated it.  I felt it was dumb, but when an insurance policy mandates running current, with current signature, AV and would refuse to pay out if any of our systems doesn't have it, then not having AV running on any computer would be stupid.  When we didn't renew that insurance, the BoD voted that for our non-desktop systems, it was foolish, so I got approved to remove it and the rootkit search tools as well.  They never found anything but false positives all the years we ran them.  We did insist on AV running on BYOD MS-Windows systems, since those are the primary target and our IT department didn't manage them. All the Unix/Linux/BSD systems were maintained by the IT team.
