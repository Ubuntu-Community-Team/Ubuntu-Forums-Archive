### Post by ajgreeny on 2020-09-01
I suspect that firefox is simply too resource hungry for  your hardware so if you want to stick with LinuxLite you might want to look into another browser.

Have a look at dillo, epiphany-browser, midori or if you can manage with a text only browser, elinks.

You might also have better luck with a completely different OS, as the published hardware requirements for LinuxLite put yours right on the edge. So have a look at some others such as puppy or one of its derivatives, most of which run best from a live USB, all completely in RAM, even when you have only 800M.
