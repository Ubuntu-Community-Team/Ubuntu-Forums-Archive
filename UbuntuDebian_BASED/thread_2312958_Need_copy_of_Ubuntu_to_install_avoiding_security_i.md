### Post by sandyd on 2016-02-08
Hi,

What you will need to do is verify the checksum of the ISO that you have downloaded.

The checksum of the ISO can be found at [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/releases/](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/releases/), and choosing your release (14.04?)

Click on "release", and open the MD5SUMS file.

For example: [http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/releases/14.04/release/MD5SUMS](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/lubuntu/releases/14.04/release/MD5SUMS)

Once you find the correct md5sum for the iso that you have downloaded, you can then check the md5sum of the ISO that you have downloaded with instructions at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM#MD5SUM_on_Windows](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM#MD5SUM_on_Windows)

If you download via torrent, the iso is automatically verified.
