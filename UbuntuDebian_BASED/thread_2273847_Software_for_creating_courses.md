### Post by bapoumba on 2015-04-16
I use scenarichain, you can include exercisers (not as thorough as Storyline) and multimedia files. I generate scorm packages to upload to moodle and html files for presentation when I need them. The basic themes are just OK though. I spent a whole summer 2 years ago fiddling with theme packages. It can be done, I did it, but I found it complicated as not in my skill box (I went mostly by trials and errors, did what I wanted to do but wont do it again).

[http://scenari-platform.org/projects/scenari/en/pres/co/](http://scenari-platform.org/projects/scenari/en/pres/co/)
