Not sure if this will help anyone, but just leaving the blue/green screen running for 30-40 minutes caused the machine to lock itself.  Unlocking restored regular desktop functionality and got rid of the blue/green screen.

Do NOT interrupt, as that causes a need to go into recovery mode and clean a bunch of stuff.
