The only way I've found to anonymously support people/projects is to buy a Gift Visa card with cash and mail it to them anonymously, or just put currency notes into the envelop with a note. 

If the place where you buy the Gift Card has video cameras and there was a desire to trace the purchase, it could happen, but it wouldn't be automatic.

I'd be less certain with bank notes, since the serial numbers could be tracked, I would assume. I think tracking of currency is the exception and only for specific reasons when requested by law enforcement.  Most banks have hundreds of thousands in currency in and out daily. The money is churning and mixed between customers.

I don't know of any truly anonymous methods to pay otherwise.
