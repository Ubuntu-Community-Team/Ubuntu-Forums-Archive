Despite unchecking g-c-c / Displays / "Sticky Edges", and disabling associated settings in ccsm, I feel like sometimes the mouse gets stuck in my second monitor for a really small time when I'm switching monitors back and forth rapidly.

I have the launcher enabled only in my primary one (left). The one on the right (secondary) has no launcher. The mouse gets stuck for 1 second (if that) in the right monitor left border when moving back to the primary monitor every now and then. 

What would be a proper way to test if it is actually holding the mouse? (I mean, not visually but get some output of something to prove it).

Visual test (fully reproducible behavior): 
- Disable sticky edges
- Move mouse to secondary monitor at any speed.
- Start moving the mouse very slowly towards the primary monitor until it touches the screen border. 
- Continue moving mouse slowly - pointer is stuck in the secondary until you apply more "Speed" to mouse pointer. If you apply more speed, it shows in the primary monitor as expected.
