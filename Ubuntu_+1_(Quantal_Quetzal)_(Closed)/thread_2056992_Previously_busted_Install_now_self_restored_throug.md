### Post by ventrical on 2012-09-12
One of my  machines is  an Intel based MoBo with Dual Core processor and Nvidia GeForce 210/218  w/ a 500GB hdd SATA.  I have three Quantal installs at various stages on that drive. One of those installs was totally borked when there was a major nvidia/xserver problem back a while ago. All I was able to do was  log in from root and keep updating. After a time I was able to run fvwm-crystal DE and then  Gnome (no effects). Lastnight I upgraded and now have all my original  Unity 3D DE .
