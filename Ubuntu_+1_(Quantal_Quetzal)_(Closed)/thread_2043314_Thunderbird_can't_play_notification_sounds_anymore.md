### Post by effenberg0x0 on 2012-08-16
This was detected on Thunderbird 15.0, which seems to be up-t0-date with QQ repos. I have found mentions to this exact bug posted in 2005 to 2010, but apparently this was fixed. 

- Go to Thunderbird Preferences at Edit->Preferences->General.
- "When a new message arrives", check "Play a sound" and "Use the following sound file". 
- Click the "Browse button" and select a sound file. 
- Click "Play": No sound. Mail yourself for test: No sound.

One can work around this with sound add-ons. I have tested with Tonequilla and it works OK.
