### Post by cariboo on 2012-05-03
This is reminder about *UDS* (Ubuntu Developer Summit). Most of you know
about it already: it's a five-day event, happens at the beginning of
each cycle with the goal of bringing together Canonical staff, Ubuntu
contributors and users, partners, etc to plan for the upcoming release.
You can real all about it at *[http://uds.ubuntu.com](http://uds.ubuntu.com)*

The tracks are listed at *[http://summit.ubuntu.com/uds-q/tracks*](http://summit.ubuntu.com/uds-q/tracks*) and, as
you can see, there's a *QA Track*  at
*[http://summit.ubuntu.com/uds-q/track/qa/*](http://summit.ubuntu.com/uds-q/track/qa/*). There an *Etherpad* instance
for each meeting, as way to allow for remote participation. A good
example is this one:
*[http://summit.ubuntu.com/uds-q/meeting/20464/qa-q-community/](http://summit.ubuntu.com/uds-q/meeting/20464/qa-q-community/)* .

You can also participate via Live Audio/Video streams. See all the
options at *[http://uds.ubuntu.com/community/remote-participation/](http://uds.ubuntu.com/community/remote-participation/)*

Last piece of info: Take a look at this blueprint:
*[https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+spec/other-q-prior-release-feedback](https://blueprints.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+spec/other-q-prior-release-feedback)*
("Feedback on Precise Release and Improvements). You can (and are
encouraged to) add your comments to the ongoing discussion. It's an
opportunity to express your opinions.

Hey guys -- consider this my personal invite to participate in UDS :-)

Do you have feedback about the calls for testing or perhaps iso testing? Have an idea on how to engage with more people? Do you wish QA would adopt a specific tool? Check out the blueprints here and plan to attend in person or via IRC.

[http://summit.ubuntu.com/uds-q/track/qa/](http://summit.ubuntu.com/uds-q/track/qa/)

All comments are appreciated and encouraged. I need everyone to show up and speak up. I know many of you have some great ideas and now is the time to give your ideas life and recruit people to join in to make it happen! Thanks for your participation everyone and let's have a wonderful UDS and 12.10 cycle.
