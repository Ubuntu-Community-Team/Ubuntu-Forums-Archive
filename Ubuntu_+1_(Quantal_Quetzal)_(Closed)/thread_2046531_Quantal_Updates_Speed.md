### Post by VinDSL on 2012-08-23
Here's a "Trick n' Tip" I use...

[LIST]
[*]Open Synaptic
[*]Go to Settings -> Repositories
[*]Click the down arrow on the "Downloaded from:" box
[*]Choose "Other", then "Select Best Server"
[*]After the testing is done, choose the best mirror for your location
[/LIST]

Here in Arizona, USA, (currently) the best download server is in New Zealand (ftp.citylink.co.nz).  Go figure...

This, of course, is subject to change, and needs to be tested from time-to-time for optimum results.

Generally speaking, I avoid the "Main" & "United States" servers like the plague.
