### Post by jerrylamos on 2012-08-11
> **irv said:**
> Why don't you try fixing the USB with gparted.
Verily.  I use gparted to format the whole USB to F32, do a sudo sync, then try unetbootin.  

On topic, was it a QQ .iso daily build?

On some .iso like late Ubuntu's I do a gparted format F32, sync, then do a 
sudo dd if=quantal-desktop-i386.iso of=/dev/sdb oflag=direct bs=1048576
sudo sync

Jerry
