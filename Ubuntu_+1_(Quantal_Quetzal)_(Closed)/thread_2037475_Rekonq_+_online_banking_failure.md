I can think of several possibilities .

1. it's a bug either in rekong or their site , go ahead and report it against rekong and reference the site ( no personal information of course ).

2. the site don't like something in the rekong settings . redirect , plugins ,security, etc  . try running rekong from a terminal and see if that gives any useful info .

3. something at their site isn't uptodate and rekong don't like it . the above may give info about that too . I had that for a few months at one site (work related) , would not auto login from Opera's wand  but I could manually login . they updated their site and now the wand (opera' "wallet" ) works again . So you may have to wait for the bank to update their site .
