### Post by ts3 on 2012-05-12
The wl driver (bcmwl-kerner-source) for laptops with Broadcom wireless cards such as BCM4313 that can't use the open source drivers could not be installed or built on 12.10 Daily.

There's a bug reported on launchpad and a patch has already been released.  I tried it and it works beautifully.  Link to bug report and patch here: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/bcmwl/+bug/994255](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/bcmwl/+bug/994255)
