### Post by plucky on 2012-10-17
12.04 is a LTS release,so it won't offer to upgrade to 12.10,which is not a LTS release.

You have to make in a change in the Settings Page to **Normal Releases** before it will offer the upgrade.

Good Luck

### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-10-17
If you have unofficial Gnome 3.4 themes many may not work or display properly . There are only a hand full 3.6 themes available.  Disable any PPAS if you have them because they may not be compatible.  In update manager settings set to check for all available releases.  Good Luck !
