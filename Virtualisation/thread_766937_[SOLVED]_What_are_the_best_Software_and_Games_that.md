### Post by mimada on 2008-04-25
I don't have a list but, games that don't rely on 3D acceleration such as Starcraft, Diablo I and II, Fallout, Baldur's Gate, Age of Empires I & II, etc. Should work. I have Starcraft running in a XP VM. The only issue is that the host needs to be at a low resolution (i.e., 800x600) to play in full screen mode otherwise the VM resizes into a window. Also, you need to set the CD ROM for "pass through" access for games that have copy protection on the disc.
