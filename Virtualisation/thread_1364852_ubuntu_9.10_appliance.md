### Post by hyperdude111 on 2009-12-26
Virtualbox is currently more popular than vmware for linux virtualisation. Virtualbox uses far less system resources offers brilliant product and at no cost. Vmware is better for graphics support it is the only virtualisation software to support windows aero in a guest machine. Apart from that their features are almost identicle.
