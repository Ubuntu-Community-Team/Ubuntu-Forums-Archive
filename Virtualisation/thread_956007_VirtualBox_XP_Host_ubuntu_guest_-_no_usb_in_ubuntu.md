Two things:

1.  This MAY be due to what version of Virtual Box you installed.  There's two flavors of VB and only one supports USB.

2.  I've run XP in a virtual machine for ages and I always have trouble getting USB devices to work in Virtual Box.  My printer is USB and it's recognized and connects effortlessly.  Just about everything else USB related, though, is a total pain.  Still there are lots of solutions "out there" but yes, most are for those running Windows *guests*.  The Virtual Box forums would be your best bet.

Sorry to be such a downer but USB in VB has always been a thorn in my side.
