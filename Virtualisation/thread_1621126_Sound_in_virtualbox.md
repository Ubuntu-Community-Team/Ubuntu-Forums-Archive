### Post by slooksterpsv on 2010-11-13
In your VM Window go to the menu Devices then down to Install Guest Additions. Otherwise you can, while in the VM, press the Right-CTRL and press D.

That will mount a cd that will let you install guest additions which will allow you to enable sound, higher resolutions, etc.
