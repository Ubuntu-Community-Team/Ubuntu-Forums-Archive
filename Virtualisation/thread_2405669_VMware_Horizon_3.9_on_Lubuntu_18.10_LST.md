### Post by TheFu on 2018-11-09
I know nothing about VMware stuff since 2010, but 18.10 isn't LTS.  I don't know what *LST* means.  18.10 gets 9 months of support from the release date last month.  Further, with 18.10, you have to plan to upgrade the OS every 6 months until the next LTS is released - 20.04.

If you are running a server, then please, please use either 16.04 or 18.04 (both are LTS releases), not 18.10.

A 32-bit system isn't state of the art. Some commercial programs won't be released for 32-bit OSes anymore.
