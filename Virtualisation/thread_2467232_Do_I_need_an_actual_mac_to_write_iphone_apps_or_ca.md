### Post by QIII on 2021-09-20
While you can install any OS you wish on Apple hardware, Apple's EULA forbids installing their OSes on anything but Apple hardware.  A VM is not Apple hardware, therefore an installation in a VM would be a violation of their EULA.

We don't support such violations here.  See [this]("https://ubuntuforums.org/misc.php?do=showrules") and note "We do not support circumventing TOS, EULA, etc here.
