### Post by Shazaam on 2008-08-07
At this time neither VMware nor VirtualBox virtual machines recognize your physical video card. They use hard coded video card drivers (vmvare video adapter, etc) built into each virtualization program. You probably won't be able to get effects working on your virtual machine.
From what I have read VMware has limited 3d functionality built into their Workstation Beta. But since it is a beta you take some risks running it.
