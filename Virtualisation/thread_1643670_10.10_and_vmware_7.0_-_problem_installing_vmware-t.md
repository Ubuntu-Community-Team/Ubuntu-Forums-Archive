### Post by sb900 on 2010-12-13
linking autoconf.h and utsrelease.h worked for me.

```

cd /usr/src/linux/include/linux/
ln -sf ../generated/autoconf.h .
ln -sf ../generated/utsrelease.h .

```[http://bovitron.com/blogostu/2010/06/01/vmware-workstation-with-linux-kernel-2-6-33-4/](http://bovitron.com/blogostu/2010/06/01/vmware-workstation-with-linux-kernel-2-6-33-4/)
