### Post by Toz on 2010-08-26
From within the VirtualBox application, right-click the vm and select 'Delete'. In the confirmation dialog box, click the 'Delete' button. 

Then on the menu, click on 'File->Virtual Media Manager'. On the 'Hard Disks' tab, right-click the drive and select 'Remove'. In the confirmation box, click 'Remove'. In the second confirmation dialog box, click on 'Delete'.

/ToZ
