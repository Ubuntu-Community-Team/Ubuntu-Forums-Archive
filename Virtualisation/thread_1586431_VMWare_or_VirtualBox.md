### Post by SeijiSensei on 2010-10-02
The virtual machine can't address your video card directly.  It talks to an emulated video card instead.  In general, graphically-demanding games [won't run well]("http://www.google.com/search?client=ubuntu&channel=fs&q=virtualbox+gaming+performance&ie=utf-8&oe=utf-8") in a Virtualbox VM.
