### Post by Jose Catre-Vandis on 2009-03-10
Click on Devices then Mount CDrom then mount image
Browse to /usr/share/virtualbox to find the guest additions iso

Next in your Xp VM open up explorer Click on My Computer and find the CD/DVD drive
Look at the files inside and run the GuestAdditions.exe file

The installation should start up and you are on your way :)
