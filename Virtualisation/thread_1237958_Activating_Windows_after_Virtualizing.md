### Post by pricetech on 2009-08-11
I've noted some posts during my search for help that mentioned being unable to activate winders after virtualizing.  Not that this well help everyone, but this is how I solved the dilemma.

Mine is a Dell Latitude E5500 that came with XP and is also licensed for Vista.  My XP disk would install but insisted on activation and the built in VLA key was considered invalid.

When prompted to enter a different license key I used the key from the COA which is different from the built in key.  Problem solved.

It's a least worth a try with any branded system.

Good luck to everyone virtualizing.  Mine is working fine thanks in no small part to the information I gleaned searching the forums.
