### Post by PilotJLR on 2008-09-26
Enterprise hypervisors are quickly becoming a commodity, so prices will absolutely decrease.
That's not to say it's free or cheap... support contracts are never free. Add on management and automation tools, and it gets pricey.

But for just a handful of servers, you can choose from Xen, ESXi, and Hyper-V... all of those hypervisors are free (with Hyper-V, free if you already buy the OS itself).

I don't see value adds like HA or DRS decreasing in price anytime soon, though.
