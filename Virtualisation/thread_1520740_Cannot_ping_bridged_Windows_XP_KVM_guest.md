### Post by vanDrunen on 2010-07-20
I noticed that you have "bridge-ports bond0" in your /etc/network/interfaces file. Shouldn't this be "bridge-ports eth0" if you want to set up a bridge to your ethernet interface?

What is the output of "brctl show" on your machine?

And another thing... in Windows XP all ICMP traffic (or at least ping) is disabled by default on the firewall if I'm not mistaken. Don't disable the firewall, but at least enable ICMP/ping and try again.
