### Post by Warthaug on 2010-08-22
> **barzagli43 said:**
> is it legal to put cracked xp on virtual machine and if inspection comes would i get a penalty?

I use virtualbox, and have tinyXP running on it.  It works amazingly well, even with the relatively low-power laptop I have it running on.  Prior to this I was running a legally acquired copy of XP - also worked great, but was more of a resource hog and had a larger footprint than does tinyXP.

As for legality - check your countries copyright and contract laws.

Using tiny XP is unquestionably against M$'s EUA, however some countries specifically allow in their copyright laws the right for the purchaser of media to use/alter that media as they see fit (so long as its for personal use, in the case of my home country).  So, depending on where you live, it may be legal to run an altered form of XP like tiny XP, so long as you own a legal copy of XP and do not have that legal copy installed anywhere.

Obviously, the legality of that will vary depending on your home countries laws, and the extent to which you laws allow you to waive those rights by agreeing to an EUA.

Bryan
