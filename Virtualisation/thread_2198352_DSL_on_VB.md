### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-01-08
DSL runs best on really old hardware (Pentium I class).  You might try a newer, supported slim distro called TinyCore:  [http://tinycorelinux.net/welcome.html](http://tinycorelinux.net/welcome.html)

Because newer kernels have dropped support for old hardware (like ISA bus), it's not surprising that DSL won't load in a virtual environment without that support.
