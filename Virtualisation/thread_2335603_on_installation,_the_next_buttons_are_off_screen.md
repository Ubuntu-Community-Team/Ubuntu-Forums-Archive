### Post by sudodus on 2016-08-30
If you keep pressing the ***alt key*** and keep pressing the ***left [mouse or touchpad] button***, you can move a window in a very free way. ***For example, you can locate the cursor in the middle of the window*** and move it in any direction until the hidden buttons appear.
