### Post by Scooter_X on 2009-12-23
Running VirtualBox, ubuntu host, Win XP guest. I have compiz enabled, I really enjoy using the cube. HOWEVER, when the guest is on my current workspace, I cannot move to a different workspace using the ctrl + alt + arrow/mouse combo. I have to either click on the workspace I want (in the taskbar thing at the bottom) or close the guest. 

Does anyone know what's up?

---

### Post by blur xc on 2009-12-23
yes!  You need to have your guest relinquish control of your ctrl-alt keys!

press right ctrl, let go- now press ctrl-alt arrow or left click drag is you should be in business...

BM

edit- if you do anything else after the pressing right-ctrl key, the guest os will recapture the ctrl-alt keys and the cube won't work again.
