### Post by Ng Oon-Ee on 2009-02-25
Well, not many can advise you on the dual-screen issue if we don't know your video card.

Even 8.04 was pretty okay for dual-screen, I believe. There's HOWTOs strewn around these forums for those, just a quick search away. I'm only familiar with the nvidia side of things, though, and I'd recommend for your purposes separate x-screen, full 3D acceleration on both screens, allows different resolutions. Only problem is you can't drag windows between the screens, but if you're running VBox full-time on the other one, no problem!
