The place to ask questions about Ubuntu Pro, the commercial pricing, what it supports and doesn't would be to go to: [https://ubuntu.com/pricing/pro](https://ubuntu.com/pricing/pro) during normal business hours, and use the chat dialog there in the lower right to talk with an Ubuntu Pro Staff member from Canonical.

We here are just volunteer Users of Ubuntu who share our own experience with others. None of us are from Canonical, nor do we represent them.
