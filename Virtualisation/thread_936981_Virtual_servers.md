### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2008-10-03
You have several options, I suggest you look at KVM, VirtualBox, or VMWare to start.

You may also wish to look at OpenVZ.

There is information to get you started in the stickies on these forums. Each option has advantages and disadvantages, and some have vocal supporters as well.

Personally, for virtual servers, I suggest OpenVZ.
