Searched the forums here for the answer and couldn't find one but did find this:

VirtualBox Toolbar placement
Today I wanted to move the toolbar of Virtualbox (fullscreen mode) to a different location. Because it was really annoying to have it at the bottom where the taskbar is located (usually). Moving the toolbar is done by opening the settings menu (Machine->Settings) in the main window of Virtualbox. Then go to General->Advanded (Tab). And there select "Show at top of screen" in the mini toolbar section or turn it completly off ;).
