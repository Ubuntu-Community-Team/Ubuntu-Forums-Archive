### Post by Thelasko on 2008-11-21
You didn't mention what type of virtual machine software you are using, but most of them don't support hardware graphics acceleration.  This could be the cause of your problems.

---

### Post by Therion on 2008-11-21
Virtual Box does not support 3D Acceleration.  VMWare's 'Workstation' supports DirectX 8 (or DX9 if you're willing to use a beta release).
