### Post by howefield on 2011-03-13
It is a fairly straight forward process. First stage being to decide which application you want to use for creating and managing your virtual machines, I'd recommend VirtualBox.

There are some video howtos on the virtualbox website, and although a year or so old and possible a little dated on some of the detail, might be worth a watch to give you an idea of what you need to do.
