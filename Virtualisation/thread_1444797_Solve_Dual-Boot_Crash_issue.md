### Post by Shazaam on 2010-04-02
I don't have an answer for your problems but I have questions that you need to research for your own peace of mind..
1. Does your Win7 licence allow you to run it as a virtual machine?
2. How many times have you authenticated your Win7 install? Which goes back to question one in case you have to call Microsoft to get it authenticated.
3. Is your pc under warranty? Removing Windows may void it.
4. Do you plan on keeping your failed Win7 install AND running 7 as a virtual machine in Ubuntu? AFAIK Microsoft frowns on multiple install instances.
