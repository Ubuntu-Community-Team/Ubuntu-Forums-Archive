### Post by cwsnyder on 2013-02-16
Were you in seamless mode (Host+L), full screen mode (Host+F), scaled (Host+C), or normal (all modes switched off) when this happened?

In case you don't know what I am talking about, the host key is the key set by you to be the key used to disable mouse integration, etc. and is usually the right Ctrl key if you have not changed it.  Host + L means to hold down the right Ctrl key while pressing the lower case L key.

If you were in 'normal' mode, try changing to full-screen mode to see if your controls come back.  Your VirtualBox controls will be hiding off the bottom of the screen in full-screen mode, but will show up if your mouse cursor hovers at the bottom of the screen near the center.
