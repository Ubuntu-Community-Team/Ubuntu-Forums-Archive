> **KuroYoma said:**
> How do they work on Hardy Heron?
They work great.
> What is the easiest one?
Use virtualbox from virtualbox.org. Download the closed source binaries, as the open source version from the repos does not have USB support.
> Will it let me put the Virtual machine on my External HD.
You can keep the virtual hard disk image on an external HDD. Just copy it from the .virtualbox folder in your home directory and re-assign it to the VM.
