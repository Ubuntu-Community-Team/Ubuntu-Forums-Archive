### Post by basilebasilic on 2009-05-18
Hello,

I have install virtualbox 2.2.2 version x86 32bits in my laptop dell d631 AMD processor.

For those who launch a virtual machine and then virtualbox crash by freeze the system or reboot automatically the system, you must disable this option in attributes of the virtual machine : VT-x/AMD-V and after your system doesn't crash anymore !!
