### Post by zipperback on 2011-06-01
> **bamim2 said:**
> 
I have tried the Ubuntu 11 upgrade & I am sorry to say I do not like the new look, so I went back to my "good old Ububuntu 10".

The older classical look is available in Ubuntu 11.04.
If you decide to give it another try, install it as you normally would and then when you get to the login screen, select your user account, but before you enter the password, look toward the bottom of your screen and you will see a session selector that says Ubuntu, click the selector and choose "Ubuntu Classic", then log in. You should now have the more familiar desktop interface with the gnome panels instead of the Unity desktop interface.

Sorry I couldn't be more helpful with the Virtual Box issue on Windows, but I checked google and it looks that you're experiencing a pretty common problem. I'd suggest checking the VirtualBox forums for more specific help with it.

[http://forums.virtualbox.org/](http://forums.virtualbox.org/)
