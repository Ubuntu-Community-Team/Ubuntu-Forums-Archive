### Post by PureLoneWolf on 2009-07-15
I have had moderate success with gaming on vbox3.  The thing to remember is that it will not use your nvidia/ati drivers - vbox uses it's own virtual graphics card that is limited to 128mb ram.

I couldn't say how your game would respond.  That said, the website suggests that it is a free game - It certainly can't hurt to try it.  Also, maybe you can get it going through Wine - The last time anyone posted results at WineHQ was April last year, so that has to be worth a shot too.
