### Post by Quadunit404 on 2010-12-12
VMs created in OSE will work with the PUEL version. The only differences between the two are PUEL allows for USB 2.0, RDP and something else I forgot while OSE has VNC. Beyond the license those are the only differences between the two versions of VirtualBox.

### Post by CharlesA on 2010-12-12
Just use the PUEL version.

The only difference between the two is USB support and RDP vs no USB support and VNC (and the fact that PUEL is closed-source)

See here: [http://www.virtualbox.org/wiki/Editions](http://www.virtualbox.org/wiki/Editions)
