### Post by Dellguy on 2008-12-28
You could have used Intel's processor finder tool.

[http://processorfinder.intel.com/](http://processorfinder.intel.com/)

Select Intel® Core™2 Duo Desktop Processor and use the filter. Visualization is the last one under Supported Features. 


Prices vary so look for a good deal & make sure your motherboard supports it.
