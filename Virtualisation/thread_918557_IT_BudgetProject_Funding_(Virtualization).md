### Post by PilotJLR on 2008-09-13
It depends on who is signing off on these... if the approval is ultimately from a finance / C-level person, then demonstrate the ROI or value to them.

With virtualization, illustrate the cost savings by consolidating hardware - less power costs, less cooling cost, less ongoing hardware support costs.

If you are going to an enterprise platform, you can also try to sell the automation tools, such as leveraging live migration for load balancing, etc.

You would be most successful if you can show your approver how the project will either enhance the business and/or reduce ongoing cost.
