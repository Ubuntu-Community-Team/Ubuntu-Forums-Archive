### Post by Regnulify on 2009-02-17
you can use the vmware converter to convert an existing os to a vm. vmware (player at least) will not detect any os.

Note: you will have to reactivate Vista when you start it up the first time. the vmware hardware driver represent a major change to the installation and makes a reactivation neccessary. You can usually do it online.
