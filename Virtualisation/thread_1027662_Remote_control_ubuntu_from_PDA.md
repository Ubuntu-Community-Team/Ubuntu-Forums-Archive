### Post by scorp123 on 2009-01-02
You could use VNC on your client PDA and something such as MythTV on your PC. Search these forums please, there are plenty of threads. 

To give you a quickie overview over your options: 

you could enable "Remote Desktop" (System > Preferences > Remote Desktop .... disable "Ask you for confirmation") and then use a VNC client on your PDA to remote control your PC (where MythTV should be running so you get a similar easy interface). iPhone users will find several VNC programs in "AppStore"; you mentioned having a "PDA" so I assume that this is a Windows Mobile device? You could probably use clients as this one:
[http://handheld.softpedia.com/get/System-Utilities/Communications/VNC-Viewer-8718.shtml](http://handheld.softpedia.com/get/System-Utilities/Communications/VNC-Viewer-8718.shtml)

Google will give you more possible clients when you search for "vnc windows mobile client" ...
