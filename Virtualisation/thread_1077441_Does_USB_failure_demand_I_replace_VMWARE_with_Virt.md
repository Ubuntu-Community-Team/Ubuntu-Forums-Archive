### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2009-02-22
VMWare, virtualbox, KVM, etc, these are all tools, each with advantages and disadvantages.

USB devices work with VMWare, at least for flash drives.

I do not think virtualBox is any easier or harder then VMWare, see the sticky on the top of these forums to enable usb devices.

If you have done that, and you are still having problems, you can try VirtualBox, but the instructions are generic and the same "fix" works for me on both VBox and VMWare.
