### Post by &amp;KyT$0P# on 2016-11-13
Ah, VirtualBox's dialog.  I've noticed that if certain settings (never figured out which) are changed before the VM is first started, VirtualBox won't show that dialog.

Anyway, all it'd do is prompt you whether you want to insert a disk (image) in the VM's optical drive.  You can do the same thing in the VM's Storage settings.  Once there, select the VM's optical drive.  You will notice a CD icon appears in the area to the right, click it and select your ISO from there.
