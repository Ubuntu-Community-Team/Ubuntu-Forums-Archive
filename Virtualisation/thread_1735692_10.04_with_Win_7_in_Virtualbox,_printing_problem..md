### Post by anejo on 2012-04-24
> **rberger said:**
> Can anyone give instructions on setting up printing for Win 7 in Virtualbox?Treat the win7 machine as though it were a physical device.

If the printer is a LAN device, add it as such and if necessary, point it to drivers but most LAN devices would auto install that for you. But this is win7 so your LAN admin might not have win7 stuff already there.

If the printer is local and usb connected, then simply follow the vbox rules for sharing a usb device. Once it is visible to win7, it's just a question of loading drivers.
