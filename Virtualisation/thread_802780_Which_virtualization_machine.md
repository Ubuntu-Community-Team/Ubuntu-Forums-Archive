### Post by Skeet on 2008-05-21
VMWare Workstation's USB support is excellent, but it comes with a cost. Not sure of the price but its available from [here]("http://www.vmware.com")

Virtualbox is simple, but free. [Available Here]("http://www.virtualbox.org")

VMWare Server is probably your best option. Alot of similar features to Workstation and its free. Available from same place as VMWare Workstation.

Id go with VMWare Server imo. At the end of the day they are all pretty good and it comes down to what works best for you.
