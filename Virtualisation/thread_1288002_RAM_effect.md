### Post by Albert Clarke on 2009-10-11
The amount of RAM on your computer allocated to the         virtual machine. Ideally this value should match the amount of RAM         that was installed on the machine from which you created your image, but clearly         must be smaller than the amount of RAM on your machine running Live View.
