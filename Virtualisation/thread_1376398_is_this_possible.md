### Post by SecretCode on 2010-01-09
I think the answer is YES, but it depends what your question really is!

What is your host OS and what is(are) your guest OS(es)? I presume you have ubuntu as a guest since you're talking about cubes. For this, get ccsm - compiz-config-settings-manager - and play around with the 'general options' settings for desktop size. If you make vertical size more than one, you get (I think) more than 1 cube. 

Also in the ccsm menu, you can swap the window manager back to metacity effectively turning off 3D.
