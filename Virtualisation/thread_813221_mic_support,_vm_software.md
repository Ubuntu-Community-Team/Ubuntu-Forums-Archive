### Post by bradmkjr on 2008-05-30
Here are 2 things you could try, 2 free 1 may cost you money.

1. Try wine, It allows you to run Windows applications inside of linux without modifcation or the overhead of virtualization. [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=127](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=127)

2. Buy a USB microphone, then you may (MAY!!) be able to control the USB microphone from within Windows, if you have  webcam with a Microphone that may work also.

3. If you are running linux, find a linux app, such as Pidgin, kopete etc.
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/gutsy/kde/kopete](http://packages.ubuntu.com/gutsy/kde/kopete)
