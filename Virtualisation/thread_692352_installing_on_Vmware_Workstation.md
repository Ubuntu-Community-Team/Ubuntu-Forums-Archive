### Post by tribaal on 2008-02-09
When you reboot your VM make sure the CDrom drive containing your iso image is marked "connect on boot", otherwise nothing happends :)

Default behavior is to connect on boot only the first time you power the VM on.
Sometimes Ubuntu installation is a little slow in the first stages of installation... Make sure you wait long enough.

I also suggest you try with the alternate installation CD (the text-mode installation).

Hope this helps :)
