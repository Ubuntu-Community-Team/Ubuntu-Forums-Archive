### Post by ihelpyou59 on 2014-06-21
Nevermind, I managed to fix the issue. Seems that the copy of VB that I had installed some how became corrupted and would only crash when trying to load a VM, even if it was just a LiveCD. Reinstalling VB seemed to do the trick in getting it to recognize all USB devices connected.
