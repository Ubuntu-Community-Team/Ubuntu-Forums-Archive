If you don't like WINE try Crossover Office. That is the default for Crossover office, it puts an icon on the Linux desktop and away you go.

A year's subscription is not expensive and the Crossover help team is top notch.

as all virtual systems use their own emulated hardware including the video card, not good for an image manipulation programme.
