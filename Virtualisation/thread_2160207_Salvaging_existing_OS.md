### Post by DJ_Max on 2013-07-06
There are ways to boot an exisiting Windows install using Virtualbox, But I have not tried it, and it's looks like more trouble than it's worth. You can research this method, but I can't speak on how reliable it is.

I would just backup/restore on a new install. Keep your exisiting drives as the backup, install your OS(s) on new HDD, and restore data from your existing drives.
