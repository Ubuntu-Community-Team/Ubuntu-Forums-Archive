### Post by fjgaude on 2008-05-11
My feelings, much of it based on past expereince, is that VMware and Virtual Box are about 60% as fast as prime. Your new setup will end up about as fast under virtual as your old under prime boot.

The 2GB of RAM will work nicely with Nikon software.

I would go with the AMD 4200+ dual core if the price is right. Quad core, dual core, both will only run best under virtual using just one core. One day that might not be true but for now it is.

I've used the Nikon software on many of my setups, but have gone with Paint Shop Pro, can't stand Photoshop CS because of the work flow, etc. Notice my signature.
