A telco in my country is planning to add public cloud services to their offerings. They would provide services such as collocation, dedicated hosting, cloud/virtual servers, and hosted apps for customers.

An example of this is provided by "The Planet" (see this page [https://www.theplanet.com/servers/server-cloud.aspx](https://www.theplanet.com/servers/server-cloud.aspx)).
