### Post by seanc7 on 2008-02-02
Probably VMWare. But Virtualbox and QEMU are very good as well.

For QEMU, you need to find a good tutorial on the command line options or download a GUI.

VMWare and Virtualbox both have a similar interface.

I've read people saying QEMU is the fastest out of the three. I found QEMU and Virtualbox faster then VMWare but between those two, I don't notice any difference.

I've never used XEN so I can't comment on that.
