### Post by sammiev on 2015-10-10
Play around and see what works for you.

I use 2gb's ram and 2 processors but I do have 8gb's ram and 4 processors.

I also use HTOP to monitor what I am using and I use no swap as I have a SSD.

I notice no difference using 4gb's ram as I never used up anything close to 2gb's ram.

This is what works for me but you may need more resources depending on what you have loaded in memory.
