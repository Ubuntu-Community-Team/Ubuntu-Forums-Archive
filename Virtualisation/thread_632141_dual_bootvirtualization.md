### Post by Odd-rationale on 2007-12-05
It really depends on what you want to do. Dual booting means, as the name implies, that you will have to reboot in order to switch OS's. But it is an entirely functional, full feature PC. Virtualization has its advantage in better integration. For example, VirtualBox includes a "seamless" mode for XP. But you may not have stuff like 3d acceleration.

The choice is up to you.
