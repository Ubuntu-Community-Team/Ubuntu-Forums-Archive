### Post by Ginsu543 on 2009-12-06
I too chose to download the .deb file directly from Sun rather than use the one in the repositories. Sun offers two versions of VirtualBox, the regular version and the OSE version. The regular version is closed source but available free of charge. The OSE version is open source. The regular version offers more features such as usb support.
