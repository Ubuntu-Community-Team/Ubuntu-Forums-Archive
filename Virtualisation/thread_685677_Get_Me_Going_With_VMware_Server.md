### Post by fjgaude on 2008-02-02
I don't think I'm much at creating HOW-TOs, but I can get you started with VMware Server.

Open Synaptic, click on Settings, and then Repositories and then, Third-Party Software. Check the partner item of canonical. Close.

Search on the word: vmware. Check on vmware-server. Apply, apply. It is installed.
