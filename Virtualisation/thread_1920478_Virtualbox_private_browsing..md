### Post by japzone on 2012-02-06
Using a Linux LiveCD(Running in VM or not only effects Convinience) is the best way to leave no trace of your Activity on the PC, next step is to make sure your Not using a Public Network, connect to a Website with HTTPS and SSL, and for extra Security you can use a VPN or TOR (although setting TOR up on a LiveCD could be abit of a Headache). One thing you could do is Install Ubuntu into a VM, setup TOR, and then Take a Snapshot. Now whenever you use the VM you can just Restore to the Snapshot and all you're Activity will be erased but you'll still have everything Setup the way you want.
