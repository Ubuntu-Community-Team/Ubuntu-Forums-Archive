### Post by Ng Oon-Ee on 2009-02-25
There's tons of guides on making firefox faster, google is your friend.

Epiphany on Ubuntu is a faster browser, I'd think. But you must explain what 'faster' means to you, is it in page-load speed or responsiveness of the program? Because most people would judge the 'fastness' of a browser based on the page-load speed, especially of complicated Java pages, in that area Chrome is very fast, but the firefox beta is supposedly faster. In program responsiveness, however, Firefox is a memory hog and therefore quite slow (not as slow as IE though), while Chrome doesn't have anything else to run besides its web-browsing (not many addons etc).
