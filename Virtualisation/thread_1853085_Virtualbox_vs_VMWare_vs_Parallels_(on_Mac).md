### Post by Adam's AI on 2011-10-01
I just wanted to share my experiences installing Ubuntu 11.04 (many times) on mac using Virtualbox, VMWare, and Parallels, in case it is of use to anyone getting into it:

**VirtualBox**
Pros: 3D support, free
Cons: very laggy, even the mouse pointer

[B]VMWare
[/B]Pros: Super fast and responsive, (optional) express installation, can access a boot camp partition as a virtual machine
Cons: no 3D support, not free (~$50)

[B]Parallels
[/B]Pros: Everything runs perfectly (3D support, responsive and fast), can access a boot camp partition as a virtual machine
Cons: Most expensive (~$80)

**Overall:** If you can afford it, Parallels was the only one to work perfectly. I spent so much time and tried installing so many times on all of these that I wanted to share my results. I really wanted VirtualBox to work best since I've heard such good things, but Parallels was the only one that gave me everything I needed.
