WEBINAR: [**Enterprise-Grade Virtualization Management for KVM and Xen**]("http://convirture.com/webinars/webinar_ent-mgmt.php")
Tuesday, August 13, 2013 2pm EDT/11am PDT


Not so long ago, the virtual world was a VMware world and we all just lived in it. Today, open source hypervisors – KVM and Xen – have gained acceptance in the marketplace as enterprise-class, open alternatives. But successful virtualization efforts are ultimately about more than just the hypervisor. Given the growing numbers of virtual machines being deployed and the complexities of virtualized infrastructure, enterprise-grade virtualization management is absolutely critical to success. 


In this webinar, you will learn how to administer, provision and manage your current KVM and Xen infrastructure with ConVirt, a comprehensive server virtualization management system. In particular, we will walk through ConVirt’s key management and orchestration capabilities for large scale enterprise deployments, including:

[LIST]
[*]Server pool-based management
[*]VM lifecycle
[*]Real-time performance monitoring
[*]Network management
[*]Storage configuration
[*]High availability
[*]Dynamic workload management
[*]Backup and recovery
[/LIST]
