The latest version of VMware, 6.5 now supports Direct X 9c

This is awesome, because now we don't need to keep a dual-boot just for games, We can just have a Virtual machine for them.

Of course, this functionality is still in its early stages, so there are glitches, lets compile a tread of what games work well under the new 3D visualization and which don't.

I'll start:

My Guest OS is Windows XP SP3
My Graphics Card is a NVIDIA 8400 GS
**The Sims 2 Double Deluxe - **Works in full speed, the house's walls have graphic glitches, all other graphics are perfect[B]
Spore - [/B]Works at full speed, some missing graphics, can't see the planets on the galaxy screen
