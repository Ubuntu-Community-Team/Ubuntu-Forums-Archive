### Post by Hero of Time on 2008-03-22
Create a new and bigger vdi file, use a clone disk program and clone the small one to the large one. Make sure you also add the mbr. Simplest solution is, what would you do if your physical drive would be full and your too lazy to remove something? Indeed, you would buy a new drive, clone the lot and continue to use the larger drive.
