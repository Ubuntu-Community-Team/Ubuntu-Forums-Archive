### Post by Fire_Chief on 2010-08-15
Couple of things...

Did you enable the Intel-VT extensions in your laptop BIOS? This is normally found under the CPU options.
Did you enable the Use VT Extensions option in your Vbox setup?

This will likely need to be turned on (usually disabled by default). This should greatly improve the VM performance and the load on the host.

Cheers!
