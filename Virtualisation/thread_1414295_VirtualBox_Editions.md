### Post by Objekt on 2010-02-23
I don't see a picture.  I also can't find any reference to something called "Virtualbox xVM." 

It appears that Sun once had a virtualization product called "xVM Virtualbox" but has since dropped that branding.  Oracle, Sun's parent company, has lately been the proponent of Virtualbox.  It is only available in two flavors, OSE and PUEL.
