### Post by Mark20 on 2009-06-24
Ummm I would suggest making your primary OS the OS that you use the most.  If you spend most of your time in Ubuntu then make that your primary OS Ubuntu and install an XP Virtual Machine.  If you spend most of your time in Windoze make Windoze your primary OS with an Ubuntu Virtual Machine.  

That being said, if you choose Windoze as your primary OS it will be a more vulnerable / bloated system, but the choice is up to you :p

In regards to the title of your post - I prefer VirtualBox, but if I were you I would use whichever program gets along with Photoshop/Lightroom/Flash the best.
