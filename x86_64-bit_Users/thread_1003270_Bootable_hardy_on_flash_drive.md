### Post by Spr0k3t on 2008-12-06
You can either follow one of the many guides on pendrivelinux.com or use the built in Ibex "Create a USB Startup Disk" option in System/Administration.  You will be given the option to create space for storing your configuration or to discard the changes.  You can use almost any ISO image to make the bootable drive.
