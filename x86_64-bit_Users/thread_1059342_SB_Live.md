### Post by paulisdead on 2009-02-03
I think the third option's the one you likely want.  Have you tried right clicking on the volume icon by the clock, select Open Volume Control, make sure that everything on the playback tab is turned up and not muted, and that on the switches tab the analog/digital output jack is set correctly.  You probably don't have digital speakers, so you more than likely don't want that checked.
