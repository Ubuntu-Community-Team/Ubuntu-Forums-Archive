Uninstall Flash 10 as it is a 32 bit wrapped.
```
sudo apt-get autoremove flashplugin-nonfree
```

Easiest way to install Flash 10 on x64 is to first:

Get true 64 bit Flash10
[http://download.macromedia.com/pub/labs/flashplayer10/libflashplayer-10.0.32.18.linux-x86_64.so.tar.gz](http://download.macromedia.com/pub/labs/flashplayer10/libflashplayer-10.0.32.18.linux-x86_64.so.tar.gz)

Then untar the archive into your ~/.mozilla/plugins folder.
