I found that you cannot have an exclamation point (!) in your password or Richard Jones's method of connecting to a Gmail drive will not work.  I was having problems connecting so I removed the ! from my password and it worked fine after that.

If the administrators want to append this bit of information to the archived thread and delete this one, that is fine, actually it would be better that way.  I just wanted to let other people who are trying to accomplish this to know they cannot have a ! in their password.
