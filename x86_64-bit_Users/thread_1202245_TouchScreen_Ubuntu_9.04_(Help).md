### Post by Favux on 2009-07-02
Hi Dean,

Welcome to Ubuntu!

You are in luck, Ayuthia just figured out how to get the stylus and touch both working using Rafi Rubin's patches.  Before that you could only get the stylus working in Jaunty.

Go to Appendix 2 here:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=6274392#post6274392](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=6274392#post6274392)  That will explain more.  You can pick up the xorg.conf there and the link to Ayuthia's instructions and deb.s.

Take your time and read things through a few times.  Good luck!

PS:  Compiz may not work with rotation of your screen.  You may want to take a look at the Compiz off rotation script.
