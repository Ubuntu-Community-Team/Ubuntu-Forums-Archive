### Post by jespdj on 2009-05-01
There are more people who have this problem. Have a look at [this thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1134201) for a possible workaround.

It looks like it has something to do with the wireless drivers. The workaround in that thread is to disable the wireless drivers on shutdown. I had the same problem, and the workaround works on my XPS M1530.
