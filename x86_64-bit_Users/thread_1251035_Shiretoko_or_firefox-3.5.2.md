### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2009-08-27
Sometimes, sites (like Facebook chat) will check the browser string instead of the rendering engine. Anyway, you can change your browser string by going into about:config, looking for the general.useragent.extra.firefox preference and changing the word 'Shiretoko' to 'Firefox'. Restart FF after that and confirm the change in the Help -> About menu.
