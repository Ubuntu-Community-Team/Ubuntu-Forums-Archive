### Post by r m h on 2009-08-30
Dual monitors *do* work with Ubuntu 64-bit.

I've been running with 2 monitors for almost a year now (and I keep very current with updates and patches) - I even tested 4 monitors successfully a number of months ago.

If you are having problems, I'd bet your xorg.conf is wonky.

I did find a how-to for setting up dual monitors - try these suggestions:
[http://wiki.osuosl.org/display/howto/Set+Up+Dual+Monitors+-+xorg.conf](http://wiki.osuosl.org/display/howto/Set+Up+Dual+Monitors+-+xorg.conf)
