### Post by eluminx on 2008-11-20
a sym link is easy just open up a terminal and type ln -s /target/_space_"link name".  That "space" is for you to put a space b/w the "/" and the name of the link.  Otherwise you should be able to do ith through nautilus just like in windows, right click--> make link.

Also you can always type "ln --help" in the terminal and it'll give you more info on how to use different options.
