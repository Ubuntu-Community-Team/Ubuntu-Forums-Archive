### Post by touchlikefire on 2008-09-27
Who do you mean by 'they'?

a 64-bit or 32-bit value for the CPU describes the word size of the processor, meaning, essentially, how many bits the CPU can process in an instruction.

On the other hand, a GHz rating is how many instructions the CPU can process per second.
