### Post by Slim Odds on 2009-09-04
> **Moop said:**
> I think just about any Nvidia card is supported so you shouldn't have a problem. After you install, check System-Administration-Hardware Drivers for a driver for your card.

That's not necessarily true. Some of the older cards are not supported in the newer releases (especially for compiz).

An example is the dropping support for the FX5200 in Intrepid. It's a perfectly decent card that worked great in Hardy with compiz but won't work in Intrepid (at least not officially).
