### Post by YMS_1975 on 2009-01-25
Hey guys, 

I too have noticed this problem. Just like **stevovee** I find that pressing F11 (twice) fixes this problem, however I too would prefer not to have to do this.

I think I've found a fix for this. Go to **System > Preferences > Appearence**. Click on the **Visual Effects** tab and select **Normal** & then click **Close**.

Re-open FireFox and all should be well. At least, that's what worked for me.
