### Post by ryank76nz on 2009-01-01
Hi, I have got this to work through lots of trial and error. This is the combination of addons I used for my system:

Gutsy Gibbon 7,10
Thunderbird 2.0.0.18
Lightning 0.8 from here:
[http://releases.mozilla.org/pub/mozilla.org/calendar/lightning/releases/0.8/contrib/linux-x86_64/](http://releases.mozilla.org/pub/mozilla.org/calendar/lightning/releases/0.8/contrib/linux-x86_64/)

Provider for Google calendar 0.4 from here:
[http://ftp.mozilla.org/pub/mozilla.org/addons/4631/](http://ftp.mozilla.org/pub/mozilla.org/addons/4631/)

Hope this helps someone! I installed an update to Provider for Google calendar 0.5 and it all broke - I'll stick with this setup for now.
