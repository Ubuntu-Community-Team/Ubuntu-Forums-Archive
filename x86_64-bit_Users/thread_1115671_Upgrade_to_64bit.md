### Post by stmiller on 2009-04-04
There is no way to 'upgrade' to 64bit. You have to download a 64bit Ubuntu install disc, and do a fresh install.

You CPU must be a 64bit CPU. (AMD64, Core2Duo, AMD X2, etc).

Go for it! I'm ready for operating systems to get out of the 32bit-dark ages...
