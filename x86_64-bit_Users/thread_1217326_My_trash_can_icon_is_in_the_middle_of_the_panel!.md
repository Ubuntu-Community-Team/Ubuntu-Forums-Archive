### Post by JerommekeH on 2009-07-19
You can change the place of icons by clicking right on them and then choose move. You just click now where you want it. Some items are locked, so you have to unlock them before moving (right > Lock to panel)
I think that, if you want to have your trash extreme right, you have to put it next to the switch desktop icon and then unlock the switch desktop icon and move it a little bit to the left (the trash will jump to the extreme right). When you are ready, you can lock the icons on the panel again (it stays there).
