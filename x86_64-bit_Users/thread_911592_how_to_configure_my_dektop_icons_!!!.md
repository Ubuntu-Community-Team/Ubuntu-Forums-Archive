### Post by ad_267 on 2008-09-05
So you want to prevent the drives from showing up on your desktop? Press Alt+F2 and run gconf-editor. Browse to apps - nautilus - desktop and untick "volumes_visible"

To install themes see [http://linuxowns.wordpress.com/2008/06/16/guide-to-customizing-ubuntus-look-and-feel/](http://linuxowns.wordpress.com/2008/06/16/guide-to-customizing-ubuntus-look-and-feel/) and [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=809695](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=809695)
