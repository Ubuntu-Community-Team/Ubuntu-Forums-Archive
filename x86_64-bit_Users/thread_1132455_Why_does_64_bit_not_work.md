### Post by jespdj on 2009-04-22
> **kenneth730 said:**
> Yes i have a 64 bit processor i think it is an Intel duo core, ...
You need to be precise with the specification. There's the Intel **Core Duo**, which is 32-bit only. And there's the Intel **Core 2 Duo**, which does support 64-bit. If your computer came with 64-bit Vista, then it's most likely a Core 2 Duo.

I also had problems with a bad checksum once, and the cause turned out to be an error in the RAM in my computer, and I had to get a new RAM module.
