### Post by Thelasko on 2008-08-22
> Most thigns I download are in tar.gz format but there is never anything to double click on to install.
That's where Ubuntu differs from other operating systems, including other types of Linux.  You don't go find software, download it, and install it.  You tell Ubuntu what software you want, and it does the rest.  Once you get used to it, you will discover that it's much better this way.  You don't have to worry about software being compatible with other software, or even viruses.  By getting all of it's software from a single, secure source, Ubuntu makes installing software much easier and safer.
