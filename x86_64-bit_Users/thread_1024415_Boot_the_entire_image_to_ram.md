### Post by kabloink on 2009-01-10
Unless you constantly starting a wide variety of programs, your not going to see any real benefit from loading everything into ram. The Live cds benefit from ram loading due to the slow read and access speeds of the optical drives. Which isn't a real problem with any modern hard drive. 

You may want to try the program preload, that preloads your frequently used programs into ram to decrease load time if you have 8gigs.
