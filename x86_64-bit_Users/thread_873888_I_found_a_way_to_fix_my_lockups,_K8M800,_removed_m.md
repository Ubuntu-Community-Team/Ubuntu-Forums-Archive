### Post by jaakan on 2008-07-29
I was searching the other night and found this

[http://wiki.openchrome.org/tikiwiki/tiki-index.php?page=3DStatus](http://wiki.openchrome.org/tikiwiki/tiki-index.php?page=3DStatus)

"The 3D driver for the unichromes is at present not being maintained and has seen several regressions, such as misplaced software fallbacks and slow texture rendering. Some bugs even result in lockups, particularly on the K8M800 hardware."

I have a K8M800 based Motherboard + a NV GF6200 AGP card.
I just took a shot and removed the card and now zero crashes/lockups but no 3D. 

I wonder if all the locks others are having are on hardware with a onboard video card + an addon video card?
