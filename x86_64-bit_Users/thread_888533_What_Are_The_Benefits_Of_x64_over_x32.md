### Post by tuxxy on 2008-08-13
The advantages to 64bit are increased RAM usage, increased performance for all 64bit apps comapred to the equivelant 32bit apps, you will see good benefits in video editing, image editing etc

On the downside, you will have to install the 32bit adobe flash plugin web plugin as there isnt a native 64bit one yet, it runs fine for me though and there should be 64bit one soon.

### Post by elmoosecapitan on 2008-08-13
Aside from the lack of some programs, e.g., flash, silverligh, etc..., which will be available in time, x64 is better than x32 when it comes to processing efficiency. Even when running 32-bit on a 64-bit machine, you are still running some programs in parallel, i.e., on two processors. Ultimately, this is an optimized form of multitasking.
