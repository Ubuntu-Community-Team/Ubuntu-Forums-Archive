### Post by Thelasko on 2008-10-10
Go to System>preferences>sound and make sure all of the settings are correct.  From that window you can not only change your settings but test them as well.

If everything works out in the sound window, open VLC and change the sound preferences there.  Every time you change a preference in VLC you have to restart VLC for it to take effect.

If it's not a problem with your sound preferences, or VLC preferences, then it's probably a problem with [PulseAudio]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=789578").
