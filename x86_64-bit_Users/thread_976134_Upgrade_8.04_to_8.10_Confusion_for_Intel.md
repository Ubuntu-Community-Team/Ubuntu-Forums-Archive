### Post by jespdj on 2008-11-09
The 64-bit version works on AMD as well as on Intel processors. There is no practical difference between 64-bit AMD and Intel.

Don't be confused that it's called "amd64". AMD invented the 64-bit extensions, that's why it's called "amd64".

There is no way to upgrade from 32-bit to 64-bit. You'll have to reinstall.
