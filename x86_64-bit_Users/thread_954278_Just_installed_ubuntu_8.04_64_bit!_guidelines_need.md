### Post by cariboo on 2008-10-21
To peruse the programs available list go to Applications-->Add/Remove Programs. Make sure Show All Open Source Applications is selected. To load the restricted drivers for your video card, go to System-->Administration-->Hardware Drivers and follow the instructions.

Just as an side you will see reference to restricted drivers quite often. Restricted drivers are closed source drivers supplied by hardware manufacturers.
