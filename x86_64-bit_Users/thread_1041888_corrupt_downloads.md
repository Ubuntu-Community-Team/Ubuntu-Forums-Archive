### Post by earthling30 on 2009-01-17
This sounds like to me it's hardware related, more than likely the motherboard. Does your system have either a ECS Elitegroup or PC-Chips brand motherboard in you PC? I've experienced the same issue on 5 different occasions where the motherboard would cause these type of issues on no matter which OS was running. Plus they were a board from one of the manufactures listed above, neither of these systems were mine, I fix PC's on the side. BTW, both these Manufactures are the same company, kind of like Chevy and GMC. My solution was to replace the boards (different brand) and they're all good now.
