### Post by dcstar on 2009-02-14
Time to stir the pot and find out if we can convince Canonical to resume shipping 64 bit Ubuntu CDs.

As fas as I am concerned not shipping them and forcing people who want 64 bit to download goes directly against the Ubuntu ethos of making this distro available to all people around the world. The obvious assumption that anyone who wants a 64 bit version has an adequate Internet connection with an adequate quota must basically stop anyone without these resources from using the 64 bit version.

Time to reverse this policy methinks, what does everyone else think?

BTW, yes this probably should be in a different forum, but us 64 bit users may want a say as we now seem to be "discriminated" against!   ;-)
