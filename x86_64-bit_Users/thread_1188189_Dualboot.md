### Post by murray92 on 2009-06-15
The minimum needed is 4GB, but I recommend at least 10GB. I dual boot too, and I have 15GB for Ubuntu 60GB for Windows and 35GB for other random files.
Bear in mind that you can access your Windows files from Ubuntu, but its a little more difficult to access your Ubuntu files from Windows, so your Ubuntu partition doesn't need to be as big
