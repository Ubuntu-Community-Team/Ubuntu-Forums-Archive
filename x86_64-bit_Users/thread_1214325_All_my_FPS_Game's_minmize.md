### Post by Rhubarb on 2009-07-16
Turn Compiz off and your screen savers off and it should no longer minimise your games.

To turn compiz off:-
System --> Preferences --> Appearance
Visual Effects tab, None

To turn your screen saver off:-
System --> Preferences --> Screensaver
Uncheck the "Activate screensaver when computer is idle" box
