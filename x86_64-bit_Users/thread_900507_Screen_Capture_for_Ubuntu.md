### Post by jdwinfodesign on 2008-12-11
Short answer: No.

I have been looking for months and struggling with xvidcap, Wink, and there is nothing that lets you do what you can do with SnagIt or Camtasia. If you want professional screenshots and screencasts, you have to bite the bullet and spend the money.

Unfortunately, that means no native screencasts of your Ubuntu desktop unless you can cap it from a machine running Windows, which I suppose you could do. Bottom line, for me: If you have to have a professional-grade screen capture or screencast (without many hours of tweaking and fussing), TechSmith still rules, and they haven't even come out with a Mac version of those apps, much less a Linux port.
