### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2009-02-20
IMO, it depends on your intended usage. If you're primarily using 3D/gaming/video playback, nvidia is the way to go. If you just want good, stable 2D acceleration and compatibility, then the ATI card will give you less headaches because it works with open-source drivers.

ATI's proprietary drivers have come a long way, but still leave much to be desired compared to nvidia's.
