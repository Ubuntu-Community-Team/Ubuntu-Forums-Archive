### Post by nikgare on 2008-10-23
It's not an "issue", it's the way it's designed.
Maximised windows in most desktops don't cover task bars.
One sort of work around is to set the panel to auto-hide.

---

### Post by C.S.Cameron on 2008-10-23
Right click on a taskbar and select Customize Panel.
Click on Autohide.
Windows can now take up the whole desktop.
Taskbars will only be there when you hover over them.
