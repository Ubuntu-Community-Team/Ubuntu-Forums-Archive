### Post by markbuntu on 2008-10-29
If you miss your xmms (not xmms2) and would like to have it back:

[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/hardy/a...070601-1build2](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/hardy/a...070601-1build2)

Beware of dependency problems with libglib1.2 and libglib1.2dbl on the amd64 build. You can read about that here, (it may even be fixed by now):

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=774461](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=774461)

Change the output plugin to alsa and you should be good to go.
