### Post by uberlube on 2008-08-24
You can do either or. If you want to keep vista, defrag your hard drive and resize it to make room for your Ubuntu partition. Or you can just format the whole HD and get rid of vista all together during the install. If you want an excellent tool for all your partitioning needs, check out partedmagic.
