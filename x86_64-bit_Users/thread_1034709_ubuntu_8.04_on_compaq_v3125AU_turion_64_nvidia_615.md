### Post by phlembob on 2009-01-10
Video resolution is the 1st problem most people run into.  Pick System>Administration>Hardware Drivers.  You should get "Nvidia accelerated graphics driver (latest cards).  Pick the box under enable.  If you don't see this you may need to run Hardware Update.  Then restart your computer and do what is said above again.
