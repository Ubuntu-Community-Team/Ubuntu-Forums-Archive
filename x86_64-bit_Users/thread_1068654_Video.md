### Post by wfp on 2009-02-13
Not sure if this will help anyone, but on my machine i had problems streaming video, while having pandora or slacker in a tab in firefox. Videos would load but would not play. The only format that would work was flash, so tonight i tried nvidia-settings and and under openGl settigs i checked Sync to Vblank. Now i can stream all video while listening to pandora or slacker. Before i would have to download the file, close firefox then play the video which was a pain in the you know what.
