### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-05-24
Does your network include a router? If so, you need to look into something called port forwarding. The System -> Administration -> Network Tools will tell you what IP address your devices have, but setting up port forwarding varies between routers. Generally, you enter 192.168.0.1 into your browser to login to the router and hopefully you can follow your router vendor's instructions.
