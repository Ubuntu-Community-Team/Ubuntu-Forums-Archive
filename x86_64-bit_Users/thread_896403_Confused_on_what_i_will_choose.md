### Post by Stunts on 2008-08-21
Why not try them both?
Just a hint - x86_64 will have some issues with 3rd party debs.
If you are new to linux I'd recommend the i386 (the one you call x86).
When you get a little experience, you should switch to a x86_64 architecture, in order to take full advantage of you processor.
But that's just my opinion. Hope it helps.
