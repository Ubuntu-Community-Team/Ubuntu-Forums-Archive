### Post by darco on 2009-08-30
Ya I know I posted above and found out later that there is a fix...

"When you click on the Menu, there is a side bar on the left side. This hides Item Headers that have been double-clicked. (Places, System, etc.)
If you see a small icon in the upper left, click it to restore the hidden menu, and you won't get the error! And the Menu will auto-close like it was intended."

darco
