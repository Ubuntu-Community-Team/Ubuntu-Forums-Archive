### Post by sanderj on 2009-07-05
You can *not* convert nor upgrade a 32-bit install to a 64-bit install.

So you have to reinstall.

If your /home is on a separate partition, you can easily keep your personal stuff over the reinstall.

---

### Post by 3Miro on 2009-07-05
if /home is separate you can keep all the personal data (without the need to backup, however, I suggest that you backup anyway). Also you can keep your personal settings.

Other than that, you have to make a clean install.
