I can't help with all your issues, but for starters, right-click on the sound icon (loudspeaker) on the desktop panel and select Open Volume Control. This will open a dialog box with a myriad of sliders for various parts of the sound system. Experimenting with them will probably restore sound to normal volume. While exploring, click on File > Change Device to find additional sliders.
