### Post by ticket on 2009-11-07
Don't tick 'Use system colours' and don't tick 'Allow pages to choose their own colours ...', and then set your prefs for visited & unvisited links.

Trouble is, you will lose the colour scheme in some web pages that can make some text invisible.

And right now, if I tick 'Allow pages to choose their own colours ...' I lose the colour change for visited links - they now all appear blue, whether they have been clicked on or not. 

I'm sure it didn't used to behave like this. Oh well...
