### Post by piratejake on 2009-01-12
Are you concerned with the idle RAM or CPU usage or both?

When I switched (about a month ago) the largest difference I noticed was not usage at idle at load. In vista one of the CPU's will near hit 100% without much of a problem. However, I really really need to push Ubuntu to get my Q6600's at 3.0 GHz to reach near fully capacity.

If you're worried about idle usage try to close any programs you might not need to be running (ex. no need to keep a music player open if you're done listening to music, same with movies, etc).

Overall, Ubuntu manages resources better than vista and you should notice the difference moreso when things are at load.

Good luck and enjoy!
