Then, I would switch to a couple of different Linux distributions to see if the printer would function normally with another software. To my great surprise, Ubuntu 9.04 printed out a test page normally, without any intervention on my part!

At times, the most defective piece of equipment proves to be the human brain - mine. All that was needed was a reboot.
