I am using RealPlayer 11 on Intrepid x86_64 and it is not very stable. However, I have no problem connecting to BBC. Try this link:

[http://www.bbc.co.uk/radio3/ram/r3g2.ram](http://www.bbc.co.uk/radio3/ram/r3g2.ram)

(That's for BBC 3, the classical music part.)

To use that link go into Favorites > Manage Favorites and add it manually. Then close the Manage Favorites window and connect with the entry in Favorites.

Similarly, I can add other entries to the Favorites menu. Instead of connecting directly on the website I copy the URL, then add it to the favorites. 

Also note that RealPlayer won't play all streams. But most stations that stream on the internet have more than one format. Sometimes you have to try one or the other until you find a format that works. I currently have almost 50 classical stations in my Favorites.
