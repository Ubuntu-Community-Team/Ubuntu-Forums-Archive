### Post by Stunts on 2008-10-13
Editing GRUB is text based, it's not so simple until you get the hang of it.
But the good news is that there is an alternative:
SuperGRUBdisk: [http://www.supergrubdisk.org/](http://www.supergrubdisk.org/)
