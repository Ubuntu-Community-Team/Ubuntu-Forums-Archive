### Post by twright on 2009-07-12
In my experience 64bit Ubuntu works pretty much as well as 32bit - the only problem is the poor support for flash but there is already an alpha of the plugin (and of course ndiswrapper) which fixes that and should be more stable soon. The main problem is most people still only test on 32bit due to shear inertia. Sadly due to the popularity of 32bit only atom processors (including the one in my eeepc) the switch to 64bit as standard could be delayed even further.
