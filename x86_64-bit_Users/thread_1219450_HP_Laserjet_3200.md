> **ve3rpm said:**
> I just got this workhorse (for free) due to a paper jam.  It prints fine, but for the life of me I can't get the scanner to work.  any ideas?

According to [openprinting]("http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=HP-LaserJet_3200"), for scanning etc you will have to use the HPLIP driver (which includes HPIJS).

Which HP Linux Imaging and Printing web site states that (Ubuntu 9.04 supplies HPLIP 3.9.2 and it does support that printer.)

However. the site does allow you download a more current driver.
