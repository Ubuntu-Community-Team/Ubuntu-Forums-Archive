### Post by beniwtv on 2008-06-25
Hi,

Does it boot into the GUI?

If not, try doing the following:

1) Restart your computer
2) When you see the message 'Press ESC to show the boot menu', press ESC.
3) Highlight the first 'Ubuntu....' line
4) Press 'e'.
5) Go to the back to the line and remove the words 'quiet' and 'splash'
6) Press 'Enter'
7) Press 'B' to boot

This way, you will get a ton of messages (don't be scared :lolflag:), which might help us seeing where it hangs.
