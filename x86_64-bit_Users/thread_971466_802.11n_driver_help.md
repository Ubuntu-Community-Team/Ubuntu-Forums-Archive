### Post by Fil0 on 2008-11-05
LAST RESORT HAVING TROUBLE WITH PCI WIRELESS N, My pci adapter is a broadcom 802.11n chipset I am running 64 bit XP  and had to install drivers manually. I had to tell the driver manager to install 802.11n driver from broadcom  download the drivers you think will work for your pci card and right click the INF file and click install, then open up device manager and tell it to update driver and install specific driver, DO NOT AUTO SEARCH FOR IT,  it tells you that your system may become stable, and it very well may, so as a last resort this is what i had to do to fix my drivers
