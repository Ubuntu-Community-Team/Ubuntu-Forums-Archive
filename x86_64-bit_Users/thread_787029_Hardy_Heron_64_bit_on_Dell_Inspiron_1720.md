### Post by grkuntzmd on 2008-05-08
I installed Hardy Heron x64 on my Dell Inspiron 1720 about 1 week ago. So far I am pretty happy with it.

My configuration:
```

Core 2 Duo (T7700) at 2.4 GHz
4 GB RAM
160 GB 7200 RPM HD configured as 2 partitions (55 GB for Vista, rest for Ubuntu)
NVidia 8600 GT graphics card with proprietary driver (latest downloaded from NVidia).
DVD RW
Built-in webcam
Built-in Dell 802.11g
Built-in Bluetooth

```

My experiences so far:

Everything except the microphone works. I have not tested the bluetooth.

I got the wireless working by installing the ndiswrapper and XP driver downloaded from Dell.

The webcam works in Skype (x86 version), but I can't call anyone without the microphone :-).

I have VMWare Workstation 6 installed with a Vista Home Premium virtual machine that works well except for host-local printing (see my other post if you know how to do this). I can even sync my iPhone with iTunes inside Windows using the latest VMWare (6.0.3).

All the rest, with the exceptions noted above, worked right "out of the box". I burn CDs and DVDs with Brasero (nice program, especially compared to Nero in Windows -- what a pain in the asterisk!).

I have not gotten hibernation/suspension working yet.
