### Post by Artemis3 on 2009-06-14
Please stop overclocking. This cannot be stressed enough, plus ASRock is not exactly "Rock" solid... Use stable settings in bios (No Spread Spectrum!). Monitor CPU/Chipset/GPU temperatures, etc.

I find it silly people bother with OC when they can't even get a proper mobo or gpu for a few bucks more and much better perfomance they'll ever get than overcloacking their cheap hardware. OC is not just about cooling, there are voltage, clock, and timing values involved. IMO not worth the trouble for very little (if any) gain. You'll get much better framerates if you replaced your video card with a 90$ 9800 GT instead of OC an already fast processor.
