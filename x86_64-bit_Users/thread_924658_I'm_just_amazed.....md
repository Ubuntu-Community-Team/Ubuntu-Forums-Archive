### Post by jhenager on 2008-09-19
I just accidently discovered another feature.
When on the taskbar, I can scroll through open applications with the wheel.
It totally makes sense, but was a bit of a surprise.
To see what I am talking about, open up a broswer, text editor, and a game.
Click on one of the minimized apps on the taskbar. Roll the scroll wheel back and forth. It shifts focus to each app in turn.
See what I mean?
