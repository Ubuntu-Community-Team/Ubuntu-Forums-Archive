### Post by lswb on 2008-12-18
This is usually an easy fix, likely you just accidentally deleted the window list applet from the panel.

Right click on the bottom panel, select "Add to Panel" then scroll down the list that appears and click on the "Window List" applet. 

There are a few preference settings that can be accessed for the window list display by right-clicking on the barely visible vertical double row of dots that anchors the left end of the button display.
