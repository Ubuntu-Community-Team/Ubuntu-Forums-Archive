### Post by Melk79 on 2008-11-12
Do you have softkeys on your laptop for adjusting volume?  Not knowing what you've tried, you could try those, try the speaker icon in the panel at the top of the screen (or right click on it and open volumen control).  If it's not there you can right click on a panel, click Add to panel and type sound in the filter to bring up volume control.  You can test sounds via System>Preferences>Sounds.  System>Administration>Hardware Testing will also test your audio.
