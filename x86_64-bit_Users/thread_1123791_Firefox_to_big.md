### Post by Therion on 2009-04-13
Are you running Compiz-fusion?

If so open CCSM from the System/Appearance menu.
[INDENT](If you don't have CCSM installed, search for *compizconfig-settings-manager* in Synaptic and install it).[/INDENT]
Scroll down to the "Utilities" section.
Open the "Workarounds" plugin.
_Clear_ the check-box for **Legacy Fullscreen Support**.
Restart Firefox.


Joy?
