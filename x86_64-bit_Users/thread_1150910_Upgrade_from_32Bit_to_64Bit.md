That's a tricky question.  Personally, I can do a complete install, and have my system re-tweaked to perfection, in about two hours.  

That's not a huge investment of time or energy in my opinion.

Is 64-bit Jaunty superior to 32-bit Jaunty on MY machine?  Yes, absolutely.  64-bit Jaunty + ext4 on my system positively rocks my world.  Having used 64-bit I would NEVER want to go back to 32-bit.  Period.  But that's me.  In short, I think it's worth it, yes.  I can't know what you'll think.
