I recently installed the global menu applet on my system, and, after trying it out for a little while, I decided not to use it.  I then removed the applet from my panel (but its still installed), and restarted my session.  However, upon opening up Nautilus and Terminal, I find that the menu has not returned.  How do I get my menu back?

Thanks.

EDIT; Found Solution, in gconf-editor, changed the BOOL of /apps/gnome_settings_daemon/gtk-modules/globalmenu-gnome from true to false.
