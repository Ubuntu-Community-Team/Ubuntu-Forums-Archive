### Post by MechaMechanism on 2009-07-02
Since the speedy JavaScript in Firefox 3.5 is not available in 64 bit, the 3.5 release is more of an very minor point release for 64 bit users.  Now I have heard that Firefox 4 will include 64 bit speedy JavaScript but don't hold me to that.  The main thing that I find annoying is that Mozilla and Canonical did not feel the need to communicate this point to end users.  I wonder how many 64 bit users are unaware of this issue and will be sorely disappointed in 3.5.  For myself I have no plans to upgrade as it makes no sense since 3.5 is no faster than 3.0 and 3.0 will be fully supported at least through the release of Karmic.  Once Karmic comes around then I'll upgrade along with everything else, but until then there's no point in going out of my way.

This issue could have been communicated better by Mozilla and Canonical. :frown:
