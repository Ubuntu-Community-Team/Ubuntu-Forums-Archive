### Post by 3Miro on 2009-03-27
AMD64 refers to both AMD and Intel 64-bit architecture.

VBox is free so unless you need something that vmware cannot do for you, I would go for Vbox. Consider both the OSE that comes form the repositories and the free edition that comes from virtualbox.com (it has USB support and other extras).

You can tun either 64 or 32-bit hosts, again it depends on what you want to do. It depends on what kind of software you wish run under the guest.
