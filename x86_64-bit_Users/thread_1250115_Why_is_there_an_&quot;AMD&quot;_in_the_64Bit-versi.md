### Post by tenghoward on 2009-08-28
IA-32e
EM64T
Intel 64
AMD64
x64
x86-64

All refers to the same thing. Just that AMD invented the instruction set first. That is why it called AMD64.

To be very technical, there ARE differences between Intel 64 and AMD64. The differences, however, are SO MINOR that they behave pretty much same.
