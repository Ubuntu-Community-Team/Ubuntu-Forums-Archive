### Post by Stunts on 2008-12-15
Civilization 4 runs quite well on wine.
You can follow the how-to to make it run in [www.winehq.org/](www.winehq.org/)
I play it often on my Ubuntu x64. It has however, 2 glitches: The textures in some leaders' eyes do not render prpperly making them look, well, scarry. The city construction bars do not display correctly on the main map.
Everything else works just like in windows.
Good luck!
