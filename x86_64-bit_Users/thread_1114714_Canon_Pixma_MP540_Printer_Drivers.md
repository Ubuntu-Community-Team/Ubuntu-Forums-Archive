### Post by 3Miro on 2009-04-03
The 64-bit part might be a problem. For the IP series for example, Cannon simply refuses to release 64-bit drivers.

I got my problem "resolved" with VirtualBox. I instaled a 32-bit version of Ubuntu under VB. Then I go to the cups settings and share the printer over the virtual network. Then I go to the host cups settings and select "see shared printers". Now I can just go to Open Office or FireFox and just hit print and it prints.

Let me know if you need help with such a set up. The ideal solution is a 64-bit driver, look around before you mess with VirtualBox.
