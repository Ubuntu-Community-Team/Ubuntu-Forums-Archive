### Post by Jouke74 on 2008-09-10
Yes a major annoyance. Also I have noticed this happening when converting documents with page numbers Word > Openoffice. Not to mention what happens if your file contains lots of large images and graphs.

You can try to install the MS core fonts package. Finally, you might want to check your paper settings. A4 vs American letter, and maybe your DPI settings.

Another option is to save Word images already under DOC format. SUN has brought out a tool to implement that.
