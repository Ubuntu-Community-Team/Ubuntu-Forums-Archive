### Post by ian-weisser on 2014-10-12
No. Ubuntu is not a rolling release.
What you see in the 14.10 repositories is, with minor exceptions, what you will see on release day (October 23).

Debian Import Freeze was August 7.
Gimp 2.8.14 was uploaded to Debian after August 7. It was uploaded on September 21, according to [https://tracker.debian.org/pkg/gimp](https://tracker.debian.org/pkg/gimp) . 
Look for it in Ubuntu 15.04 .
