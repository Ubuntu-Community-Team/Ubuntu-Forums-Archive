### Post by cariboo on 2013-04-10
Where are your scripts located? in /home/<user>, or some other location? have you tried setting the permissions from the command line? Just open a terminal and type:

```
chmod +x <script_name>
```

if the scripts are located in other than your home directory:

```
sudo chmod +x <script_name>
```
