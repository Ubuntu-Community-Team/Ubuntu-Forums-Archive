### Post by #&amp;thj^% on 2018-09-29
Try either [TopIcons Plus]("https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/1031/topicons/") or [Unite extension.]("https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/1287/unite/") Please take the time to read about each to decide which will work for you. 
This the best I myself can offer I am not a TopIcons-Plus user myself and not a Gnome user anymore.

Moreover, the Gnome project is pushing towards dropping status icons and any kind of legacy tray, making the project useless on the long term and difficult to maintain due to bugs or lack of features that are out of its scope.

For the Topicons with 3.30 read a possible fix by user** "0x3ax"** On the Topicons link I posted.

> fix 3.30 ~/.local/share/gnome-shell/extensions/TopIcons@phocean.net/extension.js:181 replace: -tray.manage_screen(global.screen, Main.panel.actor); +tray.manage_screen(Main.panel.actor);
Let us know how it works out. :)
