### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2014-03-19
Ok, I figured out what happened.

Turns out the Unity cheat sheet won't work when workspaces is enabled. It works again if that is disabled. It has nothing to do with the other settings in ccsm. You can test it with even a live cd/usb.

I am not sure if this is intended or a bug. Though a bug is more likely.
