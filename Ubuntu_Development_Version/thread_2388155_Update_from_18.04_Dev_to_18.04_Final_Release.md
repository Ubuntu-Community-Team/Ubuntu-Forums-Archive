### Post by Smask on 2018-03-31
I will just add to Mr. Phelps comment that you might want to reinstall if you come from an upgraded install that is a release or more older and you have proposed enabled. Less than a month old from full release means that they're just making minor bugfixes at this point.

One of my boxes here lost all contents from displaying in the toolbar menus in LibreOffice, I had to hover the mouse over the empty buttons and see what the tooltips said to get any work done. Later on I fixed the problem by purging L.O. and reinstalling it.

Yes, I have proposed enabled on all my machines here. I'm old enough that in the beginning configuring your computer meant fiddling with jumpers and dip-switches. And when the kernel spewed some error code you just emailed the Finnish bloke who wrote it.
