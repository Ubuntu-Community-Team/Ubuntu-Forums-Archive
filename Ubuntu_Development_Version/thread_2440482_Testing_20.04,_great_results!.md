### Post by hannu-h on 2020-04-11
I am back to Ubuntu after years with related distros, Debian, Mint etc. I am glad to report that the Ubuntu 20.04 DV works remarkably well. Hardware detection worked perfectly with my rather new AMD computer, Gigabyte AMD X570 Gaming, AMD Ryzen 7 3700 X, AMD Radeon RX 5500 XT, Lenovo 3840x2160 monitor. 
 In addition to general computing, I do large projects of photography, image editing and video editing. First time my related programs, Gimp, Rawtherapee, Kdenlive, Openshot, Ffmpeg, Totem, all work on Wayland. 
 The Wayland session is now my default, it not only works but is fast, responsive and reliable. This at a time when many Linux users are still wondering if it is ever going to work. 
 Thanks to the Ubuntu development team, great work!
 Hannu
