Please see this thread on the subject

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2315727](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2315727)

Show the menus for a window - in the menu bar - in the window's title bar. 

Menus visibility - Displayed on mouse hovering - Always displayed.

As stated, it may take a reboot for changes to take effect but from then on changes take effect immediately I am finding.
