### Post by Icarus315 on 2013-10-04
I had a BIOS system but a GPT partitioned drive.  The Beta Final installer barfed on it when I told it to format and use the entire drive.  Booting from a different live-cd and configuring the hard-drive to an MBR scheme allowed the Beta Final installer to operate.
