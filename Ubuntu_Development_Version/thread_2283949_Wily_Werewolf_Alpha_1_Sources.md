### Post by PaulW2U on 2015-06-26
Your signature says that you are using Ubuntu rather than one of the flavours. Ubuntu did not participate in the Alpha 1 release anyway.

An Alpha or Beta is just a snapshot of development on the release date. Wily sources are Wily sources, nothing different for any of the milestones.

By all means test the milestones but keep updating what you have installed and you'll have the full Wily in October.

---

### Post by steveo314 on 2015-06-26
Whatever is in your sources.list or update manager is what is used unless you add a ppa.

And say in November if you want to go to the next development version (16.04), then you would just change 'wily' in your sources.list to the next codename.
