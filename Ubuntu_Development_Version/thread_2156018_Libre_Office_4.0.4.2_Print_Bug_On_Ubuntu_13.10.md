### Post by dino99 on 2013-06-20
Go to Format > page .  Set your size to A4 and portrait.

After that go to File > Templates > Save

Give it a name.

Go to File > Templates > Organize

Double click on "My Templates" and right click on your template.  Choose "Set as Default Template" .

Done!  This will be the page coming up when you open LO-Writer.
