### Post by ventrical on 2014-09-10
Testing xubuntu-utopic-daily/current amd64 bit .iso live session and I am very impressed at how it has developed.  I hope nobody takes this the wrong way but it appears that xubuntu desktop has  adopted  a sort of slight  theme that is similar to the earlier Linux Mint desktops. I mean to say it is a lot smoother and not as choppy and the pull down panel has a smoother look and response to it.

  There is a great advantage that xubuntu has over ubuntu unity-desktop as far as accessability goes. That is that I can use the macro hot-keys to increase the size of the text/page/images and the mouse hovers exactly  where all the field links are, no matter how big they are. With Ubuntu-unity it will always be off the mark and I have to zoom down at times to get the mouse to behave properly on the increased size of the links.

  I filed a bug about this back a while ago. haven't got it listed in my sidekick. My bad.
