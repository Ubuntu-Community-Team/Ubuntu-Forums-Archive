### Post by deadflowr on 2016-02-07
KP = keypad as in the 10 key pad, if your desktop has one.

You can set drag and drop with mouse, in the Corners and Edges section of the grid plugin in ccsm (the compizconfig-settings-manager)
Drag and drop is defaulted to only drag and drop to the top or the full left or right sides of the screen.
