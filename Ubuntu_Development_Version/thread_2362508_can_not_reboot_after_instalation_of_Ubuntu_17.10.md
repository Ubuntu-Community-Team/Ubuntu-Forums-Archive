### Post by HereInOz on 2017-05-29
I am not sure how far you are getting with the reboot, but I have noticed that with some machines and 17.04 the reboot stalls and needs a hard power off to finish it.  After the hard power off, the system will often boot up OK from the hard disk.  You may have already done a hard power off and then had the subsequent boot up fail, but if not, give it a go.
