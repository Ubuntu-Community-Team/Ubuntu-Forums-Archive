### Post by rrnbtter on 2017-04-18
Greetings,
I am a Unity 7 user and no fan of Gnome Desktop, however last weekend (April 15th) I decided to put Gnome Ubuntu 64bit  on my wife's 10" Intel Atom netbook. I did this due to the greatly reduced system demands of Gnome Ubuntu vs Unity.  
Results = a very much robust performance on the lighter netbook system . And this is the interesting part, my wife actually likes it! Much to my amazement I have found that it is possible for two people living in the same house to like two completely different desktops. So far the dreaded network bug hasn't reared it ugly head on the Gnome system. Also the multi desktop panel on the right side of the display is handy on the small screen. So far, so good. 
Personally I think I will continue using Unity until the bitter end if the end comes. Just in case we will be happily testing Gnome on my wife's system.
