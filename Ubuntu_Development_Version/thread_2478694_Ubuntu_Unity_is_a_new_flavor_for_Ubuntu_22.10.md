### Post by guiverc on 2022-09-04
I don't see them as a new *flavor* yet, just 99.9% there.

My summary of how I see it can be found in UWN 751

> [h=3]Ubuntu Unity as an official flavor[/h] Steve  Langasek thanks the Ubuntu Unity team for their submission, and writes  that they have "met the requirements for inclusion as a daily flavor"  and thus are included in daily builds. On successful completion of the  22.10 Beta milestone, Ubuntu Unity will become an official Ubuntu  flavor. 


[https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/technical-board/2022-August/002669.html](https://lists.ubuntu.com/archives/technical-board/2022-August/002669.html)

ie. official *flavor* status is *almost* guaranteed, they just have to not blow up between now & *kinetic beta* and then they're official.  :)

*kinetic* ISOs aren't building now due to *problems* (*detected in CI*), thus until that gets resolved, people can't test out the new *daily *Ubuntu Unity builds.. If there were building they'd show older packages than were in their unofficial Ubuntu Unity ISOs as they included some newer 7.6 Unity packages from PPAs (*official builds will not include*)... however Rudra (& team) are already busy uploading them so they're available soon, and maybe they'll get that done before *dailies *return to being available anyway.

It's a job well done for sure.
