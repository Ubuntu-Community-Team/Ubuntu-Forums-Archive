### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-07-13
Trying to install Plasma Next on today's Kubuntu 14.10 daily. I'm seeing some odd behavior:

1. Installed from the daily, updated and dist-upgraded, rebooted.
2. Added the kubuntu-next ppa.
3. Updated and dist-upgraded. All packages installed without error.
4. Logged out. Logged in. Still the 4.13 desktop. 
5. Muon Updater popped up.  Ran it, but it quite immediately without displaying any messages.
6. Did apt-get update, apt-get upgrade, and apt-get dist-upgrade.  Apt wants autoremove of a few dozen "kf5" packges.
7. Against my better judgement, an "apt-get autoremove" removes all those packages.
8. And.. an immediate update/dist-upgrade re-installs all the packages just autoremoved, and the suggestion to autoremove them is re-displayed.
9. Goto 6

So, I'm stuck in this loop:  apt wants to autoremove packages, then reinstalls them, wants to autremove them.....

And, there's no sign of the new desktop.

### Post by zika on 2014-07-13
Break the loop by not using/accepting_offered dist-upgrade. Easy to do and useful..
Just do one```
sudo at-get install -f
```ro be sure everything is correctly set up...
Read always what is proposed and do not approve removals You're not sure about...
Common and very often visited point of Ubuntu life, especially in U+1.
