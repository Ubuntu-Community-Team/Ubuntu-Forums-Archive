### Post by dino99 on 2016-04-15
[http://mjg59.dreamwidth.org/41713.html](http://mjg59.dreamwidth.org/41713.html)

if the video was working well under willy, then try reinstalling the same nvidia driver version.
But first clean the system as much you can: clean/autoclean/autoremove/gtkorphan/bleachbit. Also remove xorg.conf if needed (a borked one often ends with troubles)
And you can also PURGE all the installed video drivers, then reinstall them. Logs can give you some ideas to fix the issue too (mainly xorg.0.log & journalctl)
