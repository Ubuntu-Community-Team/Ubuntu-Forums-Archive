### Post by raptir on 2015-03-02
You should be able to move them freely. Right click on the item you want to move, uncheck lock, right click again and click move. Then you can move the items wherever you want and relock them.

[ATTACH=CONFIG]260395[/ATTACH]

My guess is that it has something to do with the way the VMWare handles resizing the display, though I do not run into the issue with VirtualBox.
