### Post by ventrical on 2015-03-24
I was working with a problem system during this cycle that presented re-boot problems in that it would not completely shut down or re-boot and stay on unless I removed power cords. I checked memory, hdds .. etc.. It has a new ASUS ATi HD 5450 Silent video adapter card installed. I finally removed the PCIe adapter card and booted on it's onboard 965Qx86 MMX SSE2 intel  chipset. Voila'..  problem solved.  

  There must be a conflict between the adpater and that particular motherboard because I had the ATi card working on another machine that ran the unity-next-mir very well.  During all of my testing during this cycle I had noticed that Intel Graphics has presented the least problems. It must be  the developers choice to work with :)
