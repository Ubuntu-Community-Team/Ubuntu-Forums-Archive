### Post by P-I H on 2017-12-13
Nice discovery.

I have Dash to Dock installed and get the window to change settings in Dash to Dock.
Dash to Dock supports many more settings than Ubuntu Dock.
I use some:
I move the "Show Applications" icon from the bottom to the top of the Launcher.
I have change the behavior of the click action on icons in the Launcher to minimize a running application.
I have also set opacity to always be 70%.

The extension "Ubuntu Dock Settings" do not work as Gnome desktop 3.26 isn't supported.
