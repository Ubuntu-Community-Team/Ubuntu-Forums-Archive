After an unattended install of 14.10 beta2 desktop, /etc/init.d/rc
terminates after calling lightdm start, no other scripts in /etc/rc2.d are executed.
This is during the S to 2 runlevel transition.  It feels like some kind of signal propagated up
through the controlling tty, but there are no messages output on the console.

The original symptom was that /etc/rc.local didn't execute.  I have narrowed
it down lightdm start but am unsure how to proceed further.

At this point I am looking for suggestions on how to proceed.

It's using /etc/init.d/rc, not systemD (if I understand things correctly).
