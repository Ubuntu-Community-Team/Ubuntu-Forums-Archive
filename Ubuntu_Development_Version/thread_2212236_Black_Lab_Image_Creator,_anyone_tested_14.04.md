### Post by rjdohnert2 on 2014-05-24
Yes Black Lab Imager does work with Ubuntu 14.04.  One of the changes we made is that Black Lab Imager will definitely work with LTS releases, but its going to be hit or miss on incremental releases.  The problem being that there are only 2 of us who work on it, very little contribution to it from the outside and keeping up with every ubuntu release is a pain in the neck.  We fell behind after we took it over and missed the 12.10 and 13.04 releases because by the time we got it working with 12.10, here came 13.04 and 13.10.
