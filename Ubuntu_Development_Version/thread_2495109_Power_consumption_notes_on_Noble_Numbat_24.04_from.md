### Post by kaushik-ghose on 2024-02-06
I'm on a ThinkPad E14 G4 i5 running Noble Numbat. I went from 22.04.3. 

I *think* the waking power consumption is better with 24.04. 


When I do light tasks (editing using VS Code with a few linting plugins active) my 22.04 consumption used to be 8-10W. On 24.04 it is ~6W. I'm always on power saver mode. 


The power draw in sleep is indistinguishable, which I estimate to be 0.3 W on 22.04 and 0.288 W on 24.04.


Wake from sleep has generally been fine, though with both 22.04 and 24.04 I will have the occasion when the keyboard does not work, or the mouse will not work, or wake up takes a long time. Mostly though it has been reliable.

UI-wise, I am enjoying the popup menu that lets me mess with WiFi and display. I especially like that I can switch night lite on and off so easily now.

Thanks for all your work!
