### Post by Bucky Ball on 2014-02-13
Welcome. All posts regarding 14.04 LTS go here. It is still testing and not released until April. There will be problems. Expect them and the unexpected. 

The purpose of this sub-forum is to report bugs and discuss the testing of the new release. Operative word being 'testing'. 

If you are looking for a reliable release try 12.04 LTS (very stable) or 13.10 which will both upgrade directly to 14.04. Otherwise, consider it testing and not advised for production, stability or work environment. 

You observations will be helpful here, though, if you wish to continue with 14.04. ;)
