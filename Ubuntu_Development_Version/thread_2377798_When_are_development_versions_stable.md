### Post by dino99 on 2017-11-16
Since Karmic, the 'quality' process is used, and that makes a huge difference compared to previous releases. As you might have seen, now multiple build-tests are also at work and have to get positive, before landing into the proposed archive. At that step, it always could happen some unknown conflict or require more dependency to be also updated. If no test fail and no bug known, then the package move from 'proposed' to 'main' archive. That is where you can say the version is stable. So stay with 'main' archive and unselect 'proposed' archive as devs have always recommend (software-properties-gtk).
