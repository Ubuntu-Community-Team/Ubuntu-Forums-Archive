### Post by Doug S on 2014-03-14
Not that it is relevant, but I use a different time for "Turn screen off when inactive for:" and "Lock screen after:"
A couple of days ago it broke, such that now the screen turns back on after the extra lock time and stays on (if the blank time and the lock time are the same, it just stays on).

If you are observing this issue, then click the "effects me also" thingy in the [bug report]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gnome-screensaver/+bug/1292041") (to turn up the heat).

If you have the issue (very rare for me) of having to unlock twice, then see and bump this [bug report]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/gnome-screensaver/+bug/1291365").
