### Post by Claus7 on 2022-10-20
Hello,

checking ubuntu this was one of the first things I noticed missing from flashback: no ability to mute and unmute, and while unmuting, to restore sound to the level it was before.
At that time there was an extension available: Mute/Unmute from mcastaldo. If you install it, you will be able to both mute/unmute with three fingers on touchpad simultaneously, while having your mouse on top of speaker icon, and at the same time be able to do exactly what you mention: the latter was not possible I think until recently.

Regards!
