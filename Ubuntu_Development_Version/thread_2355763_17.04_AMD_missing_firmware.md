### Post by zika on 2017-03-17
Two ways to resolve:
1. Just disregard those warnings (they are warnings not errors)
or
2. Collect those files [from the net]("https://people.freedesktop.org/~agd5f/radeon_ucode/polaris/") and put them in places giiven in those warnings...
or
3. Or grab and install package: [https://packages.ubuntu.com/bionic/all/linux-firmware/filelist](https://packages.ubuntu.com/bionic/all/linux-firmware/filelist)
