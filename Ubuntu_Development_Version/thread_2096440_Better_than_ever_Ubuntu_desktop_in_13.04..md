I started out in Linux with Karmic and struggled to success at the command prompt.  Maverick was super fast and really a fine system.  But truthfully what I compare my desktop to is Windows not the older Linux distros.  The reason is that I used MS since 1986 through all of the changes and evolutions and that is what I am used to.
