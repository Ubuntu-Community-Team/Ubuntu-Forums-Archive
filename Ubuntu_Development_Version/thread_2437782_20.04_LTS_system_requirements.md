[https://discourse.ubuntu.com/t/boosting-the-real-time-performance-of-gnome-shell-3-34-in-ubuntu-19-10]("https://discourse.ubuntu.com/t/boosting-the-real-time-performance-of-gnome-shell-3-34-in-ubuntu-19-10/13095")/ which despite the title, includes aims for 20.04 & 20.10 releases)

My desktop is a decade old (dell optiplex from 2009) and runs 20.04 fine anyway; though I prefer lighter LXQt (Lubuntu) or XFCE (Xubuntu) which both perform faster, but even ignoring speed I prefer those desktops anyway over gnome.
