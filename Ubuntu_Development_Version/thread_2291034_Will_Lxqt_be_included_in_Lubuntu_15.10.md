[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lubuntu/Testing](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lubuntu/Testing)

> News
Work continues on integrating LXQt into Lubuntu, but we'll likely not see it released until 16.10 (Y cycle). If you're curious about development, check out the blueprints.
There is a prototype of Lubuntu with LXQT (aka lubuntu-next) available. Info and instructions from the boss included.
LXQt can also be installed on an existing system by installing lxqt-metapackage from the lubuntu-daily PPA.
