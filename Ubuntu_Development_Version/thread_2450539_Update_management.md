### Post by P-I H on 2020-09-16
In case you have installed gnome-software there is a new way to manage updates. It's done in the same way as in Fedora and similar to W10. Number of updates are shown in the Updates tab in gnome-software.
When you click the tab you get a list of the updates and the tab "Restart & Update". When you click this tab the system is rebooted and the update process is shown.
The first two pictures show info in gnome-software before the update and the las picture info after the update.
