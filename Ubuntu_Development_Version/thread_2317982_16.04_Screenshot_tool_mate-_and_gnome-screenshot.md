I compared
[http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/xenial/en/man1/mate-screenshot.1.html](http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/xenial/en/man1/mate-screenshot.1.html)
and
[http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/xenial/en/man1/gnome-screenshot.1.html](http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/xenial/en/man1/gnome-screenshot.1.html)

For some reason, the mate version is without
[LIST]
[*] -c, --clipboard *Send the grab directly to the clipboard.*
[*] -p, --include-pointer *Include the pointer with the screenshot.*
[*] -f, --file=FILENAME *Save screenshot directly to this file.*
[/LIST]

And mate's *-e, --border-effect=EFFECT* is without *"vintage" (desaturating the screenshot slightly, tinting it and adding rectangular space around it)*.
