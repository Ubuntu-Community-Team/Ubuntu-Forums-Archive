### Post by esteeven on 2018-04-22
I have an updated 18.04 box. It was running as expected until yesterday, after an update. There are 4 accounts, three of which log in to Gnome-Shell without issues. One account - the administrator account - won't log in. Log in returns to GDM. This acocunt has more extensions than the other accounts so I removed them from .local/share but that had no effect. I then removed all . files and directories with rm -rf .* . This had no effect. I am trying to log in to Xorg but the same thing happens with Wayland and on an XFCE session. i3 logs in fine.

Puzzled!!

---

### Post by esteeven on 2018-04-22
It seems that the Workpaces To Dock extension is the culprit. Unfortunately, only a reboot put everything back to normal once this extension was removed. Problem solved but still puzzled. :)
