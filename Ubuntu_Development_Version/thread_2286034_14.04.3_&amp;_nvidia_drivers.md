### Post by mc4man on 2015-07-09
14.04.3 will use the lts-vivid kernel (3.19) & currently 14.04 only offers 2 nvidia driver, 304 & 331
331 is broken on the 3.19 kernel so as it now stands only 304 will be usable from official repos.

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/nvidia-graphics-drivers-331-updates/+bug/1409190](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/nvidia-graphics-drivers-331-updates/+bug/1409190)

Laptop users of recent nvidia gpu's (optimus) will be out of luck as 304 doesn't support nvidia-prime.

Easily fixed by providing a 346 or newer nvidia package for 14.04 but no movement on that 
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/nvidia-drivers-ubuntu/+bug/1461958](https://bugs.launchpad.net/nvidia-drivers-ubuntu/+bug/1461958)

(whatever happened to the testing tracker site? - [http://iso.qa.ubuntu.com/](http://iso.qa.ubuntu.com/)
