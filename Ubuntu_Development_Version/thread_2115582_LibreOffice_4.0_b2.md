### Post by Harry33 on 2013-02-13
The newest prerelease of LibreOffice (4.0 b2) will soon hit the RR repos.
Here:
[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/raring/+source/libreoffice/1:4.0.0~beta2-0ubuntu1](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/raring/+source/libreoffice/1:4.0.0~beta2-0ubuntu1)

There is build dependency wait, until the package libmspub-dev is in repos.

However, you can get this same LibreOffice 4.0 b2 from this PPA:
[https://launchpad.net/~libreoffice/+archive/libreoffice-prereleases](https://launchpad.net/~libreoffice/+archive/libreoffice-prereleases)

I installed it replacing the LibreOffice 3.6 b2 and it works just fine.
