### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-03-12
I'd second what howefield has written. I have 16.04 on another partition which is fine with some things but crashy with obscure things, so yes, unless you're prepared for a little breakage and a possibly bumpy ride, possibly smoother to go for the upgrade 14.04 or 16.04 clean option.

You could go to 14.04 LTS then 16.04 LTS later in the year when it has settled down, but then again, you could just clean install 16.04 LTS in a month or so. Up to you.

14.04 LTS is an LTS so you can upgrade that to 16.04 LTS anytime until 2019. No rush. :)
