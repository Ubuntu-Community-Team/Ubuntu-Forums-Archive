> Hello everybody,

I have been hosting regular Google+ Hangouts on Air for a while now and
they've been a great and fun way to demo and talk about what we're
doing, get new people involved and get people to ask questions they
might have.

If you or your team want to be part of this, I set up a schedule on the
wiki where you can easily add yourself and what you want to talk about.

  [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuDevelopment/Hangouts](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuDevelopment/Hangouts)

It'd be great if you would join in, talk about what you're doing, demo
something, explain whatever you want talk about related to Ubuntu
development, as it will make our work more open to the world and get you
in touch with contributors and people interested in Ubuntu.

If you have any questions, please let me know.
