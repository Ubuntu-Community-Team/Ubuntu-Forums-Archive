Quoting from the UWN #623

> [B]*[SIZE=4]Canonical managed services and Ubuntu support during COVID-19 outbreak[/SIZE]*

Mark Shuttleworth writes about how COVID-19 is expected to impact Canonical/Ubuntu’s support; in that it isn't. Due to the remote work environment already in place for most teams, there are minor impacts. Those who worked in offices are now also working from home. Finally Mark reminds us that it's a testing time, he provides links where updates will be provided, and where team members can be contacted.[/B]
