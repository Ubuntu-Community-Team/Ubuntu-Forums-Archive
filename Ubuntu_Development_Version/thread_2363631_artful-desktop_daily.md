### Post by P-I H on 2017-06-12
Installed today.
Much is working with Wayland.
Installed Kodi in snap version. It works OK, which is quite amazing
Installed Chrome, but you have to install "Gnome Shell integration" manually. Further installed Gnome Tweak Tool and Psensor.
Installed these gnome-extensions
- Better volume indicator
- Dash to Dock 
- OpenWeather
- Status Title Bar
- No Topleft Hot Corner
- Alternate Tab
- TopIcons Plus 
- system-monitor (requires gir1.2-gtop-2.0 och gir1.2-networkmanager-1.0)
Made some settings in the top bar and in Dash to Dock.
There are some differences compared to Gnome without Wayland. Settings in OpenWeather don't works in Wayland but works in Gnome without Wayland. However presentation after data settings works fine in Wayland. The Psensors indicator isn't shown in Wayland.
