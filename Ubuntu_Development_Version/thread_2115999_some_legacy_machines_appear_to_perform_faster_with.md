### Post by ventrical on 2013-02-14
I was just following up on another topic and decided to load up one machine with a bunch of programs and noticed right away that the performance of that machine and raring install seemed to auto-boost it's performance , especially the Unity interface and app switcher. I am not saying that this will happen on all machines but some legacy (and even newer machines) do have the capability to increase continuous power (in some cases to slight over voltage) and this extra power will exhibit the properties of appearing to free up what otherwise might seem to be a log-jam on the system bus.
