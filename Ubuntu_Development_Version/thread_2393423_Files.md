### Post by PaulW2U on 2018-06-03
Are you seeing the [Single click in icon view not working in nautilus]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/nautilus/+bug/1758745") bug?

Do you have the experimental views checked in Preferences | Views | Experimental? If so, uncheck the box, restart Files and single-click should start working again. At least that is what I'm seeing here.
