### Post by zika on 2018-02-11
You could always grab a file from a place like [this]("https://git.kernel.org/pub/scm/linux/kernel/git/firmware/linux-firmware.git/tree/radeon") and put it in appropriate place on Your disk... ;)
Hint:```
/lib/firmware/radeon/kabini_ce.bin
/lib/firmware/radeon/kabini_me.bin
/lib/firmware/radeon/kabini_mec.bin
/lib/firmware/radeon/kabini_pfp.bin
/lib/firmware/radeon/kabini_rlc.bin
/lib/firmware/radeon/kabini_sdma.bin
/lib/firmware/radeon/kabini_sdma1.bin
/lib/firmware/radeon/kabini_uvd.bin
/lib/firmware/radeon/kabini_vce.bin
```
Or grab and install package: [https://packages.ubuntu.com/bionic/all/linux-firmware/filelist](https://packages.ubuntu.com/bionic/all/linux-firmware/filelist)
