### Post by buzzmandt on 2011-07-30
um, I don't think so
kde 4.7 integrated messaging is telepathy-kde.  Telepathy-kde is "not ready to be packaged along side kde and will be included at this time"  Unless he built it from source it is not kde 4.7 integrated messaging.  And I'd be if he had built it from source he wouldn't be asking what it is.  (Also available in PPA)
sources:
[http://www.kde.org/announcements/4.7/](http://www.kde.org/announcements/4.7/)
quote (emphasis mine)
> The KDE-Telepathy team is proud to announce the technical preview and historic first release of the new IM solution for KDE. Even though it's still in its early stages, you can already set up all sorts of accounts, including GTalk and Facebook Chat and use them in your everyday life. The chat interface lets you choose among many appearances with its support of Adium themes. You can also put the Presence Plasma widget right into your panel to manage your online status. **As this project is not yet mature enough to be part of the big KDE family, it is packaged and released separately, alongside the other major parts of KDE. The source code for KDE-Telepathy 0.1.0 is available on download.kde.org. Installation instructions are available on community.kde.org.**
