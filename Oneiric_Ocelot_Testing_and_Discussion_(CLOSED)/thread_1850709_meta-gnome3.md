### Post by jbicha on 2011-09-27
Those of you who attempt to remove Unity to replace it with GNOME Shell might be interested in meta-gnome3. It hasn't yet made it into the archives but you can manually install the packages.

meta-gnome3 is the new GNOME 3 version of the Debian GNOME metapackages which are sort of similar to the ubuntu-desktop metapackage. The vast majority of Ubuntu users don't need them. (I don't keep them installed as I don't have much use for them, nor do I have any reason to remove Unity.)
