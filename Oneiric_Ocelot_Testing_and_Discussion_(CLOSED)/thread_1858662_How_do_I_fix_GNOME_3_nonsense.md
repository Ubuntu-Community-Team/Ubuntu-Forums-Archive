### Post by VinDSL on 2011-10-13
GS is broken, on my install, right now, but...

In the Unity Dash, when you type "menu"... "Main Menu" pops up.  (I assume GS works the same).

Check out the "Main Menu" app, and you'll probably find some categories where the entries are double-checked.

For instance, in my install, Opera-Next is checked twice, in the "Internet" menu category.  This causes the Dash to display it twice.

Truthfully, I don't care enough to uncheck one of them.  I think it's funny.  LoL!

But, if I did care, that's how I would go about fixing the double entries, e.g. uncheck one or the other, in the "Main Menu".  ;)
