### Post by MacUntu on 2011-06-18
If you get "empty" icons in the launcher bar and Nautilus won't start, then you are hitting this one: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/nautilus/+bug/799007](https://bugs.launchpad.net/nautilus/+bug/799007)

Installing the package gnome-icon-theme-full fixes it for me.
