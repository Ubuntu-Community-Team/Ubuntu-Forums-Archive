### Post by VinDSL on 2011-05-13
Thought I should report this...

After I upgraded to pre-alpha Oneiric Ocelot, my Chromium bookmarks, theme, other miscellaneous settings, et cetera, disappeared. 

I tried all the usual stuff, new profile, import bookmarks, installed the UbuOO version of Chromium from the repos, blah, blah, blah.  Nothing worked.

Finally, I:

[LIST]
[*]Booted into 10.10
[*]Opened Google-Chrome
[*]Sync'ed to Google servers
[*]Restarted 11.04
[*]Opened Chromium
[*]Sync'ed to Google servers
[/LIST]

Bingo!  Everything was back.  Whew!

I love Firefox, but it isn't Chromium/Chrome, you know?!?!?  :D
