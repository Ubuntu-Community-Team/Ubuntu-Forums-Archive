This is odd.
I have tested both Oneiric's Chromium (v. 13) and beta Chromium from PPA (v 14).
Both these have problems to work with the dev version of libcairo2 (1.11.3+git20110926) from xorg-edgers PPA. Flashplugin just crashes immediately and every time.
Very repeatable.

However, Firefox works OK with that dev libcairo2.

And, Chromium (and Firefox) work well with the stable libcairo2 (1.10.2) from Oneiric's repos.
