### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-06-28
The Global Menu Bar Integration add-on is also buggy and known to prevent Firefox from starting, among other problems. See [Firefox 4, 5 & Beyond Mega Thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1712247")

BTW, UnityFox add-on is experimental, which means the developer is still developing it or it hasn't been fully approved by Mozilla, only reviewed to ensure it is safe. You should avoid experimental add-ons if you want stability.
