### Post by mc4man on 2011-07-18
That depends on what you want to change it to - on a .jpg try a right click > properties > open with and if what you want is available either as a recommended or in the 'show other ..' pick it and set as default.
If you just want to see/add choices use the right click > open with - Other Application menu

I believe the default in O would actually be eog.desktop, you could ck. ~/.local/share/applications/mimeapps.list

(custom commands thru the context menu have been removed in gnome/nautilus 3, but can still be used if that's what you wanted, doubtful in this case - 

Edit: the old media tab is currently seen in System Settings, but just deals w/ removable media other stuff is in SS > system info
