Do you mean the pop-out drawers on the GNOME panel where you can store things? If so, check out xfce4-xf-applet,which lets you add GNOME panel applets to the xfce panel. Just install it, add it to a panel in Xubuntu, and add your drawers! I use xfce4-xf-applet for Talika, and it works well.
