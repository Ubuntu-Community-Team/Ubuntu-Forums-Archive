### Post by kansasnoob on 2011-09-01
No nvidia here so I can't comment about that, but I tested on two machines, one with Intel that runs 3D fine, and the other with VIA which will only run 2D.

There are a couple of superficial bugs in 2D, nothing big, and the Intel machine seems to run 3D fine.

My biggest complaint is that the dash (I think that's what it's called) is sloooooooooooow!

Even when we had separate app and fs menu buttons I found myself fumbling around, but this isn't a matter of me fumbling.

When I click on the dash it almost reminds me of dial-up. I'm used to things working so fast that if I blink it's done, now I actually have to wait.

The same is true after typing in a search for an app or a location. Slooooooow! I mean after I'm done typing, and hit enter I can actually **sit, watch, and wait**!

In reality I'm probably talking about an additional second or two but it's still visibly slower. Maybe my cheap 1500 and 1600 MHz CPU's just won't make the grade with Unity.

With the same underlying kernel in Lubuntu I'm as fast as always though.
