### Post by gunksta on 2011-09-02
Before filing a bug report, see if it will work with your external monitor. There is button in the top right of your desktop. It looks like a power button. It is in the extreme top right corner.

Click on it and then click on displays. This will pop up a configuration panel for monitors. Do you see one or two monitors. You can force the system to look for monitors and there are other options as well, including using both monitors at the same time.
