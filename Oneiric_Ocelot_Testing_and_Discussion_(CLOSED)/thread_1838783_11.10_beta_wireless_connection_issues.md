### Post by TunaCanTommy on 2011-09-04
Here's how I was able to fix the same problem:

1. Click on the wireless icon (top right)
2. Open "Edit Connections"
3. Open "Wireless" tab
4. Click on you SSID, and "Edit"
5. Open the "Wireless Security" tab
6. Choose your security mode and enter you key
7. Save and close . . . .

Should work OK.
