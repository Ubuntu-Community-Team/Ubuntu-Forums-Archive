### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-09-27
You should have gone to System Settings>System Info>Default Applications.

Up until the release of 10.10 we could assume that one release would be similar to another in many respects. There was enough rumour regarding 11.04 that we should have known that there would be differences. The same applies to 11.10.

So, when I installed 11.10 I went clicking everything in sight and not in sight. I do it again after every update to try to identify anything added in the last 24 hours.
