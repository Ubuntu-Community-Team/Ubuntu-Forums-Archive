### Post by manzdagratiano on 2011-10-10
Hi all!

Just thought I'd check in with how Oneiric's been looking for me so far - I upgraded about two weeks ago, one on my:
*) Sony VAIO VGNAR520E, which has an nvidia 8400GT, and other dreaded proprietary devices that are known to be problematic,
and another on my
*) Dell Vostro V13, which was a very well behaved laptop with integrated Intel graphics and other decently supported stuff since it came with Jaunty installed (I had wizened up by then!)

I had some early compiz crashes, but they seem to have been fixed and are gone. Some issues with getting the nvidia HDMI out to TV working properly (after selecting Twinview and clicking 'Ok' to accept, compiz had crashed) - but the nvidia drivers have received an update today so I shall check it out again.

Aside from that, working flawlessly! Unity is more awesome than before (I am content to not install Gnome-shell since my Arch install has it and I love it too over there), and it seems like Oneiric shall be a really solid release!
