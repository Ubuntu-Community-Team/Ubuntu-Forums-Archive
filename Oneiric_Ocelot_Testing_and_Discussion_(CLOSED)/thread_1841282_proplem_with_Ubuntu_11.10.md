### Post by gunksta on 2011-09-09
More or less, 11.04 and 11.10 both look more Mac-like than previous versions of Ubuntu. When you log in, you should have a menu bar across the top of the screen and a dock-like bar on the left hand side of the screen. I've attached a screenshot of an empty workspace on my system. Your screen will look a little different, I've got some applications running and I tend to remove some of the default stuff and re-arrange, but it should give you a good idea of what you are looking for.

If your system looks similar, you don't have any big problems. If your desktop looks nothing like mine, take a screenshot and we'll see what can be done. Also, to access the applications menu / dash, hit the "windows button" on your laptop or you can hit the big black looking button that you should see on the top-left of your screen.
