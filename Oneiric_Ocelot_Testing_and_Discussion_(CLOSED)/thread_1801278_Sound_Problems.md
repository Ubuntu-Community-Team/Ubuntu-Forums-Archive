Hi,
after upgrading to oneiric alpha 2 (from natty) I had no sound anymore,  ```
sudo chmod -R a+rwX /dev/snd
``` fixed the problem, (before that I deleted all .pulse* files, but this actually made the problem worse).

I hope this helps someone
