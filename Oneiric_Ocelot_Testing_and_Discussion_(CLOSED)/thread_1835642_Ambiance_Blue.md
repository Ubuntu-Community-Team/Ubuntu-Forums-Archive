I couldn't stand the orange title bars etc. in gnome shell any more so I downloaded the **Ambiance Blue** theme from here:

[https://satya164.deviantart.com/art/Ambiance-Blue-Theme-Suite-196833665](https://satya164.deviantart.com/art/Ambiance-Blue-Theme-Suite-196833665)

and then:

[LIST=1]
[*]Unzipped the archive
[*]Extracted "ambiance_blue.tar.gz"
[*]Moved the "Ambiance Blue" folder to ~/.themes
[*]Used GNOME Tweak Tool to change the theme
[/LIST]

After a reboot the horrible orange is gone - well except on the lightdm screen. I haven't bothered looking into how to change that.
