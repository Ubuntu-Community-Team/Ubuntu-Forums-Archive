### Post by effenberg0x0 on 2011-09-02
I just wanted to congratulate the Ubuntu development and support teams. I am surprised with the quality and stability of the current alpha version. 

I hated the new interface on Natty and the Unity bar at first, to the point of moving to other Linux distros. Right now, after using the improved versions in Ubuntu alpha release for a couple days, I became a fan. It takes a while, but it is a great improvement both visually and in terms of organization and productivity. 

There obviously is a big road ahead in terms of improvements: I am seeing some issues with installation, sound, poor performance of SATA 6.0 Gbps disks, incorrect use of AMD Phenom II X6 1100T speeds and governors, etc. But the overall status is good and promising.

So thank you for your efforts on this great software.

Regards,
