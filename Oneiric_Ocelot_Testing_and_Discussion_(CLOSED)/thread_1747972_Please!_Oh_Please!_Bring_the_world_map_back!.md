### Post by bond17_007 on 2011-05-08
Hi,
  The new clock still supports the multiple clock however the 'daylight map' is gone.
  To add the clock, you can simply click on the Clock on the task bar. Click on 'Time & Date Settings...' found at the bottom.
You should see a pop up come, click on 'Clock' tab.
Find 'Choose Locations...' button and click on it. Now you can add other cities.
By doing this you will see the cities listed when  you click on the Clock widget on the task bar.

I still miss the Daylight map... as you guys said, its nice to see know if they still have daylight etc.. hope this helps.

### Post by UnSandpiper on 2011-06-14
I was very disappointed that my fresh install of 11.04 was missing world map and weather indicators, but also wondering why my other installation, that I've been upgrading since about 9.10 still had the old clock.

So what I did was the following:
-remove the default "Indicator applet complete", which includes all indicators, the clock and the session buttons.
- from left to right add following indicators: "Indicator applet", "Clock" &  "Indicator applet session"

Now it looks exactly the same as before, but with the good ole clock applet.

And while you are at it, if you've been missing some icons in your panel, e.g. skype, guake etc. I also recommend adding the "notification area" panel.
