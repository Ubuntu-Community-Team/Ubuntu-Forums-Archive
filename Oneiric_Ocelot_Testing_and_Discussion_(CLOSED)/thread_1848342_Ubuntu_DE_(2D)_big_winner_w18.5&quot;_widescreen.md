### Post by kansasnoob on 2011-09-22
As much as I've complained about Unity I now find it quite usable, and almost preferable, with my new 18.5" wide-screen monitor :D

But with my larger 22" wide-screen it's nearly unusable due to my limited vision :(

It's kind of difficult to explain all aspects of the usability limitations but first, and foremost, is the unmovable top panel. Of course with the global menu it kind of needs to be there, but it's simply a neck breaker with a large monitor if you have multi-focal lens glasses.

Now, if I disable the global menu completely, and if I could move the panel to the bottom, with a few more minor tweaks I could fall in love with the Unity DE :D

Can you imagine the number of tweaks that will become available in the next year or so ?????????

In the meanwhile Lubuntu is an awesome alternative for me. The bottom line is; Ubuntu still rocks :guitar:
