Everybody has a different opinion on this.

Here's mine...

If you like working with Ubu dev releases, pre-alpha1 is a GREAT TIME to upgrade!

I do a fresh install, when the final release arrives. Then, as soon as possible, I upgrade to pre-alpha1.

IMO, pre-alpha1 is heaven on earth.  The devs are busy, regrouping, going to meetings, getting stoned (and whatever), and testers are given the keys to the fort, while they're gone.

Unfortunately, this pre-alpha1 period only lasts for a few weeks, then all hell breaks loose - which is fine with me.  I love chaos and mayhem.

Anyway, if you're into this sort of stuff, have at it.  Do a fresh install and keep on truckin'.

If you don't do a fresh install, you won't get the full effect when pre-alpha1 hits the repos.  ;)

Either way, wipe your partitions, and do a fresh install...
