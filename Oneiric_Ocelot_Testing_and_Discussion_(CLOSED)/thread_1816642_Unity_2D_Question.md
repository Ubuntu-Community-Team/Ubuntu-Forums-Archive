### Post by MARP1961 on 2011-08-03
The current Ubuntu 11.04 does not have 2D Unity as a default option but it is available for separate installation. Why don't you upgrade your old PCs to Natty Narwhal first. If they can't run 3D Unity they will log in to the old classic desktop just like Lucid or Maverick. Once installed and updated just open up Synaptic Package Manager and search for Unity 2D. When installed just log off and then log back in by clicking your name and selecting Unity 2D as a login option underneath.
