### Post by sureshkrshukla on 2010-03-04
I use Tata Photon + with ubuntu 9.04 and fedora 12 at home.

Works out of box and very good experience.

Summary of configuration :
- Plug the device
- click on the right top cornet network icon.
- mobile broadband icon tab
- number is #777
- username : internet
- passwd : internet

Exactly same steps for ubuntu 9.04 and fedora 12.

The method given in their Tata manual about wvdial utility is old and doesn't work.
