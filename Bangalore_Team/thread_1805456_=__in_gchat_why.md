### Post by saimanoj on 2011-11-14
It is not specific for just five stars.
If you enter any no. of stars greater than 2 they will reduced by 2.
But it is neither a bug nor an easter egg.
From my observation what i found is that if you place any text between 2 stars then it is converted to bold
e.g.
*hi*
will look like 
[B]hi
[/B]so, 
*****
looks like
*******
