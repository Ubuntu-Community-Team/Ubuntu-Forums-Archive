### Post by althara on 2010-01-09
At the January 3rd Meeting it was determined that the best leadership structure for the group is a 3 person committee. We also agreed on a two week nomination period and a one week voting period. See the meeting minutes here: [https://docs.google.com/Doc?docid=0AVu0CEKVbc6vZGdmemJxZG5fMjNkNnMzbnB2dw&hl=en](https://docs.google.com/Doc?docid=0AVu0CEKVbc6vZGdmemJxZG5fMjNkNnMzbnB2dw&hl=en)

We are opening the call for nominees today, January 9th.

The call for nominations closes on January 23rd.

The voting will open January 24th. We will be using [http://www.cs.cornell.edu/andru/civs.html](http://www.cs.cornell.edu/andru/civs.html) for voting.

Voting will end at January 31st.



The elections process and timeline are documented at: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WashingtonTeam/LeadershipElections](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WashingtonTeam/LeadershipElections)

The nominee template is at: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WashingtonTeam/LeadershipNominations](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WashingtonTeam/LeadershipNominations)

Please nominate yourself using the template and place your nomination page at: https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WashintonTeam/LeadershipNominations/January2010/<yourname>


-Linda
