Did you know that we have our own local Lucid software mirror. Located in Olympia, WA, the mirror includes the base mirror and extras, various software, to include Firefox 4 + beta channel, Handbrake, Dropbox, LibreOffice, VirtualBox4, and others. A complete sources.list is available at [http://nwlinux.us/sources.list]("http://nwlinux.us/sources.list").
