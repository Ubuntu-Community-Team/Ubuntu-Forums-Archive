### Post by Photonic Nature on 2007-11-27
Just wanted to let new users know that the reference to **Gaim** Instant Messenger found on this page: ([https://wiki.ubuntu.com/FloridaTeam/NewUser]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/FloridaTeam/NewUser")) may be a little confusing for you if you cannot find Gaim on your computer as instructed ... (depending on which version of ubuntu you are using).

That is because Gaim was scrapped and the project is now known as **Pidgin**. [URL="http://www.pidgin.im/"]http://www.pidgin.im/
[/URL]
If you do not see "Gaim" in your Internet menu, look for Pidgin instead. :)

However...

If you _do_ see Gaim in your menu, uninstall it through this menu sequence: 
**Applications** > **Add/Remove...**  (choice is at the bottom)

[LIST=1]
[*]Search for Gaim using the Search bar on top, then uncheck the box next to Gaim.
[*]Click on the **_A_pply Changes** button.
[*]Search for Pidgin and check that box.
[*]Again; Click on the **_A_pply Changes** button.
[*]Close the Add/Remove Applications window when it is finished.
[/LIST]

You will now find Pidgin in your** Internet** menu.

Enjoy!
