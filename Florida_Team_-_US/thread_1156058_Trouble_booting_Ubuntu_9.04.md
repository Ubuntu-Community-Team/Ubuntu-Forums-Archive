### Post by mlsquad on 2009-05-12
Is that you Derrick?  You had installed Ubuntu with Wubi, so it sat within your Windows partition.  If you reinstalled Windows then you may have wiped your Ubuntu install in the process.
Since you like Ubuntu and are in a sort of state of flux with your OS installs, and need Windows, you may be best served by partitioning and having Ubuntu installed on one partition and Windows on another.
Since you already have Windows installed, download and burn an Ubuntu CD and then reboot with it in your drive.  The installer will walk you through repartitioning your drive so that you can preserve your Windows side and build a new Ubuntu side.
With this setup you can reinstall Windows as much as you wish and preserve Ubuntu.
