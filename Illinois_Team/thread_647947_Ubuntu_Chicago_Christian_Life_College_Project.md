Hey all. Not to sure who reads the forums versus mailing lists and IRC logs. Oh well, here goes. 

Ubuntu Chicago is working on a project at Christian Life Colllege in Mt. Prospect, IL. What we are doing is upgrading the network infrastructure, and moving their systems away from Windows 2003 (on their servers) to Ubuntu. Also, we are trying to remove wifi dead spots, setting up a print sharing system, and securing the school from hack attempts in general. 

wiki.ubuntu.com/ChicagoTeam/Projects/CLC
