**_Who:_**
The Chicago Loco and *ANYONE* who is interested in joining us for the Doc Jam!

**_What:_**
Chicago Loco Documentation Jam!!

**_When:_**
Sunday, January 17th - 12:30pm to 5:30pm

Loco get-togethers are a GREAT way to meet fellow Ubuntu users in your area, and to have a good time with like-minded people. It's always fun and you're sure to come away enjoying the experience! The doc jam will be valuable for the experienced and new alike. It will be an opportunity to tackle some work, and will be a great chance to learn more about documentation!
