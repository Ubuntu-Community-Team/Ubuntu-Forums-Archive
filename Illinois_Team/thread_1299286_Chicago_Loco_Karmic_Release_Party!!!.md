**[COLOR="Red"]** Schedule Updated 10/25 **[/COLOR]**

Well everybody, it's about that time again! Karmic Koala is due to be released later this week, so we all know what that means...

\\:D/ **_Karmic Release Party!_** \\:D/

**_Who:_**
The Chicago Loco Team and *ANYONE* who is interested in joining us for the event!

**_What:_**
Karmic Koala Release Party!

**_Where:_**
Illinois Institute of Technology's Institute of Design 
350 North La Salle Street 
2nd Floor 
Chicago, Illinois 60654

The Institute of Design is within easy walking distance of the Merchandise Mart on the CTA Brown Line, the Grand stop of the CTA Red Line, or even Chicago's Union Station.

**_When:_**
Saturday, November 14th
12 noon - 3:00PM

**_Why:_**
Loco get-togethers are a GREAT way to meet fellow Ubuntu users in your area, and to have a great time with like-minded people. It's always a great time, and you're sure to come away enjoying the experience! Release parties are also a great place to delve into the changes for Karmic, get a hand with installing the new release, as well as a place to ask questions you may have about the new version.

**_RSVP:_**
If you're interested in joining us for the party, please add your information to the [Karmic Release Party wiki page]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ChicagoTeam/KarmicReleaseParty"). If you don't have a LaunchPad account, please feel free to let us know that you're interested, and we'll be glad to add your info for you :)

Also, feel free to bring drinks and snacks to the party! We would be happy to eat your cookies! If you plan on bringing something, add it to the RSVP info on the wiki as well.


**_Contact Chicago Loco Team:_**
**Team Wiki Page:** [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ChicagoTeam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ChicagoTeam)
**Mailing List:** [https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-chicago](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-chicago)

Thank you everybody, and I look forward to seeing you guys there :D
