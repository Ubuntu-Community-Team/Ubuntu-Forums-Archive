### Post by Antman on 2008-02-02
[www.suseforums.net](www.suseforums.net) has over 25,000 members and 221,795 posts

[www.opensuse.us](www.opensuse.us) has over 4282 members and 16668 posts

The official Novell forum has 3,865 members with 36,823 posts in the Suse section.

So I would say that the Suseforums.net is the most active with the novell site coming in second. I haven't really looked at any others.
