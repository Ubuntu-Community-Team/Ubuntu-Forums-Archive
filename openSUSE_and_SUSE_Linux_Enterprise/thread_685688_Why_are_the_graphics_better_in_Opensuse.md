### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-02-03
Are you running with the exact same resolution and refresh rate?

In Gnome I have two options for refresh rate: 50 hz and 51 hz. Surprisingly enough, the 50 hz option is slightly fuzzy, yet 51 hz is clean and crisp. If your monitor is the same sort of thing, you might have OpenSUSE automatically using 51 hertz, and Kubuntu using 50 hertz.

### Post by tgalati4 on 2008-02-03
Print out the xorg.conf files from both and you will see the differences.  The work around is to copy the tasty bits from OpenSUSE and copy them into Ubuntu's xorg.conf.  For me, the difference was running 99 Hz (SLED10) vs 85 Hz (Linux Mint 4 XFCE).
