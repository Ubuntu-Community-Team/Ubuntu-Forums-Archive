### Post by Incense on 2007-11-06
In case anyone else wants to know, I installed FECU from here:

[http://download.opensuse.org/repositories/Emulators/openSUSE_10.3/i586/fceu-0.98.13pre-1.1.i586.rpm]("http://download.opensuse.org/repositories/Emulators/openSUSE_10.3/i586/fceu-0.98.13pre-1.1.i586.rpm") 

This will give you a CLI to FCE Ultra. I wanted a GUI so I used Alien to change this [FILE]("http://dietschnitzel.com/gfceu/gfceu_0.6.0-0ubuntu1_all.deb") (The GUI to FEC ultra) into an RPM, then installed it using SMART. Works perfectly. Thanks again for the help!
