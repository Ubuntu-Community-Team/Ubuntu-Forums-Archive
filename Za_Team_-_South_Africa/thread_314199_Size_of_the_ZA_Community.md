A statistic I didn't see mentioned here is that we have 280-something people on the ubuntu-za mailing list.

Then, as far as people using Ubuntu that we aren't tracking, most people in the Cape Linux User Group ([http://wiki.clug.org.za](http://wiki.clug.org.za)) run Ubuntu, and a good number aren't involved in our list or community. There are many organisations using Ubuntu, like universities, schools and the CSIR etc who perhaps only have one or two representatives in our community.

So the real number of Ubuntu users in South Africa is much higher than we can track...
