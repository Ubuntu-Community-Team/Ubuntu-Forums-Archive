There are a few places offering Linux courses around here. I suggest googling for companies in Pretoria. In Jhb I recommend Obsidian systems, although their main focus is RH.

### Post by revanb on 2008-09-04
Hi Newbie

Since you live in Pretoria there is an easy way to obtain linux distros as long as you know a student at the University of Pretoria main campus (possibly other campuses too?). Go to the university, go inside the main library to the student computers and access ftp.up.ac.za and save and burn the distro image to CD/DVD. You can access ftp.up.ac.za from any computer, but going to the university prevents downloading since the files are hosted there!

Cheers!
