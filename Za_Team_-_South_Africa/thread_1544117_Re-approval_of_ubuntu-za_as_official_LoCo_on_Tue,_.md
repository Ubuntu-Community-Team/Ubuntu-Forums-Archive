### Post by who_da_fly on 2010-08-02
(Reposted from the mailing list and web site)

Ubuntu-za is up for re-approval as listed on the [agenda of the LoCo Council]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LoCoCouncil/Agenda") for Tuesday, 3 August 2010 at 21:00 SAST. The meeting will be held in #ubuntu-meeting on IRC ([freenode.net]("http://freenode.net/")). Approved teams are eligible for certain benefits and are also  considered officially by the Ubuntu project. The criteria for  re-approval is the same as for approval which is outlined on the [getting approved instructions]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LoCoGettingApproved").

 The ubuntu-za team was first approved in 2005 and got re-approval on 10 November 2008.

 Whether you are an experienced Linux user or you are just [getting started]("http://ubuntu-za.org/get-started") with open-source software, there are lots of ways to [get involved]("http://ubuntu-za.org/get-involved") with our ubuntu communities.

 In order to keep our official LoCo status we need all the support we  can get, a big turnout at the meeting always helps. Talk to us at any  time on IRC at #ubuntu-za or on the [mailing list]("http://ubuntu-za.org/mailing%20list") and let us know if you have more information to add on our [re-approval application page]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ZATeam/ReApprovalApplication").

 **Be there!**
