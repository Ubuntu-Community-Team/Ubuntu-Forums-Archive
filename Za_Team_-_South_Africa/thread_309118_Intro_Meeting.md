### Post by Circus-Killer on 2006-11-29
Hey there guys.

I think we need to have an intro meeting. Although I haven't been around for very long, one thing I've noticed is that there are very few people on the team, even less taking part, and I'm assuming that nobody knows who anyone is (except for maybe the odd one or two people).

So, there will be a meeting on IRC on Monday, 15th January 2007 from 8pm till 10pm. I made it for the evening to accomodate those who have to work and can't IRC from work. Please invite and encourage as many people as you can to be there. Newcomers are a must!

Other than getting to know eachother and form some sort of cohesive bond, let's also get some suggestions on what can be discussed. Please try keep the suggestions relatively on-point (as good topics of an intro meeting). The more technical, planning, and initiating can be done at a later stage. Let's just try get the team acting like a team.

Hope to see you all there.

IRC Server: irc.freenode.net
IRC Channel: #ubuntu-za

EDIT: Okay, we are going to have a meeting a little bit earlier. There will be a meeting on Thursday, 7th December 2006 at 8pm. By the looks of things we will still be having the meeting in January, so check back for more updates.
