### Post by Frogs Hair on 2019-04-15
I go back more than a week for unanswered posts and when going over two I find if OP is new they often don't come back. They can bump the thread if needed. When it comes support I don't often subscribe and If I see that the user is being aided by a member with more knowledgeable than myself I consider them to be in good hands and bow out. :)  

Some times real life gets in the way of fixing a computer problem so, it can be more than a week before some people get the chance to reply. I don't have any specific rules because as you know people often use more than one source to resolve a problem and don't bother to come back and share the solution. There are those who just want a quick solution to a problem and others who want to be part of the community, but that's another topic. The posting style is often telling as to whether a poster will come back.
