### Post by wildmanne39 on 2020-11-04
Ubuntu Membership Boards Call for Nominations is open to all Ubuntu Members, the Board Members work as a team and are responsible for evaluating Candidates to determine there qualifications to become Ubuntu Members, if interested in being a Board Member please see the following link for the qualifications required  and how to nominate yourself or someone else.

[https://fridge.ubuntu.com/2020/11/03/ubuntu-membership-boards-call-for-nominations/](https://fridge.ubuntu.com/2020/11/03/ubuntu-membership-boards-call-for-nominations/)

Nominations are being accepted until Tuesday, November 17th, at 23:59 UTC. At that time, the Membership Board will forward all nominations to the Community Council, who will make the final decision and announcement.
