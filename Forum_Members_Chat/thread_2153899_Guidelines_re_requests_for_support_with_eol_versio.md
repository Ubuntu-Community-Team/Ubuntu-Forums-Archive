### Post by CharlesA on 2013-06-12
If it's EoL, it is unsupported here, outside of maybe telling them that the repos moved to [http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/](http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/)

Best thing for them to do is upgrade to a supported version as running unsupported software is just asking for trouble.

### Post by cariboo on 2013-06-12
+1 to what CharlesA said, I've flat out told a couple of posters that if a release hits EOL, that there won't be any support either from Ubuntu, or the Forum. I'd liken it to those that are still using Windows 2000, they know they won't get any kind of official support, but there are enough resources on the internet, that they really shouldn't need to ask for support on a forum.
