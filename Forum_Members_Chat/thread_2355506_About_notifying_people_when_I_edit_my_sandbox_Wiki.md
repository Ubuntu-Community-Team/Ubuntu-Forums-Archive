### Post by howefield on 2017-03-13
I don't think you can stop the "spam" Paddy. :)

I believe those subscribed have deliberately subscribed to many if not all wiki pages, by design or possibly accidentally. 

Sign in to the wiki and click on your username, then on Notification and you'll can subscribe to individual pages, several or pretty much all. 

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HelpOnSubscribing](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HelpOnSubscribing)
