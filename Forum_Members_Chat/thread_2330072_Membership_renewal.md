### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2016-07-07
According to [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuEmail#Expiration_date](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuEmail#Expiration_date)
> Your Ubuntu membership will expire in two years. You'll receive an automatic email to renew, then your Ubuntu membership (and email) will be extended. 
I have no reason to believe that this has has been changed.  However you need to ensure that your email address on launchpad is current.
