Hello,

We have created this area on the forum for Ubuntu Forum Members only. It is completely hidden from general view.

Our intention is to use this area to do the following:

1)  Give you all first notice of forum problems / issues that are being addressed, as well as feedback on how they are going.

2)  Allow you to nominate forum members that you feel should be encouraged to apply for forum membership.

3)  Suggest ideas you have to improve how the forum operates.

4)  Provide a means for us to relay possible candidates to you all to vote for future moderators (we are still working on the details, but we want to become a little more "democratic")

So, from me - Thank you for helping make this forum one of the best on the web.  

Welcome :)
