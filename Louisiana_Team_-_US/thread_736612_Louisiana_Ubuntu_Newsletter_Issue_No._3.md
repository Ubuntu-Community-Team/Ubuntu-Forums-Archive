We are going to be printing team business cards with general information about the LoCO team.  There is a poll on the forums where you can vote on the design you like best. If you would like to contribute, please contact either Rob Rogers or Wesley Harp.


----------------------------
Monroe Ham RadioFest Update
----------------------------

If you will be attending the Hamfest, make sure you have signed up on the wiki page at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LouisianaTeam/Projects/MonroeHamFest](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LouisianaTeam/Projects/MonroeHamFest).  Use @SIG@ to add your wikiname and timestamp automatically.  Be sure you have created your wiki page so we can contact you directly.  So far we have six people signed up.  We need to have at least 3 people in the booth at all times.  Shifts will be shorter as we get more volunteers.

Instead of paying a booth fee, each person will need to pay admission at the door.  Admission is $5 per person

We need to organize the presentation for the hour long forum we have been asked to do.  If you have ideas or know of material we can borrow or adapt please contact Rob Rogers.  Some of this time can be used for questions and answers so our presentation will not need to fill up the entire period.

System76 has confirmed that we will be getting a demo laptop for the Hamfest.  We have also received flyers from this great sponsor. Rob Rogers will be mailing the last batch of fliers for distribution Thursday March 27th.  Visit System76 at [http://system76.com](http://system76.com).

The wiki page also details the progress on a PC we will be giving away at the Hamfest.  We hope this will be a big draw to our booth and may provide a good chance to offset the costs of this event if we raffle it off. Please use the wiki to tell us if you have parts you wish to contribute to this effort.  If we have more parts volunteered than we need for this event, we will use them for other PC giveaways.

If you have any new details or questions regarding the HAM fest project, please post to the forum thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=702784](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=702784).

There will be a Hamfest organization meeting on IRC Tuesday April 1st at 9:00pm.  If you are planning to help with the HAM fest, please make every effort to attend this meeting.  If you cannot make the meeting but will be at the Hamfest email Rob Rogers and make sure you've signed up on the wiki page.
