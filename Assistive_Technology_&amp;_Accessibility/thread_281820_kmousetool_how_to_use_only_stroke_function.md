The question right away: 

Is there a way to disable the left click functionality of kmousetool and only use its stroke functionality for the right click?

PS: background 

For people using only the left mousebutton, kmousetool could be interesting for doing the right click with its stroke functionality. 

However, because kmousetool does a left click every time the mouse is paused, it is impossible to use the stroke functionality to open the contextual menu on a link in the browser, as the mere fact of positioning the mouse on the link performs a left-click on it.
