### Post by frafu on 2006-11-23
I have just checked on my Ubuntu Edgy installation (Ubuntu 6.10). 

Go to the Layout tab in the Keyboard Preferences and click on the button Add. You are presented a new window where you can choose the keyboard layout. 

Under the U. S. English layout, there is among others also a 'Left Handed Dvorak' layout. I suppose it is what you are looking for. 

Have a nice day.
