### Post by arky on 2009-10-19
You can use any of the text to speech (TTS) engines. For example you make espeak say something with a command like "espeak hi". 

Some of the TTS engines are 

espeak - A multi-lingual software speech synthesizer

epos - Text-to-speech system

flite - A small run-time speech synthesis engine

festival - General multi-lingual speech synthesis system
