### Post by pegasus2000 on 2010-05-06
The original FireVox is not compatible with Firefox 3.6.X because the contents.rdf
support has been removed by the Gecko engine. 

You can use the new ML-FireVox, that supports both italian and english voice:

[http://visilab.unime.it/~filippo/MLFireVox/MLFireVox.htm]("http://visilab.unime.it/%7Efilippo/MLFireVox/MLFireVox.htm")
