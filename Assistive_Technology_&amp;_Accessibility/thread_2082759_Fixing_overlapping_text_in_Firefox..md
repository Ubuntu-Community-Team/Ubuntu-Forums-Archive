Have you noticed that when you increase the default font size in Firefox the text overlaps on some sites? Also, the ads on the page obscure some of the text. I discovered that Adblock Plus takes care of most of this problem!

I had a lot of trouble with this problem before I fully recored from my eye surgery and wish I had discovered this sooner.
