### Post by RKCole on 2011-12-20
Hello, de Bacon.

Unfortunately, I do not believe that Orca works completely with KDE or XFCE (the desktop environments for Kubuntu and Xubuntu, respectively) at this time. However, this will very likely change in the near future as there have been a lot of accessibility-related developments in KDE and in XFCE.

With no disrespect to this forum, you may be able to find more help on the Orca mailing list (see the end of this post).

I am sorry that I did not answer your post sooner; I do not come to the forums too much nowadays.

Take care.
