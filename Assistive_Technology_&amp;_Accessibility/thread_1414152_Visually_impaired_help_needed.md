 [COLOR=#000000]First, make sure it is enabled by going to System> Preferences> Appearance> Visual Effects. Select Preferences and then enable Enhanced Zoom Desktop under the Accessibility category.[/COLOR]
 > [COLOR=#000000]To use Ubuntu&#8217;s zoom, press and hold the windows start button (between ctrl and alt) and move your mouse&#8217;s scroll wheel up to zoom in and down to zoom out. You can move the zoomed in area with your mouse.[/COLOR]
