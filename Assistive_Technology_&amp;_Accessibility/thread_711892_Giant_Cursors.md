### Post by jpeddicord on 2008-03-02
If you are running Gutsy, check System > Preferences > Appearance. Hit the Customize button on the bottom and go to the Pointers tab. At the bottom of that window is a Size slider. Just drag it all the way to the right and you have a gigantic cursor. :)
