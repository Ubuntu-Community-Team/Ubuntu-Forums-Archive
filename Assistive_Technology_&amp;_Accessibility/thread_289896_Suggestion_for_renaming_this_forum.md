As you probably have noticed by yourself, there are a many threads in this forum that do not belong here. It seems that people tend to interpret the word 'accessibility' in a large sense; for example how to access specific hardware. 

Consequently, it might be appropriate to rename this forum to make it more clearly related to assistive technology for disabled people.
