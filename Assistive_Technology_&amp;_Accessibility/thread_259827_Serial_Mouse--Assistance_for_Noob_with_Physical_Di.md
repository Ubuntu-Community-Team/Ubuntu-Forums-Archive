Hi Mark,

Unfortunately serial mice are not automatically detected because probing the serial port is sometimes problematic. 

It is possible to configure a serial mouse manually though. See: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SerialMouseHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SerialMouseHowto)
