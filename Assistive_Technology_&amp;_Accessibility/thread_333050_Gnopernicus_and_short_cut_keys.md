### Post by frafu on 2007-03-08
Hello, 


There is an option in the Assistive Technology Preferences panel to make it startup on login: menu
System->Preferences->Accessibility->Assistive Technology Preferences

If you are the only person using the computer, you might consider enabling automatic login in the security tab in the "Login Window" preferences under the System->Administration menu. 


Making the login more accessible is one of the targets for feisty. 

I hope somebody else will be able to help you more. 

Francesco
