### Post by d-_-b.za on 2008-06-09
Hi

you can try to install "ghfaxviewer". To install from a terminal:

> 
sudo aptitude install ghfaxviewer
ghfaxviewer


and then from ghfaxviewer you can open your tiff file. Or after you installed ghfaxviewer you can also use the file explorer and right click on your tiff file goto "Open With >" and then "Open with aplliction" and in the "Open With" window "use a custom command" and enter "ghfaxviewer" as the command.
