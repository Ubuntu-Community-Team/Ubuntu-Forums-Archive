explanation - work in progress.

You may just need to transfer a wave file over a network and so you might just need to be in range of a microphone depending on your location.

software that I know of that may do this that has a phone app is  Dragon Anywhere Mobile Solution, but now most smart phones have ios and and android(linux open source) software that has voice recognition software built in.

[U]**Speech Disabilities**
[/U]*If you have something such dysarthria, you may want to be sure you can double the speech volume.  Also cell phone voice recognition may drain the battery, so you may want to use a blu tooth headset*.  

You may want to use a cup or some type of way to limit background noise.

**_Past Solutions_**
In the past I used software from the same company called DragonDictate that worked under windows, you may be able to use under wine as it was only compatible with windows 95/98
