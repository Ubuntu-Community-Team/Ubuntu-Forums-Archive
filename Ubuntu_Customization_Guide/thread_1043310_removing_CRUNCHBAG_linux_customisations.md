### Post by Mehall on 2009-03-09
Well you could change the gui for Gnome on a user basis, but if you just want to remove all the #! changes from your computer completely, then you can just do:

"sudo apt-get remove --purge crunchbang-look" (without the quotes)

Also: you would probably get more results from the [Crunchbang Forums](http://www.crunchbanglinux.org/forums)
