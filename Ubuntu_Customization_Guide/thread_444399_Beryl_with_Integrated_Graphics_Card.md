### Post by mpgarate on 2007-05-15
Its Green! Im typing this on my new dell with integrated graphics and beryl. At first it did not work. gave me a white screen. I reinstalled everything, then enabled the restricted driver for Nvidea. restarted, in bios I changed graphics memory reservation from 64 to 128 (just for the heck of it) and then it worked perfectly! Now to update to ubuntustudio!
