### Post by iride4ugurl21 on 2008-01-09
Hey, i am also using a acer extenza 5620 and believe it or not our graphics card is blacklisted from the compiz fusion list, BUT there is a way around that just open up the terminal and type SKIP_CHECKS=yes compiz and the screen will flash a couple of times and then you will need to go back to the visual effects settings tab in appearance and you will see a new option at the bottom of the screen it will say custom and will have a preferences tab next to it press the preferences tab and start applying settings for compiz fusion

Make sure you have the gnome compiz manager and the other compiz settings managers applied in synaptic.
