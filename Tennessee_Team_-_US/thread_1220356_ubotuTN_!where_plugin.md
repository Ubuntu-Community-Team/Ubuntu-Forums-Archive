### Post by binarymutant on 2009-07-22
This plugin will show the location and miscellaneous information of Ubuntu Tennessee Team members that have added themselves to [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/TennesseeTeam/Members]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/TennesseeTeam/Members")

Input/Output will be:
!where <nick>     =>    <location> <misc info>

Some aliases for the plugin:
!about
!411
!who
!location
!info

Thanks to exodus_ms for the great idea! And like all the other blueprints for ubotuTN your input is valuable.
