### Post by tlepes on 2007-11-30
Okay... my vote for monthly broke the tie between bi-weekly and monthly meetings.  But I am not opposed to bi-weekly, or twice a month.  My main concern is inclusion.  Here is my thinking...

Monthly is great if it is on a certain day of the month, like the 20th or some such, because the day of the week will be different each time.  No one will be left unable to attend because they can never make "third tuesdays" or what have you.

But this would be dependent on the scheduling options with the venue.  If the place chosen needs to be scheduled, and preferes some "third saturday" sort of arrangement, then a second meeting each month might make sense - so long as it is on a different day of the week.  Die-hards can attend both meetings, and those with fixed schedules can make the one that works for them.

Like I said, my main concern is inclusion for others.  Myself - my schedule changes week to week.  So sometimes I will be free, sometimes not, regardless of how the meetings are scheduled.
