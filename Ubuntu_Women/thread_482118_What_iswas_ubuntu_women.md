### Post by elizabeth on 2007-06-24
As the description clearly states:

*Ubuntu-Women is a team functioning under Ubuntu to provide a platform and encouragement for women to contribute to Ubuntu-Linux, a Debian based free and open-source GNU/Linux software.*

This means encouragement in programming, packaging (and other heavily technical development), community involvement, documentation, translations, artwork and a variety of other things. The project is very much alive.

You can find more information at our website: [ubuntu-women.org]("http://ubuntu-women.org/").
