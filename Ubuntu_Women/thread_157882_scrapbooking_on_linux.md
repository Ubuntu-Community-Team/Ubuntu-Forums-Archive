### Post by nikkiana on 2008-05-31
An old thread I know, but I figured I'd revive to post some of the resources I've found:

[http://www.scrapbooksgonedigital.com/TUTORIALS/](http://www.scrapbooksgonedigital.com/TUTORIALS/) - Digital Scrapbooking oriented tutorials.
[http://www.scrapbooksgonedigital.com/Forum/](http://www.scrapbooksgonedigital.com/Forum/) - GIMP friendly digital scrapbooking forum.
[http://www.scrapbookflair.com/groups/GIMP_Digital_Scrapbooking_Designers](http://www.scrapbookflair.com/groups/GIMP_Digital_Scrapbooking_Designers) - Small GIMP oriented layout sharing community.
[http://brainsongimp.blogspot.com/](http://brainsongimp.blogspot.com/) - Blog with lots of tutorials, mostly focused on how to make your own elements.
[http://scrappygimp.blogspot.com/](http://scrappygimp.blogspot.com/) - Various scrapbooking realated tutorials with The GIMP.
[http://cheltzey.blogspot.com/2007/04/gimp-tutorial-creating-clipping-masks.html](http://cheltzey.blogspot.com/2007/04/gimp-tutorial-creating-clipping-masks.html) - Good clipping mask tutorial.
