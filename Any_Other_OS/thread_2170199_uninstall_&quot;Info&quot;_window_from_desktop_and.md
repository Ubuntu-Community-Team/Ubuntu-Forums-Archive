### Post by ronniew on 2013-08-25
[http://lxle.net/index.php?id=lxleconfigurationguide](http://lxle.net/index.php?id=lxleconfigurationguide)



    Menu > Accessories > ROXTerm
    Enter &#8220;pcmanfm ~/.config/autostart/&#8221; without the quotes and hit enter.
    You can either delete the file or simply remove &#8220;.desktop&#8221; from the filename (right click rename). This will disable it, to renable it in the future re-add &#8220;.desktop&#8221; to the filename.


Two things, one fast forecast doesn't really get 'loaded' its simply a shortcut to a program.
Second the info box can be stopped entirely or it can be hidden with the hide/show info button (next to weather)
