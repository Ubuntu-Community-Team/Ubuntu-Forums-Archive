### Post by LinuxFan999 on 2011-09-23
I have tried ReactOS many times, and I have not seen much change between releases. ReactOS is still very incompatible and inconsistent, and applications that worked on one build may not work on another build. I test ReactOS on Virtual Box, and the install usually hangs in ether the first stage, or the second stage. Installing applications sometimes breaks the system, the GUI is sluggish, and doing even the simplest things can lock up ReactOS, or give it a BSOD. The ReactOS project offers a big promise, but they haven't quite fulfilled it yet.
