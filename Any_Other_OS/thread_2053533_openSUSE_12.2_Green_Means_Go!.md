### Post by BigSilly on 2012-09-20
Well, decided the other day to bite the bullet and install this latest release. I opted for the Gnome edition, not because I don't like KDE but because I just have a soft spot for openSUSE's Gnome 3 version. Everyone always says openSUSE is a KDE distro first, and while this may be true to some degree, I've always found they put equal effort into their Gnome version making it worth considering on its own merits. I find it very nice to use and much nicer than Fedora, for example.

Anyway, have to say, so far it's really great. I've not come up against any hitches or showstoppers at all, which to me is perhaps a good indication that it was smart on their part to delay the release. If you've ever used openSUSE, you'll not it's not as easy going (not quite) as the Ubuntu based distros. You find you have to add a layer of polish yourself, with font hinting needing an extra repo and also codecs needing to be manually installed etc. But once it's set up you cannot fault it.

I dunno why folk hate on Gnome Shell. Sad really.
