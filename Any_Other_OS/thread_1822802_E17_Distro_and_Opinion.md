### Post by kazuya on 2011-08-11
E17 is nice overall and elegant. They have added more customization options, but I find it sometimes buggy when trying to add certain themes. This was the case in the past though. I have had the DE crash from merely installing a perhaps corrupted theme I downloaded.
 
Then I have to go to the .e folder and rm the config file to reset e17.
 
I would love it if I could get my e17 to resemble Elive.
But I am not willing to pay for Elive.
 
It is fast, elegant, almost fully functional for me. Sometimes opening up certain kde or gnome related apps do not appear as gorgeous or fitting as they would on their natural DE.
 
But if I overlook that minor aesthetic, e17 is very nice. I just wish I knew better how to customise it. Entrance, now replaced with elisa is nice, but I do not know how it does with selecting other DEs from login.
