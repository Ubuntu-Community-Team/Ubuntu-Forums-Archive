### Post by Plumtreed on 2012-05-11
You can use a USB drive in the same way as any hard drive and can install additional Operating systems. The only restriction is the capacity of your drive. Once installed the 'live' characteristic is lost and speed is diminished. 

There are 'frugal' installs to USB flash drives that usually retain the speed and offer a means to preserve any info/changes you have made during the session.

You may need Grub to 'index' the selection. You will also have to ensure you bios is set to boot the USB drive.
