### Post by mips on 2013-06-05
Windows 8 has built in support for burmese script. You need to enable "[COLOR=#444444][FONT=Open Sans]Myanmar Text" somewhere in the keyboard/language options.

Many of the fonts you can download are not unicode compliant and don't work with windows.

[/FONT][/COLOR][http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burmese_script](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burmese_script)
