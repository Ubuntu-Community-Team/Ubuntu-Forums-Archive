Either way,** I will donate money to Canonical to show my support for the great job they've been doing but also as part of my penance for buying Windows 8.**

### Post by quequotion on 2012-11-03
If WD is smart, they'll ask you to test the drives in Windows AND Linux, and even OSX.

Certainly they'd like to sell to the massive-collocation and server administration crowd, who are neck deep in linux and bsd, and burn through hard drives like alkaline batteries.

Linux also gives (knowledgeable) users more fine-grained control over their hardware, so you could do more effective tests.

More importantly, it's fundamental to the scientific process:  the environment and tools used in an experiment inevitably skew the results; in order to demonstrate conclusive results they must be retested in different environments and with different tools.
