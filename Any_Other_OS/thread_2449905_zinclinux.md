### Post by zinclinux on 2020-10-09
Hi, Jarp53. I was just checking search results for "zinclinux" and found this thread. 
I'm the creator of zinclinux, and I'd be glad to answer any questions you may have.

ZincLinux is a modified and rebranded Ubuntu + XFCE. I wanted to make Xubuntu a little better, by my own tastes.

I made it exactly as I would want it, and then I made changes based on testers and reddit users input.
I tried to make it all things, for all people, which is impossible, of course. Then I created the WP site to share it, without any marketing effort.

I use it daily on 8 home systems, and I know of a few households that have used it as an introduction to Linux.
I'm always happy to see other people appreciating the effort I've made.

It was a fun learning process to make the changes to Firefox and write the basic scripts.
But, to be fair, I have done light polishing and owe everything to the developer who made it possible.

-Joe
