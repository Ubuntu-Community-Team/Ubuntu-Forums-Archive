I agree with this. Most modern systems should have no problem running regular ubuntu. My netbook runs Mint 11 like it was made for it.

Why people have speed problems, I don't know. Could be *anything*.

And we're all quick to blame hardware when things don't work in linux, but remember, hardware problems also affect windows. You would be amazed at how many times hardware *is* the problem. I've seen tons of bad HD's, memory, and mobo's. Learning how to do proper hardware diagnostics should never be overlooked.

People are sometimes too quick to blame the OS for problems.
