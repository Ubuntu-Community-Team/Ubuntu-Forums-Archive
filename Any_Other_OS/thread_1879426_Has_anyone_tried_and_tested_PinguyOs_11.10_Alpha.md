### Post by shuttleworthwannabe on 2011-11-12
I think you will be pleasantly surprised--I felt the same way about Pinguy in the past, but this edition just blows; also it is much slicker and faster than Mint 12 RC (both on live USB). 
Problem is I am not sure how to make the live USB in Pinguy persistent--Mint gets a persistent install.

I figured it out: install linux-usb-install from here [http://live.learnfree.eu/download](http://live.learnfree.eu/download). PinguyOS 11.10 Alpha is not listed, but you could use 11.04 works fine. You can also create persistent from there.
EnjoY!
