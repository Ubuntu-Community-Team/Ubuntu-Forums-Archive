### Post by BrokenKingpin on 2011-06-06
So I have been running Mint 11 for about a week now and I have to say it is very good, and probably the best Gnome2 desktop to date. This will probably be the last Gnome2 release from Mint, so they really have put the effort into ensuring it was the best release of Gnome2 out there.

After Ubuntu 11.04 dropped with Unity and Gnome going to version 3, I hopped around for a while trying to figure out what I wanted to use. I thought I would end up with XFCE, but I found it far too buggy right now. I also tried KDE and also found it to be unstable. So in the end I have decided to go back to Gnome2 while things settle.

So for anyone wanting the new repos of Ubuntu 11.04, but with a good Gnome2 implementation, I highly recommend Mint 11.
