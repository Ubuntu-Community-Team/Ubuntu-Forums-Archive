### Post by Ricx94 on 2011-12-24
oh...i see. there might be little blue numbers on the keys of your laptop. these form a number pad. look also for a little blue "Num lock" or "NumLk" on a key somewhere. you will need to hold down the "Fn" key on the bottom left of the keyboard, and then click the num lock key, and then i believe the keypad of blue numbers will be active, but the letters will not. to revert, do same thing --> Fn + NumLk

hope this helps
