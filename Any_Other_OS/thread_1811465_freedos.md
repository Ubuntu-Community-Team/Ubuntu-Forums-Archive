### Post by Primefalcon on 2011-07-24
step 1: download freedos iso: [http://www.freedos.org/freedos/files/](http://www.freedos.org/freedos/files/)

step 2: install virtualbox from software center

step 3: click new and follow the prompts

step 4: click your virtual machine in the list and click start and select your freedos iso as the media source......
