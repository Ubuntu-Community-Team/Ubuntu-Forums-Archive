### Post by Herman on 2012-01-01
To clear the RAM in any computer is very simple. 

You shut the computer down and remove the power for a minute by unplugging it, (remove the power cord either from the back of the power supply or from the wall socket, or from your UPS if you have one.
In a laptop you need to remove the battery too.

In many desktop computers there is a small LED on the motherboard that will remain illuminated for several seconds, maybe up to a minute after the computer has been unplugged.
After that LED goes out your RAM is empty. :)
