### Post by Tar_Ni on 2014-03-28
First off, Chrome OS by google is not available for installation. It's based on Chromium OS and designed specifically for chromebooks and chromeboxes, it's not released as an OS for installation.

Are you refering to *Chromium* OS? One can make a built, I know the most populars available to all are that of Hexxeh ([http://chromeos.hexxeh.net/](http://chromeos.hexxeh.net/))

The problem with using this as a main OS is that there is no flash, mp3 and pdf supported. That's why I never used Chromium OS though I would like to.

Lubuntu or Peppermint OS are probably what's closer to what you are looking for. Simple and lightweight operating systems that works great for cloud computing.
