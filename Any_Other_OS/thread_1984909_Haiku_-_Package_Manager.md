### Post by rai4shu2 on 2012-05-22
Haiku is pretty straight-forward, actually. Everything either goes into /boot/common/apps or /boot/home/apps (depending on whether it's user-specific). There's no specific package manager, though (other than looking at that folder and seeing what you have unpacked there).

see: [http://www.haiku-os.org/docs/userguide/en/applications.html#install](http://www.haiku-os.org/docs/userguide/en/applications.html#install)
