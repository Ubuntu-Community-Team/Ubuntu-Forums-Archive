### Post by bug67 on 2011-07-11
Might give [Airfoil]("http://www.rogueamoeba.com/airfoil/") a try.  Airfoil runs on your computer (Mac or PC) and is able to stream any audio source from the computer running it to any device running [Airfoil Speakers]("http://www.rogueamoeba.com/airfoil/speakers.php").

I'm using Airfoil to stream audio from my Windows 7 PC to my living room stereo via AirPort Express and then to my Linux machine in the kitchen via Airfoil Speakers for Linux.

I don't have Airfoil Speakers on my iPhone so I don't know how that works but, the other stuff works flawlessly for me.
