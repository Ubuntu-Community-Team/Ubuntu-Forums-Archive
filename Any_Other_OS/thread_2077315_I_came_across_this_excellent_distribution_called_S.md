### Post by georgelappies on 2012-10-28
Hi all

I have been distro hopping a bit lately after 12.10 came out. A distro that I have settled on now is called Sabayon. It is based on gentoo but comes with pre-compiled binaries. I'm running the KDE version and must say this is by far the smoothest, best looking OS I have ever used! Never before have I seen this quality of font rendering (it uses the [www.infinality.net](www.infinality.net) pathces)

By default all proprietary drivers for the display as well as all codecs are installed. It has a nice package manager called rigo. But best of all is that it has amazingly sane and cool defaults.

Do yourself a favor and check it out:

[https://www.sabayon.org/](https://www.sabayon.org/)
