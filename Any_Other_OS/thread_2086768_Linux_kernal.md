### Post by QIII on 2012-11-21
I think you most often see that as an expression indicating that, as a tool, there is no magical, mystical, metaphysical advantage of Linux over Windows or Apple.

It's part of a system that does the same things those others do:  provide a tool for getting useful work done.

However, some may mean by that that the kernel is the essence of Linux, the rest is attractive window dressing and packaging.
