### Post by sanderd17 on 2011-07-21
OpenSuse has not a lot to do with MS, but Suse is owned by Novell, and Novell works together with MS for some other projects.

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Novell#Agreement_with_Microsoft](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Novell#Agreement_with_Microsoft)

The danger of working together with MS is that MS already has a few times sucked out the life of one of his partners. So if that would happen with Novell, than OpenSuse would be lost (not the code, only the project).
