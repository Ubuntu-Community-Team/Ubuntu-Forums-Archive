### Post by papibe on 2011-10-27
My guess is that you are missing a cable (or adapter) that came with the card.

I've seen a few cards like that. Instead of having two outputs, they have one unusual output that connects to a special cable that has two ends, each equivalent to a vga (or DVI) output. [This]("http://accessories.dell.com/sna/productdetail.aspx?c=us&l=en&s=corp&sku=320-0190") in an example.

Try to look for the cable on the packaging.
