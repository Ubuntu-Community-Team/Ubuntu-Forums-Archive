Good day.



On one computer, we have Linux Mint 11 Gnome running.
We find that the Typing Break applet in the panel does not behave as well as on Ubuntu.

The Typing Break applet can be found in Control Center > Keyboard > Typing Break tab.
 
In Ubuntu, the bar starts off empty. 
As time goes on, it fills with green, starting at the bottom. Then the bar will be full of green.
Then the bar will be solid red.
Then the bar will blink red.
Then the bar will blink red more quickly. 
Then the screen locks.

In Mint, the bar starts off fully green.
Then the bar becomes solid red. 
Then the screen locks.


Mint info:
The GNOME Panel 2.32.1


If you're using Mint, could you try to use this Typing Break thing and let me know how yours behaves?
