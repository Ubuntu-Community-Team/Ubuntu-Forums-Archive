Give it a try.

### Post by vanlong441 on 2011-12-24
hello everyone, I have been using GNOME Shell since the earliest release of PinguyOS 11.10 came out. I can say that it is not much different from other GNOME Shell spins so do not keep your expectation high installing this distro. However, if you are just a guy/gal who is desperately looking for a place to try out whether you are comfortable with the new technology in a PinguyOS way, this distro will not disappoint you for such task. It gives you two flexible Dockies to interact with running applications and launchers, a Conky that tells vital information that you may never know about, a sexy set of default wallpapers, and most of all, the good old Synaptic. They are just a few things I'd like to give my compliment to in PinguyOS 11.10.

After trying out the OS, if you still have something irritating your mind about GNOME Shell that may stop you from installing PinguyOS, just go to forum.pinguyos.com and open a thread and tell us. If your questions are polite enough, not only me but a couple of nice guys there will join helping.
