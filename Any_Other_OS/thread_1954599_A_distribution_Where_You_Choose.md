### Post by Paddy Landau on 2012-04-08
> **chris1216 said:**
> I do not want to save anything to the hard drive...
In that case, install Ubuntu to a USB with persistence. Instructions are on the [download page]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download") for Linux -- you can't set persistence ("Stored in reserved extra space") in Windows or Mac.

I suggest you use a lightweight distro such as [Lubuntu]("http://lubuntu.net/") for faster loading.
