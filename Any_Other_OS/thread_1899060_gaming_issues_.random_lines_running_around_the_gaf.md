### Post by mips on 2011-12-23
What version of the Catalyst driver is he running?

Here is the current version: [http://sites.amd.com/us/game/downloads/Pages/radeon_win7-64.aspx](http://sites.amd.com/us/game/downloads/Pages/radeon_win7-64.aspx)

Try rolling back to a older version or try the latest Beta version to see if it improves things.

[http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/windows/previous/Pages/radeonaiw_vista64.aspx](http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/windows/previous/Pages/radeonaiw_vista64.aspx)
[http://support.amd.com/us/kbarticles/Pages/Catalyst114earlypreview.aspx](http://support.amd.com/us/kbarticles/Pages/Catalyst114earlypreview.aspx)

Use Driver Sweeper, [http://downloads.guru3d.com/download.php?det=1655](http://downloads.guru3d.com/download.php?det=1655) , after you unistall the drivers via the normal method run Driver Sweeper to cleanup any remnants and then reinstall new drivers to test with. Repeat this every time you install/uninstall drivers.

If problems still persist you might have one of two issues, 
1) the GPU is faulty which means you would have to return it for a replacement.
2) your PSU might have issues supplying enough power to the card. Even though it's 550W not PSU units are made equal and some brands really suck and don't deliver enough current. Try to stick to brands like Antec, Corsair & Seasonic 80+ high current rated. Did he connect all the power connectors to the GPU?
