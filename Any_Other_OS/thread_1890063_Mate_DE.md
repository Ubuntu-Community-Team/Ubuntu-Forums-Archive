### Post by 3Miro on 2011-12-02
Mint added Mate to their release, it is installed by default, but it is not selected as the default session. Right now, you can install Mate on 11.10 by adding some Mint repositories, but things may not be very stable. I expect 12.04 to have Mate available in the official repos.

When KDE was undergoing the horrible transition to 4.x, there was Trinity as a fork of KDE 3. It hung around for a while and eventually became extinct. Basically, it was just something to help people transition. I have a feeling Mate will go the same way.
