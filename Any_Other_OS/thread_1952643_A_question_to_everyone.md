### Post by Atomic-Fanboy on 2012-04-04
Several weeks ago, an ISO file (Anonymous-OS_0.1.iso) was posted to Sourceforge and also posted as a torrent file. Supposedly, it is an image of Ubuntu 11.10 using the Mate DE and containing a reasonable number of tools to 'assess the security of web pages'.

These well-known 'security testing tools' included:
Low Orbit Ion Cannon
High Orbit Ion Cannon
Ddosim
SlowLoris

Anonymous themselves were very quick to respond, reporting that Anonymous-OS was fake, had nothing to do with Anonymous, and may be absolutely packed with Trojans etc. It was pulled off Sourceforge on March 15.

I'm just interested in what you all make of this thing.
