### Post by BBQdave on 2012-06-12
I believe Mint-X and Mint-Z (the two available themes) have better support for GTK3.

And they are building off of the Gnome 3 scene, which is a singular themed environment... hence all the uproar with Gnome 3 and Unity's tightness on the UI (users interface).

With time and development, there may be more themes offered in Mint (MATE). It would be nice to see the two projects merge, MATE and Cinnamon, might see more and quicker progress.

That said, I am very pleased with Mint 13 (Maya). Stable (and IMHO) sensible DE, with long term support :)

If you are looking for more control over your DE (customizing themes), you might want to consider Xubuntu or LMDE Xfce.
