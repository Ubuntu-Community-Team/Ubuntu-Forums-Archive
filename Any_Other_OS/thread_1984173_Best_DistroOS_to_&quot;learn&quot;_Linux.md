### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2012-05-23
Most of the pulling apart and putting together in any distro is going to center around the package management system, which is pretty much distro-specific.

If you're doing this to educate yourself on Linux professionally, I'd stick to well-established, mainstream distros like redhat or debian (ubuntu's not a bad choice either).

Nothing wrong with Arch but you aren't likely to encounter it in a corporate data center.
