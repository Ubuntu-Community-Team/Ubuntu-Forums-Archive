### Post by viperdvman on 2013-01-10
I played with Fuduntu back when it was still based on Fedora 14, before it forked from Fedora. And I loved running it. It was, back then, pretty much the Linux Mint of Fedora, with a lot of apps and all non-free codecs (if I remember correctly) pre-installed. And I like Fuduntu's Mac OS X style interface too, which it still has to this day.

All in all, it was a neat distro to play with back then. Since it forked from Fedora, I haven't played with it. However, I may give it a try again just to play with a Fedora-style distro that still runs GNOME 2.x
