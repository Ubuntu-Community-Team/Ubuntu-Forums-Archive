### Post by mips on 2011-07-29
The best nVidia AGP card you can get is a GeForce 7950 GS AGP from 2006. If you can't find a 7800 get a 7900,7800, 6800, 6600/7600 nothing lower.

The best AMD/ATI card you can get is a Radeon HD 4670. If you can't find a 4670 get a 4650.

The nvidia cards are older and harder to find so you will be better off getting a AMD HD 4670 as they are from 2009 and probably offer a better price/ratio as well.

You can look at [http://www.gpureview.com/show_cards.php](http://www.gpureview.com/show_cards.php) to compare different card specs.
