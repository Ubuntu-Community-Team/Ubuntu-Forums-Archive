### Post by wolfen69 on 2011-10-27
Sounds like a BIOS problem. You may need to reset the CMOS by taking out the battery on the motherboard. Also,sometimes there will be jumpers (close to the battery) that you need to move from 1 set of posts to the other. That will clear the cmos and put the bios back to original defaults.
