### Post by cariboo on 2011-11-22
If your flashdrive is larger than 4GiB, you can install Ubuntu on it just like you would to a hard drive. Select Something Else when you get to the partitioning section, and select your flash drive, create a / and swap partition, and you're ready to go.

I did the same thing with an 8GiB SD card and Lubuntu.
