### Post by buzzingrobot on 2013-10-12
System76 products are sold with Ubuntu. The odds of getting an answer to a problem with running another distribution on a System76 machine here are pretty small.

Slackware -- essentially a one-person effort -- does not have an official forum.  The Slackware forum on Linuxquestions is the next best thing.

In all likelihood, your problem with fans is a driver issue.  Slackware offers little hand holding; no dependency resolution, for example. It assumes you can resolve your own problems. You may wll need to track down the appropriate driver and build it from source.
