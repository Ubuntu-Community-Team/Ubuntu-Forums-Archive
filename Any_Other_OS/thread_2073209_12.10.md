### Post by black veils on 2012-10-22
you say you booted into the live cd/usb and cannot find your files.

when in the live environment, you need to locate your zorin partition/drive from the left pane of the file manager. it will says something like **100 GB File System**, think what size you made the zorin partition, you could look in the application gparted.

click it, and go into the folder **home.**

your username will be in there, so go into that folder. this is where your files should still be. now copy them to another usb stick! (or external hard drive, or burn to dvd using **k3b**, and maybe upload some to an online storage site like **dropbox**).
