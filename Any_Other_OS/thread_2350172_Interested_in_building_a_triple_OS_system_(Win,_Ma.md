### Post by QIII on 2017-01-21
Because part of the Apple EULA is that their OSes may only be installed on Apple hardware, any multi-boot system including an Apple OS would necessarily have to be installed on an Apple machine.

You have indicated that this is not your intent.  As this is a violation of a EULA, we do not support this course of action.  Please see the [Ubuntu Forums Rules]("https://ubuntuforums.org/misc.php?do=showrules"), especially:

> We do not support circumventing TOS, EULA, etc here. Such threads will be closed and offending users will be penalised with infractions and warnings.
