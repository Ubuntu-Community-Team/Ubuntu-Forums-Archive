### Post by ratcheer on 2012-07-12
Many distros main web page will have a link somewhere to the effect, "Contribute", which is not even mainly talking about money, but is making suggestions on how to become a part of their projects. Those links would be great places to start looking.

Also, they often have websites such as "Distroname Planet" e.g., [http://planet.ubuntulinux.org/](http://planet.ubuntulinux.org/) , where the developers post about their accomplishments and plans. These are great places to see what they are doing, thus see what you would need to do to become part of it.

As to what languages to learn, C++, Java, and Python would be great starts.
