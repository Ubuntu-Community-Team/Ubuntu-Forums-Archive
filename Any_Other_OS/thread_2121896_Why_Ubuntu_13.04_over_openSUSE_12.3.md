### Post by iamkuriouspurpleoranj on 2013-03-03
It depends what you want to do. Both are user-friendly distros but I think Ubuntu has more packages. Try them both live and see which you prefer. However, I wouldn't recommend 13.04 yet unless you're testing. Go for 12.10 - the latest - or 12.04 LTS.

OpenSUSE is officially just as much Gnome 3 as it is KDE but in practice its user base tends to favour KDE.

Both Ubuntu and OpenSUSE are excellent choices.
