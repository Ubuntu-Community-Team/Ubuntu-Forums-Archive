### Post by king9174 on 2012-07-31
Ok so this distro has been around for awhile but it hasnt really been pushing the user base and getting the name out there. It is basically a lubuntu varient which has been slimmed down sped up and made prettier. If your a fan of openbox you will love this distro, if not you will enjoy its features! It has a few key applications preinstalled (not too many its a light distro) and it works really well. This is a very responisive varient to lubuntu, and for any Linux enthusiast I would recommend checking it out, If your sick of heavy bloated Linux distros why not give it a try? After all it's free!!

Point your browsers towards [http://www.proxlinux.co.uk](http://www.proxlinux.co.uk) and have a look, try it out, join the forum and review it to them! This is a distro to watch so why not avoid the rush and get an early look?
