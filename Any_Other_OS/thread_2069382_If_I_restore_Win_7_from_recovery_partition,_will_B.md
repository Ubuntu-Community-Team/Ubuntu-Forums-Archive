### Post by UrielCantarero on 2012-10-11
Nope....there are BIOS utilities that will allow you to update it from within the OS, but outside of that, they are very much separate.  The OS is actually more reliant on the BIOS than the other way around.

About the only way you can screw up your BIOS is by flashing it with the wrong firmware or frying it with some sort of static.  It is seldom that they go bad on their own.
