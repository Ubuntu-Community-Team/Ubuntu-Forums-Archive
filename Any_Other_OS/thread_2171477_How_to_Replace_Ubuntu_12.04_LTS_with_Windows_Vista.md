### Post by deadflowr on 2013-08-30
When running Ubuntu, in the Home Folder's left side pane, under "Devices", is the Vista disk listed?
It might say something like Windows, or DVD-ROM, or something, probably with .iso at the end.

If it doesn't show in that list, then it might be bad disk.

You can also look in the drive by opening "disk utility" and going to your cd/dvd device in the left side pane and click it and then look at what the main window shows.
