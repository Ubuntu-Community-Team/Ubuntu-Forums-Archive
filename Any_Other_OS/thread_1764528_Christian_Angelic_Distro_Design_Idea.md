It  would save time, and would not create further fragmentation.For 1: The Operating System won't be specifically "Ubuntu", nor shall the Ubuntu packages, utilities & programs be outrightly implicit within its' design. And rather it shall have the implemented software packages exclusive to its native environment & such shall be selected from the Gnome 3 Applications list or created specifically for the purpose to be used with Angelico Linux. 

For 2: The Angelico Linux Distribution is goaled to as well convert various native Linux processes, similar converting the "Daemon" or "Eudaimon" processes and using the name "Domino" processes au lieu. More can be learned about such on the [Wikipedia]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daemon_%28computer_software%29") or various other [Linux Forums]("http://www.linuxquestions.org/questions/showthread.php?p=4370065&posted=1#post4370065") similar [LQ]("http://www.linuxquestions.org/questions/showthread.php?p=4370065&posted=1#post4370065") .
