### Post by mips on 2011-12-05
I did this on cruchbang xfce, you have to enable the testing repo and do apt-pinning.

I still ended up with hassles later on wrt to dependency issues which I eventually sorted out by uninstalling stuff and reinstalling from testing. I also kept looking for newer versions of some packages.

My honest opinion is to just enable the testing repo and upgrading your entire distro to testing. Contrary to it's name debian testing is pretty stable.

There should be some useful guides/posts on the cruncbang forum (it's essentially debian 6)
