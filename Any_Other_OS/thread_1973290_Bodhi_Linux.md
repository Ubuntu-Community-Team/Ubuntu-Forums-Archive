### Post by BigSilly on 2012-05-29
Well I've had to put this on our ancient Eee PC 701. I say *had* to, because it was a last resort even attempting it as the last time I tried it I could not get it installed, and the usual Lubuntu we use is now too big to fit on the small 4Gb internal drive.

However, what a wonderful surprise. Not only did Bodhi install perfectly fine this time, but it's a massively impressive distro. Even more so when you consider how light it is. The desktop is utterly gorgeous and so configurable. I could spend hours just tinkering to create a unique look. But it's also (barring one well known dbus-init error message bug) very stable.

So I would definitely recommend it if you have limited hardware, because it won't make you feel as though you have. :)
