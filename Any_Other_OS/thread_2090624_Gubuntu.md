Okay, now that you have Gnome Remix 12.10 or Gubuntu 12.10, you can make it Gubuntu 13.04.

Open your Terminal and,
sudo sed -i 's/quantal/raring/g' /etc/apt/sources.list

then, 
sudo apt-get update; sudo apt-get dist-upgrade

and once, that action is over you are in Gubuntu 13.04

Good day!
