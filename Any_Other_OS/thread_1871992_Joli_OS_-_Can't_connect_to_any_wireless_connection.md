Joli Os is a derivative of Ubuntu. If your wireless works in Ubuntu it will work in Joli. You just have to do the same thing you do in Ubuntu to get it working. I don't have it installed at this moment but you have to search in the local apps or local settings. Which is located in the same area where all your cloud programs are.
