### Post by Moofu on 2011-12-18
I just recently tested out Joli OS and am very happy with it. I am already dual booting Windows 7 Starter (netbook was a graduation gift) and lubuntu on my eeepc 1005pe. 

My netbook has a 5200 rpm HDD and an SD card slot. I have a micro SD card adapter and a 4GB micro SD card. 4GB seems like enough space for just the OS to me. So my question is which would run Joli OS faster, the HDD or SD card?

---

### Post by xyzzyman on 2011-12-18
Once it's loaded you won't see a difference. Depending on the speed of the card and the adapter you might boot quicker from the SD. JoliOS you're only looking at a few second difference either way. I used it as a backup OS for awhile.

A plus to leaving it on your SD card is that as long as you don't mount your HDD, you'll get even more battery life.
