### Post by ojdon on 2011-11-09
I think the problem is actually just limitations of the Jolicloud launcher. It was originally designed for Netbook screens which all have a resolution of 1024x600. So it's not very flexible when working on larger screens never mind with additional accessibility needs.  

Not sure about the accessibly side of things but Lubuntu might be a good choice for working on older hardware and the interface can be as simple as you like. I haven't tried it out myself but it might have a few more useful accessibly options as well. 

If it doesn't suit your needs, maybe Xubuntu will?

Hope this helps!
