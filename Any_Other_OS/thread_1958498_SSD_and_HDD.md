### Post by anejo on 2012-04-18
Of course you can!
Remember the folder structure for Docs, Pics, Downloads, etc is something that you can manage
MS by default keeps that structure on the C drive
But if you had say a drive E, there is nothing stopping you relocating the LOCATION property of any one folder from drive C to somewhere on drive E
