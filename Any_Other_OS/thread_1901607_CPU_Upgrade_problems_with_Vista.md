Pentium 4 6x1 series (Cedar Mill) up to 661

### Post by 2F4U on 2011-12-29
Since your processor is not in the list of possible upgrades the mainboard vendor provides, there is no guarantee that it works and you are on your own if it doesn't. As far as I know, you have to register your Vista copy again with Microsoft after a processor upgrade.
