I tried this one over the weekend. It's based on Elementary OS, but it comes pre-customized for  you. Overall, I like it. A lot. It's going to be an OS that I use as regularly as I do Fedora, which is something that can't be said for many other OSes I've tried.

The sound worked out of the box, I like it, and I'm going to stick with it.
