### Post by BBQdave on 2013-01-03
> **rapattack1 said:**
> ...should i have the slave drive not connected and then reconnect it after the install? Will my files still be there?

As long as you do not designate the slave drive in the install-upgrade process, your files should be there.

But, yeah, disconnecting -  removing the slave drive would be the absolute safest protection of your data. And personally, that is what I do, with a portable drive - back up data and remove the drive before an upgrade :)
