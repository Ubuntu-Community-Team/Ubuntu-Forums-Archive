### Post by iamkuriouspurpleoranj on 2013-03-16
As far as I am aware, the Fedora Forum is non-official but is very good and surely exactly what you are looking for. There is no **official** Fedora Forum in the sense that the Ubuntu Forums are officially part of the Ubuntu project. The Fedora project does however maintain a help site called Ask Fedora.
