### Post by qyot27 on 2011-11-04
The classic menu itself seems to be implemented by an alternative shell:

[http://classicshell.sourceforge.net/index.html](http://classicshell.sourceforge.net/index.html)

As for *really* alternative shells that you could try:
Cairo Shell comes to mind immediately, because it does have some general similarities to GNOME 2 in its default configuration, but A) it's still branded as being of pre-release quality and B) you'd have to really be dedicated to customizing it to get the menus right.  I've played with the Milestone 6 Beta some, but my computer is a decade old and therefore can't do so very well - a Vista- or Win7-level setup would probably be fine.

Other shells are Emerge Desktop or SharpEnviro.
