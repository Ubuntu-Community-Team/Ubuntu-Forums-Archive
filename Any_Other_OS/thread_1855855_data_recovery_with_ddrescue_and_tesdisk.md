### Post by mips on 2011-10-20
When you plug in a usb drive it gets mounted and in linux it appears under /media (not sure where OS X puts it.

ddrescue /dev/sdYn /media/sdaXn/*imagefilename.img* *logfile.log* Where Yn & Xn is the drive/partition.

Alternatively from the commandline cd to the USB drive, create a folder/directory called recovery etc, cd recovery and just run *ddrescue /dev/sdYn imagefilename.img logfile.log* and the image file will be saved in that folder.
