### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-03-18
I can not comment on **Joli OS** as it is now known, but I have **Lubuntu** on my netbook and love it.  It is fast, a near match to the standard layout of gnome that I am used to, and has all the packages of ubuntu at its disposal, and as I can't manage without Firefox, and personally don't like the layout of chromium, I have that as well as LibreOffice installed.

As for other possible OSs, what about bodhi, very fast and an interesting DE, using E17 Enlightenment.  The DE takes a little bit of getting used to, but I suspect it would be very good on a netbook.  Again, it uses the standard ubuntu repositories, so any ubuntu packages can be installed.

I am sure there are many other possibilities, but at least if you stick to OSs that use ubuntu repos, you know that here is huge potential for installing what you want.
