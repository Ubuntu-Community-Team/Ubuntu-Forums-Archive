This is an odd one. I suspect it may have something to do with Pulseaudio.

I've just installed Mint 11 (based on Natty) and Flash videos play back in double speed.

So do songs in Spotify: the seeker just zips along as fast as it's buffering.

Any tips?

**EDIT:** In sound preferences my default sound device was set to my HDMI on the graphics card. I disabled this device and chose my internal analogue instead. This fixed the problem.
