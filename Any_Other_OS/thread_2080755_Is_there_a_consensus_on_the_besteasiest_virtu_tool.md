### Post by ibjsb4 on 2012-11-04
+1 to Derek.  Although others report good gaming in vBox, I too think dual boot would be a better choice for heavy cpu use.   How much power (cpu) and ram you have will be a big factor.

The two big dog's are VirtualBox and VMware for the everyday user.  I tried both and went with vBox, but both work good.  Im running dual core and 3G of ram so heavy gaming is out for guest systems.

Here in the virtual subforum its mainly vBox that gets discussed.
