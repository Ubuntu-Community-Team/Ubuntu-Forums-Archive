### Post by Kirboosy on 2014-04-10
Welcome to the forums! I'm going to move your thread to the Other OS support.

From my experience using DBAN if I left the USB stick plugged in DBAN would wipe the stick out with the rest of the drives. Try to recreate the stick as before and boot off of it. Once it boots up and you start the wiping sequence try pulling the USB stick out before it registers the USB devices. See if that keeps the stick working properly.

Hope that helps!
