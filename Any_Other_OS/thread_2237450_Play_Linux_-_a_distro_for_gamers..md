Most of the gamers are students, and they have to work too, right?
So we bring you this distro to make your life easier, it comes with everything a gamer needs.

**Play Linux** comes with:
[LIST]
[*]**Cinnamon** Desktop Environment, a fast, fluid and stable Desktop Environment; 
[*]**Steam**, so you can play native games; 
[*]**LibreOffice**, so you can do your homework; 
[*]**Nvidia**, **AMD/ATI** and** Intel** GPU drivers and automatically enables the right one needed; 
[*]**Firefox**, a powerful open-source browser that does not spy you :) ; 
[*]**Spotify**. Music players are old. Stream more than 20 million songs, for free. 
[*]**VLC** media player; 
[*]**Skype**, because you need to chat; 
[*]**WINE**, Winetricks and PlayOnLinux, for those who don't want to leave their Windows applications and games; 
[*]**Mint** **Desktop Manager** with a beautiful login screen theme; 
[*]A set of themes, icons and wallpapers: 
[/LIST]
          -> **Numix** GTK Theme adapted for Play Linux;
          ->** Foba **and **Moka** Icon themes;
          -> **Tyr himinn** Cinnamon Theme;
          -> A gorgeous **Orion nebula** wallpaper.
[LIST]
[*]Adobe **Flash Player**; 
[*]**Java** 7; 
[*]Ubuntu **Software Center**; 
[*]Ubuntu **Software Updater**; 
[*]**GIMP**; 
[*]and many more apps. 
[/LIST]
