### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-04-19
I doubt that you installed Ubuntu on an HFS+ partition.  So you would have to reformat the drive to HFS+ using the disk utility, which is a pick on the boot-up menu.  It's possible that your DVD or the DVD drive is dirty.  It's also possible that the DVD drive is defective.  Can you read other Ubuntu DVD's?

Regarding video editing, give [http://openshot.org](http://openshot.org) a try.
