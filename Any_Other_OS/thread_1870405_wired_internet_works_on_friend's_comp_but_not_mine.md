### Post by CharlesA on 2011-10-27
Just unplug it from power for about 2 to 5 minutes then plug it back in.

Hook up the router to the ethernet port on the modem when it's unplugged as well.

When everything goes back up, it should be able to get to the internet after hooking up to the router.
