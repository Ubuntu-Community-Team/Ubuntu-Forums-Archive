### Post by el_koraco on 2011-07-10
The sam way you would rewrite Ubuntu with Ubuntu. You pop a CentOS CD, boot it, and when you get to the install to hard drive section, you choose manual partitioning, and choose to use the Ubuntu partition as the one for CentOS, and to format it. Shouldn't take more than 20 minutes with the install.
