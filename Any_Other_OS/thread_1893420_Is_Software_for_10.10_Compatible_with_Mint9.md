### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-12-10
I presume (from your previous thread) that you mean [APTonCD]("http://aptoncd.sourceforge.net/").

Yes, you can...
Yes, there may be compatibility problems...

Provided you install using the package manager, the package manager will not permit you to install something that breaks the compatibility.

However, some of those programs may not work properly with Mint -- they may be regressions, or tailored for Ubuntu.

I know that you have slow broadband, but you will save yourself much trouble if you just install the normal way while connected to the Internet. You have spent an entire week on your previous thread worrying about this; if you had simply installed the normal way, your problem would have been solved a week ago!
