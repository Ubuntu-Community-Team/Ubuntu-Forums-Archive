### Post by PhilGil on 2011-10-30
> **AZBoy28 said:**
> The USB is mainly booting from a Windows computer. But I really like JoliOS but unfortunately my 2GB USB won't fit it! Is there a way around this?
Not that I'm aware of. Most standard Linux distros are going to need a 4 or 8 GB drive. On a 2GB USB you'll need a lightweight distro such as:

[Puppy Linux]("http://www.pendrivelinux.com/puppy-linux-on-usb/")
[DSL]("http://www.damnsmalllinux.org/")
[CrunchBang]("http://crunchbanglinux.org/forums/topic/12594/crunchbang-statler-live-persistent-usb-in-windows-without-unetbootin/")
