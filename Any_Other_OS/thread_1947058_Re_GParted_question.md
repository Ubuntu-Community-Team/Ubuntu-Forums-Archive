### Post by PhantomTurtle on 2012-03-27
Since you already have Windows, just boot into Windows. Run Disk Management(diskmgmt.msc). If you have not already delete the Mint partition. Right click your Windows parition (I assume you have windows installed on "C") and click extend volume. Just select the amount of the free space and add it to the Windows partition and you are done.
