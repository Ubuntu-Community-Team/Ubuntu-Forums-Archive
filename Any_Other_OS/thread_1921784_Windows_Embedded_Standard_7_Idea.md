### Post by WinRiddance on 2012-02-08
Do what we did ... get yourself MagicJack PLUS. Have one single computer with a small windoze partition, the remaining space for your Linux. You only need Windoze to register the new MJ PLUS. Once registered, remove it from the computer and plug it into your wall outlet and router instead. The new MJ PLUS has much better voice quality too. We have two MJ PLUS lines that are running directly from our router and wall outlets (power strip actually).
Search the main forum for magicjack (one word) and you'll find everything you need to know.
