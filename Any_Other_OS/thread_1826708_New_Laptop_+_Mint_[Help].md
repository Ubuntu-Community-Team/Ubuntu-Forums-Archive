### Post by halj32 on 2011-08-17
Hi
as long as you don't delete your recovery partition when you install Linux Mint it will be available to use if you ever want to reinstall windows 7 from it. 
You can also back it up to a DVD or external hard drive, the easiest way is just copy your key & certificate & use a win 7 DVD to reinstall, you can use something like this "Advanced Tokens Manager" if you don't know how to do it yourself
