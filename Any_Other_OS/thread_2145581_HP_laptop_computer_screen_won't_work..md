### Post by mips on 2013-05-16
Does the screen come on at all under BIOS, Win7 or Linux LiveCD?

If 'no' then you have a hardware issue. It could be something as simple as a lose connector or it could be dead power circuitry or dead backlight wtc.

With the laptop on and windows booted up shine a torch or light onto the lcd and look closely to see if you can see any image in the 'background'
