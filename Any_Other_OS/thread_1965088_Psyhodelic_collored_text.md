### Post by mips on 2012-04-25
Looks like a font issue related to ClearType & Subpixel rendering. [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ClearType](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ClearType)

You can turn it off somewhere in the settings but I can't remember where. Some applications however implement their own instance of it so it might not help turning it off. Maybe look at the font settings in winamp as well and try changing the font, [http://malektips.com/wa50015.html](http://malektips.com/wa50015.html)
