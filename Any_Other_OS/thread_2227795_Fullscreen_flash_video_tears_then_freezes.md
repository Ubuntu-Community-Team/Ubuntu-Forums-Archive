### Post by d-cosner on 2014-06-04
Here is how I fix full screen flash on my computers. Turn off hardware acceleration in Firefox. (It is not supported anyway.) Then I start a flash video, make it full screen, right click on the video and turn off hardware acceleration in flash itself. You may need to restart Firefox for the change to take effect.
