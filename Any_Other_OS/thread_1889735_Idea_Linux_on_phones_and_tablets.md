### Post by hamolton on 2011-12-01
Base it off Ubuntu, Debian, Puppy, or whatever, I think it would be super cool to have Linux on phones, like, phone-skinned.
Major problems with current cell phone platforms (and tablets) are existing programs take time to port. If we based a OS off of Linux, we could have all the cool Linux goodies we have on desktop :D
I kinda thought of this when thinking about how Windows, WebOS, and Blackberry is failing so bad on phones and tablets without apps, it would be cool to port a platform with apps.
What we have right now is a bunch of random ports to phones and tablets. They are just like the desktop version, but impractical to use. Somebody posted [this]("http://www.ollie-reardon.co.uk/create-a-linux-tablet-distribution/") about tablets.
