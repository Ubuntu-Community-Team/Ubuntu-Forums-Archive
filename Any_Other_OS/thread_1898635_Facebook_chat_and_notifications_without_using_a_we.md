### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2011-12-21
Empathy (the default) is currently working fine for fb on MGSE *and* ubuntu 11.10.

You do need a username, though, get one here if you haven't already: [www.facebook.com/username]("www.facebook.com/username")


then fill in the Empathy setup form accordingly. Empathy is really cool in GNOME shell, the notifications are awesome. :D

If you use email at all, Thunderbird is really cool, that can do RSS too.
