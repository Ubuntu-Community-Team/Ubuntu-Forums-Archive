Nevermind. Questioned solved. Found my answer [here]("http://wiki.winehq.org/FAQ#head-faf9617c53607e583f6e6ff70a4ac9522d490faf")
```
WINEPREFIX=/media/nameofharddrive/foldername wine winecfg
```
