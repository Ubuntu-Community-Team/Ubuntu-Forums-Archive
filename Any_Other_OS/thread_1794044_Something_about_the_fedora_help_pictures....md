### Post by FormatSeize on 2011-06-30
Click on the link that says "Get Fedora" under Downloads. It will take you to another page that will have a big "Download Now!" on it. Don't click that, as that link seems to be taking me in circles. Instead click below it where it says "More download options". That will take you to a page that gives you the option of choosing 32 or 64 bit download. Either of those links will take you to the actual download. Make sure that any pop-up blockers are turned off, or you will go in cirlces again. 
 
I have no idea why the links are so much like jumping through hoops, though. But it shall all be worth it once you get to experience the best OS imaginable.
