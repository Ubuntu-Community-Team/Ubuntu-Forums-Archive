### Post by buzzingrobot on 2013-03-26
The current Fuduntu release is very nice. Check their site to see what they mean by "rolling".

Debian Squeeze is Gnome 2, and the original Solusos release is based on it.  

You can even do an install of Ubuntu Server 10.04 LTS and add "ubuntu-desktop" to get Gnome Lucid Lynx-style. The desktop is EOL at the end of this month, but the server won't be for two years.

At this point, I'd wait until MATE 1.6 comes out before I committed to it.  Believe that's due in April.

CentOS and Scientific Linux, recompilations of RHEL, are solid Gnome 2 products, but are beginning to show their age for desktop use.

We will see interfaces modeled on Gnome 2 -- MATE, SolusOS, probably Fuduntu at some point -- but Gnome 2 itself is dead and getting deader.
