### Post by Chdslv on 2012-11-22
After playing with upgrading many Ubuntu based distros, I decided to make one myself. It would be called Araliya Linux, if I ever be able to upload it to the Internet for all of you.

It is made out of 12.04 mini.iso. I had opted for Gala as the WM, and partly Pantheon. There is no top panel, even though Slingshot opens there. I had placed expanded Docky as the panel and Slingshot as the launcher. This Docky could be placed on any side of the screen. Whatever elementary OS Team's applications I'd use, they'd be frozen, once I feel that they are stable for everyone. They talk too much about bugs, to depend on the applications, even though they are nice looking. I'd opt for known and tested applications, such as nautilus, Shotwell etc.

It could be minimal distro of CD size or loaded with applications to 1.4-1.8 GB. The Ubuntu base is quite good, so the applications would work well.  

The distro works very well in nearly 4 year old desktop, laptop and a netbook. Hope I'd have enough time and finances to support the venture.

Araliya is the most popular flower in Sri Lanka.
