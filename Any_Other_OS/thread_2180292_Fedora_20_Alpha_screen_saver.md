### Post by buzzingrobot on 2013-10-12
Top panel, far right side:  Click on the rather small triangle/arrow.  A dropdown panel will display.  Click on the screwdriver/wrench icon in the bottom left corner,  "Settings" should open. (It's a bit crashy here.  After all, it's still an alpha, with final release almost 2 months off.)

The "Privacy" setting will expose a screen lock toggle. The "Power" setting will expose a screen dimming toggle and an option to avoid screen blanking.
