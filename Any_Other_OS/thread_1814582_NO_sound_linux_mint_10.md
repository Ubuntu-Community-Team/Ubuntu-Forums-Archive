### Post by zobcat on 2011-07-29
In Terminal run alsamixer and check for "MM" on any of the outputs. "MM" means it's muted. If you find one that is muted pressing "M" on your keyboard will unmute it. Also check for outputs with the volume set to "00" pressing the up arrow on those will raise the volume. Good luck.
