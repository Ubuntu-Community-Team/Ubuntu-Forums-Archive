### Post by DuckHook on 2016-07-30
Welcome to the forums, peichy.

You must change both your language settings and your keyboard layout. Just changing one of them isn't enough. Both settings are available via the settings gear at top right of system tray (assuming you use Ubuntu&#8213;you do not state your flavour): System Settings &#8594; Language Support &#8594; Install/Remove Languages, and then System Settings &#8594; Text Entry &#8594; "+" sign
