### Post by eric71 on 2012-06-07
Been using an install from the KDE live cd. I'm very happy with it - but I have very specific things that I'm looking for in a distro. Aside from that, it's very professionally done, and they seem to be really responsive responding to bugs and keep things very transparent regarding their organisation.
