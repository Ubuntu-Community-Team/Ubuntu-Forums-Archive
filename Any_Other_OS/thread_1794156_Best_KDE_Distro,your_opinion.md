### Post by isaacj87 on 2011-07-06
If it hasn't been mentioned already, the [Chakra Project]("http://chakra-project.org/"). Chakra is simply the best KDE distro around. It's clean, fast, and user-friendly. It's pure KDE with no GTK stuff added. If you need an app that has GTK deps, they have their Chakra Bundles that contains everything the app will need to run into one file. It's similar to a *.dmg file for OS X.

Another benefit of running Chakra is they keep their packages up-to-date. I just saw a posting for KDE 4.6.5 in their testing repos and the annoucement hasn't even been made on KDE.org!
