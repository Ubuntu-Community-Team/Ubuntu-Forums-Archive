### Post by snowpine on 2011-07-04
Pentium 4 with 512mb RAM is perfectly fine for either Xfce or LXDE. I recommend you try a Live CD of each to see which *you* prefer. You could even install both desktop environments simultaneously and choose between them depending on your mood that day... a Desktop Environment is simply a user interface or "skin." It is the same "core" operating system underneath, and the same user applications on top.

At the end of the day, the applications you choose (and how you use them) will have a bigger impact on performance than your choice of distro/desktop environment. For example I gave my old laptop a big performance boost by installing ad-block and flash-block plugins for my browser...
