### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-07-19
Like most things, it depends. 

If I had a reason to update Arch/Antergos daily, I'd subscribe to the right Arch mailing lists so I'd know when an update had gone awry, and I'd check the site and the forums before updating.

But, if I didn't have a reason to update daily, I might be happy with a weekly update. 

Avoiding updating as soon as some large, complex multi-package system hits the servers -- like the new KDE Plasma and Frameworks packages -- seems a good idea.  Let other people get hit with any problems lurking there.

(BTW, I've always wondered what really happens while Manjaro waits a week.)
