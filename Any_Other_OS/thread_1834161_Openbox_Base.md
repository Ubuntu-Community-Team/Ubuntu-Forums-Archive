### Post by snowpine on 2011-08-27
Openbox is a very small download. You can install Openbox on your distro of choice (Arch?). If you have the bandwidth to download the very frequent Arch upgrades, then you have the bandwidth to download Openbox. Arch is unquestionably one of the very best and most popular "Openbox-based distros" so I see no compelling need for your to distro-hop. :)

I am a big CrunchBang fan and moderator on their forums. I have only good things to say about CrunchBang, however if you are an Arch user then you probably like "rolling" upgrades of the latest software; CrunchBang will not provide this (since it is based on Debian Stable with older, well-tested software). In other words, stick with Arch+Openbox is my advice. ;)
