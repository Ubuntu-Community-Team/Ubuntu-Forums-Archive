### Post by coffeecat on 2013-09-16
Linux Mint question.

*Thread moved to **Other OS/Distro Support**.*

@africanlion, The version of Mint that you are using is based on Ubuntu 12.04. Support for mounting Android devices was not ideal in 12.04, but I have no problem with Ubuntu 13.04 and my Android tablet. I suggest you try a live CD of either Ubuntu 13.04 or Mint 15 Olivia and see if your phone is recognised in the live session. If it is, you might consider upgrading to (or re-installing with) one of those later operating systems. However, they are not long-support releases, which Ubuntu 12.04 and Mint 13 are, and this might be a consideration for you.
