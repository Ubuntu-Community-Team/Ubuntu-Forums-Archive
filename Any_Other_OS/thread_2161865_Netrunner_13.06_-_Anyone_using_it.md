### Post by speartip on 2013-07-12
This has to be one of the best KDE distros out there. (IMO)
I know some would say that it's just Kubuntu tweaked, but a lot of care & attention to detail seems to have gone into this Distro. It just works & looks good.
 All I did after installation was update, change the login screen background, (which again IMO is too loud) & adjust the font size.
Nearly everything you need is preinstalled.
 I was waiting for Mint 15 KDE, but think this one is a keeper.
 Amazes me that Netrunner sits at no.60 in the Distrowatch table.
 A must have for distro hoppers everywhere.

### Post by buzzingrobot on 2013-07-12
I'm using and very much liking it.  It's been on my machine for 4 days, which is 3 and 1/2 days longer than I typically stay with KDE.

I've been trying and rejecting KDE for years.  Netrunner has got it right. It's fast, it looks very good, it's been stable and solid.

Folks who've never been able to get along with KDE ought to take a look.

(The default wallpaper wasn't to my tastes, either, but few are.  The "Kubuntu tweaked" charge isn't fair or relevant. Kubuntu feels undone compared to this.)
