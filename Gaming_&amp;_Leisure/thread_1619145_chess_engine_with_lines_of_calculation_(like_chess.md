### Post by dev.null on 2010-11-11
Back in my windows days (10+ years ago) I used chessbase. I loved it. I didn't play against it, I used it to examine the board and have it's engine calculate out the next 5-10 most powerful lines.

It helped a lot to see what the calculation showed to be the strongest lines and if I wanted to see why I could either explore that line by following it's moves or just visualize the moves because the lines would be listed out to the next 5 or so moves.

I'm looking for a good replacement to this tool. I've used a couple chess "engines" in ubuntu, but for the most part they just seem to play you in chess, not actually show you lines of calculation based off of the current position.

Any suggestions?

Thanks!
