### Post by Perfect Storm on 2012-09-01
Try remove and then re-install gnome-games. It had undergoing change since 10.04 and it may it have been messed up doing your upgrade.

gnome-games, gnome-games-data (If you want sudoku also install gnome-sudoku).

Then hit <super key> (the key with the windows logo), then type; **aisleriot**
click it. Now you have your card games :)


You can also use other words to search on, like; card, game, etc.
