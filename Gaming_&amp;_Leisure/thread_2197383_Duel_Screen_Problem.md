### Post by dannyboy79 on 2014-01-03
if you're running the game in fullscreen, it will always launch on the main screen. I think you'll have to rearrange your screens so that the 24" is your main screen and the laptop is your 2nd display. then it should work.  OR you launch the game in windowed mode and you should be able to drag the window over.

### Post by MrMichaelHill on 2014-01-05
Easy fix!

Under the video settings it should have Full Screen (0) and Full Screen (1) and Windowed.

The 0 and 1 represent monitors :)
