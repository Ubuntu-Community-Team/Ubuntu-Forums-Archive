### Post by jukingeo on 2011-01-12
Hello All,

I know this has been asked before, but it has been a while.  As the subject says, I am looking for single player point and click adventure games that are in the vein of the old Windows 3.1 classic game "Myst".  

Basically these games usually involve pointing and clicking (hence the name) to find various items that you need.  You also use the name point and click interface to navigate around.  Depending on the game, sometimes you do interact with non-playing characters, but for the most part you are presented with puzzles to help you progress through the games.

Other types of games that are like this are:

1) Siberia
2) Alone In The Dark
3) The Nancy Drew game series
4) Lighthouse: The Dark Being

I prefer something that happens to be in Synaptic, but that isn't a prerequisite.
