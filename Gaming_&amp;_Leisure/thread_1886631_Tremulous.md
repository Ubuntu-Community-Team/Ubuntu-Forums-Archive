### Post by donkyhotay on 2011-11-25
Tremulous doesn't have bots, it's not a regular FPS where you run around shooting things (though that is a large part of it). You have to build up a base and work together to eliminate the enemy. It's really a team game and Bot's don't do well working as a team. You'll probably be branded as a noob but try to find some online server with people that allows noobs.
