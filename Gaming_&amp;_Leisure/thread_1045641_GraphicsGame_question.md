### Post by Irritant on 2009-01-21
Wolfy - if the version of Alien Arena is 7.21, it's likely that the reason your experiencing framerates dropping with rockets and others that produce light is that your card/drivers cannot handle GLSL shaders very well.  Try disabling dynamic lights in the game options, or GLSL in the video options.  Either should signifigantly improve your framerates.  I believe Nexuiz and a couple others use GLSL as well for many effects, and that would cause them to perform poorly too.
