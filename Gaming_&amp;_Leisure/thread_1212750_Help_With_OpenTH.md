### Post by JaxWeb on 2009-07-16
Hello, this is Jack from OpenTH.

As charlieg pointed out, OpenTH is not playable yet. In fact, as we've just changed the internals it is not worth compiling at all unless you are working on it!

However, if you want to follow progress, then the best thing to do is check the Blog (maybe sign up for the RSS) and also sign up for the (very low frequency) mail list, which can be signed up to [here]("https://lists.sourceforge.net/lists/listinfo/openth-announce").

If you are a Theme Hospital fan and you want to help shape the game, post your suggestions or ideas on the forum. Artists, translators, C++ programmers, Lua programmers, etc., are all more than welcome too.

Thanks,
- Jack
