### Post by Con_Brio1993 on 2011-01-09
[http://www.realmofthemadgod.com/](http://www.realmofthemadgod.com/)

Realm of the mad god is a pretty fun game. 

Pretty much, you are dropped in a world where you must fend for yourself by going into the wild, killing monsters, and picking up equipment. As you level up by killing monsters, you will be able to fight more powerful monsters and this goes on until you reach level 20. At level 20 you can continue to pick up superb equipment or you can work on unlocking other classes by making a new character. What makes the game shine is the simplicity of it, there aren't any complex things to remember or storyline. 

Also, when you die, it's like fire emblem. Your character is dead forever and you must start fresh. If there is someone nice around you they might pick up your stuff, but don't count on it. Be cautious when exploring new monsters.

Not sure where to post this bit, but it is related to the game so I might as well bring it up: 

I played the game extensively a few years back. At the time I was running Windows XP. My current machine runs Ubuntu and I've been unable to get past the tutorial level and the main world.
