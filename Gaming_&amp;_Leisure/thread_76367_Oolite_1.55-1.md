[*]Eye candy updates: completely new ship models, completely new explosions
[*]AI improved - enemies are much better shots, docking computer much improved

[*]Equipment damage, Trumbles (bless 'em), ship servicing, atmospheric friction, witch space fuel required indicator, nasty new ECM-immune missiles
[*]Options (F2) menu now available in flight when paused
[*]Many bugs fixed
[*]Joystick support
[*]New autopackage installer
[/LIST]
