### Post by sakuramboo on 2010-09-08
The CD/DVD does not have the Linux installer, but it is needed by the installer.

[ftp://ftp.idsoftware.com/idstuff/etqw/](ftp://ftp.idsoftware.com/idstuff/etqw/)

This is the installer you need when you get a copy of the game.

[ftp://ftp.idsoftware.com/idstuff/etqw/ETQW-client-1.4-full.x86.run](ftp://ftp.idsoftware.com/idstuff/etqw/ETQW-client-1.4-full.x86.run)

When you run it, it will ask where you are installing from, select CD/DVD, enter the registration code and install.
