**Storyline:**
(Events before the actual game)

> In July, 1947, a Droma spacecraft crash landed on Earth. The US Army quickly seized the wreckage along with the alien bodies and erased all evidence of the crash. The US Army studied the aliens and their technology for years until a Droma search and rescue craft located the distress beacon aboard the earlier craft.

The leader of the second craft made first contact with the commander of the Roswell Army Airfield on May 14, 1956. The Droma presented themselves as a peaceful race in search of other such peaceful worlds. After the US releases the Droma craft, the Droma and the US sign a secret peace treaty. As a sign of good faith, the Droma provided the US with some of their technology.

However, the Droma only appeared to be peaceful and were in fact, planning Earth's demise. Quel-Droma was overpopulated and the Droma were searching for new places to expand. Planets like Earth and Quel-Droma are very hard to find so when the Droma found Earth, they decided to conquer it.

For years the Droma worked on plans to conquer Earth. The Droma decided to take their time so as to prevent the Earth being destroyed by nuclear weapons in a massive war. The Droma decided to turn the Earth's population against itself.

Over the next hundred years, war destroyed Earth's population and the Droma were behind it all. The Droma had decided to instigate wars between neighboring countries in an effort to break down the population of Earth.

On January 2, 2059, after shooting down an attacking craft, the French discovered the Droma and exactly what they were up to. As soon as the Droma learned of the lost craft, they decided to make an all out attack. By this time, the Droma had amassed large forces out of view of Earth. It only took the Droma 6 days to get their forces in position. Over the next 6 days, billions died. One of the last surviving outposts was in Leiden, The Netherlands. This last group, in a final effort to kill as many Droma as possible, infected themselves with a man made virus called SDIV-024. These brave people were called the Mortea They succeeded in killing millions of Droma. Even so, the war only lasted 12 days.

During the invasion, the Droma took many slaves. The human slaves were put on prison ships bound for work camps and medical stations. The slaves managed to take over some of the prison ships, and discovered a new world of their own. They called the new world Azurea. The survivors made a new life for themselves. Over the next 300 years, Azurea became the last free human colony and it's existence was unknown to the Droma.

Now in 2365, a rumor exists that tells a tale of a surviving Mortae. If this rumor is true, the descendants of such a survivor would be key in producing a virus that would kill the Droma, and producing a cure for the humans.

The leaders of Azurea have been sending small scouting ships to other planets in search of new places to colonize. You are on one of these ships when it is found and attacked by the Droma. Facing overwhelming numbers, your captain decides surrender is the only option. Now as a prisoner on a Droma ship on your way to an interrogation center, you must insure the secrecy of Azurea. 

Subject to change.
