### Post by oldrocker99 on 2009-12-12
For turn-based strategy, Battle for Wesnoth is top-notch and has tons of plugins available. For RTS games, there's glob2 (Globulation2), a very original RTS in which you cannot directly control your player characters but rather provide upgrade buildings and, through it all, you assign each structure needing maintenance or construction how many workers you wish it to have. There's also Spring, which aims to recreate that great old Total Annihilation feel and succeeds quite well. Those are the best two RTS Linus games, IMHO.
