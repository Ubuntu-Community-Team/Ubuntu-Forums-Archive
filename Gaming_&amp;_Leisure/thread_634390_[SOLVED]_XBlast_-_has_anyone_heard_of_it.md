### Post by acoustibop on 2007-12-07
Just make your own launcher in the menu(s) you want it to appear in, MozartlovesUbun2.  If the installation package doesn't put a launcher in the Games menu, there won't be one.

Right click on Applications and click Edit Menus.  Select the menu you want and click +New Item.  Fill in the Create Launcher window that appears - the Name and Command boxes are the only ones you have to fill in - and click OK.
