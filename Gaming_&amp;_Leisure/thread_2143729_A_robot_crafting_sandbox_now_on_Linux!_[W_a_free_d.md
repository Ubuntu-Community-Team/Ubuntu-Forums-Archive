### Post by javehdez on 2013-05-09

Rawbots is a robot crafting sandbox game where you can play with amazing robots build and program by you.
It has been under development for almost a year now and still has a large way to go, with plans of online multiplayer, blueprint's marketplace and multiple universes, possibilities like robot 1on1 or team based fights, role-playing on huge cities/universes, RTS, CTF, FPS scenarios come to mind.

[IMG]http://rawbots.ipbhost.com/uploads/gallery/album_4/gallery_6_4_208706.png[/IMG]

The game is at a "raw" stage at this time and even so it delivers tons of fun for those who like crafting and engineering stuff, [youtube]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_embedded&list=PLftdLQvODK8aBPFaWpR1olLJuJTYT9bbI&v=DDAN7nNJteo") has great examples of bots made by the community that shows the great potential of the game. Some of the worst reviews points to it's steep learning curve and at the time poor designed UI, that's the reason why the developers are throwing a [kickstarter]("http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/rozgo/rawbots-multiplayer-sandbox") campaign so they'll be able to take the game from the current state to the desired one, they have also given a free demo while the kickstarter campaign is running that can be found on a project's [update]("http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/rozgo/rawbots-multiplayer-sandbox/posts/475539"). 

Stop by and check what this game has to offer to the great Linux community!
