### Post by lnxnut on 2009-10-22
I have 4 computers that Runescape frequents.  One with ATI, two with Nvidia, one with Intel.  All four of them are capable of running Runescape HD.  All of them will freeze when running Java6.  It may be 5 minutes or it may be 30 minutes but they will eventually freeze.  What I have found is that I can remove Java6 and install Java5 and I will lose the full screen HD but I will still have the browser HD.  And it will run all day long and never freeze.  I tested karmic some a couple of weeks ago and I found that they had removed Java5 from the repository.  If you don't have the same problem I do you will probably be fine.  But if I had Intrepid working well I would probably duel boot for a while.
