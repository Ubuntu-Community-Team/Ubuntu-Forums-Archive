### Post by Fred_E _krugar on 2008-06-15
I believe on Ubuntu it is at System> Administration> System Monitor.


It is much like windows ctrl alt delete,


Once there just scroll down till you find Pulseaudio and right click and Kill process. You should be able to restart your sound sources with no problem.


I hope this helps ya.
