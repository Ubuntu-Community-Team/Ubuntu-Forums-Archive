### Post by kutagh on 2009-01-29
I used to play RoboRally regulary too. Still got the board game :)

If there is a pit, you will always fall in it once you walk on/over that square or get pushed there or whatever.
Those pits are always active, and don't open up. However, your can make a variation where the pits don't open till the fabric elements start working. RoboRally is really open for customization.

Feel free to ask more things.

I hope that the guys who are digitalizing RoboRally include several options for it too, that you  can customize it to the extend that you like, such as shutdown.
