### Post by keltren on 2008-04-04
I don't want to scare you, but I've had the same and it turned out the memory on my gfx card was shot...

Does this also happen in other games? A screenshot might also be helpful to identify the problem.

There's a known problem that manifests as dark grey/black areas spreading out from objects in the game - setting GLSL to no in the cedega settings helps with that from what I remember.

But if you're seeing fine multi-colored lines on the screen that smells of gfx ram corruption...
