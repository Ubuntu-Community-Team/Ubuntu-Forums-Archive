### Post by wizardbox on 2012-08-25
Heya everyone... Just wanted to fill you in on some updates to my now-somewhat-infamous project "Fall of Eternity"

Link to my Indiegogo: [http://www.indiegogo.com/FoE?a=865678](http://www.indiegogo.com/FoE?a=865678)

This is an action-RPG being developed with Blender and Python in Ubuntu 12.04. Compiled versions will be licensed under the GNU GPL 2.0. I will compile it for Windows too, but OSX folks will have to compile it on their own because, well, I can't publicly endorse Apple in good conscience since they refuse to share GPL software in their app market. 

Your character is a cursed demigod.. The player begins the game at level 99 and ends at level 0. You must choose which skills to sacrifice as the game goes on. You begin the game as a valiant young soldier, and end as a crippled old man. The game features bizarre psychedelic randomized dungeons, an explorable hub town with NPCs, 33 skills, real-time physics, and a multitude of items and weapons.

If you could be so kind, please help spread the word about this game, or leave a small donation if you can. Every dollar goes a long way.. I'm making this whole thing by myself :) Regardless of how well-funded this project winds up being, I'm committed to making this game because it's simply what I enjoy doing with my time. 

Given the severely limited budget, it's not going to break any new ground in terms of graphics, but the gameplay is truly unlike anything you've ever seen.
