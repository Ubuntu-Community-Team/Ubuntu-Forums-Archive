### Post by charlieg on 2008-02-29
There was Zugspiel, but it's not been updated for years:
[http://www.ph2.net/zugspiel/](http://www.ph2.net/zugspiel/)

Other than that these three are your best bet:
FreeTrain: [http://freetrain.sourceforge.net/](http://freetrain.sourceforge.net/)
Simutrans: [http://simutrans.sourceforge.net/](http://simutrans.sourceforge.net/)
OpenTTD: [http://www.openttd.org/](http://www.openttd.org/)

Please note that you have to download abandonware to run OpenTTD at the moment (it requires the original TTD data files but they are easy to come by).
