### Post by module0000 on 2010-01-25
If you can continue to hear/play the game after your screen goes black, you probably have a screensaver/power-management setting going into effect.

Check if you have a "blank screen" screensaver, also check if you have "blank screen after <x> minutes" in power options.  Currently input from a joystick/gamepad does not count as "activity" from power management's view - it thinks you are sitting there doing nothing and will enable screensaver and/or blank screen.

Some emulators have an option to disable power management while they are active.  If yours' does not have this option, consider turning off your screensaver and disabling the "blank screen after <x> minutes" option from power management.

Hope this helps.
