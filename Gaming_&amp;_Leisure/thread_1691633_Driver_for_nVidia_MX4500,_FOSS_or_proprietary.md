### Post by cascade9 on 2011-02-20
There is no MX4500. You probably mean MX4000. 

The drivers arent that old. The newest version was released 16-11-2010. Which isnt that bad, considering that the MX4000 is just a rebadged (and downclocked!) GF4MX440 8X, released in october 2002.....

The few times I've tried the nouveau (open source) drivers with a GF4MX440, they havent been as good as the closed source drivers, even for desktop use.
