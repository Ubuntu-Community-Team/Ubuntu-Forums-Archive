### Post by scu-ba-de-buntu on 2008-08-25
Hey, this may be late, but I found your question while searching for an answer for mine. I use the Pelican DDR Pad. It is designed for Playstation, XBox, and Wii. It is not designed for PC, but after understanding that M$ couldn't design there own crap hardware, you realize that they had to use someone else's. They cannot patent USB, but they can change the face of the plug... yep - Xbox controllers are USB devices. Just solder the corresponding colours and leave the yellow wire out. I don't have a link, but you could probably find one easily - and really don't need one.

Cut
Solder
Electrical tape up
Plug-in

sudo modprobe xpad
./stepmania

Note you may need to modprobe one more thing depending on your setup. If you do, search for it, as I cannot remember what it is since I don't use it.

Cheers.
