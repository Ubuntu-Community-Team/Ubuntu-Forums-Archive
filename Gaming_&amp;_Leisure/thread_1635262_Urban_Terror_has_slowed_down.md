### Post by NightwishFan on 2010-12-01
Urban Terror has a nice little habit of accumulating cruft in it somehow. Try extracting it fresh from the zip again and see if that helps. Also, you might be affected by the one bug that causes performance issues on some machines. The fix for that is to use the optimised executables.

Linux 32-bit: [http://www0.org/urt/ioq3-1803-urt-011210-linux32.tar.bz2](http://www0.org/urt/ioq3-1803-urt-011210-linux32.tar.bz2)
Linux 64-bit: [http://www0.org/urt/ioq3-1803-urt-011210-linux64.tar.bz2](http://www0.org/urt/ioq3-1803-urt-011210-linux64.tar.bz2)

Also some simple tips are keeping the resolution at say 800x600 for improved performance. Though you of course stated performance was better before.

With the latest Lucid updates Urban Terror performs as expected (40-50fps) on my Intel Series 4 Mobile. On 10.10, it is sluggish and requires me to use 32-bit, and the optimised executables do not work. It kept me on 10.04 for a time though I upgraded anyway.
