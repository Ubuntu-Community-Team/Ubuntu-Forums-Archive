### Post by Carpentr on 2012-08-15
Check out Castle Marrach:
[http://www.skotos.net/games/marrach/]("http://www.skotos.net/games/marrach/")

It is a pay-to-play MUD ($12.95/mo), but it does come with a 30 day free trial. It is RP-Enforced and more of like a MUSH than a MUD. Success and advancement is dependent on role-playing, not skills. There are barely any skills in the game.

It is the best online text-based game around, when it comes to role-playing, in my opinion.
