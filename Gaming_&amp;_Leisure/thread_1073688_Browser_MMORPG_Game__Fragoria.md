### Post by MaximB on 2009-02-18
Today I came across a very good looking free browser mmorpg game named Fragoria.
This game is developed by a Russian company named RUSOFTWARE Co.

From the website : 

"Lots of quests, monsters awaits You together with thousands of Fragoria players all over the World.
Now it's in Russia and Germany.
Next steps will be China, USA, Japan, Taiwan, Hong-Kong, Macao, Vietnam.
Soon. Lots of Europe countries and Middle East.

And we will start Global servers of Fragoria to see who's the best, who's the strongest and who's the wisest one!

We salut You in Fragoria."

So sadly for now this game is only available in Russian and German.
To play the Russian version you must register a free account at [www.mail.ru](www.mail.ru) .

This game is a Flash based game so no right click ;)
But it is working on Linux.

Website : [http://fragoria.com/](http://fragoria.com/)
Screenshots : [http://fragoria.com/art.php](http://fragoria.com/art.php)
