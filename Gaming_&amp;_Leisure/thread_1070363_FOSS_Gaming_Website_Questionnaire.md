Hello,

My name is Canes, I will be doing research on some of the Linux Gaming community interests. I wanted to get some data collected from everyone and ask them some questions about their favorite games.

I wanted to put together a website that I will be working on over the next month, or however long it takes me to produce a solid product. I would also appreciate everyone's ideas and input on what they like to see on their gaming websites.

I have collected data from commercial gaming websites, (i.e. ign.com) but I would like to get right into the barracks and get the soldiers opinions, so to speak. Without further ado, let us begin!

1) When you go to a gaming community website, what is the first thing you are looking for? (i.e. reviews/previews/cheats/forums/strategy_etc)

2) Do you mind advertisements, as long as they do not interfere with your browsing experience and are of related content such as a Newegg ad?

3) Would you rather a website with, or without a forum?

4) What Linux-platform games do you most enjoy?

5) If you had it your way, what would you like to do with the website?


This website is going to be a "Network" of more than just gaming. Since I am such a gaming enthusiast though, I had to add a Free and Open Source gaming section to my network.

I am not only an advocate of Freedom and Liberty within one's personal life, but I am also guilty of advocating Free and Open Source Software. I would like to see the Linux Gaming Community grow to it's full potential, so I will do my small part to make sure that happens.

Along with the gaming section of my network, everyone will have access to the News and Educational channels as well. I rely purely on community feedback, so what the network becomes, is what the people have demanded of it.

I appreciate all who post their opinions here, it really helps me to focus on the interests of the group I am trying to target. Thank you all in advance for providing me with first hand information. 




Best Regards,

### Post by Nevon on 2009-02-24
**1) When you go to a gaming community website, what is the first thing you are looking for? (i.e. reviews/previews/cheats/forums/strategy_etc)**
Usually reviews and/or cheats.

**2) Do you mind advertisements, as long as they do not interfere with your browsing experience and are of related content such as a Newegg ad?**
Not sure what a Newegg ad is, but no, I don't mind advertisements as long as they don't come in the way of huge flash banners or popups. Contextual links are fine (as long as they're not that annoying type that displays a huge tooltip when you hover over them).

**3) Would you rather a website with, or without a forum?**
Depends on the type of website, but for this, I would prefer to have some sort of forum. Make sure it's VERY well intergrated into the website though. I hate it when websites use stock forums that don't blend in with the rest of the website at all.

**4) What Linux-platform games do you most enjoy?**
I don't play a lot of Linux games. At least not free (as in speech) ones. I just don't think they're up to snuff. However, I tend to buy good quality commercial games whenever they're available for Linux.

**5) If you had it your way, what would you like to do with the website?**
Not sure, because I don't know what it is you're trying to accomplish.
