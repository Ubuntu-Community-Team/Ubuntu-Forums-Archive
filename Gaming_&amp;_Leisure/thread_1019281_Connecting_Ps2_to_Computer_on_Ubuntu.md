### Post by mikedep333 on 2008-12-23
What are you asking to do exactly? Are you asking to use your monitor as a screen for your PS2? If so, you have two options.


1. Go with a TV Tuner (with a composite or s-video input) to hook your PS2 to your desktop itself or your laptop. However, They often don't have the best compatibility with linux, and often introduce a delay in the process of making it show up on the screen. The advantage to doing this, is that it would make it possible to record the video from the PS2.

2. Go with a TVbox. These devices often let you hook up your PS2 with composite, s-video, or component (the best input which supports 480p resolution, not just 480i) directly to your monitor.
