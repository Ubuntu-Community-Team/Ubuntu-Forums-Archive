### Post by Naiki Muliaina on 2010-09-02
I assume your using Ubuntu 10.04?

Click **System > Preferences > Main Menu**

Click **Games** in the **left column**

Click **New Item**

Type : Application
Name : DJL
Command : (click Browse and find djl.sh)

Click the picture and add whatever picture you want.

Click OK


Should be done :)

I haven't used Djl, for over a year. So I haven't got it installed currently.
