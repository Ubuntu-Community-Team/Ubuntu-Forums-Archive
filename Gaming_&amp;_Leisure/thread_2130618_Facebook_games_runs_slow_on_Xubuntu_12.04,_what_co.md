### Post by ManamiVixen on 2013-03-30
Well, Flash in general runs slower on Linux primarily because Adobe never really fine-tuned it on the linux platform and so runs slower and uses a lot of CPU. Have you tried Chrome? Google Chrome has a built in Flashplayer that is well suited to linux. It might run faster. Anyways Xubuntu will be fine on your Father's laptop. Heck, Try Unity 12.04 if he wants to use it.
