### Post by TidusBlade on 2007-10-20
It's an awesome [Rouglike](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roguelike) which evolved from [Hack](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hack_%28video_game%29) which evolved from [Rogue](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rogue_%28computer_game%29).
It's a pretty old game, but theres still people developing them, since theres tons of other evolutions, like Mines of Moria, ToME, Incursion and Crawl, just to name a few. Too bad many people these days tend to think that they suck because they got no graphics, just text :(
But you should really give it a try, you can get Rogue, hack and crawl by typing them in apt-get install ;) And use nethack-console for nethack.
