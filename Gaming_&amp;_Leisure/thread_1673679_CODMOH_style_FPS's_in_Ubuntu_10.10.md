### Post by Hur Dur on 2011-01-22
Depends on how you define "COD/MOH style." This could mean team-based. This could mean slow-paced. This could mean good graphics. It's a rather vague request.

I personally recommend Cube 2: Sauerbraten for a free FPS. It's not anything like Call of Duty or Medal of Honor, but one of the best free shooters in my opinion. 

Serious Sam 2 is available on Linux. It's not free, but it's an amazing game. Well worth the money.

Other good FPS for Linux:
Postal 2 (Costs money)
Amnesia (Costs money)
Penumbra (Costs money)
Warsow (Free)
Xonotic (Free)
Red Eclipse (Free, but appears to be dead.)
Unreal Tournament 2004 (Costs money)
Assault Cube (Free, if Cube 2 is too fast paced for you.)
Open Arena (Free, Quake III clone)
Doom series (Costs money)
Quake series (Costs money)
Duke Nukem 3D (Costs money)

RAGE is also coming out soon. You can also run Call of Duty on Wine.
