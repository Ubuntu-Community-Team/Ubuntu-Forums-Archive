### Post by leech on 2006-04-06
I don't think that's even a contest.  ATI's drivers suck, to put it bluntly.  Sadly, nVidia is really the only choice if you have a higher end system.  If you have an older system, the Matrox G series of cards have open source drivers and work quite well.  Ther P series cards... well when I sold my Parhelia, it had triple-head working, and I could even play Neverwinter Nights across three screens!  But Unreal based games would not work, and the performance of the card was good, but not spectacular.  I since bought a nVidia 6800GT and it kicks some major butt.  It's nice having a video card that actually works well in Linux without a lot of headaches.
