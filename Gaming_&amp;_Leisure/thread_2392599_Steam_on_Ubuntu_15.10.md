### Post by QIII on 2018-05-23
Hello!

You might want to direct your question [here]("https://help.steampowered.com/en/").  If your issue is that Steam is requiring fglrx (for very old Linux releases) or AMDGPU/AMDGPU-PRO (for more recent releases), then there is not much we can do to help you get around that.  As far as I know, Steam has always required the proprietary AMD driver for AMD graphics processors.

Unfortunately, you card is not supported by AMDGPU or AMDGPU-PRO (and there is nothing the open source community can do about that) and the fglrx driver is no longer under development and will not work with your release (also, nothing the open source community can do about that).

However, the Steam community may have some insider knowledge that will help you out.

And I will stress that 15.10 is well beyond its EOL, is no longer supported and has long since run out of security updates.  I would most certainly not expose it to the web.

Sorry that I don't have better news. for you.
