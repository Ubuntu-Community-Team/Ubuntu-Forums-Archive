### Post by spartan777 on 2006-06-03
yeah, like meng said, just cd to the directory, and sh the .run.

hehe, if you really are nubs, and don't understand this, here are the directions. be sure AA is on your desktop. the go to the terminal (applications/accesories/terminal). then type

```
cd /home/YOURUSERNAME/Desktop

then

sh armyops250linux.run
```

of course replacing "YOURUSERNAME" with, your username.
