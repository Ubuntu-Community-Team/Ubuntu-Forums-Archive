### Post by Martin_Caine on 2014-01-22
Hi fellow Linux gaming fans.

I'm a hobbyist game developer working on a game in my spare time (along side a day job and having four kids) and I'm trying to get it Greenlit on Steam right now.

The game is inspired by the classic Tron lightcycle games including the likes of GLTron and Armagetron, both of which I played first on Linux many years ago. I've added my own touch to the graphic style of the game and have vastly overhauled the camera and control systems in my game to give it a much smoother (and more modern) feel.

I'd really appreciate it if some of you who play games through Steam on Linux would spare a minute to check out the game and give it your vote on Greenlight.
