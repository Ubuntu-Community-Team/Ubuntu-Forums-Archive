### Post by perspectoff on 2010-09-10
When you see the Hoggarwort come out of the left corridor, throw your gilded rope around his swaggiehorn. 

The Hoggarwort will let out a tremendous thunderous bellow that will make the walls start to crumble, but you can use the Finderpot that you discovered while flying through the Eldermist to keep the falling rubble off your head.

You will be pulled out of the yellow quicksand by the gallumphing Hoggarwort, so quickly fix a braid of the Soupywitch's beard onto the tolling bell (you know, the one that tolls for thee) to signal your companions which direction they should head to find the imperious bandersnatch (or alternatively to snatch the impervious finderwatch).

Be careful of falling face down in the muck, because there are bugs in it that will make one of your legs grow larger and the other leg grow smaller, so that you will then be forced to lean to the left the rest of the game (if you get bitten).
