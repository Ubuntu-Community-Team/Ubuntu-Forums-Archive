Hey all,

Underworld Hockey Club (UHC) is an arcade hockey game featuring ragdoll physics and 5v5 multiplayer over the internet.  Deliver devastating bodychecks, weave around ice mines and barbed wire traps, and score goals to win games for cash.  Spend cash on new equipment & affiliations so your team can challenge Hades himself for a one-way ticket out of the underworld!

Here is a gameplay video: [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t4xjAI6YYvo](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t4xjAI6YYvo)

UHC is looking for people running 32 and 64-bit linux to help beta test the linux versions.  Please visit the forums at [www.underworldhockeyclub.com](www.underworldhockeyclub.com) and make a post in the "Beta Testers Needed" thread to get a link to the game.

Thanks for your time,
Jason Gauci,
Lead Developer, Digitalghost Productions.
