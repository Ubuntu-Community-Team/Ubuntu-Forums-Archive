### Post by ThoseTimesAreGone on 2013-10-08
Hey everyone,

I dont know if everyone here knows, but you can get money for playing steam games. When you play and get "rewards" or whatever it is called... you can actually sell them on the steam marketplace.Their worth varies from $.03 to .40 cents. Most fall somewhere in between. I mean its just pointless icons and some people collect them for steam level or whatever... but if you dont care about that you might as well get paid so you have more money to buy more linux games.

The other day i was gaming HL2, and managed to get 50 american cents in like an hour and a half or something. Now obviously you arent going to make a fortune, but you are getting paid to do something that entertains you. So thats pretty cool. 

Some of you might have entire collections of items in your steam collections and they are just sitting there. This is just a tip.

Also if you are going to sell items make sure you look at what the current going rate is and price it like 1 cent under if you want a quick sale. 

Since Steam games are so cheap, you can get a free game once in a while. I just got hl2 epi 1 and epi 2 for 2$ each. so valve basically gave those games free to me b/c whatever awards i get i will sell on the market place.

you get the idea. hopefully this helps someone.
