### Post by rudeboyskunk on 2012-12-31
Adding a game is a bit obscure, and only came to me when I stumbled across it.  Next to your avatar in the top left of the Desura client you see the Desura icon in a blue strip.  Click on that, and go down to "Tools."  Click on either option and it takes you to a screen where you can either A) activate a CD key or B) just add the link to your game.  However, all it's doing is making it easy for you to launch said game from your Desura client, it doesn't do anything extra (you could even just launch non-games...).
