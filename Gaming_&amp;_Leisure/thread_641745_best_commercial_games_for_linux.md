### Post by weblordpepe on 2007-12-16
**Penumbra** has a native linux client, and its a top-notch commercial game.

I've fanboy'd this game before but its AWESOME. Its by a smallish dev company I think but the game is top-notch.
[IMG]http://frictionalgames.com/images/news/200608penumbra_promo01.jpg[/IMG]

Its a very spooky game. You spend a lot of time in each room, and its very (very) dark. You wander around an abandoned mining complex searching for information about the death of your father. 
It has a unique interactive+physics handling system which I haven't seen a any other game. Essentially you have to manipulate objects in the environment by actually applying force to them. It plays like a SCUMM game (puzzles, no guns) although the perspective is FPS and you don't see any other humans besides yourself.

You have to push/pull doors by moving the mouse. You have to move leavers by pushing/pulling on the mouse and so on. Very fun. This is a game that you can turn out the lights and play on your own. I rate it as one of my all time favorites. Something about being forced to use your brain to get out of a spooky situation where you're all alone that gives me a ....that I find fun.

[In-game videos]("http://www.penumbra-overture.com/media.php")
[Download the demo (Linux binary, Windows binary)]("http://www.penumbra-overture.com/demo.php")
