### Post by ChamPro on 2008-08-01
I know this is a really old post, but I'm going to put the answer here anyways.

Based on directions for Quake III, I derived these commands.

For my widescreen laptop, I put these in the RT:CW console (press the ~ at the main menu)
```
r_customwidth "1280"
r_customheight "800"
r_mode "-1"
```

After you restart the game, you should have your custom resolution.
