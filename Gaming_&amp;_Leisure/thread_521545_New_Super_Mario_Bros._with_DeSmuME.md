I don't see it on the compatibility list, so it probably is not supported.
[http://www.desmume.com/nds-roms-compatible.htm](http://www.desmume.com/nds-roms-compatible.htm)

You should always check the compatibility list of an early-in-development emulator before you ask people why something doesn't work. The simple answer is that the emulator is not complete yet.

Besides, while people on the Ubuntu Forum can probably help you get an emulator working in the first place, you are better off seeing if there are any tips to get a ROM working on a forum for the emulator itself.
