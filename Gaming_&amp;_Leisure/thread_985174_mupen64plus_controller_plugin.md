### Post by BigSilly on 2008-11-17
You just need to install libSDL_ttf-2.0 from Synaptic. It's a dependency of the plug in, and from what I gather the Mupen64Plus team hope to be removing the need for it in the next version. If you ran the program from the terminal you would have seen the error yourself.

Good luck! :)
