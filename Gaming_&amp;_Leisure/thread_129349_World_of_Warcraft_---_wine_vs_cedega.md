### Post by RonJohnson on 2006-02-13
I personally use wine. After trying Cedega TimeDemo I was very unhappy with the results. I could not get Cedega to work with WoW and then they were wanting me to pay for it too. No thank you!

I did have quite a few problems with installing wine to work with WoW. But following the instructions in this [thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=120615") will get you 95% of the way there. The one thing that they didn't explain was that you need to apt-get the dev files for opengl support for you video card prior to compiling wine the first time. After I found that information and played some with config settings everything was working great. I for one can't imagine paying some other company $55 a year for something that took me maybe 3 hours to fix myself.
