### Post by Omnios on 2006-12-17
Hi I had a listing of mud games but am making a thread that is more than a list of possible games but rather reviews of games that players have actually played. 

 First off for people who are new to text based gaming I would reomend 
[www.materiamagica.com]("http://ubuntuforums.org/www.materiamagica.com")
 It is very newbie friendly and easy to navigate in game with directional compasses. 

 Another game that I enjoyed playing was Shadow Gate which is based on the older D&D rules and is a very interesting game to play.
[http://www.firedragon.com/shadowgate/](http://www.firedragon.com/shadowgate/)

 Right now I am playing a game called [FONT=arial,helvetica][SIZE=+1][COLOR=#00ffff][I]
 Achaea, Dreams of Divine Lands 
 [COLOR=Black]right now this is my vaforit text game so far and it is very challenging in that you do not type kill and sit back but rather type in attack combos in battle. It also all original in skills etc which makes it even more interesting to play. Navigating the world is a bitch though so it takes a while to learn how to get around though there are world and city maps which at first are hard to read. Read about it here.
[/COLOR][/I][/COLOR][/SIZE][/FONT][http://www.achaea.com/main.html](http://www.achaea.com/main.html)

 If you have a mud plug please share.

 Also I have been visiting [www.mudconnect.com]("http://ubuntuforums.org/www.mudconnect.com") for years to find new muds to try out and they have a very good search and listing layout.
