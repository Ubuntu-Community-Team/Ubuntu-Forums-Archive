Hello there,

just today I received my copy of the Linux port of ANKH, a current adventure game in the tradition of classical point and click adventures. I think it's been discussed here already, but didn't see any info about it's actual release here yet. So, it IS available now!

Thus if you're serious about Linux gaming and like a good adventure game head to [www.ixsoft.com](www.ixsoft.com) now and grab your copy! As far as I see they offer international shipping. The CD comes with both english and german language versions and even the manual has both.

I think it's really cool to finally see some newer game beeing released for Linux. There won't be much better opportunities to support commercial Linux games, so consider it ;)
