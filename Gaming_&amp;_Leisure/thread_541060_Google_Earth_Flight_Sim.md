### Post by staib on 2007-09-02
Yes it's really rather good - Page up for Throttle up (and vice versa) and G for gear up/down on the F-16. F and Shift F for Flaps down and up.  Other than that just put on your favourite music and go cruisiing in the sky...
Unlike MS FS it doesn't take ages to load up either - but flying is strictly VFR.
