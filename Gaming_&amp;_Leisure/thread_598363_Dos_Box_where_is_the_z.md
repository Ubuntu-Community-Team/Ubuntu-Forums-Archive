### Post by jfordwashere on 2007-10-31
So put the game in a directory call "games" or something in your home directory, then in dosbox type:

mount c ~yourname/games

then just goto the c drive.

### Post by FranMichaels on 2007-10-31
The other other option ;) is to install DOSBox 0.72, as they added support for ~ type folders... 

The meat and potatoes of the matter is you can just right click on the game and Open With dosbox, or if you right click and Open With the folder, it will mount it.

I haven't had any problems with this method, and no need for a front-end. :)
