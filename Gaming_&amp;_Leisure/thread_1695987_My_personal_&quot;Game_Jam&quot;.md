### Post by theraje on 2011-02-26
Hey folks, I thought you might be interested in keeping an eye on game development, so I figured I'd let you know that I'm working on a "Project-A-Month" exercise, where each month on the first I start a new game project, and on the last of the month I release it -- finished or not.

The games play in your Web browser, and are written in JavaScript/HTML5. They will work in Firefox 3.6+, Opera 11+, Google Chrome, Safari, and possibly IE9 (as if anyone here cares about the last one :P). No Flash plug-ins required.

Anyway, if any of you are interested, I would really appreciate it if you'd keep an eye on the blog, check out my progress reports, try demos, and give any feedback you have on it all.

I begin my first entry on March 1. The updates can be found at: [http://highscoreunderachiever.posterous.com/](http://highscoreunderachiever.posterous.com/)

Thanks for any feedback/input you may have! :)
