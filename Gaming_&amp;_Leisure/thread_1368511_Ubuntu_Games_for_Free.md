### Post by Possum Films on 2009-12-30
All flash games will work in Ubuntu, here are some good sites with lots of free games: [http://www.miniclip.com/](http://www.miniclip.com/) [http://www.addictinggames.com/](http://www.addictinggames.com/)

If you want more than just flash based minigames you should check out [The Linux Game Tome]("http://www.happypenguin.org/") for free games, or if you want to buy commercial Linux Games, [Tux Games]("http://tuxgames.com/"). 

Hope you find those links helpful.
