### Post by ecowan on 2014-11-27
I am a volunteer with a local project we call 'The PeaceMobile'. It is a set of activities for children of the age range 7-10, organized around the themes Peace for Me, Peace of Us, Peace for Everyone, and Peace for the World. We take it to schools and other venues in and a round our city.

For the most part the activities are hands on and physical, e.g. tandem skis. But a couple of our stations use computers. One popular station uses FaceMaker, and ancient DOS program in the vein of the old Mr Potato Head. Unfortunately the lap tops that run it are almost as ancient, with Windows 98, and it is getting very difficult to persuade them to start up.

I am thinking that we could get a couple of refurbished laptops load them with Lubuntu or something like. Perhaps among the myriad of games available in the Ubuntu Software repository there are suitable replacements. The games should encourage social interaction, co-operation, conflict resolution, respect for others' differences, etc.

I would very much appreciate suggestions. - Erny
