### Post by Sleaka J on 2008-05-22
Turn off compiz. Your screensaver is kicking in causing the problem. I had the same problem with ioquake3 and Enemy Territory Quake Wars. Turning off compiz solved the problem. Compiz can also cause some loss of framerate.

I suppose I should add that compiz is your "Advanced Desktop Effects". Accessible through System -> Preferences -> Appearance -> Visual Effects
