Hey guys, :p:p wassup

Yesterday I installed this visual novel, DA CAPO, but the game had me with some troubles, but finally I made it. 

It is a solved problem, but I post this on internet to help future people in the same situation with the same or similar problems, since is not usual to talk about fixing problems on visual novels (tutorials).


Anyway, let's go to the tutorial. I've got and old acer aspire and lubuntu on it. First of all (basic) you will have to install wine. Then, the installation of the videogame DA CAPO must be normal, with no problems. 

Once the game is installed, launch it. 

**First problem. Not full screen**. Even trying to force the game full screen in configuration. I've got a 1280 screen resolution. 
***How I solved this***: 

**1-Preferences > monitor adjustment I changed resolution to 1024**. And woala the game is in full screen now. Easy right? I turned in to 1280 again and now it works in full screen too. Sometimes fails it's windowed when you launch the game, repeat the process. 

[B]

Second problem[/B].** Reading spacebars beetween words. Font problem**. 
***How I solved this***: 

First of all I tought it was about windows fonts, but I reinstalled them and nothing happened. 

desesperated I was surfing internet to find something else. 

How lucky I was today finding this: 

[http://www.doddlercon.com/main/?page_id=157](http://www.doddlercon.com/main/?page_id=157)



With an exe patch of DA CAPO font trouble. **You will have to download the patch, and open the exe with wine**. 

The page has also repairs font patch for visual novels like shuffle! soul link, kira kira and suika 


And that's all, now you can play DA CAPO at full screen and with a perfect reading! Here is an example on internet of what is has to be like:
