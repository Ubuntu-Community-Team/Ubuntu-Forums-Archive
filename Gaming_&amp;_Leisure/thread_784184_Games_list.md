### Post by eragon100 on 2008-05-06
Savage 2 costs money, savage 1 is free

Savage 2 linux client has a ton of bugs, savage 1 doesn't

Savage 2 requires a pentium 4, dual-core with 1.5 GB of ram recommended, savage 1 runs on an old crappy pentium 3 600 mhz with 128 MB ram :)

Savage 1 is fun, savage 2 isn't :lolflag:

Thus I recommend you to choose savage 1 from [www.newerth.com](www.newerth.com), most players agree it's more fun than savage 2, which also doesn't have much players, servers are almost always either completely filled, so you can't play on them, or completely empty.
