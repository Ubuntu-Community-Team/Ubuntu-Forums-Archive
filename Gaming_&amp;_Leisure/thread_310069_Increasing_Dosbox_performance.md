### Post by Quicky on 2006-11-30
I've had a fair bit of help on these forums over the last few months since migrating to Ubuntu, but as a newcomer, so far I haven't been able to give much back. However, today I discovered something that may be of help to some new Dosbox users, so I thought I share the wealth:

If you set the CPU cycles to 'auto', rather than a value, and the core emulation to 'dynamic', both either in the dosboxgui frontend or by editing any dosbox .conf file, then in many (not all) games running on dosbox, you will experience huge improvements in emulation speed and general performance. For example the classic adventure Bioforge went from unplayable to almost perfect on my underpowered laptop.

This may well be common knowledge to people who have been using Dosbox for some time, but I haven't seen much reference to it previously, so apologies if this is old news.
