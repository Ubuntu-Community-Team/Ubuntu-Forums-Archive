### Post by Tr00per on 2006-06-19
What you want to do is download steam and put it on your desktop, just the exe. Now open cedega and click install. A new window will pop up. Create a new game folder, you can just name it steam. GDDB entry, scroll down and you will find Steam. Next click the browse button, and select your Steam.exe file and then press continue. 


That should install it. when its all done just click on your steam icon in Cedega, then press play and steam will load, then you can download your games and such
