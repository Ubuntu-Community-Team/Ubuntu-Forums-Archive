### Post by lakersforce on 2009-11-17
> **Clopin said:**
> I have not played ET, but ETQW, so if you're talking about multiplayer, and it works the same way, the EXP in-game is only based on 1 game/round. Then you have to start over.

But that is not how it works on the most popular ET servers.

A lot (if not most) servers implements xp save in ET. If it does it usually tells you somewhere (often in the server name). Xp are saved locally on your machine, often in the .et (or similarly) folder in your home directory.

Try give the BBA or Kernwaffe servers a go. If your xp are not saved when playing on those servers, ET has a problem writing to the above hidden folder.
