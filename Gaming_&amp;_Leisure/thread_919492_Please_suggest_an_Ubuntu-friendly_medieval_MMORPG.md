### Post by merlyn on 2009-01-06
I've played Planeshift quite extensively in the past and found it quite good considering it's level of development.

As some one mentioned earlier, the main focus of Planeshift is roleplay, so it's very different to WoW in that regard.

Also when last I played the quest system was somewhat lacking. It does have an interesting craft system though.

The game used to be poorly documented and hence 'newbie' unfriendly, (that may have changed). 

If not though, unless you happen across another player who is willing to help you out & show you the ropes it takes a while to get a start.

Nevertheless it's free so it could well be worth your while checking out. No doubt a number of improvements have been made since I last played.

If you are interested in the 'commercial' offerings Guild Wars has gold status in the Wine appdb.

Once I'm 'over' WoW Guild wars is where I'll be going.
