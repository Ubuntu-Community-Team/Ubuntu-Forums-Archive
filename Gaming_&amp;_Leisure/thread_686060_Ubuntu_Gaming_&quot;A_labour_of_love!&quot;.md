### Post by acoustibop on 2008-02-02
Hm, I notice the only Playstation emulator you mention is ePSXe, Mindless1.  ePSXe is a bit long in the tooth now, the last update having been 4 1/2 years ago and, as you mention, it's a pain to set up and run.  It also doesn't run that well, being plagued with slowdowns  and, of course, the inaccuracies inevitably introduced by trying to enhance the game.

Have you tried [pSX Emulator]("http://psxemulator.gazaxian.com/")?  It's much newer than ePSXe, being only 2 years old and is still in active development.  It aims at accurate emulation rather than enhancement and succeeds very well - it already has much better compatibility than ePSXe; the only emulator that challenges it for compatibility is Xebra, which is Windows only.

It's not plugin based, making it easy to install and run, and the vast majority of games will run - without the problems inherent in ePSXe - pretty much perfectly, even on quite low spec machines.  I think you need to try it! ;)
