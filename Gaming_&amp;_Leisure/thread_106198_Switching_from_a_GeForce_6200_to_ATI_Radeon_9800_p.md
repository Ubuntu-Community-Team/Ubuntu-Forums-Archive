### Post by Miguel on 2005-12-20
It is funny. 

The 9800 is about 3 to 4 times faster for gaming in winblows (if I am not wrong)... but it will probably be only twice as fast than the 6200 on linux... and the cool composite effects will only run acceptably on the 6200. 

Off Topic: I must admit that ATi has put things into perspective for me. At least when considering a laptop. Today, I would go for a 5 pound 14'' widescreen laptop with integrated graphics card (if possible, without the windows tax). More battery life, less weight, and say goodbye to the ati hassles. As long as it runs xscreensaver and scorched3d ok...
