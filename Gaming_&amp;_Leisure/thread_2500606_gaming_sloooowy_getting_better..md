### Post by nicolasdentremont on 2024-09-06
Steam runs games fairly well with Proton. You can enable all games in the settings under compatibility. By default Steam will only run the ones verified to work well on Linux. I've had good success running most of my games on Steam.

You can look up ProtonDB. It lists most of the games on Steam, whether they worked for other people, and what steps they needed to take to make them work.

Other than Steam, you can check out Lutris. It combines Wine with a few other things to run games on Linux. I've had a very good experience with Lutris so far. Some of my games work better on Linux with Lutris than they ever did on Windows.
