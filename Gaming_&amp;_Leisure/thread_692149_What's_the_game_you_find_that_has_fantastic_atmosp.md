### Post by eljoeb on 2008-02-09
Undying, Planescape: Torment, the Fallout series, and maybe the Call of Duty series (not my thing, but I guess it does it well).  I feel these games do a great job using their theme to pull you in and immerse you, even if it's not graphically.  Games with crappy atmosphere: the entire Quake series (number 4 literally had bad atmosphere!  Worst. Skybox. EVER.) Neverwinter Nights (after the classy BG 1 and 2, Torment, and Icewind Dales you get the most artificial, soulless game I have ever played), and (the worst offenders): The Elder Scrolls series.
