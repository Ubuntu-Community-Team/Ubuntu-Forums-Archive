### Post by damis648 on 2008-11-28
A quick google search reveals at least one does, but it is irrelevant and a waste of money, as they aren't event needed. I have hacked my PSP and other people's several times, all you need is to make a specially partitioned memory stick with the firmware files (Magic Memory Stick) and a Pandora's Battery (for putting the PSP into service mode for installing the custom firmware). It is quite easy. A mod chip is pointless. The stock mobo is fine.
