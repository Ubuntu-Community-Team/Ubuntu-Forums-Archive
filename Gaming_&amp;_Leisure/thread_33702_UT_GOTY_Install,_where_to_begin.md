### Post by qstone on 2005-05-12
it's easy, guy...
Find and download a file called ut-install-436-GOTY.run (google it, or look at some ut download sites)
open a terminal, and type :
chmod a+x ut-install-436-GOTY.run
sudo ./ut-install-436-GOTY.run

Here come the graphical installer. It will ask for your CD.
You'll be fragging a in minute !
