### Post by lotusleaf on 2006-04-26
The Soldier of Fortune II: Double Helix Multiplayer Test Demo works great in WINE 0.9.12, and there's still some remote servers up and running it! While the demo only has one map, it's still fun and the graphics are nice (considering the game was made in 2002/2003). It's a neat little FPS.

Download for the Multiplayer demo (only 51 megs!) is available [here](http://www.3dgamers.com/dlselect/games/soldierfortune2/sof2_test.exe.html). ([Readme](http://www.3dgamers.com/dlselect/games/soldierfortune2/sof2_test.txt)) - [Screenshots](http://www.3dgamers.com/screenshots/games/soldierfortune2/) - [Info](http://www.3dgamers.com/games/soldierfortune2/)

It's one of the few multiplayer demo games I've ran via WINE that works flawlessly. If you decide to play, please post your experiences here about it! :)
