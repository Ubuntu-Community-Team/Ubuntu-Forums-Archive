### Post by AlexVSharp on 2011-09-13
So I presume everyone had one of those games they would really like to see ported to their favourite OS, right? :o

Well after much hesitating some friends and I have finally decided to take matters into our own hands.  Thus on another forum I talked to some GMs and, surprising, they decided to  support us for a petition to try and appeal to a developers team to make  us a native client for a free-to-play game called Lost Saga. Unfortunately, in order to make it effective we were given a rather large number  of required signatures - something that turned out to be quite troublesome... :(

Yet we just refused to give up. I've muster up an article to ask other  Linux gamers like ourselves to help us reach the needed goal. Thus if you're  feeling generous, or would just like to see a game ported to Linux, would you mind  checking it out: [Trying to bring Lost Saga to Linux]("https://docs.google.com/document/pub?id=1-PhL5K5akvqRsOmX4YgASgF1GCIG7EOTmVnQXsx1BJA")

Oh, and I apologise because the petition is on another forum - the GMs requested it be that way. At least you can log-in using Facebook....

We do appreciate all the help we can get. :D
