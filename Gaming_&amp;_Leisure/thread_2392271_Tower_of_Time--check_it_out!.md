### Post by oldrocker99 on 2018-05-18
An action game called Tower of Time is out of early access and is $24.99 on Steam. The Linux version is rock-solid. This is an action top-down RPG, in which your characters (you start out with two, and more become available as you play). It has a completely unique and original "slow mode" combat system, with weapons and spells each character can cast. The tank can erect a temporary wall, dash to an enemy and attack, and HEAL himself. Such it is with other characters' spells.

For an indie game, this is of very high quality; I, in fact, consider it an AAA game.

The developer is **very** active on the forums, and listens to the comments and plans for future patches. In other words, they really want to please their lucky purchasers.

***HIGHLY recommended***.:D
