### Post by HackerFinn on 2014-09-26
Not true. You do not have to repurchase a game for other OS's if the game is multiplatform on Steam.
If the game has only been released for Windows through Steam, you do.
Also if the game, like Rockstars GTA San Andreas Mac OS version is ported by a third-party developer, it might not be automaticly added to your library,
and you will have to either contact Steam, one of the developers, or buy the game again.
