### Post by sakuramboo on 2010-04-11
I find it funny that everyone replied with a slew of FPS's but no one mentioned any MMORPG's. Here are some MMORPG's.

Open Source -
Planeshift - Fantasy based, community driven
The Mana World - Top down, ragnarok-esque
Daimonin - Isometric, keyboard controlled

Free -
Regnum Online - Fantasy based, 3D

Commercial -
Vendetta Online - More like an MMOFPS but set in space with 360 degree movement

There are a few others like Eternal Lands, Auteria and Dofus, but I feel that they are not quite there, quality wise, as some of the other offerings.
