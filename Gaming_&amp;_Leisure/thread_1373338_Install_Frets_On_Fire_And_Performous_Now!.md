### Post by Maheriano on 2010-01-05
I heard of Frets On Fire sometime mid last year and never had a chance to really check it out. Plus I never wanted to since I have Guitar Hero II: Legends Of Rock anyway. I finally got around to checking it out last week and wow, this thing is slick! I downloaded a torrent with all the Guitar Hero and Rock Band songs plus about 2000 custom/other songs and immediately started playing Michael Jackson and Queen tunes, it's awesome! I even got an adapter cable from [www.dealextreme.com](www.dealextreme.com) to hook up my Playstation guitar to my computer for $5 with free shipping. It's awesome, the guitar is recognized as a joystick so you just calibrate the buttons.

I then wanted to see which other party games were out there and found Performous which is like Singstar for the Playstation consoles, it's awesome! I downloaded a torrent for this one to get 2400 songs, everything from Neil Diamond to Eminem and it actually makes you sing in tune. The notes are on the chart on the screen and when you sing into the microphone, there's a real time overlay of your voice and you have to modify your pitch to hit the notes, it's a lot of fun and even more drunken fun!
