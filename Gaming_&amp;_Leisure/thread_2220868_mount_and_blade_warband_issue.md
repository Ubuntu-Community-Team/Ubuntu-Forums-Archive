### Post by oldrocker99 on 2014-05-01
AMD/ATI cards that run just fine (better, usually, than nVidia) **on Windows** perform not nearly as well **on GNU/Linux**. It's all because of the less-than-great AMD/ATI Linux drivers. Several Steam Linux games' forums are full of complaints from AMD/ATI users. The bottom line is if you want good GNU/Linux game performance, think nVidia. IMHO, even Intel is doing a better job of supporting GNU/Linux graphics.
