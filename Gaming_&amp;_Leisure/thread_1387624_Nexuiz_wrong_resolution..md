I changed the resolution in Nexuiz from 1280x768 to 860x555 (I think.)

---

### Post by portets on 2010-01-22
go to your home folder and hit ctrl+h.

look for the folder titled ".nexuiz".

double click "data".

open config.cfg.

find the lines with your resolution and change them. should be at the bottom
