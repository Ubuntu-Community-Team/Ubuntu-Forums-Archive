### Post by freaknigh on 2011-03-24
Ya man it's still a great game. They've actually released a ipad / iphone version of the original game recently. The zod engine though is a lot more advanced with coop player, multiple bot players, 8 possible teams instead of just 4 (infinite players per game). You can also edit the stats of any of the units etc with a text file. Upgraded RTS engine in that you can set rally points, waypoints, starcraft style mouse control. Has a commercially dedicated online multiplayer server. And the engine is still being expanded on too.

It's more then just a "revisit to the past". It's more the like past finally moving forward.
