### Post by lol on 2005-08-10
AFAIK, some games cannot be minimized. My suggestion is to start the game on another X server.

Using gdm, what I usually do is "New login" (in the system menu), and then log on using a failsafe xterm session, which I use to start my game. You can then switch between the game and all the other apps with Ctrl - Alt - F7 or F8...

### Post by GreyFox503 on 2005-08-10
Thanks for your answer.  Although it takes slightly longer than I would like, it accomplishes what I wanted to do.  I knew you could have several virtual terminals, but I didn't know that they could be graphical or that there is a link in the Gnome panel!

More specifically, its in Applications > System Tools > New Login.

Or enter 'gdmflexiserver'
