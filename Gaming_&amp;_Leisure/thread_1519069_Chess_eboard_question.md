### Post by M!K3_$ on 2012-11-15
Because I stumbled accross this in my search for an answer I will leave a solution in case somebody else is looking.

Under the "Windows" menu, click "Games on Client...". This will bring up a "Local Games List" window which you can use to save/load your games.
