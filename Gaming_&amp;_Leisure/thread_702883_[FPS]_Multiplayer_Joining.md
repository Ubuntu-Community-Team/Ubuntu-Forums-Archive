### Post by ShodanjoDM on 2008-02-20
Usually there's a "Server List" or "Multiplayer" option in the menu. You only have to click it and the game will look for available servers including the ping time from your location to those listed servers.

The lowest ping times are usually located near your location, and therefore usually the best ones to choose (won't give you too much time lag).
