### Post by Ferio on 2006-07-26
Or maybe you could delete .freecraft (I'm not sure about the name, but it surely includes the dot at the beginning to hide it) in your home folder. Just press Ctrl+H to show your hidden files and folders, and delete the Freecraft one; that should reset the config when you re-install it.
