I assure you there are people (and badgers) who agree 120% with what you said. I'm playing Fantasy General, and I find it much more varied, fun, and inspired than wesnoth.
I'm also thinking about modding/forking Galaxy Mage (which is nice from technical side - Python/Opengl), but GM developers like wesnoth too much for my taste.
---------------

You can download Fantasy General here:
[http://www.the-underdogs.info/game.php?id=397](http://www.the-underdogs.info/game.php?id=397)

You'll need dosbox to run it. It's in repositories.

After unpacking the game to a directory (I think I used either wine, or unrar (nonfree) for that) run dosbox.
Then:
```

mount c /path/to/your/directory/containing/dos/games
c:
cd fgen (unless you used another name for directory)
fg.bat

```

If it runs too slow, hit CTRL-F12 a few times, but don't overdo it. You need to find optimal amount for your cpu cycles. Decrease with ctrl-f11.
alt-tab to toggle fullscreen and free mouse. The sound doesn't seem to work for me, even running setup doesn't help.
