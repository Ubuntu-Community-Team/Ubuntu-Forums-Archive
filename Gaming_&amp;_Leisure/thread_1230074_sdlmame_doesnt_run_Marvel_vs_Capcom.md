### Post by BigSilly on 2009-08-03
The Metal Slug games change with every new MAME, so if you want to play those you have to have the rom that matches your version of MAME. For Marvel Vs Capcom I'd have to say again download the version that matches the version of MAME you're using. Roms get updated all the time, and you may have either an older version of the rom or even a newer one that doesn't work with the emulator you're using.

Hope this helps.
