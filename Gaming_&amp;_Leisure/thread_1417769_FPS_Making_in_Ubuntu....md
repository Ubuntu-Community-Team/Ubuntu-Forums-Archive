### Post by RichardLinx on 2010-02-28
I have a feeling you will love the Cube/Cube 2 Game engine. Just press "e" and you can fly around in edit mode and add models by right clicking and selecting things like health packs, armor, trees, etc from the menu.

AssaultCube is good for this as well since it has a lot of modern map models and you can upload your maps in-game online in seconds for people to test out your map.
