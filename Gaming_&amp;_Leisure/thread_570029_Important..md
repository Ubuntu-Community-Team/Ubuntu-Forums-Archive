### Post by mivo on 2007-10-09
> EDIT: and is the 256MB NVIDIA GeForce 8600GT-DDR3 good for 3d games like oblivion?

Sort of. The 8600GT is a DX10 card, so if you wanted to play something like Oblivion (DX9 game), you would get much better performance from a 7950GT -- possibly for less money. The 8600GT is not a bad card, mind you, but the top-end card of the previous generation (the 7950) still beats it, unless you play DX10 games. And if you play DX10 games, you should probably wait for the cards of the next generation (10.1 cards) ... but in that case your only choice is Windows.
