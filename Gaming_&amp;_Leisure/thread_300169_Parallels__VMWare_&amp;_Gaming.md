### Post by Breepee on 2006-11-19
Neither VMWare of Parallels offer 3D acceleration, in any form. There are rumors that the Parallels-team is working on it, but those are just rumors.

With CPU-virtualization enabled, CPU-rendering can offer quite satisfactional performance with low-complexity 3D-graphics.
