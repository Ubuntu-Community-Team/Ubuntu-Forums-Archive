### Post by doorknob60 on 2010-05-22
Mupen64plus is simply more up to date and compatible with many more games. Also it natively supports 64-bit processors.  There's no repository for ROMs, as that would be illegal (and can't talk about that kind of thing here anyway). All I can say is that there's a certain popular search engine out there that can help you find what you need. Once you get the ROMs, just open up mupen64plus, click "Open ROM" (or something like that, browse to your ROM, and go. Of course you might want to mess with controls and stuff, but overall it's mostly pretty self explanitory.
