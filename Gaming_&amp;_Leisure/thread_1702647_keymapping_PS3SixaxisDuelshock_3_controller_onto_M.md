### Post by disturbedite on 2011-03-08
not to step on your toes AI, but that shouldn't be necessary. barring any significant differences between ubuntu & opensuse, the ps3 buttons & axes should be able to be mapped without any calibration, configuration or tweaking. that has been my experience.
there seems to be a more serious problem with the OP's setup...

a few things to remember though:
mame has an option in joystick mapping called 'sixaxis' to make using the ps3 pads axes easier.
if you want to use your ps3 pad wirelessly, you need bluetooth capability and the app called 'QtSixA'.
