### Post by Zeroangel on 2006-03-22
You could try CTRL+ALT+D to minimize everything, then just switch to a different desktop/workspace.

Or you could run two different sessions, one with the game and one with out. Then you can press CTRL+ALT+F8 and CTRL+ALT+F7 to toggle between them.
