### Post by GWBouge on 2011-03-17
From what I've read, the ASUS Xonar series works great, and has some good, affordable cards.  I'm pretty sure HT|Omega cards work fine as well, but those are a bit more pricey (although, the ASUS line gets expensive as well ... depends on what outputs you need and your budget).

I'm still using an old X-Fi Extreme Music, and it works a LOT better than it did a year or two ago.  But Pulse still has issues with it, so I've resorted to ALSA + jackd.  Wouldn't really call that 'out of the box'.
