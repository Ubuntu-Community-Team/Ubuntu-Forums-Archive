### Post by crhylove on 2008-08-03
There are many great emulators that will run either natively in Linux, or under Wine.

Sadly there is only one for XBOX, and it really only plays one game, rather badly.

It's called Cxbx, and has been out of development for over 2 years:

[http://www.caustik.com/cxbx/index.htm](http://www.caustik.com/cxbx/index.htm)

There are some great n64, playstation, playstation 2, gamecube, Wii, Sega, Super Nintendo, and NES emulators out there though.  Many, MANY games are playable for every system (except the Wii, which is still damned new, but showing dramatic and exciting results already).

Look up Mupen64plus, Dolphin, Zsnes, PCSX2, dgens and PCSX for starters.  All those emulators play many games, and play them well, some natively and some under Wine.
