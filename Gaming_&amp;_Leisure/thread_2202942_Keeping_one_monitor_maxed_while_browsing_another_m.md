### Post by -kg- on 2014-01-31
I would think so, unless your game preempts the monitors.

Simply launch one or the other program, unmaximize it, and resize/reposition it to fit on one monitor.  Then launch the other, unmaximize it, and resize/reposition it to fill in the other monitor.

That's how I usually do it.
