### Post by Agent86 on 2008-06-25
I don't know how popular the Pioneers implementation of SoC is now, but I can recommend a couple of active online places for Settlers.

The first place is [BrettspielWelt]("http://www.brettspielwelt.de"). It's a very good place to play Settlers and many other boardgames against human opponents.  

The other place is the [Java Settlers of Catan]("http://ladder.jsettlers.com/index.html") site. In addition to human ladder and non-ladder matches, the JSettlers site has some very good AI opponents. In fact, the JSettlers bots were created as research into a doctoral thesis in artificial intelligence.

Unfortunately, both of these sites support "vanilla" Settlers only - no Seafarers or other map variants. If you want those, Pioneers may be the only way to go.
