### Post by Tadaen_Sylvermane on 2022-08-14
So what is your video card? It's odd to post about a video card problem without that information. Just saying... 

If Nvidia you may need to install the proprietary driver for it as the open source one just isn't very good comparatively. In my case on an older GT730 the open source driver is less than half the performance of the closed one. AMD is far superior these days since they open sourced their driver and assist the Mesa people with making it.
