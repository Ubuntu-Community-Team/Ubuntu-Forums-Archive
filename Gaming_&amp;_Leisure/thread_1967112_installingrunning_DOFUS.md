### Post by iseijin on 2012-04-28
Since June 2011 Adobe AIR withdrew support for linux. You will have to install Wine and then run Adobe AIR from there.

[http://forum.dofus.com/en/4-problems-solutions/192956-cant-play-game-adobe-air-broken?page=1#entry791598]("http://forum.dofus.com/en/4-problems-solutions/192956-cant-play-game-adobe-air-broken?page=1#entry791598")

Also with copying from the terminal you should be able to select the text, without unselecting it, swap to the window you want to paste to and click the middle mouse button (or left + right buttons together).
