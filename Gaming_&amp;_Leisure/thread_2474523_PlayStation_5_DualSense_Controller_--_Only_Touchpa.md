While the DualSense controller on the PS5s has wireless capabilities, it lacks any form of adaptor that would allow it to communicate with your PC. It does offer ubiquitous Bluetooth connectivity, although this isn't available on every PC.[COLOR=#d3d3d3]

You'll need to provide a Bluetooth adapter if your PC doesn't have one built in (most mid-range gaming laptops have). Bluetooth adapters that connect to a computer via USB are relatively widespread and inexpensive these days. After connecting the dongle to the computer, start pairing your DualSense by pressing and holding the PS and Create buttons together until the center light blinks.
