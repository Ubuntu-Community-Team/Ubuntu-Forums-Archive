### Post by blaise69 on 2008-09-23
I play both Regnum and Vendetta Online.

Regnum does have 'Premium content' that requires real money for extra items, but from what I can tell you really don't need to purchase any of them to enjoy the game, even if you're a hardcore MMO gamer, things such as XP bonus scrolls and potions (you can rest to heal) I don't consider too important to the game. It has a really good community, and that's the most important thing

Vendetta Online also has a great community and this is the reason why I paid for a months subscription to see how I enjoy it.  There are loads of missions and the combat is twitch based as in you need good keyboard and mouse/joystick control as opposed to relying on stats and experience.  This I find refreshing and it's similar to a FPS space combat game like Elite in this way, that's certainly not a bad thing.

But are there any others out there?  Especially something that doesn't look like it's been made in 1993?!
