### Post by DeusExM1 on 2009-11-22
Hey Everyone! 

As you may or may not know a new game is being commercially developed for linux called "Heroes of Newerth". Their main website can be found here ([http://www.heroesofnewerth.com/](http://www.heroesofnewerth.com/)). this game is based on Dota (warcraft 3 Mod), but is a standalone game.

I have just played it for a few hours so i thought I would write up a quick review for anyone who is considering this game. I can firmly say this game exceeds the original in every single category. I am extremely impressed with it. Game was played on Ubuntu 64 bit 9.10. 

Here are some things i found great about this game:

- The graphics are vastly improved over the outdated warcraft 3 engine.
- All soundboards are new for each hero.
- This game has a lot of attention to detail. Extremely nice interface, and greatly worked out pre-game mechanisms (hero-pick screen / player join screen). 
- Built in Voice Chat is Great ! No longer will you be at a disadvantage if you play a team who are using team speak. Now you can chat with people on your team, even if you dont know them !
- The reconnect feature is definitely a game-changer. No longer do people massively leave if a single person leaves. (also due to leaver rating). Very nice touch.
-The Game-search screen is excellent... You can customize it to find exactly the kinds of games you are looking for.
-This game really leaves an impression. I mean it. The interface changes are pretty big, which makes the whole experience very smooth.
-Most importantly: The game is just as addicting as the original Defense of the Ancients, except contains many improvements.

---------

The Bad:
[I]
Technical[/I]
-Its still in beta, and me and other users have found 2 small bugs.
1. If you leave sound settings on high, you will experience sound crackling.
2. If you use compiz/effects the game will glitch. 

However these 2 can easily be worked around by disabling desktop effects and reducing sound quality. This eliminates these bugs and the game works great !

*Game*
-S2 Games have not yet implemented an anti-"Back-Dooring" feature into HoN. It took only 4 games to find someone who happily backdoored the towers while creeps were in the middle of the map. On the other hand not everyone plays by this rule (although i would assume majority consider this a dirty trick), but the game is still in Beta so that may change.

---------

Im tired of playing games on Wine because emulation of any game will never be as good as the real thing. Finally linux users get a top notch commercial game they can play natively on linux ! 

Now that i have tried out the game i can firmly say i will be ordering this pretty soon. Just havent decided if i should get 1 or 2 copies (1 for brother - xmas gift , 1 for me). 

- at 30$ this is a very good value. A lot of current games that come out run for 60$. I definitely recommend this for anyone who likes this genre !

-If you have no idea what the game is about, just go on youtube and watch a video or two of gameplay so you get an idea.

Please post questions/thoughts/comments about this review below. 

Thanks for Reading !
