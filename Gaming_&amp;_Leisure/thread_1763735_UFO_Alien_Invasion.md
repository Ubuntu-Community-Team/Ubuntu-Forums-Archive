### Post by tastygrue on 2011-05-21
You might want to look at this thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1615865](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1615865)
The command in that thread, "sudo ln -s /usr/lib/games/ufoai/base/game.so /usr/share/games/ufoai/base/game.so", minus the quotations may work for you.  I used to have this problem as well.  Good luck!  It's a great game.
