### Post by ThisEndlessFall on 2009-07-14
I think your error is Compiz. Make sure it is disabled before running a game.

Right click the desktop >> Left click "Change Desktop Background" >> Left click the "Visual Effects" tab >> Left click the little circle next to "**None:** Provides a simple desktop environment without any effects".

This will disable Compiz and hopefully allow you to play Urban Terror without any trouble.
