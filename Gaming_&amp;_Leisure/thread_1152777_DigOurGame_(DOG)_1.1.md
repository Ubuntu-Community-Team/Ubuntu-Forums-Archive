### Post by rodneymillerpca on 2009-05-08
DigOurGame (Dog) 1.1 is a free open source multi-platform on-line role-playing game built on the Arianne game engine. Created by the former lead developer of WorldofOurs. Like WorldofOurs, Dog still carries on the original concept of "ours". OURS meaning it does not belong to any one developer, programmer, coder, administrator, moderator, player, or participant. It is property of all who participate with in it. Feedback, suggestions, comments, and questions are it's lifes blood. The only real differences between DOG and our big sister WOO is 1]WOO is converting back from version 7 to version 3 and remaining with it's vb6 windows based game engine where DOG is using a cross platform Java platform opening it's doors to all Java compatible operating systems. 2]Once WOO has converted back it will remain the same with no further development. DOG on the other hand will be in an ongoing development process and remain cross platform friendly with thanks to Ubuntu, Sun Java, and Arianne! :) 

The game itself is is a 2D Multi-player on-line role playing game. Our first continent Aisoyimstan (Native American god of snow and ice) has just opened this week to full public. Aisoyimstan has many areas to hunt, gather, forage, create, buy, sell, trade, socialize, and have fun. We have daily contest, an amazing in game tutorial system, very friendly players, moderators, and NPC's to help you in the beginning. 

For further information please visit us at [http://dog.servegame.org/](http://dog.servegame.org/) and enjoy! Feedback, comments, questions, or suggestions welcome. We will be checking here often. If you have any questions or need assistance feel free to post here as well.

Thanks again,
             Rodney aka life
