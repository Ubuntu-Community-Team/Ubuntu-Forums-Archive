### Post by ubu-for on 2007-05-14
[Beyond Unreal](http://www.beyondunreal.com/daedalus/singlepost.php?id=10535)

> Mark Rein On UT3 DX10

After Roy Taylor's NVidia blog was updated Thursday with his blurb about Unreal Tournament 3 and DirectX 10, a lot of questions began to come in regarding whether or not Taylor meant to say DX10 would be required or supported:

More good news, I just had lunch at Vesuvios here with Mark Rein and he confirmed that we can now come out and say that Unreal Tournament 3 will be DX10 and in "The Way Its Meant To Be Played" program for launch. We have a long standing and close relationship with Mark and the team at Epic. We're delighted to be able to confirm this. The official site for UT3 can be found here. Thanks to Bill Rehbock, the father of 'The Way Its Meant To Be Played' program for lunch. There will be more news on developments with the Epic team here soon. Thanks Mark. 

Because things can change from what has been stated in the past, and to ease the fears of those tinfoil hat wearing gamers out there, we wanted to get a direct quote from Epic. Mark Rein had this to say:

Supported yes but absolutely NOT required. 

Doesn't get more direct than that. Rest easy, conspiracy theorists, DX10 will be an extra goody for those that have it, while those that don't will be pleased to know that Vista will not keep them from enjoying Unreal Tournament 3.
