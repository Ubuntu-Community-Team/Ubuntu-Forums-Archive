### Post by BigSilly on 2007-06-22
If you don't want to have to compile the latest version of Zsnes, get the Debian package of Zsnes 1.51 from [GetDeb.]("http://www.getdeb.net/category.php?id=3") Download the file, double-click it like you would in Windows and the Debian installer does the rest. When it's done, you'll find Zsnes in Applications/Games on your panel. 

Hope this helps. :)
