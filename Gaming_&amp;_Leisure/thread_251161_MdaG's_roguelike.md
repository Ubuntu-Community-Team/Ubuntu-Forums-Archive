### Post by B0rsuk on 2006-09-22
Take my good advice and make it more like Linley's Dungeon Crawl than A.D.O.M.

Crawl just plain rocks, especially for roguelike conneseurs. Great variety, great balance, great replayability, high difficulty level, resistant to abuse and powerleveling, flexible character development, best god system I've seen in a roguelike.
I voted for SF. There aren't any polished SF roguelikes.

Just a though - how about long turns, like in Fallout / UFO / Jagged Alliance, where you have a certain amount of action points and each action costs some points. In my opinion more flexible. It will be easier to implement multiplayer later, because long turns are more multiplayer-friendly. And no roguelike works this way.

If you don't make it a SF roguelike, I swear I'll either make one myself one day, or fork yours. I want a roguelike with flamethrowers, shotguns, and handgrenades !
