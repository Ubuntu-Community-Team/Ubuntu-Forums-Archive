### Post by chuckyp on 2009-09-07
From id: QUAKE LIVE is a new and innovative multiplayer game experience from id Software - the creators of DOOM and QUAKE - that offers game players of all skill levels a totally free and easily accessible multiplayer game and community through a single website. Every element of the QUAKE LIVE experience, from finding and playing games to post match stats review and social networking are delivered through a single state-of-the-art website.

I've been away from gaming for a while but now I found some good news today.

For those of you that don't know quakelive has released the long awaited linux plugins. Head over to quakelive.com and play quake for free via a browser plugin. Also don't forget to head to the forums and thank id for their work. There's a thanks for linux port thread.

Its still in beta but they are actively developing it. The game will be free for all supported by banners in the 3d worlds. It's quake at its best just log on and frag em.
