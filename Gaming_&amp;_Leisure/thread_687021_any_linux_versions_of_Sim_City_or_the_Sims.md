### Post by manimal347 on 2008-02-04
Simcity for DOS runs under DOSBOX, and Simcity 2000 for DOS likely will, too, though as a protected mode game, it might be slow. Simcity Classic does run under Wine, and Simcity 2000 likely also does. The above programs, of course, all require you own a license for them.
