### Post by Roush 427r on 2011-07-25
I had a pretty incredible dream one night when I was camping in Concrete, WA. I posted the story on my G+ account and I guess I shall share it.

Okay it all started as an underground revival project. College students were experimenting with what they have gathered from various science projects and have started to integrate it into a dark form of science in order to bring back their loved ones.
So they brought down a slab of steak and injected it with a solution extracted from a compost that was for some reason built into the lavatories in the male bathroom. Of course the outgoing material from the body was put there which is extremely unsanitary and weird. After injecting it in the cut steak, it began to move, darken and mutate into what appeared as a deformed scorpion. No eyes, legs or pinchers but did infact have a very odd looking 'stinger'. It pierced the skin of an unaware underground scientist. An organic reaction in DNA occurred within the next 20 minutes and he died slowly.
The next part of my dream I was facing towards a massive stone hallway and hearing screams and moans. I looked behind me and 5 people were focusing and stressing out on what to do as about 3 zombies were trying to push through a chain locked door. I guess by this point you can really call them mutants but I guess it is all the same thing to an effect. Everything went black again as if 
curtains fell.
Curtains reeled up again as I was panting through fog heavy woods, I looked around me and I saw tall dark pines and burnt up trees or dead ones that looked dark. I couldn't really make it out. I heard a scream somewhere and then silence. As I standed over panting for breath I looked up and saw a human like silloutte in the fog. it seemed to be limping as I heard a moan. Then I heard a scream right by me and then a sharp pain in the back right of my neck. That's when I woke up and realized I was sleeping on my neck wrong. I am thinking the story should continue on from there. As if it was a vision of what is going on in a place nearby.

Of course I wish for the game to be F.O.S.S. so it can come out with other amazing mods, fixes, further development and on other OS' besides various Linux distributions.

The combat experience I believe should be using your entire environment to your advantage. Set ambushes, traps, hiding spots,using planks and other objects as weapons, distractions, etc. Of course because the setting is set as a survivalist, you will have to be stealthy.

This is just a base plan and I am sure it can branch out into other incredible stories.
