### Post by pmjdebruijn on 2005-08-10
Hi,

Well, if you take a look at the minimum system requirements:
- Intel® Pentium® III 600Mhz processor or AMD equivalent
- **32 MB** 100% **fully OpenGL® compliant** 3-D video card

So your CPU is barely adequite... And an ATi Rage it's good enough at all... These card have very little real 3D acceleration...

You basically need a **Radeon** of **Geforce** for ET...

Also note that the Minimum System requirements for most games, are the bare minimum requirements, this means you can technically play the game, but I don't think 30fps is acceptable...

Also note that the DRI driver do not perform as well as the windows drivers... I did a comparison of the Radeon driver with Quake 3, and the DRI driver perform at about **75%** of the speed of the native windows driver.

Anyway to conclude... you need a much faster system...
