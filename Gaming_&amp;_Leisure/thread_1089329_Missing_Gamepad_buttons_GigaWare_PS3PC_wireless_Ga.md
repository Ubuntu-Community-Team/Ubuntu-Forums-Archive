### Post by tylenol187 on 2010-09-11
hey, i was having a similar problem, but with a Sony Dualshock 3 sixaxis controller.  link in the thread was very helpful for me as well, thanks!     

homots : if you havnt already broken it...  I had a similar problem with the battery casing on a third party PS3 controller, heres how i fixed it:  if the casing covering, specifically the little latch that is supposed to &quot;snap&quot; when it's in place, is made out of plastic, then just heat it up with a lighter or something (very lightly) and bend it in a way that will make it fit more snugly.  In my case, I held the battery cover upside down and heated the clamp with a lighter, and let gravity pull the notch open more, then let it cool and voila!  fits perfectly now. :-) hope this helped.
