For those looking for a decent FPS style game, I would suggest getting Bungie's Marathon.  It's a game Released by Bungie back in the 90's originally made for the Mac, but has been released as an open source game for Linux, Mac OS X, & Windows.  It has an excellent story line for solo play as well as plenty of networkable maps included as well one floating around on the web.

Here's the link: [http://source.bungie.org/get/](http://source.bungie.org/get/)

Enjoy and I will see you all top side.
