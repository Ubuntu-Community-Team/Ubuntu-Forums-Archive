### Post by MaxIBoy on 2008-11-02
Well, since huge amounts of people already mentioned Urban Terror, I'm going to recommend Starsiege: Tribes.
[http://ayavaron.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2006/PortableTribes.zip](http://ayavaron.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2006/PortableTribes.zip)
Although it's technically not open source, the engine is so moddable and flexible that a community-created patch to version 1.30 has been made. It's free of charge, redistributable, and modifiable. It's basically open source anyway. 

The engine is the ancestor of the Torque engine. The Torque engine has been licensed so many times that the technical workings of it are very well-understood.
