> **Pough913 said:**

Check out openBVE - Train Driver Sim from Ub Software centre. Its a good driving simulation which runs really well in 10.04/32 on an NVIDIA m/board + AMD64 3800 with 3Gigs (nb its good using m/board graphics without any flashy graphics card).

The taster route / trainset provided with the prog is good but a worlwide collection of trains and routes is downloadable from

[http://wiki.bve-routes.com/index.php/Main_Page](http://wiki.bve-routes.com/index.php/Main_Page)

Well worth a try.
