### Post by Tibuda on 2010-01-08
Because those games are designed for Windows. To run them in Ubuntu or other Linux, you could try to use [Wine]("http://www.winehq.org/") ([install on Ubuntu]("apt:wine")), but it won't work for every game. To see people experience playing games using Wine, browse [http://appdb.winehq.org/](http://appdb.winehq.org/).
