### Post by Tampio on 2010-04-13
Anyone here play, or even heard of Hockey Arena? It's a browser based online hockey manager game where you train players, edit lineups etc and play against other people. If you know what Hattrick is, this is basically the same but with different sport.
When I started playing this game 4 years and 3 months ago, I never would have thought it would hold my interest this long, but here I still am logging in every day to check how my team is doing.
It's always nice to have more players, so if there are some who are willing to try this game out here's a [referer link]("http://www.hockeyarena.net/index.php?ref=92530") to the game, and for those who dislike referers for one reason or another, here's a [normal link.]("http://www.hockeyarena.net")
