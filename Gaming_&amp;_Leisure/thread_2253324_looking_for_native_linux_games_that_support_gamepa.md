Don't Starve

### Post by deadflowr on 2014-11-20
I play super maryo chronicles with a gamepad.
fwiw

I just adjust the button setting in options > joystick.

Also, if you do install the game, depending upon which Ubuntu version, it might have a broken installation, where as it does not create a campaign folder, and causes the game to seg fault.
A simple workaround is to make the folder /usr/share/games/smc/campaign.
They say a [fix was released]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/smc/+bug/947358"), but I do not think so...
