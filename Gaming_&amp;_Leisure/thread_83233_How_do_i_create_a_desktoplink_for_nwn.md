### Post by Havoc on 2005-10-28
I bet you're referring to a shortcut, no?

If NWN is in your menu, then just drag 'n drop it inside the desktop space.

If no, then right-click on the desktop, and choose "Create Launcher"

Enter name, comment, and in the command space, put the full path to the NWN executable.Also, find a nice Icon.

You're done!
