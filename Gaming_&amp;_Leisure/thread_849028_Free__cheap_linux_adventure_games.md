### Post by FranMichaels on 2008-07-04
There is Flight of the Amazon Queen in the repos.

It is worth taking a look at what scummvm is compatible with.
[http://www.scummvm.org/compatibility.php](http://www.scummvm.org/compatibility.php)

As for Sierra, you can run those (some in scummvm) and "all" of them in DOSBox (I can vouch for every single Sierra game I have working in it.) Some are up for sale again, fortunately bundled with dosbox rather than being "ported" to some icky Windows thing (so it's playable anywhere DOSBox can be run, and across multiple architectures, yay!)

You can also look at some homebrew stuff (again works with dosbox or scummvm.)

[http://www.agidev.com/games/](http://www.agidev.com/games/)

Happy adventuring!
