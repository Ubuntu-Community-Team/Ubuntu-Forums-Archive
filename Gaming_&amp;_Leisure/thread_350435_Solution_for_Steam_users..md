### Post by Masterchafed on 2007-01-31
As many of you know, and have had this happen (me being one as well) many people cannot get Steam to work on their linux distros using wine or cedega as it seems to always fail at 26% updating. Well, I have finally found the solution (if it has not been posted before) and that solution lies withing a program known as CrossOver. I had no problems installing steam, but it still failed at 26%, but then restarted automatically and continued updating. Now, this seems to happen with cedega as well, but there is good news. Whenever it finishes updating from the crash it actually boots into Steam. Whenever it would do this in cedega it would just fail, and nothign would happen. So Crossover seems to be the solution. Post your stories and tell me if it works for you, I was starting to become desperate, and am excited to know it works.

--Masterchafed

edit: I would also like to mention that it has not crashed once on me yet, and that all the text is displayed as well as the store connecting and working perfectly. Cheers.
