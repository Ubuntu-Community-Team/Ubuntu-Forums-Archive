### Post by Grey Dog on 2007-05-30
If you are referring to 'castling', move as though you are moving the king two spaces towards the rook. The game will take care of moving the rook.

Keep in mind that you cannot castle:
- after moving the king or respective rook in any way
- to escape check
- if you move through or land in check
