### Post by Joshefson on 2006-04-14
I had the same problem with the sound drivers in Call of Duty.
The way that I got it to work was to open up winecfg in a terminal.  That will open up the wine configuration menu if you have xwine installed I believe.  From there just click on the audio tab and it will select a sound driver for you.  I don't think wine selects one by default so that should fix your miles sound problem ;)
