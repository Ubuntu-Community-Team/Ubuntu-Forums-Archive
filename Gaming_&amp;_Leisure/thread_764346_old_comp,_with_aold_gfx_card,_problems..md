### Post by chiefwhosm on 2008-04-24
In case you're not aware, the card that you've got is a DirectX 5 generation card, so don't expect to be playing any games that require a minimum of a DX8 generation gfx card with it.

On the subject of the OpenGL error however, this is because the card doesnt support OpenGL properly. This is the same card used in the Apple iMac G3s, which annoyed many G3 users for the fact that the graphics card was incapable of running the new OS X Aqua GUI due to the lack of support for full OGl.
