### Post by handy on 2008-03-25
[I]Hi Folks,

I am very happy to announce that we have now shipped our new product,
CrossOver Games.  CrossOver Games will allow you to install and play
some of the most popular games available for Windows on your Mac or
Linux system, all without needing to buy or run a copy of Windows.

This includes support for Guild Wars, World of Warcraft, and many
games through Steam, including Team Fortress 2, Portal, Half Life 2,
Civilization IV, Peggle, and many others.

You can learn more about CrossOver Games and download a free trial
version at our web site here:
  [http://www.codeweavers.com/products/cxgames/](http://www.codeweavers.com/products/cxgames/)

Additionally, we are giving all current CrossOver Mac and CrossOver
Linux Professional customers a complimentary subscription to CrossOver
Games.  Those customers can simply register the trial using their
existing email and password.

If you'd like to learn more about why we decided to launch CrossOver Games,
you can read the story here:
  [http://www.codeweavers.com/about/people/blogs/jwhite/2008/3/25/how-the-fun-an](http://www.codeweavers.com/about/people/blogs/jwhite/2008/3/25/how-the-fun-an)...

I hope this brings you joy on your computer by letting you play some of
your favorite games on your favorite operating system without having to reboot
or otherwise hassle with Windows.

Thanks for all of your support through the years, and I hope you
enjoy CrossOver!

Cheers,

Jeremy White
CEO
CodeWeavers, Inc.[/I]

A gamer requiring support from Codeweavers will usually come down to how well that game runs under CrossOver to start with, & then how well it runs on the users particular hardware.
