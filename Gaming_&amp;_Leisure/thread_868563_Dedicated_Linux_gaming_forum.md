### Post by Crank Parity on 2008-07-24
Hello all,

I'm considering starting a forum devoted to gaming on Linux. This will encompass both open-source and commercial games, including those that run in Wine and various emulators - basically any game that you can play on Linux.

If you're interested in helping out, please send me a PM. Right now this is all in the planning stages, so it's the perfect time to express an interest and jump on board. I do not plan on paying anybody, as I assume most of the expense will be coming out of my pocket in the form of hosting, domain, and forum licences (if you're willing to chip in, please let me know!). Eventually I might look into obtaining sponsors, whereby we ask companies to donate either hardware (computers, graphics cards, etc) or software (games) and raffle them off to help pay for upkeep.

I don't have much set in stone at the moment, and I look forward to any feedback. If you're interested in helping out, and/or have anything to say, please reply or send me a PM. While I think the Gaming & Leisure section on this forum is an invaluable resource, I was hoping to create a forums site devoted to Linux gaming exclusively- I don't mean to compete with this one, but rather serve as an extension of sorts.

Thanks, and I look forward to hearing from you.

[[COLOR="Blue"]_http://www.ubuntugamingpc.com_[/COLOR]]("http://www.ubuntugamingpc.com") Thats where the site is being setup, and [[COLOR="blue"]_http://ubuntugamingpc.wordpress.com_[/COLOR]]("http://ubuntugamingpc.wordpress.com") is where the idea is being setup and a few articles have been written.
