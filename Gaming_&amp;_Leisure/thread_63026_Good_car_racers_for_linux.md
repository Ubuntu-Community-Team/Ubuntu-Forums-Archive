### Post by Mishura on 2005-09-06
Some I have installed right now:

1.  Torcs  (Very good.. hard to play though.. more "simulation" than "arcade-y")
2.  VDrift (Very hard.. you have to use clutch and manual shifting and stuff.  Good graphics.)
3.  Trigger (A rally car racing game, with more of the Need for speed "arcade" racing feel)

All three are Free/Open Source.  Search HappyPenguin.org for em.  They're cool.
