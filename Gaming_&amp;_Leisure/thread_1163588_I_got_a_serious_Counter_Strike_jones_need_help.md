### Post by Duskao on 2009-05-18
Well, there are a couple other things that we will need to know to be able to give you the information. Firstly... video card: Ati or Nvidia? Model. 
secondly... as far as I know you cannot get it to run quite as well in wine as in windows YET. Each new wine version seems to be speeding it up on my system. 
Thirdly... What according to you is unacceptable play performance? This differs from person to person. ie. resolution, graphic quality, ability to use DX 9. 

Just to give you a for instance, My specs are AMD X3 2.3 ghz processor, 2 gigs ram, Radeon 4850 512ram. 
I run it at 1024-756, high quality, DX 8.1, and I get generally 45-60 fps. 
With a couple registry tweaks. 
UseGLSL - enabled
VideoMemorySize - 512 (use your video cards memory size)
OffscreenRenderingMode - fbo

and I find this completely playable, I'm more into the fun then the graphics but some people are more into the graphics to go along with it ie. higher res and what not

Hope that helps man. Best of luck in all your counter strike endeavours
