### Post by zerosystem on 2007-04-15
I use dvd decrypter to burn PS2 discs. 

[http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=2587&iTestingId=10004](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=2587&iTestingId=10004)

You'll need to set it up so that it'll detect your optical drive, but other than that one time thing, it works great with no problems. I'm not sure about other people. but I need to put a blank dvd into my drive and then start up dvd decrypter for it to detect the drive, even after putting in the right settings.

Refer to the above link, and you'll be fine.
