### Post by mister_k81 on 2014-11-03
I use the Logitech F310 gamepad, which works great for a general purpose gamepad  and they are cheap too. But the Logitech f310 doubles as a Playstation styled gamepad, and it has Xinput support for modern games. The Xbox 360 wired gamepad is a great choice too, as said above. 

I don't think there are any general "all in one" emulation pads though that have the button symbols for different consoles. But for retro pads, the [Buffalo classic control pad ]("http://www.amazon.com/Buffalo-Classic-USB-Gamepad-PC/dp/B002B9XB0E/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1415007129&sr=8-1&keywords=buffalo+controller") is nice, but it is designed to look and function exactly like an SNES gamepad. It is great for both NES and SNES styled emulation. There are other USB styled retro pads out there too that are designed to look like classic controllers, but they vary in quality and I haven't tried most of them myself. The Buffalo pad is great though.
