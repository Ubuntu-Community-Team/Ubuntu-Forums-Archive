So I tried the demo of a game called Trailer Park Tycoon in Wine.

I'll say this, I've played a lot of *sim*ulation games, and I haven't seen anything like this. I'm not saying it's good, or even worth trying, but if you're bored and want to build a trashy trailer park to attract residents so they can be beamed up by aliens, then the Trailer Park Tycoon demo may help you waste some of that extra time. If you do end up trying it, post your thoughts in this thread about it. :)
