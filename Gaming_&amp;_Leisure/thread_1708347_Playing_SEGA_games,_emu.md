### Post by ojdon on 2011-03-16
GerbilSoft's Gens G/S has a port for Ubuntu. You can download it at the bottom of here: 

[http://segaretro.org/Gens/GS]("http://segaretro.org/Gens/GS")

This should be able to play Megadrive/Genesis and I think Master System ROMs. You could also try the Linux port of Kega Fusion:
