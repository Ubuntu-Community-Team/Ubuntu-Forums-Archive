### Post by omrikap on 2007-08-02
Your graphic card is not installed.
I had the same problem.
try to go to 'System -> Administration -> Restricted Drivers Manager' and then enable your graphic card driver (if it's on the list).

if you didn't find it, go here:
[http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty)
and look for the installation instractions of your card.

good luck :)
