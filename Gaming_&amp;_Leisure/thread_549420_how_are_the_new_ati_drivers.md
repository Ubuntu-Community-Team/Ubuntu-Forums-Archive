### Post by sloggerkhan on 2007-09-12
I'm using them with an x700 mobility. They seem to work fine, though some weirdness.

The major benefit I've seen in Enemy Territory isn't so much better framerates as more stable framerates. The ATI Catalyst Control Panel works ok. It behaves a bit weird, though: it's changes take effect in realtime, but it always gives an error message saying that the computer must be restarted for them to take effect... not sure why. Also doesn't have options for dual head. My glxgears framerate went up by almost 50%, though it looked like there might have been a slight geometery error on one gear.  However HUGE feature for me (I have a second monitor for my laptop) is image scaling (kinda like constraining aspect ratios or not). Makes a huge difference for me at sub-max resolutions w/dual display.

I've never really used nvidia's drivers, but I'd guess it's a good start, but still a little ways to go.
