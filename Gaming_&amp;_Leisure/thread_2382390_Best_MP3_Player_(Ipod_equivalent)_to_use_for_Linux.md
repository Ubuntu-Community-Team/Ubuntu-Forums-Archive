### Post by Dennis N on 2018-01-12
Players that have MSC (mass storage class) setting will be seen as a flash memory device by Ubuntu when plugged into USB port. You can then transfer music files back and forth. Check the specs of the player - I suspect most of them are MSC compliant. Mine is an old Creative MUVO V100 with 2 GB storage that I value highly. Very small and uses one AAA battery.
