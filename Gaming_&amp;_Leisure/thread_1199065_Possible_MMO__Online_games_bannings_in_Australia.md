### Post by Naiki Muliaina on 2009-06-28
Gots a notecard today from the owner of Happy Hippsters education centre in Second Life. It read as follows :

> RE POSSIBLE BANNING OF SL IN AUSTRALIA
Saturday, 27 Jun 2009 22:52:52 GMT 

I have looked at the best course of action to take for those interested in doing their part to stop this proposal....
visit the website for the Minister for Broadband, check contact details (and other content) and send strong protest email or letter.
[http://www.minister.dbcde.gov.au/media](http://www.minister.dbcde.gov.au/media)

A mini google later and i found this article : [http://www.smh.com.au/digital-life/games/web-filters-to-censor-video-games-20090625-cxrx.html]("http://www.smh.com.au/digital-life/games/web-filters-to-censor-video-games-20090625-cxrx.html")

This could include all online games in Australia, which of course would suck royally. Its not certain yet if its going to happen or not, but as the article says, it places a cloud over any game with user generated content. World of Warcraft, Regnum, Second Life, pretty much anything where you play with other people. Puts games that its possible to mod out straight away.
