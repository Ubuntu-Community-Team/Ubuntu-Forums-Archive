### Post by ReapeX on 2007-05-21
For everyone that plays Guild Wars and is frustrated about the mouse pointer disappearing on zoning, this is for you.

Simply press the m key to bring up the map.
Press it again to make the map disappear and BINGO.

You have your mouse pointer in less than 10 seconds of zoning.

Now get out there and kill some Charr! XD
