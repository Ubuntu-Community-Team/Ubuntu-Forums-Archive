### Post by Robin T Cox on 2008-04-10
As a result of yesterday's update, version 2.3-2. of Nexuiz in the current Gusty repository will no longer work.

> Package nexuiz-data has been backported in gutsy-backports at version 2.4-1~gutsy but package nexuiz hasn't. It is still in gutsy at version 2.3-2. Upgrading nexuiz-data removes nexuiz, thus the game becomes unplayable.

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/nexuiz-data/+bug/214453]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/nexuiz-data/+bug/214453")

Let's hope that a fix is soon forthcoming, although this bug does not apply to the Hardy Heron version and therefore it's possible that Gutsy fans may have to upgrade.

EDIT:
Nexuiz 2.4 is best downloaded directly from Sourceforge, and works fine in Gutsy.

[http://switch.dl.sourceforge.net/sourceforge/nexuiz/nexuiz-24.zip](http://switch.dl.sourceforge.net/sourceforge/nexuiz/nexuiz-24.zip)
