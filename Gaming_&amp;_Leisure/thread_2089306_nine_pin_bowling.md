### Post by FrankMeyer on 2012-12-21
Hi Jronic,

this is the developer of ninepin bowling for Linux. 
Please try to click in the bottom of the screen and while keep clicked, move the mouse to the upper part of the screen. Release the Mouse Button in the Upper part. 

The Ball only returns to start position, if the move was not long, fast or strong enough or if the left mouse button was released to fast.
