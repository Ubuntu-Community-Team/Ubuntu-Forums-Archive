### Post by jiff on 2007-11-20
Lots of windows programs work with wine, and you can get that easily through synaptic, or by typing

sudo apt-get install wine

in the terminal, then typing

winecfg

and setting it to Win XP or Vista or whatever

As for Gunz, however, I do not think that there is any way to play it in Linux (and oh how I have tried).  It's really not Internet explorer that's the problem, it's that the installer loads an anti-cheating monitor along with the program for Gunz when it loads, and that won't let you into the game client unless you are running Windows.  As a matter of fact, Gunz *does* work with Firefox, just only in Windows :(

Sorry to disappoint, but I will continue to look for a solution.  In the meantime, you might want to ask a mod over at the ijji forums when they will fix this problem.  Maybe if enough people complain...
