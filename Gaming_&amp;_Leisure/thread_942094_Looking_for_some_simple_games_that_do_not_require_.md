### Post by rick08 on 2008-10-09
Great simple linux games would have to be [supertux]("supertux.lethargik.org/"), [tux kart]("supertuxkart.sourceforge.net/"), and [frozen bubble]("www.frozen-bubble.org/").  These games are really simple and can be downloaded from the repo's or on the internet and transfered via sneaker net.  These games should run fine on your eeepc.  Although they are not solitaire or minesweeper they still are fun and basic games.
