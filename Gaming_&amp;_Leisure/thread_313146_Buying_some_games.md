### Post by Naegling23 on 2006-12-05
ut2004 is great (UT2007 is due in Q1 2007), doom3 is a decent fps (as good as you'll get on linux), nwn is old, but good, with a lot of play time, and cheap (the game and 3 expansions sells for 20bucks).  

I dont know about creatures, I have never heard about it.  Darwinia has been around a while, but recently gained some following when it was released on steam about a year ago.  The graphics are unique, they are not great, but they are stylistic enough that it doesnt matter what the graphics are....and blocky graphics is actually the point of the game.  There is a linux demo available for darwinia, so I would suggest trying it out.  It looked interesting, but wasnt really my type of game.
