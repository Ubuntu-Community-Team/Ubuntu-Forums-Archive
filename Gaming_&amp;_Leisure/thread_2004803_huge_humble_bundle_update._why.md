### Post by thatguruguy on 2012-06-16
The Limbo update deals with [some installation problems and sound issues]("http://support.humblebundle.com/customer/portal/articles/582034-limbo-linux-6-8-12").

The Bastion update, among other things, [adds gamepad  support]("http://support.humblebundle.com/customer/portal/articles/585756-bastion-6-12-12").

You can follow changes and fixes to games included in the various Humble Bundles [here]("http://twitter.com/#%21/humblesupport").

If you don't want to update the games, don't.
