### Post by HorstJENS on 2006-12-15
For all the curious gamers out there:
A new commercial game with native linux support is out there:
[http://www.imitationpickles.org/galcon/index.html](http://www.imitationpickles.org/galcon/index.html)
The game cost ~20 US$ / ~17 € but the demo and a older, less polished version is free to download. Interesting fact is that the game is written in [pygame]("http://www.pygame.org").
Description:
Conquer  planets by launching armadas of little spaceships.. all in real-time, and all over after a few minutes.
