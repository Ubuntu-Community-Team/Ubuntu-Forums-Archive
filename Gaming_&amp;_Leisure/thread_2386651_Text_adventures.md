### Post by lisati on 2018-03-08
This takes me back to the 1980s, when I occasionally played a variant of the [Colossal Caves]("https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Colossal_Cave_Adventure") game, seen by some as the classic text based adventure game. I don't remember having encountered a version playable on Ubuntu.

Side note: also in the 1980s, my attention was drawn to a game called Grow, which works a bit like your typical adventure game, but with a catch - users can extend it by extending its repertoire of possible actions in specific locations, including directing the user to places in the adventure that don't exist yet. I think I've got a GW-BASIC version of it saved somewhere in plain text format.
