### Post by Conoktra on 2011-06-11
Gravity Games is pleased to announce the second public release of  Galactic Vice:  Alpha 2 Pre-Release A.  Galactic Vice is a FREE,  real-time strategy game for both Windows and Linux that features two  races at war.  Vital mistakes made by a corrupt human empire lead to a  catastrophic war against an alien race--one in which humanity is pushed  to the brink of extinction.
