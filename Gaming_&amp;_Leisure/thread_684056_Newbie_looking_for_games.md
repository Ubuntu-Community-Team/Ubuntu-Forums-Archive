### Post by Vadi on 2008-01-31
Go to Applications - Add/Remove. Then click on the games section. The list is *huge*, so it's hard to repeat it all every single time someone asks for a list (and they do often, if you tried searching). Another list is on getdeb.net ([click]("http://www.getdeb.net/category.php?id=3")).
