### Post by {BzF}~JOKesTER on 2008-03-13
Yes You Can Run GB Games In VBA!!!!

They Don't Look Very Good In Fullscreen However..

Just Right Click Any Regular Gameboy Game And Click The "Open With"
Tab.

Now Delete Any Items On The List By Pressing "Remove" Now Click "Add" And Click "Use A Custom Command" And Type This:

'/usr/bin/vba' 

Now Click The Add Button And Thats It!!

Now All You Have To Do Is Double Click On A Gameboy Game And It Will Play!!!!
