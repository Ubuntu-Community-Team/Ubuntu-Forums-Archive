### Post by Naiki Muliaina on 2008-11-07
The costs are about £3 a month. The games free to download. Not much realy. You dont have to pay the sub right away, the whole bit up to level 30 is sort of like a demo you know? They had to limit people to part of the world on the demo accounts because of scripting (at least they try to take steps against scripters aye?).

Is it populated? VERY! Lotsa french servers, quite a few american, british and internationals too.

The install is straight forward. Download, put it in its own little file and run it from that file.

As far as being good is concerned, thats a matter of opinion. I realy enjoyed it when i played it but i chose a bad server to start on. Shikas known for being a power gamer server with folks with power hungry attitudes on (lets be honest, every big MMO has one). I think Rushu was meant to be a good international server.
