### Post by tekni5 on 2012-08-26
Take a look at [AssaultCube Mapping and Modding forum]("http://forum.cubers.net/forum-11.html"), they have a lot of user contributed mods. Also [AKIMBO Vault]("http://www.akimbo.in/files/index.php") has a big collection.

I actually made a few mods for AssaultCube.


[LIST]
[*]_[AssaultCube Protox High Quality Reskin]("http://primoturbo.deviantart.com/art/AssaultCube-Protox-HQ-Reskin-202784715")_ - A high quality reskin of all the default AssaultCube player skins. Released under public domain and also here are the [source files]("http://primoturbo.deviantart.com/#/d3cqku7") if you want to edit the skin yourself.
[*]_[AssaultCube Protox Mod]("http://primoturbo.deviantart.com/gallery/#/d39gv6j")_ - Modifies most visible icons and effects with custom ones.
[*]_[AssaultCube Protox Simple]("http://primoturbo.deviantart.com/art/AssaultCube-Protox-Simple-203460522")_ - Similar to Protox Mod but uses simplified icons.
[/LIST]

I haven't played the game in a while but generally use my own mods, I also really like some of the [AssaultCube mods PrimeviL has made]("http://forum.cubers.net/thread-205.html"). Unfortunately he is no longer active and his mod doesn't work with the latest version, his license prohibits **Derivative Works **so you can't really make a fixed version and release it as far as I can tell. But for personal use you can [download the old mod]("http://primevil.fr/index.php?dir=assault") and re-use some of the weapon/item skins.
