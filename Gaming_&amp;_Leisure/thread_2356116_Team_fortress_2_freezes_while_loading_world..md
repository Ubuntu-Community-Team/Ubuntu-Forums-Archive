### Post by efflandt on 2017-03-20
The 3/14 update broke TF2 for Linux and Mac users. After that update TF2 would not even start for me. The temporary solution is to right click on TF2 in Steam Library and under **Properties** > **BETAS** (even though there is no beta) chose "**Previous - The previous release**". That works for me. Not sure when they will update the update to fix it, but all that the update added was some hooks (gone wrong) for a future update.
