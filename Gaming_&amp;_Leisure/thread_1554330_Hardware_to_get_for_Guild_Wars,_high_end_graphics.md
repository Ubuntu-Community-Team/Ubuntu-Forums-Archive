### Post by Ferrat on 2010-08-16
If you follow Guild Wars specifications you should be good since Guild Wars works very well in WINE.

Recommended system specification

    * Intel Pentium 4 2.0 GHz or equivalent
    * 1 GB RAM
    * 4 GB available HDD space
    * NVIDIA GeForce FX 5700 series video card or above

Also don't BUMP threads younger than 24hours, it just looks bad.
