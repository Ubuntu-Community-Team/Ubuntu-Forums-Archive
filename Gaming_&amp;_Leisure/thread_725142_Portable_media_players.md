### Post by MONODA on 2008-03-15
i doubt that you will be able to put linux on any portable player except an old ipod. This is called ipodlinux.
[http://www.ipodlinux.org/Main_Page](http://www.ipodlinux.org/Main_Page)
 With that said i suggest you check out Cowon and Archos websites.
EDIT: I suggest you get an ipod off ebay that works with ipodlinux. I used to have it on my ipod untill my ipod broke :'(
if you dont like ipod linux, check out rockbox
[http://www.rockbox.org/](http://www.rockbox.org/)
btw you can run gameboy roms on both :) rockbox is more stable and is easier imho.
