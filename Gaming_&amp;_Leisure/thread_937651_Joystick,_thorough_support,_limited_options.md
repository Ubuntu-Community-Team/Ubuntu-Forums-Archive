### Post by Allochtoon on 2008-10-09
Let me guess what it does. It lets you calibrate the axes so it defines a "zero" on the axes and provides minimum and maximum ranges.

What it however does _not_ do is let you remap buttons, and define buttons as mode switches; i have 2 mode switches with 3 states, and a squeeze switch.

What this means is that i cannot for instance play Comanche 4 because it sees my mode switch 1 (button 9, 10 or 11) and mode switch 2 (12, 13 or 14) as being constantly pressed. Hence ingame i cannot remap any buttons.

So what would be nice:
-remapping buttons
-defining buttons as mode switches

Or for the time being:
-blocking buttons/axes

For the record, i have a Saitek X45.
