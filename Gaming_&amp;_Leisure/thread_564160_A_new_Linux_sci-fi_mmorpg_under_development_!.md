### Post by Hallsofvallhalla on 2007-10-02
hey I was directed here by MaximB, I am project manager of Forsaken Sanctum.  Wanted to give a little more input on the game and be free answer any questions. 

 First thing is first, as far as the N64 comment...the graphics of FS IMO will easily stand up to many of the indie mmorpgs that are being developed for Linux. At least the ones I seen. But as another note we are more focused around game play than graphics at this point in time. 

 A little bit about the game....

 The main storyline and gameguide is on the website but i will quickly go through it for those to lazy to check it out...hehe I know I can often be...
 Basically in a  nut shell the world becomes extremely overpopulated and drained of all its resources. So the world leaders come together and build 4 massive colony ships. Each ship sets out in search of a new planet, after 50 years of floating in space the colonist begin to mutinize and the ships loose course and basically get lost. They do this for a few years until one day they come apon a planet much like earth. They go to send pods to the surface but are attacked by a advanced race of aliens. The four ships all crash apon the surface of the planet. The aliens do not follow onto the planets surface to finish off the humans. The surviving colonist began building cities around the hulls of the massive colony ships, and the next 10 years is filled with war, famine, and encountering other alien races. 
 This is where you come in... You start in Magellion city, the city built around the largest colony ship. The world is Sci-fi/fantasy, some have guns some have nothing more than daggers and swords. Various alien creatures roam the planet. 

 Its a hacked up version of the story but you get the idea.
