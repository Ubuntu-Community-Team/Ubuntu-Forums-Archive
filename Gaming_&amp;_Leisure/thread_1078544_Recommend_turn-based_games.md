### Post by donkyhotay on 2009-02-23
While being right on the border between being turn-based or real-time strategy game you might want to check out [outer space](http://www.ospace.net). It's basically like Master of Orion but designed to be played as a MMOG. Since it's open source you can run your own servers and tweak the timing so you can play a complete game in a few hours rather then a few days/weeks/months which is what it's originally designed for.

//edit: Another free native TBS is 'pioneers' which is basically a settlers of catan clone.
