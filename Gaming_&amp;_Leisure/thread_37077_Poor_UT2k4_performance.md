### Post by Masato on 2005-05-25
I'm not...entirely sure about this from UT2k4, but in UT Gold, you go into the preferences menu and you can reset your video settings. Mine did the same thing when I was at a high resolution and still playing in an even higher resolution. I had to crank UT down to 640x480, and run my system at 800x600. I'd recommend trying that.
