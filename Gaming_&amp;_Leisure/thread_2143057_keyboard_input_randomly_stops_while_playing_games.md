### Post by Ranko Kohime on 2013-05-07
The general consensus is that wireless keyboard/mice are not suitable for gaming, and you're experiencing the why.

As to why it does it inside games and not outside, my SPECULATION is that several simultaneous key presses causes the keyboard to pause it's transmission for some reason.

### Post by Ranko Kohime on 2013-05-09
> **netopalis45 said:**
> ...if there was anything I could do to stop it.
Likely not.  Like I said, it's just my speculation, but I don't think that wireless keyboards are designed to handle the multiple simultaneous key presses necessary to play most games.  Most gaming keyboards advertise that they are "anti-ghosting", for this very reason.

And as much as it pains me to link to M$, this page actually presents some legitimate info on the subject.
