### Post by Scunizi on 2009-03-03
It sounds like your video card isn't set up correctly.  First check in System --> Administration --> Hardware Drivers and see if there is a video driver that is listed that isn't activated.  If there is then activate it and restart.  That will probably do it.  If your driver is already activated then you might need to turn off Compiz and the effects.  Go to System --> Preferences --> Appearance --> Visual Effects and choose 'None'.
