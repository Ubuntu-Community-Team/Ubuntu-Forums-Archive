 boxes and chests are looted by standing on them and pressing the 'l' ('loot') key. you can unlock locked boxed by (for example): 'k'icking them, 'alt-f'orcing them open, unlocking them with a key, lockpick or credit card (if you are James Bond).

you should find lots of amulets, among them one 'Amulet Of Yendor' and loads of cheap plastic immitations of the same.

if you think you cannot go further, without spoiling too much: have you been at the bottom of the mines? winning sokoban? solving your Quest? seeing Medusa (hopefully not)? visiting the castle and Fort Ludos? shaking a stick at Vlad? 

if you did all this and more and are on a level with unimpregnable ground then you need further, more specific advice, but if you can still dig you are not trying hard enough...
