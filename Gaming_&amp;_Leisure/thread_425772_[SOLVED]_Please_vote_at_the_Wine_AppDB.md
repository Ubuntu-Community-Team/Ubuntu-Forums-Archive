### Post by noerrorsfound on 2007-04-27
Please vote at Wine's AppDB for your favorite programs and games.

First, you need an account at the AppDB. [If you don't have one then click here to register](http://appdb.winehq.org/account.php?sCmd=new).

Once you have an account (and are logged in), search the AppDB for the application. If there are multiple versions of the program listed then you'll have to click a specific one before you can vote. Once you're on the right page, it will look something like this:
[http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=7377](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=7377)
If you're on the page you need to be on, the URL will have _iVersionId_ in it, and **not** iAppId.

Scroll down until you see the *Votes* section on the sidebar at the left side of the page. If you don't see it, you're not logged in or aren't at the correct page. Click on one of the 3 slots and then click the *Vote for App* button to submit your vote. You can remove it later if you change your mind, simply by clicking on the slot you used and then clicking *Clear Vote*.

It is possible to use all 3 of your voting slots for the same application, if desired.

To see which games currently have the most votes, click *View Results* on the voting bar or go the the following address: [http://appdb.winehq.org/votestats.php](http://appdb.winehq.org/votestats.php)
