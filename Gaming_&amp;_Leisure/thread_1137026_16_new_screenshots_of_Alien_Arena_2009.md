### Post by Irritant on 2009-06-10
New video :)  

[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gqnZB9PnVDg](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gqnZB9PnVDg)

Some the engine features:

Full real-time per-pixel lighting using GLSL on all surfaces.

OpenAL with Ogg-Vorbis support.

GLSL post process heat/pain distortions.

Parallax, normal, specular mapping using GLSL(plus a low-cost FF trick to enhance the look).

Stencil shadow volumes.

Light volumes and lensflares.

Decals, textured particles and beams with flexible properties, weather effects.

Reflective/refracted glass.

Vegetation shaders.

Fog, both FF and GLSL.
