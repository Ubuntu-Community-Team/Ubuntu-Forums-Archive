### Post by MaximB on 2007-01-24
there are several game developers that make Linux Clients of their games and a few Publishers as well that make our dreams come true.

Post here ANY game developers and Publishers so we can know what to expect from them in the future.

[www.idsoftware.com](www.idsoftware.com) - our dearest developers, almost every game they make has a native Linux client.
[www.bioware.com](www.bioware.com) - the makers of the first NWN games.
[www.runningwithscissors.com](www.runningwithscissors.com) - All of their funny killer games have a native Linux client (it's actually 2 games so far ;)). and soon there be a movie based on their games !
[www.savage2.s2games.com](www.savage2.s2games.com) - the savage games makers, they released the first savage for free !
[www.epicgames.com](www.epicgames.com) - the makers of the Unreal engines and games !
[www.introversion.co.uk](www.introversion.co.uk) - the British developers of Uplink and Dawina


that's what I can think of, but I'm sure there are MUCH more. (I'm **NOT** talking about Linux games online stores like [www.linuxgamepublishing.com](www.linuxgamepublishing.com) and [www.tuxgames.com](www.tuxgames.com) ).
