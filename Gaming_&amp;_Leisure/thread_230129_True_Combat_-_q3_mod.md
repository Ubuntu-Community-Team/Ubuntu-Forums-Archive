### Post by FindWaldo on 2006-08-05
I got it installed, its fun but not as many people play it. You have to download TC1.1full and then TC1.2. Unzip both and manually drag the TC1.2 files into the TC.1.1 folder to update it, then pop the TC1.1 folder in your Q3 folder. Start Q3 and click MODS, and choose True Combat mod.
