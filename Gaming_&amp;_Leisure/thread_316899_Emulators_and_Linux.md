### Post by leech on 2006-12-19
Well, here's the breakdown of emulators I use.

Atari800 for Atari 8-bit games.
Vice for C64
E-UAE for Amiga (normal uae is in the repositories, but it's a older version)
hatari for AtariST (XSteem is also excellent, though not in the repositories)

Gens for Genesis.
mednafen (haven't tried this one yet.)
epsxe for Playstation 1 games.
