### Post by glenmo on 2008-06-15
go to your enemy territory game folder (probably /usr/local/games/enemy-territory/

rename your current et.x86 to et.x86.old (same deal with etded.x86 -- rename to etded.x86.old) 

put the new files (2.60b) into your enemy territory game folder
