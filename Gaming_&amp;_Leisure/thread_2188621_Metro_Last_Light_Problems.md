Hmmm.... to be honest, AMD drivers are flaky till next year.  I was able to play Metro LL on just below medium settings on an HD 5870 but performance got bad when the action, physics, and environmental effects kicked in on later levels (like surface levels).  I was playing that on 1080p.  
It got bad to the point where I just decided to go and get a new card as I really liked the game and couldn't wait for better AMD drivers.  Nvidia is really the way to go for this game but if you want Mantle in the future then 7770 is the way to go, it is equivalent to an XBONE.  That said however, I'm not sure if you will be able to max things out with even with 7770 as this game will never use Mantle.  The only way really is if AMD optimised this game in their driver like in Windows.
