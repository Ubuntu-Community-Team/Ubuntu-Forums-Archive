### Post by aesis05401 on 2009-08-01
You are going to think I am joking when you login, but apart from a well put together MUD, this is the best dungeon crawler ever:

```

telnet
open
nethack.alt.org

```

Check out #nethack on freenode for conversation, and nethack.wikia.com for a spoiler wiki.  

Additionally, there are forked versions that have different graphical interfaces.  One - called 'falcon's eye' IIRC is 3d front-end 3rd person POV, and another is a FPS type orientation.  ASCII is best for playing the game in pure form, though. 

Be careful - many a telnet n00b has started a game of nethack thinking it was child's play, and woken up a couple decades later realizing they had wasted half their life in pursuit of the amulet of yendor ;)
