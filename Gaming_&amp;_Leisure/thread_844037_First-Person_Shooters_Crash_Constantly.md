### Post by Unanimated on 2008-06-29
The title might be slightly misleading, but I would consider this a "crash."

Sometimes, when I am playing an FPS in fullscreen, the screen will sometimes randomly shrink down to a window, and neither my mouse or keyboard responses will do anything to what's on the screen. Pressing alt+tab will do nothing, and I end up either having to do a hard reset or pressing ctrl+alt+backspace. I'm serious when I say this: when this happens, I can do **absolutely nothing.**

So far, this has happened in OpenArena, Sauerbraten, and Tremulous. It seems to happen more often in Tremulous...

Also, for some reason, it won't happen when I'm playing a game like Frets on Fire, ManiaDrive, Glest, Pingus, Wesnoth, Lincity-NG, Warzone 2100, or any of the games that came preinstalled with Ubuntu. If it involves the terminal, please post the code that I need. Thanks everyone!

EDIT: It also shrinks down when playing Frets on Fire and Warzone 2100, but the game does not ignore my commands.

### Post by eragon100 on 2008-06-29
You just have to disable compiz (desktop effects), they are horribly unstable with games and cause the problems you are describing.

Go to system -> preferences -> appearance

Go to the tab "Visual Effects", and select none.

Everything should work fine after that :wink:
