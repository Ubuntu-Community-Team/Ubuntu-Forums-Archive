### Post by someone23554 on 2010-06-11
Try RPG Reborn. [http://RpgReborn.com](http://RpgReborn.com) 
 
Its realtime, browser based, and free so you can play it from anywhere without a download or install. 
 
Its similiar to the old Legends of Kesmai, in the art and UI aspects, and although its still in beta so the graphics are simplistic the actual game play is pretty unique and the world is huge. Experience is skill based and you can train any or all skills so everyone is different. Your not stuck in a certain class. Its pretty challenging and keeps my attention.
