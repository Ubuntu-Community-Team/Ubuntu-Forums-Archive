computer can be freezing for a number of reasons.
 
- If everything works fine and if it only freezes in more demanding games then it could be that it is overheating. this can be checked and verified by various programmes. you need to consult your motherboard and processor manual on optimal temperatures.

- i don't know how old your system is but it could be that your motherboard or graphics card are in poor condition. they work just not under stress. capacitors may be damaged. the easies to see this is to open the box look for capacitors on mother board and grpahics card and see if they are bloated (they should be sort of flat on top)
