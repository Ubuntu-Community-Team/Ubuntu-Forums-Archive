### Post by agtownz on 2007-12-29
Well, I figured it out some time ago, forgot to post back until now.  My problem was that I activated auto updates from the little weather widget that goes on the top bar.  Deactivating the auto update feature should fix the problem, I'm not exactly sure why,  but auto update seems to disrupt any fullscreen application that's running.  Hope this helps!
