### Post by dgel on 2012-07-10
So, not sure if this is the right place to plug a kickstarter project, but here goes:

[Z.]("http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/downwardviral/z-2") is a humans-vs-zombies Card Game, which the creators say is best described as: "Magic: The Gathering meets The Walking Dead". It's multiplatform and includes linux, and will have online multiplayer as well as a single-player and coop rpg mode. Exclusively for this kickstarter, there will be a physical card version as well.

Gaming celebrities are already lending their support to the project, including Tim Schafer, Ron Gilbert, Tex Murphy and Jessica Nigri. Unfortunately the project hasn't made the goal yet, with only 4 days to go now, but I'd really love to see this project completed.

So, is anyone else stoked about this project?
