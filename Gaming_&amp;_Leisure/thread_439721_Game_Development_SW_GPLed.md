### Post by saulgoode on 2007-05-10
I know nothing about computer gaming but while browsing around the web, I noticed that the [Cryptic AR](http://www.crypticar.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=11) game development software has just recently been made available for free download under the GNU Public License. They are also creating public  forums for game developers and artists to share their activities.

Shayne Herrera, Art Development Director for Cryptic Studios announced:

> "Our goal is to foster a community of animators by providing them the power to generate animations without having to worry about supporting a toolset. Since we were already developing the rig for our core technology team, we decided to release it to the public under the GNU GPL. We feel that the development and gaming communities will benefit greatly from a professional tool like the Cryptic AR."
