I'm not usually into Humble Bundles - too much indie stuff that doesn't do it for me and all that. But this one?
[https://www.humblebundle.com/](https://www.humblebundle.com/)

DAMN. Ever wished companies would just sell you the ROMs to old games they're not doing much with? Well, that's exactly what SNK is doing. Every game comes with a barebones emulator that can be played standalone, but the package also contains standard ROMs that run on most emulators - perfectly legally. At least FinalBurnAlpha/Fightcade definitely works, and I've heard these run fine on MAME as well, but don't take my word for it.
