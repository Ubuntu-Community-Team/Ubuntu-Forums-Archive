### Post by Zeven on 2015-05-28
[QUOTE=GamingOnLinux]Endless Legend was [originally going to come to Linux](https://www.gamingonlinux.com/articles/endless-legend-will-come-to-linux-suffering-delays.4722), and then the [developers decided it wasn't worth it](https://www.gamingonlinux.com/articles/endless-legend-linux-port-is-now-on-hold-not-looking-good.5112). The Linux client [had even been started and was looking good](http://www.gamingonlinux.com/articles/endless-legend-linux-port-started-months-ago-looked-good.4786), but was dropped after the Windows and Mac OS clients were deemed too buggy. Now SteamOS has appeared in Amplitude's voting system for what to work on, and it needs votes.[/QUOTE]

[Source]("https://www.gamingonlinux.com/articles/endless-legend-could-come-to-linux-with-more-votes.5413"). You can vote for SteamOS support (and therefore Ubuntu support) [here]("http://g2g.amplitude-studios.com/G2G-Votes2").
