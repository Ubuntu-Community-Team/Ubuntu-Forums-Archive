### Post by fuzzix on 2004-12-19
I have this problem with a couple of emulators - you can play with the desktop res to increase the size of the viewable window - or maybe it's decrease :). Ctrl, Alt, + and Ctrl, Alt, - change the viewabale window size of X - use the mouse to scroll to the Gens window when you've found the appropriate size. You can get close enough to full screen using this method.

I also use a KDE tray app called "Resize & Rotate" to play with the X res - this has the advantage of resizing the desktop to match. Not sure if there's a Gnome equivalent.
