### Post by ELD on 2010-05-04
Over the past month or two i have been developing [GamingOnLinux]("http://www.gamingonlinux.info") it grew out of my frustration of a few websites just constantly being out of date and not having the things i want from a gaming website. Also for my own need to improve my web skills :)

I have today added an initial screenshot to the items pages as well as tags under it for open source, free/commercial, online play etc to make it more useful. to people searching it and i have added a few more listings to the directory.
More about that is in the latest news post though -> [http://www.gamingonlinux.info/news.php?view=20](http://www.gamingonlinux.info/news.php?view=20)

Still working on the website for the rest of today just trying to get a few back-end features for myself finished.

So what i want is people with some good ideas to come forward and tell me what they would like to see in a Linux gaming website, good ideas will most likely be implemented (like other tags to add to item pages for example).

Thanks for reading!
