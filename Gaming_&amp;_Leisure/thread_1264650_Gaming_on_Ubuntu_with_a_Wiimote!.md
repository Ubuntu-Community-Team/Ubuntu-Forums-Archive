### Post by N00b-un-2 on 2009-09-12
so, I have a slightly older computer that once was a gaming powerhouse several years ago, but is now aging and obsolete.  I was actually going to get rid of it after it had been dropped while we were moving and the entire front panel had broken off.
Well, my "MacGuyver-ness" refused to see this old desktop as being trash.
Long story short, I now have the tower tucked away behind my TV entertainment center in my living room hooked up to my nice big Plasma TV :D.  I fed it an extra Gig of ram and a 1TB hard drive and I now use it as a media center (using Moovida).
After about a week of having the computer setup this way, I had ripped all of my DVDs onto my hard drive, transferred mine and my wife's music collections onto it along with all of our photos, but try as I may, my wife simply refused to use simply because she did not want to use a keyboard to watch movies.  Thus irritated by having to use my clunky wireless keyboard with no mouse to interface with it and my wife's refusal to use this fantastic bit of techno-wizardry, after much Googleing and forum trolling, I came across the idea of using a Nintendo Wiimote as a pointer device/keystroke emulator.  I purchased an Wiimote and a sensor bar and now we are all very happy using the mediacenter with the wiimote.

But then I got a CRAZY idea! Wouldn't it be awesome to use a video game controller to play video games?  I'm downloading Urban Terror right now.  I'll let you all know how that goes.
