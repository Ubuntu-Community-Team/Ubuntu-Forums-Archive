### Post by efflandt on 2013-05-12
And what video card and drivers are you using. I have not tried any mods or textures lately, but when I did, the only way I could get everything working together was with [MultiMC]("http://www.minecraftforum.net/topic/1000645-multimc-43-windows-linux-mac/"). I did not have any success trying to manually install mods directly into minecraft (note that until 1.6 comes out which hopefully will have a common API, mods have to be specifically for the minecraft version). 

I have run plain minecraft on a 2 core 1 GHz AMD-50 tablet PC with 2 GB RAM and keyboard and wireless mouse, ATI HD 6250 video w/fglrx drivers 1280x800 in 64-bit Ubuntu (or Lubuntu w/common /home) 12.04 booted from 32 GB SD card. That is a minimal system that does not run it very quickly, but works when out of town.

It might be very sluggish and unplayable on an old laptop with integrated Intel video (like my 1.66 GHz Core 2 Duo from 2006).
