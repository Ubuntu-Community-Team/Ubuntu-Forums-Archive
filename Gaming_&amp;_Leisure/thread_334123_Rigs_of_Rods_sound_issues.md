### Post by Fred the Penguin on 2010-03-16
I had the same problem.  If you try to follow the directions on the rigs of rods website you add the rigs of rods packages to synaptic.  I noticed that one of the dependencies is libopenl0a.  This cannot be found in synaptic because it is an old package.  You can get it by adding the repository from an older version of ubuntu.  I think that I used ubuntu hardy's.  The sound still doesn't work perfectly, but it at least works sometimes. Even the newer versions of rigs of rods have sound problems.
