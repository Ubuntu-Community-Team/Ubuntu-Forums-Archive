### Post by dannyboy79 on 2013-11-29
I was having a real tough time trying to get my wired afterglow xbox 360 controller to work in any of these 3 games (most likely any source game), I couldn't understand why it wasn't working when the controller worked just fine within Bastion and Serious Sam 3 BFE. I did so much googling and reading and finally something did the trick. All you have to do is launch big picture mode from the steam client, go to the little settings icon, click it with your mouse, then go to controller, then hopefully it says Controller Detected. If it does you're in luck (if it doesn't you have other issues I can't help), then just click on edit controls and basically you're mapping each button on the controller to each command. It goes through each button on your controller, then when you're done you can name it something if you want, then you can exit big picture mode if you want OR play your game from here but the important thing is that your controller should now be working. I had to mess with the x & y acceleration when playing TF2 because movements were really slow so i moved it to about half way BUT in L4D2 the analog stick movements were fine. Good luck and have fun!
