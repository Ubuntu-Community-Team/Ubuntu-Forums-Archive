### Post by jazzpi on 2013-03-07
To find the root directory of MC, go to your home folder, press Ctrl+H and then open .minecraft and then the bin folder.
You can't run .exe applications without WINE, although some might not even work with it. Most applications should be available as .jar file as well though, then you could open them.
