.
SolarWolf was hanging my entire system when gameplay started.  The sound would keep going, but nothing else would work.  I had to completely shut down my computer and reboot to recover.
.
.
The solution seems to be to turn off Compiz via the System > Prefs > Appearance > Visual Effects > None option.  Once the screen effects are turned off, the game works fine.
.
.
Hope this helps anyone else trying to run this game.  It seems that Compiz causes problems with alot of other games. . .
.
.
