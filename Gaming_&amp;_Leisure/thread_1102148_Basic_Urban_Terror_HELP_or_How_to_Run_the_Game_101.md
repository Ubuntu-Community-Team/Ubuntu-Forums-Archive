### Post by gjoellee on 2009-03-22
Some tips for UrT (I use them my self):

[B]1. Never camp unless you absolutely have to!

2. Use a weapon and gear combo that fits you nice[/B]

**3. If you play on a new map, learn how the map is and then use the map for your advantage!**

4. You can use a knife by pressing the "1" button above the characters at your keyboard

5. Do not use the number under "num lock" on your keyboard

**6. Learn to jump, you can ei: jump on walls, even from wall to wal. (Jump maps like Icy Jumps will teach you a lot)**

**7. Give yourself a name that makes you feel strong! **(That will make most people play better)

[COLOR=Red][U][B]8. Reload your weapons often (I reload if I have under 15 shots left)

[/B][/U][/COLOR][U][B][COLOR=Red]9. In your downer left corner you will see your health. If you bleed a part of the body will be red. If you bleed press "Q" (by default), to put on some bandage. Do not put on a bandage if you are in combat/duel![/COLOR]
[/B][/U]
10. Become better by studying how other people play.

[COLOR=Blue]***_11. Change the game settings so they fit you good...a little secret is to increase the brightness. That will do so you can see your enemies better._***[/COLOR]

(BOLD=Really Important)
