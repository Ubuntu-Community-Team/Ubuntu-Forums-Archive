### Post by Raizor on 2009-04-14
Your autoexec.cfg file needs to go into:

/home/YOURUSERNAME/.etwolf/etmain

Replace YOURUSERNAME with your login ID for your box.  Once there, when the game starts it will automatically load the autoexec.cfg file.
