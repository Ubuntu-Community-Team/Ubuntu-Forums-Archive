### Post by Chronon on 2011-01-22
> **IWantFroyo said:**
> Ubuntu is freeware, and the software center is minimal.

Ubuntu is supposed to be free (libre!) software, not freeware.  Freeware generally denotes proprietary software that permits free (gratis) distribution of binaries but offers no access to source code.  This is in contrast to shareware, which asks a fee/donation if users use the software beyond a trial period.

Sorry, it's just one of my pet peeves when people confuse free software for freeware.
