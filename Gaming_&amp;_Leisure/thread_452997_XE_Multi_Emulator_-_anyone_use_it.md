### Post by dpzektor on 2007-05-26
To reply to my own post, I solved the problem:

There seems to be some sort of bug in the current release of XE that is boosting the FPS over 60 frames (62-63) which is causing the sound to crackle, not to mention the emulation is sped up slightly of course. I scoured the net and found v6.0.2.11, compiled it, and lo and behold it works perfectly. It might be noteworthy to add that this emulator is perfect in emulating many systems, and is currently the *best* Genesis emulation you will find in Linux.
