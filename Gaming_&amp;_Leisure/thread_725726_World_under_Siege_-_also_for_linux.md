### Post by Perfect Storm on 2008-03-16
The Game company Cateia, the maker of [The Legend of Crystal Valley](http://locv.cateia.com/) (adventure game - comming soon) are also making [World of Siege](http://worldundersiege.cateia.com/index.php) available for Linux.
The release date is set to this year.


Game title: World under Siege: European Front (abbreviated: WuS:EF)
Genre: Historical Turn-based Strategy
Gameplay: Single-player Campaign, Multiplayer
Platform: PC - Windows – 9x, 2000, XP, Vista, UMPC, Linux x86/x64

WuS:EF is using the latest 3D effects and features of your video card such as shader model 2.0 and 3.0, per-pixel lighting with many light sources, normal and parallax mapping, HDR light and a whole lot of particle effects. If you have a slower PC, you can always scale down these effects and still enjoj the game-play.



**Gameplay Features:**
- innovative single-player and multiplayer campaigns
- three huge WW2 forces : Axis (German and Italian troops), Allies (English and American troops) and Soviet
- fight battles on land, sea and in the air with all means necessary (grenades, torpedoes, depth charges, rockets, projectiles, TNT, mines...
- manage building of infrastructure of war factories
- gain command over more than 80 different army units and war machines
- special units like spies, engineers, long-range artillery (Dora and Gustav) and Maiale
- organize supplies of food, ammo and medicine for battlefields
- astonishing and never seen before spying and counter-spying system in multiplayer
- blow-up bridges in order to slow down enemy troops
- assign marshals, feldmarshals and army generals to units on the map to ensure maximum effectiveness on the battlefield
- get through various of missions and quests to earn bonuses
- special screen filter effects to create more authentic WW2 atmosphere
- original soundtrack to provide the genuine WW2 atmosphere
- music that dynamically changes depending of situations on screen

**Engine Features:**
- screen resolutions: from 1024 x 768 upwards, including wide screen resolutions - pixel and vertex shaders
- dynamical lightening and high-quality shadows (texture and stencil)
- advanced particle effects
- reflections and water animation
- HDR light
- motion blur effects
- high quality animations
- normal and parallax mapping
- unlimited camera control
- very detailed and realistic map and terrain

**Other Features:**
- 5.1 surround – EAX
- original soundtrack and sound effects
- multiplayer developed with high attention
- detail step-by-step tutorial
- audio old LP effects for entire soundtrack
- screen filters like washed-colors and old film to ensure the maximum of WW2 atmosphere
- dozens of Audio – Video – Gameplay options and settings
