### Post by hikaricore on 2009-01-14
Not bad for the price, but it looks like your barebones kit's motherboard comes with an integrated video card?
You may consider buying a case and a motherboard seperately so you can better see exactly what you're getting.
It may take a little longer to pick but you won't be suprised when you get it.

Also expect power supplies that come with a case to last around a year, they're usually quite cheaply made.

Avoid cheap motherboards like the plague.
Anything under 40$ or so might die on you in 6 months, I've seen direct proof of this.   >.<



*edit* Also you may want to avoid micro-atx boards as they will limit your expansion abilities if you ever want to add say another video card or something.
