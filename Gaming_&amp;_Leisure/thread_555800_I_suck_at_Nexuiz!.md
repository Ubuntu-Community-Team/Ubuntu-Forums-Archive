### Post by mikedep333 on 2007-09-23
Nexuiz is self-described as a "basic" first-person shooter. I can give three pieces of advice that apply to nexuiz, quake 2 DM, etc:

1. Strafe (move to the left or right) alot when fighting an opponent: and learn to circle-strafe around an opponent too

2. Get familiar with the weapons: eg. with the shotgun get used to getting up close and then firing, or with the rocket launcher get used to aiming ahead of where they're going and then exploding the rocket in mid-air

3. Get familiar with the maps and go pick up the powerful weapons: eg. go for the rocket launcher if it's available
