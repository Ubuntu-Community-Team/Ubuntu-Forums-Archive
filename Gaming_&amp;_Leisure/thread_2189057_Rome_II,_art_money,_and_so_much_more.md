### Post by oldrocker99 on 2013-11-20
If, by CTRL-ALT-DEL you mean a way to reboot, in the GNU/Linux world CTRL-F1-6 will give you a blank screen asking for a login and password. From there, you can issue a

sudo shutdown -r now

which will reboot safely.

Some, but not all desktops, will display a shutdown/suspend/restart dialog on a CTRL-ALT_DEL sequence.

If you mean something else, I don't really know what you do mean.
