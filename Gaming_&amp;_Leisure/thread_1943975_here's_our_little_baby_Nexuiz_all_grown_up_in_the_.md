### Post by leilei on 2012-03-30
> **BeRoot ReBoot said:**
> Nexuiz was basically stolen from the Free Software community. You should all be playing Xonotic, it's Free successor.

Many people aren't aware about the origins of Nexuiz as an upcoming commercial product, scrapped and relicensed for the Free Software community for reasons involving unsatisfactory asset quality (most models were still untouched since their creation in 2002). 

The console Nexuiz is just a second professional attempt at that. There is no "stolen from the community" here.

The anti-freedom of choice in the quoted post is rather ironic.  I haven't played the new game yet as it's yet to release on Steam, but I wouldn't call it terrible for being a proprietary game with the same name of something that had been released for free prior.  

Besides, I wouldn't bother to compare it to Xonotic, or even Nexuiz 2.x.  You should be at least grateful that they're trying to tap a market that has been stagnant since Unreal Tournament III.
