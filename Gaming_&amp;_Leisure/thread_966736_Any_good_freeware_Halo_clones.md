### Post by MaxIBoy on 2008-11-01
Such a game does not yet exist, but it would be fairly simple to make.

I don't think a Quake-based game really *could* emulate Halo. Quake-based engines are optimized for indoor maps. However, the Cube 2 engine is easily capable of handling Halo-like terrain, sacrificing its ability to cope with complex architecture. 

Adding an only-two-weapons-at-a-time restriction would be fairly trivial. After that, it's only a matter of mapping.
