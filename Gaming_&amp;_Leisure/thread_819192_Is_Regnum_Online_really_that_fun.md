### Post by starcannon on 2008-06-05
I like it, it reminds me of a mix of EverQuest 1st gen, and Dark Age of Camelot.

I joined an english speaking clan/guild right away just so I'd have people to talk to.

If you like the older mmorpgs like EQ and DAoC you'll like Regnum.

Keep in mind the game is still a work in progress, but it is very playable with lots of quests, a great pvp (actually rvr) system, and they have been kind enough to make a linux client (which was just updated if you couldn't get it before, its again available now).

Anyway, I always try to support games of interest if they give me a native linux client.

GL and have fun, my character is named Bloodrott, our clan/guild leader is Joel, hope to see you in there one of these days.
