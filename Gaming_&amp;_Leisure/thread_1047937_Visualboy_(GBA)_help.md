### Post by FranMichaels on 2009-01-23
Mednafen does.

[http://mednafen.sourceforge.net/](http://mednafen.sourceforge.net/)

Right click your rom, choose Open With, mednafen.

Press F1 for hot keys.

alt + c is the cheat search.

Enjoy!
