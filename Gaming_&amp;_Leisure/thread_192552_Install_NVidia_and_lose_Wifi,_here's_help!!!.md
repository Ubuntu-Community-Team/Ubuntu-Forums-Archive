### Post by GmAz on 2006-06-08
I installed the newest nvidia drivers (version 6782) and I lost my wifi because the nvidia drivers install process removes the Linux Restriced Modules.  This sucks because I only have wifi access and I installed the new nvidia drivers so I could play World of Warcraft.  If you want your wifi back, use the guide here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=38972](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=38972)
he used it on Hoary, but I found it to work on Dapper Drake just fine.  The only change is you need to download the newest Madwifi snapshot from here:
[http://snapshots.madwifi.org/](http://snapshots.madwifi.org/)
Hope this helps some cause it helped me tons and I am a complete n00b.
