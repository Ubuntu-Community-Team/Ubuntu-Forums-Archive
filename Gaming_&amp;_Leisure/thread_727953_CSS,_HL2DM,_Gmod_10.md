### Post by soxs on 2008-03-18
Well, in this case, wine does NOT suck, just because you failed.
In case you have a top panel just drag it to the bottom to remove the malpositioned cursor in steam.
Wine can handle msi-files aswell, which you would have known if you first ead this page: [http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=1554](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=1554)
For secial setup for Garryäs Mod 11 take a look at: [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=10990](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=10990) and for CS:S use [http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=3731](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=3731) and last, use the instructions placed in [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=2890](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=2890) to get HL"DM to work(at least it should work similar).
I hope you now get along with wine, and not ban it to hell.
Edit: In general the best way to get steam woking, is to download th official steam msi installer from valves homepage and register your games and download them.
