### Post by Ghouli on 2011-12-09
Battlefield 3 doesn't work at all, but Modern Warfare 3 should run fine, just install from Steam and you're ready to play. Other title I can recommend is Rage, just installed the game and it ran fine without any additional packages. My fps was even higher than on Windows 7 :popcorn:

Edit: Yeah, World of Tanks also works fine using WoTFLIX: [http://forum.worldoftanks.com/index.php?/topic/7289-wot-under-linux/](http://forum.worldoftanks.com/index.php?/topic/7289-wot-under-linux/)

Fps is significantly lower than on Windows, but still playable 20 - 60 with my 8800GTS (G80).
