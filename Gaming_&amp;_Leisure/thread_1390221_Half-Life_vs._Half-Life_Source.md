### Post by KrisInfinity on 2010-01-25
> **ydnom89 said:**
> Is there any difference between Half-Life and Half-Life Source, aside from HLS being based on the Source engine?

Not many... The game, the maps and all the story is the same. Graphics are nearly the same except some higher quality textures and ragdoll animation on kills. Biggest difference is the water quality which is really higher.
