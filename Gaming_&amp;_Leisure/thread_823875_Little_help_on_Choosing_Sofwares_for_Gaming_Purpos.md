### Post by eragon100 on 2008-06-10
Hi there!

Urban terror ([http://www.urbanterror.net]("http://www.urbanterror.net")) is a bit like counterstrike, but it's free and has a native linux version :popcorn:

Other good, free games for linux are:

- savage 1 ([http://www.newerth.com]("http://www.newerth.com")) (1 is freeware, 2 is paid, but one is better anyway) :KS
This is a fantasy themed team-based multiplayer fps which you can also play as a rts, if you command your team. It's a lot of fun!

- nexuiz ([http://www.nexuiz.com]("http://www.nexuiz.com"))
multiplayer shooter like unreal tournament

- battle for wesnoth ([http://www.wesnoth.org]("http://www.wesnoth.org"))
This is a truly magnificent turn-based fantasy strategy game. Singleplayer (about 10 campaigns with some of them having 20 to even 25 scenarios. Lots more campaigns downloadable with a single click from the main menu, as addons.) and multiplayer, both online (with lots of players playing on the official server)
and offline (hotseat, LAN). 200 game units, from elves to orcs to dwarves and mermen and magi. Play it!

- open arena
[http://www.openarena.ws]("http://www.openarena.ws")
exact clone of quake 3 arena

- tremulous ([http://www.tremulous.net]("http://www.tremulous.net"))
a team based fps with strategy elements, aliens vs human. A tiny bit like savage, but there is no pure strategy mode.
Everyone has the strategy elements (I prefer savage, strongly :))

- sauerbraten ([http://www.sauerbraten.org]("http://www.sauerbraten.org"))
Another shooter, yeah! :)

- warsow: [http://www.warsow.net]("http://www.warsow.net")

A multiplayer first person shooter with tron-style graphics and trick-jumping, lots of fun!

Have fun gaming, and believe me, windows is not neccesary :) :popcorn: :guitar:
