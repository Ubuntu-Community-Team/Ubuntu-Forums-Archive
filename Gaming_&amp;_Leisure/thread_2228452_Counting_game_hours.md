### Post by Peter_Solver on 2014-06-15
Tgalati4 already mentioned Gnotime, a GNOME Time Tracker in Club Penguin. It acts like an electronic stopwatch. You can download this software for free and runs perfectly on Unix as well as Linux and Mac OS.
