### Post by FruitieX on 2007-11-12
I have been playing Nexuiz since v1.5 now, so I would say Nexuiz but it's included in that list... There are a lot of gamemodes, with new ones like assault mode or nexball coming soon with the release of v2.4. The community is very, very nice. If you get hooked, I'd say go to the IRC channel after some practice to find someone to 1on1 or even 2on2 (loads of fun). There are some special things in the game like damage thru floor/walls, the laser that is the weakest weapon (mainly used for moving around with), and the slow, in mid-air detonating rockets that have kept me, and still keeps me hooked to this game. :)

More info @ [http://www.nexuiz.com](http://www.nexuiz.com) :)
