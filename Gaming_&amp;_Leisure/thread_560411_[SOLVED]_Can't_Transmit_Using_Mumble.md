### Post by hikaricore on 2007-09-26
This might be a silly question, but do you have the MIC set as the method of recorded input under volume control?
You being able to hear yourself just means that the output volume from the MIC is turned on, it doesn't nessicarly mean that the MIC is selected for designated recorded device.  ^_^

Failing that, check Mumble's configuration to see if you may have overlooked anything.
