### Post by crane on 2006-04-07
Just copy all the files from you saved games folder to a CD. The folder should be in your home Directory.
 /home/jason/.quake4/q4base is where it is located. Just copy the whole savedgames file.

Another thing to think about, When you reinstall. If your HD is big enough, create a partition just for games. I have a 60gig partition  hda5 just for game installs. It's mounted under /usr/local/games. That way I can reinstall the OS over and over again. When reinstalling, all you have to do is mount the partiton but make sure you do not format it. Then all your games are there. No need to reinstall. Oh and just back up you home folders for the game.
Like for what yuo are looking to same, just save the whole .quake4 directory from you home directory. That way you save all you save points and special CFG's.

Good Luck!
