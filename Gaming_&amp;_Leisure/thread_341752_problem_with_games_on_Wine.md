### Post by EvilMarshmallow on 2007-01-25
Your problem is your choice of games.

BF2
[http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=3438](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=3438)

BF 1942
[http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iAppId=1370](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iAppId=1370)

If you look, the regular releases of these games are listed as "**Garbage**" right now.  That means they won't work under Wine at all yet.

I have had good luck with games that are listed as "**Gold**" or better.  Basically, the lower the rating in the appdb, the harder it is to get it to run.

Search in the appdb to find out which games work.  Another good source for this information is the Loki page... they create Linux installers for games.  Google for Loki Installers and you'll find them.
