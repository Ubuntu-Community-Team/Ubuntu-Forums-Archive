### Post by Gnobody on 2006-05-29
[LEFT]For those of you who haven't heard of it, Warsow is a very unique cel-shaded deathmatch-style online FPS with a twist, there is a greater emphasis placed on bunny hopping and doing neat tricks.  It is GPL and doesn't require any additional games.

Check it out here:
[http://www.warsow.net/]("http://www.warsow.net/")

For those of you who have played previous versions, 0.1 fixes all network latencey bugs associated with the QFusion engine and is EXTREMELY well polished.  I will be playing this game for months to come!  Hopefully we can help make it as popular as it should rightfully be. ;)
[/LEFT]
