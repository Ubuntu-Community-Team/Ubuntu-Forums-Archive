### Post by PsamtikNerd on 2008-09-28
The problem is probably with your graphics card driver.  OpenGL apps used to completely freeze my rig after running for 5-20 minutes, until I installed a better driver for my Radeon X300.  Go System -> Administration -> Hardware Drivers and see if there is a proprietary driver available for your card.
