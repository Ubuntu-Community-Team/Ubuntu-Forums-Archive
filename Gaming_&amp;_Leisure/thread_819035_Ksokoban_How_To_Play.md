### Post by RIchard James13 on 2008-06-05
Tricky but doable:

first move to the left of all the gems and push them right. Then go around the gem on the goal square to the right and push the three gems NOT on a goal square to the left. Then go up and push those three same gems down one square. Basically the rest revolves around pushing the gems around in that fashion to get them into position. You can probably also do it the other way round. Start by pushing them all down one, then the three NOT on a goal square up one, then push them all right. The main trick is that you start pushing from a certain end. If you start pushing from the wrong end you will get stuck.
