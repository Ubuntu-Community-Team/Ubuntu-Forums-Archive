### Post by tipsqueal on 2008-03-11
You're most likely not going to be able to play CS:S on your laptop. The graphics card is too limited, it could probably barely play CS:S in Windows. Even if it could play CS:S in Windows, when you use Wine to emulate CS:S you're going to take a significant performance hit compared to Windows. For example in my computer I have a 7600GT, in Windows I can  play CS:S at 1680x1050 with everything on high at a good framerate (minimum of ~30 fps). In Ubuntu I can only play it at 1280x720 with low/medium settings and get a decent frame rate (minimum of ~15fps).
