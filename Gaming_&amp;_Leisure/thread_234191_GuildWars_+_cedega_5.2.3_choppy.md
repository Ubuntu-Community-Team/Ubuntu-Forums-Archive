### Post by User_Program on 2006-08-11
Here are some tweaks [http://cedegawiki.sweetleafstudios.com/wiki/Guild_Wars](http://cedegawiki.sweetleafstudios.com/wiki/Guild_Wars)

I've used the suggestions on that page but noticed I get beter performance with "fixed program" off.  Everything else I followed.  I get about 20-30 fps in town and outa town I get anywhere between 40-60 just depends on the landscape.  I have also noticed that once the map loads for either in town or outa town, doing a couple of fast mouse movements all over the screen reduces video hiccups. Also setting GW's profile (in cedega) all to default and just having fixed program  off works well for me also. As for in game settings.  I can crank it to high and notice no major decrease in performace. Hope this helps out

I have similar system specs.  except for video card mine being the 6800 GT.
