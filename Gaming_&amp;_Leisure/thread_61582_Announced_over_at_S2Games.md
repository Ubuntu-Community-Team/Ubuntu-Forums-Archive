### Post by KingBahamut on 2005-09-01
[IMG]http://www.linux-gamers.net/uploads/img4315bd2d624d0.jpg[/IMG]
S2Games introduced their next title, a successor toSavage2 Linux Savage. The multiplayer fantasy based action/RTS game Savage 2: A Tortured Soul will continue to provide the gaming community with innovation.

 It is a sequel so it will have some similarities to Savage: The Battle for Newreth. Mainly the general concepts and world that made Savage: The Battle for Newreth great, but with a ton of new innovative ideas and the polish to really bring it together. The gameplay is now all unit based, 20 unique units. The few you can see in the screenshots are a builder, scout, warrior, etc.

All with very unique abilities. Units will NOT simply be larger versions of themselves as they were in Savage. There'll be a great deal more RPG elements this time as well, items, drops, buffs, debuffs. With that will come some very unique gameplay.
