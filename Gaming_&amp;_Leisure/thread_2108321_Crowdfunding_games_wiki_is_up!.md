Greetings all just to let you know that [GamingOnLinux]("http://www.gamingonlinux.com")'s new crowdfunding wiki is open and in the spirit of open source anyone can contribute and edit it.

It keeps a list of ongoing, completed and failed funding from kickstarter, indiegogo etc.

So you can keep track of games for Linux that are being crowdfunded, enjoy!
