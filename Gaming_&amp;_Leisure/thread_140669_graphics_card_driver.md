### Post by handy on 2006-03-07
Be sure to investigate **Automatix** too!

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=80295](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=80295)

It will give you about 40 choices of top quality, very popular software.  After making your choices, it will go about the task, (takes about 2 hours on my machine @ 512k broadband for my choices) of downloading & installing ***your*** choices!  One of these is the nVidia closed source drivers.

Automatix has worked for me multiple times on both 64bit & 32bit Breezy 5.10.

I can't recomend it highly enough to the Ubuntu (linux) beginner.  You get results, ease the stress, & give yourself room to be able to get into the depths of the OS, at your leisure!
