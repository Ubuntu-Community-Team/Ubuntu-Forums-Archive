### Post by thatguruguy on 2011-07-31
Although it's already been mentioned, [Heroes of Newerth]("http://www.heroesofnewerth.com/") is worth a look, particularly since it's now free to play.

My son also likes [Spiral Knights]("http://www.spiralknights.com/").

EDIT: As far as lists of games, you may want to check out both the [Linux Game Database]("http://www.lgdb.org/") and [Penguspy]("http://penguspy.com/#/All/free_and_commercial/open_closed/sort=1/view=1/limit=0").  They both provide listings of games by categories, and both provide video previews of the games so you can get a rough idea of the quality of the graphics.
