### Post by cogadh on 2007-06-29
You will need to install the Intel driver in order to get 3D graphics running at their best on that card. Search Synaptic for "intel" and you should be able to find it.

There is no such thing as a program that can alter or improve the running graphics of games in general. The textures and poly counts of 3D models in games are fixed. The only way they could be improved is through an update or mod that is specific to the game. The update/mod would have to contain brand new models and textures of a higher poly count and resolution. There aren't many that will do that, since the quality of the graphics in the game is often a limitation of the engine the game was built on.
