&#9734; Hello guys. How are you doing? I hope all of you are fine.

&#10032; I would like to introduce you a new (started at 9th July 2014), 100% free (not even donation not to mention there is no premium market etc.)

&#10031; Fan made Pokémon MMO RPG Game Pokemon Pets: &#8694; [http://www.pokemonpets.com](http://www.pokemonpets.com)

&#9812; You can register from here to try out the game: &#8694; [http://www.pokemonpets.com/Register](http://www.pokemonpets.com/Register)

&#10030; The game has all 721 Pokemon and almost all mega form at the moment with including shinies and other forms total number of Pokemon is 1560

&#10025; Each week there are events and new Pokémon being added to the game

&#9734; Game has 520 game maps and they are pretty good actually
