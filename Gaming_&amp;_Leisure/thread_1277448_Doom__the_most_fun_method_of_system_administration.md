This could potentially be in other categories as well, but as Doom is involved, I'm posting in G&L.

Not much of an explanation is necessary. It's normal Doom, but an enemy is spawned for each of the processes running in your system. If an enemy is injured, its nice level increases and thus runs slower. If it is injured many times with a weak weapon or hit with a strong weapon, it is kill -9'ed.
[Doom for SysAdmins](http://www.cs.unm.edu/~dlchao/flake/doom/)
