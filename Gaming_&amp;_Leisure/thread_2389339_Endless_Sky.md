### Post by galacticstone on 2018-04-15
Anyone else playing this addictive game? It has a nostalgic look to it that reminds me of Starflight and Star Control. 

It seems to have a sizeable following and is under active development, yet online information about the game elements is sparse. No good wikia (only a bare bones one) and just a few rudimentary and dated play guides available - the game has been updated more than once since many of the guides were written. A shame, because this game is really big/rich, and it could benefit from better documentation.

BTW, the version in the Ubuntu Software Center is grossly outdated - download the newest version via Synaptic.
