### Post by GerbilSoft on 2009-04-28
> **CharmyBee said:**
> Because Gens doesn't emulate 32X properly. The sound emulation in it is an ear killer too
Gens' 32X emulation does need improvement, but from my experience, it's good enough for Chaotix.

One thing you'll find in Gens/GS that isn't in regular Gens is increased PWM volume (meaning that drums are actually audible in Chaotix). This feature has been implemented since Milestone 5.

The upcoming Release 7 doesn't add any 32X-specific changes, but there's general improvements in other areas of the emulator, including the new MDP plugin system.
