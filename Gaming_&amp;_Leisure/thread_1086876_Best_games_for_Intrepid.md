### Post by oldrocker99 on 2009-03-05
:P Beating the drum again...

Neverwinter Nights Diamond Edition is available for $20 wherever PC games are sold. The Linux client for the game is right on the DVD, and can be completely installed by following this link:

[http://nwn.bioware.com/forums/viewtopic.html?topic=656261&forum=72](http://nwn.bioware.com/forums/viewtopic.html?topic=656261&forum=72)

It is THE most game in a box, it will run great on the system you describe, and the nephew in question should have a high old time playing the included modules. Neverwinter Vault has literally thousands of playable adventures for free download.

It is commercial, but BioWare should be applauded for supporting Linux; there aren't that many designers who will even try.
