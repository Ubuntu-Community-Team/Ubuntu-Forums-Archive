### Post by which_chick on 2008-03-18
Same Gnome scores for the number of balls that you remove.  

2 balls - 0 points
3 balls - 1 point
4 balls - 4 points
5 balls - 9 points
6 balls - 16 points
12 balls - 100 points.

I'd say that the points-per-move is "(# of balls - 2)^2"

Take the number of balls you remove.  Subtract two.  Square that number.  You get a LOT more points for removing more balls at one time.

This appears to be the case in the "small" and "medium" game sizes.  There is also (in small) a 1000-point bonus for completely clearing the board.

Hope that this helps.
