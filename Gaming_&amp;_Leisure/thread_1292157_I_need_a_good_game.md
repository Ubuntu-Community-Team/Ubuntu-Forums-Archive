I currently work at a "3D department" in a media company as a programmer. This department specializes in 3D art, similar to what you see in TV and in games. One of the artists there - a very talented one - is passionate about creating cars and other vehicles. His results beat most detailed 3D models you can find out there (with exception of course).

The problem is, it takes a lot of time to build one. I'm not sure how long it took to make his latest car, but he's working on an F16 for more than a month and is still far from finished. I assume he needs about the same time for a car.

Now lets assume he needs about two months for a single car. That means, if he is to work on this car for two months, he has to earn expenses for these two months. In Greece, from where i am, you need at least 1000 euros per month to have a decent life as a single (i think he's not, so he needs more but anyway). That is about 2000 euros.

In other words, his car costs at least 2000 euros to him to make.

A game contains much more than a car.
