### Post by FoolsGold on 2007-04-15
Enemy Territory is self-contained in a single directory, it doesn't stick bits and pieces of itself around the place.

Just delete the "enemy-territory" directory (or whatever you installed it to) and if you really want things clean, delete ".etwolf" in your home directory.

If you're getting PB violation messages, make sure you've updated your PunkBuster files - after installing ET and running it once to make a profile, download the pbsetup.run program and add ET to the list, then update:

[http://www.evenbalance.com/index.php?page=pbsetup.php](http://www.evenbalance.com/index.php?page=pbsetup.php)
