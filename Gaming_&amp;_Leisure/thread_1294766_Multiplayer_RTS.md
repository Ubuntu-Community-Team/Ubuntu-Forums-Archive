### Post by NertSkull on 2009-10-18
So I've looked at hundreds of posts trying to find something that will suit my needs, but thought I'd get the input of others.  I'm looking for a multiplayer game that I can play over the internet with my two younger siblings at home while I'm in college.  We are pretty close and I want to have a better way to keep in touch with them than trying to get awkward teenagers to talk on the phone.

I've tried a lot of the RTS games out there but with limited success.  I wasn't a fan of Globulation2 because of poor control over the units.  BosWars wouldn't work over the internet for multiplayer (I think having two computers connecting from the same IP like my siblings do at home causes issues). Although if I could get BosWars to work it would be about perfect for what I'm looking for.  

Glest may be okay, but I still would like something else.  I also tried Warzone 2100 which was fun, but a little to difficult and involved.  Although difficulty level wouldn't exclude other possibilities if the game was fun enough. 

I also tried freeorion but it was pretty complicated, and its turn-based so I suspect the multi-player would be really slow.  I need a game that can be played relatively quickly (1-2 hours) and be done with.  I'm a med student and don't have a ton of time to play long games with them.

I've tried a couple of other games like wormux or cultivation, but I'm looking more for something more RTS like.  I'd really like something in the space realm but I don't know what.  Something that was akin to freeorion but RTS I think would be fun.  Something like Boswars but set in space would be absolutely perfect.  Like I said, BosWars was fun, the internet multiplayer is just really really patchy with the four of us.

So if anyone has any opinions I'd really appreciate it.  I'd go commercial if they were great games, but I'd really want to go open-source type things.  Similarly I would prefer something that runs on Ubuntu just because I hardly ever boot anything else, but a windoze game only would work as well.  

Thanks everyone, sorry for the long post, but I'm excited to see what games I have missed.
