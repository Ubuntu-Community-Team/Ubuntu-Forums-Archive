### Post by meldroc on 2007-01-21
Since you mentioned you were into Infocom text adventures, you might want to install Frotz, jzip or xzip - they're interpreters of Z-Code, the story-file format for Infocom games.  If you can extract the z-code files from your atari disk images, or get the same files via ahem... other means, you can play them directly.
