### Post by Curlydave on 2005-06-25
Just a heads-up, it's possible to get Red Orchestra working in Linux if you have UT2k4. Aside from the expected shit performance, (I have an ATI card) lack of 3D options and lack of a working minimize, the only problems are some minor issues such as no detailed shadows, the rain looks red, the blur effect is messed up, and modeled scopes don't work. These are iirc features that are specifically coded for Direct3D, which is probably why they're not working. (it's no fault of Linux, Epic or the RO team.)

If you have UT2k4, here's another game that works on linux, and it's actually one of my two favorite games on any OS.
