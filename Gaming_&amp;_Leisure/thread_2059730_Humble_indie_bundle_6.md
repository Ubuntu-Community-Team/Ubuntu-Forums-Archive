I'm already installing the game, and it seems there's a bunch of known unresolved bugs:

> Known Bugs
----------------------
- Performance is slower than on Windows.
	Vessel on OpenGL currently supports a single-threaded engine only. The reason for this is because the multithreaded engine will attempt to make rendering calls from multiple threads... this does not work. Will be looking at workarounds for this issue in v1.1.
- The water meter should show blue water, but is always empty.
	Cause is currently unknown. However, you can measure the water content by listening for a "filling" sound, which increases in pitch as it gets closer to being full.
- Fluro eyes are googly/lazy.
	There is an unfixable flaw in the scripting engine that causes fluro eyeballs to be destroyed before the Fluro can actually use it. A workaround was made to support the eyeball existing, but it may get a bit googly in some cases.
- No custom cursors.
	I am showing the regular mouse cursor in-game; I don't know if I can make a nice replacement for the Win32 cursors in-game.
