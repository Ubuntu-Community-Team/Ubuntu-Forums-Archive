### Post by Quicky on 2007-01-29
Twonkymedia is a pretty good piece of software, although non-free. You can download a 30 day trial from [here]("http://www.twonkyvision.de/"). It&#8217;s pretty easy to set up (there&#8217;s a manual included in the archive, but essentially all you need to do is extract it and run it, then point it to where your media files are). 

Works perfectly streaming music, pictures and video, although only WMVs at present (MS DRM and so forth) to a 360. Not sure if it works with the original Xbox, but there&#8217;s no reason why it shouldn&#8217;t. In fact, you can probably stream more media formats to it than the 360.
