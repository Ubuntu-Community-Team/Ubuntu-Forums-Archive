### Post by CharmyBee on 2009-09-20
Nope, but there should be one by the year 2024.

The hard parts:
- Physics
- Tearing up the trees with ol' painless
- Voxel-based terrain engine (not rendered in voxels, but processed with it)
- Vehicles (go ahead, try to name one open source FPS with convincingly realistic vehicles that exists in a releasable form)

Easy parts:
- the run-of-the-mill story
- the 'creative imaginative' weaponry (JUST GUNS)
- the dumb AI
- the graphics engine (I bet writing glsl shaders for ambient occlusion faking and sea water is actually easier than doing the hard parts)

Idea:
Fork Rigs of Rods to do this as it has the physics and terrain stuff ready to go.
