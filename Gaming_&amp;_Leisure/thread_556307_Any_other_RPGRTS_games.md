Considering that Battle for Wesnoth is a completely Free Open Source game developped by a community of enthusiasts and that it is currently considered as one of the best games for GNU/Linux out there, I find your statement that "it sucks" quite insulting.
It is a turn based strategy game, so if you don't like that kind of game, it's understandable that you don't like it, but please don't just dismiss it as a "game that sucks".
