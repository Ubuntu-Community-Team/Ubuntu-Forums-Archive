### Post by nrayever on 2005-12-29
there's a way to really delete thjose files:

a) empty the recycle bin can while psp connected 
b) delete the .trash directory

this is perfectly normal, because, even with gnome or kde files are moved to the recycle bin. if you empty your recycle bin, files disappear. but with devices "as/or with" usb memory, files go to the recycle bin, but recycle bin is actually a folder called .trash, so delete .trash folder!!

i hope this works for you:razz:
