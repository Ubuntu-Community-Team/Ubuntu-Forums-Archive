VisualBoy Advance never worked well for me under Linux. 

I'd make a suggestion to try out the Mednafen emulator, which works really well. You can find it in the repositories, or by typing  "sudo apt-get install mednafen" into the terminal. 

Plus there is also a good front-end for Mednafen here too: 

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=813785&highlight=mednafen](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=813785&highlight=mednafen)
