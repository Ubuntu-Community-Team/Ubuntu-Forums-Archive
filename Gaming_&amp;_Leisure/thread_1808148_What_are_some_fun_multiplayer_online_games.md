### Post by Ograws on 2011-07-21
Urban Terror is my personal favorite

It's a free FPS game that is based on Quake III Arena (a modified game engine called ioquake3) and looks and feels like Counter-Strike but its much better in my opinion.

The graphics are not the best but they still do the trick :-)

As for ''hosting your own server'' yes you can host your own UrT server and choose the maps and the options and more.

Go to [http://www.urbanterror.info/](http://www.urbanterror.info/)

It's also avaible on Windows and Mac OS X
