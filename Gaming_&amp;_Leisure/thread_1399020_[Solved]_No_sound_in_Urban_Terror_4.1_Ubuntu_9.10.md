### Post by Yaspoon on 2010-02-05
I had the strange problem of no sound with urban Terror 4.1 with Ubuntu 9.10 to solve this problem I installed the libsdl1.2debian-alsa package this made me have 2 seconds of very glitchy sound the nothing so isntead i removed libsdl1.2debian-alsa and instead install libsdl1.2debian-pulseaudio this worked like a charm now i have perfect audio. Also trying using the gstreamer-properties utitlity to choose your sound device
