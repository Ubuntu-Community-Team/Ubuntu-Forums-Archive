### Post by Gerbil King on 2011-04-28
I was having this problem too. I used jscalibrator and jstest/jscal and both of them said everything was working properly, but whenever I tried to play Yo Frankie with a controller he'd just spin in a circle.

It turns out I was using the wrong mode on my controller. My controller has two modes and I was trying to play with it in in digital mode. When I switched it to analog before booting the computer the controller worked fine.

If I switch the mode to digital, though, I have to switch it back to analog and restart the computer again for it to work. Just restarting the game is not enough.

Hope this helps.
