### Post by arcdrag on 2009-07-03
I was just wondered if anyone else is as excited about this as I am.  Basically, from what I read, its a game server that streams directly to your browser, allowing you to play virtually any game on any OS without any installations if you have a high speed connection.  

Basically, rather than having the game on your pc, you're watching a streaming video of a game and sending your controls to the server.  Since your computer is doing no computations or anything, resource heavy games like crysis could run on antiquated machines with a high speed connection.

However, there's no mention of cost on the website [http://www.gaikai.com/]("http://www.gaikai.com/") so perhaps I'm getting too excited over something that will cost me a ton on getting a better internet connection and subscription fees to the service...but its at least a very innovative idea.
