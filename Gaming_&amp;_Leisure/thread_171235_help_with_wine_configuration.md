### Post by greenstar on 2006-05-06
Select the version of Windows you want to emulate, click the sound tab and let it autoconfigure, then click the drives tab and click the autodetect option about 1/2 way down. To the best of my memory, that's it. Just click a Windows binary (.exe) to run it in wine. 

Note: the names of the tabs might not be exact, this is all from memory. I install wine on many of the Ubuntu installations I do for other people. I use Dapper Testing personally & won't have wine installed until Dapper is stable. As a result I can't run winecfg and look at the names of the tabs.
