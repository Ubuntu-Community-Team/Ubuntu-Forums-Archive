### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-05-16
Has anyone played Voxelstein? It's a Wolfenstein 3D-like FPS for Windows that has completely destructible environments. It works perfectly on Wine.

It's pretty cool although I couldn't figure out how to place the bomb without it blowing up in my hands. As it's a Wolfenstein 3D-like game it has pictures of Hitler on the walls, but I found it hilarious that it also has pictures of George W Bush hanging on the walls :-)  (I'm not a detractor of Mr Bush but I thought it was funny).

You can download it from here:

[http://voxelstein3d.sourceforge.net/](http://voxelstein3d.sourceforge.net/)

I hope they do a Linux version, the game looks portable to me.
