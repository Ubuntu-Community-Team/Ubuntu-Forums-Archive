### Post by wadelewis4 on 2008-05-02
I just downloaded Phun, because I was bored and it looked interesting. I must say it is addicting! It a game/toy where you're given a set of tools to create a scene obeying laws of physics. You create the elements like, for instance, a stack of boxes with a ball suspended above them - then press play to watch how they interact. With planes, boxes, freehand brush, chains, springs, etc. you have infinite possibilities. It's good geeky fun. Thought I'd share for anyone who hasn't heard of it.
