### Post by Joh_ on 2005-11-06
I had the same problem with installing Guild Wars using cedega. It wouldn't let me switch CDs. So I grabbed a needle, found the little "eject forcing hole thingy" and pushed the needle in it to forcefully eject the tray. Then I replaced the disc, pushed the tray back in, and pressed the eject button on the player. Worked like a charm. :p
