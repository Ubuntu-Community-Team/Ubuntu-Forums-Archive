### Post by eragon100 on 2008-11-25
Urban Terror with team deathmatch!

Or savage 1 (but they would need to get into it first)

Or the battle for wesnoth, if you like fantasy. It's a turn based strategy game.

One of my favourites currently is nexuiz, another fps.

And then we have openarena and warsow. Warsow is also a fun FPS because it has animation-style graphics.

Thent there is aliean arena, a really good looking and fun game, and finally there is assaultcube, but that doesn't look so good and I would highly recommend you to choose urban terror over it if at all possible.
UT uses quake 3 engine, so it doesn't need much hardware.

All these games are 100% free, and, with the exception of savage 1, they are all open source, should you let that influence your decision. 
(I wouldn't) I recommend you urban terror, it's essentially a free counterstrike-like game with tons of game modes, and more than enough blood, tought it's not overly gory or anything. :wink:
