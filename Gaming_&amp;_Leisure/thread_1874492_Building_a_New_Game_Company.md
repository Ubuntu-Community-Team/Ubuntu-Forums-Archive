### Post by Galilei on 2012-06-25
Hello everyone!
 
Wanted to let everyone know about this game we’ve been working on (and still are) for Android, Linux, and iOS: introducing Flushed
 
[IMG]http://img411.imageshack.us/img411/7132/splashiu.png[/IMG]
By [jokkelpware](http://profile.imageshack.us/user/jokkelpware) at 2012-06-24
 
Google Play Link: [https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.lpware.flushed&feature=search_result#?t=W251bGwsMSwyLDEsImNvbS5scHdhcmUuZmx1c2hlZCJd](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.lpware.flushed&feature=search_result#?t=W251bGwsMSwyLDEsImNvbS5scHdhcmUuZmx1c2hlZCJd)
 
At its core, Flushed is a very simple game. I’d go as far as to say that anyone with fingers can play it.  The premise is pretty much this:
 
Charlie the Goldfish gets flushed down the toilet. Thinking he has reached the end of his short life, Charlie comes to discover that there’s much more beneath the surface than any of us could have known.
 
[IMG]http://img864.imageshack.us/img864/293/pic2cropped.png[/IMG]
By [jokkelpware](http://profile.imageshack.us/user/jokkelpware) at 2012-06-24
 
The Player takes on the task of rotating and placing pipes to create a path for Charlie, who’s speeding through the sewer system. The gameplay is thus very much based on ability to react to sudden changes with a light dose of puzzle elements. All the environments, which are unique in their theme and music, are filled with obstacles and items that are randomly generated, therefore offering a slightly different gameplay experience each play time.
 
[IMG]http://img690.imageshack.us/img690/5391/pic5hg.png[/IMG]
By [jokkelpware](http://profile.imageshack.us/user/jokkelpware) at 2012-06-24
 
 [IMG]http://img811.imageshack.us/img811/9514/pic3cropped.png[/IMG]
By [jokkelpware](http://profile.imageshack.us/user/jokkelpware) at 2012-06-24
 
[IMG]http://img37.imageshack.us/img37/4161/menuev.png[/IMG]
By [jokkelpware](http://profile.imageshack.us/user/jokkelpware) at 2012-06-24
 
The game gets incrementally harder, as you reach higher levels by collecting points and rescuing other fish that are trapped in the sewers. The question really becomes, then, how long can you hold out until things just get too crazy for Charlie to go on?
 
Here's a link to our Commercial
 
[http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VacvWvE-1ys](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VacvWvE-1ys)
 
Let's us know what you think.
 
the guys at Ligths and Perfections
