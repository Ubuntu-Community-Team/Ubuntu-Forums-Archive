### Post by xtiago on 2005-03-25
The problem it's GxMame version.
It can't read the last version of xmame list of roms.
Compile the latest version of gxmame, download the *0.35beta* version from here:

[http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=50621&package_id=44329&release_id=301071](http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=50621&package_id=44329&release_id=301071)

Now it works perfect for me! 8)
