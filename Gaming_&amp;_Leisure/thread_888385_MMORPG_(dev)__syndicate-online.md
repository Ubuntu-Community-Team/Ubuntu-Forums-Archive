### Post by MaximB on 2008-08-13
Sorry for not posting much gaming news lately, but I have a job now and don't have much time to seek for new games for GNU/Linux.
But from time to time I do find some interesting games...

Syndicate-online is a new MMORPG in development (like most of the games I post here) that uses the new unigine engine ([http://unigine.com](http://unigine.com)) that supports Windows and GNU/Linux.
One of the other game being made with this engine is Afterfall ([http://www.afterfall.pl/index/en/25](http://www.afterfall.pl/index/en/25)) which I posted earlier.

Syndicate-online is being made by a Russian company and their website is in Russian (but you can use the google translator for that).

If you're tired classic fantasy universe, similar to each other, and you want to plunge into the world total buystva high technology unusual interplay of realism and fantasy - the universe "Sindikat-Online" gives you a chance.

Game Universe combines elements of many distinct cyber-punk, science fiction, mysticism, who fancy intertwine, forming a variable and mysterious world.
Action games are deployed on the territory of megalopolis.
The development of information systems and the enormous potential of technology against the backdrop of global energy crisis leads to the formation of a huge megalopolis, which focused centers of civilization.

Metropolitan - is a kind of bulwark of civilization of mankind, which is fully automated life-support systems enable society to choose the path that it could move into a new quality of existence or die.

Metropolitan, as the game Wednesday, has many unique areas.
The huge city with the future infrastructure, unusual means of transport, luxurious shopping centers, luxury restaurants, night clubs and casinos constitutes art, technologically advanced city with elements of futurism and cyber-punk.

Communication systems are presented as outdated modes of transport - the port, railway station, subway, and high-tech modes of transport - terminal, WIG, teleportation system.
Along with the luxury of rich art quarters of large corporations, in the city thrives, poverty and hopelessness, violence and corruption, rampant greed and desire for power.
The place where the focus is truly enormous resources, becomes the scene of the struggle of two ideologies, each of which has its arguments are far from harmless.
You have to make a choice to speak on whose side in this conflict, and your choice may prove decisive in the weights distribution of power over the fate of millions of metropolis and its inhabitants.



"Sindikat-Online" - multiplayer online role-playing, where thousands of players live and communicate with each other in a unique game world in real time.
They factory new acquaintances, may join with each other in trade relations, war or simply to communicate.
Our users have access to unique world with Seamless gaming space, where they can influence the further development of storyline.
The game is geared to a wide audience, but above all it will be interesting lovers bright and dynamic PvP regime.

Key features of the game:

       Huge, technologically advanced city of the future, consisting of a set of unique areas that harmoniously combines elements of the modern world and cyber-punk.

The basis of gameplay the game.
Major PVP (players against players) fights: the players association will be able to capture and control the strategically important areas and sites of the city thereby influencing the placement of opposing forces, and further development of the world game.

The unique economic system

Innovative technological capabilities of the client platform based on next-gen engine Uningine
In combination with thoughtful design game players will get a number of exciting and comfortable in using gaming services.

Absolutely free igra.Klientskaya part of the game is free, and pay for playing time does not need!

The nearest plans of developers - to ensure maximum stability client and server, the development of a number of interesting and convenient gaming services, as well as the gaming community support and active interaction with him. "
