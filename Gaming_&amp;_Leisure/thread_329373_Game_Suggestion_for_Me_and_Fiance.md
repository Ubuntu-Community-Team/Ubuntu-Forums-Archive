You could try ADOM. It takes a while to get into, but it's really fun. It uses a text-only interface, but it's complexity, difficulty and enjoyment is paralleled by few games I have played. It's also free and runs natively on Linux - no compilation required. You can grab it here: [www.adom.de](www.adom.de)
