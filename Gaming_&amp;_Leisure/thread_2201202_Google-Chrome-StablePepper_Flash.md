### Post by jack39 on 2014-01-25
Hi, I've had this problem too while playing flash games. Though I am not an expert I found a way that works.
Download the macromed flash player thingy: [http://www.adobe.com/support/flashplayer/downloads.html#fp11.9](http://www.adobe.com/support/flashplayer/downloads.html#fp11.9)
go to: chrome://plugins
click the "+" on details
find adobe flash player
disable pepflash.dll and leave macromed alone
this should work
