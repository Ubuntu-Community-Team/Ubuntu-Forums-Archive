### Post by Mike'sHardLinux on 2006-12-22
You will most likely learn more from doing it that way, which is a good thing, but if you want to do it the easy way, and have a spare $15-$25, get a small 4 port switch and an extra cable, and cascade it off of one of your ports on your router. Then you'll have extra ports and there will be no special configuration needed.
