### Post by mr-daniel-lemke on 2016-03-24
It may be capable, but only on the lowest settings. I am very familiar with using Intel Graphics 3000, and I had poor performance on any game newer than about 2006 unless it was 2D. MikeCyber makes a good point about getting the latest drivers, but even then it's not going to be a speed demon. 

If you are playing a game that is performing well and then has lag spikes after playing for a minute, you might also look into playing with a cooling pad under your laptop. Overheating can cause lag spikes too.
