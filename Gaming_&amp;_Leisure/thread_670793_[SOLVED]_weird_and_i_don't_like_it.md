### Post by Vadi on 2008-01-19
Try selecting "Choose driver by name" and select "radeon - ATI Radeon cards, including Radeon Mobility and FireGL". That's what I use on my 7500.

Edit: Oh and get Compiz-Switch. It's this button thing that you just click and it toggles desktop effects on/off. I disable them before playing games.
