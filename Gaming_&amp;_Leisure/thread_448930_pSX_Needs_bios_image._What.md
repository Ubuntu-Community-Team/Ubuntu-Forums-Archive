### Post by hikaricore on 2007-05-19
Playstation one bios images exist in serveral versions named: scph****.bin

The **** is replaced with the version number of the bios and depends on which version of the ps1 it was ripped from and what area of the world that machine was produced for.
Please do not ask for bios images (or roms in any form) on this forum, they are easily found with a simple search but are generally considered not legal.
