### Post by PurplePenguin on 2007-02-23
Here's a good [link]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Games") for you to start off.

Some of the games you mention are playable with Ubuntu / Linux, such as Doom 3, Unreal Tournament, World of Warcraft, Counterstrike, lots of older games like the original Doom, and others that you might not have tried (Nexuiz and Warsow will blow your mind).  Wolfenstein: Enemy Territory is also pretty fun, as is America's Army.  

Some games can be played natively, others need wine or cedega to run.
