### Post by Perfect Storm on 2005-10-07
A little review of a game I tried

**_THE LOST LABYRINTH_**
*[size=1]A review by Artificial Intelligence*[/size]


For those who like Rogue like games with Zelda graphic *the Lost Labyrinth* is a must. Play as single player or up to 3 on hotseat helping eachother through the ever changing labyrinth your hero(s) need to eat and drink, buy weapons, armor, learn spells or find them. Dangerous monsters will appear while you searching for treasures, healing wells will help on your way to fortune. Not to mention class selection ability skills when you build your character(s). Though you don't get experience by killing monsters you get skill points to spend on your character(s) everytime you make further into the labyrinth.

It's quite fun game to play especially with diffrent class options. To name a few you can play as a stealth character, a ranger, necromancer (my favorite ^^), knight, mage etc.
Though one thing I find annoying is the way to control your character(s). It takes a bit time to learn the hot-keys which I prefer in Lost Labyrinth because the mouse control is a pain in the butt. The graphic is nice, the sound could be better in my opinion, no music which some people will praise other curse. _BUT_ it's fun to play and not a single of the times I tried Lost Labyrinth was the same, and with alot of options to build the character(s) is a big plus!

1=Poor  7=Excellent
-------------------------------
