### Post by jualin on 2008-10-15
Wine [claims that it works]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=6517") on linux, however there are known problems with this game. Basically if you want to play games in your computer Linux is not the best operating system since not that many games are supported. IMHO I wouldn't get a gaming laptop for Ubuntu. I would still buy the laptop but I would dual-boot XP to play games on it and Ubuntu for the rest. Hope this helps!
