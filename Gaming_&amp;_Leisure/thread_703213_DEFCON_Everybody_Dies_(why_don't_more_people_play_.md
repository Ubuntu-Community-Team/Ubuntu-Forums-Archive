### Post by darkstar8 on 2008-02-21
This is actually a really good, pretty original real-time strategy game. 
And Introversion seems to be a small company that supports linux and could use the money!
Aside from id Software this company seems to be one of the only ones out there that will release a native linux client for their commercial games (all three of them).

No, it's not open-source, and no it probably never will be, but it's a tiny company trying to increase their user-base (and admittedly, to a greater extent, their bottom line).
There may be better RTS games out there, but they're all commercial and with most, if not all of them you have to run them through wine.

So why not give the little guys a chance? At least download the demo and give it a try.
