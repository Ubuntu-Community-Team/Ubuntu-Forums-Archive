### Post by Peter_Solver on 2014-05-17
Are you using virtual desktop enabled in wine.config? If so, try disabling the virtual desktop. If the game automatically asks for the resolution, it gets its correct resolution. Have you tried changing your resolution to a higher one? If your game resolution is 800 x 600, then your window will change its resolution to 800 x 600.
