### Post by MunkyJunky on 2011-06-02
If you need to open the ports on Ubuntu, and not your router, you could try Uncomplicated Firewall ([https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UFW](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UFW)). If you want something with a GUI, try searching for 'Firewall Configuration' in the software centre. That should allow you to open the necessary ports.
