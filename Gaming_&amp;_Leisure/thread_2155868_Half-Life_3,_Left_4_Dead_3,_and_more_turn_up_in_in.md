They will probably continue Linux Support. It might be niche and small, but it has a dedicated and loyal community. Although, I imagine it's mostly a bi-product of their gaming console pursuits. The fact that Linux is open-source and already has a ton of programming resources and community helps make it easier for developers to pursue it for consoles.
