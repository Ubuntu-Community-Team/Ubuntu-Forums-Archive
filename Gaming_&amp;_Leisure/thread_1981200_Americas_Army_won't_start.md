### Post by regor210 on 2012-05-16
[http://icculus.org/lgfaq/#dotarmy](http://icculus.org/lgfaq/#dotarmy)

Q: Where does America's Army store its configuration files?
			
				A: ~/.armyops230/

I would look in your home folder for.armyops230

You will need to look under View and select show hidden to see folders with a . in the front of there names.

Or search for files with armyops in there name.

Once you find it rename it and try restarting America's Army.
