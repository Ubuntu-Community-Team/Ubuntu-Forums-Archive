### Post by hikaricore on 2010-06-28
I'd say the only limits are bandwidth and the refresh rate of your devices.
To be reasonable however it'd be almost impossible to play 3d games this way, but you could probably play solitare or something of that nature.
Movies you could watch but probably not hear unless you had a very specific setup and a remote client that supported this.
You couldn't really do anything with your files like copying them to a moblie device unless again your remote software provided such a function.
