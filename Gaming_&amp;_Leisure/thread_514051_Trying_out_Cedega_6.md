### Post by DoktorSeven on 2007-08-01
Basically it's that plain Wine has gotten good enough to make Cedega almost irrelevant.  A few years ago, WineX/Cedega was the only thing that could play many new games; now Wine plays them just as well or better.

If you have to have a certain game that only Cedega supports, and supports well, it is worth getting, but generally, sticking with plain Wine is about your best bet.
