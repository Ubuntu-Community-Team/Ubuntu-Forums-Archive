### Post by MarblePanther on 2008-12-23
Do not run compiz and that game together on that chipset...period

It simply cannot handle it. I have two computers with that intel chipset--trust me.  In fact I recommend you not use compiz at all with it as it causes other problems with video playback and resource hogging.

Netbooks weren't designed for gaming, so you have to squeeze all the resources you can out of it.

I would recommend turning off compiz and logging in to an alternative window-manager like openbox when you want to game.  Also try setting your cpu frequency to performance before gaming.

Oh, and another game you might like is Urban Terror:  [http://www.urbanterror.net/news.php](http://www.urbanterror.net/news.php)
