### Post by BigSilly on 2008-02-12
> **Cresho said:**
> tempest 2000 aka typhoon 2001

[http://typhoon.kuto.de/index.html](http://typhoon.kuto.de/index.html)

download for linux, right click on typhoon and allow executable.  double click on it and it just runs fine.  after closing, config file will be created.

+1 here for Typhoon 2001. It is absolutely fantastic. Retro yet modern, fun, frantic and really well judged. It's my absolute fave Linux game, and it's completely free.

If you do download it, you may need to give the Typhoon binary permission to execute as a program. Otherwise you'll be double-clicking and getting nowhere. To do this, right click on the typhoon launcher (you'll most likely see it as a diamond shape once you extract the zipped file), select Permissions, and tick the box that says "allow executing as a program". Then you're away.

Download it now! :)
