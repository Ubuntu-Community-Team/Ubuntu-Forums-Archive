### Post by hikaricore on 2007-04-22
On another yet equally important note.

Make sure you have a video card with OpenGL support that's up to the task.

WoW CAN be made to work on Integrated Intel chipsets as well as ATI cards, but due to driver issues
(which are the fault of your manufacturers and not the linux community btw) this is sometimes an uphill
battle.  Your best bet is something in the the Nvidia GeForce series 6000 or higher for an AGP of PCI E
slot.  I wouldn't suggest a standard PCI slot unless you have a fast FSB.
