### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-03-31
The truthful answer is **no**.

But sometimes we get lucky and we can get games to work with Wine, Cedega, or Crossover.

But the honest answer is never just expect *any* Windows program to work in Linux.  That's like expecting Mac programs to work in Windows.  But we get lucky sometimes.
