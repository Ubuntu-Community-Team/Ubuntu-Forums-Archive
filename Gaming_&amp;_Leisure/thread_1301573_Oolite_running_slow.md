### Post by Fell on 2009-10-26
Hi :)

I guess you are wanting to go the upgrade path rather than fresh install so this may not be what you want to hear about the intel drivers.

My laptop uses the intel drivers and I never noticed any improvement in graphical performance between Hardy and Jaunty. However, Karmic does have new intel video drivers which *may* solve your performance issues

"New Intel video drivers: The Intel video drivers are moving away from EXA to UXA which will solve major performance issues seen in 9.04 for Intel graphics."
