### Post by zwygart on 2008-11-04
Hello,

Personally i don't very like games with violence. Here is a list of games with a short description off games that interested me. (Mostly races).
They are some games by default on ubuntu, which may interest you.

I installed :

armagetron : 3D moto races (The rules are simple: you ride a light cycle (a kind of motorbike that can only turn 90 degrees at a time, leaves a wall behind and cannot be stopped) and have to avoid running into walls while at the same time you have to try to get your opponent to run into them.)

frozen-bubble : Frozen-Bubble is a clone of the popular "Puzzle Bobble" game, in which you attempt to shoot bubbles into groups of the same color to cause them to pop. It features 100 single-player levels, a two-player mode, music and striking graphics.

neverball : you guide a rolling ball through dangerous territory
           also in this neverputt : 3D golf

planetpenguin-racer : The goal of the game is to slide down a snow- and ice-covered mountain as quickly as possible, avoiding the trees and rocks that will slow you down.

pyracer : It features multiplayer (on the same computer), tournaments, high scores, replays, 6 tracks (really 12, because each track can be used Normal or Reverse), 9 cars, a basic robot player, 3 different levels of difficulty.

slune : Slune is a 3D game. It includes racing, car-crashing, and lots of action. In addition, a multiplayer mode is available.

xmoto : X-Moto is a challenging 2D motocross platform game, where physics play an all important role in the gameplay. You need to control your bike to its limit, if you want to have a chance finishing the more difficult of
the challenges.

torcs : TORCS, The Open Racing Car Simulator, is a car racing simulation, which allows you to drive in races against opponents simulated by the computer.

trigger : Trigger is a free 3D rally car racing game. Fun for all the family! You race a sequence of 6 courses, with increasing levels of difficulty.

Probably you will find more for your interests if you go on synaptic. It is this way that i found the games.
