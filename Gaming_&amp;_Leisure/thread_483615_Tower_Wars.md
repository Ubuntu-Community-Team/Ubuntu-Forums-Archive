This is just a quick profile for a game thought. How many people here have heard of warcraft III tower wars? If you havn't here's a brief overview. 

The objective in the game can either be as a team you try to defend against waves upon waves of little men walking towards a certain point on a map, or go head to head and for each unit you kill you gain money to buy more troops to send to the other teams side. What do you defend with you ask? Towers you build and upgrade towers to defend against these little minions.
