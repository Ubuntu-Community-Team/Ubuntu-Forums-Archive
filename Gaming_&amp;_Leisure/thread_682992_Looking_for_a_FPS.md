### Post by mivo on 2008-01-30
There is a How-to for Enemy Territory: Quake Wars available [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=583762&highlight=howto+etqw").  This will work flawlessly with the retail version and up until the current patch 1.4 Very cool game, and in 1.4 it even supports thread rendering on Linux! 32 bit, but works fine in 64 bit Ubuntu as well.

There is also a number of free shooters available. My favourite here is [Nexuiz]("http://www.alientrap.org/nexuiz/"), which you can find in the repository. It's very accessible and actually quite good looking. :) Available for both 32 and 64 bit.
