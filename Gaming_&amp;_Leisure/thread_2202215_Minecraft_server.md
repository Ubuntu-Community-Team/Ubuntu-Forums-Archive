### Post by efflandt on 2014-01-30
For some ideas for plugins you might look at [http://sdf.org/?minecraft](http://sdf.org/?minecraft)

**MultiVerse** - Multiple worlds, for different capabilites or as new biomes are added which may be difficult to find in older widely explored worlds. For example they have some worlds where users can switch between creative or survival with /c or /s, or other worlds with different personal inventory that are survival only, or in one case survival only, hard mode (cannot take anything there from an easier world).
**DeadBolt** - To protect chests or doors with signs.
**Residence** - To self set areas protected from griefing, creeper or tnt explosions, lava flow, etc. Even if you give other players permission to use doors and chests you can protect certain ones with DeadBolt. You can tp to or between your residences. Might need a way to prevent players from claiming large areas near spawn.
**MyWarp** - For players to add warps, even between worlds, or using right clickable signs. That and Residence tp are especially helpful if you get lost or trapped.
**Motd** - /rtp <user>, to be able to tp to another player (with their approval)

**SDFEconomy** - A form of money that seems to be tied into the rest of this group
**ecoCreature** - Killing hostile mobs often gives you money, along with a chance of extra drops
**Scavenger** - Charges you a fee from your econ balance (possibly to negative balance if you have none) to restore personal inventory upon death. Maybe makes it too easy to take chances, but also saves your stuff if someone else kills you.
**ChestShop** - Unattended trading

**TimeAndWeatherControl** - Call a vote to change day/night or weather
**Dynmap** - Web map to show other players, Residences, possible interesting areas, or figure out where you are yourself.
**Whitelist** - Not sure how you would handle this. On SDF people whitelist themselves or friends (by MC name) from their *nix shell account. Maybe there would be a way to configure the whitelist using the same login method used for ubuntuforms.org.
