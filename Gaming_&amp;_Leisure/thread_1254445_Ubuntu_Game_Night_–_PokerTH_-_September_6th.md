Greetings UGN types again,

On **[SIZE="3"]Sunday September 6th @ 1800 GMT[/SIZE]** ([http://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/converter.html](http://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/converter.html)) UGN will be playing [COLOR="Red"]**PokerTH**[/COLOR]. The event will go on until people stop playing. Break out your poker face, and get your hands ready.

> PokerTH is an open source Texas hold 'em simulator . The game is faithful to the Texas Hold 'em rules and betting system. PokerTH allows for up to ten human players, with computer-controlled players filling in if there are not enough humans. Players can also play against other PokerTH users online.

For more information about past UGN events please visit [http://gwos.org/doku.php/event:start](http://gwos.org/doku.php/event:start). 

The game information is listed [here.]("http://gwos.org/doku.php/games:alphabetical:p:pokerth")

Members of the UGN team will be hosting regular tables on the PokerTH network. 

Show your support for UGN by joining the IRC chat channel [SIZE="3"]#UGN[/SIZE] on irc.freenode.net. If you don't have an IRC client try [http://java.freenode.net//index.php?channel=UGN](http://java.freenode.net//index.php?channel=UGN).

See you Sunday.
