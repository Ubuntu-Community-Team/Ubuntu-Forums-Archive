### Post by brinked on 2012-02-08
A game that really fits what you are talking about would be SPARK.

This is a 2D top down online multiplayer capture the flag based shooter.  It has been around for over 15 years and used to be called ARC.  It was created by the owners of popcap and it was one of their first games they ever made.

SPARK has been much improved on and is actually a better game today than it was back then.  The community is strong but since the game was dropped by the gaming networks that died (TEN, WON, SIERRA) it was taken over by the community, they just would not let it die.

If you are looking for a top down shooter, I highly recommend you give it a try, it is extremely fun and easy to pick up but very hard to master.
