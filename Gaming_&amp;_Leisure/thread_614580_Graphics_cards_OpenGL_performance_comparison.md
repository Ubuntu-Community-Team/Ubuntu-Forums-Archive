### Post by eye208 on 2007-11-16
Linden Lab, maker of Second Life, recently [published](http://blog.secondlife.com/2007/11/15/typical-frame-rate-performance-by-graphics-cardgpu/) a comparison chart of graphics cards/GPUs and their FPS (frames per second) performance with the Second Life rendering engine. The data has been collected automatically from SL users in-world. While Second Life may or may not be your cup of tea, this comparison is interesting anyway because the SL rendering engine is OpenGL-based and multi-platform (Linux, Mac, Windows).
