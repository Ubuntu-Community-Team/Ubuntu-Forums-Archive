Stallman is against anything where undue power is given from the consumer to a corporation. He believes that consumers should have full freedom to use, play, alter and develop what they like. Hence him creating the foundation of open source (free) software where the philosophy is anyone can change it, share it, use it and be free of any restrictions.

With Sony and PlayStation, a single hacker tried to take a piece of hardware that he owned and change it in the way he wanted, then [this happened]("http://www.theinquirer.net/inquirer/news/1940372/playstation-hacker-restrained-sony").

I hope this explains it.
