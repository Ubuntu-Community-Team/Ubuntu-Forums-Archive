### Post by xtang on 2008-05-24
nothing is supposed to come up in the "selectable sound devices".

Firstly, can you make any recording at all? If you are using gnome then open the terminal and type in "gnome-volume-control", edit->preferences, and make the digital, and capture tracks visible (very important). Now look at the recording tab and bump up the capture and digital volume sliders.

Test your microphone with Sound Recorder. Experiment with the values for each track.

Admittedly, i use the old fashioned audio jack so the fact that your headphone is usb will be an additional complication.
