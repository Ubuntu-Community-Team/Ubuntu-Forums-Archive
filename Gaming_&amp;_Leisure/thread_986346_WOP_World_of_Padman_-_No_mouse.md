### Post by Bright_View on 2009-01-02
Okay, I think I've solved it, but I haven't had time to play to really test it out since the fix.  The problem was Compiz interference, and the solution was to simply un-check one of the options in Advanced Desktop Effects Settings (under System > Preferences).  

Go to the General options, and un-check the Unredirect Fullscreen Windows option.  

Can you guys confirm that this works for you too?  If so, I look forward to being blasted to bits by both of you (not much of a gamer).
