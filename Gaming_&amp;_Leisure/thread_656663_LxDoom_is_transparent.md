### Post by knattlhuber on 2008-02-04
To play LxDoom, you'll need a .wad game file. You can get it either from an old copy of the game that you may still have kicking around, or download the "freedoom" or "doom-wad-shareware" package from the Ubuntu repository. The .wad files need to reside in the /usr/share/games folder.
Oh, and switch compiz off before you start LxDoom.
