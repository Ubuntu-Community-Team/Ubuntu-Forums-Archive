### Post by R33D3M33R on 2011-06-05
You don't need many ventilators for this configuration. It will be enough if you put a fan in the front part of the case (if the slot is there) so it will blow the cold air in. Then at the back of the case put a fan under the PSU so it can blow the hot air out. That should be enough. It's impossible to keep all the dust out. You could buy a filter and put it over the openings, but the side effect might be overheating.
