You guys are all invited to the beta test for our new browser-based game, GOODBYEROBOTARMY. We previously had a test with members of the forum, and it went swell.

I, one of two developers on the project, did most of my development in ubuntu. The server runs debian.
Helping us beta test this game would be great!

The game is centered on a town of school children that build robot suits out of cardboard and fight with waterguns. The goal is to make a team or join a team and then fight over cardboard box factories for control of the map. Owning more factories gives your team more points. The team with the most points at the end of one week wins.

It plays in firefox version 2 or 3. Recommended resolution is 1024x768 +.

Log into the game with any username / password combination:

[http://game.goodbyerobotarmy.com/about.html](http://game.goodbyerobotarmy.com/about.html)

If you want to report a bug, just post it here!
