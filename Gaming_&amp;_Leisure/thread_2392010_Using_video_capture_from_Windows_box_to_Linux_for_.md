### Post by QIII on 2018-05-15
If it were possible, you wouldn't get the "feel of Linux", but a stream of electrons from Windows.

Besides that, by the process of capture and processing for however you planned to render it on your Linux monitor, you'd be adding enough latency to drive a truck through -- possibly rendering the game unplayable.
