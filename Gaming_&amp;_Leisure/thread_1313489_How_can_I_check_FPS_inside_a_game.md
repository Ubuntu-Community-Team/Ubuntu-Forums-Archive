### Post by synicalx on 2009-11-03
It depends on the game, generally it can be done by a games console (hitting ~ and typing a specific command) but some games have an option to display it constantly in the Options menu.

Quite easy to do for most games, a Google search should tell you what to type into the game's console to display your FPS ;)
