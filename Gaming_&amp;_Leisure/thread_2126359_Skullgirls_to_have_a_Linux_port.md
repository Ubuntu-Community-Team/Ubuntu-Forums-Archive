### Post by x0pwsk on 2013-03-16
A bit of interesting Linux gaming news:
[URL="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skullgirls"]
Skullgirls[/URL] was just recently confirmed to have a [Linux port.]("http://www.joystiq.com/2013/03/15/skullgirls-pencils-in-linux-port-squigly-to-be-voiced-by-lauren/") The game will also be released through Steam.

As it stands, Skullgirls will be the only AAA fighting game to be released natively on a Linux distro.

You can [pre-purchase the game]("http://www.indiegogo.com/projects/keep-skullgirls-growing/x/604755?c=activity") and receive the access to the beta for $30. This also serves to help add new content into the game before it releases on PC and Linux in the summer.
