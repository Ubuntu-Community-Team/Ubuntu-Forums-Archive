### Post by Carpentr on 2011-04-02
> **galacticaboy said:**
> 
NVIDIA GeForce 6150se NForce 430 Graphics, and AMD Athalon 2 x2 250u Processor.

These two pieces of hardware are probably going to need upgrading. That processor runs at 1.6 ghz on each core, I believe. That's pretty slow, and a much better processor could probably be found online for a relatively low amount of money. The integrated graphics need to be upgraded which, also, won't cost a large amount of money. You can get a fairly decent video card for $75 to $150 nowadays. 

If your friend does decide to upgrade his computer's hardware, he should make sure that his motherboard supports the new processor, and that it has the available PCI Express slot that most newer video cards require. The computer's power supply also needs to be sufficient(those requirements can be found on the piece of hardware's box, usually).

Good luck!
