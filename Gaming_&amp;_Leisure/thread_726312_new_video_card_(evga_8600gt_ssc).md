### Post by jacob01 on 2008-03-16
I just got my new video card (evga geforce 8600gt ssc) last Wednesday and i am so impressed with the performance and how easy it was to install. I just poped it in and rebooted and ran the nvidia proprietary .run driver and i was up and running.

i ran some tests and in glx gears  with the stock core clock (684mhz) and (810mhz) mem i was getting around 1900 fps in full screen, but after i enabled cool bits and overclocked it to (714mhz) core and (880) mem i got 2000fps. The stock 8600gt is clocked at 540 mhz :lolflag: so this is oc'ed alot

In counter strike source i am getting about 60 fps all on high setting, i know thats not much for this card but it is more than playable and its running thought wine.

 I just downloaded the quake wars demo and wow it ran beautifully other than a little grainy around the leaves on the trees. i unlocked the fps which is caped at 30 fps and in screen shot i recorded fps ranging between 40 and 200 but mostly hanging around 100fps 

I am vary impressed with this card and for the price it is more than worth it.
