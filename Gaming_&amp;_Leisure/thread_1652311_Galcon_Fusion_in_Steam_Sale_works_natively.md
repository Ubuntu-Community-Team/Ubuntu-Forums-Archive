Seems Super Laser Racer [works somewhat in wine]("http://www.codeweavers.com/compatibility/browse/name/?forum=1;app_id=7304;msg=77061"). However thinking about wine is a non-issue as even without the other games Galcon Fusion is 50% off at the price of the whole pack.
