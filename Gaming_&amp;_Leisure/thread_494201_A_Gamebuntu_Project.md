### Post by cogadh on 2007-07-06
This has been discussed a number of times and there are many reasons why it won't happen. I won't go into all of them but the biggest issue is the licensing and copyrights. Many games and drivers have restrictive copyrights that prevent them from being included in any distribution by default. That's why you have to manually install the Nvidia or ATI drivers on your system.

There is an Ubuntu gamers edition in the works based on Ubuntu Ultimate, but I don't think it will have extras added to it, just the unnecessary stuff removed.
