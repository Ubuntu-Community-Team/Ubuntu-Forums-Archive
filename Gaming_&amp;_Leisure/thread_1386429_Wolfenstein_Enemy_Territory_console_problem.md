[http://www.linuxquestions.org/questions/linux-games-33/enemy-territory-console-locks-up-in-linux-161959/](http://www.linuxquestions.org/questions/linux-games-33/enemy-territory-console-locks-up-in-linux-161959/)

seta r_fullscreen "0"

in my config and change it to:

seta r_fullscreen "1")
