### Post by buntunub on 2008-01-10
Unfortunately, you will not find any "free" games that can rival what is on the market today in terms of functionality, playability, and visual effects. However, there are one or two that had the code open sourced for one reason or another, but they are but shells of what they are based upon now, if even still actively developed. Its like taking a step back into the last decades greatest hits, or even back further then that. There is hope though that true development will occur with some of these to make them truly great or at least on par with current gaming technology. America's Army is one project which is free with OK graphics and gameplay, but I do not think development is possible on it as im not sure its truly open source. EVE Online offers a free Linux Client (TG Wrapper), but has a monthly fee after the 2 week free trial. Beyond that, good luck!
