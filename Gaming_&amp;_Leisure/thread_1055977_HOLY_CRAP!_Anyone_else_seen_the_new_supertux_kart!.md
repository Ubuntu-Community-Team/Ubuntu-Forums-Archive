### Post by xakh on 2009-01-31
Sorry, you have to check out the new SuperTuxKart, released on the 22nd. I started playing it yesterday. A few things. It's crazy hard compared to the last version, the weapons are pretty much all new, and there's no more wheelies. The new version replaced coins with Nitrous oxide cannisters, so that you charge up a spare booster, but you don't get extra power ups based on how many you've collected. The interface is totally beefed up, and much faster. It's a pretty fantastic improvement, all in all. I suggest you go get it from sourceforge soon.
