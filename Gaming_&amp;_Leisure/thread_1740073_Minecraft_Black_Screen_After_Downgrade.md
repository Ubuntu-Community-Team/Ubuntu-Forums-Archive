### Post by donkyhotay on 2011-04-26
Copy your world/settings files into a safe place then delete the originals and try again. The latest version has many world changes and if the world/settings are for 1.5 (they'd convert automatically when the program launches) they won't work on version 1.4 and could easily explain the crash. If that doesn't work try launching from CLI (terminal) and post the output here for further investigation.
