### Post by raydeen on 2009-02-23
Crossfire might be fun for you. It's a 2D MMO that looks a bit like Ultima 6 graphics wise. You can even run your own server if you want to. Look for it in the repos or at the official page:

[http://crossfire.real-time.com/](http://crossfire.real-time.com/)

You'll probably have to launch it from the terminal by typing 
```

cfclient

```

After that, the GUI will launch and show a list of servers to connect to.
