I thought I'd point out an interesting new space mmorpg in the making, [Celetania]("http://celetania.com/").

It's been known that they'll be cross-platform for a while (they've been sending announcements to [linuxgames.com]("http://www.linuxgames.com/")), but today they've released some technical details about their game, which include:

> One of the other ambitions Vulcando Games has, is to break the barriers most of the games on the market have and allow Celetania to be run cross platform on many different operating systems (OS) – just like it’s natural to expect it to be in different languages. Things such as surround sound with hardware support or utilization of multi-core processors goes without saying.

Multi-core support = sweet :)
