### Post by FranMichaels on 2008-07-31
> **clarkec321 said:**
> Thanks for the reply, I'll stick to my Gens until then... I wanted a MasterSystem emulator so I could play Y's The Vanished Omens I've got fond memories of wasting hours on it

Just install [mednafen]("http://mednafen.sourceforge.net/") from synaptic. Right click on your rom, choose Open with, select mednafen. 

Press F1 to see the key combos to configure controls etc. Simple as that.

P.S. I know the homepage doesn't advertise it, but the emulator does support sms/gg. :)

### Post by BigSilly on 2008-08-01
+1 for Mednafen. It's in the repo, so just crack open Synaptic and install there. There's an excellent front end available too [right here.]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=813785&highlight=mednafen") You can download a .deb file so it's easy to install and use. For MAME I recommend SDLMame as disturbedite does above. It's definitely the very best option on Linux for MAME, and is just the same as the Windows counterpart. I don't even have a front end for it to be honest, since it comes with it's own that's good enough for me.
