### Post by WarholsGhost on 2007-09-10
I have played both games and i think over all i prefer Enemy Territory to Tremulous but i just wanted to post some pros and cons about both games. Please vote on your favorite and post comments. 

Enemy Territory
Pros:
Great Dynamic Levels (and lots of them)
Less Talking, More Shooting

Cons:
Long download times for new levels (up to 2 minutes)
Punkbuster Problems

Tremulous
Pros:
Easy game play
Runs faster than ET

Cons:
Clans are a large part
Banning is often abused

### Post by Darkness3477 on 2007-09-27
Whilst I really do enjoy playing Tremulous as I can run it on my Laptop (256 ram, cruddy generic GPU) I find the hole Alien Vs. Human thing a bit boring. 

When playing games I either want to escape reality altogether and be fully immersed in another universe or have a highly realistic game. Urban Terror, which I started playing the day before last offers that and more. 

It runs quite nicely on this computer, although I only get around 15-35fps which is a little cruddy compared to Tremulous, but it's got a lot of players, and at least two Australian Servers (melbourne based, which is close to where I live) that are nearly full (and sometimes full!). 

That, and I used to play Tremulous all the time. It was pretty much my first Open Source game I ever got into and one of the first FPS's I ever got into. 

So, if there wasn't the 'other' vote I would have chosen Tremulous, as ET is a little strange for me.
