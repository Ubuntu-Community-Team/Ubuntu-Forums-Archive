### Post by kiddykoff on 2011-09-28
New Humble Bundle!

[http://www.humblebundle.com/](http://www.humblebundle.com/)

**[SIZE="4"]Update below[/SIZE]**

Well, not entirely new. 5 of these games are from a previous bundle (if you've been keeping up) The games are:

Trine
Shadowgrounds: Survivor
Shadowgrounds
Jack Claw (not a complete game, but the source code is available for download)
Splot (a pre-order for a game, not yet complete)
*and the new game is*
Frozen Synapse

Here's how the deal works for those who haven't bought the humble bundles before. 
You pay what you want through amazon, paypal, or google checkout. 
The games are cross compatible through Mac, Windows, and Linux. 
You can download them through a bit torrent client or a direct download.
You can also elect to have them downloaded through steam, desura, onlive, and direct2drive.
The games are DRM free.

Here's some new terms. If you pay above the average price paid by others then you get all the games. Otherwise you only get Frozen Synapse. Not bad for those who already had the previous bundle. I chipped in 10 dollars, but the average was 3.87.

**[SIZE="4"]Update:[/SIZE]** The Indie bundles are now known for adding things in the middle of the event to keep things fresh and they've done it again. Another game has been added, [Trauma]("http://www.traumagame.com/").

This game is about recollecting forgotten memories through photographs. It's a pretty game and according to my girlfriend. she wants it. I guess i'm buying another bundle.
