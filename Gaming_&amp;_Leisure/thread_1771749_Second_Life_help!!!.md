### Post by holastickboy on 2011-05-31
Unfortunately the ATI rage is not a supported card, and falls below the minimum specifications provided for Second Life.  Looks like an upgrade is in order!

The ATI Rage is an older card than the Radeon series listed, and was mainly used for laptops and servers, etc.  If you have an AGP port on your computer, you may be able to source a card that will plug in and give you compatibility (a check at my local computer store shows they have AGP radeon 3650 cards, which will be more than enough for second life).
