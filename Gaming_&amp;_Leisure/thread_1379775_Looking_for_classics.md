### Post by Naegling23 on 2010-01-13
there are several engines that can run those games, and they have been ported to vitually every platform...no wait, every platform, even rockbox ported doom.....

You likely could just search synaptic for doom, or duke nukem.

However, the game engines were open sourced, allowing for the engines to be ported, however the art,levels, etc were not.  They are commercial games, and thus you must pay for the .wad files that you will need to play them.  Sorry, we cannot help you pirate games on this site.

However, if you do a little searching, you will see a huge number of user generated levels, or wad files for Doom.  And there is a shareware version of Doom as well.  So you wont get the original game, but you will get the gameplay experience.  And the best thing is that the user generated levels are 100% legal and mostly free (Im assuming free, I've never seen anyone charge for one)
