### Post by evolutionaryit on 2009-02-02
Are you a PC gamer?  Do you play Free and Open Source games?  - Complete this short survey and you could win a $50 Amazon gift certificate + $100 donation to the Open Source game project of your choice.

If you are a gamer, we want YOU to give us your feedback with this short 10 minute survey.  Open Source software has had a tremendous influence in nearly every area of computing - including the gaming space.  The goal of this survey is to get a deeper understanding of the current general perception of Free and Open Source games.  It is our hope that the survey results help reflect and constructively improve the state of Open Source gaming.  

[FOSS Gaming Survey]("http://www.evolutionaryit.com/limesurvey/index.php?sid=72676&#9001;=en")

**NOTE:** This survey is anonymous and the results are intended to help us better understand the FOSS community and NOT commercial purposes.  The insights of this survey will be detailed in my (Joseph Guarino) presentation on FOSS games at the Southern California Linux Expo (SCALE) as well as archived online.  For further details see the SCALE website:
[http://scale7x.socallinuxexpo.org/](http://scale7x.socallinuxexpo.org/)
 
**Prize details:**
Participants in this survey will have a chance to win a $50 gift certificate from Amazon.com.  As a part of Evolutionary IT's (survey sponsor) ongoing commitment to the Open Source world we will also be giving $100 to the Open Source game project chosen by the winner. 

**About SCALE:**
[http://scale7x.socallinuxexpo.org/]("http://scale7x.socallinuxexpo.org/")
