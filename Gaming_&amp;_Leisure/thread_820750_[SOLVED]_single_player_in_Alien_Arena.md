### Post by Irritant on 2008-06-06
What you can do is go into the "host server" submenu, and choose your starting map.  You can then set "public server" to "no", and it'll be offline.  You can also select the skill level to play at from this menu.  When you play it'll then cycle through the maplist from whichever map you selected as your starting map.
