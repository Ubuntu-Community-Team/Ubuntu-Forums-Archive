### Post by handy on 2006-06-27
CedegaCVS is NOT the same as the the one you pay for.  CVS version has no GUI or the copy protection benefits of the commercial Cedega.

You have to pay for a minimum of 3 months.  So, you can't cancel your subscription after only 1.

If you take up a Cedega sub' you can very easily download all the previous versions of Cedega, by using a menu option in the Point2Play GUI provided.  I highly recommend that you do that, for the reason that some games only work, or at least work best on certain versions.
