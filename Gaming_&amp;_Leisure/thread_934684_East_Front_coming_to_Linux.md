### Post by nixHead101 on 2008-09-30
For those of you that don't know EastFront was a enemy territory mod that has been in development for awhile.

The devs have moved to the XreaL engine. The XreaL engine is based on a heavily modified Quake 3 Arena engine (id Tech 3). This means that East Front will be a free stand alone game.

New Site
[http://www.eastfront.co.nr/](http://www.eastfront.co.nr/)


I posted on their forums asking if they were planning to release a Linux client and the answer is yes. :D  Turns out some of the members of the dev team are using Linux as their development platform.


So if you get a chance stop by their forums and give them your support.
