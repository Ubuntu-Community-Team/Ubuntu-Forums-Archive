### Post by acoustibop on 2008-01-02
The PadJoy plugin is the only one I ever managed to get an analog controller working in in ePSXe, kbless7.

However, like most Playstation emulator users, I'm aware that ePSXe is a good 4 1/2 years out of date, and I've been using a much better Playstation emulator, [pSX Emulator]("http://psxemulator.gazaxian.com/"), for the last year or so in Linux (since the first Linux version was released), and in Windows for another year before that.

It's pluginless, so no messing around with plugins needed.  It does seem much easier than ePSXe for getting controllers working, although 360 controllers are often difficult anyway.  My guess is that, if your 360 controller is working Ok in ZSNES and Mupen64, it should work Ok in pSX.
