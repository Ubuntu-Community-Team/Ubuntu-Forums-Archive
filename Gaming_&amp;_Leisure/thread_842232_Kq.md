### Post by acoustibop on 2008-06-27
Search for it in Synaptic, Vadi.

Sorry, Xbehave, it's ages since I last played KQ, so I can't help you much.

I have to warn you that it's an unfinished game; eventually you'll reach a point where you can't go further because those parts of the game aren't finished yet - but you're nowhere near that yet.  You'll get clear messages that you've hit an unfinished part when you do, as well.  But the game's great fun so, unless you're someone who can't handle not being able to finish it it, persevere.

It's a golden rule with a lot of RPG/Adventure games, that you can't just hack and slash your way through.  You have to talk to everybody, sometimes several times throughout the game, remember what they say and put things together from that.

I can't recall details but, yes, you can get to the main Orc camp, and you can open all the doors in the tower.  The places you need to put the stones aren't always by the door you want to open, though, and I think there are more than 4 stones.

### Post by z9484 on 2009-03-23
> **creek23 said:**
> If the game is Open Source, does this mean the sprites used are too? cause I can't extract the sprites from "*ssprites.dat*".


KQ's about page states "The entirety of KQ is licensed under the GNU GPL v2" I am guessing they compressed the graphics to binary files for ease of access in the code. It's actually really easy to extract the images. The file you want though is called mpcx. ssprites contain the attack images. If you have liballegro4.2-dev installed you should just be able to enter this in the data folder. 
```
grabber mpcx.dat
```
It opens a program that is able to import/export the stored images. You can also download the new graphics that we have made for kqthebetrayer [on sourceforge]("http://kqthebetrayer.svn.sourceforge.net/viewvc/kqthebetrayer/trunk/data/") Some of the original graphics were just repainted sprites from FF1 and should be redone but its a start. 

I am working on kqthebetrayer but I could try to see what's wrong with that Dungar Estate error. Tell me what version of kq you are using and send me a save file that is at that part and I can take a look. KQ is unfinished so I'm not sure how much farther the game goes after that. And though development is pretty much stalled they still usually answer the kqlives-main mailing list.
