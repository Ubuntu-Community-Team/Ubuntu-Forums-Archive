### Post by SoFl W on 2011-05-23
I was looking in the software center on laptop's xubuntu install and I came across **[Blockout II]("http://www.blockout.net/blockout2/")**  when looking for something else.  I remember playing the original Blockout 20 years ago when it was a DOS game and really enjoyed it.    It is a 3D tetris like clone, and you play looking down from the top.

>  BlockOut® II is a free adaptation of the original      BlockOut® DOS game edited by California Dreams in 1989. 
BlockOut® II      has the same features than the original game with few graphic improvements. The game     has been designed to reproduce the original game kinematics as accurately as possible.     The score calculation is also nearly similar to the original.

It is available in the software center or you can download linux or windows versions from the main website.
