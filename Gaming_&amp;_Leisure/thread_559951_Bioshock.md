### Post by aoanla on 2007-11-02
I think OpenGL 3.0 can match * all* of the improvements in DirectX10... it's also not released yet as a specification.

Sticking only with OpenGL 2.x, we can still match geometry shader support, and some of the shader model 4 features. Since they're mostly what developers are using out of the DX10 featureset, this means that OpenGL 2.1 is competitive with DX10, for the moment.
