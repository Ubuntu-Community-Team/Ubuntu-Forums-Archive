### Post by DoktorSeven on 2008-12-19
And the short answer is that you'll need to buy a copy of Doom (Doom/Ultimate Doom, Doom 2 or Final Doom) and get the WADs from there, or download the shareware version and extract its WAD (it's the first episode of Doom 1).

[This site](http://www.doomarchive.com/ListFiles.asp?FolderId=216&ContentsFolderId=216) has the shareware WAD as a separate download.  

Then run **doomsday -game jdoom -file /path/to/your/DOOM1.WAD** (changing the path, of course, to where you downloaded and extracted it to).
