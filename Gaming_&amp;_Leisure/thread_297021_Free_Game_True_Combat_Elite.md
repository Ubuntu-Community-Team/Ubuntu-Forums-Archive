### Post by shawn on 2006-11-11
That all depends on where you installed it, from your description I think you have ET in your home folder so try it in there first. 

You could also do it this way if you aren't sure where enemy territory is installed:

If you right click the .zip file in gnome then choose "extract here" you should get a folder called "tcetest". Go into your home folder and click View in the menu, choose "show hidden files". Move the tcetest folder into the hidden folder called ".etwolf". That should do the trick.

Good luck!
