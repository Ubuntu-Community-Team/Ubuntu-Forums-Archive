### Post by Chris on 2015-10-22
Pretty much any 2.1+EDR (or better) adapter should be good. If you are looking at a specific brand (like ASUS, IOGear, or something) then you might do a quick search to make sure it works in Linux. I have found almost all of the generic China ones off eBay will work in Linux. I personally have been looking to upgrade my generic 2.0 adapter for the IOGear GBU521 since I have had good luck with IOGear in the past and it isn't expensive. I haven't actually tried it though so I can't say if it's good but other people say it is.

Should work pretty much out of the box. I would install jstest-gtk so you can make sure it works. There is the possibility that you may run in to the same issue as me where no kernel newer than 3.14 works (I'm using a DS4 though).
