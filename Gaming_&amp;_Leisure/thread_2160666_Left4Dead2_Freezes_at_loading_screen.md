### Post by efflandt on 2013-07-07
Actually it would be under **Additional Drivers** in **System Settings** (the gear in Unity bar on left with typical installation). See if any suggested drivers show Activated. If you do not know about that or manually installing other drivers you may be using default radeon drivers.
