### Post by handy on 2006-02-27
[QUOTE=cdsboy]is the cvs totally free, and does it do the same thing as the main program[/QUOTE]

The CVScedega is **not** the same software as the commercial version.

I spent a lot of time trying to get Guild Wars to run, & *almost* getting there.

Commercial Cedega ran it straight away!

Commercial Cedega gives you tech' support & updates while ever your subscription is current.

You can also, & I strongly suggest that you do, download & install (all easily, through the Point2Play GUI front end) all the previous versions of Cedega, as some games require older versions to run, or atleast to run at their best.

It is my understanding that if you find the $5- US / month a bit rich, you can cancel your subscription after 3 months.
