### Post by SuperSonic4 on 2009-11-22
That's a legal move - it's taking *en-passant* which is a special property of pawn capture. If an opposing pawn moves two squares forward and opposing pawn can capture it as though it moved only one square forward

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/En_passant](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/En_passant)
