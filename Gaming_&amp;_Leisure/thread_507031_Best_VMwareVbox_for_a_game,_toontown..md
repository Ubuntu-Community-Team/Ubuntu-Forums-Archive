### Post by denali on 2007-07-23
I was one of the original alpha/beta (A1, A2, B1, B2, LE, Open Beta, B3 & Release) testers on Toontown online, back when a lone toon opened the door for all of us after Disney closed them.

TTO uses IE, ActiveX, Flash, Python and (if my memory serves, it's been a while) the Panda 3D engine.  The way Disney designed TTO, I'm not certain it can be successfully ran under Linux.  It's a great game, but Disney has really that extra mile to make sure they control the run environment.

Honestly, your best bet is a dual boot setup with Grub with a small partition that has nothing less than Windows 98 SE and IE, along with Java, Flash and Toontown.  I hope this helps!
