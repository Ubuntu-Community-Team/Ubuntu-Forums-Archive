### Post by MaxIBoy on 2008-10-14
Very nice review. I think you did a good job covering UrT.

Might you consider reviewing Alien Arena? It's in the repositories, but that version is out of date. 

You can either get version 7.10 from [the game's main website](red.planetarena.org), wait a couple of weeks until version 7.20 comes out, or do it properly and [install from SVN]("http://corent.proboards42.com/index.cgi?board=bugreport&action=display&thread=3252&page=1#32179"). SVN (short for "sub-version") will get you the latest development version of the game, with updates every few days. New maps and graphical effects are being added all the time, and you can watch it all happen with SVN and in the forum. The game has a really great community.
