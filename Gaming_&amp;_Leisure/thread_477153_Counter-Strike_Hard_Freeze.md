### Post by dfreer on 2007-06-18
Sometimes I have problems with CS not loading or freezing. My best advice would be to backup the *.gcf files in ~/.wine/drive_c/program files/Steam/SteamApps/, and then uninstall wine, wipe the .wine folder, reinstall wine, and reinstall steam. then just copy the *.gcf files back so you don't have to spend hours redownloading the game.
