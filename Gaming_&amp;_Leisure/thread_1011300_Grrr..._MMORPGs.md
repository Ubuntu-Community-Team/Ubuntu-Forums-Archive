### Post by .arean on 2008-12-15
> **RomanticWolf said:**
> FF11, is pay to play, but I have been a player on and off, and i think you would be very happy with its level system, party orientation, dung crawling, and story line

I agree. Final Fantasy XI is an amazing game with much more depth and story than all the grindfest MMO's like WoW, Lineage II, etc. The story and backgrounds of all the races is very RP friendly and there's almost no solo questing so there's plenty of room to RP, though I rarely see anyone actually role playing but I'm sure there's role playing linkshells. One thing to keep in mind though is it's vastly different from any MMORPG you've probably ever played. It's similar to a real FF game in that there's no NPCs with questionmarks over their heads giving out quests or anything. You have to explore and talk to people to get quests and find out new parts of the story. It'll take quite a while for you to get used to it. Just go into it with an open mind and realise it's not a hack 'n slash grindfest (It's definitely a grindfest but in a different way that's actually kind of fun) and you'll probably enjoy it. Since you're running linux you're probably already pretty open minded. The people over at [www.ffxionline.com](www.ffxionline.com) are really helpful and willing to help you get started.

Also, I highly recommend you pick up a controller with a similar layout to a playstation controller. The game's controls were designed for a playstation controller making a keyboard and mouse very awkward and cumbersome. Some people do just fine with a keyboard and mouse but I've met many people who quit playing because they didn't realise how much easier and natural the game plays using a controller.
