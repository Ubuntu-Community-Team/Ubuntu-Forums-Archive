### Post by Rustybolts on 2011-07-07
Its just a setting you can un-select in ubuntu.
To change this goto System > Preferences > Mouse
In the mouse settings window click the touchpad tab and de-select the option "disable touchpad while typing".

You should now be able to use the touchpad and keyboard simultaneously.

*Edit* I am using gnome2 if your using Unity select the power button top right and go into settings that way, then find your mouse settings
