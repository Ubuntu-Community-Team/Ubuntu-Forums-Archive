### Post by sisyphus1978 on 2010-01-02
Does anyone know of a version of "ghost: the word game" for linux? I can find info on a mac version (google ghost mac wordgame) but can't seem to find anything for linux.

It's a two-player game, where the players fight to not build a word. Everytime you complete a word, a letter is added to your ghost score (think hangman). The loser is the first one to spell ghost.

eg:

Person A: A
Person B: B
Person A: I
Person B: L
Person A: I
Person B: T
Person A: I
Person B: E
Person A: S

EHHHHH Person A completes the word and gets a G for ghost. Repeat until someone has spelt out ghost. 

It is fun. Any ideas?
