### Post by schmildo on 2007-12-07
I just wanted my old text-based games working. One of them was giving me the above error... I finally discovered the fix:

Asuming the game is 'adom' and you are in the console, and in the same directory as the game, (and assuming you have execute permissions on the file)

linux32 ./adom

Hurrah!
