### Post by cogadh on 2008-05-25
Generally speaking, Compiz and games don't like to cooperate. It is usually best to disable Compiz before you do any gaming. You can create a launch script for your games that will turn off the effects at game launch, then re-enable them upon game exit. Someone posted a sample of how to do this a while back, if you search the forums I'm sure you could find it.
