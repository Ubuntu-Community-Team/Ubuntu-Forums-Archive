### Post by charlieg on 2005-08-03
Stratagus is simply a flexible RTS engine.  It was borne out of the closed-down Freecraft project, and is now independent of Warcraft - however still able to run Warcraft games through the Wargus project.  There are a list of games in development that use the Stratagus engine should you want to try it but do not have a copy of Warcraft I or II to hand:
[http://stratagus.sourceforge.net/games.shtml](http://stratagus.sourceforge.net/games.shtml)
