### Post by acoustibop on 2008-05-30
Unless you have an older ATI card, kaldor, you can install a recent (not the most recent) fglrx driver (the proprietary ATI one) very easily in Hardware Drivers (used to be the Restricted Drivers Manager).

You just tick the box you want the driver installed for, Ubuntu will download and install the driver for you, and then you'll need to reboot.
