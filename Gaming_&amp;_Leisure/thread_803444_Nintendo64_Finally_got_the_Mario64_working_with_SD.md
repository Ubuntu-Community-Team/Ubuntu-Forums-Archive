You may try this, since you certainly also do not like playing around hours with plugins and to finally have something that do not work.
  
With the sdl mess, you have to copy the pifdata.bin into the  /usr/share/games/xmess/bios/ with sudo or su. 
```
chmod ug+rx /usr/share/games/xmess/bios/*
```
  
then run sdl mess wiht the options:
```
n64 -cart Mario64.n64
```
  
and you get the game !
