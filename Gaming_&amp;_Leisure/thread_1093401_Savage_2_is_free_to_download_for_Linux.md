### Post by jdunn on 2009-03-12
Savage 2 has been free since December.  For $10 you get a premium account which allows you a few extra features:  unlimited hellspawns and two extra item slots.  It is an online team based FPS/strategy hybrid game that's loads of fun.  One player per team is the commander who directs troops in a purely RTS (real time strategy) roll.  The other players play an FPS (first person shooter) roll.  The game runs very well on Linux.
