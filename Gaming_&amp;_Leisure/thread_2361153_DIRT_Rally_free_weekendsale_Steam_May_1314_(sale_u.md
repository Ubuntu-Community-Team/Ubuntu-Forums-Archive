### Post by efflandt on 2017-05-13
DIRT Rally on Steam is having a free weekend, although, I guess their idea of a weekend is Friday, Saturday, and Sunday until 1 PM PDT. But it is on sale until Monday May 15 at 1 PM PDT 70% off ($18 US). It is a big download which took overnight on DSL, I think 26.5 GB which when uncompressed and playing a few rally circuits ended up at 43.1 GB. I hear that it is very hard to play using a keyboard. A steering wheel would be optimal. But I am getting decent results using my Logitech F710 gamepad with settings in the game to reduce steering sensitivity to 35 and shrinking the deadband from 20 to 10 for finer control.

The button for the free trial does not seem to work in the normal Steam client, but works in Big Picture mode.
