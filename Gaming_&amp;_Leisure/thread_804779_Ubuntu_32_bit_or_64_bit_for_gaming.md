### Post by Vadi on 2008-05-23
Stick with 64bit, and do not use glxgears for video performance measuring. Use either SpecViewPerf 10 ([http://spec.unipv.it/gwpg/downloadindex.html](http://spec.unipv.it/gwpg/downloadindex.html)), or the Phoronix Test Suite ([http://www.phoronix-test-suite.com/](http://www.phoronix-test-suite.com/)).

glxgears is just very inaccurate, and a lot of people say not to use it. For me for example it gives me 3.8k fps, while my 8600GT 512ram video card runs ETQW on all settings set to highest.
