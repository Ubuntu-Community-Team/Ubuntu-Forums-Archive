This is perfectly legitamate.  Ubisoft re-released these games as free AD-supported games.  Before anyone asks, I believe it is restricted to USA users.  To get around this, simply create an Ubisoft account and select USA as your country.

I believe the ads only come up during the menu and before loading scenes.  Reports have said that there is a 20-30s delay whilst displaying ads.  I guess they gotta make their money some way.

For Far Cry at least, you can disable the ads by following the following article [http://digg.com/pc_games/Remove_FarCry_Advertisements_from_the_free_version](http://digg.com/pc_games/Remove_FarCry_Advertisements_from_the_free_version)
