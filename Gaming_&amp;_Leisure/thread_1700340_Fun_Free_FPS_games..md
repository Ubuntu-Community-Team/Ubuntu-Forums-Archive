### Post by L1cH on 2011-03-06
Gonna have to agree with nickleboyblue here, Nexuiz is first of all free.

Graphics are great, however if your machine isnt so great it's got a lot of quickly explained setting you can change to get better performance without losing too much eye candy,its auto detected setting are rather well done so it shouldnt really be a worry.

Liked Unreal Tournament?, Liked Quake 3 Arena?, well when you take all of the best strong points from those 2 games you will end up with a Nexuiz. the gameplay is fast,a lot of fun and extremely easy to learn and pickup. 
By the the whole Quake series and Unreal Tournament series (apart from ut3) are all natively available on linux.

It even has a Mutator system similar to the unreal tournament series, has the bunny hopping from the quake series and the wide range of super cool and unique weapons and really REALLY well designed maps.

Well that basically my review and recommendation of Nexuiz, Happy Fragging!
