### Post by MaximB on 2008-05-05
As always I try to give you updates on the finest games be it free or not, as long as it runs natively on Linux.

About a year ago (more or less) the development on postal3 has started and while I was 100% sure that there will be a Linux version (All the other Postal games had a Linux version and I've sent an email to the developers about Postal 3 and they have confirmed it) in/despite being developed on the Source engine an official announcement has not been made , till now !

In this interview Vince the CEO of RWS (running with scissors) has mentioned that Postal3 will indeed have a Linux client !
Interview : [http://www.omgpcgames.com/content/view/45/37/](http://www.omgpcgames.com/content/view/45/37/)

Official Postal website : [http://www.gopostal.com](http://www.gopostal.com)


POSTAL III will include motion-captured performances by a wide array of celebrities – from film stars to Playboy Playmates – as well as a storyline that offers gamers vast latitude in terms of their behavior. "We've always maintained since publishing the original POSTAL that the game is only as violent as the player wants it to be and we're committed to expanding this unique form of gameplay in this next addition to the ongoing storyline," Desi vowed.
