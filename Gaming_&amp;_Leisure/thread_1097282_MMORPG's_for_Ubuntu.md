### Post by jnewl on 2009-03-17
There's a new game called Celetania with a Linux version. It's sort of a dumbed-down Eve Online with a maddening interface. If you want space combat, Vendetta Online is a much better choice, imo.

Beyond those, I don't know of any native Linux MMORPG's. However, WoW, Guild Wars, City of Heroes/Villains, and Ryzom all run very well in Wine without having to do much if any tweaking. Several others will run more or less successfully if you have the skill and patience to mess around with them a little (LotRO, D&DO), but those four are probably your best bet. Oh, wait. I left one out: A Tale in the Desert. I can't remember if that one has a native Linux client or not, but it runs great.

In reply to jnewl, while iris is a native client for UO (and there are others like sunuo and iris2), it is not supported by most shards.
