### Post by Frogs Hair on 2018-12-01
Some desktop environments have panel indicators but the GPU has no support on my desktop. A conky configuration  will work depending how much screen is used when gaming. It is possible to create a horizontal conky that can be placed on the top/bottom edge of the screen, but this can take a little work and at least for me was fun. Many examples of conky [here]("https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=281865&highlight=conky").
