Before the race starts, the camera is looking at my car from the front-left or front-right. After it starts, the camera shifts between various views of various cars.

### Post by Thomas_SD on 2007-07-22
I have seen this type of thing before. When it happened to me it was because when I was setting up the race I had the focus set on one of the computer drivers. When you are setting up the race make sure that you set the focus to your driver.
