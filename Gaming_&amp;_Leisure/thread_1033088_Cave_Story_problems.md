### Post by Dirjel on 2009-01-07
While playing Cave Story, the game will sometimes jump out of fullscreen mode and into windowed mode for no reason whatsoever; at least, not any reason I can tell.  This wouldn't be a really big deal, but whenever the game goes into windowed mode, it stops responding to the keyboard >.>

### Post by Dirjel on 2009-01-14
Alright, I think I figured out what the problem was.  I think the screensaver was activating, and that was what was kicking me out of the game.  I disabled the screensaver, and haven't had a problem since.

As for the configuration thing, I just downloaded the Windows version, ran the configuration app, and copied the Config.dat file over.  Works great now :D
