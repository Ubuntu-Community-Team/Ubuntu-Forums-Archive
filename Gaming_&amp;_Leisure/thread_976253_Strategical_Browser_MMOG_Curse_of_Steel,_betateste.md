### Post by GGLucas on 2008-11-09
For the past month or so, I've been working on an idea I've had for a while, a full-blown turn based strategy browser MMO, not in the cop-out way that tribalwars and such do it, no, an actual hex grid, nothing more, nothing less :P (Thank God/Apple for <canvas>)

The world is somewhat persistent (every week is a new round, so your empire doesn't persist indefinitely), everybody makes their moves simultaneously and every 30 minutes, those moves are executed. (It's hosted on a Linux VPS I manage, naturally)

I was wondering if there were any nice people here who would like to help me betatest my game, it can be a bit confusing at first, I've heard, so if you're interested, it might be good to read the little [Beginner's Guide at http://wiki.curseofsteel.com/index.php/Beginner's_Guide](http://wiki.curseofsteel.com/index.php/Beginner's_Guide) I cooked up first, as it explains a lot of stuff.

I hope some of you will like the game, and any feedback and feature requests and such are welcome.

You can click my status image below, which also has a snapshot of the map surrounding my capital, to go to [http://www.curseofsteel.com](http://www.curseofsteel.com)
