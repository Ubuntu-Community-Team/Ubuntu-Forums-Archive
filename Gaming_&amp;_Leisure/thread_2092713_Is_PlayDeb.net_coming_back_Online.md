### Post by tomreyn on 2012-12-25
> **TurtleKing said:**
> Thanks. I wonder if there is any way we can help them. Maybe donate some cash to help pay for the hardware?

This surely is a good idea, they've always had a hard time to reach their fund raiser goals to pay their server costs. *Many* users, few contributors. 

According to the source code of their [*About* page]("http://web.archive.org/web/20110724020150/http://www.playdeb.net/about/") they accept donations by Paypal to 
```
lamego.pinto [AT] gmail.com
```The last thing I heard of them was on Dec, where I was told that "by now we have a new server and files are copied to it" and that they need yet to make sure everything works as it used to be.

I'm sure more volunteers would also be welcome. They have a [Launchpad team]("https://launchpad.net/~getdeb-collaboration") where anyone with a Launchpad account can apply for membership (which would also allow you to subscribe to their mailing list). There's also [#getdeb on the Freenode IRC network]("ircs://irc.freenode.net/getdeb") which is also available via [webchat]("https://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=getdeb") (but please don't pester them about when the site will be back).

For people interested in the code, there are [these Github repositories]("https://github.com/ckorn").
