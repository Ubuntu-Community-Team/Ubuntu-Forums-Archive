frets on fire works with the ps2 guitar controller, all you need is a usb adapter, thats what I use.  I hear it also works with the xbox360 controller as well. The focus is also on the gameplay, so the notes take up more of the screen...but you do loose the animated characters in the background......in my opinion, FoF does some things better, GH/RB does some things better, they are likely on equal footing in my eyes...but coming from an open source game!!!!!
