### Post by RIchard James13 on 2008-05-24
I think the difficulty for beginner level should be lowered. Too many storms in storm season.

The game takes time to learn how to play. It is not a normal strategy game, it is more about building a network that is able to withstand different types of attack.

Recently my strategy is to try and upgrade the city as much as possible during the first two seasons (quiet and alien). Then I try to rebuild any damage during the quake and storm seasons. The higher the city level is the more build units you have and the easier it is to rebuild from the damage.

I have upgraded the city during the quake season a couple of times. Every time I upgrade the city during storm season I lose the game.

Note that each type of attack on your network is different. 
Aliens attack single points. Quakes do a straight immediate line through the entire system. And storms do a line but move slowly, also there is only one quake at a time but multiple storms.
