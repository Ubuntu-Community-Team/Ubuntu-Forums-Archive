### Post by oldrocker99 on 2016-04-29
PlayOnLinux creates a "bottle," a separate folder, for each installed game, and each script downloads the best version of wine for that game, and any other winetricks-style dependencies. Your mileage might vary, and sometimes wine itself works better that POL. 

Accroding to [winehq.org](winehq.org), neither game runs very well. Winehq.org is the best resource for running games on wine.
