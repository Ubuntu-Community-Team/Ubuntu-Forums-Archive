### Post by Vadi on 2008-03-26
Woohoo! Savage 2 Linux client, both 32 and 64bit, is now out.

News story: [http://www.linuxgames.com/archives/10137](http://www.linuxgames.com/archives/10137)

About the game: 


Videos (including actual gameplay): [http://savage2.s2games.com/media.php?t=m](http://savage2.s2games.com/media.php?t=m)

While the linux client release was indeed held up (though, not really their fault, and not something you expect to plan for - the guy who was supposed to do the linux port dissapeared), it does promise amazing graphics. Most of the time was spent on porting 'shaders' - which wikipedia says are things that make a game look very kewl if you got a good video card.

The demo has 5 hours of free gameplay, there are no monthly fees, and the company is great (they released their original savage for free now), so if you want something new of a very interesting genre mix, give it a try :)
