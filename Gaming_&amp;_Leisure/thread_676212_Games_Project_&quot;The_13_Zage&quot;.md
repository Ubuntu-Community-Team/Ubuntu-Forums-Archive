Game Project: "The 13 Zage" 

This is one of the Game project That I want to start long time ago, but take 9 class at school doesn't give me a lot of time. So now I am in collage. and I get more free time. So I finally begin this project. 

This is a Action game that is happening in real time. (12 hours) So it is not a really long games. But what is interesting is you are pretty much free in this game, however, every second count. And some stage or Quests will be need to finish in sometime limit. 

Instead using a gun or sword, which people are using it in 1000s of action games. The main character in this game will holding a "Fan" as the main weapon, (Of course not electric fan  you are using in summer.) It is a Chinese - Japanese fighting fan, and of course, the main character will get a better one as he goes along. Beside that he get a few special weapon that he will found at some hidden place. And it will use it to complete some special move and some special effect. 

Here is some basic element that the game will has. of course there are much more, and I will post what they are as the games goes along. 

Main Plot. 

The story begin at 7pm and will be end at 7 am. (12 hours) (13 stages-chapter). The main character (haven't named yet) is a skillful explorer who want to know the beginning of the city (haven't named that too) which no one know beside the 13 Zage (some kind of God visitor who try to reach god, and found out how he "look" likes.) However, they wouldn't lets any one that doesn't have "the mind" and "the force" to know any of the beginning history of the city and 3 books that tell, the greatest knowledge. The book about power, "Xipho's dairy". The book about order. "The final Law". and the book about all the history, from before to beyond "The last word of Destiny" At the night when the story beginning. MC's "The wheel of life" start go dis order. MC know someone is doing something to him, and if he doesn't stop this, something bad might happen, and he has to stop this within 12 hours. So he is going to "Towel" where the 13 zage located. and if he made it, not just he is going to save himself, but also found out the truth. 

So that is the basic plot of the story line. of course thing would be change to a better way.
