### Post by SishGupta on 2007-04-23
I find that wine rarely regresses so im sure it is safe for you to upgrade to .35. If it doesn't work as well then you should be able to grab .33 off of the official wine site, and there should be an archived .deb of that version.

Check the [appdb]("http://appdb.winehq.org/") for any fixes regarding your screen but also try and make sure you are using a good resolution for your monitor.

I always have beryl off when I play games as it reduces frame rate dramatically.
