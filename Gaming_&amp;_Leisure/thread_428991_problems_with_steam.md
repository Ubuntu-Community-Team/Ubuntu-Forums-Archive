### Post by FoolsGold on 2007-04-30
Download this: [http://www.webpagepublicity.com/free-fonts/t/Tahoma.ttf](http://www.webpagepublicity.com/free-fonts/t/Tahoma.ttf)

Stick it in your fonts folder in Wine (normally ~/.wine/drive_c/windows/fonts)

Run Steam, hopefully things will be better

For any future issues with Steam, check the Wine App DB:
[http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=1554](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=1554)
