### Post by sloggerkhan on 2013-06-06
So I tried pairing my Ouya controller with my Ubuntu box to play Awesomenauts from the latest Humble Bundle and it's all good fun once I rebound the buttons except for one thing - the analog stick snaps to perfect left, right, up, and down when it's within ~5 degrees of exact N/S/E/W. No idea why. It didn't seem to happen with Super Tux, though with Super Tux it'd also be hard to tell if it were snapping. Anyway, I was just wondering if anyone had any ideas as to what might be happening and how to fix it. It's really obnoxious when you try to aim slightly down but it snaps to perfect horizontal instead and your shots go right over someone's head.


Oh, and anyone else, also make sure you don't bind the triggers because they don't work right with Awesomenauts, either.
