### Post by GWBouge on 2010-08-15
> **mooreted said:**
> Looks interesting. Wonder how the levelling is. One of my main beefs with RPG's is: Kill 50 rats so you can get a sword. Kill 50 worgs so you can get a shield.... Etc.

It's been a little while since I've played it, but the leveling/skilling is a bit different than most games if I remember right.  You have both skill levels and your overall rank, so you can be Rank 50 and still not be able to use a level 20 item because you haven't gotten a certain skill up enough.  The quests aren't bad if you like a quest based game, but there's a bit of 'Kill 10 of this', 'Collect 20 of that', 'Make 50 of those', etc etc.  Spells are actually items that you can make or buy ... not attained through the leveling like most other MMO's.

It's worth a shot if you're bored.  I'm not really big on quest-based games, but I admit it was pretty fun due to the skill/profession system.
