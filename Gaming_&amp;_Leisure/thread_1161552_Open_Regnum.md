### Post by slave-zeo on 2009-05-16
I just started playing regnum today, it's a pretty fun game. There are both windows and linux installers. Make sure you get the linux one (make sure you get the 32bit or 64bit depending on your version of Ubuntu). If it's wanting to open with wine you got the wrong one.

I also had to right click >> properties >> premissions >> "Allow executing file as a program" to get the installer to run. But after that it was smooth like butter.
