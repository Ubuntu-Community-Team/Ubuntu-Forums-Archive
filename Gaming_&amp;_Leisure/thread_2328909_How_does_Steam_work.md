### Post by oldrocker99 on 2016-06-25
**If** you don't have to reinstall your system, to answer your question: There are quite a few free games on Steam, and purchased and free games will be downloaded to your machine. You can play PvP or co-op online on Steam, but it's basically an overlay from which to play games. If you want to play DOTA2, that game will be downloaded to your machine and played online, for free, incidentally.
