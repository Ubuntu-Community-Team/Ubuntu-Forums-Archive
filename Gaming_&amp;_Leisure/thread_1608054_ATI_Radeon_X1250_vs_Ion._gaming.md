### Post by chewit on 2010-10-29
The nVidia ION is a better card than X1250!

Basically, the ION is a nVidia 9400. Which is a shader 4.0 DX10 card. X1250 is a Shader 3.0 DX9 card.

I wrote a blog post about me gaming on Intel Atom with a nVidia ION - [http://www.edhewitt.co.uk/2010/02/15/gaming-on-acer-aspire-revo/](http://www.edhewitt.co.uk/2010/02/15/gaming-on-acer-aspire-revo/) - You should expect better results than me since I am guessing you will be using a CPU with more power.

CPU: dual core 2.0 GHz

Video will be too low with a Athlon X2 + x1250, and CPU to low with ION + Atom.
