### Post by iheartubuntu on 2009-07-21
You might not have flash installed? Try going to the Applications > Add/Remove and installing flash. If that doesnt fix your prob, I suggest installing the "Comprehensive Multimedia & Video Howto" and enable the Mediabuntu repository here...

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683)

It installs a lot of good stuff that doesnt come preloaded on your new Ubuntu system like flash, ability to play MP3s, java, and allow you to burn watch, copy and DVDs.

Follow the instructions and if you have any probs, ask around here or specifically in that thread if you need help installing the multimedia stuff. I think its important to install. I do it on every new Ubuntu system I set up.
