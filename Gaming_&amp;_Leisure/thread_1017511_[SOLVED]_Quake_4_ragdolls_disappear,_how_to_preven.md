### Post by crazyfuturamanoob on 2008-12-23
This one makes corpses last 30-9999 seconds, didn't test it:
[http://quake4.filefront.com/file/30_Seconds_Body_Fix;49955](http://quake4.filefront.com/file/30_Seconds_Body_Fix;49955)

And this is the one I use, makes corpses last forever:
[http://quake4.filefront.com/file/Self_Shadow_and_Corps_Stay;49194](http://quake4.filefront.com/file/Self_Shadow_and_Corps_Stay;49194)
If you don't want weird shadows, then don't extract materials folder.

To install them you must extract the contents into your quake4/q4base folder.

Sometimes that mod I use doesn't work, like in bosses or vehicle maps.

And if you get ridiculous laag with 200 corpses lying around, type "killRagdolls" 
in console to get some breathing space.
