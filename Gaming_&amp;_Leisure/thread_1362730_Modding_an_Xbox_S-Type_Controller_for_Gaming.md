### Post by farrell2k on 2010-01-02
It's easy.   The original xbox controller is connected to the system by a usb cable.   You can do it the easy way, or the hard way.  The easy was is to simply cut off the end of the xbox pad as well as the enf of a usb cable and match up the wires.  The harder way is to open it up and solder the usb wires into the board.    You will likely needs drivers after this.  I know you need them for WindowsXP.   I have no clue about Linux.

You're probably better off buying one of those usb adapters online somewhere that will aloow you to plug it in and use it as standard usb hid.

Oh, and the s controller is just a smaller form factor of the original, so the instructions you saw online should work for both.
