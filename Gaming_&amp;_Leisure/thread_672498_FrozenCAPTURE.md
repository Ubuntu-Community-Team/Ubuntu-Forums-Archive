[FrozenCAPTURE](http://pivot.servut.us/w/FrozenCAPTURE) is my first *playable* game created with Python (& Pygame). The basic idea is to have more blocks than your opponent. You may either duplicate or move your blocks. 

**Features:**
[LIST]
[*]superior gameplay
[*]superior installer (just doubleclick START.sh and it handles the rest)
[*]up to four players
[*]many intelligent bots included
[*]custom levels
[*]custom AIs (be sure to make one!)
[*]Windows/Mac/Linux -compatible!
[*]opensource
[/LIST]

[Download & Enjoy :popcorn:](http://pivot.servut.us/w/WFile/FrozenCAPTURE.tar.gz?fileact=download)
