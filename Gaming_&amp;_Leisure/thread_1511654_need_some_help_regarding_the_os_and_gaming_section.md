### Post by Shazzam6999 on 2010-06-17
You can re-install XP without messing up your Ubuntu install, you just need to make sure that you don't overwrite anything from your Ubuntu install and it can be a little confusing at first so make sure you do some research. I'm not the best at it myself.

[http://appdb.winehq.org/](http://appdb.winehq.org/) is a great place to go to figure out what programs work well under WINE and to find out how to get those programs running at their best. Just enter in whichever NFS you're trying to get running and see what they say.
