### Post by Bike To Australia on 2015-06-01
Found the Update server Droid. If the entrance to MS office is on the east side and the restaurant is on the west side, then go south from Bill Gapes hideout, through the red brick wall, angling to the right and to the east, past the swimming pool, through the east side double doors. Immediately turn right and back north through the office area there. Turn left at the end of the blue green wall, going east again, and keep the blue green wall on your left shoulder. keep going when the wall turns red. Turn left at the red brick wall, going south now through the double doors and through the open single door to the left of the closed single door. The Control droid is in that little office area and the update server is actually just a gate switch but it is one of the few terminals in those seven cubicles. Now to find the gate it opened and the real update server.

### Post by 12Infrared on 2015-08-28
Thanks for sharing your answers here! I'd like to point out that IRC is where the devs hang out, and it's often the better place to ask gameplay (and other) questions. Our room on Freenode is #freedroid.
In the meantime:
[LIST]
[*]After taking down the disruptor shield, you need to go back to Spencer to proceed. 
[*]The stock certificate is shown to a droid at the entrance to the Hell Fortress offices. 
[*]For the desk lamp, talk to Bruce, in the south-east corner of town. 
[*]About what Dixon says - that part of the game has never actually been implemented! The player currently has no way of checking what he's talking about. As with everything, contributions are welcome! :) 
[*]We added a new graphic for "important" terminals in the recent 0.16 version, so hopefully they'll be easier to spot in the sea of "normal" terminals. 
[/LIST]
