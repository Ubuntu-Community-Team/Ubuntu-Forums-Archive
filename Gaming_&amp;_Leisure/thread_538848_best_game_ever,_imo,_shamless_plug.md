Okay, this is a shameless plug for my favorite game. Links...

[http://www.evernightgame.com/](http://www.evernightgame.com/)

and a community link...

[http://wyrmmlore.com/evernight/](http://wyrmmlore.com/evernight/)

Evernight is a cross between chess and an online RPG. You negotiate among an online community for alliances ect. It is the most engaging game I have ever played. It is completely browser based with a wonderful community and very few bugs which are fixed quickly when they are found. I have only come across one in over 2 years of play and it was fixed in 5 min.. You can play it for free to get a good idea of the game. You should know you get *much* more out of it as a subscriber which is under $6.00 a month. Even the free version is very enjoyable!
