### Post by matthewcraig on 2007-12-17
Seems like you have a good handle on the situation already.  There is iTunes with DRM, download from broadcasting company with DRM, the series DVDs with DRM, and computer tuner cards.  You may also be interested in Tivo systems.  Some people do bypass the DRM and share the television content, but that is not legal, at least where I live.  (If I may offer an alternative: try to find and support DRM-free content that you enjoy and that you will be happy as your entertainment.)
