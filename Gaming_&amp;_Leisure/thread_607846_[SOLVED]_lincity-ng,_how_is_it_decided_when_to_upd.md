### Post by hikaricore on 2007-11-09
Aside from bug fixes and security issues, **updates are only made in the repos when a new release of Ubuntu comes out**.

Sometimes if the recent version of a software title isn't considered stable or the maintainers of the universe/multiverse repos overlook it, an older version is left in its place.
