### Post by BoyOfDestiny on 2009-07-19
Install gweled from Add/Remove for a nice bejeweled clone. 

As for emulators many work fine. Assuming you have the latest intel driver (jaunty did eventually provide it.) 
Considering a standard NTSC tv (back in the day) were 640x480, pretty much any system from that era fits on the screen with room to spare. I recommend mednafen, zsnes, and sdlmame.
