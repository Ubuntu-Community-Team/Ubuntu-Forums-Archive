### Post by anaconda on 2006-08-31
eboard isn't enough. It doesn't have a chess-engine, so you can't play against the computer with it unless you install gnuchess (a chess engine)

I have eboard (with gnuchess) and xboard with gnuchess and phalanx. And I like the way phalanx plays more than gnuchess...

I was amazed at how difficult it was to get a good chess program for ubuntu!  Couldn't find a program that would include everything.. just separate chessboard-programs and chess-engines. (luckily the engines are good)
