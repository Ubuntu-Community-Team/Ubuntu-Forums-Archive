### Post by ZarathustraDK on 2012-06-18
You are using the "Download for Ubuntu"-buttons, yeah?

It's kind of wonky and not working as intended at this point I think. Here's what worked for me though:

1. Enable your Ubuntu One account (it should be in the dock if you haven't disabled it), and sign in.

2. Go click on each "Download for Ubuntu" button for Humble Indie Bundle 5, dismiss the popup, don't try and open it with anything. This should tie your HIB5-games to your Ubuntu-One account.

3. Open up Software-center, choose "File"->"Reinstall previous purchases" from the global menu; your games should be there, and if they aren't then give it a minute, sometimes they don't pop up right away. Double-click to install.

### Post by oldrocker99 on 2012-06-20
If you clicked on the buttons on the the HIB V page, you can find all the games in Synaptic. Click the Origin button on the left and you'll see

LP-PPA-commercial-ppa-upoaders-[name of game]

and you can download and install them that way. Except for when I'm buying software, I seldom use the Software Center; Synaptic queues your selections and installs them in one operation, rather than the Software Center's method of installing each program one at a time.
