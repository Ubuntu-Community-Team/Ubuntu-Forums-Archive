### Post by Sofox on 2012-03-25
For Linux games check out:

[Linux Game Data Base]("http://www.lgdb.org/") for an extremely comprehensive catalog of games available for Linux, commercial and free (with screenshots and good browsing)

The in build Ubuntu Software Center, both for open source and commercial games (not many commercial games right now, but a few)

[Desura]("http://www.desura.com") which is a Steam like client for buying, managing and updating a wide variety of Linux games, while often will give you access to the games on Windows too. Currently 138 Linux games, both commercial and free, but often good quality.
