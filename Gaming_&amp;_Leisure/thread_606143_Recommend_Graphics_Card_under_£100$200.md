### Post by nerdman978 on 2007-11-07
If you want to play new games (usually supporting DX10 or DX10 only) buy a GeForce 8600 GT ($200) if you have Vista and are ready to play direct X 10 games. If not, my friend plays alot of modern games on his 7600 GT (less than $130) even though it is a fairly old card. If you don't have PCI Express, go with a ATI, they have kickass AGP cards that absolutely crush nvidia. I used to have a Radeon 9550 XL card, which ran some newer games, but I would go with a much newer Radeon card (i'm not up to date on ATI card except for their new DX10 card, so check the website).
