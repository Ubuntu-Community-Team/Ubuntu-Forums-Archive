### Post by DanglingPointer on 2014-10-04
Yeah Metro Last Light is awesome!  I like how it is from a different perspective, an eastern european perspective.  You get a glimpse of their culture, issues that they face, and manner of thinking through the game.

When all the fighting erupted in Ukraine, it reminded me Metro LL straight away.  Even the way people were dressed, the troops of both Ukraine and the pro-Russian rebels, the neo-fascists in Kiev, etc.  You can see that the game actually really portrays the microcosm of real issues that faces Ukraine and the former soviet block as a whole.

However I heard that the developer has opened up a new studio in Malta and is moving many projects down to the island.  I wonder how good their future games will be without the environment that Kiev was in which so inspired the culture, theme, dynamics, and visual art of the game?  Malta is drastically different.  More like Sicily in the Godfather II.
