### Post by Tweak42 on 2011-04-22
I use a Logitech MX518 (8 button mouse) using a combination of lomoco and btnx to get the extra buttons working.  Also I use a usb foot pedal bought off ebay that emulates a usb keyboard; it is programmable via a small utility.

I've been eyeing a Logitech G700 and according to [http://www.kromhouts.net/blog/?p=28](http://www.kromhouts.net/blog/?p=28) it will work in linux.
