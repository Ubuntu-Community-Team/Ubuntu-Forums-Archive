### Post by GrouchoMarx on 2013-02-26
Hey all,

I posted this in the community forums, but I realize now I should have posted it here in the game forum. Just wanted to give a shout out to this amazing game. I played it recently and was blown away. Honestly I never thought I'd see games of this quality released native on Linux, so I really feel like getting the word out to support the devs. Gorgeous immersive world, with mysterious, complex characters. The pacing may be a little slow for some people's taste (though I absolutely loved it). Overall, it's a beautiful work of art, and one of the best I've played in a long time. Has anyone else played it?

[http://kentuckyroutezero.com/](http://kentuckyroutezero.com/)

Also available in the Humble store and on Steam.

From their website: "Kentucky Route Zero is a magic realist adventure game about a secret highway in the caves beneath Kentucky, and the mysterious folks who travel it."
