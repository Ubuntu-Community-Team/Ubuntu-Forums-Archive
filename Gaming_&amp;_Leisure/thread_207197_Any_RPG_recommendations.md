### Post by francisco_athens on 2006-07-04
[QUOTE=charlieg][Scourge](http://scourge.sourceforge.net) is promising.  The next release (0.15) will be the first to make waves as it addresses a number of the game's shortcomings, I think, but the current release is still fun.[/QUOTE]

I was a big Dungeon Siege addict some time ago.  After playing SCOURGE for a about 30min I think I have found another fix :D. 

It mixes realtime (for just exploring) and turn based (for battles) with a decent 3D camera.  There seems to be lots of story, and right away you have a number of "jobs" that your party can take on. Lots of items (almost too many) to discover as well.  Furniture and objects may be moved around and there seem to be many NPC's to dialog with.  

With the rumor that MP may be a future feature, I'm even more excited.  So far, so good!  Install with the .package was painless enough.
