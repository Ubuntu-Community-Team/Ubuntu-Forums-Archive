### Post by slux on 2006-01-03
Contacting the developer(s) of the respective games would be a good way to go about doing that.

You can also stop by at [http://happypenguin.org]("The Linux Game Tome") which is frequented by many GNU/Linux game hobbyist developers. Their Game of the Month project also highlights games that are in need of a facelift and music could very well be part of such an effort. Finally, there's the accompanying IRC channel #gametome on Freenode where many productive individuals take part.
