### Post by Yes on 2008-02-13
OpenGL isn't dead, it's just not used in many mainstream games.  There are some popular games that use it, such as Enemy Territory or Counter Strike.  There's a full list of games [here](en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OpenGL).

[Wine](www.winehq.org) is an implementation of Dx, and there are developers working on getting Windows programs (including games) working in Linux.  Many programs already work through Wine, there's a list of programs and how well they work in Wine [here](appdb.winehq.org).
