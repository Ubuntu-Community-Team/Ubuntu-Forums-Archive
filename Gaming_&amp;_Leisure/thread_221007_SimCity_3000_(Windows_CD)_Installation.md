### Post by Carrots171 on 2006-07-22
I've got SimCity 3000 working under Linux. To make WINE recognize your CDROM drive, bring up winecfg with this command:
> wine winecfg
When winecfg comes up, click on the "drives" tab, and click "browse". You can select and add your CDROM drive there.
