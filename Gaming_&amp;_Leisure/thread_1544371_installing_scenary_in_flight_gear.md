### Post by itismike on 2010-09-14
you can install FGFS 2.0 through the Software center or through Synaptic. 

To install scenery, you can follow the directions [here]("http://wiki.flightgear.org/index.php/Howto:_Install_scenery#GNU.2FLinux"), but understand that the folder you need to uncompress them is:
> /usr/share/games/FlightGear/Scenery

HTH,
Mike
