### Post by adamkane on 2006-04-21
Updates very often don't work on Wine or Cedega. If you want updates, then you must find a kind soul, who has posted details on their blog or on a forum like this one. 

The whole idea behind Wine or Cedega is that some hard-working user has already figured out a tweak to get their favorite game working on their favorite distribution with Wine, and has decided to share the knowledge with the ignorant.

There is nothing special about Cedega. It just incorporates the tweaks that are already freely available to the wide world. Cedega makes your life a little easier by implementing many of the freely available tweaks for you.

If someone hasn't posted any info about updating, then the information doesn't exist, until someone decides to share the info with the rest of us.
