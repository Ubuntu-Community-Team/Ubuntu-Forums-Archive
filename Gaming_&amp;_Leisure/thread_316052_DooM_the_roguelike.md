### Post by B0rsuk on 2006-12-10
Linux version of DoomRL has been recently released.

[http://doom.chaosforge.org/index.php?module=downloads](http://doom.chaosforge.org/index.php?module=downloads)


Tired of bland Angband clones which make you die from boredom ? Tired of SF/modern roguelikes where weapons feel like waterguns ? Try DoomRL. It differs from others in that ranged weapons are actually good at killing stuff. And tactics, the way you explore rooms and use explosives actually matter.
It has sound (ripped from original DooM without permission, but still), and it adds a lot to the game. I like it a lot.
