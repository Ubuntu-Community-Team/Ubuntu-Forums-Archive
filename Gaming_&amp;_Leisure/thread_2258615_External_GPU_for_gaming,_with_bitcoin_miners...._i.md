### Post by R33D3M33R on 2015-01-12
Graphics cards are good at mining bitcoins, because they have a lot of tiny cores which are very efficient in manipulation of some data. Bitcoin miner ASIC also might have multi cores, but they are intended for mining only. Without this, their hash rate/watt would be much higher and mining would not be worth it. So you are probably out of luck.
