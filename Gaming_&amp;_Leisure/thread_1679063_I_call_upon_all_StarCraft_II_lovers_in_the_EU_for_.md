### Post by LoloftheRings on 2011-01-31
Hello fellow StarCraft II players,

I've been loving Blizzard games for a long time. Not only for their quality but also for their great wine compatability and performance. Warcraft III and World of Warcraft support an opengl engine, which greatly improves framerate with wine. StarCraft II does not have this kind of support.

StarCraft II does have an opengl engine ready, as it has been released for Mac OS X. I estimate the performance of StarCraft II at 70% of the Windows version when it has opengl support, which is about 20% right now (maybe less).

StarCraft II is fully compatible with wine, it just needs some performance boosts to run smoothly on non high end machines. I can run ultra graphics with 60fps with ease on Windows, but only 60fps on Low with wine.

I started a thread on the EU forums, asking why Blizzard is not supporting opengl, and if they intend to do it. I have not yet recieved an answer. Please support my thread by leaving a post of support at the thread if you have an EU License: 
[http://eu.battle.net/sc2/en/forum/topic/1549440464#1](http://eu.battle.net/sc2/en/forum/topic/1549440464#1)

Thank you for your time.
