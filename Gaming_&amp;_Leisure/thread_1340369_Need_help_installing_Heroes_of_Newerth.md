### Post by PariahVayne on 2009-11-28
Right click the HoN.sh file, select _**Properties**_, go to the _**Permissions**_ tab, then tick _**Is Executable/Make Executable**_ (whatever it says on GNOME), then click _**Ok**_. Now double click the HoN file and select _**Run**_ or _**Run In Terminal**_.
