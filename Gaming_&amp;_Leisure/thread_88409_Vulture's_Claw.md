### Post by quietglow on 2005-11-24
Slashem is a Nethack derivative, so I don't think it actually starts Nethack. When you start the program, its in old skool ascii mode (which I like and play often) but you can get the very cool 3d tile version (a la Vulture's Claw etc) by selecting game>option and then "misc" on the next pane. Pick "3d tiles" and you're set. I can't find any way of making this a default setting. 

Have fun! I spent a good 2 hours playing yesterday at work :-) I awlays tell people that it is seriously the most complex and involving computer game I've played!
