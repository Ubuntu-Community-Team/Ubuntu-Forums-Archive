### Post by alfalfa31 on 2008-07-16
There are loads of them.

Go to Applications>Add/Remove

At the top, click the box labeled "Show" and select All Available Applications"

Click "Games" and read the descriptions until you get sick of the plethora of options...

Install the games of your choice and enjoy...
