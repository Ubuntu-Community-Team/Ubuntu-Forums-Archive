### Post by hikaricore on 2007-06-15
The biggest problems with including more software disc space and personal opinions.

What one person considers useful another does not.
Hell I think WINE should be included by default, but then you would argue that wine can take up near 50mb of disc space.

Ubuntu includes the BASE elements of a modern desktop, and that's all it needs to include.

You are more than welcome to enable the Universe repos and install these games yourself, I've even done this from a LiveCD with a decent amount of RAM.  ^_^

**p.s.  There's also licensing issues to deal with when including various software titles.  What if Frozen Bubble's license says (it doesn't from what I know,
just making an example) that it can be freely distributed but never on CD?  Then Ubuntu does it anyway and you're +1 big mess.
