### Post by Z3n0s on 2007-01-29
I am running Ubuntu Edgy

Pentium 4 2.6 Ghz
1 Gig of ram
256 Meg NVIDIA video card


Here is what I did:

1. Made a complete copy of .cedega/World Of Warcraft (just in case ya'know)

2. Started Cedega and selected WoW Make sure the 5.2.10 engine is installed and going to be used. Change the Winver OS setting. Win98 works here.

3. Inserted first disk and clicked Install in Cedega

4. In the popup I clicked Mount then browsed to the Installer and clicked Continue.

5. WoW TBC Installer started and did it's thing.

6. When a new disk was needed you will see a dialog box prompting you for the next disk. I pressed the eject button on the drive. The disk will unmount and you can insert the next disk. Wait until the disk spools up and then mounts. It will display  the contents of the disk just close that window and then click ok on the dialog box. It will continue to install.  You will go through this process for all the disks. 

7. After the WoW TBC Installer completed a dialog box appeared and asked if I wanted to upgrade my account or just play the game if my account had been upgraded. 

8. Clicked the upgrade account button  and upgraded my account.

9. Started WoW. The intro movie rocks.

10. Yet another patch is required (159M). This time I let the Blizzard Downloader do it's thing (I used to download directly and install with wine).

11. After the download finished there was an Error popup which I simply clicked OK on.

12. Continuing on, the download finished and I clicked OK in the Blizzard Downloader window. The Blizzard Updater then started and installed the patch. After the update was finished I clicked OK in the Blizzard Updater window.

13. Started WoW.... YET ANOTHER PATCH (3M). Repeated steps above for previous patch. Blizzard Installer / Updater worked perfectly.

14. There are three patches but as I said before the updater does a good job.  Just remember through the updater process to click ok through the error message it complains about not being able to open an html file. 

Everything works great and on to the outlands!:mrgreen:
