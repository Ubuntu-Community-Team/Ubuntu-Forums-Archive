### Post by AusIV4 on 2006-12-31
I use a TV tuner and tvtime, but I use a desktop with an internal tuner. Most USB tuners use hardware encoding, I believe, which creates a small delay that is undesirable when it comes to video games.

I might also mention that I had trouble when I tried to use the coax output from my Gamecube, and had best results with S-Video.

### Post by thomgeo1 on 2007-01-05
I was able to get my ATI tv wonder pro to work with my Xbox using TVtime out of the box.  Try to avoid the hardware encoded models of tv tuner cards like the Hauppgauge 150/250/350/500 cards since they will introduce a delay of 2-3 secs.  The WinTv models may work but I do not know.  I was able to use an Adaptec Gamebrigde usb tv tuner card with great success for my laptop but it only works in Windows.
