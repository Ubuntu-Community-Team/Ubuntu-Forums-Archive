### Post by mivo on 2008-02-24
You'll basically need to get Fritz working in Wine. Which version of Fritz do you have? Take a look at [this page]("http://theorie.physik.uni-wuerzburg.de/~arwagner/chess/chessbase.html") for some solid information, though it's a little dated. Of interest are the bits about installing Fritz.

I have Fritz 10, but haven't tried it in Wine yet. Need to test this one of these days. :) I took the easy way and just purchased Shredder 11, since they have a [Linux version]("http://www.shredderchess.com/linux.html"). If all else fails, there's always the [FICS]("http://www.freechess.org/") server. There's a variety of clients for Linux available, too, for example [PyChess]("http://www.getdeb.net/app/PyChess").
