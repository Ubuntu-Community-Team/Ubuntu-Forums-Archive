### Post by MystiqElph on 2009-11-23
Most likely your joystick/joypad is causing the problem.
Several workarounds: 1. Disable joystick support in dosbox.conf 2. Simply disconnect the thingy 3. Run chasm with "-nojoy" commandline option (type "chasm -nojoy" w/o quotes)
