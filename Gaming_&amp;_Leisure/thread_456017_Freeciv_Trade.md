### Post by engla on 2007-06-14
It can be hard to estimate, but there are some notes: there are two things: trade plus from trade route that is added to both cities, and trade bonus that is paid out when the route is made.

Both are higher with longer distance, if the cities are of different nations or continents. When a caravan is active, you can middle-click a target city and check how much the trade plus would be for a trade route to there from its home city.

There are also some formulas at [the freeciv wiki]("http://freeciv.wikia.com/wiki/MathInFreeCiv")
