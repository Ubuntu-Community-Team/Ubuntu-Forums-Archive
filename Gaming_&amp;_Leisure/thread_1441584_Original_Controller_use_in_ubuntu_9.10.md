### Post by dfreer on 2010-03-29
NES - Nestopia or GFCEU if you can't get nestopia working.
SNES - BSNES, if system can't handle BSNES use either snes9x or zsnes either one is good.
N64 - Mupen64plus.

I use joypads as I haven't found any reliable source for good quality windows/linux supported adapters.
