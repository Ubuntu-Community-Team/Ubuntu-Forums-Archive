I used Cedega years ago, & learned then that they offer a miserable service & their support is all but non-existent.

If you can't do what you need via Wine, try Codeweavers Crossover office/games demo.

Codeweavers are the prime movers behind the Wine project. It is where they get money to live on.

You can't loose giving the demo a try. :)  If you do need to buy Crossover, its pretty cheap too.
