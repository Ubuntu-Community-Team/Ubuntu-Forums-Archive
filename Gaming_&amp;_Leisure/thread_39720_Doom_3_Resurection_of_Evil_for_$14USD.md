### Post by tristan on 2005-06-07
[QUOTE=jdodson]anyone want to comment how good this game is?  i havent gotten around to purchasing it yet.[/QUOTE]

I've got Doom3 and ROE (linux versions) and they are both excellent. The gfx and sound are absolutely mindblowing, and it's genuinely creepy to play in a dark room with the sound cranked! It's like walking around in a movie. People whinge about the simple gameplay but  I love it, I don't have to think too much - just kill stuff.

Yes it is a bit of a demanding application! My box is a P4 2.6Ghz, 1Gb RAM, Nvidia 5900XT. I play it in 800x600 high detail, it runs at 40+ fps most of the time (might chug occasionally) It runs at a constant 60fps in 640x480 medium detail. 

If you can get it as cheap as you say, then do it by all means. I don't think you'll be disappointed.
