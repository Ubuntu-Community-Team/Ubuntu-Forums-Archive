### Post by Nekiruhs on 2008-01-11
Silkroad Online works great in Wine. It only has a few minor texture glitches. Free to play too. It has a really neat stall system where you can open a store anywhere and you can choose the items in your inventory you wish to sell and the prices. It also has a great 3 group trade system, best I've seen in any MMORPG. Traders carry precious goods bought with their own gold from one town to another; the difficulty of the trade route and the length of it increase the value that the goods can be sold for in another town. Thieves try to kill the Traders transports and steal the goods while hunters get payed by the traders to protect them from thieves. Its really fun
