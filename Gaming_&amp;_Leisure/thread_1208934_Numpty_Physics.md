### Post by mister_k81 on 2009-07-09
If you installed it via .deb file, you can uninstall it through the synaptic manager by going into: --> **System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager** ... then click on "status" in the left side bar and then open the "Installed (local or obsolete)" menu, then right click and uninstall Numpty Physics. 

Or, option 2 would be to open up the terminal and type in (or copy and paste): 

```
sudo apt-get remove numptyphysics
```
