### Post by PoHandle on 2008-12-27
Hmm... as far as the double mouse thing, I'm not quite sure.  Are you playing the game with compiz running?  Please give us some info on your setup.

As for the mouse issue, I know you can have the game "grab" your mouse in windowed mode by default and "un-grab" it if you press CTRL+G.  It seems possible that this could be the problem, because when mouse hits the edge of the window, it no longer controls the player's view.  Check that you are in fact in fullscreen mode and press CTRL+G to see if that solves the problems with your limited view.
