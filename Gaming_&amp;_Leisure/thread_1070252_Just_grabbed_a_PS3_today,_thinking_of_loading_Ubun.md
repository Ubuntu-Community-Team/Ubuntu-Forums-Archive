### Post by doorknob60 on 2009-02-15
I have Ubuntu (with Openbox, 256 isn't enough for me to want to run Gnome or Xfce. Goes much much faster this way) on my PS3. If you download and compile snes9x-gtk [http://www.snes9x.com/phpbb2/viewtopic.php?t=3703](http://www.snes9x.com/phpbb2/viewtopic.php?t=3703) then fullscreen, sixaxis w/ bluetooth, sound, and everything works just fine. Good framerates too. Actually I think I might have had problems getting it to compile (works on all my x86 machines), but I used alien and this package worked just fine: [http://pleasantfiction.ipower.com/ps3linux/snes9x-gtk-1.51-33.ydl.1.ppc.rpm](http://pleasantfiction.ipower.com/ps3linux/snes9x-gtk-1.51-33.ydl.1.ppc.rpm) For controlller support, see this, easy and works: [http://psubuntu.com/forums/viewtopic.php?f=4&t=764](http://psubuntu.com/forums/viewtopic.php?f=4&t=764)
