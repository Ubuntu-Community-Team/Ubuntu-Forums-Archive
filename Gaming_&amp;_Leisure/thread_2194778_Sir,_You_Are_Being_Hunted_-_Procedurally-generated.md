Another game I've picked up lately, that works well on Linux, is the Alpha of Sir, You Are Being Hunted.  In it, you are a Sir, (or Madam) who was working with or on some unknown device, and ended up transported to an archipelago.  There you must find the pieces of the device, so you can return.  And also, you are being hunted.  :)
