If you have an Nvidia graphics card, they already provide full support and have for many years. Intel does not directly provide support, but they do sponsor the open source project that creates Linux drivers for their graphics products. The only manufacturer that has been slow to provide any support has been ATI, and that changed recently when they released their drivers to the open source community. It will probably be a while before we see good drivers from ATI open source project, but they will come eventually.
