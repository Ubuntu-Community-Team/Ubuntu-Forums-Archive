> **ELD said:**

Will look into it more later and do a possible write up for you on [www.gamingonlinux.com](www.gamingonlinux.com)

A 10$ pledge is essentially a pre-order for the game on one operating system we support.  For PC we are stable in windows (available on Desura), currently approaching stable on Linux 32 and 64 bit, and investigating Mac OSX.  

We will have prototypes on each system, available on invite through Desura.  We are currently deploying the Linux prototype, and just waiting to clean up some build distribution errors (1 missing library, easy fix).

The 1$ access gives you our demos of the game throughout the development cycle.  We're considering giving a discounted price for the full version, post release, for people who participated in our demo program.

Also, the 1$ pledge can be cancelled at any time, if people don't feel satisfied with our progress.  They can keep access to our build, and see it develop regardless.

We are also considering a 1$ single player version of the game on release, which is essentially everything we wan't to roll out in our demo build.  It'll include first set of campaigns, and all the battle systems + fleet creation tool / fleet data, but with no map editor, and no co-op capability.

Thanks for the interest.  If you have any questions, please contact [email]corporate-espionage@mercenary-games.com[/email]
