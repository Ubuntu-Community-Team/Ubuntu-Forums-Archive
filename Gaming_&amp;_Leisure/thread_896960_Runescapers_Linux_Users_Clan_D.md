### Post by doorknob60 on 2008-08-21
Well, me and King Peter (aka Link) are starting a Runescape clan just for Linux users. There's no RS level requirements, you just have to use Linux :) Here's more info:

> **Q. What is the Linux Users Clan?**
A. The Linux Users Clan is a clan of [Linux](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linux) users. If you didn't get that from the name, then stop reading now, because you're too stupid to join  ;) . As for what we do as a clan, we're a [RuneScape](http://www.runescape.com/) clan; if you're looking for a clan for another game, you've come to the wrong place.

**Q. Who founded this clan?**
A. It was founded by [doorknob60](http://lu.penguindevelopment.org/memberlist.php?mode=viewprofile&u=54) and I. Doorknob put his Clan Info field on [Zybez RuneScape Community](http://www.zybez.net/community/) to "Linux Users" and asked other Linux users to do the same. I decided to take it one step further, and asked him if he'd co-found the clan with me. And here we are!

**Q. Who can join?**
A. We typically expect our users to use Linux (duh), although there are no objections against Linux enthusiasts who use Linux part-time. If you hate Linux or penguins, or are a Microsoft/Apple fanboy, then begone. (Insert deeply offensive statements here.)

As for the requirements, **there are no in-RuneScape requirements**. Outside of RuneScape, we expect you to not be an idiot, and to like and at least occasionally use Linux.

**Q. How do I join?**
A. First off, register an account and validate it through email. After you've done that, post a topic in this forum with your **RuneScape name**, your **name on these forums**, a short introduction about yourself, and preferably some information about other clans/forums you're a member of. If your "résumé" checks out, your account here will be added to the clan members group and you are officially a member :) . **You will not get full access to the board unless and until you post an introduction topic or personally contact me or Doorknob.**

**Q. What do you do, as a clan?**
A. Basically, we hang out and be geeks :D . At the time of writing (the day the clan was founded), there are no particular events that we have planned, but we're always open to suggestions!

**Q. Is your clan based on any geographical locations/time zones?**
A. Nope.

**Q. What is the Linux Users Clan's homeworld?**
A. [s]To be determined :) .[/s] doorknob says World 60 :D

Linky: [http://lu.penguindevelopment.org/index.php](http://lu.penguindevelopment.org/index.php)

So if you wanna join, go do what the FAQ says, and you can PM or IM me if you want to ask me any questions.
