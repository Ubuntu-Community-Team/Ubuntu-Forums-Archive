### Post by Duskao on 2009-11-02
You will probably have to go to the website and compile it from source or maybe if your lucky they will have a .deb for ya. 

I would recommend downloading djl and install it through that, then you can update it through djl when new versions come out, alot with 120 other games for linux. 
[http://en.djl-linux.org/?q=node/19](http://en.djl-linux.org/?q=node/19)

ensure you have the dependencies (can get through synaptic if you don't have them)
extract the tar, then (if you downloaded the file to your desktop) 
it's 
"cd ~/Desktop/djl"
then
"./djl.sh"


Best of luck, works great for me. Oh yeah, do NOT use sudo for the installation.
