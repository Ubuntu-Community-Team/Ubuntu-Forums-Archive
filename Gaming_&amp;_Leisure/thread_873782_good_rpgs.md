### Post by Zeotronic on 2008-07-30
I skimmed over the entire thread, but I'm not sure if anyone mentioned Planeshift.
Planeshift is a 3D, Open Source (only the code is, they own their game content), MMORPG. Its not exactly complete yet, and because of that the size of the world is rather limited (though it could still take you an hour or two to traverse it), but it might be worth checking out if your into that kind of thing. A warning however, in one of the 'sections' of the overworld (unfortunately a frequently traveled one) they have a lot of content, which demands more than 512mb of RAM... it gives me more than my fair share of trouble. Other than that one area though, you probably wouln't have any trouble with it unless you have a connection with high latency... even on Dial-Up it worked well enough.
