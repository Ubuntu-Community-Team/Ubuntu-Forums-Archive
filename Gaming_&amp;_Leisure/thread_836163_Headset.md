### Post by Cappy on 2008-06-21
I use the eDimensional Audio FX (not the AudioFX Pro 5+1!). I think it is fantastic. It has a subwoofer-like sound whenever there is bass (explosions, etc). 

I've used Plantronics headsets and they sound great too. I REALLY like Plantronics, just not as much as the Audio FX. The Plantronics in general have fantastic treble and makes music come alive with background instruments you've never heard before. I don't listen to music, and if I happen to on a blue moon it's dance which has a ton of bass, so I really don't care that the Plantronics has superior treble.

But do yourself a favor and NEVER buy a logitech headset.

None of the headsets I've had last longer than 6 months or so. Considering I use them 16/7 it's not that weird.
