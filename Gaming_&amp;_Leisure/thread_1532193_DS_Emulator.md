### Post by Grishka on 2010-07-16
there are two Linux DS emulators. the aforementioned DeSmuME, and [iDeaS](http://ideasemu.biz/). also, No$gba works very well with Wine. No$ is the fastest, but it doesn't support as many games as DeSmuME, while iDeaS has the most features and ok compatibility, but it needs the fastest machine. though all should be playable on a relatively modern computer.
