### Post by stuart.crouch on 2007-05-22
I can confirm you'll need the latest patch and the ECE edition. That will give you everything.

Go here - [http://www.beyondunreal.com/main/ut2004/ut2004essential.php](http://www.beyondunreal.com/main/ut2004/ut2004essential.php) and fill out the essential files wizard.  That will tell you what you need and give you links to get them!

As for the games to play - all of these mods are released and 100% pure good fun..

air buccaneers
troopers: dawn of destiny
red orchestra
killing floor
alien swarm
chaos ut 2
frag ops

And the best one of all - action unreal tournament  ( but there arent any servers anymore :( )

You should be able to find info about each of them here - [http://mods.moddb.com/?search=true&game=78](http://mods.moddb.com/?search=true&game=78)
