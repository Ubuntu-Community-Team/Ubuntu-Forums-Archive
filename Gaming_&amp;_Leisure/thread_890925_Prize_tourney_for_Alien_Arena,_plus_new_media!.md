### Post by Irritant on 2008-08-15
(Official press release)

COR Entertainment LLC announces it's Qexpo 2008 prize tournament "Aliens For Animals"!
 
32 combatants will compete in the freeware game Alien Arena 2008 ([http://red.planetarena.org](http://red.planetarena.org)) in a 1v1 format.  Entrants are kindly asked to make a voluntary contribution to the Humane Society of America ([http://www.hsus.org/](http://www.hsus.org/)) of 10 dollars.  Donations will be matched by COR Entertainment!  
 
Prizes are as follows: 
 
1st Prize: Nvidia GeForce 9800 GTX
2nd Prize: Razer Tarantula gaming keyboard
3rd Prize: Razer DeathAdder gaming mouse
Consolation FFA(top 8 non-prize winners compete): Saitek Eclipse keyboard
 
Please sign up at by August 20 at the COR Entertainment forums [http://corent.proboards42.com/index.cgi?board=tournament&action=display&thread=3070&page=1](http://corent.proboards42.com/index.cgi?board=tournament&action=display&thread=3070&page=1)
