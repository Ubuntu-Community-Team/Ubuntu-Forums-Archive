### Post by WalmartSniperLX on 2009-04-20
You MIGHT be able to run Perfect World International.

Just google the game :P

I really want to try it out but my integrated Ati IGP 3xx chip just cant render fast enough. Here's info including a nice video tutorial on how to set up the wine registry to render at its best (recommended that you watch). There is a shader key option in there. He tells you to enable it, however with your video card I suggest using the 'disable' option. Check it out : [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=9923](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=9923)

**EDIT: I play runescape. My username is drlesser. It's a fun mmo. I really like it because it isn't combat oriented like most other mmorpgs. There's a lot to do as far as crafting, quests, farming, jobs, and even combat if you will. However, the two cons to the game in my opinion are that it does tend to require a lot of grind, and there is no team combat (like guilds, clans, or teams). Usually this is a good feature of an mmorpg because you can share XP among clan or guild members while in combat and leveling. Maybe we can convince Jagex to add it to the next build.

A good shooter, that will run on your hardware, would be Nexuiz, Urban Terror, Enemy Territory, and Open Arena. They're all Linux native.
