### Post by jISh on 2006-10-11
No.
It only matters for the exact round you are playing. Map changes, server change, etc will remove your experience. Savage is not an RPG, the experience is only there to provide you with small character boosts each round (armor upgrades, stamina upgrade, stronger damage etc).
