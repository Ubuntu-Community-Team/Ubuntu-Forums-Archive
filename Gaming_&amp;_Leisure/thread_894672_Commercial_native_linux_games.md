### Post by gnawingonfoot on 2008-08-19
There are some really good commercial indie games that run natively in Ubuntu.  I'm really addicted to the [*DROD*]("http://www.caravelgames.com/Articles/Games.html") games right now, and I would highly recommend them to anyone who enjoys good logic-ish puzzles.

[Greenhouse]("http://www.playgreenhouse.com/") is also publishing two commercial games that will run on Linux.  [*On the Rain-Slick Precipice of Darkness, Episode One*]("http://www.playgreenhouse.com/game/HOTHG-000001-01/"), the Penny Arcade game was their first, and they recently released [*Eschalon: Book One*]("http://www.playgreenhouse.com/game/BASLX-000001-01/"), an more classic-styled RPG.
