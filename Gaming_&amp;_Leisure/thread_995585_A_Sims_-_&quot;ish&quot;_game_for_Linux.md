### Post by Perfect Storm on 2008-11-28
I know people have been looking for a Sims game for Linux. Well it's not quite but close.

The Game Company [Gruppy Games](http://grubbygames.com/), the maker of the Professor FizzWizzle, Brain Train and FizzBall have released;

[My Tribe](http://gaming.gwos.org/doku.php/games:alphabetical:m:my_tribe)


> Create a perfect island paradise with My Tribe! 

Shipwrecked on a beautiful tropical island, your brave little tribespeople must build a new home! With your guidance, the tribe will uncover mysterious secrets, research new technologies, and construct massive wonders! Come back often to feed your tribe, clothe them, and look after the babies and children!

Real-time gameplay! 
*Your tribe is alive, even when your computer is turned off!
* Tribespeople will grow up, learn new skills, and have children of their own.
* Tons of different hair styles and hair colors.
* Design your own clothing colors, and choose from 18 style combinations!

Literally billions of islands to explore! 
* Each island is unique, and has a variety of mysterious objects to solve!
* If your tribe is prosperous, they can travel to new islands.
* An endless number of possibilities &#8211; the fun will never stop!

Design your own paradise! 
* Plan the layout of your village using 14 different buildings.
* Plant beautiful flowers and tall trees; landscape as you choose!

So much to do! 
* 25 trophies to unlock for accomplishing amazing feats!
* Solve 8 mysterious objects by using your wits and your tribe's skills.
* Over 40 powerful potion recipes to discover!
* 8 levels of scientific advancement to research.
