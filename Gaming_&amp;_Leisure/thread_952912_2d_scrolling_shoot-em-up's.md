### Post by Abras on 2008-10-20
To be perfectly honest, the pickings are a bit slim. There are a few games that are at leas worth a look though. First I recommend Kenta Cho's games. Just about all of them are in the repos (just search his name), and they're a lot of fun. Each one is based around a different gimmick or feature but all are shmups. There's also Chromium and Tenmado (both of which can also be found in the repos). I know, none of these are all that close to R-Type, but like I said, there aren't many choices. Hopefully you can still enjoy them!

EDIT Oh, I forgot to mention emulators. For the best shoot 'em ups I suggest you look into MAME, TurboGrafx, and Sega Genesis/Mega Drive emulation. Other game systems of yore had shoot 'em ups too of course, but those three I just mentioned cover most of the best games. I also recommend you look into Saturn and Dreamcast emulation if you're feeling ambitious and if your computer is powerful enough.

PS If you need any help with emulators or maybe game recommendations (or anything else for that matter) feel free to PM me.
