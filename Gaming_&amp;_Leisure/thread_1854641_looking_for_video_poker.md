"9/6 Jacks or Better" is the most type of casino-based video poker game.  The "9/6" part refers to payouts; a full house pays $9, and a flush pays $6.  More information about video poker can be found [here]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Video_poker#Jacks_or_Better").  

I put the 9/6 label on the game because the long-run plans are to allow the player to choose between various types of games at run-time, and the game graphics will change accordingly to reflect the player's choice.

And no, that's not a straight. A straight would be 8-9-10-J-**Q**. You have 8-9-10-J-**K**.
