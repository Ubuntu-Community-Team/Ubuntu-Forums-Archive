### Post by KhaaL on 2008-02-03
*cd ~/Desktop/* changes your current directory to your desktop directory in the console
*chmod +x rolauncher* gives the file (rolauncher in this case) permission to be e**_x_**ecutable
*./rolauncher*  simply executes the file.
