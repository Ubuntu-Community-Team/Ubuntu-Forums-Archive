### Post by raydeen on 2009-02-11
The Elder Scrolls: Arena

[http://www.elderscrolls.com/tenth_anniv/tenth_anniv-arena.htm](http://www.elderscrolls.com/tenth_anniv/tenth_anniv-arena.htm)

Very fun old 1st person RPG. The only snag is the download is a zip in .exe form. You'll have to unzip it on another computer or attempt to unzip it with WINE (don't remember if that actually works or not). After that, just run the instal.exe with DOSBox to initially set up the sound card and then run arena.bat from DOSbox. It actually runs better than when it did on a real PC (used to crash out constantly). The only change to make is to up the CPU cycles in DOSBox to 15,000 to 20,000 cycles to get smooth performance. I have copies on my Linux box and my work MacBook.
