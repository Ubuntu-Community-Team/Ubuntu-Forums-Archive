### Post by Kenjitamura on 2013-06-22
Just thought I'd bring it to the gaming forums attention that the game Legends of Aethereus is currently giving away random beta keys on their twitch channel.  Here: [http://www.twitch.tv/threegatesstudio]("http://www.twitch.tv/threegatesstudio").

Just log into Twitch and leave a message in the chat channel that you do not yet have a beta key and a random person will be picked by Threegates to be given a Beta key via private chat.  This game currently has a working linux client.

Update:  The live stream has finished which marks the closing of the free Beta key giveaway for this week.  They will be doing another live stream with beta key giveaways this coming Friday the 28th.  The turn out was kind of poor with only 20 people showing up and only three signaled they wanted a beta key, hence all three of them got their keys.
