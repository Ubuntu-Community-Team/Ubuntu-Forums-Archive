I agree with this. You will definately be flamed. The main reason for this is in HoN, having a new player around won't just be a 4 vs 5 fight. In most other games, the other 4 have a shot at turning it around by playing well and just ignoring you. In HoN, you'll be feeding the opposition (giving them free xp and gold). This leads to them outleveling your team and your team will subsequently lose. Because of this, the community is extremely hostile. 
One tip is play defensively at first. Overextending to try and get a kill will often result in your death. Once you understand the game better you can play a bit more offensively. Read up in the HoN fora. A lot of thing are counterintuitive: for instance, attacking creeps will result in your creeps moving up more, and thus closer to the enemy tower, which will make life harder for you. 
Practice last-hitting (scoring a last hit will grant you gold) and denying (killing your own creep to prevent them getting the gold) as well.

Good luck to anyone trying out the game.
