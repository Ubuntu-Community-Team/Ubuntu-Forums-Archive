### Post by AthlonMDK on 2007-07-24
Hi

I got myself a GP2X and it is a very nice handheld console

1.) Is Gamepark really bankrupt?
*YES, but GAMEPARK HOLDING manufacturer of the GP2X is not. Gamepark made the g32x*

2)Does this mean that firmware updates and improvements (such as a rechargeable lithium battery) will never be out
*See 1st question*

2.) How's the battery life
I read its 5 hours when playing games (which will be it's main use)
*It depends on the kind of battery. The rechargeable batteries with the GP2X-F100 MK2 value pack if fully loaded go approximately 5 hours and 10 minutes with the battery test.* with 2650 (duracell rechargeable batt.) it goes 5,5 hours... so it really depends on the mAH.

3.) How good are the emulators?
Are they stable? Does it constantly flicker? Sprite collision in good order?
Those are a few of my concerns off the top of my head

I use GnGeoX and with metal slug sometimes (when alot of action on the screen) you get framedrops. Other emus are all ok no problem whatsoever (gameboy, (s)nes, mame, sega etc.). PSX emu etc are all alpha / beta / heavy development so they are still very slow.
