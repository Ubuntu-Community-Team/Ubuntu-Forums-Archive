### Post by Carrots171 on 2006-05-13
[QUOTE=DSn0wMan]I am not exactly sure what to ask, but I like playing Multiplayer shooters, and Realtime Strategy Games.[/QUOTE]

All of the Quake and Doom games are available for Linux. Another good multiplayer FPS besides Nexuiz and Tremulous is CodeRED: Alien Arena. [Enemy Territory: Fortress]("http://www.etfgame.com/") is a great game,too. (You'll need to have Enemy Territory, which is available for free, to play).
