### Post by acoustibop on 2007-12-21
Well, I don't play fighting games, but I have a Logitech Rumblepad 2 (wired) and it's an excellent controller.  Needed no configuration, is very comfortable and has excellent analog control.  It's in the Playstation format.  It also has a nice little wrinkle in that it has a Mode switch: this enables you to swap the functions of the directional pad and the first analog stick, so when you're playing games that don't have analog support, you can still use the analog stick as the directional control rather than having to use the directional pad.

Seems very durable as well.  I think I've had this one about 1 1/2 - 2 years; still plays like new.
