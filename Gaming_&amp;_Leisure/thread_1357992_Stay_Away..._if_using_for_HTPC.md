### Post by Lee_Machine on 2009-12-18
Sound over HDMI works under 9.10 aka Karmic Koala.

What is your output of 

aplay -L 

You have to go to the sound preferences settings and change the output to HDMI. 

Also, what remote? If you need help getting a remote setup the easiest way is to use an iPod/iPhone app called HippoRemote. If using an IR remote is your preference let me know. I will point you in the right direction. k :P  

Lastly the video will work with the Nvidia X Server Settings.

Saying Win7 is stable is far from the truth. :lolflag:

### Post by Chris2fur on 2010-03-31
Sorry you had a bad experience, I on the other hand fell in love with XBMC on the Ion platform. I bought an Acer Revo 1600 (Single core Atom+Ion) and installed XBMC Live 9.11.
