People that are currently sitting in a chair,

The UGN ( Ubuntu Game Night : irc.freenode.net #UGN ) worked with your local lottery to pick the next gaming event. The first ball says... **_[SIZE="3"][FONT="Arial Black"]Urban Terror[/FONT][/SIZE]_**.

Urban Terror is a First person shooter (FPS) where you try your best to show off your pull-up skills. Think of it as something close to the movie Over The Top, but with pull-ups and guns.

The event starts at **[SIZE="2"]1800 GMT ( 2 PM EST )[/SIZE]** on [SIZE="3"]_Sunday_[/SIZE] October 4th. It usually runs about two hours or until everyone leaves. You can see the results of past events here [http://gwos.org/doku.php/event:followup](http://gwos.org/doku.php/event:followup), or at our new location [http://gwos.org/ugn/?q=node/7](http://gwos.org/ugn/?q=node/7) 

The best way to install Urban Terror is by using playdeb.net. Below is a direct link to their install using apturl.
