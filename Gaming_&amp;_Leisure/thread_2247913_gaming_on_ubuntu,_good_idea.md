### Post by Christmas on 2014-10-11
Counter-Strike: Source and Counter-Strike: GO will work, since they have been ported natively to Linux. The rest will probably work under Wine or PlayOnLinux, with or without issues. See [this link](https://appdb.winehq.org/) and find your games to see how they work under Wine.

And yes, I believe it is a good idea if you want to use Linux. Now Steam supports hundreds of games natively on Linux and more will be developed. GOG.com also has a good selection of Linux games, and the rest usually work under Wine/PlayOnLinux. You can always make a dual-boot as well. Also, I recommend installing the NVIDIA drivers rather than the Nouveau driver for those games.
