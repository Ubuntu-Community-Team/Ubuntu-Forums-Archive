### Post by oldrocker99 on 2013-11-20
Also, don't forget the roguelikes, which are all descendants of rogue, the mainframe-only (at first) single-player RPG. It eventually morphed into nethack, which is the most gameplay you can find (arguably) in one game. Swell graphics are optional.

Searching in Synaptic for "roguelike" presents

angband
crawl
gearhead
moria
omega-rpg
powder
tome
zangband
...and that search didn't include nethack, the ultimate game of its type, which is where I'd start, but seriously, any of these can give that dungeon-delving experience for free, and a look at a bit o' computer history to boot.
