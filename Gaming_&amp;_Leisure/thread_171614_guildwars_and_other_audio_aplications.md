### Post by handy on 2006-05-07
[QUOTE=bunnieofdoom]im useing my onboard 5.1 that came with a new dell xps400 i need a hardware sound? which might you reccomend for compatiblity?[/QUOTE]

The Soundblaster Live, is fairly cheap these days.  They are also well supported by Ubuntu, & probably everything else on the market.  They are no longer cutting edge technology, that does not mean that they aren't still a great card.

I use a SBlive Entertainment 5:1 suround sound capable card.

Try to buy oem, meaning no fancy box.  The one that manufacturers use when putting together a machine.  You will save money, & cardboard! :KS

By the way, ***hardware mixing*** is a capability of the processing chip on the card.  Not all sound cards have this ability.
