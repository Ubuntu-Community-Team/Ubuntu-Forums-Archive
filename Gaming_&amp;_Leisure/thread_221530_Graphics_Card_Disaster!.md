Hell, I run it very smoothly (except for some graphics driver problem) on a Geforce 420 go laptop card (32 meg). Your best performance/price choice would be a 6600-6800 or a 7300GS. They go for about $130(USD) at places like newegg, and can actually play any game that is out there at reasonable quality.
