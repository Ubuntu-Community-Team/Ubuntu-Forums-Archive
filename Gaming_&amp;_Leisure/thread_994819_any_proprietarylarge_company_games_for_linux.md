### Post by Abras on 2008-11-30
[2DBoy]("http://2dboy.com/") is another one of those little indie studios that seems to support Linux. At least World of Goo, their one and only game thus far, is planned to have a Linux version. At least their first two games based on the PA universe have Linux versions, and their distribution service, [Greenhouse]("http://www.playgreenhouse.com/"), has a few other games that have Linux versions. And there's a good chance that these companies will continue to support Linux.
