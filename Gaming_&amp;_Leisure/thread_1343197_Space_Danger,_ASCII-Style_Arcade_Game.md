### Post by adeypoop on 2009-12-01
I just had a shot of it and these impressions are based on a quick look at the game only. The game itself seems to have a really nice retro look and feel which I did like. Having said that there were a couple of things that annoyed me quite a lot (maybe its just me being a noob or maybe just the fact its a java game) but these would probably stop me bothering to play it again.

It wasn't easy to get up and running (i didn't have java installed on this comp) and when i did get it running I was inside a full screen window even though the resolution was smaller than the screen size, ie a big black border around a smaller playing area. I couldn't then find a quit button to exit the game. It seemed like the quit option just brought you back to the initial menu rather than letting you close the thing down. In the end I had to resort to pressing Control Alt F1 and logging in a terminal then finding the java process and killing it. 

Don't mean to be overly negative about it, I think I'd have prefered a game running more nativly than in a java machine but aside from that it did have a very nice style and good gameplay
