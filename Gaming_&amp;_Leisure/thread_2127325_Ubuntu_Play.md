First Ubuntu Touch; second Ubuntu TV; third Ubuntu Play. But what is Ubuntu Play?

Well, although this is purely an idea, Ubuntu Play is, what I think, sounds like a GREAT games console platform on Linux for cross-platform games that can play on PCs AND other games consoles. Ubuntu Play could be installed onto a PC, laptop, tablet, Mac, etc.; virtually anything that plays video games on. SO far, Steam has started off gaming on Linux and has proven to be very popular, and so, this games console could also play online games downloadable from the Ubuntu Software Centre.

Granted there is not much call for gaming in the Linux world, but remember that Linux is still young and anything new can be achieved. Great idea or what?
