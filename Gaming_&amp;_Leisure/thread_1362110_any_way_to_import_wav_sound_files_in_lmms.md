### Post by Grishka on 2009-12-23
the default sampler reads audio files in many formats, including .wav. drag AudioFileProcessor from the instrument plugins tab into the song editor. when you open it's settings, you'll see an inconspicious green button with a folder icon. you can load your sample with it.
