### Post by towsonu2003 on 2006-01-11
I'm playing my childhood game!! now that's cool (or I'm becoming a geek)

And for others, I see that repos have uae amiga emulator but I went to their site (above post) and got the binary, untarred it and just did ./uae in the directory where I untarred it. and the emulator opened up. no installation, no compilation. but you will need an Amiga ROM to work this thing. Depending on your country, you can either download it or purchase it (copyright issues as usual). I personally prefered to wait some time...

than you go to [www.ko-gathering.com/wiki/index.php?title=Download](www.ko-gathering.com/wiki/index.php?title=Download) and get the adf image file (the emulator sees this as a floppy) and use the image from inside the emulator to play the game. very simple. their wiki faq page also has some nice information. 

again, cool.

for those geeks who'd like to know, this game is from Dino Dini & Anco. Copyright belongs to Dino Dini but he apparently doesn't care what you do with the software (as long as it is kept alive). 

PS. what if someone reprogrammed this as a 3D Linux-only socer game... wish I were a programmer... and have a 3d working ati card ;)
