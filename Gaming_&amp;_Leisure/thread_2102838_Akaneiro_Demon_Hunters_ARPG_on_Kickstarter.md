### Post by rudeboyskunk on 2013-01-08
Yeup, another game on Kickstarter that's promising a Linux port *if* they meet their stretch goal (original goal: $200,000; Linux stretch goal: $233,000).  I'm kind of annoying recently by the Kickstarter games making Linux a stretch goal, but most of them make it that far if they promise it, so I can't complain.

Anyway, this game is a free-to-play action role-playing game with a feudal Japan-style feeling to it.  It's inspired by the classic Little Red Riding Hood story.

They're pretty close to being Greenlit on Steam as well, so thanks to the Linux client it shouldn't be too hard for us to play.
