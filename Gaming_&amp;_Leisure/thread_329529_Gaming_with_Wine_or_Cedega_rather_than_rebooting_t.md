### Post by Sammi on 2007-01-02
Both Wine and Cedega are in my opinion very good at running OpenGL based Win games, but DirectX support is not very good.

You can look through Wine's application database to see how the game or program performs, that you want to use: [http://appdb.winehq.org/](http://appdb.winehq.org/)
