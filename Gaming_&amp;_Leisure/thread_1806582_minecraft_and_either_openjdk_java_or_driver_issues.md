### Post by Jepic Phail on 2011-07-18
As far as I know nouveau only supports 2D. You won't be able to run Minecraft even if you have installed experimental 3D support. You will have to install closed sourced, proprietary video driver provided by Nvidia from the Additional Drivers.

If your Minecraft is crashing for reasons other than your graphics driver, try launching Minecraft from a terminal and see what kind of error message it produces. You can post your error message [here]("http://www.minecraftforum.net/forum/10-support/") or [here]("http://getsatisfaction.com/mojang") for more help.
