### Post by charlieg on 2009-04-02
Your choices are somewhat limited.

You have plenty of open ended Nethack-likes.  I recommend IVAN:
[http://ivan.sourceforge.net/](http://ivan.sourceforge.net/)

There is the Dungeon Hack project to create a spiritual successor to the Elder Scroll: Daggerfall game:
[http://dungeonhack.uesp.net/](http://dungeonhack.uesp.net/)

Scourge has randomly generated levels, and soon will have randomly generated NPCs:
[http://www.scourgeweb.org/](http://www.scourgeweb.org/)
