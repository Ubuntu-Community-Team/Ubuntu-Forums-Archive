### Post by jimboberella on 2005-11-07
In my experience signal 11 is usually heat related, possibly a memory error, but usually processor of gfx card overheating. Try running with the case side/lid off and see if you still get the error. If you dont then consider getting a fan or better HFS for your cpu.
