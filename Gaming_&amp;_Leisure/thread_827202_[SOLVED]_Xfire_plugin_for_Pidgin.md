### Post by Sleaka J on 2008-06-13
Download the DEB file and double click on it. Click on "Install Package" and it's installed.

Then you need to open Pidgin click on "Accounts" and "Manage". Choose "Add" and select "Xfire" as the protocol. Put in your Xfire username and password and click on "Save".
