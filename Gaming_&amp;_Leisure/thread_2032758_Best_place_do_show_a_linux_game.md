### Post by vexorian on 2012-08-03
As long as your game is FOSS, I learned that just letting LGDB and [freecode.com](http://freecode.com/) is enough to let dominos spread all over the community. Even when your game is rather mediocre (e.g. mine). Somehow things like playdeb or even the ubuntu software center end up picking it eventually after all the dominos fall out without you doing much effort.

I think imho, making it cross platform and trying to compete with windows or iOS or Android games is necessary to reach the more casual part of the Linux community. These sites that spread news about Linux news, how many people do you know that use them? So maybe you could also try posting about it in these forums as they at least have plenty of people. I dunno if it is even allowed in the rules though.
