### Post by Bölvaður on 2012-12-17
> **Jakin said:**
> Maybe they will port the new Nexuiz 2012 to linux. Here's hoping...
It runs on CryEngine 3 which THQ will not port to Linux for two good reasons. They do not own it, and the cost of porting a whole engine is too expensive for a test to see if the cost benefits of the port would be positive.
<added>
Then obviously if they get a positive findings out of cost/benefit analysis there is nothing in the way of CryTech to port the engine to Linux. But then THQ will have to evaluate if it is profitable to go back to their previously released games and re-release them for Linux. We have seen tonnes of old games being ported to Linux, with more competition on the market it might not be as profitable to port old games any more.
But as [holastickboy]("http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=969773") pointed out, the future is bright. CryTech may well go into openGL and Linux in the future. But that doesn't mean gaming companies are going to bother with their old games again, but use their time on new releases that would perhaps include Linux in the picture.
</added>
