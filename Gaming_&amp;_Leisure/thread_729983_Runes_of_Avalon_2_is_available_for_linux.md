### Post by Perfect Storm on 2008-03-20
Anawiki Games, the maker of Runes of Avalon (also for linux), have now released Runes of Avalon 2 which are also for Linux.

> Match magical runes on a quest through Avalon!

Evelyne has beaten Morganna la Fay and now it's time to find way home. To find a way on the Path of Magic.

A stunning puzzle-driven game with addictive game play. As, Evelyn the Protector of Avalon, you must make your way through Avalon with over 100 challenging levels, 10 image puzzles, and 2 bonus games. Match magical runes to strengthen your abilities to clear Avalon of evil and find your way home before its too late!

Game features:

    * 100 great levels
    * Amulet of Spells with 4 new spells
    * 6 power-ups
    * 10 new enchanting images to reveal
    * Three game modes: Quest, Endless and Time Attack
    * JigSaw mini-game
    * Mystic music & sound effects 

[http://www.anawiki.com/game/runes-of-avalon-2](http://www.anawiki.com/game/runes-of-avalon-2)
