### Post by oldrocker99 on 2011-10-31
Shrapnel Games, publishers of the (GET IT) Dominions 3, have made a somewhat cut-down version of their wargame WINSPWW2, which means Windows (as opposed to DOS) Steel Panthers World War 2. Gamers as old as I may recall Steel Panthers as a superior wargame, back in the day.

It's FREE, too:

[http://www.shrapnelgames.com/Camo_Workshop/WW2/WW2_page.html](http://www.shrapnelgames.com/Camo_Workshop/WW2/WW2_page.html)

It runs just fine under wine, by the way.

### Post by weasel fierce on 2011-11-01
There's a modern day counterpart, Win Steel Panthers Main Battle Tank.


It's quite an intense game. Definately very old school and hardcore, but with TONS of stuff. The wealth of detail is amazing, and campaign play is insanely fun. Currently playing a Soviet vs NATO long campaign.


There's another Steel Panthers remake out there too, SP World at War, but this one from Shrapnel Games is quite superior.
