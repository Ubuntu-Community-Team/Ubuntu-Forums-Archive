The wired 360 Gamepad works out of the box with Linux and 3rd Party Pads *should* work also fine.

From my experience I can also tell you that the Sixaxis, Wiimote/Classic Controller, any Playstation 2 Pad with Adapter, Competiton Pro USB and many Sidewinder and Logitech Gamepads work also great under Linux.

At the end of the Day it just comes down to which style of Gamepad you prefer.
