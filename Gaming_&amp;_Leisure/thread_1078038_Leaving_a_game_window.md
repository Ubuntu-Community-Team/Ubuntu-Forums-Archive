### Post by cguy on 2009-03-03
Although late, here's the answer:

For Tremulous and other quake 3 engine based games, press Alt+Enter to enter windowed mode, then open the console (tilda key ~). (or open the console first, then press alt+enter)

This also works in many Windows games, but it's mainly dependent on how the game was conceived.

### Post by jlaw6402 on 2009-03-03
