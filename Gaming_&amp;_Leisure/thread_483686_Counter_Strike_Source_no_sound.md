### Post by Jordan777 on 2007-06-25
Ok I just got mine to work and I'm using an onboard sound with my mobo.  What you need to do is go to terminal and type winecfg then go into audio.  Then what i did was clicked all the sound options including emulate drivers and now my sound works.  Sorry I'm not too technical about this stuff but it worked for me.  Hope it does for you too.
