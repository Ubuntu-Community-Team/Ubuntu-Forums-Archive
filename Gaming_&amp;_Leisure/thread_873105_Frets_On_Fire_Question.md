However, no one actually plays the core game.  We all play one of the great mods like alarians (sp) hot coffee, or one of the others. Its too much work to put the mods in the repo's, and to keep them updated, not to mention that the mods have spotty linux binarys (it can sometimes take weeks until someone comes along and compiles it), and since they are unsupported, they sometimes are buggy, or dont work at all.
