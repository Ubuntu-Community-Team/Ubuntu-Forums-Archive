### Post by timzak on 2007-06-29
Toss up.  The XFX will be marginally faster out of the box for $5 more due to it slightly higher core and memory clocks (not counting the $15 mail in rebate on the EVGA).  If you have the patience for mail in rebates, then the EVGA is the better deal.  Otherwise, the XFX gets a slightly higher customer review ratings (96% 4 and 5 ratings vs. 92%).

I think either will serve you well in a pure Linux environment.
