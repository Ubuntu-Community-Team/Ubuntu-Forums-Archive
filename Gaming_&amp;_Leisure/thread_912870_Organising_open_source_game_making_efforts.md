### Post by dagoth_pie on 2008-09-07
Hey everyone


 A few days ago I read through a thread about how there were no original open source Linux games, also, many people said that there were no origina games at all, even commercial windows games, are all a close upgrade of a predecessor. 

 So the past few days I've been thinking, and I've come to a conclusion, game companies, won't risk funds, trying something new, so any original gaming ideas have to come from the open source community as we must have thousands of people with an intricate Idea for a game, 
however, those people don't have the skills/time to make a game, as seeing as even if a programmer came up with an idea, he alone would most likely give up on it, after a year or two of achieving nothing (it's happened many times before). 

 So I've come up with a plan, to start an open source game development community, this way there would be a place for people to gather, maybe a team of programmers and artists, could meet and assemble aggreeing on what type of game they'd like to make, finding an idea, from among their group, or from suggestions. 
 Then they would (at least according to my logic :)) be more likely to have the motivation to complete the project.
 These games would all be open source (hopefully even game models and textures, etc) so that then they could be used to help speed along other game developments, in a way that it wouldn't look too copied, but woud cut down the workload required to make a game. 

In all honesty it's my hope that this idea/community will grow into something, that eventually has games (such as an mmorpg with premium content) that will generate revenue, thus allowing some developers to work full time on games. 

I'm not too hopeful of it reaching that stage yet, I figured it may as well be posted somewhere it'll get plenty of people looking, so anyone interested or who can see a fatal flaw in this, leave a comment.



**EDIT:**

I didn't put much emphasis on this, but the main point in this is, not just to make open source games that are to the quality of modern commercial games, mainly. 
It's to let a flow of original ideas flow into the gaming world, I honestly have gotten board with most games, because so many games these days, are just a slightly evolved clone from their predecessor. 

I do have an idea for a game that I actually think would be quite good with good effects and stuff (its a online team based multiplayer wargame, where its supposed to be realistic (as in camo will actually help hide you) so both teams would have to stratagise and coordinate themselves, to defeat their opponents, rather that just charging in and killing them(those games are too fast paced for me)). 
I do have much more detailed ideas on this, but if you want to know, ask me, I've rambled on enough :lolflag:
