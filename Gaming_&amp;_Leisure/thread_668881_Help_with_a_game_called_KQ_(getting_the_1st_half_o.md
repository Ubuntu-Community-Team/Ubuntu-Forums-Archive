### Post by acoustibop on 2008-01-16
No, KQ isn't a King's Quest remake, steevols, more a tribute.

@Smatt454: there are black dots and white dots in the areas you can access.  They are stones: pick them up and stick them in the holes on either side of the doors upstairs: certain combinations in certain doors will open doors; sometimes the same one, sometimes a different one.

BTW you should be aware that KQ is not a finished game: you'll eventually reach a point in the game where this will become obvious and you can't continue (although you may still have quests to complete).  If you're Ok with this, just carry on: it's great fun 'till you get to where you can't do any more.
