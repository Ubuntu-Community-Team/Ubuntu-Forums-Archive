Ditto on all that. Especially the dual boot part. If playing Windows games is an important requirement to you, then you are not ready to give up Windows and switch completely to Linux yet. You should dual-boot to give yourself the best of both worlds. That's what I do now, at least until Wine matures to the point that it can play all my Windows games. :)

EDIT - Also, just to expand on hikari's statement, "Linux does not, can not, and will not use DirectX ever"... however Wine does have DirectX functionality built into it that is nearly fully compatible with DirectX 9. So you can play DX-based games on Linux through Wine, there just is no "Linux version" of DirectX
