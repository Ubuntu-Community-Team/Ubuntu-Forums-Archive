It would be a computer that didnt do anything but play games.....it would undercut the systems on the market, be compatible with the logitech or whatever generic USB joysticks on the market for PC gaming, have access to Ubuntu gaming for free, let you download lots of PC only MMORPG as if it were a PC, but with the comfort of playing on a joystick on your TV.....It wouldnt FEEL like a linux desktop, it would feel like a gaming console, but it would be linux on some generic hardware with a tweaked OS for gaming only....and it would LOOK like a gaming console and fit under your television in your entertainment system. People who only use Windows for their PC would buy it for the gaming.

Considering that Linux is free to use, I think that production could be affordable with great results....and as an added bonus, this would create a lot of games that we could play natively on linux.
