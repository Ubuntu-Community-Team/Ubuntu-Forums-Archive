### Post by ewangr on 2006-12-27
Greetings all. It's been a while since I've been on the forums, and I wanted to share the game I just finished creating with y'all.

Senior Year is a fully voiced, original english language visual novel. It follows a protagonist (you), as you go through your last year of college. It is a story of love, relationships, and fun. Senior Year was coded using the RenPy engine, and weighs in at just under 83 megs. Playing time is between 2 and 2 1/2 hours for all the paths.

More information on the game (screenshots, DL locations, etc) can be found at [www.bklovr.com](www.bklovr.com).

Enjoy!
