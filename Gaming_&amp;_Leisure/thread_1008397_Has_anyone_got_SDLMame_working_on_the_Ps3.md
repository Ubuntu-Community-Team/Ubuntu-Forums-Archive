### Post by notoriousdbp on 2008-12-11
I've always got on well with Generator as an emulator for the Mega Drive although Gens is also available but I found that a bit CPU hungry.

As for Mame, if you install kxmame through Synaptic it works well as a front end.  You'll have to configure the right directories for your roms and make sure they are left in the zip archive or they won't work.  You'll also need to do an audit of all games so it knows which ones you've got and which ones work.
