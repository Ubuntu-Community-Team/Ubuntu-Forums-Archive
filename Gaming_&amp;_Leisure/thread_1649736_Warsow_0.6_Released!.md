### Post by Irritant on 2010-12-27
> **_outlawed_ said:**
> Game netcoding is designed to lower latencies, granted you aren't having ISP issues.

Ping itself can only be improved to a certain point by netcode.  Good netcode will minimize the effects of poor ping however.  You cannot really test the ping difference of one game compared to another unless they are both running on the same server.  From my location, I noticed most Warsow Euro servers having similar pings that I see in other games, but there were a couple(some duel servers in London) that I got a bit lower pings, presumable they are located on servers with exceptionally good connections.  Ping is simply the speed that a packet gets from the client to the server, so there is only so much you can do to lower actual ping with netcode, since it's mostly a factor of something external to the game itself.

That said, the Warsow netcode is very good :)
