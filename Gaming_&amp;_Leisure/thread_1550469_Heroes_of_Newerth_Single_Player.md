### Post by MaximB on 2010-08-11
HoN is an online MULTIPLAYER game, so there is no single player action apart from the Tutorial.
Recently they had a free to play week, you could have tried it for yourself as there is no demo ;)
But yeah the game is well worth the lousy $30.
Since I've bought an account I can't stop playing the game.
$30 is very cheap for an online game as it's non recruiting, you pay once - play forever, unlike other online games where you pay each month.
  
Minimum requirement (google) :
Processor: Pentium 4 2.2 Ghz or Athlon XP 2200+
Ram: 1 GB
Video Card: GeForce 5 and ATI 9800 w/ 128 MB VRAM or Intel w/ PS 2.0 and 128 MB VRAM .

Although I have never tried to play on that kind of machine I recommend to have at least 2GB of RAM (I have 4).

P.S
Did I mention it has a native GNU/Linux 64-bit client ?
