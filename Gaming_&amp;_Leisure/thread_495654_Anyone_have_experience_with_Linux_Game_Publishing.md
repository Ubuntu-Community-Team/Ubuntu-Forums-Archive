### Post by the_eternal_dark on 2007-07-08
Yes, get Cold War and Ballistics if you can. 

Cold War is fun. Think of the Splinter Cell games, but in first person. One of the best I've played. Pick it up, you'll love it. 

Ballistics reminded me of Extreme G for the N64. It is wicked fun. The graphics are pretty good, but not outstanding by any means. Pick this up if you like racing.

The prices aren't bad at all either, IF you can find them, though they may still sell them both via their website.
