### Post by Mookawaka on 2007-10-27
I recently upgraded to 7.10 and was pleased to find many new easy to install games with the improved ADD/REMOVE.

I am having a problem though that the FPS games have a tendency to lock up on me for reasons I do not understand.  I will be running around blowing things up in SAUERBRATEN or TREMULOUS and then the game will suddenly minimize to a small screen and I cannot do anything except log out of Ubuntu.

Where should I be looking to tinker and solve these crashes?

---

### Post by Mookawaka on 2007-10-28
It appears that having the fancy desktop effects enabled while you have graphic heavy games running makes things get twitchy.  I turned off the effects and problem solved.
