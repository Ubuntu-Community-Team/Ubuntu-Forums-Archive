### Post by illu45 on 2008-05-05
Looking at the possible downloads, you need to know your processor type and version of Ubuntu. 

To find out if you have a 64- or 32-bit processor, go to System -> Administration -> System Monitor and select the "System" tab. Under 'Hardware' it should say something along the lines of "Processor 0: Intel(R) Pentium 4 @ 2.00 Ghz." Please type out this line and post it here. If it does not say 64-bit or x64 anywhere, then you probably have a 32-bit processor. 


To find out what version of Ubuntu you're running, Go to System -> About Ubuntu and look for a line that says something like "Thank you for your interest in Ubuntu 8.04 - the Hardy Heron - released in April 2008". If it says "Ubuntu 7.10 - Gutsy Gibbon", then download the Gutsy 32-bit or 64-bit .deb (depending on your processor type) , if it says "Ubuntu 8.04 - Hardy Heron" then download the Hardy 32-bit or 64-bit .deb.
