### Post by sakuramboo on 2011-04-04
The effort was made. There was a project a long time ago, I think it was called GalaxyMage, or something to that affect. They used the sprite sheet from BfW, but used a square based grid and included elevation. But, it died and since it wasn't on sourceforce or anything like that, it is now lost.

The closest we have right now are [UFO2000]("http://ufo2000.sourceforge.net/") and [Silver Tree]("http://www.silvertreerpg.org/"). I played UFO2000, but quickly lost interest when Eschalon came out and haven't picked it back up since. The latter I never heard of until I started looking in linux-links for TBS games.
