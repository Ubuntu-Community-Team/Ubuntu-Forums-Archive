### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-06-04
There are many, many games to choose from.
It really depends what sort of games you like.

If you like FPS games, then try out:
Free: sauerbraten / assault cube / urban terror / nexuiz / world of padman
Non-free: quake1 / quake 3 / quake 4 / unreal tournament 2004

Stategy games: tremulous, battle for wesnoth, lincity

Then there's armagetron advanced, wormux, and frozen bubble.

There's plenty more as well.
Perhaps you should check out:
[http://gaming.gwos.org/doku.php](http://gaming.gwos.org/doku.php) (Ubuntu gamer's arena) for more.
