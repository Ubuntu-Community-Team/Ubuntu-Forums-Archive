### Post by Sega Loco on 2010-06-01
First of all this is highly risky stuff to be talking about (legal issues). Second, an eboot version of a PSX game is designed for play on a PSP, and I am not aware of any way to do EBOOT->ISO, only ISO->EBOOT.
