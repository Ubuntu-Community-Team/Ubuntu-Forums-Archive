### Post by RIchard James13 on 2008-09-07
[Stones of the Gods]("http://andru.2x4.ru/games/sotg.html") it is similar to Gweled but is different in the way you move the "blocks" around. In Gweled you can move horizontally or vertically. In Stones you turn a Quad (four blocks) clockwise or counter clockwise. You still have to match 3 or more "blocks". It is hard to get used to at first but after a while it becomes easy.
