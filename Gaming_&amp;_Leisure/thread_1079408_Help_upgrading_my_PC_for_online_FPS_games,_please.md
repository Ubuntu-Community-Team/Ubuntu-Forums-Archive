### Post by Vadi on 2009-02-24
Yes, the ATI card is a bad idea right now. They're working on improving the drivers, but don't get the card yet - really, my friend got a 300$ ati one, and he has had a *lot* of trouble with it on linux (not to discourage ati fans out there, it simply happened).

So 4gb ram is good, + some nvidia card - I was told that a 9600 is equivalent would be good. (btw I have an 8600 512mb card - plays oss games on highest settings fine, savage 2 on medium-high. x600 means it's a "medium" power card, x800 means it's a "high-end" card x500 and lower are "low" ones [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nvidia_9600](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nvidia_9600))
