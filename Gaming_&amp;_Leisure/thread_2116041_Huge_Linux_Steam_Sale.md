Hehe, you really must be new to this if you've never heard of Steam.

Steam is basically one of the biggest platforms which enables you to buy and download video games, typically at a lower price than your average retailer does. It also manages your licenses for them. This not only enables you to play your games on whatever computer you are, but also eliminates the need to buy a fancy intrusion protection mechanism to prevent your little brother from stealing your games in your room and potentially destroy them. :C ...sorry, I digress, but I hope it's a bit more clear what Steam is.
