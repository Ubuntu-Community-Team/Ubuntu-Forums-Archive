### Post by bhagabhi on 2009-03-01
DotG is looking for graphics artists. DotG is an opensource version of the classic game Dungeon Master, and I am currently looking for graphics artists that would like to provide open graphics. 

I have made the decision to only accept contributions that are truly open, so if you are an artist that release your stuff under open licenses, this is a chance to get your work some visibility. The cpp sourcecode is released under GPL, and I accept graphics that are released under a similarly open license. (Creative Commons seems to be popular amongst graphics-artists.) The reason for this is of course that I would like to distribute DotG in linux-distros and similar which wouldn't be possible if I would accept non-"open" contributions.

See the following links for more info:
[http://dotg.sf.net](http://dotg.sf.net)
[http://sourceforge.net/projects/dotg/](http://sourceforge.net/projects/dotg/)
