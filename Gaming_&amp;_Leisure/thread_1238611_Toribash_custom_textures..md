### Post by dragos240 on 2009-08-12
For those who haven't heard of it, it's a ragdoll game that uses the joints of the human body to fight. Very addictive ;), anyways, in version 3.8 you now have the ability to create your own custom textures. In the toribash forum there are many "shops", that "sell" textures for your head, legs, and other parts of your body. So it's very customizable. The only drawback I see is that to use a custom texture you need 2000 tc or toricredits, you normally win 10 a match, so it'll take a while :p. I wanted your opinions on my textures :P
