### Post by acoustibop on 2008-03-26
Try joystick (in the repositories; it installs 2 command line utilities, jscal and jstest) to establish whether your controller is working properly.

Do not use jscalibrator: it often disables controls in game controllers, particularly directional ones, for other applications, while still reporting them as working itself.
