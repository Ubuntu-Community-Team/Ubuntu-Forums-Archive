Hey guys, recently Nvidia released the driver which has a feature built in for livestreaming to sites like twitch and hitbox. I am curious if the Linux Nvidia driver has shadow play?

UPDATE: i looked into this and it's currently NOT supported within the linux nvidia driver, open source or proprietary. I read a comment from a person that stated, "Wayland (replacement for X Server) allows something similar for people with Intel IGPs in addition to their discrete graphics, so you also get recording at minimal performance cost. The "shadow" feature should be trivial to add." So maybe it's possible that nvidia and wayland may someday have something similar.
