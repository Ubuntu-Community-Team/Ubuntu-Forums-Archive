### Post by Crazedpsyc on 2010-04-19
try installing padevchooser and pulseaudio from the synaptic if you don't have them. then run the pulseaudio device chooser from Applications>Sound & Video. it will make an applet appear in your notification area on the panel. right click and select volume control. then choose the playback tab at the top and start the game. it should show a sound stream with the name of the game. make sure the volume is turned all the way up and unmuted, then if it still doesnt work, click the down arrow in next to the volume control and change the device you want it to play on. Still not working? try in the game meny going through the options to find sound control.
