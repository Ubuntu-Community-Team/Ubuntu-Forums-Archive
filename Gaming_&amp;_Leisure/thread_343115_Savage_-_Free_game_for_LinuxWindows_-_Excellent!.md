### Post by futz on 2007-01-21
SAVAGE: The Battle for Newerth
[http://www.s2games.com/savage/index.php](http://www.s2games.com/savage/index.php)

It's sort of a cross between a FPS and a RTS. Has some of both styles of gameplay. It's an online multiplayer game. No single player mode as far as I can see. Lots of swords and arrows and magic, but there's guns too.

I'm a total noob at this game, but I'm havin fun so far. Pretty nice game for the price. :biggrin: 

Updating automatically doesn't work on either of two machines I tried it on. It makes like it's doing something and says it's restarting, but it never restarts.

But you can do the update easily by just going to the Savage/autoupdater directory and unpacking newmain-3T+-linux.zip into the Savage directory. What newmain-3T+-linux-libs.zip does I'm not sure yet.
