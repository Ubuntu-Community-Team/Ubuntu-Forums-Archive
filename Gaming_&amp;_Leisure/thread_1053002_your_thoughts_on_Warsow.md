### Post by Bölvaður on 2009-01-29
Warsow is in my opinion the best FPS out there. The weapon balance is quite good, specially because you can pick up large variety of weapons and "power ammo" for them, which makes the game play much more competitive than most FPSs.
But it is very difficult game to get into as it is based upon trickjumps and special movements... they even have 2 different movement systems to gain speed :S

I used to play instagib but now Im very much into normal weapons mode :) Still learning the maps but it is well good.

As for Quake Live, we will probably not get to play it for a LONG time. Even though it is browser based it is run on a plugin. Which would be fine if Linux would be on top of ID's list of getting that plugin...
Im looking at RAGE rather than Quake Live

### Post by compiledkernel on 2009-01-29
To the OP -- 

Warsow reminded me of what I like to often time call the UT-Shakes. The required jumping, wall jumping, and structure bounding thats required in that game (much as the same as in UT2003/2004 style play) tends to make me walk away from a terminal with the jitters. That be said the engine is quite attractive (even in its Neo-Tron era look). The game itself is certainly wide arrayed in the number of available game types , which is always a plus. The Mid-Air game style reminds me of tryin to midair with a disc gun in my Tribes2 days (which Im fond of having remembered being able to do, now if I just find a game that lets you drop a satchel charge and blow people up, id really be happy). 

Pluses and minuses aside, I will go into a warsow server on occasion, but Im with Detrate, I still like Nexuiz a bit better.
