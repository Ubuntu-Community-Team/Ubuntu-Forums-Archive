VERY retro I might add. Those, like me, who are ancient enough to have played games on the Spectrum (I think...Alzheimer's is setting in) or the Commodore 64 might remember Zaxxon. I just came across a rather playable Linux-clone of it, if someone would like to try (might be old news to many...).

[http://www.allegro.cc/files/depot/1503/zaxrr.wip.0.4.125.tgz](http://www.allegro.cc/files/depot/1503/zaxrr.wip.0.4.125.tgz)

Not open source, and installation couldn't be made simpler. Just unpack and click on the executable. Have fun!
