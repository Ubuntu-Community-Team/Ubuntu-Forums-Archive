### Post by glaze on 2010-06-20
Welcome to the club. I've been playing for several years and have ascended a couple of times. Here's some tips:
* If you're in a bad situation, stop and think. There's no hurry.
* Identify scroll is much cheaper than others.
* You can identify some wands by engraving on the floor with them.
* You can identify some wands by zapping monsters with them.
* Potions costing < 100 are safe to drink, but be careful if you drink booze because then you'll easily hit friendly creatures.
* You can drop items to altars (_) to see their BUC status (blessed/uncursed/cursed).
