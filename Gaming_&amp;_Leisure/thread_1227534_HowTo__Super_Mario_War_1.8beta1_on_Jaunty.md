### Post by williamts99 on 2009-07-30
I wanted to try out the latest beta of [Super Mario War]("http://smw.72dpiarmy.com")

There are two mirrors listed in the post for the beta [http://forum.72dpiarmy.com/viewtopic.php?f=1&t=8843]("http://forum.72dpiarmy.com/viewtopic.php?f=1&t=8843")

All I had to do with the archive was extract it to root, created a launcher for SMW at /usr/games/smw and the final step was to install libpng3.
I used sudo apt-get install libpng3 and all is working well.

Please report all bugs to the [Bugs Post]("http://forum.72dpiarmy.com/viewtopic.php?f=1&t=8844")
