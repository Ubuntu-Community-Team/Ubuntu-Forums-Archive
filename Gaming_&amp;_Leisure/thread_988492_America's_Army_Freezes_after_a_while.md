### Post by shade73 on 2008-11-26
So by freezing do you mean it hard locks your system, and you have to shut it down, or just that you have to go into your system monitor/ps to kill it?  If it's hard freezing on you I would say it could possibly be a heat issue.  If it's a process thing, then it could be the driver you're using for your graphics card.  

Try checking your logs to see what it's doing when it crashes, and try running the game over the command line without the & to see if you can see the error output as it freezes (or at least the last line).

That's all I have, but hopefully it will help.
