### Post by mikedep333 on 2008-12-23
Give us more details. Exactly what model is your monitor? How did you get the details of what to enter in the manual xorg.conf?

Please note that you say your full/native resolution is 1280x1024, but you have your manual xorg.conf include 1280x800, which is the wrong aspect ratio and may be the source of your trouble.

You would presumably want the lines of your xorg.conf to look like this.

```
		Modes		"1280x1024" "1280x960" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"

```

Also, please note that you have the following line:
```
	Device		"Intel GMA 950"

```
The intel GMA 950 is the model from before the x3100. Seeing that kind of makes me worry.

Also, we need to make sure the following lines are correct, which is why we need the model of your monitor.
```
	HorizSync	30-70
	VertRefresh	50-160

```
