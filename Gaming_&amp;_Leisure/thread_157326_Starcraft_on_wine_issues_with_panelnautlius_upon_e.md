### Post by Toxicity999 on 2006-04-11
Set a hotkey for fullscreen mode in the config of Ubuntu... run starcraft in windowed mode and press the fullscreen hotkey... now its appearing in fullscreen but in a nice native way. Should help as windows programs do funky things to resolution positions and such.
