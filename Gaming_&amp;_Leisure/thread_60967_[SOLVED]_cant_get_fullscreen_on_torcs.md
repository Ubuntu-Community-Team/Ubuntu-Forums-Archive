### Post by thomasleveil on 2006-03-01
You can put it full screen "manually" by assigning a keyboard shortcut.
Go to 
**System > Preferences > Keyboard shortcuts**
then look for the section [B]Window management > Toggle fullscreen Mode

[/B]I choose <*Ctrl*><*F11*> as <*F11*> was kind of conflicting with firefox.

_Note:_ to remove a shortcut, assign the *Backspace key* to it, as written in the hint a the bottom of the page.
