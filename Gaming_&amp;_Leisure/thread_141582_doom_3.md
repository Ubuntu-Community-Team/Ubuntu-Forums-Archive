### Post by audax321 on 2006-03-08
REQUIREMENTS: 3D Acceleration Configured, Full Version of DOOM3 (needed for the .pk4 files in step 3)

1. Download the installer from ID's FTP (the non-demo one): [ftp://ftp.idsoftware.com/idstuff/doom3/linux/](ftp://ftp.idsoftware.com/idstuff/doom3/linux/)

2. Run the installer and finish the base install.

3. Follow the instructions under the section "Patch Installation Instructions" to complete the install. ([http://zerowing.idsoftware.com/linux/doom/](http://zerowing.idsoftware.com/linux/doom/))
