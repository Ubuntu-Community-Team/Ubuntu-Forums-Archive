### Post by Sef on 2006-04-09
Use Wine (Windows Emulator.)  To download it: System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager > Search (type in Wine) > highlight Wine > apply

Note: not all games will play on wine.  LInk to Wine:

[http://www.winehq.com/]("http://www.winehq.com/")
