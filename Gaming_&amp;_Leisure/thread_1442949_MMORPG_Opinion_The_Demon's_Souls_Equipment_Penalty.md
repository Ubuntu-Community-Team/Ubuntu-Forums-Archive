### Post by Psumi on 2010-03-30
Normally, MMORPGs have a level requirement system that forbids you from equipping the armor/weapons at all. Demon's Souls however does it the smart way: Issue a stat penalty on the equipment itself to lower its stats according to your level/stats.

So I ask people who play MMORPGs, would it be better for all MMORPGs to follow this trend? Or should they keep the level requirements? I'm for the Demon's Souls method.
