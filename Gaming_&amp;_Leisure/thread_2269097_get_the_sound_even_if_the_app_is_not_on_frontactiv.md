### Post by sffvba[e0rt on 2015-03-15
> **cosy2 said:**
> i really find this annoying that i lose sound if the app is not in a active window just becuase on the second monitor the active window is Firefox

This would depend from application to application.  In some games you can specify that you want full-screen windowed mode as opposed to only full screen and that often results in the sound staying persistent even when not having focus.  Other games you can specify in the sound options that you want this behaviour.

I am not aware of a way to force this regardless of application.
