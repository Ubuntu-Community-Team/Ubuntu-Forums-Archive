### Post by Melcar on 2010-02-08
SDLmame tends to work better for me hardware wise; sound does not pop or skip, and my Saitek pads work.  Recent versions of SDLmame have problems with many of my ROMs though, so I have to resort to a rather outdated build; reading through the net seems to indicate this being a common issue with old ROM files and newer builds of the emulator.
As for frontends, I don't know why you would need one.  Configuration is fairly simple and the default browser is good at listing all your available games.
