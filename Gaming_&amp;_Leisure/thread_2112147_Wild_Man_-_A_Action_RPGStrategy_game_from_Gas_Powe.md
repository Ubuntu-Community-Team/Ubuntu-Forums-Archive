Hey all!

I am backing a project on Kick starter by Gas Powered Games called[ Wildman]("http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/gaspoweredgames/wildman-an-evolutionary-action-rpg"). The game is planned to be on Windows, Linux, and MacOSX. Currently as it stands Mac and Linux are Stretch goals.

[IMG]https://s3.amazonaws.com/ksr/projects/417840/posts/399157/image-208370-full.jpg?1360035389[/IMG]

Wildman is a Single player/Cooperative play Action RPG/RTS hybrid.

If you are not familiar with Gas powered games they have developed Dungeon Siege, Supreme Commander, Total Annihilation, and Demigod just to name a few.

Also there is a live stream @ [http://www.twitch.tv/gaspoweredgames/new](http://www.twitch.tv/gaspoweredgames/new) - That is offering allot of insight into the development process of Game development in general.

The Next Stream will start @ 12:00 PM PST on Monday the 4th of February. There will be a live Q&A with Chris Taylor the CEO of Gas Powered Games.
