### Post by @B6Mwu8fN9M on 2009-11-03
Go to your home folder. CTRL+H to view hidden folders. Find the folder ".spring" . Enter it. If there isn't a folder called "mods", make it. Download the mod you want and save it to the folder you just made. 

More info is here: [http://springrts.com/wiki/Template:Finalizing_linux_install]("http://springrts.com/wiki/Template:Finalizing_linux_install")

Hope that helped:D
