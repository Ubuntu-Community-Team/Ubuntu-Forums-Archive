### Post by Ericj1186 on 2009-07-22
Savage is a great game (in fact the sequel is a blast too).  So I highly recommend those.  Thanks to lack of marketing, and lack of interest, the game is now free, but I can't see there being any future releases.  Regardless, get it. It's fun.

Planeshift looks like it could be great, but I really feel MMOs are dead thanks to WoW.  Unless you are WoW, you probably won't tap into the thriving MMO market, and unless you offer something entirely unique (ala EVE), you won't be tapping into the former MMO players.  Non-MMO players probably don't play for a lot of reasons such as hating to grind and paying to play.

Now, if you can get a copy of Ultima Online, THAT is an mmo worth playing (Free Shards only, EA destroyed everything good about that game).
