### Post by alexan on 2010-11-14
To gain more fps during recording:

1. disable compiz (desktop effect)
2. run fullscreen at "tv" resolution (800x600 are fairly enough)
3. record trough tv-out if available (low resolution to 640x)


keep 30~50fps in mind... if during recording you gain more fps: increase graphic details (for massive graphic effect) instead increasing resolution.
