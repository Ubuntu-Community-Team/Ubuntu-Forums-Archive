### Post by handy on 2006-04-26
Have a look at the [**tom's hardware**]("http://www.tomshardware.com/") site.  You should find a great deal of info' on graphix cards, including the ones that you are interested in.

It would seem that whatever chip set (GPU) is used, the difference in performance between manufacturers is negligible.  So if you can get a card that has games bundled with it that you would like, that can help.  Or if you don't want a retail pack with windoze games, you may be able to source an oem card, which will come with only a manual & windoze drivers.  These can be significantly cheaper.

Happy hunting!
