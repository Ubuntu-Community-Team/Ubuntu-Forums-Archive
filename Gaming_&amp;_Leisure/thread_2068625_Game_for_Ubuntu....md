### Post by holastickboy on 2012-10-09
There are lots of great games for Ubuntu.  There are quite a few in the app store, but they may be hard to find.

Keep an eye on [http://humblebundle.com](http://humblebundle.com) for when they release a new pack, you can pay whatever you like and 99% of the games are Linux compatible.

Furthermore, head over to [http://www.playdeb.net/welcome/](http://www.playdeb.net/welcome/) and add their repository to your system. You can then browse the website and add games to your PC by clicking the links on the website.

Good luck!
