I'm beginning to think every single ubuntu FoF player lives in central jersey.  (Including myself)

PS2:
Guitar is ~$40 and the adapter is $8-10.
I use this.  Specifically, I use a Rock Guitar II and the [Radio Shack adapter]("http://www.radioshack.com/sm-usb-adapter-for-playstation-2-controller--pi-2348187.html").  [EDIT: The link I put here has a place where you can check for local store availability, saves you having to call ahead or other such nonsense]

The answer to your first question is no, the only brick and mortar store that appears to carry any sort of adapter is Radio Shack.  You can also find the adapters online, though I would check the [Stepmania USB Adapters List]("http://www.stepmania.com/wiki/USB_Adapters") for compatibility.  Note that while Stepmania obviously requires the ability to make a controller go both up and down and/or left and right at the same time, FoF has no such restriction.

Note, too, that wireless guitar controllers are possible if you go the PS2 route, although I didn't (pikey).

----

Xbox 360 X-Plorer guitars: ~$90.  Yes, as much as the game.  If you can find one. I expect this to drop once demand for the game has settled a tad.  If you could find one for $50 like you say, I would definitely take it.  The last I checked (admittedly quite close to when the game was released) they could be had on ebay for $90-120.

Yes, the X-Plorer works without modification, although per several threads in this forum, Feisty has broken Xbox 360 controller support.  I believe [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=404577&highlight=360") has had the last word there.  Some simple recompilation and you should be good to go.

----

I think that was all your questions!  Please check out [my howto]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=415624") on installing RFMod and generally making FoF spectacular.  Cause seriously, it's better than regular FoF.  Trust me.

Also, rock out.

### Post by tonyr1988 on 2007-12-21
> **Mr.NiceGuy said:**
> ok i bought a ps2 guitar and a usb adaptor. How can i get it to work??

Which USB adapter did you buy? If you bought the Radioshack one, you shouldn't have to do anything - just plug it into the USB port on your computer, connect the guitar, and open FoF.

The only catch is that adapter won't work unless you plug it in *after* your computer is turned on, at least for me. So, you may need to unplug the adapter, restart your computer, plug it back in once logged in, plug in controller, start game. Then go into Settings and set the keys. Tada.
