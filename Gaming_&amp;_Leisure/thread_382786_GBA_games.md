### Post by DoktorSeven on 2007-03-12
VisualBoyAdvance should be available, as well as a multisystem emulator called Mednafen.  (They may be in the extra repositories, search to learn how to enable them).

Mednafen is better since it uses OpenGL to do direct drawing of the screen, making the emulator faster, but it requires launching from commandline, while VBA has a GUI.
