### Post by rick08 on 2008-10-07
[**Open Arena**]("http://openarena.ws/") is an underated fps that is great for online play.  Sure it doesn't have the graphics of UT3 or other fps's but it has great gameplay and its free.  Some other games I like to play regularly is [**Wesnoth**]("http://www.wesnoth.org/") and [**SuperTux**]("http://supertux.lethargik.org/").  There is also [**pSX**]("http://psxemulator.gazaxian.com/") which runs extremely well in Linux.  Most of these games can be found in the repositories.
