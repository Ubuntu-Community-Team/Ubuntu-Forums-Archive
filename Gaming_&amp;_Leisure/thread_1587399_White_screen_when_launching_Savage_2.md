@Fableflame after installing Savage 2 on my Ubuntu I had the White Texture problem, some textures or models are in white. I am not sure if it is the same with your white screen issue, updating the game fixed that issue for me.

You might encounter the update loop all you have to do is set **SetSave upd_checkForUpdates** in the** ~/.savage2/game/startup.cfg** to **"false"**. I found the solution from [http://forums.savage2.com/showthread.php?t=23759](http://forums.savage2.com/showthread.php?t=23759) I think you should be registered on Savage2 forums to view it.
