### Post by oedipuss on 2010-08-23
I haven't tried with this particular game, but in general dosbox is not hard to use. 

Assuming there's a copy of the game in /home/user/thegame , you start up dosbox, use a 'mount /home/user/thegame c:' command to map that folder to the driveletter c:, and just proceed as you would have on DOS. Go to c:, run, setup, whatever each particular game needs.
