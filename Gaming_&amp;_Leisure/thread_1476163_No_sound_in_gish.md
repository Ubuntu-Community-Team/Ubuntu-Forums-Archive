### Post by alienjon on 2010-05-07
Just got my hands on a full copy of Gish but found that the sound wouldn't work.  After searching the Internet I couldn't seem to find anyone with a similar problem so I decided to post here.  After toying around with the game/system sound settings I ended up changing my speaker setup from 5.1 to simply stereo and the sound started to work.  The sound worked fine for other applications, but for this one game it seems that 5.1 will halt all sounds.  I'm in the process of setting up an account with the gish forums to make them aware of it as well.
