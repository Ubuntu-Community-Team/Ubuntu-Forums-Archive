Has anyone experienced this problem? I'm wondering if the problem is my OS or it is the XCOM issue itself.

My System:

I run Ubuntu 16.04 with default kernel 4.4. I checked also the 4.8 kernel and 3.13 (since this is the kernel of the only distro officially supported by XCOM / Feral Interactive which is Ubuntu 14.04)

I have an Nvidia Card and I checked the following proprietary drivers:  378.09, 367.57 and 340.101.

My hardware:

CPU: Athlon X4 640 3000MHz
GPU: GeForce GTX 650 Ti BOOST 2GB
RAM: 8GB DDR2

The game uses all cores of my 4-core CPU in the 90-100% percents. Is it the correct behaviour under Linux?

On the Windows 10 the game runs fine on this machine with the highest resolution and details.

I use XCOM version distributed by Steam.

I have similar freezes in other games since December 2016 or January 2017 but they were a far less frequent in other titles:

- Civilization IV under Steam and under Wine:  it happened one time per hour on the average
- Trine II:  it happened 2 times per hour or so.

Switching to another tty also helped in these games. I didn't have this issue before December 2016 in these two games. I cannot say it about XCOM because I started to play it this week.
