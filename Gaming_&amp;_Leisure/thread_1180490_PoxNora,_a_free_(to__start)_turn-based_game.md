### Post by oldrocker99 on 2009-06-06
I ran it under Java, and it ran perfectly well. You can download the Windows client and run it under wine as well. It's a turn-based game, with revealable "cards" with you deploy onto the battlefield, cast spells, etc. Each game lasts about 30 minutes, your unkilled "champions" get more powerful, and it's free to play for quite a while, and you can purchase new "decks" somewhat reasonably.

It's certainly worth a look at, especially for Battle for Wesnoth or other turn-based strategy game fans.

[http://www.poxnora.com](http://www.poxnora.com)
