### Post by ex00r on 2006-08-01
the latest wine version is 0.9.18 and has many bugfixes and direct3D improvements.

look here for the newest version: [http://www.winehq.com/site/download-deb](http://www.winehq.com/site/download-deb)

basicly add this to your sources.list:

deb [http://wine.budgetdedicated.com/apt](http://wine.budgetdedicated.com/apt) dapper main
deb-src [http://wine.budgetdedicated.com/apt](http://wine.budgetdedicated.com/apt) dapper main

and then update through synaptic/ apt-get ;)
