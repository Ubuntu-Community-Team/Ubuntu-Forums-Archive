### Post by Bek Ohmsford on 2010-09-28
Hey, I just had a quick look at the gaming section here and didn't see any mention of the Penumbra Series or Amnesia: The Dark Descent! These games all natively support Linux/Win/Mac and are developed by Frictional Games. Here is their site:

[http://www.frictionalgames.com/site/](http://www.frictionalgames.com/site/)

The three good games are Penumbra: Overture, Penumbra Black Plague (part 1 and 2 respectively) and Amnesia: TDD Penumbra requiem does not directly continue the events of Black plague and is not as highly regarded.

Basically, these games are amazing - intensely frightening, engaging with a intriguing plot to boot. Personally I rate the experience up there with that of Deus Ex, Mass Effect, STALKER etc.

But don't take my word for it, check out the Amnesia Demo here: [http://www.amnesiagame.com/#demo](http://www.amnesiagame.com/#demo)

(please don't play the black plague demo, has some slight spoilers) I also believe Overture was released open source after the humble indie bundle finished.
