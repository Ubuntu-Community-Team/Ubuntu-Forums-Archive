### Post by MaxIBoy on 2008-11-02
The BIOS is the ROM that holds the operating system of the console.

In a normal computer, the BIOS searches through your hard drives and stuff, and picks one to boot from. The booting hard drive then has your bootloader on it, and the bootloader has a map of all your partitions, so it can give you a menu of operating systems.


In both cases, the BIOS is proprietary. Copying one, or decompiling it, is illegal.
