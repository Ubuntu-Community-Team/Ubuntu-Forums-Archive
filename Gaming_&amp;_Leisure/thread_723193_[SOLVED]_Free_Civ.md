### Post by Ozor Mox on 2008-03-13
I play it. It's one of my favourite free and open source games, since I love the Civilization series. After playing the commercial Civilization games, Freeciv may seem like it has a few rough edges, but it is a great game, good for single player against the AI and online multiplayer. The new 2.1 version has very nice looking graphics. There are plenty of multiplayer servers, though not always that many players. There's also the "longturn" community, for one-turn-per-day style games using an unofficial modified build of Freeciv 2.0.

Hope this helps a bit. It's well worth giving a try, especially when it costs nothing to see if you like it!
