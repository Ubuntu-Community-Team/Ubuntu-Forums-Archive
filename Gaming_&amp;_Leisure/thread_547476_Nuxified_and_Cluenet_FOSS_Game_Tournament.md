### Post by KIAaze on 2007-09-10
This is already on [http://freegamer.blogspot.com/]("http://freegamer.blogspot.com/") (not yet on [UGA]("http://gaming.gwos.org/doku.php")), but in case some haven't heard of it yet:

> [Nuxified]("http://www.nuxified.org/") and [Cluesoft]("http://www.cluenet.org/") have come together to put together a [tournament]("http://www.nuxified.org/article/nuxified_and_cluenet_prepare_a_gaming_tourney_you_are_invited") using solely Free Software games. They haven't yet chosen the exact games for the tournament so [contribute your opinion]("http://www.nuxified.org/poll/which_games_should_be_selected_for_the_upcoming_tournament") if you want to be playing your favourite game if you want to take part.

You may also suggest FOSS games for the tournament here: [http://www.nuxified.org/topic/multi_player_games_to_be_considered_for_the_tournament]("http://www.nuxified.org/topic/multi_player_games_to_be_considered_for_the_tournament")
