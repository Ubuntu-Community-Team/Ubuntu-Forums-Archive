### Post by cogadh on 2007-08-31
Even cards with a DVI port also have a VGA port (usually). The card that I just got has DVI, VGA and S-video, plus a DVI to VGA adapter, which allows me to hook up two VGA monitors at the same time. The card is not that old (GeForce 7600GS, about a year and half old) and every newer card I have looked at has a VGA port. I don't think they usually list it on the card specs because it is still standard to have a VGA port, while DVI is an optional selling point.
