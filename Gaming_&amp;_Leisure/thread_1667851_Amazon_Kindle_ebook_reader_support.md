### Post by Jazzy_Jeff on 2011-01-16
You can use the Kindle for pc app if you use Wine. It works pretty good. You don't need it to use the Kindle device itself though. Ubuntu treats the Kindle like a usb drive. 

I also use a program called Calibre to manage my e-book library and Kindle device.
