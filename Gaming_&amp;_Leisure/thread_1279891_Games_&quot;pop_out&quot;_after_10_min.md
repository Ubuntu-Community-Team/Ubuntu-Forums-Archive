### Post by Nevon on 2009-10-01
If you'd like to keep your screensaver enabled so that it works when you're not playing any games, you can use [Caffeine](http://www.blastfromthepast.se/caffeine/index.php?title=Main_Page) to inhibit the screensaver only when you're playing games. You can either manually activate Caffeine every time you want it to inhibit the screensaver, or you can set it to do its magic automatically whenever certain applications are running.
