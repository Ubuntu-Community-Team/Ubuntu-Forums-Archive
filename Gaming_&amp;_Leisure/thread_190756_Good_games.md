### Post by Corey on 2006-06-08
[Wesnoth]("http://www.wesnoth.org/") is a fantastic strategy game with great graphics, music etc. 
[Adom]("http://www.adom.de") is a free as in beer roguelike game. I like it much better than nethack, rogue, angband and all those. It has a much more consitent world (no aliens and stupid humor monsters), a great skill and combat system too. I just wish it would be opensourced :(
I'm not a fan of any of the nethack interfaces mentioned here. [Vulture's]("http://www.darkarts.co.za/projects/vultures/") Eye is pretty interesting though, Best non-ascii GUI for a roguelike yet I think. There is also Vulture's Claw which uses Slash'EM instead of nethack. Slash'EM is a patched nethack with more gameplay features.
[Sauerbraten]("http://www.sauerbraten.org/") or "cube 2" is my favorite of the freeware FPS. Its still in Dev but its loads of fun to play. The whole map editor concept is very intriguing as well.
