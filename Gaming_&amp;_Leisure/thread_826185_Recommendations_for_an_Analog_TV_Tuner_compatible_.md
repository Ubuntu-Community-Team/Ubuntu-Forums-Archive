### Post by tkoopa on 2009-03-26
I run a couple of mythtv boxes for recording analog tv in Belgium (PAL-B) and have had great success with Hauppauge cards, specifically a PVR-350 and PVR-500 (the 500 has two tuners, but I find the 350 to have a slightly better image quality). They are both "hardware" frame grabbers rather than software. I started with a Pinnacle PCTV-Pro software card and had many issues with it so I'd recommend avoiding that sort.
