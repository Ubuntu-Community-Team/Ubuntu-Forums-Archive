### Post by Naiki Muliaina on 2010-02-27
Had a look back over the last few days posts didnt see it posted here yet. Linden Labs have put out a native linux client for a long time now, and the client is open source so theres some awesome spin offs. However, after a loLinden Labs have unleashed the new viewer as beta to the world. Lots of changes to UI and a couple under the hood. I have had it working fine except media on 2 seperate PCs, 64 bit Karmic.

To download, see videos, and a picture, look here : [http://secondlife.com/beta-viewer/]("http://secondlife.com/beta-viewer/")

Voice works!!!! Woooooo!!! Off the bat crystal clear and SLVoice even closes when you close SL now!!!!




**Some stuff to be aware of (its Beta, names in the title of the download!**).

Theres a few inventory problems. No inventory losses have been reported as of yet, but it is clear theres bugs there. If your on your main and lose non copy items, tuff poo poo.

It apparently deletes IM logs if uninstalled (windows of course, but incase you have it work better in the MS side)

Media on 64 bit, it sucks. It apparently goes looking for all the 32 bit libs. Someone put out the getlib commands to install the 32 bit gstreamer stuff on a 64 bit system and had semi success. Dont be doing that yourselves unless you either know what your doing or dont mind fudgin up your system. Give it time, someone smart will figure something out >.>

Again ill repeat, its beta, if it blows up or you lose stuff, tuff booboo. If ya fancy giving it a spin and can accept that, give it a go. Its oddly like playing a whole new game :)
