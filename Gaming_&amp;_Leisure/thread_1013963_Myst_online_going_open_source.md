### Post by Brian Vaughan on 2008-12-17
This interests me a great deal. I never played the original Myst, nor its sequels. I have, however, spent a lot of time doing online roleplaying, and real roleplaying, and actual exploration, were always hard to find.

One of the things that held me back from switching to GNU/Linux was gaming, and part of what finally broke me from that was when I realized that my favorite aspect of gaming was collaboration to create richly detailed imaginary worlds -- and that proprietary software and privately copyrighted material were barriers to that, as my experiences with online roleplaying demonstrated again and again.

So, I'd been hoping to find some more collaborative roleplaying in the free and open source world.

I'm curious how they'll handle opening up a storyline and setting that have always been closely guarded secrets.
