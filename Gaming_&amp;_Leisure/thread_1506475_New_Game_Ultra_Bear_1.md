### Post by Simian Man on 2010-06-10
I'm announcing the release of Ultra Bear 1, an open source side-scrolling platform game.  You can [download]("http://code.google.com/p/ultrabear/downloads/list") Ultra Bear 1 for Linux and Windows systems.  There are [screenshots]("http://code.google.com/p/ultrabear/wiki/Screenshots") of the game available as well.

Ultra Bear 1 is a side scrolling platform game in the spirit of the original Super Mario Brothers. You will have to help the Ultra Bear travel to south to Antarctica, defeat the Evil Penguin King, and free the South Pole from penguins! Outwit merciless enemies, navigate treacherous levels, and collect pickups in 30 levels of platforming fun!

Features:
  - 6 worlds to travel through
  - 30 levels of platformming fun
  - Music and sound effects
  - Many types of enemies
  - Smooth, simple graphics
  - An easy to use installer
  - A totally meaningless point system


If you find a bug either in the game itself or in the installation methods, please [let me know]("http://code.google.com/p/ultrabear/issues/list").


Also in case anyone is curious about the fact that the main enemies are penguins, it is in no way anti-Linux.  I started working on this game before having been a Linux user and the concept and character of Ultra Bear actually date back to my childhood.
