### Post by brian62 on 2015-06-03
I did a benchmark comparison between Xfce, Mate, and LXDE with Openbox to see which desktop environment yields the best GPU and game performance. Each of the desktop environments were installed via the default Ubuntu software repositories and benchmarked using GpuTest, MadShaders, Unigine Valley, and War Thunder.

I created a spreadsheet of my findings here - [https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1-FjMWPHq2SpmV6Z3yK35ScVYOG-o7CD9aJn5GSD42Ew/](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1-FjMWPHq2SpmV6Z3yK35ScVYOG-o7CD9aJn5GSD42Ew/)

I also made a video of the benchmarks here - [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nNGG_dUBBU0](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nNGG_dUBBU0)
