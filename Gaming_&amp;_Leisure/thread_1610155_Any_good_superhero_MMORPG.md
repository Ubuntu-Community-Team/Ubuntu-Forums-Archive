### Post by gonesolo on 2010-11-02
WINE is your best bet for Superhero MMO's to be honest.

Currently the main two contenders are 

City of Heroes 

Champions Online.

City of Heroes definatly works with WINE (and runs well) but Champions Online gets only a Silver rating (I've seen sound and mouse issues)

However both games run well on Crossover Games (developed by WINE team but commercial and with support) 

DC Universe Online comes out Q1 2011 however I doubt it will work in Linux from release (even using WINE)
