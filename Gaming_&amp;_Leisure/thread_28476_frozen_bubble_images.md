This might be something I need to address to the developer of this game, but it seems like for every problem someone on here is able to come up with an answer.  Here is my issue:  my son loves to play this game, but unfortunately is color blind.  Distinquishing the blue from purple and the green from yellow bubbles is impossible for him.  I want to know if there is a way to take the bubble images and edit them (maybe with a letter "b" and "p" imbedded in the bubble) as to allow him to tell the bubbles apart and so be able to play the game. ### Post by Burgundavia on 2005-04-20
If you are colourblind, we already have what you're looking for: please use the -cb commandline parameter, and bubbles will be printed with little geometrical symbols inside.

from [http://www.frozen-bubble.org/troubleshooting/](http://www.frozen-bubble.org/troubleshooting/)

You may also want to email [email]contact@frozen-bubble.org[/email] to ask for a gui way to do this. 

Corey
