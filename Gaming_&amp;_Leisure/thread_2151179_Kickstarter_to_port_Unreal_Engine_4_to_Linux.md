### Post by Romeo9 on 2013-06-03
The development team working on Heavy Gear Assault have started a Kickstarter project to port the Unreal 4 Engine to Linux. As we all know there are a ton of AAA games that make use of the Unreal Engine, and if native Linux support with Unreal could have the same affect with AAA games making use of this engine as what the porting of Unity had on Indie games making use of Unity, we'll that could only mean amazing things for Linux and especially Ubuntu as a gaming platform moving forward. Here's the link if any of you guys are interested in donating: [http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/stompybot/heavy-gear-assault-0/posts/492404](http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/stompybot/heavy-gear-assault-0/posts/492404)
