No, unless you ask more specific it will be hard to help :)

An exe (as always) is a Windows-specific file format. If you are happy and they just compressed their weapon mod stuff as selfextracting zip-file, you can extract them directly in Ubuntu. And if they are not:
Then simply use their install exe under Wine, grab the files from there and put them in the Q3A folder.

Typical mods which work with Q3A under Linux nicely are:
Generations Arena, Excessive Overkill.

Google will reveal quickly the download links.
