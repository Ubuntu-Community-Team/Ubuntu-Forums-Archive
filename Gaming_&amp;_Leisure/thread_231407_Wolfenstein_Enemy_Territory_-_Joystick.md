### Post by jgordon510 on 2006-08-08
Found the solution here:

[http://www.planetwolfenstein.com/features/dearhelga/dearhelga_06-01-12_a.shtml](http://www.planetwolfenstein.com/features/dearhelga/dearhelga_06-01-12_a.shtml)

> To get the gamepad to work you need to enable joystick support...

Since there is no way to activate the joystick from the menu system you will have to enable joystick support from the console.

1) While in the game, press the Tilde " ~ " key to bring the console down.

2) Type the following:

/in_joystick 1 (Press the Enter key)

3) Quit the game and restart it. Once the game is restarted you will have joystick support.

This also solves my problem of getting the mouse pointer out of the game when in window mode.
