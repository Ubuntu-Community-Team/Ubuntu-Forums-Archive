### Post by JoelFB on 2011-05-17
Unfortunately the Linux (and Mac) version of Trine does not support multiple keyboards and mice. I think it's got something to do with the version of SDL used, and this most likely won't be fixed. Sorry! (You can always get a refund from the [Humble Bundle](http://www.humblebundle.com/contact) guys if you wish, we probably didn't/don't have enough information available about the differences between the versions.) Sadly the gamepads are quite broken in Linux as well, although some users seem to have had luck in setting them up (I couldn't do it myself yet, but I also did have some trouble downloading the xboxdrv package which seems to help a lot with the Xbox 360 Controller). 

- Joel, Frozenbyte team, developers of Trine
