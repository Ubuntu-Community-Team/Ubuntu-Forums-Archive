### Post by Romeo9 on 2013-01-15
The most popular is probably Open Shot:[Link]("http://www.openshot.org/") The Latest version has a few blender effects incorporated.

If you're looking for something more advanced I would suggest looking at [Cinelerra]("http://cinelerra.org") and [Ramen]("http://ramencomp.blogspot.com/") These two are more in line with Adobe's After Effects.

If none of these programs are exactly what you're looking for here's a list of Video Editors both Commercial and Open Source and the platforms they're available on: [Link]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_video_editing_software")
