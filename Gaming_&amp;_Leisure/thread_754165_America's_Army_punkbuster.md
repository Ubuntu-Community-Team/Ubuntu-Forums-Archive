### Post by noerrorsfound on 2008-04-15
I haven't tried running AA in Wine, but the [listing for version 2.8.3 at the AppDB]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=10748") says:
> 
**Non-PB servers work fine, but PB servers kick within one minute for an unknown Windows API function.** To update PB outside the game, go to your AA installation, then System/pbsetup and execute "wine ./pbsetup.exe". Add America's Army and update it. This will bring PB to the latest version. Sometimes logging in will fail with Wine mentioning a "failed to create stream" error. Removing System/AA.key will fix this.
Also, another annoying bug listed is:
> Mouse still leaves the window.
Some people have submitted patches for the mouse problem (affects many games) but I haven't tried any patches, even though SWAT 4 and Rainbow Six: Raven Shield (games I really wish I could play properly) are also affected by this since they use the Unreal engine.

I recommend just playing 2.5 to avoid the hassle.
