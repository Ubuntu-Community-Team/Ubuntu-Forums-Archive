### Post by acoustibop on 2008-01-30
You won't in Linux, Zzzach.  AFAIK there are no effective Linux rumble drivers.

BTW dump jscalibrator (configuration, too) unless it's the version that's included in Kubuntu.  It has a nasty way of disabling the directional controls of controllers while still showing them as working - but they won't work in anything but jscalibrator.

### Post by acoustibop on 2008-02-01
Look in the repositories for joystick - it includes jstest (controller tester) and jscal (calibration).  They're command line applications: just do jstest --help or jscal --help for simple instructions.
