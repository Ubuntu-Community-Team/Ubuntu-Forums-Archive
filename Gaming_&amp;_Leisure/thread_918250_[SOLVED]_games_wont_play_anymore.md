### Post by jacksaff on 2008-09-12
Your onboard video isn't as powerful as the card you have taken out and this might be your problem.
You also may now be running the open source nv driver (which doesn't support a lot of stuff like 3D) where before you probably had the closed source nvidia driver (which does).

### Post by Perfect Storm on 2008-09-13
As jacksaff said, your motherboard chip isn't powerful enough and can't run 3D video games.

Solution;

Plug-in your 6800 nvidia card and install the restrictive drivers.
