### Post by Abras on 2008-11-03
Turn-based games are probably your best route if you want hold on to more complex games. In addition to UFO:Alien Invasion, I suggest you also take a look at Battle for Wesnoth, FreeCol, FreeCiv, and [possibly others]("http://gaming.gwos.org/doku.php/games:strategy").

Otherwise, you'll be relegated to relatively simplistic game design. Frozen Bubble is a good choice, yes, and I would imagine there are more casual puzzlers that you can control with one hand. Try one of the various Bejeweled clones or Fishie Fishie.

Also, you may be able to get away with playing some platformers, though you'll have to reconfigure the controls on most games. You'll want to stick to platformers that only have arrow keys and a jump button. It might get a little harry with a run or attack key thrown in there, but you may be able to manage.

Anyways, good luck!
