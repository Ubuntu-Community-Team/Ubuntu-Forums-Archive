I've been using linux since 2005 and personally really like what Canonical is doing with Ubuntu. I want to see the Linux desktop in become a choice for the average consumer, I thought I would do what I could in regards to this desire by myself and 2 other people getting together once a week and talking about linux gaming and linux stuffs on a podcast. Since we started the podcast we soon realized that we needed a rss feed for our podcast so others could easily find us if you're into podcast's so that resulted in wanting to create the best website possible to not only host our podcasts rss feeds but to also inform the average consumer about linux stuffs and gaming.
[http://linuxtechandgaming.com]("http://linuxtechandgaming.com") was born. I'm sure there's some useful info on our site that you may or may not already know but if you're interested in checking out what myself and my 2 friends have to offer go check out the site today. :)
