Argonium is a standalone Multiplayer First Person shooter powered by id Softwares Quake II engine.
It was developed by Daniel "Fusion" Schneider and created by Daniel "Fusion" Schneider and Robin "???" Wennberg.
Development time was around 250 hours by one person. It includes over 100 sound files, over 250 image files and over 50 models.
Argonium includes 5 different weapons, 1 player character with 3 skins, 3 powerup items, armour and health items.
The game is strictly a Deathmatch game and comes with 3 levels. The engine is released under the GNU General Public License
and the sourcecode is released with the game.
