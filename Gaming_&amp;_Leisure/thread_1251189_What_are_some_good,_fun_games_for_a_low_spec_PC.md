### Post by Dark Aspect on 2009-08-27
Ok, well the only variable at this point is the video card, can you check to see if you have direct rendering with:

```
glxinfo
```

Should be the third line. Now give us a rough estimate of speed with the output of glxgears (I know its not 100% accurate)

```
glxgears
```

As far as games I would try [astromenace]("http://www.viewizard.com/astromenace/index_linux.php") and [Nexuiz]("http://www.alientrap.org/nexuiz/") on low to medium settings. Also, [Nexuiz]("http://www.alientrap.org/nexuiz/") will give us an idea of what kind of video card you have. It won't work at all if you don't have direct rendering or if you have a Nvidia/ATI card without drivers installed.
