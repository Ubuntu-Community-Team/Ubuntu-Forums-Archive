### Post by MonkeyBoy on 2007-08-03
I think [this live distro]("http://live.linux-gamers.net/?s=games") is worth a look particularly for passing on to Windows using mates who need some encouragement to try Linux.

It is a live disk that just runs games.  It installs Nvidia and ATI drivers automatically and found all my hardware without a problem.  All the games seem to run pretty well and pretty fast too once they have started.

I realise there have been posts about this before but I am so impressed that I think it is well worth reviving as a subject of interest.
