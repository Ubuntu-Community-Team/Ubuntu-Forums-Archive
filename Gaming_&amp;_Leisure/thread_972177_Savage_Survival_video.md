> This is a video of the [Savage Survival]("http://forums.s2games.com/showthread.php?t=13852") mod for the [Savage 2]("http://savage2.s2games.com/main.php") game.

The point of the mod is to stay alive as human for 15 minutes. The game starts out with one human randomly chosen to be a zombie - and when a zombie kills a human, the said human becomes a zombie.

Humans get new weapons and health potions by killing zombies, and refill their ammo by going to the ammo depot that randomly pops up.

(The holy bolt machine gun was nerfed since then, and humans now wield hammers, because they aren't supposed to be meleeing. Also, this is my first video, so go easy on me!)
