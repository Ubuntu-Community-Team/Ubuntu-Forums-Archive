### Post by donkyhotay on 2009-08-26
I'm a big fan of [moonbase commander](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moonbase_Commander), even beating out starcraft which got dropped down to 2nd place in my personal list of best strategy games of all time because of it. Due to it's age and poor marketing the game has pretty much become abandonware over the past 7 years since it was released. 3 years ago an open source project was started to try to [recreate a FOSS version of Moonbase Commander](http://code.google.com/p/tether). For most of it's history I've been limping the project along trying to keep it moving forward but I'm not a very skilled programmer. Despite my lack of skills I've managed to make some progress over the years however the game is still alpha stage and not really playable. If anyone is interested in helping this project along I'd appreciate it. Mostly though I'm curious if anyone else even remembers/enjoys this game or if I'm the last Moonbase Commander fan left on the net.
