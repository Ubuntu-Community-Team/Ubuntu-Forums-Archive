### Post by Naegling23 on 2008-08-08
if your into DandD type games, I strongly recommend Neverwinter Nights 1.  (the first one, the second one wont work).  It runs native (after a slightly difficult instal, but there are guides to help you out).  Its a little dated, 2002 I think, so the graphics are not great, but the isometric viewpoint sort of helps to hide that fact(I looks better than diablo2, but it isnt as good looking as some modern rpg's like oblivon, still, its not "bad looking").  Other than that, its a solid DandD type video game.  Lots of add ons and user created content, multiple expansions, an online component (with some persistant worlds, ie mmo like, if you take the time to set them up).  It was a very well recieved and popular game in its time, so your getting a great game, and you can get the diamond edition which includes the game and all commercial expansions for about 20 bucks. (USD)
