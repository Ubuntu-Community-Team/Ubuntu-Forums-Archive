### Post by Vadi on 2009-11-16
A year and three months in development, the 1.0 release of **Mudlet** is here!

[center][Watch the introductory Mudlet 1.0 screencast]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c1Llvwy0Y_Y&feature=PlayList&p=A40A1E6E5AEB8874&index=0&playnext=1")[/center]

What is Mudlet? It’s a **free**, open-source and cross-platform MUD client that works on **Linux**, **Windows**, and **Mac** OS! Designed to be fast, efficient, and user-friendly, you can get your own copy for free right here: [http://www.mudlet.org/download/](http://www.mudlet.org/download/)

Mudlet features Lua as it’s scripting base and a rich client API to support it. Split screen, auto-completion from output and input, multi-session gaming, HTML and text logging, replay recording and playback are all part of the package.

Mudlet also features a rich UI api for creating your own labels and buttons anywhere on the screen and changing their looks:

[center][[img]http://www.mudlet.org/wp-content/uploads/2009/11/download-150x150.png[/img]]("http://www.mudlet.org/wp-content/uploads/2009/11/download.png")[/center]


Special focus has also been placed on the speed of the client ([watch]("http://blip.tv/file/1693706")).

Now go out and enjoy Mudlet! We’d love to hear your feedback on [our forums]("http://mudlet.sourceforge.net/phpBB3/index.php").
