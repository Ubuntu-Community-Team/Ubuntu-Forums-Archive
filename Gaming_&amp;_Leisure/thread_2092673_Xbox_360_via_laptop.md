### Post by bobipod on 2013-02-09
After much investigation this is now sorted.

The cross over cable i bought was not actually a cross over cable it had been mis packaged.

So for anyone else wanting to know this, it really is quite simple (i managed it).

1: You have one laptop with wireless internet connection switched on.  You will need to switch on the Internet sharing in windows settings (if you use windows)

2: You plug one end of the cross over cable into the laptop rj45 socket.

3: You then plug the other end into the RJ45 socket on the xbox, the xbox must be off at this point.

4: Switch on the xbox.

5: Wireless connectivity without the expensive xbox wifi adaptor.
