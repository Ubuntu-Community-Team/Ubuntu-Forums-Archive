you can reinstall Amnesia this way:

Open up the Ubuntu Software Centre and make sure it is, what I like to call, the front window (i.e. all other windows are behind it) and move your cursor to the TOP LEFT CORNER of the screen (that is above the ubuntu logo). The words 'File', 'Edit', 'View', and 'Help' will appear. Click on 'File' and then click on 'Reinstall Previous Purchases...'. You should be able to reinstall from there.
