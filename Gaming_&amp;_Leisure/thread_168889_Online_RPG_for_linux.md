### Post by leech on 2006-05-01
Depends on what you mean by "Good" and "Online"  Neverwinter Nights is both good and you can play it online, but it isn't like an MMORPG.  

At one point in time there was a native client for Ultima Online, but that was dropped like an ugly step-child.

You could look into some of the free ones.  Mind you the graphics won't be quite on par with World of Warcraft, but they could be fun.  Google for Worldforge and Planeshift.

Also, there is the game called A Second Life that has a native Linux client.
