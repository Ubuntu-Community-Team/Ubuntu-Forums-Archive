### Post by kaldor on 2012-06-11
If you're doing any heavy 3d gaming, get NVIDIA; AMD will just annoy you otherwise.Since you mention the Humble Bundle, you don't need heavy graphics. A low-mid range Geforce 440 or 450 should work fine. Requires proprietary driver due to low open source support.

If you want something that works out of the box and has relatively decent 3d, get an HD 5000 series AMD card like a Radeon 5770. The proprietary drivers usually suck, but the open source support is overall quite great in non-laptops. The "AMD is bad" mindset is becoming less and less relevant, especially in the older generations. The open source driver tends to improve bit by bit every Ubuntu release.

Intel is out of the question. Not fast enough for serious 3d. 

Hope this is useful.
