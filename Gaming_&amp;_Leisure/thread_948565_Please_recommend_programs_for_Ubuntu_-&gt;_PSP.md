### Post by Xzoky on 2008-10-22
Avidemux does pretty well indeed, especially with H.264, though the sound is sometimes a little bit delayed ; I'll have to find the proper offset for this ...
The only trouble with moving videos in and out of a PSP is that when you browse your memory stick from your PC, if you delete a file, this file will go to the trash. The problem is the trash keeps the deleted files on the drive they come from, in that case your memory stick in some ".trash" folder. So always empty your trash before disconnecting your PSP, just in case ( or be sure to always shift+del your files ).
