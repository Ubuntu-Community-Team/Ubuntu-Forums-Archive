The Press release:

> **Tomsk, Russia - November 22, 2011** – [UNIGINE Corp.]("http://unigine.com/")  announced today that the global war for oil in the flooded  post-apocalyptic world of Oil Rush begins on January 25, 2012. Oil Rush  will be released for Windows, Linux and Mac OS X. Oil Rush is a  real-time naval strategy game developed by an internal Unigine team. It  is based on group control, that combines strategic challenge of classic  RTS with sheer fun of Tower Defense, and features state-of-an-art  visuals. Easy to learn but hard to master, Oil Rush offers a variety of  different strategies for each map. Thousands of people who pre-ordered  Oil Rush already got access to beta version of the game – so join up and  get ready for your big oil war in January! Official website: [oilrush-game.com]("http://oilrush-game.com/")
