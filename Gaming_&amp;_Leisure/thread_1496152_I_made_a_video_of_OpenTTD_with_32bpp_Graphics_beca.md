Here's a HD video I made of OpenTTD with the unofficial 32bpp graphics  mod and Extra Zoom Levels. This is not the officially supported graphics  set, the standard OpenTTD still uses the 8-bit graphics set.

The community over there have put a whole lot of work into creating a 32-bit graphics set and while it's still a work in progress it looks great. 

You have to compile it for Linux, but the instructions are easy enough to follow.
