### Post by xbaez on 2005-12-16
I'm owner of a site [www.SoccerAccess.com](www.SoccerAccess.com), for over 7 years we've been editing video games, and I will like to see Pro Evolution Soccer 5 supported in Linux.

I registered to transgaming to see if I was able to change this. So I submitted a Pro Evolution Soccer 5 request, and from 10 people needed, 10 people already voted for Pro Evolution Soccer, therefore it will enter the official cedega polls of next month.

My main idea is to have this game officially supported by future versions of Cedega.

We're already working on a patch for Pro Evolution Soccer 5, in order to have the 2006 World Cup on this game.

I personally think that Pro Evolution Soccer and Winning Eleven support will boost cedega sales in asia and europe, were this game is extremelly popular.

If you are registered to transgaming, go to this URL, search for 'Pro Evolution Soccer', and vote 

[http://www.transgaming.com/postlist.php?forum=1263](http://www.transgaming.com/postlist.php?forum=1263)

Go to this URL in order to read our posts inviting people to support PES 5 in Linux, this is our personal effort in order to see this project become a reality

[http://www.gamingaccess.com/index.php?news_id=4140](http://www.gamingaccess.com/index.php?news_id=4140)

Go to this URL to participate in the active forum of transgaming, forum that I started and suggests this project to transgaming

[http://transgaming.org/forum/viewtopic.php?t=4762](http://transgaming.org/forum/viewtopic.php?t=4762)

**please visit transgaming and make sure to vote for Pro Evolution Soccer 5 next month**, when it will be available in the official polls in order to determine which games transgaming developers will put their efforts into making it Linux compatible
