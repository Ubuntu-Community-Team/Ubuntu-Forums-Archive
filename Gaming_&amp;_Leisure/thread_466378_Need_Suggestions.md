and look under the games section for single/multiplayer games that sound good to you.

But if you're looking for more variety, I suggest GetDeb's game section: [http://www.getdeb.net/category.php?id=3](http://www.getdeb.net/category.php?id=3)

Joe was right about one thing though, if you mention what genre you're looking for it 
may be easier for the community to make suggestions.  ^_^
