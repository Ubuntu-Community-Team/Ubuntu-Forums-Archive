### Post by Vadi on 2008-01-31
You don't need the "Scorched3D-41.3-src.tar.gz", delete it. That's for people who want to compile it themself.

First, click on this: [http://getdeb.net/download/2043/1](http://getdeb.net/download/2043/1). When it asks you to either open or save the file, select open. After it downloads, click on 'Install Package', and after that's done, close it.

Then, click on this: [http://getdeb.net/download/2043/0](http://getdeb.net/download/2043/0) and repeat the same procedure. After that, simply go to Applications - Games and enjoy the game!
