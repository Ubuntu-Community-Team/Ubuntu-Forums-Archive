### Post by Ms_Angel_D on 2009-05-28
[Regnum Online]("http://www.regnumonline.com.ar") is a FREE TO PLAY (F2P) MMORPG inviting you to play with no level or time restrictions. All that is required is that you fight for your realm.

It's a pretty fun game, with lot's of questing. Graphically it's one of the better mmorpgs I've come across for linux. If you enjoy MMORPG's I suggest you give it a go ;)

Just fyi here's some tips on installing.

[LIST=1]
[*] After registration, the file your prompted to download has no extension but is a .bin file.

[*] Once the download is complete just go to the folder you have saved it to and right click on it then click properties.

[*] Under the permissions tab make sure the file is executable, then close properties.

[*] Now you can click on the file to run the installer. from there just follow the on screen instructions.
[/LIST]

[YouTube video of Regnum]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KZfG8FxXeac")
