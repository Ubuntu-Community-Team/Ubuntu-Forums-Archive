Well, that's how the LGC came about. Basically we got tired of not seeing anything coming to the forefront on the gaming scene for a while.
Not only that, but if you head into any of the big box stores, the pc gaming section is down considerably from what it used to be.

That's why we got the idea. Bring Developers to us and end-users running linux will have a place to go. We may also be one of the biggest customers. :D
