### Post by Jim_in_Omaha on 2008-08-25
You can demo both of them. I actually have X-Plane 9. At first I was disappointed until I began download new aircraft. Then things started to change. Some of the free aircraft to download are really incredible the amount of effort and work people have put into it. The weather is neat and bird strikes are interesting.

The copy of X-Plane I have is for Windows but I was able to make it work using the demo for Linux. With the latest patch it is incredibly fast with multicore CPU's.
