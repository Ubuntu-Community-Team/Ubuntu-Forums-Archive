### Post by Perfect Storm on 2017-02-27
Hi, you run/install it like this;

```
cd /path/to/file
chmod +x MCEdit.v1.5.6.0.Lin.Universal.run
./MCEdit.v1.5.6.0.Lin.Universal.run
```
When it comes to [end] press Q
then there will be a couple of questions about dependencies and so on. It may require running as sudo, I havn't tested it through.
