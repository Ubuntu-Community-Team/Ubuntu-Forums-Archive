### Post by FyreBrand on 2006-11-27
I would ask on the Planeshift forums for a good answer.  I do know that when I had a Radeon X300 installed I had to use the fglrx driver and not the ati/radeon driver or the game wouldn't load.

If you don't get an answer on the Planeshift forums you could just try installing the fglrx driver if you're comfortable with that and see if it makes the difference.  Look in the [**Multimedia and Video**]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=138") forum for HowTo's on installing fglrx.
