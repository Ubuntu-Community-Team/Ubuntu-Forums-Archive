### Post by quadomatic on 2008-03-29
I only have a GP2X F100 MK2 but I suppose I could give a crack at answering your questions, since the only difference between the two is a different D-Pad and a touch screen.

I was in a very similar situation as you, as I also had a DS, and then got my GP2X because I was bored. I then sold my DS, which should give you the idea that I enjoyed my GP2X, and I do.

You really can't compare the GP2X to the DS or the PSP, unless your comparing it to their capacities for emulators and homebrew games. The GP2X has great emulators, and there are plenty of homebrew games. The community is very strong as well.

But, since you don't already have a GP2X, I wouldn't recommend you buy a GP2X right now. Why? Because members of the community are making the Pandora handheld!

Here are some good forums for GP2X related stuff:
[http://www.gp32x.com/board/](http://www.gp32x.com/board/)

And here's the forum for Pandora:
[http://www.gp32x.com/board/index.php?showforum=62](http://www.gp32x.com/board/index.php?showforum=62)
