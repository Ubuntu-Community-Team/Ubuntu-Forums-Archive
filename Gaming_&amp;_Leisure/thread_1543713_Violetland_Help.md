### Post by sakuramboo on 2010-08-04
How is Violetland difficult? You move around with WASD, use the mouse to aim, left click to shoot, right click to reload, Q to switch to teleport mode, SPACE to throw a grenade (it lands where you are aiming) and once you level up, C to distribute the points. That's all there is to it.

EDIT: I just committed the default key binding layout to the English README. It's available in the trunk branch. But, really, what I just posted is what I added.
