### Post by mivo on 2009-11-16
The card is a bit of a bottleneck. I think Nexuiz (a 3D shooter *should* run, if you play on low settings, perhaps with a few medium options. It is in the repository, though the latest version came out in September and I don't know what version the repos have. Official site: [http://www.alientrap.org/nexuiz/](http://www.alientrap.org/nexuiz/)

There is also Quake Live, which you can play in your browser. Linux is supported: [http://www.quakelive.com/](http://www.quakelive.com/)

Both games have single and multiplayer modes. Nexuiz also has a campaign.
