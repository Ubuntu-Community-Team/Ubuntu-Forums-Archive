### Post by maxamillion on 2006-12-12
There are currently no "complete" windows emulator. Wine ([http://www.winehq.org/](http://www.winehq.org/)) and cedega ([http://www.transgaming.com/cedega](http://www.transgaming.com/cedega)) are both very good at supporting the more popular applications but if you are extremely stuck on windows simply for gaming purposes, i might recommend dual booting or trying your luck at parallels ([http://www.parallels.com/](http://www.parallels.com/))
