### Post by IanW on 2007-06-27
I wouldn't recommend an Nvidia 8600GT. Even the 8600GTS isn't all that. If you can stretch to an 8800 card, do it.

1: The 8600 cards have 128-bit memory bandwidth and 32 stream processors.
2: The 8800 cards have 320-bit memory bandwidth and 96 stream processors.

It's like Nvidia have left a gap for an 8700 series card to take on ATI's 2400/2600 cards.

Pay 2/3 the price for 1/3 the horsepower? Not me.
