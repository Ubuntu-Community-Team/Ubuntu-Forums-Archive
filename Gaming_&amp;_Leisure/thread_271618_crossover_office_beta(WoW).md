### Post by dom02 on 2006-10-04
Just tried WoW on the beta for the new version of crossover office.  The new version is supposed to support WoW and Steam based games.  WoW runs amazing.  All I did was install crossover office and then just ran WoW, which I had already setup under wine.  Performance is amazing I have the setting close to max and I'm getting great fps.  And I have a amd 64 2800+, ATI 9800pro and 1 gig of ram.  WoW is running almost as well as it does under windows at the same resolution. 

Though it has crashed a few times so it still has some bugs.  But performance wise it's pretty amazing.
