### Post by Tyke91 on 2008-03-19
Are you running compiz-fusion (aka. visual effects)? those will often kill a game. also, try downloading the nwn override from this website

[http://www.trustinginthefuture.com/3towns/downloads.asp](http://www.trustinginthefuture.com/3towns/downloads.asp)

it's called the NwN Original Animations override (crash fix) and it's around two thirds of the way down. It reverts back to the animations from the original NwN and makes the game much more stable, albiet the cloaks will look like crap, but you get used to it, and the performance boost is worth it.

just download it, unzip it, and put it in your ../nwn/override directory (wherever you put it)


that's all i can suggest.
