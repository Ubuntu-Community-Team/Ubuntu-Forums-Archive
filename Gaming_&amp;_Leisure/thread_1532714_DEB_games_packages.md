### Post by ucal on 2010-07-16
I can't help you much on the deb front as I can't for the life of me remember how to get debs from playdeb and getdeb (I'm not crazy for thinking they used to provide them am I?).  However, you might not need debs, if you can figure out how to compile from source, which a lot of games are released under.    For example, Assault cube [here]("http://assault.cubers.net/").  Download the source file, as well as the dependencies (if you have them installed already, great!  Otherwise you might have to download the source of those as well), and then compile it from source using a guide like this [one]("http://www.tuxfiles.org/linuxhelp/softinstall.html") (If anyone has a better guide, please recommend it, I remember learning how to compile from source was one of the hardest things for me, because of various terminal errors on my part >_<)
