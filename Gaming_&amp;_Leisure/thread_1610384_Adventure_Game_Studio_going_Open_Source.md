### Post by Sofox on 2010-10-31
This is a bit of a cross post of this: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1607495](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1607495)

Essentially, Adventure Game Studio is this IDE for developing Adventure Games. It's popular and has a lot of games for it ( [www.bigbluecup.com](www.bigbluecup.com) ) but up to now hasn't had a Linux port. Well, recently its creator, Chris Jones has decided to gradually open its source. There's an underway to make a Linux port of the IDE. It's been held back by some dependencies and non-open sourced parts of the IDE, but it's being discussed both at the above link and in the offical AGS forum here: [http://www.bigbluecup.com/yabb/index.php?topic=42063](http://www.bigbluecup.com/yabb/index.php?topic=42063)

Good news for everybody.
