### Post by charlieg on 2006-04-12
Try [scourge](http://scourge.sourceforge.net).  There's also [FreedroidRPG](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=157770).  If you like Baldur's Gate, give [GemRB](http://gemrb.sourceforge.net) a whirl (you need BG to use it).

There's also [Vulture's](http://www.darkarts.co.za/projects/vultures/) which is a continuation of the seemingly-defunct Falcon's Eye project.  It recently had a major release (v2) so looks pretty healthy.

On a simpler, quicker level there's [Lost Labyrinth](http://laby.toybox.de/) which is good fun.

Other than that you're looking at running WINE & commercial games/mods.
