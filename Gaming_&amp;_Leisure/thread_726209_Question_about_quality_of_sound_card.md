### Post by ShodanjoDM on 2008-03-16
IMO, if you have an ordinary 2.1 speaker system, Audigy and Audigy 2 series are fine cards for the purpose.

The main advantage of the Audigy cards, as far as I know, is that they reduce the workload of the CPU since they have their own to process the sound signals. Not so with the Live series, though.
