### Post by Hur Dur on 2011-02-13
> **robro said:**
> I'm looking at the screenshots for cube 2 and it looks very interesting :) and has outstanding graphics :D
but, does it have a lot of blood/gore, games with gore and etc, always make me sick :lolflag:
It has a lot of blood, and I believe you can be gibbed, but I have never seen it happen. You can turn off the blood if you want. It's in the graphics options. If the game is too fast paced for you, or you can't run it, try AssultCube. It uses the same engine as Cube 1 does, which has very low requirements. Note that Cube 2 isn't the most active game around (Usually about 3 servers with 6 - 16 people in it, and the rest have 3 - 5) but you can usually find a good game. It also has pretty good AI for bot matches.
