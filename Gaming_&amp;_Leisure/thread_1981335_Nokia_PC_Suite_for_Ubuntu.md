### Post by raja.genupula on 2012-07-04
actually to connect internet from nokia mobile to PC ,you no need of any PC Suite . Just connect your mobile and PC with a USB cable and select PC suite mode .Then click at network connections of your Ubuntu ,it will display there a new connection . Click it , then it will direct to a wizard that will have your country , service provider  , accesspoint information filling step-wise .

after all of them you are ready to use internet by clicking at the network connections .


hope that helps.
