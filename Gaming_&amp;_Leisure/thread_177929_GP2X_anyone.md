### Post by snailian on 2006-05-26
I have a GP2X, and I feel that it's worth getting. I've had it since December. 

Back in December, there wasn't much available for it, the battery life sucked and the firmware wasn't up to par. It also cost $200 USD, I wouldn't have reccommended it back then.  

Today there is a strong growing community, the tools required to build your own games are readily available, and firmware 2.0 is good. The battery life has been extended as well (by no longer powering unused "devices"). The price has gone down to $169USD.   It's worth getting if you want to make your own 2D console game, or are into roms.   The video player works, but is still sort of lacking.
