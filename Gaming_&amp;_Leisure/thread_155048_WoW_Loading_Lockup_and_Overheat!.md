### Post by LauraSakura on 2007-10-02
I used to have a lot of overheating problems with my laptop. It sounds simple, but make sure you are running it on a hard surface. Sitting it on an actual lap, or bed can make it hard for the air to flow at all. I solved this on mine by getting a small laptop cooling pad to sit under my laptop if I'm going to actually use it on my lap or bed. Also, make sure your fans are working. I've heard stories of people whose onboard fans stopped working and the laptop would continually overheat.  As for the lockup, It probably has something to do with openGL, but someone else here would be able to help with that more than me.
