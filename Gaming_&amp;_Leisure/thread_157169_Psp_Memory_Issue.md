### Post by nixon on 2006-04-09
[QUOTE=cayalee]i have a similar issue, tried to copy an mp3 across and now ive lost 3mb on my 32 mb card and it doesnt even show up on the psp[/QUOTE]

I had this problem when I first started moving files back and forth from my pc.. i found that if i do not unmount the disk, ie.. go to my computer.. right click.. unmount drive.. if I had deleted some files they wouldn't show up on the card but the card would still show the space missing.. 2 things you can do..

1. ensure that your unmounting when moving files back and forth..
2. empty your trash when the card is plugged in.. it may have put the files in the trash but not actually removed them from the card..

i've made it a habit to unmount the disk each time now and I haven't had a problem for a while.. :)
