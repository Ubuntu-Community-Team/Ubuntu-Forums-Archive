### Post by Rustybolts on 2011-10-05
We have now released Helena the 3rd on the Ubuntu Software Centre priced at $7.99 or you can purchase direct from our website if you prefer [http://www.helenathe3rd.com/](http://www.helenathe3rd.com/) . We have a few more digital distributors in the pipeline.

About:
Inspired by old school gameplay back to the days of the original playstation and n64, Helena The 3rd is an action platformer where you pilot a tank as you explore underground caverns and battle hostile robotic enemies. you will need to leave your vehicle to explore rooms you can only get to on foot. Gameplay switches from 3rd person to FPS as you search for powerful upgrades you'll need to reach new areas.

Inspired by games such as Blastermaster, SuperMetroid, Megaman.
