### Post by Geezanansa on 2011-08-06
Hi Pimentel28

I too am a new ubuntu user and thought i would post a reply just to encourage and hopefully guide you to getting your ps3 controller working on natty.

Natty should see your ps3 controller after you plug it in. (Natty has drivers allready in package)   Simple as.

I am unfamiliar with your game you mentioned but basically if your controller dont work with your game (just by plugging it in) you need to install and configure QJoypad which is a mouse and keyboard emulator and can allow you to use your ps3 controller on your desktop/workspace or just about any app/game.

Simply use Ubuntu Software Centre to install QJoypad.  Just type qjoypad in ssearch box and install.  THen configure qjoypad.
