### Post by shat on 2006-09-06
IIRC, 

Starcraft is written in 640x480.  You basically have to change your resolution down to 640x480, and it will run fullscreen.  Otherwise, it will take up a 640x480 splotch on your screen.  Make sure you have your "emulate a virtual desktop" (winecfg -> graphics) set how you want it.
