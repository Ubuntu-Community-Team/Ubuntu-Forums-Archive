### Post by Grant A. on 2009-11-01
From my experiences, RuneScape works great in Karmic, however, a few things need to be done to get it going right.

1) Go to the Ubuntu Software Center, install OpenJDK Runtime and IcedTea. These are open source Java plugins that work, in my opinion, better for RuneScape than the proprietary ones.

2) Install Adobe Flash Player in the Ubuntu Software Center.

3) 

A. Go to the System menu, and then go to the administration menu. Click on "Hardware Drivers", and then check to see if proprietary drivers are available from your Graphics card vendor. 

NVidia users **must** do this step:

B. Download the recommended driver and reboot.

4) If you're a member, go to the RuneScape website and log into the website. This will remove the ads from your game in Linux.

5) Log out and go to the game.

6) Check "always accept content from this publisher"

7) Now, you may notice that the RuneScape game is on the left side of the window, with a large grey area on the side. To fix this, change the mode to high detail, and unmaximize the window. Then remaximize it, this should bring the game to the center, and remove the box.

8) Log in

9) Go to the graphics section of the game, and change your option to "resizeable window".

10) Enjoy the game!

**Note:** Step 7 **must** be repeated every time you navigate to the RuneScape game.

**Note:** On my system, the game freezes up randomly in 10 minute intervals. This is fine for me, considering that all I have to do is open up a new window and re-login.
