### Post by dopefish7590 on 2009-10-30
You could get the Marathon Series: [http://source.bungie.org](http://source.bungie.org)

They were released as freeware quite a while ago, but no one seems to have noticed. They have a long Single Player campaign, and they run natively on Linux (It's called Aleph One)

I would recommend starting with Marathon 1 and after the three games, play Marathon Eternal X (So awesome, and seems to work well as a continuation of the story.)

Keep in mind though, Marathon 1 was released Dec 1994, so don't expect amazing graphics or cenimatics. Marathon 2 and Infinity have better graphics since they were from around 1996, and Eternal X was released just last year, so the textures for that are well done, but still no weapon or enemy models. (You can download Hi-res packs if you *need* graphics though)

Also, not sure what's up with the very beginning of Marathon 1, so just ignore that.
