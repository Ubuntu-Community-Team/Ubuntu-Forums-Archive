### Post by Soulcage on 2010-12-06
Download and extract [http://prdownloads.sourceforge.net/tremulous/tremulous-1.1.0.zip?download]("http://prdownloads.sourceforge.net/tremulous/tremulous-1.1.0.zip?download") [http://prdownloads.sourceforge.net/tremulous/tremulous-gpp1.zip?download]("http://prdownloads.sourceforge.net/tremulous/tremulous-gpp1.zip?download") got these links from [http://tremulous.net/files/]("http://tremulous.net/files/") then extract 1.1.0 then gpp to the home folder making sure all the folders and files extracted. Then: cd tremulous && ./tremulos-gpp.x86_64 or .x86.
