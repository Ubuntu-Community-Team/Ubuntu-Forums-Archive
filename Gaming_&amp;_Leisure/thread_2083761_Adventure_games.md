### Post by Sofox on 2012-11-14
[ScummVM ]("http://scummvm.org/")should allow you to play countless classic Adventure games on your Linux machine, including some Space Quest but also stuff like Monkey Island and Broken Sword (you need the old files though). Check the compatibility page for a full list.

If you go to the download page, you can also get a couple of freeware adventure games that run in ScummVM. I'd recommend Flight of the Amazon Queen and Beneath a Steel Sky.
