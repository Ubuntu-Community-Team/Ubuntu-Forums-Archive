Hello to all,
i would point you attention to this game. Is a Sci-Fi MMORTS in a first person prospective, view with a native client for Linux tha works pretty well. It's in developing stage but is total playable with an one hundred of active players.

The game looks promising, and the developers are pretty incline to incorporate player suggestion of form of ideas but also in artwork and coding.

For now is free to play, if anyone wanna take a look below are the links:

[http://www.hazeron.com/](http://www.hazeron.com/) (Main Site)
[http://ptmc.globalgamers.de/](http://ptmc.globalgamers.de/) (Forums)
[http://www.hazeron.com/manualf.html](http://www.hazeron.com/manualf.html) (Game Manual in English)
[http://hazeron.wikia.com/wiki/Shores_of_Hazeron_Wiki](http://hazeron.wikia.com/wiki/Shores_of_Hazeron_Wiki) (Wiki)
[http://www.hazeron.com/softwaref.html](http://www.hazeron.com/softwaref.html) (Downloads Win & Linux)

OpenAL (libopenal1) is required to play, you can install it from Synaptic.
