### Post by Hobbiticus on 2007-08-23
I'm in a position that I'm sure many of you are in.  You want to game on Linux, but all of your buddies are on ventrilo, or are forced to use teamspeak because of you.  Ventrilo says that a linux client is in development, but it doesn't seem to be coming any time soon.

Well, I thought that it was finally time to have the voice quality of Ventrilo with the linux support of Teamspeak.  Therefore, I have made [VoiceChatter](http://voicechatter.org) - a completely free and cross-platform voice communication application meant for gaming.

It's not completely done yet, but is has all of the basics.  I want to get the word out so that people can try it and give me feedback while it is still under development, along with spotting problems.  So feel free to spread the word!

So check it out and let me know what you think either here or on the VoiceChatter forums.

Enjoy!
