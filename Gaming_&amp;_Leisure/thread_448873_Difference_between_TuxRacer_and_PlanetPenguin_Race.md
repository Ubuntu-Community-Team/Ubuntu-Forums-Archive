### Post by Surkow on 2007-05-19
Even though TuxRacer is open source Sunspire Studios also created a commercial TuxRacer game. When they stopped selling the commercial version a fork called PlanetPenguin Racer was created from the last GPL-licensed version of Tux Racer. Read [this]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tuxracer#History") wiki entry for more information. PlanetPenguin Racer includes additional levels created by the community.
