### Post by Carpentr on 2009-10-18
I just ran across the game today after upgrading to 9.10 and taking a look at the new software center.

It has very classic looking graphics, that remind you of much older RPGs. It's played in a 2D isometric view. I've only played it for around an hour but I'm enjoying it so far. The game has a nice sense of humor in it's story line but also some seriousness and interesting (but sometimes classical) plot twists that make you wonder what's going to happen next.

I thought I'd share this so any Linux goer can give this a try. It's a must try for anyone who likes classical RPGs.

**Link:** [http://www.freedink.org/](http://www.freedink.org/)
