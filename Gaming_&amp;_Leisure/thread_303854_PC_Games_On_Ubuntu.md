### Post by hikaricore on 2006-11-20
I don't know for sure if any of the myst games were ported, but some other titles that are natively playable through [Scumm VM]("http://www.scummvm.org").  I'm sure if you like myst you've probably played other point and click type games.  Here's a few that might remind you of the mid 90's (the the [full compatibility list]("http://www.scummvm.org/compatibility.php")):

[I]Maniac Mansion
Day of the Tentacle
Sam & Max Hit the Road
Full Throttle
Beneath a Steel Sky
Flight of the Amazon Queen
[/I]

Flight of the Amazon Queen & Beneath a Steal Sky have been released for free to the public and can be downloaded via synaptic, apt-get, or the add/remove menu. (along with scummvm).  The other titles are still comercial status, but if you own any of them you can run them in linux with only slight modification.

Secondlife also now has a [native linux client]("http://secondlife.com/community/linux-alpha.php").

And [Return to Castle Wolfenstein]("http://games.activision.com/games/wolfenstein/") (and all it's [spawnoff deathmatch versions]("http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/FPS_Games")) all have [native linux versions]("http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/Return_To_Castle_Wolfenstein"). 

Hope you find more info on your myst games,

--Aaron
