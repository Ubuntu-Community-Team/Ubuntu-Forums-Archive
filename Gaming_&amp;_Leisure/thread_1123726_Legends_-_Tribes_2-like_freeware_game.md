### Post by noseferatu on 2009-04-12
When people are talking about linux fps games, Urban Terror, Nexuiz, Warsow, Tremulous and Alien Arena etc are always mentioned, but this great game is shamefully forgotten.[Legends]("http://legendsthegame.net/") is free, but sadly not free software. Its gameplay strongly resembles Tribes 2, and for those of us who have played Tribes 2, it is a good thing indeed :p Sadly the community is quite small, but that is fixable :D There is no .deb package, but there is an easy-to-use graphical installer for linux. Installer can be found [here]("http://legendsthegame.net/index.php?m=fileswap&view=92"). Happy gaming!
