Hello everybody,

for those of you who don't know Steam Greenlight: It's a initiative of Steam for Indie Developers to get to the Steam Platform. The crowd can upvote games which will be released on Steam if they get enough votes. So here's my plan: You guys can post your favorite Steam Greenlight Games and include a link. Maybe some more people over here like your games and will support your vote! I propose that you can post games which will be coming to Linux and games that don't - so we can post in the comments that we want a Linux version!!!
