### Post by WarrenSH on 2010-08-05
**Linux M.U.G.E.N (2001-2002)** 

Starting 2001, Elecbyte began to release new versions exclusively for Linux. The last beta release of Linux M.U.G.E.N was made available on their website in 2002: M.U.G.E.N 2002.04.14.

2002.04.14 was to be Elecbyte's last public M.U.G.E.N release.

[M.U.G.E.N 2002.04.14 (Linux)]("http://files.deliann.eu/mugen/mugen-2002-04-14.tar.bz2")

Click it to download ;) enjoy this was found over at [http://www.deliann.eu/create/mugen/index.shtml#download](http://www.deliann.eu/create/mugen/index.shtml#download)
