### Post by tuxyboy on 2012-11-18
Hello (this is my first post)
I have just installed this game(flare 0.15.1-1) on my 64bit Precise Ubuntu , and find it very nice. Wonderful relaxing music and very nice and cute graphics. :)
Some of controls of this game I've found are:
c- character menu
i - inventory
p - power, similar to diablo spells menu
l - log, quest and messages
drag-n-drop things to belt or hand from inventory(but be careful, i ve lost my dagger when trying to move main weapon to inventory).
hover over life and experience to see numbers.
Your gold is seen in inventory in middle.
There is also some forum, but it is also for game engine:  [http://opengameart.org/forums/flare](http://opengameart.org/forums/flare)
You can also do `man flare` there are some conf files in system:
~/.config/flare/settings.txt there you can configure screen resolution, fullscreen ...
~/.local/share/flare/ - here are your save files - dont cheat ! :P
Sorry for the length, but maybe it will help someone.
