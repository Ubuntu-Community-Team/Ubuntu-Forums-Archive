### Post by Jayschwa on 2006-08-11
I just thought I'd let everyone know that there's a cool game called [Savage 2]("http://savage2.s2games.com") in development. They've promised a Linux support at launch (in addition to Windows).

For those of you who have maybe never heard of it, Savage was, in my opinion, a fairly fresh game released in 2003. It mixed first person shooter gameplay with real time strategy. Each team (Legion of Man and Beast Horde) start with a base in which the usual RTS style build up is done. Each team has a Commander which is a player with a top down RTS view that can research and order troops. All other players are ground troops.

They're going to be starting a beta test soon(tm), and we have been told that Linux players will get to participate (not right off the bat like Windows users though). This is a great chance to help get a AAA game working great on Linux. People who preorder the game (expected to be released in November or December) are the people who get into the beta. It's $30, which I'd say is pretty fair. They're not going through a publisher, so it will only be distributed online, but it's better than paying an extra $20 for a box that collects dust.

Just thought I'd give a heads up in case there are some original Savage fans out there, or new people who may be interested.
