### Post by guine on 2010-06-19
You want to jump on one of the ice cube guys before you get there so you can pick one up and throw it into the last wall to open up a hole in it.  Its easiest to take out the bottom block but you can get through a higher hole even if youre big by ducking down when you just, although it may take a try or 2 to make it through the gap.
