### Post by GFree678 on 2007-08-25
The latest version of WINE (0.9.44) is a MASSIVE improvement. I've been trying to get Tomb Raider Legend/Anniversary to look respectable in WINE for some time, and although Legend was OK with most graphical features, Anniversary had a bad yellow overlay in many areas (particularly with water being rendered). Now, I can enabled just about everything apart from reflections, and both game render identically to what they looked like in Windows, at roughly the same frame rates Totally amazed how much changed from the previous version of WINE.

I admit though, it feels kinda dirty playing a Windows game in Linux, even if it works just fine. Really won't help us it getting more commercial support for games if Wine keeps getting better. But compromises need to be made I suppose.
