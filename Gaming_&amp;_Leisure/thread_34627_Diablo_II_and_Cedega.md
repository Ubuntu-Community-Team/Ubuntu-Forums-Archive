### Post by ante0 on 2005-05-15
Hi, this is just for people who is having problems with installing Diablo2 with Cedega. The most common thing is that if you start cedega using a terminal, and cding into the /media/cdrom0 the cdrom gets locked and when you are about to insert the next diablo2 cd its impossible to eject the drive. What you should do is close the terminal and then eject the cdrom. that way it should work. Just so you know! =)
//Ante0

Edit: One more good thing to know is that Battle.net doesnt work with a cracked version of diablo. Sorry.
