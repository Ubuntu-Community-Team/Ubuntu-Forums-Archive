Full article here: [http://www.ubuntuvibes.com/2012/11/cryengine-3-coming-to-linux.html](http://www.ubuntuvibes.com/2012/11/cryengine-3-coming-to-linux.html)

Basically the game Star Citizen just had a successful kickstarter campaign and as usually there have been many request for a Mac and Linux version of the game. The Star Citizen team has already ask the CryEngine guys about it but now are doing a vote on their website to determine how important of an issue this is to the people. If they think it is very important, they will but more pressure on the CryEngine team.

Use the link to vote for linux support (and anthing else you want from Star Citizen): [http://www.robertsspaceindustries.com/poll-additional-stretch-goals/](http://www.robertsspaceindustries.com/poll-additional-stretch-goals/)
