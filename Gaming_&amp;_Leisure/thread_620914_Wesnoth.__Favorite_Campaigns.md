### Post by gcrussell1 on 2007-12-08
You could always try the latest versions of some of the campaigns you played when you first got the game. Unfortunately, I haven't gotten to the point of needing to try out the add-on campaigns, but I know a lot of the official ones have been heavily updated, especially in the latest dev releases (UtBS comes to mind as one that's been updated a ton lately).

You might want to try the forums on wesnoth.org as well, though you've probably checked by now. It's worth a look, and make sure to mention what you're looking for in a campaign, there are many different modes of play you could go for, so you'll get better answers if you look for campaigns that are similar to your favorites in some way.
