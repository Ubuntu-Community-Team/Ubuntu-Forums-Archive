### Post by Melcar on 2009-12-08
Download the .tar archive from their website, unpack it, and run the installer "vsinstall.sh" from a terminal.  That works every time for me.  You can also [compile]("http://gwos.org/doku.php/guides:64bit:vega_strike") it as described in the game guides, but I never tried that before.
It doesn't look as good as X3, but most of it is space anyway :o.  Gameplay is fun and should keep you entertained for ever.  It's fun just to do cargo missions and sit back listening to the bitching soundtrack while your ship cruises he Universe; that is until you get ambushed by pirates ;).
