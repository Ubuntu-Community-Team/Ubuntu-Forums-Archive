### Post by hikaricore on 2007-06-06
The basic games in gnome come in one package.  So you can't remove just some.

However they don't take up much space, so your best bet is to remove the ones you
don't want from your Applications menu and just forget they exist.  ^_^

You can get to the option to edit the menu by right clicking on the menu button.

Hope this helps.
