### Post by Erik1984 on 2011-08-24
Bastard Tetris (bastet is the package name) is a small tetris variant to play in the terminal. Graphics are of course very simple but that's not where it's about. Bastet does exactly what you expect: being a bastard. It determines the block you need the most to complete lines and makes sure you are NOT getting that block :P This renders some traditional Tetris tactics (like leaving space open for a big block) useless.

I find this game extremely challenging and I have to confess that my high score so far is 350 points or 7 lines on the lowest level... :oops: What's your highscore on this little frustrating game?
