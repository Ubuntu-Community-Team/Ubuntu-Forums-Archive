### Post by Matt Yun on 2006-10-09
I play ET: True Combat Elite regularly at 800x600, and I do not get your problem when I exit the game; the screen resolution goes back to 1024x768 no problem.  

Try playing ET in "windowed" mode, instead of "fullscreen".  Although you lose the fullscreen immersion of the game, the nice thing about windowed mode is that you can still access the rest of your desktop when you use the ET "~" console.

BTW, I'm using an ATI Mobility 9000 with the open-source radeon driver.
