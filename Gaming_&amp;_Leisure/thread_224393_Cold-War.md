### Post by leech on 2006-07-27
Been waiting for this one for a long time, it came out for Windows at the end of last year, and the sad thing is, the only reason it really took this long to come out was because of Dreamcatcher being slow on the ball (if you read through Mindware's forums (Mindware Studios is the developer, Dreamcatcher is the publisher for Windows) it sounds like what happened was that Mindware had developed Windows and Linux simultaneously, but Dreamcatcher didn't want to publish the linux version, so linuxgamepublishing.com sent a letter to Dreamcatcher, and the guy in charge of such things was on a month long vacation!  Or it could have been longer, anyhow, basically most of the testing had been done, but because the Publisher was basically lazy, it's taken a lot longer for this to come out for Linux, but at least it's finally here.

Really, this just goes to show that a lot of developers themselves are willing to develop games for Linux, but the publishers aren't willing to risk the venture (basically it comes down to "Well, we'll certainly get x amount of money from Windows buyers who can get the game at Wal-Mart, but if we sell it for Linux, no stores carry linux games...")

I am going to have to save some $$ for this one :D
