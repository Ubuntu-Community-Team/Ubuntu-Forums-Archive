### Post by hikaricore on 2006-12-06
I used to have the game, but I don't anymore and I never really got into it.  The mouse issue you're experiencing is probably like alot of mouse related issues with wine and various direct3d applications.  In GTA Vice City the mouse will not leave the center of the screen no matter what you try, and I have a Hentai game called Masyou with the same issue.  I've experience issues with the mouse either being nonexistant or stuck in half a dozen other d3d games and even a few opengl games.  There won't be a quick fix for this, but you may want to check the [Wine Application Database]("http://appdb.winehq.org") later on today when it's back online to see if anyone has found a work around for your specific game.  Good luck.
