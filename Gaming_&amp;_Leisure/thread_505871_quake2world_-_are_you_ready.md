### Post by gdubbus on 2007-07-24
> needs a menu, an ingame server browser, and a way to setup you hud to show your weapon.

Quake players generally have a brutal fixation on killing efficiency, and it is my belief that this ideal of efficiency has gone into q2w. Having the weapon model shown in either the right or left hand is just a distraction from the target in front of you (cross hair), almost all competitive quake players play with the hand centered so the weapon doesn't show at all, also, many of the weapon model themselves are being remodeled so an entirely new hand would have to be developed for the sake of game consistency. As for the menu, the non-menu leans towards players with experience changing settings using the console, its actually quite an easy and powerful way of customizing all of your settings, and much more efficient than the bulky menus associated with quake3 based standalone games. In game server browsers also tend to be bulky/crappy, until they figure something out a streaming server list is at:

[http://dale.us/q2w/status/](http://dale.us/q2w/status/)

Try in the console : cmdlist and cvarlist

for a complete list of the quake 2 console commands check out : 

[http://console.planetquake.gamespy.com/commands/quake_2.html](http://console.planetquake.gamespy.com/commands/quake_2.html)

the game is under development so who knows what exactly will change as far as a menu/server browser goes, I'm not directly involved in any of it, I'm just a quake fanatic. :guitar:
