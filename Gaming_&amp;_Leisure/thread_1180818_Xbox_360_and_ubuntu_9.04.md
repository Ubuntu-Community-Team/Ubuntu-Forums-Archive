### Post by Dimitriid on 2009-06-07
If you don't mind using the play and charge kit? Yes, in fact its detected on Ubuntu 9.04 as soon as you plug it in, you only need a small extra app for joystic calibration sometimes.

If you want to use it wirelessly, then you need to buy an extra adapter and make those drivers work which is imo not that worth it, you might as well buy a pc wireless gamepad that is known to work on Ubuntu easier.

But as a wired USB controller it works great, I use one in fact ( I have the wired 360 controller )

### Post by AKK9 on 2009-06-07
Actually, you can't use a Play & Charge kit as a joystick cable.

Unfortunately, the Play & Charge kit USB cable is only capable of charging the controller, nothing more. Thus, you cannot use the wireless controller as a wired PC gamepad.
