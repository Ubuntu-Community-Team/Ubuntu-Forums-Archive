### Post by UtopianG on 2007-04-05
[http://www.tibia.com](http://www.tibia.com)

I currently play Tibia. Both windows and Linux clients. It's not the greatest, but they offer free play and pay-to-play options, with the free-to=play accounts being limited to the main continent, and other minor limitations.

They also offer non-pvp, semi-pvp (skull system limits amount of kills in a day, resulting in auto-banishment for going over the kills) and pvp-e with unlimited kills and faster rune delays.

I've played this game for about 7 years, and though while not the greatest, it offers a decent chat system, and you can multitask while playing it... i.e. I'm writing this while training a character's skills :)
