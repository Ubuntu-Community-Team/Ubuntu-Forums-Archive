### Post by Zelphien on 2007-11-11
I have Diablo 2 and I like it.

I believe you can run it on WINE, it worked for me fine. I used that link you looked at

I'd say you go buy it because its a great game created by Blizzard. Make sure you get the Expansion Set (Lord of Destruction) though. Diablo 2 is no good without



Diablo 2 has classes you can choose and play with(either Single Player or Battle.Net)

In Battle.Net you create an account and a list of characters. The Multiplayer is the same as Single Player, You create rooms and party up to fight monsters and complete quests or do special "runs" and level up faster. There is a good amount of Online Players and its fun. There are 4 Acts(5 Acts if you get the Expansion) and you complete quests and there is a boss at the end of every act. On Battle.Net, when you finish the 5th Act, you go back to the 1st one, but you go on the Nightmare difficulty. Once u beat the 5th Act on that, you go to the 1st one again on the Hell difficulty(the last one). The level cap is 99, may take a week or 2 to get a character to level 99 if you get really pro at the game. There is a chat feature in the room selection part of Battle.Net where you can talk to people

You can also switch servers to play on that have different chat rooms
You can also switch realms to play on if you live somewhere different in the world.


I give the game  4/5 stars. Good game, fun to play, you won't get bored of it that fast, and the gameplay is fun. The only problem is that it lags a bit if people either hack or massive cast spells/skills. The graphics are out of date too since the game came out around 2000?
