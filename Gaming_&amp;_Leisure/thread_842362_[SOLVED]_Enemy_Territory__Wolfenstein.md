### Post by lillypop on 2008-06-28
Like the previous poster said press L you will open up the Limbo screen menu there you will see 3 flags one for spectaor with a light under it wich it lite they meansd your are spectator. There will be 2 other flags one for axis and one for allies click on either flag to join a team.
If team balance is enabled you will have to join the team with the least amount of players unless teams are even.
once you have chosen your team then you need to chose which class you wish to be.
As default as soon as you join a team you will be  a soldier.
You will see under the flags some circles which represent the classes eg Soldier is a helmet inside the circle. 
Click on whichever class you want to be once you have clicked the class it will be highlighted under this you will then get a choice of weapons for that class click the weapons a box should display with all available weapons for that class select which weapon then click ok at the bottom of the screen.
You will then join a team.

Note some servers have limitations on how many medics solders and field ops are allowed per team so if you chose one of those and they are up to the limit you will probably be forced as a solider until you change class in limbo menu again.
