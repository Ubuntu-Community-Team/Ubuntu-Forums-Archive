### Post by straxus on 2008-05-28
I'm a host on the Evil Avatar Radio podcast, and Monday we interviewed the Joel DeYoung, COO of Hothead Games and producer of Penny Arcade Adventures.  I asked him what was behind their decision to release the game on Windows, Mac, and Linux, and whether that was something we could expect more of from Hothead, or if it would be unique to PAA.  The first (and best) part of his response was "It's just the right thing to do."  :D :D

I hope I don't seem self-promoting since I'm on the podcast.  It was just so good to hear I had to share it with the community.  I can't wait to see what else comes out of Hothead.  I haven't played many PC games since switching a few years back, because I loath dual-booting.
