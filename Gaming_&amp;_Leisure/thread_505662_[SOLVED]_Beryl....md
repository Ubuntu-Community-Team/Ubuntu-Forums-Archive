### Post by weirdlookinguy on 2007-07-20
Hey guys, I don't know if this is the right spot to post this, but I installed Beryl (XGL on 7.04) and it's working great, the only problem is that every time I start up my laptop I have to go into terminal and type beryl-manager.  Can I get this to start up by itself every time I log in?  thanks

---

### Post by Steven_B on 2007-07-20
Go into System>Preferences>Sessions, and click "New".  Put something like "Beryl" for the name, and "beryl-manager" for the command, and click ok.  That should be it!
