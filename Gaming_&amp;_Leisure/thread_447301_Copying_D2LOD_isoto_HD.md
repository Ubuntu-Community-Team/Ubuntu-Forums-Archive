### Post by Prisoner_97p904 on 2007-05-19
Hi.

I wanted to do the same thing, with a D2LoD install, where I don't want to insert my LoD cd all the time, so I had to use a crack, and then i made an ISO of my LoD cd.

First i installed D2 and the Lod and then update. Now I downloaded a crack for LoD and then I made an ISO of my LoD cd (with this command: "dd if=/dev/cdrom of=LoD.iso", remember to umount the cd first).

Then I used a script for enabling b.net, found here:
Description: [http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=315](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iVersionId=315) 
Script: [http://www.poksi.org/%7Ehifi/lnxd2load/D2Loader-current.sh](http://www.poksi.org/%7Ehifi/lnxd2load/D2Loader-current.sh)

I renamed my Game.exe to Game.exe.orig and then the cracked to Game.exe.crk, and changed the paths in the script to match my system, and then it worked!:)
