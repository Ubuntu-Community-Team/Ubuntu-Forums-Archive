### Post by Romeo9 on 2013-01-15
Paradox Development Studio has ported Crusader Kings 2 and all DLC to Linux and is now available for purchase on Steam either individually or as a [collection]("http://store.steampowered.com/sub/14254/").

Paradox Development Studio is looking to bring all of its future games to Linux with Europa Universalis IV' (coming in Q3 this year). 
Here's a [Link]("http://www.ubuntuvibes.com/2013/01/much-awaited-strategy-game-crusader.html") to the article.

Looks like Paradox is also bringing out another game in Q1 of this year still, Salem. 
Here the link to more information on [Salem]("http://www.paradoxplaza.com/games/salem#about_game-tab")

If Paradox was serious about releasing all their new games on Linux then we have a few good games to look forward to this year:[Link]("http://www.paradoxplaza.com/games/upcoming")
