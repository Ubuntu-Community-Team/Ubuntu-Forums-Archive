### Post by sloggerkhan on 2008-04-02
Nexuiz-- Great Free FPS
Tremulous-- Class based FPS
Alien Arena-- FPS
Warsow-- Difficult and very fast wall-jump circuit FPS
World of Padman-- Toony FPS
Wolfenstein: Enemy Territory-- Objective based class FPS
OpenArena-- FPS

I don't recommend installing any of them from the repos. Instead get the binary for the latest version, as often times the repos are a bit out of date and this you can't join games 'cause of version mismatch.
