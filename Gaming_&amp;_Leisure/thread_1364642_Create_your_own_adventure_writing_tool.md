### Post by tadcan on 2009-12-26
I have good news, bad news and sorta good news on this front. 

The good news is a GUI tool exits by adventure game studios to do this kind of Crpg [http://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/](http://www.adventuregamestudio.co.uk/) Many of its components are GPL'd and overall the licence says its free to use as long as the games you make are not sold. If you do want to sell your creations then the licence says you must pay a fee. 

The bad news is that it only works in windows.

The sorta good news is that the build tool has an Ubuntu port that is being worked on. This will allow you to make a native version for 9.04. 

I haven't tried any of this, but from the tutorial it looks fairly straight forward.
