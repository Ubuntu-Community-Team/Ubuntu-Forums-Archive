### Post by compiledkernel on 2008-02-15
Memory serving properly, the NeoGeo was an attempt to bring an Arcade machine home to a home unit. Its media (which were essentially a glorified PCB cards) were the same level of card used already in many stand up machines, just relying on the NeoGeo console to provide the additional hard ware (I seem to remember that the Console itself was relatively large in size). 

The console went for like 600$ and the games where in the 175$ to 250$ range.
