### Post by desicratn on 2007-06-01
I have no idea about the keys. However the brightness is probably the screensave kicking on. Try disabling the screensaver and see if that helps. This was happening to me in ET and was driving me nuts until I noticed it took about 13 minutes to happen, the same amount of time as the saver. ONce I shut it off I have not had the problem since.
