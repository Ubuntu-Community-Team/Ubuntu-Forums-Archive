### Post by lykeion on 2010-09-11
With GNU Backgammon you can 

Play Human vs Human:
Settings > Players (set both  players as Human)

Configure graphics settings (board appearance, 2D/3D colors, etc):
Settings > Appearance

As an avid backgammon player with 15+ years of playing experience I can easily say that gnubg beat most bg software out there, including Windows software like Snowie, BGBlitz, JellyFish

I'd suggest you try gnubg out and get familiar with the menu/settings, it's one of the best bg software there is.
