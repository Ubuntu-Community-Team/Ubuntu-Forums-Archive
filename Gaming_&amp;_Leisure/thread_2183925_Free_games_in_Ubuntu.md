### Post by General_Faliure on 2013-10-27
You can find lots of free games in the repositories (software center or Synaptic)
There are also lots of emulators; Mame, for the arcades, Mess, for old (game) computers, Daphne for laserdisk games.
Also you have emulators for older game consoles; Nes, Super nintendo, Nintendo 64, playstation 1 and 2, and still a lot more.
You may have to add extra PPA's for the best results.
I have built an arcade cab based on Lubuntu: here's the link: [https://picasaweb.google.com/102745845720316376753/ArcadeCab?authuser=0&feat=directlink](https://picasaweb.google.com/102745845720316376753/ArcadeCab?authuser=0&feat=directlink)
