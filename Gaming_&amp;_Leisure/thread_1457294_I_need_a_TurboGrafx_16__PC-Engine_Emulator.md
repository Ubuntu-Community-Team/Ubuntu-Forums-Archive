### Post by williswalteris on 2010-04-21
You can try Mednafen. 

*"Mednafen is a portable, utilizing OpenGL and SDL,  argument(command-line)-driven multi-system emulator with many advanced  features.  The Atari Lynx, GameBoy (Color), GameBoy Advance, NES, [COLOR=Red]PC  Engine(TurboGrafx 16)[/COLOR], SuperGrafx, Neo Geo Pocket (Color), PC-FX, and  WonderSwan (Color) are emulated."*
