### Post by oldrocker99 on 2009-05-05
I know it's not free, but a mere $20 will get you Neverwinter Nights Diamond, certainly one of the best RPGs ever, and still going strong after 7 years. There are 60-80 hours of gameplay included, and a nearly infinite number of absolutely free modules (a module is a separate adventure) on Neverwinter Vault. Some are multichapter and epic, some are 15 minutes long, and everything in between. You can't go wrong with any of the Vault's Hall of Fame modules. It has a NATIVE Linux client, and all the official downloads and patches come in Linux forms.

The Bioware Linux forum has a step-by-step guide to install the entire game, including the movies, to run on Linux.

This is a company which has supported this platform since 2003, and it's not much to reward them for their perseverance. 

Unless you refuse to have non-free software on your system, in which case my boosterism is moot.
