### Post by Rubykuby on 2011-10-30
Guild Wars, The Sims 3 and Adobe Photoshop CS4 (not CS5) work under Wine with minimal tweaking.

Under My Documents you'll find an "Electronic Arts" folder. Back it up and copy it to Linux (/home/yourusernamehere/.) once you've completely installed the Sims 3 under it. This should carry over saved information.

The rest I'll leave to other posters, as I have no clue about your other requirements.
