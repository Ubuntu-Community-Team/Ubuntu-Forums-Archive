### Post by Warren Watts on 2009-09-22
Sort of off topic, but my favorite memory of my C64 days involved the making of "flippy-floppies".  

I had the [1541]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Commodore_1541") single sided floppy drive, with a whopping 170**KB** capacity.  A box of ten 5.25" floppies cost $10, basically $1.00 per disk.  

In order to cut down on the number of expensive floppies I had to buy, I would take a handheld paper punch and notch the opposite side of the floppy so I could flip it over and use the other side of the disk too, thus reducing my cost per disk to fifty cents.
