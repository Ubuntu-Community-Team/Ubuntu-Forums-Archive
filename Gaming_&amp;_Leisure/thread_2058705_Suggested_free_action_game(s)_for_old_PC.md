### Post by Am Elder on 2012-09-20
I have several recommendations.  I'm pretty sure your computer could run [BZFlag]("http://bzflag.org/")!  It could certainly run [Ur-Quan Masters]("http://sc2.sourceforge.net/") -- though that may not be enough of an action game for your friend.  Also [Maelstrom]("https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maelstrom_%281992_video_game%29"). All of those are available in the repositories.  It might do [Urban Terror]("http://www.urbanterror.info/home/")?  Worth a shot, anyway.

The up-to-date AlephOne engine that runs the Marathon games that Sealbhach mentions also runs some great Marathon Infinity mods.  The best of them, IMO, are [Marathon Rubicon]("http://www.marathonrubicon.com/") and [Eternal]("http://eternal.bungie.org/main.php").
