### Post by Rhubarb on 2006-09-27
Yep, UT2004 is gooooooooooood.  Just get the DVD version to save you changing all the CDs.

Tried UT2003, had no vehicles.
vehicles = goodness = vehicular manslaughter.

You don't need Cedega to run UT2004, 'tis as easy as:
sudo sh linuxinstaller.sh

Then by using wine or unzipping the bonuspacks into your /usr/local/games/ut2004/ directory.

If the resolution is fixed at some crappy setting (say 800x600), change the x and the y resolution in ~/.ut2004/ut2004.ini (or was it user.ini?)
The ECEbonus pack was a little tricky to install, had to use 7zip in wine to do that

There is a UT2004 installation thread here somewhere in the forums.  I'm just too lazy to look it up for you at the moment. :cool:
