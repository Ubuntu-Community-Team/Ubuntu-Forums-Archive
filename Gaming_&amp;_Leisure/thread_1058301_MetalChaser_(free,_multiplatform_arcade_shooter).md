### Post by nickless on 2009-02-02
[CENTER][IMG]http://metalchaser.hannes-flor.de/img/shot2.jpg[/IMG][/CENTER]

In the last two semester a few of my friends and myself developed a little 3D-Shooter. Though it is not very good, it is fun to play (for a while) and there is a online-highscore! :D
We wrote the game in java so it runs under linux, mac and windows. You don't even have to install it! Just go to [www.metalchaser.de (link)]("http://metalchaser.de") download the latest version, unpack it in your homefolder (or wherever you like) and run the "linux starter" script by double clicking on it or rightclick -> run in terminal. :)

The Goal is to get through the 5 level as fast as you can, while elimination as many enemies as possible. On the beginning of every level you can place 10 points in skills to enhance your "mech". Your enemies will also leave upgrades which you can use to make your mech even stronger.

If you like, give it a try, maybe you'll like it ;)
