### Post by holastickboy on 2012-08-12
I am currently using the Razer Lachesis mouse on both Windows and Linux.  I do play quite a bit of games, so a mouse with adjustable DPI is what I like. Despite not using any Razer software on Linux, the buttons to increase and decrease DPI appear to work correctly (and I haven't done anything special to get this working either).  I have owned both Logitech and Razer mice, and both are excellent.  The Razer Lachesis is fairly cheap, and has some nice blue lighting that "breaths" according to DPI sensitivity (the higher the DPI, the faster the breathing pulse of lighting, etc).
