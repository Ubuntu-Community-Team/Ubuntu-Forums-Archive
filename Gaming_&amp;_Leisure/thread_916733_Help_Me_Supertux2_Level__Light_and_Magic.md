### Post by drewster1829 on 2008-11-11
Use the "Action" key (Left-Ctrl, by default) to pick up and carry the lamps (you have to hold the key down).  The colored blocks that are dashed lines will turn solid when you get the correct colored lamp's light on it.

The first thing you do is pick up the green lamp and move it far enough away from the block the red lamp is on, the pickup the red land after it falls to the ground and carry it and jump on the blocks over the ravine.  They will turn solid as the red light hits them.

Then, when you get where the bomber guy is, take the blue lamp and set it on the pair of blocks above the ground.  The single block above that takes blue *and* green light, so you have to keep going to the right and get the green lamp, and carry it with you.  When there's blue and green light on the single block, it should turn solid.  There's a mushroom/flower in the block above.

If you keep going to the right, you'll notice that your path is blocked by the green lamp on the right side of the three vertical blocks.  If you grab one of the red lamps on the way, you can jump up to the top along the red blocks.  At this point, however, you'll have to get the red lamp far enough away that the three blocks don't block your path when the red light makes it solid.  Then, go to the right, get the green lamp out of the way (so the green blocks are **not** solid), then grab the red lamp to get over the next ravine.  There's a white lamp around there, too, but it's pretty useless, since there's no way to carry it to the right of the 3 vertical green and 3 vertical red blocks (both sets turn solid in the white light).

You should get to the next ravine with a blue lamp in the middle.  It's a little tricky with that yellow guy, but a few tries should get it. Grab the red lamp and jump onto the red blocks.  Wait until the yellow guy is out of the way, then jump on the set of blocks it's circling (with the blue lamp). Note that these blocks take red _and_ blue light to become solid.  Avoiding that yellow guy, grab the blue lamp, then carry it to the next set of blocks (blue).

There's a red lamp here, and you can either carry one or the other (you won't need them if you're Big Tux, but I never am at this point.  If you're big, you can just break a wooden block and run up top.)  You'll need them where there's a dark patch before the last ravine.  There's a candle in a valley with a bad guy..drop the lamp or jump on him to kill him.  Carry the lamp to the right side of the ravine, and drop it somewhere that it's not in your way (but so you can still see).  There are blocks in the middle, but I couldn't figure out which colors they need to become solid..blue and green, maybe.  It's irrelevant, though, because it's a narrow enough ravine to run and jump over.  Then, walk into the outhouse thing, and you're done!

I hope this helps. I actually just beat this level yesterday.  If you need any other help, let me know.
