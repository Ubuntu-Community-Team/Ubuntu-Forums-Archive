### Post by Crunchy the Headcrab on 2009-08-02
If you are interested in strategy games give The Battle for Wesnoth a try.  It's free and open source.  I just beat the main campaign this week (Heir to the Throne) but there are a lot of other campaigns to play and multiplayer too.  I haven't tried multiplayer because I've never been good at strategy games online.  Anyway Wesnoth is amazing.  It seems really simple at first but your units level up and depending on which way you decide to upgrade them there are a lot of combinations to use.  It's got great music and gameplay and the art, while simple, is very good.

Current stable version is 1.6.4  I'm using another distro, so I don't know which version is in the Ubuntu repositories.
