### Post by gwoodard on 2007-11-02
The Intel 82815 Graphics Controller is the "onboard video" that you normally see below the parallel plug (video is blue)... eye candy is just graphic mods to improve the look of the desktop and certain programs, usually you would want a video card (64 mb video card at least and you can go up to 1 GB ((if AGP=brown slot, 512 mb if PCI=white slot (((Highly DO NOT suggest))); or 2 GB if PCI-E))=orange or purple slot)...Now thats if you want 3-D effects but the onboard is good for 2-D programs (If the computer came with Windows Vista auto installed then onboard is 64 mb)

Oh and by the way how much "ram" (Random Access Memory) do you have?
512 mb, 256 mb, 128 mb?  4 gb, 2 gb, or 1 gb?

GBs or GigaBytes are higher than the MBs or MegaBytes
