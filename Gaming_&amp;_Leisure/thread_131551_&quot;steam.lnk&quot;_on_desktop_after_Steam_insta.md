### Post by polpak on 2006-02-16
Wine just makes windows programs think that your ~/Desktop directory is your windows desktop, so the installer for steam tried to create the shortcut to Steam on your desktop. In windows shortcuts have a .lnk extention. You can delete this file, it doesn't do anything.
