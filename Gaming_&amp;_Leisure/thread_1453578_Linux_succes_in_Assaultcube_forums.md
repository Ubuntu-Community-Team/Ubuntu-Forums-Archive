### Post by MindFusion on 2010-04-13
Hi all, as you might or might not know i'm a dedicated Assaultcube player for over 2 years now, and I recently joined the official AC forum. There I started a poll on what OS the forum members were using, since Assaultcube is a cross platform, open-source game.
I got the following results:

Windows : ** 53.49% **
Mac : **16.29%**
Linux : **30.23%**

I was really surprised that Linux is being used by almost a third of the Assaultcube community, because I had expected Windows' dominance to be way bigger.

Positive result if you ask me!
