### Post by dli8ilb on 2011-01-24
this has been a problem with debian (sid) since at least October 2008 - which is about when i first started using linux.

thankfully, i have found a temporary solution..
[LIST]
[*]go here: [http://packages.debian.org/unstable/games/flobopuyo](http://packages.debian.org/unstable/games/flobopuyo)
[*]scroll down to the download section, and grab the package for your architecture.
[*]run the .deb file and upgrade to the new version.
[/LIST]

that's all.  have fun, and enjoy sound in FloboPuyo! :KS
