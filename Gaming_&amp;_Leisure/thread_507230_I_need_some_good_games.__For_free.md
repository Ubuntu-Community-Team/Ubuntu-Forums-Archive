[http://linuxemu.retrofaction.com/](http://linuxemu.retrofaction.com/)

And one quick stop you might want to make is to GetDeb.net's gaming section for a few quick downloads (if you see something you like): [http://www.getdeb.net/category.php?id=3](http://www.getdeb.net/category.php?id=3)

This has been asked before many times.  The problem with "really good games" is personal matter of opinion.
Try to be more specific when asking about such things and differences of opinion make answering you in a way you would like very very complicated.

To be honest I suggest you look a little more and see what's out there, and decide for yourself what you like.  That's part of what free software is all about.
