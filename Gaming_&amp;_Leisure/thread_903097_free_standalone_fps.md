### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-08-27
It's possible to run sauerbraten faster by disabling the shaders:
Start the game up, go in to options, then gfx.
Turn shaders off, water, pretty much everything until it's running smooth.

You could try out assault cube, it doesn't require much graphics power at all:
[http://assault.cubers.net/](http://assault.cubers.net/)
