### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2018-08-20
Like most keys in MAME the volume controls are user definable.  By default they are assigned to the keys 'minus' and 'equals' (top-row of the main key field, just to the left of the backspace-key). Strangely, I've never heard any change in volume when using those keys or to any other I've set. The only volume controls in MAME that work reliable for me are in the menu you get when hitting 'tab' while in a game and selecting 'Slider Controls'.
