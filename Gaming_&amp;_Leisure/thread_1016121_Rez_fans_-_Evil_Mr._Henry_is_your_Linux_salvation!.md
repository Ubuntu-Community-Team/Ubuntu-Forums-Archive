### Post by BigSilly on 2008-12-19
Anyone here heard of the [Kenta Cho]("http://www.asahi-net.or.jp/~cs8k-cyu/index_e.html") shoot 'em up games? They are a collection of electro-charged 2D classic shooters, with an abstract theme. They have been ported over to Linux by a chap called Evil Mr. Henry (I don't know when), and they are absolutely fantastic. Truly addictive, with some over the top visuals and tremendous techno soundtracks.

If you're a fan of classic 3D shooter Rez, you'll find much to enjoy with these games I promise you. I'm using Intrepid, and they look to be all available at your fingertips through Synaptic. Look for Torus Trooper, Titanion, Parsec47, rRootage and A7Xpg in there. There's probably more, but that's a pretty good start I reckon. Plus, I don't know if this will be the same for you, but I've found they work automatically with my joypad without any configuration.

I don't know who's responsible for getting these in the repository, but well done! What a fantastic collection of free games. Thanks Ubuntu! 

Give 'em a go shooter fans.
