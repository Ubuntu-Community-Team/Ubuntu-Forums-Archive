### Post by forrestcupp on 2008-12-11
Other than Nexuiz, there aren't any free single player FPS with campaigns.  You can get plenty of commercial ones to work with wine, though.

It takes too much time and work and manpower to make a viable campaign FPS.  That's why they're all commercial.  So for free, we get the same cookie cutter multiplayer game reincarnated into a thousand different sceneries and names.

It's not a FPS, but check out Yo Frankie.
