### Post by dragos240 on 2009-08-05
Pretty easy actually.

Just start a singleplayer game,
Press 'C' to hold or relax all the joints.
Press 'Z' to extend or contract a joint or muscle
Press 'X' to relax a single muscle or joint.

And just experiment. The tutorial really helped also.
