### Post by anil_robo on 2005-12-23
Slune is a 3D car racing-crashing game. It's free, and included (but not installed) with Ubuntu repositories by default. The game features nice, clean 3D graphics using python, and enjoyable soundtracks.

** Screenshots:**

[ATTACH]4784[/ATTACH] [ATTACH]4782[/ATTACH] [ATTACH]4783[/ATTACH] [ATTACH]4781[/ATTACH]

** Features:**
Single, multiplayer (peer2peer) support
Campaign mode available (Tux and Gnu must supply AIDS medicine to Africa)
Four characters to choose from: Tux, Gnu, Shark and Python
Five vehicles to choose from: Car, Truck, Scooter, Tanker and Biplane
Three difficulty levels to choose from: Newbie, Hacker and Guru.
Resolution: Minimum 320x240, Maximum 1680x1050 (both window and fullscreen modes available)
Graphic quality can be set to high/medium/low (useful for slower systems)
Sound and Music available!
You can choose sound output method (Open AL or SD mixer)

** Installation:**
From terminal: ```
sudo apt-get install slune
``` From menu: Applications-->Add applications--> Games--> More Programs--> Slune
Tip: Installing python-psyco significantly improves the quality of the game.
```
sudo apt-get install python-psyco
``` 

**How to play:**
From terminal: type slune and press enter
From menu: Applications--> Games--> Slune

** Keys:**
right, left, up and down arrow keys (standard racing games)
Shift: Jump (keep pressed for very high jumps)
Alt: Nitro booster!

** Mouse actions:**
Move mouse left and right to steer
Move mouse forward and back to control speed
Left mouse button: Jump slightly
Middle mouse button: Jump very high
Right mouse button: Nitro boost

Replayability of the game is low for advanced gamers, but it's a nice game for a change after a long typing session! :) Go ahead and try it!
