### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2022-06-09
'mednaffe' (**m**y **e**mulator **d**oes **n**eed **a** **f**...ing **f**ront-**e**nd) isn't an emulator, it's front-end for mednafen (**m**y **e**mulator **d**oesn't **n**eed **a** **f**...ing **n**ame). The keys for mednafen are documented in '/usr/share/doc/mednafen/mednafen.html'. Alt-Shift-n - with n being a number - configures key assignments for controller number 'n'. 

In mednaffe you can do key/button assignments by selecting the 'Systems' tab, then selecting a system in the left pane and select the 'Input' tab in the right pane.
