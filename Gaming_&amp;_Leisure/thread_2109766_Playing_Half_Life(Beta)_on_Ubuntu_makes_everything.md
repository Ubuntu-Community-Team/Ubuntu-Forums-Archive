### Post by BlushBerry on 2013-02-05
I am having the same issue but I have found a temporary fix.

1. Right click half-life (beta) and click properties
2. Click set launch options
3. in the launch options type -window -noborder
4. Click ok and close out of the window
5. start up the game and if its not already. put it at the highest resolution

It should look like it's full screen but its really not. once again temporary fix. I am actually looking into it as i type this

Hope this helps!~
