### Post by oldrocker99 on 2012-11-08
NetHack is pretty ancient; I started playing it in 1989 on my Amiga, long before Linus even got started. It was a much-modified (by a collaboration of people connected by early networks) version of Hack, a UNIX game derived from the grandaddy of all games of its type, Rogue, which is why all top-down dungeon-crawlers are called "roguelike."

BTW, NetHack is still out there, and packs more entertainment per byte thann just about any other game out there.
