### Post by jerrygofixit on 2006-06-19
For the life of me, no matter what I did, which guide I followed, I could NOT get Steam to work without some stupid error. File this thread under duh/obvious if you want, but what I did was copied all the files from a working Windows Steam install to my Steam wine directory and voila, it asked me for my login, updated the platform files, and I was on my way. So if you have the means, and you've tried everything else under the sun, I suggest you give it a go, what have you got to lose? Good luck everyone who are in my former predicament!
