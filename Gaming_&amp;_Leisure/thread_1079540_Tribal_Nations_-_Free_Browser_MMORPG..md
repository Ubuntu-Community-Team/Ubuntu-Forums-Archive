Hello again friends !

This is another high quality browser game - free to play.
This game is not about fighting but about living.

You begin this game as a villager in a 15 century town and work your way up as in real life.
With realistic economy, politics and laws of the old time and on top of all great graphics you strive to survive, gain money and maybe even become a mayor of the town.

There are no screenshots to post but you can enter the website and see a preview.
