### Post by AKK9 on 2009-06-07
I can't believe it runs so much better in Ubuntu!

In XP I have to turn of Decals and lower the resolution to 1024 x 768 to get an averaging 27fps. In Wine I can clearly see I'm averaging way above 30fps at my native resolution and everything on high.

My specs are:

AMD Phenom X4 9500
2 GB RAM
Nvidia GeForce 8400 GS

Note: Some of you may already know this but the 8400 GS, despite it's fairly new model number and it's 'G..' suffix, is NOT a gaming GPU. This card is solely a multimedia card and mostly contains loads of media decoders, even some that the later 8xxx and 9xxx GT's don't have. It obviously has decent 3D capibilities and can run a few new games (on very low settings) but even an old 7 series could destroy it when it comes to gaming. I thought I'd just let you know before people start questioning my fps in Halo.

So yeah, I'm in shock?! I can play Halo better on a system that it wasn't built for! Wow! :D

The only problem is that the mouse movements are very rough and jittery. I've turned the mouse sensitivity down in the game but I think it's the way Ubuntu or Wine moves the mouse. Any ideas on this?
