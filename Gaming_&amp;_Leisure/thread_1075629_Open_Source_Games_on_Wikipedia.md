### Post by Abras on 2009-02-20
Hi,

I just thought I'd drop in and call your attention to something that's been bugging me for some time: the [articles about open source games on Wikipedia]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_open_source_games"). At first glance, it seems like a decent list of notable open source games, but it has its problems. The various game articles simply aren't of particularly high quality. Most of them are rather short and have some kind of problem (often not enough sources/citations). I think we should do something to improve these articles if we can, before some of them get deleted or merged. I have plenty more to say on the subject but I'll leave it at that for now. 

In the meantime, what do you guys say? What do these articles need?

Abras
