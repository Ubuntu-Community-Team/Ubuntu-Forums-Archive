### Post by oldrocker99 on 2014-10-15
Ths is hardly the first Linux game from Steam with a rocky rollout. Patience; it's in their best interest to make the game playable. I remember having to tweak something to get DOTA 2 to display properly on Day One.

And everything you read about nVidia versus ATI on these forums and on just abut every other Linux forum is true. The ATI proprietary drivers are (sometimes) actually inferior to the FOSS ATI drivers, and the proprietary nVidia drivers are far superior to the FOSS nVidia drivers. The bottom line is that, whenever possible, buy nVidia if you're using Linux.
