### Post by Velociraptor on 2007-11-16
I had a similar problem, it would come out of full-screen mode shortly after starting up (at the select user screen, i think). And I couldn't use the mouse/keyboard.

What worked for me was turning off advanced desktop effects (compiz) before starting the game. (System -> Preferences -> Appearance -> Visual Effects tab ->  None)
