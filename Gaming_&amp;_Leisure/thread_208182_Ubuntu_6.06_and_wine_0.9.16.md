### Post by DarkGreen16 on 2006-07-03
It has been a while since I have ran world of warcraft on linux. Just got it going again a couple of days ago.

I first tried using the ubuntu package of 0.9.16 but the game crashed when you walked inside with your minimap open. I also couldn't target anything either. However, I found out they released a patch on the winehq site with directions on building wine from the source. So far I am happy with the results. Before in windows with my geforce 6800 GT i had serious performance issues in places like MC (tis why I quit playing world of warcraft) but in linux the game never stutters when loading characters and I seem to get a pretty constant FPS. Still have a couple sound issues though.

indoors not to busy = 60 fps
indoors busy (ironforge AH) = 30ish fps
outdoors =30ish fps
outdoors busy = lowest i've seen so far is about 20 fps

hope that gives you an idea on what to expect.
