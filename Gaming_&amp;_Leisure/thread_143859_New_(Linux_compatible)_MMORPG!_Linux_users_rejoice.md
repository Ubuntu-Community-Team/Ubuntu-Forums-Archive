### Post by webcrawler03 on 2009-09-24
Quite possibly the worst MMO iv ever played. First off... its not free. Secondly... pvp is non existent. Iv tried and gotten suspension on my account and a huge loss of gold. You can try it but i definatly dont recomend it. Although you do get a huge xp reward in the end the loss doesnt seem worth it. The career path of the game also sucks. You have two options, pay for a great paying career and after about lvl 40 or so it starts to even out and you can become quite wealthy, or opt out and take a normal career and lose most of your weekly earnings to expenses. You of course could go without shelter or transportation to cut those expenses but its not likely that you will keep your career. Either way you take be prepared for a mundane career experience. 
Gathering wealth takes up almost 50% of your daily game play, and the devs created it so you cant play more than one days worth in 24 hrs with really sucks. I cant find any bots to simulate the work experience for me. There is a career path that is free (you dont have to pay for the "education upgrade") and is quite easy and dosent cost you hardly any of your daily game play. You simply buy and sell illegal products to other members so they can enhance their game play. The problem is if the mods find out your account gets a huge suspension. If you want a real in depth account you can simply make decisions to have it that way. The learning curve is slow, lvling takes forever, you get greater xp with higher lvls. All in all, your better off running a dual boot system and playing something like The Gates of Anderon or WOW than trying to play Real Life.
