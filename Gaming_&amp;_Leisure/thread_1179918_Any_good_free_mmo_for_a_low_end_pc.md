### Post by Scouto2 on 2009-06-09
Three words:
Kingdom of Loathing

[www.kingdomofloathing.com](www.kingdomofloathing.com)

It's a very addicting game. I have played it for a loooong time. It's completely free, and if you donate $10 in one sitting (not donating $5 today and $5 tomorrow), you can get something called a Mr. Acessory, for use in Mr. Store, which has a monthly Item of the Month. There are no donater-only parts of the game, and it's entirely browser-based. It is pretty much a collection of hyperlinks, so even a "Netbook" could use it.
