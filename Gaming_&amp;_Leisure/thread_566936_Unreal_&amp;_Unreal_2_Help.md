Assuming you mean the original Unreal game and its sequel (and not Unreal Tournament):

There was a native installer for Unreal 1 using modified Unreal Tournament binaries, but I never could get that one to work properly at all.  I think it was slightly flawed since it never wanted to even try to run right regardless.

Unreal 2 never had Linux binaries.

Therefore, the best way is through Wine (or Cedega).  I have no idea how well Unreal 2 runs (check WineHQ's database) but I know from experience that Unreal 1 runs *perfectly*, and at full speed, from both the original CD and the Unreal Gold (Unreal + Na Pali) CD.
