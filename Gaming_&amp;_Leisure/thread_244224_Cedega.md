### Post by DoktorSeven on 2006-08-26
Go to [Transgaming's site](http://transgaming.com) and pay $15 for 3 months of Cedega; you'll get a download and instructions on how to install.

Another (more advanced) option is to [build Cedega from source](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=193814&highlight=cedega+cvs), but it won't have all the features (like the CD authentication that some games need to run).  This method is free, but completely unsupported by Transgaming.
