### Post by Grishka on 2008-12-28
it looks like not many point and click adventure games are published these days, even on Windows, but there are a few titles available. first, you might want to try ScummVM versions of several classic games: [Beneath a Steel Sky]("apt://beneath-a-steel-sky"), [Flight of the Amazon Queen]("apt://flight-of-the-amazon-queen") (in the repos, just click the name of the game in my post), as well as Lure of the Temptress and Drascula from ScummVM homepage ([http://www.scummvm.org/](http://www.scummvm.org/)). a few modern titles are available as well, for example the Penny Arcade Adventures series ([http://www.rainslick.com/](http://www.rainslick.com/)), or Sam & Max series (Windows only, but work well under Wine. [http://www.telltalegames.com/samandmax/](http://www.telltalegames.com/samandmax/)).
