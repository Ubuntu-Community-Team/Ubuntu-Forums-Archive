### Post by R33D3M33R on 2012-06-16
[Spiral Knights]("http://www.spiralknights.com/play.xhtml") is a browser based free to play MMORPG. It's team based hack and slash, but unless you pay or trade your hard earned coins for energy, you can play only for limited time a day. You could also check out [Bastion]("http://supergiantgames.com/?page_id=242"), it's not free, but extremely fun to play.
