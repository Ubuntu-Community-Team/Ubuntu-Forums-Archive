### Post by dannyboy79 on 2014-10-14
i believe all those games you will have to play them within an emulator, it'll be easiest to use playonlinux since you're new to linux (ubuntu) i'm linking you the POL ppa you should enable to get that installed and then to WineHQ which has each of the games listed and whether they work in Wine (wine is the emulator that will run the game like it's running within windows)
WineHQ = [https://appdb.winehq.org/](https://appdb.winehq.org/)
PlayOnLinux = [https://www.playonlinux.com/en/download.html](https://www.playonlinux.com/en/download.html)

good luck!
