### Post by tommcd on 2009-03-01
> **inuit-joe said:**
> How do i get Quake to work on linux?
If you are talking about Quake 4, download the linux installer from here to your home directory:
[ftp://ftp.idsoftware.com/idstuff/quake4/linux/](ftp://ftp.idsoftware.com/idstuff/quake4/linux/)
Then do **chmod +x quake4-linux-1.4.2.x86.run** 
to make it executable. Then follow the instructions in post #9 of this thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=168539&highlight=install+quake](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=168539&highlight=install+quake)
