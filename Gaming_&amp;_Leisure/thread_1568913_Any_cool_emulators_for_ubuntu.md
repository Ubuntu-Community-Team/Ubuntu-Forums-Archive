### Post by slave-zeo on 2010-09-06
Sure there are a lot of good emulators for Ubuntu Linux. Emulation is about the only gaming I do really. Below are a little list to help you out in your search.

Nintendo64 - Mupen64plus - Install from software center - Works really well with really good compatibility.

Dreamcast - lxdream - [http://www.lxdream.org](http://www.lxdream.org) - Works so so, not as good as NullDC under Windows. Getting better though. Needs DC BIOS to work.

Playstation 2 - PCSX2 - [http://pcsx2.net/](http://pcsx2.net/) - Only works under 32bit linux. Works really well and great compatibility. Needs PS2 BIOS to work. Real fast machine helps.

MSX & MSX2 -  I'm not sure about really since I've never messed with it.
