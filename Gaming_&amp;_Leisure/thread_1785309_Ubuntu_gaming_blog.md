### Post by brencameron on 2011-06-20
Boris, your blog seems to be off to a good start - keep it up.

I also have a gaming blog, based indirectly on Ubuntu (I use Mint 11, which uses the Ubuntu repositories). I do not score the games but give a "recommended" or "not really recommended" sort of rating. I also mention whether the game is in the repositories or has to be installed separately, and also if the game has a Windows version.
 So far I've been covering whole genres with one or two posts per genre. Fairly soon I will start focusing on one or two games per post, and talk about them in more detail. (Mainly because I am running out of genres to talk about). 
I think there are several other blogs out there too, but we should definitely support each other - more blogs about using Linux to play games is certainly not a bad thing! 
I'm going to add your blog to my blogroll.
I blogged for 1 month, took a year-long break, and finally got serious and have been doing between 3 and 5 articles a month since last December. It's definitely fun and a bit of a challenge. See my signature for the link.
