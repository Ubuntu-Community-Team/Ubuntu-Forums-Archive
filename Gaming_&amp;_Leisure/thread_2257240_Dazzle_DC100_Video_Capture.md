### Post by dhostick on 2014-12-18
I've been capturing old VHS PAL home videos and editing in Kdenlive to eventually put them on DVD and my Humax. It took a while to get the resolution and sound to a decent quality and I thought I'd share my scripts with anyone else trying a similar exercise. I use Ubuntu 14.04 LTS.

**showDazzle**
Shows what's playing on the VHS on the screen.

**captureDazzle <filename>**
Captures what's playing on the VHS to the supplied filename (.ts).

**convertDazzle <filename>**
Converts the filename.ts to dv and de-interlaces (I couldn't work out how to get VLC to capture de-interlaced - maybe the Ubuntu kernel missing a module)

**convertDazzleFolder**
Converts the a folder of ts files to dv

Enjoy,
