### Post by slickymaster on 2014-04-14
[Calibre]("http://calibre-ebook.com/") it's free and open source. It allows to manage e-book collections, as well as to create, edit and read e-books. It supports a variety of formats, including the common EPUB and Amazon Kindle formats, e-book syncing with a variety of e-book readers and conversion between e-book formats

There's also [Evince]("https://wiki.gnome.org/Apps/Evince"). Have you tried it?
