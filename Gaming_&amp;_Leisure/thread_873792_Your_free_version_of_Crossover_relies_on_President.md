[I]Hi,

It is with great pride (and not a little trepidation) that I announce
the launch of the CodeWeavers Great American Lame Duck Presidential
Challenge! The Challenge is CodeWeavers’ way of ensuring that the
nation’s business does not languish during the upcoming election season.
Oh, and also of ensuring that our press buzz doesn’t totally languish
while we develop CrossOver 8.0, and that we encourage a different set of
eyeballs to look at CodeWeavers :-).

The goal of our challenge is to present reasonable goals for President
Bush to achieve during his last six months in office. You can see them here:

[http://lameduck.codeweavers.com](http://lameduck.codeweavers.com)

If President Bush achieves any, or all, of these goals, we’ll respond by
giving away our software for free for 24 hours. Full copies. Fully
supported. Totally Free. It’s just that simple.

So, as I mentioned in a prior email, I'd like to ask your help in
promoting CodeWeavers. If you find this entertaining, or are willing
to help the US accomplish something (*anything*) useful in these dogs
days of summer, please visit this link and Digg us up:

[http://digg.com/2008_us_elections/The_CodeWeavers_Lame_Duck_Presidential_Challenge](http://digg.com/2008_us_elections/The_CodeWeavers_Lame_Duck_Presidential_Challenge)

Thank you, again, for your ongoing support of CodeWeavers!

Cheers,

Jeremy White
CEO
CodeWeavers
