### Post by ViperKnight on 2007-01-23
As said...you don't have to completely turn off Beryl.

What I do is:
1.Click the Beryl Diamond thingo
2.Click "Select Window Manager"
3.Click "Metacity (Gnome Desktop Manager)" -The screen will flicker a tad as the window managers change.
4.Play the Game
5.Do steps 1-3 again to activate Beryl again.

It's not too much of a fuss for me.
