From what I've heard, it doesn't work too well in Wine. But, it seems the dongle uses a hacked rt2570 driver:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=380638](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=380638)

Be prepared for a struggle... nothing I've found shows good support for any other modes other than kiosk.
