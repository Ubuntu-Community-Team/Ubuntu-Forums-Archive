### Post by jaikat on 2007-03-22
hello all

I've "had" the same problem as alot of people who enjoy 3d games on linux, and that would be when the game(i play enemy territory) crashes randomly with a "recieved signal 11, exiting) note in the terminal..
well after quite some googling and much desperation, i installed kubuntu dapper, having had ubuntu edgy, and installed my video drivers (nvidia GeForce 6200tc) through Envy.

After i got direct rendering on, i installed ET and tried my luck..
much to my surprise the problem was G.O.N.E...GONE

now i know there's nothing special with what i did, except for the following:

I had been using the video card on my intell motherboard when i had the signall 11 problem, so i thought i might be needing another card, so i went and bought the Nvidia 6200, but surprise surprise, the problem was still there..even on the Nvidia..

so i tried another installing from scratch, and installed kubuntu dapper and tried again and it worked, no more signal 11 crashes.

now i know some might think its a hardware problem, but according to my list of events, its not.

I think that the problem comes up when installing video drivers incorrectly, or maybe when changing the video drivers; i had used the ATI the drivers to which were installed alright but i can remember having problems during the install, so i installed and installed till it worked, maybe if i had used envy from the first time to get a clean install i wouldn't have been through all the hard time.
even when i installed the Nvidia, i installed the drivers for it without removing the drivers for the ATI, and so the problem kept on appearing..

now after the clean install of the system and the video drivers, it works without a problem..

i hope this helps..
