### Post by holastickboy on 2012-11-19
Hi all!

I am a regular here on the forums, and an avid Linux gamer.  I hold a lot of LAN parties for my friends and I, and always spent hours searching for games to LAN with my friends.  I always wanted particular details, such as if it had computer AI controlled bots, did it need a CD, how many players it supported, and did it have co-operative modes.

I have made a website, all hosted on a Ubuntu server, that fulfills this very target.  I have made [http://lanpartymania.com](http://lanpartymania.com) and am constantly adding games to the database, explaining detailed information on each game, as well as a high definition screenshot for each. 

 As a Linux gamer, I have also noted for each game if it runs on Linux natively, or if it runs via WINE.  I am currently adding multiple games each day, so the list is ever expanding.

If you enjoy LAN parties, or like to play games with others, hopefully this site might make your life a little easier.  

The site is here if you are interested.  If you have any comments or suggestions (particularly in regards to Linux), please let me know (I am on these forums all the time).

[http://lanpartymania.com](http://lanpartymania.com)
