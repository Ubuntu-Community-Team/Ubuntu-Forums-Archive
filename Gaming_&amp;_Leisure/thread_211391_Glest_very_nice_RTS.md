### Post by BuffaloX on 2006-07-08
> **vem0m said:**
> but then again u gotta think whats better gameplay or GFX? GFX are nothing without a game

A truly great game needs both and sound imo. :p 

The graphics dosn't make the game, but it does have A LOT of good elements besides the graphics and sound.
I like the tech tree, very solid and logical, and at the same time intuitive.
I like how resources work especially food. And I like the fact that it's actually playable.

I think the game has good substance, and good gameplay
but it would be nice with more developed AI, and of course multiplayer.

One detail that is very nice, is you can make more men, than your farms support. But if you dont add to your food source, your men will begin to die.
This is much smoother than any other rts I've played.

But the most striking thing is the graphics, which is better than any commercial RTS including the newest ones.:razz:
