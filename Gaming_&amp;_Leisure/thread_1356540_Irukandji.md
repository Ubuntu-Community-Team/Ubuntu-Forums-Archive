### Post by charliesgames on 2009-12-16
Hi all,

I'm Charlie, and i make games, mostly arcade style shooters. Anyway, i've had quite a few requests on my website for native Linux versions of my games, so i bought a new hard disk, installed Ubuntu and i've been busy porting my games to Linux over the last week or so, starting with my most recent game, Irukandji!

The game is a score attack shoot-em-up, where you get to blast lots of little sea monsters with a variety of different ships. It's simple, addictive fun! Take a look here:


[http://www.charliesgames.com/wordpress/?page_id=399](http://www.charliesgames.com/wordpress/?page_id=399)


Anyway, please give the game a go, and let me know what you think :) You can grab the demo version from here:
[http://www.charliesgames.com/demo/IrukandjiLinuxDemo.tar.gz](http://www.charliesgames.com/demo/IrukandjiLinuxDemo.tar.gz)



I'm also trying to work out what the minumum specs are for running the game, so far it seems to run on most Linux boxes using a fairly modern distro and hardware accellerated graphics.If you give the game a try, please let me know your specs :)
