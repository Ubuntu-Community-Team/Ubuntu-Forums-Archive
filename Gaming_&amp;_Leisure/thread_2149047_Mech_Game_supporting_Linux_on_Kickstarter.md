### Post by Vam on 2013-05-27
Hey Ubuntu Gaming Community,

I'm the Producer of the new [Heavy Gear Assault]("http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/stompybot/heavy-gear-assault-0") game in development. I've been working with the MechWarrior franchise for the past 15 years and I've never had a chance to support Linux with that franchise. However, we've been talking about Linux support for a long time for our next title. I wanted to let everyone here know that we are on [Kickstarter]("http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/stompybot/heavy-gear-assault-0") looking for support to bring a mech game to Linux. Its really not been done before with the exception of the Heavy Gear II port by Loki Games back in the 90s. The Heavy Gear franchise is a big supporter of Linux and its why we want to carry the torch onward and support Linux right out of the gate. We are developing with Unreal Engine 4 and while Epic Games has been supporting Mac OSX and other OpenGL platforms there hasn't been any support for Linux yet. Our team has been looking at the code and we've determined that we can support Linux and Ubuntu with a release. You'll have to have fairly new hardware to handle the graphics that latest OpenGL is capable of but we feel it will be totally worth it! We are also offering a dedicated server console for the community to enjoy.

Please come over and show your support on our [Kickstarter]("http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/stompybot/heavy-gear-assault-0") page and we will give you the best damn experience we can on Ubuntu. 

Thanks for listening and I hope I did not violate any rules here with this post. 

If you have any questions for us please leave them here or post them on our Kickstarter page and we will respond ASAP.

Cheers,
Vince McMullin
Producer of Heavy Gear Assault
