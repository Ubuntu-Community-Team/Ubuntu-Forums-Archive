Team Bahamut Game Update 

**X-Moto**

X-Moto is a 2D motocross platform game where the goal is to complete various levels as fast as possible by collecting all the strawberries.

[http://xmoto.sourceforge.net/](http://xmoto.sourceforge.net/)



**Biloba**

Biloba is a turn-based game for 2 to 4 players. Each player moves pawns on a hexagonal board and attempts to eliminate opponents' pawns. Biloba includes an AI opponent, and supports local or network play.

[http://perso.wanadoo.fr/biloba/](http://perso.wanadoo.fr/biloba/)



**X2: The Threat for Linux is now in Beta Testing**

Yesterday LinuxGamePublishing (LPG) commenced the beta test of X2: The Threat. To take part as tester you need to register at their beta testing site. With some luck you may be confirmed as tester, otherwise your can also access the beta by pre-ordering X2.

The story of X2: The Threat

Take the role of Julian Gardna, convicted of the theft of a transport ship, and explore the immersive universe of X2. You can choose the way by your own: Life of piracy and crime, beeing trader, mercenary, or become a combat commander of a whole fleet. It is your decision. But be aware of the remote areas of the galasy! Something is going on there.


Features

    * stunning graphics
    * open end gameplay
    * over 60 classes of ships
    * a huge carrier class ship over 2km long
    * complex yet intuitive interface allows complete control of your empire
