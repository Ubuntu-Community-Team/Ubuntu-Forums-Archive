### Post by Daveth on 2007-07-08
If your Dell is off the shelf you probably have a  Intel Graphics Media Accelerator 950. There is quite a bit written about them on the Forum. I have Steam running with Half Life2 on the older WINE version in the feisty repository and that runs just fine, so it may be a graphics card problem. Intel do a range of linux drivers for their GMAs, but worth reading around a bit before rushing off!
