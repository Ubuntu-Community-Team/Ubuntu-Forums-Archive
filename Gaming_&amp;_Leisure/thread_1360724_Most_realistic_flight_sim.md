### Post by Zularan on 2009-12-23
Akaik FSX will not work on linux. XPlane is one I've been using for a long time now and is on par with FSX (some argue better) in terms of realism.

It is more technical and a steeper learning curve but not only has a native Linux build, is available as an FAA approved trainer.  I've been a professional pilot for years and am a Linux convert, XPlane runs perfectly and is invaluable for instrument and procedure training.   If your customers are after a more gamey type of flightsim then FSX is the way to go.

The benefits of both have been argued to death on FS forums but it comes down to personal taste, these are the two main players as far as flight sims go but if OSX/Linux is a consideration, XPlane is the only option.

Hope this helps, forums worth checking out are [http://www.x-plane.org](http://www.x-plane.org), [http://www.flightsim.com](http://www.flightsim.com)
