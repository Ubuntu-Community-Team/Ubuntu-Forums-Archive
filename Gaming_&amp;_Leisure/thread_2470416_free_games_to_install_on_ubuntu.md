### Post by mastablasta on 2022-01-27
in the repositories:
Oolite
Flight of the Amazon queen (funny but very old adventure game)
Battle for Wesnoth


if you don't mind downloading .deb files:
Enemy territory or also another version ET Legacy which comes bundled with Omni-bot if you don't want to play against other players.
World of Padman
Smokin Guns
Cube
Cube 2
Xonotic
Red Eclipse
Red Eclipse 2
...

a bunch of interesting games can also be found on Itch.io such as for example: 
anger foot
blood cube
...

Then you have plenty on Steam. some of which you already found like TF2 and Dota2.

there are also: 
Open Transport Tycoon deluxe
Counter strike: Global offensive (though without premium there are even more cheaters, but you can always play with friends or against bots)
Oil rush strategy game just became free 
...

They would often offer games for free and often they work well via proton or have a linux version. sometimes they are quite good games. the ones i got at some point for free on steam:
Company of Heroes 2
Tiny Little Nightmares
A story about my Uncle
Call of Juarez Gunslinger
Bomber Crew
DiRT Rally
...

Finally there are steam games that on big sales (Summer/winter) go for really low money and are great or good games.

For example the base game Dishonored (no DLCs) costs 2 EUR when on Sale 
Payday is 1 EUR or thereabouts (again with no DLC)

Valve bundle will cost 10 EUR when on sale but has a bunch of games in it to keep you occupied if you haven't played them already. But you can also buy them separately for 1 or 2 EUR each during the sale. Like Left 4 dead 2 or the Half-life series. we did this and then installed mods like Synergy and Svencoop so we can play them together.

My point is, for a small amount of money, you can get some really good games. 

then of course there are the opensource engines for some older games. for these you need to have the game files. but the engines make it so that game works on modern PC and often nativ elinux. dhewm for Doom 3, open MW for Morrowind, Arx Libertatis for Arx Fatalis, Doomsday engine or zdoom for doom engine games, RTCWCoop for Return to Castle Wolfenstein (and coop mod at the same time) ...
