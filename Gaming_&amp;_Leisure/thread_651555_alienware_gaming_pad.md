### Post by ZOOstation on 2008-02-28
Well! I've solved my problem so I'm gonna put this out there for other people with similar problems:

I got an Alienware Dual Compatible Gaming Pad so I could use it with pSX on Ubuntu. I had previously been borrowing my brother's controller, which the emulator detected automatically. This one was not detected by the emulator, and I couldn't find any specific instructions.

But I went in Synaptic and downloaded the psemu-input-omnijoy and psemu-input-padjoy packages and now pSX recognizes my Alienware controller.
