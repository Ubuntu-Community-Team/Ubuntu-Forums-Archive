Here are some nice games on the skinny side of things. Most of these also work well on laptops. 

Tee Wars (like 2d quake)

SimCity  (Old school strat)

[http://www.getdeb.net/app.php?name=Micropolis](http://www.getdeb.net/app.php?name=Micropolis)

Gridwars2  (who needs an xbox?)
[http://www.getdeb.net/app.php?name=GridWars+2](http://www.getdeb.net/app.php?name=GridWars+2)

Typhoon 2001  (Tempest 2000)
[http://typhoon.kuto.de/index.html](http://typhoon.kuto.de/index.html)

Maelstrom ( Nice Asteroids like game)

Mania Drive (Wacky 3d driving)
[http://maniadrive.raydium.org/](http://maniadrive.raydium.org/)
[http://www.getdeb.net/search.php?search_distro_id=5&keywords=mania](http://www.getdeb.net/search.php?search_distro_id=5&keywords=mania)
