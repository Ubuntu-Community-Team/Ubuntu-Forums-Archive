### Post by hikaricore on 2009-01-28
Sdlmame is mame: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MAME](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MAME)
Sdlmess is mess: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MESS](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MESS)

Personally I would suggest setting up individual console emulatiors to use through a frontend gui.
Support for the various console is MESS is lacking and nearly all other emulators have far surpassed it.

_My suggestions (just as examples):_
Mednafen - NES/GameBoy
ZSNES - SNES
GENS - Genesis
Mupen64Plus - N64
epsxe - Playstation
pcsx2 - PS2
