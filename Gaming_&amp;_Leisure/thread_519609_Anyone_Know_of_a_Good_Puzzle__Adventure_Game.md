### Post by Chaotic Thought on 2007-08-21
Lots of games you can play with scummvm. There's also DOSbox which runs my favorite adventure games because I grew up on them--Sierra adventures. If you can get ahold of old games like King's Quest or Space Quest.  Also, there's a fan remake of King's Quest II that is very fun. Search for "AGD interactive" to find it. Unfortunately, their interpreter is Windows only the last time I checked.

Also for a pure Linux option try the Ur-Quan Masters (Star Control 2). It might not be exactly adventure, but it certainly combines adventure and puzzle elements. It's not just an arcade game.
