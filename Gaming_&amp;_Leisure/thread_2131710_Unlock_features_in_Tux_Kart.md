### Post by LillyDragon on 2013-04-03
You need to play the Adventure/Story mode to unlock more tracks, (You don't have to go for the Gold rank with each mission, but it does help you progress to the next one, and unlocks new content. Lesser ranks don't unlock anything as far as I know.) and there are at least two karts that can be unlocked too, I do believe. I've already played through the story and unlocked pretty much everything that way by going for the Gold rank with most missions.

### Post by Arthur_D on 2013-04-04
Open ~/.config/supertuxkart/challenges.xml in a text editor and use Replace All to replace all instances of "false" with "true". Or download some add-on tracks. Have fun. :)
