### Post by calgarystevens on 2007-05-22
Right click your 'Applications' button (top left) and go to 'edit menus' and look for them in there. Sometimes they make a menu item but do not enable them so you can see them. (The boxes have to be checked to see them) A warning with BF2, punkbuster does not play nice with the battlefield series, you might have to play on non-pb servers. If you know that already ignore me ;) BTW, if they are not on the menu, you can make your own shortcut... check here for a start: [http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iAppId=2424](http://appdb.winehq.org/appview.php?iAppId=2424)
