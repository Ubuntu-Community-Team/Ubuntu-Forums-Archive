### Post by mivo on 2008-02-18
If a commercial game is an option for you, I think [Enemy Territory: Quake Wars]("http://enemyterritory.com/") is a good choice. I've put in about 300 hours, and I always found full servers with human players, no matter what time I wanted to play at (even at 5am on a work day when my schedule allowed for it). There are some good US servers, too. Quake Wars is a team-based game, though, and it even has depth (for a shooter). There's a native Linux client available, and thread-rendering works  on Linux. It runs wonderfully in Ubuntu, both 32- and 64-bit (the latter requires the installation of the 32.bit libs which is a matter of three minutes).
