### Post by FG123 on 2007-11-13
> **primski said:**
> Nexuiz uses quake or ut engine? or smth else?
Nexuiz uses the DarkPlaces engine, which is a heavily modified advanced version of the original Quake engine.

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DarkPlaces](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DarkPlaces)

You can even take the DP binaries themselves, stick the PAK files from a Quake CD and you'll be able to play Quake in all the new technology glory, more or less. You can't get any bump mapping cos the original Quake didn't have the maps needed for it, but real-time shadows and HDR and all that good stuff, sure.
