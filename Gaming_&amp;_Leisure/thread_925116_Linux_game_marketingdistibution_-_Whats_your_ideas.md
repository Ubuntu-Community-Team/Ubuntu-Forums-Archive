### Post by Vadi on 2008-09-21
It was [Loki Games]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Loki_Games"). Imho they were way too early... (read, there was no ubuntu back then!). I wish a company like them existed now.

Marketing is the same way as other games - advertise, promote, let people know about the game... distribution imho shouldn't be a hand-made installer (mojosetup and like), but integrated into the package management system (like adobe air. You install an adobe air app, it automatically makes a package - so you can uninstall it with synaptic after just fine).

Oh, and an important factor - the game needs to be something that many would find interesting... even if the graphics aren't top-notch, at least the gameplay should be captivating.
