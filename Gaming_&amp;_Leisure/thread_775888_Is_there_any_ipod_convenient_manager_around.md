### Post by kostkon on 2008-04-30
More or less, all the "big" media players (like *Rhythmbox*, *Amarok*, *Banshee*, etc) can transfer files to/from an *iPod*.

If you want more of a management application and less of a media player you can try [*gtkpod*]("http://www.gtkpod.org/") or [*Floola*]("http://www.floola.com/"). You can find the first in the repos and the latter in [*getdeb.net*]("http://getdeb.net/") or its home page.
