### Post by davidork on 2007-06-16
I Recently purchased a logitech precision gamepad for about 10$. came home and tried to use it with zsnes... no luck. after some digging at sourceforge.net i found a lovely program called rejoystick 
that can be used to remap the keys on the joystick to keystrokes.   

you can download it at [http://superb-east.dl.sourceforge.net/sourceforge/rejoystick/rejoystick_0.3.3-1_i386.deb](http://superb-east.dl.sourceforge.net/sourceforge/rejoystick/rejoystick_0.3.3-1_i386.deb)

then open a terminal and type rejoystick and follow the instructions.  (i just remapped the d-pad because the rest of the buttons worked fine)


I hope this helps!
