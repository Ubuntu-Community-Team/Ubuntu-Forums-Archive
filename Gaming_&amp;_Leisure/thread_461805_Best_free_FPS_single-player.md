### Post by B0rsuk on 2007-06-02
**Penumbra: Overture** is very nice an innovative FPP game. It's not a shooter, but it has first person view and realtime action. Very interesting adventure/horror/puzzle game. Inspired by work of H.P. Lovecraft.
Demo is free, full version $20 . The game is quite short but it's meant as part 1 of 3. It was originally meant as tech demo of new engine, but it was so successful that developers decided to give it a try and make a full game.
Full version can be downloaded, too !
