### Post by Impavidus on 2018-02-10
User data like saved games should be saved in the user's home directory. Uninstalling a program won't delete it. Search in ~/.name_of_the_game or in ~/.config/name_of_the_game or something like that. ~ is your home directory, directories starting with . are hidden.
