### Post by sffvba[e0rt on 2011-11-25
So I decided to change my gaming rig to a dual boot setup so I could try out Desure and also Oil Rush (and by getting it on Linux support gaming on Linux too).

I am impressed.  After finally getting the right nVidia drivers to be used it was very smooth sailing.  I was initially disappointed when Desura was a tar ball and not a deb (or even available in a PPA).

But the install application that I got was straight forward enough (but still, in the spirit of making things easier I hope it gets added to the software center ASAP).

Now as far as Oil Rush goes it looks amazing.  Now on my system I can't play any of the newest games on the highest details so I can't compare directly but it does look very good.  As for the game thus far my biggest gripe is I am spending so much time looking at the mini-map and I miss all of the awesome looking battles... and when I stop to look at the prettiness I loose (quicker, as I am still struggling with the tactics in this game)...

All and all I am glad I could help the cause and hope one day that we can all game no matter our preferred underlying OS. (or platform for that matter)
