### Post by Naegling23 on 2007-12-19
what about diablo II?  One of you could host a game permanently and have a password...I dont think youll fit 32 people in there though, cant remember the limit, it might be only 4.

Guild wars is an mmo without a montly fee.

At first I wasnt a big fan of NWN, but after giving it a second chance, Ive really gotten to like the game.  The issue with it is that there is no hand holding at all.  From character creation, leveling, and questing, you are left completely on your own.  The game seems pretty steep when you first start, but once you get the hang of it you realize its depth.  And from what you stated, a non montly fee, private persistant world...nwn fits that bill.

The only downside that I have to nwn is that its a pretty old game at this point, and its pretty dated graphic wise, although, isometric games hold up better than other styles.  Of course, the bonus is that its based on D and D, so anyone who plays the game will feel right at home with the story/characters/gameplay.
