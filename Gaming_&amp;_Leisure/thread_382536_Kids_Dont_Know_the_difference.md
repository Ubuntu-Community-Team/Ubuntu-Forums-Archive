### Post by Freddy_C on 2007-03-12
After being forced to troubleshoot, disinfect, reinstall XP several times on my 3 Childrens different machines I have made a deal with them.

They should begin searching groups, online support forums, and chat communities to help them repair their own machines or i would be forced to switch them to Linux.

Well friends,  that day has come and the first machine is running 6.6 dapper splendidly.  This was not an easy choice because my children are gamers , as well am I. 

Cedega has their favorite steam games running full throttle.  And they may interact in the way that they execute their games a little differently but so far playback is a joy.

I spent more time on cofiguring his system than i did my own dapper by finding the correct codecs , flash, java, css, mail,  games, proper nvidia drivers.  it has inspired me to step up my own linux from a second system to my main entertainment os. my only hurdle now is myth tv.

i can no longer say that i cant use linux because i cant play games. it is so insulting to run vista that i am joyed to finaly get my frag on in linux.

big thanks to all involved in this project.  is it really the last stand?
