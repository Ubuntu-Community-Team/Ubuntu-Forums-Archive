### Post by Giraffemonster on 2011-04-26
[http://members.quicknet.nl/blankendaalr/dbgl/](http://members.quicknet.nl/blankendaalr/dbgl/)

This is a DOSbox frontend. I used it to play TESII: Daggerfall for a while. What I did, was I downloaded DBGL, then downloaded the Daggerfall, "Game Pack" that comes with it. That game pack had all of the info tags and file/directory destinations in it. Made the setup 10x easier for me.
