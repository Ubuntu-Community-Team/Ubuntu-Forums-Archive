### Post by rCXer on 2009-08-24
This is a bug in dosbox 0.72, the one you probably have.  It is fixed in dosbox 0.73.  You can get the newer version from [GetDeb](http://www.getdeb.net/app/Dosbox) or the [karmic repositories](http://packages.ubuntu.com/karmic/dosbox).
