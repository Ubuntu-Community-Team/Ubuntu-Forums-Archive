### Post by mario_ramalho on 2009-10-02
Auteria has come a long way since these previous posts. There have been many changes from what you guys talked about, i.e. PvP areas, which are set as arenas where you enter voluntarily, and in those arenas you can play a "capture the flag" kind of game with others. While these are PvP areas, one does not lose experience points for dieing, nor is the character's death permanent. Also, There have been more maps added, and there's another one in the making, soon to be added, along with more quests. 
Also, from time to time, there are events, set up by  either moderators or the developers themselves, in special event maps that one can access from a very early level. This is mainly a quest-based MMORPG, and i've been playing it for a few months, and it is one of the best MMO's i've played, and what makes it even better is that the present community of players are very polite and helpful. I recommend you guys have another look. Also, the installation is quite simple, even for a Ubuntu newbie like myself.

[http://www.auteria.com/?c=download](http://www.auteria.com/?c=download)
