### Post by CLCressman on 2010-01-11
System/Administration/Synaptic Package Manager

Type *gcompris* in the search box. In the list below you should see *gcompris-sound-nl.* If that looks like what you want, click the check box. It will bring up a menu, select *Mark for Installation*. Now the big green check mark at the top that says *Apply* is available click it. If you are asked for your password and permission to continue, supply it and tell it to continue until it is installed, and you should be good to go.
