### Post by LwIh%*7 on 2018-11-05
Just out of curiosity have you not considered using Steam Play? If its not yet enabled for the required game go to steam in the menu then settings, under Steam Play go to and click yes for "Enable Steam Play for All titles" and if you want click and activate the following option "Use this tool instead of game-specific selections from steam" this should after steam being logged out and logged back aka restarted the game should be enabled then for Ubuntu. Note that with those options being enabled you'll have games that aren't properly tested yet for Steam Play but its worth a try rather than loading it through a virtualbox which I know isn't great for gaming since it doesn't properly use the graphics card that well.
