### Post by Grant A. on 2008-12-06
Well, you need to have a 32-bit architecture for it, so depending on your processor, you can usually figure this out. All you need for runescape is Java. If you are on ubuntu simply go to runescape.com, click the play now button, and when the pop-up comes from your web browser to install an add-on, click it and it should simply install to your system like that. Good luck and have fun! :popcorn:
