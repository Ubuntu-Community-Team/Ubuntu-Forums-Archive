### Post by Naegling23 on 2008-10-01
emulators are your best bet.  There are a few websites devoted to updating the rosters for the greatest football game ever made, tecmo super bowl for the nes.  You'll have to google for it, but I'm sure you can find it.  You may even be able to find roster updates for some of the madden games (not sure...I never checked, but it seems like something you could find).  Im also a big fan of mutant league football for the sega genesis. 

As for commercial....EA sports games do not work in wine, so your out of luck.  This is frustrating since apple made a huge deal that they would work in macs about 2 years ago....they dont....but a mac version would likely mean stripping out the launcher that causes those games to choke under wine.  

I'll say this in all honesty though.  I have been a loyal pc gamer since the mid 90's.  Sports games on pc's (that includes windows) are second rate.  If you really want to play sports games, consoles get better versions than windows (and more options), and linux gets nothing.  

If your looking for a quick fix, I would recommend finding an updated or hacked rom for the madden games, or tecmo super bowl.  If you seriously want to play weeks worth of madden, then suck it up and get a console - an older psp is another option, and that can be found for pretty cheap.
