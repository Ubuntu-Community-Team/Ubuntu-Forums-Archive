### Post by curvedinfinity on 2008-09-04
This is actually really easy to do. Open a file browser, and go to your home directory. Press CTRL-H, and then scroll and find ".spring"

In there, make a "mods" and "maps" directory. In the future, when you download a mod or map, just put them in the appropriate spot and refresh springlobby. :)
