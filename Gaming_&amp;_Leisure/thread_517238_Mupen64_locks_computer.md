### Post by DoktorSeven on 2007-08-04
If you're playing fullscreen, does the whole computer really lock up or are you able to switch to a console with CTRL+ALT+F2?  If you can, it's likely Mupen64 crashed and you can't get back to the desktop (odd thing about crashing games in fullscreen, that).  Log into the console if you're able and type **killall -9 mupen64**, then go back to the desktop (CTRL+ALT+F7).  (You may have the screen in a lower resolution, CTRL+ALT+keypad + or -, or typing **xrandr -s 0** at a terminal inside X may help.  Worse thing is you might have to restart X with CTRL+ALT+backspace).

If you can't get to a console and it's really locked up, you might have some difficulties with the ATi drivers, overheating, or any number of odd problems.
