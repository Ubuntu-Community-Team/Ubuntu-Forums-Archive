### Post by qamelian on 2007-07-18
> **FleetAdmiral74 said:**
> Well, are they basically the same game? Is one more open, just plain harder, takes more strategy, takes longer, more actively developed, more open source/free...etc, just contribute what you know about each game, thanks!

No, they are opensource clones of two different Sid Meiers games originally for DOS/Windows. FreeCiv is essentially a clone of Civilization/CIv2. FreeCol is a clone of Colonization. Civ is about developing technology/civilizations. FreeCol is more about the resource management of colonies. Think of it as SimCity -four or five hundred years. Both are about the same depth strategy-wise, but focus on different types of strategy.
