### Post by DoktorSeven on 2007-05-13
No, but there's a .deb package on [gxmame's sourceforge site](http://sourceforge.net/projects/gxmame) you can install:

**sudo dpkg -i gxmame_0.35beta2-1_i386.deb** (or open with gdebi)
