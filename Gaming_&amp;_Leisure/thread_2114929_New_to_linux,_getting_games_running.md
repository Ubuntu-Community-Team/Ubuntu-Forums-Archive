### Post by rudeboyskunk on 2013-02-11
1) Upgrade to Ubuntu 12.04 LTS.  As a previous poster said, 11.10 reaches end-of-life in April 2013, but 12.04 is supported until 2017.

2) Get a Desura account and a Steam account, then install Desura and Steam.  There are plenty of free-to-play games on there, and you can find super-cheap games that you and your sibling might enjoy more.
