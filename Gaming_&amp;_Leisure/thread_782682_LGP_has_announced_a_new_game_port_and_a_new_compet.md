Good news everybody:

LGP is going to port another game. However, they haven't told the public yet which game it is! There is a picture on [http://competition.linuxgamepublishing.com/]("http://competition.linuxgamepublishing.com/"). It shows the game, but it is revealed at a rate of one pixel per second. The first person to guess the name of the game to be parted wins the first copy of it that will be made :lolflag:

When you think you know the name of the game send an email to [email]competition@linuxgamepublishing.com[/email] with the name of the game, and your reason to think it is the game you say it is. You may only guess once a day, otherwise you will be disqualified.

Good luck!
