### Post by thx_1138 on 2006-12-02
Finally got some time to play this the last few days, had to download the newest beta release (RC2.1). Well I found...
-Objects in levels flicker a bit (seemed to get better once I tweaked the settings down a bit)
-The water reflection do look a bit off (blurry and dull), and when submerged under the water the top of the event horizon (surface....) seems quite dark and dreary (It looked like a bug to me........)
-Most of the movies I saw played fine but I still heard slight stuttering on a few of them (Only playing through first section of game for 1st piece of stone)
-Once I shut the game down my mouse cursor in gnome was stuck as an "X", I simply restarted the gui(crtl-alt-backspace) and logged in again to solve issue
-Frame rate seemed solid for the most part on my machine except when the enemies onscreen grew quite numerous (my machines no dual core but does the job quite nice still)

I ran the latest beta on these specs

2.4 Ghz Celeron
1.5 Gb Ram (pc3200)
Geforce 6200 (128MB) with Nvidia driver 1.0-8776
Sound Blaster Live 24Bit 
300Gb SATA Seagate Barricuda 
Ubuntu 6.06.1 (fully updated as of post)
(intel 686 Kernel)
