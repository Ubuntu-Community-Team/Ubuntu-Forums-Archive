Hello Everyone! Ash Monif here! My long-time friend and partner Randis Albion came together to form a new indie game studio called Grimm Bros.  We&#8217;ve been hard at work for over a year now and are proud to share with you our debut title - **[Dragon Fin Soup!]("http://dragonfinsoup.com/") **As well as **[news about our Kickstarter!]("https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/grimmbros/dragon-fin-soup")**



**Dragon Fin Soup is a classic console RPG which blends together Tactical Action & Roguelike elements set in a charming, twisted, dark, fairy tale inspired world.**
 
Key Features:
- SNES style 2D Fairy Tale World in Full HD
- Procedurally Generated Dungeons
- Tactical Decision Making
- Form parties with NPCs
- Deep RPG Combat Systems
- Extensive Crafting & Armory
- Mini-Games such as Fishing & Rune Tracking
- Day & Night cycles with random weather
- And much much more!

Dragon Fin Soup is a hybrid experience that combines both turn-based dungeon crawl exploration and persistent story progression. Dragon Fin Soup comes with 2 distinct game modes. You can choose to unravel the tale of your chosen character in Story Mode. Survival Mode plays a classic roguelike with permanent death. The 1st tale begins with Red Robin, a cheerful yet raging alcoholic whose dark bloody past has come back to haunt her with a vengeance...

Morgiana will be a dual wielding Assassin class that will come a range of new weapons featuring Katana, Dao, &#332;dachi, Katar, Sai, Kunai and other cool weapons.


Enslaved and trained as an Assassin and dancer Morgiana was used as a deadly weapon for as long as she remembers, until one day when she decided to turn her sharp blades against her oppressors. Her signature moves are short-range shadow teleportation and backstabbing. As a playable character Morgiana will have unique abilities and distinct play style.
