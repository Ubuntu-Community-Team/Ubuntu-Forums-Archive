### Post by CharmyBee on 2010-01-03
- People took it too seriously
- It went out of sync too easily
- States over the internet are tedious to deal with
- Difficult to maintain. Unlike MAME, ZSNES is a 'fast optimal shortcut' kind of emulator that isn't accuracy focused on emulating the system internals. Game specific hacks are everywhere in the code and those, along with the netcode (which was experimental since day 1 of its introduction) is a headache making obstacle in development.
