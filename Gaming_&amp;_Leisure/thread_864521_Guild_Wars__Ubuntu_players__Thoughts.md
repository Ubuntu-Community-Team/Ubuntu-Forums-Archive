### Post by dorath on 2008-07-21
It has been a while since I've done anything productive, but I still log in from time to time.  It's really nice not paying a monthly fee, because I don't feel compelled to play.

First, I'll recommend *not* starting with Factions.  Not that it's bad, but Prophecies and Nightfall are just better.

Prophecies, the first campain, is the largest.  More areas, more missions.  Prophecies also has many quests that reward you with skills, which is nice because you wont have to spend your hard earned coin on them.

Nightfall, the third campain, has Heroes.  While Nightfall is shorter than Prophecies, Heroes are a very nice addition if you intend to play by yourself often.  All of the campains offer henchmen, but they have a fixed skillset.  You can customize the skills and armor of your Heroes which allows you to make them quite a bit more powerful than henchmen.

Eye of the North is a great expansion, I enjoyed it a great deal.  EotN also allows you to pick up some Heroes if you started out in Prophecies or Factions.  EotN is for characters who have hit the level cap of 20, but it is not necessary to beat any of the campains to play.

Personally I would recommend Nightfall over Prophecies.  I started out with Prophecies, got maybe halfway through, and then picked up Nightfall.  Beat Nightfall and then went back and finished Prophecies with my newfound Hero buddies.  Picked up and finished Eye of the North, and got a copy of Factions.

As for replay value, once you beat a campain you'll unlock Hard Mode.  In HM the creatures are higher level and a tougher AI.
