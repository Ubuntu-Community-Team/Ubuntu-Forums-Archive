### Post by jualin on 2008-06-23
Virtual Machines are not made for gaming since the video cards drivers are emulated (usually 8mb). Therefore don't try running a game under a Virtual Machine. What you can try doing is running it through Wine. Check out [http://winehq.com]("http://winehq.com") and check the AppDB. I have run several games this way. Counter Strike, Unreal Tournament ... Hope this helps!
