### Post by stoffe on 2006-01-02
Well, you could simply do:
```
sudo apt-get install kubuntu-desktop
```
and then you'll have the option of logging into Kubuntu from your login screen.

I haven't actually done that myself, but I have done this for Xubuntu, and it worked just fine. The really nice part is that you get to keep your old system too, no matter which you choose as default. :)
