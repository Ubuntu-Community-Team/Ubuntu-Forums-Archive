### Post by blueshead on 2012-08-11
Download and unzip it. Move it to your Home directory.

To start the game.. Select xonotic-linux32-glx or xonotic-linux32-sdl or xonotic-linux64-glx or xonotic-linux64-sdl.

There seem to be 2 starters for 32 bit or 64.  glx or sdl.

In my case xonotic-linux64-sdl worked for me as I got no sound from xonotic-linux64-glx.
