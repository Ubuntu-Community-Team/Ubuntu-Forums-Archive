### Post by hikaricore on 2007-10-25
The ability to record games (especially games run via WINE) depends on a lot of factors.

* Your video hardware.
* Processor speed and power.
* Gaming/Desktop resolution and colour depth.

Assuming you're running at 1600x1200 (which is what I run at) you'd need atleast a dual core 2ghz processor and a high end video card (8600gt PCIE personally).

With this setup I can squeeze about 25-30fps while recording WoW at this resolution.

At lower resolutions and colour depth, you'll have better results.

Hopefully this makes some sense to you friend.  ^_^
