### Post by AM Ramakrishnan on 2013-01-30
There's 0 a.d, which is a great RTS. It is similar to the total war games except that instead of recruiting units before the battle you create your own civilization to fight others in real time. In comparison to total war it is kind of similar to Rome Total War. however, it requires a more powerful system than Rome, so depending on what computer you use it could or could not work well.
