### Post by oldrocker99 on 2009-11-28
There's Warzone 2100:biggrin:, a very good RTS originally published in 1999 and since made open source, Glest (mechanics vs magic and a real](*,) for me (so far)), Globulation 2 (a very original concept8) and well worth checking out), Freeciv, the open-source version of Sid Meier's classic, and don't forget Spring. All these are in Synaptic, and I used the Quick Search tool and typed in "strategy" to locate these quickly.

There also seem to be about a dozen FPS available for Linux, of course. And heed the advice above and get Wesnoth, as good a turn-based game as you can find.
