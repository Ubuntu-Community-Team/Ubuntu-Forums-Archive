### Post by donkyhotay on 2009-05-02
As always you get what you pay for. Personally my favorite free MMORPG is puzzle pirates (on the doubloon oceans for full content) but unless you like puzzle games it's probably not for you. If you're looking for something more WoW-ish you might try regnum online. It's been awhile since I've played it (so it might have changed) but it's free to play with premium content but the premium content is mostly stuff to help you level up faster to avoid grinding so if you don't mind grinding a bit between missions you play for free. As mentioned there is savage & savage2 which I don't really consider to be MMORPG's as they are more of a FPS (especially savage1). If you're okay using wine (rather then being solely linux native) then guildwars runs flawlessly for me (so long as you don't ever minimize) and doesn't have a subscription though you have to pay for the game itself up front.
