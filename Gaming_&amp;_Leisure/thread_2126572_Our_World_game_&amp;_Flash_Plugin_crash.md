### Post by tancrackers on 2013-03-21
Try using [Google Chrome]("https://www.google.com/intl/en/chrome/browser/") and take advantage of its built-in Flash. After installing Chrome, type "about:plugins" in the omnibox, click Details, and you will see two plugins for flash. Disable the one for Firefox. Keep the one "/opt/google/chrome/PepperFlash/libpepflashplayer.so" enabled. It'll say PPAPI (out of process).
