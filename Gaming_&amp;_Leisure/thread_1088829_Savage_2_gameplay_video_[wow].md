*is it friendly to newbies?*

Yes, if you do the tutorial first, and stick to the newbie servers while you can.

*Do I have to devote a lot of my time to the game, or can it be played casually?*

It is *very* casual-friendly. Matches last between 20 - 50 mins (sometimes shorter, sometimes longer, but that's the average range), and you can hop on / leave a match at any time. I usually play a match a day myself.

*Is this a one-off or a monthly thing?*

One-off.

*Is there a single player mode to keep my sucky skills off the net?*

Yeah, there is a 'practice' mode where you can choose any unit you'd like to be and choose npc's you'd like to fight against.

Hope that helped, the game is worth trying out.
