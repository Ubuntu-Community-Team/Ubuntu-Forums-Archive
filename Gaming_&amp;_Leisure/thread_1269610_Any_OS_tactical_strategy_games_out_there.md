### Post by Crunchy the Headcrab on 2009-09-20
If you haven't tried The Battle for Wesnoth, definitely give it a try.  It is both very simple to learn and difficult to master.  There are a ton of different unit types that level up into other unit types.  There are bonuses and handicaps based on time of day, terrain type, weapon type, etc. for each unit.  It's like mix between a strategy game and an rpg.  You buy your new or returning units at the start of each battle and then if they survive you can recall them for future battles.
