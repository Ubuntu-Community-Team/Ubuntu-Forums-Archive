### Post by QIII on 2016-08-17
With 16.04 and an R9 290, you will need to stay with the default Radeon driver that is installed with Ubuntu.

AMD is going through a massive transition in the scope of its drivers.  Soon, it is to be hoped, the new AMDGPU driver will be available for GCN 1.1 GPUs like yours.
