### Post by myromance123 on 2012-10-28
If you are using Ubuntu, then you have the Ubuntu Software Center to download free apps or even buy apps that are purchasable.

If you do not see the orange bag icon on the Unity bar to your left, then you can open the Dash to search for the Software Center.

Once in the Ubuntu Software Center, to view games or applications that can be bought you will need to click the down pointing arrow next to the big Icon with the  text "All Software". Then click "For Purchase". I've attached a screenshot.

This will show you all purchasable applications, if anything piques your interest.

I personally recommend the games Amnesia, OilRush, Rochard and Family Farm. :)
Good mini games would be World Of Goo, Braid, and 7 Wonders: Magical Mystery Tour.

Other places you can buy Linux games? Here's a short list:
[http://www.desura.com/]("http://www.desura.com/")
[http://www.gameolith.com/]("http://www.gameolith.com/")
