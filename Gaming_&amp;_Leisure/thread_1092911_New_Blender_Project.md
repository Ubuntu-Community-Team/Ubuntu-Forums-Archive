### Post by meborc on 2009-03-11
i suggest you take a look at [Yo Frankie]("http://www.yofrankie.org/") project... there is free content there... you can get a good picture of how games are made using blender...

start by making new levels and characters to yo frankie... if you find yourself being good at it, you can try tackle the game engine :)

don't start from zero... you will never get further than the idea itself... you need to learn from other open source projects and then move to your own

just my thoughts ;)
