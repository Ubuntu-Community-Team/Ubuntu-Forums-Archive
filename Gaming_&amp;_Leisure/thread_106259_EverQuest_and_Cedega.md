### Post by varunus on 2005-12-20
Wine and Cedega can be on the same system (they run independently of each other) but you'll have much better luck with games in Cedega.  Wine runs some games quite well, but I know on Guild Wars at least it just fails to load.

There should be options in Cedega's control panel/configuration file to enable pixel/vertex shaders, I think.  However, you may not have access to the control panel until you register...you should check it out.
