### Post by Cup of Squirrels on 2008-06-17
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=304528](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=304528)

This thread does a pretty good job of performing a basic install of wine and steam. Just follow the step by step instructions and you should be fine.

A couple of notes from my personal experience:

-The steam installer is no longer a .exe file - it is a .msi file. Instead of installing it with "wine SteamInstaller.exe", do "msiexec /i SteamInstaller.msi" or whatever the file name/location is

-The friends system is very broken and bugged with wine. You will find it very difficult to communicate as you will not always see your or your friends messages immediately (GUI does not refresh). As far as I know this cannot be fixed, but it can be bypassed by either
   -Right clicking the "top bar" of the chat windows (where your friends display picture is)
   -Clicking the "dont give out your personal info" safety link
   -Opening your friends list and resizing the list. This should force your chat windows to "refresh" and display missed messages


Good luck!
