### Post by rbaleksandar on 2009-04-19
Well, I played them both. And I honestly can say that HW (aka Hedgewars) is way too better than WX(aka Wormux). Here´s a small list of comparing these two Worms-clones:

1.Graphics - in HW are flawless. When you look at the screen you can´t see something that looks it doesn´t belong there. The hedgehogs are very cute with great animation, funny voices and ability to modify each and every one of them with a hat (something like a mask that the small guy wears during the game). Backgrounds are also unique. They are not Worms-style but non the less feel great. About the animation in action scenes - awesome. Hedgehogs fly and roll in pain all over tha map. All the weapons look cool too. In WX - nothing like that...The guys you play with look dull, kind of stiff. And they definitely don´t fit with the background. Weapons - some are nice, but they also stay unnatural in the hands of the little cows, chickens and whatever farm animals you have in your team.

2.Interface - in HW menus are not such an eye-candy, but that's no big deal since you spend there 1/1000000 of your time. :) About the in-game interface - just like Worms that is - great, fully functional and doesn't take half of your screen. In WX it's the opposite. Main menu looks great really, but as I said - you spend 1/100000 of your time there. In-game interface - sad story. Ok, I understand they wanted to make it different from Worms but i)when something is 100% cool, functional etc. and you're making a clone of that game, you are allowed to take it and ii)there are so many things that you don't need. Take the map for example. What do you need that for? Answer: you don't need it at all. After all it's not that your screen is 9" and the resolution of the game is 5000x4000 or something and doesn't fit in the screen so you have to scroll all over the place.

3.Weapons - both clones have a "+" here. HW has less weapons but some of them unique. WX has so many weapons like in Worms. That's also cool.

4.Single player - HW beats WX with 100:1 here.
5.Multiplayer - both have same cool multi.

Well that's of course only my humble opinion. Try them both and then judge for yourselves.
