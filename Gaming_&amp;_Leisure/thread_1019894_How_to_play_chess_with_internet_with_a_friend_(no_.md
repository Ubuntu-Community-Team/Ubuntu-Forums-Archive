### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-12-23
[http://www.freechess.org/](http://www.freechess.org/) is no-strings-attached free, browser-based and should work well under any operating system (I think they have an offline client that you can use if you prefer).  You need to create accounts, but it's very quick and simple--just enter a username and password and you can start playing.
