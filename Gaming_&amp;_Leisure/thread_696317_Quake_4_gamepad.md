### Post by Frem on 2008-02-14
The PC version does not allow joypad input, even though there are ports of the game avalible for consoles.

This is partially because moving and aiming is much more accurate with a keyboard and mouse. The versions for consoles are probably tweaked somewhat to compensate for the loss of agility and accuracy that comes with controllers.

You might be able to use something called "Joy 2 Key." It'll map keys to buttons on the gamepad. I've never used it myself, and I've heard mixed reports of sucess with FPS games, but still. It might work for you.
