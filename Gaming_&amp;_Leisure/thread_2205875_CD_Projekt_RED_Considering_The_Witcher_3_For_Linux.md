### Post by mips on 2014-02-17
Let me play devils advocate here. We all know ubuntu is going down the MIR path (not the community spins) so you will only be using the opensource drivers and not the proprietary drivers, these are not good enough for gaming period. Everybody else is adopting Wayland and if nvidia & amd are gonna possibly support any of these platforms in the future they are gonna go where the majority goes which is Wayland. So if gaming is your goal you would have to use something other than Ubuntu. I suspect this is also one of the reasons besides legal issues, & stability as to why valve chose debian after making ubuntu their official support platform initially.
