### Post by angryfirelord on 2006-12-15
I have an nVidia GeForce 6200 AGP and that works perfectly fine (on edgy too). Just do a apt-get install nvidia-glx after you've updated it.

When buying any video card for linux, nvidia has the best support. However, plinking down $90 for a PCI based video card is a bit much. If your 3D needs aren't too extreme, an FX 5200 would have a better price/performance ratio. That should easily run most of the free Linux games out there.

VGA only($55): [http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.asp?Item=N82E16814150040]("http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.asp?Item=N82E16814150040")

DVI only ($44): [http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.asp?Item=N82E16814130188]("http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.asp?Item=N82E16814130188")
