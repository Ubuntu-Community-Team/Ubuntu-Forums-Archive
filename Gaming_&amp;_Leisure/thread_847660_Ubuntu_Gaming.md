### Post by Irritant on 2008-07-03
> **Melcar said:**
> Warsow and Urban Terror are favorites.  Alien Arena is cool too, but to bad it runs like crap on my ATI (runs slower than Nexuiz on Ultra :confused:).

Are you running version 7.10 of Alien Arena?  Try disabling dynamic lighting.  There are sometimes issues with certain ATI drivers and gl_texsubimage commands that AA's dynamic lighting uses(it literally cripples framerates).  When dl's are disabled, you'll still see players and objects light up from dynamic lights, but not bsp surfaces.
