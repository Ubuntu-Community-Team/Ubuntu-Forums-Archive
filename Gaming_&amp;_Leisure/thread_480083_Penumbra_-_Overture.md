### Post by Dodger73 on 2007-06-21
This has probably been posted before, but Penumbra is an episodic 1st person action adventure developed by frictional games:

[http://www.penumbra-overture.com/index.php]("http://www.penumbra-overture.com/index.php")

The first episode, Overture, is now available for Windows, Mac and (tada) Linux. They have a demo available for download that allows to play a short part of the game. I've run the demo on both Dapper and Edgy, with a GeForce 6600 and a Radeon X1900XTX, and it worked well in all configurations. The graphics are fairly state-of-the-art with depth-of-field, motion blur and shadow volumes. It looks good, sounds great, and plays well - the control scheme is pretty interesting, as you interact with things in the world via the mouse cursor, but as if the character was actually interacting with objects. Quite fun, and the full game is available as a download to purchase to boot!

Check it out when you get a chance - it's well worth it!
