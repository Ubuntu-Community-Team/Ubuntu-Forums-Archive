### Post by Nick Blinko on 2008-04-09
Well besides quake and unreal, there's a couple of other commercial games available for linux that feature multiplayer.

Soldier of fortune (can't play online since the 1..07 patch was never released, at least that I know of, maybe you can play with other linux players.)

Sin (no one plays it online though, used to be popular, can't seem to get the retail sin client working but the demo at [www.ritualistic.com](www.ritualistic.com) works fine and has multiplayer)

Kingpin (like sin, but there's a few who still play, oh and you can't play on most servers since they use a cheat protection client called monkey mod and they never ported it to linux nor released the source code. I host a kingpin server for linux clients but I'm the only person I know of who plays it online using the linux client)

Rune (haven't installed it yet, but I'm sure there's problems)

Hexen 2 (look up the hammer of thyrion source port, works well under linux. I couldn't find anyservers for it though so I decided to host my own, no one's played on it yet though, and the demo can play with retail players, so you can grab the demo and hop on a server)

Tribes 2
