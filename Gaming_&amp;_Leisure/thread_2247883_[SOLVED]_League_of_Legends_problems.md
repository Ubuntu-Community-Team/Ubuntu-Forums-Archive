### Post by reid2 on 2014-10-11
Hi. After the recent league of legends 1.18 patch i can't get into the client. I can make it to the login screen but whenever i try to log in i get "Connection failure: unable to connect to the PVP.net server". Before the patch it was working perfectly and i was wondering if anyone knew a fix or a workaround to this issue.
I'm running Ubuntu 14.04.

Found this in the error log: "err:ole:CoUninitialize Mismatched CoUninitialize"

Fix: 
[http://forums.euw.leagueoflegends.com/board/showthread.php?t=1939551](http://forums.euw.leagueoflegends.com/board/showthread.php?t=1939551).

I had to change my wine version to "1.7.24-LeagueOfLegendsCSMT". This fixed the login issue but whenever i tried to open the in game shop it crashed, installing this patch [https://github.com/A-Metaphysical-Drama/LoL-Linux-Tools]("http://euw.leagueoflegends.com/board/redirect.php?do=verify&redirect_url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FA-Metaphysical-Drama%2FLoL-Linux-Tools") fixed that problem.
