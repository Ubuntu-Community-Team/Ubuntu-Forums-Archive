### Post by BLTicklemonster on 2007-05-12
Okay, it's about time.

After years of working one way or the other, and building on the initial code of HenryHouse with his HoverBat, and Crazy's work, BlackCheetah has gotten some excellent meshes for vehicles, and has single handedly gotten the replication worked out on them, and the vehicles now work very well online now. 

Beta versions, so there's still a glitch here and there, but there's nothing stopping us now. UT Vehicles are here to stay.

Come to our server at 

8.6.74.123

and look for any map with the word 

manta 

in it, and choose to play it from the mapvote that shows up between maps.

To use a vehicle, walk up to it and press fire, to fly, press alt fire, to exit the vehicle, press crouch.

Not all vehicles fly, mind you.

We'll be working on the code to get it bug free, and also working on more maps for the vehicles so there will be more to play on.

New game types will be in the offing in the future. Onslaught type game, etc.

Come visit!
