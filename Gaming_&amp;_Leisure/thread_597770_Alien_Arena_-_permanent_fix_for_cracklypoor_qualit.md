### Post by FG123 on 2007-10-30
I recently tried out Alien Arena 2007, and notice several other people had the same problem I did - crackly sound, which to me rendered the game unplayable. I found the solution just now, and it works so well I figure it's worth making a mention here for future use:

[http://corent.proboards42.com/index.cgi?board=aatech&action=display&thread=1178685646](http://corent.proboards42.com/index.cgi?board=aatech&action=display&thread=1178685646)

> Ok, while running crx.sdl, here is what you type in the console:

s_khz 48
snd_restart

That should fix the static.
Works for me, plus you only need to do this once and the new audio settings are stored in the game's config.
