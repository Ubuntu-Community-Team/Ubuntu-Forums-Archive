### Post by Zeven on 2013-08-22
[quote=Duffadash]I just learned about the existance of the Xoreos project. The project aims to re-implement Bioware's Aurora engine and all the games based on it.

Eventually this will mean that the following games will run under Linux:

    [LIST]
[*]Neverwinter Nights 1 and 2
[*]    Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic 1 and 2
[*]    Dragon Age 1 and 2
[*]    The Witcher
[*]    Jade Empire
[*]    Sonic Chronicles: The Dark Brotherhood.[/LIST]
The Projects homepage can be found [here]("http://xoreos.org/"). And the current code is hosted at [GitHub]("https://github.com/xoreos/xoreos").

Xoreos is currently being maintained by [Sven Hesse]("http://drmccoy.de/") and [Matthew Hoops]("http://clone2727.blogspot.com/") who also has had a lot of experience from working on ScummVM. Although they have [requested some support recently]("http://drmccoy.de/gobsmacked/?p=530") to help keeping the project going.

[A mailing list has been created for those interested.]("https://xoreos.org/mailman/listinfo/xoreos-devel")

I have also created a [GOG wishlist entry]("http://www.gog.com/wishlist/site/help_out_the_xoreos_project_with_supporting_the_witcher_on_linuxmac"), for official support from CD Projekt in supporting The Witcher. (Which I doubt will happen, but I figure it's worth a shot.)[/quote]

The above was quoted in its entirety. [This is the source.]("http://www.reddit.com/r/opensourcegames/comments/1kvy0k/xoreos_reimplementing_the_aurora_engine_very/")
