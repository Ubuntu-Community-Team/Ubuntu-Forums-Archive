Alright guys, hear this. Remember True Combat: Elite, that mod for Wolfenstein: ET? Well, being an avid player of that mod as I was, the site ([http://www.tcelite.net](http://www.tcelite.net)) is now sadly going down. However, one of the players of that game, RACE, has decided to give something bacl to the TC:E community, and is developing his own game called Team Combat: Urban Assault. The game is still in its early days, but development is going on. Here is the site: [http://www.teamcombat.net/](http://www.teamcombat.net/)

It would be great if we could get a few of you guys to join, just to let them know that the Linux gaming community is behind their back!
