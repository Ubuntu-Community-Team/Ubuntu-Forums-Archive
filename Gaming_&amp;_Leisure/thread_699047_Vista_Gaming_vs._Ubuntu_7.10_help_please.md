### Post by ashmew2 on 2008-02-17
The answer is Dual Boot! :P
I wouldnt recommend removing Vista because if you want to try any other games in the near future , they may or may not run Under Wine (for now..).
In my opinion , you should set aside about 4 gbs of space , install Ubuntu in it and Dual Boot with Vista. This way you can check whether WoW is running better under Wine/Ubuntu . If you like it , you can always put aside more space for it. 
If you dont get the performance you want (quite unlikely) , you can get that fixed or you can go back to Vista.
