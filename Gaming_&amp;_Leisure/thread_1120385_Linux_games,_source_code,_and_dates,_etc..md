### Post by Stupendoussteve on 2009-04-08
> **accLinux said:**
> I guess there are current games for linux, but are windows games better????

Better? Depends. More polished? Often. Most developers still do not devote much time to making Linux versions of their games. It's not that they hate Linux, but Windows (and Mac) has the market share and that is where the money is. Creating games for multiple OSes adds complexity and expense to the development process.
