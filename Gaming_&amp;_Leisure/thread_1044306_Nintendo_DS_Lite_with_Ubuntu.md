It's actually one of those schrodinger cat things. It's both legal and illegal at the same time. Some sections of copyright law state you are supposed to be able to make your own backups as part of fair use but other sections state otherwise. It's something that is being fought over in the courts (not ROMS directly but similar issues). For the purposes of this forum though I'm pretty certain the mods don't allow any discussion about creating ROMs here. If you want more information on copyright law a really good site to look at is:
[www.eff.org](www.eff.org)
