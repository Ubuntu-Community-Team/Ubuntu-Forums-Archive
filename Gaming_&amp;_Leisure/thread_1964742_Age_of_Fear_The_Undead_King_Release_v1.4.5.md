**Age of Fear: The Undead King version 1.4.5** is available in [URL="http://www.age-of-fear.net/index.php/aof-downloads"]Downloads section!

_Age of Fear: The Undead King_  is a fantasy turn-based strategy. The game features a novel  battle   system, two campaigns with solid fantasy stories and very flexible  multi-player and skirmish modes.
 The story takes place in an untamed fantasy land, where three    forces: the Human Kingdom, the Greenskins' Horde and the Undead Legion -   are fighting for  domination and survival.
 You will encounter quick skirmishes, where wise unit's cooperation is   essential. You will visit distant lands either as a honorable knight  or  an evil necromancer. You will conquer cities and destroy ancient  evil!

[http://www.age-of-fear.net/](http://www.age-of-fear.net/)


_Changes:_
- items - items are given during campaign to heroes (for example Ring of Protection +1 DEF)
- new unit portrait backgrounds - see screenshot below
- new experience/evolve system - check note below
- Royal Guard got new skill - Defence Stance (skip action for +2 DEF until next turn)
- new graphic mode (Linux only) - Render X
- few graphic fixes and optimizations
- few minor bug-fixes

New version **v1.5.1** is available in [URL="http://www.age-of-fear.net/index.php/aof-downloads"]Downloads section.

_Changes:_
- New Experience/Evolve System - _this could be real game changer_!
Based on players feedback we have implemented a choice of unique  attributes for every unit. In this version, attributes can be purchased  for experience points (details are discussed [here]("http://www.age-of-fear.net/index.php/component/kunena/2-general-discussion/350-new-experienceevolve-system-choice-poll")). That feature gives a tons of customizations' options for all units and introduces a totally new strategy layer!

**We plan to use this system in sequel, therefore your feedback is much appreciated!**

- Terrain Effects - we have experimented with animations for terrain  (bubbles in swamps, water drops in caves, fog, fireflies, animated lava,  lights, etc.). Let's be honest - AOF will not suddenly look like [Diablo III]("http://www.rockpapershotgun.com/images/12/may/do6.jpg") or [HOMM6]("http://www.rockpapershotgun.com/images/11/august/homm63.jpg"). It's small improvement, but it should add to experience.

- few other bug-fixes and optimizations
