### Post by Naegling23 on 2009-01-22
neverwinter nights (the first one)

its commercial, but you can pick up the diamond edition (game plus all expansions) for ~$20USD.  Deep story, dated, but decent graphics, multi-player availability, 100's of hours on the single player side of things.

There are others available, for the mmo side, eveonline, planeshift, eternal lands, regnum come to mind, there are also a bunch of other smaller single and multi player rpg's available.  I've tried most of them, and for me, neverwinter nights is the best of the lot, but dont limit yourself, check out some other ones.
