### Post by iheartubuntu on 2009-10-18
I have two desktop systems with nvidia and dont have a problem with the game. My laptop has ATI graphics card and the game comes up similar to yours and is unplayable.

There are no longer ATI specific drivers for Ubuntu after 8.04. If you want to run 3D type games (even though this one isnt) like Regnum Online or WoW or Future Pinball, SuperTuxKart, etc... then you probably need to have no later than Ubuntu 8.04 installed. But, it doesnt look like you have ATI so... I dont think it will work for you unless you change your video card. My dad has a Dell laptop with an intel video card and many games just dont load up for him.
