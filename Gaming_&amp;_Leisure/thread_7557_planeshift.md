### Post by Wertigon on 2004-12-10
Well, I haven't played it yet due to two things; first, until the Crystal Blue release comes out there really isn't much more to do than gather crystals, and second, being stuck on a 56k in a country that charges your calls on a Pay-Per-Minute basis really helps to keep you off all kinds of dangerous internet addiction. ;)

However, from what I gather from reading forums and stuff Planeshift will be much more focused on the Roleplaying aspect than other MMORPGs. For example, it'll be possible to actually be a woodcutter and *stay* in that role if you so desire.

Of course, there will be hack n' slash adventuring stuff for those who desire it; it's just that, well, you can just as well be a miner, or a smith, or a barrel-binder, or whatever you want. You'll not be limited to just adventuring, you'll hopefully be able to craft beautiful items, and perhaps even get a husband/wife and kids in-game. So I'm definitely looking forward to it. :)
