### Post by Ubuntiac on 2009-01-11
I just came across a great one I'd never heard of before, and it runs native.

[http://www.puppygames.net/droid-assault/](http://www.puppygames.net/droid-assault/)

It's called Droid Wars, and is a very shiny, neo-retro (?!) action, strategy shooter loosely based on the game Paradroid. The idea is that you have to destroy or capture all robots in an arena. You can do this blowing them up, or when you destroy enough, you get the opportunity to capture them and bring them onto your side to battle. The strategy is that different droids are more powerful (better guns, armour, speed etc) but take more points to capture. You're always trying to capture the big guns, while doing as little damage to them first, staying alive and blowing up all that come before you that you don't have energy to capture.

It's all very high speed, rapid trigger finger but with the constant question of capture or destroy.

It's commercial, but cheap and they have a demo that goes up to the first boss. Me also likey supporting great indie game makers. :)
