### Post by curvedinfinity on 2008-09-04
The Mechwarrior series is based off of the premier mech simulating table-top game *Battle Tech* (which is a bunch of fun also, by the way). The idea in Mechwarrior is "What would it be like if 100 ton walking tanks actually existed?" Simulation games are designed to be relatively hard to use, because that's what it would probably be like in whatever circumstance they are simulating. There are generally tons of buttons to memorize, and the gameplay is relatively slow paced, but that's part of the reason its so attractive to me at least. I can't tell you exactly why though... :D
