### Post by Contrast on 2010-06-09
I'm guessing you didn't mean to post this in the Gaming & Leisure forum? :P

The first thing I'd check is whether you accidentally set up some crazy sorting scheme-- at the top-left of the playlist section of the window, you should see a tiny button whose tooltip reads: Clear the playlist sorting configuration. Click that.

Next, which browser are you adding files to the playlist from, Local Music or Files? If you're in the Files browser and you drag and drop a folder to the playlist, it will add them in alphabetical order (as expected per general *file* management behavior).
