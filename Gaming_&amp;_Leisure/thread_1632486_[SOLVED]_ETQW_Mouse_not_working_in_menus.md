### Post by Dingo_aus on 2010-11-28
Posting this to hopefully help others.

Basically ETQW and Unclutter do not work together.

If you use "unclutter" to automatically hide the cursor on your desktop and the try to play ETQW, the first menu unclutter will keep resetting your mouse pointer to the centre of the screen every 2 seconds or so.

The trick is to stop running unclutter.
