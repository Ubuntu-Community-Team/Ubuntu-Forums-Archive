### Post by MaxIBoy on 2008-10-21
Well, for a system like that, you're not going to get much in the way of advanced graphics, but there are still some good classics you'll have fun with and won't cost you money.

First install DOSbox from the repositories (you'll find it in Synaptic.) 
Then, you can get the Wolfenstein 3d shareware, the Doom 1 and Doom 2 sharewares, the Quake 1 shareware, the Duke Nukem 3D shareware, and the Descent shareware (none of them are in the repos, you'll have to Google for them. Descent was especially hard for me to find.)


Another game that's in the repos is Abuse-SDL, which is widely considered to be the platformer shoot-'em-up equivalent of Doom. (Not the same as Mario Brothers-style platformer or River City Ransom-style platformer; this is more like Metal Slug.) It didn't invent the genre, it didn't popularize the genre, but it is an awesome game.
