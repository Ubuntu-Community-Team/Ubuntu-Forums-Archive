### Post by WienerWuerstel on 2010-11-19
You should get Crossover Games because it's cheaper and you don't need Crossover Professional if you just want to play Games. I use the newest Version of Crossover Games and can play Games like TF2, L4D and HL2 without any Problems and with good Performance. But it depends on which Graphics Card you use because Crossover/Wine works better with Nvidia and AMD has more Problems and the Performance for most Source Games is bad. I used a AMD Radeon 3870 myself for a while and only had Problems with Source Games (Bad Performance, Crashes, Games not starting). But now i use a Geforce 250 GTS and everything works like it should. Keep that in Mind when you buy a new PC.
