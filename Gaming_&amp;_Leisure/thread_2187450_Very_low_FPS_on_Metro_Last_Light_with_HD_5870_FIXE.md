### Post by DanglingPointer on 2013-11-12
I would just like to post here in case there are other people who may be experiencing the same sort of issue...

I was experiencing very low FPS with Metro LL with my ATI HD 5870 with the latest AMD Beta driver (CCC 3.11) after the Bolshoi level even at the lowest graphics fidelity levels.  It got to the point where I gave up and made a rant on this thread 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2183281&page=2](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2183281&page=2)
But after a couple of days and a couple of restarts I decided to give it a shot again and I was back to averaging 45-60 FPS!  
It didn't make sense!  
Eventually I realised my GPU overclocking app "AMDOverdriveCtrl" ([http://sourceforge.net/projects/amdovdrvctrl/](http://sourceforge.net/projects/amdovdrvctrl/)) was interfering with the latest Beta driver and not letting it do its job properly.  So I experimented and so far I can say that Metro LL works better without AMDOverdriveCtrl.  So now I'm running the 5870 on stock settings and getting smooth FPS from all Chapters I've done so far on medium slider settings!  :D

So in a nutshell if you're using AMDOverdriveCtrl with a Radeon HD 5870 and playing Metro LL and getting bad FPS; turn it off and restart your PC.  In fact I would say uninstall it!
