E.V.E. Paradox is a game suite consisting of three games:
Flick is a turn-based puzzler, Orbit is an arcade puzzler, and Arena is a top-view action game. E.V.E. Paradox includes an integrated chat system and online awards. The Free Edition contains a reduced number of levels and is not otherwise restricted.
Enhanced editions are available for purchase with additional levels and features for those who want a little something more.
