### Post by Rytron on 2008-06-21
Hi,
I figured out how to progress on Level(map) 4.
In the empty cage shoot it, then the bars go down. Then press the button on the panel.
Go back to the other cage where the red key card is(the bars are down here now). Then you can open another door in the main room.
Now awaits level 5...
