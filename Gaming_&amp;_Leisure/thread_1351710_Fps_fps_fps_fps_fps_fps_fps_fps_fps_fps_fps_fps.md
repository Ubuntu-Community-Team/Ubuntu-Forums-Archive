### Post by Bölvaður on 2009-12-10
see my signature
We play 2 games each Sunday starting at 20:00 GMT


Look through posts in this subforum for others asking the exact question.


If you are a dedicated gamer then you'll love **Warsow**. It is pretty difficult for noobs as the moving system is much more advanced than in any other fps. It is a must to turn on oldmovements.

Who havent heard of Quake3: Arena? It is THE professional fps to play, even though me and others may consider it a noob game for reasons that is too fun to talk about here ^^
Well please.. go on then go play **Quake Live!** on [http://www.quakelive.com/]("http://www.quakelive.com/#home")

**Enemy Territory : Quake Wars** is cool, but too complicated for most people. Perhaps Quake Live is more for you. Note that this game is neither open source nor freeware. But it is made by awesome company so giving them money is a good thing, whilst (like any gamer knows) giving EA money is like paying for the downfall of gaming and buying a welcome gift for casual "gamers".


Well then **Enemy Territory** is obvious choice also for a gamer, but then again, isn't the community pretty weak after et:qw came out? I can say the same thing about Unreal Tournament so Im not gong to suggest that game.


**Urban Terror** is quite good, it's pretty much "back to the basics and lets build on that". the movements are more advanced than in many games, but it's much much more noob friendly and extremely easy to learn to do the most astonishing moves. just like Warsow UrT comes with a race mode.

**Nexiuz** is cool but mostly for graphics. I havent really got into it as any gamer knows... turning off all graphics is the first thing to do.
