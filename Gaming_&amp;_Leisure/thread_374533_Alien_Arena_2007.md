### Post by Phenax on 2007-03-03
> **Anthony Haslam said:**
> Does it support 1440x900?

Yes, it supports any resolution your hardware and monitor are capable of.
I added custom resolution support in 6.04.
All you have to do is go into the "Video" menu, then right-click resolutions. Just go one back from 640x480 (which you can achieve by just right clicking until it says "Custom" really) and it will say "Custom." Type in 1440 for width, 900 for height, then hit apply and you should be good.

### Post by dxdemetriou on 2007-04-24
The problem fixed
Just edit the ~/.codered/arena/config.cfg and change to: set s_khz "48"
It doesn't work with 22 or 44 on some sound cards
Every time you change something from options must be changed, because returns to 44
It worked for me :)
