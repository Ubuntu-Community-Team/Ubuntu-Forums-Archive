Another indie game which also support Linux, but really didn't get any attention by the Linux Community - which is sad actually. It's a great game (gameplay), good spacy 70's retro sci-fi styled theme (like in the movies) and nice background music.

Little Space Duo is a Puzzle Arcade Game.

[i]Little Space Duo is a classic, puzzle-based arcade game starring an unusual pair of heroes - a little girl and a small robot. Join them on a mission through the spaceship and help them to overcome all the challenges and hazards they will encounter on the way.

The game offers 2 gameplay modes and 40 levels of challenging puzzles.[/i]


There's screenshots, video and demo on its homepage.
