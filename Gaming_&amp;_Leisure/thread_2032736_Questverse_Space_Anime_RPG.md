I saw the update to your campaign , it is better than before, but there are still a few issues.

I would say start small with your goal, since it's a "psychological and attention grabbing" war out there. And usually People are afraid of jumping in if they see that the goal has little chance to be met, so big goals can scare off many.

Also once you get **just one** successful campaign and build your audience (essential!), you can create new ones after a while that highly likely to be successful too (so don't place all your eggs in one basket).

Try to study as many campaigns as possible, specially indie ones with few developers which might be similar to yours.

Don't rush and don't worry, since Rome wasnt built in a day :)

Just keep reading and studying the links I posted earlier.

Now, one important thing I noticed was the video. While I really like the art style:), I could not understand almost anything that was going on (apart from customizing the character).
