### Post by scubanator87 on 2006-11-10
I was just thinking, there are a lot of great online games too. and Why don't we share our favorites and try out some new ones?

My personal top favorite currently is:

[SIZE="3"][::CyberNations::]("http://www.cybernations.net")[/SIZE]

Basically, you get to build a nation (based on Google maps), and develop it each day. There is inter nation trading, wars, peace, nuclear power/weapons, and tons other nifty aspects to the game such as nationality, religion, and government type. Another great feature is the anti cheating system which attempts to keep people from making more than one nation and trading with themselves.  
[SIZE="4"]
TIP:[/SIZE] If this sounds like fun (and even if it doesn't give it a shot before you discredit it) and start playing, defiantly read through the forums. You will learn the game a lot fast than through trial an error.  

Be sure to check it out and share others!
