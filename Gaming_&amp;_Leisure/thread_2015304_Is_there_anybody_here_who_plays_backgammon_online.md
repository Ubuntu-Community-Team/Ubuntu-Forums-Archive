I played Backgammon regularly on Yahoo! ([http://games.yahoo.com/bg](http://games.yahoo.com/bg))

It works on Ubuntu+FireFox+non-Sun Java. (Also works if you have Sun JRE installed).

I don't think you can save games, though. And the crowd is rather rough at times; for example, certain players insist on continous doublings at the start of the game, to "max out" the points, so that their rankings can move swiftly. (Typically these are 99 point games).

There are always players, 24/7. But many games are abandoned half-way (25%+).

It's still the best I find, though.
