### Post by Hobo Joe on 2008-04-07
Set the game to your resolution, and windowed mode. Then, set a hotkey for 'fullscreen' with your window manager.(System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts) Then, launch the game and press the hotkey and you're set!
