### Post by rockoprem on 2010-06-06
:) a single 'a' is considered as 'aa ki matra'. Whereas, 'A' is taken as 'aa'. So, to type amar.. u need to press right alt+a, to get 'a' and then m and r as usual. I know it sounds a bit stupid to type 'a', but that's the way it is designed. You can change the key grabbing, though.. but it would just mess up the layout. So, the solution is to press right alt and the key.
