I want to remove the short drum beats when the Login window is displayed. How do I do that?

---

### Post by sayakb on 2008-06-30
Goto **System->Administration->Login Window**
Click on the **Accessibility  **tab and **uncheck  **the *Login screen ready *option. 
Press Close and logout to check.
