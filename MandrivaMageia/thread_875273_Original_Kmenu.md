### Post by wxnker on 2008-07-30
Are you thinking about kickoff? If so then just right click the kmenu button and select the default kde menu.

If you're thinking about the menu structure, go to "configure your computer/system/menu style".

Cheers,
wxnker

---

### Post by 67GTA on 2008-07-30
I don't care for kickoff either. Configure Your Computer>System>Menu Style>Original KDE is exactly what I was looking for. Thank you.
