### Post by AdamWill on 2008-10-31
Xfce is not in fact that light any more (a stock Xfce desktop in 2009 uses 10MB *more* RAM than a stock GNOME desktop). And you also have to factor in that the most significant factor for performance is the apps you run: xterm opens fast in KDE 4, and OpenOffice.org opens slow in IceWM.

LXDE is emerging as a good choice for a truly lightweight desktop alternative, you might want to try that (task-lxde).
