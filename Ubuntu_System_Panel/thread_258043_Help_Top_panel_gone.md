### Post by delfick on 2006-09-15
> **enesbitt said:**
> I know this sounds stupid but how do I get into the terminal

don't feel stupid, everyone starts somewhere :D

In the applications menu, go to "accessories" and "terminal" should be near the bottom of that menu.

To make it easier for you, you could create a shorcut on the desktop....basically
right click on the desktop, and choose "create launcher"
choose any name you want (i.e. terminal)
then in the command box type "gnome-terminal"
for an icon, you can use whatever icon you want. I suggest one that looks like a terminal, like this one here [http://delfick755.googlepages.com/terminal.png](http://delfick755.googlepages.com/terminal.png) just download that to a folder somewhere, then in the create launcher dialog thing, click the "no icon" button, then browse to the folder where you have your icon, and choose that icon. Then press ok

then you double click the shortcut and terminal comes up :D
