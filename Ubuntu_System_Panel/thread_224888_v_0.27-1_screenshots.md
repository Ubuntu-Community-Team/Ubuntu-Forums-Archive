### Post by agurk on 2006-07-28
> **Ziox said:**
> how did you make your panel/menu like that?
Hmm, I'm not sure exactly what you mean... to rearrange things you:

1. right-click an object (e.g. the trashcan) and deselect "lock to panel"
2. right-click the object again and select "move"
3. move it around (you can even move objects between different panels) and left-click when you're happy
4. right-click and select "lock to panel"

Besides some rearranging, I decreased the height of the panel somewhat, added USP, removed the standard menus and decreased the number of virtual desktops from 4 to 2.
Oh, and of course, I removed the bottom panel completely after putting some of its stuff in the top panel instead.

Hope that explains things.
