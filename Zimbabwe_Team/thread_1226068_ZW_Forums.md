Hi Guys,

Unfortunately, the ZW international forum seems to have very little attention as of recently..

Most of us LoCo team members communicate via email mailing lists at the moment, particularly because we all have access to email during the day.
Getting to the internet sometimes can be a problem in zimbabwe, so we mainly communicate via email.

If you have questions, or would like to speak to us, please consider contacting us via the following mediums until the international forum gathers momentum later on.

Our team can be contacted on the email address "team@ubuntu.org.zw",or you can join the ubuntu-zw international mailing list, where you can communicate with a large number of ubuntu and other OSS enthusiasts.

To join the ubuntu-zw list please see the link below:

[https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-zw](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-zw)
