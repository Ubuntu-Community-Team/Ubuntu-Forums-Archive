No other input yet?

Anyway, just to give you guys an update: we have made good progress on the site, but still need to spend a lot of time on it. Our biggest problem has been, and continues to be, the irratic power supply and constant power cuts. If it weren't for that I think we might have been finished by now. Unfortunately, the cost of running generators is also becoming too high, so that hasn't helped either.... the joys of living in Zim :)

I haven't had any feedback from Yo!Africa yet on domain registration and repo. I'm sure they're doing their best but are obviously also struggling along like everyone else here.

Will keep everyone informed.

### Post by NIT006.5 on 2008-11-08
Well since I was the only person posting to this thread anyway, I've decided to carry on! :)

As you can see, looking back at the date on my first post, it has taken a long time to get this off the ground. I am happy to report however that the local Zimbabwe Web site is taking shape now. It has not been "officially" launched just yet, as we are still finalising a few things, but it is functional. [http://www.ubuntu.org.zw](http://www.ubuntu.org.zw)

Huge credit must be given to Kalpesh and Ronald for the many hours of hard work that have gone into getting this site moving. 

As I said, it has not been officially launched yet, so if you have any comments or criticisms, NOW is the time. We hope you will look forward to the official launch and join us in celebrating it when the announcement is made.
