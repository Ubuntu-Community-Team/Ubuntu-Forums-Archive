### Post by quietas on 2009-02-26
I'd highly recommend Zimbra. It works, it's easy, it's even enterprise ready if you need it to be. Best yet, there is a free version that is nearly as feature filled as the paid version. I use it in my professional life with 250 users, there are colleges using it with 10,000 users, and Comcast has moved their millions of users to Zimbra now as well.

[6.06 LTS Zimbra Install]("http://wiki.zimbra.com/index.php?title=Ubuntu_6.06_Server_(Dapper_Drake)_Beginner%27s_Install_Guide")
[8.04 LTS Zimbra Install]("http://wiki.zimbra.com/index.php?title=Ubuntu_8.04_LTS_Server_(Hardy_Heron)_Install_Guide")

There is a how to for 6.06 LTS, it's essentially the same for 8.04. The support the stable LTS versions, it will work on 8.10, but they don't have official support.

The forums are spectacular with very knowledgeable people, much like here at Ubuntu.
