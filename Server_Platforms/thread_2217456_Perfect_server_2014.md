### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-04-17
What is your Use Case for the server?  What specifically do you want the server to do?

There is no perfect server.  There are working servers and non-working servers.  You want to maximize one and minimize the other.

You are confused because there are several frameworks that do similar things.  Some work better than others.  Some have more features.  Others are more focused.  Until you identify your Use Case, the selection of what frameworks to use will be a mystery.
