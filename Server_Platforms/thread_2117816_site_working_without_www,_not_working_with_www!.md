### Post by Novice2013 on 2013-02-19
The problem originated in the histories of my browsers, after deleting the history of one of my browsers the problem was solved!

### Post by MrKaliman on 2013-02-19
Just a remark, normally a webaddress only contains 1subdomain.
For example: subdomain.domain.suffix

Subdomain.subdomain.domain.suffix is no web standard.

Hence the problem is solved!
