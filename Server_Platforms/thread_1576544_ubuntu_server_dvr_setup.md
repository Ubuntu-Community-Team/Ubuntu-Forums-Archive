### Post by bmathis on 2010-09-17
Take a look at mythbuntu which is based off of MythTV (PVR Software). Myth can schedule and record your shows for you. To change the channel on your box, you'll need an IR blaster. 

Also, Myth has the ability to let you watch the shows via flash from its web interface.
