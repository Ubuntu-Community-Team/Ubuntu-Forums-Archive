### Post by kemra on 2011-07-19
Get the IP of the servers from your VPS and then go to your Domain Registrar's website and they should have an option somewhere to let you point your domain names at your VPS' IP's.

It might take a few hours after doing this for the changes to sync through the DNS servers but that's all that should be required.
