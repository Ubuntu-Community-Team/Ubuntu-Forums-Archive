### Post by snowpine on 2010-08-13
Ubuntu 10.04 (the Long Term Support release) would be a fine choice, and is the most popular option here on these forums. :)

Really the only one on the list that's a *bad* choice is Fedora, and I say that only because it is a fast-moving distro that gets a mere 13 months of support for each release. The other 3 are all slow-moving distros known for their stability and long-term support; can't really go wrong with any of them. Ubuntu is the most beginner-friendly and has the newest "stuff."
