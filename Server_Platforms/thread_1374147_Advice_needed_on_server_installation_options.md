### Post by volkswagner on 2010-01-06
You may want to consider running a VPS from a provider such as [Linode]("https://www.linode.com/") or Fivebean.  [Fivebean]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1128094&highlight=fivebean") is offering really inexpensive solutions for Ubuntu forum members.  Linode has some better options such as ability to backup your image and store it for recovery/replication.  Fivebean is great for folks on a budget such as myself.  Of course there are many others if you want to shop around.

This is a great way to go as you don't have headaches such as battling Dynamic IP from your provider (unless you are fortunate to have static), Redundant internet connections and power supply backup, etc.  Plus you don't even have to pay the electric to run a machine 24/7.
