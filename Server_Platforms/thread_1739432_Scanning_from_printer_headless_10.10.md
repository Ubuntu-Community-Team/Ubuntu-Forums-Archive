### Post by volkswagner on 2011-04-26
For scanning documents use Sane-Project.  It may help to tell us more about your setup (model/brand/connection type).

If your scanner is an actual network scanner it may have the option to scan to ftp.  This is a nice setup as all work can be done at the scanner.

The Sane-Project has a long list of [available frontends]("http://www.sane-project.org/sane-frontends.html") to use.

This is an [old thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=679028"), but still may be relevant.

Enjoy!
