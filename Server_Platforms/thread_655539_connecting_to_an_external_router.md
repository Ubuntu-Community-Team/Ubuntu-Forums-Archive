### Post by rsw686 on 2008-01-01
What do you want to access on your home server? You need to know the ports of the services and then add port forwards to your router configuration.

From the outside you will be able to access the above by the public IP assigned to your router. If you want you can use a free service like dyndns.com which will provide you yourname.dyndns.com so you don't have to remember your IP. When your IP changes it will be updated automatically.
