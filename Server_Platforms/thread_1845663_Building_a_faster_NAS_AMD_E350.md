### Post by papibe on 2011-09-17
IMO, yes.

Any 'computer' will have better results than an appliance-like NAS (although lately I've seen very good ones).

For example, I have a P4, 512Mb RAM server that max my download speed. My brother has a Celeron Dual Core, 1Gb that can take full advantage of his 1MB/s connection (that's one mega bytes per seconds).

Just some thoughts,
