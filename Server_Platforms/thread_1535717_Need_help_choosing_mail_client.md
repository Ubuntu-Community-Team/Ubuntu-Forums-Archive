### Post by Vincentlaborant on 2010-07-21
Depending on your needs, I would advise Evolution or Thunderbird.

Evolution can easily connect with an Exchange server, while Thunderbird has a very good lay out and is very easy to configure.

Outlook is not available out of the box, since you would have to install it under Wine (which you first need to install and configure).
