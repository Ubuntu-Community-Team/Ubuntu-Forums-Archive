### Post by spynappels on 2011-05-24
You basically need to enable packet routing in the kernel. The easiest way to do that is to edit /etc/sysctl.conf and uncomment the line 
```
#net.ipv4.ip_forward=1
```
so it looks like
```
net.ipv4.ip_forward=1
```
save and reboot.

This allows your system to route packets between different subnets such as your LAN and your VPN. A script in one of your installs may have done this for you but the change may not be persistent after a reboot until you change the sysctl.conf file.
