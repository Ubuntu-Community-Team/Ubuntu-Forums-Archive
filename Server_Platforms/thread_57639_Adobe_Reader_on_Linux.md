Adobe Reader on Linux or Solaris:

-- For version 7.0, users should upgrade to Adobe Reader 7.0.1 from [www.adobe.com/products/acrobat/readstep2.html](www.adobe.com/products/acrobat/readstep2.html) .

-- For versions prior to 7.0, users should upgraded to 7.0.1 from [www.adobe.com/products/acrobat/readstep2.html](www.adobe.com/products/acrobat/readstep2.html) .

[http://www.adobe.com/support/techdocs/321644.html](http://www.adobe.com/support/techdocs/321644.html)
