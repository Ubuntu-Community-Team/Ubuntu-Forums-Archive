### Post by LordHunter317 on 2005-06-05
[QUOTE=CrustyDOD]Where's the problem here?[/QUOTE]Postfix isn't configured to recieve mail for your domain.  Unless VCHS setup your domain in a virtual table (I'd have to see those files to tell),  your myhostname, mydomain, myorigin, and mydestination parameters are not setup correctly for an Internet recieving host.

Also, postfix will need correct DNS so it sees itself as those things.  This may mean editing /etc/hosts, or changing your DNS.

However, before changing them, you need to be sure VHCS didnt' try to setup your domain as a virtual one.  Otherwise, you may end up with schiophrenic configurations.  

This is why I really don't like things like VHCS.
