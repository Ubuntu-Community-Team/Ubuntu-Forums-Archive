### Post by cdenley on 2010-04-27
There is no such thing as "Ubuntu LTS 9". LTS releases are only released every two years. The version represents the release date.

 6.06 released June 2006 (a couple months late)
 8.04 released April 2008
10.04 scheduled release April 2010

You should not drop the minor number when referring to ubuntu versions.
