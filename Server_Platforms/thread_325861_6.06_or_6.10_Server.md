### Post by PilotJLR on 2006-12-27
The main differences is that 6.10 has the upstart system to replace Sys V Init, and it has a newer kernel and support for newer chipsets.

If 6.06 installs and runs in your hardware, then I would go with that for the LTS...

### Post by Sef on 2006-12-27
