I'm thinking it could be related to these, but that's only based on noticing "cciss" in the screenshot after seeing a similar bug report mention it:
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/666828](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/666828)
[https://bugzilla.redhat.com/show_bug.cgi?id=615543](https://bugzilla.redhat.com/show_bug.cgi?id=615543)

The blocked processes are probably calling sync() or fsync() to wait for writes to finish, and something is causing them to wait forever. I've seen it happen to dpkg on 10.04 when another process is writing heavily and there's a usb drive plugged in, but this could be another issue.
