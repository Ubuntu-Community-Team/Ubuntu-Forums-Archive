### Post by Vegan on 2011-09-04
This year I have suffered 2 dead disks so far. Both died earlier in Q1 and I was able to get one replaced so I am only minus one hard disk.

On my [hard disk reliability page]("http://www.windows-it.tk/wp/disk-reliability.html") I do keep track of operating hours and I also take the MTBF value from the spreadsheet that has the source data.

Slowly my MTBF has improved after the [2 disks managed to skew]("http://www.windows-it.tk/wp/disk-failures.html") the value down significantly.

Business has been slow so no new disks this year yet.

### Post by Vegan on 2012-01-16
I have since purchased a 2 TB ST2000DL003 hard disk. Bought it 2 days before the floods caused disk prices to go berserk.

I have it on the server as a secondary storage volume.

After dissecting the dead disks I noticed the 5V TVS diode on one disk was fried.

Makes the case for a better PSU for the machines.

My [PSU page]("http://www.hardcore-games.tk/wp/psu.php") is well regarded and I have found it linked to all over the place.
