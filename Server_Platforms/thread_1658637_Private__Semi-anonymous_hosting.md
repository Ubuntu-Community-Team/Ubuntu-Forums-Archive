### Post by James78 on 2011-01-03
No real users should find you (at least not easily). However bots and zombified computers will still find and attack you, so make sure to still secure things like SSH, FTP, etc etc.

### Post by wongo888 on 2011-01-03
If you want to be found using a regular domain name then you can probably just use any of the Whois Privacy services. This will stop most regular people from knowing your name and address.

The larger question is: "Who will be trying to find you?"

If the answer is the US Government, then a whois privacy service just isn't going to do it. Google "rapgodfathers seized by ICE" to see what the US just did to seize domains straight from the dot-com TLD registrar Versign.

You might also try googling "bulletproof hosting" to get some other ideas.
