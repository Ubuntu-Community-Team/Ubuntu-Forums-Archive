### Post by volkswagner on 2010-01-26
Dive in head first.  There are several books and resources, but reading such material can be difficult to understand without the context.

In my opinion having such books and content on the net used as a reference is great.  I suggest starting out with a good Ubuntu how-to on a site like [howtoforge.com]("http://www.howtoforge.com/howtos/linux/ubuntu").

Pay close attention to the code, and where you need to edit for your personal info.  If you have a particular question or problem, search the net and the forums for answers.
