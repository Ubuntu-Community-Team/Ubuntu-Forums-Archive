### Post by papibe on 2011-11-30
Hi hego555.

For a home server it is more than enough.
For hosting stuff that will be access by you, and maybe family, it sound just fine (like a personal blog for example).

You may want to upgrade RAM and hard disk in case:

- You will be running more apps and services at the same time (RAM).
- You will be hosting lots of media files (hard drive).

In any case, since your upload speed is kind of low (actually a very common household upload), you won't be able to serve well hundreds of clients any way.

What kind of apps/services you'll be hosting?
