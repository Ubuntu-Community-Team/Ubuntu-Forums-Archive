### Post by rich on 2006-02-10
Oddly enough, for me the question isn't ease of use it's money. 

If I had the money (+ space) I'd have them on separate boxes. The principle being that if one box breaks people can still do "stuff" - if all your eggs are in one box then you have 10 pairs of eyes looking at you try to fix it. 

It also helps that smoothies are rock solid and a piece of cake via the web interface. Plus they run on cheap hardware. 


my £0.02
