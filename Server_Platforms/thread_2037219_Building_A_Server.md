### Post by volkswagner on 2012-08-04
> **throese said:**
> Update: I got both covers off, but now trying to remove the piece that holds the motherboard in place...all of this just to remove the floppy drive. Lol

You should not need to remove the motherboard to take out the floppy.

The floppy and hard drive are mounted in a cage.  the entire cage should pull out the front.  There should be two tabs to squeeze allowing you to remove the cage.  It looks like the two tabs are covered a little by your red circle.

You may need to wiggle the cage a pull at the same time, as it can be a tight fit.
