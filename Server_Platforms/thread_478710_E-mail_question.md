### Post by chris.zeman on 2007-06-19
It is certainly an attainable goal. Just be prepared to spend a great deal of time on it. Regarding HTML or PHP...

I don't think you can create a web-based e-mail client in straight HTML, but I might be wrong. You would need to use PHP or some other language to handle the communications with the mail server.

Squirrelmail, for what it is, is a decent program. There are lots of others out there for you to check out if you'd rather have a ready-made solution. I use a program called DWMail that is pretty nice. It's not free, but VERY reasonably priced. ***DISCLAIMER: I'm not affiliated with the company that produces DWMail.*** I just like what it does and they have good support through their forum. In fact, the guy who produces the program is very active in the forum and helps out a lot.

Good luck with whatever you decide to do.
