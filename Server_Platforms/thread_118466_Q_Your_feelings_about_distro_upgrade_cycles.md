Debian had 4 years between woody and Sarge, the next version may take just as long or longer...

### Post by Glut on 2006-01-18
We cycle hardware every 3 years, so our needs are for a release that lasts that long. However, frequent releases are appreciated as new hardware gets released and it's nice to have built-in support for it - SATA is a good example of this.
