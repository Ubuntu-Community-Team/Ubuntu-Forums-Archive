### Post by windependence on 2008-12-31
Spindle speed is also a big marketing trick and it hoodwinks most people. It is actually no indication at all of ow fast a drive can access data. For that you have to look at seek time. People got so caught up in this that they don't even pay attention anymore to important other statistics. Just because a drive runs at 7200 RPM does not mean it's faster than a 5400 RPM drive. It can also depend on the number of platters and read heads as well. Overall, seek time is a better indicator.

-Tim
