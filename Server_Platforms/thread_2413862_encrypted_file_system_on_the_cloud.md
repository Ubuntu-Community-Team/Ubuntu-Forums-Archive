### Post by LHammonds on 2019-03-04
Slow NAS hardware?  Are you sure writing/reading over the Internet is going to be faster than a local NAS?

We (my place of work) avoid outdated and unused hardware laying around everywhere by leasing the hardware and having it under a maintenance contract so that when hardware fails, a tech can be onsite and swap it out (or opt to replace hardware myself and ship back the dead equipment when done).  Using a lease also ensures the hardware is kept refreshed and does not get outdated like "buy to own" servers that you have to keep alive until it finally dies the good death...which is a support nightmare.
