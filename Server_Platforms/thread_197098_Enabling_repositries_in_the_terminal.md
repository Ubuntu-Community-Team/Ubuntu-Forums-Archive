### Post by vigleik on 2006-06-16
[QUOTE=bluenova]sudo vi /etc/apt/sources.list[/QUOTE]

Doesn't ubuntu come with a user-friendly editor, such as nano or pico, as well? Try "sudo nano /etc/apt/sources.list" or "sudo pico /etc/apt/sources.list" instead.

I have never understood how to use vi, and I know I'm not the only one. While it might be better for some, and indeed the fastest editor in the world once you learn its ins and outs, I know that a lot of newbies have been stuck figuring out how to do anything in vi. Even exiting vi is hard.

(I hope this doesn't start a flamewar, and if it does I apologize.)
