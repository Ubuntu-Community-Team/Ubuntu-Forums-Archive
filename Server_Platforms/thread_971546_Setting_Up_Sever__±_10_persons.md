### Post by Thirtysixway on 2008-11-05
For filesharing and backup, you don't need too much ram.  although I only have 2 people sharing files at one time, I have ftp, mysql, lighttpd, webmin, and vmware running on my server with 512 MB ram.

I would get at least 2 GB if you mean running a private gaming server, but you could probably get by with 1 GB even.
