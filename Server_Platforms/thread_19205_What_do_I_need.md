### Post by az on 2005-03-18
[QUOTE=JMY1983]Since my Ubuntu box is connected to the internet, do I need an antivirus, firewall, and trojan program? Any recommendations would be great.[/QUOTE]

Out-of-the box Ubuntu does not listen on any ports, so you do not really need a firewall.  If you install software that listens on ports, it is assumed that you know what you are doing and will take the neccessary measures (If that means installing a firewall, go ahead - Shorewall, Firestarter, Guarddog)

Just keep up to date with your updates and you will be secure enough.
