### Post by gubemton on 2007-12-03
Do you guys think Ubuntu should include [StartCom]("https://cert.startcom.org/?lang=en&app=110#auth") CA in the "ca-certificates" package? Firefox includes that CA in its own certificate list and I think including it in Ubuntu packages as well would encourage people to use encrypted connections on their web, mail and other servers. Everybody can get free certificates from StartCom and in my opinion there are not any real reasons why people should have to pay large amounts of cash to get a certificate from Thawte  or Verisign to secure their servers.
