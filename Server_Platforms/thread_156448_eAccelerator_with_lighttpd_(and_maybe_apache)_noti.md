### Post by dermotti on 2006-04-07
I currently run Xubuntu with lighttpd as my webserver with php5 /w eAccelerator. 

After i did an update from PHP Version 5.1.2-1ubuntu1, to PHP Version 5.1.2-1ubuntu2, my webserver would not start. 

I ended up finding out it was eAccelerator causing the issue. I recompiled eAccelerator and reinstalled it and everything worked fine.

If you wanna see my expanded resolution I did a writeup on my site [http://www.xorg.us](http://www.xorg.us)

Hopefully it will help someone else who runs into this issue! I know i was sweatin for about 2 hours trying to figure this out. Lucky it was in the middle of the night and things were slow :-)
