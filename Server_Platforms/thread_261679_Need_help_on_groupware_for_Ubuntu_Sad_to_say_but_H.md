### Post by StickyStyle on 2006-09-20
Groupware is the holy grail of linux/FOSS, unfortunately nobody has gotten it quite right as you will see by searching this and any other linux message board.  The hula project is still in development and features are changing and breaking (last i remember calendering was messed up), plus it is focused on the web aspect of the system right now and you mention wanting to use evolution, i feel you are looking in the wrong direction.

Not trying to discourage you from hula or down play the project, as it will be awesome once its finished but i wouldn't expose my users (and my job) to something still in development.

Check out [http://www.open-xchange.com](http://www.open-xchange.com), you might find it more suitable for the desktop environment you are looking for.
