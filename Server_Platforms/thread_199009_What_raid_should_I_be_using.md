### Post by jinx099 on 2006-06-18
With 2 drives you are limited to either RAID 0 or RAID 1.  If you want speed, do RAID 0, and also you will have 1 9 GB logical drive.  If your data is really important you can do RAID 1 which will only allow you a 4.5 GB logical drive, but you will still ahve all your data if one of your drives dies.
