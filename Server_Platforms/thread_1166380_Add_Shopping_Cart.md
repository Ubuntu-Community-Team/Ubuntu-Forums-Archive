### Post by LepeKaname on 2009-06-24
The most stable and tested system (according to many articles) is [OsCommerce]("http://www.oscommerce.com/") which is included in many hosting services. In that case, only HTML+CSS may be required to have 
your site running. 

If you are planning to sell many things, then I recommend you to go with a commercial software as they will do all the customization for you or pay to someone to customize any open source system. Specially if you require special payment/shipping methods that are not included in the Cart system.

There are many open source carts out there...

### Post by windependence on 2009-06-25
Oscommerce is quite stable and does use a backend database such as MySQL. It is a full featured cart and supports most common payment gateways. I have done several sites for customers without problems. Modification is easy as there are many community supplied plug ins available to get the result you want. IMHO it's as good if not better than some paid solutions.
