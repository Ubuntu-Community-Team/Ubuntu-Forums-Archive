This is the wrong forum for your question. The Server Talk forum is for problems relating to Ubuntu *used as* a server, not for connecting *to* servers. 

Anyway, the ed2k network uses servers where clients connect to and launch their searches. You'll need to download a list of servers. A good place to start is [Google](http://www.google.com/search?q=server.met). Moreover, you'll probably also need to configure portforwarding of the relevant ports to your eDonkey client on your router/firewall, which is explained in the manual of said client.
