### Post by GavinX on 2005-08-07
I would recommend installing some form of antivirus for two reasons 1. Someone could send mail to you which you may need to forward to someone who uses Windows. If this mail is infected, then the person receiving it can have their computer infected from mail received from you. 2. Although there are no known viruses for Linux, you never know just when one may appear. Although the damager that it may inflict would be considerably less that what Windows users sometimes experience, antivirus software would help to eliminate any possible damage. It's like having car insurance...... not because you have never been in an accident doesn't mean that you'll never get into one.
