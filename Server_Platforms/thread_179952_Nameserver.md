### Post by Jussi Kukkonen on 2006-05-21
Not exactly sure what you wanted to know, but... Normally your ISP has (two or more) nameservers, and they should give you their IP addresses. You just add those in the list in DNS-tab of *Networks settings* (System --> Administration --> Networking).

The DNS servers are used to translate domain names to IP addresses e.g. when you write *[www.example.com](www.example.com)* in your browser.
