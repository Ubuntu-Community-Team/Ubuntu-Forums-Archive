### Post by windependence on 2008-07-17
Yep, that would do it. You will have to reset the router to factory defaults using the little reset hole on the back of the router. You may have to hold it in while booting the router to do it. Then, set your subnet mask to 255.255.255.0 and your router IP address to 192.168.1.1 or whatever you want but remember the IP, in fact write it on the router so yo can acces it.
