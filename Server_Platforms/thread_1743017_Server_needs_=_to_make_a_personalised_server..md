### Post by arrrghhh on 2011-04-29
Well, I'm not going to make any human rights comments.  I'm not sure how to achieve the spy network you want off hand, maybe with a forced squid proxy...

But the best option IMHO?  Put OpenDNS on your router, have it filter out sites you don't want them going to.  Assuming they don't have access to the router, they have to go thru its DNS - which means no shady sites.  You can make the filters as strict or as lenient as you wish, and even filter by category or per-site.
