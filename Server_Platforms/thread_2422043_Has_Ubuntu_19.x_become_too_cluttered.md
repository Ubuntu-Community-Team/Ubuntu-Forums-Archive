### Post by rbmorse on 2019-07-01
When you do the installation, select the "minimal install" option.  It will forgo most of the cruft, and if there is something not included that you want you can get it from repository. 

Although i share your opinion, we should note that for every two of us there are at least two people asking why Ubuntu doesn't include xxx package with the default installation (gparted for me).  

All in all, I think the devs strike a good balance in the regular package, and I appreciate the minimal install option for saving me a little trouble weeding the system after the installer is finished.
