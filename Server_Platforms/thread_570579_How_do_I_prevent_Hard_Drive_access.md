### Post by netlogic on 2007-10-08
You don't. If someone has a physical access to your machine, you don't have security. That applies to every operating systems.  That is why many corporations have different security polices for laptop users than desktop users.
You can encrypt your /home folder if this is a home machine. In the corporate environment, data encryption is a challenge to maintain. For the corporate environment, you need to work on a security policy documentation.
