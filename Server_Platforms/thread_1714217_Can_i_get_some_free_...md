### Post by BkkBonanza on 2011-03-25
Only thing like a VPS I know for free is Amazon EC2. They now have 1st year free for new signups. Not exactly 1st year free but a given amount of cpu hours and traffic per month for 1 year.

In my opinion that is an awesome deal. I don't think their low end micro and small instances are as fast as a typical small VPS (according to my benchmarks anyway) but for the price you can't beat it. I don't think I've ever seen another hosting company offer free VPSes beyond maybe a first month.
