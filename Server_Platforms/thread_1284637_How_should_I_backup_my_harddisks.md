### Post by Lucky. on 2009-10-06
I'm sorry, I don't know of a DVD solution like that.  However using 4.7GB DVD disks, you would need almost 100 DVDs to make a full backup.  Chances are, at least one of them would get corrupted during the writing process.  If all of your files were merged into some big gooey backup series, there's a HUGE chance you would lose your backup from the very beginning.  Plus 100 DVDs could cost anywhere between $20 to $50...for a single backup.

For less than $150, you could probably get a 750GB hard drive and an external hard drive enclosure.  Make the backup, encrypt it, and take it to a friend's house or a bank's safety deposit box.  Get two hard drives...keep one at home, and one at the bank/friend's house.  Swap them back and forth every once in a while.

If you do decide to go the DVD route, don't use a fireproof safe.  They may not burn, but they'll melt and warp!
