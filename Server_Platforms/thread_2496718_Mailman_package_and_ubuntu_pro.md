### Post by ian-weisser on 2024-04-09
We are not Canonical employees and have no special insight into Pro.

The "Mailman" package was removed from Ubuntu after 20.04.
In Ubuntu 18.04 and 20.04, the package was in the Universe pocket of the Ubuntu repos.

Pro promises security updates to some (not all) Universe packages.
So maybe they will, maybe they won't.

The free tier of Pro does not include any further maintenance or support. (No bugfixes or version bumps)
There is no promise to free-tier customers that Canonical engineers will adopt the source code.

For an authoritative answer from the Pro product team... 
Paid tier customers should consult their service contact.
Free tier customers can ask at [https://discourse.ubuntu.com/t/ubuntu-pro-faq/34042](https://discourse.ubuntu.com/t/ubuntu-pro-faq/34042)
