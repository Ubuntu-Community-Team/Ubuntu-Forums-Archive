### Post by dorowa on 2009-02-04
Ok, as it clear from your config you have relay permitted from
MAIN_LOCAL_DOMAINS=@:localhost:localhost:domain.do m
and you dont relay mails for other domains, which is really good :-)this is from
MAIN_RELAY_DOMAINS=
This means that you cant send any mails from other domains, including domain.com or webmail or any other...
if you want to relay (use your own mail server for sending messages from webmayl mailbox) you need just to allow relay for that domain and other domains you need:
MAIN_RELAY_DOMAINS=webmail.com
