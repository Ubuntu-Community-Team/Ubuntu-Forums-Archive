### Post by LHammonds on 2018-12-25
DDoS isn't just one type of attack and thus, you cannot use just one type of DDoS solution.  It really needs to be tailored to the service you are providing.

Here is some reading material on the subject: [https://www.netscout.com/what-is-ddos](https://www.netscout.com/what-is-ddos)

Trying to cover all types of attacks can be very expensive so it is best if you can narrow down the most-likely type of DDoS attack you can expect.
