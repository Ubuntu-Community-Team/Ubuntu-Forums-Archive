### Post by gottr on 2011-02-10
I am unsure what you are looking for exactly.  Each client will need some way to connect to the ltsp server, normally this is accomplished with a thin client of some sort.  A thin client can be a specific product marketed as a thin client, probably like the HP item you are looking at or it can be an old laptop or desktop that connects to the ltsp server via PXE or Etherboot.
