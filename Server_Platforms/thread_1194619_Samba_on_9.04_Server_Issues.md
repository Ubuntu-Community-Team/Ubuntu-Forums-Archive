### Post by dmizer on 2009-06-23
Remove the following options from the share definitions:
directory mask
create mask
force user
force group

These options were necessary in previous versions of samba but are now causing problems.

Edit, you should also be explicit with your path names.
while media/store2 may or may not be ok, you're much better off with /media/store2 the leading "/" is important.
