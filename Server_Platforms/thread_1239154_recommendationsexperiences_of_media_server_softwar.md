I recall having an issue with audio being out of sync too. At the time, I was running XBMC off a Win XP machine and assumed the problem was with the original video file. 

While playing a video, you can adjust the audio offset quite precisely. When I found a video that was out of sync, I could offset the audio ahead or behind the video by anywhere form 1/100 of a second up to 10 full seconds. (It's been a while, so I don't know how to do it, but it is doable). 

Now, if ALL your videos are out of sync, that's definitely something else causing the problem.
