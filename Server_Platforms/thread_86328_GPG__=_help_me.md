### Post by az on 2005-11-04
I have trouble with this too.

[QUOTE=HJThis]
4)To decrypt a message they have to use my public key[/QUOTE]
You will need your passphrase, the one you chose when you made your key.


[QUOTE=HJThis]5)Ok this is where i start to hit head on wall
what is a **signature key** or as i have seen some
members using **signature.asc** in some of there post
[/QUOTE]

Well, it is a signature.  It is a bit of encryption which proves that the possessor of your private/public key signed the document, that's all.  It's a form of authentification, but it is different than encrypting the whole message.
