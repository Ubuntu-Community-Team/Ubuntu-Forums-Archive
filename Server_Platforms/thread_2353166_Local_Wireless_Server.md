### Post by gordintoronto on 2017-02-20
The common way to do this is to have an Ethernet connection to the Internet, and use the computer's wireless to serve other computers.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/net-wireless-adhoc.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/net-wireless-adhoc.html)

You can probably to plug the router into your Ethernet port and use it to serve other computers, but that setup is above my pay grade.
