### Post by Disorder on 2006-03-05
Use Samba and FAT32 partitions. Should be easy enough. There are a few guides within the forums I suggest searching for one, and then asking a ton of questions once you get started. Unlike most forum you can actually ask questions here with out people telling you to google it. 

As far as streaming to a big screen goes, most of that is going to be hardware , and network dependent. You can eliminate the streaming all togther by placing the UServer in close proximity to the TV.

Otherwise your most likely going to have to place PC near the TV anyway, and stream from one to the other and configure the display to out put to the TV. 

Just a guess cause I am no means an expert with video, but any video that can properly be displayed on a big screen TV would have to huge and thus take a lot more space on your HD and hinder network performace if/while streaming.  I sure someone in your house plays WoW or CS and they're not gonna like insanly high pings. 

And it's probly much easier to do in windows.
