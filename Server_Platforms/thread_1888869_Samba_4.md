### Post by collisionystm on 2011-12-06
> **maverickaddicted said:**
> Thanks for the quick response. I am reading the guide, but I will not be able to do it practically before Christmas.

However, I didn't understand one thing 

"When you make your domain, just be sure to point your netbios / wins server in DHCP to the zentyal box"

Does this mean that I have to also make it as DHCP server?


No it does not need to be your DHCP server

It just needs to answer to WINS / NETBIOS requests 

Essentially the server will act as a domain controller and all domain information is transferred to windows clients via WINS / NETBIOS

So you change your DHCP config to include the zentyal box as your WINS / NETBIOS box and your xp machines will automatically know to look at that box for domain information

easy stuff, just don't think to far into it !!! :)
