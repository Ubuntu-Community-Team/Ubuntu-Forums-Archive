### Post by Iowan on 2010-02-09
I used FREESCO (a single-floppy router/firewall) as a dial-up router/firewall on a '486 until I upgraded to DSL a while back. There's a lot of options on it - it can also be configured as mail server, Samba server, etc. The 2.0 kernel is used to make it fit on a single 1.44M floppy.
