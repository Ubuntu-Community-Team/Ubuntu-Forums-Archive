### Post by georgerobbo on 2009-07-08
You will need to register a domain, you can do use through 123-reg. Once you have done that you need to point your domain to your server. As you have a dynamic IP address that will be difficult, so you would need to register for a premium account at DynDNS which would allow you to link your domain to your dynamic IP address. 
 
For what its worth your much better off buying your own domain/hosting package. I use [http://xilo.net](http://xilo.net) . Their base package gives your 5GB of bandwidth a month, CPANEL, unlimited subdomains, 2 MYSQL databases, PHP, Perl, 1GB hosting, unlimited email addresses. 
 
You should then just use your machine for developing your website and then upload the final product to your server.
