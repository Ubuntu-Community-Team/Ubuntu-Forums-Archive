### Post by PilotJLR on 2007-07-01
Just an FYI to those of you administering any web/email/ftp/etc server... check out Splunk:
[http://www.splunk.com/](http://www.splunk.com/)

It's a free download (up to 500MB per day indexing). It is like google for your log files... helps find problems' root causes.

It's a new company, but I've been using the free version for a little while, and it's pretty cool! Most people I've talked to haven't heard of it, so I wanted to spread the word.  :-)
