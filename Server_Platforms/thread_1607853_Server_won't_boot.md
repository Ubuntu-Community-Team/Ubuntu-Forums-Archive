No signal - Means the monitor is not getting any signal from your video card. Check your connections. If you have a onboard video card and an upgrade in your pci / pcie slot then make sure your connected to the right one. 

Reseat video card AND check to make sure you didnt bend any pins on the video cable connector (DB9).

Most every time you move equipment and have a problem its related to hardware issues.
