### Post by procras on 2006-11-30
Possible Hardware problem:

Fan noise from the lubrication running out and the bearings getting work.

Fan noise from some wires hitting off the blades.

I have heard squeeks and intermittent noises from my fans as they were
wearing out.

You can look into the case and see if any wires are too near the blades.

Try and disconnect one or two of the case fans to see if they are the culprit.
