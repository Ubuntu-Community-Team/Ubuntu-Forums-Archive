### Post by Ryan Dwyer on 2010-09-16
Check your logs to see why it's failing. I don't remember if bind has it's own log files or if it uses the syslog.

But anyway, you don't need both ice.7rocks.com and just ice. And putting the CNAME in there when it's already got an A record might be causing issues.

### Post by terazen on 2010-09-16
Everything Ryan said is right.  The errors in /var/log/syslog will tell you the problem when you restart bind.  Add "$TTL 72h" to the top of your 2 zone files and remove the cname and extra A record (you have ice.7rocks.com listed 3 times).

You can change the ttl to something other than 72h...I just threw that out there.
