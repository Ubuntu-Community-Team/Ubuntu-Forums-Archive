### Post by noway2 on 2010-01-15
You may be running into a problem where since your system is either a dynamic IP address and/or is not recognized as a legitimate mailing domain that your mail gets discarded.  Unfortunately, thanks to the spammers, unless your system is already on a whitelist your mail won't be accepted at the SMTP level.  If you have a static IP you can get added to the registry.  If you have a dynamic IP address, set your relay host to the SMTP of your service provider.  The mail will still show as originating from your domain, but it will pass to the world through their SMTP which will avoid this problem.
