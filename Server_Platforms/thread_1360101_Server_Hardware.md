### Post by tgalati4 on 2009-12-20
Do a search on Dell Optiplex and Dell Poweredge.  Compare the technologies between the two.  

Higher-end server machines are designed for 24/7 use.  Bigger power supplies, built-in RAID controllers, hot-swap drive chassis, higher density motherboards (8 layers vs 4 or 6 layers), Dual or Quad processors, higher throughput main bus, higher RAM capacity I/O chipset, the list goes on.
