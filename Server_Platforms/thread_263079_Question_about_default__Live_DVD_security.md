Most of what you're doing is completely unnecessary imo. It sounds like an awful lot of trouble to go to, just to browse a website.

The biggest contribution you can make to the security of your PC is to delete microsoft internet explorer. After that, killing windoze altogether is the next most useful step you can take. Beyond that, there's the usuals:

[LIST]
[*]Keep you passwords high-quality.
[*]Use SSL and actually _read_ the certification info.
[*]etc.
[/LIST]

Anything you do beyond that is sheer paranoia really, although checking critical system files for tampering and making sure you recognise all running processes wouldn't be completely crazy.
