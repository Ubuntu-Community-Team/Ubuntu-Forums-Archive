-QUOTE=Lars Noodén;13278455]Yes, you probably want the [server edition]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download/server") rather than a desktop flavor.

In contrast, 14.04 LTS is good through [April 2019]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Releases") and as a bonus has less experimental [systemd]("http://forums.debian.net/viewtopic.php?f=20&t=120652&start=0") material than the subsequent versions.[/QUOTE]
