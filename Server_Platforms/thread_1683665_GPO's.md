### Post by kakashi_12 on 2011-02-07
Group Policy Object.
They are applied to OU's (Organizational Units) in ADUC (Active Directory Users and Computers) on a Server.

In other words, it is a centralized admin console with various settings that allow you to control settings for all users or specific users/groups within a Domain.
