Free-libre is much better than free-as-in-beer.  Most freeware CMSes have add-on for-money features which you end up regretting you don't have.  Not a problem with a well-supported GPLed CMS.  Check out cmsmatrix.org

How you manage your database is a question for your hosting provider.  Phpadmin sets up the database and the CMS uses that database.  They have nothing to do with each other directly.  If you are setting this up on a box in your basement, you will have to set up phpadmin as well as your cms.
