### Post by Maggot on 2006-07-10
You can either stream it or download from gnump3d.  They select what song(s) to listen too. It can select 20 to 100 random songs or they can make a custom list.

I used gnump3d for a couple years and its good but not completely secure. If someone knows the directory structure, I believe they can d/l your mp3s.

I just switched to ampache and like it.

Both are good.
