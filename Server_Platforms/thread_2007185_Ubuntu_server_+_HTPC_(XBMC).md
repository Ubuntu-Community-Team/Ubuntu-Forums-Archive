### Post by rubylaser on 2012-06-27
Personally, I would keep the two separate (I do in my own home).  A media server is typically louder (spinning disks) than an HTPC.  I have a couple fileservers and home that are in my basement, and I have (3) Openelec (XBMC) frontends on Shuttle XS35GT's with SSDs for all of my frontends.  I would definitely go the separate route if you can afford it.
