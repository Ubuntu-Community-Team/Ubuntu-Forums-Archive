### Post by hictio on 2009-03-24
Sorry, but I don't understand your post.
What do you mean by resolution? Are you running a Desktop Environment (like Gnome) or a  Window Manager on your Ubuntu Server? Or you mean the console resolution?

If you are running a plain vanilla Ubuntu Server, that is only a CLI, and no graphical environment, the best thing you can do if you want to have full screen console, is, for instance, login thru SSH to the Ubuntu Server box (using Terminal) and maximize that screen; you'll have a full screen console with a resolution that it will be hard to beat (or down right impossible) on the real life.
