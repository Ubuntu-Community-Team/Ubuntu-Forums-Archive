### Post by volkswagner on 2012-05-20
Try and see :P

As you likely know already, laptop hardware is not designed with 24x7 use in mind.  One good thing is you will have a UPS built in, if the battery still holds a charge.

One other thing to keep in mind is the small heatsink fins.  You may need to do maintenance to keep the fan/heatsink clear of debris.  I think blowing out with air monthly is good, because this may prevent the thick buildup (think clothes dryer lint trap) which often requires disassembly.

Other downsides are likely 5400RPM single drive so RAID is not easily accomplished without some sort of USB arrangement or and add on PCMCIA SATA card or similar.

I think it will work just fine for a home setup.
