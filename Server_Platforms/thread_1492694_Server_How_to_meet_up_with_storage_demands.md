### Post by BoneKracker on 2010-05-25
Some alternatives are NAS, SAN, and external RAID arrays.

NAS is limited to the speed of your network, but if it meets your performance requirement, then that's the lowest-cost way to add large volumes of storage. If you need more speed but can't afford a fiber-channel or SCSI SAN, then an external SATA-2 RAID array may work for you.
