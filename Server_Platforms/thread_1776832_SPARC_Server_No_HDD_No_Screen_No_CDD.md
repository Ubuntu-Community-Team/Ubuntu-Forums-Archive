### Post by PeterSprague on 2011-06-29
All Sun hardware has tags with model & serial numbers.  Best to start there and figure out exactly what you have first.
Then download the appropriate manuals on-line and read them.
Sparc hardware is completely different than Intel/AMD based servers
Read up on OpenBoot.

From your post I can't tell if you even have a hard drive or cd/dvd.  Figure out all your hardware first.

This will not likely be a plug & play install.  Part of the learning curve.

Attached is a cookbook I recorded for installing Ubuntu to my Sparc workstation.

Hope that helps point you in the right direction.
