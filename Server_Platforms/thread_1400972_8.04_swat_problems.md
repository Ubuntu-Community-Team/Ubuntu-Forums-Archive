### Post by gmargo on 2010-02-09
You have to either start the xinetd daemon manually or reboot.  The xinetd installation tries to start, but it said "superserver not started" because nothing was configured to run, so it "helps" you by not starting.  And right after that, swat installs and gives xinetd something to run.
