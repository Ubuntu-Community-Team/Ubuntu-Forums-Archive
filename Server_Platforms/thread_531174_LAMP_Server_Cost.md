### Post by rickyjones on 2007-08-21
Personally I always charge for the hardware and time.

If the hardware costs me $500 then I add a 5% fee on top of that for handling, etc...

I then charge for my time. This can range from $45/hour to $65/hour depending on the customer and the work involved. One hour minimum. Time and a half after 9pm and on weekends.

### Post by southernman on 2007-08-21
I've seen prices ranging from 1-3 hundred bucks to setup the server. You can't really ask what others are charging for the same services... it's technically against the law to do so. Though it happens all the time... ;)

Figure out what your time is worth to you. Set a minimum charge and an hourly rate. You could either go the flat rate fee, or opt to charge an hourly fee. I suggest the hourly route. YMMV

If your going to sell hardware also, a good rule of thumb for charge back to the client is cost x 1.30. Adjust to make your accountant and tax man happy... then you'll be happy too.
