### Post by SeijiSensei on 2016-10-31
Free means no support and no reliability.  That's not what I would choose if I'm running a production server.  I use [Linode]("http://www.linode.com/") and pay $10/month for a solid VM, excellent technical support, big pipes, and a well-managed hosting facility.  You can choose among half-a-dozen Linux flavors including Ubuntu, Debian, and CentOS.  I pay a couple more dollars a month for nightly snapshot backups in case disaster strikes.

If you run a server for less than a month, you'll get the remainder credited back to your credit card, too.  I've put up and tore down servers in the same day and paid essentially nothing for them.
