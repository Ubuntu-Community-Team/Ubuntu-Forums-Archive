### Post by andguent on 2008-06-12
Depending on your needs and desires for the TV out, you should at least be aware of MythTV and its associated distro spawns: Mythbuntu, KnoppMyth, and probably more. 

Do note that the older versions of KnoppMyth really were based on Knoppix, and so upgrading packages gets weird very quickly. The newer version I believe is debian based.

Do also note that MythTV and its associated projects can keep you busy for days if not months. It isn't known for being easy to setup.
