### Post by nerdtron on 2014-04-06
Zimbra is good but very heavy on resources.
We've been using Roundcube. Feature rich and quite good looking. Still, you can stress the server when many users are logged in the web mail and doing searches.
Anyway email clients like Thunderbird is what I recommend.

EDIT: Just tried the live demo of Rain Loop (which looks like yahoo mail) and After Logic.
I think it just a matter of preference on which interface you look and stability and support of each interface.
