### Post by BkkBonanza on 2010-01-03
I've used several VPS services over the last few years. Probably the most reliable and best of the lot was KnownHost, though not the cheapest they are very good.

I have also used CheapVPS.co.uk and their related companies vaserv, fsckvps. They are fine too and cheaper, though I'd place them a step below Knownhost. I've used a couple others I wouldn't recommend so much. The main issue for me has been that some will over sell their resources and so you can end up with crappy performance. This isn't usually a problem at first but as they continue to fill the machine you're on it gets more degraded. Both the ones I mention here didn't have this problem but some others did.

A very good place to do research on VPS and other things hosting related is [http://www.webhostingtalk.com](http://www.webhostingtalk.com) forums. Lots of good info there and well worth spending some time on before signing up for any hosting.

I think vpslink is likely a good low price provider as well. They are run by Spry.com and I used to use their VPS hosting before they started vpslink. I found that over 6 months it got slower and slower until I gave up and moved over to KnownHost because even though I complained to them they never improved it. There are a lot of decent places and some bad ones. 

Any place you are thinking about I would type their name into a search on webhostingtalk and see what comments and reviews you find. I have done this and if you get glowing reports or even solid feedback with the odd complaint then that is a good thing. Mostly you want to hear about how their support deals with issues because you will have a few and if the support is solid then they take care of them quickly.
