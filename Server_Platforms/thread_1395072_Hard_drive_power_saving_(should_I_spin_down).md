### Post by gobbledigook on 2010-01-31
hi,

there's a discussion about this [_**here**_]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1000058&highlight=Hard+drive+power+saving") which discusses some options and also raises the point that it can shorten the life of the disks to have them spinning up and down often. The point is that when they spin they heat up... when they spin down they cool down, the temperature variation can cause more wear.
