### Post by cmcgeecc on 2014-12-21
You can do internet connection sharing very easily through the Graphical Interface.  All you have to do is go to network connections.  Then click add connection.  Then choose which device you want to share your internet to.  Then change the ipv4 settings to share with other computer.  And you're done.  Here is a youtube video that someone made to show you exactly that. [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZK-JHfJqmkY](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZK-JHfJqmkY)  Hope this helps someone.  I was looking for hours and finally found this.
