### Post by MartinG on 2006-02-02
There's no "should" about it.  Your present setup is safe, but some people are paranoid and like the extra layer, even if it's really just a "comfort blanket" (I include myself in this:) )

Ubuntu comes with iptables installed, and Firestarter is the easiest front end, IMHO.  Have a look at the online documentation and decide:
[http://www.fs-security.com/docs.php](http://www.fs-security.com/docs.php)
