### Post by windependence on 2008-08-23
Hehe, it's not SUPPOSED to be visible. If you mean you are in the GUI (God forbid) and you can't see it, all you have to do is turn on the option to show hidden files in your file manager. From the command line, you can do an:

ls -la

You really shouldn't have your files at 777. 755 is usually normal for a web server.
