### Post by BkkBonanza on 2011-06-13
Why don't you just sign up for Google apps on your new domain. Then you add the google mail servers to your DNS entries and all your domain email goes thru gmail. You can config that to forward to your personal gmail if you like on the control panel there but it also supports email clients via pop3/imap/smtp directly. I've done this in the past for many domains I own and it works well. No need to run your own mail server for your domain then, and they have multiple redundant servers.

[http://www.google.com/support/a/](http://www.google.com/support/a/)

It's free. I always disable and not used the other apps except email.
