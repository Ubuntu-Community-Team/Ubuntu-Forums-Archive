### Post by SeijiSensei on 2013-07-18
This really isn't an Ubuntu question, but ...

You can [submit sites to Google]("http://www.google.com/webmasters/") as well.  I do this for new sites I build in order to force the spider to index the site as soon as possible.  Otherwise Google finds sites by following links on other sites.  They might also follow new domain registrations, but I don't know if that is the case.  A site that isn't registered with Google and not linked to anywhere on the Internet will take a while to appear in the index.
