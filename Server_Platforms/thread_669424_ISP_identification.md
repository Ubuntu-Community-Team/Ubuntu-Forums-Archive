### Post by cdenley on 2008-01-16
By ISP, do you mean IP address? Is it a LAN IP assigned by your router? Your router administration page shouldn't be accessible from the internet, or the wireless network (especially unencrypted or WEP). You're probably not going to be able to find the actual user, maybe their ISP (internet service provider). I suggest configuring your router.
