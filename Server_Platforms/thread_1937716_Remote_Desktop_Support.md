### Post by Sonsum on 2012-03-08
I believe the included Gnome "Remote Desktop" application works quite well. It is a VNC client, so that would work with any OS, allowing them to connect and see your screen.

It also includes SSH, VNC, and RDP for connecting to other shares, so you could easily connect to another Windows or Linux system.
