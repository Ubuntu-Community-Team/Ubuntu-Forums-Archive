### Post by HermanAB on 2009-03-21
Hmm, Id say get two drives and make a periodic backup from the one to the other (RAID1 is *not* a backup system).  Buy a UPS to improve reliability.  

In my experience, most small computer problems are caused by bad power quality, since the difference in relibility between a PC on a UPS and one that isn't is very marked.
