### Post by scorp123 on 2009-10-15
Totally depends on the situation. Sometimes I get a few PC's and I can play with those. 

Sometimes I can snatch a brand-new server that just arrived and use it for my own tests. Such situations can arise if the guy who is supposed to take over that server isn't around (e.g. holidays). So until he's back I get to play with the equipment he ordered. For as long as I give the equipment back exactly when it's needed nobody has a problem with it.

Also: We have a few customers who even insist that we unpack their equipment and give it a thorough test-drive before we ship it to them. So that's even better. They order and pay for all the fantastic equipment and I get to play with it for a few days (or even weeks) before we put everything back into the boxes and have their stuff shipped to them.

One of my customers has ordered a 4 socket six-core server ... so that's 4*6 = 24 CPU cores. Can't wait to lay my hands on that thing :D

(I imagine 3D shooters will run tip top on that beast ... ?  :D  )
