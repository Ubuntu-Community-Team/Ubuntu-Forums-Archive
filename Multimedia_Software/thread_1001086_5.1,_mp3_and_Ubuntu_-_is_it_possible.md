### Post by element_G on 2008-12-03
Depends on what 5.1 system you are buying. 

If you are getting a home surround sound reciever, then it will most likely have Dolby Pro Logic II which is quite capable of turning stereo into 5.1 (even for movies). This is what I use at home.

If you are buying a 5.1 speaker set for your PC and are hooking the speakers directly to the sound card, things are a bit more involved, but definitely possible with Pulse Audio 
see this thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=795525](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=795525)

hope that helps
