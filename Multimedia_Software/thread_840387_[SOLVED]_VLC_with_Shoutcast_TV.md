### Post by markbuntu on 2008-06-25
Start vlc. Go to Playlist/Manage/Services Disvovery and check Shoutcast TV. vlc will then find the shoutcast tv stations and you can click on Shoutcast TV to see the list.

If you have the url you can just add it in Playlist/Manage/Add URL/Network and click HTTP/HTTP/FTP/MMS and insert the url. There is also the vlc plugins for firefox and you can set them up in Preferences/Applications.
