Just confirming that i have got iPhone 4 working with version 4.3 in xubuntu.

I have tried using it in ubuntu 11.04 with no success, the latest libgpod (version 8 ), libimobiledevice, etc.

I ended up having the success in xubuntu, installing 10.10 and simply upgrading the packages libgpod, libimobiledevice, etc.

very strange... it only seems to work in this distro.
apart from upgrading packages i also changed my DBversion from 4 to 2 within System/Library/Lockdown/Checkpoints.xml within the iphone itself.
