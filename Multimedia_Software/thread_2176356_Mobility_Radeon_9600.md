### Post by QIII on 2013-09-24
Hi!

I wouldn't get too wrapped around the axle about having the proprietary driver.  That's the driver that won't work.

The default open source Radeon driver installed initially with Ubuntu works just fine for the vast majority of users.  You would probably be just fine with that.

Since the specs you give don't seem to offer much for hard-core gaming, just go with the default driver.


Best wishes!
