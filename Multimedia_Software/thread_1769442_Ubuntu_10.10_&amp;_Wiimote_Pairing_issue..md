### Post by Rhubarb on 2011-10-23
Hi,

There is currently no need to pair with a wiimote in Ubuntu.
All connections to it can be made without pairing.

The only case for pairing it I've seen in Linux is a very recent effort by David Herrmann [here]("https://github.com/dvdhrm/xwiimote") - which is so new it doesn't exist in (m/)any distributions including Ubuntu.

[This]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CWiiD") or [this]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=993376") may assist you in getting your wiimote working in Ubuntu.
- I haven't tested this with Ubuntu 11.04 onwards though, so I could be wrong.
