### Post by seanksg on 2008-11-23
Is there a way to stop Banshee Media Player from auto-syncing my library to my iPod?

---

### Post by infinitelink on 2009-11-12
Having been a year unanswered, though the problem is solved, I want to answer in case anyone else stumbles here with this same question: when your iPod is mounted in Linux (usually it will auto-mount, if not click the iPod symbol on your desktop with the right mouse button and choose the mount option), in Banshee one selects the iPod (which appears shortly after the iPod is mounted: Banshee may also be set to auto-start once an iPod is mounted on the system) in the left pane: just click on it. The interface will display an iPod icon and preferences related to it in the large main pane; there one may select or deselect (by checking the box to the left of the description) 'Manually manage this device', and if deselected one may select or deselect (another checkbox) 'Automatically sync the device when plugged in or when the libraries change', which option deselected will mean Banshee won't auto-sync your music library.
