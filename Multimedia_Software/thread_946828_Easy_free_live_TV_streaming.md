Free to watch live TV streaming.

These all use your flash player. 
 
These 2 seem mostly lag free.

[http://beta.tidaltv.com/](http://beta.tidaltv.com/)

[http://www.americafree.tv/](http://www.americafree.tv/)

Mogulus has 1000's of channels but sometimes lag's.

"Mogulus is totally free. We simply insert a short ad onto your channel every 10 minutes or so. Your channel is free for everyone to see on Mogulus, or embedded elsewhere on the Internet. There is no software to install,"
Note. When you find a channel you like Book mark it to make it easer to find.

[http://www.mogulus.com/](http://www.mogulus.com/)

[http://www.mogulus.com/guide/categories?cid=&lid=&count=](http://www.mogulus.com/guide/categories?cid=&lid=&count=)

If your new to Ubuntu and can not get these channels you simply need to goto
Applications>Add/Remove...>  search for macromedia  and install Macromedia Flash plugin.
