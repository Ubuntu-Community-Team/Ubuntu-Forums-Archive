### Post by lifestream on 2008-11-03
On your tray, double click on sound icon to open the utility.
You should see:
Master, PCM, etc...
Check if PCM is all the way up. If not,put it all the way up. Then put master halfway the bar.
Check if BOTH bars on PCM are up.

Sounds silly, but it fixed it for me  O___O I kept putting "master" up, and no sound, because PCM was all the way down! How was I supposed to know?:P
