Was plug and play using Lubuntu 18.04 (32 bit) on my old Acer TravelMate 4060 laptop and guvcvideo. The leads aren't long enough for desktop usage though.


I also bought this one for my Dell Optiplex 7010 desktop with Ubuntu 20.04 (looks to be out of stock)


[https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B087L6NSXK/ref=pe_2515581_431334481_fb_remail_prodpg](https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B087L6NSXK/ref=pe_2515581_431334481_fb_remail_prodpg)


Wasn't so plug and play and had a few crashes initially - maybe I didn't have all the right video codecs installed as it seems better now since I installed the ubuntu-restricted-addons and ffmpeg. Cheese video was choppy so I gave up on it and tried to use guvcview which was a very green screen initially. Couldn't understand what was going on but changing the video codec under Video - Video Codec to anything else than the initial setting in guvcview seemed to snap it out of its malaise. Zoom and Google Meet were fine. The camera audio seems to get muted sometimes especially if I fiddle too much with the settings under Settings - Sound - Input on the top menu, but playing with the mic volume control in the same menu seems to reset it.
