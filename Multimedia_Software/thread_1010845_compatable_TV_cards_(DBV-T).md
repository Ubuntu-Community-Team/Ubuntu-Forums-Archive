### Post by pseudonym on 2008-12-14
I can highly recommend the [AverMedia DVB-T USB 2.0]("http://www.avermedia.com/avertv/Product/ProductDetail.aspx?Id=163&device=1"). It's not on your list but is somewhere around that price range. It does both SD and HDTV and performs really well.

The advantages of this over solutions? -

* It's marketed as Linux-compatible.

* A tuner connected via USB is less subject to the kind of interference that a PCI card is, sitting inside the computer.

* Being USB, you can connect it to more than one computer eg a laptop.
