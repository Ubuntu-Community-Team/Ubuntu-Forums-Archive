### Post by ddraper2 on 2013-12-02
The screenshot indicates that it is trying to send audio to the HDMI sound device. This would be the speakers in your monitor. If there are no speakers in your monitor that could be the problem and then you need to reset the default output device to your external speakers. This can be done use Pulse Audio Volume control.
