### Post by echotech2 on 2016-01-29
If your DVD drive supports Dual-Layer DVD's they can hold up to 8.5 Gb's.

  FWIW, I have used k3b extensively to burn old home movies to DVD.  (A friend had the equipment and knowledge to take them from VHS tapes to an ISO.  I then used k3b to burn the ISO.)  I've had no problems and they all turned out great.
