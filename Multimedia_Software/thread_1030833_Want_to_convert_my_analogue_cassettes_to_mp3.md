### Post by snapback77 on 2009-01-04
I use Audacity a free aplication with a good owners manual from the website although it took me a while get it.
You can download Audacity. Click Applications and Add/Remove to get it.  Use a cord with 2 RCA plugs on one end and an eighth inch stereo plug on the other end. Plug it into "line-in" jack on the back of your computer and have fun.
