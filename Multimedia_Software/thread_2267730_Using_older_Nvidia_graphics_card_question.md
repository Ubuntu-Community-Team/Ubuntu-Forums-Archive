### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2015-03-04
The Quadro NVS 290 is based on a G86 core, so it is roughly equivalent to the first revision of the 8400GS. Off the top of my head, I can't remember what proprietary nvidia driver versions were available in Precise/12.04, but I'm pretty sure it has at least the Nvidia 304.xx driver series, which should work. I believe Ubuntu's driver dialog will offer it automatically, but don't quote me on that.
