### Post by cariboo on 2008-08-04
If you are talking about recording streaming music, give streamtuner a try. You will also have to install streamripper and either have audacious or vlc installed to listen to the output. I record streaming music quite often using the above mentioned programs. Streamtuner, streamripper and audacious are all available in the repositories and You can use Add/Remove Programs, Synaptic Package Manger or the command line to install them.

You will also have to go into View-->Preferences and change xmms %q to your prefered music player.
