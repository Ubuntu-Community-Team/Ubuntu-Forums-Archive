### Post by neu2buntu on 2010-08-22
try double left mouse click in the audio-file-processor in the black space above the 16 steps. or alternatively right click here and choose "open in piano roll"

### Post by neu2buntu on 2010-08-24
no problemo, and if you want to add a sample in the sample track create a block (left click) and then double left click this block and navigate to any .ogg or .wav sample regardless of the sample size as it will auto expand :)
