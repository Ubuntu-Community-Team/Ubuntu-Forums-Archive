### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-07-29
I'm running Ubuntu 8.04 amd64 with the M-Audio Revo 5.1. It's a great card for analog output when used with OSS4 (disable vmix for the best sound quality).

If you're interested in the bluegears card, get it while you can. C-Media cut a deal with Asus, so it now has exclusive rights to CMI-8788 chipsets: [http://www.techreport.com/discussions.x/15206](http://www.techreport.com/discussions.x/15206)
Who knows how many chips bgears has in their inventory...
