### Post by Da CalebMan on 2010-01-07
My sister bought the highlander mp4 player, she asked me if I could download some songs on it, it was incredibly easy too! all you have to do, is drag and drop files onto the device, which is detected easily by linux. It is also supported by banshee, comes with an mp3 converter on a tiny little disc, and also has a little onboard speaker! The only thing I didn't like about it was, it had a slow interface, like an old mobile phone, and the controls were difficult to use. I don't know if this mp3 player supports rockbox firmware it would be fun to try, but I don't think it would work.

All in all, I'd give it a 'B.' It's not the incredible ipod nano type mp3 player with cool effects and features, but it does what it says, and if that's all you want it's a cheap choice.
