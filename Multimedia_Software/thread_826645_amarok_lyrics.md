### Post by aquavitae on 2008-06-12
You have to run a lyrics script: go to "scripts" in the amarok menus (not at linux right now so I can't check exactly where it is) and it should open up a window with a list of scripts. Click on "lyrc" (I think!) and then the "run script" button.  In the amarok main window, click on the context browser in the sidebar on the left, and then on the "lyrics" tab. That should show the lyrics.
