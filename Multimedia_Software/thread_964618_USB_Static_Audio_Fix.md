### Post by mrgstiffler on 2008-10-31
So I was just about to post a thread asking for help but I had an idea and fixed my issue.

The problem:
My Creative X-Fi 5.1 USB was recognized just fine but whenever there's sound, there's overhead static. A halfsecond or so before the sound starts there's static and it stays "staticy" until a halfsecond after the sound stops. Adjusting PCM Volume affects the volume of the sounds being played but the static stays the same.

The fix:
I remembered that it's going through a USB hub and on a whim I decided to connect it directly to my computer. It worked! Audio now plays 100% crisp.

Hopefully this helps someone else. If you are using a USB audio device, DO NOT USE IT WITH A USB HUB.
