### Post by KevinJaeger on 2011-07-24
I built my first one with an ancient PC that was retired from service - a 600 MHz Celeron with 192MB of memory, with just the addition of a PVR-350 capture card.  Cost me a total of about $80.

It eventually died last year at about 12 years old, after serving as a Myth box the last 5 years of its life.

I've now upgraded to an HD TV and upgraded my Myth box to something current - a nice HTPC case with a near-silent CPU cooler and HD capable graphics and digital sound capability.  It cost maybe $600-$700 all in.

See an example here: [http://www.linuxjournal.com/magazine/build-mythtv-box-without-breaking-bank]("http://www.linuxjournal.com/magazine/build-mythtv-box-without-breaking-bank")
