### Post by cbowman57 on 2011-03-14
Have you installed the ubuntu-restricted files? I can't remember exactly but I think most of your codecs will be installed with that.

The url you used was for the site itself, not an individual radio station.  Click on their shoutcast link and locate the radio station you want, right-click copy address, then add it to the radiotray configuration.  The entry should look something like this: [http://yp.shoutcast.com/sbin/tunein-station.pls?id=1116397](http://yp.shoutcast.com/sbin/tunein-station.pls?id=1116397)

IIRC they should all be the same except for the id=number at the end.
