### Post by indiworks on 2008-07-17
The best open-source NLE is the Blender Video Sequence Editor: [http://wiki.blender.org/index.php/Manual/Using_VSE](http://wiki.blender.org/index.php/Manual/Using_VSE)

You need Blender 2.46 for all the latest VSE features: [http://www.blender.org/development/release-logs/blender-246/sequencer-changes/](http://www.blender.org/development/release-logs/blender-246/sequencer-changes/)

Once in the VSE: Space (to add an effect) > Speed Control...
