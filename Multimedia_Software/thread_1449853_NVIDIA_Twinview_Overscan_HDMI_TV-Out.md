### Post by pkohler01 on 2011-05-14
There are two things you can do - the first is to make sure your Sharp TV's "view mode" is set to dot-by-dot. The second is to check the settings in the Nvidia control panel:
- finding the tv's name in the left hand column (under GPU 0 on my system), mtry the different scaling options. I found that aspect ratio scaling works best for me.
