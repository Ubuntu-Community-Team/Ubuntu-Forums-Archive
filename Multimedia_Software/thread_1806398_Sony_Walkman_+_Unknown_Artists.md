### Post by darkmakozu on 2011-07-17
I had a similar problem with a friends mp3 player ages ago. Not matter what software I used in linux to re-tag the songs, it would just not stick. Yet tagging in windows seemed to work perfectly. I did a little research online and it seems that windows uses an outdated ID3 tag standard that many mp3 players have adopted and therefore will not recognise anything newer. I was unable to find a useful solution other then having to re-tag them in windows but I hope this points you in the right direction.
