Some video editors can extract a JPG from a video file, but it'd be nothing more than a still image...like a screenshot of the video at that point in time.

### Post by howefield on 2010-03-06
Devede will take your avi and convert it to an iso which you can burn to a DVD.

Devede is in the repository, so can be installed with Synaptic Package Manager.

System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager.
