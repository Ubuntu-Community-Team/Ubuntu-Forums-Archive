### Post by robbmyers on 2013-02-10
[FONT="Trebuchet MS"][/FONT]
In case anyone sees this thread to this point and is frustrated that there is no solution (as I was), here is the solution.
On the Video and Files tab, you have to click the "open" to the right of the image and video sections (the side, NOT the bottom).  Save capture.avi in the folder you want.
NOW, you can start capturing and saving.  
First, click capture to start and click it to finish, then click save.  I haven't quite figured out the naming, but stumble around and it will work.
Then, open the folder and you can play that .avi file that you just saved.  
Bonus:  I have been playing with Pitivi as a video editor.  Very early into it, but already it seems to be a natural fit.
Good luck!
