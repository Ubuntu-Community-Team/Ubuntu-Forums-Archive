### Post by punx45 on 2009-02-25
if i remember correctly, both ATSC and QAM are transmitted in MPEG2 transport streams [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MPEG_transport_stream](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MPEG_transport_stream)
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atsc](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atsc)
[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quadrature_amplitude_modulation](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quadrature_amplitude_modulation)

it looks like the card just grabs that stream and passes it to the pc.   that device says it plays mpeg2, but it doesnt list m2t or ts as a playable file extension, so i would say it is possible it wont play the files without transcoding.
