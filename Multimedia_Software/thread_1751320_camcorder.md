### Post by fballem on 2011-05-06
Two questions come to mind:

1/ What happens if you plug it in? If I understand correctly, it may work like most digital cameras and be treated as if it were an external hard drive. You might then be able to use Nautilus to browse and copy or move the files from the camera to your computer.

2/ The other option is to remove the flash card from the camera and plug it into a suitable slot on your computer. It should be recognized as a hard drive and you can use Nautilus to copy or move the files to your computer.

Just a couple of thoughts. Hope they help,
