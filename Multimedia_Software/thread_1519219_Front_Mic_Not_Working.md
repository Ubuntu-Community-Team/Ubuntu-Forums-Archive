### Post by wesleybishop on 2010-06-27
Problem: When I plugged a microphone into the front microphone port on the computer it would not work.

Solution: 
1. Go to Preferences 

2. Click Sound 

3. Click on Hardware

4. Click on Internal Audio

5. Under the drop down menu for Settings for the selected device: Select Digital Stereo (IEC958) Output + Analog Stereo Input.

6. Click on input tab under the connector drop down menu select Analog Microphone / Microphone 1

7. UN-mute Microphone and adjust volume from the input tab.
