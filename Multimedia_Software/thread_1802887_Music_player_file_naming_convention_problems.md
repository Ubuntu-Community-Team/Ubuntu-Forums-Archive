### Post by Man_Beach on 2011-07-12
All of the albums in my music directory are stored in separate folders with the format: 

Artist - Title

The individual tracks are then stored by track number in the format:

track number - title.flac

So, to take an example, Peter Frampton's classic Frampton Comes Alive! is in a folder called 

Frampton, Peter - Frampton Comes Alive!

Within that are the tracks

01 - Somethings happening.flac
02 - Doobie Wah.flac
03 - Show me the way.flac
etc.

So, what I want is a music player which understands this naming convention and shows it, rather than reading the underlying metadata.
Currently, GMPc shows that I have 6 albums, one of them called unknown by unknown with thousands of tracks on it lasting over 6 days, and all the other media players which I've looked act in a similar way.
I don't have to use GMPC - but as all of my music is in this directory and I don't stream music or require access to last.fm etc., most other option seem to be too complicated.
