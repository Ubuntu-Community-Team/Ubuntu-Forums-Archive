### Post by BeRA on 2010-04-26
Hmmm... strange... did you mute Rhythmbox by accident?

Open any song with Rhythmbox (let it play) and then System>Prefereces>Sound>Applications tab (increase volume and uncheck Mute if checked). Hope this helps...
