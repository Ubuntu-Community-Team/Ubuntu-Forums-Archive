### Post by radio_listener on 2009-01-13
Probably you won't need any advice on this issue anymore since Christmas is long gone by but this is one of the first hits on Goolge for "itunes university ubuntu" so I will share my thoughts.

Apple high-jacked many podcast with there non-standard rss. That is indeed a shame.
The universities need to offer their podcasts as standard rss-feeds as Berkeley for example.
[http://itunes.stanford.edu/faq.html](http://itunes.stanford.edu/faq.html) states that they are planning to offer standard rss. To accelerate the process you could try to enquire them to offer standard rss-feeds.

A workaround would be to use wine or crossover office to run itunes. I suppose that the podcast-feature should work with an older and well tested itunes version.
