### Post by tomwbro on 2013-06-17
Well, the whole reason I started this is because my old TV died.  All I wanted was a replacement, but I didn't want to buy one if I could get the TV tuner on my Linux box to work.  After fighting with firmware and MythTV for a week, I just looked at the back of my cable box and, lo and behold, it has an HDMI output!  So, my old HDMI computer monitor is now my new HDTV.
