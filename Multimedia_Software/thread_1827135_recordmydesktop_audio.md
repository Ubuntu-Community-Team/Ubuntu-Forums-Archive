### Post by kvarley on 2011-08-17
It may be easier and better for you to grab the source of the flash player applet rather than trying to capture it yourself. There is a great addon for Firefox which can do this called Video Download Helper.

gotbletu explains how to record the speakers output on this 2 part video: [http://youtu.be/QapKpjv-w24]("http://youtu.be/QapKpjv-w24") You may also want to look up Pulse Audio Volume Control in the Ubuntu Software Center as that will enable you to achieve the same results.

However, if this doesn't work you can record the audio which your speakers play with an application called OutRec - [http://outrec.sourceforge.net]("http://outrec.sourceforge.net/") Then afterwards you would have to attach this audio to the video. This method is time consuming and not recommended.
