### Post by blm-ubunet on 2015-07-02
AFAIU there is no special secret sauce with VLC, they have an out-dated list of mkb keys (up to aacs 28?) & a list of BDs VUKs. The people who find/have this info have rightly gone to ground.
Neither of these lists/key files are very helpful for any recent BDs anyway. They should work with old BDs if your drive has not been revoked.

You can use makemkv's "libmmbd" library as a symlink replacement for libblueray & libaacs. This makes makemkv work seamlessly with MythTV or any media player.
There's nothing special about types of codecs utilized in BDs.
