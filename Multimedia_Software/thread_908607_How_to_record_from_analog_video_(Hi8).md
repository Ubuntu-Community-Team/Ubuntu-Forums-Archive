### Post by lovinglinux on 2008-09-02
You need a capture card with S-video or Composite inputs. I guess even those cheap analog cards have at least Composite. Then you will need a software to record the input from the camera. 

I have a pinnacle PCTV Stereo card (really cheap) with Antenna input, Composite input and S-Video input. With the antenna input I can tune TV stations and with the composite input I can capture from a camera or VHS. S-Video never worked really well for me.

### Post by Erlander on 2008-09-03
If you also have a digital video camera with analogue in then you can use it to transcode from analogue to digital and capture via firewire using programs such as Kino.  Leastways I think Kino will capture in this situation.

Another option is a Digital8 camera that will play Hi8 and output via firewire
