### Post by little_penguin on 2011-11-09
While you have a youtube video or online audio file currently playing, check the following:

1) Make sure the Volume icon at the bottom of the youtube video itself is at maximum (bottom left of the video).

2) On the gnome panel, click the Volume icon -> Sound Preferences-> Applications tab and make sure the slider for the currently playing app is at maximum.
