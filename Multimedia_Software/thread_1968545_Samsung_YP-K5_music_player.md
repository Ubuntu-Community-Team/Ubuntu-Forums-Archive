Hello,

Just to let know the Samsung YP-K5 users (Ubuntu Lucid Lynx 10.04 LTS), 
ignore the Samsung YP-K5 shortcut on the desktop when connecting the device. Go instead in Rhythmbox program > Edit menu > Plugins menu > Portable Players - MTP entry and make sure it is ticked on. Within Rhythmbox, it is then possible to transfer songs and see them on the device after ejecting it safely (right click)...
