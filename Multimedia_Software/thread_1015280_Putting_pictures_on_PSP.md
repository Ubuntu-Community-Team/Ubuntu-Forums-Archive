### Post by Killeroid on 2008-12-19
The files are not immediately written to the psp memory card. Before you unplug the psp usb, make sure you right click on the psp icon on the desktop and select unmount volume (or in nautilus, you can click on "eject" for the psp(its in the left side pane, the eject button next to the entry for your psp). It forces the files to be written to the psp. After you unmount, you can end the psp connection from the psp.
