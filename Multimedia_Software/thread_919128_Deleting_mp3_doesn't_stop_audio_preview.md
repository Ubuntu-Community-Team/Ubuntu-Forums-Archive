### Post by manolomanolo on 2008-09-13
Hi.

I wanted to delete two mp3 so I selected the first one, then pressed CTRL and selected the second one too. Meanwhile the audio preview of one of them started and it lasted for a minute at least, even if I moved both mp3 into the thash and also emptied it.

I expected the preview to stop just as soon as I moved those files into the trash.

I realized, for example, the preview starts when moving your mouse over the icon of an mp3. If you leave your mouse on that icon for a little while and also select that icon, you can press the CANC (or DEL) key in order to move that file to the trash and still hear the mp3 playing. The preview stops when the buffered part is exhausted (I suppose) or when you move your mouse over ANOTHER multimedia file starting it's own preview.

Thanks for your attention.

### Post by OutOfReach on 2008-09-13
Yes, it is a known bug. A way to stop the preview is to move the file from the trash back into the original location.
