### Post by rrwo on 2009-09-05
Since upgrading to Jaunty, I've never gotten audio to work correctly in some applications. (Sound used to work fine in Intrepid.)

Banshee doesn't work at all, but it doesn't give an error message. It just stops playing.

Totem plays movies without sounds. No errors, but the Sound menu shows Volume Up/Down options greyed out.

Sound Juicer is unable to play CD, but gives an error saying that it "could not open audio device for playback".

Rhythmbox does work. However, it cannot read CDs.

VLC media player also plays movies with sound.  

Websites with Flash (e.g. Youtube and other video sites) work fine.

Any idea why the audio for some programs work and not others? This is driving me crazy. 


UPDATE: I am using Xfce, but have Gnome installed as well. I ran gnome-sound-properties and set the playback options to "Autodetect". That's fixed Totem and Banshee.
