### Post by Keith Fox on 2009-08-01
I came across a problem in Linux Mint, a distro of Ubuntu, when trying to upload photos to myspace, it would black out in flash and wouldn't let me upload.  So I searched and came across a fix for this problem.  First go to your Synaptic Package Manager.  And search for kubuntu-desktop .  Install that, and when it prompts to install recommended software, allow it.  And now that is fixed and you will be able to upload normally in flash multiple photos, just like you would be able to in IE on windows, or anything other browser.  Thanks and good luck to you flash myspacers.
