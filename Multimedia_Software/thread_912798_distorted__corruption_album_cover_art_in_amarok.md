there is a bug filed here, 

[http://bugs.kde.org/show_bug.cgi?id=151657](http://bugs.kde.org/show_bug.cgi?id=151657)

from that bug report there is a workaround to re embed the cover art using easytag with the older id3v2.3' tag which renders properly in amarok. those who want a quick fix now can re embed their artwork for the time being. otherwise it may be a while before amarok gets patched to render id3v2.4 properly.
