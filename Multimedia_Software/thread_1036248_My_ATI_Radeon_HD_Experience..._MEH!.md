### Post by vs8 on 2009-01-10
I have a Radeon HD 3200. It works well but I have some gripes against ATI. One of them is that whenever I want to watch a video I have to turn Compiz off because videos don't play well, they look very, very bad. Another gripe is that the card could handle Compiz like nothing, but no. If I'm listening to some music in Songbird, Compiz' framerate drops in a significant way (Wobbly Windows, Change Desktop with Desktop Wall etc.).

Another thing is the HDMI support. The other day I bought an HDMI cable just for the PC. When I plugged it in, the resolution was in 1080p so I went to the screen resolution window and changed it to 1680x1050 which is my native resolution. Everything changed except the GDM screen. I tried everything to put it to 1680x1050 again but no luck. If I reconfigure X it displays in the resolution I want, but whenever I reconfigure X the ATI proprietary driver gets deactivated so no Compiz for me. When I activate ATI's driver again I get compiz but no good GDM Resolution. I tried KDM, it displays in the correct resolution, but, I'm a Gnome user, I want to use GDM! Right?

I hope that ATI knows about these problems and are in their way to fix them.

Thank's for reading!
