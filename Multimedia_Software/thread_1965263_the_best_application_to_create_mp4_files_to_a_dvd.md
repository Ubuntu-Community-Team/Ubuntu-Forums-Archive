### Post by SeijiSensei on 2012-04-25
> **geordiejohn said:**
> i know i can use devede but i would have to change the bit rates it was recorded in

Perhaps the bit rates are too high to fit within the DVD standard.  [This thread](http://forum.videohelp.com/threads/284993-Maximum-Bitrate), for instance, suggests the maximum is 9.8 Mbit/sec.  

If that's the case there's little you can do other than re-encode them to lower rates.
