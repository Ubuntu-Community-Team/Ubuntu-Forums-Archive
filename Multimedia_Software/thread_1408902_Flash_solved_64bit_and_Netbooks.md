I have download Minitube for 64bit and its great, For netbooks you have to download Minitube from the old Getdeb web page and playback is smooth in full screen with GMA950. Minitube is for 9.04 & 9.10 only.

Netbooks   [http://old.getdeb.net/search.php?keywords=minitube](http://old.getdeb.net/search.php?keywords=minitube)

64 bit go here  [http://flavio.tordini.org/minitube](http://flavio.tordini.org/minitube)

Go to youtube, copy the title of whatever you want to watch and paste it in the Minitube search window, enter. Alt + enter button goes full screen and back to small screen.

Enjoy !:D
