### Post by sleepingdragon on 2008-10-26
+1 for DeVeDe, but it does take a little practice. The newest version supports menu making if you're inclined to provide chapter selection for a multi-part DVD. 

My advice for DeVeDe is to import the videos in their correct order. I've had problems in the past where I've made that simple mistake, and despite correcting it in DeVeDe, it still creates a DVD in the original wrong order (go figure...)
