### Post by evilsoup on 2012-05-05
Handbrake can do this, it's in the repositories.
It can only encode to MP4 and MKV, and only uses a small number of video encoders (x.264, and mpeg-2 and Theora), which is a deliberate design choice.

It will automatically crop any black borders around the video, but if you want to set the crop manually you can, quite easily by clicking the 'picture settings' button.
