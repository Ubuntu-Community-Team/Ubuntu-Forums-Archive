### Post by goatgonads on 2010-04-21
After pulling my hair out, this ended up being a physical problem with the cable coming into my house. The signal strength was too low, new cable, all channels work!

### Post by lavinog on 2010-04-21
Thanks for the update.  It will be helpful information for others that have similar problems.

One thing you should pay attention to is the cable type.  RG-59 is not suitable for digital cable or internet.  Everything should be RG-6.
