### Post by markbuntu on 2008-10-11
Are you running in full screen?
Is compiz running?
Compiz uses indirect rendering with the ati driver. Unless you are running in full screen and have unredirectfullscreenwindows checked in the compiz settings, wine and WoW will run without direct rendering. You also might want to get the latest ati driver, 8,9 since the 8.6 driver you are using is the first linux driver with support for your agp card and improvements have been made.
