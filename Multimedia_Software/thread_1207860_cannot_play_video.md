### Post by lisati on 2009-07-08
Yes there is a plugin. From the Add/Remove menu, do a search for "flash" plugins.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/9.04/musicvideophotos/C/video.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/9.04/musicvideophotos/C/video.html) has links to some useful information.
