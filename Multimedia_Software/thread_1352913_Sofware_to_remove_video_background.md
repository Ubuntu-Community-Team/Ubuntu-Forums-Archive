### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-12-12
Your are looking for [chroma key](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chroma_key).

[Cinelerra](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cinelerra) has a [chroma key plugin](http://jcornet.free.fr/linux/chromakey.html).

Although is not a standalone video editor, [Blender](apt:blender) has video editing capabilities with features such chroma key. It's not easy to learn tho.
