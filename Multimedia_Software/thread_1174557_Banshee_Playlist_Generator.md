### Post by crumbs on 2010-01-09
I know this post is pretty old already, but in case you never figured it out here's my explanation.  First you need to scan your music library from tools - mirage playlist generator if it has not been scanned before.  find a song in your music library that you like and drag it onto the playlist generator on the left hand area.  The playlist generator then adds 5 other songs that should be similar to the one you dragged into the playlist generator.  The mirage site is here [http://hop.at/mirage/](http://hop.at/mirage/)
