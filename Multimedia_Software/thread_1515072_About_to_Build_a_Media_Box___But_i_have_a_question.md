### Post by coskierken on 2010-06-22
Any MB with the Intel chipset works well with HDMI playback, especially the Intel 4000 series HD.  Don't expect to play games at any decent frame rate, though.  If you intend to upgrade later to a better video card, remember Nvidia does not process HDMI audio on board the vid card as they do not have the audio processor for this.  They pass through the signal via a small cable from the motherboard SPDIF header, if the motherboard has one (important).  ATI on the hand has the audio processor on the video card for HDMI, but their drivers are not as well supported.   They do work well, though.  Hope this helps!):P
