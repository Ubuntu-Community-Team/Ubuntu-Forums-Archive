### Post by dj-toonz on 2009-10-25
Managed to fix the problem :). here's how I did it, Installed the pulse audio Device chooser, then went into Volume Control & Playback & the top & just ticked & un-ticked mute on the system Sounds (what's showing) then closed it, then went into the volume preferences for gnome-volume-control-pulse & everything is working again, phew (knew I forgot to install something else at the same time as gnome-volume-control-pulse) listening to online shoutcast stations with my bluetooth headphones :)
