### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-01-04
Use openshot to edit your video files as you want then try **devede** which will produce an iso file that you can burn as a DVD to later play in a normal DVD player.  If your video files do not need editing you can just use devede to produce the iso for burning direct from the video file or files.

I have used it a few times with no major problems
