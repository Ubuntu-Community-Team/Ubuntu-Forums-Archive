### Post by fazavon on 2009-01-01
Well this has been a fun three days.. I have now got the solution to syncing your iPod (any gen) with Banshee (I know some of you hate banshee, but it takes the cover art with it.. i don’t have to place it somewhere for it to pull over)

1. Install the newest version of Banshee
2. Install Sound converter (sudo apt-get install soundconverter)
3. Check, Double Check.. and for the love of sanity Triple check that all of your muzik is .mp3.. if it is not use Sound Converter to make sure it is..
4. Start Banshee then plug in your iPod (let banshee convert your database)
5. Set Banshee to “Manually Manage this device”
6. Add songs to banshee (change genera, Names, etc as you see fit)
7. Select Songs and drag to “iPod” on the banshee menu on the left..
8. Sit back and let it sync…

You will now have all of your fav muzik on your iPod with album art!!!

Enjoy
