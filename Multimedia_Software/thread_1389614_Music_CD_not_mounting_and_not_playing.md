### Post by mörgæs on 2010-01-25
The same goes for one of my computers (with 9.04). 

I found some kind of workaround. If I do the following

Install VLC
insert a music CD
open VLC
select media > open disk > music CD > track 1 > play
and wait for 15 minutes (!) 

the music begins. 

After that I can close and open VLC, and everything works.
