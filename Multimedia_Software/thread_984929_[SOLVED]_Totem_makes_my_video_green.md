### Post by tj.milligan on 2008-11-17
This exact problem used to happen to me in Totem. I used to have to do the following every so often:

Totem > Edit > Preferences > Display tab > Reset to Defaults.

If color is STILL not corrected, swing the Hue (above "Reset to Defaults") all the way to the right or left (can't remember, it magically "fixed" itself when I installed 8.10 intrepid). All other sliders should be in the exact center.
