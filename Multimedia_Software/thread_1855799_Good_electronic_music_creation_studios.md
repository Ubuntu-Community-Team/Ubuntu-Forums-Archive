### Post by shantiq on 2011-10-07
The perfect tool for this is **rosegarden** in your synaptic
it takes a little while to find your feet with it but it can do a lot [instruments are sampled sounds of real instruments and sound perfect]
i have been using it for a couple of years now and it has allowed me to do all that i want see sample [**here**]("http://shan.bandcamp.com/track/starting-again") entirely composed midi-style (wrote all the notes) i do not use a keyboard either; always say if you cannot write this in your toilet on a laptop it is not worth making :KS:KS



to activate the midi side you need to run timidity to have sound

```
timidity -iA
```


extra info [**here**]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=10528034&postcount=2") also a **[B][tutorial]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0-X6_q1iRzc")**[/B] for basic startup
