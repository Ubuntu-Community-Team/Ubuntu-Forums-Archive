-installing asound-gtk from the synaptic manager, setting default card to UA-25

-Setting the sound preferences to UA-25 USB Device Under OSS, everthing that I could

-installing the macromedia flash plugin under applications>add/remove programs, then uninstalling it under applications>add/remove programs


-Manually DL'd the linux .tar.gz flash package from the adobe web
site extracted to desktop, than ran the installer in that folder in terminal

-rebooted my system

and I heard the system sound and it seems I now have sound in VLC with .avi's and .mp3's, sound under flash (youtube videos of 50 cent play fine)
