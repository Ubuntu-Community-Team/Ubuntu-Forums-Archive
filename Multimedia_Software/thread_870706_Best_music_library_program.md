### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-07-26
What's wrong with Rhythmbox, which is the default player in Ubuntu?  It does all you want, though I'm not sure how like iTunes it is as I do not have an iPod and have never seen iTunes in action.  I must be one of the few people left who don't use it, I think, but for a variety of reasons I prefer to keep to non drm limited files and not get caught up in Apple's lock in.

Some people like exaile, but I much prefer Rhythmbox personally, but have a look at that as well, or even Amarok if you don't mind the kde interface; it's certainly a good player.
