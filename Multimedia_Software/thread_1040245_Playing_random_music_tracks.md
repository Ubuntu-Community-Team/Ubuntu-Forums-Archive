### Post by Maheriano on 2009-01-19
Okay, I'm at my machine now. So once you have Audacious open, follow these steps.
1. There is a circle in the bottom right of the player with the letter a in it. Above this circle are 2 buttons, one has vertical lines and the other has horizontal lines. I mentioned earlier there is a button with a P on it (I was wrong, sorry), it is actually not a P, it is the button with horizontal lines. Click it.
2. Once you click this, a playlist (blank black box) will open.
3. Drag a folder you want to listen to into this playlist and the songs will be listed.
4. To the left of the circle with the a in it, there are 2 more buttons. One has an A on it and the other has an S on it. Click the button with the S, it's for shuffle. Now once you start playing, the songs should play randomly.

Let me know if you need more help.

### Post by Maheriano on 2009-01-19
No worries. Forget dragging it, just use the ADD button to add whichever directory it is you want to play. Simply click the ADD button, then choose your directory, then click OK and it should load in the playlist. But before that, do a check on the web to make sure that .oga files will actually play in Audacious, not sure if they will.

Another thing you can try is right clicking on one of the .oga files (a song) and choose PROPERTIES. Then click the OPEN WITH tab and choose Audacious, then click OK. Now whenever you double click a song to play it, it'll play in Audacious. If you don't see Audacious in the OPEN WITH list, then it's not compatible and won't work.
