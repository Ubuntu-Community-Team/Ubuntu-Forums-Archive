### Post by The Spectre on 2012-12-11
I had the same issue with DVDStyler & Devede and what fixed it for me was installing the "Real" FFmpeg the one that is installed by default is Libav.

FFmpeg Homepage...
[http://ffmpeg.org/](http://ffmpeg.org/)

FFmpeg PPA Site...
[https://launchpad.net/~jon-severinsson/+archive/ffmpeg](https://launchpad.net/~jon-severinsson/+archive/ffmpeg)

After I installed the latest FFmpeg both of the DVDStyler & Devede audio issues where gone. 

Hope this Helps.:)
