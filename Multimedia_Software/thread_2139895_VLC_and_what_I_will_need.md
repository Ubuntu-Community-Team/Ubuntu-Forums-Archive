### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-04-28
If you installed it using the software-centre or apt-get, everything that is needed to run VLC will have been installed as dependencies.  If you installed from source, I can't help as that is something I have never done, nor needed to do.

What you may still need is to install the ***ubuntu-restricted-extras** package for your OS version as that may contain some codecs you don't already have, and will also allow you to play commercially made DVDs if you follow the instructions at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)
