### Post by darkapec on 2012-08-18
I have used all three of the programs mentioned, all have worked... kinda. My end result was just deleting everything and reimporting the music. 

The programs seemed to have success with about 80% of the music, the problem is of course the other 20% which may accidentally get the wrong name / artist. Then you have to manually listen to every song to make sure they are all correct and if there not you end up manually googling lyrics to find out what they are. 

The other issue is if you have mix cds eg ("NOW" thats what I call music) or any modern hip hop. You will not get the results you are wanting, you will end up with either a completely wrong naming scheme or single songs that have 10 or more artists attached. 

There definitely worth a shot, I would just back up all your music to another location so if the results are undesirable you can just delete the renamed stuff.
