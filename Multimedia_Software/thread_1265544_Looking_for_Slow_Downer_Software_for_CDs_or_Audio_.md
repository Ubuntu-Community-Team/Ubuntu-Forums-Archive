### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-09-13
Audacity is what you need.  You can either edit and save the new speed music file, or if you prefer, just load it, slow it down and play it, according to your requirements.  It's in the repos, but also get the tap-plugins and swh-plugins, which give you extra effects availability.
