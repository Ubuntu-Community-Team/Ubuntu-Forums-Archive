### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2010-12-31
Considering how cheap the media is, it may be better just to use CD-R & fill it up..
The CD-RW has to have existing data blanked off & then new data written.

The CD-RW requires more sensitive laser optics so old players may have problems.
Panasonic has had it's fair share of optical drive failures in PC & consumer equipment.
