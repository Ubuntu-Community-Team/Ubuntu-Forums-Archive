### Post by ajgreeny on 2022-02-05
There is a PPA for audio-recorder which is vailable for all supported, and still to come, ie Jammy, versions of Ubuntu.
It works splendidly in Focal (20.04) and also in Jammy (22.04) and as you obviously kno, is a great deal more simple to use than audacity.

All details of the package and how to enable its repository can be found at [https://launchpad.net/~audio-recorder/+archive/ubuntu/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~audio-recorder/+archive/ubuntu/ppa)

Good luck.
