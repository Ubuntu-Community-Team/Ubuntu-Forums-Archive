### Post by gordintoronto on 2009-12-06
Mythbuntu is primarily for people with a supported TV tuner card.  It will let you record programs when they are broadcast and play them back at your convenience.  You can also watch a program "live," and pause it while you go and make a coffee, etc, then fast-forward past commercials to catch up.  You can do your own replays on any program.

You also might want to check out VLC media player.
