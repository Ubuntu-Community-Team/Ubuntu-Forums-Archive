### Post by NetTechGuy on 2010-06-14
ok so the fix was very simple..

Step 1- connect your camera with the original usb cable, from camera to computer.

Step 2 - click the menu button on the camera,  click usb connect and select mass media storage option.

Thats it your ubuntu install will detect and mount the memory card on your camera automatically.
