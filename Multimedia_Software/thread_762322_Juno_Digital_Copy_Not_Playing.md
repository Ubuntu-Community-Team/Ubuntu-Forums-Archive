You can watch it with Totem Movie Player (Applications > Sound & Video > Movie Player). Usually, just double clicking on the file should automatically play it. If that doesn't work, you may need to install the "gstreamer ffmpeg video plugin" package.

Go to Applications > Add/Remove and search "gstreamer ffmeg video plugin" and install.
