### Post by kostkon on 2008-12-23
Yes, this is the [*Music Applet*]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/intrepid/music-applet"). You can install it using *Synaptic*. Then to add it to one your panel(s) just right-click on your panel and select *Add to Panel...*
