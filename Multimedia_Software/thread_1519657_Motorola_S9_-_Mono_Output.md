When I connect Motorola S9 through Bluetooth, the output I listern is in Mono mode. (Music is coming from Left speaker only)

---

### Post by bharanik on 2010-06-29
Hi All,

I have changed the sound preferences --> Hardware --> Motorola S9 and selected the profile High Fidelity Playback (A2DP).

Now working like a charm....:guitar:
