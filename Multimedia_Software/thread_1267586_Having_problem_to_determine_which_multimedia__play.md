### Post by Rhubarb on 2009-09-15
> **sumantri_84 said:**
> ...What you think about Songbird, Cinerella , Avidemux, Totem, Xine. GC Star, Audacity? 
Or else can someone suggest other multimedia player that have similar function as VLC?

I recommend you try out smplayer for videos, it's mplayer with a nicer front-end.

Cinerella, Avidemux, and Auacity are Sound / Video editors, they're not really suited for playback.

You could also try out quod libet, it's a great little app for playing back audio, and can play back video as well.
