### Post by tommcd on 2010-09-07
> **Nostigex said:**
> I miss the way Windows Media Player and WinAMP sorts tracks - by album artist instead of individual track artists. ...
If you would like a simple, old school, Winamp like media player for Ubuntu, try **audacious**. It looks and works just like Winamp. Most importantly, it will also sort your music library exactly the way you have it sorted on your computer. 
Another Winamp clone is **xmms**; but xmms is not present in the Ubuntu repos.
