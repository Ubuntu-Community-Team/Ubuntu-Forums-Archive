### Post by lyceum on 2009-01-01
I looked for ever to figure this out,so I am going to post my fix in case anyone else has this problem.

No not look in System Settings, there is nothing there to help with this problem (which is where I kept naturally looking). If you right click on the speaker icon on your system try then chose the mic-in/line-in you want. The icon will change, left click on it and click on "mixer". Mess with the setting until you get what you want.

Hopefully this will get added to the System setting so it will be easier to find in the future.
