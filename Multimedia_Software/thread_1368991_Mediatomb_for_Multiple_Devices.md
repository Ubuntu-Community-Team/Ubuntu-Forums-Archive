### Post by oliver.greg@gmail.com on 2009-12-31
If you are in the same boat as myself, I have spoken to Jin - a developer with the mediatomb development team, and they (he) is willing to provide a means to differentiate profiles for separate rendering devices by IP address.

I have directv receivers in every room that play from mediatomb, and I also have PS3s in certain rooms.  The lack of profiles has caused me some issues such as:

o transcoding bitrates - Some clients are wired, while some are wireless 11.g - This would provide a means to transcode accordingly with separate bitrates per device.

o  Not transcoding everything - currently I must transcode everything since my xeternal scripts have to figure out where it is going and act accordingly.  This is fine for the dtv receivers, but the PS3 obviously handles most new codecs natively, so I would like to not transcode them if possible.  This keeps my seek functionality intact as well.

If you have any questions regarding more details of what you can do with these changes, I will be more than happy to answer them.
