### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2011-10-21
The best HTPC card price/performance(PQ)/size/heat/noise is:

nVidia GT430 PCIexpress

There is a ITX mobo (i3 CPU socket) with GT430 onboard from Zotac.
But small = expensive.
Power efficiency of i3 sandybridge & GT430 is almost unbeatable.

Depends how many tuners (& type) you need..

Do research the idea of separate backend & frontend machines.
Consider DLNA on the TV with myth backend & mythweb on a tablet/phone.

Mythbuntu would be better route than Ubuntu because you avoid the nVidia/unity/compiz mess.
