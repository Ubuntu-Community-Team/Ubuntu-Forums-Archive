### Post by coskierken on 2010-06-22
Sound is under preferences.  You pick the hardware there, then input and output devices. I take it you are running through the on-board hdmi or are you running a seperate video card?  What is the rest of your hardware?  It does make a difference as Nvidia cards do not have the on-board sound on the vid-card and you have to pass through the digital audio via a small cable from the motherboard header to the vid-card.  ATI supports it fully on their cards.  Hope this helps!
