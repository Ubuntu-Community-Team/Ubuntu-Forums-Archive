### Post by deadflowr on 2014-03-11
I use hulu all the time on Ubuntu and never have a choppiness problem.
I would suggest looking over the support page at hulu.com.
And maybe contact them for help.

It might take a day for them to reply to you, but they do help solve problems when asked.

If you do try contacing them, give as much info on the system you have as possible.
Include system specs, Ubuntu version, and if possible any network speed you have.
(chances are they will point you toward speedtest.net to test network speeds)
