### Post by JackD on 2009-12-31
This is just weird. All video playback on my system has suddenly been afflicted with a shift in color. It doesn't make sense, because if I use a separate login account, then the playback is fine.

- All codecs, all playback software is affected equally (mpg, avi, et al; VLC, mplayer, et al.)
- Photos are fine, nothing else is affected.
- Logging on with a separate account "corrects" the problem.

There is a default setting, I'm guessing, somewhere for gnome that is throwing off color. Any ideas ?

---

### Post by VertexPusher on 2009-12-31
Movie Player (Totem): Edit --> Preferences --> Display --> Color Balance

---

### Post by JackD on 2010-01-01
Fixed it ! That was it.

So, I'm at a loss as to why (1) the Hue preferences on Totem was altered (I dont' use Totem, but I do use VLC), (2) why it would affect all the other players, and (3) how anyone would think to go there.

Thanks very much
