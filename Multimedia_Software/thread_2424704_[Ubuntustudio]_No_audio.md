### Post by Autodave on 2019-08-13
I would then go back to 18.04 with a clean install.  18.04 is an LTS (long term service) release with support for at least 5 years.  19.04 is a short term release and has to be updated to the next release every 6 months. 

Ubuntu Studio, as I understand it, use JACK for its sound control.  Personally, I have never had any luck with getting JACK to play nicely with any install.  Unless there is a specific reason that you need Studio, I would suggest Xubuntu which is quite similar.  Any programs in Studio can be installed in Xubuntu.
