### Post by TBABill on 2010-03-01
If the driver is already installed (the proprietary nVidia driver), go to the nVidia settings application and choose a higher resolution. I'm not sure how selecting a higher resolution would help with crash problems though. Higher resolutions require greater power than lower resolutions, on any OS, not just Ubuntu. That would usually be a contributor to a problem, not a remedy for it.

I would suggest trying a different nVidia driver if the current is not working well and you are sure it's the driver that is the problem. The repositories have older drivers available and the most current may not be best for your card.
