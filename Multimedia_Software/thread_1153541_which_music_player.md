### Post by jasonsjunk on 2009-05-08
I use VLC myself it is the swiss army knife of players.  As for collection management I like Amarok, it works well and many people have used it for insanely large collections.  

However since I run MythTv on my network it is the main music manager for my house.  All of my music is stored on one computer and I can stream music to all of my computers/tvs using mythmusic.  Granted Myth hoses up how your collection is organized but it keeps things nice and tidy within MythTv that and the fact that I have some of my remote controls working allows me to change tracks without having to get up.  

I know VLC can do streaming and I think Amarok can as well I just haven't wanted to put in the effort to setting it all up.  

Amarok is probably one of the best for managing music on a portable device as well.
