Hey,

lspci |grep VGA
``` Stuff in terminal is CaSE SenSItIve, by the way. If you have trouble copying it, copy in gnome-terminal is shift-ctrl-c , or you can highlight and right click to copy/paste.

### Post by goshka on 2009-08-26
Try this on the file xorg.conf
 
Section "Monitor"
Identifier "Configured Monitor"
Option "DPMS" "true"
HorizSync 30.0-60.0
VertRefresh 50.0-70.0
EndSection
 
Section "Screen"
Identifier "Default Screen"
Monitor "Configured Monitor"
Device "Configured Video Device"
DefaultDepth 24
SubSection "Display"
Depth 24
Modes "1024x768" "800x600"
EndSubSection
 
EndSection
