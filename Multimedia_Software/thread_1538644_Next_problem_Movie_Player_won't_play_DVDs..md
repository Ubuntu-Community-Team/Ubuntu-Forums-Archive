### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2010-07-25
You need to add the medibuntu repository and then download libdvdcss2, you should also download ubuntu-restricted-extras if you haven't already.
See [this page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu") for instructions and an explanation of why it is not included by default.
