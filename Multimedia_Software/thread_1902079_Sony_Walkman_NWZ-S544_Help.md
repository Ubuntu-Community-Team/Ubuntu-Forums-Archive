### Post by callmebruce on 2012-01-25
I'm not sure what format your Walkman uses, but I had to convert some OGG videos (awesome Film Noir from the 40's from the Internet Archive!) into 3GP to run on my phone. Phone uses same resolution as your Walkman.

I did it manually with ffmpeg, but I wasn't able to synch the sound properly, and I couldn't watch more than a few minutes of video. I installed MobileMediaConverter - and it converted 6 films into a format that worked on my phone!

Give it a try! I got Detour, DOA, Impact, Quicksand and Too Late For Tears loaded on my phone (over USB cable using Banshee) in 3GP format in 320x240 resolution. It's kind of funny watching old black and white movies in small screen format.
