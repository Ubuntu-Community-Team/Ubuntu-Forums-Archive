### Post by Ubuntist on 2009-05-08
I lost sound (music) after upgrading to Jaunty.  I know this will sound weird, but I went to System -> Preferences -> Sound and ticked Play alerts, Play sound effecst and Play alert sound, which should have nothing to do with music.  I now have music again.
