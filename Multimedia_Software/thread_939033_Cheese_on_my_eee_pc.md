### Post by dje on 2008-10-05
turn on the eee and when the bios screen (eee logo) comes up press F2. In the bios setup navigate to Advanced >> Onboard Devices Configuration and set Onboard Camera to Enabled. Press F10 to save and exit. Load up cheese and it should be working :)
