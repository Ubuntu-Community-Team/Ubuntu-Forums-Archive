### Post by ravenus on 2010-03-27
Wow, that's a really old card and probably has really old drivers only. I'm also a newbie so far as these issues are concerned but one thing is that with VGA connections, HDTV's will not give you the full resolution (mine is not supposed to give me greater than 1024 x 768 on my 1080p plasma). Of course, you should still get better than 640x480 but like I said, it could be a case of old hardware + drivers. You might think about putting down a small investment for a newer low-end card with a DVI output.
