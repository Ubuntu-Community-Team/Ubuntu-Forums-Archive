Same experience here, but I managed to get my divx played on the PS3 doing this :
- start a browser to open mediatomb web interface (ubuntu menu > sound & video > mediatomb),
- select the file you want to play from the Video entry,
- edit its properties, and change the mimetype to "video/divx"
