### Post by sfraga on 2008-05-25
hey the Style..

not sure if ur still strugglin with those volume control buttons.. i just happened to have managed to fix my problem.
here is what i did..

Go to
System-> Preferences-> Sounds

under Devices select which device u wanna use and dont forget to select something in the drop down box too.. personally what worked for me was.. my sounds card (Sigmatel OSS mixer) in the device box and PCM-2 in the drop-down box.

Then right click on the volume icon on the top right corner of ur screen to bring the preferences of the volume control.. and there u select exactly the same thing.. for me.. SigmaTel and drop-down box.. PCM-2.

and voila.. :D

hope it helps.
