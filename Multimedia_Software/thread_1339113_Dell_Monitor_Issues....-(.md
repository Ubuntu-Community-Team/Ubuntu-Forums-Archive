### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-11-27
What graphics card do you have?   What is the native resolution of the screen?  I think it should be 1024x768, but you may be trying to get something else.

There is probably a way to get the resolution you want, but it may need a graphics card driver, so more information is needed about hardware.  To find out what Ubuntu thinks your card is, if you don't already know, enter ```
lshw -C display
```in terminal and copy the output back here.
