### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2011-05-31
That's cheap compared to other parts of the world..

You need to decide if you need:
dvb-t/t2 &/or dvb-s/s2 (most of the world) or
atsc/cable
SD or HD capable (mpeg2-ts output H264AVC)
Then single or dual tuners & if 
analogue or HDMI input capture is required.. (STBox)

In my corner a dual or triple dvb-t/t2 + dvb-s/s2 setup is ideal..(+ analogue HD capture with h264 video for any STB for good measure).
