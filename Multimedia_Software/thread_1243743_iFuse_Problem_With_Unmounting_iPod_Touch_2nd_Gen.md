### Post by Katalog on 2009-08-18
Looks to me from the image that unmounting the device would require you to have root privileges, which you obviously don't. I'm not familiar with iFuse or the Touch, but what I would try is to go into the terminal and type "sudo umount /media/(whatever disk name the sytem gave your iPod Touch, i.e. disk, disk-1, etc.) and see if that doesn't unmount the drive.
