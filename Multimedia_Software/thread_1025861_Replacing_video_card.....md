### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-12-30
if you are staying with the same kind of card nvidia to nvidia/ati to ati, just take out the old one and pop in the new. if you are going let's say from nvidia to ati, i would remove any proprietary drivers first, just to be on the safe side. if you are not using any drivers, you have no worries, just replace the card and boot up.
