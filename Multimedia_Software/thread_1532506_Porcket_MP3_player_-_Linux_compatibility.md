### Post by Chronon on 2010-07-16
Music synched while in MTP mode will often be stored in a hidden location.  Try removing it while in MTP then switch to MSC and store your music as you see fit.  

Of course, how SanDisk's firmware presents the music to you is a separate issue.  You are pretty much stuck with their database system.
