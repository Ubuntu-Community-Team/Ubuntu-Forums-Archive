### Post by luisromangz on 2008-05-09
You can use DVDLab Pro with Wine (in fact they kind of advertise that as a feature AFAIR).

Depending on the features you are looking for, you might try Avidemux (adding subtiltles, recoding, audio/video filters), Kdenlive (non linear video editing, several tracks, filters, transitions etc). If you are looking for DVD authoring software (as DVDLab is) maybe either DVDStyler or ManDVD does the trick, but I haven't used those.
