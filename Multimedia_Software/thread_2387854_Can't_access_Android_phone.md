### Post by ajgreeny on 2018-03-24
You may need to change the settings in your phone to make sure it is detected as either a USB mass storage device, or if that does not work, as an MTP device.

I have no idea where those settings will be but look around in the phone and you should find a way to change from one to the other.
Often these days using 16.04 it is simply a case of attaching the phone and all works OK, but I imagine each hardware manufacturer does things differently.
