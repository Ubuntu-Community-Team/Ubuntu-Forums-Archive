### Post by DJ_Peng on 2009-02-09
You can rate files in Amarok and Exaile, but you won't see the ratings you've already applied. Windows Media Player stores them in a database, not with the file's tags. You'll also find that ratings made in one Linux audio app won't be used in another one. I don't know if there's a way to get ratings from one app and imported into another.
