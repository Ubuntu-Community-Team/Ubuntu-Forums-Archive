### Post by mc4man on 2008-07-27
Sometimes a simple solution is to look up on your top panel for the little speaker icon, usually next to the date. Double click on it, if in the pop up there's a tab for switches open it and set the analog/digital output jack to the opposite of what it is now.

Otherwise try running  ```
alsamixer
``` in a terminal and make sure the various outputs are turned on. (master thru surround )
