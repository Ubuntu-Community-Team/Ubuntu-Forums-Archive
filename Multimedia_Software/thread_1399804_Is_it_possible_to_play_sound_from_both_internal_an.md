### Post by tgalati4 on 2010-02-06
Three possible ways to do this:

1.  Get a splitter
2.  Check for a "headphone detect" switch in your Volume mixer console
3.  Check for a physical headphone switch in the jack and bypass--this requires dismantling the front-panel jack assembly

4.  Check for ALSA options for your sound card.
