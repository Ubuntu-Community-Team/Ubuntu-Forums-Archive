### Post by Manuel Cappello on 2012-08-22
I write this post only to confirm that the USB audio interface Focusrite Scarlet 2i2 is correctly working just out of the box on my ASUS 1225B netbook with Ubuntu 12-04 32-bit.

Kernel Linux 3.2.0-29-generic-pae
GNOME 3.4.2
AMD E-450 APU with Radeon(tm) HD Graphics × 2 

The USB device is soon recognized as 1235:8006 Novation EMS.
Alsamixer can see it, even if there is no regulation available.
I made a brief recording test with Audacity, and i found it worked properly.

Manuel
