### Post by SuperFreak on 2012-02-21
If you go into Preferences/Other you will find that you can specify how the files are labelled, as well as the folders the album is put into

For example under standard I have the following labelling indicated: %a /%b   %y/%n - %t

If you look below those fields and click on Show options it will explain what the symbols and letters refer to
