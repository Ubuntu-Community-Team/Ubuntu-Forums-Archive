This morning I have accidentally deleted the soundmenu in the panel that comes with the new ubuntu 10.10. 
After going through various posts all I can see, is that the solutions provided only provide the applet for sound control and does not have any integration with any of the music players (in my case Rhythmbox).

### Post by dondiego2 on 2010-12-06
Just right click on an empty space on the task bar and click "add to panel" and add the "Indicator Applet".  It will put the sound and mail indicators on your task bar.

---

### Post by bnjmnwroberts on 2010-12-06
I did this by accident once. Right click on an empty area of the panel you removed it from, and select"add to panel".
Scroll down the list until you see the speaker icon, and click on it. This will ask you if you want to add it. Do it. Now you should have your sound menu back.
If this dosent work, install the indicator applet from the same list. One of the two should definitely work. hope this helps.
