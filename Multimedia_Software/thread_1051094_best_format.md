### Post by mcduck on 2009-01-26
h.264 for video codec, for audio either MP3 or AAC.

The container (.avi, .mov, .mp4) makes no difference if you think about quality/filesize, that's more of a compatibility question. I recommend using either. avi or .mp4 container as they both are widely supported.
