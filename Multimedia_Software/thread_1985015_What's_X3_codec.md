### Post by Rodney9 on 2012-05-22
Googled X3 Codec and [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/3wPlayer](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/3wPlayer) says - 

> 3wPlayer is a rogue media player software application bundled with trojans that can infect computers running Microsoft Windows.

The DivoCodec or Divo Codec or X3Codec has also been identified as a trojan similar to 3wPlayer.
