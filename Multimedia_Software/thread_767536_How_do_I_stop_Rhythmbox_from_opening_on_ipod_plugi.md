### Post by kostkon on 2008-04-25
> **knavarathna92 said:**
> system-> preferences-> prefered applications then select your media player if you don't want rythymbox.
Actually, *Blarion*, you must go to *System -> Preferences -> Removable Drives and Media*.

There, in the *Multimedia* tab, change the command for *Portable Music Players* from *rhythmbox* to *banshee* and you should be fine.

After doing this, *Banshee* will open everytime you connect your *iPod* to your PC.

The multimedia tab is now part of nautilus 
Go to Edit->Preferences in nautilus and select the media tab.
You can switch to have nautilus open your ipod folder in the section that says Music Player
Although I believe the setting for the ipod thing though is to disable the ipod plugin in rhythmbox
