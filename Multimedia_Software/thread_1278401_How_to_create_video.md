### Post by cotcot on 2009-09-29
Yes this is possible. There are many possibilities.

You can use a dedicated application to make slideshows. [[COLOR="Red"]Smile[/COLOR]]("http://smile.tuxfamily.org/?q=node/2") is an example. It is in french : the first link gives you a .deb package that is easily installable. Another one is Qdvdauthor. Here is a [[COLOR="Red"]tutorial[/COLOR]]("http://linuxappfinder.com/blog/make_a_dvd_slideshow_using_qdvdauthor").

You can use the slideshow function of picture browsers such as Digikam.

Or you can use video editors such as Kdenlive, Blender VSE, Pitivi, Openshot, ... , putting pictures on a timeline and rendering it to a video.
