I used to use Avidemux and Mpeg Streamclip to extract snippets from an avi file. I would open the file, put a marker at the beginning and end of the area to snippet and then select save to save the snippet of video. Can I do the same thing with Openshot. ### Post by gggecko on 2011-11-17
What I would do, is fire up openshot, import the video you want a snippet from, and drag it onto track 2. After this, select the razor tool and click at the beginning of the section you want, and then at the end. There should now be 3 video clips. Reselect the pointer tool and right click one of the bits you don't want, and click on remove clip, or something similar, repeat for the other unwanted clip.Drag the remaining clip to the beginning of the timeline, and hit Ctrl+E , you will now be able to export the video in a wide range of commonly used formats. Hope this helped, please post back to let me know how it went or if you need more information.
