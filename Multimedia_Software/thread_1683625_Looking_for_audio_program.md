I only chop and splice voice; however Audacity is in the repositories and has some advanced features.  If your looking for more complex or professional level stuff have a look at :

The audio programs can be installed from synaptic (ubuntustudio-audio) into you regular Ubuntu set up.  They've got it all, drum machines, mixers, virtual PA heads, effects boards you name it...
