### Post by donato roque on 2009-09-18
Answer to the 1 question:  You can try and adjust your system volume in the panel.  Just click with the mouse and a slider control comes out.  I leave the application volume to full but that's just me.

Answer to the 2 question:  After opening the audio disk a window should come out just hit alt+a.  This will select all then hit enter.
