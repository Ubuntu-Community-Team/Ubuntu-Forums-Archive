### Post by tgalati4 on 2009-08-31
If you are running Jaunty (9.04) then I believe the fglx drivers are incompatible with many older ATI cards.  You have to downgrade to Intrepid or Hardy to use the fglx drivers to get proper 3D performance.  

Right now, you are running software rendering, which is slooow.
