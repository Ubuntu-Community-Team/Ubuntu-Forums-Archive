### Post by Mr_JMM on 2012-11-09
Go to the sound settings menu and double check that the correct output is selected (once Rhythmbox has started). I've had occasional issues where an application has taken it upon itself to change it.

Oh yes, more obvious perhaps but double check the volume control in Rhythmbox isn't set to mute or very low (it has it's on volume slider separate to the main volume).
