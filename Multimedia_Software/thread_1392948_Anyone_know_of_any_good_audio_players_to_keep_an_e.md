### Post by Alex Libman on 2010-01-28
I'm keeping an eye on the idea that your Web browser will soon also be your ideal media player thanks to [HTML5's use of Ogg Vorbis / Theora codecs]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Use_of_Ogg_formats_in_HTML5").

This means you'd be able to play standards-compliant media without any proprietary [BLOB]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Binary_blob")'s (ex. Flash), and even play them on an entirely [permissively-licensed]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Permissive_free_software_licence") desktop software stack thanks to a FLOSS fork of [Google Chrome]("http://code.google.com/chromium/terms.html").
