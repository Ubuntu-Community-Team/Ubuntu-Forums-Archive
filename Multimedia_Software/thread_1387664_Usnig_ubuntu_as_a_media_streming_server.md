### Post by Lars Noodén on 2010-01-22
You can do video streamin on Ubuntu with [vlc](http://www.videolan.org/vlc/streaming.html), [icecast](http://www.icecast.org/), or [ffmpeg](http://ffmpeg.org/ffserver-doc.html)

You might consider using one of the above as the source for encoding  your videos as well.  That way you can get a better video format like MPEG4 or Theora:

[*] Audio clips will use Vorbis/Ogg, MP3 or FLAC/Ogg."
[/list]
