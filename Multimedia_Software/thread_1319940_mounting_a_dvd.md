### Post by dansi on 2009-11-09
if you are just mounting the image on an image mounting program (Alcohol, Daemon Tools...), then you dont need to worry about what to mount it as because it automatically mounts it for you.

if the image is 698mb and you are going to burn it, then try a cd. most disk burning programs won't let you burn an image thats larger than the disk itself. and if it doesnt work, then try for a dvd instead.

only advantages cd's have is that they are cheaper, and most are read faster than dvd's.
