---

### Post by noerrorsfound on 2008-04-26
[LIST=1]
[*]Open any Nautilus window (*Places --> Home* works)
[*]Click *Edit --> Preferences*
[*]Under the Preview tab, next to "Preview sound files:" should be a drop-down menu. Click it, and select the **Never** option.
[*]Click *Close*
[/LIST]
