### Post by Earendil Star on 2009-08-04
For all owners of ATI Radeon HD 2600 & HD 36xx video cards:
latest release of ATI Catalyst drivers (9.7) finally gets rid of flickering when using visual effects!
Celestia, Google Earth, etc. are now usable even without disabling compiz!
Still some minor issues (seems slow with the a.m. apps, and "Screensaver Preferences" behaves oddly, needs xserver-no-backfill patch) but otherwise fantastic! :D
To install the latest drivers, download them from the [ATI]("http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/Pages/index.aspx") site, and follow the instructions in the [Unofficial ATI Wiki]("http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Jaunty_Installation_Guide"). Before proceeding, as specified in the ATI Wiki, take care to install all needed packages and be sure to remove previously installed fglrx drivers if you had them, otherwise installation will fail.
Hope this may be helpful! Cheers!
