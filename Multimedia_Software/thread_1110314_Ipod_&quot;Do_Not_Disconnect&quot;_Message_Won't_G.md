### Post by 2j4ez on 2009-03-29
Have you tried to reboot the ipod if its a video then press MIDDLE BUTTON and MENU button (for 6secs) then it should reboot and come back to life this also might work with other ipods with the click wheel

If you press MIDDLE BUTTON and PLAY it will force it into disc mode so you can restore the software if needed 

hope this helps
