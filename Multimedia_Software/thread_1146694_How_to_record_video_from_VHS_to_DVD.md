### Post by bumanie on 2009-05-03
I successfully did what you are wanting to do when I used 'that other OS', but there is no reason why you couldn't do it in ubuntu. Firstly you would need to buy a video capture card, unless you have one already incorporated in your graphics card. Look up capture cards that work in linux. [This should help]("http://hardware4linux.info/type/87/"). The video will be changed to a dv format via the card and then you should be able to use a linux tool such as devede or something similar to change it to dvd format.
