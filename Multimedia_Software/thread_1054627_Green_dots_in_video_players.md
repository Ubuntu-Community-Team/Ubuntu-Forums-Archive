### Post by x33a on 2009-01-29
if you have compiz (desktop effects) enabled, you might have to disable it.

if not, try updating your video drivers.

---

### Post by Tienshinan8 on 2009-01-29
It worked!

I had moderate desktop effects enabled. I turned it off completely, and it worked!

Thank you very much!
