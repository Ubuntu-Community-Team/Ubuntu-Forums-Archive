### Post by droadtrip on 2010-01-04
[FONT=Tahoma][SIZE=3][COLOR=Navy]When I was new to Ubuntu (specifically, Xubuntu), I had sound problems too and found a really easy solution. I use "Pulse Audio" from Add/Remove Programs. In the app, I have to click the sound to basically tell it to turn on. Then it always works for my session. If I restart or put the system on standby, then I have to use Pulse Audio again, therefore, I just keep the app constantly open on my desktop. I tiny extra step, but worth it for being away from Windows. [/COLOR][/SIZE][/FONT]
