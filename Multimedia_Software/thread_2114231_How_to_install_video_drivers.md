### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2013-02-11
You don't. That driver doesn't support kernel modesetting, so it's deprecated. The openchrome project is still working on a modern driver that will at least give you decent resolution and some 2D acceleration. Really, your best bet is investing in new hardware.
