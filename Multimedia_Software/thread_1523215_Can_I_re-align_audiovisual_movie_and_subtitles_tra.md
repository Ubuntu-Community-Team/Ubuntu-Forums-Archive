### Post by Joe of loath on 2010-07-03
In VLC go tools > track synchronisation. There are options to change the time delay and speed of the subtitles.

### Post by papibe on 2010-07-29
If they are not too out of sync, you can use shortcuts:

h for delaying the subs 50 milliseconds.
g for advancing the subs 50 milliseconds (displayed as delayed -50ms).

You can change the step (50) by going to Tools > Track Synchronization.

Good Luck.
