### Post by dmitric300 on 2008-06-11
**EDIT: RESOLVED**

It turned out that I was booting into kernel 2.6.22 when kernel 2.6.24 was available (menu.lst in Grub was never updated). After making the Grub entry and booting into 2.6.24 fglrx was configured and loaded. 


**Original Entry**

I currently have Hardy installed and I've been trying to get the fglrx driver working for quite some time. I have already gone through many of the generic howto websites without success.
