### Post by mattharris4 on 2015-05-25
For the record even if the MP3 player is not "compatible" with Canonical OSes you can still copy and paste music files onto it manually through the file manager.  That is what I do with my Sony MP3 player, it will even sync using Rhythmbox although 12GB of music files (about 3300 songs) took about eight hours to copy compared with about two with Windows.  Also, the Rhythmbox software will format and reinstall all files during a sync, it doesn't just check for what is not there and only copy those files like Windows Media Player does.
