### Post by meditatingfrog on 2009-10-12
Depends on what Ipod you have.  It seems the more recent your ipod, the greater the difficulty it is in getting your ipod to work.  I  have a 5th gen ipod, and getting music off the ipod is possible with Rhythmbox and Gtkpod (not DRMed music though).  Can't delete files from it, but I haven't tried Rockbox yet, from Rockbox.org.  So, first step is to find out what generation ipod you have, after this you can try searching the forums based on your ipod model.
