### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2011-09-29
This is where all the tuner card support comes from.
[http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Pinnacle_PCTV_HD_Mini_Stick_%2880e%29](http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Pinnacle_PCTV_HD_Mini_Stick_%2880e%29)

The patches/beta driver for your USB tuner do not work with current natty kernels.

Search that website or mythtv.org or ask on the forum: 3rd party projects/mythbuntu sub section..

PCI bandwidth is more than enough for tuner cards so don't rule out the old nova-500-t dual dvb-t.

PCIe cards that could worth looking at:
HVR-4000 dual dvb-t & dvb-s/2 & analogue encoder. Not perfect tho.
HVR-2200 dual dvb-t & analogue mpeg encoder.

You need to determine what signals you have available (now/soon)  as there is a wide variety of tuner cards.
