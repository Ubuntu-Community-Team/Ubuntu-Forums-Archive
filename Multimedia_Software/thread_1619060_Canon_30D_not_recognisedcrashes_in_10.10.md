### Post by TVTrukChik on 2010-11-14
Have you thought about getting an external USB card reader and using that instead of plugging the camera in directly?  I have never plugged my camera directly into the PC.  

I figure it this way:  A card reader transfers faster, I don't have to worry about tripping over the cable and knocking my camera onto the floor, and using the card reader doesn't run down the camera battery.  Plus, I can take out one card, start it downloading with the card reader, then put the second card into the camera and go shoot some more while the first set of photos are downloading.
