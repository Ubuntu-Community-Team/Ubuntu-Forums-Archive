### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-08-16
get vlc player first. then when you go to pick which size video to play, right click the one you want and choose "copy link location". open vlc and go to file > open network stream > http. right click and paste link. hit OK. done. it seems like alot of work, but in reality is only a few seconds. it's the only way i've gotten them to work correctly.
