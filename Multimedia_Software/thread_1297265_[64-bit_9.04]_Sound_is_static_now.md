### Post by butsam on 2009-10-23
I fixed the problem!

Furthermore, I found a bug in the mixer (most likely).

Here are the steps to reproduce the "static" I was hearing:

1) Open up the Volume Control mixer (I am using the default for 64-bit Jaunty (9.04), whatever that is -- alsa?)

2) Mute the PCM volume

3) Unmute the PCM volume -- static is heard

4) Change the volume level of the PCM volume -- static goes away and sound is heard again.

I am not familiar with the bug-reporting process but I will try to find the right place to report this.

EDIT: Bug reported, bug #459346 in yelp.
