### Post by mdg1019 on 2009-05-05
This is a problem with configuring Flash. It really makes no difference what operating system your using. It is more pronounced on slower machines and larger window sizes.

To correct it, right-click on the video when it's playing, from the menu select the settings option, then just uncheck the box for hardware acceleration. This should clear up the problems you're having.
