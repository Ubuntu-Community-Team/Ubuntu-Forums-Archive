### Post by bumanie on 2009-11-24
Winff is a gui for ffmpeg and it should do what you want. If you want to convert to mpeg for making a cd/dvd, you could use devede which will encode both files, make a .iso which you can then burn. Avidemux will also convert things to mpeg.
