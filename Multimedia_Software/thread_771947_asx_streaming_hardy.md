### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-07-29
click on the radio tab on that page to listen live. pick which one you want to listen to. when the little box opens up to choose high or low bandwidth, just right click and "copy link address". next, open up vlc and go to file>open network stream>HTTP....and paste address. click OK. there you go. it may seem like alot, but only takes a few seconds.
