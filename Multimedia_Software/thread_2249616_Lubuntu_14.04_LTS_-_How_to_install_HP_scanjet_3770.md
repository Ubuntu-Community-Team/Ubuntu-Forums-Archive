### Post by Rob Sayer on 2014-10-25
According to:

[https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/simple-scan/+question/153282](https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/simple-scan/+question/153282)

you need a 32 bit system.  It's not that new a source of info but neither is the scanner all that new.

And according to this:

[http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html](http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html)

HPLIP doesn't support scanjet single purpose scanners.  It links to:

[http://sane-project.org/](http://sane-project.org/)

Good luck.
