I'm just starting to use HandBrake and realize that good presets are the key. I've tried some of the presets on the HandBrake website and they don't work for me so I tweaked the Classic a little bit and finally got a rip to work properly.

If you have any presets that you've created that you particularly like, could you share them here? Thanks....

This is Classic using lame, a 700MB limit so it will fit on a CD, and .avi, and 2 pass:

HandBrakeCLI -i /media/cdrom0 -o ~/Videos/movie.avi -e ffmpeg -S 690 -E lame -B 160 -R 48 -f avi -2
