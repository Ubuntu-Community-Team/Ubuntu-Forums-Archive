### Post by MattPhillips on 2009-03-05
Think twice.  I have experienced nothing but pain (see my threads) with Ubuntu + OpenGL, and the problem may well be that I'm using an ATI card, since many people (at work, and some who have responded to my threads) who use nVidia don't appear to have these problems.  A first-person shooter which runs perfectly under Mac OS (same machine) flickers and has rendering discontinuities with Ubuntu, and weirdness with glxgears and other opengl demo programs, for example.

Matt
