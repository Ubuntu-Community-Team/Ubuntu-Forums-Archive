### Post by BenAshton24 on 2009-11-29
It probably thinks that they are videos even if they only contain audio streams. Maybe you could try renaming them to .mp3 and just see if that works (I don't know how rhythmbox detects filetypes, it might base them on extensions). If that fails then you can convert them with ffmpeg or use a different music manager.
