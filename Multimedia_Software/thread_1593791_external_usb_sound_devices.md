hi all
i'm currently looking into purchasing a new sound card for my ubuntu 10.04 32bit pc.
the main reason i've decided to do this is because i just purchased a set of studio monitors (wharfedale diamond 8.2 pro active) and i'm not getting the best sound quality out of them as on my current unbalanced system (sound blaster audigy) they hum a lot. however when i tested them on a balanced system they are totally silent with no hum at all, and the sound quality is much better.
so i'm looking for something that will work specifically on ubuntu 9.04 and 10.04 or greater. i do not use windows or mac, the minimum specs beyond this for the sound device 

[LIST]
[*]external usb
[*]usb powered (preferred) or AC adapter powered
[*]x2 balanced outputs (left and right) TRS or XLR
[*]x2 balanced inputs (instrument and mic)
[*]phantom power
[*]MIDI in and out
[*]budget approx $200
[/LIST]
finally it must work with ubuntu 10.04 (preferably out of the box) and be detectable by ALSA and JACK.
Apparently [M-Audio Fast Track Pro]("http://www.m-audio.com/products/en_us/FastTrackPro.html") has all of these specs and works out of the box on Ubuntu according to this [http://en.andrenoel.com.br/2008/08/06/usb-sound-card-on-ubuntu/](http://en.andrenoel.com.br/2008/08/06/usb-sound-card-on-ubuntu/)
has anyone got any experiences with their sound devices matching specs listed above that they would care to share?
thanks for your help :)
