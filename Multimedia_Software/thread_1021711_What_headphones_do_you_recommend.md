### Post by it_fixxer on 2008-12-27
Personal reccomendation is to read the specs  it should say something like

Frequency response: 20Hz - 20kHz

The lower the first number means better low end(bass). The higher the second number the better high pitches it can produce. So far I have gone for studio quality (full over the ear) and have never been dissapointed.
