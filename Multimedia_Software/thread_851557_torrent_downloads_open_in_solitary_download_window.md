### Post by Bakon Jarser on 2008-07-06
Go to edit > preferences in firefox.  Click on applications at the top.  Look for Bittorent Seed File in the list.  Click on it.  Now a drop down arrow appears on the right.  See if your torrent client of choice is listed.  If not choose open with other and look for the program in /usr/bin
