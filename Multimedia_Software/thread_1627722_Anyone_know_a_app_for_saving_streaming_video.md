### Post by northern lights on 2010-11-21
I dunno what file format and codec you're aiming at, but unless it's divx encoded avi, it ought to get saved to your /tmp folder once buffered.

So just start the stream, mute the volume or hit pause, open nautilus, navigate to "File System", then to tmp. Video file will be in there.
