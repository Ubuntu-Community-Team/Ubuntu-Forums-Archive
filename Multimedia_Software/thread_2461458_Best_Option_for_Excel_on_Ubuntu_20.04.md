### Post by QIII on 2021-04-30
POL is a graphical front-end for Wine.  If you use POL to make things easier, you are using Wine in the background.

The best place to go to see if your application runs well is the app database at [https://www.winehq.org/](https://www.winehq.org/)

Apps are rated from "garbage" to Platinum.  But you have to bear in mind that your mileage may vary.
