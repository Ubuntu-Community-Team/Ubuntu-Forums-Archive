### Post by alien878 on 2018-04-23
I don't know of any headphones (analog) that will work with S/PDIF (digital).  You will probably need a S/PDIF to analog converter.  I don't think they are too expensive.  Just make sure they have the correct plugs as S/PDIF can be fiber or RCA.  Alternatively, if the headphone jack is working, why not use it?
