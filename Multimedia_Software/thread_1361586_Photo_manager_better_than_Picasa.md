### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-12-22
What else have you looked at?  There are several photo management apps available, including f-spot, digikam, gqview, even gthumb, but really it all depends on exactly what info you need and what you want of the search facilities.

Personally I prefer digikam for some things and f-spot for others, so swap around between them depending on what I'm doing.  I certainly prefer the folder management possibilities of digikam, but as it's a kde application, prefer the look and feel of f-spot.

Enjoy the choice!
