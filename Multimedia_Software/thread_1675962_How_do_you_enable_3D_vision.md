### Post by amjad22 on 2011-01-26
After installing the latest drivers (you will need v186.18 or higher for 3D Vision Discover), a shortcut will appear on your Start menu that allows you to disable/enable 3DVision Discover, but of course you can also access it via &#8220;C:\Program Files\NVIDIA Corporation\3D Vision&#8221;
