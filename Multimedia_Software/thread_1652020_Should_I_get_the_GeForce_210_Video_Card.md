### Post by gradinaruvasile on 2010-12-24
If you want a card for that kind of computer, buy at least a nvidia 220 or 240.

The 210 has 64-bit memory interface, the 220 and up has 128-bit. There is quite a bit of performance diffrence between the 64-bit ans 128-bit addressing - my old 7600GS with 128 MB RAM (128-bit) runs circles around a 210 with 512 MB RAM (64-bit) i tested once.
