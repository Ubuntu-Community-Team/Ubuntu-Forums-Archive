### Post by sikander3786 on 2012-05-23
VLC can do it, with or without mounting the images:

[http://askubuntu.com/questions/12546/watching-mounted-dvd-image-with-vlc](http://askubuntu.com/questions/12546/watching-mounted-dvd-image-with-vlc)

DVD support needs to be enabled on your Ubuntu install before any media player would play it:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs)

### Post by mister_playboy on 2012-05-24
You can also use VLC to play DVD copies in VIDEO_TS format.  Just open the VIDEO_TS.IFO file in the folder with VLC and it works exactly like an ISO file.
