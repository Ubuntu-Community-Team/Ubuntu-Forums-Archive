> **AGTG said:**
> If I open up Pulse Audio, I can see the meters are sensing the audio, but there's no sound coming out of my speakers.
Playback tab in Pulse Audio Volume Control can show activity, and volume slider can be set up, but if muted from panel icon, you still get no sound. Check that.

---

### Post by AGTG on 2015-12-26
The mute button wasn't on. After doing some searching, I found a thread that said that Pulse Audio drivers can become corrupt, and if you delete the program and reboot it will default back to regular settings.

This got my audio back, but now my original intent to record audio from the desktop is shot, unless I try to load up Pulse Audio again.

I will make another thread about recording audio from the desktop to see if there are other options. 

thanks for chiming in.
