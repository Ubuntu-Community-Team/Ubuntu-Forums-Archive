### Post by Pat500 on 2013-08-31
Hi all,
I've found AcidRip to be the best & easiest DVD Ripper in Ubuntu. I used to have a problem with it always adding subtitles to movies though. I found out how to stop AcidRip from adding the subtitles, and thought that I'd share how I did it. Just add the command "-nosub" (without the quotation marks) into the "Misc." box, in the "Other stuff" section in AcidRip, and it will stop doing the subtitles. Easy as, and it works!
Happy Ripping!          ):P
