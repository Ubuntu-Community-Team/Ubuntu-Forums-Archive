### Post by sedawk on 2009-09-24
Depending on your sound hardware (and your Ubuntu version)
your volume control might have a slider especially for
headphone volume.
So it might be possible to use a low "master" or "speaker"
volume and still get a good headphone volume.

On the other hand this is exactly what I wanted to do
yesterday with my netbook and there is no slider
to change the headphone volume (only mute/unmute in alsamixer).
