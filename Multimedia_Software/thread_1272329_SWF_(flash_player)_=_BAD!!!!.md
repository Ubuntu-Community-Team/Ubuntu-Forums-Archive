### Post by DreamsofanEagle on 2009-09-22
Ok, if you read my previous post (before the edit) I had flash issues. lets start with last night.....

I had finally gotten Ubuntu to burn onto a CD after 4 burns :/ and after some time toying around in the trial load which loaded automaticly, I installed Ubuntu, gotten it running around 8:30 or so after some 4 hours of non-stop trouble. I figured what the hay since Linux already had everything going, sound video and what not with no input from me. Saddly it caused nothing but issues, it was SWF.
