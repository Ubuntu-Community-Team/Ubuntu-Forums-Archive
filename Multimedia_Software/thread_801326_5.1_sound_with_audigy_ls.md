### Post by Daveth on 2008-05-31
try, in System/prefs/sound, altering the "autodetect" option to ALSA for "sound playback" and "music & movies". 
Fixed my 2.1 to 5.1 + full functioning sub tranaistion after upgrade to Hardy. This on an Audigy 2 card.
