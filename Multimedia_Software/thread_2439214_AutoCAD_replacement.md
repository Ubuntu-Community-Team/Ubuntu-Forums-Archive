### Post by kesetyan on 2020-04-04
Hi this isn't a replacement as such but it is a viewer and converter that might be useful.  As far as I know it is not in the Ubuntu repositories but can be downloaded from: [http://lx-viewer.sourceforge.net/](http://lx-viewer.sourceforge.net/).

You may also be interested in visiting this site: [https://dwg.winsite.com/linux/ ]("https://dwg.winsite.com/linux/")for a selection of possible solutions or alternatives. 

A fairly basic CAD program that is in the Ubuntu repositories is Dia but I suspect it is too basic for your needs.

I'm not sure if Autocad would run in Wine and if it did, it might not be as smooth as it would be in Windows.  The following website gives a few possible Linux Autocad alternatives: [https://www.ubuntupit.com/free-autocad-alternative-top-15-cad-software-for-linux/]("http://needs.") that might be worth investigating.

Good luck.

PS: you may get more response but putting your query in the 'General Help' category as Autocad is not strictly multi-media software and experts in computer aided design may miss your post if they are not interested in sound and video.
