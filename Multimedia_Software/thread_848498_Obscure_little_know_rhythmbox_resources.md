### Post by gsiliceo on 2008-07-03
While searching for a solution to my rhythmbox to itunes problem i stumble upon a few rhythmbox resources that could help you.
Here i make that list:

[Airmind plugins]("http://airmind.googlepages.com/rhythmboxplugins")
Here its included
**Capuchin**: a plug in manager for deskbar(see webpage for more information)
**Ratings Import/Export**(saved my life): it does what it says, import/exports your ratings from the rhythmbox library to the tags of the files, so you'll never lose em, kinda experimental.
**Cover Art Browser**: allows you to download and browse the cover art for your music albums.


**Itunes to rhythmbox scripts**: there are actually a few ones to do this, to export your itunes ratings to rhythmbox.

[One written in python found in google pages]("http://code.google.com/p/itunes-to-rhythmbox-ratings/")

[One written in perl from an unknown developer]("http://www.mythgaming.net/transferratingsperl.zip")

[Another one written in python found in the rhythmbox mailing lists.]("http://www.blergl.net/~jfortier/itunes2rhythm.py")

As always make a backup of your files before trying this scripts.

[Rhythmbox alarm plugin]("http://www.nedrebo.org/code/rhythmbox/alarm_clock/"): to start or stop music at any given time

[More rhythmbox plugins in this page you will find]("http://fabien.carrion.free.fr/Rhythmbox.html")
[B]Rhythmbox Cddb Plugin
Another Rhythmbox Alarm Plugin
Integration between Gnome Osd and Gaim
Integration between Gnome Osd and Pidgin[/B]

[And the official webpage with plugins.]("http://www.gnome.org/projects/rhythmbox/utils.html")

[Another list with even more plugins]("http://live.gnome.org/RhythmboxPlugins/ThirdParty")




Hope it worked for you.
