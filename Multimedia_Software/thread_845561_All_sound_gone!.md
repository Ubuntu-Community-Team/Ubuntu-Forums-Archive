Some thing along these lines happened to me while i was using the computer (didnt play with the sound)

Check the sound levels (and if you have muted it).Im new myself and using GNOME, but shouldnt be too different for sound.
Right click the volume control (if you have one on your panel. then make sure "mute" is not ticked. Then try going into "open volume control" (or similar if its reworded) and make sure any obvious sliders are not mute or at very low volume.

Apart from that, i can only suggest the really obvious such as make sure its turned on/ cables in back of pc not fallen out(or laptop). Also could be an application problem (just one movie player, or for all apps?)
