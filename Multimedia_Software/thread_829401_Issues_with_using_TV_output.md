### Post by Jose Catre-Vandis on 2008-06-14
Is it just in Firefox? If so, try setting your preferred font and telling firefox to use it:

Edit--> Preferences-->Content-->Advanced

Untick "Allow pages to choose their own fonts, yada yada"

See if that makes any difference. You can also play with fonts and sizes too.
