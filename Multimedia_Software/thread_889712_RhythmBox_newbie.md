### Post by damis648 on 2008-08-14
After starting up Rhythmbox, Click Edit>Preferences and then click the "Music" tab. Make sure the "Watch my Library for new files" checkbox is checked and, if it is already, reset it by unchecking and re-checking it. Also, make sure the field above it points to /home/[your username]/Music. Good luck!
