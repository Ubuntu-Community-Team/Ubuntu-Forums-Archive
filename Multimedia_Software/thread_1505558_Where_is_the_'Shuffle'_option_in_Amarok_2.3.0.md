### Post by malspa on 2010-07-22
Using Amarok 2.3.1 here.  This seems like a very strange thing, there's no "shuffle" button, really.  But at the top of the playlist, there's a right hashmark or whatever it's called (">").  If you hover the cursor over it, it says "Add a playlist sorting level."  I clicked on this, and clicked on "Random."  The playlist got resorted in random order, and the songs now play at random -- shuffled.
