### Post by beew on 2011-07-04
Hi, 

just go to firefox Tools > Addons and make sure that only Totem Quicktime addon is enabled. I find it to be the best for quicktime streaming such as Apple Trailers (better than the quicktime player for windows) On the other hand it is pretty useless for everything else. I use Totem for QuickTime streaming and gecko-mediaplayer for everything else by selectively enabling/disabling the addons (see screenshot)

BTW, you apear to have the gecko-mediaplayer addon for Quicktime enabled from your screenshot, it doesn't work (a version I install from PPA does stream quicktime but streaming is slow and quality is poor comparing to the Totem one). Disable that and use the Totem one.
