### Post by zpzpzp on 2011-01-31
Inspired by the following post:
[http://art.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10412012](http://art.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10412012)

I turned the volume of the microphone to 0 in alsamixer and saved the settings.

Now the microphone still works, and I do not hear my own voice or breathing in the speaker any more.

Problem solved.
