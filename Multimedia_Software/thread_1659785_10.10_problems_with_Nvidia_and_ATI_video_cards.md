### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2011-01-11
I would not get to concerned about the results from glxgears.
I don't think it is meant to be a metric/benchmark.
It shows you have openGL 3d driver running.

The fps changes as you change the size of the window..
The test involved is  very simple/small not like real world.
The fps changes if you enable sync to vertical blank.

If there is a problem with 10.10 & nvidia then it is nouveau kernel module.

Lots of people seem to be having EDID problems, not just nvidia ..

EDID over hdmi thru' HT amp (AVR) seems to be getting worse but this could just be increasing numbers of people using hdmi.
