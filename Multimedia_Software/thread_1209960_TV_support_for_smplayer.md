### Post by rvm4000 on 2009-07-10
Hi!

I'm adding support for TV in smplayer (mainly intended for dvb-t). I would be nice if some people test it before the new release.

You can find deb packages with TV support here:
[https://launchpad.net/~rvm/+archive/testing](https://launchpad.net/~rvm/+archive/testing)

(Anyway I'd recommend to compile smplayer from svn, as new things are added every day).

There's a document here that tries to explain the support for TV in smplayer:
[Watching_TV.txt](http://smplayer.svn.sourceforge.net/viewvc/smplayer/smplayer/trunk/Watching_TV.txt)

but summing up, if you already have a ~/.mplayer/channels.conf, the TV channels will appear automatically in the menus Open -> TV and Open -> Radio.

More info (and screenshots):
[http://smplayer.berlios.de/forums/viewtopic.php?id=1244](http://smplayer.berlios.de/forums/viewtopic.php?id=1244)
