### Post by markbuntu on 2008-12-14
My HD3650 1GB works great and performance improves with every driver release. The program is to drop support from fglrx as the radeonhd drivers become fully functional for each card. ATI is supporting the open source driver effort with cards, info and cash. The only hold up right now is the ati ip lawyers and time.

You can go to the Phoronix forums and ask Bridgeman, he is the ati guy in charge of the open source initiative and he is active there.
