### Post by Greg_Brown on 2011-06-23
It is possible, just not apparent.  There is a too small down arrow/triangle next to the button to move forward a track.  Click there, and you will see options to shuffle.  Once found it seems intuitive, but it was not so at first, especially since the repeat options are on the other side of the screen.  The help files don't mention it to my search ("shuffle" and "random").  A tooltip might be nice.
