### Post by handy on 2011-05-11
If you are lucky you may be able to salvage the card by cleaning its contacts.

To do so, remove the card from the machine, & rub the metal contact surface that sits in the slot with a pencil rubber.

It is amazing how many seemingly failed cards have been resurrected via this method.

Not that I wish to get your hopes up. But there is a chance it could bring your card back to life.
