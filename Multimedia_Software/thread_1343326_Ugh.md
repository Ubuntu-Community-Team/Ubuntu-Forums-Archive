### Post by BaddestCross on 2009-12-01
No need to download anything from ATi.  Open System->Administration->Hardware Drivers and if the ATi driver has not already been activated for your system there will be an option to do so from that screen.  Choose "Activate" and Ubuntu will download and install the needed driver for you.
