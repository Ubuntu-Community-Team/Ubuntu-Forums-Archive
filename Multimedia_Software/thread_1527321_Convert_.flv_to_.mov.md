### Post by skarme on 2010-07-09
> **anchorschmidt said:**
> How do you convert .flv videos to .mov format? I've searched but I couldn't find anything. I also tried to use openshot and kdenlive but they don't have the export option.
Install the Download Helper Plugin for Mozilla Firefox from [http://www.downloadhelper.net/](http://www.downloadhelper.net/).
There is an option for download and convert. Whichever video you are viewing can be downloaded and converted to the desired format.
In case you want to convert saved .flv files, this software should work for you. Found it in a forum, its called ConvertIT. 
To download the debian package, click on this link: [http://www.sciallo.net/modules/archivio/archivi/ConvertIT/convertit.deb](http://www.sciallo.net/modules/archivio/archivi/ConvertIT/convertit.deb) .
For more info on the software, click on [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=567016](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=567016) .
Hope this solves your issues.
