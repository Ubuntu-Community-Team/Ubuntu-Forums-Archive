### Post by VeeDubb on 2009-11-25
The only thought I have, is that the best use for the LinDVD disk, is to play frisbe with it whether your running 32bit or 64 bit.

I know that sounds  bit harsh, but it's true.

There are several (maybe a dozen or more) excellent A/V apps for linux which are vastly superior to LinDVD.  

All you need to do is make sure that the medibuntu and universe repositories are enabled, and install libdvdcss2

That will let you watch pretty much any DVD you want, encrypted or not, through just about any linux app you want.

It really is a much better solution.
