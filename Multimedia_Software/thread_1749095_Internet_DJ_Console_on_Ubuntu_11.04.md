### Post by StephenF on 2011-05-05
First read this:

[http://idjc.sourceforge.net/tutorials_icecast.html](http://idjc.sourceforge.net/tutorials_icecast.html)

Note at the end the Join the stream as a listener instructions. Just change localhost to your internet IP address and forward port 8000 on your router.

Help on that available here: [www.portforward.com](www.portforward.com)

The stream will be accessible to Windows *and* Linux listener clients.
