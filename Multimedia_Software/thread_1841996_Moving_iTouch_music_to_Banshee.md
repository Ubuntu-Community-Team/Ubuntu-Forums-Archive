### Post by dniMretsaM on 2011-09-10
You could use SSH to transfer all the songs out of the /var/mobile/Media/iTunes_Control/Music/ (requires jailbreaking). The names are different though so it would take a lot of work to organize it properly on your hard drive. The development version of libimobiledevice (1.1.1) has experimental backup support, I don't know if that could help you or not. Other than that, I don't know of a way to do what you need. It is possible to import iTunes libraries directly into many media players (including Banshee I believe), so it you still have access to the correct one, you might try that.
