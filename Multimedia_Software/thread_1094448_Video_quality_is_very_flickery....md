### Post by carm on 2009-03-12
I am no expert and new also to Linux, I have the same problem. You probably have an ATI graphics card. I have tried out some stuff. I do have compiz but I downloaded an icon for it and I can enable and disable the program as I wish. When it is disable stuff run smoother.. my screensaver runs good. When compiz is enable my screensaver is horrible like crazy. 

Bad for me is youtube (basically flash videos online).. lagging and flickering. Once again a little bit better when compiz is disable.

### Post by carm on 2009-03-12
Also in VLC, you can improve it by...............

Opening the Program
Hit Tool
Prefences
Extent Video (Left Side)
Hit Outmodules
and change video output module to... X11 Video Output
