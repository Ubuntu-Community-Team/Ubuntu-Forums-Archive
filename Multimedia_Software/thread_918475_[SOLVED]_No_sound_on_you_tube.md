### Post by Robertjm on 2008-09-28
Hi all,

I had the same problem after upgrading to FF v3.03. When I checked in Synaptic I did not have Flash 10 installed (even though videos WERE showing, which was odd).

After installing Flash 10 and the Flash 10 extensions it worked fine. From what I've read in another thread libflash was only intended for use with Flash 9, so if you're using v10, you shouldn't need it.
