### Post by kostkon on 2009-03-12
Since you are using 8.10 then set all the sound playbacks in your sound preferences (i.e. *System &#8594; Preferences &#8594; Sound*) to *Autodetect*, in case you have changed them.

And then follow [this guide]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=795525") to enable surround sound.
