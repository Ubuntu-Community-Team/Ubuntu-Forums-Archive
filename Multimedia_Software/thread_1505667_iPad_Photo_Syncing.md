### Post by moody_mark on 2011-03-02
The new 3rd gen iPod iPad and iPhone need an update to your libimobiledevice package, take a look at this link here

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1677258](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1677258)

Note this does not solve the OP question, sorry for going off topic. The only method I use at the moment to put photos onto the iPad is export pics from Fspot onto a USB connected hard drive in a caddy and then connect this to a windows box running iTunes. I then sync the iPad using this drive as a source for the pictures. It's clunky but it works.
