### Post by markbuntu on 2008-06-10
This is a common problem. That said, you should be able to just restart any app that uses sound and it should work after flash is finished. Since this does not seem to work for you, try changing your settings in System/Preferences/Sound to Alsa. You can also read the guides at the top of the Multimedia and Video section of the forums.

If you want a list of running/sleeping applications go to System/Administration/System Monitor. It can tell you that and more.
