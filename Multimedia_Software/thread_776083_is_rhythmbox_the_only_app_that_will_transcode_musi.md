### Post by BandD on 2008-04-30
Banshee should work.  Exaile will work with the correct plugin installed.  There are several stand alone apps that interface with ipods as well.  gtkpod being the most popular.  Try typing 'ipod' in the add/remove programs window and quite a few things pop up.  

Just for the record, it's not a format thing.  ipods can only play mp3, mp4 and acc files, those are the only formats that the apple firmware will handle.  The difficulty with ipods is that structure of the database.  They used hashed databases, which basically means they completely randomize where the songs are stored based on this hash.  So when you try to find your songs on an ipod it is virtually impossible!    That's why you can't just drag and drop music onto an ipod.  Apple wants complete control.
