### Post by nathanhillinbl on 2010-01-20
Do be aware that hardware acceleration through the video card isn't supported at the moment. On my laptop, which runs a Core 2 P7350 (Not too bad of a CPU), it can't keep up and doesn't play 1080p .mkv's without choppiness. I'm sure if you have an i7 or something like that, it may be better, but I'm at this moment converting a .mkv which I downloaded to a m4v of lower quality.

Hopefully support will come soon, as from what little I have been able to watch of 1080 on my laptop, it has been of INCREDIBLE quality.
