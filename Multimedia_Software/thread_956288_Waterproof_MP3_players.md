### Post by expatCM on 2008-10-23
I have not heard of any other products similar to the Aquabeat in the market yet.  I understand that there are some housings available for general mp3 players but I have not seen them at first hand.  The problem with the housings apparently is that they are quite bulky and that the ear buds are of questionable quality.

Whilst I do about 3km a day in the pool (90 minutes) I have not rushed out to buy a player.  I feel that having your ears free is important and I would also find it confusing to listen to music and do a lap count at the same time.  I also am a bit uncertain as to how much the wire to the ear buds would get in the way .....  If you wear a swim cap perhaps you could store the player under your cap and then shorten the exposed wires.

If however this is to be your incentive to swim regularly well go for it ...
