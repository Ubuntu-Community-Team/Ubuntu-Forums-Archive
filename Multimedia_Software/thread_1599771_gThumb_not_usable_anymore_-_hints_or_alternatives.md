### Post by mjc1 on 2010-10-18
You can import photos to any desired folder. They can be grouped into date-based subfolders if you want, but you don't have to.

After importing, gThumb shows a catalog view of the most-recently-imported photos. To go the the folder instead, right-click on a photo and select "Open Folder".

I personally don't like the catalog view, but it's no big deal to switch to the folder view.

The browser preview is gone. However, there is a thumbnail bar in the viewer - enable/disable with F8. That may or may not help you.
