They will only work with any measure of reliability if the audio recording is of the voice of you or someone else who has put in lots of time going through all the training excercises. Even then, if it's a rough source like a pocket memo recorder, expect many errors. I know it's unfortunate, but these programs have yet to learn a universal speech of sorts, just as translating programs have yet to think in a Chomsykite "universal grammar." They'll put out their best foot, but with no training... yeow.
