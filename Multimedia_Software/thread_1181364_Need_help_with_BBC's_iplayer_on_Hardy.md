### Post by drifter2502 on 2009-06-27
This is how I got things up and running.

To play all BBC Radio stations in ubuntu including Listen Again follow the instructions here ...[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683) . Download Downloadhelper ...[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/fir...helper&cat=all](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/fir...helper&cat=all) . Now go to BBC I Player (make sure you allow site in No Script or whatever)and select say Radio 4 listen live. Click on drop down on Downloadhelper icon in tool bar and download the the r4.ram file to your desk top. This should open Totem and play R4 listen live. If you want to 'Listen Again' you may find it better to select Low Bandwidth at the bottom of I Player window and it should load and play. I have all BBC national radio station ram icons on my desktop including a local station.
