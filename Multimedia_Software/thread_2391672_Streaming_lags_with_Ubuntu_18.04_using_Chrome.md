### Post by searchgurus007 on 2018-09-12
OK, so I just watched a youtube video of some guy who had the same issue.
Turns out, going into chrome settings and disabling hardware acceleration does the trick.
video now steaming properly and scrolling of the pages and such are much smoother.
worked on ubuntu 18.04 
lenovo x220

will try on my other systems later tonight.
