You might wish to try the plug in here:

For a complete list of Rhythmbox plug-ins you can look here:

[http://live.gnome.org/RhythmboxPlugins/ThirdParty#Album_art_search](http://live.gnome.org/RhythmboxPlugins/ThirdParty#Album_art_search)

#7: Desktop Art and
#33: Skype

both work very well in Ubuntu 10.04.1 LTS 64bit running Rhythmbox 13.0.
