### Post by Mud.Knee.Havoc on 2009-03-23
So that makes three great places to start... :)  I'd like to add that if you have a favorite radio station that streams online, you can often also add it to most of the more full-featured linux audio programs (like Quod Libet or Rhythmbox).  There are quite a few stations that offer online streams.

My favorite station started streaming with some flash player that made it deliberately difficult to listen to in a normal audio player.  Just because they tried so hard to force me to see their website ads as well as listen to their on-air ads, I took a good look at the packets and figured out the real stream. :)
