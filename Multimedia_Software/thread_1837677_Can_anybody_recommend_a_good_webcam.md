### Post by SeijiSensei on 2011-09-02
I use a [Logitech Webcam Pro 9000]("http://www.amazon.com/Logitech-720p-Webcam-Pro-9000/dp/B002M78ECK"). Works fine with Cheese and Skype after you tell PulseAudio to use the camera's microphone rather than microphone jack built into the motherboard.

### Post by lparsons42 on 2011-09-05
FWIW I picked up a low-end Logitech webcam, about $30 (sorry don't have the model number) and it works fine in skype in kubuntu.  Sits on top of the monitor and looks somewhat like a grey ping-pong ball.  Skype identifies it as "uvc camera 046d:0804".

In other words if all you want to do is skype, you don't need to spend much for a webcam.
