### Post by Jonathan Métillon on 2010-11-02
Just want to point your attention to this fantastic plug-in for Rhythmbox. No more copy-pasting URLs from Shoutcast.com!

[http://code.google.com/p/rhythmbox-shoutcast/](http://code.google.com/p/rhythmbox-shoutcast/)

Use the "Featured downloads" links to retrieve the plug-in and follow the install instructions at [http://code.google.com/p/rhythmbox-shoutcast/wiki/HOWTO_Install](http://code.google.com/p/rhythmbox-shoutcast/wiki/HOWTO_Install)

Really you need to quit and restart Rhythmbox after activating the plug-in.

Cheers!
