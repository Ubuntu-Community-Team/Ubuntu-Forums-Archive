In the xsane main window, click on "Window" and then on "Show advanced Options." This dialog contains predefined settings for scan size or you can manually change the width and height to whatever you like. 

You change to inches or other unit by clicking in the xsane main window on "View" and then by scrolling down to "Length unit."
