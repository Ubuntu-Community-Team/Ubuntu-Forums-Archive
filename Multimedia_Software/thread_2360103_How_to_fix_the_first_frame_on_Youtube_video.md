### Post by satimis on 2017-05-02
Hi all,

I got it done.  It is NOT complicate at all.

Steps:
1) Login my Youtube account and upload the video
2) After upload finished, click -> Advanced Settings
3) Click -> Video Manager
4) On next page there are 3 thumbnails displayed.  They are selected by Youtube.
5) The 1st thumbnail is the Title Page at the beginning of the video uploaded.  That is the cover page which I need.
6) Click the 1st thumbnail -> Set as thumbnail
7) Done
