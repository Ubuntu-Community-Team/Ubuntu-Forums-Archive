### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-07-16
Do you have libdvdcss2 installed?  Without it even vlc will not play many commercial DVDs as they may be encrypted.  That package will mean just about anything will play in vlc.  Have a look at the [Medibuntu]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu") pages and you will be able to get the package if you do not have it.
