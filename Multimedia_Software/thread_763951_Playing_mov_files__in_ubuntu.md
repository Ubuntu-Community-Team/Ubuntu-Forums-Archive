### Post by Simpotico on 2008-04-23
To access your Compiz settings click: System > Preferences > Advanced Desktop Effects Settings. This will open your CompizConfig Settings Manager. If you do not have this button installed, which I didn't, the click: Applications > Add/Remove and open your Add/Remove Applications screen. Search "Compiz" and install Advanced Desktop Effects Settings.

I hope this works for you. I'm still trying to tweak mine so who knows, it may just be a dead end...
