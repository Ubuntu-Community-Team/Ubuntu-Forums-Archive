### Post by mc4man on 2010-01-03
Probably not what you want to hear but....

dvdshrink running in wine is ideal for this, though it has to be a re-authored, no menu split. Generally movie only. but you can add add. titles to the 2nd disk, if extra room. ( for example an extra or 2, accessed by 'next title' or after movie end.

The advantage is it's easy to do and you can pick the exact (within 15 frames) to end 'part 1'. (used to add a little .vob to the end of part 1, sorta like an intermission video with music, kinda corny but fun..

Too late tonight if interested but I'll leave a few screens.

screen 2 - reauthor screen, moving the main movie over and removing unwanted screens, set compression to none to see new size (hidden by pop up
screen 3 - the split screen showing total chapters and size
screen 4 - ck.'ing out a split at chap.16, arrows move foward or back, looking for a good split
