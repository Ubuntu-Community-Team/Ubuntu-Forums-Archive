### Post by paulphillips on 2008-05-31
Open up a "home folder" from "Places" menu.
Go to "Edit->Preferences" and check the "Show Hidden and backup files" (and close window)
Click on top of "Name" column to sort your home directory files in alphabetical order.
Go down the list until you find the offending files
Select files and hit shift-delete.
