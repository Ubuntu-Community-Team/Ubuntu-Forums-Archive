### Post by ihatethetv on 2009-06-23
Well I've worked on this quite a bit.  I find that my nvidia 7600gt and 8600gt cards work pretty well.  I was also surprised at how well an AMD 780G chipset worked....then the problems started with that one...VLC would play then stutter, play then stutter.  

The real hard part for me to find has been a card that works consistently well with flash video (youtube, etc).  The 7600GT was probably the best I've had so far, but then a driver update came along and killed the good performance.  I've run into situations where the vesa (default) driver plays youtube much better than the accelerated one.  I've posted on here about it before, but haven't heard anything.

ATM I've got windows XP dual booting for when I need youtube...which sucks.  

If there was just one card that didn't blow with FLV, I'd probably be happy because most play HD content well enough in VLC etc... 

anyway, good luck and keep us posted if you find anything.
