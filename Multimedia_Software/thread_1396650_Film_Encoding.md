### Post by benmoran on 2010-02-02
Yeah, seconded. Handbrake is excellent. I use it all the time to do simple re-encodes for my phone. And the newest version accepts any video as input. 

If you want more advanced options, give avidemux a try.
For DVD ripping, OGM rip is also excellent. Despite the name, it does a variety of codecs and containers.
