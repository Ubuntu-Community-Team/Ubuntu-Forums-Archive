### Post by specvthis on 2009-12-15
boxee is by far the best all-around application for a HTPC project. The new beta is looking excellent and with it's app store will only get better. It can stream Revision3, youtube, and play mkv (with hardware accelerated decoding) easily. As of now I do not know its capability as a Bluray player. It also can play hulu (someone of a hackjob due to Hulu constraints), but is fully functional. It also has Netflix (for mac/windows), and tons of other apps. I think the MLB.tv boxee app is one of the best applications to come out for any HTPC in a long time. Finally a sports provider that gets it.
