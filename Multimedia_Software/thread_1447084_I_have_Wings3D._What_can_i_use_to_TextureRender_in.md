### Post by Alan James on 2010-04-05
I see what you are saying. Well as far as I know Blender is the only open source software that will do what you want. You would probably use it in conjunction with GIMP to create the textures.  
 

 I know for tent pole CG driven movies studios use Maya for modeling and either CinePaint, GIMP or Photoshop for texturing.
 

 If you have the money to buy it and time to learn it, Maya is a great piece of software. It runs on Windows, OS X and best of all Linux, however as I said before it is pricey ($3500 last I checked). If you want free it is best to go with Blender/GIMP for your 3D modeling and texturing.
