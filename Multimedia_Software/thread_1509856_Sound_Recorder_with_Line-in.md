### Post by Stonebow on 2010-06-16
Sound Recorder from the Ubuntu Download Centre works fine for in-line recording, but you may have to download plugins.  Synaptic Package Manager has a big selection of "pulse audio" software that you may need.  You also have to open System>Preferences>Sound to make sure you've got line-in and outputs configured properly, and to make sure that nothing has been muted before you start recording.
