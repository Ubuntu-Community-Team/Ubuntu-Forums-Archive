### Post by zZ0nN on 2012-03-25
After struggling a lot with streaming radio stations, I thought I might as well share my solution.
A playlist of a few South African radio stations. Just open with your favourite media player! ;)

It is quite easy to edit to support more radio stations, just open with a text editor and append:

FileX=mms://196.35.64.36/702_16  //Radio Station Streaming URL
TitleX=Talk Radio702                       //Name of Radio Station
LengthX=-1

Remember to change the X in FileX, TitleX and LenghtX, with the next iteration and update the NumberOfEntries field.
