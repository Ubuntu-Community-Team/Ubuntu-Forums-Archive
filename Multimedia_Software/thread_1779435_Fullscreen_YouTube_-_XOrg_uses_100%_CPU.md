### Post by Thucydides411 on 2011-06-10
I've been having problems with fullscreen Flash videos on and off for about a month now. The first peculiarity is that VLC and other desktop applications run fullscreen Flash videos smoothly, even when streaming from the YouTube URL. In both Firefox and Chrome, however, watching a fullscreen YouTube video brings my computer to its knees. I have an Nvidia GTX M460 graphics card, and no problems when running these videos in Windows, so hardware isn't the problem.

One peculiar thing I've noticed is that when I hit fullscreen, XOrg jumps from a very low percentage of CPU usage to 100%, and stays there. I get no more than 1-2 fps, and exiting fullscreen mode takes about 10-15 seconds.

I have flashplugin-installer, with version 10.3.181.22ubuntu0.11.04.1 installed. I'm running 64-bit Ubuntu 11.04.

I have two questions:
1. Why do Flash videos run so slowly in the browser, but smoothly in other applications?
2. Why does XOrg take up 100% of the CPU time in fullscreen mode?
