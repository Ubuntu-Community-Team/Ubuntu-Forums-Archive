Hey

I thought id just write a quick post about how i fixed my sound problems.

I had audio coming from windows but not from ubuntu intrepid. I spent days going through the forums trying to fix it and just by chance i did.

All i did was go to:

Volume Control > Switches

There is a check box names "External Amplifier"

On my system this box was checked. I assumed that it meant turn on the speakers so i could hear the audio without using headphones. I was wrong.

By chance i just unticked it whilst rhythembox was playing a song and voila. Problem solved.

If anybody could please explain what external amplifier actually does id appreciate it.

Hope this helps at least 1 person :)
