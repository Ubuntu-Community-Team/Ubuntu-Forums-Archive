### Post by hxcobd on 2010-04-16
Unfortunately, Flash performance is a rather strange beast in linux, as Flash 10 performs VERY poorly in linux compared to its Windows/OSX counterparts.

Some video cards/drivers perform better than others, though it does seem odd that adding a (I'm assuming) BETTER video card would result in WORSE performance.

First thing I'd do is uninstall Flash and go to Adobe's site to get the newest release. 10 has gotten more stable since it first came out.

If that doesn't help, I'd assess your video driver situation.

If that STILL doesn't help, there are a few "Flash alternatives" for linux worth trying.
