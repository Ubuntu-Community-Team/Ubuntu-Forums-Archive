### Post by Cracauer on 2008-11-21
Let's assume, purely hypothetical of course ;), that there's a user who needs to make some simple 3D models.  Tried Blender, books, tutorials and all, and failed miserably.

Is there a simpler 3D editor?

The models in question are nothing fancy. Just tiles mounted at angles and the only "complicated" things are a couple round surfaces and tubes that need to be broken up into rectangles later when a polygon count is known. Absolutely no need to do things like model a face using some mouse trick to pull out and shape a nose and ears with variable polygon count.
