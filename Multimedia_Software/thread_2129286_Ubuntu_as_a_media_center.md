### Post by frankcm3 on 2013-06-03
I used to use XBMC on Ubuntu and it worked pretty well. Now I use Plex on my Ubuntu media center and have a Vizio Costar(Google TV) hooked up to the TV. I run the Plex for Google TV app on that.  There is virtually no setup required.  Tell Plex what folders to scan on the media center and they automatically appear in the app on the TV.  It also automatically pulls all of the cover art and the metadata.  The Costar also has Netflix built in but I don't use that so I can't vouch for it.  I spent hours and hours tinkering with XBMC.  Plex is also "wife" friendly.
