### Post by ron999 on 2010-10-08
Hi
Use VLC to make a playlist.

View > Playlist.
Then right-click in the window and 'Add file...' or 'Add directory...'

Then save the playlist.
Media > Save playlist to file...

Save the playlist as **swimming.m3u**

Then play the playlist with VLC.
