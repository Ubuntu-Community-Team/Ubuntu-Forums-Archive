### Post by needhelppeeps on 2010-02-23
I was having a time finding something similar to Windows Movie Maker for Ubuntu. I needed to make a slide show with pictures and sound. I think it is possible to do it with AViDemux but it's a pain because it won't auto fix the resolutions for you and such. Several other video editors either had no obvious way to do this or crashed all the time

I finally found imagination which is perfect. Just add the pics, add the sound, and export as an flv or vob file. It's about as easy to use as Windows Movie Maker and does not crash on my machine even when working with a large data set. I offically am windows free at this point and can do 100% of what I need in ubuntu.
