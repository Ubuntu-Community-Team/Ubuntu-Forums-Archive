### Post by tropnevad on 2009-02-08
Hey nmcry, i have had the exact same problem with you, however my sound worked on a fresh install on 8.10, but as soon as i downloaded the updates it stoped working on my laptop..  However in my case i struggled for some time to try get it working,  

What i did was when my headphones where pluged in i went to the audio mixer, and then look through all of the dropdown sections, in my case it was HDA intel, and reltek ALCxxx.. and then in one of them look for PCM volume control and my PCM 2 under the realtek ALC section was muted.. i unmuted and then my headphones worked!

Hope that can help you
