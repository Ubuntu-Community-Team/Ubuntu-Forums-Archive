### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-07-11
What exactly are CBT nuggets and what file type are they, assuming they are files and not some other strange term for something unknown to me.

OK, a quick search shows me they are streamed videos of Windows Media Player v9 format, so you should be able to see them by installing either the **ubuntu-restricted-extras** or enabling [medibuntu]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu") repos and installing **w32codecs** and **libdvdcss2**.  They should then show in firefox with whatever video player plugin you have.
