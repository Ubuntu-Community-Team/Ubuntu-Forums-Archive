### Post by Scott Baker on 2012-01-18
Go to [http://www.openoffice.org/](http://www.openoffice.org/). Click downloads. Click get Open Office.org extensions, then look for the following; OOoBlankLabels. This package has what you're after. (***NOTE, in the last several months, since OOo has gone in it's direction, and Libre office has gone its way, the OOo website seem to be hit or miss. Sometimes it will open right away and work fine. Other times, it won't even open, leaving you with an error message. If it doesn't work for you right away, try again later, it will EVENTUALLY work***)
You can also consider installing the G-Labels package, which is found in the software center. I believe I saw the labels that you're looking for in that package, but I wouldn't swear to it. :confused:
