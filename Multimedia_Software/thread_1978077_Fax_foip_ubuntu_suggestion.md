### Post by JBeardsley on 2012-07-10
If you need to fax than two times a day and you don&#8217;t want to buy a fax machine or have a dedicated phone line, using an []("http://www.faxcompare.com/")[online fax]("http://www.faxcompare.com/#whatis") service (I included an explanatory link in case someone doesn't know what online fax is) is a cheap way to send many documents until Ubuntu adds a fax feature.  Online fax services give you a dedicated fax number and let you receive and send faxes through an online dashboard and your email.  These email-to-fax services are especially useful if your clients/employers prefer to receive documents via fax to maintain confidentiality.
