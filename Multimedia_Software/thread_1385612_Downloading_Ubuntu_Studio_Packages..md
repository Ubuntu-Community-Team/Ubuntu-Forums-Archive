Well, it is quite a large amount of software. The multimedia production forum is where you should go for questions and answers about ubuntustudio. There are also some wiki pages and a bunch of how-tos scattered around the interweb. You should find them and read them first before doing anything, especially anything about setting up jack which is absolutely critical to having a good experience.
