### Post by markbuntu on 2008-06-10
Turn off the machine, take the card out and plug it back in. Check that the bios recognizes the new card by choosing setup in the initial boot menu and looking at what it says. You should also check that your on board video is disabled there if you have it.

Also, that card will not work properly on a 300w power supply.

Other than that Ubuntu should recognize that card right out of the box and then ask you if you want to get the restricted driver.
