### Post by cozmicharlie on 2008-05-18
It sounds like Rythmbox is reading from the file name.  I assume thse are mp3's.  In Rythmbox add your music then in Rythmbox you right click on the file go to properties and change/add the information (artist etc).  If you use a program such as easytag you simply add the information artist - title etc.  Then Rythmbox should read the tag.  You may want to do a little reading just to familiarize yourself with tagging - this will get you started ([http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tag_editor](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tag_editor)).

Enjoy!
