### Post by mocha on 2010-03-16
Did you install Gutenprint?  I got rid of my stylus photo due to daily head clogging and switched to an HP all in one model.  HP printers use the open source HPLIP driver which works extremely well.  The HP's do borderless 4x6 on regular 4x6 paper without problems.  There is an option in their print dialog that says "full bleed" which means borderless.  I swear the epson gutenprint dialog had a similar option.
