### Post by bcschmerker on 2010-12-27
Unfortunately, most PCS devices are NOT designed for direct access to their on-board camera CCD's (if any) via the Datalink port, as I found with several Nokia®, Samsung®, and Motorola® cellphones; I know of no workarounds as of 27 December 2010 due to the closed-source nature of most PCS firmware.  Ye're probably better served with a dedicated USB Video Class camera such as the [Logitech® Webcam C500](http://www.logitech.com/).
