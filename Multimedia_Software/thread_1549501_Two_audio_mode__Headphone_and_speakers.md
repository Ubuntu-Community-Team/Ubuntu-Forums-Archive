### Post by flick on 2010-08-09
With my headphones plugged in, playing music, I clicked on the speaker icon in my top panel. Clicked on Sound Preferences. Clicked/put a checkmark in the Mute checkbox at the top, by the Output Volume slider. No more sound from my headphones though the music player I was using ( Rhythmbox ) continued to play. Unplugged headphones. No sound from computer's main speakers. No annoyance to those around me. Plugged headphones back in, unchecked the Mute, sound again.
