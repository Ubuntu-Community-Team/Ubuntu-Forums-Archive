### Post by TheFu on 2015-05-29
youtube-dl
and there are a few plugins for XMBC/Kodi which do this for many popular video sites.  Check out FreeCable, Navi-X, and tools like that. Some of these sites may not be legal in all places around the world, but they are in many.
Normal video download browser extensions work with the easier sites - "video download helper", for example.
