### Post by lockettpots on 2009-06-16
Hi all

As I was having loads of problems with Soundmax onboard sound, not just in Linux but in my Windoze partition as well (something to do with a windows update which screwed things up) I took the plunge, disabled onboard sound from BIOS and fitted a very cheap Cmedia CMI8738 card (£13 from Maplins labelled ProSound) In Jaunty playback worked out of the box and with very little tweaking I could record with my microphone:D

Oh and by the way with an update to the latest driver it worked great in Windoze too.

So if anyone out there is getting frustrated with Soundmax take the plunge and get rid of it!
