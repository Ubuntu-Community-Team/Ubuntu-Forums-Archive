### Post by TheFu on 2018-05-21
I use me-tv.  My USB-TV stick doesn't work with VLC, at least not easily.  VLC does work with networked TV tuners from Silicon Dust that support DLNA, however.

In many parts of the world, the types of broadcasts used have changed, so it may be that a TV tuner from 5-10 yrs ago won't work anymore where you are located.  Lots of places have switched from analog to digital broadcasts and a few have moved from gen1  to gen2 broadcast signals, which aren't backwards compatible.
