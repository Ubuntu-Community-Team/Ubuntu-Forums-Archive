### Post by DJ Scribblinni on 2008-10-18
Ever heard of Pandora?  [www.pandora.com]("htt://www.pandora.com")

It is not exactly what you are looking for.  But it is an Internet Radio that lets you create your own stations to listen to music similar to what artists and/or music you feed it.  You can provide ratings(thumbs up/down) to individual songs in a particular station that you like/dislike which will help the Music Genome Project determine the next best song.  Can't help but check it out.
