### Post by Favux on 2012-09-22
Hi jvilla1983,

Any of the Wacom tablets should work out of the box for you.  From what you say I'm guessing a Wacom Bamboo Pen and Touch might be what you are looking for.  Maybe the Pen only model?  If so I'd get it fairly quickly as the new models are probably coming out soon, and it might be a few months before they are supported.

Or you can get one of the non-wacom tablets.  Aipteks have their own driver.  Others are described on the DIGI*mend* site.  Here's the [tablet support page]("sourceforge.net/apps/mediawiki/digimend/index.php?title=Tablet_support_status").
