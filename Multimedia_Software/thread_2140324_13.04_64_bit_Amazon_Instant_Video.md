### Post by aaronrumple on 2013-05-01
Confirmed working 13.04 64bit Chrominum.
Did the following...

sudo mkdir /etc/hal/fdi/preprobe
sudo mkdir /etc/hal/fdi/information
/usr/sbin/hald --daemon=yes --verbose=yes
rm -rf  ~/.adobe

That did the trick.
