Are we talking HD or standard definition television on the note of what you plan on using to play it back on?

HD TV's are more or less a monitor so if running from a PC there is little to no issues with whether say PAL or NTSC, standard definition is a totally different animal, but still good practice to format to your region.

AVC H264 can be a really good choice regardless of the video being standard or high definition. Another option is ogm video format. Some claim it is comparable to AVC H264 but never used it myself.
