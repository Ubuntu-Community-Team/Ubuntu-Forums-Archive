### Post by cottfcfan on 2012-09-26
The mp3 file isn't "thrown" anywhere, it will always be downloaded to and stay in the folder you specify in your web browser, "Downloads" is the Default.
The easiest way to deal with this is to move all your mp3s to music & specify music as your default folder in Banshee.
Then you just need to Rescan your Library in Banshee everytime you move new mp3s in.
To have Banshee open mp3s by Default, just find any mp3 file, right click it, click "properties", at the top choose "open with" highlight Banshee & click "set as default".
All your mp3 files should now open with Banshee.
