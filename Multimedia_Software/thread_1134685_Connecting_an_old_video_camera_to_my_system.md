### Post by ieee488 on 2009-04-23
Your option is probably with the audio/video jacks. From your description it is mono not stereo sound.

You'll need a video capture card. I don't use one so I don't know which one works with Ubuntu.

Personally, I'd just buy a standalone DVD recorder and be done with it. Record straight to DVD-R. If you want to edit the captured video, record it to DVD-RW and edit on your PC.
