### Post by varunendra on 2011-04-16
I am an absolute idiot on sound issues, but have come across HDMI a while ago, so here's what I got:

HDMI audio is associated with the HDMI port on your motherboard (you must have one if you have this option). It is the advanced Audio/Video interface beside the traditional VGA port. So you can use this audio only when you connect the HDMI port to a device (like plasma TV) which is HDMI capable.

About its quality, I don't think it is any better than regular high definition audio.

End of story :).
