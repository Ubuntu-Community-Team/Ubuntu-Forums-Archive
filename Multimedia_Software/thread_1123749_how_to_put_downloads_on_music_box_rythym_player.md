### Post by ad_267 on 2009-04-12
Yes you could do this many ways. The obvious way is to move your downloaded music into your Music directory once it's finished downloading, but I guess you want to keep seeding the files. 

You can change the Music directory used by Rhythmbox to the one where your torrents are downloaded in the Rhythmbox preferences instead of the Music directory. You can also go to Music - Import Folder to import the folder where your torrents are downloaded to. You could also change the settings of your torrent application to download torrents to your music directory. 

The best option would probably be to create a link in your Music directory to the directory where your torrents are downloaded to, so rhythmbox will automatically update its library with your new downloads. You can browse to your torrent download directory, right click on it and select "Make Link" then drag that into your Music directory.
