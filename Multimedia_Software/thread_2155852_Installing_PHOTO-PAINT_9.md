### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-06-19
There are a few points worth making about your question.
[LIST]
[*]Ubuntu 10.04 and 10.10 are both now out of support, so you should really update, whichever one you are using, as no more security updates will appear for either OS.
[*]Corel Photo-Paint 9 is now a very old application, and probably not worth persuing.  It appears to have been around in the year 2000.
[*]Have you looked at alternative open source applications that might do the same job, eg gimp, inkscape, etc.
[*]What do you want the application to do for you; is it to draw images or to edit existing ones?
[/LIST]
