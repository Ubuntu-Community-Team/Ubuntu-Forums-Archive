### Post by houseworkshy on 2010-01-04
A quick way is to highlight the program in the drop down menu, then drag it to the tool bar at the top ( where the browser, email and help icons already are on a default installation ) or bottom ( where the destop icons and trash icons are at default instalation ).  That should put an icon there.  To remove it again just right click and "remove from panel".
