### Post by pszpak on 2009-08-18
I recently purchased a Radeon 4770 and installed the newest Catalyst driver (9.8) on Jaunty. I had a lot of trepidation in going the ATI root (especially when reading these forums) but it works great. With compiz enabled and emerald installed my desktop is snappy and looks good. I have noticed no artefacts or annoying delays and DVD/video playback is perfect.   I'm on a single monitor. 

One tip I have for newer Radeon card owners: Do not use the restricted driver offered by ubuntu, it will work, but has a nasty little graphic from AMD saying the hardware is not supported and removing it after installation is a pain. Download and manually install the newest driver from ATI/AMD here: [http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/linux/Pages/radeon_linux.aspx?type=2.4.1&product=2.4.1.3.40&lang=English](http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/linux/Pages/radeon_linux.aspx?type=2.4.1&product=2.4.1.3.40&lang=English). 

It will work perfectly, or at least it did for me.
