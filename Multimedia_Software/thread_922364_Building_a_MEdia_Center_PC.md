### Post by paulg on 2008-09-17
See the capture card support articles on the [mythtv wiki]("http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Category:Video_capture_cards"). There are many well documented cards that should just work. Some of the wiki entries have old information or information specific to other distributions but should give you an idea of the level of configuration required.

For analogue SD TV the Hauppage PVR 150, 350 and 500 all look nice. I have an ATI TV Wonder that works alright but I wouldn't recommend rushing out and buying one. Silicondust HDHomeRun looks very interesting for HD signals.
