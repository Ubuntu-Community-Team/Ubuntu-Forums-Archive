### Post by cotcot on 2009-11-29
Kino is an easy, simple and rock stable editor that replies best to your requirements especially on your frame-by-frame demand. The timeline shows each frame separately. For extracting a jpg from a clip : go to "timeline" , select the clip and click the "save still frame" button. Kino is also well known to grab the clips from your camera. Kino is in the default repositories.
Other editors are fine as well; more possibilities, less easy to learn but compensated with more possibilities, some are not by default in package repositories etc.
