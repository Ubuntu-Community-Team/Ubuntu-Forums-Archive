### Post by markbuntu on 2008-08-15
For the moment, OSS4 seems to have figured out the X-FI cards while alsa has not.

The backstory is that creative, after trying and failing miserably at making linux drivers for their cards, released their information to the open source developers and OSS4 has won the race for support for X-FI cards.

In any case, it requires work to get this card to operate.There is an OSS4 thread in this forum. You should check it out.
