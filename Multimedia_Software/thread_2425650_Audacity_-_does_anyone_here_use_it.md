### Post by CatKiller on 2019-08-28
Audacity is a sound editor. It doesn't know anything at all about videos. You could use a video editor - or a command-line tool like ffmpeg - to combine your video with your new audio.

### Post by Dennis N on 2019-08-28
> [I]t only wants to export the sound, can i not export the video with the new sound applied to the old video?
Audacity is only an audio editor. Audacity doesn't import any video content with the audio, so can't export video. 
You need a video editor to edit the audio in a video.
