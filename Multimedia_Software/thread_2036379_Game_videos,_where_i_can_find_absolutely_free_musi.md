### Post by evilsoup on 2012-08-01
[Jamendo]("http://www.jamendo.com") offer most (all?) of the music in their catalogue on various creative commons licences. Most of those will allow you to use the music in any non-profit way you want (so long as you credit the artist), a few of them might allow you to use the music even in commercial videos for free. It all depends on the specific license for each song.
