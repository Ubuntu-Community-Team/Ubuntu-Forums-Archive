### Post by eaglemob on 2010-04-23
> **bfh80 said:**
> Im using the 32-bit and I have both installed I think how would i check to make sure

To check go to:
System->administration->synaptic package manager
And search for *Flashplugin-nonfree and Flashplugin-installer*.
If they are green on the left side , then they are installed.

You can all ways reinstall again, to do that you remove *Mark for complete removal*,
And then reinstall it again.
