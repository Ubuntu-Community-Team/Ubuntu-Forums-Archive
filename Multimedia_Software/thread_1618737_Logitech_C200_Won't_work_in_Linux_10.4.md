### Post by necrosymbiont on 2010-11-26
I'm using 10.04 and I *thought* my Logitech C200 wasn't working because the light doesn't come on, and nothing happened automatically.

What you need to do is open up Synaptic and search for things like webcam and uvc (uvc is the name of the webcam driver). Have a browse at all the packages available and install whatever you think you might be useful/fun.

In particular, I recommend guvcview, at least to demonstrate to yourself that your webcam does work.
