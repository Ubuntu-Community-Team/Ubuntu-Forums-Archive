### Post by Citadel1980 on 2009-12-03
I think Miro's default is to download HD over SD. I ended up looking for podcasts and if I find 2 of the (supposed) same I add each as a feed and then look at their download size. If I want the SD feed I remove the HD feed.

It's not a good answer but it works. Make sure you then export your feeds so you can have a backup.
