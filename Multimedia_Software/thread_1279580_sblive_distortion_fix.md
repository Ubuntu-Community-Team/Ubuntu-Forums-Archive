Hi all,
I have an oldie but goodie sblive running under 9.04, The problem was raising the bass and/or treble volume sliders past 1/3 would result in distortion. In prior releases the fix was to lower the wave slider but the sound channel doesn't seem to be routed through it. 

The fix for me was to install gamix and lower the EMU10k1 PCM sliders. 

This use to be available by selecting it in the gnome mixer preferences or under the alsamixer.. but I didn't see the option in either.

hope this helps
