### Post by PorcupineTREE on 2011-02-03
Guys after extensive research I found two wonderful apps that allow me to do what I was asking for. The apps are:

1. Remote 4 Media Player (Remote4MP)
   Lets you control Rhythmbox, XBMC, XMMS2 and VLC players over Wi-Fi.

2. MPoD
   Its a remote for MPD (Music Player Deamon)over Wi-Fi as well but you must have an MPD server running in your home for this to work. Other than that is another great app from the
   AppStore.

Hope others who are searching for the same thing find this useful. Cheers to those who viewed this threat to try and help out :) case closed. SOLVED!

### Post by christiaan_ on 2013-02-05
The two best media players on Ubuntu is XBMC and VLC. Both are capable of playing and managing music as well.

Sybu Data has two apps on the app store that are free and have no advertising. Both work perfectly with Ubuntu and the VLC app has a Ubuntu Host Configuration Helper application to make the setup and configuration very easy.

The two remote control apps are :

- [Remote Control for VLC ]("http://www.sybu.co.za/wp/projects/iphone-ipad-remote-control-for-vlc/")
- [Sybu Remote Control for XBMC]("http://www.sybu.co.za/wp/projects/xbmc/")
