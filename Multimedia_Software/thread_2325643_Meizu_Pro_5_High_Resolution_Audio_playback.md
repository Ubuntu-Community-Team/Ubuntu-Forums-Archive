The Meizu Pro 5 has a very good ESS DAC inside which is capable of playing back high resolution audio, even DSD!
I did a test but unfortunately the current "Ubuntu Music App" doesn't make any use of the capabilities of this High End ESS DAC..

On this website you can download high resolution test music files: [http://www.2l.no/hires/](http://www.2l.no/hires/)

It would be great if this "high-resolution audio" functionality could be put on the roadmap, because the Meizu Pro 5 is a nice high-end telephone with
great potential, and this way: Ubuntu Touch + Meizu Pro 5 could become a real Audiophile Music Player!

Who can help? and what have to be done to accomplish this?
