What kind of advice are you looking for, hardware, software?

I've been using MeTV as a recorder for about 3 years on a PC at the top of the house that doesn't get used for much else. Because wifi coverage is not good up there, I just copy recorded programs via a memory stick to my media computer and play them back through our main TV.

As it works, I've left Ubuntu at 10.04 and MeTV at v1.1.2 and not bothered updating. I did write an app in Gambas to start-up and shut down the pc based upon my recording times. But I think that broke with a MeTV update, and I haven't had the time or inclination to fix it. Now I just turn the computer on before the program starts, set the record feature (which starts the recording 5 mins before, and stops 10 mins after) and then try to remember to power the damn thing off before I go to bed.


I'm using a Digital TV card, rather than a USB device.

We often miss programs AND forget to record them....so thank goodness for BBC iPlayer!
