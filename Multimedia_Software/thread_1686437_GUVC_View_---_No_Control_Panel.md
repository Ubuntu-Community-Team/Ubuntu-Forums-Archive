### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2011-02-15
You need to click & drag the tiny gadget in the middle of bottom border of the window just below the scroll gadget.
The button controls section has been minimized down.. 
Drag this up & the buttons will appear..

The normal dialogue/form/window does not have the scroll gadget in the bottom border.

Have seen this with Thunderbird address book window/form.
