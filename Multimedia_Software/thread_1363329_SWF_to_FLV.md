### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-12-24
The two formats are completely different classes - SWF contains instructions to the Flash Player to generate imagery and interact with events, and FLV is just a plain video format.

I think Adobe Flash (the multimedia authoring package) can export your SWF files to a video by rendering each frame individually out to a video file.
