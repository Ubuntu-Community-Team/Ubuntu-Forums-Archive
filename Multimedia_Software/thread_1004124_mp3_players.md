### Post by banana jama on 2008-12-06
creative mp3 players work great with ubuntu through rhythmbox. just make sure to turn on the plugin so rhythm can recoginize the mp3 player. go to applications--->sound and video----> rhythmbox--->edit--->plughins----> and make sure portable players MTP is checked.

theres one downside through if you have large amounts of music on your zen it is difficult to transfer your creative library to your computer. i have a 4gb creative zen with 600 songs on it. after my vista crashed i switch to ubuntu but realize that ubuntu doesn't recoginze my creative zen unless rhytmbox is open also i do not see a way to transfer large amounts of music to your computer library. other than that creative mp3players are great.
