### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2011-01-03
Depends on video decode or openGL performance is what you want..

For best VIDEO decode acceleration you need:
 - Nvidia of course
 - GT220 or better
 - feature set C (or better)

GT430 is recommended.
beware the GTX family is not feature set C

If openGL is your goal I do not have a recommendation.
The openGL performance is better on fast GPU/more RAM
