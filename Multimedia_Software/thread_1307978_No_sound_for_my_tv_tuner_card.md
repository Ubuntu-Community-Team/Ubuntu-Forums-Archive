### Post by MIJ-VI on 2009-11-19
The audio in your machine may be muted by default under Ubuntu 9.10 (it is for line level inputs).

In Terminal:

$ alsamixer

<RETURN>

and you'll see this (the 'M' key mutes & un-mute the audio channels): [http://www.talkbass.com/forum/showpost.php?p=8259080&postcount=16](http://www.talkbass.com/forum/showpost.php?p=8259080&postcount=16)
