### Post by FakeOutdoorsman on 2009-01-19
Your computer actually shuts down and powers off during the encoding?  Encoding is often CPU intensive.  If your CPU overheats it could cause the computer to shut down.  What is your ffmpeg command and the full ffmpeg output?
