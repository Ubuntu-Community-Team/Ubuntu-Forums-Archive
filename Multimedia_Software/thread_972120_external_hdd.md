### Post by Therion on 2008-11-05
You just want your media handler to use your external hard drive for your music library?

Easy... I do that.  Rhythmbox is what I use but I would think just about any good media handler would do the same.  

Look at maybe **songbird** and **amarok** as well.

Edit: Just re-read your post.  iTunes is what's fouling you up.  Some media handlers don't like the folder system that iTunes creates.  It's rather... Unique to iTunes.  **Rhythmbox** can handle it easily.  I don't know about any other media player though.  You'd just have to try them or ask in the forums.  Songbird is pretty much iTunes for Linux though, so I'd start there if you really like iTunes for Windows.  That or Rhythmbox, since I know Rythmbox can deal with the iTunes folder arrangement.
