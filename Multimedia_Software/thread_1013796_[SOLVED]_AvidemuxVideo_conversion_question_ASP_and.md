### Post by eye208 on 2008-12-17
The MPEG-4 specification defines two video encoding formats:

SP/ASP = (Advanced) Simple Profile
AVC = Advanced Video Coding, aka H.264

H.263 is a predecessor of H.264 but not part of the MPEG-4 suite (although some of its features went into SP/ASP). The old Flash video format was based on H.263.

DivX 5/6 and Xvid 1.x are MPEG-4 ASP codecs.
DivX 7 and x264 are MPEG-4 AVC codecs.

AVC is more efficient than ASP in terms of compression ratio but requires more processing power to encode and decode.
