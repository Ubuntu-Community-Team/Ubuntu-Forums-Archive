### Post by radi0j0hn on 2008-08-04
Scratchy can mean that the recording sampling rate is lower than you think.
You could be recording at so-called AM radio quality or even telephone quality.  Or you could be overdriving the audio and getting clipping?

BTW, I took a tutorial I published on digital audio recording and made it into a series of podcasts (free) if anyone is interested.  Find it on acpress dot com...my site.
