### Post by TheFu on 2015-04-04
Not sure I understand the question ...

Are you trying to create new subtitles and sync those to a video/audio stream?
Or
Do you have some that are off at the start by a fixed amount of time - just need to shift all of them?
Or
Do you have a frame rate issue and need to progressively shift 25fps for 30fps input or vice versa?
Or .... 
Are we talking about SRT files and not image-based VobSub or Pgm files?

srttool - is an answer for some of these.
subplay, subs, subtitle2pgm, subtitle2vobsub are other tools.  

Sometimes a GUI is easier when there is a framerate issue. Gnome Subtitles is the tool for that.
