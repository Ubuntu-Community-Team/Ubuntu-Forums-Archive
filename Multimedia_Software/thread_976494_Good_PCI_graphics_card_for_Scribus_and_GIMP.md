### Post by bertd2 on 2009-11-05
In my experience, Scribus and GIMP do not need more video power than that offered by all on-board video chipsets I've seen in recent years. If GIMP and Scribus are killing your performance, you'ld probably do well to check got other bottlenecks first. If you have less than 1GB of RAM, you should get more memory: that's the most common culprit.
