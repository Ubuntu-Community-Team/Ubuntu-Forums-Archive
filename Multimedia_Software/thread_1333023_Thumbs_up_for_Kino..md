### Post by NJC on 2009-11-21
I had typically had poor luck with Linux video editors. I've tried Kdenlive, Avidemux and Kino (and probably others I've forgotten). I've only used Kino once before for a few mins and don't have video editing experience. But I wanted to mash 2 videos togethers - one mp4 and the other flv. 

Kino was PERFECT for this editing. I dragged the videos into a window, imported and easily edited out the pertinent sections before Exporting the 2 videos into one mpeg file. I'm impressed - very easy and intuitive for editing work.
