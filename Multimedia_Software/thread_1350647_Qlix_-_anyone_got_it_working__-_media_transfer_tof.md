It crashes every time I try to use it under Kubuntu 9.10 on a desktop and netbook.  My experience comes closest to that reported by Inspired on 27 Feb 2009 in [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1082037&highlight=qlix](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1082037&highlight=qlix)

Most of the time it fails to close and Kwin has to kill it.
