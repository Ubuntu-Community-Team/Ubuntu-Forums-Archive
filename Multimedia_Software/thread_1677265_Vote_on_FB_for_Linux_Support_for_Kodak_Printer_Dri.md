### Post by MDSBigPaPa on 2011-01-30
The reason I'm so interested in a Kodak printer is the PC World article a few years ago that tested manufacturers inks against third party manufactures. [http://www.pcworld.com/article/147208/headtohead_printer_manufacturers_ink_vs_cheap_thirdparty_ink.html](http://www.pcworld.com/article/147208/headtohead_printer_manufacturers_ink_vs_cheap_thirdparty_ink.html) In this study the images from Kodak printers with Kodak printer cartridges were consistantly better than any other manufacturer.   Kodak uses dyes rather than inks so the images are sharper and far out last the reciprocity of other manufacture's inks and paper not to mention the poor results of third party printer cartridges.  Kodak printer cartridges are also considerably cheaper than other manufacturers. - another reason for my purchasing decision.
