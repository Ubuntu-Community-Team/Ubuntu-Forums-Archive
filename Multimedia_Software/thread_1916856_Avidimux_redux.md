### Post by papibe on 2012-02-05
I would do this:

[LIST=1]
[*]Install ubuntu-restricted-extras.
[*]Install ffmpeg with the h264 codecs (check [this]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1117283&highlight=ffmpeg")).
[*]Install w32codecs.
[*]Use ffmpeg to convert wmv to mp4.
[/LIST]
Alternatively, you can install mplayer and use mencoder to convert it.

Just some thoughts.
Regards.
