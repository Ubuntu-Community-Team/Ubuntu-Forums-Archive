### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-02-09
I'm not sure what you mean by "online" and "offline" - do you mean linear and non-linear?

There are a couple of non-linear video editors for Linux, but you may be disappointed if you're expecting to do professional work on them (which, I assume, is the reason why you mentioned two pro-level editors?). Here's a quick list:

Kdenlive
Open Movie Editor
Cinelerra
Diva (for DV footage)
Pitivi
Blender (3D animation package but can do video editing too)
Lives

Give them a try during non-work hours. If you find them wanting, then give Adobe a call and ask them to port Premiere or something to Linux.
