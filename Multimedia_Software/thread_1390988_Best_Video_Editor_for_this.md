### Post by LuridCinema on 2010-01-26
Cinelerra has the most features ( definate learning curve ) , Openshot, Lives & Kdenlive next with some FX and other features.  All of these editors do not like to have different formats on the input side as they are horrible converters. Convert to a format they can easily handle first like .MOV and same resolution with ffmpeg first so they will be more stable is my best suggestion. Cinelerra is the most powerful and requires the most effort to learn, depends on your experience w/ Video editing.


If you want to add effects and the like go with one of the above... If you want simple go with one below.

Pitivi can take most any format in and do simple cut & paste & trimming along with fades and the like. Kino is similar to Pitivi and it will encode everyting into dv before importing to the program.
