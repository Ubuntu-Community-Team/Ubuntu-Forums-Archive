But with direct vendor support for various components for my year old lapbook not readily available or out of date, for me it became a protracted treasure hunt trying to get things up and running.  When the external VGA port on my laptop failed to produce readable output at a not-for-profit meeting I was presenting at, that was a sign it's time to move back to Windows.  Ubuntu will of course continue to mature, and I may try Wubi at some point, but for now I need something that works out of the box and Ubuntu for me isn't there yet.
