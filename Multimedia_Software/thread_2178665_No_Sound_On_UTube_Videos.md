### Post by wbee on 2013-10-04
Solved.

The problem is with U Tube. When you look at the bottom left line under the video screen,you will see a red "X' beside the speaker icon,indicating the video is muted.<p>Use your mouse to move the slider to the right and that disengages the mute function. The updates download was coincidence.
