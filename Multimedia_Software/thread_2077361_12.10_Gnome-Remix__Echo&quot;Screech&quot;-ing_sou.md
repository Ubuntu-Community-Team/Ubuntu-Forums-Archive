### Post by jaiks on 2012-10-28
I did some further research and found this :

[http://askubuntu.com/questions/157891/skype-and-vlc-sounds-sizzle-distorted-bad](http://askubuntu.com/questions/157891/skype-and-vlc-sounds-sizzle-distorted-bad)

Basically, modifying /etc/pulse/default.pa 

and adding "tsched=0" to
load-module module-udev-detect  solved the problem!
