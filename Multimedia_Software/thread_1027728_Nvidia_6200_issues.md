### Post by ubersolid on 2009-01-02
Hi, I have the same card and it is working just fine. You should uninstall the driver that envy installed for you, and then get rid of envy too.
For the installation of the correct nvidia driver go to 
*System -> Administration -> Hardware Drivers* and choose driver version 177 (recommended). Then reboot.
