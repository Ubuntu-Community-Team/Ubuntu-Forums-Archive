### Post by mikezila on 2009-05-27
Pretty much any nVidia card.  If you don't care so much about 3D game performance then a GeForce4MX440 would be perfect.  It's got more than enough power for simple stuff, and they were like $100 max when they came out, so you should be able to find one for like $20 now.

I would avoid any ATi cards.  The support is terrible for older cards, no matter what drivers you use it's a huge farce.
