### Post by Niniel on 2009-05-02
That most certainly works. 
Just browse to the /media folder, your hd will be in there, unless you mounted it somewhere other than the default location. 
For me, it's /media/Games/Music, where "Games" is the name of another HD in my computer (NTFS) and "Music" is the folder with, you won't believe it!, my music collection. :)
