### Post by ronnielsen1 on 2010-03-27
How ready is it? Will the older Nvidia cards (96 series) work.

> 
[LIST]
[*][KernelModeSetting]("http://nouveau.freedesktop.org/wiki/KernelModeSetting")  (KMS) is mandatory 
[*]Suspend  and resume is working for many chips 
[*]2-D support is in fairly good shape with EXA  acceleration, Xv and [Randr12]("http://nouveau.freedesktop.org/wiki/Randr12")  (dual-head, rotations, etc.).  
[*]Any 3-D functionality that might exist is still  unsupported. **Do not ask** for instructions to try it. But  you can read [GalliumHowto]("http://nouveau.freedesktop.org/wiki/GalliumHowto")  in case you are brave enough. 
[/LIST]
