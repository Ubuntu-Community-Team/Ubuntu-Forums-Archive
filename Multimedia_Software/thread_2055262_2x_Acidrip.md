### Post by bestdarncomputers on 2012-09-09
I figured out a way to get two of them open at the same time.

If you have a disc in one drive, have the video in in que and ready to go, click start, then quickly click the acidrip icon again, it will bring up a second window before the first window has a chance to come up. select the other drive dvd1 in my case, and have at it.

### Post by robert shearer on 2012-09-09
I am presuming that what you want is to have two instances of **acidrip** open at once and select a different source drive in each...?

To open **two instances** first open one and then go to the Unity launcher where it's icon will now be showing and **middle click** on the icon to open a second instance of the same app.

Works for all other apps too... :-)
