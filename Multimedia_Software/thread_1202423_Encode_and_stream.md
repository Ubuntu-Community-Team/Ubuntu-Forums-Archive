### Post by gobberpooper on 2009-07-22
Well one would be for them to use something other than VLC  such as MPlayer.

Another would be to use something like Flowplayer and embed that into an HTML page once you've converted to .flv

Your third option is to, instead of having to download the flash player after converting, use the HTML 5 <video> tag. But that's still working out the kinks and has some issues and requires the latest browser but it's worth looking into
