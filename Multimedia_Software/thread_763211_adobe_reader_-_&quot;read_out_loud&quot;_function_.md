### Post by Mr_VeRiTy on 2011-02-21
I know it's been two years since this question was asked but this is one of the top results when searching "read out loud adobe reader ubuntu" so I thought I'd say how I got mine to work. I installed festival and espeak and then installed the latest adobe reader from a tarball (Just un-install acroread from synaptic, download the .tar.gz from adobe and then double click the file "INSTALL" in the folder, select run in terminal and then answer the questions it asks you.) Now mine works, but sounds a little robot-ey.
