### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-04-20
That means that there are no restricted video drivers available that will work with your Ubuntu version and your video card/chipset.

Since you didn't bother to tell us the make and model of your video card/chipset, open a terminal and enter "lspci".  Look for the line containing "VGA" -- and post that information back here.

If you're using one of the "legacy" ATI cards or an Intel chipset, there are NO restricted video drivers available.
