### Post by gordintoronto on 2013-05-26
Tell us about your physical setup. You have earphones/speakers/a TV set, plugged into the back of the computer/the front of the computer/the HDMI port.

There's a speaker icon, probably near the top-right of your screen. Click it and select Sound Settings. Select the Hardware and Profile you want to use, then the Output tab, and "connector." At each place, make sure the sound is "ON" and not muted. The Test Speakers button is handy. (At least 90% of the time when there is no sound, it is muted. Sadly, there are multiple places where you can mute sound in Ubuntu.)

What program do you run which should produce sound?

BTW, I find Google much better than trying to search within the forums. A typical search: HP G62 no sound linux solved
