### Post by jhomie1 on 2010-11-28
Hi, this is a reoccurring  problem for many ubuntu users but it's very simple to fix.

1) go on a video streaming website that uses flash(preferably youtube.com)

2) Play a video

3) Right-click on the video screen go down to where it says settings.

4) Then under display uncheck hardware acceleration.

5) Enjoy!
