### Post by sybille on 2011-04-16
Hi,

Once you've added a text box and some text, right click in the text box, and choose "Position and Size..." from the right-click menu. A dialog box should appear, and you should see a tab called "Rotation." That's where you can change the rotation - to 180° for upside-down, for example.

If you don't see "Position and Size..." when right-clicking, make sure the mouse pointer is within the area of the text box.

Hope that works for you.
