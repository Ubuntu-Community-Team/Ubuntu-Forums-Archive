### Post by lasconic on 2012-12-13
MuseScore is the right tool for the job and it's pretty easy to use. Check this tutorial [http://musescore.org/en/node/11723](http://musescore.org/en/node/11723)

On a related note, there are a lot of leadsheets on [http://wikifonia.org](http://wikifonia.org) and [http://musescore.com](http://musescore.com) and you can download them in a format compatible with MuseScore (being MSCZ or MusicXML)
