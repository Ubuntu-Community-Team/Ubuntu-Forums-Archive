### Post by dawiba on 2011-03-11
Well I found out why I couldn't play music previews in Amazon.

In Banshee's Preferences, I had selected 'Sound Menu Integration for Banshee' under the 'Extensions' tab. Using that to open Banshee from the sound menu, it wouldn't play previews in the Amazon Store.

However, after opening Banshee from Applications->Sound and Video, Banshee would play previews in the Amazon Store. After creating a launcher and adding that to the panel, then using that to launch Banshee, also meant that previews would play.
