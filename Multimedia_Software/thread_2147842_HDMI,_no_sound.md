### Post by p-dh on 2013-05-23
You can install **pavucontrol** (eg. **sudo apt-get install pavucontrol**) and change the HDMI setting under output. This program will give you much better access to ALL of your channels - analog, digital, etc. My daughter has to use it from time to time on her Dell to get sound through HDMI.
