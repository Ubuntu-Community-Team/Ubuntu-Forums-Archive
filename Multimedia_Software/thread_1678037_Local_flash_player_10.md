### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-01-29
Adobe flash 10 is available for ubuntu.  The best way for firefox is now probably the add-on, **flash-aid** from forum member **lovinglinux**.  It will find what is already installed, remove any conflicting applications such as gnash, then download and install the latest adobe flash.  It will even get the beta 10.2 version if you want, which I am using, and it's a good one.

Brilliant  add-on!!!
