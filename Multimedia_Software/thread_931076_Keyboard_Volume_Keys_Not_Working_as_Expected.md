### Post by kostkon on 2008-12-03
Try to go to sound preferences (*System &#8594; Preferences &#8594; Sound*), select the first tab, and in the *Default Mixer Tracks* section make sure that your sound card is selected in the *Device* drop-down menu and the *Master* volume is selected in the list below.

Hope that helps.
