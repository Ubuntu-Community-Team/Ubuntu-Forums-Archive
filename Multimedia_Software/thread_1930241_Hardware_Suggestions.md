### Post by dave0109 on 2012-02-25
Interestingly my fave supplier shows that Asus as no longer available.  

Anyway, I'm no hardware expert, but I did recently do a new build with another Asus Z68 socket 1155 board.  I've had issues with the Intel HD Audio on it, which I thought I'd sorted with a work around, but still occasionally get problems - just a quick glitch or drop out... but it would be annoying in the middle of a movie, especially playing through an AV amp.

Video wise, the onboard graphics pretty much do the job.  I can play HD video at full 1080 and haven't noticed any issues.  That said, I haven't played a lot on this system yet.  Most stuff is put onto the PVR and played on the TV.
