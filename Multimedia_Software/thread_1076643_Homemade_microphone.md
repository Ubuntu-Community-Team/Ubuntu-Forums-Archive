### Post by lswb on 2009-02-21
The original style of microphone (like from Alexander Graham Bell's day and still in use today) for a telephone is unlike anything that will work with the microphone input jack on a computer or other electronic device. Many or perhaps most telephones these days use an inductive type microphone may or may not be electrically compatible. And there are piezo style microphones also, they generally require specifically designed circuitry as well.

A cheap microhone designed to work with computers, tape recorders, etc. will make your experimenting a lot easier. Chances are good that everything will "just work" if you have a compatible microphone.
