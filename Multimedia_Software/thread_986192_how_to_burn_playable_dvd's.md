### Post by wylfing on 2008-11-18
DVD authoring is something of a black art (even on Windows). Although it seems difficult at first, I would actually recommend trying [tovid]("http://tovid.wikia.com/wiki/Tovid_Wiki") and todisc. They are command-line tools but it's really fairly easy to understand their use given the friendly documentation on the tovid wiki.

### Post by Sweet Spot on 2008-11-18
This is easy as humble pie. If you're using Gnome, just right click on an ISO file and choose "burn to disc".

Yes, it's that easy. 

I back up my DVD's using DVD Shrink (under WINE) because it is by FAR, the best and easiest way to compress/rip a DVD.  When it is finished backing up (choose to save as ISO) right click and burn away.
