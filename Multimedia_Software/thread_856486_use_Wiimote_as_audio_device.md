### Post by Stochastic on 2008-07-11
I haven't come across anything personally to get communication through the speaker, but for extending the capabilities of the wiimote, check out this PureData external: [http://mikewoz.com/index.php?page=pd-stuff](http://mikewoz.com/index.php?page=pd-stuff)
I did a project with it where synthesis was controlled by the pitch of the controller, filters were controlled by the roll, and amplitude was controlled by the yaw; I also had various buttons mapped to trigger (via MIDI sent to QSynth) playback of sounfonts.
