### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-12-15
Videos in ogg format will play with no added codecs, or you could use Linux Mint DVD version which has all codecs on the live DVD.

You could also try a live CD of several other distros which have codecs in them as the default, eg Mepis, CentOS, PCLinuxOS.
