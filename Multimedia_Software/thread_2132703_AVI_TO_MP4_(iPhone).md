### Post by evilsoup on 2013-04-05
HandBrake is the best anything-to-MP4 converter you'll find. IIRC it even has presets for the iPhone.

You'll have to install it from [this PPA](https://launchpad.net/~stebbins/+archive/handbrake-releases) - it isn't in the 12.04 repositories, and the version in the 12.10+ repos is crippled.
