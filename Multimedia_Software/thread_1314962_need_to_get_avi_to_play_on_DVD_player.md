### Post by disturbed1 on 2009-11-04
Winff converted the files to dvd format, you then need to author a DVD. Look for DVDStyler or something similar.

DeVeDe is a one click (maybe 3 clicks ;) ) application that can import your video file, and output a DVD iso image ready to burn.

[http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&safe=off&q=linux+dvd+authoring&aq=0&oq=linux+dvd+autho&aqi=g6g-m1](http://www.google.com/search?hl=en&safe=off&q=linux+dvd+authoring&aq=0&oq=linux+dvd+autho&aqi=g6g-m1)
