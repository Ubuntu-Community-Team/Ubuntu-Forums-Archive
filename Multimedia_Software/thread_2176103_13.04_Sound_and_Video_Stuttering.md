### Post by Ekkoria on 2013-09-22
Hello,

Ever since I upgraded to 13.04 on my laptop, my sound and video stutter occasionally - the video will freeze for a fraction of a second, and the sound will cut out for the same amount of time. This happens about once every few minutes. At first I worked under the assumption that pulse audio was causing my problems, but after uninstalling it and using strictly alsa I have the same problems, although the sound stuttering sounds a little different. 

At this point I really have no idea where to start in troubleshooting this issue further, and I would really appreciate some help!

I have an ASUS laptop, with Intel HDA audio and Intel graphics, if that's any help.

---

### Post by Ekkoria on 2013-09-23
I solved this problem myself. It turned out that my problem was caused by the Intel graphics drivers found at [https://01.org/linuxgraphics/](https://01.org/linuxgraphics/) and I uninstalled them by following the instructions here: [http://askubuntu.com/questions/343390/how-to-remove-intels-open-source-technology-center-driver](http://askubuntu.com/questions/343390/how-to-remove-intels-open-source-technology-center-driver) and viola! My problem was gone.
