### Post by alibabahck on 2009-04-30
I had similar problems with my ATI card which was probably due to a conflict with the proprietary drivers. I just ran 
```
$ sudo apt-get remove --purge xorg-driver-fglrx
```
in the terminal and the problem was solved. I found this in
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver")
