### Post by mprince on 2010-05-31
If you are still interested. Some of the codecs allow disabling the black border. Theora does. When you choose 'Render Project' click on the 'Modify' button (so you could change the export codec). Then at the very bottom click the 'Video Codec Settings' button. Look for the option called 'Border' and change it to 'No border'.

This doesn't always work though. Hopefully, it will be improved. I'd like to be able to switch borders off globally-- regardless of the codec.

### Post by splicerr on 2010-05-31
Yeah I had that too at first.  Turns out that you have to change the project settings before hitting the render button. Changing the project settings from the render dialog won't work. If you get black borders in the video window, you'll also get black borders in your output video.
