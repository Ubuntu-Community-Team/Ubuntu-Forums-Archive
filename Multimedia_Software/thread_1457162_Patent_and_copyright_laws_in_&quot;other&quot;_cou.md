### Post by quixote on 2010-04-19
Well, I'm pretty sure libdvdcss is illegal in the US, although I'm at least equally sure that would never stand up to a court challenge.  There's no simple instructions on any of this stuff I ever heard of.  I think that's the whole point.  If you're not rich enough to have a legal department, you don't count.

One possible solution, if you're worried, is to run your OS from a 16GB SDcard.  Then either mail it to your next destination, or pack it with your toothpaste or something.  If they want you to boot your computer at the border, it then just boots into its plain-vanilla OS.
