### Post by mhgsys on 2010-05-24
Well; You could start with installing a nice supported videocard; f;e most nvidia cards run quite nice on ubuntu..

However; A cheaper option/workaround is to disable hardware acceleration in the flash settings... 

That's what works for me on my laptop; containing a sis 671 card, with no 3d drivers.
On all other pc's here flash runs fine, also in fullscreen.,Most of them have a nvidia card, except the ati radeon one, .. but I can't really recommend the ati card though,,.
