### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-01-15
recordMyDesktop has been suffering this problem since Ubuntu 10.04. What I do is to convert the video to avi before uploading, using mencoder.

Better than that, you could jump into the WebM bandwagon and convert your video to webm. You can do that with ffmpeg compiled with support for webm. See [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=9441742&postcount=2](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=9441742&postcount=2)
