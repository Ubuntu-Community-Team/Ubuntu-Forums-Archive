### Post by adempewolff on 2009-08-10
the adobe flash plugin for linux has a tendency to become a runaway process and use most of the CPU.  This is probably your problem.  I fixed it by upgrading to 64-bit Ubuntu, the beta 64-bit flash plugin that adobe provides is much more stable than the 32 bit one and faster from my experience.  This was one of the main reasons I upgraded to 64-bit linux.
