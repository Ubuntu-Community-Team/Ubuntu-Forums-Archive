### Post by Macintosh SE on 2009-04-16
I recommend DeVeDe, which can be found in the software repositories. It will create an .iso file from nearly any video file. The video will play in most DVD players when burned on a DVD. DeVeDe also allows for the creation of simple DVD menus.

To burn the .iso, place it in the CD/DVD Creator folder under the Places menu and choose "Create From Image" or something similar.
