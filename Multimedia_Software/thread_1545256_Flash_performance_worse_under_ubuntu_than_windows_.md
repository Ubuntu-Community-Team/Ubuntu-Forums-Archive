### Post by Sneekster on 2010-08-05
I have Xubuntu 10.04 on an old Celeron 2.2Ghz machine with half a gig of ram and a 64mb Geforce card. It plays full screen avis just fine, but the flash plugin from the repositories was extremely choppy - unwatchable. I tried all sorts of Greasemonkey scripts to invoke external player plugins with the same result. Gnash was no better.

As a last result I took everything off, went to YouTube and let it tell me I needed Flash. I followed the link to the Adobe website and let it install the deb Flash player (10 point rc something). Hey presto. Flash plays properly. In the random world of Firefox, Youtube, cookie bugs and Xubuntu what worked for me might not work for you, but give it a go.
