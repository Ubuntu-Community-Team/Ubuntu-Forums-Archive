### Post by davidgillen on 2011-06-28
It's a bug in Banshee. If you're looking to RIP your CDs you can do it with sound-juicer ( install via synaptic, apt-get, or whatever your preferred method is ). This will give you an option when there are multiple possibilities to pick whichever you feel is correct.
E.g. I just did it with Pink Floyd's The Wall CD1 and was given about 20 different options, mostly all identical, picked one, and it's ripping away.
