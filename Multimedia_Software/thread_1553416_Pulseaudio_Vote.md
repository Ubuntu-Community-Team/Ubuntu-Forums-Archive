### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2010-08-15
Depends on hardware. Briefly, assuming you don't need pulseaudio's other features, your other options for mixing:
Hardware mixing - great if you have it (and a good driver), but most people don't
ALSA's software mixer (dmix) - not as good as a properly configured, working pulseaudio
OSS4 with vmix - great if it works, but doesn't support a lot of the intel-hda quirks that ALSA does
Deprecated stuff (arts and esound) - I don't hear too much of arts anymore and you have my deepest sympathies if you're using esound
