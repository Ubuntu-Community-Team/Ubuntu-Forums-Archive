### Post by kurja on 2015-07-01
Yes, you can use patterns in gimp. [http://docs.gimp.org/en/gimp-concepts-patterns.html](http://docs.gimp.org/en/gimp-concepts-patterns.html)

Quick and dirty way to use those would be to open up the image you want to use as a pattern, select all and ctrl+c, then edit -> paste as -> new pattern. Now in bucket fill tool options you can choose pattern fill with your new pattern to fill out your selection with (if that's what you wanted to do).
