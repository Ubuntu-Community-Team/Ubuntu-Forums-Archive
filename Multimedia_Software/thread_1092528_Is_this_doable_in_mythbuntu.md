### Post by issih on 2009-03-12
Not sure myth can quite do this yet, its almost there in that there are plugins to do real time transcoding and streaming of any recordings (dependent on the pokiness of your myth server), but not live tv yet.

You can presumeably work around things by scheduling a recording of whatever you want to watch, waiting a couple of minutes then streaming the ongoing recording, but its not yet a seamless process.

I could be wrong, but thats where I thought things were
