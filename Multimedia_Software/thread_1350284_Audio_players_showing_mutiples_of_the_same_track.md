### Post by LuisGMarine on 2009-12-09
I couldn't tell you exactly why it's happening, but I have felt your frustration with this issue. The best thing that I can recommend is that you install Sonbird.  You can follow the link in my signature to install it on your system.

Once you get songbird installed.  You want to make sure that all your files are copied and managed by Songbird.  You can turn this option by going into:
 **Tools > Options > Manage Music** and click on "Turn Manage Music On."  

Songbird works like iTunes in the way that it will only remove files from you computer unless they are copied over to it's own folder.  The other good benifit of doing this, is that everytime you change the metadata on your songs, it will auto rename, file, and sort your songs.  It helps trust me, keeping 30,000 song library in order with proper file names is more than you can ask for.

Also when you install it, download this plugin for it.  This is the magic maker.  It will identify, and eradicate all your duplicate & ghost tracks.  This is a huge money maker.

[http://addons.songbirdnest.com/addon/216]("http://addons.songbirdnest.com/addon/216")

Hope this helps, best of luck!
