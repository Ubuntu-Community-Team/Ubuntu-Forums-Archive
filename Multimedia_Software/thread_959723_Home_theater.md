### Post by aeiah on 2008-10-26
there's mythtv and there's linuxmce. mythtv is more established and probably the way to go but you should check both of them out i suppose. mythbuntu is quite good apparently, and you may find its the best way to use mythtv. as for your system specs, you will probably struggle playing HD stuff with that cpu, and you'll also probably struggle getting it to stay cool and silent, but you might as well give it a try.

if you go on the mythtv website they should have a long list of compatible tv cards and remote controls.
