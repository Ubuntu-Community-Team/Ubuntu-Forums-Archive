### Post by mankygitt on 2008-05-13
You will need to install restricted codecs: I used those from the Medibuntu repository.

For DVD playback, I find the best tool for the job on 7.10 is OGLE.
You can find and install this via synaptic. 
Ogle understands the DVD menu format, where MPlayer will just play the feature (once the codecs are installed)

None of the options are as simple and bug free as the Win or Mac options as yet, but I see that the launchpad contains several discussions about the importance of DVD playback support, along with other must-haves, such as better dual monitor support.

I'm no programmer, so it's a wait and watch game. I test and feedback on these forums as my contribution.
