### Post by winh8r on 2012-03-01
In VLC you are not limited to using the slider to control the playback of the DVD.

There are functions which enable you to speed up and slow down playback, advance frame by frame, all using dedicated buttons and sub menus rather than the slider.

That being said, I am still having a bit of difficulty understanding why you have a problem with VLC, it does after all fulfil its purpose of playing a DVD, which to most people would consist of inserting the DVD , hitting play and watching the DVD.

You seem to have a desire to "skip around" the DVD which was probably not in the remit of the developers as they were setting out to develop a multimedia player,which they have done.

If you need to access only short sections of a DVD then perhaps you need to be using editing software rather than simple playing software.
