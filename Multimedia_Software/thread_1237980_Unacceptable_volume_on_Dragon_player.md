I've posted this before and seen no response:

I have a laptop.  It actually has reasonably loud speakers.  Not great quality, but loud enough that they can be too loud.

I'm trying to watch movies with Dragon player.  I have the DeCSS etc installed so it all works fine.

Sound quality is fine.

But... the sound volume is too low.

Movies are now doing a better job of setting their audio levels to take better advantage of the higher dynamic range possible with improved sound systems these days.

But those of us with laptops don't have those improved systems.  We have low-dynamic-range speakers.  We need a volume boost to be able to listen to modern movies.

Mplayer itself can do it fine:

mplayer -softvol -softvol-max 3000 dvd://1 

But what about in Dragon?

This isn't just "I wish", this is, "Dragon is useless for playing movies because it can't put out adequate levels of volume".

Yes my speakers are at 100% and it's nearly impossible to hear anything.

I have good hearing.
