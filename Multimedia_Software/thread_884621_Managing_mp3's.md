### Post by ProbablyX on 2008-08-09
The ratings should be stored either in your home directory in a hidden folder called .banshee or in the directory where your music is in.

As you (unless you are running as root) can't access anything outside your home directory, banshee has to write there. And as upgrading ubuntu only affects files outside your home dir, it shouldnt touch the ratings.
