### Post by Icarus315 on 2010-01-15
Have you seen: [This Page]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu")?  Or more to the point, have you enabled the Medibuntu repository and added the libdvdcss2 package which allows you to deal with encrypted DVD's?  This package is in Medibuntu because of some laws like the DMCA in the US which make it illegal there.  It's silly and panders to special interest but that is the situation right now.  Of course that won't stop you from installing it, just be aware of it.
