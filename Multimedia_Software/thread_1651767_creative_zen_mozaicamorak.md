### Post by samfuzz on 2010-12-23
it seems creative zen doesn't support id3v2.4 tag

3 solutions :
1 - ask for creative if there 're firmware update that support id3v2.4 tag (released since 2003)

2 - add id3v1.1 tag to your files but long field will be truncated
(limited to only 30 characters)

3 - convert your mp3 tag to id3v2.3 with tools like kid3-qt, easytag, eyeD3 (cli) ....
