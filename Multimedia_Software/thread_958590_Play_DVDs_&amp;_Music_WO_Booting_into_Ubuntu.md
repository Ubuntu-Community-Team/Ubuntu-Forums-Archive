### Post by netyire on 2008-10-25
The media player, as mentioned above, is nothing but a specially modified linux operating system (probably packaged with mplayer and chucked on the hard drive with a customized boot menu).

It's definitely copyable, and hence doable on other non-dell systems too.

Check out GeeXboX, it should foot the bill here (plays practically everything and boots from a cd-rom drive, thereby bypassing the operating system on the hard disk). Doesn't make any changes to your computer either.

Screenshots here: [http://geexbox.org/en/screenshot.html](http://geexbox.org/en/screenshot.html)
Download it here: [http://geexbox.org/en/index.html](http://geexbox.org/en/index.html)
