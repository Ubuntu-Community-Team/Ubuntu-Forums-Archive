### Post by sv87411 on 2010-03-26
Some sort of audio server with a hardware player like SqueezeBox would suit. It depends on your budget though.

[http://www.logitechsqueezebox.com/](http://www.logitechsqueezebox.com/)

The newer players are quite expensive, but the (free) server software still supports the older V1/V2 SqueezeBox players which were wireless and can probably be picked up cheaply second hand now.

I use one of the original V2 players and the sound is fantastic and it's wireless too.
