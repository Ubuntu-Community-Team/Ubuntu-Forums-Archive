### Post by Desert Sailor on 2010-07-12
I am having a difficult time recording my Vinyl to Digital.  I have a USBTT10 USB Turntable with Audacity ver 1.3.12 (beta) running on Ubuntu 10.4.  

I have no problem getting the turntable connected, and getting play through to my speakers.  BUT, after starting the recording, Audacity works perfectly for 2 mins and 35 seconds (2:35) Then it just stops recording.  

Audacity says I have enough disk for 204 hours 12 min of recording, but I can't get any thing to record past 2:35.

### Post by Desert Sailor on 2010-07-14
Found the problem, but it wasn't what I would have thought.  Apparently there is a bug in the new beta version which requires shutting off "Play-Through"  I got the tip from the Audacity Forum board.  

Once I disabled the play-through on my install everything worked fine.  I hope it's something they fix as they work through the new release.  

Anyway...thanks for your help.
