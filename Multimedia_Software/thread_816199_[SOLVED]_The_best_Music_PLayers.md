### Post by Dylnuge on 2008-06-02
Banshee is great, and so is Amorak.

I find that Banshee is simple and works, whereas Amorak has a lot of extra features like lyrics.

I prefer Banshee, because it runs smooth and 1.0 looks great.

Also, you are coping in much higher quality sound formats. Banshee gives you a choice in what to rip/burn in, I recommend Vorbis (for high quality, low file sizes) or FLAC (for seamless quality, high file sizes).
