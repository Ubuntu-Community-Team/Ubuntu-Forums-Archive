### Post by Carl H on 2008-08-15
I've had the same problem with children and non-child-proof DVDs, and I've used AcidRip before now. It's also good for removing all the guff at the start of DVDs that can't be fast-forwarded. 

As an aside.... use fragrance free babywipes to clean your original DVDs. Start at the middle and wipe in a straight line to the edge. This has revitalised quite a few DVDs that I thought were scrap. (The scented wipes seem to leave a tiny residue on the disc, which upsets the player).
