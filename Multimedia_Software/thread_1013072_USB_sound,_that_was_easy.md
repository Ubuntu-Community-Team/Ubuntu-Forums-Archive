### Post by studo77 on 2008-12-16
I bought an eee box a little ago, and it is great, except for audio.
I knew it wass not possible to use anything but USB devices for the machine, but the power savings alone did it for me.

BUT i missed my quality audio, even though i had an old soundblaster, it delivered good audio.

So i just received an Audiotrak Maya EX5. And it was plug and play.

Connected the coax to my speakers, choose Autodetect in audio settings....

and it played! And it played great! Again Ubuntu(Linux) amazes me.

This is how simple it should be.
