### Post by vanadium on 2011-01-29
> **BarryDocks said:**
> I want to rip all my CDs to flac at the lowest compression (space is not an issue) via Banshee.
Why would you want to? The only reason you would want to do that is if your computer is very slow: lowest compression will indeed be the fastest compression. Sound quality wise, it won't make a difference.

Advice: leave it at the default if you do not care about file space. 1) You probably won't significantly notice a difference in compression speed between default and lowest 2) if you notice, you compress only a single time, whereas the gained disk space is something you enjoy for a longer time (even though it is not an issue for you now).
