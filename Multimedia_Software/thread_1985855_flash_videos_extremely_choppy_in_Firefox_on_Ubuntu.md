### Post by Eddie Wilson on 2012-05-24
Chrome and Chromium has built in Flash support. Firefox does not. Adobe will not be supporting Flash on Linux any more in the future, I believe it has to do with the quality of developers in the Adobe environment. In order to get up to date Flash support you need to use Google Chrome or Chromium, in which they take care of Flash development themselves, or instead use something like gnash. There are no other choices to play Flash content unless you do so in another OS.
