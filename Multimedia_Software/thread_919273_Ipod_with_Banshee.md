What version of Banshee are you using?

Hardy (Ubuntu 8.04) uses Banshee 0.13.2 whereas the latest (and supposedly greatest) version of Banshee (1.2.1) is included in Intrepid (Ubuntu 8.10).  From the looks of the website (and what Brian says on the Linux Action Show) Banshee is rapidly improving - as, hopefully, is the iPod support.
