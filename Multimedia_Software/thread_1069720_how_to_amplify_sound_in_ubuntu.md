### Post by GDR! on 2011-09-15
I'm aware that this thread is very old, but it's the first hit in Google, and doesn't bring any solution.

- Start Pulse Audio Manager (cli: paman)
- Go to Devices tab
- Under "Sinks", identify your device. Double click it.
- Under "Basic", there is a volume control allowing you to set volume to as much as 480%

Tip: to have clear sound, click "Show Volume Meter", and turn the volume up so that level is close to maximum, but doesn't touch maximum.
