### Post by andrew.46 on 2013-07-14
In general you cannot really 'improve' the quality of a file that has more than likely already been transcoded from an original source. Your best options are to either:

[LIST=1]
[*]Find the original source and make a direct copy of it, retaining its original specifications.
[*]Transcode the original source to the codecs and specifications of your choice. This will downsample the original quality but with skill this will not really be noticeable.
[*]
[/LIST]
