### Post by bcschmerker on 2010-10-05
The first problem that I anticipated is the apparent lack of LinUX-supported video capture devices for Component video interlinks.  My house has a [Wii](http://wii.nintendo.com/), and this device only supports analog video output---no HDMI (unlike both [Microsoft XBOX and XBOX 360](http://www.xbox.com/), and the [Sony PlayStation series](http://us.playstation.com)), which complicates the video-capture task.  There are very few standalone DVD-Multi machines with Component inputs, and these would be unable to record metadata from the Wii due to the same analog-only output constraint.
