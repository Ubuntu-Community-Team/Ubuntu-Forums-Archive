### Post by rmjohnson144 on 2010-07-12
I just checked after I had Ubuntu install some updates and I had to reinstall the 10.6 drivers and now I have the scaling option.

Go to System --> Preferences --> ATI Catalyst Control Center (Administrator)

Click on Display Manager and click the little down arrow on the main (Large) monitor pictured and select DTV then select the Adjustments tab and the Scaling Options is at the bottom.  Just slide the slider to taste (Usually 0% Overscan)

Hope this helps someone
-=Mark=-
ps, you must use the administrator version of CCC for it to work properly.  If you weren't asked for a password, then it probably didn't work for you.
