### Post by TripitakaUK on 2008-11-25
After much Googling I thought I'd post some info here for future reference by others.

If you are using Fuppes to stream DivX / XviD to your 360, you will have read much on the ability of the 360 to handle these formats natively (without transcoding) that came with the Fall system update sent out by M$ in Dec 2007.

The reality is that it is not the Fall update that provides this functionality but another download of the DivX codec that is locked to both your gamer profile and the individual console.

If you do not have access to Xbox Live or, like me, you have a banned console then this feature is not available to you. It is highly unlikely for a number of reasons that this codec will be made available in any downloadable format.

For those that simply have no access to Live, the future is brighter - you just have to hook your box up to Live once, create a free Silver account, get the system update then play a disk containing DivX content. The Xbox will connect and get the codec for you. Once this has been done I understand that you can disconnect your box from Live and play DivX content without having to connect again.

For those with a banned box, there is no solution and you require transcoding.

Hope this helps others.
