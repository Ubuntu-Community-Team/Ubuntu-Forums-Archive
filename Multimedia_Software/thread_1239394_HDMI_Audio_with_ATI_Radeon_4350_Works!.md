### Post by donchichi on 2009-08-13
Ubuntu 9.04
I've gone through all the web searches and nearly tried most of them except those I thought were too dangerous and too lengthy of a time to try. 

I went back to basics. 

Pull out the audio control panel, click on preferences, selected ATI-HDMI, go to preferences, There's an option to select tracks. I ticked the IEC958 Switches. Go back a tab there is a tab column now tick it and then there was sound!

This is totally unexpected from my part and was just about to settle in on one weekend reading and trying to fix the HDMI sound.
