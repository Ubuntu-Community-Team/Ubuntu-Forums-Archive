1. *File --> Save As*

it should have an audio filetype or you may have to put it in yourself

2. Press back on your browser, go to *save target as* and do as above

3. Use an extension such as [[COLOR="Red"]downloadhelper[/COLOR]]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3006") to download embedded media files
