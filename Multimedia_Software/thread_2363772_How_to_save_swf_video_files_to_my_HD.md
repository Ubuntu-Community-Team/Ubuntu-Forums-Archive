### Post by mc4man on 2017-06-14
To playback .swf files you need a decent video player that doesn't use gstreamer. So install either vlc or mpv & you can play them.
vlc would be easier for you to use, mpv would do a better job.
 If vlc opens the file(s) in too large a window for the file's resolution then simply resize the window smaller with your mouse cursor. (mpv will always open a window at files resolution size, if too small then increase with cursor.
