### Post by Erlander on 2008-12-06
My Sony HDR-SR11E is recognised as a 60 GB hard disc and I can copy the files across to the Ubuntu pc.

In another thread 2 days ago I read about Handbrake which will convert the AVCHD files to other formats.  I have tried it on both 1440 x 1024 and 1920 x 1024 and can get very good quality conversions.

Handbrake can be downloaded from [http://handbrake.fr/]("http://handbrake.fr/") and it is also in the repositories.  You need the 0.9.3 version.
