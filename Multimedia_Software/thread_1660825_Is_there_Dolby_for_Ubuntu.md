### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2011-01-06
The majority of external PC speakers are just analogue. And poor sound quality to boot (junk).

The speakers are likely just part of a 5.1 analogue kit.
 
Or are your speakers the USB external sound card type ? 

By Dolby you could mean Dolby Digital AC3 or EAC3.  This can be played back by soundcard decode to PCM/analogue out or digital pass-thru' to HT amp / AVR.

The PCM or AC3/DTS pass thru' could be via S/PDIF or hdmi.

The other Dolby surround modes are ways of encoding multiple channels into stereo signal (matrix encoded).
There are *buntu players that do all that..
