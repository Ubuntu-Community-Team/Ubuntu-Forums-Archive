### Post by dyerbods on 2009-01-31
Have a look at /usr/lib/Xorg/modules/libglx.so
It needs to be a symbolic link to the appropriate library in /usr/lib/Xorg/modules/extensions
I have noticed that after an update that changes the driver version the link is left pointing to the previous driver library which isn't there anymore - hence the failure to load the GLX module. I have just had this problem and after fixing the link I could start opengl apps again.
Hope this helps.
