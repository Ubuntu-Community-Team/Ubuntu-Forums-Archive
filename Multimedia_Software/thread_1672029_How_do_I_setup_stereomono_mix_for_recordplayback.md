I also need to know how to play the microphone through the speakers.

### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2011-01-20
Try Audacity. Import the backing track you want, then add another track for your mic input. Then under Edit>Preferences>Recording check the software playthrough and overdub boxes.
All you need to do after that is press the record button.
