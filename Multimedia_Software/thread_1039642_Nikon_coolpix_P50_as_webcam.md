### Post by xc3RnbFO8P on 2009-01-14
I am able to  use my Canon 590 IS , by using 
Composite video/audio output (AV)
to Composite video/audio input on the computer
(requires TVcard  and Tvtime )
Start Tvtime and choose Compsite, and turn it off.
Now I can use my camera as webcam in Skype.
