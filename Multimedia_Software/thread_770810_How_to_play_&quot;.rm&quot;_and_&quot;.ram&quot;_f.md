### Post by cor2y on 2008-04-27
you either need the w32codecs/w64codecs from the medibuntu or mplayer site, and depending on your media player a few plugins.
Example mplayer just requires the w32codec/w64codec pack alone.
Totem-gstreamer needs the codec pack installed as well as its media plugins, the same with totem-xine.
Finally you have the option of installing the real media player although i never got that last option to work for me.
To get the real player option (Not the helix player) enable all the repos universe and restricted.
