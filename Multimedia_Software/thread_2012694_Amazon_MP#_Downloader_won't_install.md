### Post by cottfcfan on 2012-06-30
+1 for clamz.
Install it from the repos then go to this page:
[http://code.google.com/p/clamz/](http://code.google.com/p/clamz/)
At the bottom of the page click the link to your location and then proceed to Amazon.
When you pay for mp3s, Amazon offers to download a file.
Download it to your Desktop.
Then Right click the file & "open with" clamz.
A terminal opens & you will see your mp3s being downloaded.
Nice handy little tool.
