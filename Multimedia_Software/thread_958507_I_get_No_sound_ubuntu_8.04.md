### Post by HotCupOfJava on 2008-11-06
OK - this actually worked! - Its weird, but it works......

I found it here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=161193&highlight=82801db-ICH4&page=2](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=161193&highlight=82801db-ICH4&page=2)

Double click the little speaker icon on the top bar. Select "edit" -> "preferences". Scroll to the bottom and check "external amplifier". Click close. Now select the "Switches" tab and UNCHECK "External Amplifier". Suddenly the sound will work! Go figure......
