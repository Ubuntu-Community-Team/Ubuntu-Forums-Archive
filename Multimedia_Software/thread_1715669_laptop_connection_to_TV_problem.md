### Post by SeijiSensei on 2011-03-27
There's usually an Fn key that controls dual displays.  You can often cycle between one monitor, the other, or both.

You might find investing in a wireless keyboard and mouse a great help if you want to connect the laptop to the TV and control it from across the room.  I use Logitech products for this purpose; they're rock-solid with Linux.
