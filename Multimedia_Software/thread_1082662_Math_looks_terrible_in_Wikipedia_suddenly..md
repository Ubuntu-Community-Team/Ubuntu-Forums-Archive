### Post by squaregoldfish on 2009-02-28
You haven't by any chance increased the zoom in a wikipedia page have you (on purpose or otherwise)? Since the math stuff is show as images, when they get zoomed they'll look horrible.

FF seems to remember zoom settings based on domain, so even if you zoomed another page, it will apply to all wikipedia pages.

Try hitting CTRL-0 to return to 1:1 zoom.

Steve.
