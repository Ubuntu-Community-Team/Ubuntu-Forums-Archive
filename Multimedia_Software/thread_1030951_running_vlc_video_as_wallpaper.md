### Post by sticky_icky on 2010-11-04
yes, it's possible, with vlc 1.1.x, anyway.

tools>preferences>video(don't expand the + sign)>1/2way down you can toggle "enable wallpaper mode"

the "directx wallpaper" button beneath fullscreen/always on top is defunct.(duh, it's ubuntu) so the vid should go straight to wallpaper after toggling that one setting.  

still buggy, on the smaller of my screens, the video is scaled to 1/4screen, there might be some more options, idk, but it works for me!
