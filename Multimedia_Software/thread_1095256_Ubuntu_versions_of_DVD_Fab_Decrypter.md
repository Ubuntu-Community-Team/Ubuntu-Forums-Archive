### Post by cariboo on 2009-03-13
Have a look at [K9copy]("http://help.ubuntu.com/community/K9Copy"), it may do what you need. You will also need libdvdcss2 to play DVDs. To get libdvdcss2 you will need to enable the [Medibuntu]("http://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu") repositories.

Check your local laws to see if it is legal to use libdvdcss2 in your area.
