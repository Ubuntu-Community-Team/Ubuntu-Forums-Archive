### Post by s-p-n on 2011-03-27
You can try this:
System->Preferences->Preferred Applications. Click on Multimedia

If it's not in the list, find the command (online) you use to open the mms radio in VLC, and put that command in the box.

I don't use mms radio or vlc, so.. hopefully it works for you :)
