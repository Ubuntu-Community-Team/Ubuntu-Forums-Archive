### Post by browndog on 2009-01-28
> **ThewhiteLynx said:**
> I have used Ubuntu before and had this same problem, and fixed it, but now I can't remember how.  My computer has a built in speaker, but also two external speakers plugged in, which Unbuntu can't seem to recognize, isn't there a way to manually set it to see the speakers?

Ensure your speakers aren't muted.  Right click on the speaker icon in the upper panel and select Open Volume Control.  Once in volume control click Preferences and select any boxes that aren't already...this will show you all controls.  Then go through each control and ensure the volume is turned up as high as possible.  Let me know if this does anything.
