### Post by Automat2 on 2011-10-30
To burn DVD's, the original disc has to be unmounted and ejected from Unity.

I was trying to eject it from the drive, Unity did not realize that the DVD was not in the drive any more and that was creating the conflict.

Hope that is useful to somebody.
