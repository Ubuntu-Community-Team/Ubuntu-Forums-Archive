### Post by Soo on 2009-01-17
For burning avis as DVDs, you can use DeVeDe to make an iso that Brasero can burn that will work in your DVD player.

For making 1:1 copies, I *believe* DVD::Rip might do the trick, though I'm not 100% sure.
