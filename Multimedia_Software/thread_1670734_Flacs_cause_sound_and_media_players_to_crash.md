In the last couple of days a problem with flac playback developed. I first noticed it with files I converted from wav with SoundConverter, they have a cackling buzzing sound at the beginning and sometimes playback stops in the middle of a track (which sometimes causes the media player to freeze). I usually use Clementine, but now switched to Banshee as it appears to have somewhat less problems. Still it inexplicably skips tracks or the window vanishes. Sometimes the sound button next to the clock greys out and takes a while to come back. Sound doesn't work then at all.

This also happens with flacs that previously worked fine.

The only change I can recall making to my computer is installing soundconverter. I ran the files through flac on the command line and they don't appear to be corrupted. I did not encounter problems playing them with aplay from the command line. I have not tested other media formats yet.
