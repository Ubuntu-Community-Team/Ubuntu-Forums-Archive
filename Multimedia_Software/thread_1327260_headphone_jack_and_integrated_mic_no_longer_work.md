### Post by SeeJayDee on 2009-11-15
Hi all,
I'm running Xubuntu 9.04 on an Acer TravelMate 2483WXMi, which has HDA Intel sound card (as listed in Mixer).

Yesterday I installed updates for the kernel which updated it to the 2.6.28-16-generic kernel.

When I tried to use skype today I noticed that the inbuilt mic was not working. I opened mixer and turned the mic and mic. boost volumes to their maximum. All I heard was a faint hiss from the speakers.

Also, it played music through the speakers just fine but when I plugged my headphones into the front jack they wouldn't work either. I know it must be a prblem with the software (i.e. ALSA) because when I booted into XP headphones, speakers and mic all worked perfectly.

I think that the version of ALSA bundled with the new kernel may not be compatible with my sound card...

Anyone have any ideas on how to fix this?
