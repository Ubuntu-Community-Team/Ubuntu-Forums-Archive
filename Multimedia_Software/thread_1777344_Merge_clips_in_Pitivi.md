### Post by fallenshadow on 2011-06-21
No there was no gaps in the clips.

I had 3 clips...I exported clips individually by deleting clip 2,3 and rendering clip 1. After rendering I named clip 1 to just 1 so the other clips would not overwrite the clip because they would have the same title.

I then pressed undo until I had 3 clips again but this time I would delete clip 1 (that I just rendered) and delete clip 3. I then moved clip 2 to the start of the timeline and clicked render. Rename clip 2 like above.

Lastly I would press undo until I had all 3 clips. I would then delete clip 1,2 (which are both rendered), move clip 3 to the start of timeline and render it.

This sounds complicated but its very simple and I just wanted to give you a good explanation. :)
