### Post by ciscoboy on 2013-01-05
the first thing I recomend you should do is like what evilsoup said; try VLC. It can play just about every video and audio that's out there. If it's not a software issue, then might I recommend that you take a look at the CD drive (I'm assuming that you listen to the songs/audio straight from the CD). If you have an external drive like what I have, the USB connexion might be a bit slow. If your drive is internal, it might be dirty. They sell CD cleaning kits that you can get for a few bucks. This problem may also extend to external CD drives as well. If it's none of the above, then I guess you're out of luck.

CiscoBoy

P.S.: I know it might sound dumb and you may have already done it, but check to see if the disk itself is dirty.
