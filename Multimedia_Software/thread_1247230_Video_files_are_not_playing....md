### Post by anomis66 on 2009-08-24
There seem to be a lot of posts about this type of problem (Totem crashing) with different files types.  After reading through a lot of them, I eventually found - [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1130582](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1130582) - staring straight in the face.  I must have been blind no to see it earlier.  After reading the 'HowTo' article I decided that it was too heavy for a NooB like me so choose the easy option - I replaced my onboard GFX with an old NVidia 64MB card.  

Result: all video types are now playing happily. :)
