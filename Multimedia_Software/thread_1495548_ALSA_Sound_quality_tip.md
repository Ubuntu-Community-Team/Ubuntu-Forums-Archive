Hi, 

just thought I should share my experiences with sound quality running Ubuntu.

I have a pretty decent sound system, which is connected to my computer, but still I have struggled with lousy sound quality for months. I have tried 'everything' to alleviate this problem, but to no avail, until today that is:

I removed ALSA, and installed OSS4, following this:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/OpenSound]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/OpenSound") 

My speakers have come back to life, all hail OSS!

ALSA 0 points
PulsAudio 0 points
OSS 1 point
