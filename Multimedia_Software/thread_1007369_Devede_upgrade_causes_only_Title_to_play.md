### Post by notquitestr8t on 2008-12-28
I changed a couple of options and was able to get my DVD to play under version 3.11. First, under menu options where you indicate the title, look for the "Disc Startup options". Select "Jump to the first title at startup". 
Also, if you click on the Title properties you have an option to select the "Action to perform when this title Ends". I selected "play the next title". This worked for me. Good luck.
