### Post by Tootler on 2010-06-23
I think you will find that none of the Linux video editors are as simple as Windows Movie Maker. Pitivi comes pretty close but is lacking in features such as transitions and titles - or was last time I looked at it. 

I use Kino. It is a little more complex to use than WMM and is quirky but it comes with a pretty good help package. I found that once I got used to it, it is more powerful than WMM but still pretty easy to use and you can get results reasonably quickly. 

Good Hunting.
