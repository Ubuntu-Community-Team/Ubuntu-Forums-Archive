### Post by amadness on 2015-11-19
[SIZE=2]if you don't want to use an online voice recorder app like _Vocaroo_ or _Speakpipe_ (which are easier to use than Audacity), then
*open _Audacity_ and hit the RECORD button (red button in upper left corner of GUI).
*then open _PulseAudio Volume Control_.
*hit Recording tab.
*where it says "ALSA plug-in [audacity]: ALSA Capture *from*"--
   make sure you select "Built-in Audio Analog Stereo" and _**NOT**_ "Monitor of Built-in Audio Analog Stereo".
*now start speaking into mic.[/SIZE]
