When I was back on XP, I used to really like Winamp 5. I haven't been able to find anything as good on Ubuntu. (No, I don't want to run Winamp under Wine...)

Of course "good" is quite subjective. Here's what *I* want; I appreciate that it's not what everyone wants:

[LIST][*]Ability to play .flac, .mp3 and .wma files [/LIST]
[LIST][*]iTunes style filter panes which show "genre", "album artist" and "album" and which are independently resizable [/LIST]
