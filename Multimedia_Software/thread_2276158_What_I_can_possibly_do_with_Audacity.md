### Post by deadflowr on 2015-04-30
What can you do with it?
edit sound files.
convert sound files from one format to another.
add addtional tracks to a sound file.
clean up a track that has noise problems.
cut and splice sections of tracks.

Most of what you can do with audacity can be done with other tools, like ffmpeg, but Audacity is easy for a new user to use.

### Post by deadflowr on 2015-04-30
> **remmas-sido said:**
> How to cut a section of a track?
How to convert a set of files or a folder containing files?

first highlight the section of the track you want to cut/remove and then click on the scissors icon.

to convert multiple tracks use chains.
click on apply chain select add files and then highlight select the tracks.
more here on how to edit and add chains
[http://manual.audacityteam.org/o/man/edit_chains.html](http://manual.audacityteam.org/o/man/edit_chains.html)
