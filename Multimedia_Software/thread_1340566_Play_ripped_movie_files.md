### Post by benerivo on 2009-11-29
If you ripped the dvd, then there should be lots of .vob files which can be opened by media players. These are just mpeg videos and can be played in order if the media player allows queuing of files to be played one after another. Generally you don't have to rip anything as if you can play the movie from disc, then most movie players can play the movie from an iso image of the disc.
