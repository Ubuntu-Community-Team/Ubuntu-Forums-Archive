### Post by Chronon on 2012-05-11
When I used an iPod the files were always stored in //iPod_Control/Music/Fnn/ -- where "nn" are two numerical digits.

I'm pretty sure the files must be in this obfuscated file structure and written with correct entries to the iTunesDB file.
