### Post by spaceboy909 on 2009-04-17
I'm not a Linux techie, but I do know some things about burning and compatibility issues.

1) Do the burned discs play properly in your computer's player?  They should.  If not, then that narrows it down some.

2)  Assuming they play ok on your computer, then there's a number of different things that could be preventing them playing on stand alones.  I'll list a few off the top of my head:

* Your players aren't designed to play R/RW's, OR, they aren't compatible with the particular brand of media.

* Wrong burn format.  They **must** be burned in the proper format for stand alone players, with the proper layout and bit rates, etc.

Well, I'm rusty on this stuff....that's all I can think of at the moment.  Give us some more info on your source files (DVD rips, vs downloads, etc), file types, burn formats, program options you've chosen (burning ISO's or just straight files, etc)....
