### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2011-09-23
From reading this thread, I get the impression you guys are confusing what/how HDMI digital audio is used.

It is used to output HDA digital audio over the digital video link to external HT amp (AVR) or DFP monitor or screen.
HDMI is used to satisfy HDCP requirements.

There is zero benefit/use for HDMI audio internally in a laptop etc.

Arguably, there is little to no benefit for average audio setups except for cable clutter minimization.
