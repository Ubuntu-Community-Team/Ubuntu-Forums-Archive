### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2011-03-26
Empathy is just a messenger program, yahoo, google, jabber etc. are the actual service providers who each have their own messenger protocols. Perhaps an analogy might help you understand the relationship: Empathy is like a TV set, and the protocols are like TV channels you can subscribe to.

So first you need an account with one of these services, setup your contacts, then you just need point empathy to your account. I would recommend using google talk as the guide I linked to shows explicit instructions on how to set it up.

I don't think facebook has video/audio chat. If it does, empathy does not support it.
