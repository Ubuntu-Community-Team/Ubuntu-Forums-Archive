### Post by Otiosedodge on 2009-10-01
Hi Music,

I don't remember the details of my fix. But I think that the front mic was the one that needed tinkering, along with the PCM level. I got sound (not great, but acceptable) when I had the PCM at about 80% and the front mic all the way up. Also, I set all the boxes in the devices tab under sound preferences to the PulseAudio sound server because it matched up with the Skype options.

Good luck!
