Ran into the following issue today. I can't get Ubuntu to work at 60hz refresh rate no matter what I do. Reason why I'd like this to work is because XBMC Vsync's video material to refresh rate, making playback of 60fps material impossible(it plays at 50fps and is jerky as hell).

My hardware setup is an Asrock iON330 with integrated GPU (I think it's a GF9300) hooked up to a Onkyo TX-SR508 receiver with a HDMI cable, with another HDMI cable going from the receivers output to the TV. XBMC is latest build of XBMC Eden, NVidia drivers are version 280.13. I am using VDPAU for hardware decoding , as software decoding obviously is a no-go using a atom CPU machine.
