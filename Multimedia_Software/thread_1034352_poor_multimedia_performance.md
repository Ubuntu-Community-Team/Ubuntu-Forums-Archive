Hi all,

I'm having intermittent problems playing back audio and video files. Desktop is a Dell Optiplex GX270, 2GB RAM, P4 2.8GHz, 26Gb free on the HD, running Ubuntu v8.04.

I can play divx movies using Totem, but after the machine is running for a while, the movies play incredibly slowly (I can see the frames go by), and there's no audio. It feels like it's a system resources issue, but at the moment my CPU is @ c. 15%, Memory is @ c. 25%, and Swap is @ c. 2.5%.

### Post by wolfen69 on 2009-01-08
by trying a different version of ubuntu (like 8.10) you can narrow down the problem. if it happens with a different version, it's pretty much a hardware problem. believe it or not, hardware is responsible more times than you think. i know, i have a pc repair business.

components can overheat after prolonged use and start to give errors. that's the one thing that is overlooked when people are troubleshooting. everyone thinks their hardware is perfect and in no way could be the source of the problem.

i think it is worth trying 8.10 to see if the problem reproduces itself.
