### Post by papibe on 2012-06-16
Ohh, I see.

Those fields you're trying to edit are internal 'fields' called [ID3 tags]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Id3_tag").

You'll be better off using a proper 'Id3 Tag Editor'. There are several on the Software Center including EasyTAG, and MP3 Diags.

My favorite is Kid3-qt.

I hope that point you in the right direction.
Regards.
