### Post by MikRose on 2011-03-26
Hi, this is late but just saw the post.  I had a similar problem with a Toshiba laptop and traced it to the FL inverter board.  When the laptop is opened and closed a lot the connectors to and from the board and the LCD panel are flexed.  Replacing the FL board was pretty easy and was only $15 or so on eBay.  I used this website that has several videos showing how to do specific things...you might find a similar site for your brand.
