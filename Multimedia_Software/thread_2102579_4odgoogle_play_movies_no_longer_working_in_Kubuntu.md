### Post by techhelp on 2013-01-07
I have had problems with 4od from channel4.com since the (not very) recent changes to the Flash Player system.  I could load the adverts but not the programme content, receiving a copyright error message if I was very lucky, but more often than not it would just show a black screen.

I then ordered a film from Google play, just to test.  That also game me a black screen and refused to play.

Then said:

"
In order to watch on Google Play with computers running Linux OS you must install a HAL module. Please follow the instructions provided in the link below to install the necessary module:  [http://helpx.adobe.com/x-productkb/multi/flash-player-11-problems-playing.html](http://helpx.adobe.com/x-productkb/multi/flash-player-11-problems-playing.html)
"

and it turns out that they are correct, that did fix my problems, not only with Google Play but also with the channel 4 4od system.

Hope this helps someone out.
