### Post by markbuntu on 2008-10-09
OK, in the System/Adminstration menu there is something called Synaptic Package Manager, click on that, it will ask for you password and then open. There is a search icon along the top, click on that and type ubuntu-restricted-extras in the box and click search. It will find the package for you. click on the little box and choose Mark for Installation. Then click on the apply button along the top. The package will be automatically downloaded and installed.
