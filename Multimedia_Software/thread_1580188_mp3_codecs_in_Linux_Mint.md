### Post by tommcd on 2010-09-23
> **halen666 said:**
> 
I understand there are license copyright, patents, etc issues; however, wouldn't those issues apply to Linux Mint as well?? ...
Yes the patent issues also apply to Mint, but the Mint developers have apparently decided they don't care about that. Mint is based in Ireland. Presumably the software patent laws in Ireland allow them to get away with this. 
Many distros, either because of their philosophy or fear of lawsuits, decide not to include proprietary codecs. Other distros (mostly those based in less draconian and less fascist countries than the USA) have decided they don't need to worry about patents or lawsuits.
