### Post by Steveway on 2008-06-09
Because it's not updated anymore upstream.
Including a program with no future support or bugfixes etc in the repos of a LTS-release is not a good idea so it has been removed. Also there are actively maintaned players in the repos, some are even based on xmms like audacious.
