### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-04-17
You want a dynamic range compressor.  If you are not satisfied with that, then a clip limiter with rather loose settings might work.  Classical music, by its nature has a very large dynamic range and thus you need a sound system and a listening environment that accommodates that.  I installed a sound system in an office building that the owner of the company wanted to pipe in classical music.  The employees were not impressed when a loud piece would wake every one up and interfere with phone calls that the employees were making.  I installed a dynamic range compressor, but the owner was not happy with the sound quality.  The employees were constantly fiddling with the volume.

Classical music does not work well in an office precisely because of the large dynamic range.
