### Post by tgalati4 on 2011-09-24
There is no network-based, synchronized music playing protocol that I am aware of.  You can use pulseaudio to send audio to another device, but once it gets on the network, it is subject to latency.  Latency of playback between speakers will sound wonky--echoes, cancellation of frequencies, tunnelling, combing, honking, you name it.

Plug your sound into a decent stereo receiver/amplifier and use more watts to get more volume.  Having multiple sound sources without precise control of audio delay will result in an incoherent sound field--sound like crap.
