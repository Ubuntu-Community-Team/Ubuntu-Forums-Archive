### Post by EkuquoL on 2011-08-01
Hello peter,

Ensure your device-jockey is installed... It is a small applet that helps install needed drivers. You can find that applet at Ubuntu's Software Center / Package Manager - Synaptic.

If your card is *brand-new* then you should attempt to contact the manufacturers of the Radeon card and ask for drivers.
