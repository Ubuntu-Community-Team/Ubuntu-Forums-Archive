### Post by Zen_Jackal on 2008-11-29
Have you tried resetting your Ipod once it's disconnected?

**How to reset your Ipod:**

Step 1: Slide the hold switch to 'on' then 'off'
Step 2: Press the 'menu' and 'select' (central, round) buttons down at the same time
Step 3: Release the buttons _only when you see the apple logo_ (after the blank screen)
Step 4: Wait
