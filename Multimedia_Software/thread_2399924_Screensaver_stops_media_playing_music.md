### Post by Dennis N on 2018-08-31
Light-locker is known to have that behavior. Possibly gnome-screensaver too? What do you have? Depends on which Ubuntu release.
One solution is to disable existing screensaver, then install and use xscreensaver instead and the music will keep playing without interruption. 
Or try Setting light-locker to lock the session &#8220;When the screensaver is deactivated&#8221;. Music should play while screen is blanked.
