### Post by TheFu on 2019-09-07
Like obs?  [https://obsproject.com/](https://obsproject.com/)
The number of video and audio inputs is limited by the capabilities of the machine.  If you don't need "live" broadcasting, you can use OBS on less capable machines to mux different video inputs in post.  Lots of people use a webcam recorder to get the "talking head" for screencasts and use some external $65 HDMI recorder to get the presentation slides/videos between the PC and the projector.
