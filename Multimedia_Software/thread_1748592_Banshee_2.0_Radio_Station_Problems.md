Pretty disappointing this is the default music player especially for the radio stations.  Maybe it works better for mp3, etc. but I am sort of still invested in iTunes for that stuff with my iPod sync for now.

Here is a brief list of issues in case anyone is interested:

- Will not play a number of stations that Rhthmbox plays no problem and they are not just the .asx type of stations.

- On stations that work some times I need to start and stop it 4 or 5 times to get it to play.

- Sometimes when I do get a station to play it will play the correct station but the wrong station name is listed as playing.

- Sometimes the station will play with the correct name in the title and everything appears to be correct but it is actually playing the wrong channel altogether.

- Sometimes the sound will just stop but the station is still playing.  There is nothing wrong with my sound system because I can go to another app and get sound no problem.  To correct in Banshee I need to start and stop the app a couple of times or reboot.

Really bad experience on this one and just not ready for prime time in my opinion.  I will keep it loaded and watch for updates but stick with RB at least until it is completely stopped development.
