### Post by markbuntu on 2008-10-08
Just find the station you want with firefox and go to the streaming radio page and drop the url into rythmbox/add new internet radio station box. That's what I do. Local NPR and other news stations, BBC, CBC, ABC, you can find them pretty easily yourself. Sometimes they have many formats, some work, some don't. Amarok sometimes works better for some urls.
