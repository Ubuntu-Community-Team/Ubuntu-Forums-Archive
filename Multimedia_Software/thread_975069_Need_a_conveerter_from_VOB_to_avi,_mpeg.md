### Post by keplerspeed on 2008-11-08
I would recomend mencoder, I have used it to convert ogv to avi. It is a simple command line program.

for example, to convert ogv to avi:

mencoder inputfilename.ogv -o outputfilename.avi -ovc lavc -oac lavc

For a vob, see here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=121081](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=121081)
