### Post by issih on 2009-03-13
Just go into rhythmbox's plugins and enable the daap sharing.

Suddenly all itunes machines can see your library, and so can any linux pc's running most media players (certainly works with other rhythm box clients)

Hope that helps

N.B. an alternative is to use mediatomb to just share out your library, that way you don' even need rhythmbox open.
