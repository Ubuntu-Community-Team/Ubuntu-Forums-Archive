### Post by itix on 2008-05-22
Yes I have many ideas :P
I'm quite closely involved in the development of exaile and do some bug reports and stuff. You might say it's like this; it doesn't work. The radio and the podcasts are still at an early development phase, sorry buddy.
Radio still works many times better at the latest 0.2.13 release which isn't in the repositories. It can be downloaded here: [http://www.exaile.org/downloads](http://www.exaile.org/downloads)

Feel free to join the forum for feature requests, support and so on.
Bugs should be reported here: [https://launchpad.net/exaile](https://launchpad.net/exaile)

EDIT: the radio bug is already reported. Note that Exaile is an extreme development version and should not be regarded as a full release. If you want wonderful radio support, iPod support and last.fm integration, try Banshee. Not counting Exaile, it's my personal favorite player.
