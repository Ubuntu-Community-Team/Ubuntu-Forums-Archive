### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2010-07-29
To use your computer as a real time effects box then you should look into using jack with a real time kernel. Look [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuStudioPreparation") under the section Real time support.
Once you have that set up, you could use jack rack or calf plugins or rakarrak (for guitar).
