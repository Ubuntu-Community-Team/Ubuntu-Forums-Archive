### Post by sports.car.guy on 2010-01-29
It depends on your location. If in the US, you can purchase a MagicJack and run a small Windows virtual machine for it. You disconnect the phone line where it comes into the home then plug the magic jack into the wall. When you do that your whole house is connected to it.

More and more people in the Linux community are using it for their phone service and doing what I suggested here. I am going to be one in the near future as soon as the Xeon Server I have at a friend's is brought into the house and setup.
