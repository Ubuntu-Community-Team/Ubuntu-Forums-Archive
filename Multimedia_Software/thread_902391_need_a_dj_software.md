### Post by Stochastic on 2008-08-27
lmms really isn't DJ software, it's audio production software.

The three main contenders (IMHO) for DJ software are: 
Mixxx - best overall
TerminatorX - best for virtual scratching of samples
XWAX - best for using timecoded vinyl (though mixxx has recently ported xwax's timecoding libraries into their software)

There are a couple other pieces of DJ software out there too, but these are the three I think top the list.
