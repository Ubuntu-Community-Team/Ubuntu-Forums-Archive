### Post by FakeOutdoorsman on 2014-03-22
See the examples in [How to Encode Videos for YouTube and other Video Sharing Sites](https://trac.ffmpeg.org/wiki/EncodeforYouTube). Some of the examples won't work with avconv or the fake, so-called "ffmpeg" in the repo since these are from a fork and the guide is written for ffmpeg from FFmpeg.

You can simply download a [Linux build of ffmpeg](http://ffmpeg.org/download.html#LinuxBuilds) or follow a [step-by-step guide to compile ffmpeg](http://trac.ffmpeg.org/wiki/UbuntuCompilationGuide).
