### Post by pickarooney on 2011-01-05
I would have to say, as a semi-prefessional photographer, that you would be better off sticking with Mac OS X for your business requirements, especially as you've paid good money to have the tools at your disposal. 

If you're just looking at linux photo tools out of interest or for comparison's sake, you'll find that everything will fall some way short of what you're used to but you will still be able to carry out most of the tasks you need with applications like **Cinelerra **(A/V editing)  and **GIMP **(photo PP).  **Workflow **or **RawStudio **should be able to handle your RAW batch processing although I've never tried either myself. 

This site gives a pretty good comparison list for known programs from other platforms:
[http://alternativeto.net/software/](http://alternativeto.net/software/)
