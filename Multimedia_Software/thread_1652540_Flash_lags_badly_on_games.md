### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-12-25
> **joseph2153 said:**
> nope didnt work on firefox or chromium, any other ideas

If you have installed the 64bit version of flash using Flash-Aid, then you might need to disable the integrated plugin of Chrome, otherwise it will take precedence over the plugin installed with Flash-Aid.

You can do that by typing about[noparse]:[/noparse]plugins in the address bar.

Also  take a look at:

[http://www.webgapps.org/flash/optimization](http://www.webgapps.org/flash/optimization)
[http://www.webgapps.org/flash/issues-and-solutions](http://www.webgapps.org/flash/issues-and-solutions)
