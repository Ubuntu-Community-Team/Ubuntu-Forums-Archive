### Post by FakeOutdoorsman on 2012-12-29
"mpeg" has become a fairly generalized word often referring to: Moving Picture Experts Group, the container formats (.mpg, .mpeg, .ps, .ts), the standards (MPEG-1, etc) or their parts (MPEG-2 Part 2, etc), and/or the video formats such as MPEG-1 video and MPEG-2 video. DVD video can be either, but I've never seen a commercial DVD with MPEG-1 video.
