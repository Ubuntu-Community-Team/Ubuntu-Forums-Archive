### Post by warm cardigan on 2012-09-21
In my experience, Ubuntu and Linux-based systems in general are very weak in terms of multimedia production. The audio frameworks (PulseAudio, JACK, ALSA, ...) are an absolute mess, and there are not many quality facilities for video editing.

That being said, I someties use gtk-recordmydesktop for screen recording and kdenlive for editing.

Good luck! :)
