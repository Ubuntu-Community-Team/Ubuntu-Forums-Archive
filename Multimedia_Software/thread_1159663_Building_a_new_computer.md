### Post by SuperSonic4 on 2009-05-15
I would go with nvidia - you get what you pay for in Linux support. I use a 8500GT and it's cheap now (about £40 now), it does what I need it to but perhaps you should get a higher one like the 9600.
A dual core processor will suit your needs fine IMO.

In general be *very* careful of static - get a metal sheet to ground yourself and follow the instructions on your motherboard's manual
