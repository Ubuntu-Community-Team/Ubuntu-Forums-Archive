### Post by pytheas22 on 2009-06-17
If songbird appears to be playing mp3s but you don't hear any sound, I'd wonder if you have hardware problems.  To check, either boot to another operating system on this computer (if you have one), or boot to the Ubuntu live CD and try playing some kind of sound file.  Does sound work there?

You could also run alsamixer to make sure that none of your sound channels were accidentally muted.  Just type 'alsamixer' in a terminal and it will open.

I don't know of any easy way to restore your sound settings to the defaults.
