### Post by Blackenedsummit on 2012-02-19
Had some issues getting flash videos to work. Had flash installed and updated but some Internet video players wouldn't play/only play sound. It would load the page but the video on the sites would just be a black box. Finally figured out a fix and thought I'd share it.

download this:
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/)

It removes conflicting plug-ins and installs the correct ones. It worked for my so hopefully it will work for you.
