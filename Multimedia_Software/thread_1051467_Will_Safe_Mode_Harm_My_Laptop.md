### Post by Linuxdork on 2009-01-27
I will put it this way.  I've never had a problem with it.  I've used it on multiple different configurations.  Laptop displays shouldn't have problems with resolutions or the vesa driver.  When I have accidentally set a higher resolution on my laptops in the past, it has simply made the image larger than the laptop display and scrolled when the mouse hit the edge.  In other words, I wouldn't hesitate to use it if I were in your situation.  I hope this helps.
