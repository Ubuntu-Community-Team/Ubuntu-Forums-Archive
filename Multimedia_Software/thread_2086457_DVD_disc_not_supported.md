It looks like the disc is not re-writable, from [Blank DVD R DL (Double Layer) Media - DVD+R DL, DVD-R DL - DVD R Dual Layer Discs - Printable | Verbatim]("http://www.verbatim.com/subcat/optical-media/dvd/dvd-r-dl/") ..

**Can I record over video/audio/data stored on a DVD R DL disc?**
No. DVD R DL discs are dual layer DVD’s designed for one-time recording.
