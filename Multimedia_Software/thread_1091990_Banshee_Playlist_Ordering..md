### Post by hypn0t0ad on 2009-04-16
that's a nasty little bug in banshee. the solution is to click three times on any column header until the little arrow disappears (e.g. "Name" -> 1st click: a -> z, 2nd click: z -> a, 3rd click: arrow disappears, ordering is possible/restored). 

with all the things they do really well in banshee it's hard to comprehend some of its huge shortcomings...
