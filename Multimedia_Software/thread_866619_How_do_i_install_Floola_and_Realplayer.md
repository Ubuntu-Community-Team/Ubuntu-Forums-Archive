### Post by ufugu on 2008-07-22
Floola doesn't get installed on your computer like most applications.  It actually lives on your iPod, and needs to run from there to work properly.

1) Mount your iPod.

2) Drag the file "Floola" that was inside the Floola folder onto your iPod.

3) Navigate to your iPod, double click "Floola".

4) Give it a minute to read your iPod. The first time you use it it will ask you to confirm the correct model.

Enjoy, Floola is great for iPods.

Hope this helps.
