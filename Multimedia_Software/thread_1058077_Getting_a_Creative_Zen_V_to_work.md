### Post by Ug on 2009-02-02
I'm running Ubuntu 8.04.2 and when I plugged in my Creative Zen V it didn't work by default with the system. And I couldn't find anyway to sync my music to it.

I've since discovered that if you open Rythmplayer and go Edit --> Plugins and then check "Portable Players - MTP" then the Creative Zen V springs to life straight away.

Alternatively -- to access the whole hard disk, install Gnomad-2 via synaptic.
