### Post by Michael Schmidt on 2009-07-02
I just got a refurb Sansa Clip 4GB for a very reasonable $35.00.  I like the size, price, and screen.  Sound quality is really pretty good.  Had to do a little reading on the Internet to get it working, but now am very happy.  Mr. Cheyne's instructions are useful.  Mine came with firmware 01.01.29a and I upgraded to 01.01.32a because it is supposed to have better .ogg support.  To use it with Ubuntu, you need to change the Clip's USB setting to MSC (default is MPT).  This is done from the Settings option.  Then it works just like any USB storage device.  I've been transferring music from my CDs using SoundConverter and EasyTAG to prep the files.
