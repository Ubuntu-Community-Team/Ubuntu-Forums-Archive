### Post by Autodave on 2018-01-02
You need to make sure that in the Power Manager, it is not set to sleep, or turn off hard drives after 15 minutes of inactivity. Even though a movie is playing, with no input from the mouse or keyboard, the system will go into sleep, hibernation, or just shut down: depends on what it is set to.
