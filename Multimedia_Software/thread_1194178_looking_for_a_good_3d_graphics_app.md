### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-06-22
[Blender](http://www.blender.org/) is not just decent, it is a very powerful application that can do 3D modeling, animation, video editing and other stuff. You can even do [animated movies](http://www.bigbuckbunny.org/) and [games](http://www.yofrankie.org/) with it.

Unfortunately, Blender is not easy to learn and master. 

These might help

[http://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Blender_3D:_Noob_to_Pro](http://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Blender_3D:_Noob_to_Pro)
[http://www.blender.org/education-help/tutorials/](http://www.blender.org/education-help/tutorials/)
[http://www.blender.org/education-help/video-tutorials/](http://www.blender.org/education-help/video-tutorials/)
