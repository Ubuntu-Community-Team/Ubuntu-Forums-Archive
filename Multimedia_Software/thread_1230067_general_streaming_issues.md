### Post by tonylyates on 2009-08-03
ding ding ding.  I went to myspace music player, right clicked and went to about.  I was running swfdec for mozilla, but it didn't come up in my plugins, or in add/remove.  I found it in synaptic package manager, removed it, and now flash player handles my flash, and is working like a charm.

I feel silly, but maybe this'll help someone else out in the future.
