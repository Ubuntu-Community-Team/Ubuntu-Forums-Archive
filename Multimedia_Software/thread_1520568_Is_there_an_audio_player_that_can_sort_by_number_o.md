There is a small subset of that library with more than 10 songs per artist, and that subset is what I usually listen to. It seems this has been proposed for Rhythmbox as a feature request a few years ago, but has not been implemented.  I can't find any other information for the other players that can handle my library (Amarok, Banshee, etc), but maybe I just fail at searching.
