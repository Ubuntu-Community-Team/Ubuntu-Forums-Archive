### Post by Wesley Dawson on 2011-02-09
Ok easy fix, go to the ubuntu software center and download "pulse audio volume controll" open it up and click on the input devices tab. Unlock the channels by clicking on the lock button. Move the front left to 100% and the front right to 20%.
 Also under the options menu in skype click on sound devices and uncheck the box that says "allow skype to automatically adjust my mixer levels."
Hope that helps! let me know if theres anything else i can do for ya
