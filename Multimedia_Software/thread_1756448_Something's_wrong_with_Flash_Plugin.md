### Post by handy on 2011-05-12
Try installing the beta version of the Firefox "FlashVideoReplacer" add-on, made my our good friend lovinglinux.

It solved my Flash problems, & I'm using 64bit Arch. :)

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flashvideoreplacer/#install-beta](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flashvideoreplacer/#install-beta)
