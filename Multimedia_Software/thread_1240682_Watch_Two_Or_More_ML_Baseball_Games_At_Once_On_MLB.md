### Post by NoVista on 2009-08-15
Well I don't know why this never hit me before, but you can watch more than one baseball game at a time at mlb.com on linux.
Using mlbviewer, just open another browser window before selecting your next game. I had three going at once in mini view, and I kept the HD off.

[http://mlbviewer.sourceforge.net/](http://mlbviewer.sourceforge.net/)
