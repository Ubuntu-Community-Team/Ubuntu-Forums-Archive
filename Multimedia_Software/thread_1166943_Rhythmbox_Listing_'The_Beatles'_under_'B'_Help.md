### Post by drphilngood on 2010-08-01
You can also make the desired change from Rhythmbox, itself.  Just select all "The Beatles"/"Beatles", files, right-click on them, and then change the "Artist:" field to whatever you desire the files to be listed under: Beatles, The Beatles, etc..  To be sure, I just tried this and it worked fine for me.
Hope this helps. =)
