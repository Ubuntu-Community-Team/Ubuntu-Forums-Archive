### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-07-28
I've never had any success with vlc plugin for firefox and used *mozilla-mplayer* until jaunty, at which point that plugin would always crash abd take Firefox with it.  After a longish search of this forum and google, I eventually found that mozilla-mplayer was no longer being developed and *gecko-mediaplayer* plugin was suggested.  So far that has not let me down once, and it seems to play absolutely everything I chuck at it.

Give it a try; it certainly will play the Apple movie trailers with no difficulty.
