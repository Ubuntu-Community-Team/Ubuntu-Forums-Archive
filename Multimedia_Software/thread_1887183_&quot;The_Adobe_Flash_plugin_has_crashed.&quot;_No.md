### Post by The Bright Side on 2011-11-26
Hey guys!

ever since I updated to Flash version 11,2,202,96 (using Firefox's FlashAid plugin), Flash keeps crashing on me, at least on most sites.

I tried to revert to the stable version, but though FlashAid says it is doing that, I seem to be stuck with 11,2,202,96.

I installed "FlashVideoReplacer", which saved youtube for me, but e.g. The Daily Show with Jon Stewart is screwed.

Do you know how I can get rid of Flash altogether and go back to the working version from the repos?

Alternately, are there some working alternatives to Flash?

Thanks!

EDIT: I'll give Chrome a shot in the meantime.
EDIT 2: broken in Chrome as well.

---

### Post by The Bright Side on 2011-11-26
Solved. I removed Flash-Aid, uninstalled everything Flash-related using Synaptic, rebooted and installed flashplugin-installer. Now Flash is working again.

---

### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-11-26
Your problem is most likely to be an issue with the new version of the plugin that affects nVidia users. If you disable HWVideo Decode in Flash-Aid tweaking options it will most likely stop crashing.
