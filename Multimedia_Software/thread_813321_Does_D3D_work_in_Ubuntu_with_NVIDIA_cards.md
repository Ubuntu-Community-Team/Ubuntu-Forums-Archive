### Post by 16777216 on 2008-05-30
Direct 3D ( D3D ) is a Microsoft owned and manufactured API.
Their is no version of any part of DirectX in whole or part available for any other operating except those made by Microsoft.

So in short no, and there never will be.

However WINE will let you play many Windows games in perfect or near perfect quality while you use Linux / Ubuntu.

The WINE homepage [http://winehq.org/](http://winehq.org/)

This is the Wine Application Database (AppDB). Here you can get information on application compatibility with Wine.

[http://appdb.winehq.org/](http://appdb.winehq.org/)


I hope this helps you.
