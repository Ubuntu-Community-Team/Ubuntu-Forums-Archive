### Post by Gold3n on 2012-09-11
Since my company recently banned internet streaming I brought the ol' iPod into work and installed Banshee and Mirage. It appeared, from the old screenshots, that Mirage wasn't working and maybe it actually doesn't; however, when you go to the "Play Queue" you now have the Fill option of "Similar" which you can choose various seeds.

I've included a screenshot where I played a track from Modest Mouse, selected similar and from recently played, it populated this list once I right clicked "Play Queue" and selected refresh. The list is far from perfect but it's pretty close.
