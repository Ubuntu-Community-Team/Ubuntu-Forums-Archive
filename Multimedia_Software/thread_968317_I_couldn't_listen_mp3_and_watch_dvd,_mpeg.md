### Post by mcgarry83 on 2008-11-03
Im having a little trouble understanding what you're saying. I guess its the language barrier, but it sounds like you're trying to get DVD's to play in Ubuntu. Just do this in a terminal...

sudo apt-get install ubuntu-restricted-extras


then get libdvdcss2 from here
[http://packages.medibuntu.org/intrepid/libdvdcss2.html](http://packages.medibuntu.org/intrepid/libdvdcss2.html)

and install it. You should be able to play dvd's now.
