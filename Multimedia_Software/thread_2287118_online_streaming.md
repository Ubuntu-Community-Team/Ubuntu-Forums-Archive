### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2015-07-16
For quicktime, window media player streams etc,  install Ubuntu restricted-extras and gecko-mediaplayer from the repo (only for Firefox)   But I don't think anything works on realplayer (do people still use that crap??!)

*the mpv-addon can be used in place of the gecko-mediaplayer [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2286848](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2286848)

**Oh, actually mpv addon cannot replace gecko-mediaplayer just yet because gecko does audio stream as well.
