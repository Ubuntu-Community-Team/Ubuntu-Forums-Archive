### Post by speartip on 2017-04-22
This may or may not be relevant. But several months ago I bought a batch of TDK CDRs. Brasero started burning coasters. Played on my computer, but not in any other audio player. The problem ended up being, me setting the write speed to 16x, the slowest burn on my system. If I allowed the CD to burn at Brasero's maximum speed all was fine. Apparently this is an issue with some brands of blank CD.
Just thought I'd mention it.
