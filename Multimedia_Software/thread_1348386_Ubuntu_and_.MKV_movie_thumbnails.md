### Post by smkozak on 2010-07-14
Ok this will be my first fix it post so let me know if it helped. I had the same problem as mattym123, I had some mkv files showing up with thumbnails and some not.

What I did step by step was go to my home folder, then I went to view and show hidden files. Next I went into .thumbnails/fail and /normal and deleted the contents. After browsing back to my external hard drive all 100+ movies I have made all new thumbnails and got rid of that annoying film strip.

/home/user/.thumbnails
