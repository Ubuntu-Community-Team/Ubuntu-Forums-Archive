### Post by wubijacq on 2010-12-16
To have a new startup sound in Ubuntu Gnome you have to put a new sound .wav or .ogg in usr/share/sounds* and then go to Preferences => Startup applications and see here: [http://www.wubijacq.com/freedesktop/]("http://www.wubijacq.com/freedesktop")
