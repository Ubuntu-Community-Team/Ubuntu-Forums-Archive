### Post by Mr_Mischif on 2009-12-25
This bug has been reported and fixed in Rhythmbox 0.12.6; in the meantime, someone has figured out a workaround:
> For example, I watch the folder
 /home/steve/Music
 and store my podcasts in
 /home/steve/Music/Podcasts
 This gives two workarounds for now,
 1) Leave the podcasts where they are, but uncheck the "Watch my library for new files" option.
2) Move the podcasts to a folder which is not being watched
