### Post by cariboo on 2009-01-26
I would suggest you install the ubuntu-restricted-extras meta package, this will install most of the codec needed to play almost any type of audio/video media. 

You need **libdvdcss2 **to play DVDs, that particular package is in the Medibuntu repositories to enable them, go [here]("http://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu") and follow the instructions for your version of Ubuntu.
