### Post by papamat on 2009-02-24
If you have compiz enabled go to System->Preferences->CompizConfig Settings Manager (if you haven't installed do to Synaptic package manager and add it to you system) and then in Window Management At Extra WM Actions, enable Toggle Always-On-Top and select the shortcut of your choice. xD
