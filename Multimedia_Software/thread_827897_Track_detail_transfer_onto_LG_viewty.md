### Post by aeiah on 2008-06-13
from a quick google it seems the problem is to do with the ID3 tags. use something like ex falso to change the ID3 tags to a version the LG likes. here's a random quote from [this thread](http://www.whatmobile.net/forumvb/showthread.php?t=23166&page=200):

> look at the ID3 tag and make sure it is not V2.2 or V2.4, the viewty does not like these and does not read the ID3 tag and sets alot of the information as unknown. Select the music files in itunes, right click and select convert ID3 tags. Set the files to V2.3 and when you transfer them to your phone it will regonise all the tracks and artist information as long as they have been tagged correctly in the first place. 
I have had one occasion where V2.3 did not work but changed the ID3 tag to V1.0 and it then regonised it perfectly.
