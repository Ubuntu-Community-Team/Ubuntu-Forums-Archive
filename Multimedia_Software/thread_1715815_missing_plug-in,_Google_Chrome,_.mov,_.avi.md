### Post by PowerBarry43 on 2011-03-27
Try disabling the VLC multimedia plugin in Chrome

---

### Post by sgriggly on 2011-03-27
that solved it, thanks... 

I went to "Preferences"...

Clicked "Under the Hood"; under "Privacy", click "Content Settings"; under "Plug-ins" there is an option to "Disable Individual Plug-ins". I disabled "VLC Multimedia Plug-in".
