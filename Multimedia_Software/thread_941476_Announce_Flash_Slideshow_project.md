### Post by demwz on 2008-10-08
Hello,

I've created a little flash application that lets you create interactive flickr style slideshows on your own webserver.

If you are interested have look at [http://code.google.com/p/open-flash-slideshow/]("http://code.google.com/p/open-flash-slideshow/")

Just unpack the archive on your webserver and point your browser to swfSlideshow.swf$url=demo.xml to see how it is.

I would be lucky to get some feedback
