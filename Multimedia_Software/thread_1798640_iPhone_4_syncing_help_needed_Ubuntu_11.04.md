### Post by tommawat on 2011-08-02
Hi. I have iphone 4 and same issue.  

Can be read and copied from to computer but cannot see files copied from computer to phone in the Apple music programme.  I believe this is due to the database on the iphone: although the Ubuntu programme moves the files to the phone, the phone will not register the files into its database yet.

This is the home page of libgpod's comments:  Most non-jailbroken iOS devices (iPod Touch, iPhone) are also supported with the notable exception of the iPad and the iPhone/iPod Touch 4 which are only supported as read-only devices.

[http://www.gtkpod.org/wiki/Libgpod](http://www.gtkpod.org/wiki/Libgpod)
