### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-05-12
The latest Firefox should not crash with flash, since it has a feature to separate plugin content execution.

Are you using the 32bit flash or 64bit? I recommend the 64bit. See the [[COLOR="Sienna"]**Flash Optimization**[/COLOR]](http://firefox-tutorials.blogspot.com/2010/05/flash-optimization.html) section of [Firefox optimization and troubleshooting thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1193567).

Also, uninstall **libmoon**. It is causing issues to Lucid users when starting Firefox or viewing flash content.
