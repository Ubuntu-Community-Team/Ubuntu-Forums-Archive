### Post by Vryko Lakas on 2008-09-13
I had a problem with games flashing like that, even with an ATI videocard in my laptop. The problem disappeared with I disabled Desktop Effects, so I am thinking it is an issue related to Compiz-Fusion. You might try to turn off all Compiz related effects and see if the problems persist.
