### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-03-10
> **krishna.988 said:**
> I have installed Ubuntu 10.10 with ubuntu restricted extras pack..I heard about this Fluendo DVD player and  fluendo complete playback pack.. Do I really need to purchase these packs to play DVDs, WM formats, Divx and MP4? Please clarify.. Thanks!!
Not really.  You can do all of that with free (ie, no cost in money) available from the repositories.

I suggest you enable the [medibuntu]("http://www.medibuntu.org/") repositories, and then install ubuntu-restricted extras, libdvdcss2, w32codecs or w64codecs, and then wait and see if anything else is needed as you use the system.  That should cover everything you have listed, anything unusual you need will soon be shown in error messages as you try to play media.
