The keys routinely used for vlc and bluray are here:

[http://vlc-bluray.whoknowsmy.name/](http://vlc-bluray.whoknowsmy.name/)

This is intended so people who have legally bought their bluray disks can play them back on their own linux-based computer. For a great discussion on keys and bluray playback in general head over to the Arch wiki:

[https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/BluRay](https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/BluRay)
