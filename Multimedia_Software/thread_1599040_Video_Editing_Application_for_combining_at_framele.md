### Post by cotcot on 2010-10-17
Sorry, for me it is not clear. 

You want one video with 2 areas, one area for each character ? (this can be done with "picture in picture" effect in common editors such as kdenlive or openshot or resizing and overlaying the clips in editors such as blender and cinelerra.) 

Or do you want one video with only one background where your both characters are acting ? This can be done with editors supporting mixing of clips. But than you need to record one character on a green background : search on the net with "green screen effect"). Blender and cinelerra can do this. I do not know about the other editors.
