### Post by ubuwatson on 2009-05-01
I just started using it to process my NEF files for my Nikon D200, considering this is an open source project the quality is very good.  I compared the results to Adobe Camera RAW (via CS3) and found that Raw Studio produced a comparable result with better texture definition and color.  The software doesn't have all the bells and whistles of other editors, but the included tools are very good, the speed is fast, and overall operation is great.  It's good enough to sway me from ACR and Nikon Capture for RAW conversion and that says quite a bit.
