### Post by sittingpenguin on 2010-09-06
The underlying problem is that Gnome cannot execute scripts at logout, the module for it is completely missing.

Not only the logout sound cannot be played, but administrators cannot have their users execute automated actions when they leave.

GDM can execute scripts at logout, but after logout, after the session is killed along with all the Gnome sound system. That's why the hacks around try to have GDM play the sound using various programs, extraneous to Gnome - aplay, bplay and whatnot.
