### Post by cotcot on 2009-05-16
Convert it to .mpeg using Avidemux (use version 2.4.4 : open your .flv ; select under the 'auto tab' DVD and save), WinFF, Handbrake, ffmpeg ...
Then use Devede to make the DVD.
