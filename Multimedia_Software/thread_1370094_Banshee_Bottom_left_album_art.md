### Post by boombox1387 on 2010-01-07
Someone just asked this on Banshee's mailing list as well.  Just in case you were not that person:

The 1.5.1 version does not have album art in the lower left corner of Banshee.  This was added to Banshee more recently (maybe 1.5.2? it's definitely in the development version).  You can upgrade to the development version by adding the [Banshee-daily PPA]("https://launchpad.net/~banshee-team/+archive/banshee-daily") to your software sources.  After adding this source, you'll want to run the update manager.  The option to show cover art will then be under View > Show Cover Art.

If you don't feel like adding the development version, a new stable release will happen sometime in the next few months.
