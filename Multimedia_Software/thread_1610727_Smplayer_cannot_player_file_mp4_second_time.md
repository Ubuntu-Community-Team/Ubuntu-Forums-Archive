Here , the lastest Smplayer 
Version: 0.6.9 (SVN r3447)
Using Qt 4.7.0 (compiled with Qt 4.6.2)

Not able to play certain file.It works at the first time you player but not the second time.

The solution is to going through preference , under the general tab and uncheck the (remember settings for all files)

It works for me , but for those who are having problem , try disable it. :)
