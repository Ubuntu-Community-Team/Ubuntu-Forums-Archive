### Post by wollygoggles on 2008-10-15
[COLOR="Red"]**RESOLUTION**[/COLOR]

If anyone else is getting the fault listed above, here is how I solved it for myself.

STEP 1.

Search for "Firefox" in Synaptic Package Manager. Scroll to adobe-flash-plugin and select "Complete Removal".

STEP 2.

Download Flash Player 9 [Here]("http://kb.adobe.com/selfservice/viewContent.do?externalId=kb406791&sliceId=2")

STEP 3.

Install and smile as your videos play without a hitch!
