### Post by mc4man on 2008-04-29
You'd probably be better off using powerdvd or vlc on vista.  In any event backups created on ubuntu _will_ playback on mediaplayer.( assuming they are authored and burned correctly ) When media player opens it's usually to the library - you'll see your dvd but you _can't_ play it from there. Go to top panel -> play-> DVD, VCD, CD - choose that and mediaplayer will switch to player mode....ect.
