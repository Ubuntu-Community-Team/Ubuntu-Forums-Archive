### Post by cor2y on 2008-05-22
if you are using firefox get the downloadhelper addon for most flashbased and .mov, asf streams.
I say MOST not ALL there are some that it can't download for those if they are non flash use the mplayer mozilla plugin which allows you to save video to harddrive unlike the totem mozilla browser plugin.
For flash video that can't be downloaded with downloadhelper play the whole video then visit your tmp folder via nautilus a copy of the flash file is usally there easy enough to rightclick to copy it to your home folder.
