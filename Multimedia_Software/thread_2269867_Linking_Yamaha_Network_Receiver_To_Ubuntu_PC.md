To the OP: R-N500 supports AirPlay and DLNA (you want the latter). Here's the official and slightly outdated guide for configuring a MiniDLNA server in Ubuntu: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MiniDLNA](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MiniDLNA) 
A better but also way heavier alternative is installing Kodi Media Center and activate UPnP (default is off).
