### Post by bumanie on 2009-06-08
Try mythbuntu or linuxmce. Even though linuxmce can be used for entire home automation, it also can be used as a stand-alone media centre with pvr function. linuxmce is more restrictive re the hardware it is compatible with than mythbuntu, ie it is best to have a nvidia gpu 6100 or later.
