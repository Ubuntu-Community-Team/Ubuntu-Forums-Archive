### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-04-03
You have not mentioned what those "several ways" were that you have tried, so other than suggesting you look at the **Sound Settings** from the volume icon in your panel it is impossible for us to help much more.
The **pulseaudio-volume-control** which is what opens from that Sound Settings menu item should show separate volume levels for speakers and headphones in the drop-down box of the Output Devices tab; your hradphones volume level may be set too low.
