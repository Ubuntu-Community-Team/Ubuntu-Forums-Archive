### Post by Fairdinkum on 2010-12-18
Hiya,

you may consider giving Mplayer another chance. Controlling it via keys has several advantages over a GUI: while it plays you can adjust image and sound settings, make it run faster or slower (very useful for analysing what guitarists or drummers actually do) skip back and forth, pause and loop. With a little practice this is a lot easier than trying to find and control all this with a mouse.

Mplayer plays all well known audio and video formats, disks and ISOs.

See 'man mplayer' for all the key commands and enjoy.

#! FD
