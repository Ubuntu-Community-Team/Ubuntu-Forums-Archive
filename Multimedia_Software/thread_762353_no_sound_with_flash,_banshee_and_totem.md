### Post by Dekunuts on 2008-04-22
So, I went to System -> preferences -> sound and set everything to alsa. Now all is fine. If I remember correctly, this has always been the setting I had to choose in ubuntu, don't know why or how the settings were altered. 

I can see pulse audio is also available now, but I do not know if you benefit from using it. 

Hope this can help you.
