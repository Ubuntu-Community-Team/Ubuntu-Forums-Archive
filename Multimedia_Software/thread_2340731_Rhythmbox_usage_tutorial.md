### Post by speartip on 2016-10-25
Within Rhythmbox, click Help, Contents for usage.
As far as your Qus are concerned:
1) The browse window is as you say, a way to browse your whole music library. You can play music this way, but as soon as you shut down Rhythmbox, what you were playing is lost.
    You can right click any song, & add it to the play queue, or highlight a single artist or album, click edit / select all. This highlights all the songs in the browse window. You can now right click & send all the songs to the play queue at once. 
2) In the play queue, just click the shuffle button.
3) Don't think it's possible.
4) The buttons depends on the overall theme you are using.

If you want something a bit more configurable, try Clementine.
