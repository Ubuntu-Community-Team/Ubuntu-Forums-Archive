If you are interested in making a small use of the commandline interface there is a nice script available in the Ubuntu repository called abcde that should serve you well. I have written a small guide for its use:

HOWTO: Convert music CDs to MP3 using the Command Line
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=535950](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=535950)

After a bit of setup all that will be required is to place your audio cd in the drive and type *abcde* at a terminal.
