### Post by scottuss on 2010-02-28
Just thought I'd follow up and explain how I fixed it, just in case anyone else is having this issue.

It **isn't** the issue of SWF authentication, it was my Flash plugin. I've installed the native 64 bit plugin from Adobe's "Lab's" site and this seems to have fixed the problem. Fingers crossed, Flash performance and stability seems to have improved overall on other things, iplayer included.
