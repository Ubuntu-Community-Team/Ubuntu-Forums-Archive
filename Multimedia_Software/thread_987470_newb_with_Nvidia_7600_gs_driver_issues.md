### Post by amauk on 2008-11-19
On the main menu at the top,
click System->Administration->Hardware Drivers

There should be a list of drivers available for your nvidia card
click on the recommended one, and press the Activate button

they'll be a short download, and the button should turn green

click close, then restart your machine
If all's gone well, you'll be using the nvidia binary drivers (and can enable the desktop effects)
