### Post by jon890 on 2010-11-09
Constant Mythtv freezing on one of my Ubuntu systems where Mythtv was within desktop, not full screen. Solved by going into 
System>Preferences>Appearance>Visual Effects Changed to none.

Mythtv is now fullscreen with no freezing.

System:
Ubuntu 10.04 LTS
Kernel 2.6.32.25
Motherboard- Gigabyte GA-M68M-S2P with onboard Video
Processor -  AMD Athlon AM2 64x2 4000
2gb Ram
Hard Disk for this Ubuntu - Samsung SpinPoint F3 1TB 7200RPM SATA
