### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-03-22
In firefox click on Tools > Add-ons > Get Add-ons
Search for Download Helper and install it.
Works great and can convert them to other formats.

Or go to Home then up twice then open the tmp folder and when you start watching a video on youtube it will be downloaded there, just copy it to somewhere before closing the youtube page or navigating away from the video.
