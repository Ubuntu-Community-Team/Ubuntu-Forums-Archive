### Post by Avoozl on 2011-08-10
Not sure why it's not showing up in your menu but you can run it from the terminal by just typing in "avidemux" or you can hit alt-f2 and launch it from there.

You can also add a new menu item by going to system-preferences-main menu or something similar.  Add a new menu item under sound and video and put /usr/bin/avidemux in the command line.
