### Post by varangian on 2009-11-25
You could have a look at Sound Juicer, it's been my preference for such tasks. It's in the repositories, does OGG and Flac, and mp3 if you've installed the extras. Also meets the simplicity criteria as it just pops up when you insert an audio CD and, once you've set preferences, it's a one click operation to start ripping.
