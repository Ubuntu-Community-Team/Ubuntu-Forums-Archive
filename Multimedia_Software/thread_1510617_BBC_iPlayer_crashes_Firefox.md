I have a Compaq 6715b laptop with a Radeon X1250 card.
I was running Ubuntu 9.10 64 bit and every time I tried to watch a BBC iPlayer video Firefox would crash.
However it would work fine on Youtube even though I only have a 512kbs connection
After reading that Adobe are not supporting  64bit Flash and there is a security risk with the current version I decided to install Ubuntu 10.04 32bit.
See: [http://www.theregister.co.uk/2010/06...or_linux_dead/]("http://www.theregister.co.uk/2010/06/11/64_bit_flash_for_linux_dead/")

Now it works fine, I just caught up with the new series of Dr. Who :p
