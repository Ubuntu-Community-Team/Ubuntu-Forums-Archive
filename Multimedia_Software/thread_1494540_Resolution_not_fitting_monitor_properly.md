### Post by kwalters on 2010-05-27
Have you tried going to System/administration/system testing?  One of the sets of tests is for your monitor and, at one stage, it offers to cycle through all the different resolutions detected for your system.  I found the one I had been using was not on the list and, once I changed to one that was, the problem of overlapping the screen disappeared
