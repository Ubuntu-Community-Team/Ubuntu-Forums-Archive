### Post by IanVaughan on 2011-02-22
It was the device selected.
Under Volume Control->Configuration->Profile.
Selecting Analog Surround 4.0 Output worked.
Even though I dont have surround!

Get this awful high pitched noise when the Output Devices bars are set above 50%!
