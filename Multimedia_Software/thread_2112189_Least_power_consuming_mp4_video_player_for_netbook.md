### Post by SeijiSensei on 2013-02-04
Battery consumption is largely driven by the screen and to a lesser degree disk activity.  I doubt any other players will have much effect.  If the videos are on the hard drive, you might try creating a [ramdisk]("http://www.ubuntuka.com/ubuntu-ramdisk-ramdrive-easy-way/"), copying videos to that, and viewing the copies.  Then it would no longer be using the hard drive.  Or copy them to a USB thumbdrive and watch them from there.
