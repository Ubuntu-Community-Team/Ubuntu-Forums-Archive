### Post by mirearts KING SUNNY on 2010-11-16
Go to PREFERENCES>SETTINGS>SOUND or on your top right screen right-click on the audio icon and choose settings.

If you want 5.1 sound , select the 5.1 Chanel with stereo input as you want to capture sound in your case.

Go to the recording tab and select the default recording source.

NB: do not increase the leveler too much else you'll get glitches, leave the LFE meter to the middle
