### Post by cozmicharlie on 2008-06-22
I would use gtkpod (gtkpod-aac if you code your music in aac).  A quick search of the forums will show you that a lot of people are having problems with ipods through Rythmbox, Amarok or Banshee etc etc.  I have always had good luck with gtkpod.  It is in Synaptic so it is easy to install and it is very easy to use.
