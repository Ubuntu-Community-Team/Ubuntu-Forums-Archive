Video capturing in Linux is a real pain in the rear end.  There are commands that you use in the terminal to capture video, but they are tedious and long.  I have converted all of my old VHS into DVD using XP...very easy.  If you still have XP on your computer then this is how you do it:

1. Purchase Dazzle video capture device or equivalent ($50 - 70).
2. Use the supplied software to capture and burn the DVD in one step (Dazzle only allows 104 min for direct burn.  You can capture longer video to your hard disk and use Nero to burn your DVD).
3. Or, you can use Windows Movie Maker to capture and burn DVD (you can set the recording time using this method).

Good luck.
