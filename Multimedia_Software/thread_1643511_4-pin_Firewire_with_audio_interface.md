### Post by Aztek on 2010-12-25
In case anyone has a question like this in the future, yes you can use the 4-pin connector on your laptop, but you need either a 4-pin to 6-pin adapter or a 4-pin to 6-pin cable (that's what I did). Audio interfaces almost always have their own power supply, which you'll need to use.
