Hi,

For the benefit of all other users i am specifying the steps i followed to get HDMI audio to work after a lot of toil.

Laptop configuration: Acer 5738
Ubuntu (10.04) Lucid Lynx

                          
[LIST=1]
[*]Please visit the link     [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonHD](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonHD)     and read specifically the HDMI Audio section. Don't worry if you are     unable to test for the sound.
[*]Connect the HDMI cable from the     laptop to your LCD tv. This should be trivial. Make sure you switch     your TV to HDMI input from its menu options.
[*]After enabling the HDMI     configurations on your laptop using Step 1. Click on System-->     Preferences --> Sound.
[*]Click on the Hardware tab. By     default it might be set to “Analog Stereo Duplex”. Go to Output     tab. Change the Connector drop down from “Analog Speakers” to     “Analog Headphones”.
[*]Go back to Hardware tab and select     “Digital Stereo (HDMI) Output”.
[*]Function+F5 would direct the video     output to your TV. Incase you have a problem redirecting your video output to the TV. Restart your machine with the HDMI cable connected to your TV.
[*]With the configurations you would be able to hear     sound from your TV. Have a blast!
[/LIST]
 I have tried this with Acer 5738 it might work with other Laptop configurations as well.

Regards,
Anand
