### Post by markbuntu on 2008-09-14
ATI has specifically stated that their linux drivers will not support the tv capture mode of their all-in-wonder cards. It has to do with some third party IP rights. 

You can read the release notes here:


[https://a248.e.akamai.net/f/674/9206/0/www2.ati.com/drivers/linux/catalyst_88_linux.html](https://a248.e.akamai.net/f/674/9206/0/www2.ati.com/drivers/linux/catalyst_88_linux.html)
