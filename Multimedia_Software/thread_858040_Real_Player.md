to make it more simpler, paste the RealPlayer11GOLD.bin file in your home folder, next open the terminal and type or paste **chmod 770 RealPlayer11GOLD.bin** and press enter, then type again **sudo ./RealPlayer11GOLD.bin** hit enter, that's all, it should be installed.
