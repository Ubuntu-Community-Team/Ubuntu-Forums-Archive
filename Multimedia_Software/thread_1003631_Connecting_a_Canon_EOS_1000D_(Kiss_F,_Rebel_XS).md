### Post by MatthewJS on 2008-12-06
Not a question - more like a solution!

I tried to connect a Canon EOS 1000D to my standard Hardy (Linux 2.6.24-22 generic).
When F-Spot came up it asked me to select:
USB PTP Class Camera usb:
USB PTP Class Camera usb:003,006

It didn't matter what I selected it said something like 'Could not claim the USB device'

I went into synaptic package manager and installed gphoto2. When I next connected my camera I selected 'USB PTP Class Camera usb:' and it downloaded the photos perfectly.
