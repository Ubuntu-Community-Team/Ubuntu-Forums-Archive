### Post by marbertone on 2011-12-22
For who's still interested... I solved! 
It suffices to install Pulseaudio volume control,
end enable "Monitors" as sources.
Then, everything coming out from speakers will be redirected to input.
Thanks to Ubuntu "Pulseaudio" wiki page.
