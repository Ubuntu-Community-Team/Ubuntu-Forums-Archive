### Post by scorpiooooooh on 2010-11-22
You may want to look at an app called VLC Media Player(VideoLAN).  It is very powerful as a player but has far more capability beyond the player function.  It also functions as a streaming server and a recoder/encoder for most video and audio formats.  It is available in many Linux flavors including Ubuntu.  VLC is also open source.

[www.videolan.org](www.videolan.org)

VLC will probably do the trick, I cannot say it will with 100% certainty as I have not loaded it on a Linux box yet, I do use it extensively on all my MS systems and love it.  If you do try it, please let me know how it worked out.
