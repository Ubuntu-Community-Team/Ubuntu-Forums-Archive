### Post by cotcot on 2011-03-01
Blender can do all what you ask. Blender is very powerful in combining 3D animations with text and objects and video clips. However be prepared to spent some learning time.
Key issue is a video editor that allows you to put video clip on different tracks upon each other and allows you to add, substract or multiply. 

Kdenlive and Cinelerra are also editors I have used to do what you want. Kdenlive is the easiest of the 3.

There are other useful video editors, but I am not sure enough of supplying correct information because I have not enough experience with them. I leave it to there users to comment.
