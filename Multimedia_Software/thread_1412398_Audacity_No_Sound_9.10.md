```
padsp audacity
```

Nothing.   There must me a simple way to get sound running.

---

### Post by Robster2 on 2010-03-01
The problem was that the volume of the recording was too low.

I could see the volume bars in the record and play back. So I thought everything was working.

The playback was just fine.  Volume of the recording  was simply so low that I could not hear it.  Right clicking on the speaker. Selecting input (Which by default was ten percent). 

Adjusting the input volume the problem was fixed.

Solved !   :D
