### Post by cotcot on 2008-12-30
[[COLOR="Blue"]QDVDauthor[/COLOR]]("http://qdvdauthor.sourceforge.net/"). It is in the repositories. You might get thumbnail error signs (yellow letters on blue background) when you insert clips.
You get this error when you add video with the "Add / organize video" tab. You can add your video in another way. Clicking in the white field under "All" "Video" and "Audio" tabs opens your browser. Go to the folder with your clips. Double click on a clip and it is added without error.
