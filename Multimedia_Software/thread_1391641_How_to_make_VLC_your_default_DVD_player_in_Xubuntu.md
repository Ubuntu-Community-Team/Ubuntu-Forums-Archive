### Post by Nylo on 2010-01-27
If you are running Xubuntu 9.10 here is how to make VideoLan your default DVD player.

Applications > Settings > Removable Drives and Media.  Choose Multimedia tab, check "play video CDs and DVDs when inserted."  Then click the brows folder to the right and find, VLC.  Click on that and you are done.
