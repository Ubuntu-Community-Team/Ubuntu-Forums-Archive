Just a note: 
Auda*city* ([http://audacity.sourceforge.net/]("http://audacity.sourceforge.net/")) is a Free/Open Source sound editing application. Think "GarageBand." 
Auda*cious*([http://audacious-media-player.org/]("http://audacious-media-player.org/")) is a Free/Open Source audio player application. Think "Winamp."
