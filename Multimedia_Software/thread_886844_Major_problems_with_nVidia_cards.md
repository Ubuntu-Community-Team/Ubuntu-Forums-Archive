### Post by cariboo on 2008-08-11
Usually reinstalling does not fix a problem, remember Linux is not Windows. As to your problem when you reinstalled did it tell you that restricted drivers where available for your video card? Usually most Nvidia are detected properly at boot, but because the monitor isn't detected properly you may not get that choice to install the restricted drivers. THere is a very good howto here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=797270](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=797270)

A word of advice, follow the instructions and don't skip any steps, and make backups of your configuration files before modifying them.
