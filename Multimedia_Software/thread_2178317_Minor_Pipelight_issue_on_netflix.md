### Post by jawilljr2 on 2013-10-02
Try this.

Start a Netflix movie, then click on the Netflix 'Full screen' button on lower right, Then while pressing and holding Shift+alt buttons, left click anywhere on the movie.

A diagnostic menu should popup. Click on 'A/V Sync Compensation', A slider window should open and you can adjust the sync there.

Hope this helps
