### Post by mastermindg on 2008-08-02
There is always a risk related to hacking a piece of hardware, be it a router, mp3 player... However (from my understanding) you can simply plug the iTouch into a windows system with iTunes running and upgrade the firmware, essentially overwriting any changes.

To transfer music to your iTouch or iPhone, simply install ssh after jailbreaking it. Then ssh to the IP of the device, setup the keys, and then use sshfs or similar to transfer files. There is a process spelled out in ziphone's forums.
