### Post by Alex Deez on 2009-09-26
Hey man, way late on the reply but I found your topic on a Google search because I was trying to do just this.  Anyway I figured it out.  You can organize your collection by genre and then right-click -> load on the specific genre.  This ends you up with a playlist of all the songs with that genre in the id3 tag.
