### Post by Tarvok on 2009-05-13
I don't know a final answer either; it's a bizarre bug and I don't know if its limited to Ubuntu, Debian, or Linux in general.

But one thing that can help is if you max out your left channel volume temporarily. Just slam it up there, as high as it'll go, and leave it there until left channel sound abruptly returns to the proper volume. It may happen right away, or it may take a few minutes. At that point you can either link the right and the left back together, or adjust it to wherever you want it.
