### Post by rubylaser on 2011-07-07
> I need to crop these down and increase the size of picture, but still remaining the quality of them

Gimp will perform the cropping and scaling with ease. Unfortunately, the task you've asked to do is not possible.  Your photos are raster images (made up of pixels), and when they where scanned at the smaller size, you had a certain number of pixels.  Upscaling, unless with vector graphics, inherently causes a loss in quality because you're expanding the pixels you have and guessing the one's in between.  The good news is, that if your images where scanned at a high dpi, you can  upscale and still have a very good looking image.
