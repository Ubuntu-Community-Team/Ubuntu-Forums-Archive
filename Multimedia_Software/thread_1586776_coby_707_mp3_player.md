### Post by iAkOul on 2010-12-30
There is a workaround, not a direct way to create playlist. Unfortunately, you have to sacrifice the Album information. 
 
What you do is simply batch edit the Album info for all of the songs you want to be in the same playlist. The Album info will be the "title" of the playlist. For batch editing, iTunes does it easily, for all you have to do is select the songs, right click and select "Get Info," then a prompt will come up. Then go to the Album section in the player and voila.
 
I don't know if this answered your question, but I do hope it proves useful.:-o
