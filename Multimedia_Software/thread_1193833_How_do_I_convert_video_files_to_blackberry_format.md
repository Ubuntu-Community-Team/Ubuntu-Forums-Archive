### Post by billgoldberg on 2009-06-26
I have no idea what video formats a Blackberry can play.

It will most likely be something that is supported by ffmpeg or mencoder.

Add the medibuntu repository if you haven't already and install ffmpeg.

Then google for "Winff" and download the Ubuntu version.

Winff is an easy to use, yet powerfull video/audio converter.
