### Post by TheFu on 2021-01-27
Support for 16.04 ends in a few months - before the end of this school session.  Upgrade to 20.04 (any DE) or 18.04 with the gnome3 DE.  All other DEs on 18.04 have support that ends April this year too.

I have a system that lost audio 2 weeks ago. I haven't looked into it. I suspect it was just a boot-driver thing, but since I reboot that machine only once a week or every other week, I simply haven't gotten around to seeking a solution.  Mine is an 8th-gen Core i5 with onboard audio/video. No external AMD or nVidia anything.

I don't use lossless cut, but other audio players and the browsers all stopped working.

For most audio issues, there is the "audio troubleshooter" which should be run to capture everything about the system, then post the created file here or on paste.ubuntu.com and hope some guru sees a problem.
