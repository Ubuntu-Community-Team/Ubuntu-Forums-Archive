### Post by Bungo Pony on 2008-05-30
SoundKonverter fits all my needs quite well. Rips CDs, converts audio files to other formats, nice adjustable bitrate.

However, beware of SoundKonverter's evil Gnome cousin "Sound Converter". The bitrates are preset and .wav formatting is permanently set to 32 bit.
