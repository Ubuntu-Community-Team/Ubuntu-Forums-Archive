### Post by bovender on 2009-12-06
Hi,

I don't think there is a good replacement for IrfanView. What that guy Irfan created is just a unique piece of software.

Other programs can do parts of what IrfanView does, but IrfanView is unsurpassed as the Swiss army knife of image viewers/editors.

Two alternatives that I tried are digiKam (it's a KDE application, but I installed it in Gnome and it works well) and gThumb. Both are certainly good software, but again, not quite like IV.

By the way, what's wrong with running IV under Wine? Is it a matter of principle for you? Then be advised that, as they say, the exception proves the rule! ;)
