### Post by psyke83 on 2008-11-24
You need to upgrade to Flash 10 (which is PulseAudio-compatible), and also configure PulseAudio correctly.

This guide covers all you need: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=789578](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=789578)
