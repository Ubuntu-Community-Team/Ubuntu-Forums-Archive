### Post by sekinto on 2008-12-27
Avidemux is my favorite converter, but there are many others. The reason I like Avidemux is because it is cross-platform, free, supports many formats and has many filters. The resize video filter will come in handy when converting, you can resize the videos to fit perfectly on the iPod's screen if they are bigger than that... the file will then be smaller but look the same. :P
