I did quite a bit of debugging and found the root of my problem, in case anyone finds this later on.

It turned out that when I installed a new sound card way back when it was causing my Xorg to corrupt.

I fixed this by increasing the size of my "AGP Graphics Aperture" in BIOS.

This seems to be the reason why VESA wouldn't work either.
