### Post by cpetercarter on 2008-06-28
System > Preferences > Advanced Desktop Effects

Enable "Desktop Cube" and "Rotate Cube"

General Options > Desktop Size > Horizontal Virtual Size

Increase the number to 4, leaving the other two options at 1

A good guide to setting up Compiz is [http://forlong.blogage.de/article/2008/4/26/How-to-set-up-Compiz-Fusion-074]("http://forlong.blogage.de/article/2008/4/26/How-to-set-up-Compiz-Fusion-074")
