### Post by stafio on 2009-12-27
If you have the Homebrew Channel installed on your Wii, there are a few media players you can use. See: [http://wiibrew.org/wiki/List_of_homebrew_applications#Media_players]("http://wiibrew.org/wiki/List_of_homebrew_applications#Media_players"). For this, you would just need to share your media from the htpc using Samba. Also note that Wiicr was basically abandoned when the Hombrew scene started taking off. See the last item under recent news [here]("http://wmc.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/WiiCR").
