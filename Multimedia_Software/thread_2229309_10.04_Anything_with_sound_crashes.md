### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2014-06-12
It's an issue with the last kernel update: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/1327300](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux/+bug/1327300)

Boot into previous kernel (2.6.32-60) until it's sorted.
