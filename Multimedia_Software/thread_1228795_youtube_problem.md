### Post by starcraft.man on 2009-08-01
> **entaroadun said:**
> Hello, im using ubuntu 9.04 on 64 bit, everything works except i cant figure out why i dont hear any sound on videos from youtube (only image) Music sound and movies sound works just fine (soround 5.1) any suggestions ?

I assume you have installed the restricted extras package and have flash video working fine. Right click the sound icon in the tray on GNOME or KDE and open up the mixer (Open Volume Control). On this window, ensure that Master, PCM, and and any other applicable settings aren't muted and are set at least 80% or more. Usually, it's the PCM setting that was muted which flash uses.
