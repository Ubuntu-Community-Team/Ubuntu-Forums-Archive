### Post by martinbaselier on 2009-07-24
FYI: There's a 7.04 and a 7.10 version (standing for month 4 and month 
10) 

It would be possible to use old sound drivers, though not easily. You would need to recompile them for the current kernel and since there are quite a lot of changes in API between kernels, that would not be an easy task. 

I've recently switched to OSSv4 and found it to be a very large improvement in sound quality. Check their site:

[http://www.opensound.com/](http://www.opensound.com/)

Choose download, open sound system, DEB and now you can download their installer and a pdf with instructions. In the pdf you can find all supported soundcards. Or visit the link below to see if you device is supported.  

[http://www.opensound.com/osshw.html](http://www.opensound.com/osshw.html)
