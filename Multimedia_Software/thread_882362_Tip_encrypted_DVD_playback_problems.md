### Post by slumbergod on 2008-08-06
In my enthusiam to get rid my new laptop of Vista and replace it with Xubuntu, I stumbled upon a completely unforseen problem.

Try as I might I just could not get a DVD to play. I installed libdvdcss2, all of the restricted extras, all of the popular dvd players and their extras but still no joy. Almost all of the problems with DVD playback are to do with missing codecs and that is what I assumed too.

After searching for ages I stumbled upon this thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=486604&highlight=setregion](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=486604&highlight=setregion)

The problem is that because I had never used an encypted DVD in the new DVD writer, the region was unset. I installed setregion to make it region 4, rebooted, and now it works.

Thanks to the contributor who posted that solution. Rather than reply to the thread I decided to post it as a new thread so it would be easier to find for people looking for solutions. All the threads are about codecs so it is easy to get lost with them!
