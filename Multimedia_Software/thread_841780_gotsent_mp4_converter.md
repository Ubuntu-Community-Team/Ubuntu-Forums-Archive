### Post by aeiah on 2008-06-26
i dont know off the top of my head, but since gotsent uses ffmpeg and other tools that have linux versions, it shouldnt be too difficult to get a working solution. gotsent probably doesnt do anything apart from link the underlying programs together.

anyway. yea. so the xbox360 can play mp4 files so you dont have to convert your video, you just remux it from the mkv container to the mp4 container.

anyway, try:
[http://fuppes.ulrich-voelkel.de/features/](http://fuppes.ulrich-voelkel.de/features/)
and
[http://forums.xbox-scene.com/index.php?showtopic=640413](http://forums.xbox-scene.com/index.php?showtopic=640413)

note that the xbox-scene one says it doesnt work under linux for files larger than 2gb, but later on in the thread a workaround is posted.

i know its a horrible set of commands but you could package them in a bash script pretty easily even if you dont know anything about programming. there's also a thread on ubuntuforums detailing how to get linux to convert and/or stream to the ps3, and i think it will work for the xbox since you're converting to an mp4 container.
