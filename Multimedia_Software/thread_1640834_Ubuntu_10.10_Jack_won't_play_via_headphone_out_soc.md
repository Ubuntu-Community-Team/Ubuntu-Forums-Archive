### Post by surfmonkee on 2011-01-14
in the jack connections panel, click on 'system' in the right pane to open it, playback 1&2 are your laptop speakers, playback 3&4 is the laptop headphone socket.

**whatever u want connected to the headphones, connect to playback 3&4 in jack.**
