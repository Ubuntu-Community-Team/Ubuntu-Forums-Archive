Try lowering the resolution for the webcam, and make sure your Ubuntu is fully updated (just got some updates today that webcame works better).

I had this issue too - webcam was saying it can support too high of a resolution, but wasn't unable to handle it and was laggy. Lowering down to 800x600 made the video go quite smooth.
