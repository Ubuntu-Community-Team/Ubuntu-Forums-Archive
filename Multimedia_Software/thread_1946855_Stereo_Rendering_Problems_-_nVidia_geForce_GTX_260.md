### Post by skullmunky on 2012-07-08
I thought you needed a Quadro to get a stereo quad buffer - are you saying you used to be able to do it on a GeForce!?  If so, then probably what happened is nvidia got wise and disabled that in the geforce driver.  

There's a stereo mode option you can set in xorg.conf:

[http://us.download.nvidia.com/XFree86/Linux-x86/173.14.09/README/appendix-b.html](http://us.download.nvidia.com/XFree86/Linux-x86/173.14.09/README/appendix-b.html)

and scroll down to the section on Stereo

try 
```

Option "Stereo" "4"

```

in the "Device" section, or other numbers for different stereo modes.
