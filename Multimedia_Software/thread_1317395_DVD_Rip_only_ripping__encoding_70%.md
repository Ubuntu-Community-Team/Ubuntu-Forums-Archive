### Post by KiLaHuRtZ on 2009-12-09
PSU = Program Stream Unit.  Sometimes a title has more than one.  It can cause numerous issues, one of which is A/V synchronization.  You're "chopped" encode may be a result of this.  If the title has more than one PSU, dvd::rip will automatically enable it upon encoding.  You can check the logs after a rip to see if the title does have more than one PSU.  And, to enable/disable it, there is a radio button for it under the process niceness selection within the transcode tab.
