### Post by Benzaa on 2008-10-08
Try this, it help me to change the colors on my computer.

In vlc go to settings >> preferences >> video >> Subtitles/OSD

There you will find some options for your subtitles.

You can change the color, the font type and size.

Also in the advance options you can change the background of the subtitles.


hope it helps
