### Post by fishtoprecords on 2010-02-04
> **happymcc said:**
> Is it better to go with two cards or one card with twin outputs and what brand or card is best to work with Ubuntu ? 

Most good video cards can drive two monitors using a single DVI connection with the proper splitter cable. Or get a mid-level gamer card with two DVI connections.

Both ATI/AMD and nVidea cards work well, and can directly support two monitors.

I'm using a EVGA GTS 250 1GB DDR3 PCI-E, driving two 21" LCD screens and it works fine. There is no reason to spend more than $100 on the video card unless you are a serious gamer.
