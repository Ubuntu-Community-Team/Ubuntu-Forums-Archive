### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-11-28
Not necessarily; if you have a snowy picture now on analogue, you may still get a clear picture on digital TV. You can maximise your chances by using a higher quality aerial cable from your wall socket to your set-top-box or tuner card. A cable that costs $10 more is very often the difference between watchable and unwatchable with a digital signal. I was a TV installer for 3 years, I should know :-)

I think probably the reason why there are few sites offering live streams is because there are lots of sites offering legal and illegal video-on-demand. VoD is widely believed to be the future of entertainment, so it's a lot less exciting to just retransmit a live TV stream.
