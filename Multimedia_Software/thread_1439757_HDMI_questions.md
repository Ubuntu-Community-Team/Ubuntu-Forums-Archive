### Post by Manyette on 2010-03-26
For me this works.  TV on first.  Plug in cable.  Preferences -> Display and then check Detect monitors.  For sound go to Preferences -> Sound and select Digital stereo output.  You might have to play with those two a bit, but it did work for me.
