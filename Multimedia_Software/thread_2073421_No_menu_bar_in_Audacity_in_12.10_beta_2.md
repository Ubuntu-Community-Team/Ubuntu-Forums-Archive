### Post by ankspo71 on 2012-10-20
I had this problem too, while briefly previewing kubuntu 12.10. One way to use audacity is by using the plasma widget called "Window Menubar" after adding it to your panel. 

Currently I use the LXDE desktop in Lubuntu 12.10, and I don't have the audacity menu problem there, so the problem might only exist in the KDE desktop.
