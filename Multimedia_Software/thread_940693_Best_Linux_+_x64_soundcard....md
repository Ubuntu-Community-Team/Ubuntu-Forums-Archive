### Post by pseudonym on 2008-12-13
I have an X-Plosion as well but there is not much point to getting it unless you plan on using it's main feature - real-time Dolby Digital encoding - which is only available in Windows. I use that for games, which I only play in Windows. Everything else bar one thing works fine in Linux. There is an issue with the digital out voltage switch in mixer programs - it will only stay on the 5V setting, which means you are limited to optical out if you don't want to risk damaging your speakers. This is a flaw in the driver and has been around for years in all C-Media-based soundcards.
