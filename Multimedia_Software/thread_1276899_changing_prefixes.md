Linux does not care what file suffix (not prefix) you put onthe files, it will still find them to be what they actually are, not what the jpg or gif suggests.  What are the files you download according to ubuntu, and why do you say they are in the wrong format; wrong for what reason?  Provided you can open them in something, you can always change them to something else by using **File ->Save as** or you can use gthumb (and perhaps other apps) and do a batch conversion to another format, or you can even do it with the terminal command ```
convert
```
