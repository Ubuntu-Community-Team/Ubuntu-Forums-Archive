### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-12-09
That's a huge number of tags to add, assuming most photos will have more than just a single tag.  F-spot would probably be the best answer, once you have imported all the pictures into the application.  Just be aware that f-spot will by default copy all your photos into a folder called Photos as it imports them.  Deselect this if you don't want two copies of every photo you have.  It could be useful if you edit and then still want the original, but I find it un-necessary for my purposes.
