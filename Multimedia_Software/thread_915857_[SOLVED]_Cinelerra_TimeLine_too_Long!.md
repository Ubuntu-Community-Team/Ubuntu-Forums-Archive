### Post by xx.canes.rites on 2008-09-10
Nevermind I fixed it. The issue was this, on the timeline the horizontal scrollbar widget was dang near non-existant because I had the timeline resolution set too high. On the lower left hand side of the timeline window there's a set of numbers that look like this 0:00:00:00 V  <-- that's the little arrow pointing down that you can click to lower the timeline resolution so that you can see more of your video in your timeline and it also gives you back your horizontal bar on the timeline. Alot of people were having issues with their horizontal timeline bar going missing, but this is the problem. I will flag this thread as solved.
