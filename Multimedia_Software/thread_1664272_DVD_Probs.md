### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2011-01-10
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats)

You must end up with libdvdnav libdvdread libdvdcss installed.

The latest DVDs (& some old ones) are problematic with some players.

mplayer, MythTV & VLC are the best IMO.

HandBrake & makemkv  can be useful.

Some DVDs may take up to 2 minutes to access.
Some DVDs you have to navigate to title menu immediately.
