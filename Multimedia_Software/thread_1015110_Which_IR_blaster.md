### Post by ironslave on 2008-12-27
An IR blaster Simply mimics a TV remote. you put it next to things like your VCR so that Myth TV will tell your VCR (or whatever you have hooked up) to start recording, change channels, turn off/on, Ect. 

So if you wanted to turn off your TV with your MCE remote, all you do is press the power button on it and it will turn off your TV and the PC. you get the point right?
