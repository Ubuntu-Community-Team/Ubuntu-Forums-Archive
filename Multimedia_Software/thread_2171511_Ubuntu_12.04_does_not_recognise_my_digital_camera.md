### Post by TheFu on 2013-08-31
Quick searches didn't find anything current for this camera with either ubuntu or linux. There was a video-4-linux driver for the 2.6 kernel.

At this point, looks like you'll just need to pull the SDHC memory out and connect it to your computer. Most laptops have a port for this already. If not directly, you can buy a 15-in-1 memory card to USB adapter for under $10.

Sorry, there doesn't appear to be any answer is Fuji doesn't have their camera setup to act like a normal USB storage device.
