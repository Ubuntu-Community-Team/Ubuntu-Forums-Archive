### Post by surfmonkee on 2011-01-16
look at the tool box (on mine its to the right of the transport controls) and choose the tool that looks like two arrows back to back. bottom row middle of tools.

use that tool to click and slide audio along the track.
return to the arrow tool once you are happy

the tool with two arrows up and down with a line between will allow you to drop in markers and then draw volume curves over the audio so you can alter he balance of different segments of audio along the track. (where as the volume control on the left of each track affects the entire track.

if both tracks move together, then go back to the arrow tool and click the track you want to alter, sounds like you have two tracks highlighted.
