### Post by ubergosu on 2013-01-14
I got it about a week ago - pretty good so far. Not like regular TV (don't get live local stations or anything), but lots of on-demand stuff. They've got full episodes of almost every show I've searched for so far (including old shows and more current ones), even some of the more obscure stuff. They also have a bunch of free movies, but none of them are new ones (you have to go for premium section for new stuff).  Overall I'd say definitely worth the $10.
