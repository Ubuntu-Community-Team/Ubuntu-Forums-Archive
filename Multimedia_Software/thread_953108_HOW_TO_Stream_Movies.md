### Post by Grams79 on 2008-10-19
This is as simple as pie!
Solution for everyone who has been posting about buffering, streaming and viewing troubles for years.


[B]a. Choose your movie or media.

b. Download with Firefox.

c. Wait until the download is passed 50-100MB.

d. Then select the ".part" file and open with movie player.[/B]

[COLOR="Red"]Note:
You may have to reload/open the file a few times depending on the download speed and codec type.[/COLOR]


I suggest GNOME MPlayer.
_Remember to install all the GSteamer Plugins first._
*divx, mp4, mpeg, wmv, mkv, avi, mpg, mv1, mv2, m2v, vob, mov, rm*
