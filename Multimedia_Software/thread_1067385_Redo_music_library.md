### Post by Gramps on 2009-02-11
You could do a search in Nautilus open Nautilus in your home folder and then do a search for .mp3 after a little time all your mp3's should show up. You could then highlight them all and cut/paste them where you want, like Home/Music Go to Home/Music and in Nautilus do View/Arrange-by/Name-accending. Now all your music will be in a single directory and in alphabetical order. This is assuming I understand your question.

The above procedure I think will distroy any artist/album folders that they mp3's might be in.
