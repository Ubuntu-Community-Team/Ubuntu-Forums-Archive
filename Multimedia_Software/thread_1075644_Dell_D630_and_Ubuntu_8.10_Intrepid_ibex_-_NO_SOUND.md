### Post by MatthewMetzger on 2009-02-20
I support Ubuntu on Dell D620s, similar to your D630. I haven't had any problems with the sound not working on the D620.

Test some things:

Can you use the keyboard or laptop media buttons to mute and unmute sound? Graphic should show up breifly on screen.

Can you use media buttons to increase and decrease volume? Again, graphic should show up on screen.

What types of sound files are you trying to play? Do you get any errors? If so what are they?

There are no drivers to download. They are built into Ubuntu. So troubleshooting is necessary.

Wish you well. Keep us updated.

Matthew
Orange Computer
