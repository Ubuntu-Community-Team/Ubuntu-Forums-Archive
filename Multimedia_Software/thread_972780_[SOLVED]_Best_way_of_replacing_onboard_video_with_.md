### Post by m_l17 on 2008-11-06
I received the video card early so I installed it. All I had to do was:

1. Shutdown and disable the onboard video in the bios.

2. Install the new video card.

3. Reboot

Ubuntu recognized the video card [I already had the nVidia drivers installed for the onboard video], so I didn't have to do anything :)
