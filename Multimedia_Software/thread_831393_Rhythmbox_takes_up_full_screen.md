### Post by wannadumpwindows on 2008-06-16
If you hold down the <alt> key, and then click and drag the window, can you move it so you can see your buttons?

It's not really a solution, but it'll save you from having to restart x every time you use it.

You can also click on the window, and then use <alt> f4 to close the app, just like that other OS. :)
