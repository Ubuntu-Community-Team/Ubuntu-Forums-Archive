### Post by pacoau on 2008-08-17
There is a known bug in Banshee ([https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/banshee/+bug/134120](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/banshee/+bug/134120)) that causes it to hang when deleting over 999 entries. I just removed 2000 from mine in 3-4 hits so just make sure you are deleting less than the threshold each time.
