### Post by FakeOutdoorsman on 2014-02-21
You can use ffmpeg (the real one from FFmpeg, not the fake one from Libav that is in the repository). See [How to burn subtitles into the video](https://trac.ffmpeg.org/wiki/How%20to%20burn%20subtitles%20into%20the%20video).

You can simply download a [Linux build of ffmpeg](http://ffmpeg.org/download.html#LinuxBuilds) or follow a [step-by-step guide to compile ffmpeg](http://trac.ffmpeg.org/wiki/UbuntuCompilationGuide).
