### Post by CharleyGnarly on 2011-06-15
Got it figured out. Not really a fix per say, but got it to stop locking up. What I did is open the program, do what ever changes I needed,* then* loaded the cd. Hit rip and off it went.
Then, when the ripping was done, I ejected the cd before I closed the program. No hang ups.
For what its worth, the program works well and is simple to use.
