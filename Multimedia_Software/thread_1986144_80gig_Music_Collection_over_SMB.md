### Post by zensawa on 2012-05-24
First your going to want to invest in some hard cables. I have tried doing the media center over wifi and I have found that it buffers too often. Next I would recommend setting up an XBMC machine (runs on linux, windows, or can be partitioned on its own drive).   Set up the xbmc and stream with smb over hardwire. I have this setup and it works like a charm.( and its pretty too)(album art, artist information, etc.)  else   you could copy the files over temporarily to the computer you want to use them from and then play from there.
