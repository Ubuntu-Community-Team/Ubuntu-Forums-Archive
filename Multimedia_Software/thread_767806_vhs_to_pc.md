### Post by Jon Monreal on 2008-04-26
Yes, you would need external or special hardware to get the video from a VHS tape to your computer or to a DVD.

You could use a Video Capture Card that is supported by Ubuntu and by [Video4Linux]("http://linuxtv.org/v4lwiki/index.php/Main_Page"). If you could do that, I found [this guide]("http://forum.videohelp.com/topic307679.html") on how to convert VHS tapes to DVD, but you should be able to use it for other purposes as well.

If you need further help or would like to explore more options, please feel free to reply or to [PM me]("http://ubuntuforums.org/private.php?do=newpm&u=364860").

Hope this helps you.
