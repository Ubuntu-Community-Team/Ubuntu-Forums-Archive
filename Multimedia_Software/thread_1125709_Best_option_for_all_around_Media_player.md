### Post by conorsulli on 2009-04-14
Other than songbird I would recommend Banshee media player, I have to say it has come a long way. I know it has ipod support, can view shared itunes libraries, video support, equaliser (something songbird lacks even with plug-ins) and best of all it uses native gtk+ (songbird uses the cross-platform xul-runner) which means it start up a lot quicker than songbird. Its also less buggy.
