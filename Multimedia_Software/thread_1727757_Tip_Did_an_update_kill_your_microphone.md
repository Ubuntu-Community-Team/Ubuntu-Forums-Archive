### Post by decoherence on 2011-04-12
I've been frustrated with Ubuntu recently. System updates would occasionally kill my microphone such that, under the Volume Control panel, no input device was listed.

While I was, on several occasions, going to post a rant about how these updates were messing with my audio, I decided it would be more productive to find and post a solution.

For the record, I have a Toshiba Satellite A660. To solve the problem I installed pavucontrol and, under the Configuration tab, changed Internal Audio from Analog Stereo Output to Analog Stereo Duplex. This solved the problem.

Why an update would mess with that configuration in the first place is definitely the subject of a rant but I'll spare you because I'm so pleased to have found a solution.

All the same, I'm sorry but I just gotta say it: "#$%#ing Ubuntu!!!"

Hope this saves someone some frustration.
