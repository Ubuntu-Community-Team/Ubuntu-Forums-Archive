### Post by tommcd on 2010-10-20
What video card do you have? If it ATI or nvidia, did you install the proper drivers? 
If you have installed the nvidia driver from nvidia.com, keep in mind that you may have to reinstall the driver when there is a kernel update for Ubuntu.

You can also try using **xrandr** to add undetected resolutions for your monitor:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Config/Resolution](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Config/Resolution)
