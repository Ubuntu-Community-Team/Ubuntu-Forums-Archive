### Post by itix on 2008-10-21
Gnash is a project to open up the flash interface. Unfortunately, it doesn't work 100% perfect yet and youtube among other sites have bugs with gnash.

For the moment you will have to install the official flash player to make youtube work.

It can be downloaded here: [http://www.adobe.com/shockwave/download/download.cgi?P1_Prod_Version=ShockwaveFlash](http://www.adobe.com/shockwave/download/download.cgi?P1_Prod_Version=ShockwaveFlash)

choose the .deb version.
