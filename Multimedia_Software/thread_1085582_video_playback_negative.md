### Post by rjmoerland on 2009-03-03
I had this problem once with Totem (Movie Player). Turned out that I needed to go to preferences and then somewhere slide the **Hue** slider all the way to the left. For some reason it was 'reset' to the middle position but that resulted in the wrong colours. HTH.
