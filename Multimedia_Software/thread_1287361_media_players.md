I believe Movie Player and make sure you have  Ubuntu restricted extra's.

Gives you Gstreamer Codec's.  Flashplayer.  msstcorefonts.   mp3 decoding.

In Multiverse repository these items.  Just go to Synaptic and load.

Will play just about anything you got to throw at it. Movie Player that is. 

A bit different way to install in 8.10, 9.04 or 9.10 or what ever you are using.
But you get the jist you need Gstreamer Codecs for Movie player to work properly.

In some versions need to make sure 3rd party repositorys are installed to get certain
packages, Partners and such. Medibuntu for some items. Could go on but you should be fine.
