### Post by angryhomer17 on 2008-05-13
If you haven't already, check to see if the plugin is activated. Go to edit> plugins and make sure "portable players- ipod" is checked. If it is, try unchecking it, then restarting rhythmbox and checking the box again.

This may be related to an issue I am having with rhythmbox. I get an error for Jamendo and Magnatune Store. Unable to activate those plugins, even though they are not checked. I just click close, because I don't want or need those plugins. Seems like a bug, perhaps.
