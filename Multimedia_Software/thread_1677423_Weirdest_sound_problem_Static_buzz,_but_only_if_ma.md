### Post by towheedm on 2011-01-28
This sounds like a ground loop between the amp and the sound card.  Is your amp using a power cord with a ground pin?  You could also check the cables between the amp and the soundcard.  Could be that the connection on the shirld is broken.  You may also want to try it with some desktop speakers and see if the noise is still there.

### Post by idi0tf0wl on 2011-01-28
Furthermore, see if the problem persists in headphones. If it is not your amplifier, you will (read: should) be able to hear the noise through headphones as well. If you do not, then you can be almost certain that the problem exists beyond your computer's internals.

EDIT: Though this won't necessarily explain the apparent phenomenon concerning the color ratios of your screen, it is important to approach these things empirically.
