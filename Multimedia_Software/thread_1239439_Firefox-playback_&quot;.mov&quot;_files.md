### Post by HappyFeet on 2009-08-13
Are you talking about the movie trailer site? If so, when you see the link to start the video (small, medium, large), just right click the link and "copy link location". Then open VLC and go to Media>Open Network>HTTP (from pull down menu) and paste link>Play. There may be other ways to do it, but it's the only way I've found. Apple does not want linux users to be able to view these.
