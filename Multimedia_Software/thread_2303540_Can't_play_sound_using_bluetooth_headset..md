### Post by tgalati4 on 2015-11-20
You will have to dig into the specifications for the bluetooth chipset installed on your laptop.  Most are cheap and only support mono, low-fidelity, headset profile, not A2DP which is the stereo, CD-quality, headphone profile needed by most headphones.  You can try getting a USB/Bluetooth dongle that supports A2DP.  Read the reviews for linux compatibility.

Some reading:  [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BluetoothAudio](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BluetoothAudio)
