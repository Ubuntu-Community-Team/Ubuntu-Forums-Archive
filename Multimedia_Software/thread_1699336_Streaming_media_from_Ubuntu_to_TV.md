### Post by oracle2b on 2011-03-03
Sounds like you need [ps3mediaserver]("http://code.google.com/p/ps3mediaserver/"), a PPA can be found [here]("https://launchpad.net/~paissad/+archive/pms-linux/+packages"). You could also give [MiniDNLA]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/minidlna/") a try.
