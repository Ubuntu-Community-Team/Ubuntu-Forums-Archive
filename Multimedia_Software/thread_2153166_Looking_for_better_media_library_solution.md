### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-06-10
I'm with you on the organisation of pictures, which I have always put in appropriate layered sub-folders of Pictures in my home.

I did once try just using tags, having imported into Photos, as a test, but then when I wanted to change the application I was using to manage photos, the tags were not read and it was a terrible mess.  Luckily I was still importing into the sub-folder arrangement, as I mention above, as well as just tagging, so I still had a sensible layout of pictures.

I now use digikam even though I'm on Xubuntu, or for more simple management uses I also use gthumb as I think them so much better than the alternatives.  However, I think to get the arrangement of photos you want you are going to have to manually import and sort, which in both apps you can do by date or timeline.

I can't really help with videos as I don't take them enough to worry.
