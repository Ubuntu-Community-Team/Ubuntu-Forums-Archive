### Post by Cresho on 2008-11-04
There is 5.1 audio.  Intrepid ibex has sound issues with certain or all hardware.  I am in hardy.

first off, system->preferences->sound->devices

change all to alsa-advanced linux sound architecture and under default mixer tracks, use audigy 2..well this one is mine but it must say "alsa mixer"

now double click on the speaker icon in the taskbar. edit->preferences and put a checkmark on everything.

now you can manipulate all your pcm's to full minus the actual pcm and keep surround, front, lfe, rear, side to full.

open up terminal and do a 

```
speaker-test -D surround51 -c 6
```

or a 

```
speaker-test -Dplug:surround51 -c6 -l1 -twav
```

or you can do this before.

anyway, also your application needs to be configured.  like xine or amarok, you can get 5.1 audio you just need to select alsa and 5.1
