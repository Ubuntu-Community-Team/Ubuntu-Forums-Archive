### Post by bryanl on 2012-04-20
First is to figure out what software you are going to use. Tvtime and muthbuntu come to mind as possibilities.

Then check for hardware compatibility. The [http://www.mythbuntu.org/](http://www.mythbuntu.org/) has a good rundown on this last I checked.

I am using an Hauppage 1800 TV tuner card and, while it does seem rather popular, was not well supported in Linux last I checked.

For capturing VHS output, you don't need a TV tuner and there are some USB devices that do simple composite or S-video capture.

My experience with tape capture has not been very good, even with Windows. The quality is usually rather poor, even comparing to equivalent resolution YouTube videos.
