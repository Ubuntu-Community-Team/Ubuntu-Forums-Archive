### Post by InspiredIndividual on 2009-04-03
The "Comprehensive Multimedia & Video Howto" is a great place to start: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683). 
It's very helpful for installing the necessary codecs and the like. As for my personal preferences, I preferred mplayer and vlc in Ubuntu 8.04. I think Totem has also improved tremendously in 8.10. On my system it was able to play some formats that got me in a lot of trouble with mplayer and vlc. However, in general I believe general opinion will like mplayer and vlc best.
