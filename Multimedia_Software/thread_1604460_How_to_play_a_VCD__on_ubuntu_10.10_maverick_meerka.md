### Post by sudsiv on 2010-10-24
[B]Ok
to play a vcd open up a terminal and enter: [/B]
apt-get install smplayer

now open up smplayer in aplications> sound&video > SMPlayer
next go to open> VCD
it may ask you to set a drive. set a drive and do open> VCD again
if the drive you chose does not work go to options> preferences> drives and try out each drive one by one. One of them should be the correct one.
Tada! VCD playing:popcorn:
