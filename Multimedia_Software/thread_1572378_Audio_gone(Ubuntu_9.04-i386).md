### Post by jeight on 2010-09-11
Could be a lot of things. Check your alsamixer and make sure nothing is muted. If that is not the case go to sound preferences and start looking at both hardware and output settings. It's hard to diagnose without any additional information but it should be in one of those 3 areas. My guess is it is just one change in the output settings but it's just a guess.
