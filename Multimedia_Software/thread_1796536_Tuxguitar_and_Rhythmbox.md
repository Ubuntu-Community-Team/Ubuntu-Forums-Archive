### Post by Giraffemonster on 2011-07-03
It turns out that all I really needed was to install a package which for some reason must have been uninstalled during my switch to 11.04. For anyone interested, or who is also having this problem, the package is "tuxguitar-jsa", and gives Tuxguitar access to Java's Sound API.

Issue's been fixed now. :D
