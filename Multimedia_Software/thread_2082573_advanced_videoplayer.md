### Post by SeijiSensei on 2012-11-10
I second [SMPlayer]("http://smplayer.sourceforge.net/").  It displays drop-down boxes at the top of the video window to choose audio and subtitle tracks.  It's also compatible with both the standard [mplayer]("http://www.mplayerhq.hu/") binaries and the recent [mplayer2]("http://www.mplayer2.org/") fork.

SMPlayer also has a Windows port if support for multiple platforms matters to you.
