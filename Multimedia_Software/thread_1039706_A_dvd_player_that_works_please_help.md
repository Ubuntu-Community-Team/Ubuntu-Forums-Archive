### Post by brjoon1021 on 2009-01-14
Are you getting an error message, if so, what is it ? I ask because ALL of those should work well unlessw:

1. you don't have the necessary codecs or packages installed such as libdvdcss or libdvdcss2, for example.

2. The OS can't find the DVD drive which I have had happen a time or two. Perhaps someone here is smart enough to  follow up with the command that shows what the OS sees each drive as being. From there, you can simply edit the preferences or option of each DVD player to point it in the right direction as far as finding the DVD drive is concerned.
