According to "http://mp3licensing.com/royalty/" the cost is $0.75 for decoding and up to $5 for encoding. So the cost may be $5.75 for every copy of Mp3gain.

Mp3gain's FAQ ("http://mp3gain.sourceforge.net/faq.php") claims it does not actually encode or decode the MP3.

So is possessing and using Mp3gain legal in the US without an encoder or decoder license for it? Any lawyers out there have an opinion or know of any related rulings?

Since it is in the Universe repo one would assume Debian and Ubuntu consider it legal (without a license).
