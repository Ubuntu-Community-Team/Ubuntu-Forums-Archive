### Post by Origin415 on 2008-05-24
Sometime after upgrading to Hardy and pulseaudio, VLC stopped playing sound in videos. It started with not playing the sound in only certain videos, and has progressed to be unusable. This problem does not exist on my 64-bit laptop, only my 32-bit desktop.

Totem hasn't suffered this problem, but vlc, mplayer, and gxine all have. I haven't messed around in gxine, but I made sure to manually set mplayer and vlc to pulseaudio with no difference (on my laptop, mplayer wasnt setup to play through pulseaudio, but setting this got sound playing again).

This wouldn't be so much of a problem, but Totem doesn't handle HD movies well (vlc better, but still stutters), and whenever I mess with Mplayer, it taunts me with beautiful seamless video from a 6.3GB HD movie.

These movie applications are the only programs that dont have sound, as far as I can tell.

Update: for some reason those applications and those applications alone were playing on the wrong sound cards.
