### Post by KaptRoger on 2012-11-05
Ok - problem also persists in 12.10 (an upgrade).

However, editting /etc/pulse/daemon.conf and changing

default-fragments = 8

to 

default-fragments = 16

fixes the problem.

The changed required a logout to put it into effect.
