Hi,

For those who are missing the very useful Nautilus Image Converter extension on several flavours of Ubuntu, [imgd]("https://github.com/jarun/imgd") is a multiprocessing batch JPEG/PNG image resizer and rotator. It does everything that nautilus Image Converter does and is much faster.

I've seen queries on an alternative on several forums (I was looking for one myself) and thought this is the best place to notify Ubuntu users of this new utility.
