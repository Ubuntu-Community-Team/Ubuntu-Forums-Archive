### Post by prkos on 2009-08-02
It sounds a bit weird that it should crash. Does it only happen on flickr upload? When exactly does it crash, as soon as you get to the upload page or when adding photos or when hitting Upload?

What happens when you view a flash video, does it also crash or can you view the video and use the video controls? 

Do you have greasemonkey installed with custom flicker scripts? Maybe they are the problem. If you have them try disabling them and try to upload. 

Flickr uses the flash uploader [http://www.flickr.com/photos/upload/](http://www.flickr.com/photos/upload/) but there is also a basic uploader that doesn't use flash [http://www.flickr.com/photos/upload/basic/](http://www.flickr.com/photos/upload/basic/) you can use that untill you figure out where the problem is. Also Digikam if you use it for sorting the photos has an upload to flickr feature, you may want to check it out.
