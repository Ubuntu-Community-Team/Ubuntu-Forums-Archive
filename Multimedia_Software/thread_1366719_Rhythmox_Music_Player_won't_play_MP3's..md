### Post by mac9416 on 2009-12-28
Hi, qkzoo,

To play mp3's in Ubuntu, you must install ubuntu-restricted-extras. For instructions on installing it, see [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/#Ubuntu%209.04%20%28Jaunty%20Jackalope%29%20and%209.10%20%28Karmic%20Koala%29](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/#Ubuntu%209.04%20%28Jaunty%20Jackalope%29%20and%209.10%20%28Karmic%20Koala%29)

Good luck!
