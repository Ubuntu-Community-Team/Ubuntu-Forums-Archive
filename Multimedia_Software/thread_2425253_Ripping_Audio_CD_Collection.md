### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2019-08-24
> **speartip said:**
> Thanks Seiji, I was leaning toward flac myself. Just wondered if any other format offered any extra quality.

By definition, lossless formats all have the same quality as the original source. They just compress the original source (rather than discarding information like lossy encoders).
I can't think of any reason to use ALAC unless you use iTunes and/or have Apple devices that don't support FLAC.
