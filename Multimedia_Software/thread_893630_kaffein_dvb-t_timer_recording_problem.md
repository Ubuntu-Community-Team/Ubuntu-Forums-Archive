### Post by Erlander on 2008-08-19
I am in PAL DVB land so if you have ATSC it could be different.

I would look under DVb/Configure DVB/Recording and adjust the Begin Margin and End Margin.  I think these are to allow for programs starting early and finishing late and if you have only 5 minutes between they will produce an overlap.
