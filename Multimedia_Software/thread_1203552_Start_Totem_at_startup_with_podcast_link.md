### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-07-03
If it's a local podcast on your hard disk already, it would simply be ```
totem /path/to/podcast/file
```If it's an online podcast it is probably just totem <url>.  Certainly this latter works with a stream from the internet so should work with a podcast, as far as I can see.
