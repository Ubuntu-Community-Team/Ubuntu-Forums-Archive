### Post by mc4man on 2009-12-28
As far as subs on dvd's you are for  the most part sol, very little can be done.
Vlc (or at least the ver. I'm using - 1.0.4) can force a dvd's subs position, though it's only up or down. Font size/color and left/right can't be adjusted.

(in windows there is a vob editor for dvd subs that can pretty much adjust anything on a ripped dvd - 'DVDSubEdit'

maybe try forcing them up/down and see if that helps, otherwise try another player, it may position them differently. (sub support for software dvd players is a bit of hit or miss
