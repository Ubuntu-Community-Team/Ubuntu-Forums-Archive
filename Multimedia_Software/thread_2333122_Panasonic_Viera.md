### Post by Pumba! on 2016-08-07
Hello, some more information would help, but I think the problem might be that the "D partition" is shared in WinXP, but not in Ubuntu. 
You could probably solve this by:
a) Sharing the "D partition" in ubuntu using samba
b) Pointing your TV to the share you created in Ubuntu.

Consider that your TV might see your machine with a different name when you run Ubuntu depending on how you installed it.

Hope this helps.
