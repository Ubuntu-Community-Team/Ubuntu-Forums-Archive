### Post by bpont on 2009-12-27
Brasero is unreliable.

Linux requires a certain amount of patience for a n00b...even experienced users need to google and learn some new tricks from time to time.  There's a learning curve involved.  If you want 'quick and easy', Ubuntu might not be right for you.  Sometimes it is quick and easy, sometimes it's not.

Having said that, other burner apps you can try are Gnomebaker and K3b.

Devede is very good, but it's meant to be a DVD authoring tool, and it sounds like you just want to burn an mpeg to disc.  Try one of the aforementioned apps.

Also, avi isn't a codec, it's a container format...it will eventually be replaced by mkv, a better (and open) container format.

Learn about mencoder while you're at it.

Good luck!
