### Post by rubylaser on 2011-12-28
> **VTPoet said:**
> Thanks rubylaser. I looked up your projector, but the model looks discontinued. How much do you recommend paying for a projector? What criteria did you use to get yours?

Maybe it's better to ask what you intend to do with your projector.  At the time I bought mine, 1080p projectors where $5000+.  Now, you can get a very nice 1080p projector for under $1,000.  For a HTPC, the [Optoma HD20]("http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16824219062") is a very nice unit. Or the [Epson Powerlite 8350]("http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16824157131&Tpk=Epson%20Powerlite%208350") is another very nice, although more pricey option.

If you don't need 1080p, you can find a projector much cheaper than $1,000.  Also, you'll need to factor in what the lighting conditions are in your room.  If you don't have window coverings, you'll want a projector with good ANSI lumen numbers (2500+).
