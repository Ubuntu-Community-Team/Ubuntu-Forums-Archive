### Post by mc4man on 2010-11-13
> I tested one of the disk and it played fine in Windows
The question there is - was that with the same dvd drive or on a different one?

What you're describing could be playback failing at the layer break. Most commercial dvd's these days are dual layer, the layer break would typically be in the main title.

What it also could correspond to is the outer edge area of the disk if not at the layer break itself.

If you were to start the main title in vlc and then go - Playback -> Chapter and pick one near the end can vlc navigate directly  to it?
