### Post by Bucky Ball on 2011-01-07
+1 for VLC! ;)

Easiest way is System->Admin->Synaptic package manager. Search for 'vlc', click the box next to it, apply and you're there.

It will then be in Applications->Sound and Video. Easy. No need for terminal.
