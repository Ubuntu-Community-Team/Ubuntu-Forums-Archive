### Post by NMFTM on 2011-04-17
This isn't a bug, more long the lines of something that annoys me.

In Rythmnbox, the play count section only counts a song as being played when the song ends. I'd like it to count it as played if I played at least 51% of the song.

Quite often, when a song is winding down I'll just hit the next key on my keyboard because I don't feel like hearing the final guitar chord fading out for 10 seconds. But this messes up my play count because there are a ton of songs I listen to but aren't counted. Which means, when I sort by play count, it's not entirely accurate account of what I actually listen to.
