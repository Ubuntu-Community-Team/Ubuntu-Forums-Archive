### Post by andrewc6l on 2010-02-24
It's certainly possible. What kind of card to get depends a lot on the area you live in and the broadcast standards that are in use there. In the US, I'm using a pcHDTV 5500 card connected to an antenna on the roof - that gets both HD signals and analog signals.

The Mythbuntu forums would be a good place to ask for more specific advice - there are a lot of opinions about the best card :-)
