### Post by Tarvok on 2008-07-26
Last night I decided to get Rhythmbox downloading podcasts. I simply copy/pasted the url into the "New Podcast Feed" dialogue, and it started downloading stuff; great! Then I discovered it was defaulting them to my home directory; I didn't like that. (The help file claims it defaults to the Podcasts directory; it lies!) So I changed it in preferences.

First off, the change never holds. If I quit and restart Rhythmbox, my preferences revert to default: download to my home directory.

Secondly, now that I've deleted the old locations in the home directory, it just plain won't take a podcast rss feed any more. I copy/paste into the "New Podcast Feed" dialogue, and absolutely nothing happens.

I couldn't find anything while searching the forums, which suggests that either I am having a very unique problem, or the solution is so incredibly obvious I look like a total fool. I am hoping it is the later. 

Using Rhythmbox 0.11.5

EDIT: It was, in fact, the later. I discovered, by looking at screenshots, that I was missing a panel: the Feed list. I deleted the feeds from this list, and now it works again.
