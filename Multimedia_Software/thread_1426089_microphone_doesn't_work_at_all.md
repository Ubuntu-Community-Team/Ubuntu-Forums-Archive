### Post by alextop30 on 2010-03-09
maybe some more information might be in order. Is your microphone usb. or is it connected right into your on board audio. If it is usb you have to go to the sound options and select it as the default device. if it is on the on board audio than you have to work with the settings to see if it can be made to work. Also have you tried your microphone on another computer to make sure it works. If you have another pc or you dualboot you can try it under windows and see if it works. It could very well be a hardware issue since these things break very easily (trust me I use skype too and i have changed more than 10 different microphones the last year).
