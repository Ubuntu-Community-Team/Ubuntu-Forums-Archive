### Post by BLTicklemonster on 2008-08-07
Try 

Tools, Options, and look down the list for 

Player

click it, and see where it has a checkbox for using frostwire's own player? Is it checked? Uncheck it, click apply, then click okay, then go to your library, and try to play a song, see if it comes up in your system's default media player.

If it isn't checked, then check it, and try it that way.

Hope this is of some use to you.
