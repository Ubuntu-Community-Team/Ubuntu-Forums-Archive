### Post by mingohills on 2009-03-08
There was a discussion here [http://www.uluga.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=252505&page=3](http://www.uluga.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=252505&page=3) on this. But it didn't work for me.  I installed, reinstalled, removed and reinstalled, etc.  I restarted amarok and so on. Anyway my solution.

The problem is that amarok is not downloading the media.  I solved this by downloading the media (right clicking the podcast and selecting download.)  This also solves another issue which is that when I pause for any period longer than five minutes, when it resumes it cuts off.  The download won't take but about thirty seconds for an hour program.  Hope that helps!!
