### Post by Oldhacker on 2011-09-04
After more searching without result, I went back to the basics and discovered the problem. Although the Sound Preferences slider was turned all the up to max, there is another volume control on the lower left of each Youtube screen. This being dark gray on black and small, it is easy to overlook. This slider was turned OFF. ;-)
How it got turned down is another mystery which I shall overlook for now.
