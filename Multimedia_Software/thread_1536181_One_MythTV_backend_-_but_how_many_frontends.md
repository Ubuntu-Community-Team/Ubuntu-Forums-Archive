### Post by rubylaser on 2010-07-22
Well that's kind of two different questions.  One myth-backend can handle lots of frontends for everything from Movies, Pictures, Games, but the LiveTV is totally dependent on your available tuners. Honestly, I can't answer the second part.  When I used to run a myth-backend, I had 4 tuners in it, so this wasn't really an issue for me. ### Post by aeiah on 2010-07-22
it makes sense that if you have only one tuner, you can only watch one tv channel at once.. some tv cards will have 2 tuners built in, so you can record one channel and watch another.. if you've got one of these, you could either record two channels, watch two channels, or a mixture of the two. as the above poster said, its possible to have multiple tuners and i guess if you're to take full advantage of mythtv and you have a household of people that will use it, you'll want to put quite a few tuners in the backend.
