### Post by hansdown on 2010-06-07
Hi einstenian.

Click applications> accessories> cd/dvd creator.

Click edit> preferences> media.

Change the preferences to

```
ask what to do
```

For cd and dvd.

You can add applications to open when the disk is inserted.
