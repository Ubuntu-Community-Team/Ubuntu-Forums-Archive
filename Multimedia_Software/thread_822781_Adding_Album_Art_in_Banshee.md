### Post by bnmohler on 2009-08-02
not sure if you ever figured this out but just in case:

it will add art to the album if you have a picture in the folder named cover.jpg or folder.jpg.

ex. /Music/bandname/albumname/cover.jpg

banshee will add that art to any song from that folder.

it takes a bit to update so drop in the art and give it a few and it should show up. it has always worked for me. not positive on alternate file extensions like .png, .bmp etc i always just jpg and it has worked fine
