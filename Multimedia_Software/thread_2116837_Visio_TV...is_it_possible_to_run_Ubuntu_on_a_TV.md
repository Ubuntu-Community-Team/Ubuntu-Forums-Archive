### Post by Rsxhawk on 2013-02-18
Off the top of my head I would say that this is NOT possible.  You would somehow have to tell the TV to boot off your USB stick like you would a computer through the BIOS.  But I know of no such methods to boot to external media on a TV.  You're better off just running a small media server or a laptop with Ubuntu on it and then hooking it up to the TV's RGB or HDMI input. Not to mention that I doubt visio tv's are based off an x86 architecture, which is required for ubuntu.
