### Post by amauk on 2009-12-11
check out picard (it's in the repos)
this scans music files and matches them against entries in the musicbrainz music database

Picard differs from other music taggers, in that it works on collections of tracks instead of individual tracks

group files from a single album together, then lookup info about the album
This means that it's far more accurate than conventional taggers

Check out the documentation
[http://musicbrainz.org/doc/Picard_Documentation](http://musicbrainz.org/doc/Picard_Documentation)
