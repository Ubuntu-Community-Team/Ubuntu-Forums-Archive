### Post by CarpKing on 2008-09-10
You should be able to silence it by going into the mixer (double-click the volume icon on the panel) and muting "Line-in" by clicking the speaker button in that column.  Unfortunately you'll have to unmute it next time you want to use TVTime.  There may be a better way, but I do not know it.
