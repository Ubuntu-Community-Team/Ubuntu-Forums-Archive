### Post by svref on 2009-12-30
By default, I think Nautilus should open .jpg files with an **image viewer**, but on my system it opens them with slow-starting, image-hard-to-see-because-of-window-cluttering **GIMP**.

I thought this would be easy to fix, just use Nautilus's Edit > Preferences > Media > Images: Open Image Viewer. But that has no apparent effect on the click-behavior of Nautilus!

Help!

---

### Post by HappyFeet on 2009-12-30
Right click an image and go to Properties>Open With and select what you want. You may have to do that with each type of image file. (png, jpg, etc)
