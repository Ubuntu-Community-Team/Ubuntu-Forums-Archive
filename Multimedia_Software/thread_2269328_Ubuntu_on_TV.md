### Post by Bucky Ball on 2015-03-15
You should be going to 'Settings>Display' and setting the resolution to the same as the monitor's. Your monitor will have two numbers for resolution. Yours will be probably 1080 x *** (where *** is the other number). Set the resolution in Display to the same as the monitor's. With it plugged in, you should see the laptop monitor and the external monitor. The resolution can be adjusted for either independently.

You should NOT be scaling by using the scroll wheel + control at this stage. Get the resolutions matched first.
