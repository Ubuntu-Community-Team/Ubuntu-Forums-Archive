### Post by dearephesus64 on 2011-01-14
falafel,

I was having trouble with Banshee not watching my folders, and it may have been because I had told it to watch a folder on a mounted drive.  Banshee watched that folder, but continued to watch the /home/user/Music folder as well.  There were some problems and slowdowns because Banshee got confused.

I found it works best (if your music is on another partition) to tell Banshee to watch the regular /home/user/Music folder, and just put a shortcut to where my music is located in that folder.

I have no idea if that will help, but I agree that a music player that doesn't watch your folders isn't nearly as useful.  Might as well be using itunes if you have to add the music in yourself.  Good luck!
