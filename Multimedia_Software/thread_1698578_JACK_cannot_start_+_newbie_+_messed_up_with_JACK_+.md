### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2011-03-02
JACK is a sound server, it allows you to connect audio apps and hardware together at low latency. You should have it installed already as Ardour depends on it. 
Go to the sound menu and click on jack control, press start then post what the message window displays. 

If the whole jack approach is too complex for you, take a look at Jokosher or LMMS which are easy to set up DAWs.
