### Post by Marius N. on 2008-10-26
Hi Prisma,

I have a Cowon D2 player for six months now (it's simply amazing) and I am using it with Ubuntu 8.10 RC at the moment... I have also used it with Ubuntu 8.04... It's true that I don't use any media player such as Amarok, Banshee, Exaile.... but I was just wondering why you need such a player to transfer files in D2 ?

I connect my D2 using the MSC (Mass Storage) protocol and I just drop the files in the MUSIC folder :) I also have a 2 GB Lexar SD card attached, which pop-ups every time I connect the player.

I also update my firmware with Ubuntu :) Just works, every time!

You can switch to MSC like this:

System -> Settings -> USB Connection -> MSC

Have fun!
