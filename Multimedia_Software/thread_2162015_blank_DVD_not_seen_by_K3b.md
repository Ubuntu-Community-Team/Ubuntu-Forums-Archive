### Post by Oldcowboy on 2013-07-12
Ever since I had to re-install Ubuntu 12.04 LTS I am unable to burn any DVD's from an ISO file. K3b just tells me to insert a blank DVD or Blue Ray even though I already have one inserted. The START button is grayed out. Apparently, others have encountered this behavior in the past but the solution remains elusive.

---

### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2013-07-12
Are you able to burn discs with other software? If other software fails then it might be a failed disc drive causing the issues.

---

### Post by Oldcowboy on 2013-07-13
SOLVED:
I downloaded Bonbono and it saw the file from another drive, but complained that the image would not fit on my DVD disc. Then, I returned to K3B from this other drive.
It saw my disc this time, but said that the disc was not empty. Put in another blank DVD+ and K3b burned it normally. Then, I returned to the first drive, which is Lightscribe compatible
and it burned the label as usual. My conclusion is that the blank DVD+ that I was first trying was defective and therefore was the root of the problem.

Thanks for giving me a hint that pointed to the correct answer.
