[COLOR="DarkRed"]An .avi or .mpg file needs to be encoded first to dvd mpeg-2 format.  For encoding, I used command line tovid-interactive, a subset of the tovid package.   Once you have the .mpg then you author the disc.  For this I use devede.    devede can also encode, I just prefer the tovid.

Be prepared to make many 'coasters' before getting the process down to factory work :-):popcorn:[/COLOR]
