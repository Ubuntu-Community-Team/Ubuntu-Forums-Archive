### Post by Pinoy Tux on 2012-07-27
Not meaning to sound curt, but most -- if not all -- of your questions are answered if you familiarize yourself FIRST with OpenShot by perusing the OpenShot Manual.  Click Help > Contents (or press the F1 key).  Even if you're an absolute beginner, the manual gives step-by-step tutorial-style procedures on how to do basic things with OpenShot.  There are plenty of illustrations/screencaps as well.

You'll also find a ton of video tutorials on YouTube if you do a search.  There are plenty of information at the official [OpenShot Users forum]("http://openshotusers.com/forum/").

That said, the answers to your questions are found under "Separate Audio from Video," "Move & Trim Clips on Timeline," and "Clip Properties" sections in the manual.
