### Post by damnated on 2009-10-30
I recently upgraded to Karmic Koala, and surprise surprise, i couldn't install Amarok 1.4 any more, so i opted for the next version that was available. 

However, there was a problem. After i load in a folder of songs, they wont play, instead the player skips through each and every one of them, stopping at the end of the playlist. 

I have the restricted drivers installed, sound works etc, everything plays fine in Rythmbox, so it's definitely an Amarok issue. 

Any ideas?

EDIT: I was missing **libxine1-ffmpeg. **Problem solved lol.
