### Post by Merrattic on 2013-02-14
In Devede, when you add a file (your DVD compliant file!), go into **Properties**, then **Advanced options**, then the **Misc** tab, there are two options there that will stop Devede re-encoding the file.

### Post by speartip on 2013-02-15
It shouldn't sacrifice any quality.
What quality in kbps is the video on the original file?
Most are only 1500-2100kbps, whereas devede trys to burn at 5001kbps. So really you're just wasting disc space, as you cannot improve on the quality of the original file.
