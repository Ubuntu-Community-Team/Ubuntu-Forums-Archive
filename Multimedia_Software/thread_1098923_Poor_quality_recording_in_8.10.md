### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-03-17
Have you tried audacity to record from the sound card?  I use it quite often with very good results, but it takes a bit of fiddling to get the levels right.  I set the *gnome volume control* window switches tab to Mix, the recording capture level quite low, and the playback PCM level also quite low, otherwise there is a lot of clipping and too high a level.  See my attached pics for a sample of my levels.

Give this a try if you haven't already; it certainly works well for me.
