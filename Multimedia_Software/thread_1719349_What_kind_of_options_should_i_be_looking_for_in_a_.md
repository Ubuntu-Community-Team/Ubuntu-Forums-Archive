### Post by pdwerryhouse on 2011-04-02
Most important thing is the tuner sensitivity. I bought a Gigabyte USB digital TV tuner, and the reception on it is terrible, even with a good antenna connected. It's pretty much useless. I've compared it with my TV's tuner and the tuner on a PCI card, and it's definitely the USB tuner's problem.

Also, a card with multiple tuners on it is always a nice feature.

Perhaps a TV card with a digital radio receiver too?
