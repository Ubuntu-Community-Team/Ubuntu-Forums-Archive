### Post by SVEN1 on 2011-05-18
CPU is getting too hot and or your video card. Open up your case and check for dust and lint clogging up your heat sink fans.

Another possible problem if cleaning doesn't solve it is the CPU thermal paste. Might not be applied correctly.
