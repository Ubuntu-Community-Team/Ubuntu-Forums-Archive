### Post by jamesrl on 2008-10-10
Well you could simply copy all the mp3 files you can see on the Ipod in their weird directory format and file names to your new hard drive.  And then have programs like easytag (sudo apt-get install easytag-aac) move/rename the files to logical locations based on the ID3 metadata in the mp3 files.

A google search also finds a program that claims to do exactly what you want:
[http://www.yamipod.com/main/modules/home/]("http://www.yamipod.com/main/modules/home/")
