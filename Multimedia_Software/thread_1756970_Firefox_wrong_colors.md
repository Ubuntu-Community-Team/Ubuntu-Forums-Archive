### Post by blaz_boy on 2011-05-12
i found the solution,
i installed a firefox color management plugin, tried to turn it on for all images, didn't work.
turn it on for ICC only images and also didn't work
tuned it off totally and uninstalled the plugin and tada it fixed the problem.
