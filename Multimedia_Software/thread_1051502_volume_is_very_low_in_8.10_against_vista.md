### Post by Mud.Knee.Havoc on 2009-01-26
Your volume control in the taskbar only controls the master volume.  Doubleclick this instead of adjusting it.  It will bring up a mixer.  In my case, I need to turn up Master and PCM and I'll get full volume (it's way too loud, so I turn both of them down a bit).
