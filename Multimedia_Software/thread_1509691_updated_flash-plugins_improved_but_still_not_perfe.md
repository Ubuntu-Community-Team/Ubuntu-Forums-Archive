### Post by robério1 on 2010-06-14
If you go to teoria.com you'll see a page with a green background color. Look for Exercises (second title, right side); click on it and in the new page search for Intervals, and beneath it, Identification; click on it and you will hear some music and see a page where you may choose the intervals you want to train - you can just accept the default; click OK. The next page is the actual exercise page; it should have a widget at the left, with a speaker and you should hear an interval, which you would try to recognize. But there is no widget and no sound.
The rest of the site functions OK. You may have clicked in a keyboard that was somewhere just for demonstration purposes: there are places in the tutorials where  no sound is needed and none is produced; but if you try to read about intervals-2nd for example, the notes "played" in the keyboard will be shown with a dot on them, and you'll hear the corresponding sounds. You won't be able to play anything by clicking in it, though.
The site (which I consulted) considers that  the problem originates in the recent linux flash-plugin, and recommends that I keep a close look for any updates.
