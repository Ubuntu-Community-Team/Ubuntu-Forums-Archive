### Post by faust99 on 2013-12-05
Sound Converter gives three compression options for conversion to FLAC; they're a bit confusing to me. Perhaps somebody could please explain the diffrerence between the first and third options, which are Less (Faster) and Better (Slower).

These seem counterintuitive to me as I would have thought that Less compression = Better sound, but Less and Better seem to be mutually exclusive.

---

### Post by bachtobach on 2013-12-05
The less and better are not referring to sound quality, they refer to the compression itself. Less compression is faster because the program has to do less work, and Better is slow because it is doing everything it can to make the file smaller.
