### Post by LowSky on 2011-07-16
Those speakers probably need at least 20 watts of power to perform well. Your PC wont even put out 2 Watts from the sound card. Most bookshelf or free standing speakers operate near 100 Watts. I would either eBay a new receiver/amp or real computer speakers.
