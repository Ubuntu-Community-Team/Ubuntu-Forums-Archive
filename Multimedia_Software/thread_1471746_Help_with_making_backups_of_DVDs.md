### Post by dagrump on 2010-05-03
Just off the the top of my head, acidrip, dvdrip, omgrip, thoggen, & my favorite handbrake (which seems to be borked at the moment).
All of these will crush those movies into more useful sizes for back up, it does require a bunch of processor power though.
Thoggen gives ogg files, if you can live with that. Large external drives are cheap anymore & sometimes it's just easier to create an iso file & mount that with VLC. 
I find myself falling behind in backing my collection up, as even with a quad core it takes about the same amount of time to rip & crush, as to watch the movie.
Good luck.
