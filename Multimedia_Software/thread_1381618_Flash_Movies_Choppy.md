### Post by quixote on 2010-01-16
If you right-click on a flash clip, a pop-up appears.  Click on Settings, and then the little folder-icon at the bottom.  That lets you increase the size of the buffer flash uses.  Sometimes *reducing* the size of the buffer helps because then the flashplayer doesn't stop to refill the buffer.  Sometimes it needs a larger buffer.  This hasn't always worked for me, but it is one thing you can try.
