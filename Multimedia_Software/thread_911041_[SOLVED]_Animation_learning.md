### Post by lovinglinux on 2008-09-05
Blender is the first choice, that's for sure!

Take a look at this [short movie]("http://www.bigbuckbunny.org/") produced with Blender.

It takes some time to get use to the interface and the endless amount of options, but if you practice and follow the tutorials you will feel very comfortable with it.

Visit [http://www.blender.org/](http://www.blender.org/) for tutorials and stuff.

A good starting point [http://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Blender_3D:_Noob_to_Pro](http://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Blender_3D:_Noob_to_Pro)
