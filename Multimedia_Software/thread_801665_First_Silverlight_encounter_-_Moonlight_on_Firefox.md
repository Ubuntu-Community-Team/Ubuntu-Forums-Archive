### Post by hotweiss on 2008-05-29
I actually got the Silverlight Comedy Central South Park episodes to play on Firefox 3.

1. install mplayer and the mozilla plug-in
> sudo apt-get install mplayer mozilla-mplayer

2. when prompted, click on watch without installing Silverlight

PS-if it instantly stops, just press play again and it should work
