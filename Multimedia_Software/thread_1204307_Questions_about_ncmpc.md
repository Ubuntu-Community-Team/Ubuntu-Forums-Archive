### Post by masque7 on 2010-07-21
Sort the playlist:
Delete d : Delete song from playlist
                   c : Clear playlist
              Ctrl-K : Move song up
              Ctrl-J : Move song down

Alphabetical?: 
It just reads the database. Sort it via song number for me. If I'm in /Music and I add all of it, think it does it alphabetically.

Que a track:
Press 3 or 4 to browse the directory as normal, or do browse via artist. Press A or spacebar to que/add the track


Lyrics:
Press F7 or 7. U to download. Space to refresh.

Just press F1 or 1 to read the controls.
