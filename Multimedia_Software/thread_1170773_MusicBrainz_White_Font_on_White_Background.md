### Post by UltraAnders on 2009-05-26
I was going to post a question, but after typing it all out I  worked out a kind of fix. I thought I'd share this still because it really annoyed me.

The issues seems to occur with themes like "DarkRoom" where the "Window Text" is white. MusicBrainz displays file names with a white font on a white background. Despite the fact that the "Window Background" is not set to white.

First attached screen shot attached shows you the initial problem. The really annoying thing is sometimes MusicBrainz opens with a complete white background, black text, other times it attempts to use the theme colours, badly (see second screen shot).

On a side note, when changing the font colour, most apps update as soon as you change it. MusicBrainz must be re-loaded before it will update the colours.
