### Post by foo-monger on 2009-05-05
Your Vista partition should be already mounted within > PLACES.

Then just find the folder- C Drive > Users > 'YOU' > Music > iTunes > iTunesMusic.

All your Music is in here, but it may not be labeled as you expect; its all in numbered files.

Hope this helps.
