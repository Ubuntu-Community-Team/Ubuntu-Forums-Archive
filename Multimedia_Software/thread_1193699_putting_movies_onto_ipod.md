### Post by davidryderuk on 2009-06-22
Hi,
Banshee seems to have limited support for transferring videos to the ipod.

[http://banshee-project.org/](http://banshee-project.org/)

You can install it from the repositories. I would offer four pieces of advice though.

1. Upgrade your ipod to the latest firmware version with itunes before using banshee. Banshee does not work well with older firmware (at least it didn't with my ipod). 

2. When you first import music into banshee it will split up compilation albums into multiple parts. In order to reverse this you have to add a "Complimation album artist" in the "Edit track information" window.

3. itunes does not like to share with banshee, so it's best to stick to one or other of the programs otherwise you risk loosing information (banshee does warn you of this before doing anything to your ipod)

4. Lastly please make sure you backup your music collection before trying this. 

This worked fine with my ipod nano, but i did end up wiping my ipod a couple of times before getting it to work properly.
