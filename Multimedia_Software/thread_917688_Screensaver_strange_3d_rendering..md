### Post by Eggbertx on 2009-03-21
Well, I'm not an Ubuntu expert, but I know enought about 3d and 3d modelling to know it is either your video card or it wasn't made correctly. In a 3d model, you have things called normals, which determine which is the inside and the outside ofthe model, and where the textures go. The normals could be inverted (which is probably the maker's fault).

Also, is it just that one? You may want to get the X Screensaver instead of the Gnome one. The instructions are [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=195557")

Hope this all helps.
