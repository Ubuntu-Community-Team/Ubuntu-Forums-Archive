### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2009-06-03
Not sure how it might all work out but I remember when buying a Bluetooth adapter for my desktop that there are adapters that support the data protocol (what most computer devices have) and some that support audio protocol (like cell phone headsets) and some that do both. Unless you have one that does both in your computer then it probably won't work, let alone the other things to sort out to get it working. 

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bluetooth#Bluetooth_protocol_stack](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bluetooth#Bluetooth_protocol_stack)

That's some more information about the different protocols for the Bluetooth devices, remember that not all device support all protocols (or at least their software/drivers don't). Cheers!
