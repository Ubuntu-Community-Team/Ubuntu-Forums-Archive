### Post by tijmz on 2008-07-02
Clicking files in both the collection menu and the files menu adds them to the current playlist (the central list of songs). From this playlist, clicking will play the song, but it's impossible to play a song without having it in the current playlist (which is, I think, what you're asking).

If you want to have a certain song played next in line, you can right-click it and queue it. This can also be done from the files and collection menu. If you want to control the placement of the song, you can drag-and-drop it somewhere in your playlist. Adding songs by using the clicking you mentioned will always add them to the end of your playlist.

If you have no idea where in the playlist the song you want to play is, there should be a search form above your playlist. Typing (parts of) the artist's name, the song name, the release year, etc, will narrow down the entries in your playlist.

Hope that helps. Amarok won't do what you're asking for exactly, but I think the abovementioned methods work well, too :)
