### Post by mc4man on 2009-04-29
Yeah overall it's not so great.

With a razor mouse can actually drag the progress tab fairly well, though better control backwards.

Your best bet are the hotkeys which are editable, the easiest to use are very short (Shift+arrow key), short (Alt+arrow), and medium (Ctrl+arrow)


One click on my mousewheel with the cursor on timeline gives me the "long jump"

What absolutely doesn't work well is if you slowly move the mouse cursor along the timeline you'll get a time/position in a little yellow box, clicking a specific time is always wrong (for me always moves to the cursor position but time is usually 2 min. or so off


Edit:
your plus and minus keys give you a fast foward of sorts 
range of 0.03x - 8x
