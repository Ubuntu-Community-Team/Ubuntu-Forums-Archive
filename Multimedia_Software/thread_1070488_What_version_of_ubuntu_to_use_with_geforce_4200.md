### Post by mikewhatever on 2009-02-15
You could try 8.04, but I don't think it matters. The restricted driver for your card, and any other, is not on the cd, and when you run from live cd, an open source driver is used. Both 8.10 and 8.04 have restricted nvidia drivers for your card, so it is only the matter of what works for you.
