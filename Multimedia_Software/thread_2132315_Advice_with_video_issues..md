### Post by SeijiSensei on 2013-04-09
The "video filters" warning means that you cannot do things like take screenshots or otherwise apply one of mplayer's various post-processing filters.  That is because the video processing happens on the NVIDIA card out of reach of mplayer.

What if you try xv or one of the opengl alternatives instead of vdpau?

Another possibility is that you are trying to watch an H.264 video that uses "10-bit" color encoding.  The VDPAU hardware has no circuitry to decode these so you need to fall back on using the regular CPU with xv or opengl.

You can look in Options > Logs > MPlayer to see what errors mplayer threw when it tried to play your file.  An easier option is simply to run mplayer from the command line and see what it reports:

```
mplayer /path/to/your/video/file
```
