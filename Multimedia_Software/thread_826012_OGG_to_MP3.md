### Post by TenPlus1 on 2008-06-11
Pop into Add/Remove in the menu's and search for SoundConverter... from there you can drag & drop your ogg files into the window and setup the prefs to convert them to mp3 audio (you may need gstreamer mp3 libraries installed for this part)...

### Post by suri.mahendra on 2008-06-15
In addition, you will need to edit your preferences.  Click on edit and then click on preferences.  There will be a section for "convert to" there will be numerous options.  Running for the first time the MP3 button will be light this means it is not accessible.  Scroll down there is MP3 help with a link.  Click on this link and a redirection will occur.  Provided you are running (K)Ubuntu 7.x and greater, follow the links to download the MP3 software.  Reopen and the ability to convert to MP3 should be present.
