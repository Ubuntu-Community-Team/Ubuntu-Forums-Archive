I wanted to post this here before filing a bug report.  I will warn everyone ahead of time, I am using a xorg-edgers ati driver (to enable DRI support for r600 chipsets, like my Mobility HD2400), however, this problem was present even with the driver that is in the Ubuntu repos.  

Whenever I try to suspend my laptop, all seems to go fine, but it will not wake up.  Usually, I'm greeted by a garbled display (some green/other colored lines or just a black screen).  

I've finally got my graphics card working well, and this is one of the last things on my "to-do" list.  It's getting a bit annoying having to shut down/restart every time I want to put my laptop down, or to class or anywhere really.
