### Post by eddier on 2012-12-02
Install Gnomad2 from the repo's.

Connect your device (usb)-open Gnomad and your good to go.

I am assuming it is similar to the Zen device.

For anyone trying to convert videos using FFmpeg and the Creative Zen preset,you will need to set the video bitrate to 500k,the default setting is way to high and wont work.
