### Post by g7viu on 2012-01-01
Hi

Don't know how much this will help but I have an audiophile 2496 from m-audio.  It is a pain to get going through ubuntu as you need to download and install alsa-tools-gui to configure the audio output side of the card.  It has loads of input/output options for sound as well as full MIDI implementation.

The audiophile card is not a cheap option, and if you are only wanting the midi capability have a look at the edirol offerings by Roland.  I have the UM-5 which I have used through Ubunto 8.04 which was plug n play (perhaps I was lucky I don't know)
 
Hope this helps and good luck with it
