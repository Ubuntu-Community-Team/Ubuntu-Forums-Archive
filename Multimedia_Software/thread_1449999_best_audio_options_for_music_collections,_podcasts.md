### Post by ron999 on 2010-04-08
Hi
I have Gnome desktop with Ubuntu.
For listening to streaming radio I'm using Rhythmbox.

RadioParadise 128kbps aac plays well with this link:-
[http://www.radioparadise.com/musiclinks/rp_128aac.m3u](http://www.radioparadise.com/musiclinks/rp_128aac.m3u).
:guitar:


Rhythmbox can also be used for podcasts and organizing local music files too.
It uses gstreamer plugins.

I'm not saying it's the best, but it seems to do the job.
