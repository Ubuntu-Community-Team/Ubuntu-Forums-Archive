### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-03-14
Why do you need MythTV? 

MythTV is an excellent media center and powerful application, but it has a lot of options and configuration details. So maybe there are better solutions for you at this moment, depending on what do you need.

If you want to configure a media center PC server that will record and stream TV or videos to other computers on your local network, then MythTV is the right tool. But if you just need to watch videos and listen to music on your desktop, then maybe you should consider [XBMC]("http://xbmc.org/").
