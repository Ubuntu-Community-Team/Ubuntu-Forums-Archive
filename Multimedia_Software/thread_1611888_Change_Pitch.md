### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2010-11-02
I doubt there is a video player that will do this during play. Likely it will have to be done offline and rendered, then played. There are plenty of video editors out there that can slow down the video but you might need to use a separate audio editor to keep the audio on pitch. Audacity should be able to handle it and then import the audio back into the video editor.
