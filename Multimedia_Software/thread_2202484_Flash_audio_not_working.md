Hi All,

Prior to yesterday I had all my sound working with no issues.  Yesterday something popped up about upgrading flash player which I did, and now I have no audio for flash applications in both chromium and firefox (youtube, pandora, etc).  My regular system sounds work fine when playing videos, and when playing youtube, it is showing under the sound settings application tab....there is just no physical sound.  I have done some searching but pretty stumped on this.  I have uninstalled and reinstalled flash to no avail.

### Post by matthewhrose on 2014-01-29
I was able to get this fixed.  I noticed that my HDMI was set as the default output somehow.  I simply installed pavucontrol and turned off HDMI and now it is working fine again.
