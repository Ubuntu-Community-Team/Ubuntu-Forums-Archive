### Post by boombox1387 on 2010-02-22
Assuming your using Karmic, there's an unfortunate bug in the included version of Banshee that doesn't allow Banshee to interact with iPods.  Updating to the newest version of Banshee should do the trick.  You can update by adding the [Banshee Team PPA]("https://launchpad.net/~banshee-team/+archive/ppa") to your software sources list.  After that, run update manager, and things should work fine.  If they don't, report back here and I'll do what I can.
