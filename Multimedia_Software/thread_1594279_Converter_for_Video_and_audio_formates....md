### Post by seventhsamurai on 2010-10-12
Handbrake works best for everything. Provides the best quality using FFMpeg libraries. Almost all devices today can play mp4 files.

If you are an advanced user, and know the specs you want, its a good tool. It doesn't have device presets such as Arista. You'll have to create your own.

You can also try Transmageddon. It's decent.
