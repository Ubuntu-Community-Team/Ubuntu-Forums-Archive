### Post by Anxious Nut on 2009-08-09
From what I have recently read, there is still no MSN client for linux that has voice chat(conversation) or video chat(conversation).

Most of MSN Live messenger's properties and options are found in [amsn]("http://www.amsn-project.net/") and [mercury]("http://mercury.im/").

What [aMSN]("http://www.amsn-project.net/") has over [Mercury]("http://mercury.im/"):
-Voice Clips

What [mercury]("http://mercury.im/") has over [amsn]("http://www.amsn-project.net/"):
-works well with right-to-left languages (Arabic and Hebrew)

Those two client are the best -to me- of the most similar MSN clients to MSN messenger

I hope I helped.
