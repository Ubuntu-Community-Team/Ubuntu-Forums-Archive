### Post by Schrute Farms on 2010-10-20
Just about anything will work.  It's all up to your ears and budget.  Even a cheap vintage amp from a garage sale would work great.
It just has to have a line in or tape input, as most do.  Going from the computer speaker out port to the line/tape in is the easiest way to set it up, although I'm sure there are others.
If you don't have one, you would have to buy a RCA to 1/4" adapter, but they're pretty cheap at radio shack or online.
