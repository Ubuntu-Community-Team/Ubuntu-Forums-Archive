### Post by AKADAP on 2009-10-17
> **fkachinski said:**
> My monitor is classified as unknown in the display option.

This can happen if you are using a VGA to coax breakout cable to connect your monitor. This happens when the PC can't query the monitor as to its capabilities. If this is the case, try a VGA cable, HDMI, or DVI connection, and see if it fixes the problems.
Otherwise, you are going to have to figure out how to manually tell linux your monitors capabilities.
