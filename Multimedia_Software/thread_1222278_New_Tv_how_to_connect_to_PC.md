### Post by Whiffle on 2009-07-24
There should be a cord that came with your nvidia card to convert from whatever connector they used on it to various outputs.  The one for my 7600GS had svideo, composite, and RGB.  

I think for an HDTV you'd want to avoid S-Video and go with HDMI or DVI.

### Post by Whiffle on 2009-07-24
Should be pretty much plug and play, just like any other computer monitor.  So long as you're using the restricted drivers, which it sounds like you are.

One thing I have noticed is that if I plug the Svideo into my computer while its running, it won't pick up the TV.  But if I hook it up, and then boot the computer, it works perfect.
