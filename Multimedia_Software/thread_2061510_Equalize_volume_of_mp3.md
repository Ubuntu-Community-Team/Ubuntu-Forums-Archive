### Post by wheeze on 2012-09-22
**easymp3gain-gtk**

I have a 1800 MHz CPU and more RAM than your computer, and it runs just fine. It's not terribly memory intensive, it uses the CPU and disk more than RAM.

You can process your files in batches if you want. It would probably take most of a day to do all 1000 in one go.
