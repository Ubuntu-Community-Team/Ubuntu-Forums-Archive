### Post by treehouse on 2008-09-20
After a stupidly long time I fixed it. Here's how in case anyone else has a similar problem.

The card is a 3dfx Voodoo3 running a Samsung Samtron 76DF monitor with a max resolution of 1280x1024.

I still don't know why my card stopped running on that particular PCI slot, I now have nothing plugged in but I'll try swapping in a non-video card to see what happens.

The problem was caused in my xorg.conf file (which I REALLY wish that the dpkg-configure utility stilled worked for) because the card moved slots, but the info in the file didn't reflect the change in the "BusID" line.
