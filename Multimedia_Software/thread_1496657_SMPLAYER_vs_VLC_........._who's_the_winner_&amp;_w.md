### Post by beew on 2011-05-28
Smplayer hands down. Try play a 1080P in VLC and it chokes. Same in Windows as well since VLC uses only one core even in multi-core machines (that will change as ffmpeg is now officially multi-threaded) I often have smooth playback with Smplayer when VLC just can't cut it. If you have a Nvidia card then all the more reason to vote for Smplayer(mplayer) because vlc can't use vdpau.
