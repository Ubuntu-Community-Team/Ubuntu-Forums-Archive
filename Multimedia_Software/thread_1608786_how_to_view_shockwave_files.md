### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-11-10
I have just found this post by accident!

BBC videos will not play in countries other than UK unless you are connecting through a UK based proxy server.  That may be the reason why the problem has occurred in all cases mentioned in this post.

Also could be a java problem.  Try installing **sun-java6-jre** with all its dependencies and also **sun-java6-plugin**.  I think they are needed for many flash sites.
