### Post by TheFu on 2015-04-24
Try **youtube-dl** and/or **simplescreenrecorder** - one downloads and the other captures. You'll need to edit with some other tool - **avidemux** I suppose. I use Windows for editing, I'm sorry to say.

**guvcview** is another tool for recording stuff on a webcam. 

To record audio, I use **audacity** with a $35 USB microphone. It matters.

Of you want to capture the screen AND have a webcam put your "talking head" in the corner and control different audio sources, there's software called OBS [https://obsproject.com/](https://obsproject.com/) . It is a little flaky but if they get that ironed out, it will be awesome!
