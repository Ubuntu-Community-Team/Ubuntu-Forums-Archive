### Post by bmac on 2008-06-03
Open Volume Control
Edit Preferences
check the PC speaker box
Close edit preferences
PC speaker should be shown in the main volume control window

Left click on the speaker at the bottom of the pc speaker column - which should mute your pc speaker....

Hope this helps.....
