### Post by Psychoscorpic on 2008-07-15
I've had good success with the UnPlug add-on for Firefox, combined with the DownThemAll add-on.
UnPlug will isolate content on sites like YouTube, allowing you to download the video clips.
It picks up on DownThemAll, giving you a multi-stream download for the video etc.

VLC Media Player, on top of playing almost anything else very well, also happily plays the resulting getvideo.flv files from YouTube.
