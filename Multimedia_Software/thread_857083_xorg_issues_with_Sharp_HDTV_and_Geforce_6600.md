### Post by Gr3y on 2008-07-14
Okay so as far as I can tell here's what was wrong. The old chaintech I had in there wasn't outputing through the DVI cable in a way my TV could understand. I bought a new Radeon HDxxxx, plugged it in, and it worked in both 7.10 and 8.04. The resolution display on my TV even showed that it was accepting 1080p instead of 1400x1050. 

Best I can tell is that the 6600 may have only been speaking in DVI-a while I needed either DVI-d or DVI-i. 

Anyhoo this is fixed for now.
