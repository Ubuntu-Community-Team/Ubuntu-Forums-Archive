### Post by wolfen69 on 2009-04-12
since the driver for the card is installed to the hard drive, and not the card itself, it is highly unlikely the driver killed it. as a pc repair tech, my first thought would be that the card went bad on its own. a power surge or spike can be at fault, among other things. i actually see this kind of thing alot. i realize people find it hard to believe that their hardware can just quit like that, but it happens. i myself had 2 mobos, 2 power supplies, a video card, and 2 dvd drives all die on me in 2 years. there's nothing you can do about it.

i hope you find a solution, but it doesn't look good. like i said, i highly doubt it was the drivers that killed it.
