### Post by mjitkop on 2010-02-17
Hi, Nero60.

In order to transfer video from VHS to DVD, you will need hardware. 

There are 3 possibilities I can think of:

1/ video capture card connected to your computer (external card via USB or Firewire) or internal capture card (PCI, PCI-X)

OR

2/ use a standalone DVD recorder that receives in one of its inputs the video output of your VCR

OR 

3/ use a VCR/DVD recorder combo, which would be the easiest way to do it

I use the second option and I capture the VHS as is straight to DVD and I have a master copy. 

Once you have a master DVD, you can easily extract the video from it in your Ubuntu. There are several DVD rippers available in Ubuntu for free (vobcopy, DVD::Rip and more). Finally you can edit the video and burn the to DVD as you please.

I think your main concern at first is the actual transfer from VHS to DVD.

Good luck. :D
