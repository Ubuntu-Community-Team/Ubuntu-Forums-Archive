### Post by bcarlowise on 2012-01-10
Why not have an external USB drive to act as your photo storage medium instead of the internal HDD. Once the drive is connected it can be accessed by whoever is logged into the machine. If you need the drive to be secure you could use Truecrypt to encrypt the drive and mount it when needed.
