### Post by Silverwulfe on 2009-05-12
As far as installing software goes, it's not supported.  But go ahead and plug it in.  You can still see the menu structure.  Go to the DCIM folder in the root and you can browse to the Videos under 100Video.  They are AVI files and I was able to load up the codec immediately and begin playing the video.  
I just copied them to my Videos folder and it was quick and easy.  Maybe it doesn't work like it does on PC or MAC, but if you treat it like a USB drive, it works as a really portable video camera, particularly with the netbooks.
