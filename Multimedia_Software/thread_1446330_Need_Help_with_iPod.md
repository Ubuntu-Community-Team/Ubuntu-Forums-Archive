### Post by DreVinci on 2010-04-04
Hi, for recognizing your iPod, I guess it would depend on what you have installed already? Doing an install of ubuntu-restricted-extras should get your iPod recognized on your system.
Although for me, after installing Xubuntu 9.10 straight out of Wubi, my 3rd Gen iPod Nano was recognized in /media.
From there, I just set up Exaile to get to /media/my_ipod_name/iPod_Control/Music which holds all of the individual music files on the iPod. (Although it still couldn't play .mp3 files so I had to download some other packages)
However, this is just for playing music. If you would like to add/delete and manage the music, you can get gtkpod as Chemical Imbalance pointed out.
