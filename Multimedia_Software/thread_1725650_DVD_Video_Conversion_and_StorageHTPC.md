### Post by andrew.46 on 2011-04-10
> **PipeBoss said:**
> What's the best format to convert the DVD movie into? 

Just to answer one of your queries :). I believe the best current format is h.264 video and aac sound in an mp4 or mkv container. For myself I tend to use FFmpeg + x264 for the video, NeroAacEnc for the sound, mkvtoolnix for the matroska container or MP4Box for an mp4 container. There will be many other opinions though I'm sure :).
