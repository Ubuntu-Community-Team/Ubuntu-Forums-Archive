### Post by bkadoctaj on 2010-04-09
Well, folks, I chose to automatically have Rhythmbox open after mounting my iPod with Nautilus in Ubuntu Lucid Beta 1 and regretted the decision once I discovered how much better Pana works with an iPod than Rhythmbox does.

I could not figure out how to prevent Rhythmbox from opening automatically until just now.  I clicked Computer in the Gnome menu, then clicked Edit > Preferences > Media and changed the Music Player entry to "Ask what to do".  I could just as easily have chosen "Do nothing".

I hope this helps someone.  :D
