### Post by speed32219 on 2008-08-11
The absolute best is XBMC (you hack an old Xbox with a linux kernel).  It does almost everything you could want.  You can make your own Media Center for less than $100 and use your Ubuntu box as a video server like I do. 

The only thing that Xbmc deosn't support is native 720 and 1080 becuase of its procesor limitations.  It upscales 480i/p to 720p or 1080i and does a very good job of it.

Here is a link to a community forum that is very active with it (It has an old skin, many more updated ones are available but that is what mine looks like on a 110" screen).  I will also post the link directly to the xbmc site.  Good luck on your searching for a viable Media Center.  PS.  I use Xbmc as my front end and Ubuntu as my video server back end with 1.5TB of storage and growing.
