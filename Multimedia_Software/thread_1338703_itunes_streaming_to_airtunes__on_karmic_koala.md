### Post by pc10pc on 2010-01-16
ubuntu can stream any audio to airport express, using the pulse audio control.
in UNR i had to install a few packages, pulse audio raop play and padevchooser. you get them in the synaptic package manager.
then all i have to do is right click the volume icon and in the options dialogue under 'output' my airport express is available to chose.
ive just started with it, a wee bit choppy so far but the other posts ive seen say this is normal to start with.
this way you can use amarok or songbird, or whatever, using your windows music folder
