### Post by vandorjw on 2010-05-14
I was wondering the same thing.
I just installed LMMS on Lucid and selected pulse-audio.

I find no more latency or lag using pulse than when I use alsa,

The one problem I have with LMMS is that the tooltips are blank. There is no visible text inside...although that might be because of the colour scheme. (White text)

EDIT:
To switch to pulse-audio, click edit---> Settings --> Audio Settings ---> Then use the drop down menu and select pulse.
*Restart for changes to take effect.
