I'm getting a weird problem in Rhythmbox in Ubuntu Hardy. If I have a bunch of songs in the play queue, it plays through fine but when it finishes the last track it just stops on the very end and the name of the track is still showing, and the play button is still on. Like it doesn't recognise that the track has finished, until I manually turn off the play button and it goes back to saying "Not Playing". I attached a screenshot of what it looks like when the queue finishes.

This only happens if I'm using the cross-fading backend (which I enabled to remove gaps between songs). But I've used Rhythmbox w/ cross-fade backend on previous installations of Hardy and didn't have the problem. It's not really a huge deal, just a bit annoying. It also means that the last track doesn't show up on the Recently Played list because it technically didn't finish playing. Which is kind of dumb.
