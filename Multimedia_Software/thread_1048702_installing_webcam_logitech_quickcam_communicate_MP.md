### Post by richarduie on 2009-12-04
A little over a year after this question was posted, I found this thread after Google-ing for related topics. In hopes that anyone else who gets here by that same path will find this reply to offer something useful, instead of simply an unanswered question, here goes...

Cheese came as part of the base installation for Intrepid 8.10. My Communicate MP works with it out of the box. I'm a KDE (Kubuntu) guy - so, Cheese, if installed, is listed under the graphics submenu. 

I note that in Synaptic, when I search on "webcam," I find that, in addition to Cheese, the qc-usb-utils package is also installed; that may be a dependency for Cheese - don't know for sure, since, as I mentioned, these packages were part of the default installation.

Skype also properly recognizes and uses the cam.
