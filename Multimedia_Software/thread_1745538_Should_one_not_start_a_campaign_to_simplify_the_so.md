Well basically I think that was the idea behind pulseaudio. And when pulse works properly (especially if you only use one sound card) it really is a very simple way to get sound working for the various programs on your box. The problem is that pulse quite often does not work properly, whether because of its own problems or those of the various other sound systems (Alsa, OSS, etc) it's trying to work with.
