### Post by cascade9 on 2011-01-05
Scratches only affect the surface layer, but can ruin CDs/DVDs. The scratches make it harder for the laser to read the disc, and can also cause other probnlems, like oxidisation. 

Thats not the only way that CDs/DVDs can lose data/become unreadable. The dye that is written onto can degrade as well. 

A different DVD drive might read the discs, its worked for me in the past.
