### Post by quixote on 2009-08-17
Well, the obvious choice would be [Blender]("http://www.blender.org/").  There's a steepish learning curve because they have a user interface unlike any other.  Once you know your way around, it's a phenomenal program.  It can do all the things you mentioned, and anything else all the way up to full animation movies.
