### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-06-11
It depends on what you mean by "(good!) music player" as it means different things to different people.

Of the media players that I know neither banshee nor rhythmbox will play m3u playlists.  However gnome-mplayer, vlc and audacious will all play m3u playlists and will all show taskbar icons which allow control and information to be shown.

I like audacious with the gtk interface, which I think is the default view in Lubuntu, but then I'm just a simple fellow who likes to use simple apps that do not get in the way of the music.
