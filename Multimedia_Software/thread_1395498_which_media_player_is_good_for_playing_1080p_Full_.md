Rgds,

I prefer MythTV myself... It has the largest community and was designed to be a Linux solution from the start with the HTPC. XBMC was written to specifically for the X-Box to be a multimedia outlet, then adapted to other platforms. Don't get me wrong XBMC is a great piece of software but not my first pick.

MythTV has direct support of VDAPU built in as of 0.22's official release as well. You shouldn't have to worry about switching to another program, having issues with playback, or the like.
