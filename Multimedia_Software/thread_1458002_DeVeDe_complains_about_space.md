### Post by Oldhacker on 2010-04-19
In the past, I have made a number of ISO's for burning to DVD using DeVeDe without problem. Lately, no matter what size file I insert (AVI, Xvid or other) DeVeDe complains that there is not enough space to create the ISO.

According to Disk Usage Analyzer, I have only used 22.9% of my available disk space and have 184.9 GB available!

Can anyone shed any light upon what has happened and what steps I may take to be able to make DVD's again?

Thanks

---

### Post by Oldhacker on 2010-04-23
In the interim, I did some further digging myself.
The free space reported was of my total hard disk, but DeVeDe was only concerned with the partition upon which it was mounted.
A considerable amount of extra space was needed for swap files while the application was running. Therefore, I went through that partition and deleted every large file (including a couple of ISO's that I had forgotten were there) which freed up enough space to get DeVeDe operating normally.

I hope that this may be of help to others who experience similar problems.
