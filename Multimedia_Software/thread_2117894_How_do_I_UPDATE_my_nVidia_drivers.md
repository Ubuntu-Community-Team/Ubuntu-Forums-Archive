### Post by FlameFireXxX on 2013-03-11
I believe what the OP is referring to (and I could be wrong here) is the PlayOnLinux installation of League of Legends.  It's fairly simple to get up and running, and works quite well on most hardware.

As for the Shader Model 2.0 error, I've seen it myself.  I run a GeForce GTX 680 under Mint 14 and have the same issue.

OP, updating nvidia drivers has not helped me, but checking is fairly simple.  Open Software Sources, go to the Additional Drivers tab, and there you can select different nvidia drivers to try.  The "nvidia-current" is the current stable version, and the "nvidia-experimental-310" is the latest & greatest, but possibly with bugs.  Now, for League, I've tried both of these myself and have had no luck.  If you (or anyone in the community) has any luck, I'd be grateful to hear any details on a fix.
