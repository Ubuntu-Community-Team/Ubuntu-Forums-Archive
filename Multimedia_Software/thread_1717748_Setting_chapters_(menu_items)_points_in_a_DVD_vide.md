### Post by SeijiSensei on 2011-03-30
DeVeDe will set chapter marks every N minutes.  See the "Advanced Options" in the "File Properties" dialog.

Unfortunately that approach doesn't let you link the chapter marks to the content of the video.  Perhaps something like mencoder or mkvtoolnix can let you create the marks manually.
