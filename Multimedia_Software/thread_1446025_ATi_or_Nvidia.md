### Post by xzero1 on 2010-04-03
Personally, for the first time I will consider not using proprietary drivers on my ati (hd4770) card. Video, even hd, was nice, with no noticeable tearing and reasonable cpu usage, though gstreamer had a few audio sync issues. Nexuiz played well enough. Compiz was functional. I even suspect due to KMS that there may be fewer crashes. This is all "out of the box" -- just add codecs. There are issues, noticeably a gamma bug, but that this was the state of my early beta testing. They may never be as optimized as the proprietary drivers but they are an option, at least for me.
