### Post by Lime84 on 2008-06-09
I'm looking for a GTK player that can recognize an album with songs from multiple artists. Every Linux player except Amarok puts each song under a separate artist. So, if you have let's say 10 compilation albums, each with 10 songs you'll end up with 100 different artists in your media library. I don't have to mention, that this makes the media library function totally useless, unless you sort the music by albums which is inconvenient if you have a large collection.

It's really weird that only Amarok has this feature, 99% of movie soundtracks are compilations, so don't tell me nobody listens to those...
