### Post by Earl_Maroon on 2008-11-01
Do you have the proprietary driver installed for your card? If not you could try installing it. envyng is the simplest way to do that in my opinion, but it's not officially recommended. It's in the repositories if you want to try.
Do you have desktop effects on? If you do, try deactivating them to see if that makes a difference.
