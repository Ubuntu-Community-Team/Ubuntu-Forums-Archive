### Post by laidback on 2009-10-31
In case anyone is interested I bought a Logitec 3000 for Business webcam from Ebuyer (UK) for use with Skype. It uses a USB connection. Just plug in and off you go, no problems at all with video, I just needed to adjust the 'Sound In' within the Skype Options to get the built in mic working.

I've also used it with Cheese and Pitivi Video Editor. Although with Pitivi it records but doesn't allow me to do anything with the clip. Cheese on the other hand does allow me to save the file as a video clip.

A friend has also bought the Logitec 3000 webcam and is having the same success. He also runs Ubuntu 9.04.
