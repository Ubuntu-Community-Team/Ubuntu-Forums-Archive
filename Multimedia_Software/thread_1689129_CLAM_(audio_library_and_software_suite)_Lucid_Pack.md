### Post by scotty64 on 2011-02-16
There are good news for those who where looking for Lucid packages to get the CLAM audio library and software suite on their machines.
Thanks to David immediately acting to my question regarding Lucid packages for CLAM we do have working Lucid packages now. You can get them here:
[http://clam-project.org/download/linux-ubuntu-lucid/svnsnapshots/](http://clam-project.org/download/linux-ubuntu-lucid/svnsnapshots/)
When I tried installing them I found one circular dependency problem between network-editor and network-editor-examples. But the packages worked fine despite the warning messages I got during installation and the problem might be fixed already.
CLAM needs Jack in realtime mode.
I have to say this is an awesome system when it comes to realtime audio processing !
