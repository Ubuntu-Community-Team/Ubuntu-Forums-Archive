### Post by arvinoids on 2009-02-07
Hi,

If you want VLC to use a preset equalizer, just go to Tools>Preferences. Under "Show Settings" select "All". Then expand Audio, click Filters, tick on the 10 band equalizer. then under Filters, go to Equalizer to choose your preset. then click on save. You can also use the parametric equalizer if you prefer to have your own equalization.

hope this helps.
