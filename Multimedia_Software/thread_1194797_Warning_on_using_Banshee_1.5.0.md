### Post by grahams on 2009-06-23
I've been happily using Banshee for a long time. However after trying 1.5.0 I'm no longer a happy camper. 

As Banshee, will be the default in 9.10, I'm hoping this gets some attention.

My issue is that Banshee 1.5.0 now decides to rename your files and directories without warning. If like me you've stored music by genre or some other scheme that is logical to you this is a major pain. 

I'm now restoring 50GB of music, as my music is on shared media and accessed by several devices (pch, squeezebox & Linux PC), and the name Banshee thinks are good completely breaks everything else (Banshee 1.5.0 = iTunes?).

I've logged this as a bug with the Banshee devs and I hope they agree and don't see this as a "feature".

For now I reinstall 1.4.3, reloaded my music and crossed my fingers.
