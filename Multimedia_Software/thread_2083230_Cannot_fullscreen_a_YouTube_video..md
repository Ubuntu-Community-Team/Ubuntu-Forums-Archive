### Post by deadflowr on 2012-11-12
Have you installed flash on your system?(outside of pepperflash)
If so, in chrome in the address bar type: chrome://plugins, then click the details thingy on the right to expand it, then go down the list of plugins and find Adobe Flash Player and click disable on the pepperflash version(11.5), leaving the older system-wide version(11.2).
See if this helps. If it does, go to the chrome settings tab and go to About Google Chrome, then report an issue and start complaining.
