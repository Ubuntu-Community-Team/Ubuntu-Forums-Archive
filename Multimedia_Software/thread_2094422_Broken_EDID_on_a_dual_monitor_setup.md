### Post by Joshjet on 2012-12-14
Alright, an update for whomever might come across this in the future. I found the problem, and I feel stupid for not noticing it. Instead of putting the "Option      "CustomEDID" "DFP-2:/etc/X11/AOC_EDID.raw"" under the device section, I had it under the monitor section for some reason.
