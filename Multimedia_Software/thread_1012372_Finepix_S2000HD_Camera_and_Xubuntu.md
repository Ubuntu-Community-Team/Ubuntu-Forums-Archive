### Post by linux4life88 on 2009-01-10
I own the same camera but I use Ubuntu. Nevertheless, how I get my photos from the camera to the computer is that I simply take the SD/SDHC card out of the camera and hook it directly into my laptop. Although, if you don't have an SD/SDHC slot, I don't see why the folder would just not show up. When I plug the USB cable into my laptop a window pops up and I choose to open the camera files in Nautilus.
