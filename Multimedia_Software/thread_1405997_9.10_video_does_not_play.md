### Post by lucasart on 2010-02-13
Hello

Out of the box Ubuntu 9.10 has a very simple and good program: Applications -> Sound & Video -> Movie Player

Just open your file in there and if it doesn't have the codec, it will propose to get it from internet and download it. Once this is done, close it and reopen it (otherwise it doesn't work i found). I managed to read mose popular formats, which are *.avi and *.m4v

Hope it works for you too :)
