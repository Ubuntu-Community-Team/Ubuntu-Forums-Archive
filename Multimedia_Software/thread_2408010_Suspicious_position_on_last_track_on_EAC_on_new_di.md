### Post by shantiq on 2018-12-12
This is an addendum to earlier thread [on EAC under Wine]("https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2092214&highlight=Eac")
and is a common occurrence EVEN ON NEW DISCS puzzling folks all over the planet :)
Simply check google to see all the articles many of them giving up in effect


The answer is so simple one could cry after having tried 3 rips of same NEW disc


It is to untick [I][B]Overread into lead-in/out
[/B]F10  to open: Drive options   Offset/Speed  >> Untick *Overread into lead-in/out*[/I]

THAT IS ALL

Yep . That simple
