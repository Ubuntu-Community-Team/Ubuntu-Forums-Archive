### Post by iokhahon on 2010-08-09
In case someone else is struggling to get HDMI sound on their TV.
My problem was with a Nvida based Zotac ITX HTPC set up, I was unable to get sound by using only an HDMI cable. I am running Lucid "vanilla out of the box".
The solution for me was to install the gnome-aslamixer which allows you experiment by enabling (via a checkbox) the various types of PCM until you find one that works with your TV.
