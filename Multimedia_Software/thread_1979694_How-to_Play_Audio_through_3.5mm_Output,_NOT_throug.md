### Post by Entropee on 2012-05-13
I just upgraded the video card on my 12.04 desktop to an nVidia G210 so I can properly run the Unity GUI.

I have it connected to my monitor via HDMI, however, the sound is also going to my monitor. For most people, this is exactly what they are trying to achieve.

I want to do the opposite. I would like to continue running audio out through the 3.5mm jack coming off of my motherboard, but "Sound" settings is not giving me another sound hardware option to choose from.

What do I do?

---

### Post by Entropee on 2012-05-13
Figured it out! I had to enable the Intel Integrated Audio in BIOS. Found it while I was enabling Intel VT-x for VirtualBox.
