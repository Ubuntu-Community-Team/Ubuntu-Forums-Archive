### Post by wolfen69 on 2011-12-14
In the case of wruv.org, just under the play button is something that says "no flash!!, no prob!!". I just right clicked the 256k option and selected "copy link location". I then opened VLC player and went to "Open Network Stream", and pasted in the link I copied. Worked fine.

Sometimes a link will be javascript, in that case, there's no way I know of to listen to it. Perhaps someone can enlighten me. But any streaming link that ends in .m3u can be listened to via vlc or other players.
