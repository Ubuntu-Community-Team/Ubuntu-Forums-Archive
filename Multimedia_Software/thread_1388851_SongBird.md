how can i reset the "media library" of Songbird? !  :S

### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-01-23
You should be able to press Ctrl+A to select your whole library and click remove. Then a window should pop up and just unselect "Also delete these files from my hard drive". After you unselect that just click "Remove". That will reset your library and your music files will still be on your hard drive.
