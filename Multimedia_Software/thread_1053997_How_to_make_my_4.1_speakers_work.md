### Post by overlord.gaurav on 2009-01-29
I'm not really sure if this will solve your problem or not...but it had solved mine.
Initially I had the green plug inserted in the green jack & the blue plug in the blue jack.
And once..while trying to get all the speakers to work..I removed the the blue plug from the blue jack..and miraculously I got all my speakers working.

So, according to me, just make sure that you've inserted only the green plug [in the green jack, off course!]. Hopefully, it'll do the trick!
