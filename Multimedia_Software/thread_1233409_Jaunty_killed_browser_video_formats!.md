### Post by grikdog on 2009-08-06
I installed Jaunty from .iso, instead of upgrading, on my Dell Inspiron 1525 notebook.  Previously, I had Hardy running, and just about any video stream on Firefox worked fine, including YouTube, Vimeo and MSNBC.  After Jaunty, however, only YouTube works -- but that is jerky and scratchy.  Non-browser apps like VLC seem to be ok.

I tried reverting the Intel driver according to [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ReinhardTartler/X/RevertingIntelDriverTo2.4](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ReinhardTartler/X/RevertingIntelDriverTo2.4) but that has not had much effect, unless YouTube is a bit better (hard to tell, it's YouTube).

Any ideas how to improve this situation?  Hardy was fine.  Intrepid was fine, I think, although I didn't run that for very long before jumping to Jaunty.

TIA,
d

UPDATE:  Solved.  Install ubuntu-restricted-extras, maybe.  If that's not it, track down as many DVD ripping and playing packages as you can find and eventually the problem goes away.
