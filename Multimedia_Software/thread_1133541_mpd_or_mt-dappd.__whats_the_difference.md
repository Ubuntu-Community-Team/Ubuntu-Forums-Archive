[Music Player Daemon (MPD)]("http://mpd.wikia.com/wiki/Music_Player_Daemon_Wiki") is a flexible, powerful, server-side application for playing music.

The [Firefly Media Server project]("http://www.fireflymediaserver.org/"), previously known as "mt-daapd," was made to serve digital music to the Roku Soundbridge and iTunes; to be able to serve the widest variety of digital music content over the widest range of devices.

MPD plays the music on the computer on which it's installed.
Firefly Media Server project, previously known as "mt-daapd" plays the music on a computer separate from where the music is stored.
