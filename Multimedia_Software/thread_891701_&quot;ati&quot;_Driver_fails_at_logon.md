### Post by podfish on 2009-03-15
thanks to another user, I got mine running in Intrepid--so don't give up on Ubuntu just yet!

I used your xorg.conf, with an additional line in the "Device" section that reads:
```
Option   "AGPMode"  "1"
```
For some reason, it appears that xorg is trying to autoconfig the ati driver to set a higher AGP mode (2, 4, or 8) that our laptops can't support.

Give her a shot.  Good luck!
