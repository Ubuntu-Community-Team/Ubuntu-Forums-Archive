### Post by HappyFeet on 2009-12-20
The [Hauppauge WinTV PVR150]("http://www.hauppauge.com/site/products/data_pvr150.html") has been around a long time, and is supported natively in ubuntu. You just need to install ivtv-utils. I use VLC to watch TV with it. You might be able to pick one up used for about 30-40 dollars.

If you think you might get this card, PM me and I'll send you my mini-tutorial for setting it up. It's really easy.
