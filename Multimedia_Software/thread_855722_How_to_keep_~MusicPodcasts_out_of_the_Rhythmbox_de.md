### Post by jessebean on 2008-07-10
It's annoying sometimes when I'm listening to music and then a podcast starts.  (I use shuffle quite a lot.)  Is there a way to set rhythmbox to exclude the Podcast folder from the default, auto-include path? 

There is a selector for podcasts.  How do I keep them out of the music?

---

### Post by sweetlandj on 2011-01-27
Edit -> Preferences -> Podcasts

Change the podcast directory to ~/Podcasts (or similar).  Then go into ~/Music and move all of your podcast directories to ~/Podcasts.  This will prevent the podcasts from appearing in the Music folder.
