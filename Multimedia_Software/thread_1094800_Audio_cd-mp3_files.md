### Post by mcduck on 2009-03-13
Are you talking about Audio-CD's or MP3 CDs? They are two completely different things..

There are no MP3 files on Audio CD, and usually they cannot be mounted either, only used with music players. If you want to copy music from Audio CD to MP3 player you need to use some program that rips the music from the disk and then converts it into MP3 files (Soundjuicer or Grip, for example).

"MP3 CD", on the other hand, is just a normal data CD with some MP3 files in it.
