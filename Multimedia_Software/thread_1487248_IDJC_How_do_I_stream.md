### Post by StephenF on 2010-05-20
The next step is to have a server to stream to. You can configure one on your own machine if need be (listeners use up your bandwidth) or rent one from a datacenter (costly). Or you could sign up to one of the many internet stations and use theirs in your allotted time slot·

Supported servers are Shoutcast and Icecast.
