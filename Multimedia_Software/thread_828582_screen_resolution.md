### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-06-15
Have a look at this thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=829020](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=829020)

Which tells you to open up a terminal, and enter this in:
```
gksu displayconfig-gtk
```

That will bring up a nice GUI where you can configure your video resolution settings.
Once finished, just log out, then log back in again.
