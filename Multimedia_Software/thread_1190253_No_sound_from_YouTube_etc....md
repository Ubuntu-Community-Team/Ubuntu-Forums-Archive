### Post by Sxeptomaniac on 2009-06-18
Have you checked your mixer?  Depending on the configuration, there's sometimes a slider that affects this, and may be all the way down by default.  

The next thing to check is your multimedia settings in your System Settings panel.  Certain sounds can be set to default to different outputs.  See if the order of output devices is the same for all types of sound.
