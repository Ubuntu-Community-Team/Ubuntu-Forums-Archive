### Post by the_real_fourthdimension on 2009-10-27
If you double-click or right click on the sound icon in the top right of the task bar, you should get a sound options panel aka mixer.  Have you gone through those options yet?  Some of those options have fixed similar problems.  Also, you can try different audio drivers in the sound settings (try pulse, alsa, oss, etc) and see if any of these fix your problem.  They've also fixed this problem for me in the past.
