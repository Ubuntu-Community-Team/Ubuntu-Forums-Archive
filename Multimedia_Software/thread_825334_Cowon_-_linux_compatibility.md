### Post by doughnuts64 on 2008-06-11
Your computer *should* see it as an external HDD. I have the Cowon iAudio A3, and that is how it works. It simply allows me to drag-and-drop files onto the device.

---

### Post by VanCardboardbox on 2008-06-11
The reason I chose a Cowon player is that they work as UMS devices and do not require 3rd party software. Hardy and Mepis both see my Cowon iAudio X5 player as an external HDD. I'm sure the A2 is the same.
