### Post by n4mgr on 2009-06-13
I am writing this after having just picked up a Sony E-series 4Gb model NWZ-E436F at my local Wal-Mart. I plugged it in and Ubuntu immediately recognized it and mounted it as an external drive. It works drags and drops perfectly from the hdd to the E-series. I am impressed with the out-of-the-box performance of this little gem. I don't think I could give it a better review. I would most definitely recommend this product to anyone looking for an mp3 player to use in conjunction with Ubuntu.
