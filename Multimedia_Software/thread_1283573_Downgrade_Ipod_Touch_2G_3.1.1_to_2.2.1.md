### Post by mastermindg on 2009-10-05
I recently decided that I'm tired of settling for iTunes and the 20th century method of listening to music :) I had upgraded my ipod Touch to 3.0 and so on and realized that I needed it back down to 2.2.1. I had an old backup of 2.2.1 but a restore from iTunes 9.0 wouldn't work. That's when I went on a magical journey for 4 hours which ended with a successful downgrade.

NOTE: It probably wouldn't be a bad idea to take a backup of your current configuration.

STEP 1: Uninstall iTunes 9.0 (or any version 8.2 and above)
STEP 2: Install iTunes 8.1 (or any version below)
STEP 3: Open iTunes 8.1 (it won't recognize the iPod, don't worry)
STEP 4: DFU while connected (this is where I got stuck...)
    Hold down the Home/Power Button for 10 seconds
    Let go of the Power Button but NOT the Home Button
    Release the Home Button after 20 seconds
Your screen should be black/white. If not, try it again until it is!
STEP 5: Click Restore and follow the prompts. It'll update to 2.2.1 by default.
