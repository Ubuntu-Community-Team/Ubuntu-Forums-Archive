### Post by coffeecat on 2010-04-20
> **prabath_fun said:**
> It wont gives 5.1 surround / ac3 output. I am getting only stereo output. I tried by clicking option "Create DVD with 5.1 channel sound" in DeVeDe. But, No use.

It will give you 5.1 surround sound if you click the option 'This file already has AC3 sound (copy audio data instead of recompress it)'. Of course, your AVI has to have an AC3 5.1 audio track for this to work. Are you sure your AVIs have 5.1 surround sound? If they only have stereo then you're not going to get surround.
