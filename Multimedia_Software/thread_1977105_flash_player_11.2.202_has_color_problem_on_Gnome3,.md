### Post by hobby boy on 2012-05-10
I've had this problem before, I read it was something to do with Nvidia graphics drivers and the Latest flash player. To fix it, you just right click on the youtube video, click settings, and on the first tab, unclick "enable hardware acceleration". Then just restart the browser and the blue tint will be gone.
