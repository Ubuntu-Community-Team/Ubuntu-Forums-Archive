### Post by Bucky Ball on 2013-08-25
Well, you need to have made contact and paired the devices before moving any further. Have you paired the bluetooth devices using something like Blueman? They won't show up until they are present on the system in the first place. Rhythmbox, or any other software, is not going to magically make the bluetooth connection (to my knowledge). It will only detect devices that are already present on the system.

Look for Bluman, pair the devices and make sure they are present on the system and try again. Good luck.
