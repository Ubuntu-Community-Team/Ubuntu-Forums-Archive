### Post by rdumas on 2009-04-08
I have had this problem for a while.  Well I found the cure.
If you go to system - preferences - main menu A window will open on the left go down to Sound & Video when you click on it you will see on the right side an icon marked volume control put a check in the box and then close.
Go to applications - Sound & Video and you will see the icon Volume Control click on that and then turn up all the volumes and you will be able to hear like never before.
Hope this helps.):P
