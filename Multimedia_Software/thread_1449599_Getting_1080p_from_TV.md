### Post by aeiah on 2010-04-08
> **cascade9 said:**

No, its not actually. 720p = 1280x720.

whilst this is true, almost all 720p HDTVs are 1366x768. turns out its cheaper to make them like this and put in a 1280x720 -> 1366x768 upscaling chip than it is to make them 1280x720. a lot of 720p HDTVs wont even let you directly use 1366x768.

anyway. OP: try not to be mislead by 1080i and 1080p. 1080i can be thought of as equivalent to only 720p.

if you choose to stay with your 720p tv, whilst it may not be ideal for surfing and whatnot, 720p movies will still look great because at 32" you aren't really benefiting from the difference between 720p and 1080p anyway.
