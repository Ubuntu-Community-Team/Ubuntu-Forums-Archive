### Post by Jim_in_Germany on 2008-10-15
Alright Chimp,

Cheers for the suggestion. I gave banshee a try but it didn't work I'm afraid.

I did solve the problem (kind of) and will post this for anyone who might have the same problem as me. 
After a lot of googling I found a suggestion for "Rhythm Box" (installed by default on Ubuntu) which works if you have a look at the source of the D&B Arena page, find out the proper url to subscribe to the podcast, and type that into Rhythm Box.
You will have to download various codecs to cope with all of the formats, and the playback is a bit buggy, but it works nonetheless. 

Yay!
