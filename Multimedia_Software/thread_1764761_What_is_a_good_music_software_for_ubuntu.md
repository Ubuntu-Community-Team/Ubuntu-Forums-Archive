### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2011-05-22
For basic recording and edits of a single instrument Audacity will be fine. 

For multitrack stuff Jokosher would be a good start (if you get gstreamer errors switch all audio settings to default). For something with more features, look at jack plus Ardour or Qtractor. Hydrogen is good for making drum tracks and other sequencing.

You should also mention he will need a preamp if he wants to plug the guitar into the computer soundcard.
