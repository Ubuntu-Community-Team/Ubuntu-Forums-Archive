### Post by DirtDawg on 2008-08-06
I don't think you need restricted drivers as Intel has released open source drivers for their products (I *think* they are called "xserver-xorg-video-intel" in the repositories). The open source Intel drivers are very good for desktop applications such as word processing, but not so good at games or other 3D intensive applications. 

However, I have not tried to install an Intel video card before, so I don't know how easy or difficult it might be.
