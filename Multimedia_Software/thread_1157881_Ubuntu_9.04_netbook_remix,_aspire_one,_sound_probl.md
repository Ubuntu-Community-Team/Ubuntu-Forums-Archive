Hi i have an aspire one which i installed Ubuntu 9.04 netbook remix on. Everything has been working just fine for a month or so now until suddenly my sound has started doing nothing but making crackling sound. I was listening to music off my external drive, plugged in a USB flash drive to transfer some data and it wasn't picking the drive up so i restarted, but on restarting it kinda froze up so i held the button down to turn it off. ### Post by kerileigh on 2009-05-13
Fixed this problem by opening terminal and typing 

gnome-volume-control

and found that my PCM volume was turned right down, turned this up to 100% and the sound seems to work again now.

Maybe my pcm volume got turned down when i had to force a shutdown.
