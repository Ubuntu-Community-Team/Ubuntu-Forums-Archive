### Post by Floola on 2009-05-12
Hi,


I'm looking for beta testers willing to stress the upcoming new release of Floola, version 5.1. Floola is a full featured and cross plattform iPod manager. It supports all iPods models (except iPhones and iPod touches) and all features. You can manage music, videos, audiobooks, podcasts, artwork, photos and lyrics. And there's even more like duplicates detection, transcoding of incompatible formats (with ffmpeg), Google Calendar and Last.fm integration.

This beta version is focused mainly on improving performances and stability; if you've used Floola before you'll notice a dramatic speedup, but since large parts of code were changed everything has to be verified to be working yet again. 

As stated in [_docs_ ](http://www.floola.com/modules/wiwimod/index.php?page=docs_install), you'll need a 100% compliant iTunes iPod library, otherwise unexcepted behaviour may occurr since other apps may write db in a slightly different (unsupported) way. It may be a limitation but it is required just once as long as you keep using Floola (and of course iTunes). All you'll need to do is run iTunes once with your iPod connected, after that you're iPod is ready to be used with Floola under linux, mac or windows.

Download urls and bug posting can be found [_here_](http://www.floola.com/modules/newbb/viewtopic.php?topic_id=1067&forum=2).


Enjoy,
