### Post by adzik on 2016-04-05
You'll need to clarify a little more what you are looking to do, with specifics. MIDI signals only provide information on an action (ex. Eb at x time, for x long, at x velocity, using x sound mapping, x channel etc.). Inputting actual "sound" is not possible with MIDI.

Hardware or software synth. Doesn't matter. The main thing is they understand MIDI messages, and pretty much all hardware and software synths nowadays do. 

Literally thousands of MIDI files are available online. All you need to do is [take five seconds to look for them]("https://duckduckgo.com/?q=midi+files&t=h").

Download one, load it in to the software/hardware synth and hit play.
