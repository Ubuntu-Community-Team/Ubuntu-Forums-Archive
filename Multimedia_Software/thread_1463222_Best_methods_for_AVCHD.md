### Post by WSparrow23 on 2010-05-10
I use TsMuxer for much of it.  It's not in any repos that I know of, but just Google it.  I don't know if it handles dv but it's great for M2TS, TS, MKV, and some other HD capable containers.  A majority of HD cams have the option of recording as TS or M2TS.  It outputs to AVCHD or Blu-Ray and lets you do splitting, remuxing, manipulate subtitles, etc.  Once it's in AVCHD format, I use ImgBurn  (most reliable app out there for building UDF 2.5) in Wine to make a UDF 2.5 iso and burn it with whatever (I use Brasero myself). 

 What type of editing are you trying to do? Cut and Paste, Filtering, etc...
