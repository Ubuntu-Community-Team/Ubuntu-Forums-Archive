### Post by cascade9 on 2009-11-16
As far as I know, you can't convert from file-> file with grip (I could very well be wrong) 

Soundkonverter works very well, though. It will let you rename the files from metadata, etc. 

BTW, lossy -> lossy transcodes are not a good idea. All lossy codecs discard information, and the information they discard varies. So even if the source file (file1) has a nice fat bitrate, and you convert to a smaller bitrate (file2), it will not sound as good as CD-> file2.
