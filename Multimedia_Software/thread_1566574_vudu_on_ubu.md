### Post by chitowner2 on 2010-09-02
Guess I found an answer:

vudu is an app embedded on a flash or prom chip. It is not available as an application for computer installation. Therefore one is forced to purchase retail hardware containing the chip. 

At present these include certain flat-panel TV's (eg, tuner included, not just a monitor) or external BluRay player. No plans for other devices. No plans for a software app.

Seems to me they're going for the richer and/or dumber end of the market.
