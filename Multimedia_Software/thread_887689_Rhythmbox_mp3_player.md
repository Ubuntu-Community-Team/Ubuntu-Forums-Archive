By saying your system sees the generic device, do you mean that your MP3 player is recognised as a USB drive and you are able to browse it? If this is the case, then to make *Rhythmbox* to recognise your MP3 player you'll have to do the following:

Open the text editor, create an empty text file and save it as *.is_audio_player* (with the dot in front of the filename) in the root folder of your MP3 player.
