### Post by Darkmatter5 on 2009-10-15
Thanks to AgentY's post at the following link, [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1287702]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1287702"), I fix my video problems and so far it boots all the way into the desktop.  I am running a Jaunty install on a Toshiba Tecra A8-EZ8311 with an ATI Radeon Xpress 200M.  The thread listed above totally did the trick, just boot into recovery mode by pressing Esc upon boot up and select Recovery Mode.  Then select the one that says something like terminal with networking.  Then when you get to the console type in the lines from the thread each at a time and with a little bit of luck you'll also be up and running on Jaunty.
