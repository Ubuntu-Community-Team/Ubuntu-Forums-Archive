### Post by curlybap on 2009-01-23
Hi,

In Firefox, click

**Edit -> Preferences**

then select the **Applications** tab, and type "**torrent**" (without quotes).

Click the drop-down, and click **Other...**, then browse to

**/usr/bin/**

and select and open **deluge**.

Hopefully this'll work!
