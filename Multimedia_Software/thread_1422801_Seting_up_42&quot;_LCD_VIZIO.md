### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-03-06
> **sandman3838 said:**
> I get a blue screen from the LCD?  

Suggestions?
Do you mean the colors are wrong as in the hue needs adjusting or do you mean the screen is just bright blue?

If it's the first search the forums as I have seen this discussed before. 

If it's the latter then your connection is not detected. Try the PIP (Picture In Picture) and Swap them back and forth. PIP and Swap are both buttons at the bottom of the remote control for the TV. Happens to me sometimes because I use both the TV and the PC and swap them. Sometimes the PC will just show a blue screen when I first start it up but swapping it fixes it right up.
