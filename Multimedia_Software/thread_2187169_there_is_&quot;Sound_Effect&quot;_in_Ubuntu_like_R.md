### Post by ajgreeny on 2013-11-11
So do you want an equaliser for the sound system, not for separate applications?

Many sound applications and music players have their own equalisers, eg, VLC, audacious, banshee, and probably many others I don't use, which can sometimes use presets for different ambience settings.

Depending on your version of ubuntu it looks as if the ppa at [https://launchpad.net/~nilarimogard/+archive/webupd8/+index?batch=75&memo=300&start=300](https://launchpad.net/~nilarimogard/+archive/webupd8/+index?batch=75&memo=300&start=300) may be able to help you with a pulseaudio-equaliser.
