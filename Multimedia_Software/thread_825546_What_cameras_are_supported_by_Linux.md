### Post by yodermk on 2008-06-11
That's only a question if you have to transfer the photos directly from the camera to the computer.  If you use a flash card and have an appropriate reader hooked up to the computer, any camera will work fine.  That's what I do.

I don't have a list, but any camera that supports the USB storage protocol should work and look like any external disk mount.  I think programs like digiKam support some "regular" camera protocols, but I have no experience with those.
