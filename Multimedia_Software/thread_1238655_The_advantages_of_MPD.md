### Post by mcduck on 2009-08-13
[LIST]
[*]Extremely lightweight
[*]Handles large music libraries better than any other app I've tried
[*]Sonata has the best interface I've seen for a music player yet, and it's MPD client.
[*]Many different clients, and you can switch between them as you like without having to create new libraries etc. like you'd have to do if you moved from one music player to another
[*]same music player accessible from command-line and desktop
[*]remote-controllable through network, and even from Win/OSX machine or with web-based clients.
[*]supports gapless playback, which seems to be hard for some players..
[*]doesn't stop playing music even if you log out, switch between desktop environments, break X or something.
[/LIST]
I could probably think of many more reasons, but I haven't had my cup of morning coffee yet so I'm not completely awake yet. :)
