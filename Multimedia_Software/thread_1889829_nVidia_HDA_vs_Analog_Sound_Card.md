### Post by efflandt on 2011-12-02
If you are using the same speakers either way, there is probably no difference.  I experimented with HDMI audio in Maverick, just to get it working.  But HDMI audio goes to the speakers of the 32" 1080p HDTV I am using as a monitor, and analog stereo goes to a 2.1 PC speaker system with subwoofer.  So in my case I get richer sound from my analog speaker system than from the TV speakers.

Unless you are using a turntable or cassette player as a source, it all starts out digital and eventually ends up analog for the speakers themselves.
