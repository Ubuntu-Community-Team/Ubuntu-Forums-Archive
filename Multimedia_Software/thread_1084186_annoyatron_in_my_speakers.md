### Post by Shazaam on 2009-03-01
You are being watched! :)
Not really lol. Start with the basics...
1. Turn ALL sound volumes down and listen for a while. Beeping still happening?
2. Go to System>Preferences>Sound>Sounds and uncheck "Play alerts and sound effects". Still beeping (you may need to reboot)?
3. Physically unplug your speakers (if you can), see if it still beeps.
4. If you have a dual-boot with other os's (Windows, etc) see if they beep also.

More than likely it is either..
A. Problems with your pc (bios beeps, overheating, etc).
B. See basics above.
