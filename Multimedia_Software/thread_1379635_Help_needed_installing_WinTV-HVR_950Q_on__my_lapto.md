### Post by wkulecz on 2010-03-16
Is your 950Q working on the old Sony box with MythTV?

I've had no success with the 950Q on 9.10 other than using the Composite and S-video inputs which are too sensitive to video input level to be very useful in general.

On my Win7 box I found it to be flaky, only one of the three front panel USB ports would work with it. Perhaps a power issue since the thing gets pretty warm during use, many notebook USB ports don't meet the USB max power output spec. I found the ATSC capture quality to be very good in Win7 but it needs a much better antenna than what came with it.
