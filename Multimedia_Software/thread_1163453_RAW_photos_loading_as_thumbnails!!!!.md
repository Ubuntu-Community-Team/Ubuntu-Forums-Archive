### Post by heffo_j on 2009-05-19
Hi there,

My (Canon) RAW images will show as thumbnails in a few image-viewers out there. Gthumb will handle them these days but my favourite is Geeqie (available through Synaptic). 

As the previous poster said, UFRaw is good for working with GIMP. RawTherapee and Raw Studio also work as good stand-alone editors. 

A rapidly improving application is Fotoxx (latest version is now on GedDeb), it has a number of useful tools including a (fake) HDR option. It handles RAW these days too.

Best of luck
John
