### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-05-15
Install pulseaudio if don't have it already, then go to "System Settings >> Computer Administration >> Multimedia" and move pulseaudio to the top of all lists. It should stop complaining about HDA not working.

What bugs do you have in Firefox? It works perfectly for me. [Firefox optimization and troubleshooting thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1193567).

Aside from a couple of Gnome related issues, my KDE installation is extremely stable and reliable. No complains whatsoever. so perhaps you have a hardware issue or your settings are not good. Have you upgraded or did you do a clean install? Have you tried to create a new user to see if the problems persist?
