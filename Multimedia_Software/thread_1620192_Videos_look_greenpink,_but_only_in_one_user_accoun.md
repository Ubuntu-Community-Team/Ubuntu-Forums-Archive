### Post by crazycatlady0 on 2010-11-13
I solved this problem.  My HUE setting got changed some how.


Solution:
Open Movie Player.
Click Edit > Preferences
Click the Display tab.
Under the "Color Balance" section, click "Reset to Defaults"
