### Post by sbernath on 2010-05-24
Okay, I know that there have been a million posts on this, and I've read almost all of them and followed their various suggestions with no success.
My problem is, when I plug in my iPod touch 3g I *do* get an icon for it on the desktop.  However, when I open Rhythmbox, or Amarok, or Banshee or anything else, there is no icon for the iPod touch.  I have tried the 'scan removable media' within Rhythmbox and it asks me to initialize a new iPod.  Initializing results in the iPod making an audible bleep as if it were charging but does not result in its icon appearing in Rhythmbox.  Also, I have been able to upload snapshots from the iPod onto my computer but what I need is audio syncing with the iPod.  Any help would be greatly appreciated, though I know the community is probably exhausted of hearing this issue.

### Post by sbernath on 2010-05-27
To anybody who cares, I have figured it out.  Although I did an initial initialization on a Macintosh computer I did not name my iPod.  Once I went back to a Mac computer and named my iPod, Rhythmbox now recognizes my iPod.  weird...
