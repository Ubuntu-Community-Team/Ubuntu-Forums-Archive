### Post by marin123 on 2011-08-08
you cannot do that.
you could try to quiet down the original speech with eq or filter, but then you would change the music and the rest of the background.
you can try pitivi or open shot. that are two video editors you can use.

this is what i would do:
1. separate video and audio (convert it to whatever with soundconverter)
2. put some effect on the audio (eq or filter) with audacity
3. put everything in video editor, audio as track 1 and video as track 2, quiet down the video. thats how you separate video from audio.
4. record your own speech (audacity or sound recorder) and put is as track 3 in editor

if the video sounds are muted, you will get mix of your voice with original recording.
if you didnt understand everything, just say what and i will try to make sense :)
