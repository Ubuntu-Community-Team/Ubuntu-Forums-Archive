### Post by cascade9 on 2010-04-16
MP4 isnt a video format- its a container, and it can have different video formats in it (and audio for that matter). 

If you are trying to play a 720p/1080i/1080p HD file, it might have nothing to do with the player you are using, and more to do with your hardware not having the power to decode the video without dropping frames, etc.
