### Post by mc4man on 2010-12-10
open rhythmbox, r. click on Radio - 'new Internet radio station'

Enter the url, in this case for the .mp3 stream
```
http://listen.radionomy.com/fearfm-hardest
```

After entering you can r. click on the new entry - properties and edit the name under the general tab  - fearfm (hardest) or whatever
(occasionally rhythmbox may not open - click stop, then play till it does

Other players can be added similarly or will accept either the .pls from website or you can create a .m3u 
eX. - fearfm.m3u
```
#EXTM3U
#EXTINF:0,FearFM__Hardest
http://listen.radionomy.com/fearfm-hardest
```
