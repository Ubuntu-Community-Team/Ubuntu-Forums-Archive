### Post by little_penguin on 2009-02-20
This reply comes a bit late, I know, but for the benefit of anyone with the same question, I own an Archos 504 (40gb) and it works fine in Hardy Heron. It's recognized as a hard drive. Just plug it in, then after connecting, on the Archos click 

Menu -> Enable USB -> In PC Hard Drive Mode

then you can drag and drop your music, videos etc. to the relevant folder on the Archos via your preferred file manager.
