### Post by blm-ubunet on 2016-01-23
I would think the old BDs should still work with dumpHD as long as your drive is not revoked.
This option is not helpful for new releases..
You did find the "key" file on videolan site or more up-to-date list from doom9?
VLC, mythtv & kodi can all use this file.
But that's not enough to play the later titles.
MakeMKV (&libmmbd) works very well here.. I play the optical disc directly, no ripping or streaming.
AFAIK Those are the only native linux solutions.

There are other working wine solutions but all cost.
