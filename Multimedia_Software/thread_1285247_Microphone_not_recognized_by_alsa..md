### Post by tgeer43 on 2009-10-07
In the Volume Control go to the options tab and make sure the correct input source is selected. There's probably a MIC and a FRONT MIC. On your computer I believe the built-in is FRONT MIC and the jack is MIC. Then make sure the corresponding slider is activated, turned up, and not muted.
