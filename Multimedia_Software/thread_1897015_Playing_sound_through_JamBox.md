Check out :

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BluetoothAudio](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BluetoothAudio)

And install BlueZ from Ubuntu Software Center to easily configure the Jambox as a a2d audio sinc.

Worked form me after removing Jambox and adding it again in BlueZ Bluetooth manager.

I'm not sure if BlueZ bluetooth manager could have managed setup on its own, without calling gconftool as suggested in the BluetoothAudio page.
