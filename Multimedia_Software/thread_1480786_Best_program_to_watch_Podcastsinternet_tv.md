### Post by xzero1 on 2010-05-18
Miro probably uses gstreamer to play your video, but since the video is first downloaded, it can be played by other programs. My favorite is mplayer. As a test, try playing your video with 'movie player' which uses gstreamer. It will probably play just as badly, if so, your problem is with gstreamer not miro.
