### Post by cotcot on 2009-01-26
Your description is not enough to know if you get the same error as i and other users get. If you have the same error as me then i have a work around.
I see a thumbnail error. You get this error when you add video with the "Add / organize video" tab. You can add your video in another way. Clicking in the white field under "All" "Video" and "Audio" tabs opens your browser. Go to the folder with your clips. Double click on a clip and it is added without error.
