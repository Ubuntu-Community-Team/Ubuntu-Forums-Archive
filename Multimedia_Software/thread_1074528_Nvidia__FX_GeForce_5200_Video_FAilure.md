### Post by hannah_a on 2009-02-21
PROBLEM SOLVED WITH CRT MONITOR.

Clean install of 8.10, rebooted when finished and allowed ubuntu to choose my undates. Once they were finished, I rebooted. When the desktop came up I went to System/Administration/Hardware Drivers and allowed it to pick the necessary driver (96 & 173), I used 173. I then activated it and rebooted again. Whala, video working only with a CRT monitor. System still boot up with a black screen when I use my Kogi LCD Monitor. So If anyone find a cure for this, Leave me a fix for it.
