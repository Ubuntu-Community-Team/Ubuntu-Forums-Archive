### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-03-18
You say you have installed the codecs you need for WMAs, but it appears that you may not have everything. Try installing **ubuntu-restricted-extras** and also enable [medibuntu]("http://www.medibuntu.org/") repositories and install **w32codecs**.  That should do it.
