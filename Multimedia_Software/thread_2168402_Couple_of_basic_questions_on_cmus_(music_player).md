### Post by howefield on 2013-08-17
> **james32 said:**
> How do I play just one song on loop ? cmus loads all the songs in the folder and plays all of them one after the other.

The letter r key should toggle repeat.

> Related to this, is there a way I can make temporary playlists without needing to save it ?

Uhm.. you could make a playlist by navigating through the music and pressing the letter e key to add a song to the playlist. Edit the playlist pressing the numeric 4 key.
