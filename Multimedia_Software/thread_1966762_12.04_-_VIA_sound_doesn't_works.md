### Post by unnamed201 on 2012-04-27
Never mind.. resolved it.

Installed and compiled the ALSA driver from here: [http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Main_Page](http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Main_Page)  ([alsa-driver-1.0.25](ftp://ftp.alsa-project.org/pub/driver/alsa-driver-1.0.25.tar.bz2)) and used snd-hda-intel at modules.

Installed alsamixergui and unmuted almost everything, but the most important was Smart 5.1.

Hope I helped others that have this issue :)
