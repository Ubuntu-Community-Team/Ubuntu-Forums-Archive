### Post by ChuckyDuckster on 2010-12-14
Hi!

if you go to System -> Preferences -> Sound, then click the output tab, and select analog output from the dropdown menu, the sound will play only out of the headphones. (To enable the speakers again, change it back to Analog Speakers).

Cheers
