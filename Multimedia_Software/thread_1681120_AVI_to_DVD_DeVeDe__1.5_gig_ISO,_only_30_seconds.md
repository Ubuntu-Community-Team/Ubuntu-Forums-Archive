burned burned the ISO to a DVD with K3B.   All I got was 30 seconds of

1.  I chose VideoDVD when DeVeDe launched
2.  I added the AVI file in the window at the right
3.  I made a title in the window on the left

5.  I checked NTSC ( I'm in the U.S. )
6.  I checked "create ISO"

I already trashed two blank DVDs with having spent several hours per
each trial.

### Post by tinker123 on 2011-02-05
It turned out that the white screen with the title on it was actually a menu.

I made a new ISO with the "create menu" checkbox unchecked.

The DVD worked fine.

Americans, watch out, the first time you use DeVeDe it defaults to PAL, DVDs in the US need to be NTSC.
