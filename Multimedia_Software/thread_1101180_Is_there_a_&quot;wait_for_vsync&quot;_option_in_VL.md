### Post by diablo75 on 2009-03-20
As a benchmark test for full screen video, I'd like to be able to force video playback to do something that I see as a standard option in most video games.  Namely, wait for [vertical synchronization]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vertical_synchronization") before outputting a frame of video to the screen.  Without the vsync, you end up with video that, while it plays back okay, looks "ripped" during high-action sequences.  In the simplest terms, if you had video containing alternating frames of white and black, you wouldn't want to see a white frame halfway painted over the previous frame and vice versa.
