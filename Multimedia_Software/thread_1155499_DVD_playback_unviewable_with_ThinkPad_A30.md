### Post by TimPy on 2009-05-10
So.   Even though I struggled with this for about a week before I posted this, I figured out what was causing the error in five minutes.   

I cannot have both desktop effects enabled and have watchable DVD playback.  Playback is working fine with desktop effects completely disabled. Don't know why I didn't think of it until after I posted my problem.
