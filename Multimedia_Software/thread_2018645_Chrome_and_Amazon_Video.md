### Post by jparchem on 2012-08-29
I was having a similar problem.  What I ended up doing was just disabling the built-in Chrome flash plugin and installed the Adobe Flash plug-in with the HAL stuff.  

[LIST=1]
[*]Installed Flash plug-in so it worked under Firefox

[*]Went into Chrome --> Settings

[*]In the Search box, entered Flash

[*]Selected Content Settings and scrolled down to the privacy section

[*]Under Plug-ins clicked the link to disable individual plugins

[*]I disabled the built-in pepper plug-in and left the adobe plug-in enabled.  I was then able to watch the videos under Chrome again.
[/LIST]

For sure, this is a temporary workaround, but until they get it fixed again this allowed me to view the Amazon videos from within Chrome.
