### Post by Robbyx on 2014-02-08
Is it possible to use one sound card for skype and another for VLC and  have the choice remembered so that swtiching between the two apps causes output to go to the desired source?

---

### Post by kostkon on 2014-02-08
Yes, install the PulseAudio Volume Control (pavucontrol) utility and set a device for each app and pulse will remember your choices.
