### Post by artrbinfo on 2008-07-20
Hello!
 In your case - motherboard with AGP X4, and ddr. I'd like to suggest to save little money and upgrade to new mobo, cpu, ram, and vga. Your motherboard is with very old technology video card slot - AGP X4. There isn't many used cards out that would fit in your mobo; FX5200,FX5500, 9800 and some others, but now with so low AMD CPU prices you could get much bigger benefit, with motherboard that supports PCI-E and AM2+. Because now you want to invest to practically "extinct" platform. After year maybe you won't be able to but AGP X4 card at all. But it's just suggestion.

 Video cards what will fit in you mother board:
All GeFource FX series
ATi I wouldn't suggest for AGP platform.

All the best to you!
