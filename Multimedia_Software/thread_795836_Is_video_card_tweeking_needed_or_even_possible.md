### Post by jocko on 2008-05-16
The only way you can get continous play is if the movie downloads quicker than it is being displayed, otherwise your move player will have to stop and wait once it reaches the end of what has been downloaded.
This has nothing to do with your graphics card, more likely the connection to the server that hosts the files you're trying to watch is not fast enough.
Save the files locally and try again.

### Post by EXCiD3 on 2008-05-16
As mentioned, streaming videos must be downloaded onto your computer temporarily before they are played back in your web browser. The connection between you and the website is what causes the jumpiness you are experiencing. If you put in a DVD on your computer or watch a video saved to it, you will see no problems like this because it is not having to be downloaded from a source over the internet.
