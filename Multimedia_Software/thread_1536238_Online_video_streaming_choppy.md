Here is what I do to watch flash video flawlessly:
Go to page with video, press start and pause the video so that it is loading
Navigate to your /tmp folder and open the flv file there with movie player or whatever you like to use
Enjoy

If you have a slow connection then it is not perfect as the video file will stop whenever your download speed becomes slower than the rate of playback (ie whenever you see a buffering or loading message in the flash video).
