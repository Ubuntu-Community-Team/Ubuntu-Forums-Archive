### Post by itix on 2008-05-04
Didn't find that, but never mind because I found where you choose what program to open the DVD's with. 

Go to *system => preferences => removable drives and media => multimedia tab* and the videoDVD section and change the word "totem" to vlc. Don't remove the %m since it is important. Click the close button and VLC will open your DVDs in the future.
