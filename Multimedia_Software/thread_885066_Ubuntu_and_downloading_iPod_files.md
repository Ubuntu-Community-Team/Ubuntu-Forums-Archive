### Post by cawpin on 2008-08-09
If you are looking to have a program download the podcast episodes for you, Rhythmbox is installed by default and will handle podcasts. On the page you linked, click the "Download the Samantha Brown podcast" link. On that page you'll have two links at the top right of the main section labeled subscribe. You want to copy the link of the RSS feed and paste it into Rythmbox, or whatever program you use, as a podcast link.
