### Post by Joe of loath on 2010-12-21
Musicbrainz is a track listing database. When it asks you to add the CD to the database, it's asking if it can upload the names and lengths of the tracks, along with the ID number for that particular album (Individual to the pressing of the CD, not to each individual disc).

Nothing to worry about, and by uploading you help the community :)

### Post by HOLOGRAPHICpizza on 2010-12-21
MusicBrainz is nothing but a database of metadata for CDs that cd ripping programs can use to identify the tracks on a disc, instead of ripping them all as "Track 1", "Track 2", etc. All you are uploading is the metadata, no audio or copyrighted content. There is nothing at all to worry about, you're just helping people keep track of their music better.
