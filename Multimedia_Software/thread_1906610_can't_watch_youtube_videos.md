### Post by wolfen69 on 2012-01-09
Uninstall any instances of flash you may have. Go [here]("http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/") and download the .tar.gz flash file. Right click it and *extract here*. Then take the resulting *libflashplayer.so* file and put it in ~/.mozilla/plugins  (you may need to create the plugins folder)

Reboot just for good measure. (even though you shouldn't have to)
