### Post by tgalati4 on 2009-08-11
Put the memory card in a reader and use photorec:
sudo apt-get install testdisk
man photorec

It's possible the pictures are deleted, but the thumbnails of the pictures are still on the camera.  They are probably in a hidden directory.

If only the thumbnails are recoverable, then that won't help you in court because the resolution is bad.
