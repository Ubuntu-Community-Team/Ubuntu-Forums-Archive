### Post by Johnny Buffalkill on 2011-12-13
I would guess you are overloading your CPU.  Even with a graphics card, your CPU will still get used.  I have a PhenomII x4 940, and when I watch blu-rays, usually there is at least one core maxed out.  OTA HD broadcasts also use a fair amount of CPU.  Probably net based videos are lower quality so they don't stress it as much.  Try running system monitor while you watch a video and see if that reveals anything.

Also, what programs are you using to tune TV and watch videos, and what type of GPU and drivers do you have?
