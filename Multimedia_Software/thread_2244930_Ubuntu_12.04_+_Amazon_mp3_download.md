### Post by redrumrogue on 2014-09-20
Hi there ...
It's disappointing that Amazon is not making .amz file available anymore when you purchase music.  I used to use clamz to read the amz files on my ubuntu box and download the entire album directly to my music folder.  Was quick and easy!  I found this thread which mostly is full of people complaining but there a couple of work-arounds described there.  I've not tried any of them.  [http://www.amazon.co.uk/forum/mp3?_encoding=UTF8&cdForum=Fx28IXQIV6LBRU4&cdThread=Tx3SF3IF9K4F2ZK](http://www.amazon.co.uk/forum/mp3?_encoding=UTF8&cdForum=Fx28IXQIV6LBRU4&cdThread=Tx3SF3IF9K4F2ZK)

You can still download your music from the website by doing this:
1.  Log into Amazon
2.  Go to "Your Account" - > "Your Music Library" ..
3.  You can select one or more songs (or entire albums) and click download. The songs just get downloaded through your browser.

If it's the first time you atempt to download on your computer you will be asked to authorize the device for downloading.
