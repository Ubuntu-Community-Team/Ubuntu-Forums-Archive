### Post by vanadium on 2013-06-30
The zooming must be done at the level of your desktop manager. A standard Ubuntu installation has the "Enhanced zoom desktop"plugin enabled, yet there are no keybindings defined by default. If you define keybindings or mouse gestures, you will be able to zoom in/out.
You would end up with very poor quality if you would attempt to zoom through a video editing option, which, in addition, would probably not be easy.
