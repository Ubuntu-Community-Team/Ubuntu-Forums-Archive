### Post by Acradon on 2009-01-02
I think this is the wrong forum to post this, as this should be for UBUNTU questions. However, you plug the cable into the "line in" plug. This might be in the front of the stereo. If your stereo does not support a line in try using a 3.5mm to cinch plug (the cable that ends in a red and a white plug) and connect it to the AUX port.
