```

- Master

- Line In
- CD
- MIC
- Phone
- Pc Speaker
- Aux

```
My question is:
What are the sint, Phone and aux for?

---

### Post by kruegger on 2009-03-28
It seems a very specialiced sound card you have.

Sint. is to plug in a sinthesizer (that piano-like instrument).
Phone is to plug in an old vinyl player.
Aux. to plug in almost whatever you like.
