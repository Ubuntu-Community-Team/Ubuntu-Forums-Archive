### Post by Schrute Farms on 2010-10-26
If normalize doesn't work well enough for you, then you can go to Effect>Compressor.

In my experience it can raise the overall volume more than just normalize, especially if there are a few loud clicks/pops in the recording.  Compression can also raise the level of the quieter parts up, evening out the whole recording, so I'm not a big fan of it if it's not necessary.
