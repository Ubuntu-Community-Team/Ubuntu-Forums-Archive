### Post by minky311 on 2008-10-17
Just take a picture and rename it in this format: "artistname-albumname"(without the quotes). Then open up your home folder and check to make sure that view hidden files is checked. Next open up your .config folder -> banshee -> covers and drop in the album art. It should now show up. Or just take the picture you would like to set as the album art and once it is saved to your computer from wherever you got it drag and drop it into the album art frame in banshee. It will save it into the folder I mentioned earlier.
