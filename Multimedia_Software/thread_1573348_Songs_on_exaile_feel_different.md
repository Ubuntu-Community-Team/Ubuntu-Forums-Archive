### Post by MechaMechanism on 2010-09-12
For compressed formats there is the possibility that the 2 music programs are using different decoders and on some songs one decoder may be better than the other one and on others the decoders may sound worse.  But I think this is unlikely as I would assume that both programs are using the system libraries for decoding.
