### Post by Victormd on 2008-05-28
Have you tried Amarok?
```
sudo apt-get install amarok
```
It's very nice! I manage ~100Gb of mp3 with it... It will even let you drag and drop (holding CTRL while doing this so you copy the files and not move them) from the play list to anywhere as long as it can be mounted(i.e., removable disk, mp3 player or even your phone though I haven't tried it on the phone... :))
