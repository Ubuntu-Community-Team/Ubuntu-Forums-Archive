What I think I have to do is to setup/determine what settings actually fit on the screen and then use xrandr to actually set my screen up. ### Post by impliedconsent2 on 2014-06-12
Per [Samsung FAQ]("http://www.samsung.com/us/support/faq/FAQ00000591/55200") you have to turn off over-scanning on the TV itself. I had the same problem, turned off Just Scan - done. On old upconverted non-HD signals, you "might" get an irritating line at top (I rarely notice it now), but other than that, still a beautiful picture.
