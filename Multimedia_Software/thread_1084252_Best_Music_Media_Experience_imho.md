Hello All,

I just wanted to share my favorite music media setup.  I am using Hardy 8.04 with Gnome Desktop on an Asus M2N68-VM motherboard and have installed the following:

frostwire[http://www.frostwire.com/?id=downloads]("http://www.frostwire.com/?id=downloads")
amarok-xine and amarok (both found in the medibuntu repositories through synaptic package manager)
liprojectm1 libvisual-projectm and libprojectm1-data (also found through synaptic) This is a great visualization effects project to watch with your music library[http://projectm.sourceforge.net/]("http://projectm.sourceforge.net/")

Try it out...It's very nice>
