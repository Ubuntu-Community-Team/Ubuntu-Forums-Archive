### Post by sargetech on 2011-03-27
Hello to all my Ubuntu brothers and sisters, I have been using Ubuntu since Dapper!! and stuck with Dapper until Hardy. I finally made a clean install of Lucid, and discovered something, I was having big trouble playing some video streams on some news sites using Firefox (that do not use flash) the video streams are Windows media format. I noticed that video playback was not up to par with as is was with Hardy!!! until I installed the following through synaptic:
totem-mozilla
xine-ui
xine-plugin
totem-xine
totem
mplayer
mencoder
pulseaudio
totem-plugins-extra
These are in addition to ubuntu-restricted-extras, Through numerous installs I found out that I should check if the above extras are installed. some are some are not. After installing all of the above, video playback is handled by xine and works perfectly:D. Thanks for reading my post and I hope to help someone out there, as I have received great help here and want to contribute to the cause, Thanks folks....:KS
