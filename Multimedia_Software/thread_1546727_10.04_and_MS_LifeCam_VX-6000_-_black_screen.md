### Post by WildeBeest on 2010-11-26
Out of the box it worked with cheese and gstreamer-properties.

Skype - This script worked for me.

10.04 64 bit. For 32 bit, replace the [COLOR=Red]lib32[/COLOR] with [COLOR=Red]lib[/COLOR].
```

#!/bin/sh
export XLIB_SKIP_ARGB_VISUALS=1
LD_PRELOAD=/usr/[COLOR=Red]lib32[/COLOR]/libv4l/v4l1compat.so skype

```
