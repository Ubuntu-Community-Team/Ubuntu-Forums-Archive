Use amarok as normal. I believe you have to specify it as an iPod (Tools -> Configure Amarok -> Media Devices [tab]). It works on my iPod and I think it's 6th gen.

I wouldn't bother with rockbox, amarok does the job well for music but not really video and I shouldn't worry about updates. Nothing else I can think of, I am unsure about album artwork since I have that off anyway
