### Post by hacikho on 2009-10-28
I found the answer, for those of you who have same problem here is the solution:
To correct it I right clicked the speaker icon and selected preferences. Then left clicked on the speaker again to see the volume slider. in the preferences I clicked line out and slid the volume all the way up the line in I did the same(you probably haven't tested your mic yet but this wwill fix the volume on that also.)
