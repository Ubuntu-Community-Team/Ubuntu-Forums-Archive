### Post by drifter2502 on 2008-07-10
I hope this helps some people who are having sound problems after booting into Ubuntu. I am new to anything linux but downloaded Wubi 8.04 and ran it piggy back fashion on windows XP. I could play video and sound files stored on my hard drives but only with the Totum Movie player. Any other player would not work. Anything on the internet would not play sound at all. I was particularly 'pissed off' because I could not listen to BBC programmes. Even ubuntu log in sound would not play. I trawled all the stuff in the forum and tried all to no avail. On the point of giving up I uninstalled pulseaudio and installed esound all from the Synaptic Package Manager. Now I can use everything I need! It worked for me and I hope it works for you.
