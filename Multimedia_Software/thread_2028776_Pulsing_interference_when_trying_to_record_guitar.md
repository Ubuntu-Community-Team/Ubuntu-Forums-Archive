### Post by srt5 on 2012-07-21
Just thought I'd update this thread to let people know it's been solved. Originally, I had been using the mic socket at the front of my computer for convenience. I tried the socket to the rear of the case and did not observe the same interference pattern.

I can only suppose that the interference was caused by some other nearby component.
