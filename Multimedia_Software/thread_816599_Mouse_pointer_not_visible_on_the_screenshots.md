### Post by helino on 2008-06-03
I don't know anyway to do this, but an easy workaround is to take the screenshot, then open it with GIMP. Then click "File" -> "Open as layers" and select the mouse pointer that I've attached to this post. You can now move the mouse pointer to the place you want it in the screenshot.
