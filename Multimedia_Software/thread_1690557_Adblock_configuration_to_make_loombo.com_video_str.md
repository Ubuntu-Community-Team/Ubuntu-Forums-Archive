### Post by vacco on 2011-02-18
After going around installing different codec packs etc, I figured out that Adblock is the reason why loombo.com videos are playing with sound but no picture.
The video is working fine if I disable Adblock in Firefox, but that does come at the expense of several annoying popups and ads.

How would I configure Adblock to let the video through? Deactivating Adblock and restarting Firefox all the time is pretty troublesome.

---

### Post by SeijiSensei on 2011-02-19
Many commercial video sites won't play properly if ads (and sometimes scripts) are blocked. I don't blame them; the ads pay the bills.  In AdBlock check the box to disable the blocker on the loombo.com site.

Alternatively if it bothers you that much, you can try selectively disabling blocks from the "Open Blockable Items" page until you find the right combination.  This approach may fail the next time you visit the site, though.
