### Post by mgmiller on 2010-03-11
That choice no longer exists.  

Open your home folder, then click on Edit > Preferences and go to the Media tab.  You should see what you are looking for.  

There is also a menu shortcut to this same dialog, but they don't enable it by default, so this method always works.
