### Post by amsterdamharu on 2010-02-04
Right click the workspace switcher applet. This should be on the bottom panel on the right.
Choose preferences and you can change the amount of workspaces here.

If you don't have the workspace switcher just right click on a panel -> add to panel -> workspace switcher.

I don't know why there are 2 workspaces in Ubuntu but there should be no problem if you change it (I did and have encountered no problems).
