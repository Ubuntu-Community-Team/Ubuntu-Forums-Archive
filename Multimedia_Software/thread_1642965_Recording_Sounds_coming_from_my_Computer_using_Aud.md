### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-12-11
Click on your volume icon and go to Sound Preferences and in the Input tab choose one of the other options in the dropdown box.  One of those should give you the output of your system, rather than the Mic.  Your options may be different from mine as it depends on hardware.  You may also need to play around with the levels in alsamixer in order to get the input to audacity.
