### Post by N00b-un-2 on 2010-01-01
Banshee can do all of the above except for the Photos.  Another program called gtkpod can sync photos.  If you choose to install Gtkpod, if you have ANY music that is not mp3 on your iPod (like m4a, m4p, aac, etc) you will need to install the package 'gtkpod-aac' instead of just gtkpod.  I learned that the hard way after messing up a friends iPod and accidentally erasing about half of his music.
And no, you shouldn't have to erase your iPod to make it work on Linux.
