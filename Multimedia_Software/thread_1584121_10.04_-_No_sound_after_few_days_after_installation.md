### Post by nem75 on 2010-10-30
> **dMaggot said:**
> Hi all,
On alsamixer, the second channel, named S/PDIF, should be muted while everything ese is unmuted/full volume

That solved my problem. My kernel is 2.6.32-25-generic, btw.

Hooray and thanks for the tip, this actually works.

Summary:
1. Mute sound on an Ubuntu with SB Audigy (e.g. CA0106) and shut down the computer.
2. On next boot the sound is still muted. Even when trying to unmute it.
3. Open terminal and run [FONT="Lucida Console"]alsamixer[/FONT]. Press right arrow key to move to the S/PDIF channel and press the M key to mute that channel.
4. You have audible sound again.

Thanks again, great forum!

P.S.: at least that's how it is for analog setups. If you use digital equipment YMMV since you probably need the S/PDIF channel. Then again you might not have the whole problem to begin with.
