### Post by VastOne on 2010-10-14
Now that Guayadeque is available in the repositories, there a lot more people that will be seeing it and wondering what it is.

Guayadeque is a music management program designed for all music enthusiasts. It is a Full Featured Linux media player that can easily manage large collections and uses the powerful Gstreamer media framework.

You can find out all about it at the Guayadeque Wiki in several languages.

[_English Guayadeque Wiki_]("http://guayadeque.wikidot.com")

[_Spanish Guayadeque Wiki_]("http://guayadeque-es.wikidot.com")

[_Chinese Guayadeque Wiki_]("http://guayadeque-ch.wikidot.com")

[_Russian Guayadeque Wiki_]("http://guayadeque-ru.wikidot.com")

[_German Guayadeque Wiki_]("http://guayadeque-de.wikidot.com")

These are full fledged Wiki sites with a Forum to discuss issues in the same language getting help from people who have been using Guayadeque for 2 years.
