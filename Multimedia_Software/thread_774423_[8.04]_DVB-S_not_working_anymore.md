### Post by Verzweifler on 2008-06-02
I made it work, finally... <sigh!>

Following the instructions from
 [INDENT][http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/How_to_install_DVB_device_drivers]("http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/How_to_install_DVB_device_drivers")[/INDENT]
I downloaded the latest v4l-sources and built them the usual "make", "make install" way. After rebooting, my Pinnacle PCTV 400e was able to tune to "initial transponder 12551500 V 22000000 5" using scan.

Hope this helps
