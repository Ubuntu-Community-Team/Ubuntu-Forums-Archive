### Post by aeiah on 2012-03-02
the problem is that vga is analog, and hdmi and display port are digital. however, it isn't unusual for laptops to be able to output analog over these ports. you'll have to confirm this is the case, and then get a hdmi-vga or display port - vga adapter.

if your laptop can only output digital and your monitor can only accept analog, you're out of luck. you could buy a hdmi-vga converter but they're either expensive, rubbish, or both.
