### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-06-07
By default, the answer is NO.  ATI dropped restricted Linux driver support for this card long ago.

However, someone may respond with the location of a PPA you can use to get other drivers ... but ... and this is IMPORTANT, do NOT download drivers from the AMD site.  Forcing the installation of those drivers will corrupt your display.
