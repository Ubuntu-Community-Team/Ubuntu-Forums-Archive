### Post by Blue Rose on 2009-01-26
Today I got the idea to get my ol' nintendo gamecube out of the dusty shelves again and hook it up to my computer with my super-el-cheapo tv card. I searched the internet for some information and 'beware of this'-warnings before I tried. But thanks to ubuntu it worked right away :-) it has the saa7134 chipset, ubuntu recognised and set it up, I installed tvtime through adept and my gamecube instantly worked :D

just wanted to share my joy right now ^_^ I hadn't used the tvcard in a long time, since it was just a hassle to get it even recognised (and not yet working) in windows.
go ubuntu! :D

:popcorn:

ps: for people interested: I have not tried to watch tv through the card, because I have no cable up here in my room, we have a tv downstairs.. I only wanted to get the nintendo running on it, which does so now :-)
