### Post by Shompol on 2010-07-26
Ubuntu should handle 2-3 monitors without a problem. However, i do not understand the meaning of > **WA1DH said:**
>  run the HDMI out at the same time as the graphics card output?

HDMI has to be connected to a graphics card! If your motherboard comes with one, that means you have a built-in graphics card on the motherboard.

A setup with dual graphics cards: one on motherboard and one in AGP is unorthodox. I do not know if you can freely switch between them.

Btw, you can convert DVI to HDMI with a simple $5 male-to-male adapter.
