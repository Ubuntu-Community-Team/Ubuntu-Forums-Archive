### Post by endotherm on 2010-11-11
well, the updates do come, but they come when approved and set by the guys that administer the repositories. when you get a kernel update, you will also get a vid card driver update for that kernel. it may be the same driver version, or it may be upgraded to a newer version of nvidias driver. for the most part, each distro has a default driver version, and you can expect it to change rarely, though I think new drivers may appear in Additional Drivers. either way, every time you open additional drivers, it checks wiht the repositories to see what drivers have been approved for your configuration.
