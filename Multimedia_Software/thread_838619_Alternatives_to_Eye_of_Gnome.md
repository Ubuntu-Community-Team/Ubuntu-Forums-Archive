### Post by gcbzzzz on 2012-06-12
awesome suggestions.

feh is incredible clean. open the image in a window with no frills. but has a few dependencies. Right clicking will give some options, like rotating and deleting.

mirage depends on python-gtk and imagemagick and is much more feature complete. you can crop, open in gimp, navigate the other images in the directory, show thumbs of the directory on the left nav bar, and has plenty of  

I'm settling for Mirage. just having crop will save me much time over waiting gimp to open every time. Unplanned benefits of gnome3 screwing your life. :D
