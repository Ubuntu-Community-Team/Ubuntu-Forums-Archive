### Post by emptythevoid on 2011-03-01
I'm just going to throw out a couple of options that *might* do what you're wanting, but they're dependent on what site you're using.  

I use ClipGrab:
[http://clipgrab.de/en](http://clipgrab.de/en)

And I use the Firefox plugin DownloadHelper:
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/video-downloadhelper/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/video-downloadhelper/)

I know that these work on *most* websites that have flash video, but I don't know if they'll do anything if your video is live.
