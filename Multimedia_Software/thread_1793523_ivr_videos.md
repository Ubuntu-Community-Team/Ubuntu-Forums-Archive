### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-06-29
It looks as if ffmpeg will do it for you, or winff if you want a GUI.  You may need to get some of the unstripped versions of the libav packages and also the gstreamer bad and ugly plugins for it to work, depending on what you wish to encode to.

The various liobav packages I always add are:
libavcodec-edtra-52
libavdevice-extra-52
libavfilter-extra-1
libavformat-extra-52
libavutil-extra-50
