### Post by speartip on 2014-03-18
Someone may need to correct me here, but I don't think you can just burn compressed format videos to DVD & hope they will play in a normal DVD player.
You should be able to burn them with Brasero as a "data project", but these won't play in a normal DVD player.
To burn them as a "video project" you will need to use a program like DeVeDe first, to uncompress them & create an iso file, which you can then burn with Brasero under the "burn image" option. This should play in all DVD players.
