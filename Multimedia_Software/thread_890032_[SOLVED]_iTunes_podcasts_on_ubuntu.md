### Post by unseend on 2008-08-15
I recommend you Rhythmbox. The default music player in Ubuntu. It's really simple.
Open Rhythmbox then go to Music>New Podcast feed (Ctrl+P) and add the RSS feed URL there. And you are ready to listen to your favorite podcasts. By the way if you are online all the time you don't have to download each episode seperately; Rhythmbox can stream it to you. :-D cool huh? xD

I used to use iTunes, but 1) I switched completely to Ubuntu 2) Rhythmbox does it better.
