### Post by xc3RnbFO8P on 2008-06-05
In Lenovo 3000 N200 internal mic doesn't work. (maybe yours to)

If you open the Volume Control Edit> Preferences,
check all boxes Capture and Input
in Recording activate all Captures 
in Option choose front mic (all if got more than 1)
You should be able to hear sound in the loudspeakers if it works.
In my laptop I have to switch from front mic to mic and back again,to get it to work.
