### Post by sports.car.guy on 2010-01-18
The point of running Xubuntu is because you need a low demand desktop environment due to hardware constraints, being too old and slow for example. To turn on composite windowing features kind of defeats the point for loading Xubuntu.

All composite windowing and Compiz is, is bells and whistles. It is nice eye candy don't get me wrong, but it is far from needed. I don't use it and have a more then powerful enough videocard.

Don't dog your system down with it being an older one. Not worth the eye candy for the penalty on older hardware.
