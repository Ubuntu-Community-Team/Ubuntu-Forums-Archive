The problem for me and it may be for you too is that my display setting weren't set up right if you open up a terminal and the following command a window will open. > ~$ gnome-control-center
From here you can click on "**screen resolutio**n" under the section named "**hardware**" (should be the second from last thing in that section) and try different screen resolution settings.  For me the resolution was the top setting "**1280 x 800**" and the refresh rate at "**60 Hz**"(This was for my 15.4" laptop screen and the resolution for your monitor may be different, but I am sure that you could find the recommended resolution on the internet).

I hope that this will be of some assistance to you.
