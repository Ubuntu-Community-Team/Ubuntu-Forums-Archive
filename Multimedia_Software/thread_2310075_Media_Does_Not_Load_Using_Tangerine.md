### Post by jcjanko2 on 2016-01-15
A few years ago, I would listen to my music throughout my home by having one computer loaded with Tangerine, which shared my music to any computer or media player that I liked. Then, it just stopped working one day. I could still see the "Share Name" in Banshee and Rhythmbox, but when I clicked on it nothing happened. I searched all over for this problem, and could not find anything that solved the problem, so I simply stopped using it. I decided to try again this week, and the same problem persists. Let me explain what I can and cannot do:
-Installed Tangerine on both systems
-Enabled music sharing
-Set "Share name" as "Jason's Music" 
-Selected my Music folder (where all of my music is located)
-Opened Banshee on my other system
-The computer is listed under "Shared Music" as "Jason's Music" in the left pane
-I click on it, it says "Connecting to Jason's Music" then nothing
-I try to import, but nothing happens
-I set a password on the machine with the music
-It does the same as above, but then asks for the password
-I enter it, and nothing again
-I still cannot import media

I have no idea what I can do to get this working. I have my music folder fully shared as well. I can play music files from the shared folder in the network, but I can't import or see anything through Banshee or Rhythmbox (other than the "Share name"). I apologize if this is something very simple or has been answered before, as I have yet to find anything related to this. Thanks!

### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-01-16
This may be off the mark but worth throwing in. Have you tried the share name (and perhaps others) with no spaces? Also, you cannot use ' in the name as far as I know. In other words, try:

> Jasons_Music

Avoid spaces and +, ', ", * ... try to stick to alpha-numerics and - or _ .
