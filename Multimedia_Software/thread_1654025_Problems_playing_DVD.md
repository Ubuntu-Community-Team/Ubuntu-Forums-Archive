### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2010-12-28
Read the DVD playback support/help on the Ubuntu website.

You need the 3 libs dvdread, dvdnav, dvdcss.
May need libavcodec,format,device,filter-extras  from medibuntu ppa.

The latest mplayer (from 3rd party ppa or source) is recommended to at least check playback is possible. 
Some configuration of all the software players is necessary to get the best PQ.
