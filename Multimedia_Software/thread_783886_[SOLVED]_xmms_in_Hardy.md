### Post by mfratus2001 on 2008-05-07
I finally figured it out. Easy stuff if you do it a lot, but I just wanted to hear the streams!

Start Streamtuner, and go to Edit, Preferences. Click on the second column of the entry "Listen to a stream."
Change "xmms %q" to "totem %q" and close Preferences.

That's it! I'm sure there are better players, but totem comes with Ubuntu, and should already be working.

Mike.
