### Post by lidex on 2010-03-25
> Is this a red herring or the cause of my problems:
(EE) Failed to load /usr/lib/xorg/modules/extensions//libglx.so
(EE) Failed to load module "glx" (loader failed,
(EE) NVIDIA(0): Failed to initialize the GLX module; please check in your X

I had a problem with a broken symlink for libglx.so out of nowhere. I would check that first. Just use nautilus to browse /usr/lib/xorg/ and child folders. If you find a broken link, delete it and create one with the proper target.

BTW, look in /var/log/Xorg.0.log for X errors.
