### Post by markbuntu on 2009-01-07
The driver for that card is alredy installed, it is intel hda. The problem is that there are so many varations on the HDA ALC chips the driver has a difficult time parsing which configuration your particular chip was programmed for. You can go here and look in the Drivers/HDA section where there are some links that may help you out along with some basic troubleshooting.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=843012](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=843012)
