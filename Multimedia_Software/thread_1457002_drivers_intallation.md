### Post by quixote on 2010-04-19
Go to System > Administration > Synaptic.  Search for "ubuntu-restricted-extras".  Select for installation.  It'll say it needs a whole bunch of other files as well.  Say you want it all.  Hit apply.  That should at least be enough to get you started.
