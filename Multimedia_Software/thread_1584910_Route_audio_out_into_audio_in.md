### Post by gordintoronto on 2010-09-29
You need to decide where you want the sound to go, and it's not "into your microphone." Pulseaudio is all about routing sound to where you want it; you might want to read up about it. If you're running Karmic, I suggest you update to Maverick when it becomes available (less than two weeks!) because Pulseaudio (or the Ubuntu implementation) has improved greatly over the past year.
