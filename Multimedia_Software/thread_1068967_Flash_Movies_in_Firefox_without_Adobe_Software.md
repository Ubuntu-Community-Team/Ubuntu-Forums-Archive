### Post by gandaran on 2009-02-14
> But it appears that it doesn't hold out much hope for seeing flash video within browsers without the use of the nonfree plugin.

swfdec works but you get a play button on every flash item, gnash in ubuntu 8.04 only works or is compatible with flash 8, in ubuntu 8.10 gnash works fine, gnash is a good alternative to adobe flash, if you still have problems you can get the latest gnash release and compile it yourself 
and remember you can only have one flash plugin installed for it to work either adobe, swfdec or gnash.
