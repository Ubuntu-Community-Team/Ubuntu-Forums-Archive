### Post by gordintoronto on 2011-11-04
I have a 9600 GT. When I installed it, I used the included cable to go from the audio-out header on my motherboard, to the video card. That way, the sound is available on the HDMI.

So, it just depends on whether the person who installed your video card also installed the cable.

---

### Post by YigalB on 2011-11-04
It is working now.
The problem was that the SPDIF cable was wrongly connected to the SPDIF_IN on the motherboard.
Once I connected it to the OUT, it is working.
