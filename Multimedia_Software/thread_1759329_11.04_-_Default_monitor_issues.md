### Post by peterson43 on 2011-08-31
I did figure out how to switch which monitor is used as the primary one (where the Launcher menu shows up). It was as simple as simply switching the cables from the monitors to the computer. Even though the ports on the computer were labeled '1' and '2', it seems that Ubuntu is treating port '2' as the primary monitor when I have two monitors plugged in. 

It seems kind of silly to me that there isn't any option (in either System Settings > Monitors or in System Settings > Catalyst Control Center) to choose which monitor is regarded as the default. Similarly, I like the launcher menu, but I wish ther was a way to choose where it appeared on any computer (top, bottom, left, right).
