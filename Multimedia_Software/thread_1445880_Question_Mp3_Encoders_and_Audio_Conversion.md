### Post by ron999 on 2010-04-03
Hi
SoundJuicer is the default CD ripper for Ubuntu. It's easy to use and does a reasonable job.

If you want something that's more like EAC then RubyRipper is the one. You can use flac and lame straight off, and can specify the parameters. Also other codecs can be used.

There's a tutorial here telling how to install RubyRipper on Ubuntu:-[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=799621&highlight=ruby+ripper]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=799621&highlight=ruby+ripper")

Edit. As for converting your lossless files to mp3, SoundConverter will do the job with lame OK.
