### Post by zefrieg on 2008-04-28
Well, I fixed the issue with the nvidia drivers not working on my system.  In my case I never changed my grub settings after doing an update, so I was loading up an older kernel.  I changed it to use the 2.6.24-16 kernel and that seemed to fix all my issues.  Just though I would point out what worked for me in case anyone else is dual booting, did an update to Hardy, and found that the nvidia drivers wouldn't work.
