### Post by dmy on 2008-10-28
I have the D2X, and except the quite disappointing floppy power supply connector, I'm very satisfied with the card. 

> Does anybody know if...
>    * 5.1 surround sound (analog)

It works for me in digital optical SPDIF. I tested it in analog stereo at first without problem, no reason it should not work in 5.1.

>    * Optical in/out

Optical "out" fine here, optical "in" not tested.

>    * Headphone jack

not tested, I use the headphone jack of my ampli.

>    * Switching between 5.1 and headphone

see previous

> Does it work out of the box with 8.04 or do I have to manually install/compile alsa drivers?

It most likely won't work with 8.04 out of the box, alsa is too old (of just one version). You just need to recompile alsa drivers or upgrade to intrepid kernel (a few days to wait for final).
