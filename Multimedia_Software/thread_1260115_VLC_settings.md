### Post by Struki on 2009-09-07
I got VLC tru synaptic, and it's set to diplay video output in a new window while the playback control and panel remain as an individual window. How can I set it so that my video output and controls are in the same window (eg. like in windows)?

---

### Post by JCoster on 2009-09-07
Under Tools>Options, selected Interface and then choose the 'Native' interface.  Ensure that 'Embed video in interface' is checked and that display mode is 'Classic look'.

---

### Post by howefield on 2009-09-07
