### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2011-01-10
The sliders on the left adjust the low frequencies (bass), the ones in the middle adjust mid frequencies, and the ones on the right adjust high frequencies (treble).

An eq won't actually improve improve sound quality. I'd recommend you not use it if you don't know what you are doing.
