### Post by blastus on 2009-05-07
I use the MythVideo plugin in MythTV as a front-end organizer/catalog of multimedia content. I don't know why you want to *stream* Just setup NFS shares and configure MythTV to use those shares. MythTV supports various remote controls but I've never used them. I use a compact wireless keyboard and wireless mouse.

### Post by halloween2311 on 2009-05-08
Greetings,
Are you hooking the laptop to the TV?  IF so you can use XBMC and then "stream" the content from your bedroom pc to the laptop over your LAN.  XBMC is also compatible with various remotes if you have one with your laptop.

Either way you will need some sort of hardware hooked up to your TV to receive and display the media.  You can pick up media centers at most electronic stores that will do this for a pretty penny or you can turn an old xbox or pc into a media center with programs like XBMC or MythTV for next to nothing.

Hope this helps!
