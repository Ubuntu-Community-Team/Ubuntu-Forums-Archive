### Post by Johnny Buffalkill on 2011-10-03
I would recommend using a dedicated cd ripper, like Asunder to do the rip, then import them into Banshee, or whatever you end up using as a music player.  I've found that Asunder does a much better job of finding the cd track information than the music players.  It will write the cd metadata to the files, and once that is done, Banshee is pretty good about using that to find the cover art for its display.  I assume that you are ripping the CDs first, and not playing them directly.

I'm not sure what to do about Banshee stopping at the first song - I can't get mine to do that.
