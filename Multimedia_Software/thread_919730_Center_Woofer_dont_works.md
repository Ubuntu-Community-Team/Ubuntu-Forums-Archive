### Post by markbuntu on 2008-09-14
Many sound cards have options for the limited amount of plugs they have on them so you must configure these to match what you have plugged into them.

Look in the options section of your sound mixer. Right click on the speaker icon and choose Options/Line-in Mode. Change it from Line-In to Bass Output. I have a CMI8768 so it is probably the same for you. 

If you are having other surround sound problems, you can view my thread, which has a small section on surround sound setup:
