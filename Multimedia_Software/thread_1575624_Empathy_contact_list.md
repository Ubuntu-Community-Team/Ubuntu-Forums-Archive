### Post by daqron on 2010-10-30
Here's a little project I'm working on. It is in no way a real skin engine for Empathy. But I dunno, maybe it's a start. Still very rudimentary and you'll probably have to hack at it a bit to adapt it to your needs and screen size.

If you're timid about running command-line scripts, just remember that Ctrl+C stops the script. When you do kill it, your contact list will disappear and can be resurrected with the "Chat" option in the white mail icon in the Ubuntu notification area. 

Script doesn't directly edit any Empathy settings so I'm pretty sure it's safe to play around with.
