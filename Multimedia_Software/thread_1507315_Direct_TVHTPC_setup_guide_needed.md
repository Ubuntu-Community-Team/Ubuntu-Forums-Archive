### Post by steefjeqv on 2010-06-11
Hi,

I've been using VDR and it's plugins for several years now. It basically turns your computer into a settopbox and lots of other stuff. It's the only way I watch/record/stream media (TV, IPTV, music...) and it works great.

For an Ubuntu based version the best thing to do is to use the yaVDR team's ppa or their Ubuntu based distro yaVDR.


[http://www.yavdr.org/]("http://www.yavdr.org/")

Hardware : make sure you use an Nvidia graphics card or onboard gpu with at least 512 mb memory (anything from 8xxx series upwards). These cards use their GPU to decode/deinterlace video (also HD) and their deinterlacing methods are very good.
