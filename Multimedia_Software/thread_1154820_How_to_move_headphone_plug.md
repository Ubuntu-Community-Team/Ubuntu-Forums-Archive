### Post by Unhban on 2009-05-10
I presume you mean you destroyed the headphone *socket* on your laptop, and not the *plug*, which is on the end of your headphone's lead?

Yes, it is possible to rewire the cable that's inside the laptop that goes to the headphone socket and re-attach it to the line-in socket. Of course you couldn't use that socket as a line-in then. Or you could use the mic.-in socket, if you don't use that one.

You need to open the laptop, unsolder the cable going to the mashed headphone socket, un-solder the line-in socket's cable, tape it up, and then solder the headphone socket cable to it, making sure the individual wires are the correct way round.

If this all sounds a bit daunting take it to your nearest electronics hobby shop and they'll no doubt do it for you.

(nowt to do with Ubuntu, but there ya go! :) )
