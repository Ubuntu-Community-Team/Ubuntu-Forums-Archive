### Post by trinsnet on 2011-06-13
I have successfully paired and tested my Nokia AD-42W bluetooth audio gateway with Ubuntu 10.04. Once I stop playing audio for a length of time, the connection is closed and the bluetooth audio device disappears from System>Preferences>Sound>Hardware. I can see the the Nokia device in Bluetooth preferences (as a successful pairing) but I cannot figure out how to reestablish the connection - double clicking and right clicking has no effect. The only way I can get the device back into the System>Preferences>Sound>Hardware list is to re-pair the devices. Any ideas?

Thanks

Solution: I installed blueman and hid the regular bluetooth icon. Blueman allows the initiation of a connection to the audio sink.
