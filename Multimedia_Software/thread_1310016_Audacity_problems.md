### Post by coggins on 2009-11-01
Have you checked that your capture volume is up?  I'm sure there is a GUI way, but I have found the most reliable way is to open a terminal and run: ```
alsamixer
```
Press TAB to show the capture volume(s) and turn them up with the cursor keys.  Also check if anything is muted (MM at bottom of fader).  The M key toggles mute on/off.
My apologies if this is all old news to you, but it is a simple thing that is easy to overlook.
