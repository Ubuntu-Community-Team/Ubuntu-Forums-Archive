### Post by fart_flower on 2010-01-09
I too have this problem.

After a bit of fiddling, I discovered QTPFSGUI dislikes the upper case JPG extension that my camera automatically appends to image files.  Manually renaming photos with a lower case extension (jpg) fixes the issue.
