### Post by x C0MMAND0 x on 2013-05-17
At a quick look, (haven't tested it myself)

[https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.iandrobot.andromouse.lite&hl=en](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.iandrobot.andromouse.lite&hl=en)

The above app, or any similar should do it.  This category of apps connects your phone/tablet to your computer via Bluetooth - so you have to have a bluetooth receiver in your computer you wish to control.  Then, the app just works as a mouse or keyboard, so a simple mouse click or up/right arrow *should* advance the slides in your presentation for you.

You can also buy a bluetooth receiver if you choose to go this route and don't have one built in, but at that point, a simple USB - IR receiver would likely be a cheaper option, but eliminate your initial desire of using your phone.

Good Luck!

Edit: It looks like the App link I posted will also allow control over a Wifi network, if both your computer and phone/tablet are on the same network.
