### Post by ludovicc on 2008-06-01
Hayhay sir!

Read this guide, and install WinFF:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683)

Then convert your videos with WinFF: select your videos, the output folder, and select Convert to... 'Websites', then in the second dropbox, select 'Flash (flv) video for Web Use (4:3)'

Finally, login in Youtube, and upload your video.
