### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-11-16
The mp3 player may give you some distortion, so be careful with output level from the sansa when you plug it in by keeping its volume level right down and raising it slowly.

As for an app to play the sound, if you can hear the mic input through the netbook, you should also be able to hear the sansa, however you may need to play with levels in alsamixer to get the sound output direct, as I did with my desktop system, where I could record with no problem from the mic, but could not hear any output direct until I unmuted and raised one of the levels using that, (I can't remember which, but try them all one by one)
