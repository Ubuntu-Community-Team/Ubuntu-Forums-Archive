### Post by LuridCinema on 2010-04-10
Audacity should get the sound out. I can click on a video and audacity will extract the audio out and open it for editing... you can do the noise removal w/ Audacity as well. You would then need to drop the sound file and the video file into an NLE and render.. I recommend either Openshot or KdenLive
