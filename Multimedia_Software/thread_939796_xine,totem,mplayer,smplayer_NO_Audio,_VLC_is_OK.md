### Post by jlh68 on 2008-10-06

### Post by jlh68 on 2008-10-08
**SOLVED**:popcorn:

Well no-one responded to my post (45 views).  I have solved the problem by looking into all the pull-down lists in SMPlayer.  Under the [Options] was a checked [Visualize _m_otion vectors].  I unchecked (unticked) it and then checked it out.  It cleared up the problem.

So note, if you see dots with arrows that go with the motion, then uncheck the [Visualize motion vectors]
