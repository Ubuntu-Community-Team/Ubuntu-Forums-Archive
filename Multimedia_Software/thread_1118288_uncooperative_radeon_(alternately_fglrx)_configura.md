While I was able to get fglrx "working" in dual-head mode (ATI Big Desktop), I'm rather disappointed in the result.  It'a hardly faster than the intel GMA 3000 onboard graphics my motherboard has.

Even if you get your card working, it seems like the drivers are not quite at the level of nvidia's yet - in spite of the problems nvidia drivers have had with kde4.

I have another machine running kubuntu with an agp geforce 6600 and while the blur is a little too heavy for it (you see the saturation of the area before it blurs), it at least works, and this is a 5-6 yrs old graphics card!  On this HD 4350, blur doesn't work, and more often than not X crashes when changing settings for compositing.
