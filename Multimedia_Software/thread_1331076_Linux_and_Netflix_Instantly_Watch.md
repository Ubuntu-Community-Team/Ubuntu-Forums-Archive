### Post by bluepop13 on 2009-11-19
A lot of people including myself have been searching the internet for some possible way to watch movies instantly with [Netflix]("http://www.netflix.com") using Linux as we don't use Windows or Mac. This has troubled many and I'm sure some people have just given up on using Netflix - what a shame. It is a shame because of the great variety of movies available on the website that one can watch if they were using a supported Operating System.

If you're in this situation and just happen to have a Playstation 3 you're in luck. You can simply have a free DVD shipped to you that you just insert in your PS3 and it will give you a code to activate it. All you do is login to your netflix account and put in that code, it activates and viola! At this point you just login to netflix to add movies to your instant watch queue and insert the DVD into your PS3 and you're set.

Unfortunately for people who do not own or have access to a PS3 Console it will be difficult and not something they probably want to bother with as it will cost people the price of the console in order to do this. You could actually buy Windows and it would save you money in this way but we like our Open Source software. This is something I have discovered and am very happy with so I will continue to stay with Netflix as long as I feel it's got great movies I like to watch.
