### Post by sapple on 2012-06-11
I know it is too late to answer, but for anyone who faces the same problem:

You probably deleted the **system tray** from the your panel. Right click on the panel, select add widget and then select system tray. If you volume control is not displayed in the system tray, right click on the system tray that you have just added, go to settings, click hide/show and make volume button always visible. Play around, it is somewhere around there.
