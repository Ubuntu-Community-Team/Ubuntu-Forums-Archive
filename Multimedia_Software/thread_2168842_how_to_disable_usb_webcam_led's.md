### Post by 1clue on 2013-08-19
I can think of a few reasons someone would want this, but all of them are nefarious.

Higher quality webcams should be impossible to run the camera without the LED in place, meaning that the camera is run in series with the LED.

Cheaper cameras might have the LED as a separately controlled feature.

I can't think of a single ethical reason why somebody would disable the LED while the camera is running.
