### Post by Raymond Petit on 2008-09-18
I hope that what I am about to tell you will help.  I also had a problem with flash player in Firefox 3.  I installed shockwave only to find that it did not work with my architecture (x386, 32bit).  When I visited a site in Firefox that required flash 9 to play it would never install.  All of the forum advice did not work either.  Then, I noticed that when I was visiting those sites ther was a box with that little puzzle piece telling me to install Flashplayer 9, and also that information 'drop down' box just below the menu bar telling me the same thing.  That last time I left clicked on the 'drop down' box instead of the box on the web page with the puzzle piece and Flash was installed with no problem.  As you can probably tell, I am a non-technical noob, who is just trying to learn.  Hope this helps you.  I amost forgot.  I had to un-install shockwave first using synaptic. Just search for in when you open synaptic and when it is hilighted choose to un-install.  Visit a website where you will need flash and install like I described above. Good Luck
