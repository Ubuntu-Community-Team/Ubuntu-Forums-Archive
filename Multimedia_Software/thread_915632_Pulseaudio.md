### Post by markbuntu on 2008-09-10
Unfortunately, Ubuntu left out the controls for pulseaudio in the standard installation. Luckily, you can get them from the repository:

padevchooser ( device chooser for setting up networking)
paman (PulseAudio Device Manager)
paprefs (Pulse Audio Preferences)
pavucontrol (Pulse Audio Volume Control)

For setting up networking, etc:

[http://pulseaudio.org/wiki/FAQ](http://pulseaudio.org/wiki/FAQ)

If you have a lot of time, you can read my overly extensive guide to audio in Ubuntu:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=843012](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=843012)
