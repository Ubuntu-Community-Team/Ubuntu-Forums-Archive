### Post by peta_50 on 2010-03-04
Getting podcasts on my iPod in the Podcasts playlists has been a bit of a problem for me ever since I've started using Linux. (Which has been about a month now, n00b!) Anyway, with some tinkering, I've figured out a solution to my problem and may help other people (I hope).

Anywho, let's get down to business. In order to get your podcasts in the "Podcasts" playlist of your iPod, follow these steps:

1. Connect your iPod.

2. Open Rhythmbox (you should see your iPod on the left column and all of your playlists should be there along with your Podcasts folder too.)

3. Right click on the name of your iPod and create a new playlist. (I called mine Podcast 2)

4. Assuming you are subscribing to some podcasts through RhythmBox, drag the podcasts you want into the original "Podcasts" playlist.

5.Then, from the Podcasts playlist, drag those podcasts into the main folder of your iPod, if they aren't already there. 

6. From there, drag the podcasts into the "Podcast 2" folder.

7. And then drag them back into the original "Podcast" folder.

8. Lastly, wait for about 2 or 3 minutes, then Eject your iPod and your podcasts should show up in your Podcasts directory on your iPod.

I know this seems like you are going in a big circle, but it works. I hope this will help the developers and users. I wish there was an easier way, but this works for me for the time being.
