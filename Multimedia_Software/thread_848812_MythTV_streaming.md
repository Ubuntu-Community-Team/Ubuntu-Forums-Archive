### Post by funkydan2 on 2008-07-04
I'm not quite sure what your asking, however MythTV runs as a client/server.  So what you could do is ...

* Put a MythTV server somewhere in your house (out of the way) - this would have 1 or more TV tuners in it.
* Install the MythTV frontend software (the client) on any Linux PC that you own.  Then you can watch TV from any computer you have.
* For each of your HDTVs use either a cheap, small PC or something like a modded XBox so that you can access your MythTV server on these TVs (or your main MythTV box might be best connected up to your main TV and then just have a cheaper computer in the bedroom).  (You could then also store video files, photo albums, music files etc and access them at these computers.)
