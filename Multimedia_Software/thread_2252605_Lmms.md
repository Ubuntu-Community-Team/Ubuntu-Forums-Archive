### Post by Entheo on 2014-12-13
I have that book as a PDF and it really is worth reading for a beginner who wants a good knowledge base to build from. There are some things in there that you will not learn on doc sites easily unless you have many hours available for research. 

Btw it may need a little expertise to configure at first but i have no problems with VST compatability at all on LMMS running on xubuntu 14.04. Every VST i have downloaded has worked perfectly. Open source DAW's especially LMMS have come a long way and there are many people out there in music production i have spoken to who would argue that open source DAW's can be as good as pro one's when used right (like any software).
