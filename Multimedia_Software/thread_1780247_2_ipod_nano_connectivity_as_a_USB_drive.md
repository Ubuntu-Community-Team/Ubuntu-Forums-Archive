### Post by nothingspecial on 2011-06-11
As it stands, a 6g ipod nano, updated, will not work with linux.

You cannot simply drag and drop like you do with your Sansas.

Any benefits of an iPod over a sansa (if there are any), are lost if you have no access to iTunes.

If you do have access to Windows or a Mac (and therefore iTunes), go for it. If you don't, you will be buying a very expensive lump of plastic.
