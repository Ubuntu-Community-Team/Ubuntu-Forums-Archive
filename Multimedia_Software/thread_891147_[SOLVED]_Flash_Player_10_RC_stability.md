### Post by Browser_ice on 2008-08-27
I installed Flash 10 3 days ago. At the time I installed I think I realized what was my main Flash problem (too many Flash animations at the same time even in different Firefox pages).

But now since yesterday, my Firefox 3 just closes down without warning if it tries to display a Flash animation (did a thread about it just now).

I think I will go back to Flash-9. As for Gnash (don't recall exact name) as for an alternative to Flash, I had it at first but then could not play ANY flash videos.

I would recommand you wait. Watch the forum for any additional complaints about Flash-10 and see if there is a workaround for its bugs/stability. In the mean time, use Flash-9 but reduce the amounts of pages with Flash animations to the strict minimum. Only keep the page you actualy want to see and close all the other ones.
