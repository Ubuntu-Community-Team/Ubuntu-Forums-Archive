### Post by marcus.pearlman on 2014-01-05
This part in the "metamodes" line concerns me:
{viewportout=1774x997+73+41}

I am by no means an expert, but in my experience, that line is always equal to the resolution of the monitor.  When I played with this, it made the resolution "appear" different.
try not using auto-select for the resulution for both monitors, and do not fiddle with the viewport.  I assume you probably tried this already, so I am probably no help.  good luck
