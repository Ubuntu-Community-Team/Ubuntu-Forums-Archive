   I am running Ubuntu 10.04 64 bit and have a 40gb 4th generation iPod.  I installed the newest version of Banshee(1.6 I think) and started the painstaking effort of reorganizing all of my music files. ### Post by Bulletproofmask on 2010-06-22
This problem seems to have worked itself out.  I upgraded Ubuntu today and when I attached my iPod to the computer Banshee alerted me that the iPod was not recognized and the iPod database had to be rebuilt.  After it finished rebuilding I could access the music in the device and it syncs properly with Banshee.

Thank you to everyone who took the time to read this post and I am glad to report that this issue has been resolved.
