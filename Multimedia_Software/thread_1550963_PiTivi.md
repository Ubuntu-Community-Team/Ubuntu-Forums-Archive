### Post by HannesBln on 2012-02-29
Pitivi likes PNG ... and especially transparent ones ;-)

So create a picture with your video's resolution within GIMP (e.g. 1280x720) with transparent background. Add your text.
Save the GIMP-picture as PNG.

Open up Pitivi, import the PNG-picture into the media-library and pull this picture into the "track"-view.
Import your video too, drag it into the track-view too, but **below** the picture.

Now you should see your text in front of the video.

Adjust the track-length of your PNG-resource to match the desired duration of your text.

Good luck! :D
