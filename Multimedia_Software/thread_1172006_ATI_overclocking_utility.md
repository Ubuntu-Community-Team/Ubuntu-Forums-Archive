### Post by Melcar on 2009-05-28
For those interested in overcloking their ATI cards (and don't want to mess around with CLI based Overdrive):

[http://www.phoronix.com/forums/showthread.php?t=17331]("http://www.phoronix.com/forums/showthread.php?t=17331")

It works (already tested it) provided:

- You have an ATI card supported by fglrx (and the driver installed obviously)
- Your card is able to overclock with Overdrive (some can't, like certain mobile chips)
- If on a non-KDE DE, you at least have the appropriate libraries (libqt4-core, libqt4-gui, libqt4-network should do it)
