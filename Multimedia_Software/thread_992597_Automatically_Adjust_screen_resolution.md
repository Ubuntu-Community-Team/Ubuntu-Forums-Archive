### Post by djbushido on 2008-11-25
There is not a way to reset the resolution without re-logging in that i know of. What happens when you do ctrl-alt-backspace is that you reset X11 and gdm. There might be a way to reset these programs in a session, but i wouldn't recommend messing with these files unless absolutely necessary.
