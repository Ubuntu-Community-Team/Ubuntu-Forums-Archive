Hi,

I have been recording Internet radio since 2006 using Suse, Mepis and then Ubuntu.  I have had nothing but hassles even just upgrading form a to b and no longer able to record.  With 10.10 it happened with sound recorder, I searched for "something" that would work.

[http://outrec.sourceforge.net/](http://outrec.sourceforge.net/)

OutRec is worth a look, it is simple and automatically names with a date & time stamp what was recorded. Also option to record in MP3 or Ogg.

I would love to see a scheduler like for my DVR.

Hope this helps for now.
