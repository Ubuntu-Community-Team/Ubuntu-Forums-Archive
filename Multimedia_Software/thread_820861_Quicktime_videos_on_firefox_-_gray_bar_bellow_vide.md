### Post by wild_oscar on 2008-06-25
I have managed to solve this problem. The solution is quite simple: send the mplayer plugin to hell and use the totem plugin. 

Uninstall the mplayer-mozilla plugin and keep the totem-mozilla plugin, which works much better.
