### Post by AKADAP on 2009-01-15
I had the same problem with an HD 4850 card.
It is possible to fix the problem after the install by installing the proprietary ATI driver (can't be included on the disk due to licensing conflicts).
After you get Ubuntu installed with video safe mode, one of the things that pops up in the upper right corner of the screen is a little green circuit board symbol. Just click on that and enable the hardware driver.
