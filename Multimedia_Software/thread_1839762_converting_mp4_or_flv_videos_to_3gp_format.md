### Post by andrew.46 on 2011-09-06
FFmpeg can certainly do this for you but you may need to experiment a little with your 3gp container + phone. The lowest common denominator with 3gp is usually h.263 video and amr sound which may be enough for your phone but aac sound is also possible which will improve sound quality greatly. Depends what your phone will handle....
