### Post by cyfur01 on 2008-12-12
Note that most movies are **illegal** to download as far as I know.

Sites such as [isohunt]("https://isohunt.com/") host many torrents. You download the torrent file, and then open that with transmission to start the download.
(Technically, isohunt is a search engine and doesn't host the files, but it makes the process much easier.)
