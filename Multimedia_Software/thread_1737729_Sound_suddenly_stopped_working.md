### Post by psytech on 2011-04-23
Hello, I have an Asrock M3A770DE motherboard with a VIA VT1708S sound chip. I am running 10.10 amd64, and the sound suddenly stopped working. I have tried the troubleshooting methods in the sticky, besides compiling alsa, and they did not work. I have tried booting into a previous kernel. I dual boot windows, and sound still works in windows. What can I do? Has anybody else experienced problems with this chip? I really don't want to go buy a sound card.

Edit: I solved my problem; I went into "sound preferences" in the top bar and changed the connector from "analogue headphones" to analogue output".
