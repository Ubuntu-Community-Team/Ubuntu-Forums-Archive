### Post by seanos on 2015-08-20
If you&#8217;re talking about e-books with copyright protection then see above. There are also ways to get around Adobe DRM, but this isn&#8217;t the place to ask about that!

If you&#8217;re talking about regular epubs without protection, then [install calibre immediately]("http://calibre-ebook.com/download_linux") if you haven&#8217;t already. It&#8217;s the best e-book manager out there with heaps of features (including format conversion, an epub editor, a reader, etc.), can recognize all sorts of readers, has lots of plugins available and runs on Linux, OSX & Windows.

**Edit:** also check out the [mobileread forums]("http://www.mobileread.com/forums/") for more than you ever wanted to know about e-books and e-readers (including a support forum for calibre) as well as heaps of [free e-books]("http://www.mobileread.com/forums/forumdisplay.php?f=132").
