### Post by Precipitous on 2009-04-20
wbee:

What is the URL you are trying to access? More than likely it is pointing to a secondary page, where the stream is then launched  (which is why you are being prompted for a plugin to decode the HTML). The various Soma FM stations are good examples of this. Oftentimes all that is needed is to find or figure out the actual stream address. In the case of Soma FM Drone Zone, for instance, the URL link on their website is [http://somafm.com/play/dronezone](http://somafm.com/play/dronezone) but the URL of the actual stream is [http://somafm.com/dronezone.pls](http://somafm.com/dronezone.pls)
