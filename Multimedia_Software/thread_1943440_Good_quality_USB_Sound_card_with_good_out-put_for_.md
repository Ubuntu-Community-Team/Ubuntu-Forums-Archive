### Post by FulciLives on 2012-03-20
The **[NuForce Icon uDAC2](http://estore.nuforce.com/products/NuForce-Icon-uDAC2.html)** is a very popular USB headphone amp. It is popular due to the low price and great performance (i.e., sound quality).

I know it works well with MS Windows and Apple Mac. Not sure how well it works with Ubuntu or Linux in general but it is a USB device so I would imagine it 'just works'

If you want high quality but a "low" price then this is the way to go. Assuming it plays nice with Linux.

You might want to read this post that I just found: [CLICK HERE](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1628787)
