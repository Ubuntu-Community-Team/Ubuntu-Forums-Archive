### Post by Rasa1111 on 2011-09-21
you can stop it / pause it in your "sound menu" (in top panel)

or..
with clementine window open..
open "File" menu, and choose "Quit". 

(or just use the "stop" button)?

or, open a terminal and type ```
killall clementine
```
