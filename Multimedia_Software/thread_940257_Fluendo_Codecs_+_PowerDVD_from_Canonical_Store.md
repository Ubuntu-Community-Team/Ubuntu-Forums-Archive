### Post by deanjm1963 on 2008-10-11
UPDATE

I purchased both the Fluendo Complete Playback codecs and PowerDVD.

I'm "very impressed" with PowerDVD, and just "very happy" with the codecs.

After quite a few years of using the same ol' hacks of libdvdcss2 and other things to get DVDs working it's very nice to install one package that does it all for you.

Colours are rich and playback flawless. The interface is very modern, nothing like LinDVD which was stuck in the 1980's ;-)

The codecs are very good too. Almost everything plays out of the box. The only thing that needed to be installed on top of the fluendo codecs was ffmpeg (I prefer the Medibuntu version), gstreamer0.10-ffmpeg and gstreamer0.10-ugly/ugly-multiverse.

Even Totem is behaving itself and not grinding to a halt when playing an AVI.

For those of you who have a few dollars to spare and want the easy way to play your DVDs and video files you can't do any better. A lot cheaper than purchasing the same software for Windows.
