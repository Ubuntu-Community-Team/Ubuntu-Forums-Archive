### Post by ghostborg on 2010-03-24
Frustrated are we, Yoda speak.
For VLC:
You can disable the filename from flashing up on your video in the preferences under Video with the "all" view selected, not simple.
Until you find a solution.

Under VLC's Tools menu => Media Information - it pops up with a panel with fields and as soon as you type into the Artist field a save button appears at the bottom right of the panel but nothing seems to ever get saved. Maybe a work in progress.


The best I could find was an old program called abcAVI Tag editor that runs under Wine but is for AVI as it's name indicates.
