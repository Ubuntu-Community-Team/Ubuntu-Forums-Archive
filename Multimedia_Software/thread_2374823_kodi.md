### Post by Tadaen_Sylvermane on 2017-10-19
Generally new versions include new features that are quite helpful. Before trying to downgrade may I ask why you want to downgrade? If it's something as simple as the skin then you can get the old Confluence in the newest Kodi easily.

It's also a bad idea imo to version lock yourself. Mainly because if you get stuck on a single version, that version won't be kept up to date forever. Better to adapt to newer software than get stuck with something that just becomes more and more of a problem as time goes on.
