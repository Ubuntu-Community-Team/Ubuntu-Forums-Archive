### Post by bill-lancaster on 2011-08-03
It seems that pitivi doesn't like either 1) a lot (or too large) jpegs or 2) mixing video with stills.

It also seems that Bombono DVD doesn't want jpegs at all.

So by using pitivi render to produce small Ogg Theora video clips from groups of jpegs, then using these with video clips in Bombono produces a working Video DVD.

Messy, I know but it works.
