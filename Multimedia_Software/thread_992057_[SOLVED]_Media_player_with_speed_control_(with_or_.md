### Post by rvm4000 on 2008-11-24
> **bazzer said:**
> I know SMplayer can do it, but it decreases the pitch at the same time, which obviously is no good to me.

The pitch won't be changed if you use a recent mplayer. The one included in Ubuntu (1.0rc2) doesn't provide the scaletempo filter needed for that.

You can get MPlayer SVN r27900, for Hardy and Intrepid from here:
[https://launchpad.net/~rvm/+archive](https://launchpad.net/~rvm/+archive)
