### Post by Laurent B on 2013-03-02
Hello,

My brand new Nexus 4 isn't recognized by Rhythmbox and apparently this is due to the libmtp underlying library. It is actually obviously impacting all libmtp based software.

Currently in Ubuntu 12.10, libtmp is in version 1.1.4-1 and therefore does not recognize the device (VID=18d1 and PID=4ee2). There is a thread about this [there]("http://sourceforge.net/p/libmtp/bugs/708/"), and the fix may be released with version libmtp-1.1.6.

Eager to see it arriving in Ubuntu repo.
