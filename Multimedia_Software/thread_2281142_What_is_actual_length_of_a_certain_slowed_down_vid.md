### Post by kulen19 on 2015-06-05
What kind of question is this :p ? And doesn't 1.20x mean 20% faster(not slower) ? 

If so, and you don't mind the seconds:

```
113 / 1,2 &#8776; 94 minutes 
```

The runtime should be approx **1hour** **and 34 minutes**.

If 1.20x means infact 20% slower, the equation should be

```
113 / 0,8 &#8776; 141 minutes 
```

which means **2 hours and 21 minutes**
