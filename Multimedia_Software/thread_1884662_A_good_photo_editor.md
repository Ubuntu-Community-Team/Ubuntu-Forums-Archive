### Post by kurja on 2011-11-27
There are many ways to sharpen an image (is that really all you want to do?), what I usually do in gimp is to use the **unsharp mask** (found under filters->enhance->unsharp mask), with that you have a couple parameters (radius, amount, threshold)with which you can adjust the sharpening to your liking. The right parameters depend on a kazillion variables, so just try and see how you like it - it does have real-time preview =)
