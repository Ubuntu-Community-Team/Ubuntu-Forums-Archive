### Post by stafusa on 2010-03-28
The question is a little old and you probably already found an answer somewhere else or gave up anyway, but just for someone googling the question I use:

ffmpeg -sameq -i arq.mov arq.mpg

I don't really like the result of it (maybe it's my outdated linux, though) for I certainly can't believe the -sameq (same quality) is being applied, at least not for decent resolution videos. I use it just because coudn't live with those huge mov files in my HD.
