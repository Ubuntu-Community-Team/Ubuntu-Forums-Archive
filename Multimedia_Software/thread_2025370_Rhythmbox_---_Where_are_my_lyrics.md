### Post by JRBASTIEN on 2012-10-05
Go to View/Song Lyrics.  This will a pop up a window that will hopefully show you the lyrics.  That does not work too well for me.  It does not find a lot of lyrics.

I prefer the lLyrics third party plugin because it displays the Lyrics in a side pane.  Unfortunately, it makes Rhythmbox crash a lot but I'm being told it is just my system.
