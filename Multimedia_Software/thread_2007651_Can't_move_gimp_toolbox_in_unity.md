### Post by joyousmoo on 2012-09-06
> **c-m said:**
> 
The problem I face is that the unity header bar at the top of the screen covers the gimp toolbox meaning that the tool box cannot be moved, or closed.


The Alt+Mouse_drag didn't work for me, but this did:

In Gimp, go to Preferences > Windows Management 

Then set the "Hint for the toolbox" and "Hint for other docks" to "Normal Window".
