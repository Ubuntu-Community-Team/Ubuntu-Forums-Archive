### Post by kelt65 on 2012-08-02
1.5GB is not terrible for a 60 min video. I normally do not use the bitrate to calculate anything, but .2 or so should result in fairly good quality. With dvdRip my rule of thumb was about 700MB per 60 minutes. That said, dvdrip pales in comparison to handbrake, which has a PPA with 12.04 debs.

### Post by JohnAStebbins on 2012-08-03
> **foxy123 said:**
> Handbrake does not support AVI or does it?

HandBrake does not support AVI output.  It will only output mp4 or mkv.  The avi container has many limitations.  Unless you have a device that only supports this format, it's time to leave the 90's and update to something a bit more modern.  AVI is 20 years old now and support for it is slowly evaporating.
