### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2011-02-07
I have one of these, but I am just using it as a pre-amp. I have tried using it as a device but only the input works, the output does nothing. If using Audacity it could be useful as you could set your onboard card as output, and the Art as input. Latency and quality are not an issue though.

Unless you find the Art cheaper, I would go for another device. M-Audio fast-track pro or Edirol UA-25 are good choices.
