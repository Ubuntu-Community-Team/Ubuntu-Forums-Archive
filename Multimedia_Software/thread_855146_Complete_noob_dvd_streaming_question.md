### Post by cozmicharlie on 2008-07-10
I see know one is helping you so I will take a shot.  I am no expert in streaming media over the internet but I can point you to a Ubuntu App that will help you.  Since you refer to avi files then I assume by "DVD jukebox" you mean a movie from a dvd streamed over the internet?  You may find it very difficult.  DVD are in vob files and these tend to be very large.  That's why most movies on the internet are streamed as avi, flash, quicktime or some other compressed format. It can be done though.  This may help [http://www.allformp3.com/dvd-faqs/47.htm](http://www.allformp3.com/dvd-faqs/47.htm).  For streaming music over the internet you can use vlc (it is mentioned in the article as videolan project)- it is a very handy player that can handle most any format. It is also available for Linux and Windows machines and there is a firefox vlc plug in.

good luck
