### Post by Platypus123 on 2009-11-27
I Know this reply is WAY overdue, but if you select your iPod on the left-hand side, it takes you to the Eject iPod page which has five boxes you can select.  If you de-select the first two options (Manually Manage this device & Sync Music), the Synchronize iPod button appears in the upper right-hand corner.

I hope this still helps!
