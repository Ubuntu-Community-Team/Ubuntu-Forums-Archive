your hardware is to old for the 180 drivers from what i can see. if you Deactivate the driver with Hardware Drivers i think it will tell you which major version number you have when you are presented with the option to Activate.

if Hardware Drivers says NVIDIA accelerated graphics driver (legacy cards) is enabled and nvidia-settings does not, nvidia-settings probably does not work with your configuration.  i think your best bet is to read then manual pages for Xorg.conf and nvidia-settings and then create the best Xorg.conf you can with nvidia-settings and then edit Xorg.conf yourself.
