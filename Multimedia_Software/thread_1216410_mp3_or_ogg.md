### Post by Andras B. on 2009-07-18
I use my Nokia 6300 as a portable media player, and it doesn't play ogg or flac either, but that's no problem, because I use Rhythmbox to transfer music to the phone, and the files get transcoded into MP4 format automatically during the transfer, which my phone supports.
My point is, files can be transcoded during transfer. Use e.g.  Rhythmbox to sync files to your device, and you will not need an ogg or flac capable device. :)
