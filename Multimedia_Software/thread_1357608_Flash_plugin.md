### Post by HappyFeet on 2009-12-17
Go [here]("http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/?promoid=DXLUJ") and download the .tar.gz flash file, right click and **extract here**. Then put the resulting **libflashplayer.so** file into /usr/lib/opera/plugins directory.
