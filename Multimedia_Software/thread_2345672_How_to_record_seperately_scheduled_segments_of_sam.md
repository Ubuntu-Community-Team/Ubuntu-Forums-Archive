### Post by PaulRSte on 2016-12-06
When a program is scheduled in say two parts, e.g. with a news program in between, then mythtv treats both parts as the same program. One of the two parts will not be recorded as it is either a later or an earlier showing of the same program. Is there a way round this please?

### Post by deadflowr on 2016-12-06
