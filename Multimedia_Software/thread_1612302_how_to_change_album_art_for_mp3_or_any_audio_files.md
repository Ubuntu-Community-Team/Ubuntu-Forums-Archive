### Post by Capta!nCrude on 2010-12-12
Easytag can change album art, but the interface of the software leaves a lot to be desired. But it works for me, and should work for you. This is how I do it;

1. Locate your folder containing the music files you want label art for.

2. Select all these files and the go to the pictures tab to the right

3. If no image is displayed, then press the green plus icon, and locate a .jpg file of your album art. Select that file and then click on the little round button to the right of the image. And then save the changes.

I hope this helps you.

### Post by mutex023 on 2010-12-16
MP3 tags have two versions V1 and V2. Usually most tag editors allow you to edit/add both the tags, or any one. Some even provide the option to copy the id3v1 tag to id3v2 tag. The V2 tag can store more information about the song than a V1 tag, like album art for example. Some times the information about a song having just a V1 tag, will not be displayed on modern mp3 players, since they might be just reading the V2 tag. Hence the suggestion.
