To convert DVD to avi/xvid etc. try k9copy
[How to backup your DVDs (in dvd, xvid, mpeg-4, x264 formats) using k9copy](http://www.my-guides.net/en/content/view/77/26/).

For batch video conversion I also like winff:
[How to convert any video format in Linux](http://www.my-guides.net/en/content/view/90/26/)

To convert video to dvd there is devede:
[How to create a custom DVD using DeVeDe](http://www.my-guides.net/en/content/view/75/26/)

In the past I also installed ripit4me, dvdshrink and dvddecrypter in Linux using wine:
[How to install RipIt4Me, DVDShrink and DVDDecrypter in Linux](http://www.my-guides.net/en/content/view/79/26/)
