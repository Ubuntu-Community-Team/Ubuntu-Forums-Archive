### Post by mc4man on 2015-09-29
It's still being developed [a bit]("https://git.gnome.org/browse/banshee/log/") but hasn't progressed past 2.6.2 in Ubuntu/Debian except in Debian 'experimental' (2.9.1-4).
For a short time in 14.04 it went to 2.9.0 but was reverted back to 2.6.1 & has remained basically that way, at 2.6.2 for all Ubuntu active releases after 12.04 - 
> banshee (2.9.0+really2.6.1-0ubuntu1) trusty; urgency=low
