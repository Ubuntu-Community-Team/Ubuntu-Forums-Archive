### Post by sam.reader on 2010-03-03
The kind of effects that you have mentioned can be brought without the need for any other external program. All you need to do is got to Settings>Video Performance>Effects>Themes
Here you will need to make a hardware detection of your webcam and sync with it.
You will 11 various effects to look at.
