### Post by chewearn on 2009-12-22
I'm not sure about the Mythbuntu part, but sure it's possible to have "dual seats" from a standard Ubuntu.

I have the following set-up on one computer:

1. Where I do work
a) DVI output (primary) connected to a LCD monitor
b) Stereo output connected to desktop speakers

2. Where I watch video / listen to music
a) VGA output (secondary) connected to a LCD TV
b) SPDIF connected to 5.1 surround amplifier

That's ONE video card, and ONE sound card (probably) whereas the poster wants 2 of both.

The "2 sound cards" thing is not an issue: sound servers handle that very well, thank you. just chose to which sound card you want to send the audio stream and you're good to go.

The "2 videos" might be a trickier though. 2 X configurations, possibly 2 X servers ... it would be very much easier with a single card using two outputs.
