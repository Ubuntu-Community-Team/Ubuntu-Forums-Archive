### Post by gandaran on 2009-03-07
totem gstreamer won't play dvd's with menus (only totem-xine can do that), for dvd's it's best you use other players like vlc, mplayer or gxine and don't forget you need libdvdcss2 installed ([medibuntu]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu") repository) for playing commercial encrypted dvd's.
