### Post by benerivo on 2010-01-18
If it is just a backup you want, then i would just copy your music folder straight on to DVD, as a data disc rather than burning an audio disc. As you have 10GB, then you'll  have to copy the first third of your music files (eg. artists A - H) on to a disc and then do the other two thirds on two other discs.

I also use a directory just for burning CD/DVD
I copy the files into that directory to the size of the CD/DVD that I am going to burn to CD/DVD.  That way I can check the size of all the files so that they will fit on a CD/DVD.  On a CD that is 700 MBs, I only copy files up to 680 MB.  You have to have room for the table of contents, some going for a DVD.  If you try to burn 795 MBs you might not how room for the table of contents, and waste a CD/DVD.

good luck and have fun learning
