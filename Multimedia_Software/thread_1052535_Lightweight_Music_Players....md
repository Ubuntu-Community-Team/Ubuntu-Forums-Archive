### Post by cozmicharlie on 2009-01-28
There are a lot of good lightweight players for Linux.  A few that Ilike:

XMMS - one of the best.  It is no longer in Synaptic (xmms2 is in Synaptic- they are not the same) but there are debs available to make installation easy just search the forums).

Audacious - Similar to xmms and it is in synaptic.  I find it crashes more than xmms and it does not support all the formats that xmms does but it is a good basic player.

alsaplayer - a very nice lightweight player

Juk - also a nice lightweight player

This would be my top list but I am sure others will have thier favs.

Enjoy!
