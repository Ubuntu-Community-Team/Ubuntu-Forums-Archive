VLC and Totem both do this. Upon opening any video *file*, not disc, the hue is 180° off center, and has to be manually corrected. Any fixes?

---

### Post by mocha on 2010-01-21
It's because nvidia changed the center point of hue in the newer drivers.  Put the hue slider to the mid point in VLC and totem and save the settings as default.
