### Post by Autodave on 2024-05-22
OK...since no one else is jumping in, let me offer a couple things.  First of all, you must have a bidirectional sound card in that machine.  By that I mean, a lot of sound cards are not capable of recording and playing sound at the same time....they can do one or the other at a time.  If you cannot find the option in Audacity to allow it to play through while recording the sound, then you probably don't have a bidirectional card.  Have you looked into *pavucontrol *yet?  That may allow you to play through.
