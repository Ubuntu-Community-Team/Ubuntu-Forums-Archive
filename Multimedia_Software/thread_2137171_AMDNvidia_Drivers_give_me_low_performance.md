### Post by SifGrey on 2013-04-21
The drivers for graphic cards are indeed different for Windows and Ubuntu. If you are still playing the same exact games with the same settings, it probably is the driver. Otherwise it might be because of the game.  What you could do is to take a look at System Settings > Additional Hardware for possible upgrades.
