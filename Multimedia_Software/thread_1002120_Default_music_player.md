### Post by mc4man on 2008-12-04
Songbird can be made default "music" player as in ipods and such.

It also can load, (and by extension 'play') music stored on data cd's/dvd's, mp3 cd's, ect. 

As long as the location is something like this 'file:///media/cdrom0/'

Otherwise ripping and loading into your songbird library is the only way atm (audio cd access is on the songbird roadmap, nothing imminent though.
