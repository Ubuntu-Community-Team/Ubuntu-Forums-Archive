### Post by donato roque on 2009-11-27
I used to have annoyances like this.  Manipulating the metadata of my music collection.  I have a 40+ GB of music folder and it's a pain to manage without something like Easytag.

It is easy to tag my songs, even assign the album cover with Easytag.  If you find Easytag to be difficult to use, I read that Cowbell is simple to use.  Both are in the repositories. 

It makes sense to me.  Let the media player handle the music or video.  Let another application handle the metadata.
