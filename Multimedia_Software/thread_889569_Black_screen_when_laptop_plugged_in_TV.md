### Post by lambdacore on 2008-08-14
Ah well it's ok, I solved it.
Thanks anyway.

I'll just put it here, in case it happens to someone.

First I went in the Settings>Preferences. Check "Advanced options" in the bottom right corner of the window. 
Then go under Video>Output modules. In the list to the right, select X11 video output.

Then the video playback was ok even when not in fullscreen.

And this made it work when plugged on the TV.
