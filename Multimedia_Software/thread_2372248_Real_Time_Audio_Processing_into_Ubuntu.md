Hi, gang!!!

I discovered a very nice app to Ubuntu, which let us to handle the level and quality of our audio, in real time, from pulseaudio to our speakers system.

The app is called **PulseEffects**.

You can download it from here: [U][B][http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2017/06/install-pulse-effects-ubuntu-ppa](http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2017/06/install-pulse-effects-ubuntu-ppa)
[/B][/U]
It is:
1) A **Monoband Audio Peak Limiter**,
2) A **Monoband Audio RMS or Peak Compressor**, and
3) A **10 Bands Graphic Equalizer**.

Each stage has all the necessary controls to get the exact amount of processing we want.

As an old audio recording studio technician, I really missed this kind of software, which should be into the main Ubuntu Audio Menu Apps, by default.

Please, **I repeat it is a REAL TIME audio processing**. It doesn't change your already saved audio files. If you want to change the sound quality of your audio files, please use **AUDACITY**.

Of course, as any human working, it could be improved. But... I think it is more than enough to the "standard" Ubuntu user.

Thanks a lot to the creator(s) of this wonderful app!!!
