I'm not certain as to your problem.  I have my PC wired into my router, and I have both had my PS3 wired into my router and connected wirelessly to my router.  I have never operated with both PC & PS3 connecting wirelessly to the router.

I recommend setting your PC to a static IP address, instead of auto DHCP; it makes it easier to access the mediatomb database from within the Applications menu; to the best of my knowledge, it's just calling a specific IP address, and if yours is dynamically assigned, its' bound to change sooner or later and therefore won't find it... this, of course, doesn't necessarily affect the broadcasting to the PS3.

After setting up your internet connections for your PS3 and PC to the way you ultimately want them, try (1) turning off your PS3; (2) rebooting your PC, and waiting until Mediatomb fully loads; and then (3) powering on your PS3.
