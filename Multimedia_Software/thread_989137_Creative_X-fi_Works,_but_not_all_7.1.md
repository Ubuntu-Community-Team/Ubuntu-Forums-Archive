### Post by notbitmonk on 2008-12-06
Check the PulseAudio wiki ([http://pulseaudio.org/wiki/FAQ#IhaveasurroundsoundcardbutPulseAudiousesjustthefrontspeakers]("http://pulseaudio.org/wiki/FAQ#IhaveasurroundsoundcardbutPulseAudiousesjustthefrontspeakers")) on how to configure support for 5.1/7.1.  I have a 5.1 setup that couldn't use with PulseAudio for ~6 months because I didn't knew this.  I also had a Soundblaster Audigy 2 ZS (it died three days ago after 4 years of use) and now I'm using the motherboard sound (which is noticeably poorer).  You also have to check that PCM is not muted and you may have to enable some other controls until you find the ones that control your audio.
