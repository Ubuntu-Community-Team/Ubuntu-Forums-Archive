### Post by LakeWind on 2008-04-26
I just installed 8.04LTS and am having a problem disabling autoplay of dvd's. Every time I insert a DVD into the drive, Totem begins playing it. I checked "System - Preferences - Removable Drives & Media" but there is no multimedia tab for disabling autoplay on the CD or DVD drive anymore.

I also checked in the Gnome Configuration Editor "Desktop - Gnome - Volume Manager" but nothing is checked off next to "autoplay DVD". This was quite easy in previous Ubuntu versions. What happened?

How do I disable CD/DVD Drive autoplay in 8.04 LTS?

Thanks

---

### Post by mc4man on 2008-04-26
go to preferences>main menu and on left side click on preferences. On right side enable file management. Then open up file management from system > preferences  and it's under media tab
