### Post by SlappyPappy on 2008-05-19
Well I ran a bunch of tests ripping with K3b and it's just not that good unfortunately, and after all the hassle I went through to get it installed no less. It's pretty slow at ripping and the size varies so much. Kept tweaking but never found a sweet spot.

My best ripping is a combination of DVDFab HD Decrypter to get it to the hard drive and then HandBrakeCLI with some tweaked settings. Looks good and I'm getting every disk this way. I know I could go straight from the disk with HB but it's crazy slow that way. Running from the HD goes much faster and DVDFab is pretty quick in its own right.

But for those who want to try K3b ripping, the link above is your best bet.  :)
