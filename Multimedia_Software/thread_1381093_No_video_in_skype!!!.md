### Post by Fire_Chief on 2010-01-14
Have you verified that your camera is recognized and working in Ubuntu? A quick test is to install Cheese and see if it will display video from your camera.
In 9.10, many USB webcams are not properly initialized if they are plugged in after boot (can be corrected but you have to unload and reload the module). If you boot up with the webcam connected (and if it is supported), then you should be able to use it straight-away.
