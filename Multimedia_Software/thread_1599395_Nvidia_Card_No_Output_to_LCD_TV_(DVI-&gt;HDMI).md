**_Video card 1 _**
VGA -> DELL 1707FP
DVI -> DVI-HDMI cable -> Viore 22" LCD TV

**_Video card 2_**
VGA -> DELL 1707FP
DVI -> DVI-HDMI cable -> Viore 22" LCD TV


nvidia-settings detects the TVs as TV-0 and TV-0 which seems wrong since it's connected to the DVI jack not the S-video jack.

The only resolution option it gives is 1024x768. I have tried modifying my Xorg manually to use a modeline etc. but no matter what I try I can't get the TV to display anything -- just says "No Signal"

The TVs work fine if I use a DVI->VGA adapters and connect to the VGA input on the TV but I'd like to use HDMI not VGA.

Both of the Dell 1707FP show video fine so I don't think this issue is related to having 2 video cards, I couldn't get it to work with just 1 card connected either.
