### Post by bcschmerker on 2010-12-19
As of 18 December 2010, ye'll be better served with the Creative Laboratories® Sound Blaster® Audigy 2 than anything more recent.  I found out last year that [Creative Technology, Ltd.,](http://www.creativetechnology.***/) has been uncooperative with the [Advanced LinUX Sound Architecture Project](http://www.alsa-project.org/) concerning drivers for the CA20K1 (PCI) and CA20K2 (PCI-Express x1) chipsets; to my knowledge, development has taken far longer than I expected to be the case, if it is not still stalled, for this reason.

I actually use the Creative® SB0350 Sound Blaster® Audigy 2® ZS in my own hybrid Everex® (Gigabyte® MA78GM-S2HP mobo, Advance Micro Devices® Athlon 64® X2 5600+ MPU, AMD 780G chipset), with an Audigy 2® I/O Drive for headphone amp/mic-line in/aux-in, and have only had problems to date with the onboard E-mu 10K1 synth and the I/O Drive MIDI; the I/O Drive has a known firmware bug concerning the affected subsystems.
