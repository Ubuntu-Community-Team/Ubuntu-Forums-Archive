### Post by evilsoup on 2013-01-05
IIRC the two main GUI options are RecordMyDesktop and Kazam, both are in the Software Centre.

From the command-line, [you can record your desktop with ffmpeg](https://ffmpeg.org/trac/ffmpeg/wiki/How%20to%20grab%20the%20desktop%20%28screen%29%20with%20FFmpeg). Ask here if you need any clarification on how it works.
