### Post by Idefix82 on 2008-11-14
You can search for Avidemux using the "Search" button on the top. Or you can look for it in Graphics (multiverse). When you find it, right click the white square next to it and click on "Mark for installation", then on the "Apply" button on the top.
You can use the button which looks like a ribbon (or at least is supposed to look like a ribbon) in the lower right corner of the post which you want to thank for.
