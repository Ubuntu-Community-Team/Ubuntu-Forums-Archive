### Post by dschaller on 2010-11-14
This is a bug of longstanding with Brasero. I ran into it about three years ago and I don't think there's a solution yet. I did find that when I tried different optical drives, that could make a difference. I tried swapping my DVD drive with a different one and then it worked. So I think whether or not Brasero works for you is somehow dependent on your hardware. Don't know why that would be. Just my observation.

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/brasero/+bug/157478](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/brasero/+bug/157478)
