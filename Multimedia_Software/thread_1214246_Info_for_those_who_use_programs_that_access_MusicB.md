### Post by justinmiller87 on 2009-07-15
I was trying to rip a CD yesterday and the both Rhythmbox and Sound Juicer continuously crashed after I put in the Bloodhound Gang's CD, Use Your Fingers. I decided to run both from terminal, and pop in the disc again. It turns out that dansguardian was blocking access to Musicbrainz and causing a crash whenever it tried to get the information about the CD but was blocked. Adding musicbrainz.org to the exceptionsitelist corrected the problem.
