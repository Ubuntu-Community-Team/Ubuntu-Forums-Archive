### Post by mcduck on 2008-07-28
Not really. There's no way any program (or even hardware tool) would be able to recognize which parts of the mixed sound belong to which instrument.

Depending on the original sound file, you might get acceptable results with graphical or parametric equalizer. But they only work with sound frequencies, no matter what you do you still won't be able to get a  perfect result as most of the times different instruments still produce sounds with same frequencies as the other instruments.

### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-07-28
On some music the vocals are in the center channel (same phase and volume on the left and right channels).  It may be possible to use audacity to filter out frequencies and / or channels / differences this way.

Depending on the music, it may be easy to do / very hard / impossible to do.
