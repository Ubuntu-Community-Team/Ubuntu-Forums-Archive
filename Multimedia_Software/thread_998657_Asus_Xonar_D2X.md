### Post by Emily_T on 2008-12-13
Well I installed Ubuntu 8.10 on two systems now (though no expert) and had trouble getting my xfi to work. I ended up swapping it out and replacing it with an Audigy I had around and that worked just fine. On my second pc I have the Xonar D2X and had no problem at all getting it to work. I worked right out of the box. Though the digital out-put doesn't work the analog 7.1 surround sound works great. You will have no trouble at all with the D2X. Don't buy that creative junk, the D2X is a really great card.
