### Post by mickee384 on 2013-11-11
Today I had this low frequency humming (at high volume with Clementine open but stopped, sounded like a jet engine. Turns out that somehow I managed to get the Clementine audio test wave on. Couldn't see that. Downloaded the Pulse Audio volume control, here I could see 2 entries fro Clementine, I disable the audio test and its fixed. Interesting to note, asll other sound devices had no background noise, just Clementine.Hope this helps someone. Cheers!
