### Post by lykeion on 2010-10-22
Well, it's kind of hard to tell what you're asking here but I'll try anyway.
* Audio CDs are limited to 16-bit 44.1 kHz audio
* DVDs on the other hand may handle 24-bit audio
* The difference of 24-bit and 16-bit is hardly noticable by the ear (if you don't listen on a very high-end hifi), so if you aren't producing audio professionally it doesn't matter

A link explaining the technicalities of bitdepth:
