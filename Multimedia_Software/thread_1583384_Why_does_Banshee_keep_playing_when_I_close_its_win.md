### Post by perrti-y02 on 2010-09-28
This is normal. The idea behind it (as I understand it) is that you can close it and you get a little icon in the notification area (the bit on the panel where there are icons such as the network connections) rather than the entire player. This frees up more space on the desktop without minimizing it.

If you don't want it to do this I think you can change the behavior in the preferences box in Banshee.

Hope that helps.
