### Post by papibe on 2012-03-19
AFAIK, 10bit video playing was just recently added to VLC (version 2.0). That version will be available on Precise (actually, it is available now on the beta releases).

I know of at least of one ppa for installing VLC 2.0 (ppa:n-muench/vlc), but since I haven't use it, I can't really recommend it.
