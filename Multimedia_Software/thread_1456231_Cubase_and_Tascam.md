### Post by cchhrriiss121212 on 2010-04-17
Audio on linux uses the jackd sound server for all the production programs, allowing them to be linked together easily in a modular style.
Ardour + jackd is the best replacement for cubase. Tascam is a soundcard manufacturer, and m-audio have a good line of supported sound cards. Check if your tascam card is supported before replacing it here:
[http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Vendor-Tascam](http://www.alsa-project.org/main/index.php/Matrix:Vendor-Tascam)
