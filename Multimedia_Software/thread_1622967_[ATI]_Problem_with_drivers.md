### Post by KaYnemO on 2010-11-16
I have ATI Radeon Mobility x1600 video card. ATI stopped supporting our cards sometime ago, thus fglrx is not going to work for us - will not support 3D and, therefore, effects. You have to go with the ubuntu supplied xserver-xorg-video-radeon drivers. It is a bit glitchy, but supports 3d, compiz and games... Hope this helps!
