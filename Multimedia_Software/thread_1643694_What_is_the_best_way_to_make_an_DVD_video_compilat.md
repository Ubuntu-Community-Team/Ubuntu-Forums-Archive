### Post by cotcot on 2010-12-12
Check out the video editors for linux : kdenlive; openshot; lives; pitivi; blender (VSE); cinelerra. Forgive me if I forget one. 
Blender and cinelerra are advanced editors, which means more possibilities and more time to learn. 
What you want can be done with most editors I mentioned. It is better that you check the editors (look and feel) and select one that suits you best rather than getting an advice of 'the best' editor.
