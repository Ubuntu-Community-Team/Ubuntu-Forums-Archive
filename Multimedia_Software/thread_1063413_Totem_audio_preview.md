### Post by mc4man on 2009-02-07
Go to Places -> home folder. Look up in the upper panel and click on Edit -> Preferences. In the pop up click on 'preview', make change in 'sound files' drop down. (never)

Or just run this in a terminal (copy, paste, press enter

```
nautilus-file-management-properties
```
