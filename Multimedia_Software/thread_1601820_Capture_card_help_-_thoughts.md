### Post by P4man on 2010-10-21
Read up a bit on MythTV. YOu may be confusing mythbuntu with Mythtv and not quite understand the difference between frontend and backend.

MythTV is an app, consists of 2 parts mainly; a backend, which is like the server, which does the actual recordings, it runs silently in the background, and a frontend which can be on the same and/or any other computer, which you interact with to see and schedule your tv/recordings etc. Frontend is just an app like any other, it doesnt have to autostart (though it does on mythbuntu), it runs independently of the backend. Quitting the frontend doesnt stop the backend. You can have multiple frontends on different PCs connecting to the same backend. You can use different frontends, like xbmc or others. 

Its not an easy app to setup or understand, but its brilliant once you do.
