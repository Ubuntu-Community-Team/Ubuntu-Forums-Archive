### Post by mkokotovich on 2008-09-17
This is a long-standing bug in the Adobe Flash plugin for linux.  I've written them, and they don't seem to care too much about linux users... or at least not enough to fix the bug.  So basically, you will have to live with it.  One workaround is a FlashBlock or other ad-blocking extension for Firefox.  That way, when some piece of flash is in the way of a menu you need, you can block it and use the menu.
