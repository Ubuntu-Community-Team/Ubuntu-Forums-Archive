### Post by cotcot on 2009-09-28
My best guess is that the left guy was recorded with greenscreening (or blue) and put upon the video with the right guy. (look at the contour of the left guy on the chair : it is fairly sharp compared to his alter ego right).
It is possible with blender and cinelerra. [[COLOR="Red"]Here[/COLOR]]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3U54EatpSLI") is a tutorial for blender and [[COLOR="Red"]here[/COLOR]]("http://www.kdenlive.org/forum/green-screen") is a thread on the kdenlive forum about greenscreening.
