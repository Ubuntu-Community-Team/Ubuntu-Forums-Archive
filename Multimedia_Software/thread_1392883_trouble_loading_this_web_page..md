### Post by earthman on 2010-01-31
The problem here is not with your computer or operating system, but with a silly web designer who doesn't seem to understand that websites should be platform neutral, and requiring some kind of odd codec or player is poor practice. When I see a web site that wants me to download some player I never heard of I go elsewhere, it's their problem, not mine. This is a prime method of delivering viruses and malware, and it's hard to believe they would use this method to present something. I tend to get alarmed when I click a link and some program wants to download and install. This site is never going to work for you because it's asking you to download a and install a Windows executable.
