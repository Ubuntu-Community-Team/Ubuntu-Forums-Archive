### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-03-09
The BBC no longer streams programmes including World Service, in the Windows media-player format as it did until about a month ago with url  [http://www.bbc.co.uk/worldservice/meta/tx/nb/live_infent_au_nb.asx](http://www.bbc.co.uk/worldservice/meta/tx/nb/live_infent_au_nb.asx) so you will now have to find another stream to do what you want in radiotray.

have a look at [http://www.radiofeeds.co.uk/mp3.asp](http://www.radiofeeds.co.uk/mp3.asp) which shows you alternative streams which I have just tried in radiotray and using the stream url [http://bbcwssc.ic.llnwd.net/stream/bbcwssc_mp1_ws-eieuk](http://bbcwssc.ic.llnwd.net/stream/bbcwssc_mp1_ws-eieuk) which I simply copied from the saved mp3 shoutcast playlist it seems to work fine.
