### Post by yuanfangcan on 2011-06-26
This is just my experience, which may not help you. 
I have a Iphone 3GS. 
When I had ubuntu10.4, after I plug in my iphone, both rhythmbox and banshee shows nothing.   
When I updated to Ubuntu 10.10, after I plug in my iphone, a error message pop up, said "can not mount" something. 
Then I updated to Ubuntu 11.4, iphone is mounted immediately. I can sync using both rhythmbox and banshee. But I can not see the song in my phone sync-ed by banshee.  Fortunately Rhythmbox works perfectly for me. 

So  Ubuntu 11.04 + Rhythmbox worked for my Iphone 3GS
