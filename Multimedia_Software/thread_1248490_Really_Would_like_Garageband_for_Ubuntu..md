### Post by joey-elijah on 2009-08-24
Jokosher is a semi-garageband-a-like, but it doesn't (currently) use virtual instruments, but still useful for podcasting and the like.

LMMS is a free clone of fruityloops/flstudio and comes with a whole host of virtual instruments, effects, etc. And yes, you can use your keyboard to play them.

Audacity has a lot of fans, and it is useful, but doesn't have that 'user friendly' garageband interface. 

There are loads more than those i mentioned. If you don't like one, move on to another. 

I do think that, at some point, linux "user friendly" audio production apps will be developed, just like easy to use "imovie" like video editors currently.
