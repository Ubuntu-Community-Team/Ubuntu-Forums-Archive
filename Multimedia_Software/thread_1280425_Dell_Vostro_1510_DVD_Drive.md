### Post by BQAggie2006 on 2009-10-03
> idk but why would it read cd's and not dvds? its also able to burn.

If I'm not mistaken, on a combo drive, the read/write head has different lasers for each function. Therefore, it's possible for one laser to go out while the others remain functional. So it's possible that you could have a hardware problem. As I suggested, try using a Live DVD to boot from. If it can't boot from a DVD independent of the OS, then it's more than likely a hardware problem.
