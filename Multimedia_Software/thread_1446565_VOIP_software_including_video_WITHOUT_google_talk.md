### Post by huwnet on 2010-04-04
> 
But I am using them with a gmail account - which I personally dislike.



Are there alternative XMPP accounts or VOIP-clients?

There are plenty of public XMPP servers. [Here](http://www.jabberes.org/servers/) is a partial list. Alternatively if you have a VPS or dedicated server, you can run your own. There are also other [clients](http://xmpp.org/software/clients.shtml) you can use although so far not many of them support video.

Alternatively there are some SIP (same protocol as Ekiga) clients that support video although I haven't used SIP with video myself. Wikipedia has a [list](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_SIP_software)
