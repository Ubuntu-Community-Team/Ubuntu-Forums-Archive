I've tried my UM-740 under Ubuntu but I couldn't get it to work at all. The drivers provided are for Windows only because it uses some technolgy called DisplayLink which I believe is totally software-driven.

I called [www.carcomputer.co.uk]("http://www.carcomputer.co.uk"), the people who I bought it off, and the guy on the phone was very helpful but said that the only drivers he was aware of were the Windows ones (though Mac drivers are available there is no support for webcam/touchscreen).

It's a very, very cool monitor but it's a shame the drivers are limited to Windows at the moment.
