### Post by kdiddy on 2011-12-28
I am using a 22 samsung 1080p HDTV/Monitor. When connected with HDMI the fonts on ubuntu 10.04 and previous have always been blurry and hard on the eyes. I tried every setting and font  I could think of , I finally resolved the issue by following these steps on the monitor/tv. It is basically renaming the HDMI input to pc and it works now 

Press the "Source" button.
Highlight the HDMI input.
Press "Tools" -> Edit Name -> And then simply using the scrolling (up & down keys on ur remote), select "PC" or "DVI PC" for your HDMI.
