### Post by lswb on 2009-11-08
Are you creating the CD as an audio or music cd, or as an ISO? If the latter, there will be filesystem information and other overhead not found on a music only cd, so the total playtime would be somewhat less. Check the settings of your burning program and make sure it is creating a music CD and not some type of data CD that happens to contain music.
