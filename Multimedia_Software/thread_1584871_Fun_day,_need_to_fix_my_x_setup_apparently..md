### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2010-09-29
Good luck with that. SiS chips have terrible linux support (no 3D acceleration). See this for help: [http://ncc-1701a.homelinux.net/~linux-sis/](http://ncc-1701a.homelinux.net/~linux-sis/)
You're better off finding a cheap AGP Geforce4 or 5series, or maybe even AGP Radeon X100 or X1000 series.
