### Post by BiZARRe_T on 2008-11-22
This is how we see a video with **Greek subtitles in vlc**

- We open VLC |  File -> Open File -> in Open: we browse for our video and click open.

- Next we check the box "Use a subtitles file" in File: we browse for our subtitles file and click open.

- Then we go to "Advanced Setting..." button and in the "subtitles text encoding" we choose "ISO-8859-7"

Enjoy your movie with greek subtitles.
