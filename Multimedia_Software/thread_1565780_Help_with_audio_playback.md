### Post by fallenshadow on 2010-09-01
It seems you are trying to use a CD with Sound Juicer. Sound Juicer is for copying music from CD's to your PC.

However looking at the error message it appears not to be installed. Go to Ubuntu Software Center.... search and install "Sound Juicer".

Or try running your CD with something else like Rhythmbox.
