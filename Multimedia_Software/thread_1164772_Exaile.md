### Post by jimbo99 on 2009-05-20
I've been a fan of Amarok for some time.  But when 2.x came out I looked for a way to keep Amarok 1.x installed.  I chose to use Kubuntu because the interface is superior to gnome in so many ways it's not even pretty to think of them.

The other day I noticed a program called Exaile in someone's screen shot, so I downloaded it. I then scanned my library and set up my last.fm account in it.  As I began to use it I found it satisfied my needs as much as Amarok 1.x did and in some ways more.

I'm pretty critical of software, and I'm extremely critical of those programmers that program for themselves. These guys at Exaile seem to have done a wonderful job and getting the right balance of usefulness and features.  I would say that I would have no problem recommending it to others.

I especially love the "Dynamic" feature which finds songs in your library using the Last.fm interface.  It's neat watching it find the songs that are similar.
