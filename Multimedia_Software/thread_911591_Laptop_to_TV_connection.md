### Post by tamoneya on 2008-09-05
i would be very cautious about a usb to hdmi cable.  There have started to be external graphics cards on the market but I cant attest to their linux compatibility.  Can you provide a source.  Also the usb connection to the cable box does not provide any video.  It is typically some sort of debugging interface or if even possible a interface for an external drive.

I personally recommend either VGA or svideo.  Then use a 3.5 mm cable to the back of the tv for sound.  If you dont have an audio jack on the back that will work you can get a 3.5mm to RCA y adapter so it works with the red/white audio input jacks.
