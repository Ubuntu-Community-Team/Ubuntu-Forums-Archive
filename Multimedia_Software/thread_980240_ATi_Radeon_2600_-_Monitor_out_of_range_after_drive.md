### Post by rasheek on 2008-12-10
I'm going to bet your monitor sucks and does not give out EDID information. has happened to me with a few of them. So, try to change this:

Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"Configured Monitor"
EndSection



to This:


Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"Configured Monitor"
        HorizSync   60-90.0
        VertRefresh 30-70.0
EndSection
