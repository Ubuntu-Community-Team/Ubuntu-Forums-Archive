### Post by langelgjm on 2009-01-25
Hi,

You are really asking two separate questions.

1. Yes, you can have three or four displays with two cards, at least in Windows. I currently run a three display setup in Windows, using an ATI Radeon X800 and X300. I occasionally connect a fourth monitor. The desktop spans all three or four without issue.

2. You will probably not be able to span accelerated video across more than than two monitors. There are issues with doing this even with two monitors, but it is possible. However, doing it across three or four monitors (and thus two separate video cards) is probably not possible. But I could be wrong.
