### Post by fabricator4 on 2011-01-06
Hi,

click on "Applications" at the tops left of the menu bar.
On the drop down click on "Ubuntu Software"

Once the package manager (aka Ubuntu Software Center) opens you can click (over on the left frame) on "get software" to find packages to install, or on "installed software" to show packages for deletion.

You can also change where Ubuntu will look for software.  Under the system menu, click on "Administration" and then "Software Sources".
