### Post by robata on 2009-12-14
I had the same problem. I was able to disable the hardware accelerator by going to the macromedia web site, letting a flash video play on that site and then right clicking in that video to get the settings panel, then I had the ability to uncheck the hardware accelerator box. This change then seemed to stay on any other flash video that I played after this.
