### Post by kiisu on 2008-12-12
There's a few ways to do this.

Easiest for me, is to let the video load fully and then search your computer for the 'tmp' (temporary) folder. In there you'll find a file called 'Flashxxxxxxxx" where the x's are a random stream of characters.

Right-click, copy, then paste the file into wherever you want to store it. Make sure to rename it then ... I got lazy with that and after they piled up I couldn't tell them apart!

Good luck.
