### Post by Alucard-sama on 2008-11-30
For some reason the version of sound juicer I have rips Vorbis with the .oga extension rather than .ogg and this confuses pretty much all media players except mplayer.
How can I get Sound Juicer to rip me good ol' .ogg instead without downgrading?

---

### Post by tmetzcc325 on 2008-11-30
What you need to do is edit the profile that says something along the lines of CD-Lossy.  After you dig down into the preferences, you will find a field that allows you to specify the file extension.  Change this to ogg, and you should be all set.
