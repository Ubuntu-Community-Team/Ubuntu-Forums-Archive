### Post by vanadium on 2011-05-08
Try setting the Sync to vblanc option off. This fixed the problem for me with 720 p video. My graphics card is not powerfull to play full HD, though.

The setting is in CompizConfig Settings manager, which is not installed by default. Type "opengl" in filter. The setting is in the opengl plugin.

In previous versions, this setting was under "General".
