For some time I've been looking into what hardware I can use for recording music on Linux. There are lots of options, but not all are supported. It can be frustrating trying to find out what works as the information is spread over lots of sites and a lot of it is out of date. I know about the Alsa matrix, but that's not aimed at musicians.

I hang around on the Linux Musicians forum, so I've added a section to the wiki there

[http://wiki.linuxmusicians.com/doku.php?id=hardware_matrix](http://wiki.linuxmusicians.com/doku.php?id=hardware_matrix)

I've added a few items that I have experience of. Further contributions are welcome. If you prefer you can post the details here and I will add them to the wiki.
