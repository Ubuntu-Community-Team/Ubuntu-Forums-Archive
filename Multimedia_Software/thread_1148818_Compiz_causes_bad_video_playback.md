### Post by jack0rlando on 2009-05-04
I'm not sure if this is just me but when I disabled all desktop effects the full screen in flash started running a lot smoother, vlc loaded more quickly and looked less blocky and I had the problem where nexus kept loosing focus and I had to minimize and maximise to get control back . Iam pretty sure compiz was the cause of all this. I'd like to see other people try it and tell me if it works for them to.

To disable go to System>preferences>Appearance 
click on the visual effects tab and select none
