### Post by jordon on 2008-04-25
Since the vocal is in the "center" of the stereo picture (in both the left and right channels at once), the following technique will work.

Open the file in Audacity. Click the name of the track, and in the drop-down menu that appears, select "Split Stereo Track." Now you'll have two tracks. Select one of them (doesn't matter which) and go to Effect -> Invert. Then click the name of the left (first) channel and go to "Make Stereo Track." Finally, go to Tracks -> Stereo to Mono and then save.
