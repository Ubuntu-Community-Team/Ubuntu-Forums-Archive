### Post by wannadumpwindows on 2008-04-29
There's an alternative to Limewire, called Frostwire. It's still built from their open source code, and it runs great. I used to use it all the time.

[http://www.frostwire.com/?id=downloads](http://www.frostwire.com/?id=downloads)

Click the Ubuntu/Debian link and it'll download the .deb file for you. Then you can just double click on it to install. 

As for your mp3 player, I have no advice for you there. Sorry. But I'm sure there are all kinds of other people here that can help you out with that. Just be patient. :)
