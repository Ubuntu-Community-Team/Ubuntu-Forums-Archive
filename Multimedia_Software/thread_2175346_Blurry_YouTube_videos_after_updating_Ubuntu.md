### Post by tkabir11 on 2013-09-18
Alright. I've seemed to somehow solve this problem on my own. I was noticing that YouTube was automatically choosing 144p video quality and hence making the videos to appear unbearingly blurry. So then I just went into YouTube settings>playback and selecti[FONT=arial black][/FONT]ng ''I have a slow connection. Never play higher-quality video'' and boom- now the videos are playing at 360p clear watchable quality!!
