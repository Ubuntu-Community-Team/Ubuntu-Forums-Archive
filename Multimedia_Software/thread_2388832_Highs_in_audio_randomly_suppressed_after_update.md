### Post by accounts0 on 2018-04-07
On Ubuntu 16.04.3 with kubuntu package installed, everything is as up to date as it can be. Am playing music through Amarok.

A week or so ago, maybe- don't recall exactly since I didn't realize the scope of the problem until tonight. The highs, or treble, is lower than it once was. I thought maybe it was something where I just needed to adjust my EQ & be done with it. However while doing so I've discovered more details about the issue & am now convinced just redoing the EQ isn't the right way to resolve this.

What happens is if I raise the volume via the sound icon in the system tray, sometimes the treble comes back & is way too high from having tweaked the EQ- but only for a second or so then its back to muffled. Also it doesn't happen every time I raise the volume, seems random.

I don't see any settings either in Amarok or in KDE System Settings that might cause anything like this. Not sure how to proceed.

EDIT:
Was 3.5mm cable in back of sub with a bad connection. Reseated & all is well.
