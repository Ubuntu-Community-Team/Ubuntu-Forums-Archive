### Post by PewPew Lazer on 2008-05-27
Whenever I have more than one application that plays sound, whether it currently is or not, all sounds stop.
I can't play any music/sounds until I close all of the applications, this includes flash videos/banners, and reopen only one of them so I can play it.
It wouldn't be such a pain if I could always figure out which programs use sounds. Sometimes they are hiding/sleeping but it's still blocking sounds.

What to do?

### Post by jarelkamar on 2008-05-27
Actually I had the exact same problem and it's solution turned out to be so simple . 


System/preferences/sound preferences 
on devices tab just set all options to ALSA specially first two : sound events& music and movies

should work fine then

### Post by PewPew Lazer on 2008-05-28

Works perfectly now.
