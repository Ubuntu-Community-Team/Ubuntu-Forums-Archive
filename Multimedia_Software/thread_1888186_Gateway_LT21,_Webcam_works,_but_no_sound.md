### Post by callmebruce on 2011-12-03
For anyone else searching Cheese and Gateway LT21 - I have a Sony stereo mic I picked up for my Kodak ZI10. For kicks and grins, I plugged the mic into the sound port on my netbook, did a few Cheese sessions - and it worked just fine. Video was slow at 640x480 (well, this is a small little Netbook) - but video response was kinda sorta okay at 320x240.

So if you want to do a video in Cheese but don't want to mess with sound issues, just pop a mic into your sound port. 

For me - I could do a talking head session with the Kodak Zi10 on a mini-tripod, or use Cheese, the webcam and the mic for a talking head session. I don't use Skype, but don't see why the webcam and external mic wouldn't work.
