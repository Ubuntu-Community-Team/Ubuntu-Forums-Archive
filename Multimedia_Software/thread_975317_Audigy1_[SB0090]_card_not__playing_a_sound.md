### Post by mc4man on 2008-11-09
You can try this - it's what I needed to do with an audigy2

Click on speaker icon in bottom panel, then click on 'mixer'.
When it opens -> Settings -> Configure Channels. Scroll down and enable 'Audigy Analog/Digital Jack'. Click ok and then in the mixer check the box for it under 'Mute' (or uncheck if checked
