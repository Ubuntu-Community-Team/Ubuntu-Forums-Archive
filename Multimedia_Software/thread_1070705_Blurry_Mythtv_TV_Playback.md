Just got all my mythtv ubuntu setup working as i wanted until i discovered a problem!

### Post by kempy1000 on 2009-02-15
Problem fixed!

Thanks to this thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=723345](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=723345)

Settings -> TV Settings -> Playback  -> third page (named Playback profiles)

Changed to normal from CPU+

Works fine now 

Thanks
