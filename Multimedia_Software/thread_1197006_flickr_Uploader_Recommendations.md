### Post by Bealer on 2009-06-27
Well for me f-spot would be the ideal, but it doesn't work. 

I've been commenting (as Rob) on:
[http://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=538482](http://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=538482)

So for now I use JUploadr. It's the best of the bunch out there. There's another one, called uploadr I think, it's in the repo's. I didn't like it, couldn't create new sets when uploading.

Downsides of JUploadr:
- It does seem to crash/freeze when trying to upload a huge amount of photos (100+).
- It's not a photo management suite like f-spot, whereby I can edit and version my photos before putting them on flickr.
