### Post by johnwesleyhardin1 on 2019-01-18
I wanted to put this out there because I had the issue in KdenLive where title clips had a black background which was completely overlaying my video, and Affine nor Composite would fix the issue.  I searched all over the internet and found lots of people asking how to solve it, but none of the solutions worked for me.  Finally, after playing around a lot I found the "Templates" at the bottom of the title clip window.  Those templates all have an invisible background (or no background).  That's how I solved the issue and I hope it helps someone.
