### Post by helios16v on 2010-10-29
Fixed it..

Found out my CPUs were turned down to 1.60GHz making the media run really slow and glitchy.

To fix this all I did was Right clicked on my **Panel** at the top and selected ***"Add to Panel..."*** I then added ***"CPU Frequency Scaling Monitor"*** to the panel. I clicked on the icon that appears on the panel and I can select different speeds to run both of my CPU's at.

I just clicked ***"Ondemand"*** and I haven't had a problem with it since plus its still energy efficient cause it goes from 2.30GHz back down to 1.60GHz automatically when I'm not using that much :P
