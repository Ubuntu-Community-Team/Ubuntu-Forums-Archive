### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-07-20
Get [FLASH-AID](http://flash-aid-extension.blogspot.com) for Firefox. It will remove any conflicting plugins that might be interfering with flash from Adobe.

Make sure you have the latest video driver installed. Go to "System >> Administration >> Hardware drivers".

Also check these:

[Flash Issues & Solutions](http://firefox-tutorials.blogspot.com/2010/06/flash-issues-solutions.html)
[Flash Optimization](http://firefox-tutorials.blogspot.com/2010/05/flash-optimization.html)

Just to let you know, if you are worried about security, the tutorials and the extension are written by me.
