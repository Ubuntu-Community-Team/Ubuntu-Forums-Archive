### Post by Nathan Dial on 2009-06-20
I actually found this thread looking for solutions of my own, but I have some ideas for you:

First, have you considered using the projector as a "second monitor" from your desktop?  I haven't found a way to do it without logging out / in, or rebooting, but you can set up a second monitor with a different resolution.  If you have an extra CRT monitor hanging around, you can go ahead and test the settings -- actually that was my other idea, most techies I know have an old CRT sitting in the corner of their garage or something, and if you do you can probably use it to test your projector setup.

Of course, it's been 2 weeks, so you've probably already moved on.  In any case, good luck!:D
