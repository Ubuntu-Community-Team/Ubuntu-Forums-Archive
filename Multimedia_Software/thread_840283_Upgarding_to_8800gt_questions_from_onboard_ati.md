### Post by timcredible on 2008-06-25
i've had no issues using the drivers from the repos, so i wouldn't recommend you go through the effort of getting them to work from the nvidia website.  however, i think you're going to want to disable the ati drivers before installing the nivdia card (just go to System-> Administration -> Hardware Drivers and uncheck the ati driver).  if it doesn't seem to work, then you might consider installing your new card and re-installing the system and making sure you only enable the nvidia driver.
