### Post by seancyril on 2012-03-08
Hi there,
My jpeg files in nautilus are just displaying as a blank black box instead of a thumbnail of the actual image. This makes it really difficult to find a particular picture in a folder if it contains loads of photos and I have to individually click my way through them all in order to find the pic that I want.
However after exiting the folder and then re-opening it the pics that I had opened now display in nautilus as thumbnails.
Can anyone advise me how I can get my thumbnails to display straight away instead of having to click my way through them as it would be much more convenient

I am using Ubuntu 10.10 
Thanks
Sean

---

### Post by gordintoronto on 2012-03-08
In Nautilus, select Edit/Preferences. Click on the Preview tab, change the "Only for files smaller than" value.
