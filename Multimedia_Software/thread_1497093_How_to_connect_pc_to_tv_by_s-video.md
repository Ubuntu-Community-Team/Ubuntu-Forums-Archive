### Post by Paddy Landau on 2010-05-30
I'm not sure exactly what help you need.

Assuming that your computer has the right connection, you need to buy the correct cable and connect it to your TV.

Then set your TV to accept signals from that cable. Some TV's will do this automatically, and others need some kind of setting. Refer to your TV's manual.

If your computer doesn't automatically recognise the TV, there are a couple of things you can try. The easiest is to reboot. Otherwise, go to System > Preferences > Monitors and follow the simple instructions there.

If your computer still doesn't recognise the TV, then post back telling us exactly what happened, and hope that someone with more knowledge than I can help.
