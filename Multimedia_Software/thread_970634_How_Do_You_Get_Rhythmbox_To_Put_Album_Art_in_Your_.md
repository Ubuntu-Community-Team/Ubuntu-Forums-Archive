### Post by punong_bisyonaryo on 2008-11-04
Ok, I think I've found a solution to my own problem! Not a very good one, but a solution nonetheless. Remember how when you play a song in Rhythmbox, it fetches album art from the net? It seems that the only album art that gets loaded is the current song playing or have played, even if all the songs are from the same album.

So, you either play a song, upload it to your iPod, or load the songs into your iPod and play all songs one by one. You don't have to play it through, just enough till the album art is downloaded of the net.

Now, can anyone suggest a better fix for this and where I can report this as a bug?
