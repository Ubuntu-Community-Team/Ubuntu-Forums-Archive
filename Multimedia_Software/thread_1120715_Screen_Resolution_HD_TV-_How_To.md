### Post by freetolio on 2010-06-14
Are you using the restricted drivers?  if you were, you would be using the Catalyst Control center to configure your display options rather than the standard gnome display config.

I have a low-grade ATI card with Ubuntu 10.04 and it runs 1080p out of the box with the restricted drivers enabled.  Resolution caps are often encountered when the drivers used aren't capable of fully utilizing the hardware video acceleration.
