### Post by kostkon on 2014-09-21
> **curtis6 said:**
> I found a real-time voice changer, but it output to my headphones. I want to to send audio to skype or teamspeak
To do that:
First, install the Pulseaudio Volume Control utility (pavucontrol). Then, start Skype (or Teamspeak) and make a call or whatever. Open pavucontrol, click on the Recording tab and you should see Skype/Teamspeak listed there. Click on the device drop-down menu and select the Monitor of your sound card. After that, every sound being played on your desktop will also be heard on Skype/Teamspeak, including your voice changer.

Hope that helps.
