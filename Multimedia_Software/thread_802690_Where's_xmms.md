### Post by DirtDawg on 2008-05-21
For whatever reason, XMMS was removed from the repositories. I would recommend using Audacious, which is in the repos and is, I believe, a maintained branch of XMMS.

If you find Audacious does not suit your needs, there's is a thread here with links to XMMS deb packages for Hardy: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=774461](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=774461)

XMMS2 is nothing like the original and I would recommend you give Audacious a spin first.
