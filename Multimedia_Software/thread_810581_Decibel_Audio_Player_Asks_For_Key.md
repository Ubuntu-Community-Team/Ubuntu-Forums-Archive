### Post by Athropos on 2008-09-17
If it's not too late: When you activate the Last.fm module, DAP needs your login/password to submit track information. If you choose to save this information, it will use the gnome-keyring if it is installed. I guess DAP now asks you to unlock the keyring to retrieve its information. If you don't want to submit information to Last.fm, just disable the corresponding module.
