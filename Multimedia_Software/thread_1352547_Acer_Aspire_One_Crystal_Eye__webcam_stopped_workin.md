Hya everybody,

Just wanted to drop a solution for the Acer webcam.
My webcam disappeared a couple of times while tweaking Ubuntu (and a couple of other distro's). It didn't show up when I did

```
lsusb
```

or

```
dmesg | grep Crystal
```

I found that shutting down the netbook and removing the battery for a couple of minutes brings back the camera.

Hope I helped some of ya!!
