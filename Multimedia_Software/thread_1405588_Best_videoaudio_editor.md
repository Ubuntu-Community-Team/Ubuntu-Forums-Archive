### Post by Melcar on 2010-02-12
For video I like kdenlive.  Avidemux is a close second.  For audio Audacity.  Beware that they sometimes have issues with Pulseaudio; Audacity not no much anymore, but kdenlive simply doesn't work with it (PA can still be installed, just tell kdenlive to use alsa instead, but then recording audio doesn't work :mad:), and audio in avidemux starts to skip.
