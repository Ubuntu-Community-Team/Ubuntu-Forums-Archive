### Post by MrSnower on 2011-10-26
Hello,
  I ran into the same issues (Ubuntu on a T43 w/ integrated graphics card) when attempting to utilize the s-video out port.  I eventually gave up due to other reasons.  But, if I were still trying to make that work, I might start by looking at available drivers from the manufacturer's website.  In my case, I had a Mobile Intel(R) 910GML (often pared with the 915GM/GMS) and Intel does indeed have a Linux driver release.

[http://downloadcenter.intel.com/Detail_Desc.aspx?agr=Y&DwnldID=8203&lang=eng&OSVersion=Linux*&DownloadType=Drivers](http://downloadcenter.intel.com/Detail_Desc.aspx?agr=Y&DwnldID=8203&lang=eng&OSVersion=Linux*&DownloadType=Drivers)

I might try this again at some point, so please let me know if this works for you!
