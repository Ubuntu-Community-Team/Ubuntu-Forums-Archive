### Post by sdowney717 on 2009-12-06
possibly vlc
i open a video capture card like this 
vlc /dev/video0

you can then select advanced controls and you get a red record button. 
it records mpeg2 video with sound.

I have not tried with a camcorder device.
also mplayer could capture it, read this thread
