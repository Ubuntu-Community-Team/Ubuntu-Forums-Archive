### Post by leafpaul on 2008-11-04
Without error messages it is difficult but I had similar symptoms when the firmware for the tv card was missing.  Check /lib/firmware for the file "dvb-usb-dib0700-1.10.fw" or "dvb-usb-dib0700-1.20.fw"

Follow the instructions on here for installing on 8.04 [http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_WinTV_Nova-T_500_PCI](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_WinTV_Nova-T_500_PCI)
