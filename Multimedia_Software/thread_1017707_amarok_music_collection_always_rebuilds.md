### Post by logos34 on 2008-12-21
Settings>COnfigure amarok>collection>uncheck "Watch folders for changes".  Also, in the tree directory above only check the folders with music/audio that you want it to keep track of.  After adding music do ctrl+u to udate collection (NOT "rescan"!)

hope that helps
