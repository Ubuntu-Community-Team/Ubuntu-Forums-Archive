### Post by little_penguin on 2009-01-11
The Hauppauge WinTV Nova-T Lite (DVB-T) works fine for me in Hamburg, and was pretty cheap at around 30 euros.

I'm using it with 8.04 Hardy Heron; I set it up about 6 months back and had to install the latest v4l-dvb drivers and the firmware file. But it is now said to work "out-of-the-box" with 8.10 Intrepid. In case you speak German (I notice that you live in Berlin), here's the corresponding German Ubuntu wiki article:

[http://wiki.ubuntuusers.de/Hauppauge_WinTV_Nova-T_Stick]("http://wiki.ubuntuusers.de/Hauppauge_WinTV_Nova-T_Stick")

Good luck!
