### Post by hairy one on 2010-04-22
I have DVD Decrypter and shrink installed with wine, no issues. Make sure that in dvd decrypter that you go to settings and set I/O to ASPI-WNASPI32.DLL (the top 1) thats it.

Use shrink to shrink it to an ISO and set decrypter to burn it. I have never had a hassle with this system except bad discs.

I do this for my home made movies. I have found no easier or quicker way to do the job.
