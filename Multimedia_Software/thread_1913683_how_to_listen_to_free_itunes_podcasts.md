### Post by cfhowlett on 2012-01-24
iTunes entries typically list the source website - a blog or something.  Go there and search for the rss or xml feed.  Copy/paste that into a New Podcast in Rhythmbox.

### Post by cfhowlett on 2012-01-29
I went to the CNN News Update page on iTunes Podcast.  Clicked on "Podcast Website".  Clicked on "Subscribe" and it opened a firefox browser tab.  Copied the address [http://rss.cnn.com/services/podcasting/newscast/rss.xml](http://rss.cnn.com/services/podcasting/newscast/rss.xml).  Opened Rhythmbox, clicked "New Podcast Feed".  Pasted the URL into the subscription box and had a new item display immediately on my list of podcasts.
