### Post by Flynn555 on 2008-10-07
ok i got amarok to work with my ipod photo.  

when you plug it in. go to amarok and click on portable devices.  then your ipod should be at the top of the browser window...then press connect at the top. (it may ask you to delete a certain file in your ipod just hit ok)
then you should be able to drag music from your playlist to your ipod.
when you have all the songs you want hit the button "transfer" then hit disconnect ( same as the connect button metioned eairlier) then right click your ipod image on the desktop and hit unmount.
now you should be able to listen to your tunes on the go!

works like a charm for me but i stumbled upon it on accident. :)

i hope this helps...
