### Post by ghetto2ivy on 2008-08-25
GTKrecordmydesktop has a follow the pointer option. It will auto pan as you need. (as vitium pointed out above)

I find it works best if you turn off the display box, otherwise while moving the pointer it will capture the box itself.

I find Wink to be much better than most other options though, despite its lack of a follow the pointer option.
