### Post by peter228 on 2024-08-31
I have just accidentally found something new.....      I use a Logitech "960" headset and went to the sound settings to test it.  Left and Right tests make sounds.   The sound balance slider was set to the middle setting.  I accidentally nudged it to the right so it was just off the center position and all of a sudden the sound returned to normal.  I guess the system upgrade set the sound to equally balanced but for some reason that has caused a problem.

Is my problem solved?  Well no.   Have I got a workaround?   Apparently.
