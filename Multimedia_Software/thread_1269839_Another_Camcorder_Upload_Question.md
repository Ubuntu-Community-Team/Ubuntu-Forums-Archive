The camcorder comes with a USB cable.  No special software is required to transfer the video files from the camcorder, simply plug the USB cable in and mount it as a USB Storage device.  The video files have the extension .mts and are stored in the STREAM subdirectory.  A quick description of how to edit AVCHD files in Cinelerra may be found at

[http://www.renomath.org/ejolson/video/avchd/](http://www.renomath.org/ejolson/video/avchd/)
