### Post by bcschmerker on 2010-12-27
The ideal USB camera for Ubuntu® is a [USB Video Class](http://www.ideasonboard.org/uvc/) unit.  [Logitech®](http://www.logitech.com/) has several such models with excellent performance for most installations.  (I tested the [Dynex® DX-WEB1C](http://www.dynexproducts.com/) and found that, although it has excellent depth of field, it requires studio-grade lighting to get full speed.)

The QuickCam® Communicate Deluxe, QuickCam® S7500, and current Webcam C500 use the Sony Philips SPCA527 CCD; the QuickCam® Pro 9000, QuickCam® Vision Pro, QuickCam® Orbit AF, and Webcams C905 and C910, the SPCA522.  Some earlier models than listed above suffered from a firmware bug affecting signal timing, compromising their performance in the LinUX environment.
