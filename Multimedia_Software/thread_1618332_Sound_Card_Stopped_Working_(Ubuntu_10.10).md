[PROBLEM SOLVED]

I finally fixed the problem, but not the way I wanted to. I decided to buy a USB audio adapter that is compatible with Ubuntu. I got it for $9.60 at 

[http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001MSS6CS/ref=oss_product](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B001MSS6CS/ref=oss_product)

For those who are tired of this issue, I will recommend you to just buy a compatible sound card or USB audio adapter. The USB audio adapter I bought is for Windows 98SE/ME/2000/XP/Server 2003/Vista, Linux, and Mac OS 10 or higher. By the way, I did not need to install a driver on my computer for the device to work. It worked right away when I plugged it in my computer.

I hope this works for you guys.
