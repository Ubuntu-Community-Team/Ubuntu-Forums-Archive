> Exceptions

If you use any of the following operating systems and/or browsers, or your browser does not support JavaScript, you may experience problems uploading content to your Photobucket account:

    * Windows ME (must be running most current version of Firefox or IE available on this platform as your web browser)
    * Windows 98 (must be running most current version of Firefox or IE available on this platform as your web browser)
    * Windows 2000 (must be running most current version of Firefox or IE available on this platform as your web browser)
    * Mac OS 9
    * Linux OS
    * America Online Browser (AOL)
    * Internet Explorer for Mac
