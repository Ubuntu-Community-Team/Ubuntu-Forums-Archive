### Post by prshah on 2009-06-09
> **tito_pt said:**
>  wen't to the "mixer" to see if it was "muted".. No.. even with the sound 100% there is no sound, 

Open the mixer, click "Preferences", and enable the "Switches" tab (If it is not already visible)

Then, in switches, see if you have an option "External Amplifier". If you find it, toggle it; click it, wait a few seconds, then click it again (Should be checked/ticked).

Works best if you have some sound / music playing in the background while doing this.
