### Post by Greg Xix on 2009-02-10
I might have found the solution.

I was playing around with my settings(*System>Preferences>Screen Resolution*) and changed the "Refresh Rate" from 60hz to 75hz for my LCD.

Now the problem seems to have diminished greatly. I'll keep observing the results as I go and report anything useful.
