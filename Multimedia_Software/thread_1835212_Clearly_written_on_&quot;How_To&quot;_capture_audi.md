### Post by robert shearer on 2011-08-29
The simplest way would be installing 'Audio recorder' from here......
[https://launchpad.net/~osmoma/+archive/audio-recorder](https://launchpad.net/~osmoma/+archive/audio-recorder)

I have installed this on both my Natty boxes and it works just fine.

It records anything that is currently playing through your sound card and saves it to a folder named Audio in your home folder.

Note to **install** audio recorder use the link above and it's instructions.

To see info from the developers thread here and for source code .....
