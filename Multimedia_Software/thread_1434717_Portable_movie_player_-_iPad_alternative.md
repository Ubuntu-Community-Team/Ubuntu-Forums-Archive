### Post by hxcobd on 2010-03-20
I actually stumbled across something like this at work the other day:

The MiBook was marketed as a cheap sort of video learning player, with a bunch of tutorials for cooking and stuff on SD cards. However, it does play all video files and music files on whatever SD card you slide in it.

Also, it's appallingly cheap: $49.99, to be exact:

[http://www.bestbuy.com/site/miBook+-+miBook+Player/9477318.p?id=1218123550270&skuId=9477318&st=mibook](http://www.bestbuy.com/site/miBook+-+miBook+Player/9477318.p?id=1218123550270&skuId=9477318&st=mibook)

You would have to buy an SD card as well, but they're super cheap.

I'm not sure exactly what fork of what distro it uses, but I'm sure it's a linux implementation in some way, shape, or form. And that price cannot be beat.
