[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/VMware/Player?action=show&redirect=VMWarePlayerAndWindowsHOWTO](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/VMware/Player?action=show&redirect=VMWarePlayerAndWindowsHOWTO)
