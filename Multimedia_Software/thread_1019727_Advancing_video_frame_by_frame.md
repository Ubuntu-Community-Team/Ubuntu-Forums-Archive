### Post by cotcot on 2008-12-23
Blender video sequence editor (Blender VSE). Put the video on the timeline, zoom in on the timeline. Right of the "start" and "end" button you find a similar button with left and right arrow. You can use these to go to the next or previous frame (manually). See screenshot in annex. I am sure some other editors (avidemux, kdenlive, cinelerra) can do this as well.
