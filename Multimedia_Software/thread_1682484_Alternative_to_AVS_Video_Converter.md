### Post by FakeOutdoorsman on 2011-02-06
How about FFmpeg? AVS Video Converter uses FFmpeg to convert videos. Unfortunately, AVS Video Converter is a proud member of the [FFmpeg Hall of Shame](http://ffmpeg.org/shame.html) and is currently violating the FFmpeg license.

There are hundreds of similar converters and most are made by the same guys in China.
