### Post by Stilgar on 2010-06-28
Rhythmbox works really well. You can easily search your library add songs to the play queue, etc. They have a few music stores already pre-loaded, so you can search for songs and download them. Not sure how well the music store works as I've never used it.

In my opinion rhythmbox is the best app out there for organizing/searching/playing music out there. And yes I've tried iTunes, rhythmbox is by far better.

Just a tip, go to preferences->sound and select "no sounds" for your sound theme. This will avoid having the system making beeps when dialog messages come up. They may sound cute on your laptop speakers, but would be really annoying on the loud speakers in the middle of your reception.
