### Post by xp15 on 2014-02-19
I finally made it to make my SMPlayer to show Hebrew at the right way!

Instead of:
&#1491;&#1493;&#1512;: &#1488;&#1492; &#1495;&#1489;&#1512;&#1492;?

It will be "?&#1491;&#1493;&#1512;: &#1488;&#1492; &#1495;&#1489;&#1512;&#1492;" ^^ .

I've found the solution here: [http://www.mplayer2.org/docs/mplayer/](http://www.mplayer2.org/docs/mplayer/)

Go to SMPlayer, Ctrl+P (Preferens), Last choice Down ("Advanced").
There the middle Len/Tab ("Options for MPlayer").
At "Options" (First Text box when writting this ^^ ), add this:
--no-flip-hebrew-commas

It's 2 (-) in the begining! and they are at the start!
Also in a code:
```
--no-flip-hebrew-commas
```
