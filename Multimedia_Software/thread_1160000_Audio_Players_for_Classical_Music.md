### Post by Ubuntist on 2009-05-15
Are there any audio players out there that have been designed with classical music in mind?  Most players organize music solely by track, but for classical the more useful entity is the piece, in other words a set of movements, each of which is a track.  It would be nice, for example, to be able to program in a random selection of pieces as opposed to a random selection of tracks.

Elsewhere I'd heard that Quod Libet was best for classical.  Although I do generally like it, I have not been able to find any way to get it to deal with whole pieces.
