### Post by vyruss on 2008-06-09
If you're using PulseAudio for sound and everything is working except your games (e.g. Supertux2, Sauerbraten etc) you should install this package:

**libsdl1.2debian-pulseaudio**

And presto! Your games have sound playing properly!
