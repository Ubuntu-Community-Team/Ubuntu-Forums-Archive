### Post by mc4man on 2010-01-21
> So, base on the replies I can't change the region from the setup menu?


There is no direct relationship between a region setting of a dvd player, a region code on a disk, and the format of the video other than most R 2 disks are encoded as pal, all R 1 disks are encoded as ntsc.

Being in the us your tv is ntsc, to get a pal video to display you'd need a dvd player that can do the conversion from pal to ntsc (or a display monitor that can do both ntsc and pal with a player that can be set to either) Has nothing to do with the region setting of the dvd player and the region code, if any, of the disk.

Sometimes very cheap dvd players will do the conversion (with varying quality), and many better dvd players can also. ( most sony dvd players don't

pc monitors can display both pal and ntsc, that's why it works on your computer
