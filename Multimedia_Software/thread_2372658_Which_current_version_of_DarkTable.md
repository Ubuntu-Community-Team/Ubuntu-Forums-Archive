### Post by mc4man on 2017-09-27
The difference in the size of the 2 snaps would be from how they were constructed. The kyrofa one is a typical limited snap, the empanada one may allow browsing/working for/on  files not in $HOME dir. 
(- what it says about ' Not sandboxed, access to Documents'  doesn't make complete sense as the other one should also work in that location.

The ppa version is your typical linux app with no limitations.
You can definitely have a .deb & snap version installed at same time, maybe you can have both snaps also (don't see why not..) so give them all a try & use what suits you.
