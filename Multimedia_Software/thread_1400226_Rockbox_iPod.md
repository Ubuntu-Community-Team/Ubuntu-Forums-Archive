### Post by dondiego2 on 2010-02-06
I installed Rockbox on my iPod yesterday and loved it!  Then I found out it didn't handle podcasts very well so I found out how to write a script and make a custom menu item for podcasts and it worked great.  I love how you can customize the menus.

Then I saw that it couldn't find any of my album covers and digging into it I found out that it could not display album covers that are attached to the meta data.  Unfortunately all my 7500 songs are like that because the album covers were all added in iTunes.  Too much work to fix all the album covers for Rockbox.

Then I found out it didn't find my video podcasts and it couldn't play mp4 videos.  A deal killer for me and I'm bumming because other then those things I loved it.  Oh well, back to the Apple menus.  Also I had an audiobook on there and I could not find it with Rockbox and now that I am back to Apple software it is gone from my iPod.
