### Post by SeijiSensei on 2010-10-11
Are you trying to watch an "HD" video on a traditional (not widescreen) monitor?  Any widescreen content in the 16x9 aspect ratio will have black bars on top and bottom when displayed full-screen on an older 4x3 aspect ratio screen.  It's not being stretched; those are the correct proportions for HD content.  Since the shape of the monitor doesn't conform to the shape of the video, there are black bars on top and bottom.
