### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2014-02-10
> It's strange that the audio device uses a kernel driver snd_hda_intel, i have an AMD motherboard...
snd-hda-intel is the correct module. A lot of sound chips conform to it: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intel_High_Definition_Audio](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intel_High_Definition_Audio)

Try this first:
```
rm -r ~/.pulse*
pulseaudio -k
```

If sound is still giving issue, give more information:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Audio/AlsaInfo](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Audio/AlsaInfo)
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PulseAudio/Log](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PulseAudio/Log)
