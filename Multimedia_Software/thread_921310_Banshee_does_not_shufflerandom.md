### Post by domino1241 on 2011-12-08
I was able to play all the songs in my library in random order by going to the left pane -> Libraries -> Music and double clicking a song.  Then in the top menu go to Playback -> Shuffle -> Shuffle By Song.  The songs play in random order and the "Next" button now looks like a shuffled next button, and clicking it takes you to a random song.

Hope this helps someone.
