### Post by Abandon on 2008-05-15
I'm using a Hauppauge PVR 150 and that one does not work with tvtime because of the mpeg decoder within this card. No worries. You can use mplayer, totem, vlc, xine-ui to mention a few. 

I use vlc with this command: 

vlc --video-on-top -I dummy pvr:// :pvr-device="/dev/video0" :pvr-norm=0 :pvr-frequency=-1 :pvr-bitrate=-1 &


and Xine with:

ptune-ui.pl -d /dev/vdeo0 & cat /dev/video0 | xine stdin://mpeg2 &

Totem works with:

totem /dev/video0

BUT..you need to intall ivtv-utils for setting up the right frequenty. I live in Haarlem, the Netherlands, so I creates little scripts and placed theme in a local /home/user/bin map.

#!/bin/bash
#Nederland 1
ivtv-tune --frequency 216,00MHz
