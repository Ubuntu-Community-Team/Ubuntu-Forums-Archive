### Post by Heer2play on 2010-01-31
On your touch goto Setting&Tools->Tools->USB Mass Storage. After clicking it a window should pop up with all of the folders on the card in your phone. Place the pics that you want in to the 'my_pix' folder and there you go. Now I don't know what pic formats that the phone will display.
