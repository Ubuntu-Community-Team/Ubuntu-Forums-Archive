### Post by mihalybaci on 2011-06-22
I like GnomeBaker and k9copy.  k9copy has a nice feature that allows you to shrink DVDs when ripping so a standard 7 GB DVD, from say Netflix, can be reduced to fit on a standard (and cheaper) 4.7 GB DVD. I usually make .iso files from DVDs with k9copy, then actually burn them with GnomeBaker because I like the interface a little better.
