### Post by little_penguin on 2009-11-10
I have the Pinnacle DVB-T Stick Solo version 72E (you'll find this at the bottom of the box) USB digital tv tuner.
I think it is supported out of the box in Intrepid, I can't remember if I had to do anything as it's a while back, but it is definitely supported out of the box in Jaunty, because I just installed that for a friend with the same Pinnacle tv stick. 

So you just plug it in and start your DVB-T digital TV software. My favourite is Me-TV. You can get it from the repositories but there is a more up-to-date version (1.0) on the developer's launchpad page in deb package format (for Intrepid and Jaunty).

[https://edge.launchpad.net/me-tv/stable/1.0.0](https://edge.launchpad.net/me-tv/stable/1.0.0)

Other TV viewing and recording software: Kaffeine, Klear, MythTV.

Hope that helps,
Regards,
