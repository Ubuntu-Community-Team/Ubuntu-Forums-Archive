Well, I guess everyone knows about this problem by now. I spent a couple of hours googling and trying things but nothing worked. I probably could have cracked it eventually, but I just needed to get this box up and running for people to use.

I'm really happy with this solution. Going from 9.04 to 8.04 felt like an upgrade in some ways. Other sound problems went away, and I now have a menu editor, which makes life a bit easier. Skype is a really important app for a lot of people. Please can this problem get fixed in a future release of Xubuntu?
