### Post by Dynamo_Joe on 2008-07-14
Hi Koori23, 

I've been running a GeForce 6200A since FF and now in HH. 

One small "gotcha" you have to watch out for ... you may need to reset X to use "VESA" first as I had to use the "Safe Graphics Mode" option on both installs. 

I haven't bothered with the restricted drivers yet due to the amount of kernal upgrades in HH where others have reported problems. 

The card is good for "Youtube" videos and DVD playback but don't expect "blazing 3D game-play performance" for modern games.
