### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-09-18
USB audio is standardised, so any USB sound card you connect should work in Ubuntu.
The two USB units you linked to at the start of this thread look very good, as they have a mic input knob you can adjust easily.

Other alternatives that should work (but probably aren't quite as nice as the m-audio):
[Hercules Muse Pocket LT]("http://www.hercules.com/uk/Sound-Cards/bdd/p/56/muse-pocket-lt/")
[Creative Sound Blaster Play!]("http://au.creative.com/products/product_CreativeStore.asp?category=1&subcategory=207&product=17892&listby=")

I personally try to avoid creative products where possible, from my personal experiences I've had nothing but trouble from their hardware (and when using windows, their software).

I do have a Hercules DJ console, that's effectively a 6 channel sound card with mic that works fine in Ubuntu.  Just plug it in and it works.
