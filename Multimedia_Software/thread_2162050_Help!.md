### Post by Mr.Dunham on 2013-07-13
Go to Ubuntu Software Center (it's on your Xubuntu menu) and once there, in the search box type the word "webcam" without the quotes of course and you'll find a bunch of software for your webcam. There's Cheese and Camorama, both widely used, and some more. Or if you install Skype or other videoconferencing software, it should find the built-in cam.
