### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2013-01-14
[http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge](http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge)

doesn't look very promising..the PCI version had some support.

Analogue video capture hardware doesn't generate much enthusiasm..
Might be easiest to record on cheap DVD recorder & then import the mpeg2 video.

This seems supported:
[http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_WinTV-PVR-USB2](http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_WinTV-PVR-USB2)
get a mpeg2 encoder for analogue video in & a dvb-t tuner..

or internal HVR-4000
[http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_WinTV-HVR-4000](http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_WinTV-HVR-4000)
