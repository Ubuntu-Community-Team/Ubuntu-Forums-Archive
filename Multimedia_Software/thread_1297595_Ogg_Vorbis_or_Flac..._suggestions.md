### Post by spillin_dylan on 2009-10-21
Unless you're overly concerned about the 'legality' or 'free-ness' of an MP3 format, don't convert them at all.  You're only going to lose quality by converting, as Vorbis and FLAC are different compression methods than MP3, and since MP3 is already compressed, it's like "double" compressing the audio.  Not good.  FLAC is actually way less compressed, and would definitely be ideal for ripping your CD collection, for instance, but I don't think there is any software that will 'up-convert' to a FLAC format for you.

It's kind of like burning a VHS tape to Blu-Ray, and expecting high-definition quality.
