### Post by motionbox on 2010-01-05
Hi there!
 
An update from Motionbox on using Ubuntu:
 
 
-Make sure that the flash installed with your version of ubuntu is up to date, and installed using the ".deb (Ubuntu 8.04+)" option, clicking "open with the default handler, and clicking the install button. 
 
-Note that with this, the broswer based uploader will freeze during the uploading process, and will only unfreeze after the upload is complete. 
  
I hope this helps! If not, I can also contact our engineering team if the above isn't working for you.
