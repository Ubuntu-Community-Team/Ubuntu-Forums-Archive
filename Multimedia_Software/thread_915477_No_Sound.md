### Post by mike1234 on 2008-09-09
I have an Audigy. Double click on sound icon in upper right hand corner of desktop. Select switches, and uncheck box that says Audigy Analog/Digital Output Jack. (if checked). Just a suggestion because by default for some reason it is selected and I have no sound at all.
