### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-12-12
Click on the Volume icon and go to Sound Settings where in the output devices tab you may find headphones listed in the dropdown box and can make sure they are not muted.

You can also run **alsamixer** in terminal and check that the levels of outputs are not set too low.

Move from slider to slider, and raise and lower levels with the cursor keys;
Mute and unmute with the "M" key.
Tab will move you from "playback" to "capture".
Esc will save and exit.
