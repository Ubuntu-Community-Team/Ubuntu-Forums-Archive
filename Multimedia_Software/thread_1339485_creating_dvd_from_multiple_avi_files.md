### Post by GrandpaLeaman on 2009-11-27
I use Todisc, which is a suite of programs that will convert the files to a DVD format, help you create menus, and burn the whole thing to DVD. It works great and even has a gui called (of course) todiscgui.

You can find more info here:

[http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/karmic/man1/todiscgui.1.html](http://manpages.ubuntu.com/manpages/karmic/man1/todiscgui.1.html)

Also, Google "todisc" and "tovid".

Have fun!
