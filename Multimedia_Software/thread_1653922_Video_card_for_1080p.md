### Post by BicyclerBoy on 2010-12-28
Assuming your mobo has PCIe slots...
Video playback recommendations from mythtv users:

The minimum recommended nvidia video card for HD playback is GT220.

Ideally you want feature set C. GT220 GT240 etc see wikipedia.

The GT430 is a recommended model. Excellent performance & price is okay. 

9500GT,9600GT can do HD decode with advanced de-interlace but GT220 is better.
