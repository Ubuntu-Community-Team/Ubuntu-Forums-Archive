### Post by a z on 2012-10-21
if you mean rip internet radio, i would put in a terminal:
streamripper ~/Music [http://wfmu.org/wfmu.pls](http://wfmu.org/wfmu.pls)
(or whatever url floats your boat)
this assumes the package, streamripper is installed.

to play/stream, i like radiotray(in repo).
it just sits there - a little icon in your systray. lots of preset stations, and easy to config with your own. a lot of times, i go to [www.shoutcast.com](www.shoutcast.com) to find new stations, then rightclk/copy the url into radiotray. piece 'o cake.

there are console-only players out there, i think - just never went that way, myself.

good luck
