### Post by ticket on 2012-04-25
To get rid of the blue faces, you have to sacrifice accelerated video rendering.  Switch off accelerated rendering by opening a youtube video in full screen mode and right-clicking on it and choose the settings option.

Flashgot works very well for grabbing and downloading web videos.
