Hello tijmz,

I use a M-Audio Keystation 49e (it is cheap!) - just plug it in and play, it works fine with ubuntu.

More expensive but with 88 weighted key action I also use the "Kurzweil SP2X" - it too works as a plug and play device in ubuntu.

Both keyboards use an usb cable and offer an extra midi port. Additionally my M-Audio Midisport **_2x4_**!!! works without problems in ubuntu.
