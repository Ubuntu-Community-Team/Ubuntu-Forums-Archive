### Post by DeMus on 2009-01-30
How about PiTiVi?

PiTiVi is a free program for video editing based on the GStreamer framework. PiTiVi can:

    * capture and encode audio and video
    * mix, resize, cut, apply effects to audio sources
    * crop, trim, enhance audio
    * render/save projects in any format supported by the GStreamer framework

It is installable using Synaptic.
