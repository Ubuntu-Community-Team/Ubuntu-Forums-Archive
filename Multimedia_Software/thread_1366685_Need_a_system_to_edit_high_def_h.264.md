### Post by gordintoronto on 2009-12-28
Have you considered using winff (graphical front-end for ffmpeg) to convert the video into another format?  What video editor are you using?

My computer specs are fairly similar to yours, and Cinelerra works OK.  When it is creating the final output it's a little slow, but that is to be expected.  I've edited an HD video that was 15 minutes long.

Depending on whether your video editor supports it, a faster quad-core CPU might make a difference.
