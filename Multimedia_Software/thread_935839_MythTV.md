### Post by noisymime on 2008-10-05
I'd recommend sticking with mythbuntu, its neater than doing things piece by piece. At the end of the day, its all the same software. 

My guess with your sound but no video problem is that its a codec issue. If you open the Mythbuntu Command Centre (MCC) from the Applications menu, there's an option in there to install all codecs from medibuntu. That will probably be the easiest way for you to get everything you need.
