### Post by winh8r on 2012-04-08
Just right click on an empty space in the panel and select "add to panel" then scroll down to "Indicator Applet" select it and click on "Add"

The speaker icon and the mail icon should be back in place, you can move them around or lock them in place by right clicking on them and selecting "Move" or "Lock to panel"

Hope this helps.
