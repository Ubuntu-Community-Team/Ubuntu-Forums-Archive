### Post by Melcar on 2009-05-02
Are you using Pulseaudio?  If so, you need to configure it for 5.1 sound (default is 2 channels):

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SurroundSound]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SurroundSound")

### Post by wallyba on 2009-05-03
Allright. THANKS A LOT Melcar, i've solved it. Alsa was working, the only thing missing was a this line in .xine/config:

audio.driver:alsa

After this modification, i have sorroud sound in ubuntu, not only with Xine but with VLC and Rhythmbox (don't ask why).

Thanks again, i'm happy!!!
