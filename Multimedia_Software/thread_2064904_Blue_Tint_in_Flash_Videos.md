*In another thread someone said that going into fullscreen and unchecking hardware acceleration works but it didn't do it for me.

### Post by TenPlus1 on 2012-09-30
Right-click on a flash video and select SETTINGS, then UNTICK "Enable Hardware Acceleration"... This will cure the blue tiny effect and hopefully all crashes...  If it won't let you untick this box, run a youtube movie in full screen and try it again...
