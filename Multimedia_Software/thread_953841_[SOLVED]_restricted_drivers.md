### Post by blakjesus on 2008-10-20
It only means that the drivers are proprietary. Meaning that the Linux community is not free to modify it. It should work but if it doesn't you are probably out of luck because most hardware developers aren't as concentrated on Linux.

EDIT: [This]("https://help.ubuntu.com/8.04/hardware/C/restricted-manager.html") site will explain the meaning of restricted drivers, and [this]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedDrivers") site will tell you whether you should use a restricted driver. (Only lists Intel Nvidia and ATI)
