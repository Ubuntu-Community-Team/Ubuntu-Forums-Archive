### Post by sgosnell on 2010-03-28
Get a PC to TV converter.  It takes the VGA from your PC's connector, and converts it to S-Video or component outputs.  You'll need a separate audio cable if you want to have the sound go through the TV, though.  I got one at Fry's, but Google should find a few in your area, or from online resellers.  Mine is a KWorld brand and works OK.
