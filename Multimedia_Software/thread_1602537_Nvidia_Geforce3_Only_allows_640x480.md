### Post by erniesthings on 2010-11-05
[solved] Not sure how to change the thread title to [solved]

I think the problem was that the card didn't recognize the monitor. Which the config had has unknown. It was an older HP 15" monitor.

Here's what I did.

I un-installed the nvidia driver. 
Attached a Dell 17" that I had.
Re-installed the driver, restarted, and now I have resolution options up to 1900x1200.

I don't know if I want to attach the HP monitor just to see if I have more resolution options.
