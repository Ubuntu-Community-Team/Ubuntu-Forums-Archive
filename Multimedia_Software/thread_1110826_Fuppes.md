### Post by Nostalos on 2009-03-30
I don't use Fuppes, so I have no knowledge of how to configure it for transcoding.  However,  I have found that real time transcoding to be a major waste of time and effort as the quality is poor in the best of circumstances.

Grab Handbrake [http://handbrake.fr/](http://handbrake.fr/)  and use the Xbox 360 Profile to transcode into an mp4 format.


There are ways to get around having to transcode the whole thing, but that dependant on the source. Avidemux can remux just the audio for example.  It just has to fit inside the restrictions placed on the 360.

[http://support.xbox.com/support/en/us/nxe/GamesandMedia/Movies/VideoFAQ/ViewVideoPlaybackFAQ.aspx](http://support.xbox.com/support/en/us/nxe/GamesandMedia/Movies/VideoFAQ/ViewVideoPlaybackFAQ.aspx)

Example I prefer x264 mp4's, so It has to be an mp4 container with x264 video at a maximum baseline of 4.1 and 2 channel AAC LC audio and a maximum size of 4GB.
