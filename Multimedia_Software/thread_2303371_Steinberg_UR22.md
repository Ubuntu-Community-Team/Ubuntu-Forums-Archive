### Post by O)9(yo&amp;# on 2015-11-25
I use the UR-22. I only do midi with it, so can't comment on the audio latency. but it has a good reputation, and is considered a pro level interface. As for how it works on Linux, once you fully update, it is recognized by Ubuntu 14.02, mint cinnamon 17.2 and Xfce 17.2 out of the box and works great. I have not tried doing music yet on Linux, but that is definitely in my future. For the money, one of the best buys out there.  not sure about Mate as I did not fully update it.
