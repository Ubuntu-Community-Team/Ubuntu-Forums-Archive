### Post by milok on 2010-10-22
There are plenty of posts about how to prevent delays between songs in rhythmbox, but none about putting them there.

The delays are normally caused, it seems, by the users' systems waiting to load the next song until after the current song is finished. That is fixed by turning on crossfading and setting to zero (or more by preference).

My problem is that I *want* a delay between songs. If the cross fader bar went to negative numbers, it'd probably insert a delay, but that's a sad hack.
