After you get the output iPod compatible video format, you can drag the iPod MPEG-4 video files to your iPod with the sync of iTunes. Below is the step by step tutorial showing you how to convert DVD to iPod and convert video to iPod touch, iPod nano, iPod touch, iPod video..

The guide is composed of the following two parts:

**Part 1: How to convert DVD movie to iPod**

1. Download **DVD to iPod Converter Suite** from [http://www.softdiggs.com/dvd-to-ipod-converter-suite-cucu.php](http://www.softdiggs.com/dvd-to-ipod-converter-suite-cucu.php) and install it.

[IMG]http://www.softdiggs.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/07/dvd-video-to-ipod.jpg[/IMG]

2. Run the DVD to iPod Converter. Click the "Open DVD" button to load your DVD. Or you can choose "Open DVD" from the "File" drop-down list to import the DVD. One or several titles/chapters will appear in the list.

[IMG]http://www.softdiggs.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/07/dvd-video-to-ipod1.jpg[/IMG]

3. Generally, choose the longest DVD title, which is the main content of the movie. You can remove the title you don't like to convert by unticking it. Select DVD subtitles and audio tracks from the available ones.

6. Locate the .MP4 file that DVD to iPod converter outputed and drag it into your iTunes library. Or select "Add File to Library" from the "File" menu and select the encoded movie to import to iTunes. Now you can watch it with iTunes or Quicktime Player on your PC.
