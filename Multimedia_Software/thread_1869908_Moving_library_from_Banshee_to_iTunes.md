Hi there,

I switched from Ubuntu to Mac and I'm wondering if there's any way to transfer my music library from Banshee to iTunes. In particular I want to keep the ratings (actually, that's the only thing that matters).
Anybody has an idea on that? Didn't find anything on search/google.

cheers!


**EDIT**
I solved the problem with a simple work around. I used the id3 tags to save rating information. In banshee I selected all songs with rating X (x being 1 to 5) and saved "ratingX" as Grouping. That was okay because I never usually use the Grouping field in id3 tags. Then in iTunes i selected all the tracks with a specific Grouping and assigned the appropriate rating.
