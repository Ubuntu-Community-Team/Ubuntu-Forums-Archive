### Post by tatsuya6400 on 2010-03-29
In case any one runs into this problem uploading to YouTube:
Simply my problem was I'd be on the upload page and I'd choose my video. it would say it was uploading but I didn't see the progress bar i was supposed to see. It didn't take me long to figure out what I needed to do.
heres what you do.
1. look at the upload page.
2. scroll down a little ways and you will see a little link that says
     "Upload problems? Try without the progress bar."
3. Click on it. The upload page it takes you to will work. Guaranteed ^_~
