### Post by djrobthaman on 2009-11-01
Hello again.  Just wanted to say that I solved my problem and actually feel a little foolish about it.  If you go into the configure system>settings.appearance>screen section (I think I got that navigation right... if not, it's something close) there is a config item for screen resolution.  And that allows you to decide how big or small the viewable screen is.
