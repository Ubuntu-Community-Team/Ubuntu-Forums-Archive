### Post by cb951303 on 2009-02-23
[http://a4tech.com/ennew/product.asp?cid=77&scid=89&id=560](http://a4tech.com/ennew/product.asp?cid=77&scid=89&id=560)

This worked out of the box for me. I last tried with ubuntu 7.10 if I remember correctly. The quality is actually quite good.

A word of advice. Don't be fooled by cheap webcams stating that they are 1.2, 2 or 3 megapixels. In truth they are vga cams with software interpolation to augment the resolution. So, no real quality gain.
