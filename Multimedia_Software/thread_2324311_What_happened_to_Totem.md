### Post by mc4man on 2016-05-12
It's actually pretty simple. When you open it you'll see 2 tabs,  Videos & Channels. Channels verges on completely worthless unless you like rai.tv or apple trailers.
Under the videos tab you can add videos using the + button. 
The search button will let you name search based on what you pick from the drop down. (getting out of search will prove difficult other than closing totem
To play a dvd  would likely work from the file manager, the optical media plugin is either missing, removed or non-functional ( most plugins are grllo plugins & most don't work.

All in all represents where Gnome is heading with the app & generally useless for Ubuntu users.
