### Post by markbuntu on 2009-03-16
I have a SIIG Soundwave 7.1 PCI. It was about $30 I think. Sounds great and works OOB with Hardy, Intrepid and Jaunty. It uses the C-Media 8768 chipset and has none of those wacky soundblaster problems. It also has about twice the output of my on-board ALC883. For the price it is just great.

I run it through my tiny onkyo reciever to my Klipsch KG1.2 speakers.
