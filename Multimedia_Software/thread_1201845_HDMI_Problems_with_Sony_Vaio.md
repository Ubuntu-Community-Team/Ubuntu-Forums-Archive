### Post by stepore on 2009-11-25
> **i, fuzzbot said:**
> 
I recently installed Ubuntu 9.04 on my Sony Vaio FW laptop. It has an HDMI output which I would routinely plug into my HDTV when using Windows. I have tried various fixes posted here and elsewhere, but whenever I plug the HDMI cable into my computer Ubuntu either freezes or shuts off entirely. Does anyone know how to fix this?

I've found the only work-around is placing the computer in suspend/standby first, then plug in the HDMI cable, then wake from suspend. Works fine. Unplugging the HDMI cable will also completely lock the computer unless you suspend computer first, then unplug HDMI cable, then wake from suspend, again.
