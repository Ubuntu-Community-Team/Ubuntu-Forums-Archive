### Post by laceration on 2009-12-03
TV streaming is one thing and is available from Hulu, etc.  But *Live* TV streaming is something that the Web totally sucks at, in its current state, do not waste your time.  And if you find a service to pay for it will be a rip-off operation.
I know people like the idea of TV over the web as its a shiny new object, but imo, OTA(over the air) TV is still by far the best way to do it and it is very possible to "stream" this into your computer--that's how I watch TV.  You should think about that and when the next generation web comes, reconsider your original idea.
