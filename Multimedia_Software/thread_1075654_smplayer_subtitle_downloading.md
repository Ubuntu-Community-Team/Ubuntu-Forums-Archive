### Post by rvm4000 on 2009-02-20
The version of smplayer in intrepid is 0.6.1 while the option to download subtitles was added in 0.6.2.

You can get v. 0.6.6 from here:
[http://smplayer.berlios.de/downloads.php](http://smplayer.berlios.de/downloads.php)

Or better, packages from svn:
[ftp://ftp.berlios.de/pub/smplayer/ubuntu/](ftp://ftp.berlios.de/pub/smplayer/ubuntu/)

There are also 2 PPA repositories:
[https://launchpad.net/~rvm/+archive/ppa/](https://launchpad.net/~rvm/+archive/ppa/) (for "stable" packages)

[https://launchpad.net/~rvm4000/+archive/ppa/](https://launchpad.net/~rvm4000/+archive/ppa/) (for experimental ones)
