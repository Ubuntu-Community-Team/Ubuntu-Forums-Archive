hmmphh im not exactly sure what your asking but i'll answer the title of your thread.

do you have gnomebaker? - its awesome, if you don't have it: its in the universe repositories.

launch gnomebaker: drag down the desired files : hit burn on the lower right (DVD Dl format will give you the most space if you jsut creating iso for fun)

after you hit burn the first tab on the "create data dvd" window is writing. Very bottom of that window lets you just create the iso without actually burning it. - ive used this MANY MANY times and its worked perfectly for me every time, hope it does for you.
