### Post by 3startuna on 2009-05-17
All I can say is WOW. 

I have a set of altec Lansing 5.1 Surround sound speakers so even with the onboard card my sound quality was good. This card though takes it to another level.

It is incredibly clear, there is a definate improvement in mids and highs, you hear alot more of the tweeters, and alot more of the symbols. The bass response is also stronger and clearer.

Its a very loud card too but even at high volumes it plays incredibly well. 

Today I played AC/DC Thunderstuck and I thought it blew out one of my rear speakers :D Thankfully the cord in the back of the subwoofer just got lose, causing it to distort. 

Overall im very happy with the card. Playing Journey on this card makes you feel like your actually at a live concert same with watching videos on youtube it feels like your in the theatre. Im a huge 24 fan and it really brings you into the action.

Installation was easy, 

I had to disable the onboard card from within Bios  and it needs Jaunty to run. I was on 8.10 and it wouldnt play, When I upgraded to 9.04 it played. 

Since your computer is 9.04, just plug it in and it will work!

The D2 should be better than the D1 which I have, but the D1 is a very very tough card to beat.

The `price was high but the ease of install and the sound quality more than make up for the 40.00 extra
