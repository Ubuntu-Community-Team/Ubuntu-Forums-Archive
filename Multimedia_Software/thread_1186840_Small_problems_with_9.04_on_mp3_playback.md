### Post by Student Driver on 2009-07-24
I had the same issues with my MP3s and flash video.  However, I removed PulseAudio and now both work perfectly fine.  I realize that it's "the future" and it should work ([http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=789578](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=789578)) but it trashed my audio, and makes flash skip.  Truth be told, I didn't even think to test my flash playback until I saw chavash's post here.

See the directions on post #13 if you aren't sure of how to do this, and want to try removing PulseAudio:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1187099&highlight=mp3+skipping&page=2](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1187099&highlight=mp3+skipping&page=2)
