1) [https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/lucid/+source/rhythmbox/+addquestion](https://answers.launchpad.net/ubuntu/lucid/+source/rhythmbox/+addquestion) which gets no answers EVER

2) [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/rhythmbox](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/rhythmbox) which has a completely broken support system imho (people constantly changing status from undecided > triaged > undecided > unimportant > triaged > haven't got a clue

3) [https://bugzilla.gnome.org](https://bugzilla.gnome.org) a third place to report bugs....

Can't update tags on large MP3 files.

Numerous Gstreamer bugs, even telling you to report a bug, but then not how to....
Consistently rebuilding library on start-up when you are using a Samba network share
Timeline not progressing

I could go on, but I'd end of checking my PC out the window due to thinking about how buggy Rhythmbox is.
