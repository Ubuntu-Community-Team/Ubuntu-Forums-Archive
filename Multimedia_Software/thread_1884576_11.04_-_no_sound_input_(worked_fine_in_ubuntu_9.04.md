### Post by noob5000 on 2011-11-21
after i went into the terminal and opened the alsamixer ("alsamixer") and turned the input volume down and up again, it suddenly worked. i don't understand why it didn't before (db gain of input sound was at 0%, i.e. sound input should have been received 100% as it is now), but i am sure glad that it does now.
