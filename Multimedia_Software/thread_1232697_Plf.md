Hi Cotux.

Penguin Liberation Front [http://plf.zarb.org/](http://plf.zarb.org/) (PLF Died. Medibuntu is a reincarnation of it.) 

Plf for ubuntu is now MEDIBUNTU.

To enable medibuntu repositories, click system> administration> software sources.
Enter your password. hit enter.
Click third party software.
Make sure medibuntu box is checked.

This tutorial should help.
