### Post by wkulecz on 2009-06-11
I scoffed at the very idea of these things, but gave one a try when I needed to add a third monitor to a Windows system with no free slots, so in desperation I took a chance and got one.

Bottom line, at 1280x1024 32-bit color (max this one supports) performance in 2D is suprisingly good -- I could play a talking head video from Fox news without any issues when the window was dragged to the USB monitor.

I specifically have the EVGA UV+, but there are a fair number of brands of these available, some of the DVI ones support 1600x1200 resolutions.

I could find these useful in general if I had any hope of Ubuntu working with them.  Unfortunately the third monitor is on a system I can't just pull parts from to play with.
