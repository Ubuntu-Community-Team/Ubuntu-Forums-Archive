### Post by WaffleCode on 2009-02-25
That's probably an error with Wine, not your video card.

Try fiddling with the settings in *winecfg* and go to [this page]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=9194") to see if anyone has anything on getting it to run.

And to answer your question: Go to a terminal (Applications -> Accessories -> Terminal) and type *lspci*.
