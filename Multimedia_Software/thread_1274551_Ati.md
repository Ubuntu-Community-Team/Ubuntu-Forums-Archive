### Post by Melcar on 2009-09-24
Compiz hates ATI; it's true.  Rotating the cube shouldn't be much of a problem (I had that card before and it was smooth), although for some reason the effect loads the CPU quite a bit for some reason (I'm guessing it's a conflict with how the driver does things and how Compiz wants to do things).  The choppy window resizing is "normal" on Jaunty; something to do with the xserver version in Jaunty and how it screws up ATI drivers a bit.
 
As for the WINE part, well WINE also hates ATI drivers.  I don't play WoW so I can't say for sure, but many games exhibit performance and graphical problems.
