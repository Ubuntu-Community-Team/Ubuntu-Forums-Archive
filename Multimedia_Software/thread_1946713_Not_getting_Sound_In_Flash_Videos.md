### Post by gordintoronto on 2012-03-26
Usually it means sound is muted or the volume is set very low somewhere. Click on the speaker icon and select Sound Preferences, have a look at the hardware tab and the output tab. If you don't see anything muted or volume set low, try different connectors in the hardware tab.

If that doesn't work, tell us what the hardware device is, and how your computer produces sound: built-in speakers, external speakers, headphones...
