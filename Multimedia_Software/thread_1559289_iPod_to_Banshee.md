### Post by Lars Noodén on 2010-08-23
Please take the time to look at the audio kits with better interfaces and sounder underlying design, language and licensing:

[Rhythmbox](http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/rhythmbox), [Amarok](http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/amarok), [Totem](http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/totem), [Exaile](http://packages.ubuntu.com/lucid/)

My current iPod is ancient and about due for replacement. 
It mount as HFS and I get at the files that way.
Or you can use GTKpod.

Amarok has more support for iPod built in.
