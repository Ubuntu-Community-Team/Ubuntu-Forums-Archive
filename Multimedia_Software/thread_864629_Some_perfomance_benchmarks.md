### Post by Melcar on 2008-07-19
What kind of performance?  Graphics, CPU, memory?  Suffice to say that my superPi runs (1M) are a full 6 seconds faster than under Windows XP, and 3D performance seems to be around 85-95% (nvidia) and 75-95% (ATI) in comparison to XP (when running crossplatform games like Nexuiz).
Phoronix should have some old Windows vs. Linux 3D comparisons somewhere stashed away in their databases, but aside from that the general consensus is that processing performance is generally better under Linux.
