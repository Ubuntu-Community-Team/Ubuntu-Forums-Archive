### Post by quip on 2009-01-06
It depends on your video card and setup.

I had that problem with Desktop Effects enabled, and Ubuntu's most recent ATI driver.  I went to ATI's site, removed the Ubuntu version, and installed the one from there, and the flickering went away for apps and games, but remained for videos.

Once I set the video out in mplayer to X11, the flickering went away there, too.

Just a couple of things to think about.  You will have more luck if you list (and search for) your video card and driver.
