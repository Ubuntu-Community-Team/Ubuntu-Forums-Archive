### Post by Harpie Queen on 2009-05-17
With your query about recording sound, go to Applications, sound and video, sound recorder. I found it very simple, I plugged in my guitar to the adapter, the adapter to the mic port, and then told the program to record. As for the 2 audio input things that would be unique for your computer, I have Line in and Mic in for example. You can work that out by yourself, by putting one on full, one on 0 and trying to record. From the results you can deduce which is which.
