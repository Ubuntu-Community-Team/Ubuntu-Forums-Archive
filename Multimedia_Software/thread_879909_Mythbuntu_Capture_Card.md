### Post by Fire_Chief on 2008-08-04
The Hauppauge PVR-150 is an inexpensive card that works perfectly well with MythTV. The newer ones may not use the proper chipset though (see info on the PVR-1600 I think). I have an original one and it was fine out of the box.
