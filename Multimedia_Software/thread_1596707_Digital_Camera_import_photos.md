### Post by jeannacav on 2010-10-15
Here is how I do it.

(In system preferences you could turn off fspot, or leave it on and close it when it opens.)

Doubleclick the icon for the camera when it appears on the desktop.
It will open in a browser window.
(I usually open a second browser window at this point.)

Click to select, then drag any pix or vids from the camera drive over to a folder in your second browser window.
I usually put them in the same file-tree format that fspot uses, but you can use anything or nothing... your choice.

I like to remove the originals by hand too, which I do from the camera after I have done the above.
