### Post by f37u5g0d on 2008-08-08
I was once searching for an itunes replacer but I was never able to find something that copied that experience completely on linux.  I wound up switching to rockbox which is like an alternate operating system for the ipod.  I am now able to rip songs from CD's to .flac (free and superior to .m4a) on my desktop and simply drag the song files from the music folder onto the ipod.  Rockbox picks them up and integrates them into my database.  I am not sure about album art since I don't really care about it lol but I know that rockbox does support it, I just don't know how well.  [http://www.rockbox.org/](http://www.rockbox.org/)
