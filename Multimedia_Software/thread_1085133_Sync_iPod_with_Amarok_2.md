### Post by duffmckagan on 2009-03-06
I haven't yet used Amarok 2.0 but if KDE is mounting your iPod properly then you might need to set it up in Amarok first. 

Go to Settings > Configure Amarok > Media Devices > Apple iPod

You can do this provided your iPod is already mounted.
