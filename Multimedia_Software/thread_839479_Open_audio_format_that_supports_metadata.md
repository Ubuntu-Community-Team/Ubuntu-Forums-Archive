### Post by dsm iv tr on 2008-06-24
Yes, Vorbis will work for it.

You can also add arbtrary tags other than Track #, Artist, Title, etc. with both of those formats. Check out something like "Ex Falso" (avilable in repos) for fast tag editing.

Afterthought: for that matter, mp3's "ID3V2" tag format will also work. I just don't like mp3 personally, for a variety of reasons.
