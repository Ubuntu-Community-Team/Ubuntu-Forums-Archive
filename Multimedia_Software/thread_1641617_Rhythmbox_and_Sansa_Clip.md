### Post by tommcd on 2010-12-09
I have a Sandisk Sansa Fuze. 
If your Sansa Clip shows up in Nautilus, then you can just use Nautilus to copy music to it.
I normally just use the terminal to copy music to my Fuze, but if you prefer a GUI, then using Nautilus to copy music files to your Sansa Clip will work fine. You don't need to use Rhythmbox to do this.
I never use Rythmbox, and I can easily copy music to my Fuze in any linux distro.

Have you tried copying files to your Clip with Nautilus?

Write back if you need more help.
