### Post by Lonnie82 on 2010-01-10
Ok, so I fugured it out.

Here's how I did it if anybody runs into the same problem: Run Mplayer and right click the down in the right corner of the control panel. Choose preferences -> video and hightlight the "xv" option. This has to be done in the player window and will not work in the online screening window.
