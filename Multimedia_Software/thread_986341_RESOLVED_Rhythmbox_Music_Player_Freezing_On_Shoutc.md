### Post by jpongin on 2008-11-18
After much frustration of having Rhythmbox freeze on random Shoutcast streams at work and embarrasing me as the unofficial office DJ, I finally resolved this issue.  Hopefully this will help other people who are experiencing the same problems.

Open Terminal:
```
yourterminal:$ sudo apt-get update
```
```
yourterminal:$ sudo apt-get install amarok
```

From your menu, go to Applications => Sound & Video => Amorok.

ENJOY!
