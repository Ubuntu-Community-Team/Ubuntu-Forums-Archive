### Post by dumitru on 2012-02-29
I wanted to know your opinions about these two great applications.

In my opinion, VLC is a good player, but it doesn't have the logical (for me) functions which SMPlayer has. For example: 
- VLC doesn't remember the last position of the movie; 
- in order to move forward and backward you need to press shortcuts and not just Left or Right key. 
- in SMPlayer it is easier to load subtitles due to the better toolbar. 

In a nutshell, for my personal use, VLC has to be customized to get the usable and default functions of SMPlayer. Are there any other pros and cons?

### Post by Linuxisfast on 2012-03-01

VLC 2 is shaping up nicely, also VLC does encoding, radio, TV, streaming and more which would need separate programs, its truly a Swiss knife of media players around.
