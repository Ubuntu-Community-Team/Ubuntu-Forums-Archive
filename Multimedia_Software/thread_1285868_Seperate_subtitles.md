### Post by axel206 on 2009-10-10
Since your DVD player doesn't support xvid/divx video files with external subtitles you must convert your video to DVD format.

There are a few good programs for this job.

Read this [How to author a DVD using 2ManDVD in Linux](http://www.my-guides.net/en/content/view/170/26) and [How to create a custom DVD using DeVeDe](http://www.my-guides.net/en/content/view/75/26/).
