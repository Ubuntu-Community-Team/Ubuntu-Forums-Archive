### Post by Zero Gun on 2013-01-19
Go to [http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/Pages/index.aspx]("http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/Pages/index.aspx"), and look at the notebook drivers for the Radeon HD 6xxxM series. I had the same problem on my AMD Radeon HD 6570. You should have an AMD 6770M card in your laptop if I'm not mistaken. AMD just so happens to have released new drivers for Linux that are compatible with Ubuntu 12.10. Don't download the beta drivers, download Catalyst 13.1.
