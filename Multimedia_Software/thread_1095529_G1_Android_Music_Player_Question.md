### Post by dartmusic on 2009-03-13
I'm having trouble easily transferring playlists from ANY music player app (Rhythmbox, Banshee and Amarok specifically) to my G1.  The songs that the playlist describes transfer fine, but the playlist file (the m3u) does not copy to the G1.  I can do it manually (exporting), but playlists from Rhythmbox and Banshee are immediately deleted by Android.  Playlists exported from Amarok are truncated in the music player, meaning that the m3u file STILL shows all the tracks that I put into the playlist, but viewing and playing the playlist from the music player app only shows a few songs.  

I HAVE been able to work around this a bit by plugging in the G1, opening Amarok and manually re-creating the playlist from the files ON the G1 and then saving the playlist onto the G1.  This, obviously, is clunky and duplication of effort and completely unacceptable when you want to have multiple playlists on your G1.

I'd love to hear how others are managing their music on the G1.
