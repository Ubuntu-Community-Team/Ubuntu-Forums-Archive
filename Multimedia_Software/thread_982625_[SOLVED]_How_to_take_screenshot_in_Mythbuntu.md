### Post by sofasurfer on 2008-11-14
I got it.
You edit the main menu to contain "gnome-screenshot". You open "screenshot" and then open Mythbuntu backend or frontend or whatever. Then you use "alt+tab" to switch from Mythbuntu to "screenshot". Screenshot must be on top of Mythbuntu. Now take your screenshot.
