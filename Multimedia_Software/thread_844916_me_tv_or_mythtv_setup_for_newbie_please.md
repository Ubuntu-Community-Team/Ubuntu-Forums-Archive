### Post by dersk on 2008-06-30
The best place to start is the MythTV wiki: [http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Main_Page](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Main_Page)

It's a good idea as well to google around for setup directions for Oz, since different countries use different channel designations and different schedule grabbers.

The basic process is:
- Get Ubuntu to recognize your video cards
- Set up the video cards in mythtv_setup
- Set up your sources (e.g., a cable input) in mythtv_setup
- Associate the cards with the sources
- Set up your channels

It's really pretty easy if you take it step by step. Just start by installing the mythtv packages, and run mythtv_setup and it'll walk you through most of it. 

Good luck!
