### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-11-27
On the assumption that the process is a very cpu intensive one, is it likely that overheating may be the cause of the shutdowns?  Years ago I had a machine with a bad cpu heat sink and it would do exactly what you are seeing at the first opportunity.  It was eventually sorted out with some new thermal paste between the cpu and the heat sink.  Have a look at the air vents and make sure they are clear.  It may just help.
