### Post by TheFu on 2015-02-22
[https://trac.ffmpeg.org/wiki/EncodingForStreamingSites](https://trac.ffmpeg.org/wiki/EncodingForStreamingSites)

* camera / microphone
* PC able to stream
* network bandwidth able to push sufficient bits upstream.

Most people also get a separate microphone for better audio quality. Vlogcasters push reviews of this hardware all the time.  Might be good to go through some of those for recommendations.
