### Post by dj-toonz on 2009-10-25
If you haven't fixed it, right mouse click on Audacious & goto preferences , audio & in the current audio plug in , select the drop down box & choose ALSA output plugin (-ng) then select close & bobs your uncle, if it still doesn't work, restart Audacious 

Hope that helps
