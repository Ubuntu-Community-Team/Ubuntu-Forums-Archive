### Post by LuridCinema on 2010-04-14
you can do it in Cinelerra. it is a more advanced NLE and has a learning curve. I have been learning it for several months now and still have much to learn.... you can do the credits by simply pasting the text into gimp and creating a straight forward image w/ the text, then put it into cinelerra and add the perspective effect .. viola ! star wars credits. there is an article on how to create smooth credits on cinelerra on the net.. just google it. all you need to do is add the perspective effect in your NLE
