### Post by xzero1 on 2009-11-10
If your card can do UVD2, then VC1,H.264 video hardware acceleration is here. I have tested this with Karmic 64 bit on my 4770 card and it works well -- video is smoother and does not have the washed out look of textured xv acceleration. The driver also works on later model Nvidia cards.

See [http://www.phoronix.com/forums/showthread.php?t=19983]("http://www.phoronix.com/forums/showthread.php?t=19983")
