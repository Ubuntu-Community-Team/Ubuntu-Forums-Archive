### Post by Old_Man_Unix on 2010-10-30
You need to go to your TV's menu and choose the option to have the audio always played through the TV speakers.

If you don't know how to use the menu, please refer to the documentation  for your TV.  If you don't have the documentation for some reason, you  can always download it from the vendors site.

Unfortunately, some TVs will disable that option if you use the HDMI *output*.  If that's the case, you are out of luck.  Again, this will be covered in the documentation for your TV.

This really doesn't have anything to do with Ubuntu or even with your computer - it is  how your TV is configured.

### Post by marin123 on 2010-10-30
you go to System -> Preferences -> Sound and in Output tab check right output (HDMI), to get back audio to laptop speakers, check laptop speakers... if this dont do the trick, go to hardware tab and disable  sound card (just leave hdmi)...
