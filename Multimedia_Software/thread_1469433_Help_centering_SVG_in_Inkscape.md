### Post by Jose Catre-Vandis on 2010-05-02
No real easy way. I used some geometry. Your outer shapes align with a hexagon, so I drew one to get their alignment right, then horizontally centred the triangle to the hexagon. Had to manually/by eye centre the triangle horizontally though, but use of another shape will probably help with that. You could also draw some guides, or draw a shape then convert that shape to guides. If you do not know, press the ALT key with arrow keys for fine movement of objects for manual adjustment.
