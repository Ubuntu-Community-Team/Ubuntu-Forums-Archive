### Post by Boondoklife on 2010-06-06
I wrote a small nautilus script to do exactly what you are trying to do. Drop it in your ~/.gnome2/nautilus-scripts folder and mark it as executable. After that just right click on the video file and goto scripts -> convert-to-dvd.

here are a few links:
Ubuntu One: [file]("http://ubuntuone.com/p/6GF/")
Dropbox: [file]("http://dl.dropbox.com/u/4804818/scripts/nautilus-scripts/convert-to-dvd")
