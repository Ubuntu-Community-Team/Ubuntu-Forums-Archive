### Post by wallywally on 2009-07-26
Hurrah!

For (my) future reference I needed to open VLC and go to Media > Streaming then choose Network with protocol: MMS and address: orfcblivewm.fplive.net/orfcblive-live/radio_english

Then tick Stream Output Play Locally.
