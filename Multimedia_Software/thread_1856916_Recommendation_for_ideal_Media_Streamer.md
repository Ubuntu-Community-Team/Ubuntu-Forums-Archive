### Post by 2F4U on 2011-10-09
If you have a mixture of PC (I guess you mean Windows) and Mac you should look into Samba. I have Ubuntu server running on an older PC and it works great. You can mount (map on Windows) folders from your server on your local machine, so that it seems as if these folders are local. You can also point iTunes toward the server directories where your music is stored.
