### Post by Chronon on 2010-12-24
Some media players (such as Amarok) can sync to any UMS device.  I loathe any portable player that locks you into using a particular application to sync.  I mostly end up just drag-and-dropping anyway.

SanDisk has good offerings that support MSC mode (i.e. they can act as UMS devices) and support Ogg Vorbis and FLAC out of the box.  Several of these can also run Rockbox.

Cowon players tend to be reviewed well too, but I don't have any firsthand experience with them.
