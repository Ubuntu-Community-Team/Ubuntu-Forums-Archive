### Post by freackout on 2011-05-08
i had simple problem mine is 64 bit system, ubuntu 9.10

i simply installed removed and reinstalled pulseaudio, and gnome-alsamixer

ie for gnome-alsamixer

sudo apt-get remove --purge gnome-alsamixer
sudo apt-get install gnome-alsamixer

open up gnome alsa mixer in the menu and adjust the settings on more than one bar, same goes for pulse (ie right click the volume /prefrences pick output 5.1 ect).

dunno where me sound went but its back now :-)
its actualy a 7.1 system but happy with 5.1 for now as only got 5.1 speaker setup though card is 7.1 capable.
