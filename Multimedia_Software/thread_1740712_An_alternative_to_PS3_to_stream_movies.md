### Post by Joe of loath on 2011-04-27
A media centre PC. If you can find a PC that was made after about 2004, it can cope with streaming movies in anything up to 720p. You'll need a more modern PC for 1080p.

Our media centre is an old Pentium 4 HT office workstation (found for free of course). It fits in neatly on top of the PVR.

### Post by Ghost_Mazal on 2011-04-28
Output options is important too (from the pc to the tv)

For example , Does the tv have HDMI-in ? Then the PC needs a card with HDMI out. Does the tv only have RCA-in ? Then the pc needs a card with something like s-video to rca and will also then need seperate audio.

See what I mean.
