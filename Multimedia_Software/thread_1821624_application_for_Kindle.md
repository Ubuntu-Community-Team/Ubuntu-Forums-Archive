### Post by Hughze on 2011-10-10
Intstall Chromium.  It should be in your software center under internet.  Go to your amazon account via Chromium and install Kindle Cloud Reader.  You'll be able to read your kindle books through your Chromium browser.  Amazon doesn't accept Mozilla so you have to use Chromium.  After the cloud reader installation, all you have to do is run Chromium and click the cloud reader button and there you are,  right on your kindle home page just like it would be on on your pc reader  in windows, only you don't seem to have to download any books.
