### Post by cotcot on 2009-01-01
[[COLOR="Red"]Here[/COLOR]]("http://www.linuxprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=HP-OfficeJet_6200") is the driver you need. Check in synaptic if it is installed. (it is normally installed by default)
Go to [COLOR="Red"]http://localhost:631/[/COLOR] with your browser (firefox, opera, whatever). There you can configure the printer.

Alternatively you can use [COLOR="Red"]--> system --> printing[/COLOR] as well to configure the printer.
