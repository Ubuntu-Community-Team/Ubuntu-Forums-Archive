### Post by lovinglinux on 2008-09-16
You can preview, but is not Deluge that will do that. In the torrent list > right click the torrent you want to preview and select "open". If your player supports playing unfinished files it will play. It's a good idea to set Deluge to prioritize the first and last pieces of the file being downloaded.
