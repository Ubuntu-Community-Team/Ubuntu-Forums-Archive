### Post by lykeion on 2010-10-02
There's no magic effect it's just increased Brightness. 
Gimp > Colors > Brightness-Contrast

If you want to mimic it in detail you could take screenshots of the preview when hovered over and not and compare them.
Applications > Accessories > Take screenshot (set delay to a couple of seconds)
Then take screenshots, save and open them in Gimp.
With these as reference you can find out exactly how much you'll need to increase the brightness.
