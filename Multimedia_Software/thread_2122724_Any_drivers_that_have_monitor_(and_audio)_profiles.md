### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-03-06
I used to use the restricted AMD drivers, but then, even with my new card, they dropped driver support ... so I can only comment based on prior experience.

While AMD does, in fact, provide Linux drivers for the newest cards, in my experience, they do not always provide the same full set of features for the Linux drivers that they do for the Windows drivers. 

What I would suggest, is that you go to the Phoronix forums, where they do a LOT of detailed AMD driver testing and diagnostics, and post the question there.  Folks may be able to tell you if the Linux drivers do what you want, or a way to make that happen.

Good Luck
