### Post by radox1912 on 2009-11-03
don't know if this is the right section, just wanted to share my solution
i was just having this annoying problem with my dvd-rom, that produced sound to my headphones. i was desperate, because the volume-control panel got changed in ubuntu 9.10 (in 9.04 it is easy to turn it off). i don't know if this is obvious to anybody, but it wasn't for me. :) But i found a solution.

just open up a terminal a write:

alsamixer

then navigate with cursor keys to the cd column and lower it down to zero, and you're done.
