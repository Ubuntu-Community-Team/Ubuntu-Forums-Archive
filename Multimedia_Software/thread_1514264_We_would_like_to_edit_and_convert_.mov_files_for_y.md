### Post by steveneddy on 2010-06-20
I believe YouTube will accept .mov files. I upload .mov files all the time to YouTube.

Try PiTiVi for editing. It should be installed by default in 10.04.

Look in Applications --> Sound and Video --> Pitivi

EDIT:

As far as capturing a YouTube video - just watch the video and open the /tmp folder and you will see it there as a Flash file. Simply drag it out to your desktop and there you go. No tricks and so silly software. I just wait until it is finished downloading from the site and drag it over. 

Easy Peasy
