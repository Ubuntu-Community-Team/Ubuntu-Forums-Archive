### Post by Calerid on 2011-10-14
Well a simple solution, though time consuming if multiple albums are present.Edit their meta-data. change them to the same name, all the album artist's and the Album name. It will then combine them.

### Post by Matt Pellegrini on 2011-10-14
> **Calerid said:**
> Well a simple solution, though time consuming if multiple albums are present.Edit their meta-data. change them to the same name, all the album artist's and the Album name. It will then combine them.

This is correct, check that everything matches. Caps is important as is the placement of spaces. It's not ideal that banshee can't just merge albums by drag and drop or something that would pick one of the differences and use that for the new total album but it's all we have for now.

I should point out if you don't know what meta-data is and that term sounds confusing.. right click the track in banshee and select properties then make sure this info matches in both albums and banshee will start seeing them as the same album. Hope this helps.
