### Post by gregphil on 2011-10-29
The recent 'upgrade' in Kubuntu 11.10 broke my sound system, (was working at freshly installed 11.10, no sound at 11.10 + suggested package upgrades).

I FINALLY figured out that I could boot into a Ubuntu install, look at the AlsaMixer settings that work for six speaker audio, and then boot back into Kubuntu and use those settings (53 of them!).  This worked and I now have six speaker audio (not sure if it is decoding 5.1) and it sounds good.  It seems the recent Kubuntu alsa upgrades attempted to configure my mixer and failed for the emu10k1 / E-MU 1616m external dock combination.  Below I show the alsamixer settings that work for my system (under Ubuntu 11.10 or Kubuntu 11.10)

Good Luck!
