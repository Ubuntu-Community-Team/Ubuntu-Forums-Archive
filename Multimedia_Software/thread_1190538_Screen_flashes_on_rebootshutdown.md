### Post by angelsguitar on 2009-06-23
Well, I finally solved it by reinstalling. Here's what I did:

[LIST=1]
[*]Reformated and installed Xubuntu 9.04
[*]After reboot, installed LXDE
[*]Uninstalled XFCE and the xubuntu desktop packages
[*]Rebooted again
[/LIST]

So, basically, I installed Xubuntu, installed LXDE then removed every package of XFCE. Now the intel graphics card is working alright. I'd still would like to know what was causing it, though, but anyway it's solved at last.
