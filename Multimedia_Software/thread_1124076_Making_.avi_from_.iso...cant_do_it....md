### Post by Cybie257 on 2009-04-13
An ISO image is a CD/DVD image and not a Video file. If you are trying to convert the video within the CD/DVD image, you need to extract the data out of the image. 

With Ubuntu, you can mount the .iso image by right-clicking and selecting mount image, then just like any drive, go into it and you will see the Data (video) file(s).
