### Post by sports.car.guy on 2010-01-28
No matter what you do, a standard definition television will not get any better quality converting a DVI signal. If you googled a bit about the topic, you would know that. S-Video is the absolute best quality picture you can get on a standard definition television set. Then there is how low of a resolution a TV runs at as well. All of this is well documented online, you just need look.

You want a better quality, you are going to have to upgrade to high definition television.

Not trying to be a jerk here but, if you had even taken 5 minutes of your life and googled, you would know this. This forum is here to help after you try to help yourself. This subject is well covered online and you just need to look.

Please research something first, then if you can't find the answer ask out here.
