### Post by hairy one on 2010-04-22
For those that just want to get their Digital tuners up and working as quickly as possible use Kaffeine!

For some time (over two years) I have looked at Linux as my OS of choice but digital tv kept me linked to Windows. Just install the software and within no time I could view and record tv. 

Enter Kaffeine Just as with Windows just install, view and record.

I have tried so many times to install Myth when all I wanted was my computer with the ability to watch and record a bit of tv, not loose my computer to tv.

kaffeine has its own scanning tool built in and is an awesome application. Just open it and follow the prompts to set things up.
