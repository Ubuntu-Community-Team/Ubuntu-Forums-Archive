### Post by Wheel_nut on 2008-08-14
Fire GL was the chipset name when it was developed by IBM Microelectronics in the late 90's. The Chipset and its IPR was sold to ATI soon after the turn of the decade and became the basis of the ATI Radeon chipsets. The Chipset signature is being identified by Linux as its original incarnation.
