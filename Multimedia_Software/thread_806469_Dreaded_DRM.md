### Post by tamoneya on 2008-05-25
since you are in the US there is no fully legal way to de drm music since the DMCA says that any copyright protectiong(DRM) circumvention is illegal even if you are doing it with the purpose of breaking the copyright itself.  

Breaking copyright is illegal.
Breaking the protection of copyright even if not breaking the copyright is also unfortunately illegal. 

Now there are also fair use laws which contradict the DMCA in some ways so it is a legal grey area.  But that means that by definition you cannot have a fully legal DRM removal.
