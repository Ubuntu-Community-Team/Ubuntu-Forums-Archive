### Post by ohgodnotanother1 on 2009-02-08
Did you try setting it to "ISO8859-15" manually?

You can do this in the VLC Preferences:
"Input / Codecs"
...."Other codecs"
........"Subtitles" --> Subtitles text encoding: ISO8859-15

Click "Save" and that should do.
