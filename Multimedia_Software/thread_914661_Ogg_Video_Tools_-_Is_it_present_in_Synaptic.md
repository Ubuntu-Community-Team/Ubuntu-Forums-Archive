### Post by yorn_sn on 2009-01-06
No, Ogg Video Tools are not included into ubuntu until know. You can find rpms for openSuse at [http://packman.links2linux.de/package/oggvideotools/](http://packman.links2linux.de/package/oggvideotools/) or packages for fedora testing. 

It is tested under ubuntu as well and the source can be loaded from:
[http://dev.streamnik.de/files.html](http://dev.streamnik.de/files.html)

just use "configure && make && sudo make install"
