### Post by sgosnell on 2009-09-25
To change the filename, just navigate there in Nautilus, right-click on the file, and select Rename, just like in Windows.  If you want to edit the tags (metadata), vlc or most other players will do that.  It's in the Tools menu for vlc.  In Rhythmbox, right-click on the file in the window, select Properties, and edit as you like.  Other players should do it one way or another.

About 10 seconds on Google found [Easytag](http://easytag.sourceforge.net/), which looks like a nice app, but I haven't tried it.
