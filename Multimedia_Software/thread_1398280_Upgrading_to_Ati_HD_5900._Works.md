### Post by markbuntu on 2010-02-04
It works but only with the latest drivers from ati. Do not expect it to work with the driver in jockey (hardware drivers). Be sure to remove the nvidia driver completely and go back to the generic VESA driver before installing the ati card. There is a thread here about changing from nvidia to ati.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1373813](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1373813)
