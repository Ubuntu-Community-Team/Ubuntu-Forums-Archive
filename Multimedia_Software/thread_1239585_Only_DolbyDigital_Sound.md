I just reinstalled ubuntu cause i broke the sound by installing the latest VLC. I only get 0.9.9a. I finally figured out what broke the sound. After I did this: [http://ubuntumanual.org/posts/195/install-all-new-vlc-1-0-in-ubuntu-jaunty-intrepid-hardy](http://ubuntumanual.org/posts/195/install-all-new-vlc-1-0-in-ubuntu-jaunty-intrepid-hardy) I got vlc to work.

Beware of a big problem here!!! You will get updates after you've installed vlc. DO NOT install them!! Go back to synaptics and click the  Repositories you've just added away. Otherwise the sound will be broken!
