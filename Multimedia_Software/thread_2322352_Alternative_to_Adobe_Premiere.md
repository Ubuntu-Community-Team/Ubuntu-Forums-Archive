### Post by marseille2 on 2016-04-27
There's a lot of choices but for power editing, I stick to LIGHTWORKS <https://www.lwks.com/>. Natron is probably the hottest compositor <http://natron.fr/>.

The one thing about Lightworks is that even though they offer a free version, IMO it's worth getting the cheap, no-commitment, month to month subscription for the better resolution. Free version is 720p max.

If you must absolutely do the open source / totally free scene ... then Cinelerra is a nice pro editor with 4k capability. <http://cinelerra.org/2015/> ... The difference really boils down to interface and workflow, as well as hardware and codec issues. BTW... Blender is a nice alternative for NATRON, but it's not for every classical editor.
