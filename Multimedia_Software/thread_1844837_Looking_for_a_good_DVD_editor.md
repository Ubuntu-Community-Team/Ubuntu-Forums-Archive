### Post by dniMretsaM on 2011-09-15
Well if you just want to split the videos up, just about any editor would do. Here's a small list:
Avidemux - [http://fixounet.free.fr/avidemux/](http://fixounet.free.fr/avidemux/)
Cinelerra - [http://heroinewarrior.com/cinelerra.php](http://heroinewarrior.com/cinelerra.php)
KDEnlive - [http://www.kdenlive.org/](http://www.kdenlive.org/) 
LiVES - [http://lives.sourceforge.net/](http://lives.sourceforge.net/)
OpenShot - [http://www.openshotvideo.com/](http://www.openshotvideo.com/)
VLMC - [http://www.videolan.org/vlmc/](http://www.videolan.org/vlmc/)  

These are some of the more popular ones. I have never used anything but I've heard good things about VLMC. And it's made by the same people as VLC, so that might be a good indications of quality.
