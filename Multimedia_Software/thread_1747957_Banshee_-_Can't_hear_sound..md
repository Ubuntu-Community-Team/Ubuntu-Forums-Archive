### Post by talofo on 2011-05-03
Thanks. For some reason, I believe after an update my sound settings were changed. And this could be the cause.

In order to solve this:

System -> Preferences -> Sound -> Output -> (choose the appropriate one).

It was pointing to some Radeon HD 3600 hdmi settings, but now is corrected to "internal audio".

The weird stuff was that, all other were playing well, like the doube click, or the over over some mp3 file, being Banshee the only who complained. 

Oh well, solved now anyway.
