### Post by adam814 on 2010-02-18
The only thing I could think of would be to adjust the VLC preferences.  I recall one about "frame skip" in particular.  You could try with and without this enabled to see if either gives you better performance.

What kind of videos are these exactly?  It might be worth checking whether it's any combination of codecs at 1080p that skip or just one or two.

If nothing helps it's probably hardware related.  Your specs don't look too bad, if it's maxing out your memory that might be a place to start (at least run a few less apps in the background to free up more).  Otherwise the two biggest hardware bottlenecks I've encountered are slow hard drives and a slow FSB (both happen pretty often in laptops actually).
