### Post by qr25de on 2010-12-30
I hate to burst your bubble but .rem files on a BlackBerry Smartphone mean that the files were taken when content protection or encryption was (and probably still is) enabled on your BlackBerry Smartphone.

There is NO way around this. You would have to be on a Windows PC running Roxio Media Manager for it to decrypt the .rem files and even sometimes that does not work.

I would suggest disabling content protection / encryption. Any files that were encrypted, will only be viewable on your BlackBerry Smartphone since it contains the decryption key.

Any new videos/pictures you take with encryption turned off will be able to be transferred and played on your computer. Videos will typically be in a 3GP format and playable by VLC and other media players.

If you are unable to turn off the encryption features, you may have this option hard set by an IT Policy (if you use this device in a corporate environment).
