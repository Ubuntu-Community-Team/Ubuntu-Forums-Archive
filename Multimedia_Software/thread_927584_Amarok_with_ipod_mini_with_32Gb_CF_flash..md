### Post by scharkalvin on 2009-02-09
I don't see any reason why you can't use a 32gb cf card in the ipod mini.  Just beware that not all CF cards support the IDE interface mode, and if the card doesn't it won't work.  Also cards slower than about 133x will not perform as well as the original hd when loading files and could 'skip' when playing certain codecs.
