### Post by cotcot on 2009-09-23
> **carfield said:**
> I just install Avidemux and don't know how to do that, I have read [http://www.avidemux.org/admWiki/index.php?title=Main_Page](http://www.avidemux.org/admWiki/index.php?title=Main_Page) , would you point me which part it saying?


Open Avidemux
Load a video clip
At the left side of the avidemux window you see video, audio and format.
Under video you see Copy and shaded you see 'configure' and 'filters'. Click on 'copy' ; a fall down menu lets you select an encoder. Select for instance dvd. This unshades the options 'configure' and 'filters'. Click 'filters'. This opens a menu with all kind of filters. Double click on rotate and select 90 degrees.
