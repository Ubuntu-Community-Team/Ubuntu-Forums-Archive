### Post by manimal347 on 2008-10-12
> **iambodin said:**
> SO there is none ??!! :(

When you view a video in Mplayer in fullscreen mode, you can get to a visual control panel by moving your mouse down towards the middle-bottom of the screen. Among other things, it includes a slider which lets you jump backwards or forwards just like the ones in other video players.

I never do this, however. In Mplayer, your up and down keys make big moves backwards and forwards in a video, and the left and right arrow keys make small jumps. Perfect for one-handed video-watching.
