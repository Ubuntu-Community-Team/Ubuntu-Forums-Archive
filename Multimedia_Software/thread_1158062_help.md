### Post by LoloftheRings on 2009-05-13
Looks like you need the flash player for this, you might want to install flashplugin-nonfree or ubuntu-restricted-extras.
flashplugin-nonfree gives you the flash player, restricted-extras also mp3, dvd playback and other restricted (source is not available) applications.

Installing is easy, just go to Applications -> Add/Remove software, search for either flashplugin-nonfree or ubuntu-restricted-extras and press the 'apply changes' button.
