### Post by kryten35 on 2015-06-07
The problem with openshot is your camera does output AVCHD, but it uses a variant  called AVCHD lite
which uses a less savage video compression than normal AVCHD.So the encoder is different and an editor would have to support it.

The only editor that i know supports  AVCHD lite is Sony vegas,but thats windows only.Not sure about other Linux editors
such as cinelerra,kdenlive and lightworks.Only way to find out is to try them.

So you may end up having to use a transcoder such as Handbrake to convert your footage to standard  MP4  or some other format
that would then work in openshot.


.MTS is mpeg transport stream.
