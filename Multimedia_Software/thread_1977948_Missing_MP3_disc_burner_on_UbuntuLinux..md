### Post by fritsmartfu on 2012-05-11
Dear friends, 

I am not an expert but I faced the same problems: I can not burn MP3 discs who I can play on any player. I was also searching on many places without any result. What I found is that both of you are right, in some way.
MP3 is a digital data compression of music and the compression mode is based on the very protected MP3 codec.
Only when you have paid for the MP3 codec (you have to pay for Windows, (Ubuntu)Linux is free of charge(!)) and you are able to implant your MP3 codec in a handsome program, you will be able to burn universal playable MP3 dics. 

To give some suggestions, Google for "mp3 codec for linux".
Good luck to all of you!
