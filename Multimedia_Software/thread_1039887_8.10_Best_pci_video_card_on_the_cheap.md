### Post by user11 on 2009-01-15
You mean the standard PCI video card and not the PCI-e right? I got an evega 6200 256MB PCI from newegg for $45 (not the one that cariboo907 recomended, that's AGP), but apparently ubuntu/xubuntu doesn't like PCI video cards so much. No matter what I try it goes into low-graphics mode.

 If PCI graphics is your only option, I recommend wNop. It's a puppy linux derivative that has the full compiz-fuzion and graphics drivers pre-installed. It's the only Linux distro I found that works well with PCI video cards, and the best part is that you don't have to spend time getting the drivers to work.
