It could be many things; for example, the audio player in Windows may enable the equalizer to custom settings. 

Try several audio players in Windows for the same music; if they give different results, then it's the audio player that alters the sound to make it sound more pleasing. If all audio players give the same result, then it could be a setting in the audio driver.
