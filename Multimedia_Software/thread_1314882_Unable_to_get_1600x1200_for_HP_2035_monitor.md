### Post by srikesh on 2009-11-23
Good news: My HP 2035 works with my Ubuntu 9.10 at 1600 x 1200 resolution.

Bad news:  I have no idea how it happened.  I am not sure if it was one of the many suggestions that I followed on the internet or some OS updates that fixed the problem.

### Post by kyrealtor on 2011-12-04
HP usually has great Linux support, but apparently not for the HP 2035 monitor. It seems that with some video cards and some newer versions of Ubuntu, you get 1600x1200, but no choice for lower resolutions such as 1280x1024 or 1024x768. Ubuntu 11.04 got me 1600x1200 on my Gateway P-4 2.4 GHz machine.
