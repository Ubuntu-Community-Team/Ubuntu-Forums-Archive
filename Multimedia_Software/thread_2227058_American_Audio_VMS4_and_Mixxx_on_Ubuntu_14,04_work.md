### Post by majk2 on 2014-05-30
[COLOR=#000000]I have recently dumped Window7 off my Alienware M17X R2 as I am tired of all the glitches and unsolicited notice interruptions from 3rd parties and Windows.[/COLOR]
[COLOR=#000000]I must admit the change has been very smooth and I am loving Ubuntu Best decision I ever made.
[/COLOR]
[COLOR=#000000]Even though I am working full time as a technology adviser, I am an old DJ and still do the odd gig for charity. [/COLOR]
[COLOR=#000000]So I recently purchased an American Audio VMS4 controller so that I can have better control over my computer when DJing. 
Well I tried quite a few software packages including those that came with the controller. Now keep in mind that I was using my Alienware with Windows7. I was having no success setting it all up. I had better success when I plugged it into my MacPro desktop machine. But I needed to be mobile and wanted to use my Alienware m17X laptop for DJing. Well to cut a long and tiring story short I found Mixxx on my Mac and when I found it also runs on Linux I thought why not!..                                ...I dumped the Windows OS and installed Ubuntu. Well it works out the box no setting up needed. I just picked the correct corresponding theme and off you go. So I am just looking for other who use an American Audio VMS4 controller with Mixxx on Ubuntu 14.04 to make contact with and exchange ideas.[/COLOR]
