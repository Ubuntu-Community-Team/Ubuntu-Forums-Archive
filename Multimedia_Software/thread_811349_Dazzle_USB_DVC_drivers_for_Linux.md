### Post by U2XS on 2008-08-18
Dazzle didn't create drivers for its programs in GNU/Linux and it seems to be too new (or too unpopular) for our the open source community to have taken care of it.  For now your two options are to do the conversions in windows/mac and later manipulate the footage or to use [http://www.winehq.com](http://www.winehq.com) to run the official software.  It's not the best resolve, but it's what we have.  In the future you may consider Hauppauge products who do take GNU/Linux into consideration.
