### Post by howefield on 2010-07-04
Most CD burning applications will have a "create audio disk" option, eg, Brasero > Audio Project > Add mp3 files and burn, or for K3b, it is K3B > New Audio CD Project, add mp3 files and burn.

The software will convert the mp3s to the correct CD wav format.
