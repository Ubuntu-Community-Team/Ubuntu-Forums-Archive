### Post by mastermindg on 2009-11-28
Simplest method that I've found is using SFTP:

sftp://root@itouch/var/mobile/Library/Downloads

transfer any IPA files to this path.

Download Installous (Google It!) thru Cydia, click on downloads and click on any App to Install.
