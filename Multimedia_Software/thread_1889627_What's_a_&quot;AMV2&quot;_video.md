### Post by M_Mynaardt on 2011-12-03
Right!  The solution was posted to another thread of mine; and *OldTimer777* gave me the direction to get fixed.

And here be the solution if anyone else needs to fix this in Firefox:
1. Open Firefox
2. Go to; [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/)
3. Download Flash Aid 2.2.1 (or whatever version it is)
4. There will now be flash button on the right by the bar; open it
5. Use the wizard to set up said Flash *STUFF*
6. Bob's your uncle!
