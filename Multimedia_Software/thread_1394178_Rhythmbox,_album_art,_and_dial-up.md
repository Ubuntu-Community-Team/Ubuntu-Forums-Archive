### Post by Bartender on 2010-01-30
Is there a way to ask Rhythmbox to STOP trying to get album art?  We're on dial-up, and it's convenient to let Rhythmbox get track info when archiving music CD's.  But when I try to play any track, Rhythmbox hogs our pathetic connection trying to get album art.  It never seems to find the album art anyway...

I haven't been able to find a setting to turn that off.

---

### Post by amac777 on 2010-01-30
Go to Edit->Plugins and then uncheck the first plugin called "Cover art".

---

### Post by Bartender on 2010-01-30
That was easy!!  Thanks, amac!  

I'd ignored the Plugins menu, assuming that was for plugins manually downloaded by the user.
