Sorry to be an attention whore, but I've been seeing a lot of people with video trouble and wonder if more people notice this correlation:

First, I have an HD 3870 with Hardy, binary ATI drivers from hardware driver manager. My sound card is realtek integrated, which play music and flash fine.

**Here's the problem:**

When trying to play full screen games (aka Nexuiz) the screen is choppy, like a little blinking or frameskip every second or so. I soon noticed it correlated with the beat of the soundtrack in the background. 

This ONLY happens in Ubuntu, not Xubuntu or Kubuntu.

I wonder if this has to do with the new sound server, PulseAudio, in Ubuntu? I didn't try using GNOME in Xubuntu, so I don't know if it's a GNOME thing or and Ubuntu thing. In any case, there is a definite tie to sound and video.
