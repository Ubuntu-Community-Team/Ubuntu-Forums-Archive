This forum NEEDS a werewolf game. Badly. So, here I am to the rescue! :p

I aim to get 20 players. Signups will close on the 26th if we don't get 20 players by then.

Werewolf games are simple. Here are the rolls (which I assign randomly)

Townie: One townie will be killed each round by a werewolf in the nightime. Then, during daytime, the townies will vote to lynch a person if they think s/he is a werewolf. I will reveal the results, then the cycle repeats.

Werewolf: During the day-time, the werewolves pm each other deciding on who to kill. Majority vote wins. If it's a tie, or all the werewolves agree not to, then no-one will be killed that night.

Seer (15+ people): A seer will pm me a person name during the daytime, and I'll respond with weather they're a werewolf or not.

Each day lasts two real life days.
