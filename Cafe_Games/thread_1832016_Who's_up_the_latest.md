Game Goal:
Find out who is awake the latest time by allowing users to post their local time, in an attempt to say that they've been up the latest :-)

Eample:
Bob: 1000
Sally: 1200
Fred: 1504

Rules:
- You MUST use your LOCAL time (NO MAKING TIMES UP)
- 0000 is the reset time. If someone is on 0000 they trump everyone and the clock is reset.
- 0000-0005 is acceptable also for resetting the clock. Time MUST go by an atomically correct internet server. So we may judge that you are correctly posting the minutes.
- At clock reset any given time is acceptable
- If it is noticed that a certain user is cheating, the next posting user may "(Override post1 post2)" where post 1 & 2 reference thread numbers of the user making time claims that contradict each other.
- You may NOT claim to be traveling (All times are to be referenced from the point of where you live.)

EDIT: You may also leave comments to entice your opponents. Stay friendly :-)
