In the English language the letters RSTLNE are the most used letters of the alphabet, so this game is to not to use those letters in a sentence.
The name of this game comes from the secondary title of the game:
The no RSTLNE game, take out the T and the E you get H, take out the N in no, you get o, and take out the E in game and it all spells Hogam!
