[size=6]The Acronym Game[/size]

[size=4]The Rules[/size]

Start out with AA, then work through the alphabet continuosly, as if you were counting in letters (e.g. AA, AB, AC ..., AZ, BA, BB, ... ... ZX, ZY, ZZ) Once we get to ZZ, go back to the beginning, adding a third letter to the mix (AAA)

Simple words need not count, but they can if you wish (especially when we get into the triple-letter acronyms).  Simple words may include: the, and or if in, of, etc.

Post as many variants as you want, some will be easy, some will be harder. The more you post, the funnier some posts may be.

Please remember to stay within the [Forum CoC](http://ubuntuforums.org/index.php?page=policy) with your posts.

[size=4]Lets Begin![/size]
