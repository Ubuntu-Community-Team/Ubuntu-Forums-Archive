### Post by GepettoBR on 2009-03-23
This is a simple game. I'll start with a sentence and each consecutive poster will make a new sentence by changing, removing or adding only one word to the sentence. Changing a verbal form doesn't count, even if you have to add an auxiliary verb (jump>jumped>was jumping>shall jump) so you can do that and change another word. Nouns that can also be verbs can be verbified without counting as well (like "fish" or "fly")

Every new page, you can choose to start from scratch with a new sentence or continue the current one.

[quote=example]Poster1: Sally loved strawberries
Poster2: Sally loves dancing
Poster3: Sally loves break-dancing
Poster4: Sally loves breaking things[/quote]

I'll start:

**I always take my dog for a walk in the park**
