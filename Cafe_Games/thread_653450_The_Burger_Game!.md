### Post by Epic Plecostomus on 2007-12-29
Ok, the game is simple.   Someone posts a phrase or a slogan or such and the next poster has to decide what ingredients would make that into a burger.

Example:  

I post "The Cheeseburger"

The next poster would say somthing like "A slice of meat with a piece of cheese on it with lettuce and tomato served on a sesame-seed bun"

Then that poster would come up with his own phrase:

"Windows Vista Burger"

And the next poster would reply: "Two slices of **** and fail with a side of DRM served on a bun that you don't own but merely license for the duration."

and so on...  the object is to be as whimsical and silly as possible.  The sky is the limit here.
