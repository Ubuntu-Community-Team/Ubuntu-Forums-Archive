### Post by _sluimers_ on 2009-01-31
I like stories, but the "The Finish The Story Thread" doesn't seem like it would ever create a coherent story. So I propose a hopefully more of a chance of making a more complete story, despite posters trying to take it into so many directions.

Unless you're the new story opener, the last and first sentence of the story is already known. The story opener then decides how many 
and what minimum and maximum amount of words can be used per post.
The next poster will copy/paste the story so far, otherwise the new poster will more likely read only the last post and disregard the rest of the story.

Writing suggestions/comments/questions for he next poster is also desirable as it might steer the next poster into your direction.


Here is my idealized example:

_sluimers_ writes:

Ortega passes the ball..

5 posts to go..

HE SCORES!!

min words: 5
max words: 20
num posts: 5
