### Post by geo909 on 2008-12-02
In this game I mention where I come from and another country I have been.
The person who replies should be from the second country mentioned above.
The chain continues like this. For example;

"I come from Italy and I've been in Holland"
"I come from Holland and I've been a student in Germany"
"I come from Germany and I've been as a tourist to the U.S."

Probably we will shortly run out of countries, but it will be interesting to see how many nationalities there are around. It can be impressive if we have a long chain. Repetitions are permitted :)

Ok, let's start.

I come from Greece but I study in Canada now.
