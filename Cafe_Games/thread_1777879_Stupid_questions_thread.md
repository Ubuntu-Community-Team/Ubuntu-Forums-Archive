Rhetorical question - a question that has no real answer and is not asked because an answer is sought. sometimes rhetorical questions are meaningful, sometimes they simply waste your time. i thought it would be fun to post questions that are silly and have no real answer. an example would be:* "If a pig is upset, is it disgruntled?"*, or *"How can 'overlook' and 'oversee' mean entirely opposite things?"*. there is no answer to either of these questions, but they are fun to ask and think about. if you dont know one, be creative and make one up! :P

Let the rhetoric begin!
