Sorry guys :-) I couldn't think of a better name for this.

Basically, someone says a category: (Animal) and everyone else has to alphabetically stay in the category.

1 - Ape
2 - Bat
3 - Cat

When the letter Z is reached, the next person gets to decide the category and the A word :-)

Have fun and my category is: Fruit

Apple

***EDIT: If you say a word which doesn't fall in category, the user after you must pick up your slack. If a trail of users forget to correct you then, you get a shiny bozo button for pointing it out :-)
