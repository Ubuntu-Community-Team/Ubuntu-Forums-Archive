For all of you who don't know, Helbreath Legion is a Free medieval based MMORPG- with player vs player combat, blacksmithing, mining, fishing, alchemy, guilds, Warriors mages, archery, and many more skills to choose from. Raid barracks or dungeons of the enemy town, (of which there are two to choose, Elvine and Aresden), kill monsters in hunting parties and get Hero Armor from killing Enemies. There is much to offer here, in a Diablo-like game which offers more than just fighting, but if fighting is all you're looking for, then you've come to the right place as well. With our game developers still working, we are currently in Beta Mode, but all progress is kept upon entry into the game. We look forward to new members, and value all players in our growing community. The Story of Helbreath is that there are two towns in eternal combat with eachother, and what separates Helbreath from other MMORPGs is that it is a community effort towards an epic war.
