The object of this game is to post what about you is as far away from the last poster's stats from your.



How far are your stats to the last poster's? This can be by current post number, thanked number, place of origin (Even the Klingon Galaxy can count!), the amount of Albums you have, the number of Groups you belong to, the number of referrals, or the number of contact/"friends" you have on Ubuntu forums. You must state the actual thing you are comparing in your post. The reason is that it may change over time and will cause a lot of confusion.

I live in Montréal. I have currently 86 posts at an average of 0.26 per day. I have two friends/contacts currently. I have thanked 7 times. I have been thanked once. I have 0 referrals listed. I have no Albums. I am not a part of any group. Here we go. Who thinks they are far from me?
