### Post by Phrea on 2010-03-17
Simple game, take out your favorite online translator [[Google](http://translate.google.com/#), [Babel Fish](http://babelfish.yahoo.com/), etc] and translate the sentence posted.
When translating, please keep in mind that you can't translate the sentence back to the original, you have to translate it to another language than the original AND the second translation.

As soon as the sentence is no longer translatable [still, please post ! :D ], post your own !

I'll start:

*While working on the 14th floor, some bricks I had to clear, now to throw them down from such a height was not a good idea.*
