### Post by ki4jgt on 2012-09-18
Rules:
-Each player begins their turn by reading the sentence requirements of the player before them.
- The player must then compose a sentence using only the parts of grammar given in the last post (no more, no less).
- Each set of requirements must contain at least two different grammer classifications.

Example: 2 nouns, 1 verb, and 1 preposition

Dogs hunt at night.

It does not have to be factual.

first turn: 1 verb, 1 noun
