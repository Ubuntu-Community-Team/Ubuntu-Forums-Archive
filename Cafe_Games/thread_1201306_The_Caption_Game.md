Here's how the game works, I post a picture, and then y'all try to write a caption on it. After 24 hours, I will pick the winner, the winner posts the next picture, and then the winner picks the winner after 24 hours, and so on.

[LIST]

[*]All pictures must be redistributed under the same license that they were found under (after captioning) if they are posted in this topic.
[/LIST]

**Note:** Copyright still applies to a picture, even if it is not claimed by the copyright holder.
