No, this has nothing to do with swearing... ;). In fact this is a language play game! You can use any language, say english, binary, spanish... whatever. 

The rules are simple, you translate the previous sentence(s) to another language, then start a new sentence into the language that you translated the previous sentence into. 

Example: (the bold and normal represent two different people)

[B]I would like a new car
[/B]
Je veux un noveaux auto
J'aime ta Ordinateur
