### Post by lespaul_rentals on 2008-03-14
Distro Elimination is a fun way to see what the community's favorite Linux distros are.  How to play?  Simply copy and paste the post immediately before yours, and **add** one point to your favorite distro's score, and **subtract** one point from the distro you would like to vote down.

**Some rules:**

1. Don't post anything other than the running score and your votes.  This will keep the thread from becoming cluttered, while preventing arguements and flamewars from starting.

2. Only vote once, and you can only +1 a single distro, and -1 a single distro.

**Distros up for vote:**

I am taking the distros from DistroWatch's list of [Top Ten Distros]("http://distrowatch.com/dwres.php?resource=major").

Ubuntu
openSUSE
Fedora
Debian
Mandriva
PCLinuxOS
MEPIS
Knoppix
Slackware
Gentoo
FreeBSD

Each distro will start out with 15 points.  When a distro reaches 0, it is removed from the list.

**Example:**

Someone posts the following score:

Ubuntu    15
openSUSE    15
Fedora    15
Debian    15
Mandriva    15
PCLinuxOS    15
MEPIS    15
Knoppix    15
Slackware   15
Gentoo    15
FreeBSD    15

If I want to vote down Mandriva and vote up Ubuntu, I would post as follows:

Ubuntu    15 + 1 = 16
openSUSE    15
Fedora    15
Debian    15
Mandriva    15 - 1 = 14
PCLinuxOS    15
MEPIS    15
Knoppix    15
Slackware   15
Gentoo    15
FreeBSD    15
