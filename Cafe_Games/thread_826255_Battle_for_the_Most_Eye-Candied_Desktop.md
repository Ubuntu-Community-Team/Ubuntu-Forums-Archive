### Post by Th3Professor on 2008-08-05
I don't know if ^v works but you can click on the little square picture (in the reply window) that has a yellow background and a couple gray/grey mountains in the foreground and, what I suppose is, a gray/grey sun in the background. Then link it. Though the better way is to attach the file. In your reply page, scroll down below the actual "reply window" where you type stuff, and down to "Additional Options", click on the button "Manage Attachments". It should let you "browse" to find your files and add them to the post (5 max, I think), then click "upload", after it's done (you'll see it change) scroll down that window (it's a pop-up window) and click "close this window". You'll be back in the normal reply window/page, and can click on "submit reply". :KS:KS:KS:KS:KS
