### Post by PCaddicted on 2011-04-30
Simple really...just tell the person above you that you want them to do something-in Uncle Sam style(see [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uncle_Sam](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uncle_Sam),if you like).Remember to write "I WANT YOU" with uppercase.Type the rest of the sentence under "I WANT YOU".
Example:
"I WANT YOU
to use Linux."
