This is exactly the opposite of the "Thread necromancy game".
In this one, the sooner you post, the more points you get:
                     
                      1 minute since last post= 1000 points
             2 to 15 minutes since last post= 800 points
     16 minutes to 1 hour since last post= 600 points 
                   2 to 6 hours since last post= 400 points
                 7 to 12 hours since last post= 200 points
          13 hours to 1 day since last post= 100 points
         2 days to one week since last post= 10 points
           2 weeks to 1 month since last post= 5 points
              2 months or more since last post= 0 points

Remember to post your number of points.
