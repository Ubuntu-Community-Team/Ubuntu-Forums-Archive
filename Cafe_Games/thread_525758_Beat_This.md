### Post by init1 on 2007-08-18
> **g2g591 said:**
> darn the highest I can get is 37, what is your stratedgy?
I'll make a tutorial.
1. Make a red turret
2. Upgrade the rate twice (when you have enough money)
3. Upgrade the damage to max
4. Make a yellow turret near the red turret. Make sure that it is not in range of the path. 
5. Upgrade the yellow turret's damage to max. 
6. The first combo has been made. Upgrade all of the stats on the red turret to max. But do not affect any more of the yellow turrets stats.

This combo is so powerful, that it will kill enemies even before you can see them. This will get you to at least 100, probably farther.
