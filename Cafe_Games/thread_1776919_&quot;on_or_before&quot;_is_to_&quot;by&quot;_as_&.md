### Post by doas777 on 2011-06-07
I believe the answer is 'from'. in US english however, usually when you have 'from <time>' you follow it with 'until <time>'

"Open from 9:00 until 5:00".

I believe in the UK it may be more common to just use from:
"Open from 9:00"
