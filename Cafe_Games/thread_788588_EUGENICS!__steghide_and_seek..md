### Post by RumorsOfWar on 2008-05-09
The mission: Save your country's people from corporate slavery.
Mr. X was bringing the "Rath tribunal" document to a secret congressional hearing when he disappeared at the airport.  The Rath tribunal document must be recovered!
 Fortunately, Mr X concealed the document in the maze of the internet. 
Our only clue to its whereabouts is this investment chart, and a reference to the Nazi war criminal 'farben'

  Use StegDetect and a search engine to recover the Rath tribunal document.

The most powerful tool is your mind.

### Post by RumorsOfWar on 2008-05-15
BTW, this is a detective game like an Alternate Reality Game, but smaller and simpler. this one has just 3 steps. 
Stegdetect is a small program in the ubuntu repositories, (steGUI may be available, and has a graphic interface.)
The jpeg images in the game have text files embedded in them, if you know the password, stegdetect can extract them, revealing another clue. 
Its kinda fun try it out!

Ya, the subject of the game has political overtones, but that just adds intrigue. These are interesting times after all...
