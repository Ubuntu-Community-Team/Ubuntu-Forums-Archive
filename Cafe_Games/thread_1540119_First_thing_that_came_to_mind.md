### Post by Eisenwinter on 2010-07-27
The purpose of this game is to write the first personal thought that came to your mind when you read the previous poster's thought.

For example:

Poster #1: Man, the sun is really high in the sky.
Poster #2: I love marijuana.
Poster #3: I once met this girl called Roxanne.

and so forth.

The point is to create a seemingly weird conversation, with people not filtering their thoughts.

Write whatever was your initial first independent thought after reading the previous poster's first independent thought.

To kick off:

Chiptune music is really awesome.
