### Post by ki4jgt on 2011-12-16
The rules are simple. Download the rag script below and use it to play the game. Each player takes a word from the previous player's acronym and creates their own from it. (This game is like the acronym game from before except the players don't get to make their acronyms, they get to choose the word that forms their own)

Example:
The player before me used "hate immature malice" as their acronym. I choose "hate" as my own and feed it into the rag. I get hot apples tango and example.
Try to choose the best acronym you can.

If you need help running the python script, simply extract the files (2) into your home directory, open the terminal and type "python rag.py"

Have fun!

First Acronym: rag
