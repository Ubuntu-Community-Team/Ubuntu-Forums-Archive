Alright, I need to get my post-count up so I can start leaving Visitor Messages whenever I have to go on another food-run and want to leave a msg up saying when I'll be back! :-P

Anyway, Bump Line is pretty simple.  You know how sometimes you come up with (or just simply know) an interesting rhythm or drum line and you tap it out?  Do that here with bumps!  Trick is to try to work out the timing and the accents just right.  If you do a line that is known, please give credit to its creators!
 
Alright, I'll start:  This one is called "The Crackfiend Rukka" by Tony Jankowski as a warmup exercise for his drumline at Deer Lakes High School in Russelton, PA:

Ba-Bum Ba-Bum Ba-Bum Ba-Bum BUMP BUMP
Ba-Bum Ba-Bum Ba-Bum Ba-Bum BUMP BUMP
Ba-Bum Ba-Bum BUM Ba-Bum Ba-Bum Bum
Ba-Bum Ba-Bum BUM Ba-Bum Ba-Bum Bum
Ba-Ba-Ba Ba-Ba-Ba Ba-Ba-Ba BUM BUM
Ba-Ba-Ba Ba-Ba-Ba Ba-Ba-Ba BUM BUM
Ba-Bum Ba-Bum Ba-Bum Ba-Bum BUMP BUMP
Ba-Bum Ba-Bum Ba-Bum Ba-Bum BUMP! BUMP!
 
Alright, NEXT!!
