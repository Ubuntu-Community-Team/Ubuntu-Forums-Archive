### Post by sqrooup on 2010-07-09
The original game of British Bulldog can be seen [here]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/British_bulldogs_(game)").

The object of the game is to count to 200; however there is a “gang” who will reset and capture players (who then joins the gang).

At the start, the game is easy, but as more and more people are caught and join the gang, the game gets harder and harder.

To join the gang a gang member will post “reset”, “gotcha”, “welcome to the gang” (or anything else to that effect), and the previous poster is now a gang member; the count reverts to zero.

If the count gets to 200, that poster will be the winner, starts a new gang, reverts the count to zero, and all previous gang members will now become normal posters for a new game!

If there are 15 or more gang members, a non-gang member can call for a “restart” or “new game”; again, that poster will be the winner, starts a new gang, reverts the count to zero, and all previous gang members will now become normal posters for the new game!

Mods can join in as players (unless caught), There is no restriction on the OS used.

A few house rules; No double posting, no requests to join the gang, no flaming!

As OP I will start the gang (and I will allow a small amount of time before I capture any of you!!!)!
