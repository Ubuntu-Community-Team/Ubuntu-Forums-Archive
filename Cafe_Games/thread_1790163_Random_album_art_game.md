### Post by Macskeeball on 2011-06-24
Adapted from a game I've seen on Facebook recently.

RULES:
1 - Use Wikipedia's [random article](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:Random) feature. The title of the first article you get is the name of your band.

2 - Load a [random set of quotes](http://quotationspage.com/random.php3) quotationspage.com. The last four or five words of the very last quote of the page is the title of your first album.

3 - Explore [the last seven days](http://www.flickr.com/explore/interesting/7days/) of interesting photos on Flickr. Third picture, no matter what it is, will be your album cover.

4 - Use GIMP or similar ([picnik.com](picnik.com) is a free online photo editor) to put it all together.

5 - Post it here. You can use the "Attach Files" section under "Additional Options" to host the image. If one of your friends made one you really liked, you can post that too (that's what my second one is).

**[size=+1]IMPORTANT:[/size] Follow the [Code of Conduct](http://ubuntuforums.org/index.php?page=policy). If one of random things violates it, reload until you get something that doesn't.**
