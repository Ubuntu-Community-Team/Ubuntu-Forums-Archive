I think the thread title is pretty self-explanatory. The idea here is we take some number of images and kick them around, progressively adding more images as we go along, and all of us take turns captioning the images posted in this thread.

Rules:

1. All photos and captions must be in compliance with relevant UbuntuForums policies and be reasonably "work-safe".

2. All images must be stills of scenes from either TV shows or movies (i.e.: No promotional "cast" photos, "hero" shot publicity photos, etc.)
