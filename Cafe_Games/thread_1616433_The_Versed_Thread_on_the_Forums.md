### Post by cpmman on 2010-11-08
The point of the thread
Is a place to be read
For those whose verse
May not get a ten
From the editor's pen
And a rejection or worse

Plagiarism is out
Do not tout
Any published work
From anyone else
Except yourself
For that would irk

If you wish to rhyme
That is fine
And poetic license
May be taken et al
(it can be a pal)
Even with cadence

The meter is yours
To alter the course
Of the posting
Even if blank
It qualifies in rank
And may earn a toasting

There is no prize
Of any size
For the best
Or the worst
Of your surmise

Please keep it clean
Don't want to be mean
But the COC is demanded
Or be reprimanded

My apology is done
For the foregoing
Which is weak at best
So I hope the rest
Will entertain
With zest
