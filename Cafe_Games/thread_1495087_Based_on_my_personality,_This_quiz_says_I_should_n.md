### Post by BoneKracker on 2010-05-28
> **wishwish55 said:**
> I was actually considering trying it, a few of my friends smoke. Is this just a crap quiz, or do you guys think seeing what its like is a bad idea?[Drug Quiz](http://bit.ly/ceVl3v). [http://bit.ly/ceVl3v](http://bit.ly/ceVl3v)

It's crap.  No matter who you are, cocaine and heroine are far more dangerous (because of their intensely addictive properties) than those others shown in towheedm's quote.

Over the long haul, alcohol is actually worse for you than weed.  You don't see people pissing on themselves in the alley under a cardboard box because they're a pothead.  Being a pot-head is way better than being an alcoholic (and a lot more people become alcoholic than anyone wants to admit).

Acid, while intense, is pretty harmless (provided you don't rip your eyeballs out of their sockets or stick your hand in fire or something).  However, it can actually cause people who are prone to psychosis to break.

Don't touch coke or heroine.  Just don't do it.  If you want to know why, meet somebody who uses it, then go visit them a year or two later.

Weed can give you a giddy high or, once you're used to it, a relaxing one.  The problems with using weed regularly are:

a) Like any euphoria-producing drug, it becomes a substitute for causing yourself to feel good via productive behavior.  Instead of feeling good about yourself because you studied hard and had a good work-out today, you just smoke a bong and feel good.  After a while, this tends to erode your motivation and sense of purpose.  Most regular pot smokers do a lot of sitting around engaging in passive activities (watching cartoons, listening to music), when they could be out hiking, meeting people, or otherwise actually enhancing their own life and others'.

b) It seems to reduce your ability to focus and concentrate.  Yes, you might have an intense high where you have some thought you think is an epiphany, or where you experience creative inspiration, or where you engage in brilliant intellectual discussion.  But during other phases of the experience, and when you come down, it makes you kind of "blah" and stupid-feeling.  Some people I know who have used it for decades show no effect at all.  Others have turned into complete pudding-heads.

c) For the time being at least, it's illegal.  I smoked a lot of dope during a particular phase of my life (a phase that I now look back on and realize that it sucked, in part because I was smoking a lot of dope), and I'd say that it's not worth getting busted for.

My advice would be: find productive, beneficial, legal ways to make yourself feel good.  If you feel you must try it, try to defer doing so until your at least a legal adult if you can (that way you're at least somewhat less likely to do it for monkey-see-monkey-do or peer pressure reasons alone).  If and when you do, try to avoid the temptation to do it regularly.  Oh, and never let anyone take a picture of you doing it. :P
