### Post by LemursDontExist on 2011-07-25
Turn on the first switch.
Wait a couple minutes, then turn it back off.
Turn on the second switch, and go downstairs.

The bulb connected to the first switch will be warm, but off.
The bulb connected to the second switch will be on.
The bulb connected to the third switch will be cool and off.
