Hey folks,

I'm just announcing Ubuntu Cloud Days. This is an IRC event where the Ubuntu Cloud Community gets together around tuition sessions and information sharing. During the Cloud Days, you’ll learn lots of exciting stuff, you’ll interact with tons of smart people, and you’ll just have fun! Be sure to mark your calendar, spread the word, join in and bring your friends!

Read more at: [http://cloud.ubuntu.com/2011/03/ubuntu-cloud-days-approaching/](http://cloud.ubuntu.com/2011/03/ubuntu-cloud-days-approaching/)
