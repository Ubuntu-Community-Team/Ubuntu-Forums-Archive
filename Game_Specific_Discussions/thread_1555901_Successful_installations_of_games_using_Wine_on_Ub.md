### Post by Zorgoth on 2010-08-18
Yes.

Sometimes after a lot of fiddling with the amazing winetricks script (google it), I have:

Civilization IV all expansions, Europa Universalis III (no music), WoW (opengl mode, so no shadows and no hardware cursor - dx mode works but opengl is better performance), Starcraft II, Majesty II

all installed and working well on the computer in my sig - sometimes there is a performance hit - my guess is that if you have a fast processor and graphics card (most decent desktop-level ones probably) you won't notice so much.

Also I have older and/or less demanding games including

Disciples II, Plants vs Zombies, Tachyon: The Fringe
