Microsoft is a good thing.  They are competition.  They make Ubuntu better.  That's all we should care about is how good we can make Ubuntu.  If Ubuntu and Linux wants to show it can compete in the gaming world it needs to land a deal with a major player like E/A and have one of their popular games ported like The Sims.  Or for example the upcoming release of Spore.  I know I would purchase it, just to play it.  And I'm sure a lot of people would purchase just to support Linux.

You wouldn't have overnight success but you would spark interest and this annoying wall that the Linux world just keeps trying to get over may finally start to come down.

That's exactly what we can't do to these people.  We want to make sure Ubuntu will be an asset for them, not a liability.
