### Post by blazercist on 2007-05-31
yes you can do beryl/ati i use my x800 and my x1600 and beryl works with both...  beryl ,compiz and metacity are window managers and GTK is a theme manager , you can have 1 window manager 1 theme manager and 1 window decorator like emerald.  the manager handles the windows lol, the decorator handles the window borders like the titlebar and stuff like that and the theme manager handles the colors of the insides of the windows and the drop down menus and such.  hope this answers your question
