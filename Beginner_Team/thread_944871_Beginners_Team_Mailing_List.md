### Post by nhandler on 2008-10-11
I know this is a little late, but better late than never.

The Beginners Team has recently aquired a mailing list through Launchpad. All members of the ~ubuntuforums-beginners team on Launchpad are able to subscribe to the list. You can subscribe by going to [https://launchpad.net/~ubuntuforums-beginners](https://launchpad.net/~ubuntuforums-beginners). The mailing list is going to be used to discuss meeting agenda items prior to the meetings, and to send out important reminders to team members. As a result, all team members should subscribe to this list. The other day, I sent out an email to all Beginners Team members who have joined the Launchpad team (which all Beginners Team members should have done) but have not subscribed to the mailing list. If you are not sure if you have subscribed yet, please feel free to send me an email/pm, comment in this thread, or /msg me on irc. I would be more than glad to let you know if you are subscribed.
