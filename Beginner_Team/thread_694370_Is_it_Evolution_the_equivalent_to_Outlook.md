### Post by mivo on 2008-02-12
Evolution is somewhat similar to Outlook, though it isn't quite as complex and feature-rich. It works very well for me, though, and I use it for both private and business mail. As for your mail problem, the mail host is usually something like mail.domain.com or pop.mail.domain com. If you only put RCN.com, this won't work. Try pop.rcn.com or mail.rcn.com, though your provider should be able to supply the detailed information you need.
