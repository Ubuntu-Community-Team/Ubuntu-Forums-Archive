### Post by Bodsda on 2008-08-13
@ Hello, Living2007 (in gimp-speak)

1) Open gimp
2) Load image
3) hold CTRL + roll your mouse ball forwards
4) press shift + o
5) click on the white
6) press delete --> if this doesnt work
...7) Go onto the layers window
...8) Select new layer
...9) Make sure layer is transparent
..10) Move the new layer to the bottom
..11) Repeat steps 1 through 6
