### Post by useResa on 2007-04-28
Click on User CP from the menu at the top and select User Control Panel from the drop down box.
Scroll down a bit and select "Edit Profile" in the left menu.
Scroll down again and you will come across an option "Ubuntu Distro" where you can "Select your Ubuntu flavor" from the drop down box.
