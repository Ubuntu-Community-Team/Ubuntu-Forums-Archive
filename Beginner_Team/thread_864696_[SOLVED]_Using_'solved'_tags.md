### Post by Dr Small on 2008-07-19
Since this new vBulletin feature of tags for threads, whenever I see a [SOLVED] thread (or even one where the OP says the thread is Solved, yet fails to mark it as [SOLVED]), I add the 'solved' tag to the bottom of the thread.

Then users can search based off of 'solved' tags to find Solved threads, and plus the word solved shows up beside the thread in the forum.

Anyone else picked up this practice yet?

Dr Small
