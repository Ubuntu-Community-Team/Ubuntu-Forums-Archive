### Post by ScottSanbar on 2011-09-10
I have 11.04.  I learn that this way:

Click at the top of the screen on System, then on the drop down menu the results from that on Administration then on the System Monitor item.  There are some tabs along the top of that window - just make sure the System tab is selected, and you will see your version number along with other stuff.

Also, you could open a terminal window (Applications|Accessories|Terminal) and type the following since the above may not work for versions before 11.04:

lsb_release -a

This should give you the version number and some other info.  Also, with the above, you can learn a lot about your name and kernel version, etc. with the following at the terminal prompt:

uname -a

Scott Sanbar
