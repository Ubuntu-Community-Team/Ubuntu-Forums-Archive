After looking through your past (3) posts, it seems that there are two reasons for this and is simply due to the informal structure that has risen up to help manage the overwhelming volume of activity that the ubuntu forum receives: 

Number one, posting a new question at the end of someone elses answered question. Once a question is answered most of the people involved in the thread ignore any further posts to it. 

Number two, posting replys to your own message-- there is a group that specifically looks for unanswered posts to answer, so if your post was passed for whatever reason, the team that would catch it will assume that it has been answered.
