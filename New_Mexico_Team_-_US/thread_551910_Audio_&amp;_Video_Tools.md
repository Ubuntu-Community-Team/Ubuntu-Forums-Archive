### Post by GrammatonCleric on 2007-09-15
At today's Software Freedom Event it was asked what Multimedia tools do I use  for my audio video needs. 

All of the following applications, with the exception of HandBrake, are available from the Ubuntu repo's.  Enabling of the multiverse, universe, and restricted repo's are required.



-----------------------------
** Audio Playback**
-----------------------------

Audacious

[http://audacious-media-player.org/Main_Page](http://audacious-media-player.org/Main_Page)

or 

Banshee

[http://banshee-project.org/Main_Page](http://banshee-project.org/Main_Page)


-----------------------------
** ID3 Tags and Renaming**
------------------------------

EasyTag

[http://easytag.sourceforge.net/](http://easytag.sourceforge.net/)

or

Banshee

[http://banshee-project.org/Main_Page](http://banshee-project.org/Main_Page)


-----------------------------

[http://banshee-project.org/Main_Page](http://banshee-project.org/Main_Page)
