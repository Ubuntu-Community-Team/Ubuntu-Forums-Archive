### Post by Cheesehead on 2011-10-04
The [Ubuntu Leadership Team]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuLeadership") is pleased to announce that we are organizing a set of IRC-based leader-skill training sessions.

We are seeking TRAINERS and ASSISTANTS for the [sessions listed here]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuLeadership/Projects#Online_Leadership_Tutorial_Sessions"). When the schedule is published, it will be at the same link.

***Want to teach?*** Respond in #ubuntu-leadership, or send me a private message in this forum!

***Want to learn?*** Feel free to respond in this thread, or by private message. Or just check the schedule and show up!

***Should we cover a skill that's not listed?*** Add it to the thread!

*The Ubuntu Leadership Team aims to be a community resource that helps gather, build, and form a leadership training forum within the Ubuntu Community.*
