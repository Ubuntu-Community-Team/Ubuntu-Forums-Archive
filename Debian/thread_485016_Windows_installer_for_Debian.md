### Post by MethodOne on 2007-06-26
When I was looking through DistroWatch, I found a Google ad that made references to Debian (18,000+ packages).  After clicking on the ad, I ended up finding a FOSS Debian installer for Windows.  You can get the installer at [http://www.goodbye-windows.com.]("http://www.goodbye-windows.com")

Unfortunately, I can only install on a real partition instead of a file, which means I had to resize my NTFS partition and install on the free space.
