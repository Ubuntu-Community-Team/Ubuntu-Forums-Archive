Basically, they only had imcomplete packages for gnome 3.8 in experimental and all other releases had 3.4 (they skipped 3.6 all together after releasing some packages earlier in the year and then 3.8 came out) I installed 3.8 in SID but for months something or othe are missing and I always ran into dependency problems.
