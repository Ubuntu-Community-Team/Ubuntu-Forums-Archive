### Post by jsmidt on 2006-01-02
For those who are also affiliated with Debian:

In response to the "How to Increase Contributions from Volunteers" post on debian-devel at [http://lists.debian.org/debian-devel/2006/01/threads.html](http://lists.debian.org/debian-devel/2006/01/threads.html) , the Debian Contributer Project has been created. Please go to it on the Debian wiki [http://wiki.debian.org](http://wiki.debian.org).

The main point of this project is to organize all contributions to Debian into one place. This will improve Debian's organization. Please begin adding your wiki pages with your contributions on DebianContributerProject on the wiki.
