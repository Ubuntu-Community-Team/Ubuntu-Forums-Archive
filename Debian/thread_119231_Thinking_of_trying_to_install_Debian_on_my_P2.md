### Post by Qrk on 2006-01-19
You only need the first one: it has desktops (Gnome, KDE and perhaps a few others) included. But the second one has things that are nice to have; I believe Firefox and abiword are on it, as well as the rest of KDE.

You can read the included packages on all of the CD's on Debian's website.
