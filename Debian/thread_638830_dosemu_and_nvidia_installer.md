This is not a thread asking for help, is just an experience that would help to someone one day :) This morning i update my debian system and downloaded new kernel. I installed the headers and when i was installing my nvidia driver it failed. After reading some error reports i realized it was "dosemu" the trouble. So, if someday it happens to you try removing dosemu and reinstall the driver.
