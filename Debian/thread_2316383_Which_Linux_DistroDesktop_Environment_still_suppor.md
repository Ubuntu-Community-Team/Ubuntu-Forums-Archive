### Post by buzzingrobot on 2016-03-07
I don't believe Debian supports Compiz for 8 in any way.  It's not in their repos.

Canonical maintains Compiz because it is required for Unity. It is shipped by Ubuntu MATE.  It can be made to work, I believe, in Xubuntu, replacing xfce4wm. It ships with Linux Mint Mate and Linux Mint XFCE. It's also in Fedora's MATE spin.
