### Post by darrelljon on 2008-04-22
Check out D[eb-Ice]("http://digg.com/linux_unix/Debian_Etch_with_IceWM"). Its built on Debian Etch with IceWM window manager and appropriately low memory apps only 29Mb. Smaller than Damn Small Linux, multi-user unlike Puppy Linux, more software (compatible with Debian repositories) than SliTaz. Prepared as an installable image for your convenience. Minimum system requirements Pentium (or equivalent) with 64Mb RAM.
