As a follow on from the suggestion in the post at [Ubuntu-Marketing]Internet Marketing I've started creating a page over at the wiki to help organise the idea.

The concept is to create a firefoxflicks type of site to advertise Ubuntu. In this way people could submit funky, funny entertaining videos to get people interested in Ubuntu.

To find out more or get involved please visit:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MarketingTeam/UbuntuViralVideos](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MarketingTeam/UbuntuViralVideos)

Stuff is at a very early stage at the moment so is not of course ready for public 'announcement' as it where. The more people offer their skills and ideas, the quicker we'll get there though.
