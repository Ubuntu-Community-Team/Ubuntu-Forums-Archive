Have you been a good boy/girl this year?...  You have?...

Well in that case you deserve a visit to the Museum of Computings 'Challenge Santa' day!  

On **Saturday 8th December **from 09:00-16:00 we will have some of the greatest Christmas video games to ever grace a console. Get steamed up on 'The Polar Express' on the Gamecube, take a plummet in 'Holiday Lemmings' and burrow away as a festive Monty Mole on the Sinclair Spectrum.  Plus many, many more, see our [**website**]("http://www.museum-of-computing.org.uk") for more details.

Due to popular demand our 'Pong to Playstation' exhibition is still running and we now have the amazing Nintendo Wii so come along and give it a go!

There will be competitions, prizes and free crackers, so don't miss this fantastic event.  And best of all its totally FREE, now there's a reason to 'ho, ho, ho'
