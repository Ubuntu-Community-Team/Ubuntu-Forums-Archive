### Post by bom28 on 2007-08-18
I think the standard way to store images of audio CD, is ripping the CD to a single wav and create a [cue sheet]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cue_sheet_%28computing%29") (.cue) that store informations on the different tracks.
The wav file can then be compressed losslessly with flac.
Not many players support cue sheets though.
