### Post by Cresho on 2007-06-03
Anyway, I'm just putting this information for newbies like myself.

In order to record audio, line in or aux or microphone, i noticed it required 2 settings and not just one.  the first part is to go into your mixer prefferences and activate the ones you want such as line in record switch and this is important.....the adc capture switch.  if you are using a microphone, activate microphone and the adc switch and now you can record audio.

I spent about 2 days trying to find out and I read a post about turning everything on and then I started narrowing things smaller.  those are the switches I ended up with in order for me to record audio and video through my xdtv and tv tuner card.
