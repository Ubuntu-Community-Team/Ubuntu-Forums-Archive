### Post by tagra123 on 2006-12-26
easyubuntu for codecs

tovid will convert videos (easiest if you are new to ubuntu)

mencoder and ffmpeg  will also convert.

grip works good to rip cd's to oog or mp3

To make a backup copy of your DVD.

k9copy works on a lot of disks.

If you have a DVD that is hard to copy (encryption) follow the guide below. Installs dvd shrink and dvddecrypter using  wine

[http://www.google.com/bookmarks/url?url=http://mrbass.org/linux/ubuntu/dvdshrink/&zx=c6da4XR5hf0&ct=b](http://www.google.com/bookmarks/url?url=http://mrbass.org/linux/ubuntu/dvdshrink/&zx=c6da4XR5hf0&ct=b)

For burning CDs and DVDs k3b is great. It is set up similar to nero to burn data cd/dvds VCD etc...

Most of these programs are in the universe repository. Googling for any of them will yield a lot of info.
