Some of the top of my head, dunno if it helps.

EDIT:
Acidrip - DVD to .avi and .mpg, sorry not .mp4
DeVeDe and K3B - most video formats to Video DVD
dvd::rip - DVD to .avi and other formats, might be able to do .mp4
mencoder and ffmpeg - can convert most known video formats to another format, although neither have GUI's so you'll have to google or read the manual on how to use them
