### Post by smbm on 2007-03-28
Hi

If you right click on an MP3 (or any other file) file and go to Properties at the bottom of the list, you should see a new window open with about 6 tabs across the top. If you go to the tab labeled Open With it should give you check boxes for what app you want to open this type of file with in future. Change it the one you want, click close and then when you double click on it it should open with what you selected.

Hope this helps
