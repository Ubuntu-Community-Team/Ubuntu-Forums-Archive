### Post by MicahCarrick on 2008-05-21
Amarok, Banshee, and Rhythmbox all support MTP devices, however, Amarok seems to support the Sansa View the best (including playlists).

I spend a lot of time figuring out how it works with MSC mode vs MTP mode, album art, playlists, etc. I wrote up all my findings regarding the Sansa View and Ubuntu on my blog: [_Sandisk Sansa View on Ubuntu Linux_]("http://www.micahcarrick.com/05-21-2008/sansa-view-ubuntu.html")
