### Post by Chappy7777 on 2007-08-09
I use XMMS for listening to my MP3s, and I also use it along with Streamtuner (very lightweight) to listen to 'net radio.  My Linux Box is by no means a big honking PC. I have an AMD 1Gig Duron CPU, and 1Gig DDR Ram. My video is an old ATI 3D Rage Pro 8mg card that I am using because for some reason this old thing I got real cheap doesn't like my AGP TNT2 card. 

XMMS is pretty much like an older version of Winamp. It uses way less CPU than the newer Winamp 5.2 (?) does.  I would compare Xmms to Winamp 2.75 or so. The nice thing about XMMS is that you can get it via Synaptic and need do no installing or use any command lines. Very handy for NooBs like myself. Streamtuner is the same thing.

If you do find a light weight MP3 that works as easy as XMMS let us know about it. ok? Thnx.
