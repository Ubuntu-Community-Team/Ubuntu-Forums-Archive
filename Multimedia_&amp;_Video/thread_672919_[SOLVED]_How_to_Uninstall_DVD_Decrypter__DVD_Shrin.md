### Post by yabbies on 2008-01-20
Applications>wine>uninstall software

click on Applications in your menu bar you should have an installed version of wine in your applications menu, you can uninstall any wine software there. With wine a lot of times the software is uninstalled but it still shows in the wine menu.
Just manually remove the wine menu tag, right click on applications>edit menus>
scroll down to wine and open wine's menu via the arrow
left click on programs and in the right menu right click on the application and choose delete.

if all else fails you can just delete the files in program files.
places>home folder

do a control+h to show your hidden ./ files
scroll down to .wine>drive_c>program files>

dvd decrypter and dvd shrink both have uninstall.exe in their directories try to run these first.
