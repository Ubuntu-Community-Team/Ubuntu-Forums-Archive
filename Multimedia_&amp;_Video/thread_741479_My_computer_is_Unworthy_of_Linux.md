### Post by aeiah on 2008-04-01
what are your pc specs? if its older than 5 years old, you may find something for just a few bucks on ebay that'll suit you. i have a dell optiplex i grabbed for £20 that runs xubuntu fine.

if you dont want to buy an old one because you plan on saving for a new one, but would like this old thing you have to work for a little longer, you should be able to track down what's getting so hot and put a better fan in it. see if your power supply fan is running ok, and see if it is actualy powerful enough too. 

see what gets the hottest and stick a desk fan next to it and see if that helps. if it does, spend a fiver on a new fan
