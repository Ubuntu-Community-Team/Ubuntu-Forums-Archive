### Post by Sabrage on 2007-03-15
Is it possible to play a folder in rhythmbox? I have different folders with music and sometimes I just want to play a folder.

---

### Post by lizzard on 2007-03-15
You could create playlists each for every folder you want to play.
Go to  Music -> automatic playlist  and select "path" in the dropdown menu. Select "is" for the condition,  enter the path to your folder and click on "new". Repeat that for every folder you want a separate playlist for.
