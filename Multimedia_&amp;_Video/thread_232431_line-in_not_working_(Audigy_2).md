### Post by crackseller on 2006-08-08
Applications > Sound & Video > Volume Control then select Edit > Preferences, there you can add an "Analog Mix" scroll bar.

---

### Post by zardoz on 2006-08-08
Thanks a lot ... just also found it. Everything is working fine now. So for all who have that problem in the future:
Right click on that little speaker icon, select "Preferences", select "Analog Mix", turn that volume up, select "Master" in "Preferences" again, activate the Line-in in the Volume Control. Thats it.
