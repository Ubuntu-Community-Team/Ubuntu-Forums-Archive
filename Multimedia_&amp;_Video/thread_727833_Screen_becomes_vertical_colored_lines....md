### Post by erginemr on 2008-03-19
I suggest you not to bother with trying to track down corrupted files, download the Ubuntu Live CD image again, burn it to a CD-R but now with the lowest speed settings, have it do a CD check on itself, insert your NVidia MX 4000 graphics card (provided there is nothing wrong with the hardware) and reinstall ubuntu onto the existing Linux partitions but by allowing the Ubuntu installer to re-format them. Once this is done, do not hurry up for the installation of Envy, post back here and we shall work out the rest together.

You will understand whether Ubuntu will like your graphics card or not by the looks of the Live CD environment. I am also an GeForce MX 440 user and, although it is archaic by today's standards, am very happy with its 2D and 3D performance under Ubuntu.
