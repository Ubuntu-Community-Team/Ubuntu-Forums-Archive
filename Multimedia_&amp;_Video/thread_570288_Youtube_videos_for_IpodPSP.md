### Post by Camron-roks on 2007-10-08
1. go to getdeb.net and download QTtube

2. find your favorite youtube video, run QTtube, put in the address to the video

3. use mencoder to convert to mp4:

```
mencoder -idx inputfile.flv -ovc lavc -oac mp3lame -o outputfile.mp4
```

Then they are ready to add to your psp/ipod
