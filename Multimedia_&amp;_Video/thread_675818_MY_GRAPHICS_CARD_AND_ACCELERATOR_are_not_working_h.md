### Post by luisromangz on 2008-01-23
I think the command you are asking for is lspci (to list the devices connected to the PCI bus). What you should do depends on your version of Gutsy, and the manufacturer of your laptop's graphics card. If it is an Nvidia or ATI based card, the Restricted Driver Manager would help, if you are using Gutsy. For ATI cards I would recommend using one of the latest drivers instead of the one Gutsy downloads and install.
