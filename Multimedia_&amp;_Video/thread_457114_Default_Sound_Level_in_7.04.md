### Post by NilsE on 2007-05-28
If you have the speaker icon on the panel you just single click on it and set the volume and it should hold.

If you don't have the icon right click an open area on the panel and select "add to panel" then select the volume control under system and hardware to get it on the panel.  Then do the above.
