### Post by halfmanhalfbug on 2007-01-19
Hi Loonux
I had the same problem. The advice from this [post]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=341085&highlight=Option+AIGLX%26quot%3B+%26quot%3Boff") fixed it. Your original posted xorg.conf looks like mine. Simply add:
```

Section "ServerFlags"
        Option      "AIGLX" "off"
EndSection

```
What does the the "ServerFlags" directive do? I have no idea but I have direct rendering from fglrx now. Hope this helps.
