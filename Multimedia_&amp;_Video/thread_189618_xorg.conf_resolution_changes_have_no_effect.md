### Post by Pejalo on 2006-06-06
For anyone who is interested, and to avoid anyone from explaining the answer to my problem to me: I figured it out. After reading the chapter "The X Window System" of _Running Linux_ by O'Reilly (an amazing book for Linux newbies), along with scrolling up and down in the xorg.conf file enough times, I now understand how the file works. I created my own "Screen" Section which you can see at the bottom that correctly links to the Device and Monitor I was changing the properties for.

### Post by Pejalo on 2006-06-08
You can have as many "Device" sections as you want.
The only one actually taken into account by the X Window System is the one referenced in your "Screen" section. Again, you can have multiple ones of these as well, but I assume you can only have one "ServerLayout" section that ultimately defines what "Screen" settings to use.
I hope that makes sense.
