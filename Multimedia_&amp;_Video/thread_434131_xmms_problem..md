### Post by orangep on 2007-05-05
Check to see if "always on top" is enabled in the "options" menu.
Click on the 'O' in the vertical menu of O-A-I-D-V, and the "always on top" is about
halfways down.
Uncheck it.
I see that control-A might also do it.
