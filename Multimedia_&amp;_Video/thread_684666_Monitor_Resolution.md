If this is your monitor :

[http://www1.ap.dell.com/content/products/productdetails.aspx/1908fp?c=in&cs=inbsd1&l=en&s=bsd](http://www1.ap.dell.com/content/products/productdetails.aspx/1908fp?c=in&cs=inbsd1&l=en&s=bsd)

As far as I know, lcd panels are meant to be configured always at the max resolution. Iy you try to put a lower resolution you'll end with blurry and crapy output. In certain way, The thing is that your monitor has **physicaly** 1280x1024 real pixels, so it has no sense to try increasing the resolution. In a CRT monitor of the same size, you could do that, I suppose because there are more "real pixels".

Correct me if i'm wrong, i'm aware it's not the best comparision between lcd and crt pixels...
