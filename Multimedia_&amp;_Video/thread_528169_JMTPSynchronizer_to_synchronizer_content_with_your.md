Hi There -

I have started a new project called JMTPsynchronizer, which is a java based front end for synchronizing content with your MTP device. Unlike Gnomad, in this interface i dont show the tracks which are common to your machine and your device and thus its very convenient to sync content.

I am working on documentation and some minor enhancements to do a first release.

Please check it out and share feedback with me.

[http://code.google.com/p/jmtpsynchronizer/](http://code.google.com/p/jmtpsynchronizer/)

Please raise issues/enhancements you badly want.
