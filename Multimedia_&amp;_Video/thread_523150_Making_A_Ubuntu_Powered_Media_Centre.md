### Post by Vince4Amy on 2007-08-11
It's my mum & dads anniversary soon, so I thought it would be a great idea to make a Ubuntu powered media centre as a present as they like to watch & record a lot of films. 

I've searched around on ebuyer & with the spare parts I've got I can make a Media Centre that will cost me no more than about £40. The things I'm including with this are:

TV Tuner Able to Pick Up Free View Channels (And Composite connectors)
DVD +/- RW Drive
160Gb Hard Disk Drive

I've managed to make this work by running a customised version of Gnome, this runs a media centre frontend on startup & I've also altered the boot up processes to bring the boot time down quite a lot. I've also changed the splash screen & removed all the unnecessary applications. I've managed to make it out of hardware that's actually quite old but does the job very well the following spec was used:

330 Watt Power Supply
512Mb Memory
1GHz Pentium 3
64Mb ATI Radeon PCI
Cheap TV Tuner
Cheap USB Wireless Stick
Lite-On DVD/RW drive
Ubuntu Linux 7.04 With ALL Proprietary Codecs
Customised GNOME
LinuxMCE

I'll report back & see how they make use of it. What are your thoughts on this?
