### Post by disturbed1 on 2008-01-24
If your CD player supports mp3s, it gets the information from the ID3 tag. Check out a tag editor like EasyTag.

If these are normal audio CDs, then it's called CD-TEXT. Brasero does this just fine for me. When you import mp3s or oggs, Brasero will read the ID3 tag and fill out the Artist and Track info for you, or you could edit it on your own. K3b should support this as well.
