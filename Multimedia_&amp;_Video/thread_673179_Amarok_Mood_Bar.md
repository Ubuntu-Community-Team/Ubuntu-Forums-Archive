### Post by sub2007 on 2008-01-20
The moodbar is a nice piece of eye candy but is (in my opinion) completely unfunctional. If you want to install it you will need to install the moodbar package. Open a terminal and enter

**sudo apt-get install moodbar**

After installing, open your Amarok options and check the moodbar. You can then add a mood column to your Amarok playlist if you chose and the moodbar will replace the time slider for the song. First time you add a song to the playlist it'll take a short while to calculate the songs mood.

To add Amarok themes:
In Amarok, click Tools > Amarok Options. Click Appearance, then in the Context Browser style click "Download Styles..." You can then install styles there.

Or you can browse Amarok themes at kde-look.org: [http://www.kde-look.org/?xcontentmode=55](http://www.kde-look.org/?xcontentmode=55) I'm not too sure how you add them, I think you click Install New Style... and then open the style that you downloaded.

Enjoy ;)
