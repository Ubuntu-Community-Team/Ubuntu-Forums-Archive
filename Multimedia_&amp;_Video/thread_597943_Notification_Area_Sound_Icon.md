### Post by kostkon on 2007-10-30
Right click on one of your panels, select *Add to Panel*, then find the *Volume Control* applet and add it. Afterwards, if you right click on the sound icon you will find the option to move it. 

Also, when you right click on it you will find the *Preferences* option. Select this option and make sure that the icon controls the *Master* volume level.
