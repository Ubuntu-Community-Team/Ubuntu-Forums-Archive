### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-01-15
It's certainly not anything with a Creative X-fi chip in it (Creative or 3rd-party) I'd guess it's something with C-Media CMI8788 "Oxygen" chip at the core. The latest driver for that chip is supposedly much improved in ALSA.

Personally, I own an M-Audio Revolution, based on the ICEnsemble, errr VIA Envy24HT chip. These are good cards, but the envy24ht ALSA drivers need a bit of work and the OSS4 drivers could use a minor bug-fix too to allow hardware mixing.
