### Post by Dale61 on 2006-10-29
As a regular 'Stumbler', I happened to come across a music player by the name of Songbird.

I was having niggling problems with XMMS and Rhythmbox, so when I found Songbird, I decided to give it a go, and after 4 weeks, I haven't had a single problem.

It is only version 0.2, and still under development, but sound quality is excellent.

For simple, straight forward instructions on how to install, go to:
[http://www.arsgeek.com/?p=615#more-615]("http://www.arsgeek.com/?p=615#more-615")

*Songbird is shaping up to be an amazing media player. It’s not only got audio and video capability (with a HUGE range of media types) but it’s built on the Mozilla engine, meaning it’s also a web browser. Not only that, but for a preview release (0.2 people!) it’s looking slicker than a puddle of oil with George W. Bush floating in it.*
