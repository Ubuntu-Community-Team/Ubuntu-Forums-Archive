The volume level is very low compared to other sounds. Even when I turn the volume in the program up to max, it's still about 1/4 the volume of music and applications... Which means I have to turn my speakers up to max to hear the sound at about a normal level (not good if I forget to lower it afterwards, of if another sound plays while I'm watching the video!).

Does anyone know why this might be happening, or how I can fix it? Thanks, I appreciate it.

### Post by darth_indy on 2007-06-20
OK, never mind... I fixed it. *sheepish grin* - if anyone else is wondering, you just need to open alsamixer and turn up PCM. That's it - I don't know why it's so low by default.
