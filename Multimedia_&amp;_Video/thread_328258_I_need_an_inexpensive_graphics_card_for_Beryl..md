### Post by kwilliam on 2007-01-01
I did some research (tomshardware.com had an excellent [beginners guide](http://www.tomshardware.com/2006/07/24/graphics_beginners/) to understanding graphics cards) and determined my PC has an AGP slot (either 4x or 8x).  I determined this by looking inside the computer and comparing it to the wikipedia article and AGP, and by looking at computer [manufacturer's website](http://www.powerspec.com/systems/archives/system_archive.phtml?selection=4985).

I have ordered an nVidia GeForce FX5200 128MB 4X/8X AGP card from eBay for $30.  Hopefully I won't get ripped off.   It was made by VISIONTEK, I think.
