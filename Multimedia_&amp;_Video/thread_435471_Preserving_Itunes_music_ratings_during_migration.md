### Post by aysiu on 2007-05-06
There's probably some way to convert the iTunes database to the Rhythmbox (or Amarok or whatever) database, but I think the simplest solution might just be the lowest-tech one.

If I were you and had a ton of songs rated, I'd "delete" the 5-star songs first, copy them over to Ubuntu, rate them all 5 (which should be easy to do all at once), "delete" the 4-star songs, copy them over to Ubuntu, rate them all 4, etc. You need to do that only six times (5, 4, 3, 2, 1, and no stars). Of course, information like when the song was added to the library or the last time it was played won't be brought over, though.
