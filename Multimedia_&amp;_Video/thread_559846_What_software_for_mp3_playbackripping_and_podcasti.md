### Post by tombott on 2007-09-25
Sound Juicer comes pre-loaded with Ubuntu and when you insert an audio CD should auto load.
Sound Juicer will allow you to rip the CD to ogg or Mp3.

Am pretty sure Rhythmbox has support for Podcasts and is installed by default.

[Amarok]("http://amarok.kde.org") supports Podcast as well

Hope that helps mate.

Tom.
