### Post by drobvice on 2006-11-10
Try running alsamixer in teminal and make sure your channels are not muted and turned up properly (also double click the sound icon in the taskbar).  Also, you might check the Preferences-->Sound menu.  I think the second tab has your playback device.  Make sure that is correct.  Every time I have changed any of the settings on the first tab (basically changing anything from autodetect) it has made matters worse.  Not sure what you have tried so far.  I did my upgrade by clean install but automatix (have since reinstalled) did something to kill my sound and I think it was just changing to a different device.
