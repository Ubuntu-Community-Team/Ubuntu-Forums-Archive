### Post by josesanders on 2007-11-30
I would definately recommend Hauppauge.  After struggling with a generic saa7134 card, I caved in and bought a PVR 150, and if I remember correctly, all I had to do was put it in my computer and it was working in just a few minutes.

I definatly recommend you get something with hardware encoding, so that your processor resources aren't taken up doing the encoding.  It ends up being much more stable, regardless of your processor speed.
