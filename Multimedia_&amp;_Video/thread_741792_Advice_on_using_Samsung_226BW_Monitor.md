### Post by xzero1 on 2008-04-01
I have a samsung 216bw and a nvidia 7600GT. You will likely boot up in a low res mode util xorg is configured correctly for your monitor. Then you will have to edit xorg config manually to fix the problem. Once done, there should be no other issues. The Ubuntu issues have nothing to do with  the size of the monitor. Xorg autoconfiguration simply does not for some monitors.
