I just installed Edgy, and for some reason, it would NOT set up my monitor to go past 800x600 in resolution.  I found that it was not putting the HorizSync, and VertRefresh lines in my xorg.conf file.  I couldn't understand why it would work in the past, but now, with this new version it's missing by default.  So, I placed those lines in, and now my monitor is fine.  On to the next issue.

When I installed Edgy on my laptop, which has an Nvidia GeForce FX Go5100 video card with 32 MB of ram, everything worked out of the box, with the correct 1280x800 resolution.  Even the boot up logo for ubuntu was gorgeous.  On my desktop, with my ATI card, the boot logo looks like hell, almost in 16 colors.  Can anyone answer why that is? :-k
