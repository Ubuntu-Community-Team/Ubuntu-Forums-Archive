### Post by fbusy on 2007-08-12
NEVERMIND :)

for anyone else with this same issue.. 

for some reason adobe wont play back 32 bit in multitrack view.. so just set it to 16 and it plays fantastic.  

THE FIX:

GO TO -> Options > device properties > WaveOut TAB > checkmark 16 bit > 

then hit ok and your golden :)
