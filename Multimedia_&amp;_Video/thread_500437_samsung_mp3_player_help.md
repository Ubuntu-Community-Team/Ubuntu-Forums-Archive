### Post by Damanther on 2007-07-18
Depends on what exact model you have,

Mine is a YP-U2JZW. Look on the back and find the exact model.

It is going to take a firmware upgrade on the mp3 player to get it to work. I had to download a version from the euro site. Which made the mp3 player work, but I lost the fm tuner because it wasn't the exact same model.

I would try upgrading to the latest version for your model from the samsumg.com/uk or samsung.com/au site if you can find it there. The US sites try to force you to use the windows software.
