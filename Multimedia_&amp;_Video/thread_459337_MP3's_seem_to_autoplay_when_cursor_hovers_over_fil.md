### Post by diablo75 on 2007-05-30
Let's say I wanted to enable/disable this feature.  When I pass the mouse over a file, and let it rest there for a couple of seconds, the MP3 that it is hovering over starts to play allowing me to preview the file before actually opening it with something like XMMS.  How do I disable this feature if I don't want it to bother me?  

I've had sometimes experienced a bug with this feature.  The sound effect may not stop playing until it's run through it's entirety.  This has usually happened when I click on a file, let the mouse rest there, and then hit Delete to send it to the trash.

---

### Post by reclusivemonkey on 2007-05-30
In any nautilus window,

Edit --> Preferences --> Preview

in the Sound Files section, set Preview Sound Files to "Never"
