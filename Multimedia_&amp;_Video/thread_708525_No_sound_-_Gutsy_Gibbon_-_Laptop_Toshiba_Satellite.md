### Post by DrFT on 2008-07-23
Don't know if this will help or not - but might be worth a try.

I recently switched my wife's laptop (Toshiba Satellite P105-S6024 - original windows XP machine) to Ubuntu 8.04 (Hardy Heron).  Everything worked perfectly except - you guessed it - no sound.  I spent the better part of a week searching forums and trying everything remotely similar (playing with Alsamixer, adding code to modprobe.d, downloading/installing Alsa drivers, etc., all to no avail.

On more of a lark than anything, I decided it might be worth a try updating the BIOS, which was original to the (nearly 2 year old) computer - version 2.20.  I flashed version 4.30 (available for P-100 notebooks on Toshiba's website), and reinstalled Ubuntu 8.04.  Sound has worked perfectly since.

Give it a try if no one has a better suggestion.
