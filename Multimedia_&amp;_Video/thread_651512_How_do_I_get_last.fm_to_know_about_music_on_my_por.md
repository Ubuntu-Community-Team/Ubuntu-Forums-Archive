### Post by moron on 2007-12-27
Are you playing music on your Linux box from a mounted external volume (i.e. the D2) or are you just pressing play on the D2?  last.fm will only know about tracks that are played via an Internet connected, compatible player.  So unless your D2 has a wireless connection and supports last.fm directly (very unlikely), you are not going to see tracks played directly on it show up on your last.fm song history.   

If you are just using it as an external harddrive then they should show up assuming they have proper ID3 tags on the files.

Cheers
