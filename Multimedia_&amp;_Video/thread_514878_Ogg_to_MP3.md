### Post by keyboardashtray on 2007-08-01
> **benhagerty said:**
> Is there a way that I can convert my whole library from ogg to mp3 at the same time? If not then i'll just re rip all my cds

Yes, you can with SoundConverter (from Add/Remove), it has worked very nice for me for converting my Mp3 to Ogg. It is a sleek interface, you can choose the target directory format (Artist, Album) (filename) or just replace them back in their current directories. You can also have it delete the original file.

You can also select target bitrate, so you could select highest quality (but still lossy) to lose as little quality as possible, since you are going from lossy to lossy.

I know a lot of the audio purists will say rerip, but if you have a lot of music and want to get into your desired format right away and slowly rerip the albums where every bit of quality counts over the next few days/weeks, this might be your option.

Do a test track with SoundConverter to make sure you've got your directory preferences set right before doing all of your music, especially if you're choosing the delete original option.

Good luck!
