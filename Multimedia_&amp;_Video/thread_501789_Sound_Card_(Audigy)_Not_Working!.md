### Post by Maxxarcade on 2007-07-15
Try going into the mixer switches and toggle the "Digital/Analog output jack" box.

### Post by Maxxarcade on 2007-07-15
Another thing- if you haven't done it,  go into the mixer properties, turn on Tone, Bass And Treble, then go back to the mixer and check off the tone switch.  Then the Bass and Treble sliders will function.

I noticed that with an SB Live card, the Digital/Analog output check box uses the opposite setting of the Audigy.  Really threw me for a loop when using one of each card in the same system.
