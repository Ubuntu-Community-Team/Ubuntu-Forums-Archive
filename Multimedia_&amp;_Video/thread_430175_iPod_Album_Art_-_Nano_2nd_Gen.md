### Post by monstermunch on 2007-05-01
I use amarok. There's a button in the menus for finding album art automatically for all your mp3s and there's a button on the media devices section for uploading art to your ipod. You'll need to use gtkpod first though to set the correct model number on your ipod (this is an amarok bug I think; it won't work if you don't do this).
