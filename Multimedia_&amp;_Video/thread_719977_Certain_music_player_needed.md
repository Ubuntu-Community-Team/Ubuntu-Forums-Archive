### Post by itix on 2008-03-09
First of all, mp3-support is global and handled by one of the G-streamer plugins (I don't remember which since I always install all of them). You can find them in the synaptic package manager and they are called Good, Ugly, Bad, bad-multiverse and ugly-multiverse. If you just want the mp3-play-support I suggest that you read the specifics of each plugin.

Audacious is a fairly lightweight player. I haven't tried it myself since i prefer hevy-duty-stuff.
