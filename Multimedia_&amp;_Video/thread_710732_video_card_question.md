### Post by Melcar on 2008-03-01
A 8600GTS or a HD3850 will do nicely.  They should be able to handle anything in Linux, except maybe Nexuiz 2.4 in Ultimate @ 1600x1200 :).  If you want more "budget" cards, go with a 8600GT, or I hear the HD3650s are very good.  Stay away from the 9600 for now, thought, as there is no official driver support for that card.  As for the vRAM size, 256MB is more than enough for nearly anything (Compiz and such), but consider 512MB if you plan to do heavy gaming with the card (especially at high resolutions).
