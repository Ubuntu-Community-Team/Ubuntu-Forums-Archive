### Post by gvoima on 2007-10-20
Well, the only thing you have to do everytime you upgrade your kernel, is to recompile the module for the terratec. It's not a long process so I don't see it as a problem :)

And you don't really often update your kernel to a newer version.

So there should be no problems there. But I Strongly recommend you to go trough the documentations about the cards/sticks on the page I linked earlier, there might be problems so it's good to be prepared, never the less I have had no problems with my stick. It uses the [em2880]("http://mcentral.de/wiki/index.php/Em2880") module.
