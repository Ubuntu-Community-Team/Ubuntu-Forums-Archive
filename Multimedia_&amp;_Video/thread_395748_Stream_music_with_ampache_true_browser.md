### Post by cjssmo on 2007-08-28
Louie

I am a little confused here, you say ampache is a site that allows...., do you have ampache setup on your box or is this someone else's site?  You should not have to "download" the m3u file every time you want to listen to your music.  When the download dialog box comes up select "open with other application" and choose your favorite music player.  You can create multiple playlist with amapche, not to mention you can go through your library and check the song you want to listen to and then click play.  Ampache also has a XSPF Flash MP3 player built in, this was put in for those folks who want to listen to their music at work and are unable to install their favorite player.  On ampache's main page look for the drop down menu that says "Stream" and change it to XSPF Player.  Please if you are having problems with ampache take a look at the ampache wiki page at 

[https://ampache.bountysource.com/wiki](https://ampache.bountysource.com/wiki)

or you can post question to the forum at:

[http://ampache.org/forums/](http://ampache.org/forums/)

Or you can use IRC (usually the fastest way to get help)

IRC freenode #ampache

Hope this helps
