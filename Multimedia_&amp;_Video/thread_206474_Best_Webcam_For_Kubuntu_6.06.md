### Post by cbeaudin on 2006-06-30
Not sure exactly what you are trying to do but ill give some advise anyways. If you want three cameras all connected to the same computer i would recomend a network camera, one you can connect to a router or switch via ethernet that uses web config to use and access them. However that wouldnt be a great idea if you want to use these camera's for msn.

An expensive example would be a DLink DCS-5300. I play around with these at home only cause i get them cheap as loners.They are quite usefull and come with software to moniter, record or take snapshots of up to 16 camera's at once over the network as well as some other options.

Keep in mind i have never tried to find software for this on a linux OS, you shouldnt have a problem viewing video through web config but it may be tricky to find a program with recording and snapshot features.

Also instead of sending webcam through msn you can allow your friends to log into your camera from thier house using IE or Mozilla and they will be able to see the video feed as well, and if you allow them, they can also control the camera (i.e. left right up down)

I dont know if that helped. I just have one of these things at home and its awesome to play around with.
