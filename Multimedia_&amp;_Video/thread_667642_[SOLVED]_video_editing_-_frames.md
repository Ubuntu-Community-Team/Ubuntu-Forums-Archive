### Post by cor2y on 2008-01-14
You can use the gtk or Qt4 python frontend for recordmydesktop, i know of that the gtk oneis in synaptic not too sure about QT version.
From there you can edit the capture ratethe quality of the capture etc the default is 100% but if youy have filesize issues you can reduce this from the options settings in the frontend i can't give a specific percentage to use since it will be subjective and depend on what you plan to do with the file, example maybe something for youtube or to email to someone.
see the main site [http://recordmydesktop.iovar.org](http://recordmydesktop.iovar.org)
