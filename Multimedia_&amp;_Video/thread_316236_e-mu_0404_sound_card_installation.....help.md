### Post by mustang on 2006-12-10
As far as I know, there is zero linux support for any of the EMU line by creative.

There was a lot of lobbying going on at the unofficial EMU forums (where apparently some EMU developers hang out) but there never really was a response. 

I read a couple months backed that the EMU folks sent some specifications to the ALSA group but I have yet to see anything come out of that.

So you don't have any options at the moment in the linux world.
