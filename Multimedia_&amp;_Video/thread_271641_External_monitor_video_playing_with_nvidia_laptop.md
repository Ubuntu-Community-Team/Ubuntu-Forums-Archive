I hav a sony laptop (S-480 line) with nVidia GeForce 6200 Go video card. I've perfectly gotten it to detect and use a 1680x1050 external monitor. I trick the TwinView in my xorg.conf (using nvidia drivers) to have metamodes of NULL forhte laptop screen, and full resolution for my external. So essentially I'm running single screen with a widescreen external.

The problem arises whenever I try to play video. Without the monitor and just on the laptop, I can play video of all kinda (avi mpg whatever) in any media player (gxine vlc etc.). [btw the laptop is 1200x800 res] But is I play vieo on my widescreen monitor as I've described above, as a single display, in any player, the screen will freeze up, novideo will play, sound will continue, but essentially nothing else is accessable. I can still move the mouse curson, but cannot select anything, and as I've said the sound will still play. I have to manually turn off the laptop to get out of this problem.

Any ideas on why I cannot play video with my external widescreen monitor without using the laptop screen?
