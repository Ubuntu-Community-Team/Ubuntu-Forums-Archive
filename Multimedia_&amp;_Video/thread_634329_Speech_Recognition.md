### Post by rvk on 2007-12-09
If you want up-to-date information on speech recognition on Linux, you should take a look at the [VoxForge speech recognition project](http://www.voxforge.org). They are working hard on collecting speech for the creation of so-called acoustic models. Without that 'training material' speech recognition will never work. So donate some of your speech (we still need hours).

If you want to get an idea of what it will be used for, then check out the [gnome-voice-control speech recognition applet](http://live.gnome.org/GnomeVoiceControl). That works with a model created using speech from the VoxForge speech corpus.

The speech that we collect can be used on more projects than Gnome-voice-control, so donating some speech will really contribute to open source speech recognition.
