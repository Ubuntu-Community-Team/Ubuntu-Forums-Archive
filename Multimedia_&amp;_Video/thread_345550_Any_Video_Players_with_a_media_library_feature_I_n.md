### Post by pharcyde on 2007-01-25
I would go with **Amarok**.  It's the player I use.  It has a ton of features including media library, portable media device support (ipod, iriver, creative zen, etc), wikipedia artist info, and Cover management if u want to add covers to ur albums.  There are a whole slew of other features I haven't even touched on but I really enjoy it.  I've also heard that rythmbox is a nice player.

Oops I just realized you were asking about a video player and not an audio player.  Amarok is for audio.  For video players you could look into using Kaffeine which is a KDE video player, though it doesn't have a media library from what I can tell.
