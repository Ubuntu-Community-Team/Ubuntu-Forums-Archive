### Post by jorbuntu on 2008-06-26
I think "gthumb image viewer" is installed by default. This application has a batch option to resize your pictures. 

1) In order to get all your pictures from a folder, change your view. Menu -> View -> Folder (Alt+1).

2) Select all the pictures you want to resize.

3) Menu -> Tools -> Scale images. 

4) Sit back and :popcorn:

Good luck!
