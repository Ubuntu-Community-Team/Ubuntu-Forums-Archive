### Post by radicaledward on 2008-03-04
I recently purchased a behringer uca202. After a little messing with settings, I got it working. The problem now is that using either the multimedia keys on my laptop (dell 1420n) or using my mouse to change the volume solicits almost random behavior from the mixer. Generally, the first touch brings the left channel down to mute and slightly increases the right channel. Holding the button down or forcing the mixer up with the mouse makes the sound go in and out, while keeping the left channel mute. Strangely enough, changing the volume via alsamixer works flawlessly. Any ideas?

### Post by Stason on 2008-04-07
Try setting System==>preferences==>sound===.default mixer tracks . Set it to "master" and make sure both channels are linked in volume control.
