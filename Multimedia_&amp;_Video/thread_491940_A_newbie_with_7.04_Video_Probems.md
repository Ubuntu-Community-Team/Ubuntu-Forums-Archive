### Post by merwizard on 2007-07-04
I get some yellow vertical lines on my Compaq DeskPro EN (Pentium III 1GHz). It has onboard video,  i815  based. The problem is with the video driver. These lines appear when the screen resolution is greater than 1152x864. It works fine at 1024x768. Try 1024x768.

To my surprise, Beryl works with this 6 years old computer It works only with 16bit colors, and the titlebars of the windows are a bit messy,  but it DOES work.
