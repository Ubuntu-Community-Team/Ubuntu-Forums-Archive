### Post by Hallvor on 2007-06-23
It is perfectly normal that it takes a while before the download starts. The average user has hundreds of shared files and only a limited upload capacity, so getting the first chunk may take a while.

It may be a frustrating experience if you are used to bittorrent and other "fast" networks, but you will find a lot of stuff you can`t find anywhere else. So it is worth the wait.
