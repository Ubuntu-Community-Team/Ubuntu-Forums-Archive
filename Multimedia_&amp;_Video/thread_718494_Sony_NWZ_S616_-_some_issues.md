### Post by tristan on 2008-03-08
Howdy all, long time no post. After promising myself not to get another mp3 player, I went out and got myself a 4GB Sony Walkman anyway (the reviews were just too good).

Anyway I immediately connected to my ubuntu box and transferred some mp3s, some jpeg photos and a few videos I'd converted to mp4 using avidemux.

So everything went pretty smoothly. The mp3s, pix and videos all transferred fine and play/display nicely. The S616 is definitely a very slick little unit. I have encountered two annoying things which I'm hoping other owners of A810 or S610 series walkmans can enlighten me on.

1 - The unit is very hissy.Pause a song and the hiss is immediately noticeable.  It's hissier than any other mp3 I own. You get used to it, only to notice how bad it hisses when the power is switched off and the silence returns. 

2 - My windows box won't see it as a UMS device, and MTP is very flakey.

So what do you think, do I have a dud? Or have any of you experienced similar?

Thanks in advance for any answers.
