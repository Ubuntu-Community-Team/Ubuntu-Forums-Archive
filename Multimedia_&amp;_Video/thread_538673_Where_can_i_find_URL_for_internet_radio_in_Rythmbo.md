### Post by Vadi on 2007-08-30
You didn't specify which music you like, so here's the first of the first links that Google gives:

[http://www.live365.com/stations/chubbyparrot](http://www.live365.com/stations/chubbyparrot)

When it open, click the 'Play' button. A small window will pop up from their site, and then Firefox will prompt you to open the radio with Rhyhmbox. If it doesn't, click the green play button and it will.

After you click OK in the firefox window, Rhyhmbox will pop up. On the left side, select 'Radio'.

In the section that comes up you'll se an address that starts like [http://www.live365](http://www.live365) ....

Select that adress, and click play in Rhyhmbox.

Enjoy!
