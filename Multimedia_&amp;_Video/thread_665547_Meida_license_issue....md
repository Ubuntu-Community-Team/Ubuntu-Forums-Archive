### Post by steeleyuk on 2008-01-12
If the files were WMA tracks and are DRM'd, theres no chance of being able to get them playing in anything other than Windows. WMA is a proprietary Microsoft technology and you can only play them under Windows.

In fact, you'd be struggling to play them on anything other than Windows Media Player.
