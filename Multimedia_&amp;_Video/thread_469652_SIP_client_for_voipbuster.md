### Post by pgilmon on 2007-06-10
I have been trying to place calls by using **Ekiga** and voipbuster, following instructions [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=161282"). However, sound quality was very poor.

I tried **Twinkle** instead, and configured it to use G.711 u-law codec. The rest of the settings are the same than for Ekiga, including STUN settings (just disable STUN). Now the quality is much better.
