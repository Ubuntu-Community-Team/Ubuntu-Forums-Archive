### Post by d3v1ant_0n3 on 2006-12-29
I don't see anything particularly wrong with your xorg.conf - the formatting maybe :p

I also have Video ram forced in my xorg:

```
Section "Device"
	Identifier	"Intel Corporation 82845G/GL[Brookdale-G]/GE Chipset Integrated Graphics Device"
	Driver		"i810"
	BusID		"PCI:0:2:0"
	VideoRam	65536
	Option "XAANoOffscreenPixmaps" "true"
```

Note that the amount allocated is shown in BYTES, not Mb.

*EDIT* I *MIGHT* have spotted a problem- Your xorg says ```
Option "XAANoOffscreenPixmaps"
``` where it should say ```
Option "XAANoOffscreenPixmaps" "true"
```
