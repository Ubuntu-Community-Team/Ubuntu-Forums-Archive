### Post by scourge on 2007-06-23
You should definitely get an NVIDIA card. You didn't say whether you need an AGP or PCI-E card, and it would be helpful to know some other specs like CPU model and the amount of RAM.

In any case I recommend the GeForce 7 series. Looks like the GeForce 7300 GT would be the best one for you. You can probably get one for about 60$, maybe less. It delivers decent performance, runs Compiz or Beryl without problems, has low power consumption and has stable (proprietary) drivers. Oh, and get a silent passively cooled model like the ASUS GeForce 7300 GT Silent 256 Mt PCI-E. The actively cooled ones can make a hellish noise, and the fans have a habit of breaking at some point.

If you're looking for more performance please see the [Best Gaming Cards for the Money: June 2007]("http://www.tomshardware.com/2007/06/11/the_best_gaming_video_cards_for_the_money/") article at Tom's Hardware.
