### Post by pinf on 2006-11-24
Are you using tv-out in dual head or Twinview configuration?
I'm using Twinview (DVI+tv-out CRT) in clone mode. This way all you do is shown in both displays (including videos).
Just remember to tweak Xvideo settings (using "nvidia-settings"). I had to choose tv-out for optimal video quality (also choose sync-to-vblank).
