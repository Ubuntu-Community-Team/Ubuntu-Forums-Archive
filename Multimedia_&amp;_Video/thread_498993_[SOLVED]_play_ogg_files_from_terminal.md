### Post by kevinlyfellow on 2007-07-12
ogg123 in the vorbis-tools package works great.  Also there are full fledged commandline media players out there.  You can also use aplay, but I don't know if it depends on ogg123.  Also if you have sound previews enabled, hover over a sound file once you install vorbis-tools.  Cool huh?
