### Post by ron999 on 2007-12-19
Hi
There's an app called **ConvertIT** that I've been using.
This will convert 'any' to mp3.
It's based on ffmpeg, so it should be able to convert anything that ffmpeg can see.
There's information here and a deb file to install it:-[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=567016]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=567016")

:cool:

If you don't like the look of it then there are two other apps in the Ubuntu repos.
One is called **soundconverter** and
one is called **soundkonverter**.
