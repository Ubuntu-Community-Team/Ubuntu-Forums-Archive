### Post by jkwarras on 2006-12-09
Under each "Monitor" section add the refresh rate like this:

```

Section "Monitor"
	Identifier   "MD30219PH"
[B]	HorizSync    30.0 - 83.0
	VertRefresh  56.0 - 76.0[/B]
	Option	    "DPMS"
EndSection

```

As simple as that :) 

**Be sure you know the values your hardware can achieve, you could damage it if you don't know what you're doing.**
