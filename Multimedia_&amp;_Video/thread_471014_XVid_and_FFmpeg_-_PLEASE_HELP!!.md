### Post by cantormath on 2007-06-11
The easiest way is to use Automatix2 or Easy Ubuntu.  I usually recommend Automatix2.....goto [http://getautomatix.com](http://getautomatix.com) and follow the directions for install for your version of ubuntu.  Its easy.  Then open automatix2, Applications>System>automatix, and choose the video/dvd/audio codecs.  That should get you up and running.

Of course it is better to do it all manually, but this is the easy way.
