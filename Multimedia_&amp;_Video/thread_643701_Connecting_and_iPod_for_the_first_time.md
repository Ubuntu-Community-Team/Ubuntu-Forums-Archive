### Post by eth on 2007-12-18
amarok and banshee are ok, but still the iPod is an MTP device (i.e. the firmware is set so that you cannot use its memory as a pendrive) and you have to set up the MTP enviroment properly. 
If Amarok or Banshee or Rhythmbox recognise your iPod, you're done ;) Else...well, I suppose you'll soon be back here :)
