### Post by bryonak on 2007-04-27
Long live Feisty! Finally we can select which mixer tracks to control from the keyboard. In Feisty, just go to System->Preferences->Sound and highlight the desired track in "Default Mixer Tracks" at the bottom (Analog Front in my case)
