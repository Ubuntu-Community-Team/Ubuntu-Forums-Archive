### Post by CarpKing on 2008-01-26
SMPlayer (a frontend for MPlayer)  remembers where you were in a movie when you last had that movie open.  It's in the repositories; it's in Qt so it will probably have lots of dependencies, but it won't pull in half of KDE or anything.  It works just fine in Gnome, and is a great program independent of this particular feature.
