### Post by amirman on 2008-05-04
in case anyone is searching for this solution and ends up here the way i did, i figured it out. as long as you already have your proprietary driver installed and working all you have to do is to go to add/remove programs and look for ATI catalyst Control center. if it doesnt show up you need to enable 3rd party software under software sources. once you get the ATI catalyst control center make sure your TV is hooked up to your Svideo port. in ATI catalyst control center go to your display manager and select your 2nd screen (shown in gray). then go to display modes, its the second tab on the bottom. and click on enable display. now you can watch your favorite videos stored on your laptop with your TV.
