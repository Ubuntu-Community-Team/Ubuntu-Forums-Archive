### Post by GooglePlexity on 2007-01-14
Certain last.fm streams are only available to subscribers.  These include: Loved Radio, Personal Radio, and Individual Tag Radio.  Streams that are available to all users include Neighbor Radio, Global Tag Radio, and Similar Artist Radio.

I highly recommend subscribing.  For only $3 a month, you can tag a bunch of your favorite artists and (streamable) songs and then listen to your personalized radio station.

[http://www.last.fm/subscribe/](http://www.last.fm/subscribe/)
