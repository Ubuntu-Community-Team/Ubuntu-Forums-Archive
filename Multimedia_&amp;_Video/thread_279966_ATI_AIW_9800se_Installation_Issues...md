### Post by murkin on 2006-10-20
looks like you forgot to disable composite. add this to the end of your xorg.conf file:

```

Section "Extensions"
        Option  "Composite" "0"
EndSection

```

### Post by gRoberts on 2006-10-20
Sorry, I totally forgot to post that I resolved this.

I tried everything, and finally gave up, inserted the CD and reinstalled Ubuntu.

This time tho, I downloaded Easy Ubuntu, and selected the ATI Drivers.

Although this didn't work out of the box, I installed the latest drivers from ATI, and recompiled FGLRX (spelling) and rebooted and all is well.

Finally I can play my PC games on Linux with decent graphics.

thanks all the same.
