### Post by gilgongo on 2006-11-18
I think this is in fact simply because the movies are put together sloppily. The "sniffer" code that Flash outputs when exporting a .swf will default to requiring the version of Flash that the movie was made with (or above), regardless of whether the movie can be viewed with a lower version. It's up to the person who saves the .swf manually to edit the version requirement if the move does not in fact depend on Flash 8 (or whatever). Most people don't do this, and this is one of the ways Macromedia ensure that Flash clients are kept up to date.

So I would bet that you can in fact see the movies - but the creator isn't letting you out of ignorance.
