### Post by LorisMaria on 2008-02-18
Don't know if the problem is similar, but I've been having this intermitently for a while. Sometimes, after an system update (I don't bother to check what it's doing, I approve any updates available), the sound is gone, completely. Last two times, it was fixed solely by doing the next upgrade. This time it wouldn't work.

But looking at the answers here in the forum, I went to the volume controls to check possible muted PCM option. Wasn't muted, but the second tab "switches" showed that the external amplifier checkbox was checked. I unchecked it, as I'm not using the external amplifier, should that make any difference. Bingo.

So: volume controls, second tab, "external amplifier" option, uncheck. See if that works for you.:KS
