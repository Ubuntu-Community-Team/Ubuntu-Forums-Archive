### Post by vkkim on 2006-08-10
I have owned several laptops in the past and had always run (or tried to run) various Linux distributions on them.  One of the major problems I always had was sound--you can find one huge thread I had started on a Panasonic laptop I had.  The problems on all my machines had always baffled me, especially since other users of the same laptops had said sound worked fine for them.  Anyway, I finally figured out my problem: I had muted my sound using the hotkeys in the pre-installed Windows before I had installed Linux, and since the distributions at the time did not support the hotkeys, (they work fine on my ThinkPad X60s in Dapper) I could not fix it no matter what I did.

Just popped into my head today, just one more suggestion to try for all those frustrated users out there.  I know, I've been there so many times before.
