### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-03-26
open vlc, go to settings>preferences. check advanced options at the bottom. next, go to playlist>services discovery and check off shoutcast tv. save. you will be able to launch shoutcast tv from playlist.

or you could download Tunapie. all it does is shoutcast radio and tv. it is in synaptic.
