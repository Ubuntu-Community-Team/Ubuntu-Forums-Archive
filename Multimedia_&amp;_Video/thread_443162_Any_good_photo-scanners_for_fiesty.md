### Post by ruibernardo on 2007-05-14
Hi Xadder,

I have a HP PSC 1210 that has a scanner built in. I use it with Gimp (installed by default in Ubuntu). I open Gimp, then I select from the menu File->Capture->Xsane and select my scanner. Xsane starts and thats it. You can edit the fotos in Gimp and save them in TIFF or PNG format or any other format supported by Gimp. I think you don't have to install anything. Try it.

### Post by disturbed1 on 2007-05-14
Look at some of the Epson Perfection models. For most part Sane support is complete.
[http://www.sane-project.org/sane-mfgs.html#Z-EPSON](http://www.sane-project.org/sane-mfgs.html#Z-EPSON)

The perfection series starts at around $100, and the sky is the limit. For ~$140 you can get an Epson scanner that has an auto feed film loader. I'd fire off a message to the Sane list to make sure it's supported before you plop down a decent chunk of change on one though.
