### Post by digby280 on 2006-06-09
I had a similar problem with audio tracks on my computer.  On mine the sound went a bit tinny for some tracks after upgrading to Dapper.  I found that if I turned the PCM volume down to below 74% the sound was fine.

See: [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=183924](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=183924)
