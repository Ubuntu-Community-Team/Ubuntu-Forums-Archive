### Post by Mateo on 2006-12-25
I love Rythmbox (which is probably already preinstalled).  It's so simple but keeps things in order very well.  It also holds internet radio stations and downloads podcasts.  Also it's 1 click to minimize to your tray, which is nice since it doesn't take up space on the taskbar.  There's an applet available too, if you want it, for the taskbar.

Not sure if it syncs to iPod since i don't have one, but shouldn't be hard to figure out.
