### Post by DJ_Peng on 2008-03-25
I've never lost tags when converting with the SoundConverter. For retagging those files that need it for whatever reason (and hopefully someone can help you diagnose why it happened soon) I recommend EasyTag. I'm converting directories full of wma files that don't come out right in SoundConverter for some reason so I'm using the nautilus audio conversion script (which strips the tags during the conversion process) and retagging them with EasyTag. It's saved me a ton of time and effort in my own retagging.
