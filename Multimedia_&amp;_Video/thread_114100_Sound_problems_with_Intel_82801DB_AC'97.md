### Post by racin on 2006-01-08
Good News!! I got my sound working. I went to these tutorials and did most all the stuff in them. [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SoundProblemsHoary]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SoundProblemsHoary")
[http://linux.iuplog.com/default.asp?item=94639]("http://linux.iuplog.com/default.asp?item=94639")
After that I opened alsamixer prefs and turned on external amp then backed out to the alsamixer volume setting and used keyboard m to shut the external amp off and when I did I had sound! I hope this works for you! Good Luck
