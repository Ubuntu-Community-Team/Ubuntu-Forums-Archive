### Post by taurus on 2007-02-14
Your **VertRefresh** needs to be from some lower value to a high value instead of just one value.

And you don't even have a section for your Depth 24 which you are using.

So, edit /etc/X11/xorg.conf

```
sudo /etc/X11/xorg.conf  /etc/X11/xorg.conf.bak
gksudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf
```
and replace

```
Depth   1
```
with

```
Depth   24
```
Save it and reboot.
