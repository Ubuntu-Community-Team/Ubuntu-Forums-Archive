### Post by tamoneya on 2008-04-09
1.  Test your port to make sure that it is open in your firewalls:[http://www.canyouseeme.org/](http://www.canyouseeme.org/)
2.  Enable encryption of packet header.  THis makes it harder for you ISP to detect your packets and block them.
3.  Try to pick torrents with high seeder to leecher ratio.
