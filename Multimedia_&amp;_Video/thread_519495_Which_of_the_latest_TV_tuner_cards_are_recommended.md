Hmm,  I would get an pcHDTV 5500.  Works great in feisty.  Does HD (Although you will probably not be able to get all of your HD channels, I get local only with this) and SD (although no hardware encoder).  For a remote I would get a MCE remote (MCEUSB2).  Great remote

Or you could get a firewire card and stream if you have an STB.  Does HD and SD, but available channels are up to your cable provider.  Again, for the remote the MCEUSB2 is awesome.
