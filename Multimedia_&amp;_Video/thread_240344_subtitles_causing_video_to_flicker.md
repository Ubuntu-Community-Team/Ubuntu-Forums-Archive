### Post by rumli on 2006-08-20
When I use Totem-xine to watch a DVD with subtitles on, the window flickers blue whenever subtitles appear or disappear.  Anyone have a similar experience or know how to fix this?

I found that this flickering only occurs when I use Xinerama (I have a dual monitor setup) and, furthermore, only occurs on one of my monitors.  
The problem goes away if I turn Xinerama off, but I would prefer to keep Xinerama on since it enables me to drag windows between monitors.
When Xinerama is on, this blue flickering only happens on one of my two monitors -- in fact, if I drag the Totem-xine window so it straddles both monitors, only the secondary one flickers.
 
Here are more details of my setup:
I have a an NV35 [GeForce FX 5900 Ultra] video card with dual monitor support.
The two monitors are a 17" Micron 700Ex CRT (main screen, plugged into the video card's VGA slot) and a 19" widescreen Hanns-G HW191D LCD (secondary screen to the left of the main screen, plugged into the video card's DVI slot).
The flickering only happens on the Hanns-G HW191D LCD.
