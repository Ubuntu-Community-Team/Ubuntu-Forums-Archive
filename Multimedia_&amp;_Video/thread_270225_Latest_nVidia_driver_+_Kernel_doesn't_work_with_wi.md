### Post by darkxb on 2006-10-12
I'm running a ViewSonic VA1912wb lcd at 1440x900 and was having similar problems. I went looking for an answer and as a last resort I tried the line from post #5 here. [http://www.nvnews.net/vbulletin/showthread.php?p=1001310]("http://www.nvnews.net/vbulletin/showthread.php?p=1001310")

> Add this to your Section "Device":
Option "ModeValidation" "NoEdidDFPMaxSizeCheck"
That's helped me to use my nativ resolution of 1680x1050.

It worked to get me running widescreen again, but your mileage may vary. I don't know exactly what that line does though...
