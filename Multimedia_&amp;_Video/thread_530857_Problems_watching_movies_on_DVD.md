Are the movies that you are trying to watch from the US?  If so it is probably that the movie format (NTSC) does not match the format supported on the DVD ROM.  The US only supports NTSC while most of the rest of the world supports PAL.  Also did you download the libdvdcs2 package?  That will let you decrypt the DVD's to watch them.
