### Post by wetmoose on 2008-02-11
I originally had this problem when I installed mythbuntu.  To fix it I ran the mythtv-setup. Go through the menus until you get to where it says "Channel Frequency table".  I think the default on this is "us-cable" but you may have to change it match the broadcast that your cable company uses.  I had to change mine to use "us-bcast" and then all of my channels showed up.

Hope that helps.
