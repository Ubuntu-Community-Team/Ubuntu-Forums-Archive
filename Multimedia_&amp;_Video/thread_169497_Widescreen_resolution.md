### Post by wsmoser2004 on 2006-05-02
I used to have this problem too on my widescreen.  I don't think the 8.16.20 drivers support widescreen very well (or maybe not at all), and unfortunately that looks very bad on a widescreen display.  Your xorg.conf file does indeed look correct, it is just that the driver doesn't support it.

In order to get support for your widescreen, you'll need to get the latest drivers ( 8.24.8 ).  There is a how-to [here]("http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Breezy_Installation_Guide").  You will want to scroll down to the section about installing the "newer" ( 8.24.8 ) drivers.  They are a bit harder to install, but they do support widescreen resolutions.
