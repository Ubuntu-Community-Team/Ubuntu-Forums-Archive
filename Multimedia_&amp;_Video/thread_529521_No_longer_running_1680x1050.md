### Post by stinger30au on 2007-08-26
try this....

at the moment your monitor section reads like this
Section "Monitor"
    Identifier     "CMV221D"
    VendorName     "Chimei"
    ModelName      "CMV221D"
    Gamma           1.11 1.03 1.04
    ModeLine       "1680x1050@60" 25.2 640 656 752 800 480 490 492 525 -hsync -vsync
EndSection 


change it to this

sudo nano /etc/X11/xorg.conf


Section "Monitor"
    Identifier     "CMV221D"
    VendorName     "Chimei"
    ModelName      "CMV221D"
    HorizSync 30-110
    VertRefresh 50-70
#    Gamma           1.11 1.03 1.04
#    ModeLine       "1680x1050@60" 25.2 640 656 752 800 480 490 492 525 -hsync -vsync
EndSection


now quit and save and restart.this is what i had to do to get my LCD running right
