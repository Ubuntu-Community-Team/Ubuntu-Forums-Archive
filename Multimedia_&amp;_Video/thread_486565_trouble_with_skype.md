### Post by karhulitos on 2007-06-28
I had same problem althought I'm not sure  if this will directly help you.

I needed to enable capture 1 or 2 (or Mic 1 / Mic 2) in audio settings Spaeaker right click -> open vol control -> edit -> preferences and pick all capture & mic related to vol control view.

Then I just tested enabling Mic 1 and Mic 2 one by one and calling Skype test call until I heard myself.

Hope your case is easy as mine was.
