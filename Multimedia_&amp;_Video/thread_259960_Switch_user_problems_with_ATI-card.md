### Post by mpvano on 2006-09-19
One of the standard "User Privileges" checkboxes in the User's and groups properties dialog is "Use Video Acceleration" I think this must be checked on any new accounts you make for them to get access to the hardware. I can't use acceleration on my machine, but I suspect this may be your problem.

While you're in there, it doesn't hurt to see if some of the other settings are what your second account needs to burn cds, etc....

hope this helps someone!
