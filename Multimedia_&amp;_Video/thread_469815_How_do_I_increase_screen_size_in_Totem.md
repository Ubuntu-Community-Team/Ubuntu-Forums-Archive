### Post by reclusivemonkey on 2007-06-21
A widescreen monitor is not at the same ratio as a widescreen movie. Widescreen movies are at 16:9 whereas your monitor will be at 14.4:9 at the same measurements. You can adjust the ratio of your movies in Totem with View --> Aspect Ratio.
