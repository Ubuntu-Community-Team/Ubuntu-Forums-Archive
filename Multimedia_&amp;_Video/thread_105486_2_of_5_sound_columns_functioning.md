### Post by Galaxyz on 2006-02-25
I also have Creative Sound Blaster 24 and I have the same problem 
In the volume Manager (via ALSA mixer ) only the Analog Front Speaker are working.
The Analog Center (LFE) and the Analog Rear are enabled but I have no sound on this channels.

I think this is not a driver fault but a configuration problem .
Remember That  for Windows , Creative shipped their driver with the EAX Console application and  CMSS application.
After installation in Win I've had the same problem (2 sound channels active)
(After the enable of the CMSS via EAX console I've had the sound on all 5 channels)

So the problem is that for Linux there is no EAX Console or CMSS environement for Sound Blaster 24 .
In the other words there is no application for Linux to properly configure the Sound Blaster 24 driver.
