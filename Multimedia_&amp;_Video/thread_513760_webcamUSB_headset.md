### Post by kyphi on 2007-07-31
Camorama WebCam Viewer is the name of the programme and you will find it by going to Applications (top panel) then Add/Remove then Graphics.  Place a tick into 'Show Unsupported Applications' and look for Camorama.

I use Ubuntu 6.06 - you may find that your menu is differently organised if you are using another version (unfortunately you left that information out).
