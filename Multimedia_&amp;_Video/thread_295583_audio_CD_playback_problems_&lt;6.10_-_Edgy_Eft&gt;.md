Hi,
     I was able to correct the problem. Find your volume control and go to Preferences. You will see "Select the device and track to control". There should be more than 1 choice. In my case there was Navidia and Realtek. Select the one that is not currently selected and reboot. That should do it unless your audio never worked correctly.:-D
