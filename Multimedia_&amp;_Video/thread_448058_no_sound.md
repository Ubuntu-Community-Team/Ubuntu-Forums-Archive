### Post by GhostZrydA on 2007-05-18
Hi, best thing to do is to play a sound file , and then open the volume control by right clicking on the speaker icon (top right hand corner) then using the "edit" tab and choosing "preferences" you can tick all the boxes to show the audio outputs as sliders.

Then one by one slide up the various sliders till you hear the audio playing, then take note for next time which audio output needs to be setup for your system.

If you still have no sound, then your sound card has not been detected and setup right during the install of Ubuntu, thats when it starts to get tricky finding drivers etc!.
