### Post by vamsibethapudy on 2007-12-31
i had a similar problem.
but now its solved.
go to SYSTEM>PREFERENCES>SOUND
in it change the device to PCM.
now lower the volume. & restart.
u might be able to do it.
if that doesn't work then place the cursor on the volume control icon & wait.
u will see the mode in which its set (either MASTER or PCM)
if its set in PCM ,well & good.else change to PCM.
