Hi, Im using a couple of OLD boxes as MythTV boxes.

One is using a SiS6326 8Mb AGP with component (RCA) tv out. note that I had to play with xorg settings to get this to work, and that it works only at resolutions at 800x600 or below. I have it connected to only a TV, no monitor.

Another box uses an ATI card with RCA TV out but I have not been able to get that working yet.

However both of these are RCA only; my suggestion; use an old card like this until you get your new TV at which point you can buy a new card as well. Who knows what technology will show up in 3 months?

### Post by aeiah on 2008-04-17
my nvidia 7800gt has component out, as well as two dvi outs. hdmi is just dvi + digital audio. if you cant find a card with both component and hdmi out then you can easily just use a dvi to hdmi cable and run the audio out seperatley (either digitally to an amp or analogue to an amp / speakers / tv

i have a big fanless heatsink for my 7800 which makes it nice and silent. im sure newer nvidia cards will also have component out but i cant confirm if the newest ones do. mine achieves component and s-video with one of these that came with it:
