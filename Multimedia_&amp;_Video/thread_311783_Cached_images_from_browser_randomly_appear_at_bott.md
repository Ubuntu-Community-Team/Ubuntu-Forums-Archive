### Post by fisherman783 on 2006-12-03
I'm running a dual head setup and have had a very peculiar problem.  From time to time, when I move my cursor from one display to the other, the secondary (bottom) display will shift everything up ("off" the monitor) and a few dozen pixels (varies from instance to instance) at the bottom of the screen will be replace by a horizontal bar made up of a collage of images that have been loaded recently in the web browser.  I fix this by moving the pointer to the top pixel row of the bottom display, which scrolls the collage out of view permanently (meaning it can't be retrieved just by moving the cursor back to the bottom).

The most peculiar thing is that this has happened in a number of different situations.  The two I know for sure that I've experienced this with are Kubuntu Dapper with Xinerama and Konqueror and Ubuntu Edgy with Firefox without Xinerama (current).
