### Post by lixy on 2006-11-17
I found a workaround to that thing, if anyone's interested; Instead of Twinview use the Separate screens option in the display config of the Nvidia settings app. It enables videos fullscreen with the downside that the computer's screen is blacked out.

Anyway, I was curious about why the video would stop playing when lowering the screen's resolution (counter-intuitive). I still am.
