### Post by sisco311 on 2008-02-28
Try to disable your integrated video card from the bios. Read your motherboard manual if you can't figure out how to do it.

EDIT: When you reboot your computer try to press F2 or Delete to enter in the bios menu. Search for the option to disable the card, save the settings and exit.
