### Post by reyfer on 2006-11-17
Hello.

I think it will be difficult to find a single multimedia player that supports all those formats in Linux by default, simply because some of those formats are propietary (DivX, MP3, etc... ) There must be one that does, but you have to install the codecs for those formats yourself.

That said, I use two players: for audio I use Amarok, greatest audio player out there. For video, I use VLC, just because it can play almost every video format knowm to mankind. But that's just me, I prefer to have an app that works great at one thing, that having one that works average at lots of things :)
