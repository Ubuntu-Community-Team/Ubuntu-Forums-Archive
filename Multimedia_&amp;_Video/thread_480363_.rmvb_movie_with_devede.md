### Post by dyous87 on 2007-06-21
Well I just converted it to an .avi using mencoder with the following command:

```
mencoder in.rmvb -oac mp3lame -lameopts preset=128 -ovc lavc -lavcopts vcodec=mpeg4:vbitrate=1200 -ofps 25 -of avi -o out.avi
```

Now I am running it again through DeVede. I wil post my results after I have burned the dvd.
