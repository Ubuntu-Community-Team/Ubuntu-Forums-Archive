### Post by reclusivemonkey on 2007-06-21
Hmm, it seems that Sound Juicer may have timed out when trying to download the CDDB information for your CD off the internet. I imagine this might take some time over a dial up connection. Have you filled in the information in System --> Preferences --> CD Database Server? Its not in the menu by default, so you will have to enable it in System --> Preferences --> Main Menu
