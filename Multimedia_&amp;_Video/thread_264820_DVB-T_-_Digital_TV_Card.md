### Post by pseudonym on 2006-09-25
The place you want to go to is [www.linuxtv.org](www.linuxtv.org) , in particular the wiki. This should answer a good many queries you may have about DVB under linux.

FYI, I use a slightly older DVB-T card made by the same manufacturer, and it performs great under linux, better than in windows (although the analogue input ports are a bit iffy). So even if support is not yet there for your particular model, it's usually only a small matter of time before it is.
