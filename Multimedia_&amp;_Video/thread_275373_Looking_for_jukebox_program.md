### Post by skymt on 2006-10-11
I don't think there's anything like that. I suggest you make a new user and lock it down (low permissions, nothing on the desktop, only one panel with an Amarok launcher the only thing on it). Then, in that user, open Amarok, hit Alt-F3, and choose Advanced > Full Screen. This will only work in KDE, I don't think Gnome has any way to do this.
