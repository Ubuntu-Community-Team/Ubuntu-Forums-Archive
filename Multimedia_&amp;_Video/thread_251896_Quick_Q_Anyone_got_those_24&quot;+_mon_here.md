### Post by plainzwalker on 2006-09-07
You will have to edit the xorg.conf file to get larger resolutions. The biggest problem your going to have is will you video card be able to run the native resolution of the monitor? I know most 17/19" LCD monitors have a native of 1280x1024 while some of the larger 21"+ monitors get into the 1900+ resolutions. Just something you might want to think about.
