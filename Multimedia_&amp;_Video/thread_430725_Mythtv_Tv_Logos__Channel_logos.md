I have been recently playing with the look and feel of mythtv and I would like to share these UK tv logos with everyone thanks to my days of using of Media portal.

As you well know there is more than one way to skin a cat but i use them as follows:

1. Uncompress the logos into a folder out of the way.
2. Write down the names of the required icons on paper.
3. Open myth-backend and manually enter location and name of each logo in the channel boxes on option 5 for example /media/sdb1/UK DVB TV LOGOS/BBC ONE.png

and voila the logo will appear in your guide and bits. 

It is possible to change the names of the logos to replace the ones you already have IF you used xmltv to grab the logos, however i have never done this so i cannot comment.
