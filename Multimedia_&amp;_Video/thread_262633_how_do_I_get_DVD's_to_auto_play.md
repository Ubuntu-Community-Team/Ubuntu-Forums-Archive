regarding VLC: click Applications -- Add/Remove... -- check "Show unsupported applications", wait for it to refresh its package list and search for VLC. MPlayer from mplayerhq.hu is another great (unsupported) player, but you need to download codecs seperately there as well for it to play ball -- and the usual disclaimers apply.
