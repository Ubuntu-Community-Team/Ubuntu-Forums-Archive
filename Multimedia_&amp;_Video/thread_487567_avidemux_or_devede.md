### Post by timcredible on 2007-06-29
the mpeg files are not the problem when it comes to playing on dvd players.  it's more about the dvd media (dvd-r or dvd+r) and if you have multiple audio tracks (ie, if you convert the menu to mpeg2 audio but your videos were converted to aac, then some players have an issue with automatically switching between the 2 - this is similar to having the menu audio in english on a dvd, then having all your chapters in french).  and yes, all dvd menus have audio, even if it's a blank track.  so, any tool will convert to mpeg2 correctly, and, if you have a lot of files, ffmpeg will convert them the fastest and you can probably do multiple files on the same command line (although i don't really like to do much with cli anymore).
