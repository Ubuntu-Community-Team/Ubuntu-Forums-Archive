### Post by Steve H on 2007-04-21
I think i have now got it sorted.

I bought a new DVD-RW yesterday , and fitted last night when i got home from work. When I opened my PC box I noticed there was quite a build up of dust and gunk on the CPU heatsink and fan. So I spent a while cleaning and de-dusting the box, after which i managed to use avidemux to create a DVD mpeg. It worked fine, no lockups or freezes.

It is always the easy stuff that will trip you up.......Turns out it was just overheating and locking and nothing to do with Avidemux, after all!!!
