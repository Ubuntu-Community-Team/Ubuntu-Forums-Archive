### Post by pseudonym on 2006-12-29
Hi, two possibilities are [Acidrip]("http://untrepid.com/acidrip/") and [dvdrip]("http://www.exit1.org/dvdrip/") . I use Acidrip and get good results just with mpeg2, but the tool is designed more for the higher compression formats so will probably suit you fine. I don't use dvdrip but many people swear by it, apparently. Both are available in the Ubuntu repositories.
