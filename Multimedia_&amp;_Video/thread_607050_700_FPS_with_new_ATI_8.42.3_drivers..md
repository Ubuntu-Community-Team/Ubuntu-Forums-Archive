### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2007-11-09
Rippie and Kozmoracer, it sounds like your drivers are not properly installed. Run:
```
fglrxinfo
```
and see if it reports Mesa software rendering instead of your graphic card. Something is definitely wrong because I get 1000fps with my bottom-of-the-line X300SE (64-bit mem bus)

Oh, and Rippie: you need to add 
```
BusID   "PCI:1:0:0"
```
to the Device section of your xorg.conf. **The actual Bus ID may be different**. You can find it with an "lspci" command and look at the numbers in front of the "VGA compatible controller" listing. Take those three numbers and put them in place of 1:0:0.
Also, I would suggest adding the following to your file
```
Section "Files"
	Fontpath	"/usr/share/fonts/X11/misc"
	Fontpath	"/usr/share/fonts/X11/cyrillic"
	Fontpath	"/usr/share/fonts/X11/100dpi/:unscaled"
	Fontpath	"/usr/share/fonts/X11/75dpi/:unscaled"
	Fontpath	"/usr/share/fonts/X11/Type1"
	Fontpath	"/usr/share/fonts/X11/100dpi"
	Fontpath	"/usr/share/fonts/X11/75dpi"
	# path to defoma fonts
	Fontpath	"/var/lib/defoma/x-ttcidfont-conf.d/dirs/TrueType"
EndSection

Section "Module"
	Load		"i2c"
	Load		"bitmap"
	Load		"ddc"
	Load		"dri"
	Load		"extmod"
	Load		"freetype"
	Load		"glx"
	Load		"int10"
	Load		"vbe"
EndSection

Section "DRI"
	Mode	0666
EndSection
```
