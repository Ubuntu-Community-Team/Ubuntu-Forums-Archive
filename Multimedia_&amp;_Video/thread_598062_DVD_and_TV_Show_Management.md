### Post by daengbo on 2007-10-31
I think Eisa (by Fluendo) is a project that will do what you want in the future, but isn't there yet. I use a Mythtv frontend to do this work for me, and it's great. I don't need to do TV recording or anything. Just Movies and music. You can install Mythbuntu and have a system ready to go.

As far as I know, there's nothing to do DAAP for videos yet. I used mt-daapd to share my music from the aforementioned myth box, though.

Best of luck.
