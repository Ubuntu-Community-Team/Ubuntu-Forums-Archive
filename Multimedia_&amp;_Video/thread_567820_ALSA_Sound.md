### Post by abhilash82 on 2007-10-06
I also had the same problem. Open a terminal window. Run alsamixer. Use the Tab key to switch to the capture levels. Try lowering the level of your microphone to see if it helps with the feedback. Hit Escape to exit alsamixer. In alsamixer, use the arrow keys to move left and right and to raise and lower the levels.
