I don't believe you'll see any improvement from getting the driver directly from the Nvidia site.  I tried it once, and found that it was the same driver as the one listed in the repository as nvidia-glx-new.  I just use the restricted driver manager, which gives me the known stable version of the driver and also has the benefit of notifying me if the driver has updates available.

I think some people have found improvements from the "new" driver, but I suspect those are edge cases.. people with some strange card which isn't adequately supported and for which Nvidia had recently added support.

It can't hurt to try, but I very much doubt you'll see an improvement in anything.

HTH.
