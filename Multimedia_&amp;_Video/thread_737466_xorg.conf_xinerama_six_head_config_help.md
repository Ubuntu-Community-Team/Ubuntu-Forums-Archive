### Post by DarthSensate on 2008-03-27
I am building a new Ubuntu system with three dual head nvidia PCI cards.
(six head system)

I am able to set the xorg.conf with the device,monitor, and screen configs for each display, but I am only able intialize two of the three cards at the same time.

The cards are located at PCI:0:8:0, PCI:0:9:0, PCI:0:10:0 respectively.
Devices are "device[0-5]"
If I set the screen layout to enable screens 0-5 all with RightOf orientation respective to each other only four of the screens start.

I can choose which bank of two screens work by assigning screens 0-3 to device0-1 and 2-3 or 0-1 and 4-5, but screens 4-5 never enable regardless of which device is assigned to them.  Xorg.0.log reports that the third card to be enabled, whether it currently be PCI:0:9:0 or PCI:0:10:0, "Failed to initialize..."

ex.  "(EE) NVIDIA(4): Failed to initialize the NVIDIA graphics device PCI:0:10:0."  

Is there some other limitation in xorg that prevents more than four devices enabling??
I have referenced this conf example as well to try and fix this issue to no avail:
[http://www.rrlug.org/forums/viewtopic.php?t=49](http://www.rrlug.org/forums/viewtopic.php?t=49)
