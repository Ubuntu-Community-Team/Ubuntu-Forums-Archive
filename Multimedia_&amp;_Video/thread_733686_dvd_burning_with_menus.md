### Post by warp99 on 2008-03-24
You can use qdvdauthor, which is the GUI for dvdauthor:

[http://qdvdauthor.sourceforge.net/](http://qdvdauthor.sourceforge.net/)

or you can use KDVD Creator:

[http://www.kde-apps.org/content/show.php/KDVDCreator?content=73014](http://www.kde-apps.org/content/show.php/KDVDCreator?content=73014)

Qdvdauthor is available in the multiverse repositories while with KDVDCreator you would have to download and install, plus it needs the KDE 4 libs. Qdvdauthor is most likely you best bet. There are some little quirks to the program, but these forums are a good source of information:

[http://sourceforge.net/forum/?group_id=98243](http://sourceforge.net/forum/?group_id=98243)

Other then that it works rather well. :)
