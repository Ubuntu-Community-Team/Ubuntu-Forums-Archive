### Post by WiFi Ed on 2007-09-24
I don't believe that you can run the built-in (motherboard) video and an add-in video card at the same time. You need a video card with two output connectors (two vga connectors, or one vga, one DVI, or two DVI's).

Edit: I just googled your ATI card, it looks like it has one vga, one DVI connector. Connect the hp 1502 to the vga connector and the vp17 to the DVI connector and try it again in Windows. It "should" be a piece of cake. Getting it to work in Ubuntu will be a little bit more difficult...
