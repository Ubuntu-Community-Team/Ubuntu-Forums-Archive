### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2007-05-28
If you are using the default Gnome installation, there should already be one there.  The little speaker icon next to the date.  If not, right-click the panel and choose add to panel.  Then look for the volume control applet.

### Post by VirtualPirate on 2007-05-28
you can also apt-get any mixer application and when installed look for it under Applications in the panel.... right click on it and 'add this launcher to the panel'.
