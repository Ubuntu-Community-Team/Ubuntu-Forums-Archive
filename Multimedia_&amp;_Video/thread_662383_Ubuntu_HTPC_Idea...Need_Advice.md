### Post by tgalati4 on 2008-01-09
It might be simpler to run long component cables to your TV from your computer.  Use high-quality RG-6 cables.  You can run up to ~100 feet without noticeable degradation.  Get a bluetooth/USB adapter--one of the extended range ones--out to 100 feet.  

Load a bluetooth remote control jar application on your phone, and use it as a remote/mouse.  You can also use a bluetooth keyboard as well for big screen googleearth traveling.
