### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-10-12
Hi. Launch them from a terminal (Applications->Accessories->Terminal) and see if they leave any error messages there when they crash. To launch them from the terminal, write **totem** or **vlc** and press enter. If they do leave errors there, please post them here.

Afterwards, if you have desktop effects on, try disabling them and then launching the programs again to see if they crash this time. Do you have an Intel video card?
