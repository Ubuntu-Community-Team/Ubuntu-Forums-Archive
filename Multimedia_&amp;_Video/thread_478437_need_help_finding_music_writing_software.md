### Post by Gwasanaethau on 2007-06-19
[LilyPond]("http://lilypond.org") is pretty good. It's a markup language for music (apparently it's similar to LaTeX but I've never used LaTeX so I can't verify that myself). It makes some nice looking scores.

There is one drawback however; it can take a bit of time to get your head around some of the syntax. But, once mastered, can be quicker and easier than Sibelius or any other GUI based notation software.
