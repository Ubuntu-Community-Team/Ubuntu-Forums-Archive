### Post by kostkon on 2007-08-07
*Amaro*k and *Rhythmbox* (or even *Banshee*, etc.) will allow you put only songs into your *iPod*.

If you want an one for all solution for songs, videos, podcasts, there are two good programs.

The one is *gtkpod* and the other is *Floola*. You can install *gtkpod* from *Synaptic*, better select the *gtkpod-aac* package and you can download Floola from [its official site]("http://www.floola.com/").

I have to say that I prefer *Floola*.
