### Post by kostkon on 2007-07-22
I have Ubuntu and when I connect my Ipod Video it shows up as a mounted device; I can access its contents from Nautilus and actually copy any file I want into it.

What happens when you connect your iPod? Can you see it mounted and appearing as a device in *Computer*?

### Post by Valderrama on 2007-07-23
Check out gtkpod and gnupod (gnupod-tools). Gtkpod provides for a syncable "library" but doesn't require one. Gnupod is a set of perl scripts that will allow you to work straight from the command line.
