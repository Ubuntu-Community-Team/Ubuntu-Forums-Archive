### Post by hugmenot on 2008-03-10
It submits the track playing as soon as 50% of the track has been played consecutively. For me the website lists the last track as "just listened" after 50% has been played.

[http://www.audioscrobbler.net/development/protocol/](http://www.audioscrobbler.net/development/protocol/)

The Now-Playing extension of the protocol apparently came later than the QLScrobbler plugin.
