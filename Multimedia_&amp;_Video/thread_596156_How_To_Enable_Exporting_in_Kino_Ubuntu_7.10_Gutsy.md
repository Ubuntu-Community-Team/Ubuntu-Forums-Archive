### Post by Sabot on 2007-10-29
When I first installed Kino I clicked on the mpeg tab and all the info was grayed out.  It said I was missing mpeg2enc. I did a search in Synaptic and could not find that file.  After a little research I found the solution.  Here are the packages you want to install with Synaptic to get the full complement of exporting options:

mjpegtools
ffmpeg
mencoder

Once you have these files installed, Close and reopen Kino and you should be good to go.
