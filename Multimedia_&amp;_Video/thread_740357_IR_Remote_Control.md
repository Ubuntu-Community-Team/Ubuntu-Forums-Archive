### Post by teet on 2008-04-02
Just got my remote in today...I'm really impressed...especially since the thing was <$30 with shipping and everything.

The cord for the USB IR receiver is super long (it has to be 10 t0 12 feet).  The MCE remote that came with my hauppage card was only like 3' long at max.  Also, it didn't say so on the website, but it actually came with 2 IR blasters.  These could come in really handy someday.

I'm using it with mythbuntu.  I just went into the mythbuntu control center, and chose to activate the "new version" of the windows MCE remote.  Rebooted (I really only needed to reload LIRC and mythbackend but it's just easier to reboot) and it worked like a charm.

-teet
