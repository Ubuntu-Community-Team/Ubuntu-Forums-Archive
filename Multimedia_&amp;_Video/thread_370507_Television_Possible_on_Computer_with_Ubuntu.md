### Post by Jose Catre-Vandis on 2007-02-25
Yes, there is a wide range of internal, external and usb tv devices that work on Ubuntu/Linux.

I recommend a trip to [www.linuxtv.org](www.linuxtv.org) and the gentoo wiki on getting tv setup.

You may need to download the latest dvb-kernel to get things going, depneding on what you use

### Post by n3gbz on 2007-02-25
I picked up an inexpensive PCI TV Tuner (a LeadTek discontinued model) and it works great with tvtime (available in Synaptic Package Manager). I do use it with MythTV but the analog/digital conversion uses up all my processor resources.  

For simple TV watching inexpensive works great.  Mine was discounted since it only handled 100 channels.  Came with a very nice remote.  

For MYTHTV recording you should go with a better Hauppage.with a hardware-based video encoder.
