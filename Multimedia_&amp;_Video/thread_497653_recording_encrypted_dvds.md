### Post by TwistedLincoln on 2007-07-10
I don't think either Mplayer or Mencoder contain the libdvdcss necessary to defeat the encryption.  He did ask about encrypted DVDs.

Keep in mind that if you live in the US, it is currently a crime under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act to bypass copy protection such as that on your DVDs -- even if you are doing so to use the product in a legal way... such as play it back on Linux.

Not sure what the rules are on this forum regarding the issue, but I for one would hesitate to give instructions on how to break the law.  It's easy enough to find with a Google search, however.
