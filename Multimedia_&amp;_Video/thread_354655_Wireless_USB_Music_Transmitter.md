### Post by Karl S. on 2007-03-17
Came up with a much better answer. I dragged out an old computer and copied all my music onto it. With that plugged into my stereo, I can play music through the stereo from my laptop without having to buy anything new or having anything extra plugged into my laptop.

By setting up the old computer to logon automatically, I can access it through vnc viewer and throw the old computer's keyboard and monitor back into the basement. Perfect for listening to and controlling music, saving memory on my laptop (since Amarok's such a memory hog), and for getting my music to last.fm.
