### Post by greghill on 2007-07-06
Your symptoms seem all too familiar to me ("shudder"!)
I had the same problem about a month ago. It does indeed sound like the motherboard may be bad. Check the capacitors. They should be "flat" not rounded and should not show any signs of leakage. If you see a liquid or even a dry powdery coating around the top of the capacitors, they are probably bad. My solution was to replace the motherboard with a new one (which also meant upgrading the processor - OH JOY!) and getting a gig of RAM to boot. End result - this machine now flies! :p
This is what worked for me - not a guarantee that it's your problem, but it does sound like the board to me. Maybe someone else with more experience can help with another suggestion. Good luck.
