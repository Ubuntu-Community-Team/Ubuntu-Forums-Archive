### Post by britjm on 2007-11-09
PNG is the best for web images because it's a lossless format. Granted, you'll take a size hit, but unless you're using photographs or animation, PNG is what you want. JPEG is STILL the best for photos for some strange reason.

As far as music goes, either 256k Ogg Vorbis or 320k MP3. If you're going MP3, go with VBR.

I'll add that if you have a decent pair of headphones or speakers, go with FLAC for your favorite music. That way, you get the quality without wasting all your space, you dig?
