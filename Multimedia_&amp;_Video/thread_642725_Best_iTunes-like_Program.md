### Post by ycason on 2007-12-17
Hello,

'Amarok' is very good with Ipods.  'Rhythmbox' is installed by default, so you could try that out and see if it meets your needs.  'Listen' is another good program and probably my favorite this program will even convert songs from formats other than mp3 to be loaded onto the Ipod.

As far as deleting your Ipod when you plug it in... Only Itunes does that, other programs have enough sense to realize that's not what you want.  

I'm not sure about built in music stores, I prefer to just buy cds from places like BMGmusic or amazon.  Usually ends up being cheaper per song and comes in a lossless format (CD) :).  

Hope I've been of help.
