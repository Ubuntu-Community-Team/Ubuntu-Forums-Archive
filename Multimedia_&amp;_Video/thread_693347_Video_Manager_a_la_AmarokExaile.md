### Post by p_quarles on 2008-02-22
You can actually use Amarok itself to track and launch videos. You just need the "Autoplay Video" script, available [here](http://www.kde-apps.org/content/show.php/Autoplay+Videos?content=72793)

It uses Mplayer by default, but it's easy to edit the script to make it use VLC or Kaffeine, too. Anyway, I had been using Miro for a while, was always frustrated by its bugginess and bloat, and found this to be a perfect solution.
