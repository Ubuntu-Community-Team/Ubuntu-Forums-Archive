### Post by hdixon on 2008-04-12
Just FYI to anyone wondering - I got the motorola D200 USB bluetooth adapter (supposedly windoze only) and the Motorola DC800 stereo adatper. Setup the DC800, plugged the dongle into an Apple laptop with OSX. The laptop has VLC installed. Under the Audio menu, VLC "sees" the D200 and changing to that the audio stream plays great on my home stereo.

Wondering if I would have the same luck under Ubuntu 7.10 I plugged in the dongle on my office Ubuntu desktop, started VLC but the device was not initially present. I went into the prefs, Audio section , and refreshed the ALSA devices list and the D200 audio device showed up. I set it to the default and was able to stream music from my web server thru VLC to my stereo system downstairs.

VLC is one great app!
