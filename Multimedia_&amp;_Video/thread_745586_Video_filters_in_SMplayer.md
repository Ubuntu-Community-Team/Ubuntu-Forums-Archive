I found that I can switch on the filters manually via Options==>View info and properties==>Video filters in SMplayer, but same filters are not picked up via ~/ .mplayer/config. Any way to make them persistent?

---

### Post by rvm4000 on 2008-04-04
You can also add the video filters in Preferences->Advanced->Options for MPlayer. That way the filters will apply for all videos.

SMPlayer doesn't modify any of the mplayer config files.
