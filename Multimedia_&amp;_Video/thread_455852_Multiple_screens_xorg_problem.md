### Post by Curtisc on 2007-05-28
I think the problem is that you haven't named your monitors.  Your monitor identifiers (names) are the same as those of the video card.  You should be able to just change:
```
Section "Monitor"
Identifier "0 Generic Video Card"
Option "DPMS"
EndSection 
```
to
```
Section "Monitor"
Identifier "Main Monitor"
Option "DPMS"
EndSection 
```
and do the same thing to the other monitor (change "1 Generic Video Card" to "Second Monitor").  Hopefully that should clear things up.
