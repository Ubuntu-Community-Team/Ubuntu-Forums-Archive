### Post by j0eb0b on 2006-06-10
Fixed,

All multimedia helper applications are called from within Foxfire (Edit, Preferences, Downloads).  From here i could define helpers that seem to have gotten all  the streams I am interested in playable.  I guess this is comon knowledge to others but new for me.  For anyone having sound problems on streams, check this configuration.  I had a number of entries for Windows Media Player which couldn't have been right.
