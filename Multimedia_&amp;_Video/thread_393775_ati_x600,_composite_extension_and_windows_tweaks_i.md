### Post by alien21010 on 2007-04-02
Installing fglrx will do you no good... ATI's proprietary drivers do not support Composite either, so you will never have a fully functioning Beryl as someone with an Nvidia card would.  However, you can have a mostly functioning Beryl (just lacking true transparency).  

There are many, many guides on how to get beryl properly working with ATI cards already out there on the net.
