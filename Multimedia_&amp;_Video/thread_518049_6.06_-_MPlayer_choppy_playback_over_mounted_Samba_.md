### Post by yaustar on 2007-08-05
At present I have mounted a samba shared folder from Windows onto Ubuntu 6.06, in Totem, the AVIs play smoothly over the network, MPlayer shudders every second. Is there a setting MPlayer I need to change?

Cheers

---

### Post by yaustar on 2007-08-05
It turns out there is:

Open up MPlayer
Go to Preferences
Misc tab, tick Caching

No more choppy playback :)
