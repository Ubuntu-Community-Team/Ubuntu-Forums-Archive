### Post by omegamike3 on 2007-12-13
Without doing some major googling, off the top of my head, what you'll want to do is setup a UPnP server on your machine. Then using UPnP clients on the other machines, you'll be able to stream your media. As far as streaming with xbmc, I can't think of anything off the top of my head, so hopefully someone else will help you out there. Also, depending on the kind of way you're hoping to access your media, you could always set it up as a small web server, using a web interface to list all of your media. Granted that would take some work, unless there's something out there than can help you out. Again, this is just off the top of my head.
