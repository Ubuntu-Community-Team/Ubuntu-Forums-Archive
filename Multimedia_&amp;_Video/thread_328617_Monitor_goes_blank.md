### Post by hikaricore on 2006-12-31
It could be a video driver issue.  But to figure out what is happening you'll need to figure out why it's happening.
Is it just with firefox or does it happen when you use a different browser?
Are you using a flash plugin?  Is it flash 7 or the flash 9 beta version?
What other software is running at the time?
Do you get a terminal login when you press: Ctrl+Alt+F1?  Or the the monitor make a sound then stay blank?

I had an issue somewhat like this after getting a new monitor which I was using with my old integrated intel video chipset, all screens would just be blank no matter which on I switched to.  The system was still running and if could login blindly and force a: **sudo reboot -t now** or I could login from an ssh client on a different system.  Very odd issue which I never solved, aside from buying a new video card.

Good luck,
