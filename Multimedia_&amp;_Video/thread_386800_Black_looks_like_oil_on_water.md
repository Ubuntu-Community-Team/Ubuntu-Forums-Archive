### Post by hikaricore on 2007-03-17
One thing you may want to consider is that beryl has options for lighting effects (General/Main/Lighting Checkbox), transparency (everywhere, can't list them all), and reflections (lost the location of this function in all the changes to beryl, I'm sure it still exists somewhere.  this reflects your skybox image or another image of your choice on your desktop if you're using a cube desktop).  What i'm saying is that there are alot of things in beryl alone that can cause things to look weird, and you may only initially notice them on really light or dark colors.  Take a look at your settings, even try temporarily disabling beryl via beryl-manager to see if this has any effect on your problem.
