Even though I fear, this post could be swallowed in the usual "Help! My Mp3s do not play!"-Posts before it reaches  the ones adressed by it,..


I hereby express my gravest thanks and compliments to the makers of Ubuntustudio 7.10!!

It installs and works just great on my new MSI-Laptop and on my P4-Audioworkstation. Technically its 32bit-flavour beats even the mighty 64Studio (same amount of xruns but whith much less  distortion/clicks in the alsabackend) when running synthsoftware. Suse JAD is technically level in terms of performance but Ubuntustudio looks much better with XFCE and has better Desktopintegration.

These are the results of the tests in the first 3 Days after installation. I will keep testing it in realworld-scenarios (multitrack-recordings with rockbands, working with 40+ tracks in Ardour and the like...).
