I'm sure the settings I use for VLC may not be the right ones for you, but in tweaking VLC to look good, one of the key steps was to change Deinterlace mode to Linear.  I'm not even sure what that means, but in experimenting I found that it helped.  Make a backup of the .vlc folder in your home directory, then experiment a bit with the settings.  Remember that some of then won't take effect until you restart VLC, so this can be a long and tedious process.  But, having gotten VLC working to my satisfaction, I wouldn't use anything else.   It's a very nice player!

HTH.
