### Post by bb002 on 2006-10-31
I can't help but think that your xorg.conf is overkill. Everytime I've had ubuntu not listing the proper resolutions for my monitor all i have had to do to fix it was add the proper refresh rates to xorg.conf.


I'm sure this xorg.conf will work as while being less complex.
