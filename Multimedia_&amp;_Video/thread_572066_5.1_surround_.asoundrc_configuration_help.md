This page explains what you need.

[http://alsa.opensrc.org/index.php/Playing_stereo_on_surround_sound_setup_%28Howto%29](http://alsa.opensrc.org/index.php/Playing_stereo_on_surround_sound_setup_%28Howto%29)

(right click create document, empty file name it .asoundrc)

If you do this im not sure what will happen when you play 5.1 sound. I think you will need to restart alsa and remove/rename your .asoundrc to get 5.1 to work properly again.

Also if you have the ac97 audio sound thing you can just double click the speaker on the top menu strip. do edit preferences check duplicate front then goto switches enable duplicate front and it will go. then you just uncheck whenever you want 5.1
