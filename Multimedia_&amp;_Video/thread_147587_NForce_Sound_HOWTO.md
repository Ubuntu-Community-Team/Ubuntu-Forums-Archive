### Post by mjwood0 on 2006-03-20
The actual installation of the NForce sound driver isn't too bad, depending which chipset you have.

If you have the NForce 410 chipset, I may be able to help.  However, you'll probably have to compile a new kernel (2.6.15) to fully support the OSS sound system you'll need.

Basically, the driver install is easy if you have the right kernel.  Otherwise, it won't work.  There's a good thread on the nvidia linux forum right now about this.  It's quite long, but did work for me!
