### Post by pyrr on 2007-10-30
You may need to copy or softlink the plugins to Opera's "plugins" directory, the automated installer may neglect that. I seem to recall having to do that with Swiftfox, the automated installer just copied them to one location, which isn't necessarily the working plugin directory for browsers added later. The plugin files for Flash are "flashplayer.xpt" and "libflashplayer.so"
