### Post by UpMarc on 2007-01-03
dnccnd,

      I have the same interest as you and have used the Avidemux to do what you want (my second main interest with my PC). But must say that I couldn't find yet an equivallent to Windows Movie Maker to Linux / Ubuntu.
      Avidemux has yet some problems like:
      - it has no timeline to easy the visualisation of your cutting, so you take a much longer 
        time to reach the point you want to cut;
      - this also difficults the "copy/paste" operations;
      - sometimes, it "forgets" the cutting points;
      - sometimes it doesn't save properly the project you are working on;
      - sometimes it can't read the file created by utself...
      - and others
      Nevertheless, despite of those difficulties, specially the time you take to complete a work, I have used it with very good quality in the results.
      I'm Brazilian and would like to get in touch with people with the same interest/problems so that we could all discuss these problems and - who knows? - get a good application to construct/rebuild our movies, clips etc. You and others can find me at Skype, IRC ubuntu-br or, less frequently, at MSN.
