### Post by fearevilleet on 2007-05-11
You should be able to play the videos with VLC. Just drag the VIEDO_TS folder into vlc and it should play. If you want to burn the files to a dvd, use gome baker and drag the contents of VIEDO_TS into gome baker. Make sure you specify that you want to create a video dvd.
