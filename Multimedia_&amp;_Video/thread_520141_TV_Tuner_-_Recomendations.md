1.  PVR-150 for SD, pcHDTV for HD
2.  The PVR-150 will give you channels 1-72 (maybe 125, i havent' checked).  The HDTV will only get you local HD channels
3.  see 2
4.  The way mine is setup (Comcast in Salem, OR), I have a PVR-150 giving me SD on 1-72, a pcHDTV 5500 giving me local HD, and a cable box streaming over firewire that gives me all HD channels*.  This way I get all my SD (SD over firewire is all distorted), local HD channels on the pcHDTV, and since I also have HD over firewire it acts as a backup to the 5500 (for show conflicts) and also gives me extra HD channels (ESPN, ESPN2, Discovery HD Theater, etc)


*your local cable company determines which channels you can record from via firewire.  Check your box for copy control and the 5c implementation.
