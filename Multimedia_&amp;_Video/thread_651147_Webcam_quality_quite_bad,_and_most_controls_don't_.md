### Post by Kevin Funnell on 2007-12-27
Good day,

I have a cheap WebCam (under$10 Aust), had the same problems as you are having (skin tones - grey, Hair orange instead of grey etc.,,etc.,), WebCam worked with no extra drivers etc., had to be installed.  
I installed Camorama believe from the Ubuntu Repositories.  In the Camorama clicked on the clear area below Effects - then Add Filter  - Color Correction.  Now I have a crystal clear picture.

Hope this helps you,
Old Kevin
