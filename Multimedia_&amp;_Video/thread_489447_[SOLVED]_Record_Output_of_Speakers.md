### Post by Super TWiT on 2007-07-01

---

### Post by Super TWiT on 2007-07-02
I fixed it!:KS  I set the recording and playback device in Audacity to Alsa instead of OSS in the settings menu.  It also enabled me to record in stereo instead of only mono (I had to record in mono because it would be distorted before if I didn't.)  I also had to set the recording device to Mixer in the Alsa mixer.
