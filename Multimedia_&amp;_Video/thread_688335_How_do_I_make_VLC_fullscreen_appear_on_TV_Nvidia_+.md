### Post by Leonin on 2008-02-06
I'm at work now so I cant reach my xorg.conf but I can explain how I did. But remember that i'm a beginner so the whole thing might not be correctly done, but it works for me.  
1)Download  [Envy]("http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html"). 
2)Install the drivers. But be sure to uninstall your old drivers first if you have installed them before.
3)Restart you system.
4) Go to terminal ```
sudo nvidia-settings
```
5) Choose X Server Display Configuration
6) Now you should see the nvidia settings UI and hopefully 2 screens, your TV and your computerscreen. If not turn on your TV/check your cables)
7) Click on the TV "box". And enable it. 
8 ) I choose "Twin View". You can find it under the "configure" button. Don't know if thats the best thing to choose but it works for me. 
9) Now your TV should be working. I hope. 

If you have some problem when watching Video in fullscreen on you TV (the screen continues from your TV to the Monitor) choose Clones which you can find under "Position:" when you want to watch a movie.   I know its not a perfect solution but it works for the time being.
