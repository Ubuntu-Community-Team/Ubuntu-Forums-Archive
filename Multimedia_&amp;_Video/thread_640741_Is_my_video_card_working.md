### Post by sloggerkhan on 2007-12-14
Okay, 2 things here.
One, you only have 1 monitor, I assume.
get to that in a sec.
1st, in the "Module" section,
I think you need lines for
Load  "GLcore"
Load "glx"
Load "dri"
(I think there are more modules that can be put there, but at a minimum, I think you need those.)

2nd: get rid of the sections that aren't ati config one. They are probably left over from using the ati driver.
They are:
the 17s monitor section
the Generic Video Card device (though you might want to add the Bus ID to the other device sections
the "Default screen" section.

You could also try dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg. 
In any case, your xorg.conf isn't quite right.

You might also look up other threads on ATI card drivers, there tend to be a fair number.
