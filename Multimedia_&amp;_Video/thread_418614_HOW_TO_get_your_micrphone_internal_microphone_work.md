### Post by storky on 2007-04-22
I had been using ubuntu edgy for around a month but was disappointed that i could not seem to get my microphone working. I had searched every thread i could find but everyone was having the same problem i did, they would enable all the capture and mic settings, make sure the volumes were up in ALSAmixer or in the volume control but would still get no volume. 
This morning i upgraded to fiesty and was checking out some of the new apps when i ran across the gnome alsamixer under application -> sound and video. To tell u the truth i had actually gaven up on my microphone but decided to see what this was all about, inside I found all the usual control but even though i had all the mic settings activated in ALSAmixer, through the terminal, there was still one here that was muted, all i did was uncheck that box and low and behold, audacity could now here my voice.
