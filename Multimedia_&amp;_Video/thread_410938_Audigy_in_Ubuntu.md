### Post by Smuve on 2007-04-16
Honestly, I'm fairly new as well and haven't tested this to see if it is true 5.1 or simulated stereo, but I do have all five speakers making noise from my Audigy 2 ZS.

I'm not at my machine now so you'll have to use a little of own brilliance here.  Anyway, go to your volume control, right-click around and work your way to the properties dialog.  Then there is an additional Edit or Options menu item that allows you to enable many more controls (more than the 5 or so defaults).  Enable all appropriate for surround and turn them all up.

It's not the most scientific approach, but my rear speakers are working.  It's enough to get me by until I really figure it out.  Hmm. . . I should probably just try an HD movie trailor.  Maybe I'll do that tonight.
