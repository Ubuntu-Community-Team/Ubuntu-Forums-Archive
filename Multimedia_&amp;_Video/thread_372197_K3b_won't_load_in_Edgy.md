### Post by MichaelSM on 2007-03-23
I had a few problems with K3b in Edgy(Gnome) but they sorted themselves out after I went to Synaptic and searched K3b and selected the packages which made sense ie. anything which looked useful especially mp3 codecs and lib/dev whatever which looked right. Re-start of course. I tried without a re-start and everything froze up! 
K3b at least on my PC cracks the shits if a cd is waiting in the cd writer. If you  insert one when prompted, a box pops up with a few options. Click ignore. Assuming you've dragged and dropped your mp3s into the main box, everything should work without a hitch. It's a slow burn, but it works. And I could play the cd in my car's crappy Kenwood. Which was the whole idea! Cheers, Mike.
