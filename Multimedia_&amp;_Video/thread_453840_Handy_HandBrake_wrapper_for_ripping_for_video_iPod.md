### Post by soze on 2007-05-24
dvpod is a simple and handy wrapper for ripping tracks from DVDs suitable for playing on a video iPod.

In particular you can batch rip tracks to individual files, this is particularly useful for DVDs of TV shows, as disks typically contain multiple episodes which you would likely want as individual files on your iPod.

dvpod can be found at: [http://sourceforge.net/projects/dvpod](http://sourceforge.net/projects/dvpod)
