### Post by Chappy7777 on 2007-06-09
1st of all, I am fairly new to this forum (joined in Feb., sameday I loaded Ubuntu), however I have noticed that most of the time if one asks a question like you did it's best to include info like your Soundcard make, version of Ubuntu, CPU type, amount of Ram, etc. Especially, in your case, info on the sound card.

Is your card an onboard card or is it in a PCI slot? What make?

I was having no sound after I hooked up a new modem and assumed the prob was a conflict. I started looking around and in the help file I looked up Sound and saw it was suggested that I download the newest Gnome Device Manager. I went to add/remove under Apps and did a search for Device Manager, the a download and install. After the install I looked at it and saw my sound card listed and enabled a couple things and bingo. sound from Streamtuner. 

I had tried just checking Sound under System/Preferences and that did nothing. Check out your Device Manager under System. See if Ubuntu is seeing your card. Do you get the opening sound when the Username box pops up at the start? 

God bless, Chappy

PS. Ask your question again and put in the needed info. At least it will move to head of the list. ;-)
