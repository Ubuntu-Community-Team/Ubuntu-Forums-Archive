Hey Everybody.
I was having trouble with my nVidia GeForce 5200 FX Video card.  My computer became really unstable and would crash all the time.  I found away to fix this and I think other people should now how.  I went to:

System->Administration->Screen and Graphics Preferences->Graphics Card

I then set the driver to NVIDIA Geforce 6800 (generic).

I have experienced no problems since and I hope it works for you.
