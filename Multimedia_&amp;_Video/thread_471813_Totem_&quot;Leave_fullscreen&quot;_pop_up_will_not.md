### Post by tinker123 on 2007-06-30
I fixed this.

I remember that after installing the never version of Ubuntu  I installed the Beryl window manager and uninstalled it after I got odd and buggy behaviour of various kinds.   I went back to the web site that had the directions I used to install Beryl.    Though I had removed all of the Beryl packages I still  had a call to Beryl Manager in my startup list at  System | Preferences | Sessions .    I removed it from startup,  rebooted and the problem seems to be gone.

If you suspect that you might have a similar problem try an experiment.   Switch to another desktop and back.  If the "Leave Fullscreen" box goes away where it would not before, you may have this issue.
