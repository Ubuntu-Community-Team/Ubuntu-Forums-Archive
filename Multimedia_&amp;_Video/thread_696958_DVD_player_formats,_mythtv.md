### Post by jeffus_il on 2008-02-15
If your camcorder has dv capability and can connect using firewire, I would not use the analog connection through rca, since the quality is much lower and the capture process is much more complex. The firewire connection simplifies the process, is a digital to digital copy rather than digital to analog, then analog to digital, also the computer can control the camcorder, like a vcr, really simplifies the process ...
