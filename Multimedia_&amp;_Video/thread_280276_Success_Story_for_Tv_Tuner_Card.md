### Post by babwe on 2006-10-19
hi just wanted to tell my little story of success installing COMBRO TECHNOLOGY VideoMate TV PVR 
Tuner Card On Ubuntu 5.10
First I Installed the card on the PC, then I downloaded tvtime-1.02.tar.gz from [http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=64301]("http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=64301")
moved this download to /opt 
did cd to /opt then 
# tar xvzf package.tar.gz
# cd package
# ./configure
# make
# make instal
Installed perfect
connected kabeltv and sound cabel
and after that restarted the pc and configured tvtime
and got all my channels working + sound :KS
