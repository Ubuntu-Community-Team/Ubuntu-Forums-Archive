### Post by mud205 on 2006-01-01
gnome-sound-recorder used to crash alot for me here is my fix:
system>preferences>multimedia systems selector change input and output to OSS.
Bam, now i can record sound through a mic.:D 
if it doesnt work for you check the mixer settings and make sure capture and mic capture are unmuted and turned up.click on file>run mixer in gnome-sound-recorder.
