### Post by dpar on 2007-04-22
I wasn't getting any sound playback from audio cds. In sound preferences everything worked except sound capture, which was ALSA. I went to Synaptic and did a search for ALSA and installed alsa-firmware-loaders. Playback worked after that.

Hope this helps somebody.
