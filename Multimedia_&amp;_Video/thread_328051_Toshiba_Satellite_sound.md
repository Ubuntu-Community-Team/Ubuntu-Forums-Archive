I have a toshiba satellite P100-407 laptop with Nvidia 7600 and Intel HD audio. After installing Edgy I managed to get everything working except the sound. I looked on google and found you could get the sound to work by booting with acpi=off in menu.lst (obviously a laptop without acpi isnt great but better than no sound). When I rebooted it i found X would not start, however i managed to get it working again by doing sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg. After reinstalling the drivers for Nvidia the same thing happened again. So it seems i have a choice of no sound or no 3D acceleration ](*,) . Just wondering if anyone else has had this problem?
