### Post by kah00na on 2008-05-17
I've also figured out that the *.MOD files created by some Panasonic flash camcorders are actually ASF files.  If you change the .MOD to .ASF, then they may play.  With the .MOD file extention I was getting about 1 frame per 3 seconds.  After renaming it to .ASF, it was playing like normal.
