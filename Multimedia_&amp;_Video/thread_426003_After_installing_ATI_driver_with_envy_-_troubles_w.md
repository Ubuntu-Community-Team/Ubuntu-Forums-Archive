### Post by cap_gemo on 2007-04-28
Hi everyone!

  Using Ubuntu Dapper and Edgy I had no problem with installing the official ati driver. But when I installed Feisty and tried to follow this guide [http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Feisty_Installation_Guide#Method_2:_Install_the_8.36.5_Driver_Manually](http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Ubuntu_Feisty_Installation_Guide#Method_2:_Install_the_8.36.5_Driver_Manually) , I was not successful. Then I tried envy ([http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html](http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html)). Without any problems it installed the official driver and it worked. But...

  After I rebooted my laptop I got a problem with sound. At some point the sound would "freeze" and play the same tone every time non-stop. I made a backup of the xorg.conf file and restored the original xorg.conf with the stanard Ubuntu ati driver. the sound worked normally again.
