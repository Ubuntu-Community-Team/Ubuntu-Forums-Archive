One thing I noticed with my set-up, which also has the sound on the motherboard, is that the scratchy sound goes away if you turn down the pre-amp somewhere.  I say "somewhere" because I just went and tried to find where I did that while watching a DVD last night, and now I can't find it!  :confused: 

However, if you look around you may discover that there is a setting somewhere in the audio adjustments of either your movie player or the Alsa mixer which is cranked up too high and causing something in the sound system to be over-driven into breaking up.  I was experimenting with getting good audio and video last night, and found this.. turning down one volume control, and then compensating for it by bringing the master up higher, took out that nasty crackling and popping stuff.  Wish I could find it again, but this much may give you a start on things to look for.
