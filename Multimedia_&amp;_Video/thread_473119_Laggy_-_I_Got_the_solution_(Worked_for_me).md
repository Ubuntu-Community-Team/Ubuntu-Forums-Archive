Hey guys!  Your probably reading this because on your Windows OS or your other OS your graphics card is nasty and on Ubuntu, wtf?  It sucks!?  Well here's what I did and I worked for me!

You need to enable the Restricted Drivers!

Just go to 'System -> Preferences -> Desktop Effects'

This prompts you about the restricted drivers and actually speeds up your graphics card's ability to perform it's best.

I tried looking for the solution, I finally found it and wanted to share it with the community!
