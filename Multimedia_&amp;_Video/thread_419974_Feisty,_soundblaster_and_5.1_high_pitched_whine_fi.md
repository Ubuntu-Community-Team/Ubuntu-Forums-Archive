### Post by Polluted on 2007-04-23
I just upgraded to Feisty Fawn and had a screaming high pitched whine coming through my speakers..  After some coffee and swearing I figured it out.

I'm using a Sound Blaster Audigy Gamer and Cambridge Sound Works 5.1 analog speakers.

The centre channel/subwoofer output is also the digital output.  The modes are software switched (Linux and Windows).  If you have the centre channel output switched to digital mode and you need an analog output, you hear a terrible, continuous sound.

Open up "volume controls", click the "switches" tab and uncheck the "digital/analog output jack" box.  (For some reason, Feisty sets it to digital by default)

Hopefully this simple fix is the one you need.
