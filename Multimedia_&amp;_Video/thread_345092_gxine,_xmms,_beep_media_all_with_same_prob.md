### Post by Belyel on 2007-03-05
Hmm, I'm pretty sure that it is because of your internet connection.  Even with cable internet (6MB), if I try to stream radio while my roomates (who share the connection) are downloading or using a lot of bandwidth, I get frequent buffering.  Basically what your computer is doing is downloading a chunk of music and then playing it, but in your case, the downloading is slower than real time, so it will not be continuous.  I suggest downloading a whole broadcast then listening to it later.
