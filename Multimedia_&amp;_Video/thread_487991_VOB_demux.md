### Post by thilo61 on 2007-06-30
Did you try ProjectX?
[http://sourceforge.net/projects/project-x]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/project-x")

Great tool for demuxing all kinds of video streams and wonderful for converting DVB-S TS files to MPGs. You'll also need the JDK from sun.com to compile the sources or you can download a precompiled binary (.JAR) from [http://www.oozoon.de/main_en.html]("http://www.oozoon.de/main_en.html"). Newest version is ProjectX 0.90.04.00 from 2006-03-30.

Good luck!
