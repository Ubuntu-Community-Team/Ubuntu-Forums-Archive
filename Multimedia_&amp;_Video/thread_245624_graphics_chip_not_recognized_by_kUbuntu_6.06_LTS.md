### Post by tseliot on 2006-08-28
I don't know if it can work with your card but I think it's worth trying:
```
sudo nano -w /etc/X11/xorg.conf
```

Get to the Section Device and change the driver from "vesa" to "sis"

Then log out and press CTRL+ALT+Backspace
