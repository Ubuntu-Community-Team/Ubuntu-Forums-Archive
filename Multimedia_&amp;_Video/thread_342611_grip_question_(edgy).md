### Post by david_2001 on 2007-01-20
Go to Config>Encode>Encoder and check the "Encode file format" string.  This defines both the filename format and the storage path.  As an example, mine is > ~/Music/%A/%d/%t %n.%x (the various % switches are listed in Grip's help).
