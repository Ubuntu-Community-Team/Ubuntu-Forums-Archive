### Post by bpny on 2007-11-25
There are two options I know of for sound with .3gp in Linux. One is to install Realplayer for linux  and the other is to build mplayer from source with amrnb and amrwb support enabled. I used Realplayer but sometimes sound would stop for me while the video continued playing. Mplayer has worked very well so far.
