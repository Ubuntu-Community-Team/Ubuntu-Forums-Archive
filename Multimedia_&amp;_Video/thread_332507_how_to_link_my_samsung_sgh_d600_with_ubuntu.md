### Post by trainwreck on 2007-01-30
Hey. Before you connect the cellphone to your computer with the USB-wire you have to change some settings on your phone. Menu --> Settings --> Phone settings --> USB-settings --> and change to "memory card". You have to do this each time you're going to connect your phone. All you gain by this tough, is being able to copy files to/from your phone (mp3, video, photos etc.). 
These settings may wary depending on which language you use.
