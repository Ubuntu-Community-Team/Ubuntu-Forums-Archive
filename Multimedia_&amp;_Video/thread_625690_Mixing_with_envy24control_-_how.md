### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-11-28
I use the envy24control with an M-audo Delta 66 and it seems to work fine.  There is a digital mixer control that you must unmute and then select it as the output in the patch bay.

It's possible that the audiophile card (despite its name) has limited capability.  Since the card can process sound at 24-bits and 96 kHz, M-audio gave it the audiophile name.  However it can only handle unbalanced inputs and outputs--this makes it difficult to interface with other professional equipment.  It's possible that other compromises were made in the design of the card--including internal mixing--I don't know.  Others with the card will have to weigh in.
