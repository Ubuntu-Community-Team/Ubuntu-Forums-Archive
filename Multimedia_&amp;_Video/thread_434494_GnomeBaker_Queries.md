### Post by hyperair on 2007-05-06
1. Just make a data cd and dump your mp3s in.
2. Uh.. I don't know how.. is it even possible?
3. On The Fly means: burn straight to the disk without creating a disk image (ISO) first
Dummy Write: Write simulation. Doesn't really burn to the disk, but it causes your drive to spin and all. It's useful for seeing if the burn process will complete successfully or not.
Burn Free: Buffer under-run protection. Allows the burning of a CD to be completed successfully even when the device burns faster than the computer cansend the data to the device.
