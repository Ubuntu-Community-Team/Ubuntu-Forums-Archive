### Post by sizzam on 2005-10-25
I would recommend the [MediaPlayerConnectivity]("https://addons.mozilla.org/extensions/moreinfo.php?id=446") plugin for firefox.  It turns all embedded videos into an icon that you can click on to launch it into the video player of your choice.
