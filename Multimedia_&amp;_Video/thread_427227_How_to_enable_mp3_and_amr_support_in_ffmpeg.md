### Post by 4ebees on 2007-04-29
Hi all,

I had serious trouble getting this to work until I came across this blog:

[http://blogger.rukker.org/2006/07/12/enable-mp3-and-amr-support-in-ffmpeg-ubuntudebian/#comment-6](http://blogger.rukker.org/2006/07/12/enable-mp3-and-amr-support-in-ffmpeg-ubuntudebian/#comment-6)

by Danny "The Animal".

Instructions are available for Dapper and for Edgy.

I thoroughly recommend this site for ease of use and simplicity of instruction.

It worked first time for me.

If you're not aware of the use of AMR support, it allows you (amongst other things) to convert other video formats to 3gp, the video format used in many mobile (also called 'cell') phones. It also assist with converting video to the flv 'flash' format.

This is great when using Kino.

I also recommend:

Multimedia Converter  

[http://kde-apps.org/content/show.php/Multimedia+Converter?content=53457](http://kde-apps.org/content/show.php/Multimedia+Converter?content=53457)

by Almusaihij

A brilliant one-stop shop for video and audio conversion when AMR support in ffmpeg is set up.
