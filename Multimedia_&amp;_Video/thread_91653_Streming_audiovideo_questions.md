I did this successfully just last night.  Look at the 5.10 starter guide available from the lifebuoy help icon on the toolbar.  There is a good guide under Applications > Music and Videos. To run the M$ stuff you need to download w32codecs, which are not available on the Ubuntu site - but the site gives very clear directions about what to do if you are in a country for which downloading these codecs is legal.:p
