### Post by rsambuca on 2007-11-12
If you go to the "[download management]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/browse/type:1/cat:5")" of the Firefox extensions page, there are a ton of them that allow you to save flash videos.  Sometimes you can also find the video in your firefox cache, as well.
