### Post by kwacka on 2008-04-20
Right-click on speaker icon in panel. Click on 'Open Volume Control' --> Edit --> Preferences --> Scroll down to External Amplifier and tick box. 
Click 'close', and you are back to Volume Control.
Click on 'switches'. Make sure the external amplifier box is clear.

Do you have sound?
