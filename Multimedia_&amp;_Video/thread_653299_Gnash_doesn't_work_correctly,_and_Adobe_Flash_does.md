### Post by carandraug on 2008-01-04
> **UbunNooB said:**
> I've just installed the two plugins to view videos at youtube and it pretty much causes my browser to crash. After which I have to reboot my pc. How do I uninstall the plugins?
1 - Go to System > Administration > Synaptic package manager
2 - In the left field select "installed" and in the top field click in "Search"
3 - Type the name of the plugin
4 - Look for it in the list that appears, click on it and select "Mark for removal"
5 - In the field select "Apply" and you're done
