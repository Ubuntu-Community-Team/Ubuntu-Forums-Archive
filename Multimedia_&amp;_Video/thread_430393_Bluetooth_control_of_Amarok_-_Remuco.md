### Post by jackmc on 2007-05-02
I just thought that this was really cool - [remuco]("http://remuco.sourceforge.net/")

You install a server (on PC) and a client (on j2me mobile phone), and then you have full control, with feedback!

By feedback I mean that track name/artist/album is displayed on the phone (album art would be really cool)

[Screenshots]("http://remuco.sourceforge.net/index.php/Screenshots")

I'm using "canvas" on mine, so its all themed. Worked "out of the box" on my K800i

Just thought I'd let you all know :)
