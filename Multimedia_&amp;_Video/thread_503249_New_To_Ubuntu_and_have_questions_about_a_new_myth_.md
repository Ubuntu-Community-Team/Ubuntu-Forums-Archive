### Post by tgm4883 on 2007-07-17
You need 1 tuner for every show that you want to record at the same time (and presumly, also a directtv box).  If you want to watch a show while it's recording then you only need 1 tuner, if you want to watch a tv show while it's recording something else, you need 2 tuners.

As for your hardware, was their a question there?  All your hardware should work fine.
