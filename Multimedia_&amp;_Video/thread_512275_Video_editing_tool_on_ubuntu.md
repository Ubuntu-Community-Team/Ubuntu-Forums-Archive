### Post by chewearn on 2007-07-29
Avidemux do show whether its a keyframe or not.  If you look at the bottom of the window, there is a line of text (see attached example).

The last part of this line indicate whether the current frame of "I", "P", "B", etc.  Furthermore, you can use the << and >> buttons to jump between keyframes.
