### Post by challahc on 2006-12-26
I just received a clix as a christmas gift, and was frustrated at first because from what i had read i needed to recompile amarok and compile my own libmtp.  After screwing with this for a couple of days i decided to try the ubuntu repos.  I reinstalled amarok and installed libmtp from the repo.  I works more now than it did before.  It seems to choke on certain song titles.  In particular ones with ?s.  There are some others that cause a crash too.  I'm still trying to compile MTPdude to upload other files, amarok only seems to deal with the music files. 

So, the clix is somewhat supported by amarok and I really like the interface on the player and the ogg support.  I wouldn't discourage anyone from buying one, but it was alittle work for me to get it working.  It's really hard to say what actually got it working.  But start by trying the repository for libmtp and amarok that was the last thing i did before it started transferring music.

### Post by rarmstr on 2008-01-13
I just wanted to let everyone know that I've got the iRiver Clix2 8GB working great with Ubuntu. I did beat my head against the wall for a while, but have everything sorted out now.

I wasn't aware of this, but to play and rip DVD's on Ubuntu you must install extra packages: [http://www.medibuntu.org/](http://www.medibuntu.org/)

Then I installed the latest libraries for MTP support which makes Rhythmbox problems go away. I just followed the MTP compile and install instructions from here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=651894](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=651894)
NOTE: without doing this, the MTP support in Rhythmbox is a bit flakey.

Then, add the MTP Rhythmbox plugin.

I also found iriverter to be useful for ripping DVDs for iriver.
