Now that I have vented, can someone recommend a nice PVR type card for my Linux Ubuntu Feisty 7.04 machine that is under $200? It will be a P4 3GHz Pentium 4 machine (PCI/AGP) after Christmas. That machine will be a hand-me-down when I build my dual core.
