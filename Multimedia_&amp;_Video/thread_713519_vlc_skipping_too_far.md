### Post by mc4man on 2008-03-02
If you click on the seek bar you'll get a 16min advance (or retreat) per click. I'm not sure how or if you can alter that. If you grab the bar you can seek fairly accurately. The scroll wheel will advance or retreat 3 sec. per click to fine tune. There are also hotkeys for seeking that can be adjusted to suit.
Ck. out the videolan site for more info
