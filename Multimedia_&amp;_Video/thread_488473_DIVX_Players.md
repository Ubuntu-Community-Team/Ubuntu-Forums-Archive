### Post by csisgro on 2007-06-30
Yes a divx certified DVD player will play divx 3,4,5 and 6 as well as xvid videos. xvid is divx backwards. it is the open source fork of divx from a while back. I put many videos on a dvd-rw and can play them in my dvd player. you get a directory listing and you can pick which one to watch or it can play through all of them. They are now available for under 50 bucks US.

### Post by Gremlinzzz on 2007-06-30
Why don't you watch your divx movies on your computer smplayer plays divx movies you downloaded.
[http://smplayer.sourceforge.net/linux/index_en.php](http://smplayer.sourceforge.net/linux/index_en.php)
mplayer plugin plays them online but you cant use totem plugin with mplayer plugin.heres the commands you need to watch divxs movies online.
cd /usr/lib/firefox/plugins/
sudo ln -s /usr/lib/mozilla/plugins/mplayerplug-in-dvx.xpt
sudo ln -s /usr/lib/mozilla/plugins/mplayerplug-in-dvx.so
