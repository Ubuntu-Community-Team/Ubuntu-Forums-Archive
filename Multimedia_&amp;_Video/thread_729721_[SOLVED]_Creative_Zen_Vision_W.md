### Post by granny6989 on 2008-03-30
Gnomad2 shows support for the Vision and Vision M. There are some other posts where people are using the W with some success, but not able to create folders, etc to organize files. Sounds like it's kind of limited suport at this time. I am currently running a VisionM with Gnomad2 and Rhythymbox, which works well, although I can't use playback directly from the player, but not such a big deal...

Looks like you could maybe use Kio or Neutrino for very basic tasks, but probably still wouldn't work for the W model, and I doubt you'd get very far importing and managing video with them.

So, audio connection should work for you, but I wouldn't count on video support just yet.......
AcidRip and Thoggen will get DVD video onto your computer at least.
