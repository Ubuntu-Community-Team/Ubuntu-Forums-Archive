### Post by ubuhick on 2007-07-15
After more tinkering, I found out it has nothing to do with the drivers, Configuration, or Ubuntu.  It's just a playback bug in Democracy Player.  Easy way around it if you are using Democracy player is this.  Instead of clicking the play button over the video thumbnail just hit show file.  You'll get a list of the videos Democracy has downloaded and then you can open them up in a different player.  I use Totem Movie player and it works great.
