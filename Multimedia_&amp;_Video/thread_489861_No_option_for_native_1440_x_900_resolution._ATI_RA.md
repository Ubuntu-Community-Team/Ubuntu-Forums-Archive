### Post by zigarth on 2007-07-02
Actually, the "#" lines are just descriptions of the modeline beneath them.
I was able to resolve the problem through the following command in the terminal:

```
sudo dpkg-reconfigure -phigh xserver-xorg
```

I then selected ATI (I hear vesta works well with ATI chipsets, as well) as my driver and selected the appropriate resolutions using the space bar (you can tell you've selected it because an asterisk [*] appears in front of the resolution)... I hope that is helpful for anyone else having a similar problem.

I find it rather weird though, because everything that I did through xorg.conf didn't seem to change any of my options in the drop down menu of System --> Preferences --> Screen Resolution. Even when I started removing all the different resolutions, it didn't seem to make any difference in the options that were appearing in the menu. I still can't wrap my head around it, can anyone offer an explanation?
