Anyhow, it works for me with the mplayer plugin. Instructions [here]("http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_dapper#How_to_install_Multimedia_Player_.28Mplayer.29_with_plug-in_for_Mozilla_Firefox"). ```
