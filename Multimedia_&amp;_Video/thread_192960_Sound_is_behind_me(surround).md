### Post by rmjokers on 2006-06-09
First, make sure you have the correct speakers plugged into the correct audio ports on your system (I have made this mistake before).  Second, open volume control and make sure the front volume is actually set to a reasonable level and not muted.  You should see controls for each channel on your soundcard from this interface.  Not sure what else could be causing the problem.
