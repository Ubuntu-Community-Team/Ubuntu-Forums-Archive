### Post by deadgobby on 2007-08-14
If you have realplayer as the media player for firefox, it is not realplayer issue. You may want to check your ASLA mixer sound levels. It is distorted because of the sound levels of the ALSA mixer settings are set too high for your head phones or speakers. There is several ways to edit this volume settings. One is to double click the speaker icon. Then play with it
