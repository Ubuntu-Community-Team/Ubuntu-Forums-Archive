### Post by madcow72 on 2007-09-11
If the video card is working, including 3D Rendering, and Xinerama is correctly set up, then Beryl (you should switch to Compiz-Fusion) should be working by default.  If you're having trouble with Desktop effects, try bumping down to a single monitor, make sure that Beryl (Compiz) works there, and then try adding the 2nd monitor back.  

I have an NVidia 256MB card in my system and have never had trouble with dual-head Beryl or Compiz.  They both work automatically after I have my video card driver installed.
