### Post by Mottq on 2007-06-18
I am now running Feisty on my desktop and laptop and was REALLY bummed out when I realized that when I upgraded to FeistyI could not burn CD's. I tried all the applications and I finally discovered one that worked. If you Go to the Add/Delete programs in the "Applications" button, there is a CD/DVD burning application (XFCE)in the "applications," not the "sound/video" heading. It worked perfectly for me. I hope this helps anyone with the same problem.
