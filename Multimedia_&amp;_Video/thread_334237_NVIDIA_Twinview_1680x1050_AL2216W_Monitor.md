### Post by JayRoe on 2007-01-08
Try this:

```
Section "Monitor"
    # HorizSync source: edid, VertRefresh source: edid
    Identifier     "Generic Monitor"
    ModelName      "Acer AL2216W"
    HorizSync       30.0 - 82.0
    VertRefresh     56.0 - 76.0
    Option         "DPMS"
EndSection
```
