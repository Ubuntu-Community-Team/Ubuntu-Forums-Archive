### Post by Chymera on 2007-08-22
Alsa claims to support most 5.1 gfx cards, but the reality is IT DOESN'T.
However, you may be the lucky owner of one of those few sound cards which alsa supports. Just go to the speaker icon in the upper panel, click it twice go to preferences and see if you can add front / rear channels, if you dont find any, youre screwed :)
