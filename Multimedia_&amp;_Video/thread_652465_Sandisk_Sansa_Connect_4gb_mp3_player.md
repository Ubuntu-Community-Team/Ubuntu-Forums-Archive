### Post by Sami_Sdata on 2008-05-17
With most Sansa players you usually just need to swith it to MSC mode.  After that it your system will see it as a flash drive.  You can just drag and drop the files.  I'm using a Sansa Clip under Kubuntu and it works great.  As of the new firmware upgrade it now support Ogg files as well as MP3s.  They have fairly active forums at the URL below.

[http://forums.sandisk.com](http://forums.sandisk.com)
