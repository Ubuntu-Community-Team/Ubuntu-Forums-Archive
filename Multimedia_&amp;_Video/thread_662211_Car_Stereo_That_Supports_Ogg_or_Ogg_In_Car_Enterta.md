My current ogg vorbis capable car stereo is the insignia ns-c5111. My only complaint about it is that it takes a long time to scan your usb flash drive, which it needs to do everytime you start your car. For a 4GB stick, it takes 15 seconds, and for a 60GB HDD, it takes about a minute. It is a discontined model so it may take some searching to find.
