### Post by wizard10000 on 2008-04-12
SO got me a PS3 yesterday for being a good boy.

Soon as I got it connected to my wireless network I see a "MythTV media server" icon on the Playstation's main menu and sure enough, I can watch recorded content from the myth backend on the PS3.

Got my music library showing up on the PS3 but haven't had quite as much luck with my photo archive - I'll get it, though.  The only real downer so far has been if your mp3 tags aren't up to scratch it'll make browsing your music with a Playstation more difficult than it needs to be.

Amazing.
