I setup xine to use passthrough audio to get true 5.1 sound.  After watching a movie, sound from anything else doesn't work.  I tried turning on and off switches (digital output jack) but it doesn't do anything.

The way I fixed it in gentoo was to disable alsa from saving any settings, then when you restart alsa it starts working fine again. 

Can anyone tell me how to do this, or if someone else has the same problem let me know how to fix it?
