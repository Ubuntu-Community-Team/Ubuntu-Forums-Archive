### Post by passonno on 2007-02-11
Hey!!!

As I have just recently decided to abandon Winxp, I have noticed that what I miss the most is winamp.

Now, I love XMMS just fine, though I cannot stand the default skin so, I am sharing this info with others in the same boat.

Go here:

[http://forums.winamp.com/showthread.php?threadid=189504](http://forums.winamp.com/showthread.php?threadid=189504) to download the Winamp base skin.

When it is downloaded, simply do this:

  cp downloads/winamp5_classic_base_skin.zip .xmms/Skins/ 
(of course mv works as well)

Enjoy
