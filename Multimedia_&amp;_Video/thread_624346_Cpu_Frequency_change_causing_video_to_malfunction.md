### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2007-11-27
If you want to disable frequency throttling, you should be able to do that in your BIOS. There should be an option for Cool'n'Quiet or Powernow. Disable that.

I think there's also a better way to do it in software, by disabling cpufreq or powernow-k8. Google around for that if you don't have the option in your BIOS.
