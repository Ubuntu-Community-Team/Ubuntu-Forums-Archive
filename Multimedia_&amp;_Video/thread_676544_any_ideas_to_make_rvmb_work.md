### Post by disturbed1 on 2008-01-24
RVMB is a Real Networks format. Both RealPlayer and HelixPlayer are available for Linux. Though, mplayer will also play back these formats with some success.

W32Codecs works best with mplayer, and somewhat with GStreamer apps (Totem) as long as you have the pitfdll GStreamer plugin installed.
