If you want a replacement for Rhythmbox there are many. :)

Try Amarok. I use it even on GNOME. It slaughters WMP into the dust. A bit violent of a comparison but that will about do it. I also hear good things about banshee, and VLC is the best simple media player of all time.

sudo apt-get install amarok
type your password (it will not show up but invisibly type) push enter and your good to go.
