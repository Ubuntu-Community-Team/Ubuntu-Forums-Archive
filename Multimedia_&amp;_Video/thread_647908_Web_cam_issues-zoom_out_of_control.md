### Post by ericcmi on 2007-12-27
The problem is not with zoom, its with the size of the image the the flash site is requesting from the web-cam. If you have a 640x480 webcam, but the site only supports half that, then you only get that amount of pixels from the CENTER of the full 640x480 image, giving the appearence that it is zoomed.
