### Post by jcwmoore on 2008-01-12
open volume control, right click on the speaker on the top right of the screen, and go to Edit>Prefrences.  Make sure all the options are check and then click "OK".  now in the volume control make sure that there is nothing set to Mute.  I had this problem when installing Ubuntu version 7.04 and again when upgrading to the current version.
