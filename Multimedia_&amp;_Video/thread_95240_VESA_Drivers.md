### Post by mo_x on 2005-11-26
You probably need to adjust the HorizSync and VertRefresh settings in your /etc/X11/xorg.conf file to match your monitor. See my example.
```
Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"monitor0"
	VendorName	"Belinea"
	ModelName	"103010"
	Option		"DPMS"
	HorizSync	30-70
	VertRefresh	50-120
	DisplaySize	320 240
EndSection
```

I googled for the specs of the 170S:
