### Post by majoridiot on 2007-02-17
first thing you need to do is find out which of the two dvi ports is primary and set it up with the
DFP on the primary and the vga on secondary.  you likely just need to switch them around.

then boot up.  if X crashes (likely) login to the terminal prompt that comes up and do:

```
sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
```

and restart.

if it makes it as far as the logon without crashing the first time, hit CTRL-ALT-F1 and then log
in and do the reconfigure and restart anyway.  you don't want to risk damaging your DFP.

once you've restarted, (only one monitor will be active, don't panic!) do:

```
sudo nvidia-settings
```

and use that to set up your twinview, then save.  you should be good to go.

once you get that far, post your new xorg.conf and we'll help you sort out the NULL monitor.
nvidia-settings generates some strange metamodes... it took me a couple tries to sort out
mine.
