### Post by bernardfrancois on 2006-01-30
If you configure xorg.conf the right way it's possible to use two video cards I think. You can have different display modes between which you can switch using CTRL+ALT+'+' and CTRL+ALT+'-'.

I can't help you any further, but maybe you could try searching the [wiki](wiki.ubuntu.com), which is another great source of solutions to ubuntu problems.

Or you could read **man 5 xorg.conf**.
