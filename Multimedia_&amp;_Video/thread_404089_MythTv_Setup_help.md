### Post by Titus A Duxass on 2007-04-08
Start off by reading everything you can find about MythTV.

Then, make a combined backend/frontend, keep doing that until you have a 100% problem free installation. Once you know how to install a combined setup then you can start with your standalone backend and frontends.

Good luck, at first it's not easy.
