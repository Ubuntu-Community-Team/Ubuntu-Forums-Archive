### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-04-12
Hi. Maybe **gstreamer0.10-plugins-ugly-multiverse** and **gstreamer0.10-plugins-ugly** can help. Look for them in Synaptic (System-->Administration-->Synaptic Package Manager). You'll need Universe and Multiverse repositories enabled for those: In Synaptic, click on "Settings-->Repositories" and on the first tab check all the cases; close that window, and press the **Reload** button on the top left corner of Synaptic. After it finishies reloading the package information they'll be available, so search for them. If the plugins don't help, there's also **beep-media-player-wma** and **xmms-wma**; those are plugins for Beep Media Player and XMMS; they're similar to Winamp.
