### Post by pkunal on 2006-06-11
OK I started with Ubuntu 3 days back and was initially very frustrated as I could not get anything to work.

This is what I have done successfully
Firefox plugins
Installed RealPlayer for my Online News, Videos, etc
Installed Totem-Xine for DVD
Installed Flash Plugin
Played MP3 & CD Audio with Rhythm Box.

I am not claiming they are best or anything, all I can say is it all worked. Great for newbies.

For RealPlayer refer to instructions at:
[http://yolinux.com/TUTORIALS/LinuxTutorialMozillaConfiguration.html](http://yolinux.com/TUTORIALS/LinuxTutorialMozillaConfiguration.html)

For Totem-Xine, Flash & Multimedia Codecs (w32codecs), Firefox Plugins refer to:
[http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_dapper](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_dapper)

To test RealPlayer goto:
[http://guide.real.com](http://guide.real.com)
When it asks you to open with use: /opt/RealPlayer/realplay (or your appropriate path of realplay)

I have bugged people with lot of questions over the last few days and did lot of searching on the web. These instructions turned out to be the best.
