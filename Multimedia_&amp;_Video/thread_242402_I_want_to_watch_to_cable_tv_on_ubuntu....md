### Post by crispy_420 on 2006-08-25
Just watch or record?

Watch =

a bt878 based card is easy setup and tvtime works well to just view tv. easy setup and cheap. it looks like you live in the US so a card can be had for easily less than $50 (US). there are other chipsets but this one has worked well for me so far

record =

hauppauge based card is the only way to go. MythTV to record and watch. This is a massive pain to setup and get working, full of errors and problems. Also freevo is an option but not as mature.

Just my 2 cents maybe we can get a second opinion.
