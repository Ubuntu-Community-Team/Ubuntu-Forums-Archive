### Post by augegr on 2007-05-03
You must either change the encoding of the .srt to UTF-8 or specify the encoding in the program you use to watch the movie. For example, if VLC, go to Settings and find the subtitles encoding setting. Change that to cp1253 or iso-8859-7.
