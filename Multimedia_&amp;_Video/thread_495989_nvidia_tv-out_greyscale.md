### Post by Andcor on 2007-07-09
You should Change your
    Option         "ConnectedMonitor" "Monitor[1]"
To something like
    Option         "ConnectedMonitor" "TV"
Or
    Option         "ConnectedMonitor" "CRT"

That does it for me

By the way, if you are using both CRT and TV-out you should use 
    Option         "ConnectedMonitor" "TV,CRT"
In both device-sections
