### Post by Grard on 2007-05-08
Not easily.

Banshee and mt-daapd/firefly can share files like iTunes does but the AppleTV needs to be paired with a key first and that isn't supported by the Linux programs (yet).

I enabled SSH on my AppleTV and installed ATVfiles, now I can copy media over from Ubuntu to AppleTV and watch/listen to it. Check [http://awkwardtv.org](http://awkwardtv.org) if you want to try it that way.
