Hi,

well first of all I must say I have not tried the exact thing you try (To be true, I'm one step behind you, just trying to get my tv-out working ;) )

I do have the same graphics chipset in my amilo Si1520 and did encounter the same problems as you have, but with Winodws XP. From my point of view, it is not a software problem at all. It took me over a week just to figure out what it takes to get the thingy working.

I browsed over to Samsung and found out that they advice their customers to buy an adapter from s-video to cinch for >50€...
Browsing over to ebay and searching with something like "s-video color adapter" turned out to be a 5€ solution to the problem.

(Looking further into the problem, it seems to be a capacitor between 2 of the pins in the cable that is missing in most cables to get a color picture on your PAL TV if your TV is not s-video compatible)

I hope this will fix the problem rather quickly ;)
