### Post by julian67 on 2008-03-08
unfortunately gutsy seems to be very prone to stuttering and freezing. Numerous people have been experiencing it with all different configurations, a quick search of these forums reveals a lot of tales of woe. It never happened for me with Feisty so I was happy with my hardware but very frustrated with the most unstable OS I used since Win 98.   Eventually I took a day out of my life and  installed a different Debian based OS (not Ubuntu based) and the freezing stopped, my pc now runs without issues, no more system freezes, no more losing the keyboard and/or mouse, no more having to do hard resets because even Alt+Sys Req + reisub failed. My PC just hums along nicely even under heavy load and I feel I'm using GNU/Linux again instead of being in the waking nightmare of crash/reboot/crash/reboot loop that I thought I'd left behind when I quit using Windows 98.  Let's hope Hardy is as stable as Feisty was. It just has to be an improvement on Gutsy's lack of stability.
