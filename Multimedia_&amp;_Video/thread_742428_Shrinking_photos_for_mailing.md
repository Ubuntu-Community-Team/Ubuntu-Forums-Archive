### Post by marpstar on 2008-04-01
You can do this using gThumb, which should be installed by default.  

Open your main menu and go to Graphics>gThumb Image Viewer

Navigate to the folder containing your images, highlight the ones you want.  Then you can go to Tools>Scale Images and set your resolution and it will resize them all.

Good luck.
