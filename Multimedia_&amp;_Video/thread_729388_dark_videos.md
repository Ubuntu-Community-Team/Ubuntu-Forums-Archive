### Post by garolou on 2008-04-26
Had the same problem; I was wondering why all the movies I was watching were set at nightime. Totem movie player was the culprit.

from this thread : [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=610471&highlight=video+too+dark]("http://http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=610471&highlight=video+too+dark")

> If you open the Totem Movie Player and open Edit/Preferences, in the Display tab you can reset the colour balance to defaults. Some process must have set mine quite dark, and resetting them restored everything to normal.
