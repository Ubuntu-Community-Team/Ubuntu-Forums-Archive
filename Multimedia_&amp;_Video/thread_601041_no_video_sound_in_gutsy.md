### Post by jubilee33 on 2007-11-03
I had a very similar problem.  I used the following solution found in this forum: go to System -> Preferences -> Sound and uncheck ¨enable software sound mixing (ESD)¨.  This gave me all the video sound but disabled the mouse-over audio preview of MP3, which I can live without at this point.  You may want to try it out.
