### Post by diepruis on 2007-07-19
Over the past few days, I've noticed a number of posts complaining about the performance of the new 8 series GeForce cards under Ubuntu. I myself recently installed an 8600GT, only to be disappointed by its performance under Linux.

According to [this news post](http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=781&num=4) there is a problem with the Linux drivers for these cards and all we can do is wait. I just thought I'd bring this problem to the community's attention in order to assert that the problem is known and doesn't lie with Ubuntu.
