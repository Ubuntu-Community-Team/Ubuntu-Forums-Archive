### Post by ibchristie on 2007-07-16
I've got an Audiophile 2496 in an AMD Sempron box with Ubuntu Studio (32 bit OS not 64). My son is the music wiz and he has just started using it. U Studio recognized the card without me needing to supply explicit drivers. No big problems so far but we haven't tried many of its functions yet. I had to disable the on-board sound in the BIOS, that was the only non-obvious thing. He mostly uses the system to compose and arrange using a notation editor directly rather than playing on a MIDI keyboard and then loading the result into the editor. That means he isn't using the sound card as much as someone who composes on the keyboard.

I must say I like the printed sheet music that Lilypond produces. It lives up to the hype.

Cheers
