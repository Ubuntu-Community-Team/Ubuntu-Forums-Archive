### Post by gerowen on 2006-10-28
I use Audacity to convert between media types.  To get all of the multimedia codecs so I could play my music files, I used [Automatix.](http://www.getautomatix.com/)  Remember that if you use Audacity, you have to have lame installed, and when it asks to find the liblamemp3.so file, it's in /usr/lib.
