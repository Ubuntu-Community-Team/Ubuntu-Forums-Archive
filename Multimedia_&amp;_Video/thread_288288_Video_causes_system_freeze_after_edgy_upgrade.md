### Post by gstool on 2006-10-29
Ok,

I found my problem was that a non-supported ati driver called xserver-xorg-driver-ati was installed and being used.  I used synaptic to install one that had a "supported" icon next to it called xserver-xorg-video-ati.  That removed the unsupported one.  My Radeon 7500 card now functions as it used to.
