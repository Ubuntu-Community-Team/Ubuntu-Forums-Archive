### Post by DrBob on 2008-04-17
If you're running Gutsy and want to try it out, you'll have to compile from SVN (or a 2.22 tarball). However, if you're running Hardy (or don't mind waiting a week until the release), the version of Totem in the Hardy repositories has the YouTube plugin in it, and once Totem's installed, no further work is required to get it working other than enabling the plugin in the Totem plugins dialogue.
