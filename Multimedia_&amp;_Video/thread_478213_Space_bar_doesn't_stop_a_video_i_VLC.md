### Post by chazchaz101 on 2008-02-16
I was unable to pause non-streaming videos (avi files etc.) with the space bar, and manually specifying the WXwidgets skin fixed it for me.

As for streaming media, most streams, especially those that are streamed live, don't normally support pausing. For that to work, the player would have to be caching the stream to disk a la Tivo, and as far as I know, VLC doesn't do this.
