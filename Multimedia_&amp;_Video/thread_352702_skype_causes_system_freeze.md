### Post by ]Nbx*cmD[ on 2007-03-14
Looking at the bug tracker I found out a little pseudo-solution which has its counterpart.

Since this is an issue related to the incoming chat sound, if you switch off the sound for incoming chats there won't be any freeze anymore. 

Of course, the counterpart is exactly this: you won't have a sound signal when you recieve a chat.
