### Post by aysiu on 2007-08-26
I don't know if this is the answer, but since no one else has answered you in the past eight hours, I think Nautilus "deletes" files from removable drives to the .Trash folder on the removable drive.

In other words, those files weren't deleted. They are still on the iPod, just in the iPod's trash.

Try enabling the delete (that bypasses the trash) command in Nautilus, or delete from the command-line (using *rm*).
