### Post by mrmark24 on 2007-08-17
luckyd,

The iPod "can" play MP3 files, so if you convert your existing files to MP3 format (using Audacity, for example), you'll be able to put them on your iPod.  Or, if you're ripping CDs, rip them to MP3 format, and it will work... Unless your CD drive rips CDs and makes them all crackly and screechy like mine does.

Hope this helps,

mrmark24

P.S. I know MP3 isn't open source, so, sorry, but this is the only way I know of to get songs onto an iPod without changing the iPod itself.
