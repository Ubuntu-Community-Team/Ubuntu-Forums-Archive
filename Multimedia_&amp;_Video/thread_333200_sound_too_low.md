### Post by moma on 2007-01-07
Double click (or right click) the volume-speaker icon on the system tray. It should start "gnome-volume-control" application.  You can also start it from command line:
$ [COLOR="SeaGreen"]gnome-volume-control[/COLOR]

Check, enable and change the Master and PCM values.

Is your speaker-wire ([jack connector...]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jack_plug")) plugged to right hole.
