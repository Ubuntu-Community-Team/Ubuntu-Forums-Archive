Ok, I figured out how to figure what numbers to use on the BusID Section.

Open Device Manager which currently is under System>>Administration>>Device Manager

If your OS recognized your card it will be listed on the left side of the screen, highlight it by clicking on it one time.

Then on the right side of the screen click on the "Advanced" tab.

look at these three lines

linux.sysfs_path
linux.sysfs_path_device
pci.linux.sysfs_path 

In my xorg.conf file the BusID of my card that was working was 1:0:0 and those lines all ended with 01:00.0 and those lines on the card that wasn't working ended with 04.02.0 sooooo... I figured I would make the BusID for that card in my xorg.conf file read

BusID          "PCI:4.2.0"

now it works!!
