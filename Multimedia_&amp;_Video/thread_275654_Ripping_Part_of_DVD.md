### Post by Wight_Rhino on 2006-10-13
Me Again! I did some extensive research and found a solution to my problem, and in true "Open-source" fashion, I decided to post it here so if someone in the future has a similar problem and does a search, hopefully they'll find this "Thread":D 

Chaplin. No really, that's it. It's a Command-line App that will divide a DVD into however many "chapters" that you specify, then you can rip that chapter(s) using Acidrip or DVDRip. I'm not going into a "How-To" here, it comes with a Man page and I did a little experimenting. Good Luck!

Hope this helps someone in the future.:D
