### Post by adlin5000 on 2007-11-05
don't know if you still need help but, I think I may have an answer.
I have a 9250 also and when I look at the xorg log it something to the effect of no device listed for PCI:1:0:1 right after it goes through the normal card settings. It acts like two cards one at PCI:1:0:0 and one at PCI:1:0:1. you need to put in a second "device" section in your xorg.conf file for the "second" card and change the "screen" section for the second monitor to this card.
