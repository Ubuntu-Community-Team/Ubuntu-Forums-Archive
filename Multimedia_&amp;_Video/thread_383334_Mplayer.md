### Post by panch0 on 2007-03-13
Choose a different video output for MPlayer. Try xv (XVideo), gl (OpenGL), gl2, and x11, in that order.

KPlayer is a great KDE frontend for MPlayer, it has the video output setting under Settings - Configure KPlayer - Video.
