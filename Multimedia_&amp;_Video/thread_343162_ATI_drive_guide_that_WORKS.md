### Post by Shatrat on 2007-01-21
Your xorg.conf doesn't seem to have 
```
Section "Extensions"
        Option  "Composite" "Disable"
EndSection
```
Try adding that to the end, save it, and then ctrl alt backspace to restart the x server.

I have the same chipset in my laptop and I managed to get mine working fairly quickly (although unfortunately I have had way more experience with ATI drivers than I would like to have in the past).
