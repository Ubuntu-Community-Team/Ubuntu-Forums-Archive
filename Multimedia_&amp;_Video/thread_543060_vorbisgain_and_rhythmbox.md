### Post by a-musing amazon on 2007-09-12
I don't think there is any option to turn the gain tags on or off in Rhythmbox.

Have you used "Album" (vorbisgain with the -a option) or individual track ("Radio") equalisation?

If the former then Rhythmbox will use this setting by preference. As I understand it Album equalisation preserves the differences in level between tracks within an album (since its assumed that is what the artist intended) and only varies the level between albums - which will be then be significantly less.
