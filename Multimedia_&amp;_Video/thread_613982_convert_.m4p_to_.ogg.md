### Post by exoren22 on 2007-11-15
.m4p is the file extension for apple's protected media files, and it would be therefore illegal to convert them to vorbis because you would have to remove the copy protection. 
That being said, this thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=515467](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=515467) seems to have your answer. You can get the package from [http://gnormalize.sourceforge.net/](http://gnormalize.sourceforge.net/)
