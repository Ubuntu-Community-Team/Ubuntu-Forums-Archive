### Post by reclusivemonkey on 2007-05-04
No problem. If your particular card is supported, you could get away with installing tvtime (its in the repos, and doesn't have any dependencies AFAIK), running the setup and it will "just work". If you are not averse to buying a new card, I believe the Hauppauge PVR range are the ones to go for. The better ones have a dedicated chip for converting the video to MPEG, and so use your CPU very little.
