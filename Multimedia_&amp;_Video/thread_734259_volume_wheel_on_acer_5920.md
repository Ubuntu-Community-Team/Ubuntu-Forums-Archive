### Post by -X- on 2008-03-30
Howdy!

Go to System -> Preferences -> Sound

Under "Default Mixer Tracks" (Devices tab, the first one) select "Master" if you want it to control both the headphone jack and speakers (recommended ;)), or "Surround" if you want it to control only the speakers.

That's what I did to get it working on my 5920. Good hunting!
