### Post by teknorah on 2007-04-08
I have a _[COLOR=Purple][**Gateway M275**]("http://support.gateway.com/s/Mobile/Gateway/M275/3501734nv.shtml") [/COLOR]_w/ and _[COLOR=Purple][**[FONT=arial, helvetica][SIZE=3]Intel® 82852/82855 Graphics Controller Family[/SIZE][/FONT]**]("http://downloadfinder.intel.com/scripts-df-external/Product_Filter.aspx?ProductID=922&lang=eng")[/COLOR]_.  I am able to run the screensavers.  My configuration is the following:
Primary Internal Monitor: Laptop LCD w/ max 1024x768 resolution (on left)
Secondary External Monitor:_[COLOR=Purple][ [SIZE=5][IMG]http://www.sceptre.com/Products/LCD/images/X9-logo-50.jpg[/IMG][/SIZE]**[FONT=Arial][SIZE=5]g-NagaIII                   19" LCD[/SIZE][/FONT]**]("http://www.sceptre.com/Products/LCD/Specifications/spec_x9gNagaIII.htm")[/COLOR]_ w/ max 1280x1024 resolution (on right)

This is a dual-monitor extended desktop version.

Built-in screensavers do not crash gdm/xorg with this configuration.  I did not have to disable DRI or GLX as offered as solutions on other posts. I am not using the 915resolution solution. However, I did add the AIGLX option to the ServerLayout section.

I forget where I found out about AIGLX, but [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CompositeManager/AIGLXOnEdgy?highlight=%28aiglx%29") is how to enable it.
