### Post by blair on 2007-10-27
The mozilla-helix-player package can be downloaded and installed using Synaptic. Helix is the Real Audio open source project. If you don't see it in your packages list you may need to enable the commercial apt repositories: System > Admin > Synaptic > Settings > Repositories > Third Party Software.  Then reload your packages database and look for the mozilla helix package.
