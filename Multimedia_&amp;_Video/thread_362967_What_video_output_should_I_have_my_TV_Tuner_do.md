### Post by Jovec on 2007-02-16
How important is video quality to you?
How much storage space do you have?
How powerful is the computer that is going to be doing the encoding? And is the encoding going to be offloaded or done purely by your CPU?

As a general rule, encode first at a higher quality, then down sample if you need to, since you can't do the reverse.
