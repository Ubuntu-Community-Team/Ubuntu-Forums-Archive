### Post by Sporkman on 2007-08-15
So I did a system update recently, after not having done one in a while (I'm running 7.04). Afterwards, Firefox became slow & buggy, and video on Firefox was garbled.

So I went into the synaptic package manager, & reinstalled anything to do with Firefox & multimedia/flash, then tried Firefox again - same problem. I go into Automatix & un-install the few fancy non-free codec packages I had installed. Tried it again, Yahoo video no longer works, and YouTube video looks funny (or didn't work at all, I don't remember).

But when I went back into Automatix & re-installed the fancy non-free codecs I had uninstalled, Firefox & its multimedia started working perfectly again.


Moral of the story: after doing a system update & problems are encountered, uninstall & reinstall Automatix packages...?
