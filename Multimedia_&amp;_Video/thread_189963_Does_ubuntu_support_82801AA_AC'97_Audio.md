### Post by fer5437 on 2006-06-06
I had the solution for this. Actually Ubuntu does support 82801AA AC'97 Audio. It use alsa. What I do is configure the alsamixer.

Before that I didnt turn on the pc speaker on alsamixer. So I just turn on the pc speaker and now it working fine. Hopefully this can help.
