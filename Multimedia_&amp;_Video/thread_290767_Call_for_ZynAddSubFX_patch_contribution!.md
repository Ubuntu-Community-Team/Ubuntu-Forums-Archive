### Post by dolson on 2006-11-01
Hey all.

Atte Jensen has publicly requested people email him any patches that they wish to contribute to the next release of ZynAddSubFX, as he has worked it out with Paul that any submitted patches can be included in CVS, and therefore the next release.

Since ZynAddSubFX is a great-sounding synth, and hasn't had a new release in over a year, this is a good time to pitch in.

If you have any patches you wish to contribute, Atte will not ruin them - the only changes he will make are volume levels to make everything consistent together, as well as tagging the files.

Here is the thread where he posted his request: [http://sourceforge.net/mailarchive/forum.php?thread_id=30855330&forum_id=27340](http://sourceforge.net/mailarchive/forum.php?thread_id=30855330&forum_id=27340)

And here is the list so far:
[http://atte.dk/sounds/contributed_patches.html](http://atte.dk/sounds/contributed_patches.html)

If you have anything to contribute, please send it to him as soon as you can.
