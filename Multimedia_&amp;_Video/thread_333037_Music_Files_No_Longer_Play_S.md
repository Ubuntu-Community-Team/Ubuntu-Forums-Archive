### Post by Quilan on 2007-01-07
Can i suggest that you 'right click' on the volume icon and choose prefs..

I'm typing this from work so excuse a little vagueness...

You'll see a box that has a dropdown menu at the top. First check 'master vol' and make sure the volume is turned up (just like you would do it normally on your destkop). Next from the dropdown choose 'PCM' and check that it's not set to zero (again, make sure the vol is turned up just like you would normally from your desktop...)

If the sound server hasn't quit and you don't get any error messages, that could be your answer. It's happened to me before!
