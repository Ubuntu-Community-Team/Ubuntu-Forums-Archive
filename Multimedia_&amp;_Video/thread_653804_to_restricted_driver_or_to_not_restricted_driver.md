### Post by JasonValdron on 2007-12-30
When using the restricted drivers, you should be getting a much more better performance and, usually, better compatibility. But this is not always the case, but for most users, the restricted drivers are far better, they have 3D acceleration, which allow you to use Compiz or any 3D application.
