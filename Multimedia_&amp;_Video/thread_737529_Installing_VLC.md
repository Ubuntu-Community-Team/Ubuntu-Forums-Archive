### Post by KMuchane on 2008-03-28
It is far much easier to install through  the Synaptic Manager, on your Desktop click  System on the drop down menu click Administration then on Synaptic Package  Manager. You will be asked for a password, use the one you used to log in.          
Now you are in the Synaptic  Package  Manager environment.  Click on the search button at the top and simply enter vlc. Check the box next to vlc in the search results and mark it for installation.  A dialogue box will pop up asking you to Mark or Cancel, click on Mark and ignore the warning, I am using vlc and so are many others.  Now click on the Apply button at the top and then click on the  Apply button on the dialogue window which pops up to confirm your choice.      
Synaptic will start downloading vlc, after it has finished downloading it will install vlc automatically and all that you need to do is close that window.                           You can now access vlc from Applications > Sound & Video. ENJOY !
