### Post by Paerez on 2006-07-31
try adding this to the Device section in your xorg.conf:
```
Option "IgnoreEDID" "true"
```

edit: there is also this [http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/ChangeResolution#How_to_adjust_position_of_your_screen.3F](http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/ChangeResolution#How_to_adjust_position_of_your_screen.3F)
