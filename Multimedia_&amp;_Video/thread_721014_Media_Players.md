### Post by rudihawk on 2008-03-14
My thoughts:

I have tried(in no particluar order):
1)Rythmbox
2)Exaile
3)Xmms
4)Amarok
5)Banshee

Of all the players I have used I prefer Banshee the most, - its the only one that I have used so far that is able to read my albumart that was embedded into mu music. (Rythmbox,exaile,amarok) have the support for albumart but as far as I know they can only download it from the net and use it like that. (I've also heard that Amarok stores the albumart in a whole separate database, which might be slower.)

I think Banshee can do anything Rythmbox and more, Exaile is a faster less bloated version of amarok.

So if I had to reccomend a music a played i would reccomend Banshee or Exaile.
