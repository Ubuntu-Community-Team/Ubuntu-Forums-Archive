The 6.7.195 release is NOT amd/ati's driver (fglrx). It is the Xorg open source driver (ati/radeon).

The next release of amd/ati's proprietary driver (fglrx) will be 8.42.xx. The most recent release was 8.41, but as that driver was a complete re-write, and it wasn't really finished, amd adviced against installing it on anything else than radeon HD (r600?).
Not because it wouldn't work on other cards, but because the older driver (8.40) would still be better than the new one for all cards except the HD series, which wasn't supported at all in the older driver.

The 8.42 driver should be released sometime this month and will hopefully support all cards from radeon 9500 up to the HD series. And it is supposed to support aiglx. [More info here]("http://www.phoronix.com/forums/showthread.php?t=5422"). [And here]("http://www.phoronix.com/forums/showthread.php?t=5051").
