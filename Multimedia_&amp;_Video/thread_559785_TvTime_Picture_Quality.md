If it is much better on a tv screen than your computer monitor, it is most likely due to two things: 

1.  You are sitting much closer to your monitor so artifacts are easier to see
2.  Interlacing on your monitor.  Use something like VLC to view it and make sure you turn the deinterlacing filter on and it will look much better.
