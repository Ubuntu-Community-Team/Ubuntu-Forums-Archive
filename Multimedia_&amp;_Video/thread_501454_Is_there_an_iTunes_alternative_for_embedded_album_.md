### Post by Bazirker on 2007-07-15
Rhythmbox seems to be set up like iTunes...for example, it displays album art, shares music libraries over a LAN, and has that bar over at the left side to list music sources (library, radio, playlists, etc).  Give it a shot and let us know what you think.
