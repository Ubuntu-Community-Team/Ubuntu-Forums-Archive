Not a big deal (except to me!) but my Sony Vaio VGN-A60GP has ZERO sound when plugged into the docking station.

I doubt there is any way around this, though if anyone knows of a fix, please holler.

One possible work-around is to use USB speakers.  That ties up a USB port of course, but I was able to hear audio from movies over my USB VOIP phone set.  

I will point out that on the Vaio VGN-AXX the audio is very weak over the built in speakers...you really need to either have a headset, external speakers, or a docking station.
