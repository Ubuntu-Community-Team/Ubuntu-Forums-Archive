### Post by SilviuM on 2006-07-14
Hi,

Line in does not output to 5.1. Line in is not ouputing digital sound it only redirects the analog sound to the output of the sound blaster. No digital transformation there :p . To make your sound card output to 5.1 the line in input you must use some software that can record the line in input in digital format and then output it with a 5.1 sound format. Try some of the winamp plug-ins that can do this, i do not know exactly their name but I know they exists cause I used some of them.

Good luck.
