### Post by barry253 on 2007-06-27
ALL -

I finally tracked down this problem and solution:

[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux-source-2.6.20/+bug/78684](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux-source-2.6.20/+bug/78684)

The problem is with the motherboard and AGP detection.

To fix:
[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux-source-2.6.20/+bug/78684/comments/31](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/linux-source-2.6.20/+bug/78684/comments/31)

You need to change "2.6.20-14-generic" to your current kernel version (go to /lib/modules/) and see what the latest one is.

Worked for me.
