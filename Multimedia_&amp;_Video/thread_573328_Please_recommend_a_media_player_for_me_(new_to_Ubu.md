### Post by swoskow on 2007-10-11
Since you mention listening to live music, what formats are your music files in?  Are they lossless (flac, shn etc) or lossy (mp3 etc)?  The reason I ask is if you have music in a lot of different formats and want to play them all then you need a player that can do that or you have to convert them all to a format that will play them.  Look through the repos and find a player that can play the files.  I also listen to a lot of live music in lossless formats such as flac and shn.  Most players can handle flac but not shn.  One of the posters mentioned xmms.  That is the player I use because it is one of the few that plays shn's.  I think it would be a good choice for you.  Make sure you also grab the plug ins.
