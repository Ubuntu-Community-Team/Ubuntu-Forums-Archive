### Post by shakabra on 2008-06-09
I finally found an easy solution.  The player on the original site wouldn't work because it required windows media player and directX.  Today I found a site at [http://www.thestreamcentre.com](http://www.thestreamcentre.com) that allows me to listen to my favorite station.  So if you are having trouble listening to streaming radio and you have all the proper codecs installed try this site.
