### Post by sygin on 2007-10-02
Hi,
I have the PCI version of this receiver. I am in the UK, Kent and
have got it working using mythtv.

Go to the Mythtv wiki and follow the how to. Mythtv scans for 
channels after you select the area you are in.

I had to download files from linuxtv.org and compile some drivers for my card. 
It was all explained in the how to and is not too much of a challenge.

I have looked at the instructions for the USB2 version and believe that if you 
follow the instructions for the Nova 500 T PCI it will work. The PCI version
is very similar to the USB2 version, the PCI card acts as a USB hub.

Try: 

[http://wiki.efficientpc.co.uk/index.php/Install_Nova-T_500_On_Ubuntu_Linux](http://wiki.efficientpc.co.uk/index.php/Install_Nova-T_500_On_Ubuntu_Linux)

or (does not seem to be a step by step guide)

[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_WinTV_Nova-T_USB2](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_WinTV_Nova-T_USB2)


All the best,
Sygin

PS:  It is well worth the effort. Fantastic with Mythtv.

PPS: You could also try Mythbuntu [http://www.mythbuntu.org/](http://www.mythbuntu.org/)
