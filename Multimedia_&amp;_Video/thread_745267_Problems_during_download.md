Try installing pidgin if you are on Ubuntu or Kopete if you use Kubuntu. There are alternative applications for just MSN that provide more functionality, but Pidgin and Kopete are unified messengers ( much like Trillian ).

The proprietary Linux messengers are all old and pretty much useless as they have not been updated in a long time.

Pidgin is installed by default in Ubuntu. You can also get updated versions from [getdeb.net]("http://www.getdeb.net/") or by [compiling from source]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=613049&highlight=compile+pidgin").
