### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-02-08
I agree that gnash is unstable, but you shouldn't blame the programmers too much, as they're doing the best they can trying to reverse-engineer a player for a file format that's not open-source.

You also may not know that you can install a proprietary, closed-source flash plugin from adobe that should work without a problem.  See [http://packages.ubuntu.com/gutsy/web/flashplugin-nonfree](http://packages.ubuntu.com/gutsy/web/flashplugin-nonfree) . That will allow you to view flash until the gnash player gets to a stable release.
