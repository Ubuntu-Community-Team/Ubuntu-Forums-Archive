### Post by dolson on 2006-03-17
This is the only official forum for [the Ubuntu Studio Project](http://www.ubuntustudio.com/)!

We welcome any and all discussion here, relating to using Ubuntu as your Digital Audio Workstation, contributing work on audio software to Ubuntu, or discussion about our wiki project.

If you want to post some music you recorded in Linux, please do that as well. I would ask that you put a tag in your title so that we can recognize posts that have links to your music. For example, put **[MP3], [Ogg], or [SONG]** at the start of the Title of your thread. This way, people who want something to listen to can quickly find it, and people who don't want to read posts that they may consider "spamming" can simply pass them by easily enough.

I look forward to what you guys have to share, in terms of art, contributions, and support.

Rock on!
