### Post by Melcar on 2007-11-07
The current version, 8.42.3, supports AIGLX, but performance is crappy and people are having a lot of issues with them under Compiz (screen flickering, screen corruption, etc.).  The drivers themselves offer substantial 2D/3D performance boosts compared to previous releases, so they're worth installing.  You may want to keep using XGL for Compiz support though, until the next driver release (hopefully).
