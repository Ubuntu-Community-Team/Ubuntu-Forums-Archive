### Post by jis on 2006-09-13
I got less flickering by changing the resolution of other screen higher (actually same as the other monitor) by changing xorg.conf. The flickering redused especially in the other screen, in the one of which settings I did not change. Not to be too good, I got some horizontal, about 5mm thick, slightly darker line across both monitors: In the primary monitor it is in the upper half of monitor and in the secondary monitor it is in the lower half of monitor.
