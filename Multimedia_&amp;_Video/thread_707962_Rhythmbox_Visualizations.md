### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-02-25
no downloads, but:

Rhythmbox supports all libvisual-based visualizations, including the
G-Force libvisual plugin.  You need to ensure that you have
libvisual-plugins and have gstreamer support for said plugins
installed and enabled on your distribution in order for it to appear
in the list of plugins.
