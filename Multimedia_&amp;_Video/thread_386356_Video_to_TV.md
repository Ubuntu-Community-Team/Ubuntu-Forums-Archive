### Post by barney_1 on 2007-03-16
Hi there,

If you don't have a TV tuner card (allows you to hook cable tv up and use your linux box as a tivo) I don't think mythtv is what you want.

I would check out [My Media System]("http://mms.sunsite.dk/").  It is a bit of work to get everything configured the way that you want to, but you can use it as a full service media center.

Make sure you check out their wiki about installation.  There is a repo available for edgy so you don't have to compile from source if you don't care to.
