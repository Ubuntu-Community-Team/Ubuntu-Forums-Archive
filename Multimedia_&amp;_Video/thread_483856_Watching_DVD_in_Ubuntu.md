### Post by mig5 on 2007-06-25
You need libcssdvd and some other stuff to play 'proper' DVDs, as they are encoded.  Look at this howto, [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=396738](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=396738) for details.  DVDs will play in many players, such as: mplayer, vlc and xine, so there shouldn't really be any need to download another media player, just the libraries and codecs.
