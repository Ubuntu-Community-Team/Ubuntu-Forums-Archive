### Post by tgm4883 on 2007-08-23
The different colors say what type of show it is (for instance, green is sporting even I believe)

It says it conflicts because one starts an 1:001 and one ends at 11:00.  It will still record both shows though and should be fine. 

As to the multiple conflicts, it's just letting you know that in case you decide to manually record an episode, what it will conflict with.  It should also tell you whats happening to the episodes that have a dotted line surrounding them.

There is the key on the far left that tells you whats happening.  IE, if/when its going to record and on what tuner, and if it has been recorded previously.

I guess they feel it is better to have more info than less with mythweb.
