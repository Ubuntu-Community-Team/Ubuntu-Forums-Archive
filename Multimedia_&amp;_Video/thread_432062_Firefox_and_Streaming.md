### Post by Linux_oid on 2007-05-04
Simple solution is to install "[MediaPlayerConnectivity]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/446")" (Firefox Add-ons).  

As soon as you restart Firefox, it checks your media players and let you assign player to play different files (streams).
