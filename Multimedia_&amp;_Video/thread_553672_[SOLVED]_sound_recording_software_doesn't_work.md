### Post by eye208 on 2007-09-18
Check out your soundcard mixer. It's in the upper right corner of the screen, next to the clock.

There's an output tab and an input tab. The fact that you hear yourself through the headset means the mic is enabled in the output tab. But you want to record stuff, so you need to switch to the input tab and enable it there. You might even want to disable it in the output tab to avoid feedback since the mic will also pick up the sound from your headset.
