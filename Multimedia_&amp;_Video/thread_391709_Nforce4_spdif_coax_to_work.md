### Post by Jeroi on 2007-03-23
Ok, how to enable spdif out in kubuntu:

1. Open terminal
2. type: alsamixer
3. go with right to [IEC958 P] and press : M

MM should now change to green GO and it should work now.

Now your stereo jacks plays analog music, and spdif plays digital music for your hometheatre.

Hope all can find this fix usefull.
