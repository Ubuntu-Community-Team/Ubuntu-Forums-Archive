### Post by logos34 on 2006-12-21
here try this (freeware):

[http://mediacoder.sourceforge.net/index.htm](http://mediacoder.sourceforge.net/index.htm)

You can also get lame at sourceforge. You'll end up having to point mediacoder to whatever folder contains the downloaded lame.exe.

Just takes a few secs to transcode a song (forget that alive real time recording).  

Avoid lossy-to-lossy conversion whenever possible because it degrades the sound.  But in this case I can't imagine you'll notice the diff above the road noise, and hey, desperate times call for desperate measures!
