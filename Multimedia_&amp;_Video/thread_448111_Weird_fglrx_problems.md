### Post by Caligula on 2007-05-18
The good news: I've finally found the reason that I couldn't play dvds either in kaffeine OR mplayer!
The bad news: There are none :P

The thing is, I've just installed the proprietary ATI driver for my video card ATI RS482 [Radeon Xpress 200M].

I had all kinds of problems, kaffeine would just show a blurred image, kmplayer wouldn't show the movie in maximum size, and so on. The only player that worked was vlc. But in vlc the picture was somewhat pixeled and not great fun to watch.

I've decided to try and switch back to the native ati driver, and it worked! I can now watch good old walk the line in kaffeine :)

I hope this helps anyone with a similar problem.
