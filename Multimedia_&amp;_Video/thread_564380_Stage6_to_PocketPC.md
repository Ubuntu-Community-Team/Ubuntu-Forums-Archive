----------------------------------------- Introduction --------------------------------------------------------------
Its a program that automates everything from downloading a video from stage6 to converting it so you can play the video on a pocketpc, smartphone, psp or whatever device you have. Install TCPMP (its free) on the pocketpc or smartphone in order to play the file, PSPs can play them automatically.

First install TCPMP on your palm, pocketpc or smartphone:
[http://picard.exceed.hu/tcpmp/test/](http://picard.exceed.hu/tcpmp/test/)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Just download and unzip the archive attached. Open a terminal and type:

1) python fetchit.py (Make sure you have python-tk installed from the repos)

--------------------- What Do I Type -----------------------------
Title: Enter The Name Of The Video
Link: Enter The Page Address (eg: [http://stage6.divx.com/channel1/video/1234567/video-title](http://stage6.divx.com/channel1/video/1234567/video-title))
