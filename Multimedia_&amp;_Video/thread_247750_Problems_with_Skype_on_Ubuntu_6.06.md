### Post by eye208 on 2008-01-18
Check the settings in the volume control. Right-click the speaker symbol in the upper right corner of the desktop, then select "open volume control" from the menu. This will open the mixer panel. Open the preferences and check all the options to reveal any hidden controls. Then switch to the recording tab and check if all the sliders are up and not muted. Finally switch to the input tab and select the recording source, e.g. "mic", "front mic" or similar.
