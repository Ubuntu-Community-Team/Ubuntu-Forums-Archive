### Post by wererabbit on 2006-10-10
1. Make sure your speakers are plugged in. (sorry, had to ask)
2. Try different audio jacks on the back of the PC to see if the sound is being routed out a different path
3. You say you cannot hear music - how about any sounds at all?
4. Determine whether the sound is coming off the motherboard or out of a soundcard
5. Probably would be helpful to get some system specs from you on your pc brand/model/configuration.  Without this info, it's hard to give anything beyond general advice.
