### Post by warp99 on 2008-03-24
With gmplayer you can scroll through the subtitle menus and pick which subtitles you want. Right clip on the player to bring up the menu, then choose **DVD > Subtitle Languages** to scroll through your choice. Also check you settings in Preferences under the Subtitle & OSD menu and see if they match you DVD.

With Xine you would right click on the player, choose **Subtitle > Channel ** then you can choose which channel the subtitle may be on instead of the **Auto** tick box. Also check under the menu** Settings > Setup > Subtitles** to see that they match you DVD.  Your  DVD may be using some different encoding. Hope this helps. :)
