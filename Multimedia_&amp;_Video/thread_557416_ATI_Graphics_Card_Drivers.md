Hello everybody,
I was having loads of problems installing the ATI fglrx drivers for my ATi Radeon 9600, and after great difficulty and lots of terminal usage, I came across a method for the installation of the card on the Ubuntu help site.

The main problem was that I could only use the 640x480 resolution, on a 19 inch monitor. The graphics were awful, you couldn't do anything with the xorg.conf file and the x server randomly crashed. Annoying. :confused:

The page tells you how to install on Feisty Fawn (7.04), Edgy Eft (6.10) and Dapper Drake LTS (6.06). All editions of Ubuntu are supported.

Only ATi Radeon 9500 and newer work with the drivers mentioned here.

Link:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI]("http://ubuntuforums.org/newthread.php?do=newthread&f=138")

I hope this helps,
