### Post by jmate24 on 2007-08-15
It happens when a disk is not recognized by Gnome Baker Or K3b

the disk is of an unknown (but possibly) legitimate manufacturer.
most common occurrences happen with Rewritable media (i.e. Maxell etc.)

It causes a error... that says it cannot recognize the manufacturer of a disk and therefore burns the disk very slowly and can lead to data loss and failure of the media (coaster effect).

It is recommended that you use a well-known manufacturer (i.e. Sony TDK)
