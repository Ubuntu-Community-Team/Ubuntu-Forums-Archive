### Post by jmate24 on 2007-08-12
Bulk convert ogg to wav or mp3to wav?

try going to the Applications Menu at the top left-hand corner of the screen click it.
then at the bottom click 'Add/Remove..'
next the 'Add/Remove' window open and at the top right-hand corner of the window (below the minimize button) you will see a box with up/down arrows on the very right.
click the arrows and chose 'All Available Applications'
then near the empty box at the top left of the window you see the word 'Search:'
type in the empty box 'Sound Converter'(Ubuntu) or 'Sound Konverter'(KDE).
(Make sure you click OK in the new window to enable Multiuniverse and or Universe Repositories.)
Then click on the appropriate application for your 'distro'.
next click OK on the bottom right-hand corner of the window(it has a green 'return' looking arrow).

it will install and will be available to you in the Applications>Sound & Video Menu:)
