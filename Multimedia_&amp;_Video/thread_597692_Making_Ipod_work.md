### Post by Blackcomb on 2007-11-01
New iPods have encrypted firmware. This makes it impossible for amarok or other applications (non-iTunes) to put music on the devices the "right way". iTunes 7.4 implements a secret algorithm for writing data to the iPod. The only way to synchronize your iPod is to use iTunes. Since iTunes has no windows port and Wine isn't an option I'll share you my method:

[http://ipodvirtualos.blogspot.com/]("http://ipodvirtualos.blogspot.com/")
