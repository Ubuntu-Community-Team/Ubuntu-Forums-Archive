### Post by dmgursky on 2007-12-16
After much consternation, hand-wringing, head banging, and other assorted, well, expressions of frustration, I came upon a post that asserted that there was a bug in the version of X.Org ATI driver that shipped with Gutsy Gibbon / 7.10 and there was no way to run a multi-head system.  Lo and behold, I downgraded to 1:6.6.3-2 (the version that shipped with Feisty Fawn / 7.03) and the system works.  Not flawlessly, but I'll ascribe that to the wonderful quirks of Apple's 23" HD display, whichever variant I'm sitting in front of (FWIW, the screen has a really annoying flicker, which is weird since it is a DVI interface, and shouldn't flicker at all, but I digress)

Has the ATI driver been fixed?  I keep getting update notices for 1.6.7.195-1, but I've forgotten if this is the broken version that ships with Gutsy, or if it is a new version that actually works...
