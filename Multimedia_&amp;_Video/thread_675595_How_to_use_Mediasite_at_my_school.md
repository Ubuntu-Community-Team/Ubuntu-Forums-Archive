### Post by NoGuarantees on 2008-02-03
Recent versions of Mediasite use Windows Media to encode their video.  If your school is using 4.x, AND it is encoded properly, you should be able to view (but not control) presentations by installing Automatix.  To test it, you can go here:  [http://www.mediasite.com/](http://www.mediasite.com/)
If you can view the presentations, AND your school encoded them the same way you are set.

Medialandscape will NOT be of benefit to you at all.  Your school's Mediasite administrator would have to use it to convert the presentation, since it must be done on the server.
