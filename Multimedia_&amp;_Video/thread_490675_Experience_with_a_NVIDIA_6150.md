### Post by pleban on 2007-07-18
Answers:
1. BIOS update was smooth and painless. I experienced no problems whatsoever.
2. Compiz-fusion sort of works (it is nvidia chipset after all). I don't see more problems with 6150 than with 7300 I have in second computer. You just need to configure xorg.conf properly. Be warned that compiz-fusion is buggy and crashes quite often anyway.

My impressions in general. After bios update I am quite satisfied. Board is quiet (no fans!) and realiable. Sound quality is acceptable. It has every connection I need and more (like HDTV-out). It is not for gamers though. I tried RTW2 demo and it was unbearable. 

For my requirements (TV watching, movies, internet, basic office) it is more than enough.

I had troubles with old BIOS - TV watching in higher resolutions was not possible, because of occasional video lockups. I mean only tv program window locked, not the whole X.
