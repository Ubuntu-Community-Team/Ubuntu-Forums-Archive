this is just a quick piece of info.
do you problems with sound in ubuntu ?
do you your sound work one minute and stop the next?
have you been round the forums looking for answers?

Well I have !
think I have found the root problem. 
2 sound cards!
I got into Ubuntu 2 months ago so i'am not a Linux genius. After 20 or so attempts to get the sound right I have finally got it sorted.
Very simple in fact. All I had to do was go into bios and turn off the sound card there and BINGO sound all the time and its great at last! I have an on board sound card. the 2 sound cards were in conflict
Please to all new ubuntu o/s users dont give up, keep trying you will get there in the end.
This worked for me (it might not for you).

Remember if you solve a problem LET US ALL KNOW IN THE FORUMS. WE can all do with any help we can get.
