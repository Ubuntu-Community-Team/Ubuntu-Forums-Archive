### Post by avik on 2007-06-14
Actually, for me, it's the monitor.  My monitor has a button labelled "2," which I need to press if I switch to or from Windows.  Basically, the two OSes (Windows and GNU/Linux) use different settings.  Your monitor should have an auto-calibrate button, or maybe a menu to use (which is not related in any way to the OS, but is a part of the hardware itself).
