### Post by Aphorism on 2006-01-03
You will want a sound card that has opened its documentation up to the public.  If you want a very comprehensive list of 'play by the rules' chipsets, check out the OpenBSD.com hardware listing -- they are very similar to Ubuntu in their goals -- perhaps even more 100% free mandate...

I _will_ say that you shouldn't have too much problem in sound land.  Also, send nvidia an email telling them to release their documentation and specifications to the public so that we all don't have to wait on them to fix problems in their drivers...
