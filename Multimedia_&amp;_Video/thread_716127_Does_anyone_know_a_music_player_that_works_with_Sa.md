### Post by haitianp619 on 2008-03-05
1. connect the sandisk to comp, should be mounted on your desktop
2. verify the it is mounted at /media/whatever
3. open up amarok, configure amarok, media devices, add device.
4. select, generic audio player, name it whatever you want, mount point is 
/media/whatever
5. hit ok apply ok
6. hit the device tab, choose whatever you named it and hit connect. 

its should work
