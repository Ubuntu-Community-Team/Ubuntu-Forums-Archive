### Post by eggdeng on 2007-10-12
Ubuntu does not support proprietary formats like mp3 by default, you have to install the appropriate codecs. Instructions at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats")
