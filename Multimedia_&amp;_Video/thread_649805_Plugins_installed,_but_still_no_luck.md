### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2007-12-26
Uninstall flashplugin-nonfree and download the latest version from Adobe's site.
[http://www.adobe.com/shockwave/download/download.cgi?P1_Prod_Version=ShockwaveFlash&P2_Platform=Linux](http://www.adobe.com/shockwave/download/download.cgi?P1_Prod_Version=ShockwaveFlash&P2_Platform=Linux)
Get the .tar.gz and follow the instructions on the web page.

Enter about:plugins in the URL bar to see if you installed it correctly.
