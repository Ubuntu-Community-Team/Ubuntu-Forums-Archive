### Post by Peregrine88 on 2006-12-04
Sometimes a corrupted file will sound really horrible. Try playing some other songs or files. If it still persists then it might be hardware. I find my speaker/earphone wires get old and drop out and/or make funny noises. I have to twist them or find the right position to get them to work right. Try using another pair of headphones and see if your headphones are the problem. If they are I would suggest replacing them with something in your price range, or re-wiring them; that would take soldering skills and tools though. I doubt it is interference, but if it continues with different files and different headphones then it might be.
