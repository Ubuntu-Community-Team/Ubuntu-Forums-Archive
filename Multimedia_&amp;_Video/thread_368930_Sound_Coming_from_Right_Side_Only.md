### Post by louis_nichols on 2007-02-24
Sound volume can be set independently for left/right channels. What you need to do is right click on the sounf icon in the system tray and select sound settings. There, check the sliders. PCM and Master are of interest. From the sound of it, I think you will find that one of PCM's sliders is on mute. my suggestion is that you check the option under each pair of sliders to move them at the same time.
