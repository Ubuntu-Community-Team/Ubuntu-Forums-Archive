### Post by jpeddicord on 2006-11-22
Newer versions of most programs are not available in older distributions due to feature changes and unresolved dependecies on other programs. The Totem 2.x line is available in Edgy and up; it can be downloaded here:

[http://packages.ubuntu.com/edgy/gnome/totem](http://packages.ubuntu.com/edgy/gnome/totem)

Be warned that it most likely will not work on a Dapper system, as it requires much newer libraries only available in Edgy.
