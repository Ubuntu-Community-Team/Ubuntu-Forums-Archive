### Post by jpmontalbo on 2007-10-09
Hello, I have confirmed that Samsung K3 and K5 now both work in Gutsy Beta 1. Transferring songs and playing songs from the mp3 player's work. I guess Gutsy's newer libmtp ironed out the problem's people have been having with samsung mp3 players. I will test out my sisters YP-T9 just for the heck of it to see if it will work as well.

By the way I used both Amarok and Rhythmbox to do the testing. Rhythmbox seemed more stable handling transferring songs and such.

if you need more information heres another thread of what people did to resolve this issue for Feisty. 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=317863&page=1](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=317863&page=1)

Looks like what they did was upgrade the firmware which does away with MTP and makes the mp3 player be detected as a USB mass storage device. So drag and drop would be how to transfer songs to the unit. So if you can't use Gutsy at the moment, you could always change the firmware on your mp3 player. However, Gutsy's final release is just around the corner.

Good luck!
