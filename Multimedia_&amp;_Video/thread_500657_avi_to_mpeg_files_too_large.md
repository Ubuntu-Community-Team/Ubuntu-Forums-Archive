### Post by vanadium on 2007-07-14
mpeg 2 simply is not as effective in compressing video as mpeg 4 which is in your avi. You need to specify a lower  bitrate. This will create a smaller mpeg at the expense of lower video quality (possibly not yet noticeable). How long is your avi? You should be able to fit about two hours of DVD video with very good quality.

Anyway, you might want to try DeVeDe, a gui application that is designed to do what you want, convert avi (or other video sources) to a video DVD. It'll probably figure out the optimal bitrate settings automatically (I have no experience yet myself with DeVeDe).
