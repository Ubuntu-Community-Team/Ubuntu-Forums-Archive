### Post by fela on 2008-03-26
you want something like itunes...

rhythmbox is based on gtk, and is a good music player imho. So if you get a gtk theme (or make one) you could get rhythmbox to look just like itunes if you want. Or you could get it to look like anything you want. As always with linux: if it's not there, make it yourself.

I'm saying rhythmbox cause i don't sacrifice functionality for themes. If you have a good app with a terrible theme, make a theme for it.
