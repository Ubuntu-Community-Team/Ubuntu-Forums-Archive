### Post by rainwalker on 2007-09-08
It takes a while to startup because it has to load the KDE libraries it needs to run, which aren't native to GNOME (of course). I don't know of any way to make it load faster, though.
As for the Xine thing, that's just the sound engine it uses. There are different engines, but I can't name any off the top of my head. As far as I know, the engine doesn't affect load times.
