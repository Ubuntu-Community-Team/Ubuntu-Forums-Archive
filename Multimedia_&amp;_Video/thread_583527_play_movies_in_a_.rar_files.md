### Post by vikhr on 2008-07-04
these type of movies comes with lots of rar files like
the-life-before-her-eyes-done.r01
the-life-before-her-eyes-done.r02
the-life-before-her-eyes-done.r03
.
.
.
the-life-before-her-eyes-done.r48
the-life-before-her-eyes-done.rar
....................................
Now all u gotta do is first install rar program
**sudo apt-get install rar**
then
**rar e the-life-before-her-eyes-done.rar**

All the small 12-14 MB rar files will be decompressed automatically
