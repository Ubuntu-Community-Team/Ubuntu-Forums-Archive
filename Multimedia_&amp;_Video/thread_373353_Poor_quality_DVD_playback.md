### Post by c-m on 2007-03-01
I have a DVD that plays back perfectly fine in good quality with windows. In ubuntu however, that same dvd when played back give off a poor quality picture.

I have used mplayer, xine and vlc but get the same problem on all of them. Whenever there is any sort of movement especially at speed, a series of lines canbe seen, as if the picture is heavily interlaced or something.

Why do i only get this in unbuntu?

---

### Post by Datalanche on 2007-03-01
In xine, start your DVD and try hitting the i button. This will toggle deinterlacing on or off. The same can be done in vlc by right clicking on the video and checking a deinterlace option, but xine works much better.
