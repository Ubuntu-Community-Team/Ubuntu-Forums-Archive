### Post by SirBaer on 2007-08-06
Hi, 

I've had the same problem for the last few days, and I think I've finally found a solution.

Try

sudo cp /lib/modules/2.6.20-16-generic/misc/fglrx.ko /lib/modules/2.6.20-16-generic/kernel/drivers/video/

to copy the module to the right place
and then

sudo depmod -a

to correct whatever got wrong in the first place. 
Works for me like a charm.
Now I've only got to find out why on earth the XServer sucks up to 70% of my CPU-usage. *GRR*

Hope it works for you too.
