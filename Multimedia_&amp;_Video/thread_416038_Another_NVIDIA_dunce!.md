### Post by johnc4510 on 2007-04-20
Toward the bottom of your xorg.cong file is this section:

Section "Screen"
Identifier "Screen0"
Device "Videocard0"
Monitor "Monitor0"
DefaultDepth 24
Option "metamodes" "1440x900 +0+0; 800x600 +0+0; 640x480 +0+0"
SubSection "Display"
Depth 24
Modes "1600x1200" "1280x1024" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"
EndSubSection
EndSection

Where yours says:

Identifier "Screen0"

Mine says:

Identifier "Default Screen"

You might try changing this, but keep in mind, you may have to change it back if it doesn't work.
