### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-07-13
I use an M-Audio Delta 66 card with a PII, 300 MHz machine running a Live Dynebolic CD.  I also have Dapper installed so it's technically dual boot.  The Delta 66 card is basically 2 channel (2 microphone preamps), but with some clever connections you can get 6 inputs and 6 outputs (line level and digital).

I use it for mixboard church concerts.  I can record 2-hour concerts without a hiccup.

Dynebolic is a low-latency kernel that is slimmed down and optimized for audio work.

I boot into Dapper because I can.
