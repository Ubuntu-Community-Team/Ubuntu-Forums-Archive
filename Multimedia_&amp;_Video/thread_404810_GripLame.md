### Post by krussell on 2007-04-09
Hello :-)
lame is not a dependency for grip, as seen here [http://packages.ubuntu.com/edgy/gnome/grip](http://packages.ubuntu.com/edgy/gnome/grip). anyway, you can download lame from here [http://packages.ubuntu.com/edgy/sound/lame](http://packages.ubuntu.com/edgy/sound/lame). Install it and give grip a retry.
