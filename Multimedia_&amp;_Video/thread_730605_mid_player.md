### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-03-21
Timidity.

Files with the extension .mid are MIDI files, so you were on the right track.

Kmidi sends the MIDI data to an external MIDI device, Timidity renders it to audio internally. That's why nothing was coming out of your sound card with Kmidi.
