### Post by terminateprocess on 2006-03-08
A new sound card might improve sound quality, but I'd imagine it would have little or no effect on performance, in general. If it's bad sound quality, describe how so. This could have something to do with the card itself being cheap and low quality, or it could be some sort of driver issue. If it's system performance in general, it might have nothing to do with the sound, and almost certainly nothing to do with the hardware.

If you really want a new soundcard, you might want to check out a SoundBlaster Live; they're cheaper than the Audigy (around $25) and have good sound quality.
