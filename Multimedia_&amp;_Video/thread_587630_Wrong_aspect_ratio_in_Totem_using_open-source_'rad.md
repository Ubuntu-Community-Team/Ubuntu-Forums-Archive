After a day or so I read online that the open-source radeon driver ('ati') has modest support for XV, so I thought I would give it a try. This would at least let me enable Compiz =P

So I disabled the fglrx driver and went back to the ati driver. Compiz works fine, and I can indeed play video without any screen-tearing (thought occasionally it can be a tad sluggish), but now my problem is that totem-gstreamer is playing video at the wrong resolution. Its stretching the video horizontally a little bit. Its not bad, definitely watchable, but would be much nicer if the video would play at the normal aspect ratio.
