### Post by daletan on 2007-04-22
Hi there, I had a similar issue with my twinview ... 

To fix it, I booted with _only_ the analog monitor plugged into my NV 6600GT. Went in, reinstated the Nvidia drivers using System>Administration>Restricted Drivers Manager. Things should be fine after another reboot.

I'm not sure whether it'll work with your system because I don't see any reference to analog monitors (dual DVI?) but I hope that helps.
