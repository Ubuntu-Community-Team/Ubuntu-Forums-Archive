### Post by deepwave on 2007-05-18
I am guessing that your DVD is encrypted.  You will need libdvdcss2 installed to play encrypted DVDs natively under Linux.  Please not that in the US, that anti-circumvention laws apply and libdvdcss2 is not legal per se.
