### Post by jackmc on 2007-04-23
Strange, I just noticed this today in Ubuntu. Major fuzz where there didn't used to be any distortion. Heres how it went for me:

In VLC, vol was around 3/4, system vol was about 1/4.

Turned up the vol in VLC to full (has always been fine) got major fuzz.

Turned it back down, then increased *system* volune - perfect.

I was able to play it louder, with less distortion, by changing system instead of app volume. I dont know if that relates to what you are experiencing, but it sounds similar..
