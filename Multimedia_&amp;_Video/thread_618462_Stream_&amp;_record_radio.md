I have difficulties with with some of the tools I have tried mostly that they overlap or cut the songs short. I am partial to The Lastripper, it records from lastfm and has good quality but more importantly it add's the id3 tags and album art for me. I have also read lots and played some with the following, but mileage may vary.  I have yet to find a app/util as good as media catcher for windows, this worked on pandora, slacker, and lastfm and provided all id3 and album art and would just continue to run 24/7.  

kstreamripper for ripping directly from the stream

exaile (GTK music player) has a plugin for streamripper

rhythmbox (GTK music player) has a plugin as well.

I have also read that you can do the same with mplayer, but haven't tried this,
