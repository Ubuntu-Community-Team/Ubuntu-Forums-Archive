### Post by liquid circuit on 2008-04-05
I'm essentially doing the same thing (Behringer Eurorack MX 602A -> M-Audio Audiophile 2496).  Of course, make sure you are connecting the "Tape Out" on the mixer to the Line-In on your soundcard; its probably not a good idea to use the Master output on the mixer.
