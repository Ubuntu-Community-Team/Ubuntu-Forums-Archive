### Post by deadgobby on 2007-05-13
Install Lame, liblame0, and  liblame-dev in Synaptic. This will encode MP3 in Audacity. Next you can just open up a mp3 file right in Audacity.  That can be under File>Open . Now to record sound and lets say you want to take your vinyl. You want to be in stereo right. So now you need to go to Edit>Preferences and check if the recording channels are set to 2. Some time Audacity defaults to 1 = mono.
