Wow, a year old and no replies.  I hope you found a solution, phy5ik.  In case others end up here, or your pod is sitting in a drawer somewhere, this is what worked for me.  "Check ipod's files" in the file menu.  It will locate orphaned files and reunite them with the pod's db.  You can save yourself future stress by setting "automatically import itunes db on startup" in preferences.
