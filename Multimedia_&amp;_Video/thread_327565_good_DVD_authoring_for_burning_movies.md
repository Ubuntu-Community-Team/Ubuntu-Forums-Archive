### Post by pseudonym on 2006-12-29
I find [DVD Styler]("http://www.dvdstyler.de/") does the job. You can't get overly fancy with the menus but if you're looking for something simple to use you can't go past it. It's not in the Ubuntu repositories but you can download from the project's homepage (in my link) and install via 'sudo dpkg -i <package_name.deb> (full instructions are at the DVD styler site, anyway).

A similar program called 'qdvdauthor' is in the Ubuntu repositories but I find it a little clunky and more difficult to use than dvdstyler.
