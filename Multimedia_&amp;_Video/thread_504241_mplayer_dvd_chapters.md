### Post by slothjammin on 2008-01-03
mplayer dvd://1
where the #1 is the chapter number.

---

### Post by Sudz on 2008-02-05
That actually just picks the title.
What you want is 
```
mplayer dvd://1 -chapter 2
```
To play title 1 starting with chapter 2 for example.
