What kind of tuner are you looking for (QAM, ATSC, NTSC)? the Kworld ATSC 110 used to be available at tigerdirect for 39.99 after rebate and regularly can  be found between 50 and 75. It does all three (software encoding for NTSC). If you are looking for ATSC only you can find Air2PC cards on ebay for $40.00 or less.
