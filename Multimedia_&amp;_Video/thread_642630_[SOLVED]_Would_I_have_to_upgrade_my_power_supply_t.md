### Post by Existentialist on 2007-12-16
You could upgrade, but a quality power supply will outperform even a higher wattage cheap one.  The most important part of a power supply is its 12V rails. The number of these and their amperage are what determine prices/quality of the psu.  As for damaging the hardware, in the long run, yes you could do damage.  Initially, the computer will probably not boot if the power is insufficient.  But if it boots, and runs stably through some gpu and cpu stability tests you should be fine.  What are you looking at upgrading your card for?
