### Post by StephenF on 2008-02-27
This bug is due to the recent addition of gapless mp3 playback which drops the silent sound samples generated by the mp3 encoding and decoding process resulting in the resampler being fed nothing at the start of the song. This causes the resampler to emit an error message which you can see the text of in your console output.

This bug has been fixed upstream and a workaround exists. Simply configure JACK to use a sample rate of 44100 to match that of your mp3s. Obviously not all mp3 files have that sample rate but the vast majority of them do.

Here is a link to the latest source code.
[http://web.bethere.co.uk/idjc/download/idjc-0.7.3_pre17.tar.gz](http://web.bethere.co.uk/idjc/download/idjc-0.7.3_pre17.tar.gz)
