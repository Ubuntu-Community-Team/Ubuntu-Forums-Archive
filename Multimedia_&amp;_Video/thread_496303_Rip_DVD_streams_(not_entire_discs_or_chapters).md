### Post by reclusivemonkey on 2007-07-11

Ah, sorry no I've confused you there. To rip the DVDs you will need something like AcipRip (which is in the repos) or OGMRip which is my personal favourite. I believe there are some other who's names escape me now. A quick search in Synaptic will help there.

Kino is a video editor, which will allow you to add titles/credits to what you have ripped. It will import many formats so you should be fine with whatever you choose to rip with.

I've personally struggled to get any of the DVD authoring programs to work for me. Admittedly I may have been trying to get too many options to work at once as many people claim success with various ones that have not worked for me.

You can export to DVD directly from Kino, although you won't have titles and chapters this way (or at least I don't think so).
