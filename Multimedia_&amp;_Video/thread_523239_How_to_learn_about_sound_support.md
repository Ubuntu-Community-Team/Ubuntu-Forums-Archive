### Post by duffrecords on 2007-08-16
ALSA is a suite of hardware drivers, libraries and utilities which provide audio and MIDI functionality for the Linux operating system.  OSS is the same idea, but it's older and deprecated.

[The Unofficial ALSA Wiki]("http://alsa.opensrc.org/index.php/Main_Page") is a good place to learn more about it.

[Comprehensive Sound Problem Solutions Guide]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=205449") can help you to troubleshoot your system.
