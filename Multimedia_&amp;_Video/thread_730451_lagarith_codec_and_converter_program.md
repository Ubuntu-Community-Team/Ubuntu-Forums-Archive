### Post by Ossipon on 2008-04-09
I was also looking for a way to encode lagarith on linux, but so far I've been unable to find anything.
But in your case, converting the VOB files to a lossless format isn't really useful. VOB is already lossy and when converted to lagarith you'll only end up with a bunch of enormous files with exactly the same quality. If you would want to preserve your VHS tapes in lagarith, you'll have to re-capture them. And I'm not even sure if that's useful; many video capture cards use hardware mpeg or mjpeg compression, which makes your video input lossy. You would also need lots of disk space for lossless video. At least tens if not hunderds of gigabytes.
