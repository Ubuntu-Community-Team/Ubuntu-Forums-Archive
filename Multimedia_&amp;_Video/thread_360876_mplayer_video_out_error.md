### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-02-13
Hi. Try changing video drivers in Mplayer: open it, right-click on the Mplayer screen, select "Preferences", go to the second tab ("Video"), and from the "Available Drivers" select **xv**; close Mplayer and open it again, and try to play a video file.
