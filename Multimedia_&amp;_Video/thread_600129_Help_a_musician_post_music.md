### Post by darxoul on 2007-11-28
if you will mainly post music, i would recommend [www.mypodcast.com](www.mypodcast.com) . i tried it once just to see if it works or not. it is quite well. you have to change some of the parameters in your encoding (or you can re-encode using Audacity) to be able to hear the music as you post them, but the result is quite good. as far as i have seen blogger doesn't have a well integrated interface to post music directly from the editor or by using a third party application.
