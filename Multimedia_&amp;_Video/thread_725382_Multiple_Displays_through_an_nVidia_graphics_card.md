### Post by xc3RnbFO8P on 2008-03-15
In Add/Remove (All Available Applications) install NVTV tv out.
Start it in Application> Sound & Video.
If it don't, use terminal:
> nvidia-settings

Second from top in nvidia settings you should see the TV.
You have to play around with the resolution on your monitor and TV,
sometimes you have to have same resolution on both and use clone.
