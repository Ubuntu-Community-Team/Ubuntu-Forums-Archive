### Post by Crenfinkle on 2007-09-22
The best thing to do would be to try and get your favorite backup software working in wine. I have been able to do this with ripit4me and DVD Shrink, both of which can defeat any encryption algorithm, including ArcOSS. (winecfg will be your best friend when trying to set these up!) Also, you might look into K9Copy, which works similarly to DVD Shrink. It doesn't work with ArcOSS, and produces a much lower quality burn, but is the best native solution I have been able to find.
