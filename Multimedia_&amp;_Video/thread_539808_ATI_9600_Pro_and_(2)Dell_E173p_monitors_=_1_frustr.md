### Post by bandad on 2007-09-03
Cracked it!!!!

Got it working on my dual 17 inch LCD monitors and ATI 9200 (something) card.
The secret was ...

Option MonitorLayout  "CRT, CRT"

This is treating the DVI and the VGA both the same as VGA - but it works.
I also used the Virtual keyword in the Screen/Display section

```
Section "Screen"
	Identifier	"screen0"
	Device		"Radeon9200"
	Monitor		"IIyama"
	DefaultDepth	24
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth		24
		Virtual		2560 1024	
		Modes		"1280x1024" "1024x768" 
	EndSubSection
EndSection
```

I had to play around with the "LeftOf" and "RightOf" stuff to get it right, but you could see it was working when the cursor cam back on the other monitor - even though it was coming from the wrong side initially.

Hope it works for you

Bob
