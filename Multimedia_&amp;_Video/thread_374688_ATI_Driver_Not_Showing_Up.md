### Post by ubx on 2007-03-02
[SIZE="5"][COLOR="DarkGreen"]Solved[/COLOR][/SIZE]

Add either of these lines to the xorg.conf file important: 

> 
[COLOR="Red"][B]Section "Extensions"
Option "Composite" "Disable"
EndSection

Section "Extensions" 
    Option "Composite" "false"
EndSection [/B][/COLOR] 

Voila~
>
