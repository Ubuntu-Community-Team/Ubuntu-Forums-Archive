### Post by moeFinley on 2006-07-30
Do you have more than one AV channel?  If you flick through your AV channels do you get audio on any of them, even ones without a picture?

Most TVs have options title 'Dubbing' or 'Sources' which allow you to take audio from one source and route it to another.

I can tell you why you had a black and white image when you tried scart.  You graphics card is outputing S-Video which transmits red blue and green separatly.  Your TV was expecting composite which transmits red blue and green as one.  Hense your TV only showed the red channel.

Most TV can be switch from composite to S-Video.  But I'm guessing being able to use your scart cable doesn't help much?
