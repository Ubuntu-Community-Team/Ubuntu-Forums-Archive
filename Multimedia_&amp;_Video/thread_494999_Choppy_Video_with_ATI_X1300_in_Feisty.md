### Post by Waappu on 2007-07-07
Hi

Remove these lines from xorg.conf```
Section "ServerFlags"
    Option        "AIGLX" "on"
EndSection
```
and change this line```
Option        "Composite" "true"
```to```
Option        "Composite" "Disable"
```because you are using "fglrx" driver that not support AIGLX and composite
