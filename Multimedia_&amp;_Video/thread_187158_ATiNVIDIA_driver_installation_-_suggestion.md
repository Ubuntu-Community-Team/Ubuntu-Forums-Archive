### Post by usernamed on 2006-06-02
A question for the developers or release coordinators,

Given that at present almost all performance graphics cards are powered by either ATi or nVidia gfx cards, why can users not have the option of the installing the binary drivers as part of the install process?

I appreciate that the user may have to accept an additional (and non-free) license agreement, but it's got to be better to give the user the option, than force everyone through the painful installation/configuration/troubleshooting process that we go through now.

As long as users were informed of the implications of installing the drivers, wouldn't it be a major step forwards for usability?
