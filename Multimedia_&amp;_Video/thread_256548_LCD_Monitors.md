### Post by _simon_ on 2006-09-13
There is no such thing as linux compatible. 

All monitors are plug n play, just choose one with a decent physical screen size, res and response time. i.e. 8ms and below.

You don't need to look at big brand monitor names. I have an LG monitor that I would happily recommend to anyone else. (Flatron L1917S - it's a 19" LCD, max res 1280x1024 @ 75Hz and 8ms response time)
