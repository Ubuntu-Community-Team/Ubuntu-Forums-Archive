### Post by kostkon on 2007-10-27
You can try *Amazon.com MP3* which has a sensibly big collection of songs from the major labels and they offer them without DRM.

For independent labels, you can try [*Magnatune*]("http://www.magnatune.com/") and [*Jamendo*]("http://www.jamendo.com/") which are also embedded in *Rhythmbox* if you enable the appropriate plugins. [*eMusic*]("http://www.emusic.com/") is also a good option.

I hope I helped you somehow with my recommendations :)
