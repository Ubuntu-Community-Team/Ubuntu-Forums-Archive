### Post by lexen on 2006-12-28
I am having the same problem as you and I still have not figured out how to fix it, but I did get one step farther.  For XawTV, if you don't see anything on the screen, right click anywhere on the screen and the options menu should come up.  Where it says "capture" change it from "overlay" to "grabdisplay."  I still couldn't record, but I could at least get a video feed.
