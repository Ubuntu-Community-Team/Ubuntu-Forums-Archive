### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-02-25
after you upgrade the RAM, i would go with Mythbuntu 7.10. it uses the XFCE desktop and is lightweight. i use it, and couldnt be happier with it. you probably wont need more than another 256 RAM. XFCE doesnt use much. if you dont have a tv tuner card yet, i suggest the Hauppauge PVR (150/250/350) line of cards, since they are natively supported in ubuntu and myth tv. (not much else is supported) and get the windows mce edition to have remote control support. good luck and have fun.
