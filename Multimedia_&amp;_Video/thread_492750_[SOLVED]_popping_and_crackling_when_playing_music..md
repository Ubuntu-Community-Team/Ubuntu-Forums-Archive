### Post by zheepeez on 2007-07-05
That would be the electrical signal of your computer reading from your hard drive. Happens to me too. My solution in the end was to use an external sound card (although i got one for different reasons). If you can, turn the volume on your computer to maximum and the volume on your speakers to minimum. That should minimise it.
