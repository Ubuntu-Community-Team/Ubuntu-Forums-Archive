I am sorry, but that is the way it is right now.  Until WS monitors are the norm for PCs, game developers won't devote many resources to WS gaming.  Same way with HDTV, there is more and more programming available since HDTVs are becoming more and more popular.

With gaming I believe it is a matter of just rendering more of the scene so I don't know why game developers are not including it in all their code.  I believe the new games have WS resolution support built in.

As far as monitors, I have seen 19" CRT monitors for well under $100 and 19" LCDs in the $170 price range.  If you go to any used computer store they are almost giving away CRT monitors.
