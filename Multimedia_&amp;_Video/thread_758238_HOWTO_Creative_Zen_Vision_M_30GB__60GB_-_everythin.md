### Post by oli888 on 2008-04-17
**Charge via USB**

If you plug in your ZVM via USB, it should charge.

**Transfer songs**

To transfer songs on and off the player, you need to install the "gnomad2" package. To install it, go to Applications > Accessories > Terminal and enter the following command and press [Enter]:

```
sudo apt-get install gnomad2
```

Then reboot, and go to Applications > Sound & Video > Gnomad 2. All tracks on the player should now be listed.

**Play songs on the player on your PC**

For this, you need Rhythmbox, which is already installed. Go to Applications > Sound & Video > Rhythmbox. Then go to Edit > Pluigins, and tick the "Portable Players - MTP". Your ZVM will now be listed in the "Devices" section. Click it, and all your tracks should be listed.
