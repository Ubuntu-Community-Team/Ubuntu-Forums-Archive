### Post by IanW on 2007-05-31
To use the "VIDEO_TS" folder ("AUDIO_TS" can be ignored, as it's always empty) created by Tovid, start k3b and:-

1: Click on the "Further actions" bar below the 4 main choices
2: Click on "New Video DVD Project"
3: In k3b's file selector, locate the folder created by Tovid and drag it onto the _bottom-left_ window (which already has a "VIDEO_TS" folder in it)
4: Click "Burn"
5: Have a coffee while you wait for k3b to spit out your shiny new DVD video.
