### Post by booljayj on 2007-07-25
Right click on the ruby icon and under select window manager check 'beryl'. If nothing happens, then beryl is getting a fatal error and shutting down to protect the rest of your computer from freezing, which means that you may not be able to run beryl without getting down and dirty with your video card and system settings. search for your card and the word beryl to find help on getting it to work. If checking beryl makes it run, congratulations!
