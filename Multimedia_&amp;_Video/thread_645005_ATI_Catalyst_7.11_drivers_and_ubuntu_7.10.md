### Post by acoustibop on 2007-12-19
Many of us have been using the 7.11 driver for nearly a month now, OmNiPoTeNcE.  You'll have to download the driver from the [AMD/ATI driver site]("http://ati.amd.com/support/driver.html") and install it.  Although some people report success installing the driver with ATI's built-in installer, it's probably best to use one of the howtos for installation.  The one I'd recommend is the [Unofficial Wiki for the ATI Linux Driver]("http://wiki.cchtml.com/index.php/Main_Page").

Yes, the driver should improve performance and also enable you to use the Compiz desktop effects.  The howto site I've linked explains how to get Compiz working, too.
