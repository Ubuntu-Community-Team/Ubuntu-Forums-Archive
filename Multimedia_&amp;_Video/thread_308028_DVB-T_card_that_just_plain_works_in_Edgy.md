### Post by IanW on 2006-11-27
If anyone (in the UK) is still looking for a DVB-T card for their home theatre build,
I can recommend the 30 pound Kworld PCI card currently in Maplin's Xmas sale [here.]("http://www.maplin.co.uk/Module.aspx?ModuleNo=48464&DOY=27m11&criteria=SALE")

As long as you disregard the tiny little antenna it comes with (which won't give a signal
unless you're directly under the transmitter), this card works out of the box on Edgy with the Kaffeine player. :D
