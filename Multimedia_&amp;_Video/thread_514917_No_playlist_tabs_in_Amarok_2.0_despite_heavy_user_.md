### Post by stokedfish on 2007-08-01
> The most requested feature by users of Amarok on bugs.kde.org has been quietly tagged as resolved/wontfix. This means that the upcoming QT4-based version of the audio player for Linux/Unix (and Windows/Mac) will not have (playlist) tabs, an UI concept successfully used by such popular apps like Firefox, Foobar, OpenOffice, Konqueror and Gaim/Kopete.
