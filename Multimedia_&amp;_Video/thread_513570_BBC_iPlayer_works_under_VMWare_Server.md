### Post by patslap on 2007-07-30
Just to let anyone who may be interested know that I'm running the BBC's iPlayer beta in VMWare Server running WinXP with no compatibility issues. All works fine.

You will need a copy of WinXP installed in VMWare server, Windows Media Player 10 or above, and  IE6+.

I know this won't help anyone who doesn't have a copy of WinXP, for but for those who do and want to use iPlayer without the hassle of dual-booting, at least it's a reasonably quick way of trying iPlayer until a Linux-friendly version gets released.
