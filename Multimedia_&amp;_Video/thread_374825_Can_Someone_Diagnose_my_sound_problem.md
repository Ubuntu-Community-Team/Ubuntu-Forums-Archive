### Post by Daveth on 2007-03-03
the sockets where you plug your speakers and mic etc into can be on a separate pci sound card, but are always present on the basic motherboard to provide feed out of the on-board sound chip. You may therefore be plugged into the motherboard. You will have had to open the case and insert a sound card into the pci slot on the motherboard, asumming you are the only owner of the pc. If you did not do this then you are using on-board sound i.e built into the motherboard.
