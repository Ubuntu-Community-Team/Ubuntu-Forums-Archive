### Post by timcredible on 2006-11-28
convert files to dvd format with ffmpeg (ffmpeg -i <input file> -target ntsc-dvd -o <output file>.mpg), then create dvd iso with dvdstyler.  can burn iso with dvdstyler or k3b or probably any dvd burner app.
