### Post by ronmarley1 on 2007-11-23
Is your microphone muted?  In a terminal, type ```
alsamixer
```
Use the arrow keys to move to Mic.  The m key on your keyboard will mute/unmute.  The up and down arrows will increase/decrease the input level.  There may also be a "Mic Boost" setting.
