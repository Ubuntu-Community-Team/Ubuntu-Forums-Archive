### Post by Kilz on 2008-01-10
No question asked is stupid. But you are going to find that Dapper packages will be getting scarce. Compiz was really quite experimental in Dapper, it even in Gutsy only a little reliable. Add to the fact that the next long term release is only 2.5-3 months away and you should see why the packages are not around as much as they once were.
Dapper was a great version, I personally used in until Feisty was 3 months old. But I installed some new hardware and thought it would be easier to update than get it working under Dapper. You might also want to consider that option. If not also consider putting off compiz and updating to Hardy Heron when it is released. It is going to be a long term support release like Dapper. Compiz should be an easy option if it isnt installed by default.
