### Post by josesanders on 2007-06-06
If you have an effects board that you play through, or a line out on your amp, you can just plug that right into the line-in on your sound card.  I have been able to get a pretty decent sound from it, using Audacity to record.  It's not as fancy as that usb thing, but it won't cost anything, and I wouldn't count on any obscure hardware working in Linux.
