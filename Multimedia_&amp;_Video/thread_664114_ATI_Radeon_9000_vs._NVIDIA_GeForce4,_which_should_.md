### Post by disturbed1 on 2008-01-10
nVidia. Better XV and XVMC support, better overlay support. These are needed for low resource video playback. Also easier and better TV out in Linux.

You can get an fx series card new for around $30. The advantage of using an fx series card vs. the geforce 4 series is the drivers. GeForce 4 will use the legacy driver which may be missing some bug fixes in the newer series driver.
