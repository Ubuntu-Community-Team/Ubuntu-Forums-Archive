### Post by reclusivemonkey on 2007-04-23
What sort of frequency are you looking for? You can get some fairly simple apps that will take a picture every minute from a web cam, or v4l device (camorama will do this), but if you are wanting something more like a frame every second, you may be better off with something like Avidemux and importing the video, then exporting it to jpegs.
