Each one of those has a specific purpose ;)

What is it you're looking to do? What type of capture card are you using to get your footage, this will narrow down the selection.

Cinelerra is an advanced NLE which is geared towards producing HDTV content. With professional level composting and color correction. If you don't know what or how to use a compostor, Cinelerra is clearly not something you'd need :)

Kino is a DV only NLE

Avidemux is the Virtual Dub of Linux

Devedee is a one click simple version of convertXtoDVD.
