### Post by KoRnholio on 2006-10-01
While I haven't used that particular product, I do have an M-Audio USB keyboard (midi controller), and it works fine in Dapper, no driver needed.  USB devices that are midi standards-compliant shouldn't need a driver, and from my experiences (and those of many others), M-Audio is pretty compliant.

You won't be able to use that software, though.  I'm not familiar with what the product does.  There's plenty of synthesizer programs in Linux that work with devices like that.  More information is at [http://www.ubuntustudio.com](http://www.ubuntustudio.com)

Also, Ubuntu Studio has a forum here, where your question is more likely to be answered: [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=128](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=128)
