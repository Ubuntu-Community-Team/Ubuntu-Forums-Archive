### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-09-14
Audacity has meter bars.  If the microphone input is too hot, then the red microphone bar will max out and you will get distortion.  There is a slider in Audacity to turn it down.  Also, you can turn the microphone gain down in the volume control.  Double-click the desktop speaker.  Edit-->Preferences and turn all the channels of your sound card on.
