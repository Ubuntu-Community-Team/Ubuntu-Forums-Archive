### Post by elephant007 on 2007-03-21
Your card is a 128MB ATI Radeon Mobility x1400.  The card itself has 128MB of memory on it and it uses 128MB of your system memory to complete the 256MB of memory.  So 128MB on the card and 128 from the system (HyperMemory).
There's nothing wrong with your card or how Ubuntu is reading the memory.
