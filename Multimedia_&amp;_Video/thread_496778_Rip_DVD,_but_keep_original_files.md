### Post by Scotty1982 on 2007-07-09
are you looking for a way to make dvd backups that have all the titles and menu's included?

if so, you can use k9copy ([http://k9copy.sourceforge.net/](http://k9copy.sourceforge.net/)). it'll make a ISO though, but you can probably extract it. 

k9copy works good, it'll reduce the ISO to fit on a single layer DVD. you can also go into settings and have it make the ISO smaller or larger if you're gonna use it for something else such as viewing the DVD on the PC or putting it on a mini dvd.

also, you can leave it as a ISO (don't extract it) and use Gmount-iso to mount it as a drive  to watch it on the pc.

::EDIT:: i just noticed that when k9copy is doin' it's stuff, it rips the dvd into a 'DVD' folder inside the folder you tell it to store the ISO in then it makes it into a ISO, so when it's finished you actually have both the folder with all the files the way they go on the DVD and a ISO for if you want to burn it.
