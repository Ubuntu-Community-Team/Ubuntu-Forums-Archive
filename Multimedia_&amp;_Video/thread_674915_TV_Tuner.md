### Post by randomnote1 on 2008-01-29
Upon further investigation I found this video which is an hour long demonstration and question/answer time about MythTV.  It is really informative and is conducted by Michael Still of Google.

[http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=-6641045817693171683&q=mythtv&total=100&start=0&num=10&so=0&type=search&plindex=0]("http://video.google.com/videoplay?docid=-6641045817693171683&q=mythtv&total=100&start=0&num=10&so=0&type=search&plindex=0")

The video is great for anybody looking to build a PVR or media pc.

As far as TV Tuners go, as long as it's not an ATI and not branded "for Windows Media Center", the card will probably work.  Hauppauge comes the most highly recommended with the WinTV cards.  Their website is pretty bad, but you can find their stuff on NewEgg.com with much better tech specs.

Oh, one other tidbit of information that I forgot about.  Make sure it codes video at the hardware level.  You'll end up wasting valuable cpu power if you get one that codes at the software level.
