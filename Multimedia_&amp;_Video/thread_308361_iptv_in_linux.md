### Post by thaibuntu on 2008-03-17
Hello,
        My wife is thai and she loves thaitv.tv. I have just recently updated to 7.10 and I have only found one way to do this thus far. If you install mplayer with the plugin for firefox, you should be able to watch the videos on Thaitv.tv. You may have to download the media wrap add-on as well. You can find that right on the thaitv.tv homepage on the right hand side towards the bottom just bellow the System Support banner. Just click that and install media wrap, then you can watch all the thai tv you want. 
        The only problem I ran into with that is you can't fastforward through the video as smoothly as you can in windows. In fact the only way you can is by hitting the fast forward button which will jump forward a few seconds of the cached video. So if you want to jump into the middle of a video, forget it. At lease I don't know how. I saw another user say that he was able to use xine to do this, but I tried it and can't get it to work. If anyone out there knows how to do this, please help us out.
