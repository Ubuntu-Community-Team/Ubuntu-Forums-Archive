### Post by kyphi on 2008-02-27
Unless your motherboard is fairly recent, i.e. 2004 and thereafter it would most likely be AGP for your graphics card.  AGP and PCIe have different slots.

The 8400 will be fine with either Feisty or Hardy and will also accommodate Beryl, etc.

The graphics card has 256 MB of RAM which is independent of the system RAM.

When adding RAM to your computer it is always best to double what you have - so, if you have a 512 MB RAM module and want to increase it add another 512 MB RAM module.
