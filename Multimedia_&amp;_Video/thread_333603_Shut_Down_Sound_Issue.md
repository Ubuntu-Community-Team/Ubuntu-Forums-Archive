### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-01-07
It sometimes depends on how you shut it down. If go to System > Quit, usually it will play the sound. However, if you have your shutdown mapped to your power button, it will not play the sound but still go through the rest of the shut down process.

Sometimes, however, the sound does not play on either. It could be because an application is open that is using the sound system 100% when you shut it down.
