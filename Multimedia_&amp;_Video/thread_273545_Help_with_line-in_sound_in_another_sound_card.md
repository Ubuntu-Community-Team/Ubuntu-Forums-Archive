### Post by acoustibop on 2008-01-16
Open the Volume control,and go to Edit -> Preferences.  You'll then get a long list of the inputs etc for the card: I've found that, with my Audigy, the line input that's active is actually Aux2.  Try different Line-in and Aux selections 'till you find the one the sound is actually on.
