### Post by chalkos on 2008-05-04
I had the same problem for months, but in my case, I connect the headphones to my laptop and I hear the buzzing, I disconect them and I don't hear any buzzing from the speakers.

It was driving me crazy, but then I tried the simpliest solution to that problem...

I opened my sound preferences, took a look at it and after 3 seconds i discovered where was the problem: I had the volume from the CD drive maximized (now I understand the symphony when i insert a disk..:lolflag:). I decreased it and tcharan! weird songs were gone.

It was so simple...
