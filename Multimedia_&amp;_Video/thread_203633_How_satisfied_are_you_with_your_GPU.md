### Post by evil_elman on 2006-06-25
I haven't seen a poll of this kind here on the forum and I'm very interested to see what everybody thinks of their GPU's with or without the XGL/Compiz set-up.

**It works like this:**

This poll is primarily created for Nvidia and ATi GPU's. Other GPU's from VIA, Intel, SiS goes into the 'Other' categories.

A 'Satisfied' option would 'roughly' include the following:
* You got the latest driver working with 3D / OpenGL.
* The performance of the above driver is satisfactory with regards of the age and intended performance of the GPU (Don't put 'Not satisfied' just because you've got old hardware, basically).

A 'Not Satisfied' option would 'roughly' include the following:
* You're having problems with getting the latest drivers to work
* The driver does not give good performance with regards to the intended performance of the GPU.

Of course, you're free to use the terms 'Satisfied' and 'Not satisfied' as you wish. If you strongly disagree with the above definitions please mention it with a post with your vote...

If you haven't got Nvidia or ATi hardware, please use the 'Other GPU' option. Since I can only add 10 different choices in a poll I had to make the 'Other' options non-specifc to whether or not you're using XGL/Compiz.

This should give us a rough idea of how good of a job Nvidia / ATi has made with their drivers and how compatible they are with Linux in general.

Example:
I'm personally using a Mobility Radeon X600 with XGL/Compiz. Everything is working fine with the latest updates and the latest ATi propriety driver. I would go with 'ATI with XGL/Compiz - Satisfied'...
