### Post by telic on 2007-11-12
When you install the MPlayer plugin, be sure to uninstall all other Mozilla plugins (e.g. Totem and VLC), because they can interfere.

I actually prefer to have streaming video pop open in my favorite media player, because then I can resize the picture as I please. To make this happen automagically, I use the Firefox **MediaPlayerConnectivity** add-on...

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/446](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/446)
