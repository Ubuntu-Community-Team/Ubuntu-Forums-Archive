### Post by scott_g on 2007-06-13
Hi,

Seems like I'm responding to a lot of your posts lately :).

What type of video are you trying to playback and what program are you using?  WMV's have always been slow for me in Totem; I find WMV playback is better in VLC.

If you want to install the proprietary drivers, things can get complex with ATI cards.  I don't have one myself so I can only try to point you in the right direction.  Under System>Adminsitration>Restricted Drivers Manager, it should show your card, and give a checkmark to use the restricted driver.  It will then downlaod and install the driver; a reboot is usually required.  Hopefully this works, if not, someone who has more experience with ATI cards will have to help.
