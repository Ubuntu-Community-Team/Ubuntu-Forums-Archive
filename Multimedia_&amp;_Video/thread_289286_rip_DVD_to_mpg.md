### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2006-10-30
If you have the disk space, install vobcopy (in the universe repository) and you can make a mirror copy of the disc to your harddrive.  It can also rip a title to a single vob file.  (VOB is the format used on DVDs)

### Post by x64Jimbo on 2006-10-30
I recommend XviD. It's available on all OSs, and it's open-source! 
To compress down to XviD, get dvd::rip. It's in the repos if you have all your extras enabled.
