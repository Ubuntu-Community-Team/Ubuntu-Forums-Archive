### Post by gfd_2 on 2007-12-18
I'm a noobie to Linux but in the Windows world I use an external video capture card... plug my VCR into that, then plug the ext. card into a USB port.   Press Record then Pause in the capture software then press Play on the VCR... then un-Pause the capture software and viola..... Once I rip the VHS tape to hard drive I use Nero 6.6  to make an MPEG file, add chapters then burn it to DVD.  

Now here's the tricky part... finding a capture program that will work with Linux.... can't help you there.
