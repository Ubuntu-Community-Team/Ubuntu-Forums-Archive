### Post by kj1h234lkj1234 on 2007-02-01
apt-get install libk3b2-mp3

Then restart K3b!

Good luck!

Edit: This thread reminded me, I needed to review how to do it myself... here's a guide I found: [http://www.overclock.net/faqs/127519-how-burn-mp3-cd-ubuntu-without.html](http://www.overclock.net/faqs/127519-how-burn-mp3-cd-ubuntu-without.html)
