### Post by elmagno on 2008-03-02
Logitech QuickCam Communicate STX-- I installed this yesterday on my Feisty machine. It worked immediately with Camerama and also passed the test on Skype with no configuration needed. I am not using the webcam's built in mic, because I have a Shure USB mic already on the machine. I have read that the mic in the cam may be problematic, but you search and see what you think. Anyway, in my particular situation, it was pure plug and play.

### Post by Fingolfin on 2008-03-03
I have installed Logitech's Quickcam Pro 5000 some time ago. 
After a real trouble with installing the right drivers, it just stopped working after about six months for no reason. 
A while ago, I came across a thread somewhere on the Net suggesting there is a non-fixable bug in the firmware of this particular model which exhibits under Linux OS! 
Whether this is true or not, after reading that my webcam went to a Win Vista home where it has worked perfectly ever since. I hope this is not universal problem with Quickcam models, in which case I'll also try the one recommended here.
