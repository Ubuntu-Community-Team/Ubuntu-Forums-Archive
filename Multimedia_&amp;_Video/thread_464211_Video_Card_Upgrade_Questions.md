### Post by esavato on 2007-06-04
I would look at what many of the major system builders, such as System 76 and Dell, support in their offerings.  I just bought a Dell and it has the Nvidia 7300 LE 256 MB which works great.  I was able to get the wobbly window effects and Beryl to work seamlessly, without crashing.  The Gimp also runs without issue using this card.  Stay away from ATI for the time being, because although they state they will have open sourced drivers, there is a big question as to the extent of hardware the drivers will support.
