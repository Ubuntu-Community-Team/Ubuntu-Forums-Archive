### Post by newlinux on 2008-03-03
here is a list of tuners known to work with Feisty:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MythTV_Feisty_hardware_list](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MythTV_Feisty_hardware_list)

Unfortunately this list doesn't tell you which signals they capture. I don't know about cband satellite, but I am fairly knowledgeable of  ATSC (OTA digital) and NTSC (analog) tuners.

Some that do both:

Kworld ATSC 110/115
Dvico fusion 5s
pchdtv 5500
Airstar HD 5000

for more info:

analog: [http://linuxtv.org/v4lwiki/index.php/Main_Page](http://linuxtv.org/v4lwiki/index.php/Main_Page)
digital (some do analog as well):  [http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/ATSC_Devices](http://www.linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/ATSC_Devices)
