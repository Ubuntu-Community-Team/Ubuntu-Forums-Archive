### Post by raffa on 2008-04-22
Yes, on screens less than 800 pixels in height (e.g. 1024x768 resolution) the Preferences window is not displayed completely. 
The easiest solution is to move the window upward: hold the Alt key and drag the central part of the window. Dragging the top bar won't work.

Have fun!

### Post by indiecast on 2008-06-26
make sure you have compiz settings manager installed (synaptic).

then go to system > prefrences > advanced desktop effects settings


scroll down to the bottom or click "uncategorized" in the left menu.

then click on move window

then uncheck the "constrain y" checkbox


now exit and you should be able to alt click and drag that box anywhere : )

i had that same problem and it fixed it for me.
