### Post by SteveJM on 2006-12-04
rip the cd tracks to mp3 files.  Then organise them as required and burn the files to dvd. You'll get far more than 4-5 cd's worth even at highest quality rip, on a dvd.

Beware though, not all DVD players/ home cinema can play mp3's from a dvd even though the support dvd-rw etc.  They only support video.  Check before wasting any time.

Edit:- might be worth just ripping them to mp3 then storing several cd's worth on one cd (mp3 being more compact than the original cd format.  Can't see any dvd player having problems with a cd full of mp3s.
