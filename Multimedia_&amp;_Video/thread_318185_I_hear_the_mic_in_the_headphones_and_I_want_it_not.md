### Post by siddartha on 2006-12-13
I'm using a set of heaphones with mic for net telephony. The problem is the mic is too loud in my headphones. Ok, it was nice to hear it work. But now I have no use to hear myself. Also with the mic booster from the driver my voice level is ok for the other party but is way too loud for me. Can I mute the mic only for my side?

---

### Post by jpeddicord on 2006-12-13
Open the volume control, and go to the Capture tab. Play around with the sliders until you find the one that controls the microphone. When you find it, click the left button under it to make a red X. It should now be muted in your headphones, but it will still work in conversations.
