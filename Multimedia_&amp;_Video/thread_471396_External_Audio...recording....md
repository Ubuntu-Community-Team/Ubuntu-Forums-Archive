### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-06-12
I use an M-Audio Delta 66 external/internal audio card on an old PII, whitebox.  Look for a pcmcia audio card that uses the Envy24 chipset and you will have decent quality (24-bit vs 16-bit CD quality, 192 or 96 kHz vs 44.1 kHz for CD).

I'm not familar with USB soundcards that work with Linux, but there are some budget ones available.
