### Post by wilberfan on 2006-12-31
Here's the latest:

I discovered that I had the 6.17-10-generic kernel *installed,* but I wasn't booting into it!  (It was the 2nd one in my GRUB menu, and the first one was booting by default.)

The Edgy generic kernel is the one with the dual-core support!!   My whole system was only running on half-power!

Now that everything is back to normal, Amarok runs just FINE under Gnome...  :cool:
