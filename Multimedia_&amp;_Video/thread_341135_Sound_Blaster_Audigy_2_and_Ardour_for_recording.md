### Post by deadgobby on 2007-01-18
I can run from 1/2 to RCA adapters from my stereo out puts from my mixer to stereo 1/4 input to the sound card. You can get XLR to go into a DI box. From the DI 1/2 box to 1/4 inch. Only I can find these adapters/cables at Radio Shack. I did the simple way and run the RCA output from the mixer and got a adapter from RCA to 1/4 stereo and plugged it in to SB. It depends on what kind of set up you have. Some mixers only have 1/2 or XLR from the out put. Or you can take the feed from head phone jack out put. There is many ways to do this. Just patch and feed. Just like working a old analog keyboard. You have many patch bays to hook up all the filters to make the beast sing like opera. 
Gobby
PS yep I need Jackd to run Ardour.
