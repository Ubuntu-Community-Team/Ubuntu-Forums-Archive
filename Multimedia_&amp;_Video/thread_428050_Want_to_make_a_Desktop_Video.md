### Post by charly4711 on 2007-05-02
Also, when using xvidcap with beryl note that XDamage is disabled by default. Try right-click on the filename->Preferences->General and manually force use of XDamage there. That may improve performance for you. Or use the beryl-vidcap plugin integrated in development builds of beryl.
