### Post by scottie4442 on 2005-12-01
I wanted to post this to help all those with dual monitor issues with breezy and the new ati driver, 8.19.10-1.  I played with xorg.conf until I figured out that if you put two screen sections, you will see that I commented my second one out, then you get dual head, but with one screen section, with the second monitor stuff underneath like I did, then it will give you the proper "Big Desktop" with the proper resolutions.  I found most of this through this forum, but in many different locations, just thought I would post this and consolodate it into one post. Hope this helps some folks on here.
