Is this a problem with the nvidia driver for edgy (it worked fine under dapper), mplayer, or my personal settings?

As a side note, when using the nvidia driver, adjusting the screen's gamma with xgamma affects any movies being played, which seems odd to me, as if there was no hardware acceleration. I'm afraid I don't know enough about this to suggest anything else.

### Post by raskolnik on 2006-10-29
I tried running "nvidia-xconfig --no-composite", and this solved my problem. Mplayer is rendering properly, and games are working. Everything's dandy. Thanks to tseliot for his response to another thread, inadvertently helping me as well.
