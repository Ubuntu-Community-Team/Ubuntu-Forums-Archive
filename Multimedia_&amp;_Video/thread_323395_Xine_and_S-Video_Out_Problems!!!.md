### Post by josesanders on 2006-12-22
Depending on which card you have, you might want to try GATOS:

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=215763&highlight=gatos](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=215763&highlight=gatos)

It's the only thing that worked for me, but I've tried it with two different cards, and only one worked.  Unfortunately, the way that you describe your TV display, with the black bars on the top and bottom of the screen, sounds a lot like my failed attempt.  What was really frustrating was that I could see everything during bootup, but then when the login screen came up, I got nothing but the black bars and a dark gray screen.  You could also try the fglrx thing, but it didn't work for my cards:

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=204910&highlight=ati+howto](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=204910&highlight=ati+howto)

Good luck.  You will certainly need it.
