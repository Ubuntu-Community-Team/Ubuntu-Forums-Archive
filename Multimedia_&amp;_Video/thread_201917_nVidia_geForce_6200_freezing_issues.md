### Post by sethmahoney on 2006-06-27
Apparently, for anyone interested, it is a bug that affects computers with the nVidia 6200 card and an ATI RS480/482 chipset when using nVidia's proprietary drivers.  A bug report has been filed on nVidia's end, but who knows how long it will take to be fixed.  I pulled out the nVidia card and am using the integrated ATI card and everything, including compiz, is working perfectly.
