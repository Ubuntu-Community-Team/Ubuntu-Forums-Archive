### Post by pjbgravely on 2006-06-18
Your xorg.conf looks fine to me, at least to get the cards working, you will have to adjust after that. 

The no signal error of the monitor means either you cable is not hooked up, or is bad, your video card is dead or not seated correctly or your monitor is bad. Which the second monitor to the other side. 

Try  sudo X :1 -scanpci to see if your second card it still working.
