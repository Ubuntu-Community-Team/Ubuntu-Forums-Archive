### Post by Brad_au on 2008-05-18
I had a similar jumppad error which I could get around by manually putting movies into different titlesets:

-Right click on the movie (where the name is in text, not the picture) and pick assign to titleset (give a number)

-I only have two or three 800mb titles per titleset.

Found this in the FAQs:
[http://qdvdauthor.sourceforge.net/faq.html](http://qdvdauthor.sourceforge.net/faq.html)

Good Luck!
