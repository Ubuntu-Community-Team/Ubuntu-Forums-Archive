### Post by jhollon on 2006-06-16
Problem Fixed:

On a previous thread I was unable to install packages through synaptic due to a cluster management problem, and this appears to be a further problem that I only noticed after I upgraded (from a non-functioning 3dfx card to this fx5200).  I used 
top -d 0.2
to look for processes coming up frequently and found one called ccsd that came up frequently.  I killed it and then re-ran glxgears and the stuttering was gone.  I also found a connection between ccsd and /etc/init.d/csc which apparently starts the unwanted process.  Killing ccsd, and i assume deleting ccs, will do the trick.

EDIT:  Before I do delete anything else from init.d, anyone know if it is safe to remove ccs?  or if there is a better way to make it not load up?
