### Post by Fatjoint on 2007-02-13
If you have an SVIDEO port on your video card and you have a SVIDEO in on your TV then you should be fine.

If after connecting to both it still doesn't work, I believe you'd need twinview to set up the SVIDEO out on your video card - main thing being your resolution is set low enough for your tv, and also directing the signal to that port.
