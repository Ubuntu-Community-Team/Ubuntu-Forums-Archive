### Post by kyphi on 2007-07-20
To put an icon shortcut on your top panel, just open Applications (at the very top left of your screen), go to Sound and Video.  Hold the left mouse button down over your choice of music player and drag to top panel.

Right click on your new icon to 'Lock into position' or remove from panel.

Most music players will accommodate your requirements.

Enjoy.
