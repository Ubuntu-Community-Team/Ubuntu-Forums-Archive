### Post by thepyronaut on 2008-03-03
Seems I spoke too soon, I (roughly) followed a guide on another thread. Thought I'd post it here for my fellow ubuntu users suffering with the same asus sound card.

1) Right click the speaker on panel, choose "Open Volume Control"
2) Click "Options" tab. If you don't see the options tab, edit preferences and enable "Digital input source", this will make it appear. (I selected "Input Source", all three of them)
3) Change the "Digital Input Source" to "Digital Mic 1" from "Analog Inputs". (I changed all three to "Mic" instead)
