### Post by The Pinny Parlour on 2006-09-10
From here: [http://www.compiz.net/topic-4206-latest-plugins-update-breaks-compiz](http://www.compiz.net/topic-4206-latest-plugins-update-breaks-compiz)

To correct the issue:

Open Terminal and code:

csm 
then hit enter

This will bring up Compiz Settings Manager 0.9.  Scroll down to where it says "Resize Window" and uncheck it.  DONE.

Start by opening Terminal and code: /usr/bin/compiz-start.  

Your off and running again.  At least I was.  :biggrin:
