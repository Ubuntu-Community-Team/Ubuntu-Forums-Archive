Hi.. I had exactly the same problem. I solved like this:
Lower the screen resolution. I had 1280x1024 and no recording could be played...the avi, the ogg closed every time I doubled clicked...and when I changed to 1024x768 my screen resolution BEFORE recording... voila! the recording with gtk-recordmyDesktop, and then played the file, it was working.
