### Post by netztier on 2007-05-11
> **Knowles said:**
> just so you know you wont be able to use

Xinerama

as thats pure nvidia

also BACK UP your original.

Plain wrong.

Xinerama is X11/X.org's native mode of having a desktop span multiple displays. Works with any Graphics adapters. See: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xinerama](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xinerama)

**TwinView** is what is nvidia specific - and it has some feature and Flags to emulate Xinerama-like behaviour, so that X.org/X11 apps can be Xinerama-aware.
