### Post by Drakkn on 2008-02-27
I have been trying everything to make this HP NC8230 go into suspend mode for a year now. I always use the ATI proprietary drivers and could never make it work due to a known bug in the driver. I installed the Catalyst 8.2 driver using Envy last night.... tried to suspend and almost fell over out of my seat. It worked! Then it came back up with no problems! The release notes for Catalyst 8.1 said it was fixed, but it didn't work, but 8.2 does. 
Note that if you installed Envy a few weeks ago you will need to get the new one as it will not grab the new 8.2 driver. 
Anyone that was in the same boat might want to give the new driver a try! =D>
