### Post by benjy on 2007-06-15
Really? That is strange as it worked perfectly for me in almost all regards. I'd still recommend Quod Libet, specifically the album view, changing it's settings to:

```
\<b\><artist></b\>
<title|\<i\><title>\</i\>|Songs not in an album><date| (<date>)>
\<small\><~discs|<~discs> - ><~tracks> - <~long-length>\</small\>
```

So that the artist is listed first, then the album name.

But you can set it up as you like - that's the beauty :D
