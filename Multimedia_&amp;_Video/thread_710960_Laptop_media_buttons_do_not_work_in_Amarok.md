### Post by JaggedOne on 2008-02-29
I have a dell XPS M1530 with touch sensitive media buttons on top of the keyboard. They work for controlling totem movie player. However I can not get amarok to use them.

I can bind Amarok's global shortcuts to the buttons. However when I press them nothing happens. If I bind Amarok's global settings to a combination of a normal key and the media key it works fine. For instance shift+"the pause/play button" works just fine. 

How do I get amarok to respond the media buttons by themselves?

---

### Post by zachalekos on 2008-02-29
in Amarok click on Tools>Script Manager.

Then Click on Get More Scripts>Gnome Multimedia Keys.

Select and Install.

Go back to Tools>Script Manager.

Start Gnome Multimedia key script.
