### Post by daengbo on 2008-01-20
If you use Rhythmbox, you can enable the MTP plugin then drag the songs from the MP3 section of Rhytmbox into the Music section of Rhythmbox. Your songs will be copied onto your drive into your default Music location (probably Music).
