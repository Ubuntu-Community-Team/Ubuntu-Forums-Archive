### Post by luke16 on 2007-02-25
The easiest way to enable digital out is to double click on your volume icon in the upper right, then  once the volume control comes up, go to edit>preferences, and select every option there so everything will be displayed, then you should have a 'Switches' tab that you can go to and select 'iec958' output, and anything else that needs to be selected.
