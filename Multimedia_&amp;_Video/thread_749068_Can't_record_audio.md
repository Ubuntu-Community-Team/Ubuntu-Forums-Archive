### Post by erginemr on 2008-04-08
Can you please check if there is any change in your /tmp folder from nautilus (file manager)?

I am asking this, because when Firefox shows any Flash video, say in Youtube, it starts to cache the video to the /tmp folder of your hard drive. So, when you finish playing the video (but not close the web page yet), you will have a complete copy of the video file (*.flv). Once you get the FLV file, there are several ways to view / convert them.

I am not sure but this may be the case for your audio file as well...
