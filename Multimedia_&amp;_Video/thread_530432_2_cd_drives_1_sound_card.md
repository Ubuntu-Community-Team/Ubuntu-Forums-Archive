### Post by wolfen69 on 2007-08-20
i will assume you dont have sata capability. if not, most motherboards only come with 2 IDE slots,(unless your board is raid capable, then you will have 4 IDE slots) you will have to run 1 cd player as a slave off of the hard drive and connect the other cd player to the other IDE slot. if you have a sata HD, you could hook up each CD player to its own IDE slot.
