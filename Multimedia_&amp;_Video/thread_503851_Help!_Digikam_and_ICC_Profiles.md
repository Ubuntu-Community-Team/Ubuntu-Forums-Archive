### Post by stadt on 2007-07-19
Some widely used ICC profiles can be installed using Synaptic /AP, search for icc-profiles.

Special profiles come normally with the device (scanner, printer, monitor, camera) 
If your device has none, or wasn't delivered with any you have to create your own using IT8 targets

and profiles for printing are available by e.g. paper-companies, by color initiatives ...

some examples

[http://www.eci.org/eci/de/060_downloads.php](http://www.eci.org/eci/de/060_downloads.php)

They have lot's general european ICC profiles and too lot's of media-houses special profiles

[http://photogamut.org/E_Index.html](http://photogamut.org/E_Index.html)

to give photography some standards 

Adobe has too some more widely used ones

But be aware there won't be any one sit's fits all. Correct Colors are rather complex and often needs calibrating.
