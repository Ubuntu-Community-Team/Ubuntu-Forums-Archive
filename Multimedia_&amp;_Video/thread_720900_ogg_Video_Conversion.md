### Post by ron999 on 2008-03-10
Hi
There's a program called WinFF.
It has options for WMV, MOV, AVI and others.
It's a front-end for ffmpeg so I expect that it will convert OGG video OK.
Available from here:- [http://biggmatt.com/]("http://biggmatt.com/")
