### Post by archivator on 2008-01-28
Personally, I use gtkpod to sync with the RetailOS on my iPod. Its interface is kinda.. unprofessional .. but it's not impossible to figure out. It gets quite messy if you have iTunes installed in Windows and the iPod tries to sync to it, though (read: missing music and all).

If only [Rockbox](http://www.rockbox.org) had better battery life on my iPod 5G, I'd stick to it, no RetailOS at all. You might want to have a look at it, though; it provides drag and drop functionality (really, copy and paste) and has a wide range of supported codecs. Oh, yeah, and you can run Doom on it :)
