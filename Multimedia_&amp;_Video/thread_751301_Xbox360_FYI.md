I recently bought the Nyko Xbox360 usb/multi card reader from a friend.
So I decided to plug it into my desktop. (I think I did it before start up not sure) but if I did I have my wireless mouse a logitech vm400?? something like that.(I'm not home right now) 

On start up it worked flawlessly as it should. Also I had to copy some pictures from my camera but didn't feel like trying to get drivers to work, so I just took out the card, put it in the reader and BAM! worked right away. 

So if you have it or want to buy it, they are cheap at the store. Marked down to 7 bucks or so. You get a cheap multi card reader and a 3 port usb hub and it works. And just plug and play!!
