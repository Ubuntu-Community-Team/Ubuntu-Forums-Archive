### Post by Baka no Kami on 2007-06-06
On many systems the onboard video has to be disabled for an add-on graphics card to work properly. I've seen it where the monitor is plugged into a PCIe card, but it's using the driver for the built-in video. I don't know if this is just how some systems are made, or if builting the graphics into the MB force the video to work that way.
