I have my mp3s on an NTFS partition that is automounted as my computer starts. But when I use MythTV with the music plugins so it can play music it can't seem to find it. Even if I choose scan for music. Where do I specify for it to find the music?

---

### Post by newlinux on 2007-07-08
Utilities/Setup->Setup->Media Settings->Music Settings->General settings

or something like that depending on the theme/layout you use.
