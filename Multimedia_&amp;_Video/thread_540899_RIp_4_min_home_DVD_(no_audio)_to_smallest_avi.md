### Post by Amazona aestiva on 2007-09-02
See the first link in my signature, and I think You should try Avidemux.

You should resize it to lower res.(approximately 400x300)
Use Xvid4 codec with 2 pass - final size.(I set 4mb, and set VHQ mode to 4(default is 1))
And mp3 for sound.(I used 80kbps)

I compressed 4 min to 4,7mb without sound.(6,7 with sound)
This compression won't give You nice quality.
