### Post by rplantz on 2007-09-01
> **zweiundzwei said:**
> Right now I'm using NVIDIA GeForce 6100 and since I keep on having problems with it, I started thinking about replacing it. 


What types of problems are you having?

I have an nvidia geforce fx5200 (128Mb) that had a very noisy fan. I could see that the fan would slow down when it got noisy. (The noise sounded like something was hitting the fan blades.) I decided that I did not have much to lose, so I took the fan off and dropped a few drops of oil onto the shaft. I used a high grade electric motor oil.

At first, I thought that my fix did not work. I kept using the computer, expecting the video to burn out at any time. After about an hour, the noise went away. I've been using the system several hours a day for a week now, and the noise has not come back.
