### Post by nicedude on 2008-04-18
Well your mobo's built in graphics is an NVIDIA C51PV (GeForce6150) Chipset - with that said problem with integrated graphics cards is allot of the time they use the system's ram memory instead of having their own ram. As far as what card will work with your motherboard, yours has a PCI Express x16 slot (if you look at your mobo it's the tiny slot next to your other longer PCI slots). Not to sound like a NVIDIA ad or something but I personally prefer them to ATI and have always got better results with them. So I would just buy the best NVIDIA PCI Express x16 card you can (or want) to afford. I always buy from newegg.com as they have user reviews for all the things they sell (which has helped me dodge some good looking but poorly designed products) & they have some of the best prices to boot.
