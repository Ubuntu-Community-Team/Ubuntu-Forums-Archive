### Post by hackerb9 on 2006-02-04
The solution is to record in stereo.

Edit -> Preferences -> Audio I/O -> Recording Channels -> 2 (Stereo)

I found the solution myself after poking around the [Audacity Wiki]("http://audacityteam.org/wiki/index.php?title=PlaYingTrackWhileRecording").
