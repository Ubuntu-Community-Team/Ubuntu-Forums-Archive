### Post by humanzoo on 2007-09-12
I had a similar problem with installing on the PS3 and my flat-panel TV. I had to edit /etc/kboot.conf and add: 

```
video=ps3fb:mode:4
```

to the end of the kernel line (the 'linux=' line). Setting that mode to 4 is good for displays that can do 1080i. Other options are 3 = 720p and 5 = 1080p (1 and 2 are options too, but they are low resolutions of some sort). 

In the end, my /etc/kboot.conf looks like this:

```

message=/etc/kboot.msg
default=linux
timeout=10
linux='/boot/vmlinux initrd=/boot/initrd.img root=UUID=f5296346-d33b-4354-96a9-111117b24b47 quiet splash video=ps3fb:mode:3'
old='/boot/vmlinux.old initrd=/boot/initrd.img.old root=UUID=f5296346-d33b-4354-96a9-111117b24b47 quiet splash video=ps3fb:mode:3'

```

Perhaps this will help?
