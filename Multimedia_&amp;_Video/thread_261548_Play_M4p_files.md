### Post by skymt on 2006-09-20
M4P files have iTunes copy protection on them. You can't play them on Linux. If you bought them with a version of iTunes prior to 6 and haven't played them in iTunes 6 or later, you can use [hymn](http://hymn-project.org/) to decrypt them. Otherwise, the workaround is to use iTunes to burn them to a CD, then rip the CD.
