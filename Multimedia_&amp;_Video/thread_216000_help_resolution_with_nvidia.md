### Post by sblanzio on 2006-07-15
Ok I made it.

I tried to login in gnome and I switched to 1280x1024@60. So I thought "I can do it!".

I logged in Kde (still 1024x768) and I tried again from system->display but again it didnt work! so, in synaptics, I installed "Resolution switcher". Then I tried to switch to 1280x1024 with this tool and it finally did it!

So I rebooted.

At the login screen now it was 1280x1024 but as I logged in kde it went down to 1024x768. So I tried again setting the settings->display to the right values (even if it still didn't work) and I clicked apply. then I switched with "resolution switcher" to the right resolution and i checked "default in this computer" and then rebooted.

So, now it works! it is 1280x1024@60Hz both in login screen and in kde (and gnome too!).
