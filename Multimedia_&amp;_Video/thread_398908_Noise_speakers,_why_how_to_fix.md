### Post by 351W on 2007-04-01
Speakers don't make noise (except maybe a soft hiss if they're powered) unless a current is applied to the voice coil. If your speakers have more than one connector, make sure they're both/all plugged in properly, and not touching metal on your computer case. Try different speakers (or headphones) on your Edgy system, and try your speakers on another system. This will tell you if it's your speakers or your system making the noise.
