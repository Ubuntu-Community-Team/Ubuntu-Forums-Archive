### Post by TekNullOG on 2007-09-25
I have been able to use the sound card. The earphones work great. I can even plug my 5.1 surround system to it. It is probably just stereo playing threw 5 speakers but it is still very nice. I didn't configure anything. It was more of a plug and play thing.

On the other hand, not all software use it by default. Firefox sends sound out on the laptop speakers rather than the 5.1. A little annoying, I must say.

My main challenge over the last couple of days has been to get the input to work on it so I can record or broadcast dj performances via IDJC (incredible piece of software for broadcasting via shoutcast and icecast - better than simplecast, oddcast and shoutcast under windows).

I hear creative is working on making their sound cards more compatible with linux. I hope they eventually fix this. Sound has been my main problem with Ubuntu and is the only reason why I still have a Windows partition.
