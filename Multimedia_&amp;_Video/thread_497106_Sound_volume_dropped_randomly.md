I'm using a Turtle Beach Montego DDL audio card. It has both analog and digital inputs and it works great. However, it just randomly dropped its output volume on both analog and digital. I checked alsamixer and it's maxed for the analog and master. The digital just has a mute toggle. I could comfortably listen with my speakers at -35dB before. Now I have to set them up to -15dB. I wouldn't really care except that you can start to hear noise in the signal as I have to put the volume up so high. Is there some setting that tells the card what volume to output?

Ok, I figured it out. My PCM was set low for some reason. Not sure how it happened in the first place, but it's good now.
