### Post by MattBD on 2008-03-22
I don't know if you've heard this before, but you may well have difficulties with syncing a 6th generation iPod because when they were released in September Apple made changes to them to prevent third party apps being used to sync them. This was overcome fairly quickly but I don't know if the changes made it into any of the applications available in Ubuntu Gutsy in time, as it would have been approaching feature freeze then. The plus side is that they should support it in Hardy.

You could have a go with Amarok - I know that there is a version that supports 6th gen iPods, I just don't know if it ever made it into Gutsy, as my iPod is 5th gen. Alternatively, why not give Floola a try? It's in Getdeb at [http://www.getdeb.net/app/Floola](http://www.getdeb.net/app/Floola) and as it's more recent than the players already available in Gutsy, you may have more luck with that. If that doesn't work, maybe Yamipod will do the trick - [http://www.yamipod.com/main/modules/home/](http://www.yamipod.com/main/modules/home/)
