### Post by kyphi on 2007-12-17
All of the programmes that you have installed should work and play your mp3s as well as ogg files.

I use Rhythmbox, XMMS, TiMidity (for midi files), and Totem.  They all work fine.

Does your LG player, when you plug it into an USB port, register on your desktop?

You could check if this is a permission issue by going to System, then Administration, then Users and Groups.

Left click on your name (in Users) and the Properties button will become active.
Select the screen called User privileges and tick all the boxes.  Then click on OK to close the display.
