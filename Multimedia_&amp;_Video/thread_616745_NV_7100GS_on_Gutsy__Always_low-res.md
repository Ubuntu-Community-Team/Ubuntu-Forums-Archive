When gdm starts, I get the "Low Res Mode" box.  I have enabled the proprietary driver, I have run "nvidia-xconfig", I have specified the driver from the GUI screen settings.  No matter what I do, the system boots into low-res.

If I try to "Test" the settings, the screen goes blank for a second, then returns to the desktop and the settings dialog goes away.  No confirm, no message.

The proprietary driver dialog says the drivers are enabled.
