Just wanted to pass along an easy load mp3 encoder/decoder and effects source that works great with kaudiocreator and probably K3B?

sudo apt-get install lame mpg321 flac libk3b2-mp3 kaudiocreator sox

Kaudiocreator isn't real clear that no encoder is loaded at install. The menus seem to imply otherwise.

Worked great with both fiesty and gutsy for me. Thanks
