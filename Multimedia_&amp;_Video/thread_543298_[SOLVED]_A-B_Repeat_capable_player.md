### Post by mrforest on 2008-02-06
This is a little late, but you can also use Audacity. [http://audacity.sourceforge.net/]("http://audacity.sourceforge.net/")

Load up your file and shift click play. Then select the area you want to loop by click / dragging across the timeline. An advantage of using something like Audacity is that you can zoom around your waveform. For me this is an easy way to pick the areas I want to loop.

Hope that helps.
