### Post by criss2002r1 on 2007-08-10
Mainboard: Intel D975X bad aX2   Using suckey onboard Sigma Tel Audio device....

Multimedia/System Sound is working now YEA!!

Right click speaker icon in desktop panel select Preferences,  device selections will open I had two options 1: HDA Intel (Alsa Mixer) it was the default  2: Sigma Tel STAC9274D (OSS Mixer) Select #2 Sigma Tel (OSS Mixer) it changed lower window selections to volume and Digital 1 removing other listed selections beginning with PCM....

Also I had to rt click speaker icon again select open volume control then File and Change Device select the device consistent with the one mentioned above..Once complete still in volume control select edit then preferences and check the appropriate items and hopefully your luck will be as good as mine.

Please Note: I am using a digital interface (Optical Cable) to connect my PC to a Onkyo receiver to generate my sound so a selection of analog instead of digital my be necessary if you are not using digital speakers or connecting to a external digital device....:)  Hope this helps
