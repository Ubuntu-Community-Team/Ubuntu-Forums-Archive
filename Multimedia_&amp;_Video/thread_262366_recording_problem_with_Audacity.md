I had the same problem and found the solution on the Audacity homepage forum. You can find out the actual explanation of the problem there (i can't explain/remember) But basically Audacity can't handle recording in mono when you have previous tracks playing simultaneously (which most people probaby will want to do...). Solution: 1. record in stereo (select this option in the edit menu/preferences) 2. convert these tracks to mono afterwards. Pretty simple :-D 
Hope that's helpful to anyone with that problem. check out the Audacity website forum for more details.
