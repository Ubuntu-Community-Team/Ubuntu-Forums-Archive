### Post by mcduck on 2006-12-20
I bet you are missing some font package, but I'm not sure which one as I've stopped using XMMS long time ago.

I recommend either Beep Media Player (think abot XMMS but more up-to-date, and using GTK2 for menus) or Audacious (Think about Beep but even more cool stuff and features).

Both of these look and work exactly like XMMS does, use same themes, and pretty much support same plugins. It's just that XMMS development stopped years ago and these are the followers..

At least Beep is in Ubuntu repositories, and searching the forum you would most likely find packages for Audacious too.
