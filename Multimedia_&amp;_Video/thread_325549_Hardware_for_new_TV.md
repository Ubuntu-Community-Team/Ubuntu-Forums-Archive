### Post by josesanders on 2006-12-26
What you need is a TV tuner card.  I strongly recommend the Hauppauge PVR150 TV tuner (around $70).  It has built in hardware MPEG encoding, so your machine doesn't use all of the resources for that.  It is well supported in Ubuntu, and there are a few HOWTO's.  Which version of Ubuntu are you using?

If all you want to do is watch TV, though, you might be able to get by with something less expensive.  There are a ton of cheap cards out there for around $30 that don't have the built-in hardware encoding, and you should be able to get them to work with a simple TV viewing program like TVTime, although I can't make any guarantees.
