### Post by bigbrutus on 2008-02-19
thanks for the reply.  I think i found a fix.   I went under Applications > System Tools > Configuration Editor and changed the /desktop/gnome/screen/default/0 to the proper rate of 60 and resolution of 1920x1200.

The "Configuration Editor" was not visible in the menu until i right clicked and chose to edit the menu.
