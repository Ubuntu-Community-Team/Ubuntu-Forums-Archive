### Post by Tarmael on 2008-03-13
You're narrowing your search there down a lot..

I wouldn't think there would be too many people who use Vista & Ubuntu.
And then you're asking for those who do use Vista & Ubuntu about their sound cards.

You'd be surprised how many people don't use sound cards and instead just use their onboard sound.

So here's a handy hint.


Go and find a sound card that is Ubuntu compatible, as the current market is revolving around the Windows world. Means that if you get a card capable of Linux, then more likely than not, it'll be usable in Vista.

Now, the biggest problem is this: You won't find many manufacturers that clearly display: GREAT FOR LINUX, or Linux compatible. So that is homework you'll have to do elsewhere.

Good luck,

Bas.
