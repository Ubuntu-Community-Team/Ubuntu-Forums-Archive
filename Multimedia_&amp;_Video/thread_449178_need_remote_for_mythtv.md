### Post by newlinux on 2007-05-20
If you like newegg, they have a $40 MCE remote and IR receiver that work with LIRC:

[http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16880100851](http://www.newegg.com/Product/Product.aspx?Item=N82E16880100851)

This is well supported

or you could go with:

[http://tekgems.com/Products/tg-pbr.htm](http://tekgems.com/Products/tg-pbr.htm)

Which I've heard others have used, but can't personally vouch for it. Many people use MCE remotes and IR receivers.

There are a lot of options for IR receivers and remotes. These are just two of them... I use an IR receiver and a universal remote emulating an MCE remote from my mythboxes. LIRC can use any linux compatible IR receiver and many different remotes.
