### Post by yishai on 2006-11-13
I am currently running Rhythmbox 0.9.6. I'm keen to stick with Rhythmbox as my primary audio player due to the simplicity and practicality of its design.

A number of my podcast feeds however, are not functioning correctly. Namely, while the program does retrieve the available feed list, upon trying to download the actual files, the downloads stall ("Waiting" status remains). Eventually, Rhythmbox returns that the downloads have failed ("Failed" appears in the status column). I'm baffled however, as this only applies to three of my current feeds, while the others function correctly. Moreover, these feeds at one time functioned correctly, and only now stall/fail.

Deleting the program and reinstalling the more current versions of Rhythmbox have not seen the problem disappear.

Have others experienced something similar? Does anyone know of a work-around?

---

### Post by yishai on 2006-11-21
It occurred to me that perhaps the reason the downloads were failing was that I didn't have the proper permissions to write the files to the specified download location - namely, to another of the partitions on my HD. I therefore changed the download location to my 'Home' folder, and they are now downloading correctly.

Why some subscriptions could download to the partition while others couldn't, I'm not certain...

Regardless, if others are experiencing problems with their podcast downloads, perhaps 'write permissions' is the source of the problem, as in my case.
