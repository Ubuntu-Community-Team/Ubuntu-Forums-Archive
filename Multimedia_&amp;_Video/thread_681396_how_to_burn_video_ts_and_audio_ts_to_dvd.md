### Post by ron999 on 2008-01-28
Hi
If it's a movie DVD then it probably won't fit on a regular DVD+/-R blank disc.
You need to use a program such as K9copy.
It lets you strip out the parts that you don't need eg subtitles, and then shrinks the rest to fit on a disc. It puts the shrunk version on your hard drive, then you remove the movie disc and insert the blank one and burn it with a program such as K3b.
