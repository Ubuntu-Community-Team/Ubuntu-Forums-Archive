### Post by dearephesus64 on 2007-03-16
Bump, if that's alright.

There's a separate volume control for the headphone jack already appearing in Kmix and the Gnome equivalent.  This is on my laptop and on another computer with separate headphone and speaker out plugs.  It seems logical that a crossfeed and eq could be applied only to one output relatively easily in Linux.  

(Compared to doing so in Windows of course- I'm no programmer so I don't know how hard it would be to write that code.)

I'm actually surprised that no one has made a crossfeed plugin for something already, such as Amarok or Jack, like nbv4 said.  You could always buy a headphone amp from headroom at [http://headphone.com]("http://headphone.com") - they have an excellent crossfeed built in.

They sound great-  I just got inspired to plug mine in now.  They claim their USB headphone amp works with linux, too.
