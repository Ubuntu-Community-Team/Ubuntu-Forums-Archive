### Post by PhilOSparta on 2007-11-11
My normal work flow is to connect my digital camera via USB port whereby gthumb is automatically activated and imports my digital images to my PC.  I scroll through the images with gthumb and hit the delete key on the images that I don't want.  After an image is deleted, gthumb moves on to the next one.

All that changed in the recent upgrade to gutsy.  When I hit the delete key on the keyboard, gthumb deletes the image and immediately aborts.  I believe this to be a bug in the gthumb program.  Has anyone else had this problem, and where can I list it as a bug?

UPDATE:  A bug was reported and fixed in version 2.10.7.  The current Ubuntu version is 2.10.6.
