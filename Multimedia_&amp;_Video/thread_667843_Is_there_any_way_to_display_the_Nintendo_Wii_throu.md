### Post by slamgauge on 2008-01-15
I got a nice little component to vga converter off of ebay.  It cost me about $100 but it was well worth it. Converts both ways so I can output composite from my 8600GT or hook up any component/composite video to VGA.  This works really well for me because I have my video card hooked up to the DVI input and my Wii hooked up to the VGA, all I have to do is hit the "source" button to switch between the two.  
[Here]("http://search.ebay.com/search/search.dll?sofocus=bs&sbrftog=1&dfsp=1&from=R10&_trksid=m37&satitle=hdtv+component+to+vga+-cable&sacat=-1%26catref%3DC6&sargn=-1%26saslc%3D2&sadis=200&fpos=78731&sabfmts=1&ftrt=1&ftrv=1&saprclo=&saprchi=&fsop=1%26fsoo%3D1&coaction=compare&copagenum=1&coentrypage=search&fgtp=") are a bunch of converter boxes like the one I got on ebay.  Try to find one that converts both ways though, you will thank yourself later.
