### Post by paradroid on 2007-06-21
After a new installation of my laptop I didn't have an appropriate resolution on the screen.

At the systems settings I can see, that I have an i810 video driver installed, but could not switch to a higher resolution.

I checked lspci and it revealed, that I have a 915 chipset.

After this I figured out, that there is a package called 915resolution. (In the top 10 lines of adept :p )

I installed it with apt-get. ;)

This installation makes a video bios hack while processing. :o

After a reboot of my system, I had the option to switch to a higher resolution.

Now I am happy not seeing fuzzy like fonts.
