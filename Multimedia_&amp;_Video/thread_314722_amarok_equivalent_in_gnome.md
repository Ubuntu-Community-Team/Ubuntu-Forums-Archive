### Post by tbroderick on 2006-12-08
How about Sonata? It's what I use. You'll need MPD installed and there is a deb for Sonata at the Sonata homepage. There are no default global shortcuts like Amarok but Sonata can be controlled through the GUI or terminal commands. You can assign the commands 'sonata next/prev/pause/stop/play/' to any shortcuts you want in GNOME.

[http://sonata.berlios.de/index.html](http://sonata.berlios.de/index.html)
