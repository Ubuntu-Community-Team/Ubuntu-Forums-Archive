### Post by smolko on 2007-01-15
Well I use 1.4.4, but it should be the same.
All you have to do is Settings > Configure Amarok... > last.fm > Retrive similar artists. With checking this box you'll get much more, e.g. in the context menu you'll be able to find Artists Related to the one playing and Suggested Songs.
To use the Dynamic Playlists > Suggested Songs, append a song to the playlist and then click on the Suggested Songs, Amarok will add similar songs to your playlist, to generate other songs click on the Repopulate button.
Maybe there's an easier way to do this, but this worked for me. If you want to use additional functionality of last.fm create a profile for free.
