### Post by Ecclesia on 2008-03-17
Songbird is great, just download it and give it a try.

I would steer clear from the ipod classic though, you will be able to get your mp3s on it, but people have issues with stuff like album art.  Also, people have issues with getting podcasts to show up in the podcast menu on the ipod.  It's not going to work right out of the box for you.

I'd hold off purchase until it becomes easy to do this stuff... (potentially in the upcoming Hardy) or buy a device that has a more open framework.
