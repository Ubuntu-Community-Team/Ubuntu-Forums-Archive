### Post by rklauco on 2008-03-06
Well, I strongly NOT recommend the converting - problem is, that there will be huge loss of quality, if you convert from e.g. DVD MPEG2 to DV and then back to DVD.
The resulting image will be terrible.

Kino is only suitable for editing video from DV camcorders. There is pretty good number of effects, lots of things to do with the video in very easy interface, but converting to and from DV is not a good idea.
