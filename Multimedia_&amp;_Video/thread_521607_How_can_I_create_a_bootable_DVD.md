### Post by ruibernardo on 2007-08-09
Hi King_Critter,

the more graphic (as in GUI) and easiest way I know to do it, is using "devede".

If you want to convert all kind of video files to MPEG (the DVD format), you can use "avidemux". 

After converting them to MPEG in Avidemux, DeVeDe as an option in the advanced tab to just add the MPEG files without converting them once again.

Anyway, the final result of DeVeDe should be an ISO file. Just right click it in Nautilus and burn it on a disc.
