### Post by janga on 2007-08-20
Easiest way is to drag it feom the menu to the desktop, but the shortcut may be hidden, so you have to right-click on the menu first and make the vol.control shortcut visible. You also can make a new shortcut to the panel and drag it to the desktop from there.
