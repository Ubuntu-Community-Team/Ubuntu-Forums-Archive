I have been using Ubuntu for about an year now and I must say that I love it. I have almost forgotten working on other OS's. I have been using Rhythmbox music player lately. While, it's a simple music player and manager, i noticed a bug in it. (not sure if its really a bug or feature).

Towards the left (bottom left above the CD art cover), there is a section called play queue. I enqueue all my favourite songs there and it plays all the songs in the sequence, i queue it. Everything is fine until here. As each song gets played, it gets taken off the queue list and the next song plays. But when all songs are played, the queue list gets empty and the song after the last song (from the main music library) played, starts playing. 

This is a bit annoying, since i would like the player to play only the song that i queued up and not to switch to playing songs from the main library after it is done with playing songs from the Play Queue section.

### Post by kostkon on 2008-02-16
The solution I found to this problem (or bug, if think to be one) is that after I put everything I want to play into the queue, then I select *All Artists* and *All Albums*. 

This way, I deselect any song that may have been selected (in most cases the last song put in the play queue) and this makes *Rhythmbox* to stop after playing all the songs in the queue.
