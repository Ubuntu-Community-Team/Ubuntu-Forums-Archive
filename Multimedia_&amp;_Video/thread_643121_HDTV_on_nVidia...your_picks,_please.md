### Post by MCMcButtah on 2007-12-17
I am pretty sure all those cards you listed will be just fine. I currently use an nvidia 7900GS for a 2560x1600 30" display and 1920x1200 24". HDTV looks great on both. Setup is pretty easy once you get the nvidia driver is installed. I choose vsync for the monitor I plan to use and then choose a de-interlace method in my video player software. 

Obviously though since I am using a monitor I have not needed to try and send it an interlaced signal.

If you go over to the mythtv forum they tend to have allot of recommendations for nvidia cards and discussions on what/why is the right choice. Allot of people there use dedicated hdtvs and the issues they have encountered with them. Come to think of it though i setup a friend with kubuntu on his 1366x768 hdtv and I didn't have to do anything special to make it work. His video card is a built in unichrome.
