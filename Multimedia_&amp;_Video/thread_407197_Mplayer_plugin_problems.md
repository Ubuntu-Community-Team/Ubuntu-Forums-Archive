Right-click on the mplayer-plugin to configure it. Select X11 as video output, alsa as sound output. Also unmark the "pause when hidden" checkbox. If you still have problems, grab the full codec pack for mplayer from one of the  [www.mplayerhq.hu](www.mplayerhq.hu) mirrors.
