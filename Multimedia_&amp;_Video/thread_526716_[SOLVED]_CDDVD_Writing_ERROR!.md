### Post by jmate24 on 2007-08-15
Most Errors are caused because the Writing Software and it not being able to recognize (possibly legitimate) certain manufacturers disks(i.e. Maxell etc.).
Because they may not have an identifier code on the disk which identifies the manufacturer of the disk.
The most common errors happen with CD-RW and DVD-RW Media.
They Burn very slowly and most of the time it leads to not being able to read the media or data errors/loss.
This is called the "Coaster Effect".

Please be calm there is nothing wrong with your drives or your media that you purchase it is a software problem. There is only a few manufacturers in the list that the software may recognize(i.e. Sony TDK).

For More Information about this error and to possibly fix the error please click:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=217472&highlight=gnomebaker+root](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=217472&highlight=gnomebaker+root)

If you do experience this error please return(WITHIN THE WARRANTY PERIOD) the media you just purchased and say "This media will not work with my writing software. Can I get it exchanged?" the store where you purchased it from may give you a replacement or a refund.

If you have more questions please feel free to use the online forums and commercial support from UBUNTU. They and others like you are happy to help. As am I.
