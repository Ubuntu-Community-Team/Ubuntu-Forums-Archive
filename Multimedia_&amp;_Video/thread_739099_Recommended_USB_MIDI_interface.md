### Post by FSHero on 2008-05-26
Okay, so since nobody replied, I shall type up the "epilogue" of my episode for future reference.

I got the Edirol UM1EX USB-MIDI interface from a shop in central London called Turnkey ([http://www.turnkey.co.uk](http://www.turnkey.co.uk)). I connected everything up... and everything worked out-of-the-box!

I was able to enter Rosegarden (MIDI sequencing program) and select the keyboard as my MIDI input and output devices.

Most interestingly... I discovered that if you set a track in Rosegarden to channel 1, the keys on my keyboard (Yamaha EZ 200) will light up to the notes of that track! I envisage this to be very useful as I am learning to play the keyboard, and this will enable me to see which buttons to press to any MIDI file I download from the internet. This was a most pleasant surprise.

By the way, I was not paid to say this, but I am merely giving advice for anyone else who may find it useful!!
