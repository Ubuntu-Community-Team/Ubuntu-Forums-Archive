### Post by Kobalt on 2007-03-24
You could start by adding the refresh rates corresponding to your monitor in the 'monitor' section : ```
gksudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf
```
Then find the 'monitor' section and modify it accordingly to your monitor specs : > Section "Monitor"
Identifier "Philips 170B"
Option "DPMS"
HorizSync	28-51
VertRefresh	43-60
EndSection
Then restart X with Ctrl+Alt+Backspace.
