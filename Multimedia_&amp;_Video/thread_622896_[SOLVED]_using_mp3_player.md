### Post by aidanr on 2007-11-25
If you search the forums for the make and model of the mp3 player you should find out whether the mp3 player works out of the box or if it needs some extra steps to get it working.

Also, if your friend wants to work with tracks they've already bought, whether it be through iTunes or someplace else, they may want to check to see if those have some form of copy protection or digital rights management with them. These tracks will not play on ubuntu and in future be sure to buy drm-free mp3's instead. (drm = bad)

If they don't have an mp3 player already and are looking for suggestions, I'd check out [Cowon]("http://www.cowonglobal.com/") players.
