### Post by staib on 2006-11-04
All is revealed...

Half way through a document I lost the entire screen and then started smelling an acrid smell - not normally good :-? 

Whipped off the side panel and wow was that ATI Radeon graphics card sizzling - and the fan under it was barely turning...  

As an emergency I propped up a room fan blasting cold air into the box - and everything is now back to life - guess I need a new fan for that card...

Thought I'd post this as a warning - don't ignore any strange sounds from any fan - it may not be the fan management system, but the bearings!

Nick
