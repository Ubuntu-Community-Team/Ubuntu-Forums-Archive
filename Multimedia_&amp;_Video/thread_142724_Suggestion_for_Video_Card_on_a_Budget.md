None. 64+mb memory video cards are for handling textures in games and even some of those are overkill really. For instance my laptop with an nvidia chip and 32mb vram handles everything in World of Warcraft just fine. I have pauses once in a while because agp texturing is slower than if my vram was larger to handle it. But my 2d, text etc... is no different with it than it is with my 128MB Radeon X300 ATI card on the big rig.
