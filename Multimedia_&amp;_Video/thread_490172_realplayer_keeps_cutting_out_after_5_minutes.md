### Post by sputnick on 2007-07-03
it turns out realplayer was ok. realplayer wasn't the problem. 

it turns out that some idiotic application called automatix installed an mplayer plugin for firefox, which supplanted realplayer. just like that! this mplayer, which took over from realplayer without nobody's permission, kept cutting out. 

uninstalling the mplayer plugin for firefox allowed the stream to be handled by realplayer again, and thank heavens everything is back to normal. 

sorry realplayer. i love you after all. go to hell mplayer!!! you're the lamest!!!
