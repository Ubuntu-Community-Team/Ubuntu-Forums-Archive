### Post by kostkon on 2007-07-29
Indeed, gtkpod has a confusing interface. In my case, I got confused with the repositories concept and I never gave some time to try to make the program to work as I like, i.e. with automatic syncing.

Thus, I decided to use [Floola]("http://www.floola.com/") which I believe is much better and easier to use. It is not in the repositories, like gtkpod, and you will have to download every new version, but I believe its quality out weights this small inconvenience.

Another very good program that does a great work at syncing is Amarok! Thus, if you use it, you will have two things in one; a very good media player that also syncs your music library with your iPod (and supports syncing of the album art of course).

I have to add that more or less all the media players in Ubuntu support syncing with the iPod, others work not so good, others very good, like, for example, Amarok.
