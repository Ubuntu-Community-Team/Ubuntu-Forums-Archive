what exactly do you want to do? Also what are the rest of the system spcs? *edit just saw 2nd post*

To find out what type of vid card you can get you either have to know the model of the motherboard, or open your case and have a look. I can almost be certain you can take a regular pci video card, Agp is a possibility, If your system came with 256mb ram you can be 99% certain you dont have pci-e.

If you just want a bit snappier of a system then the ram upgrade wouldn help some, as would a video card. You could probably get a 512mb ram stick and a vid card for under 80$ combined. Wouldn't be a top of the line card though, but if the rest of the system is not top of the line either it really doesnt matter
