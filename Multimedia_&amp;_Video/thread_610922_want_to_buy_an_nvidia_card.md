### Post by taurus on 2007-11-12
It depends on how much you are willing to pay and what do you plan to use it for.  If you do games, then you want the high end.  But if you just want to use your machine as normal use with compiz, then you don't have to pay an arm and a leg for a good nVidia card.  I have eVGA Geforce 5600 in my office and XFX Geforce 6800XT at home and they both run great.  Check out [http://www.newegg.com](http://www.newegg.com) for your nVidia card.  Great selections with great prices.
