### Post by namaste on 2007-01-18
You can use Audacity to create separate tracks.
I have used Audacity to record from the line-out of my cassette player to the line-in of my laptop. Make sure all the appropriate volume controls are opened. Make sure Audacity is set to [Line-in]. If you do not have line-in/out, use [Microphone].

1. Record your music into Audacity.
2. Open another Audacity session.
3. Cut and paste a 'track' from the first session into the second. Then save that 'track' into a directory called 'My Music'.
4. Repeat step 3 for all your 'tracks'.

You now have all your 'tracks' in one directory. You can now burn those tracks to a CD.
