### Post by bill45 on 2007-10-16
A few days ago my 7.04 Dell Intel 815 P3 indicated updates were available.  About 50MB later out of curiosity I tried unplugging my old dim CRT and re-trying my relatively new LCD.  By golly she works at 1280x1024 but still indicated 60Hz, the CRT setting.  Previously the LCD would revert to 800x600 default which is impossible to use with any efficiency.   I checked pref/screen res and found the menu now included 75hz as recommended by Monitor mfg (LG).  Thank you THANK YOU developers out there in OSS land!  Much obliged to now have full screen real estate, crisp graphics.  

Great stuff - an unexpected gift.  I can't wait until 7.10 arrives!
