### Post by spych102 on 2008-03-28
What do you want to do with the disc images once you have mounted them - use them in Wine or play DVDs etc?

If you just want to get inside the discs you can use gISOMount, if you want to make Wine see the images as discs rather than hard drives you can use gISOMount and then tell Wine to see it as a disc in the config program under the drive tab.
