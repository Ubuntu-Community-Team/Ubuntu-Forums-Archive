### Post by hilde on 2006-12-14
I had the same problem on my mother's computer. Thanks to this thread and some other thread about composite, I managed to solve it. The solution consists in adding the following lines at the end of /etc/X11/xorg.conf:
```
Section "Extensions"
    Option "Composite" "Disable"
EndSection
```
Of course you have to restart your X session afterwards.
