### Post by joe.turion64x2 on 2007-08-05
You may want to give avidemux a shot once you know the video parameters. Wait a minute, if you have a compatible video (I mean, one that plays in your device), open it with avidemux and there is an option within there to find out its characteristics (and hopefully avidemux will be able to reproduce).

An alternative option is to open Nautilus to see your file, right click on it, select Properties and go to the last Tab (I think it is the last), there should be useful info.

avidemux is in the repos.
