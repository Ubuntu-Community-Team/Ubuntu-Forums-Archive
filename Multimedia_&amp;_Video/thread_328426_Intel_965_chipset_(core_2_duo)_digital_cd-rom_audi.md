Hi all,

I've build a new mythtv computer with an Intel branded DG965WH motherboard, which uses the 965 chipset and Core 2 Duo processor.
The install itself has issues (well documented), but I've worked around those and now have Edgy running very well.

One issue... the analog audio connection from CD-ROM to motherboard is NOT present on 965 chipset boards. The MPC cable (the tiny one that connects cd-rom to motherboard) simply doesn't exist... instead, the IDE data cable carries digital audio.

So... these chipsets require digital audio to make playing cd's or watching dvd's work!

I have had no success so far. I can see the picture on a DVD, but no audio, as the new "digital audio" requirement is clearly not working.

Has anyone has success in overcoming this??
