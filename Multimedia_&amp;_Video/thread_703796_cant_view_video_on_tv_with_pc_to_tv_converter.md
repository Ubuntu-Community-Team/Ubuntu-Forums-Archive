### Post by Jose Catre-Vandis on 2008-02-21
Can't offer definitive answer, only my own experiences. Had similar happen when connecting my Shuttle PC to my big screen LCD following a fresh install of XP. Got the screen but no video. Found I had to have the TV turned on and set to the correct port (in my case HDMI) then start/reboot the PC.

Other things to try:

Toggle laptop screen modes

Check overlay settings in xorg for your second montior
