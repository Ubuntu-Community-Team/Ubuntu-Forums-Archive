### Post by zedlander on 2007-07-18
Here's the fix for Camorama:
Ctrl+E to view effects, right click and add filter -> Color Correction.
Found here: [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/camorama/+bug/78704](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/camorama/+bug/78704)
