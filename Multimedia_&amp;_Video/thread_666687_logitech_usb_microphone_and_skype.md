### Post by glendavee on 2008-01-29
Think I have got an answer, though it seems too simple, in view of number of threads relating to this problem.
Skype > Options> Sound Devices > Sound In >  set this to  AK 5370 (hw:default,0)
Be interested to hear if this works for anyone else.
