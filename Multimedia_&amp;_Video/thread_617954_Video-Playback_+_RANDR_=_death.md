If i play a video with the screen rotated upside down the video will not be played upside down but in normal orientation.

If i play a video with the screen rotated to the right or left X will turn blue, freeze and the Computer dies (till reboot). It also emmits a fuzzy noise which is quite comforting.

This happens both with totem and vlc.

Only workaround i found was using x11 instead of xv and whatever is default.
It works with x11 and strangely the Video still can be resized with it.

Ubuntu version is Gibbon, Latop is a Thinkpad X60T, intel945gm.
