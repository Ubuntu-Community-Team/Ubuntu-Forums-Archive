### Post by cgbier on 2007-05-22
you might run into a problem with the Codecs: They are proprietary, and their use is illegal in some countries. When you as a business man get caught installing them, you can close shop. 
The best way (and closest to being legal) is to give your customers a leaflet with step to step to step instruction how to do it. But put a disclaimer on it that is illegal and you don't take any reliabity...

The wireless: It is depending on the WiFi card's chipset. My wife's Asus (Intel chipset) ran perfectly out of the box. My own Compaq (Broadcom) worked after some tweaking, but the I still don't get the roaming to work, and have to choose my network manually.
