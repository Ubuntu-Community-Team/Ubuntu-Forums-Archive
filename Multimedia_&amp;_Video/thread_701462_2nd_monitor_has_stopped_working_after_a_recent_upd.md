When X starts, it displays at 640x480 (It previously supported 1680x1050) and mirrors the top-right portion of my screen, but in a corrupted fashion -  the bottom ~100 pixels of the area are at the top of the screen and the rest are below.

I've ran nvidia-settings, and it auto-detects the 2nd monitor as an unnamed 640x480 monitor (Both my monitors are identical).

Setup:
I'm running Gutsy on an amd64 system. I use two identical LCD screens, through an nvidia 7600GT. Both monitors are Chimei CMO CMC 22 W. I'm using the nvidia restricted driver and twinview.
