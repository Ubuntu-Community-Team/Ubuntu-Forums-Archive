### Post by ron999 on 2007-12-21

I think what toydoctorrs meant to say is:-
Right-click on the mp3 file then choose '[SIZE="3"]**properties**[/SIZE]', then choose the 'open with' tab. Then look for exaile on the list and select it. If it's not on the list use 'add' to find it.
