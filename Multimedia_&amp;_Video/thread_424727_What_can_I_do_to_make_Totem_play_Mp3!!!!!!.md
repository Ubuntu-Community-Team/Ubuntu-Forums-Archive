### Post by WiFi Ed on 2007-04-27
Are you using Feisty Fawn? If so, try to open an mp3 file with Movie Player. It should then prompt you to install the necessary codecs to play mp3's. After installing the codecs you can play mp3's with Rhythmbox or the Movie Player.
