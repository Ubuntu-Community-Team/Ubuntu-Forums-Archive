### Post by cotcot on 2008-01-10
I rendered a number of photo with the --> tools --> create mjpeg slideshow function of digikam (0.9).
The photos are a mixture of portrait and landscape. The rendered video shows all portrait photos in landscape (so 90° rotating compared to the preview in digikam (and any other app such as gimp gthumb, nautilus, .).
I do not find any setting to correct it.
Anybody else the same problem ?

---

### Post by cotcot on 2008-01-12
Problem is solved.
I found out that the photo needs to have the correct orientation in the exiv data.
If I turn photo with gthumb or digikam the preview is rotated without any change in the exiv data. So when I want to make an mpeg slideshow it makes no sense to rotate photos with digikam. (i had a mail  on the digjkam mailing list on this subject)
