### Post by jacktar on 2007-05-28
I don't know of any way of capturing DV by USB in Ubuntu. I did get it to work on Windows using the Sony software but the result was disappointing VCD quality. You would be better buying a 1394 firewire card, they are quite cheap, then you will be able to use Kino.  Strange thing about Kino though is it tells me my camera is not switched on unless the camera is on playback.
