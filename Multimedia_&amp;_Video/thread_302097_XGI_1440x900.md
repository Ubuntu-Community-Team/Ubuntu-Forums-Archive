### Post by AAM on 2006-11-18
Hi, **ddan**,
I am running a 1440x900 screen at present.
you have to add the configuration to the xorg.conf file.
type: *sudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf*

where ever you find a line like this:

**Modes		"1280x1024"**

add in **"1440x900"**, to make it look like this:

**Modes		"1440x900" "1280x1024"**

a restart should then enable you to choose the resolution under *System|Preferences|Screen Resolution*

Hope this helps.:)
