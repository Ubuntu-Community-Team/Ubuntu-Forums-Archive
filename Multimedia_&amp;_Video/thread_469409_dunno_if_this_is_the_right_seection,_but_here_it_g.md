### Post by reclusivemonkey on 2007-06-21
Right click on the volume control on your desktop (I'm presuming you are using gnome). Choose "Open Volume Control" then click on "Edit" and "Preferences". Tick "Rear Jack", "Centre Jack". Now close that window, and on the "Volume Control" box, on the "Options" tab, you should have "Centre/LFE Jack" and "Rear Jack". Just set these to "Mixer Output" and you should be able to hear sound from all your speakers.
