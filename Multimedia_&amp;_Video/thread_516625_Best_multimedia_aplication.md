### Post by tennisplaya05 on 2007-08-03
Audacity is like photoshop for audiofiles. 
XMMS is a very nice compact music player (apparently like winamp, but i don't know)
Kaffeine, which runs off of the xine engine is very very nice, and reliable.
if you want to play dvd's however, i'd suggest mplayer, i think the navigation menus are better in mplayer

and last but absolutely not least, 
AMAROK
hands down the best program i have ever used. in my life. period.
Its perfect, and getting better.
Its got a nice ipod interface (rare), dynamic playlists, great gui, and SO many features. if you havent tried it yet (unlikely). Do it now.
