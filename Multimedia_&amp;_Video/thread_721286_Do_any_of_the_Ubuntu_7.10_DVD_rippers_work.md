### Post by mc4man on 2008-03-11
I don't know about the other ones but k9copy can handle the majority of titles in any manor chosen. Out of the rest some can be processed by altering what and how you backup, some can't be done at all. None of the 'gui' apps are pure rippers, depending on what you choose to do they attempt to rip, blank, reauthor, fix structure problems, transcode, ect. 
On the other hand vobcopy is a pure ripper and can deal with almost all titles, the determining factor on structure protected disks generally is how many unreadable sectors need to be skipped. Even with a modification to the source once you hit around 5000 or more the time spent and wear to drive isn't worth it
