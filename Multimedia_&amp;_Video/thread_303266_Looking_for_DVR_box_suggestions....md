### Post by majoridiot on 2006-12-28
i run mythtv on an edgy backend with a hauppauge pvr-150 single-tuner card and a firewire
connection to a motorola 6212III dual-tuner digital cable STB.  the driver install and config
for the tuner card were quick and painless using mario's guide @ [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MythTV](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MythTV)

i am very satisfied with performance of the pvr-150 and mythtv.  if you are looking for a
dual-tuner version- PVR-350 or PVR-500 (although i've read a lot of complaints about the 
500). the 150 i picked up was $89 and came bundled with an MCE remote that is lirc 
compatible for linux use.

look into mythtv for your software- it's a top-notch PVR with WAY more abilities than
i care to list atm.

as far as TV output goes, all you need is a gfx card with composite or s-video out.  i
definitely recommend sticking with an nvidia, as they are reliable, (fairly) easy to
configure and can be had cheaply.
