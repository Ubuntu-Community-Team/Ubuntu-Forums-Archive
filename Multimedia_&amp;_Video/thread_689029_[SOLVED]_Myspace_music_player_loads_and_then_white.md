### Post by Saghaulor on 2008-02-21
Apparently the r115 version of flash 9 is extremely buggy. I uninstalled it via synaptic, and used Kilz;s script found in this thread which install r48, a bit older but more stable build.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=476924](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=476924)

Everything works beautifully now.

Also, fyi, I installed Swiftweasel, it's a bit faster than firefox. Flash works fine in swiftweasel also.
