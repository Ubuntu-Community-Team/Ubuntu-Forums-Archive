### Post by Erlander on 2007-04-23
I struggled to get my onboard sound going and finally put in an extra old sound card.  I was convinced the onboard sound had failed.

Much later I realised that in a previous pc I had front panel sound for this motherboard and that in the Ubuntu pc I didn't.  I hadn't put the jumpers back on the motherboard!!!

Just mentioning this in case there is another cause to your problem.
