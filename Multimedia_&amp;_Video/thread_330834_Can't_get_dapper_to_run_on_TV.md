### Post by Jose Catre-Vandis on 2007-01-04
Given that you can get it to work, sounds like you need to sort out your resolution for the TV. Most older TV's will only run up to 1024 x 768, and your nvidia card might only be able to handle a single resolution for both your monitor and TV, regardless of whether you are using either, or, or both. I found this to be the case with my media centre box, using some very basic twinview settings.

To help, please post your hardware details, and a copy of your xorg.conf, preferably one that at least gives output to both CRT and TV, we can then see whether tweaking the settings sorts this out.
