### Post by mojoman on 2007-09-08
Now, if you're referring to Totem (I think it's just labelled Movie Player) my personally experience with it is that it sucks more than any vacuum cleaner I've ever seen. I haven't used it since Dapper or Breezy but my guess is that you'd want another movie player anyway. So, you could try another player. I like vlc, it really plays anything (video, audio, streams, you name it) and also handles subtitles well. It got loads of configuration options as well (too many for some and I myself haven't worked them all out yet). However, for video my personal preference is xine (called xine-ui in the repos) and between it and vlc I can play just about anything. For sound I use xmms or audacious because I don't mind having different apps for video and audio. If you want 'one app to rule them all' mplayer might be an option. It's a good player and lots of people love it but, personally, I don't care that much for it. Can't you play mp3 in, say, xmms? With the right codec I think it should play mp3, wma, flac, you name it.
