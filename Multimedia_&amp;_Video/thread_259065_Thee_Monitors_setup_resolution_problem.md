### Post by aPello on 2006-09-21
I fixed my problem, I did not have the vertical and horizontal refresh rates high enough for my main monitor. (I had lowered them because they were to high for my second monitor and were causing it to mess up).

So jmorgan, you might try setting your refresh rates a little higher.
