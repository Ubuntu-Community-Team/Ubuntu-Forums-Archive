### Post by natalia on 2006-03-06
I just stumbled on your post today, I hope this might help you.

I have got a PixelView PlayTV Pro Ultra (not the USB one, mind you) working almost flawlessly here on my Ubuntu 5.10 box. However, I have not fixed one minor glitch: I can get no colour from it, everything is black and white! Anyway, my take is that it is not the card's fault (well, it is not, it works perfectly at Windows); it is, instead, due to the video system in Brazil (PAL-M, which is a mix of NTSC and PAL) -- one can envision the kind of troubles that may arise from that. I will hopefully find a painless solution; I have just not had the time to search for it yet.

So, that all depends on what type of analog signal (is that the correct term?) you are going to get through your card, I'd guess. The odds are you will have no problem at all.
