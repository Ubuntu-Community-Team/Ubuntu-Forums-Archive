### Post by human-ness on 2007-11-26
Actually, your sound settings have everything to do with your microphone, if your soundcard is not selected properly as the recording device.

What desktop are you using, gnome, kde or xfce?  If you are in gnome, you need to go into your preferences menu and click on sound.  Then you need to click on the pull down menu, beside input or recording and select a device.

Do this and then try recording again.  If it doesn't work, follow the steps above again and select another device until you get it working.

Once you finally have it working, be sure that you have your microphone set as the recording port.
