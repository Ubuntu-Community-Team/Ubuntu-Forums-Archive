### Post by claes on 2006-02-25
You could try and switch your driver to use the AGP 4x speed instead of the default 1x  speed. You just change this:

Section "Device"
Identifier "ATI Technologies, Inc. Radeon 7000 VE (RV100 QY)"
Driver "ati"
BusID "PCI:1:0:0"
Option "UseFBDev" "true"
Option "AGPMode" "4"   <--- Add this.
EndSection

### Post by gborzi on 2006-03-01
I have an ATI Radeon Mobility 7500 (i.e. for laptop). I improved the fps of glxgears from 460 to 1300+ by the following modifications in xorg.conf:
in  Section "Device"
1) Driver "radeon" instead of Driver "ati"
2) added
    Option "RenderAccel" "true"
    Option "AGPMode" "4"
    Option "AGPFastWrite" "on"
    Option "MonitorLayout" "LVDS" (this is for laptop screens, maybe you should not use it)
    Option "EnablePageFlip" "true"
in Section "Screen" I lowered DefaultDepth to 16 from 24.
Also I used driconf ([http://dri.freedesktop.org/wiki/DriConf](http://dri.freedesktop.org/wiki/DriConf)) to set "Use software TCl pipeline).
I have seen other tweakings, but I have also seen that these may cause some programs to misbehave.
