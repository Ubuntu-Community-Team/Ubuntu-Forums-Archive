### Post by melissawm on 2007-08-08
When you plug it in, do you tell your laptop to use the second screen (aka Alt+F6 or something like that...) Usually in your laptop keyboard there is a symbol next to the key that does this, and if you try it once it will give you an output on both the lcd and the projector, and twice will toggle output on the projector only.
