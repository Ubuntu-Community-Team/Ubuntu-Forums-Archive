### Post by autocrosser on 2006-07-16
It all depends if you like Nvidia or ATI--I think that Nvidia has the better drivers--Twinview & better open-source support. Need to know if you want a PCI, PCI Express or AGP. I'm running a Nvidia-chip card made by XFX--it's a Geforce 5200 AGP 8X 256 DDR memory card that I bought from TigerDirect for under $50 including shipping--very good card for not much cash--will run XGL really well with my Pent Dual 2.66 (granted, I have 3G of ram & a Asus P5P800 motherboard)--in any case, you can pay much more & not get more......
