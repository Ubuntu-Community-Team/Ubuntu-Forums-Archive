### Post by slugkilla on 2007-05-07
Hi, my band and I want to record demos of a few of our songs. We had planned  to do this on my computer so then we can burn cds to show our friends. We have cables, mics and mixers; what it all boils down too is the output of the (makeshift) PA system. Now I know that most soundcards have terrible quality through the mic-in and line-in ports. My question is: What is a cheap soundcard that can record one mono channel without any added feedback and noise? I also have an absolute budget of $80. Thanks for any advice.

---

### Post by EvergreyNY on 2007-05-07
you can get an SB Live! or Audigy SE (both 24-bit) for around $30-$40 (in the US) and if you're running everything properly through the mixer, you should have no problems with the quality. if your mixer has stereo outs (assuming it does) just see if you can find a dual 1/4" to stereo 1/8" plug and go right into the line in.
