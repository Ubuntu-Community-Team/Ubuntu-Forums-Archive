### Post by Belyel on 2007-03-21
AFAIK even dual-layer DVD's cannot hold that much video in mpeg-2 format (the format needed by your DVD player).  You could probably compress it using the divx codec or other *.avi methods to be small enough to fit on a single or dual layer dvd (4.5 or 9 GB respectively), but that will only play in your DVD player if it has divx, .avi, or other video playback capability.  Compressing that much video would result in quality loss, too.  Your best bet would be to split the movie into two pieces and burn them on seperate DVDs.  That would eliminate the quality loss problem.
