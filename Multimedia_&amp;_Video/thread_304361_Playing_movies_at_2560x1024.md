### Post by x64Jimbo on 2006-11-21
The problem lies in the fact that your computer is still considering your monitors to be two separate screens. In order to have something maximize to full screen across both, you'll have to make your computer think it's only one monitor. Look into your twinview configuration to see if this is possible. I advise against it, as it will make all other programs on your system maximize over both screens, and you don't want the plastic edges of your monitors splitting your web browser or MP3 player in halves.
