### Post by KCPokes on 2008-04-02
The only drawback to XMBC is that it can only run on a modded XBox, whether it be with a modchip or a softmod.  There are a ton of guides around the internet on how to mod your own Xbox, using the softmod method.  I hate having to go through the process just to have a decent media player, but it honestly is the best one I've found.  

In regards to purchasing an Xbox, I'd look around on craigslist.  Since the original Xbox is "outdated" in regards to a game console, you can typically pick them up for relatively cheap.  I never store movies, tv shows, etc... on my Xbox, I just pull them, over the network while watching.  Means that your files can stay on your server all the time.
