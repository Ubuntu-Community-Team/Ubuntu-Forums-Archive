### Post by crispy_420 on 2007-06-02
Why do you have the CD/DVD drive connected to sound card? That data is usually sent via the EIDE/SATA connection to mobo. I'm assuming an internal/PC CD/DVD drive. The TV card should be connected to line in on sound card. And if possible avoid using two sound cards, it will add too many issues.

If you have an external CD/DVD, get a componet switch from you local electronics store. 

Would it be possible to us mic-in and line-in?
