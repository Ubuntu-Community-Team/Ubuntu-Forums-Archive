### Post by tmryan on 2007-11-19
I think Eye of Gnome took a step backwards in the version included with Gutsy (v. 2.20.0) compared with Feisty (v. 2.18.1). In the earlier version, clicking on the left mouse button changes the cursor into a hand tool so you can move the image around without having to use the scroll bars. In version 2.20.0, this function has moved to the middle mouse button (clicking down on the scroll wheel on most mices); the left mouse button doesn't do a thing now!

What were the programmers thinking? 90% of the mice out there are two-buttons with a scroll wheel. I mean, why take a function out of the left button and not replace it with something else? At least let the user configure the buttons themselves!

Arrghhhh!
