### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-10-12
The last time I put linux on my iPod mini was in the spring.  Ogg playback was roughly 90% speed and sometimes glitchy.  Music playback was OK.  Development is proceeding slowly.  Its OK to play with for a few days, but Rockbox will probably give you better functionality.

Inability to add new music to Rockbox may have been a permissions problem or an fsck problem.  If your iPod has any errors on it, it will only mount as read-only.  You need to run fsck to clean up the file system before you will be able to write to it with gtkpod or other programs.
