I just installed my new PVR-150 capture card and installed Mythtv this evening. The channel scan only goes up to 13. When I select watch TV, it works great but will not pick up anything above channel 13.

### Post by ksujason on 2008-01-16
Well I figured it out. When I set up my MythTv backend end for the station scans, I had it set to scan us-brdcast instead of us-cable. I was only scanning the first 13 channels because of this.

Its working great now.
