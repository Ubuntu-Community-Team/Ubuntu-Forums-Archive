### Post by bernt on 2007-02-16
So you want the LCD panel directly connected to the server? Not to a client computer?
In that case you need some kind of distributed amplifier with amplifiers in each zone. Sounds complicated and expensive. Sonos?

With Slimserver you need a computer or a Squeezebox connected to a amplifier in each zone. 
If you use a computer as a musicdevice install for example softsqueeze and run it in headless mode. Connect the LCD panel to it and control the player with Slimserver webgui or with Moose.

Take also a look at [http://www.plutohome.com](http://www.plutohome.com)
