### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-07-13
Go to the DVD icon on the desktop and choose browse.  Look through the folders for the video files.  Many times these files are broken into video chapters.  Pick the one closest to the spot that you are interested it.

Open in Kino and see if you can extract the audio snippet.
