### Post by wrtrdood on 2006-01-18
I own a Plextor PX716UF which has worked just fine as USB or FireWire.  I can even boot from it with no problems.  Granted, this is one of the more expensive devices but for me, it was worth it.  I also own an older external USB Plextor CDRW drive which works great but my system will not boot from it.

When I had automounting enabled, it seems I remember the drive being easily recognized and providing a desktop icon and window.  I've had no problems at all using USB devices under Ubuntu.  In fact, most of them work much better than on my wife's XP laptop.  ;)
