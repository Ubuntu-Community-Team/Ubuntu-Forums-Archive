### Post by blackvd on 2008-02-10
I had a bad experience with Cowon iAudio X5 the battery went out and I was unable to replace it. However, I strongly suggest any iRiver H300 you can find. I have the H320 which is a 20 gig player that supports ogg and video playback and has a FM tuner. It also has a built in mic and a line out and line in port! That means you can record directly to it, say to record your records! It shows up as an external HDD so it works on any O/S I've tried it with. You can also install rockbox on it which allows you to play games on it and display album art. The case is super sturdy and I've dropped it a million times. The only problem I did encounter is the battery on some of the H320s were defective, mine included, so I had to buy an iPod 3rd generation battery to replace it. Other than that its a solid player and you should be able to find a cheap one as it's an old model.

Good luck!
