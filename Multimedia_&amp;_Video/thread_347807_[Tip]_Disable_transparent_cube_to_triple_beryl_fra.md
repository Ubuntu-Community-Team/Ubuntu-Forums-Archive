### Post by Epskampie on 2007-01-27
Basically just to inform you all of a thing I was messing about for over three hours.
After I had installed and configured beryl, it was much slower than expected. This was especially noticeable during scrolls and window moves.

It turned out it was caused by **turning desktop cube transparency on all the time**.
I thought it looked neat, but as I figured out afterwards, since beryl is always rendering all your desktops (even if you are using for example firefox full screen), it will basically divide your framerate by the number of desktops you have.

Hope this helps some people, since I couldn't find a proper thread about it myself.
