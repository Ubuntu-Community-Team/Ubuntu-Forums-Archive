### Post by airtonix on 2007-03-18
VLC does this.

Not sure if it will save them for it seems like its designed for use with transcoding or choosing a timeframe to export to another video file.

To get to it : 
[LIST=1]
[*]goto **Preferences => Bookmarks**
[*]click the "add" button to add the current timestamp as a bookmark to jumpto.[/LIST]Unfortunatly, yes i have found that this list of marks will disappear when you quit and reload VLC.
