### Post by vahnx on 2008-03-12
Yes, Compiz does work on the 1501. [Here]("http://www.ubuntu1501.com/") is a site dedicated to running Ubuntu on the 1501. When you do a fresh ubuntu install, it will use the default display driver. You can then download Envy from Synaptics, and install the latest ATI driver. But be warned, Suspend and Hibernate will then stop working and your video playback will look rougher. The only upside to the ATI driver is Compiz will work.
