### Post by alaaji on 2007-10-30
I found the solution!  Until Sansa updates their firmware, you need to tag your music using ID3v2.3.  When you upgraded to Gutsy, easytag was updated to use v2.4.  

Change it and retag all your music and you shoud be in business.  :guitar:
