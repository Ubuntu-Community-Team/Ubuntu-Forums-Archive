### Post by sammydee on 2007-07-23
hey guys

I have been having problems with torn/choppy XV output on an HP dv6330ea laptop and a nVidia 7400.

It's very frustrating because at one point it was working perfectly and I left the config files alone. However, after a restart it was back to being choppy again and I have not been able to fix it.

The problem I think is that the graphics card is updating the buffer halfway through a screen refresh, leading to two frames being displayed at the same time. It could be easily fixed by enabling vsync and updating the buffer only when a new frame is ready. I tried enabling this in nvidia-settings and the first time it worked just great.

However, after a restart the option is still enabled and there is no more vsync. I have tried enabling/disabling the option in nvidia-settings to no avail.

I can enable vsync in beryl and i works just fine, some slowdowns when dragging the cube with video displayed but it mostly works fine.

Any ideas on how to get back to non-choppy output? I can post results of commands or whatever. This is really annoying me, I had it working but now it is not!
