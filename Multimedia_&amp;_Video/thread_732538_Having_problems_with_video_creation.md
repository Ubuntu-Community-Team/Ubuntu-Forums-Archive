### Post by chewearn on 2008-03-24
Avidemux is only suitable for straight video decoding/encoding, and minor splicing/editing.

For major video editing, you should look into more sophisticate programs, e.g. Kdenlive, or Cinerella.

Beware, the learning curve for video editing/creation is quite steep.  (even with commercial platform e.g. Mac, or Windows with Adobe Premier Pro).
