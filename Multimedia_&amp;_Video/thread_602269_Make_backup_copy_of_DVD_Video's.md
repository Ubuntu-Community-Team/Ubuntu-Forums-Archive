### Post by sdowney717 on 2007-12-31
i just made a backup of a movie.
used k9copy to rip  an iso
then used k3b to burn the iso.

I select all the dvd titles and everything in the left panel and make sure to check box the keep original menus intact.  This makes an exact duplicate of the original.shrunk down to 4400mb. And the picture quality is good. It played in an old emmerson dvd player. I need to try it on some more dvd players to know for sure my setup is working.

libdvdread3 and libdvdcss also needs to be installed.
