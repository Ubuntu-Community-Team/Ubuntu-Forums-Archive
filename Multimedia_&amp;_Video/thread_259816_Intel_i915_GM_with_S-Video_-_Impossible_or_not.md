Here is a quote:

> Marking as fixed since I also can confirm that the RandR 1.2 included in Gutsy allows the intel video driver to output in color through S-video. Simply do:

xrandr --output TV --set TV_FORMAT PAL;
xrandr --output TV --auto;

(Use xrandr -q --verbose to list what TV_FORMATS are available)

That's one down in the number of issues that historically has forced so many of us to boot into Windows occasionally!
