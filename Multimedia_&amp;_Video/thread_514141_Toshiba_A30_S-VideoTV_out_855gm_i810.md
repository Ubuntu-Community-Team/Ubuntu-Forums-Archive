### Post by abacus1980 on 2007-07-31
after some experimenting and reading the forums here i have got  a working xorg.conf file that allows me to use the TV out (S-Video) on my Toshiba A30 laptop with an intel 855gm card.

the only problem with this is you have to reboot the system after changing the xorg.conf file for TVandLCD or just LCD.  it appears some settings get stuck and when you go from TVand LCD to LCD the output is only on the TV.

I am running Feisty  and can play movies with no issues in full screen at 800x600.  there is a black border around the image on the tv but it is fairly small.  800x600 is the highest resolution i could get the tv out working with.  640x480 works as well but the black border is just larger.

Also when using TV out your lcd display with drop down to 800x600 res as well.  kinda annoying, but hey tv out works.

if someone could let me know a better way of switching between profiles or how to change the conf file to "unstick" the settings so i could just use ctrl+alt+bksp after editing my xorg.conf file it would be appreciated.
