### Post by el Arm on 2008-07-22
> **Lightmaster said:**
> The only problem is, they have a nasty blue tint :(

Start Totem Movie Player and go to Edit->Preferences then select the Display tab.  In the Colour Balance section change the Hue slider all the way to the left or right rather than in the middle and your movies should appear normal.

I had this problem a month or so ago - the post I read with the solution suggested it was a recent bug (or rather a deliberate change) in the Nvidia restricted drivers.  It appeared for me after I installed various media players in an attempt to get my DVD player working, although that may have coincided with a driver package update.
