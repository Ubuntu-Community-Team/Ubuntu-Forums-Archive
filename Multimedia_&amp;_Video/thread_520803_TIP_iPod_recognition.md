### Post by fsager on 2007-08-08
Hi everyone,this is only my second post and I'm new to ubuntu (I used gentoo for years, but it looks like that distro has no direction as to where to go), but I have a problem with my iPod being seen as an iPod consistently.

You see, I love my ipod and I love the fact that banshee can sync my iPod's music with it's library much in the way iTunes does, but with two way sync. However, getting my ipod to be recognized as an ipod was frustrating as it would only do so after a reboot. Any other time I plugged my iPod in to my firewire port, it came up as a removable hard drive, and banshee didn't like that.

Well after days of experimenting and googling and banging my head on the desk, I found that after I went to Gnome Menu --> System --> Preferences --> Removable Drives and Media, and unchecked "Browse removable media when inserted", my iPod now comes up as a media player device and Banshee can see it.

So now I can sync away and enjoy my music, and come back to my computer and plug it in and sync the latest tracks, without a reboot.

I hope if anyone else experienced this, that this little tip helps.
