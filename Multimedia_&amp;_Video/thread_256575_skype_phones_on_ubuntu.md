### Post by AgenT on 2006-12-10
Or you could just buy a special cord where one end plugs into ANY regular POTs phone (normal telephone line phone) and the other end plugs into your sound card on the computer. It works just like a microphone headset, except you plug your phone into your computer. If you have a wireless phone then just hook the phone base using the special cord to the computer and talk through the wireless phone headset.

The big advantage being that it is much less expensive. All you need to buy is a cord and use an existing phone. Even if you have to buy a new phone, a regular wireless phone is much less expansive then a special Skype phone. Another advantage is in how this works: you just need a sound card, a normal telephone and the cord. The special cord plugs into the telephone in the same place that the normal telephone cord plugs into. Any normal telephone will work which means there is no worry about compatibility.

Unfortunately I do not know what the cord is called, so do some research. And being new technology, it probably has more than one name.

Also note that Skype is no-freedom software and the same goes for the Skype phones. It is doubtful anyone has bothered to reverse engineer them.
