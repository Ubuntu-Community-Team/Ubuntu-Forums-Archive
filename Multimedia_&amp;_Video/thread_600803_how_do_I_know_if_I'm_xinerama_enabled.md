### Post by teknorah on 2007-11-03
Xinerama is an built-in option for dual displays (configured in xorg.conf).

You may find you like TwinView better.  The Ubuntu page you referenced is just stating that if you use dual monitors and use the built-in dual monitor extension, called Xinerama, Ubuntu has a resolution manager for it.  Only recently, within the last 2-3 years, has there been much work/interest in having more than one monitor or running 3d apps (games) on linux.  Ubuntu has made great strides in providing built in tools for this increasing demand.

[Wikipedia Definition]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xinerama")

NOTE: There are a ton of posts on xinerama, ATI/Nvidia Video Drivers, Dual-Screen, etc.  Do a search, you will find lots of info! :)
