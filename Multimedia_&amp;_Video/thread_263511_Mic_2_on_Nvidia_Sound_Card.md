### Post by wvernon on 2006-09-23
It's always the case - I spend several days trying to find the answer to my problem, and then stumble across it a minute after posting.

Here's what I did:

Double-clicked my volume control to bring up the alsa mixer screen.

Next I clicked on edit - preferences and ticked everything in sight.

Finally, I clicked on the options tab and selected mic 2.

Now I should be able to use Skype.....
