### Post by MrHippocampus on 2007-07-31
Interesting, the edid information says "dtiming: 1600x1200@78" but the same edid information also says "monitorrange: 30-83, 56-76", if you need 78Hz to run 1600x1200 your going to have to change your max vsync from 76 to 78, e.g:

```

Section "Monitor"
Identifier "DELL 2007FP"
Option "DPMS"
HorizSync 30-83
# VertRefresh 56-76
VertRefresh 56-78
Option "NoDDC"
EndSection

```

you might have to increase the Horizsync aswell, not sure by how much though
