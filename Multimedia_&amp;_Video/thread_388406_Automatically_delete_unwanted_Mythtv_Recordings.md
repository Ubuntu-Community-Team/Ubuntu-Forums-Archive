### Post by majoridiot on 2007-03-19
there is a setting in the frontend setup for "autoexpire", which will automatically delete recordings
if it needs space, or if the "expire" limit is reached... i think it is under Setup-->general and then
on a page of its own.

i *believe* livetv autoexpire is set to 3 days (it used to be)- but you can set it to 1 and it
will faithfully expire your livetv recordings in 24hours- mine does.
