### Post by vanadium on 2007-12-11
I am afraid your choice will be either compiz fusion, or your 3 D games. It is xgl that is causing the problems. You need it to run Compiz, but other applications that need 3D acceleration won't anymore. If you remove the xserver-xgl package from synaptic, you won't be able to run compiz anymore, but your games will work as before (provided you have the proprietary driver active) (This is the ATI mess in action!).
