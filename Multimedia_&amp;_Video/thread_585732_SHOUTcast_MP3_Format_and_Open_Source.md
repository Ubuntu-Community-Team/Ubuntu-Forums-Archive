### Post by Manolicious on 2007-10-22
Problem solved by going here [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats#head-36fedf24fdc9c37cf0bcef220af3abaec8aede27]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats#head-36fedf24fdc9c37cf0bcef220af3abaec8aede27")

look for gstreamer in the Synaptic Package Manager and mark "gstreamer0.10-fluendo-mp3".  After I had saved the station's url to Rhythmbox it now plays just fine!
