### Post by issueperson on 2007-06-07
I have used mplayer in previous versions of Ubuntu fine with the hardware I have now.  But now when I try to play a DVD in mplayer I get the following error message:
> 
Fatal Error!
Error opening/initializing the selected video_out (-vo) device.

DVD playback works fine in Totem and gxine, but I prefer mplayer.  So if anyone has any suggestions, I'd appreciate it.

---

### Post by taurus on 2007-06-07
Start mplayer.  Then, go into Preferences -> video and pick **xv** as the driver.  Shut it down and open it up again.  Now, see if you can play anything with mplayer.
