I think some of what you are looking for is here -

[http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/AtiProprietaryDriver](http://www.mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/AtiProprietaryDriver)

If you notice, they mention that the ATI driver should be set for the OpenGLOverlay when used on a TV, and VideoOverlay when used on a monitor.
