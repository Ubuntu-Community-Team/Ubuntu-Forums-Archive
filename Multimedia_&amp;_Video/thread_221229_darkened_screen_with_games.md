### Post by LordTureis on 2006-07-25
Try turning off the screensaver when you play.  

System > Preferences > Screensaver > Uncheck "activate screensaver when session is idle"

(I think, I'm not sure, though) Frozen Bubble and Tremulous both grab your mouse, so it doesn't register to GNOME.  

If it doesn't work, check back here.
