### Post by tgalati4 on 2008-04-03
Sounds like video interference.  Have you tried changing the the PVR card to a different slot to see if the behavior changes?  Make sure your cabling is high-quality and that the shield is intact on your coax cable.  If the crimped connector is loose, that can cause noise due to poor shielding.
