### Post by granny4linux on 2007-03-17
Connect your IPOD ,bring up Amarok, select   "connect".

With "Media Devices" selected in Amarok left pane (your Ipod contents will be viewable) you will see a folder labeled "Playlists" right at the top. See screenshot1

Drag any of your albums or songs from the list below to this Playlist Folder. Make sure the arrow that comes up while you are "dragging" lands right on the word "Playlists" You can also right click on an album or songs from the IPOD list and choose "Add to Playlist". 

One quirk I don't understand is that it doesn't show the name of the album you drag, you have to rightclick on the "NewPlaylist" after you've dragged it in and then rename it.
See screenshot2  See screenshot3 for renamed example

Once you've added all the playlists that you want, click on "disconnect" See screenshot4

Once your IPOD is updated you can remove it from the desktop per usual.

I hope this helps, I sometimes can't find the words to explain things properly.
