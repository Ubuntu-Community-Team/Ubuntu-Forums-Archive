### Post by haldean on 2007-11-01
[Last.fm](http://www.last.fm) is a service which keeps track of your music listening habits then recommends new music to you based on what you listen to already. When it says "Submitting to Last.FM", it's submitting track information to last.fm so it can add the currently playing track to your profile.

That shouldn't happen, though, unless you've got a last.fm account and you've put your username and password into Exaile...
