> Ok, I have an LCD monitor that loads up just fine.  but the problem is when I load up my 19 inch CTR monitor.  every thing is just really small. Your LCD monitor and CRT monitor are running on seperate resolutions, it would seem. When you plug in your LCD monitor, it comes up with the resolution you are used to. When you plug in your CRT monitor, it uses a resolution much higher then your LCD, and so everything looks much smaller.

Essentially, a resolution determines how much information you want to try to fit on the screen.

> **adam3d90s said:**
> OH, and when I switch users or something my screen does this weird color thing,  were it gets filled up with a hole bunch of colors for a few seconds and then it is fine!

This is normal - this happens on my machine (and others I'm sure). It's only the display manager getting set up. You are good to go as long as those colors don't decide they want to stick around. :)
