What I want to do, but have not been able to do, is set my screen to 1366×768, which is the specific widescreen resolution that the TV uses. It will not accept any other widescreen resolutions, but will accept some regular 4:3 resolutions. Right now, I 'm stuck sending 1024x768 to the TV. It works okay, but it stretches the image. Which is annoy when I'm watching movies.

I have tried everything I can think of to make the computer put out 1366x768, but I cannot figure it out. My current xorg.conf is posted below, but this is the even weirder part. The Screen Resolution Preferences seems to ignore my xorg.conf. The only resolutions I have in my xorg.conf are 1600x1200, 1366×768, 800x600, and 640x480, but Screen Resolution Preferences gives me about 20 resolutions to choose from. Some are widescreen, but none are the specific resolution I need.

I've had another problem with Screen Resolution Preferencs that  may or may not be related. Sometimes when I change the resolution in it, it gives me the confirmation window but did not change the resolution. When this happens I have to restart X (and in some cases reboot) to make able to change the resolution.
