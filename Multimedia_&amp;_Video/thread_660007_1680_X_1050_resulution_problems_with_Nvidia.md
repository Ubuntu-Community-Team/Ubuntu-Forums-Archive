### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2008-01-06
I believe the problem is that you're specifying plug'n'play and it's not detecting widescreen correctly. I looked up your horizontal sync and vertical refresh values in [this](http://www.anz.aocmonitor.com/pdfs/210v.pdf) pdf. Try this for your monitor section:
```
Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"AOC 210V"
	HorizSync         30-80
        VertRefresh      55-75
        Option "DPMS"
	Gamma	1.0
EndSection
```
