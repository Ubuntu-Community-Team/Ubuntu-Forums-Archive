### Post by dolson on 2006-08-09
You really should contact Mark Shuttleworth.

He has stated to me that there will be a multimedia-oriented derivative of Ubuntu. If you really don't want to reinvent the wheel, you should probably collaborate with him and his team, which I am not directly involved with.

The aim of the Ubuntu Studio Project is simply to be a documentation resource. The fact that some of us work on packages for REVU and thereby inclusion into Ubuntu is only a by-product of our desire to see Ubuntu proper flourish and include the most audio software possible.

Again, I don't want to discourage you, but Mark spoke of a group out of Brazil who had already started on such a project. I highly recommend you find out what they are doing, and try to see if/where you fit in there.

His email address, which has been posted by others here before me and is not a secret, is [email]mark@canonical.com[/email].
