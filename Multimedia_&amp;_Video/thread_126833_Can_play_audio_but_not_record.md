### Post by ispmarin on 2006-02-07
Just some ideas:
1) Check that the line in is open. You can do this by clicking twice int the volume control, and then open the channel and adjust the volume. 
2) Select the right input in the audacity. Line is line, not Line1 or microphone.
3) See if the cables are working... and the connections are correct. This is dumb, but it happens more often than you imagine...

Just my two cents.
