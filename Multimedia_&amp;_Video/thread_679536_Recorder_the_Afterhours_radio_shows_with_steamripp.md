### Post by MrClearPore on 2008-01-29
> Would have a possibilities to recorder with streamripper without cut please

Typing **streamripper <URL> -a** will rip the audio stream as one large file.
For a list of Streamripper options, type **streamripper** and it'll bring up the help file with its list of options :)
