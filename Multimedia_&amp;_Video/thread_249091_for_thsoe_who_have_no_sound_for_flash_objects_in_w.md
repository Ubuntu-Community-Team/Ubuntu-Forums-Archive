### Post by spoopryme on 2006-09-02
i tried everything i could find in these forums to fix my problem of stuff in web pages not having any sound.  installing stuff and running tests, tweaking config files, and nothing seemed to work until i double-clicked on the speaker icon... you know, right there next to the date and time section...


     where i soon discovered that if you unmute the 'pcm' found in the sound mixer (version 6.06 dapper) that flash things will have sound.  for some reason mine defaulted to mute when i installed ubuntu, and i never thought to double-click that until after i tried for just over 2 hours to fix the problem.  knowing that now, im not even sure if i had that issue in the first place!
