### Post by Yellow Pasque on 2007-11-13
The "ati" driver is the correct open-source one for your video card. It supports the Radeon family up to and including the Xy00 series (also called R400). The "radeon" driver is older and is based on specs ATI released severaly years back under a Non-Disclosure Agreement.

You can also try the proprietary drivers if so desired, but I would not recommend it unless you;re having issues with the "ati" driver.
