### Post by neilgg on 2006-06-18
After upgrade from Breezy to Dapper, I was stuck in 640x480 mode, and could not get any higher resolution (i.e. 1024x768).  I tried manual edits of xorg.conf and using dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg, all to no avail.  xorg.conf looked correct (to me anyway), and I could not determine what was causing X to stick in 640x480.  

Another user mentioned in these forums that Dapper has problems probing Dell flat-panels, and suggested using a LiveCD to solve problem.

I used Knoppix 3.7 Live CD (XFree86 with autoconfiguration) and copied Device, Screen, and Monitor sections from XF86Config-4 (Knoppix) to xorg.conf, and now it is working correctly.

Note: I would use a breezy (xorg) LiveCD to do this if you can.
Note: make sure you unset read-only flag on drive prior to trying to edit xorg.conf

Below is my complete xorg.conf that gives a 1024x768 resolution on Dell 17" FP with "Intel Corporation 82845G/GL[Brookdale-G]/GE Chipset Integrated Graphics Device (rev 03)" Controller in a Dell Dimension Desktop.

Note: Areas copied from Knoppix are marked, and failed areas they replace are commented out below.  Also, make sure you update ServerLayout and Screen section to use correct Identifiers.
