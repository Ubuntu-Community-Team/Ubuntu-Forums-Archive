### Post by liveB on 2008-02-26
I had this problem with Gutsy 64 bit and then with Gutsy 32 bit.  I used this post [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=661833](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=661833) tutorial and tried updating everything.

Finally, after 2 months and much frustration, I partitioned my hard disk to hold my folder data, wiped the whole thing, and installed Hardy 64 bit.  I again went through the tutorial, installing Java 7 and only running the scripts for VLC (seemed to be a popular choice).

Voila! I now have a 64 bit system that runs streaming video and plays DVDs without a hitch.  I'll never know if it was the upgrade to Hardy or if I did something by accident.

So far, I really like Hardy (including the background).  I hope they don't break it getting it ready to release.
