### Post by 23meg on 2007-02-11
Yes, they'll work. Just install them with apt-get, aptitude or Synaptic, and the required KDE libraries will be installed with them. 

Keep in mind that these libraries will have to be loaded when you run KDE apps, and thus they'll take extra disk space and memory.
