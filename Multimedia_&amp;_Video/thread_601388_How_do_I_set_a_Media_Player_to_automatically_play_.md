How do I set a Media Player to automatically play a link, instead of having to click on the file? Streaming Internet radio for example. When I go to [Steel Guitar Radio]("http://www.steelradio.com/"), I click on the "on air" button & then I get a pane on the left of the page that I have to click on again to play. 

Can I configure it to play auto. when I click on the "on air" button? I tried r/click configure, but the settings I chose didn't seem to enable this feature.

### Post by NilsE on 2007-11-03
That's the download manager trying to help you.  Go into tools>addons>Extensions>Get Extensions and look for "Download Statusbar". 

You will still see the status of your downloads but it won't  ask like the internal download manager.
