### Post by NeoLithium on 2007-06-08
To install the skins system wide, for all users to see, install them in /usr/share/xmms/Skins  (You'll need root permissions.  For just your account, they go in /home/USERNAME/.xmms/Skins/

You just move the .tar.gz to that folder, and extract it there :)
