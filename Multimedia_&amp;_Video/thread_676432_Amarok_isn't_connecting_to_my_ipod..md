### Post by Game Theory on 2008-01-25
I also have gutsy ubuntu and it wasn't mounting my iPod. 
I went to settings --> configure amarok --> media devices (put your ipod type here) and then auto detect.
then i went back to the "devices" window on the mainframe and there is a drop down menu on the top saying that you can mount the ipod.

not sure if this helps, but just in case :KS
