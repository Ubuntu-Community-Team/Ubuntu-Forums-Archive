### Post by Unisted on 2007-02-12
Mythtv requires MySql for a database it uses to drive certain aspects of it.

I don't think Mythtv is really what your after though, you could probably do what you want a lot easier without it.

Assuming you have a TV card of some sort then you could connect your VCR to your PC and get some sort of output and then save that to whichever format your happy with and then use one of the many dvd authoring programs to burn to dvds. (some are available via Synaptic or by Automatix2)

I know the above is a bit scarce of real information but if your old VHS tapes are of currently available dvds then I'd suggest binning them and buying the dvds for better sound/ picture and extras, subtitles.

If they are home videos then its the start of a whole lotta fun!
