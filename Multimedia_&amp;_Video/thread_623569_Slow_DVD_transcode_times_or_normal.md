### Post by human-ness on 2007-11-26
Yes around 2 hours for your computers speed.  34 FPS ain't that bad actually.

If you tick off 2 pass, your time will be somewhat less.  Almost around half that time, but then your conversion will be somewhat of poorer quality.

You can also lower the conversion resolution to something like 320*240, however again, it lowers picture quality.

2nd pass and "640*keep aspect ratio" should be compulsory.  They make all the difference.
