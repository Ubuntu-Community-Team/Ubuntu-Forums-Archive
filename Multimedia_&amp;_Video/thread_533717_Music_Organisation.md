### Post by SendDerek on 2007-08-24
+1 for Amarok.  

Once Amarok is open, goto Settings->Configure Amarok->Collection.  Navigate to the directory that has all of your music in it and check it.  It will automagically check all the subdirectories.

[IMG]http://img.photobucket.com/albums/v203/send_derek/screenshot2.jpg[/IMG]

Another tip for organizing your music is to use EasyTag.  I just went through a 5 day rampage re-organizing and cleaning my music.  I also used SoundConverter (installed by sudo apt-get install soundconverter) to convert my .wma files into .mp3.
