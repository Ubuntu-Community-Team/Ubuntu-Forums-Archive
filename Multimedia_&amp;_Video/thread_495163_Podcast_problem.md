I use RhythmBox to play my podcasts. Here's what I do:
[LIST=1]
[*]Find the website of the podcast you want to subscribe to.
[*]Look on the page for the link to subscribe to the podcast.
[*]Right click on that link (with Ubuntu, choose the ogg format if it's a choice, unless you want to play it on your iPod), and choose (copy link location)
[*]Make sure RhythmBox is open, the podcast section is selected, and choose "add new podcast feed"
[*]Right click and paste the link[/LIST]The new podcast should show up in the window below, and the latest podcast will download automatically. You can right click on previous podcast and choose to download those too.
