### Post by mac.ryan on 2007-05-01
To me, it sounds like you did all the proper steps to have your DVD working... maybe something with your hardware? (sounds unlikely, yet... checking doesn't cost much).

Have you checked if you have DMA (Direct Memory Access) enabled on your DVD from bios? (not all bioses have that configurable, though).

Also, have you checked that mem and cpu usage while watching a DVD are ok? (under gnome you can see that via a utility called "system monitor"... I don't know under xubuntu...)
