### Post by antiemptyv on 2008-01-03
How do I remove "Open with Totem" and "Open with mplayer" from the menu brought up via a right-click on a video file?  Totem and mplayer are not installed on my system.  I'm sure there's a config file somewhere from which I just need to delete a line.

---

### Post by irv on 2008-01-03
Try going to Applications> Add/Remove. Fine the ones you want to remove and take the check mark off that app.
Irv

---

### Post by antiemptyv on 2008-01-03
Ah!  I JUST found out how to do it!  Right-click on the file.  Go down to Properties.  Under the Open With tab, you can remove applications used there.

Thanks for your quick reply, irv.
