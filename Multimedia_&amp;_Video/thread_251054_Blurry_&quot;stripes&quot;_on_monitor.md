### Post by msingletary on 2006-09-13
I actually noticed the "exact" same thing with my 17" NEC LCD. Fortunately I did a bit of searching on the forums here and found that simply doing an 'auto adjustment' in the monitor's OSD (on-screen display) completely fixed it up. Everything (even the previously non-blurry) sections look fantastic now.

Give it a try! Unfortunately I can't be of much help past the auto-config as that's as far as I dug when looking for help here. You might try searching for 'blurry' in these forums as that turned up quite a few results that could prove useful for you.
