### Post by RudolfMDLT on 2007-05-26
Hi,

double click the speaker icon to the left of the time, top right in the screen. 

Click on file, and select the right device. then under edit, select the preferences that you would like to adjust.  You said you tried changing LFE, well, maybe on the wrong sound card. See if Ubuntu is running both, your onboard and the SB Live card. It worked on my brothers machine without hassle, 7.1 surround.
