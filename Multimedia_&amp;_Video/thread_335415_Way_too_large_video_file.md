### Post by kidders on 2007-01-10
Hi there,

You have a few basic things you can play with (or rather compromise on!) to reduce the size of a movie:

[LIST]
[*]Encoding ... some audio/video formats are more compressed than others.
[*]Dimensions ... you could just make your movie smaller (lower the resolution).
[*]Bit rate/frame rate ... lowering these too far can have a devastating effect on audio/video quality, but make a huge difference to file sizes.
[*]Etc.
[/LIST]

I tend to use tools like ffmpeg and mencoder to reformat movies. Getting used to them takes a little work, but it's well worth learning. Can I ask what the end purpose of the re-encoded movie will be?
