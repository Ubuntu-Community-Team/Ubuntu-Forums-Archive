I have a collection of FLAC music as well as a collection of different music in mp3 format. Amarok does a wonderful job of merging these, even on removable drives. Is there an existing way to transfer a FLAC song to an ipod, and have Amarok convert it to mp3 on the fly just to copy to the ipod?

### Post by jamboarder on 2007-03-01
I think you'll need a transcoding script.  Check out amarok website (amarok.kde.org) or kde-apps.org for a transcoding script (amaKode or transkode or something similar).  I'm not sure if those scripts will do FLAC to mp3 but I've had them convert some oggs and wmas to mp3.  You'll need to add and run the script in the Script Manager in Amarok.  Then you can tell amarok to transcode on the fly in the Media Device section.

Good luck!
