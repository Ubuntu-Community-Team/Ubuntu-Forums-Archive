### Post by Joakim Stokland on 2007-10-16
I'm missing a real good online MP3 store!
Most existing stores require some kind of Windows software, either to download the files in the first place (such as iTunes), or because of protected WMA files and equal.
What I personally also miss is the ability of alternative payment methods, such as Payex or Paypal in addition to credit cards and such.
So what I want here is an overview of the existing online music stores with MP3 downloads, where Ubuntu-ers are as welcome as our fellows who has not yet seen the light.
I've not yet found any threads like this, but please point me to it if it does exist.

So if you know about a real good music store, please share it with the rest of us! Please tell about what kind of music it offers, which payment options there are, which file format you get to download, bitrate and other information you may find relevant.

The one I would like to tell about is **[www.musiconline.no](www.musiconline.no)** .
This site sells MP3s from **Norwegian artists in all genres**. There are no software requirements, and no copy protection. All songs have from 256 to 320 kbps.
Prices are average, and alternative payment methods are Payex and Visa/Mastercard (all transactions are handled at a secure Payex site). There's a minimum limit of NOK 25 (approx. 3$) for credit card purchases.
