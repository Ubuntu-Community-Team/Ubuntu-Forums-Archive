### Post by rockingmtranch on 2007-04-10
Just wanted to share this. I have Ubuntu 6.10 with all current updates. A one gig AMD processor and 768 megs RAM. Nvidia 5200 card and Diamond sound card. I wanted to watch television off of the satellite reciever using a KWorld Xpert DVD Maker USB 2.0 Video Capture Device that I've had for a couple years. I finally got it working and here is how:
The device was in device manager after the install of Ubuntu (it was hooked up during installation). I used the info found in 'info.udi' to do a modprobe and it started streaming using TVtime Television Viewer. 
Works great.
