### Post by CatKiller on 2006-10-04
Your xorg.conf looks very strange to me. I guess it was auto-configured by some Ati tool? Anyway, from **man xorg.conf**:>               Relative  "screen-id" x y
                  These give the screen&#8217;s location relative to another screen.
                  The first four position the screen immediately to the right,
                  left,  above or below the other screen.  When positioning to
                  the right or left, the top edges are  aligned.   When  posi&#8208;
                  tioning  above  or  below,  the left edges are aligned.  The
                  Relative form specifies the offset of  the  screen&#8217;s  origin
                  (upper  left  corner)  relative  to  the  origin  of another
                  screen.
This would go in the ServerLayout section instead of the "RightOf" that I'd expect to see there.

HTH.
