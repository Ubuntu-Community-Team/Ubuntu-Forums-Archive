Hey everyone I just wanted to say that I had been looking for something to list all my purchased dvd's with info about the movie and covers, and I found it in CeeMedia. It's a great program. And no I'm not from CeeMedia so I'm not promoting this software to get more hits or whatever. Just thought I'd give me reccomendation.:)
