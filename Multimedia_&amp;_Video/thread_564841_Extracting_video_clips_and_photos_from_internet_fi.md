### Post by MrFSL on 2007-10-01
hidden in your home folder should be a folder for firefox (Probably .mozilla). Inside there is everything you need, including: 

cookies.txt -> Text file containing cookies

and

Cache/ -> A folder with cached internet garbage. 

From inside firefox Ctrl+Shift+Del is a speedy way to clear out this garbage.
