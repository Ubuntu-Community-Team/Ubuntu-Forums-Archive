### Post by srhlefty on 2007-11-08
I've used [xvidcap]("http://xvidcap.sourceforge.net/") in the past to make videos for the purpose of showing off Beryl plugins, so it can certainly record desktop video for you no sweat.  

You'll need a different tool to record and overlay audio though.

---

### Post by srhlefty on 2007-11-09
There's also the program called [recordMyDesktop]("http://recordmydesktop.sourceforge.net")

There's a GTK frontend for it that you can find in Synaptic: search for gtk-recordmydesktop

It looks like that tool records the audio as well as video (I've never tried it)
