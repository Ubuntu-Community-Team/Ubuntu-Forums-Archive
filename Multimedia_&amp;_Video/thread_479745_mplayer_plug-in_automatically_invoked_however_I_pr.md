When I click on the links the mplayer plug-in is automatically invoked and the page loads the streaming video. ### Post by groovomata on 2007-06-20
Okay, I just answered my own question. Simply go firefox's preferences, go to the Content Tab, and configure how firefox handles file types. In my case specifically Real Player and QuickTime file types. If you choose to invoke a player, the executables are generally in the usr/bin directory.
