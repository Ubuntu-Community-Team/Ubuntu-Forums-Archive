### Post by Jive Turkey on 2008-01-08
Assuming you are using gutsy:
from the menu click:
Places > CD/DVD Creator

This will open a window where you can drag and drop your files from where they are stored.  Then click the button that says "Burn to Disk."

from any nautilus window I think you can also type "burn:///" in the location bar also to bring up the burning window.
