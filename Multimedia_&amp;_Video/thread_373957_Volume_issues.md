### Post by thebigkevdogg on 2007-04-21
OK i booted up my laptop to see exactly what it is. Just right click on the volume icon in the notification area (top right corner) and click on Preferences. Then make it so that "Master" and "Headphone" are both selected (CTR + click). This is on Ubuntu 6.10 and 7.04. Let me know how it works for you!
