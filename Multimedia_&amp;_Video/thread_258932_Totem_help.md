### Post by Scunizi on 2006-09-16
Try using mplayer from the repositories through Synaptic.  You may have to enable, universe, multiverse, backports to find it.  It seems to work out-of-the-box where I'm still searching for the solution for totem.  (missing codecs). By the way, if you're running a P4 or something with hyperthreading you might want to upgrade your kernal to i686 instead of i386.  It fixed some jittery issues in mplayer for me and now plays smooth.
