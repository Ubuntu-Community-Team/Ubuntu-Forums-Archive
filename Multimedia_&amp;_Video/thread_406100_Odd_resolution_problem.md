### Post by heimo on 2007-04-10
You should probably change HorizSync (check your monitor specs). I'll just make a guess what might work:
```
HorizSync    28.0 - 67.0
```

Also add custom modeline to your monitor section.
```

# 1680x1050 @ 60 Hz  (GTF) hsync: 65.22 kHz; pclk: 147.14 MHz
ModeLine "1680x1050" 147.14  1680 1784 1968 2256 1050 1051 1054 1087 -hsync +vsync

```
