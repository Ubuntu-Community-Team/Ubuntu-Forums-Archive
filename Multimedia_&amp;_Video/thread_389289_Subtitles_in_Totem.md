### Post by Cappy on 2007-03-20
Soft Subtitles don't come up automatically in Totem - you must Goto **View-->Subtitles**. Automatix can install not only the codecs, but a better version of Totem (I believe the default is g-streamer and the better one is xine-lib but you don't really need to know that) and VLC. On another note, Totem is a bit weird with soft subtitles. If you have your video full screen they appear at the VERY bottom (maybe just for me?) whereas on Windows the positioning of Soft Subs was always exactly the same as Hard Subs.

Community Codec Pack was great, I completely agree there! I always installed CCP on a fresh computer to make sure that some other crappy codec package wouldn't mess everything up =) Automatix works just as flawlessly for me =)
