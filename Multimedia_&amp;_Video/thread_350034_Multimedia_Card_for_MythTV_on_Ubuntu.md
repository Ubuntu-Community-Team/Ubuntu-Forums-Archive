### Post by fatemonkey on 2007-01-31
I am interested in building a PVR system and I have a tower that has (I believe) an AMD 2600, an Geforce 5500 (AGP), and 512 MB RAM.

The component that I am missing is a capture card.  I am looking for stereo audio and quality video!  (Standard Def as I do not have an HD TV.)

I have been looking seriously at the [Hauppauge PVR-500]("http://www.hauppauge.com/pages/products/data_pvr500mce.html") and also at the [Hauppauge PVR 250]("http://www.hauppauge.com/pages/products/data_pvr250.html").  I like the idea of doing MPEG2 encoding on the card (leaving more tasty processing cycles for background transcoding) and I am seriously hoping to avoid sending the audio back out of the card and into the line in on the sound card as the integrated sound on the mobo is mediocre at best.  

I would like to have 2 tuners so that I can enable the record one channel watch the other and picture-in-picture features.

Any thoughts?  I have heard that some of the PVR-500s have been found to have poor picture quality.  Does anyone have experience with these (or other comparable) cards?
