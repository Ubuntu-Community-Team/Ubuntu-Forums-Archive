### Post by dreamnid on 2007-10-08
Hi,

Take a look at PulseAudio.  It not allows you to control volume for each application, but you can also re-direct sound to a specific sound card or even another computer running the PulseAudio server.

[http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#How_to_setup_PulseAudio_Sound_Server](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#How_to_setup_PulseAudio_Sound_Server)

I'm having some trouble with it with getting sound to work with Flash and Skype on my 64bit system, but there are work arounds for the 32bit systems.
