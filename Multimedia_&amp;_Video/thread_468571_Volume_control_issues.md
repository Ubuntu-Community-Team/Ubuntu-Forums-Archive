### Post by Bohlio on 2007-06-09
Wow, this is incredebly odd. I was fiddling around with the audio options, both in System>Preferances>Sound and from right clicking on the sound icon and selecting properties. I thought these two were connected but apparently they are independant from eachother. After switching them back and forth, opening and closing them, matching them together, and mixing them up. I finally got the Sys>Pref>Sound controls to control PCM, and the Volume control to control Master. This combo worked for some odd reason. I then changed the volume control to PCM so they match, and it works. I had this setting at least a dozen times while I was trying different options, Its very weird, but now i have it working :-)
