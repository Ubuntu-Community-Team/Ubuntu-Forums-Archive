### Post by crispy_420 on 2007-04-08
What is the exact model of card? Is it a software encoding or hardware encoding card?

Tvtime is generally set up to work with the older software cards running on chipsets like bttv or saa. Maybe some coexant based cards too. So if you a newer hardware based card (Hauppauge PVR-150, 250, etc.) it may not work. You may just need a different app to view. I hear xawtv is good for just such a purpose. Also with many hardware based cards, driver setup is half the challenge.

Let us know the card and we can help you with your next step.

There is some information on setting up your device to work with MythTV at this URL:
[http://www.mythtv.org/docs/mythtv-HOWTO-3.html](http://www.mythtv.org/docs/mythtv-HOWTO-3.html)

Scroll down to the section "USB Capture Devices" about half way down the page, and there is a link describing how to get this working in MythTV. If this is your only computer, I would invest in a second hard drive and do a fresh install of edgy, and then follow one of the guides for setting up MythTV. That way you can always swap drives if your installation messes up your system.
