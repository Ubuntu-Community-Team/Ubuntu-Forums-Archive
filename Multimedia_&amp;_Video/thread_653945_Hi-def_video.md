### Post by shad0w_walker on 2007-12-30
DVDs are not hi-def. They are standard definition. Your TV is standard definition and if you have ever done TV out from your comp you will see that everything is fairly blurred because SD has a much lower resolution.

It looks decent because DVDs are designed to be used with TVs which have the lower resolution and will end up blurring the image slightly on most screens,
