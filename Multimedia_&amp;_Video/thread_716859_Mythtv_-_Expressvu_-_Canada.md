Hmmm, well, you won't be able to get HD output from the tuner card. That card is analog only. So, you won't be recording to HD. Hopefully that's no big deal. The other issue will be changing channels. I think you will have to change channels on the Sat box to have an effect in myth. If that's a big deal, you can get IR blaster that transmits to your Sat box and changes the channel on it for you. Does that make sense?

Hauppauge does make a some new HD tuner cards but the linux dirvers are beta. I was thinking of getting one once the drivers are more stable. (hvr-1600 and hvr-1800).

[http://mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_HVR-1600](http://mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/Hauppauge_HVR-1600)

Some info on IR Blaster
[http://mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/IR_Blaster](http://mythtv.org/wiki/index.php/IR_Blaster)
