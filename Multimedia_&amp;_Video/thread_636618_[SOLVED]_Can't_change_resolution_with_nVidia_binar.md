### Post by Rumpty on 2007-12-11
xorg.conf is surely a can of worms

I suggest you get rid of the Modeline entirely in the section "monitor"
In the "screen" section, here is what I have-

"section "Screen"
    Identifier     "Default Screen"
    Device         "nVidia Corporation G72 [GeForce 7300 SE]"
    Monitor        "Philips 190P"
    DefaultDepth    24
    Option         "UseFBDev" "true"
    SubSection     "Display"
        Depth       24
        Modes      "1280x1024_60"
    EndSubSection
EndSection

Try altering yours to this layout, putting in the resolution you want, of course, leaving the monitor as generic, add the UseFBDev line, and add the depth 24, to be after the SubSection "Display", as well as where DefaultDepth is.
