### Post by dolson on 2006-10-14
Mubuntu was forced to rename because another project us using the name now (an embedded Ubuntu derivative).

I decided to give up the name of Ubuntu Studio for this distro project, as it seemed like the best idea.

On top of this, the wiki will no longer cease to exist. The following is the new link to find the documentation that previously existed on ubuntustudio.com:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Sound](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Sound)

### Post by MetalMusicAddict on 2006-10-14
Id like to encourage anyone who is into multimedia creation on linux to get involved and/or follow [Ubuntu Studio]("http://wiki.ubuntustudio.org/") in its new and improved form.

We are very excited about the new [Ubuntu Studio]("http://wiki.ubuntustudio.org/"). We have a good group of people but we are still small and in need of people who can package or have good audio hardware to test on.

In a little while I am going to open up a wallpaper contest for ones to be included in [Ubuntu Studios]("http://wiki.ubuntustudio.org/") release.
