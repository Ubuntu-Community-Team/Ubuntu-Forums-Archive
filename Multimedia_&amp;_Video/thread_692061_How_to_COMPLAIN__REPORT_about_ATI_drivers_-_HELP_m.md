In this thread, I just want to provide some links for those who want to contribute some feedback to ATI about their binary (proprietary) drivers, or about the open source drivers - you can help making a change... well, apparently, the change is already in progress! ;-)

**[COLOR="Red"]IMPORTANT:[/COLOR]** Try to be objective and precise, provide error messages/short logs if possible and most important: **don't hassle/shittalk please!!**

:arrow:If you're unsatisfied with your ATI **binary/proprietary/restricted drivers**, or if you want to report bugs, I suggest you create an account under that links below and/or complain there, to push ATI/AMD a little... 
[http://ati.cchtml.com/](http://ati.cchtml.com/)
[http://support.ati.com/ics/survey/su...D=508&type=web](http://support.ati.com/ics/survey/su...D=508&type=web)
