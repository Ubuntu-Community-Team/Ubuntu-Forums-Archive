### Post by neilb on 2007-07-10
Hi

It would be nice to get two monitors running on this Dell Dimension 3100, which comes with a built-in Intel Display Controller and has been happily running Edgy since the day it arrived.  There is a spare PCI slot and I found an old ATI Radeon 7000 PCI card.

To hand are two monitors, both gnr TS902W (1440x900).

The built in Intel Display Controller works with the i810 driver, and is plugged into the right monitor.  I saved the xorg.conf file.

I opened the case and dropped the PCI card in and plugged it into the left monitor.  After installing the ATI drivers with apt-get and then reconfiguring with sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg, it's visible if not perfect.  I saved the new xorg.conf file.

After reading around here, ubuntuguide.org and even the gentoo xorg tutorial, I still haven't got the two xorg.confs combined into one which lets me see both monitors at once.  I have seen them both work independently.

The right monitor (connected to the onboard Intel Display Controller) hasn't showed pixels since I plugged in the PCI card.

The resolution is kind of sucky through the Radeon.

Thanks for any pointers.
