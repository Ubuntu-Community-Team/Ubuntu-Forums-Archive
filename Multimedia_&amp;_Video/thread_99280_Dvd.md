### Post by frodon on 2005-12-05
a) Check that you have a good source.list file with good repositories : 
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=92672](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=92672)

b) To read dvd you need libdvdcss2 : 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=81577&highlight=libdvdcss2](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=81577&highlight=libdvdcss2)

c) You may need to enable DMA for your cd/dvd drive : 
[http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/DMA](http://doc.gwos.org/index.php/DMA)
