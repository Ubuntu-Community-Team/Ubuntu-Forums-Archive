### Post by tubasoldier on 2007-03-01
I assume you are new to linux and sound in linux?

Midi in linux doesn't just work. It is actually lacking quite a bit. I personally bought a sound blaster audigy so I could load a soundfont for easy midi playback. This makes playing back compositions in NoteEdit very easy. 

However, if you dont have a snazzy sound card then Timidity and FluidSynth is your best bet.
