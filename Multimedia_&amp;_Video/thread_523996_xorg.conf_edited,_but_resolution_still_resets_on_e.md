### Post by dougfractal on 2007-08-12

failing that

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=507843&highlight=nvidia](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=507843&highlight=nvidia)
> My guess is that the "save X configuration" isn't working.
So click "save X configuration" then "Show preview". Select text.
Code:

sudo mv /etc/X11/xorg.conf /etc/X11/xorg.conf.mybackup
sudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf

paste text and save.

You will need to renable Desktop effects, as nvidia-settings removes these.

### Post by tehshay on 2007-08-15
Well I was having the same trouble just a two days ago when I switched to Ubuntu.

What got it fixed was to go to nvidia-settings (through the gui not cli), and choose your resolution, then press "apply", and then "preview", that will get you the current xorg.conf file with the new resolution settings set, just replace that file with your original xorg.conf file and you are done.

if u need any further feel free to ask i hope that helps.
