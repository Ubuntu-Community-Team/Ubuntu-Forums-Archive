### Post by Ex-Cyber on 2007-06-21
There are legal issues with DVD playback software in some countries, so it is not installed by default. More information on how to play DVDs (and other popular restricted formats like MP3)  is available on the [RestrictedFormats](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats) page in the Community Documentation wiki.
