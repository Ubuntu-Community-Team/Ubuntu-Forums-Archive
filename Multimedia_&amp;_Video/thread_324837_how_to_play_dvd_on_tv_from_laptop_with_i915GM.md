### Post by djRobbieF on 2006-12-24
Once you've connected your laptop to the TV, turn on the laptop.  Not before.  If you've already booted, reboot.

Laptops tend to detect displays upon boot (during POST)... and because your FN-F4 will most likely not do anything within Ubuntu, this should make it work for you (rebooting with the TV connected and turned on).

Cheers.
