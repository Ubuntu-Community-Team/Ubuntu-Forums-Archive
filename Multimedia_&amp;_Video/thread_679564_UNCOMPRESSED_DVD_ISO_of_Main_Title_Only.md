### Post by mc4man on 2008-01-27
While vobcopy does have options to rip an individual title or files you'll end up with either 1 large file or a  video_ts with no navigation. It's better suited for mirroring the complete disk to keep as is or for additional processing. Versions 1.02 or 1.10 work very well. What you may want to try is k9copy. Go to settings>configure k9copy>dvd and adjust the size to above the source. (7400mb should do). Then just check the main movie title, remove any audio, subs you don't want ect. You can uncheck  Keep original menus. (If you get a dvdauthor error while ripping then retry keeping orig. menus)
