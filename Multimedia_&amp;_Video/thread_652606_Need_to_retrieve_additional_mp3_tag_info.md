### Post by ron999 on 2007-12-29
Hi
I have used EasyTag.
To do this I have each album in a folder.
I open  EasyTag and select a folder and let it search the cd database.
When it brings the results for the album (including year) I look through them and maybe make corrections or add comments.
Then I 'save'.

As far as I know, this works for whole albums. I don't think that you can find information just for individual tracks.

Perhaps this is what you could use. It's in the Ubuntu repository.
