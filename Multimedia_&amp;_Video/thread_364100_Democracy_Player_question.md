### Post by cactaur on 2007-03-04
Actually, Democracy does in fact download the video from YouTube. Only, the video has a funky name once it's downloaded. Although there are problems with playing it in Democracy (for me, at least) you can find the downloaded movies in your Democracy folder (~/.democracy in Ubuntu) then in Movies. You want to make sure you have a player that can play .flv files for YouTube. Hope this helps.
