### Post by andrewsawyer on 2006-02-24
I would get rid of Load "GLcore" and Load "dri" from your Modules Section.

In Section "Device" for the graphics card, try changing the Driver "nv" to Driver "nvidia".

In that same section, you have Option "ConnectedMonitor" "dfp,dfp" however you say you have one vga and one dvi?  Change it to:

Option "ConnectedMonitor" "CRT, DFP"

or which ever way around they are when sitting in front of you.

In Section "Monitor" you need two more lines:

Option "TwinView" "true"
Option "TwinViewOrientation" "RightOf"

I would keep going, but your xorg.conf is so completely different to mine that it probably makes more sense just to show you what mine looks like:
