So everytime I try to tune into a new radio station with xmms, it thinks the radio station is same so it just plays the same station i was already playing. I have to close firefox and xmms and reopen it to switch to a new station. Is there a way to fix this? Thanks.

### Post by tgalati4 on 2007-08-31
If the station was added to your playlist, then yes, if the new station doesn't stream right away, then xmms will pick off the next item on your playlist.  If it was your old station, then that is what you will hear.

Try clearing your playlist and see what happens.
