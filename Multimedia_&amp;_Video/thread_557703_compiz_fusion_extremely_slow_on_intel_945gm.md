### Post by chappejw on 2008-06-29
I have solved this problem using Simple CompizConfig Settings Manager. After customizing some of the animations and screwing something up, all animations including wobbly windows were extremely slow. I checked through all possible settings verifying each one was not set too high or too low, but still all animation effects were freezing 10 to 20 seconds at a time. I opened Simple CompizConfig Settings Manager and set effects to "Hollywood Got Nothing" > Applied and everything worked as good as new. Whatever it was that was slowing down Compiz Fusion I could not find it, but this was a quick n dirty solution...
