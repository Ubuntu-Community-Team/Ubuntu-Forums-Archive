### Post by disturbed1 on 2008-01-18
No it doesn't.

In Windows, AVIVO accelerated playback (as well as nVidia's Pure Video) require special codecs to take advantage of the hardware offloading. AVIVO + h264 accelerated playback require Cyberlink's h264 codec, or Nero 7+ Ultra edition. You can not use mplayer/vlc, FFDshow, or even CoreAVC in Windows to take advantage of AVIVO.
