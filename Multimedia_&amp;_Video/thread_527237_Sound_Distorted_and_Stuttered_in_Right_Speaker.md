### Post by jmate24 on 2007-08-16
hi, My name is jmate24 i have the same problem sometimes.

the answer is that there is too much Electro Magnetic Interference. Sometimes old Monitors, and some speakers are not Shielded, and old computers give off a lot of EMI. Also If you have a few external devices with power sources next to the speakers.

just try setting the volume of Ubuntu(play some music on Ubuntu) until you don't hear the scratchy sound and then raise the volume on the new speakers until they scratch, then turn it down until you do not hear the scratch any more. if you can hear it still then maybe those speakers are unshielded(cheap).
