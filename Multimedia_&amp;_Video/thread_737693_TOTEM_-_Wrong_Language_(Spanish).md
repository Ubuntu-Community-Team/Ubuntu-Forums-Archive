### Post by doswald on 2008-03-29
got it ..

After looking more closely on the main menu of totem I see that there is a Menu option called  "Sound" and a sub option called Language...

the option selected was AC-3 Audio   - seems to be Spanish

   Setting it to AC-3 Audio #2 - seems to be French
   Setting it to AC-3 Audio #3 - gives me English ... (and the little guy is happy)

Might be a nice way for me to teach him spanish of french though ...

ok guys - thanks for looking - have a good day...
