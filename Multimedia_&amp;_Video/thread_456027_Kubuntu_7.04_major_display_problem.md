### Post by nuktar on 2007-05-27
Some of my friends and me have big display problems of Kubuntu 7.04. The next restart after configuring the desktop, it drops all the config and sets and 640x480... The driver nv goes mad...
  Well, if you have a such problem, enter the X11 setup conf file in /etc/kde3/kdm/ and set your appropriate resolution. It fixed the driver and made Kubuntu rock again...  Smile !
