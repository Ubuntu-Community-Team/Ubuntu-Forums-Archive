### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-02-05
Not sure about this, but **geexbox** could fit the bill.  OK, normally it's run as a live CD, but once loaded you can remove the OS CD and play another DVD in the drive or play any files on your hard disks or even on usb flash disks. All codecs and libdvdcss seem to be there, so no worries of that sort; it's played anything I've thrown at it when I tried it.

I suspect the biggest load on battery is spinning the CD drive, so the USB flash drive would be a better choice, I think.  As to how much power geexbox itself requires to run, I haven't a clue, but I suppose their own website may be able to help if you do a good search.
