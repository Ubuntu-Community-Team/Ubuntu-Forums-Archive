### Post by mcduck on 2006-12-11
no need to download anything from WWW. That's not the way you install things in Ubuntu.

see the links:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats")
[http://monkeyblog.org/ubuntu/installing/]("http://monkeyblog.org/ubuntu/installing/")

in short: Open System/Administration/Synaptic Package MAnager, search for gstreamer and right-click the ones you want & select 'Install'. When you are ready click 'Apply'-button and Synaptic will automatically download and install them for you.
