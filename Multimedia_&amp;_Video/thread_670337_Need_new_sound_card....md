### Post by alterpinguin on 2008-01-17
i use a cheap usb-sound-device, its called "speed-link" and has only a speaker-output and a microphone-input. Its size is like an usb-memory-stick. I use it to get easy sound-in-output at any computer with usb-device for example to use teamspeak or to have a standalone music-device. The prize was around Euro 11,-. If i plug it in, ubuntu autmagically loads the snd-usb-drivers and it can be used as a second sound-card (alsa hw:1,0).
