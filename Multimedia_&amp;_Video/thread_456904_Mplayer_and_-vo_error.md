### Post by in_flu_ence on 2007-05-28
Hi,

    For those that has the -vo error whenever you try to watch a movie using mplayer. You might try doing the following.

    Open Mplayer (not open your movie). Right click on the control panel of mplayer for the menu. Select Preference. Under Video tab, select xv instead of other options. It does not only solve my problem on -vo but also crashes whenever i try to change aspect ratio or press stop.

    For your info, I don't have any external graphic card and stuff so it might be slightly different if you do have one. But it is a good start to get things fixed.

    Please add your comments if you have any better solution as it will be very useful for newbies to touch up on when they buy the new 'dell' computers without much media support:P
