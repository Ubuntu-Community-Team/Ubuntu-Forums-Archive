### Post by Meson on 2007-10-22
I'm not sure about the wallpaper thing.  When you use twinview you're basically making one giant screen.  If you want to have separate screens you'll need to use xinerama and not twinview.  In addition your can chance your metamodes option to "nvidia-auto-select, nvidia-auto-select"  That might help things when you're switching on and off the dock.

In the screen section add 	
Option		"TwinViewXineramaInfoOrder" "DFP, CRT"
Option		"TwinViewOrientation" "leftOf"

The first option changes which monitor is the primary (it will have the login screen and all that) and the second changes their orientation
