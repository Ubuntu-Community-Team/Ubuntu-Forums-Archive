[http://forum.matrox.com/mga/viewtopic.php?t=20813](http://forum.matrox.com/mga/viewtopic.php?t=20813)

Have you done it? Please share your information with the rest of us. It would also be nice if these drivers were included with Ubuntu when the next version is released. Suse, Redhat and some others will start supporting Matrox Parhelia cards out of the box now.
