### Post by Drunky on 2007-08-18
I think I can answer part of your question;  I do not believe there is a 64 bit version of Ubuntu Studio at this time.

As to the other part; I have read where someone has compiled the source for a 64 bit machine but if you have found that your processor is not supported then, and I'm speculating here, the -lowlatency and/or -realtime kernel may not work with your processor.


You could always use GParted Live CD and repartition a little bit of your hard drive and do a test install.
