### Post by aparigraha on 2007-09-23
The video was never choppy on the monitor, only on the TV-Out. After I changed the video to sync to the TV-Out instead of the monitor, all choppyness disappeared.

sudo nano /root/nvidia-settings.rc
change
0/XVideoSyncToDisplay=0
to
0/XVideoSyncToDisplay=1

(if your TV-Out is 1 ;) )

Check out my post #38 iif you haven't already:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=3299678&postcount=38]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=3299678&postcount=38")

The only problems I had with performance was connected to images loading on the monitor. It seems the image and the video problems I had were not related... so the video solution (post #38 ) might be what you are looking for.
