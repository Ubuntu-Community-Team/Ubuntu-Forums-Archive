### Post by vanadium on 2007-07-24
Transcoding the AVI's will  further reduce the quality. If that is fine with you, go ahead. An easy way to do this is to open the AVI in Avidemux, then select the apropriate codec and settings, and save as a new AVI. You might want to reduce the image size to obtain better video quality at smaller image size. Also look at the audio. If it is an ac3 track, you surely want to transcode to a more compact format at lower bitrate to gain space.

The best you can do, though, quality wise, is rerip the DVD's and this time, have the settings right to obtain the file size you want.
