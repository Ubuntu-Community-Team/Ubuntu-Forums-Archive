### Post by jmate24 on 2007-08-16
can you purchase a jack splitter.
one goes into your on-board audio jack and the speakers plug into the two jacks on the plug.

I use one, i got it from a local technology store it works great!

i don't have any use for another sound card, sound comes out of my on-board audio device and
the splitter equally splits the sound to my 2 pairs of speakers(one is a 2.1 surround) now it is a simple semi -4.1 surround system. 
no need for configuring Ubuntu.
