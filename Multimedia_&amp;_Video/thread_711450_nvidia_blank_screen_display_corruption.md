### Post by komoto on 2008-07-23
I'm using Ubuntu 8.04, with the nv display driver.  I've got a Geforce 2 MX.  This may be of some use to you guys:

I used to get display corruption about once a day, which at first seemed to be random (I thought maybe overheating), but I have found that it only happens when display power management does anything to my monitor.

Go to: System -> Preferences -> Power Management
Then:  Slide the Display bar to "Never"

It's been months since I disabled display power management and haven't had any corruption since.

-Kom
