Do you guys know of an AGP 8x video card that runs 1280x720, with good support for GNU/Linux drivers, which my 200w power supply can handle for under $100 US dollars.

### Post by Soverign on 2007-03-26
Just something to try--have you tried changing your AGP speed in the BIOS to 4X? When I first tried to load up ubuntu off the live CD, the graphical interface would not initiate (and I'd just get a blank black screen after the greyscale loading screen thingy). Changing my AGP speed to 4X fixed the problem. I have a Radeon 9800 PRO AGP 8X. 
just something to make sure you try before you go out and get a new card
