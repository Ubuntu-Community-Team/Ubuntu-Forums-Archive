### Post by Monsieur le Comte on 2006-08-06
From what I've heard it seems that nVidia cards are probably the most likely/easiest to get going with a linux box.  The Geforce4 MX cards are darn cheap now too ($30-50) for 256MB of video ram.

Pop one of those in your box and hook up the S-Video to your TV.  Set your resolution low, like around 640x480 or thereabouts so that you can actually read the txt.  As long as you have a decent sized TV (20"+) then you shouldn't have any trouble.
