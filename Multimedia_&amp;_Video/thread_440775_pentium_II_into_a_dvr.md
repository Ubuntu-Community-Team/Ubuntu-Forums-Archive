### Post by josesanders on 2007-05-14
It's difficult to get a MythTV system running with old hardware, and I would bet that most people on this forum would tell you to not even try with such an old machine.  I, however, think it may be possible, depending on your processor speed.  How fast is you machine?

If you are going to try it, I would definately recommend a TV card that does MPEG compression, such as the Hauppauge 150.  Also, if you want to watch your recordings on an actual TV, I recommend getting an NVidia card with TV out.
