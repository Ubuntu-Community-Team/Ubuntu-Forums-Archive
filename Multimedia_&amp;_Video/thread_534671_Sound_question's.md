### Post by kyphi on 2007-08-25
If you right click on the speaker icon on the top panel and then choose 'Open Volume Control', then go to 'Switches' and put a tick into the box next to headphone boost you might get an increase in volume.

Don't forget to adjust  the volume control on your headphones.
