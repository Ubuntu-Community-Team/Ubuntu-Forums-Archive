### Post by IanW on 2007-11-28
The simple answer is to NOT have QDVDAuthor create an ISO image.

1: Have QDVDAuthor output to a VIDEO_TS folder.
2: Open K3b and select "Create video DVD" from it's "Further actions" menu.
3: In K3b's upper window, open the folder QDVDAuthor made.
4: Drag its contents into the VIDEO_TS folder K3b shows in it's lower window.
5: Click"Burn"
