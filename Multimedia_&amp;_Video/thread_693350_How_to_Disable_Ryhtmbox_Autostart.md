### Post by jonkulp on 2008-02-11
Have you checked to see which app is your default media player?  Go to System>Preferences>Preferred Applications, then click on the multimedia tab.  If Rhythmbox is listed as the default player, then that could be the problem.  I haven't used audio devices with Linux but this is the first thing I can think of to check.  Good luck.

---

### Post by HappyFeet on 2008-02-11
go to system-preferences-removable drives and media. then multimedia tab. uncheck the box where rhythmbox is listed.
