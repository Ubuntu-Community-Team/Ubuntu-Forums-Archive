### Post by penvzila on 2007-02-13
There are no 64bit drivers for the audio card.  They have even release Vista drivers which say they are 64bit, but in fact are 32bit.  So, basically you have a notebook manufacturer who will sell you a machine with a 64it processor and an integrated audio device that won't work if you use the computer in 64bit mode.

**At this time, there is an ALSA patch that sort of works, but the audio quality is poor.
