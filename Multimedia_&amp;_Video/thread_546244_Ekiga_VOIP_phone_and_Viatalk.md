### Post by arvinoids on 2008-02-09
I have tried it last night and I have confirmed that Ekiga works with Vonage. Of course, a softphone account is needed for it, since the username and password are provided by them. Check out this link: [http://www.jpugh.org/2008/01/vonage-and-ekiga-on-suse-linux.html](http://www.jpugh.org/2008/01/vonage-and-ekiga-on-suse-linux.html)
And also, remember to set the default to the Vonage account so that the dial-out format will append the proxy.
