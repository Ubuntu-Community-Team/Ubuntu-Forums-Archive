### Post by NET WT on 2007-10-30
I had a similar problem earlier today. I had color for a short time, while playing a video in Totem. But then after a while my video went black and white. Maybe try adjusting the saturation until you have color again. In Totem, under edit>preferences, on the Display tab adjust the saturation bar until color returns. And hopefully it will.
