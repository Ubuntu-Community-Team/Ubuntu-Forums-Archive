### Post by stooshbunutu on 2008-02-21
you need a range of [gstreamer]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/search?suite=all&section=all&arch=any&searchon=names&keywords=gstreamer") and the [lame]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/search?keywords=lame&searchon=names&suite=all&section=all") codec

The links are starting points as I'm not sure for edubuntu where you get codecs and what distros

Try the gstreamer set in add/remove from the applications menu, this installes most likely to be needed ones
