The bin/cue format is for disc images - kinda like an iso. You would normally either burn the image to disc or mount the image directly. I'd recommend k3b for burning.

As for converting the movie in the image to avi - this will largely depend on what format the video is on the image (VCD/SVCD/DVD/etc)
