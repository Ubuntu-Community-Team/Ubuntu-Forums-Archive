after you load a sound, choose 'select all'
from the edit menu...then the fx menu becomes active...
or if you want fx on instruments,
install ladspa fx, ecamegapedal, and creox, then
route the instrument output to ecamegapedal, or creox
input through qjackctl. Hope this helps!
also  /usr/share/audacity  check here to see fx
