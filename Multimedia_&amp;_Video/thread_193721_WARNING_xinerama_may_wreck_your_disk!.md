### Post by malv on 2006-06-10
The following has been probed several times for repeatability:

When running 6.06 with the mga driver, having xinerama on produces an irrecoverable error when pressing the standard xorg [CTL+ALT+BACKSPACE] multikey combination. The xinerama had seemed to work OK up till that point. No way to recover from this. Only re-install from scratch is possible. Many OS-functionalities appear to be gone. I discovered in re-installing that the partition label I had assigned to the 6.06 partition was also gone, this confirmed by independent partitioning software.

Having several other linux distro's on this disk, I also found malfunctions in these which are likely linked to the xinerama ubuntu 6.06 failure. I cannot confirm this linkage. I am quite positive about the previous paragraph as I repeated the re-install 4 times, each time with identical failure.
