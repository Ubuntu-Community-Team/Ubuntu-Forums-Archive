### Post by mbrang00 on 2006-07-22
Well I Was fumbling around and found the answer to my own question, I think i will leave this in here just incase someone else has this issue...


My answer was in the volume control panel...double click the volume icon. Click on edit/preferences,  and make sure you mono output is on. This is where the sub gets it input it seems, raise the slider and hopfully you get sound out of it like I did...


Hope this helps someone in the future...
