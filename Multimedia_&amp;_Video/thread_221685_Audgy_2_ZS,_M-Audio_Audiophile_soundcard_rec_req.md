### Post by dolson on 2006-07-24
I have a regular OEM Audigy2 ZS. What I can tell you is that whenever I start Audacity, it sets the PCM record level up to 98%, which captures everything, and it's annoying that I have to go into alsamixer and continually reset it down to 0. The way that it records analog is very much an annoyance to me, and if I had a choice, I'd rather have an SB Live! 5.1 card for recording. But I don't know if you would have these same issues with the Platinum.

I couldn't ever afford the 2496, so I can't help you there.
