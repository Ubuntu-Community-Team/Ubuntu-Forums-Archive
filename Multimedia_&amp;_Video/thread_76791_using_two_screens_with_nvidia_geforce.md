### Post by polar on 2005-10-16
well...i managed to solve this faster than i though.

here is my new xorg.conf which gives me the desired output.
thanks to another thread i found the error.

it is important to give the "device" attributes in the screen-sections different names.

the bold marked parts are the important ones.

> 
Section "Device"
**	Identifier	"NVIDIA Corporation NV43 [GeForce 6600 GT]"**
	Driver		"nvidia"
	BusID		"PCI:5:0:0"
	Option		"IgnoreEDID" "off"
	Option		"VideoOverlap"	"on"
	Screen 0
EndSection

Section "Device"
	**Identifier	"video1"**
	Driver		"nvidia"
	BusID		"PCI:5:0:0"
	Screen 1
EndSection

Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"SDM-HS73P"
	Option		"DPMS"
EndSection

Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"Samsung"
	Option		"DPMS"
	HorizSync	30-91
	VertRefresh	50-85
EndSection

Section "Screen"
	Identifier	"Screen0"
	Device		"NVIDIA Corporation NV43 [GeForce 6600 GT]"
	Monitor		"SDM-HS73P"
	DefaultDepth	24
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth		16
		Modes		"1280x1024" "1024x768" "800x600" "720x400" "640x480"
	EndSubSection
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth		24
		Modes		"1280x1024" "1024x768" "800x600" "720x400" "640x480"
	EndSubSection
EndSection

Section "Screen"
	Identifier 	"Screen1"
	Device 		"video1"
	Monitor		"Samsung"
	DefaultDepth	24
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth		24
		Modes	 	"1280x960"
	EndSubSection
EndSection

Section "ServerLayout"
**	Identifier	"Multihead Layout"**
	Screen		"Screen0"
	Screen		"Screen1" RightOf "Screen0"
	InputDevice	"Generic Keyboard"
	InputDevice	"Configured Mouse"
	**Option		"Xinerama" "on"**
	Option		"Clone" "off"
EndSection

Section "DRI"
	Group	0
	Mode	0666
EndSection


so, this thread can be closed. :-)
