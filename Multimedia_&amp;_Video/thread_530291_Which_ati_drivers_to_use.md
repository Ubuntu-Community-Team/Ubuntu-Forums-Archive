### Post by tseliot on 2007-08-20
> **benny999 said:**
> hello i have just done a fresh install of Ubuntu um i have a ati x1300 wondering which drivers i should download to enable desktop effects and every thing like that thank you in advance
ATI's proprietary driver "fglrx" doesn't work with the Desktop Effects (AIGLX+Beryl or Compiz).

You will have to use XGL instead of AIGLX (which is rather buggy).

I don't know whether ATI's open source driver "ati" supports your card (with 3d acceleration) (I guess not).
