### Post by ron999 on 2007-12-01
Hi
To make a VCD compliant mpeg1 file with Avidemux.
Open your file
Then use Auto > VCD
The boxes down the left hand side will be:-
VCD
Configure
Filters

TwoLame
Configure
Filters

MPEG PS A+V

Then save it.

It's no use setting it to DVD if you're trying to make a VCD.
