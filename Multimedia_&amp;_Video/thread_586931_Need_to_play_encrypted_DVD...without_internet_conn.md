### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-10-22
Unless you can get hold of files etc through the internet or alternatively from other sources, you will, I think, be unlucky and unable to play encrypted DVDs.

For possible future use, when you can, download a copy of geexbox, a small linux distro specially for media playing which seems to be able to handle anything I've thrown at it.  Burn it to a CD and boot from it.  It's a live CD but once loaded it ejects it's CD and leaves the DVD drive available for DVDs or CDs or whatever.  Great little prog for the times when you can't get your own computer to play disks.
