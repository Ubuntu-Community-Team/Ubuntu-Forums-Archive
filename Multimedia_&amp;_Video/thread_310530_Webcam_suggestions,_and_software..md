### Post by jameswilhelm on 2006-12-01
Hi, Andrew.

I have used the following webcams on Ubuntu:

[LIST]
[*]Philips Vesta 675 - the little fatboy cam.
[*]Logitech QuickCam Express 2
[/LIST]

Ubuntu (Hoary, I think it was) was the first distribution on which the Philips webcam just worked - really - no kernel recompiling, and other silly nonsense, no politics. This was one of the Ubuntu selling points for me at that time.

The Logitech used to freeze Ubuntu Hoary, so the driver must have been there but just did not work on both my PCs. It has been fine since at least Breezy.

Both cameras work nicely with Ekiga. 

I think the Philips isn't available anymore, but I think the Logitech is still around in some form or another.

I hope this helps.

James
