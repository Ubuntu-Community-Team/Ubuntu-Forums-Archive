### Post by Tur Third on 2007-07-31
On further investigation, it looks like Rythmbox does not read mp3 with a bit rate of 128kbps correctly (nothing to do with file formats). I have changed sound juicer to rip at 160kbps. 

The same CD ripped at this rate now appears under 'artists' rather then 'unknown' in Rhythmbox. Looks like I will need to re-do the others.
