### Post by KenLin on 2007-09-15
MTP is a microsoft-specific transfer protocol. There should be a settings menu on the clix where you can switch the usb mode to MSC, which is drive-mode. Then it should show up OK on linux when you connect it.

Let me know if this works as I'm eyeing a Clix as well.

### Post by michael_teter on 2007-11-05
NEW INFO here.

Despite not finding how (or if it's possible) to switch my player out of MTP mode, I discovered that Rhythmbox does indeed support MTP, it's just not obvious.

In Rhythmbox, go to Edit->Plugins... and click "Portable Players - MTP".  And Bam, there's my player!
