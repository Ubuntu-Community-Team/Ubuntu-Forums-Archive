### Post by vanadium on 2007-06-13
If you want to retain the full quality, then your only option is to copy the DVD as is. DVD Video can be recoded (slow) or transcoded (relatively fast) to occupy less disk space, but as always, the more you compress, the lower the quality. Stronger compression can be achieved using mpeg4, and when properly done, near DVD quality is achieved, not a quality "worse than your old VHS". It all depends on the skills and the judgement of the person doing the transcoding.
