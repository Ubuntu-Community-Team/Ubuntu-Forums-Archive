### Post by montydonsheaddoctor on 2007-11-30
The simple way to cure this is to switch of desktop effects. Go to System_Preferences_Appearance and then click the visual effects tab and select None from the list and apply. You will lose the 3D effects though.:-|

Or another simple way to solve this one that I found is to ditch Limewire and download Frostwire instead its almost exactly the same program as far as I can see.:razz:
