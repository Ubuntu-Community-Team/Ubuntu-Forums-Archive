### Post by Scunizi on 2007-03-23
Could you post your xorg.conf file for me?  I'm also trying to help someone else set up twinview on an edgy system and would like to see how you've implemented it.  Also, are you running edgy or Dapper or something else?

As for your resolution if you are using a standard aspect ratio monitor (lcd) the native resolution is 1280x1024 and you won't be able to get more out of it.  Twinview should automatically generate 2560 x 1024 and spread everything across both monitors providing they are the same.  If it's a wide screen, I don't know what the native resolution would be but it should function similiar to what I mentioned before.  If you don't want to spread "one" screen across two monitors you might look at implementing xinerama instead.  It will give you two unique monitors so you can open a program on one and the other independantly, drag from one side to the next etc.  It's just another option.
