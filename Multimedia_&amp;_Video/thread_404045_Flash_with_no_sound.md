### Post by deadgobby on 2007-04-07
If you are using a sound card like Sound Blaster. Ubie see your on board sound card and the other sound card. To resolve this issue is very simple. Reboot your system and go into your BIOS. Disable your on board sound card and save your new BIOS settings. Boot up the system and all sound should flow through your sound card.
