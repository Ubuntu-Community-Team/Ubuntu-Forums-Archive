### Post by frefactory on 2007-10-22
Hi,

By default, the download directory for podcasts is the /Podcasts folder in your home directory. You can change this location in Rhythmbox  when you go to EDIT - PREFERENCES - PODCASTS. There you can set the default directory to be your iPod Nano instead. Then download the podcasts you're subscribed to and bob's your uncle. 

If you want the podcasts to be downloaded onto your harddrive AND your iPod Nano, I'm afraid you will have to make a tiny copy script. Or you could try and see if you can add a second Podcasts location by use of gconf-editor. I know that in a similar way, you can watch over more than one library directory, but you have to fill it out manually.

Cheers.
