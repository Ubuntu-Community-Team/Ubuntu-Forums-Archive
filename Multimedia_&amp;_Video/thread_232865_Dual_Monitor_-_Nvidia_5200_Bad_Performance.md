I just followed a great how to on here for setting up Dual Monitors. Didn't have a single problem following the instructions. I'm using the nvidia driver with a 5200 GeForce 256 PCI card. My motherboard has a integrated intel video adapter which I disabled. 

I've noticed a big performance impact with dual monitors. Like scrolling thru my bookmarks in Firefox is real slow, programs are slower, I can drag one window from one monitor to the other and it leaves a trail. It displays all the traits as if my processor was running at 100%. I checked the cpu utilization out and its running at 5-10% max. 

How can I increase performance?
