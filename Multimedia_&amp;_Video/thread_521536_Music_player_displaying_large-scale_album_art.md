### Post by fwwarr on 2007-08-12
gmusicbrowser is a great music player that supports custom layouts. This means that a layout can be made to display a very large album art image. By default in fullscreen mode the album art is huge. There are many layouts available as well as user contributed layouts. I think I saw one that shows quite large album art in the normal window, but you can always make your own so that it looks exactly the way you want.... Actually I've made a layout that is loosely based on WMP11, I could modify it to add large album art. I'll post it here when I'm done.
