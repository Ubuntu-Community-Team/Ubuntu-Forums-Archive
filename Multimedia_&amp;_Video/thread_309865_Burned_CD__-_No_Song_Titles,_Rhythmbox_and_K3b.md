### Post by mickeythecat on 2006-12-02
Still have not figured out how to get the "tags" on burned Cd's; however I did figure out the very last part of my post about linking Amarok with K3b.  If you want to burn a CD of your Amarok playlist, you have to first add the "burn" button in Amarok by going to Settings / Configure Toolbars and adding "burn".  Now you you have a button to press in Amarok.  Note that all that does is open up K3b and place you Amarok playlist in K3b.  Which you could have done easily enough by opening up K3b yourself and dragging the files there.

In any case, my problem is still there.  I cannot get the "tags" to appear on audio CDs that are burned.  Tags are critical if you want to get the music to a ipod or nano via a Mac and be able to see real titles and other details.

If anyone has a solution, please let me know.  I am giving up for now.
