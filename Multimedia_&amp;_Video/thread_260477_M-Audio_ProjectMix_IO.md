### Post by stevejesus on 2008-01-27
I'm a little late replying, but I would guess that the MIDI interface would work with no problems on most apps (Hydrogen, Ardour, etc..) that follow the MIDI standard.
This means that all the mixer dials, start/stop and most other basic controls will work.  Other than that, I think you are out of luck.
But to answer your question, drivers should be unnecessary.
