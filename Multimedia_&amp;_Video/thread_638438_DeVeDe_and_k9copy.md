### Post by ron999 on 2007-12-12
Hi
K9copy and DeVeDe are different animals.
K9copy is the one to use for making backup DVD copies on 4.7GB DVDs. And you can use K3b to burn it.
DeVeDe is meant to be used to make (author) a DVD from a video file.
