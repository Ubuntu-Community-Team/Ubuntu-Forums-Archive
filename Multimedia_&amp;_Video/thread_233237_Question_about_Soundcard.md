### Post by mpvano on 2006-08-12
I haven't tried this one, but USB sound devices generally work quite well with Dapper.

The only problem is that they can be confusing to operate because:

1. It can be confusing to deal with a second sound card. Many programs are hard to convince to use anything but the first card they find.

2. Most USB sound adapters do NOT have the usual mixer controls and this can be confusing to understand sometimes.

Having said that, I own about half a dozen different sorts of USB sound devices from very simple to very complex and am quite pleased with how they all work on a variety of Ubuntu installations on several notebooks, desktops and even rack mount server computers here.
