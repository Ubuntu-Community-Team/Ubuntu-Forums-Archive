### Post by DarthMandeep on 2006-06-13
I haven't used either of those cards, but I used a GeForce 4200 with XGL for some time and it worked fine. It would probably be cheaper than the 5500 if you can find it.

Currently I'm using a 7600GT and this works great. The 7600GS model is cheaper and based on the same hardware, so it should also work very well for XGL.

The GeForce 4 Ti series seems like the cheapest way to get an Nvidia-based XGL setup. If you go that route, be certain not to purchase the MX models, as they are nothing more than GeForce 2 cores.
