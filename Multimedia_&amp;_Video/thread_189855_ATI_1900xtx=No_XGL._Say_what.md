### Post by yatt on 2006-06-23
[QUOTE=Visceral Monkey]So I downloaded Dapper and prepare to install when I heard from someone on another forum that my top of the line 1900xtx video card won't run it with XGL support? What the hell? Is this correct? Am I XGL hosed?[/QUOTE]
Yup, your hosed. ATI does not yet make a driver that supports your card, and will not allow anyone else to modify their driver (fglrx) so that it will support it. It took ATI about 8 months to make a driver that supported my card. Yours should take less time, as the your card has the same basic internal archatecture.

A FOSS driver (radeon) is being worked on. It has 3D and (from what I've heard) XGL support, but only works for certain older cards (9500 and the like).
