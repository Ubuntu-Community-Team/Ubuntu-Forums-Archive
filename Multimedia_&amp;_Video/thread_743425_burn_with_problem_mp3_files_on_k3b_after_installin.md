### Post by process91 on 2008-04-08
You should be burning the disc as a data CD. It depends on many factors, such as bitrate and size. You can find out by selecting those 100 songs in Nautilus or Konqueror (the file browsers) and seeing how many MBs they are. A CD will hold 700MB, regardless if it is one song or 500 songs.
