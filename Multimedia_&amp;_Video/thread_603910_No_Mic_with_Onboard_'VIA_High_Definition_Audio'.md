### Post by NJC on 2007-11-15
I'm using Gutsy with an Ensoniq 1371. Go to Sound icon top right, rick click on Open Volume Control, then Edit/Preferences. Select all of the Mic boxes. 

In Audacity I have Mic:0 selected and that FINALLY provides proper volume.

Scroll up in this thread [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=3774427#post3774427](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=3774427#post3774427)
