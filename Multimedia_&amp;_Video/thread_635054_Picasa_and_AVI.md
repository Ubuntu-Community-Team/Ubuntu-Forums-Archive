The linux version of Picasa still runs under wine. It is packaged with wine to make it linux 'compatible'.  There are linux native alternatives such as Digikam and f-spot.
I just checked with Digikam on my box (gutsy gnome) and movies are played fine. For that you need to change "Show embedded preview" to "open with image editor"  in --> Settings --> Album Settings --> Thumbnail click action    of Digikam.

So please have a look to the native alternatives.
