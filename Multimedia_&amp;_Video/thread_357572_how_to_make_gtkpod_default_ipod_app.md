### Post by RomeReactor on 2007-02-09
Hi. Maybe this will help: Open **System-->Preferences-->Removable Drives and Media**, click on the **Multimedia** tab, and in the "Command" section of **Portable Music Players** write **gtkpod**. See if that brings up the application when you connect your ipod.
