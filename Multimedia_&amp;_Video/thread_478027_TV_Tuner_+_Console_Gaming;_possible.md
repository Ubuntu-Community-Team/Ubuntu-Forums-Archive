### Post by crispy_420 on 2007-06-19
You have a second or two delay due to the hardware encoder in the card. It is the same on Windows too with Media Center and SageTV. There is no way around it with your current card since it is the driver. You just want to watch TV and not record, MythTV in your future?

But the good news is one of those cheap cards based on the bttv chipset are in real time. And as a bonus they are cheap, think less than $40(US). You can't record with them but they will meet your needs.
