### Post by timzak on 2008-03-17
My first guess would be an off-balance disc.  Try a different disc...just try inserting and reading it.  If it sounds normal, then try another write function and see if it happens again.  Make sure there are no labels on the disc---they tend to throw off the balance much like when a lead weight comes off your car wheel and it loses its balance.
