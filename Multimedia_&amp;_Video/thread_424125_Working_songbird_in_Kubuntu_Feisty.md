Hey campers,

After some hiccups, I now have a working copy of Songbird running on my Kubuntu Feisty system, and I'd like to share my hints.

I first installed Songbird using Automatix. Songbird would start fine, but when trying to play a playlist from a music blog, the player would be silent and would skip to the next song after only a couple seconds. I tried re-installing and rebooting to no avail.

I then found that my Kubuntu installation was missing a lot of required packages, as listed at the Songbird site [here]("http://publicsvn.songbirdnest.com/trac/wiki/SettingUpGStreamer"). I installed all the required and recommended packages for Ubuntu Dapper, restarted Songbird and... still no dice. This time, I was getting an "error" message showing up in the player window, as well as the other symptoms.

I then un-installed and re-installed Songbird using Automatix and voila! It works!

Now I can talk about new music like all the cool kids.
