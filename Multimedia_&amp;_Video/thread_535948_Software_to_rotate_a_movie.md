### Post by Igniteflow on 2008-05-11
I've just done this for a video I made with my Canon camera too.  Here's a how to:
1.  Open the video with Avidemux
2.  Select the video format you would like the finished video to be (Xvid etc) from the drop down menu 
3.  Select the audio (I chose MP3)
4.  Click on filters, double click rotate and select the angle you want to rotate the video, click close
5.  Now click "Save" and choose a filename.  Avidemux will now create a new movie file with the adjustments you requested in the home folder, unless specified otherwise.  The original will be left untouched.

Note - It's important to do step 3, without it I had a loud clicking noise in my rotated video.
