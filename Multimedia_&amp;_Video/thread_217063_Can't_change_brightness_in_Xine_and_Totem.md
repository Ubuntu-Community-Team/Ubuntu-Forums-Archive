### Post by orb9220 on 2006-07-17
Go to xine setup make sure the first tab for config experience level is set to expert. click apply then click on video tab first entry is video driver to use change auto to xshm hit apply shutdown xine restart.

You now have control on contrast,brightness,etc.

Hope this helped.
