I have a Novation Remote SL 61 which I use with Kubuntu and Rosegarden with softsynths. Linux detects it with no problems and all controllers also work with no problems (JACK even recognises it as a "ReMOTE SL" in the connections window). I wish that Rosegarden could respond to all those controllers that this keyboard has! It can control Volume, Pan of each track plus a couple of others that aren't that useful. It also responds to play/stop/continue from the transport buttons.

I would say go for it, the Remote SL can be configured to transmit anything anyway (if you can spend time programming each control) and the MIDI support in Linux will only get better with time.
