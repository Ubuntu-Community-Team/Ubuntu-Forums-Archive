### Post by Elif Tymes on 2007-02-22
So, I'm an Avid gamer, I like listening to music, and I love playing my 360. I also just bought a house and am moving out on my own.

I'm no stranger to linux, I've been working with it for the past couple years with servers, but now I want to make it my desktop enviroment. The arrival of Vista seems like the ideal portent of chagne, and before I jumped onto that (Hah!) I wanted to see how an OpenSource alternative works. This will very likely be my household media hub for the next few years.

ALL of my music is stored @ 192Kb/s MP3s (I find this format to be the most flexible from a Portability/audio quality/storage size perspective. I'm all about flexibility!)

[SIZE="4"]Goals[/SIZE]

[SIZE="3"]1) Be able to stream Audio/Video to my XBox 360[/SIZE]
a) Utilize uShare?
b) Other Streaming software?

[SIZE="3"]2) Be compatible with a large amount of Windows games.[/SIZE]
a)Cedega?
b)Wine?
c)Maychance a Virtual Machine With WinXP?
d)Crossover?

[SIZE="3"]3) Organize my Audio/Video collection quickly and effectively, as well as distribute it to my iPod AND/OR PSP[/SIZE]
a)GTKPod
b)[QT PSP Manager]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/qpspmanager/")
c) Avarok?
d) KDMedia Player?

[SIZE="3"]4) Shared storage drive across my local network, accessible from work.[/SIZE]
a) Simple SAMBA Sharing for the home network
b) SCP sharing for everywhere else.

[SIZE="3"]5) Simple, Elegant, and darn sexy desktop enviroment[/SIZE]
a) KDE is my first choice.
b) Beryl? 

[SIZE="3"]6) Wireless Keyboard/Mouse[/SIZE]
a) Microsoft Wireless 5000?
b) Logitech G7
c) Nintendo Wiimote?

[SIZE="3"]7) Remotely accessible[/SIZE]
a) VNC?
b) SSHd
