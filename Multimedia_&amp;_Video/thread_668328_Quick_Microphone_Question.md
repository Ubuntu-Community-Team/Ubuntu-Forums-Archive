### Post by gobuntu on 2008-01-18
Hi Kussie,

In Applications/Sound and Video/Sound Recorder you get the options:

Record From: Mix, Microphone, etc ....

Pick Microphone if you have an analog Mic plugged in, and Mix will also record theanalog microphone along with other sound sources as per the sound mixer.

If using a USB-mic, it's another issue. I am still trying to Mix a USB-mic with no luck yet.

I actually use Audacity for recording sound. But one still needs to tell the Recording Application which Recording Device to use.
