### Post by FlyingCheese on 2007-04-22
My laptop is plugged into an amp and speakers through the headphone output. On windows, the Master Volume would change the headphone output volume as well as the master volume and I like this functionality so I can use my media keys for volume/muting. Currently pressing the media keys adjusts the master volume which does nothing unless I'm using the laptops speakers (ie: crap).

How can I make the master volume control the headphone output as well, like how it is in Windows?

### Post by avb on 2007-04-22
> **FlyingCheese said:**
> My laptop is plugged into an amp and speakers through the headphone output. On windows, the Master Volume would change the headphone output volume as well as the master volume and I like this functionality so I can use my media keys for volume/muting. System->Preferencies->Sound

In 'Default mixer Tracks' select 'PCM' or 'Master' and 'Headphones'. 

this will do the trick for sure
