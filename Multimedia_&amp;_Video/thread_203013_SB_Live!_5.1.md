### Post by woedend on 2006-06-24
go to the terminal and type: alsamixer

use your arrows to go side to side.  push the 'm' key to toggle on/off.
You would need the value Tone to be ON (green box) for bass/treble to take effect.  Digital output should be 5th from the far right(scroll way over)
let me know if this helps you.
