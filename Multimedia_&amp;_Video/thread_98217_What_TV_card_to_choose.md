### Post by qrazi on 2005-12-03
if you want to pause you need xawtv or mythtv.

tvtime onlyshows the frames, while mythtv actually records it to the harddrive, and then play the recorded frames. this is why you can pause in mythtv. windows MCE works in the same way, it records in a modified mpeg2 format.

For normal viewing, tv time is indeed more then enough, but pausing, recording, scheduling etc. you have to go with mythtv.

as for the card. i believe the hauppauges do work with mythtv, and with the pvr150 you get a remote control. i am not sure wether linux supports the fm tuner of the mce150.
