### Post by conjur3r on 2007-03-18
If you're after budget, I honestly cannot see any issues with using the onboard sound card most motherboards come with.  It is only when you wish to go high end where you would then need to spend money.

That said, stay away from the Sound Blaster Audigy 4 as drivers for them aren't officially out yet.  You could purchase a Sound Blaster Audigy 2 as this works quite well.
