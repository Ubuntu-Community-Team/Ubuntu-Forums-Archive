### Post by kyphi on 2007-11-30
Assuming that you are using Ubuntu (I know very little of Kubuntu), to make VLC the default player for DVDs proceed as follows.

Go to your Home folder and open 'Examples' and right click on 'Experience ubuntu.ogg', then go to 'Properties' and 'Open with'.  You will see that it is selected to open with Movie Player (Totem).  Select 'Add' and from the drop down list choose VLC.

I would recommend that you keep Movie Player - you may change your mind about VLC one day and Movie Player 'does not eat much hay'.
