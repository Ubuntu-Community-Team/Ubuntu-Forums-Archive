### Post by warp99 on 2008-04-10
On your frontend setup go to Utilites/Setup > Setup > TV Settings > Playback then to screen 8/9 and turn on automatic commercial skipping.

### Post by fenian on 2008-04-10
Mythtv only flags the commercials (marks where the breaks are) as it records a show.When you play back a recorded show you can skip over the commercial by pressing the z key.If you transcode the show you can cut the commercials out entirely (this will also greatly reduce the file size),instructions for doing this are[ here.]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=346778&highlight=transcode")
