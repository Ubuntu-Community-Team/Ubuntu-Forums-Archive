### Post by Jimlas53 on 2007-10-21
Hi Flexgrip,
I use live365 every day.  The best (for me) is to install xmms, which can play .pls files.
Log onto Live365, pick a station and  and when you click on a "play" button firefox will put up a dialog box asking what you want to do with the file.  Select "Open With", click other, enter /usr/bin/xmms.  Firefox will launch xmms whenever you open a .pls file.
You can also make shortcuts, or bookmarks, to his favorite stations and stick them on the toolbar.

Hope this helps!
