### Post by RedGhost on 2007-06-04
All these are in the repositories:

xmms     - very good winamp-like media player, good for radio streams and mp3s.
vlc           -  one of the best video players out there, plays nearly anything.
mplayer - another good video player, download the full codecs pack and extract them to usr/lib/win32 and you can play nearly anything, even those Windows codecs that vlc fails to play.

### Post by ynnhoj on 2007-06-04
as mentioned, a good winamp clone is xmms.  two derivatives of xmms that, imo have turned out much better than xmms, are beep-media-player and audacious.  i'm not sure if audacious is in feisty's repository, but beep should be.

on the whole though, i think mplayer is the one application that can do the most (providing you know how to use it :)).  another good player is gxine.  both are in the repositories.  also, keep in mind that you might need to get some codecs still.
