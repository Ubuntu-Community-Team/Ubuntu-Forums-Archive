### Post by bom28 on 2007-10-30
> 1 The system of putting a check next to songs you want played
Some players have a system of queue, where adding a song to the queue will have a similar effect. My favorite player, and for some reason not well-known, gmusicbrowser allows you to double-click on a song with the middle mouse button to enqueue it, which put a queue icon on the row if you added the column for that.
Of course it is a bit harder to remove them from the queue than to uncheck the song.

> 2 When you press the first letter in the name it will bring you to it, not via a search but within the listing.
I'm sure quite a few players have that feature, gmusicbrowser has it at least. :)
