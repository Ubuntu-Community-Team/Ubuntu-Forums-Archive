### Post by prezbedard on 2008-03-22
What I found I had to do was do a right click copy the url then open up mplayer and do an open location. It seems to work in mplayer ok but not the plug-in strange but it works. I'm watching now. I do find this happens often on windows codec based streams even though I have the plug-in installed.

good luck

I should also note every so often it gets interrupted. just do an open location again.
