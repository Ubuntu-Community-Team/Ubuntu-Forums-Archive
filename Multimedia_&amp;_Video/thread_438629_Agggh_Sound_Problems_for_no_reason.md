### Post by takethepain on 2007-05-09
Ubuntu was going along swimmingly for me. I was genuinely enjoying it much more than WindowsXP. Then all of a sudden, for no reason what-so-ever I got sound troubles. Aamorak wouldn't open....Any other media player that I used to play either .mp3 or ogg files would freeze when I pushed play. Going into the sound panel and pressing the Test buttons would lead the sound panel to freeze. Attempting to play Youtube videos would crash my web browser. To the best of my knowledge I did nothing to bring this on! 

After fiddling around in the Sound panel and changing the driver from ALSA to OSS, while Aamorak still wouldn't open I could play mp3's in other media players. I could still not use you tube without freezing my browser.  Can someone please tell me what is going on?

### Post by takethepain on 2007-05-10
Okay I found the solution. For some reason selecting sounds to play in login/logout  and login failed in the Login Window Preferences under the tab 'Accessibility' was the cause of all my problems. This seems like a pretty big bug to me....
