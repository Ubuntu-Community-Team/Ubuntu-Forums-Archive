### Post by agim on 2008-02-04
If this is withing Firefox you can set your preferences in Edit->Preferences->Content->Manage file types.

If you want this to be system wide go to the top of your screen, enter System->Preferences->Preferred applications and choose mplayer as your multimedia preference.
Of course, this means that mplayer will be preferred for all multimedia.
