### Post by Sord_Fish on 2007-01-11
Just though i'd post and let people know how i "fixed" my no sound problem.

I had no sound on anything apart from Totem. nautilus preview didnt work and i had no boot up sounds.

Then i looked in System > preferences > sound. they were all set to auto detect, i changed it to my sound card (VIA  8237) and everything is working again.

### Post by deadgobby on 2007-01-12
If you have a sound card like Sound Blaster 16 for example and then your on board sound card. You may need to go into BIOS and turn off the on board sound card. When the system boots up all sound should head for the sound card. Then use ALSA to bring up volume levels to your liking. I use GNOME ALSA myself.
