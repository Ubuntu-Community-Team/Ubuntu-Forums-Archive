### Post by {BzF}~JOKesTER on 2008-03-13
Ok First Do This:

{You Need To Be Connected To The Internet}

Open A Terminal

Type:

apt-get autoremove  

And Hit Enter -{If Theres An Option Type Y and Hit Enter}

Now Type:

apt-get update

And Hit Enter

Now Press Y And Then Press Enter -{The Files Will Download -DON'T CLOSE THE TERMINAL!!!!}

Ok Now After Thats Done - Restart Your Computer

When Its Back On - Login And Open The "Movie Player" -{Assuming Its Totem}

Now Try And Play A Video,

Check Both The Codec Boxes - And Install Them.

Thats It!!!!:)
