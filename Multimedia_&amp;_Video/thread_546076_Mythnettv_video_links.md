[SIZE="3"]What is mythnettv?[/SIZE]

Mythnettv is a VOD (video on demand) / podcast collecter for mythtv made by Mikal over at stillhq and can be found here;

[MYTHNETTV]("http://stillhq.com/mythtv/mythnettv/")

Mythnettv allows the user to suscribe to their favorite podcast or whatever and it will then download any shows / episodes, transcode them if needed and insert them into your mythtv recorded tv folder with all the required metadata!! cool eh?
