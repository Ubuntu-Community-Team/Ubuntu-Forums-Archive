### Post by Vadi on 2008-01-31
Oh. The normal radeon driver is easy to do, that's what I use too..

Just do "gksudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf", then search for a section "device" or something similar - make sure it says something ATI Radeon in there, And in there, make sure it says

Driver		"radeon"

If it says something else for the driver, change it to radeon.
