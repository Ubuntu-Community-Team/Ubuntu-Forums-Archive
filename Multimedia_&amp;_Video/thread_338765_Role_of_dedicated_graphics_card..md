Will having a dedicated graphics card of say 128MB or 256MB increase the performance when viewing flash heavy Web site? 2) What kind of specification of a dedicated graphics card should I look for if I want it to support high definition video (up to 1080p).

### Post by zgornel on 2007-01-16
1) 100% processor issue. Flash does not use opengl functions, only processor so, it's a processor issue. Check the flash-opengl player here :D [http://www.globfx.com/](http://www.globfx.com/) .
2,3) A Geforce 7600GS (256MB ram)is a good card and the price is ok. Photo editing does not require a powerful graphics card, just a very good processor and lots, lots of memory so invest more in these last two rather than into a graphics card (especially if you don not play games).
