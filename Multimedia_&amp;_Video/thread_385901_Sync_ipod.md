### Post by volksolwagen57 on 2007-04-23
do this:
> sudo apt-get install gtkpod
gtkpod isn't pretty at all, but you can import, extract, make playlists and easily sync your ipod with your computer's music files without adding duplicate songs. it's as easy as plugging in your ipod, connect to it using gtkpod, adding your music directory and pressing the save button. it is really a piece of cake. do it. i dare you ;-).
