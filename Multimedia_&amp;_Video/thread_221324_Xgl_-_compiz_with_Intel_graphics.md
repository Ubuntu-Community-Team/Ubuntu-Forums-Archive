### Post by reacocard on 2006-07-22
i doubt XGL will work, but AIGLX might. i have i915 graphics and it works excellently with AIGLX, but not at all with XGL. Also, use the compiz-vanilla packages. they run a little faster. gset compiz also lets you adjust the quality of its rendering so it runs better. i run compiz using 'fast' and 20, and i see virtually no difference in quality from the default 'good' and 60. it just uses less cpu.
