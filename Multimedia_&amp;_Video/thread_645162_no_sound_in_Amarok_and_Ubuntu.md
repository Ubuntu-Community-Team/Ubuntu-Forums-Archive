### Post by AlasdairMacLeod on 2007-12-26
I Found out what was wrong :D 

The problem was due to me having 2 sound cards in my machine.  

1st card was the motherboard sound card and the 2nd one was a pci 7.1 card

I removed the PCI card and everything worked fine
