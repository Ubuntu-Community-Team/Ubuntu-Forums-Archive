### Post by Udibuntu on 2008-04-04
Hi,

Please note - Iriver players are not Linux compatible. It says so on their website (ask their assistance applet and you'll see).

The most painful issue when trying to operate Iriver players with Linux distro's is that when you transfer album audio files from your Linux PC to the player, files will be stored in the player in what would seem to you as a random order in each album. This means songs will not play in the order they usually play on the CD.

This is very annoying. The reason is the player places the songs in the order they were transfered to it via USB from the PC, not in alphanumeric order.

This problem can be resolved with the use of shell scripts and rsync order. 

I have an Iriver T60 which I love, I just reboot to XP when managing my portable collection...:(
