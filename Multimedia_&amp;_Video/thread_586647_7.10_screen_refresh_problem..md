### Post by AceRimmer on 2007-10-29
Here is how I fixed it.  I looked up the vertical and horizontal max refresh rates for my monitor and entered them into the xorg.conf file under the monitor section.  Then when I restarted the X server I got the option to select the resolution from Monitor and Graphics, which would then tell me I had to reboot.  But once I rebooted I'd get the failsafe x server and be stuck in low graphics mode!  Well what I finally figured out was that I needed to load the modified xorg.conf file, then use Screen Resolution to select the proper resolutions and refresh, and not mess with the Monitor and Graphics options window at all.  Now its running in 1280x1024 @ 75Hz like a charm.
