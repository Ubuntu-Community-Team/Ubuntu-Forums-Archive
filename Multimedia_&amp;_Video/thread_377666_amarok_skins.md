### Post by rainwalker on 2008-02-21
Amarok is a KDE app and uses Qt instead of GTK (which GNOME uses). To change the way the controls look you would have to download a KDE theme rather than a GNOME/GTK one. I think you can install "kcontrol" and it will let you tweak everything about your KDE apps, including their themes.
