### Post by markballermann on 2008-01-07
I had the same problem with videos coming up grey that would play in color on other pcs. Found that (in totem) going into edit>preferences>display tab and then adjusting the 'saturation' slider to the right (was turned all the way down somehow). 
This fixed the problem! Hopefully your problem is the same?
Also, these prefs seem to also affect mplayer (grey before, color after) so maybe they affect vlc too.
