### Post by stooshbunutu on 2008-02-19
You mentioned that mov is one of the desired formats. For .mov I use a program called [winff]("http://biggmatt.com/files/winff-0.33-i386.deb") that uses the [ffmpeg]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/gutsy/graphics/ffmpeg") codec to covert the file.

hope this helps you, it works great for me.:)
