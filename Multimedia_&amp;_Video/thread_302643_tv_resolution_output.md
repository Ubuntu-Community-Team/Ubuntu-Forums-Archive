### Post by NT4usB on 2006-11-20
What kind of plug do you plug into your video card that goes to your TV? Is your monitor also connected at the same time?
If you're just connecting your TV and nothing else, it's either svideo (round plug with pins in it), Composite (like a stereo plug) or Component (3 stereo plugs, **[COLOR="Red"]R[/COLOR]**ed, **[COLOR="Lime"]G[/COLOR]**reen, **[COLOR="RoyalBlue"]B[/COLOR]**lue) or (doubtful) 15 pin connector just like a monitor.

There are a ton of posts on how to configure TVout using ATI.
look [here]("http://www.google.com/search?num=30&hl=en&lr=&q=site%3Aubuntuforums.org+configure+tvout+%2BATI+%2B7500&btnG=Search") for starters:

**_Before you go messing with your xorg, back it up and know how to restore it from a black screen, using a terminal and a non GUI editor like vi or nano_**

If the TV is the only connected monitor, then your monitor section needs more info for the TV. Something like this:

Section "Monitor"
Identifier "Q71B-8" #or whatever you want to call it
HorizSync 30.0 - 50.0
VertRefresh 60.0 - 60.0
EndSection


Section "Screen"
....
*may need some options to tell the video card how to treat it*
...
Option "TVStandard" "HD480i"
Option "ConnectedMonitor" "Q71B-8"
...

_This is all from memory **and** for my nvidia card so before you start editing your xorg, read some of the other posts in the above link_
