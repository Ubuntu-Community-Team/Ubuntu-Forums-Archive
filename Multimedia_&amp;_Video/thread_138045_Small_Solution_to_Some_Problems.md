### Post by Ubuntist on 2006-03-01
Here's a little something that worked for me.  Maybe it will help someone else.

For a long time, despite much effort, I had little luck with sound.  At one point it worked, but then mysteriously stopped working.

After a few months of living without sound, I thought I'd simply try using a different media player.  So I tried XMMS instead of Rhythmbox -- and IT WORKED!  :) 

About three days later, I hibernated.  Later, I restarted Windows, having forgotten that I had hibernated Linux.  Sound went away again.  :(

Then I opened the volume control and selected Edit | Preferences and selected Audigy Analog/Digital Output Jack (I have an Audigy sound card) as one of the tracks to be visible.  And voila -- sound works again!  :)
