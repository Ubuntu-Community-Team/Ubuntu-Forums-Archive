### Post by bdg0584 on 2005-12-15
I don't know if your problem has been solved, and I am not an *nix expert, however in the hardware world, there is a wire that runs from your cd-rom drive to your sound card.  This wire is usually used to play cds straight from the drive.  That way there is no load to the other hardware of the system.  It is a simple 4 wire cable.  I would check to see if that is hooked up.

Good luck.
