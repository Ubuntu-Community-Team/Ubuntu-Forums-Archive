### Post by kebes on 2007-05-09
I've been told that Kaffeine can be used as a good video library software. However what I use is MythTV. In addition to recording from a TV signal, MythTV can be used just to play a video library off of the hard drive (and can be used to play your music collection, can output other things to TV, like weather and news, etc.)
