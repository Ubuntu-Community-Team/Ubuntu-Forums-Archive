If you want to game you'll need a different card. I personally would suggest a NVidia based card. I have an ATI that got passed down when I bought a newer one.

A good test of if your card is game worthy is running glxgears. If you get bad fps or it looks choppy, time to look a different card for gaming.

As a side note NVidia based cards have gotten pretty cheap. You could easily pick up a decent card fro less than $100.
