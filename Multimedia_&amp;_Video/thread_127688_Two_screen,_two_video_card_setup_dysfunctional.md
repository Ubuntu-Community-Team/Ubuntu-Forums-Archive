### Post by mikeytag on 2006-02-10
Merry,
Your problem lies in the fact that you have this line under the device section of your AGP card:
Driver		"nv"
Your PCI card on the other hand has this line:
Driver      "nvidia"

You can do one of two things. Change the PCI line to be the same as the AGP line (change it from "nvidia" to "nv") However if you want to get the best performance out of your cards then I would load Synaptic Package Manager and install nvidia-glx Then sudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf and change both lines to read "nvidia". I bet you don't have the nvidia-glx driver installed and that is why X dies when you use your PCI card.
