### Post by rvandam on 2006-12-05
Ok, I've got this partially figured out.  If you right click on the volume app then you can use ctrl+click to select multiple volumes to control with the app.  Then at least you can control the volume outside of whatever program is playing sound.  However, this still doesn't fix the fact that only the master volume responds to the dell multimedia keys.
