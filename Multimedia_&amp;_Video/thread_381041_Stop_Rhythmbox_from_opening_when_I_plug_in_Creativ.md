### Post by airtonix on 2007-03-18
This is simple (at least in dapper 6.10)

[LIST=1]
[*]open up **System -> Preferences -> Removable Drives & Media**
[*]then change the tab-view to Media
[*]Then, look down the bottom of the config window....their should be a tickbox to toggle the use of a program to run when connecting a usb media device...or not to use one at all.[/LIST]Done...i hope
