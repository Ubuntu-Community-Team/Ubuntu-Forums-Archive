### Post by Jessard on 2007-02-10
I used a logitech USB headset on linux without a hitch for a long time, so hopefully that's not your problem (though mine broke just before I installed Ubuntu, so who knows). Microphones can be a real %#)!$ to set up since you have to be sure to set "capture" for just the right part, turn up the volume, mute/unmute the right stuff, etc. Have you tried looking at the settings in alsamixer? You might need to finagle those a bit to get it to work (gnome-sound-properties can give basically the same stuff in a nicer interface, if you have Gnome.)
