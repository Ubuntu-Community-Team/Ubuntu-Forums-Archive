Your modelines (subsection display) are in the wrong order. They shoud be from bigger to smaller.

Also, entering the right vert/horiz refresh rates is important for some screens. Check you monitors manual (or website) for them.

EDIT: furthermore, are you sure than your crt can make 1400x1050 (this is usually a laptop res.) ? If it does not work try 1280x1024 which is a standard res.

### Post by JoWilly on 2006-06-15
This config file is quite messy.

1. remove all modelines, the system will detect what is best according to the vert and horiz rates, so if you force modes you screw everything up.
2. put the exact vert and horiz refresh rates for your screen.
3. on the "modes" line put all the modes you want in _**order of preference**_ (first mode will be used first by the server), and _**remove the forced refresh rate ! **_(i.ex put 1280x1024 and not 1280x1024@60)
