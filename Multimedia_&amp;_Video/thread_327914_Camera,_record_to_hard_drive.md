I think it depends more on the camera than anything else. If it's video-out port allows recording as well as playback (though TBH I've never heard of a camera which does), you should be able to hook it up to a suitable capture device like a capture card or the video-in port of a TV/VIVO card.

However, again depending on your camera, I imagine doing anything like this will put a serious drain on the battery, so an AC adpater would be recommended. But if you're going to buy an SD card anyway then the AC adapter would just be an extravagance.
