### Post by ron999 on 2008-01-05
Hi
FLAC is the best quality. But you may need to install a (free) codec/plugin for Winamp to play them.

It's simplest to go for CD Quality MP3.
(MP3 is not CD quality, but you probably won't be able to tell the difference unless you are using hi-spec speakers and you have hi-spec ears).

### Post by icheyne on 2008-01-05
ron999 is right. FLAC Is lossless (perfect), but the file sizes are huge. You have to be an audio fundamentalist to use it.

I would go for OGG myself. Setting Q6 is transparent (indistinguishable from the real thing) for most people. The only problem is that most of your mates won't have digital audio players that support OGG. If that's a problem then choose 192kbps MP3 instead.
