### Post by prosit on 2007-05-02
I had the same thing on my TV the other day after following the same guide - only wallpaper, no icons on the TV.
My solution (maybe too simple in your case): Change the display setting on the TV. My was default to "Cinema" and the icons was outside the viewing area. Changing it to "Normal" let me see the whole desktop, including panels and icons, on my TV.
Your TV may have different terminology for display settings (16:9, 4:3, Pano...etc). Try it out.
