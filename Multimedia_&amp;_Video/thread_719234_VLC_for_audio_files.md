### Post by mc4man on 2008-03-09
If you go to one of your audio files - right click>properties>open with - you can set default.
For cd's go to  system>pref>removalable blah.blah>multimedia for audio cd's use 
vlc cdda:///dev/scd0  or whatever cd drive is if not scd0
