### Post by shad0w_walker on 2007-08-13
I'm afraid your looking for an impossibility as far as I'm aware. The process of re-encoding the video to DVD format takes a hell of a lot of processing power and time. 

I don't have any better times with my system, the problem has nothing to do with software design, its simply a fundamental limit. Everything takes a certain amount of time to do and something as large and complicated as DVD conversion is no exception.

Sorry to inform you but your hardware has limits. Just because core 2 duos are great at running things fast it doesn't mean they can do things beyond their power. 

Your best hope for speeding things up is that there is a setting DeVeDe that forces mencoder to use both cores of your processor (Not sure if its default or not) otherwise you have to accept that your hardware has its limits like everyone else's.
