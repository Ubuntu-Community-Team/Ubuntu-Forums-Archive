### Post by BuffaloX on 2006-07-04
I had the same problem, just ditched an ATI, and bought Nvidea. 

Couldn't find any tests comparing the two directly. But from my experience with graphics adapters I conclude like the following:

The ATI 9500 was very good, not too far from the top at the time the Radeon 9700. ( that is good for windows... )
The Nvidea 6600GT is about on par with Radeon 9800Pro.
The 6200 is at best about half that, which places it below Radeon 9600.
The Radeon 9600 wasn't much better than 9500.
So all in all, I think Nvidea 6200 isn't much different from your ATI 9500.
Yes it's newer, but you went down 1½ levels.

All in all the 9500 is not vastly supperior, and Nvidea drivers are better.
so you should be able to get about the same performance.

Best of luck. ;)
