### Post by josesanders on 2007-05-14
You definately aren't the only one who has had this problem, but I don't think that anyone has a good solution.  The fact that it is garbled may be a good sign.  With my ATI card, I have TV out until the login screen, and then nothing but a grey screen.  I was never able to fix it.

Where do you live?  A garbled screen may mean that you have the wrong TV standard.  If you are in North America, you need NTSC, but if you are in Europe, you may need PAL.  Also, make sure that your refresh rate is 60 Hz.  If you'll post your xorg.conf file, I will take a look at it.
