### Post by steevc on 2007-12-24
I wouldn't expect that you can record them separately as most sound cards just pass the inputs to an internal mixer with a single audio feed going to the PC. What may be possible would be to have something like the left channel of the mic and right channel of line in feeding different tracks, but I don't know for sure if that can be done. It may depend on the sound card.
