### Post by kebes on 2007-01-08
A program called avidemux can be used to cut out segments from video, change the encoding format, and things like that. But it's cumbersome if you want to *move* a clip from one section to another. If you want to do complicated "non-linear" video editing then cinelerra is a better choice. (I've never used it but I'm told it's good.)

So I would try installing kino, avidemux, cinelerra, and see what works for your needs. They are all free/open-source so it's quick to install/uninstall them with Synaptic.

EDIT: Also worth checking out "kdenlive", which can also do non-linear video editing...
