### Post by mojoman on 2007-10-07
I think what you're looking to remove is a package called libxvidcore4. Open up synaptic and do a search for xvid and it should pop up among the results. Just mark it for removal and apply the changes and see if that does the trick. If it doesn't and even causes you to not be able to view certain types of movies or removes other stuff as well you can always backtrack the changes with synaptics history function.

/mojoman
