### Post by RWC on 2007-09-17
I'm an Ubuntu Newbie and have had to re-install Ubuntu many, many times to get things just the way I wanted.  I kept looking for a solid method or 'how-to posting' to use on what to do to get my 'foot' into the door as a new Ubuntu user.  I have come across two sites that have been super helpful in making sure that I get my multimedia needs set up correctly.

1.  Enable the 'extra, essential' software repositories.
Go to [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/sources]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/sources")

2. Enable your Multimedia
Go to:
[http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty]("http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty")
Look under ' Multimedia Codecs & Browser Plug-ins'
