### Post by Mega Man X on 2006-06-23
I've no experiences with ATI cards, but I would start by editing the following line at your ***/etc/X11/xorg.conf***:

```
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth		24
		Modes		"1920x1440" "1600x1200" "1280x1024" "1024x768" "832x624" "800x600" "720x400" "640x480"
	EndSubSection
EndSection
```

To

```
	SubSection "Display"
		Depth		24
		**Modes		"1400x1050" "1280x1024" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"**
	EndSubSection
EndSection
```

Don't forget to backup your /etc/X11/xorg.conf first:

```
sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf /etc/X11/xorg.conf_backup
```

You might consider reinstalling ATI drivers as well.

Good luck!
