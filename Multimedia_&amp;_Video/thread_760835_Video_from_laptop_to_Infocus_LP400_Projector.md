### Post by nairatinu on 2008-04-20
Just figured out how to get a video stream to show up on my 800x600 projector which is connected to my Vaio laptop.  First change laptop screen resolution to 800x600.   If you're not seeing anything on the projected desktop, then log out and back in (worst case reboot with the projector on and connected to the laptop).  Pop in your DVD and open it using VLC media player.  In VLC select the extended GUI option (ctrl+G) and then select the image clone option (fun to play around with the other options as well).  You should see a window with your video on the laptop desktop as well as the projected desktop.  Hope this helps, it took me a while to figure this out!  VLC is a much more reliable player than Totem and Mplayer, at least as far as DVDs go, IMHO.  The stream slider is awesomely quick, and it has infinitely more control over video and audio settings, etc. although the GUI is a bit sparse.  Check it out (and maybe send them a few $).  :)
