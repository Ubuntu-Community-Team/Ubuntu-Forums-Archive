Im converting my DVDs and home made DV tapes to H.264-AAC but in the case of DVDs, I am not sure which audio format to use. I really want to use Matroska, as it supports more codecs and is open source, but seems like only MP4 will play on commercial players. ### Post by Major_Kong on 2007-06-14
AAC could be a better format than AC3, but you always loose something in the transcode. If you can keep the original AC3 stream, do so.

And yes, there is no quality loss if you later decide to remux into mp4. Remember a container does not encode or reencode.
