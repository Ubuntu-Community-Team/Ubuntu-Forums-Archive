### Post by doundounba on 2007-02-13
It's not à-la-carte, so probably not what you're looking for, but [Emusic]("http://www.emusic.com/") works fine with Linux, selling you un-DRM-ed MP3s at high bitrates.  I use [EmusicJ]("http://www.kallisti.net.nz/EMusicJ/HomePage") to manage my downloads, because I'm told the Linux client offered directly by Emusic isn't very good (though it's nice that they *do* offer a Linux client).  I've never had a problem with EmusicJ, and I'm pretty happy with Emusic.
