### Post by funchords on 2006-08-24
Does your computer have audio jacks front and back?  If so, do you have the problem on both sets?

If you have two sets of jacks, and only one of them are affected, then the problem likely has something to do with the path of the wires (which you can fix) or the path of the mobo traces (which you cannot).  I have a Dell Opti-somethingorother with this problem.
