### Post by jacob01 on 2008-03-13
i just bought and installed an evga nvidia geforces 8600gt ssc and i am truely amazed at how easily it was to install both physically and software wise. I downloaded the drivers from the nvidia site, put them in my home directory, shutdown my system..., installed the card, started it up with the igp dissabled, ran the .run file and i was up and running in 10 minutes. After the install i was using 195mb of ram compared to my 350mb before install wow!!

i also started up css which i could not even begin to run on my old igp and it ran good, around 60 fps

i know its not a high end gaming card but hey for $100 you cant beat it

i also enabled coolbits in the xorg for over clocking, it is pretty much oc to its limits at 684mhz and 810mhz mem but i upped the mem a little to 835mhz and it runs good. I couldn't go much higher because i started noticing little white patches on the screen and i decided to back it off.:lolflag:

i cant wait to try out et qw and the other native games to see how they run.
