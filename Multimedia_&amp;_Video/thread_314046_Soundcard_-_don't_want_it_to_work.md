Perhaps the way around your problem is this.
Install the card and it should be seen by the software, that you are likly to have to install as well unless it is seen already.
Then in the sound system settings deselect that sound cards option, Selecting instead the sound options for the in-built sound card.
In affect the same as in XP, you can have multiple cards in the computer but only one can be active. 
I am not sure if this helps but it may resolve your situation.
