My problem is that the volume control in the toolbar seems to only adjust the PCM volume, not the surround channel.

### Post by croak77 on 2006-09-22
Right-Click on the mixer and select Preferences. You should be able to select the 'master' channel or something similar (I'm running KDE not GNOME but I know there is an option ther) which is the same name as the channel in alsamixer that worked for you.
