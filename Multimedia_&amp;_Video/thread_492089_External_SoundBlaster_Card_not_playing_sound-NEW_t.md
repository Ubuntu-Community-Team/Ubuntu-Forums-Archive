### Post by Daveth on 2007-07-04
so a Creative Soundblaster card in a PCI slot on your motherboard? What does the master volume by the date and time time i.e. is it turned up? Also, have you disabled the on-board sound on your motherboards- you go into the BIOS on boot to do this. Also worth loading alsamixergui with Synaptic and playing with the settings- I'm assuming that the "devices" tab on you system - prefs- sound actually says Alsa and Audigy 2 or whatever i.e. ubuntu has recognised your card. My Audigy 2 plays just fine with 5.1 sound, internet, everything.
