### Post by NoVista on 2007-09-17
The memory speed difference between the two cards is negligible.
As far as having additional memory on hand, well, from my experience in this modern era of video cards, a couple extra 256/512 of ram means nothing. I have never used anything near the memory capacity of my card, even when intense gaming was going on.

With the one card having a Core clock of 675MHz , to me it's a no brainer.

They're both overkill. HTPC is a perfect application for onboard video. Think about it, unless you use for htpc to play games, decoding x264 1080p doesn't need any 3D. And it's unlikely that you can offload x264 decoding to the GPU anyway.
