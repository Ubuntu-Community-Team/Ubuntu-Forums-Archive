### Post by kostkon on 2008-04-01
It does not matter what the official site says about the supported OSes. 

Nevertheless, there are many chances that your camera will work in *PTP* mode. Although, it is not in [ the list of supported cameras for *libgphoto2*]("http://www.gphoto.org/proj/libgphoto2/support.php"), it may work using the driver for S*ony DSC-H1* or *Sony DSC-H2* that are supported.

But, the easiest and best way to test if your camera works in *Ubuntu* is to use the *Live CD*.

You could, also, change the camera mode to *USB Mass Storage Device* (if it offers such a mode). in case the *PTP* mode gives no results, and your camera will be recognised as a drive. Not so practical as the *PTP* mode, but a solution nevertheless.
