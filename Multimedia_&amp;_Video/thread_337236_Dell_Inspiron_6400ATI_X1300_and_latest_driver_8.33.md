### Post by karolbe on 2007-01-12
I have spent last 5 hours fighting with 3D support on my new laptop and just have found the solution. I am so happy that decided to share it with you ;)

So, if you have Dell Inspiron 6400 laptop with ATI X1300 card and using latest ATI drivers 8.33.6 then it might not work. On my configuration it simply locked the GDM after logging in (I could hear the welcoming 'music', move mouse cursor but nothing else). I have literally tried everything and what helped was  downgradng the driver to 8.28.8 version. 

Of course will have to turn off the composite extensions as well as the AIGLX, there are lots of
places where you can find details on that.
