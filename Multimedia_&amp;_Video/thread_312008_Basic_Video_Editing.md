### Post by patslap on 2006-12-03
Just thought I'd post this tip, as it has taken me weeks to find software to carry out basic video editing with Linux.

I have a 20-minute video in .vob format (copied it from a DVD), of which only a 40-second section is all I require.

I wanted to cut this 40-second section and save it as a smallish-sized avi file to email to friends.

After trying numerous video editors, the one that has finally allowed me to do this is Avidemux. It's a great lightweight program that allowed me to cut a section out of the large .vob file, resize the video and save it as avi. Perfect.

The homepage is at [http://fixounet.free.fr/avidemux/]("http://fixounet.free.fr/avidemux/")

Hope this saves someone else from having to search for this.
