### Post by Craig Watson on 2006-07-07
Ok, I'm not sure about this and can't check now since I've uninstalled the fglrx drivers, but I think dual head is to have two seperate displays, and dtop=horizontal is to have a big desktop. So what you want would probably look something like this:
```
# aticonfig --initial --dtop=horizontal --overlay-on=1
```
If it's the wrong way round (left screen appearing as right, and vice versa), then redo that command, but add ",reverse" after "--dtop=horizontal" (--dtop=horizontal,reverse) There might be a space after the comma, but I think it's that...
Anyway, good luck with those greeaaat drivers ;)
