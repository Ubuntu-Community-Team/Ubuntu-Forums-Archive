### Post by lgvier on 2007-06-28
Don't mind, guys.

I've got it working following these instructions:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=301941](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=301941)

To turn both on, 
aticonfig --enable-monitor lvds,tv --effective=now

To turn only tv on
aticonfig --enable-monitor tv --effective=now

and so on...

To change the resolution:
xrandr -s 1280x768

Geovani
