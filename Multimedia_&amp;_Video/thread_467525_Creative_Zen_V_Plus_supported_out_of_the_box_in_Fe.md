### Post by grege on 2007-06-09
You will need to install "libnjb5" to allow the other programs to access the Zen. If you install Gnomad2 it will be installed as a dependancy and then Amarok and Rhythmbox will also see it.

Podcasts are nothing special nor iPod dependent. Most audio podcasts are just mp3 so the Zen will play them correctly.

My wife has a Zen V and daughter has an older Zen and both work flawlessly under Feisty.
