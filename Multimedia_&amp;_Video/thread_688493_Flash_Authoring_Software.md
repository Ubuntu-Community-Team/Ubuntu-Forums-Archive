### Post by link141 on 2008-02-05
I've done alot of web-research (out of boredom) about open-source alternatives to flash, and svg+javascript or svg+smil appear to be the most promising.  SVG has just started to be adopted be browsers like Firefox, Opera, and Konqueror.  The spec is there, but it might take a while before it becomes well supported.  Personally, I'd love to see it replace flash on the web, especially on poor quality sites like youtube (I think their video playback stinks).  

SVG is also a nice alternative to certain bitmap pictures
