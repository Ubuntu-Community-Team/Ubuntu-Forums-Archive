### Post by drobvice on 2006-11-06
Thought I would update this thread.  For now I am using Penguin TV and gtkpod.  I like Penguin TV alot and it is automatic in pulling feeds and downloading new content.  I was resolved on using gpodder, but I had to update each feed individually and download manually.  I made a link to "media" in .penguintv folder and placed it in my home folder.  When I open up gtkpod, I just point it to the dated folder for todays downloads and upload the directory straight to the podcast list on the iPod.  Still miss the album artwork I had in iTunes but this is a realtively hassle free solution.  I also found a feedburner feed for buzz out loud that works nicely.  Google ftw.
