If anyone is interested in converting their iPod MP4 movies into DVD's, then use a program called VSO2DVD. It runs well in wine and easily converts MP4 into DVD format. Of course the quality is not stellar but just in case you want to watch them on TV.

I just thought I would share this bit of info.
