### Post by Erlander on 2007-04-26
It can happen with almost any video viewed on a computer instead of a TV.

TV's correct this by having overscan which hides the extremities of the picture off screen.  Computer monitors do not so you get the line.  It can be at the top or bottom.

Some computer TV programs like Mythtv allow you to adjust the overscan to correct this.
