### Post by mc4man on 2008-02-03
You might want to try k9copy, i think it's a little faster, quality wise for transcoders I would think very similar.
One cause of out of the blue performance drops is cpu overheating. Video processing can push cpu use very high. I'm not familiar with pentium M's (i think that's what you have), but most pentiums will throttle back after reaching a certain threshold and will remain in that state if temp increase is contained. Obviously laptops are harder to clean/ keep clean - check the vents, fan, blow clean if needed.
