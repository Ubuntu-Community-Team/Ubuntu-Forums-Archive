### Post by rockin_goliath on 2007-06-19
Of course Rhythmbox can write to iPods. You may just not have the plugin enabled (Edit, plugins, check Portable Players-iPod). I use rhythmbox with my iPod all the time and it works great. I can even take music OFF my iPod and import it into my library all within Rhythmbox! (select music you want to copy from iPod, right click and hit "copy," and then in the library window press ctrl+v) Not to sure about album art, but the music is the most important thing, right?
