### Post by flewis7777 on 2007-08-05
I wanted to play the .avi files from a website that
had Blender video tutorials in the XVID format.

Despite installing the usual codecs, and reading the 
postings I was not able to get any of the videos
to run.

Then I got desparate. After some experimenting I found
a way to do it in Feisty. This is weird but it works for me:

1.] Close all movie players and close Firefox.

2.] Right click on the .avi file and select "open
with Firefox." For me the first try opened Firefox but
no sound or video came out. That's OK, just MINIMIZE Firefox.

3.] Right click again on the .avi file and select
"open woth Firefox" again. Now I could hear sound.
Halfway there!!

4.] Immediately go back and right click on the .avi file and NOW
select "open with movie player" (Totem).

5.] Now I have sound from both instances plus video from
Totem!!!

6.] CLose the Firefox tab that opened and the Firefox soundtrack
will go away leaving just the video and sound (in sync) from Totem.

So I told my friend I have the MAGIC WAY and only I can play the
videos for Blender. She is very impressed.....

IS this weird or what????   :lolflag:
