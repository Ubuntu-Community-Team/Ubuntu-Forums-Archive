### Post by ubuntu27 on 2007-04-03
Hello galactus1. Welcome to Ubuntu.

Adobe haven't made their Flash available for 64bit Operating Systems (that includes Windows).

I don't have a 64bit computer so I will tell you what I know by reading.

The only way you can have a working flash in your 64it Ubuntu is to install 32 bit OS, or you can install 32bit Firefox with 32bit Flash under your 64bit Ubuntu.

Or maybe you can have a virtual environment...


[Check this thread, click here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=191205") for a How-to install some apps for 64bit Ubuntu.
