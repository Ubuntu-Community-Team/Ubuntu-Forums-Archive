I recalled just now that somewhere else on this forum that someone mentioned that you had to completely remove and  reinstall the nvidia drivers for every new kernel install. So, I did, and now it works! The only other change was the "nv" to "nvidia" in the xorg.conf file and everything is happy again. It's a bit of a shame that it needs to be done that way, but at least I now know what I'm doing and it's easy when you know how.
