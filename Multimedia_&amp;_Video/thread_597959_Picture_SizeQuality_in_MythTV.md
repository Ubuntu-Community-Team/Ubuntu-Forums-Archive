### Post by newlinux on 2007-10-31
What settings do you have on your PVR-350 (I think you'll want to look in recording profiles)? What resolution and what encoding? I use DVD 2 special and I think 720x480 or something like that. Myth by default sets the analog recording to 480x480 so you might want to change that. Those two changes significantly improved the quality of my analog recordings. You may want to play with the bitrate a bit too...

When you say widescreen settings what do you mean? Which settings are you referring to? the aspect ratio? If not what aspect ratio are you using.?
