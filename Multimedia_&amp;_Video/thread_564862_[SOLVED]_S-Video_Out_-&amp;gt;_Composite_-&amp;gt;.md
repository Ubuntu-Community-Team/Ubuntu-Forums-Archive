### Post by abrichr on 2007-10-01
I sent an email off to [email]linux-bugs@nvidia.com[/email], and received a reply in a matter of mere minutes from a very helpful chap named Roland.  He referred me to [http://us.download.nvidia.com/XFree86/Linux-x86/100.14.19/README/appendix-b.html](http://us.download.nvidia.com/XFree86/Linux-x86/100.14.19/README/appendix-b.html), and advised me to include | Option "ConnectedMonitor" "DFP,TV" | in my xorg.conf.  At first I added this only to my second screen section, which didn't work, but after adding it to both Device and both Screen sections, it worked!!  I hope someone else can find this helpful.
