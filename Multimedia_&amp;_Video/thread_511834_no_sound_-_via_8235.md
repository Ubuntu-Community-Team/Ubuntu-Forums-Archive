### Post by fredj on 2007-07-29
Open the Alsa mixer. Go to the Edit->Preferences menu and add all the possible volume controls and
switches for playback and record. Make sure that they are all unmuted and that suitable volume levels
are set.

### Post by Erlander on 2007-07-30
If you are not using the front of case connections then make sure the jumpers are on the motherboard to disable them.

I searched for months on why I had no sound and that was it.
