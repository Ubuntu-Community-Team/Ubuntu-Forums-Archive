### Post by masc on 2006-11-30
Hi,

as this is my first post here I hope it's the right place. I found a severe bug in F-spot, which causes data-loss.
When you import Rawfiles (.NEF in my case, I have a Nikon) these show up in your image browser and can also be viewed in full view.
But, when you try to upload such files to your Flickr account via the provided plugin, you get an error message (which is probably ok) and then the file is gone. GONE!

So be careful
