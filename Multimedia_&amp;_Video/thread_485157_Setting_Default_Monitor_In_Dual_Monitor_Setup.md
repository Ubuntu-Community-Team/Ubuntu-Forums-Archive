I thought the default screen was a BIOS setting, assuming it's configurable at all.  The screen that comes alive when the computer is booting, by definition, can't be controlled by the operating system as it hasn't loaded yet.

If your BIOS has a section for AGP settings or something like that, that's where I'd be looking.
