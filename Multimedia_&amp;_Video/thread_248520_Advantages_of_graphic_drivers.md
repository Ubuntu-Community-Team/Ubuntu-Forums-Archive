### Post by yota on 2006-09-01
Proprietary drivers are usually required for gaming or special features like tv-out or SLI.

But in general they don't add anything to the standard 2d desktop experience (some crash maybe).
IMHO is much better to stick to FOSS ones (if they work well) unless something specific is needed.
