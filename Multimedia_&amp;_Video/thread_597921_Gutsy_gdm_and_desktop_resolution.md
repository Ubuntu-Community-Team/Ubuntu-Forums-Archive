### Post by pdslacker on 2007-10-30
I have resolved the problem. Turns out it was GNOME resetting my resolution to 1280x960 every time I log in! You can fix this by using: System -> User preferences -> Screen resolution

Change the resolution and check the "Make default" box. After I did this my monitor was still annoyingly resetting because of the different refresh rates between gdm and gnome though. I couldn't select the correct refresh rate in the control panel so I just deleted all of my gnome settings (a little extreme I know but I didn't have that many settings) so that the next time gnome ran, it corrected itself.

Hope this post helps someone else out there.
