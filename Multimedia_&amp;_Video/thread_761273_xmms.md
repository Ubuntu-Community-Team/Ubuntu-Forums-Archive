### Post by Roukoun on 2008-04-27
**1)** Put your new skins in **/usr/share/xmms/skins** or in /**home/[your name]/.xmms/skins**

**2)** Open the xmms, and press **ALT+S**

**3)** Locate your skins and choose one

**4)** Press F5 and your new skin will appear!!!!!!!!!!
