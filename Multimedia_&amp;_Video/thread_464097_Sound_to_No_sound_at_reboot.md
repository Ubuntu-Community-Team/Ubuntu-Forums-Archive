### Post by Chappy7777 on 2007-06-07
well, since no one else who likely has the answer has spoken up, I will give you my Linux knowledge. All of it, all 2 cents worth.

I was getting no sound at all yesterday. Just the baroom that sounds when Ubuntu 6.06 opens. But I tried the sounds and could get none of them to work and I was getting no sound from anything. I read the help file in Sound, and was lead to download Device Manager using add/remove. Synaptic will do it also. After downloading Device Manager I used it. Checked out my sound card which it found, followed my instincts and voila, I have full sound working. 

If you've done this, then I don't have a clue what to say. I'll pray for ya tho.
