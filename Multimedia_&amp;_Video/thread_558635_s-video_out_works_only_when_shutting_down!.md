### Post by steefjeqv on 2007-09-24
Hi,

You've got to change the driver in your xorg.conf :

In stead off "ati" you should use "fglrx". This is the proprietary driver.

Hopefully this driver still supports your 9200 card. Otherwise this may well be the reason for your tv-out troubles.

If you're using this ATI card only on your TV there is another solution which gives a high quality RGB image on your TV. This however requires a specific vga to scart cable which you've got to make yourself. It's not that hard, you just need some basic soldering skills.

Greetings,
Steven

I really don't know why the tv-out is working upon shutdown.

The driver you've downloaded from the ATI site is the correct one to use. Problem is that it doesn't support X server 7.2 which is used by Ubuntu Feisty Fawn. Hence the not matching directory failure.
