### Post by Gremlinzzz on 2007-06-23
If your having problems with divx i use this command with mplayer plugin. totem plugin and mplayer plugin bump heads so its or thee other.
cd /usr/lib/firefox/plugins/
sudo ln -s /usr/lib/mozilla/plugins/mplayerplug-in-dvx.xpt
sudo ln -s /usr/lib/mozilla/plugins/mplayerplug-in-dvx.so
