### Post by Tony Hanna on 2008-02-29
I had a similar problem. With mine, the keyboard buttons would move the master volume slider which didn't change the volume at all. I needed the keyboard volume buttons pointed to PCM.
Here's what I did:
Go to system, preferences, sound.
Under "default mixer tracks" you'll see a list.
highlight PCM and close the window.
This should have your keyboard buttons controling the PCM track of the mixer.
Alternatively, you can use the control key to highlight more than one option (eg. PCM and Master or PCM and Headphone, etc) if you find it to be necessary.

If you also need to change what the volume control by the clock controls, right click it, select preferences, and highlight the track you want it to control.

Hope this helps,
Tony
