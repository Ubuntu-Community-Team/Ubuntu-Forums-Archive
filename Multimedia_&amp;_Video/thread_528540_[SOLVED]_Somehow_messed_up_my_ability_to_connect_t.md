### Post by rainwalker on 2007-08-18
[b]SOLVED:
After connecting it and getting that error, I opened up the File Browser. In the left panel, it listed "Places", one of which was "Sony PSP media device" or something like that. I could right-click on it and choose "mount", but I would get that error again. Instead, I went to Places -> Computer and it listed all of the places in the main window, and I right-clicked on my PSP and chose "Properties". It turns out I had changed the mount point to "/media", so I just deleted that and re-mounted. IT WORKED! 

This has been yet another instance that shows me _"if it aint broke, don't try to fix it"!_[/b]
