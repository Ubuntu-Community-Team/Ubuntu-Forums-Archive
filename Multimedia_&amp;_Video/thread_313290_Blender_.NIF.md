If it is supported in blender, then it is supported in every platform that blender runs! so you just download the *.Nif
scripts for import and export from here (am posting this as an example, maybe there are other locations to get the scripts from)
[http://cs.elderscrolls.com/constwiki/index.php/NIF_Importers_and_Exporters](http://cs.elderscrolls.com/constwiki/index.php/NIF_Importers_and_Exporters)

and then put them in your .blender/scripts folder which is located inside your home folder. to see the .blender folder hit Ctrl+H or View>Show hidden files in nautilus.
