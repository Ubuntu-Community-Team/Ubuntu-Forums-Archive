### Post by nola504 on 2007-10-21
Nevermind, figured it out.  Pretty easy actually.

For reference, in case anyone else gets stuck on this...

CTRL-ALT-BACKSPACE (to return to login manager), then click "Sessions" (under login textbox), then choose "GNOME".  After you hit "OK" it will ask if you want to make that your default session.  Click "OK" to get rid of autologin the next time you reboot.
