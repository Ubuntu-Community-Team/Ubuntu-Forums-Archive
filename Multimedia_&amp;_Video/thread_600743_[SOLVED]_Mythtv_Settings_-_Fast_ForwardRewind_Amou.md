### Post by jruss on 2007-11-02
I recently upgraded my box to Gutsy and with it I upgraded mythtv to 0.20.2. I wanted to change the default fastforward amount (i.e. the amount that is skipped forward when I skip ahead). According to the mythtv documentation the settings are supposed to be available under Setup->TV Settings->Playback->Seeking. The documentation says I should have options for "Fast forward amount (in seconds)", "Rewind amount (in seconds)", and "Jump amount (in minutes)". However, these three are not available on this page. I also checked in my mythconverg database and I could not find and place for these settings. Is the documentation outdated? Does anybody know where these settings are? Is there some problem with my install (does anybody running Gutsy and mythtv 0.20.2 have these settings available)? Any help advice would be greatly appreciated.

---

### Post by jruss on 2007-11-02
Well after a little more careful search of the mythtv-user list archives I found the answer. The documentation on the mythtv wiki is just out dated. The settengs were moved under playback groups instead of playback, way back in 0.19. They are called skip ahead and skip back. Hopefully this can help somebody else out.
