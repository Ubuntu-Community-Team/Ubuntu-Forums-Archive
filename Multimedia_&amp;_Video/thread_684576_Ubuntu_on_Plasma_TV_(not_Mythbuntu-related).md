S-Video only carries a max. resolution of 640x480.  If you must use it, then I would switch your desktop resolution to match.  Otherwise, I would use HDMI, DVI, or VGA and switch the resolution of your desktop to match the native resolution of the plasma.  If you don't know what that is, you can check your owner's manual or post the model of the plasma and I'll look it up for you.

Good luck.
