### Post by Izobalax on 2007-05-16
Post up your xorg.conf file. Bring up your terminal and type in:

```
sudo gedit etc/X11/xorg.conf
```

When the text editor appears, copy the text and paste it here. Usually, altering the resolution involves just adding a couple of resolutions in the text yourself, though this has shown not to work so much on Feisty. So, post up your xorg.conf. :grin:

/izo
