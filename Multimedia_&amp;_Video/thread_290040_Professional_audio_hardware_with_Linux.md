If you're using a desktop, you could use an M-Audio Delta XX box , which has great support in Linux, to do the AD/DA stuff and use whatever modeling software. You may need to use phantom power if using a Mic, guitar, etc. Personally, I plug the output of my Roland MMP2 modeling hardware to the Delta and Voilà.

This also gives you a multichannel sound card for digital recording from multiple sources (live guitar, external keyboar, mic,...) and outputting to analog if needed.
