### Post by rmjb on 2007-08-12
I dunno how many of you use Banshee for your music and also use a Digital Audio Player with Banshee, but I do and I had an itch to scratch so I wrote a simple plugin for Banshee. It simply runs any command you set after you've synced your DAP. I personally use it to run easypmp from [pmplib]("http://pmplib.sourceforge.net/") to rebuild my on player music db when I add/remove music from my iriver u10. You can use it to do something similar or anything else your heart desires.

Here it is: [http://richard.bailey.name/blog/2007/08/01/dapcommand-plugin-available/](http://richard.bailey.name/blog/2007/08/01/dapcommand-plugin-available/)

I'm also asking for help in doing up a proper configuration gui for it since that is currently lacking.
