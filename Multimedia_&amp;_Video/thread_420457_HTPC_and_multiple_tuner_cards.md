### Post by Jussi Kukkonen on 2007-04-23
> If I have 2 or more cards, how do I connect them to my Cable box so that I can record 2 shows at once?
Just use a cable splitter. Some people say the signal degrades a lot with cheap models, but I have 2&#8364; splitter in use (two actually) and everything works fine,
By the way: you didn't mention the tv technology, but at least with DVB you can record more than one channel with one card (as long as the channels are in the same multiplex) -- I've recorded six channels with two cards. 
>  Do I need 2 cable boxes or what do I need to do?
I assume a cable box is a device that decrypts the signal? The answer is probably the same as the answer to this one: Can you attach two TVs to the box and watch different channels?
