### Post by cyberdork33 on 2007-04-02
```

Section "Monitor"
    Identifier "AOC DLR"
    Option "DPMS"
    HorizSync 28-70
    VertRefresh 50-120
EndSection
```

You need to make sure the HorizSync and VertRefresh rates are correct for your monitor. Look on the net for the correct ranges, or maybe in your monitor documentation. If they are correct, then you should get the refresh rates and resolutions that your monitor supports.
