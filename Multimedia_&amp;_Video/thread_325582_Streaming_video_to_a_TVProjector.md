I'm using a nVidia (7600) and I got a projector connected. I opted for a dual monitor setup with TwinView instead of TV-out. It took me some time to get it going and there was a lot of cursing involved but it didn't require another university degree.

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=221174&highlight=twinview](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=221174&highlight=twinview)

Just a word of caution though. When I entered all the information in the howto (for TwinView) it contained parts that were unnecessary and actually crashed my x-server. Babysteps is a good strategy, editing one line at the time and restarting x-server every time. Tedious as h**l, I know, but if/when something goes south you'll know what it is. That's how I got mine working.
