### Post by nicholaspaul on 2007-07-11
I just bought a Griffin RocketFM, thinking I would just use it on OSX if I couldnt' get it working in Ubuntu.
I did some googling, and found that the FM setting on the RocketFM can be set with another machine, and then removed, and it will keep that setting. So I plugged it into my Powerbook, set it to a station, and then plugged into my Ubuntu box. Lo and behold, when I right click on the speaker control applet (near date, top of screen by default) and open Volume Control, the File menu has a setting for Change Device which lets me pick RocketFM. 
Now I havent got it working PROPERLY, but it does at least broadcast, so I'm making headway!!
Hopefully someone else can get further...
