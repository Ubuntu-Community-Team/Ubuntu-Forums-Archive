### Post by edward4130 on 2008-03-29
the drivers are IVTV and they are firmware that need to be added.  Last I looked they were still working on the 1600 but all the 150-350 worked well.

The 1600''s were being shipped in 150 boxes and hauppage was replacing them
> Hauppauge has been shipping the HVR1600 as a PVR-150. The box will be the normal PVR-150 but inside will be a letter stating that it was substituted for a HVR1600. Hauppauge will allow you to return it, and they will guarantee it will be with an actual PVR-150.

A beta is all I know of so far for the 1600, no idea if it works.
[http://ivtvdriver.org/index.php/Cx18]("http://ivtvdriver.org/index.php/Cx18")

I'm running a mythtv mediacenter and it is running sweet for over a year now.  hardly a headache considering all it can do, just have to put in the time to get them running the fun stuff.  You may be able to find more help on this on the mythtv forums.
