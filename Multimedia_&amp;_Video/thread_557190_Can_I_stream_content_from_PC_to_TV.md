### Post by ddrichardson on 2007-09-23
If you're in the UK and you just want to stream DivX/XviD and record to TV then you can't go wrong with [this]("http://www.ebuyer.com/customer/products/index.html?action=c2hvd19wcm9kdWN0X292ZXJ2aWV3&product_uid=129392").

I looked into building a home media system with Ubuntu, but for minimum fuss compatability you need to spend a fair bit on the TV tuner compatability, TV4Linux is probably the most user unfriendly site in the world, period. The cost became quite prohibitive and my main use was DivX to TV so opted for one of these embedded enclosures.
