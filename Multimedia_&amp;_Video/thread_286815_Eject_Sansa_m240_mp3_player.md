### Post by Tomlin on 2006-10-28
I just bought an m240 Thursday. Mine does the same thing. That is, the screen says it's connected to the USB port even AFTER you unmount it. I believe that's because the m240 gets power from the USB port. You can operate the m240 like a flashdrive WITHOUT a battery while it's plugged into the computer.

I was told by the salesperson that if you use re-chargeable batteries, leaving the m240 plugged in will re-charge them. I have no idea if that's true, since he also told me I needed windoze media player to transfer files to it.

The important thing is that you UNMOUNT it before you unplug it. Otherwise there is a chance of data corruption (although I HAVE unplugged flashdrives before without any problems - it's just not a good idea).
