the kind of video card you want to get all depends on what exactly you want to do with it.  if you're simply looking to have hardware acceleration and run stuff like compiz, then you can pretty much go w/ anything in the geforce 5 or 6 series.  however, if you're into gaming, then you would probably want to choose something in the geforce 7 series.  however, be aware of the limits of your system.  your power supply unit may not be able to handle certain video cards, or if you have a sluggish cpu, it may bottleneck a good video card (meaning restraining it from its true capabilities) so you might want to get an older video chipset instead.  but yes, i would agree w/ *erandur* in saying that you're best off sticking w/ nvidia graphics cards.
