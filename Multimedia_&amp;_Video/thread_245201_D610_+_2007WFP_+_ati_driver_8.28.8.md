### Post by hemish on 2006-09-03
With aticonfig. Look at the help it shows if run without parameters.

Try 

aticonfig --query-monitor 

and look for tmdsX where X is a number, properly 1. tmds is the external monitor, lvds is the laptop lcd.

sudo aticonfig --enable-monitor=tmds1

will enable only the tmds1 / external monitor.
