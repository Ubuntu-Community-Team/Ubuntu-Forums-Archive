### Post by trinikrono on 2013-02-21
Hello Fellow LoCo Contacts,

I wanted to encourage you to invite members of your Local Community Team's to take part in bug work and documentation work. These two areas are critical to each release cycle and getting involved in both is not as hard as it may seem. If your team would like assistance from the Bug Squad or the Documentation Team in some ideas for your Global Jam please do not hesitate to get in touch.

I would like to emphasize that the Documentation Team is currently one of the smallest in the community which means we always have lots of work that contributors can help with that will be very valuable to Ubuntu Users. Better yet there are lots of good resources for getting started on bitesize bug work.

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DocumentationTeam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DocumentationTeam)
