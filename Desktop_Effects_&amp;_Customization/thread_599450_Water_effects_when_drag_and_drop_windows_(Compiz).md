### Post by usewisdom on 2007-11-23
There is a compiz setting that makes windows look like jello rather than water when being dragged around the screen, but perhaps this is the effect you're after. Take a look at:

**System | Preferences | Advanced Desktop Effects Settings**

Then scroll down to **Effects** and checkmark: **Wobbly Windows**
