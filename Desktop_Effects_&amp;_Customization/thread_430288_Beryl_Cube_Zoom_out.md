How do I get the view to zoom out when spinning the cube? I have tried every configuration option I thought would logically do it but have gotten nothing. Help?

---

### Post by blackened on 2007-05-02
Desktop -> Rotate Cube -> Behavior Tab

Under "General" settings, move the "Zoom" slider to the right to increase zoom-out distance.
