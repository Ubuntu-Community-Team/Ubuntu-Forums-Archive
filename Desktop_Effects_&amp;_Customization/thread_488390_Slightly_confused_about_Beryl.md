### Post by chronic on 2007-07-01
you DONT disable one or the other. they both work together. beryl 2.1 doesn't work with xgl. but in case you have the right one just use the "bery-xgl" command to launch it. otherwise your best bet is to reinstall beryl with the older 2.0 version. i would recommend that you install compiz-fusion though. i have been using it for a while now and it has all the features of beryl plus compiz. its more stable and feature rich, its compatible with xgl too.

EDIT: as long as you have xgl working fine then installing compiz fusion will be a breeze.
