### Post by mysticrider92 on 2007-07-10
If Beryl runs well on your computer Compiz should also. I just switched last week and am really enjoying it. I have noticed a few stability problems (virtual desktops and beryl-manager don't seem to get along), but the eye-candy is quite nice and fun to play with.

I personally run it on a 2ghz AMD64 system with a 7600 GT (graphics cards are most of what will affect the performance) and the only thing it can struggle with is over 25 virtual desktops with Expo (don't know why I tested this, but it was fun). 

One thing I would be sure to do is enable the crash handler plugin and put metacity --replace in the command-line box. That will transfer everything back to Gnome if Compiz crashes, so hopefully you won't end up stuck in a command line.
