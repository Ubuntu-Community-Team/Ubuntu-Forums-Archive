### Post by notwen on 2007-06-07
> 2. In KDE I have no idea how to make it so that the dock at the bottom of my screen starts when I reboot. It's the AWM dock.

---EDIT--- this is for gnome, not sure w/ KDE, might be similar, perhaps someone more familiar w/ KDE can tell you.

System--->Preferences--->Session

Under the startup tab add this to your startup apps
```
avant-window-navigator
```

---EDIT---

You may also want to copy launchers from your menu and place them in your .avant folder, if one doesn't exist create one.  You can press CTRL+H in Nautilus to show hidden directories/files.  Hope this helps.
