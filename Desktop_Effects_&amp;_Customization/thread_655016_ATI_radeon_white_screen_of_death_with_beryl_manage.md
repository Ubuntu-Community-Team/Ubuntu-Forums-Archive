### Post by overdrank on 2007-12-31
HI and welcome, If you run this command in the terminal ```
sudo update-pciids
``` The terminal is locate under applications, accessories. This will update and Identify the output. 
Also beryl has become compiz fusion.It appears your drivers for the graphics card are not installed, You can look under system,administration, Restricted Drivers and enable them. Good luck!
