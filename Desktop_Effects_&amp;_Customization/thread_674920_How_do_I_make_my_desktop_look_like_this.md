### Post by mcgarry83 on 2008-01-22
Go to [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) and search in GTK (if your using gnome/ubuntu) themes that say osx or mac and download them to your desktop, then go to system > preferences>  appearance if your using 7.10. (If not I think you just click on themes). A window should open that has a tab that says "theme"  Most of the time you can drag the theme you downloaded right into the theme window and it will install it for you. Then just click on it to use it.

As for the menu bar, google for Avant Window Navigator. I use it and its awesome, you must have compiz or some other effects manager to use it. It takes a little playing around with to get it going. 

With these sources you can pretty much make Ubuntu look exactly like a mac in almost every way.
