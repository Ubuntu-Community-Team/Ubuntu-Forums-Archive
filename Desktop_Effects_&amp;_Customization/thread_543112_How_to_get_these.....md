### Post by steveneddy on 2007-09-04
These are typical Beryl effects on the desktop. Beryl really isn't supported anymore and the best desktop effects and 3D stuff can be found with Compiz-Fusion.

This is actually an "old" video by development standards. When that video came out, that was new stuff. Now, most of us that have been around the block, so to speak, with Compiz, QuinnStorm, Beryl and back to Compiz-Fusion have experienced these effects for many months and even helped develop them to the point that we have a very smooth 3D desktop in Linux now.

If you have the resources to run a 3D desktop, try Compiz-Fusion.

You won't be disappointed.
