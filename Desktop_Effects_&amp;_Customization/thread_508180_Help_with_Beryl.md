### Post by Orochi on 2007-07-25
Changing the depth to 16 means that you have less available colors to display on the screen. Changing back to 24 will give you more available colors, but some graphics cards can't display Beryl properly at 24. There's really no fix for it without upgrading to a new card (unless future versions of Beryl/Compiz provide a workaround).
