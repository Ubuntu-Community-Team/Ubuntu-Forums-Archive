### Post by jpittack on 2007-12-13
It looks like that when you rotate the cube.  In compiz settings manager, you can enable rotating the cube and then use ctrl+alt+mouse 1+move the mouse or use ctrl+alt+left/right.

I don't know why you would want to shrink the screen.  I personally enjoy as much viewing space as possible.  A side note.  When you "shrink" the cube, everything gets a jagged edge, or is aliased.
