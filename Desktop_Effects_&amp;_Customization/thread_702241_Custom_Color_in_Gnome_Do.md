### Post by dustigroove on 2008-06-11
It might be useful to some to note that Gnome-Do can be launched with the options "--glassframe" or "--mini" and you will get either a black with white/translucent border or a small glossy black theme.

This at least allows a consistent look and avoids the auto color adjustment in the main theme.

Cheers,

### Post by SlalomMan on 2008-07-14
I hate to revive a dead topic, but I was googling this issue and I came up with a solution by looking at my custom options in appearance menu. It turns out that my only color that was blue (which is also the color of Gnome-Do and the notification bubble of RhythmBox) was the "selected items" option. I changed that and Gnome-Do changed accordingly. Problem Solved.
