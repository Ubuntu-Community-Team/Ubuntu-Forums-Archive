### Post by crimesaucer on 2007-08-04
> **Zuse said:**
> Thanks alot for the help!!! by any chance do you have the link for the theme used in  beryl


You can find a black and neon green gtk-2.0 on Gnome-Looks.org: [http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/)


... and you can also find an Emerald theme to match on the old Beryl site: [http://themes.beryl-project.org/](http://themes.beryl-project.org/)


...and you could always use a theme like this: [http://themes.beryl-project.org/theme_details.php?id=81](http://themes.beryl-project.org/theme_details.php?id=81) 
...(which was my old Black and Green theme that I uploaded to Beryl), then just change the colors in the "Emerald Themes Manager-->--Edit" to match your Matrix colors, you can even change the "Pixmap Buttons" to the Matrix Glyphs...


And as for wallpaper and skydomes, you can either find a Matrix one on a Google search, or make them yourself form images on Google Image Search...


Using the GIMP to make a Matrix wallpaper should be easy, to make a Beryl Skydome is a bit more difficult, you need to make the image a certain ratio, the ratio that works best for me is 2:1


...which is 2048x1024 pixels, or 1024x512 pixels.


...I think 4:1 also works for some people.
