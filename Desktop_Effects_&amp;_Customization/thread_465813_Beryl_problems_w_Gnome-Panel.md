### Post by emid on 2007-06-06
I'm on Feisty and I can get Beryl to work for the most part.  The issue I have is that my gnome-panel doesn't seem to be getting styled by Beryl at all.  It isn't getting the drop shadow.  All my windows and menus do have the shadow and related Beryl functionality

### Post by emid on 2007-06-06
I figured it out.  I had to go to Beryl Settings Manager and from there go to:

Visual Effects-->Window Decoration-->Misc.Options-->Appearance

and then check "Draw Shadows on Panel Type Windows"

Not sure why it wasn't selected by default.
