### Post by Old_Grey_Wolf on 2008-03-30
I have several accounts on the computer. One for myself, and others for the children and grand children.

I have compiz set up the way I like it in my account. How do I share or copy those settings to the other user accounts?

---

### Post by azanoncello on 2008-03-31
under the ccsm go to "Preferences" on the left and then click on the "export" button. You can then go to the other accounts and press the "import" button.
