### Post by BobCFC on 2007-06-26
Do you mean so that the cube zooms out when you spin?


In Beryl Settings Manager click on **Visual Effects** at the top then **Rotate Cube** on the left then on the **Behaviour** tab click to reveal the **General** part

There is a **Zoom** setting with a slider... I have mine on about 7.7.  This means the cube zooms back so it looks small when I spin I can see the sky then when I let go it goes fullscreen again.   


Is that what you want?   It's how it is in most of the videos


If you want a skydome the image has to be in powers of two.  For instance I have a jpeg sized at 4096x2048   you could use a smaller one at 2048x1024 or something but it will be pixelated.   You can use gimp to resize images.   

Let me know if you want me to send you my skydome.  it's about 448kb


When you say you can't control tilt?   Hold Crtl-Alt-Mousebutton1  then move the mouse

### Post by speaker219 on 2007-06-26
Beryl is not supported and will not be updated anymore. You should use Compiz Fusion (there's a howto sticky at the top of the forum) which is a merge of Compiz and Beryl. It works much smoother, has more plugins, and will be supported and updated. Although the howto states it's not for beginners, it is VERY easy to install and setup.
