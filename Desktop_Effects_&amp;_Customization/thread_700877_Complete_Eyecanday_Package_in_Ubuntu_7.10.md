### Post by psyopper on 2008-02-18
There are several options...

For the glassy looking tool bar most people prefer [Avant Window Navigator]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=385981"). Personally [I prefer Cairo-Dock]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CairoDock") which is a little bit older but currently undergoing a refresh.

Following those the next thing is usable widgets, in this case [Screenlets]("http://forum.compiz-fusion.org/showthread.php?t=6889") which have recently been re-developed and are looking like they are making it into the next release, Hardy Heron.
