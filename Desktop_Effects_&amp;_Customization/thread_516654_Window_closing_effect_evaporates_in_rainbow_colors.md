### Post by ScottFW on 2007-08-03
Without actually watching the video, I'm assuming it's the "burn" animation, and yes it's available (by default) in compiz fusion. You have to enable "animations" (symbol is the genie's lamp) and then in the options you can adjust the various effects for opening & closing windows, including burn. For the rainbow colors you tick the option for random color fire particles, or if you want it to look more like actual fire, you can set the particle color to solid red.
