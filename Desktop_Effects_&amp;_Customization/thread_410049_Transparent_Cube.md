### Post by voyagertomb on 2007-07-15
I can help you wiht that, 1st go into your Beryl Setting Manager, along the top of the window youll see everything from General Options to Image format, click on "Desktop". Now the list of items on the left will change, click on "Desktop Cube". Now just to the left, youll see 5 Tabs under the category Desktop Cube, click on Transparency, check the box for Transparent Cube, and then Ubder "Opacity During move" adjust your desired opacity.

ALSO, to have a background behind the cube itself just lcik the tab next to Transparency called "Skydome", check the box under sky dome, then select your desired background next to "Skydome Image", and your all set! :)

Heres another tip, why have a cube? why not rotate the windws around you? Under Desktop, Desktop Cube category, Options tab, check off inside cube. Its very interesting.

Note: You must have Beryl installed to use the 3D cube and advanced features, to install beryl, I recommend following this guide "http://onlyubuntu.blogspot.com/2007/03/how-to-install-beryl-with-latest-nvidia.html"
