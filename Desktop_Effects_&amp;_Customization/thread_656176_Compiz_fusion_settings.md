### Post by Mr Greencastle on 2008-01-02
Luckily Gavin, the guy who made that video, is nice enough to offer his .ini file. Right-click and 'Save Link As' to your Home directory.

[Gavin's .ini]("http://fusioncast.fsckr.net/misc/gavintlgold.ini")

To use it open up ccsm and click on the preferences button on the bottom-left. Click on import, and import the file (make sure you select it from the drop-down menu). Log out then log back in and it should set it up exactly to how he has it.

Hope that helps!
