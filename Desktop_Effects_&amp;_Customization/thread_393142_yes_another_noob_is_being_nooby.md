### Post by wpshooter on 2007-03-25
To find out what video card you have, the simplest and fastest way may be to just open the case and pull out the video card and look for the model on the card (of course, make sure the computer is NOT running at the time).

As far as Beryl and XGL is concerned, IMO, you don't need this stuff, it might make your computer look "Cool" (but why do you really need that) and it **MAY** also mess your computer O/S up.  A word to the wise is sufficent !!!

Good luck.
