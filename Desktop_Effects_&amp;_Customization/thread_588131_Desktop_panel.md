### Post by signifer123 on 2007-10-23
You can get the top to not take up the whole thing by right clicking a empty spot of the panel, And unchecking the Expand box. Then you can use a theme to get it to look black., such as Shiny Black

[http://art.gnome.org/themes/gtk2/1356](http://art.gnome.org/themes/gtk2/1356)

All the other cool gizmo's are probably desklets, which you can get from the gDesklets package in Synaptic.
