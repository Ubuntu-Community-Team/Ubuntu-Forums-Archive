### Post by aoanla on 2007-05-02
Ironically, you may have posted a partial solution to your problem in this thread.

Your xorg.conf file lists your graphics card's information (in the Devices section) as having a BusID of PCI:4:0:0

however, you later note that the logs show that X fails because it is detecting a device on PCI:4:0:1

You *did* try changing the BusID entry to match the reported value, didn't you?
