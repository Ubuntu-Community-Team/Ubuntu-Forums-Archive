### Post by Steve1961 on 2007-11-03
> so should i take it that gnome users are heavily into bash then, what im really
saying is gnome philosophy to make the user learn bash ?

I don't think that's necessarily true, although many of us find we make a lot of use of the cli as we gain more experience (power, flexibility and speed being the main reasons).  As a Gnome user who's occasionally played around with KDE I have to say that I much prefer the simplicity of the Gnome menu structure - it just seems more logical to me, and a lot less confusing.  But hey, this is simply about personal preference.  Just use whichever DE you feel most comfortable with.  

On a final note, I think that it's simplicity actually helps users.  My wife, who is a self-confessed technophobe, feels much more comfortable using Gnome because it doesn't over complicate things.
