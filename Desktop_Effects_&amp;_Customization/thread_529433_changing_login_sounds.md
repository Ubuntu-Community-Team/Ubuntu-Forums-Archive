### Post by AndrewGene on 2007-08-19
or type "gksudo nautilus" in the terminal
cd to usr/share/sounds
copy and paste whatever sound you want to in there.  The sound needs to be .wav format and the title can't have any special characters (e.g. no -, _, or &, etc.).
It worked for me.
