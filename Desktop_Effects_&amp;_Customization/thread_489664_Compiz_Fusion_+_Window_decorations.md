### Post by autocrosser on 2007-07-01
If you have the CompizConfig Settings Manager installed:

1. check the Window Decoration pane
2. In the "General" tab of Window Decoration look for "Command"
3. put "emerald --replace" in the "Command" field
4.Start or restart Compiz & then use the Emerald Settings Manager
5. enjoy your Emerald themes!!!!
