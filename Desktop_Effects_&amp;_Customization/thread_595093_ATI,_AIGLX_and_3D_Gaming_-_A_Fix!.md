### Post by kshane on 2007-10-28
As has been mentioned more than once here regarding gaming, Compiz and ATI, You can't run 3d games under XGL, ***but you CAN under AIGLX! *** 

Also, it's been mentioned that when you run a 3D game, the graphics are so dark, it's nearly impossible to play.  Well, the fix is to go into the ATI Catalyst Control Center, go to "Color" and increase the color (all three at equal amounts) to JUST before things begin to wash out.  Then, go ahead and run your game.  Between increasing the color depth in the Control Center and increasing the brightness in the game itself, the improvement is MORE than enough to increase the brightness to play.

And everyone says the ATI Control Center is worthless! :lolflag:

I hope this helps some of you out.  I was really bummed to find Quake/Open Arena so dark I couldn't see to play...
