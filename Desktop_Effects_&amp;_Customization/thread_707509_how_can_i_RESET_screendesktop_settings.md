### Post by Therion on 2008-02-25
Is it just the font size, or is it the monitor resolution?  

If it's JUST the font size you can change that by going to System/Preferences/Appearance/Fonts and then upping the the font sizes.

If it's the monitor resolution that's too high, that's a different matter.
