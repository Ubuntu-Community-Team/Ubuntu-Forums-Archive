### Post by spartus4 on 2007-09-08
I have seen on some sites that with Compiz Fusion you can peal back a maximized window to see what is under it.  How do I do this?  I have gone to the bottom right corner but it doesn't do anything.  Any help would be great.

Thanks in advance,

Spartus4

---

### Post by isaacj87 on 2007-09-08
There's 2 ways you can do this. First maximize the window...then move up to any of the top corners and when the cursor changes to the "resize" cursor, click and peel it back! You can do it anywhere on the top as long as the cursor is the "resize" cursor

The other way is to go down to the bottom corners of the window and hold alt and click and you can peel it back. You can do it anywhere from the bottom of the window as long as you hold alt down too.

### Post by DaymItzJack on 2007-11-24
To stop windows from moving while maximized, go to

ccsm->Window Managment->Move window

Uncheck "Snapoff maximized windows" and you can now pull back windows while maximized from the top corners/middle.
