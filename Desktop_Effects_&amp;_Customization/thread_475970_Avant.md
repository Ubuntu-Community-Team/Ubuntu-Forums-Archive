### Post by notwen on 2007-06-19
if you mean drag your icons in a rope-esque style around your desktop, you're likely referring to Kiba-Dock.  Kiba-Dock has a bit more eye candy in it's ability to make icons do just about anything, whether bounce off screen sides, drag them around like a rope, or spin your rope of icons around in a  circle.  Check out youtube and search for kiba-dock.  If, on the other hand you indeed are referring to AWN, open up gconf-editor in a term and browse Apps--->Avant-Window-Navigator and there would can find all of AWN's preferences.  Good luck and I hope this helps.
