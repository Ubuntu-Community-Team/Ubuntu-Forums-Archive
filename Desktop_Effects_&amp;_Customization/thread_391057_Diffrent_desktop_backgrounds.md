### Post by eXcentra on 2007-03-22
the point of viewports is to organize your windows rather than cluttering them up into one space... (ie. having your work on one side, entertainment on another, etc) Having multiple wallpapers is more of a customizable goodie since it isn't the actual point of viewports..

Anywho, you can try setting it up using these instructions (it works for me):
[http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Wallpaper_Manager](http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Wallpaper_Manager)
**Note:** This turns off you desktop icons and what not; this is purely for multiple wallpapers **only**
