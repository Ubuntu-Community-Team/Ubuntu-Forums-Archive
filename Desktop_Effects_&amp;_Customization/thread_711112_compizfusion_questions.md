### Post by em3raldxiii on 2008-02-29
Going with the 7600GT is probably a good idea, and will probably get you some good results with compiz-fusion.  Now, running it "perfectly", I don't know ... especially if you plan to use transparency effects.  Also, once you get comfortable with the 3D effects, you will eventually install the advanced settings manager for it, and probably get into enabling stuff like fire and explosions and stuff.  The 7600GT will probably be okay for that (I mean, I was running them relatively comfortably on a FX5600GT).  Your FPS will probably be better than 30FPS for everything.

As far as the rest of your hardware, I can't see any problems with them at all.  Your radeon 9550 is probably your bottleneck.

The only trouble I predict will be switching to the new vidcard - you'll have to remove the old drivers and change the module entries in your xorg.conf file ... which in itself is not difficult, just that you might have a bit of stuff to learn so you understand the changes.  Depends on how new you are to Linux.

Anyway, good luck, and let us know how it all goes. :D
