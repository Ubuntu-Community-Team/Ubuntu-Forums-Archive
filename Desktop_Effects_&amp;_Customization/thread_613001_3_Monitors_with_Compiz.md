with gutsy, xinerama is replaced by xrandr.  The only limitation you may run into is with the two monitors attached to the same card - will your hardware support 3d over two monitors. (all but the latest cards only allow total virtual monitor size of 2048x2048).

See these instructions: [http://wiki.debian.org/XStrikeForce/HowToRandR12](http://wiki.debian.org/XStrikeForce/HowToRandR12)

good luck.
