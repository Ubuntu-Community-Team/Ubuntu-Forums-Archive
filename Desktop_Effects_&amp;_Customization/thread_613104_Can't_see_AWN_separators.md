### Post by AsoSako on 2007-11-14
I have had the same problem.  Here is what solved it:
1. Go to System > Preferences > Awn manager
2. Go to Pattern Engine
3. Remove the check mark from Enable Pattern
4. Click Refresh
There you go :)!
That fixed it for me I hope it fixes it for you.

Oh and by the way I know the fix for the Notification Area too.   You can only have ONE notification area, thus you need to remove any other Notification Areas you have (Probably on your Gnome Bar or whatever).  After you do that you can enable the Notification Area on you AWN Bar.  Enjoy :popcorn:
