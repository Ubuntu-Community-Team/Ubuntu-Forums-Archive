### Post by Hitchhiker427 on 2007-05-18
I figured it out, and it was actually a stupid little thing I did.  Apparently, there's an option in the Beryl Settings Manager (Under General > General > Desktop Background) called "Desktop Manager Supports Viewports".  Apparently, this error is the result of checking the box.
