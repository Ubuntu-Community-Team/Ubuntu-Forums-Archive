### Post by SloYerRoll on 2008-03-06
Try using a keyboard shortcut to see if Compiz is broken or you just have some settings tweaked. 

Try Super + E  (the super key is they windows key:))

If that works, you should post a screen shot of your desktop settings. Since you probably have a conflicting setting. 
HOWTO screngrab:
Applications>Accessories>Take Screen Shot

If that doesn't work. Go to System>Preferences>advanced Desktop Effects Settings

Then go to preferences in the left column, then select, "Reset to Default". 

Adjust your cube to taste again and see what happens. (make sure you have "Rotate Cube enabled)
