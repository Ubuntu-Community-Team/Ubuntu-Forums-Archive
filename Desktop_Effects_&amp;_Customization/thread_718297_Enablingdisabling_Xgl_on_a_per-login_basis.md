### Post by BooBooNTZU on 2008-03-08
If you are using the latest ATI drivers from their website , not the restricted ones , you can login into a normal GNOME session everytime you want to have the Compiz effects and then login into a Safe Gnome session everytime you want to play games and not have the Desktop Effects enabled.


I guess you have to customize your sessions so that one has the Compiz effects and another one has the Direct rendering only , without the xgl.
