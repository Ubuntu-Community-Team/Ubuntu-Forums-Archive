### Post by miggols99 on 2007-04-13
According to [this page]("http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Install_Beryl_on_Ubuntu_Feisty_with_AIGLX"), you can add it to you startup programs by doing this:
> Go to System > Preferences > Sessions 
 Go to the Startup Programs tab 
 Click the Add button and type in beryl-manager 
 Exit

### Post by ruibuntu on 2007-04-13
Just press the system tab, then preferences, sessions and choose the Startup Programs and Add.

Then write "beryl" or "beryl-manager".
