### Post by DocHoliday52090 on 2007-11-12
Make sure the Blur effects are on in Compiz's settings. 

To do that, you have to have the compiz-config utility installed. Once installed, you'll find it under System > Preferences > Advanced Desktop Effects Settings > Effects

Try Window Blur first, and then maybe Motion Blur if you want to go all out (and if you have the power).
