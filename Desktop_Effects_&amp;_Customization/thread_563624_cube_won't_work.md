### Post by Linicks on 2007-09-30
Try this.

Right mouse on the 'panels' in the toolbar -> preferences.  Increase workspaces to 4.  Now go back to desktop effects and turn OFF the cube.

Go back to the panel and check that the workspace is still set to 4.

Now go back to desktop effects and turn on the cube again.

It should be working now.

The above fixed me up.
