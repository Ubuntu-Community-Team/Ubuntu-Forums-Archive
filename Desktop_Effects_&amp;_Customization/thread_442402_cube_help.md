### Post by srhlefty on 2007-05-14
I think what you are looking for is called the "Skydome".

It can be found in the Beryl Settings Manager, Desktop section, Desktop Cube plugin, Skydome tab.

I'm not sure what most people use, but I use a program called Xvidcap to do my capturing.  It is mostly used for recording video of your desktop, but if you set the framerate low and have it record a sequence of images instead of an MPEG, then you can get screenshots of Beryl in action.
