### Post by Rupertronco on 2007-10-03
The beryl settings manager won't do you much good unless you have beryl. Emerald won't decorate your windows without a compositing manager (compiz or beryl). Installing beryl will do the trick. 

Follow one of the many howto threads in this very forum to get beryl up and running. Make sure you correctly identify your video card and follow a guide that is appropriate for your hardware.
