### Post by Kokesh on 2007-12-16
Really strange problem with Compiz is happening to me:
After enabling Compiz, everything works fine - except one app: Swiftfox 2.0.0.9. If I maximize Swiftfox, it maximizes. But after I do single one click in app's window, the window shrinks, leaving cca 150-200px free space around all sides. It is centered than. 
I believe it has nothing to do with my GPU's maxtexturesize, since all other apps are OK and SwiftFox is maximized before click. Anybody has any ideas?

---

### Post by Kokesh on 2007-12-18
I found the reason - enabled TV with 1024x768 resolution - some of windows were sized to fit in the TV, which view is in situated absolute posiltion of my monitor (1152x864). That's why.
That's not logical, but that was it.
