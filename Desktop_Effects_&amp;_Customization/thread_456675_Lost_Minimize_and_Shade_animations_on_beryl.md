### Post by Astrodemoniac on 2007-05-29
Ah, found the bug. It happens only when transparency of desktop (when not rotating) is less than 100. Two possible workarounds:

1 You can set transparency when not rotating to 100 always
2 You can have a transparent cube but you have to set "Inside Cube" and work inside cube :(
