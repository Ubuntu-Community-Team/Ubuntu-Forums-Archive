### Post by rainwalker on 2007-12-24
I enabled Blur Effects a little while ago and that quite effectively crashed Compiz Fusion. Now, whenever I log in, it tries to enable the blurring again and dies.
How can I disable Compiz Fusion before logging in, so that I can turn off these blur effects that keep killing my computer?

[B]
EDIT - Solved[/B]
Okay, so at the login screen I set my session type to Failsafe GNOME and that kept CF from loading, and I disabled blurring and now everything works.
