### Post by the_maassk on 2007-05-04
The simplest is to use a gnome-panel, increase its size, place icon shortcuts on it, switch off 'expand' and make its background transparent.

Alternatively, you can try kiba-dock from tuxfamily repos.

Avant-window-navigator (AWN) also looks promising and I have been using it for a while.
