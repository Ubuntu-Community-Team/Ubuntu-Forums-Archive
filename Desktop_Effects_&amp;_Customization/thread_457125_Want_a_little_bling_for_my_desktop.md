### Post by notwen on 2007-05-28
This may or may not be considered bling to some, but I like the way it looks on my desktop.  Conky is a very light-weight system monitor that you can configure to show you up-to-date cpu/ram usage, current HDD usage, cpu temp, or if you want it can show your current track playing in select audio players.  Check out this [thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=353362") for more details.  

[Here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=281865") is a thread of ppl showing off and sharing their conky configs, if you cant decide on what you want, why not copy someone else's and tweak ti to your liking. =]
