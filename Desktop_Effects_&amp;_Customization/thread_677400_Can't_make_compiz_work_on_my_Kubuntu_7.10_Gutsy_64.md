### Post by overdrank on 2008-01-26
HI and welcome, in my opinion you need to uninstall sudo apt-get install xserver-xorg . You xorg is showing you are using the nv driver instead of nvidia. Then under restricted driver, located under system, administration  enable them. This will allow you to use the nvidia drive which will support compiz. Good luck!
