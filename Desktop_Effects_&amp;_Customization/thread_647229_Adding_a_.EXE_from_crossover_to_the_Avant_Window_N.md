### Post by radlations on 2007-12-22
So I was having problems adding Microsoft Word(and others) onto the AWN dock. It either would not add or would not run correctly(IOPL error) for many reasons.

Well I found the solution. What I did was I ran "cxrun" and selected M$ Word. Then I clicked on "Create Shortcut" and dragged this onto the dock.

Don't delete this shortcut or else it wont show up on the dock after a restart. Leave it somewhere. And then drag it onto the dock.


Well there you go.
