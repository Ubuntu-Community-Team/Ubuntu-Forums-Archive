### Post by andrewsomething on 2008-01-23
Open up compiz config settings manager and go to General Options>Desktop Size

You can adjust your work spaces there. To get the cube, set horizonatal virtual size to 4 and all other options to 1.

As far as docks, I think awn is the most promising. Check out the wiki in my sig line.
