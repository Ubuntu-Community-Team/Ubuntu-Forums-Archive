### Post by schauerlich on 2007-12-11
The option you're looking for for the wallpaper behind the dome is called a skydome. It's in the Desktop Cube>Appearance panel, and it's menu is collapsed at the bottom. You'll have to find special images to get it to look right, but compiz-themes.org has a bunch. Just download a file, select it from ccsm and you're good to go.
