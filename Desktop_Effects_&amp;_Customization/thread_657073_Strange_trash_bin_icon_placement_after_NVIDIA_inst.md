### Post by chrisccoulson on 2008-01-03
My guess is that they have 'moved' as a result of you increasing the screen reolution after installing the NVIDIA driver, and you can't move them back because the buttons are locked to the panel.

What you could try is right clicking on each of the panel applets you want to move back, and uncheck 'Lock To Panel' You should then be able to drag them back in to position.
