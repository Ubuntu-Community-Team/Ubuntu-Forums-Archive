### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-04-22
If it is a laptop, try messing with the brightness settings on the keyboard. For me, I press Fn and Up at the same time to make the screen brighter. You can also try adding the Brightness Applet to the panel to control it. You do this by right-clicking on a blank area of the panel and selecting Add to Panel, then drag the Brightness Applet onto another blank space.
