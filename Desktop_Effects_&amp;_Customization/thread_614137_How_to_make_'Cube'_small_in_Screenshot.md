### Post by DaymItzJack on 2007-11-15

CCSM

Rotate cube menu, set zoom to around 3.0 or 4.0 for that small.

Then once you want to take a screenshot, press ctrl+alt+mouse1 and then hold mouse1 and press prtscrn.
