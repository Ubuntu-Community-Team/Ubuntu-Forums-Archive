### Post by kombucha on 2007-06-19
Actually, I did a bit of research and found the answer myself.

Apparently only some styles support custom tooltip colors, but I was already using Domino and it does support it. Interestingly, it's not the Colors section of KControl, it's under Style>Style>Configure

:D Wonderful. I also figured out how to make Firefox not default to my system coloring for fonts, so now Gmail is readable again.
