### Post by FuturePilot on 2008-03-11
Try just going System&#8594;Preferences&#8594;Advanced Desktop Effects Settings and changing the settings.

To install an icon theme, just drag and drop the archive into the System&#8594;Preferences&#8594;Appearance window. Don't extract it.
