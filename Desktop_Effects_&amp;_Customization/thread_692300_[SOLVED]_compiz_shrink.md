Hi!
I think the closer thing there is is the **Scale** plugin. What it does is show all windows separately, shrinking them so they can all fit in one screen. Then, you can left-click on the one you want, to bring it to the top. However, all windows are resized to their original size.
Give it a try!
Good luck! :KS
