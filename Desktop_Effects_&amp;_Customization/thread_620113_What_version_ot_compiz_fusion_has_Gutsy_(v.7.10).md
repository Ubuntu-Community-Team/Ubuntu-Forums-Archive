### Post by Forlong on 2007-11-22
> **vampire666eng said:**
> So I guess that the 0.5.2 is meant for the Compiz Fusion Setting Manager and not for the compiz fusion it self. :)
No, the version of Compiz Fusion is 0.5.2 in Gutsy and Compiz' version is 0.6.0

Compiz and Compiz Fusion are two different projects. Compiz is the window manager itself (with some basic plugins) and Compiz Fusion is most of the plugins + ccsm + Emerald.


P.S. both versions are git-snapshots, so they are in fact closer to 0.6.1 (Compiz) and 0.6.0 (CF)
