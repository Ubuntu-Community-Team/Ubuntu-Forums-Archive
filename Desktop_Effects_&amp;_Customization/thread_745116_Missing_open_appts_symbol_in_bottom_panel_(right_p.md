### Post by GOROSSI on 2008-04-04
If I Understand fully what your saying the panel is still there,

so try the following:

go to the offending panel **right click on it** or left If you set the mouse up for Left handed use

then Select "Add to Panel" it will open a dialog box scroll down to the "desktop & Windows" section and select "Window List" should have a little thumbnail above it Then Click "Add" and Close the dialog box.

you will be able to stretch it the length of the panel By a left click and drag move once you have it the desired size right click on the dotted lines at the end an select "lock to panel" that should keep it there.

Hope that helps
