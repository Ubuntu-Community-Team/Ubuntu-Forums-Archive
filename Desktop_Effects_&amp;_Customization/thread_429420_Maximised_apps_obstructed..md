### Post by chewearn on 2007-05-01
Don't know any automatic way to do this; but a manual way would be to enable hide button for the panels (rightclick on the panel > Properties > Show hide buttons); then whenever you need "full" full-screen, click on the hide button to slide the panels out of the way.
