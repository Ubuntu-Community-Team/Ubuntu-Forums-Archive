### Post by psyopper on 2007-08-16
In the CompizConfig Settings Manager enable the Rotate Cube plugin. The Zoom slider in the Rotate Cube pugin determines how far back the camera pans from your cube whenyou initiate the rotate. The Timestep determines how long it takes (time) for the camera to pan back after you initiate the rotate. Speed determines how fast the cube rotates.

To see how you initiate the rotate cube go to the Actions - General - Initiate/Rotate

I have my initiate set to Button2 (the mousewheel button) and I have the box checked for Zoom on Mouse Rotate so that it only zooms when I use the mouse.
