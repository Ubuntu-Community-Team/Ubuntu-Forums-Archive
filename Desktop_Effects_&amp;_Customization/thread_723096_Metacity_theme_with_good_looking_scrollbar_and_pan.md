### Post by BluntBox on 2008-03-13
As king_grimson said, the scrollbar and panels are just parts of the GTK2 themes. Metacity themes affect the window decorations only, titlebar with buttons and the window borders, nothing more.

You can narrow your search on gnome-look to just gtk themes. I really don't think I could suggest a theme to you though, as there are literally thousands of them floating around, and personal taste means what I like is rubbish to someone else :D

You could take a look through some of the posts in the Community Cafe of peoples desktops for some ideas. [This months thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=711179).
