### Post by oedipuss on 2008-03-05
While I don't know how to do what you're describing , there's the autoraise alternative. Have "Click to focus" disabled and autoraise enabled with a reasonable delay, in CCSM , general settings. 
It isn't the same, focus will follow the mouse pointer which takes some getting used to, but you'll be able to raise and focus windows without clicking on them.
