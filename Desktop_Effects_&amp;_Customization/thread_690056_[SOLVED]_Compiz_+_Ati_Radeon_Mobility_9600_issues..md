### Post by Agroth on 2008-02-08
UPDATE:

I Managed to solve the problem! Yay! Only solution was to change drivers from fglrx to the open source ati drivers.

I followed this guide: ** [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver) **
The good part about this is that Direct Rendering is still working (which it wasn't earlier) so I'm happy!
