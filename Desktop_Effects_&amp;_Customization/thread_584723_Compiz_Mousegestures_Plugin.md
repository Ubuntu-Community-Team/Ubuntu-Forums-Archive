### Post by Despot on 2007-10-22
I don't know of any mouse gestures plugins for Fusion, but the screen-corner activation is part of the action bindings for the Expo plugin. Just goto the Actions tab, expand the Bindings tree, and double click on 'Expo'. You can pick any screen edge you like.
