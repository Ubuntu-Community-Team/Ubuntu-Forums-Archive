### Post by weblordpepe on 2007-05-26
I can fix this one I think! (Look at me Im helping!!)

This is an option that you can find in the Beryl settings. It's an option for

You can tick/untick and it takes effect immediately. I got no idea how it would enable by default, though. Follow these monkey-instructiosn:

1. Open the Beryl Settings Manager.
2. Click the 'Desktop' icon. The third big one along the top.
--This switches to new bunch of options--
3. Down the left click on 'Desktop Cube'.
4. Now on the left scroll down to the very bottom. You'll seen an option 'Stick Sticky Windows to Screen'. Tick or untick that.

If this doesnt fix it, then well...I dunno! :D You could always just put up with it. I actually like that feature. I have myself 'inside' the cube too. Lets me enjoy the 3Dness without it being in the way.
