### Post by arimaniac on 2008-03-02
**PROBLEM:**
As described.

**SOLUTION:**

1) Right click on desktop > Theme > Customize > Colors
Here you can find all color options about windows

2) Right click on taskbar > Properties > Backround.
Here are options for your taskbar,

Setting different font color for taskbar and windows
is **[SIZE="3"]not possible [/SIZE]**for all I know.

[SIZE="4"]DO AVOID SETTING THE BACK COLOR TO[/SIZE] [SIZE="5"]BLACK[/SIZE]
Select a very dark gray color instead,
that is my best advice.
