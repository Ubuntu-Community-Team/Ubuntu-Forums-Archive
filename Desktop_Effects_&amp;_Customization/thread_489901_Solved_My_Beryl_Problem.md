### Post by zero244 on 2007-07-01
I had beryl working with a ATI9600 card. I decided to upgrade my card to a Nvidia 7600 card. That is when my problems really started. I wont go into details but I tried everything to get beryl running stable.
No dice, it might run for a while or even several hours but would eventually freeze or crash the desktop.
I was having trouble running games in XP so I decided to install a new 6200 card I had for a backup and ever since all my games run fine.........beryl has not crashed on me since.
So all along it was a defective card. That is the first defective peripheral I have ever gotten out of the box.
So I cant complain too much. 
I suspected the card from the beginning. It wasn't until I saw a post from someone who had the same card and the same symptom's playing games that made me decide to swap out the card.
It wasn't fun getting to this point but at least its fixed.
Beryl is very cool..........and so is Linux.
So anyway for some of you having beryl or compiz problems consider it might be your video card.
The card worked fine using it for 2D.
