### Post by mcduck on 2007-03-17
Beryl has nothing to do with the way your applications look, its just a window manager. (and thus only handles moving your windows, drawing window borders and such tasks.)

The progress bar is controlled by the GTK theme you are using. It's possible to make your own theme using the image you have, but it's not going to be an easy task ;)
