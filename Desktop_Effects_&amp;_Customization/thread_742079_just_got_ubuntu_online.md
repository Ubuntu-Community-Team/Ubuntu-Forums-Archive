### Post by Giradman on 2008-04-01
Congrats!  I'm fairly new to Ubuntu 7.10, but enjoying the experience - :)

Plenty of 'online' help - e.g. check out the [Community Support]("http://www.ubuntu.com/support/CommunitySupport") page & links; also, I found the book below quite helpful for a beginner.

The *Synaptic Package Manager* under the System->Administration menu provides a list of downloads for your Ubuntu system - just do a re-load to update the list and check out the offerings; and search or ask the forum for advice on using this module, if you are uncertain.

Finally, you might want to define your needs, e.g. audiovisual, games, et al - plenty of great experience here - good luck!
