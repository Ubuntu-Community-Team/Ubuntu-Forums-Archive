### Post by hardyn on 2007-05-04
Of course a fancy card will run beryl better, but you should be just fine with the 950.

The beryl project is a fork of compiz, they started from the same code.  the beryl people seemed to push be fancyness of beryl more so than stability, what compiz was concentrating on; this was the cause of many a flame war.

beryl may have caught up to compiz in terms of stability, i do not follow the development very closely.  The short, expect more odd behavious with beryl, but alse expect more ooooh factor as well.

both are easity installed and uninstalled, there is no commitment if you wish to try each out.

good luck.
