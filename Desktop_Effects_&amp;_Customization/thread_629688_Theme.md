### Post by rainwalker on 2007-12-02
1. Download the theme, a good place to get them is from [http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/) 
It will usually be a .tar.gz or some other tar archive. 
2. Save it to your desktop or whatever location you want
3. Go to System > Preferences > Appearance
4. Simply drag the theme from wherever you saved it into that window, and it will install it

To mix and match different parts of themes, choose one and then press the "Customize" button
