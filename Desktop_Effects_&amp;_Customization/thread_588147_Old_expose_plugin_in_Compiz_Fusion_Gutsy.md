### Post by beesthorpe on 2007-10-23
I think the effect you want is, perhaps rather confusingly, called "scale" rather than "expo". As a default this latter seems to be bound to super+E whereas "scale" is activated by clicking in the top right corner.

### Post by mattaphore on 2007-11-01
Quick note: You probably have found this already, but just in case others were wondering...

You can get back the "move mouse to top-right" feature. Under the options for "Scale" you can double click where it says "none" under "Screen Edge" and set whichever corner you want for the Scale effect.
