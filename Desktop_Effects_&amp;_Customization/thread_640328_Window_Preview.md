### Post by arigram on 2007-12-14
Open Compiz Config (if you don't have it, you have to install it through the repositories),
slide over to Window Previews in the Extras category and tick Generate Mipmaps. 
That will antialias and smooth out the preview windows. This option exists in other effects as well if you want a smooth looking desktop.
