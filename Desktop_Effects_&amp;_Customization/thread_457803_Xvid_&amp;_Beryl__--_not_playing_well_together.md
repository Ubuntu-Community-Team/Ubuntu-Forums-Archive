### Post by Soybean on 2007-06-05
Yup, that's the trick there... the default driver setting that most video players use doesn't work with Beryl. I think the link is talking about how to fix mplayer, rather than VLC, though. I think this should help: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=451287](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=451287)

Note that this will reduce performance (the reason the other option is the default is because it works better), so depending on your hardware, you may find it's better to just switch to metacity when you want to watch videos.
