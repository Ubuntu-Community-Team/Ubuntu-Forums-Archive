### Post by KRossKoWolf on 2007-07-11
Ok to install the icons, leave them on your desktop as the tar.gz file you downloaded, now click the "System" Menu at the top, then "Preferences", then "Theme", click the theme you are using, then click the Customise button on the Right of the window, a new window will open, here click "Icons", then the Install Button, another window will appear, in the places area to the left of this, select "Desktop", then double click the tar.gz file you downloaded. Select the icon pack from the list of icon packs, then click close and close the theme windows, Good Luck!! :)
