### Post by SmokinJuan on 2007-09-07
For the scroll lag you might disable smooth scrolling [Edit, Preferences, Advanced tab, General tab].
For the typing lag you might disable spell checking [same as above].
For the cube/middle-click problem add a modifier key [beryl-settings-manager, general options, shortcuts, rotate cube, bindings, initiate rotation].

I know, none of these fixes are appealing (disable this and that functionality) but it's worth a try.  Good luck.
