### Post by psyopper on 2007-09-30
Which driver did you select? I believe with that video card you should be using "nvidia-glx-legacy". 

Further, your xorg.conf Device section says that the driver is "nv". Unfortunately this is the open source nvidia driver that has no 3d acceleration support. This should be "nvidia" instead, but don't change it to that unless you know you have the "nvidia" module installed. 

I wonder if your restricted driver manager installed the right driver, or installed it correctly...?
