### Post by wormser on 2007-06-26
Woo Hoo!!! I got it working.  I found the solution on this [thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=439670&highlight=scrambled").

The solution:  Right click on the Beryl icon> Advanced Beryl Options> Rendering Path> Switch to Automatic.

The Beryl icon is near the clock.  Beryl manager needs to be running.  To start beryl-manager open a shell and the command is beryl-manager.
