### Post by Nythain on 2008-02-16
it depends on what sort of windows you want transparent... im assuming since you were cruising lynucs.org you are wantin some pretty "desktop terminals"

there's a few different ways to accomplish thsi, and many many guides on these forums for it...
The compizconfig-settings-manager (ccsm) has an entire section on matchign window titles and how to decorate said windows... certain terminal programs offere psuedo-transparency on thier own via using a pixmap of the wallpaper (while effective for "desktop terminals" this method doesnt work so well for terminals above other apps as it will look like an odd "window" to your desktop, sort of weird)

thats about all i can tell ya know, short of forum search for "transparent terminal" or "desktop terminal", find a guide that seems easy enough, and follow it...

side note... on my pc, devilspie and compiz fusion DID NOT play well together, so i dont recomend that route if you are using compiz
