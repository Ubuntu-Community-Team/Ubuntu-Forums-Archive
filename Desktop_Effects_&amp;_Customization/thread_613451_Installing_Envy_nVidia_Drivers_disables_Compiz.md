One other note:  you may notice menu delays (ie, clicking on Applications takes a few seconds for the menu to drop down) once you have Compiz enabled with the nVidia driver installed.  If you experience this, you can fix it by adding the following as a Startup item in Sessions (which is in System -> Preferences)

#!/bin/bash
sleep 5
compiz --replace --only-current-screen &
gtk-window-decorator --replace &
