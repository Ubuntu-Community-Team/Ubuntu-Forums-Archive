### Post by ticopelp on 2007-06-06
If you're using Beryl, you can get lots of Emerald themes, skydomes, caps and so forth from beryl-themes.org or gnome-look.org. The means for customizing your caps and skydomes in Beryl are in the Beryl settings. Themes can be changed from the Emerald settings, or you can download and use tons of themes for Metacity.

### Post by NeoLithium on 2007-06-06
Themes are easy, just head to somewhere like [http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org) And grab yourself some GTK and Metacity themes that you find attractive.  Icons as well. You can to go System -> Preferences -> Themes and drag and drop the .tar.gz files in there to install them. Just select them from the menus :)
