### Post by petit.padavoine on 2007-08-09
I had a problem with installing themes too, it said "Error calling tar".

Just install it manually.

Open nautilus. Right-hand click on your theme, rename it to <yourtheme>.tar instead of .emerald, extract it, and place the files you just extracted into /home/<youruser>/.emerald/themes/<yourtheme>.

Open emerald theme manager, your theme will be there :D.

You could also switch to compiz fusion...
