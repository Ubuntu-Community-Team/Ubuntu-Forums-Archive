### Post by Therion on 2008-03-12
Okay, this one IS a little odd.  Here's what you need to do:

Download the file to your desktop.
Right-click on the file and click on "Extract Here".
You'll wind up with another folder on your desktop called "Black_2_White".
Open that folder and unzip the .tar.gz file inside it by right-clicking on it and clicking on "Extract Here".
You'll have a folder called "Black-White 2 Gloss" now.
Move the Black-White 2 Gloss folder to your desktop.
Navigate to:  System > Preferences > Appearance
Drag the Black-White 2 Gloss folder onto the Appearance Manger window.
You'll get a message the file was installed.
Click on the "Customize" button in the Appearance Manager window.
Click on the "Icons" tab at the top.
Select the Black-White 2 Gloss  icon set.
You're done.
