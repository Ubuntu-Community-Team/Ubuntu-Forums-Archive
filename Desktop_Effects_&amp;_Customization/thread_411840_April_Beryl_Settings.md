### Post by locke.dragon on 2007-04-17
Hey Everyone!  I thought it'd be fun for us to post our Beryl settings.  I'm assuming most of you already know how to export your settings, but for those of you who don't, here's how...

How to Upload:

1. Right click on the Beryl Manager icon
2. Choose "Beryl Settings Manager"
3. Click "Export" in the bottom-left corner
4. Choose the save destination
5. Save
6. Open the file in Open Office
7. Save as a .odt file
6. Upload as an attachment to your post

After downloading the file you want...

1. Open it up in Open Office
2. Open a blank document in Gedit
3. Copy all the text from Open Office (edit>>select all) and paste it into Gedit
4. Save the document that's in Gedit as a text file
5. Import the text document into Beryl Settings Manager
6. Click quit
7. Right click on the Beryl Manager icon
8. Choose re-load window manager
9. Restart X (control>>alt>>backspace)
10. Be patient

Here are my settings.  Since I use a laptop that's not exactly optimized for 3D, I had to dumb-down some of the effects.  Through hours and hours of tweaking, I even made Beryl look smoother and run faster!
