### Post by penguin steve on 2007-06-23
you need four work spaces. go to the desktop effects menu and uncheck everything. then turn off the effects with the button above the check marks. once that is done,  right click on the workspaces at the bottom right go preferences and make 4 workspaces. then go back to the desktop effects menu and check everything again and turn the effects on. it should work now. i experimented with this. when you are in the workspaces preferences, you can make 3 if you want and you will have a triangle instead of a cube. you can make pentagons too. i personally like the cube better though.
