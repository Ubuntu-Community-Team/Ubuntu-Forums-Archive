I think I found an answer [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/nautilus/+bug/66141](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/nautilus/+bug/66141)

Copy everything on your desktop to a separate folder.  Then delete the folder Desktop in you home folder.  Put everything you backed up back onto your desktop
