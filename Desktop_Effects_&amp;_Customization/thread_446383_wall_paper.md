### Post by nicepants on 2007-05-16
to have wallpaper "behind" the cube, there's a setting for "skydome" in the beryl config. as to wallpaper on the cube faces, there's a setting to change the default diamond top and bottom to others, and beryl should take wallpaper you set with anything else (gnome, xfce, etc) and use it for every cube face. i think you can get different wallpapers for each cube face, but i'm not sure how.
