### Post by orange2k on 2007-06-13
Your skydome picture must be of certain size like 1024x1024, 1024x512, 2048x1024 (your card got to have enough memory for this), 2048x2048 (enough memory?) etc...
Experiment with the lower resolutions and then go higher to see if your hardware supports it...
My card, for instance, (nvidia MX4000 with 128MB RAM) supports only 1024x512...
