### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-08-18
There's nothing wrong with doing that. Linux is about choice. 
Make sure you have the latest version of AWN. In the Preferences control panel (System>Preferences>Avant Preferences) click on the tab that says bar appearance and select 3D Look from the drop down menu.
Bar Angle: 45
Bar Height: 45
Icon Offset: 15
