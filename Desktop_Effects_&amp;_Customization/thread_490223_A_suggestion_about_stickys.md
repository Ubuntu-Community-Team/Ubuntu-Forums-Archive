### Post by LouisvilleLIP on 2007-07-02
People are posting replies onto Stickys.  This isn't the worst thing, but, it complicates the sticky, making users unsure if it's still valid, safe, etc.  Plus, any issues raised in a sticky and then resolved in the sticky are very difficult to find unless you happen to read through it.  

Is there a way to lock a Sticky, and have new threads/issues reference the applicable sticky?  It might help people get their issues resolved faster, make it easier for other users to find answers, and give people some level of comfort that the sticky is still valid.
