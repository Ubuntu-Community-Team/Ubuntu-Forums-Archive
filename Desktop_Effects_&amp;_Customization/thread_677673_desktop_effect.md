### Post by Ub1476 on 2008-01-28
To add icons to AWN, just drag the app from  Applications>Internet>Firefox (Example) to the dock, so you get a little + sign.

I believe the sidebar on that screenshot is a Conky trick, but can't remember were I found it. Might be on gnome-look.org. To get gadgets like Vista, download [Screenlets-manager]("http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Screenlets+0.0.12+?content=73346"). If you look under the section "Desklets" there are lots of more screenlets there.
