### Post by Rui Pais on 2007-10-02
hi,
that means that you have edubuntu-artwork-usplash installed instead (or simultaneosly) of usplash-theme-ubuntu package. 
So, either you uninstall the edubuntu one and (re)install the usplash-theme-ubuntu, or keep both and change default for the ubuntu. 
Here an howto on Usplash customization:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/USplashCustomizationHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/USplashCustomizationHowto)

Here another How-to on Softpedia:
[http://news.softpedia.com/news/Change-Ubuntu-Bootsplash-Theme-55237.shtml](http://news.softpedia.com/news/Change-Ubuntu-Bootsplash-Theme-55237.shtml)
that have a link for an [app (with deb)]("http://linux.softpedia.com/get/System/System-Administration/Usplash-Switcher-27321.shtml") that gives a GUI to change/choose among different usplashs themes. :)
