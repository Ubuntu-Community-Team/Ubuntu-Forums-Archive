### Post by bash4fun on 2007-08-01
hi dude
yeah you are able to add icons
and launchers by simply dragging
and dropping them onto the awn bar

the glassy effect you have
seen can be achieved by modifying
the awn settings in the "gconf-editor"

-open a terminal
-type **gconf-editor**
-browse apps - avant window navigator

as far as i remember there must be some 
options like bar_angle and icon_offset
just set bar_angle to the value 45
and the icon_offset to 18

then you will have some pretty nice reflections on your bar :cool:
