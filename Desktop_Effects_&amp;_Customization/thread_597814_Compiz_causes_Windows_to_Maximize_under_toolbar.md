Here's my setup:

Kubuntu 7.10
Nvidia 6800GT running nvidia proprietary drivers, configured with nividia setting to use Twinview on two screens (primary resoultion: 1680x1050 and secondary resolution: 1280x1024)
Compiz Fusion Installed and working

Here's my issue:
With Compiz enabled, whenever I maximize on my main screen (1680x1050) the window maximizes the full height of the monitor, to the point where the window is maximized *behind* the toolbar (meaning I can't see the bottom 40 pixels of the window unless I move/hide the toolbar). 

If I disable compiz, KDE recognizes the toolbar and maximizes up to the edge of the toolbar (which displays the full window). With both window managers they recognize the two displays correctly and will only maximize a window to the space of the monitor, it's just that on my primary screen with compiz enabled it doesn't see the toolbar and ends up maximizing behind it. 

Any ideas on what I can do?
