### Post by notwen on 2007-09-27
Do you have emerald installed?  Beryl is only your window manager now, emerald will be your window decorator.  Right click on the red gem in your notification area, go down to Window Decorator and select Emerald, then reload it.  If you do not have the red gem icon in your notification area, press Alt+F2 and type in "beryl-manager".  Is there any reason why you're using Beryl and not the new project release Compiz-Fusion?
