### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2007-06-27
To enable different backgrounds, in Beryl Settings Manager > General Options > Desktop Background you have to tick the 'Desktop manager supports viewports' box.

You want to enable the skydome for the cube "background".  The issue you may be experiencing is that the images have to be a power of 2.  So for example, images must be 1024x1024 or 2048x2048.  You can also use 2:1 ratios like 2048x1024 and 1024x512.
Also, make sure that all your Image Format plugins are ticked.

To have the windows stick to the cube instead of floating off of it, disable the '3d effects' plugin.
