Hi,
I've installed beryl on feisty and it's great. So great that I've messed up all of my settings and now it doesn't work properly (right top corner doesn't work, keyboard shortcuts don't work, etc).

Does anyone know how to restore breyl to it's default settings ?

---

### Post by flyingrhino on 2007-04-21
Ok, found it !

mv ~/.beryl ~/.berylbroke

in your home directory. I then rebooted (though I believe you could restart beryl) and it worked. A new .beryl directory was created.
