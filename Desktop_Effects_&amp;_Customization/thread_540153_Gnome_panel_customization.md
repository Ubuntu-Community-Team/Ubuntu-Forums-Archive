### Post by mcduck on 2007-09-02
Getting rid of the handles at the ends of short panels would require editing the source code and compiling your own panel. (At least I've never heard of any other way).

But at least getting rid of those handles on the left side of Window List- and Notification Area-applets is fairly easy. You just need to replace them with small, fully transparent images. I have written instructions and attached a ready image you can use, just follow the link: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=2229666&postcount=3]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=2229666&postcount=3")
