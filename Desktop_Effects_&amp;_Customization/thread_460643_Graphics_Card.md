### Post by nutz on 2007-05-31
I just picked up a GeForce FX5200 256MB AGP 8X for a mere $52 at a local retailer. This card performs very well under Ubuntu. I have all the eye candy turned on and it runs smooth as glass. For someone looking to speed up their interface on a budget I would highly recommend this card. It is also backwards compatible to 4X which is actually what I have it running at since my PC is ancient. Even at 4X which I suspect is more than sufficient for the GUI it runs perfectly smooth and the graphics look flawless. Very Ubuntu friendly card...

All this assuming you run Ubuntu, not sure how well it works under other OS's...


It also has dual output! So as soon as I can get that working we will see what it is like in TwinView!
