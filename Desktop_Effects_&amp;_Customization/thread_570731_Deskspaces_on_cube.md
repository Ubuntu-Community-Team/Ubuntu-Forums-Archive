### Post by Linicks on 2007-10-08
OK, you haven't got the full compiz stuff, so here is how to work around this bug.

Turn on the cube.  Then turn it off (but not desktop effects).

In the panel, right mouse click the workspace area and select properties.  In the properties, increase workspace to 4.

Now go back to desktop effects and select the cube again.

It should now work.

Nick
