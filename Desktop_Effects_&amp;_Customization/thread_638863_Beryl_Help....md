### Post by omegamike3 on 2007-12-13
You'll also want to install the 'advanced desktop effects settings' which is listed as 'compizconfig-settings-manager' in synaptic. It will allow you to handle the plugins and tweak everything to the way you like it (or what your video card will handle) :D
