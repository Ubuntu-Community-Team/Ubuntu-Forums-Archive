### Post by Claus7 on 2008-02-06
Hello

if you have Gnome open your home directory or type in a terminal nautilus and then go to Edit -> Preferences and from there you will see that you can change the icon percentage (the size of the icons on your desktop).

In order to do a shortcut in case of a folder if you do a right click you will see the option. Drag the icon to your desktop. Or you can go to Applications and drag any item you like to your desktop or your desktop bar.

Regards!
