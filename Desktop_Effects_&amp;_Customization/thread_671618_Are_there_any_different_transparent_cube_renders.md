### Post by Espreon on 2008-01-18
Yeah, theres 3:

Photowheel: Allows you to render images and photos in the cube

Atlantis and Atlantis2: They render sand, water, fish, and sea mammals. Atlantis2 is Atlantis but better, it lets you also render coral, crabs (hopefully they'll add more crustaceans) and bubbles.

Snow Globe: Renders snow, a snowman and falling snow in the cube.
