For those of you, who, like me, have been fighting with Comipiz, Ubuntu, and ATI video hardware, here is an official post in the ATI (AMD) website at the following URL:

Quote

Knowledge Base  	
AMD Customer Care Site Map > Archived Drivers and Software > Linux >
737-26907: Unsupported Features with ATI Linux Drivers

    The information in this article applies to the following configuration(s):

        * AIGLX
        * Beryl
        * Compiz
        * MythTV
        * TV TIME

    Currently, the following features are not supported with ATI Linux drivers.

        * AIGLX, Beryl, Compiz, MythTV, TV TIME

This is posted at 
[http://support.ati.com/ics/support/default.asp?deptID=894&task=knowledge&questionID=26907](http://support.ati.com/ics/support/default.asp?deptID=894&task=knowledge&questionID=26907)

For others, owners of hadrdware that have managed to make Compiz work with ATI video, you have been lucky... you might have a very particular chip that does not care.whre it was made.
