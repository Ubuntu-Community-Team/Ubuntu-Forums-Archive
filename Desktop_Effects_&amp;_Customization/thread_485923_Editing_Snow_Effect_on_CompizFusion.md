### Post by hyperair on 2007-06-27
You could always install Beryl and steal their snowflake png's ^^

Here it is: /usr/share/beryl/snowflake2.png. Just add it as a texture to the Snow plugin in your CompizConfig Settings Manager, and you should be on your way.
