### Post by pizpot on 2007-11-14
Bert,

Maybe your problem is that when you move the mouse off of a small window, on to a big window behind, the first small window disappears. Then when you move the mouse back on to it, it re-appears.

As you can guess, Ubuntu is trying to be nice and let you see the behind window. I find it annoying though.

TO TURN IT OFF: System-> Preferences-> Appearance-> Visual Effects->(Custom) Preferences-> Turn OFF Opacify.
