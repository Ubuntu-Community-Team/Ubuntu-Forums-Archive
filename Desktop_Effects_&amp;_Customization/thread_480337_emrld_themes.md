### Post by crimesaucer on 2007-06-21
Wallpapers are easy to make with the GIMP. And easy to install as either a Wallpaper, a Skydome, or a Cube Cap for Beryl.

Just find an image that you like, and add your own personal style to it with the GIMP.

...and it's easy to make your own Emerald window themes, just open up the edit feature in Emerald and play around with the settings and save it under a different name...that way you don't lose your original installed theme but you get your own theme variation.
