### Post by kyphi on 2007-10-06
When you create a new panel, it defaults to appearing on the right hand side of the desktop.  You can right click on the new panel and select Properties to specify via Orientation where you want to place that panel.

If your bottom panel is still there but not visible, it is possible that you have ticked Autohide on the panel properties screen.  To find it again, drag your cursor to the very bottom of the screen and the panel will reappear.  Right click on it and select Properties and remove the tick from the Autohide box.

You should still be able to add new panels even if your bottom panel is not visible.
