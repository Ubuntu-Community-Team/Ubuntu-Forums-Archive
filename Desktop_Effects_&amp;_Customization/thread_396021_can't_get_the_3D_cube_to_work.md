### Post by robertchahine on 2008-03-29
go to synaptic manager and install "compizconfig-settings-manger"
when installation finished , 
go to System->Preferences->Appearance
click in the tabs on "visual effects" then select the fourth option "Custom" and chose in the preferences of this option Desktop Cube.
Now right click on the workspace and choose Preferences and put 4 in the columns.
And that's it.
So now you can press Ctrl+Alt+letft click on the desktop and drag.
