### Post by Trance Gemini on 2007-06-02
This is realy strange!
I have fiestly and GeForce mx 440 card. According to NVIDIA driver istaller I need to use nvidia graphic driver of version 9631. I have downloaded it and used according to instruction.
Then I reboot and get startx error regarding you_know_what. :) After that I press NO and I am at text login. I enter my username and login.
I type startx and get that startx cannot start because it cannot find suitable display, or something like that.
And then starts the mystery! 
I type sudo **nvidia-glx-config enable** and get, that md5 sums do not match.
Then I type **startx**.. and it starts showing me the nvidia logo!!!

After that I am like "wow, it finally works (even after **nvidia-glx-config enable** get me an error)". In such happy mood I reboot and I get the same blue window (of death :) ) of X saying you_know_what.

Just to check I repeat the procedure from login -> nvidia-glx-config enable ->startx and it works again!

I don't know about You, but this looks to me like something realy strange...
