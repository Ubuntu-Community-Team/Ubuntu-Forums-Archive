### Post by rfdeshon on 2007-08-12

However, this makes your windows snap to the edges which annoys the hell out of me (I liked how beryl did it where you had to hold shift to make non-maximized windows snap).

### Post by mschrenk on 2007-12-07
To the pull back windows go to the settings for Move Window and uncheck "Snapoff maximized windows" then you can do the pull back without unmaximizing the window.
