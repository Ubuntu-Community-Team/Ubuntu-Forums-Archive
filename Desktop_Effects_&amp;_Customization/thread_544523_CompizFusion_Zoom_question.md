### Post by forrestcupp on 2007-09-06
I use the Zoom plugin in Compiz/Fusion to view pictures and videos that are small.  The problem is that since they changed it to accept input, it's hard to zoom in such a way that the pointer is not in the way.  Does anyone know if there is a way to either temporarily hide the pointer while the screen is zoomed, or temporarily allow the pointer to move without the screen moving?

### Post by forrestcupp on 2007-09-07
I went over to the Compiz Community Forums and found the answer there.  

With the screen zoomed in you can press Super+L to release the mouse from moving the screen.  Super+L will toggle it back on, too.  Hope this helps.
