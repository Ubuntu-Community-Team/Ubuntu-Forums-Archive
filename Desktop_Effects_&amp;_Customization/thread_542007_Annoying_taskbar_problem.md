### Post by Inxsible on 2007-09-04
The length of the window list depends on the name of the app.

For eg, if you open a Google page, the page title will be "Google" which takes very little space. Now if you search for "jellyfish and spiders and crabs and sea horses" for eg, the page title changes to "jellyfish and spiders and crabs and sea horses - Google Search" which lengthens the title thereby resulting in lengthening the window bar in the window list  :)
