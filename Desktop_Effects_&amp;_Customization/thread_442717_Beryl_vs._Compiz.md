### Post by Ateo on 2007-05-13
Compiz is better for a couple of reasons.

For one, it's staying. Unlike Beryl, Compiz is not a dead project so it seems rather pointless to tinker with a dead project. However, Compiz and Beryl have humped and created Compiz-Community and returned to their true roots (of being one project in the first place).

Second (in case the first one hasn't scared you away), Beryl has lots of bugs. Compiz does to but the difference between the 2 is that Compiz is being developed where as Beryl will soon cease (if it already hasn't).

Third, if you experiment with Compiz, your experimentation won't be a waste.

Of course, this is all just my opinion. Happy choosing.
