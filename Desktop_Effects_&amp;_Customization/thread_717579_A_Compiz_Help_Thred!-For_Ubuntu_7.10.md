Heres how to group windows 

1. Go into Compiz effect manager 

2.Enable Grouping windows

3.Select window you want to group and press super s

4.do to all windows you want

5.Than press super g

6. Than press super t to tab through theme

7. If you go to top border you can see all the windows cool eh

8.Than press super u to ungroup
