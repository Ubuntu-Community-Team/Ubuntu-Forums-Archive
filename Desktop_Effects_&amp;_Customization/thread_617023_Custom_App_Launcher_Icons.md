### Post by deeesseee on 2007-11-19
Assuming you've already clicked the Spring Icon, you should see the Browse Icon... window. From here, if the icon you want is not in one of the folders in the drop down, click Browse. Now this window only shows folders, no other files. What you want to do is highlight the folder you want (you'd probably be in your home directory) and click Open. Then you'll go back to the Browse Icons... window, where you can choose an icon to use from the folder you selected.

Confusing, I know. I didn't get it at first either.
