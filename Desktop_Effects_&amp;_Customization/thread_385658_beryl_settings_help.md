### Post by kevinf311 on 2007-03-16
I think there is a section in the general Tab of the latest release that says "Shortcuts" that covers most of the key bindings.

Here are some of the general ones that I remember though:

Ctrl+Alt+Hold Left Mouse Button- will put you in cube mode as long as you hold the mouse button
Ctrl+Alt+Click Middle Mouse Button- will do the above until you click the middle button again
Shift+F9- Toggle Rain
Ctrl+Super- Pointer wave
Super+F3- Toggle Snow
Those, along with the Ctrl+Alt+[Directional Button] are the most used ones for me. I have the scale effect bound to the bottom left corner of my screen.
