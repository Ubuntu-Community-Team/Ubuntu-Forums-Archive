Just copy your entire Fonts directory from Windows to /usr/share/fonts/truetype. That's what I did. Then regenerate your font cache:
```

sudo dpkg-reconfigure fontconfig

```

For a good looking sans for the screen you could try Luxi Sans. It's in the the ttf-xfree86-nonfree package.

About Trebuchet, my statement should be taken cum grano salis, and I mainly hate it because it is so overused at the moment.
