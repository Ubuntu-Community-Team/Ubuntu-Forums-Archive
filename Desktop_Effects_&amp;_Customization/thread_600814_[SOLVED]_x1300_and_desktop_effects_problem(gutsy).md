### Post by ozymo on 2007-11-02
In your xorg.conf, you have the "Composite" extension disabled.

In the last block of code, change "Disable" to "Enable" and remove the second line for composite.  This should, after restarting X or GDM, give you the composite extension.

Hope it helps!
