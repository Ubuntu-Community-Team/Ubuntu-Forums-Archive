### Post by crimesaucer on 2007-06-21
Emerald with either a vrunner theme engine or a tru glass theme engine will get you the transparent gloss look...you can create your own window themes really easily, and have any color or level of transparency.

Emerald themes are located in your Emerald Theme Manager.


...also, the junky animation stuff is only on if you enable the plug-ins for it located in the Beryl Settings Manager. 

There are some really useful plug-ins in the Beryl Settings Manager that won't slow your computer down like those plug-ins with the tacky effects.
