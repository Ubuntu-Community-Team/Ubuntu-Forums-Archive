Not sure if anyone double checked this, since I didn't see it mentioned, but I what I found worked for me was stupidly simple.  

Right-click on the Beryl Manager and go down to Select Window Decorator and make sure Standard Beryl Decorator is selected.  

Once I switched to that, just selecting any theme in Emerald switched my theme right away.
