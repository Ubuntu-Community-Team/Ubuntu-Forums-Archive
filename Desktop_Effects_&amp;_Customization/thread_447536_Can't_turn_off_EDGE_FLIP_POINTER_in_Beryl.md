### Post by hyperair on 2007-05-18
I meant the entire plugin, not just the settings. Anyway the setting is at Desktop->Rotate Cube->Edge Flip->Edge Flip Pointer. Edge Flip DND is for drag and drop, Edge Flip Move is for moving windows.

EDIT: Aha I found it! Under General Options->Shortcuts->Screen Edges, change the left edge to none, and right edge to none also. Then it'll stop. :p
