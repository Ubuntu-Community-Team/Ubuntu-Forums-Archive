### Post by rune0077 on 2008-01-12
If you're using Gutsy, you should look for themes in System > Preferences > Appearances. 

If you want to add something to the menus, simply right-click them and select "edit menu"

Many "themes" are actually not complete themes, but just icons, or cursors, etc, etc, and does not show up in the list of full themes. Instead, under themes, click the customize button, then find what you installed there (look for icons, gtk controls, cursors under the relevant tab, it should be there).
