### Post by RedSquirrel on 2007-12-10
Welcome to the forums! :)

First, generate the default menu for Fluxbox:

```
sudo update-menus
```(See the link in my signature if you want a more interesting menu at some point.)

To run Fluxbox, you just choose it from your sessions list at the login screen.

For themes, you just extract them to the directory ~/.fluxbox/styles. After that, they will be available in your right-click menu.
