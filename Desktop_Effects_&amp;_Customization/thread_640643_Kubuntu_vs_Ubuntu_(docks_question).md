### Post by Depressed Man on 2007-12-14
You should be able to. The only difference between the two is the user interface and the apps. KDE for Kubuntu and Gnome for Ubuntu.

So you may come across instructions such as "sudo gedit /etc/apt/sources.list" (to add a repo to your repos). 

In this case you would then switch it to "sudo kate /etc/apt/sources.list"

Gedit, being the text editor in Gnome and Kate being the text editor in KDE (only an application difference).
