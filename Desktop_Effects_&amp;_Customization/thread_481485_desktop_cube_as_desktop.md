You mean, always in rotate mode?!  That would be unusuable, because it locks everything while in rotate mode!

But if you want it to rotate while when your mouse comes close to the screen edge, then it's

"Manager" > Desktop > Rotate Cube > Behaviour > Edge Flip > 'Edge flip pointer' set to on
