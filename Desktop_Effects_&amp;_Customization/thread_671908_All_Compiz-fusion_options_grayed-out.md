### Post by ronmarley1 on 2008-01-19
I think I just answered this a few days ago for someone...
Go into CCSM and click "Preferences" in the lower left.  Then click the Plugin List tab.  I bet the "Automatic plugin sorting" is unchecked.  Tick the box and you should be able to change your settings as before.
Hope this helps.
