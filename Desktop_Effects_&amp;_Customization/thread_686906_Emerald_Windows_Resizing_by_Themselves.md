### Post by PierreQuebec on 2008-02-03
Oh well fixed it myself. Turns out that in my general preferences (System -> Preferences -> Advanced Desktop Effects Settings - - - General Options) the Display Settings Output string I had both 1024x768+0+0 and 640x480+0+0 (only had the 640x480+0+0 after initial configuration,  I added 1024x768+0+0 manually later), so I removed the 640x480+0+0 and everything works fine now.
