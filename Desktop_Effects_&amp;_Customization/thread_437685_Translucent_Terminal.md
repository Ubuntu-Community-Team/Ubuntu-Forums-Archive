### Post by blackened on 2007-05-09
> **Matthaeus said:**
> How do you make the terminal screen translucent? That is, the title bar and typed text are still visible, but the rest of the area is not? I am running beryl, and cant find the right option, if in fact there is one - search turned up little.

Open a terminal window (or alt+F2 -> gnome-terminal):
Go to "Edit" then select "Current Profile...". On the subsequent dialog choose the "Effects" tab and change
the radio button to select "Transparent background", then adjust the slider to your preference of opacity.

> 
Also, is there a way to change the colour of "application, paces, system" writing on the task bar?


Not without editing the gtkrc file for your selected theme. Though this will also have the unwanted effect of changing some other system-wide text color settings if I'm not mistaken.
