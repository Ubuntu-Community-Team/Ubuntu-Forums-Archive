I have no desktop effects enabled. I moved the top panel to the side, added some new icons, made it transparent (just basic configuration stuff). Every time I reboot all icons rearrange themselves randomly. I've tried locking them in place, but this has no effect. I have to move them back manually every reboot. How can I keep them in place?

Thanks for any help.

---

### Post by notwen on 2007-07-04
After arranging your icons in the order of your liking, try closing all applications then go to System--->Preferences--->Session and save your current session.  You could leave some applications open if you want them to be open after a reboot/restart. =p  After you save your session try rebooting and see if that resolves your issue.  Hope this helps. =]
