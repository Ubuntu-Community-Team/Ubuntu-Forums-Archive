### Post by rainwalker on 2008-01-04
If you're running Gutsy, everything is in System > Preferences > Appearance.
Any themes (Metaciy, GTK, icon) you download, usually a .tar or .tar.gz archive, you just drag and drop into that window.
You can easily switch back to your current theme if you don't like it. You don't even have to use every aspect of the theme; you can click the "Customize" button and take different parts of each theme and put them together however you like.
You won't mess anything up switching or installing themes. The worst that could happen would probably be installing a theme that you don't have the correct theme engine for, but that can be easily fixed by searching Synaptic (System > Administration > Synaptic package manager) for the theme engine. Though I have heard a few times that OpenOffice has trouble with dark themes, but I haven't had any trouble.

Enjoy :)
