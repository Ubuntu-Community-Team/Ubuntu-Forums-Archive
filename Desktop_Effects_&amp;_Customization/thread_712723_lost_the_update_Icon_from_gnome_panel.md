### Post by LuisGMarine on 2008-03-02
Oh that's no problem, its quite an easy fix.

Go to *System > Preferences > Sessions*, and under the Startup Programs make sure that "Update Notifier" has a check next to it.

This should do the trick for ya, if it doesn't show up right away don't freak out, just means you have no updates :lolflag:

Hope this helps!
