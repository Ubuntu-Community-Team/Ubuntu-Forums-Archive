### Post by crimesaucer on 2007-07-13
You should try Swiftweasel, pages load almost instantly: [http://swiftweasel.sourceforge.net/](http://swiftweasel.sourceforge.net/)

Choose the correct Swiftweasel for your processor: [http://swiftweasel.wiki.sourceforge.net/Which+Build%3F](http://swiftweasel.wiki.sourceforge.net/Which+Build%3F)

Download it here: [http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=195473](http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=195473)


You can download the correct build for your computer, put it in your /home folder, then install the .deb: [http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#How_to_install.2Funinstall_.deb_files](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu:Feisty#How_to_install.2Funinstall_.deb_files)


...and it should install it to your: /usr/local/swiftweasel

...and also (for your profile folder and your chrome folder): /home/folder/.mozilla/swiftweasel


So now you will still have your old Firefox 2.0.0.4, AND your brand new Swiftweasel 2.0.0.4 installed, and both will have all of your same extensions, about:config settings, bookmarks, and themes...and you can choose which Browser you want to be default.
