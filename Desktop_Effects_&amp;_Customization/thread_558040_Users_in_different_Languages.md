### Post by ddrichardson on 2007-09-25
System/Administration/Language Support

You may be asked to download some files that are required for your current language (files for OpenOffice in my system for example).

You then have a screen showing the languages you want support for, in your case select English and Spanish.

You can then pick the default language for new logins, so in this case you could select Spanish and then create the other users account.


Hit apply and the required language packs are downloaded and installed.
