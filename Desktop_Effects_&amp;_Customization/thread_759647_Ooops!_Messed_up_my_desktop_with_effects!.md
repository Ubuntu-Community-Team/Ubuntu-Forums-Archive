### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-04-19
If you can get a terminal up and running use metacity --replace which should get rid of compiz for the moment at least,  Now change your settings back to something that works and try again.

It's worth getting **compiz-switch**, [http://forlong.blogage.de/article/pages/Compiz-Switch](http://forlong.blogage.de/article/pages/Compiz-Switch) as it lets you enable/disable with a single click, very very useful.
