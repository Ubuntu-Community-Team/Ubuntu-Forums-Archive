### Post by hyperair on 2008-03-18
Just so you know, the problem was due to ARGB visuals not working with GLX by default in NVidia legacy drivers. The proper solution is:
```
sudo nvidia-xconfig --add-arg-glx-visuals
```
and restarting your computer, or just X (Ctrl+Alt+Backspace)
