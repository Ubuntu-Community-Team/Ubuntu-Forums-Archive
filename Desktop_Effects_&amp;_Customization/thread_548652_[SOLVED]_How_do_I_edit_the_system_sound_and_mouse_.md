### Post by smartboyathome on 2007-09-11
You can edit the system sounds via a GUI located at System>Preferences>Sound. You can edit the mouse in System>Preferences>Theme (Feisty and lower), or System>Preferences>Appearance (Gutsy), clicking customize and clicking pointers.
