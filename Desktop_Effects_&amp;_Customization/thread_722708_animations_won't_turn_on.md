### Post by chewearn on 2008-03-13
Are you referring to CCSM?  You need to create a profile.

In CCSM main window, click on Preferences (lower left of the window).  Next to Profile, click on [+] button, type in a name.  Under Backend, choose "Flat-file Configuration Backend".
