### Post by xenoph0be on 2007-05-21
I dont know that I fully understand your question.  But to rotate the cube with the mouse hold <CTRL> <ALT> and your left mouse button.  to rotate with the keyboard only use <CTRL> <ALT> and <LEFT> or <RIGHT>.  If you hold shift while using the keyboard method you can move the window with focus to the next desktop.  Hope that helps.  Also fire up Beryl Manager and go through the short cuts, it'll be a huge help.

### Post by rsambuca on 2007-05-21
If you start the beryl-manager (right click the beryl emerald icon).  Under the general options tab, there is an option titled "virtual desktops".  Set the number to 4 and you are done!
