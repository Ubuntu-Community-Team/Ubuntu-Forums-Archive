### Post by st33med on 2007-04-21
Ok, so you're trying to get to this window that is down at the taskbar. But, they have the same title. What do you do?

Well , instead of shuffling through them, hit ALT-CTRL-UpArrow and hold Ctrl-Alt after that, and you will see all the windows in the current workspace, but they are smaller! Just click on a window to go to it!

This rivals Vista's Aeros interface. And it looks GOOD!
