### Post by Rupertronco on 2007-08-28
You need to enable "animate skydome" to get your desired effect. As far as zooming the cube out go to the Rotate Desktop Cube plugin in your compiz settings manager and at the bottom of the general tab you'll see the option for cube zoom.
