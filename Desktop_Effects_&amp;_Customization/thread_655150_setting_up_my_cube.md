### Post by rune0077 on 2008-01-01
You can also hold down the middle button while the cursor is on an empty space in the desktop. 

The things inside your cube (fishes or gears) are there all the time, but you won't be able to see them through a solid wallpaper. You need to change the transparency of the cube to make them visible.
