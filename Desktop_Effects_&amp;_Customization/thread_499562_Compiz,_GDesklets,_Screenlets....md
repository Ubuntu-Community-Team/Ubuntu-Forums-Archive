### Post by qamelian on 2007-07-12
First, you can't compare Compiz to Gdesklets or Screenlets. Compiz is a window manager while the other two are set off applets that sit on your dekstop. 

Second, Screenlets tends to look nicer than Gdesklets, in my opinion, but their requirements differ. You can run Gdesklets on essentially any PC. To use Screenlets, you must have a compositing window manager like Compiz or Beryl first.
