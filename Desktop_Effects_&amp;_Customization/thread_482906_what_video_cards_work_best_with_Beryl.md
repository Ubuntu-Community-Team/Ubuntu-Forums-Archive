### Post by gkiller on 2007-06-24
> **caseyg said:**
> Hello, I'm looking for insite as to what video cards seem to be most stable and work best with Beryl and / or 3ddesk-top. Thanks
Intel integrated graphics chips should work the best because of the high quality open source drivers. But they are only good for 3D desktop and such, not very much for newer 3D games.

Nvidia cards should work well if they are reasonably new (eg GF 4 series and above). For 3D you need the proprietary Nvidia drivers and I think the older cards are not supported anymore by them.

ATI cards work somewhat ok if you can use the open source driver, AFAIK this is possible with Radeon 9xxx series, and X3xx, X6xx, X8xx cards. Not sure about the X1xxx cards.
