Is there any way to save a konsole session and automatically make it open when I login?

### Post by kpolice on 2007-06-21
Control Center > KDE Components > Session Manager
On Login > Restore previous session

When you turn off your computer or logout leave your konsole window open and the next time you login it will be there.
