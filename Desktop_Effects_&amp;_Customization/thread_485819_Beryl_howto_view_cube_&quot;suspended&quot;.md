### Post by hyperair on 2007-06-27
Ok for the slowmo animations thing.. go to your Beryl Manager, and under General Options, go to Shortcuts, General Options, Bindings, and then look for an item called Slow Animations. The shortcut should be there. Otherwise, you can put your own, or if it's there already you can change it too. 

For the moving cube thing, what do you mean you cannot move backwards? As for zooming, there's a setting in Beryl Settings Manager for toggling how much the cube zooms in. Under Desktop->Rotate Cube->General, you can change the value for Zoom.
