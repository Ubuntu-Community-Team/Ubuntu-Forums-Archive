### Post by santiagoward2000 on 2007-10-25
OK, maybe nobody cares about it (it's been one month and no reply), but still, I solved it and wanted to post it, in case anybody has the same problem.
Just type:
> com.limegroup.gnutella.gui.Main
as Class in Kiba's FrostWire launcher.
That did it for me.
