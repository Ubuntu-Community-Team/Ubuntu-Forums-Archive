People will be more likely to help you if you make the title of the thread more specific than "What should I do" (which is a question and should end with a question mark). That way, people with knowledge on the subject will be able to read your thread as it pertains to their experience. There are a multitude of guides and threads discussing the very things you asked. 

I encourage you to read some of the other threads, and if you have questions that are un-addressed in this forum, then post them. The search tool and sticky posts are your friends. 

I love the support the Ubuntu community has but it's hard to find different topics in this forum seeing as how most threads are like this, in the fact that they are completely redundant, and instead of reading other threads, people post new ones.
