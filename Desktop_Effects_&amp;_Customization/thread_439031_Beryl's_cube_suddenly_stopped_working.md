### Post by hyperair on 2007-05-10
Check and make sure that your "Desktop cube" and "Rotate cube" plugins are enabled. After that, under general settings or something similar to that, make sure your horizontal virtual size is set to 4.

### Post by 65536 on 2007-05-10
I had just the same problems myself, but then I realized beryl window manager was off.
Right click beryl icon and set "Select Window Manager" to "Beryl"
Just hope that's it
