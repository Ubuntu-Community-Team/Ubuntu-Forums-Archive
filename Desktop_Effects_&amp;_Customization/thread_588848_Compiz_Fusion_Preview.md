### Post by Forlong on 2007-10-28
That's an issue with the X-Server. If a window is minimized it's just gone, so Compiz can't show you anything.
Beryl used to make a screenshot of the window but that's just a workaround and the Compiz devs choose to wait until this is fixed in X instead of working around it.
