### Post by Rupertronco on 2007-09-26
To activate the cube by default, hit "Ctrl+Alt + Mouse Button 1 (left click)" and drag it to spin the cube. To flatten the cube press "Ctrl+Alt + Down Arrow"

### Post by Rupertronco on 2007-09-27
Once you've released the buttons, it snaps to that face of the cube. That's kind of the point of the whole thing. You don't actually work on the desktop while it's in cube form, that wouldn't make much sense. 

Try dragging an application off of the screen to the right or left to get it on another side of the cube. You could also use the expo plug-in to accomplish this moving of windows. That way you'll have different applications on different sides of the cube.
