### Post by fazavon on 2007-10-23
I had that same issue. I had to manually download Emerald themes.

[http://themes.beryl-project.org/themes.php?cat=0](http://themes.beryl-project.org/themes.php?cat=0)

You can download them, well anywhere and hit install, once they show up in Emerald manager you can delete the "theme.emerald"
