### Post by xalexbladex on 2007-09-09
To enable desktop effects go to SYSTEM, PREFERENCES, DESKTOP EFFECTS and click on it. then a window will pop up choose enable desktop effects. and tick ether windows wobble when moved or workspaces on a cube or both. In the beggining they will not work because the software is not downloaded, so tell you administrator to go to APPLICATIONS, ADD OR REMOVE PROGRAMS. when you go there in the search type in "beryl" then <enter> from the resaults choose Beryl Manager and Beryl settings manager. After you have downloaded them open the programs from APPLICATIONS,SYSTEM TOOLS. from there configure desktop effects.

NOTE: desktop effects is a new and experimental progect from beryl,it still has flaws such as logging in the the top and bottem panels do not appear or logging in and the a whote screen shows. all though it's a cool feature i suggest you don't use this feature until the next distribution of ubuntu, kubuntu, etc.
