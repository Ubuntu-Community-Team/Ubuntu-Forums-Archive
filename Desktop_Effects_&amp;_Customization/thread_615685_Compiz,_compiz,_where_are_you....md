### Post by Zorael on 2007-11-17
You mean, Emerald? Compiz by itself doesn't change the 'looks' of your windows, but it does load a window decorator. This could be Emerald, as well as gtk-window-decorator, kde-window-decorator, among others.

So just run 'compiz --replace' to launch Compiz. And/or 'emerald --replace' to replace your current window decorator with Emerald, for that session.
