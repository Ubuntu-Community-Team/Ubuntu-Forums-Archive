### Post by justsomedude on 2007-10-28
1st question:

Create a launcher on your desktop: 

Type: Location
Name: (whatever you want)
Location: (path to your home directory)

Drag it onto AWN;

2nd question:

*System --> Preferences --> Sessions --> +Add*:

Name: AWN (or whatever you want)
Command: avant-window-navigator
