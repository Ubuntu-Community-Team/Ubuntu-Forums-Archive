### Post by notwen on 2007-05-07
Beryl Settings Manager ---> Window Management ----> Set Window Attribs

Here are my settings in there:
n:Gnome-panel:90
w:PopUpMenu:80
w:Menu:80
w:DropdownMenu:80

You can get more options and an idea of hwo to get them to your liking at beryl's website. Hope it helps. =]
