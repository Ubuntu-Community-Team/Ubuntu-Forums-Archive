### Post by Fireblend on 2007-11-04
What kind of theme? Making a login-window theme is not very difficult, just download one, extract it and analyze the contents yourself, modifying it with your own picture or changing the position of the password box is pretty easy, I did this a few days ago and although I do study programming, it didn't require lots of knowledge.

About a metacity/emerald theme, I'm not too sure. I'm guessing it shouldn't be too difficult, but I don't know the details myself, as I've never wanted to make one.
