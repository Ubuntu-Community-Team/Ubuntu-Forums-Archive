### Post by pbw on 2007-05-21
If you like it being consistant with beryl and gnome (or would like to set it to whatever else you want), just pop open ol' kcontrol. Under Regional and Accessability, click on keyboard shortcuts and you'll be able to choose any shortcut you'd like to apply to switching desktops, or any other command for that matter.
