### Post by esodin on 2007-03-22
I am running beryl on top of GNOME (Ubuntu 6.10) I love it and, after hours of playing around, it looks and performs great on dual monitors. 

I do have a problem that I can't seem to fix: Somewhere in those hours of playing around I managed to set some parameter so that (any and all) new windows whether they are file manager, apps or pop-ups, load behind the windows already on the screen. 

How do I get windows to open in the front rather than behind everything else?

(I assume this is a setting in beryl as that's where I have been messing around the most. If I am wrong and it is some other setting please let me know and I'll be double embarrassed :oops:  :oops:)

---

### Post by Waappu on 2007-03-22
Hi

Open Beryl Setting Manager and change General options "Level of Focus Stealing Prevention" to Normal or Low
