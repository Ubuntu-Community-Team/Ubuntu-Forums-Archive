### Post by bbqsauced on 2007-08-31
I finally got Compiz installed, with alot of the different effects and such working fine.  The one main problem I ran into was that the cube wasn't showing correctly.  When I Ctrl + Alt + left click and drag, it only shows a flat pane, and on each side is my first workstation.  I went into the Compiz Setting Manager's General Options and set the number to 4, but it still doesn't do anything.  Anyone know what I can do to get the cube working right?

---

### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-08-31
Open the CompizConfig Settings Manager and go to the General Options>Desktop Size and change the Horizontal Virtual Size to 4. The others should be at 1

---

### Post by bbqsauced on 2007-08-31
Alright thanks for the help, that worked.
