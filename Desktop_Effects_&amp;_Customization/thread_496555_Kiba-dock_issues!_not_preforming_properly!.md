### Post by steevols on 2007-07-16
It's OSS: could be a bug, more often UE (User Error!). So, open the settings for the bottom panel (If you don't have one, make one), and allow windows to overlap it.

Then, poke around in Kiba-Settings and see if you can find an appropriate option.

As to the physics, they are C.P.U. INTENSIVE.
