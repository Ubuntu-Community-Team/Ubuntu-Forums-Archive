### Post by Ianman on 2007-07-19
There are two ways to do it. Just click on and hold on the window titlebar and drag to one side and keep going unti lthe cube spins by itself.

Or you can select the window you want to move and press ctrl+alt+shift and arrow left or right. The window will follow you to the next side of the cube.

Hope this helps.

### Post by Redeyes_Gambit on 2007-07-19
Ahh, yes this can be accomplished by going into the settings for "wobbly" and altering it to have less spring friction. The main annoying thing is throwing the window too far upwards or downwards and losing it LOL. If you do toss a window into oblivion, right-click it in the window list and select "move".
