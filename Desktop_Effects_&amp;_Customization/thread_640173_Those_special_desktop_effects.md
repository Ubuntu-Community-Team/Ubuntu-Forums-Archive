### Post by fracturedmorals on 2007-12-13
Hi there.

Getting cool effects is easily enough done.

First open a terminal and copy and paste the following:
```
sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager
```

Then go to:
System > Preferences > Advanced Desktop Effects Settings

In this dialog, you will have just about any option you can think of.

For the burn effect, just click on the button that says "Animations", and double click on the entry under close that reads "Glide 1".

Another dialog will appear that has a drop-down menu with a rather large selection of animations to choose from.

(Hint: To speed things up.... if you're using an nvidia card, click on "General" and go to the "Display" tab. Uncheck "Detect Refresh rate" and turn the refresh rate bar up to 200")

Hope this stuff helped.
