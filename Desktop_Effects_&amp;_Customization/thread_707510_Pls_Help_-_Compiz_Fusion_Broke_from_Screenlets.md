### Post by Akovia on 2008-02-25
I managed to fix it myself. Somehow compiz shut down all effects when it couldn't do what was asked. I finally noticed that "Visual Effects" was reset to none. Once I put it back to Custom, everything returned to normal. I did back up my compiz settings this time so at least I won't have to start from scratch when I break it again.

I am very happy that compiz decided to shut down the effects instead of just breaking, making it possible to reboot and get to my desktop. I was certain after it happened that I would be hand editing files from the command prompt to get back functionality. Kudos to the programmers.
