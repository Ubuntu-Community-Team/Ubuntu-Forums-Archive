### Post by Happy_Man on 2007-07-11
I'm not sure with the first one, but in response to your second question, those may be different icon themes you are seeing. 

To get some icons, go to [http://gnome-look.org](http://gnome-look.org) and download one you like. Save the file to your desktop. Now open up System --> Preferences --> Themes. Click "customize", go to the "icons" tab, and click install. Navigate to your Icon theme, and press OK. If everything's all right, your theme should install.
