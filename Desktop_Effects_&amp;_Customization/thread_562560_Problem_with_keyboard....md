### Post by BrownCoal on 2007-09-29
Is it a USB keyboard (most likely)? It (should) tend to go away after a few restarts or restart after loading an OS. It has nothing to do with the operating system but rather the BIOS. Try mashing the keys as soon as you turn on the computer or quickly unplugging then replugging the usb connector. Either way once you get the chance, make sure that "usb keyboard support" is enabled in the BIOS, or you may just need to use your previous keyboard to check it. Please reply as to how it goes.
