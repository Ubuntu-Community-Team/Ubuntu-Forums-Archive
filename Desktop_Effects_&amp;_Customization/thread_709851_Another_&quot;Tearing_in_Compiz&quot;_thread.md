### Post by cookieofdoom on 2008-03-02
You'll need to find out the actual refresh rate of your monitor. Mine reports incorrectly as 50 hertz in Ubuntu, but when I use nvidia-settings (program that comes with Nvidia drivers) it shows as 60hz. You'll need to find out what your framerate is, turn off auto-detect like others have told you, turn on sync to VBbank, and set your refresh rate to your monitor's actual refresh rate. You may be able to find the refresh rate in the screen resolution settings dialog, otherwise you can try the nvidia-settings or look at your monitor's specifications.
