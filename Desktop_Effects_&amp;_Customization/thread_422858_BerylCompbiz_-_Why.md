### Post by Ender Black on 2007-04-25
Firstly, I think it is a great novelty.  It just looks great and when you have it working it actually speeds up several applications because it forces your desktop to be rendered on your GPU instead of the CPU.  

Secondly, it really speeds up switching applications if you are a "power user" or a unorganized user who has loads of windows open on lots of different desktops.

I haven't quite figured out why so many are having so much trouble with it though, they both just work out of the box most of the time.  The only gnarly part is using compiz and not selecting a window decorator.  Personally, I found Beryl to be more of a matured program than Compiz with better theme management and better functionality.
