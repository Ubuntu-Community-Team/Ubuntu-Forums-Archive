### Post by rsambuca on 2007-04-12
OK, the screenshot is an easy one:  Press ctrl-Alt-left mouse, then start to move the cube.  You can then keep the left mouse button pressed and let go of the ctrl and Alt keys.  Then press Prt Scr to take a snapshot.  An alternative is to simply put your mouse cursor somewhere over the desktop background (not a window), and press the scroll wheel.  Then move the mouse around while holding down the scroll wheel to move the cube.

The "super" key is the Windows key (usually between the Ctrl and Alt keys).

For the top and bottom of the cube, just change the image in the beryl settings manager.  It should be in the Desktop -> Desktop Cube -> Caps section.  Just insert the images you want for the top and bottom.

Can't help you with the others.
