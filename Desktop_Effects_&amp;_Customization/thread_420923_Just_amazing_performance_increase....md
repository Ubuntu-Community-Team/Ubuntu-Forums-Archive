I think a lot of people forget that the real driving force of XGL,AIGLX,Beryl, Compiz, et. al. is to increase performance. Most people have a 3D sat in their machine doing nothing when you aren't playing games. In fact your card is having to "downgrade" to show 2D graphics. Using Beryl/Compiz *should* increase your performance on the desktop and the better the card, the more noticeably.

To the other poster, make sure you get the fastest hard drive you can, a lot of people miss this one. I really noticed the difference going from 5700 rpm to 7200 rpm.
