### Post by Jerr973 on 2007-06-15
Well I am a nebie, but did figure it out this is how I done it.  Open a terminal window type 
sudo hit enter, if it asks for a p/w then put in your root password hit enter. Then Type "sudo gdmsetup" and hit enter  You will get  a window says Login Window preferences click on local then just drag your new bootslash to it and select it.

Here are a few blue Ubuntu boot-slashes [http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=150]("http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=150")
