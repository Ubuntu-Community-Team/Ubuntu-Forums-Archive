### Post by kellemes on 2007-09-08
Everything will continue to work normally..
The only difference is you can choose your prefered window/desktopmanager (Gnome or KDE) from the login-screen, probably from some button or link named "sessions". You'll see..
If KDE isn't your cup of tea.. you simply choose "Gnome" from the loginscreen session-link/button or whatever.. :popcorn:
From KDE you can install the themes you want to see..
Like diatribe said.. just install kde-core (and if you wish other KDE-goodies) to get going..
