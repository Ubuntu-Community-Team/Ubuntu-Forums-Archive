### Post by Forlong on 2007-09-09
> **zabien1970 said:**
> 1) Filters - Going through ccsm there is a box for filters, what are they and how do you use them? Or is it better to leave it alone
Are you referring to the input field in the top left corner of the application?
That's to make finding things easier. Just type *scale* in there for example and you'll see.
> **zabien1970 said:**
> 2) Crash Handler - In the compiz crash handler there is a box for 'Window Manager Command Line', what should be put here?
Your usual window manager, that came with your desktop environment. If you're on gnome: metacity
> **zabien1970 said:**
> 3)Background Images - I've seen people with backgrounds while using the cube and such and see where to put the image but being a noob to Linux I'm not sure of the format to put in the box.
That's called the *skydome* and the appropiate input field is located at *Desktop Cube &#8594; Appearance &#8594; Skydome*- just google for that term and you'll find lots of pictures you can place there.
