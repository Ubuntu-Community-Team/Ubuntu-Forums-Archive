### Post by aberflasm on 2007-07-30
Did you ever figure it out? I think the shadow around a transparent tilda window looks tacky.

Nevermind. I figured it out. In the CompizConfig Settings Manager  go to Window Decoration  and where it says Shadow Windows just and -program_name of the one you want to remove the shadow for.

EX. Shadow Windows: any -tilda -katapult
