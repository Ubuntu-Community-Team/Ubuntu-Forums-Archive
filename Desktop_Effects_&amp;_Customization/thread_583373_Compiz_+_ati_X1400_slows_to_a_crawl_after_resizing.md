### Post by squashbrain on 2007-10-20
Hello all,
  After carefull investigating and testing, I fixed it.

Steps to fix:
  1: navigate to **System>Preferences>Advanced Desktop Effects Settings**
  2: at the very bottom of the effects manager click on **Resize Window**
  3: changed **Default Resize Mode** to "*Normal*"

Hope this helps anyone out there

Keep at it!!!:)
