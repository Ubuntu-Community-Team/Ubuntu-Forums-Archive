### Post by Het Irv on 2008-04-11
Yes it is normal not to have a desktop on the top and bottom.
To get a different number of desktops goto System->Prefrences->CompizConfig Settings Manager.  
Under the General Tab go to Desktop size and change the Horizontal Size.

If you don't have the CCSM, you can find it in add/remove.
