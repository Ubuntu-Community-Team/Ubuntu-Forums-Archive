### Post by timinphx1 on 2007-07-01
geovino-
     In order to change an Icon that appears in K-menu, you need to use K-Menu Edit.  It's very simple.  Just right click on the item you want to edit, this will bring up K-Menu Edit, open to the Item you chose, where the picture of the Icon is, just click onto that, and it will bring you to the available icons selections.  Hope it helps.  Timinphx1
