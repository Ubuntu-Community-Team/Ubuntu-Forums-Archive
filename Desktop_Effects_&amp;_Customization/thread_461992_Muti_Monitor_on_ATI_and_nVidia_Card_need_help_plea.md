### Post by krismatth3 on 2007-06-06
The errors I see are "Screen 0" and "Screen 1" in your device sections. Remove both of them. Those are only used when you have more than one monitor on a single video card. (Make sure you restart X/gdm or reboot between tries).

If that doesn't work, try changing "Xinerama" to "off" - and restarting gdm/X (or rebooting) - see if you get two seperate X sessions on each monitor.
