### Post by Redeyes_Gambit on 2007-04-20
Pretty easy actually :) . Just click on system>preferences>session . That should pop open a new window with three tabs. The first tab "Startup Programs" is the one you want. Then, just click the "New" button and type whatever you want in the name field and:

```

gdesklets start

```

in the command field. Now whenever you start Ubuntu Gdesklets should be sittin'' pretty on your desktop :) . HTH!
