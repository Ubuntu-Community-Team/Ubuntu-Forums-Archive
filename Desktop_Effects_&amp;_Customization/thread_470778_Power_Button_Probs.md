### Post by Soybean on 2007-06-11
Ah... something has probably gotten "locked to the panel." If you right click on the various things in the panel (the date, the power button, etc), you'll see a "lock to panel" checkbox in the context menu. If that's checked, that particular object won't move. So either the thing you're trying to move is locked, or something is in the way that's locked (the clock, maybe?).

Also, a related tip -- I find that right-clicking and selecting "move" is better for moving things around the taskbar than dragging. You still have the problems with locked things, but I find that it works more consistently and intuitively.
