### Post by kdarkentity on 2007-09-21
to activate the cube open up beryl settings manager, go the the desktop section and and check off the box on the left where it says Desktop cube, if its already checked off than simply control alt left click and than just spin the cube!

if you'd like you can also adjust how many desktops you have. Under general options simply just up the horizontal vertical size you can have anywhere from 1 to 16 sides of the cube.
