### Post by notwen on 2007-06-05
After you've selected and d/l your emerald theme, you can right click on your Beryl Gem in your task bar, go to Emerald Theme Manager.  You should automatically be on the Themes tab, you may want to double check, but from here simply click Import and browse to your said emerald theme.  Hope this helps, be sure to post back if you run into any issues. =]

### Post by Alehbye on 2008-04-26
I know this is really... REALLY old, but when I searched for instructions on how to update an emerald theme, this is the first thing that came up. When you install the theme, if it doesn't automatically update, press Alt+F2 and type```
emerald --replace
``` it should now update automatically when you select a new theme. (This works in Hardy as well- at least it did for me.)
