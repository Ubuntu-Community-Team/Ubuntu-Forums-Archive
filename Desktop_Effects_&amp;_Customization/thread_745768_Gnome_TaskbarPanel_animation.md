### Post by domace on 2008-04-06
Yeah You have to have a 3d video card, if you do, install compiz or compiz manager and go to System-preferences-Advance Desktop Effects Settings
once there you can enable all effects and don't forget to enable custom effects in appearance, system-preferences-appearance- and one last thing if you don't have a 3d video card you still can use metacity themes and gdm themes and stuff like that but no 3d effects or any animations
