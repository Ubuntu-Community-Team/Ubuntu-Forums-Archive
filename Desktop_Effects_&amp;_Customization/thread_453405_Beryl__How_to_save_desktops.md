### Post by Kuoi on 2007-05-25
I found a way for Firefox !

Open Beryl Settings Manager , go to "Windows Management > Set Window Attribs by various criteria"

Click on "Initial Viewport" , and click on the + sign at the right to make a new command.
In the Above left  you must select "Owning programm" , in the middle you type now "firefox-bin" (without qoutes).
At the bootom you can select 1,2 or 3 to make firefox open in an other 'viewpôrt' than the default "0" .

It's the only thing I can get working till now , but it's a start.
