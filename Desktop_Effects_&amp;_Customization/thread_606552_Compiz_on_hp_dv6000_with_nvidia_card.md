### Post by Redenbacher on 2007-11-08
Have you tried installing Emerald Theme Manager through Synaptic and then running it with Alt+F2 "emerald --replace"?

Once you do that, you can go in to the compiz settings, find the window decorator plugin, and replace the command with "emerald". That *should* take care of the problem, in theory. I know I had a similar issue.
