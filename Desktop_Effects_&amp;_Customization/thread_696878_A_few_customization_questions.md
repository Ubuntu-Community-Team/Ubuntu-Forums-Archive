### Post by FuturePilot on 2008-02-14
1. For window themes that have transparency you might want to look into Emerald
```
sudo apt-get install emerald
emerald --replace
```
You can find tons of themes [here]("http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=103&PHPSESSID=a2cada3d7f824e9dc8fe7c44a1708332")

2.That's a little bug right now. There's a solution around here somewhere.

3. There's a how-to [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=659282") for installing extra Compiz plugins.
