### Post by kvonb on 2007-04-25
In the Beryl settings manager, on the very first page under "Desktop Background", is "Desktop manager supports viewports" selected?

If so, then deselect it as it is only for KDE at this stage.

You could also try turning transparency off from the "Desktop Cube" tab.
