### Post by kinngg on 2007-08-20
what are you using? compiz core? beryl? or compiz-fusion?

for compiz core i don't think you can add a skydome by default
but with beryl you go into beryl manager and settings for desktop cube or something like that, look for something called the skydome and add your image

For compiz fusion you go to compiz-config settings on your preferences and you go to desktop cube and appearance tab. enable the skydome and add your image and you're done
