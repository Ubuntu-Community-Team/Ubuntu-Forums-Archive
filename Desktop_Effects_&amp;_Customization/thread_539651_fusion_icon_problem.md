### Post by Iehova on 2007-08-31
I also have this problem, having installed fusion-icon from the .deb provided on this forum.

EDIT: I _had_ this problem. Right click the top menubar and select Properties. Make the panel a little bigger (for me 26 pixels - I assume it'll be the same or similar for you too). *Poof* - there's your image. Looks like it was just a little bit too big for the default panel size (which was 24 for me).
