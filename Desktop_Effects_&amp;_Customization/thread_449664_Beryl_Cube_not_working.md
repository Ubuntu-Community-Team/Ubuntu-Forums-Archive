### Post by Gus_T_Reer on 2007-05-21
As far as I'm aware the only way to get the angle shown in the link is to use ctrl/alt mouse drag. You can rotate the cube in the horizontal plane using ctrl/alt left/right arrows; scrolling the mouse wheel while pointing to the desktop or scrolling the mouse wheel while the cursor is near the side of the screen (just in case you have a maximised window not showing any desktop). Ctrl/alt up arrow shows the top cube cap, ctrl/alt down arrow shows the bottom cube cap. If your caps are black then check the image specified under the caps tab in the cube settings, alternatively check the level of transparency/opacity set.
