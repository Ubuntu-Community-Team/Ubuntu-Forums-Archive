### Post by psyopper on 2007-07-05
When spinning the cube by moving the mouse between desktops there is no way to zoom. However, if you initiate the rotation with your key command then you CAN zoom -- 

In the Beryl Settings Manager go to Desktop and Rotate Cube General Tab. There is a slider for Zoom

Transparency in the cube while rotating is handled in the Desktop Cube - Transparency tab.
