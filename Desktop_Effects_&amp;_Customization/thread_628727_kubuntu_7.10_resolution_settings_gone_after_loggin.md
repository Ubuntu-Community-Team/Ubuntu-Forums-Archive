### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-12-01
Try this
```
Section "Screen"
	Identifier	"Default Screen"
	Device		"nVidia Corporation NV44A [GeForce 6200]"
	Monitor		"S/T 97P/96P"
	DefaultDepth	24
        **Option          "metamodes" "1280x1024_85 +0+0"**
```
Add the line in Bold to the screen section. I'm assuming your res is 1280x1024
The _85 specifies the refresh rate.
