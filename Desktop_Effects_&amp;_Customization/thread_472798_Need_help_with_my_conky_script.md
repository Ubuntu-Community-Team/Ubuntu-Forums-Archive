### Post by shearn89 on 2007-06-13
hey herbster; i don't think there's a particular variable you can set for that - you can change "alignment" to "bottom_right/left", and change gap_x to put it in the middle, but apart from that i think you'd just have to format all your text with tabs/spaces to be horizontal...
I had a look at the possible settings here:
[http://conky.sourceforge.net/config_settings.html](http://conky.sourceforge.net/config_settings.html) and there's nothing on making it horizontal.
