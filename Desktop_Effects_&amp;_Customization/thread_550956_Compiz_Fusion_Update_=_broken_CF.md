### Post by 1337455 10534 on 2007-09-14
OK
happened to me too, but I have reason to believe that it was emerald's fault.
I suggest first of all to remove your Compiz Fusion repos. Then go to system->preferences->desktop effects and turning it off.
Then go to Synaptic and uninstall every compiz and emerald package (do a search to find them [ctl  + F]).
Then pretend everything is perfectly normal, and follow [http://forlong.blogage.de/article/2007/8/26/The-best-way-to-install-Compiz-Fusion-on-Ubuntu-Feisty#](http://forlong.blogage.de/article/2007/8/26/The-best-way-to-install-Compiz-Fusion-on-Ubuntu-Feisty#)
to the letter!!!
