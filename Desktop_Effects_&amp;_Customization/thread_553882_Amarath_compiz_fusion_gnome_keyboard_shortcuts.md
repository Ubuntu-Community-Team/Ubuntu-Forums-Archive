### Post by Forlong on 2007-09-18
As far as I know you just can't bind something to [Alt]+[Super] on Compiz, since they are both second level chooser, which means they require a "regular" key for a shortcut.

But you could bind that feature to [Alt]+[Space] for instance, that wouldn't be too far from what you are used to.
