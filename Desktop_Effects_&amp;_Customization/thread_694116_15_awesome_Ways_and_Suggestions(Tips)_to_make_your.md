[B][CENTER]100 ways to make your ubuntu computer look awesome, yet still run as well as any.
1. visit [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org), and download some awesome themes for ubuntu. I personally suggest Darker theme customized with white3d cursors and edubuntu colour border. (Select Custom

2. Firefox users - upgrade your firefox theme to iFox smooth, or any other black or smooth/clean theme. Opera users - download a cool theme for opera web browser. i personally like lix1_5 it is an awesome black theme.

3. on gnome-look.org, download a wallpaper or create a very cool looking wallpaper. i personally like ubuntu flames, or ice fire ubuntu.


4. Select a screensaver under system/preferences/screensavers/solar_system, It's a cool one that you dont even have to download!

5. Activate special affects under system/preferences/appearence/special_graphics, and select the awesome ones you want. ( i suggest personally the window affect, SUPER 3D!!)

6. (For good code writers or editors only) create your own opera theme with custom pics from your computer, which can look very cool.

7. combine all of your themes into one colour, like black (example: i have firefox black, opera black, and ubuntu black... sometimes black pointers and borders - WHOA!)

8. If you are a good programmer or installer, simply install a graphical doc onto your computer desktop. I suggest spotlight black!

9. Get cube affect under computer graphics.

10. DO NOT DOWNLOAD ANY VIRUS CURSORS OR THAT STUFF

11. Install GNOME icons by Kamel Alpha 1_5. It is very cool!

12. Hook up your Xbox360 LIVE player card profile onto your desktop, its a cool pic.

13. To make your cursor blend in with your black theme on videos, select Black theme on RecordMyDesktop.

14. Add bookmarks to your opera browser on black theme, IT IS SOOOOOO COOL.

15. search google images, so you can have cool pics under your control.
