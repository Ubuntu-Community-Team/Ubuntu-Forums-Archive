### Post by psyopper on 2007-11-04
As previously mentioned, for the Zoom issue have you tried the Enhanced Zoom Desktop? 

On the Animation tab you can adjust the Timestep to give it that bouncy feeling. The higher the number, the bouncier the effect.

On the Mouser Behavior tab you can enable Sync Mouse to have the mouse movement pan the zoomed area - in other words have the zoomed area follow the mouse.
