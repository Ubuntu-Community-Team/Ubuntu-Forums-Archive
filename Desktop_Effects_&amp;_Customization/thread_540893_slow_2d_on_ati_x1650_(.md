### Post by kellemes on 2007-09-02
These are the magic error-mesages..
```
(EE) AIGLX error: dlsym for __driCreateNewScreen_20050727 failed (/usr/lib/dri/fglrx_dri.so: undefined symbol: __driCreateNewScreen_20050727)
(EE) AIGLX: reverting to software rendering
```

You should **disable** AIGLX in xorg.conf
Like so..

Section "ServerFlags"
        Option  "AIGLX" "off"
EndSection 

Instead of AIGLX you need **XGL**, maybe [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=511166") will give you the method of getting it to work?
