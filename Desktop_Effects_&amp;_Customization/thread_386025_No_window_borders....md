### Post by rsambuca on 2007-03-16
We need to see your xorg.conf to know for sure, but I assume you will need to add this line to your "Screen Section"```
Option 	   "AddARGBGLXVisuals" "True"
```

Then you will need to restart X Server(control-alt-backspace)

If that doesn't work, then post your xorg.conf here.
