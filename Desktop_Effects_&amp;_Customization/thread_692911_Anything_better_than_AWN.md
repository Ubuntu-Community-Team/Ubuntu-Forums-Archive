### Post by tact on 2008-02-10
I had been using AWN for about a year now.  Just recently gave cairo-dock a go and am liking it.  

Check it out here....
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CairoDock](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CairoDock)

Install from the debs and its easy and get going.  Download from here...
[https://developer.berlios.de/project/showfiles.php?group_id=8724](https://developer.berlios.de/project/showfiles.php?group_id=8724)

Grab the dock and the plugins and install dock first.

### Post by tact on 2008-02-10
Yep - I like the smoothness of the animations, the graphics all look good.  Nice selection of themes.  Very nice how it works when set to autohide etc.

AWN has a bigger selection of applets but apart from trash and workspace switcher (allowing me to get rid of the standard panel from the bottom edge of my screen) I didnt use them anyway.

I did try to use the AWN media icons applet(s) to control rythmbox but didn't like the amount of real estate it needs(separate icons for play/pause and next).  The single applet in cairo-dock is nice - one icon and you select play/pause by clicking left mouse button and next song with button 3
