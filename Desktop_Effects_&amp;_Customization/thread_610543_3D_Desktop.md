### Post by ajgreeny on 2007-11-12
Firstly you must have a graphics card and driver that allow 3D; without either of those you can not get the effects, so let us know your hardware and driver setup; one or other must be missing at the moment.  In a terminal, type glxinfo and see if there is a comment near the top that says **direct rendering: Yes**.  If not then you don't have 3D graphics running.

If it says yes then go to **System>Preferences>Appearance** and on the right hand tab you can set the desktop effects.
