### Post by mcduck on 2008-04-17
The possible sizes for skydome images depend on your graphics card, but as general rule both horizontal and vertical resolution should be powers of 2.

For example, 512x512, 2048x2048 and 1024x4096 are correct sizes. 1024x1000, 500x500 and 2000x1000 are not.

Because of the way the image is mapped into the skydome, the horizontal resolution is more important for how good your skydome looks like. So I'd rather use 2048x512 than 1024x1024..
