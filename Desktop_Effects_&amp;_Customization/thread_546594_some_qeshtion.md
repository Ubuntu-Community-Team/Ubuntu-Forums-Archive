### Post by Chymera on 2007-09-12
you can't freeze it to a 3-face position.
but you can change the wallpaper behind the cube via an option in the "rotate cube" dialog (you find that somewhere in the settings manager). However keep in mind that the skydome (as it's called) must have a height to width ratio of 1:2, and depending on you graphic card should not excede certain sizes - 2048x1024 works best in most cases.
