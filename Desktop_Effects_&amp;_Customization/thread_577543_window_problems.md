### Post by ChrisShome on 2007-10-26
After lots of mucking around with this problem, I found some advice that I followed and it has worked.

 I am using 7.10 which I graded from 7.04 and on which I had experimented with Compiz - and got into the same problem before the upgrade. I uninstalled Compiz on 7.04 and it fixed the problem. 

In 7.10 all went well until suddenly I lost the bottom panel and tried to restore it - with many  problems. Eventually achieved. Then lost the frames on windows and could only control them from the bottom panel.

Reinstalled Compiz, no luck; uninstalled Compiz, no luck; fresh installed Compiz, no luck. 

Solution: go to System/Preferences/Appearance/ and choose the Visual Effects tab. Select "None". Immediately restored the top and bottom frames on windows. Then switched Visual Effects back on and the change stayed in place.

Hope that this helps other sufferers!
