### Post by DocHoliday52090 on 2007-11-12
Usually themes without transparency and blurring are very lightweight.

In general (and this is a Big generalization), simpler, less extravagant themes are more lightweight. I used to use Emerald for these really fancy ones, but I just uninstalled it and went back to Metacity. The themed 'Blended' on Gnome-look is very simple and fast, runs without a hitch.
