### Post by c0mp13371331337 on 2007-10-26
IMOHO, Compiz-Fuzion, which comes standard on Gutsy, seems much cleaner and more refined than Beryl did.  I don't use either under normal circumstances, as my dual-monitor setup doesn't allow dual-screen compositing at the resolution I want to run.

But I have bumped myself down to a single display temporarily to play around with both Beryl and Compiz, and Compiz definitely takes the cake.  The settings manager is much more cohesive and comprehensive than that of Beryl.

But don't take my word for it, back up your important files and install Gutsy to see for yourself!  Or just install Compiz-Fuzion.  Ya know, depending on how dirty your hands want to get. ;-)
