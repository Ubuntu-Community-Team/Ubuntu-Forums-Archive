### Post by zero244 on 2007-07-20
If your not having stability problems with beryl I think your right uncheck using metacity as a fall back.
I have it unchecked in the beryl settings manager too.
In other words uncheck the crash handler plugin.
That should prevent beryl dropping back to metacity.
Since I replaced a defective video card, beryl has run flawlessly and completely stable.
Its a great piece of code and very cool to use.
