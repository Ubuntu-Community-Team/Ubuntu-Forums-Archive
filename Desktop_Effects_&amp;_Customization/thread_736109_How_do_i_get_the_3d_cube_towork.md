### Post by sparkguitar05 on 2008-03-26
Go to the synaptic package manager (System -> Administration -> Synaptic Package Manager).

Search for compizconfig-settings-manager and install it (right click it, click mark for installation, and click apply).

Once that's installed, go to System -> Preferences -> Advanced Desktop Effects Settings.

Enable the following effects: Desktop Cube, Rotate Cube, Cube Reflection, Cube Caps.  (It might be easier to type the word "cube" into the filter so you can find these more easily.

Once those are enabled, your cube should work.  To move around the cube, hold down CTRL+ALT and hold down the left mouse button and move it around.  You can also hold down the middle mouse button (scroll wheel) if you are at the desktop.
