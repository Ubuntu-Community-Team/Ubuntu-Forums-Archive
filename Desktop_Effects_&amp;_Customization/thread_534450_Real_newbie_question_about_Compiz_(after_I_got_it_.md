### Post by MrSootentai on 2007-08-25
LOL I spent around 20minutes on this till I figured this out.
Ok here's what I did.
After you've downloadanagered the CCSM (compizfusion-settings-manager).
Type alt+F2 and type 'CCSM' and press enter.
Once you've done that click the first icon choice of general options.
Then click the desktop size tab and turn all those up to 4.
Now it should work!.
If you have a problem make sure the ROTATE CUBE option is on.
