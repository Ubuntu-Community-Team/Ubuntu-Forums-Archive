### Post by Tanjer1588 on 2007-12-25
Ok for the Icons at the bottom i found a nice aplication called gDesklets and there nifty little things to add to your desktop like the clocks on the right hand side and the peace at the bottom.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=77694](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=77694)

The Icon launcher on the bottom is called StarterBar, when you have gDesklets and open it, scroll down to the file Toolbar/Launchers and its the only one in there, Then you can drag icons into it and they will show up just fine.
