### Post by mike2357 on 2008-04-12
Try this:

1.  Right-click in an empty area of your panel.
2.  Select "Add to Panel"
3.  Select "Application Launcher" in the upper-left hand area of the window.
4.  Select the Application sub-folder of your choosing, such as Accessories, Games, Office or whatever.
5.  Click "Add"
6.  Click "Close"

That should do it.  Now you have a new button you can click on that will bring up the sub-folder.
