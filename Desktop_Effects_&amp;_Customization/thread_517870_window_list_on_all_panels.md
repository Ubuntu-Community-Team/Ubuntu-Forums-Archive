### Post by hyperair on 2007-08-05
1. You need to configure it at CompizConfig Settings Manager. In the rotate cube plugin, go to actions. For Initiate, check all screen edges, and for the edge button, use Mouse2. Then you can move your mouse to any corner and middle click to grab.

2. Right click on the window list applet and click properties. Uncheck show windows from all workspaces.
