### Post by Mramius on 2008-01-21
Install a little app named cmatrix.

Fire up your favorite editor and enter:

```
#!/bin/bash
echo "[+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+]"
uptime
echo "[+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+=+]"
cmatrix -sb
```

Press any key to stop the Matrix.

I know it's not much and probably stupid to most people, but I thought it was cool as I'm just starting to learn scripting and I'm starting to really dig in to customization.
