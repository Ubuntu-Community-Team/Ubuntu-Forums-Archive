Open the Terminal (Applications>Accessories>Terminal), then paste this

**sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager**
 
Then go to System>Preferences>Advanced etc. Then enable wobbly windows (untick the invert thing) then enable desktop cube, rotate cube, cube reflection and cube caps (near the bottom somewhere.
 
Then you press ctrl alt mouse1 (left click), hold them down and move your mouse around. 
 
The super button is the windows key (you will need that for some plugins.
 
To get multimedia and such working, go here

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=661833](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=661833)
