### Post by magli on 2007-11-29
I am having the exact same problem. I just installed 7.10 on a toshiba tecra M2 with an nvidia Geforce FX Go5200 (32MB ram).

I just found this thread, which was helpful:
[http://ohioloco.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=620812](http://ohioloco.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=620812)

You can skip the check which is causing the abort by running:
```

SKIP_CHECKS=yes compiz

```

When I did that, I got:

