### Post by eczajnik on 2007-10-23
Hi,

I've just turned compiz on but I can't find a way to adjust gamma.

When I had compiz disabled I used "xgamma -gamma 0.65" but now all I get from this is an error message.

Is there a way to change the gamma setting back to 0.65 with compiz enabled?

---

### Post by eczajnik on 2007-10-24
Nevermind, I've added a "Gamma 0.65 0.65 0.65" line in the "Monitor" section of my xorg.conf file. Now it also works on login screen. Problem solved!
