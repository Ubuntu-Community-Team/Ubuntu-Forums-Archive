### Post by chuckyp on 2007-06-18
If you are unfamiliar with tilda its a drop down terminal controlled by a hotkey. i.e. like the consoles in most FPS games.  Default hot key is F1 pressing it will bring down a terminal from the top of your screen.  Add it to System > Preferences > Sessions to have it start on login in and enjoy.

I just installed tilda again after a long absence.  I must say the new GUI and config options are nice.  Upon first running it you are greeted with a GUI for configuration and you can select transparency, hot key, size etc...  Definately one of the most functional eye candy apps out there.
