### Post by rlross4455 on 2007-11-02
I have seemed to have solved the issue myself again. This seems to be happening more than not lately. What i found out is that you have to install Esounds thru the Synaptic Package Manager and restart the computer and the sounds you have choosen thru the Preferences-Sounds and Sounds Tab will become active. Every time you make a change in the sounds selection menu, you have to restart the computer (ie windows) in order for them to take effect. Hope this helps someone. By the way i am using Ubuntu Studio (Gusty Gibbon 7.10) and up-to-date on all the updates.:popcorn:
