### Post by vertigo1980 on 2007-07-17
just noticed a lot of screenshots in the desktops threads that do not have mipmaps enabled where possible. listen to me when i say it is a must, especially for the expo, task switcher and cube plugins, since it gives better scaling (ie no jagged edges). enable them through your beryl or compiz configs.

then open a web page (or anything with text, really) use expo/task switcher and be amazed by the difference. expo plugin examples below. :D

without mipmaps:

[img]http://img174.imageshack.us/img174/9481/nomipmapsws4.png[/img]

with mipmaps:

[img]http://img300.imageshack.us/img300/2664/mipmapspz2.png[/img]

note: if your graphics card is capable of running beryl/compiz, it can probably handle mipmaps. i've been using them from way back ever since the option was implemented in beryl, and now ofcourse compiz fusion, and no perceptible performance decrease.
