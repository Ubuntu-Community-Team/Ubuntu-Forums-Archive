### Post by igknighted on 2007-05-16
Any high end graphics card should do.  ATI is open-sourcing their drivers, so in the long run they might be the better option, but in the short term Nvidia is the way to go.  As for the mobo, make sure you get a model that is proper for your processor.  Then look for DDR2 ram and pci-e for the graphics slot.  After that, look for any reviews of the boards that fit your criteria and how they work with linux.  Also search the forum for the model and look in the hardware db in the wiki as well.
