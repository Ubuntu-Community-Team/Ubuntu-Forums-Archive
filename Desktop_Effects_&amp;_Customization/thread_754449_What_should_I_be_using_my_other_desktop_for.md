### Post by Takmadeus on 2008-04-13
easy, that is called "virtual desktops" and every desktop enviroment (except windows based ones) have them. It is meant to help you organize your windows in the way you see fit.

For example, I have 2 virtual desktops, and my internet based windows (browsers, IM, mail, etc) are located in desktop 1, and all the other stuff (serious work, databases, writer, impress, etc.) are in desktop 2, that way the windows are not cluttered and let me focus on doing my work ;)

Of course that's the way I do it, you can by experimentation find an use that suits you :)
