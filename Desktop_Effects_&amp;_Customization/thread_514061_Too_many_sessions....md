### Post by AtrejuT on 2007-07-31
I'm afraid I can't really tell you how to turn them off but why would you want to in the first place?

I think as long as you don't want to use them no one is forcing you to, you don't even see them, and I don't think they use up any system ressources, at least not noticably.

However if your system crashes for some reason it can be quite useful to have the virtual terminals around - so if I was you I'd just ignore them.
