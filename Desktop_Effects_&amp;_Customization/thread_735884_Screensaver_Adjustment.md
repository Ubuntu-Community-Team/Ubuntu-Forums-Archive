### Post by unoodles on 2008-03-26
No, as of yet there is no simple way to customize them in GNOME.

the hard way to do it in GNOME is to go to /usr/share/applications/screensavers/ and modify the .desktop files. You will have to read the man page for the screensaver to know what switches to apply

the xscreensaver package can customize them, but they will not affect the gnome settings
