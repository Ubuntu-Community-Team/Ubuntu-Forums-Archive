### Post by FuturePilot on 2008-01-19
I've often wondered myself how to change the theme of the dialogs in GDM.
I think you can get a screenshot of GDM with this
```
gdmflexiserver --xnest
```
This will open GDM in a window, then you can take a screenshot of that window.
