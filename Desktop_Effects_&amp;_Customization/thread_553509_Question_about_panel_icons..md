### Post by notwen on 2007-09-18
Some icon themes simply include more icons, depending on what icons you have on your panel, set A may include new custom icons for all of your applications, while set B may only include 3-4 custom icons for your applications.  If the icon theme is constantly maintained by its author, they generally take requests to make new icons for specific icons and can include them in their next release.  I've found that I like to create a custom icon set, generally copied a icon theme w/ the majority of my favorite icons, then change what few icons I do not like or have a better icon for that individual application(s).  Please post back if that is not clear enough for you and I'll try to explain it better. =]
