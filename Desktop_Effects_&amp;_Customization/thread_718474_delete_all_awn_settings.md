### Post by ronmarley1 on 2008-03-08
I'm not positive, but in your Home folder, there's a hidden file called .config (to show hidden files, hit <Ctrl>+H while in your Home folder).  Inside .config is a folder called awn.  I'm guessing that the setting are stored in that folder.  If you delete it and reinstall AWN, you should be back to the default settings.
