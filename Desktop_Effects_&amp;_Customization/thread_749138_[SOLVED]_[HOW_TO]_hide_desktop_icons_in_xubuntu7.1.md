### Post by morgengenuss on 2008-04-08
if you want to hide all icons, simply right-click on your desktop -> desktop settings (or alternatively go to your menu -> settings -> desktop settings), there change to the tab "behavior" and that's where you can decide whether to show no icons at all or whether to hide those mentioned by you.
