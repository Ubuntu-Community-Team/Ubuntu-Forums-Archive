### Post by SomeGuyDude on 2008-02-21
In your home folder, hit ctrl+H. That'll show your hidden folders. Open one called .screenlets (with a . in front). Then, take any archive you downloaded, open it, and put the contained folder inside the .screenlets folder.

Open up your screenlets manager to make sure it's there.
