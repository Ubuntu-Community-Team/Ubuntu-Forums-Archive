### Post by What The Deuce on 2007-07-20
Engage is a great dock, but I am not too sure about its stability.

I am currently running AWN, Fiesty and Compiz Fusion and it has not crashed on me.  It seems to be a very stable dock, but if you want to remove both panels and just get by with a dock then it might not be your best choice... you will lose your clock, and some other features that I don't want to part with - which is why i stick with a top panel.
