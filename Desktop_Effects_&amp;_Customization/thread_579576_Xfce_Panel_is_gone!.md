### Post by Tlon on 2007-10-18

Well, I guess the *first* thing that I do is enable the program-menu on right-click option.  THEN I kill the panel.  For one thing, I think the panels are redundant.  For apps that I use so frequently that most people would put them in a doc, I just assign key-bindings, e.g., Thunar is windows-key+E (same as opening an explorer window in XP).  With that I can access them more quickly than from a doc, AND I free up screen real-estate.  That's my second reason: my dual 24" flat screens sort of double as art displays when I'm not using them for something functional :)
