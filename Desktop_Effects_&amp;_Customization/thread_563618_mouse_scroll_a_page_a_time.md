### Post by jrib on 2007-09-30
In firefox you can visit "about:config" with your address bar and edit "mousewheel.*".  Filter on "mousewheel".  

You specifically want mousewheel.withnokey.action to have value 1 but others may be of interest to you.  See [http://kb.mozillazine.org/About%3Aconfig_entries](http://kb.mozillazine.org/About%3Aconfig_entries).
