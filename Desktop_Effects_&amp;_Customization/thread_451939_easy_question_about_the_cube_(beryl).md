### Post by Bladerunner71 on 2007-05-22
1. Open Beryl settings Manager
2. Go to "Desktop"
3. Check "Desktop Cube" and "Rotate Cube"
4. Click MMB to rotate.

From there, its all just a matter of taste.

Enjoy
