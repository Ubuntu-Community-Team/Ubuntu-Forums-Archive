### Post by FuturePilot on 2008-02-08
Despite the fact that Nvidia has closed source drivers, I think Nvidia wins, no doubt. Their driver has full compositing support, so no XGL hacks or anything like that. 
Intel does have open source drivers, but you probably only get 50% of the performance out of that same card under Linux than under Windows.
