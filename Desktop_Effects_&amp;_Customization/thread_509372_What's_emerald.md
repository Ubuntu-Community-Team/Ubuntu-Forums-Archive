### Post by hyperair on 2007-07-25
@coront: *kills you* =P

There are several kinds of themes, you'll have to mix and match until you find a good combination.
1. Emerald themes -- only skins the window borders.
2. GTK themes -- skin the controls, such as buttons, scrollbars, etc. I think they skin the panels too.
3. Metacity themes -- skin the window borders when not using Emerald with Compiz Fusion/Beryl
4. Icon themes -- skin the icons, such as folder icons and certain program icons.
5. Cursor themes -- skin the cursor.


You can get themes from [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org)
