### Post by Paqman on 2007-12-05
Just to clarify that a little, Compiz-Fusion IS Beryl. The two projects merged, and Compiz is installed by default on Gutsy. So the good news is, you've already got it installed!

Try installing compizconfig-settings-manager from Synaptic to tweak Compiz settings.

Beryl was a fork of Compiz. Beryl rejoined Compiz and Compiz-Fusion is being developed. Compiz-Fusion is Beryl like, and has many plugins.

Basically, Compiz-Fusion is the new name of Beryl after it joined Compiz.
