### Post by suziq on 2007-11-06
Flyingsolo

I'm not a ubuntu genius, but have been playing around on Gusty and advanced Desktop effects setting.
I found out how to initiate the cube while not holding keys down or any mouse button.
I found out how to do it in the CompizConfig Settings Manager
Go to System>Preferences>Advanced Desktop Effect Settings>Rotate Cube>Actions>General>Initiate
What ever your initiate key is, should start you cube to rotate by using the mouse, it keeps it in this setting until you press <esc>

Hope i explained that ok - first time helping out!

You can change the key by selecting it and pressing any key you wish to use!

This works for me - hope it works for you!
