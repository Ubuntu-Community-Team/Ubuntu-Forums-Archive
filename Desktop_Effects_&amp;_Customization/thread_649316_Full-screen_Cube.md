### Post by Afkpuz on 2007-12-24
It sounds like you are using the "zoom" function, not the rotate cube function.  To rotate your cube, first goto "Rotate Cube" then "Actions" then click that little drop down arrow (if you already haven't before).  The option to rotate your cube is "Initiate".  I personally set mine to super+button1, but you can set it to whatever.  Then, to change the amount of zoom out when rotating, goto the general tab within "Rotate Cube" and you'll see the zoom setting.  The bigger the number, the farther out the cube will zoom.

### Post by Afkpuz on 2007-12-24
I already posted how to change the zoom settings earlier.  Here it is again.   In the "Advanced desktop effects settings", goto the "Rotate Cube options".  In the general tab, change the settings for "zoom".  It's at the bottom of the settings on this page.  Move the slider to the right to make your cube zoom out when you rotate it.

EDIT:
If you're trying to make it so that your cube is zoomed out even when you're not rotating, I don't think thats possible
