### Post by dragomirov on 2007-08-24
From your xorg.conf you're using a vesa driver, as you can see here
```

Section "Device"
	Identifier	"Generic Video Card"
	Driver		"vesa"
	BusID		"PCI:0:2:0"
EndSection

```

what kind of graphic card d'you have? AFAIK composite managers (beryl/compiz) work only on ATI/nVidia/Intel chipset, so probably you need to configure your xorg correctly.

You can have a look here[ http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=508769]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=508769") and [here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=533201")
