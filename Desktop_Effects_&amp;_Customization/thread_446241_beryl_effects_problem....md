### Post by dodgePT on 2007-05-20
I use Gimp ofcourse :)

Open your image with Gimp, you'll have the option in "Image" menu (something like "Resize Image"). Before resizing, make sure you turn off "mantain aspect ratio" option (the chain icon, next to the W/H dimensions input fields), otherwise the resulting image will mantain same aspect ratio and your efforts will be lost ;)

Use 1024x1024 or 2048x2048 and you're all set!
