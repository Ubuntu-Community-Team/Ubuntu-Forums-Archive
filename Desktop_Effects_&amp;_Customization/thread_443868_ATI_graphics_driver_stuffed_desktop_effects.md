### Post by CarpKing on 2007-05-15
The closed-source official ATI driver (fglrx) does not support an extension (AIGLX) needed for Desktop Effects to work.  You'll either have to uninstall the driver and go back to the included open-source driver (which isn't usually quite as fast as fglrx) or install XGL.
