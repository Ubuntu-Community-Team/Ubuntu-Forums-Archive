Good, I'm really happy to hear that! It's too bad I couldn't get on here before you used Envy to install drivers. Envy is great when it works (about 90% of the time) and really sucks when it doesn't. The bigger issue is that because you aren't using the drivers from the repos you'll have to reinstall the driver after every kernel update. 

And by reinstall the driver I mean making sure you download both the kernel and the kernel's headers and be prepared to remember where you can run Envy from (or where Envy saved the drivers) in the shell . The next time you reboot after a kernel update X* will *crash.

In the mean time, have fun with Compiz!!
