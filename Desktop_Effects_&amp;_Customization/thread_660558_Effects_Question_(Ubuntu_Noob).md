### Post by PurposeOfReason on 2008-01-06
Do```
sudo apt-get install compiz-config-settings-manager
``` or browse synaptic for something similair. I forgot the exact name. Then right click the background, choose desktop effects and go custom. Choose animations on that screen, go to the close tab, and switch it to burn.
