### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2008-02-13
No.  (not without a lot of work and possibly messing things up)

You would have to prevent nautilus/gnome from drawing the desktop.  This means no right click menu, no desktop icons - including mounted CDs/DVDs/USB drives.  Then you would have to find a video player that can draw on the root window and can loop playback.  That is how you get screensavers to work as a background basically.
