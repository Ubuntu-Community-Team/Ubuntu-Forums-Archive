### Post by 4KvRMU7Nnv on 2007-10-02
After being annoyed that plain old redirecting the game to an offscreen pixmap did not work for all games, I tried to find an alternative to turning off Compiz Fusion before starting up Nexuiz, Xmoto, or some other fullscreen game when I tried something very trivial.  In the compiz settings, under the "window rules" section, put "metacity --replace" in the fullscreen section.  Don't forget to enable the window rules section and then test it out with a 3d game.  I was not so much surprized that it worked as I was that the compositing came back automatically when I quit out of the game! try it for yourself.
