### Post by psyopper on 2007-11-04
Wow, that's a big question! The answer is "not really." As with nost things Linux (as I am sure you are learning) Compiz-Fusion is in a constant state of "Development" and never quite released in a stable state. As such features change, get improved, removed, etc., at the whim of the development group.

I am a huge fan-boy (at 35 yrs old no less) of C-F and though  I'm not much of a dev I have actually been giving thought to wirting one up on a Wiki somewhere as my "donation" to the project. I do recall stumbling upon a wiki-ish manual online not too long ago, I may even have bookmarked it, but I'm at work so it'll have to wait until this afternoon to see if I can find it.

Edit: Here it is, [http://wiki.compiz-fusion.org/]("http://wiki.compiz-fusion.org/"). And it looks to be a good start from the right people. It's made a decent amount of progress since I last looked at it, I'll likely try jumping in and assisting it's development if I can.
