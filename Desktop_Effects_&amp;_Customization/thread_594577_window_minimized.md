Can you retrieve it using "alt"- "tab" (hold alt and press tab until the window you want is highlighted then release alt)

Most simple explanation for this is that you have inadvertently removed your "window list" from the lower panel. You can add this again by right clicking on the panel where you should see the window list and choose "add to panel" you can then add a "window list" from the menu.
