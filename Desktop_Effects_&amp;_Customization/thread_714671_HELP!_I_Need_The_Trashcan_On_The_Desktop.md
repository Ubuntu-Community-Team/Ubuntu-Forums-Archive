### Post by joshrobinson on 2008-03-04
Right click where it says applications on your top panel, click "Edit Menu's"
in the system tools section, click the checkbox next to configuration editor
now click ok and get out of there, click applications go to system tools, and open the configuration editor.

now browse to /apps/nautilus/desktop
click the checkbox next to what you want on your desktop, trash can, computer icon, home folder, network etc.
