### Post by bcmiller on 2007-07-09
Try to look at your System Tools in your Application Menu...

You should find Beryl Manager.  After that is running you can right click on it from the panel icon and highlight select window manager.  Most likely Metacity is selected, switch it to Beryl.

You will have to add this to your startup services to get it to start when you boot.  That is in your admin preferences.

Hopefully this solves your problem.  If it is no longer installed or working you may want to look up a thread for installing Compiz-Fusion.  You get some extra features anyway.
