### Post by ronmarley1 on 2008-02-07

2.  I believe that is Avant Window Navigator (AWN) in the screenshot.  There's a ton of threads here on installing it.

### Post by Spoken on 2008-02-07
Beryl is the now "outdated" version, it has merged with Compiz to form "compiz fusion". search that in your synaptic if you ever need reinstalling of any sort.

1) Open advanced desktop effects setting, and go to desktop cube, under "behavior" there are settings for your "opacity" when zoomed out.

2) that dock is AWN, its a popular dock navigator, just search it on these forums, there are lots of threads about it.
