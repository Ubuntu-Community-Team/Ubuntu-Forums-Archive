### Post by Sam Lars on 2008-02-22
Try installing these packages...
compiz-core
compiz-gnome
compiz-fusion-plugins
compiz-fusion-plugins-extra
compiz-plugins
compizconfig-settings-manager

You should be able to enable it in the Appearance window in visual effects.  Let us know how it goes. :)
