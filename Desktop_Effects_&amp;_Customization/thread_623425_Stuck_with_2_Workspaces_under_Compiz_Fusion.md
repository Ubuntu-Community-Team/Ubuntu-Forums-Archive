### Post by Strofcon on 2008-02-14
I know this is an older thread, but still...

Something I've found is to make sure you set, in the Advanced Desktop Effects Settings manager, the "Number of Desktops" value to 1 (in General Options -> Desktop Size). If this is set to anything more than 1, CompizFusion will force the switcher to show that many workspaces, and they won't work quite right (at least by my preference - they work, just not the way I'd want them to). Once you fix this, either change the Horizontal and Vertical Virtual Size or right click your switcher and set rows/columns there.

Hope it helps!
