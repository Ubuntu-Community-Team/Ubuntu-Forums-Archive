I'm not too sure what you mean about the different themeing engines. I presume you are using Gnome? There are very few "complete" themes for Gnome other than the ones installed by default. You can go to art.gnome.org or gnome-look.org and download icons/gtk themes/nautilus themes and just drag and drop them to the theme manager. Then in the theme manager, click on customize and choose whatever components you like. They change instantly, so you should get an idea of what is being changed.
