### Post by Cresho on 2007-04-22
I'm a linux newbie and just started playing with beryl.  After I finished settings things up, I noticed problems trying to play Nexuiz, neverball, Trackball, or any 3d game for the matter.  Then i ran into something interesting.

Going into the Beryl settings manager->General options->Advanced, I ticked the "unredirect fullscreen windows" and now my games play just fine.  In addition, I also used the beryl manager->advanced rendering options->rendering options->and checked force aigxl.  Now this combo made my setup run very nice.  one last thing, i noticed I had problems with some-things so I clicked on disable gl under beryl manager->advanced rendering options for better performance.

what i want to know is if anybody has or know any combinations which greatly improve the performance of the hardware handling both beryl and games?
