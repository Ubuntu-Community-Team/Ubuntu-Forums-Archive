Also: Is there a list anywhere that shows what will run on which laptop? I know there are hardware compatibility lists out there, but are there some for complete laptops and not just the components?

The list from the [LaptopTestingTeam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/LaptopTestingTeam) does deal with complete laptops, but of course a brand-new laptop on the market may not have been tested yet.

I haven't had too much trouble with Compiz on my low-end Acer. From what I've seen, people with the X1400 ATI card have had much more trouble.

My suggestion: find out what card a laptop has that you're looking to buy and do a search for that card along with "compiz" or "desktop effects" and see what people are saying about it.
