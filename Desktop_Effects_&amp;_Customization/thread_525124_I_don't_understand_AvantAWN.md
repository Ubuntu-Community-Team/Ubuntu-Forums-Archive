### Post by superyounan1 on 2007-09-06
if all you want are launchers, and you're using gnome, simply modify your lower panel by right clicking and choosing properties. Uncheck "expand" and make it larger, then hit ok and remove or move all the extras away from panel, such as the trash can, show desktop button, desktop switcher, and window list. Then you have a simple, pure launcher "dock", and you can modify its look by chaning its color, transparency, or adding an image background.

theres no need to much around with any of the fancy docks like awn, kiba, kxdocker etc. if all you want are launchers and nothing more.
