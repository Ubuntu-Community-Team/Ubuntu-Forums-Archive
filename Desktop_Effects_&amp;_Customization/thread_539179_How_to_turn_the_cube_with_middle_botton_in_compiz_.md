### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2007-08-30
I use the Viewport Switcher Plugin to do this.  Under the actions tab, change the 'Move Next' property to use Button3 (usually the middle mouse button, but not always, so you may have to experiment).

---

### Post by Rupertronco on 2007-08-30
If you just turn on the plug-in the default setting will be - mouse scroll up = next face (button 5) scroll down = previous face (button 4) and if you hold in the center button (3) it will allow you to control the cube the same way ctrl+alt would let you.
