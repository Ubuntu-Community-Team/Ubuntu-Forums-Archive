### Post by rainwalker on 2007-12-03
There is, because I have it set to do that. Let me find it...

Okay, in CCSM, enable the Viewport Switcher plugin. In the "Actions" tab of the plugin, under Desktop-based Viewport Switching, set "Initiate plugin action" to "Button2" or whatever the name of your middle button is.
