### Post by lazyart on 2007-05-17
Right click on the Beryl icon and go to advanced settings.  The first option I believe is Rendering type.  Set it to copy and no more black screens.  This is the official answer from Beryl wrt Nvidia drivers.

I've read also that turning off blur and water will make this go away but I don't know for sure.  Bottom line is the card has run out of texture memory.
