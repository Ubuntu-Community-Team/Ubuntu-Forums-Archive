### Post by AsoSako on 2008-02-22
Installing your graphics drivers through Envy should fix your problems.
[http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html](http://albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html)
Change your setting in Nvidia Settings.
You might also want to try NvClock 
[http://www.linuxhardware.org/nvclock/](http://www.linuxhardware.org/nvclock/)
You can overclock your video card with this and perhaps get the visual effects you like.  (be carefull though Overclocking can be dangerous if you don't know how to do it so try to read some on it first)
