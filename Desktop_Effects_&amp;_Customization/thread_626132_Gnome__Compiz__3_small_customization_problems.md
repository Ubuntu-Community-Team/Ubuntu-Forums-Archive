### Post by lian1238 on 2007-11-29
If you want to rotate the cube at the VERY edge, you could disable Rotate Flip"Left/Right. That's the one where you drag your window to the edge and the cube flips.
To disable it, fire up ccsm, go to Rotate Cube, go the the Actions tab. Rotate Flip is at the bottom. Change the Screen Edge to None on both.
