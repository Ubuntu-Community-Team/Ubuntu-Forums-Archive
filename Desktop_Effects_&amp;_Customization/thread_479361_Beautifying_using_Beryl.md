### Post by tact on 2007-06-20
With your mouse cursor over some part of the desktop background:
- middle mousebutton click and hold then move left/right/up/down to move the cube "freehand".
- scrollwheel quickly flicks cube left or right

THE most useful feature - don't click any button, just "slam" your mouse pointer to the upper right corner of your screen to zoom all open windows onto one face of the cube.
(cannot remember if there was a setting I changed to make ALL open windows on ALL cube faces to zoom like that - the default may only zoom open windows on the current face of the cube)

Note: any windows that are minimised dont show when you "zoom"  like that.
