### Post by Genius314 on 2008-04-06
Go to System>Preferences>Emerald Theme Manager. Click on the "Edit Theme" tab, and go to the "Titlebar" tab. In the box on the right that says "Title-bar object layout," remove the "I" from the box.

Hope that helps. :)
