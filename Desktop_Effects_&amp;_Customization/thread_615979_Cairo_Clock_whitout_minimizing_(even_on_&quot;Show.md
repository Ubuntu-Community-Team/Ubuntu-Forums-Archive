### Post by _Stevie_ on 2008-01-09
go to the "advanced desktop effect settings" and click on general.
in the general options tab head to the 2nd item and untick it (i use a german ubuntu, but the
item should be called something like "hide applications without taskbar entry).

---

### Post by Forlong on 2008-01-09
The option is called **Hide Skip Taskbar Windows**
