### Post by K.Mandla on 2007-02-21
As an added disclaimer from the staff: If you decide to install Beryl, you should be aware that the software is still in its early stages, is under development and comes with the real **[COLOR="Red"]risk of breaking your system or rendering it unusable[/COLOR]**. 

**Beryl is not for beginners**. We realize that a lot of people see the effects and the eye candy and want to try it; we encourage and support that, and hope you enjoy it. However, be aware that it is not necessarily newbie-friendly, and your satisfaction with Beryl will be directly proportional to your expertise with Linux on the whole, and your patience for setting it up.

You should also be aware that **Beryl is not an Ubuntu project**, and **questions about Beryl are better fielded in the [[COLOR="Red"]Beryl forums[/COLOR]]("http://forum.beryl-project.org")**. If you're looking for a distro that uses Beryl by default, [Sabayon linux]("http:/http://www.sabayonlinux.org/") is a preferable choice to Ubuntu.
