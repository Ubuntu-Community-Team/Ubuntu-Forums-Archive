### Post by Apple.boi on 2007-11-23
your honestly expecting too much from both a laptop and a built in chipset. intel gma950 is not meant to be "pro" by any means, thats what intels x3100 is for on laptops. i have a intel GMA 945 and gutsy worked great with it. water effects, fire, wobbly windows, cube all worked but they are of no use, now i didnt turn them off but i toned it down for the sake of battery life if you haven't looked at that aspect yet. if you wanted compiz fusion working at its fullest you should have got a desktop with one of the supported nvidia graphic cards and even some ati cards too now. they have a real GPU for real hardware decoding instead of putting all the strain on the cpu most likely in this case. 
Have you downloaded the compizconfig-settings-manager? thats about all i can offer, but if you computer first and foremost works thats important. sidenote nobody buys macbooks for the gaming, and a tad overpriced for what they got in some cases
