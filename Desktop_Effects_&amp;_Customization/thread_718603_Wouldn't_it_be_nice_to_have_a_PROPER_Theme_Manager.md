### Post by PiggiePaul on 2008-03-08
Wouldn't it be nice for someone to put together a proper theme manager, that pulled in all the custom elements into one "focussed" package.

Rather than the (dare I say it) mess we currently have, where you pick a wallpaper, download a mouse pointer, then the frames you like, oh, and the icon set you want, then Emerald to give you some transparency and drop shadows etc etc etc.

For a beginner it's confusing and frustrating when you see a screen shot of a theme someone has to download, you go WOW that looks cool.

You download it, and then find out, all you got was the window frames and nothing else.

Of course, you'd still be able to customise each element if you wanted.

But how about a FULL theme package, that did just that.

Wallpaper, Icons, Controls, Frames, Mouse Pointer even, Transparancy, Shadows..........

So you could see a demo desktop, go WOW, I want that. Download and instal the theme and you have it.

Be nice......... No?

Actually, I thought the above was the actual meaning of a THEME in the 1st place.
I always understood a THEME changed everything, hence why it was called a THEME.
