### Post by -random on 2008-02-15
hi!
go to
System > Administration > Synaptic package manager.

Then search for 'compiz'. In the list you'll see a 'compizconfig-settings-manager' next to a blank block. Click on it and install.

After installing, right click on your desktop then click on change background and go to visual effects > custom.

Click on Prefrences and you'll see a desktop cube option. Enable it,
have fun!

note: in the settings of a plugin you can find/change the keys in the 'Actions' tab.

Have fun! :D
