### Post by praxis22 on 2007-10-25
Yes and no.

Provided you still run emerald, your beryl theme will stay, This is what I did, even yanked beryl and emerald and reinstalled, compiz-fusion & emerald. But if you try to force reload emerald, it seems to screw up your settings.

compiz --replace& seems to work, but if you try to do the same with emerald it seems to screw up and then drop to a default emerald theme. At least it did with me, and there is no way I can see of getting my old setting back save to re-install parts of Beryl.

YMMV
