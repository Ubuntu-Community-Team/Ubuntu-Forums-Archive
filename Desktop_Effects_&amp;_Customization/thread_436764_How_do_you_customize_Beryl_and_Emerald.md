### Post by Tanker Bob on 2007-05-08
Beryl has a host of customization in the Beryl Manager.  Right click on the system tray jewel and select Beryl Manager.  Browse through the host of settings and play to your heart's content.  To change the skydome backgroung you'll need a graphics file to substitute for the default under Desktop/Desktop Cube/Skydome.  To change the cube rotation characteristics, look in Desktop/Rotate Cube/Behavior/General.  Take the time to explore the individual settings.
