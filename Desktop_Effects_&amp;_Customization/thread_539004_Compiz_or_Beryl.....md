### Post by Rupertronco on 2007-08-30
> **Mia_tech said:**
> I have a confusion, what actually installs the 3D cube on the desktop for ubuntu, Compiz or Beryl?


If you're running feisty (7.04) you already have the desktop cube installed if that's all you're looking for. Go to System>Preferences>Desktop Effects and turn them on. 

The more elaborate effects come from the same program, but with more plug-ins. Compiz-Fusion as said above is the newest version, and it's the one they're currently supporting and updating, I think that would be your best bet.
