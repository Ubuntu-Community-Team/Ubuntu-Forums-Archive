### Post by atomkarinca on 2007-11-09
after you install compiz i suggest you to install compizconfig settings manager, too. with that you can change preferences of the effects.

for the zoom out effect, open ccsm > effects > cube reflection > auto zoom. for the jump effect, select jumpy from reflection mode.

emerald is one way to go for window decorations. the default decorator is gtk-window-decorator. but there are very cool themes for emerald. you can check out both emerald and gtk effects for gnome here:[ gnome-look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org") 

by the way, don't be so afraid to go around changing stuff. the worst case scenario is that you make a fresh install of ubuntu. just remember to backup your stuff every now and then.

I've been goofing around with compiz starting today and I just discovered how to do that!

Press control+alt+left mouse button and drag the cube around part way. Release control and alt but hold left mouse key down and press Print Screen (next to F12 key on my keyboard).

A dialog will pop up on one of the desktops asking if you want to save image; click yes. The result should be something like this.
