### Post by notwen on 2007-04-26
Download the theme.  I believe you'll need to go to System-->Preferences-->Themes.  Once there, you'll need to Import the theme from where ever your browser saved it to, after importing you should be prompted asking if you would like to apply the theme you just imported, if not just go through your currently installed themes until you find the one you just imported.  I'm not positive, but I seem to recall the ability to drag & drop themes into your Theme window as well, you may want to try this method first.  This should work as long as you're not running Beryl/Emerald.  Post back if you have any issues. Good Luck. =]
