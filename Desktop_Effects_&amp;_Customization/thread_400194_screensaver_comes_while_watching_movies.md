### Post by Lexyboy on 2007-04-03
One way would be to turn off the screensaver (System --> Preferences --> Screensaver and uncheck 'Activate screensaver when idle'), though this is obviously not a great solution, though better than wiggling the mouse constantly.

I get this sometimes when watching DVDs with Kaffeine, though not in Totem - I wonder if it's because it's not a gnome app, so gnome doesn't know not to activate screensaver.
