Hey I was just messing around and installed nvidia-settings but it screwed everything up so I re-installed nvidia-glx-new, and now the text on my title bars are all really small, to the point where it is hard to read them. So how do I make the text in my title bars bigger?

---

### Post by Tenken on 2008-02-13
Try going to System-> Preferences-> Appearance -> Font tab, and changing the "Window Title Font" size.
