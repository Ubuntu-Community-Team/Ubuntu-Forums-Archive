### Post by _Pieter_ on 2007-05-07
Of course I can, Mandela! 

In Beryl Settings Manager, (right click on the red diamond next to your clock when Beryl is loaded, choose Settings Manager), select 'Desktop', 'Rotate Cube', and in the 'Behaviour' tab, click on the little arrow next to 'General'. You should see a variety of options, including 'Zoom'. On the 'Misc. Options' tab, you can set the rotation speed. Have fun playing with the settings:) 

You can take a screenshot to make your friends jealous by pressing the print screen button on your keyboard. Ubuntu will automatically prompt you where to save the image. 

Good luck!
