### Post by zero244 on 2007-08-18
If you look around the black screen is fairly common if your drivers and xorg.conf is not setup right.
Your card should be supported to run Compiz-Fusion.
Just do some searching on Google and this forum.
Everything looks good in your xorg.conf, there may be a option or line that is missing to cure the black screen.
I have gotten the White screen and the black screen before. And in both cases it was a inappropriate entry in my xorg.conf file.
By removing those lines the black screen went away.
On this machine I am running edgy with beryl and its running completely stable. I am not having any problems at all.
And I think Compiz-Fusion is just as stable if not more so.
Look around for How Tos with people running a card similar to yours.
If your loading XGL but your Xserver is actually using aiglx that will cause a conflict.
Or if your loading XGL and Compiz is using the nvidia server that will cause a problem
You need to determine which server is actually running and which server Compiz is using.
A single wrong entry in your xorg.conf can cause the black screen problem.
There are a lot of people running the ATI 1600 card so seek them out and find out whats working for  them.
Someone suggested a bios update might fix your problem.
It might fix your problem but I would do that as a last resort.
After youve tried everything else.
Updating your Bios 99 times out of a hundred will not cause a problem, but as you probably know there is a slight risk that you could wreck your motherboard.

Something you might try if you haven't already. Uninstall Compix-Fusion and install Beryl 2x and see if you can get it running ok.

Good luck keep us posted on your progress.
