### Post by tnseditor on 2007-10-24
I would start out by installing Emerald from the repositories.  Next use the emerald theme on this site: [http://www.kde-look.org/content/show.php/Beryl+Aero+Blue?content=62594]("http://www.kde-look.org/content/show.php/Beryl+Aero+Blue?content=62594").  Even though it is kde-look.org it should still be fine.  Here's an icon set you can use: [http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Vista+Transformation+Pack+for+GNOME?content=63106]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Vista+Transformation+Pack+for+GNOME?content=63106").
A GDM (login window) can be found at [http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Avio-Vista?content=64922]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Avio-Vista?content=64922").    A panel bar and "start" button is here: [http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/LiNsta+3+%28Linux+is+Not+Vista%29?content=44570]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/LiNsta+3+%28Linux+is+Not+Vista%29?content=44570").  Also, don't forget to dress up Firefox:  [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/4988]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/4988")
