### Post by bkly-dog on 2008-06-04
Since it looks like nobody ever responded to Rafucho, and I was trying to solve the same problem, here it is:
To disable cube rotation by mouse wheel movement in desktop area, go to compizconfig, click viewport switcher to open the options, and for move next and move prev, (under desktop-based viewport switching) click on the edit buttons and remove the current contents (probably mouse 4 and mouse 5; those seem to be the names of the mouse wheel rotate movements).
