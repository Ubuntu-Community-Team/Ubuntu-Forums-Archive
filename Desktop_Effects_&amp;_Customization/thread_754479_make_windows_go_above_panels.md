### Post by mike2357 on 2008-04-13
The panels have an "auto-hide" feature that may get you a bit more real estate on your screen, if that's what your after.  If you want to give it a try, right-click in an empty area of the panel, select properties, and then experiment with the "Auto-hide" and "Show Hide Buttons" check boxes.
