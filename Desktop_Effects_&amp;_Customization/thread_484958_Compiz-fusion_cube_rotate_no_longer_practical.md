This is something they are working on. Right now what you want to do is reset what you changed to its default. Then enable the "viewport mouse switcher" plugin. That will allow you to rotate the cube with your scroll wheel. Unfortunately, there is now way as of yet to grab the cube with Button2. I'm not sure why they weren't able to just port that functionality from Beryl (has something to do with Compiz-Core upstream), but it is a known issue which I imagine will be fixed soon. It seems that it is keeping a lot of people from switching from Beryl.

If you haven't found it yet, <Ctrl><Alt>Button1 is how to grab the cube.
