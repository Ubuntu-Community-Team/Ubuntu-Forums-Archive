### Post by Ender Black on 2007-04-22
You apply emerald themes from the Beryl Manager's Icon on your taskbar.  If you don't have a red ruby icon on your taskbar then check <applications< <system tools> Beryl Manager.  If it isn't there grab it from synaptic.  Then all you have to do is right click on the ruby and go to select window decorator and choose emerald.  Too easy.
