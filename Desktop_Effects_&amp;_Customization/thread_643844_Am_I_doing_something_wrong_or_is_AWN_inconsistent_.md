### Post by taseedorf on 2008-01-09
The reason you guys are getting the white lines by certain applets, is simply because they process which uses the applet (such as for volume control) is being started by gnome already (what used to be on the panel).....you must stop that from starting up, and than add your applet.
Sorry, I am a Ubuntu noob, but I've found this too work, read it somewhere I believe.

Go to preferences, sessions, and go to current session, and than click on the trash icon next to certain processes which you want to use an applet in awn for.  Than click apply.  I believe a restart is needed.
I also don't recommend doing this for any wireless, since when I shut mine down I believe it wouldn't let me connect to the internet.
