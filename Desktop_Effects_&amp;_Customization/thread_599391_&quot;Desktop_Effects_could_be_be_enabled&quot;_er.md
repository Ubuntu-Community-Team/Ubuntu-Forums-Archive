My Graphic card is ATI X1300 on DELL Laptop.  At the beginning, I was having so much trouble getting the effect to work, followed so many tutorials and still got the error message saying "The Desktop effect could not be started".  I decided to do a fresh install and right after fresh install, I enabled the restricted driver for my graphic card and then i was able to enable my effect without any problems.
