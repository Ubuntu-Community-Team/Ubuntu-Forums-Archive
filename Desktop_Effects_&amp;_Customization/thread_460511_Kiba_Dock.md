### Post by kasulstyls on 2007-08-10
if you want the kiba dock show up on other wondows add this to your fusion setting manager.

1. scroll down to windows management
2. click on windows rules
3. past this in the sticky section "  !class=kiba-dock & type=dock  "    no quotes of course.
4. enable the rule.  

this should add it to all windows.  did on mine!
