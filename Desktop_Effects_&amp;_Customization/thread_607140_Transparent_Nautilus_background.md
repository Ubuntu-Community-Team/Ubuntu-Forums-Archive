### Post by mcduck on 2007-11-09
There used to be a Beryl plugin that made one color (of your choice) transparent, allowing that kind of stuff. But doing it that way caused a lot of problems. There is no way to get partially transparent windows until GTK gets a support for transparency, or program developers hack the support for transparency on their own (like gnome-terminal does).
