### Post by reponzo01 on 2008-04-07
If you installed Compiz and you enabled it in the settings (Right-click the desktop -> Change Desktop Background -> Effects tab -> the Custom Preferences -> Enable Desktop Cube), to activate it, you need to:

Press and hold Ctrl + Alt, then hold down the Left mouse button. Then you can let go Ctrl + Alt, but keep holding down the left mouse button. Then just move the mouse around and you should see your floating cube.
