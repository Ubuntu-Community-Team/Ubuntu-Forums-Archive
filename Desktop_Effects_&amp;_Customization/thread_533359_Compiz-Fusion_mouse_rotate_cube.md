Well after a few updates it appears that by assigning the rotate to button 2 will allow for the two button rotate

System > Preferences > Compiz Settings Manager > Desktop > Rotate Cube > Bindings > Rotate Cube > Initiate > Button 2 (uncheck the cntl alt)
