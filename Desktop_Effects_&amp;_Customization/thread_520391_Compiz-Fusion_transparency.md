Although I had seen this before, it sure didn't seem like something labelled "opacity step" (tooltip = opacity change step) could help me so I didn't bother to experiment.

Not sure what I'm missing by having chosen a reduced opacity step but if it's imporatnt, I'm sure it'll show up.
In the meantime, my VMs are once again looking good.
