Does anyone know of a way to edit the width of the seperator in cairo-dock, or remove it completely?

---

### Post by IanW on 2008-04-07
Right click on your cairo-dock and select Cairo-Dock > Configure
In the resulting window select the lower left button tab marked "Do you want to know more?"
Select the "Icons" tab, and scroll down until you see "> Separators" - click it.
Make your changes here, then "OK" or "Apply" when happy.
