### Post by sultanoswing on 2007-08-29
What version of compiz are you running? I had problems with compiz-fusion on my Feisty 7.04 install (nvdia Geforce 6600GT w/97.55) until I absolutely uninstalled all old versions of Beryl / Emerald and Compiz (including manually deleting old directories and settings folders in /home/user.

I then reinstalled using the CF installer bash script (v. 3.0) and all is well. Who knows - it may solve your problems too (YMMV, and I have to accept no responsibility for a b0rked system :) )
