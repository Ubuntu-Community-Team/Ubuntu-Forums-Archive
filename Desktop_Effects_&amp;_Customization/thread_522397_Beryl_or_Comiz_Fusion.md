### Post by sep1318 on 2007-08-10
Beryl was originally a fork of Compiz. Compiz Fusion is the result of they're re-merging into one, with the Compiz being dericted towards the core, and Beryl focusing on the plugins. Thus Compiz Fusion, once they release, will be the replacement/renaming of whatever you had before, either Compiz or Beryl. Sort of like the Gaim -> Pidgin thing, except with a little more than a name change.
