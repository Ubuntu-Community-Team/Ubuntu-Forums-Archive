### Post by russ.man32 on 2008-03-27
Open evolution

Go to:

[LIST=1]
[*]Edit
[*]Preferences
[/LIST]

Then choose "Mail Prefrences" from the side bar.
Find and open the "HTML Mail" 
Under the Label "Loading Images". Select the option you want from the box:

- Don't want to get any images
- Get images from your contacts only
- Get All images from the internet
