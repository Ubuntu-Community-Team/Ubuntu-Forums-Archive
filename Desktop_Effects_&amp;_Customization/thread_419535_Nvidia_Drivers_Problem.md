### Post by Thiago Tomei on 2007-04-23
You need to install the "new" NVidia drivers. They're called nvidia-glx-new, I think - just search for nvidia in Synaptic and you'll find them. After that, you need to edit your xorg.conf (/etc/X11/xorg.conf - don't forget to make a backup beforehand) and change the driver's name from "nv" to "nvidia".
