### Post by try2lickurelbo on 2007-06-20
I use a tv as my main monitor for my setup, and I used to have a problem with it, but not related to yours. (The tv would only accept 60Hz and all of a sudden, ubuntu switched and then I'd have to restart the machine).  Turns out the only problem with mine was that I had an ATI video card, and the drivers seem to be a little bit shoddy when dealing with tv's.

A couple suggestions:
1. Make sure you're using an nVidia Card.
2. Switch to digital input if you have not already (DVI vs. VGA).

Other than that, I've got no idea what is going on with your system.:confused:
