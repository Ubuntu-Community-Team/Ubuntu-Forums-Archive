### Post by Loevborg on 2007-11-20
Don't worry, there's no harm in removing meta-packages such as "xubuntu-desktop". The packages that were installed because of it will not be removed. The only thing to remember is to re-install this package before upgrading your distribution, which happens at most every 6 month.
