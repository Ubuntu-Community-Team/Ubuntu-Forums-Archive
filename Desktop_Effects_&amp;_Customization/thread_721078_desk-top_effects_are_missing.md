### Post by Live-Dimension on 2008-03-11
First, you have to install the drivers for your graphics card. I don't know about ATI/Intel, but nvidia's aren't installed/enabled by default due to them behing closed source. Go **System - Administration - Restricted Drivers Manager** and enable your display driver. This should download necessory drivers (if needed) and enable 3d hardware acceleration, which in turn means you can enable enhanced desktop effects.

If this doesn't work, please state any information about your GPU that you can.
