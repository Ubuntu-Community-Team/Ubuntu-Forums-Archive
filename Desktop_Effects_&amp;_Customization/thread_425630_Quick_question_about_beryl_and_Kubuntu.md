### Post by raja on 2007-04-27
The quick answer is "no". I have beryl running smoothly on my laptop with an integrated Intel card. It seems a little confusing because all tutorials address ATI or Nvidia cards. Just install beryl, make the necessary changed in xorg and you should be fine.
