### Post by renzokuken on 2008-01-18
[www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) has sections for compiz, beryl and emerald.

the best source about for gnome skins

quick note,

there are two types of 'skin' used in gnome

1. GTK/Controls skins, these are independent of Compiz Fusion so will work regardless
2. Window Borders (Metacity, Emerald etc), i think you have to install emerald for this and then use the Emerald themes for them to work with compiz fusion
