The white screen of death is very elusive -- especially for ATI users. Please don't take this the wrong way,  but you are more likely to find the answers you need at the OpenCompositing forums located here: [http://forums.opencompositing.org/](http://forums.opencompositing.org/)

The one time I had a white screen I rolled back my drivers a version (the binary ATI ones), but that was a while ago, and will not likely help in your case (but its worth a shot)
