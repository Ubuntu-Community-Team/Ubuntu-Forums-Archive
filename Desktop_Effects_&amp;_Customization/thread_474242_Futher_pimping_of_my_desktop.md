### Post by andrewsomething on 2007-06-14
On Gnome, the program you're looking for is called gDesklets. You can find it in Add/Remove Programs, under accessories I believe. Just search for it. It has some issues with Beryl though. There are work arounds in the forums if you look. The key seems to be having gDesklets load before Beryl on start up or else it won't look clean.

### Post by avik on 2007-06-16
> I am looking for a statbar that would go on top of my screen to tell me my network and hard drive usage, memory available, etc.

My personal recommendation on GNOME?  Just right-click on a panel, click Add to Panel, and choose the System Monitor.  Change its preferences so it shows everything you need.  It's much simpler than anything else, at least for that particular usage.
