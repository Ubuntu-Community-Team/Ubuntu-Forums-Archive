### Post by Harv on 2007-12-11
I have GE 6100 graphics on my motherboard and it seems to run the fusion effects just fine.  You have to enable the cube, and cube rotation, and viewport switching, and cube caps.  For these settings I have the compizconfig settings manager(sorry,can't remember where I downloaded it from).  Under cube rotation, make sure you adjust the zoom.  I had windows flipping like a cube but could not see the top and bottom until I adjusted the zoom.


You may have to have the number of workspaces set to four as well.
