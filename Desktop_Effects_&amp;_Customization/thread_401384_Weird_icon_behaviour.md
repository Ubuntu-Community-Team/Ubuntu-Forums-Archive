### Post by PaulFXH on 2007-04-04
I've used avant-window-navigator (svn version) with Beryl in Edgy on three different computers over the last 6 months and I'm very, very impressed.
However, something weird is happening on the third computer (HP Pavilion dv1000 laptop with Intel 945GM card).

Now, when I click on an application's icon from the AWN dock, the app opens and a new icon for that app appears to the left of, and well outside of, the AWN dock.
If I have a number of apps running, all will display icons to the left of the AWN dock with the one that is currently in focus having its full colour but with the others present in B&W only.
Clicking on any of these icons will re-open the app.

But isn't all of this supposed to happen from the AWN dock? It certainly did before for me.

OK, so this is not a really major problem but it does lead to some unnecessary crowding at the bottom of the screen.

Anybody know what's going on here?

---

### Post by PaulFXH on 2007-04-04
Sorry folks, I found out what this was.
Nothing at all to do with AWN. But I also have Screenlets (from [here]("http://forum.go-compiz.org/viewtopic.php?t=358")) on my desktop and I had inadvertently opened the Windowlist screenlet which gave me the additional icons.
Easy to fix and everything back to normal.
