### Post by FuturePilot on 2008-02-12
I had a similar problem with my GeForce2 Go. This is a workaround that worked for me, it may or may not work for you. It's worth a shot though. Go System&#8594;Administration&#8594;Services. Uncheck CPU Frequency Manager (powernowd). Close and reboot. See if it still freezes.
