### Post by notwen on 2007-09-11
Have you considered trying [tilda]("http://tilda.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Main_Page")?  A set key-stroke will drop it down and re-hide it once you're done.  It's not quite what you want, but may suffice to your requirements.  There is a patch to give it real transparency instead of the default pseudo-transparency.  You use x & y parameters to define it's location on your screen.
