### Post by climatewarrior on 2007-10-18
Just go to add and remove in the applications menu and look for Advanzed Desktop Effects Settings. You can find it just by typing compiz in the add and remove search. After you install this a new option called Advanzed Desktop Effects Settings will appear in your System/Preferences menu. With that you will be able to configure  compiz fusion as you wish.

Enjoy Compiz!
