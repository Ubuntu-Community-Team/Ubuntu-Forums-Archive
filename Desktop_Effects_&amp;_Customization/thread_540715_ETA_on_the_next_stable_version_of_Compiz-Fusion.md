Thank you.There has not been even a single "stable" version. Thats why they are called Beta versions. Compiz is still in development and is NOT guaranteed to work for everyone.

AFAIK, even Gutsy is not going to have Compiz Fusion in the standard repos (This could change if things go "really really well" for the CF developers) 

But being a programmer, I know this usually never happens :)

The different versions you see floating around are the repos made by ppl who work on Compiz and have provided it for users who want to use it on Feisty. This way they can provide a lot of feedback to the developers who can rectify the bugs
