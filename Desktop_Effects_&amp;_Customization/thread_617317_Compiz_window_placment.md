### Post by ugm6hr on 2007-11-19
I think you have to turn on some of the Compix Plugins:
Resize Window
Move Window

Go to System->Preferences->Advanced Desktop Effescts Settings
Ensure the above 2 Plugins are ticked / enabled.

Then close all open windows, and reopen a program.
Hopefully that should work.
