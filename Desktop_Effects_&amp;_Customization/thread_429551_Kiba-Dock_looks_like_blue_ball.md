### Post by gashcr on 2007-05-01
That's because there are no launchers on it yet. 

You'll need to some of these 2 things:

1. Open a term and type "populate-dock.sh"
2. Open your menu and drag and drop the apps you want to have in kiba ( drag the icon over the dock until an arrow apears, then drop it and the launcher will be created )


Hope this will help!!
