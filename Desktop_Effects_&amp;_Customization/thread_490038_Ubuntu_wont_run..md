### Post by dwebb86 on 2007-07-02
put the CD in
reboot your computer
wait for an option to appear on one of the bootup screens (this is usually F2 or F12 or something, it varies with different computers)
Hit that button, change to "boot from CD/DVD drive" or whatever variation you have of that... 
depending on the computer you have it will either do that immediately, or restart and do it
you can edit the BIOS to do the same thing, just put your CD/DVD drive at the top of the boot order menu and restart again... and don't forget to put it back to normal once you're done installing Ubuntu

even though these directions are kinda foggy because I don't know what type of computer you have or what you have installed, if you follow the general idea you should be able to do this easily.
