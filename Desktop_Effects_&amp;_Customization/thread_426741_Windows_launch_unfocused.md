I'm running Feisty with Beryl and when I launch new windows, and even dialog boxes from within windows, they come up unfocused. I have to click on the taskbar to get control of them. This does not occur when using Compiz or no window manager, so that tells me it is some setting within Beryl that is the problem. I'm sure I'm just missing something though and it's kind of making me mad.

---

### Post by fwojciec on 2007-04-28
Open Beryl Settings Manager and change "Level of Focus Stealing Prevention" to "None"
