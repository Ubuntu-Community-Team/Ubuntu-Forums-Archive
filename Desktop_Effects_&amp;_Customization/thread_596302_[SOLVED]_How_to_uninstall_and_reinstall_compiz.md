### Post by It_Was_Luck on 2007-10-29
This is very simple...

Go to System > Prefrences > Adavanced Desktop Effects Settings

(This is if you have compiz's advanced settings manager installed, if you don't you can get it via the respos if you search "compiz"

Anyway after your there look on the left-side bar. There should be a bunch of options there. There is one near the bottom that says prefrences. Click that. Then right under profile (I'm guessing yours says "Defualt) you can just press Restore to Default.

That will put everything just the way it came.
