The 5600 IS supported by nvidia-glx-new. It's in the Extra repo's so no need for envy or other scripts. I am guessing that you're problem was that your xserver was failing because you had a kernel module conflict befause you didn't properly uninstall ALL nvidia drivers and PURGE them. It's a very common problem. For some reason when you try to use the restricted modules manager, it won't pick the correct driver, then when you do want to try a later driver, like the nvidia-glx-new (9755), it won't work because xserver trys to load the previous driver and not the 1 you just installed. If you don't believe me you can read the compatibility list yourself!

[http://us.download.nvidia.com/XFree86/Linux-x86/1.0-9755/README/appendix-a.html](http://us.download.nvidia.com/XFree86/Linux-x86/1.0-9755/README/appendix-a.html)
