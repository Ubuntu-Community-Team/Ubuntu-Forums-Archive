### Post by jpeddicord on 2008-02-24
Installing Compiz Fusion on Feisty was and is still a bit of a pain. Your best bet is to use Beryl. It is a little outdated, but it is essentially Compiz before the merge. You can find it as "beryl" in the repositories.

Another option would be to upgrade to 7.10 (Gutsy) which has Compiz Fusion on by default. Just run Update Manager from System > Administration and hit the Upgrade button at the top. (If there is no upgrade button, hit Alt+F2 and type **gksu "update-manager -c"** and it should appear.)
