### Post by averagebeatboy on 2007-12-26
No program is perfect.  I am including Mac4Lin in this perfection aspect.  When I install Mac4Lin I install everything with the exception of the PDF section that says IF YOU ARE NOT USING EMERALD AND GUTSY 2.18, I skip those parts of the PDF.  I am using Gutsy Ribbon 7.10, 2.20.1.  Be sure you know what System and Gutsy version you are using.

Otherwise follow the directions carefully and set aside a slice of time when you will not be interrupted.  The MS Fonts are readily available from your applications so if the fonts are confusing you only need to install the Mac fonts and the MS Fonts from the Applications section.  That way you are one step ahead of Mac4Lin.

If you install one package too many say Graphite Icons and Aqua Icons you have done yourself no harm at all.  You can change from the Aqua look or the new Graphite look.

Just be very careful about GRUB, the Start-up Manager, et cetera.  That is why i suggest you have a nice slice of time to look over the PDF before you do anything with Mac4L in this way you have a grasp of the coverlet being laid over your Ubuntu.

A lot of work went into this program: now you just need to give the program back a little of your time and you will find yourself finding it difficult to live without Mac4Lin.

I just love the fonts and once you have them installed you will see what I mean.  Anymore I do not think I could survive without the Aqua Base OSX Font.

Take your time and have fun.  It is not hard nor frustrating.  Just give it a little of your time.

Best of the Holidays.
