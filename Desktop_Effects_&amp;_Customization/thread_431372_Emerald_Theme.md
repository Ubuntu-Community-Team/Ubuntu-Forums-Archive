### Post by mcduck on 2007-05-03
Do you have Beryl installed & running? Then just right-click the Beryl icon in your notification area and select 'Select Window Decorator/Standard Beryl Decorator (Emerald).

To change Emerald theme, choose the theme you want from Emerald Theme Manager, and the from the Beryl icon select 'Reload Window Decorator' to activate the new theme.
