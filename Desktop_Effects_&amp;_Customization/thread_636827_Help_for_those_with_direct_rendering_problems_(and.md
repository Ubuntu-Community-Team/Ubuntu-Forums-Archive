### Post by zaomaster on 2007-12-10
So I know that there are plenty of people on this forum who have the same problems with direct rendering as I did.  Especially because I have an older ATI card that does not work with the xgl or the restricted fglrx driver.  But I found help!

If your problem is with direct rendering then you need to go here:

[http://dri.freedesktop.org/wiki/DriTroubleshooting](http://dri.freedesktop.org/wiki/DriTroubleshooting)

working through this troubleshooting guide solved all my problems.

As well, for those with ATI cards check this out from the Compiz-Fusion website:

[http://wiki.compiz-fusion.org/Hardware/ATI](http://wiki.compiz-fusion.org/Hardware/ATI)

the two links on this page are how to set up compiz with aiglx and fglrx, respectively.


I hope that this helps some people out, and maybe we can get it to be a sticky or highlighted or something.  I know I finally had peace of mind after stumbling upon these.
