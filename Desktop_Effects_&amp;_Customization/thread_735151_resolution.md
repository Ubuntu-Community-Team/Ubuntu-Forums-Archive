### Post by prshah on 2008-03-26

> thank you prshah it worked but the monitor is flickerin too much when i set it to 1280*1024 , a lot, i put the refresh rate at 75 hz as prescribed in my monitor manual. can u suggest what to do.

Change your refresh rate to 85 and give it a go; you will have to run the sudo dpkg-rec.. command again.

Before you do that;
```
sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf .
```

Do you have an LCD or a CRT monitor?

When reconfiguring, choose simple if you have a CRT or Medium if you have an LCD. 

In Simple, choose your monitor size.

In Medium, choose 1280*1024 @ 60 Hz (Most LCD monitors dont use higher frequencies).
