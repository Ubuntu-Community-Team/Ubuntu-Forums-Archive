### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2007-11-06
GTK 2.x - controls (buttons, text boxes, etc), windows color, panel color, scroolbar, etc.
Metacity - window borders (minimize, maximize, close)
Emerald - same as Metacity but made for Compiz/Beryl...thought you can use a Metacity theme in Compiz/Beryl

Compiz and Beryl did merge, but some still use Beryl because it's more stalbe for them.  This is why seperate categories still exist.

This best thing to do is experiment...theming isn't going to do any permanent damage, so don't be scared of it.  Just have fun!

### Post by smartboyathome on 2007-11-06
Compiz and Beryl themes are just Emerald themes, but in different catagories. You are limited to what each engine can do with GTK themes. You are limited to whatever you can do with a graphic editor or coding with Metacity/Emerald.
