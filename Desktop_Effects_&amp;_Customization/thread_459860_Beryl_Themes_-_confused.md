### Post by shof2k on 2007-05-31
The 'normal' gnome desktop uses a window manager called metacity.  Metacity themes can't be used with Beryl.

However Beryl themes are controlled by Emerald.  To open it, right click the red beryl diamond and select  "Emerald Theme Manager"

Sorry can't help you with KDE.
