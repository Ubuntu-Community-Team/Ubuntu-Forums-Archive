### Post by reclusivemonkey on 2007-04-23
Just drag and drop themes from gnome-look.org to the Theme manager window. They should automatically install. Its rare you get a complete theme, so just go to the "Customise" button and look for the theme you installed in the relevant section. Most of them should work in Dapper I think. If you have trouble with a particular theme, just unpack it to see if there are multiple versions (some have different colours), then just drag and drop the folder with the ones you like.
