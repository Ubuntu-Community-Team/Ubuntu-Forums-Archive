### Post by rolf-c on 2007-06-12
> **bertlacy said:**
> I don't see a setting for Pref's, can you tell me what it is under?

Sure, click on Applications, Accessories, Terminal.

This will fire up the default term.

Now here click on Edit, Profiles

Default should be highlighted -

click Edit and select the Effects tab.  

Here you will find the transparency slider. among other options.


Or you can just drop in a different term and use that.  I like aterm for all the options it has and ease of setup.  xfce4-terminal is another good one for transparency.
