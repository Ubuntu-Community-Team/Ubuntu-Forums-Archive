### Post by muadnu on 2007-12-18
If I understood what you want to do, you can use Gnome Color Chooser (you can download it from [http://www.alice-dsl.net/gybrush/]("http://www.alice-dsl.net/gybrush/")). It allows you to choose colors for many different elements in Gnome, and if you go to the Panels menu, it has the option of choosing the size (and type) of the font used in your panels. After shrinking the fonts, you can right-click on the panel, then go to properties and make the panel smaller.
