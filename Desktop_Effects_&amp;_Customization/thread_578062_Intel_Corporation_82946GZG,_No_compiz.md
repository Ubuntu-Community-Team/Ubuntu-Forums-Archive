### Post by dhaneshr on 2007-10-17
Hi ,

It seems that certain Intel graphics chipsets have been blacklisted by Compiz. See [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/140836](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/xserver-xorg-video-intel/+bug/140836)

You may need to put a comment on /usr/bin/compiz entry as follows: 


# blacklist based on the pci ids 
# See [http://wiki.compiz-fusion.org/Hardware/Blacklist](http://wiki.compiz-fusion.org/Hardware/Blacklist) for details
T="   1002:5954 1002:5854 1002:5955" # ati rs480
T="$T 1002:4153" # ATI Rv350
T="$T 8086:2982 8086:2992 8086:29a2 8086:2a02 8086:2a12"  # intel 965
#T="$T 8086:2972" # i965 (x3000)
T="$T 1002:3152 1002:3150 1002:5462 1002:5653 " # ati X300 X600,X600 X700 
BLACKLIST_PCIIDS="$T"
unset T
