### Post by rainwalker on 2008-02-18
Those are called "docks", and there are a couple different ones for Linux.

The most popular, and as far as I can tell, the most stable is Avant Window Navigator. I use it myself, and it's great! See here for installation instructions:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=385981](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=385981)

There's also Kiba-dock, but I haven't used it and I know it's not as popular

The one in the second screenshot is a gDesklet, which are basically like OS X's widgets. If you want widgets, I would go with Screenlets, not gDesklets; they're way more popular and developed a lot more.
To get screenlets, just search [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) for them; there's a nice .deb package for them

Have fun :)
