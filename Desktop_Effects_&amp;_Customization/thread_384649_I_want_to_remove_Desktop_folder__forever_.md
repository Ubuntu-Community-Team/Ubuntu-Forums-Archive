### Post by old_geekster on 2007-03-17
There is a suite of applets that will do what you desire.  They are "gTweakUI" --- Galeon, Menus, Nautilus and Session.  I believe the one that you would use to do what you want is "Nautilus".

If you have the correct repositories installed, you can install "gTweakUI" from Synaptic.  They will be installed in System/Preferences.
