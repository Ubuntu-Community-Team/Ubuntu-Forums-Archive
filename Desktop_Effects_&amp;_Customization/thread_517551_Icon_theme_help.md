### Post by crimesaucer on 2007-08-04
Well, your in the "Windows Icons" section of DeviantART, so they are going to be in Windows format and named after windows apps.


You could download them and convert them into a Linux Icon pack by re-packaging them into one of your Linux icon packs that you have already, like the Human icons.


I would just take the Human icons folder, make a copy of it, and place it into your home folder.


Then, use those windows icons and resize them and rename them in the exact same way as the Human Icon pack.


Then rename the index.theme to your new name, and then in sudo, upload them into your /usr/share/icons directory.
