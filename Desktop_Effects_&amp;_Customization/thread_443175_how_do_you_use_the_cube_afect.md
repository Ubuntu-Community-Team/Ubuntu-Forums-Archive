### Post by weblordpepe on 2007-05-14
Well you gotta have Beryl installed for once thing. Or Compiz. Compiz is installed but not enabled by default on Ubuntu 7.04.
Under System -> Preferences -> Desktop effects.

And ctrl+alt+left arrow and ctrl+alt+righ arrow will spin the cube. ctrl+alt+middle mouse will let you spin the cube.

But if you're asking what people actually use it -for-....then well I use it for the real estate it gives...but you can do that without 3D anyway. I think it'll be more useful once you can have indipendant icons on each side of the cube. And you can apply effects to icons without applying them to the wallpaper.
