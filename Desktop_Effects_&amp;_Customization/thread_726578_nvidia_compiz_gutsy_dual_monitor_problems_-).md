### Post by rune0077 on 2008-03-16
I would suggest you get the latest nvidia driver for your card, since these has fixed a lot of issues that are still present in the drivers that ships with Gutsy. They're available at nvidia's site, and they come with a nice little app called "Nvidia Settings" that allows you to do things like change resolution and set up two monitors relatively easy. 

If you don't feel up to compiling the drivers yourself, you can always get Envy from this site: [http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html](http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html) It's a program that fetches the drivers from the site and installs them for you.
