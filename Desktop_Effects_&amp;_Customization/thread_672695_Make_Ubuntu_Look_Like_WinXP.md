### Post by aysiu on 2008-01-20
I understand your logic, but I disagree.

People who switch from Windows to Mac OS X do not need to have Macs skinned to look like Windows. Apple markets Macs as different, and so people do not expect Macs to act like Windows. Apple's marketing slogan from the 90s was "Think Different."

If you set up Ubuntu to look like Windows, people will all the more expect it to *act* like Windows and then be sorely disappointed when it does not.
