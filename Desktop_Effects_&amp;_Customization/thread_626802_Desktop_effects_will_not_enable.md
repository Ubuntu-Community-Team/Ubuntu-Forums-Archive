### Post by Motorhead Kaze on 2007-11-29
I'm no expert, but finally got Compiz to work with my machine.  First, let me say, I find Compiz to be really unstable (Beryl was too).

I used Beryl with Feisty and now use Compiz with Gutsy.  With me, it's mostly about the Nvidia vid card being a problem.  I had Compiz running with Feisty too but only without Emerald window decorator. (for ME, Compiz didn't work well with Emerald, Beryl did.  Why?  Dunno)   Here's exactly what I did, just *yesterday,* and Compiz is working right now: 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=626666](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=626666) .

About Feisty versus Gutsy, basically they are both good, it really depends on what we're trying to do with them.  For ME Feisty worked better with Beryl and Emerald and worked with video software like Kdenlive.  Gutsy was terrible when I just clicked "Upgrade" but seems to be good with a fresh start.  If you are going to upgrade, make sure you backup everything from your HD, right down to your Firefox bookmarks, and remember what those extra programs you liked were or where you got them, and start fresh.  Oh, and most of the programs I got from Automatix in Feisty, are now in the Synaptic Package Manager...and there is no Automatix.

In addition, in Compiz I didn't need to go fetch a new driver for my printer (I had to in Feisty) or search for NTFS support for my external HD.  Gutsy just came up and asked me right away about the driver for my HD.

Hope this helps in some way.
