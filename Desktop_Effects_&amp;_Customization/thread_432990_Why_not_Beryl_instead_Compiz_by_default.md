### Post by Pox on 2007-05-04
Compiz has been the default up until now because it is simpler, slightly more integrated and doesn't have as many intrusive effects, whereas Beryl is designed to be eye-candy heavy.

And yes, Beryl and Compiz are currently merging - you can check in on the progress at [http://www.opencompositing.org/](http://www.opencompositing.org/).
