1.)  Restart X sans Beryl with CTRL-ALT-BACKSPACE

2.)  Click on System -> Preferences -> Desktop Effects

3.)  Ensure that 'Windows Wobble when Moved' is checked.  Both can be checked but we need at least one.

4.)  Click the 'Enable Desktop Effects' button.

Now, you may lose your window decorations - things like the various window title bars, the minimize-maximize-close buttons....that's ok for this test.  But without those window decorations you'll have to move your windows the old-fashioned way to see if the wobble effects are truly enabled.  To do that:

1.)  Hold down the ALT key.

2.)  Move your cursor to any window.

3.)  Left click and drag that window!  

You should notice some wobble effect as you drag and when you let go.  Let me know your results.

If you did lose your window decorations and want them back, go back to System -> Preferences -> Desktop Effects and click the Enable button again - it's actually a toggle.  That will stop the effects in place.
