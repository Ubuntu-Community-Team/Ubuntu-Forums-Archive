### Post by old_geekster on 2007-03-17
Yes!  Open "Beryl Settings Manager" and go to "Desktop Background".  Check the box "Desktop manager supports viewports".  This will allow a different background on each side of the cube.

I have had problems with my rig freezing when this box is checked and I rotate the cube, however.
