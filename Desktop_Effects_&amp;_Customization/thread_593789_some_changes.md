### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-10-27
If you remove the bottom panel and want it back at some point, just right click on the top panel and select New Panel. And it will come back. But you'll have to add the applets back. Then right click on the panel you just added and select Add to Panel.

To add custom launchers to AWN you can just drag and drop items from the menus on the top panel
