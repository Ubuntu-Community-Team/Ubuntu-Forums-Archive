### Post by jvc26 on 2007-04-17
Depends whether you want the eyecandy. It does have some useful bits, but to be honest its not hard to live without - and also being BETA software its not 100% glitch/bug free and depending on gfx cards can be a pain to set up for a newbie. If you're keen you can get round most of the issues and if you're willing to work and search for a solution that works then thatll be ok. It all depends on whether you want to and what gfx card you have - ATIs are notoriously harder to set up than Nvidia cards.
