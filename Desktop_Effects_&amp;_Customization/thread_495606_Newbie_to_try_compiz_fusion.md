### Post by greenstar on 2007-07-09
Your card won't run Compiz or any 3d desktop, imho. I tried it on a box with Athlon XP 2400 & Radeon 8500 LE & 1 Gig of RAM and it struggled while causing the system to become moderately sluggish. The lowest specs I've seen Beryl run smoothly on is a P4 3.0Ghz 800FSB with GeForce FX5200 & a Gig of RAM. That should give you perspective. 

For some low-demand eye candy, check out Elive at [http://www.elivecd.org/](http://www.elivecd.org/)
