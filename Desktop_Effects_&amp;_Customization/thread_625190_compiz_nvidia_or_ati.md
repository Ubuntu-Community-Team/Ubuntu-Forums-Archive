### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-11-28
I have 3 computers with Nvidia cards. I'm not sure how to compare to an ATI card since I don't really have one. Well actually I do but it's ancient and isn't even supported by any of the drivers except the "ati" driver. But anyway I have a GeForce 6600 256MB card in the PC and it handles Compiz great. Very smooth. It doesn't slow it down at all. 
I also have a laptop with a GeForce 8400 GS 320MB (512 if you count the extra it can pull from the system RAM) and it also handles Compiz great. It's so smooth it's like liquid! :D
My other older laptop has an old GeForce2 Go 32MB in it. Never worked too well with Compiz. The black window bug was the biggest issue with Compiz. But I don't think you would have to worry about this because most of the cards today will have enough VRAM to avoid this, plus Nvidia improved their Texture From Pixmap extension which greatly reduced the problem. I'm waiting for the next Legacy driver to be released because it's also supposed to include the fix and I may finally be able to run Compiz on this old thing. (Something Vista could never do :twisted: )
