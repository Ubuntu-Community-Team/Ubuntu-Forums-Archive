### Post by kryliss on 2007-12-18
Okay, I'm new to compiz But I did find the area that you were talking about. I'll list where it's at for those that weren't able to find it.

In the CompizConfig Settings Manger, click on
Preferences
Then up on top click on Plugin List
Take the check out of Automatic Plugin sorting
Next scroll down on the left colum and select move
now click on the arrow pointing to the right, this will add it to the Enabled Plugins.
Now you should be able to move the friggen windows :)
