### Post by gamelord12 on 2007-10-06
So I wanted to get rid of one of the panels that are set up by default by GNOME.  I moved my workspaces, trash can, and desktop button to my top panel and then deleted it, forgetting that now I can't see any of my minimized windows.  How do I make the other panel do that?  If I can't, how do I get back the old panel the way it was.  A GUI way to solve this would be preferable if it exists.

---

### Post by Stebalien on 2007-10-06
Right click on the panel, select "Add to Panel", then drag "Window List" on to the panel.

---

### Post by kyphi on 2007-10-06
Right click on your top panel, choose 'New Panel'.  Right click the new panel and choose Properties.
Select 'Orientation' Bottom.  Then right click the new panel and choose Add to Panel and select what you want on your panel.
