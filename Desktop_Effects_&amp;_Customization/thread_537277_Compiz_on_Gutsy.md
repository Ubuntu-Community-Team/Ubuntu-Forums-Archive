### Post by st33med on 2007-08-28
By the way, it's Compiz Fusion that is installed on Gutsy, not Compiz.  Try 'ccsm' in the terminal. And, desktop effects are what Compiz Fusion is activated by, *not* 'compiz --replace'. I don't have Gutsy, however, I do know what repository it is using. compiz --replace ends up trying to use emerald when it isn't installed.

So, just go to 'Desktop Effects' and turn it on.
