### Post by D-- on 2007-04-03
Hi all.

I love the look of QNX but always hated how the Quinx theme left you stuck with blue. After reading some documentation on the Xfce site, I updated Quinx so it now uses the new features of Xfwm4.

This means Quinx will inherit your GTK colors, so Quinx can be colored like Human. I've attached two screenshots using my Quinx4 theme where you can see the Human GTK theme and the Xfce 4.2 GTK theme in action.

I also dropped one pixel of the black border to make the edges less drastic when you are using the Xfwm4 compositor to put drop-shadows on your windows.

To install:

```
$ tar zxpvf Quinx4.tar.gz
$ mv Quinx4 ~/.themes/
```

Then just open the Xfce control panel, click Window Manager and pick it.

Hope other QNX fans/Ubuntu users enjoy.
