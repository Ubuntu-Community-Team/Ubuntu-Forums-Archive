### Post by mayfer on 2007-03-15
here's how i fixed it on my radeon 9600xt:
right click on **beryl-manager**, go to **advanced beryl options**, **rendering path**, and then choose "**copy**"

that did the trick for me.

if that doesn't work, look at what some people did [here]("http://forum.beryl-project.org/viewtopic.php?f=36&t=3756")
