### Post by Ub1476 on 2007-05-27
Go to [Gnome-look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org/") 

THEMES:
Click on the category "Metacity", and download the one you want.

To install, go to: System>Preferences>Theme

Click on install and navigate to the downloaded theme.

ICONS:
Click on the category "Icon Themes", and download the one you want.

To install, go to: System>Preferences>Theme

Next click on Customize>Icons>Install.

Finally, navigate to the downloaded icon theme.

Hope that helps:)
