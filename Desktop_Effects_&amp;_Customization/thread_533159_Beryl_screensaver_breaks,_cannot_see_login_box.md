### Post by sr4794 on 2008-01-16
I may have found a fix for your problem... well it worked for me.

Go to the Beryl Settings manager. 'beryl-settings' or by right clicking the icon.

Under general options scroll down until you see the advanced tab. open that up and uncheck the 'Unredirect Fullscreen Windows' option. 

Reload Beryl and this should have worked. This also fixed a bug I had on Gutsy where when I was trying to run a picture slide show the photos would flicker in and out. So annoying. Beryl is definitely a labour of love.
