### Post by sirix on 2007-12-28
I had a similar issue--resizing brought my computer to a halt. After a while I figured out that it was the "Blur" window title/border plugin that was causing the issue. I disabled it and now I'm fine.

Thinkpad T60 with FireGL 5200
