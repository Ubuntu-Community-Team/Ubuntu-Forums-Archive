### Post by muffinhead on 2007-10-23
Well I'd thought I'd do a bit of troubleshooting myself, since a friend dropped me a hint about the emerald theme/manager.

I was having very terrible performance on Gutsy, long lockups, continual graphics glitching/refreshing. Overall the whole interface was extremely sluggish. Viewing the Task/Process manager  said that the desktop environment was using between 45% - 50% of both cores of my Dual Core AMD64 X 2 3800+ @ 2.0 Ghz, meaning that each core of my Cpu had a 45% - 50% load on it.

After doing some searches on this forum, I came across a thread about new Nvidia Drivers released a couple days ago that have a performance benefit. After a bit of fiddling getting the old drivers uninstalled and the new ones installed I restarted GDM, however the problem still persisted.

I remember a friend tipped me off about the emerald theme/manager for Compiz and it could be my problem. Guess what? It was, after uninstalling emerald my CPU load drastically decreased from the 45% - 50% down to the normal 1% - 2% jumping slightly when there is activity. So now everything is back to normal and speedy with the desktop effects/cube.

My guess is that emerald is still bugged, only those who have modern rigs won't notice the extreme performance decrease or as much.

I have a Geforce 6150LE, but it's not the problem since those with much lower specs than mine ran Compiz, AiGLX on Gutsy with no problems or extreme performance decreases.
