I am running Compiz of Gutsy in default configuration on an Aver Travelmate. When moving the mousepointer around by the touchpad very often a switching of the desktop takes place which is quite boring. The moving of the cursor is misinterpreted as a go-to-next-desktop  command. 
Where is the button to disable switching desktops by mouse gestures? Or more precisely: Which of the countless buttons does the trick? :confused:

Friedrich

---

### Post by lian1238 on 2007-12-03
Run ccsm.

Alt+F2, then ccsm.
or System->Preferences->Advanced Desktop Effects Settings

There, you can adjust the settings for the desktop switching.
I'm not sure, but I think the default effect is the Cube.

If it's the cube, click on it, go to Actions and there, you can choose the key combination, mouse button, etc.
