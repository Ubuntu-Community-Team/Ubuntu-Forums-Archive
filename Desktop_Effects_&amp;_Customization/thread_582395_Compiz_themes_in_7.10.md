### Post by DagMan on 2007-10-19
It is supposed to pick up the metacity windows border.  Emerald is in the repos and compiz is supposed to look for it and use it if it's installed.  That worked for me a while back, simply installing it, however at that time it came with no themes and getting them from the emerald theme manager gui did not work.  I uninstalled it and am really happy with the metacity theme I found.  I don't know if themes have been added to emerald in the repos, but try just installing it and then restart the computer, or ctrl-alt-f1, stop gdm , killall compiz, then restart gdm, and see how it goes.
