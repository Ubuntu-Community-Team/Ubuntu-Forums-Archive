### Post by PurposeOfReason on 2008-01-21
What do you want? The clock to be ignored on show desktop? If so, in the compiz setting go to the window rules option and add ```
name=Cairo-clock & type=dock
``` to below, sticky, non resizable, non minimizable, non maximizable, non movable and non closable.
