### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-12-29
Right click on the folder you want to change and go to Properties. Click on the folder icon to the left. Then find the new image you want.

### Post by RockHaxor on 2007-12-29
To customize the way your folder icons look:

Right click the folder icon you want to change

Select Properties

Double click the icon image in the top left hand corner

A window will open allowing you to navigate to your custom icon image you want to use.

Select the new icon and click Open.

Enjoy your new icon.
