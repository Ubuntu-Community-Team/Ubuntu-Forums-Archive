### Post by hyperair on 2007-08-14
My screen's around that size too. I just put it to 1024x768, and run any theme I see which looks nice.

Anyway the GNOME theme cannot change the layout of stuff, just the appearance. The entire layout thing remains the same. So if you want less toolbars, you should remove them yourself. Or arrange them neatly into one toolbar, that sort of thing.
