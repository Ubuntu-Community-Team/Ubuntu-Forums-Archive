### Post by craigyjack on 2007-10-24
The background is simply just the blueish background, a regular image.. The purple orbs are not part of the background, they are just icons. Icons for launchers to various directories he has.

To create a launcher, just right-click on your desktop, click "create launcher", then instead of application, select Location from the drop down box, and enter the location you want it to open.
You can then select the icon that will be used for the launcher, and this guy has used his purple orbs. you can change the size by right-clicking on the icon, and select stretch icon (for the big orb).

the drawer thing is just a panel with hide buttons (right click on panel, properties, select show hide buttons)

and the menu button i am actually not sure how to change easily.

### Post by craigyjack on 2007-10-29
i dont know where you got the icons from, but if you want the icons to blend well, you want to make sure the icons have a transparent background so the desktop will be the background of the icon.

you'll most likely want a .png or .svg image as the icon so you can put transparency as the background. many icons come with transparent background already, but if not (or if you are making your own icons), you can use gimp (for the png) or inkscape (for svg) to edit and insert tranparent background.
