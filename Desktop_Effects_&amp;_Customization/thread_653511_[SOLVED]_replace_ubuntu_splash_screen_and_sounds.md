### Post by empthollow on 2007-12-30
the login sound you can change by going to system-> preferences -> sounds there  is an option to choose your own sound for not just login but many other events as well.  the splash screen you can point to a different screen using gconf-editor.  it is somewhere in the apps, i think apps -> gnome-session.  just edit the key to the location of your custom spash screen.
