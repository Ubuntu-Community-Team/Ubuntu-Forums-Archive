### Post by hyperair on 2007-09-07
Firstly it all depends on your window manager. What are you using? The default or "Desktop Effects" or Compiz Fusion or something else?

Secondly, you can use alt+click to move your windows. Just hold alt and click anywhere on the window, then drag.

### Post by Phurious on 2007-09-07
Under the CompizConfig Settings manager, do you have the "Place Windows" plugin enabled under "Window Management"?
