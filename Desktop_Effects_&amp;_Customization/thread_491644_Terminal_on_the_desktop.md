### Post by NeoLithium on 2007-07-03
Sure, you just open up a terminal as normal, then click on EDIT -> PROFILES -> Then click new

It will let you create one named DesktopConsole as it says, and badaboom, badabing, done. OH! One thing I did come across as a problem, that might help you, when you are creating the profile, click the title tab, and where it says Dynamically Set Title, select Isn't Displayed.  Not sure, but those instructions i gave you wouldn't work without that.
