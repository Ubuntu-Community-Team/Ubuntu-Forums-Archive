### Post by Steven_B on 2007-11-06
My guess is that compiz crashed.  Does rebooting or restarting X (ctrl-alt-backspace) not solve the problem?
The background picture is referred to as the "skydome" in the compiz configuration.  Make sure you have  compizconfig-settings-manager installed, then go to Advanced Desktop Settings>Desktop Cube>Appearance>Skydome (at the bottom).
