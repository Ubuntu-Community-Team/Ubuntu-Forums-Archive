How do you in essence zoom out on your cube while it is rotation. I see screenshots of the cube from fairly far away. When I rotate the cube on my desktop its a lot closer.

### Post by smartboyathome on 2007-10-08
You edit the Zoom in the cube preferences (system> preferences > advanced desktop effects > desktop cube). Also, if you don't have advanced desktop effects, install it via synaptic by installing ccsm.
