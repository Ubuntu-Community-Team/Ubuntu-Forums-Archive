### Post by rune0077 on 2007-11-20
Expo: For some weird reason the key-bindings to Expo sometimes gets deleted (or maybe thats just on my system?). Check that you actually have a keybinding (super+E by default) enabled.

Rotate with Wheel: Was your mouse-cursor on the desktop? 'cause it won't work if your pointing inside a window or some other application.

Cube Gears: Your desktop needs to be transparent, then you can see the gears inside the cube.

Shift Switcher: dunno about this one.

Scale: There's several extra scale plugins. Maybe the right one is enabled and its just the extras you don't have on. Or not, I never use it so I wouldn't know.
