### Post by geeree on 2007-11-01
> **bit_racer said:**
> I am having this problem with screenlets,  each time I hit the show desktop button, it also minimize my widgets.... someone knows a way to stop this behavior?

I solved this by enabling the widget layer in Compiz and putting all Screenlet widgets on it. This way the widgets are visible only when you press F9 (default) and the Show Desktop button does not touch them. To put the Screenlets widgets on the widget layer, right click on it and click on "widget".

HTH,
Girish.
