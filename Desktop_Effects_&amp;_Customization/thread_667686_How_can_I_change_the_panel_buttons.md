### Post by mgmiller on 2008-01-20
right click a blank area on the panel, select properties and click the background tab.

Now you can select the solid color for the background and move the transparency slider to make it look the way you like.

I have my color set to white, but the transparency slider all the way to the left (transparent)  This makes the selected window in the bottom panel have a lighter shade of my desktop image.

Hope this helps.
