```
compiz --version
```

### Post by kevinlyfellow on 2007-08-06
> **rainwalker said:**
> That thing about the bug was supposed to be in a different post, oops

I do have a different question, though; What should I do if I'm having a problem and no one else is, and no one seems to know how to fix it? Filing a bug report wouldn't make sense because it's only me (or at least that's how it seems).

Sometimes it is helpful to submit a bug even if you can't find another who has the same problem, because people will usually help you to identify the reason its not working and if it constitutes as a bug.  Unless you've done something unusual to your system, then someone else must have the same issue.  I suggest you submit a bug if you feel like it is something that isn't just something that's going to blow over and it's not induced by yourself.  If you submit a bug that can't be replicated, the worst that will happen is that it will be rejected and a few people would have spent a few moments looking at it.  But if you do submit a bug, then there is a higher chance you will meet someone who does have the same problem.
