### Post by backflop on 2007-07-13
For the Gnome panels, on gnome-look.org, click Other on the menu on the left of the window and in there I found the PNG image I used for my [panel ]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Glass+Panel?content=46587") (If you want some thing else, there's others on that site.)
When you click the download link it will just open a page with the image in the upper left hand corner, but since it is transparent and white it is impossible to see, so just try your best to highlight it and right-click and save image as.

To get transparent window borders, download emerald (via sudo apt-get install), and I think I this will help:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=481314](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=481314)
