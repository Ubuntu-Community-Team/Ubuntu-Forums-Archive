### Post by ichigo on 2007-09-17
If you have the packages installed you need for japanese, just choose japanese as your session language under "options" on your log in screen...

If you find that all your messages are still in English you should check if the packages

language-support-ja language-pack-gnome-ja language-pack-ja ja-trans

are installed.

With this you'll get a japanese desktop, but are still not able to input japanese. for japanese input install anthy & scim (you'll find plenty of information on this in this forum...)
