### Post by {BzF}~JOKesTER on 2008-03-04
For The Transparent Menu Bars {Top, Bottom, Sides, Whatever}
Right Click The Menu Bar You Want To Change - Click Properties - Goto The "Backround" Tab -
Select The "Solid Color Box" {You May Change The Value To Whatever Color You Please} Under The "Style" Bar Slide It Back To "Transparent" For A See Through Look And "Opaque" For A Less Clear Look..

As For The Custom Icon Themes - 

Assuming Your Using Ubuntu Gnome:

Right Click The Desktop - Click "Change Background" - Select The "Theme" Tab - Click And Drag Your Theme {Might Be .tar - .gz ....etc} Ito This Window And When It Askes You If You'ed Like To Apply It Click Yes...

Hope This Helps
