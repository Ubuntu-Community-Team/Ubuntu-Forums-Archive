### Post by computerex on 2008-02-27
> **calman said:**
>  Compiz is the product of years and years of work, and i think it's fantastic..

Umm...Actually it's only the work of a few months....However you are absolutely correct, it is fantastic. The objective of compiz fusion is to increase productivity. It does an excellent job at that, that is if you have configured it correctly to behave in a way that makes you more productive. As an example, work is done a lot faster if you use keyboard combinations more often. So I have routed all the window management combinations to the ALT+SHIFT+cursor keys. ALT+SHIFT+DOWN activates expo, ALT+SHIFT+UP activates the scale plug-in which gives you a real-time graphical view of every window maximized, and allows you to switch focus to one using the cursor keys, and SHIFT+ALT+LEFT or SHIFT+ALT+RIGHT activates the "window ring". If you use it correctly, compiz fusion can make you many times more productive and can make you get your work done a lot faster. Eye candy is great, but don't forget the actual objective, to make you more productive, and of course, to show off windows and Macs ;)
