### Post by rolf-c on 2007-05-01
Regarding compiz vs. Beryl - the Beryl Project is a fork of compiz.  So compiz is the original and Beryl sprung forth from that project.

Now the Beryl Project is merging back with compiz, and that should be complete yet this year.

As for Beryl itself not working, share some specifics (nVidia make and model, driver you are using, xorg.conf) and maybe someone will have an idea.  There are lots of threads and info here on getting Beryl to work with nVidia cards.  I have it running on a GeForce 4200 Ti.
