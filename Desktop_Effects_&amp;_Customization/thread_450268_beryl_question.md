### Post by ericallen on 2007-05-21
open "Beryl Settings Manager", go to "Desktop" then click "Rotate Cube" then "Behavior"  then in the little "general" tab; check the option "zoom before rotate", then set the zoom slider to the level of zoom you want
I usually click 'in' on the mouse wheel and drag the mouse to see the effect
