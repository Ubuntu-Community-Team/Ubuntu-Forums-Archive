### Post by Statik on 2007-12-23
Well, I found it.

I tried both solutions above to no avail. I was always left with the same problem. 

I remembered that on the laptop I had a finer control over the effects being used, so I went looking through the list in synaptec again. I had tried gnome-compiz-manager and it didn't help. This time I tried compizconfig-settings-manager and that added the 'custom' option to my 'visual effects' options. In there I started disabling effects . . . to no avail. Then I started re-enabling effects. When I re-enabled window-decorations, the title bars came back! I tried this a couple of times, disabling and re-enabling that section, and they left and came-back as expected.

It seems like the initial install had the toggle messed up or something. Now everything works great! I just have to figure out how to use them now!

Thanks y'all

Statik
