### Post by harold4 on 2008-02-17
In my experience, avant-window-navigator in atl + f2 does not launch awn.

Open a terminal and run "avant-window-navigator &"

You can also add awn to launch at session open.

EDIT: AWN will not be visible without a composite manager running i.e. compiz
