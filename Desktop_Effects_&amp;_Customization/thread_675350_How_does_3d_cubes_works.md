### Post by rmcder on 2008-01-22
To see the whole cube you need to use CNTL-ALT-LEFT MOUSE BUTTON and hold them down.  Then moving your mouse will manipulate the cube.

### Post by ronmarley1 on 2008-01-23
Use the instructions above to show the cube.  Make sure Desktop Cube and Rotate Cube are enabled.  Zoom under Rotate Cube will regulate how far the cube zooms away from the user.
