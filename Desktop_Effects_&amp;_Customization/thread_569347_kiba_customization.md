### Post by amadeus266 on 2007-10-06
Which version did you install? If it is 0.1.1 from Trevino's repo then just right click on the dock and click populate dock ->add launcher and select the launcher you want from the window that pops up. For the window selector right click the dock, select Kiba-settings and on the taskbar setting uncheck enable. That should do it for you.
