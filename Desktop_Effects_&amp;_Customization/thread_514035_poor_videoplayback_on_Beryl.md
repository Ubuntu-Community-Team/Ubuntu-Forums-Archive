I too am having this problem on my new HP notebook. I had to turn Beryl/Compiz off to clear it up in fact. I am sure those guides posted above will fix the issue with the video's, but I do not think they will improve overall performance in Fiesty much at all --which is very very sloooooow-- for pretty much everything, with 3D enabled. And thats on one of the latest and greatest dual core machines out yet! With 3D off, Fiesty runs blazingly fast on it. This is kind of a shame, because said machine runs even faster than Fiesty on 2D, in Sabayon 3.4 with Compiz Fusion enabled out of the box. The primary issue, I believe, is that Fiesty was not optimized with 3D in mind, as was Sabayon, which is also source based --another huge boost to performance-- but im not knocking Fiesty, which has many great plusses. However, if your all into the whole cutting edge, 3D stuff, then go with Sabayon which is the absolute and clear leader in that arena (and also the reason why Sabayon is the new rising star of the Linux world sitting now at #5 on DW and STILL rising up fast).
