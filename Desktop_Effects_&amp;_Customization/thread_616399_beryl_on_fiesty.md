### Post by Monster_user on 2007-11-18
For Beryl on KDE/Kubtuntu, I've added to the Compiz-fusion wiki.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CompositeManager/CompizFusion#head-126b0241eb27ce7a29b67928c8d941804f97a7be](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CompositeManager/CompizFusion#head-126b0241eb27ce7a29b67928c8d941804f97a7be)

> echo "beryl-manager" > ~/.kde/Autostart/startberyl.sh 

chmod +x ~/.kde/Autostart/startberyl.sh


For Gnome/Ubuntu,...
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CompositeManager/Beryl/Feisty](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CompositeManager/Beryl/Feisty)

> add beryl-manager to the list of startup programs, by going to System -> Preferences -> Sessions, and under the tab Startup Programs and clicking New.
