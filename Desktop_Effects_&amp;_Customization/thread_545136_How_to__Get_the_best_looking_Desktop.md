### Post by hidden_leaf_sasuke on 2007-09-07
Hi !
I've read some comment regarding the looks of Ubuntu which seems too bulky even in high resolution displays.Yes its true. Well , i got a simple tips for you to make your desktop more good looking and not too bulky.

[LIST]
[*]Go to System>Preferences>Fonts
[*]Click Detail. You should see there are Resolution Dots per Inch.
[*]Change the value. The less value indicates smaller fonts. I stick to 85 value (its best for me)
[*]Now for the theme..
[*]Go to [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org). Search for Dream theme or get it [HERE]("http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Dreams?content=61568")
[*]Download it. Open Theme (System>Preferences>Theme) and install it.
[*]Now, apply the theme, but click Customize>Window Border and choose Human (and you can choose anything practically). Now your desktop should be fancier..:) You can change any theme that you want, but window border sometimes getting bigger, only for some theme it get normal or even smaller. Try to customize it yourself. 
[/LIST]
