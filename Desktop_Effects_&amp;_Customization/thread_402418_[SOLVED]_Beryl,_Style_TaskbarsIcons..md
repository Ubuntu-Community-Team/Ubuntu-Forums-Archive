### Post by Happy_Man on 2007-04-05
No, taskbar and icon theming is handled by System-->Prefs-->Theme. You can edit practically everything from there, and installing new ones is as easy as dragging and dropping a .tar.gz file. A great place to find themes for practically EVERYTHING is [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org). Hope this helps!
