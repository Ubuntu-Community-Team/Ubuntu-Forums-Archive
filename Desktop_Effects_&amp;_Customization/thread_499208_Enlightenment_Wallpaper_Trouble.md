### Post by Theimon on 2007-07-16
From their wiki:
> 
**To assign a static picture to your background, simply do:** 
LMB-Click on an empty desktop *"confuguration >> wallpapers"*. You will then see the **Wallpaper Settings window**. From this window, in Basic Mode, you can select either: 
- "Personnal", "System", "Theme Wallpaper" or click the Picture button, gradient button. 
In Advanced mode, you can define different wallpapers to suit your several virtual desktops. When importing a picture, you have the choice of the way E will adapt it to your screen resolution (Stretch - tile & such). 
Animated & static wallpapers can be found on [www.get-e.org]("http://www.get-e.org") - *beware the animated ones can drag your system quite a bit*. 
**To assign an Animated Background**, you'll need to download it first into ~/.e/e/backgrounds - they'll be available then in your "personnal" tab on the Wallpaper Settings. 
**Shelves**

 Please look **here** [[[1]]("http://wiki.enlightenment.org/index.php/Gadgets")] first. 
To create a new Shelf, LMB-Click on an empty desktop, then *"configuration > Shelves >> add"* Once there, click on the advanced tab to get much more opportunities to tweak their look. 
-You can have as much of them as you want 
-They can all bear a different configuration 
-They will appear the same on every Virtual Desktop
