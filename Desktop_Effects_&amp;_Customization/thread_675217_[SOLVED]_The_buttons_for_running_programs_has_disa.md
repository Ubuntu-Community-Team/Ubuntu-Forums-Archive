### Post by balaknair on 2008-01-22
I'm not absolutely sure what the problem you've described is, but I think the 'window list' applet is what you need.

Right click on the bottom panel>Add to panel> there under Desktop and Windows select the Window List applet and click +Add. You can then move it to a position of your liking, then Right click it, and Check "Lock to Panel"

Hope this was helpful. 
If this solves your problem please mark the thread as solved.
