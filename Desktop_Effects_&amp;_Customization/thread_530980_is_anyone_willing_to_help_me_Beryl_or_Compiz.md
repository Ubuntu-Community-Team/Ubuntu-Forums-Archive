### Post by Rupertronco on 2007-08-21
Just out of curiosity why did you switch to KDE if Gnome was running just fine? Also, the AiGLX that you commented out is what allows compiz to run, that's why you're not getting any effects. You probably need to purge all of your compiz packages, update your driver, and reinstall them. 

AiGLX is what enables GL accelerated (3d) desktop effects. It's a newer version of the old XGL and is needed to get your effects to work.  Nearly every nVidia card is compatible with AiGLX. This is where your problem lies.

Edit: I forgot to mention, the newest nVidia drivers don't even use AiGLX if you update your drivers and re-install compiz and let kubuntu configure your xorg.conf you should be just fine.
