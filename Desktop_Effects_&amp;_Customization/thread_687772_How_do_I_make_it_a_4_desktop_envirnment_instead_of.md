### Post by ispy on 2008-02-04
Go to system>> Preferences>>Advanced Desktop Effects Settings and click on the General Options Button. click on desktop size tab, and change the value to 4 Horizontal desktops. (three works too, gives you a prism).


that shoudl solve your problem.

### Post by ayoli on 2008-02-04
If you don't have Advanced Desktop preferences settings:
```
sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager
```
And in the prefs, you'll also need to enable the cube if you prefer a cuibe instead of the wall.
