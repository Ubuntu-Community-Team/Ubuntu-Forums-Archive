### Post by tuto on 2008-01-18
i'm getting the same issue too. It's really annoying, but i found a workaround which indeed doesn't solve the problem, but allows me to get rid of those pesky shadow borders without closing mi session:

- Go to the Advanced Desktop Effects Settings
- Look for a window effect, such as "Wobbly Windows" or any other which has effect on the windows (Wobbly Windows works perfect for me, also "Window Decoration" works)
- Disable the effect and wait for the change to take effect. This action will make the stuck shadows dissappear.
- Re-enable the effect.
- Close de settings windows.
- Go get a beer and drink it.


PD: i have 2 computers running gutsy (1 desktop and 1 laptop) and i'm having this problem in both of them.
