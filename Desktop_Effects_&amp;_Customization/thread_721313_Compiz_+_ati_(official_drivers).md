I found out today that  removing entirely the composite option in my xorg.conf does add to the speed of compiz (I assume that removing it -not just disabling it-, equals the same with enabling it).
This was tested on the latest ATI drivers (8.3) and I strongly recommend trying it, compiz is much faster and responsive and finally my VLC video ain't that lousy :)
