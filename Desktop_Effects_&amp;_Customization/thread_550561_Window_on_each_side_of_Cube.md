### Post by njknight on 2007-09-16
I'm not sure what you are really looking for here - but if I'm correct then you just want to open a window, and to have that window then displayed on all other sides of the compiz cube.

Well if you have also got emerald installed together with the emerald themes then for example on my fiesty fawn system all I have to do is click the button on the top left of the open window (the button with 4 dots on it) - the theme is the slate-horn red, and I have uploaded the following image to show you what I mean

When this button is clicked the window appears on all sides of the cube, and the button itself changes to display a single dot.

The button is not available in all themes.....so perhaps a change of theme will give you what you want !
