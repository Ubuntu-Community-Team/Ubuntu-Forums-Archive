### Post by srhlefty on 2007-05-14
It sounds to me like you are on the right track.  The default way to make the desktop cube turn is to either:
--press CTRL-ALT-(arrow key, right or left)
or
--hold down CTRL and ALT, then click and drag with your left mouse button.

Another thing to check is to make sure that the Beryl window manager is active.  Check this by right-clicking on the ruby icon and looking under the "Select Window Manager" sub-menu.  In that sub-menu there's a few radio buttons, and if "Beryl" is the option that's selected, then you have Beryl successfully running on your computer.  If you want to turn Beryl off, then you change the option back to "Metacity"
