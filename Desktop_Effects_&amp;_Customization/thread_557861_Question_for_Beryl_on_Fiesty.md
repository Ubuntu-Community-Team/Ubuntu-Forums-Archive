### Post by spiderbatdad on 2007-09-23
You should be able to edit your panels normally. Right click on the panel, select properties-->Background. From the set the color or transparency to match your desktop.
I recently got Kiba-Dock working with Beryl-Emerald and have been able to eliminate almost everything from the panel.
