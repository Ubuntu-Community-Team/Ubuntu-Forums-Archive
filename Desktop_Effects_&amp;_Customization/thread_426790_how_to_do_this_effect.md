### Post by Mr Greencastle on 2007-04-29
I think I know:

Open Beryl Settings > Visual Effects > Wobbly Windows

Then down to maximize, and set the 'friction' and 'friction k' really low, just mess around with those on low settings and you should be able to duplicate it.

Edit: Ah, I see what you mean now...nevermind

Edit 2: Got it, change the 'Grab' settings and click 'fullscreen' option
