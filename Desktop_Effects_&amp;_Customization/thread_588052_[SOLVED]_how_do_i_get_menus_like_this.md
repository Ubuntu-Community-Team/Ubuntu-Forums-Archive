### Post by 505 on 2007-10-23
If you have Ubuntu 7.10 go to System, Preferences, Appearance and then the Visual effects tab. Select Extra. Now right click on the desktop switcher applet in the lower right of your screen. Choose preferences and set columns and rows to 2. With control+alt+arrow key you can switch.

### Post by denham2010 on 2007-10-26
They are running AWN with Xfce. That is the Xfce menu.

With the Xfce menu, you can just right click anywhere on the desktop to see your menu, or in AWN you can just add a launcher using the command xfdesktop --menu to be able to launch it off the dock.
