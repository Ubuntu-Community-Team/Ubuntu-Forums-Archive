### Post by ericdegen on 2007-07-30
For what its worth I run both on two different boxes (both feisty).

I'm with you about being quite happy with beryl. its on my desktop with an Nvidia and has totally solid, so I have not messed with CF on that one.

My notebook with an ATI x1600 is another story.  It never ran XGL/Compiz/Beryl cleanly so I figgered what the heck, and threw CF on it.  It has not been any worse than Beryl on that ATI, but that's not saying much.

My two cents worth, if you are using Beryl and its stable, stick with it for now. I look at all this every month or so and continue to be disappointed that these projects are just not "there" yet.
