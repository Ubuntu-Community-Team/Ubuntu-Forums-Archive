### Post by Talomon on 2007-04-28
Well my computer has a Ati x1600 graphics card, and beryl works fine...so if yours is using ati as well it should be a pice of cake. 
Take a look at this [http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Main_Page](http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Main_Page) it's the beryl wiki, they have described how to install beryl on alot of different systems. This link in particualr [http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Install_Beryl_on_Ubuntu_Edgy_with_XGL](http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Install_Beryl_on_Ubuntu_Edgy_with_XGL) is particularly useful. For getting your graphics cards drivers in order you can either turn to envy [http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html](http://www.albertomilone.com/nvidia_scripts1.html) which is a "installer" of ati/nvidia drivers or just have a look around ubuntu forums or the beryl wiki as they both describe how to do it.
