You know how you press <ctrl>+<alt> LC to rotate the cube in compiz, is there any way to make that happen when I press one of the unused side buttons on my mouse?

### Post by prodigalson666 on 2007-12-12
Yes, go into ccsm, under rotate cube select the action tab, click on general, a drop down box will appear, click on initiate and change the <alt><ctrl>Button1 to Button 2, worked for me. I only have to use my middle button or my side button to lift and rotate my cube now.

### Post by PointyWombat on 2007-12-13
Thanks for the reply and by looking into this further, I ran 'xev' and saw that the button I wanted to use to on my mouse was Button8. I put Button 8 in ccsm and it works like a charm.
