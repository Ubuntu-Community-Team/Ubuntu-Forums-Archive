### Post by avik on 2007-10-11
You can get rid of the part that lists the windows by right clicking on the bar near the list of windows and choosing Remove.  You can remove the entire bar (clock, menu, and all) by right-clicking an empty part of the bar and choosing Delete this Panel (though it's grayed out for me; weird).

Finally, you can the window list back by right-clicking on the panel, choosing Add to Panel, and choosing Window List.
