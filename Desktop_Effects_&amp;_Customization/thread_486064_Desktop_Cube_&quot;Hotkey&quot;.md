### Post by llamakc on 2007-06-27
ctrl-alt & left-click on your mouse will grab the cube.

No, there's not a 'beryl-manager' for compiz-fusion but if you need to launch it you can create a launcher for your desktop or panel. If you want a launcher for the config editor, create one and make the executable 'ccsm'.
