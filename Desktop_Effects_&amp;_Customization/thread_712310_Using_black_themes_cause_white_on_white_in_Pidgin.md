### Post by {BzF}~JOKesTER on 2008-03-05
In Pidgin :

Goto -

Tools >> Plugins Check The Box Conversation Colors - {If It Isn't Already}

Select It And Click "Configure Plugin" 

Under The "Receive Messages" Click The Color Palatte - And Change To The Desired Color.

Hope This Helps
