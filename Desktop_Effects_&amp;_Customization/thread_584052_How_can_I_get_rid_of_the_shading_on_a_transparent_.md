### Post by Tachyon1000 on 2007-10-20
Here's one solution, but I wish I knew exactly the domain of parameters for this kind of stuff.

"In the Window Decorations plugin settings, under the Effects heading, change the "Shadow Windows" section to "any & !(class=Gnome-panel)"."

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=380383](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=380383)
