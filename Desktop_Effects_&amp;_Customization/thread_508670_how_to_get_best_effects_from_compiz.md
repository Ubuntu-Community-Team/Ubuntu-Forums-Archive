### Post by Waappu on 2007-07-24
Hi

I don't know about Compiz and best performance.

But you can reset Beryl settings
First change window manager to Metacity and then type in terminal```
mv ~/.beryl ~/beryl_old_conf
mv ~/.beryl-managerrc ~/beryl-managerrc_old
mv ~/.emerald ~/emerald_old
```Re-start Beryl and setting are defaults
