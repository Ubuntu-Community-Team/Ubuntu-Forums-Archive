### Post by philidox on 2008-02-14
I found what I was looking for its called "Art Manager".  I downloaded it from the repos.  It does it all backgrounds, themes, splash screens, etc...  I just went to Applications>>>Add/Remove.  Then I just type in art manager in the search bar.  I found the program called "Art Manager" and installed it.  After I installed it I went to System>>>Preferences>>>Art Manager(or you can just press alt+f2 and type in gnome-art in the search bar).  I used Art manager as an easy method to DOWNLOAD backgrounds, splash screen, and login managers.  For me, I use Art manager as a splash screen manager only.  I use other programs as a background and login manager.  Personally, I like to a program called "Drapes" as my background manager and use the "Login Window" as my login manager.  Drapes can be installed from the repos.  I believe the "Login Window" is installed by default and can be found at System>>>Administration>>>Login Window.  Go to the "local" tab and press the add button and look for the themes you downloaded thru Art Manager.  Thanx and enjoy
