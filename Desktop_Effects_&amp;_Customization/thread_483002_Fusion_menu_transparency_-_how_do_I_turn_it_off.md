### Post by gloscherrybomb on 2007-06-24
SOLVED.

For those who have the same problem: Go on to general options, and then opacity settings. You'll see a string of values. Remove the ones that you don't want transparent. If you delete the whole string i doesn't seem to work though, so just remove the individual entries.
