### Post by codesplice on 2008-01-07
I personally prefer Compiz.  It seems more stable, and also (to me) appears more polished.  Beryl kind of feels like just grasping for every excuse to use intensive eye candy, while Compiz uses the eye candy to make the desktop a more friendly and usable environment.

In the end, I think it just boils down to a personal preference.
