I've had bad experiences with Elitegroup ECS in the past, especially when it comes to video cards... You might want to step it up a small notch for better quality, especially if you want to be able to play **most** games.  You might consider getting an EVGA e-GeForce 7300 GT 512mb.  I got one from newegg.com for around $45 after rebate.  Plus, the EVGA version has a lifetime warranty and EVGA's step up program, so if you wanted to upgrade to something nicer in the future it wouldn't be as expensive.  You could probably find a slightly more expensive 7600 GS 256mb, but the performance difference will be negligible compared to the 7300 GT.

FYI, my 7300 can run every source game there ever is, and BioShock at Medium settings as well (no AA/AF).

P.S.: Does your motherboard have only a PCI-Express slot or is there an AGP slot?
