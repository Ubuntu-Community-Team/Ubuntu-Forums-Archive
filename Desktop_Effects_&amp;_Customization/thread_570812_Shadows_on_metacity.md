### Post by ThrobbingBrain66 on 2007-10-08
Metacity is really only your window borders.  If you don't like emerald you can use gwd to keep your metacity theme.  You must have compiz-gnome installed to get gwd.

### Post by smartboyathome on 2007-10-08
