### Post by Locutus_of_Borg on 2008-01-05
under settings manager there is an option in rotate cube to set the zoom percentage
to take a screen shot, you go to the general options > commands and set the key binding to take a screen shot to whatever you want
mine is control + print screen

to press print screen on my omputer i have to hold the function button, so to take a shot of my cube, i rotate it with the mouse and while still holding the mouse button, press and hold the Fn button, the control, then the print screen button

thats all i have to do to get it to work

### Post by brett123 on 2008-06-22
You need CTRL-ALT-Mouse to *start* the cube moving, however once it's moving you can *release* CTRL-ALT but keep the mouse button down. You can then hit your normal keyboard keys for printscreen (whatever they may be).

No need for timing, or special bindings I think. Works for me anyway.
