### Post by Vadi on 2007-11-09
That's called a dock - and there are two main ones, Avant Window Navigator (AWN) or Kiba-dock. The latter features a really fun physics engine, but the project hasn't been developed for a while. Awn is still being developed, and there's a thread on how to get it working in this forum ([click]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=385981")).
