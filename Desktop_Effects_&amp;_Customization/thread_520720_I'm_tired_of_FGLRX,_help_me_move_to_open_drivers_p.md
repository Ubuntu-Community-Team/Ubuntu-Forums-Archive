### Post by bakster on 2007-08-08
When I installed fglrx drivers the open source driver was removed by the fglrx installer.Check if the xserver-xorg-video-ati package is installed.Also (maybe) you need to remove some stuff from monitor and device sections.Here is how mine looks with 9600 card(non AIW,no TV out!!!).

```

Section "Device"
        Identifier      "ATI Technologies Inc RV350 AR [Radeon 9600]"
        Driver          "ati"
        BusID           "PCI:2:0:0"
        Option          "XAANoOffscreenPixmaps" "true"

EndSection

Section "Monitor"
        Identifier      "SyncMaster"
        Option          "DPMS"
        HorizSync       30-85
        VertRefresh     50-160
EndSection

```
 
 I also deleted mine xorg.conf and to created a new one(with dpkg-reconfigure again),after I installed open source driver.
