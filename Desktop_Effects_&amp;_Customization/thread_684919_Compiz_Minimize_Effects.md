Most of the eye candy that was in Beryl is in Compiz-Fusion.  The sprakle effect you are seeing is actually the Burn animation.  Since the video on YouTube isn't his res, and the speed of the Burn animation is set for a short duration by the person that made the video, it looks like sparkles.  You could duplicate the appearance by changing the default color and speed to get a "sparkle" effect.  There's a bunch of variables for the Fire.  The links posted above explain how to change the settings.
