### Post by elliotjreed on 2007-10-27
If you go into System > Preferences > Advanced Desktop Effects (or System > Preferences > Appearance > Visual Effects > Custom)...

Then click on your action (eg. desktop wall), then go to the  last tab, click on the little '>' arrow, and choose your hotkeys.

I have done this for the desktop wall slider thing (F1, F2 and F3) and it works well!
