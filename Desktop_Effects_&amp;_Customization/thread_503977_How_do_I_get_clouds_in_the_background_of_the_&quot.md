### Post by testube_babies on 2007-07-18
There are so many different versions of Compiz running around out there, but this is the general idea:

Download a picture of clouds (it has to be a certain size, so go with one from beryl-look.org or compiz-themes.org):
[http://www.beryl-themes.org/content/show.php/Beryl+Cloudy+SkyDome?content=55604](http://www.beryl-themes.org/content/show.php/Beryl+Cloudy+SkyDome?content=55604)

In whatever you use to manage your Compiz settings, you can enable Skydome somewhere in the cube settings and choose this picture as the skydome background.
