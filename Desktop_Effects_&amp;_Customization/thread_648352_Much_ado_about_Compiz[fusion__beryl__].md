### Post by Sef on 2007-12-25
> Just how does Compiz interact with Emerald or Beryl. What are their differences and similarities?

Originally there was Compiz, but a disagreement broke out, and a fork, Beryl, was started.  After a few months, the compiz and beryl developers made up, and compiz became compiz-fusion: the compiz developers work on the core, and the Beryl developers work on the plug ins.  Beryl is no longer being maintained.

Emerald is a theme manager.
