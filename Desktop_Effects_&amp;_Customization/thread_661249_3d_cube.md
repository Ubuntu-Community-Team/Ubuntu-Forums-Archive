### Post by mstrkrft on 2008-01-07
check out this link: 

[[CENTER]http://forlong.blogage.de/article/2007/8/29/How-to-set-up-Compiz-Fusion[/CENTER]]("http://forlong.blogage.de/article/2007/8/29/How-to-set-up-Compiz-Fusion")

 very helpful when configuring compiz, esp. the cube.

under 'system > preferences > advanced desktop effects settings' and check out the 'rotate cube > actions' tab.  This tells you what button combinations do what.  For example, I set "Initiate" to one of my mouse wheel buttons, so when I hold it down and move the mouse, the cube rotates.  The above  guide is a great help.
