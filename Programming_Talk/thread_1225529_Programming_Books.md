### Post by JordyD on 2009-07-28
If 'for beginners', you mean 'beginner to C', not 'beginner to programming', the [K&R book]("http://www.amazon.com/Programming-Language-Prentice-Hall-Software/dp/0131103628/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1248831680&sr=8-1") is the only book you need. In fact, it's written by the authors of the language. It's actually a quite famous book, most people know exactly what you're talking about when you say K&R (if they know C).

If a 'beginner to programming', then I cannot recommend the K&R book.
