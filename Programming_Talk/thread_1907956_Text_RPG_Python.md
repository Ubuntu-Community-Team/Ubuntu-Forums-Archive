### Post by Telengard C64 on 2012-01-12
I can't advise you about Python, but here's what I can tell you about programming.

Just get started doing it, the sooner the better. Procrastination is your enemy.

Learn as you go. Don't let inexperience be an excuse for procrastination. 

Not sure how to implement a feature? Code it however you know and revise later. Eventually you'll develop a sense of what works and what doesn't.

Expect you code to change drastically over time, especially during early revisions. Trashing hundreds of lines of ill conceived code and starting from scratch is not unheard of. It's part of learning.

Share your ideas with others, and  remain open to better ideas.

HTH
