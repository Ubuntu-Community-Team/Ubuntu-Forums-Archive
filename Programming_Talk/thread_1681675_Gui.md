As Tony mentions, of course it's possible to edit the GUI and change stuff!

But, you don't sound too convinced of your abilities to do this.

Exactly, what do you want to change?

You certainly do not need a "developer version", or mess with the "kernal", to change the "takebar" (what is this?) or menus.

Programming Graphical User Interfaces for user applications is a very different matter which is accomplished by using programming languages such as C, C++, Java, C#, Python, and graphical toolkits such as GTK, Qt, WxWidgets, and so.

If you just want to alter the size, shape, colors, of some titlebars, buttons, that is mostly accomplished through "themes". But it's a complex topic of its own.

I suggest you take a very careful look at what you want to change and then investigate that. Otherwise, just "GUI" is a very broad topic.
