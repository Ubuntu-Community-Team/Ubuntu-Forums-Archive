### Post by GeneralZod on 2007-11-29
[kdevelop3](http://packages.debian.org/kdevelop3) is simply a meta-package for kdevelop, so installing it will not add really do anything :)

kdevelop-dev is indeed for writing plugins for kdevelop (kdevelop is almost wholly plug-in based).
