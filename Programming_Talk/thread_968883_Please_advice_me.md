### Post by pmasiar on 2008-11-03
> **Jayadheer said:**
> 1) How can I contribute/participate in projects (Linux)?

Find a project you like, and ask developers how to submit patches.

> 2) If I work for any project, can I add that in my resume?

Definitely. But you might be interested to work on project which are "relevant" to potential employers. Solving some real end-user problem.

ie web applications are popular: becoming expert in Satchmo web-based shop could get you contract job or even employment. Writing yet another MP3 player is likely less interesting for potential employer. Choose your time investment wisely.

I have no experience in open-source projects in  embedded area, but Google's Android might be something worth to master. Maybe ;-)

> 3) I am just a beginner to programming. What Are skills do I need to get to involve/join in the projects?      

- Communicate about what you want to do
- Write code according to coding standards easy to accept to project

Good luck!
