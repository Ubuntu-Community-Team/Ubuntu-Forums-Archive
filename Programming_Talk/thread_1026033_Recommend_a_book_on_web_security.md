### Post by curvedinfinity on 2008-12-30
I'm not a fan of books. I find you can find much more up to date and condensed material on the web. "Web Security" is a marketing-esque biz-lingo. This coming from an ex-firmware developer for encryption devices. Basically, there are good programming practices, and proper use of encryption, and that's it. As long as you have "your bases covered" you are 100% secure. The act of securing a something-or-rather is basically making sure the person who wrote it in the first place wasn't an idiot. Wikipedia has excellent articles on tons of this stuff, so if you are interested in it, start there.
