### Post by Tomosaur on 2007-02-09
Take a look around on launchpad:
[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu](http://launchpad.net) and take a look at the specifications. If there is a project similar to the one you're talking about, then try messaging the lead developer, checking out the progress reports etc. Launchpad is the place to be for Ubuntu developers.

However, it's entirely plausible that you just create your software without being 'tied' to a specific distribution (either by branding, or otherwise). This way, more people can take advantage of your work, and the software can still be put into the standard edubuntu installation.
