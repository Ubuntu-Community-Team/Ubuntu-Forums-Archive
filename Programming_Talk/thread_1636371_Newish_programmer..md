### Post by DangerOnTheRanger on 2010-12-03
Just how big? Anything beyond a simple 10-30 level platformer of reasonable quality will take several people *months*, if not a year or two.

Have you done anything this size before? 
Because a game is probably not a good first serious programming project, as it requires a *lot *of design before creation. This just isn't the game plot, script, and assorted graphics design, you must design the *code*, too. Most beginning programmers don't know how to properly design programs larger than about 800 lines.

Although if you think you can do it(though if you answer "no" to the above question, I would advise against it), go for it!
