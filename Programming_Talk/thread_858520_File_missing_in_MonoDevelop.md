### Post by Rucas on 2008-07-13
Yes i got that too. 
Anyhows here is a step by step of how to fix that:

1)Go to your System>Administration>Synaptic Package Manager
2)Click under search and type gmcs
3)It will show up a bunch off alternatives or just the mono-gmcs
4)Simply click its check box and install.

Hope this helps.
