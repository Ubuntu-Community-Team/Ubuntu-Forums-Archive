**If** someone has a lot of patience, starting from bottom up **does** make sense. I am only afraid that n00bz0rs these days do not have the stamina to overcome the very steep learning curve, to get anywhere. In old days, bottom-up approach was even standard and normal. So you cannot just mindlessly fix something and try again - you had actually to **think** to debug your program within a semester. :-) Waist deep in snow or mud all year around, uphill both ways. :-)
