### Post by youknowme on 2012-06-28
> **piriton said:**

'Generic algorithm' has a particular meaning in computer science and I am not sure by your post that this is what you are interested in. Are you simply interested in learning some about basic 'commonly used' algorithms? If that is the case I recommend you read some of Dr Knuth's work. A very good introductory book is 'Selected papers on Computer Science' (Donald E Knuth). For a real in depth exploration I recommend you invest in his multi volume "The Art of Computer Programming". - The first volume has fascinating info on stuff like the origin of the word algorithm (which happens to have an Arabic root). It goes on to examine fundamental algorithms, sorting / searching, semi numerical algorithms etc in great detail.
