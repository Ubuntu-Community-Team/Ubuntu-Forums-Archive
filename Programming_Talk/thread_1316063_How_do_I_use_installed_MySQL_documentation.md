### Post by Barriehie on 2009-11-07
May be other ways but this is what I do.  Go back into synaptic and find the package you installed and under where it has installed files look for /usr/share/doc/..../html files.  To put this in your drop down you go to System > Preferences > Main Menu > Programming, then on the right side click on 'Add Item'.  Fill in the boxes!  Best way to avoid typos is use FF to open the file and then copy/paste the URL.  Where the command box is: **firefox <path to index.html>**.

Barrie
