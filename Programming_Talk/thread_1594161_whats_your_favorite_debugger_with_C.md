### Post by Tony Flury on 2010-10-12
From what I know about all of those IDEs - they all use the same gdb debugger - what they might do is place a nice GUI on the top of it.

My recommendation (since they are all free) - is use any and all IDEs, and find which one works for you - it is all subjective - and a person may like a given IDE (or tool set) for reasons which actually turn out to be completely unimportant to you.
