### Post by pharcyde on 2006-01-25
The only thing is to keep practicing, reading, and talking to other programmers.  I know this sounds kind of generic, but it's the truth.  The best way I've learned really advanced programming techniques is from talking to people and reading various books.  The benefit of both is seeing how people apply algorithms to solve different problems.  There is usually someone who has already spent the time to work out the kinks for any programming problem that may come up.  The trick is learning how to piece these algorithms and techniques together to solve your particular solution.
