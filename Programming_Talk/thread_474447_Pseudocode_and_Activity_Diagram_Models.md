### Post by xtacocorex on 2007-06-15
It was required in my intro to FORTRAN class at college, but I've only ever planned out around two or three codes.  One of those codes I happened to start the other day, and while it's not a flow chart or syntax like structure, I've mind-mapped my idea to keep me focused on the scope of said project.

A lot of big projects use UML to model the code, which can also autogenerate source for the programmers. 

It's up to you to determine if you need it, but it is a good skill to have since it may be required in your future.
