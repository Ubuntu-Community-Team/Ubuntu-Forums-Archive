### Post by Ascenti0n on 2010-04-20
I just thought I'd share my experiences with the community on text editors/IDE's of choice.

I do a lot of web development work (PHP, HTML, CSS etc), and have used a wide variety of editors including:

NetBeans
Eclipse

Gedit
Gvim
Notepad++
PSPad

to name a few. I have noticed that, rather unintentionally, I am using Geany more and more, in my day to day work. That's not to say that the other's are poop, in fact I like Gedit a lot, but for me, at least for now, Geany hits the sweet spot, a nice balance between text editor with good features and a few IDE like ones thrown in for good measure.

The Geany project seems to have good momentum ATM, and I hope it continues.
