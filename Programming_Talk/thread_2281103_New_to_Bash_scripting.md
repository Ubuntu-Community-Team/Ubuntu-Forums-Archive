### Post by sisco311 on 2015-06-05
As TheFlue mentioned, there are many, many, many, bash scripting guides. Don't trust them. Most of them are outdated and/or inaccurate and don't teach you good practice.

I do not recommend the Beginning Bash Scripting and Advanced Bash Scripting Guides unless you know how to filter out the junk. ;)

If you are new to bash, then check out the guide from my signature and [http://wiki.bash-hackers.org](http://wiki.bash-hackers.org)

You can check your scripts at: [http://www.shellcheck.net](http://www.shellcheck.net)

If you have a bash related question, you can ask it at the #bash IRC channel on irc.freenode.org or of course here on the forums.
