### Post by holocene on 2010-06-11
I just wanted to share my experience with learning bash.

I have the "fish" book "Learning the Bash Shell" by O'Reilly, which I found somewhat difficult to absorb. 

Then I discovered the GREAT book, "bash Cookbook" by the same publisher, which has a (wood) turtle on the cover.

What a difference in readability! 

The style of writing is that a task is presented, and then a solution, and discussion. I've gone through about 40 pages, and have understood every sentence. Most of the time, the problems they chose to write about were very appropriate to my experience.

If you are struggling to learn the bash shell, I really recommend this book.
