### Post by Rubicon421 on 2011-02-14
If anyone out there feels up to the challenge I've got a great idea for a script, audio player extension or stand alone program- however you want to write it. 

There are a ton of MP3 tagging programs that access on line databases and gather missing information on the music you have in your library. I think it would be helpful to have a program that found the music you are missing from your library for the artists you already have by accessing the same databases and reporting back a list of albums NOT in your library for the bands that are found. 

I have a huge music library and like to keep up to date on any new releases from the bands I follow but checking for new albums from hundreds of bands by manually searching can take a while. If you could just right click your music folder and have a list generated it would be  a great way to find new music to add to your collection. 

If you like the idea feel free to run with it. I don't even care if you give me credit for the idea, I'd just like it if someone could make it happen and I could use it :)
