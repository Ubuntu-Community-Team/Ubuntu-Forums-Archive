### Post by dv3500ea on 2010-08-01
Are you sure you mean swf not flv. flv is generally the format used for flash videos. swf has interaction so can't really be converted into a static video. [ffmpeg]("http://www.ffmpeg.org/index.html") and its libraries would be suitable for converting flvs to other video formats.
