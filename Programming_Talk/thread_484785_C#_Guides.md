### Post by cunawarit on 2007-06-26
I don't know of any books that concentrate on C# development with Mono. 

As pmasiar points out C# is a Microsoft language, therefore MSDN is a good place to start reading up: 

[http://msdn2.microsoft.com/en-gb/library/kx37x362(VS.80).aspx](http://msdn2.microsoft.com/en-gb/library/kx37x362(VS.80).aspx)

Mono currently supports up to .NET 2 nearly fully. And full support is due soon late this year (including WinForms). As for books, there are tons of C# books around, probably the best thing is for you to go to a bookshop and explore which one most suits your needs.
