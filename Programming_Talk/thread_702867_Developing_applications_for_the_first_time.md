### Post by CptPicard on 2008-02-20
KDevelop is an IDE directed at KDE and Qt development, so the language of interest is actually C++, not C.

I would be of the opinion that yes, actually you can learn with the IDE. You don't need to necessarily start with just text editor and compiler. On the other hand, with KDE in particular, there is quite a lot of other extraneous stuff to be aware of that can be overwhelming for someone who doesn't even know the language.

Btw, in Qt you do have the ability to automatically resize your buttons, but it requires you to understand the container hierarchy system and the "spacers"...
