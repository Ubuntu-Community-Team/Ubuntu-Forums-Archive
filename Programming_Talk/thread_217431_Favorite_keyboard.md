### Post by bieber on 2006-07-20
I'm currently using the crappy HP keyboard that came with my computer, and on my other computer I have Das Keyboard.  Das is nice, has a good feel to it, but the single biggest help to my comfort and performance is the layout I use, not the keyboard.  For those who haven't tried it, you should seriously consider checking out the Dvorak Simplified Keyboard Layout.  Much, much more comfortable and faster.  My fingers barely move at all for most of the typing I do...
