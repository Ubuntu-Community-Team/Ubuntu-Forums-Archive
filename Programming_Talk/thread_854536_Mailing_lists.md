### Post by tamoneya on 2008-07-09
most of these mailing lists have a site you can visit so that you can configure your subscription.  It has things like: mail daily, weekly or every message.  There is also typically and option to send your own posts as well.  Im guessing that they setup that list to have a default of not sending you your posts.  Should be a simple matter of logging into that site and clicking two buttons.
