### Post by nadazero on 2009-10-19
It seems that [http://www.pygame.org/docs/ref/examples.html](http://www.pygame.org/docs/ref/examples.html) only lists the examples. 
To just get the examples for pygame-1.8.1 you can go to [http://www.pygame.org/download.shtml](http://www.pygame.org/download.shtml) . 
On that page scroll down and left click pygame-1.8.1release.tar.gz . 
That should give you a dialog box.
Select "Open with Archive Manager (default)" , then left click OK. 
That should give you a new dialog box. 
Double left click "dist\pygame1.8.1release.tar" .
Double left click "pygame1.8.1release" .
Right click "examples" .
Left click "Extract" .
That should give you a new dialog box. 
Select an appropriate folder and left click the Extract button.
