### Post by wmcbrine on 2010-07-03
I don't mean to "dump on" anything, but -- while Gambas may be a modern Basic that runs on Linux, the role Python fills in Ubuntu really is closer to the niche VB occupies in Windows. So:

If your goal is to bring existing VB developers over to Linux, teach them about Gambas. BUT --

If your goal is to get novices with no particular experience developing in Ubuntu, teach them about Python.

IMHO. Disclaimer: I don't know Gambas myself. Registered member of the Python mafia. :D
