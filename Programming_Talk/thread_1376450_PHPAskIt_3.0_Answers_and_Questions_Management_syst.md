[FONT=Impact][SIZE=5][COLOR=Green]About[/COLOR][/SIZE][/FONT]

PHPAskIt was written in June 2005 and is a question and answer script.I was originally going to edit existing scripts when I realised thatfirstly, those scripts were so insecure and out of date that I wouldessentially be rewriting them completely, and secondly, the creatorswere no longer around and I couldn't ask their permission.
[FONT=Impact][SIZE=5][COLOR=DarkOrange]Main Features[/COLOR][/SIZE][/FONT]

[LIST]
[*]Ability to sort questions into categories
[*]Automatic question permalinks
[*]Easy installation and customisation
[*]Import questions from other scripts
[*]Spam word and IP address blocking
[*]AJAX technology for quick question answering
[*]Question searching
[/LIST]
[FONT=Impact][SIZE=5][COLOR=Lime]Requirements[/COLOR][/SIZE][/FONT]
- PHP 4.3 or higher (PHP 5+ also supported)
- MySQL 4.1 or higher (MySQL 5.0+ also supported)
