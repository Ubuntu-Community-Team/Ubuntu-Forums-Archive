If your webspace has PHP/CGI/whatever, there are a couple of blog packages you could install, including the one used for WordPress (which *does* allow custom styles).

As far as I know, there are also some which also work "offline". In this case you have to install a web-server locally and they enable you to extract static pages from your blog you can upload to your server.
