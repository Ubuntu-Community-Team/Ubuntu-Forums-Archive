Hi All,

Is anyone ever worked with RTML the proprietary language for yahoo stores? My company is making me learn it and it's firutrating the heck out of me. RTML is what is used to build custom templates for yahoo stores.

First off you don't get to use a text editor like most traditional languages instead you have their web interface which is **aweful**.


It's hard to explain but the editor for RTML requires you to click on a button and select a function you'd like to use, then once it's in the editor depending on what the function is you have to click on the function again then click the edit button again, then define your arguements declare your variables, etc.


The editor also doesn;t have a way to search through the code either. A template may call other templates and those templates may call other templates and so on and so forth. And this is just the beginning of my frustrations with this language.

I have a couple books on it, but they're not much help and yahoo provides very little documentation on this.

I just kind of wrote this because I needed to vent and was wondering if anyone had any experiene with RTML? ](*,)
