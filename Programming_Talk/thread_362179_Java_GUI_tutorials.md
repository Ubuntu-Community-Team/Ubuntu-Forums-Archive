### Post by hod139 on 2007-02-15
One possible route is to go with Swing.  Here is the official Sun [Swing tutorials]("http://java.sun.com/javase/6/docs/technotes/guides/swing/index.html").  Swing was designed to follow the model-view-controller GUI paradigm, so reading up on [MVC]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Model-view-controller") will be good too.  Note, there are alternatives to Swing, and some people will complain about the MVC design and how Swing didn't do a good job of following it; but in any case this gives you a good starting point.
