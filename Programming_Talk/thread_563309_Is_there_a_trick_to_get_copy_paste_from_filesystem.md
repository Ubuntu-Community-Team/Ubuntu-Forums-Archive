### Post by dwhitney67 on 2007-09-29
What do you mean by the "filesystem"?  Do you mean a terminal??

From a terminal, you can use Shift-Ctrl-C (to copy) and Shift-Ctrl-V (to paste), or you can rely on the pansy pull-down menu options.

When I need to copy from a terminal to the web-browser (and I'm sure it would be the same if going to eclipse), I use Shift-Ctrl-C to make the copy, and then Ctrl-V to make the copy to the destination application.
