Hmm... [http://www.google.ba/search?q=netbeans+change+background&ie=utf-8&oe=utf-8&aq=t&rls=org.mozilla:en-US:official&client=firefox-a](http://www.google.ba/search?q=netbeans+change+background&ie=utf-8&oe=utf-8&aq=t&rls=org.mozilla:en-US:official&client=firefox-a)

At least 2-3 useful results among top 10. 

Anyhow...

Tools->Options->Fonts & Colors

In the list on the left, 'default' should be selected.
On the right side, choose a color from the 'background' drop down.

Note that there are already few installed themes you can choose from, using 'profile' drop down, at the top of that window.

Also, you may want to click 'duplicate' first, to create your own theme and not change the profile you chose.
