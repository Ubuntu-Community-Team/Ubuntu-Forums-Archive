### Post by rplantz on 2006-05-02
General rule in C/C++ -- multiplication and division have higher precedence than addition and subtration; use parentheses everyplace else.

I've always found the C conditional expression a bit odd. I find it difficult to read compared to an if-else. And when I look at the assembly language generated by each, it's the same. (I did not investigate this thoroughly; I suppose that somebody can come up with an example where the conditional expression is more efficient.) The if-else construct is found in most languages, so I find it easier to read.
