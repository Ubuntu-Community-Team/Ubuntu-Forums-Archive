### Post by Stephen Howard on 2007-07-03
Have a browse of the [SourceForge repository]("http://sourceforge.net/") - maybe something will catch your interest.

Start small, find a small but active project and if it doesn't do something you think it should, then submit a patch.  Unless you already have a reputation for coding, its best to just write the patch first and send it in to the developer - this will quickly overcome the problem of a verbose new arrival at a forum promising the world and never being heard of again.
