### Post by MicahCarrick on 2007-10-01
You know, you can look things up in google by doing "define:tty" in the search. In this case, I get this from Wikipedia:

> Teletypewriter or Teletype, a typewriter with an electronic communication channel
 - Teletypewriter as a common name for telecommunications device for the deaf (TDD)
 - A generic term for any computer data terminal or associated serial interface
