### Post by nixternal on 2006-06-23
Like creating your own slipstreamed version?  I am sure it can be done, as the creators of Ubuntu, which is OpenSource, has pretty much slipstreamed a version for us to enjoy. As for [Slipstreaming Linux](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/search.php?searchid=6289771) there isn't much in the forums as you can see, and not much on google. Now maybe searching for repackaging or packaging linux might get you a little more. You can also check with the [Ubuntu CD Image Team](https://launchpad.net/people/ubuntu-cdimage) as they may have some information that could be usefull. If you find out anything or create your own slipstream, I would recommend coming back and telling everyone else how to do it. Good luck!!!
