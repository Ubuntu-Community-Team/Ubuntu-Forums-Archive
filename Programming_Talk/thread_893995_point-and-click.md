I am confused as to what you are asking.

What do you mean by "point and click"? For GUI's you can use a various number of libraries. For buttons and windows and such, you can use wxPython, PyGTK+, PyQT (all in my wiki). For other multimedia you can use SDL or OpenGL (PyGame, which uses SDL is great for games)

I am not sure what you mean by "making documents open to other documents". That sounds like a hyperlink, and is easy to do with XHTML or HTML. If you don't know Python, I don't know how you are going to be able to do anything in it. If you want to have a linking sort of interface, learn the basics of HTML and use a browser.
