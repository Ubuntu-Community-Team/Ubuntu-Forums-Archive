### Post by pmasiar on 2008-08-09
See sticky, or go directly to wiki in my sig.

Stay away from OOP (Object-oriented programming) for now, just learn to use existing objects, not create your own, and your task will by 50% simpler. Functions and plain old modules will be enough for you. If you don't know regular expressions from other use, skip over them too for now: plain string methods (startswith, endswith, substring in text_variable, and split is enough to get started). Learn RE and OOP later.

But do learn list processing, and possibly generators - generators are very powerful to chain to parse huge text files in many different ways.
