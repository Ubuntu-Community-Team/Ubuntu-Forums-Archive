Something I just stumbled upon the other day, if you go to the bottom of any page on facebook, there is an 'about facebook' link.

From there, to the right is a 'we are hiring' link..

And from there, they have a list of 10 programming puzzles for people to solve, that I am pretty sure they update at least every month or every week.

I tried the prime number of bits problem with c++, and was able to solve it in what I believe is close to the recommended running time.

Another cool thing, they recommend giving them a solved puzzle along with your resume if you felt like applying.

Also, I noticed the requirments for almost all the jobs they have available include extensive experience with Linux.  Just something I thought I would pass along!!
