### Post by Flecko on 2008-12-01
I personally recommend Focus on SDL: [http://www.amazon.com/Focus-Premier-Press-Game-Development/dp/1592000304/ref=pd_bbs_sr_1?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1228183621&sr=8-1](http://www.amazon.com/Focus-Premier-Press-Game-Development/dp/1592000304/ref=pd_bbs_sr_1?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1228183621&sr=8-1)

I picked it up when I was first getting familiar with SDL and it helped out a lot as a reference. It doesn't have a ton of tutorial stuff if you're new to programming in general, but if you know how to program and just want a good source of SDL specifics, its a great book.

Plus, its cheap and is pretty thin.

Best of luck with SDL, I know I love it!
