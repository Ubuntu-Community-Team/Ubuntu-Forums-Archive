### Post by greenpeace on 2012-06-20
Hi, 

Python's urllib library will give you access to the right tools to fetch content over the internet.

More information on their docs:  [http://docs.python.org/library/urllib.html](http://docs.python.org/library/urllib.html)

You'll probably be most interested in the [urlopen]("http://docs.python.org/library/urllib.html#urllib.urlopen") method

Then once you have the data that you've requested, what you do with it is for you to decide.  Perhaps have a look at the "[quickly]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Quickly")" tool to help you get started with the GUI.
