### Post by imdano on 2007-12-14
Your best bet is to take a look at what the plugins already included with Exaile are doing.  Taking a look at them gave me enough to start on an MTP plugin.  Once you spend some time looking at those and the relavent parts of Exaile's base code, it becomes pretty simple to do whatever you want to do.  The hard part is putting in the time to familiarize yourself with the code.
