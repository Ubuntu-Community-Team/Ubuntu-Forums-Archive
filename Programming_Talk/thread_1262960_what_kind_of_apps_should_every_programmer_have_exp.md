### Post by mbeach on 2009-09-11
I seem to bump into the problem of time entry every client I visit. Dealing with holidays (including auto-calculation of hours for part timers), vacation, export to payroll systems, varying payroll cycles for different employees, reports with every type of drilldown, clients which are just not happy with any off the shelf program.

So I'd say give writing a timeentry system a go. It is easy enough to understand and can be as complicated as you want to make it.
