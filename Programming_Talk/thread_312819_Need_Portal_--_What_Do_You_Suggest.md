### Post by SuperMike on 2006-12-05
I need a very simplistic portal for Ubuntu that comes to a close approximation of this:

* Supports users, groups, and roles for groups.
* Users login and get dashboards that they can assign gadgets to from a global catalog.
* Gadget assignment is specified by what gadgets have been published to a particular role.
* Non programmers can make their own gadgets by configuring them to screenscrape some other page, extract just some particular piece of information, and return the result, with images coming back as well.
* Dashboards are pages of gadgets and can be renamed per user.
* Gadget state is remembered no matter where you login. If the gadget is collapsed, it remembers that on any workstation.
