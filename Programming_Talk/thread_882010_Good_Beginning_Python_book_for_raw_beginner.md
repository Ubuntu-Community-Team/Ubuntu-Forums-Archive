### Post by elithrar on 2008-08-06
> **days_of_ruin said:**
> 
No matter what anyone says, DO **NOT** READ "DIVE INTO PYTHON".

I'm going to agree here - it gets pushed to a lot of newbies, and unfortunately the book isn't for them. It's for those who have a background/foundation in another language and are looking to move into Python.

As for newbie books, I'd highly recommend [Learning Python](http://oreilly.com/catalog/9780596513986/index.html), the [Python Cookbook](http://oreilly.com/catalog/9780596007973/index.html) & [Beginning Python](http://apress.com/book/view/1590599829) - I have all three and can vouch for them; the former being the ideal starting point if you're new to programming.
