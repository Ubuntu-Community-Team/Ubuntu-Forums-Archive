### Post by yther on 2009-03-02
**Fingers**: 11111 11111
**Looking**: I don't look unless I'm typing numbers without using the keypad; I was never good at hitting the numbers way up there.
**Layout**: Dvorak FTW!

@shadow_code, I was touch-typing on QWERTY before I switched to Dvorak.  I haven't tested my speed in years, but I'm sure it's no better than 60 WPM because I really don't type all that much. For me it isn't as much about speed as comfort; after switching, I found that my hands weren't nearly as tired after lots of typing as they were on a QWERTY layout.

If you are thinking of switching, I can recommend typing with your eyes closed as an exercise.  It really makes you think more about what you are doing.  When I find that I'm making lots of mistakes, I still do that sometimes.  :)
