### Post by smartbei on 2007-11-04
First things first: please stop posting a 'bummer' post less than an hour after your first post. Come on, it wasn't even close to off the front page of programming talk yet.

About your question. That is not exactly a very widely used language (which is perhaps why you are having difficulty finding resources). Here is what I could find via google in around 5 minutes:

[http://gambas.sourceforge.net/](http://gambas.sourceforge.net/) - check out "Getting Started" on the left.

[http://www.gambasforge.net/](http://www.gambasforge.net/) - Many Gambas projects - download source and learn from that.

[http://www.gambasforge.net/codeslist.php?by=cat&id=7](http://www.gambasforge.net/codeslist.php?by=cat&id=7) - for the tutorials section of the same site.

[http://davidasorey.net/static/gambas-tutorial/gambas_tutorial_en.html](http://davidasorey.net/static/gambas-tutorial/gambas_tutorial_en.html) - An actual tutorial for Gambas (it's pretty long).

[http://www.foss.lk/node/5](http://www.foss.lk/node/5) - scroll down and download "Gui Programming with Gambas"

[http://forum.stormweb.no/](http://forum.stormweb.no/) - Gambas forum

I hope that helps you get started.
