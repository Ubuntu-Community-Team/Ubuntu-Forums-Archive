### Post by geirha on 2010-10-25
90% of the "tutorials" and "guides" out there on shell scripting are junk, so google is not recommended for learning scripting.

I recommend you stick with [http://mywiki.wooledge.org/BashGuide](http://mywiki.wooledge.org/BashGuide) and [http://mywiki.wooledge.org/BashFAQ](http://mywiki.wooledge.org/BashFAQ) and the other pages at that wiki. It's mainly meant for bash, but most of the pages take care to mention what is bash/ksh-specific and what will work in POSIX shells and the bourne shell.

What do you mean by ".sh shell" though? Does it need to be bourne compatible, or just POSIX?
