### Post by kalaharix on 2010-12-13
You are making this more difficult than it is!

Open Gambas, click on 'examples'. scroll down to the example which is, unsurprisingly, called 'Barcode'.

You will be warned that the project is read-only. Click on OK.

<Files>, <Save project as...> and save to your home directory.

Press the run triangle in the icon bar or <debug>, <run> or just F5 key.
