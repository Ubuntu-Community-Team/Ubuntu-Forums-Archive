### Post by Tony Flury on 2011-09-30
I wouldn't create one by hand : 
As well as having the problems doing business with your family, any e-commerce site has a lot of other issues to contend with : 

1) As well as users needing to register/log on, "staff" will also need to log on, reviewe orders, maintain stock levels etc - can you keep the two separate , and ensure that a customer can never get to the staff area, and a member of staff can only get to the customer data they require.
2) Can you say with a high degree of certainty that your hand crafted site wont leak personal information (names, phone numbers, what they have ordered etc) of customers or staff in some form ?
3) Can you guarantee that no financial information will be leaked(payment or credit card details, purhase records) ? 
4) Can you guarantee that no order will get lost or get double dealt ?
5) Can you system log who does what and when i case of disputes ?

Potentially you as the developer would be legally liable (in some jurisdictions) for any losses caused due to your software, either losses by a customer or by the business.

All in all - I would recommend you save yourself a lot of heartache and recommend she goes with a commercial e-commerce site - if she is running a small business, why not help her set up an ebay shop - got to be a darn sight easier, and quicker, and far less risky.
