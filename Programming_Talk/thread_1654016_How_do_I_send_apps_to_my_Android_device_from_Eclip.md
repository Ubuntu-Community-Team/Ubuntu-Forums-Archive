### Post by Queue29 on 2010-12-28
This article is for taking screenshots, but the same procedure needs to be followed before you can push apps to your phone:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AndroidScreenshots](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AndroidScreenshots)

In particular, the section about setting up UDEV rules.

Once you do all that, your phone will appear as an option when eclipse asks you which device to run your app on.
