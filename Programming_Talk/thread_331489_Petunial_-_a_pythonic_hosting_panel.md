### Post by gummibaerchen on 2007-01-04
Hello,

I was just looking for a control panel for my new webserver and found something new.

Besides SysCP, which is written in PHP and which I have actually choosen, I found Petunial.

They are developing a hosting panel from scratch, written in pure Python.

I will see how it works and start to contribute, as I am not 100% happy with the actual solutions and I feel Python is a good language for a control panel.

If you are interested check their site out:
[http://www.petunial.org/](http://www.petunial.org/)

And if you also want to get involved in development have a look at their Trac:
[http://www.petunial.de/devel/](http://www.petunial.de/devel/)

They also have an IRC-Channel:
**#petunial **on irc.freenode.net

Of course it's all Open-Source under a BSD Licence.

If your just a user I would advice you to check out their site again at the end of the year and download the best panel available :D 

This may be a good opportunity for all Python developers to extend their skills. Just take you favourite server application and write a plug-in for Petunial for that.

I will join them for sure, and maybe we will see us on IRC later.
