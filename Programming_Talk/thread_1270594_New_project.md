### Post by Tony Flury on 2009-09-20
Is it just me, or is it that having read that you want a spreadhseet (with a graphing capability) ?

to be honest your requirements are not very clear, for instance

"Record progress" - progress in what, how many variables ? presumably this is progress of one or more variables against time ?

"Calendar facility" - to do what - do you want to be able to pick a date for data entry, or run a diary with events, appointments, recurring appts etc ?

"Templates" - templates for what, what would they do ?

"Ability to edit icons" - are you talking about the ability to skin the application ?

It might be worth looking at other projects on Sourceforge and see how the specifications for them are written. Personally I would use the Agile style User stories to write specifications (to me the advantage is that they say what you want to do, why, and how to test it - the why is incredibly important to make sure that the focus is kept on the reasons for doing it).
