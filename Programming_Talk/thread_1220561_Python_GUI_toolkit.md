The 2 major GUI toolkits are PyGTK and PyQt, both of them are python bindings for their respective toolkits.
I cannot say much except find out the advantages and disadvantages of both and decide which is best for you. (Also, there have been dozens and dozens of threads on this, so maybe you can look over some of them). Remember that there are also other toolkits out there (e.x. Tkinter and wxWidgets)
