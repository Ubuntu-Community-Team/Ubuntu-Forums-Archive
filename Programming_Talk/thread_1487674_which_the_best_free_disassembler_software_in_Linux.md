### Post by nvteighen on 2010-05-19
First, I hope you know that disassembling software can be illegal. Read the program's license or better don't even think of it.

Second, objdump works for this. Look at its manpage; it's pretty straightforward to use. Of course, you better know Assembly or won't understand anything of the output...

Third, for my defense and just in case, pointing out that objdump can disassemble stuff is not illegal... the manpage tells you that. So this post isn't encouraging illegal cracking activities nor the like. The same as the objdump developers aren't responsible for what people do with objdump, I'm not responsible of what the OP does with the information I posted, even warning him of the risks this could bring if used wrongly.
