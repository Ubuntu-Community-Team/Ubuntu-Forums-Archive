### Post by Tomosaur on 2007-06-25
With all due respect - it sounds like you want someone to give up their time to privately tutor you for free. I don't think you're likely to find anyone here willing to do that - your best choice really is to just ask questions now, then they'll get answered as soon as someone with the relevant knowledge is around.

Here's a few excersises you could do:

Hello World program.
Question and answer command line program ("What's your name?", "Hello, <name>" etc etc). Make it loop until the user explicitly tells the program to quit.
Bounds checking (Input an integer, check whether it's between 1 and 10, stuff along that lines).

Try those out, if you get stuck, just ask your questions here and they will probably get an answer fairly quickly.
