That's quite a risky thing to do for a final year project...
You can't easily measure the results (unless of course you find a killer flaw, which is quite unlikely) and if you look at XP, it's been out for a while now and has been patched to bits.

If you're interested in DB and web services, there's plenty of scope there for e-commerce type applications.  I'm guessing since this is a final year project you don't need to come up with something new and novel (i.e. something you would need to do for a phd thesis).

The best thing would be to find the lecturer at your uni that specialises in DB and web stuff, and have a chat with him/her. A very common pitfall is for people to get over ambitious and try to do too much. You have limited time and most lecturers prefer something solid and well presented ;-)

Good luck,

### Post by lnostdal on 2007-03-13
