### Post by thenetduck on 2007-06-17
Hi Guys, 
 I have created a project on lunchpad.net (it's my first project so I hope I am going about this correctly) and feel I have a neat idea for spreadsheets. 

This project came about because I wanted a way to do more with a "Basic" spreadsheet. I wanted to be able to just highlight a part of it and "Label" it as a section and have it interact with another section that I just highlighted and labeled. Because of OO Programming I think it's possible to do this. Also  I wanted my spread sheet to be more graphic friendly. This doesn't not mean taking out the robust working of traditional spreadsheets but creating an interface that allows the user to not have to by a special program to make things easy etc for money or statistical things. 

I know a lot of the functionality that I just said is all ready their, however the idea is to make it easy and simple but allowing the users to really get into it if they wanna dig deeper. The idea of taking sections of the sheet and having them interact with other sections (for instance a graph) most likely exists but I wanna make it easy for instance just rolling the mouse over a section of sheet and the graph appears on the side (a little transparent) etc. 

What do you guys thing? Do you think this is something programmers would be interested in? Or do you think they will just keep with the same traditional style spreadsheet? 

Thanks for feedback
