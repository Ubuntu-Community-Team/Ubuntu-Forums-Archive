### Post by LaRoza on 2008-10-13
The best and most flexible way of transmitting ideas for a design is called "paper and pencil". Colour helps, but is optional.

For a website layout of pages (not the actually page layout, the entire website), you need a wall or piece of cardboard, sticky notes and a pencil. String is extra, and can help.
