Wow that's a can of worms.

MY personal opinion is that you should concentrate on C / C++ because virtually everything nowadays is written in C.

I know Motorola and Intel X86 assembler like the back of my hand. So too with GWBASIC and QBASIC. I'm learning and slowly getting better at C (with the help of lots of nice people here!).

BASH scripts are useful but... they only work in BASH.

I guess it depends on your goal(s).

If you want to have fun, write a few utilities and learn on your own, have a go at C.

If you want to become a paid, professional programmer, ask a college counselor (who will probably recommend C++).

I am really liking C because it has high level functions, but it also has lots of low level bit operations. It's like assembler with the "easy button" pressed!  :) 

So much for my 5 cents (inflation!)  :)
