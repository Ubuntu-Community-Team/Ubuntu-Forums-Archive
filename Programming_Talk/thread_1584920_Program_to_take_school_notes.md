### Post by r-senior on 2010-09-30
When I take notes, I never write them in lists like that. Despite people often being amused by my "spider diagrams", I generally use mind maps. Gedit isn't too good at those, although pencil and paper works well. 

Ubuntu has been sorely lacking in the mind-mapping department (one of the reasons I keep a Windows VM around is so I can run MindManager) , but I discovered Xmind recently and enjoy working with it. More for planning than note-taking but no reason it can't do both.

[http://www.xmind.net/]("http://www.xmind.net/")

If you can cope with being branded as a mind-mapping weirdo, you'll find it in Software Center/Synpatic.
