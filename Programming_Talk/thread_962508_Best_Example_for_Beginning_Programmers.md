### Post by Ferrat on 2008-10-29
Really first priority should be comments about the text, spacing and separating stuff in to smaller groups. 

Think of it as a grocery store, in a grocery store (the program) you separate things by category, then for ex. in the fruit and vegetable that is separated from meat and dairy sections you separate fruit and vegetables again to make it easiest to get what you need (so you don't have to run all over the place), lets say you then got two different sorts of tomatoes, you separate them as well but you put them close so they are easy to find and you label them accordingly and give any necessary info about where they are from and what is different about them etc but you don't over state that they are tomatoes over and over again cause people know that :). 

Approaching programming code setup like this and your program should be "easy" to read, understand and maintain for anyone that wants to.
