### Post by howefield on 2009-07-18
Right click the .eps file and select "open with" option.

Gimp may be one of your choices but if not, click on the "Open with other Application" option and navigate to gimp.

Or open Gimp (from the Applications > Graphics menu) and select open from the file menu and navigate to your file.
