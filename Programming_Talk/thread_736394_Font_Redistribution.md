### Post by geraldm on 2008-03-27
There are fonts that you can use without paying for them if you satisfy the terms.  You might want to look into the [http://www.gnome.org/fonts](http://www.gnome.org/fonts) to check the terms under which you can use those.  Because you are shipping the program and fonts together,
you can qualify for their terms; otherwise, you cannot sell the fonts alone.  Be sure you understand the complete terms.
Regarding the purchase of the licensed fonts, be sure that the license allows you your redistribution rights.  I think a normal license would not include that, as it would be directed to you only as purchasing for your own use.
I am not a lawyer, and am not offering legal advice.

Gerald
