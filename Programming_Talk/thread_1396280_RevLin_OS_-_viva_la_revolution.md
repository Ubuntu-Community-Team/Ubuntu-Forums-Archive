### Post by RevLin on 2010-02-01
Hello

Some of you may have heard of RevLin OS via various linux news sites over the last week, but i just wish to inform those who havent, about it. An introduction to the OS can be found ["]here]("http://p-quinn.com/entries/announcement/announcement-presenting-revlin-s-testing-framework-for-rave-[updated)  and further more in-depth info can be found [here]("http://p-quinn.com/entries/report/report-revlin-features-and-how-it-all-works")  .

In short the OS uses a new kernel called PopCorn which is a derivative of the linux kernel and has a desktop environment that is coded in web language (js css3 and htm5, like palms WebOS) meaning full integration into the cloud without the hassle of needing an active connection to the internet to access you files and personal data.

We are looking for any develops who have either native and/or advanced web language coding skills and have an interest in the project to get in touch with the team as soon as possible either here or via email (which can be found on our site)

We look forward to hearing from you all and hope everyone is excited as we are.

Regards

Team RevLin
