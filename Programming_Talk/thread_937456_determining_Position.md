### Post by Mr.Macdonald on 2008-10-04
Do you have the elliptical formula of the earth aka. the path the earth follows. Graph that. at a foci draw the sun (doesn't matter).

The furthest point from that is the longest day of the year (july 21)
The closest point is the shortest day (december 21)

in between those points (following the ellipse) are 182 (183 on dec to July side due to leap year)

You would need to fit the formula to suit your purpose



Now I am no astrologer or star gazer. I just thought of this and i am wondering why you didn't (not to insult you, maybe you were stressed) but just a tip. Programming is applied math. if you think like a mathematician then you can optimize your programs to not need as many loops and stuff.
