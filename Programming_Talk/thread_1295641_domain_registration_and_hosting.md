### Post by unknownPoster on 2009-10-19
I personally use Register.com as my domain registrar. I also use the VPS service provided by Linode.com.

If you choose to use a VPS, I would really suggest that you do your research and find one that best meets your needs. Some VPS providers are fairly restrictive. I've used one in which I could only access the server via a poorly designed and terribly slow web-interface. I had no root access at all. On the bright side, it was only $29.99 for an entire year of service.

As I stated before, I use Linode. Linode really doesn't place many restrictions on use. As long as it's legal, they don't really care what you do. My forum is hosted on a Linode. The basic package is $19.99 a month. I have a 16 GB storage limit and a 200GB bandwidth limit. For a basic website, this is more than enough resources to accomplish your goals.
