### Post by smartbei on 2007-07-12
There are many existing frameworks that allow you to build a site using templates, so it is not always ncessary to build your own. As the above poster mentioned, Drupal is one of the leading examples. Others are Joomla, Wordpress, PHPNuke. Try searching google.

If on the ther hand, you are looking to write your own, I would recommend going over to [http://www.opensourcecms.com/](http://www.opensourcecms.com/) and downloading one of the simple CMSs to take a look at its code. Many times if you are encountering a problem you can find its solution easily by examining similar projects.
