### Post by Xzallion on 2008-03-30
I'm glad you want to make a game, and hope all goes well, but theres a few worries.  A lot of what you have is very general and none of the links actually show the games your talking about, so I and others can't tell what kind of game your making without having played those games.  I searched on Google and found a few screenshots but still can't tell exactly how it differs from other games, other then the screenshots I saw make it look more like a turn based strategy then a real time strategy.  I couldn't read the language the websites where in so im not even sure they were for the right game, but it does confuse me.

Even more worrisome is that you don't think this will be hard because the filesize should reach only about 200MB.  The filesize has very little to do with the complexity of the games engine and the logic behind the scenes.  I'm not much of a programmer, only knowing Java and a little C++, but to me thats going to be a pain to program.

But lets get down to business.  First you need to answer some questions because what you have right now is far to vague.  All we have so far from you post is that it will feature:
[LIST]
[*]Intricate storyline
[*]Be a Real Time Strategy game
[*]Have a large cast of characters and/or monsters
[*]Use the Panda3d engine
[*]Use blender for the graphics work
[*]Ogg music
[/LIST]
We need something more concrete then "an intricate storyline".  What will be the stories genre?  Will it focus on a single character or a group of characters?  How will the story be presented in the game?

Then how large of a cast of characters do you want?  Will each have a story or will there be mostly background-less people that don't matter in the game?

What program language(s) will be used?  Why did you choose the Panda3D engine over OGRE/Crystal Space?  This needs to be answered so the other programmers that join have a better idea of the projects requirements.

What will be the art style and music themes?

What license will the game be released under?

You mention payment for hard copies, are you hoping to turn a profit or just cover the cost of the hard copies?  If your making a profit, how will the profit be spread among those that contribute?

Also, what will you name the game?

When you have those answered, I suggest writing up a game design document.  If you need a reference for one hese an article from Gamasutra on [Game Design Documents]("http://gamasutra.com/features/19991019/ryan_01.htm")  After you have that done, post it in a thread in the developer and programming section under [programming talk]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=39").  You will find people to help program and of other technical talents there, while its a little more hit and miss in the community cafe.

I'll leave you with that, and I hope your project goes well.

	Zage is a turn-base strategy game run on Linux. Background:

	This game is a Open Game that use free engine, library, and Open Source software. It will be always free for download. Although you can buy a hard copy to support the team. It will be develop under C++(or Python). And Use Blender as it’s main graphic tool along with Gimp and inkscape. .ogg will be the format use for music file, and Finally .avi will be use for video. 

Description:

	The requirement of this game wouldn’t need you to have a cutting edge computer in order to play. although you will need your keyboard and your favorite mouse to play it. Speakers are recommended but not require. 

Key Features:
	
	The main game play of Zage is in screen turn-base strategy, which mean it will go into a difference screen which where the battle will take place. Like never before. A Key Hit system will be introduce. For example, when you choose a skill to attack your enemy. A percentage of the full attack potential damage will be give to the enemy, and the player will need to complete a series of key hit like what you did in DDR in order to give the enemy the full potential damage. 

	Beside the main stream of game play, there are many stage in the game which you will need to solve some puzzle in order to continues. There are also number of secret stage and items, that will tell you more about setting and background of the main story, and the realm the story told place. It is up to you to discover the game world. You are allow to go back to anyway of the map, and discover more about it. 

	There are also few secret characters you are able to meet during the journey. They will make your journey much more interesting. There will also be a rich collection of items. It will give a excreta challenge to the game.

	Job up grade, there will be a job grouping system. As you up grade your job, you will gain with many new skills, and new looks. 

Genre: 

	The main focus of this game is the game play experience. The graphic focus on this game is not toward to realistic, which will increase the system requirement. The graphic will be well made as it is in it’s own art, and gorgeous. 

Platform(s):
	
	The game will release in Linux only first. It will have a On-Line play mode after the Main game is finish. Windows and OS X version might or might not be develop by the original term. That will be discussion after the game is complete done, and run perfectly under Linux.
