### Post by aks44 on 2007-11-20
Usenet newsgroups:
- comp.lang.c++
- comp.lang.c++.moderated

Both are about the C++ language itself (and the STL), not about specific OSes or libraries (yet things like boost are tolerated, after all it has become *the* unofficial STL). But they are great resources when it comes to *standard* C++.
