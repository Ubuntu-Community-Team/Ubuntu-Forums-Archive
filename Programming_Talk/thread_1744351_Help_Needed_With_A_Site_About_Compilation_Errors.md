### Post by maximzavadskiy on 2011-04-30
I've created a site about **compilation and linking errors during programming** - **Get Rid of ERRORS** - [http://getridoferrors.sourceforge.net/](http://getridoferrors.sourceforge.net/). This WIKI will gather and sort out errors, their meaning, special cases and clear examples of them with easy solutions in one place. And I really need some help with filling the site! I believe that the idea is nice. Imagine, you won't have to spend plenty of your time searching the whole Internet for only one error!
If are in, then register there and start creating some articles or filling existing ones. Please follow the template - [http://getridoferrors.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Cannot_allocate_an_object_of_abstract_type](http://getridoferrors.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Cannot_allocate_an_object_of_abstract_type).
 A good example - [http://getridoferrors.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Expected_unqualified-id_before_%E2%80%98smth%27](http://getridoferrors.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Expected_unqualified-id_before_%E2%80%98smth%27).

If you have some suggestions or advice about the idea or template, please feel free to write it here.
