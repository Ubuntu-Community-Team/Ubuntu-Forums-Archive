### Post by slavik on 2006-12-26
a signal is something you send when an event occurs.

one of the easiest examples is if you are standing outside watching for the cops while your friends are opening the bank safe. when the cops show up (event) you alert them by sending a signal (flashing lights or something).

or when you run away and get hitched (event), you send a text message (signal) to your parents.
