### Post by SeanHodges on 2008-10-01
Have you taken a look at some of the projects on Launchpad or Sourceforge?

For example, search for some keywords of topics you are interested in here:

[https://launchpad.net/projects](https://launchpad.net/projects)

The project overviews will often give you lots of information about each project (language, licence, contact details...). You can just branch the project onto your local machine and play about with it.

The most useful forms of contributing is usually bug fixing and documenting (see the bug list for the project), but to start you can just modify bits and learn how they work.
