### Post by ankursethi on 2007-06-26
Okay, I compiled it. To those wishing to know what dependencies i-team needs, here's a list :
libsdl1.2-dev
libsdl-mixer1.2-dev
libsdl-image1.2-dev

Just apt-get install these and the corresponding dependencies. Compilation guide is available here : [http://iteam.wikispaces.com/Compiling](http://iteam.wikispaces.com/Compiling)

Now for a short review :
1. The landscape is very good.
2. The one character (SuSi) looks very nice,
3. No performance glitches. No FPS problems.
4. No walk cycles. Makes the game look slow.
5. Sprites needed for jumping positions and getting hit.
6. I can simply walk off the map. Feature? Bug?
7. What are the controls? I couldn't figure out how to use weapons.

Question : Is single player planned?

All in all, a very nice piece of work. Keep going, guys.
