### Post by dshuck on 2009-05-19
Adobe has created a really awesome platform for doing Flex development on Linux with Flex Builder 3 alpha.  I have been using it about 9 months and could not imagine having to go without it.  

Recently, Adobe has put up for debate whether or not they want to consider this project and are asking people to vote for it if they would like to see it continue.  

Even if you are not a Flex developer today, perhaps you will be tomorrow.  A strong showing by the linux community would probably go a long way toward the hope of even seeing future linux apps besides FB.  

Regardless, I urge you to vote for this.  NOTE:  A comment is not a vote. They actually have a little voting app in the thread.

Here is a link to the discussion:
[http://bugs.adobe.com/jira/browse/FB-19053](http://bugs.adobe.com/jira/browse/FB-19053)
