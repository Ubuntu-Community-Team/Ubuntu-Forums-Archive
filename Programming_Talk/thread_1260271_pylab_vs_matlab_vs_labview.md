### Post by cszikszoy on 2009-09-08
I can't recommend [Octave]("http://www.gnu.org/software/octave/") enough.  It's essentially an open source implementation of Matlab.  They even have quite a few (if not all) of the extra libraries that Matlab comes with.  I had a couple of image processing matlab scripts that worked flawlessly in octave, no modification necessary.
