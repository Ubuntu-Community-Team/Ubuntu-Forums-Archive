### Post by cmat on 2009-01-01
Depending on the icon set you can view the licences on their respective websites. They are available on GNOME's or Tango's homepage. I use them all the time in my apps. If I can remember they are located in "/usr/share/icons" or "/usr/share/pixmaps".

### Post by nvteighen on 2009-01-01
GNOME icons are most part of the so-called "GTK+ Stock" and therefore, are licensed under GTK+ terms (LGPL).
