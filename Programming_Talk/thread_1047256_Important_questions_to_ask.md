### Post by myrtle1908 on 2009-01-22
Ensure that once objectives are agreed upon, a specification is created and signed off by *all* parties before *any* work takes place.

Too often have I had to deal with clients whinging that "But hang on, it was supposed to do this!".  With a signed spec/contract/quote etc you can confidently reply "No.  See here in the specification you signed, it is supposed to do this not that".  Then you can charge them for the change.
