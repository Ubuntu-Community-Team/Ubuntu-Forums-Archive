### Post by matthew.ball on 2010-08-26
Just a few that I have read:

Operating Systems: "Operating Systems: Design and Implementation" by Andrew Tanenbaum and Albert Woodhull - [http://www.amazon.com/Operating-Systems-Design-Implementation-3rd/dp/0131429388](http://www.amazon.com/Operating-Systems-Design-Implementation-3rd/dp/0131429388)

Databases: "Database Systems: Design, Implementation and Management" by Peter Rob and Carlos Coronel - [http://www.amazon.com/Database-Systems-Design-Implementation-Management/dp/0760049041](http://www.amazon.com/Database-Systems-Design-Implementation-Management/dp/0760049041)

Object-Oriented Programming: "Design Patterns: Elements of Reusable Object-Oriented Software" by Erich Gamma, Richard Helm, Ralph Johnson and John Vlissides - [http://www.amazon.com/Design-Patterns-Elements-Reusable-Object-Oriented/dp/0201633612](http://www.amazon.com/Design-Patterns-Elements-Reusable-Object-Oriented/dp/0201633612)

I would recommend all of them.
