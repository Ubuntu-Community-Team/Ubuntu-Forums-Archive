### Post by CptPicard on 2010-03-11
I find it very difficult to believe that anyone could be a decent designer/analyst without knowing how to actually code -- the way to learn what works is to read and write code, not mess with UML diagrams :)

This is also why I like high-level languages... they allow for very flexible, fluid experimentation with implementation strategies. Especially a beginner will get exposed to much more stuff faster. These ideas will then eventually allow him to do "formally proper" design...
