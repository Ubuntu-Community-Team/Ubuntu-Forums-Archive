### Post by Ferrat on 2008-10-22
So what you really want is a program (no web interface) where you got browser capabilites, can listen to other peoples music no matter what they are listening to? 

It's a major undertaking since you would have to just for the listening part need to stream the music and in that case independent from what the user is listening to meaning the guys acting as "server" his computer would slow down the more people listen to his music. 
otherwise you could use multiple sources in which case you got a even bigger task, also in using all your friends computers as your personal mp3 players it would have to be very effective since not many would want to act mp3 player if it affects their own usage. 

First of list in detail what you want your app to do, the look at what you need to create in order to do that and so on and try to find the simplest solution :)
