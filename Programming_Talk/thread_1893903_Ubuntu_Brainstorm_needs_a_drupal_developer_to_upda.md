### Post by DarwinSurvivor on 2011-12-11
The brainstorm team is looking for a drupal developer that can update the code for the website.

Brainstorm is where users posts ideas for improving ubuntu, then others get to vote on the ideas. Brainstorm has been key in quite a few brainstorm features. Go take a look at [http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com](http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com)

As you may know, brainstorm is powered by ideatorrent ([http://www.ideatorrent.org/](http://www.ideatorrent.org/)) so check out the website to take a look at the feature requests, bugs and source code.

Our wish list for ideatorrent includes:
- New theme (to match the ubuntu.com theme)
- Upgrade to drupal 7 (currently works on drupal 5 and 6)
- Bug fixes (tracker is at [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ideatorrent](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ideatorrent))
Even if you can only help with one or two of the above, we would greatly appreciate it.


If you are interested, please post a reply or visit us in #ubuntu-brainstorm on irc.freenode.net
