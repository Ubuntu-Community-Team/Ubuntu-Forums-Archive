### Post by curvedinfinity on 2008-10-28
Hey all, we're running a competition for developing very small games over at mirthkit.com. Our target development time for the games is a week of spare time. The winner gets to pick an open source project to receive a $500 donation. :)

[http://www.mirthkit.com/?p=92](http://www.mirthkit.com/?p=92)

Just some background about MirthKit:

Though the website is all polished up and fancy, in reality, we are just three long-time friends who are trying to change the way games are made. We are tired of the current game industry, and we aim to bring more power directly to the individuals who are passionate about making games.

MirthKit is a game platform, kind of like Flash and Steam smashed together. The first game we made for it is open source under the X11 license, and the next game we are making is an actual commercial game that you buy.

However, the big twist is that games you buy on MirthKit give you access to their full source. -- They aren't open source, but they are sort of a hybridization of commercialism and open source. We hope this arrangement enables more people who care about openness to make games as a living, as opposed to just as a hobby.

Anyway, feel free to drop comments/questions about MirthKit or the actual competition. :)
