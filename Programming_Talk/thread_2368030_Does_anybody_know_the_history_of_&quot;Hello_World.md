Wikipedia refers to various implementations of "Hello World" from 1967, 1972 and 1974. See - ["Hello, World!" program]("https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/"Hello,_World!"_program").

As a former amateur radio operator myself (G6AVH & G4MBY) I like the idea of "Hello World" being a call to anyone "out there" but if Wikipedia is to believed then it originates from within the BCPL community. 

As an aside I used BCPL on a BBC Model B back in the 1980s when programming on a PC was far far simpler than it is today.
