### Post by Zugzwang on 2010-03-11
This is called cross-compiling; there exists the "mingw32" compiler for windows executables in the Ubuntu repositories. The terms "cross compiling" and "mingw32" should get you started with a google search for explanations, manuals, HOWTOs, and the like on cross compiling.
