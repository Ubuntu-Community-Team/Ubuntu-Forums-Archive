### Post by aj_ on 2009-03-06
As a novice programmer, I strongly urge you to NOT begin with C++. I recently switched to C, and after one chapter of KnR's C book, I had learned much, much more useful stuff than in 600 pages of a C++ doorstop.

As a rather humourous example, one of the exercises in the KnR book is to write a rudimentary C syntax checker. When I was testing it, it spotted an unclosed single quote in one of my source files :). Really made my day. Quite a nice change from the nonstop Cow goes moo, Sheep goes baah inheritance garbage of many C++ books.
