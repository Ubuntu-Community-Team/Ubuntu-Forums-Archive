### Post by Skardal on 2007-06-13
To show the "Console"-tab at the bottom of the window, do this from the menu:

Window --> Show View --> Console


When you run your program, nothing will happen until you write something in the console textarea. When you hit Return/Enter the program will print out the exact same text as you wrote...but you may already figured that out ;)
