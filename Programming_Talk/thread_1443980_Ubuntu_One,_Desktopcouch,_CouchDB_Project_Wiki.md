I have started a wiki page with the intention of bringing together people who are developing projects using Ubuntu One, CouchDB, and desktopcouch. I have talked with lots of people who are developing projects that could benefit from talking to one another, sharing experience, and code. If you are making an app that uses CouchDB or Ubuntu One, or adding this functionality to an existing application please add your project to the wiki.
