### Post by Hellkeepa on 2010-03-29
HELLo!

It depends upon what you classify as a "large number", from my experience pure web development fora are not as active as more general fora. Reason is quite natural, as a pure web development forum is more specific and thus only attracts specific topics, which there are far less than compared to general topics.

If you're Norwegian, or can understand Norwegian, I'd like to recommend [Norsk Webforum](http://www.norskwebforum.no). ExpertsExchange seems to have quite a few knowledgable people too, but that's a commercial forum, so maybe not something for you.
Other than that, you could just post here; Are a couple of web developers here too, and lots of other programmers and developers. ;-)

Happy codin'!
