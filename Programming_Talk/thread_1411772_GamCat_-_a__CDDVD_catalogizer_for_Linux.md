### Post by minthaka on 2010-02-20
I'd like to announce the availability of GamCat 0.0.49, a program written in Gambas2, developed by me.
Key features:
- Scanning of CD/DVD 's and folders into sqlite database
- Scanning through archives: ZIP, TAR, RAR, ACE and ARJ
- Search through the database and saving the searching steps
- Creation of virtual compilations: from database, until K3B project or ISO image
- Creation of html reports of a volume, or creation of a website for the entire collection
- Thumbnailing the images: JPG, PNG, BMP, TIFF and PCX
- Register of DVD movies, of rent volumes...

The gambas2-gb-db-sqlite should be installed manually. I hope you'll like it!
