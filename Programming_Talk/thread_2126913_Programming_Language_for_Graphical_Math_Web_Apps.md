### Post by slickymaster on 2013-03-18
There's [AMPL]("http://www.ampl.com/") which is a modeling language for linear and nonlinear optimization problems and lets you use common notation and familiar concepts to formulate optimization models and examine solutions, while the computer manages communication with an appropriate solver.
