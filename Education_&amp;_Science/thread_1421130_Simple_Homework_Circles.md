### Post by howlingmadhowie on 2010-03-06
Here's a simple solution:

AC and BD are parallel.
angle(CAE) + angle(EBD) = 180 deg.
angle(FAE) + angle(EBF) = 90 deg.
angle(AFB) = 90 deg.
CE and AF are perpendicular
ED and FB are perpendicular
so angle(CED) = 90 deg.
