I've been researching this subject for a while and some people wonder &#8220;how does the system create more energy than it uses?&#8221; The answer is that the introduction of HHO gas into the combustion mixture reduces waste of your primary fuel - energy that is already available but not put to full use. The vast majority of fuel that reaches the typical engine is wasted as it is not properly combusted. HHO works by mixing with and making more of that gasoline or diesel fuel ignite. The result is a more complete and cleaner burn, translating into a 15-50% increase in mileage. This way you get more power out of the fuel you already paid for, instead of it going out the tailpipe as emissions.
