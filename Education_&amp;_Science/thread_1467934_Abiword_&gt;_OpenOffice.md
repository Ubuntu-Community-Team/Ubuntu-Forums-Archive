### Post by MindFusion on 2010-05-01
To anyone who has irritated himself on the various glitches and user un-friendly interface of the OpenOffice text processor, I bring to you: Abiword. It's a fun little text processor, boots up way faster than OpenOffice (1 second compared to 3 seconds, lol) and I find the interface to be way more pleasing -- as it resembles the Microsoft Office versions somewhat. I discovered it when I was using PuppyLinux, where Abiword is a standard app to the entire pack. I'd personally recommend it.
