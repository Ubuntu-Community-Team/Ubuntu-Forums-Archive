### Post by ddarsow on 2008-10-14
I have not found a LInux specific one, but ESB Unit Conversion Utility is Win32 freeware and runs fine with Wine.

It converts pretty much any unit you can think of and does things such as: distance, weight, volume, speed, and many more. Very comprehensive and very easy to use. It has stuff for use in the home as well as stuff for a rocket scientist or electrical engineer!
