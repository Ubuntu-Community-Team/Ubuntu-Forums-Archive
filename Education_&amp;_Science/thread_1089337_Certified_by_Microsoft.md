Among the important IT certifications desired by IT professionals, a [ **Microsoft Certificate** ](http://www.timenetlearning.com/courses.aspx)has no match. Being a reputed company, Microsoft has a loyal base of customers. A certification from Microsoft would mean a solid and superior knowledge of the IT systems. 

The company has introduced a wide range of certification exam for entry level to experienced professionals and for domains ranging from networking, system administration, database management to programming, web development and other advanced technologies. The Microsoft certification exam ensures whether or not information technology professionals have the necessary skills to successfully implement business solutions using Microsoft Technology.

Microsoft certification is a tough exam which needs hours of preparation and practice. Mastering the test would involve taking practice tests on the Microsoft certification exam pattern and understanding the correct as well as incorrect answer explanations to clear the doubts.
