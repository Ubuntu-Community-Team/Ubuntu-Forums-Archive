### Post by hubie on 2009-11-27
There are a number of cell counting plugins for [ImageJ]("http://rsbweb.nih.gov/ij/").  Check out [here]("http://www.bioimage.ucsb.edu/downloads/automatic-nuclei-counter-plug-in-for-imagej"), [here]("http://labs.fhcrc.org/fero/Protocols/cellcountsimagej.html"), and [here]("http://rsbweb.nih.gov/ij/plugins/cell-counter.html") for starters.
