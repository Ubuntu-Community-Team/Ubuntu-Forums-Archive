### Post by hubie on 2010-06-23
I've been refreshing my long-dormant German skills, and one of the tools I use on my Ubuntu machine is [Anki]("http://ichi2.net/anki/") to serve up flash cards.  You can download user-provided decks for them, or you can make your own.  Anki is in the repositories, but their download page warns you that the version is very old.  They suggest you use [this DEB file]("http://anki.googlecode.com/files/anki_0.9.9.8.6-1_all.deb") instead.
