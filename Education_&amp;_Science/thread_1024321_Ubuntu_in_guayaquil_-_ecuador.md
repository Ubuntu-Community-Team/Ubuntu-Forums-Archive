The free software community KOKOA

We are a group of educators and students of the ESPOL interested in promoting in our university and in the society the use and distribution of the free software. With the firm conviction of which the software must be a free and accessible resource for all, our mission is to announce to the world hisbenefits stimulating his use at the personal level, academic and professionally across different types of events. 

The chats, exhibitions, workshops, holidays(parties) of installation that we organize help themselves our visitors to be interested and to familiarize with the wonderful world of the free software. Besides this we offer to our members the opportunity to demonstrate his(her,your) doubts, commentaries and opinions across interactive forums, to which they can accede across this portal.
