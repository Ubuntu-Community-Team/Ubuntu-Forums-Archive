Without this exam passed the high-school studies are not validated. 

This test should probe that the child can use a computer and do simple tasks. Open a computer, enter on a webpage, search something, edit a text, ..things like that. First time when i've heard about this i've said: "very cool", but now i found the details.

- The students should use only the following
    * Ms Windows 
    * Ms World
    * Internet explorer
    * Bing 

If they request to use Linux for example, or at least google instead of bing, they will fail the test and they should try next year. 

From my point of view this is shocking and i understand it like: 
-A big corporation managed to monopolize the education system in a country with 23 million citizens. They use kids, and force them to learn their products in order to use them and buy them. Is it moral to not have a choice and to be forced to learn to use a commercial product? 
I got friends that tell me there will not change their os, even if they use windows only for browsing and they have problems and need to reinstall that crap at every 2 weeks.
They put the stamp with "linux is not safe", "linux is hard to learn" ... even before trying it. Why this? Because they were brain-washed (from the marketing pov). If adults can be brain-washed, our kids are even more vulnerable to this. 

Now i have some questions, that maybe one of you can help me. I knew that there is a kind of department of the European Union where you can complain about things like that 
(liberty to choose, violations of the concurrency, monopolization..)  Can someone guide me?
