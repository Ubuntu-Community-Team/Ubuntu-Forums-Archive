I sponsor an electronics club and we use Xubuntu on old computers to run sims and to learn the basics of logic gates, etc.  For playing around with logic gates, I prefer [ktechlab]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/ktechlab/"). For experimenting with full circuits, [Oregano]("http://oregano.gforge.lug.fi.uba.ar/") is great.
