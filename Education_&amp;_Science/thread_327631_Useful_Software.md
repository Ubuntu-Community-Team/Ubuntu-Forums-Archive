### Post by mikeyandmary on 2007-01-02
> **originof said:**
> school is not a prison [-(

You're right school is NOT a prison... However... Recording of negative behaviours is an important tool used by teachers to be more proactive with students who may be at risk of harm to themself or others. It is also used to track improvement in students who regularly show poor behaviour i.e. they may not receive any merits but they may be showing less negative behaviours. Recording referrals and comments regarding negative behaviours (as well as positive comments etc.) also enables teachers to set up their class more effectively rather than having to recollect all the information about the children they teach. 

Sorry about my rant but most people who throw out the "school is not a prison" comment have little idea about effective classroom management in which info about ALL BEHAVIOURS (positive and negative) is worth more than gold!!!

BTW... I agree with jonathan.lees post that this would be a great idea for new software.
