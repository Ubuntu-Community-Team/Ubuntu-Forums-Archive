### Post by manueluribe on 2014-10-14
CAD is part of it. You'd need software designed for computational fluid dynamics simulation...

Through the magic of Open-Source :) Just so happens Blender has impressive fluid dynamic sim capability. And of course, Blender is -totally- free. It's not a ready-made solution, but it can get you there. As for wings... I don't know how familiar you may be with the Blender  Foundation. I highly recommend hitting YouTube up for "Sintel" in high  def! It'll give you an idea of what Blender can do.
