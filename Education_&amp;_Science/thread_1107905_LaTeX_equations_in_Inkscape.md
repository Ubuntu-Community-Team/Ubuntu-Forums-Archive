### Post by parktownprawn on 2009-03-27
You can used the inscape plugin [Textex]("http://www.elisanet.fi/ptvirtan/software/textext/index.html") to put Latex formulas in your inkscape drawings. The nice thing is that you can re-edit the formulas.

To install, follow the instructions on the website above
