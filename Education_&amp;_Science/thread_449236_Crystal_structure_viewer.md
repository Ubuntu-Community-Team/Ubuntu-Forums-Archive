Good depends on your needs, but (in approximate order of increasing power and difficulty): Rasmol, VMD, Pymol.  Deepview (Swiss PDB viewer)'s Linux version is broken, but overall is probably the easiest to use and about equivalent to VMD in power.  Grasp is good for some very advanced things, but the free version is Windows-only.  In the non-free cateogry ($1000+), there's InsightII, Discovery Studio, and a few others I've not actually tried; my lab's experience has been that the free programs are just as good 99% of the time.  Most of the free ones will even create POV-ray files to generate extremely nice images.
