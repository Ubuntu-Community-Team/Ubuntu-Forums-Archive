### Post by Jad on 2008-01-29
Weather Report applet has it all, except "any server weather alert"

Right click on your panel -> Add to panel -> weather report
and then right click on it and set your preferences.

You can also read the manual of [Gnome-Weather-report]("http://library.gnome.org/users/gweather/stable/gweather-introduction.html.en")
