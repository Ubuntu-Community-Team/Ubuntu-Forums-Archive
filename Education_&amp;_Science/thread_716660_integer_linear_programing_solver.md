### Post by WW on 2008-03-06
Yes, for example:

[lp-solve](http://lpsolve.sourceforge.net/5.5/) (Ubuntu packages: lp-solve, lp-solve-doc, liblpsolve55-dev)

[glpk](http://www.gnu.org/software/glpk/glpk.html) (Ubuntu packages: glpk, glpk-doc, glpk-utils, libglpk0, libglpk-dev)
 
 All the packages are in the Ubuntu **universe** repository.
