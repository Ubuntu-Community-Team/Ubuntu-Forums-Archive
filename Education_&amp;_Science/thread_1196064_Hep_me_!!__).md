### Post by machoo02 on 2009-06-24
Things you will probably need to buy (unless you bring them with you):
[LIST]
[*]Sheets/bed linens
[*]Pillow
[*]Towels
[*]Hangers for any clothes you would like to hang in the closet
[/LIST]

After that...the list is really up to what you want to have in your dorm room.  You'll likely be purchasing a meal plan for the dining halls, so you shouldn't need any cooking equipment, although some dorms do have small communal kitchens.  You may want to have a small refridgerator or microwave in your room for snacks and such, but you can talk about the purchase of this with your roommate if you have one.
