Ugh!

I just finished this book and thought it was fairly bad. I'm not saying I didn't get 'any' information out of it....but I got pretty sick of reading 10 pages on how to operate a mouse and entire chapters about programs like Firefox. However, all truly technical topics were glossed over in a paragraph or two.

The worst was the fact that half the words in the book were sentences saying about "what I will discuss in Chapter XX, <full topic name>" or "as I discussed in Chapter XX, <full topic name>".

Seriously...if he'd cut all the cross references (or at least shortened them) and talking about what he's going to talk about, the book would have been about 1/3 the size.
