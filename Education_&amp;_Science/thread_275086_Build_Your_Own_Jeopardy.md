### Post by PWill on 2006-10-10
BYOJeopardy ([http://www.wolfescience.com/byojeopardy/](http://www.wolfescience.com/byojeopardy/)) is a 'jeopardy-like board creator. BYOJeopardy helps you make custom boards that you can use in the classroom or play with friends.'
Here are some screenshots: [http://www.wolfescience.com/byojeopardy/screenshots.php](http://www.wolfescience.com/byojeopardy/screenshots.php)

It is great for use in the classroom, IMHO.

I built a .deb of the latest version, for anyone that wants it.
You can also grab the source from SF, if you'd rather build it yourself: [http://prdownloads.sourceforge.net/byojeopardy/byojeopardy-1.2.11.tar.gz?download](http://prdownloads.sourceforge.net/byojeopardy/byojeopardy-1.2.11.tar.gz?download)
