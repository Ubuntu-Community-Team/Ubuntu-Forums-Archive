### Post by alenis on 2008-12-22
We use Moodle, because it has almost everything we need to effectively manage our courses. Probably the only thing we are missing is a good videoconferencing module. We also tried eFront: it's easy but it lacks good cooperative learning tools. Another product you may want to try is Docebo, not mentioned in the link you provided but still good especially for business environments.
