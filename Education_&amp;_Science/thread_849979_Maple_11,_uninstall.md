### Post by Sef on 2008-07-05
From [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=206471") (slightly modified):

>  Re: how do I uninstall a .bin file?
Programs that come as .bin's often have uninstallers with them. If you navigate to the (Maple 11) directory there should be a subdirectory called something along the lines of '_uninst'. Then in terminal you should be able to run the uninstaller by typing something along the lines of './uninstaller'.

I hope this works for you.
