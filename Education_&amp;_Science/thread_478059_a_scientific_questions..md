### Post by Pablitox on 2007-06-22
Hi, I think your machine would be a bit complex and not efficient. First of all, you want to condense the water (humidity) from the air... to do so, you will have to refrigerate the atmosphere air and you will need lots (I mean LOTS) of condensed water to use it in the hot plate (because you are going to need to heat that water over 100 ºC, to produce enough pressure and mass flow to move the engine).

That engine, would be an "external combustion" engine. That's because you must heat the water before it goes into the machine. 

Between al those process, your system will loose a lot of heat (pipes, etc.). That lost of energy causes the positive variation of entropy...
