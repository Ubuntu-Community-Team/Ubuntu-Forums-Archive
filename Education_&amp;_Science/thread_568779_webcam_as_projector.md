### Post by gbutler69 on 2007-10-07
This should work just fine.

I used a Webcam to take photos (up close) of jewelry (which is notoriously difficult to photograph).

In experimenting with getting my solution to work, I often put pages beneath the camera and could read them very well. Projecting them onto a "Big Screen" should work fairly well.

You'll probably want a small table lamp and a 3-sided cardboard box.

Mount the camera inside the box looking down onto the table.

Also, mount the small reading lamp or some-such on the box pointing down.

You'll need to experiment a little to get everything just right, but it should work fine.

Then, simply use the program "Camorama" along with your projector hooked up to your video out and you should be good-to-go!
