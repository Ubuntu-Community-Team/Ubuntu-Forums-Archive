### Post by LaserJock on 2006-11-24
A lot of the applications are in Applications->Education . However, for younger kids there is a cool app called gcompris. You can find it in Applications->Games->Educational suite gcompris .

If that's not enough or what you're looking for the edubuntu-users mailing list (more info on [http://lists.ubuntu.com](http://lists.ubuntu.com)) should have more info.

Hope this helps,
