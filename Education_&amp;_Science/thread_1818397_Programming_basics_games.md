### Post by benedictusk on 2011-08-05
I don't know of a good game to teach programming, but I do know a simple language that's great for younger students. It's called Netlogo, and it's what they teach sophomores at my high school in an introductory course to computer science. 
It's a good language for young students because it's surprisingly easy to get images on the screen, and even to make simple games. 

You can download it here: [http://ccl.northwestern.edu/netlogo/download.shtml](http://ccl.northwestern.edu/netlogo/download.shtml)

After you download it, you have to extract it. Then, to run Netlogo, you have to run the file called netlogo.sh

I actually finished my school's course with Netlogo quite recently, so if you need any help, just ask me.
