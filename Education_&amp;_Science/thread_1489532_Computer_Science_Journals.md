### Post by PC_load_letter on 2010-05-24
I'm not 100% sure about CS but in Mathematics, some of the subscription are really expensive as they are mostly priced for institutions. As a grad student, I get access to many (if not all) the journals I need via a proxy connection to the Univ. library web page. 

If you're not affiliated with a research/teaching institution, you may just try [www.scholar.google.com](www.scholar.google.com) to search for articles. Also the preprint server [arxiv.org]("arxiv.org") is priceless.
