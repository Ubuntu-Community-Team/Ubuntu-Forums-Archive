### Post by bunditt on 2011-08-12
Hello everybody, I am studying at the University of Southampton in the UK. I am conducting a research about open source ERP. I would like to invite you to take part in a questionnaire which is part of the research project to identify factors affecting open source ERP adoption in organisations.

However, the research requires you as a participant to have a role in software or IT project acquisition in your organisation. Your responses are very important in enabling me to gain a better understanding about this topic.

The questionnaire should take you about 5 minutes to complete. You will be asked a few questions about you, your organisation and your opinion on factors affecting open source ERP adoption in your organisation. This is the link to the questionnaire -> [LINK]("https://www.isurvey.soton.ac.uk/2992")

Participation in this study is fully voluntary and you have the right to withdraw at any time with no penalty. I treat your participation as anonymously as practically possible. All data is treated as confidential.

Your responses and others will be used as the main data set for my research project for my degree in Knowledge and Information Systems Management at the University of Southampton. The resultant of the study will be posted on this forum after I have completed the data analysis.

I hope that you will find completing this questionnaire is a pleasure experience. If you have any question or would like further information, please do not hesitate to email me at [email]bt1e10@soton.ac.uk[/email]

Thank you very much for your time.

### Post by tommpogg on 2011-08-13
I believe your research project is very interesting.
However, I have some doubts about the reliability of your questionnaire: anyone can access and fill it even without knowing anything at all about ERP. So the results of the questionnaire could be biased by partecipants who are not expert in the field.
I am not saying that there are malicious participants that intentionally want to harm your research. I just want to warn you that some peole could fill your questionnaire with good intention but also with a lack of knowledge about ERP.
Maybe you should consider posting this thread on forums about ERP or similar fields. I believe the response would be more reliable.

Anyway, good luck.
