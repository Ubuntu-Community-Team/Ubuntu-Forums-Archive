### Post by nateperson on 2009-11-02
Say, A1 has 10 and B1 has 100.  If you want C1 to show 10%, you input in C1 ```
=(A1/B1)
```
which will give you .1 as a result.  You then change the format of the cell to percentage and 10% will show.
