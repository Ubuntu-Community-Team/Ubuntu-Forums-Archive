### Post by jbrefort on 2008-04-22
Abiword stores internally the equation as mathml code as far as I know. No chance to export either latex or mathml to rtf, at least. Even exporting to word format is not easy at all (I totally ignore which is the format used by word). You might send your friends a pdf file.
