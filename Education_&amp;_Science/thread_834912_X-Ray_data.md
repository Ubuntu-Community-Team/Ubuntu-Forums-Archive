### Post by rajan on 2008-06-20
what output format do you have? if you look back at the data in emacs or another text editor, you can see it's just a bunch of numbers (at least in the data i'm used to seeing). they correspond to the intensities at each increment. you can put the numbers into oocalc and make a spreadsheet out of it. some people in my lab have used excel and oocalc is just as good for this purpose. otherwise you can try xmgrace which is much more elegant and complicated.
