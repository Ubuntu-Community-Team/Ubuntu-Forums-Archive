### Post by knattlhuber on 2008-10-13
I just took the Savoir-Faire Linux course 1. It's quite easy and provides a good framework for more in-depth reading. You'll certainly need some other prep material for the exam (like e.g. the IBM prep tutorials or O'Reilly's book on LPI Certification). The course does not include the LPI 1 exam. You have to register separately for that (another $155..).
I don't work in the computer field and I only switched to Linux less than a year ago. Before, I had been tinkering with the Unix command line on OSX. I passed the exam without problems. You can do it, too! :)
