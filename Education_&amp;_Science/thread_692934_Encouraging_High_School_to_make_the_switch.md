### Post by .nedberg on 2008-02-10
Do they have to "switch" completly?

Why not persuade them to set up a couple of Linux clients and maybe some Linux print servers / Samba servers. If they are going to set up a new server/client make them try Linux first (here is where you _must_ help them!).

If you get them to use Linux on some machines they will get used to it and eventually see that they have less problems with them. Then they will probably switch more machines. They will also see that Linux might be cheaper than the licence they pay for Windows.
