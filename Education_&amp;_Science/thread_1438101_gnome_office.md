### Post by ugm6hr on 2010-03-24
My personal experience of Gnome Office - MS Office (2003) converison:
AbiWord - much faster than OO.org, but does tend to get formatting wrong.
Gnumeric - files much smaller than those created by OO.org, but they wouldn't open in Excel 2003. Had to open them in OO.org and then resave to be able to use in Excel.

This is my personal experience from about a year or 2 ago - so things have hopefully changed.  In the interim, I've moved fully to OO.org, despite using a Xubuntu base.

Have no idea re: MS Office 2007 formats though.
