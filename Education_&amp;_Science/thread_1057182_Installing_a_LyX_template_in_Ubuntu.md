### Post by kleeman on 2009-02-01
Templates are like normal lyx files (something.lyx). Create a subdirectory in your home directory and put it there. Then in lyx go to 

File---->New from Template

The dialog allows you to select the subdirectory you have created. Your template can then be opened.
