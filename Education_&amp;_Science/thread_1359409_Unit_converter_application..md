### Post by gunksta on 2009-12-19
It appears that ConvertAll is going to do what you wanted, but I thought I would mention Alt-F2.

If you are running KDE, Alt+F2 (the Run dialog) is a competent unit conversion tool.

Example

12 in > feet

12 in > meter

etc.

Enjoy.
