Hold down the Alt key, and try dragging the window upward to see if you can drag the needed buttons into view. When you are holding the alt key, you should be able to click near the bottom of the window to move it upwards. From your screenshot, it looks like you might be able to move the window to see the bottom. (although this wouldn't work for me when I ran into a similar situation described below...)  
I had the same problem when I was creating a new style for a journal that I typically submit to.  I couldn't see the "Save" or "Cancel" buttons, and thus had no idea what to do.  I ended up having to find a theme that made very small window borders, and then I could see the tippy top of each button.  I could then at least click the button that I hoped was "Save" to get things to work!  I posted this in the Bibus forums, but never got an answer or workaround.  Hope you get things figured out!
