### Post by PC_load_letter on 2011-06-12
I'm no expert in Maxima, but I think it's a symbolic algebra program. Maxima does very little if nothing numerical in nature. What you want is something like **Sage** from [www.sagemath.org](www.sagemath.org)  (it can also be installed as a google chrome plugin from the Chrome web store), or **Octave** (in the repos).

Sage is very close to Mathematica, having the notebook worksheets that could be published etc. It has a great online manual, with examples.
