### Post by lewac on 2011-01-25
uhh.. you can search on *.sym to find it but here it is... /usr/share/gEDA/sym/quite a few directories under here that contain the symbols. note that you can build your own using one of them that is close to what you are looking for (just save it under a different and descriptive file name so you can find it later. you need to shut down gEDA and restart though to see your changes/additions.
