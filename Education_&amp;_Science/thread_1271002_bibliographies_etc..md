### Post by samden on 2009-10-04
The OP does not want to export to Endnote, they want to export a reference list formatted in a particular style, to put into their documents.

Unless you use LaTeX (which you should learn!) you'll be better off using Bibus, Zotero or Mendeley, as previously suggested. All of these will directly interact with OpenOffice Writer or MS Word to not only give you a reference list at the end of your document, but also format all your references the whole way through, so you don't accidentally forget to put one in the reference list.
