### Post by mips on 2013-01-06
2x + [24-2 (y)] = 20

2x + 24 -2y = 20

2x -2y = -4

x - y = -2 

-y = -2 - x

y = 2 + x

Now sustitude the above answer into the y of the original equation to determine the value for x, once you have the value for x put it into the original equation for x and then determine y.

In this case, they mean the same as the parentheses (round brackets). Square and curly brackets are often used in place of a second and third set of parentheses when they are nested, to provide a visual distinction.

[SIZE="3"][FONT="Lucida Console"]{[1 + (x^2+2x+3)(x+2)]/[x + (3x^2+3x+1)(2x-1)]}/3=0[/FONT][/SIZE]
