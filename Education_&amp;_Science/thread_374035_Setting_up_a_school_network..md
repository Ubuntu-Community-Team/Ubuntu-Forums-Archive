### Post by jethro10 on 2007-03-02
How about using your good computer as a terminal server so your clients can be any old rubbish.
You get a bit more control here as well. Mind, you need loads of memory in your "server", but after that its suprisingly quick

Its already being done, look here [http://www.ltsp.org/](http://www.ltsp.org/) and here [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/LTSPHowTo](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/LTSPHowTo)

if you go to here [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/) and search for ltsp you get a lot of info.

This seems a common way to solve your problem.

ltsp stands for "Linux Terminal Server Project"
