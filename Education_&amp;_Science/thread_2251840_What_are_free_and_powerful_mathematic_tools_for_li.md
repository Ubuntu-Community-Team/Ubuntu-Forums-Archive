### Post by rewyllys on 2014-11-08
For the specialized area of statistics, a superb program is R.

It's free (for Linux, Windows, and OS-X), easy to install, and straightforward to learn, with lots of online help and tutorials.

To learn how to download it, go to this URL:

[http://cran.r-project.org/](http://cran.r-project.org/)

In my opinion, R is best used in conjunction with an extremely convenient GUI: RStudio, which is also free (and available for Linux, Windows, and OS-X). It is available from:

[http://www.rstudio.com/products/RStudio/](http://www.rstudio.com/products/RStudio/)
