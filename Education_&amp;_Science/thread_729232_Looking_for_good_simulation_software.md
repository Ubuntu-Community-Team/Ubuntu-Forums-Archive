### Post by LaserJock on 2008-03-19
You might want to look at Step ([http://edu.kde.org/step/index.php](http://edu.kde.org/step/index.php)) which is a KDE (although you can run it in Gnome) physics simulator.

The boot time is odd. Generally Ubuntu (and by extension Edubuntu) are faster or at least as fast as Windows.

As for as a catalog you might want to check [http://www.osv.org.au/index.cgi?tid=155](http://www.osv.org.au/index.cgi?tid=155) . It's a quite comprehensive look at educational software for Linux.

-LaserJock
