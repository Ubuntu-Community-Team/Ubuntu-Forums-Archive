### Post by hayden92 on 2008-11-29
Try:
cprogramming.com (This site is great for a broad sweep of most of c and c++, I recommend it whole-heartedly)
programmersheaven.com (for general source code and an active community where you can ask questions and get (usually) good answers.

Personally though, I recommend a good book for learning... it seems to help consolidate new knowledge and concepts faster than online tutorials. Also, watch out because there are many tutorials that are vague and poorly written and will most likely give you bad habits.

Hope that helps!
