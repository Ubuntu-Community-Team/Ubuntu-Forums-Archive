      I came across this page some time ago, and thought it would be fun to try to answer all the questions on it.  I'll start with an easy one:

**Q**: If you had an infinite supply of water and a 5 quart and 3 quart pail, how                  would you measure exactly 4 quarts?

**A**: Empty both buckets. Fill the three quart bucket.  Pour the contents of the 3 quart bucket into the 5 quart bucket. Fill the three quart bucket again. Pour the three quart bucket into the 5 quart bucket until the 5 quart bucket is full.  Empty the 5 quart bucket.  Pour the remaining 1 quart form the 3 quart bucket into the 5 quart bucket. Fill the three quart bucket.  Pour the three quart bucket into the 5 quart bucket.  There are now 4 quarts exactly in the 5 quart bucket.

I tried to be specific here, because you never know with those interviewers :P.

I think these questions are a good way to learn to problem solve, and what better place than here?
