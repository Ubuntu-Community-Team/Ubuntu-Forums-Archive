### Post by quixote on 2010-02-02
well it's too late for last night's homework, but gimp might sort of work.

Open the image in gimp, select the rotate tool, select the shape you want to rotate, move it till you line up with the second line of the angle, and the dialog box tells you what the angle is.

Was this an angle you were supposed to measure with a protractor?  In which case this method is just as good.  Or was it something you were supposed to figure out with geometry or trig?  In which case this will give you only very approximate results!
