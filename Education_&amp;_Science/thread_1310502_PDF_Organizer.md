### Post by bovender on 2009-11-01
Hi wirporte,

check out Zotero or Mendeley and see if they do what you want.

Zotero is an extension for Firefox. I use it to collect references and write scientific manuscripts (using the OpenOffice plugin). Zotero can store PDFs for you. I don't know off the top of my head if it can reorganize an existing PDF collection for you, but I am sure you will find out.

Mendeley is a very promising tool to manage one's PDF collection. However I must say that when I tried it (~ 6 mo ago) it crashed a lot (back than I ran it on Windows XP). It is under active development, so maybe they've "ironed out the details" by now.

Good luck!
