### Post by Dannys514 on 2015-12-09
You won’t find out to swim by analysis a book on swimming, so when you’re looking to encourage your practice while preparing for a Linux documentation, open basis projects are a fine place to dive right in. Try to match those wanted skills among the work the mission needs. Sign up for the mission mailing list and/or join its IRC. Come up to the project with assurance and enthusiasm, and you’ll likely find a community equally eager to put you to work.
