### Post by gesslein on 2010-01-04
Mathomatic is easier to use than Maxima and may help people become more interested in mathematics.  [http://packages.ubuntu.com/search?keywords=mathomatic](http://packages.ubuntu.com/search?keywords=mathomatic)
I recommend downloading and compiling the latest version from [http://www.mathomatic.org](http://www.mathomatic.org) for more beautiful results.

If you are going to use Maxima, I recommend wxMaxima for a nice GUI to make Maxima easier to use.
"sudo apt-get install wxmaxima" gives you Maxima with wxMaxima.

Think of Mathomatic as for High School mathematics and Maxima is for college mathematics.
