The equation aX + bY + cZ + d = 0 defines a plane. All points below the plane satisfy aX+bY+cZ+d <0.

A simple way to plot would be to use gnuplot's splot command. Look for here for lots of tips and examples.

[http://t16web.lanl.gov/Kawano/gnuplot/index-e.html](http://t16web.lanl.gov/Kawano/gnuplot/index-e.html)
