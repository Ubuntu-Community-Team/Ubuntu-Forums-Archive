### Post by tgalati4 on 2008-02-28
There are a lot of online translators and resources available.  Try your local library for old Latin text books or garage sales.  Once you get a collection, start translating passages using the web for words or phrases (idioms) that you don't know.

Of course one of the easiest Latin texts is the Vulgate (Common) Latin Bible.  Since many of the Bible stories are familiar, it helps with translation.
