### Post by kirk.mactavish on 2011-03-26
We are able to map complete indoor environments with the Microsoft Kinect. (AKA SLAM, Simultaneous Localization and Mapping)

It's written using Willow Garage's ROS (Robot Operating System) for inter-process communication, OpenCV for computer vision libraries, and the network optimizer TORO out of the University of Freiburg for loop closure.

A quick video of our Final Year Design project for the University of Waterloo:
