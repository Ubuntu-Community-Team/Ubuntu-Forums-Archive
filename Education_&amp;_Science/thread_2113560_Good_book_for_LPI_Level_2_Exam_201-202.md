### Post by El Tito on 2013-02-20
Hi. There is a lot of pages and information in the web, like [http://www.happy-monkey.net/LPI/](http://www.happy-monkey.net/LPI/) or [https://www.lpi.org/exam-preparation/self-study](https://www.lpi.org/exam-preparation/self-study). In the first there is a study book and in the second tests.

I used the Roderick W. Smith book LPIC-1:Linux Professional Institute Certification Study Guide, but the most important is to do tests. Search a little and you will find kingtests (for example), which show you the typical questions in the exams. The questions could be tricky, so I think this is the most important.

Hope this helps.
