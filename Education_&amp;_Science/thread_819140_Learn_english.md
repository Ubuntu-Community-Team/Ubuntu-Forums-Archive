### Post by Buzzdee on 2008-06-06
Hey Sandrish, 

there's a whole bunch of learning programs for children in the package 'gcompris'. Since you're new to ubuntu, you might need a more detailed explanation. (Otherwise, just finish reading here and go install it :D )

What you do is you go into System, Administration, Synaptic Package Manager. You're prompted to type your password. Then Synaptic opens, you click 'Search', make sure 'Name' is chosen as the field to search in and type 'gcompris'. This will show you the package, now click the little square left of it, say 'install' and click 'Apply' in the main bar of Synaptic. This then takes some time and will soon tell you that all changes have been applied. Close all programs, go into 'Applications', 'Education' (probably?) in the ubuntu menu (you might call it 'Startmenu' for obvious reasons, although you can stop your computer in there as well, but that's a completely different story...). 

If there are any questions, just reply here, I'll check back.
