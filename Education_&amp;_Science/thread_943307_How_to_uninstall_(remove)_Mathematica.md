### Post by Darkstar_444 on 2008-10-11
Hello

Go to System/Administration/Synaptic Packet Manager and enter your password. 

Click on Status (Bottom left of page).

Then click on Installed (Top left of page).

This should give you a list of all files on your system. Look to see if mathematica is on your system and remove it that way as whenever you install a program it should get listed here. 

Should do the trick
