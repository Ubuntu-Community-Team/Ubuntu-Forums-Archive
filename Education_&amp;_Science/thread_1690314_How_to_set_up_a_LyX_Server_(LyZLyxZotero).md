### Post by 334455 on 2011-02-19
Go to the Homepage of LyZ: [http://www.klubko.net/wp/?page_id=945&lang=en](http://www.klubko.net/wp/?page_id=945&lang=en). Under "Installations and settings" is info on how to set up the lyxserver or lyxpipe. You must enter the path both in LyX and in the LyZ settings inside Firefox. You may have to restart LyX and Firefox after setting the path.
