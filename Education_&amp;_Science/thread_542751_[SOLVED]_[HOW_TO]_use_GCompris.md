### Post by mocoloco on 2007-09-04
There should be a launcher for it under Games.  If it's not there open a terminal (Applications -> Accessories -> Terminal) and type "gcompris" and see if its starts, or post any errors it lists.
Once you get it running it's fairly self-explanatory.
Another app you can look at is "Childsplay", although there's not much to it at this point, but it's one to watch as it develops.
