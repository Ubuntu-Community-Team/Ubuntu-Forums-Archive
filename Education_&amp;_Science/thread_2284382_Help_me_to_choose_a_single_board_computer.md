### Post by samueltx on 2015-06-29
Toradex Customized [Single Board Computer]("https://www.toradex.com/customized-single-board-computer")  is an off-the-rack platform that comprises of a Computer on Module  (COM) and a carrier board. The platform can be effectively scaled up to  accommodate future requirements and latest technology by switching to  another pin-compatible COM based on latest processors. The platform is  likewise adaptable as significant processor, memory, and I/Os can be  chosen based on the end-application's requirements.

### Post by ssam on 2015-07-03
If you really need flash then you'll have to stick to x86. If the only video will work with HTML5 then arm is probably ok.

For x86 you could look at the intel NUCs which are fairly low power, or mini-itx boards (beware some of the intel atom boards have the intel GMA500 or GMA600 which work very badly in linux). Also machines like [http://www.fit-pc.com/web/purchasing/order-mintbox/](http://www.fit-pc.com/web/purchasing/order-mintbox/) .
