### Post by Adam1545 on 2013-05-22
Essentially, Google Drive allows for more than one person to work on the same file. You can start a new file and then provide permission to others to edit the same file. Once you've jumped that hurdle, it's simply a matter of looking at different files that (for example) two students also have access to. As they write, you could suggest changes in capital letters.

With regards to making things beautiful, that involves visiting [https://developers.google.com/google-apps/documents-list/](https://developers.google.com/google-apps/documents-list/) and then figuring the rest out for yourself, depending upon your personal preferences!
