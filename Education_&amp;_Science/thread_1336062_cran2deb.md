### Post by Alexqw on 2011-03-31
Sorry to revive such an old thread, but I had the same question and figured I'd share what I found.
[http://www.mail-archive.com/debian-med@lists.debian.org/msg06538.html](http://www.mail-archive.com/debian-med@lists.debian.org/msg06538.html)

The following is the appropriate lines for sources.list:
```
deb [http://bioinformatics.rri.sari.ac.uk/cran2deb/rep/](http://bioinformatics.rri.sari.ac.uk/cran2deb/rep/) testing main
```

It is worth noting that currently they're only building for Lucid.
