### Post by hugmenot on 2008-10-28
There&#8217;s also a preview mode for Emacs that renders the formula in the editor as soon as you close the $$ or \[\] tags.
[http://www.gnu.org/software/auctex/preview-latex.html](http://www.gnu.org/software/auctex/preview-latex.html)
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/preview-latex](http://packages.ubuntu.com/preview-latex)

Alternatively there is a plugin for Tomboy that does the same:
[http://www.reitwiessner.de/programs/tomboy-latex.html](http://www.reitwiessner.de/programs/tomboy-latex.html)
