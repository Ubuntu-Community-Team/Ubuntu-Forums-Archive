### Post by vanadium on 2008-02-24
One of the strengths of Bibus is that it very much allows you to tweak the formatting of the citations and the bibliography. If you do not find an AMA style, create it yourself. You won be able to automatically add pagination to the citations superscript, though: you will indeed need to add that manually.
