### Post by crasic on 2011-08-15
Seconding memilanuk. 

Sage is a fairly well developed project that can compete pretty well with commercial CAS options like Maple and Mathematica.

What makes it perfect for you is that it's  "notebook interface" (similar to mathematica) is actually browser based. It hosts a small http page that acts as a direct interface to the computational kernel. Saving you lots of work of integrating it onto a website.
