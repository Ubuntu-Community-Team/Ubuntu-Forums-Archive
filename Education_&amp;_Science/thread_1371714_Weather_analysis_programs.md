### Post by jabl on 2010-01-16
Some of the packages used for production weather forecasting are open source, and as supercomputing including weather prediction is dominated by Linux these days, those codes should be more or less straightforward to install on Ubuntu. The wikipedia page for NWP contains links to some of those: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Numerical_weather_prediction](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Numerical_weather_prediction)
