### Post by bsamim on 2013-08-11
I know how to speed read having taken a course. Basically you can learn the various hand motions online and then practice on a book or you can use your monitor if you don't mind putting your fingers on it or very near to it while reading.  The 3 main things to learn are "wide underline" , "s pattern" and the "x pattern" I pretty much use all 3 of these and that is all you need. I would just find a you tube video that shows these.
