Here's the specs for it:

Specifiations: Drive Type: DVD+/-RW (+R Double Layer) / DVD-RAM Drive. Storage Removable / Interface Type: IDE. Enclosure Type: Internal. Speed: 2X(2.4X)/4X/24X/24X/8X/24X.
