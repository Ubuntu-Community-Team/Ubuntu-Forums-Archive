### Post by 3Miro on 2010-10-26
I don't have specific information for your model in particular, however, in my opinion, if everyting is working well, there is no real reason to go from 10.04 to 10.10. Gnome (the Ubuntu desktop environment) is currently in a "calm before the storm", brefore they release the next Gnome 3.0, so there is almost no difference between 10.04 and 10.10 in terms of graphical environment. There are upgrades on some of the "under the hood" components, however, if it ain't broke, you should probably not be trying to fix it. Basically, if unsure, stay with 10.04.
