### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-10-01
Just FYI, we do not recommend experimental BIOS flashing for the following reasons:

1. When there is a BIOS update that helps System76 computers, we will actually *recommend* the update and provide instructions for performing it. But most updates enhance Windows functionality.

2. If you perform a BIOS update and it destroys your computer -AND- we did not recommend it or provide instructions for it, we cannot cover that under warranty.

THAT SAID, it is your computer and you can do what you want with it. This isn't Windows. I just want to help you understand the risks involved.
