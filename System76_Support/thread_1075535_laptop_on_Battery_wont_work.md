### Post by Maheriano on 2009-02-20
You've confirmed the adapter is charging the battery, now you need to figure out why power is not going from the battery to the machine. It's likely a connection on either your power or ground, put the computer on some sort of insulated surface where it won't be grounded externally. Fire it up, remove the plug and let it shut off. Hold some metal and touch the computer base, does it shock you? You got ground problems. If not then I'd look at examining the contacts for power from the battery to the machine.
