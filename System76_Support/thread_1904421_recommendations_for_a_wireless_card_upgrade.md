### Post by dodo3773 on 2012-01-04
Two things that helped me out a lot when I was faced with the same problem was disabling ipv6 for that network in networkmanager and setting a static ip address for my machine (not sure if that is possible since your at work though). The only other wireless cards I have (usb) are the alfa awus036h (realtek chipset I think) and they have never given me any real problems.
