### Post by perce on 2008-03-16
Hello to everybody,

in the white Darter if you tap the touchpad with two fingers or if you tap it on the top right corner you get a middle button click and if you tap on the bottom right corner you get a right button click. I also got some button 5 clicks by tapping the right side and moving toward the, outside, but not in a consistent way.

Sorry if you already knew, but for me it was quite a discovery which I wanted to share.
