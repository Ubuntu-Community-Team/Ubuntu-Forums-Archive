### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-09-05
To activate the webcam:
1. Run your System76 driver's 'Install Functionality'.
2. Restart your computer.
3. The Fn-F10 keys toggle the webcam

Cheese will detect it, it just takes several seconds. If you get the multi-colored screen in cheese, shut cheese down, press Fn-F10, and restart cheese.
