### Post by crichell on 2007-01-05
The earlier referenced post:  [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=161830](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=161830)

worked perfectly for me.  You'll have to modify the resolution lines to match your external monitor. i.e.

```
Section "Screen"
Identifier "builtinlcd"
Device "intel0"
Monitor "Internal"
DefaultDepth 24
SubSection "Display"
Depth 24
Modes "1280x800"
EndSubSection
EndSection

Section "Screen"
Identifier "externallcd"
Device "intel1"
Monitor "dell1905"
DefaultDepth 24
SubSection "Display"
Depth 24
Modes "1280x1024"
EndSubSection
EndSection
```
