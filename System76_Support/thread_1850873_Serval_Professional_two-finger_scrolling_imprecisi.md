### Post by Tristan Schmelcher on 2011-09-27
I recently bought a serp7 but I have am having problems with the two-finger scrolling. If I scroll, pause, and then release my fingers from the touchpad, the document sometimes scrolls an extra amount during the release, even though I am not moving my fingers anymore. Usually it scrolls in the upwards direction. So for example, if I am trying to scroll down the document will keep "bouncing" back up when I release. This makes the scrolling very imprecise and kind of frustrating to use. The problem does not occur with edge scrolling, but I'd really like to use the two-finger scrolling.

I did some experiments and I think the problem is caused by releasing one finger slightly before the other, because if I deliberately do that slowly then the problem happens almost every time. It also only happens if one finger is higher on the touchpad than the other (but that's pretty much always the case because I scroll with my index finger and middle finger and the human middle finger is much longer than the index finger). I think that when I release one finger, the touchpad still thinks that I have two fingers pressed down and I have just moved them to the same spot.

Does anyone else experience this problem and know of a solution?
