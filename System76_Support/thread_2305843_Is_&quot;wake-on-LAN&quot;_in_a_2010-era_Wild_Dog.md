### Post by watchpocket on 2016-01-01
Answering myself for anyone else who may have this question. 

My Wild Dog does have ***Wake-on-Lan*** and it can be turned on or off from the BIOS menu.  

To get to the BIOS menu, simply hold down the ***F2* *key*** while you're booting up.  Once in the BIOS menu, it's simple to navigate among the 5 or 6 windows with the different settings, and you'll easily see where Wake-on LAN is.
