### Post by IAmWhatWasDavidBowman on 2010-09-08
You will need some sort of device that will convert the digital signal to an analogue signal before your TV will be able to use the output from the computer. A cable alone will not do it unless it's got some sort of circuitry built in.

Don't expect good results however! HDMI works well from a computer if you've got an HDTV, but I remember outputting signal from an old video card of mine to an analogue TV and the resolution was terrible!

I can't offer you any more specific advice than that. Happy Hunting!
