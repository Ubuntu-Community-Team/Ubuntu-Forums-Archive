### Post by thepoeticdragon on 2008-03-27
I can't begin to describe how anxious I am to receive the Gazelle Value that I ordered last Thursday. As a college student, I have been having a rather difficult time getting around without a laptop on hand that I can just pop out and work on at anytime.

I do run Ubuntu regularly and it was the main OS on my previous laptop (which I had sold, for reasons I will explain later). While I do jump back and forth on a dual boot, I must admit that Ubuntu has so many things that I enjoy more than stuff in a Windows install. Actually... if I had to really point out one thing that makes me love Ubuntu Linux over Windows, it would have to be simply... Amarok. 

:guitar:

That being said, I finally got sick of my 17'' monster of a laptop, made by HP. I decided to put it on sale and get a smaller one. I frequent the UbuntuHQ website often, and I stumbled across System76. The specs were very good for what I wanted to do, and to make matters much easier, the Gazelle was a 14'' with a brushed metal panel finish.

Sold.

My HP gave me nothing but problems - the monitor panel started to give out, and although I got that fixed, I ended up hating having to lug the huge thing around. I ended up selling it to someone that didn't care much for the size, and used to the money towards the Gazelle.

I must say, I give you guys at S76 a lot of respect for making these machines with a wonderful OS... a great way to show the open-source community the support it needs. One of my hallmates has a previous model Serval, and it is a very good system. I'm looking forward to using my Gazelle in every which way possible.

Now I'm just waiting for it to come in...
