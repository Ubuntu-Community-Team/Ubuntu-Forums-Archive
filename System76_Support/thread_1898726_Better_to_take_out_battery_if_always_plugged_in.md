### Post by isantop on 2011-12-22

### Post by JohnSmithNot1882 on 2011-12-23
My opinion on this matter is leaving the battery in serves as a back up in the event of a power failure.  Consumers frequently pay $100 or more for this feature in a desktop.  When the battery does fail in about a year, you can replace it for probably less than $100.  Two batteries should easily last 4 years and that is the length I normally keep my primary computer.
