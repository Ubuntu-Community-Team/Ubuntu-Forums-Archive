### Post by crichell on 2006-08-25
Hello fisherman - there are some considerations:

Shipping depends on country and weight.  Shoot us an email with city/country/zip and the machines or machines you're looking at and we'll provide shipping estimates for each.

Taxes: This is typically 17% VAT to EU countries - I'll have to confirm this is correct depending on your country.  Our delivery company (typically DHL) handles foreign taxes.  After the computer is delivered you receive a bill from them for the amount of taxes due.

Power: Laptops and desktop are built to handle both US and European power systems.  You'll need a plug converter to go from our flat two prong to your round two prong.
