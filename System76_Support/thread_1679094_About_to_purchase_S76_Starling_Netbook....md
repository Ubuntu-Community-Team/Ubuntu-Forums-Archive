### Post by thomasaaron on 2011-01-31
> 1. I recognize there is a significant battery problem with S76. How bad is the battery problem with the Starlings currently, because I might just get the 3-cell battery and save myself $60.

TA: There is no battery problem with the current iteration of the Starling (the Star4). Most of the posts you see to that effect are referring to the Star1, which has long been out of production.

> 2. How long to the 3-cell and 6-cell Li-Ion SMART batteries last in comparison.

3-4 hours, and 6-7 hours respectively.
   
> 
3. If I need to replace the battery for any reason, (i.e. upgrading or battery failure) where can I go to do so?

If there is a hardware issue, you would contact us via email at support...at...system76...dot...com. If you just want to buy a new battery, you can do that through our website.


> 4. How bad are the design flaws?

There are no known design flaws with this system. I would ask for any other star4 customers to chime in on that. A previous forum poster did take exception to this premise; unfortunately, we were unable to pay for replacing his cracked screen.
> 
5. Is this purchase worth it?

I'll leave that to other customers as well.
