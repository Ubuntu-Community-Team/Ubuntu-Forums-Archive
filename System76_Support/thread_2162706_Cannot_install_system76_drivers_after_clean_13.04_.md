### Post by rorschachwalter on 2013-07-15
From [this website]("http://knowledge76.com/index.php/LemU2"), it seems the only thing the Driver did was the restore function, which as I understand it is just adding the System76 repositories to your system. But if there's nothing the repositories do for your system, that could explain why the buttons are grayed out -- there's nothing to do on your system. No need for the Driver.

### Post by isantop on 2013-07-15
This wasn't very well documented, so it seems to have generated some confusion, but there was recently a couple of big changes in the driver. You can download the latest version from a PPA, and the driver installation is automatic when the package is installed. If there are any additional steps, then the buttons will be available.
