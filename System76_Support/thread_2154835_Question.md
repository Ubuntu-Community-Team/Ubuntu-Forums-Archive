### Post by 3Miro on 2013-06-16
Over the past 6 years I have owned 3 System76 laptops and one desktop. I never regretted a purchase. The machines are very well build and definitely higher quality than main-stream Windows machines.

Everything is cheaper than Apple, you can get most of the same hardware on other platforms for much lower price. Unless you are doing something very mac specific, there no reason to pay the high premium for just the OS.

System76 isn't cheap, however, if you boost the specs of the machine that you are buying, in my experience System76 has always won on price.

One potential drawback is the lack of Windows support, which is meaningless if you don't use Windows. I had experience in the past with installing Linux on Windows machines and I don't want to deal with this ever again. I want something that is guaranteed to work with Ubuntu right out of the box.

In my humble opinion, the major selling point for System76 is that they not only provide top-quality hardware, but also provide a warranty for the OS.
