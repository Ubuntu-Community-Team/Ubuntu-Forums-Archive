### Post by jsmidt on 2007-05-19
Apple keeps winning awards for being such an innovative company.  This is largely due to the innovative nature of their hardware.  This makes them successful.

On the other hand, companies like Dell have been successful because they can ship a good product at a great price.  However, you don't see any back-to-back-to-back awards given to Dell for how innovative their product is.  To make a cheap computer you can't have the greatest innovation.

I would love to see system 76 follow Apple's model over Dell's.  I would love to pay a little extra to get the extra innovation.  I'm not saying don't make inexpensive models, I would just like a place I could get a Linux computer with hardware on the same innovation level as Apple delivers.  (I know some will argue this is already the case.  Well then please keep it up.)
