### Post by jml on 2007-09-22
Do you get the same error when you try to burn an .iso image of a Linux distribution?  If you don't have a handy .iso image available, go to distrowatch and download a copy of Damn Small Linux, its less than 50 megs.  After it is downloaded, right click on the icon and select Write to disc.  If that also fails, then you may have a faulty optical drive.  If it works, then the other applications you are using may be the problem.
