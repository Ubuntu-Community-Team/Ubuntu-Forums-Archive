### Post by macabrem on 2009-09-21
Is the ink on metal or plastic?  Have you tried anything yet to see if you can get it off?  I would probably try starting with a damp washcloth first.  If it is on plastic, I might consider a screen cleaning wipe if there aren't any other suggestions for cleaning the exterior from System 76.  If metal, I saw some suggestions on the internet to use a little toothpaste - maybe you can rub a small spot with a Q-tip to make sure nothing scratches or gets damaged.  Also, you might want to browse through Apple or Dell websites and see their cleaning recommendations for their products.
