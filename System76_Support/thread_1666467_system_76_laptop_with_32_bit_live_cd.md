### Post by isantop on 2011-01-13
It is possible, as 64-bit processors are backward compatible with 32-bit programs. The process would likely be very similar to your old laptop, though we do have instructions for installing the System76 Driver, which, apart from providing any drivers or fixes for the system, also is handy for support purposes. Those instructions are here: [http://knowledge76.com/index.php/Restoring_Your_System](http://knowledge76.com/index.php/Restoring_Your_System), and they're applicable to both 64-bit and 32-bit installations.
