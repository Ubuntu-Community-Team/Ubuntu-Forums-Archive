### Post by thomasaaron on 2010-04-19
> Able to run Ubuntu 10.04 out of the box

Yes. It may need our System76 Driver in 10.04.

> Fast enough to play Farmville and Runescape

Not sure about those. Probably, but I'll let some other Lemur owners chime in.

> Ultra thin, like thin as possible WITH a DVD drive

No DVD Drive. You would have to use an external drive.

> Large display (I currently use a 10.1" netbook as my main machine, and it does not work well)

If you netbook plays runescape and farmville, the Lemur will. It would be much more powerful than your netbook. It lso has a bigger display.

> 3GB of RAM

It only has one DIMM slot. So you would have to go with 2GB or 4GB.

> 320GB HDD

320 GB 7200 RPM SATA II ( +$20.00 )

> Bluetooth

Yes.

> Built in Webcam

Yes.

> 802.11n

Yes.

> Fast (I can't emphasize this enough, but it needs to open apps quickly, be able to run 5-6 apps at a time, and have good enough graphics to use compiz and play online games such as farmville, runescape, and other flash games)

It definitely will be much faster than your netbook. It does have a nice core-2-duo. The owner of System76 uses a Lemur for all of his work (and he could pick whatever he wanted). It's a good combination of power, portability, and efficiency. It's not really intended as a gaming machine.
