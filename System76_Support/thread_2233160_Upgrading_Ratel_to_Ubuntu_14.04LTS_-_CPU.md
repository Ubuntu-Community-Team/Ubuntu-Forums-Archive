3rd Generation **Intel Core i5 3470** (3.20GHz, 6MB cache, 4 Cores, HD Graphics 2500)

### Post by Bucky Ball on 2014-07-06
Good to go. AMD64 is for ALL 64bit CPUs, AMD and Intel. i386 is for ALL 32bit CPUs. Good luck. ;)
