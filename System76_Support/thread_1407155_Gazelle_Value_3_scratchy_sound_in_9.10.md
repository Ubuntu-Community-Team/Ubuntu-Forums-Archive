### Post by thomasaaron on 2010-02-15
7an, I've found two issues that would cause that on the older Gazelles:

1. You don't have the volume turned up all enough. I don't think there is a PCM slider on 9.10. But you should turn the output volume up OVER 100%. (Don't max it out, just keep moving it up until you get it where you want it.)

2. You have blown speakers.
