### Post by sflicht on 2012-09-20
I have a gazp6 with this configuration. I use the SSD to boot from and have a 500GB second drive in the caddy in the optical bay. It is internally attached and cannot be removed easily. Certainly, you can still use external USB optical drives.

When the system arrived, and ever since, the second drive does not automatically mount when I boot. I never cared enough to mess around with /etc/fstab and figure out how to get automount to work properly. This is because it is very quick to mount the drive manually, either by command line or using the Disk Utility. Overall the configuration has worked very well for me, but I wanted to let you know that there might be kinks -- of course, maybe these have been worked out since I purchased my system last year.
