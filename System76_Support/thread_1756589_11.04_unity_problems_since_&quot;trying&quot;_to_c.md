### Post by HotShotDJ on 2011-05-12
I can't tell you specifically what went wrong with your set up, but I can tell you that on my Pangolin (PanP5) with Nvidia graphics, Unity pitched a fit when trying to work with a dual-monitor set-up.

In my case, it worked fine with the second monitor plugged in on my desk at home.  BUT, as soon as I'd try to run it with only the laptop monitor (such as at work, on my back porch, etc.) the screen would get all wonky and non-responsive, requiring a hard shut-down.  Switching to "Ubuntu Classic" mode corrected the problem for me.

Unity is definitely NOT ready for prime-time, at least not for me.
