### Post by isantop on 2011-08-18
Go ahead and reset your sound setting to how it was when your system was shipped. Once you have that done, we need to make sure you have the latest version of the driver. open up the System76 Driver application, then click on about. Make sure the version you have is 2.6.8.

If you do have the latest version, then click on the "Install Drivers" tab and then click on "Install". Once this is finished, reboot your system, and then the internal speakers will mute when you plug in you Bose speakers.
