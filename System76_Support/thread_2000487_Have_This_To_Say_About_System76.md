### Post by re1d on 2012-06-09
**2 1/2 years in with my Pangolin laptop.** I was using it this morning, and realized it had been a long time since I'd checked the forum! Then I thought about why that was. 

After the first rush of euphoria with my new laptop, I put it to work. It's my most important work tool, and I use it for hours every day. It runs a VM with other OSs on it (including THAT one, regrettably - can't get around having to use it once or twice a month, unfortunately). I work it hard, and it would seriously inconvenience me to have it go down.

I trust this laptop to do everything I need to do, and it always does. So except for a brief problem I had back at the very beginning (which turned out to be something I'd done during customization) I've had **no problems and no reason** to check back to the forum. I'm busy, so I gradually stopped checking into the forum for social reasons, and haven't done so at all in the past six months or more.

That occurred to me this morning, and I thought I'd say something about it: ***You guys make real tools - thanks for the products you put out and the service you provide! It's been a real blessing to me, and I appreciate it. Keep it up!***:)

Thanks,             Reid
