### Post by AusIV4 on 2007-04-23
I suspect you'll notice a steep improvement between the PIII and the Celeron M. I have a desktop I built that has a 2.93 ghz Celeron D inside, and it's able to process video with speed I found to be an impressive leap from the 1.2 ghz P4. I haven't used a core 2 duo (though I hope to upgrade my Celeron D this summer), so I can't really comment on how much improvement it would offer over the Celeron M.
