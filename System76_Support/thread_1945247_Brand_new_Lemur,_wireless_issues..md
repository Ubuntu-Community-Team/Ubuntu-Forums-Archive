### Post by ccrs8 on 2012-03-23
Do you know which wireless card came with your Lemur?  I had problems with the wireless in my Lemur as described here:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1916776](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1916776)

The solution was to get what was at the time the "upgraded" Intel wireless card for the Lemur (I had opted for the default Realtek card that proved to be less-than-stellar).  Recently System76 stopped offering the lower-end Realtek card and only offered the Intel card.  Depending on when you ordered your Lemur, you may not have been able to get the Realtek card - in which case, disregard this post.
