### Post by isantop on 2013-11-27
Our systems all use LED backlighting, so there's no inverter. 

Try opening the System76 Driver and using the restor function. I'm thinking that the ColorPro profile may have gotten moved or deleted, which would result in a yellow-ish tint (The screens natural uncalibrated profile isn't great, which is why we include the profile).

Failing that, contact us via a support ticket (from the link in my signature below) and we can set up a repair.
