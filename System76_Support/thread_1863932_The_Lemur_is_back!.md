### Post by isantop on 2011-10-18
Actually, there are a lot of cases where Glossy screens are better. Matte screens are good if you have high-contrast glare in front of the screen, since they will disperse the glare evenly of the entire screen. However, because of this diffusion, they get washed out very easily. Glossy screens aren't as subject to this. So for working in bright areas (like outdoors), glossy screens to offer some advantages.

The other main benefit is clarity. Just like they diffuse light hitting the surface of the screen, Matte screens diffuse the light produced by it as well. This makes glossy screens a bit clearer and brighter.

That said, personal preference plays into it for some customers. For customers who do want matte screens on their laptops, we do offer them on the Gazelle and Serval, in both standard and high-color variations.
