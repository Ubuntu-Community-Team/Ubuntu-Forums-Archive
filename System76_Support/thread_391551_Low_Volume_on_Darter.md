### Post by MarkID on 2007-03-23
So far, everything is going well with my Darter.  However, when I watch videos (DVDs, YouTube, etc.) the volume is very low.  I do the obvious like raise the volume, but it's still a bit too low to really enjoy. Any advice?

### Post by crichell on 2007-03-23
My guess is that one of the Volume Control toggle buttons isn't active. Open up volume control (right click on speaker > Open Volume Control). Click Edit > Preferences.

Make sure all the boxes are ticked on. You should have PCM and Front. FN + F12 increases PCM volume and FN + F11 decreases PCM. Increase Front to it's highest level and you can then use the hot keys for increase/decrease with a broad range of volume. If there is any distortion when PCM is increased all of the way lower Front slightly until the distortion is gone.
