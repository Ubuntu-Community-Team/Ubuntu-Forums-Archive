### Post by thomasaaron on 2010-03-29
The mobo has four memory slots, but you only use three of them to take advantage of the triple-channel memory. In other words, memory has to be installed of sets of 3 cards, thus the options...

3 GB - 3 x 1 GB - Kingston HyperX Triple Channel DDR3 - 1600 MHz  
6 GB - 3 x 2 GB - Kingston HyperX Triple Channel DDR3 - 1600 MHz
12 GB - 3 x 4 GB - Kingston HyperX Triple Channel DDR3 - 1600 MHz
