### Post by francisco_athens on 2008-07-24
HP is supposed to releasing a tiny notebook with an aluminum exterior and magnesium interior.  The specs are pretty modest but its built for portability and longer battery life. 
It's supposed to be bundled with SUSE GNU/Linux.  
[http://www.linuxdevices.com/news/NS4205653001.html](http://www.linuxdevices.com/news/NS4205653001.html)

IMHO, You may be better off with a quality plastic on the outside vs aluminum, as the soft metal bends and dents easily (tho it is no too hard to repair).  If you want to have something in the rugged category, you would probably find the outside of a notebook made of a more ridged yet light metal like magnesium.

EDIT: I forgot to mention that metal on the palm rest is not all that pleasant when the device becomes warm from use/charging.
