### Post by steveneddy on 2011-02-19
With the exception of my monitor on the laptop getting a little pixel death (due to a drop I believe) my four year old Serval Performance continues to run to this day.

I replaced the monitor on the laptop while in the middle of an 18 month road trip covering most of the 48 lower states and the hardware never failed to deliver the same performance I received the very first day I powered up the laptop. The machine was jostled and tumbled through some of the roughest terrain and weather anyone could encounter and never failed to boot up at the end of the day and let me communicate with work, family and friends, help me get to the next job and keep track of all of my business needs - without fail.

I am now on 10.04 (this lappie started on 6.something) and has been going strong ever since.

I used it for Beryl testing back in the day and have used it for daily business functions for most of the last four years.

Now it is merely a good old companion that I rely on daily for communication and entertainment.

Thank you, System76, for a fine product that has far exceeded any expectations anyone could have foreseen.
