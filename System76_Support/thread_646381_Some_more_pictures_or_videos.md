### Post by asmiller-ke6seh on 2007-12-24
the hardware is not that different from similar products - there are not that many companies that manufacture laptops - most companies buy their laptops from a handful of manufacturers (most are in China).

So, a 15.5" wide screen laptop won't be too different in size from any other 15.5" wide screen laptop. And if you;ve seen one 13" laptop, you've pretty much seen them all. There are variations in specifications (chipsets, hard drives, etc) but the sizes are pretty standard.
