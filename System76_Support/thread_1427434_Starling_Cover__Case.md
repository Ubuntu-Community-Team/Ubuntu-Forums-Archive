### Post by waterdragon687 on 2010-04-15
I know this thread is a little old, but I thought I would put my 2 cents in--I just got this Hadaki-brand case from eBags for my Starling:

[http://www.ebags.com/product/hadaki/eva-111-netbook-case/138398](http://www.ebags.com/product/hadaki/eva-111-netbook-case/138398)

It's not *quite* as good as I'd hoped it would be when I ordered it--turns out I have to detach the battery for the best fit, but on the plus side, there's enough room for both the Starling itself AND the battery inside, and it's got a little inner pocket for an SD card or USB drive, etc. as well. I don't think I could fit the power brick and cord in without worrying that something would get damaged if the case got compressed, but that's not a huge deal to me--I don't take mine around with me everywhere I go, just when I travel, and I always have something else to carry cords and so on in then anyway. I'm more concerned about protecting the Starling itself, and this was the only molded-EVA case I could find that was big enough for it, so overall I think it should work fine for my needs.
