### Post by juelze on 2010-08-17
So I've had my Meerkat Ion for about 6 months or so.  When I first got it everything was great.  A few weeks into ownership the CPU fan was making lots of clicking sounds that were pretty loud.  I contact System76 and they sent me a new CPU fan.  The fan seemed a lot quieter and the clicks were very hard to discern.  It was still very audible but I figured it wasn't that bad.  

Now, my Meerkat Ion is on ALL the time 24/7, but one day I shut it off since I was going to be out of town.  When I powered it back on the CPU fan was making that loud "tick click tick click tick click" sound that the first one made.  I thought to myself "Here we go again."  After 10 minutes or so the noise went away and it was back to "normal".  Today I come downstairs and I can hear my Meerkat Ion from 50 feet away and around the corner from where I keep my PC.  For those of you who own Xbox 360s, my Meerkat Ion is LOUDER than my Xbox 360 when the DVD is spinning.  At this point, I'm thinking I need to just buy some higher quality fans as it seems these fans are junk, poorly designed, or I just got 2 band fans in a row.  

With that said, any recommendations on a quality fan that I can use in my Meerkat Ion?  

Thanks,
Juelze
