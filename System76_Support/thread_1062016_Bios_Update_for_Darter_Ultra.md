### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-02-06
Unless you have a specific reason for doing so (which typically would have to do with Windows functionality), we do not recommend arbitrarily upgrading your BIOS.

If you hose your computer with a BIOS update that we didn't recommend, it could void your warranty.
