### Post by ackey on 2010-02-23
I fixed it last night with some help (lots of fingers crossing)...

I removed the screws on the back black panel, allowing it to slide towards the battery.  The harddrive is near the external monitor connector.  There is a ground (?) wire (black) connected to a screw on a metal casing to the harddrive.  Removing this screw allows the hard drive to slide out, and there were two connectors underneath.  We reseated these two connectors, presuming one or both of them were power for the backlight.  We re-assemble and it works!  Yay!
