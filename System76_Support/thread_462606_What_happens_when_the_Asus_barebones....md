### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-06-04
Yes, we will definitely continue to support our previous computers when we move to newer models.

The System 76 driver will pretty much always get tested with new versions of Feisty against every computer model we've sold, and hardware warranty support will continue for as long as the computer is warranted. After that, as long as we can procure parts, we will provide hardware support for older models.

As far as non-hardware-relates support, we always stand by our customers. (Do I hear a country song in there somewhere? :guitar:)
