My opinion: If you are going to use a desktop, go with a wired connection. If you have a laptop, use wireless.

My reasons (radiation is irrelevant):
1. Stability, wired connections are more stable.
2. Security, if you don't set up wireless correctly, other people may come and hook to your network and do bad things. There is no chance of that happening with a wired connection.
3. The only disadvantage of the wired connection is the extra cable hanging on the back of your computer.
