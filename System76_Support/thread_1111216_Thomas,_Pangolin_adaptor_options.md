### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-03-30
With adapters, smaller *usually* means less maximum amperage. If your adapter can't support the proper amperage for your machine, it can cause the adapter to get very hot and/or die, or it can cause damage to your machine.

If you just need a second adapter, you can order it from our website...
[http://system76.com/index.php?cPath=30](http://system76.com/index.php?cPath=30)

I don't really have any after-market recommendations, but if you find one that matches the specs on the back of your adapter and has the proper size jack, you should be fine.
