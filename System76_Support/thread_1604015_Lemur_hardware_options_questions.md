### Post by thomasaaron on 2010-10-27
1. The wireless upgrade is better for video streaming. +1 on the better speed and range.

2. The Intel X25 SSD is about the equivalent of a 10K RPM hard drive. Very fast. This is most noticeable during boot up and opening applications. It will also be noticeable if you do alot of heavy read-write activity (video editing, for example). The SSD typically helps battery life somewhat. Don't have exact metrics, but we're talking minutes, not hours.

4. Very easy. Just give us a call. Make sure to keep it in pristine condition, though. If there are scratches, we have to make deductions.
