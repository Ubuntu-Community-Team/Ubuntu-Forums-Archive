### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-02-21
The speakers are definitey difficult to access on the Gazelle. There are some things that could definitely go wrong in the process, so we wouldn't recommend that.

If the crackling is just coming from your left speaker, it could be blown. The wires are soldered onto the speaker so, it is not likely to be a loose wire.

Does it make that sound even if you turn your sound volume down?
