### Post by isantop on 2010-07-07
The Serval's DVI port uses a version of the DVI specification that is not compatible with VGA. In general, we've had trouble with DVI to VGA adapaters, and we haven't seen one that works correctly with our machines. Direct DVI connections seem to be the only way to do it.
