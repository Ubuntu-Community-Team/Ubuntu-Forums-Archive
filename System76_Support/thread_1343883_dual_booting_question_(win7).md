### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-12-03
With dual-boot, you can only have one operating system running at a time. With VirtualBox, you can have Ubuntu and Windows running at the same time.

Performance-wise, Windows *should* be faster on hardware than on a VM. However, it's been my experience that Vista on Virtualbox is *very* close to hardware speeds. I really want to say *faster*, but I can't really prove it.
