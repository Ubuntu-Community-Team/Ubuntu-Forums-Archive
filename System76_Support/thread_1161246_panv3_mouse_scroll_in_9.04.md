### Post by drewbenn on 2009-05-16
It's the new 9.04 behavior (I don't like it either) and there's no way, that I know of, to revert to the previous behavior.  You can scroll by using two fingers on the touchpad at the same time (for example, put your forefinger and middle finger next to each other on the touchpad and move them, together, up or down).  To middle click, you now need to press the left and right keys at the same time.
