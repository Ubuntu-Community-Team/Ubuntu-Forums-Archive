> What is your ratio for sold:returned laptops?

Very low

> How much does the Gazelle Per. weigh with the 9 cell battery?

Good question - I'm sure the weight increase is minimal (we don't have 9 cell batteries at the Denver office to check it out.)

> Is there an option to get a 9 cell instead of 6 cell installed

No, the base system comes with the battery so the 9 cell is an add on.

> And my last question, what is the battery life like with each battery?

With the 6 cell you achieve about 4 hours and 6 with the 9 cell.  This varies with the config - different processors with different memory and different hard drives vary the battery life.  I've heard that wifi has an effect as well but I haven't noticed a big draw on my Gazelle Value.
