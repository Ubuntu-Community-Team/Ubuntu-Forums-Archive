### Post by Paradoxdruid on 2006-11-14
I'm the author of one of the above-linked posts.  You can see a little testimonial I wrote here:  [http://system76.com/forums/viewtopic.php?t=26](http://system76.com/forums/viewtopic.php?t=26)

I've had my Koala for a long time now, and it's still working perfectly, seamlessly upgraded to Edgy and doing its job--  I use it as a media center hooked up to my TV (I don't have cable, but I have a vast collection of video files, e.g. Simpsons episodes, and a lot of DVDs).  It plays my videos fine, it browses the web, the wireless keyboard, mouse, and internet Just Work(tm).*

If it's your first foray into Linux, I'd suggest just dual-booting your current system.  But if you actually need another computer (need a replacement desktop, want a media center, whatever), I highly recommend them.  Very powerful for how small and cheap they are.
