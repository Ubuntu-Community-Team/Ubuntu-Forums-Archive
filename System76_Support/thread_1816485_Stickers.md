### Post by LMP900 on 2011-08-02

The stickers are small, so a standard envelope or smaller will suffice. When I ordered mine a few months ago, it took a few weeks for them to arrive. And, as Noah mentioned, both envelopes need a stamp.

Envelope 1 (The one you send to System76)
[LIST=1]
[*]System76's name and address
[*]Stamp
[/LIST]

Envelope 2 (The one you put inside Envelope 1)
[LIST=1]
[*]Your name and address
[*]Stamp
[/LIST]
