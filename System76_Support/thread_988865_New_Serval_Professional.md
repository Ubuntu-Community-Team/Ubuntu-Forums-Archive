What a BEAST this thing is.  We had to wait a long time for this kitty cat to come back to the S76 lineup, but I'm telling you it was worth the wait.

CONSTRUCTION--It's heavy, but that's perfectly fine as long as you're not trying to make it into a road warrior (LAN party rig, perhaps...).  The thing is build like a brick :KS:KS:KS:KShouse.  The textured keys feel great, and their action feels as strong as the gorgeous brushed black metal case.  Which brings me to...

COSMETICS--It's such a sinister looking machine.  Brushed black metal with silver highlights.  Having experienced a matte finish screen with my gazv2, big ups for the high-res glossy screen.  I wouldn't recommend the display upgrade on this model.  Oh, and no Windows key.  Rawk.

PERFORMANCE--Dude, Penryn, 1066 bus, DDR3, **GDDR3** fercryinoutloud.  7200RPM 320GB hdd, won't miss dragging an external drive around just to have my armada of virtual machines.

SERVICE--Again, dude, it's System76.  Were it not for them going above & beyond in this department, they'd have a reputation for having high performance machines instead.

And now for the cons:

PRICE--Sad that I didn't wait 2 more weeks.  Good time to buy now kids.  (get your parents' permission!)

INTREPID--Not sure how I feel about this yet.  There are a number of things I like.  I haven't changed from the default background yet; it looks great on this gigantic screen.  But it doesn't seem to like vmware, or VirtualBox.  Still working on figuring that out though and I can't blame s76 for problems with Intrepid anyway.

It all adds up to achieve maximum rock. :guitar:

Can you tell I love that little guitar guy?
