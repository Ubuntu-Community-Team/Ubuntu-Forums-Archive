### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-04-15
We always use the highest quality components for our hard drives and memory: Seagate, Hitachi, Kingston, Apacer, etc... However, in order to provide our customers with the best product for their money, we often switch back and forth between top brands with no notice. So, it is difficult to anticipate which brand of hard drive or memory you will receive with your computer. But you can rest assured that it will be of the highest quality.
