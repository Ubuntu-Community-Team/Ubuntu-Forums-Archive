Somewhere in some message in some thread of this forum there was a link to a good article on this.
Maybe the person who posted it can do so again.

The gist of it was that a few big companies, mostly in Taiwan, make the bare bone laptops and all the "manufactures" just stuff them & brand them with their logo.
A couple of bare bones companies that come to mind are Asus & MSI, Asus being one of the few that also sells finished laptops.

So when system76 buys a bare bones laptop, stuffs it & puts their logo on it, they are just doing the same thing that Dell, Sony & HP do.
