### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-10-08
Note:

We've had people try to pop off the keys and re-arrange them.
Generally, this has proven to be unsuccessful, as the key connections can be easily broken.

If you break the keyboard doing this, we probably won't be able to cover it under warranty.
