I had experienced a similar problem with Mandriva Spring2011.
The best thing to do is the following to preserve you settings as long as they are working under your id.

01. From your working user account create a new user. Call it Test or anything you like.

02. Logout of your own user account, and login to the new Test account you created. Now you can add all the cool screen stuff etc. and preserve your account.

On my Ratel I have added three additional accounts to play around with. On one of them I have changed the compiz settings, have a different theme, etc. I have also setup one of the accounts to look exactly like a MAC.

In this way if I test something that I like and there are no problems I can easily add it to my user account on my box.
