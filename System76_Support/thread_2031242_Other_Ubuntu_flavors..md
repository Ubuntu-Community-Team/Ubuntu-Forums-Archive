### Post by philbert on 2012-07-24
Regarding the question on whether the inclusion of an  i3 processor, etc. can lower price significantly....

I am not with System 76, but I did work in this type of business for a while. While the price of the CPU makes up a large portion of the cost of the laptop, the difference in the street price between the ICore 3 and the ICore 5 may be less than you would think.  The cost of  the other parts  and the cost to build the system are unaffected.
 
So, I doubt you will see much of a difference in the cost of Lemur if System 76 went with the CPU. But you might be disappointed when you want to do something where the Turboboost support in the I5 would be appreciated.
