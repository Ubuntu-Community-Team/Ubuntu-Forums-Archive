### Post by pete04 on 2012-04-12
I don't own the meerkat, but I suspect it would work very well.

 I own a little foxconn box with an atom 330 running ubuntu 11.10. It has 2 gigs of RAM and relatively weak integrated intel graphics. It handles Unity, and KDE 4.8 with standard effects like a champ. For fun, I tested some of the 3D games like open arena and it works great. I have a GMA booster that helps it play higher def videos, but otherwise, it handles most day to day tasks with ease.

The fact that the Meerkat has the nVidia GPU and a much newer atom chip than my box suggests to me it will make for a very slick experience for the casual user. I think it would be plenty for your mother-in-law.
