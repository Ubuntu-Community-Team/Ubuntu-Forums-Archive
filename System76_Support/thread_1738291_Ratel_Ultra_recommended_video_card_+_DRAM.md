### Post by isantop on 2011-04-27
You'll definitely get the fastest RAM performance with two identical modules, since the RAM in the Ratel is Dual Channel. If you currently have 2 GB (2x1 GB), then I would go to 4 GB (2x2 GB) to get the best bang for your buck.
