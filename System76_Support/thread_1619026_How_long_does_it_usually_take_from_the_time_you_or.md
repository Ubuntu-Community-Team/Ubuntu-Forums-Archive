### Post by isantop on 2010-11-11
Our typical turnaround time is 5-7 business days from order placement to delivery. 8 days is a maximum, so you'll probably get it sooner than that.

The reason for the extended manufacturing time is because each system is custom built for each user's specification; we don't have a shelf of pre-made systems to draw on.

All of our systems are built in the US. We have server manufacturing facilities in many states, we build desktops here in Denver, and the laptops ship out of LA.
