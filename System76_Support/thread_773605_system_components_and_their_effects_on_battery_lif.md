### Post by farruinn on 2008-04-29
> **meanburrito920 said:**
> I was looking at the System76 laptops and was wondering- how much does adding a graphics card take off of battery life in general? And how much more battery life does a 9 cell battery produce than a 6 cell?

I only have one System76 laptop, a Serval Performance, so I can't provide a comparison but I can share my experience. It comes standard with an nVidia card and 9 cell battery. I'm getting roughly 4 hours of battery life which is better than their advertised 3 hours. This may mean that I'm in a lucky minority, but hopefully it might answer your question.

I don't know a whole lot about energy consumption of different components, but I would think the biggest drain would be from the 15.4" screen which is *very* bright or the DVD drive if you have a disc in.
