### Post by formol on 2008-10-27
I just got my Pangolin one week ago.  I recommend it you without hesitation.

On their website, the cover of the Pangolin is gray and the one I received got a black cover.  And I certainly don't complain about that, that's perfect.

The AC adapter size is standard, but it get less hot than the Toshiba I previously had.

I had little (big) concern with the solidity of the case, but I was surprise, it's very solid, good conception.

I don't have picture yet.

On this review [http://www.marcbelanger.org/gibbon3/]("http://www.marcbelanger.org/gibbon3/"), Marc Bélanger said that after one year, the "hinge" (the mechanical part between the laptop and the monitor) is still as solid as it was when he got the computer one year ago.

The customer service is, until now, very good.  Send a email to Tom and he'll answer you in 15 minutes.
