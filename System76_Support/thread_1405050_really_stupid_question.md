### Post by vttay03 on 2010-02-12
I always just double click the top of the window area (where there's a title indicating what application is open).  I am running UNR 9.10.  In UNR 9.04 I recall I could resize windows by holding down the alt-key and the left mouse button to drag the window from being fully maximized.  However, I'd try the double-click method first to see if that works for you.
