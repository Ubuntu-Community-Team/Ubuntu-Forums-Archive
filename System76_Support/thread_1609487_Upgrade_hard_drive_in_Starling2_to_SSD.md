### Post by PabloH on 2010-10-30
You will gain a noticeable improvement with a good SSD drive. Is it worth the money? I would say... maybe. Good SSD drives are pretty expensive, but getting cheaper all the time. You will likely have less storage space, but the I/O will be much faster..

If most of your computer usage involves a web browser and a text editor or word processor it will help quite a bit. If you are playing CPU intensive games and programs, not so much.

The SSD drive should be more resistant to impacts.
