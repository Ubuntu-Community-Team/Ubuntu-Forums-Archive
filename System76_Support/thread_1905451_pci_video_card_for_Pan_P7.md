### Post by isantop on 2012-01-16
No, the video card in the Pangolin is built into the motherboard. There are no half-mini pci video cards that would serve as an upgrade you your video card.

In general, laptops aren't as upgradeable as desktop because so much of the basic hardware has to be built into the motherboard or other components.
