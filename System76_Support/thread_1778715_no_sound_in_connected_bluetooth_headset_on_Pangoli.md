### Post by ldt on 2011-06-09
I'm trying for the first time to listen to sound with a bluetooth headset with my Pangoli Performance pan6. I had no problem with discovery and connection to the headset but when I play sound, a YouTube video for example, I only hear sound from the speakers. No sound at all in the headset. The options from the icon are connect/disconnect only. It shows connected. Any suggestions?

---

### Post by isantop on 2011-06-09
After you've got the headphones connected and paired, click on the volume icon in the panel, then click on Sound Preferences. On the Output tab, see if you can select the headphones as the output device.

### Post by isantop on 2011-06-10
On the hardware tab, what do you have selected for the Profile box? You'll need to click on the headset first, then check the profile field.

---

### Post by ldt on 2011-06-11
OK, the only change I made was switching the profile from telephony duplex to HiFi playback in the hardware tab. But works now. Thanks a million for your help.
