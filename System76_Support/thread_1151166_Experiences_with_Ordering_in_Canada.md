### Post by serval1 on 2009-05-06
I got a Serval Professional about a month ago. The transaction went quite well, there was an issue processing my Canadian credit card, the US address validation would not work (obvously) and that lead to some initial difficulty. This was easily taken care of by the folks at System 76, however.

The UPS delivery went well, it took about four real days (there was an Easter holiday in there, so calendar time was about six days). I had to pay GST and PST of course, as well as a broker fee. Total was about a 20% surcharge (tax and fees).

I could have purchased a less expensive computer, but not one of this calibre. The hardest part of the whole purchase was the waiting for the machine to arrive. The anticipation was high. The computer worked very well, right from when I took it out of the box. Overall a good purchase experience, if not instant gratification.

By the way, the delivery address was in Ontario as well.

### Post by Brian_Newbie on 2009-05-07
I ordered a panp4n Pangolin laptop from system76 and paid for it with my TD Canada Trust Visa card and had no problems with the payment at all. I live near Kingston, Ontario. 

It took just over a week before the system76 site said my order was being shipped. Once it read "shipping" I plugged in the UPS tracking number at ups.com and discovered the first delivery attempt had already been made that very day, went down to the front lobby of my building and saw the ups tag on the front door. They attempted a 2nd delivery a few hours later that same day when I was at work and so I took delivery the following day when I was home. UPS gives you 3 delivery attempts so if you don't think you can be home for the 3rd delivery, it's best to pick up your package at your local UPS store to be safe.

I paid $146 to the UPS guy to cover the gouge and screw tax (GST). I glanced down the bill and did not note any customs/duty charges. I'll take a look at it again but I believe that only the GST was charged and payable upon delivery. 

Just make sure that you have enough to cover the GST with smaller bills and change so that you're able to give the UPS driver the exact amount if possible. 

Everything went well without any problems. I spend a lot more time now on my laptop from System76 than my iMac desktop I had purchased a year ago from Future Shop. I love the open source philosophy and am only sad about not discovering Linux sooner.
