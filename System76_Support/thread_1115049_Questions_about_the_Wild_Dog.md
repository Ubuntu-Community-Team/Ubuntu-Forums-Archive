### Post by jjacobs2 on 2009-04-03
The free ATI drivers are still under heavy development with support from ATI but they are mostly functional.  Currently the binary FGLRX has much better performance compared to the free driver and is much more likely to have functional hardware video acceleration.  Last I checked ATI's drivers were both inferior to the nvidia ones but that certainly is changing fast and they may be caught up this year.
