### Post by jml on 2011-07-04
I just received my New Pangolin and thought I would share my first impressions.

Shipping:  The laptop was delivered by UPS, my wife signed for it.  When I examined the packaging, there was a 2 inch hole in both the outer and inner box.  Luckily the laptop did not get damaged.

Construction:  Typical of the other S76 laptops I have purchased, this unit is solidly built.  Good keyboard feel with no flex.  Solid hinges, beautiful display.

Hardware:  Intel Core i7 - 2630QM
           4 GB RAM
           750 GB 7200 RPM HD

Setup:  No problems.

Updates:  No problems.  Including adding the Medibuntu repositories.

Performance:  This system is fast.
              Power on to login prompt:  51 seconds
              Login Prompt to desktop:  22 seconds.
              I do not run CPU intensive apps.  So YMMV.

User interface:  The jury is out on this one.  It is very Mac like.  I may 
                 get used to it.  If not, I can always add Gnome.


Final verdict:  This laptop does everything I need it to do.  A good buy.

JML
