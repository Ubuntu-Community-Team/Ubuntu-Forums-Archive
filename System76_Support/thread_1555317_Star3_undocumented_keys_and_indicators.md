### Post by isantop on 2010-08-18
The BIOS Access key is F2. And, in my opinion, the fact that it flashes by so fast is a feature, not a bug! ;)

The four front indicator lights are, in order from left to right: Power, battery, wireless, and HDD access. 

AS for the erroneous hotkey. The keyboards are manufactured for a bunch of computers, and some of those include bluetooth. We decided it was not in the scope of the Starling price to include bluetooth, but the keyboards are manufactured to include every possibility.
