### Post by ccrs8 on 2013-02-06
As Mint is based on Ubuntu (unless you go with Mint Debian Edition), it will almost definitely work just fine.  The only thing you'll want to check is to see what the System76 Driver adds to the system you decide to get.  I don't believe the System76 driver is meant to work with Mint, so whatever it addresses on the system you decide to get, you'll have to get to work yourself in Mint.  I have two System76 machines, and on one the System76 driver doesn't add anything, and on the other it adds support for the keyboard controls for screen brightness.  I could no doubt figure out how to get screen brightness keyboard controls to work myself, but I let the System76 driver do it for me.
