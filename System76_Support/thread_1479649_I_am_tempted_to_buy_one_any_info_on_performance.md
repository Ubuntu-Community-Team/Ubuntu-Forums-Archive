### Post by jml on 2010-05-10
Since you did not specify which System 76 computer you were interested in, I will comment on my experiences with the company in general.  I am not an employee of System 76, just a very satisfied customer.  Over the years I have purchased three laptops from this company, a first and third generation Darter and a first generation Starling.  I do not have any experience with the ATI cards you reference. 

 The three computers are very well built.  Pretty much all hardware and special function keys work out of the box.  All three computers continue to run well on Ubuntu.  I have been very satisfied with their performance and reliability.

Support from the company was superb both before and after my purchases.  Any question I could not find an answer to on this forum was quickly answered via e-mail by either Thomas or Carl, (moderators on this forum and employees of System 76.)

The issue of price is a relative one in my opinion.  How much is after purchase support worth to you?  While you may be able to buy a similarly configured computer from another company with Windows installed, generally, support for Linux is very poor or non-existent.  If you compare System 76's prices to other companies that sell and support Linux on computers (Emperor Linux comes to mind,) System 76's prices are very competitive.

If there is a specific model of computer that you are interested in, you might want to search this forum with the model name as one of your search terms.  You will probably turn up some useful information.  I hope that this information is of help to you.

Joe
