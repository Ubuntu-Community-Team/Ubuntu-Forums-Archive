### Post by Noah0504 on 2010-02-21
Okay, so I installed Windows 7 on my PanP6 (I know, I know... blasphemy!  I just wanted to play a few games, I swear!).  The only two drivers that are not installed by default are the nVidia drivers, which are easy enough to get a hold of.  However, I still had two devices missing in the device manager.  After using Google to look for the hardware IDs, I found out what was missing and thought I would share it with anyone else who keeps Windows as a dual boot.

The drivers missing were for the media-card reader.  It's the JMB38X MS SD SD/MMC xD Card Reader Driver and you can get it on HP's website here: [http://h10025.www1.hp.com/ewfrf/wc/softwareDownloadIndex?softwareitem=ob-62275-1&lc=en&dlc=en&cc=us&product=3773703&os=2093&lang=en](http://h10025.www1.hp.com/ewfrf/wc/softwareDownloadIndex?softwareitem=ob-62275-1&lc=en&dlc=en&cc=us&product=3773703&os=2093&lang=en)
