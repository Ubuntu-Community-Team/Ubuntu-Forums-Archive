### Post by jml on 2007-10-13
Not the Daru2, But I have had success with the Daru1 (white).  On that laptop Kanotix 2007 RC1 runs everything I want out of the box.  Accelerated graphics, wireless, memory card reader, optical drive, and it recognizes both of the Core2 Duo processors.

I have also gotten pure Debian Etch running as well.  Same level of success.  Except wireless.  I installed Etch while connected to the net with a wired connection.  Once everything was installed, I got wireless running from searching the Debian Users Forum.

I don't know how much the Daru2's hardware varies from the Daru1 so YMMV.

As to what I like about Debian over Ubuntu, on my computer it seems to run a bit faster than Ubuntu does.  Second, since I value stability over cutting edge applications, I simply prefer pure Debian Etch, there are fewer updates (usually just security updates.)  No flames please, Ubuntu is a great distro, and the people supporting it are top notch, but Debian just suits my tastes a bit better.
