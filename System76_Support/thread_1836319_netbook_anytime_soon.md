### Post by jml on 2011-09-03
If System 76 does not supply a new netbook.  I can recommend the products ACER sells.  Both their 9 inch and their 11 inch netbooks are reasonably well made (not as solid as System 76's but adequate) and will run many flavors of Linux.  I have run both #!CrunchBang Linux, Linux Mint, Linux Mint Debian edition, Straight Debian Squeeze (with a little work to get wireless working,) and several recent versions of Ubuntu. But, if you are seriously thinking about getting a netbook with a 9 inch screen, act fast.  I think most manufacturers are moving way from the smaller screen size.
