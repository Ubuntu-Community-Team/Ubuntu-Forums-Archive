### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-06-26
Yes, the ac adapters for that model are pretty generic. You can pick one up at a local retailer. Just match the specs on the back of your adapter to the one at the store. The amperage will be the most important spec.

And the universal adapters have a number of jack attachments. One will surely work.
