### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-11-04
The Carbon Fiber print lid is the standard (and only) lid for the Gazelle.

Our machines are tested with 64-bit. Unless you have a specific reason for needing it, 32-bit probably isn't necessary. We don't test with 32-bit or offer 32-bit-specific drivers, but the System76 Driver (which applies) model-specific patches to our systems will run on  32-bit. As far as I know, all of the fixes work in 32-bit.
