### Post by isantop on 2010-06-25
The open-source ATI driver that comes with Ubuntu performs very well with the HD 4570 in it in pretty much every field except for 3D. We do ship it with the proprietary ATI driver, but that can be removed upon arrival.

Other than that, every thing works out of the box with Ubuntu. I'm not sure about Debian, but I imagine you should also be okay there. We don't test with other distros an Unices, but you can always install them if you want.

### Post by isantop on 2010-06-25
The 3D performance is pretty bad, but all of the 2D stuff is fine. And, the OSS driver is undergoing pretty serious, active development. So it will get better with time.

You are correct. All other drivers are OSS. However, the Fingerprint Reader had a regression late in Karmic that caused it to stop working at all. we haven't figured it out yet, but we are working on it.

Battery in the Pangolin is a 4400 mAh battery. If you could find a 4800 mAh battery, you may get a little extra time out of it. It needs to be the same size at 10.8 volts.
