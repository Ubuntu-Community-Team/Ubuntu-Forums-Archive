### Post by jeffran on 2010-10-18
Hi Carl, when I had similar issues to this (different machine, a pan4 and 10.04), it turned out to be a corrupt CD-ROM. Try downloading 10.10 again, and check the MD5 sum, and do the same when you burn the CD-ROM (or whatever medium you're installing from). All MD5 sums should match what's printed on ubuntu's site. I hope this helps. - Jeff
