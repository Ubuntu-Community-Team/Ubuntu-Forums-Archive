### Post by linuxhippy on 2011-11-23
2 days ago I took the plunge and upgraded my system76 star1 starling netbook to an Intel Ultimate N 5300 wi-fi card which supports 802.11a/b/g/d and has a driver automatically detected in Ubuntu 11.04.  I followed this upgrade guide for a couple hours after buying the card for ~ $40:

[http://www.vrekk.us/2010/08/taking-apart-system-76-star1.html](http://www.vrekk.us/2010/08/taking-apart-system-76-star1.html)

The card works fine with encrypted WPA g networks.  I don't have an N router to test with it, but I saw no error messages in dmesg.
