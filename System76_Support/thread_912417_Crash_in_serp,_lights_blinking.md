### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-09-08
Before doing a hard shutdown, you might want to try pressing Alt+SysRq and, while holding both down, press these keys one at a time: R S E I U B.

If it works, there will be less chance of hosing your filesystem.

If the problem occurs again, reboot and go to System > Admin > System76 Driver > Support > Create and send the log archive it creates in your home directory to support(at)system76(dot)com. I'll have a look at it and try to figure out what happened.
