### Post by HotShotDJ on 2010-11-24
> **tc101 said:**
> There was some problem with Cheese though.Cheese has been problematic recently.  Try GUVCView.  On both my PanP5 and my friend's Star1, I am able to capture the AV stream without a problem while Cheese stutters when it works at all.

Keep in mind that on the starling's smaller screen, you may not see the buttons across the bottom of GUVCView.  Use Alt+mouse button 1 to drag the window up off the top of the screen and then stretch the window open at the bottom until the buttons appear.

GUVCView is NOT as intuitive as Cheese.  So be patient.
