### Post by drewbenn on 2011-11-22
System76 does have a mailing list.  I think you'll need to create an account at system76.com to sign up for it.  From one of their emails:

> To unsubscribe from our newsletter either reply to this email with "Unsubscribe" in the subject line or login to your account @ system76.com. Click My Account on the top right of the screen and click Subscribe or unsubscribe from newsletters at the bottom of the page. 

I haven't kept all the emails I've received from that list, but it is very low-volume (less than one per month, I'd say): I don't recall receiving any emails that weren't related to new product announcements.

They do also maintain a Facebook page, and Twitter and identi.ca accounts (username on all 3 is 'system76').  The Twitter and identi.ca accounts are also very low volume, and roughly half product announcements.  Of course you can turn any Twitter feed into an RSS feed, e.g. [http://api.twitter.com/1/statuses/user_timeline.rss?screen_name=system76]("http://api.twitter.com/1/statuses/user_timeline.rss?screen_name=system76") (I don't know how official that use of Twitter's API is; it could go away without warning).

I'm not aware of any RSS feed direct from System76; a google search for "rss site:system76.com" returned zero results, so I suspect they don't have one.
