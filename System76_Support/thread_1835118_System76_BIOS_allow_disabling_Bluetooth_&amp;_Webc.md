### Post by nrundy on 2011-08-28
If I buy a System76 Lemur Ultrathin, will I be able to disable Bluetooth and the Webcam via the BIOS?

---

### Post by isantop on 2011-08-30
They can't be enabled in the BIOS, but you can press Fn+F10 (for the camera) and Fn+F12 (For the bluetooth) to toggle power to their respective devices. This function is tied to the BIOS, and can't be overridden from the OS.
