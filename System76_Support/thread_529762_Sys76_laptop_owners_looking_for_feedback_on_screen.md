### Post by nicedream on 2007-09-04
This is the original poster here, checking in with my new Gazelle Value.

I am happy to report that the screen is very nice and bright.  I am completely satisfied with it.

Some other initial impressions:

1. the top of the lid is covered with a black System76 sticker, which was peeling off on the edges.  I took the whole thing off and there was a nice grooved surface underneath.  I think it looks better now.

2. the keyboard is a bit too springy for me, but I supposed that's minor.

3. Its a little warmer than Dells I have used in the past, but not nearly as bad as the HP DV2000T that I had to send back because of excessive heat.  The temp is bearable.

4. I was a bit surprised to boot it up and find it in 1024x768 resolution.  It was simple enough for me to install the 915resolution package, but a newbie probably wouldn't know what to do.  I don't know why this wouldn't be preconfigured by sys76.

5. The touchpad is nice and accurate (Synaptics all the way)
