### Post by mhedges48 on 2009-07-26
I recently purchased a System 76 Serval Professional Laptop:
64 Bit, 500GB hard drive, 4GB RAM, dual Intel 2.80GHz processor, Jaunty, about $2100. 

I am very happy with it and so far would recommend it. I have been using Thinkpads for ten years and worried I wouldn't find a replacement as sturdy and dependable. However, while at the beginning figuring out how to make Ubuntu run on a ThinkPad was a joy, it had gotten tiresome. The ThinkPad fan speeds were out of whack (and using the Ubuntu programs to manually adjust fan worried me) and I was unable to upgrade to Jaunty with my existing ThinkPad b/c Compiz was no longer supported on the ATI graphics card. So I decided to try System 76 which comes with latest Ubuntu presinstalled, working right out of box (all you have to do is your username/password and time zone).

PROS:
Ubuntu out of the box no worries about compatibility issues and saved me a ton of time in setup.
 
Slick, fast, quiet laptop at a good price.

Very good customer support.

CONS:
Poor battery life, and no option to buy an extended longer life battery.

Needs a trackball.

Laptop and AC cord are a little heftier than comparable Thinkpad.



I just received the computer, so if I have any problems as time goes by I will post them to this thread. But as of right now I would definately recommend it, [www.system76.com](www.system76.com)
