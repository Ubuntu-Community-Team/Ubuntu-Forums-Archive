### Post by thomasaaron on 2010-04-29
The Sony's T4200 has a 2GHz clock speed and only 1MB of L2 Cache.

The Lemur's SU7300 has a 1.6GHz clock speed, but 3MB of L2 Cache.

In other words, the T4200 takes little bitty bites of data and processes them a little bit quicker. The SU7200 takes three times bigger bites and processes it a little bit slower.

The Lemur is also more energy efficient, so it's probably quieter and cooler as well.

I've never played with a Sony, but my guess is that, if the Sony is faster, it won't be by much. Most likely, the Lemur will be as fast, if not slightly faster.
