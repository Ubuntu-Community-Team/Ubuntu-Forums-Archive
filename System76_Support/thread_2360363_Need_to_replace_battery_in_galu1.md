### Post by april-system76 on 2017-05-05
Hi Alex,

That's strange! Were you calling +1-720-226-9269? That's our main support/CS line.

If you create a ticket on your System76 account, we can quote you for a battery replacement and provide instructions for you. As fallenshadow said, just remove the bottom cover. The battery is held in with 3 screws and 1 plug. Remove and replace, then reassemble and you'll be all set!
