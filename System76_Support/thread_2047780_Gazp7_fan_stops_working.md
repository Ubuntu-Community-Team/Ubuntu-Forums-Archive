### Post by Carborundum on 2012-08-26
I recognize the symptoms you're describing, right down to having to shut down completely in order to reset it. I've been in contact with System76 support about it, and according to them the computer would overheat within seconds if the fan really wasn't spinning.

Try to get it to 80°C. As long as the fan starts spinning rapidly then, you run no risk of actually overheating.
