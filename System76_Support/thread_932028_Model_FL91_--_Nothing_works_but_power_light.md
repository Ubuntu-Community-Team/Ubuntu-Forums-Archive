### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-09-29
First, remove the battery and find a different ac adapter to test with. This will verify it's not the adapter. 

If it is the adapter contact me at support. If your still under warranty (which I think you probably are) we can replace it. If not, you can buy one at a local retailer.

If it is not the ac adapter, we can try resetting the cmos. You can contact me at support(at)system76(dot)com for instructions.

Also, you may want to try re-seating your hard drive and memory cards. Make sure the battery and ac adapter are removed before doing this. Contact me at support for more detailed instructions.
