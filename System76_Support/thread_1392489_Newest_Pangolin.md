### Post by revlimiter on 2010-01-28
I've got a panp6. Not the newest hotness panp7 with the i5/i7, but it DOES have the same chassis. The keyboard is outstanding. The keys press very nicely and typing is easy. 

The keypad on the side is great. I've not used it in numlk mode yet, but the placement of the standard-mode buttons is great. PgUp/Dn are wonderful. And having TWO delete buttons? So so sweet. 

The trackpad has pips all over it. Gives an interesting tactile sensation. It's not a smooth pad, but rather has some feeling. Hard to describe, but I really like it. Kinda like the Asus EEE books with the bumpy pad, but about 3x as many. 

The screen is bright and the hinges are wonderful. The whole thing feels very strongly built. It's a wonderful chassis. 

Sorry to not have any panp7-specific stuff to add...
