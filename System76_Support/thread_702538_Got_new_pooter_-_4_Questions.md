### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-02-20
> Wonder what I'm supposed to do to "register" it? Anything in particular? 

There is no registration. Just keep track of your order number. That is what we need to process warranty issues.

> Any tricks I should know about in transferring from a user name (puppydog) on the old machine to another user name (pup) on the new machine... or is this not an issue?

Shouldn't be an issue. Just drag and drop your files onto a USB flash drive. Then put the drive into the other machine and drag and drop them off into your home directory. Or wherever you want them.

> 
I wonder about protecting the screen.... it looks vulnerable to scratches. Any suggestions ?

[http://www.ergoindemand.com/laptop-protection-computer-filters.htm](http://www.ergoindemand.com/laptop-protection-computer-filters.htm)

> the touch pad seems waaaaaaaaaaaaaaay sensitive. Probably needs to be toned down. Where do I go for that?

The only adjustment currently available is: System > Preferences > Mouse.
