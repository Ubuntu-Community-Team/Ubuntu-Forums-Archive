### Post by rubbsdecvik on 2009-08-13
Just to make sure you know this. Atom processors do NOT have hardware virtualization support. This means that you're VMware would run, but take a serious performance hit compared to other modern systems. That being said, if you're not doing a whole lot, you should be able to do something. I've virtualized stuff on a similarly clocked processor and it works. It's just not near native speeds like hardware supported Virtualization.
