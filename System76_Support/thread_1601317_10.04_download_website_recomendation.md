### Post by zitch on 2010-10-20
It's actually trickier than it probably should be to find; you have to seek out the download mirror pages.  Here's a URL that will choose one for you:  [http://www.ubuntu.com/start-download](http://www.ubuntu.com/start-download)

Choose the version you want (10.04.1 in this case), and you should see the netbook images available.  I'd guess you want to choose the i386 iso file (or torrent if you want to get it that way), but I might be wrong.

On a side note, I'm not sure if the Netbook editions are part of the LTS release cycle, but I can't find where I thought I had read that.
