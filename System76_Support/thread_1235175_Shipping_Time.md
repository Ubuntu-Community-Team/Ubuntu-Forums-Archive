### Post by Eldera on 2009-08-09
Although I am not connected to System 76 except as a happy customer, from reading these forums I know that order turn around depends on several factors. One is whether the components for your customization are in the factory. Another is the number of other orders for that particular model which are being processed.

If you contact Tom Aaron Monday at [email]support@system76.com[/email] or 888-468-6482 ext603 and tell him the configuration of the Gazelle you want, he can give you a better answer than any of us posters.

Most of us have had orders shipped within a few days, but there have been occasional exceptions when someone has wanted an unusual configuration,
