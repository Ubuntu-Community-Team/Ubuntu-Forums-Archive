### Post by jml on 2011-09-22
I am not sure why you do not have the "upgrade available" message on update manager, but to be frank, I find it preferable to backup my data, then do a clean install with the version of Ubuntu you want.  Then go to the system 76 web site and download the drivers for your particular Starling. After that run the S76 application and you should be good to go.
