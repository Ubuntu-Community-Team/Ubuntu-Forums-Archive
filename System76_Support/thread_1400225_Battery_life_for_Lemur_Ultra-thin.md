### Post by jml on 2010-02-08
Those netbooks and laptops that offer extended life batteries often require larger battery packages.  In the Acer Aspire One, for example, the long life battery extends beyond the case, altering it's profile.  Unfortunately, the Lemur does not come with one of those batteries.  If you want/need 8 hours off the mains with a Lemur, the only option you have is to buy a second battery pack at $95.00 US.
