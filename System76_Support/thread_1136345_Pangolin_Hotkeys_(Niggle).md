### Post by Cygnia on 2009-04-24
Greetings, on my Pangolin received a few weeks ago there are three built-in hotkeys on the upper-left side of the keyboard that are supposed to open the email client, open the web browser, and engage silent mode, respectively. Well the middle one does not open Firefox, it instead opens the home folder window. I know it's a stupid niggle, but if there's any free time out there would someone know how to reconfigure it, if possible? In reality, I like having one of the keys opening the home folder, and could do without the one for silent mode, but it would nevertheless be cool to have one open Firefox.

I've already seen fit to go into the Prefs and configure a shortcut for Firefox, so this post is simply a matter of curiosity, nothing serious. Thanks for reading.

---

### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-04-25
Go to System > Prefs > Keyboard Shortcuts.
Scroll down to "Launch Web Browser." Highlight the right side of that row.
Press your button.

That should do it.
