### Post by aranaea on 2011-05-22
Since I had just done the installation I decided to try a clean install, just in case I had something corrupted.  I installed 10.10, then the system76 drivers before anything else, even the nVidia drivers.  It went smoothly and returned normally.  Then I went a head with all the other updates and software installs.  Everything seems back to normal.
