### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-09-04
The Starling does not have an 800x600 mode. It does have 1024x576, 640x480, 720x400, 640x400 and 640x350.

Most likely, if you just plug in the Starling and activate the monitor via Preferences > Display, you will see the Starling's native 1024x576 resolution as 800x600 on the projector.

There's no way I can verify that the two items are compatible, as I have no way to get my hands on one for testing. My best estimate is that they are. And there's a money back guarantee.

### Post by Georgesl on 2009-09-07
I've set up a 2102 and it will work when fed 1024x768 from an XP system, but it converts that to 800x600 for display which is a bit fuzzier than the real thing.

If the projector is new, you might want to exchange for the 2104 which has 1024x768 native.
