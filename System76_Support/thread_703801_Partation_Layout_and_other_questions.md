### Post by osx424242 on 2008-02-21
The plug-icon button on the left will enable power to the USB ports when the computer is turned off.  Plug in your mp3 player or camera or whatever, press that button, and your device will start charging!  The button will also light up to show you it's working.  Pretty handy from time to time.
