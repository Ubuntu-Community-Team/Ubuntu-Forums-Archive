### Post by isantop on 2010-08-23
We do generally have a rolling release cycle. Generally, we don't update everything at once. However, this has been a bit of an usual few months. We released the Starling, then about a week later, released the new Lemur, which, in my opinion, doesn't sound like a rolling release.

A more apt answer would be that we release new products as new technology becomes available. We just got a suitable option for a ULV i3, so we updated the Lemur. 

As I said in the other thread, our unofficial ETA on the Pangolin is October or November. typically, the Bonobo has shared the Pangolin package with a larger screen, so I'd say to expect it not long after that.

If you don't mind holding out until Maverick, I'd wait for the PanP8 as well. If you can't, I'd get a new one now.

I hope this help everyone out!
