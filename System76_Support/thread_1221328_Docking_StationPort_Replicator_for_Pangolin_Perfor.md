### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-07-24
The Pangolin doesn't have a docking station port. You pretty much have to hook your monitor up to the VGA port, and plug a keyboard and mouse in via USB.

### Post by jbelmonte on 2009-07-24

[Cables To Go 28652 USB 2.0 Laptop Docking Station with Video ]("http://www.amazon.com/Cables-Go-28652-Docking-Station/dp/B000RMVIE8/ref=sr_1_3?ie=UTF8&s=electronics&qid=1248453170&sr=8-3")

This or something like it may be what you need.
