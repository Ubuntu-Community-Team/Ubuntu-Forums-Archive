### Post by shochatd on 2014-10-31
I have a Lemur Ultra, which I assume is different hardware, but perhaps my solution will still be applicable. I took a look at the existing battery and wrote down all the numbers. Using this information, I found a suitable replacement online, which is still working fine after a little over a year. I also peeled back a label to see what the original manufacturer and model number were (obviously, the mods made by System76 would not alter the battery requirements). The battery I bought was made by AGPtec and came from Amazon affiliate Mambate USA.
