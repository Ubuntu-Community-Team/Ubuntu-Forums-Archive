### Post by jjacobs2 on 2009-08-08
A desktop processor rated at 65 Watt TDP doesn't actually use 65 watts all the time.  In fact TDP doesn't even measure energy usage at all, it measures the amount of heat that needs to be dissipated.  The processors will downclock while idling to use less power.   A core 2 duo uses around 5 watts while idling and around 40-50 watts while running at full power.  The GPU is usually far more energy guzzling in a desktop even in an idle state.  My entire desktop with a core 2 duo and nvidia 9400 gpu only uses around 80-100 watts while idling and that's with the older c2d before they shrunk to 45nm.
