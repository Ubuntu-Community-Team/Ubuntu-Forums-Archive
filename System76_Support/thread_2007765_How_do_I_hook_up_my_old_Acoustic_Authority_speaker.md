### Post by isantop on 2012-06-22
> **alanjackson said:**
> Tried every available setting

My guess is that it's the way the speakers are wired. That is, the subwoofer is wired to it's own channel. Usually, 2.1 speakers (especially those with a single plug) will have the sub wired to both channels, and it picks up the bass more than the treble due to it naturally having a lower frequency response. It may also use a simple High Pass filter to route audio to the sub specifically. I would consult with the documentation that came with the speakers.
