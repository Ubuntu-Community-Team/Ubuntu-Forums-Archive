### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-06-28
There's been some BIOS updates with System 76 computers lately.

However, I'd strongly recommend contacting me at support[at]system76[dot]com
if you have a reason to believe you need a BIOS update.

If done improperly, they can utterly hose your computer and reduce your brain to a permanently vegetative state. ;)

Also, if you are not experiencing some problem that is likely to be BIOS related, there is always the potential of messing up things that are already working fine.

In other words, just because a BIOS update exists, does not mean that everyone needs it. Some of them are designed around Windows functionality, for instance.

Best,
Tom
