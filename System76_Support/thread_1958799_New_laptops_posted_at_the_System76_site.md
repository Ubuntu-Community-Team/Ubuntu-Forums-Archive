### Post by isantop on 2012-04-17
Definitely. The beats have a lot of extra support software running in the background that monitor for bugs and crashes that final releases won't have. This uses resources and reduces battery life. Once the final version of 12.04 is released, we'll have a much more accurate sense of the battery life.

I should point out that it's looking like it will be much longer than Oneiric, so you're looking at at least 3-4 hours.

[*]CPU: i5 2nd Gen
