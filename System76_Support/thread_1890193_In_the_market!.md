### Post by jml on 2011-12-03
You are right.  with $2,500.00 budget, you pretty well can buy any of System 76's laptops. As for recommendation, that will rest on your needs.  Any of the laptops will do the tasks that you listed.  If you plan on getting into serious photo editing, you may want to focus on the Gazelle or the Serval to capitalize on their nVidia graphics cards.  I would also opt for increased RAM and a larger than standard hard drive.

If battery life is important to you, the Lemur is said to get between 3 and 4 hours of battery life per charge. The Pangolin between 2 and 3.  The Gazelle and Serval between 1.5 - 2.5 hours.

Or, you can really go wild and buy a base Lemur for portable use, a Gazelle or Serval for desktop use.  :-)

Over the years I have purchased 4 laptops from System 76 and I was completely satisfied with all of them.  Hope that this helps.

Joe
