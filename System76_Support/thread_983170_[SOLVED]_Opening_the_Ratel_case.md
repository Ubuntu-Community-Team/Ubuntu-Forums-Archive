### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-11-17
There are no hidden screws. Just the ones on the back. If you have a NEW Ratel, after you remove the screws, the TOP panel comes off first (by sliding it backwards). Then the side panels slide UP.

On older Ratels, The side panels simply slide backwards.

Shoot me an email at support with your name/order number, and I'll check which Ratel you have.

---

### Post by eadwacer on 2008-11-18
SOLVED. Yes, it was that simple -- on this model the side panels slide back individually, not as a unit. Since they hadn't been moved since I got it, they just required a little firmness. Thanks.
