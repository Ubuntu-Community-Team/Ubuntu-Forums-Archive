### Post by halibaitor on 2011-06-27
> **isantop said:**
> Actually, it looks the same from the bottom as it does with the standard battery installed.

This is fairly common.  Both batteries will use the same case.   The "regular" one comes with a bunch of empty space.  The extended life battery will just fill the empty space with another set of cells.  If there was yet another, larger battery available, then the battery case would stick out a ways from the computer.

Not all instances work this way however...
