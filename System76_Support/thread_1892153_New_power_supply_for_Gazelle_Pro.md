### Post by isantop on 2011-12-07
One thing I noticed about the second two that I didn't see on the first one is that they aren't grounded. We always recommend a grounded power supply for safety and to prevent damage to the laptop, which would not be covered under your warranty.

Other than that, then any 120 Watt power supply will work, assuming it operates at 19 Volts (i.e. a 19 Volt, 6.3 Amp supply is what you're looking for).
