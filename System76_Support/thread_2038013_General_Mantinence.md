You seem to be pretty close to having taken care of it all already. Keeping food away is the big one. It will get into {i]EVERY[/i] crevase, crack, and cranny on the system (damn food!) and really mess with the appearance (and possibly operation! D= ). 

As for the screen and bezel, a duster to remove the dust from the screen, or a slightly damp cloth. Be sure the computer is powered off if you use a damp cloth, and give it a half-hour or so to dry before turning it back on. A dry cloth (extra points for microfiber) will take the fingerprints right off, though take care not to hit the rubber screen bumpers. Those will pull every speck of lint off of the cloth and leave them on the bumpers. Trust me; that's a pain to get off.
