That's a very strange issue. Can you try the following procedure to reset the configuration for your wireless networks:

[LIST=1]
[*]Click on the networking icon in the top right, then click on "Edit connections".
[*]Click on the Wireless tab.
[*]Click on the first connection in the list on the right, then click "Delete...".
[*]Repeat this for each network listed.
[/LIST]

Once you've done this, reboot the computer and attempt to connect again.
