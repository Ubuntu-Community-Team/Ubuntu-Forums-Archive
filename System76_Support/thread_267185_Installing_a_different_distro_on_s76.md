### Post by crichell on 2006-09-28
We support Edgy on all our our systems (as well as future releases).  Most everything works OOTB - we make modifications for sound and microphones on some models but with our driver it's easy to install them.  We also have some drivers in development for cameras and other advanced devices we're intereted in.  They're being developed under the GPL license so they will be available to everyone once released.

### Post by crichell on 2006-09-28
You'll receive a restore disc that takes the system back to it's original state.  Edgy driver updates won't be available until we get closer to Edgy. Feel free to put Edgy on there or any other distro - you can always use the disc to restore.  The only caveat is that we only officially support Ubuntu so I can't gaurantee results with any other distro.
