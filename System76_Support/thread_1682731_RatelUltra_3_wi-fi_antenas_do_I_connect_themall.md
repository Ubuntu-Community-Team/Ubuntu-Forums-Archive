### Post by jml on 2011-02-07
Jeff, There are three antenas due to the fact that your card is covering both b/g and n protocols.  Many 802.11n routers have two antenas.  The third is for both b and g.  Since a wireless card is strictly speaking a transceiver, (radio transmitter and reciever,) running it without one of the antenas connected my damage the transmitter portion of the card.
