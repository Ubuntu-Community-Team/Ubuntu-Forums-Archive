### Post by bonestonne on 2009-10-23
if you let your system run down completely, it doesn't matter what charge state the battery is in, just let to run down to zero.

then recharge the battery completely. Not 24 hours, just as long as it takes to recharge the battery.

leaving a battery plugged in 100% of the time is very bad, but constantly letting it run down to 0% is also bad. it puts an unnecessary amount of load on the cells. they don't last forever.
