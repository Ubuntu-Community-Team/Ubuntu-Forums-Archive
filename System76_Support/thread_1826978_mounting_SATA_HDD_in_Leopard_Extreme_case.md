### Post by isantop on 2011-08-18
You'll want to get four HDD mounting screws and rubber bumpers. Then, thread the screws through the bumpers and screw them onto the first and last holes on the sides of the drive (you can ignore the middle holes). With all of those in, unscrew the screw on the cage, slide the rail to open the cage up, then slide the drive into the cage, making sure the slots in the cage slide onto the bumpers. Then slide the rail up and tighten the screw down.
