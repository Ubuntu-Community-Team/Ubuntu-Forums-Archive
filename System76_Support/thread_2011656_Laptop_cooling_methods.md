### Post by joe4ska on 2012-06-30
Install [Jupiter Applet]("http://www.jupiterapplet.org/") and set it to power on demand or power savings. When set to power savings it does reduce processor speed by 1/2 and in turn runs about 10 degrees centigrade cooler.
