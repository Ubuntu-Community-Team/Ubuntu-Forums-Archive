### Post by Ubun2to on 2012-07-27
Hibernate will, according to many users on these forums, be "hit or miss." Sad, right? I hate not being able to put my laptop into hibernate so I can swap the battery for a new one. But, considering I have a 21.53 second boot time (thanks, Intel SSD), it's not too bad (it was originally created when boot times were around 5-10 minutes). However, suspend works perfectly
I'm not sure about battery for the PanP9, as I have a LemU4, but my laptop gets around 2.5 hours with WiFi off on a single charge. With WiFi on, about 2 hours. I got an extra battery, so I can go on short flights without having to worry about running out of juice. Your numbers will differ from mine, as I have a smaller screen. In any case, battery life should increase with the new kernel releases.
