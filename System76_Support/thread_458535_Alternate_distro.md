### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-05-30
The System 76 Official Line:

Of course, what you do with your computer is up to you. But we only officially support Ubuntu. That translates into: Your hardware would still be covered under warranty, but we have very limited ability to help you with other distributions or derivatives of Ubuntu, as we do no testing at all with them.

The System 76 driver will run on Kubuntu or Xubuntu. However, it is not designed to patch holes created by other desktop environments. In other words, we cannot vouch for any OS modifications working out-of-the-box on our computers.
