### Post by gdgilda on 2011-10-16
Hello,

FYI, I was seeing freezes when using libreoffice calc on my panp8 running 11.04.  The following thread describes what I was seeing:
ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1813246

The workaround in 11.04 was to turn off anti-aliasing support in libreoffice.  I have seen no system hangs since.  11.10 is supposed to have a new driver and I'm hopeful that will allow me to turn anti-aliasing support back on.
