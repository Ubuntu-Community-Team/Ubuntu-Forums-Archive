### Post by Carborundum on 2012-08-12
> **Nardon said:**
> 
- I live in The Netherlands so how is the shipping outside of the US? What are the shipping costs and did it arrive in good condition?

I recently bought a Gazelle Professional and had it shipped to Sweden. The shipping cost ended up at $132. Keep in mind that you will also have to pay VAT, which is calculated as:
VAT = VAT RATE x (COMPUTER PRICE + SHIPPING COST)

For example, in my case I payed $1233 to System76 for the computer, $132 to UPS for shipping, and 0.25 x (1233 + 132) =  $341 to the Swedish state in taxes, for a grand total of $1706. I believe the VAT rate in the Netherlands is 19%, as opposed to 25% in Sweden.

I placed the order on June 18 and received it on June 28, which is very fast indeed considering the machine had to be assembled before being shipped. UPS delivered it to my door in perfect condition.


> 
- How is the gaming performance on this laptop? I like to play a game once in a while, are the latest games at least playable on an Intel HD 4000? Of course it doesn't have to play them on highest settings.
For the most part it's pretty good. Some examples of games that run well are Oil Rush, Amnesia, Super Meat Boy, Bastion and The Journey Down. The only game I have encountered so far that doesn't achieve an acceptable frame-rate is Trine 2, but I think that is mostly due to poor optimization on the part of the developers. There are also a few games that have issues with the open source Intel driver and don't work at all. The only one I can think of right now is Shank, but there might be more.

> 
- For some small tasks I am still locked to using Windows. So I am probably going to make a small Windows partition for that. Are there also any Windows drivers available for the Gazelle Professional?
 [Yes.]("http://www.knowledge76.com/index.php/GazP7#Windows_Drivers")
