### Post by marshmallow1304 on 2009-12-27
Have a look at the [Welcome Manual]("http://knowledge76.com/images/Star1_welcome_front.pdf").  Most of what you want is on that.

In answer to your questions, Fn+F3 is the LCD/VGA switch for external monitors/displays and Fn+F6 is mute.  On the right side, you've got jacks for audio in and out, an SD card reader, and a slot for a Kensington lock.  The toggle switch is to switch between wireless and 3G, for the Starling has a slot for some sort of wireless connectivity card (SIM card?, other 3G card?), but such cards are not well supported in Ubuntu.  As for the lights, my best guess/recollection would be one for power, one for battery, one for hard drive activity, and one for maybe wireless card status or caps lock status.
