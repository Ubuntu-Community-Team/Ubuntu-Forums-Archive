### Post by crichell on 2007-01-06
Yes, the Gazelle Performance card reader is supported.  With the addition of the camera driver the only un-supported device is the dial up modem.

> Also, there is an option to get an extra 9 cell battery with the Gazelle Performance. Will the cd/dvd drive come out so that this extra battery can be placed in it? How much battery life can I expect to get from an extra 9 cell? Finally, how much more weight does it add?

The 9 cell battery goes in the same slot as the 6 cell battery.  Simply remove the 6 and insert the 9.  You should get about 4.5 hours out of the 9 cell.  It doesn't add much weight - I don't have a precious number on it.

> Do both hibernate and suspend work with the Gazelle Performance?

Not currently but Suspend/Hibernate are our top priority.

> If I bought a 1year warranty, can I upgrade it within that year?

Yes you can.

### Post by vjones777 on 2007-01-09
I bought a Gazelle value from them a couple of months ago.  So far my experience of their support has been that it is excellent.  When I was looking at buying my laptop Carl patiently, professionally and knowledgably answered all my questions and was up front about any limitations on the machines and work in development.
They do develop and provide their own drivers to ensure that all of the stuff is working.  My experience has been that when they promise drivers they do come up with the goods.  The only negative I had was when I first bought I was promised a 'factory restore' DVD to re-install Ubuntu and all the drivers within a couple of weeks.  It turned out to take about 4weeks IIRC since they changed tack and instead developed a driver to be used with a standard Ubuntu CD.
However, when it became available they sent a CD overnight - quite impressive as it was about 4pm on the Friday of the thanksgiving weekend.

One nice thing about System76 is that they add Linux onto bare systems - so you're not paying the microsoft tax.  Many other companies including Emperor strip Windoze off so you end up lining Mr Gate's pockets for the privilage of NOT running his software!  [-(

FYI Suspend to disk works on the value.  Bottom line is I think you can trust them to deliver the driver within a reasonable period, assuming they dont run into an insurmountable technical problem.
