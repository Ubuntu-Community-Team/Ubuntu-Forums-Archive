### Post by isantop on 2012-08-28
> **zenlunatic said:**
> I assume since all laptops have HDMI, they support 1900x1080 out on hdmi.  What resolutions do the 3 laptops support on HDMI and VGA?

All the laptops will do up to 2650x1600 on the HDMI. VGA is analog; there is theoretically no maximum resolution. However, you'll see image quality start to degrade above 1280x1024.
