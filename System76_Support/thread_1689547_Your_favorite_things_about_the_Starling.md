### Post by gamerchick02 on 2011-02-17
I'll bite.  (For the record, I have a Starling 1.)

Pros:

- small size
- pretty fast
- runs Ubuntu really well
- decent screen resolution
- great keyboard
- decent battery life
- great touchpad
- solid build

Cons:

- flaky wireless (on the Star1; I think this has been fixed with the newest machines)
- battery issues (happened last June for me; I'm using the battery less and the power cord more when I'm not traveling)

~~~~~~~

Enjoy your new netbook and I love to read people's reviews of them!

Amy

### Post by jml on 2011-02-19
I also had a STAR1.  Bought it when it was first offered.  I second everything that AMY listed as pros.  Luckily, I never experienced the battery issue, but I did have to deal with the poor wireless performance.  I would also like to add another very big pro, the excellent aftermarket customer support System 76 offers.  When I was dealing with the initial wireless problems, System 76 did a great job keeping me and their other customers in the loop until they were able to correct the wireless problem.  I have purchased three computers from this company and I am planning to buy number 4 soon.
