### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2016-08-10
IF I have actual issue(s) with my Sys76 Galago laptop or with my Sys76 Sable AIO desktop, I place a trouble-ticket with System76 on their website.   Response for me has been excellent (within 24 hours).    All Sys76 systems have lifetime OS/admin support.    Re instructions for update - - it's on their website also (they have a simple wiki).     The other thing you want to be sure of, is to add their Official Sys76 Drivers PPA.     Instructions again on their website (as well as other places).    Be sure to ask SYS76 via phone if you're confused or don't know how to add a PPA.
