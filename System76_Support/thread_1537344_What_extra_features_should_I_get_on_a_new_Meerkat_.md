### Post by isantop on 2010-07-23
Unless you plan on using **many** applications at once, you shouldn't need more than two GB of RAM. If you plan on doing one or two tasks at a time, you should be fine with two.

The external USB optical drives are external, which may not fit your form factor requirements. Other than that, nothing.

Since Ubuntu's new splash screen system automatically sets the resolution, you should be fine with your current monitor.

The HDMI port will play both Audio and Video. You'll need a supported TV. Usually, HDTV's have support for HDMI.

The Logitech Wave is more comfortable and Ergonomic. It also has some additional hotkey features that the MK300 doesn't have.
