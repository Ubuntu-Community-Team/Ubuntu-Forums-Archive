### Post by jjacobs2 on 2010-01-27
Both of those GPUs offer h264 hardware decoding but the drivers do not yet support it for the intel in the lemur and support was only recently announced for the ATI so I don't know if it works yet.  From what I've read Intel wants to have decoding working in their chips by Q2 but there's no guarantee it'll ever happen.  I doubt either CPU could decode 1080p on it's own but 720p works fine.    Intel support for decoding would probably be open source if they  add it while ATI's version requires the use of their proprietary fglrx driver.
