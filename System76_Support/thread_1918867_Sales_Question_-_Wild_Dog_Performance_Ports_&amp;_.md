I'm considering a Wild Dog Performance very soon.  Some questions:

### Post by isantop on 2012-02-02
The Wild Dog will support an addition SATA drive or two, but it doesn't have any IDE ports. You wouldn't be able to carry the DVD writer over.

As for DVI, any card from the GTX 550 Ti and up will have two DVI outputs.
