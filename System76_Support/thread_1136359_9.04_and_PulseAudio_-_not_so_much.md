I use this command to reset PulseAudio when it is acting borged

sudo killall -9 pulseaudio; pulseaudio >/dev/null 2>&1 &

I found this command [here at CommandLinFu.com]("http://www.commandlinefu.com/commands/view/1890/fix-ubuntus-broken-sound-server")
