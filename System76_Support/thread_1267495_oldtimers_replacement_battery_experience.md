### Post by jml on 2009-09-16
While I have a Darter rather than a PanP, I did buy a new battery about 6 months after I got my Darter.  Not because the original one died, but to have a second larger battery available when traveling.  I ordered the second battery from S76 and it works well.  It fits my Darter perfectly, and it seemed to be manufactured by the same company that built the original one as it looked identical except for size.

The one thing that I do not know is how long after a laprop is discontinued will S76 carry the replacement battery.  Hopefully Tom will answer that question for you.
