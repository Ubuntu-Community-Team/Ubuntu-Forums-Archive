### Post by Vadi on 2008-11-04
Thomas, I think I got it. Between Cheese freezing, you need to go to preferences and set the resolution to 800x600. It works fine then.

Setting it to one resolution higher makes the video slow and colors weird, and setting it to max completely kills Cheese. Unfortunately the default is max.

Let me know if this works for you too, probably should file a report about it...
