### Post by isantop on 2013-01-30
> **jcllings said:**
> Well, another few updates and now the behaviour seems to have changed. I can close the lid which shuts off the screen but if I hit the escape key a few times, it comes back and everything is fine.

This is the normal behavior. When they say "Do nothing", they mean "Do nothing with regard to power management". You should be able to wake up the screen by jiggling the mouse or hitting a key on the keyboard.
