### Post by thraxy on 2008-12-31
The safest thing to use for displays is rubbing alcohol. It evaporates really fast, so it doesn't do any damage. Get a microfibre cloth and make it moist with rubbing alcohol. That'll make your screen nice and clean. Also good for cleaning keyboards.
