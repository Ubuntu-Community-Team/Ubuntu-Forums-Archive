### Post by isantop on 2012-01-26
If you're going to be using it outside and with direct sunlight, glossy is definitely the way to go. 

Matte and Glossy screens both have glare. The difference lies in how they deal with the glare. Matte screens diffuse the glare over the surface of the screen, meaning the glare will affect the entire screen (or most of it) evenly. Glossy, on the other hand, does not diffuse it at all, and thus, the surface will simply allow light to pass straight through. 

Matte screens will wash out in direct sunlight because the light can't reflect clearly.
