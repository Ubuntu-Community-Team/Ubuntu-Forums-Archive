### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-04-26
> I saw a video showing some fan openings on the bottom (is this true?) - Does this make the lap of the user too hot?

TA: No. That is an air intake, not an exhaust port.

> Would a 35 watt processor cause the system to overheat?

TA: No. 

> Does the Serval have a metal frame, or is it just painted metal?

Hard plastic and inlaid brushed steel panels.

```
Is the battery life of the beauty 3 hours because it has an 8-cell battery? This somewhat concerns me, for I have a 6-cell laptop currently and it did 3-4 hours when I got it (if I remember correctly).
```

It lasts about an hour. We provide a tutorial that can extend that somewhat, but the graphics card in it uses a lot of energy.

> A question regarding Serval vs. Pangolin (or just computers in general): is DDR3 that much better than DDR2?


TA: The biggest differences show up during intense usage. So, yes. But somebody who is just going to use it for browsing the web, checking email, and creating word processing documents wouldn't notice as much as someone doing 3-D gaming, video editing, etc...
