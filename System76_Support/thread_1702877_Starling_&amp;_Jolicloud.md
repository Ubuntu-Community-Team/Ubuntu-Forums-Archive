### Post by isantop on 2011-03-08
Jolicloud is an interesting concept. We don't test with it, and I've never used it personally, so I can't give you the best recommendation. That said, it appears to be an Ubuntu-based OS, and I don't expect you'd have too many problems getting it to run on a Starling, especially given that it is listed on their supported page. 

In the end, that's probably the best thing about Free Software; it is free, and you can try out just about anything you like without really losing anything (barring data and format problems, etc. of course).
