### Post by isantop on 2013-08-26
You 'll need to ensure that you're logged in to Ubuntu One before attempting the restore. You can check this by looking for the "Ubuntu One" folder in your home folder. In 13.04 or later, you'll also get a cloud icon in the upper panel (without a "pause" icon in it).

then you should be able to restore from Ubuntu One.
