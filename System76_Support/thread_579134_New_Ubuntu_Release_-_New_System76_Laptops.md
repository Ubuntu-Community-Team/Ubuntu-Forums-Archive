### Post by crichell on 2007-10-18
Gutsy Gibbon brings us the latest drivers, desktops effects, desktop search, and latest and greatest open source software.  Along with Gutsy System76 are releasing a new Serval Performance and some cool new options for the Gazelle Value and Pangolin Value.

The Serval Performance sports a Piano Black Polished finish, 15.4" high resolution LCD, and nVidia 512 MB GeForce 8600GT.

The Gazelle Value now has the option of Intel X3100 graphics or 256 MB dedicated nVidia GeForce 8600GT graphics and a 2 Mega Pixel Built-In Webcam.  The brushed steel on white looks awesome.

The Pangolin Value also has the option of Intel X3100 graphics or 256 MB dedicated nVidia graphics and a 2 Mega Pixel Webcam.  Again, I love the brushed steel on these.
