### Post by eg-maverick on 2007-01-24
How do I enable my System 76 laptop to boot from CD? I have a known good bootable CD. When I put it in the CD drive, my System 76 does not boot from it. It boots from the Hard drive. 
Thanks,
Craig

---

### Post by Pobega on 2007-01-24
When you see the screen that says "*Intel*" on the bottom right corner, hit the escape key two or three times. You should be prompted with a menu containing a few choices; Choose the CD drive from there, and the computer should boot into your CD.
