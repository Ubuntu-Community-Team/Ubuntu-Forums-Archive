### Post by Vrekk on 2010-08-27
Wow. I was brought up on a thread that I didn't start! That puts me in a good mood! If you guys what me to, I can post a full how to with pictures tomorrow. Its not to to hard to disassemble compaired to other laptops and computers I have taken apart. To answer the question of how to take off the keyboard (AT YOUR OWN RISK!) take a close look right about your f8, halfway between your f1 and 2, and your ins key. You will see little tabs that you can press down on and CAREFULLY get under and pry out.
