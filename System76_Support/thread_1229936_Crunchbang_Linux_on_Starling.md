### Post by jml on 2009-08-03
Amy, since #!CrunchBang is a live distro, you should be able to test it before you commit to an install.  If you have a USB optical drive, just download and burn a copy to an optical disc, boot it up and see how well it supports the Starling's hardware.  Pay particular attention to the wireless functionality as the wireless card is not yet well supported in Linux.  In fact, Tom from S76 said that they had to use a driver from the backports repository to get it to work at all.  As for CrunchBang's compatibility with the S76 driver, that may be a sticking point, as S76 does not officially support any distro other than Ubuntu.  They do not even support Kbuntu, nor Xbuntu.  Good luck.  Oh, and by the way, I like #!CrunchBAng as well.  Its a very nice distro.

Joe
