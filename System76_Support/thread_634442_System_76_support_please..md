### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-12-07
In Firefox, go to Tools > Add-Ons

In the resulting window, click the "Get Extensions" link at the bottom of the window.

This will pull up a web page. On the left column of the page, click the "Plugins" link.

Scroll down to Adobe Flash Player and click the Install Now button.

Download the tar.gz file to your Desktop, double-click it and extract it to your Desktop.

Go to a terminal and enter these commands:
cd Desktop/install_flash_player_9_linux/
./flashplayer-installer

Just follow the installers instructions and you should be good to go.
