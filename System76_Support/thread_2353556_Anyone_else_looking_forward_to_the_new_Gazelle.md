### Post by april-system76 on 2017-03-15
True, the forum is aimed at community-focused support efforts. Both the Gazelle and Kudu have been pushed back a little bit to mid-April... :'(

@samalex, the virtualization issue on the Lemur 7 was caused by a BIOS update intended to fix another issue related to VT-d, the full issue is here: [https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2351455](https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2351455)

Lastly, VT-x is enabled by default on all of our hardware out of the box, with VT-d being an option in the BIOS. No issues with virtualization here!
