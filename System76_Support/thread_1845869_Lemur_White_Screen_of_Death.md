Instructions
[LIST]

[/LIST]

### Post by Conzar on 2012-05-03
The keyboard is mounted on a tray.  The tray is affixed to the laptop via screws (accessed from the bottom of the laptop) and tabs.  You need to remove the screws and slide the tray off the laptop (the tabs need to be removed from the slots - be-careful not to break them).
