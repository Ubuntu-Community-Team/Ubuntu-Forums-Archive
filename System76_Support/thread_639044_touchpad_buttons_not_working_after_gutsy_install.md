### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-12-13
Hi, Underdog. 

You are posting a lot of threads with various problems, which makes sense (to post different problems in different threads). However, I'm starting to form a picture of what is going on with your computer. Touchpad buttons not working, firefox freezing, etc...

It sounds like you are having a firmware problem with your cd drive. This has been known to happen on the older darters and gazelle values. It causes intermittent freezing of open applications, mouse, etc...

The System 76 driver helped in Feisty. But in Gutsy, that fix no longer works. The best fix now is flashing.

Please email me at support(at)system76(dot)com and let's work on getting your drive flashed.
