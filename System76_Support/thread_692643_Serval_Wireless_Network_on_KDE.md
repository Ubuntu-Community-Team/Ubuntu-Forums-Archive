### Post by imhavoc on 2008-02-10
Here's what I've done under KDE:

- installed Wireless Assistant (and added a link to the kicker bar)
- installed wifi-radar (and added a link to the kicker bar)

(I typically use Wireless Assistant for my wireless needs, but Wifi-Radar seems to do some things better when I'm looking for other networks away from home and office.)

Wireless Assistant can be configured to automatically reconnect to authorized networks.

I also have added Wireless Network Information applet to the taskbar.
