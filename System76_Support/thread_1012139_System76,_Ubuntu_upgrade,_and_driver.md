### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-12-15
By the time Ubuntu officially releases a new version (i.e. not alpha or beta, but the final), we have completed testing it on our machines. If anything is broken, we put the fixes in our System76 driver, which you will need to run after upgrading or doing a fresh install.

The System76 driver can be downloaded from our repositories.
