### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-10-22
> Not that I would want to do it, but can these laptops run Windows?

Yes, they all run windows just fine as is.
Windows drivers are included for those customers who decide to dual boot or
virtualize.

> Will they work with Kubuntu or Edubuntu (or other Linux distros)?

(K/Ed)ubuntu: The driver will run on these, but we do not test with or support them.
Other distros: Yes. We occasionally get reports back from customers using other distros successfully. Again, we only test with Ubuntu. So there might be problems with other distros that you may need to figure out on your own. We will be happy to supply you with whatever info we have.

> How is the "real world" battery life for the Gazelle?
Depends on how you're using it.
I'd say 3 - 3.5 hours is pretty reasonable.

> What is the typical boot time?
I just timed it at 1 minute 15 seconds. This included a 6-second grub menu and me typing in my password.

> Is the touchpad too sensitive?
Usually when people ask questions like that (or questions pertaining to heat or noise) it means that these issues are particularly irksome to them. But they are not irksome to me. All I can say here is that I don't have a problem with any of it, but I'll have to default to any of our customers who wish to answer.
