### Post by moody_mark on 2013-05-15
Hi, generally if you are playing games then the CPU will work harder and need more cooling. If your fan is noisy the it may well be worth popping of the cover to take a look. I have a Dell E6400 laptop and every year or so the exhaust will get clogged up with dust and i'll have to clean it out. If the fan is coming on more often this is usually a sign of the machine needing a bit of a clean out.

For temperature monitoring in Ubuntu have a look at this article: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SensorInstallHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SensorInstallHowto)
