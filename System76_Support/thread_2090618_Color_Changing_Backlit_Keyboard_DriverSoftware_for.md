### Post by isantop on 2012-12-04
It's pretty simple, actually. The color change key (Fn+ ) is tied into the BIOS, rather than the OS. The other benefit to this is that it will work regardless of what OS you have installed, and even if the OS isn't even running yet (i.e. in the BIOS).

I know I've mentioned this before, but this is an excellent example of why our systems are unique, and aren't really compatibile with *any* other manufacturer's systems. :)
