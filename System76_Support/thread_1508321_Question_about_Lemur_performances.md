### Post by Nchewbacca on 2010-06-13
I think that since running games in Wine is more RAM intensive than running it normally, you'd encounter some choppy playing and maybe some lag. If you have the spare money, a Bonobo or Pangolin would be perfect for the job. I've done some research on the subject, and it seems like the processor and RAM on the Lemur just aren't enough to get the job done well. You could certainly still play it, but your fps might be low. Not that I've tried it personally on the Lemur- it just seems like with its specs, you would encounter sub-par performance.
