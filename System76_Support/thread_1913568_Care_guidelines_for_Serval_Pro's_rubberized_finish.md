### Post by Tristan Schmelcher on 2012-01-22

### Post by isantop on 2012-01-23
I wouldn't use any corrosive solvents, and I would probably steer clear of any solutions in general just to be safe. Personally, I would remove the battery (always a good idea when water is involved), then gently clean the computer with a slightly damp cloth. After letting it dry, if anything is left, then a lint roller should remove any more debris.
