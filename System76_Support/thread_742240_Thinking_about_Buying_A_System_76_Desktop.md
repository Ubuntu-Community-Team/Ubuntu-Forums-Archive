Setting up a secure Linux (Ubuntu) desktop computer is just about as easy as placing an order for a System76 computer.... no, not "just about," it is that easy. Is the OS different than Mac OS? Well, yeah. It is. There's the very real chance that you might not like it, but I'd say it's a small chance.
