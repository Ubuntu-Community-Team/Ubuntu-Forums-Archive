### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-07-07
In your volume control panel (the one you get when you double-click your speaker icon), make sure Headphones is checked. 

If that checkbox is not visible, click the Preferences button and make sure all checkboxes are selected.

If that doesn't fix the problem, try your headpones (and following the above directions) from a live Jaunty CD.
