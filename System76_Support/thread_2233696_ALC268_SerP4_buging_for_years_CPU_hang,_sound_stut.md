### Post by HandeH on 2014-07-10
Technology changes quickly, threads are being closed, ... this bug have been with us for years, about half a decade! 
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/alsa-driver/+bug/882566](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/alsa-driver/+bug/882566)
This regression (CPU hang bug) happened somewhere between Ubuntu 8.04.4 and 10.04 (introduced also in Kubuntu, at least 14.10). 

Workarounds to make these laptops usable: 
1) disable AHCI in BIOS (to prevent hang during booting) 
2) buy an external USB sound card. 

Any hope for a fix?

Realtek ALC268 SerP3 SerP4 Compal IF90 JFL92
