There is at least one option that extends below the bottom of the screen, and there may be more.  I can't resize the pane or move it further up or scroll the pane to reach the other options.

Is there somewhere else I can find those settings?  I looked around in gconf-editor, but the best I came up with is /desktop/gnome/fileviews, which doesn't have all the preferences from the File Browser preference pane.

### Post by StephenDavison on 2011-02-16
Holding down the Alt key might allow you to drag the window by grabbing anywhere in it (not just on the window borders).  At least you can move it around to reveal the hidden portion of the window.
