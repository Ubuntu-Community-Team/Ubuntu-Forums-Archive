### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-09-12
Well, the graphics chip in the Daru1 can go quite high. The limiting factor is the LCD, which only supports up to 1280x800.

If you use an external monitor with a higher resolution, you can reach the higher resolutions you mentioned.
