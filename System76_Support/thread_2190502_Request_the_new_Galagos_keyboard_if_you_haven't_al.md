### Post by jbuncher on 2013-12-01
+1 to "Order the replacement keyboard immediately"

Replaced mine the other day (I believe I had the 2nd-Gen keyboard).  The 3rd gen is amazing by comparison.  It is absolutely worth it to get this replacement.  Keypresses that "don't show up" are down to zero, and as a result the typing is much more comfortable than before (since I don't have to make sure that my fingers hit dead center on the keys, I can type in a much more relaxed manner).  Typing speed is also up as well, due to the increased "tolerance" of where you hit the keys.  

Replacement itself was a breeze, though I only had to remove one screw from the bottom of the laptop (the other screw-hole looks like it is covered with some type of plastic fliling).  

After the keyboard issue with the Galago Pro, I was unlikely to buy from System76 again.  With this replacement, I'll be sure to check out any new models that they release (though this one should last me for quite some time I hope)!
