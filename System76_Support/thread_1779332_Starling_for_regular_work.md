### Post by isantop on 2011-06-10
A lot of people seem to have pretty good luck using Debian on our machines. Definitely not Slackware, but it's a start.

As for an 11 inch model, we don't really have any plans to put one out. For people who like the smaller screen, we find the Starling does very well. If you need a system with a larger screen, the Lemur is probably the best bet.
