### Post by andrewdied on 2008-09-07
I bought a serval performance a few weeks ago, and have liked it quite a bit.  A handful of hardware suggestions for System 76:
[LIST]
[*]Eraser.  I've always preferred the eraser / nubbin / pointer device over the touchpad.  Only a few laptops in the linux realm have these, so this would be a good differentiator.
[*]Middle mouse button.  Lots of applications like having a middle mouse button.  The laptop is big enough to support it well.
[*]Volume scroll wheel.  A toshiba I owned had a scroll wheen for sound on the left-hand side.  It naver broke, and was much handier than using the Fn combo to raise and lower volume.
[/LIST]

I knew that the laptop didn't have these when I bought it, so I'm not griping.  I do think it'd help System 76 sales if you could sell a laptop that had these, so that's why I've brought it up.
