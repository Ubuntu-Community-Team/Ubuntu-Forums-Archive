### Post by tjagoda on 2011-04-13
I own a Starling, and I have thus far been very pleased with it.  I purchased mine with the SSD option and the 6-cell battery.

My only complaint is something which seems to hold true through all System76 devices - the battery life is a bit short when compared to some of the competition.  BUT, that is no reason not to buy this great little netbook, as you can still get plenty of hours of use out of casual use before it needs recharging.

### Post by isantop on 2011-04-13
Building on this, many of the points that he brought up are true, but we have changed some things:

We're using a different wireless card in the Starlings now than when we shipped the Star1's, and they now work much better. 

We did go from a Synaptics touchpad on the Star1 to a Syntellic touchpad on the Star3 and up. They are a bit different, but they still work just fine. Instead of scrolling along the edge, you touch the upper and lower right-hand corners, and it scrolls as long as you hold your finger down. It works very well for smaller systems like the Starling.
