### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-02-04
Just a couple of quick thoughts.

1. The "moving the koala from one room to the other" idea sounds a bit cumbersome. If I could afford it, I'd go with the two-box option.

2. While the wild dog is pretty quiet, it IS a workstation. It may be slightly distracting to have it in your living room while you're watching a movie.

3. You need to verify that the graphics cards in the Wild Dog and the Koala will support the resolutions of whichever monitors you decide to hook up with them. You might run into trouble with the Koala and a 24" monitor. You can email me at support@...... and I can help you research that.

Best,
