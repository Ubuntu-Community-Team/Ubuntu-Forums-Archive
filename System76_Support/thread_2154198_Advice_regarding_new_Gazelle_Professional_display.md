### Post by 3Miro on 2013-06-13
I got the mate on my Gazelle, it was definitely worth the money. Under ideal situation glossy can look better, but mate is not as effected by glare and you will almost always get some glare. You can use a mate screen at lower brightness setting and save battery life too. The better screen on Gazelle also gives a wider color range.

Thank again "looks" are subjective.
