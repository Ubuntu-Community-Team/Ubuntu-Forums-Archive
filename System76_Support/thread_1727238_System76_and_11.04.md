### Post by isantop on 2011-04-12
The driver typically isn't used to fix stability issues. The crashes you're getting are likely due to the fact that throughout Beta, there are lots of bugs that need fixing, and you're probably hitting some of those.

All of our development on the Driver is done internally. At this point the only thing that changes from version to version, and the platform that applies these changes are quite stable. That said, if you notice anything wrong in Natty on either system, do let us know. That we, if we can replicate it on our systems in the shop, we can get a fix out in the driver.
