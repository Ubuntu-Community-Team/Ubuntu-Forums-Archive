### Post by isantop on 2011-12-07
Essentially, you'll want to choose which one you like the best; you can opt for the Serval if you want the premium finish rather than the plain, matte polycarbonate.

One thing I do want to point out is that you may want to look at the High-color screens. They offer 95% NTSC output, which makes the colors look more more accurate. We can also get color calibration profiles for them as well. Due to all this, it would be an excellent screen for doing graphics work, and it's good either way.
