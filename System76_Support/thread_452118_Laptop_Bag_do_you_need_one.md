### Post by atoponce on 2007-05-23
Backpacks have laptop spaces in them now, which is pretty cool.  However, I usually carry a lot of books, so the pressure of the book weight pushing the screen against the keys has left a keyboard impression on many a laptop screen that I have seen, so I would recommend against that.

I would recommend getting a separate bag for your laptop to sling over your shoulder.  Depending on how much you want to carry in it, would determine the size.  Because you'll be hiking all over campus, I would recommend against a large bag that promotes stuffing a lot of crap in it.

### Post by kevinlyfellow on 2007-05-23
I'd be worried about a laptop getting knocked around with your books in backpack.  I do suggest a laptop bag.

Since your going to school, you may want a bigger bag than the one that Sys76 offers.  It can carry two college textbooks at best, but one is more reasonable (it'd be tough if its an introductory calc book).  The price is good, and the quality is ok (I had a minor issue with the joints for the shoulder strap).  

I'd suggest a larger third party bag for your situation unless you don't have the need to carry around too many books.
