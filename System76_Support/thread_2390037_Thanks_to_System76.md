### Post by rican-linux on 2018-04-24
I had my Gazelle for over a year. One of my biggest issues with was the fan. It would run high at odd times. The support team was great in getting me a replacement fan but that only helped a little. Then recently noticed that the System76 driver had a firmware update ready for me. This was the first time I see one. I followed the instructions and after the update I noticed that my fan problem has stopped. Now the fan run high only when I am doing really intensive work.The rest of the time this machine runs fairly quiet. It is like I have a new machine! So I just want to say thanks to the team for their hard work!
