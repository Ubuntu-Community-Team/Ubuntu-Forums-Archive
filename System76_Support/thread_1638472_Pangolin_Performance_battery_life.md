### Post by isantop on 2010-12-06
The Pangolin should last around 1.5 hours on one charge. If you need a system that lasts longer, I'd recommend the Lemur, which can get about four to five hours per charge on the extended battery, or six to seven if you swap the extended with the default when one goes dry.
