### Post by MarkID on 2007-03-16
OK.  So I ordered a Darter this morning.  The tipping point came when I went into my local Apple store and saw the 13.3" screen on the MacBook.  That size screen with the 1200x800 resolution is quite nice and quite readable.  The comments from jml and Carl on this board also helped.

I consider the purchase of the Darter to be still somewhat of a compromise, as (1) it is still white, and (2) it lacks the camera of the Gazelle Performance.  But the Gazelle is bigger and heavier than the Darter and portability won out in this case.

(In fact, if I were S76, I would discontinue the Gazelle, and find someone to make a Darter with optinal Nvidia and webcam.  The Gazelle is too close to the Darter in the light/ultralight segment and I think confuses the product line.)

BTW, I found this website [www.sfbags.com](www.sfbags.com) that makes sleeves to fit a very wide variety of laptops (well beyond S,M,L), including the Asus z35, which appears to be the basis for the Darter.  They're a bit pricey but looks like they make a good product.

Now, does anyone know of an outfit that can paint a white laptop custom colors? ;)
