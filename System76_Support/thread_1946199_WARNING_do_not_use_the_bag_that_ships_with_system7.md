### Post by sam271828 on 2012-03-24
I was carrying my new gazp6 around Boston yesterday in the carrying bag it shipped with, and all of a sudden the stitching that connects the shoulder strap clip to the bag ripped off entirely, sending my computer crashing to the concrete. Luckily it seems to be ok, but if I had been on a bike (as I am 90% of the time) I doubt things would have ended so well. 

I know I'm not the first one with this problem after reading several other forum posts. I have only positive things to say about System76 computers and support, but including a defective bag is not really ok. I get that it's free, but I strongly think that not including a bag with the new computer would be preferable to including one that breaks after a few weeks.

So, to you prospective buyers, get one of their laptops. They are awesome. But throw that bag in the trash and invest in a decent one.
