### Post by isantop on 2013-06-18
With the caddy drives, there are a couple of factors that make it more expensive than the equivalent option for the primary drives. The prices of all the options for the first hard drive all include the cost of the standard 500 GB hard drive. Since a second hard drive isn't included, this cost isn't built into the price of the caddy drives.

Second, there's the physical caddy itself, which has to be added to the total cost of the secondary drive options.
