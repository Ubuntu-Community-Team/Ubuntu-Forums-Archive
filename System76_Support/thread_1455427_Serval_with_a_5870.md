### Post by edwinbmiller on 2010-04-18
I've priced the Serval out before buying a pengolin from system76 (didn't need the full HD display or high end graphics) and while they are a little higher (on the serval you'll find there markup is about $150 over a identical model from xoticpc or rc computers) I believe there support of ubuntu both from the point of drivers and the forums make the price difference very much worth it.
