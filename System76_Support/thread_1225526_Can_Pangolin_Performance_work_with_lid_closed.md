### Post by jeamer on 2009-07-28
Go to System>Preferences>Power Management. 

Choose "On AC Power" or "On Battery Power" tab, depending on your preference, and set "When laptop lid is closed" to "Do Nothing". 

Revel in your laptop-lid-closedness. 

:D
