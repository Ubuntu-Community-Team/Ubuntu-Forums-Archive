### Post by isantop on 2010-08-19
Picture clarity is often dependent on the monitor. Some of them are clear, some aren't. I have one in shop that always seems to look fuzzy.

At 1920x1080, I don't think that fuzziness will be a problem, though you never know. I  should point out that you may need to use HDMI to connect your monitor in order to get 1920x1080, as I'm not sure VGA will support that resolution.
