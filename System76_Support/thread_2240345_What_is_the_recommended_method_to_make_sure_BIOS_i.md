### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2014-08-19
Usually the BIOS will show the build or version information on the first screen after entering the BIOS. When booting there's typically a key to press to enter the BIOS. Sometimes it's F1, F2, F10, a special key if the laptop has one like "Help" or "ThinkVantage" or whatever other branded key.

### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2014-08-26
Cross reference the version you get from your BIOS with the updates on the manufacturer website that are usually under the download section for the product. Generally speaking people don't mess with the BIOS unless something is very wrong so there's no update checking or automated updates.
