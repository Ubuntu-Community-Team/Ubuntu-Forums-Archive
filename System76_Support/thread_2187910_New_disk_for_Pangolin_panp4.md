### Post by isantop on 2013-11-15
They're okay. Our benchmarking has indicated that while they do have a good burst speed, the sustained read speed is not much better than a traditional hard drive.

I recommend avoiding OCZ drives specifically. We've had nothing but problem reports regarding OCZ drives, and a buddy of mine had his go out after only two years.
