### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-11-04
The components are tightly packed. Just FYI: modifying the components will void your hardware warranty.

Upgrading hardware (RAM/Memory/Hard Drive) will not void your warranty, as long as you are using parts with approved specs.

The CPU is not upgradeable.
