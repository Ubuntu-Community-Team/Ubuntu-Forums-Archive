### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-03-19
If it does it with two different (known-good) dvd's/cd's, there is a chance that your drive is going out. But if that were the case, I wouldn't think that rebooting would fix the issue. 

It could also be the head on your DVD getting dirty. You might blow some canned air into it to clean out any dust.

Are these disks that YOU have burned? If so, did you burn them with that particular drive? Can you reproduce the problem with a commercially-made disk?

The bottom line is to completely eliminate a dirty drive and imperfect cd's as the problem. Once that is done, we can pursue this via warranty.
