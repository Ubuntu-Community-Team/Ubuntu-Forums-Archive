### Post by isantop on 2011-03-02
What's the status of the HDD indicator light? If it's constantly on, then there may be a problem. However, if it's mostly off or occasionally flashing, then that's probably normal.

Also, the thermal issues are due to a bug, but they don't indicate a problem. You should be able to safely disregard it.
