### Post by isantop on 2011-07-22
Actually, you do need to set up a partition on the drive. Go ahead and open up the Disk Utility Application. From there, you should be able to see the physical drive. Now click on it, click on the free space on the drive, and click on "Create partition". You can use the default settings. You can now see the drive in the file manager on the left hand side.
