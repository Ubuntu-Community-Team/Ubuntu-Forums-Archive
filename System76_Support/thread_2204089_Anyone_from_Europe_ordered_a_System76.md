### Post by nico11 on 2014-02-25
I am also from the Netherlands and have bought my Gazelle Professional laptop about a year ago. Also had to deal with import duties etc, but the price I paid in the end was competative with buying a laptop local with the same system setup but with windows installed. Also you need to deal with the hassle of dual booting other OS'ses such as Ubuntu.

 Since then I upgrade to Ubuntu 13.10 and plan to upgrade to 14.04 when it becomes available. I run alpha versions of ubuntu in a VirtualBox as well as Mint and Windows 8. There are some minor issues such as not be able to shutdown other then using the power button. This one came up after upgrading to 13.10. Still haven't found a way around it yet, but it hasn't had any impact on performance.

So in general I am very happy with my laptop and would recommend it to others.

UPDATE: I did a quick check on the pcspecialist site provided by another user  on the laptop specifics of my current system. They are about EUR150 cheaper then having it configured and delivered by system76. OTOH you have to install and configure Ubuntu yourself without the advantage of specific drivers that you may need for your hardware setup. System76 will include these and they have their own repository from which they also supply updates when available.
