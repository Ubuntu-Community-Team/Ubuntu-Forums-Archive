### Post by Noah0504 on 2010-09-30
> **jaa75 said:**
> Thank you. Fn-F1 works.  How did you find that, may I ask?  Thanks again.JAA
Well, I assume our keyboards are identical or at least similar.  If so, just try and make sense of the blue symbols on the functions keys.  FN + F1 will turn off the touchpad, FN + F2 will turn of the LCD until you interact with the computer (mouse click, etc.) and so on.  The other handy one is FN + F11 to turn on Bluetooth.
