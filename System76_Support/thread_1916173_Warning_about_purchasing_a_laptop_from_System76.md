### Post by wi! on 2012-01-27
Hi All,

I just wanted to write about my experience with System76 for anyone considering purchasing one.

I run Ubuntu and decided to give System76 a try mainly because their hardware is tested and supported for Ubuntu and it was always a pain getting everything to work with my previous machines. I went with the Serval Pro totally decked out. I was extremely excited to get it.

Two months after having the machine my cpu and gpu fans increased to max velocity and would stay at this state. After troubleshooting it myself and reaching out to System76's tech support I came to the conclusion that it was a hardware issue and had to be returned to be fixed (my profession is a computer engineer).

The lack of feedback from System76s support is a joke. I shipped my computer back with a prepaid shipping label and monitored the tracking. 3 days after the package was received by System76 I still did not receive an update. When I contacted them about the diagnostic report I was told that they gave me the wrong shipping label and it was shipped to the wrong location however it was now enroute to the correct facility but would take another 6 days! Today I called to get a status as to when I could get an estimated completion date. I spoke with a support representative and he told me it would need an additional 4 days to diagnose and make repairs to the machine and then an addition 5 days to ship it back to me on the east coast! This would put this repair job to about a month total!

As mentioned before I am a computer engineer and having my work computer is obviously essential. I tried to explain my situation to the support representative on how being with out my main work computer for a month is unacceptable. I asked him why after System76 made an error in the shipping that my machine did not have expidited shipping to the second location. He did not have an answer. However he told me that I would not be charged additionally for it having to be rerouted (Hahah seriously?? That's pretty ballsy to say when the mistake was on there end). I also asked him why my machine could not be expedited back to me. He explained that the cost would be to great even though I explained to him that System76 was costing me money everyday that they delayed my machine getting back to me but that didn't seem to matter. I asked to speak with a manager or supervisor and he said there wasn't one. Since my hardware malfunctioned after only 2 months, and there was a delay in my order due to a mistake on there side I wanted confirmation that there was absolutely nothing they could do for me. He responded that there was nothing he/they could do more.

After this experience it is very clear to me that System76 will not take responsibility when they make an error and will not do anything additional to make things right. This will be my last purchase from System76 and I will not recommend them anymore to others. I really wanted to have faith in supporting a company like this but I can't afford to deal with this type of customer support in my line of work.

  **Update**: 
The repair door to door took 23 days and an additional 3 days to process the RMA totaling 26 days.So it ended up taking less time than the original month estimate I got.
