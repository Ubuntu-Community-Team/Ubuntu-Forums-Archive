### Post by drewbenn on 2009-12-05
> **mrchuc said:**
> Sometimes I get a dialog box that has the bottom inaccessible.

I _think_ this is enabled by default: hold down the Alt key (probably the left one), left-click _inside_ the window (not on its title bar), drag the window up, and release the left mouse button and then the alt key.  You should be able to drag the window around like that.  Because you can use this method to move the window by dragging from the middle of the window, instead of from the title bar, you can use this to move a window up above the top of your screen.
