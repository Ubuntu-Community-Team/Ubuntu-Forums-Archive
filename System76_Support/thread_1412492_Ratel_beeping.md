### Post by thomasaaron on 2010-02-22
It's probably nothing. But you might want to test your memory by running memtest86 on it. Here are instructions for doing that...

> Reboot your computer. When you see the grub countdown menu, press the 'Shift' key. When you see the grub menu, press your 'down' arrow until Memtest86 is highlighted. Then press 'Enter' to start testing.

Please start Ubuntu in the evening before going to bed and let it run all night long. Memory card problems can be subtle, and sometimes they will not show up until the cards start heating up. Often it will take several passes before errors appear.
