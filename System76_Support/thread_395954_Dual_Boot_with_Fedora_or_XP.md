### Post by crichell on 2007-03-28
Windows is no problem - We haven't tested Fedora though.  We have a How To for adding Windows to your machine here:

[http://knowledge76.com/index.php/Windows_-_Add_MS_Windows_to_Your_System76_Machine](http://knowledge76.com/index.php/Windows_-_Add_MS_Windows_to_Your_System76_Machine)

> As well, general documents created with the included Open Office should be readable in Microsoft Word/Office?

Yes - just save your document in MS Office format - you can set OpenOffice to always save in MS Office format.

> As well are most general files, such as word processor, music,gif/jpg/ graphics, etc compatible betwee linux platforms such as done in ubuntu, but read in Fedore etc?

Yes - anything you create in Ubuntu can be read in Fedora.  Generally you won't have any problem sharing your work, pics, etc. with Windows or other Linux distribution users.
