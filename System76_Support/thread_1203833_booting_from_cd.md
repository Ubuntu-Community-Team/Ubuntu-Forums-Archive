---

### Post by robtg on 2009-07-03
When booting, enter the BIOS setup (I think by pressing F2) and go to the Boot menu; change the boot order to put the DVD drive ahead of the hard disk.  My Pangolin arrived set-up to boot from the hard disk first; yours is most likely set-up the same way.

-Rob
