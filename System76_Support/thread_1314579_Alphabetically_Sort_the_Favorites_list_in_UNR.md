### Post by Altay_H on 2009-12-02
> **thomasaaron said:**
> But I've got one here with 9.10 on it, and it seems to sort alphabetically by default..
I'm actually using 9.10. For me it definitely sorts by chronological order: the newest favorites display closest to the end. All the other lists (Accessories, Games, Internet, etc.) are sorted alphabetically though.

Also, if you don't mind a *really* hackish solution, you can manually re-order your favorites by changing gconf-editor > apps > netbook-launcher > favorites > favorites-list. Be careful when using gconf-editor though. It's easy to mess things up from it. Here's a link to the hackish solution: [http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/idea/22652/](http://brainstorm.ubuntu.com/idea/22652/)
