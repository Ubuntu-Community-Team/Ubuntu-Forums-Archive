### Post by isantop on 2012-02-23
You should be able to get pretty clear access to the fan by removing the keyboard. 

First, unplug the system and flip it upside down. Remove the battery. Next, near the middle of the underside of the computer is a screw with a keyboard icon. Remove this.

Now, turn the computer back over and open the lid. Along the top edge of the keyboard (closest to the screen) are four small tabs. Using a small, flat screwdriver, slide those tabs up towards the screen. The keyboard will pop up as you do. I do find it easiest to start in the middle and work outwards from there.
