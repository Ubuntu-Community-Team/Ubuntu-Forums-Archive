### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-11-24
The DarU2 keyboard is a little difficult to remove, but doable. However, removing the keyboard doesn't really give you access to the underside of the keys, so doing so is not overly helpful.

Your best first bet would be to get a can of compressed air and blow it all around the keys. This usually works to dislodge particles, etc...

If that doesn't work, and you still want to try to remove the keyboard, let's connect via email (support(at)system76(dot)com) and I'll provide further instructions.
