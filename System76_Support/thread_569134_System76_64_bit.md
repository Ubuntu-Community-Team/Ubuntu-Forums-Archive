### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-10-09
I understand that, but we're trying to sell systems that work out of the box. While you can make them work by chrooting, it's not really the ideal situation. Also, we're not trying to sell systems that do OK with just the basic stuff. Both the beginner and the experienced user should be able to get the most out of the system.

I will say this though, Carl and the R&D team HAVE noticed that there seems to be a growing desire for 64-bit. We're getting more queries about it every day. And they have noticed a couple of pieces of hardware that seem to work under 64-bit BETTER than under 32 bit!

I'm sure it will be an option at some point. But my understanding is that there are still some kinks that need to be worked out so that we can release it in a way that we can really stand behind.
