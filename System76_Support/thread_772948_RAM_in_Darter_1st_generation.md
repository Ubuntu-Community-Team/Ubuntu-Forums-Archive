### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-04-29
I can't find anything about it in the specs we have available.

I know that you mentioned in your email putting 667Mhz RAM in it. The Darter's onboard memory is 555MHz. There is a possibility that running certain memory-intensive softare (WoW comes to mind) could cause problems because of the speed mismatch.

But all-in-all, that's pretty neat.
