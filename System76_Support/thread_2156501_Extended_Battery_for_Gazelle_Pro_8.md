### Post by isantop on 2013-06-24
You would log into your account on our website, then open a support ticket from there. We dropped email support about a year and a half ago.

We don't recommend using batteries ordered from a third party, and if there's any damage or problems with the power system after using one, it wouldn't be covered under warranty.

I've seen cheap batteries destroy smartphones before. I wouldn't put it past it to paperweight a laptop.
