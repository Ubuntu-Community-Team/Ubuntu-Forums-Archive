### Post by Line-X on 2014-03-26
I have a Lemur Ultra (lemu4). While in the midst of any kind of work or browsing a key will seemed to get "stuck", usually resulting in a window being scrolled, though it will manifest as a symbol being repeated in the terminal, and then the computer will suddenly shutdown, whether it is connected to a power source or not. I had only observed this behavior while it was using the battery, so I concluded it must be related to the battery, but the last time it occurred while the power adapter was connected. It doesn't happen often, but when it does it is without warning or time to save work. I have in fact, lost some work due to this. Needless to say, that this unreliability is big issue, even if it is not frequent.
