### Post by dlavi1976 on 2009-11-02
My theory (and its only that )is that the scrolling touchpad is enabled by default in Ubuntu but that the touchpad on the Starling does not support scrolling.  This combination leads to the weird results.  I am not sure if this is the case I just know that I had the same problem and when I turned the touchpad scrolling off the problem went away.
