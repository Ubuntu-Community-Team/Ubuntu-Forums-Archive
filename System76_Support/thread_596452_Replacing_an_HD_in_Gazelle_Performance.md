### Post by sail on 2007-10-29
I've been pondering upgrading my lappy's hard drive for a while. I opened up the covers on the back of my computer and found that there *seemed* to be no easy access to my computer's HD. Will I have to get a professional to replace my drive for me, or is there an easy way to access it and just do it myself?

---

### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-10-29
Nope.

Remove the battery and a/c adapter.
Remove the long panel that runs along the *side* of the computer.
Pull the hard drive straight back (NOT UP).
Remove the metal case that covers the hd.
Put the case on the new hd.
Reverse these instructions to re-install :)
