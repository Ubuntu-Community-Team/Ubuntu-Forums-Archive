### Post by Kareeser on 2009-06-01
HP and Dell are large multi-national cooperations who can afford volume pricing... System76 is a much smaller business :)

Also, the Darter's a 12.1 inch laptop, the laptop you linked to is a 15.4 inch model, which are usually cheaper. It's much harder to cram things into a small space :)

I like to think about it as paying the extra money to support a Linux business.
