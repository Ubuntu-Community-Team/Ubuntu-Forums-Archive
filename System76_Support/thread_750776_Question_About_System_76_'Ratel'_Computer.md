### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-04-10
> I hear that the Hardy Heron (Ubuntu) OS will be included on the Ratel after April 24th, 2008. Is that correct?

As soon as Canonical releases Hardy, we will too. From what I've heard, everything is on schedule at Canonical.

> And I've heard that a firewall will come installed on out-of-the box Ratel's, from System 76 (after April 24th, 2008.) Is that the 'Firestarter' firewall?

No, it is called UFW (Uncomplicated Firewall). Here are the specifications of it:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuFirewall](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuFirewall)

Just for clarity's sake, this is not something unique to the Ratel. It is a part of Ubuntu 8.04.

> If I added a 256 MB nvidia PCI-E card to it, how much $$ would that cost me?

We do not offer that option, but I think you can pick up a GeForce 7600 online for about $75.

> And what kind of video card does it come with 'out of the box'?

Integrated Intel 950 Graphics
