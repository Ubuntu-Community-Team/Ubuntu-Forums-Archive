### Post by jpoRS on 2008-11-03
1. Battery Life:  Running OO.org, Firefox, Thunderbird, Compiz-Fusion, and a wealth of background tasks, and a irresponsibly bright screen, I average about 2:45-3:00 hours.  BUT it takes a long time to re-charge.  I have never timed it exactly, but it is significantly longer to charge than discharge.

2. The charger is quite good.  Reasonably sized (I don't have it with me but I want to say it is about 1"x2"x3") with a good length of cord.  Long enough to reach any reasonable outlet, but not so long that it is a burden.

3. I am touch-pad centric.  I have never used a USB mouse, and I don't see that changing any time soon.  It took me a day or two to get used to the particular nature of this pad, but it is as good as any other I have used before.

4. That question appears to rely wholly on how warm my palms get.

5. This thing runs cool.  I have used it on my lap for quite a long time, and have lap-topped it while charging, and it has never become hot, and even when charging it was barely warmer.

Hope this helps, let me know if there is more you want to know,
jim
