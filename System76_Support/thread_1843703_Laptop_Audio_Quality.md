Just got my new Pangolin. Overall, I'm very satisfied - except in one respect. I'm a bit disappointed in the sound quality from the external speakers. I find the audio tinny and muffled. Some of this is probably due to the speaker location -- on the front underside of the case. (I know, I know it's a laptop and I shouldn't expect great sound, but I was hoping for better.)

The Gazelle looks like it might be. The speakers appear more substantial and are located atop the keyboard. And the description mentions "THX TruStudio PRO", which sounds like a plus.

### Post by isantop on 2011-09-15
The Pangolin has decent sound for a laptop, but I' definitely qualify it as laptop audio. The Gazelle on the other hand, has some of the best audio I've ever heard on a laptop. It rivals many desktop setups, and it actually has a small subwoofer on the bottom to help boost low-end range. Officially, it has 2.1 surround sound.
