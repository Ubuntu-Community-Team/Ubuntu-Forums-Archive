### Post by Ben2r25 on 2010-08-03
I am running 64 bit Ubuntu 10.04 on a panp7. I have the power management settings set to lower the backlight when it's on battery power. However it does not reduce the backlight until the battery drops below 10%. How can I change it to automatically lower the backlight when using battery power regardless of the battery's charge?

---

### Post by isantop on 2010-08-03
You'll want to open up power management. (System > Preferences > Power Management) and go to the "On Battery Power" Tab. The bottom two options should be "Reduce Backlight Brightness" and "Dim Display When Idle". Make sure they're both checked.
