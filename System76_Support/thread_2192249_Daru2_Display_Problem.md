### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-12-06
There is typically a thin, multi-wire cable that runs through the hinge to reach the LCD display.  Over time, the wire twists and flexes and finally breaks or shorts out.  You can probably find the cable on line.  Labor is a couple of hours because you have to take the machine apart.  If the hinges are wobbly, you might want to replace the hinges as well, because they wear out as well.  You can watch some youtube videos on how to do it.  Most laptops have a similar procedure to replace the cable.  It's a difficult procedure, but worth doing if you have the time and patience.
