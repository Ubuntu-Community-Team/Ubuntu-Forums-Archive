### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-08-03
Yeah, if bumping the screen has an effect on it, it's probably either your LCD or your LCD wiring.

You might want to remove your battery and ac adapter and make sure the memory and hard drive are well-seated, though. Sometimes if they come loose, it will have an effect on the graphics.
