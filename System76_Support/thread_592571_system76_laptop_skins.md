### Post by cwej on 2007-10-26
I believe answer is yes, if I understand what you mean by "skin." 

I just got a new Pangolin Value. It comes with a brushed metal nearly screen-size plate embedded into the laptop cover (back of screen that you see when looking at the laptop from behind, or top of cover closed).

The  logo...

 "system 76" 

...is engraved into the brushed metal in 1 inch high letters, 5 1/2 inches across. The word "system" is black on metal, whereas the "76" is silver (brushed metal) in a black square. 

Looks spiffy. I get a lot of drive-by double-takes as people walk by my office when my door is open. It gives me an opportunity to show them Linux on the desk-top, Ubuntu, and System 76.  Result is many oohs, ahhhs, hmmmms, and "I need to look into this."
