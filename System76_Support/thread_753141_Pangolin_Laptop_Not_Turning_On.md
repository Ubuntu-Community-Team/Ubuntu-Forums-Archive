### Post by alexisbellido on 2008-05-03
Hello, just wanted to report my Pangolin is back to life. Apparently power supply problems at home fried some of my mainboard's circuits and my LCD screen (yeah, I know that sounds horrible but it wasn't that bad after all).

The System76 guys were very kind and they were going to honor my warranty but unfortunately I was outside the US and couldn't ship my laptop to them on time so I took it to some tech guy a friend recommended me here in Lima.

This guy was able to replace a few chips on my mainboard and then noticed my LCD screen had died. Today I went to see him and I bought a new LCD screen, I was watching him while he replaced it (not rocket science, just knowing where to connect the cables) and my Pangolin is working great now. Total cost: US$ 360.

At some point I thought of adding some more bucks and buying a new Dell laptop, I've seen some very good deals lately, but I felt bad about abandoning my Pangolin, some kind of man to machine thing, so I decided to pay and give it a try, and I'm happy I did so. My Pangolin is with me again, working fine and this guy even cleaned it so now it looks brand new. I'm about to upgrade it to Hardy Heron 64 bits now.

Thanks again to the System76 guys, they were very supportive and did their best to help me fix the problem all the time.

Cheers!
