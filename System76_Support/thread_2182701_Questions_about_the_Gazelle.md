### Post by ddraper2 on 2013-10-25
I have had no issues with HDMI. I plug in the monitor and it works. Most of the time the sound switches over from speakers to HDMI by itself however every now and then you need to open the sound properties and flip it manually.

Don't have a sample of the microphone. Never really use it. I checked it out when I got the box to make sure it worked and that was it.

Keyboard is disappointing. Mine was kind of bad when I got it. I also noticed something rattling around inside. I took it apart and one of the mounting screws for the keyboard had fallen out and was rattling around. I put some locktite on it and put it back it back in. I then went around and starting checking all of the screws I could see. I was amazed how many were not really tight. When I was done the keyboard is certainly better than before but not what I would expect for the price.
