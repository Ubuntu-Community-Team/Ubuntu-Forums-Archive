### Post by jbelmonte on 2009-11-24
You may have wound up with wireless turned off. After you installed the System76 driver, your... > wireless LED now should be on, and you should be able to see wireless access points. If you cannot, there is a spring-loaded toggle switch on the right-hand side of the leading edge of your Starling. Toggle it *ONCE* to the right, and then release it. Now, reboot your computer.
