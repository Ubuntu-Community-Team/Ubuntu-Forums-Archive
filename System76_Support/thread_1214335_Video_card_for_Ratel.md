### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-07-16
I remember that nVidia sucks thread. That was posted because nVidia drivers hosed the virtual terminals. Not really related, though, to your particular concerns.

The ATI cards will work fine for your intended purpose. The reason we switched to them is because they provide more bang-for-the-buck than nVidia cards we tested. We may have some more nVidia options in the future. 

If you're just going to be tinkering with video editing, or just doing small projects occasionally, your config is fine. If you think you're going to get serious about it, you may want to upgrade your CPU and Memory more. Your hard drive too, if you're going to be storing your data on your machine instead of an external drive.
