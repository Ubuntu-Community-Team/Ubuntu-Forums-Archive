### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-11-19
> **tc101 said:**
> The screen layout on my new starling netbook is different to the version of ubuntu I installed on a box here at home.  How do I change to staring so it has the more standard screen with me the drop down menus at the top of the screen, and the rest of the screen clear.

Also, right now I have it plugged into a big flat screen here at home.  How do I change the screen resolution so everything is smaller?
1.  At the login screen after entering your username (or just hitting enter) go to the Session menu bottom right and choose either Gnome desktop (10.04) or Ubuntu Desktop (10.10).  That will get you back to the standard gnome desktop.  This is exactly what I have done, though I remove the top panel completely and put everything onto the bottom panel, using the **Main menu** instead of the **menu bar**, as this saves sdcreen estate.

I can not help with screen resolution, I'm afraid, but try System ->Preferences ->Monitors where you may be able to change that.
