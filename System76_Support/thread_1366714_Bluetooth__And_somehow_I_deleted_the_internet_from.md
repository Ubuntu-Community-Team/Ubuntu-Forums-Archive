### Post by jml on 2009-12-30
I have a slightly different answer for you on the Bluetooth issue.  While Bluetooth services are running, that simply represents the OS, not the hardware.  I have a Starling and I am pretty sure that there is no hardware support for Bluetooth.  There is no ""bluetooth" led on the Starling to indicate that it is on.  There is no mention of Bluetooth support in the documentation (sparse though it is,) supplied from System 76.  And, finally, if you go to the Syatem 76 web site, Bluetooth support is not mentioned with regards to the features of the Starling.  Bluetooth support is mentioned for other devices.  I could be wrong, but if there is Bluetooth, its not documented anywhere.

Joe

### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-12-30
Right, Joe. The Starling does *not* have bluetooth. If you want bluetooth, I usually recommend the IOGear GBU421.

Did you try adding the Notification Area applet? (Right-click the upper panel, choose "Add to Panel" and then add the Notification Area applet.)
