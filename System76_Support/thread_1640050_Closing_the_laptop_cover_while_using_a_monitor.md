### Post by volkerbradley on 2010-12-07
Hi,
I have a Serval Pro.  When I am at home, I prefer to connect a monitor, keyboard and mouse to the laptop and keep the laptop out of the way.
If I close the cover of the laptop, the monitor screen turns dark.
Is there a setting that allows me to close the cover on the laptop but continue working with the attached monitor?
Thanks.

---

### Post by isantop on 2010-12-07
If the laptop is remaining awake, then all you need to do is move your external mouse around to get the screen to come back. 

If it's going to suspend, you'll want to go to System > Preferences > Power Management and change "When Laptop Lid is Closed" to "Blank Screen." Then, it's the same as for if it were remaining awake in the first place.
