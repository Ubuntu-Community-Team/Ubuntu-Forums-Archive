I finally made the plunge to 64-bit 10.04 on my PanP5 laptop, and thus far everything I've tried is working like a champ, which is webcam, bluetooth, wifi, sound, and a few other things. As I did in 9.04 I have two instances of CPU Frequency Scaling Monitor, one per core, setup in my panel so i can verify the processors are running at full strength, but no biggie there since I'm used to that work around.
