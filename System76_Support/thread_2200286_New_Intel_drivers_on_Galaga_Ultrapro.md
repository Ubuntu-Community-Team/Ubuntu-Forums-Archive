Intel is very well supported in ubuntu, However to maintain stability no change is permitted to be introduced to the software repository with out it being a major flaw (rare !) or security flaw fixed, new drivers are often introduced for those reasons. 
Now that said:
> 
if you are using Ubuntu 13.10, and intend to continue upgrading as new releases become available, check out the Intel Graphics driver installer ([http://www.webupd8.org/2013/03/intel-releases-linux-graphics-drivers.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2013/03/intel-releases-linux-graphics-drivers.html)) for the latest drivers direct from intel


In this aspect you may be the guinea pig and testing how the new driver performs on unknown hardware builds. Be prepared for breakage.
