### Post by jdmelton on 2007-12-23
I have a Serval that I bought in early December 2007.

It came with two power cords. One is the continental European style that is commonly used in Germany for grounded connections. It is the round plug with two prongs and a grounding strip on one side.

The power supply is 100-240 VAC, 50-60 Hz, output is 19 VDC at 4.74 mA. The manufacturer is LITE-ON from China. The model number is PA-1900-24.

Maybe this helps?
