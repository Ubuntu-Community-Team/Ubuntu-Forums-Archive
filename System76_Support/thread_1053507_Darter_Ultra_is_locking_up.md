### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-01-29
Sounds like a kernel panic.

1. Which Darter Ultra do you have? (System > Admin > System76 Driver will tell you.
2. Make a note of the time on your computer's clock at the next lockup. Then go to the System76 driver, click the "Support" tab and the "Create" button. Then post the logs.tar archive created in your home directory. Don't forget to report the time. It makes it much easier to find the freeze in the logs.
