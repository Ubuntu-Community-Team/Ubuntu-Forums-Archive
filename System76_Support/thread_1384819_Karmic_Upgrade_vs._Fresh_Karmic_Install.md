### Post by bvandev on 2010-01-19
Keep in mind that you generally only hear about the problems in the forum, not the successes.  It's challenging to determine how many upgrades went through without a hitch.  I upgraded the day after the new wireless driver was released according to the stated instructions.  I needed to install the grub-pc package as noted in several threads, but other than that, it worked fine.

That said, I'm guessing a clean installation won't take any longer than an  upgrade.  The bulk of the time is spent waiting for the download, which will be the same either way.  As long as you don't have too much invested in the configuration and tweaking of your current set-up, you may as well go with the clean install.

This is probably not too much help, just another voice from the crowd.  

Good luck.
