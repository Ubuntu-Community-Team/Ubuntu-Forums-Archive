### Post by MoebusNet on 2016-08-11
Notebooks, unlike desktops, are not designed to be upgraded other than RAM and HDD/SSD and not always those. Sytem76, like Dell and many others, buys its computers from factories like Clevo, then customizes OS and software. Unless the factory makes a newer version of your motherboard, you are unlikely to be able to upgrade it.
