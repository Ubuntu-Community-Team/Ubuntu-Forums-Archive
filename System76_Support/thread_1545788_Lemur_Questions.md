### Post by isantop on 2010-08-04
It's not possible to buy a Lemur with no disk drive. You can always replace the drive with an SSD later, and keep the current spare for extra data, testing, or in case you need it later.

We see slight improvements in battery life with SSD's over HDD's. Usually about 15 minutes. Not incredibly significant.

The current Lemur should last about 3 hours on one charge. It's currently out of stock, and set to be replaced with a new version. We've done some testing, but not enough to say anything conclusive about the battery. I expect it will be about the same, but it could be totally different.
