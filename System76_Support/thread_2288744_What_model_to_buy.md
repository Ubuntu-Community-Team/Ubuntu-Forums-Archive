### Post by Noah0504 on 2015-07-31
I purchased the Lemur earlier this month and have been very happy with it.

I choose it for overall value.  I wanted something a little smaller, and I was able to configure it with 8GB of RAM, a 120GB SSD for the OS and a 1TB HDD for storage.  I even picked the extra warranty and it all came in under $1,000.

I think you'd be fine to choose the default configuration of the Lemur.  You can always upgrade the RAM yourself down the road.

I'm not sure if it's the current version of the Lemur, but before I purchased mine, I did read a review or two that complained about the trackpad under Windows -- something about the driver.  I know it worked, but it didn't work well.  That's just an FYI.
