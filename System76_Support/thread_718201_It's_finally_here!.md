### Post by farruinn on 2008-03-07
My Serval Performance finally arrived! It was originally delayed because System76 has upgraded the internals (from a Compal IFL90 if I'm not mistaken to a JFL92) and then DHL delayed it further - it got to Portland, ME yesterday at 9:45 and sat there **all day**, just 4 hours away from me. On the upside, it got delivered to where I work this morning so I've been playing with it all day :). Also, this new Serval has a 1680 x 1050 resolution compared to the 1440 x 900 when I originally ordered, so thank you, thank you, thank you to System76.

The Compal JFL92 is the same barebone laptop that the Sager 2092 is based on. Sager is shipping theirs with the 45nm Intel Core 2's (8k and 9k series), so I wouldn't be surprised if we see the Serval with these faster processors in the near future. But - I'm just an uninformed consumer making guesses, so then again maybe not!

Anyway, so far I'm very glad I chose System76. Also, this laptop is very good looking - I hardly want to touch the top to avoid fingerprints!
