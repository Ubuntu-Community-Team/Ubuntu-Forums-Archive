### Post by DonKaron on 2014-10-11
At the suggestion of one of the System76 support folks I went to System Settings > Mouse & Touchpad and unchecked the box "Disable while typing".  Since then (about a week and a half) the problem has not recurred.  This may be the answer but it will take more time to be sure since the interval between occurrences of this issue has been as long as a month.
