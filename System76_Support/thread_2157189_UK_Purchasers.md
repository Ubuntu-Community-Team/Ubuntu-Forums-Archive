### Post by cool110 on 2013-06-24
I ordered a Pangolin Performance back in January which took just over a week to arrive (ordered 28th, dispatched 31st, arrived 5th). Shipping by UPS cost $104.37. Laptops are not subject to UK customs duty but you will need to pay 20% import VAT plus a UPS brokerage fee on delivery (approx. £144 on $976). Also only a US power lead is included so you will need to find a C5 (cloverleaf) lead.
