### Post by mssever on 2007-04-04
My HP laptop does the same thing sometimes. I don't know what causes the problem, but for me, switching back to the other login and then back fixes the problem without resorting to Ctrl+Alt+Backspace.

Here's what I do:[LIST=1]
[*]If the problem occurs at the login screen, cancel the login by hitting F10 and selecting Quit. Otherwise, close the lid and reopen it to activate the screen lock.
[*]From the previous login, try again.
[*]Every once in a while, it takes more than one try.[/LIST]I suspect that the problem is somehow related to the Synaptic driver, but that's just a guess.
