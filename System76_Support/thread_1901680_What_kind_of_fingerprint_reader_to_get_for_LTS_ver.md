### Post by isantop on 2011-12-29
Any fingerprint reader supported by the fingerprint-gui project should do fine. The list is available on this page: [http://www.n-view.net/Appliance/fingerprint/](http://www.n-view.net/Appliance/fingerprint/)

However, if you can hold out until 12.04, we're working on getting much better fingerprint support integrated into Ubuntu itself, and setup will be a breeze.
