I went ahead and emailed sales at System76 (possibly I should have done that in the first place, I got a response within an hour). Since it looks like this question has had over 100 views since last week when I posted it, I thought I'd copy Mark's reply for those who are interested. Thanks for your quick response Mark;

"At this time, we don't have a end of life time line for our machines, however for the most part, we do continue to service the majority of our older laptops. There's a couple models we have where we just can not find the parts for anymore and admittedly, these models are from when we first opened up 6 years ago. 

From a business standpoint, we do offer an extended warranty for up to three years, so in the grand scheme of things, we need to look at servicing that model for that amount of time, so to answer your question, we would continue to service and sell parts for those machines. 

Service would need to come through us in support as we would need to get a quote for the repair to you first. Things like battereis and AC Adapters are available for sale through our site after the order is made."

So this answers my question for the most part, I hope this is helpful for others as well.
