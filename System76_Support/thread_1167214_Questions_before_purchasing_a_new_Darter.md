### Post by jjacobs2 on 2009-05-24
Those 25 vs 35 watt ratings are for the tdp not the power usage and it's for the processor when it's running at 100% roughly.  TDP is an estimate of how much heat the computer needs to be able to handle.  Since the processor is idling most the time the difference is less significant than the numbers might indicate.
