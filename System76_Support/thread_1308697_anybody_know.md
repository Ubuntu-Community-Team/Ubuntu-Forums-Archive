### Post by HotShotDJ on 2009-10-31
I can tell you that the last Pangolin (PanP5) that I have will go for just under 2 hours on battery, depending, of course, on what you are doing with the system and how aggressive you have your power management settings.  From the look of things, the PanP6 is pretty much a PanP5 "under-the-hood" with a 16:9 15.6" monitor and a very cool refresh of the keyboard/touchpad and color scheme.  I would imagine that it would have a similar battery life as the PanP5.

It is not a laptop that you would want if you need something that you can use for hours on end without plugging in.  For me, the Pangolin is a good compromise between a true desktop replacement system (such as the Serval Professional or the old Bonobo system) and a true mobile system (such as the Starling, Darter, Gazelle and the brand spanking new Lemur).  I have a nice Kensington Universal Power Adapter that I bought several years ago for my old Dell E1505 which is thin and light-weight and works perfectly with the PanP5 (I just had to switch the adapter tip to the Kensington tip #N3  -- I'm glad I never threw the extra tips away!). I just keep it in my laptop bag, making the <2 hour battery run-time a non-issue for me.
