### Post by isantop on 2012-06-14
I believe there is some PulseAudio utility that can route the audio output into the input directly, and basically record that way. If you can't get that working (it's a little complex), you can get a 3.5 mm patch cable (Like a headphone cable with a plug on both ends). Then, plug one end into the headphone jack and one into the microphone jack. All you need to do then is record using some recording software.
