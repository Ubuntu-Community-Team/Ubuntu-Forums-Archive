### Post by Wood Prof on 2011-04-08
All I can tell you about is how much I like my Lemur.  I have 250 GB HDD, 4 GB of ram and the 31.08 WH battery.  The laptop does exactly what I want it to do. I wanted a light, small, laptop that I could travel with and leave my big laptop at work.  It fills that order very well.  For what I am doing, presentations, small statistics projects, emails, etc... It works out great.  Even watching videos, I haven't had any problems and no video issues.  The battery life is a little short for me, but I wanted a lighter laptop more than I wanted something to last a long time on battery.

I have been very pleased overall.
