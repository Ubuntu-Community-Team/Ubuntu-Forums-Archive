### Post by jjacobs2 on 2009-05-11
Seagate has always had better warranties for external drives than most other manufacturers.  I think they dropped them to 3 years recently but that's still good.  Unfortunately certain seagate drives seem to work badly with linux. I prefer to buy the enclosure and drive separately.  It's fairly simple installing the drive and you get a better warranty and can rescue your data if the enclosure dies but the drive is fine (which seems to be common for external drives).
