### Post by cowbean on 2007-03-28
I just got my 9-cell battery today, it sticks out a little out about an inch or so from the back -- pretty negligible increase from the 6-cell, I'd say.

Also weight-wise, I don't really notice the difference.

On battery life, however, I'm getting much worse performance that what Carl is citing.  The 6 cell gave me at most, 3 hours, usually a little under.  This is with wifi on, and just web browsing the whole time.  9 cell is getting 5 hours under the same conditions.  Screen is set to 75% brightness, and "Prefer power savings over performance" is checked.

Don't get me wrong, I'm fine with those numbers.  A 6 cell plus a 9 cell should last an entire flight to asia from the west coast, which is the kind of performance I'm looking for.  :)  But 6.5 - 7 hours on the 9 cell seems a little optimistic.
