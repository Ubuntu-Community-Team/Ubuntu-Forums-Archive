### Post by thomasaaron on 2010-04-01
I know you're looking for customer responses on this one. But, I *doubt* you are going to hear from any customers who have *both* the current Serval and the current Bonobo. (I'm not sure any exist.)

So, I thought I'd chime in with a couple of comparative notes, as I have both a Bonobo and Serval here in the shop.

The Bonobo's keyboard is similar to the keyboard on the PanP5/6, while the Serval's keyboard is the same as on the PanP7.

And the Serval is definitely a snappier machine, although the Bonobo is definitely no slouch. It's a powerhouse too.
