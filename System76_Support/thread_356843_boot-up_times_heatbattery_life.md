### Post by steveneddy on 2007-02-11
My Serval w/ 2.0 Ghz Dual Core, 2 Gig RAM takes aprox 45 seconds to one minute total boot time from cold to surfing. Hibernate runs about 1 minute 30 seconds or so.

As far as the heat goes - I was playing a movie full screen while running Beryl and the heat issue was negligible as far as I was concerned. The fan rarely turns on and when it does it is very quiet. It has never been an issue now and don't expect it to be in the future. 

This laptop is so quiet, I use it to take sermon notes for the shut in folks in my church. I sit in the middle of the auditorium and I can't hear the fan come on at all.

Hope this helps.
