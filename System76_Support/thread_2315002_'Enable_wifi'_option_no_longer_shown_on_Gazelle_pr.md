### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2016-03-20
First, I would suggest to contact System76 directly and start a "trouble-ticket".    They may have info/records of issues with your particular chipset for networking.    As you've already tried two OS's 14. and 15.10, it sounds like it could be a hardware issue.   One relatively easy way to fix is just to get a known, compatible usb-wireless-dongle (nano size if using a laptop).    Panda is Ubuntu friendly . . . and the price is reasonable ($10 to $30 USD depending on model).

[http://www.pandawireless.com/](http://www.pandawireless.com/)
