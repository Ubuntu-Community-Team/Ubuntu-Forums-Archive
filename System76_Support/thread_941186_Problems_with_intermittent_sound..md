### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-10-08
Double-click your speaker icon and, in the resulting GUI, go to edit > preferences. Make sure that all of the available checkboxes are selected.

Then make sure Front, PC Speakers, Master or PCM is not turned down too far or muted.

Also, go to the 'Switches' tab. If there is a checkbox for "Ext" or "Ext Amp", and it is unchecked, check it. Or if it is checked, uncheck it.
