### Post by isantop on 2011-06-16
We haven't tested that process with anything other than our current Professional line. That said, you can probably pull that off pretty well. You'll want to buy a standard hard drive and a Slimline HDD Caddy Case to mount it in. As for the bezel, you can pop the existing faceplace off the old drive and snap it onto the caddy case. It's held on by a series of plastic clips, and it pretty easy to get off if you have a small flat screwdriver you can use.
