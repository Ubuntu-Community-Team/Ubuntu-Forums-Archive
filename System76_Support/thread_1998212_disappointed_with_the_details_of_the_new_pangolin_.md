### Post by Galileo3 on 2012-06-06
I have recently ordered the new pangolin performance. I really wanted to support the idea of independent ubuntu based computer company. I am disappointed with the approach this company has with regards to the details of the laptop. First of all, a huge silly blunder is the windows button on the keyboard (super button). Yes, a windows button is there. It is covered with this cheap and ugly ubuntu sticker. But undeneath - is the good old microsoft logo. How ignorant is that? If I am using this computer extensively, after the latest of a few months this will come off. Is this not one of the reasons why people buy a system76 laptop to know that it never had anything to do with Windows? At least I was happy with that thought. It is a little bit like a company selling products with the US flag to the Tea Party members but the flag is only a sticker and underneath there is an Al-Quaida logo. Someone tell me if I am silly or overreacting with this, I must be to some extent if noone mentioned it before. This is a company that assembles custom made computers, why cannot it order a button with ubuntu logo or at least a clear button with nothing on it? 100% of their computers is ubuntu. They forgot a keyborad is a part of it? Had I known it is only a sticker I would not have bought it.

Second thing - I was happy to have the company's nice logo on the cover, I wanted to promote it and be proud of it. I looked at it and noticed it is made of some gel substance, probably glued to the outer cover. My first thought was - can it come off easily? Well, scratching it a little bit with a tip of your finger makes it come off. What is the perspective then? After a few times of putting it in a bag and out of the bag you will have this half smudged, half scratched logo of something. For sure not something that can help the company promote itself, and for sure nothing to be proud of for the owner. 

Guys, this is a computer that has very good quality finishes and is worth around $1000. Using such cheap materials for the super button and the logo on the laptop-lid just does not match it. And you just lost one customer and a potential big supporter. I wish you well, but this is just not right. 

By the way, still waiting for a response to return the laptop, 24hours since I registered for return, nothing happened yet, things are pending.
