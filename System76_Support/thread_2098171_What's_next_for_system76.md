My System76 laptop wish list is:

1) Keep weight capped at 4.5lbs (current Lemur weight, which I have).  Of course less is better.
2) Increase screen resolution.
3) Keep screen 14" or increase to 15".
4) Thinner would be nice.
5) Better keyboard w/backlighting.  The current is better than Lemur 2 was, but it's still a weak point.
