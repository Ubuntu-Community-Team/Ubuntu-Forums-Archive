### Post by Guille Damke on 2008-12-26
Hi,

I think you have to go to the "speaker icon" next to the clock in the top-right corner. Right-click on it and chose "open volume control". In the new window, click on "preferences" and check all the boxes. Now you have all the controls of your sound card (Did you run the last system76 driver after upgrading? It gives the actual options of your sound card).
For talking through the internet, it is important to use move up the "capture" volume (in the "recording" tab) and make sure it not muted. In my case, I can chose the "input source" between "front-mic" -for the built-in one- or "mic" for the sound card input.
If you want to listen to the front-mic through the speakers, you have to move up and unmute the "front-mic" bar in the playback tab, but remember that for talking through internet (at least in skype) the "capture" switch in the "recording" tab is the one to use.
I hope it helps, good luck!
