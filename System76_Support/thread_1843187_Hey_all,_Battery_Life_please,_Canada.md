### Post by isantop on 2011-09-15
The Serval has two very large fans on the back that keep the system extremely cool. We had one pumping out 720p video and a full 3D flight simulator at Ohio LinuxFest last week (both were running from about 8:30 am till 4:30-5-ish pm), and it stayed very cool. Because it stays so cool, there is some additional fan noise, but it's not bad and since the fans are large, they do each stay decently quiet. It's about on par with most desktops in terms of system noise.

### Post by RedRat on 2011-09-17
I have a Serval Pro, I7 and 8Gb, and it is a good machines. However, battery life is only about 1 hour, perhaps to much power. A larger battery would be advisable if you are going to have to disconnect from the power line. Another factor is the weight of the Serval Pro, this may or may not be a factor for you, i.e., using it as a Desktop replacement. The machine is very good. 

As to the company, they are very, very good in helping customers. Kudos to them. I am sure if you call them about payment, something can be arranged.
