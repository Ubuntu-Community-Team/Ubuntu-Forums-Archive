### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-10-01
1. Just to confirm, you just did a fresh install, right? Not an upgrade?
2. Go into your BIOS. Does it detect the DVD player? (I'm betting yes, since you could set it in the boot order. Just making sure.)
3. When you insert a CD, does your drive spin up?
4. Do you get an error message? If so, what is it?
5. Try re-seating your drive: Open the drive; remove the battery and ac adapter; remove the screw on the bottom that holds the drive in place; pull the drive out. Push it solidly back into place. Replace the screw.
