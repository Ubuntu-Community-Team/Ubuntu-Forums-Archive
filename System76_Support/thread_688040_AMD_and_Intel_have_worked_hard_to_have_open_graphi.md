### Post by DRM_free on 2008-02-04
AMD has [released the documentation to its graphics cards]("http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=826&num=1") and is continuing to do so. Download the docs [here]("http://www.x.org/docs/AMD/"). They are actively supporting OpenSUSE to write open source drivers for AMD graphics cards and they have hired the leading developer of the open source radeon driver (Alex Deutcher).

Intel has also [released all the documentation]("http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=984&num=1") to their graphics components. Download the documents [here]("http://intellinuxgraphics.com/documentation.html").

I really hope System76 will offer AMD/ATI cards instead of NVIDIA for all their laptops and desktops. It's a real shame that System76 is using NVIDIA (and only NVIDIA) discrete cards, because NVIDIA is one of the most anti-open source vendors out there. Use you economic clout to **reward** vendors like AMD and Intel who have frankly sacrificed a lot and exposed themselves to patent lawsuits in order to be open-source friendly.
