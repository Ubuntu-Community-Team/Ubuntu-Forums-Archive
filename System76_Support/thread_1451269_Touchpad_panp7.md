### Post by thomasaaron on 2010-04-14
If you have an optical mouse plugged in, they can be very sensitive to dust on the lense or the desk (or mouse pad, etc...). They can also be very sensitive to minor vibration (from your body or other sources). 

If it only occurs when an optical mouse is plugged in, I'm pretty sure it has nothing to do with a memory leak.
