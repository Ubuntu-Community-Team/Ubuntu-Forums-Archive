### Post by kperkins on 2008-05-02
Luke:
1) No latches, but they do stay closed very well, never had a problem with mine in the almost year I've had it.
2) It works fine with a default Ubuntu install, although system76 has their own system76 driver to tweak things, I think that it isn't needed for the Pangolin esp. for Hardy--needed a couple things for Gutsy, if I remember right. Search for system76 driver here or in the system 765 wiki to find out more about that.
3) I don't have a webcam on mine, so I can't tell you about that one (although add-on webcams work fine).
