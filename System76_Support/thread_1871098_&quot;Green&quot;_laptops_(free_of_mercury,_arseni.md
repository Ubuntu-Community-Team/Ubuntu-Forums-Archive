### Post by GeneSalamin on 2011-11-19
If the computer has red LEDs, then it contains arsenic, not as a contaminant, but as an essential ingredient, since they are made out of gallium arsenide.  Orange and yellow LEDs may or may not contain arsenic depending on the semiconductor, while green, blue, and UV LEDs are arsenic-free.
