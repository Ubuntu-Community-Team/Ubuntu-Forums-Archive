### Post by isantop on 2011-01-06
I can't say for sure what happened, but you shouldn't need a screwdriver to open take out the battery form any of our computers. For the Starling, there are two tabs that hold the battery in place; one is spring loaded, the other is a snap. open them both up, then you should be able to slide the battery off of the back.
