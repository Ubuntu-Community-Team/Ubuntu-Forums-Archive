### Post by GeneSalamin on 2011-09-09
The Meerkat Ion upgrade from 320 GB to 2 TB costs $169, while the upgrade to 1 TB is $35.  The Ratel upgrade from 250 GB to 2 TB is only $95.  Why is the Meerkat Ion 2 TB HDD so much more costly?

---

### Post by dmoconnell on 2011-09-09
The reason the Meerkat 2TB is more expensive then the Ratel 2TB is simply because they are 2 different hard drives.
The Meerkat is a 2TB ***7200 RPM*** SATA II  where as the Ratel is a 2 TB SATA II ***300 Mbps 32 MB Cache***. There both 2TB SATA II but they are slightly different models. This is why one is more expensive then the other.
