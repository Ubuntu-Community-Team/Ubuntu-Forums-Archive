Although there is no problem with System76 creating and applying policies as they suit our business, there is a problem with the inherent ambiguity in the phrase "Also, a fifteen percent (15%) restocking fee may be deducted from your refund or credit". Our customer does not know when that fee may be applied and System76 staff may be unclear on how to apply such a fee.

The System76 Warranty and Return Policy follows standard industry practice; however, System76 aims to provide products, services, and policies beyond the industry standard. As such our Warranty and Return Policy changed this morning to "If you are unsatisfied with your product for any reason you may return it within 30 days for a 100% refund."

> 30-Day Limited Money Back Guarantee

We take pride in the quality of the products that we sell and, as a direct result, offer a 30-day money back guarantee on all System 76 hardware, accessories, peripherals and unopened software still in its/their original sealed packaging. If you are unsatisfied with your product for any reason you may return it within 30 days for a 100% refund. Shipping and handling fees are not refundable. A $40 replacement fee will be assessed if scratches or damage are present on laptop or desktop panel(s). A $100 replacement fee will be assessed if scratches or damage are present on the top "A" panel of the Gazelle Ultra laptop. To return merchandise, you must obtain a Return Materials Authorization number. Please call the System 76 customer service department and provide them with the invoice number, item description, and serial number(s) of the merchandise you wish to return. You must ship the merchandise within five (5) days of receiving your RMA number. Moreover, you must also return the merchandise to System 76 in its/their original packaging, in as-new condition along with any media, documentation, and all other items that were included in the original shipment, prepay shipping charges, and insure the shipment or accept the risk of loss or damage during shipment.

The complete policy can be viewed here:
