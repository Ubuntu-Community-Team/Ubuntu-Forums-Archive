### Post by Idyll on 2012-01-03

---

### Post by isantop on 2012-01-04
Functionally, I don't think this would be a bad idea, but as for technical reasons, I believe the snapped windows are treated as maximized, and thus can't be resized. This is a design decision made by Canonical and Ubuntu developers, and System76 doesn't really have any say in that directly.

### Post by cephalopoda on 2012-01-17
Once you snap it you can press alt+F8 and use the left or right arrow to change the window width.
