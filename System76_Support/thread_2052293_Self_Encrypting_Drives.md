### Post by Welly Wu on 2012-09-04
I got a response from System76. My System76 Lemur Ultra Thin (lemu4) does not support self encrypting drives. The BIOS does not support SEDs. It looks like I am going to keep my Crucial M4 2.5" SATA-III 6 GB/s MLC NAND FLASH 128 GB Solid State Drive. It has a three year warranty.
