### Post by msrinath80 on 2010-07-04
I had the lemur shipped to Canada too. S76 uses UPS to send it. It is delivered right to your doorstep. No tax/customs brokerage at this point. A week later, you will receive a bill from UPS for approx. CAD 50. That is the customs brokerage fee. You will need to send them a cheque to that effect. Shipping took about 2 days once they (S76) sent it. Of course, feel free to wait for someone from S76 to respond.

PS: Good choice. I bought the lemur about 2 months ago and am enjoying it like anything. I get the promised 3 hrs battery life plus, I've never seen/used such a light, slim yet reasonably powerful laptop before. The 3 MB L2 cache on the SU7300 must be optimized or something. I mean I've owned a Fujistu Lifebook S7110 which came with a 2GHz  T7200 (4MB L2 cache) and even it used to struggle a lot and generate soooo much heat. But the 1.8GHz SU7300 hardly seems to struggle when put to the exact same tasks. Most of the time it is sitting at 800MHz clock and I don't even feel that it is slow!
