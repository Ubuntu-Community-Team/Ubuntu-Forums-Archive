### Post by isantop on 2013-05-13
> 2. Will it come with mSATA support for 64 - 256 GB or more storage capacities or not?

Yes, the Sable does have a mini-PCIe slot that you can use to add an mSATA SSD. We also offer standard sized SSDs from the factory.

> 3. Will it support 1 TB 5400 RPM hard disk drives and the new Crucial m500 [m5] SSDs or not?

There shouldn't be any issues with large HDDs or the new M5s.
