### Post by traxtopel on 2014-01-04
I live in the Netherlands, bought a Sable Complete 2 months ago. 

My reasons for going System76.
- had various apple computers since 2004, however I feel their products are too expensive.
- wanted a pc in one, could not find anything on the market I liked.
- my i7 Sable Complete with 16gb memory and 240gb ssd cost me around 1350 euros shipped including tax. An iMac bought in the Netherlands, similar specs around 2000 euros.
- support the little guys i.e. System76 and not HP, Apple, Dell etc.

I am sure Clevo make a nice product, but what advantage does it have over Dell etc?
