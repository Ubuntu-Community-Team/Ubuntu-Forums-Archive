### Post by gamerchick02 on 2009-08-09
How loud do you have the volume?

I know it might be a silly setting, but having the volume too high might add to your popping and crackling issue.

Go to your volume applet on your top bar (usually next to the clock, although you may have moved it) and right-click. Click "open volume control" and see what you have everything set at.  See if you have everything maxed out and see if you change the volume bars if you can get rid of the crackling.

Good luck.

EDIT: It very well may be a sound card issue.  I'm sure Thomas Aaron can help you tomorrow with some tech support.

Amy
