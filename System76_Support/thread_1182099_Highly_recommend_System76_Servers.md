Quick unsolicited feedback here: 

Our company bought a Jackal Pro 2U nearly a year ago, based on compatibility with Ubuntu Server, and the impression of excellent support.

So thanks to all the System 76 guys, for the great hardware, Ubuntu support, and advice in configuration. Couldn't be happier.
