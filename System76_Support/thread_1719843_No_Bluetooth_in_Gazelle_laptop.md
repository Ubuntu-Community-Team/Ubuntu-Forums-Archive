### Post by isantop on 2011-04-04
Actually, we're not currently offering Bluetooth in the Gazelle/Serval. It is a very common feature request, and we may add the ability in the future. For now, I'd recommend an external Bluetooth dongle. There are many that are very small, and most of them should work well in Ubuntu.
