### Post by Eldera on 2011-10-16
Instead of giving out manuals and disks, System 76 posts information in their Wiki
[http://knowledge76.com/index.php/Main_Page](http://knowledge76.com/index.php/Main_Page)

Specific instructions on upgrades have not yet been updated for Oneiric, but the procedure for Natty would be similar and is here:
[http://knowledge76.com/index.php/NattyUpgrade](http://knowledge76.com/index.php/NattyUpgrade)

One makes one's own disk from [http://www.ubuntu.com/](http://www.ubuntu.com/)

After installing from the disk or USB, get the driver, second from the bottom in Kacey's link, run restore and you should be good to go.

Have a great day.

PS I am not an employee of S76, just another customer who has done several installs with this procedure.
