### Post by johnb820 on 2007-06-24
In case anyone has the same problem as myself, I figured out the solution.  For this system76 desktop, I had to enable the floppy drive first in the bios.  This still doesn't tell me why I had power problems but I should be all set.  I'm not sure why the system76 did not enable a floppy drive by default.

---

### Post by greg_g on 2007-06-24

Or maybe it is off by default and that is one more step they would have to take each time.  Would make sense to have it off by default because not many machines come with floppy drives anymore.

But, either way, I'm glad you got it figured out.
