### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-09-08
Make sure you run your System76 Driver (Admin > System76 Driver > Install (tab) > Install (button)). Then reboot. You probably should do that after running updates.

All you will have to do is click on the Network Manager icon in the upper right of your screen. It will show you all available wireless access points. Then just click on the one for your hotel.
