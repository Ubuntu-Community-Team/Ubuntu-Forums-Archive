Does anyone know if there is a difference in cooling in the Lemur based on its configuration with the i5 3210M vs the i7 3630QM?

### Post by isantop on 2013-02-28
It does come with the same heat sink, but it's adequate for cooling either processor at full load. Under normal use, the system will scale back and use less power, therefore generating less heat, and both will be about even.
