### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-03-28
Actually, we do not mess with the BIOS on the Darter. When we get the chassis, it already has AT LEAST 1.10 installed on it. Anything less that that, and it will not work with Gutsy.

If the chassis comes to us with 1.30 installed on it, that is what it ships with. So, I'm not exactly sure what they are currently shipping with, but I'll check next time I see one. (Same with AHCI; I'll let you know what I find.) Bottom line, we don't really mess with the BIOS without a very good reason.

Just to be thorough, we do not recommend changing the BIOS unless we determine that it is necessary in order to fix some bug. Since updating the bios is usually unnecessary, and it has the possibility of COMPLETELY ruining your computer if the process goes wrong, we cannot warranty computers damaged by BIOS updates unless we have specifically recommended the update.
