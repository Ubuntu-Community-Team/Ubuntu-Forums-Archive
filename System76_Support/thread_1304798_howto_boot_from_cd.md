### Post by Buuntu on 2009-10-29
Yeah, preferably you should change it back after you install, though it doesn't really matter unless you put bootable CD's in your drive all the time and don't want them to run :P.  

To boot off the cd, simply set CD/DVD drive above HDD in your boot priority and that should do the trick.

### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-10-29
