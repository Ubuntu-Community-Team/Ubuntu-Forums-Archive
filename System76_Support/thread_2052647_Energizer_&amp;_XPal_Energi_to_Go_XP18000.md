I wanted to let other System76 Lemur Ultra Thin (lemu4) notebook PC owners about the Energizer & XPal Energi to Go XP18000 re-chargeable lithium polymer external battery. It offers up to 18,000 mAh of extra battery juice for a wide array of electronics devices including notebook PCs, netbooks, smart phones, cell phones, etc. It is compatible with the System76 Lemur Ultra Thin (lemu4) notebook PC. Everything that is needed is included within the shipping package. It comes with a three year warranty once you purchase and register your product with XPal. For me, it offers an additional six hours of battery life using Ubuntu 12.04.1 64 bit Long Term Service. As Linus Torvalds and Canonical continue to improve Ubuntu, energy efficiency and battery life will continue to improve. It is a bit cumbersome to carry around, but the entire package including the external lithium polymer battery and the power adapter along with the various tips for different electronics devices weigh less than 1.5 pounds. It can easily fit inside your messenger bag or a backpack. It can recharge your Lithium Ion battery on the go at least three times to a full charge if you can not get access to an AC outlet. It also powers and charges up to three electronics devices simultaneously. There is a USB 2.0 port to charge USB devices and it comes with a USB 2.0 Type A to Micro B cable and tip.

For me, this is an absolute must have accessory because I am a highly mobile Ubuntu user. It greatly expands my useful computing sessions quite extensively and it has literally helped me to stay fully charged and in contact with my family members, friends, colleagues, etc. while I am travelling to different locations.

I highly recommend it especially if you have a System76 Lemur Ultra Thin (lemu4).
