### Post by brdmn on 2012-05-14
I thought it wasn't possible to change the contrast for an LCD screen, but maybe someone here will set me straight on that.  Two things I know you can do: (1) You can change the backlighting if you've installed the System76 driver by using Fn+F8/F9; and (2) You can also play around with the gamma settings using xgamma.
