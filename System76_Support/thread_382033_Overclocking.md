### Post by crichell on 2007-03-11
Hi Augie -

> My question is what can I expect to get out of the Ratel hardware without any major cooling and/or ram upgrades? Is the e6300 (or e6400) worth getting over the e6600 even without overclocking?

The Ratel is a snappy machine - we don't overclock anything and I'm not sure if I'd recommend it on that machine.  The Wild Dog is better suited for high performance and overclocking.

I generally recommend bumping up the processor if you can - it's the one component that is expensive to replace if you want to upgrade down the road.  (More hard drive or more memory are pretty cheap upgrades.)
