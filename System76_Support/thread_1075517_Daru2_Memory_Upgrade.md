### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-02-20
It has two slots. You will need ddr2, 667MHz, 200-pin memory cards.

You ***could*** add a 2GB card in addition to your 1GB card. I ***doubt*** you will run into any problems with that. **But**, it's generally not recommended. It's always better to have two equally matched cards.
