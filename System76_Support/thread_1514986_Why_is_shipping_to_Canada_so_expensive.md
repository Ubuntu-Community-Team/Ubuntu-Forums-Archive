### Post by SoFl W on 2010-06-22
I have shipped from the US to Canada using UPS.  I know if it was personal, marking the contents as a gift there were no taxes or fees.  When I shipped business the price did up significantly.  The UPS website has estimate cost of shipping and delivery time tools on their website.  The handling fee is whatever the company is charging for the cost of an employee to place it in the box plus the cost of shipping materials.   Any cost above the item plus shipping (using the UPS tools) must be taxes.
