### Post by jml on 2007-02-22
I don't have a MacBook, but I have used a first generation iBook, a 12" PowerBook, an IBM ThinkPad R40e, an HP nx6110, and am currently using a System76 Darter.  In my opinion, nothing is quite as good as an IBM keyboard, (sorry Carl.) But as far as I am concerned, my Darter's keyboard is as good as my iBook's and my PowerBooks's and way better than my HP's keyboard.  Now I am not a particularly fast typist.  Since the Darter is a designed to be a compact laptop, the keyboard is a bit smaller than a "full sized" laptop's would be, but I do not find that a problem.  Hope this helps.
