---

### Post by isantop on 2011-02-14
There is really a SIM slot, and the motherboard is capable of using onboard 3G, but due to licensing issues, we can't sell them with 3G enabled. There are USB 3G modules you can buy. See this knowledge base article:

[http://knowledge76.com/index.php/3G_Wireless_-_Enable_AT%26T_USBConnect_Quicksilver](http://knowledge76.com/index.php/3G_Wireless_-_Enable_AT%26T_USBConnect_Quicksilver)
