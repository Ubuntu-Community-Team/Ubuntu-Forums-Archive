### Post by DesiArnez6 on 2007-06-15
IN case anyone was wondering, I just connected an external USB floppy drive from "LaCie" Model 706018, because of reviews that it was Linux friendly, Indeed it is! No driver was needed (although one for Mac or Windows is included), It reads High and Low density. Anyways, plugged it in and it simply Worked! (Laptop MUST be connected to AC adapter though, if it is running on battery instead, drive will NOT be recognized) Anyways, hope this helps if anyone else out there is still in need of a floppy drive ;)
