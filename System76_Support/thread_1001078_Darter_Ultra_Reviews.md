### Post by Spr0k3t on 2008-12-05
I just got mine a couple days ago and I've got to say it, it's a damn fine machine.  The only thing I could ask for are some stronger built-in speakers.  The keyboard feels great for such a small size (unlike what you find in a netbook).  The screen is very clear.  The functionality of the suspend/hibernate is solid.  I've only found a single problem with the laptop and that's the built in bluetooth not reconnecting without pressing Fn F12 every time it boots.
