### Post by Paranoid Randroid on 2007-02-26
Well, in the interest of being accurate, Wine is not an emulator; it's a reimplementation of Windows libraries. If all things were perfect then there wouldn't be any performance penalty.

Things are not perfect, though, so I've always found Wine to be a little sluggish. WoW certainly runs under Wine and Cedega threads like this one - [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=227270](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=227270) - seem to indicate performance about half of what you'd see on Windows.
