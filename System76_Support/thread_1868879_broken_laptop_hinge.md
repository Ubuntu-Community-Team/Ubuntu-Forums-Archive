### Post by Eldera on 2011-10-25
[quote=tgoff]I should remember what model laptop I own,[/quote]

If your machine is not too old, the display panel for the 76 driver may tell you the model. In Lucid or before, click System > Administration > System 76 Driver.

In Natty or Oneiric, type "driver" into the search box and choose System 76.
