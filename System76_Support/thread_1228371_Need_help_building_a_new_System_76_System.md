### Post by 3Miro on 2009-07-31
For graphics, I wouldn't go with integrated video. It will work, but not as well as a real video card. Normally, under Linux, I would recommend nVidia, since the ATI drivers a bit behind, but I am sure system76 has all the possible issues worked out.

Atoms are definitely not heavy duty CPUs, they are more of notebook ones. For a desktop a Core 2 Duo is much better.

Good choice on the RAM, you can never have enough.

HD size and Optical Drive depend on what exactly you are using it. For most people 250 is plenty enough.

So my advice is to go with the Ratel.

BTW if 4GB of RAM is all they offer now, I don' think you will have the option of upgrading it to more later. 4GB is a lot though.
