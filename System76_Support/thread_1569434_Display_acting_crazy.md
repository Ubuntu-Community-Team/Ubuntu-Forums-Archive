### Post by isantop on 2010-09-07
Hi Mark.

Sounds like you may be hitting the scroll bar on the touch pad by accident. To use the scroll on new Starlings, you simply touch the corners of the pad on the right hand side.

You may be hitting the area that triggers this. Make sure you hit the pad near the center.
