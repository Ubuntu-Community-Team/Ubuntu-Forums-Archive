### Post by isantop on 2010-07-21
I believe I spoke with you via email, but just to make the answer public.

Any VGA or HDMI external displays should work fine on the PanP7. DVI displays may work fine, especially with an HDMI to DVI adapter, but VGA to DVI adapters may be somewhat tricky. 

I used my Pangolin with projectors in school all the time. You can toggle between external and built-in displays with Fn+F7, and you can the Monitor Preferences dialog (System > Preferences > Monitors) to set up more advanced configurations (Extended or Mirrored displays, etc.)
