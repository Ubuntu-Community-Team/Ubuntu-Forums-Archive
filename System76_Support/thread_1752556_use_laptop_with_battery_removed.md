### Post by benbash on 2011-05-07
Can the Bonobo model laptop be operated with the battery removed?

What I'm getting at is: if I intend to use the laptop at my desk for four hours straight, can I remove the battery and operate the laptop just from AC power?   
Or does that cause more problems than solutions?

My understanding is that the life of the battery can be extended if the laptop remains cool and the battery goes through as few charge cycles as possible.  I've heard that running a laptop on AC power with the battery in can cause wear on the battery.

What do other Bonobo users do when they plan on working at a desk for several hours?

Is there a good reference on maximizing the life of your battery?

### Post by isantop on 2011-05-10
That's all sound science, though the degradation isn't too fast, it will shorten the operational life of the battery to run it on AC most of the time. You can remove the battery from the Bonobo and run it without a battery installed, but you'll need to be careful not to let the AC power cord come unplugged, and be sure not to let anything short out the battery connectors.
