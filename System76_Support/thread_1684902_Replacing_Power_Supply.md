### Post by isantop on 2011-02-10
Hi Mark. 

You'll want to get a power supply that provides 19 V at at least 1.58 Amps. It's also negative on the outside, and the default power supply is grounded. 

As for the plug, it is probably best just to take the computer into the shop with you and test fit a few to find one that works. It is possible to find out what the exact dimensions are, but it may not be until next week that I can.
