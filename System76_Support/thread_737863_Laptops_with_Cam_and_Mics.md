### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-03-28
All of the laptops have a either a cam built in or a cam option. All have built in mics.
Due to the current state of ALSA, the mics (particularly on the Gazelle, Pangolin and Serval) do not do a terrific job of capturing sound. Their output is usually a bit fuzzy. 

We are working on fixing this in Hardy (which will be ready in April). For Skype, recording, etc... it is better to use either an external mic or USB headphones/mic.
