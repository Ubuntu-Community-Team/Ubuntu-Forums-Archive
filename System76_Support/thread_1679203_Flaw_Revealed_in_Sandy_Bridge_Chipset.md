### Post by thomasaaron on 2011-02-01
This recall affects our Gazelle Professional and Serval Professional Laptops. 

In a nutshell, it effects SATA port 2, which connects the DVD drive to the motherboard and could cause a performance degradation in the DVD drive over time.

We have placed our Gazelles and Servals out of stock and will not be shipping any of these systems until mid-March when Intel delivers the supply of updated motherboards.

All customers who have ordered and/or received Gazelle or Serval Professionals will be notified of the recall in the next couple of hours.

If you have any further questions, please contact support...at...system76...dot...com.
