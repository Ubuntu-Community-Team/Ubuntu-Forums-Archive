### Post by isantop on 2011-08-04
Provided your Pangolin is less than three years old, it's very simple to gain access to the fan and blow the dust out of it. There are three screws holding the large main panel in place (the exact location will differ depending on which pangolin you have). Remove them, then pull the panel off. You'll need to be careful, since the fan is attached to the panel, and plugged into the motherboard. Unplug it, then you can clean it out pretty thoroughly. Remember to plug the fan back in when you replace the panel.
