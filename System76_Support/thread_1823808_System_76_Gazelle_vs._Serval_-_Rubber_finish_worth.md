### Post by project47 on 2011-08-14
I asked the same question (although when I configured them the same, there was only a $45 difference). Ian told me: 

> The Serval uses all copper components in it's cooling system and comes with a 10 Amp power supply, both of which are needed to handle the strain of running the top end Graphics card and  Processor; the Gazelle uses an Aluminum cooling system and 6.3 Amp power supply. Also, the Serval comes with a rubberized soft-touch finish similar to the one used on many cell-phones, while the Gazelle uses a standard matte-black polycarbonate finish. The Soft-touch finish is more durable, and gives the machine more of a higher-end feel and look than the Gazelle.

I wound up going with the Serval and am happy with it.
