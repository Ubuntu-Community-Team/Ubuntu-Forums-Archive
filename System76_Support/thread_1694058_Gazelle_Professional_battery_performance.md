### Post by isantop on 2011-02-24
Thanks for your interest in the Gazelle! The battery should last around three hours and twenty minutes or so. It's one of our best performers yet as far as batteries go. As for an extended battery, we don't offer any right now, and it has an unusual shape that would make it difficult to do so. However, that's exactly what I used to think about the Lemur, then lo and behold, we have an extended battery. There really is no way to tell, but it is a possibility.
