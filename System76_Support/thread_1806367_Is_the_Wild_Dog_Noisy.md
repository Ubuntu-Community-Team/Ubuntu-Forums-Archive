I have a Wild Dog, and the noise is nowhere near as loud as my old Dell Dimension 8200, but now that I think about it, probably slightly louder than the Dell Optiplex 620 that the Wild Dog replaced.  Perhaps because the case is 100% aluminum instead of plastic so it creates a bit of an echo chamber?  Anyway, it is the only thing in my home office that creates noise and it is in no way bothersome to me.  Also, the noise level seems to stay the exact same at all times no matter how heavily I am using it, which is nice.

Not sure if this will apply to you, but I installed a second hard drive and definitely needed the rubber noise deadeners that go around the hard drive screws, because without them the vibrations were loud.  The hard drive that comes with the computer already has them, which was nice.

EDIT: I forgot to actually answer your questions.  The noise is in no way annoying, I don't even notice it.  No earmuffs or ear plugs necessary.  Conversations can be had without problem.
