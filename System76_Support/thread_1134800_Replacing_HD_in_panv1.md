### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-04-26
On the PanV2, the hard drive is installed either left or right side of the computer near the front edge. In other words, it slides in to the side of the computer.

There are two screws on the bottom of the computer near the edge that hold it in. Just remove them and pull out the drive.

NOTE: Remove all power from the machine before doing so... battery and ac.
