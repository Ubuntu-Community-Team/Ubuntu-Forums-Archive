### Post by untrustytahr on 2016-11-27
I have an earlier gazelle version.  It has always ran on the upper end of the operating temperature range.  I remember looking up the spec on the Intel page.  It was high, but within acceptable range.  It's been running years like that with no problems.  Just so you know, Fn+1 toggles the fans between full (whether the system thinks it needs cooling or not) and normal operation (which modulates the speed based on how high the temps are).

Can't help on the wifi.  I changed to an AC NIC about a year or two ago and it's been rock solid.
