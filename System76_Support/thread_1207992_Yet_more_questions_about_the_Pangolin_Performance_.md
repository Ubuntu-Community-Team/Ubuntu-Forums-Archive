 Audio over HDMI failed to work out of the box for me, it may be supported though (after messing with sound settings). In general HDMI audio support is pretty bad in linux unfortunately, so this is a feature that is tough to shop for. 

HDMI video had a hard time scaling to the proper screen size out of the box, but other wise this is liveable and it worked fine for me using miro

1- I prefer the pangolin's screen to a big tv, in most cases (if the tv is under 720p and not a +50 plasma lol)
