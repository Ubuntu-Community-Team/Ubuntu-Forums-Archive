### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-10-31
We're working on getting *extra* batteries for the Lemur. But there will be no options for a larger battery.

The battery is a flat panel that attaches into the bottom of the computer. It could not easily be increased in capacity without increasing it's physical size. And if it were any bigger, the laptop wouldn't sit flat.
