### Post by perce on 2008-04-11
In the old white Darter you can:
tap to click (of course)
2-finger tap to middle click
vertical scroll on the right side
horizontal scroll on the bottom side
top-right corner tap to middle click
bottom-right corner tap to right click.

Unfortunately the touchpad is small, so all these effects are more annoying than useful.
