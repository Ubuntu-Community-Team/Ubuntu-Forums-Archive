### Post by Uzmati on 2009-05-15
Alright, had it for a few days now...

I am VERY impressed by the build quality of this machine. I don't know who System 76 contracts through, but this device makes me want to check out their other hardware for my future use. 

The netbook itself is pretty typical otherwise. The "extended" battery it ships with does stick out some, but it's worth it for the extra battery life. I've gotten about 6 solid hours of use out of it, no problem. 

I also own/use an HP Mini and MSI Wind 100, and the Starling is much more responsive than either. The whole system feels much quicker and responsive than the XP installs on the other devices. 

I'm having no problem viewing flash video in normal rez, HD makes things a bit choppy and jumpy. Miro and Boxee both work pretty well but Boxee can get a little slow at times. 

All other internet access features are just swell. With with media-rich websites, the Starling performs like a champ. 

You CAN switch back and forth from the UNR to the standard desktop, but there's little reason for me to do so. The UNR interface works very well with the Starling. 

I'd be happy to answer any other questions you may have about the machine. Overall I'm very glad I went this route.
