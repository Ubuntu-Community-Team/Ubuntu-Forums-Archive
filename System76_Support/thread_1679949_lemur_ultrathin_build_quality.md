### Post by bearfeeder on 2011-02-02
I got my Lemur yesterday and have been very, very impressed with the build quality.

The machine feels a whole lot like my Dell Studio 15 (except it's smaller and lighter).

It's build quality, sturdiness, etc. are on par with my ThinkPads and my MacBook Pro.

Specifically, the machine is very rigid and there's no keyboard flex.

Performance-wise, the machine seems as fast as my ThinkPad x300.  It's great for surfing the web and doing word processing.

I recommend a purchase.
