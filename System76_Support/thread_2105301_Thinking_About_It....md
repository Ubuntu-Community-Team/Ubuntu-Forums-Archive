### Post by ccrs8 on 2013-01-21
> **Mega Pony said:**

I have a Wild Dog Performance and love it.  I have the wilp8 whereas the current version is the wilp9.  I haven't kept up with the product lineup since I won't be in the market for another desktop for quite some time so I'll let someone with more knowledge talk about what changed in the new version.

I chose it over the the Ratel because of the video card.  The Wild Dog had better video card options which allowed me to easily use two huge DVI monitors.  There are other improvements over the Ratel for sure, but the video card situation was the primary reason why I went with the Wild Dog.  After purchasing it, I came to appreciate the larger, roomier case which made it easier to fiddle around with internal components.

Anyway, I am very happy with my decision.
