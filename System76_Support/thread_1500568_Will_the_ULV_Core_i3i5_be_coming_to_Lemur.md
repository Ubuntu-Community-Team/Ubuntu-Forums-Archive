We don't really have a time-line in mind for this. I've heard we might see some i5/i7 ULV CPUs sometime during the first quarter of next year, but I don't know what machines they will be used with.

> I realize that it probably won't be anytime soon since they just started shipping the processors and I'm sure you all need time to do some testing and such.
