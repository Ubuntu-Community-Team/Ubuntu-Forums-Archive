### Post by crichell on 2007-01-06
You can try to make some adjustments via System > Preferences > nVidia Settings.  There's not much in there by way of color temperature however.  For greater fine tuning and adjustment you can add a "Gamma" line to your xorg.conf file.

This article:
[http://applications.linux.com/article.pl?sid=05/02/07/2244242](http://applications.linux.com/article.pl?sid=05/02/07/2244242)
describes fine tuning your display and adjusting gama.
