### Post by kadlecik on 2014-04-21

### Post by hendrik-0 on 2014-07-30
i have my gazp9 for 9 months.

the keyboard rattled on the lower right when i first received it. tech support advised me to put notecards under the keyboard, lol. after that the keyboard was fine for a while. recently, it started locking the function keys after pressing them (e.g. after muting the laptop, it will jump back and forth between mute and not mute). i hope after cleaning the keyboard it will be fine again.

the main problem is that the laptop gets extremely hot. it does not disturb me during normal working, but under full load (e.g. mathematica/matlab multicore calculations, games) the laptop becomes burning hot (to the degree that sometimes it shuts itself off under full load). that is a bit of a disappointment because in principle it is a very fast machine. 

there are some minor bothering issues: it constantly suffers from loose screws. every now and then i need to retighten them. the most annoying thing is that the fan sometimes starts to rattle. It seems in order to be able to provide fast hrdware system76 saved on the manufacturing and the "small" things.
