### Post by satsujinka on 2009-12-08
I would happily vouch for the s9. I have a 16gb model that I'm listening to at this very moment. As it just works like a regular flash drive there's no need to do anything special with it (I can't say I've tried to sync it with any kind of program as I prefer to do that by hand.) One thing to note is that the usb cord for the s9 is a standard that's common in Korea but not in the U.S. (or Europe I think) so you will want to buy a spare if you lose things.
As for other DAPs by Cowon, I regret to say the s9 was the first player I've had by them. But I would expect them to be of similar quality. You may want to ask around here: [http://iaudiophile.net/forums/](http://iaudiophile.net/forums/)
