### Post by Flyers2391 on 2010-11-23
I work with 2 monitors all the time ... I don't know what I did without them!  I have noticed that new windows normally open on the screen I last clicked on the first time I opened the program.  I hope that isn't too confusing but I'm pretty sure that is how ubuntu remembers it.  

Also, with firefox I have noticed the dialog boxes always seem to pop-up (edit > preferences for example) on the OTHER monitor (or non-primary which is always my other) ... not sure why.  

So, in short, if you want it to load on your external monitor, next time you boot up, click firefox and your external monitor's desktop quickly.  Or, just move your panels (hold down alt and drag) to your external monitor.
