### Post by jml on 2010-07-21
Since you had an unplanned freeze up, your wireless card may have been turned off.  To restart the card look for a small slide switch on the right front edge of the Starling.  Move the switch to the right "once".  Then reboot your computer.  The wireless indicater light should be on, and you should have wireless acess.  Post back here if that does not work.
