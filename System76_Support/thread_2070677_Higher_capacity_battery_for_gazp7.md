### Post by cprofitt on 2012-10-13
The  W25AES is what the Gazelle is. The Sager NP3260 is also a Clevo W25AES.

There are two battery options listed on Clevo's site:

[LIST]
[*]Battery life : 300 minutes (UMA mode with 48.84WH battery)
[*]Battery life : 380 minutes (UMA mode with 62.16WH battery)
[/LIST]
I am not sure if the battery that comes with any of those models is the 48.84 or the 62.16...

### Post by Stroik52 on 2012-10-25
In case anyone is curious, I was able to purchase the following battery: [http://rjtech.com/shop/index.php?dispatch=products.view&product_id=30154](http://rjtech.com/shop/index.php?dispatch=products.view&product_id=30154). As described by the poster above, there is also a 62.16WH version of the battery. I wish it had been provided as an option straight from System76 but alas.

After tweaking Ubuntu a bit via laptop_mode_tools and Jupiter I probably get about 3.5HR battery life. Not the greatest but much better than before. Hopefully this information helps other gazp7 owners.
