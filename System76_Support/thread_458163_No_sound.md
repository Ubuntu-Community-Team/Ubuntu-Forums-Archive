### Post by MorphWVUtuba on 2007-05-29
I had this same problem, and still working through a couple loose ends of it.  See here:
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/system76/+bug/116726](https://bugs.launchpad.net/system76/+bug/116726)

Also, I found the guide to sound problems on the Knowlege76 Knowledge Base helpful.  My situation was that I did actually have sound after installing the driver, but the volume in alsamixer was turned too low, on the speaker slider if I remember correctly.  Anyway, check the knowlegebase article & follow the troubleshooting steps.  If nothing else it will help you isolate & better understand the problem.
[http://knowledge76.com/index.php/Guide_to_Sound_Problems](http://knowledge76.com/index.php/Guide_to_Sound_Problems)

Hope this helps!
