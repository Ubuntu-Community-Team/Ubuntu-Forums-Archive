I can only get the whole desktop to display on the external monitor if I pick a very low resolution setting (below the TV's HD resolution).

### Post by Carborundum on 2012-08-19
The top, bottom and sides being cut off is called overscan, and is a feature of your TV. To fix it, simply* disable it in your TV's settings.

*May or may not actually be simple, or even possible, depending on the TV.
