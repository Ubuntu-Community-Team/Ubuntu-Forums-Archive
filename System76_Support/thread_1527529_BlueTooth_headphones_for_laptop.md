### Post by volkerbradley on 2010-07-13
In the past I never could use the headset with another computer that had built in Bluetotoh receiver.
I decided to try it with the Serval Pro and found that things are much better.
Pairing the Bluetooth headset with the Serval Pro works flawlessly.  I should mention that I also use a Microsoft Bluetooth mouse and that the headset is also paired with my Android phone.
Once the Plantronics headset has paired with the Serval Pro, I see that I can go to the Bluetooth icon and now see a Connect button for the headset.  Once the headset is turned on, it connects automatically. When I then choose this headset for the sound input and output, I notice that the mouse pointer develops a significant delay.
I have used the headset successfully with the gnome-sound-recorder and with Skype.
All in all, this is a marked improvement over what I experienced in the past.  The headset is now quite useable.  Wished that my Bluetooth mouse worked as well as it does when the headset is not on.
Thanks for your willingness to help.
