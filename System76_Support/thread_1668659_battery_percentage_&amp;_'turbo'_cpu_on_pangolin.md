I just bought a Pangolin Performance. Two questions: 

1. The battery indicator never shows a percentage and can never estimate time remaining... How do I get it to show me battery percentage remaining? 

2. I got the i7-640M Processor that has the 3.46GHz Turbo Mode. How do I enable it? The bios has no options for CPU.

### Post by isantop on 2011-01-17
Your first issue is actually a known issue, and we're working on getting a fix up. It affects nearly our entire laptop line. The icon should display the proper percentage, and all of the power management features work fine.

The Turbo mode is enabled automatically when it can. Basically, there has to be heavy load on the processor, and it has to be consolidated in one thread. If it's spread out, you'll still get a turbo boost, but it won't be as significant.
