### Post by jlh68 on 2015-07-12
You should expect the WiFi to shut down during suspend (cover closed), that is a way to save power.  Yes to turn it on is to push Fn + F10 (airplane mode), twice.  I notice the first press does noting but the second the top panel flickers and then the WiFi icon shows activity and then my WiFi is connected again.

As for the other problem I do not have that with my 3-week old Lemur, but I did reinstall within the first week of having it.  

I have to say this computer has operated the best of any I have owned.  I did have to install Bluetooth Manager to get BT to work correctly. So the factrory installed BT application is not used, and has a strange symbol in the top panel and I have a nice blue BY symbol further dwon the top panel.

I wish you success in getting your unit working great.
