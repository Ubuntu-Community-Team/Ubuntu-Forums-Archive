### Post by Kirboosy on 2013-11-25
I don't know if there are any good speakers you would be able to fit in such a small enclosure. Also if you open that computer chances are you will void the warranty. I would recommend buying some external speakers and tuck them under the desk or hide them away. I know you don't want external speakers but you do have an external mouse and keyboard on the computer as well. You will be much happier with the sound coming out of some budget external speakers. (I'm personally a fan of Logitechs)

I'm currious though, if you do find some small speakers to replace the internal ones please share back here. I haven't heard of anyone doing this. 

Hope that helps!
~Caboose


Edit: You may be able to velcro or stick the speakers to the backside of the computer. The only extra thing you would see at that point is a cable running up to the speakers.
