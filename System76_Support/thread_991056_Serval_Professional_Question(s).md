### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-11-24
> 1. Would I need a System76-modified version of 9.04 when it comes out, or could I "update-manager -d"?

Update manager should be fine. By the time 9.04 is officially released, we should have testing for that version finished and any needed patches built into a new version of the System76 driver.

> 2. Would I use the usual repositories for Ubuntu?

...um, not sure where you're going with that question. Whenever you update your software sources list is updated, and you can download whatever programs are in the new repositories. Is that what you mean?

> 3. Could I switch distros, or would I need the System76 driver? I like Ubuntu, but I also like to try other distros.

Sure. However, we only support Ubuntu. Our System76 driver will run on Kubuntu and Xubuntu, but that doesn't mean all the patches will actually work on those versions -- but they probably do, at least on Kubuntu.

> Next question is on shipping. I am a Canadian...Question #4: Are their alternatives to shipping, or do I wait until I'm back in Tim Hortons land?

Some folks have a friend ship it from the U.S./Canada to another country. However, PLEASE be aware, we do *not* provide warranty support internationally (other than Canada).
