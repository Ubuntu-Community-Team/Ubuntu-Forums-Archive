### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2014-08-02
As with any version upgrades, third party PPAs are disabled. That includes the one from System76, the one providing the proprietary drivers according to your model. 

[http://knowledge76.com/index.php/Version_Upgrade](http://knowledge76.com/index.php/Version_Upgrade)
