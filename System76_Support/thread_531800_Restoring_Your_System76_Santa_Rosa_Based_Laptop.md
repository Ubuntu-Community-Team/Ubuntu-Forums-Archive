### Post by crichell on 2007-08-22
As is pretty well known in these forums Feisty does not support Santa Rosa 'out of the box'.  Some customers have had trouble restoring so we have re-mastered a custom Ubuntu LiveCD for Santa Rosa computers with Intel graphics.  The LiveCD has been tested against our Santa Rosa models; however, it should work for anyone with a Santa Rosa notebook and Intel graphics.  See directions and download the .iso here:

[http://knowledge76.com/index.php/Restoring_Your_System](http://knowledge76.com/index.php/Restoring_Your_System)
