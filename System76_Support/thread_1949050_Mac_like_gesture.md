### Post by isantop on 2012-03-29
To my knowledge, Ubuntu doesn't support those yet.

Here's a list of all Ubuntu Gestures currently available:

These are supported by all of our current systems:
[LIST]
[*]Two-finger drag (scrolling)
[*]Two-finger tap (Right-click)
[*]Three-finger drag (Move window)
[*]Three-finger tap (Show Resize handles)
[/LIST]

Ubuntu additionally has support for these gestures:
[LIST]
[*]Three-finger pinch/stretch (Maximize/Restore Window)
[*]Four-finger drag left/right (Hide/Reveal launcher)
[*]Four-finger tap (Show/Hide dash)
[/LIST]
