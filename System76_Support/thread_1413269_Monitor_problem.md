### Post by Schrollini on 2010-02-22
There is a keypress that will allow you to drag windows by clicking anywhere in them.  I think the default is "Alt", but you can check your settings in System > Window Preferences.  If you hold down that key, you should be able to click and drag the window upwards until the buttons you need are visible.

Alternatively, have you tried pressing Fn-F3 to change the output mode?  The resolution may change automatically once it is no longer trying to output to the external monitor.
