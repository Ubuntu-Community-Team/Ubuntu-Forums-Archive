Max Memory 	2GB (2 x SODIMM Sockets)

Yes, you can put in a 1GB and a 512M.

### Post by AusIV4 on 2007-05-19
I ordered a Core 2 Duo T7200 and 1 GB of RAM. for my Gazelle. 

The RAM arrived today. It was easy to plug in, and I never would have believed the difference it makes. Programs load faster than I'd have thought possible, beryl runs even more smoothly, I loaded up a Windows VM (allocated 512 MB for it), and the rest of the system didn't even seem to slow down. I can't wait to see what kind of difference a 2.0 Ghz Core 2 duo makes over a 1.6 Ghz Celeron M.
