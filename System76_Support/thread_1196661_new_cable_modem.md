### Post by starcannon on 2009-06-25
Look for a modem that has a browser based setup interface, and you should be golden.
My Linksys routers all came with Windows Setup CD's, but I never even looked at their contents; I just hooked up an ethernet cable, pointed my browser to 192.168.1.1 and set everything up that way. Same went for my Westel ADSL Modem/Router combo unit.
