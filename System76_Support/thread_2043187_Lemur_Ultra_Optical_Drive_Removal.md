### Post by brdmn on 2012-08-17
OK, I decided to do a bit of investigating, and I can at least tell you how to get the optical drive out.  It's quite easy and involves 5 screws.  First, you remove the main panel, which has 4 black screws; be gentle when removing the panel because the fan is attached to it.

After you move the panel just a bit to the side, remove the silver-colored screw that I've highlighted.  Now, you should be able to pull out the optical drive by opening the tray and tugging gently.

Looks like a 12.7mm caddy with a SATA interface will work.  Now, the question is, which caddy do folks here recommend?  I don't know that I want to plop down a lot of cash.  Something in the $20-$30 range would be nice, but is that unrealistic?  This one is $42:
