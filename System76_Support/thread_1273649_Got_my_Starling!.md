### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-09-24
When you get your computer, the System76 Driver will have already been installed and run.

Typically, if you find that something on your computer has stopped working (let's say wireless), the first step to take is running the driver (Install tab / Install button). Then reboot.

Also, if an update breaks something, running the driver typically will fix it.
