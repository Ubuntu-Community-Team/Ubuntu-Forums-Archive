### Post by isantop on 2010-11-30
It's pretty hard to predict our product line ahead of time, but I'm pretty sure the Lemur will have been updated by then. But not to worry, as it will probably look even better!

The quality of the webcam actually has more to do with Ubuntu than the Lemur. It does stutter when the resolution is set high, but if you turn it down, you'll see noticeably smoother video. Typically, 480p is fine for a video chat anyway, and at that resolution, the video quality is fine.
