### Post by mrchuc on 2009-12-03
> **Ian505 said:**
> 
3. What size, in comparison to a normal keyboard, is the keyboard on the Starling? Is the keyboard responsive/comfortable?


I use my Starling for notetaking in my college classes. I find it to be very easy to use. The keyboard is slightly smaller that regular laptops, but it is well designed. Even though I have somewhat fat fingers, I have no trouble at all. I touch type just like I normally do. If you compare carefully, some of the keys are narrower than normal, but those have been chosen well. Placement is still where I expect them to be without looking. 

I love my Starling--more than my Dell (duh!), even more than my Powerbook!:o

Penny for penny and pound for pound it is a great machine!
