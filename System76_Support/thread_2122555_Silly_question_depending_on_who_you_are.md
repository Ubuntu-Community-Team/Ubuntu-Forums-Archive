### Post by 3Miro on 2013-03-05
"made" is an ill-defined word in this context.

A laptop consists of many different components and unlike car manufacturers, no company makes enough components to build an entire computer. Different parts come from different places and quite likely, different countries. Probably every computer in the world has one component or another that is made in China.

I don't know where the individual parts of the System76 laptops come from. I know that they get the base system from Clevo, however, they modify the machines. The last stage, where you get your personal laptop with your selection of components, that is done in the USA, by people here (again, I don't know where the parts come form or where the base is build).
