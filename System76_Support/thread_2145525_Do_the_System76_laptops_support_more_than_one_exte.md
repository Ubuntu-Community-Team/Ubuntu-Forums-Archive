---

### Post by isantop on 2013-11-18
Mini DisplayPort will have no trouble running that; it will go up to 2560 x 1600. The HDMI might have issues; it *should* support it technically, but I've seen lots of issues with it actually working higher than 1920 x 1080. It's incredibly dependent on the monitor and cable as well; some monitors will only output super-high resolutions over DVI or DisplayPort.
