### Post by isantop on 2010-08-18
Actually, the 11 mm was intentional. We have found that the spring loaded eject mechanisms tend to fail, and it is also easier to insert and remove the card with a simple insertion device.

Swapping the readers would void your warranty, and there is a strong chance that the one from your EEE wouldn't eve fit in the Starling. I'd leave it alone.
