### Post by mionescu on 2011-10-21
I am looking to buy a replacement for my desktop which died a few days ago. The Wild Dog Performance seems a good choice for my needs. I have one question though. I have two monitors, one with a DVI connection and one with a VGA connection.
Am I going to be able to use these monitors with this pc? The specifications on the website mention only "S/PDIF Optical Out" for video.

---

### Post by isantop on 2011-10-21
The monitor outputs are determined by the Graphics Card. The GT210 and the GT430 (First two options) both have one DVI and one VGA, so you could use both monitors natively on either of those cards.
