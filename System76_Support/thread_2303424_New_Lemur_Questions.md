### Post by Ender Black on 2015-11-19
I recently purchased the Gazelle Pro.  I've been exceptionally happy with the guts of the thing - it works and System76 drivers PPA mean that I never have hiccups with the WIFI or display or anything really.  Had I seen the specs of the new Oryx line and I would have held off on my order for another month ;(  The only gripes I have with the computer is the plastic body...there is a lot of flex and I worry about it in my backpack when I'm done trekking across campus and throw my bag on a desk.  The trackpad is very accurate and smooth, but alas like every other computer I've used with linux and trackpad, the system has **** ability to detect typing and turn off the trackpad.  There is a command line option to turn off the track pad when typing, but it also disables the two finger scroll, so really a non-starter.  

If you can swing it, I'd opt for the Oryx simply for the metal body construction and backlit keys.  Otherwise, I've been happy.
