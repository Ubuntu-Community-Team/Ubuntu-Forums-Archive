### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-05-08
> Specifically, is it noisy or does the system fan make a lot of noise?
It has a power supply fan, three exhaust fans, and a fan for each CPU and an integrated fan on the nVidia graphics card. So, if you have two CPUs, you end up with a total of 7 fans. It's not a super-quiet machine, but it is a super-powerful one, able to incorporate 6 hard drives, reverse engineer alien spacecraft and leap tall buildings in a single bound. :lolflag:  Seriously though, it doesn't sound like a vacuum cleaner. The sound doesn't seem over the top to me, but it might if absolute silence was a necessity.

> And also are the nvidia cards well supported, and can it do Desktop Effects and direct rendering etc.
It uses an nVidia 8800 GTS, which is well supported and does support direct rendering.
