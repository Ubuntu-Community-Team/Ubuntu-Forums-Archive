### Post by JeQhdMD on 2015-02-04
What debugging and investigation have you done yet?

For instance:

>  Does this also happen with other browsers?
>  Does this happen on the standard Unity desktop?
>  Have you enabled (and possibly mis-configured) the Compiz screen effects?
>  What other changes have you made (not including standard updates and the usual tweaks (such as installing ubuntu-restricted-extras) . . . Have you installed the Gnome config tools (not all of which are compatible with Unity)?
>  Does any of this happen if you load a LIVE usb-stick (with a different DE such as Mate)?   An easy and fast one to try is Porteus (see the distrowatch site for info and links).   Mate uses different GPU drivers by default.
>  Have you run X Diagnostics Settings?  (invoke it via the dash)
