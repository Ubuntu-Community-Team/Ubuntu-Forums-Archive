### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-10-16
Funny you should ask. My white darter was getting pretty dirty right where the heel of my hands rest. I though I was wearing off the paint.

My wife has this dish/hand-washing soap that is dispensed as a foam. I put a little bit on a paper towl and then wrung as much out as I could. The effect was like creating a "moist wipe" or something.

Anyway, that stuff restored my darter to its old pristine beauty.

Whatever you clean the keys with, though, I'd make sure it is as dry as you can get it.
