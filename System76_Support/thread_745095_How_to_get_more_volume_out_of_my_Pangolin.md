### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-04-04
Double-click the speaker icon in the upper right. In the resulting window, go to edit > preferences. Check all available checkboxes.

This may add some sliders to your sound control that will help. For example, if it creates a PCM slider, that will increase the amplification factor.

Which Pangolin do you have? (The System76 driver will tell you.) The new Pangolins can be pretty loud, so I'm assuming here you have an older one.
