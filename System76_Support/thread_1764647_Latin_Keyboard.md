### Post by isantop on 2011-05-23
Unfortunately, we only offer standard US keyboards. However, if you use the USA International (AltGr Dead Keys) layout, then the right Alt key becomes an AltGr compose key. Then, to input ñ, simply press AltGr+Shift+`, then press n. Ñ! 

It works with other special characters, too. For example, ë is AltGr+Shift+', then e. Or á is AltGr+' then a. Some keys (like /) produce a special character directly when used with Alt. So to do ¿, you just press AltGr+/, and ¡ is AltGr+Shift+1.
