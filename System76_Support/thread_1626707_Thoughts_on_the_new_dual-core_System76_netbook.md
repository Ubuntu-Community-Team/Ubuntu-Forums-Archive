### Post by macaholic on 2010-11-21
Video playing is generally really smooth, especially with the default Totem player; I've never seen skips or anything of that nature. As long as you aren't playing HD video (which would be pointless since the resolution is 1024x600) you shouldn't run into any issues.

There is most definitely a touchpad, it's just somewhat hard to distinguish in those pictures. It's directly above the click pad, and is distinguished by the little circular bumps. It's a little different from some other touchpads, but you'll get used to it in no time.

I also agree that System76 not being tied to the arbitrary Microsoft limitations is a great thing for customization and consumer choice.

If you do get this netbook, I would highly suggest using Chromium instead of Firefox, it may take some getting used to, but the speed difference is staggering; especially on a system with an Atom processor. That being said, general web browsing is quite a nice experience on this machine.
