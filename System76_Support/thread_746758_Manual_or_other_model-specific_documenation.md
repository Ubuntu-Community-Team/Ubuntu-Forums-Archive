### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-04-29
Upgrading your memory is pretty easy.
Disconnect your battery and ac.
Remove the bottom panel (held in by 4 screws)
Remove the memory cards. Put the new ones in.
Then reverse the above steps.

If you need more help, give me a call at System76.
