### Post by a-emma on 2014-08-11
Hi hourback,

Thank you for being a valued System76 customer! You will need to purchase an adapter that fits into the mini Thunderbolt port or display port with a DVI connection on the other end. If you buy a cord with display ports on both ends, you can use a DVI adapter on one end to connect to the monitor as well. Once both are plugged in, you should see both monitors in you display settings within system settings. Please contact System76 support if you have any further questions. Our team will be glad to assist in getting your displays working just the way you'd like!
