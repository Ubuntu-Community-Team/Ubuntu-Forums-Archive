### Post by isantop on 2011-12-12
Apart from the coating, there are the slight screen differences, and the cooling/power systems. The Gazelle uses a hybrid copper/aluminum cooling system and a 6.3 Amp power supply; while they work fine for the Gazelle, they don't for the Serval, where a customer could select the 580M GPU and the 2980XM processor. There, we use a full copper cooling system and a 10 Amp power supply.
