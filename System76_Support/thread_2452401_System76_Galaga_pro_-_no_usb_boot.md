### Post by jlh68 on 2021-03-14
Try booting while pushing the appropriate key to get the BIOS.  Then go to the part where you pick the boot order. Make the USB the first choice, then the computer SSD or HHD as the second choice.  You can insert as the 2nd choice a CDROM drive, in case you have a live CD or DVD and an external Optical Drive.
After making the BIOS change Save and Exit (usually F10).  Then restart the computer with the your Live Flash Drive inserted in the USB port, and push the F10 or F12 key or what ever your computer uses for the boot choice.  When the boot list appears select the USB Drive which should be the top choice.
