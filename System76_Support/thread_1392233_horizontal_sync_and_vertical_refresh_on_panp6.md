### Post by patchwork on 2010-01-27
I have a PanP6 with the standard (not upgraded) display.  My xorg.conf file has no values for HorizSync or VertRefresh in it, so I believe it is defaulting to 28-33kHz (HorizSync) and 43-72Hz (VertRefresh), per the manual page for xorg.conf.  Obviously, if you bought the upgraded display, this may not be the case.  Hope this helps.
