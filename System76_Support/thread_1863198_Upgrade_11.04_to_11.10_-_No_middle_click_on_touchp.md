### Post by isantop on 2011-12-13
From that bug:
> Please note this bug is about physically "clicking" a clickpad with 3 fingers. 3 finger tapping is different and has been intentionally overridden in Unity 3D by new gestures.

That bug refers to touchpads where the entire surface is a button, and the whole touchpad can be pressed on and will click. Examples would include late-model Macbooks, Apple's Magic Trackpad, and the touchpads on Google Chromebooks, among others.
