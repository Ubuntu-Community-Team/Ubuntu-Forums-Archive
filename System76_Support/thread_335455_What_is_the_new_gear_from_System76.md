Short answer:  'This kind of company' is the kind that will cost more.  Until they start selling enough laptops to get better prices from vendors.  If all you're concerned about is price, then you're never going to want to buy from any vendor that emphasizes service and ease of use so much.

Its true that buying from a small company like System76 is going to cost somewhat more because they can't take advantage of economies of scale like the big names can.  This is true for *any* market. The style of Apple, without the snobbiness (and the need to pay for any software ever again).  The style and ease of use of System76 computers tell you - this is the kind of laptop that you can give to Grandma and know she'll be happy with it.
