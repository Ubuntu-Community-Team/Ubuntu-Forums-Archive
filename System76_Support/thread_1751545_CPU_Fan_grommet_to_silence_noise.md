### Post by Ranko Kohime on 2011-05-07
I'd go for the rubber grommets.  The CPU should run no more than 50-60c under normal usage, which car tires typically get up to in hard driving in hot weather.

You'll probably find (though I could be wrong) that the fan screw holes are tapped, and so pinching the fins may make it difficult or impossible to put the screws back in.

If the screws can be tightened at all, but work loose on their own, consider LockTite.  Available at most major stores in the hardware section.  Green or Blue would work, keep in mind that Red is forever.  :D
