### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-06-07
Nope.

I just called the manufacturer, and they pointed me to this:
[www.synnex.com/sid/april06/s62asg.pdf](www.synnex.com/sid/april06/s62asg.pdf)

CPU Temp isn't considered critical until it reaches 105C. It is set to shut down at 110C.

So, if your room temp is high 60's to low 70's and your are cooling your CPU with room-temp air, 73C temps are acceptable.
