### Post by jmwink on 2009-05-26
In thinking about solutions for comfortable home computing with my new Gazelle Ultra, I wondered whether or not it was possible and/or advisable to run the Gazelle Ultra with the lid closed when connected to an external LCD monitor?

---

### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-05-26
It shouldn't hurt anything to try it. My one concern would be heat retention. While the vents for air intake and exhaust are on the bottom, some heat does escape upward through the keyboard area. So you might run a *little* hot.

Also, sound might not be very good with the lid closed, as the speakers would be obstructed.
