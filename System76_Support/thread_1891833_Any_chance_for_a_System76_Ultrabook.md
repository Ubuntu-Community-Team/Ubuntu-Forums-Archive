### Post by isantop on 2011-12-07
The Lemur isn't an Ultrabook. It's too thick, and the battery life isn't long enough. However, it more than matches the performance requirements (Ultrabooks' novelty is that they offer power similar to what you find on laptops like the Pangolin and Lemur in a slim form factor and with long battery life).

EDIT: To answer the question, we are working on an UltraBook, but it's still a very long way off, and it may not even make it out. They're on our radar, but we don't have any product plans yet, as we don't have anything solid.
