### Post by psylem on 2014-11-25
I got a Bonobo for work and immediately stumbled upon issues trying to connect to a Cisco VPN (using vpnc and applicable NetworkManager plugin). Here's the bug describing what's happening here... [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/network-manager-vpnc/+bug/1297849](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/network-manager-vpnc/+bug/1297849)

One way to cope with the issue in the short term so you can get productive is simply to logout and back in again. This will fix the issue until the computer restarts.

A more permanent option is to give up on Unity and install the gnome package. Logging into any of the available Gnome desktop options doesn't seem to exhibit this issue.

Oh well, I was prepared to give Unity a go this time but the universe doesn't want me to give up on my old school ways... ;)
