### Post by jweber53 on 2017-05-12
Hi,

Mine arrived 3 days ago. It's core I7, 32 GB, SSD. I've been playing with the battery statistics available in the power applet. Sitting with the lid closed, firefox and thunderbird open but not very active, it was a little over 4W (system load .2). Lid open and on 2nd from the dimmest screen brightness and light browsing, 7-9W. Screen brighter and maxing out all 4 cores rendering fractals (mandlebulber), about 27W. The  corresponding run times agreed with the 35.3Wh rating divided by the wattage. So 4-5 hrs medium usage.

It may be that the power usage is different for the core I5. This study is not very scientific and just reflects casual observation over a few days.
