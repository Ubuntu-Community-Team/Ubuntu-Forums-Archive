### Post by him610 on 2011-01-16
There is absolutely nothing wrong with your Starling netbook. It is a good machine; however, it was not designed to do what you are asking it to do. You might considering doing some research into your system's capabilities and your application's requirements before complaining publicly.

From the Blizzard World of Warcraft website:

Recommended Specifications 

Windows® System Vista64/Windows 7 OS: 

- Dual-core processor, such as the Intel Pentium D or AMD Athlon 64 X2
- 2 GB RAM
- 256 MB NVIDIA® GeForce® 8600 or ATI Radeon™ HD 2600 or better
- Multi-button mouse with scroll-wheel


Mac® OS X 10.6.4 or newer: 

- Intel® Core™ 2 Duo processor - NVIDIA® GeForce® 9600M GT or ATI Radeon™ HD 4670 or better
- 4 GB system RAM
- Multi-button mouse with scroll wheel

For a complete list of supported 3D cards, please visit [http://us.blizzard.com/support/article/wowsupportedvideo](http://us.blizzard.com/support/article/wowsupportedvideo)

...
For what platforms is the game available? 
The game is available for PCs and Macs.

Does the game require a 3D accelerator? 
Yes, the game requires a 3D graphics card. We support a wide range of 3D cards that conform to the system requirement outlined above. Specific 3D card support information can be found here.
