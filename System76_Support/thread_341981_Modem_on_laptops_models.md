### Post by crichell on 2007-01-19
> Can anyone confirm that there is a (working) modem on the Pangolin? Do the Gazelle or Serval have modems?

We don't currently support the internal dialup modem.  The drivers available have caused some trouble for us.  However, on the Gazelle Value and Gazelle Performance you can use a PCMCIA modem for dial up.  PCMCIA modems are based on hardware and work out of the box.

> two options for optical drive "CD-RW/DVD" and "CD-RW/DVD-RW(dual layer)" ... does the first option do single-layer DVD burning? (Or can you only burn DVDs with the upgraded optical drive?)

The CD-RW/DVD burns CD's but not DVD's (only plays them).  The upgrad CD-RW/DVD-RW burns both CD's and DVD's
