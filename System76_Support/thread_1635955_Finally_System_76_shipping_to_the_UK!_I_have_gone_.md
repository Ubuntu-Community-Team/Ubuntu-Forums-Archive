The Mac is well built and well designed, however, it is my personal opinion that Apple charges way too much for the hype surrounding OS X and the Mac Line. However, the total still won't be as much as the Mac; I'd say you're probably looking at no more than £700 for a base model, and that includes the system, shipping and brokerage (You'll be charged VAT separately by your local customs office).
