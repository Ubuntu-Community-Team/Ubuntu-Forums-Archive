### Post by jml on 2011-12-11
I agree with Noah0504.  Which laptop will work better for you depends on your needs/wants.  If Battery Life is important, then go for the Lemur.  The battery lasts 3-4 hours.  The Pangolin about 2 and the other more powerful laptops about 1.5 - 2 hours.  If compact size is important, the Lemur is again a better choice both in actual size and weight.  I think that performance between the two would be comparable if configured in a similar fashion.  

Unfortunately, I am not a gamer, nor do I run Windows in a virtual machine, but logic would seem to suggest that a laptop with a dedicated graphics card (Gazelle, Serval, or Bonobo,) would have better performance than a laptop with an integrated graphics card. Hopefully Ian can weigh in on this.
