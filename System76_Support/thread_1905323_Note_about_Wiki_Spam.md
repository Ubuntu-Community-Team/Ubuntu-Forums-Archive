### Post by isantop on 2012-01-06
At System76's Knowledge Base ([http://knowledge76.com](http://knowledge76.com)), we strive to create and maintain up-to-date reference documentation on our systems and Ubuntu. However, it is constantly a challenge to keep spam content from compromising the integrity of the information. We take spam very seriously, and remove it when we see it. We also take steps to ban user accounts found to be posting spam content.

In these bans, it may be that a legitamite user account is banned, and denied access to edit or contribute information to the Knowledge Base. If you have found this is the case with you, send us an email at support...at...system76...dot...com, and we'd be happy to correct the error.

Also, if happen to find spam content on the knowledge base, please let us know so that we can remove it.

Thanks for your understanding, and sorry for any inconvenience.
