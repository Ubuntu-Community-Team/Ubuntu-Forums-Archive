### Post by isantop on 2011-03-04
Hi.

It is the date 3-years from the original ship date. We don't send any notifications, but if you email us at support...at...system76...dot...com, we can check your status for you. 

We don't offer extended warranties, but we will continue to support and repair older laptops for as long as we can get parts for them.

That said, I've noticed that with many laptops, if it hasn't failed within two years, it probably isn't going to, and you're probably going to be okay, barring an accident.
