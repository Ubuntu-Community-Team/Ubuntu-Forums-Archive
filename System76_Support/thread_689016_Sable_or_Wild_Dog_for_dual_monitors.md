### Post by thomasaaron on 2008-02-06
> I am looking to get either the Sable or Wild Dog for my office. Either one will do, but I want to know whether I can use two monitors with them both.

The Sable has 1 VGA out.
The Wild Dog has 1X DVI out and 1XVGA out (unless you upgrade the graphics card. Then it will have 2X DVI out).

> I want a Xinerama or similar setup, with one desktop stretched across both. Both monitors will be identical.

While this should be doable, we've not tested it.

> If the Wild Dog, does it matter which graphics card I get?
You'll need to upgrade for 2XDVI out. Also, make a note on the order form that you need 2XDVI out. Sometimes, if we run out of stock, we will procure parts from a different provider. Sometimes the configuration can change from manufacturer to manufacturer. So, if you make a note of it, we'll be sure to get you what you need.
> 
Oh, and are the front hookups on the Sable hidden behind a door that will end up always being open?

Yes. CD drive, USB ports, Audio jacks, card reader.
