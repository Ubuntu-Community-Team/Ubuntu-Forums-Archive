### Post by isantop on 2011-03-09
The hard drive is under the smaller removable panel on the bottom of the computer. Once you remove the panel, it should be simple enough.

The PanP5 has a maximum HDD capacity of 750 GB. Larger drives are typically thicker, and the PanP5 may not fit them.
