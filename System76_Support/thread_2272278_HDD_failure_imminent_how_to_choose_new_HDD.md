### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-04-06
Fair enough.

I that case a new drive seems to be a priority.  I can't help with makes, one against another, but you will need to make sure it is the same physical size, of course, (not in GB or TB, but in cms).  Don't forget that anything larger than 3TB needs GPT partitions and partition table.
