### Post by jml on 2010-12-03
I am not a System 76 employee, but I suspect that System 76 gets their laptop motherboards from their suppliers with the cpus already soldered in.  Just my suspicion, not based on any inside information.  One reason for a soldered cpu is size.  A cpu socket adds to the hight of the motherboard, and probably has a downstream effect on laptop design and dimensions.
