### Post by isantop on 2011-04-13
In addition to the upgrade possibilities, the Serval has a rubberized finish, which helps improve comfort. The Gazelle doesn't. The Serval also has a super glossy screen, while the Gazelle has a matte screen.

We sold out of the Pangolins. We're working on a new model and hope to have it available this month.

Unfortunately, neither the Gazelle nor the Serval have internal bluetooth capability. If you need bluetooth, I recommend using an external UDB dongle. Many of these are very small, and work very well with Ubuntu.

We don't offer docking stations for our laptops. Most customers simply plug their peripherals directly into their machines.
