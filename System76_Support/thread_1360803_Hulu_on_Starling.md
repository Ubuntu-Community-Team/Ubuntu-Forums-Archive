### Post by Lee_Machine on 2009-12-21
The issue you're having is the Starling is netbook therefore wont have the number crunching power of a tradition laptop, and flash on Linux is very poor when compared to other platforms. It's very CPU intensive on Linux. 

That should change when Flash 10.1 comes to Linux, and a lot of the processing will be done by the GPU. 

If you are the testing type of person you can try the beta2 version of it for Linux.

[http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer10.html](http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer10.html)
