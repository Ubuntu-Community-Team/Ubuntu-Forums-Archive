### Post by formol on 2008-10-26
I just received my Pangolin one week ago.  It's a powerful machine:
Code2Duo at 2,53 GHz, 4gb of RAM, 200Gb of HD at 7200 rpm.

it's a perfect computer, and the case is very solid, not like a MAC but near.

the only negative point is the ON/OFF power button, it doesn't not start at the first press or I didn't get the way to start it at the first time I press the button.

another negative point, very minor, is that there is no option during booting to select the option to boot (CD, HD, USB, etc). that's mean I to go to modify the boot order in the bios each time. 
regarding this, a question for system76 people on this forum : can we expect a bios update some day?

otherwise, it's a very good computer.  

I re-install Ubuntu, with the system76 driver.  how do I set the scroll on the right of the touchpad?

anyway, I don't regret my buy, even if UPS charge me 80$ for the customs clearance, I will remember that from UPS...
(I live in Québec, a territory inside Canada's border)

so, I firmly recommand System76
