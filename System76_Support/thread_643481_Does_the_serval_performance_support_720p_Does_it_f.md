The Serval Performance Dimensions: 14.20" x 10.43" x 1.74" (WxDxH)

Timbuk2 bag fits computers with dimensions not to exceed:
Med: 14.5" x 10.3" x 1.3" (WxDxH)
X-Lg: 15.4" x 10.5" x 1.3" (WxDxH)

So, it looks like it's too thick (1.74" vs. 1.3") but since it's a bag, I would be confident that the X-Lg bag would accomodate the Serval.
