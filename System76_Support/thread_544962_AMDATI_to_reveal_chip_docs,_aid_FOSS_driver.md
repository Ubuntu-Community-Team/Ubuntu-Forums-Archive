### Post by DRM_free on 2007-09-07
AMD/ATI has [committed]("http://www.phoronix.com/scan.php?page=article&item=826&num=1") to helping the X.org developers write fully-capable Free/Open Source drivers for its graphics cards. Would System76 consider offering laptops with AMD CPUs and  integrated ATI graphics cards?

It's very disappointing from a Free Software perspective to see that you only offer NVIDIA graphics for your full-size desktops (Ratel Value, Sable Performance, Wild Dog Performance, and Wild Dog Professional). Intel and now AMD are orders of magnitude friendlier to open source development, and there are so many great motherboards with integrated Intel and ATI graphic cards to choose from! I urge you to reconsider your NVIDIA-only policy.

It's up to vendors like you to put pressure on NVIDIA, and selling their hardware when they have been so anti-open source is sending the wrong message.
