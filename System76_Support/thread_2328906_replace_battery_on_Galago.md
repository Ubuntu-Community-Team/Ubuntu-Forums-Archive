### Post by h.thomas on 2016-12-04
For the record, System76 was happy to sell me a battery, though the links suggested above would have been a cheaper option.  Replacing it went fine.  It isn't something I would recommend to someone completely un-handy -- the first time I put it back together after replacing the battery, I realized I had done it wrong.  The battery fits easily, but there are screws which have to go through the battery case and then screw into the laptop case, which I found a little tricky.  Anyway, though, this was my first time ever opening up a laptop, so probably anyone else who would consider trying this would be able to do it too.
