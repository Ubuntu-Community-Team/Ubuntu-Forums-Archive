### Post by thomasaaron on 2007-12-10
Try removing your battery and a/c adapter and letting the computer sit for a few minutes. Then plug it all back in. This might have a re-set effect on your system.

If that doesn't fix it, your battery may be getting worn out. What kind of readings do you get from your battery icon? Does it seem to be charging up? What kind of battery life does it report?
