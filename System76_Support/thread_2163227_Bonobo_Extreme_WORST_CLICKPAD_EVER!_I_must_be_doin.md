So I got the Bonobo Extreme a couple months ago. I love everything about this computer EXCEPT this HORRIBLE clickpad. I am really hoping this is something I can fix.

To begin I will explain how I used a clickpad on a Macbook before the Bonobo Extreme.

My resting position was thumb resting on lower left corner of clickpad and my index finger on the clickpad and my middle finger hovers over the clickpad.

To move mouse: Move index finger while keeping thumb still
To left click: Press thumb down
To scroll: lower middle finger on to clickpad and use two finger scroll
To right click: tap clickpad with index and middle finger.

This worked for me. I never used a mouse because I actually preferred the clickpad on my Macbook.

Now on the Bonobo Extreme those positions do not work at all because resting your thumb on the lower left corner of the clickpad and trying to move with the index finger is considered a two finger scroll, so this breaks my entire existing clickpad habits.

So I thought "ok ... new laptop, new habits, there must be a different way to use this clickpad effectively."

So I tried not resting my thumb on it but hovering it over and putting it down and pressing when I want to click. However since where you "press down" on the clickpad is still part of the "moving" area the mouse slides to the left due to putting my thumb down as well as the uncontrollable "roll" of my thumb skin as I apply pressure makes precision clicking impossible. More often it actually highlights entire rows of text when I just want to change the cursor position.

So then I thought "ok, well maybe I just won't click with my thumb at all. Sure highlighting will be impossible to do with just tapping ... but I'll figure it out." Well that would require tapping to work.

So when I try to just tap the clickpad to register a mouse click it is also unable to be precise, just the motion of rising my finger causes the mouse to jump then when I come back down again the mouse jumps again and when I raise it to finish the tap it jumps once more. That is only for a single click. Good luck getting a double click to work at the place you are trying to click!

Now I don't see anyone else complaining about the clickpad so I am praying I just have some config line somewhere leading to my sorrows.

Please help!
