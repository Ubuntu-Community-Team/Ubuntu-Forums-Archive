### Post by rmeineke on 2011-02-13
I have had my Pangolin (PanP7) for almost exactly one year now.  Glare was a huge concern for me prior to ordering.  I consulted with a few friends who have glossy-screen notebooks and they reported that glare was not much of an issue for them.  

I took the plunge and haven't regretted a moment with my Pangolin.  About the only time I ever even notice the glare is when the system is booting up.  Once the display is lit up it really isn't an issue.  An occasional tweak of the screen angle is all that is needed when I adjust my standard slouch to something more upright.  I only ever use the computer indoors so your mileage may vary.

I still **love** this computer.  I just went from Karmic to Maverick last week and the upgrade was flawless following System76's directions.
