### Post by PabloH on 2010-11-18
I have a slightly older Serval with the Nvidia GTX 285M, a Q820 processor, 8GB, and the Intel 160 SSD. It puts out little heat when idling along. When you fire up the processor and the GPU at the same time, it is a nice heater. I have never had the machine get so hot that it came anywhere near shutting down. However, it is way to hot to put in your lap. 

I get an hour out of the battery with moderate usage. The HD playback with the nvidia driver is nice.

The machine is well built.
