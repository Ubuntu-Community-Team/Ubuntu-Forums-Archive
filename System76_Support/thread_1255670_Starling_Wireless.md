### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-09-02
There is a little spring-loaded toggle switch on the front, right corner of your computer.

If you ever turn your Starling on and the wireless LED doesn't come on, press that switch *once* to the right. Then reboot, and your computer. The LED should then be on.
