### Post by Ebonwumon on 2011-06-27
I can see where thoughts of cheap could be applied to the keyboard. Let me stress this first, I very much enjoy the typing experience on the keyboard. I wrote about it in [my review](http://blag.depotwarehouse.net/2011/02/system76-lemur-ultrathin-review.html), but the tl;dr is yes, the keyboard feels a little cheap. I love typing on it, and the size of the keys is very nice, but cheap is one word that I might describe it with.

The touchpad has two finger touch. Beyond that, I'm not really sure. Maybe the hardware supports it, but AFAIK there are no linux multitouch driver.

### Post by isantop on 2011-06-27
There currently isn't support for more than two fingers in the touchpad drivers, except for some very particular touchpads, like Apple's Magic Trackpad. There is support in the hardware, though, and there is expected to be support for 11.10 in October.
