### Post by Lee_Machine on 2009-12-30
The drag, and drop method will not work. The music files must be encrypted, and hashed in a specific way so they will show up.

The iPhone, and iPod Touches once jailbroken you may SSH into them, and then drag, and drop. Still the music files will not show up under iPod. Another program must be installed. PwnPlayer is a popular one. 

It's really not worth the hassle, just use iTunes. :lolflag:
