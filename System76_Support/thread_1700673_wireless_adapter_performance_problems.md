### Post by rlowrance on 2011-03-05
I am running a System 76 Leopard Extreme system. The wireless adapter does not perform well. The adapter is an Atheros AR5008 using driver ath9k.

The adapter disconnects from the wireless network frequently and must be reconnected. When connected, the panel at the top of the sceen shows two bars, even though the wireless base station is about 2 feet away from the antennas on the card.

The wireless base station is an Apple Timecapsule. Apple laptops all connect to it and show 4 bars when booted into OS X. When I boot one of them into Ubuntu 10.10 it gets similarly poor performance with the same wifi card.

I suspect the problem is in either the wifi cards or the linux drivers for them.

Has anyone else had this problem? Is there a fix?

### Post by scarey9 on 2011-03-06
I was having a similar issue(except for the disconnecting). I found that my Panp7 with the top of the line intel card has reception issues when connecting to a 5GHZ 802.11n network. When i switched the network to 2.4GHZ my reception greatly improved. I am on vacation right now and i went ahead and tested this on my parents Apple Time Capsule and found the same to be true. 802.11n @ 5ghz 2 bars, @2.4ghz full bars. Hope this helps.
