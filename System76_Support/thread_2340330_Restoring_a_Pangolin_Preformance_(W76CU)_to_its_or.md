### Post by MoebusNet on 2016-10-20
While I do not know which version of *buntu your System76 originally came with, it is quite possible your missing driver was originally supplied by the System76 additional software software selection. Those drivers were usually incorporated into the next *buntu version (possibly in the kernel), so you will probably be best off with the newest *buntu version that worked for you.
