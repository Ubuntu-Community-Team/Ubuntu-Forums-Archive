### Post by him610 on 2013-12-21
hello watchpocket,

It is entirely possible your RAM may already be maxed out. Your wilp6, as you stated, dates back to 2009 which makes it a five year old computer.  Five years is a looong time in the lifespan of a computer. I searched the system76 support pages for wilp6 specifications. The wilp6 for one reason or another is not listed; however, the wilp7 which I assume is the successor to the wilp6 maxes out at 16GB  DDR3 RAM.  When computers are designed and manufactured, they are designed to the available specifications common at the time of design. In other words, the wilp6 was already obsolete the day it shipped. This is one of the byproducts of "Moore's Law."

I hope this addresses your issues.
