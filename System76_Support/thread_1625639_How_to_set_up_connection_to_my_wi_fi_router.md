### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-11-19
You may not need to do it that way at all.  Click on the network manager icon in the panel and available networks wlll be listed.  Click on your own SSID and enter the security password/phrase and you may find that you are then connected.

If that is not successful, come back again and we'll try to help further.

### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-11-19
Yes,  you can plug it in and use the cable connection; it will probably be found without any input from you, and it may well be faster, though if your netbook is close to the router the difference may not be too great.

Try it out; it won't make any difference to the wireless connection when you later disconnect the cable.
