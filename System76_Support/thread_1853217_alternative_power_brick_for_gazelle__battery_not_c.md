### Post by isantop on 2011-11-21
That Kensington charger looks like it would be a good match for a charger for the Pangolin or Lemur, but it isn't quite beefy enough to handle the Gazelle or Serval. The maximum output at 19V on that charger is ~90 Watts, while the Gazelle uses a ~120 Watt supply. The Serval needs a ~200 Watt supply, particularly if it's equipped with the extreme edition processor and/or GTX 580M GPU.
