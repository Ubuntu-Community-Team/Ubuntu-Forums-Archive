### Post by thomasaaron on 2009-12-31
> Does anyone know what name brand hard drives are used in the pangolin?

We only use the higher-quality brands. Right now I think it is either Seagate or Hitachi. We switch things up fairly often to give customer the best bang for their buck. If you ping me when you're ready to buy, I'll let you know what to expect. 

> Also do they come with restore disks or at least the modified driver pack system76 makes on the system?

We do not provide a restore CD with our systems. We recommend our customers occasionally download a free, current Ubuntu image from...

[http://ubuntu.com/downloads](http://ubuntu.com/downloads)

This CD image can be used both for re-imaging your system or for data retrieval in most cases.

We install (basically) standard Ubuntu, plus our System76 Driver. The System76 Driver is an open source Python application that detects which version of Ubuntu you are running and applies the appropriate, model-specific patches to our systems. It is available for free download from our repositories.
