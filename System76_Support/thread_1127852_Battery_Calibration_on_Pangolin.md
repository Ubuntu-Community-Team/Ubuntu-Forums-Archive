### Post by bill516 on 2009-04-17
"Calibration" seems to occur as you use the battery.  I used mine through abut 5 cycles and it stabilized at a projection of around 2 hours, which seems accurate.

To some extent this will vary with how your machine is configured and how you use it.  Given the size of the battery, however, I would be surprised if even a power-frugal person got more than 2:15 to 2:20 out of it.

Nice machine (indeed!) if you operate on line power most of the time.  Not the best machine for long periods of time sprawled on the carpet at your local airport using battery and wireless.
