### Post by mvaniersel on 2006-01-27
I have a dual monitor set up (normal, not xinerama), and a while ago my taskbar, workspace switcher and "show desktop" button disappeared from the panel on one of the two monitors. How can I make these reappear? 

Here is how it happened: I wanted to move a firefox window from one monitor to the other, and since firefox doesn't like it when there are two instances running, I closed the firefox window in one monitor and directly openened in the other. When I do this, the following errors appear:

The Application "wnck-applet" has quit unexpectedly.
"Workspace Switcher" has quit unexpectedly
"Window List" has quit unexpectedly

It then gives you the option to "reload" or "don't reload". One time I accedintally clicked "don't reload", and the panel has remained empty ever since, even when I restart the computer. How can I fix this?

And I probably should file a bug report about this. How do I do that?

---

### Post by stalefries on 2006-01-27
Right click the panel. Click add to panel. Now look through that menu, find your missing items, and drag and drop them to your bottom panel. Right click each item and select move to put each in its proper place.
