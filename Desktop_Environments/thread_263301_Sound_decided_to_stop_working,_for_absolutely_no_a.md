### Post by mgmiller on 2007-03-26
I have had similar, but not exactly the same, mysterious sound and NIC issues like this.  But it only happened when I tried booting off an alpha or beta Ubuntu live CD or another distros live CD (where everything worked btw) and then on restarting my regular Ubuntu on the HDD, poof, no sound or nic, etc.  The live CD's made a hardware level change on the built in sound and nic on my mobo.  I had to go into BIOS and turn off the items in question, save the BIOS changes and reboot and then go back into the BIOS and turn them back on.  This has always solved this problem for me.

### Post by kaffekopp on 2007-03-26
This probably isn't any help, but I thought I had the same problem as you guys - one day, no sound. And no apparent reason. I thought I'd checked the sound mixer too. But when I checked the sound mixer now, I found that although the Master volume was all the way up, the PCM volume was turned completely off. Turned it up, and now it's working.
