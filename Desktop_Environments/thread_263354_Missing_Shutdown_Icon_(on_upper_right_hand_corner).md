### Post by fstab on 2006-09-23
You can move stuff around in the panel by right-clicking it and selecting 'Move'.  You might have to create space for the quit button by moving other panel buttons away from that corner.  If 'Move' isn't available when you right click something on the panel, unselect "Lock to panel" and then you should be able to move it.
