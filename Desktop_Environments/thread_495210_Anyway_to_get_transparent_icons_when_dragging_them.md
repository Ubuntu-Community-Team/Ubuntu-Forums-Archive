### Post by isecore on 2007-11-28
You're not alone. I'm trying to figure this out as well. So far I've found plenty of ancient threads and bug-reports and various other paraphernalia over at among other places Gnome.org but also at other distro forums. Most of them imply that without detecting a composite window manager (i.e. Compiz Fusion, etc) it won't work.

But like I said, most of these threads are about two years old. There's a bugreport on this ([http://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=47897](http://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=47897)) that's been open since 2001 (!) and the last comment was made in 2005.

The icons are very nice, but it's annoying how they obscure where you're dragging them. Dragging an icon onto another icon is a virtual impossibility - the only workaround I've found is dragging the icon by it's name, that allows you to peek where you're dropping it.

But I sure wish that translucent icons would become a reality.
