### Post by azurfire on 2009-10-16
Does anyone know if there is a way to get the Windows7 winkey+arrowkey bindings? Preferably with support for two monitors?

For anyone who doesn't know:

winkey+up = maximize
winkey+down = minimize, or restore from maximize
winkey+left = fill left side of screen, or restore from right side
winkey+right = fill right side of screen, or restore from left side

When using two monitors:
winkey+left when the window is already on the left side of the left monitor would wrap it around to the right side of the right monitor, etc.
