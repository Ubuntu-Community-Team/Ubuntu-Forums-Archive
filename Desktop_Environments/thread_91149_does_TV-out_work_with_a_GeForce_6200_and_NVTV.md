### Post by pizpot on 2008-02-15
To wrap up this old thread, I have the same thing with 7.10 and an nvidia 8600. But, I don't need nvtv to get my tv to work with the laptop as 2nd screen. All I had to do was turn on the tv, plug it into the laptop, turn the laptop on, run "sudo nvidia-settings" and use the nice program to enable the TV and log out/in and its good. :-)
