### Post by bayr00t on 2007-11-16
Solved! In Compiz's "Place windows" I disabled "Workarounds" by unchecking it, and selected CASCADE "Placement mode". Then I started all the programs I had problems with, maximised them, restarted computer, and now it works just fine. ;)
