### Post by BarkingPerci on 2007-01-02
Hi there - apparently this is a common problem.  This happened to me a couple weeks ago and ended with me wiping my HD and trying a few other distros (figured I had broke the mouse).  

I came back to xubuntu and it was great till this happened again.  Thankfully I think I found the answer.  If you search for "xubuntu panels gone" you'll find many threads on this such as: [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=322589&highlight=xubuntu+no+panels](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=322589&highlight=xubuntu+no+panels)

From which I learned that a quick Alt+F2 (Run prompt) type "xfce4-panel"

This should bring your panels back.  However, there are still problems with the session manager and the settings aren't saved as a result.  Not sure if it is the reason for my problem being fixed, but I went ahead and created a new user and BOOM it's all back to normal again.
Wish I knew why that worked, but you give it a try.  (if anyone knows if/why this is what fixed it for me please let us know)
