### Post by dabl on 2010-12-16
Right-click the "cashew" in the upper right corner of your screen, and then click "unlock widgets".  Then right-click the cashew again and choose "Add widget". From the list or banner of widgets, find the "folder view" widget, and add it.

Don't forget to "Lock Widgets" when you are done.  Most people lose their widgets because they fail to lock them.  :D
