### Post by H.E. Pennypacker on 2006-08-30
If you are a fairly new Linux user, I'd suggest against using Compiz.  In reality, I don't know anyone who uses Compiz without AIGLX/XGL (not even sure what the purpose of Compiz is without either one).  

If you really want to go for it, you'd have to get your hands dirty, unfortunately.  But then again, whether I'd be using Windows or Linux, I always got my hands dirty reading about different ways to modify stuff or fixing problems of all types.
