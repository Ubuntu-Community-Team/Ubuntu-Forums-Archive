### Post by sxtz on 2006-04-18
It's fairly easy to take just the title bar from a theme..
all you have to do is:

1) go to the theme selection either by...
... clicking through the menu: System -> Preferences -> Themes
... or running (by pressing hotkey Alt-F2): gnome-theme-manager 

2) once there, click the "Theme Details" button..


There are 3 separate tabs there, for the Window Border (select Crux, in your case), for the Window Controls (select "Human," as you said) and for the Icon Theme. 

Hope this helps :)
