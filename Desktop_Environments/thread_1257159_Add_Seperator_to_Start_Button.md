### Post by mcduck on 2009-09-03
Unlock all the applets that are between the separator and the place where you want to move it. Just right-click the applets and make sure "Lock to Panel" is unselected.

To unlock the Window List-applet you need to click on the handle at the far left end of that applet (as clicking on tasks themselves would only bring up me nu for that window, not the applet).
