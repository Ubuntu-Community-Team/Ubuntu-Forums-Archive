### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2015-09-24
While the drag and drop option may not be available to you, there is a way to reorder the quick launch icons if that is what you referring to.  Right click any open space on your LXPanel and choose Add/Remove Panel items.  This will open a new window.  Choose Application Launch Bar and then edit.  On the left side of the window you will see the applications that are on the Application Launch Bar.  Move them up or down according to your desires.  Once you are done, merely close the window.  Hopefully that is what you are looking for.
