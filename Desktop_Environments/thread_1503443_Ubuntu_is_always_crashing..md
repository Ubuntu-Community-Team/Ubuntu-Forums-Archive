### Post by an0dos on 2010-06-06
There are a few issues here. 256MB of RAM is not very much. You may want to consider running a leaner desktop environment than the standard gnome desktop.  You may want to install lubuntu-desktop or xubuntu-desktop or just download the lubuntu or xubuntu live cd and do a fresh OS install. 

You actually don't need to download the flash player from adobe's site. Just go to the Ubuntu software center and download "ubuntu restricted extras" or "xubuntu restricted extras" depending on your desktop environment.

In Ubuntu you typically will install new software through the software center, which should be at the bottom of the list of applications when you click on the Ubuntu logo at the top-left of your screen. 

BTW, RAM is very inexpensive. You may want to consider upgrading to 1GB.
