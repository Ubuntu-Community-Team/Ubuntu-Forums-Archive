### Post by CameO73 on 2007-04-30
That huge speaker display is 'Feisty-standard' (unfortunately). I did notice one thing: if you change the volume during login, it shows the small one. During that session, the small speaker display is shown (until the next reboot, that is).

More info about this 'problem' can be found in [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=399973"). It is related to the composite mode (if you used or have used Compiz/Beryl, you're basically stuck with the big speaker display).
