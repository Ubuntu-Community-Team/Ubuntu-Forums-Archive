### Post by Zeroedout on 2005-06-21
The easiest way I know of is to select the video file, right click it and hit properties ---> open with and select mplayer from the list (if it is not on the list, right click the file, hit open with other application, and select mplayer, then it will appear on the list). Then do the same with XMMS.
