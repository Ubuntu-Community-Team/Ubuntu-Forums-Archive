### Post by denham2010 on 2007-10-26
Hi,

It's quite easy to do. This is how it is done in Gnome. I am not sure how it is done in KDE, but there will be an option for adding applets to the panel in KDE settings somewhere.

Right click on the panel and select "Add To Panel"
Select the "Drawer" applet and click "Add"

Now if you right click on the drawer on your panel, you can customise it (colours icons etc) by selecting "Preferences", and you can add launchers or bookmarks by selecting "Add To Drawer"
