[QUOTE=scottish144]I recently upgraded to Dapper (from Breezy) and during the upgrade process Breezy reported that the "trash" application has quit unexpectantly. First right click on the bottom panel, click add to panel, and add the "Thrash" applet. It should reapper on the panel, right click it and select move to move it to the desired place. If other applets are locked to the panel and you can't move the thrash applet because of that, just right click on them and unlock them.
If it still doesn't work, open synaptic, search for gnome-applets and reinstall (right click/reinstall package).
