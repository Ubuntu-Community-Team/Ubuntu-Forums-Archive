May I ask why you filed this under "[lubuntu]", which is yet another DE to mix with!?

Anyway, you might want to have a look at this:

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unity_%28user_interface%29]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unity_%28user_interface%29")
