### Post by mspacek on 2012-09-06
As of xfce 4.10, You can indeed manually reorder tasklist buttons:

[https://bugzilla.xfce.org/show_bug.cgi?id=7058](https://bugzilla.xfce.org/show_bug.cgi?id=7058)

To enable it, in the properties for the tasklist, choose "allow drag and drop" or whatever it's called. Off the top of my head, to get to the tasklist properties, you have to right click the panel, choose edit panel, then right click the edge of the window tasklist (should be surrounded by a red border), and click properties or something.

Not sure why it isn't enabled by default.
