### Post by pjbright on 2005-05-03
There is no "easy" way, unfortunately - both Mepis and Ubuntu/Kubuntu use a (different) customized kernel based on Debian.  I am a recent convert from Mepis, and tried this myself.  Still, worth the download of the iso - Ubuntu seems to offer better hardware detection, as well as a community with better support - IMHO.
