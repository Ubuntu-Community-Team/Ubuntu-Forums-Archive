### Post by varunus on 2005-08-03
Nautilus can have paths written directly into it.  Nautilus has two modes:  spatial (similar to macos system 9, default) and browser (similar to konqueror/windows explorer).  To activate browser mode, open up a nautilus window, go to edit->preferences, go to behavior, and choose "always open in browser windows".  Here's a screenshot of nautilus in browser mode.
