### Post by 3Miro on 2011-04-10
Mouse and keyboard issues are associated with Xorg not the DE. Try disconnecting your keyboard and the connecting it back. I have seen this help reset some issues.

There may be a bug with 11.04, always check for bugs known for your hardware and always test Linux with a LiveCD before you install. This counts double if you are dealing with beta releases as both Gnome 3 and Unity are.
