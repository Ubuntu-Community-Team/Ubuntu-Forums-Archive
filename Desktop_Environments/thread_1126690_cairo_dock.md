### Post by LIB53 on 2009-04-15
I think you go to System>Preferences>Startup Applications. Then click "add" and for name put Cairo Dock, and for command put cairo-dock, and then for comment, put something like "a linux dock" (this doesn't matter). Then click "add".

Edit: I don't think "Startup Applications" is in Intrepid. I have it in Jaunty. I suppose you could get Ubuntu Tweak and add the startup entry. Sorry for not being very helpful.
