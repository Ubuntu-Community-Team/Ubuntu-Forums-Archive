### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2009-06-26
You can do absolutely no harm by doing a fresh re-install. You'll have a bunch of updates to do all over again, and it's probably better to find out what settings need to be changed and just change them... but if you lose patience and just want to start over again from scratch (don't feel bad either - I've done it a few times, and I bet there's more than just two of us that have), you don't hurt a thing. You will lose data, so back up documents, songs, pictures, etc onto a CD or something first.

If you create a separate */home* partition during the install, that is where your e-mail settings, address book, browser bookmarks, documents, songs, etc all go. Just un-check the "format this partition" and all that stuff will be preserved (back it up before a fresh install *anyway* - you never know).

-Robin
