### Post by Lunar_Lamp on 2006-08-30
I think you want to try:

sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg

This will attempt to reconfigure your xorg.conf, and I think you can sort out refresh rates etc in one of the more advanced sections of the setup process.

REMEMBER - backup your current xorg.conf in case it all goes wrong!
