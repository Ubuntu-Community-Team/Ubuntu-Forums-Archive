### Post by manubreizh on 2006-02-14
hi,
just for katapult : to have it working, you must launch it once (using alt+F2 and type : "katapult", then press enter and it's launched) ; then you can use it with the alt+space keys (press alt and space), you'll have katapult splash screen, then you use the keyboard to spell the name of the app or bookmark you want it to open/launch. for example, you want to launch krita : press alt+space and type krita then press enter. katapult will propose you the differents apps (bookmarks) matching the letters you type (like autocompletion in google search bar), when it proposes the good one press enter and it's done.
the explaination is rather long, but the use is fast and rather easy
hope that helped
