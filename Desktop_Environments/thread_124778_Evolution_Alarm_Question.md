The current Breezy Evolution won't notify you of missed alarms when it starts - the previous Hoary version did!

I believe enough people have complained to (hopefully) have the Evolution developers put this feature back in a later version, but we might have to wait for the Dapper upgrade to get it back.......

Put Evolution in your System-Preferences-Sessions-Startup Programs.
