### Post by bloom on 2011-09-04
hi Luca,
I had the same problem with the canon 600d. I resolved unmounting the camera from nautilus. That's why if the camera is mounted like usb mass storage it can't be seen like ptp camera from digikam.
tip: from camera menu put off the auto shutdown when it's connected to pc. if camera shuts down (tipically after 30 sec) you lost connection and you can't download photos.
Hope it helps. Sorry for bad English.

bloom
