### Post by Jucato on 2006-03-12
Taskbar - the part of the panel that shows open windows. To get it back, right click on an empty space on the Panel, choose Add Applet to Panel, and look for Taskbar

System Tray - the part of the panel which shows apps running in the background (like Superkaramba, aMSN, etc). Right-click on Panel > Add Applet > System Tray.

Hope that helps.
