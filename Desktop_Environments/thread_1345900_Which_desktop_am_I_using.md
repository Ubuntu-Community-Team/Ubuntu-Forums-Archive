### Post by apmcd47 on 2009-12-04
Does your splash screen say "ubuntu" or "kubuntu" when you start Linux? Is the default colour scheme brown/orange or blue? Is your "start" menu at the top left or bottom left of the screen? If your answer to all the questions above is the first one you are probably using Gnome, else you are probably using KDE.

If your default file manager is called dolphin you are probably using KDE.

Google search for the Gnome and KDE home pages and look at the screen shots. See which look most like your environment.
