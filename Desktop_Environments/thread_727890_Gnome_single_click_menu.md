### Post by erginemr on 2008-03-19
I believe it is much more logical for Gnome to behave this way. You right-click on the screen and bring up the pop-up menu, but then, change your mind and click somewhere else to dismiss it. The other way around would lead to unintentional clicks, thereby resulting in a counter-productive environment.

So, with all due respect to your preferences, I don't see the point of changing that behavior...
