### Post by rivalarrival on 2007-09-11
on the bottom right corner, just to the left of the trash can should be a small rectangle. If you let your mouse sit on it, it will say "Switch between workspaces"

Right click that rectangle and click "Preferences"

Make sure the "Show workspaces in (a number) rows" box is checked, and choose at least 2 workspaces.
