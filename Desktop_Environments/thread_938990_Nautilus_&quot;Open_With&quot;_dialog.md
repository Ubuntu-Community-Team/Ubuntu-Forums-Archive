### Post by inigomontoya on 2008-10-05
I've made a mess of my "open with" dialog with custom commands and I would like to know where the list of applications is stored so that I may clean it up some.

edit: I figured it out myself. The custom applications listed in the "open with" dialog are located in ~/.local/share/applications.  They are .desktop shortcuts that can be removed or edited as necessary.
