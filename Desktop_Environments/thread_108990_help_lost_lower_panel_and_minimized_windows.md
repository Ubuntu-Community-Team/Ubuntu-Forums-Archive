### Post by varunus on 2005-12-27
Right click the lower panel, choose "add to panel", and choose "window list".  This will add the taskbar back.  Also, you can right-click the little handle at the left, choose preferences, and then  choose whether you want windows from all desktops or just the current desktop to show up.

(If you're not familiar with virtual desktops, the set of four squares you see at the bottom right is called the pager; you can pretend you have four monitors, and click to switch.  You have to set up the taskbar to show you all desktops at once though by right clicking.)
