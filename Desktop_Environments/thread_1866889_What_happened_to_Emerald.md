### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-10-22
It seems Emerald is not maintained anymore and a substitute is under development. However, you can install the last compatible version from a ppa divulged by [webupd8](http://www.webupd8.org/2011/05/get-emerald-to-work-in-ubuntu-1104.html). It is for Ubuntu 11.04, but it also works on 11.10.

Instead of installing the ppa, download the deb from [https://launchpad.net/~malteworld/+archive/compiz](https://launchpad.net/~malteworld/+archive/compiz)

Keep in mind it has some issues and shouldn't be very stable. So, use it at your own risk. That being said, I am running it without major problems for a few days.
