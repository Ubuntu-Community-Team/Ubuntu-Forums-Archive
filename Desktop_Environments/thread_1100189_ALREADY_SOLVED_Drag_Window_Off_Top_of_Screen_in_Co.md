Hello All,

I was having a problem in Compiz where I couldn't drag windows above the top of the desktop.  i.e. the top of the monitor acted like a "wall" and I couldn't drag a window so the top half was off the screen.  I searched around on Google and saw posts where other people had the same problem, but they were all exclusive mailing lists or otherwise closed topics, so I am posting my solution here to better the Ubuntu community :0

[LIST=1]
[*]Open a terminal
[*]type ccsm
[*]Scroll ALL THE WAY to the bottom to the plugin "Move Window" and click it
[*]Uncheck "Constain Y"
[*]Presto!
[/LIST]

I hope this helps someone who couldn't otherwise figure it out.
