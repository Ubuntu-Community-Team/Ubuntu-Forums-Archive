### Post by germanix on 2010-11-27
Just right click on the bottom panel. Choose "add to panel", a window will open. Search for the bin and click on it. The bin should now appear on the panel. Right click on the bin and choose "move" then move it to where you want, then right click again and choose "lock to panel"
