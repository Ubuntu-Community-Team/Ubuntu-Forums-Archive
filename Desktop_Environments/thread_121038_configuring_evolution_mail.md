### Post by manicka on 2006-01-24
In Evolution go to the Edit menu and choose 'Preferences'. In the Mail Accounts section click on the 'Add' button and an account wizard should start. You should find the wizard fairly easy and straight forward to follow

This page also contains guides on the type of info you need to enter into the wizards's fields to get this working 

[http://help.yahoo.com/help/us/mail/pop/pop-03.html](http://help.yahoo.com/help/us/mail/pop/pop-03.html)
