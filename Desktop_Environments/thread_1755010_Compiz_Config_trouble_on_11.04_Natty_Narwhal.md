### Post by coffeecat on 2011-05-10
You need to be careful with compizconfig-settings-manager in Unity. Unity itself is a compiz plugin and there are some incompatibilities with other compiz plugins. As far as the cube is concerned, here is a workaround:

[http://ubuntu4beginners.blogspot.com/2011/05/enable-desktop-cube-in-unity-ubuntu.html](http://ubuntu4beginners.blogspot.com/2011/05/enable-desktop-cube-in-unity-ubuntu.html)

Having warned you about potential problems, I have to say that the ccsm changes I've tried were OK. But it might be an idea to backup your ccsm settings as described in that link before embarking on anything too ambitious in the compiz line.

Good luck!
