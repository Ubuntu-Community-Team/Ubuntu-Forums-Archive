### Post by sniper8752 on 2020-11-18
I am trying to view thumbnails on a smb share, but I just get a generic image.  When I view images on my local desktop, the thumbnail shows the image.  How do I get the previews to show for each file listed?

---

### Post by SeijiSensei on 2020-11-18
From the menu, choose Configure Dolphin (or hit Ctrl+Shift+,), then on the General page choose Previews.  Increase the size in the Skip Previews box at the bottom. I have mine set to 10240 MB (~ 10 GB).
