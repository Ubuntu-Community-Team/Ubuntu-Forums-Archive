### Post by mrmaster on 2010-04-08
Hello,

I have a problem where my apps minimize into the workspace and do not show up on the bottom panel. So it "looks" like I have no apps on my screen. The only way I can bring them up is to use alt+tab. Can anyone please let me know how I can make the minimize feature make it go into the bottom panel instead.

Thank you

---

### Post by mcduck on 2010-04-08
Right-click on the panel, select "Add to Panel", find the "Window List"-applet and drag&drop it into your panel. That should solve the problem. :)

---

### Post by mrmaster on 2010-04-08
