[QUOTE=Digitallysick]I have installed using synaptic, how do i use it? when i restart, gnome still comes up, how can i switch between to the 2?? ideas??[/QUOTE]
 If you're using GDM, click on the Sessions button, or hit F10 and select "Sessions" and select XFCE, then enter your username and password.
It'll ask you whether you want this to be a Single Session change or if you want XFCE to be your new default.
Pick the answer which suits your fancy and be on your merry way :)
