### Post by varunus on 2005-08-15
1.  Open up a window, go to edit-preferences, go to the behavior tab, and choose "always open in browser windows"; this makes nautilus much more like conqueror in look and feel.

2.  Open up synaptic (system->administration->synaptic) and install "nautilus-open-terminal".  You'll need the backports repositories enabled to do this, instructions here:
[http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#extrarepositories](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#extrarepositories)
