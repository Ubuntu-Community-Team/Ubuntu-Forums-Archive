Hi,

I prefer the Gnome environment of Ubuntu.  But I use amarok and k3b which are KDE apps.  I have successfully installed them.

I find that the look and feel of these are too different from other apps.  On KDE I used to use gtk-qt engine.  It made GTK apps look like KDE ones.  Now I want the opposite of it.  I want Qt apps to look like GTk ones.  How do I do it?

---

### Post by NeoChaosX on 2007-01-27
Unfortunately, there's no counterpart to gtk-qt for Qt. Your best bet is to find a Qt theme that best matches your GTK theme, or use a theme that has both Qt and GTK versions, like QtCurve.
