### Post by cas118 on 2007-02-11
I just had the same problem today.

I think it's caused by switching between cube, desktop wall, and the classic workspace settings.

Anyway, I've just posted some straightforward instruction on my [blog]("http://ccgi.maxpower.plus.com/2007/02/11/beryl-cube-doesnt-work-with-the-workspace-switcher/") on how to 'jiggle' the settings to get it to work.

The key thing here is to make sure that *Number of Desktops* is set to 1, *Horizontal Virtual Size* is set to 4, and then you change the vertical settings away and back to 1. You should see the workspace switcher change as you perform this last step.

I hope this helps!
