### Post by dodo3773 on 2012-09-22
You issue is not as odd as you might think. Many people experience many bugs with compositing window managers. In the end I just switched to openbox and never looked back. Couldn't be happier. This is from a year ago but it may be worth taking a look at:

 [http://forum.kde.org/viewtopic.php?f=111&t=92993](http://forum.kde.org/viewtopic.php?f=111&t=92993)


My recommendation to you is to go in and disable effects one at a time until you find the problem one or turn them all off and enable them one at a time until you find the problem one. That is probably going to be how you get this thing solved or find a real solution.
