### Post by Kljuka on 2012-12-21
You should find the docking station that corresponds to your husband's laptop. For example:
if your husband's laptop model is Toshiba R940, you should find a docking station for that exact model. Docking stations for other laptops won't be compatible.

Staff at the hardware store should help you choose the correct docking station.
