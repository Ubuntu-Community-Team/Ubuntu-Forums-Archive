### Post by howefield on 2008-09-16
there is probably a quicker way of doing it, but you could delete the bottom panel and recreate it.

Right click bottom panel and select delete, right top panel and select new panel, then add workspace shifter, window list, show desktop and deleted items back in.

Right click each icon and move into position.

### Post by mcduck on 2008-09-16
The notification area applet has a handle on the extreme left side. You can just grab that with the middle mouse button to drag the applet where you want it, or right-click the handle to access the applet's menu.
