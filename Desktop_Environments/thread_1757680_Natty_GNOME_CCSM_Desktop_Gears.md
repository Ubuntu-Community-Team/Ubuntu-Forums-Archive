### Post by coljohnhannibalsmith on 2011-05-13
> **coljohnhannibalsmith said:**
> ***Maybe I have to make the Cube transparent first???***

Guess I finally figured it out.  This is done in CCSM:

[COLOR=Blue][I][B]CCSM > Desktop > Desktop Cube > Transparent Cube > Opacity During Rotation
[/B][/I][/COLOR]
Set this to about ***45.00;*** then select Cube Gears and close...

Turn the Desktop Cube with your mouse and ***voila*** the Cube Gears appear...
