### Post by Madpilot on 2006-01-14
One thing Nautilus can do is add icons over the folders - right-click on a directory, choose Properties, and open the Elements tab (2nd from the left), then just tick the icons you want to display over that folder.

It's not quite the same as preview thumbs, but it's still useful when you're scanning down a long column of directories.
