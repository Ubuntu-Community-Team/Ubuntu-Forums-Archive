### Post by aysiu on 2006-01-21
Rather than staying with Gnome and removing services, you can try using XFCE and *adding* services.

XFCE is super-light.

If you install xubuntu-desktop and then add in Nautilus and gnome-volume-manager, that should solve your problems, for the most part.
