### Post by Mike'sHardLinux on 2006-09-16
I am pretty sure the album art is not on the "usual" cddb servers. If there is such a server, I have not seen a software in Linux or windows that pulls the album art from the cddb servers. 

I use Amarok as my music player, and it can grab album covers from the internet, but I'm not sure if it actually adds it to the mp3 tag. It seems like it adds the artwork to its own database. (I could be wrong about this, though.) The other thing about Amarok is that it is not automatic.

If you search the forums, there is at least one other thread where someone asked if there was a way to automatically add album art. The consensus was no. You'll most likely have to do it one album at a time.
