### Post by mcduck on 2010-09-24
The Wicd icon isn't actually an applet of it's own, it's just a  notification icon, showing inside the Notification Area-applet. (you actually sem to have two of these applets, one on top panel next to indicator applets, and one on the bottom panel)

So, to move it, you need to move the Notification Area-applet. You can do it by right-clicking the small handle on the very left side of the applet and selecting "Move". Or by dragging it with middle mouse button.

edit: I completely forgot the language icon. You'll see that if you have more than one keyboard layout enabled, so if you only need one disable all the others in System/Preferences/Keyboard and the icon will disappear.
