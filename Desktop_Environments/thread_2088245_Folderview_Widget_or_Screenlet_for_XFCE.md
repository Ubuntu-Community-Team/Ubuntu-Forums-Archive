### Post by LewisTM on 2012-11-26
Install Cairo-Dock. You'll get one or more docks which you can keep as simple or as complicated as you want. A common use it to remove the bottom Xfce panel and replace it with a similar CD dock. 

Many applets can be detached from the dock and become desklets. The **Folders** applet, once detached, becomes a desktop folder view.

Cheers!
