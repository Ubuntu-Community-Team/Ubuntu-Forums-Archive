### Post by pbrane on 2012-06-06
If you upgraded you have to reset your application shortcuts to default.
Applications->Settings->Setting Manager, go to Keyboard and then Application Shortcuts. Reset to Defaults.

This may be why it doesn't work for you. It's in the release notes for Xubuntu 12.04
[http://xubuntu.org/news/12-04-release/]("http://xubuntu.org/news/12-04-release/")
