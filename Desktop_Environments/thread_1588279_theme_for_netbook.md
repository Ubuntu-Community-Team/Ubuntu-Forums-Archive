### Post by Tamlynmac on 2010-10-04
I use standard 10.04 on my mini Dell and set it up by:
1. I selected the Clearlooks theme in Appearance Preferences/theme tab
2. Then click on the customize button
3. I use the clearlools under the control tab
4. Under the windows boarders tab I use clearlooks classic
5. I install the Marshup icon theme from softpedia and use it.

Under Appearance Preferences Fonts I use Sans 10, except for:
Windows title font which is Sans Bold 10 and Fixed width font is monospace 10.

On the panel I right click and select properties. On the properties popup I select solid color on the background tab and move the slider to transparent (all the way to the left). On the general tab of the popup I check autohide.

I also turn off the visual effect (waste of mem. under Appearance Preferences/yisual effects tab/none.

I only use one panel (top) with the Main Menu. I've enclosed a screenshot of my mini for your evaluation.

Good Luck
