### Post by 3Miro on 2011-04-30
Logout then select Ubuntu Classic or Ubuntu Classic No Effects and you will be back to what is more or less 10.10.

For the record, Ubuntu 11.04 comes with Gnome 2 and a shell/interface called Unity. Gnome 3 a different things with its own interface.
