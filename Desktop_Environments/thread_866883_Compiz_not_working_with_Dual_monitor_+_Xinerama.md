### Post by Navarre on 2009-01-02
I've found compiz-check to be most useful figuring out these problems.  

The one thing I notice is that you're stacking your monitors vertically so that means your vertical resolution is 2100 (1050+1050) Compiz wants to make a texture out of your desktop.  I know that my card has a max texture size of 2048x2048 so compiz wouldn't work for me with your configuration since 2100 > x2048.  

Oh I just noticed the TwinView/xinerama part, I'm sorry I just never played with those so what I said above may not apply.

### Post by Forlong on 2009-01-03
As far as I know, Xinerama kills your composite extension so need to switch Xinerama off and use TwinView instead: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/XineramaHowTo](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/XineramaHowTo)

---

### Post by noogs on 2009-03-22
Yeah forlong is right xinerama disables composite extensions so either use twinview or you can set you monitors up as a big desktop rather than two seperate ones.
