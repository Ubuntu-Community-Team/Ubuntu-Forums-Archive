### Post by ihcnet on 2007-10-20
65 mustang, after enabling the nvida driver, add the following to the device section in your /etc/X11/xorg.conf file. 

```
Option "TwinView"
Option "AllowGLXWithComposite"
Option "TwinViewOrientation" "LeftOf"
Option "MetaModes" "1600x1200,1024x768"

```

You might have to play around with the orientation and the metamodes, depending on your monitor configuration. You also might want to take a look at [http://gentoo-wiki.com/HOWTO_Dual_Monitors](http://gentoo-wiki.com/HOWTO_Dual_Monitors) I find it pretty helpful at times.
