### Post by bogdan.veringioiu on 2009-10-14
Ok, after you unpacked in .themes, try this:
Open appearance, and click on customize.
On the first tab (controls) you should be able to select your theme. Also on Window Borders you should be able to select the installed theme borders.

Bogdan
