### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-03-19
First of all, the configuring of USB and other devices is done during the installation of Ubuntu. So, you should not need to use commands to configure devices.

Second, if you told us the exact problem we might be able to give specific advice. It would be wrong to recommend this command or that command because you might do even more damage to the operating system.

Thirdly, it would be helpful if you told us the hardware specification of your computer and the version of Ubuntu that you are using. Ubuntu is under constant development and there are different ways of doing things in different versions of Ubuntu. It is important to give clear instructions especially to someone who is unfamiliar with Ubuntu and Linux.
