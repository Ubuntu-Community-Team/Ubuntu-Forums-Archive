### Post by bigsmitty64 on 2010-05-20
You can pretty much remove anything you want through synaptic package manager OR software center. In synaptic just type in the name of the program you want to remove and mark for removal, then apply. In software center its even easier, if it's installed through the software center you just type it in the search, when it comes up click "remove".
