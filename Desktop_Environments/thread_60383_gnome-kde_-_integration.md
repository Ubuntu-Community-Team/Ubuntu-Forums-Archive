### Post by brk3 on 2005-08-27
Dunno about gnome, but on kubuntu you can go to the control centre, and under 'Apperance and Themes' there is 'GTK styles and fonts'. Here you can adjust the fonts used in gtk(gnome apps), on kde. Im not sure if you have to install the gtk-qt engine to enable this kpart, I think you do. But hey if your looking for integration(and who isnt) you should have that installed anyway. it makes a huge difference. Its in the repos..
