### Post by brenx on 2005-10-23
You can use:

kino - Non-linear editor for Digital Video data
kidvgrab - grab digital video data via IEEE1394 links
no-dvtitler - title generator for use with kino
kino-timfx - effect plug-ins for kino
kinoplus - effect plug-ins for kino

from apt-get or you can use synaptic.
