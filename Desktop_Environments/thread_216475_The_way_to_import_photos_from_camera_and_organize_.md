### Post by ltmon on 2006-07-15
Digikam has this feature, I use it myself.

In the camera dialog where you download from the camera, hit the "Advanced" button.  Then there's a checkbox down the bottom where you can tell it to organise into date-based albums.

Alternatively you can use the Linux version of Google's Picasa program.  I'm sure it would be able to do this somehow or another: [http://picasa.google.com/linux/](http://picasa.google.com/linux/)
