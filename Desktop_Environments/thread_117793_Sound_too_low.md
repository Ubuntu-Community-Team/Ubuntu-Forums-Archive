### Post by matthew on 2006-01-15
I had an idea for the volume thing...double click the speaker icon on your panel (not single click). This will bring up a dialog box with several sliders in it. Look for one labeled PCM and raise the volume on it.

### Post by KermitJr on 2006-01-15
I had this problem too. I'm assuming you are dual-booting.  Try booting into windows.  Turn the sound up.  Then reboot into ubuntu.

I had a problem with the windows control of the sound card not resetting it to baseline for linux to use.  If I turn down the volume in windows and reboot to linux, sound is gone.  Turn it up and reboot and it works fine.

NOTE: This is normally with function key control sound cards, but I have seen it with hardware based, also.

Not sure why, though.

Hope this helps.
