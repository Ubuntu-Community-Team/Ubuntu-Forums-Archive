### Post by wastrel on 2006-06-06
System->Administration->Device Manager 

Not exactly as user-friendly I'm sure, but there's a bunch of info there.

Still less user-friendly try lshw at the terminal:

sudo lshw

For more info on the lshw command:

man lshw
