### Post by Happy_Man on 2008-05-18
That's just how the icons show up in GNOME. If you really, really want to fix this, right-click your menu in GNOME, go to Edit Menus, find the entry for the program you want the icon for, right-click, properties, click the blank icon box in the dialog that pops up, and go icon-hunting. Best of luck.
