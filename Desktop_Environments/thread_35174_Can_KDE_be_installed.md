### Post by lorap on 2005-05-25
hi friend!
of course you can.
the only thing you've to do is to add additional repositories, that's why you didn't see the binaries.
so let's add the extra repositories.
go to the Synaptic-->Package-->Manager
then go to the Settings-->Repositories
once you've the repositories window open press Advanced button and mark all checkboxes except "download only upgradable packages" then press Ok.
wait until the update process completes.
the repositories window'll close itself at the end.
press Reload button and wait again.
now press Sectors button at the bottom so you'll see KDE with all its packages :-)
have a nice day.
