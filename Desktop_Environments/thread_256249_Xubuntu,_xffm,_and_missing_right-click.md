Hello all,

I installed Xubuntu on an old laptop a couple days ago and all was well until I wanted to browse the network (files on my Windows box).  I looked around for options and decided to give xffm a shot.  Well, I hated how everytime I opened any tool that had been installed as a part of xffm it changed my desktop, icons, etc.  I didn't know how to fix it so I removed it along with libdbh1.0-1 that was installed with it.

Anyway, before I installed xffm I could right-click on the desktop and it would bring up the xfce menu.  However, now when I right-click on the desktop ... nothing happens.  Does anybody know how to get it back or where to start looking?

Thanks,
Paco

### Post by mepaco on 2006-09-12
I just figured it out and thought somebody else might benefit from my discovery.  I noticed that the items in my Desktop folder also were not showing up on my desktop.  Well, somehow when I uninstalled xffm, my settings were changed so that under Settings Manager/Desktop the "Allow Xfce to manage the desktop" option was unchecked.  I checked it and all is well.  Whoops.

-Paco
