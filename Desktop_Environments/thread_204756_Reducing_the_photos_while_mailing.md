### Post by arjaybe on 2006-06-27
That sounds like a good idea.  I've never seen it.

You can do it in two steps if you install picwiz.  Right-click once and change the size, then right-click again to mail it.  Picwiz defaults to saving a renamed copy rather than overwriting, ie filename-640x480.png.

Not quite one-click, but a decent workaround.
