### Post by MLevin on 2006-07-13
That is true, the USB card reader is the way easier thing to transfer pictures from a camera. First, it does not have to be powered and turned on; second, it just gives you the file system access, no 3rd part driver/app required. And third, some cameras have USB1 only, which is soooo sloooow.

I have a Canon EOS 350d which works fine with ubuntu but I have to set the comunication settings to Print/PTP. The Powershot S50 (not slr) does also connect without problems.

I use gthumb to import the photos (gthumb --import-photos) which autostarts when I connect the camera and turn it on.
