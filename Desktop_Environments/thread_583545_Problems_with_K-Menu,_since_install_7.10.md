### Post by wacb on 2007-10-20
Had the same problem, found the answer here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=582240](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=582240) (go to post #6)

--

Just right click on the long bar at the bottom
go to "Configure Panel"
click on "Menus" at the left
and then click on "Name only" under K-Menu - Menu Item Format

--

You can also click on description only but then you don't know the name of the application you're running.  I just installed Kubuntu for the first time today (7.10) and am new to linux as well.  I figure it will eventually be fixed with an update so that you can display names with descriptions.....
