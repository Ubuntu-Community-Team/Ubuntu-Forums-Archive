### Post by BslBryan on 2009-05-15
Hello, jet-san. :-)  How are you liking it so far?

For setting the bottom cube cap's wallpaper, go to your CompizConfig Settings Manager (Which you do have) --> Utility --> Cube Caps --> Appearance --> Bottom Image Files.

For Animations, just uncheck all of the options from the pool that you do not want. 

Also, you can see hidden files with either View --> Show Hidden Files, or Ctrl + H.

I hope I've been of some help, and I hope that you're liking Ubuntu.  Welcome to the community!

Best to you, and good luck.
