### Post by Benedolt on 2006-08-01
Hey Mike!
You can access wine's sound preferences by entering 'winecfg' in a terminal window and clicking on the 'Sound' tab. That's the place where you configure your sound. You don't have to technically install drivers for wine you just have to tell wine what drivers to use.
Good luck.
-Benedolt
