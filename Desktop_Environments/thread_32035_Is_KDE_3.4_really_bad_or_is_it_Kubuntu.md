### Post by sage on 2005-05-05

1) Kate is not saving files in the project

4)KMail account settings sometimes works, sometimes doesn't
5) Sound does not work (works fine on Gnome)

2) Thunderbird won't save my user passwords either
3) Sunbird ? Haven't tried it. Does not synch to my palm pilot
