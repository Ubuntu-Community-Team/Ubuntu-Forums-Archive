### Post by Jahid65 on 2011-12-17
I think you are in KDE netbook interface. Press alt+F2 & type

```
systemsettings
```

Then go to workspace behavior > workspace, then select workspace type Desktop & click apply for the change

you may need to enable plasma desktop in Startup & Shutdown module of systemsettings (personal settings) to start the the plasma desktop automatically.
