### Post by Krytarik on 2011-08-28
This happens to me, too, occasionally, but only in Firefox. The thing is, in these cases it's like a second click (right or left doesn't matter in the context menu) is applied right after the first (right-)click. Probably Firefox doesn't get that quick at times that the mouse button has been already released again. So, this results in other actions, too, depending on where the mouse pointer is in the context menu right after the right-click.

It's quite annoying to have that happen recurringly. However, when that happens, I work around this by just leaving the mouse button pressed and releasing it only after moving the pointer over the menu item I want.

Hope this helps a bit. ;-)
