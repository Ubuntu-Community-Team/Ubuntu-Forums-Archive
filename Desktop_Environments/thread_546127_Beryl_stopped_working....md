### Post by easykillsoft on 2007-09-15
Ok I found a solution myself, was one I didn't think I'd have to do since I switched to Ubuntu, but worked beautifully.  Download the 100.14.11 drivers from NVIDIA's website (the ones in the apt repositories are 100.14.09) ctrl-alt-1 logged in, shut down gdm, went to my desktop (where I downloaded the driver to) sudo sh NVIDIA.blah blah.run it ran its installer after it was done rebooted and poof beryl is working beautifully again.
