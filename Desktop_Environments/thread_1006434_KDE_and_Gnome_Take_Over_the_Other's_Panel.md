### Post by davidbilla on 2008-12-09
Do you have a separate installation for Kubuntu(or Ubuntu, whichever way you want to look at it!)? You can as well install kde from the repositories and choose 'kde' during login in 'sessions' options. Then you wouldn't have to reboot everytime. You can just logout and login.
