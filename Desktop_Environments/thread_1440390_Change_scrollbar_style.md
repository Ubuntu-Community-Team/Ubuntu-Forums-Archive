### Post by labinnsw on 2010-04-03
I am not 100% on this but I think this is how you do it.
1. Right click on the desktop and select "Change desktop background"
2. Under the "Theme" tab, select "Customise"
3. Under the "Controls" tab, select the one that strikes your fancy.

Optional:
Follow the Gnome user Group link in my signature to find numerous other links for customizing Gnome.
