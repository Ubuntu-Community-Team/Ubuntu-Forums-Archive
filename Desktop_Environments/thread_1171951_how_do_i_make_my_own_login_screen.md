### Post by orange-wedge on 2009-05-27
The best place to start is the source:
[http://live.gnome.org/GnomeArt/Tutorials/GdmThemes](http://live.gnome.org/GnomeArt/Tutorials/GdmThemes)

Also try downloading a login theme and taking a look at what's inside the tarball.  Play around with elements inside the xml file, like changing the images or element positions.

This is how I tweaked one of my login themes to comment out an element since my netbook's resolution was making the time overlap with the login options.
