### Post by benerivo on 2007-03-31
The option in my version of k3b (v1.0 in kde3.5.6) is...On starting the program, your optical drive(s) are listed in the top left. Insert an audio cd, and click on the drive. It should have read from free cddb, and will list the tracks. Click on 'Start Ripping' - just above the track number list.
