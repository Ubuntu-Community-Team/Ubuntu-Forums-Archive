### Post by jyb3 on 2024-08-11
So the way I solved this issue was with Input-Remapper ([https://github.com/sezanzeb/input-remapper/tree/main](https://github.com/sezanzeb/input-remapper/tree/main)).

My initial stab at this didn't work because my PS2 keyboard that is attached to a PS2-USB dongle, was detected by input-remapper as a Barcode Reader, and I assumed that device was the fingerprint reader of the laptop.  And the keyboard I was trying, was probably the internal keyboard of the laptop.

With this program I was able to remap:
CapsLock -> SuperL
Shift+CapsLock -> CapsLock
