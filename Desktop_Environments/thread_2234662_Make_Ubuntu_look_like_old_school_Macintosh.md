### Post by at_first_light on 2014-07-17
You'd need a theme for your desktop environment, and an icon theme. I don't know of any off-hand, and would suspect you'd need to make them yourself from scratch. Unity is the desktop environment used by Ubuntu and it's not custom theme friendly. You might have more luck making them for KDE, or IceWM.
