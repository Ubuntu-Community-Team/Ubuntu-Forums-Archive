### Post by Therion on 2009-05-06
Right-click on the Gnome-panel that you want your minimized windows to go to...  Top or bottom panel, your choice.

Click on **Add to Panel**.

Scroll down and select the option for "Application Switcher (Use Buttons)" option.  Can't recall the exact wording but you'll know it when you see it.
