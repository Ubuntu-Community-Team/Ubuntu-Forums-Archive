### Post by shakin on 2005-05-04
[QUOTE=bk452]KDE has widgets that need super karamba.  What do I need for gnome widgets?[/QUOTE]

I think you want gdesklets. You can install the gdesklets package from Synaptic, but I believe people are recommending NOT installing the gdesklets-data package and instead going to [http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/](http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/) and downloading whichever gdesklets you want from there.

A board search for 'gdesklets' will get you lots of results.
