Hi

It looks like an issue with your filing system modules (ext4 and jbd2) crashing with a null pointer.

Run the usual checks on your hard drive such as SMART (long test) and fsck. That would be my starting place to ensure it's not hardware.

Can you give more detailed specs of your system.

```
sudo lshw
```

and 

```
uname -a
```

are good starts.
