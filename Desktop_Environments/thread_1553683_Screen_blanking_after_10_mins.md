### Post by Frogs Hair on 2010-08-15
Have you changed your monitor sleep settings in power management on the bottom of the screen saver settings screen ?

### Post by SyCo123 on 2010-08-15
Look like that was it, thanks! How stupid is that to have a second power management setting. What's even more messed up was it was not enabled in the screen saver settings (checkbox not checked). Now it's enabled with high values set and seems to be ok now.
