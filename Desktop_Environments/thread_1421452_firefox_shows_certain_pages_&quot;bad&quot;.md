### Post by claracc on 2010-03-06
well, I have solved the problem.

It was a question related to the minimum size of letters in preferences of firefox, content tab, advanced default typography.

I had to put minimum letter size to 11, and now the text of all buttons of web pages are shown correctly, not mixing.

Option let web pages to select its own typography is ticked.
