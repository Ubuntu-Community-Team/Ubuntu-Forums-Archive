### Post by coffeecat on 2012-01-03
Miniature depends on the size of your monitor. On my 23" screen the reduced size terminals are not that much smaller than a normal sized terminal. With three instances of firefox, they are much smaller than my normal unmaximised firefox. 

Interesting question about compiz. I don't think so.

By the way, my question about 11.10 or 11.04 was because I wasn't sure whether the behaviour in 11.04 was the same. I've since checked and 11.04 does behave the same, not that that matters since you are using 11.10.

Just to clarify. The behaviour is slightly different if you are running all instances of terminal (or any app) minimised or if you have one still unminimised.

**All running windows minimised**: click once on the launcher icon - all windows restored to their previous positions. Click on the launcher icon again and they are arranged in miniature around the desktop so that you can click on the one you want focussed.

**One or more windows minimised, with one or more not minimised**: click once on the launcher icon and all running instances are arranged in miniature around the desktop. Click on the one you want focussed.
