### Post by ayoli on 2006-09-22
binaries (executables) of applications usually go in /usr/bin or /usr/local/bin
if the new installed ap is not in the menu sometimes it's just a quetion of menu refresh, to refresh the gnome-panel in a terminal type in:

killall gnome-panel

btw you can hit ALT+F2 with launch the run dialog, then enter the name of your app (or browse bin dirs from here).
