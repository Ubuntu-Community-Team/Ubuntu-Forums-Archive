### Post by C1377 on 2011-03-21
Right Click on the lower panel and select "new panel"
then Right Click on the new panel (which should appear at the top of the desktop) and click add to panel to add the familiar top panel applets (Menu Bar, Clock, etc..)
if the panel does not automatically appear at the top of the screen, right click on the window and select properties.  You can then configure the location, size, and general behavior of the panel.
