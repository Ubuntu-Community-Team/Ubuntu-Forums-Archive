### Post by temcat on 2006-07-17
Right-click on an applet, uncheck "Lock to panel". Then middle-click/drag to move the applet to the desired place (or right-click, choose "Move" and move.) In your case, you'll want to do this from right to left, because when an applet is locked, you can't move other applets past it.
