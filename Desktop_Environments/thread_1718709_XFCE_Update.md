### Post by 3Miro on 2011-03-31
Your best bet for XFCE 4.8 is to upgrade to Xubuntu (or Ubuntu) 11.04 when it comes out in a month (it is still in beta so I wouldn't be upgrading just yet).

You can also try to Google an unofficial ppa for XFCE 4.8 for your version of X/Ubuntu.

Yes the new XFCE panels do look slightly different and have a lot of nice new features.
