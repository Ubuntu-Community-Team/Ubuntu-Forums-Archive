### Post by kvonb on 2006-07-05
On your "Applications" menu, select "Add/Remove applications", make sure that "Show unsuported" and "Show Commercial" apps are ticked, then in the top right hand corner type in "modem".

I found one called "GNOME PPP", it says that it has a tray icon, try that.

Also once you have connected to the net, you can add a right click on the panel, then "Add to panel", scroll down to "System & Hardware" and there is a "Modem Monitor", or if that doesn't work, add the "Network Monitor", right click on it, select properties, then select (I guess) ppp0 from the drop down list.

That should do it :)
