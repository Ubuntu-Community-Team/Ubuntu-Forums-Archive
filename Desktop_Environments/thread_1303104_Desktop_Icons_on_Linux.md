### Post by coskierken on 2009-12-15
You can use screenlets (in the repository).  You can create launchers for various programs and assign them to individual desktops.  The one problem I was having was the icons becoming distorted.  It has done it on two different machines (ATI and Nvidia).  Also, the icons would sometimes not hold their respective positions, especially when you activate another monitor and extended the desktop over, watch a movie, and then deactivate.  Maybe you will have better luck.  You can create just launcher with basic Karmic by right-clicking and you will see the creat launcher in the menu.  But, icons are limited and of only a certain type of which they are SVG+XML.  I could not find any which meet the requirements of the launcher.  Maybe someone can help.  

If you don't really like the background of Cairo dock, just make it transparent!  The amount of possible configurations in Cairo-dock is pretty robust.
