### Post by soze on 2006-06-03
You need the "Media Player Connectivity" plugin.
Which can be found [here]("http://http://membres.lycos.fr/sethnakht/").

After logging in to Sirius and selecting your channel, look for the little black "multimedia" icon (it should be right under the station banner). 

Click it and it will launch your media player.

(the media player of your choice (I use xine) can be configured by right-clicking the icon and selecting "Meda Player Connectivity")
