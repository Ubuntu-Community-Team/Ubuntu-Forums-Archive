### Post by Tomosaur on 2006-08-26
You could try turning off the hibernate function (System > Preferences > Power Management), or failing that, try to work around it with a screensaver. I set mine to lock my screen when I'm away for a long time, because if I'm doing something in particular, I don't want my sister to come along and mess it up. This should prevent the computer from logging you out (although locking your screen means you'll have to put your password in, it doesn't log you out or kill any processes).
