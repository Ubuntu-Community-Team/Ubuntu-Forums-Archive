### Post by mcduck on 2007-03-03
While some GTK2-themes do change the panels, for the best results you need to use a background picture for your panels. Just drag&drop any picture to your panel to use it as background.

I've made some glassy-looking panel backgrounds, and there's also small tricks to make them work nice. See this post for more info & link to the panel background pics: [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=2194660&postcount=4]("http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=2194660&postcount=4")
