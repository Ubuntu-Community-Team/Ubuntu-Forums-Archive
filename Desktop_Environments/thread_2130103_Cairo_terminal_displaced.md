### Post by MTariq on 2013-03-28
I have cario dock installed on my Ubuntu 12.04. I have by mistake detached the terminal (add-on). It is now in a floating position on the desktop and it's look is very odd. Any body please help me to re attache it with my dock.

---

### Post by LewisTM on 2013-03-28
Go to Cairo configuration->Advanced mode, select terminal->Desklet tab. In the Visibility section, uncheck "Is detached from the dock".

Cheers!
