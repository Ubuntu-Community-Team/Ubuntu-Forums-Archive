### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-10-14
Hi,
+1
The other poster is right, the package you are looking for is called 'ubuntu-netbook'. [COLOR="Gray"]I don't think you will find it listed in Software Center...[/COLOR] Edited: Oops, I just found it in Software Center when searching for "netbook".

You won't actually be replacing your desktop edition. You will have the choice to use either desktop.

Once you install ubuntu-netbook, log out, then click on your name on the login screen, then choose Ubuntu Netbook (Or Ubuntu Desktop) from the menu below, then enter your password and log in. 

Hope this helps.
