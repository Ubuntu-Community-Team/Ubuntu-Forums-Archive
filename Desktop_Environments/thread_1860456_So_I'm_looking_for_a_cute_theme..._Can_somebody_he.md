### Post by IWantFroyo on 2011-10-14
Male dominated? Say what? The theme is colored this way to make it easy to see. If you really want to change your theme to something pink, I second haqking's links. The Pink-White contrast is difficult to see however, so I would recommend not doing this unless you use your computer rarely.

What I would suggest doing is changing little parts in your OS that won't affect the stuff you need to see quickly (that needs more contrast). Such as adding a [persona]("http://www.getpersonas.com/en-US/") to Firefox, or changing the background in LibreOffice. Or even just changing your wallpaper.
