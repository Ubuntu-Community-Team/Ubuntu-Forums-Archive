### Post by Kethinov on 2006-07-05
A lazy way to do it would be to copy the music files to your hard drive, then use a program like EasyTag to fix the filename/directory structure.

Many OS X users use this technique. They just open the terminal, copy all the music that way, then let iTunes organize it all for them. EasyTag, or even some native music apps for Linux could do the same.
