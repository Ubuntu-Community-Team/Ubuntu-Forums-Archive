### Post by sagara on 2007-04-27
Ok I figured out how to do this.

Not quite sure if this is what you were asking but lets say you manually changed your home icon in your desktop.  If you want to revert to the original (theme dependent icon) then 

- right click > properties > click on the icon box
- a "Select custom Icon" window appears
- click on the **revert** button.  This will restore the original icon.

Hope this helps.
