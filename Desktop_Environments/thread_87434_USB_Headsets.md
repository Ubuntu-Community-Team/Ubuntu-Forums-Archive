### Post by mips on 2005-11-08
The inline volume button does not work.

Double click on the volume control button, window will open, move the dual PCM volume slider down towards the bottom quarter, close. Now adjust the volume on the panel as you would have normally done.

I had the same problem with my DSP300 and the above solved my problem.
