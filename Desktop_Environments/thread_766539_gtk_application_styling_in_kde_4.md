### Post by benerivo on 2008-04-25
Install gtk-qt-engine-kde4. You should gain an extra section in your kde4 system settings which allows gtk apps to be drawn with your qt4 style. It won't work with synaptic as this is gtk1 and not gtk2 (but pretty much all other gnome apps should now look almost identical to the kde4 ones).
