### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2014-05-27
To remove any icons from your LXPanel, right click any open space on the LXPanel->Add/Remove Panel Items->Panel Applets.  Choose which icon you want to delete and then select delete.  (Obviously you can add from here as well).  With regard to Dropbox (under preferences) untick Start Dropbox on system startup.  The icon will only appear when you manually start it.  When you are done with it you can manually quit and the icon will disappear.
