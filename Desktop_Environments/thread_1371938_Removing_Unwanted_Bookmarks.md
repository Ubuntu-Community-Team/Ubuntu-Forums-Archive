### Post by prshah on 2010-01-04
> **theyain said:**
> removing the bookmarks (Mounted and Unmounted partitions, from the Places area in Nautilus as well as the Places button on the Gnome Panel?

In the Nautilus sidebar (F9)-Places, just right-click the bookmark and choose Remove.

Once removed from Nautilus, it will disappear from the Places menu as well.
