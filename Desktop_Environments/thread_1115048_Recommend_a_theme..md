### Post by blackened on 2009-04-03
New Wave ([http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/New+Wave?content=87134]("http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/New+Wave?content=87134")) is great if you use a smaller font than is the default.  I prefer it with FreeSans 8pt. 

If you also use the metacity theme, then you'll have to keep the titlebar font at 9 or 10 pt, otherwise it screws up the min/max/close buttons.
