### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-04-24
Sometimes with updates or upgrades, it messes with your vid driver.  Go to System > Admin > Hardware Driver and let it select the appropriate driver for your system.  Then go to System > Preferences > Appearance > Visusal Effects and set it to Normal, Extra, or Custom
