### Post by pauna on 2009-04-25
> **bschwall said:**
> I'm running 9.04 Remix on my Acer Aspire One. And 1st let me say it was awesome. Untill I reboot and my desktop went away. Can someone help me. The only thing I see after it boots up is the wallpaper.

This is a bug, gnome-panel has died. Do a right click on the wallpaper and "Create a launcher". Create an "Application" type launcher for "gnome-terminal".

This should give you an icon for terminal on desktop. Open the terminal and execute "gnome-panel". That should solve it.
