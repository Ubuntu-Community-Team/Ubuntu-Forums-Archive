### Post by stinkeye on 2009-10-06
> **Cariboo1938 said:**
> I have compiz 1:0.8.2-0ubuntu8.1 installed on a LinuxMint System and the cube is rotating..(with a ATI Radeon 3200 HD Graphic Card
 and ATI/AMD proprietary graphics driver, installed through Envy.)

How can I disable the cube caps to look inside to see the GEARs running?
CCSM -Desktop cube-Appearance-Transparent Cube.
If you have the wallpaper plugin enabled the sides will also be transparent,
if not, only the top and bottom will be transparent.

### Post by onyxwolf on 2009-10-06
The only way I can find to view inside is by setting the cube transparency which is in ccsm >Desktop Cube (NOT Cube Rotation)>Transparent Cube
