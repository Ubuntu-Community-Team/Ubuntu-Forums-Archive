### Post by OU812 on 2006-07-20
In xine you may have to change the video driver. Look in preferences and choose a different video driver (I think x11 but I am in win... right now).

Before switching to another player (bmp or audacious) try importing winamp equalizer presets. Hit the preset button on the equalizer window and choose import. Then just find the winamp folder with the file winamp.q1. Then choose load preset and pick the one you want. Then turn on the equalizer. Also, changing from alsa to esound may help improve the sound a bit. If not, try bmp or audacious.
