### Post by r.osmanov on 2011-02-15
Probably, you already resolved the problem. However, I'd share my solution. 

All of my icons disappeared also; nautilus forgot what file type was for...; log-in-log-out didn't help.

My solution:
- reinstall *ubuntu-desktop, nautilus, nautilus-data, gnome-icon-theme, hicolor-icon-theme* packages;
- refresh desktop and windows(F5).

I think it should be sufficient to reinstall *nautilus-data* and *nautilus*.

Good luck.
