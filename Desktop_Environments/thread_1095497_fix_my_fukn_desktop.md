### Post by kidux on 2009-03-13
Ok, I've seen your multiple posts, and I'll give it a shot. It sounds like you don't have the correct drivers installed. What version of Ubuntu are you running, and do you know what video card you have?

If you go to System>Administation>Hardware Drivers, it should tell you there are restricted drivers available (with any luck). Choose the recommended driver, then click activate. It will ask you for your password, enter that and wait for it to download and install the driver, then reboot.

Hopefully that helps.
