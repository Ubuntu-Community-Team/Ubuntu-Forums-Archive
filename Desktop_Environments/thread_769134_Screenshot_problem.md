### Post by sdennie on 2008-04-26
What keyboard layout are you using?  Screenshots will happen every time you hit the Prnt Scrn key (it may be called something different on a non-English keyboard) but, if your keyboard layout is making some other key appear to be Prnt Scrn, it could be causing the problem.  A possible solution could be to go to System->Preferences->Keyboard Shortcuts and removing the shortcuts for taking screenshots.
