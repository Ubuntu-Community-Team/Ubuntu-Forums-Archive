### Post by 3rdalbum on 2007-01-17
Right-click on the top panel and choose "Add to panel".

Click on the Clock, hit Add, and the clock will come back at the point where you clicked. Right-click on the clock and choose Move, then move your mouse to the top-right of the screen. Click to lock the clock in that position.

### Post by mcduck on 2007-01-17
For themes, check Gnome-Look: [http://gnome-look.org]("http://gnome-look.org")

GTK2-themes are for applications, Metacity themes are for window borders and icons are, well, icons :)

Themes can be installed by opening Theme Manager (System/Preferences/Themes) and drag&dropping the theme package to the window.
