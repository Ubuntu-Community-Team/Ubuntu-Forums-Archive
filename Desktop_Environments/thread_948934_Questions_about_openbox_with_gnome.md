### Post by urukrama on 2008-10-15
Openbox is very stable, with or without Gnome. 

Is there a drawback? If you mean compared to Metacity: It is lighter than Metacity, and more customizable. If you mean compared to plain Openbox: it will be somewhat slower, as all the Gnome daemons get loaded.
