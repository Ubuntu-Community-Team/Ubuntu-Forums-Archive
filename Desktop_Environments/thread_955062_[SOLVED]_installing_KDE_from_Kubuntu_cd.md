### Post by mick316 on 2008-10-21
"sudo apt-get install kubuntu-desktop"

This will give you a new KDE Session at the log in screen, and then works and looks just like Kubuntu.

[http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/ubuntu/install-kde-kubuntu-on-ubuntu/](http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/ubuntu/install-kde-kubuntu-on-ubuntu/)

### Post by aysiu on 2008-10-22
If you have the Kubuntu *Alternate* CD, you can do it. If you have the Kubuntu *Desktop* CD, you can't.
