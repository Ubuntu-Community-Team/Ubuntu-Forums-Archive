### Post by Bartender on 2006-10-03
ibob -
This doesn't sound like the same thing I ran into the other day, because you say your machines were going fast.  Does Synaptic still work OK?  
I installed Dapper to an old Dell a coupla days ago.  The guy was on Qwest DSL.  Synaptic hauled the mail (after we plugged in a revised /resources.list) but FF and Opera were horrible.  Found out about disabling IPV6 in Firefox.  This was a simple fix.  
Others described how to disable IPV6 in Ubuntu, not in the browser, so that IPV6 is disabled across the board, but I haven't tried that yet.  Disabling in Ubuntu is described in Wiki.  Just search "Firefox IPV6" and you should find some directions for disabling in browser.  Worth a try.
