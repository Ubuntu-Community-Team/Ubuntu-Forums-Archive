### Post by oddworld on 2008-04-11
I have downloaded many different folders of files and they are all in a folder.
I was wondering if there is any way to recursively unrar all of the folders, and have the enclosed files remain in their folder.
It would take me hours to right click and extract.
Thanks

---

### Post by oddworld on 2008-04-11
solved

unrar e -r *.rar
