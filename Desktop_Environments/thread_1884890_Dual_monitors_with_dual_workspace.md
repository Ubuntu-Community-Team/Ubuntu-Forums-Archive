### Post by jimmydean886-2 on 2011-11-22
When you say "Dual Workspace" do you mean 2 virtual desktops, or maybe two screens in a layout going side by side, or two different environments for 2 different users to use one computer?

Most of this is in your monitors preferences, except some will be in any configuration utility you may have for proprietary drivers. If you don't have any, look for a menu entry in the "System/Administration" menu called "Additional Drivers"

Install the driver for your hardware, (it should be marked recommended) then restart your system.
Use the config utility that comes with them to then configure your display.

Hope it helps
