### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-12-10
When using applications in full screen, the launcher and panel do not hide. This causes part of the application not to be visible.

It happens when the screensaver starts; when I use F11 to go full-screen with Firefox; when viewing a video full-screen with VLC or while using YouTube (which means I cannot press the pause or play button); and so on.

See the attached screen shot of VLC in full screen -- the pause button is inaccessible underneath the rubbish bin.

The panel and launcher used to hide correctly, which makes me think it could be some setting in Compiz that I might have changed without realising.

Do you know how I can fix this?

### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-12-24
I have discovered the problem.

When using the cube, in Compiz you need to set:
Desktop > Deskcop Cube > Transparent Cube > Opacity When Not Rotating> 100

Don't ask me why!
