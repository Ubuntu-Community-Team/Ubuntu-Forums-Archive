### Post by misterE0 on 2006-10-01
A very simple question, but i haven't been able to find much by way of help files for easytag.  What does it mean when it highlights the line of a filename in blue?  Also, when a filename is in red, does that mean there is no tag for that file yet?  Thanks in advance.

### Post by Cable on 2006-10-01
A filename in red means that the song's information has been changed in some way or another (tag wise) and it has not yet been saved.  I believe the highlighting just means that you have selected that particular file, and can alter its tag if you wish.
