### Post by Ozymandias_117 on 2010-02-20
8.04:

Before you login, there should be an option about what session you want at the bottom of the screen, select KDE, login. During login it will ask if you want to make KDE your default session, select yes.

9.10:

Again, at the bottom, select KDE (after selecting your account for logging in). Nothing else needed, it will stay at KDE until you change it.
