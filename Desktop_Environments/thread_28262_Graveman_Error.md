### Post by wondering_jew on 2005-04-20
I had this same problem. I think the error comes from graveman not being able to find the external programs it uses (cdrecord cdrdao etc etc). Providing you have these on your system already just select File -> Preferences got to the external programs tab and there will be a button at the bottom to scan for external programs (press it ;) ). After that just cross your fingers and try again and hopefully it will work... did for me :)
