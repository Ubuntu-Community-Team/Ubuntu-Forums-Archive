### Post by n_hendrick on 2006-02-23
I have hda2 hda4 hda1 mounted at startup and the icons are always on my desktop.....is there a way to keep the partitions mounted and active without the annoying desktop icons?

---

### Post by Madpilot on 2006-02-23
You can do this - go **Applications menu --> System Tools --> Configuration Editor** to launch gconf.

Then **apps>nautilus>desktop** and untick the "volumes_visible" line.
