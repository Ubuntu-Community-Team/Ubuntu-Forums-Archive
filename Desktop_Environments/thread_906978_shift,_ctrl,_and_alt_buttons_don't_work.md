### Post by dlobo on 2008-09-17
I experience the same problem sometimes while I am running vmware server.
I used to 'correct' the problem by rebooting. However I just found a solution online that worked for me:
1- Open a command line prompt
2- Type:setxkbmap

and that's it.
Afterwards, ctrl, alt and shift were back to normal.

I hope this helps you too.

Thanks
