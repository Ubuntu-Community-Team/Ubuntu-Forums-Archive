### Post by Lord.Quicksilver on 2007-07-25
Basically you must enter Beryl option panel and go to Desktop tab and enable the cube and cube rotation plugins. Once they're enabled, you can test the 3d effect by pressing CTRL+ALT+left arrow or CTRL+ALT+right arrow. Also remember that in order have a real cube you must have at least 4 desktops.
