### Post by denham2010 on 2010-03-15
Unfortunately that feature has not been implemented in Thunar.

I believe in the development in Xfce 4.8 has a lot of new functionality, so you may want to have a look at the Xfce website for development updates.

Of course, you can still use Nautilus as your file manager in Xfce, and this will enable you to have the audio previews. Just remember to change the Nautilus launcher in Xfce to 'nautilus --no-desktop' otherwise nautilus will take over the desktop.

Cheers.
