### Post by adie273 on 2005-12-21
Do you have the Gnome menu which shows the 'Applications, Places and System' menu's?

Id so, remove it from the panel its on, then left click on the panel and 'Add to panel' and there should be 2 Gnome menu's to choose from. The original one that Ubuntu uses as default, and another one which shows only the Gnome Icon (No Applications, Places or System)

Hope that makes sense, good luck
