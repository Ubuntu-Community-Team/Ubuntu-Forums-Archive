### Post by prshah on 2009-02-17
> **JECHO said:**
> how to hide the icons in the taskbar

If you are also using Compiz, you can use the "Window Rules" plugin.

Click on System-Preferences-CompizConfig Settings Manager-Window Management-Window Rules-Skip Taskbar, and click the "+" sign to the right, and then click the "Grab" button to select the window that you don't want displayed in the taskbar.
