### Post by hagman on 2005-04-15
You can edit the menu manually. Look at the .desktop files in

/usr/share/applications (global menu entries) or 
~/.local/share/applications (user-specific menu entries)

You can edit the .desktop files directly to change/delete/add menu entries.
