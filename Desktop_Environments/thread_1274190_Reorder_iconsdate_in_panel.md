### Post by kyoha on 2009-09-24
Hi! I'm very new to using Ubuntu (9.04) and yesterday, I tried to hook my laptop (HP Pavillion Entertainment Notebook) to a TV to watch a movie.  When I removed the TV and changed the desktop resolution back to my laptop's screen size, all of my icons had rearranged themselves in both my top and bottom panels. My recycle bin is three inches into the middle of my bottom panel instead of being on the very edge, and I can't see all of my desktop pages on screen anymore, and I want to fix that.  I just want to reorder the icons on top so that the power down/lock screen icon is in the corner, not in the middle of the panel. 

Is there any way I can change where they are?

---

### Post by wojox on 2009-09-24
Right click the applet.
Uncheck Lock to Panel
Select Move
Position your mouse over the applet to move
Lock to panel again
