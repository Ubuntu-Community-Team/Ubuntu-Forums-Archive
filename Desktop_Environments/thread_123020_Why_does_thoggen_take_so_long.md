### Post by el3ktro on 2006-01-29
Thoggen uses the Theora video codec, which produces good quality & is free & patent-free, but it's not yet optimized for speed, so it's VERY slow. When they have the codec itself finished they will focs on speed. I suggest something like dvdrip, this rips CDs to several formats like ffmpeg, divx, xvid etc.
