### Post by TheFu on 2023-04-12
Standard support for Ubuntu 16.04 ended in 2021.

My suggestion is to use ssh - from "Ghost Commander" on Android. It isn't a GUI, but for an out of support release, there aren't many other choices without terrible security ramifications.

Since I know you won't listen and upgrade to a currently supported release - 22.04 is the current LTS for new OS installs - you could look at the logs on android and on ubuntu for clues.
