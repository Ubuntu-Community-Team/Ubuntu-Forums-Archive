### Post by harun3d on 2012-09-03
I use only ubuntu since 2years and I like it. I have written a word document in Libreoffice.  When somebody else opens it in microsoft office: 
- the figure and table numbers are not dynamic any more: so new captions have to be made: +30 hours of work for a 150page document
- figures have 2 unvisible contours: one for the figure and one for the figure+caption. This makes that your caption becomes invisible or that a part of your figures are not visible any more: +30 hours of work
- spreadsheets become bigger or smaller.  a part of them disappear. +15hours of work.  unnecessary columns and rows are added in comparison with the libreoffice version
- spaces in headers and footers change: 2 lines instead of one line

I think in 3 weeks I will convert a libreoffice .doc file into a microsoft office .doc file knowing that I am a professional writer in documents.

saving in docx was not possible for my document in libreoffice.  Sometimes it was but then microsoft office could not open it. 

Latex is the best but latex to word is not possible in an easy way.
