### Post by ToastedToad on 2005-11-16
Right click on any panel and select "new panel", then click on the new panel and select "properties". From there you can resize, and move it to the location of your former d.o.a. panel. Now you can "add to panel" all the features that you had before.

### Post by xdustin on 2005-11-16
you could:

Right-click on an existing panel hide button and choose new panel. Then, right-click on your new panel, choose add to panel, and select the show desktop button, window selector, etc. Try a window list.

I find the panels to be quite configurable, dockable, and fun.
