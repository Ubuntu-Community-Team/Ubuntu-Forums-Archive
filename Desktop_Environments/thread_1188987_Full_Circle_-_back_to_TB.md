Hello folks, I've been posting recently about finding a good PIM solution for calendaring, email, tasks etc.

However the annoyances I haven't found a solution for including it's in ability to sensibly use html templates for outgoing mail (don't flame I know the arguments, but I also live in the real world) have made me retry TB with lightning installed.
