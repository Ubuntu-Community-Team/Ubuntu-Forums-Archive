### Post by sudodus on 2014-10-21
Look at the attached file in my previous post.

1. Start Dash at the top left corner.

2. Click on the second symbol from left at the bottom of the dash screen (at the yellow digit **2**). This symbol is similar to the letter **A**.

3. Click on 'Show more results' (or something in your language with that meaning).

When I do so, Dash will show Firefox (with its icon and name). Installed programs with names at the end of the alphabet will be found if you scroll the Dash display with the scroll-bar at the right edge.

Please try again, I think it should work like that in your computer too.

-o-

Another option (if you know the name) is to type the first few letters of the name. For example, you know Firefox, so type 'fir' or 'Fir', both work.
