Hi,

Just installed a new computer with ubuntu 10.10. I assign shortkey alt+1 to move workspace left and alt+2 to move workspace right.

When I move workspace the focus application move with me to the new workspace. That is kind of annoying since I usually build up my workspace with mail here, music here, surf here. After sometime with this new "feature/bug" I got application all over the place.

1) How do I turn this off?
2) How do I enable dragging application from one workspace to another.

(Visual effects is set to Normal)

Thanks!

### Post by mcduck on 2011-02-07
1. Where (and how) exactly did you configure this shortcut?

Compiz plugins have two separate options for moving from a desktop to another, a normal one and then one that moves the active window with you. Sounds like you binded the shortcut to wrong one... ;)

2. In CompizConfig Settings manager, under Desktop Wall/Viewport Switching or Rotate Cube/General (depending on which one you are using) enable the the "Edge Flip Move"-option.
