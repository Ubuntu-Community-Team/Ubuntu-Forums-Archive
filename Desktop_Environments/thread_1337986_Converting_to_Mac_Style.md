### Post by blackened on 2009-11-26
Most of the things on that link are just theme changes that should have little to no impact on system performance. 

The only things mentioned that might slow you down are Avant Window Navigator and maybe Screenlets.

All of the changes are very easy to undo: revert the theme of gdm and gtk/metacity back to the originals and stop using AWN and Screenlets.
