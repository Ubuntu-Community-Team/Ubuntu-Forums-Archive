### Post by yager on 2006-07-15
The book "Ubuntu Hacks" published by O'Reilly has a section on exactly what you seem to be looking for.  Hack #4 describes how to customize the Live CD.  Basically you copy the Live CD to a temporary swap as an ISO image which you then mount, make all the required changes, ie add and/or remove packages, do the setups required... Lastly you unmount the image, repack the ISO and finally burn it to a fresh CD.

It is almost 6 pages of directions and this is at the high level without the specific detail you may be looking for in some areas.  Check your local book store or perhaps your library and review this to see if it meets your needs.  You could also order it online from Amazon.com
