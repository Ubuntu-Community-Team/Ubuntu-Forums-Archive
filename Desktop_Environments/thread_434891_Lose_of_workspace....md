### Post by jfinkels on 2007-05-07
> **Helix_Spear said:**
> I am not using Xubuntu... Mine is jus Ubuntu...

Right click on the Workspace Switcher panel applet (if it is visible on a panel; if not, right click on a panel, choose "Add to Panel..." and select "Workspace Switcher") and select "Preferences...". From here, change the number of workspaces to whatever you want.
