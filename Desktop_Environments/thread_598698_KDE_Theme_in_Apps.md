### Post by iflanery on 2007-11-01
In the Control Center, click on "Appearance" and go to "GTK Fonts and Settings". From there you can choose which style you'd like your GTK applications to use.

### Post by TeaSwigger on 2007-11-01
As iflanery says; Firefox is GTK. Also with some apps, you may be able to specify some of the appearance config in the launch command. To find out enter the name of the app and --help in a terminal to see if it has any helpful info in that direction and go from there.
