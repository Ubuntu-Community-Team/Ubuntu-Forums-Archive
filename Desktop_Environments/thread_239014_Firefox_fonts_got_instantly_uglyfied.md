### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-08-18
Those patches messed with your Fonts display.  Go to System > Preferences > Font   and reset your setting back to what they were before...sometimes trial and error in the Font panel is the best way to determine how you want you display to look....a quick way to check, is to have a browser like Firefox open so you can remember what it currently looks like, then modify your settings, then restart Firefox.
