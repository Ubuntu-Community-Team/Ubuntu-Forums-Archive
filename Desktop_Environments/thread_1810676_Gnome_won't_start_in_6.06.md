### Post by sanderd17 on 2011-07-23
The failed to fetch errors are because 6.06 has reached the end of life for quite a long time (at least the desktop edition). You really should upgrade.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/EOLUpgrades)

If you really have no possibility to upgrade, you should change your package resources to the archives found here: [http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/](http://old-releases.ubuntu.com/)
