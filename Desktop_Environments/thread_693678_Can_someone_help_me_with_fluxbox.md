### Post by mojoman on 2008-02-11
Check under the fluxbox menu (right-click on screen) under configure/toolbar. Maybe you have unchecked the "visible" option or maybe you have "auto-hide" enabled (if so, the toolbar should emerge when the cursor is at the bottom of the screen). 

Fluxbox is very bare out-of -the-box. *You* have to configure it *yourself* but it's well worth the effort. Check out_[ this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=371144")_ to get your menu up and running properly.

Also, Fluxbox is a window manager so it doesn't come with icons out-of-the-box. If you want icons, I would recommend _[idesk.]("http://idesk.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Main_Page")_ I believe it's in the repositories. 

/mojoman
