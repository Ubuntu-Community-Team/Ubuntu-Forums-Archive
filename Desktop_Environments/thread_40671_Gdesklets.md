### Post by Pitbull11188 on 2005-06-09
I Love Gdesklets! They are the best thing since sliced bread, Here is a short tutorial on how to get Gdesklets running on your system.

1.) From Terminal "sudo gedit /etc/apt/sources.list"
2.) Uncomment the two lines mentioned in the comments.
3.) From Terminal "Sudo apt-get update"
4.) From terminal "Sudo apt-get install Gdesklets"
5.) Now go under Applications -> Accessories --> gDesklets
6.) Now go to [http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/categories.php](http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/categories.php)
7.) Pick out several desklets that you like and save them to your hard drive.
8.) DO NOT EXTRACT THEM
9.) Drag and drop each desklet package into the gDesklet's window opened in #5.
10.) Then click on the category under which it was installed.
11.) Double click a desklet to start it.
12.) Configure and position the desklet.
13.) Enjoy your new desklet!
14.) Can't leave off with 13 now can I? [-X 

Also, I would appreciate it if everyone could take the time to post their favorite desklet. Thanks!
