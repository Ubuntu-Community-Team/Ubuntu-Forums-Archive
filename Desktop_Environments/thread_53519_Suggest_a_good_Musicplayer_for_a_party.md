### Post by Shiven on 2005-08-01
personally, i use rhythmbox for all music, just set up a playlist, add 10 songs or so to start the night, and tell people to drop the music into the playlist...

no visualisation though...

Amarok is supposed to be exceptional, although i have never tried it, perhaps look into that?

if you're looking for something winamp-esq then you'll not go past Beep-Media-Player. Gtk+2 interface makes it much prettier than xmms, and it can use the xmms visualisations afaik, the only drawback is that the person would have to go wading through your music directory to play a song- a search function would be better imo.

they are my picks of the bunch, of course there is muine (which isn't bad, but amarok does the same stuff) and other random stuff that i can't think of...

look around, try a few, you can always remove them ;)
