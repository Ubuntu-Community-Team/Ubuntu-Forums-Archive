### Post by Zill on 2008-04-16
Larissa:  I don't believe there is a way to change just the print fonts.  However, I have found that the following settings (Edit, Preferences, Mail Preferences, General) seem to give reasonable fonts both on the screen and when printing:

Uncheck "Use the same fonts as other applications"
Standard Font:	Sans	10
Fixed Width Font:	Monospace	  9

I suggest you experiment with these to see how they affect different mails :-)
