### Post by bingoUV on 2007-11-15
I am using intel GMA X3000 graphics (weaker than your 3100). I see almost zero CPU usage in doing compiz stuff (fast window switching, cube rotation, effects - fire, explode; etc.). 

But playing video takes about 15% of CPU (core 2 duo E 6300 1.8 GHz).

Intel graphics are much more power efficient than nvidia. nvidia 6xxx series was notorious for high power consumption, which they improved in 7xxx series.
