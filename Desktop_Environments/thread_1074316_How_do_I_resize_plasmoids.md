### Post by Monsieur Gonzalez on 2009-02-19
Hi!
Not sure about Jaunty, I'm on Intrepid here, but should be the same (4.2). Every widget/plasmoid will show a sidebar (left or right) with options to configure, delete or resize it. Just use the upper "square" icon on that sidebar, keep it selected (cursor+left click) and try moving it; size will change.

You may wanna take a look at the [visual guide for kde 4.2]("http://www.kde.org/announcements/4.2/guide.php"), lots of info there.

### Post by jacksaff on 2009-02-19
You need to 'unlock widgets' first, before the resizing bar appears. Ctrl+L is the default kde shortcut for this.
