### Post by whizbaby on 2006-09-11
As far as I know ubuntu does not (yet?) provide a rescue cd, I use knoppix for these purposes, download at
 
[http://knopper.net/knoppix-mirrors/index-en.html](http://knopper.net/knoppix-mirrors/index-en.html)

It comes with some diagnostic tools like testdisk (HD) and memtest86 (RAM, and it's part of ubuntu, too), just look over the pages here

[http://knopper.net/knoppix-info/index-en.html](http://knopper.net/knoppix-info/index-en.html)
