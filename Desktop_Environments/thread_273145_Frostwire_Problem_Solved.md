### Post by Scheater5 on 2006-10-07
A while back I posted about Frostwire hanging during connection, and no one had a solution.  I just wanted to post that I discovered the problem, and "fixed" it.  Turns out the network here at my university managed to block Limewire via firewall, and it catches Frostwire as well.  
Well, recently I read a post about someone using Apollon, and managed to get it up and running succesfully.  And, more importantly, the firewall doesn't block it.  Just a note to anyone who doesn't have control over the router or firewall, Apollon could be a viable alternative.
