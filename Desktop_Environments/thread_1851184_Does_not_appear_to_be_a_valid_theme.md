### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-09-28
Under 11.04's Unity, you want to look at the GTK 2.x themes.

However, notice that themes will sometimes come compressed with several variations in a single .tar.gz or even .zip archive, and you might have to extract it or poke around in the archive to figure out what you're looking at.

### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-09-28
Themes that include Emerald window borders and other items will need to be extracted . Sometimes this  information given in the theme description and sometimes not . 

I have found some outdated themes that simply don't work any more though the description says GTK 2.+ . The bulk of these did not come from Gnome Look or Ubuntu Art .org .
