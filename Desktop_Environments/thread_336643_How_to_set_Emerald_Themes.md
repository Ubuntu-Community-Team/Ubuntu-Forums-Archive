### Post by KipBond on 2007-01-13
> **sjpritch25 said:**
> I tried just clicking on the theme, but that doesn't work (from Emerald Theme).   Just don't know what to do to make it change.

Clicking on the theme selects that theme for use the next time the Window Decorator loads.  You can right-click the Beryl/Emerald icon, and click "Reload Window Decorator" to load the selected theme.
