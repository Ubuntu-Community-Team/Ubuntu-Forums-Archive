### Post by _Narcisse_ on 2009-08-27
Hey,

You have to go to the emerald theme manager, choose the edit theme tab, then titlebar and there's an obscure positioning section in Title-bar object Layout.

For example, mine with the text at the center and the icons on the right looks like this :

<code>I:T:HNXC:Normal Layout</code>

It think preselected layouts depends of your current theme. Play with it a bit with the letters.
