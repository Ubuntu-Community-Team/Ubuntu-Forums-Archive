Is it possible to have Eye of GNOME as default picture viewer in KDE?

---

### Post by ceelo on 2007-05-06
Yes, KDE refers to it as "Image Viewer". If you have it installed it should be under the "Graphics" section.

Right-click an image > Open With > Other > Graphics  > Image Viewer

Check "Remember application association for this type of file"
