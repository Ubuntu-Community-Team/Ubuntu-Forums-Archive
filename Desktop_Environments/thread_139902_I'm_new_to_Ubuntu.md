### Post by aysiu on 2006-03-05
Gamers can let you know whether that game will work.
There's something called Cedega, which helps run a lot of native Windows games.

As for the Word and Excel documents--they will open and will be modifiable and save-able. The formatting may be a little off (it may not--it depends how complex the document is). Why don't you try out a live CD? It doesn't affect your hard drive--it runs off the CD and your computer's RAM.
