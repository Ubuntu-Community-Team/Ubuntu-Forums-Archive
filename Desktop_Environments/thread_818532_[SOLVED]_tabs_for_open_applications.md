### Post by bmac on 2008-06-04
Right click on panel (taskbar) which you want the applications to show when minimized and select "Add to Panel".
Find and select "Window List" left click on it once.
Left click on "+Add" at bottom and close the "Add to Panel" window.

Hope this helps....
