### Post by PhilGil on 2013-06-21
The bottom "panel" is called a dock and is fairly easy to achieve. I'm not sure what packages are available in the Ubuntu repo but a search for "dock" should come up with 2 or 3 to try.

The top panel is a Cinnamon theme called [void]("http://cinnamon-spices.linuxmint.com/themes/view/104"). It's most likely a single panel with a transparent background in the center. You may be able to do the same with the KDE panel or try a replacement panel like tint2 (which I believe does permit you to place more than one panel along the same screen edge). Configuring tint2 isn't easy for a new user, but it's an interesting exercise if you're up to it.
