### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-05-29
It works that way because Nvidia failed to produce a video driver for that set up and Linux community developers had to try and produce a driver without access to Nvidia code. Recently Nvidia started to work on a driver.

[http://www.webupd8.org/2013/04/nvidia-releases-linux-graphics-drivers.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2013/04/nvidia-releases-linux-graphics-drivers.html)

Do not expect the same experience with Nvidia Optimus technology on Linux as you get with Windows.
