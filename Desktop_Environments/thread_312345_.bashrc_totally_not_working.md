### Post by 5-HT on 2006-12-04
What about just copying over the default .bashrc from /etc/skel/.bashrc to ~/.bashrc?

If that doesn't help, just copy over /root/.bashrc to ~/.bashrc.
There are a few differences between /root/.bashrc and /etc/skel/.bashrc, however, so please take note of them.

Alternatively, you could just edit $PS1 in your .bashrc.
