How do you get the groovy cube thing for when you're switching between desktops?

### Post by tuxxy on 2008-10-27
You need to enable your video drivers and then install ccsm compiz settings manager.

```
sudo apt-get install ccsm
```

Load the application at system > prefs > advanced desktop effects and navigate to General Tab > Desktop Size and switch Horizontal Virtual Size to 4 

To enable the cube navigate to Desktop Tab and enable Desktop Cube & Rotate Cube plugin.

### Post by Joh739 on 2008-10-27
To get more then 2 virtual desktops you just right click on the switcher at the right bottom and press *Preferences* and change the Columns to 4 or 16 or whatever number you prefer.

Hope this helps.
