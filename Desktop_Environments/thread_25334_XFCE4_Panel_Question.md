### Post by maruchan on 2005-04-09
Is there a way to automatically extend/fit the XFCE panel to the extreme edges of the screen? I am a bit annoyed by the fact that there are two rectangular areas at either end of the screen where you can see my desktop wallpaper. It looks a bit strange with apps maximized.

### Post by taygan on 2005-04-09
Wow, that never thought occurred to me.  

Why don't you autohide the panel (xfce panel settings>autohide) and then enlarge the maximize window to fit the whole screen (xfce workspace settings>workspace 1>tab to margins>set bottom to 0).

Then you don't have the rectangular sections and you have a larger window to work with.

### Post by stevetone on 2005-04-22
If you add "Taskbar" using "Add New Item" to your panel, there is an option to "Expand to Fill Screen".
