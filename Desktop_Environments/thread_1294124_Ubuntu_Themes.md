### Post by CokeDealer on 2009-10-17
As you have mentioned that the theme was installed, i assume you know that it's as easy as a drag and drop of the .tar.gz that you downloaded into the appeareance window when showing themes.

Some themes in gnome-look.org are not complete. Some only have window decorations (what i suppose you got), while others only appear in the controls tab, when customizing a theme.

I suggest that you check all the information written by the creator of the theme, so that you can find out what he is using to get those windows.

As for a dock (the useful thing at the bottom of the pic), there are various options:I suggest Cairo Dock (find its launchpad page and follow the instructions to enable their repository), or Docky (type in a terminal: sudo apt-get install gnome-do ). Both have their advantages and capabilities, as well as their extras. It's a matter of taste for you to find something that satisfies your needs.

Welcome to Ubuntu. Have fun learning and tweaking!
