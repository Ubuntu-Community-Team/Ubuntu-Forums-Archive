### Post by Sef on 2006-01-02
If the data is really critical, you'd want to have a back up away from your house.   If you had a fire or other tragedy, you could lose it all.   A cheap way to do that is to burn a cd and put it in a place other than your house.  If you do this weekly, you wouldn't lose more than a week of data.
