How can I disable thumbnail views for pdfs and images?  I just want to see them as normal icons.

---

### Post by Yashiro on 2009-02-02
Open any folder.
Goto the Edit menu.
Select Preferences.
Preview tab.

Change the options.

Great info, just wanted to add a little more detail to it:

If you were like me, and only wanted VIDEO files to not preview, you should follow Yashiro's advice but choose the middle option "Other Previewable Files" and just choose "Never" under the first drop-down box labeled "Show thumbnails"
