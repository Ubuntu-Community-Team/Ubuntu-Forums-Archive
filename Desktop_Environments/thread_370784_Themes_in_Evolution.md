### Post by louis_nichols on 2007-02-26
To my knowledge, Evolution doesn't feature themes. Its looks is taken from the desktop-wide GTK/Gnome settings. I can imagine that, using it in Kubuntu, it will look pretty ugly. You can solve this by installing the package [gtk2-engines-gtk-qt]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/edgy/kde/gtk2-engines-gtk-qt"). This will make it look like your KDE apps.
