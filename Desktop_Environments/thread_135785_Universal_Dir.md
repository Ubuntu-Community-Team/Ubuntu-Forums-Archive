### Post by Brunellus on 2006-02-24
if you mean the universe repository (in synaptic) there is a very easy way of doing it.

Start up synaptic, go to settings, repositories, and click universe and multiverse.  save, reload, and you're good.

Alternatively, uncomment the universe and multiverse lines in /etc/apt/sources.list
