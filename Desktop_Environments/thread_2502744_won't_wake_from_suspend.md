### Post by him610 on 2024-11-28
@Autodave
Don't know if this will work for you or not.

I had a similar problem with an adjacent computer through an KVM A/B switch. One of the PCs would go into sleep/suspend mode and would not respond/wakeup after switching to that PC.

Resolved by going into Settings ->  Power Manager, tab Display, under Display power management (On), move all sliders (Blank after, Put to sleep after, Switch off after) to far left (Never).
