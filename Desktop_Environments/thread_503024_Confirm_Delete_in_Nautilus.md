### Post by jim_p on 2007-07-17
If you open up Nautilus's Preferences (in a Nautilus window go to Edit >> Preferences),
in the Behaviour tab, there are 2 options conserning Trash.
The 2nd one will make an extra "Delete" cmd that overrides Trash AND will promt you for the action.
The 1st one will enable that prompt.
In neither situation you will be prompted wih a message "Send to trash  yes / no".
