I have solved this problem.  I'll post the solution for anyone who might suffer the same.

First, because there wasn't any reply to this, it might suggest that there isn't a way to personally configure the number.

Also, where as I had referred to this feature "Apps Lens", I was looking at and trying to fix the problem I was experiencing with "Recent Apps" and "Recently Used".  I was having the same problem with both.

The "Recently Used" lens still only shows 6.  I wish there were a way to increase this number.  However, at present (and possibly on your system) the "Recent Apps" lens gives you the option of clicking on a number or defaults to 6 when you first start the computer (Option: Recent # more results/See Fewer results).  The function is satisfactory.

On my system it was resolved by running "unity --reset".  I don't know what caused the problem.  It might have been a glitch inherent from having upgraded to version 12.04 from 11:10.

Thanks for anyone who had read this thread and gave some thought in helping with the problem when I was having it.  I hope my experience shared here will help anyone else that experience this glitch and spare them of the frustration of the lost functionality.
