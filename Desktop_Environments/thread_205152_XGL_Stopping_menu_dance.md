### Post by stipes on 2006-06-28
You can also turn it off completely. The easiest way to do this is to open up Gset-Compiz. Click the plugins button to open up the plugins settings. Find the "wobbly" tab. Scroll down, and you should see some checkboxes on the right. Uncheck "map shiver effect". This should get rid of the bouncy menus, and also the bouncy tooltips (both things that annoyed me when I first starting using XGL/Compiz).

Hope that helps.
