### Post by aaaantoine on 2008-11-16
Ah! I found it.

Enable the GNOME Session Management plugin.

**Note:** To enable plugins in gnome-do, you can either right-click the notification area icon or click the little menu arrow in the upper right corner of the gnome-do screen, and select Preferences.  Then, go to the Plugins tab, and click the check box next to a plugin to enable it.
