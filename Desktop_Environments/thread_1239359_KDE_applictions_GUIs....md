### Post by davbren on 2009-08-13
Hey all, I mean not to anger anyone, (good start i know!), but I've been using KDE recently. I love plasma, it's come on leaps and bounds since KDE4's release. I have a few concerns with the default applications though. I think it's great to have that unified look among all applications in KDE. I'm not sure that the right GUI has been chosen. 

What I'm suggesting is big and probably very difficult. What do you think the Kubuntu and KDE communities would think if a massive overhaul of the application GUIs was undertaken?

I firmly believe that the new KDE can overtake Gnome in its current popularity. Gnome 3.0 has nothing, imho, on KDE4. I also think that KDE4 can capture the non-Linux users' imagination much more than Gnome. 

Perhaps a large scale survey as to how the applications should look. I think Oxygen is nice, I have no beef there. I only have a problem with how its used. If we look at the competition in Windows and Mac, we can see the work they have put into an ultimately unified design across all aspects of OS and its applications. For Linux I understand that it is more difficult as they are made by different people in different places. 

Let me know your thoughts. I may well be totally barking in my thoughts but I'm really excited by where KDE is going right now.
