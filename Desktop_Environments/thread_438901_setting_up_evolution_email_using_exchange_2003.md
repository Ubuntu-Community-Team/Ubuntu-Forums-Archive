### Post by tact on 2007-05-10
Hi there,

The username/password  it is asking for is whatever you type in if you were to use a web browser and go to your OWA website yourself and log in to see your email.  (ie your exchange mail user/password.)

I use evolution to access my corporate mail account too.  Prepare yourself to accept that evolution is a long way from perfect.  It has some serious "quirks" but sitll I use it every day (out of necessity) and have gotten used to the shortcomings.

At a command prompt: "evolution --force-shutdown" > go have a coffee > come back and restart evolution is something to learn to love.
