### Post by gThree on 2006-07-05
Streamtuner is a great browser for Shoutcast and other services.  It lets you create bookmarks and then listen to them in the player of your choice (xmms, vlc, etc).  Best option if you're really into internet radio.  I had problems at times with Rhythmbox and don't think streams are Amarok's strong suit, but they're definitely worth a look.

Also, I find it easier to use a text editor to maintain a master playlist of favorites in m3u format.  Syntax is simple so it's not that big a deal.  Makes it easier to transport favorites between players and platforms.
