### Post by beewer on 2005-09-12
Hi.

I have a Kubuntu 5.04. I have set Finnish keyboard layout to KDE and everything seems to ok in every other application but Konversation and KSirc. If I write words with scandinavian characters (ä,ö,å) with those applications other people don't see them correctly. They just see them as 'boxes'.

Could some one please tell me what should I do to get scandinavian characters to work in Konversation and KSirc?

### Post by f1dave on 2005-09-12
It would seem strange to me that your characters work in most kde apps bar konversation and ksirc- I mean, they're all part of KDE and should follow the same language encodings, etc. The only thing I can suggest is trying gaim, xchat and irssi (text-based in a console) and seeing if those work with proper characters.

### Post by samwyse on 2005-09-12
You need to change the encoding to ISO-8859-15 in your irc clients.

### Post by beewer on 2005-09-12
