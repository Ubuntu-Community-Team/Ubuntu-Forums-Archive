### Post by flanaganj on 2013-08-27
I am also running Ubuntu 13.04, coincidentally on a couple of old Aspire laptops (5750, and 5550) and this also happens to me occasionally, but I know why. These laptops are shared among my two daughters, my wife, and myself, and each of us has a separate account. It seems the Mute setting must be set at the system level, because if one person mutes the sound and later logs off, the next person to log in has the sound muted, even if they did not have it muted when they last logged off.

I would think this is a bug, but it has not been worth it for me to look into it.
