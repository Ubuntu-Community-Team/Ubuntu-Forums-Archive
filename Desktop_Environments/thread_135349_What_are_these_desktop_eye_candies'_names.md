### Post by varunus on 2006-02-23
That's called XGL.  Its a new piece of awesome software written by Novell programmer David Reveman.  You need to have the unstable version of Ubuntu to use it however (called the dapper drake).

If you'd rather run a stable system, then wait until April, when Ubuntu Dapper will be released.  Dapper is really stable for me at least so far, with little to no glitches...its very impressive, especially for "unstable" software.  You could try installing one of the dapper "flight" CDs; these have been tested to have no show-stopping bugs.  (Flight 4 just came out, and includes the newest GNOME, along with XGL present in the universe repository.)
