### Post by mörgæs on 2011-05-21
It is possible to upgrade offline, but I do not recommend it. There have been released lots of bug fixes to 11.04, and best is to apply them as early as possible, that is during the process.

Better yet is a clean install.

If you by any means can move the computers to a place with internet access, this is the best solution.
