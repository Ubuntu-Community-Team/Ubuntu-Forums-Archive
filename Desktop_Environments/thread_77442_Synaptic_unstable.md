### Post by kayas80 on 2005-10-17
I fixed it. It turns out that it was crashing because I had configured xorg to include settings for the Composite manager. Once removed it stopped both Synaptic crashing and certain websites viewed in Firefox from crashing.

I wish they could make Composite Manager more stable, it's would make Breezy look super sweet! :p
