### Post by Darkshade on 2009-02-13
<Super+N> or <Super+M> will turn your desktop back to normal.

*Super = Meta = Win

It's a Compiz-Fussion plugin called Negative. You can find it under CompizConfig Settings Manager in your menu or running "ccsm", where you can also configure or disable it.
