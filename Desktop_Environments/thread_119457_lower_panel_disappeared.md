### Post by Wolki on 2006-01-19
[QUOTE=jason2005]my panel at the bottom of the screen was delated by accident.
please advise how can I  bring it back .  
thanks.[/QUOTE]

Right-click the upper panel, "New panel", right-click the new panel, "Add to panel", drag the stuff you want there to the new panel.

Default bottom panel is "Show Desktop", "Window List", "Workspace Selector", "Trash".
