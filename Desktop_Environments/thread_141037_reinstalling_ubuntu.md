### Post by aysiu on 2006-03-07
Files you *installed*, yes.
Data files... ? Well, it depends if you have a separate /home partition or not.

If you don't have a separate /home partition, popping in the CD and reinstalling will erase everything you have and put a clean copy of Ubuntu on your hard drive.
