### Post by vigleik on 2006-07-09
I don't quite understand what you're asking. Some screensavers do use lots of resources, and their purpose is to give people with a high-end computer a way to show off. Just as some games require a high-end computer.

These screensavers/games usually require the most of your graphics card, and will not work well if you have an old/cheap graphics card. If you think your graphics card should be up to the task, you might have to configure the driver (for example, use the official nvidia driver for a nvidia card).

Vigleik
