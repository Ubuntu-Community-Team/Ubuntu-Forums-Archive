### Post by kerry_s on 2006-10-30
You can unpack the icons and put them in /home/(you)/.icons or /usr/share/icons(as root), you have to create the folder if it's not there in your home folder. For themes it would be ~/.themes. For the individual icons just right click the icon>properties and click on the picture. Also a little tip on adding prgram launchers, you can find them with appfinder and just drag them to the launcher window.
