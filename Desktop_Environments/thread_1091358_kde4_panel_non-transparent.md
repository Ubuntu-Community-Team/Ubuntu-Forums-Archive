Don't be stupid.

@OP or for anyone else wondering: Download the [AYA Plasma Theme]("http://www.kde-look.org/content/show.php/Aya?content=76197") and apply it to the desktop. If you only want to apply it to the panels: system Settings > "Advanced" tab > Desktop Theme Details > Panel Background (Select Aya). Then right click on your desktop and select 'Appearance Settings" and under desktop theme select Customized.
