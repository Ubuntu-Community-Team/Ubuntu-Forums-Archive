### Post by hariks0 on 2010-02-06
You can use 'Add helper' under Accessibility of ccsm. Refer the screenshots.Clicking somewhere in a window makes all other windows transparent and clicking somewhere in the desktop makes all windows transparent and shows the desktop. You can also use corner bindings to show desktop without any keystroke or mouse click.

Visit the link at my signature for more tips on ccsm setup.
