Hello :) If our lines aren't crossed, there are two: gtk-theme-switch and gtk-chtheme. Both are old, flawed and in need of maintenence they probably won't get. gtk-chtheme is the more advanced in many ways, but only works with gtk2 while gtk-theme-switch has a gtk1 and gtk2 component. You should be able to just install them from the repos. Call them with 'gtk-chtheme', 'switch' (gtk1) and 'switch2' (gtk2). I don't expect there are issues with having both. However I didn't try either when I was trying Xubuntu, so I can't say if there are issues, or how to integrate them if needed. I seem to recall Xfce's theme selector being pretty nice.
