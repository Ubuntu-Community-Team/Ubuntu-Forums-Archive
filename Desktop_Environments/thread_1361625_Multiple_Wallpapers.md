### Post by freeball1 on 2009-12-30
You open ```
gconf-editor
``` and disable apps->nautilus->preferences->show_desktop

Then in ccsm you enable wallpaper in the utility-section and add your wallpapers.

P.S. This will also make your desktop-icons disappear
