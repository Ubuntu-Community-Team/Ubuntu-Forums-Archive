### Post by t2kburl on 2011-05-01
I could be wrong here, but I think you have to be running the KDE desktop environment to use plasmoids.

To go to KDE you need to install kubuntu-desktop and choose kdm when it asks for your default desktop environment. You can later choose unity or KDE or whatever else you may have from a menu on the login screen.
