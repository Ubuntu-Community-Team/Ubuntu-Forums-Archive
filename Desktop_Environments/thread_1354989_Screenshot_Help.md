### Post by leonardo_neo on 2009-12-14
I think if I am able to understand your question, then I have a solution. You want to have a screenshot with application tab open, right?

Go to Accessories ---> Take screenshot

in "Grab after a delay" increase the timer from 0 to somewhat 10 seconds.

Then press take screenshot. Now whatever tab you want to open, open at your comfort, and place your pointer over there. The screenshot application will take the screenshot after 10 seconds.
