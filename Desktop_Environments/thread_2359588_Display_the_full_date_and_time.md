### Post by again? on 2017-04-25
GNOME Tweak Tool > Top bar
or for more customization the extension [Datetime Format]("https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/1173/datetime-format/")
To move clock ---> [Frippery Move Clock]("https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/2/move-clock/")
