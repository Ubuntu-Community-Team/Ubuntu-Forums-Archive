### Post by Frogs Hair on 2013-01-04
Mate is Gnome 2 based so the current version of UT may not work properly with Mate. I saw a thread on the Mint forum suggesting an older version. You may want to check on compatibility with mate because releases for Mint or Mate are not mentioned on the UT launchpad page. Releases for ubuntu 11.10 -12.10 are listed.
