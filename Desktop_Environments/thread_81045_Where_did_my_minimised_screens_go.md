### Post by LorenzoD on 2005-10-23
What probably happened was that your window list crashed. That is why you got a message asking you if you wanted to reload it.

To fix this, go to the bottom panel and right click. In the menu that pops up, invoke the "Add to panel" item. It will bring up a window with several different applets and other things. Find the item called "Window List". If you're on Breezy at least, the item is under the section called "Desktop & Windows". Click on the "Add" button on the bottom of that dialog.

If your window list doesn't reappear, it could be related to the crash. Log out and back in again.
