### Post by mcduck on 2007-11-12
You could put your shortcuts on a panel, and make it hidden. It would slide in when you move your mouse to the edge of the screen.

Also Compiz allows you to define any program as a widget, hidden by default and only shown when you hit some key combo, just like the dashboard in OS X. If you find a suitable app for launching your shortcuts you could use that to create the functionality you are looking for..
