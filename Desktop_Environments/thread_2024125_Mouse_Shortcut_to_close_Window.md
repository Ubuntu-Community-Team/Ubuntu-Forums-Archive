### Post by stinkeye on 2012-07-14
Don't know how to bind close widow to ctrl+leftmouse
but you could use **easystroke** to bind a mouse gesture to alt+F4
or a spare side button to alt+F4.
eg: I use a "Z" gesture to close the window.
