### Post by gunnyman on 2005-04-28
Fixed it myself in case anyone runs into this.
I switched over to my KDE desktop and ran the KMix.
Under switches, there is a radio button for TONE.  It was disabled.  I enabled it, and voila, Bass and Treble now work.
