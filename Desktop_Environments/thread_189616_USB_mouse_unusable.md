### Post by pksings on 2006-06-05
Upgraded working Breezy, failed to compete, 3 times, formatted, re-installed from scratch, completed fine. 

Now with my USB mouse connected the pointer slowly and jerkily descends to the lower right hand corner.  It works fine when I dual boot to Winblows.

Adjusting the sensitivity and stuff makes no difference.  

The touchpad and keyboard pointer stick work just fine. (this is a laptop)

Solved, unplug USB keyboard/mouse PS2 adapter, Unplug USB mouse. Plugin USB mouse allow to settle, plugin USB keyboard/mouse adapter. 

Now works fine. 

Apparently they are found in reverse order when the machine boots and the drivers conflict.
