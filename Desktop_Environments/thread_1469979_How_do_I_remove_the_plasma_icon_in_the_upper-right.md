### Post by Kokopelli on 2010-05-02
[https://launchpad.net/~samrog131/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~samrog131/+archive/ppa)

This ppa has the, unfortunately named,  "I hate the Cashew" widget (the one referred to above).  It removes the cashew, does not hide it.  KDE, for reasons of their own, refuse to provide an official option to remove the cashew.

Personally this is one of the first PPA's and widget that I install.  :)
