### Post by vinoo_v on 2005-06-01
1. Install the 'ooqstart-gnome' package using either apt-get or synaptic. 
2. Add the Open Office Quick Starter applet to your panel by right clicking and selecting 'Add to panel'.
3. Right click on the applet select preferences and check the box 'Enable Quickstarter'. (Fill in the path to openoffice as /usr/bin/openoffice)
