### Post by celloandy on 2006-09-01
I couldn't say, for sure, but honestly, I doubt it.  Flash, in general, is much more poorly supported on Linux than it is under Windows.  The official Adobe/Macromedia Flash player for Linux is two versions out of date (though apparently Flash 9 is in the works), and none of the GPL flash players are really satisfactory, at this point, so trying to embed a Flash animation into screensaver software would be very difficult, at best.
