### Post by trumpcouptimmy on 2009-03-08
It took me a while but I figured it out and learned a lot about the initialization process. I solved it by installing a fresh copy of the operating system (same version of course) and copying all the init.d and all the rc*.d files from the fresh system to my broken system. It worked. That's why I fool around a bit with stuff-you learn how it works the hard way and you remember.
