### Post by ola on 2006-06-25
Hi,

There should be a Network icon on the gnome panel on top of your monitor. You can click on that and change the settings for your network.

If that doesn't work, there are a network configuration icon in the administration part of the gnome-menu.

Good luck and welcome to Ubuntu!
