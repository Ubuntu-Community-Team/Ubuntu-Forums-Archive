### Post by kazuya on 2006-10-04
Kubuntu is based on kde - has its menu or start panel from the bottom. Uses different apps. Some say it is more window-like in the menu structure.

Ubuntu is based on gnome desktop environment. - Menu or panel is from top. Some say more Mac-like in look.

Xubuntu is based on XFCE. This is my new favorite. It is much more light-weight in system or hardware resources than kubuntu or ubuntu. Things are slightly faster with this desktop environment than the rest while still being equally functional. 
It is less bloated.

Having said all this, I choose to have all three of them since they have advantages over each other in terms of look, customizations and certain apps that I prefer in one over the other. On a new PC, the speed difference starts to be negligible to your perception.

So enjoy them all and try them all out and you may start prefering one over the other in time.
