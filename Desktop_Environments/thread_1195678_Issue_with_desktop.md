### Post by Closed_Port on 2009-06-24
What you describe is the intended behavior of netbook remix as the desktop is used for other things, so you wouldn't be able to use the icons anyway. Are you really using netbook remix?

Anyway, you can tell nautilus to draw the desktop and you should have icons then:
Open gconf-editor (Alt-F2 and enter gconf-editor)
Open apps-> nautlius-> preferences and check show_desktop

Log out and in again and you should see the files on your desktop.
