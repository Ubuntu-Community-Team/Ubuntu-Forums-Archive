### Post by Krytarik on 2011-07-28
First, fix your Unity session by following this guide:
[http://ubuntu4beginners.blogspot.com/2011/04/missing-top-and-side-panels-in-unity.html](http://ubuntu4beginners.blogspot.com/2011/04/missing-top-and-side-panels-in-unity.html)

Then, after the next auto-login into a working Unity desktop, simply log out and then choose "Ubuntu Classic" as the "Session" option at the bottom of the login screen, after clicking on your username, but before entering your password.

Greetings.
