### Post by cayman1021 on 2010-10-25
Today I tried to solve the problem of including libxml library.
I removed libxml but not re-installed yet.
And I rebooted the Ubuntu again.
Then it just changed to **run in low-level mode**.

Because I am just new in Linux I don't know how to recover it.
Then I suddenly realized maybe it's the problem that I removed libxml.
So I used terminal to re-install the libxml and rebooted again.
My Ubuntu just worked again! Back to this colorful window.

I know it's **not** normal to remove something then just blindly reboot the system.
But maybe it's something about the stability or security for Ubuntu developers to improve? (In my humble opinion.)

Hope my case helps someone who met the same problem.
