### Post by hansdown on 2009-12-20
Hi VitaminCPP.

It means, while you "hold" the ALT key that is located on the left side of the keyboard.

The ALT key on the right side of the keyboard may have a different function.

### Post by paulbyr on 2009-12-20
It means to first press down the ALT key AND HOLD IT DOWN and then press the other key while you are holding ALT down.  Now you can release all the keys.  You have complete the required communication.

### Post by steveneddy on 2009-12-20

Hold down Alt then press the F2 key.

It will be written like this:

```
Alt+F2
```
