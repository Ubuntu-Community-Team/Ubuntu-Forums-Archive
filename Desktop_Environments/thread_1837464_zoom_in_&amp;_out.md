### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-09-02
Do you really mean Gnome 3, or the Unity desktop? In Gnome Shell, the magnifier is available under the Accessibility icon in the top bar. In Unity, you'll need to go into CompizConfig (compizconfig-settings-manager if you don't have it installed) and enable the "Enhanced Zoom Desktop" plugin, which can be set to zoom in when you, say, hold Super and scroll up or down.

### Post by jfed on 2011-09-02
By default (In GNOME2 at least) all you have to do is hold down the super key (the one with the windows icon) and scroll up/down to zoom in/out
