### Post by Peripheral Visionary on 2012-03-07
I'm still on 10.04, but to reach all of the Xfce settings I simply right-click the desktop, then choose *Applications > Settings > Xfce4 Settings*. From that screen I can select, add, subtract, move, and assign properties to all your panels. Perhaps your 11.10 works the same way.

### Post by 3Miro on 2012-03-07
In 11.10 it is called Settings Manager, that is, right-click on the desktop then select Applications -> Settings -> Settings Manager.

You can also go there if you right-click on the working panel on the bottom and then select Panel -> Panel Preferences. 

From there you can select "Panel" and you can add/remove panels as well as arrange applets on the panel.
