### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-06-26
Not sure what you ignored, but if you right click on an empty spot on the top panel and select "Add to Panel" you should find "Shut Down" in the list of items. Left click on Shut Down and at the bottom select "ADD" then "Close"

This should add the "Shut Down" icon to your top panel. Right click on the icon and select move. Move it to the right side of your panel and left click. Right click on the icon again and select "Lock to Panel".

If when you left click on the icon and you get an error, please note the error and add it to you thread.

Gook Luck and Hope this helps.
