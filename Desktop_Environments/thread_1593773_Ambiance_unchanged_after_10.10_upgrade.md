### Post by davidjnice on 2010-10-26
Hi.  You could try re-installing the package using Synaptic.

Using the 'quick search' find 'light-themes' (this package has the ambiance and radiance themes in it).  Right click and mark it to be reinstalled.  

You might even try changing your theme to something completely different first in case there is any trouble updating files that are in use.  (Were you using Ambiance when you upgraded by any chance?  That *might* explain why one theme is updated and the other isn't.  Just a thought.)
