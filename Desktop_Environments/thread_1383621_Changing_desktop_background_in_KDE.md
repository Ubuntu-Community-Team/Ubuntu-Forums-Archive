### Post by SuperSonic4 on 2010-01-17
KDE 4.4: Drag and drop a picture onto the desktop

KDE 4.3: [list]
[*]Right Click on blank part of desktop
[*]Select *Desktop Activity Settings*
[*]Do **One** of the following
[list=1][*]Select a default
[*]Select **Get New Wallpapers** and pick the one you like
[*]Select **Open** and navigate to the one you want
[/list]
[*]Select the picture from the main preview window
[*]Hit OK
[/list]
