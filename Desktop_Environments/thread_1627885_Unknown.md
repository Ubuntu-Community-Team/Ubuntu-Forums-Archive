### Post by asmoore82 on 2010-11-21
Most ATX-variant Power Supplies have a standard 80mm fan in them.
Some of the more expensive ones have a 120mm(DVD-size) fan.

The problem is that even though they are standard PC fans,
they are usually hard-wired in - [soldering]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soldering") work is required to replace them.

You're not supposed to, but I have been known to open them up and put a drop or two of 3-in-1 oil on them - they run smooth as silk for a few more months. If these fans ever completely fail and are left alone long enough they can present a Fire Hazard!
