### Post by the_maassk on 2006-07-28
AFAIK - Digital monitors display 'No Signal' when the video signal is out of sync for the monitor to handle. It could be the refresh rate or the resolution that Ubuntu configured and which the monitor is not able to handle.

I would suggest you reconfigure XServer. The reconfiguration process would ask you questions about the monitor, refresh rates and resolutions. Pass the correct values there and try to keep the resolution a little low for now (say 1024x768). Try restarting the XServer after that.
