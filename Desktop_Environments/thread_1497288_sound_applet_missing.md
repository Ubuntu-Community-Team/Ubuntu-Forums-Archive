### Post by marcellux on 2010-06-25

> hello,

ok. I found out what the problem was. It was not a problem indeed. things are just different now. 

with 9.10, notification area applet showed the networks on range (i.e. network manager) and the volume control.

now with 10.04 that notification applet just shows the network manager. if you want the volume to be shown you have to add the "indicator applet" to your taskbar, and it will also show an "envelope".
