### Post by Shadow ZERO on 2007-10-19
Hi, I'm using Kubuntu 7.10, and I do not like having the little vertical bars to the left of my panels on my taskbar that allows me to move them simply by dragging.  If i right click anywhere on the taskbar, i can lock the panels and remove those vertical bars.  Problem is, this disables all the menus and functionality until I unlock the panels.

Is there a way to remove that little side bar to the left of my panels without locking all the panels the entire taskbar?

---

### Post by TeaSwigger on 2007-10-20
Yup you sure can. :)

With taskbar unlocked, right click; configure panel; pick the appearance panel; under panel background hit the "advanced option" button; there you have your choice (applet handles are the things you're describing) to make them always visible, to appear when you mouse over the applet, or to stay hidden.

Very customizable, KDE :)
