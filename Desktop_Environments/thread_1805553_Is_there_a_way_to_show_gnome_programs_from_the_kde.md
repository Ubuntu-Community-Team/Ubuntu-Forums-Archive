Hmm, not sure why gnome-terminal doesn't show sorry. You could create your own launcher to it. Just right click the K menu and click Edit Applications. Then add a new item to the category of your choice (e.g. Utilities) and call it something like Terminal. Then in the command text box type in "gnome-terminal". You can also add a description and an icon and other stuff if you want. Then click save and close that dialog box. Then it will be under the category you selected and you can add it to your favourites by simply right clicking it and clicking "add to favourites".
