### Post by Firetech on 2005-05-23
The icon zooming was the mouse over effect of the taskbar in KDE 3.3. kubuntu runs 3.4, and now the mouse over effect is that disturbing big box with information.

### Post by Spleenie on 2005-05-25
You can make the taskbar transparent/translucent by modifying the layout properties under the appearance tab in "configure panel" (which you can access by right clicking your panel and choosing "configure panel"

In Advanced options, you can set the degree and tint for your translucency

As for the transparent window stuff, havent got that to work but I remember a section in window properties in the Control panel which says something about this being experimental.

You can't beat superkaramba for eye candy however :)
