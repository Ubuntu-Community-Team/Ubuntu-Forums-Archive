### Post by pommattski on 2006-10-07
It sounds as though you've lost your "window list" from the toolbar.  It, like everything else on the toolbar, can be removed or added. 

Right-click on a blank part of the tool-bar, and select "+ Add to Panel".  In the "Desktop & Windows" section, you should see "Window List"; select it and click "Add". Drag it to a position you like, right-click it and select "Lock to Panel".
Done.
