### Post by sethmahoney on 2005-04-30
Are you wanting the fancy icon bar at the bottom?  If so, do apt-get gdesklets or fire up Synaptic and get gdesklets (but don't download the gdesklets data from Synaptic), and then go to their website ([http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/](http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/)) and you'll find a fancy icon bar just like that to download, along with a bunch of other stuff.  If you need help installing them, we'll be here.

As far as the gnome bar itself and the window decorations goes, head to art.gnome.org and poke around for some themes.  You can also get icon themes from there.  Once you've got them downloaded, hit System->Preferences->Theme and then "Theme Details".  You can drag your newly downloaded themes onto the window that will open up and select them in the list box.  Hope that helps.
