I tried Docky but in addition to it apparently not having a main menu applet, one of the other problems I have with it is the "show desktop" applet.  I like to have the show desktop applet all the way in the bottom right-hand corner, and I like to have it hide underneath all the windows, so that if I throw my mouse down into that corner I can just click and have it show the desktop.  With Docky, and with Cairo-Dock for  that matter, they won't position the applet far enough to the right.

With Docky there doesn't seem to be a way to position the dock properly to make that happen, and in Cairo, you can create a secondary dock and put it in the corner but it doesn't put the applet in the very corner of the screen, it's a little to the left, so throwing the mouse into the corner doesn't work.
