### Post by zenthanian on 2008-02-09
Thunderbird has a plugin that is entitled GMailUI by Ken Mixter that enables Archiving (for any account not just Gmail).. I believe it has other features but I use it for archiving.  

How it works is you create a folder in the root of your email account entitled "Archive" and when viewing a message you simply click 'Y' and it moves that message over to the archive folder. (It even works for RSS feeds)

If you had a limited amount of storage for you account you could use this archive as a temp  folder and periodically copy them over to a local folder.

I would like to see this feature in Evolution -- or a hot key driven rule that applies to current message.
