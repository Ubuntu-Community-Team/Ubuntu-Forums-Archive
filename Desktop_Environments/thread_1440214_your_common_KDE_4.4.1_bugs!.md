### Post by jacksaff on 2010-03-27

The device manager pops up in the corner where half of it is invisible and whether or not it displays the actions for the inserted device seems entirely random.

The notifications widget pops up the same message repeatedly and annoyingly and there are very few settings for it.

The search service stops itself for no reason claiming lack of resources (often at startup) and seems very much a work in progress...

I have several users on my machine. When you tell kde to shut down it doesn't display the widget to shut all users sessions down until it has done other stuff for 30 seconds or so, by which time I have generally left expecting the machine to turn itself off. Which it hasn't.

Multiple users mucks up the volume control - it doesn't seem to work independently for each session.

Lack of firefox integration.

Amarok2 :(

Icons on the panel no longer scale with the panel.

And most important of all, I can no longer change the card table surface in kpat!!!
