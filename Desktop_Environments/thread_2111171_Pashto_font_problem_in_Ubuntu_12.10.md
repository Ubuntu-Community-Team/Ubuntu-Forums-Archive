### Post by techfried on 2013-02-01
ok apparently it's the font and the webpage that is the issue. The webpage design says to look for a certain font which is not present in ubuntu so it picks up another font. 

I don't think it's a solution but a workaround would be to go into Edit menu in firefox select preferences. In content tab, click on Advanced button in front of "Fonts & colors", uncheck "allow pages to show their own font". This way you'll force the page to show the fonts that you've selected above in the same window.
