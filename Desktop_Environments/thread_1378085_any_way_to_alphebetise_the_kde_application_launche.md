### Post by benerivo on 2010-01-11
You can right click the favourites selection to alphabetise it. To order the other sections, you can right click on the launcher and choose 'Menu Editor' where you can drag the items in to the order you want.

---

### Post by aarons6 on 2010-01-11
i actually figured out the way.. when you install program it puts them at the top out of order.. 
right click the KDE icon and choose menu editor.. then choose edit then restore system menu.

this reloaded the menu in order..
