### Post by htinn on 2006-03-14
Oops, just noticed. :P ADI V75F

Let's see:

[http://www.adi.com.tw/pro_v75F_s.htm](http://www.adi.com.tw/pro_v75F_s.htm)

So you just need to change the settings in your /etc/X11/xorg.conf where it says Section "Monitor" to reflect that:

> HorizSync 30 - 70
VertRefresh 50 - 160

EDIT: One more thing. Need to add that resolution to where it says Section "Screen" too.
