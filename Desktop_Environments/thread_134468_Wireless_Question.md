### Post by mmHg on 2006-02-22
Your current network layout and requirements are kinda vague, so correct me if I've interpreted this incorrectly.  You want to connect a kubuntu-run PC to a home network via a wireless connection, right?  To do this, you need a wireless access point in your network (a wireless router or something similar).  You also need a wireless card in the pc in question.  It's that simple.  I presume your friend was giving you a possible way to integrate a wireless AP into your network via an ethernet switch.
