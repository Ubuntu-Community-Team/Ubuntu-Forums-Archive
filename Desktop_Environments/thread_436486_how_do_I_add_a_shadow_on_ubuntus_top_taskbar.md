### Post by ahaurw01 on 2007-05-07
hey stealthbox,
Try firing up the Beryl Settings Manager, then go to Visual Effects -> Window Decorations, click on drop down arrow for appearance and check 'draw shadows on panel type windows'
enjoy!

### Post by ahaurw01 on 2007-05-08
Yeah, check out the Emerald Theme Manager--
click on the 'Edit Themes' tab, then the 'Frames/Shadows' tab lets you adjust color, opacity, radius, etc...
It does seem a little silly that you have to allow it in the Beryl Manager and then tweak it in the Emerald Manager, but I guess thats how life is.

---

### Post by ahaurw01 on 2007-05-08
Also, I forgot to add that you might have to restart the window decorator for your changes in the emerald manager to take place. Right click the beryl icon->'reload window decorator' (At least this is the case for me with beryl 0.2.0)
