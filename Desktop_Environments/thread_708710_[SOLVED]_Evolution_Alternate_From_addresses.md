### Post by MightyFlea on 2008-02-27
Thanks.

That brought me on the right track:
It is possible to have accounts for sending email only by selecting "None" as the receiving protocol.
This was necessary for me because the two email addresses share the same mailbox.

So I set up another sending-only account for the second address and all is well. :-)
