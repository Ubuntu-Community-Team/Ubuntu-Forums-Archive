### Post by avik on 2007-10-03
Those folders are tiny, and don't take up much space unless you delete something off your USB drive.  See, if you delete something off your USB drive, instead of going into the main Trash can on your desktop, it goes into the Trash can on your USB drive.  Empty the trash can on your USB drive, or just bypass it when deleting a file off your USB drive (Shift-Delete).
