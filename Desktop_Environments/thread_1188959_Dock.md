### Post by Clorow on 2009-06-16
There are three major docks out there: Avant Window Navigator, Cairo Dock, and Gnome Do (when you set the appearance preferences to "Docky").

There are reasons to like all of them. Personally, I prefer Gnome Do because of its search thing (which is actually its main functionality, not the dock), but Avant Window Navigator is pretty featureful also (with applets like those you would find for the Gnome panels).
