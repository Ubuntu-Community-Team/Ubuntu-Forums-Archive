### Post by Spider-One on 2005-10-30
Many ISP's are now throttling bittorrent upload speeds. If this is the case you would notice this globally with all torrent apps. The only app I've found to work is BitComet for Windows, which is disappointing at best. BitTornado has the option to Break-up Seed Bitfield To Foil ISP, or at least the latest version does but this does not work (at least in my case, perhaps it will work for you.) You could also try changing the port range in your preferences.
