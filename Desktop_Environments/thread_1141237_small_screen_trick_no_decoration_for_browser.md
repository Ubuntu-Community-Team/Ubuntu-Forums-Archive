### Post by gcbzzzz on 2009-04-28
Do you have a small screen?

Do you have a browser always maximized?

The tab already show the page title?

1. then go to System > settings > compiz-settings-manager

2. click window decorations

3. set Decoration windows: (any) & !(role=browser)


now your browser will not have borders or window title. the others windows from the browser (e.g. save as, options, extensions, etc) will still feature title and borders.
