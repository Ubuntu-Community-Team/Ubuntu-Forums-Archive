I installed e17 and I love it. There is just on thing I'm having trouble with. I want to turn the shelf into a panel on the top of the screen. I manage to make the shelf stretch across the top of the screen, but how do I make it so that maximized windows neither cover it or are hidden underneath it (like a gnome panel)? Thanks! :)

---

### Post by Freddy on 2008-02-11
Rightclick on the shelf you want to configure and choose 'Shelf Configuration'. Click the 'Advanced' tab and choose your layout (topcenter/corner) on stacking choose 'Below Windows' and unmark (if marked) 'Allow windows to overlap the shelf'
