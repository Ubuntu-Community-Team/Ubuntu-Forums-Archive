### Post by regal_dunce on 2009-06-18
A little bit more of a description of your problem might help.  i.e. "I have this file called myfile.tar.gz and I can't figure out how to open it!"

Here's a bit of a summary.

Tar takes a bunch of files and bundles them together into a single file.  It can compress them too.

If you want to get all of the individual files out of a tar archive, open a terminal and do:

```

tar -xvf yourfilename.tar

```
