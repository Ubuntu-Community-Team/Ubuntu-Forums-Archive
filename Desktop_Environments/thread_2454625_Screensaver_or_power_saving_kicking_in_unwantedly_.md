### Post by ajgreeny on 2020-12-03
OK. In the power-manager settings window there are options to show **System-tray icon** and **Notifications**, as in my screenshot.

Enable the system-tray icon and you will see the icon in the panel.
Right click in that and you should see a tick-box for **Presentation Mode**.
With that enabled all screen blanking, screensavers and timed system sleep will be disabled.
