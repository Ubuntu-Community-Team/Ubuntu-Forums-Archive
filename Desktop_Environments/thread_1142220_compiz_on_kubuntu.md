### Post by krazyd on 2009-04-29
> **pancham said:**
> i recently installed kde in ubuntu 9.04,,, but compizfuzion does not work in it....( cube does not form even when i have 4 desktops, instead only wall comes out as in with 2 desktops) please help....

Go to systemsettings->desktop->multiple desktops, and change to 4 desktops.

Then go to systemsettings->desktop->desktop effects->all effects, and enable desktop cube. Make sure you check out the configuration and note the activation key combo. I think by default it's ctrl+F11.
