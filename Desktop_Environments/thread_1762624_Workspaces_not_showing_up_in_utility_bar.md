### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-05-19
The bar is called a *panel*, for clarity. Are you using the new Unity interface, or the the Classic desktop? In the Classic desktop, just right-click the panel, select "Add to panel," and find the Workspace Switcher. In Unity, unfortunately, there's no equivalent.
