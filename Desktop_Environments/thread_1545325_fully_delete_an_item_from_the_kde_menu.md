### Post by Yuri sss on 2010-08-03
Perhaps there are still hidden entries for it left.
Open menu editor (right-click K menu), go to Settings > Configure and there under "misc", check "Show hidden entries".
Then look if there are any entries remaining for that item, delete them and click on "Save" (if you don't click on "Save", the deleted entries will be restored).
