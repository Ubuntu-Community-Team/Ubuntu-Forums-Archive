### Post by Johanc on 2006-01-27
From: [http://www.tutorialsforopenoffice.org/tutorial/Blocks_Of_Information.html#icon](http://www.tutorialsforopenoffice.org/tutorial/Blocks_Of_Information.html#icon)

Click Tools > Options. IF there is a + in the box before the words &#8220;Openoffice.org&#8221;, click on the + (A &#8211; (a dash) appears in the box.) Click View. In &#8220;Toolbar icon size&#8221; box select Small, Large, or Automatic. Click OK.

This basically solves my problem. The worst about the icons is that they are too big for the program so that some of them are missing. When they are small they are fine. Thanks for the help...
