You will need to choose between 1 and 3
Right click on the dock and go to Cairo-dock >> Configure
1 & 3. Under Behaviour tab, Visibility of main dock heading, choose either "Keep dock below widows" as in attachment or "Automatically hide the dock." 
2. Under the Appearance tab, Icons heading, set icon size to "very small"
