### Post by lisdavid89 on 2010-11-30
Try right-clicking the "System" menu and choose "Edit Menus".
Then under "Menus:" (left sidebar) scroll down to "System" and click that.
Then under "Items:" tick the check marks next to "Preferences" and "Administration".
If it still doesn't show up, then that means you don't have any items ticked in their respective menus. So then under "Menus:" you would click "Preferences" or "Administration" and under "Items:" you would have to check at least one thing to make it active. I would have given a screen capture but these new headers are buggy so screen capture doesn't work for me and I deleted my old headers already.

If nothing still shows, then I don't know what else could be the problem.
