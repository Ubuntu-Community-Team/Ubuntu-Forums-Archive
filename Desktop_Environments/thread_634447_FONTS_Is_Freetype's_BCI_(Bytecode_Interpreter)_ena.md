### Post by mlind on 2007-12-07
bytecode interpreter is enabled yes, as well as subpixel renderering (FT_CONFIG_OPTION_SUBPIXEL_RENDERING). You don't need to recompile anything if you're after the "improved subpixel rendering" -features. These patches all included in gutsy.
