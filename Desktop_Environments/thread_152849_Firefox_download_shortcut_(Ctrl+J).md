There is no shortcut for extension and themes. What I would do in your situation is right-click on the *navigation toolbar* and select "customize" and drag the menu bar to the *bookmarks toolbar*. Then, when you need the menu bar temporarily for the extensions window, you can do an Alt-V, T, B, Alt-T, E.
