### Post by Joey French on 2005-11-27
I had great success early on using the xirc channel to get help- quick like. Since I am fairly certain this will be new to you, let's get you setup as quickly as possible. Assuming you have xirc installed (if not, System> Administration> Synaptic Package Manager, Reload, then search Xchat IRC, select "xchat", apply), go to Applications>Internet>Xchat IRC. In the dialogue, "XChat server list", "Ubuntu Servers" will be at the top of the list. Fill in your relevant information, then hit "Connect". There you go. 
 
I have gotten help here a lot of times, on the fly, just ask away. There's almost always someone on who can help you, just be patient.

Good Luck!
