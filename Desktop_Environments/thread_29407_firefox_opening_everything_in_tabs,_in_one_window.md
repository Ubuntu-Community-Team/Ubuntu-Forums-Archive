### Post by localzuk on 2005-04-24
There are a couple of options available to you.

1. Click on links with the middle mouse button, or press ctrl and click on them
2. Take a look at [https://addons.update.mozilla.org/extensions/moreinfo.php?application=firefox&id=158](https://addons.update.mozilla.org/extensions/moreinfo.php?application=firefox&id=158) This extension adds the ability to customise tabbed browsing.

### Post by Domhnull on 2005-04-24
I use the [Tabbrowser Preferences extension](http://extensionroom.mozdev.org/more-info/tabprefs).  I use it to open all of my sites in tabs.  Other, related extensions:
[MiniT](http://extensionroom.mozdev.org/more-info/minit) - adds a few more tab features
[SessionSaver](http://extensionroom.mozdev.org/more-info/sessionsaver) - save your tab sessions
[Download Manager Tweak](http://extensionroom.mozdev.org/more-info/downloadmanagertweak) - open download manager in a tab
