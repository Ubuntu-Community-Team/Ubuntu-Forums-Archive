### Post by Tdraket on 2008-02-04
My computer occasionally does this too.  It's definitly not a window effect.  It's basically a visual notification that the program is intensely processing or is just frozen.  Either way there is nothing you can do about it as far as I know.  I've had that happen with all types of programs on my computer.  You can just leave it alone until the computer resolves the issue or you can kill the program and reopen it.
