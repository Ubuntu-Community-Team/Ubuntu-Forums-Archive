### Post by 5451vs5451 on 2006-06-24
I am running an ubuntu 5.10 breezy system. When an usb disk plugged in, each recognized partition is automatically mounted and a nautilus browser window is poped up for everyone of them. This is quite annoying for me. How can I modify the behavior?

---

### Post by Ubuntuud on 2006-06-24
System > Preferences > Removable drives and media. On the first tab you can choose not to mount them or prevent nautilus from popping up.
