### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-06-01
Hi and Welcome 

If you would like to apply one of the other themes that came with 11.04 right click the desktop and select change background and this will open Appearance Preferences . Select the Themes tab and choose one of the other themes.

If you select the customize tab in themes you will find additional controls , window borders , icons , and pointers .  With the colors option it is possible to change colors on some themes.

If you have downloaded a theme provide a link and we can help with installation.
