### Post by kenweill on 2006-02-05
Under GNOME, I used to hide other applications by minimizing the window and switch to other workspace. Switching to another workspace in GNOME hides other running application from the other workspace.

But how can I do that in KDE? Everytime i change a workspace, the minimized windows still appears in another workspace? What should I do so that it won't be displayed in another workspace?

---

### Post by GeneralZod on 2006-02-05
System Settings -> Panel -> Taskbar -> Uncheck "Show Windows from all Desktops".
