### Post by Rogmo on 2005-10-15
Someone said that Cycas is a good program, i didnt find it at repos but take a look in here:


[http://www.cycas.de/](http://www.cycas.de/)


When you have done the details at the download section, choose tar.gz format


When you have unpacked it, write at the console:

```
sudo su

(at the cycas directory)  sh cycas3_install
```

And now your ready to design your house :)
