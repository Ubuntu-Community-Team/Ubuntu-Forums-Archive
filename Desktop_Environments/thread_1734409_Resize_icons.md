### Post by Asraniel on 2011-05-03
Why would you want to do that?

You can't resize them in the panel, they will take the size of your panel.

What you can do if you want multiple lines of icons, you can use the quicklaunch plasmoid.

edit:
The reason why you can't resize the system tray icons, is a technical one. It will be possible in the future, when all applications use the new systemtray standard. All kde applications and i think most gnome ones do, but there are still too many legacy applications left that don't.
