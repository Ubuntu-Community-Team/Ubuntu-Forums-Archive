### Post by lestcape on 2015-05-18
As far as I know Cinnamon is officially supported by some maintainers of Debian. This occurs from Ubuntu version 14.10 or higher. It is true that in the wake of changes in Ubuntu, product of the internal migration to systemd, provide a modern Cinnamon on Ubuntu is complex, and perhaps is why it is supported Cinnamon 2.2 and not a modern Cinnamon like 2.4. However, the fact that is officially supported, is an excellent thing, but will avoid some external attempts to get a more modern Cinnamon on Ubuntu. Thats is because could create some conflicts with the official launch.

Anyway, all to say, if you want to know where know:
[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/cinnamon](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/cinnamon)
