### Post by chichihchen on 2009-10-04
This has happened to me as well.  Only to the 2nd monitor.
I believe it's because of nVidia's powermizer feature in its driver.  When the GPU attemps to change its frenquency, the second monitor starts to blinks for every few seocnds.  (The compiz's not happy with this features.)
If you simply logout and log back in, xorg actually stops the powermizer from doing anything and everything back to normal.  There are few tricks to disable powermizer, one's to modify the files in /etc/modprobe.d/options and the other is to modify xorg.conf to inclucde colbit options and fix the frequency permanent if you hate to logout and log back in once in awhile.
Hope this would help~
