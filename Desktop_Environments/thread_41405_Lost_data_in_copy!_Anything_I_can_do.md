 Before you unplug the USB flash drive, you need to "unmount" it - right-click on device icon on the desktop and select "Unmount Volume". Otherwise, you may lose data. Windows, by the way, behaves similarly -only there it is called "Safely remove hardware".

As for recovering the files - I am not sure how to do this. You might try plugging the flash drive  again in the computer you copied the files from, and now unmount it properly, before unplugging - but I am not certain whether it will help.
