### Post by 73ckn797 on 2011-12-10
During the setup you have options of creating partitions for the data (/) and /home. All available drives should show up during that step. You would simply have to choose where things go. In your case the / would be the SSD and /home on any other partition or drive you desire. You can resize the HDD, creating partitions and designate one of those for /home.
