### Post by ittiam on 2008-11-02
Well I removed Avant Window Navigator from coming up during startup of my KDE session, that solved the greying out panels in KDE 4.1. Actually it was not greying out of panels, but somehow starting AWN added multiple layers of panels over one another.

Also if you add some widgets, and do ctrl-alt-backspace to exit the session, the widgets will not be there when you return. You have to exit by doing a proper log out, then adding new panels and widgets will be saved when coming back again.
