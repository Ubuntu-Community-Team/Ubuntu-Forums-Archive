### Post by jeddycakes on 2010-08-24
Dude, 
That theme is on gnomelook. Its lovely, granted, but on my system I found white blocks appearing and disappearing (annoying) and sometimes the contents of folders didn't show until I refreshed. Possibly a problem with my system, but meh. I'm not a fan of overly Mac-ish themes anyway.

I prefer dark themes myself and as such would recommend 'the days of grays'

[http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/The+Days+of+Grays?content=122249]("http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/The+Days+of+Grays?content=122249")

The recessed progress bars look DELICIOUS (lol)

Attached is an image of my desktop, with a progress bar up. MMM, nice, right?

Also looks fantastic with Faenza:-

```

    *  sudo add-apt-repository ppa:tiheum/equinox

```

PPA here :-
```

    * sudo apt-get update && sudo apt-get install faenza-icon-theme

```

Enjoy dude, one of the biggest things about ubuntu, and linux in general, for me is the total customisability of it.
