### Post by Rog131 on 2015-04-26
- Drag&drop from the KDE Menu - at here the tricky part is to find an empty space.
- Start an application and right click the task manager icon - pick the 'Show The Launcher When Not Running'

### Post by buzzingrobot on 2015-04-26
The right-click on a menu item works for me in 15.04. Right-clicking on an item shown as a Favorite does not.

If you drag an icon into the panel, a gray rectangle appears to indicate where you can place the icon.  Icons, of course, can be moved once they're in the panel.
