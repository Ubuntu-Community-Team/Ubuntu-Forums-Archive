> **ramaswamyps said:**
> thats fast reply thanks.

It will remain.

**kubuntu-desktop** - the old KDE (3.5.8 or 3.5.9, I'm not sure)
**kubuntu-kde4-desktop** - KDE4

At the login screen go to Options > Sessions and select KDE (or Kubuntu)

EDIT: BTW, why do you want to install KDE ? Why don't you just install the video codecs ?
Applications > Add/Remove > search for `codec` and install all the codecs you need.
