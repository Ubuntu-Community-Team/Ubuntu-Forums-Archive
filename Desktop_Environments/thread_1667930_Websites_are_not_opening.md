### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-01-15
Not sure about chrome, but in Firefox try disabling ipv6.

In the urlbar type about:config, ignore the warnings, and in the filter box at the top enter ipv6.  If the line that appears ends in the word "false" double click the line to toggle it to "true".

Now try a bad website again and you may find that it works.
