### Post by FNM on 2005-08-11
When using Opera, whenever I go to a page I've recently visited, it only loads the page from my cache, and it's not updated since the last time I've gone there during that session. I have to manually hit reload. It's especially annoying on sites like digg.com, which is being changed, and updated constantly. I'd really like to switch to Opera since it's much faster than Firefox, but this little quirk is really bugging me.

---

### Post by FNM on 2005-08-11
Nevermind, I figured it out. I went to Tools/Preferences/Advanced/History, and then changed "Check Documents", "Check Images", and "Check Other", to always.
