You sure you didn't changed some settings, colour or accidental delete/removed it? If this is the case, just right click on the remaining one and select "New Panel" and it'll should reappear (minus any widgets), for everything else right-click again and choose "Add to Panel". From the new window add what every you like but the defaults are 'show desktop', 'window list','workspace switcher' and 'trash'.
