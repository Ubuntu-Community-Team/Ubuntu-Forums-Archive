### Post by Krytarik on 2011-05-12
You are meaning the menu bars. If the Global Menu is activated in classic Gnome, too, you can just right-click at it and choose "Remove From Panel".

As for the workspaces, right-click at the workspace switcher at the panel, choose "Preferences" and make sure there are more than 1x1 cols/rows set.
