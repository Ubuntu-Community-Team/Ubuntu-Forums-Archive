### Post by Jucato on 2006-11-10
> **carla said:**
> I know I'm looking right at the solution, and missing it somehow, but how can I rearrange the Kicker applets/application icons? I know there's some mouse-button/key combo for draganddrop that I'm missing, and I'm going to probably feel foolish when I learn the answer...

Middle-click-drag to move most icons. To move the system tray, taskbar, and clock applets, you need to make your happlet handles visible (or temporarily visible, using "Fade"). Right-click on the panel -> Configure Panel -> Appearance -> Advanced Options button -> Applet handles.
