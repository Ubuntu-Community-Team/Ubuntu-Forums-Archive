### Post by magnusbb on 2006-03-10
Indeed this is possible in GNOME, with a little assistance from a nice program called... Brightside. Yes, just install it through Synaptic and find its configuration in System -> Preferences -> Screen Actions.

Now you can drag windows from a workspace to another, and also get the "magic edges" (actions when you move your cursor to the edges of the screen), known from OS X.

Here's a link to more information: 
[http://catmur.co.uk/~ed/main/brightside/](http://catmur.co.uk/~ed/main/brightside/)

Hope this helps.
