### Post by CaveRat on 2006-07-23
Don't do it. I set mine to Auto Login after my first install. I got an error message box: "Failed to Initialize HAL" after restart. My USB ports were no longer seen which in turn my card reader was not there. I thought at first it might have been one of the updates that caused the conflict so I nuked the drive and reinstalled Ubuntu a second time. I only udated a few things this time with no dire results. I then went back in and set Auto Load. That was the cause of the error box. After turning off Auto Load, all is well even with all updates installed including Automatix installs. I'm trying to find a cure for this glitch. You can try it on your machine if you wish. Click on System/Administration/Login Window. You can reverse the process if you get the error message.
