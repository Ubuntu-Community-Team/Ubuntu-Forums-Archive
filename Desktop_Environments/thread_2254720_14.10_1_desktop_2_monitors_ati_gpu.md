### Post by Bucky Ball on 2014-11-29
arandr will allow you to have mirrored desktops or one big desktop between the two monitors, which I am presuming is what you want.

Launch arandr, drag the monitor icons into the position you want them, go to Outputs and change the Orientation of both monitors to 'Normal'. For instance, I have a laptop with a monitor above it. I have placed them like that in arandr and have the orientation of both set to 'Normal'. 

Hope that helps. Good luck. ;)
