### Post by _simon_ on 2006-09-08
assuming you are up to date (if not, then go to compiz.net and have a read)

From terminal type: csm

csm is short for Compiz Settings Manager and replaces gconf.

Then scroll down to Wobbly Windows and simply uncheck it.
