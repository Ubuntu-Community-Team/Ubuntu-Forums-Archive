### Post by 3Miro on 2010-06-23
It is either a bug or more likely you have the Ctr+C and Ctr+P commands enabled globally for the system, that is, when you hit the shortcuts OpenOffice doesn't do anything, however, Gnome (Ubuntu) does the copy and paste for you.

If you wish to disable those, check how to do that for Gnome.
