### Post by Pogeymanz on 2008-04-25
Just open Adept and install Xubuntu-Desktop. Or if you don't want all the XFCE programs, just install XFCE and not xubuntu-desktop.

Next time you are at the login screen, click the session menu and choose xfce. Done. QED. Fin.


You could also uninstall Kubuntu-Desktop if you never plan on using it again.
