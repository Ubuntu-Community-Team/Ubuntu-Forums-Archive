### Post by crroush on 2006-07-17
In the Mandrake distro, when you double click on the title bar of a window, it would minimize on your desktop, so all you can see is the title bar.  I am assuming that is a KDE thing, not a Mandrake thing, and I would like to be able to configure KDE in Kubuntu to behave the same.  Anyone know how this is accomplished?

---

### Post by crroush on 2006-07-17
To answer my own question, right click on the title bar, select **Configure Window Behavior** Change the "Titlebar double-click" to Shade under the actions menu on the left hand side.( see the attached png file for more info)
