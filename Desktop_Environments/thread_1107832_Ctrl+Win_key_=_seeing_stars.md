### Post by stone@bruti on 2009-03-27
I think it's for accessibility. an easier way to see the mouse for people with poor eyesight. quite a few OS have this type of option. In Windows(just for the example), you have a circle apear round the mouse when you hit CTRL (need to activate the option first).

and yes, it does give a bit of eyecandy as well ^^

### Post by mcduck on 2009-03-27
The purpose is to increase visibility of the pointer, useful feature for visually impaired (and also for people giving presentations or lectures).

---

### Post by stderr on 2009-03-27
Indeed. You can change the setting under Applications->System->System Settings.
Once there, click on Desktop, then the All Effects tab. It's in the list of effects under the heading 'Track Mouse'.
