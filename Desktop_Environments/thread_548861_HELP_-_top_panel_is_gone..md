### Post by akiratheoni on 2007-09-11
Right click on a panel that's already open like at the bottom (I assume you haven't touched that one...) then select New Panel and a new panel should appear somewhere. Then just drag it to the top. To restore the icons, right click and select "Add to Panel" and there will be a dialog asking you what you want to add.
