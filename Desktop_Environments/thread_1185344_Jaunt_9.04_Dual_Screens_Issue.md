### Post by cph05a on 2009-06-12
From the System>Preferences>Display you should be able to click and drag the monitors where ever you want. If you put them next to eachother you should be able to drag the mouse from one monitor to the other via the side of the screen. I usually use the nVidia configuration tool so I'm not 100% sure on this, but it's something to try.
