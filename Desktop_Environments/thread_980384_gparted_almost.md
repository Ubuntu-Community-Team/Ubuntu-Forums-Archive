### Post by jtmedin on 2008-11-12
Ran gparted (dated 2008 july) to reduce the size of my vista partition. When i install it took the full 320G of the drive. So i used vista to reduce the size. Vista dropped the size to 180G. But that was still way too much so i booted gparted & asked it to drop the size to 98G. Took a couple of hours. When i rebooted the HD i got an error (about the boot info ...) & was instructed to start the vista install, chose the language & repair the installation. Took about 5 minutes total & then vista booted & runs fine. Thanks gparted & this may be info you can use.
