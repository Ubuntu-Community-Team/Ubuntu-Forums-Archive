Hi there,

There is one simple thing that might be worth checking ... have you restarted your X server?

Log out of your graphical environment and hit CTRL+ALT+Backspace to restart X. The screen will go black for a moment and come right back alive.

The other potential issue is that you may have configured your display manager to list only a limited subset of your user base in login dialogs.
