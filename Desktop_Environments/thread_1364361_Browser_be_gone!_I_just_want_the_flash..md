### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-12-25
Some options:
[list][*] 
[*] use [YouPlayer](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/5709) to create playlists and resize FF to the sidebar width.
[*] use [MediaPlayerConnectivity](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/446) for launching the video with an external player
[*] download the video with [Video Download Helper](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3006) and play with an external player
[*] for youtube videos, you can use [GoogleTube](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/8049) or Minitube.
[*] install standalone flashplayer and open the video link with it.
[/list]
