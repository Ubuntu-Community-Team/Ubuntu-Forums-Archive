### Post by Majorix on 2007-11-17
Googling around, I found a site that told me to go to about:config in firefox and change the value of middlemouse.contentLoadURL to false by double clicking it. I did so, and I don't have this annoying problem anymore :)

And don't ask me why on earth it would go to edit.com or some other random site I have never heard of :)
