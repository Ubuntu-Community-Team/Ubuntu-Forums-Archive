### Post by igknighted on 2006-12-05
The desktop pager on the GNOME/KDE panels uses a different kind of virtual desktop than beryl/compiz.  In newer Beryl releases you can use tha pager to pick sides of the cube, but KDE (or GNOME) will still think there is only one Desktop and you can no longer right click and 'send to desktop'.  It's still in heavy development, so hopefully they will integrate the desktops better into the DE's, but thats as best we got for now.
