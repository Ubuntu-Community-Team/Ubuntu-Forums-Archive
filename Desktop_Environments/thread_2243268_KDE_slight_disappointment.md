### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-09-07
If you are having a problem with brightness control in Kubuntu, switching to another Ubuntu flavor may not resolve it since they all use the same underlying components.  

Try booting live images of another Ubuntu flavor and see if the problem exists there, as well. 

For what it's worth, I've noticed brightness control issues here -- log-in and log-off screens are dim and brightness control keys have no impact -- after recent kernel updates, on the Ubuntu spins as well as elsewhere.
