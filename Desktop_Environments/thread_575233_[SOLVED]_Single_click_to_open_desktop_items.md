I myself have no problem using double click. However, I would like to set this computer up for my parents who seem to have a hard time opening things with two clicks. I was wondering if there was any way to get shortcuts on the desktop to open with just one click. I looked through the options but couldn't find anything.

---

### Post by -grubby on 2007-10-13
open nautilus (the file manager) go to edit, then preferences, than go to the tab that says "behaviour" and click on "single click to activate files"
