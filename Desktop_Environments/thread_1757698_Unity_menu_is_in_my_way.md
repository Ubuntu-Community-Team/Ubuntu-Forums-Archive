### Post by drs305 on 2011-05-13
Install *compizconfig-settings-manager*

Take a look at the Unity Plugin.

Then open ccsm (Type compiz in Dash and it will appear):
Desktop Section; Ubuntu Unity Plugin > Hide Launcher: Dodge Windows will force the Launcher to get out of the way of open Windows. I don't think it will do what you are looking for, but perhaps one of the other options will.
