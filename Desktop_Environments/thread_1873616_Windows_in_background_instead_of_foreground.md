### Post by undrline on 2011-12-13
I was having this issue out of nowhere, too.  Researching around, it seemed to be connected to the Visual Effects setting under Appearances.  Mine had reset from Custom to None (though I was still getting Visual Effects).  I changed it to Extra, and the problem went away.  HTH.  Best of luck.
