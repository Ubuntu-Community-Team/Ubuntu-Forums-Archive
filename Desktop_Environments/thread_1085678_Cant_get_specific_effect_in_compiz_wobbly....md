### Post by NoaHall on 2009-03-04
Go to System > Preferences > Advanced Desktop Effect Settings
Then under effects, tick the "Animations","Fading Windows" and "Wobbly Windows" which should give you the effects you want. If that doesn't work, then try going into System > Preferences > Appearance , then chose the "Visual Effects" tab, and chose a better one if "None" is selected.

Hope I helped.
