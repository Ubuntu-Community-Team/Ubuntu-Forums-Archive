### Post by enzotib on 2023-07-23
I mostly solved this way:
[LIST]
[*] installed gnome-shell-extension-manager
[*] installed the extension named *Gesture Improvements*
[*] configured the extension by disabling the extension default 4 fingers substitutions for desktop gestures
[*] configured the extension by adding Brave browser (that I use) and adding Forward/Backward keybinding and enabling application specific gestures
[/LIST]
Now Forward and Backward work in Brave Browser with 4 finger horizontal scroll (not 2 finger, anyway, but it works).
I gained as a bonus 4 finger pinch to show desktop
