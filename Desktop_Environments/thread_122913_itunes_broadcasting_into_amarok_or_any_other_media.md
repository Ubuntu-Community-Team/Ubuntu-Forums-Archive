### Post by spiritraveller on 2006-01-28
There is a way to broadcast TO iTunes your mp3 collection ([mt-daapd](http://www.mt-daapd.org) for anyone who's interested)... but I have also looked for a way to do the reverse and have not found anything.

Thus, I think you will have to do a couple things to get what you want...

1) Decrypt the songs you purchased from iTunes with [jHymn](http://www.hymn-project.org).*
2) Share them with your Linux box using another protocol, such as Samba or HTTP... or simply copy them over and play them directly on the other machine.

You will also have to make sure that amaroK can play AAC files.  There is probably a plugin for it somewhere.

Hope that helps.

* Note that this might be illegal where you live.  But as long as you don't share the files with anyone else, you should be fine.  I do believe that Hymn leaves any identifying information intact... so sharing the files you've bought and decrypted is a very very bad idea.
