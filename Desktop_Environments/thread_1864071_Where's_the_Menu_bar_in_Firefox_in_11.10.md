### Post by 3Miro on 2011-10-18
Put the mouse over the top panel.  Since Ubuntu 11.04, the File/Edit and so on menu is at the top.

Right now you only see "Firefox" at the top-left. If you hover the mouse over, it will change to the File, Bookmarks, Help and so on.
