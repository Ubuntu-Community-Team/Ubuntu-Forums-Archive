### Post by 3Miro on 2010-12-06
Gnome-panel doesn't have that option, so the short answer is "Sorry, but No".

You can check xinerama and/or dual-monitor setup to perhaps achieve something similar (i.e. the panel is shown on one monitor and not the other, xinerama has other nice options too).
