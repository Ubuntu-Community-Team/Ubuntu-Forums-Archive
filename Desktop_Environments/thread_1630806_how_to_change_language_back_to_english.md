### Post by Frogs Hair on 2010-11-25
Hi and Welcome,

In language support > select install/remove language ,scroll and remove the check in the Slovak box and place a check in the English box and select apply changes. you may need to select the apply system wide setting.This is the only way I know of to change languages.
