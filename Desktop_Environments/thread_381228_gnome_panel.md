### Post by wj32 on 2007-03-10
1. You can't bring the software update icon back, it appears when you have updates. If you don't, it's not there.
2. For the volume control, right click, **Add to Panel**, in **System & Hardware** there is **Volume Control**.

Hope that helps.

### Post by gradedcheese on 2007-03-10
right-click on the panel and choose '+Add to panel...', add a Notification Area from the list.  The updates icon shows up in Notification Area, along with all the others (power manager, volume control, etc)
