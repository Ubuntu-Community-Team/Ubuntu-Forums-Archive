### Post by drs305 on 2010-01-17
PointWise,

Welcome to Ubuntu.

I'm not sure what you are looking for, but if you would like to make a custom drop-down menu, you can right click on the panel, Add to Panel, and then add "Drawer". 

It will allow you to add entries by right clicking on the drawer. You can doubleclick "Application Launcher" to add some of the popular apps or use the "Custom Application Launcher" to add others. It could be a bit tedious but it's an alternative to the default menu. It's not something you would use to recreate the entire menu, but if you would like to have a drop-down menu of your favorite apps it's one option rather than having multiple shortcut icons on the taskbar.
