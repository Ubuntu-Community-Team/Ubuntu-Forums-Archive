### Post by llamakc on 2006-08-30
I don't have a reason why the current version is packaged with Dapper but I know that Dapper won't receive a version bump. If you want newer hplip you can compile it or help the edgy eft folks test the next release, where the version they are testing is 0.9.11-2ubuntu4.

Also, the version number bumps by the hplip maintainer at sourceforge has this:

```

**02 August 2006:** HPLIP 1.6.7 released providing support for two  new products, fixes and enhancements.
 **27 June 2006:** HPLIP 1.6.6a released providing various fixes and enhancements.
 **16 June 2006:** HPLIP 1.6.6 released providing support for 10 new products, fixes and enhancements.
 **09 May 2006:** HPLIP 0.9.11 released providing support for 5 new products, fixes and enhancements.

```

. . .which means Edgy is only 3-4 months behind the current version being provided by the source site, and inline with when Edgy was started, IMHO.
