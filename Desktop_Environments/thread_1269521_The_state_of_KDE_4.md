### Post by roggenschrotbrot on 2009-12-01
running kde 4.3.2 ontop of ubuntu 9.10 and having used other desktop environments before, i would say kde is safe for work. nevertheless it is far from beeing as stable as gnome. there are no big problems, but plasma might crash sometime (though not very often anymore), some plasmoids might be displayed wrong and konqueror tends to crash quite often. also some badly needed functionality is still missing: you still cand bind your mousebuttons without using xbindkeys and some plasmoids/applets (especial kmixer comes to mind) aren't asw useable as their gnome-counterparts. 

none of these problems are real show-stoppers, if you can life with these you might be quite happy with kde. i joust won't recommend it to absolute beginners.
