### Post by MountainX on 2008-03-22
I solved this by using compiz. Just turn on extra visual effects in Gutsy or Hardy and full screen terminal server clieint with desktop switching works perfect.

Use keys:
Alt-Enter to toggle full screen on and off
Alt-arrow to switch desktops
