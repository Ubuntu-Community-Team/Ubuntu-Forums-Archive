### Post by der_joachim on 2006-11-21
From the top of my head (am at my fathers W2k box now, so cannot doublecheck): open kcontrol from a konsole or Alt-F2. Choose Appearance->Fonts->desktop. Choose the font you like and click "Apply". Done.
