### Post by Burky on 2010-01-19
I really hate the shadows in the Gnome-Panels, no big deal, I know I can remove them from the Compiz Settings under the "Window Decoration Plugin". It was set to "any", but how do I say anything *but* Gnome-Panels?

---

### Post by inobe on 2010-01-19
go into compizconfig, select window decorator option, change the shadow to windows to

```
 (any) & !(class=Gnome-panel) 
```
