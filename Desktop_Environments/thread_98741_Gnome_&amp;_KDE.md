### Post by HokeyFry on 2005-12-04
Is there a way to have BOTH Gnome and KDE installed on one system? (As in, choosing which desktop environment to load at the login screen?)

---

### Post by Iandefor on 2005-12-04
Absolutely. Open up Synaptic and choose to install whichever you don't have yet (For KDE, go with Kubuntu-desktop package; for GNOME, Ubuntu-desktop package) When you login next, there should be a button called "sessions" at the login screen. Select it and choose whichever you want to run.
