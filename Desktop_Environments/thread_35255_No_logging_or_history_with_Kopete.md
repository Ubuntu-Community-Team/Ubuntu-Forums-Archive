### Post by bluej100 on 2007-02-15
In "Configure Plugins," uncheck the history plugin and click apply. Then recheck it and click OK. Worked for me.

[http://www.mail-archive.com/ubuntu-bugs@lists.ubuntu.com/msg161634.html](http://www.mail-archive.com/ubuntu-bugs@lists.ubuntu.com/msg161634.html)

(Edit: then just right-click on a contact to view history. There's also history browsing under "Tools" in the actual chat windows.)
