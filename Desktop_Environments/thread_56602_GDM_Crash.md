### Post by jazzer on 2005-10-04
You should try disabling RenderAccel in your xorg.conf configuration file.  RenderAccel is known to be buggy with the nvidia driver (nvidia advises against enabling it).  

On a side note I've had RenderAccel enabled for over a year with my 5200 and never had a single problem, but it's worth a shot.
