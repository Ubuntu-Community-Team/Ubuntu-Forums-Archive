### Post by Netsui on 2006-08-04
Recently, I was having a problem where the mouse pointer, or 3d games would stutter with 4 very quick, succesive pauses every ~30 seconds. I don't know what the problem was, but I got it to stop by going to, System > Administration > Services and disabling all non-essential services such as, cupsys [if you don't have a printer] and Computer Activity Logger.

Soon after disabling those services, I installed the latest nVidia nForce motherboard drivers. After a restart, the stuttering ceased to exist. 

If you are experiencing a similar stutter, this solution [disabling services, installing proper drivers, or both] may fix the problem.

Here are my basic system Specifications:

Chaintec 7NJL6 Nforce 2 motherboard
Socket A Sempron 2500+
AC 97 Integrated audio codec
512 MB of RAM
GeForce 6800XT with 128MB video RAM / AGP 8X
Seagate Barracuda 60 Gigabyte HDD

Ubuntu Dapper Drake 6.06 LTS


Hopefully this will help someone that has a problem like this.


Cheers!
