### Post by x1a4 on 2008-03-20
Hi,

[xfce-look.org]("http://xfce-look.org/") has wallpapers, icons, compiz themes, cursors, and of course GTK themes.

[beryl-themes.org]("http://www.beryl-themes.org/") has themes for beryl, emerald, compiz, metacity, wallpapers, icons, etc

[compiz-themes.org]("http://www.compiz-themes.org/) has themes for compiz, emerald, metacity, wallpapers, icons, etc

[box-look.org]("http://www.box-look.org/) has themes for all sorts of WMs/DEs (non-XFCE/KDE/GNOME) as well as wallpapers, icons, fonts, etc

[digitalBlasphemy.com]("http://www.digitalblasphemy.com/") has lots of excellent wallpapers.
