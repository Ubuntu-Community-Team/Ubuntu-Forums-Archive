### Post by RikoW on 2005-09-27
Maybe I misunderstood your question, but you can easily add additional email accounts in evolution by selection Tools -> Settings -> Mail Accounts, use the add button and follow the wizard.

It might be, that all accounts that are accessed via the POP protocol may end up in the same  inbox folder. I don't use any pop accounts, so can't check.
However, if that is the case, you could sort them in different inbox folders by creating filters which work on the mail recipient, where you would look for the email adress which is associated with the POP account.  (Tools -> Filters)

Hope that helps, ;)
