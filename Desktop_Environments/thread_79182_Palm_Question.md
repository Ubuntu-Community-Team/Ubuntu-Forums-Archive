### Post by manicka on 2006-01-16
Andr&#233;, as per your email request...

The Tungsten E2 works fine for me with Ubuntu and Evolution, except I can't do a full backup (addressbook and contacts are OK) with any sync program available in Linux. It fails at the web backup process. This isn't ideal but I can live with it. 

The problem stems from the version of pilot-link that Evolution uses and will be solved when evolution uses a newer version of pilot-link. At this stage I just have g-pilot set to sync my adressbook and contacts with full backups disabled and it works really well.

The key to getting it to sync successfully is to make sure that the time zone in Evolution is set to the same as your Ubuntu system preferences.

Edit --> Preferences --> Calendar and Tasks --> Time Zone

HTH
