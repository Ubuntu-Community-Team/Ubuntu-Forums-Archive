### Post by mcduck on 2008-04-17
> **peter_t said:**
> I am new to ubuntu so I apologize in advance for my ignorance but I cannot seem to get this deskbar search applet back after it crashed once.  I have uninstalled and reinstalled the "deskbar-applet" packagee using synaptic but have had no luck.  Thanks.

Have you tried just adding it back to your panel? Right-click on some empty spot in your panel, select "Add to panel", find the Deskbar applet and drag&drop it into your panel.

For the OP: yes, that's how Deskbar is these days. It used to work nice even without mouse, but the developer decided to change the UI and now you not only need a mouse, but also usually need more mouse clicks than if you just use Alt-F2 or the Applications-menu.
