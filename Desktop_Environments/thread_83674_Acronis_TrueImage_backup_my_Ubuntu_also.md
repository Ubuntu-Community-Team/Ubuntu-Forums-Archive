### Post by davmac on 2005-10-29
I think it depends on which version of TrueImage 8.0 you've got. There is a TrueImage 8.0 Enterprise for Windows and TrueImage 8.0 Enterprise for Linux. My guess is that, to cover both XP and Ubuntu, you'd need one of the "super-dooper" versions. You can find out more at [http://www.acronis.com/products/](http://www.acronis.com/products/)

There's a thread covering free alternatives to TrueImage and Ghost at [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=80389](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=80389) that might be worth a read.
