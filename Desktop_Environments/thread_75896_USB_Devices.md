### Post by maddin on 2005-10-14
[QUOTE=Stantheman]How can I use my usb devices, ie scanner, wireless access point[/QUOTE]
It depends on the device that you want to use. If you want tu use a scanner you need sane for the backend and perheps some drivers for your scanner. Look up if your scanner is supported by sane: [http://www.sane-project.org/sane-supported-devices.html](http://www.sane-project.org/sane-supported-devices.html)

I recommend xsane as a frontend to sane.

I don't have any experience in use of wireless access points. Bu I think you have to install the drivers.

Other devices like pen sticks or external harddrives do not need any drivers. You have to plug and play ;-)
