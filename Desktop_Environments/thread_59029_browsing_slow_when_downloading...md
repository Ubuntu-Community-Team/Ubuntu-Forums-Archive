### Post by chrisod on 2005-08-22
Sounds like you are saturating your connection. What kind of connection do you have? Your answer probably lies in your router - not in the OS. If it is a bandwidth conflict, you'll need a router that lets your priority tag specific packets or ports. Then you can set it up to give priority to anything on port 80. However, that will slow down the filesharing app.
