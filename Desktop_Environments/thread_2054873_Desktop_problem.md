### Post by farrinux on 2012-09-08
As for your suspend problem with the monitor, it very likely could be a video card driver issue. The easiest way to fix it is to go to "System Settings" and then "Power" and under "Suspend" select "do not suspend". For the right click menu problem "IF" you have Ubuntu Tweak installed and "IF" under the desktop tweak you chose to turn off desktop icons, that also turns off the right click menu.  Just re-enable desktop icons.

Hope this helps
