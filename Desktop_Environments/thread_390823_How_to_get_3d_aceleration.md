### Post by hardyn on 2007-03-22
With an oldish card like the 9250, you are probably best looking at the MESA DRI project.

there are countless howtos on this forum on the installing of the 'radeon' driver.
it requires manually editing the xorg.conf file, at lease that is how i have allways done it.  there may be automatic scipts to do this as well.

I believe that ATI has dropped support for anything below the 9800s IIRC, you could use the FGLRX driver, but you would have make sure that your board is supported, by using an older driver.

good luck.
