### Post by kostkon on 2013-09-12
Actually you could just do an [OEM installation]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Ubuntu_OEM_Installer_Overview") and let your friend set up his/her account, if that's what you are looking for.

Ubuntu will ask him/her to fill in the account details when he/she will boot ubuntu for the first time.

You can even install extra software and any drivers, if needed, before giving the PC to your friend etc etc.

Hope that helps.
