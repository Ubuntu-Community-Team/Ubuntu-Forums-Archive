### Post by mcduck on 2015-05-07
I haven't used Unity Tweak in ages (I use Gnome Tweak Tool since I mostly run Gnome Shell), but ~/.themes and ~/.icons should work just fine. However you can also try placing the themes and icons in ~/.local/share/themes & ~/.local/share/icons instead. (And of course make sure the themes support correct version of GTK3)
