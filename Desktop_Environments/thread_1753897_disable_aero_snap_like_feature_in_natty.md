### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-05-09
I don't know Unity much, but I think you need to install [compizconfig-settings-manager]("apt:compizconfig-settings-manager"). I believe there's a setting in there.

### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-05-09
Yes, it's the Grid plugin. Once you get into CompizConfig, just find it in the Window Management plugins and uncheck the box next to the name.
