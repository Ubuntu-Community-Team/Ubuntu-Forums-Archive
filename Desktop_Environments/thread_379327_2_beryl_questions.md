### Post by Waappu on 2007-03-08
Hi

1. Open Beryl Setting Manager and change General options "Level of Focus Stealing Prevention" to Normal or Low
2. Open Beryl Setting Manager goto Desktop tab and in Rotate Cube plugin options change Zoom value
