### Post by mcduck on 2012-01-15
Actually that's where it's designed to be.

If I rember right the spot right under the top panel is reserved for notifications about user actions (like the on-screen display when changing volume, for example), and to avoid those notifications from moving around if there are many notifications at the same time, all other notifications appear below that region.

I rember seeing some instruction how to change that, but it was a long time ago so I can't really say if anybody actually bothered keeping instructions up-to-date in the end...
