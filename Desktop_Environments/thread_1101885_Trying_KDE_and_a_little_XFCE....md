### Post by snova on 2009-03-20
> **DaveG825 said:**
> Looking at KDE, it seems to be nice looking, but it feels a little jerky and slower than GNOME and XFCE.

KDE tends to be a bit heavier...

> Also, it doesn't seem to like Firefox or Pidgen with respect to how they look.

Open System Settings -> Appearance -> GTK Styles and Fonts

By default, GTK apps will use the Qt engine under KDE. I generally find it doesn't look too great, so try another one. It'll look a bit out of place though.

There is also a theme engine called QtCurve (for both KDE and GTK) which is supposed to help when you set both to use it.
