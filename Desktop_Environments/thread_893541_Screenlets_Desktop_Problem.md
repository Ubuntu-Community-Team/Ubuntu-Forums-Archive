### Post by PlankEyeWilly on 2008-08-20
I just saw [another thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=893737&highlight=Screenlets") where the user was having the same issue.  You may want to subscribe to that thread to see if you can come across an answer. Below are some some posts that may help:

> Right click on your widget, go to "Window", and make sure that "Sticky" and "Keep Above" are unchecked. That will keep the widgets always visible and visible on all workspaces. Do you have compiz fusion running with the widget layer plugin enabled? If you do, enable "Widget" to use it with the widget layer plugin and disable it to keep it visible at all times.

> Are you getting to your desktop by clicking on the "Show Desktop" icon? This, for some reason, also makes screenlets minimize. Try clicking it again to see if they don't come back.
