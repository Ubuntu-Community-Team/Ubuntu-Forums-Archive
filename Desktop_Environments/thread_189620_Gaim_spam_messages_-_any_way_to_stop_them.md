### Post by mscman on 2006-06-05
There is a setting in Gaim to allow only those people that you've OK'd or those that you have on your buddy list to see you.  In Gaim 2.0 Beta 3 this setting is found under Tools> Privacy, although I'm not exactly sure where it is in the older version of Gaim.  Check in your preferences menu for a privacy tab, or possibly in one of the menus.  I haven't had a huge problem with random people IMing me, though I only use the AIM portion of the program and haven't really messed with connecting to MSN Messenger.  

Hope I could help at least a little bit! :wink:
