### Post by Inkpot on 2011-11-22
I found the answer!

You need to press: super (windows key)+ alt + right-click over the launcher you want to remove and the option to remove it will appear. Worked like a charm for me!

### Post by dibuntux on 2013-01-07
Yes, thanks for editing...
I found the solution anyway:

Remove icons in Gnome Panel or show menu Add to Panel
Equivalent of Alt+SuperKey (Win Key) on panel with IBM AT keyboard= Alt+Alt Gr + right mouse button
