### Post by 5-HT on 2006-01-13
Hi, I followed the advise in [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=79283") which points to [this wiki page]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/FirefoxNewVersion"), and the install went smoothly.

I think it (someone please correct me or add their opinions if warranted) would be best to follow that wiki and not to uninstall 1.0.7 as there may be dependency/unwanted repercussion issues if it were uninstalled.

Basically this guide walks you through installing 1.5 as a separate program, and then changing some symbolic links to point to 1.5 instead of/and 1.0.7.

If needed, you can always uninstall 1.5 and get back to the default 1.0.7 installation.
