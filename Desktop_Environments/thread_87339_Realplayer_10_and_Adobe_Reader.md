### Post by Lambert on 2005-11-07
You're trying to install packages made for a .rpm based distro. Ubuntu is based on debian and their packages end in a .deb.

First and foremost click on the link at top 'official wiki' you'll find a some helpful information there about using ubuntu/linux

Go here for installing packages the easy way through synaptic

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SynapticHowto?highlight=%28synaptic%29](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SynapticHowto?highlight=%28synaptic%29)

You can also click on System in the top panel > Administration>add programs
you will find both of these packages there.
