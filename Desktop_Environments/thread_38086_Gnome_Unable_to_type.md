### Post by Cybolic on 2005-06-07
Going into keyboard settings in Gnome and pressing the middle button lowest in the window (I don't know it's name as I'm running Gnome in Danish) a new window will pop up. In this new window, uncheck the checkbox at the top of the window. This worked for me :D.
