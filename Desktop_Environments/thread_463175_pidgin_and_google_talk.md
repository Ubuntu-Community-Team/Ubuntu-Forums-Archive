### Post by DoktorSeven on 2007-06-03
Choose XMPP (or Jabber) as the protocol.
Screen name: your account username
Server: gmail.com (or whatever your google account address is)
Password: account password
Local alias: whatever (this is what shows as your name in conversations, etc)

Advanced: Connect port: 5222
Connect server: talk.google.com

Everything else default.
