### Post by snova on 2009-02-11
The skin settings in Yakuake do not affect the terminal itself, I believe.

Open up the menu, and near the bottom, click "Manage Profiles". Select "Shell", click "Edit Profile", and you should be able to achieve what you want under the Appearance tab.

Changing settings here, by the way, will affect Konsole everywhere.
