 To get more current versions of your packages (like Gaim) check out [http://www.ubuntuguide.org](http://www.ubuntuguide.org) , specifically the "adding more repositories" section. If you follow it, you'll use the Universe and Multiverse section on Ubuntu's repository, and you'll add the Backports repository to your apt configuration. This will give you a healthy amount of software, and the later will let you get new versions of software that have been released after Hoary was released, and even a few extra applications that are still not available for Ubuntu.
