### Post by rmcellig on 2012-03-15
I solved my problem. I restarted my machine and selected the recovery mode option from the GRUB menu. This allowed me to see the recovery options. When I restarted my machine again, Ubuntu booted into Gnome so I was at least able to logout and re-login in selecting the regular Ubuntu option.
