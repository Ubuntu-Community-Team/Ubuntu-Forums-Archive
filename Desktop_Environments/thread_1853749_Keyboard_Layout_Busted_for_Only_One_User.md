### Post by mankyd on 2011-10-02
I have a bluetooth apple keyboard. On the login screen, and for my admin user, it works perfectly. For another users account, however, its completely busted, with only a small number of keys on the right hand side working, and most of them output the incorrect character. This all happened after I upgraded from 10.04 to 10.10. I've tried setting the keyboard under "Keyboard Preferences" for them, but it has not helped. How do I reset the keyboard settings for this user?

---

### Post by mankyd on 2011-10-02
Solved! Numlock was on for that user. Fix (for the apple wireless keyboard) is to press fn+f6 twice.
