### Post by ubuntuuser on 2005-07-25
Hi David.

No need to apologize for posting, that's what forums are for. :) First of all, you should choose a better title for your future posts, where you describe your problem(s) in a few words. So others know whether they may be able to help you or not.

For your WLAN card, you should search the forum for threads about a program called ndiswrapper. There's a lot of HOWTOs explaining how to ;) use it (it's really easy to configure). NDISwrapper uses your Windows drivers and makes them usable under Linux. Ubuntu comes with ndiswrapper 1.1, but it doesn't work with some cards (like mine). So if you experience any problems with 1.1, like your adapter doesn't find the AP, try ndiswrapper 1.2 (again, search the forums).

Good luck.
