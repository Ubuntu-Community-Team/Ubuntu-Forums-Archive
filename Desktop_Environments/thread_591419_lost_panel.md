Hello, I'm assuming you're using regular ubuntu (Gnome).

If you are missing the lower panel entirely: Right click on the upper panel. Chose 'New Panel'. Then proceed to the next step.

If you've done the above or still have the lower panel, right click on the lower panel. Choose 'Add to Panel'. I think the one you want is 'Window List'. Add that and it's back. :)
