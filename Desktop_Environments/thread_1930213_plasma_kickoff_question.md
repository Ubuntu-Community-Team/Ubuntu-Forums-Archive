### Post by Repus on 2012-02-28
Yay Google and lots of free time... OK so you can resize the KDE kickstart menu by click-dragging the top right corner. 

FYI You _can't_ do it by dragging the top or the sides however. Just a small section in the top right corner which has no indicator that it will perform this function.
