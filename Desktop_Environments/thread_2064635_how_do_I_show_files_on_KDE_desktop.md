### Post by Erik1984 on 2012-09-30
First you have to right click on the desktop and choose "unlock widgets" then right click again and pick "configure desktop" then you can pick "folder view" from the top dropdown menu. With widgets locked that dropdown is grayed out.

### Post by kio_http on 2012-09-30
Alternatively (as the default of KDE is), you add a widget on the desktop called folder view, it basically shows contents of a folder in a transparent widget.
