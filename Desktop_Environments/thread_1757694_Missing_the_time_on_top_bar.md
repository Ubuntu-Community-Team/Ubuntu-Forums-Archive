### Post by PhantmKllr on 2011-05-13
Alright you're going to have to go into the system settings (under the session menu, which is the little "power button" icon in the top right hand corner of your screen). Click "Time & Date" under the "Personal" section, and select the "Clock" tab. Then select "Show a clock in the menubar".
