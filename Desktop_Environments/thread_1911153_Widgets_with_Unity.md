### Post by bogan on 2012-01-18
Hi!,** kmand**,

Assuming that by "Widget" you mean a Launcher, and not the Window that System Monitor opens in, you will find it in the Dash Display, and you can drag it to desktop or to the Unity Launcher.

Check that Right-Click>'Keep in launcher' is  Ticked. That's it.

Chao!, **bogan**.
