### Post by scragar on 2008-10-24
The tide is used by gedit(the text editor) to idicate a backup of the file. You can delete them if you wanted.

To turn it off open Gedit, go to edit>preferences, and choose the "Editor" tab, inside under "File Saving" turn off "Create a _b_ackup copy of files before saving"
