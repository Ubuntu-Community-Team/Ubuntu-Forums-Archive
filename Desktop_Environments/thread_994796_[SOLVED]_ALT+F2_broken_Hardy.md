### Post by dansan on 2008-11-27
I knew these forums held the answer. 

I found the answer at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=989954&highlight=gnome+terminal+shortcuts]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=989954&highlight=gnome+terminal+shortcuts"), where I learned about System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts. So, I checked there and found out the shortcut for opening Terminal was disabled. Duh!
