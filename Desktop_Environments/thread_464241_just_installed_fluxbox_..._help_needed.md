### Post by 4KvRMU7Nnv on 2007-06-04
1. you can't remove xterm...don't try to.  It will be bad.  Xterm is like THE terminal of linux.  Simply remove it from your fluxbox menu if you don't like seeing it.  I don't remember but i think the fluxbox menu is inside the ~/.fluxbox folder or the ~/.config folder or something like that.  Secondly,  that terminal is "aterm"  just sudo apt-get install aterm and you'll have it.  Of course, the border around it and the tab thing on the bottom are not terminal specific, but to be changed within fluxbox.
