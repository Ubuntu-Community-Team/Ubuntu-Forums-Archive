### Post by rabid emu on 2006-12-07
Don't do it.

Seriously though, it's a feature of Beryl to adjust the brightness of windows using shift+mousewheel.  To decrease brightness, you use shift+mousewheel down.  To increase brightness back up, use shift+mousewheel up.  If you want to disable this feature, go to beryl-settings-manager and uncheck 'brightness and saturation' or change the keys associated with it.

### Post by robgill on 2006-12-07

Try that.
