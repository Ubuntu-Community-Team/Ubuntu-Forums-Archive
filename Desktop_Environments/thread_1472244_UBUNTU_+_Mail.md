### Post by mrboojive on 2010-05-04
You can only check your Yahoo Mail with a mail program if you have paid access to Yahoo Mail Plus: [http://help.yahoo.com/l/us/yahoo/mail/yahoomail/mailplus/pop/pop-35.html;_ylt=AgthvxeB5yRdqDYTVAc9wOG7myN4](http://help.yahoo.com/l/us/yahoo/mail/yahoomail/mailplus/pop/pop-35.html;_ylt=AgthvxeB5yRdqDYTVAc9wOG7myN4)

Otherwise, you need to just use a web browser to access your mail.
