### Post by yourpalal on 2008-01-05
If you run kubuntu or xubunt you are given the option in synaptic to download ubuntu-desktop package, through this you could convert an install of either of these two into ubuntu, thus bypassing your compiz problem... perhaps first it would be a good idea to install xserver-xgl package to enable xgl for you, which might let compiz run! yAY! once you have swithced your xubuntu or kubuntu to ubuntu you can remove the other through synaptic with *ubuntu-desktop package,

P.S. to login to ubuntu instead of the other two at the login screen (GDM) under sessions choose gnome ,
(a gnome session will be a ubuntu session)

hope this helps
