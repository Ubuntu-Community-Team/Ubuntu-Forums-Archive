Did you auto-hide it or delete it?  If it is auto-hidden, just roll your mouse pointer down to the bottom af the screen and the bar should reappear.  Then, right click on it, click on PROPERTIES and uncheck the AUTO-HIDE box.  If you deleted it, go to the top panel, right click on that and click NEW PANEL.
