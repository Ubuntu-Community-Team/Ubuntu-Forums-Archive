### Post by mssever on 2007-04-05
Are you referring to regulations for exporting stuff from the United States? I'm not a lawyer, but since Ubuntu is produced by a UK-based company (Canonical), I would think that export restrictions shouldn't apply. Americans are importing Ubuntu when we use it.

At any rate, the different programs available from Synaptic are probably covered separately--particularly the stuff in the universe, multiverse, and third-party repositories. By the way, you might consider changing your /etc/apt/sources.list to use non-US mirrors.
