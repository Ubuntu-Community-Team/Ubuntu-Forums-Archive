### Post by valadil on 2005-03-14
xine does a pretty good job with subtitles, but I prefer totem with a xine backend.  Just apt-get install totem-xine.  It will uninstall some metapackages that came with ubuntu, but nothing will break.

I think you have to play with some text files to get subs colored or sized right, but thats the sort of thing you do once to get it the way you like it, then you leave it alone.  Changing which subs to display while watching a movie is simple enough.
