### Post by mdsmith on 2006-03-25
You can use the panel that is there or you can create a new one by right clicking on the panel there, then select new panel. You can right click on the new panel and select properties and move it, change  the size, cause it to auto hide etc. To add an app to a panel right click on the launcher select add to panel. If you want it somewhere other than where it is placed then right click on the launcher icon and select move then move your mouse to the place you desire and left click to drop it. Play around with it there is a lot there.
