### Post by gcvisel on 2011-05-05
That's not a bug.  That's a Feature!  Check out
[http://www.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/features](http://www.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/features) for a description of the new Unity desktop.  Try clicking on the Ubuntu icon in the top left corner to get the "Dashboard" and click around in there to find some of the old menus, or search for the program you want.
 
It's not for everybody.  I explored it for a couple of days, but then set up my login to boot the old Gnome version, with menus like they used to be.
