### Post by frogbrains on 2009-06-11
You just don't have enough workspaces.  By default, there are only 2.  See those two squares in the bottom right hand corner?  Right-click 'em, click preferences, and change the number of workspaces there.  If you want a cube, make four of 'em.  Done!
