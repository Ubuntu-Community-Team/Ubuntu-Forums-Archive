### Post by swisscow on 2008-06-27
I used it, really nice. But still went back to Ubuntu. A couple of things didn't work so well, e.g. BBC news videos and I couldn't seem to fix them. After I switched back to Ubuntu, had them sorted in no time at all.

The artwork is nicer and the menu system is cool, though I prefer the more bog standard menu personally.

Mint is designed more for the whole out of the box experience. That is what you get. I just felt I'd rather be on more familiar ground when I needed to sort out problems.

Enjoy!
