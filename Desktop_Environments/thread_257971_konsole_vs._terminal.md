### Post by etank on 2006-09-15
If by terminal you mean the one that comes with Gnome then the difference is in who wrote them. Terminal is a short name for gnome-terminal and was written by the Gnome desktop folks. Konsole is another terminal that was writen by the people at KDE. In my opinion konsole has nicer features in it than does gnome-terminal.
