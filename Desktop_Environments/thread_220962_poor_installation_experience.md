### Post by doobit on 2006-07-22
The installer on the live CD does have a few problems, but they are well noted here on the forum, as are the workarounds. The easy way is to boot the live CD and use Qparted or CFdisk to make your partitions and format them first, then skip the reformat during the regular installation process.
Also, there is an alternative install CD that uses a text based installer for Dapper, so you could use that.
