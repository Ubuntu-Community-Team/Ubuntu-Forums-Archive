### Post by guiverc on 2022-06-06
Lubuntu is the only [*flavor*]("https://ubuntu.com/download/flavours") of Ubuntu that uses `openbox`.

Lubuntu (all releases) offer the choice to login using an
- `openbox` session,
- a Lubuntu session (LXQt with openbox* as WM with Lubuntu configuration) 
- a LXQt session (LXQt still with openbox* as WM without Lubuntu configuration)

Openbox however is replaceable; it's not perfect, but has been & is the Lubuntu choice WM.

Looking at the openbox [changelog]("https://changelogs.ubuntu.com/changelogs/pool/universe/o/openbox/openbox_3.6.1-10/changelog"), it contains a lot of entries by Mateusz/mati75 from LinuxMint Poland, so maybe *outside* of the Ubuntu *flavor* world may get what you want.
