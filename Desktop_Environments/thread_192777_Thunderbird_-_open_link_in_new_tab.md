### Post by arizonagroovejet on 2006-06-09
There isn't an option for this in Thunderbird (that I have every found anyway.)

When I click on urls in Thunderbird then it opens in a new tab in the current Firefox window. I set that behaviour in Firefox's preferences. There's a section in there called 
"Open links from other applications in:". One of the options is "a new tab in the most recent window". Look for something equivalent in Opera.
