### Post by ardchoille on 2006-02-07
[QUOTE=ESPOiG]how exactly do i burn an image onto disc with it?

graveman that is?[/QUOTE]
When you launch graveman, you'll see a list of tasks on the left. Choose "Duplicate CD" and in the "Duplicate from" drop down box on the right, you can click on "ISO file..." and browse to the ISO.

when you have all the settings configured to your liking, click on "Write tracks..." and graveman will go to work :)
