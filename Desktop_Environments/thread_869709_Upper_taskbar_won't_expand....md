I have a Gateway MT6723 laptop with a 15" widescreen display.  My video drivers are installed and my optimal resolution is set, (1280x800).  However, my upper taskbar won't expand to the full length of my screen; 

[http://i36.tinypic.com/116p2bn.jpg](http://i36.tinypic.com/116p2bn.jpg)

Not a big deal but any help would be nice. Thanks!

EDIT/SOLVED: i noticed my Monitor Resolution Settings displayed two different screens, my laptop's 15" one and another unknown display. i simply set the unknown one's resolution to Off and it fixed my problem.
