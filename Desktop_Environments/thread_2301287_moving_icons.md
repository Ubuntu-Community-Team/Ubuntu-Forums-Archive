I'm running Xubuntu 14.04 on a desktop at work and something happened and the icons that are normally in the upper right hand corner are now on the left end  and I can't move them by right clicking and selecting the Move function. How do I get the calendar, sound, network icons back on the right end of the panel in Xfce?

---

### Post by Dennis N on 2015-11-01
Right Click on Panel, then select: Panel > Panel Preferences

The "Items" tab displays the panel items in order, with left-most item at the top. Select an item, and then use the up and down arrows at the right to move it.
