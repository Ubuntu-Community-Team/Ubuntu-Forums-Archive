How do I burn a DVD?
A.  How to burn an episode to DVD with Windows XP and Sonic My DVD:
1. Go to a mirror site and download the episode parts to a place you'll remember or will write down. I download to my desktop (C:/ My computer/My desktop/ New Voyages).
2. Unzip the parts and again use the desktop or another place you will remember.  Delete the zipped files from the desktop after you have successfully unzipped them.
3. Open Sonic My DVD program or try another DVD burning program (I did not have luck with Nero).
4. Click on create a DVD and open the file...go to the unzipped part 1 (C:/ My computer/My desktop/ New Voyages) and put it in the files to be burned area.
5. Repeat with the rest of the parts in order.
6. Go to the advanced or tools setting and pick a template for the title page. Sonic has a sci-fi template that works well. You can add music to the title page here, too.
7. Click on burn or record. It takes up to an hour, depending on your computer.
8. Use a DVD RW so if it doesn't work, you can just reformat it and try again.
9. Use a DVD copy program to record onto a burn once DVD so you can reuse the RW.
10. Make sure your DVD player plays the type you are using ie, DVD+,  DVD+RW, DVD-, DVD-RW.
