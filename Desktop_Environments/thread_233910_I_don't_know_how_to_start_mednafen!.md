### Post by glisteringwaters on 2007-03-05
$ cd <rom dir>
$ mednafen <rom filename>

If your game loads you can press F3 to configure controls.

Although I didn't understand the output of this command, maybe you can decipher how to use mednafen:
$ mednafen --help
