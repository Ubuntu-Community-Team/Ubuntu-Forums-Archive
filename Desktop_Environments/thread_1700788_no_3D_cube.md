### Post by tommywright on 2011-03-05
Click "Folders" on the left bar and up in the top right (when that opens) there is a little folder button that will open a normal nautilus folder.  Pin that to your left bar.

Unity (that side bar thingy) is very slow but if you pin the apps you use most often over there, they actually run quickly (just the Unity stuff is slow).

As for your cube... your netbook's 3d might not work with the composite thingy... mine doesn't.  That takes a more powerful 3d card from what I understand.
