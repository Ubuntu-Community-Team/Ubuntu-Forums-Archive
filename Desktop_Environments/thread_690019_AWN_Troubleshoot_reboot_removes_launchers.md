### Post by bdtgp on 2008-02-13
What I usually do is this:

1. Have AWN open and then open AWN Manager
2. Go to the launchers tab and add the launchers manually through the Launchers tab
3. At this point they probably aren't showing on the bar.  Leave Manager open.
4. Right click on the bar and click close.
5. Go to Applications > Accessories > AWN  
6. Your launchers should now be visible.  Now you can close the manager if you want.

It is a pain, but it works every time for me and I have been using AWN for a while.
