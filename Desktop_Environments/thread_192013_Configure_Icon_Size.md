### Post by bryanlking on 2006-06-08
To get this feature,

Left click panel
Right click, Add Applet to Panel
Scroll down, and select Quick Launcher, and click Add to Panel

If you Right click the left edge of the applet, you can choose Configure Quick Launcher from the context menu.

Hope that helps

BK
