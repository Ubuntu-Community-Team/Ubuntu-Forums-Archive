### Post by dewey on 2006-08-05
Nearly all wireless mice that follow standard rules will work, that is because the wireless receiver often plugs into the usb port, and does the wireless conversion on it's own, as far as the computer knows, it's a regular mouse.  If you get a mouse with extended features or buttons however, the non-standard buttons will not work by default, but a quick google or forum search should yield an answer.

I'm using a Microsoft Wireless Intellimouse Explorer 2.0 and it works perfectly, excluding the two additional side buttons(But that's because I haven't bothered to enable them.)
