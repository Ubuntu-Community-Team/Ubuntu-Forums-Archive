### Post by nwiggi on 2012-06-25
I am having a problem with nautilis's right click menus. The menus are no longer correct. I right click and get some of the expected items, copy, paste, delete (not move to trash), etc., but I cannot move to trash or rename. At times I cannot open directories or files, sometimes right clicking does not work at all. This began late last week (Friday, I believe). The only program installed was plex media server, for a newly acquired Roku box. Can this be the cause? I re-start the machine and will get normal function for a few minutes but then it reverts to this problem state. 
I am running 10.04.4 LTS on an HP with an AMD A6-3400 quad-core CPU; 8 gigs RAM; 600 GB HD; Radeon graphics (cannot remember the number); system is fully updated per Update Manager.
Thanks for any suggestions.

I have tried the simple solution of un-installing the plex media server but this did not correct the problem.

### Post by nwiggi on 2012-06-27
This was partially solved. I discovered that some themes I had installed a while back also installed PCManFM with them. I had changed themes and inadvertently switched file managers as well. I have removed the themes and PCManFM to avoid the problem in the future. This change did cause the problem of the 'Places' menu item not opening anything but the 'Computer' entry but that is a problem discussed elsewhere in the forums. I am now off to solve that issue.
Thanks for looking at the issue with me.
