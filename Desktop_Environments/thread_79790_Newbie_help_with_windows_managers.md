### Post by Stormy Eyes on 2005-10-20
Did you mean Enlightenment 0.17 when you wrote "X17"? If so, then logout. When you get to the login screen, enter your user name. Before entering your password, click on "Sessions", and select Enlightenment from the Sessions menu. Then enter your password. You'll then be asked if you want to use Enlightenment just for that session, or if you want to make Enlightenment your default.

If you want greater control over how X starts up, I wrote a [HOWTO for creating custom X sessions](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CustomXSession).
