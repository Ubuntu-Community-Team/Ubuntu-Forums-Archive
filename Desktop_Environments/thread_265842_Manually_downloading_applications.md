### Post by bikeboy on 2006-09-26
To check the dependencies, go to the site where you can download the applications (from above), search for the app, click on dapper and you will see all the dependencies listed with big red dots next to them. You may well have many of them already installed, you just need the extra ones.

If you try to install a .deb and there are missing dependencies it should tell you something is missing.
