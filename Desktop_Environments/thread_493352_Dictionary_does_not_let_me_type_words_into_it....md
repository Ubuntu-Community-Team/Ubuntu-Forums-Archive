### Post by erkan01 on 2007-08-27
Hi all. First time on the forums and very new to Linux. Great operating system, must say. I have also had numerous troubles with the dictionary and it took me a little while to figure it out. I had a problem where video playback would not work, it would display about two seconds of the movie and then go blank. I solved this by turning off Beryl or Compiz. Doing this also allows you to type into the dictionary. Haven't played around with Beryl Compiz settings, but turning them off did the trick in my case. Not sure if it's a bug though, but the cause is certainly the above mentioned windows managers. If you don't want to sacrifice your effects, you can just use the dictionary app in accessories.
