### Post by zombifier25 on 2013-04-22
I don't use KDE (much), but this should work. Open System Settings -> Window Behavior -> Window Rules, and click "New".

In the first tab, for "Window title", change "Unimportant" to "Substring match", and enter "Mozila Firefox" (no quotes) in the box. In the second tab, enable "Fullscreen", change "Do Not Affect" to "Force" and tick "Yes". In the third tab, do the same to "Keep below". In the forth, "No titlebar and frame". Once done, click OK, then Apply. Open Firefox and see if it works.

If it doesn't, or if you want to revert to default, remove the rule in Window Rules.
