### Post by Oropher8598 on 2011-04-23
I'm not sure why Prism isn't in the Natty repos - that caught me out too. Unfortunately Prism hasn't yet been updated for Firefox 4, so you can't use Prism-the-Firefox-add-on in Natty either.

However, you can still download the standalone version from [http://prism.mozillalabs.com/started/]().

Just extract the package to somewhere in your home folder - I usually put the prism folder in one called '.apps' (works out as /home/my-username/.apps/prism). Then make a launcher with the Menu Editor that points to /home/your-username-here/.apps/prism/prism - that will open Prism's web app creator.
