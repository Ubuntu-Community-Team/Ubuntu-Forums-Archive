### Post by molom on 2008-01-03
Hi everyone,
This is a small video stating some few features and wallpapers of the Linux Mint E17 edition coming out January 2008. There will be a CD & DVD edition. The CD edition is designed for slow/outdated computers, thus has minimal apps like mplayer, Firefox, Thunderbird, XMMS and so on. While the DVD edition is designed for decent or new computers. Both editions should be available during January 2008.

What is so good about Linux Mint E17 compared to other OS's:
   - All codecs are preinstalled to give an excellent 'out of the box' experience
   - The art is designed for many people, there is a light theme, a dark theme and a minimalistic theme
   - It has a minimum requirement of 64mb of ram
   - It has plenty of eyecandy without sacrificing much CPU usage
   - There is a CD version and DVD version
   - Already comes with apps like Inkscape, GIMP, Jokosher and more
   - It has one of the fastest modern DE's
   - Isn't very cluttered
   - And is elegant

If you weren't happy with elive, gOS, Geubuntu, OZOS, Yellow Dog Linux or elbuntu, Linux Mint E17 might be the one just for you.
