### Post by chavo on 2006-08-04
If you want the full on Vista look with transparent borders and glowing buttons, you'll need to have xgl and compiz running. You'll also need to use the packages from the quinnstorm repos and not the vanilla xgl stuff. The latest version comes with a couple of Vista-esque window border themes.
