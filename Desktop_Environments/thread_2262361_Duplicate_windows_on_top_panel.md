### Post by Dennis N on 2015-01-24
You probably have two of the "Window Buttons" plugins installed on the panel. Check by right-clicking on the panel, choose Panel > Panel Preferences and inspect the "Items" tab for duplicate entries of "Window Buttons". Remove one.
