### Post by mocoloco on 2009-05-30
Right-click one of your panels in a blank area (the top or bottom panel).  Select "add to panel" and add the applet called "main menu".  You can then right-click it and move it wherever you want.  And you can right-click and remove the "menu bar" which has the 3 Applications, Places, System menus.
