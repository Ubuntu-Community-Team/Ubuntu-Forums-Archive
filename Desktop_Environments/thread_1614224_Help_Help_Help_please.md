You can also hit Alt + F2 to run commands (like firefox, chromium, terminal).

### Post by 3Miro on 2010-11-05
> **chris0108 said:**
> Right well not quite solved, yes the application menu coms up when you right click the desktop, and iv managed to add it to the top bar, but it is on the right hand side with and 'x' symbol in front of it and wont move at all.

X stands for XFCE. You can right-click on the menu and select for it to show icon and/or text as well the exact test that you want.

For the moving part, check with the other applets on the panel (with right click) are any of them "locked" (i.e. cannot be moved). XFCE applets are more rigid and in order to move your menu to the left, you probably have to right-click -> select move then move it all the way to the left (and it will not move until you click again).

I hope this helps.
