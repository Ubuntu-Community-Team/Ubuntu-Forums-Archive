### Post by engla on 2006-02-11
Hmm, something is wrong.

I don't know how to solve it, but if the message "Metacity Window manager" and it's icon is repeated twice, then something is wrong.

Could you try to choose another session when logging in? Try standard GNOME or Failsafe GNOME and see if they take as long time to log in as it does now.


Something could simply be strange with your session set up. Look at your session settings in the Menu System > Preferences > Sessions. Look out for very strange things in the running applications list, and make sure nothing shows up duplicated.
Look in your "Start Applications" list too, and see that it doesn't have anything you didn't ask for (it's fine if it is empty)
