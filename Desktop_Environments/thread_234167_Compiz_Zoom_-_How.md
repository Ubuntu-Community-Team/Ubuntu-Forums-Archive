### Post by adam.tropics on 2006-08-11
Go to System>>Preferences>>Keyboard, the click on the Layout Options Tab. You need to actually set it to 'Super is mapped to win-keys' (6th option from the top) as 'default', at least for my laptop, doesn't do much!
