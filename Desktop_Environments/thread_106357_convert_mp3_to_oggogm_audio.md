### Post by yanik on 2005-12-20
You will loose quality for sure and your files won't be any smaller either.

You can use a nautilus script called audioconverter:

[https://savannah.nongnu.org/projects/audio-convert](https://savannah.nongnu.org/projects/audio-convert)

after having installed it, just right click on a file and chose scripts--audioconverter.
