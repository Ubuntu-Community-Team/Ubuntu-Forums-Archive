### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-07-27
You wrote that you wanted it to look like the first picture and you have posted only one picture . 

If you want to hide the folders use the directional arrows . If the arrow points down the folders are visible and sideways they are hidden  .

Under the edit tab there are many different settings you can experiment with and customize tool bar can also be found there .
