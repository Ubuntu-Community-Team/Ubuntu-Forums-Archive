### Post by winkerbean on 2007-10-16
I got it!

I editted my /etc/passwd file to change the shell I use (from /bin/bash to /usr/bin/zsh).  I then pressed ctrl-alt-F3, logged in as me, and pressed ctrl-alt-F7.  Now, all is well, or as well as can be.
