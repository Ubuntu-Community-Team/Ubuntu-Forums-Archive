### Post by daedalusman on 2006-10-22
You can change your window manager to fluxbox in gnome (replace metacity) and then set nautilus up not to draw to desktop. This should get you fluxboxe's menu in gnome but you will no longer have desktop icons. I don't know of another way of getting a similar menu in gnome.
