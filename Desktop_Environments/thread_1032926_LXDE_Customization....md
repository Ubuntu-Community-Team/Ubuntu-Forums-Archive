### Post by adamlau on 2009-01-07
1. You can get 'Application' and 'Places' icons without text on the top panel. The mouse driven config can be extremely time consuming to customize.

2. You can right-click in an open area on the Desktop to change the display order, but not the alignment. Unless the source is modified before compilation, there is no other method as of yet to remove the My Documents icon. You can always choose to not have the LXDE WM manage the Desktop.

3. You can use Ubuntu default Human and GNOME icon themes in LXDE. The look and feel may not be exact, but it comes close.
