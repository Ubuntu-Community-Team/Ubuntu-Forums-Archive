One goes to System Settings by clicking the furthermost icon in the upper right hand corner of your screen and the window opens up. You should see a section titled "Personal" and the entry for screensavers should be there. Click on it and make your choice. My favorite is GLCells, which features a rapidly growing collection of single cells undergoing mitosis. The colors of them change as well.
