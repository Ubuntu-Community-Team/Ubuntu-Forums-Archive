### Post by Zorael on 2009-07-19
This seems worthy of a wishlist report at [bugs.kde.org](http://bugs.kde.org). Registration required though, but if devs don't know bugs exist, they can only be fixed by accident. Same goes for wishlist features. ;>

I'm not sure what your usercase is, but at least in 4.3 you can define available actions in System Settings -> Device Actions. Then when you click the device in the notifier widget, it'll offer it alongside opening it with Dolphin/etc. The rules interface is fairly self-explanatory.

I removed the device notifier widget and just use the Kickoff (application launcher) to mount and unmount devices.
