### Post by guiverc on 2021-11-04
You haven't mentioned your release of Lubuntu; but Lubuntu uses `[pcmanfm-qt]("https://manual.lubuntu.me/stable/2/2.4/2.4.4/pcmanfm-qt.html")` and has done so for many (7+) releases (*all supported releases in fact*).  It's Qt5 based and not GTK3 based like `nautilus` or GNOME.
