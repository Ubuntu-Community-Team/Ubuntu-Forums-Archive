### Post by Brandon.Viking on 2009-08-10
I have a "screenlet" daemon installed which will allow you to fire up a little sensor screen which can display a number of things ... one of them is the GPU temp on my setup.

The package is called screenlet and can be easily found in synaptic.
You may require desktop effects. I have them ... I dont know if the screenlets will work without the effects turned on. YMMV 

Hope that helps.
