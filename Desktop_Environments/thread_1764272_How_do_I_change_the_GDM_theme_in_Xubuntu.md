I just tried both of these methods on Xubuntu 11.04, and neither are changing the background image of gdm. Any other thoughts?

### Post by Toz on 2011-07-05
Make sure the background image is somewhere that is accessible by the gdm account (which is the account that gdm is run as). The preferred location is **/usr/share/xfce4/backdrops**.
