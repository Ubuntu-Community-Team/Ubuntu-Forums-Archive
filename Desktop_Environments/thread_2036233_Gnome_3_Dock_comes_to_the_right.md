### Post by Copper Bezel on 2012-08-01
Yeah, that's the dock extension. If you turn off that extension (either using Gnome Tweak Tool or "Installed Extensions" from extensions.gnome.org) the normal favorites bar will still be visible in the Activities overview. 

If you want to keep the dock, you can move it to the left through dconf-editor under org.gnome.shell.extensions.dock or through the preferences icon in the extensions.gnome.org interface. 

With that said, there's also a better extension for this that just turns the Favorites bar into a dock instead of adding the dock on top:

[https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/307/dash-to-dock/](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/307/dash-to-dock/)
