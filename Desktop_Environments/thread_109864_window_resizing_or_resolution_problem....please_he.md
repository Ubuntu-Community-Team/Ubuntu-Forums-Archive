### Post by Galoot on 2005-12-30
[ALT]+F4 probably won't save your changes in most cases. For that you need to be able to reach the "Save" or "Okay" button or menu entry, or whatever needs to be done in whatever application you're using.

Of course, if that button/menu happens to be off the screen...:razz: 

Hold down [ALT] and drag the window around with your left mouse button. I know it'll work in Gnome and Xfce, and probably in most other window managers.

Some windows just don't seem to get it as far as screen resolution is concerned. It bugs me, too.
