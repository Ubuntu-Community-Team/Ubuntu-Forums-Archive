### Post by Larkspur on 2011-11-30
If you want to launch programs with a mouse gesture, I'd recommend Easystroke (it's in the Software Centre), which works almost exactly like Scrybe.  It doesn't come with any pre-defined gestures, but it's easy to make your own.
