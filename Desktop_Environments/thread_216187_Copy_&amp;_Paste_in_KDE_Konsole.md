### Post by wieman01 on 2006-07-15
Highlight the text you want to copy with the mouse cursor and press "crtl+c". Then move the cursor to the location to which you want to copy the text. Then press the mousewheel button.

This works both ways (from & to the terminal).

### Post by Jucato on 2006-07-15
When copying **from** the console, highlight the text you want to copy, right-click and choose Copy. Ctrl+C in Konsole (or in any terminal) is the 
shortcut for Cancel/Terminate.

Alternatively, you can setup your own keyboard shortcut for copy in Konsole. just don't use Ctrl+C. I used Ctrl+Ins, in tandem with Paste in Konsole, which is Shift+Ins.
