### Post by lowpan on 2009-11-08
To lose the default clutter desktop, you need to 

1. go to your top panel, right mouse click, add to panel 'Menu Bar'


2. go to the newly added menu bar: System->Preferences->Startup Applications, remove Netbook Launcher and Maximus Window Management

If I remember right, that's how I got rid of the clutter and got a clean desktop.
