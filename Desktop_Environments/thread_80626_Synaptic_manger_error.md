### Post by rjwood on 2005-10-22
you may have to remove the us part in the sources list and comment out (#)
the backports lines.

sudo gedit /etc/apt/sources.list

a window will pop up and you can make the changes and then save it after the changes close out and then do

sudo apt-get update
then
sudo apt get dist-upgrade
