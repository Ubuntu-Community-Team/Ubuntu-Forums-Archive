### Post by owain123 on 2008-10-20
When i mount a partition, it puts an icon on the Desktop.

Is there a way i can stop this from happening?

---

### Post by benerivo on 2008-10-20
You could try opening the configuration editor. I think it's in the main menu, or you can press Alt+F2 and type gconf-editor. Then go to Apps > Nautilus > Desktop and untick 'volumes_visible.
