### Post by asplode on 2006-08-24
Its very possible to delete your XP installation from the Ubuntu Live CD.  Just pop it in, and click the install button on your desktop when it loads up.

Then either select the option to completely erase your hard disk, or manually partition it yourself, and delete your XP partition, and probably make a home partition for your things in case the need arises to reinstall.

This might help a bit, although I like to put my swap partition in the middle between ubuntu and home.
