### Post by CyberCod on 2006-08-31
there are different kinds of beeps for different errors.

Usually if it is a long continuous beep, its heat-related.

If it is intermittent, then you need to take note of what order they are in, like morse code.  Longs and shorts.  

Then find out your motherboard manufacturer and google them with the words beep codes.

For example, if your mobo was by ASUS, you search "ASUS beep codes"

from there you should have a better idea what it is trying to tell you.

Good luck.
