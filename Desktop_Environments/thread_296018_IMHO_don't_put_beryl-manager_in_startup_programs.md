### Post by waldorf on 2006-11-09
I love beryl and thoroughly appreciate all the effort that has gone into putting it together. Kudos all around.

However, there are enough glitches that I find it helpful to not have it startup automatically and end up getting stuck in an unusable desktop. Its no big deal to alt f2 and then type beryl-manager when I'm ready to fire it up. And I can rest assured that I'll have good old reliable metacity waiting for me when I log in.

I know this is contrary to the advice given in the wiki here and at the beryl project, but thats my humble opinion.
