### Post by jimbo99 on 2009-05-12
I think too many people are still using compiz with kde when kwin is the better compositing manager for KDE.  It is missing a few bells and whistles but after you use a compositing manager for a while you tend to shut stuff off and use the basics.  It may take a while but ultimately that'll happen.

Kwin is a good compositing manager with many useful tools.  I can only imagine the issues one would have with compiz.  I used gnome for years until kde 4.2 came out.  Now I'm a dedicated KDE guy.  I find new things that just make sense under KDE where I struggle with some of it under gnome even though I used it for years--for example things that used to work two ubuntu releases ago but no longer work in the most current release, such as icons for devices on the desktop.
