### Post by doclivingston on 2005-07-14
It depends on exactly what format(s) the .avi uses; AVI is a "container" that can contain any number of things. The commonest formats are divx/mp3 or xvid/mp3, in which case the two above suggestions could work.

If they don't work, you'll have the fun job of trying to figure out what format it is in, and what you need to play it.
