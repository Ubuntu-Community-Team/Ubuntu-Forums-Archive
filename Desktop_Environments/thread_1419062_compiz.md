### Post by manoriax on 2010-03-01
Compiz fusion is installed by default. The only thing you really need is the right driver for your graphics card. Turn compiz on by right-clicking on your desktop, choose "Change Desktop Background" and change to the "Visual Effects"-tab. There you can pick the option to turn compiz on (see screenshot).
