### Post by BigSilly on 2011-10-30
We're using Lubuntu 11.10 on our Eee 701, and it's marvellous isn't it?

Anyhoo, I get desktop icons by going into the normal user menus bottom left of the screen, finding the program I want on the desktop, say Chromium, then right clicking on it and selecting "Add to Desktop". This should work for you.
