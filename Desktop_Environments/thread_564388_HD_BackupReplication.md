### Post by Yoooder on 2007-10-01
you can look into free imaging solutions, such as g4u [http://www.feyrer.de/g4u/]("http://www.feyrer.de/g4u/")

A commercial product that I've great luck with is called Acronis, you could use their trial version to do what you need.  It would probably be the better option if the other computers have different size/model harddrives (g4u is intended for cloning identical machines).

In either case I haven't ever burned the image to CD.  What may be easier is to setup a simple FTP server on one machine (or a SMB share if you use acronis) and use that machine to store the image and redistribute it to the others.
