### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-11-03
It depends on whether you're using the **Clock** or **DateTime** applet, the latter being better in my opinion, and more configurable.  If you want to try **DateTime** you will have to install **xfce4-datetime-plugin** in the usual way then you can right click in the panel and add it, and remove **Clock**.

I don't use Clock so I'm not sure how you can remove Week No, but probably right clicking on the clock and going to Properties will give you options; that is how you change the applet DateTime.
