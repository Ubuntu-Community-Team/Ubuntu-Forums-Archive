### Post by schmappel on 2007-06-25
Through the grapevine I heard that the author of Launchy is working on a Linux port, but that might take a while. Meanwhile, you could also give [Launch Box]("http://developer.imendio.com/projects/gnome-launch-box") a shot, it's in the repos (for Feisty):

```
sudo apt-get install gnome-launch-box
```
