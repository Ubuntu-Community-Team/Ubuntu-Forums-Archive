### Post by aysiu on 2006-10-28
1. Don't change the Ubuntu defaults unless you know what you're doing.

2. Use strong (hard to guess) passwords always

3. Encrypt personal data

4. Use the NoScript extension to block Flash/Javascript/Java on sites you haven't explicitly approved

5. Don't open attachments in emails or click on links from inside emails

6. Don't install software that's not from the repositories (Add/Remove Programs, Synaptic, Adept, *aptitude*).
