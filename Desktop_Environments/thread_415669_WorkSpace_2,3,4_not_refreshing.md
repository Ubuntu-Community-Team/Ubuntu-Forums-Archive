### Post by garg on 2007-04-20
Hello,

I installed Beryl on Feisty Fawn. Only Workspace 1 works perfectly. If I move the 3D cube to workspace 2,3, or 4, it starts ghosting all the windows and menus. What I mean by ghosting is that if I click and drag a window, it leaves a trail of windows. If I click on the menus (system, places, apps) then they open but never appear to close. 

It seems as if all those workspaces don't refresh at all. Is there a setting that I need to enable? I searched and tried tweaking the settings but that problem does not go away.

Thanks!

---

### Post by garg on 2007-04-20
I had to go to Beryl Settings > General Options > Desktop Background > Unchecked DESKTOP manager supports viewports.

That fixed the streaking :D
