### Post by kperkins on 2005-11-05
Go to:
Insert>
	Envelope...
		Type in the Addressee's info in the addressee box (or if addressing      to more than one person, and you have a db file use that), type in sender's (that would be you) info in the Sender box.
		Click on the Format tab, and choose your envelope size (eg. Env 10) see if the borders, etc are to your liking (if you want to use something besides your default font in either the addressee, or sender field, click on edit over to the right.)
		Click on Printer tab, and choose one of the icons, for which way your printer prints envelopes, click on properties, and set up printer to print envelopes, of the size you chose.
		Click on New Doc. or Insert (I prefer New Doc.) and see if you like the looks of your envelope.  You can edit it right in OOo now.
Print.
