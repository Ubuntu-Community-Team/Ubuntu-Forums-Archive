### Post by coffeecat on 2007-06-15
Have a look at [this]("http://penguinfonts.com/howto/ubuntu.php"). Don't bother with the first method. You don't need an extra app to so this.

Another GUI method if you have the gnome desktop is:

System > Preferences > Font. Press 'Details' button. Press 'Go to Font Folder' Button. Simply drag and drop your fonts into the window with all the fonts. Nothing appears to happen but they do install. You may have to restart the Xserver before you can use them, or they may be available immediately - I can't remember.

You don't have to do anything special for them to be available in Open Office.
