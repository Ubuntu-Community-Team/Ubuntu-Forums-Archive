### Post by Fedcer on 2006-05-12
In Kcontrol, under Fonts in "Appearance & Themes" you can activate Anti-aliasing for the fonts (remember to click on "Configure" and set it to maximum for best quality). Also play with the sub-pixel thing. It improved text a lot in my LCD.
