### Post by Execute_Method on 2008-07-10
I turned it off in:

system -> preferences -> sound (system beep tab)
(turn off system beep)

But i left the system sounds enabled in case of OS errors.

That way, you don't have to unplug your system speaker (so you will still be notified of a hardware problem)
