### Post by elsewhere on 2005-06-26
You'll need a kioslave that can work with the ipod to use "ipod:/" functionality.

Take a look at [ipodslave](http://kpod.sourceforge.net/ipodslave)  on SourceForge.  I haven't tried it myself but should give you the functionality you're looking for.

iPod support isn't native to KDE (yet), so Suse must have included something similar with their distribution.
