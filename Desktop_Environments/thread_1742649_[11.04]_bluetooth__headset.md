### Post by edgue on 2011-04-29
Hello there,

I am new to xubuntu; and this morning I was somewhat puzzled by 
the fact, that there seems no "build in" support; in other words:
with gnome or kde, there would be a bluetooth icon to seach/connect
to devices without me doing anything.

Anyway, I installed the blueman device manager; and that looked
quite promising. It would discover my Plantronics 590 headset
immediately ... show up a nice UI ... and I selected the
headset service.

The blueman manager tells me: its there, connected, all good.
And the headset is happily blinking too.

BUT: it doesnt work! Neither with the cmd line alsamixer, 
the "xubuntu" alsa mixer or the pulse audio device chooser
i could find any entry related to the headset. No mixer, no
audio. End of story.

So - what am I missing here?

EDIT: OK, i was missing the bluetooth pulse audio module.
I installed that; and voila, the headset shows up 
in the volume controller. 

Unfortunately audio quality really sucks; incoming
sound is extremely choppy ;-(
