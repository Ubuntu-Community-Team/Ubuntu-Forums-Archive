Sure. Just install kubuntu-desktop thrpugh apt-get or Synaptic. It will show up in your GDM log-in under Options.

### Post by Xiong Chiamiov on 2008-06-12
You can also install the kde or kde-core packages.  Kubuntu-desktop comes with everything you'd get by installing from a Kubuntu disk, kde comes with a few extra packages on top of KDE (a few games and such), and kde-core installs the minimum to get KDE to run.
