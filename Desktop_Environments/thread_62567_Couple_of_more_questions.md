### Post by krusbjorn on 2005-09-05
Right click on the panel.
Select "Add to panel". 
Select "Trash". 
Click "Add"


Or if you want it as a desktop icon:

Applications->System tools->Configuration Editor.

Apps->Nautilus->Desktop. Check the "Trash Icon Visible" checkbox.
