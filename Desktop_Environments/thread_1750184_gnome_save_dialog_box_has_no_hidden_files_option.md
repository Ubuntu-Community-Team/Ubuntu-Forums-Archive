### Post by ZekeGraal on 2011-05-05
On FireFox, under the edit menu, hit preferences. Under the general tab do you have 'always ask me where to save files' enabled? If you do, when the save box comes up, you then hit save file. Then a box should come up asking where to save it. Browse to your Home folder, and click on a file, (just to highlight it), and hit Ctrl + H. The hidden files should show up. You can then browse to your .gimp directory.
