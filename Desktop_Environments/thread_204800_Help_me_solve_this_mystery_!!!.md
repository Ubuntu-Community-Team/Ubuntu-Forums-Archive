### Post by cyberb0b on 2006-06-27
The night before you double clicked on the Firefox icon on your desktop. Then you closed Firefox.  The Firefox icon was still highlighted, but you didn't notice.  The monitor goes to sleep. Later on you want to wake up the monitor, so you move the mouse and push the space bar.  Pushing the space bar while having the Firefox icon highlighted causes Firefox to start up.  (It's an accessibility feature.  Not everyone uses the mouse...)
