### Post by jachymc on 2007-10-27
Many. KDE, GNOME, Xfce, Enlightenment and other are referred as "desktop environment". Other projects are focused at windows management only - they are "window managers". they may not be as comprehensive, as desktop environments are, however, they are focused at low memory usage and fast start. 

short list: fvwm, icewm, windowmaker, fluxbox

visit [http://www.linux.org/apps/all/GUI/Window_Managers.html](http://www.linux.org/apps/all/GUI/Window_Managers.html) for more details
