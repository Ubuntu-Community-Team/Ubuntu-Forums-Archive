### Post by Sendervictorius on 2006-07-10
Well, I like GnuCash. It's got the features I need to manage the accounts of two voluntary organizations where I am the treasurer. I find it simple and intuative. It has more of a double-entry accounting model than a simple check-book tracker, which may be a barrier to some. However, if you know your debits from your liabilities, it is quite easy!

GnuCash could do with more automation features to manage club membership subscriptions though! ;)
