### Post by Ben Page on 2009-01-22
Hey Ubuntu lovers/users!
I visited many pages where there were comparations of different distros, witch one is better, faster etc.
I have noticed that Ubuntu is mentioned everywhere, but Linux gurus are recommending others distros over Ubuntu, and one of the main argument is that its ugly...I agree that this is a personal preference, but you don't choose your distro or even OS by its looks. By installing almost all mainstream distros, I could say that they are the same, only difference between them is RPM or DEB package management and package managers themselves, and the desktop theme off course, there is DE, too.
So to cut to the case, I wanted to make a Desktop "surface" showdown, to showoff our desktops and prove to everyone that Ubuntu, like any other distro is highly customizable, and it can look like we want it to look like, that no distro is ugly or pretty, its the way we set it up...


So, Here is my desktop...with Emerald (window decorator theme, Ubuntu studio icons and fedora 7 wallpaper):
