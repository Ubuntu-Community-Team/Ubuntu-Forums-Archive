### Post by The Cog on 2012-10-27
10.04 used gnome2, which has been discontinued. 
12.04 uses Unity by default, which some people love and others hate.
Gnome2 is going to disappear so eventually you will have to cut the cord and find a replacement. 

There are alternatives that can be loaded from your current 12.04 to try out if you don't like unity:
lubuntu-desktop, xubuntu-desktop (that I use), kubuntu-desktop. There is also a gnome-3 desktop but I can't remember the package name.
