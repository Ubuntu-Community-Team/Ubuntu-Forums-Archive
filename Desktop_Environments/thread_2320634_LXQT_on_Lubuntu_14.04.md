### Post by vasa1 on 2016-04-16
Just to add to the link sudodus provided ... LXQt is still under development and, as I understand, the focus will be on making sure things work well for Lubuntu _16.10_. I'm not sure that the team will be that interested in feedback (or in troubleshooting issues) arising from use on Lubuntu 14.04.

### Post by ajgreeny on 2016-04-16
I have just tried it as a live OS on running from a DVD supplied by one of the UK Linux magazines (Linux Format) where they have it running on a non-installable 15.10 just to have a look.

However, at this stage in development it is honestly not worth your while even attempting to add it to 14.04.  In 15.10 it lacked just about everything that is needed to make as sensible and usable OS so I can not imagine 14.04 would be any better, but probably worse.

Wait till it's available in 16.10 and it may be worth trying again.
