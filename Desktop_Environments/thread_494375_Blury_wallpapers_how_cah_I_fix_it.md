### Post by orb9220 on 2007-07-07
[B]> I have noticed that those that are 128x96 pixels look ok. But the ones that I download from the internet are 80x60, 96x72... and so on.

Those are thumbnails not the actual wallpaper.[/B]

 At wallpaper sites you either click on it then it will open full size then you can right-click save image as. Or they will have links under the thumbnail showing sizes like: 800x600,1024x768,1280x1024 etc... 

If you are running your desktop at 1280x1024 then you would select that image for desktop, If they only have 1024x768 available then you still can download and select Fit to desktop and it will stretch it to fit. If it is high enough quality then you will not notice it.
