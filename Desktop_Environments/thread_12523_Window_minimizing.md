### Post by farruinn on 2005-01-25
I don't know of a way to disable *just* that effect, but you can disable all of the metacity eye-candy by opening Applications>System Tools>Configuration editor, then browse to apps>metacity>general in the left-side pane, checkmark the reduced_resources option.  I really wish gnome didn't have these "hidden features", but unfortunately I don't know of a more intuitive way to do this.
