### Post by vextorspace on 2005-05-25
If that doesn't work (which it didn't on my nvidia with NEC) then you may have to add a modeline.  For some reason Xorg kept thinking the 1280x1024 it came up with didn't work, so I used amlc (another modeline calculator) to create a modeline for my monitor

 Modeline "1280x1024" 132.35 1280 1368 1496 1688 1024 1025 1028 1089

and it worked after that.  (of course you would need to do that for your monitor.  Most of the information for the modeline is in the Xorg.log in /val/log)

-Doug
