### Post by meng on 2006-08-21
I don't know the reason why but an imperfectly burned CD is a common reason for the problem you describe. I've experienced this myself, and I try burning at about half maximum speed to be safer (i.e. 24x on a 48x-capable burner). Always easier to do this if you have CD-RWs rather than CD-Rs, even though the latter are cheap.
