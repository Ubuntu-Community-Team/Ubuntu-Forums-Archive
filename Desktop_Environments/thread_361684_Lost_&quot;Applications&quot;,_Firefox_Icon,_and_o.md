### Post by crossedout on 2007-02-15
Do you have a panel at all on the desktop?  If so, just right-click and choose add new panel, then customize it how you like.  Or you could add the items to the panel you already have setup.

Also, try:
```
xfce4-panel
```
 inside the terminal.

If you still have problems after that, refer to this link:
[http://forum.xfce.org/index.php?topic=2843.msg12240;topicseen]("http://forum.xfce.org/index.php?topic=2843.msg12240;topicseen")

-Xout
