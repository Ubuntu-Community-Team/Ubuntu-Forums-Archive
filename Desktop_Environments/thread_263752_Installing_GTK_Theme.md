### Post by aysiu on 2006-10-17
It has to be in the form .tar.gz

After you download it, do not extract the .tar.gz. Do not double-click it. Just drag it to the theme manager window as is.

And make sure your autodownload action in your browser is to download and not to use "file-roller" or some other application to open the .tar.gz file.
