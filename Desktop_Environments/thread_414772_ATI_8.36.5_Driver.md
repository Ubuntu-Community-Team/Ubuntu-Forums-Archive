### Post by BrownieBoy on 2007-04-20
ATI 8.36.5 Driver released April 18th 2007, and much improvement for me.

FPS in Doom 3 was unplayable previously, even at 640x480 (yes, direct rendering was enabled).  Now I'm playing at 1152x864 with still playable FPS.  Plus, you don't need to do that patch thing when you install the driver from .deb files any more.  (See H Janzing's post at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=394489](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=394489) ).

Anybody else seeing any difference, good or bad?

I'm running Feisty with a Radeon X800 GT 256 Meg Video RAM.  Athlon 3800+ CPU with 1 Gig system RAM.
