### Post by celem on 2010-03-09
I have a Dell Mini 9 running Xubuntu 9.04. It runs great and I love resource friendly Xubuntu on it and I've had Xubuntu on it for about a year, starting right after the Dell installed Ubuntu trashed the system during an update due to a lack of disk space (2GB Flash drive). However, today I discovered that the VGA port is not functional, so I cannot use the netbook for presentations. Anyone got any ideas??

---

### Post by celem on 2010-03-09
Solved - if a monitor is plugged into the VGA port during bootup it works. If no monitor is plugged into the VGA port during bootup it will not work.
