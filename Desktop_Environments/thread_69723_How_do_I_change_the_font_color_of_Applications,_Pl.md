### Post by FLeiXiuS on 2005-09-27
[QUOTE=vayu]Is there a config file or a place I can go to specify the font color for the menu items and the clock on the panel?[/QUOTE]

Your best bet is editing your theme.  

Navigate to your themes folder: -- cd /usr/share/themes -- Then browse into your theme folder and look for a file called panel.rc.  From there you should be able to configure the colors and what not.  

I'd reccommend reading gnome art @ [http://art.gnome.org/](http://art.gnome.org/) they've got some great FAQ's and HOWTO's.  Check it out!
