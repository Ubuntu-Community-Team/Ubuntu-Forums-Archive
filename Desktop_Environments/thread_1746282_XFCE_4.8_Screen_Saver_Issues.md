### Post by zajc on 2011-05-04
I have the same problem (xubuntu 11.04). Settings in the Power Manager (Put display to sleep... or Switch off display...) are not working if Xscreensaver is enabled and at least once started. I think this is reported by other users. It has some "PAM security issues..." :confused:

The only working workaround is to enable Power Management in Screensaver settings (Advanced tab, Display Power Management) or to disable Xscreensaver.
