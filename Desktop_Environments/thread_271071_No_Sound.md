### Post by rogier.de.groot on 2006-10-04
Since it's a laptop (which is how I understand you) there should be a blue-ish "Fn" button, which in combination with one of the Fx buttons (eg, F4) should increase the volume. Otherwise, check if the volume button is in the systray, open up the entire mixer display (lots of volume slides) and check the slides for master volume, wave, cd, etc. If there isn't a volume button check if the soundcard is enabled in the laptop's BIOS.
