### Post by fishexe on 2008-10-04
I think you may have emerald installed but not running.  To have it run automatically when you log in, go to System->Preferences->Sessions.  Then click "Add", type Emerald for the name and 'emerald --replace' for the command.  Log out and back in, and it should work.

I found that in THIS FAQ:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=809695&highlight=language+change](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=809695&highlight=language+change)

hope this helps, I was having similar problems until I read this FAQ.
