### Post by iceshaft07 on 2006-09-16
It sounds like you need Automatix or Easy Ubuntu.

Those are the first two programs I run after I install Ubuntu on my computer. It covers just about everything you need.

Just do a search on Google, or on these forums. That should get it working. Actually, I am listening to a radio station right now (202.fm), and I just installed ubuntu after my HDD failed the other day.

Also, I think you will need to follow the direcitons on the automatix website for the installation. I don't think the ones on these forums work right anymore.

And for Easy Ubuntu, after you have it downloaded, you have to go to the home folder and run LaunchSH.

### Post by mgmiller on 2006-09-16
Hi, I just went to the link you provided and it works fine with rhythmbox.  You shouldn't need to copy or paste anything.  On the buzz hompage, just click on the winamp high link.  Then in rhythmbox, go to your radio stations and you will see 4 streams for 181.fm the buzz.  double click one of the streams to start listening.
Any time you get a choice for an internet radio station for formats, select mp3 or winamp and it should just work.  (This assumes you have the mp3 codec installed, easy ubuntu works great for this).
