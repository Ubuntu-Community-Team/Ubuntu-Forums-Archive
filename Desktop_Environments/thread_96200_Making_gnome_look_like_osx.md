### Post by chiefofthejojos on 2005-11-28
As far as I know the best way to get "some of" the functionality that is in that bottom panel (zooming, bouncing...) is to use a gdesklets applet called "StarterBar".  apt-get gdesklets and then add a new applet, providing this as the url:
[http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/files.php?func=gd_downloadfile&gd_filename=starterbar-desklet-0.31.3.tar.gz&gd_fileid=742](http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/files.php?func=gd_downloadfile&gd_filename=starterbar-desklet-0.31.3.tar.gz&gd_fileid=742)
Then, just double-click on the new applet and it will ask you where to place it.
Enjoy!
