### Post by timcredible on 2008-09-08
i did this by using compiz and emerald to make the windows and browser look just like windows (window decorations and such, i even setup firefox to say 'Internet Explorer' on the window).  for making the screen look like xp, just take a screenprint from an xp machine and make it your wallpaper, then auto-hide your bottom and top bar.  unless someone realizes that the time is wrong (since it's just a screenprint), nobody will know.
