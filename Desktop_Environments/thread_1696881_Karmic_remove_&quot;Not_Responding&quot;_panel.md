### Post by sikander3786 on 2011-02-28
Welcome to the forums :-)

By panels I hope you mean the bars at the top and bottom of your desktop.

You want to completely remove both of them? If only one, you can right click and choose 'delete panel'.

Or if an app becomes un-responsive, you can restart the panels by pressing Alt + F2 and

```
killall gnome-panel
```
