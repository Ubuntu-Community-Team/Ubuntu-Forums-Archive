### Post by Alwimo on 2011-07-05
Hold Alt while left-clicking and holding that anywhere on the Firefox window, then drag it. Does the window move, so it is no longer partially obscured?
 
Alternatively, click on the workspace switching button on the launcher (third from the bottom not including the recycle bin) and while in the zoomed out view, you can move the Firefox window clear of the panel and launcher.
