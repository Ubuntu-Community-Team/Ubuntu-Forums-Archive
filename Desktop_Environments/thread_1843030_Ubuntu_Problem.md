### Post by Basher101 on 2011-09-12
Do you use a laptop? sometimes your hard disk spins down to save power. In this state it needs a few seconds to respin. In that time your OS will "lag" or "freeze" only for those few seconds.

---

### Post by aura7 on 2011-09-12
In System -> Preferences -> Power Management 

Remove the tick from spin down hard disk when possible.

### Post by Basher101 on 2011-09-12
In other words, this is not a bug, it is a feature :p If you do indeed use a laptop i would leave the option on. You may waste 3-4 seconds due to the respin time, but save 30 minutes or so battery life.
