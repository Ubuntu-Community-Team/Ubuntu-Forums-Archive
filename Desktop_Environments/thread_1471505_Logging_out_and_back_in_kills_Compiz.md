OK, I've got a real head-scratcher here.  I'm running PC/OS 10.1, which is based on Xubuntu, so hopefully any solutions offered here will work for me.

I've got Compiz and everything installed on here.  However, if I've got Compiz activated when I log out, I find myself with no titlebar or frames whenever I log back in.  Thankfully, this is easily corrected by opening the Fusion Icon and going back to Xfwm and then going back to Compiz, where I have the titlebar and frames and eye-popping effects and the whole nine yards. ### Post by fredbird67 on 2010-05-03
Never mind, I found the answer myself, in this thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1165146](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1165146)

Namely, what worked for me was setting the Compiz Fusion Icon to automatically start whenever I logged in.  This way, the command to show the Compiz borders and titlebar are there.  Works like a charm now! :-)
