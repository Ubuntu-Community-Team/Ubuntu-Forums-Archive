### Post by Go_Bucks on 2008-05-10
Find audacious in synaptic, click on "properties", then click on the "installed files" tab. I haven't used that program, but most executables (but not all) are in /usr/bin. If there is a /usr/bin/audacious or something like that, that is the executable. Set that as the program to open your files in the "open with" dialogue.
