### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-01-02
The panel icons can be moved easily enough by right clicking on them and choosing Move.  You may need to unlock them first to do this, which again is a right click option.  There are a few things like **Window list** and **Notification area** which need a right click in the small space just to the left of the total area, (you'll understand what I mean when you do it) rather than within the panel applet itself.  As to why the things have moved, I have no idea.

You should also be able to move items on the panels (when they're unlocked with right click -> untick "lock to panel") by holding down the middle mouse button and dragging, or holding down left + right mouse buttons and dragging.
