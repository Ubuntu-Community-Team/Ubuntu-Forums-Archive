### Post by keithld on 2009-02-27
If you are trying to access Windows documents while in Ubuntu like Word for Windows and you want to use Open Office to do so, when you go to File > Open,  you have choose "File System" then click on the folder "Host" because that is the Windows File system... Then you have to manuver through those folders to where you have your Windows Documents, Jpegs, & Mp3's stored...

The best way to learn is just look around Ubuntu and play with things, but do Not Changes Settings and things unless your sure of what it will do and you can Undo It..

Another way to find your Windows files is to go to Places > Computer (or what ever is similar to Computer) and click on File System and look for "Host"...
