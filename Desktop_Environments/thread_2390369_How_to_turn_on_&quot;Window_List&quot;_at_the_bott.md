### Post by sevendogsbsd on 2018-04-28
There is a Gnome shell extension for this but I do not know if it works with 18.04. Go to the Ubuntu software app, click on "Add-ons", then scroll all the way to the bottom and choose "Window List". It is official supported by Gnome but again, I am not sure it will work with 18.04. Just click the blue "install" button and it will install. You may have to restart the Gnome shell by "alt+F2" then "r" and enter.
