### Post by userundefine on 2006-06-18
I used [this guide]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=131267&highlight=xgl+thefuture") and had it up and running in 30 minutes...

And it used to crash on me pretty regularly and I just figured it "wasn't ready".  Then I disabled the dock plugin and it hasn't crashed on me once.  It's really quite stable and usable, especially for alpha software.  I use it from session login to exit, all the time.

There are small issues, like full screen video (where black bars normally should be, discolored and blocky strange colors appear although it doesn't affect the video playback itself).  Extremely minor, though.
