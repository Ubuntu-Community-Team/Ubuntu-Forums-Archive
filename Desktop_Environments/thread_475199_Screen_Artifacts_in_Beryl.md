### Post by Combat on 2007-06-25
Looks like the artifacts are where the window shadow should be. I believe this has something to do with your vid card. They say to stay away from ATI if you're running linux. You try turning the shadows off by first making sure you have the beryl manager installed. You should see the ruby icon on your system tray. right click on it, and click Emerald themes manager. A window should popup with a bunch of tabs and stuff on it. Click on the Edit themes tab. then click on the "Frame/Shadows" tab. Turn the shadows settings down until the artifacts disappear. Make sure to save your theme when you're done. Good luck bro.
