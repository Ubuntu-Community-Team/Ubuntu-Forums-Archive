### Post by gkestrel on 2008-09-13
Look in system menu and then choose network to find the network settings in xubuntu, you need to click unlock and enter your user password to be able to make any changes to the network settings. Your wireless network card may or may not be listed there depending on if the card has been recognised. If the card is not listed there, either search the forums or google for your make and model of wireless card, or else you will need to provide further details of the make and model before anyone will be able to try and help you to set it up.
