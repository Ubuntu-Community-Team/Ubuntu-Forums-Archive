### Post by cdonohue on 2009-05-12
Your problem should clear up if you change your font display settings.

1) Right click on the Desktop and select 'Change Desktop Background'
2) Select the 'Fonts' tab
3) Verify that the 'Subpixel Smoothing (LCDs)' radio button is checked under 'Rendering'
