### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-05-04
Two options - either press Ctl+Alt+T, the new default keybinding to launch a terminal, or hit Super for the Dash application search and start typing (it appeared for me at T-E-R.)

For a road map or cheat sheet, as it were, I'd recommend [_The Power User's Guide to Unity_](http://castrojo.tumblr.com/post/4795149014/the-power-users-guide-to-unity). Some fairly useful tips and tricks in there. = )
