### Post by abdulmajid on 2011-10-28
I am not sure whether it works with your phone Nokia 5233. It worked for me, with Nokia 6680. :D

[LIST]
[*]Connect the phone to your computer using a data cable. Now, you should see a new mobile broadband connection in network settings (click on network settings icon in the gnome-panel).

[*]Click on the new broadband connection in the network settings. You will have a small window opened on your desktop.

[*]Click 'Forward', select your country and your mobile service provider and your data plan. Finally apply the settings and start using the internet.
[/LIST]
Note: Choose the Access point name and your data plan carefully. otherwise it will eat-up all your money. :D

Good luck
