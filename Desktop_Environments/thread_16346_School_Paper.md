### Post by El Guapo on 2005-02-20
Ouch.

If you don't have too much formatting on the paper you can save the paper as a rich text file (.rtf) and then open that in MS Word at school.  You could try Open Office and save it as a MS Word file (.doc) but you will have to play with the formatting when you open it in word.  Open Office does a decent job from transferring between Word and itself, but it is still a little buggy.

Hope this helps.
