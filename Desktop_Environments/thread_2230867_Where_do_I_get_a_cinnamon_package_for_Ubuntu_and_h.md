### Post by gazBoston on 2014-06-21
See post #4 in [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2222794](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2222794) . The poster points at a PPA containing new stable Cinnamon and Nemo packages. They were uploaded by Gwendal LeBihan, the maintainer of the Cinnamon-nightly-build PPA and former maintainer of the now-absent Cinnamon-stable PPA.

I use Cinnamon and like it. But something in the Cinnamon installation caused Unity to work poorly, not that Unity is so important once you have Cinnamon :)

List of PPAs I have found which contain current (uploaded since June 1, 2014) Cinnamon/Nemo packages. I use the ~kranich PPA:
[https://launchpad.net/~kranich/+archive/cubuntu](https://launchpad.net/~kranich/+archive/cubuntu)
[https://launchpad.net/~tsvetko.tsvetkov/+archive/cinnamon](https://launchpad.net/~tsvetko.tsvetkov/+archive/cinnamon)
[https://launchpad.net/~lestcape/+archive/cinnamon](https://launchpad.net/~lestcape/+archive/cinnamon)
