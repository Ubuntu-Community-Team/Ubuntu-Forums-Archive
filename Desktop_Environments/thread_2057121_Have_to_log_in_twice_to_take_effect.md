### Post by 2F4U on 2012-09-14
I had a similar problem with openSUSE 12.1 KDE on a MacBook. It would always reject the first attempt to enter the password. I finally found that the problem was that during the first attempt to enter the password, the system always lost one or two characters from the password. When I started to type the password slower and verified that every keyboard stroke resulted in a bullet in the password dialogue box, the password would have been correct, even on the first attempt.
