### Post by again? on 2017-12-20
Never used Cinnamon.
If you highlight text and then right click and copy or hit ctl+c, that text now becomes both your primary(middle click) and clipboard(ctrl+v) selection.
If you now simply highlight some other text it will replace the primary selection but the clipboard selection should not change.

Most clipboard managers allow you to choose which selections to manage.
