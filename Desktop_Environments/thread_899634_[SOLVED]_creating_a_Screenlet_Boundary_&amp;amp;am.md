### Post by bobus007 on 2008-08-24
To make sure that the side bar is always visible and windows dont maximize over it.
go to the sidebar properties by right clicking on it. On the sidebar tab select "Right_reserve" from the "select Alignment" drop down menu.

To have the screenlets above the sidebar, just select "Keep above" in there properties then dock them to the sidebar.
