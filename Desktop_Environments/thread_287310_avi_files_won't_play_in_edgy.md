### Post by whatintheworldisthat on 2006-10-29
[http://getautomatix.com/wiki/index.php?title=Installation](http://getautomatix.com/wiki/index.php?title=Installation)

Go to the section about installing automatix2 on edgy and follow the instructions. Once installed you can install codecs and media players such as VLC that will play your avi files, mplayer too.

You can install lots of other goodies with automatix easily such as gaim, ff flash plugins, nautilus scripts, beagle, lots more.
