### Post by eggdeng on 2008-08-20
Quick workaround.
Right click on the panel, choose Add to Panel, Character Palette. This gives you access to several sets of non standard characters including the pipe symbol '|', which I assume is the one you are missing. You can copy characters to any app by clicking on the character and pasting it into the app.
To try to get the key to work, you could poke around in Preferences -> Keyboard -> Layout -> Keyboard model. Logout & in for changes to take effect.
