### Post by doclivingston on 2005-07-01
Assuming that by "shade" you mean what old MacOS did, folding up the window so it is just the titlebar, you can change it. If you go into "Window Preferences" (gnome-window-properties) you can change the behaviour for double-clicking on a window's title bar from "maximise" to "roll-up".
