### Post by shakin on 2006-04-06
ATI has some setup program you run and at one part it asks you how you want your dual monitors setup. Big Desktop mode lets you drag windows between monitors and Dual Head mode seperates the monitors with their own menus and programs.

NVidia probably has something similar.
