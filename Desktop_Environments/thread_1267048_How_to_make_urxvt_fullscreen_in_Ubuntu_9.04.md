### Post by urukrama on 2009-10-10
You can add a URxvt.geometry: variable to your .Xdefaults file, to make urxvt start with a particular size. Have a look at the [urxvt manpage]("http://linux.die.net/man/7/urxvt") for examples of the exact syntax.

You can also use [devilspie]("http://burtonini.com/blog/computers/devilspie/").
