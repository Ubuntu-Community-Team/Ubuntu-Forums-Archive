### Post by dekoi on 2005-05-31
You *can* install .deb's; however, Debian packages aren't fully supported and (K)ubuntu doesn't make guarantees on those, esp. in the unlikely event that it crashes your system.

It will also help us to help you if you if you tell us what you're looking to install. Have you opened up your expanded repositories?
