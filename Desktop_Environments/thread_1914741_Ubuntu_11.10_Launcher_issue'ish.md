### Post by bogan on 2012-01-25
Hi!,** BJCV86,**

If Clementine is a normal GUI application, when it is running, its Launcher Icon should appear in the Unity LaunchPad.

 [You may have to hold the Super key [Windows Logo] down, or  drag the Clementine Window to one side, so the LaunchPad is visible].

Right Click on the Icon and select 'Keep in Launcher'. - That's it.
