### Post by mcduck on 2011-10-15
There aren't any screen savers in Gnome 3. You can adjust display sleep  and screen locking in the power options.

(If you really want pretty pictures when nobody is around to look at them instead of saving your display and power, you could install the xscreensaver: [http://www.liberiangeek.net/2011/10/enable-screensavers-in-ubuntu-11-10-oneiric-ocelot/]("http://www.liberiangeek.net/2011/10/enable-screensavers-in-ubuntu-11-10-oneiric-ocelot/"))
