### Post by WeEatVista on 2008-06-26
This probably is no help to you whatsoever, and you probably know this, but when you select 'auto hide' under panel config your panel disappears until you move your mouse directly to where your panel is. In this case move your mouse to the bottom of the screen, which makes the panel pop back up again.

This is just a little thing, the problem is probably more in-depth, but make sure this doesn't work first:)

Hope this helps,
