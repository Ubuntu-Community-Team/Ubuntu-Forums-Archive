 Apps can not cover bottom panel. So if you have 48 pixel wide panel along the whole bottom of your screen, apps will not use these 48 pixels  - even if the panel has only 1 item on it, or it is empty.

However, if you go to panel properties and uncheck "expand" box, then the panel will not stretch along the whole bottom of your screen - it will only be as long as necessary to fit all items in it. This way, the rest of screen bottom becomes availabe for applications.
