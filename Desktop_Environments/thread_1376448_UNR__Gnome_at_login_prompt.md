### Post by mister_playboy on 2010-01-09
UNR's meta package will overwrite your current GNOME settings and is not presented as an separate option to choose from at login.  There was a program in 9.04 that would let you switch between UNR's desktop and the normal one after GNOME has started, but it's not in 9.10, I hear.  Just as well, as something went wrong when I used it and my normal desktop became totally blank.

I highly recommend you back up your desktop configs before installing UNR, so you don't end up destroying them like I did.
