### Post by harnadem on 2005-11-09
I am in the process of trying out xfce4 on my older breezy system.  I was used to the "clear recent documents" command that is accessed under the Places tab on the gnome taskbar.  Is there something similar available for xfce?

---

### Post by harnadem on 2005-11-18
Solved!  Under one's /home directory there is a file called .recently-used.  Delete this and you delete any recently reviewed or viewed files.

- updated:  Actually, it appears that this will only clear Totem files.  It had no effect upon my OOo files.
