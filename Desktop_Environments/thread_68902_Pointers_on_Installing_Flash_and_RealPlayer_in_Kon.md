### Post by mlomker on 2005-09-26
I'm not sure if you are using Sun or Blackdown java.  I'm guessing that your symlinks are missing or broken.  The instructions for making the links in Blackdown are on the [aforementioned site]("http://kudos.berlios.de/kf/kf1.html#j2re"). Konqueror must use the links in /etc/alternatives.
