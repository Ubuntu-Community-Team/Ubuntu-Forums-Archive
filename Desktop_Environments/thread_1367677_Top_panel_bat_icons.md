### Post by Mahngiel on 2009-12-29
chances are that the notification area applet is on 'lock to panel' mode, and your clock setting is not.  right click on the area that looks like a handle, and ensure on the pop up that 'lock to panel' is deselected. do this for everything to the right of your clock.  you can then right-click 'move' and the things will go back into place. when you're done moving things around, reset them to 'lock to panel' mode and they shouldn't move again.
