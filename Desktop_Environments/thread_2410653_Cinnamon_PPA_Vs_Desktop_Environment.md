### Post by deadflowr on 2019-01-18
cinnamon is cinnamon but installing through the PPA will give a newer version.

Packages in the default repositories are locked down, so new features are not going to be making their way into it during the duration of the release.
(Or at least packages don't normally get feature updates unless the update is very critical; something not likely on desktop environments, really)
 
PPAs, on the other hand, can get updated with all the new trimmings whenever the PPA maintainers wants.
