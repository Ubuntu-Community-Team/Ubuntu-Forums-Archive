Well, the forums are usually filled with cries for help, mine included in the past.  But I have to say that Dapper team did a fantastic job! :)  I did a clean install (I previously used Breezy, but made the mistake of ading KDE to Gnome, couldn't remove all of KDE, so I wanted to clean eaverything up) and it worked flawlessly.
