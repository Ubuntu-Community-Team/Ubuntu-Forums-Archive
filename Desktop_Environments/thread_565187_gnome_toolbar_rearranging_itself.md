### Post by gamerteck on 2007-10-02
I had this problem, where X would start at a lower resolution, then reconfigure itself when it was in the gu; the screen would go from 640x480 to 1600x1200, messing up the icons in the process.  To fix it I had to edit my xorg and remove the lower resolutions I didn't use.
