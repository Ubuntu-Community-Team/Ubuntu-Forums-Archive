### Post by molnarandris on 2009-12-28
Ok, sorry, finally I found the solution.

In fact I had to use Wammu. I exported from Evolution my calendar in ics format (and purged old events: Actions>purge), and Wammu imports it with no problem. (Backup>import).

Andras
