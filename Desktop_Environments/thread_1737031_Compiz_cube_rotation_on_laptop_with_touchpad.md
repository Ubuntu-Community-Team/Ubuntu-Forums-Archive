### Post by papibe on 2011-04-22
It's been a long time since I've used the cube. It's kind of "finger gymnastics" to do it in a laptop with a touchpad.

I believe that you hold Ctrl and Alt, and then you keep pressed the left mouse button. As you move the mouse the cube rotates.

I hope it helps.

### Post by yossell on 2011-04-23
On my laptop, placing the cursor on the background, simultaneously pressing both the left and right mouse keys enables the cube, then - still holding - sliding a  finger around the touchpad moves the cube around.
