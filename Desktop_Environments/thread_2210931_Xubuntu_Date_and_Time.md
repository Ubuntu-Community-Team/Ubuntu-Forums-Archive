### Post by Toz on 2014-03-13
For informational purposes, you can also make the same change to the Date/Time plugin by setting Layout->Format to read "Date Only" and the setting Date->Format to "Custom" with a value something like "%a %b %d, %l:%M%P". Look at the date man page for what each of the format sequences mean.
