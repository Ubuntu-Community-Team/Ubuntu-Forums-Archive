When he double-clicks a .DOC document on the Desktop, the document opens as expected, but always as a new and untitled document.  All the text previously saved is still there, but the information bar says it's "Untitled".

### Post by prshah on 2008-09-09
> **dominiquec said:**
> 
When he double-clicks a .DOC document on the Desktop, the document opens as expected, but always as a new and untitled document.  All the text previously saved is still there, but the information bar says it's "Untitled".


He may have saved it as a template. In this case, double clicking it will open a new document with the contents of the template. Opening the template through File-Open will open it as a template.

The extension is not important to identify it as a template.

What you should now do is "Save As" the document, and this time, make sure the "Type" is "ODF Text Document" or "Microsoft Word 97/2000/XP document"
