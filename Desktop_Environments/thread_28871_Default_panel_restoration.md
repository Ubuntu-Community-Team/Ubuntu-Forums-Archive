### Post by spencer on 2005-04-22
Hello,

There may be another way to do this but If you have either the top or bottom panel left, you can right click on the panel and from the menu select "New Panel" option. You can adjust the panel's various settings by right clicking on the new panel you have just created and select properties. You can place it wherever you like. After you place the panel where you want, Right click on the new panel and select "Add To Panel", there you can put whatever options you want. 
I hope this works for you. 

-spencer
