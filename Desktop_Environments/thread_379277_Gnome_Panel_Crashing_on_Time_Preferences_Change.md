### Post by LoganK on 2007-03-08
I adjusted the time preferences for my gnome panel to display as "UNIX" format, and as soon as I clicked "Apply" the gnome panel crashed, started back up again, crashed, etc. It now continuously crashes anytime I'm logged into the machine; the failsafe session doesn't work either. Is there anyway I can "reset" the preferences for the time applet of the panel in order to get the panel back?

---

### Post by LoganK on 2007-03-08
I solved it! Thanks to anyone who read my post. Just in case anyone else is wondering, I installed gconf-editor, ran it, and adjusted the format for the time applet (it took me a while to find out which one it was) back to 12-hour.
