### Post by ayoli on 2007-08-14
No, you can't have the menu in title bar but it's possible to have it on a top panel. There is a patch for gtk with an applet which do that check out [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=241868") for that.
In KDE there is an option in the kontrol center to do that.
