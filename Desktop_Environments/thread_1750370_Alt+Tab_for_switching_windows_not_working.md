### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-10-21
rajun198, if you're having problems with Alt+Tab in Gnome 2.x, you probably just need to change your Compiz settings. Alt+Tab can be any of four or five plugins in Compiz, depending on how you have it configured, and unless something is broken, you couldn't have disabled it without changing those settings in compizconfig-settings-manager.
