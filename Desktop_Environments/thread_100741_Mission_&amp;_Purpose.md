### Post by Coach on 2005-12-08
The clamor surrounding “Automatix” has drawn me from the woodwork.

I have read the “commitments” that Ubuntu has made to its users.  It is very impressive.  

I coach my clients that if their mission and purpose (M&P) has anything to do with money and/or power for themselves, that they will ultimately lose.  There is nothing in any of Ubuntu’s commitments about money or power.

Most probably, Bill Gates’ original M&P (if he had one) was not “to make a fortune.”  He has made a fortune, perhaps because money and power were not part of his original purpose.  In any case, Microsoft has become “the” OS software.

What is the mission and purpose of ubuntuforums.org, and of those who contribute herein?  I see nothing in the “guidelines” about mission & purpose.  There is not an “About Us” page.    

Where is the rudder that steers this ship?  And, why are these oarsmen paddling in opposite directions?

How can Ubuntu become “THE” OS, when its proponents have either no, or diverse, purpose(s)?
