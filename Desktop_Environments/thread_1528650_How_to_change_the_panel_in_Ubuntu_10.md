### Post by kerry_s on 2010-07-11
the panel is locked in 10.04.
what we do is log out & select "gnome" in the session menu.
in gnome you need to add "netbook-launcher" & "maximus" to the startup.
delete the bottom panel, on the top panel remove the menu bar, add the go home applet & window picker applet.

you should now have the netbook look again & be able to do what ever you want panel wise.

i'm just now doing mine, i installed about 10 min a go.
