### Post by dak-AD on 2007-01-11
after disabling the shortcut in keyboard shortcuts, go to system > preferences > keyboard > layout.

click 'add', and add a random keyboard layout (say, algerian).

now, remove it, and you should have your spacebar back; tho, if you look in the next tab ('layout options') all those settings will have gone back to default i'm afraid.

was the shortcut one of the 'sound' shortcuts (ie, pause, skip forward etc)?
