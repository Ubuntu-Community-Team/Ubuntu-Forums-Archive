### Post by Malagise on 2006-08-12
Impressed by the kindness and promptness of you folks on this forum, I have another question for you.

When I move my mouse cursor onto a button or application tab on the Panel, a (useful) large popup appears with information about it. Now, the first time I move onto the panel, there is a kind of animation, whereby the popup seems to materialize from left to right rather than just appearing istantaneously. I find that annoying - it makes me waste time waiting for the animation to complete! How can I remove the effect?

I tried browsing through the Control Center, but could not find an answer. Thanks in advance!

---

### Post by orb9220 on 2006-08-12
gconf-editor under apps>panel>default_setup>global>enable_animations uncheck it.
