### Post by ericesque on 2006-07-23
shawn,

You may or maynot already know this, but there is a *nix philosophy that each app should have one specific purpose.  Hence, you will not find many apps that will play music and play video.  As more windows users make their way over to linux, the demand for an all-in-one media app will surely increase.  This gives hope to the idea that one day there will be a high quality app such as you are seeking.

In the mean time, I would suggest taking a look at Listen for your music needs and perhaps mplayer for video needs.  I also like Democracy Player for video podcasts.
