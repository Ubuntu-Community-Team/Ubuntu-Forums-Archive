### Post by bonzodog on 2006-10-02
To my knowledge, there is no GUI for pypanel at the moment, but I find the local pypanelrc file is very self explanatory, and indeed have customised mine to the way I want it to look. It's mostly just fonts and colors, and I always use a little color map prog to work out hex codes for colors I want.

As for replacing the gnome panel, I have no idea how you would do that. It sounds like you might benefit from a lighter Wm/DE env, I now use Openbox, which can pick up gtk themes.
