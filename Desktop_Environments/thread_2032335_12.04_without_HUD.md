### Post by Version Dependency on 2012-07-23
No need to disable anything.  It only appears when you hit the ALT key by itself.  In fact, I would bet that most people running 12.04 have never activated the HUD, or even know it's there.  But if you are worried that you might hit the ALT key by accident and bring up the HUD, you can go to System Settings > Keyboard > Shortcuts > Key to show HUD and set some weird key combination that you know you will never accidentally hit.
