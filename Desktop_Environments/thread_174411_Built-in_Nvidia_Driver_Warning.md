### Post by Pcmikepl on 2006-05-11
In ubuntu 5.10 do not run any modern fan-less nVidia cards on the built-in Linux driver, for they heat up fairly fast and might damage something (?). Instead use the binary drivers from nVidia, which can be installed with Automatix.
