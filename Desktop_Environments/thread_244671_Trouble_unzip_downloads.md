### Post by NetworkGuy on 2006-08-26
Click on accessories ---> Menu Editor

Once that comes up, click on accessories and add a checkmark (by clicking on) to Archive Manager.

Close the menu editor.

Click on Accessories  -- >Archive Manager

Now you can unzip anything you may have gotten now and in the future.
