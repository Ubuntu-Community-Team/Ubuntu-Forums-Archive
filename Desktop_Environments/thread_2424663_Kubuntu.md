### Post by Dennis N on 2019-08-12
Your login options should read:

Ubuntu
Ubuntu on Wayland
Plasma

(It's Wayland, not Maryland)
The first two are the display servers - choosing Ubuntu gives you Ubutu Gnome using Xorg, the 2nd gives you Ubuntu Gnome using Wayland display server. Plasma is for KDE Desktop which you must have installed. You can't remove the two display server options. Once you choose one, it stays that way until you switch again. Getting rid of KDE once installed would be very difficult. Many packages and supporting libraries.
