### Post by Barriehie on 2009-11-30
So I've been generating my own logfiles from scripts and then viewing them with emacs; it works but would be nice if they're all in one place!  Was checking out **gconf-editor** and found under [color="green"]*apps > gnome-system-log*[/color] and you can can add your own ***pathname/filename*** to the key value and then your logfiles will show up when you go to view the system logfiles.  Pretty neat!
