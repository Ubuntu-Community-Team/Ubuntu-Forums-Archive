### Post by mcduck on 2007-03-13

> Thank you, I will be sure to look that over. Anyone have the answer to my other question about the panels and rendering them like themes?

Panels have actually nothing to do with Beryl/Compiz. How they look depends on your GTK theme, but most themes don't really do anything special with panels. 

The best way to change how they look is to use a background picture for the panels (you can just drag & drop any pic to the panel).
