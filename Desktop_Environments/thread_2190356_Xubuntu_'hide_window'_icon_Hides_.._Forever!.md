### Post by Frogs Hair on 2013-11-28
Hello and Welcome 

See if the window buttons applet is installed by right clicking the panel and navigating to panel >  panel preferences > items.  To add items  right click the panel and navigate to panel > add new items. If the window menu is installed instead of buttons a small  triangle will appear in the panel with a drop-down menu and display open applications and the workspace they are on. I am using XFCE and not Xubuntu so it may differ.
