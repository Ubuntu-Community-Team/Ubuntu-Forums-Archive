### Post by coldraven on 2015-02-25
In the top right corner of your screen there should be a little "cog wheel" icon. Click that and the third option is "System Settings". 
In System Settings choose Brightness and Lock  then uncheck "Dim screen to save power" and set "Turn off screen..." to Never.

You might also want to look at the Power settings that are found in the System settings panel. I set mine like this:
**When plugged in**
Don't suspend
Do nothing

**On battery power**
Don't suspend
Power off
Suspend

You can also get to the System Settings panel (or any thing else) by opening the Dash.
 Press the Windows key (also known as the Super key) and start typing the letters s y s, you should then see it's icon appear.
Happy Ubuntuing :)
