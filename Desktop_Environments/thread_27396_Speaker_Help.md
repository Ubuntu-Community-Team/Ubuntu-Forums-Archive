### Post by erkki70 on 2005-04-16
Hi,
Have you checked the settings of your Volume Control applet in the Gnome Menu bar?
(Should be on the top right near date and time in case you didn't change anything.
If not there, Right Click on the Menu bar and select Add to... then select Volume Control.)

Right Click and select Open Volume Control then select the Speaker and set the desired volume.

Hope this helps.

Cheers,
Erkki
