### Post by orb9220 on 2006-07-07
Well in the torrent world the main thing is not to saturate your bandwith.

If you are downing at 100KB total and your upload is also 100 or greater
you may be saturated.

The work around or known as the sweet spot is to limit your upload rate to about 80% of your download total. change to 80KB at first then lower abit until you see an improvement.

This works alot but not always. Also you need to find out what your connection supports. So to check your connection with torrent program closed goto [http://www.ookla.com/speedtest/](http://www.ookla.com/speedtest/) . Mine just showed 737 kbs that's 73.7 Kbs so I want to adjust my upload to around 80% of that value.

Hope that helps. :-D
