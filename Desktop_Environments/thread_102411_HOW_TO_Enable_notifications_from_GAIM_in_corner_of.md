### Post by anil_robo on 2005-12-12
First, install gaim-guifications.
```

sudo apt-get install gaim-guifications

``` 
Now start GAIM, and go to Tools--> Preferences-->Plugins
Enable guifications by placing a checkmark in front of it.
Guifications will now be visible just below the plugins menu in GAIM preferences. Click on it, and configure it to your liking!

Done!

Now whenever a user comes online/goes offline, you'll see a notification in one of your screen corners just as in Yahoo messenger or MSN messenger!
