### Post by vanadium on 2013-01-15
I did try that as well yesterday, and saw the same, however only when "Reveal location" is set to "Top left corner" in System settings - Appearance, Behavior tab. It can be helped by setting the reveal sensitivity high, but even then, you have to throw the mouse cursor hard in the top left corner to reveal the launcher (actually to the top then fast to the left will do). Gentle movements towards the top left corner will not do.

I guess you could tweak the behaviour in more detail with ccsm.
