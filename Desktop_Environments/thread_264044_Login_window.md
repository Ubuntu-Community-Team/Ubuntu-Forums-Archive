### Post by aysiu on 2006-09-24
You can get themes [here](http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=150&PHPSESSID=c2fc42521e8351459bb432cc0ca073e7).

Download them and do not extract them. Leave them as .tar.gz files.

Go to System > Administration > Login Window and then Add Theme and find your file. Make sure the black dot next to the theme is filled in.
