### Post by beameup on 2006-06-17
Best way i've found to partition for a dual boot system is to boot with a LiveCd (Knoppix, Ubuntu, SystemRescueDisc, etc) and use QT_Parted or GParted to partition the drive first.
Then just resize and create another partition for your Linux install. Then when you go through the install you can tell it to just "take over that partition" and let it do it's thing.
