### Post by MorrisseyJ on 2012-02-23
Click the wireless icon (top right menu bar)

Go to 'network settings'

click 'Wireless' (on the left hand side)

click 'configure' (bottom right)

un-check the 'connect automatically' box towards the top of the dialogue
