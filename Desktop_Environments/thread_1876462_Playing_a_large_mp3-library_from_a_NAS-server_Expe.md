### Post by mister_p_1998 on 2011-11-07
One word really. Vortexbox ( [http://vortexbox.org/](http://vortexbox.org/)) I've been running it at work for over a year, boots up on a timer, all my albums on it, I can remote into the albums menu via a browser, set it to play random artists, random tracks and it plays all day. It shuts down on a cron job at hometime, then starts again in the morning on a bios timer. I think its Fedora based (Yum) but I had no trouble getting to grips with it.
