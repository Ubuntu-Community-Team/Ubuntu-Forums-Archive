### Post by lippel on 2006-08-11
To copy over the feed list, copy ~/.kde/share/apps/akregator/data/feeds.opml in the respective directory on the other machine. Or use File->Export from the menu. To copy the article archive, you have to copy the content of the directory ~/.kde/share/apps/akregator/Archive.
