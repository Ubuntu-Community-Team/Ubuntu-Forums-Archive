### Post by SGC on 2005-06-10
I'm not sure about kicker, but to get the list of last used applications in kmenu:
right click on the taskbar and choose "Configure Panel" then go to the "Menus" tab. 

Under "QuickStart menu items" choose "Show applications most recently used" and then choose the maximum number of entries.

---

### Post by GeneralZod on 2005-06-10
You can re-add them to the Kicker by right-clicking on the Kicker, selecting Add To Panel->Special Button->Recent Documents.
