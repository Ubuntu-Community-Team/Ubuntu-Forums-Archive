### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-06-02
Well, are you trying to use the Classic desktop or the default (Unity)? If you log out and log back into Classic by selecting it from the menu under the login window after you select your name, you'll presumably have the Gnome Panel. Otherwise, the problems would seem to go well beyond Compiz configuration.

If you're trying to use Unity, try [_this troubleshooting guide_]("http://ubuntu4beginners.blogspot.com/2011/04/missing-top-and-side-panels-in-unity.html").
