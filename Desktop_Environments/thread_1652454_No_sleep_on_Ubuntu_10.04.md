As for what I've seen so far, to suspend and to hibernate are two of the most tricky things in gnu linux. Highly hardware dependent. If suspend doesn't work out of the box for you, you may save yourself a lot of headhaches considering whether you can have a happy life anyway without that or not ;)

Regarding hibernation, only one thought: Have you at least as much swap space as your ram? It's needed for hibernation.
