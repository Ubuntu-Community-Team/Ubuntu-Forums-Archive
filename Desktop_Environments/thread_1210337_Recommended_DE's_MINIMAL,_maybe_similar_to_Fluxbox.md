### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2009-07-11
Openbox. One ready-made for you is the Ubuntu-based distro called [Crunchbang Linux]("http://crunchbanglinux.org"). It is stark, minimalist, but enormously functional. Right-click menus and all.

The most minimal true desktop environment yet is called [LXDE]("http://lxde.org"). Very simple, ultra-lightweight. But it sounds like you're looking for more minimal than *any* desktop environment. Nevertheless for those who insist on having one (for kids, for example), LXDE is as lightweight as it gets, yet pretty and functional as well as minimal and simple.

-Robin
