### Post by Uta on 2005-06-15
I am using the lastest KDE desktop/Kubuntu of course and all I did was plug my iPod (1st generation) in via the firewire port and opened juK and navigated to the folder on the iPod that contained my mp3s, played my tunes, even got the lyrics downloaded per song in this great app.
I think the reason this was so seamless for me was I also have Linux running on my iPod and probably the folder section I was browsing for my mp3 files were in the Linux section (not sure) or maybe it just read them off the Mac section? It was a great adventure and love the fact that you can fetch lyrics to the songs also!
