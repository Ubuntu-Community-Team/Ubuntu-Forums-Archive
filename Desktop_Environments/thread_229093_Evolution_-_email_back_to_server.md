### Post by dtfinch on 2006-08-04
I really don't use Evolution, but try dragging the important emails to your outbox, then clicking send and receive. I didn't really expect it to work, but I tried it with one email and it seemed to work, forwarding it without modification. **Be certain that every email you forward like this is addressed directly to you, and nobody else.** If, for example, you resend emails you received from a public mailing list, those emails might be reposted to the entire list.

Yahoo supported imap for a brief moment in June, which would have probably allowed direct uploading, but I hear they turned it off it very soon after.
