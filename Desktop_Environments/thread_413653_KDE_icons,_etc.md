### Post by ComplexNumber on 2007-04-19
> **Weaseal said:**
> How do I make the icons on the KDE desktop smaller?  Also, how do I make it so that I have to double click to open them?
you'll be glad to know that there is a straightforward way to do both.
i haven't got kde installed at the moment, so i'm going from memory (if i'm wrong, someone should come along and correct me).

to make them smaller, go to the kde control centre(if its not in your menu, type in 'kcontrol' on the commandline). then go to 'appearance'(should be the first icon on the left hand side), then icons. click the 2nd tab. it should give you different sizes for the icons for you to select.

again, go to kcontrol. to make the mouse double click, select 'peripherals(or devices) from the icons on the left hand side, then mouse.
