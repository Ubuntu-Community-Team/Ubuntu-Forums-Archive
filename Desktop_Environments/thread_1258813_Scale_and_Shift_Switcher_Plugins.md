### Post by blur xc on 2009-09-05
Those are two of my favorite compiz plugins.  In my opinion, they both should be enabled by default in ubuntu.  

I've got my scale plugin configured to show all windows on all workspaces with a right click on the far right edge of the screen, to show all windows on the current workspace with a click on the bottom right corner of the screen, and all windows related to the current open app by right clicking on the top right corner of the screen.

My only complaint with both of these plugins, is that they boht don't show minimized applications.  That could get annoying, if you have a lot of windows open on several desktops, and you know you have something open, but don't know where.  

And the Scale plugin could use one enhancement (maybe it does it already, and I just don't know it).  It would be very helpful to have the window title displayed on top of each window.  Sometimes you might have several nautilus windows open, for example, that all shrunk down look the same, but are open to different folders.  At that point, it's a squint and guess as to which one is the one you want.
