### Post by aysiu on 2009-11-12
Gnome themes have two components: the GTK theme (window controls) and the Metacity theme (window manager).

If you really want to make the theme from scratch, these may help you:
[http://live.gnome.org/GnomeArt/Tutorials/GtkThemes](http://live.gnome.org/GnomeArt/Tutorials/GtkThemes)
[http://developer.gnome.org/doc/tutorials/metacity/metacity-themes.html](http://developer.gnome.org/doc/tutorials/metacity/metacity-themes.html)

Otherwise, it's best to take an existing theme with a non-restrictive license and just modify it.
