### Post by dncrews on 2010-10-28
This seems to happen to me a lot, though I'm not sure why. Here's a quick and easy fix, though:

Right-click on the panel and choose Properties.
Under Orientation, change it to left or right (if you're using it on top or bottom) or top or bottom (if you're using it on left or right). Then just change it back to whatever you had it at before, and your panel will be all better.

It's kind of a "pane" that I have to do it, but it's better than restarting and hoping for the best.
