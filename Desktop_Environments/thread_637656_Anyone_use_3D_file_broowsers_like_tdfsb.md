### Post by Krydahl on 2007-12-11
I just had a play with it, it worked ok for me - but I'm running compiz already. You need a graphics card capable of 3d rendering to run it, so you'll probably need the restricted drivers installed.

tdfsb itself was an entertaining diversion, but not actually very useful, I thought. It also won't give you any insight into what it's like to have compiz running on your machine - it's a completely different thing.

A couple of hints if you're going to try it:

The window it started in was rather small and I couldn't see what was going on at first. "F" gives you a fullscreen mode.

"H" is help - it gives you a list of the other keyboard shortcuts.

"R" will release your mouse back to your control - this means you can resize the window.

Esc to quit.
