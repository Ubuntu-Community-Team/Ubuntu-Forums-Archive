### Post by matthew on 2005-10-01
For the toolbar: right click on the top or bottom of your screen, wherever you want the toolbar. From the menu, choose New Panel. Once the panel is created, right click on it again to Add To Panel the things you want from the dialog box that will come up.

For the root problem: type "sudo su" and then respond with your password. You should then be root. See here for more info: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RootSudo]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RootSudo")
