### Post by iamhugeinjapan on 2007-03-04
This is most likely Beryl messing it up.

Look in your Beryl Settings Manager, there is a "Level of focus stealing prevention" drop down list in the General Options.  Set it to 'None'

---

### Post by Osiris23343 on 2007-03-04
thanks, it was set on extreme, now its working better.
