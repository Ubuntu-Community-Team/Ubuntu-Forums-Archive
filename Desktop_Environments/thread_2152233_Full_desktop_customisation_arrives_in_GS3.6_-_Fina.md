### Post by Baldrick_NZ on 2013-06-07
So excited I just have to share...

[http://www.webupd8.org/2013/06/highly-configurable-elegance-colors.html]("http://www.webupd8.org/2013/06/highly-configurable-elegance-colors.html")

**Elegance Colors** now has it's own preference manager, which changes pretty much anything within the Gnome Shell desktop to the way **YOU** want it - just like old school. ;-)

For example, there are preset options which you can choose to change the panel, activities screen, pop-up boxes and logout/shutdown boxes, along with heaps of other tweaks. Or, you can pretty much customise the whole desktop yourself, easily!

I've added pics of the changes I've made below. Most are subtle, but to my mind looks much better.

Have a play, you might be as pleasantly surprised as I am!

Cheers,
Baldrick.
