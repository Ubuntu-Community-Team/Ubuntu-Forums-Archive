### Post by draeath on 2008-10-06
Found it! For some reason, this is handled by the file manager now.

Go to your home folder, then under the Edit menu go to Preferences.

Now look in the media tab.

It's all configurable, and there's even a magical "don't do ANYTHING EVER" checkbox!
