### Post by Aleksandersen on 2007-01-29
Right click on a text area in a form (where you normally see spelling suggestions), choose **Languages >** Choose the correct language from the menu or choose to add a new dictionary.

Alternatively you can use [SpellCheck.net](http://spellcheck.net/) until you figure out what is wrong (if the method provided above did not work).
