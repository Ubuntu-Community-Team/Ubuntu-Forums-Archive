### Post by Tony3286 on 2007-12-09
If your using Synaptics manager's present version 4.1 of Mail notification - what you can do is click on properties - Status Icon and choose under "click action" - **launch the mail reader**. Now if you get a notification of an email - if you click on it, it will bring up your present email program.

You first must have your email program as your default - Under System - preferences - preferred applications - under mail reader choose your email client.

Now when Mail notifier pop appears, just click on it and your email will open up

Hope that helps !  :)
