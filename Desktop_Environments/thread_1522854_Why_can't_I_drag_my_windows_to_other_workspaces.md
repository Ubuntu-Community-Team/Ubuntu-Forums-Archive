### Post by JakeFrederix on 2010-07-02
Install compiz (enabling advanced effects).
Then open the settings manager (System > CompizConfig Settings Manager),
Enable desktop wall (tick the box next to it),
and specifically in the settings of desktop wall (click the option), go to the tab 'Edge Flipping'
Enable 'Edge Flip Move' by ticking the box.
