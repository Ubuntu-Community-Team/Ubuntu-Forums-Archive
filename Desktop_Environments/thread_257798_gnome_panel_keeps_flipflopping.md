### Post by orb9220 on 2006-09-15
Does your session manger have save sessions on exit checked?

This is required I think? to memorize the panels position also if you have autoexpand on and like taskbar applet at edge then yes the panel is going to push to make room.

Move any applets like taskbar from being on the edge of screen. Or turn  off autoexpand.

Panels properties are accessed from right-click edge of panel.
