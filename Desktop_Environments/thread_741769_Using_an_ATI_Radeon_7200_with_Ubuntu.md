### Post by talsemgeest on 2008-04-01
Ok I'm guessing you are running Ubuntu 7.10 Gutsy.

Just go to System>Administration>Screens and Graphics.

Just type in your password and change the resolution to what you want.

If you can't get the resolution that you want, make sure your monitor is configured correctly. For example, I have a Widescreen LCD panel with a resolution of 1440x900, so I have selected LCD panel 1440x900 and checked widescreen.

Hope this helps :)
