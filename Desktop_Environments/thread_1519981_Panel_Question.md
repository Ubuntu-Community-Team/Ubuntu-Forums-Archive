### Post by aphatak on 2010-06-28
I am not sure I understand a clear top panel.  If you do not need the icons in the top panel, you can right-click each and then click 'remove from panel', if that's what you mean.

I have never liked two panels, one at the top and one at the bottom.  I add icons that normally are in the bottom panel to the top panel, and then remove the bottom panel altogether.

You might want to set either panel (or both) to disappear, and re-appear only when you move your cursor to the top- or bottom edge of the screen,  Right-click the panel, click Properties, and then check Autohide.

If by 'clear' you mean tha background (wallpaper) shows through, right-click the panel, click Properties, select the Background tab, select the Solid Color radio button, and drag the Style all the way to the left (transparent).
