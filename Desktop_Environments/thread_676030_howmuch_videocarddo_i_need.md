### Post by sandr- on 2008-01-24
Depends on what you use the laptop for. If you plan for mobile gaming, go for more gpu .. and look for wine compatibility.
To get the 'best' laptop I only advise people to go for cpu and ram. The rest is optinal: for extra diskspace, buy some 500~700GB external drive.
Graphics accelerator ... again ... are you planning on gaming on that machine? I use an onboard intel chipset, works as a charm ( 1680x1050 resolution ) and I have most compiz effects enabled. Be sure that the first week you use compiz you'll enable everything just because it can, but after that just leave on the bare minimum.
