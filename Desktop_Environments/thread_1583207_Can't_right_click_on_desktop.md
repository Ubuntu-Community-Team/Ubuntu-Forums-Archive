### Post by Sir Rodness on 2010-09-27
I was originally attempting to reply back to the original post with the same title. But settings would not allow me to do so. Anyway....

I was also having trouble with my right clicking on my desktop, but I could right click everywhere else. After thinking over some of the changes I've made recently I discovered what happened.

In Ubuntu tweak I unchecked "Show Desktop Icons" found in the "Desktop Icon Settings" section.( I like keeping my desktop pretty clear). I rechecked that and just unchecked what I didn't want to see on my desktop and my right click returned. Not sure what the "Show Desktop Icons" is tied into, but that fixed my right click issue. Hope it helps anyone else out there having the same issue.
