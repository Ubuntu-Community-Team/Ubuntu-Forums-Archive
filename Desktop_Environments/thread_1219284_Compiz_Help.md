### Post by iWuzHere on 2009-07-24
okay so i figured out why it wasn't working,

in jaunty they have disabled DRI on my chipset (i865G) under bug#317347

to solve this i had to change my driver to the one found here;
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ReinhardTartler/X/RevertingIntelDriverTo2.4](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ReinhardTartler/X/RevertingIntelDriverTo2.4)
