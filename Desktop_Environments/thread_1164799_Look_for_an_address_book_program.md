### Post by tacantara on 2009-05-20
In the Add/Remove Applications tool, enter "address book" as your search criteria (make sure that All is highlighted in the left pane).  This will bring up all packages that deal with address books.  I saw at least four address book programs written for the GNOME DE (Rubrica, Osmo, Contacts, and Dlume).  Hopefully one of those will do the trick for you.
