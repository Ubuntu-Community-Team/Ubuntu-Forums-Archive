### Post by ajmorris on 2009-01-07
hi there,
im assuming that proper fullscreen is not what you want (i.e non-windowed)
To get the window all the way to the edge, try running a virtual desktop setting in winecfg to the full resolution of your monitor, then in the WCIII options, choose fullscreen, not windowed. This will run WCIII at fullscreen inside the window of your virtual desktop.


AJ
