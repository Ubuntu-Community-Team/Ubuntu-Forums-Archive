### Post by Brandon Williams on 2009-08-22
It can be difficult to get new items to show up the panel, because there's nowhere to right-click in order to select a new item to add. What I usually do is to right-click on the go-home applet, uncheck 'lock to panel', right click go-home again, and then move it to the right of the window-picket applet. This will shorten the window-picker, leaving plenty of blank space in which to right-click so that you can add another item. After adding then new item, then rearrange everything and lock it back in place.
