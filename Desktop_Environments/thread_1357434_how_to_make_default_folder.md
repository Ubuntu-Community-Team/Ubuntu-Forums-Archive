### Post by Ylon on 2009-12-17
We're talking about Firefox (the default browser delivered with Ubuntu) then.

in Firefox:
Edit > Preferences > [Main] - Download, Save files to: [choose your folder] > Ok.

[img]http://www.askdavetaylor.com/3-blog-pics/firefox-preferences-main.png[/img]


With other browser the procedure may be different.
