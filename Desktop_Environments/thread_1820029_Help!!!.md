### Post by JRV on 2011-08-07
You can not put the applications folder on the top panel, but you can put it on the launcher bar.

Right click on the desktop.
Select "Create Launcher"
Type: Location
Name: Applications
Command: /usr/share/applications

Move it to your home directory, then drag it to the launcher bar.
