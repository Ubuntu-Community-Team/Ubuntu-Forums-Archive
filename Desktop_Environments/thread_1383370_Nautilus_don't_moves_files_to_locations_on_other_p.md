### Post by John Bean on 2010-01-17
Either use the middle mouse button (instead of the left one) or press and hold the alt key during the drag and drop.

Either way you'll get a popup menu asking you what you want to do when you release the mouse button.

### Post by John Bean on 2010-01-18
Select the files you want to delete then hold down the shift key and press the delete key. This will delete the files permanently rather than moving them to trash.

Edit: on reading your question again I see you want to move to trash and not delete. If so I don't understand why you can't do this, it should be the default behaviour.
