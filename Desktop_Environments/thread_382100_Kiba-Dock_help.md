### Post by gavintlgold on 2007-03-11
You need Beryl or (i believe) Compiz for Kiba-dock to really work (this is the same for Avant Window Navigator).

It appears you don't have beryl or compiz, so you need to get it for it to work.

I don't really like kiba-dock anyway. It's very buggy, even with Beryl running.

Beryl Homepage: [http://beryl-project.org/]("http://beryl-project.org/")

If you go to the beryl wiki, there's instructions for getting it to work. I would recommend it instead of Compiz, just because it has worked for me and I like it. I've never tried Compiz, but I believe it is harder to configure and has less features.
