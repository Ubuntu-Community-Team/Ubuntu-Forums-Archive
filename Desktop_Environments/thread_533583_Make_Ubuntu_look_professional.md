### Post by hikaricore on 2007-08-24
> **davbren said:**
> I think that Gnome needs a slight redesign to bring it on par with kde and even windows for eyecandiness.

I think Gnome is nearly on par with KDE, but I wouldn't even attempt to compare it to the ***dows.
That's like comparing Bricks and Oranges.  Two completely different beasts.

The Gnome development team is very particular about the look and feel of Gnome, it's unlikely to change significantly any time soon.  Also Ubuntu has no control over Gnome.

Others have suggested you look at themes.  Since only you know what you like and don't like, I suggest you take time out to browse through the available looks for Gnome/Metacity and find something that fits your likes and needs. :)
