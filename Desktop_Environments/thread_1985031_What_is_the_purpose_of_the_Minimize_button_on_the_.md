### Post by fragos on 2012-05-22
Consider a menu with a grayed out item which means not applicable. You'll notice that the minimize button doesn't indicate that you're hovering -- another sign of not applicable. The window buttons in the Dash are a little inconsistent with lets the buttons functions when maximized.

### Post by zombifier25 on 2012-05-22
