Can anyone tell me what makes a computer prefer apm over acpi? For instance my computer  goes  wacko on  acpi and normal with only apm  configured kernels.  My award bios support  S1 & S3 type of powering.

Apm: normal

ACPI:  Hibernate: Shuts  down upon  reboot  award bios prints funny characters.

What is newer acpi or apm?

---

### Post by art on 2006-06-29
ACPI is newer and is better, but having said that the support for it on Linux may be worse, since APM was there longer. So some functions that ACPI supports may not function properly in your computer.
