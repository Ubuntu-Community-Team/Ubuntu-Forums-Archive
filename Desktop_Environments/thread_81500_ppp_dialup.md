### Post by migueljacq on 2005-10-24
Gnome ppp is a good option. If you like the command line, and since you have configured pppconfig, you can just type into the terminal 'sudo pon provider' (or whatever name you gave to the connection in pppconfig) and it will connect.
I am surprised you have to re-setup in Networking after a reboot. After I enabled the modem connection there, filled in the basic details, I can just activate/deactivate through the GUI.
