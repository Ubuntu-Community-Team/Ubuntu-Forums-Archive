### Post by Bob D. on 2005-06-19
I think you've essentially covered the reason why with the phrase "update."

The Ubuntu philosophy is not to _update_ items for features enhancements or bug fixes between Ubuntu releases. Only for security issues will items get patches backported into the existing app (as with Firefox...still at 1.0.2 but has the 1.0.4 security patches).

Like it or not, that's the way Ubuntu works, so that's just the way things are. We're fortunate that the Kubuntu team (and the KDE team) have made the 3.4.1 update available to us through their repos.

Bob
