### Post by kerm on 2005-12-05
I'm not sure what happened but after I upgraded to Breezy, the **sound-juicer** application opens up automatically after I insert a CD into my drive. 

Not a huge deal but annoying none-the-less. Anyway to fix it?

---

### Post by Ampersand on 2005-12-05
Go to System - Preferences - Removable drives and media, then under the Multimedia tab either disable 'Play audio cds when inserted' or change the command to something else.
