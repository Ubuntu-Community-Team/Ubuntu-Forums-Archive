If you are experiencing this problem it would be a good idea to indicate that you are affected on the Ubuntu bug tracker:

### Post by para-banana on 2012-01-29
Thanks!
I reported the bug.
I figured out that the problem is reduced if the XScreen0 on DFP-0 and on GPU-0 is the left one (it should not be on the second GPU). Unfortunately this also means that the main screen is the left one. 
Now I can move symbols on the middle and the right screen without an offset, but I am not able to move symbols to the left screen. Nevertheless I got rid of the offset as long as I agree with moving symbols just on two of three screens. To achieve this it is necessary to align all monitors to the upper side, if they do not have the same resolution.
If I align the monitors not to the upper side, I will end up with a vertical offset instead of a horizontal one.

[edit]
By the way, I am using GNOME 3. I do not know if that matters...
[/edit]
