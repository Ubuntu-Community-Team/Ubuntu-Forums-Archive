### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-01-05
I don't believe there is a twin panel feature (that I have found anyway), however you can set the side pane to Tree mode for dragging and dropping files.

I find it easiest to open two nautilus windows and drag between the two. It is also handy to keep them on a separate workspace.
