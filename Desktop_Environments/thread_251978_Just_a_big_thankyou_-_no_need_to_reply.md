Hi everyone, well after persevering I just wanted to let you know that I finally:

- have my extremely picky Creative Zen Touch MP3 player (with nasty MTP firmware) working perfectly with Gnomad2
- get lovely digital TV pictures (better than I got in windows and more configurable) under Kaffeine
- get excellent quality mp3 CD-ripping under Sound Juicer
- and, I'm stunned to say, I get an amazing experience running World of Warcraft under Wine (with the hacked 'wine for WoW' package) - all eyecandy set to full at 1280x1024 with not a bit of slowdown :o 

All the information and help that I've obtained has come from here. Everywhere else I go looking for tips about getting things to work, people always seem to make assumptions about your level of linux-knowledge, but not on the Ubuntuforums. The posters here always seem to be quite happy to hold your hand when explaining how you install & tweak everything, and I am very grateful for that :mrgreen: 

I'm *almost* ready to abandon Windows now (unfortunately some games I play don't perform well under Cedega, and what I'd like to say about Creative's interest in linux when it comes to the X-Fi soundcard, I can't say in public :-#  lol. Mind you, I'm lucky that I have decent on-board sound on my motherboard)

Thankyou again to everyone who has supported me directly (and indirectly) in getting a super-duper Dapper system up and running, and I hope to be able to share some of my new-found knowledge to help others getting their systems set up, frustration free :D
