### Post by TiBaal89 on 2011-09-21
If you don't have a backup of your stuff, you need to create one.  External drives with the space you require are extremely inexpensive.  Get one and copy your stuff before your internal drive takes a dump.  Once that is repaired, look into how you can use the external in conjunction with your computer to avoid this risk in the future.

A backup from scratch for the purpose of repairing and then restoring your computer is as simple as copying the files to that external disk (via GUI or the 'cp' command if you like).
