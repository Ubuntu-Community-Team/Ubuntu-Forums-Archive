### Post by gamerteck on 2006-07-30
This happened to me before, somehow the gaim preferences got corrupted and un-installing did not remove the corrupted preferences.

Go to your home directory and un-hide the folders. 

Then rename .gaim to .gaimbackup or just delete the folder .gaim.

Re-launch gaim and you should have a clean config.
