I decided to switch from Gnome to KDE, so I could see just what all has changed in KDE, etc etc. Everything was cool, except...I accidentally, it seems, switched off the Kmenu button/launcher. How do I restore that? All suggestions would be greatly appreciated.

---

### Post by Jucato on 2007-04-04
Right-click on the panel, select "Add Applet to Panel", then look for the K Menu. click on the Add button. Middle-click and drag to move the icon to a place that you want.
