### Post by rattking on 2006-03-29
i think its a feature. one is "Google suggest toolbar" the other is "search toolbar"
to remove one goto
konqueror's Settings menu > Configure Toolbars > Toolbar: dropdown menu
select the toolbar you want to remove
drag the searchbar from "current action" to "available action" 
Ok
your good to go
