### Post by GameManK on 2008-11-22
On hardy you get 2.2.5 and on intrepid you get 3.1.  You can also get 3.1 on hardy with the ktorrent-kde4 package.  Those are the versions that are packaged for Ubuntu.  If you need something different for some reason, you'll need to use a third party repository or build from source, but I wouldn't recommend it.
