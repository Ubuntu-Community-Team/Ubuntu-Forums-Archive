### Post by johnc4510 on 2006-05-30
It is in the repositories for Dapper. If you haven't enabled the Universe or Multiverse repositories, launch synaptic, click on settings, click on repositories and put check marks in the empty boxes. Then reload, mark updates and apply. Then you should find it in the package list.
