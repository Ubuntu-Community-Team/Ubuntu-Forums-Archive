### Post by mcduck on 2012-09-15
LXDE follows the same freedosktop.org menu standard, so Alacarte should work just fine. Just remember that you need to reload LXPanel (or log out and back again) after editing menu entries.

However, if you want, you can also try LXDE's own menu editor LXMED.

[http://wiki.lxde.org/en/Main_Menu]("http://wiki.lxde.org/en/Main_Menu")
[http://sourceforge.net/projects/lxmed/]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/lxmed/")
