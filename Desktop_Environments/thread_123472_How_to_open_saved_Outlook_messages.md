### Post by mattotoole on 2006-02-03
The easiest way to do this is move all your messages to an IMAP account (using Outlook), then sync with your new mail program (Evolution).

Another method is with an intermediary program that handles both formats, if your new program (Evolution) can't import directly.  Thunderbird would probably work, as it handles most mail formats.
