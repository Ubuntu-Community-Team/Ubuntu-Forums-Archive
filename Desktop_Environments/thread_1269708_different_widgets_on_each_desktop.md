### Post by krazyd on 2009-09-18
Yep, you can do this:
[LIST]
[*]click the "cashew" thing in the top right corner of your desktop
[*]select "zoom out"
[*]in the top left menu, click "add activity" (might need to unlock widgets first)
[/LIST]
Note that activities in KDE are different to desktops - if you have four desktops, then you get four for each activity.

Switch between activities using "Alt-D then right/left" when the desktop has focus, or add the "Activity Bar" widget to your desktop, or just "zoom out" and select, the same way you added an activity.
