### Post by lindsay7 on 2009-12-31
Install the Screenlets manager from Synaptic. Open it and choose the trash can. This will put on on the desktop.  Once there you can play with the settings and get it the way you want it.

### Post by pastalavista on 2009-12-31
> **Andytater said:**
> I'm new to Ubuntu, 

How do I move the trash can to the desktop in gnome?

To show the Trash on the desktop, use gconf-editor. Hit Alt-f2 and enter 'gconf-editor'. Then navigate to apps--> nautilus--> desktop and check the icons you want to show.

Then you can just remove the Trash from the panel.
