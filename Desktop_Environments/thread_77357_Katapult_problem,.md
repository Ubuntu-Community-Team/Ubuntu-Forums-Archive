### Post by Chelmi on 2005-10-20
Hi.

I had the same problem.
You have to check your "Keyboard Shortcuts" in kcontrol,  if you chosed Windows Scheme, alt+space is already used for "Window Operations Menus". You need to choose another shortcut for it or  choose another scheme. 
Then you have to logout and katapult will work next time you log into KDE.
