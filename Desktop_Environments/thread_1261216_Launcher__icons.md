### Post by drs305 on 2009-09-08
If you have opened the launcher, click on the icon symbol (upper left). That will open a folder with icons in it. On my system, it opens /usr/share/icons/hicolor/scalable/apps/gnome-panel-launcher.svg

If you don't like any of the icons in that folder, you can press the "Browse" button and look in any folder you wish.
