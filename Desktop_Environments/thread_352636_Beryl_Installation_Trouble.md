### Post by astoltz on 2007-02-04
In your xorg.conf file, look for the line that says ```
Driver "nv"
```and change it to:```
Driver "nvidia"
```Then restart X with "ctrl-alt-backspace".  When the x-server fails to start, it should give you a message asking if you want to see the detailed error message, say yes to this and then post the results.
