### Post by seth1010 on 2011-03-19
Please note that the icons on both the top and bottom bars are shifted over much further to the left than they should be.  While I don't really care about the top one, the location of the trash bin makes it so I can have less windows open before it starts to compress the titles.
[IMG]http://i.imgur.com/N2PRN.jpg[/IMG]
That monitor is 1600x1200

SOLVED:
What I ended up needing to do was to right click on all of the icons, uncheck "Lock to Panel", then right click on them again and click "move".  Then you just need to drag the icons to wherever you want them.
