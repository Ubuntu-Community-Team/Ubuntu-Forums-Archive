### Post by diaa on 2008-06-18
This is because you have your screensaver starting after 11 minutes(or may be 10 I'm not sure), GNOME sets the minimum time for screen sleep to that number(so you can't make the screen turn off before the screensaver starts), you can solve your problem by setting the screensaver to 1 minutes(or may be disabling it) and then you'll be able to have the monitor turn off after 1 or 2 minutes.
