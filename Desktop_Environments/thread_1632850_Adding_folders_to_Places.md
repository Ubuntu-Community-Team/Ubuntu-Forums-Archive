### Post by danbar on 2010-11-28
I want to know if it's possible to add more folders to Places (Music, Documents, Pictures etc..). If yes how?

---

### Post by tom4everitt on 2010-11-28
Just open a Natilus window (click on for example 'Home Folder' in Places). Drag and drop the folders you want to add to the left pane. They will be added both to the left pane and Places. 

Simsalabim :)

### Post by akand074 on 2010-11-28
You could also just go to "bookmarks" in nautilus and choose "add to bookmarks". That will add them to places.

### Post by Paanini on 2010-11-29
When you say 'delete; do you mean you removed it from your Nautilus Bookmarks / Places Menu ?
If that's the case then no harm done, your folder will still be in it's original location - you can just go back and drag and drop the folder into the Bookmarks panel to the left of any Nautilus Window.
