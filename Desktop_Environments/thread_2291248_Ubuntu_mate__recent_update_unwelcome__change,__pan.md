### Post by Dennis N on 2015-08-18
The window list expands to the right as far as possible from where the left end is positioned until it is blocked by another item. The left end of your window list is at the left end of the four little boxes (actually window buttons) your arrow points to, and there is not enough space to show the contents of the buttons. Right click at the left-end of the window list, select move, and stretch it out towards the left and the window buttons' contents will reappear.

See attached image: your (compressed) window list is the yellow box. Move left end in direction of the arrow and it will expand out and reveal the window buttons.
