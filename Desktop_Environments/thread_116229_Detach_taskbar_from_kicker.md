If I'm understanding you right, you want to have 2 pannels and the Taskbar on the other as the Kicker is.

You can do this throgh right clicking on the pannel -> Add Panel -> and add a new Controlbar. You can either pull this one to the upper, lower, left or right border of the desktop. You can then add or distract applets to the panels you want.

This way you can make KDE to look just any kind of way you like it ;-).
