### Post by Ramses de Norre on 2006-06-26
I never used automatix before because I don't like the idea of installing programs without being sure of what's been done. And changes made by automatix are sometimes difficult to undo. And yes I've seen people having trouble after using automatix, but there are also a lot of people who are very happy with the results of using it.. 
I personaly don't advise you to use it when you're comfortable with synaptic or aptitude.
