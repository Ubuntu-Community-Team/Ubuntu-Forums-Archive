### Post by az on 2006-08-22
You can certainly upgrade from the net.  You need to upgrade to breezy first, then to Dapper.

Change your sources to breezy and dist-upgrade.

Then, the update-manager should tell you (in breezy) that there is a more current release.  Click that and you will get upgraded to Dapper.
