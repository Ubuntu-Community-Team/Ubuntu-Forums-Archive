### Post by jbruced on 2009-01-02
Yes and yes, you are right!

You can have multiple desktops to organize your work and open windows. Right click the desktop icon to add or remove more desktops.

Yes the 3d cube is for neat effects, yet it does switch to the desktop in front when you are done having fun with it, so the value is a cool way to switch desktops, or just to see the status of something on another desktop.

I played with it for about a week, it's cool, but to me no real value.

Hope this helps.

### Post by keithld on 2009-01-02
Yes those 2 windows in the lower right are what's used to produce the Cube, tho you need to right click on it and change it to "4 columns" so it will make the cube... Then you open programs on each Desktop and switch between them when you rotate the cube... You rotate it by holding down the CTRL & ALT Keys while Clicking and holding the left mouse key...

Basically it's just Eye Candy...
