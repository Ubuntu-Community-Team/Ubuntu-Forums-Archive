### Post by Nythain on 2007-03-05
First and foremost... a flashy emerald theme... one with transparency, glowing borders and glowing buttons should do the trick

Im partial to Beam Up for Create 1 and 2

Flames for close 1
Beam up for close 2

I stick with fade for min and restore, though sidekick or magic lamp are alway fun for impression

the desktop cube is a must... find a good animated skydome, maybe tweak some transparencies either all the time or during rotation. Inside the cube is sometimes fun

3d world or whatever its called now, the one that puts all the windows on thier own plane

editing the opacity levels in trailfocus and scale is a must

sorry i cant give you exact values... just play around till you are thinking "wow" then you can pretty much bank on your friends respondign the same
