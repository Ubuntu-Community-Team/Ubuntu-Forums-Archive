Hey guys, I had this problem with RecordMyDesktop causing the gnome top panel to disappear, and I fix it, then after I noticed that there are still people having this issue, and some taking the long way around to fix it, when I found a really simple fix.
I would like to know if it works for others to.
First open RecordMyDesktop.
Go to Advanced > Misc and from there uncheck "Outline Capture Area On Screen"
It should work then.
Hope it helps and that this is the right place.
