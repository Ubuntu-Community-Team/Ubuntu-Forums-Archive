### Post by MikeMecanic on 2015-03-19
**&#8203;**I discover that the only way to get rid of a black screen is the sleep or suspend button on the keyboard (upper left on a HP keyboard: quarter moon symbol).  By pressing the button **(the light becomes yellow)**, you go in sleep mode or suspend mode and by pressing it a second time, the monitor wakeup from black screen.  Try it.
