### Post by 3Miro on 2012-04-08
Your issue comes from the fact that AMD dropped the support for your video card. Hence all you got is the default FOSS driver and it isn't very good. I don't fully blame AMD, they had to deal with the long history of horrible ATI support. Newer AMD cards have great support, but the older ones are lacking.

Your can try to go back to 10.04 (supported for another year) or try something like Debian 6. Debian will be supported for at least two more years and since it comes with an older kernel, they may have a proprietary AMD drivers.

You can also look for a newer machine, something with AMD (newer than Radeon 4xxx) or Nvidia (except Optimus) or Intel.
