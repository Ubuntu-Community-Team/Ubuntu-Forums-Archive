### Post by Rasa1111 on 2010-11-06
this happened to me a couple of times in the past. 

 The way I remedied it,

 Was to open up "computer" or "file system"... 
Then once in the file browser, looking at all the files, go into the home folder...

 Once in the home folder.. 
Right click any of the other folders, ie, downloads, pictures, music, etc.. 
( Or you could right click on the home folder to do the same thing)
 then choose "open with another application"
[ATTACH]174716[/ATTACH]

 when the list of applications comes up to choose from..
 choose "open folder". 
and make sure you check off the little box, that says.. "remember for this type of file"
 [ATTACH]174717[/ATTACH]

 After that, when you open any "places".. it should open as it normally does. 

 hope it helps.
