### Post by ccnjim on 2009-08-17
This assumes your screenlets are running.

[LIST]
[*]Go to Accessories> open screenlets.
[/LIST]

[LIST]
[*] Click the All Screenlets button > select running screenlets.
[/LIST]

[LIST]
[*]In the screenlet view window click (highlight) on the screenlet  you want to auto start.
[/LIST]

[LIST]
[*]Put check in auto start at login box.
[/LIST]
repeat 3rd and 4th steps until all your auto start screenlets are enabled.
Log out/in to test.
