### Post by rainwalker on 2007-12-16
In 7.04, I think it's System > Preferences > Theme
You can download new themes from [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org)
Metacity themes are just for your window borders, GTK themes are for everything (controls, buttons, etc.)
