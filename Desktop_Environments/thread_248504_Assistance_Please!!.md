Hi,
I found this from SuSE forum [http://lists.suse.com/archive/suse-kde/2004-Mar/0090.html](http://lists.suse.com/archive/suse-kde/2004-Mar/0090.html).

The solution is to set your "view/viewmode" to "icon view". Then click refresh and your previews should appear. Also check what is your maximum size for previews.

Hope this solves your problem :p
