### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-04-18
Are you talking about the official wallpapers? Or an image from elsewhere? Did you put the image in the Pictures folder? That is the place these kind of images go when we want to do it the simple way. The Desktop User guide says

> You can also browse for any picture on your computer by clicking the + button. Any picture you add this way will show up under Pictures Folder. You can remove it from the list by selecting it and clicking the - button. Removing a picture from the list will not delete the original file.

To get an image in the Wallpapers panel as part of the image choices there is much more complicated. It can be done but I have not tried it on Raring and sometimes system locations are changed from version to version. So, what worked on 12.04 does not now work in 12.10 or 13.04.

Regards.
