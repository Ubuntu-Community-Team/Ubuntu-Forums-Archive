### Post by XEtedBear on 2010-11-17
Having recently looked at my monitor property using <Preferences> <Monitors> I now have a small pink rectangular graphic at the top left corner of my screen which hides the first part of the launch panel, as well as the main controls on any window placed in this position.

This graphic is identical in colour and content to that presented by the <Monitors Preferences> app. 

How do I delete it? It's not click-able drag-able or editable. It doesn't even appear to be a window..

---

### Post by mister_playboy on 2010-11-18
Look for gnome-display-properties in the System monitor and end/kill the process.

If it still appears on screen, you can either restart X or try logging out and back in.
