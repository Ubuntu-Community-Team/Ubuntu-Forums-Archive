### Post by manicka on 2005-10-18
The way I would achieve this is to have Kopete set to not automatically login at startup. You can set this in the section under the GeneraL tab, in the Behaviour section of the Kopete config panel. Just uncheck 'Connect automatically at startup'

Close the config panel, then right click on the kopete icon in the systray. Choose the account you want to login into from the popup and then choose 'invisible' from the next popup. Kopete should then connect to the account in invisible mode.

HTH
