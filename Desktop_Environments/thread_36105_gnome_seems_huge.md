### Post by GTvulse on 2005-05-21
In Gnome, go to System/Preferences/Font and change the desktop font sizes there. For the terminal emulator and gedit you have to set the sizes in their respective preference dialogs.

### Post by stilus on 2005-05-21
I can think of two ways to "make stuff smaller ;) "
First, change your screenresolution to higher numbers. That might work. secondly, you can change the default fontsizes to something smaller, which makes things look "smaller" as well. If you do that, you might also want to go into nautilus (just open your home folder), look for settings (I'm stuck to a windows box right now, so I can't be more specific), and change your default icon view size as well to something like 50%. 

Hope this helps!
