### Post by mad_scientist03 on 2005-07-18
One first step I would take is to take a look at /var/log/messages after you try to plug a device into the second or third USB ports. Your logs are going to be your best friends here, though you may not be able to immediately realize the significance of what they're telling you. However, if you get some information and come back to the forms, you may be able to jog people's memories enough to have them help you out a little more.

It could be a range of reasons at this point. One question is, do you know if ACPI is enabled on your laptop?
