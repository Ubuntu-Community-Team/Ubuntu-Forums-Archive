### Post by neo_reloaded on 2006-06-12
Okay, so the much much hyped xgl and compiz is "do-able" in dapper drake.
I have:
Dapper Drake on Thinkpad t42p, ATI FireGL T2 128MB, 2GB RAM. and Gnome runs good. I would concede that metacity is not performing as good as windows XP. Come on guys, accept the fact. I am not artistic enough to switch to KDE or any other desktop - I hope someday meta city will be real quick on a 2GB Ram computer as my thinkpad :)

Tried xgl/compiz
Okay to all ATI Firegl users - IT WORKS ON THIS CARD!!!! (hope some desporate googler for ati firegl t2 and xgl compiz will hit this page hehe)

Follow this [TECTONIC howto post]("http://www.tectonic.co.za/view.php?id=916"). That is all you need - it works 100% and XGL/compiz is really beautiful. Especially effects like wobly and worspace cube - all works without any grief. But to be frank, the shadow sucks( i found xcompmgr draws better shades)
- now comes the spoilers
   1 - DVD( video) playbacks suffer major issues, breaks, freezes and dies on you.
   2 - You can't change your window border. You will have to live with the default window border - after all whats an eye candy with an ugly window border????
so, i renamed my .Xsession file to .Xsession.test and stopped loading xgl/compiz. I will wait for someday when all this gets fixed. 

xcompmgr ( on dapper drake, Xorg 7.0) - followed various posts in this forum - That works too. But as we know this is a dead branch of development. So, issues that you face are forever. The windows appeared with nice shadow!!!! but I lost my desktop icons, Application/Places/System menus wont refresh sometimes...all sorts of UI hell. 

I threw the towel

Back to plain flat gnome. eye candies taste bitter!!

Did anybody have complete success with all these stuff? :rolleyes:
