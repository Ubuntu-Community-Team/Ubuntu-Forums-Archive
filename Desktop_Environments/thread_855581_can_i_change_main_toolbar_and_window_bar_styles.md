### Post by OutOfReach on 2008-07-10
Right click the panel, and click on properties. There you can set the opacity, size, background picture, etc.

The other way you can change the panel's appearance is to download GTK themes from sites like [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org), then open System>Appearance and drag and drop the download file into the Appearance window.
