### Post by phibxr on 2010-05-01
After installing the Nvidia driver to enable desktop effects, I noticed that everything I did that included rendering something through the driver (like hovering over an application in Docky or clicking it) was delayed for several seconds before a visible response.

Installing the newest Linux-drivers from Nvidias own homepage ([http://www.nvidia.com/content/DriverDownload-March2009/confirmation.php?url=/XFree86/Linux-x86/195.36.24/NVIDIA-Linux-x86-195.36.24-pkg1.run&lang=us&type=GeForce]("http://www.nvidia.com/content/DriverDownload-March2009/confirmation.php?url=/XFree86/Linux-x86/195.36.24/NVIDIA-Linux-x86-195.36.24-pkg1.run&lang=us&type=GeForce")) fixed this.
