### Post by kperkins on 2005-11-27
To delete saved passwords in thunderbird:
Edit > Preferences > Advanced > Saved Passwords > View Saved Passwords > Highlight the account you messed up and click remove (or just click "remove all").
Close Preferences, and check you mail, put in the right password at the prompt.
