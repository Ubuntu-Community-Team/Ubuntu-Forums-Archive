### Post by Sef on 2006-01-05
Those two sites use .WAV format from Microsoft.  To listen to that format, you need to download, Noatun from Add Applications.  (Applications -----> Add Applications -----> Sound and Video -------> more programs ------> click on the box next to Noatun and click apply.   If a second box comes up, click apply again.

Noatun Home Page:  [http://noatun.kde.org/]("http://noatun.kde.org/")
