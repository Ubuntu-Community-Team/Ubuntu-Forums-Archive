### Post by Krytarik on 2011-04-19
To just disable those plugin again:
- log in to "Failsafe GNOME" or whatever failsafe option you have at the login screens "Session" options, after clicking at your username
- or boot into "recovery mode -> failsafeX", you may have to press Shift to make the boot menu appear
- then just disable those option in Compiz' settings
- relogin or reboot respectively
