### Post by kanikilu on 2009-10-05
I think the short answer is "no". In my opinion, there's isn't really anything available that's even close to Dreamweaver for web authoring, much less better than it.

A quick search of Wine and Codeweavers' application databases does not yield positive results that CS3 or 4 will work under Wine or Crossover, but I have not tried them myself.

In cases like this where you cannot live without an application, and there is no reasonable alternative, the only other potential solutions are remote desktop to a windows machine that has it, or virtualization ([https://help.ubuntu.com/community/VirtualBox](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/VirtualBox)).

If you know your way around the code and don't so much need the WYSIWYG aspect of DW, this blog post may be of some use: [http://robrohan.com/2009/04/28/cfml-in-gedit-the-ubuntu-text-editor/](http://robrohan.com/2009/04/28/cfml-in-gedit-the-ubuntu-text-editor/)
