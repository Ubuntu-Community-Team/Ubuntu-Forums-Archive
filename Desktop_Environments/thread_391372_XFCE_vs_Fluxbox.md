### Post by kerry_s on 2007-03-23
Stay with the server install and fluxbox. Use light application alternatives.
firefox= standard browser, not fast but full of features
dillo=very fast light browser, great for just bouncing around the web with out distractions.
emelfm=filemanager, is the lightest fastest, most configurable there is, just not the pretties. :) 
leafpad or geany= editor,leafpad is basic,geany does alot more than just editing.
vlc= music, video, if you only want 1 player this is the 1 you want.
abiword= office app
graveman= burner, if you need one
wmix= volume control
xpaint= graphics manipulation, lighter then gimp
gaim= im and mail check
gv or epdfview or both= pdf reader, gv does post script and printing as well as pdf.
pptview= for those damn power point files. this is just a viewer.
cups and xpp= for printing

And don't forget screen it is awesome for handling background process and use the & symbol for that too.
