### Post by GeneralZod on 2008-06-26
> **kaldor said:**
> Update:

I did a bit of Google searching and it turns out you can't move widgets on the taskbar in KDE4.

Yep, it didn't make the feature freeze, alas, so will be postponed to 4.2.  Some distros are patching in the functionality themselves, though.

As a workaround, you can remove your K-menu, call up the Add Widgets dialog, and drag the K-menu from the Add Widgets dialog onto the required place on your panel.

as for the Show Desktop plasmoid, see e.g. here:

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/kdebase-kde4/+bug/243062](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/kdebase-kde4/+bug/243062)
