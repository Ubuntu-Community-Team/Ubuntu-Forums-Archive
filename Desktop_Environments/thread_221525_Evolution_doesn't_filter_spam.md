### Post by fragos on 2006-07-23
Perhaps you lost your junking training.  Mark junk as junk and Evolution will be retrained.  Most mail packages recognize junk by what you've told it.  Separate packages that come with their own junk/spam data bases can be used in addition to your mail package.  My primary mail provider is gmail which has excellent built in spam filters.  I configured Evolution to get mail from gmail and the spam it detects isn't forwarded to me.
