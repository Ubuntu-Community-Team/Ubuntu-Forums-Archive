### Post by J. S. Jackson on 2005-04-06
This is just a guess - but normally your monitor saves it's settings for each resolution and refresh rate.  For example you might be running windows at 1280x1024@85Hz, and in Ubuntu at 1280x1024@75Hz.

Assuming you have your monitor setup at the correct brightness (black level) and contrast (white level) in Windows, make sure you're using the exact same resolution and refresh rate in Kubuntu.

You might also want to check the gamma setting in KDE's control center.  Somehow, it may have gotten cranked up accidently?  This can cause things to look extremely harsh and bright.

You might also want to use a site such as this to help you visually set up the black/white levels and gamma.  [Norman Koran Photography](http://www.normankoren.com/makingfineprints1A.html)
