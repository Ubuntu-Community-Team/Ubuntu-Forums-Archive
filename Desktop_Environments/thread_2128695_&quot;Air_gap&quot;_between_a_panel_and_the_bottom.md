### Post by Toz on 2013-03-24
Right-click the panel and select Panel->Panel Preferences. In the Panel dialog box, uncheck "Lock Panel" and handles will appear at the beginning of the panel. Click and drag using the mouse to move the panel to the bottom of the screen. Once you've put the panel where you want it, check "Lock Panel" again to lock it into place (unless of course if you want to leave it floating, then don't recheck).
