### Post by Arsic on 2009-12-12
So, a couple of releases back this card wasn't working for me. I decided to see how it worked with 9,10 and it works beautifully. Got the Compiz Fusion effects running on it and everything. I also realize that this is an unsupported card.

I tried the fglrx drivers, but they didn't let me have desktop effects, so I uninstalled them.

A couple of things I've noticed:
[LIST]
[*]System > Administration > Hardware Drivers shows nothing
[*]/etc/X11/xorg.conf does not exist (possibly changed in this release?, ls -a reveals nothing)
```
ls -a /etc/X11/
.             default-display-manager  xinit       Xsession.d
..            fonts                    xkb         Xsession.options
app-defaults  rgb.txt                  Xresources  XvMCConfig
cursors       X                        Xsession    Xwrapper.config

```
[/LIST]
[Regenerated xorg.conf]("http://www.osguides.net/operation-systems/217-how-to-create-xorgconf-in-ubuntu-910.html")

[I][My only problem now is the screen resolution. The maximum resolution in Display Preferences is 1360 x 768 (16:9) @ 60Hz. 
I want it to go to 1920 x 1080, how can I make that happen?[/I]][Solved]

Update: Okay, so I solved my resolution problem, by regenerating the xorg.conf, then adding:
```
SubSection "Display"
        Viewport   0 0
        Modes "1920x1080" "1280x1024" "1024x768" "800x600" "640x480"    
        Depth     24
    EndSubSection
```
under **Section "Screen"**
*[Now I got the black desktop, so I turned off my Desktop effects, but I want them back.][Solved]*

Update 2: After a little searching, thoroughly reading [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1308027") solved my problems. Thank you very much Ubuntu community. This was an ATI 9250 AGP 256MB card, by the way.
