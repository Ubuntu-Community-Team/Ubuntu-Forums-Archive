### Post by musicinmybrain on 2006-06-08
The scroll bars are strangely designed. Left-click the side scroll bar to scroll down. Right-click the side scroll bar to scroll up. The bottom bar works the same way. If this annoys you, try using krdc for vnc. It's the least annoying vnc client I've found on Linux.
