### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-03-14
A visit to Mate's site -- mate-desktop.org -- is probably your best bet.

I've seen reports of problems using compiz with Mate 1.6.  Mate 1.8 was released last week, but I do not know if it addresses that issue.  In any case, 1.8 is not yet available in Mate's Ubuntu repo: [http://repo.mate-desktop.org/ubuntu/](http://repo.mate-desktop.org/ubuntu/).

Mate does provide a 'Show Desktop' applet, and the pager, of course.
