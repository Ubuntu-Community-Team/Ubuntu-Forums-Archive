### Post by catach on 2008-04-02
Well, you could go to **System>Preferences>Appearance>Fonts** and shrink things down that way. 

 Part of your issue might be because by default Ubuntu has a panel at the top as well as the bottom, where XP only has the bottom taskbar. I suppose you could try either hiding the top bar or moving some of its stuff to the bottom bar and just getting rid of the top bar.
