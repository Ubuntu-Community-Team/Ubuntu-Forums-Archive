### Post by RAOF on 2005-10-14
Did you burn it as a bootable disc?  If you burn it as a bootable disc, nero & alcohol generally add a small dos-loading track that emulates a floppy disc, and then boots of that.

You should select something like "burn image", "write image" or the like, then select the "breezy-something.iso" to burn the ubuntu disc.
