### Post by themtx on 2008-04-16
You should be able to re-add the "Xfce Menu" item to your panel - at least I think that's the one to which you're referring.  Right click your panel, choose "Add New Item", scroll all the way down to Xfce Menu, click Add, and you should be good to go.
