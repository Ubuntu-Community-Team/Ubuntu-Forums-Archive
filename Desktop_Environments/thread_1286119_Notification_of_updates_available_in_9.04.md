### Post by ghormley on 2009-10-08
When running 8.04, I got an icon in the bar at the top of my screen when updates were available.  After updating to 9.04, that feature does not happen.  Is there some place to turn on the notification icon?

In System > Administration > Software Sources on the tab, Updates, I have Check for Updates set to every two days and Only notify about available updates selected.

---

### Post by steeleyuk on 2009-10-08
Press alt+F2 > type 'gconf-editor' (no quotes) > apps > update-notifer > uncheck auto_launch
