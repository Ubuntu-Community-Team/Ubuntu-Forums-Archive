Ok, in Warty:

1. Right click on the panel.
2. Left click on "Add to panel..."
3. Scroll through the list until you see "Custom Application Launcher"
4. Click on "Custom Application Launcher" to select it.
5. Click on Add.
6. In the box that comes up, type in your application name, and then under command, 
7. type in the command to run your script (sh ./script.sh or something similar)
8. Choose an icon by clicking on the "No Icon" button, and select an icon.
9. Click OK.
