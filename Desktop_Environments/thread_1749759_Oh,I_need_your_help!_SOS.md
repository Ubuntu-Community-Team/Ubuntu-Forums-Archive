### Post by ivanovnegro on 2011-05-04
Can you try to reboot your system, and when you are at the login prompt, click on your user name and then go to the bottom and choose Ubuntu Classic, that should boot your system into the Classic mode of Ubuntu and then try in a terminal:

```
unity --reset
```,

that should reset your system to the default settings.
Then logout and login with the option Ubuntu.
