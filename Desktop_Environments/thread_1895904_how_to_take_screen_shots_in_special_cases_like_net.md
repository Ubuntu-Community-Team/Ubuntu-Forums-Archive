### Post by Krytarik on 2011-12-15
Just initiate the screenshot *before* opening that menu and set a sufficient delay. ;-)

A more feature-rich app is Shutter, of course, if you need something more special:

[http://shutter-project.org/](http://shutter-project.org/)

It's in the official repos, but I recommend installing it from its PPA, to always get the most recent version.
