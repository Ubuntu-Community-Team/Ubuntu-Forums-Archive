Installing Ubuntu automatically gives you Evolution (which will handle email) and OpenOffice, which gives you programs to read word documents. You can install gnumeric and gnucash very easily through Synaptic (System-->Administration-->Synaptic). You could also install Mozilla Thunderbird, a popular email program, through Synaptic.

There's tons and tons of software for linux. All you have to do is look!
