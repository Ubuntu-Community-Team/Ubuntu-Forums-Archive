### Post by ashily24dee on 2011-05-12
The following may work:  remove all the ink supplies.  Remove the print head and soak it for about 5 minutes in 0.5" of very hot water.  Gently blot the print head on a soft damp paper towel to remove the excess water, then replace in the printer and then reinstall the ink supplies. get more help [http://www.fixya.com/support/t704575-printer_not_printing_black_ink ]("http://www.fixya.com/support/t704575-printer_not_printing_black_ink")

Hope this helps you.

Good Luck!:)
