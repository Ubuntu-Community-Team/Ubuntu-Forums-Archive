### Post by kerry_s on 2015-06-13
this kind of problems is usually theme related, try switching to a different theme.

gnome 3 is gtk3 but a lot of apps are still gtk2, you need a theme with both gtk3 & gtk2 to work with all apps.

you can look in synaptic at what the theme is.
