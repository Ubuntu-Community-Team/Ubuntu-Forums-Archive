### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-02-18
RAM is there to be used. The important issue is, does it have a negative impact on your user experience? How much RAM is Compiz using? What utility are you using to measure it? Compiz is the window compositor. So, it has a big job to do and it will need space to do its stuff. 

On my machine with 1GB RAM Compiz uses 8.5% of memory and it stays at 8.5% even when I move application windows around. The percentage used of CPU increases as stuff is moved around but the percentage memory used stays the same. I guess that the amount of memory used by Compiz will depend on the fancy effects that we have enabled. But I am only guessing about that. I do not use any fancy effects, myself.
