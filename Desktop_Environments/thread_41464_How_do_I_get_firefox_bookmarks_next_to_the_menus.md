### Post by SGC on 2005-06-13
Rigth click between the menu toolbar and navigation toolbar and make sure that the "bookmarks toolbar" are checked.

From the same menu choose "customize" and drag and drop the button that says "Bookmarks toolbar items" from its place under the naviagation toolbar to the right of menu toolbar.
