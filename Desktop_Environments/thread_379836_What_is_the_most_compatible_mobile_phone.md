### Post by georges023 on 2007-11-02
Well, i have a Sony Ericsson Z520i.

Works pretty well with Ubuntu Gutsy Gibbon (Feisty Fawn also) but not out of the box.

Make a search for OpenObex, ObexFTP and ObexFTP Front End.

It should work for most of the phones for transferring files only.
For contacts, calendars and other search for Wammu.

It worked for me ;).
