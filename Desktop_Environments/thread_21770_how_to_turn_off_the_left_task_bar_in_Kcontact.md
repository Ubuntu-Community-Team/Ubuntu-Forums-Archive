### Post by thumper on 2005-03-24
[QUOTE=garyng]It takes up 1/4 of the window which is too much, especially there is another toolbar at the top which serve the same function.[/QUOTE]
 If you wave your mouse over the edge of the toolbar just next to the folders, the icon changes to the resize icon.  Click and drag left to the edge of the window.  This will hide the tool bar.  

To bring it back, move the mouse to the left side of the window until you get the resize cursor again, and click and drag right.
