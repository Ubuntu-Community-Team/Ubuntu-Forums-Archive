### Post by mcduck on 2005-12-01
I have no problems playing region free dvd's with mplayer. I have also watched some NTSC movies, and ofcourse PAL movies as I live in Europe. I suppose that  if both work for me here, they should also work for you. At least with a computer, as all TV's sure won't display both standards..

By the way, I often use my PS2 often for watching movies (as it has RGB SCART connector and beats S-Video from computer any day), and PS2 actually supports both PAL and NTSC, and even 1024*768 VESA.. I don't know what kind of connectors TV's there have, but I suppose using component cable would allow you to watch PAL movies with PS2. RGB sure works, but I think you don't have SCART connectors there?
