You can install as you prefer the meta-package **kubuntu-desktop** and you will get KDE installed on your system.

> and then I may or may not switch back to GNOME.
You will still have Gnome installed on your system and you will be able to switch between them on your login manager. Then, if you want to remove KDE try searching in the forum "remove kde" or something like that, I'm sure you'll find something.
