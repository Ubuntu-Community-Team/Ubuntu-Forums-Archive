### Post by benerivo on 2007-08-18
I used to use FF, although I'm now on Konqueror as i prefer it's performance and OS integration in KDE, and it may be worthwile your dad giving it a go. It would solve all three of your questions.

If it's to be FF, then you could try going in to the Control Centre>KDE Components>Default Applications, and setting the default web browser to be FF. This might solve the Kontact problem and also the 'desktop url opening' problem. I'm not sure how to make a link on the desktop from FF, but you could just copy the url from the location bar, then right click on the desktop and make a new 'Link to Location (URL)', and paste the url in that.

I'd think that some FF users out there may be able to help you more with your questions.
