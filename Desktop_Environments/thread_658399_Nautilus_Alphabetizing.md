### Post by /home/marc/ on 2008-01-04
I'm not exactly sure what character to use to have Nautilus sort it to the top, but a good alternative is to use an emblem to indicate a file as your favourite. Just right-click on the file, go to "Properties" and then click on "Emblems". There you have a collection of emblems, from which you can choose an emblem best fit for your need. Then, back in the file browser, you just sort the icons by their emblems. (Right click > sort by > emblems)

This elimanates the use of an extra letter, which could mess up the filenames.
