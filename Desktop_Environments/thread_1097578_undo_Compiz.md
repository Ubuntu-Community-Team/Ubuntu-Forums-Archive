### Post by Anzan on 2009-03-16
Missing title bars is a common problem with Compiz. Try going to the Appearance settings (or running gtk-chtheme) and choosing a new theme and that should work. Log out and back in and the title bars will likely return if they haven't. 

Regarding screen size, I use Fluxbox as the window manager for all of my boxen. I find it perfect for the 7 inch Eee. Maximize the windows for programs, tab them together and arrange in workspaces, right click on the title bars to set persistence.

Good luck.
