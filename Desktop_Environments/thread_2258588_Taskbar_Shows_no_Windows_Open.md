### Post by SantaFe on 2014-12-28
Have you right clicked the panel, clicked on Panel Settings, clicked on  the Panel Applets tab to see if Task Bar (Windows List) is there?  If  it's missing, then just click the add button & it will be in the  list.  Select it and click add, then use the Up & Down buttons to  move it where you want it & be sure to click the stretch square so  the panel looks correct.  

If it IS there, then there's something else wrong then.
