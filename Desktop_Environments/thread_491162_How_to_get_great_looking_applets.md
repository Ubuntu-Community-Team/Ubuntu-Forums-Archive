### Post by Howler9443 on 2007-07-03
I don't recall about "Cairo" I think it may have been part of an older Gnome release...I may be mistaken...and I probably am...lol.

If you really want some slick looking icons and such, check out icons that are in SVG format. SVG is "Scalable Vector Graphics" and as a general rule are very nice looking. They scale nicely while keeping nice smooth lines...no jaggies.

[www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) has some as well as [www.kde-look.org](www.kde-look.org) 

I personally use a few SuperKaramba applets...they are called "themes" in SuperKaramba speak. I love LiquidWeather++ VERY sharp "theme".

Good luck!
