### Post by hictio on 2012-06-23
Moving the clock is easy: [Frippery Move Clock](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/2/move-clock/).

> 
Move clock to left of status menu button


For the Task Manager, I guess this is what you mean [Window List](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/25/window-list/).

> 
Add a window list to the top panel
