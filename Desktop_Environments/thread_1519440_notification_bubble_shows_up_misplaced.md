### Post by hhh on 2010-07-18
That's the correct placement for notifications in the last 2 Ubuntu versions. The gap is where system the volume notification goes if you control sound volume with your keyboard hotkeys. (and/or the battery level warning for laptops).

There is a ppa with a new version of notify-osd that is customizable... you can move it to different corners of the screen, you can revert to the old behaviour, change colors, etc... There's a ppa for a GUI for that too. Here are the links for how to enable it all...

[http://www.webupd8.org/2010/05/finally-easy-way-to-customize-notify.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2010/05/finally-easy-way-to-customize-notify.html)
