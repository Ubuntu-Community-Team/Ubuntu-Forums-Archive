### Post by sayakb on 2008-07-08
You have to first of all, **check** the *Administer the System* option for your wife's account. If she doesn't know the root password, she cant use it. Also, if you have compiz running on your account and you don't logoff but switch user, you cant have compiz on her's too (seems to be a bug)
