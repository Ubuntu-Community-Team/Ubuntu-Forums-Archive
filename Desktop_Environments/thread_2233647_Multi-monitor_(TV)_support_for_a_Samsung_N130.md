### Post by yancek on 2014-07-10
Do you just want to use the TV screen and not the netbook laptop screen?  If that's the case go to System Settings, Display.  In the window which opens, you should see the two boxes representing the netbook and the TV and should be labelled.  At least, that is the case with my setup.  The TV box should show the brand name of the TV.  Click the TV box on the left of the screen then click the On toggle tab.    Click the box on the left for the laptop and click the Off tab.  Uncheck the Mirror Displays checkbox.  You should then be able to set the resolution.  Launcher placement should be the TV name.
