### Post by TheFu on 2015-11-17
Try running these cmds before starting videos:
```
xset s off &
xset -dpms &
```
Then
```
xset s on &
xset +dpms &

```
When done.

BTW, you can probably ssh in from another box and kill the screensaver or X.  It is just the GUI having issues,not the whole OS.
