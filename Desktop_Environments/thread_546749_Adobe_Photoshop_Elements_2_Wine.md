### Post by ubun tsu on 2007-09-09
I had this same problem.. drove me crazy. I love Linux but I need photoshop. I spent hours trying to figure it out. Never did, but I found a work-around. It hangs during "initializing suites" so... if you open a version of Image Ready first, then PS will open. So now I open Image Ready, minimize it, open PS, minimize it, close Image Ready, then PS is ready to use.
