### Post by xristaras on 2006-08-19
I recently thought I would install beagle to try it out. However, after I rebooted and tried to log in again GNOME ran very, very slow.

It took while to finish loading, for example the panel would sit there with no buttons/applets on it for a while and then things would start showing up slowly, even the terminal took a few seconds to load when started. The ubuntu starting sound also did not play.

I removed beagle and I tried again but it did not help. Then a did a search on synaptic on "mono" and removed all installed components related to mono. This also caused apt to remove a bunch of other packages.

When it finished, I logged-out and in again and everything was back to normal speeds.

I thought I would post this and give some heads-up to anyone experiencing gnome running slow. This could be your reason.
