### Post by lcg on 2006-06-02

Actually, it's not an Ubuntu thing, this is part of the [Gnome HIG]("http://developer.gnome.org/projects/gup/hig/") (Human Interface Guidelines). That's why, in Gnome, the "Options" are under the "Edit" menu while they are under the "Tools" menu in Windows (conforming to some standard for Windows applications).
