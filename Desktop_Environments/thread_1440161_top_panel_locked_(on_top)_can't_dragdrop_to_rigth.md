### Post by mcduck on 2010-03-27
> **ubername said:**
> I don't think drag and drop has been available for a while in Ubuntu (for panels). Right click on the panel, select 'properties' and pick your preferred orientation.

The drag& drop feature was removed some versions ago as people were accidentally dragging their panel to desktop sides, and that tends to break the applet layout and many weren't able to fix it on their own. (although if I remember right it used to be possible to drag a panel between top/bottom, or left/right but not from top to right, for example. Doesn't seem to work that way in 10.04 though).

Like ubername suggested, you can use the pane properties to move a panel between horizontal & vertical setups. Or gconf-editor, of course.
