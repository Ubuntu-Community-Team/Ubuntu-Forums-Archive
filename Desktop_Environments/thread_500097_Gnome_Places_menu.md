At the start of my installation, i tried to connect to a windows share using Places -> Connect to server.

- From Bookmarks (There is none)

### Post by zanglang on 2007-07-13
There should be an icon for the connection on your desktop. Right-click that, and choose 'Unmount'. You can do the same from your Nautilus sidebar as well.

---

### Post by merlinus on 2007-07-13
Open Places/Home Folder, right-click on the name of the shortcut, left-click Unmount Volume.

-merlin
