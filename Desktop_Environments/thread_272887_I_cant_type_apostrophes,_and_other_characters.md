### Post by madplanet on 2006-12-23
Ah figured it out! For the benefit of others (in Edgy, and probably other vers) go to Keyboard preferences, Layouts, and add a new layout. Where I went wrong was when I chose a new keyboard I hit the twisty for 'United Kingdom' and picked International. I didn't realise that you could actually pick just the twisty title itself - so highlight the Twisty (United Kingdom) and click OK.

That should do it, or at least, it worked for me :-)

Nic
