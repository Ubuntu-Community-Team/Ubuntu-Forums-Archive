### Post by Joe_Bishop on 2008-04-22
I've solved this issue. The problem was that there were more bookmarks than shew in the sidebar.  I saw it in the Nautilus: Bookmarks->Edit Bookmarks (or just press Ctrl+B in Nautilus). I've removed them all and then put the places I need in.
