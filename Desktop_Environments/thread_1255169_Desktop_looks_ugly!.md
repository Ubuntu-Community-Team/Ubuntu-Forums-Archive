Firstly, you can make icons any size you want. Right click an icon and choose 'Stretch Icon..' from the menu. Then you can resize them as big or as small as you want.

Also, visit [http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/). There you can browse and download lots of themes and wallpapers to use on all parts of your Ubuntu system. Read [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=203093") for some information on getting themes from gnome-look.

Finally, if you have a graphics card with drivers installed, you can enable desktop effects from System > Preferences > Appearance then choosing the Visual Effects tab and picking normal or extra.
