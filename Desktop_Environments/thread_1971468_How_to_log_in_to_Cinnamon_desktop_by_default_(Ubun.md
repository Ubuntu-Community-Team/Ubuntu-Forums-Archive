### Post by buzzingrobot on 2012-05-02
Go into settings and find "User Accounts" and change back to "Password Required". Then, logout.  When you're prompted for your password, click on on the little Ubuntu symbol that's just above.  A dropdown menu should appear and Cinnamon should be an option.  Select it and continue to log on.  You can then go back and reset to automatic logon.  Cinnamon will be the default from then on.
