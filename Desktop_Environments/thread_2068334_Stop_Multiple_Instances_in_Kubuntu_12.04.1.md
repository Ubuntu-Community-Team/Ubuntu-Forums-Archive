### Post by vandorjw on 2012-10-08
Dragon player is meant to be a simple video player. If you have VLC, there is little to no reason to also use dragon player.

As far as I could tell, dragon player version 4.10 and newer support single-instance-application. Check your version number on Kubuntu 12.04.1, as your version of DP may not have an option for single instances.

"This is not ubuntu or kubuntu specific, but is application specific."
