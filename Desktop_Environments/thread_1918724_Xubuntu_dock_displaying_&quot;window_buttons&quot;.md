### Post by LewisTM on 2012-02-01
He means the Window buttons as in taskbar buttons.
And no, I don't think there is a way to enlarge the buttons to any size. 
The maximum you can go is 48 pixel by increasing the panel size, beyond that it creates a second row of buttons.
You might want to give third party docks a try. Cairo-Dock works really well with Xfce and provides powerful features.
