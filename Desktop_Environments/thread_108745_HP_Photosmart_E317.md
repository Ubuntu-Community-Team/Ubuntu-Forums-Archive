### Post by lgaboury on 2006-01-04
Looking at your Photosmart E317 online manual, I can see that you have the option between "Digital Camera" and "Disk Drive" under the USB Configuration screen.  Like my Photosmart C618, the default is "Digital Camera", try with "Disk Drive".  Ubuntu should properly detect the camera as an external USB Drive and automatically create an icon on your desktop.  I should also add that initialy I thought that my camera would not work with Ubuntu when "Digital Camera" setting was selected; however I found out that occasionally I had to turn off/on a second time before the camera would be detected.  

Good luck.
