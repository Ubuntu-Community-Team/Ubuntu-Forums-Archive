### Post by Jedeye on 2006-03-07
not sure exactly how you want to make a brochure and I don't use Openoffice for much more than simple papers but you could make a landscape page(Format/Page/Page/) and then give it 3 columns(Format/Page/Columns/) and then just fill up each column with the info you want and fold it up. You may want to do 2 pages where you can print front and back.

You may also think about trying Scribus [http://www.scribus.org.uk/]("http://www.scribus.org.uk/")
Good Luck!
