### Post by Bob D. on 2005-04-09
Glad it did the trick Leif!

I've had that same problem of hundreds (in my case) blank to-do's getting created in Evolution. The best guess I can make is that the gnome-pilot conduits just do not like it when you sync your Palm to multiple computers. As soon as I stopped syncing to my computer at work (running the Palm desktop software), all the blank duplicates stopped.

Not a good solution, but for now it seems to be working. Maybe something for you to look at if the duplicates continue. Hopefully it was a one-time thing.

Bob
