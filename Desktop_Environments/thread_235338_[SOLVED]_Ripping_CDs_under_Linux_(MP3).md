### Post by mitch.c on 2006-08-13
MP3 in Sound Juicer:

1. [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats)
2. Sound Juicer -> Help -> Contents -> Preferences (Scroll to bottom)

:cool:
