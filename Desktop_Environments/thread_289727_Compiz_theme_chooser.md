### Post by roderikk on 2006-10-31
If everything is correct you will see a 'beryl' or red gemstone in your icon bar. When you right click on that you'll see a link to "Emerald Theme Manager". There it is.

BTW, Beryl is not an 'effects manager' but a Window Manager and you can find more information over at the Beryl forums: [http://www.beryl-project.org/](http://www.beryl-project.org/)
