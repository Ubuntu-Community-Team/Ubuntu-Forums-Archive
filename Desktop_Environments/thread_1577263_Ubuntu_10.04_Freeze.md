### Post by MooPi on 2010-09-18
It sounds like it may be a hardware issue and heat. If it's not heat it may be bad memory. Shut the computer down and let rest. If your comfortable opening the case do so . Unplug the computer and lightly feel around for excessive amounts of heat. If you can sense heat without touching or if something is so warm as not to let you rest your fingers on it without burning then you may have a heat issue. If you have lots of dust or accumulated cigarette dust could be a problem blocking fans. Always be careful and gentle inside your computer.
