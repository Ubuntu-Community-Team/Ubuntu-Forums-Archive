### Post by matthew on 2005-12-09
Try this.

Right click on the volume control icon on the panel (looks like a side view of a speaker). *if it isn't there, see below then come back here

Select Open Volume Control

Make sure both the PCM and the Master sliders are up and that neither one is muted. If one is muted, click on the icon below it to unmute.

* To install the volume control applet right click on the panel you want it in and select Add To Panel. Then select Volume Control under the System & Hardware section
