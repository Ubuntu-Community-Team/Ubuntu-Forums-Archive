### Post by Nuxala on 2010-09-12
To remove send to (and I think sharing options)

Go to Ubuntu Software Center an click "installed software" and search:

nautilus-sendto
 nautilus-sendto-empathy
nautilus-share

and remove them and restart ubuntu
