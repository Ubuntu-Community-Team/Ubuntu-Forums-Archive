### Post by howefield on 2013-06-22
There is probably a better way than this, but you could open your browser and go to the desired website, bookmark it and place it on the bookmarks bar and then drag the bookmark to the desktop.

Right click on the new desktop shortcut icon, then click on the shortcut icon and navigate to usr/share/app-install/icons and select the firefox icon. You can also rename the shortcut to suit (or leave as is). You don't have to do this step, just makes it look better.,
