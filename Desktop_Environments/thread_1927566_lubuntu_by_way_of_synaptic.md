### Post by cortman on 2012-02-18
Installing lubuntu-desktop will only layer the LXDE environment and some more programs over your existing Lucid system. If you are experiencing problems with the system being buggy, installing lubuntu-desktop will probably only compound the problem. To get the best use out of Lubuntu, burn an ISO onto a flash drive or CD and do a clean installation.
