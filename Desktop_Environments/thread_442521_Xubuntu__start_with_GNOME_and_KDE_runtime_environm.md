### Post by fatbuttlarry on 2007-05-13
I think that just loads some essential compatibility stuff for running gnome or kde created apps.

KDE Stuff is any kde (Qt API) apps such as **kopete, konqueror, konsole, koffice, amaroK**
GNOME Stuff is any gnome (Gtk API) apps such as **firefox, nautilus, gaim, gimp**

Should they be checked?

You'll probably notice a slight boot speed increase without them, but programs that need them may initially load slower.

Most people use tons of gnome applications and would probably benefit from having it checked.
