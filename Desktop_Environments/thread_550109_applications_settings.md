### Post by swisscow on 2007-09-13
Usually in your home folder somewhere, but they are hidden - i.e. they start with a '.' e.g. **.mozilla**

Enable show hidden files in your file manager and you can see the lot!

### Post by mcduck on 2007-09-13
If you just remove application it's settings won't be removed.Only using '--purge remove' with apt-get or 'mark for complete removal' in Synaptic will remove configuration files. And even then your own personal settings stay in hidden files inside your home directory.
