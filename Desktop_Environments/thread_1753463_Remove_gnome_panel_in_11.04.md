### Post by Krytarik on 2011-05-09
There are two ways:

- Currently, you are running the Unity session ("Session" option "Ubuntu" at the login screen). There you can just disable the "Ubuntu Unity Plugin" in "CompizConfig Settings Manager". FYI, the panel there is in fact not Gnome Panel.

- You can also run classic Gnome 2 ("Session" option "Ubuntu Classic" at the login screen). To disable the Gnome Panel there, see my earlier post:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10789284#post10789284](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=10789284#post10789284)

To choose either session option, click at your username while you are at the login screen and choose the "Session" option at the bottom panel.
