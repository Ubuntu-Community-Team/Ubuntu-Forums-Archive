### Post by bcschmerker on 2009-05-10
Update 2:  67GTA is onto something.  Several users other than I have been encountering random shutdowns with no unmount, broken thermal management, etc.; one new suspect is a recently discovered firmware issue with recent 'boards.  Details on this new angle are posted at [The DSDT thread](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1147474) over in [Hardware and Laptops](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=332).

Addendum:  In the specific case of my Everex with its Elitegroup 'board's [nVIDIA nForce 405](http://www.nvidia.com/), the uncommanded shutdowns occur many times more frequently with the Real-Time kernel packages w/r/t the Generic kernel packages (both up to 2.6.24-24 as of this edit).  Apparently the Generic kernel better tolerates the quirks of the nForce hardware.
