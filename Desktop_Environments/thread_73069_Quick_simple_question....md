### Post by zac851 on 2005-10-08
How can I make the windows in gnome minimize instantly.  In KDE it had a option to turn off the animation and when you would minimize windows they would close instantly.   How can I change this in Gnome?

---

### Post by mcduck on 2005-10-08
Go to Applications/System Tools/Configuration Editor/apps/metacity/general, and check the 'reduced_resources'-box.
