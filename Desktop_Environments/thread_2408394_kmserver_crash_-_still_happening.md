### Post by vidtek on 2018-12-18
The kmserver crash on logout is a persistent bug that stretches way back to 2008.  

On my Kubuntu 18.04 system it is caused by pulseaudio.

To stop it happening, just disable in [I]System Settings > Notifications > Event Source=Plasma Workspace > Logout > disable sounds.
[/I]
I thought I'd post it here as multiple searches turn up no real cure-all for this bug.
