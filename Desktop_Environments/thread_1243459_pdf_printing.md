### Post by fonfi on 2009-08-18
Hi,
As I have some problems with evince and pdf printing (mainly quality issues), I have installed Adobe Reader (you can download native linux version from adobe.com), which seems to have the feature you are looking for. 
In the print dialog (the one you choose printer, and set print parameters) find section "Page Handling", and in "Page Scaling" labeled select-box, you have to choose "booklet printing" option. This should do the trick.

I have just checked evince, and it seems it has no such feature, so you're stuck with Adobe Reader or maybe there is another pdf viewer with booklet print option - there is plenty to choose from... :)
