### Post by gThree on 2006-07-15
I use Ekiga with an FWD account.  I think FWD gives you an Account Name when you sign up, but the User Name is usually your number and the Registrar is the SIP server you're trying to log on to (fwd.pulver.com, for example).

So, if your full number is "12345@ekiga.net", your User Name is "12345" and your Registrar is "ekiga.net".  

If I had to guess, "security check failed" probably just means you're failing registration because of a problem with names/password.  Hope that helps you sort it out ....

### Post by OneSeventeen on 2006-08-01
Great, that does work, but to spell it out for other newbies like me:
After you start ekiga, before you make any calls, go to Edit>Accounts, then Add one.

If you registered with ekiga.net (as I did), then you simply give it an account name, such as "ekiga.net" or "oneseventeen@ekiga.net" or something like that, then set the registrar to "ekiga.net", user "your username", and your password, then click OK.

Once it loggs in, click close, and you can make calls!
