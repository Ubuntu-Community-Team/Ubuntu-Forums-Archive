### Post by Dennis N on 2015-01-19
It can be done with existing controls:

After setting the panel's sound menu volume level at maximum (100), open pulse audio volume control, open the "output devices" tab and you can raise the level further (to about 150%).

### Post by Dennis N on 2015-01-19
Easy way to launch Pulse Audio volume control: click on panel volume icon > sound settings.
Once you set it over 100% in sound settings, it should keep your chosen setting. Mine does. No need for a check box.
