### Post by Hashte on 2007-09-16
If your Gnome desktop freezes just after the login or after pressing one of the icons on the panel, making all the environment nonrespondent (and only the mouse can be still moved), then try to **reinstall ** *gnome-panel* in Synaptic Package Manager :D (search for *gnome-panel* in Synaptic, click on it and select "**Mark for Reinstallation**"). That will be the solution for this type of freeze in 90% of the cases!

I hope that this would help (it helped for me ;-)).
