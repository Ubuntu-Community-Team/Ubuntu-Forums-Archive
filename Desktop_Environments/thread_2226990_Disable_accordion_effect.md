### Post by deadflowr on 2014-05-30
Disable 3d-compositing.
Figure out how to do that, and you should lose the accordion effect.

That said, I prefer the accordion to the long line of apps, that run on forever.
If you have 50+ apps on the launcher, it's quicker to pinpoint the one you want.
(note, most users don't need or have 50+ apps, but adding as many is possible)
