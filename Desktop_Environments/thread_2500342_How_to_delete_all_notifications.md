### Post by TheFu on 2024-08-30
The DE used is where you should look for solutions. There are addons that can mute notifications forever or for 1 hour or other time periods.  Look for Gnome-Shell addons for this.  Some ideas if you use Gnome: [https://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2019/05/gnome-shell-new-notifications](https://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2019/05/gnome-shell-new-notifications)  and 
[https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/3843/just-perfection/](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/3843/just-perfection/)


The other solution is to NOT use a DE that supports notifications, but that would be drastic for most people.  That's what I do.  **fvwm2** without any DE at all doesn't support notifications.  Works well, but not for 99% of the world.
