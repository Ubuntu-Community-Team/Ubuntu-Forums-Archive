### Post by welshmike on 2012-04-23
After trying things out this is how I mounted and viewed my XP partition contents:

Dash home and type "Disk utility" (as search and without the quotes)
[IMG]http://www.winchesternw.org.uk/m1.png[/IMG]

Click Disk Utility icon
[IMG]http://www.winchesternw.org.uk/m2.png[/IMG]

Click Hard Disk

To mount XP volume click it then click Mount Volume

[IMG]http://www.winchesternw.org.uk/m3.png[/IMG]

XP will appear in the Launcher

Click its (hard drive) icon to see content
[IMG]http://www.winchesternw.org.uk/m4.png[/IMG]
