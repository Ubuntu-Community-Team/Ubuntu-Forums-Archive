### Post by AlexR1 on 2007-08-09
I am not 100% sure that this gonna work but let`s give it a shot.

Got to System > Preferences > Removable drives

under Multimedia tab on the bottom that says Portable Music players uncheck check box that says play music files when connected,and try to see will rythmbox will pop up,you can also delete command from edit box before you uncheck check box.
