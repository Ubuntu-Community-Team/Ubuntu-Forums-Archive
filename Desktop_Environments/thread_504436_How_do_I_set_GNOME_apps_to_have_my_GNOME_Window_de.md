### Post by luisromangz on 2007-07-19
I'm pretty can't, the window decoration is not handled by the aplication itself but by the window manager, so when you set the window decoration, your choosen window decoration is applied to all windows, as you can not have two window manager running at the same time.

So either you use KWin, with your KDE window decoration, or use metacity, with your GNOME window decoration, or of course Compiz/Beryl for all your windows, but you cannot have different window decorations without a pretty big hack unknown to me.
