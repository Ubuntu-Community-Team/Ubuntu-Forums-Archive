### Post by saftaplan on 2007-03-28
I don't think Ubuntu, or any other OS, can do anything about this. The KVM sits between you PC and the keyboard and it checks that you have pressed Scroll Lock directly from your keyboard, your computer doesn't send your keys to the KVM in any way. So even if you remap them, it won't switch your KVM.
So I think it's best to just use another keyboard.
