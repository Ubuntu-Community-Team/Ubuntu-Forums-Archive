### Post by quackquack on 2008-07-17
maybe deleting the trash applet from the bar and adding it again will work?

right click trash applet and click remove from panel

then right click anywhere on the bar and click add to panel, find deleted items in the list and drag it to the spot where the old trash can was.

hope this helps :)
