### Post by vanadium on 2013-10-23
It looks like critical information on the hard drive has been corrupted. You could try if the recovery program testdisk is able to recover the partition.

I hope you succeed in recovering your information. In the future, you should make sure to always have at least one backup copy of the data on a separate hard disk. Having the information only once simply is not enough. Independent of your operating system or eventual incidents, a hard disk may always break at any time.
