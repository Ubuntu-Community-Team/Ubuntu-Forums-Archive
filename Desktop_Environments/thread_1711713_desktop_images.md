### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-03-21
Under System>Preferences>Appearance>Background you will find two sets of desktop background images that act like a slide showing changing the desktop background image after a few minutes. Is this the kind of thing you are looking for? Each set comprises half of the background images available. This feature became available in Maverick when it was released.
