### Post by labinnsw on 2010-04-24
Your request is a little bit unclear but I will do my best to answer what I can. In doing so I will also indicate the areas that are not clear.

It is not clear if you are currently running Ubuntu or Mint or another Linux distro. If so you would want to [back up your stuff]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BackupYourSystem") before you reformat.

I take it you will dual boot Windows-Ubuntu or Windows-Mint. To share your stuff, simply keep them on Windows. Ubuntu/Mint will be able to read your Windows partition but Windows will not be able to read your Ubuntu/Mint partition.

Ubuntu 9s are already out, 9.04 and 9.10 the next release will be 10.04, and is scheduled for release around 29 April 2010. Ubuntu is released in 6 month cycles in April and October each year. The digits before the point indicate the year and after the point indicate the month. Mint on the other hand is released a bit later around a Month. The release numbers are incremented by 1. The current release is 8 so the next release will be 9.
