### Post by oeffentlich on 2007-12-11
If you are still interested in removing the seperators:
 just go to home folder, press ctrl+H to make hidden folders visible
then open folder called ".themes", choose your theme, open folder, go to folder "handlers", just change  the name of the disturbing handler
