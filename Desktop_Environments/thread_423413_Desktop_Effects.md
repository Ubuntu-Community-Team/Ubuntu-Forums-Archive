### Post by nmincone on 2007-06-02
Killtown... I had the same problem with the cube effect disappearing on me. Goto your CLI (terminal) and type;
#sudo apt-get install gnome-compiz-manager
When its installed, goto System and choose 'GL Desktop' turn cube effect off and back on again. It will be re-enabled. You'll also have access to some other compiz features not available without 'GL Desktop' installed. Have fun!
