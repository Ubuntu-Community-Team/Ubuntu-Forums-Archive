### Post by duminas on 2005-08-29
[QUOTE=cham0169]i am in need of sawfish on my system. when i try to get rid of metacity, ubuntu-desktop doesnt like it. is it safe to get rid of ubuntu-desktop? if not, is there any alternative? i need my xmms keybindings in sawfish. i NEED them...[/QUOTE]ubuntu-desktop is merely a *metapackage*. That is, it itself does nothing except depend on a bunch of other packages.

In this case, ubuntu-desktop is totally unnecessary. You can remove it safely, but when you kick up to Breezy (at any time), you'll need to reinstall this metapackage.
