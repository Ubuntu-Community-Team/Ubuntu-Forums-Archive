### Post by matthew.lns1 on 2007-08-23
You can go to [gdesklets.org]("http://www.gdesklets.com"), click on archives and find the good weather desklet. It does what it's name suggests and has served me well. To install it go to file>install package and drag the .gz file you downloaded and drag it into the gDesklets shell window. To get the "Location Code" search your zip on [weather.com]("http://www.weather.com") and look in the URL for something like this "USCA0993". Cut and paste that number into the "Location Code" box.
