I have found a very useful extension that keeps the message window closed even if the mouse goes to the base of the screen. The window is activated by superkey M. ### Post by redrumrogue on 2014-09-18
Hi there - open the "Tweak Tool" and go to "Extensions".  Turn on the "Window list" extension.  This will put a bar at the bottom of the screen with an icon on it (blue icon bottom right corner) to open the message bar (same as pressing Super + M)
