### Post by Therion on 2009-06-16
As mentioned, turn OFF the "Snapping Windows" plugin.
Then **open** the Wobbly Windows plugin.  
There's a checkbox *in there* labeled "Snap Inverted".  
Clear it.  
THAT should solve the problem.
