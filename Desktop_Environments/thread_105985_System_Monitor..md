### Post by Zotova on 2005-12-19
Previously I would have said gnome as it is a lot faster to actually login and get to the desktop than kde is. However I have recently switched to kde and I've found that my laptop fan is now on a lower setting more. Before it was more likely to be on a higher setting and as a result more noisy.

Also when playing a game (openttd) I've found kde to be the better choice. In gnome the game was quite jerky, in kde it is very smooth. In the end it will be down to personal judgement. Gnome isn't awful by any means and isn't slow to the extent your waiting hours for a program to load - I've used it for around 6 months with no major complaints. However personally I now find kde to have the edge even though it does have more eye candy (which is weird... but I'm happy)
