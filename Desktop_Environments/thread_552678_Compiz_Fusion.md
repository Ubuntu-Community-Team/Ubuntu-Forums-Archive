### Post by ryno519 on 2007-09-16
> **ChopperX said:**
> what are the requirements for compiz fusion?

Basically, you just need to have direct rendering enabled with your graphics card. I use a 64MB integrated card and it works fine. To check if it's enabled, run the following command.

glxinfo | grep rendering

It should say yes. If not, look into getting your graphic card drivers installed. :)
