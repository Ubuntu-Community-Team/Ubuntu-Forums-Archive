### Post by benanzo on 2008-01-25
Unfortunately Metacity and Compiz are not entirely clear on how your existing workspaces are handled when switch from one window manager to the other.

If you're in Compiz then you can go to System -> Preferences -> Advanced CompizConfig Settings.  Then click the General Options button, then Desktop Size.  Here you can select how many workspaces to enable per row.  That will override whatever settings you had in Metacity and make all the workspaces accessible again.
