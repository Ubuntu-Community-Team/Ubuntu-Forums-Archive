### Post by kevxh on 2011-01-05
I'm shocked - I'm genuinely and completely shocked.
I looked at Xfce years ago and hated it, Kudos to all involved for the advancements that have been made.

Neither Unity nor Gnome Shell hold any appeal for me (and my Dell laptop isn't the most powerful beast on the planet) so I thought I'd have a look at Xfce as the Ubuntu future release doesn't appeal. After install it looked OK but I couldn't believe the performance gains.

So I downloaded the latest Xubuntu, backed up, clean install and prettied it up with Compiz, Emerald and Cairo (never been a fan of panels). All I can say is this is impressive. I know the packaged apps are intended to be lightweight but we all have our preferences, it took a little time to clean out the cruft and I'm so pleased with the end result. Even Eclipse is faster.

Loving it. Anyone else thinking of moving with the impending Unity issue?
