### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-09-05
CGWD is used in conjuntion with Compiz...do you have Compiz installed (it's a nifty, new composite manager)....CGWD is a front-end theme manager for Compiz...if you don't have Compiz, you can't use this theme....other themes can be downloaded and then simply "dropped" onto the Theme panel and they will install themselves.
