### Post by Medieval Cow on 2007-07-16
So I'm having a little trouble with my Kubuntu installation, and multiple Google/forum searches have proven fruitless. I have my taskbar set to auto-hide, and to come back when I bring my pointer to the bottom edge of the screen. I can't stand not having it set up like this, because then it's there all of the time taking up screen real estate, including the 99% of the time I don't need it. Drives me batty.

Anyway, several times I've had a program freeze up or crash, and if I bring up the taskbar while they're dying it will then re-hide when I mouse off, and then it won't come back anymore. Nothing I can do at that point will bring it back up, including bringing up the configuration dialogue from the command line and changing it off of auto-hide. Even logging out and back in, or restarting doesn't bring it back, except for a second when the desktop loads, at which point it then hides and won't come back. The only way to get it back is to dive the pointer down there while it briefly appears when the desktop is loading, bring up the settings, change it off of auto-hide, apply, and then change it back to auto-hide and it works again.

Am I the only one that has the problem? Does anyone know of any sort of fix? Or should I just be filing a bug report, or what? Thanks, guys.
