### Post by 3Miro on 2011-10-31
This is how to get pure KDE under 11.04. If you have pure KDE than only KDE will be upgraded and after an upgrade you will get pure KDE 11.10.

[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/purekdenatty](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/purekdenatty)

Having said that, upgrades are not always successful, so you should backup all important data from the HDD and be prepared to just do a clean install of Kubuntu 11.10.
