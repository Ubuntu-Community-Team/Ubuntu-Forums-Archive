### Post by coffeecat on 2007-06-07
Do you mean the Ubuntu logo with "Applications - Places - System" up at top left? If so you've managed to remove the main menu applet from the upper panel.

Right-click anywhere on the upper panel and choose "Menu Bar" to add to panel. To get it to the right place, simply right-click on it and select move. Move the mouse sideways and it will move with you and then left-click to leave it in place.

**Edit:** While you're about it, if you haven't come across the 'Add to Panel' utility, have a look around in it. There's all sorts of useful panel apps and you can add launchers for apps in the main menus, or make your own custom launchers if you need to.
