Regardind to add more accounts to evolution, you simply click on edit --> preferences--> mail accounts, add, here it appears a window where you can configure more accounts, you click on forward and in the following window introduce the new e mail address for other acount, then you only have to complete the proccess with the appropriate data for the new account.
