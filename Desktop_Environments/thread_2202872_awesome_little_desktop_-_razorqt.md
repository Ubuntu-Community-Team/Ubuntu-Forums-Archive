### Post by ventrical on 2014-02-04
Thanks for all your replies.  I am still using it now. Like Lubuntu devel it is operating very well here. It is very fast and does not present smirched characters on older Intel Graphics chipsets. I am also assuming it may even be better for overclocking.  I didn't notice it in the last dev release in the repos , but it is there now and I thought I would try it.

It does have a bug where it will not open screenshotxx.png files from the desktop. Probably more bugs too .. but it works nice so far.

Also the smooth scrolling in ff is more 'smoother' and not ever so slightly chunky as with Unity (on this one particular brand  of PC). Intel DQ965GF
