### Post by mcduck on 2009-05-28
Two. Although not in the default configuration, I use one panel on top with all the normal stuff (menubar, window list, workspace switcher, notification area & clock) and the other one on the right side with program launchers in it.

I've tried pretty much every available dock program and none of them have been even near the usability of normal Gnome-panel (and most don't even support placing them on the side of the screen which is the best place on a relatively small resolution widescreen display).

I uplay around with my desktop configuration a lot, but this seems to be the setup to which I always return in the end.
