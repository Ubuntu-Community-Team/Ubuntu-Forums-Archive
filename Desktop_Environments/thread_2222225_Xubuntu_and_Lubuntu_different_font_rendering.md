### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-05-05
It is most likely a difference in the window manager (openbox) font settings in Lubuntu, and the font settings in Xubuntu ->Settings manager ->appearance tab.

It may also be the font rendering settings that are different in the two, ie the hinting and sub-pixel-order.
