### Post by benerivo on 2007-10-20
You don't need emerald. All you should need is the desktop effects enabled through Main Menu>System>Preferences>Appearance>Visual Effects. Then just install compizconfig-settings-manager. From this program, you can enable the cube and further desktop effects.
