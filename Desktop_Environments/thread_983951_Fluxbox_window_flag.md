### Post by Anzan on 2008-11-16
Yes, it's a tab that can be above the window or within it, If you middle-click on a title bar you can drag it onto the title bar of any other window and they will be grouped as tabs in a group. For example, you can have Evolution tabbed together with Firefox and OOWriter as a single window and click on each tab to get that specific app or simply ALT+TAB between them. It is a very useful feature.
