### Post by Ralph L on 2012-05-23
I installed the Clearwaita theme from [http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php?content=145210](http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php?content=145210) .  See the READ.ME file in the decompressed download for how to do the install.  I then used gnome-tweak-tool (Advanced Settings in the pull down menus) to set the theme to Clearwaita and the Icon theme to Ubuntu-mono-light.  This seemed to fix almost all my problems of no real scrollbars, no scrollbar stepper arrows/buttons, and poor contrast that caused me eye strain.  Clearwaita was a godsend.

Ralph
