### Post by morgan_greywolf on 2007-11-12
Enable Remote Desktop by going to **Preferences **-> ** Remote Desktop** and  check *both* **Allow users to view your desktop ** *and* **Allow users to control your desktop**.

It's probably a good idea to setup a password, too.

You can then connect to your Linux machine from your Windows machine via any VNC viewer application.  You can get one at [http://realvnc.com](http://realvnc.com)
