### Post by oshirowanen on 2010-05-19
I decided to give this a try.  I downloaded a minimal install cd of 10.04.  After the installation and making sure to choose the full disk encryption option, I logged in and installed the Lubuntu PPA.  A few moments later, I have a fully working Lubuntu with full disk encryption!

I can finally have Lubuntu with full disk encryption on my low spec'ed laptop, so I will feel a little less stressed knowing that if it gets stolen, the thief will have a good old time trying to decrypt it. :D

I will finally make the move when 10.04.1 is released.
