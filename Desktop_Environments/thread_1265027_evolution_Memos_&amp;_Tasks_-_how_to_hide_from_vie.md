### Post by 666f6f on 2010-03-13
You can adjust the width of panels/columns by clicking and dragging the gray panel dividers ([http://library.gnome.org/users/evolution/stable/ui-intro.html.en](http://library.gnome.org/users/evolution/stable/ui-intro.html.en)). In order to "hide" the Tasks and Memos panels/columns, drag their divider to the right-most edge of your screen. These positions should be "remembered".

I'm not sure how you can have evolution start in the Calendar view.
