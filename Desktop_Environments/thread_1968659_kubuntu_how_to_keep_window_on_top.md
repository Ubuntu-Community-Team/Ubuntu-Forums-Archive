### Post by PaulW2U on 2012-04-29
I haven't tried this but go to System Settings, Workspace Appearance, Window Decorations then Configure Buttons.

It looks like you can add, delete and move the buttons around.

### Post by SeijiSensei on 2012-04-29
You can do this by right-clicking the icon in the upper-left corner of the window and choosing Advanced, then Keep Above Others.
