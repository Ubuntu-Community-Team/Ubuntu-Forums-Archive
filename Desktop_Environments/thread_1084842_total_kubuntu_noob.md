### Post by Monsieur Gonzalez on 2009-03-02
Well, usually you can get new plasma themes right clicking on your "desktop" then Appearance settings, then Desktop theme, where you have the option to download from Kde-look.org.

If you are talking about windows decorations, icons and so on, there's not too much choice, Kde4.2 just hit stable. Other themes you'll find on kde-look.org are ment for kde3.x.
