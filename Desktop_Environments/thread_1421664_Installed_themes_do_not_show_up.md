### Post by mcduck on 2010-03-04
Click the Customize-button in the Themes-dialog. Most likely the themes you installed aren't complete theme sets (including GTK2 theme, Metacity theme and Icon theme) so they won't show inn the Themes dialog directly. When you click the Customize button you'll be able to select individual theme components separately and the themes you installed should show there.
