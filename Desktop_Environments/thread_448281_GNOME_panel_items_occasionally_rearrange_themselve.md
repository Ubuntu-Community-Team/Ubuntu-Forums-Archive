### Post by CSMatt on 2007-05-19
I have noticed that on occasion items in GNOME's panels will occasionally rearrange themselves upon a restart, particulary when there is a group of several of them next to each other.  This often happens when a new item is added or moved.  Sometimes it happens for no reason at all, but it usually takes place when items are recently added or moved.  It only happens when GDM starts up after being shut down.  This ironically happens more often when items are locked to the panel rather than if they are unlocked, although it occasionally happens to unlocked items too.

This has happened to me with Dapper, Edgy, and Feisty.  It also happened on my previous laptop as well as my current one.  Has anyone else had this happen?
