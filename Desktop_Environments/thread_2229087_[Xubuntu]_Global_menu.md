### Post by slickymaster on 2014-06-11
Following a decision, [voted on 2014-02-13]("http://ubottu.com/meetingology/logs/xubuntu-devel/2014/xubuntu-devel.2014-02-13-19.01.log.html#l-216"), the Xubuntu Development Team decided to drop the old application menu replacing it by Whisker Menu.

You you wish to have the old application menu instead of whisker menu, all you have to do is to right click on the panel and navigate to **Panel > Panel Preferences > Items**. Once there, select Whisker Menu and press the red '**X**&#8203;' on the right to remove it.
Then press '**+**' on the right for new item, select _Applications Menu_ and finally press '**Add**'. Use the up and down arrows to position it where you want.
