### Post by TransitMan on 2006-10-05
If you are going to follow the instructions on your post about installing kubuntu-desktop on top of your Ubuntu install, then no, you do not need to re-install your modem.
Whatever worked under Ubuntu will work when you get Kubuntu installed, as they will reside on the same drive, and share many of the same libraries/folders.
