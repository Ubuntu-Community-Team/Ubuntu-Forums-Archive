### Post by Dennis N on 2013-07-18
There is a command in the menu when you right-click on the desktop: **arrange desktop icons**. It collects any loose icons together into full vertical columns left to right. 

That said, I think desktop icons auto-align into a rectangular grid by default. Mine do. In fact I see no way to turn that behavior off. I can manually place them elsewhere on the screen, but they will always center in the grid cells that are outlined as you drag them around. If you then use the arrange option, they fill back into columns. How do yours behave?
