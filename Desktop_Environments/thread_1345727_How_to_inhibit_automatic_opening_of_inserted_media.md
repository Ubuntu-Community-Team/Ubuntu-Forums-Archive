I don't know of a way to change the setting for flash drives only. You may want to open Nautilus and navigate to Edit > Preferences > Media. I have unchecked both the "Browse Media When Inserted" and "Never Prompt or Start Programs on Media Insertion" options, and I don't get a window when inserting a flash drive.
