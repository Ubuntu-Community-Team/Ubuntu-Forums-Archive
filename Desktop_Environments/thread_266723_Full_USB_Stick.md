### Post by wombat20 on 2006-09-27
Have you tried emptying the wastebasket?

To be sure: stick the disk in and open it up to view the (presumably) empty folder.  Press CTRL+H to see all hidden folders.  If you see one called .Trash-username then try deleting that.

Failing that you could try to reformat the USB disk.
