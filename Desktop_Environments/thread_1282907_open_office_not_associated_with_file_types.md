### Post by x C0MMAND0 x on 2009-10-05
If you have a .doc or .anything file, you can do the following.

1. Right click on document
2. Go to the "open-with" tab
3. Select the program you are looking for.

Tip:
If program is not listed, you can type it in manually.

Open office "word" - ooo-writer
Open office "powerpoint" - ooo-impress
Open office "excel" - ooo-calc

I hope that answers what you were looking for...
