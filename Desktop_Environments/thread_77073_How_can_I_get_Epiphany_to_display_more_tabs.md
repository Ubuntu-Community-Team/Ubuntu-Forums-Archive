### Post by doclivingston on 2005-10-16
There isn't currently a way of changing it to the Firefox behavour, because that's how GTK's notebook widget works.

I know of three different ways of dealing with the "more tabs than will fit" problem, each with their own disadvantages:
* Scrolled tab bar (a la Epiphany). You can't see all the tabs at once.
* Shrinking tab labels (a la Firefox). Labels are unreadable when there are too many.
* Multi-row tab bar (a la Windows). Hard to use and has _very_ bad usability problems.
