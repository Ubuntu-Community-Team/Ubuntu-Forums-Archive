### Post by howefield on 2010-11-02
Not sure if you are talking about the Dodge plugin ?

CompizConfig Settings Manager > Animations > Focus Animation > highlight the line in the Animation Selection field and press edit. Select Dodge as the Focus Effect, set the timer and close your way back out.

Don't know about your second question.
