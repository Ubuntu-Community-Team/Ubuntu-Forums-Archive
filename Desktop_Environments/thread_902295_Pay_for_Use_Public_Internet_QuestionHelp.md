### Post by ProgramErgoSum on 2008-08-27
Not a s/w solution...but, make users pay *before* like the cold-drink (Coke, Pepsi, etc.) vending machines. Since you are charging by the hour, I guess you are "metering" the usage too. So, at the end of a session, you would probably be able to compute the balance (unused part, that is).

The actual mode of payment can remain the same.
