### Post by Heart on 2005-07-19
Yes, DualHead works now on Kubuntu Hoary with my G400!

Downloaded the linux driver vom matrox homepage and then I just copied the 6.8.1 driver mga_drv.o and mga_hal_drv.o to /usr/X11R6/lib/modules/drivers/ ...

Then make your changes in the xorg.conf file.
