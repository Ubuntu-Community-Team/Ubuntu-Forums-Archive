### Post by coffeecat on 2009-07-18
Beauty being in the eye of the beholder, you'll have to decide for yourself what is "aesthetically pleasing". :wink: Anyway, a few links for you.

There's Gnome Art, but it's a bit neglected these days. Most of the themes were developed some years ago. That's not a bad thing, but some of their themes don't work properly in the newer versions of Gnome. It's particularly a problem with some of their icon themes. You can find it here:

[http://art.gnome.org/](http://art.gnome.org/)

Go to the themes tab.

Also, ubuntu-art.org:

[http://www.ubuntu-art.org/](http://www.ubuntu-art.org/)

A site with links to 10 icon sets:

[http://www.junauza.com/2009/05/10-awesome-icon-sets-for-ubuntugnome.html](http://www.junauza.com/2009/05/10-awesome-icon-sets-for-ubuntugnome.html)

From which I found the next site, and I particularly like some of the icon sets. But that's my personal taste.

[http://cine9.free.fr/index.php/gnome/](http://cine9.free.fr/index.php/gnome/)
