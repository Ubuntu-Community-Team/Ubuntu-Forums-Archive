[QUOTE=Hairy_Palms]im not sure what unroll means, do u mean highlight when u just hold the mouse over things?[/QUOTE]
It's called shade in KDE, it can be set so that when you double click a titlebar the window will "rollup" into the titlebar so that the titlebar is all that is showing.  Kind of like a window shade.  In gnome, one would have to dbl click the titlebar again to unroll the window to it's normal state.  In KDE, there is a shade setting that will unshade the window (unroll) when the mouse hovers over the titlebar.  That is what I am looking for.  Gnome will only unroll AFAIK with a dbl click or a click on the taskbar button, a PITA IMHO.
[QUOTE=Hairy_Palms]also give links to details about the theme so that people know what sort of thing you want. 
[/QUOTE] The B II theme is stock in KDE 3.x so if you have KDE installed, take a peek under window decorations.  If you don't look here
