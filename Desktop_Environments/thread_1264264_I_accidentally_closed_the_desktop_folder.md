### Post by Raboch on 2009-09-12
Click the weird symbol in the right corner of your desktop and click "Add widgets". In the window's search type "Folder view" and double click the result for Folder view. Then right click inside the folder view and choose "Folder view settings". On the window, under "Location", click "Show the Desktop folder".

Then to avoid closing it accidentally again you can click the weird symbol again and choose "Lock widgets". If you do that and want to add more things later you can click the symbol again and unlock the widgets.
