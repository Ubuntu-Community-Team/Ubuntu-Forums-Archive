### Post by Krytarik on 2011-01-24
You have distinguish clearly between tabs, which are inside a program to switch between its own "tasks", opened website, text files, whatever, and tasks, which are representing multiple programs or multiple instances of a program.

If you then really want to preview the tasks at the taskbar, then "CCSM -> Extras -> Window Previews" would do it. If of course you can't enable "Visual Effects" in "Appearance" due to the limitations of your hardware or incorrect driver/config, then this plugin will not work.
