### Post by Paerez on 2006-07-12
It is personal preference, but here is how I do it:

I have the tree on the left in the side bar, and I use icon view.

If there are a lot of files in the directory (like I am looking at /etc) then I switch to list view to sort or scroll quickly.

I especially like icon view for my media directories for the image and video previews (althought you have to click a file to get its size, which is the drawback).

Its totally personal preference, though. Both are good :-D
