### Post by ShogunMaster06 on 2007-10-03
Yes, there is a menu editor in Gnome.  You should find it under Preferences and Menu Editor.  You will just need to navigate to Applications and Office and there you will be able to uncheck Evolution.

You can also rearrange/rename any other menu options from here as well.

Enjoy!
