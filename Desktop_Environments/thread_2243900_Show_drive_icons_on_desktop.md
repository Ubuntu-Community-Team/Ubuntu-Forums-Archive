### Post by Eric_Roch on 2014-09-11
I was searching around to figure out how to make my USB show up on the desktop, and I came across a thread that put me on the right track.  The thread is closed so I can't post my findings, but I will do that here for anyone else trying to find it.

Simply right click on a blank part of the desktop --> click **Desktop Preferences** --> select the **Desktop Icons** tab --> make sure the third box (**Show connected volumes on the desktop**) is selected.  You should now see any USB thumbdrives, HDDs, or whatever as long as they have been mounted.
