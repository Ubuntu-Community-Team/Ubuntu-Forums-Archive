### Post by H.E. Pennypacker on 2006-08-05
> 2. Which apps do I need to install to be able to receive a cable television signal (MythTV?) and record tv shows to one of the hard drives.

As far as I know, MythTV does not pull any TV signals, but it can record TV.  I know of one guy who used VLC to watch television shows.  In any case, you'll need something like MythTV.  Go with MythTV because it is the most popular one.

Do yourself a favor and buy a large harddrive (for TV/DVD, I'd go atleast 100GB to really enjoy yourself).
