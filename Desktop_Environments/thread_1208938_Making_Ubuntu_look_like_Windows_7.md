### Post by UbuntuNerd on 2009-07-09
to install that theme you need to unpack it first, in fact I had to extract it twice to get to the theme. After you extract it for the second time look for "aero-clone.tar.gz" drag it to your desktop and then right click on an empty space of your desktop scroll to the bottom and click on (Change Desktop Background) when the menu opens make sure you have (Theme) selected and drag the "theme folder" to the appearance Preference box to install it.
