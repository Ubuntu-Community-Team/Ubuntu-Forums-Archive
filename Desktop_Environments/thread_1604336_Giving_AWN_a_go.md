### Post by Frogs Hair on 2010-10-23
When you open an application and the icon appears on the dock , right click and add as launcher. Right click on the bottom of the dock and select dock preferences and  you will find settings , themes and applets. You can also right click icons to customize them with at set you have found on the net.
