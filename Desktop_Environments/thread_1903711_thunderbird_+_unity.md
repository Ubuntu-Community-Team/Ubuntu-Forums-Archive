No scrolling bars, no way to move the window under the upper boundary of screen. Any way to solve this?

---

### Post by coffeecat on 2012-01-03
This will work with any application window that spills off the bottom of the screen. Hold down the alt key. Now left click and hold down the mouse button anywhere over the open window. You can now move the window with the mouse above the upper panel, revealing the lower border of the window. Once you've revealed the lower-right corner of the Thunderbird window you will be able to reduce it in size so that it fits on your screen and next time it should re-open the same smaller size.
