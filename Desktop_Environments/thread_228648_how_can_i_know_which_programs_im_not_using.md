### Post by orb9220 on 2006-08-03
Run synaptic
Go to settings filters and select new give it a name like insalled. 
Then click on Deselect All. Now click on Installed as one of the checked items. Click Ok

Now at the bottom of synaptic click the custom buttom and you will see your installed as one of your choices. Click there you go all your installed packages.

Then I use my knogin and if the description of the program dosn't turn on the little light in my head then I probablly don't need it.

Hope this helps a bit.
