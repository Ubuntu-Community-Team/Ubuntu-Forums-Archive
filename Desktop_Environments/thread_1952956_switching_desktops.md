### Post by Lloydb39 on 2012-04-05
I have Ubuntu 11.10 on a desktop, with Unity. I'd like to try Xfce, which I have on a laptop with Xubuntu. Is there an easy way to switch around the various environments, without screwing everything up as I tend to do? Everything is working now, amazingly.

---

### Post by 2F4U on 2012-04-05
You could install either of the packages xubuntu-desktop or xfce4. The package xubuntu-desktop installs additional programs while the package xfce4 installs just the XFCE desktop. You will be given an option on the logon screen to choose your desktop environment.
