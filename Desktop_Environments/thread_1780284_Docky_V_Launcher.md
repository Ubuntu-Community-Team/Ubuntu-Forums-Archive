### Post by CryptSphinx on 2011-06-14
Ok 

so first log into ubuntu classic

go into system -> preferences -> startup applications

and make sure docky is ticked
close startup applications.

then remove the panels 

right click on the panel => delete this panel 
do this to both of them

then launch docky

alt+f2 => docky

click on the docky symbol on the left of the new launcher
to bring you to settings

make sure "manage windows without launcher" is ticked

heres the link to the docky wiki

[http://wiki.go-docky.com/index.php?title=Welcome_to_the_Docky_wiki]("http://wiki.go-docky.com/index.php?title=Welcome_to_the_Docky_wiki")
