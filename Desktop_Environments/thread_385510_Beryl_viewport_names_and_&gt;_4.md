### Post by eXcentra on 2007-03-15
I don't know about the viewport naming but for the number of workspaces, go to the Beryl Settings Manager. Under "General Options" in the "Main" tab (should be the very first thing that shows up), go down to "Horizontal Virtual Size" and adjust accordingly.
