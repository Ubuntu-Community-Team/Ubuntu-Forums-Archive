### Post by deadflowr on 2015-10-11
Yep, either mate or gnome-session-flashback for the top panel.
The launcher is called docky.

An alternative to docky is cairo-dock.
cairo-dock is more malleable, imo.
And will also include a login session, which docky doesn't do.

But both very similar.

all are available in the software center.
listed as
**mate-desktop** for the mate ddesktop
**gnome-session-flashback** for the gnome flashback sessions
**docky** for the dock
and **cairo-dock** for the cairo-dock

Play around, and have fun with it.
