### Post by oldblue on 2005-10-13
1.  Yes, I am assuming it is.  The reason most likely being that typing ALT-F2 is faster.  Though I think many people are unaware of that.  That and in theory any application you install should be made available to you through the menu, and if not they may consider that a bug in the application.

2.  Probably a usability decision.  Some people may not understand the difference, and in not understanding, become uncertain.  It now default to Smart Upgrade, which can be changed in the Preferences.
