### Post by linux-hack on 2010-10-27
Yes you can do that on the same machine. The server is just a program (process) that runs on you machine. You can even have more servers on the same one :).. But at place of XAMPP try may be LAMP

You can also be more careful and may be make a virtual server on your desktop. This way you test how it works. Look at **apparmor **also before making your server or **chroot**.
