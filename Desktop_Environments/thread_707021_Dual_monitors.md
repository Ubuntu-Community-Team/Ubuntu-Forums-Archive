### Post by schmolch on 2008-02-25
I use a tri-monitor setup and my advise would be to not save on the Monitors.
Dont get cheap Monitors with a TN-Panel.
Those have bad viewing angles and bad colors,
While this might not be an issue if you have only one, you will be bugged by it as soon as you have 2 side by side (different colors, different contrast).
Almost all 22" have those cheap panels, good 20/21" Monitors would be preferable.
Depends on your budget of course.

Regarding the video-cards it depends on the status of the ati opensource driver.
I use nvidia-cards but if possible i would prefer ati.
