What is the best way to uninstall/disable wireless internet capability in Ubuntu 5.10?

---

### Post by ronmarley1 on 2006-03-30
Hi whoa_551,
Go to System -->Administration -->Networking.  It'll ask your password.  Click on your wireless connection and then click Deactivate.  The next time you log off/shut down, click the box in front of "Save current setup."
