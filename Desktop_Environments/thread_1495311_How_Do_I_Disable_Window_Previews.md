### Post by hok00age on 2010-05-28
> **dewey_daniels said:**
> Pretty simple same as the title.I enabled them but I dont know out to disable them.

Run CompizConfig Setting Manager:
1. Hit ALT + F2 and type ccsm
2. Once ccsm main window opened, in category click Extras
3. At the right pane uncheck "Window Previews"

Hope it works! :)
