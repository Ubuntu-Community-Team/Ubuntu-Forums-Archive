### Post by thrift on 2005-05-31
Just because the live version works off the DVD doesn't mean the media is 100% ok.  Also, a lack of scratches does not mean the DVD is ok.  The only way to really tell is to get an md5 hash of the media and compare that to the md5 hash the media is supposed to be, if they are the same your media is ok and then I would submit a bug report if you can't find anyone else who is having your problem.  

Unfortunately I don't know what the problem could be if not media.  Until proven otherwise I would assume it is the DVD.
