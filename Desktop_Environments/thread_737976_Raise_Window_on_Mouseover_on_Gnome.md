### Post by Bölvaður on 2008-04-02
Well I can just tell you how my settings used to be when I was lazy and didn't want to click mouse buttons.


System -> Preferences -> Windows

[x] Select windows when the mouse moves over them
[x] Raise selected windows after an interval

Interval before raising : 0.3 seconds


By that you can type in what ever window you have your mouse over at the moment the cursor is over the window. It will not be raised unless you have your mouse over it for 0.3 (which is for me long enough not to select every window there is on the screen). It should be short enough time for your brain not to feel too long lag (0.15 seconds is seen as almost no lag for our human brains).

If that is not good enough, and you want to have only the titlebar, I know who to contact.... GNOME. Im sure they'll listen and implement it in the future releases if you'd ask.
