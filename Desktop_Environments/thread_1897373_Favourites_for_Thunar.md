### Post by LewisTM on 2011-12-19
1. Can't be done, sorry. Use Gigolo to bookmark network locations.

2. Manage bookmarks in the side pane . Ctl-B for Bookmarks, Ctl-T for the tree view. You can drag and drop, remove and rename them. To get to bookmarks quickly, just click the drop down button on the location bar.

Cheers!
