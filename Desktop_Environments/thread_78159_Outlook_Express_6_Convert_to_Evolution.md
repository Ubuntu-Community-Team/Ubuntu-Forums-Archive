### Post by khantozavri on 2005-10-18
Hi,

I suggest using thunderbird on windows machine to import from outlook express, than in evolution in Ubuntu select file>import>forward>single file> and navigate to mail directory in thunderbird profile on windows partition and select folders you want to import (i.e. inbox, sent, from john etc.) Keep in mind that mail files are the ones without an extension.

The process is well documented in Evolution help under "migration from outlook to evolution".

I've done this number of times and works flowlesly, though importing 1GB of mail is about 2-3 hours of task depending on your hard drive and machine speed (I have laptop and HDD is a bit slow).

Good luck, :)
