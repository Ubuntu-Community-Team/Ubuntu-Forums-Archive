### Post by Xian on 2006-02-28
[QUOTE=geuis]How can I check the hardware my system is using and how can I find and install different drivers?[/QUOTE]

There are different terminal commands you can use to find out about your system info, but there is a great GUI application that will do this for you, as well a benchmark your system, and it's called [HARDINFO](http://hardinfo.berlios.de/wiki/index.php/Main_Page). It is available in the Breezy universe repo and will make your life much easier in this regard.

Based on that you can proceed to drivers.
