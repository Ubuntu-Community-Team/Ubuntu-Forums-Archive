### Post by undershepherd1 on 2006-09-01
Ok, something short. I have noticed that my graphics are faster with Compiz. the glxgears test shows an increase in fps. when a window is maximized, you can grab a corner and "peel" it back, to reveal what's underneath. Right click menus wobble into place, as well as tool tips and pop up menus. When you move a window, it wobbles. None of the plugins are "that great," but it just gives the OS a different feel from Windows. I commented to my wife the other day that windows has become "boring." On the other hand, I have noticed that the cooling fan often runs at increased speed, which to me indicates more CPU time.

### Post by iamhugeinjapan on 2006-09-01
To me it just gives a smoother more fluid look and feel. You don't realise how clunky moving around windows was until they are powered by GL.

Even subtle bits of transparency make you feel like you're using a more modern system. 

The mac style expose effect to see all windows at once is nice and smooth.

So it doesn't slow down my computer at all, it makes it faster, I have a good graphics card pretty much wasted when just doing browsing and chat. Why not put it to good use?


edit: I disable most wobbling because it can get a bit much when you're doing lots of menu work.
