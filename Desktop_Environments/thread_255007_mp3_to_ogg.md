### Post by meng on 2006-09-10
I'm sure it can be done, but the first question is are you sure you want to do this? mp3 is a lossy format, ogg is a lossy format, so converting one to the other would be a doubly lossy format, i.e. horrible sound quality. Ideally you'd re-rip your music to ogg format.
