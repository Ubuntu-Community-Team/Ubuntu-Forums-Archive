### Post by GoldBuggie on 2005-12-17
1) right-click on kicker(the panel you currently have)
2) choose add new panel 

thats basically it.

move the panel to whatever location you want tweak it in "configure panel". If you want your taskbar to be on that on just remove it from the current panel and add it to your new one. Add icons change size etc ... change it to your liking. Have fun.
