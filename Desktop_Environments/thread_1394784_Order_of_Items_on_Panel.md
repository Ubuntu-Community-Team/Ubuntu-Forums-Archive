### Post by NightwishFan on 2010-01-31
This will likely soon be addressed in the next releases of Gnome. As of right now, right click each applet you want to rearrange and untick "Lock To Panel". Then you can move the applets with the middle mouse button.

I have this problem with resolution changes sometimes.

### Post by clevertomato on 2010-02-01
I stumbled on how to move the portion in question. I couldn't get the panel options (i.e. lock to panel) when right clicking on the wireless, bt, or audio icons, but when I put the pointer right over the separator for those and then right clicked, it gave me the panel options, which allowed me to unlock, then drag those icons back where they belong.

Ahhhh. It feels good to fix things even when they are small things.
