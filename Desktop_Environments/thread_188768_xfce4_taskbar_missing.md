### Post by Freer on 2006-06-04
I just upgraded to 6.06 and everything went great, except the taskbar for XFCE4 has gone missing. 

The Taskbar doesn't show up in the Xfce Settings Manager either. Any suggestions on how to get it? Thanks!

---

### Post by steini on 2006-06-04
Jup, it's gone. But you now have an option called "Panel" in the settings manager. Use this one to create a second panel, and place it where you want your taskbar to be. Then click on that new panel, choose "add new item", and select "task list". There's your taskbar again ;)
