### Post by mcduck on 2007-06-28
1 panel just is not big enough for Gnome's menu bar, deskbar, windowlist, application launchers and all the other stuff I need in my panel.

I have one panel on top, and then one on the right side containing launchers for applications. The main panel is on top because most window menus and such are on top of the screen, so this way I don't need to move my mouse that much. And the launcher panel is on side as I have widescreen display so there's more extra horizontal space available..

So it's not Gnome's default 2-panel setup but 2 panels anyway ;)
