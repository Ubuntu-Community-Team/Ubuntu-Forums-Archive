### Post by quirks on 2008-01-01
Judging by your post, I assume, you have already selected the correct paper format. If not go to "Format -> Page ...", select the "Page" tab and select "DL" as paper format. Then change the orientation to "Landscape".

In case you have already done this, you can generate a preview of what OpenOffice.org sends to your printer: go to "File -> Print ..." and make sure the box "Print to file" is checked. Now click on "OK". Choose a destination location for the Postscript file and open it in Evince. This will give you a look at what is sent to your printer.

If everything seems ok in the Postscript preview, then probably the printer driver does not work properly or your printer. In any case, what I usually do in such a situation is, I fiddle around with the page margins of the OpenOffice.org document to position the addresses to where I want them to be. It's not optimal, but at least it works.
