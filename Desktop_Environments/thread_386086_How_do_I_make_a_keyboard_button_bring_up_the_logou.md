### Post by jhenager on 2007-03-16
If your BIOS supports it, you can startup the computer by hitting the Power button, and that will also bring up the shutdown screen, allowing you to log off, restart or Shut Down Completely.
That's how I have mine setup. Didn't have to do anything in Ubuntu. Just enabled the Power key in the BIOS.
