### Post by mcduck on 2010-12-27
Many of the themes in art.gnome.org are very old and use an outdated syntax for the theme definition files and image filenames.

In other words the themes are outdated, unmaintained and don't work any more.

While it's possible to manually go through the theme and rename all the files & edit the index file accordingly, it's quite a work to do. I recommend just forgetting about art.gnome.org and getting your icons from gnome-look.org instead. it's better maintained and has a larger selection of theme files anyway.
