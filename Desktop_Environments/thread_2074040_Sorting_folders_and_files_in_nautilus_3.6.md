### Post by x-shaney-x on 2012-10-20
Next to the toolbar buttons that alter the view (list or icon view), the is a downwards pointing arrow.

Click that and then select "By Type".

Or a better way:

Click the "Files" title on the panel, next to "Activities" then select "Preferences".
On the first tab there is an option - "Sort folders before files"
Check the box.

I just wish I could sort the sidebar so it shows Bookmarks above Devices.
I should imagine that many people would use their bookmarked location far more than they would click on the devices.
