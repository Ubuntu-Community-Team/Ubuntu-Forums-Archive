### Post by shawnc_nmb on 2007-11-08
With the new release of KUbuntu  I personally, didn't like using Dolphin over Konqueror as I did not find any way to add the actions back into it like I used to have.  Since I use depend KSVN, I personally required the actions area to be there.   Since I could not find informaiton anywhere about making new actions for Dolphin here is how I personally removed it all thru the GUI so no terminal commands for those who are weak-knee'd about those kind of things.


First press **<ALT>-F2**  this Will bring up your Run Command Window

Now type ( or copy and paste as I would do ) the following into it
[LIST]
[*]**KCONTROL**
[/LIST]

Now This will open up your KDE settings manager for all kinds of stuff but,  We are currently looking for
**KDE Components**  it will be in the very Far left menu,  maybe half way down the screen.  Click on this and it will open up afew more options,  now look for and Click on **File Associations**.


Ok,  now in The middle Window,  ( you should have a Left, Middle and Right window ) scroll down until your find **inode **and Once there Click to open that one.



Now inside of here, You need to open **directory** and **system directory** To make the changes, Once inside of one of them, Look at the last box and Click on ** Konqueror** then on the next set of options to the right, There is Move Up, Move Down and some others,  Just click on Move Up until it is all the way to the top.   Repeat this step for Both Directory and System Directorys and now you are finished.

And thats it. While your in here you can also adjust you file associations to open your default applications by looking in the applications tab.
