The first thing I would do is to start Open Office from the terminal - "ooffice -writer" (quotation marks for clarity only - don't use them) will start the Word Processing program.  Hopefully you will get an error message in the terminal and that may give you a clue as to what is going on.

You could also try un-installing and re-installing Open Office as a last resort.
