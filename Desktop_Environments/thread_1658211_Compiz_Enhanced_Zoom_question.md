### Post by mcduck on 2011-01-02
No, it can't zoom out.

What you actually should do is check the resolution you are using (the screen's physical size is meaningless, and it's not really important if the computer even recognizes the display at all). And also check the settings on the display/TV you are using, most have a mode that disables all image processing, and also tools for adjusting the image to location on the screen.

..actually any decent LCD TV will even have a menu option that automatically adjusts the image for you. These usually work very well, as long as you have configured your computer to use the display's native resolution..
