### Post by GTvulse on 2005-07-27
Hola Guilllermo,

I just saw this one from you... I hadn't remembered, since the other thread, that OpenOffice has its own font smoothing engine, with effects that sum up to the one used by X and the desktop manager. You need to go to the Tools/Options menu and then to the Openoffice.org/View tab. There turn off "Screen Font Aliasing" or tweak it to your taste.
