### Post by jackbuntu-common on 2010-08-23
On the top of every window, there is a bar that contains the name of the application as well as the red "exit" button and two gray buttons that "minimize" and "maximize" or "restore" the window.  Is there any way to reverse that bar so those buttons are on the right side instead of the left?

--Jack

### Post by mhgsys on 2010-08-23
Yes; 

Go to [COLOR="Blue"]System > Preferences >Appearance [/COLOR]
Click [COLOR="Blue"]Theme[/COLOR]

And select a different theme;

### Post by x19mark on 2010-08-28
Hi, Jack,

Yes, there sure is. I had the same problem. After some fumbling around, I discovered (to my surprise and delight) the answer in the Ubuntu Help Center. Just single-click the circular blue icon with the big white question mark, single-click "Customizing Your Computer", and single-click "How can I move the window buttons to the top right of the window?" (If you don't want to bother with all that, just look at the attached screen shot of Ubuntu Help Center's answer!)
