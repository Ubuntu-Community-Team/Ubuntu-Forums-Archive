### Post by cdanr on 2006-01-13
I finally solved the tilde problem on my machine by opening:

System>Preferences>Keyboard>Layouts

I had U.S. English and Japan as selected layouts with U.S. English as the default. I removed Japan and now the ~ is back to normal.

Good luck!
