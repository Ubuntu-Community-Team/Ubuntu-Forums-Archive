### Post by AnotherKevin on 2014-08-10
Howdy!  One old codger to another...  :-)

It's easy...  If it's an image you want to use, just make sure it's in your Pictures directory, right click on the desktop and choose "Default Desktop Settings" >> then choose Image from the dropdown list.  Other options are available from that dropdown...

Hope that helps!  One old codger to another...   ;-)
