### Post by jISh on 2006-09-02
You can change the Firefox fonts in it's Preferences.
I like the Bitstream Sans as my default application font.
Icons are in /usr/share/pixmaps.
You can change the desktop panels around, just right click and go to Properties, or remove them entirely. There's also a list of applets you can add to the panels, if you hit Add to Panel...

Good luck!
