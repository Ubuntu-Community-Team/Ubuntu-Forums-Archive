### Post by cwaldbieser on 2005-06-29
[QUOTE=picpak]Ok, I'm worried someone IS hacking my system...

I was just on Opera, and all of a sudden my mouse stalled to a halt...

I'm gonna run a virus scan right now...

What else should I do?[/QUOTE]
 Well, most viruses and malware tend to try and be sneaky and not give themselves away by making your mouse do fancy tricks on the screen.  If you are really worried, you could run a root kit finder from the LiveCD.  You could even try a LiveCD like Helix which is meant for computer forensics.

I would find it a little more likely that something is either wrong with your mouse or the drivers for it.  Has your PC showed any other strange behavior like sending out or receiving strange network traffic?
