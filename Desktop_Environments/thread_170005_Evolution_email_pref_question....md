### Post by davygravy on 2006-05-03
well, I guess I didn't search everywhere, because I found it actually pretty easy.

In Evolution Edit>Preferences>Mail Accounts (select the desired account) Edit>Defaults>Sending Messages> check the box in Composing Messages to always Bcc and enter your own email address.

Easy!
