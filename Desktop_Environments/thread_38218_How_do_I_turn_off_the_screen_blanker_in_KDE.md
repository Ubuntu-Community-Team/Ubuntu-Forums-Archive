### Post by SGC on 2005-05-30
Right click on the desktop and choose "configure desktop" then go to "screen saver" uncheck "start automatically"  

If you don't want the screen to turn black after while, open:
Control center > Peripherals > Display > power control

Check "enable display power managment" and then disable "standby after" , "suspend after", and "power off after"
