I ha&#1076; the same problem. Go to System Setings -> Keyboadr and Mouse -> Keyboard Shortcuts and scroll down to "Keyboard" and "Switch to the next keyboard layout". Mark "Custom" and press the radio button - now you can insert your own shortcut.
