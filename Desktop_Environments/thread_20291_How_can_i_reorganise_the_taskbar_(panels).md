### Post by Buffalo Soldier on 2005-03-16
[list]
[*] Delete one of the  panel.[list]
[*] Right click on the panel.
[*] Click *Delete This Panel...*[/list]
[*] Add applets that were on the deleted panel to the remaining panel.[list]
[*] Right click on the remaining panel.
[*] Click *Add to Panel...*
[*] Highlight the applets that you would like to add
[*] Click *Add*[/list]
[/list]
