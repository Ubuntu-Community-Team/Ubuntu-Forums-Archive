I'm having trouble with Kontact on my freshly installed Kubuntu system.  Initially, when I started Kontact, an error message would pop up mentioning a problem with libkleopatra and something about an invalid ELF header.  So I went about to launch Adept and found libkleopatra.  I reinstalled it to see if this would solve the problem.  Well now, Kontact just plainly crashes at launch with no special error messages.  How do I fix Kontact now?

---

### Post by mithion on 2007-06-02
Ok, good news.  Apparently, Kontact is a superset package of all the other (i.e. Kmail, Korganizer, etc...).  I tried reinstalling Kontact but that did not seem to fix anything.  What was actually the problem was with one of the subpackages, Kmail.  I simply reinstalled Kmail and now everything works fine.  Problem solved :D
