### Post by hodad on 2007-07-19
I can't seem to alphabetize my bookmark categories.  It is very frustrating to have to search through my 30 or so categories trying to find them, as they are not alphabetized.  A few days ago, I rearranged them in alphabetic order using "managing bookmarks", but I noticed today that they are back in their random order.  Is there a simple answer to this?  Thanks!

### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2007-07-19
While you have the Bookmarks manager dialog open, right click on any of the folders and select, "sort by name".  Unfortunately, you will have to do this in each folder as well if you want the actual bookmarks within each folder sorted too.
