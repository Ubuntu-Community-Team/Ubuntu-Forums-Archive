### Post by Minsky on 2009-09-15
This is a sort-of fix. Right-click an empty space on your desktop and de-select the **Keep Aligned** option in the pop-up menu that appears. Plug in your usb device and move its icon to the required position. Whenever you plug in that device again, its icon will always appear in that position.
