### Post by lobralleo on 2010-11-28
If you want to try something lighter, XFCE or LXDE might be even better for you.

XFCE is rather similar to GNOME, and you can obtain it by installing the xubuntu-desktop package. LXDE is featured in Lubuntu, however I am not sure whether Lubuntu is supported officially and available yet in the repositories.
For more information, you can check here:

[http://www.xubuntu.org/](http://www.xubuntu.org/)

[http://lubuntu.net/](http://lubuntu.net/)

and this can help to obtain a clean install:
