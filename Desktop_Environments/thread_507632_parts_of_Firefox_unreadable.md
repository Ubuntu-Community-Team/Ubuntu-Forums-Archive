### Post by jcarlock on 2007-07-25
I had a similar problem.  What I did was install and use a firefox theme that over rode some of the visual elements of the browser.  My guess is that you have a dark theme, so you may try redshift v2, which is what I am currently using.  If that does not work, I found a work around which lets you choose the colors, but a lot of sites look like crap, but you can read everything...

Choose 'Edit' -> 'Preferences' , click on 'Content' then choose 'Colors...' within the Fonts & Colors area.  In there you can choose to not use system colors, which if I recall, only helped somewhat, but I also unchecked 'Allow pages to choose their own colors, instead of my selections above'.  You may want to try combinations of all the things I mentioned above, specifically unchecking 'Use system colors' AND using a firefox theme, again I say RedShift V2 is pretty sweet for those who like dark themes.
