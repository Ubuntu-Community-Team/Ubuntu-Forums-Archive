### Post by sdennie on 2008-04-27
One thing you could try would be to go to Edit->Preferences->Content and then clicking the Advanced button.  Unchecking "Let web pages specify their own fonts" and changing Proportional from Serif to Sans Serif makes a noticeable difference for me.
