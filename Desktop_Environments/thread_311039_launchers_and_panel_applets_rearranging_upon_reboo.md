### Post by temcat on 2006-12-02
I've had that problem. Use Launchers List applet (from the quick-lounge-applet package) and put all your launchers in it. This way the launchers won't be rearranging. Also, Lauchers List is just more convenient to use - for example, you can drag-n-drop launchers using left mouse button as on Windows' QuickStart panel, and you don't have to freaking "lock" the launchers one-by-one. That said, you should lock Launchers List itself, as well as other plugins on the panel.
