### Post by Krytarik on 2011-08-27
Concluding that you are using classic Gnome, you can just right-click on the Notification Area, which holds the icons you are talking about, choose "Move", and then move it to where you like it. 'Just' means here you need to find a pixel where to get its context menu, and not those of the items it holds, which isn't easy most of the time. ;-)

Possibly you need to unlock it or the items between its current location and the desired location before. Re-located panel items are almost always (if not a bug or another error) the result of changing the screen resolution.

Greetings.
