### Post by gaz514 on 2005-09-19
Is there a way to stop Konqueror opening folders in tabs? Like when you double-click a folder on the desktop and you already have another folder open, it makes a new tab on the current window... The only reason I'll ever be browsing two folders at once is to drag and drop between them, which I find simpler with two seperate windows. I know I can detach a tab, but is there a way to just make folders open in new windows in the first place?

---

### Post by SGC on 2005-09-19
In konqueror go to:
 "Settings" menu -> Configure Konqueror -> Web Behavior -> "Advanced options" button
 
And uncheck the last box that says "Open as tab in exicting Konqueror when URL is called externally"
 
 This will works for both URLs and the icons on your desktop.
