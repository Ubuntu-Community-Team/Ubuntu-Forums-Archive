### Post by fragos on 2006-10-05
I recently made the change from VGA to DVI when I bought a new monitor that had both.  My Nvidia card already had connectors for both.  I went from a CRT to an LCD at the same time.  The end result is a much sharper screen image for images and text a like.  CRT was 1280x1024 and the LCD is a wide screen 1440x900.  I'd go DVI.
