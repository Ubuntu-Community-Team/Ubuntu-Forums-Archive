### Post by dvlchd3 on 2010-02-25
Because Karmic (9.10) introduces a new version of GDM, themeing is not supported, and thus the login screen cannot be changed...yet.  This will hopefully be added soon as the new GDM is being added to many distros.  I'm also keeping a close eye out for this.  Although I like the new login screen, I find I like a change of screen every once in a while.

A work around is to downgrade GDM to the old version.  This has always supported themeing.  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=8350408&postcount=2](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=8350408&postcount=2)
