### Post by prshah on 2008-05-26
> **bcn17 said:**
> When I booted up into kde4 the screen was just a tad to big for my monitor. The resolution is set at 1280 x 800 (which is what I use just fine with GNOME) but in kde4 this seems to be about a half inch to big on the top and bottom. I'm using a nvidia 8600GTM. Any suggestions?

Change your refresh rates; experiment with available rates as suggested under advanced desktop options (you will need to enter administrator mode by clicking the button; it's at the bottom, near the OK/Cancel buttons.)

Note: It's remotely possible (but highly unlikely) that this will permanently damage your monitor; use at your own risk, if no other suggestions present themselves.

I had the same problem when connecting my box with SiS 6326 to a TV.. changing refresh rates did the trick.
