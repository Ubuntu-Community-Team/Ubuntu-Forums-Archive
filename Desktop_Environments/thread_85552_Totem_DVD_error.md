### Post by zipola on 2005-11-12
I had this strange problem with Breezy too. Back in my Hoary times Totem played DVDs with sound. After I installed Breezy it didn't. 

A possible solution could be Edit->Preferences->Sound . I had AC3 as default. After changing it to 5.1 (or possibly Stereo) the DVD worked fine with sounds. 

Since this problem is rather common, I hope this will help all who have experienced it.
