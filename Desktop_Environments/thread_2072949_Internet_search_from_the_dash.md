### Post by stevedude on 2012-10-19
I read your post and didn't know the answer, but decided to experiment. 

For anything on Wikipedia, I just opened the DASH and typed [www.wikipedia.org](www.wikipedia.org) and it brought me to the WIKI site. 

Let's say you want to search about horses; Type [www.wikipedia.org/wiki/horses](www.wikipedia.org/wiki/horses)

The format seems to be: [www.wikipedia.org/wiki/whateveryoursearchinghere](www.wikipedia.org/wiki/whateveryoursearchinghere)

(Results are opened in a browser window)


With Google the format is:
[www.google.com/search?q=whateveryoursearchinghere](www.google.com/search?q=whateveryoursearchinghere)

### Post by stevedude on 2012-11-15
With Ubuntu 12.10 you have to enable "Google" as one of your "Online Accounts"

Select the Gear Icon / Me Menu [Top-right corner], Select"System Settings", Select "Online Accounts", Select "Google" from the list and enter your Google account information.

For Wikipedia, I found that a lens was created for Wikipedia. Go to: [http://askubuntu.com/questions/38772/what-lenses-for-unity-are-available](http://askubuntu.com/questions/38772/what-lenses-for-unity-are-available)

 [Other Lens' are listed there too]
