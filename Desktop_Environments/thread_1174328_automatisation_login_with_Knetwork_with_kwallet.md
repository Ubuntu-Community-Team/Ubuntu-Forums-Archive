I think i found the answer in following topic:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=349910](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=349910)

You open the kwallet program.
Than you rightclick on the wallet with your password in. 
Than you select 'change password' and you leave both field blank.
