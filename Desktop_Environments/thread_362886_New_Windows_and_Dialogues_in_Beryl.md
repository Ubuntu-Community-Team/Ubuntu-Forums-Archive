### Post by mannheim on 2007-02-16
This sounds like the "Focus Stealing Prevention" feature. You can configure it by launching berly-settings, and then going to "General Options" --> "Main" tab --> "Focus Stealing Prevention". Turn it off or set it to "Low" and see if things are better.
