### Post by Grimboy on 2006-06-25
Personally I've always found that there's a bit of an annoying flicker with 60hz. 75hz is good.

wpshooter: My advice to you would be to go as high as you can go without distorting the monitor, that includes subtle things like when weird bits come up at the side (otherwise that monitor will start smoking pretty quickly).

Edit: Oh, it's a lcd monitor, in that case you should can stick with 60hz.
