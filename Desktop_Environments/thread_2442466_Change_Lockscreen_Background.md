Hi,
 The easiest way I have been following is to replace the default Ubuntu background image with my preferred one. Please see if this works for Ubuntu 20.04 as well.

Replace the image */usr/share/backgrounds/warty-final-ubuntu.png* (default background) with your image. I would recommend to keep backup of the original, for future use.
