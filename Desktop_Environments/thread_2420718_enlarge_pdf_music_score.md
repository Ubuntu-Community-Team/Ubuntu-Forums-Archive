### Post by Frogs Hair on 2019-06-11
I opened the document in gimp and resized and save it as a .png. The resize option opened automatically and after the size was selected I used the import option displayed. The pdf opened in gimp to the selected size. You can also save the document as as a gimp .xcf  and re-save as a pdf that will open n document viewer. Exporting the resized document should also work.

### Post by ajgreeny on 2019-06-11
A quicker way may be to insert the pdf as an image into a libreoffice writer page, when it will probably fill the page out to whatever margins you have set.

You should also be able to simply use print preview from the pdf viewer used and set the page size and how it fits that way, though perhaps that depends on the pdf viewer and your printer; I can certainly do either on my Xubuntu 18.04 with an HP Envy 4520 printer.
