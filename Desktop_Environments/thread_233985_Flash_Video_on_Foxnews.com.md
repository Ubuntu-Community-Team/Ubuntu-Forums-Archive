### Post by jromer on 2006-10-30
To get FoxNews video to work:

I installed the firefox add-on greasemonkey
[http://greasemonkey.mozdev.org/](http://greasemonkey.mozdev.org/) 

and then installed FoxNews Friendly Video script from the greasemonkey site  [http://userscripts.org/scripts/show/1371](http://userscripts.org/scripts/show/1371), and it now works for me. 

It's discussed toward the bottom of the following thread [http://forums.mozillazine.org/viewtopic.php?t=469001&highlight=foxnews](http://forums.mozillazine.org/viewtopic.php?t=469001&highlight=foxnews)

### Post by Silver Surfer on 2006-12-15
For those who use the following extensions in Firefox...

Adblock
NoScript

You need to whitelist the video page with Adblock (right click on the Adblock section on the status bar) and enable two scripts...

akamai.net
doubleclick.net

This is the only way I can view video with these two Firefox extensions when GreaseMonkey is installed.  I use the standard Flash 7 player.
