To all those unfamilliar, one feature of OSX I *love* is if you press F11, all your applications hide, showing the desktop, then you press it again, and they come back.

Is there any way to do this in Kubuntu?
Or a way to bind F11 to minimize all or something?

### Post by johnc4510 on 2006-06-28
Look in the bottom left corner of the desktop. See that little square on the left side of the panel? Press it and all applications will hide, press again and they appear.

### Post by aysiu on 2006-06-28
Try Control-Alt-D

Press Alt-F2 and type ```
kcontrol
``` Then change what I've boxed in green to be F11 instead of Control-Alt-D if you like.
