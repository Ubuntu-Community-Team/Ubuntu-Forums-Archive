### Post by mister_playboy on 2010-10-23
I would suggest you upgrade to 10.04 if at all possible, so you don't have to worry about being unsupported.

The normal repos for a distro are taken down at the end of the support period, but you can check out old-releases.ubuntu.com.  8.10 Intrepid Ibex is unsupported and does not appear in the old-releases list, so I can't guarantee 9.04 will be in there, either.

10.04 is the way to go... that's the whole point of an LTS release.
