### Post by jeremy on 2005-05-21
For mail, copy over your /home/YOURUSERNAME/.kde/share/apps/kmail folder
for calendar it's /home/YOURUSERNAME/.kde/share/apps/korganizer
for contacts I think it is /home/YOURUSERNAME/.kde/share/apps/kabc/
