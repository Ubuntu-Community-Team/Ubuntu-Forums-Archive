Here is a *very* simple script I put together to download my calendars from Google Calendar.  I use it to grab the calendars, then I move them to my  iPod.  I thought some of you may be interested in having it.

To use it, just plug in your private calendar address where the XXX/XXX/XXX/basic.ics part is.  This file should be in a folder of its own.  It will delete old calendars first, then grab the up-to-date ones from Google.  Feel free to improve on it or reply here if something better is already in existence.
