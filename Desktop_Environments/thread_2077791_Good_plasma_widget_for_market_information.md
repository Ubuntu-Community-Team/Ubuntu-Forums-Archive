### Post by Monsuco on 2012-11-12
Bazinga! I found a way. Use the [web slice widget]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=12349690#post12349690")! You can get it to embed the little ticker on Google's Finance page into a widget on your homepage.

The URL is [https://www.google.com/finance]("https://www.google.com/finance")

You want #sfe-mktsumm as the element.

[IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=227057&d=1352695958[/IMG]

Now my desktop keeps me up to speed on the world around me just nicely. That was my goal. Now I've got RSS news, weather and the markets right there on the desktop. Cool huh? (In case you're wondering, the weird background is from a videogame which, sadly, doesn't run on WINE.)
