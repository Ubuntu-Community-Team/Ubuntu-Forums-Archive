### Post by fdimmling on 2007-12-31
You can disable it in Advanced Desktop Effects Settings from the System->Preferences menu. Eventually you have to install the compizconfig-settings-manager first. You go to Switching Desktops or something like this and set the mouse wheele entries - button 4 an 5 AFAIK - to None.

Friedrich

### Post by fdimmling on 2008-01-01
Well I am running a German Ubuntu. In Advanced Desktop Effects Settings->Desktop it is the first item, in German "Arbeitsflächenumschalter", which means 'switching of desktops' With -> actions->switching of desktops you can at least disable the (inadvertently) switching desktops by mouse movements. All other switches should be really nearby.
