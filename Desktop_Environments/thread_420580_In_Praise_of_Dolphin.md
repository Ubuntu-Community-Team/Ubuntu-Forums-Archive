### Post by verevi on 2007-04-23
I just installed Dolphin of Kubuntu 7.04.  My first impression is, "Hey, they've been reading my mind!"

They took my favorite parts of both Konq and Nautilus and streamlined it into exactly what I use.  

Highlights:  
[LIST]
[*]Single click to the views I use.
[*]Easy access to filtering (cool feature of Konq)
[*]Nice location tracking ala Nautilus, but it actually allows me to drag a file to a upper directory.  I always wondered why Nautilus didn't provide that!
[*]Simplified and easy to use.[/LIST]Just had to share my thoughts....
:):)
