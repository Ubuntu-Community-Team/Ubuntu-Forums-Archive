### Post by josephus on 2007-03-16
I had trouble similar to this when playing around with different media players.  I was using both gXine and totem (with gstreamer) media players, and found that the gXine volume settings would affect totem sound playback even though the global volume setting and local setting on totem were both set to the max.

Maybe you could try opening up the sound editing apps and turning up those settings to see if it helps.
