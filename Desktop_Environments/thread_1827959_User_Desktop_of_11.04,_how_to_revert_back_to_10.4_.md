I just installed the latest version, 11.04.  The desktop layout is very different from 10.04 and would like to revert back to the layout of 10.04, is there a way to do that?  Right now I have a Windows type bar on the left hand side, with no easy way to access the applications etc.

### Post by TBABill on 2011-08-18
A couple ways. Log out, then when you enter your username to log back in, don't hit enter. At the bottom, select Ubuntu Classic. That'll get you to standard Gnome 2. Or you can reinstall 10.04 or 10.10.

---

### Post by Ms_Angel_D on 2011-08-18
Hi, Log out of your desktop and select your name. Before typing your password and hitting enter look at the bottom of the screen and switch from *Ubuntu desktop edition* to *Ubuntu classic desktop*.

That should give you the old gnome look back.
