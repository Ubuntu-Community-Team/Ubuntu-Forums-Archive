### Post by luisromangz on 2007-06-16
I think you are talking about korganizer. There you can add a new calendar resource labeled "Birthdays from KAdressBook" or something similar (I don't know for sure as I use the spanish translation).

I use kontact that way and it works, I hope it works for you too.

---

### Post by mbant on 2007-06-16
Thanks a lot, that got me the right direction.

Here's exactly what I did: 

1. Open KOrganizer (I did it through Kontact -> Calendar)
2. In the sidebar, there's a box with resources (Usually the Default KOrganizer resource, ticked). If it's not there, go Settings -> Sidebar -> Show Resource View.
3. At the resources, click 'Add'. The KAdressBook birthdays are in the list. Select them and click OK.
