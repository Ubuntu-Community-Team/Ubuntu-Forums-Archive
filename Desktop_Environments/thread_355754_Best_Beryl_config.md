### Post by .robert on 2007-02-07
Try turning off the Blur Effects.  I have a pretty fast system, but a poor video card (besides being an ATI video card)  Just open Beryl Settings Manager, click on visual effects, and un-check "Blur Effects"

this had a **tremendous** performance effect for me.  Hopefully it helps you too.

Robert
