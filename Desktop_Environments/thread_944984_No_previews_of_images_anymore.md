### Post by prshah on 2008-10-12
> **fr0sty said:**
> 
when i would open up folders containing images, they would have a preview. 

Open System-Preferences-File Management (If you can't find it, right-click Applications, Choose "Edit Menus", browse to "System-Preferences" and place a check mark next to "File Management"; close, and it should now appear in the menu)

Click on the "Preview" tab, and toggle settings under "Other previewable files", or set them as you feel suitable.
