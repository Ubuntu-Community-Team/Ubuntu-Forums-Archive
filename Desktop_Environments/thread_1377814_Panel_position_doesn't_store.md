### Post by rCXer on 2010-01-10
Have you tried rearranging the applets instead of the panels? For example, try removing the Menu Bar by right-clicking on it, and selecting "Remove from Panel".  Then re-add it by right clicking on the correct panel, "Add to Panel" and select the "Menu Bar".

Moving the window list is trickier.  To remove it make sure you right click on the area to the right of the list.  I circled it in red in the attached image.
