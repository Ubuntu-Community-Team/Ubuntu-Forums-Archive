### Post by Goldnblazer on 2007-06-17
On my desktop I just had to right click on an icon and select 'stretch icon' from the list.
A box with triangles in the corners shows up around the icon and I can hover over one of the triangles and make icon larger or smaller. It is like the crop tool in an imaging program.
