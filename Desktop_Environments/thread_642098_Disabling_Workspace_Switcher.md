### Post by graham-cracker on 2007-12-16
Take a look under System->Preferences->Keyboard Shortcuts and look for the ones you want to change (probably the last two on the list).

### Post by catach on 2007-12-17
Looks like the last two entries in 7.10 Gutsy are:
Switch to workspace above this one - Ctrl+Alt+Up
Switch to workspace below this one - Ctrl+Alt+Down

So just look for those where ever they are on the list in 7.04 Feisty.
