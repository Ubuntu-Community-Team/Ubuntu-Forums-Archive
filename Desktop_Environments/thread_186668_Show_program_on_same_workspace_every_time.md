### Post by jobezone on 2006-06-02
Right click at the space between the desktop button and the window list in the bottom panel. That space actually belongs to the window list (it's not really a space, but a kind of "graber" for it). Choose preferences from the right-click menu, and you'll get that option for every program.
To do it individually, I don't really know... You could have a look at gconf-editor (press ALT+F2 and enter gconf-editor), but maybe you're not too used to it yet.
