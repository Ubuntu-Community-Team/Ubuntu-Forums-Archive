### Post by urukrama on 2008-03-24
Of the two, I think Xfe will be lighter. You won't notice a very big difference between Icewm and Openbox, though Icewm is lighter (see [here]("http://www.gilesorr.com/wm/memory.html")). If you want a panel in Openbox, Icewm will be a better choice as it has a built-in panel and will therefore use less resources than Openbox with an external panel.
