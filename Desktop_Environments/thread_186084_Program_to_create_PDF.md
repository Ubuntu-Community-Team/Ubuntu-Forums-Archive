### Post by swhit on 2006-06-01
open office can convert any open office document (or any document opened within open office) to a pdf. To do this, it's one of the buttons on the top menu row, next to the print button. It's also under the "File" menu.

Also, the print dialog box of most gnome applications allows for an export to pdf.
