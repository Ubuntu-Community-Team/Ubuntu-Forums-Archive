I'm running Feisty Fawn (Ubuntu 7.04) on an old i686 that used to run on Windows 98. You can get second hand RAM cards at computer shops for pretty cheap these days. I had a 128 MB and added a 256 MB. It runs like a charm. Compared to the Windows XP computer, it's about 5 seconds slower when loading and 3 seconds slower for most things. Most videos on the net play well but some are very slow. Music, downloading pictures and browsing is perfect. Once inside an app the speed is the same. I'm a big fan of Feisty especially because of ease of use. Have fun!
