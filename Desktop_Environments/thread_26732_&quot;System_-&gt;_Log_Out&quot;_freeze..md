### Post by xnico on 2005-04-14
It's a known issue with xcompmgr. What happens is the logout box is hidden. You either deal with hitting enter on the hidden box or turn off xcompmgr. 

It's discussed on the original thread [here](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=20769). I was having the same issue and ended up turning compositing off.
