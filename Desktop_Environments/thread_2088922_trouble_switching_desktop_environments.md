I currently run ubuntu 12.10 with unity and recently decided to try out different desktop environments and installed lubuntu. Everything at this point worked fine. I could switch environments at the login screen with no problem.

To further experiment with new interfaces, I installed xubuntu.  Installation went fine but I can't switch gui's anymore. From the login screen, I can still click the drop menu to select the different environments.  But clicking on lubuntu or xubuntu doesn't do anything.
