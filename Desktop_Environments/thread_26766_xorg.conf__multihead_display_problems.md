### Post by spaceturnip on 2005-04-14
Have you tried moving Xinerama from ServerFlags to ServerLayout.  That's the ony real difference I can see between your xorg.cof and mine.  Get rid of the ServerFlags section and change your ServerLayout to something like this:

```

Section "ServerLayout"
	Identifier  	"Default Layout"
	Screen 		"Default Screen"
	Screen 		"Second Screen" RightOf "Default Screen"
        Option          "Xinerama"  "On"
	InputDevice 	"Configured Mouse"
	InputDevice 	"Generic Keyboard"
EndSection

```

See if that works for you.
