### Post by json684 on 2007-05-10
For videos at least I have just found kplayer. And it sounds like it is exactly what you are looking for. To play files over smb you have to go into Settings -> Configure Kplayer -> Advanced and check Use KIOSlave for SMB. Works great for me. I am not sure on the amarok thing. I think you can play mp3 using kplayer as well. Not sure on how to make amarok handle it though.
