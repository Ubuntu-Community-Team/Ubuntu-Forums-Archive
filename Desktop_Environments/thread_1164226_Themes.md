Hmm, KDE doesn't have a theme manager, rather it's broken up into parts.  Right click on the desktop and you can change the wallpaper, and plasma design.  In the control-panel check appearance and you can change colors, icons, text...  Look at KDE-look.org and youll see all types of configurations.  Also alot of the control-panel options have a dialog that can download various-works.
