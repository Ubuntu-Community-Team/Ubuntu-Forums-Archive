### Post by kairu0 on 2006-01-09
I don't foresee any kinds of complications with using a dvorak keyboard in Ubuntu. Essentially, you'll just have to edit your xorg.conf configuration and specify the dvorak layout there. You do not have to download anything extra. The appropriate keymaps are already installed by default.

After that, no additional configuration will be necessary in your applications.
