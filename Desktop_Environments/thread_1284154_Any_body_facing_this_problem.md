### Post by ikisham on 2009-10-07
mlinuxuser, Firefox uses very much RAM. What's happening is that your system starts to use the swap partition and that is very much slower.
When you have 512 MB of RAM then Ubuntu may work somewhat better but it still isn't the most proper system for your machine.
I recommend you to try [antiX]("http://antix.mepis.org"). It may not look so good but you'll have a complete operating system that will perform adequately in your pc.
You may want to try [this]("http://www.pclinuxonline.com/?p=174") one if you prefer.

There are some systems that work well on your machine. [Puppy]("http://puppylinux.org") is another one. Also [Absolute]("http://www.absolutelinux.org/"), [Spri]("http://www.icebuntu.com/?q=node/1") ...

Please, read the following link so you can get an idea why it's not recommended to have the situation you have now, of having to resort on swap (specially if there are other good Linux distros that would fit better for you):
[http://www.linux-mag.com/cache/7520/1.html](http://www.linux-mag.com/cache/7520/1.html)
