### Post by mtron on 2005-06-06
check this: 
[http://packages.ubuntu.com/](http://packages.ubuntu.com/)

goto "Search the contents of packages" and switch the architecture to your needs.

Updates: 
Yes, change distro name in sources.list from the old release name to the new one. rund a "sudo apt-get update" and "sudo apt-get dist-upgrade", and you will get the next version
