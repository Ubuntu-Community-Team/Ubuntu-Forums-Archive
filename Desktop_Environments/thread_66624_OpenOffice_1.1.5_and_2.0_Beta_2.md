### Post by Zotova on 2005-09-18
It sounds like 1.1.5 which you downloaded does not have gnome integration. I had the same problem when I installed open office 2 - the interface looked totally ugly compared to the previous version. You should be able to get gnome integration from the open office website, if not I'm sure someone else should know where to get it from.

For your second question - you can download the files [here](ftp://ftp.linux.cz/pub/localization/OpenOffice.org/devel/680/SRC680_m125/Build-1/OOo_SRC680_m125_cs_native_LinuxIntel_install_deb.tar.gz)

You can also get the local language files [here](ftp://ftp.linux.cz/pub/localization/OpenOffice.org/devel/680/SRC680_m125/Build-1/OOo_SRC680_m125_native_LinuxIntel_langpacks_deb/)

Hope that helps you out.
