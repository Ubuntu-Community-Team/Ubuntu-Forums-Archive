### Post by Harderlump on 2005-02-25
I found out what the "problem" was. It seems that the touchpad has extra functionality that confused me, because I didn't knew. When I move my finger on the lower edge of the touchpad, it does some fore/back funtionality - in Firefox it is bound to next/previous page. The right edge of the touchpad can scroll up and down.

While touchpad scrolling is a nice feature in my opinion, that go back thing is dangerous. Imagine you write a long email in a webmail form, then accidently move to the previous page, then go back to your email, and everything you typed in is gone :-(
