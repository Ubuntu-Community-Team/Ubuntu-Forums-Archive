### Post by Guilden_NL on 2011-12-10
Turboscrew, asking that question raises almost religious like fervor on both sides.

I side with you, dumbing down the interface is also happening with Microsoft's Metro on Win 8, and  Apple's Mac Snow Leopard.  Some UI designers believe that the dumbing down of end users happened via smartphone UI and tablets, and that desktops should match the lowest common denominator.

After performing testing in our UI labs, I have plenty of data that demonstrates that this is the wrong path, but it falls on deaf ears with MSFT and we don't have anything to do with Apple.  So I gave up.  I make the changes I want, and just shake my head at Canonical's decision to go with the dumbed down UI with their default distro.  It's their choice, and I choose not to go down that path.  Hence the migration to Lubuntu of all 9 of my personal systems, and nearly 20 of my friends, who I have moved off of Windows and Mac, over to Ubuntu.
