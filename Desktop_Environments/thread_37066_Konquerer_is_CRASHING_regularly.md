### Post by Josey on 2006-09-27
I had a problem where konqueror was crashing everytime I went to home folder on system menu. After trying to fix it numerous ways I think I got it fixed by simply loading konqueror as a webbrowser and going to settings and hitting default.

So for anyone else having this problem mine is fixed now by hitting default settings under behavior tab and changing home url. One of those fixed the problem. :)
