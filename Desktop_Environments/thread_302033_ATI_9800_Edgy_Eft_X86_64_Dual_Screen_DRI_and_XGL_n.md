### Post by KnevetS on 2006-11-18
A couple of things - first you need to add a section to disable composite - it doesn't work with ATI.  

Second, XGL won't work on a 1280x1024 resolution with an ATI card (yay).  You have to reduce your resolution to 1024x768 maximum.

I have a recent thread with my xorg.conf in it.  You can see the disable composite part in the last subsection I believe.
