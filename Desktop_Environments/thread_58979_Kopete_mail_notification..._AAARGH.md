### Post by PorchSong on 2007-12-07
Same issue for me. 

Here is the solution.  

Goto:  settings>configure notifications 
scroll to the bottom of list.  On the entries for "new email has arrived . . . "  Click the blue circle (toggle) with the "i" in it to the left of each entry and that will do it for you.
