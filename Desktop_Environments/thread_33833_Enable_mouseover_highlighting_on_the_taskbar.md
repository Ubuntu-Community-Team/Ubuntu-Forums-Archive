### Post by thecrimsonking on 2005-05-12
Right click on your taskbar and select "configure panel".
Then select the "Appearance" tab and check "enable icon mouseover effects".
This enables those pesky descriptive popups.

If you just want to highlight the button under the mouse then 
control center/appearance and themes/style  click on the toolbar tab and check "highlight buttons under mouse"
