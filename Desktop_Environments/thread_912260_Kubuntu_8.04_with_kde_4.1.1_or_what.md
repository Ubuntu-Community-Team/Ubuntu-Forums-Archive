### Post by benerivo on 2008-09-06
4.1.1 is the most stable release of kde4 so far. Officially it has [no bugs]("http://www.kde.org/info/4.1.1.php"), although some kde4 apps may crash more often than their gnome counterparts. I don't think the default  kubuntu 8.04 installation comes with (or upgrades to) 4.1, so you will nede to add a special repository using the official kubuntu [instructions]("http://www.kubuntu.org/news/kde-4.1.1"). This is the preferred method.
