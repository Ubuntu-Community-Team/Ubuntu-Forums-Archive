### Post by deltacomp on 2011-05-03
I think there are several different options to switch back and forth between Gnome and Unity. Depending on what you want, you can log out and select the desktop environment you want by selecting it at the bottom of the screen. Or if you are using Compiz I have seen the option available in the Settings manager.  Also if you ALT+F2 you can search out GDM and switch the theme to Unity.
