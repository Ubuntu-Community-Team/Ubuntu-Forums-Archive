### Post by todak on 2008-07-10
Right-click on the Panel/Kicker/Taskbar/Dock (or whatever you prefer to call it :smile:) and choose configure. In the window that appears, there is a selection for the menus. Near the bottom is a control to adjust the number of frequently or recently used programs.
