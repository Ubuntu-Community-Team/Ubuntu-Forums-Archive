### Post by Teroedni on 2006-02-27
This have nothing to do with your grapics card
The problem here is that your monitor is wrongly configured

you can do 2 things
[SIZE="3"][B]
1:Remove the horinz and vertical lines[/B][/SIZE]

Section "Monitor"
    Identifier    "Generic Monitor"
    Option        "DPMS"
    HorizSync    28-51<<<remove
    VertRefresh    43-60<<<remove
EndSection

**[SIZE="3"]2. If 1 do not work, you need to get info about your monitors horincsync and verticalrefresh rates and add them under monitor[/SIZE]**

Section "Monitor"
    Identifier    "Generic Monitor"
    Option        "DPMS"
    HorizSync    28-xx  <xx being your monitors maximum horizontal
    VertRefresh    43-xx <xx being your monitor maximum vertical
EndSection
