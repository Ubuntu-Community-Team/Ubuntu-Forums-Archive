### Post by timcredible on 2008-12-18
try the other usb setting on the sansa, and then when you plug it in, the system should bring up rythmbox and you will see an icon on the bottom left that is your sansa.  add mp3s to rythmbox from your hard drive (or cd) and then highlight the mp3s you want and drag them to the sansa icon, wait for them to transfer (progress bar in bottom right of rythmbox), and then properly unmount the sansa (right-click on the icon on the desktop and choose 'unmount device'), wait until icon goes away.  when you disconnect the sansa, it should say it's rebuilding database, and you're all set.

Can you give us a model number for your Sansa (ie. e260, Fuze, Clip, c200)?  This will allow us to help you out better.  As far as switching USB modes, you should be able to go under **Settings** in the main menu and select **USB Mode**.  You will have two options there: The first is MSC. This means the computer will treat your Sansa like a flash drive. Second is MTP.  This setting makes you computer treat it like a music player.  Try switching between these two modes and see if one works over the other.
