### Post by maxamillion on 2007-01-29
I'm not all too sure on your setup, but if you aren't currently running AIGLX and are able to do so I would highly recommend doing that. I know a guy who runs Beryl on an AMD K6-2 350MHz w/ 192mb of ram flawlessly because his gfx card is supported under the new nvidia 9xxx driver and pushes all rendering off to the card so Beryl only takes up about 5-25mb more on the system (depending on what he is doing) then xfwm (the window manager for xfce/xubuntu)
