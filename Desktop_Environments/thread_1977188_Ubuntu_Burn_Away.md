### Post by practic on 2012-05-09
Yeah, it's in the Compiz Config Settings Manager, under Effects, then Animations Add-On. 

You need to have compiz-plugins-extra installed to see the Animations Add-On. Burn is the effect, and is included in the extra plugins.
