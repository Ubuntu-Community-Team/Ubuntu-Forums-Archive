### Post by darkali on 2006-06-04
Click 'Bookmarks' then 'Manage Bookmarks',  a new windows will pop called 'Bookmarks Manager'.
  Now click 'File' then 'Import', choose 'From file' then click 'Next', now browse and select the file with your bookmarks, then click open.
