### Post by ardyayaf on 2011-02-20
If your computer has an intel (or VERY OLD) GPU and lacking a moderately  fast processor, using compositing managers such as compiz-fusion will  decrease the performance of the computer and may even make it so slow  that it could effect browsing speed.  Try using 10.04 without  compiz-fusion and see if that fixes your problems.
