### Post by ArielEnter on 2010-03-09
Hi, one thing you may have tried is the gconf-editor. You can add it to the Application menu in the System Tools category, or just run gconf-editor in a terminal. Once there you display apps then panels and finally toplevels. From there you can chose the bottom panel the top panel or any other panel and change it's settings like the auto hide.
