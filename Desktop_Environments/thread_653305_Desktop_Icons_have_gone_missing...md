### Post by Grehan on 2007-12-29
Hi Tom,

Assuming said files/icons have not been deleted, I would try to restart Nautilus.

Open a terminal. Programs -> accessories -> terminal

In the terminal, execute this command: 

killall nautilus


This has helped me under some conditions. Let us hope it is not more complicated than that.
