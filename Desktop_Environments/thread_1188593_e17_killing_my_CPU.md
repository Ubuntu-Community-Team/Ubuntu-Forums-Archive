### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2009-06-16
**Enlightenment looks awesome!** I enjoy the idea of an animated desktop, too - the possibilities are endless! The high CPU usage seems to be an Ubuntu Jaunty issue actually, but not a bad one. Jaunty runs faster because it doesn't run to the swap file right away, but tries to run as much as possible in RAM. That makes Jaunty faster, but unfortunately it can also make your CPU hotter and keep the fan spinning. They're working on it, and there are several threads about cooling things off.

If it's a really **low resource** desktop environment you want, have a look at [COLOR="Magenta"]**Enlightenment's pretty little sister, [LXDE](http://lxde.org).**[/COLOR] She's not all fancy and highfalutin like Enlightenment is, She's just sweet and simple, adaptable, cheerful and very pretty. And so lightweight and "low maintenance" that some people have accused her of not being a desktop environment at all.

-Robin
