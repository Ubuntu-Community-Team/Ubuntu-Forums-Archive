### Post by Belizeian on 2010-02-10
I found an explanation and a workaround.  Apparently the plug-in in question and a few other things where in the compiz experimental package.  That package was in 9.04 and before by mistake, and is no longer in the repos.

The following link from the compiz forums explains this and gives you a work around.
