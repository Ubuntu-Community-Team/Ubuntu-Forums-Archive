### Post by Monsieur Gonzalez on 2007-08-09
It looks like a KDE Applet. You can add it by right clicking on the panel and selecting the right applet from the menu. I think it's called something like "fast launcher", but my KDE is in spanish, so it might not be an accurate translation

Once added, you can choose the apps you want to include, and the size of the icons.
