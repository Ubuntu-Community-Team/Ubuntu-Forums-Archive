### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-10-04
[COLOR=Black]Right-click > Remove from panel.

To add Trash to your desktop, go to your Configuration Editor...type[/COLOR]  [COLOR=DarkRed]gconf-editor[/COLOR] [COLOR=Black]in a terminal window[/COLOR][COLOR=Gray].  [COLOR=Black]Click the arrow by Apps, then click the arrow by Nautilus, then click the arrow by Deskstop.  Enable your Trash (can) on your desktop by placing a checkmark (clicking) in the box "trash_icon_visible".[/COLOR][/COLOR]
