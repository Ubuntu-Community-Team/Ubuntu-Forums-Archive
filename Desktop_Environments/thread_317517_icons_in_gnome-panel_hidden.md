### Post by mcduck on 2006-12-12
right-click on your panel, select 'Add to Panel', find 'Notification Area' and drag&drop it to your panel. You can move it by dragging with middle mouse button or by right-clicking and selecting 'Move'.

If you can't move it where you want it because some other panel applet is on the way click that applet and make sure 'Lock to Panel' is not checked.
