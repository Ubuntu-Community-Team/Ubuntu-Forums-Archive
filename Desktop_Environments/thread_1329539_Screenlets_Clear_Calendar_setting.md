### Post by sunmao on 2009-11-19
So, you have to already registred in Google Calendar and you have to already installed ClearCalendar.
Now go to your Google Cal page and click to the down arrow on My calendars, select calendar setting and click to the ical button in Private Addres. It generates you a link, copy this link.
Go to the ClearCalendar, right click, than click ICS file path and paste it the link what you get. Than update events!

Voala! Now you will see the events in your ClearCalendar.
