### Post by Version Dependency on 2010-10-12
If you upgraded to 10.10 from 10.04, for some reason it resets Rhythmbox to not show up in the systray area of your panels/docks/etc.  Open Rythmbox and go to:

EDIT > PLUGINS > STATUS ICON

Make sure it is checked and then hit "configure" and choose when/how you want the status icon to appear in your panel.
