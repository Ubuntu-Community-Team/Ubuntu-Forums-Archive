### Post by Jason_25 on 2006-03-22
Remove the DDC line from the modules section, remove the videomem line from the device section, and remove the DPMS line from the monitor section.

Then add
```

Option          "NoDDC"         "true"
Option          "IgnoreEDID"    "true"

```
to the monitor section.  You also may need to increase the horizontal sync range depending on your monitor.

If that doesn't work, a custom modeline as mentioned above will work.

You probably plugged in the tv connnector if the resolution magically reverted to 1024x768.  But this should override that.
