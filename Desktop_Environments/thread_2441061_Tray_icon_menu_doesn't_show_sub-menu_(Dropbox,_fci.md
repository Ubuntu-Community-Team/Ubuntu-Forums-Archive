### Post by Peter Great on 2020-04-19
After more searching on Google, I found this to be a known bug in Dropbox:
[https://www.dropboxforum.com/t5/Dropbox-installs-integrations/Dropbox-integration-with-Gnome-3-34-2-in-Ubuntu-19-10/td-p/392201]("https://www.dropboxforum.com/t5/Dropbox-installs-integrations/Dropbox-integration-with-Gnome-3-34-2-in-Ubuntu-19-10/td-p/392201")

There is some workaround, i.e. installing 32 bit instead of 64 bit version of Dropbox, but it seems too much of a hassle to be worth trying, at least for me.
