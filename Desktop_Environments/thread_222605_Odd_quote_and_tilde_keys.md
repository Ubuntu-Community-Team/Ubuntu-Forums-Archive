### Post by luckyduck on 2006-12-18
Kind of late but...

Have you tried: System -> Preferences -> Keyboard -> Layouts -> Add -> United Kingdom (*note - do not expand the tree -- just highlight "United Kingdom" then click OK*).

So under Selected Layouts you should end up with the entry: "United Kingdom" -- not "United Kingdom International (with dead keys)" or "United Kingdom Dvorák"
