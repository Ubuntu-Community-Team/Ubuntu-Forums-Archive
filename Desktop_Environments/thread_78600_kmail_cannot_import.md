### Post by HermanAB on 2005-10-18
Howdy,

Kmail can handle both mbox and imap formatted mail.  So just copy your old mail to the kmail mail directory.  When you then start kmail, it should show up.

Just be careful that you don't overwrite anything - use meaningful names for the old mail, eg. inbox2004, sent2004, to distinguish the old mail from the current mailboxes.
