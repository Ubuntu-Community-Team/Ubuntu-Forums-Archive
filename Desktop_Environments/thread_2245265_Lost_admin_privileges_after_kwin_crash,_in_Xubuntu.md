### Post by M4he on 2014-10-29

Also had those permission errors, couldn't use pkexec, couldn't shutdown/logout, couldn't run loginctl etc.  Long story short: Skype was installed with a bunch of 32bit libs, including some 32bit PAM libs. As per [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/systemd/+bug/1295521/comments/15](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/systemd/+bug/1295521/comments/15) it seems to be related to the 32bit PAM libs.
