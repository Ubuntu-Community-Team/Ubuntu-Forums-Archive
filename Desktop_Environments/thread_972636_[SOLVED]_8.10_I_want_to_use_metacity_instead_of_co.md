### Post by nithska on 2008-11-06
To turn compiz off you would go to Appearance (Admin>Settings>Appearance) and then to the last tab (effects?  I'm not at my Ubuntu box right now). and set effects to 'none'.  This will turn off the compositing.  Is that what you are looking to do?

What Nithska said:
"System -> Preferences -> Appearance -> Visual Effects -> None"

On 8.04, after turning Compiz off that way, I had a bit of a fight to be able to [get Compiz to work again](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=6047048&postcount=3).
