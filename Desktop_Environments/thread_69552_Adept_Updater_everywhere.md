### Post by f1dave on 2005-09-27
Yes, adept is a qt-based package manager, whilst synaptic is gtk-based. Basically, adept and kynaptic are designed for kde whilst synaptic was designed for gnome. Since most people hate kynaptic, I would recommend you use synaptic if you do not wish to use adept.
