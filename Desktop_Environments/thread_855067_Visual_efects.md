### Post by uidb4056 on 2008-07-10
Probably because your video card is not supported or may be you need to install/enable first a restricted driver in order your graphics can support Compiz. Check under System->Administration->Hardware Drivers if there is a restricted driver not yet enabled.

Also in this link [http://forlong.blogage.de/article/2008/4/26/How-to-set-up-Compiz-Fusion-074](http://forlong.blogage.de/article/2008/4/26/How-to-set-up-Compiz-Fusion-074) under Projects at right side you can download a script 'Compiz-Check' that will let you know if you can run Compiz without problems or what kind of problems you have.
