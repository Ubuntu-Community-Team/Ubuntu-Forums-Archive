### Post by spvo on 2006-09-01
I know part of it is the size of the window.  If you have a small window in your desktop, there will be a very small rectangle in the desktop switcher.  And ubuntu will only draw the application icon if that rectangle is large enough to do so.  You can always change the size of the menu bar to be taller, that would give you a better shot at having the icons drawn.  Other then that, I don't know any good solutions to the problem.  But at least you know where to start looking now :P
