### Post by Tickborn on 2009-11-18
HI,
Try to right click on the top bar. From the menu that will pop up chose "New Panel". Right Click on the New Panel now and select properties to position it where you want. Right click on it again and select "Add to Panel"; finally add the windows selector.
Hope this is what you were looking for!:p
