### Post by c0rwyn on 2007-11-05
Oh well, many views, no replies. :) I fixed my issue anyway. I got rid of my ATI card and went with a GeForce 7900.  The restricted nVidia driver and Twinview both provide much better dual monitor support IMO.  I also made sure I had DVI cables for both of my LCD monitors. The nVidia settings panel incorrectly interpreted my 2nd flat panel as a CRT when it was hooked up via the analog VGA connector.  With that set up it was a breeze to get it working correctly and now both of my different sized panels act correctly on the desktop. No more scrolling around the desktop on my 2nd monitor! Woo Hoo!
