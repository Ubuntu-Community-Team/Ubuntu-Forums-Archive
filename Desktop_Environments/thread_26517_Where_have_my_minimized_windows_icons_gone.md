### Post by Xian on 2005-05-15
[QUOTE=biglake]Would you please advise what panel are you indicating? and what is add to panel? 

I have the same problem but I don't understand your solution. Please help.[/QUOTE]
The "panel" is the one with your gnome foot icon on the far left-hand side. Take your mouse and right-click somewhere on that panel that is empty and not being used by a applet or icon. When you do that you will get a menu that includes the option 'Add To Panel'. Choose that and a new window will open.

In that new window is a list of applets and launchers that can be added to the panel. Scroll down the list until you see the title 'Window List'. Hightlight that option with your mouse and click the 'Add' button. You should now be able to see your minimized programs in your panel.
