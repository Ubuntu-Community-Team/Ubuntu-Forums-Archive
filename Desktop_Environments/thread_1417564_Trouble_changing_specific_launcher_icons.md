### Post by crash123 on 2010-02-27
I just installed ubuntu 9.10 and I'm wondering how to change the icons for individual launchers on the desktop, ones I have on the panel and ones I have in my launcher menu. 

I know to change desktop themes and all of that, but I just want to edit individual icons without having to pick apart my theme's setting. In xcfe, I was always just able to right-click on the icon and go to Properties. It would give me the option to change the icon right there. But that option isn't there when I go to the icon's properties in gnome. 

Am I missing something? This seems like it would be a simple fix.

---

### Post by gadolinio on 2010-02-27
Right-click the launcher, go to properties. You'll see the launcher's name, etc... and its curren icon. Just click on it, and a dialog will appear, where you can change the icon. 
In the properties window, the icon itself is a button, actually.
