### Post by Jussi Kukkonen on 2006-08-11
Upgrades are generally very safe, but of course there's a chance of breakage when things are changed.

 At least the alt-tab problem sounds like it could also be unrelated -- you haven't changed window managers or anything? You could check in *System/Preferences/Keyboard shortcuts* that alt-Tab is really set as the "move between windows with popup" short-cut.
