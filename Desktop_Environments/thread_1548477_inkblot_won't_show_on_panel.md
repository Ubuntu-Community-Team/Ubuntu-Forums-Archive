### Post by Steve R. on 2010-08-08
_inkblot_ for some reason will no longer show-up on the bottom panel. I assume that it has to be related to a recent update. 

When _inkblot_ is launched, it does show-up as running on the system monitor.  When I login as "root", and launch _inkblot_ it shows up on the panel as expected. I also created a new user, and _inkblot_ shows up on the new user's panel when launched. However, it won't show up on my panel when launched. Nevertheless, I have been able to add icons, such as "shutdown" to my panel.

------------------------------------------------------

Solved.  I needed to re-add the "*notification area*" applet to the panel. I went through the panel options one-by-one, including reading panel help.
