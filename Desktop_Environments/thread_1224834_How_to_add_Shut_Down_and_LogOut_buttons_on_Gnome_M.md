### Post by drs305 on 2009-07-28
If you right click on the panel, Add to Panel, you should be able to add them. As you mention, they are called Logout and Quit.

Edit: I guess you want them on the menu itself. You may be able to get them back with the "Revert" button at the bottom of the Edit Menu page, although it may only reset either the Applications or System menus. Right click the Main icon, Edit Menus, Revert.
