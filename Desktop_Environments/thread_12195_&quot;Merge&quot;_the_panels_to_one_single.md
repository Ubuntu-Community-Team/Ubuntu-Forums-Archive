### Post by Daniel G. Taylor on 2005-01-22
You can use your middle mouse button to drag unlocked panel applets around on a panel, and from panel to panel. You can create new panels by right clicking on an existing panel, and you can move the panels around using the middle mouse button.

If items are locked you will need to first right click on them and unlock them before they can be moved.
