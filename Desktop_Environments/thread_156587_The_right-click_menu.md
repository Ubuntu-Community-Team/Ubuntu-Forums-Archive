### Post by mgmiller on 2006-04-07
If you right click on your desktop you should get a choice of scripts.  If you hover your mouse over that you will see an open scripts folder.  Click to open that and then put whatever shell scripts you want in there.  Afterwards, to run them, just right click your desktop and select scripts and the script you want to run.

### Post by Madpilot on 2006-04-08
Also, you can put launchers on the panels along the top (or bottom) of your screen.

Right-click on the panel, select Add To Panel.

Choose "Application Launcher" for stuff that's already in the menus, or "Custom Application Launcher" for stuff you'll have to enter the command for.

It's a nice way to keep your actual desktop clear but have a lot of stuff available without rooting thru the menus all the time.
