### Post by vanadium on 2019-01-19
This will indeed install the i3 desktop. Next time you log in, click the cog icon somewhere on the log in screen. This should now also reveal i3wm as one of the possible sessions. Select i3wm, and then provide user name and password to bring you to your i3 desktop.
