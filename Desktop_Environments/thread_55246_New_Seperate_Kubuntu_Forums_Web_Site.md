### Post by C38a7r1zvl on 2005-08-08
AS much as I respect your commitment, I don't think this is the right approach. The only difference between Kubuntu and Ubuntu is the desktop manager, a very small part of the system. By dividing the forums for Kubuntu and Ubuntu you also divide the ressources. Assmuning your forum gets popular enough every Kubuntu user will post his problems there no matter whether they are KDE-related or related to the underlying system such as the package managment. But he will be terribly disappointed by the few responses since the Ubuntu folks aren't there to help.

Use ubuntuforums.org for _all_ distri-related problems (and this includes Kubuntu-stuff) and KDE-forums for KDE-related stuff. It's that easy, no need for another Board :roll:
