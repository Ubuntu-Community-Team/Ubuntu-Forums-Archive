### Post by Old *ix Geek on 2010-02-13
> **midaskensai said:**
> topic says it, in gnome there is the gconf editor that has this option. no idea where to find it under kde.

**Settings > System Settings > Advanced > Session Manager**

Choose the combination of choices you prefer.  I haven't tried it, but I'm guessing that deselecting both **Confirm logout** and **Offer shutdown options** under "General," along with selecting **Turn off computer** under "Default leave option," should do it.  (I have 'offer shutdown options' checked so I can choose what to do on the fly.)
