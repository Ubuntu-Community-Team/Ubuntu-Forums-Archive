Hi. Welcome to the forums. :D

You've run into the gtk2/3 problem. You can install a few more themes from the repository (gnome-themes-standard gives you adwaita and light-themes gives you ambiance and radiance). There are more: [http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=167](http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=167)

I recommend [Clearwaita]("http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Clearwaita?content=145210"). That one looks nice to my mind.
