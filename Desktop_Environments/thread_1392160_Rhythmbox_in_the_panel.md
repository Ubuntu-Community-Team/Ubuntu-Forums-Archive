### Post by akand074 on 2010-01-27
Just drag an icon of Rhythmbox to your panel and then move it to wherever you want. Should be in 'Menu > Sound & Video' you can also right click and send a launcher to your desktop. If its removed from your notification area you can re-enable that by going to Edit > Plugins within Rythmbox and selecting the plugin that puts it into your notification area when you close it
