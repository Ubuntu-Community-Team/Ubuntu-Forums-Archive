### Post by fheinsen on 2006-03-07
This may be helpful to some of you:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CheapSecureEncryptedRemoteVolumeHowTo]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/CheapSecureEncryptedRemoteVolumeHowTo")

This how-to explains how to use any hosting account with ssh access as a secure remote encrypted volume. When mounted as explained, the remote volume can be used as a regular directory; the difference is that all files are encrypted in your computer, on-the-fly, before saving, and that files are saved, not locally, but to a remote host, over SSH.
