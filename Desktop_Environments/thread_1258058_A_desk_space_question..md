### Post by TetonsGulf on 2009-09-04
Not with the current Gnome window manager it's not. I know people have had luck with getting different wallpapers on each workspace using Compiz, but I've not seen anyone get different themes to work on each workspace.

Check out how to get the wallpaper sorted out [here]("http://anuragbansal.wordpress.com/2008/05/10/how-to-get-different-wallpapers-on-each-workspace-in-ubuntu/")

One note of caution, you will "lose" the ability to see your shortcuts or files on the desktop. They will still be there, but you have to access them through the Places menu.

Hope that helps.
