### Post by brycesteiner on 2008-11-02
> **buster2209 said:**
> I cant find it in there

Believe me, I have looked EXTENSIVELY!
It's there on mine.

Menu: Go to System/Preferences/Emerald Theme Manager

Click the Edit Themes tab
The first tab should be Frame Engine click the Engine itself. Mine is on legacy.
A bunch of options should come up where you thought there was nothing.
Click the Frame tab
uncheck the buttons or change the rounding radius.
