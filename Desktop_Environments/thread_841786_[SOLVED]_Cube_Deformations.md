### Post by sayakb on 2008-07-06
Easy way: Upgrading to Compiz 0.7.6
 In System->Administration->Software Sources->Third Party Softwares, add this repo:
deb [http://ppa.launchpad.net/compiz/ubuntu](http://ppa.launchpad.net/compiz/ubuntu) hardy main

And reload the database. You will be notified of Compiz update. Do the update and reboot. You should have this plugin by default.
