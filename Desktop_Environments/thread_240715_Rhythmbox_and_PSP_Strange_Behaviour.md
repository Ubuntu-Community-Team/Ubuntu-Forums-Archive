### Post by dipswitch on 2006-08-21
Go to System > Preferences > Removable Drives and Media. Select the Multimedia tab. Under portable music players uncheck "play music files when connected" or change the command to nautilus or whatever file browser you prefer.

It would be nice to have a tab for Portable Game Device that could detect if the device is a PSP or NDS or whatever and mount with the proper icon.
