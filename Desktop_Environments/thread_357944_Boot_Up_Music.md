### Post by Rabbit_Alex on 2007-02-10
Here's what you do in GNOME:

Go to System => Preferences => Sound
Go to the Sounds tab
The sound you want to change is the Log In sound

The problem is that the sound for 5.10 isn't listed (I'm not sure though, I didn't use Ubuntu back then).  You'll have to try and download it somewhere.
