### Post by Old_Man_Unix on 2011-04-02
Make sure that you drop those files into the ***Document*** folder on the Kindle.  They must be in that folder for the Kindle to recognize them.

BTW, PDF files generally don't look very good on the  Kindle.  Some of them are downright impossible to read.  Did you know that you can have the files converted to the Kindle format and sent to your Kindle for free? Yes, that is a service that Amazon provides.
