### Post by gspr on 2006-06-14
Hi. I've always used printscreen as a hotkey to run a shell script that takes a screenshot and saves for me. However, with KDE 3.5, printscreen triggers KSnapshot, even though its menu entry has no mention of such a hotkey.

How do I disable KDE's built in use of the screenshot key? I can't seem to find it anywhere in the hotkey lists.

Thanks.

---

### Post by asimon on 2006-06-16
This is really hidden well. :-(

Start 'System Settings', then Regional&Accessibility->Input Actions. There is an entry for ksnapshot. Either disable, change, or delete it.
