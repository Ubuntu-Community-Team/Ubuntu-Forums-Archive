### Post by tom4everitt on 2010-12-03
The screensaver thing could be mended by going to 

System->Preferences->Screensaver

and uncheck the 'Lock screen when screen saver active' checkbox. 


And in 

System->Administration->Users and Groups

you can enable auto-login, but that might mean zero password-entering, which perhaps is to overshoot a little :)


Do you have to enter it twice at the login screen, or is one the Gnome Keychain?
