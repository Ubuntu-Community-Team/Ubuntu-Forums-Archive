### Post by DougZ on 2010-05-10
Running Lucid on my Tecra S10 and using a Logitech bluetooth mouse.  This laptop has bluetooth internal; There is no plug in dongle/token.

Was able to setup and connect the mouse with (relatively) no problems. 

Upon startup/reboot, the mouse is not available at the login screen, or even after login, until I manually connect the device.

There are a number of threads on the forums that solve/address this problem on previous versions of Ubuntu, but they don't appear to apply to Lucid, as they all reference editing files that do not exist on Lucid???

EDIT: Solved:  Under XP the mouse automatically reconnects with no user intervention required.  Since a reinstall of Lucid it will now connect on reboot *PROVIDED* I hit the connect button on the bottom of the mouse.  Not perfect, but I can live with it.
