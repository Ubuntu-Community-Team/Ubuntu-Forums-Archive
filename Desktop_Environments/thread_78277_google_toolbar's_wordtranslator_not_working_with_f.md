### Post by brt on 2006-01-19
**solved!**

i just used [automatix]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=66563") to install firefox 1.5,
installed [google toolbar]("http://toolbar.google.com/"),
and activated the feature at:
 Tools->Extensions->"google toolbar"->Preferences->WordTranslator
