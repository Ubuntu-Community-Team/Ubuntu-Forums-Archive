### Post by the yawner on 2008-12-23
I've never tried this one other method, but if you don't mind using third party software/services you could use [Dropbox]("http://www.getdropbox.com/") to sync your tomboy notes. Basically, if you installed Dropbox on your desktop and laptop, you'll have a shared folder that automatically syncs your files. Then from there, you could configure [tomboy notes to save the files on the Dropbox shares]("http://www.cimitan.com/blog/2008/10/11/syncing-tomboy-notes-with-dropbox/").

Hope that helps.
