### Post by chriskin on 2009-03-01
1)the first one seems to mean just that, what you are trying to install is either a wrongly made theme, or not a theme at all
2)the second seems to mean that you already have the theme installed, either as part of another or just by itself (for example dust extras has dust in it as well, so if you try to install dust after the dust extras one, you get a message of failure, or even the other way round, for a part of the extras)
3)might it say that the theme engine is not installed?
