### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-09-26
If you open the Dash applications lens by clicking super+a and then click filter you will be able to search for Games. And you will then see icons for all the games that are installed. That same filter process will search for Internet related applications, Office related applications. I see 13 filter options and a means to limit the search to local storage or the internet.
