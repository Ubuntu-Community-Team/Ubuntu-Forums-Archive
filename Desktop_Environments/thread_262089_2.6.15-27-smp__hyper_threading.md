### Post by jdong on 2006-10-03
As being discovered here, you must explicitly specify 'ht=on' for the linux kernel to use hyperthreading. HT was defaulted to off in order to work around the HT hardware security bug.

### Post by linux2512 on 2006-10-03

### Post by jdong on 2006-10-03
The kernel source has been patched such that HT will not be probed unless ht=on is specified (which is opposite of vanilla kernel's default)
