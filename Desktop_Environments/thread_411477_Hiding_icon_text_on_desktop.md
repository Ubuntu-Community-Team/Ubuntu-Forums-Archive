### Post by aashay on 2007-04-16
Is there any way to hide the icon text (icon labels) on the Gnome Desktop?

---

### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-04-16
Right click on the icon and go to Rename. Then delete the name of the icon and press the space bar once. Then hit Enter.
