I'm probably not the best person to answer these questions, but here it is.

The currently proposed plan is to release two versions.  One with KDE4 as the default desktop and another with KDE3 as the default desktop.  This is an ideal solution in my opinion since those who still love the KDE3 desktop and its stability can continue with it, but those wanting to be on the bleeding edge can still enjoy KDE4.  I also think KDE4 is still too immature to replace KDE3 just yet.

Here is the official website stating their stance:
