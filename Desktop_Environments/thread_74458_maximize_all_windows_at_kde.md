### Post by Takis on 2005-10-11
Hello. I want a coffee.

Go into Control Centre->Desktop->Window-Specific Settings.
Click New, go to the Geometry tab and select 'Maximised Horizontally' and 'Maximised Vertically'. Set the dropdown boxes to 'Force' and hit the checkboxes.

You may end up maximising every window (including stuff like dialog boxes) on your computer accidentally this way - under the Window Extra tab, select what type of windows this rule will affect.
