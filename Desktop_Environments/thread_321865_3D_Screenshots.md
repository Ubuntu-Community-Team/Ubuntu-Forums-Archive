### Post by useResa on 2006-12-21
> **x64Jimbo said:**
> Not everyone uses Beryl. But how do you activate that plugin? I have it checked on my machine, but have never found a way to use it.

With the plugin you can do two things:
1. Determine where the screenshots you take with the plug-in are placed (this is on the *Filenames* tab)
 2. Determine how to active screenshots (this is on the *Mouse* tab).

  For example in my situation on the *Mouse* tab at the Modifier Keys *Super* is checked and after Button Number, 1 (which is the left mouse button) is indicated.
If I press the *Super* key (which is in my case the key with the MS Windows symbol on it) and the left mouse button simultaneously and draw a rectangle on my desktop, the area caught in the rectangle is caught in a screenshot.

Hope this somewhat clarifies the way the plugin works
