### Post by janrinok on 2005-11-20
I frequently have to produce archives of data using Gnome / Nautilus.  Under Hoary, I would highlight several files or directories, right click and select Create Archive and everything would work as expected.  I upgraded my dist to Breezy and whenever I right clicked and selected Create Archive, Nautilus would simply crash.  Today I was looking through the packages listed in Synaptic and noted that gnome-core was not installed.  This didn't look right to me so I selected it and installed it.  Problem solved.  Archives are now created as before.  But why was it not installed as part of the dist update?
