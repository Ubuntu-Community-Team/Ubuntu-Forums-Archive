### Post by lagagnon on 2006-08-27
It appears to me you do not know how to use the Volume Control software. Your microphone has been disabled as shown by the red X. You need to click the tiny mic icon to enable it. The same goes for the other options. With Creative LS cards you often have to enable the Analog-Digital switch. Try that - if no luck I suggest you read the Gnome Volume Control Manual.
