As to why, please read:

[http://ubuntugnome.org/gnome-3-12/](http://ubuntugnome.org/gnome-3-12/)

Not everything from Gnome.org makes it into a Ubuntu Gnome release. Some bits and pieces go into 2 or 3 PPAs. Did you notice this about the staging PPA?

> [COLOR=#333333][FONT=Ubuntu Beta]staging is for the things that have known regressions and/or bugs. Basically a place to get the bleeding edge packages that are still a work in progress and under testing.[/FONT][/COLOR]

It is my understanding that the Ubuntu Gnome developers add stuff from Gnome.org as they are able to.
