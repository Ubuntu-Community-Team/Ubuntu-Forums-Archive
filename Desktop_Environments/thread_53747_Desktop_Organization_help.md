### Post by kosmic on 2005-08-02
Yes it is possible, just go to -> SYSTEM -> PREFERENCES -> and choose the 7th menu, (mine is in portuguese and says "Gestão de Ficheiros") the icon is a small drawer in brown.

Click in it and the you have an option to list all files by extension, by name or by date, that will apply to Nautilus when you are browsing :)
