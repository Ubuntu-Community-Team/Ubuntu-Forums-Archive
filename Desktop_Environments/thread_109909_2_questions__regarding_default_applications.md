### Post by cstudent on 2005-12-29
I'm not at a computer with Ubuntu at the moment, so I'll do the best I can to direct you. 

First question: Right click on the file and there should be a tab for determining what apps can execute that file. Move the one you want to the top of the list or add the one you want and move it to the top.

Second question: Look under System>Preferences and you should find a menu for default apps. Click on the email tab and select Thunderbird.
