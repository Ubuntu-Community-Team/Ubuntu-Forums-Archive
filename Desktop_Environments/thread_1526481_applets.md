### Post by mcduck on 2010-07-08
Just right-click on the panel, select "Add to Panel" and from the window that appears you can drag&drop any applets you want into your panel.

The one with the shutdown options is called "Indicator Applet Session", not a very descriptive name in my opinion but anyway that's the one you need. :)

One you have the applets in your panel you can move them where you want them by dragging with middle mouse button (or by right-clicking and selecting "Move"). If some applet refuses to move right-click it and make sure "Lock to Panel" is disabled. (also the Window List and Notification area applets have a small handle on the far left end of the applet that allows you to access the applet's menu and move it, clicking on individual notification icons won't give you access to the applet itself).

edit: Also, like the above poster mentioned, if you remove the shutdown option from your panel it should automatically appear in your System menu.
