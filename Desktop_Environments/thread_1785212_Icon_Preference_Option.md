Anyways I then installed it on my own machine after seeing how responsive it was on his even though its a fairly old computer. This is important because after installing from the same disk on both and installing one or two applications they've been behaving differently.

On his machine when I put a windows game disk in to run it off wine all the icons displayed with their windows icon images. DLL files seemed to display icons with the first two letters of their name. I've been searching high and low and can not for the life of me understand why there's the difference.
