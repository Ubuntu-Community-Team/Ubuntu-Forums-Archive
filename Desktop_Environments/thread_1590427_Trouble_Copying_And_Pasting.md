I'm using the 64-bit version of 10.4.

Believe it or not, I'm having trouble copying and pasting text. Gnome has a longstanding bug in that you can't paste something from an application once it's closed.

We've all complained about it for ages.

Your best bet is to install a clipboard manager, such as Pastie (my personal favorite) or Parcellite. They work wonderfully.
