### Post by prshah on 2012-06-04
> **Geffers said:**
> In ubuntu I often find myself typing in the middle of a previously typed sentence or paragraph,

Are you using a laptop? In that case, your palm may be brushing the touchpad while you are typing.

In Windows, the specific drivers can use "palmcheck" options to prevent such action; this can be done in Linux (Ubuntu) too, but the exact method may vary depending on your hardware. Please post specifications of your hardware for further help, if you feel it's required.
