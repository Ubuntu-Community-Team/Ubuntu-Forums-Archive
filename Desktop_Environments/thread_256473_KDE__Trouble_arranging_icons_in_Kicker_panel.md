### Post by Jucato on 2006-09-13
Middle-click and drag an icon to move it. Or right-click and select Move.

For other applets like the Taskbar, System Tray, Clock, and Trash, handles will appear at their sides when you mouse over them. You can click and drag these handles to move them.

Hope that helps.
