### Post by hhh on 2010-03-07
What the...? You moved the bottom one to the top and the top one to the bottom... ???

Anyhoo, it looks like you have a panel at the bottom set to Autohide but not to Expand. Can you bring that up and then right-click it and choose properties? Then uncheck Autohide, check Expand and work from there. If you want to move a panel, set it's Orientation to "Top" (or Bottom, or Left, or Right).
