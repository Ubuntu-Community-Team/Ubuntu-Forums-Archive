### Post by exploder on 2009-10-30
Make sure the panel is unlocked and right click on it, choose Panel Options, Add Widgets and add the pager to your panel. You can move the pager where you want it by clicking on the cashew, hovering the mouse over it, left click and drag it where you would like it. When you are finished make sure you lock widgets so things stay the way you have them.
