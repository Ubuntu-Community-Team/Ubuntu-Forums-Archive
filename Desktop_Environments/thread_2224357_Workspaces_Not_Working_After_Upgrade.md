### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2014-06-03
Well I think your problem is caused by "upgrade" especially since you have been messing around with DE and what not. 

Clean install takes 15 minutes and maybe another 20 min to re-install your software. "Upgrade" takes you a few hours even if it works, and chances are something will break if you go through a long chain of intermediate versions and/or there are some big changes and/or you have messed with your configurations so the system is not in factory condition. That being the case I don't understand the tendency to "upgrade", the lazy part in you may think that it is easier to press a button, but it is not and it will likely cost you more work and frustrations down the road.
