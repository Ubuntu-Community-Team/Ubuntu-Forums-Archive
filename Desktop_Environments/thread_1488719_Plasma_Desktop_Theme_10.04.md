### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-05-20
Hi,
Plasma themes can be selected (or added) at: 
Settings > System Settings > General (tab) > Appearance > Style > Workspace. 

More advanced settings for themes can be found at:
Settings > System Settings > Advanced (tab) > Desktop Theme Details.
