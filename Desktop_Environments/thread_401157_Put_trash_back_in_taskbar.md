### Post by bashologist on 2007-04-04
If by taskbar you mean your panel and you're using gnome, then:
[LIST=1]
[*]Right click your panel.
[*]Select "Add to Panel" from the list.
[*]Find the trash icon with the text "Trash" below it.
[*]Click it then click "Add" at the bottom of the window.
[/LIST]
