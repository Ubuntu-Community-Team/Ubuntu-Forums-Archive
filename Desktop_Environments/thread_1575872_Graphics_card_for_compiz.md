### Post by cascade9 on 2010-09-16
I dont know of a 5600EP, but if its a 'normal' 5600 then it can be faster than the 5700LE. 

The 5700LE was a seriously 'cut down' version, with a slower core and memory the the standad FX 5600. The core and memory on a 5700LE is the same speeds as the FX 5200 (naughty nVidia!), but its got more shaders than the 5200, so its marginally better.  

Those 2 are the best of those cards, I'd avoid the FX5200 and the MX4000 if you have a choice. The 4000 will not even run Win7, its a DX7 card, and you need DX9 for Win7.

BTW, all of them are going to be slow with areoglass on win7.
