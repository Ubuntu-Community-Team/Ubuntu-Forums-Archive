### Post by quixote on 2010-04-11
It is super-cool!  It takes a fast CPU and lots of system and video memory, though.  If you're running Netbook Remix, ie on a netbook, it's probably not powerful enough, unless it has an Ion CPU and nvidia graphics, like the HP Mini 311.  

The program is called "compiz."  And you enable it by going to:  System > Preferences > Appearance and clicking on the "Visual Effects" tab.  Also check in Synaptic to make sure compiz is installed.  When it is, compiz settings are available in  System > Preferences > CompizSettings.  That's where you can turn on cube effects, drip water on your desktop, and do all that other neat stuff.
