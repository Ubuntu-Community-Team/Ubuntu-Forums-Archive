### Post by Jucato on 2007-03-23
No need to reset anything, just move the applets and icons. For icons, simply middle-click and drag them to position. For other applets, you need to have applet handles visible to move them. If you hover over the applets, applet handles will temporarily become visible and you can drag them around, if they're not visible, right-click on the panel, select Configure Panel, go to the Appearance options, click on the Advanced Options button and set applet handles to either visible or fade out.

Hope that helps.
