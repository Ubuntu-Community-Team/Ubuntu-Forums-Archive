### Post by gdesilva on 2017-02-26
I presume you are running Ubuntu 16.04 or 16.10? If that is the case, the driver installed will provide the most optimum resolution. See the second answer for the following post
[http://askubuntu.com/questions/744050/im-using-ubuntu-16-04-and-theres-no-fglrx#744057](http://askubuntu.com/questions/744050/im-using-ubuntu-16-04-and-theres-no-fglrx#744057).

If your claim "the card is capable of higher resolution" is based on the resolution you get on Windows then the issue is that the open source driver provided is still not capable of doing the resolutions provided by the proprietary driver and my understanding is that there is no solution to this issue until AMD updates its open source drivers.
