### Post by descentspb on 2008-05-09
You won't need to install anything or to hack in the console. Just a few clicks in the system settings.
Go to **system - preferences - appearance - fonts**. Change all the sans fonts sizes from **10** to **9** (this is not necessary but it's much nicer with it) and then, click the "**Details**" button, and change the **Hinting** options from medium to **slight**.

That's all! That makes the fonts in the system, Firefox and Thunderbird look much nicer! That also deals with the bug [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/thunderbird/+bug/224810]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/thunderbird/+bug/224810")

In the screenshot provided, on the **left** is **Before**, on the **right** is **after**.
