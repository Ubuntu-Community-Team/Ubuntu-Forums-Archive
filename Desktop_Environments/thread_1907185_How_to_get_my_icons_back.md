### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-01-10
The appearance preferences as you knew them in Gnome 2 have been removed . Some of the older icon sets downloaded from gnome look no longer work and 11.10 will require GTK 3 themes only . GTK 2 themes will no longer work .

Here is a list of things to do after installing 11.10 one of which is installing advanced settings / gnome tweak tool needed for changing themes and icons .

[http://www.webupd8.org/2011/10/things-to-tweak-after-installing-ubuntu.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2011/10/things-to-tweak-after-installing-ubuntu.html)

Do a forum search if you have questions about installing themes , there are a number of threads on the subject . The appearance preferences can be found in system settings or by right clicking the desktop . This has limited options and you need the advanced settings for choosing themes other than the default .
