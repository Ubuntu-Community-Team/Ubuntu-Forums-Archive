### Post by oldos2er on 2012-07-23
Go into System Settings, Locale, Date & Time tab, change the drop-down Time format to HH:MM:SS, click Apply.

### Post by rimshot306 on 2012-12-01
Lubuntu 12.10
Digital Clock Settings
Right click on the time, digital clock settings. Opens digital clock window.
Clock Format
%R = 24 hour clock
%r = hour,minute,second
