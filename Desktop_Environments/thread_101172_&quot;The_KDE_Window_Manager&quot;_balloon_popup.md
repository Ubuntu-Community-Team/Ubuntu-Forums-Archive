### Post by jjmckay on 2005-12-10
I found the solution to this.  Under "System Settings" then "Sound & Multimedia" I clicked the "System Notifications" panel.   I then scrolled down to 'Virtual desktop one is selected" and clicked 'advanced'.  I then put an X in "Play a sound" and picked a sound.  I then clicked apply and this fixed it. 

No, I didn't have the box checked for "Mark taskbar entry" under the advanced setting but I have a feeling that this is what got reset when I did this.  It seems to be a bug, but I could be mistaken.  Anyway, it's fixed now.  Very obscure, imho.
