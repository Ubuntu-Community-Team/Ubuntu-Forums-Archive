### Post by un_dave on 2007-09-27
'Best' is such a personal thing, you're likely to get a wide variety of responses. 

Some people want certain features, like low system footprint, or fancy gui, or even (in my case) full ssl support. 

I've just gone through and tried a whole stack, love deluge as a solid, lightweight client, but in the end i had to go to Azureus, because a private tracker i use has a limited list of allowed clients, and requires ssl support. 

Although probably over complex, Azureus is stable, and has incredible list of features.

/2 cents.
