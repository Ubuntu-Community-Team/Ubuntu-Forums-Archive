### Post by 67GTA on 2008-03-25
This is for KDE 3.5. This isn't implemented in KDE 4 yet. To change the taskbar/window list font color, right click on the taskbar on the kicker, and select "configure taskbar". Then check the box that says "Use Custom Colors". Then select the color you want for fonts with the color boxes. They are transparent by default. Leave the "background color" transparent to match your panel color. You can change the clock font by right clicking on the clock, selecting "configure clock", and do the same as the taskbar. Most of the other kicker applets will have similar settings. Hope this helps.
