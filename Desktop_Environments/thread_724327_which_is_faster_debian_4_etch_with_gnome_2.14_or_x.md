### Post by kellemes on 2008-03-14
If you want to go Debian you better go Lenny/Sid instead of Etch.
I don't think you will notice any big difference in performance between Xubuntu and Debian/XFCE or Xubuntu/Gnome and Debian/Gnome.. Xubuntu **is** Debian really.
I'd probably stick with Xubuntu, install Gnome and tweak the hell out of it so it'll perform a little better.
Dump a hole bunch of running daemons, choose the lighter/faster apps.. so replace Nautilus with Thunar etc..
Personally I prefer XFCE over Gnome, it does exactly what to expect from a desktop-manager, no more and no less, just perfect. Well, besides the lacking menu-editor that is..
