### Post by codejunkie on 2005-05-26
go to System/Preferences/Sessions under Session Options and make sure Ask on logout is checked that should fix it hope this helps.

---

### Post by fransre on 2005-05-26
Thanks Codejunkie, that solved my problem completely, because now I can choose Shut Down as I could previously.

And yes, I had been playing around with sessions, but unfortunately it didn't support my favorite applications like Firefox and Thunderbird, so I disabled it and erroneously unchecked this particular checkbox...
