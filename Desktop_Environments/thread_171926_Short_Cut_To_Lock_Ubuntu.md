I don't know about assigning a Keyboard shortcur, but I can tell you another easy way to lock the screen.:


Right click on any panel and click "Add to Panel..." a new Window will open-up.

Under "Desktop and Windows" there is a icon that says "Lock Screen" select it, and then click on "Add"


now you can Lock the screen by just clicking on the "Lock Screen" icon that you just added on you panel.

Hope that helps.
