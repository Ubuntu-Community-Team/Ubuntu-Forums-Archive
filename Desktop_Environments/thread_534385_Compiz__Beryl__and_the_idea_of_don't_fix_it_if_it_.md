### Post by astoltz on 2007-08-25
The Compiz / Beryl packages in Feisty are two different projects each providing similar functions.  As you've seen, Beryl has more in the way of eye-candy than Compiz. 

However, since the Feisty release the two projects have combined to form "Compiz-Fusion" and this new project is not in the Feisty repositories.  It won't ever be in the Feisty Repo's so you'll have to wait until Gutsy f you want to stick with the official repo's.  As you'd guess from the name - it's a best of both worlds project.  I installed compiz-fusion on Feisty without any problems by following [ **this guide**]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=272104").
