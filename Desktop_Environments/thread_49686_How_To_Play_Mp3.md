### Post by adeen on 2005-07-17
Hey there,
Although not always immediately apparent to Newbies like us, a lot of the codecs required to play more restricted formats like .mp3 aren't included with the players or distro by default, like they are under Windows. So what you need to do is to follow the link provided by angkor, above, which takes you to the Starter Guide, which will tell you how and where to download the relevant codecs.
