My desktop GUI has been randomly restarting itself for several hours now. I haven't changed anything on my system today, or in the past week, the problem just appearedtoday. It'll be working fine, then suddenly the screen goes black, the splash screen comes up and the desktop restarts. ### Post by sabersong on 2011-03-04
I solved it. Someone else on another forum was having a similar problem, and solved it by reinstalling his nvidia drivers. I use ATI sound and video, so I installed the proprietary ATI drivers, and that seems to have fixed it.
