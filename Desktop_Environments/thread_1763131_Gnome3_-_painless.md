### Post by Allavona on 2011-05-20
After playing with Unity, which I like! I installed Xubuntu on my other box and then Gnome 3. Just added the ppa, and let update manager do its job. Then added the gnome shell and gnome tweak tool from the software center, rebooted, and viola! Gnome 3.

For the most part, I like it. But, like Unity, it is new and has issues, such as font trouble.

But now, I have two machines, Unity and Gnome 3, to play around with.

I look forward to seeing where these two new interfaces go to next.
