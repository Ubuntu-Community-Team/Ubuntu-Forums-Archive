Hello Marcelo,

Kubuntu also appears to be faster on my system, which is much slower than yours (PIII 933mhz 384mb ram), but it may not be because of GTK or Qt, or even Gnome or KDE themselves. There have been many discussions regarding an apparant slowness of ubuntu on some systems. From what I've gleaned from the discussions, it would seem that GTK 2 should be lighter and faster than QT in most cases, and GTK 1 faster still, but Gnome appears to be slowed by Metacity. I haven't researched about Metacity, but I did see a thread saying that Gnome can be ran using IceWM instead, and that this seemed to cure the slowness. 

Of course this isn't to criticize any of them as I find both ubuntu and kubuntu are largely impressive and beautiful. :)
