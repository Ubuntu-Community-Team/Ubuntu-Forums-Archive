### Post by Anxious Nut on 2009-08-06
System --> administration --> login window
-for the default message goto the local tab, look at the options below
-for the sounds goto the accessibility tab and disable the 'login screen ready' by unchecking the box

system --> preferences --> sessions
-to stop the sound that appears when the desktop shows up,scroll down till you find 'Gnome login sound', uncheck it by unchecking the box
-to stop the splash screen, scroll down till you find 'Gnome splash screen', uncheck it by unchecking the box
