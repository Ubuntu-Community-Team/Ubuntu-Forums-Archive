### Post by LeoSolaris on 2008-05-27
If you have compiz on, open the Advanced Desktop Effects tool (it's in Preferences most likely, if not synaptic search for compiz and look for compizconfig-settings-manager) Scroll down the tool till you see Window Borders. Uncheck and check it a couple of times, it's a bug. The other way around it is to log out and back in.

I get it randomly, too. It's a glitch in compiz, not Ubuntu.
