I put together an old PIII machine for my mother and recently switched the Desktop Environment from Gnome to Xfce4.  For this lower end machine Xfce4 runs a bit faster. So if anyone is having trouble with KDE or Gnome, or just doesn't like them, then I recommend Xfce4... especially for older machines.
