 As far as I know, this is not possible directly.

You can,  however, create new panels and place them anywhere on the desktop - not just at the edges. So you might cheat, creating an empty panel and putting it somewhere next to the app you want - just to prevent other windows from covering it. Yes, it is an ugly hack.
