### Post by carlwsnyder on 2013-08-11
First, an Android phone is not an iPhone, Ubuntu is not OS X.

You manage your apps on the Android device, not on your computer.

Managing your files on your computer depends on what version of Android you are using and whether you connect via USB or wireless.

### Post by davetv on 2013-08-11
With Android ICS (4.0.1) and above, you can access files on the device with the MTP protocol.  Google mtp and mtpfs. Click on the phone USB connected notification to set MTP mode. To get this working can/will require some tweaking but the are plenty of details available on the internet.

With earlier versions of Android the phone should appear as a USB device when the cable is connected.
