### Post by Operan on 2009-03-11
Select System>Preference> Main Menu
Click Accessories then click on the menu of your application. Click Properties to view Type, Name, Command, COmment and Icon. 
Go to Programming menu, create new Item with Type, Name, Command, comment and icon like your menu in Accessories. Then delete old menu in Accessories. 
Done! :D
