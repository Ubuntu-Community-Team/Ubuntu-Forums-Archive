Did it used to work? Because I think the data on your harddisk got corrupted. Things such as apt-get and fsck are automaticly installed by Ubuntu, and they shouldn't disappear for no reason. 
First you should try to run fsck from your live-cd on the partition on which you have installed Ubuntu, to see if fsck can fix your harddisk.
After that, if Ubuntu is still not working properly(Ubuntu still misses crucual programs such as apt-get), I would say, save whatever data you can save, and reinstall Ubuntu. Because most likly much more programs got lost, and I don't know if you will be able to fix your Ubuntu.
