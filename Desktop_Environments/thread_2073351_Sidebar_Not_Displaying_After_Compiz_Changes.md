### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-10-19
A fresh install of 12.10 even with Compiz Configuration Settings Manager will not let you get the Wobbly Windows effect or some of the others. They have simply been removed from CCSM. If you uninstalled CCSM and re-installed it you will see what I am saying to be true.

You had best leave these effects disabled. The Compiz developers have been working very hard. They still have a lot of work to do, apparently. They do not have the time to maintain these effects. So, they are being disabled to prevent instability which they are not able to correct.

[http://smspillaz.wordpress.com/2012/09/07/maintainers-for-the-unsupported-plugins/]("http://smspillaz.wordpress.com/2012/09/07/maintainers-for-the-unsupported-plugins/")

Read for yourself.
