### Post by GeneralZod on 2005-10-16
Also, if you know the name of any app but can't find it in the menus, it's usually possible to do alt+F2 and fill the name in there e.g. alt+F2 kmail.  

Also, check out Katapult which is a rather more slick way of doing this!
