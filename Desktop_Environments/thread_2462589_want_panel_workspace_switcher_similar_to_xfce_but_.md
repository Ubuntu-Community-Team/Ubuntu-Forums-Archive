### Post by Dennis N on 2021-05-26
The "workspace indicator" extension in Gnome Shell 40 has a similar switcher, but in it's current state, it lacks an icon to identify what application is there. You have to rely on your memory of what you have placed there. You can also drag an application from one desktop to another from the indicator. Is that possible in XFCE?

But, that said, you will have to wait until Ubuntu moves to Gnome Shell 40 in some future release.
