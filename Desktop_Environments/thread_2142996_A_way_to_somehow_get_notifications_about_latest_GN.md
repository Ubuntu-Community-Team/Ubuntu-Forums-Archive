### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-05-07
Go to [https://extensions.gnome.org/](https://extensions.gnome.org/) and click on the 'Installed extensions' link to see which of your installed extensions are outdated, they will be greyed out. If a new version is available there will be an 'Update' button you can click on.
