### Post by CaptainX on 2008-02-06
This was driving me nuts too, I found where the setting is:
system settings>Keyboard& Mouse>Mouse -- uncheck the "visual feedback on activation" check b x and the annoying fade for every click on the kmenu or a file will go away.
