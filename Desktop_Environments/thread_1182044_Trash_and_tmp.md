### Post by amingv on 2009-06-08
There should be a small trash bin icon in the bottom right corner of the screen (in the lower bar), that is the trash bin. You can also find it in ~/.local/share/Trash/files/.

/tmp gets deleted on every shutdown/reboot, if I'm not mistaken.
