### Post by ajgreeny on 2020-06-04
The applet you need to add is the "Notification Area" though it seems possible that you already have it but it is not setup properly; perhaps you have chosen to hide too many of the items it usually shows.

Right click in the panel and go to ***Panel ->Panel Preferences ->Items*** tab and check that the applet is in the panel.
if it's there already click to highlight it, then click the properties icon on the right to change which utilities show and are hidden.
See screenshot.

PS:
You may see two separate Network items listed, ***Network Plugin*** and ***Network*** so try unhiding both if necessary.
