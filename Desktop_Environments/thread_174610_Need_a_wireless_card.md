### Post by safecracker on 2006-05-12
I never liked D-Link cards as I seem to have power issues with them. The DWL-G650 works out of the box but avoid the DWL-G650+ as it's had some mixed results. I tend to like Cisco and Proxim/ Orinoco as they have given me the best results. If you wish to see a list of wireless network cards that have been tested under Ubuntu check out:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCards](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupportComponentsWirelessNetworkCards)
