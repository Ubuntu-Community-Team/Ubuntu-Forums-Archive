### Post by Mr. Shannon on 2011-07-29
Are you using Unity or plain Gnome?  If using plain Gnome you can remove the power button completely and replace it with a different type (without settings) by left clicking on the panel and choosing Add to Panel and then choose Shutdown.

### Post by Krytarik on 2011-07-30
> **Zeelon said:**

A better way to re-add the volume indicator is to add "Indicator Applet" to the panel, this would also bring back the message indicator, btw.

Before, you had "Indicator Applet Complete" in your panel, which is the default at least in Natty 11.04.

You can mark your thread as "solved" via "Thread Tools" right above the thread.
