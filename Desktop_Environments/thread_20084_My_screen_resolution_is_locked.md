its not locked, that window simply show the possible screen resolutions written in your configuration file.  to look at those options and to change them (if u are sure of what u are doing) edit the file /etc/X11/XF86Config-4 after backing it up. u have must be use root priviliges to do that so open a terminal and type:

sudo cp /etc/X11/XF86Config-4 /etc/X11/XF86Config-4.backup  (it will ask u to type your password)  --this for making a backup   then type

sudo gedit /etc/X11/XF86Config-4

now scroll down the text till u reach this part (this is part of my personal xf86config-4 file)
and add the resolutions u need
