### Post by cpcpcp on 2010-10-02
Not what you asked for but as I could not do it either I came up with a work around I could accept.

I use FEH

It runs a slide show in a fraction of my screen and if I want to stop and look at look at things full size I click the picture and fill the screen. 
It works for me because as a background much is hidden by the work anyway

HOW to

FEH can be loaded though the Ubuntu Software Centre.
Right click the desktop to set up a launcher (which when done you can drag as a button to top or bottom panel).
This command in the launcher works for me
feh -rsdD 5 --geometry 410x300  /media/path/to/pictures

:)As a thought I have just set it running on another desktop and acan simply swap to that desktop when I leave the machine and it will run until the screen-saver slide show cuts in.
