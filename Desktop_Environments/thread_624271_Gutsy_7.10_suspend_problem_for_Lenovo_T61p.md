### Post by acron1 on 2008-03-03
Thinkpad R61i with X3100 graphics.
 I read somewhere that upon coming out of suspend mode the backlight on many laptops may not come back on giving the appearance of a black screen.
So I looked carefully and sure enough my lappy WAS coming out of suspend mode without the backlight (I could see things on the screen if I looked really carefully). So Ctrl+Alt+F1 will bring me to the console and the light will ALWAYS come on then Ctrl+Alt+F7 back to the UI.
I hope it works for you too.
