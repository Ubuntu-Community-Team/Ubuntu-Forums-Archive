### Post by Ampersand on 2005-12-09
Most programs should appear in the menu. If not, the name of the executable tends to be the same as the name of the program. If in doubt, right click on the package you installed, go to preferences then the Installed Files tab, and the executables are the files installed in /usr/bin.
