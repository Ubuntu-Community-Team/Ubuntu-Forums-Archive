### Post by x1a4 on 2008-03-20
'Edit' > 'Personal Data'
When the 'Personal Data' dialog opens you will see two tabs: 'Cookies' and 'Passwords'.
On the 'Cookies' tab press Ctrl+A to select all cookies and click the 'Remove' button.  Do the same in the 'Passwords' tab if you want to remove passwords.

Close the 'Personal Data' dialog and open the 'Preferences' dialog: 'Edit' > 'Preferences'
Click the 'Privacy' tab and at the bottom of the window you have settings for 'Temporary Files' and a 'Clear' button.  Click it to remove temp files. 

To clear the history: 'Go' > 'History' and at the 'History' dialog it's 'Edit' > 'Clear History'

There is no unified way to delete them all at once.  Google a bit and see if there is an extension out there that does it.
