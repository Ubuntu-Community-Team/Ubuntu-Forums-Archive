### Post by motoracer47 on 2011-11-30
for those that find this post later when having a problem..cairo dock for some reason removed some core files and libraries for unity 3d...once I re-installed unity 3d core and libraries with synaptic im back in business. 

Cairo looked nice, but I actually prefer unity, hence why I "tried" to switch back. While granted, Im still pretty new to linux, if you do a quick search you will find several people who have had issues removing cairo-dock...so..install cairo-dock at your own risk? (some will probably disagree)
