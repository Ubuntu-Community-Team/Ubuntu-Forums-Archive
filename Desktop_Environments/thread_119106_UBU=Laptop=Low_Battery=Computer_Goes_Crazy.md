> the one thing that i have noticed is that this sems to happen to me everyday at work and almost never at home. the only thing i do different at work from home is use Evolution.

I) Is Evolution the problem?

    A) Either set up a second Evolution account or use your current one.

        1) Use Evolution at home under the same conditions of use and the same                 programs are running.

            a) At home do you hook up directly to the net or on a network?

        2) Does the same problem occur?

              a) Yes, Evolution would be the problem, or another program interferes                        with its running.
              b) No, Go to Part B

    B) Other Possibilities

         1) Networked at work

              a) Is it a problem with the network or where you are connected from?

                   1) Are you connected up to the same connection?

                        a) Try a different connection.
                        b) Does the problem reoccur?

                             1) Yes, likely the problem lies with the network itself.
                              2) No, likely the problem lies with your connection at work.
