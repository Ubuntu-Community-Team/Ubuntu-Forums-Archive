### Post by habl on 2008-11-06
I don't know why it happends, but it happends to me to sometimes. What you could do is hold your alt key, then hold your mousebutton and drag the window to somewhere so you can see the top of the window. Then you can resize the window so it fits between the two panels. I think it will remember the size when you resized it.

Edit: If it won't work, you can also try ALT+F8 to resize your window. See [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/KeyboardShortcuts](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/KeyboardShortcuts) for some usefull keyboard shortcuts.
