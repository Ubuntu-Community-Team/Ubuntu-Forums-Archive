Hello,

> **col48 said:**
> to add a panel to hold icons

To move/add/edit panels or applets press Alt+Right Click. If Alt+Right Click does *not* work, you need Super+Alt+Right Click.

Details are described here: [http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2012/03/gnome-classic-in-ubuntu-12-04-its-like-nothing-ever-changed](http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2012/03/gnome-classic-in-ubuntu-12-04-its-like-nothing-ever-changed)
