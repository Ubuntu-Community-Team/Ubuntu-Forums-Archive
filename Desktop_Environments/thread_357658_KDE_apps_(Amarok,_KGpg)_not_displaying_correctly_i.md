### Post by danns on 2008-05-21
Get a panel application like pypanel or fbpanel and run that in conjunction with windowmaker.  I chose pypanel, it's lite, unobtrusive and simple to install (apt-get baby).  You might want to stay away from matchbox-panel.  That tried to take over and screwed with my settings.
