### Post by Barrakuda on 2011-03-13
Well first off I should let you know that what I describe here is not what you asked if you could do. This is slightly different, in fact I prefer it, since it allows you to keep a 'normal' (relative to user) desktop. You will want to be able to hide the funnies..

Alright, lets show you what [COLOR=Black]mine[/COLOR] looks like first :wink:[INDENT][INDENT][INDENT][COLOR=Green][Figure 1 - Normal Beautiful Workspace]("http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=185952&d=1300040279")[/COLOR][/INDENT][/INDENT][/INDENT][INDENT] In a click, we can go to:
[/INDENT][INDENT][INDENT][INDENT][Figure 2 - Sunday Morning Funnies]("http://ubuntuforums.org/attachment.php?attachmentid=185953&d=1300040279")
[/INDENT][/INDENT][/INDENT]How'd I do it?

Two steps, the first of which may be confusing. I installed AWN toolbar.

First see if you can activate 'Extra Visual Effects'. (You may need to wait / restart your machine..)

Right click your worktop => 'change desktop background' => Visual Effects tab.

If you can thats SUPER!

                           ..If you can't its possibly a driver issue. Try updating..? Maybe your driver isn't strong enough.. Aww..

I think these instructions may prove useful:[INDENT][INDENT][http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=762363](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=762363)

[/INDENT][/INDENT][INDENT]or
[/INDENT][INDENT][INDENT][http://www.ehow.com/how_5089114_install-awn-ubuntu.html](http://www.ehow.com/how_5089114_install-awn-ubuntu.html) (.. The first one can be overwhelming..)

[/INDENT][/INDENT]Once you get that installed and running (you can make it look pretty later..) its just a matter of selecting 'Dock Preferences' 


Right-click on the Toolbar => Dock Preferences =>Applets  

drag the Comics! applett into the Active Applets area to install it.

Right-click on the applet => Comics => select comics you want to view. 

You can then add your own websearch'ed comic strip RSS feeds..  

Right-click =>Manage Comics => Add. 


Move the windows where you please and enjoy. The final results depend on the quality of the RSS feeds you find. This includes, but is not limited to, how often they update and quality.

Hopefully that works for ya, but if you've any questions I'll try my best to get ya fixed up.
