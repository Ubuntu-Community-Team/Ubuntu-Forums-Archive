### Post by blueturtl on 2005-09-12
Open the **Configuration Editor** from *Applications->System Tools* menu.
Locate the _/apps/nautilus/preferences_ inside the Configuration Editor
Check "no_ubuntu_spatial" on the right-side pane where the different settings are listed.
