### Post by Sirron on 2006-08-29
I'm having a problem with low dvd volume too... I've noticed a similar thing in Windows - DVDs are never as loud as anything else, but apps such as PowerDVD let you amplify the volume a bit, so... surely there's a similar feature available for a linux media player?

EDIT: Right, I double clicked the volume control icon on the top right corner of the screen and cranked up the PCM volume. That has made it much louder, it's not as loud as mp3s play, but it's about as loud as DVDs play in Windows. ^^
