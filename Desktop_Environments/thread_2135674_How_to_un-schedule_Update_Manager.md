### Post by Bucky Ball on 2013-04-15
You can launch update manager, click 'Settings' then set it to check for updates once a week or never (from memory, haven't used 10.04 LTS for awhile). Think you can also get there via Software Sources. It's under the 'Update' tab. 

Just a note; 10.04 reaches end-of-life at the end of the month (25th I think) so now might be a good time to move to 12.04 LTS through an upgrade via the Update Manager or a clean install.
