### Post by aysiu on 2006-06-21
Ubuntu, Kubuntu, and Xubuntu are all the same distro, and they all natively support NTFS browsing. Unfortunately, you'll have to do a little command-line mounting if you want to use Xubuntu live for that.

Knoppix will let you browse NTFS by just clicking on it once, but it uses KDE instead of the speedier XFCE of Xubuntu.
