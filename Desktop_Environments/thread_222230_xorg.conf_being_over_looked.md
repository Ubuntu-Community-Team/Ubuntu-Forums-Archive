### Post by revertex on 2006-07-25
can you post the section "Screen" of your xorg?
it should be somehing like

```
 SubSection "Display"
                Depth           16
                Modes           "1440x900" "1152x864" "1024x768"
        EndSubSection
```
the first value after "Modes"  will be your default resolution.
