### Post by Roasted on 2009-11-11
When I hover over the dock, the icons expand. This drives me insane... how do I change them? I spent a lot of time in the control panel of it but I'm not sure where the setting is. Figured why not ask. :P

EDIT - Someone in IRC was kind enough to help me figure it out.

Open the control panel of Cairo Dock - Icons - Set "maximum zoom of the icons" to 1.000. Done. Thanks!
