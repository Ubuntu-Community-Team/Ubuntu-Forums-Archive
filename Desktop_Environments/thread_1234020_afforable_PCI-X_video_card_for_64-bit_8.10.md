### Post by khelben1979 on 2009-08-07
[Video cards at Newegg.com]("http://www.newegg.com/Store/SubCategory.aspx?SubCategory=48&name=Desktop-Graphics-Video-Cards")

Whether you go for nVidia or AMD/ATi depends on what you're going to do with your system. If you're going to play World of Warcraft for example, then nVidia seems to be the best choice at the present.

The cards from AMD/ATi have cheaper cards and some of them produce less heat than what comes from nVidia and draw less power, especially the AMD/ATI Radeon 4670 which seems to be quite popular with a price tag below 100 dollars (not attractive for WoW).
