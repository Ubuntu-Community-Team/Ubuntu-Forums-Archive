### Post by izvie on 2006-05-07
[COLOR="Green"]Hey ASJD...your answer was right on target and I was able to solve it from your advice.  That was the problem (Load Images), as you said.  The problem wasn't because of Firestarter, it was because I had checked an option in **Firefox** directly underneath **Load Images**.

I had checked the _additional_ option **Load Images "For the originating wesite only." ** Once I unchecked that, everything went back to normal, graphics-wise.  Thanks!
