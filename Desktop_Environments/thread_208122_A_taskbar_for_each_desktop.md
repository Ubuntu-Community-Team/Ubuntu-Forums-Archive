### Post by henriet on 2006-07-03
Hello, 

right-click on the taskbar and choose "configure panel".
Then, in the "Taskbar" section, disable "Show windows from all desktops".

You can restore the system tray by right-clicking the taskbar, "add applet to panel", and selecting "System tray".
