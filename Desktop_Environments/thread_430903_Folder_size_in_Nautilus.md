### Post by LuisGMarine on 2007-05-03
When you open up Nautilus, go up to Edit > Preferences, and click on the 'Display' tab or the 'List Colums'  there you have a couple of options to set these preferences that you want.

Another thing is , on the left hand side of your nautilus window, there is a little bar on the left, usually this set to 'Places', if you click on that, and go to information might also be what you are looking for.  If not just keep looking around in the preferences and exploring, I'm sure you will find it.
