[QUOTE=retsel]If there's no way to get rid of it, I'll reinstall Konqueror, but just saying there aren't such problems in Linux doesn't make it go away. It's a nuisance at least, and maybe worse.[/QUOTE]

Unfortunately I was drinking coffee when I read this. To many of us this sounds ridiculous. But let's clear something up here. about:blank is not *itself* anything bad, but there exists malware / spyware / hijackers in the Windows world, which use vulnerabilities in Internet Explorer and install unwanted software on your computer. Some of these will hijack your homepage to show ads, popups and stuff like that. Some of these will set your homepage to about:blank, which is builtin page in most browsers.

Open firefox and enter about:blank in address bar. See? address bar gets blanked (1.0.6) and you're shown a blank page. Do it in any browser, opera, konqueror, IE... and you will get a blank page (about:blank may stay on address bar). This behaviour has nothing to do with spyware / adware / malware - it is a builtin feature of a web browser. To get rid of it, install lynx (text based browser), I just checked - it doesn't have this feature. ;-)
