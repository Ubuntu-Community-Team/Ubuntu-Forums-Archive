### Post by momist on 2008-04-19
I was: Bumping this - I found something somewhere on this forum about three months ago, and can't find it again!  I've tried. 

Anyway, I've found a work-around.  I really don't understand this.  I can find no trace in my files ystem of the "mounted" PocketPC, which will synce happily with Evolution.  BUT:  If I place a "launcher" on my desktop with 

Type: Location  
Name: PocketPC 
Location: synce:///

this 'just works'.  

OK, I'm happy now, and will flag this as SOLVED.  But I still don't understand.  And why should un-mounting by right-clicking on my previous icon have lost it?
