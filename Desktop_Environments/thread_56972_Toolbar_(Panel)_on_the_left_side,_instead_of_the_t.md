### Post by phibuntu on 2005-08-16
found a way myself

- remove the "main gnome menu" from the top panel.
- move the top-panel to the left side.
- add the "main gnome menu" again to the panel.

(If I didn't remove the menu, the left-panel was too wide to handle.) :)
