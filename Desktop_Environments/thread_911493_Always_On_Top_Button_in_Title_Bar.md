### Post by mcduck on 2009-02-02
I suppose you mean titlebar (which is part of the window borders and not taskbar (actually Window List-applet) which sits in your panel and shows running programs).

In that case it is possible if you use Compiz with Emerald as window decorator. Gnome's own window manager Metacity doesn't support any extra buttons.
