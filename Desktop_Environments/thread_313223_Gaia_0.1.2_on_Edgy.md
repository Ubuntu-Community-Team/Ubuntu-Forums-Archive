Gaia has been rereleased using NASA World Wind graphics! 
I compiled 0.1.1 for myself just some hours ago, and it works fine.

Some troubles though; still not nearly the great resolution that google earth has of course. The data download is very slow, and the 3d mode (globe) doesn't work that well.
