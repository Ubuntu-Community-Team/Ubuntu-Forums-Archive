### Post by jasmuz on 2005-08-17
There is an easier way, just fool the pages into thinking you have IE6.

Download and install this to your Firefox: [http://ftp.mozilla.org/pub/mozilla.org/extensions/user_agent_switcher/user_agent_switcher-0.6.6-fx+mz.xpi](http://ftp.mozilla.org/pub/mozilla.org/extensions/user_agent_switcher/user_agent_switcher-0.6.6-fx+mz.xpi)

After installed, reopen FF and under the tools  tab on the top you will be able to select another  ID for your browser.

Hope this does the trick, besides you dont loose anything.
