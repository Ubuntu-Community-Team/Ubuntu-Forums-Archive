### Post by Therion on 2009-06-07
Easy peasy, lemon squeezey when you know how...

Go to System/Preferences/Appearance - **Theme** tab, **Customize** button, **Colors** tab.

Click on the "Text" block of the Windows section to change the colors of the fonts used on your gnome panels.

*Voila!*
