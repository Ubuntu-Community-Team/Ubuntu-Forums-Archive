### Post by raeb on 2007-10-24
It depends on what kind of functionality you want.  Personally, I'm running on Fluxbox, which is very minimalist and more keyboard oriented (defining hotkeys is extremely easy).  However it takes a lot of time to get setup to the way you like it, and there's a bit of a learning curve (but there are very knowledgable people on these forums for any questions you may have regarding fluxbox.)  As far as customization goes, everything is at your fingertips.  Anyhow, just my 2 cents.  There are other others out there like e17 and xfce.  Just pick one and start experimenting  :D , that's half the fun.
