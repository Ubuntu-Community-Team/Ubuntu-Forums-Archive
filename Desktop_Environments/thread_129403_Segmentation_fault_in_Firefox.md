### Post by happyponcho42 on 2006-02-13
It seems a lot of people report similar problems with firefox's random crashing.  Personally, it has gotten on my nerves to the extent that I have totally ditched it and gone with Opera.

Some say to check your extentions, but even a clean vanilla firefox setup crashed often for me.  The extentions are great and all, but if they really are the cause of all the crashes, it's just not worth it.

However, if you really wish to stay with firefox, I suggest to you look for the sessionsaver extention.  If you're in the middle of something and firefox crashes, sessionsaver will load up all your previous tabs when you load firefox up again.
