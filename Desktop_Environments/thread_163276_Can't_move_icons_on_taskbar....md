### Post by cgjones on 2006-04-20
If you right click on the applet you want to move, there should be an option to lock it. Uncheck that option then right click again and click move. Once you have it where you want it, just left click the mouse.

### Post by aysiu on 2006-04-20
Right-click on the top taskbar and select "Configure panel." Then go to Appearance and Advanced. Make sure you click "Visible" and then apply all your changes. Then you can move the vertical squiggly thing next to each applet.
