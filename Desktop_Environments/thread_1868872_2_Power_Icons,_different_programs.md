### Post by jfacemyer on 2011-10-24

I can kill the XFCE process and get rid of that one, but the other one doesn't seem to have a specific process associated that I can find.

I think this may be causing some power issues (like not coming out of sleep correctly sometimes) and besides, it's annoying.

Any thoughts?

### Post by gsmanners on 2011-10-24
If you right-click on the notification area (not the "Indicator Plugin"), then select "Properties," you can use the dialog that follows to hide whatever icons in there you don't want to see.
