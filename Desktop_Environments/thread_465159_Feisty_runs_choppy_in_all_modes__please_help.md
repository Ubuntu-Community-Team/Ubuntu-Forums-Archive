### Post by vinnn on 2007-06-05
Rather than blaming the restricted driver for crashing X, I would suspect your xorg.conf file.
Comparing it to mine for instance there's a bunch of unnecessary stuff in there which looks like they're specific to running an Intel GPU.

Why are the **GLcore**, **dri**, **dbe** and **record** modules being loaded?
Where's the **glx** module?
Are you sure that your Nvidia card is on busid PCI:1:0:0 ?

Try backing up your xorg.conf file and running either...
```
sudo dpkg-reconfigure -phigh xserver-xorg
```
or
```
sudo nvidia-xconfig
```
to try to auto update your xorg.conf file, that should at least give you a nudge in the right direction.
