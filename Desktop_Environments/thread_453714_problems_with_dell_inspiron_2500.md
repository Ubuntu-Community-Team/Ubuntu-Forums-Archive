Im not a 'commercial buyer' meaning I don't like to buy OEM manufactured computers. I build the computers myself since it gives me more control and flexibility of what im trying to make. Although I do have a Dell Optiplex 320 that was donated to my brother by dell since my brother joined this 'Online Tutoring Program' called Catapult Online.
