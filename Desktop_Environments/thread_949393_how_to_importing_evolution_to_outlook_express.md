### Post by ilrudie on 2008-10-16
Eudora uses the same mbox mail storage format that evolution uses.  You can import your evolution emails into eudora and then import your eudora emails into outlook express.  Just so you are aware though outlook express has massive security problems and I believe is no longer supported or updated by MS so you may want to proceed with caution.
