### Post by mips on 2006-04-25
Most **SIP compliant** clients will work. Can look at something like **OpenWengo**.
[http://www.voipbuster.com/en/faq.html#1](http://www.voipbuster.com/en/faq.html#1)

Stuff like GnomeMeeting should work as well, [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_SIP_software#SIP_Clients](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_SIP_software#SIP_Clients)

Asterix is overkill as it is actually a PBX system.
Skype will not work as it is prop. and does not use SIP.
