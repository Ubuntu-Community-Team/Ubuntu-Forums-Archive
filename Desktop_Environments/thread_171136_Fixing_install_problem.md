### Post by GuitarHero on 2006-05-06

---

### Post by Gannin on 2006-05-06
In the terminal, type &#8220;sudo dpkg --configure -a&#8221;.  Typing &#8220;sudo&#8221; before the command is what does the magic.  When you hit enter, it'll ask you for your password.  Just type in the same password you used when you created your account during installation.
