### Post by sharp11 on 2012-10-24
I just upgraded from 10.04 to 12.04 and am now struggling a bit with the new app switcher. The main problem is the delay in popping up, but I'm also not sure that the new behavior of folding multiple windows into one icon will work for me.

I've seen [this post]("http://askubuntu.com/questions/45821/simpler-alt-tab-in-unity") of using compiz (CCSM) to change the app switcher, but it appears that it no longer works in 12.04.

### Post by sharp11 on 2012-11-15
Finally got around to researching a solution for this - and found a good one: switch to Unity 2D. At the login prompt, clicking on the Ubuntu symbol reveals this option. Unity 2D brings back a simple, snappy task switcher. What a relief!
