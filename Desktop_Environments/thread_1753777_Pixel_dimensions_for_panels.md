### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-05-09
For Gnome Panel? It's just defined by your screen resolution; if you raise or lower the resolution, the length of the panel will change to suit. 

You can change the thickness, too, by right-clicking and selecting Properties. (And if you don't have them expanded, the breadth will just be that of whatever items you have added to them.)
