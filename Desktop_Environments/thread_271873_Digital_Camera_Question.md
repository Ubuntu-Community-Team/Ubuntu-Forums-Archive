### Post by jethro10 on 2006-10-05
Hi,
similarly to Kateikyoushi

FSpot to manage you photos and The Gimp to edit them. I use Gimp in place of other modifying tools like the bits built into fspot as fspot and similar, like Picassa, do not modify the original. If you then were to burn a CD of photos to take to a friends house, you get the original unmodified ones if you had stuck to fspot or Picassa.

Almost any digital camera mounts and looks like a USB drive, just go to the image folder in DCIM directory and cut 'n' paste the pictures to where you want them.

Beware, any program that might modify the original picture might loose the exif information however. Test if first on a copy !!
