### Post by him610 on 2022-02-20
> When I am selecting a whole line of text with the keyboard, I press home (7) and then shift-end (1).
That inputs a 1 instead of selecting the text.
That is the correct behavior. When NUMLOCK is disabled, pressing Shift temporarily locks to Numbers as long as Shift is pressed.
I assume you are **not** using a mouse to select and copy. 
If only keyboard is being used to maneuver the cursor, select, copy and delete text, I believe there are key combinations that can be used to select complete lines of text as well as partial lines of text. I don't know what the key combinations are. If it is for a text editor of the *vi * or one of its derivatives *vim/gvim* there is help within the editor itself.
You might be able to reconfigure some of the keys on your keyboard.
