### Post by JSchwage on 2006-06-13
First of all I'd recommend upgrading your RAM. I tried Breezy on 64 MB of RAM and it's not pretty. After upgrading to 6.06 Dapper and getting 128 MB more of RAM, it runs much faster and smoother. If you're a diehard Firefox fan such as myself and are turned off by the slowness of it on such an old machine, I'd suggest trying [Swiftfox](http://www.getswiftfox.com) which basically is an optimized version of Firefox and is much quicker and snappier. I myself don't use OpenOffice as I hate Java-based applications. As you suggested, try AbiWord instead.

But before you do that, definitely buy more RAM. Either that or try [Xubuntu](http://www.xubuntu.org/), [VectorLinux](http://www.vectorlinux.com/), or [Puppy Linux](http://www.puppylinux.org/).

EDIT: You might also want to try out Epiphany for a web browser. It's a bit more lightweight than Firefox.
