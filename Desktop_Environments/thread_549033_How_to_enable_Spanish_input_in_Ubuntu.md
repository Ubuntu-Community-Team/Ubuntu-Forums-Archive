### Post by Chymera on 2007-09-12
right click on your panel, click "add to panel" scroll down until you find the entry "keyboard indicator" (it's got some flags above it, czech, irish and us i think :-P ) and click "add".
Now right click the new entry on your panel, go to keyboard prefs>layout>add and scroll down until you find your spanish character set. I've got spanish charsets, i dont see why you wouldnt :)
