### Post by ianp5a on 2012-05-23
The **Quality** setting in Shutter must have been changed when you upgraded.

Go **Edit-Preferences** and on the **Main** tab, slide the **Slider** at the top to the right.

I have **85** set on the slider and the quality is good.

I had the same problem upgrading to Ubuntu 12.04. But not such a cute baby!
