### Post by prshah on 2008-05-19
> **thezood said:**
> Hi,
I've been looking for a way to display on screen when keys like Caps and Num lock are activated,

Right click your gnome panel, choose "Add to Panel" and select "Keyboard indicator", then Add, then Close; right click to configure which indicators to display.
