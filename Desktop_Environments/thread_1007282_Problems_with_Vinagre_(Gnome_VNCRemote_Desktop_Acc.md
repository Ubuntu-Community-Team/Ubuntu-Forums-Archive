### Post by mattman85 on 2008-12-10
On the computer you want to VNC into, try right-clicking on Applications and clicking on Edit Menus.  Go to Other, and then check the box for Screens and Graphics.  Close, and then go to Applications > Other > Screens and Graphics.  is your monitor set to plug and play?  try setting it to one of the other options with 1024x768 in it.. I dont know if it will help, but it worked for me on my one desktop that I use a KVM switch on..
