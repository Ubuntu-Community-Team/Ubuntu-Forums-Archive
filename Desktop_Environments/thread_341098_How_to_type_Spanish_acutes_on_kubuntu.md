                      fa  (ú)

             +? =¿

### Post by Baptiste on 2007-01-19
I found it. I had to activate the compose touch.

compose + ' (le 4) + a or e or i or o or u = á é í ó ú
compose + two times ! = ¡
compose + shift+ two times ? = ¿
