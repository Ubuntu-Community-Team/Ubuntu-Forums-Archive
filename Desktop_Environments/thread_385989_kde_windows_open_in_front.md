### Post by coolclassic on 2007-03-16
I get frustrated when i open a new window whilst working on the original window. The new window opens over the one I am working on and I have to stop what I am doing to move it. I would like the new window to open behind or minimised is this possible.

---

### Post by dbd on 2007-03-17
If you go to the "Window Behaviour" section in "System Settings" then in the "Advanced" tab there is an option called "Focus stealing prevention level". Just increase this to "Normal" and you should have the behaviour you require :)
