### Post by 3rag0n on 2008-10-06
*Rt-Click* on background (assuming you are using GNOME) and click change desktop background. Click on the "Theme" tab, click the "customize" button , and in the new window, click on the "Pointers" tab. You get a list of pointers.:lolflag:

### Post by 3rag0n on 2008-10-06
In KDE, press Alt+F2.
Type *kcmshell mouse*. Hit Enter. You get a window with pointer prefs.
