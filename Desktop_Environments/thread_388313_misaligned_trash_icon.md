### Post by Kobalt on 2007-03-19
Right-click on your Trash to see the menu and click on "Move" to move it on your panel. If the desktop choser at the right of your panel is blocking it from being in the corner, right-click on the desktop choser and select "Unlock" it. Now it shouldn't prevent you from moving your Trash... After that you might want to lock those items into your panel (also with a right-click) not to see them move around after a reboot.

### Post by Skidpad on 2007-03-19
Before you can move it, you will probably need to unlock both it and the workspace selector.  Right-click on the workspace selector, and then left-click Lock-To-Panel, this should un-check/unlock it, allowing you to move it out of the way to put your trashcan icon there.

Do the same steps to you trash can icon, and then right click it again and select move.  Slide it over to the desired position, and then do the same with the workspace selector.

Once you are done, right-click both icons again, and select Lock-To-Panel...this will lock them both into the positions you've selected.
