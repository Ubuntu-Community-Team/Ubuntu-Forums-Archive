### Post by lidex on 2009-12-06
Go to "Account Settings" in "Edit" menu. Select "Server Settings" under your account. On the bottom-right you'll see a field labelled "Local directory". Click the browse button next to that and navigate to your mail folder. Mine is located at "/home/lidex/.mozilla-thunderbird/9kg3c33d.default/Mail/pop.fuse.net"
