### Post by Rikeshar on 2011-02-21
Hey guys, recently my window list in the bottom panel (where it shows all the minimized windows, etc.) got removed. When I added it back, it's not aligned all the way to the left as expected, it's more towards the middle. Any ideas on how to fix this?

---

### Post by Tamlynmac on 2011-02-21
On the panel, you find two vertical dotted lines, side by side. Right click on the lines and select move from the options in the drop down menu. You may need to select unlock first (by selecting it from the drop down menu) and right clicking again to select move.

Move the dotted lines as far to the left as possible without going beyond any other icons on the left side of the panel.

When done, right click on the dotted lines and lock it to the panel.

Good Luck & hope this helps.
