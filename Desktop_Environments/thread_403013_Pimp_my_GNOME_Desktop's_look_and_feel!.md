### Post by sandman94 on 2008-02-17
Have you tried Mac4Lin ? It skins the windows with an Aqua-type look. Also, as a replacement for the GNOME-Menu, take a look at SLAB:

```

sudo apt-get install gnome-main-menu

```

and then go to your lower GNOME Panel, right-click and select "Add to Panel..." then click on "Menu Bar" Applet.
