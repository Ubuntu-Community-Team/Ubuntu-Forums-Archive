### Post by bryanlking on 2007-04-05
Okay, I figured it out.  It's not really broken.  Once you install a new theme, it gets listed under a fourth tab of the add-on applet.  

At the bottom of that fourth tab is a "Restart Firefox" button.  You have to restart Firefox this way, or none of your subsequent theme changes will take place.  Once you've changed it here, you can change themes normally from now on.
