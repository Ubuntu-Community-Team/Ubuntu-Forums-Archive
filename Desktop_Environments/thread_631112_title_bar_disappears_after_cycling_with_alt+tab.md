### Post by kapello on 2008-03-10
I have this problem too! I assume you are all using Visual Effects? A slightly better solution than logging out is this:

Open Preferences > Appearance

Click 'Visual Effects' tab

Select "None (no visual effects)"

Then re-select your original setting (either 'Normal' or 'Extra'). Program will ask if you want to keep these settings, say 'yes', hey presto, your title bars are back!

Also bear in mind that even without a title bar, you can still gtrab windows by holding 'Alt' and then dragging them with the mouse.

I assume this problem is down to a combination of visual effects and themes. It started happening when I installed a compiz theme and set my visual effects to 'normal' (rather than 'none').
