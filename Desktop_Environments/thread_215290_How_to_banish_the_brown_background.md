### Post by dpm on 2006-07-14
There's two places where you have to change the background colour:

1) System>Administration>Login Window, "Background" button, as you already did and,

2) Right-click on the desktop, choose the "Change Desktop Background" entry in the context menu, and click on the "Desktop Colors" button.

I hope this helps.
