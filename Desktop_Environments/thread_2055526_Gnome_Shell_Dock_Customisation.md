### Post by hictio on 2012-09-09
Take a look at the extesion called [Dock](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/17/dock/), and then at this link: [How can I move the gnome dock to the left side?](http://askubuntu.com/questions/154034/how-can-i-move-the-gnome-dock-to-the-left-side).

### Post by jim_deadlock on 2012-09-09
Thanks, for future reference the easy method is:

Open dconf-editor

Go to org->gnome->shell->extensions->dock

toggle autohide and left/right position from there
