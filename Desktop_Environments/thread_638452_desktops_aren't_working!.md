### Post by Tweenk on 2007-12-12
Gutsy has added some Vista-like desktop effects by default (the program that is responsible for them is called Compiz), but they may sometimes render your system unworkable with some graphics cards. Go to System->Preferences->Appearance->Desktop Effects tab and try turning them off.

BTW, Compiz effects don't require a brand new graphics card like Vista does,they run very well even on a GeForce MX 440 with 32MB of RAM (though I had to do some tweaking - with 64MB of video memory it should work without issues).
