### Post by krazyd on 2009-06-03
> **lodp said:**
> Its funny -- just today I noticed that Dolphin now shows some meta info in the sidebar (image resolution, some tags for mp3s, but no codec info on videos). That's definitely a sign that things have been implemented under the hood..
[This]("http://nepomuk.kde.org/") is still being implemented. Expect more in KDE 4.3 (see the 10th item in [this table]("http://techbase.kde.org/Schedules/KDE4/4.3_Feature_Plan#kdebase-apps")).
> **lodp said:**
> while i'm at it -- why are there no id tags on videos, similar to the ones on audio files? anybody know that? I can't be the only one with a growing collection of video files, and they're a pain to organize..
There are - unless you use the archaic AVI format (which is still used everywhere unfortunately).
Other video containers such as MP4, MKV, OGM etc. have metadata. [See here]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Comparison_of_container_formats").
