### Post by coolclassic on 2005-08-03
This may be a way of working around your request.

Goto>settings>appearance & themes>backgroud

and in the kde control module 
select 'No picture' 
select 'advance options'

Here you would select 'use the following program for drawing backgroud'
>add

name: Konqueror desktop
command: konqueror
executable:konqueror

Now when you reboot you have konqueror, you may have to play around with it to keep full screen and sticky.

Hope this helps.
