### Post by improverrr on 2005-07-31
This could be an interesting deathmatch.  I have been using Kubuntu for a few months now.  Love it, although it did take some configuring to get it the way I wanted it.  But yesterday switched to PCLinuxOS, but I dont know if it will be for good, but here is the likes and dislikes I come up with so far:

**Kubuntu:**

_Likes: _ Super easy to install, get-apt installers, easy to use, alot of repositories for a ton of software.  Sound quality is the best I have heard for my soundcard (creative PCI128).  Better forum support.

_Dislikes: _ limited software upon install which leads to much configuration, needs better updates.

**PCLinuxOS:**

_Likes:_  Very good install from the CD (in some cases, too much), get-apt installers, very little setup required, outstanding updates

_Dislikes: _ Installer was alittle different than the norm, sound quality lacks alittle bit, The forum support is alittle overwhelming and  doesn't seem to have as many people on it yet.


All in all, both are incredible distros.
