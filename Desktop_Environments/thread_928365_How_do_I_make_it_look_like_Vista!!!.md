### Post by irrdev on 2008-09-24
Wait until Kubuntu 8.10 comes out in October - it uses KDE 4.1, whereas previous versions of Kubuntu used KDE 3.x. KDE 4 is extremely Vistaish to start with. Simply change the wallpaper and rearrange the panel icons/plamoids a bit, and your computer will look 85% like Vista. You can tweak it a bit further by changing the KWindow theme, or by installing Compiz and the [Emerald]("http://wiki.compiz-fusion.org/Decorators/Emerald") Windows Decorator and applying a [Vista Compiz]("http://www.compiz-themes.org/content/show.php/Vista+Basic?content=44228") theme.
