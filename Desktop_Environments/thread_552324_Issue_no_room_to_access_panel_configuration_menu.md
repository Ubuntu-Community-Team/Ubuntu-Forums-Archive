### Post by Wolki on 2007-09-16
> **lduperval said:**
> 
Is there a way (besides deleting icons) to access the Preferences (Properties?) menu of a panel?

Not that I know of, and the same has happened to me on occasion. If you have a window list on the moved panel though, you can just make it a bit smaller (drag on the small handle to the left of it), which should give you enough space to move the panel or access the properties menu.

If not, you'll have to remove some applets, then re-add them later.
