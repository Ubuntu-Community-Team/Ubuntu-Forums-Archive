### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-02-05
My up to date Ubuntu 13.04 has Gnome 3.6.2. Ubuntu will not go to Gnome 3.7 as that is for development purposes. Odd-numbered Gnome environments are development environments. The next stable release of Gnome will be 3.8 and that will come out too close to the 13.04 release 
date to be included. But you never know.

Ubuntu 12.04 has Linux kernel 3.2.0 series. Ubuntu 12.10 has Linux kernel 3.5.0 series. Ubuntu 13.04 has Linux kernel 3.8.0 series. Testing has been done to see how well the 3.5.0 kernel will work on 12.04. Wait until 12.04 gets the 3.5.0 series kernels (any time soon). It will be less risky.

I would recommend installing 12.04 in another partition and experimenting with it and not with your normal Ubuntu installation. It is an experiment. Mess up your OS and you may need to re-install. Better to experiment on another install of Ubuntu that you do not mind breaking.

Regards.
