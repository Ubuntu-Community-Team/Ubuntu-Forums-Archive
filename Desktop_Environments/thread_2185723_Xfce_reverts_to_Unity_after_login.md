### Post by Bucky Ball on 2013-11-04
Just a tip, you have probably discovered you now have duplicates and apps menus full of apps you don't want or need. *butnu-desktop anything is a bad move. If you wanted to try a lightweight desktop environment you should install just the desktop environment, xfce4, and choose that at login, NOT xubuntu-desktop. That drags in EVERYTHING, as you would have discovered.

You basically now have Ubuntu AND Xubuntu installed on the same machine, not a recipe for sleekness and speed. ;)
