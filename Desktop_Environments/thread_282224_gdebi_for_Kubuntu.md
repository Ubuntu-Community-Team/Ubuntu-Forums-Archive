### Post by Jucato on 2006-10-22
There is no exact GDEBI counterpart in Kubuntu, but you can right-click on a .deb file, and select Kubuntu Package Menu. You'll have the choice to install, remove, or view the package. But all this does is really launch xterm with a dpkg command. (It will still ask for your password of course).
