### Post by Gallardo Spyder on 2007-04-13
When you go into K3b - just go to Create Data DVD or whatever you are trying to do.

If your DVD+RW has data on it, and you did not do multisession...  Once you click burn, it will prompt you to overwrite the data that is current on the disk.

I had the same question as well - but once you get to the burn part is where it will ask you to overwrite...

Hope that helps,

If not try downloading the newest version of K3b (do a quick goolge search, I just updated mine and a few new features, it burns faster, and it overwrites DVD on burn - can't remember if the older version did nor not).
