### Post by brimlar on 2008-07-10
It's tough to say what you did, though I've personally always found that if you have an LCD screen and go to System > Preferences > Appearance > Fonts and turn off Hinting or change it to Slight, the fonts look much better (to me.)

The mstcorefonts, etc package is the package for the "base" Microsoft fonts, the ones that are pre-installed in Microsoft operating systems like XP.  It's proprietary, which is why it's not loaded by default.
