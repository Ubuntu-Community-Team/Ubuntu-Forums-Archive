### Post by bergfly on 2011-05-03
What graphics card do you have? KDE supports pretty much whatever your monitor can do, but the default drivers for you graphic card may not. Try opening your menu, clicking on applications and going to system. There should be an additional drivers icon in there. Click on that and it should find your graphics card and recommend a driver to install. Do so and you should get the full power of the card. A reboot is required.
