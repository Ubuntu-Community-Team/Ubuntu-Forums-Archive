### Post by kaamos on 2006-01-31
For disk space: System->Administration->Disks or the command "df -h" in a terminal.

If you mean the panels at the top and bottom of the screen, they are freely movable and you can remove the other if you want to. Just right click on the panel and choose to remove. You can also add stuff to the panels with a right click and "add to panel".

Hope this helps!
