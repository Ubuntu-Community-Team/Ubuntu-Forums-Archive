### Post by p_quarles on 2008-08-04
Of course it's possible. :)

I've done this with several machines. I ordinarily just copy settings for specific programs, rather than everything in the home directory, but the latter is what contains all of your settings. 

Just copy the relevant directories from one machine to the other, and you're set to go. The only question is how you plan to connect the two computers so that you can copy the files, and there are several approaches to that (the basic two categories being networking and external storage media).
