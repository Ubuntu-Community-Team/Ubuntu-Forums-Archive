### Post by dude of wonders on 2006-02-01
well i figured it out if any else has had this problem. for some reason it wanted to play them instead of download them, but heres what to do to fix it. 

i went to "edit" and then "preferences" in the fire fox browser, clicked on "downloads" and clicked "view and edit actions". from there select whatever files arent downloading and select them and click "change action" and click "save them to my computer".

in my case it was the avi. files and the wmv. files, hope this helps anyone with the same problem.
