### Post by engine on 2010-03-24
Hm. Just found the answer to one of my questions; I'll assume you all thought it was so thunderingly obvious it wasn't worth bothered to reply... If I simply wake the netbook up after hibernation, I never see (of course) the login screen. But when I've shut it down completely and start it up, then I do see the login screen and voilà: a pull-down for "language". Changing the choice there to "English UK" does the trick.
