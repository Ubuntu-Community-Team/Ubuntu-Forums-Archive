### Post by bigsmitty64 on 2011-04-30
I switched to classic desktop also (not really a huge fan of unity), but my cube works flawlessly. I checked "desktop cube" and "rotate cube" in compiz config settings manager. Also make sure you have the # of desktops set to 4 horizontal and 2 vertical. That is in the compiz/general/general options. Good luck, hope this helps.:D
