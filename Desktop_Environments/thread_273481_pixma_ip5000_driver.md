### Post by orrie on 2008-07-14
Go to the "System" (located at the main menu), then "Administration" and then "Printers".
You should then click on "Edit" and choose "New printer".

Now you should see your PIXMA iP5000 in the list to the left.
When I searched, I got "Canon iP5000 USB #1"; choose it!

Then just click "Next" and choose "Canon" on the request "Select printer from database".

After doing this, choose "PIXMA iP5000" and give your printer an name if everything goes well.

Now you can print until your toner is empty! Happy printing :)
