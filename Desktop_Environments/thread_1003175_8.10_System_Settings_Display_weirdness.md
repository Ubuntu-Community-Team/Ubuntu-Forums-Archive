### Post by Zorael on 2008-12-05
It not saving your changes sounds like a bug, might want to report that on [KDE's bugtracker](http://bugs.kde.org) and/or [Launchpad](http://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu).

You can automate the change, or limit the max resolution to what you want it to be, though. There are several ways; see [this wiki entry](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Config/Resolution). It's sort of technical but should get you started. If you botch up your xorg.conf file you can always fix it in recovery mode, or Ctrl+Alt+F1 to a tty and do 'sudo dpkg-reconfigure -phigh xserver-xorg'.
