### Post by DarkN00b on 2006-12-29
Open the Beryl Settings Manager, expand Image Format and make sure PNG and SVG are checked. If that doesn't work, check to see if your image selections carried over the upgrade.

As far as the skydome, it has to be in 2:1 ratio -- i.e. 1024x512 or 1200x600.
