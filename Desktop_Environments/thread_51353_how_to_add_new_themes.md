### Post by Xian on 2005-07-23
Okay, I picked the [d3a](http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php?content=15963&PHPSESSID=92531b1ce63f28cc13cd461a7d3552d7) theme at random on the page you linked.
I installed it on my box and here are the steps:

[Download](http://www.gnome-look.org/content/download.php?content=15963&id=1) the theme to somewhere such as your desktop.
Right-click on 15963-d3a.tar.gz file and choose 'Extract Here'.

Open Nautilus and navigate to /home/username/Desktop.
Move the folder d3a from your desktop to /home/username/.themes

Open the gnome theme manager (System > Preferences > Theme)
You should see an entry that is the same as provided below.
