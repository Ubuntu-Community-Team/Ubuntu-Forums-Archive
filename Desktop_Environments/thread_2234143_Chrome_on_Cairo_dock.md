### Post by AnotherKevin on 2014-07-13
> **ubudog said:**
> How did you do it?


The easiest way I found is to open the application you want to include on your desktop while Cairo is up and running.  It will show an icon for the app (that disappears when app closes).  While open, right-click on the app's icon and hover over the app's name (usually second option down) and choose "Make It A Launcher".  The new app launcher should appear on the left side of the launcher, ready to go!

Hope that helps!
