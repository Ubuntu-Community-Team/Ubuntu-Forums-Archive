### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-04-29
If you did your upgrade through the Update Manager, then you probably still have the Classic desktop selected as your default. Log out and change it in the Session menu.

Otherwise, this might be a video card issue (and since Compiz is presumably still working, it'll be easy enough to resolve; there are just some cards that Unity won't launch with unless you ask it nicely.)

Edit: And rather stupidly, I forgot to ask: what *is* your graphics card (make and model?)
