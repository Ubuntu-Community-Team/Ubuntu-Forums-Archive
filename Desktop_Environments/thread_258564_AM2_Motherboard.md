### Post by noeffred on 2006-09-16
You should be fine with anyone as long as you've got an NFORCE chipset on it. They work pretty well on Linux. Lately I've had good experiences with Foxconn boards, they're quite nice and also rather cheap.

Personally I've got an Asus M2NPV-MX and as far as I can tell everything works.
