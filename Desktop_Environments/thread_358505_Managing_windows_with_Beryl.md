### Post by ZeldaFan on 2007-02-10
When I use beryl and say I click on a button in the current window I am using that opens up another window, the window appears behind my current window. But I want the window that pops up to be in front automatically, instead of having to click on it manually to bring it to the front. What settings do I have to change in Beryl to do that?

---

### Post by Waappu on 2007-02-11
Hi

Open Beryl Setting Manager.
Change General options "Level of Focus Stealing Prevention" to normal or low

---

### Post by ZeldaFan on 2007-02-11
Thanks a lot! That did the trick, and with the windows always appearing behind my current windows, it was always tedious to have to click on them again. Now it works much better to my liking.
