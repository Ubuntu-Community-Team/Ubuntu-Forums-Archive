### Post by Xian on 2005-07-10
Just open Synaptic and view the Gnome package section.
Remove anything that you don't use and isn't system critical.

Synaptic will prevent you from uninstalling any pkg that would cause other applications to fail, so you are safe to experiment and be as aggressive as you desire.
