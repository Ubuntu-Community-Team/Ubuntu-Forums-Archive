### Post by manicka on 2005-10-15
You may need to restart the gnome panel to see it in the menu. You can either log in again to do this or run.

```
killall gnome-panel
```

---

### Post by PaganHippie on 2005-10-15
That works for apps that know about Gnome. For others, you may have to use Smeg to add them to the app menu, or else just run the app from a terminal.
