### Post by kyphi on 2008-03-12
Laptop or desktop?  Dependable in which way?

The only issue I have come across is if you have a mail notification programme running such as the gmail notifier or the general mail notification applet.

This manifests itself as all mail services failing and can only be rectified by either a reboot or turning both suspend and hibernate to "never".

The reason for this becomng an issue is that the mail notification programmes check mail at user-specified intervals and if the power is in stand-by mode that creates a "conflict of interests".
