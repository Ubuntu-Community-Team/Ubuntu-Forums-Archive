### Post by fdimmling on 2005-12-22
Compared to commercial Windows products gocr is not champions league. However you can improve results considerably by checking that the text lines are really horizontal, scan resolution is high (300 dpi) and by scanning grayscale instead of binary. Furthermore you should load the result in some text processor with spell checking where you can easily correct the remaining errors. By these measures I get acceptable results for reasonably clear scans.

Good luck
