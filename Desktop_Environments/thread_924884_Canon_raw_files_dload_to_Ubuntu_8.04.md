### Post by GeordieJedi on 2008-09-20
Hi there.

1. If you have a card reader. The easy way would be to remove the memory card from the camera
 pop it into a card reader (internal or external). Then open up the folder, copy the images from the
 memory card, and paste them to a directory/folder on your PC.

2. You could connect the camera directly to the PC via its usb cable, (it "should" auto mount)
then once again  copy and paste the images to your PC's hard disk.
 [However its slower doing it this way, and uses up the precious battery life of the camera 
- Personally I just put the memory card directly in to the card reader]

Hope this helps mate. Take it easy
