### Post by faried on 2011-10-16
It took some searching, but I found the [solution]("http://lwn.net/Articles/447772/").  I had to restart apps to make them read the new config.  The rilly rilly easy way: **dconf write /org/gnome/desktop/interface/gtk-key-theme "'Emacs'"** (further down the thread, and yes you do need two levels of quotes).
