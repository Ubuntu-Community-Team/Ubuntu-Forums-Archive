### Post by b_martinez on 2006-08-21
in a terminal type in the command "dmesg |less" and use the up/down arrow keys to navigate through the messages. The vertical line is a 2 piece line somewhere on your keyboard, On both my handy kb's, it is above the \ [backslash]
