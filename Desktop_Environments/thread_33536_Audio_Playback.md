### Post by Zelut on 2005-05-11
Yeah, many of the distros dont ship with .mp3 support out-of-the-box anymore (some legal crap I guess).  its pretty easy to install it if you need to though.  Here's what you can do...

[http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#codecs](http://www.ubuntuguide.org/#codecs)

that may work for you.  Otherwise you can use the addonpack which can be downloaded here:

[http://www.mrbass.org/linux/ubuntu/](http://www.mrbass.org/linux/ubuntu/)

this is a script that installs everything on the ubuntuguide (each package is optional, of course).  Just download the ubuntuaddon.zip, unpack it & run 'sudo sh ubuntuaddon.sh' and you're set.

Hope that works for you.  I ran the addonpack on my system & I'm enjoying all 4000+ mp3s :)
