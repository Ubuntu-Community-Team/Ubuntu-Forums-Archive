### Post by sagara on 2010-05-09
You could also try **tilda**.  It is a terminal that is hidden on your desktop and when you press F1 it drops down from the top of the screen.  You press F1 again and it hides.  Very convenient.

In your case you could set it up to make up rise from the bottom of your screen.
