### Post by lian1238 on 2007-11-19
In AWN, you could middle-click on a task (which is in the launcher list) to open a new instance of it.
But, middle-click on a launcher (unlaunched) will open the AWN manager.
(Middle-clicking on a task which is not in the launcher list does nothing.)
