### Post by scooper on 2006-05-05
[QUOTE=Sutekh]You could use 

[adesklets](http://adesklets.sourceforge.net/)

[Conky](http://conky.sourceforge.net/)

[GKrellM](http://www.muhri.net/gkrellm/)[/QUOTE]

or you can also use WindowMaker-style [dockapps]("http://dockapps.org/") with an appropriate Gnome panel adapter for dockapps.  I'm not running gnome, so I can't tell you what the adapter is called.  It's either built-in by now or easy to find.

Of course it just dawned on me that you're probably looking for serious eye candy and stuff you put in the panel just ain't going to look as cool.  If so, adesklets or gdesklets are probably the way to go.
