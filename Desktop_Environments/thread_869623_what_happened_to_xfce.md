### Post by mali2297 on 2008-07-25
If you study the developers' [mailing list]("http://foo-projects.org/pipermail/xfce4-dev/2008-July/thread.html") or the [SVN repository]("http://svn.xfce.org/index.cgi/xfce"), you will see that there is still activity.

Xfce is a small project in comparison to Gnome and KDE, but I don't think it is at risk of dying. Even in the scenario that the original developers would abandon the project, I'm sure that others would be interested to take over.
