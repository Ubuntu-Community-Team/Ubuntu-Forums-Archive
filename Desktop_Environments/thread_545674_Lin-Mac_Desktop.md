### Post by Bob_Mendon on 2007-09-07
I just wanted to show everyone that it is possible for a relatively unsophisticated user to transform a generic Gnome desktop into something of beauty, form and function.

This is a combination of compiz-beryl & emerald with most of the theme related stuff coming straight from gnome-look.org and random places on the net. Much of the work was trial and error and reading bits of how-tos found here and there. I will try to put together an inventory of all the pieces when I get some more time.

Here's a partial list of some of the stuff I used.

Theme-Biztoe-Tiger Version 0.1 by Ben Morris
Dock- Avant Window Manager [http://www.planetblur.org/hosted/awnforum/](http://www.planetblur.org/hosted/awnforum/) by Neil Jagdish Patel
28686-mac-osx-bundle.tar.gz from gnome-look.org
OS-X.5-Window-Border.tar.bz2 from gnome-look.org
OS-L-IconSet-Buildkit.tar.bz2 from gnome-look.org
Envy for dual-view configuration (envy_0.9.7-0ubuntu8_all.deb)
