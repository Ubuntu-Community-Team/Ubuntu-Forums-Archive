### Post by ltmon on 2005-11-07
I have a SonyEriccson k700i, which is a bluetooth phone with "Human Interface Device" profiles.

Basically that means I can set up the phone to act exactly as a bluetooth keyboard/mouse.

Assign the correct shortcuts to my phone keys (this involved a text editor and sending a zip file back and forth) and corresponding global shortcuts to my desktop means I can now turn on my music from the ground floor of my flat (I live on the 4th).

It's a really slick optino if you have a HID capable phone.
