### Post by Maxtorm on 2006-11-28
Originally I thought this problem was isolated to my video card, but now that I have installed Edgy on a variety of dissimilar machines, I think it must be inherent in Edgy.

In short, when the main Update Manager window loses focus and then regains it automatically, the last button to have focus cannot be clicked on until the user clicks elsewhere first.

The easiest way to reproduce this behaviour is to wait for your next system update.  Once the "Update completed successfully." window appears, click the Close button on that window.  The Update Manager takes over and does its normal thing (i.e. checking versions, dependencies, etc.) and then the main Update Manager window reappears.  You cannot click on the Close button immediately.  You have to click anywhere outside the button so that the button loses focus.  Then you can click on it.  Is this a bug or am I missing something obvious?
