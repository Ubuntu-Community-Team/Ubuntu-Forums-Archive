### Post by Jakin on 2013-01-19
Its even easier to have your desktop set as "folder view" so that it acts like *real* desktop.
From there you can go into your menus and drag the program to the desktop "copy here" , giving you a clean icon, without a shortcut arrow.
Though you might notice a red "!" and then you'd have to give it execution permissions.
