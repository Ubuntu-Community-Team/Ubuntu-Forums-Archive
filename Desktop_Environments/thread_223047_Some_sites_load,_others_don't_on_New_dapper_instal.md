### Post by ardchoille on 2006-07-25
Have you disabled ipv6 in Firefox? I had your problem too until I disabled ipv6, now all sites load relatively fast.

To disable ipv6, open a new Firefox tab or window, type "about:config" (without quotes) in the location bar. Then in the Filter line, type in ipv6. If it is enabled, double click it to disable it. Close Firefox and reopen it. See if the sites load faster after that. I hope this helps.
