### Post by lukew on 2007-02-03
I had this problem to. Just fixed it... wahooo Veryl Rocks!!!!!

There are two sets of instructions on the UDSF one under the customisation section and one under the Eye Candy one. 

(I installed for NVIDIA)

Use 

[http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy#How_to_install_Beryl.2FAIGLX_.28Nvidia.29](http://ubuntuguide.org/wiki/Ubuntu_Edgy#How_to_install_Beryl.2FAIGLX_.28Nvidia.29)

and do the bit about 


    * Add this to xorg.conf "Screen" section 

# Enable 32-bit ARGB GLX Visuals
    Option "AddARGBGLXVisuals" "True"

&

    * Add this to xorg.conf "Device" section 

Option          "TripleBuffer" "true"

. The result. Themes change. Min Max buttons present and terminals render correctly instead of white!!!  

Luke
