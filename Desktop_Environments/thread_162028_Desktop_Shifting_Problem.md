### Post by CornMaster on 2006-04-18
I recently rearranged my physical desktop, and moved my second monitor to the other side of the desk.  I edited my xorg.conf accordingly to place my monitor to the left of my main instead of to the right.

However, now when I try to run some full screen game (planet penguin racer and Tremulous) the second monitor goes off, and display starts on my main monitor, but my main monitor is split in half.  One half is normal desktop, and the other is the game.  This obviously makes games unplayable.

Anyone face this before or have any insights?
