### Post by ronocdh on 2006-11-08
I do not believe it's possible to download only select e-mails that have been archived by Gmail. As you've probably noticed, you can on the settings page in Gmail set POP3 downloading for ALL mail; fire up Thunderbird and wait to pull down your gig or two of e-mail. That way you have it all archived on your hard drive, if that's what you want.

Be careful, though: if you do this process twice during the same week--maybe reinstalling builds or something--Google may suspend POP activity for your account, suspecting you of abusing the service.
