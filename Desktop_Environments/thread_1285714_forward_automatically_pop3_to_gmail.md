### Post by kerry_s on 2009-10-08
gmail can check pop, then all you have to do is check gmail.

in gmail-> settings-> accounts & import-> checkmail using pop3

i have my gmail grab my yahoo & hotmail, i have sylpheed grab my gmail for local reading or i just go to gmail in the browser.
