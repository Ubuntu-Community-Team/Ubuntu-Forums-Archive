### Post by GoldNugget on 2010-06-11
Hello community. This is a minor problem but it is annoying because I cannot figure out how to change it. 
I am using emerald for a window manager and generally works fine. But there is this odd decoration in the title bar transparency like lines or swirls that I cannot get rid of. I want the title bar to be transparent. These lines are present in all the theme engines. I have scoured the emerald settings manager and compiz-settings manager and cant find an answer. Anyone remember how they turned it off?

Edit: The answer was in Compiz-Settings Manager where I had ticked the 'Reflections' tab. Much better now. Thanks gingivere0!

---

### Post by gingivere0 on 2010-06-11
You need to uncheck Reflection in the Effects section of compiz.  That should fix it :)
