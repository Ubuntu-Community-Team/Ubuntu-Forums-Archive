### Post by grahammechanical on 2021-01-25
I think that this is a fitting topic for the Cafe section of this forum. 

That being said, I have reviewed two web sites on the subject of computer eye strain. One web site is that of a computer monitor manufacturer and the other web site was a well known American medical centre. Of, the various reasons given for eye strain the two sites do not mention the computer operating systems.

For myself, I would put my eye strain down to not blinking enough. Also poor room lighting conditions.

[https://www.eizoglobal.com/library/basics/10_ways_to_address_eye_fatigue/](https://www.eizoglobal.com/library/basics/10_ways_to_address_eye_fatigue/)

[https://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/eyestrain/diagnosis-treatment/drc-20372403](https://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/eyestrain/diagnosis-treatment/drc-20372403)

I expect that there will be differing views on this matter. How many will be subjective and not objective? How many views will be based on hearsay and not scientific research?

When I was in employment I was a qualified Health and Safety practitioner. Although the management of the high street store I worked in did not give me much credit for knowing what I was talking about. Had I continued working for that company I might have eventually been require to do a risk assessment on the use of Display Screen Equipment. Anyway, here are the UK government's Display Screen Equipment regulations for employers.

[https://www.hse.gov.uk/pubns/indg36.pdf](https://www.hse.gov.uk/pubns/indg36.pdf)

> Some workers may experience fatigue, eye strain, upper limb problems and backache from overuse or improper use of DSE. These problems can also be experienced from poorly designed workstations or work environments. The causes may not always be obvious and can be due to a combination of factors

> Arrange the desk and screen to avoid glare, or bright reflections. This is often easiest if the screen is not directly facing windows or bright lights. Adjust curtains or blinds to prevent intrusive light
