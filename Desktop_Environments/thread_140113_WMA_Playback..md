### Post by skymt on 2006-03-05
If they're from an online music store (Napster et al), you won't be able to play them at all from a native Linux app. Microsoft, who made the WMA DRM, isn't exactly interested in helping out Linux users. Your best bet is to use Wine to play them in whatever Windows app you use.
