### Post by Salvatos on 2012-05-18
This seems to be a common problem with 12.04 and Cinnamon, I have reported it on github here: [https://github.com/linuxmint/Cinnamon/issues/688](https://github.com/linuxmint/Cinnamon/issues/688)

Temporary workaround:
Cinnamon Settings > Themes > Other Settings
Switch the GTK+ Theme to anything and back to your regular one and the bar is refreshed

(Menu item names may differ, I'm not using the English interface.)
