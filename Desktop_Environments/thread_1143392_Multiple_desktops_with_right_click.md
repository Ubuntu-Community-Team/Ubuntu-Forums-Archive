### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-04-29
Do you have CompizConfig-Settings-Manager installed ??  If not you can install it via Synaptic.  then go to Utilities > Wallpaper and add the pics you want.  The only thing you will not see your desktop icons, as nautilus is no longer drawing your desktops.  You can still access your desktop icons via Places > Desktop.
