### Post by SilvaRizla on 2009-02-05
What you need to do is configure your desktop, right click on an empty space and add the folder applet, then configure this to /home/<YOU>/Desktop
You can do this for multiple folders, downloads or pictures for instance

Now right click on your taskbar and add the application launcher widgets

Ta-da!

Is the** K menu** not displayed on the left side of the bottom panel? (This is a blue button with a white K.) If not, right click on the panel and select 'add widget' from the menu. Then scroll until you find the K menu widget and select it. This should be what you're looking for. Consider the K menu as the 'start menu' that you find in Windows XP, etc.
