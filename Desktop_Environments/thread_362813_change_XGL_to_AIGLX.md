### Post by louis_nichols on 2007-02-16
Well... if they're both correctly installed, then all you have to do is start beryl-manager, right-click on the icon in the tray, select Beryl configuration>Rendering platform> AIGLX.

The exact name of the options may not be correct and I'm not at my machine, but it's easy to figure out.
