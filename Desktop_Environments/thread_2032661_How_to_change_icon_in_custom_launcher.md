### Post by rewyllys on 2012-07-24
Well, I finally discovered that I'd used an incorrect method of establishing a custom launcher.

What I should have done, and what works, is the following:

Right-click on Menu (in the left-hand corner of your panel) and select "Edit menu".  In the resulting "Main Menu" window, pick your preferred category of Applications (in my case, "System Tools").  This opens a sub-window, in which you click on "New Item".  Then fill in its slots as appropriate.

Next, identify the location of the icon you want to use.  Then **double-click** on the icon image in the New Item window. This will open still another window, and in this window you can go to the location of the desired icon; then highlight it; and choose "Open".

Finally, close the Main Menu window.  The new item will appear when you choose Menu from your panel.  If you want a copy of the launcher to appear on your Desktop, just right-click on the launcher icon and select "Add to desktop."
