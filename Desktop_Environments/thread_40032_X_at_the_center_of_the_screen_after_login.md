I posted a similar topic a few weeks ago and never got a response, for whatever reason, I do not know. But, since it's been brought back up, I'll share my experience with you. 

I haven't figured out why it's there, but I have found a 'cheap' way to get rid of it, by goofing around with it a little. Drag your mouse over it so it looks like you're making a box with the highlighted area over the X. The open any window, and attempt to use your mouse to drag the window anywhere. The X will disappear.
