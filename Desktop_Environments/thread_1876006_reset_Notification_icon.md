### Post by BillyBoa on 2011-11-05
If you go to the upper right icon, you click and the first choice is System Settings. Go to Time & Date and click the tab Clock and then check the first choice to show a clock in the menu bar. Hope it was that easy.

If you don't find it in dash use the following command . Logout and in and the icon will appear in dash .The indicator will appear on the panel when the icon is selected .   ```
