### Post by rgraves22 on 2007-05-29
I think I fixed X

There was a backup xorg.conf in the /etc/x11/ folder.. it was xorg.conf.bakup.23434343234 ( a whole bunch of numbers ).

Logged in as root, renamed the xorg.conf..... to xorg.conf rebooted and it came up fine!
