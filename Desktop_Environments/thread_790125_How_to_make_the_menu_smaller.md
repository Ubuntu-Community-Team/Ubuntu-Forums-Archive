### Post by helino on 2008-05-11
Hi, if you right click on the panel and choose "Add to panel", you can add "Main menu", which is exactly what you looking for. 
You can then right click on the "Applications" menu and click "Remove from panel". Now drag your "Main menu" to the place you want it, and you're done!
