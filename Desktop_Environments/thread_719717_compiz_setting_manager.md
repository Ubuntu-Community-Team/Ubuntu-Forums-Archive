### Post by -random on 2008-03-09
Go to your compiz settings > Desktop Cube > Appearance > Skydome.

Click the empty checkbox to enable the skydome. From there you can either add a image or just change the colors (or add a image with colors you want).

For the other 'hyperspace' (i don't know what it's called either :P ) colors go to your 'Cube Reflection' plugin settings.
