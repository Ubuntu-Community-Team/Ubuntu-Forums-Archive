### Post by Dry Lips on 2012-05-22
I don't use docky or conky but these are the suggestions I have:

Go to System Settings>Startup and Shutdown (under System Administration).
Then Go to "Session Management" and click "start with an empty session" (On Login).

This prevents the programs you have open when shutting down from being restored on startup.

(You could perhaps also have a look at "service manager", and see if docky and conky are listed there. )

Just a couple of suggestions. As I said I don't use docky, so let us know if this doesn't solve it...
