### Post by NicePics13 on 2006-07-29
Flash7 sucks, v9 is on its way.

**Mozilla/Firefox + MPlayer (with w32 codecs) + mplayerplug-in**
With this combo I can play any Quicktime (even HD), Real or WMV video. And resize it, view fullscreen and save to hd.
All programs are available from repos (main/universe/multiverse). A newer version of mplayerplug-in can be built from source.
[http://mplayerplug-in.sourceforge.net/](http://mplayerplug-in.sourceforge.net/)
