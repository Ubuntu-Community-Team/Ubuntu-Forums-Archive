### Post by cc8balla on 2009-03-22
Kk, I think posting about it got me to searching a bit deeper. It is fixed, I had compiz enabled, and to change it in Compiz, you need to go to ccsm > General options > and just type in the name of the theme as it appears in ~/Home > .icons.
