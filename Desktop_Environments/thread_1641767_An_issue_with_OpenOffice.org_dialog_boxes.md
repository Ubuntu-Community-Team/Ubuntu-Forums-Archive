### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-12-09
Although it doesn't really answer your "why" part of the question, I always save attachments to a folder in /home called Attachments, then open them and edit if needed from there.  I almost never open an attachment direct from Thunderbird, my email client.

All attachment files are also moved from the Attachment folder to a more appropriate home folder after saving; it works that way best for me as I don't have to even think about where attachments are going, I simply save all of them and then sort out what i should do with them later.
