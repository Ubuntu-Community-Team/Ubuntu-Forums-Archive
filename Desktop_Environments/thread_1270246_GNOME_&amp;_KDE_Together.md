### Post by Nate8nate on 2009-09-19
Just go to System -> Administration -> Synaptic Package Manager and check the package kubuntu-desktop.  Once all of the packages are installed, you probably will have to restart your computer.  On the log-in screen there should be a menu called sessions.  Choose KDE or Kubuntu and log in.  Choose Gnome to log-in to a Gnome desktop.
