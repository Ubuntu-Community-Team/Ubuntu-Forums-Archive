### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2009-07-05
This is how you change the minimize, maximize, exit, and open effects.

1.When you open CompizConfig go down to the Effects section enable Animations and Animations Add-ons.
2.Then click on the Animations icon. It should open a whole list of options.
3.Go to the white box with the animations in it. Then select the first line.
4.Click Edit.
5.When a new window opens click on the list of effects and change it to whatever you want.

This is just for the open animations (it works the same way for all of the others) you can change the other animations by clicking the tab you want.

Hope it helps.
