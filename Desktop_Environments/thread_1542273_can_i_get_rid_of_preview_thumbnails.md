Is there some way to turn them off?Open the folder you have your image files in (or any folder, really) and go into View. At the bottom, you can choose 'Icons', 'List' or 'Compact'. Compact should help with load times.

Also, in the same folder window, under Edit > Preferences, you can choose how you view all folder contents in the 'Preview' tab.
