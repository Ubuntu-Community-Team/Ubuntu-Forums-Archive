### Post by sands on 2007-08-20
Hi everyone. I got an idea to synchronize FF & Thunderbird profiles b/w windows & ubuntu. I don't know whether the same thing is posted somewhere here. I am posting this, if my experience would be helpful to someone.

1-> Goto to the windows drive & enter into the *documents & settings* directory
2-> Enter into your profile dir & then into *application settings.*
3-> There you can see *mozilla* if you have firefox & *thunderbird* if you have thunderbird.
4-> Right click & make a link to those directories
5-> Copy those to your home directory & rename them to .mozilla & .thunderbird

Thats it.. You are done.

In mozilla dir (windows drive) you might have to rename **F**irefox to **f**irefox.

Note: Have a backup of your profiles before doing anything.

Enjoy


I think this can be added to the ubuntu installation, where it copies the windows profiles to ubuntu. Just a thought.
