### Post by 3Miro on 2010-06-30
This is known issue with flash (I am assuming 64-bit) and Adobe doesn't seem interested in fixing it. What you can do is:

1. Install 64-bit flash player under 64-bit FireFox on your 64-bit Linux.

[http://nxadm.wordpress.com/2009/04/26/install-64-bit-adobe-flash-player-on-ubuntu-904/](http://nxadm.wordpress.com/2009/04/26/install-64-bit-adobe-flash-player-on-ubuntu-904/)

2. Install 32-bit flash player under 32-bit Swiftfox on your 64-bit Linux.

[http://getswiftfox.com/](http://getswiftfox.com/)
