### Post by Tenken on 2008-02-01
To apply the theme download it to your desktop, then go the System->Preferences->Appearance and drag and drop the *themename*.tar.gz file from your desktop into the themes window. When it asks choose to apply the theme now. If you want to change it later in the same window click the "Customize" button and choose a different theme under the "Controls" tab.
