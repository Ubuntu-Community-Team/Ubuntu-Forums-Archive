### Post by yager on 2006-07-18
I use a commercial service here in the US that depends on a piece of physical hardware that I install between my cable modem and my router that my computer is attached to.  It requires no software and is therefore not dependent on the use of any particular operating system.  In fact, it does not depend on a computer in any way.  You may want to look for a hardware based alternative to avoid any conflicts.  The nice thing is that I plug a standard  telephone into the back of the device, plug the device into the cable modem and after it starts up, it is online and ready to receive and make phone calls.

You can see a description and graphic of how it is hooked up at the following link.

[http://www.lingosupport.com/install_single_voip_phone.html](http://www.lingosupport.com/install_single_voip_phone.html)

Perhaps you can find a similar system that is offered in your marketplace.

Good Luck.
