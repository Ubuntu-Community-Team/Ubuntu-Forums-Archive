### Post by lnxconvrt on 2006-06-05
Short answer: 'sudo passwd -l root'.

Longer answer if you wish to read it: go to [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RootSudo]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RootSudo"), and read the part "Disabling the root account" about 2/3 of the way down the page.
