### Post by Tamlynmac on 2012-12-06
> janderson91z

This [site]("http://www.webupd8.org/2012/08/greybird-default-xubuntu-theme-gets.html") offers instructions on installing the Xubuntu 12.10 (Greybird) theme on Xubuntu 12.04. Their located in the Quantal Quetzal repositories in the "shimmer-themes" package.

Once installed it will be labeled "greybird-git".

As follows:
sudo add-apt-repository ppa:shimmerproject/ppa
sudo apt-get update
sudo apt-get install shimmer-themes-greybird

You can also get many other themes [here]("http://xfce-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=15x100x420&PHPSESSID=9fa7ba1222bb174cc51c929a54954c7c").

Hope this helps.
