### Post by coffeecat on 2012-07-20
Device eth0 is your ethernet connection, which is why you get the not ready message when connected with a mobile broadband USB device. There may be a way of getting firestarter to recognise the Huawei, but I suggest you uninstall firestarter before you get to the configuration stage. It is unmaintained (last time I checked) and buggy.

You might want to read some things in the [Security Discussions forum]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=338") before deciding what to do abut firewall configuration. Start with these two stickies:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1871177](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1871177)

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=510812](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=510812)
