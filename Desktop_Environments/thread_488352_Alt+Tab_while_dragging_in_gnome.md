### Post by ashwin_cse on 2007-06-30
If i understand it right. You want to drag & drop mp3 files from nautilus to ryhtmbox with rythmbox in the foreground & nautilus in the background. To do this, IMO you dont use alt+tab. There is a functionality called "On Top". On top forces a app to be in the foreground no matter what you do. So, simple solution is right click on rythmbox on panel & select on top. This brings rythmbox to foreground & nautilus to background. Now drag & drop files to rythmbox directly

--ashwin
