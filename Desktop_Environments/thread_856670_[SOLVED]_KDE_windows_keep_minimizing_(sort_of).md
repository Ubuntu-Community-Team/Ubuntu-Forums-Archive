### Post by benerivo on 2008-07-11
What you experienced has something to do with the way you can either set the 'double click on title bar' to either maximise that window or 'shade' it (where it stays on the desktop, but only the title bar is visible). In kde (from what i can remember), when a window is shaded, it reveals itself when the cursor hovers over the title bar. Check your settings in the kde control centre, in the window management section, specifically the bit about double clicking on the title bar.
