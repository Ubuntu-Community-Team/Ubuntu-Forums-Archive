### Post by urukrama on 2007-12-31
You could do it if you use Enlightenment as your window manager in Gnome and force Nautilus not to draw the desktop. 

Have a look a [this]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=54476&highlight=enlightenment+gnome+howto") thread for an idea how to do so (I believe the thread is outdated, though, but it might help you along)

Alternatively, you could use Xfce, which has a desktop menu.

Have a look at [this]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=3678683&postcount=12") post for other possible solutions and more info.
