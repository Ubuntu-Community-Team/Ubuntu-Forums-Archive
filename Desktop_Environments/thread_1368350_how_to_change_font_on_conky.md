### Post by Brandon Williams on 2009-12-30
Here's what I have related to fonts in my .conkyrc:
```
use_xft yes
xftfont DejaVu Sans Mono:size=8
xftalpha 1
```

Check the man page for conky to get a better idea of what the individual settings are for.
