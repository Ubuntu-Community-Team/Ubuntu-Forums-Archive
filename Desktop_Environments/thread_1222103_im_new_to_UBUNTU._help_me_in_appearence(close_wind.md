### Post by mCion on 2009-07-24
Try

Applications>System Tools>Compiz Fusion

Then an icon should appear in your panel (task bar).  right click and choose "Settings Manager"

(This is the same as going to System>Preferences>CompizConfig Settings Manager)

Scroll to Effects
Check Animations and animations Add-on

Click on Animations
got to the Close Animation Tab

You should have one row there (the white text box)  
Select-edit
Choose Burn
Close

In that Close Animations tab uncheck all and check burn.

Open your home folder or anything else and close it!!, it should work!

After that you can play with the duration of the animations etc.

good luck!
