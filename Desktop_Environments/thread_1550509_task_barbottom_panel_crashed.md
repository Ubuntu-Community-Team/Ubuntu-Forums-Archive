### Post by MatthewAdams on 2010-08-11
Right click the top panel, select "add new panel". 

A blank new panel should pop up on the bottom. 

Click "add to panel" --> "Window List" to restore a side-by-side layout of which programs are currently running. 

Feel free to tinker around with all the other things you can add to the panel! Some are very nifty!
