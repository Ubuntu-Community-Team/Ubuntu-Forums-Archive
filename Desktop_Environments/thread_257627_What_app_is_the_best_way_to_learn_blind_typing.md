### Post by finno on 2008-01-24
You might not like to mess up your keyboard,  but blanking out the letters on the keys by something like tape etc. will help you to discipline yourself not to "hunt and peck" by looking at the keyboard. Once you have achieved familiarity with the the eight "home keys", you will find that it is only a matter of time to build up speed.
