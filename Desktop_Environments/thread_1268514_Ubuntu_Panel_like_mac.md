### Post by VCoolio on 2009-09-17
It's called a dock and there are several: cairo-dock, awn (avant-window-navigator) or gnome-do (with the 'docky' mode). Make sure you have compositing enabled (run compiz by setting visual effects to normal or high or let metacity do some compositing by checking the box in gconf-editor, apps > metacity > general > compositing_manager).
