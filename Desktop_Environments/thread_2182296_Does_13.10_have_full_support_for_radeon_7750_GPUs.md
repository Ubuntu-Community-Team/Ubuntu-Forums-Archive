### Post by Linuxman1900 on 2013-10-21
What do you mean by "Full Support?" Also keep in mind that ATI Binary Drivers tend to break X11, a lot more than NVidia or Intel drivers. Unless you want to do major gaming, I'd stick to the open source driver.

Of course if you mean "Full Support" for the Open-Sourced ATI Driver, then you should be fine. It can't play games that great, but the open source driver is pretty much okay, especially with Kernel 3.11
