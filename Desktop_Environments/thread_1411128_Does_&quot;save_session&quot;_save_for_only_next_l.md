### Post by Hero of Time on 2010-02-20
That depends, are you saving the session on the next log out or not? If you do, then the first saved was only once. If you don't save your session when you log out after you saved a session once before, that last saved session will be used.
E.g. you have FF running when you log out and save the session. Next logon, FF will start because it was in your last session. You close FF before you log off and don't save the session. Next time you log on, FF will open again because you resume the last saved session.
