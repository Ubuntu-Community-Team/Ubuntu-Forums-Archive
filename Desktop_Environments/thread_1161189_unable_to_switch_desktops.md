### Post by cariboo on 2009-05-16
I just installed the KDE tesktop on Jaunty last night. You need to logout of Gnome and at the login screen, select sessions to chnage to a different desktop environment. During the installation of the KDE desktop it asked whether you would like to use gdm or kdm, I chose gdm.
