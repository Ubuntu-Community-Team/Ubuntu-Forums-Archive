### Post by abou on 2005-10-24
If they are like the audioscrobbler plugin, you can go to System -> Preferences -> Sessions

Then click on the Startup Program tab and click "Add".  Enter a command and order number.  My audioscrobbler program is number 70, so maybe for your two setserials you can do 70 and 71.
