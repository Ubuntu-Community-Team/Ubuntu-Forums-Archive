### Post by mcduck on 2009-09-12
Just right-click each applet, select "Move" and drag it to correct place. (turn off "Lock to Panel" first if it's enabled, otherwise you can't move the applet or move any other applet past it).

You can also simply drag the applets with the middle mouse button.
