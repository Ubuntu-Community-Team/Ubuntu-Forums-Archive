### Post by dabang on 2006-06-13
I think KenSentMe is right. Right click on a panel, add applet an choose hardware-monitor. then wait and you should see an small grey line where you clicked on the panel. now right-click on that line and choose "properties" or "configure" (or somenthing like that - I use a german localization) and you may configure hardware-monitor and make it show digits instead of lines, which is better to see.
