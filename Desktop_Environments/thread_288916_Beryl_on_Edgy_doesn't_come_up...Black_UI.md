### Post by PriceChild on 2006-10-30
With beryl enabled, you can't switch ttys or you will get the black screen you have described.

Please log into a failsafe gnome. Remove beryl-manager from your startup sessions, log out and log in again.

Then open a terminal and start beryl-manager again.
