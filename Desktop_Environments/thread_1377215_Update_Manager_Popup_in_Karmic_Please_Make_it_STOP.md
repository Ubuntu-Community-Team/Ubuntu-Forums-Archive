### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-01-10
Oh, it's so annoying!

It's simple to fix. Hit Alt-F2 and type 'gconf-editor'.

In 'apps' you'll find 'update-notifier'. There you'll find the Auto Popup setting. Uncheck it and all will be peaceful on your desktop.
