You can get the rpm from [here](http://download.fedora.redhat.com/pub/fedora/linux/core/development/i386/Fedora/RPMS/).

To install

sudo alien -i redhat-artwork-0.128-1.i386.rpm

After it is finished the theme will be available.

You may want to search the forums here for a little more info as there are a couple of Fedora specific icons you may want to change and I know it has been covered in the past.
