### Post by Airjoe on 2005-12-12
One of my favorite features of linux is the multiple workspace/desktops one. However, I've noticed in Kubuntu (it may be KDE in general, I've always used Gnome) that when I switch to a different desktop/workspace, the taskbar from the first comes along with it. When I open a window from the task bar, it takes me back to the first workspace. How can I get it to leave the taskbar with the associated workspace?

Thanks!

### Post by GeneralZod on 2005-12-12
I'm not 100% sure what you mean, but I suspect this might be what you''re after :)

Right-click on the kicker, and choose "Configure Panel".  Then click "Taskbar".   Then uncheck "Show Windows from all Desktops".  

[img]http://etotheipiplusone.com/kpanel-desktop-grouping.png[/img]

Hope this helps,
Simon.

### Post by MartinG on 2005-12-12
Right click K Menu -> Panel Menu -> Configure Panel. Taskbar -> unmark "Show windows from all desktops"
