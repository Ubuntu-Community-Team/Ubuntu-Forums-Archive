### Post by LarsKongo on 2012-05-15
Unfortunately it's not 100% possible to use a custom background in gnome-panel in Gnome 3. 

As a themer I've tried to add an alpha background-color to the gnome-panel.css file. It only results in some parts becoming transparent while the clock and indicator applet just gets a bright background. It also makes the panel a little bit unstable in some cases. So it's a bug that has to be reported to the gnome-panel devs.
