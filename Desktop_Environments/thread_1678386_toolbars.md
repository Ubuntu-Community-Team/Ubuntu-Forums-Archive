### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-01-30
Any of the panel applets can be added to either panel by right clicking and using the add to panel menu . It is possible to move your menu and other panel applets to the bottom like the Windows default and delete the top panel.

You can change the width . color and auto hide the panel by right clicking the panel and using the panel properties settings.

Some docks can be extended on the bottom to appear like the Windows task bar.

If you choose to move everything to the bottom it may get crowded , so a dock may be a better option.
