### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-02-12
I'm experiencing a problem with the dashboard on KDE 4.4. 

I have configured a separate dashboard instead of displaying the desktop widgets. After a plasma crash, the widgets are gone from the dashboard, but apparently they are still running. So, is there a way of killing a widget without seeing it? This was possible from the "Add Widget" dialog on KDE 4.3, but now I can only see an option to add widgets, not removing them.

---

### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-02-13
I figured it out myself. Just needed to delete the widget entry on *plasma-desktop-appletsrc*  and log-off.
