### Post by janhenderson on 2006-08-26
> **rogdef said:**
> Hello,

You'll see the taskbar where the windows that you minimise reside. There's an arrow pointing up. Click it, you'll see unclutter windows, or cascade windows. Alternatively, middle click on the desktop and you'll get the same option. 

While you're at it, download kompose. It's quite cool. Also get katapult.
