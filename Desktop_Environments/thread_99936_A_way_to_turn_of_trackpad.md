### Post by Rinzwind on 2005-12-07
I don't think you can make a switch that turns it on when it's off and turns it off when it's on. 

So you need 2.  I suggest adding 2 ikons to your panel. 1 with the command to turn it on and 1 next to it to turn it off. 

Find an empty space on  your panel and rightclick to add 2 application buttons to your panel. And then change them to what you want to call them and have 1 do the off and 1 do the on command.
