### Post by Sam Lars on 2008-02-27
Check the resolution on them... is the fuzzy monitor right?
If you have an LCD and a CRT, they render things differently, and require different methods to look good... 
You should check in System > Preferences > Appearance, under font, and Details.  Try different rendering settings until you find one that works for you.
