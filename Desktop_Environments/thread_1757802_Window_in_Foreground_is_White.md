### Post by chichiri47 on 2011-05-14
Okay, for those who might have the same problem in future, I went to System -> Administration -> Additional Drivers, un-activated the current, "recommended" driver for NVIDIA, and activated version 173. I then restarted. After some hours, the problem has not recurred.

Many thanks indeed! ):P
