### Post by waspbr on 2009-02-05
I had a similar problem, it turns out that the cd was defective, I dowloaded ubuntu again and burned another cd at the slowest possible speed, and it worked. 

also, you may want to see if enabling safe graphics mode helps, at the livecd menu screen press F4 and select safe graphics  (something like it). 

Tho I think burining a new cd may be your best bet, I don see any reason your computer should not be able to boot it.
