### Post by an.echte.trilingue on 2006-06-09
Unfortunately, I think you are going to find this is one of those different strokes for different folks things.  You need to quantify your criteria before you can say something is "better" or "worse."

VMware player is free as in beer but not free as in liberty.  That is a major hang up for many people.

Qemu is slower, period.  However, once you turn off all the unnessesary stuf and the graphic effects in XP, you can run just about anything satisfactorily.

The amount of RAM you need also depends on what you want to do.  When I was messing with this, 512MB was plenty; you should probably devote at least 128 to either qemu or vmware player.
