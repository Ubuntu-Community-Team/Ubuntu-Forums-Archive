Linux Mint 12 comes with Gnome Shell extensions that reinstate a bottom panel with a workspace switcher. This can be installed on Ubuntu 11.10 and it pretty much restores the previously lost functionality since the demise of Gnome 2. 

I think this puts Gnome ahead of Unity and XFCE. Take a look:
