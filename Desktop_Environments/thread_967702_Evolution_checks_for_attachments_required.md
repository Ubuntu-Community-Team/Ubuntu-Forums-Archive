### Post by fballem on 2008-11-02
Just had an 'oh wow!' moment while working with Evolution.

I send PDF invoices to one of my clients via e-mail. I prepared the e-mail in the usual way, but forgot to attach the invoice.

When I hit the Send button, Evolution apparently scanned my e-mail and determined that I wanted attachments, but there weren't any. It gave me the option of attaching something or proceeding along without attachments.

Attached the invoice and it happily continued. Whoever thought of this feature, and whoever developed and tested the feature, many thanks. It is a really nice touch!
