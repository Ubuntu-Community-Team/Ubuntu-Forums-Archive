### Post by MaddMatt on 2008-11-30
Wowzers... I don't mean to be honking anyone's horn around here, but did anyone know about the left-alt + shift + up-arrow key combo in gnome? I just stumbled upon that one by accident! 

It shows all of the windows you have open on a given desktop. Combine that with compiz effects and you've got a winner. If that's not enough combine that with my custom right-alt+direction keys shortcut for desktop switching, and you have a productivity dream. Ha! 
FOSS rules. I owe a beer to whichever developer came up with that feature. 

Anyone know of more "hidden" features? There should probably be a list of this stuff somewhere.
