### Post by Protex on 2006-02-16
The bar that holds the buttons for programs when they are minimized and such has disappeared. Well the bar is still there, but it's not a taskbar anymore, just a bar.

How do I get it back to normal?

---

### Post by Madpilot on 2006-02-16
Right-click on the bar, select "Add To Panel".

Scroll down in the Add To Panel window until you see Desktop & Windows, and click on the Window List.

Done.
