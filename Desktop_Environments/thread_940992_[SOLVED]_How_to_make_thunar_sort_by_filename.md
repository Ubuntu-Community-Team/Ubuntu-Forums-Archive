### Post by ajgreeny on 2008-10-07
In Edit->Preferences you can set whether or not to sort folders before files, and then surely simply clicking in the name column-header will sort by file name, with folders either sorted separately at the top or included alphabetically in the list according to your choice.  It's a long time since I looked at xfce, but I seem to remember that is how it was.  Very like nautilus, in fact.
