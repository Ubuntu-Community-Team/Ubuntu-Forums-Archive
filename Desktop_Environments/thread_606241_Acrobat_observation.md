### Post by ekh on 2007-11-07
Although a previous post reports About Acrobat reader 7.0 spying (2005) I just want to tell that I have experienced close to 100% CPU usage by acrobat 7.0  in Ubuntu Gutsy.

It was not visible with alt tab, but the system monitor showed it. The network transmission bandwidth ran about 200kB per second. When I clicked the button to kill the process, the transmission rose to 400kB/s for approx 3 seconds. Then the process was killed, transmission stopped, and CPU load was again normal.

I do not trust Acrobat from now on, and have removed it from my system.
