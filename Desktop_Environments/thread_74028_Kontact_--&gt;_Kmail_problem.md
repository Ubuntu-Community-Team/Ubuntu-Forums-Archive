### Post by claydoh on 2005-10-11
Configs for kmail, etc can be found in ~/.kde/share/config and your mail folders will be in ~/.kde/share/apps/kmail.

You might try running kmail as a standalone, just hit Alt-F2 and enter "kmail", you maybe able to get in there that way. and adjust things.
