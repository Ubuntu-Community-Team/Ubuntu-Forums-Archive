Their isnt much difference, I would choose gdm if you use gnome more and kdm if you use kde more. Each desktop enviroment has tools to customize their login manager so if you use kde and want autologin then use kdm since kde can configure that for you.

Apart from their appearance they are pretty much alike in what they can do to the average user. If you really get into tweaking linux then you may notice their differences. I had kdm and to switch to gdm just removed kdm. Im sure their is a better way though like this one
