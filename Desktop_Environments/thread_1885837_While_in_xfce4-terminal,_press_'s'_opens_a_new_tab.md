### Post by beesthorpe on 2011-11-24
That's odd. You might try going to Edit > Preferences, then click on the "Shortcuts" tab the Terminal Preferences box. The "Open Tab" is the first keyboard shortcut in the list and should be "<Shift><Control>t" you could check to make sure this hasn't got changed to "s" somehow as this would result in exactly the behaviour you describe.
