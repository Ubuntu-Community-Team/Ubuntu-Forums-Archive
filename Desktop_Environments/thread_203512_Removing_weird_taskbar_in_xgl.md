### Post by el3ktro on 2006-06-25
If you installed gset-compiz from the Xgl/compiz repos, you can use it to configure all means of Xgl/compiz. This taskabr you mean is the "dock" plugin. Using gset-compiz, you can enable/disable all plugins as you like, change keybindings, colors etc.
