### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-05-02
When you go to the login screen and click your name, before you put in your password, you have the choice (at the bottom of the screen) to use Ubuntu classic.

However, I would press you to give the new format a chance. You can use Compiz to change various effects; I'm not sure how you would move the panel to the bottom.

One thing I think Canonical has been most remiss about is letting us know how to use Unity. Here are two excellent resources:
[The Raving Rick: My Effort at Writing Help for Unity]("http://theravingrick.blogspot.com/2011/04/my-effort-at-writing-help-for-unity.html")
[The Power User's Guide to Unity]("http://castrojo.tumblr.com/post/4795149014/the-power-users-guide-to-unity")
