Hello and Welcome to Ubuntu Forum :)

Just like in Real Life/World, there is the short way and the long way, it depends on which way we want to follow and walk through.
I believe in Keep It Simple and Short so may I give you some few advices?

1- Have a look at my signature - Lubuntu One Stop Thread. I'm sure you'll find even more what you can imagine.

2- Go to: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Lubuntu/GetLubuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Lubuntu/GetLubuntu)

3- Download the iso that match your Hardware

4- Check MD5SUM for your downloaded iso (on Lubuntu One Stop Thread, you'll find how to do that).

5- Create a LiveUSB using UNetbootin (check Lubuntu One Stop Thread).

6- Make sure your machine will boot from USB Drive - check BIOS.

7- Run Lubuntu from the Live USB and make sure it works well on your machine.

8- If everything is okay, go a head and install.

9- Have fun ;)

If you need anything, please let me know!
