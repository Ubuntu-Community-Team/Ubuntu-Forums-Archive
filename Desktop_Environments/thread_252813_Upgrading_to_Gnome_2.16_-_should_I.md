### Post by Toxicity999 on 2006-09-07
We get a ton of these messages every release. The answer is a generic no. 2.16 is a fun release sure but it's nothing anyone desperately needs, or can't wait until October (Edgy is oct. right?) I mean some tweakers like me try to challenge themselves with staying bleeding edge but in the end it's not really worth it. Gnome always releases the final just before Ubuntu does (funny how that worked out huh... almost as though it were planned!) So yea... simple answer spare yourself the drama and bandwidth and wait a short while. The type fo person who would install 2.16 on their lonesome already has something to take care of most of the new features anyway =P.
