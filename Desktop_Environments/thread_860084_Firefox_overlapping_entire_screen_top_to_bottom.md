### Post by Abnormal123 on 2008-08-31
[SOLVED]

After playing around a little while I was able to fix it.  I toggled between full screen mode and the desktop panel showed up.  I dragged it to the side of the screen, then I was able to see the window menu for Firefox and could then resize the window.
