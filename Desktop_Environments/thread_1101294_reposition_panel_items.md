### Post by mcduck on 2009-03-20
Just drag the applets where you want them (either with middle mouse button dragging, or by right-clicking the applet and selecting "Move").

If some applet refuses to move, right-click it and make sure "Lock To Panel" is disabled. Same applies if you can't move an applet past other one, make sure both are unlocked.
