### Post by yossell on 2009-12-04
For 2

it's not quite the same, but the Grid plugin allows you to place immediately the active window at various points in the screen - the left half, the right half, the top half, the bottom-left quarter...this gives you a way, using just a couple of key presses, to put two windows side by side. 

It may not be quite what you're looking for, but I ended up preferring it - maybe give it a try and see if it suits you.
