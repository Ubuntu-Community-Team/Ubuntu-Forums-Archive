### Post by aphatak on 2011-05-25
If you are already in the Unity desktop environment, log out.  At the log-in screen, when you click the user ID, a menu will appear at the bottom of the screen.  Click the spin-button to the your right of the 'Ubuntu'; this will pop up a menu of environments.  Select 'Ubuntu Classic' from it.  Key in your password and click 'Logon'.  The desktop you see will be the familiar Ubuntu 10.10 environment.
