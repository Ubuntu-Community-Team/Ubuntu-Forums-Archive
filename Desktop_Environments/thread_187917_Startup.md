### Post by taurus on 2006-06-03
What kind of things you want to startup when you log in?  You can add those to Applications -> System -> Preferences -> Sessions.  Then, click on "Startup Programs" and "Add".  Type in the name and "Close" it.  Log out and back in again to see what happens.
