### Post by charlieg on 2005-08-13
Helvetica is a Microsft font.  Whilst it can be 'freely' distributed, it's not published under a free license, hence does not satisfy the Debian policy on licenses and not really Ubuntu's either.

Indeed, Microsoft no longer distributes them:
[http://www.microsoft.com/truetype/fontpack/win.htm](http://www.microsoft.com/truetype/fontpack/win.htm)

However they can be downloaded from here:
[http://corefonts.sourceforge.net/](http://corefonts.sourceforge.net/)

Or you can copy them over from your Windows installation, if you have or had one.
