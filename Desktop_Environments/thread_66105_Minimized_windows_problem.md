### Post by Dooglus on 2005-09-23
You should right-click on an *empty* part of panel somewhere, and chose "add to panel" from the menu that appears.

Then add either "window selector" or "window switcher" according to taste.  The "window selector" takes up less space on the panel, only showing the list of windows when you click on it, and the "window switcher" shows all the windows side by side.
