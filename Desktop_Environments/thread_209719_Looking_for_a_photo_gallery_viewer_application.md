### Post by orb9220 on 2006-07-05
ShowImg is a feature-rich image viewer for KDE  including an image management system. It is highly configurable and supports numerous image formats.

Runs in ubuntu 6.0.6 great which alot of KDE apps do with the necessary libraries.

[http://www.jalix.org/projects/showimg/](http://www.jalix.org/projects/showimg/)

GwenView is also nice.

Either can be installed from Synaptic just do a search and select all of them and try them out.
