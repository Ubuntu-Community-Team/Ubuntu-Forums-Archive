### Post by booljayj on 2007-07-25
Yeah, you don't need a sensor bar to use the wiimote,  I've known that for a while. The Wii 'Sensor Bar' and all wireless sensor bars don't actually sense anything, they're just a collection of infrared LEDs. Take it apart if you don't believe me. Any two infrared sources (candles, remote controls, even a computer infrared transmitter) can be used with no problems. Quality of the signal does differ, however, so you'd better find some hot candles if you want to get some good distance.

I'm glad that the wiimote will have no trouble with a projector, thanks for your help.
