### Post by nebc on 2006-07-25
You should be able to upgrade to newer versions of ubuntu from older versions (e.g. badger to drake).  Check in administration and look for 'software updates' or something along those lines.  Also be sure that the repositories you're looking at have dapper drake (or whatever version you're looking for) in them.
