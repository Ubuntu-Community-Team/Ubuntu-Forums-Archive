### Post by soonindallas on 2006-06-16
you can download the package without applying the update (there's a checkbox for this in the synaptic gui - "download packages only" after clicking on "apply") and examine the changelog in the package.  it is there, but it takes some time to make it available for consultation through synaptic.

I believe you can also go to launchpad and find it, but I have always found it easier to do as I just described.
