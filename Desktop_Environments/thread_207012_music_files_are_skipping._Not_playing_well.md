### Post by rskovacs on 2006-07-01
I use amaroK rather than XMMS but this may work for you...

In the XMMS menu, go to Options > Preferences, then under Audio Output Plugins, make a different selection other than what has been selected, and keep trying until your music sounds good.  I cannot explain why exactly this works (hopefully someone can), but this sounds similar to the problem I was having, and this is what did it for me.
