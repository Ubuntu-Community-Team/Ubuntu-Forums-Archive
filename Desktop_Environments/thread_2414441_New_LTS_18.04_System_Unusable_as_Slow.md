### Post by GrumpyBum on 2019-03-10
Note to all, I have resolved!
Plugged into OnBoard Video and everything worked well.
Updated Video drivers from Sapphire website (DEB Package).
Plugged into RX-570 again and all working Great :)
Was never a system performance issue but rather a video performance issue
