### Post by Cariboo1938 on 2007-11-16
A program (Bit Torrent), which I have uninstalled with Synaptic Package Manager, shows still in the pulldown of 'Applications' submenu 'Internet'. I checked the installation status with Synaptic and I checked -->Applications -->Add/Remove and in both cases the program is not installed at all.
How can I delete the entry in the submenu?

EDIT: I can't find it in '/usr/share/applications/' under 'bittorrent.desktop' either.

---

### Post by rsambuca on 2007-11-16
Just Right-Click on your menu and select "Edit Menus"

---

### Post by Cariboo1938 on 2007-11-16
Thanks ***rsambuca*** for the help...
It's really simple if you know what to do...
> right click -->Applications -->select 'Edit Menus' -->window 'Main Menu' opens -->select  under 'Menus' the menu of concern (in this case 'Internet') --> uncheck not installed 'Item' -->'Close' edit
Thanks again!:)
