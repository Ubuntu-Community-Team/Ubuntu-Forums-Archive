### Post by MorayJ on 2012-04-24
Just did an update on Pangolin (still beta until Thursday) and half of gnome shell went Chinese.

Googling found this solution which worked for me...add the following lines to /etc/environment.

```
LANG="en_GB"
LANGUAGE="en_GB:en"
```
