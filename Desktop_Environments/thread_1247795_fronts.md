Fonts are locaded in: /home/administrator/.fonts  at least on my Ubuntu 8.10


When looking for the .fonts folder you must choose "Show hidden files" from Nautilus...

Not sure if they would work with Windows or not but I don't see why they wouldn't... I copied all my Windows fonts (Around 500 of 5,000) from windows to the .fonts folder and I have access to all of them in Ubuntu so I guess it could go the other way...
