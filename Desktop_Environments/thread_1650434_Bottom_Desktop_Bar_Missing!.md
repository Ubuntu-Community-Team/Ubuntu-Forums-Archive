### Post by 3Miro on 2010-12-21
Right-click on the top panel and select "Add Panel". You can then righ-click on the new panel to tell it to position at the bottom.

You probably also want to right-click and add taskbar (task manager/window list, I forget the name) to keep a list of open windows. You can also add the Workspace pager (or any other applets that you want).
