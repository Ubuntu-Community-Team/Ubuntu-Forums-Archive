### Post by pdwerryhouse on 2011-10-17
The cause of my laptop not suspending was the virtualbox kernel modules. I added the following script (/etc/pm/config.d/unload_modules) so that they were removed when suspending, and now it suspends without any problems:


```
#!/bin/sh

SUSPEND_MODULES="$SUSPEND_MODULES vboxpci vboxnetadp vboxnetflt vboxdrv "
```
