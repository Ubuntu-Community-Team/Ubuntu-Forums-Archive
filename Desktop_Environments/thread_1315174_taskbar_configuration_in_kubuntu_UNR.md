### Post by wonko on 2010-10-17
A bit late perhaps, but since I encountered the same problem in Kubuntu 10.10 and came across this thread while googling, maybe I can help someone by posting my findings here...

The trick seems to be to *first* unlock the panel by right-clicking on it or pressing Alt+D,L, *then* you can add widgets or, more interestingly, disable auto-hide by pressing the cashew in the bottom left corner.
