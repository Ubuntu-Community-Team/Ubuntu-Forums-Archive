### Post by Takis on 2005-06-14
Go to Control Center->Sound & Multimedia->Sound System.

'Enable the Sound System' should be checked. Then hit the 'Hardware' tab, and try explicitly setting 'Select the audio device' to 'Advanced Linux Sound Architecture'. Go back over to the 'General' tab and hit 'Test Sound'. If that doesn't work, try it with 'Open Sound System' and 'Enlightened Sound Daemon'.
