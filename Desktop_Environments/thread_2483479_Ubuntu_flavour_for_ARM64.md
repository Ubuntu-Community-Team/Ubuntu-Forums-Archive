### Post by guiverc on 2023-01-31
The *flavor* teams only have limited resources.

You mention Xubuntu, which has offered a *minimal* install ISO that up till recently, was in threat of being *dropped *for AMD64 due to lack of testers willing to help QA (*Quality Assurance*) test it.

Most people involved with *flavors* don't have ARM64 boxes, so couldn't test it even IF they had the time for the QA.

More *flavors* are expected to have raspberry pi images available with 23.04 though (*work on setting this up is occurring now!*), which will be ARM64, but may not be exactly what you're after (*if using apple or other brand hardware for example*)
