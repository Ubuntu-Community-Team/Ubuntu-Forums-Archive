### Post by czhu on 2006-06-11
Since subwoofers are responsible for low frequency sounds, the placement doesn't really matter. This is because low frequency sounds can bend much more around edges (eg tables, bookshelves), so the sound waves will travel quite well to whereever you're sitting.
