### Post by twister03 on 2010-01-20
Go to CompizConfig Settings Manager > click Desktop > check Rotate Cube. After that click Rotate cube to go into it's options and click the bindings tab. Click the down arrow on Rotate cube to look at the key bindings. It should be <Control> <Alt> Button1 by default. You may also click the scroll wheel on desktop (at least for me) to initiate the cube rotation. 

Hope this solves your problem.
