### Post by LongTooth on 2004-12-25
This is not really a request for help. But rather praise for the Ubuntu Starter Guide  [http://ubuntuguide.org/](http://ubuntuguide.org/)   . With every distro I've installed I've created some personal folders such as 'downloads', 'keepers', 'temp', 'wallpaper', and others. Noticed that my 'downloads' folder was restricted. How this happened, is beyond me. I didn't recall doing anything special. But thats what I had. It had a red circle with a slash. Odd I thought. I don't recall seeing that before. On opening that folder I found I could not delete anything in it. I didn't have ownership of that folder and everything in it. I went to the Starter Guide and looked up changing permission/ownership. Did as instructed and viola! I had ownership of said folder. Many great HowTo's and Tips there. Thanks to the ones working on this guide.
