### Post by happy-and-lost on 2006-12-17
I've just got Beryl working and it looks AWESOME, but it comes with a problem. Say I'm running Firefox, and I open up my home folder, where it used to pop up over 'Fox, it now appears under. This is probably a setting I've overlooked, but a prod in the right direction would be much appreciated!

---

### Post by mcduck on 2006-12-17
In Beryl Settings Manager under General Options change 'Level of FSP' to 'none'. (FSP=Focus Stealing Prevention)
