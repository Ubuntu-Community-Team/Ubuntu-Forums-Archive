### Post by RRS on 2007-05-28
Maybe I'm misunderstanding your question but to highlight a single letter simply click with the curser to the left or right of the letter and drag the curser over it. 

A double click will highlight the whole word(or a URL with no spaces in it), or two adjacent words if you double click between them, and a quick triple click will highlight the whole line.

"Delete" or "Backspace" will remove whatever was highlighted so you can enter your new word, URL, or whatever. Or you can right click and "cut"

Also, anything you highlight can be pasted somewhere else by middle clicking without having to do right click/paste, very convenient. 

Hope this answers your questions.

### Post by AnRa on 2007-05-29
You can accomplish that by setting 'browser.urlbar.clickSelectsAll' to 'true' in 'about:config'.

Also you can highlight the address bar by pressing 'Ctrl + L' or the search bar with 'Ctrl + K'.
