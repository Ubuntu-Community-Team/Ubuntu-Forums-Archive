### Post by Bietje on 2011-11-10
Hi,

I installed xfce in on my ubuntu box. I configured compiz like before but when I switch workspace with the compiz workspace switcher (wall/cube) it doesn't actually seem to switch the workspace. It does something like minializing all my programs (so a I see my wallpaper again) but I don't see my workspace number go from 1 to 2 (keeps on 1). 

When I use a the panel plugin to switch (which works fine under metacity), it just clears my entire desktop (no panels, (keyboard/desktop) shortcuts, whatever).

Has anyone seen this before and/or knows how I can fix this?

Greets,
Bietje

---

### Post by hhh on 2011-11-10
In CompizConfigSettingsManager (ccsm in a terminal), go to General>>General Options>>Desktop Size and set Horizontal to 2 and Vertical/Desktops both to 1.
