### Post by Kevbert on 2008-07-29
You can set this up via compiz.  Double click on Rotate Cube.  Select Bindings tab, Rotate to Cube Face (pull-down menu).   Click on Disabled, tick enable and then click on 'Grab key combination' and press the keys you want to use.
