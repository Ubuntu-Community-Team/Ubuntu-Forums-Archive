### Post by bingoUV on 2008-06-16
Someone has to pay the bills of hosting your files. 

1. You can buy Amazon's S3 service and mount the space on both the computers using [http://www.persistentfs.com/](http://www.persistentfs.com/)

2. If your files are small, you could use gmail filesystem : [http://richard.jones.name/google-hacks/gmail-filesystem/gmail-filesystem.html](http://richard.jones.name/google-hacks/gmail-filesystem/gmail-filesystem.html). 

Once you decide where to store the files (for gold or love), someone here will be able to suggest the best method to sync stuff.
