### Post by speartip on 2016-05-06
If I understand correctly what you are doing, I would quit Dropbox, delete the config files in your home folder (.dropbox & .dropbox-dist), you will need to tick show hidden files to see them.
Then open Dropbox, & set it up again, inputting your new details, & point the folder back to your original files.
 If you're trying to link 2 dropbox accounts to one instance of Dropbox, I'm not sure that's possible.
