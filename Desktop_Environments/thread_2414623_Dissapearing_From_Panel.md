### Post by erhollowell on 2019-03-13
I don't know why it happened but xfce4 indicator plug in was removed. strange that the bluetooth disappeared first, then the audio indicator left and several weeks later the network indicator disappeared. Once I figured out that the indicator plugin was what enabled these functions and added it as a new item everything was restored as I expected it to be.
