gnome-shell is the new gnome interface, after ubuntu made unity in an attempt to have an upper hand on other linux OS's (advertising it as an iterface that doesnt use a lot of desktop space). gnome followed suit and made the new gnome 3 desktop. gnome fallback or gnome classic is the interface from gnome 2 (from before the new gnome 3). the purpose of gnome fallback/classic, is to provide that old interface for both prefrence and compatibility on old pc's.
gnome fallback/classic actually has a big benefit to it and that is that it uses significantly less resources than gnome 3 or unity, making it noticibly faster.

even though i know use gnome 3, i still think gnome fallback/classic is much better, its so more convient, everythings just right there in front of you, on gnome 3 and unity you gotta search for everything
