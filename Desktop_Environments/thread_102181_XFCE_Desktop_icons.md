[QUOTE=Granduke]I don't have any Desktop icons in XFCE although there are programs in my /Desktop  folder.  Should'nt these be on my Desktop?[/QUOTE] No, they shouldn't be.

You may consider running Nautilus if desktop icons are important to you. Just Alt-F2 and type ```
nautilus
``` It will use your Gnome (not XFCE) wallpaper, though.
