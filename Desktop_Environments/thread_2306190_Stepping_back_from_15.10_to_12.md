### Post by QIII on 2015-12-13
I think "disk error" and problems with two OSes on the same disk should be clear.

The first thing you need to do is back up all of your important files.  The next thing is to get a new disk to replace one that is clearly failing.  That is the problem with your Windows install and more likely than not the problem with Ubuntu.
