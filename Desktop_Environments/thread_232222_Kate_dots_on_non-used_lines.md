### Post by MacMan on 2006-08-09
> **ubuntuubuntu said:**
> Is there a way to configure Kate not to show these dots?

The option you're looking for is:
Menu: Settings -> Configure Kate... -> Editing -> Show Tabulators

Turn it off and the dots are not shown.

I hope this helps.
