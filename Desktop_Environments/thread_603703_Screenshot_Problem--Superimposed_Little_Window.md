### Post by jpeddicord on 2007-11-06
If you are using the tool from Accessories > Take Screenshot, set a delay of at least 1 second. It probably doesn't have enough time to redraw the desktop before it takes a snapshot.

Another method (if you are already doing this, then I have no idea what is wrong) is to just hit the PrintScrn key near the top-right of your keyboard. You can also hold Alt while pressing it to only get the current window.
