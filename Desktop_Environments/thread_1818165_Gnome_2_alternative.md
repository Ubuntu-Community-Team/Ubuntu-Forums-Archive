### Post by 3Miro on 2011-08-04
> **JasonFWard said:**
> 

Many seem to migrating to XCFE, including some famous Linux people, but what do I do?  Do Gnome apps run in XCFE?

All apps run under all DE. You may run into issues with themes if you mix GTK and QT apps, but both XFCE and Gnome 2 are based on GTK2 so there are no issues there.

> 
Is XCFE able to do what Gnome 2 did?

Yes. The default file manager is simpler (and faster), but the windows manager has a lot more options than Metacity. Compiz also runs well with XFCE.

> 
Is there any chance someone will fork Gnome 2 and give us back a sane and workable desktop?

Some people are forking Gnome 2, which IMO is a waste of time. Some people are keeping Gnome-panel and Metacity as part of Gnome 3 (fallback mode) and those will be part of Gnome 3 for quite some time (also they will be available via the Software Center in 11.10). There is also a project to mod Gnome-shell and give you the classic interface.

> 
What will happen to Ubuntu?

Keep growing, what else?

> 
Will I be forced to using another distribution?
Nobody is forcing you, you can use XFCE (xubuntu) or Gnome-classic mode in both 11.04 and 11.10.
