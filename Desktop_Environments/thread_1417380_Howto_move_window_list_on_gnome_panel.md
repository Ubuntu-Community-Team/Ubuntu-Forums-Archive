### Post by palmboy on 2010-02-27
Try click and drag with the left mouse button a few pixels left to the window, and not on the window itself, that should make it move :D

### Post by drpjkurian on 2010-02-27
Hi
You will see a small vertical line on the panel beside to the open window on the panel.right click on it, You will get an option known as"move". Move that small vertical line to the extreme left and lo your problem will be solved.

### Post by blackened on 2010-02-27
When you right-click on the gripper, make sure that "Lock to Panel" isn't checked before you go trying to move the item.
