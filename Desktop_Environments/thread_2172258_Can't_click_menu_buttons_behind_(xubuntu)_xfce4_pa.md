### Post by Toz on 2013-09-04
Hello and welcome to the forums.

A couple of suggestions:
1. You can hold down the Alt key then with your mouse, grab and move the window up so you can access the buttons.
2. You could move the lower panel to either the left or right of the screen (set the Panel Mode property to "Deskbar") so that it frees up the space at the bottom. This will orient the panel vertically (up down) along the side of the screen instead of horizontally. Works well on the newer widescreen monitor resolutions.
