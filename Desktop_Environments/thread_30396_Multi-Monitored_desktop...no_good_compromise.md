### Post by RuKK on 2005-04-28
Xinerama treats both of your monitors like one big screen, so its a little difficult to just set different backgrounds. The easy workaround is to open up your background image in the gimp, make the canvas size twice as wide, and put another background into the other half, or clone the same one. As far as the screensaver goes, I suppose you could make your own or something that would work, but it doesnt seem like that big a deal to me. Also, I do not know of any way to move windows between desktops with a normal dual monitor setup other than twinview/xinerma.
