### Post by Kep on 2010-06-12
You can turn off the annoying startup noise as follows:

Go to System&#8212;Preferences&#8212;Startup Applications then deselect "GNOME login sound"

You might also want to have a look at System&#8212;Preferences&#8212;Sound

(I assume you're using GNOME?)
