### Post by devondashla on 2010-10-31
It seems you have compositing disabled.

Go to System>Preferences>Apperance, and click on the Visual Effects Tab. You can select either Normal or Extra, Extra if you have a decent computer. That should enable compositing-you may have to restart Cairo-Dock.
