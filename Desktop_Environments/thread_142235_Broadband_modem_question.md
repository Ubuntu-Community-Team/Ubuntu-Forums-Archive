### Post by Garyu on 2006-03-10
If the setup is done through your browser it should work on any OS, including Ubuntu. Just open Firefox and type in your router IP in the location and it should open fine.

BTW, my parents have an ADSL connection. Sometimes they have to unplug the modem, and then plug it back in. Norwegian reset we call it, but it works for them.
