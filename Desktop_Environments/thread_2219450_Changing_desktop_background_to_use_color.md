### Post by grumblebum2 on 2014-04-24
The 3 blue boxes represent a style...
[LIST]
[*]solid colour
[*]vertical gradient
[*]horizontal gradient
[*]
[/LIST]

You choose the actual colour in the box at the bottom left.
