### Post by blueturtl on 2006-09-30
Open Firefox, go to the Tools menu and select 'Remove personal information'. From the window that pops up put a tick in front of 'Saved passwords' and then hit OK.

I don't know of any other applications that would hold your passwords to anything. Except maybe Evolution and GAIM, but then in those you have to enable password saving by hand so I guess you're not talking about them?

p.s. Gnome does not remember your user account password.
