### Post by Idefix82 on 2011-01-01
I think this is a deliberate feature: when the icon on the dock is zoomed, you expect to be able to click on any part of the zoomed icon. But since windows are rectangular, this means that the dock has to expand along the whole length, it cannot just expand on this icon and have an upside down T shape.

I agree that it is annoying that this even happens even when you disable the zoom effect (I am not in front of my home computer, so can't verify this), and maybe this should be reported on the Cairo-Dock page as a bug.
