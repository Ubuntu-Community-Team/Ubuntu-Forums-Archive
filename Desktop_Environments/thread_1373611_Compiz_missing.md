### Post by donato roque on 2010-01-06
The Compiz core is installed by default.  To enable compiz and use it you will need the GUI for the compiz manager.  Since you're familiar with synaptic, kindly open it again and look for compiz manager and download it if you have not done so.  

After downloading it you will find it in System-->Administration.

Of course you will need 3D accelerated graphics and drivers for it.
