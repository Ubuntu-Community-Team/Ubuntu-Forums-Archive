### Post by YourSurrogateGod on 2007-04-24
Ok, I solved this problem by myself. If anyone has a similar problem, all that you have to do is open up the un-maximized window, re-size it to the maximum width and height of the window and then close it. Now you can re-open that folder that's on your desktop and it'll be 'maximized'.

Thing is, after I did all of that and then hit the maximize button several times, the window did not change at all in terms of size or shape (I expected it to increase at the edges after maximizing it.)
