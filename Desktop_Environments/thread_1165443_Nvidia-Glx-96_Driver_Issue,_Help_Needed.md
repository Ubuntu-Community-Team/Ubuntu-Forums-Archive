### Post by robstel on 2009-05-20
in xorg.conf add

[FONT="Courier New"]Option "AddARGBGLXVisuals" "True"[/FONT]

and perhaps
[FONT="Courier New"]Option "DisableGLXRootClipping" "True"[/FONT]

to the "screen" section
