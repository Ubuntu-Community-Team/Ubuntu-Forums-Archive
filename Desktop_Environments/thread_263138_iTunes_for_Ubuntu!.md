### Post by lwr on 2006-09-22
I'd be very surprised if Apple released iTunes for Linux anytime soon, if ever. OS X is their big selling point for all their hardware, so they're kind of taking the stance of refusing to admit that Linux exists, and that people can get pretty much all the benits of their OS on their existing machines for free. That's why Quicktime has no support either. Personally, I wouldn't mind Quicktime for Linux, but they can keep iTunes. We've got a lot of good open-source media players on Linux, and I'd rather keep things as open-source as reasonably possible. Good luck with badgering Steve Jobs and friends though!
