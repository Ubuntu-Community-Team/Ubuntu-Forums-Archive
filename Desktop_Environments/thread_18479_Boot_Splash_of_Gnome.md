### Post by bored2k on 2005-03-07
Loos like he solved it , but for anyone with the same question:

Gnome Login Manager:
> $ sudo gdmsetup

Gnome Splash Screen
> $ gconf-editor
Apps > gnome-session > options > edit splash image.
