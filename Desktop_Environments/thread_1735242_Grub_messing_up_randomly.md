I basically just need help figuring out how to get it to boot after it gives me the "GRUB RESCUE" screen.ah, well when the grub rescue screen shows it usually means that your grub configuration is messed up somehow, so you might need to reinstall grub.  the easiest way for me is to use the ubuntu live disc and use [these instructions]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Grub2#Reinstalling from LiveCD")
