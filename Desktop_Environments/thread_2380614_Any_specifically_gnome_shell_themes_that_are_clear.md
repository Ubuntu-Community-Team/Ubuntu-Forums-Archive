### Post by again? on 2017-12-20
Disable the ubuntu dock extension and use [Dynamic Panel Transparency]("https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/1011/dynamic-panel-transparency/") and [Dash to Dock]("https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/307/dash-to-dock/") to set your colours and transparency.
