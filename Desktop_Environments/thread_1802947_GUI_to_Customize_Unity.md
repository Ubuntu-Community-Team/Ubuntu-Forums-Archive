### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-07-12
I do not have the answer but before you can do the very little adjustments to Unity you need CompizConfig Settings Manager installed and the Unity plugin activated. Click on the label and select Experimental. There is a slider for panel opacity. Move it to the left and the top panel starts to go transparent. I guess that is about as good as it gets. It does say experimental. That might also mean under development. May be things will get better.
