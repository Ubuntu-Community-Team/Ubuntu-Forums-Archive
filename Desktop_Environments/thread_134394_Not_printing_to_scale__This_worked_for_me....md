### Post by fizgig on 2006-02-21
I had something I had drawn in Inkscape and I wanted to print it out.  It had some transparancy in it so Postscript wasn't an option.  When I tried to print it out as a bitmap at 300dpi, it printed about 3x larger than I had it dimensioned in Inkscape.

I tried exporting the page as a png and opening and printing it from the gimp but I had a different issue - the gimp wouldn't let me print at full screen (8.5x11).  It reduced it so it could add some margins and no matter what I tried I couldn't get it to stop it.

Finally, someone around here mentioned to use openoffice draw.  I imported my image into openoffice draw and then sized it to be the whole page (8.5x11) and lined it up to be on the page borders.  I hit print and held my breath... It worked!!  God bless openoffice.  It saved my butt - hope it saves yours.
