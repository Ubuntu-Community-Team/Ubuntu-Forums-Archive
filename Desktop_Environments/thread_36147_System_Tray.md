### Post by 23meg on 2006-12-13
Right click empty space on your panel, click "Add to panel" and choose "Notification Area".
> 
In my system tray I have a network monitor, the time and the desktop manager thing.Seemingly you don't actually have a tray; those are GNOME panel applets that are placed on the panel, they aren't in the tray. The tray is called "Notification Area" in GNOME.
