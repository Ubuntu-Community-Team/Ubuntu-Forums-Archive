Cheers

If you're referring to compiz icon in the system tray, then yes. However, compiz does not give you the option to have kwin as a fallback in case compiz crashes and you're left with window with no titlebar/border. So you will have to manually restart kwin. With Beryl, a crash would just automatically start kwin or metacity, depending on the desktop you use.
