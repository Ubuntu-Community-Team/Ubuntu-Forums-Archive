### Post by boris357 on 2007-05-03
After I un-installed an application, the Icon is still showing in the applications menu. How do I remove the icon as it is no longer needed.

### Post by HornedBeast on 2007-05-03
Right click on "Applicatons" in the top right corner and choose "Edit Menus".

On the left side you will see the categories or directories such as "Internet", "Office", Etc.

Click the one that your 'stray' program is in.

You will see on the right hand side it will change the list of programs that are inside that category. From there simply UN-TICK the program that you do not want. Click "Close" *(There is no "Apply" or "Ok" as you would expect on a Windows system. The changes are made instantly).* This should remove the Application from the list. 

Hope this helps.

[I]
As a side note, sometimes I find the "Edit Menu" window to be a little unresponsive and sometimes doesn't show that i've UN-TICKED the box. However, once you know you have UNTICKED the program you want to remove, simply click Close anyway. Sometimes a reboot will sort this out. [/I]
