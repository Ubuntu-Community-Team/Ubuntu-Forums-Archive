### Post by Zip247 on 2009-03-18
From this thread....[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=871364](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=871364)


When you removed evolution you probably remove gnome-panel in the process. Log into your ubuntu and clt+alt+F1 to get to terminal mode. sudo apt-get install gnome-panel gnome-applets. ctl+alt+F7 to go back to gdm. You may have to restart x with ctl+alt+backspace.
