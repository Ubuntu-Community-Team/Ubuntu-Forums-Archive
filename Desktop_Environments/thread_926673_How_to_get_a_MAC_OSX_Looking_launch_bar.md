### Post by Colo2 on 2008-09-22
There are loads of tutorials about on how to get a MAC OSX Looking quicklaunch bar, but some of them require that you have desktop effects enabled to run them, maybe your graphics card isn't good enough or you dont want the desktop effects enabled. I have found out a way of getting a sort of cool looking quicklaunch.

[COLOR="Red"]I am very very very sorry if this is in the ubuntu documentation or it has been posted many times before or what not, but I found this out for myself and it might come in handy to some people ;D[/COLOR]

First. right click on one of the bars, either the Application - places - system bar, or the program bar at the bottom (by default)

Select: New panel

On the right hand side, a new blank panel should appear. 

Next: drag the new panel to the bottom of the page so its on top of the open program panel.

Next right click on the new panel and select Properties

In the box which appears, set the size to 55 pixels, then click on the background tab and select solid color and make the bar completely transparent by sliding the Style bar along to transparent.

Next click applications > internet | and drag firefox into the space where the bar was (was but still is, you can't see it coz its transparent ;D)

and it should line up on it, do the same with any other programs you need there


Enjoy!
