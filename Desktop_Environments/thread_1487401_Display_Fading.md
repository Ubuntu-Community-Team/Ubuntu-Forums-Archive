### Post by wojox on 2010-05-19
That's weird. Even when watching a video it fades huh? Try looking in your Screensaver Preferences and check when it regards the computer as idle.

---

### Post by icarus40 on 2010-05-19
Ha!  Bingo, wojox -- I'd not even thought of that, as there was no screen activity -- hadn't realized that ubuntu has a screensaver "visualization" option of BLANK!  That's what it was on by default.  Thanks!

### Post by zach.detton on 2010-05-19
You should also check System> Preferences> Power Management> Then the "On AC Power" tab there is a check box that says "Dim Display When Idle". Make sure that it's not checked either. I was having that problem when watching video as well. It was fine after I unchecked it though.
