### Post by fanoodlehey on 2006-03-21
Is 100% of physical memory usually in use, even when you don't have OO open? If so you may be using swap memory too much and this could keep your Hard Drive spun up all the time. That will shorten the life of the hard drive, particularly if you leave your box switched on all the time.
