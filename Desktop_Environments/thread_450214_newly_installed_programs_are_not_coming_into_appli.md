### Post by Kobalt on 2007-05-21
Not all applications you compile create a launcher, that's the reason. You can create it by yourself on the Desktop or on one of your panels with a right-clikc ("Create launcher" on the desktop, "Add to the panel > Custom launcher" in the panels). You'll need to know the command line used to launch your application. You can give the icon you want to the launcher... 
To add one to the menus I think you can take a loot at alacarte : press Alt+F2 then Enter to access it. You can also launch any application this way, if you'd like...
