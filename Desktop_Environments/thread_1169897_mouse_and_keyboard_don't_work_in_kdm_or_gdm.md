### Post by Floft on 2009-08-19
My computer does work now, but it is not because I fixed the X problem. I just reinstalled. But, I figured out what caused the problem. It turns out that a bunch of stuff on my hard drive was getting corrupted because I was not using the AHCI thing on my mother board. I switched to that and no more crashing or problems.
