### Post by Lin-X on 2006-09-26
Maybe this is not exactly an answer for you, but I have just installed Dapper from a dvd in the Linux Format magazine. You might want to look into that. It has Gnome, Xfce, and KDE desktops. You can try it live before you install and all three desktops install. You access them by logging out of one and clicking Sessions, chosing what you want, and then signing in again. I'm pretty pleased with it and you might like to try it.

I don't see what you mean about Gnome being ugly, since you can always modify any linux desktop almost any way you want. Go to their website, or just Google it and see what you come up with. I've found linux very amenable this way.

Hope you find what you're looking for.
