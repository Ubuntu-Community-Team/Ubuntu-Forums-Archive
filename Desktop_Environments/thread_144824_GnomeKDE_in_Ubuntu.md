### Post by Adrian on 2006-03-15
[QUOTE=MichaelZ]
Installing kde, does not install kubuntu-desktop and vice-versa. What will happen if I install kde instead of kubuntu-desktop? Will I lose gnome?
[/QUOTE]

Installing the **kde** package will give you the entire KDE suite. That means more programs than what you get by installing the **kubuntu-desktop** package. 

Installing **kubuntu-desktop** will give you all KDE programs that ships with Kubuntu, i.e. not the entire KDE suite but most necessary programs.

Gnome will not be deleted.

> 
And how can I switch from kde/kubuntu-desktop to gnome and vice-versa? Would this be possible? And how?


You do that from the "Session" menu of your login screen.
