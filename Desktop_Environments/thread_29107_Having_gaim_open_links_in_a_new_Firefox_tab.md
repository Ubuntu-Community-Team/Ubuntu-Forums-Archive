### Post by Levander on 2005-04-23
In Warty I had gaim configured to open links I clicked on in a new tab in Firefox.  However, since I've upgraded to Hoary, gaim is opening links I click on in a new browser window.  

Anybody remember what the configuration setting in gaim under Warty was to open links in a new tab?

Actually, the setting was to open links in a new tab under Mozilla.  But, it was easily modified to open links in a new tab in Firefox.  All the command line options were the same, just had to change the mozilla executable to be the firefox one.

---

### Post by SGC on 2005-04-23
**In firefox go to:**
edit > perferences > advanced > tabbed browsing

**Select open links from other applications in : **
a new tab in the most recent window
