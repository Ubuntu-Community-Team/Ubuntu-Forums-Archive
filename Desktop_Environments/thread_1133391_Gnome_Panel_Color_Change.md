### Post by hansdown on 2009-04-22
Hi catsup.

Click on system> administration> synaptic package manager.
Enter password.
Search for gnome color chooser.

Check the box, click " mark for installation".

Click " mark all upgrades".

Click " apply". 

Click " apply" again.
