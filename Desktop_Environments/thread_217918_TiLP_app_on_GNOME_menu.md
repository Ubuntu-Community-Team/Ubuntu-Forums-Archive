### Post by lxevolution on 2006-07-17
The quick and easy solution to this would be adding a TiLP shortcut to the menu via alacarte menu editor.

Just open the editor (alacarte). Click on any folder you want and click file / new entry. Enter tilp, choose your icon. and you're done. This should work.
