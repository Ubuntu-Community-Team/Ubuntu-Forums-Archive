### Post by Jucato on 2007-03-30
Right-click on the clock, select Show Timezone -> Configure Timezones... to setup which timezone you want to show. Then, right-click on the clock again, select Show Timezone and select that timezone.

Alternatively, once you have setup the timezone that you want, you can just hover your mouse over the clock and it will show you the different timezones you've set up.
