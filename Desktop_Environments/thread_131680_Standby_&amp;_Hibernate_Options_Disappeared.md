A strange thing happened to my Ubuntu install (Breezy).  Sometime within the last couple of days the functionality of System | Log Out changed. ### Post by LxP on 2006-02-18
Welcome to the forums Evan!

I suspect that you went to **System** > **Preferences** > **Sessions** and unchecked the **Ask on logout** option. Rechecking this should solve your problem.

---

### Post by eklitzke on 2006-02-18
Thanks, that fixed it... I remember disabling that because I was hoping the "Save current setup" option would go away.
