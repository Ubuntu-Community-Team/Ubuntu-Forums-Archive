### Post by sjnovick on 2006-02-22
This seems to happen to me from time to time as well.  My solution is to stop gdesklets and restart it.

Open at terminal and type :

gdesklets stop
gdesklets restart

For other options, try gdesklets --help

It should start up your gdesklets subroutines.
