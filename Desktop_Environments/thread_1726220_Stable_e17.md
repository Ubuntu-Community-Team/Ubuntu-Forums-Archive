### Post by squaregoldfish on 2011-04-11
I don't know how old the version is the the repos, but e17 is is still Alpha software, so no officially stable release exists.

Having said that, I use it as my only wm, and have been for several years with few issues. Your best bet is to compile direct from SVN ([http://www.enlightenment.org/p.php?p=contribute&l=en](http://www.enlightenment.org/p.php?p=contribute&l=en)), and keep it up to date fairly regularly - bugfixes are happening quite quickly now.
