Also, I'd agree that you ought not upgrade if things are going great now and there isn't some feature you need.  I used 10.10 for a while, tried 11.04 and hated it because I couldn't for the life of me get rid of the open-source nvidia drivers and use the far better proprietary ones and there were a few other bugs.
