### Post by djgenesis on 2005-12-22
Your mistake lies in 
```

Section "Device"
        Identifier      "ATI Technologies, Inc. Radeon 7000 VE (RV100 QY) 0"
        Driver          "ati"
        **BusID           "AGP:1:0:0"**
EndSection

Section "Device"
        Identifier      "ATI Technologies, Inc. Radeon 7000 VE (RV100 QY) 1"
        Driver          "radeon"
        **BusID           "AGP:1:0:0"**
EndSection

```

You are telling the system to output the screen at the same AGP bus.

On your shell type
