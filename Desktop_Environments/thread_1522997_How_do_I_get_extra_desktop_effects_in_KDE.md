Hi,
I see that there is a few here: 
[https://launchpad.net/~samrog131/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~samrog131/+archive/ppa)

Another thought is to go to [http://kde-look.org/](http://kde-look.org/) and search for "effect" under the type of search "kde Improvement".
