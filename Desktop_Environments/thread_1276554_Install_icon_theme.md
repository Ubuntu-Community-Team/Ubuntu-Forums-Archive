Actually the theme archives are not supposed to be extracted. :)

Just go to System/Preferences/Appearance, and drop the theme archive to the Theme-tab. Then click "Customize" and you'll be able to select your icon theme for use in the Icons-tab.

This approach works form GTK2 and Metacity themes, icons and mouse cursors.

In case you get an archive that refuses to unstall this way you can extract it's contents manually, GTK2 and Metacity themes into ~/.themes and icons & mouse cursors into ~/.icons.

GDM themes are installed & selected in System/Preferences/Login Window.
