### Post by deadflowr on 2015-06-05
Try moving/removing your cache and your profile
and see about what it looks like from a fresh perspective.

The cache and the profile are in hidden folders.
You can locate them by opening the Files program and pressing ctrl +h. (this enables the show hidden feature)
The cache is in .cache/google-chrome.
The profile is in .config/google-chrome.

You can either delete them or simply rename them something like google-chrome.old.
This is all done in your own personal folder, so no need for root.
