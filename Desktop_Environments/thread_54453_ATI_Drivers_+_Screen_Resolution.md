### Post by RicgorE on 2005-08-09
Something is broken in the screen resolution applet. It seems to default to the highest resolution available in xorg.conf and not allow changes to the resolution as it should. By removing the resolutions above the one you want to use from xorg.conf, you are "forcing" the one you want.
