### Post by bswilson on 2006-05-18
Perhaps Firefox is not selected as your default Web browser on your system?  To confirm this, click on **System**, then **Preferences**, and select **Preferred Applications**.

Then choose Firefox or enter a custom command.  This *should* tell Thunderbird that when it sees a URL, that Firefox is the browser to launch.
