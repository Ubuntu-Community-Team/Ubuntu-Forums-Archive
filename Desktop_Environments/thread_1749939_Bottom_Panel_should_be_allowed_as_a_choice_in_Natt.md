### Post by Krytarik on 2011-05-05
Just add "gnome-panel" to your "Startup Applications".

You can also run it via the Alt+F2 dialog if you like.

Once running, just remove its top panel:
- right-click at it
- choose "Delete This Panel"

Peaceful coexistence! ;-)
