### Post by ajgreeny on 2011-02-20
Are you in compact view or icon view?

The two views act differently to each other.

In icon view the files and folders are arranged in rows and the cursor key highlight moves across then down to the start of the next row;  in compact view the arrangement is in columns from top to bottom, and the cursor moves down then across to the top of the next column.
