### Post by kripkenstein on 2007-04-21
Well, I am no expert on the subject, but there are various options besides the Best Shapes/Subpixel Smoothing/etc. option in Preferences->Fonts. Click on "details" in the lower right.

The difference with other Linux flavors is probably something with the smoothing or hinting, which you can change in the "details" window. Just try a few changes and see what happens, the nice thing with the settings window is that fonts on the screen change as you click, so you get immediate feedback :)
