### Post by RottNKorpse on 2012-06-26
I also had this issue with fullscreen videos and fullscreen virtual machines but I seemed to have fixed it permanently by installed the "compiz-plugins-extra" package. Once I installed that I have not seen the error again at all.

[https://apps.ubuntu.com/cat/applications/compiz-plugins-extra/](https://apps.ubuntu.com/cat/applications/compiz-plugins-extra/)

try this and let me know if this solves your problem.
