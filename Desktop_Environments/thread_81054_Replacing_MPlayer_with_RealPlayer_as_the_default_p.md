### Post by orbital on 2005-10-23
When I play RealVideo files in Firefox, MPlayer plays them as default. How can I set Firefox to play them with RealPlayer?

---

### Post by LorenzoD on 2005-10-23
You should be able to right-click on a RealVideo file in Nautilus, select Properties. In the dialog, click on the Open with tab, and activate the radio button beside MPlayer.
