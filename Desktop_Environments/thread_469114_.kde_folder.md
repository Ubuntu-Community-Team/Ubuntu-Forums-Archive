### Post by userundefine on 2007-06-09
.kde contains all your settings for KDE apps.  I wouldn't delete it.  I'd run du -h and see where the big files are in that directory.  Most of them are small configs.  If you used ktorrent, that's probably your answer because it defaults to storing downloaded files to .kde.
