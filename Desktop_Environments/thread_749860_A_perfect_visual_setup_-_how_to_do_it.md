### Post by cybertron3000 on 2008-04-08
If you want some free visual tweaking advice, and would like to make your friends regret blowing their money on a costly new computer with a "pretty" setup:

Go under your system>preferences>appearance to choose these preferences:

1) All fonts: "ae_AlManzomah Bold 12"; set it on "subpixel smoothing".

2) Under themes: use "T-ish-Brushed" theme.  You'll need to to first install (applications>add/remove) the art manager to acquire this awesome theme.

3)  Open up your firefox browser.  Go under tools>add-ons and then choose "get themes" in the lower corner (you can also just go to the mozilla firefox website on your own).  Find and download the theme "Azerty III 3.1.1".  Make sure you activate it under tools>add-ons>themes, by clicking the "use theme" button.

That's it - so easy, but so expensive-looking.
