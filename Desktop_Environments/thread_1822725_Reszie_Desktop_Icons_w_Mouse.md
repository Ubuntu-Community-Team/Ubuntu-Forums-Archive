### Post by 3Miro on 2011-08-11
You can resize with Ctr + Scroll in both Firefox and Chromium browsers as well as an open windows of the file manager Nautilus. In 10.10, the all icons on your desktop are managed by Nautilus (not Compiz) and hence you should be able to resize them. However, you may have to go into the settings for Nautilus, as opposed to using the shortcut (there may be a conflict between Compiz and Nautilus desktop).
