### Post by matthew on 2005-07-30

I had to do a small amount of clean up (mostly for people who had a middle name in my address book), but actually less than when I switched from Outlook 98 to Outlook 2005.

### Post by jeremy on 2005-07-31
There is an free app called Outport - [http://outport.sourceforge.net/](http://outport.sourceforge.net/) - that you can run in windows,  it will export from .pst to a format that can be imported by evolution.

I have used it several times (outlook 2000, xp and 2003), and it has worked perfectly.
