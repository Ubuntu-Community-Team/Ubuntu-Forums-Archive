### Post by endfx on 2006-08-02
Way more difficult than you think -- almost impossible

You could use the livecd to browse, that would ensure that you don't have anything on your harddrive. But your ISP will still know where you have gone, and the website you are visiting will still know what isp you're calling from.

You could use a proxy, that would ensure the place you are visiting doesn't know where you are calling from , but then again your ISP will see what IPs/domains you are requesting. So if you live in a country where ISPs keep track of where/what you request (United States or China) your ISP will still know what you've been up to.

If you're really curious about staying annonymous, you should checkout freenet.
