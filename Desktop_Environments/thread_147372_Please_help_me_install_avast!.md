### Post by colo on 2006-03-20
I know avast! from its Windows-version, but not the GNU/Linux-application. However, if you don't explicitly need your anti-virus-software for scanning files for posing threats to WINDOWS-systems, there is absolutely no need to run such a thing in GNU/Linux, at least yet. Repeat: Desktop-users of GNU/Linux don't need anti-virus-software running on their systems.

Btw, I don't think that avast! for GNU/Linux poses an interface similar to the Windows-version, it will most likely be command-line only.

E:\ Ok, just educated myself about that matter: There's a GTK+-UI for avast, as well.
