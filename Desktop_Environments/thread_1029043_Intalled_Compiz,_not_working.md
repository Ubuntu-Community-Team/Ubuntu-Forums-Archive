### Post by teh_yodeler on 2009-01-03
Are you sure you're not triggering "Expose" instead of the "desktop cube" effect?

If you have "desktop cube" enabled, usually ctrl alt (left or right arrow) will move the cube, when you're looking at the desktop.  Also a middle mouse click will enable you to spin the cube.

Make sure you've enabled "desktop cube," "rotate cube" and "viewport switcher."

And as far as I'm aware, you do have to use 4 workspaces (no more, no less)

---

### Post by Cameron Kloot on 2009-01-03
Cool, thanks.  Enabling "rotate cube" made it work.
