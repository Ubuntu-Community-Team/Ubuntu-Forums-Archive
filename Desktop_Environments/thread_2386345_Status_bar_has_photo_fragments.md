### Post by rapidrob on 2018-03-07
It's a little weird to fix. You hit ALT and RIGHT click over the photo fragment and you have to hold the two keys down. As you do that you drag the mouse over with a third finger . A pop-up menu will be displayed where you"Remove from Panel the whole status bar and the fragments are gone.
You then re-open the status bar with what ever you'd like by clicking on the upper Task bar and enabling the status bar and what ever panels you use.
