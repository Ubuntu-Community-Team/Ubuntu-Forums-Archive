### Post by anthonyvo on 2011-05-06
Fixed it - via CCSM:

1. Went into "Desktop Wall" under Desktop
[IMG]http://anthonymendez.s3.amazonaws.com/images/ccsm-desktopwall-01-640.jpg[/IMG]

2. Realized the "Edge Radius" was too high - brought it back down to match my wife's profile (5)
[IMG]http://anthonymendez.s3.amazonaws.com/images/ccsm-desktopwall-02-640.jpg[/IMG]

All is good in the world.
-Anthony
