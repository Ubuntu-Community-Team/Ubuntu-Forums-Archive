### Post by bford16 on 2008-01-12
Well, here is what I did.  I moved the workspace switcher, window list, and "show desktop" button to the top panel.  Then I removed the bottom panel, and repositioned the top panel on the bottom.  I set the panel to the center position, and turned off the "expand" option for the separator in the panel.  Then I set the panel to "normal" width instead of full width.
