### Post by dataw0lf on 2005-04-12
People wonder why I like mplayer.  :

```
mplayer FILE.wma -ao pcm -aofile FILE.wav
``` 

Then convert the .wav file to one of the many mp3 / ogg converters (lame, oggenc, etc).
