### Post by Vormeph on 2013-06-19
Nothing of the sort that I know of. You could try scavenging through the package manager and look for some modules you could put together. In all honesty, such a Windowsesque start menu could compromise the performance of your machine. If you really want eye-candy, then by all means try out Cinnamon/MATE. Linux Mint takes advantage of both DEs quite well, at the expense of performance.
