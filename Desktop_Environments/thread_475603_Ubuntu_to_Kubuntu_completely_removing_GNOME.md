### Post by milan_koko on 2007-06-16
there is a command: xwmconfig . It lets you choose your window manager.

So:
[LIST=1]
[*]install KDE
[*]exit or kill GNOME (ctrl+alt+backspace)
[*]xwmconfig - choose KDE
[*]startx
[/LIST]
