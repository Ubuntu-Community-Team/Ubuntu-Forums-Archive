### Post by joined on 2011-09-07
Just solved... ehehehehe...

only set the "shift with numeric keypad keys works as in MS Windows" to ENABLED in the Keyboard/Layout/Options/Miscellaneous Compatibility Options.

That's all, thanks to all ! :)
