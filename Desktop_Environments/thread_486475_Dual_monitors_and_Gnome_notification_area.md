### Post by Rhubarb on 2008-03-06
As I don't have 2 monitors here, this is just a stab in the dark.  Anyhow, try this on the monitor that doesn't have the notification area:

Right click somewhere on an empty area in the gnome bar up the top of the screen.
Click on *Add to Panel ...*
Scroll down to the Utilities section, click on *Notification Area*
Then press the *Add* button down the bottom of the window, and press *Close.*

Your notifications should pop up from the location where you originally right clicked as above.
