### Post by siorai on 2006-03-06
While it's not the perfect solution, nor the quickest, I just make a wallpaper that spans both monitors. I have a 1280x1024 to the left and a 1680x1050 to the right, so I make a wallpaper that is 2960x1050 with the left portion aligned to the top. Not quite as easy to change each monitor's wallpaper, but it does work perfectly.
