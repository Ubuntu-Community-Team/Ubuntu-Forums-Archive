Yeah, it is nice for what it is, but I am not really a fan of abstract backgrounds so I personally would not use it. I used to enjoy abstract works and vectors with simple colours, but nowadays I prefer artsy stuff which incites thought within me and others.

As an example, [my current background](http://i89.photobucket.com/albums/k235/Domei/backgrounds/1257519204026.jpg) incites deep feelings related to how the girl is morning the loss of the clockwork android whose head she is holding. I also like to think she is an android as well, and the background tends to resonate the idea that everyone has feelings, no matter what the world wants to think.

And on an entirely aesthetic note, the artwork is absolutely smashing as well. Hope this helps you understand why there might not be a ton of lovers of this. You have to remember that everyone's tastes in art are different, and not every form of art can speak to them even if they can respect it and like it for what it is. Something has to fall in line with what someone loves that you get the "omg this is exactly what I was looking for" comments. Cheers. :)
