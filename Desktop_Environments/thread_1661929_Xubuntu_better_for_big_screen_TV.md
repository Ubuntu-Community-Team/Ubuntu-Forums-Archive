### Post by cascade9 on 2011-01-07
Probably not. 

The issue you are probably having is sheer number of pixels. Newer TVs use 1920x1080 (1080p) resolution, and it puts a lot more strain on the GPU (or intel video) than 1024x786 or 1280x1024 resolutions.
