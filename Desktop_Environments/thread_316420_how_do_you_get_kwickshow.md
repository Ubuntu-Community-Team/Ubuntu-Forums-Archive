### Post by claydoh on 2006-12-10
Kuickshow has become obsolete as it depends on a library that Kubuntu doesn't/cannot use, basically it hasn't been updated in 3 years or so. There are alternatives, such as showimg (a simple viewer,which is in Universe) as well as gwenview and digikam

[https://launchpad.net/distros/ubuntu/+source/kdebase/+bug/42081](https://launchpad.net/distros/ubuntu/+source/kdebase/+bug/42081)
for some further info
