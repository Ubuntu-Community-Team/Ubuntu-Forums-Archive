by toolbar I think you mean panel?  first unlock plasma (right click >unlock widgets) now you will see the cashew icon on the panel like this :

[IMG]http://imgur.com/m1zAL.png[/IMG]

the cashew is the rightmost little icon. click it.

now hover the mouse on screenedge, and drag the panel to the top of your screen. :)
