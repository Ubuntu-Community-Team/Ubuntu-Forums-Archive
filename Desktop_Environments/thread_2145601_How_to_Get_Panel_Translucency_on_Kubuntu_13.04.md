### Post by llanitedave on 2013-05-15
I upgraded to Kubuntu 13.04 a couple of days ago.  I like almost every new tweak, except one.  My main panel (which I have moved to the left side of the screen, ala Unity ) is now perfectly transparent.  Some may prefer this, but I would like a bit of hazy translucency the way the panel used to be.

I can't find any configuration option to change the transparency of the panel.  I'm assuming its there somewhere.  Does anyone know how to get to it?

---

### Post by Nolix on 2013-05-16
The panel translucency depends on the desktop theme you have chosen. You can change the theme of the panel alone in System Settings -> Workspace Appearance -> Desktop Theme -> Details.
