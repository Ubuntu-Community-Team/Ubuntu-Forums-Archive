### Post by inobe on 2011-01-31
you can reset your desktop by deleting the .kde folder, this will reset the desktop to default out of the box, you will lose all previous custom settings.

are you able to navigate at all?

if not, press **alt+f2** then type dolphin and hit enter, when dolphin opens, press keys **alt+.** browse for .kde folder, delete it and restart.
