### Post by SuperSonic4 on 2010-02-07
1) Search your package manager for **qtcurve-gtk2** and **qtcurve-kde4** and install them. I say search because they may have different names in kubuntu

What happens if you go to **System Settings --> Appearance --> GTK Styles and fonts**

Mine is showing qtcurve but take your pick from the dropdown menu



2) **System Settings --> Appearance --> GTK Styles and fonts** and pick Qt4.


In both cases you'll have to restart GTK apps for the effect to take place
