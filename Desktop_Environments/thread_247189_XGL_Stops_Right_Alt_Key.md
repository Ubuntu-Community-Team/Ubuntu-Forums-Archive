### Post by Paul41 on 2006-08-30
I had a similar (but not exactly the same) problem and was able to solve it by selecting a different keyboard in System->Preferences->Keyboard.  I just tried several different ones until I found one that worked.  I don't know if it will solve your problem, but it might be worth a try.

---

### Post by whitewizardcoder on 2006-08-30
That worked!!  I had "Generic 101-key PC" selected instead of what it should've been: "Generic 104-key PC".

Thanks!
