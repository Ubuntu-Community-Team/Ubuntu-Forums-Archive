### Post by msnthrp on 2006-02-19
If you were using Kubuntu Breezy, I'd tell you to click on the K-start button, come down the menu to System Settings, take the level called Personal and click on Desktop.  When Desktop opens, third block down is called Behavior.  At the bottom of the box you configure the three mouse buttons to set the action you want.

I think Ubuntu may have something similar.  Good luck.
