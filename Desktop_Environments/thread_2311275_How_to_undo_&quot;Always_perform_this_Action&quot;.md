### Post by deadflowr on 2016-01-25
I'm not currently on 15.10, but I think it should be like this:

Go to top right corner of your desktop, click the cog wheel icon.
Go to About This Computer.
Go to Removable Media.
Toggle you selection; you might need to other application option and sift through the programs to find gthumb.
And then close.

That should do it,
whether or not it does is a different story.
