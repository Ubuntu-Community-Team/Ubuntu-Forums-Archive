### Post by d3v1ant_0n3 on 2006-12-29
Feisty (Ubuntu 7.04 Feisty Fawn) is the next release of Ubuntu- Scheduled for April 07. It's still very early in development, so *really* not suitable for new users, but will focus on a lot of Eye Candy type features- one of which will be the inclusion of Compiz effects as standard (like the cube you mentioned).

Depending on your hardware setup, there are a number of different ways to install Compiz (or Beryl- similar to Compiz- less stable but more effects). You might find it a little harder with an Ati graphics card.
[ HERE ](http://wiki.beryl-project.org/wiki/Install_Beryl_on_Ubuntu) is a run through of installation for Beryl on Edgy.

unfortunately I'm completely lacking on Compiz links, since I use Beryl, and a google search only seems to bring up outdated links.
