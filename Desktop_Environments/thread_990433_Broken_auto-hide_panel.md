### Post by lisati on 2008-11-22
I had both panels disappear completely with Xubuntu 8.10 a couple of times.

If you're able to get to terminal (right-clicking on the "Home" icon" on the desktop and then on "Open terminal here" should work if you can't get to your applications->accesories menu) you can then enter the following:
```
xfce4-panel &
```
Don't forget the "&". This should get your panel(s) back.
