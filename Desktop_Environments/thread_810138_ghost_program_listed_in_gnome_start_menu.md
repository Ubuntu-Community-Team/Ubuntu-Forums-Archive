### Post by bmac on 2008-05-28
Try right clicking on your menu and selecting "Edit Menus"
Find the menu you wish to change and left click on it.
Each item listed on the right can be unchecked - as to not show up in the menu or right click on the item and delete to remove from menu....

Hope this helps....
