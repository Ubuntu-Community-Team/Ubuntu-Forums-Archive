### Post by kerry_s on 2010-07-25
i just like it.

you do have standard gnome, which you can use any time.
just log out & select "gnome" in the session menu, you will then have the standard desktop version.

i like things simple, i don't use the desktop anyways so it's nice having the netbook-launcher there.
once you get rid of the useless locked gnome-panel you pretty much do what ever you want, everything else is changeable.

i use avant-window-navigator for the panel & metacity composite.
i chose awn cause it has the best system tray, theres enough applets to get by. awn has many ways to use it, i have mine floating & it pops up when i move the mouse to the bottom.

ps: you can use "multiple desktops" in netbook already, i just don't need it.
