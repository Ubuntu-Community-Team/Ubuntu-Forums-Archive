### Post by Hero of Time on 2010-02-19
Are you sure you have a tasklist or similar on either panel? If you don't, then right click on an empty part of the panel and click "Add panel item". Look for tasklist in list of items and drag it to the panel where you want it. You will now have the option to restore windows. Usually, alt+tab should work even if you don't have a tasklist.

### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-09-15
Coupla things.

* It sounds like you just have the taskbar scrunched over to the right side. Try right-clicking to the left of the first taskbar item and selecting "move" from the menu that appears. 

* AWN telling you you needed a taskbar only refers to its auto-hide behavior; you can still have AWN panels without taskbars. Obviously not your problem anymore. = )

* The window selector itself is usually called a "window list" or "taskbar," yes. The individual items are generally just referred to as "taskbar items."
