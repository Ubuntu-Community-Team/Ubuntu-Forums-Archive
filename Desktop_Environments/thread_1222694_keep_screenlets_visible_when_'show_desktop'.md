### Post by ssdt on 2009-07-25
When compiz crosses with Screenlet, many of the features of screenlets might not work, so I suggest that you do not use that.

---

### Post by m4lte on 2009-07-26
I found a **solution** for the problem:

uncheck the widget plugin and then go to general options and uncheck
'Hide skip taskbar windows'.
