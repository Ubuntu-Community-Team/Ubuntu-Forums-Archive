### Post by ssj3strife on 2008-04-14
Since your post is 3 weeks old already, you might have figured this out by now - but there is already an icon-only main menu available to you. Just right-click on your panel, select "Add to Panel," and add "Main Menu." The "Menu Bar" has the text with it, but the "Main Menu" is just an icon. That's what you're looking for, right?
