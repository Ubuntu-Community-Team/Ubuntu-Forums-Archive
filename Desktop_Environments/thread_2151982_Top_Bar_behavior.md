### Post by Frogs Hair on 2013-06-06
The XBMC  standalone session is independent and does not open inside of Ubuntu and that would explain the lack of a panel. I have not used Myth TV but I suspect there is a reason for the panel and so F11 would be the full screen option. As for Unity there is currently no way to auto-hide the panel and know of no plans  to add the feature.
