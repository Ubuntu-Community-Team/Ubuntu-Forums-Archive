### Post by Enigma12 on 2015-04-11
> If I try to print a second document in Libre office, the printer spits out page after page of garbage.

You don't state what printer you're using. Maybe, if you did, someone might be able to offer advice. 

FWIW, I have a (possibly) similar problem with a Lexmark laser printer. I've read that Lexmark printers are sometimes problematical in Linux, but that's what I had, so I use it. 

Whether I print from LO Writer or Abiword, I can successfully print many pages, but **only if the printer just keeps printing**. If I let the printer pause, subsequent page(s) come out as "garbage." What works for me, after the first page or pages are printed, is to turn the printer off, then right back on, to do additional pages. 

It is a minor annoyance, but that is simpler and quicker than closing, then reopening, the program. You might want to try that.
