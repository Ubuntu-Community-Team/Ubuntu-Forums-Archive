If it's text, it may have something to do with the default fonts and font sizes used by the browser. The site designer must have not taken into consideration the fact that users can change their default fonts or that their user-agents may have a different set of default fonts.

On the Flash always appearing in front of HTML items, I believe that is expected behaviour

On the other sites, specifically on SkySports, I get the same as your screen shot. The navigation gets "fixed" after I hit Control+Minus (decrease font size).

Um... it's not an OS nor a browser problem. The problem rests with how the websites were constructed, The best thing to do is to write to the webmaster/s and make them aware of the issue. :D
