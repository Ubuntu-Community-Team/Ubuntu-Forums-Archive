### Post by victor.pillac on 2012-04-06
Ok I found a solution here: [http://askubuntu.com/questions/100710/how-to-get-back-menu-bar-in-kile-after-switching-to-lxde](http://askubuntu.com/questions/100710/how-to-get-back-menu-bar-in-kile-after-switching-to-lxde)

"In ~/.kde/share/config/kilerc, look for the section [KileMainWindow], and within that look for the line menubar=Disabled. Change Disabled to Enabled or delete this line."
