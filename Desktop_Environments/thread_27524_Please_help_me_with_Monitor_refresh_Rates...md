### Post by heimo on 2005-04-17
So what did you try? Which resolution and refresh rate? You do know that there is hardware limits what your monitor can do. For example my 19" CRT can do 1280 x 1024 @ 90Hz, but not more. I can get 85Hz at my favourite resolution - even if I'd like it to be 90Hz. If your monitor manufacturer says that 85Hz is optimal refresh rate for some spesific resolution ( 1024x768 ), it's very probably the upper limit what you monitor actually can do.

I checked your monitors manual and I really doubt that it can do anything abowe 1280 x 1024 @ 65Hz, but I wouldn't use that resolution on 17" monitor anyway. Did you try those modelines I posted? I recommend 1024 x 768 @ 85Hz if you can get that configured - it should be good mode for your monitor.
