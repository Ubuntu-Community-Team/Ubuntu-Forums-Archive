### Post by dagoss on 2008-09-12
Hmm.  Do you have a low-end system that isn't capable of most of the snazzy GUI effects?  Maybe that pretty fade-away effect is causing problems.  Or maybe you've just found one of KDE4's many bugs.

Have you tried removing the logout/shutdown confirmation?  Go to system settings, and under the advanced tab look in session manager.  Maybe that will get you around that screen shutdown screen.
