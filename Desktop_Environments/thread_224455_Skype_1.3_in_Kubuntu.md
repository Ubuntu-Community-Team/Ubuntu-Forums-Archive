### Post by Hikaru79 on 2006-07-28
Skype 1.2 was running fine in Kubuntu 6.06, but I decided to give 1.3 a try. It installed fine, but now for some reason, the mic no longer works. I can still hear the audio, but nobody else can hear me.

I've already tried the skype_dsp_hijacker. I think it might by a Kmix problem (I know someone in another Skype thread said 1.3 resets all your settings there, but I don't really know what they should look like.

Anyone think they can help? Thanks in advance :)

---

### Post by Hikaru79 on 2006-07-28
Well, thanks to RichJ on IRC, my problem is fixed.

I was right about the Kmix settings. I had to go back and turn on the red light for both the mic and source entries, and jack the volume to max. Now it works! Thanks Rich!
