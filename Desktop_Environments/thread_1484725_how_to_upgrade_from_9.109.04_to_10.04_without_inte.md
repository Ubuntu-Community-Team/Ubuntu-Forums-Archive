### Post by parn on 2010-05-16
If you have 9.10, it is quite straight forward.

If you wish to upgrade from 9.10 -> 10.04 from CD, you will have to get the ubuntu alternate CD - not the live CD.

Boot into 9.10 (make sure you have upgraded everything), insert the alternate CD and there will be an option to upgrade from the CD, choose to perform the upgrade from the CD and not from the network.

No experience with upgrading from 9.04.
