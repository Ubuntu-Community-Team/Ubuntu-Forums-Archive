### Post by Jungle-Cat on 2007-12-07
I'm not entirely sure, but I'm assuming it is a visually-modified version of the standard applet that ships with Gnome. Thus, you already have it, presuming you are running Ubuntu.

Right click on one of your gnome panels (like where programs minimize, and where your menus are) and select "Add to panel...". Locate the weather applet, and set it up!

Hope this helps.
