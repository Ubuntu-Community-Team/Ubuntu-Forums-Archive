### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-01-27
You can remove the notification area by right clicking a thin bar just in front wired or wireless icon. Just select remove from panel.

This mark is very hard to see on some themes(almost invisible). The other option is to remove it from the synaptic package manager.

This will remove update notifications as well as others.
