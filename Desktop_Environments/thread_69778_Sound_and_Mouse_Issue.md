### Post by mlomker on 2005-09-27
> Just got kubuntu installed on my Dell laptop, everything looks fine,but...
Sound card doesnt work properly, there is little pause during the playback.

Go into the Control Center, Sound & Multimedia, Sound System.  Make sure that the *Run with highest possible priority* option is checked. 

It's normal for some stuttering when you first log in.  I also get that for a brief moment when amaroK starts a new MP3 file.

> and so does the mouse, it will stop from time to time.

You installed Hoary and not Breezy?  Breezy is known to have that problem.
