### Post by duminas on 2005-12-14
In the Gnome panel, **System > [the first menu item] > Themes**
Or you can issue **gnome-theme-manager** on the terminal--same thing, as both will get the job done. Then hit the "Install Theme..." button.

### Post by aysiu on 2005-12-14
[QUOTE=Rackerz]I've extracted the tar.gz and there is an index.theme i try to use it and it says invalid format?[/QUOTE] Don't extract it. Leave it as is (.tar.gz or however it was when you downloaded it). Go to System > Preferences > Themes and just drag and drop (with your mouse) the .tar.gz on to the Theme Manager window.
