### Post by Dogmeat on 2005-10-11
That may be because on wheeled mice, if you "click" the wheel it pastes in whatever you selected before. If you can discover a way to unbind the middle mouse wheel clicking, your problem with the touchpad will probably be gone.

However if you can't or don't want to bother, there is another way around (which is less practical but nevertheless shoud work):

After typing a URL just move the mouse away from any "pasteable" boxes (for example the URL bar or the search) if you touch the wheel area and the cursor isn't on top of something you can paste to, you won't see urls appearing out of nowhere.
