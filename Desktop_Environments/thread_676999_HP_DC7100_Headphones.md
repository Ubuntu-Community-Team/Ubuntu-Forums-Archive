### Post by biswajit on 2008-03-24
- Double-click on the speaker icon to invoke the volume control panel
- Edit -> Preferences
- Then select Headphone Jack Sense
- Click on the Switches tab in the volume control panel and enable Headphone Jack Sense

This stops audio from the PC speaker and also cuts main volume when headphones are inserted into the headphone jack.

- Hope that helps if you haven't already figured that out by now.
