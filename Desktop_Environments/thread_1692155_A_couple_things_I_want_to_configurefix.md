system settings> input devices> touchpad> scrolling> enabel/ disable.

the highlight window feature in "all affects" may be behind the window switching, i am uncertain, i think it would be some desktop affects setting.

### Post by coolbeans777 on 2011-02-21
I figured it out.

All you have to do is right click on the desktop, click desktop settings, go to mouse actions, and delete the vertical scrolling one.
