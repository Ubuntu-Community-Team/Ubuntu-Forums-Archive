### Post by jaden93 on 2009-11-21
tp zoom-in on your desktop is really simple. look on your keyboard and press the super key. on some keyboards its the button with the windows symbol on it, or the button that you would usually use to open up the windows start menu. once you find it, hold it down and use the scroll up arrow on your laptop finger pad, if you have one.
