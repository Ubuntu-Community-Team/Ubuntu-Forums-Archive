### Post by ethanay on 2010-02-11
FYI, since this was a cause of consternation for me since yesterday:

IF
1. you are using gmail
2. you are using offline mail (via Google Gears)

then there is a [bug]("http://www.google.com/support/forum/p/gmail/thread?tid=22a57e4befe39f87&hl=en") in the launching of the new Google Buzz that causes gmail to malfunction:

> The "Compose Mail," "Inbox," "Outbox," etc. under the GMail logo have disappeared. In their stead is a non-functional "More" button.

The [solution]("http://www.google.com/support/forum/p/gmail/thread?tid=435e2259c0eb3e51&hl=en"):

> 1) Disable Offline Gmail (either at the loading screen on the bottom right, or via your Settings)
2) You'll get a spash screen introducing you to Buzz. You'll now see Buzz in your left pane.
3) Re-enable Offline via your Settings.
