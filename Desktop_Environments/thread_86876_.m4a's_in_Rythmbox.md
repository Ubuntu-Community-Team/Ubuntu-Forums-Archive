### Post by RAOF on 2005-11-06
Actually, Ubuntu **does** ship with gstreamer by default, partially becuase it is so easy to add the non-free codec plugins, like aac/m4a encoding/decoding.  And you're right, it is a licensing issue.  Practically no audio/video format is free to use.

I'd also suggest looking at [this page from the user-guide]("http://help.ubuntu.com/starterguide/C/faqguide-all.html#sect-music-and-movies").
