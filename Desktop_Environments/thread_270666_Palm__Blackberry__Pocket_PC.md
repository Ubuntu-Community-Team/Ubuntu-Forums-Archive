### Post by fragos on 2006-10-03
Palm has had good support in Linux.  I use jpilot and pilot-link.  For some reason the current Ubuntu has given me trouble and I havn't been able to get my Palm to sync.  I purchased a USB bluetooth dongle and have had some success transfering files to the Palm.  The SD card reader is also good in both directions for file transfer.  I use Tealback from Tealpoint to do my backups to an SD card.  This is a superior restore vehicle because you can restore without a PC.
