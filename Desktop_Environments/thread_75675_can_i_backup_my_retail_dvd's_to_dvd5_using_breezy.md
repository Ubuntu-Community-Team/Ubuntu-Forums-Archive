### Post by manicka on 2005-10-14
I use dvd shrink with wine. It does an excellent job but isn't a native code app which some people don't like. Native apps will copy the main movie for you, but not give a complete backup copy like dvd shrink does.

### Post by pomalin on 2005-10-14
You can also search for [k9copy]("http://k9copy.free.fr/") You will have to compil it from source after installing some kde stuff and make [vamps]("http://k9copy.free.fr/vamps-0.97.tar.gz"). Or you can also alien the rpm of [xdvdshrink]("http://dvdshrink.sourceforge.net/"). You will need mkisofs, dvdauthor, mjpegtools, libdvdread, toolame, transcode, subtitleripper,gocr, libgtk2-perl that are all in the repositories.
