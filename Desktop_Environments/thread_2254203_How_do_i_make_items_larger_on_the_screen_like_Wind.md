### Post by daveharas on 2014-11-25
Hi - look in settings again, select display there you will see a slider scale for menu and title bars move it and select a comfortable setting for your eyes. In your browser on the far right at the top for Firefox you'll see 3 bars click it and the is a + & - this will increase and decrease the size of the text in the browser. Or try hitting 'ctrl + or - and you will get the same effect in any browser. Hope this helps.
