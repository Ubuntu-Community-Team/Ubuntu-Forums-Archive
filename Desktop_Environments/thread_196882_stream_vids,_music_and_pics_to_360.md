### Post by gamma on 2006-06-15
There isn't any 3rd party program out there that can stream video to the 360 as of this point in time. You'll have to get Windows Media Center Edition 2005 for that...

As for music and pics check out [twonkymedia](http://www.twonkyvision.de/), which has a demo that will let you share you pics and music for 30 minutes or [xboxmediaserve](http://sourceforge.net/projects/x360mediaserve/) which will stream just music to your 360..
