 Actually gtk2 (controls) and metacity (window borders) themes can't phone home 'cause they're not apps, they just a bunch of graphics and a text-based config file which is read by gtk and metacity respectivelly. XP themes are executable plugins and thus can do those nasty tricks. But gnome themes can't 'cause they're not executable.
