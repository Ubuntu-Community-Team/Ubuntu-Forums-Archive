### Post by cwsnyder on 2008-10-10
Do you have the documentation for your monitor to find what the supported maximum resolution is?

One thing about CRT monitors, they will attempt to display resolutions which the dot pitch on the display will not really support.  They are more forgiving than LCD monitors, but will generate a fuzzy display if the display does not match.
