### Post by maddave on 2006-01-13
I ve been using Gnome on red hat at work for the past week and just booted up ubuntu.  I have noticed that I dont have open terminals, applications etc showing up on the bottom task bar/panel.  In red hat gnome these show up so I can click on them without having to move windows around to find ones hidden behind others!

---

### Post by 23meg on 2006-01-13
Add a "Window list" item to your Gnome panel by right clicking in a blank section and choosing "Add to panel".
