### Post by kebes on 2008-01-04
> **evil316 said:**

I have tried the project looking glass demo. It has some neat ideas but is not actually functional right now--just a tech demo. Moreover, the project has existed for a long time, but appears to be developing very slowly. There isn't much developer enthusiasm for it and so it probably won't ever mature into a useable desktop environment.

In the meantime, Compiz has evolved greatly and is very powerful and functional.

So, I would say watch the online video about looking glass, but don't bother installing it.
