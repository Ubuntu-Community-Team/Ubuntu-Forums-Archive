### Post by Patb on 2008-03-21
Solarwind.  I had a very similar snag with copying menu items from my old Gnome menu to my Fluxbox menu.   

I got around it by running Gnome Alacarte and Fluxmenu at the same time and copy/pasting the launcher properties for each menu item.  Very tedious but I don't know of a better way.  I am not familiar with the Openbox menu system, so I'm not sure whether this is useful. :(

Good luck, Pat.
