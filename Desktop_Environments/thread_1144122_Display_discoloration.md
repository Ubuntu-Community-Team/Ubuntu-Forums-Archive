### Post by jonamerica on 2009-04-30
I'm running Ubuntu 9.04 for use as a MythTV box. I have it connected to my television with a DVI to HDMI cable. During boot-up the television looks fine. Initially into the desktop environment it looks fine as well. 

Here's the problem, at some point during the desktop initialization the screen suddenly changes from a nice clean black to black with a discolored, rainbow-ish haze. It's not much fun watching TV this way - people look green and purple.

If I switch away from the desktop and to a terminal the discoloration goes away.

How can I determine what is loading right at the moment the color shifts?

Has anyone else run into a similar issue?

---

### Post by jonamerica on 2009-05-09
Okay, I tried a number of things and what ended up solving it was changing the color depth to 16bit from 24bit. Just in case anyone else was having the same issue.
