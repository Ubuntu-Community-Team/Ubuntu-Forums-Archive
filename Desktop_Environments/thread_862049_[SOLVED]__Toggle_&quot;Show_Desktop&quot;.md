Just found another workaround...

In Windowmanager's settings, theres a tab for keyboard shortcuts. I just changes the shortcut "show desktop" from <ctrl>+<alt>+<d> to <Super>+<D>, which is exactly what I my intention was without writing a script or using another program!

That's very easy!
