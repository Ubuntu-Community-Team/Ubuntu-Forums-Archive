### Post by Barfoot on 2006-02-03
Is it possible to prevent the CD-ROM Disk icon from appearing on the desktop when media is mounted? I still want it to auto-mount, but I don't want that icon to appear on the desktop.

I was able to use the Removable Media tool to stop automatically browsing the new media, but there doesn't seem to be anything in there about preventing the icon from appearing.

Thanks in advance for any help.

---

### Post by krusbjorn on 2006-02-03
Applications->System Tools->Configuration Editor. Make your way down Apps->Nautilus->Desktop and uncheck whatever icons you want to disappear ("Volumes visible" would be the one to uncheck to make your volumes disappear from your desktop).
