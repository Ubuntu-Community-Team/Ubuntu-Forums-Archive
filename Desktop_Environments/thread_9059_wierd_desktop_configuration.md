### Post by burlap on 2004-12-23
Just grab your right panel with middle mouse button and drag it back to top of the screen. You can also set panel position to "top" in panel preferences. However clicking on the panel may be difficult (you will probably get launcher preferences instead of panel preferences), so you can go to Configuration editor (Applications -> System Tools) and there in apps -> panel -> profiles -> default you can tweak the settings and change panel orientation from "left" to "top".

I would also like the menus to change into icons when they are on a side panel...
