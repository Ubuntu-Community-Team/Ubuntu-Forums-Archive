### Post by Reteip on 2011-12-27
Maybe you have discovered this by now, but I have only just discovered it after finding no help from this thread.
So I thought I would offer help to others looking later at this thread.

To get the Red Shutdown (or restart) computer button / icon into the new Unity Launcher: 

1) Simply click on the Dash Home icon (top of the launcher) and type in the search box "Shutdown". 
The red shutdown icon will be in the search results.

2) Click and drag this icon over to the left-hand side until the launcher appears and drop the icon where you want it to sit.

That's it!
