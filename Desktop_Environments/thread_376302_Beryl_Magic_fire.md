### Post by robcolclough on 2007-03-04
Right click on the Beryl icon at the top (or bottom) of your screen and select "Beryl Settings Manager", from there choose "Visual Effects" from the icons at the top of the window and then in the sidepanel select animations.

The effect you are looking for is burn, you can apply it to various window actions (my favorite is (un)minimise.

Also further along the tabs still within the animations section is the actual "burn" properties where you can set its colour and speed etc

### Post by gavintlgold on 2007-03-04
All you have to do is go to the Beryl Settings Manager and then to the Visual Effects option and choose Animations.

Fool around with various actions and their animations. If you want to make the fire effect on a close, for example, you'd go to the close tab and choose fire for the animation.
