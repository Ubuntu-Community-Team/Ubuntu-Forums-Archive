### Post by pablolie on 2010-06-25
Every few months, to secure my music collection and the edits I may have made I transfer about 350GB worth of data (MP3, FLAC, AAC) from a local NTFS drive to a remote network drive. This always worked flawlessly in 8.04 LTS.

In 10.04 LTS, while doing this for the first time, the transfer just halted after 3 hours. No error message, no reason  why... it just stopped and that was that.

MY advice to LTS users henceforth will be to wait 6 months prior to adopting. 10.04 clearly has some gremlins to work out in several areas. :-(
