### Post by entropy1 on 2013-06-23
Press the windows (super) key on your keyboard; after the Cinnamon menu pops up, click on the gear icon at the left.  This will bring up the Cinnamon settings dialog box.  At the very bottom lower left, if you see the words "Switch to Advanced Mode" click on them (if it says "Switch to Normal Mode", leave it).  Then in the "Preferences" area, click on "Hot Corners".  Next, if you see "Icon visible" check boxes you can uncheck them.  Next, if any drop down menu says "Expo" or "Scale", click on it and change it to "Disabled".
