### Post by 1veedo on 2006-06-07
The cursor is the thing you type w/, right?  Looks something like this: |

Anyway, normally if you hold down the left arrow key it keeps going left.  Same thing for backspace and delete.  But w/ Ubuntu it's like it has a break, it just moves over one space and you have to hit it again to go another.

Is there a way to make it keep going as you hold the key down?  So if you hold delete it'll keep removing characters until you let up?  Or if you hold down, the cursor will continue to descend rows till it either gets to the bottom or you take your finger off.

---

### Post by tonyr on 2006-06-07
**System->Preferences->Keyboard->Repeat Keys** (checkbox)
