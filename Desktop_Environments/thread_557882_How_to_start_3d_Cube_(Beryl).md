### Post by PanopticChaos on 2007-09-23
You can't make it smaller - you just make it further away:
Go to Beryl Settings Manager->Desktop-> Rotate Cube->Behaviour-> General
There's an option called Zoom, the larger the Zoom, the smaller the cube appears when you're rotating it (either for flipping desktops, or doing the ctrl+click free rotation).
