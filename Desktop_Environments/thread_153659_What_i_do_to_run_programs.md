### Post by odrop on 2006-04-02
If you're lucky, hit ALT-F2 then in that command box, type in: kappfinder
hit enter, then kappfinder pops up.  From there, hit the Scan button and it will search your system for applications not in the KDE applications menu.

If that fails to find your two programs, you could hand create these entries inside the KDE Applications menu.  Just right-click on the menu button and enter the Menu Editor.  Thats kinda self explanitory.

Or you could always just run them from ALT-F2.

Theres probably more methods people could tell you also.
