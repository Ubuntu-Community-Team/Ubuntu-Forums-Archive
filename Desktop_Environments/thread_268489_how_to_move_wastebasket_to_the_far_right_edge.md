### Post by aysiu on 2006-09-30
Right-click on it and choose to unlock it from the panel. Then move it.

---

### Post by Buffalo Soldier on 2006-09-30
you have to right-click on the workspace first. uncheck the "Lock to Panel" option. then only you can move the wastebasket the the far right.

### Post by NetworkGuy on 2006-09-30
Right click on the wastebacket, click move, move it to the far right, click again to drop it.   You may run into problems with other things on the panel that are "locked".  You'll have to unlock these things if they prevent you from moving the wastecan where you want it.
