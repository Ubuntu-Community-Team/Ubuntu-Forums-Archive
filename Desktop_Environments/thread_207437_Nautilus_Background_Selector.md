### Post by Mikasuliel on 2006-07-01
This is what happens. I open nautilus, I go to Edit > Backgrounds and Emblems. I follow the instructions and drag the "swatch" onto the window. All that happens is that the "swatch" goes back to the Backgrounds and Emblems box. The background does not change in the window it was dragged to. I also tried it through choosing a Pattern, that did not work either. If anyone could help it would be greatly appreciated.

---

### Post by Mikasuliel on 2006-07-01
I found out the problem. Apparently you can only drag a background when its in Icon View. Even if you do select a background and then switch to List View the background disappears. So your choices are, Icon View with background or List View with no background.
