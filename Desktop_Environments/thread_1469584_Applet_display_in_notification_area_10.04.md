I was about to ask how to fix the applets in the notification area having just upgraded to 10.04, as I had a mix of the icons (some duplicated, some missing, even the icon to shutdown and restart had disappeared.

I happened to right click and select "Properties".  I then adjusted the pixel size of the bar and all the right icons were there with no duplicates.

Other having problems, try this!!!
