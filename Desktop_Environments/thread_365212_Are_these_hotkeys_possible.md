### Post by BitTorrentBuddha on 2007-02-19
There is an option to bind show desktop, it's resides either in beryl-manager, if you want the beryl style show desktop where the windows move out of the way, or under System > Preferences > Keyboard Shortcuts (menu names may be slightly off, I'm at school on windows right now,) if you want the standard show desktop, as with the button, in which windows are minimized. I don't believe the bottom bar can be hidden on button press in gnome, however, you can have it hide until you move your cursor down towards it. This is accomplished by choosing preferences after right clicking on an empty portion of the panel.
