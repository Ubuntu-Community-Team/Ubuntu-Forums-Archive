### Post by bug67 on 2014-05-31
[ClassicMenu Indicator]("http://www.florian-diesch.de/software/classicmenu-indicator/")

That'll put all your apps in Unity's menu bar.  There is also ["Drawers"]("http://www.webupd8.org/2012/08/add-stacks-to-unity-launcher-with.html").  This adds the ability to add multiple apps, web links, whatever you want to the unity launcher itself.  Or, you could just use a different DE altogether like xfce, mate or kde.  Choices, man!  :D
