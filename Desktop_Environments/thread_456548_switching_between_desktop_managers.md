### Post by Hex_Mandos on 2007-05-27
Click on 'Select Session' in the session manager, before logging into your account (when you're asked for your username and password). You can choose one for that session only, or choose it as a new default.

---

### Post by Patrick K on 2007-05-27
To jump from one to the other hit alt+ctrl+backspace to get to the login menu. On the lower right side is an options menu, select the desktop you want to use.
