### Post by KermitJr on 2005-12-01
You need to specify the amount of video RAM your card has.  256*1024=262144 KB

```
sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
```

accept all the defaults except the RAM and enter your amount in.

Log out and hit ctrl-alt-backspace to restart the X server.

Let me know what happens.
