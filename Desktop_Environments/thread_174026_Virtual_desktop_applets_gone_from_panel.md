### Post by kko1 on 2006-05-13
Hello!

Experimenting with the right mouse button on various parts of the desktop often lets you find options that you need. You should find the "virtual desktops applet" by right-clicking on an empty spot at the panel (or anywhere else on the panel, and choose "Panel Menu").

From there, choose "Add to Panel" -> "Applet", and see the options available. If I understood you correctly, what you want is the "Desktop Previewer & Pager".

If, for some reason, this doesn't work, something (for instance a lockup & hard boot) may have corrupted the relevant files, which would prevent the applet from working. This is fixable, though.

HTH, and please let us know if / when your problem is solved.
