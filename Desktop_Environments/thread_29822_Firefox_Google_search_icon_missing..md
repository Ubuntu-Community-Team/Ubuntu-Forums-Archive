### Post by jcooper on 2005-04-26
Have you changed your icon theme recently? Try adjusting your theme settings in "Themes" to see if other themes can change the icon. Changing thetheme back to your original setting may force nautilus to find the icon. If not, a helpful error message should be displayed, telling you what .png file its looking for.
