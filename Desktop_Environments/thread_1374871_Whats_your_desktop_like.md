### Post by N00b-un-2 on 2010-01-07
how to change what?  if you are referring to the icon on you applications menu, that would be found at
/usr/share/icons/your-icon-theme/whatever-size/places/start-here.png

The "easy" way to change that icon is to go open a root nautilus window (hit alt+f2) and enter
```
gksudo nautilus
```
followed by your password and then navigate to /usr/share/icons/
then open the folder for the theme your trying to modify.  Hit ctrl+f and search for 'start-here'.  edit ALL of the icons that pop up.  For pngs you may want to use GIMP and for svgs you may want to use Inkscape.

Of you could simply download another icon theme.
