### Post by duncans on 2011-10-31
The program must be presenting its visual data as part of the systray / indicator system. That is positioned at the bottom right in gnome-shell along with instant messaging etc.

There is an extension to pop all of the items from the bottom right onto the top panel,
available at webupd8.
