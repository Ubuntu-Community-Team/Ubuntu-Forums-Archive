### Post by cineboy on 2006-06-30
This looks normal to me.

_2 things_:
1. UDP (user datagram, protocol) is a broadcast, or undirected, internet/networking protocol. So these hits on your firewall are the result of a broadcast and not directed specifically at your computer. I see this all the time on my firewall.

2. If you want to know where they are coming from you can go to [http://www.dnsstuff.com/]("http://www.dnsstuff.com/") and copy in the source IP address (for example 207.69.188.185) into the **whois** option and see where it is coming from.

I hope this helps.
