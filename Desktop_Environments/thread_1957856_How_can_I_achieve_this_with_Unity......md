Firstly, I'd have to say it looks like BAD movies go on the TV... bwahahaha (OK trolling done)

In the 12.04 version of Unity, you can have two launchers, one on each display. They each show all open applications. You can't however move it to a different screen edge, so you're out of luck on having one at the right edge of the right screen. Apart from that, I can't see anything in your usage description that is patently impossible.
