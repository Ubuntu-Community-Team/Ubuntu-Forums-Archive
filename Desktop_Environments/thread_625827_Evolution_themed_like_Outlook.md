### Post by Nano Geek on 2007-11-28
To sort e-mail by date, all you have to do it click on the hedder right above the messages called **Date**. Click it once to sort from lowest to highest, and click it again to sort it from highest to lowest.

But I am pretty sure that there is no way to theme Evolution. You might want to try Thunderbird though as it does have that feature.
