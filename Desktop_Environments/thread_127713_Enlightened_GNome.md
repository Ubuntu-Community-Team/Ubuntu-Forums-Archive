### Post by karthik085 on 2006-02-09
I was able to speed it up :-). In ~/.gnome2/session.org, make every client a higher priority than gnome-wm. It worked. It speeds up just like before. Infact, it may be even faster, as I am using E17, instead of Metacity.

But, I want to know, how reliable this method is? Any other new methods are appreciated. Thanks.

P.S: Also, I started using prelink. That might have helped too in loading my startup applications.
