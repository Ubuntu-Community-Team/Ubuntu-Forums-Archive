If you want to use System Settings -> Power Management and can't, try completely removing powersaved with dpkg --purge powersaved libpowersave11. I hear apt-get won't remove leftover config files if the package itself has already been removed. Then log out and back in, or maybe reboot. I forgot where I read that but it was helpful.

Suggestions for Dolphin: Press F7, F11, and F4. In the Preferences, turn on "Show full path inside location bar" and (under "View Modes") "Expandable Folders" for the Details view. Also check everything that isn't checked in under "General" and uncheck everything that is :). Know you can click the ">" signs in the location bar. 

... matter of preference, of course.

Also find out how to remove the menu shadow in KDE 3 apps and tell me. :)
