### Post by BurntSushi on 2009-10-05
blur xc,

It looks like you're having some key binding collisions. Unfortunately, I haven't included any nice messages in PyTyle yet to indicate exactly which key binding is causing a conflict.

The best way to figure out which key is giving you trouble is commenting out the keybindings in your ~/config/pytyle/pytylerc file, and then uncommenting one at a time. (Or you could just eyeball the keybindings to see if you recognize any that are already bound in Ubuntu or some other program.)

You can of course change the keybindings in your pytylerc file.

Thanks for giving PyTyle a try! :)
