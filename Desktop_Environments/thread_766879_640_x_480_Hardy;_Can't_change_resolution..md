### Post by Afkpuz on 2008-06-13
Have you found the screens and graphics app?  It's hidden in hardy which I know is dumb.  You have to right click on applications and select edit menus.  Then, goto the "other" tab and check "screens and graphics".  That should let you manually set your resolution.  Have you been there yet?
