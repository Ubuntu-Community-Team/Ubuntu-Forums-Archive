### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-09-19
You must make the new shortcuts to the URLs wanted in the internet section first.  Right click-> Create new launcher, and there use "firefox <URL>" in the command box, as normal.

Now when you highlight or mouse over that new launcher you can click on the plus sign to add it to Favourites.
