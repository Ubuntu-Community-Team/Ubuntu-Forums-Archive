### Post by Sweet Spot on 2006-09-06
My iRiver IHP 120 (which isn't in production anymore) is UMS compliant, and the iAudio series players are too, which ARE still in production and are fine players. My iPod also is seen as a UMS device and I can drag and drop etc and file tree browse with [ROCKbox]("http://www.rockbox.org") firmware. 

Ogg, Mp3, FLAC, wavpack, musepack, aiff, wav etc.. all native to ROCKbox.  RB also does REAL gapless playback w/said formats + much, MUCH more.

### Post by puppy on 2006-09-06
I can't recommend the Creative devices (I have a Zen touch) because they don't integrate well with other programs - at this time the only application that can be used is Gnomad2 which, whilst it works perfectly well, is not the prettiest thing you have ever seen   :rolleyes:
