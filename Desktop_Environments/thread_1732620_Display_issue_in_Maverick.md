### Post by Bladecatcher on 2011-04-18
Okay I have solved this issue for the time being. I went to Preferences > Monitors and clicked 'No' when asked if I wanted to use the vendor's tool. Then I was able to adjust screen resolution and refresh rate. I just switched to a different setting and then back again to my original resolution and it cleared the problem for me.
