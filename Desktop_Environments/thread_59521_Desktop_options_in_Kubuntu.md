### Post by Thorsten on 2005-09-13
[QUOTE=OldGaf]One thing I thought I read, but can't remeber where, is that you can tell an application "where" to open in KDE 3.4 +[/QUOTE]

Click on the left-most icon in the title bar (it usually shows the application icon) and from there, click on **Advanced** -> **Special Window Settings...** -> **Geometry** -> **Desktop**.
