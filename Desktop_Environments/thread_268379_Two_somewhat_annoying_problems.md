### Post by aysiu on 2006-09-30
Freeze-ups usually occur for one of two reasons:

1. You need to have the appropriate proprietary graphics driver installed (NVidia, for example).

2. You don't have enough RAM and are using the wrong desktop environment (Gnome instead of XFCE, for example).
