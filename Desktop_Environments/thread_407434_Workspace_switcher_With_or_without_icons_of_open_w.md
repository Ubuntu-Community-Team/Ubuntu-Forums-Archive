### Post by mcduck on 2007-04-12
If the picture of your window inside the switcher applet is big enough to fit the icon, then it's shown. So the bigger the applet (and your panel) is, the more windows will get their icons too. If the applet is too small all windows shown in the applet become too small for icons.
