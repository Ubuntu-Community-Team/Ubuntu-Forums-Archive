### Post by f1dave on 2005-11-01
Are you running a console when this occurs? Consoles have a system bell/beep option that activates when you try and do certain things- for example, backspacing at a terminal when there's no characters to backspace over. You can turn the bell in konsole off by going settings -> bell -> none.
