### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-12-28
I think that 12.04 works the same way as 12.10 (which I am using now). Open the User Accounts utility. You will need to unlock it using your current password.

Then click the panel to the right of Password, where it shows 5 black dots. That will open a dialog box that will let you enter your current password and then enter a new password which will need to be confirmed by entering a second time. Then you click Change. And your password will have been changed. Now lock the utility.

So, yes, what you see is the place to change your password. Move the mouse over the 5 dots and it will become outlined as a clickable area.
