### Post by deadflowr on 2018-01-05
> Is it normal ?
Yes
The menu design changed.
To access the new menu click on the top icon in the left panel (the icon that looks like the Ubuntu logo)
It'll open an icon based menu with a search bar at top.
You can either run a quick search for apps, or look at the bottom of the menu screen and click on one of the icons to go to a specific area.
(By default it'll drop you into the Home section which will show your recent activities.
Other sections include Apps Files videos Photos and others)
You can also right click on icons to get a more advanced options; for example the top icon right click will allow you to open a menu section directly, so you could if you wanted to open your videos section.

Quick hint, press and hold down the Super (windows-icon button) key on you keyboard to get an overlay help screen of the default keyboard shortcuts.

Hope it helps a little.
