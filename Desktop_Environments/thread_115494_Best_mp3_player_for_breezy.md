### Post by zoiks on 2006-01-10
I use an iAudio (aka jetAudio) G3, with 2GB of storage, made by Cowon.  It's a very nice player generally, and is basically plug-n-play with linux/breezy.  Nautilus opens up a window automatically when it's plugged in to the USB port.  It is quite highly rated on CNET, and has an FM radio, voice recorder, line-in recorder, and .ogg support.

It uses a AA battery, and gets a helluva long time on one charge.  (They say 50 hrs, I think practically it's more  like 30-35.)

It takes a little time (~20 seconds) to start up when you turn it on (though not to mount to USB).

In short, I wouldn't have a problem recommending it.  I'm listening to mine right now.  :)
