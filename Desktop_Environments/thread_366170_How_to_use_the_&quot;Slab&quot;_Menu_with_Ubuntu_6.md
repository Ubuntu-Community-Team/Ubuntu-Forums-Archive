right click on gnome panel and click "add to panel"
scroll down in the list of applets and you will see two entries for "main menu". One of them is the default Ubuntu Gnome menu and the other is the Slab menu. Try out both and you will get the one you want. I think the slab menu does not have the Ubuntu logo in it. That should help differentiate.
