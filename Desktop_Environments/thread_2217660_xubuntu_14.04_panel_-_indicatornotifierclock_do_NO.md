### Post by s-p-constantine on 2014-04-18
Just completed a fresh install of 14.04 from 12.04. Loving Xfce vs. Unity!

I just use a Panel 0 at the bottom of my screen; I have an arrangement something like:
[INDENT]**Application Menu** | **Launchers** | **Window Buttons** | **Notification Area** , **Indicator Plugin**, **Clock**[/INDENT]

On 12.04, the Window Buttons item kept everything to the right of it 'justified' to the right of the screen.

Now, with 14.04, if there are no applications open, the Notification Area , Indicator Plugin, Clock are all next to the Launchers, and the panel looks odd.

Any ideas how I get the Notification Area , Indicator Plugin, Clock to stay 'justified' to the right?

Cheers - Steven.

---

### Post by Toz on 2014-04-18
Insert a separator between "Windows Buttons" and "Notification Area". Enable the "Expand" option for this separator.

---

### Post by s-p-constantine on 2014-04-20
Oh my, what a simple solution to a problem that was driving me CRAZY!

I have to say, I would NEVER have thought to click 'properties' on the separator, and even if I had, that 'Expand' would have solved the issue.

Many thanks!
