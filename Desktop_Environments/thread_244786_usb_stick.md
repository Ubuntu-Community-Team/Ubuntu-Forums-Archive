### Post by mgmiller on 2006-08-27
I've been using usb sticks for the last year or 2 back and forth between windows machine at work and Ubuntu at home.  All the usb sticks have lights.  They turn off when ejecting in windows, they do not turn off when ejecting from the desktop icon in Ubuntu.  It doesn't seem to hurt anything.  As long as you don't get any error messages when ejecting from the desktop icon in Ubuntu, once it's done, just pull out the stick.  Not worth losing sleep over.
