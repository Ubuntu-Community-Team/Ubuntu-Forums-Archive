### Post by gjoellee on 2009-08-08
Your theme issue looks like a bug, but your super sized icons may be fixed. It depends on where the super sized icons are. 

If they are super sized in Dolphin you can just use CTRL + MouseWheel do make the icons smaller or larger. You can use the slider function on the bottom of the window or probably also use the menu at the top of the window.

Oh, you may configure/tweak your Bespin theme by pressing Alt + F4 and run ```
bespin
```. You may also use the bespin command in the terminal. You should also check your theme in the KDE settings manager or control panel (whatever you call it). It is shown as "Settings" in the menu.
