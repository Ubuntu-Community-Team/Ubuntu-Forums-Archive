### Post by bitoiu on 2006-03-31
Hi,

If the clock is locked to the bar ( click left mouse button and see if "lock" is checked ) and closed to a corner you won't be able to change sides with another element. So when you try to move things one side to the other, see if they are "locked" , and the just right click the element you want to move , and choose move ( easier to adjust).
