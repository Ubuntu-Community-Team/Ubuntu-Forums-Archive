How do you remove the power icon in the top right hand corner? So that it's no longer displayed.

---

### Post by Mr. Shannon on 2011-05-20
If you are using Ubuntu (Classic) in other words GNOME or an older version of Ubuntu then you can right click the power icon and then uncheck **Lock To Panel** then right click again and choose **Remove From Panel**.  This may remove the clock and some insicators as well but you can get those back by right clicking on a blank spot on the pannel and choose **Add To Panel** and then add back the **Indicator Applet** and the **Clock**.

If you are using Unity then I am not sure whether you can edit the panel or not.
