### Post by Ekeluo on 2011-01-08
Simply right-click on the top panel, and select 'Add to Panel...'. Scroll down to 'Window List' (or type w l in the search box), select it, then click 'Add'. You may want to add other stuff or you can just click close. To adjust your new applet, right-click at its left most edge, then move, remove or (recommended) lock the applet. You can also change options concerning it. Take care
