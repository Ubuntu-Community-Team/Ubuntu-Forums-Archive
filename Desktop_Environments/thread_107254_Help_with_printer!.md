### Post by blair on 2005-12-22
It could be so many things. A lot of folks initially have trouble printing. You need to provide some more clues. I had major troubles with an HP and finally got it working. 

Recommend you search the forums for "cups", which is the print manager. Also, if you search for posts from me you will find multiple threads in which folks helped me troubleshoot my problems. You may learn some troubleshooting tactics this way.

Also, recommend you go to linuxprinting.org web site and search the database for your printer and printer driver. Then come back here and search the forums for your particular printer driver. You will find threads from other folks having problems and solving their problems when using the same printer driver.
