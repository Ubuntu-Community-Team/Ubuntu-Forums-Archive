### Post by Blazeix on 2006-07-15
Hi, I'm running dapper with fluxbox. I just installed openoffice.org, and when I start it up, I don't have a title bar! At the very top there is simply the file, edit, etc. menus. Oddly enough, openoffice writer and math have titlebars, but openoffice draw and calc do not. Can anybody help? Thanks.

---

### Post by Blazeix on 2006-07-15
I fixed this. It turns out that it was simply opening a little bit outside the screen. It was lined up so the title bar was not visible. I moved my taskbar to the top, and that forced the window down so I could grab the titlebar.
