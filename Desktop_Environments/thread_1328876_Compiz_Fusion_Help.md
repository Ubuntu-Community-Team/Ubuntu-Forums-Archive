### Post by Giant Speck on 2009-11-16
1.  Open CompizConfig Settings Manager

[[IMG]http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/th_Screenshot-9-2.png[/IMG]]("http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/Screenshot-9-2.png")

2.  Go to Effects

[[IMG]http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/th_Screenshot-1-6.png[/IMG]]("http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/Screenshot-1-6.png")

3.  Click on Animations

[[IMG]http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/th_Screenshot-2-4.png[/IMG]]("http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/Screenshot-2-4.png")

4.  Click on Minimize Animation

[[IMG]http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/th_Screenshot-3-2.png[/IMG]]("http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/Screenshot-3-2.png")

5. If there are extra effects in the Animation Selection area, select them.

[[IMG]http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/th_Screenshot-4-1.png[/IMG]]("http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/Screenshot-4-1.png")

6. And delete them.

[[IMG]http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/th_Screenshot-5-1.png[/IMG]]("http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/Screenshot-5-1.png")

7. Open the remaining effect by double-clicking it or clicking on it and clicking edit.

[[IMG]http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/th_Screenshot-6-2.png[/IMG]]("http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/Screenshot-6-2.png")

8.  Change the effect to Airplane and enter the remaining settings shown above.

[[IMG]http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/th_Screenshot-7-2.png[/IMG]]("http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/Screenshot-7-2.png")

9. Close CompizConfig Settings Manager.  The changes should take effect immediately.
[URL="http://i279.photobucket.com/albums/kk122/SpecKtacle/Screenshot-8-2.png"]
[/URL]
