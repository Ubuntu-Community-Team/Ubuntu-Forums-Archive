### Post by Skatertjah on 2009-07-18
I own a computer probably worse in performance than yours. I run an Ubuntu system with KDE, Gnome and LXDE installed. I run LXDE as default desktop envo. Except for a few missing features like a trash folder, I'm really satisfied with it's functionality and the performance. I'm not really a fan of xfce but that's personal opinion. Haven't really looked into that one though. For you I'd recommend installing the DE's and playing around with each of them. Compare them; swap between them and then make your decision based on* your* opinion. Just keep the other ones on your system in case you ever need a feature the other ones don't have, or in case you change your mind. See what you like best, this is the freedom Linux gives you, it's in your own hands:D.
