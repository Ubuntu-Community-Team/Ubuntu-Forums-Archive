### Post by Nomad64 on 2005-10-24
Easily. Go to System -> Preferences -> Screensaver. This will load up the configuration for the screensaver daemon that runs in the backround. Once that loads, click on the "Advanced" tab, and you will see a section in there about power saving.

You may also want to set your screensaver when you are in here. There are a lot of them....
