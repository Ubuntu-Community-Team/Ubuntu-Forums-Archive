Stoker580, that worked for me, thanks for posting it here. Here's the correct url: [http://web2linux.com/ubuntu-10-04-lucid-lynx-dark-theme-and-skype/](http://web2linux.com/ubuntu-10-04-lucid-lynx-dark-theme-and-skype/)

For anyone else, the fix is very simple. Open Skype > Options > General > Choose Style > Use Desktop Settings, then restart skype.
