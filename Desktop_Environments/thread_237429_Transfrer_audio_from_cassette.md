### Post by douga on 2006-08-16
> **teyster2 said:**
> I may have posted in wrong area, if so sorry.  Anyway I'd like to know how to transfer audion from my cassette to the computer to cd.  In windows, back when, I would use musicmatch jukebox.  Can anyone help here.  Thanks.

There's an application called Gramofile that can help. You'll just have to connect your cassette deck to your sound card input (line in, I believe) and start Gramofile. I've done this with LPs and it's worked pretty well.

Gramofile can be found at [http://www.opensourcepartners.nl/~costar/gramofile/](http://www.opensourcepartners.nl/~costar/gramofile/)

It's also in one of the repositories (that is, I could find it by doing a "sudo apt-cache search gramofile").

Hope this helps.
