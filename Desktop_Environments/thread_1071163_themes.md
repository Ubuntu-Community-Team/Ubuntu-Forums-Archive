### Post by MarblePanther on 2009-02-15
If you are using GNOME, go to [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) and download the theme file.  It will usually be a .tar file.

Right click on your desktop and choose change background
switch to the themes tab
Now just drag and drop the .tar theme file you downloaded onto the themes window
