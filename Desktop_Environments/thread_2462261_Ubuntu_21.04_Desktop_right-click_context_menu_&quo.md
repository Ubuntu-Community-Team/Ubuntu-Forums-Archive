### Post by Dennis N on 2021-05-17
Rt. Click on Desktop > "Settings" configures the 'Desktop Icons NG' Gnome Shell Extension. That's new expected behavior.
Rt. Click on Desktop > "Display Settings" will open the general "Settings" dialog, preset on Display - but from there, choose any other item on the left.
You can also reach the general settings from Overview Screen > Search for "Settings"
