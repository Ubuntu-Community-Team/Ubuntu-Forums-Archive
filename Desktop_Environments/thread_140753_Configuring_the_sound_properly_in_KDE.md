### Post by Xian on 2006-03-07
Basically you just want to goto the KDE Contol Center and navigate to the sound module. Once there look for the option to disable the sound server. This will nix arts which is a good thing unless you enjoy hearing all those extremely annoying system beeps, knocks, clicks, and whirrly-dings.
