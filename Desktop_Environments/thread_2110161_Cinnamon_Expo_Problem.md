### Post by Viper00 on 2013-01-31
Hmm if you have workspaces you should have all of the packages. In Cinnamon workspaces work differently then Gnome-shell.  When in expo view there will be a button on the far right (it will have a "+" on it), just click it to add more workspaces.  To close one just hover over a workspace and click on the "X" on the top right of the workspace.  You can also change expo view to be viewed as a grid in Cinnamon Settings under Workspaces.

Sorry if I'm not being clear enough, It's kind of hard to explain.  :p
