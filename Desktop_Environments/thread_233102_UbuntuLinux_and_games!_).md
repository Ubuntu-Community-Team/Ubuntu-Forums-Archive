### Post by petersjm on 2006-08-09
This is not a flame - in fact, it's far from any negative comment at all! :D

(firstly, I didn't know where to post this, so put it here - if it's in the wrong forum, I'm happy for a mod to (re)move it)

I just wanted to say, I've no idea why people complain about game support in Linux (let's say Ubuntu, as it is - after all - a Ubuntu forum!). I mean, you've got **[Cube](http://sourceforge.net/projects/cube/)**, a (open source) kind of Doom shoot 'em up. And, of course, there's **[TuxPuck](http://sourceforge.net/projects/tuxpuck)**, a fantastic shufflepuck game that I can't get enough of (even though I haven't won a single game! LOL). You've got **[Barrage](http://lgames.sourceforge.net/index.php?project=Barrage)**, a pretty violent if somewhat cheesy (but in a good way!) point-and-shoot-the-tanks-before-they-drive-off-the-screen game with hilarious sound effects! And let's not forget **KAsteroids** (sorry, can't find a link now, but it's in the repos) - an Asteroids clone for KDE that runs perfectly in Gnome (if you don't know Asteroids, ask your dad!). And then you've also got all the pre-installed solitare and mahjongg games to keep you entertained for months!

What more do people want? I'm having a whale of a time with the aforementioned games!

Rant (if you want to call it that) over :D
