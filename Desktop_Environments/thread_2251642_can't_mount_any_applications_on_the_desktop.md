### Post by CantankRus on 2014-11-05
Copy the application launcher from **/usr/share/applications** to your desktop.

PS The launcher is meant for frequently used apps.... for others just press the Super key and search.
You can also try kupfer which I use and like.
> Kupfer is a summoner/launcher in the style of Quicksilver or GNOME Do.

### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-11-05
I have 31 icons on my launcher. Mostly icons for partitions on two disks.

The launcher does not get over crowded because the icons stack up and as we move the pointer to the bottom of the launcher the icons that are hidden from view flow upwards and are revealed.

We can also launch applications from the Dash which acts like a slide out desktop that can hold both application icons and document icons. And unlike the actual desktop which is limited by screen size, the Dash is scrollable and searchable.

Just so you know.
