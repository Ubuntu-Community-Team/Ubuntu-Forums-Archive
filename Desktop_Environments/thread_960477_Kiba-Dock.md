### Post by gjoellee on 2008-10-27
Go to System->Preferences->Sessions. Then Click on "Add". Follow the list below:

Name: AWN
Command: avant-window-navigator
Comment:

then click OK.

Now lets find Kiba Dock by scrolling up and down. When you find "kiba-dock" remove it.
