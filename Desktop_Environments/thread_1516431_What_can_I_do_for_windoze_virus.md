### Post by howefield on 2010-06-23
Use Startup Disk Creator to make a bootable USB install of Ubuntu, install an anti virus product to it, eg Bitdefender and boot with it on your infected computer and scan the windows drive.,

Keep the flash handy, you'll most likely not be able to stop yourself from installing malware to your computer again.
