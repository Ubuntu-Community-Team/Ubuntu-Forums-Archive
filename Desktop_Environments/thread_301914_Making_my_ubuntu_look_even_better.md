### Post by urukrama on 2006-11-17
1. You can find themes at gnome-look.org

2. To make your panels (taskbars) transparent (or change some other settings): right click on them, choose preference and play around with it. For transparency, click the Background tap, tick 'Solid colour' and slide the bar until it is as transparent as you want it to be.

3. For Terminal transparency, open a terminal, go to Edit > Profiles > Effects and select transparent background.

4. Some of the things on those pictures are desklets. You can get them by installing gdesklets.

See also [http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1189203](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1189203)
