### Post by mlind on 2006-07-28
System monitor on panel is actually quite useful feature, you know when something is hogging your CPU.
Right click panel at the top of the screen, choose "Add to panel" and select "System Monitor". 

You can get it to display internet traffic too, right click on the "System Monitor" -applet you added before, select "Preferences" and tick "Network" checkbox.
