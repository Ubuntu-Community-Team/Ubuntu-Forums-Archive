### Post by benerivo on 2008-08-16
Yes you are missing something. After a desktop right click > add widgets, you'll have a window giving a small list of plasmoids to add (eg. clock, application launcher, system tray). At the bottom of this window, there should be a button called 'Install New Widgets'. For me, this is currently useless as it only goes on to list one widget (piconet).

I don't know why you might be missing this 'Install New Widgets' button.
