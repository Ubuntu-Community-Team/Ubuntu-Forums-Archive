### Post by m_duck on 2009-02-11
To edit various fonts and colours, download "gnome-color-chooser"```
sudo apt-get install gnome-color-chooser
```It ends up in System -> Preferences -> GNOME Color Chooser. Click the panel tab and there you can set the font and colour for your panel and panel text. There are also a lot of other options for changing the font/colour of other parts of the DE.
