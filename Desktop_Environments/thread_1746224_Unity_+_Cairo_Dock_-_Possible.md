I'm running Natty in VirtualBox just to see what all the fuss about Unity was. I tried to install Cairo Dock and it was a bit of a mess at first try, so said 'forget it'.

I then installed Docky from Ubuntu Tweak. Docky seems to work seemlessly on Unity. 

That will give some of the Cairo desklet equivalents, such as the clock.

Also, you can configure the Launcher to auto hide thru the Compiz Settings Manager so that you don't see both docks on your screen unless you put the cursor on the left margin.

For what it's worth.
