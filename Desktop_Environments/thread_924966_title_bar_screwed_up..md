### Post by oygle on 2008-09-20
Try and right click on one of the icons, then use the 'move' to move it to the right. You may need to unlock it first though (uncheck the 'Lock to panel'). Once everything is in place again, then use the 'Lock to Panel'.

### Post by sayakb on 2008-09-20
You could also right click and Delete the panel. Now right click on some other panel and select New Panel. To the new panel, right click->Add to panel and add: Menu bar, Clock, Mixer (Volume Control), Quit, Notification area and whatever else you need. Move them and if you are happy with them, right click on them and click "Lock to Panel"
