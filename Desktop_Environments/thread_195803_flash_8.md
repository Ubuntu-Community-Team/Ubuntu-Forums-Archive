### Post by quichos on 2006-06-13
There is a workaround:
1/Install Wine
see [http://wiki.ubuntu.com/Wine]("http://wiki.ubuntu.com/Wine")

2/Then download a version of Portable Firefox for windows here:
[http://portableapps.com/apps/internet/browsers/portable_firefox]("http://portableapps.com/apps/internet/browsers/portable_firefox")
(Use the "Zip Download" link under the big Download button)

3/extract the downloaded file on your hard drive an simply run:
wine PortableFirefox.exe

When you access a site  with flash8, just accept the Flash Plugin installation in firefox.
