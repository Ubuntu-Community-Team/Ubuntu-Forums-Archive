Having upgraded to 20.04 I have found problems with LibreOffice Writer that make it unusable. Cut and Paste doesn't work and Ctrl-H to find and replace has gone. It has been said that it is caused by Mutter and that I should replace it with Wayland. How might I do this?

### Post by vitalio on 2020-04-29
1. Log out
2. click on your user name
3. in the bottom right corner click on a settings wheel
4. choose Ubuntu on Wayland
5. enter your password and hit enter

Note: If you have nVidia drivers you probably wont have a choice to run on Wayland
