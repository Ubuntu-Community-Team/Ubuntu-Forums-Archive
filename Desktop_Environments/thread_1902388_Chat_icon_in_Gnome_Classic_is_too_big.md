### Post by ubix on 2011-12-30
You can remove only the entire "*_Shut Down / Log Out..._, ...*" menu. Depending on your other setups this may become a problem in the future, for instance how are you going to come to the initial Log In screen, if you are automatically logged in, etc. To remove it, _**<Alt>+Rght-click** the chat icon_, and select **Remove From Panel**.
