### Post by kperkins on 2005-11-22
Are you selecting paper size on your printer as #10 envelope, when printing the envelope?
(I always make a new document for my envelopes, and print them separately from the letters/what-have-you).
You can, also, adjust the position of your sender box in the Envelope dialog (as well as the envelope size), under the format tab--I set my left margin more than the default, because my HP 952C printer cuts it off at the default, and I don't have any problems with it going back to the defaults.
