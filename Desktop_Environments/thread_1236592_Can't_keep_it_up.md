### Post by TetonsGulf on 2009-08-10
@AmerNewbieInDE

Get to a Command line, or simply press ALT+F2 and type
```
ccsm
```

It will open your CompizConfig Settings Manager. From there click on the Desktop Cube. Select the Behaviour tab and make sure the checkbox for Inside Cube is not checked.

### Post by TetonsGulf on 2009-08-10
> **Sticks_uk said:**
> on a side note how do you change the "new" background when u have the cube held out there ?

Thats in those settings as well... it's called Skydome

Get to a Command line, or simply press ALT+F2 and type

```
ccsm
```

It will open your CompizConfig Settings Manager. From there click on the Desktop Cube. Select the Appearance tab, then the Skydome menu. Make sure the checkbox for Skydome is selected and you can then choose a new image at the Skydome image bar.

You may find further help here:

[http://wiki.compiz-fusion.org/CCSM](http://wiki.compiz-fusion.org/CCSM)
