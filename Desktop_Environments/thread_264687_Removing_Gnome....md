### Post by Qrk on 2006-09-25
There isn't any easy way to do that, though Edgy will fix many of the problems. I'm afraid you'll have to use synaptic to see the list of installed packages and delete the ones you don't want one by one. Once you've done that, try installing deborphan from synaptic to get rid of the various libraries that you may have missed.
