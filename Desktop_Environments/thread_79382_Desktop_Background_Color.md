I have noticed that as soon as you log into ubuntu, while the splash screen is being displayed, the background color is brown. Is there a way to change this to another color?

### Post by drummer on 2005-10-20
Go to 'System > Administration > Login Screen Setup' and go to the 'Standard Greeter' tab. Then change the colour by clicking the box of colour in the bottom right of the window, labelled 'background colour'.
