### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2014-06-03
Just put the launcher icons on the dock, this is what it is for. If you put the launcher on the desktop not only is it cluttering and ugly :) it is not actually that efficient to access since you would have to minimize your open windows to get to the desktop. With Unity you can either access the apps with the dock or with a press of the super key to reveal the dash to show recently used applications.  A lot of work has gone into its design, please give it a chance instead of immediately trying to do things in the inferior XP way by scattering launchers randomly all over the places. :)
