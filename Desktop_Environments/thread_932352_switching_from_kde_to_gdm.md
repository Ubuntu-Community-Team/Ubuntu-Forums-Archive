### Post by Pro-reason on 2008-09-28
KDE to GDM?  That's confusing.

There's KDE versus GNOME (desktop environments)

There's KDM versus GDM (log-in managers).

What exactly are you trying to do?  From either log-in manager you can start either a KDE or a GNOME session; just choose it from the menu.

To switch between log-in managers, just open Synaptic, mark one for installation and one for removal, and click Apply.
