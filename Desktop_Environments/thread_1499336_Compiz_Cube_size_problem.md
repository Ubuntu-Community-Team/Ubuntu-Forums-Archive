### Post by egeezer on 2010-06-01
I'm running 10.04 with Compiz up and active, but my desktop cube is the full desktop size.  As it rotates, the skydome background I've set appears just around the edges of the cube.  I'm embarrassed to have to ask this, but how do I make the cube small enough to stand out within a wider background?
Thanks in advance for any help you all can offer!

---

### Post by bigsmitty64 on 2010-06-01
system/preferences/compizconfig settings manager/

in that click on desktop/rotate cube. in the general tab at the bottom is a setting for zooming the cube! :)

---

### Post by egeezer on 2010-06-01
Yep, that did it, all right!  Thank you, bigsmitty64 - it's exactly what I was after!
