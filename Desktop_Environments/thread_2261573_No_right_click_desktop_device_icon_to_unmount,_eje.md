### Post by Dennis N on 2015-01-24
Probably the quickest way to unmount is double-click on a device icon to open it (or right-click>open), then use the side pane's device entry to eject. When ejected, the window will close by itself and you can remove the device. Other mounted devices can be removed as well before you eject the device you opened. I think soon the eject option will be added to the right click menu.
