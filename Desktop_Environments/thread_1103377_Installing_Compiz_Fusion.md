### Post by Sojurner on 2009-03-23
sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager

then it will be in System --> Prefs -> Compiz (or CCSM)

Right click on ur desktop, click on the Desktop Prefs option, then click on the Visual effects tab. Select "Extra", then open CCSM and ur on ur way.

You will know u have everything working right when your windows become "Wobbly" like jello.

### Post by Sarai the Geek on 2009-03-23
I couldn't get compiz fusion to work until I installed a piece of software called "Compiz Fusion Icon" - it's in "Add/Remove Software". Then I ran that and right clicked on the icon in the status panel and changed my window manager from metacity to compiz. Now it works perfectly, wobbly windows and all!
Hope this helps-
