### Post by kurt18947 on 2013-06-23
Looking on Newegg probably isn't going to help, they know Windows and OSX and that's about it.  Here might be a place to start:

[http://www3.sane-project.org/sane-supported-devices.html](http://www3.sane-project.org/sane-supported-devices.html)

A common shortcoming with community documentation is keeping lists such as this up to date.  This one is supposedly updated daily.  You could also check the manufacturer's web site for scanner O.S. support.  If the device's manufacturer is even aware that Linux is a desktop O.S. they're better than many :).
