### Post by 2F4U on 2012-08-26
Not sure what you want to compare here, since XFCE is a desktop environment while Fuduntu is a Linux distribution and not even based on Ubuntu but on Fedora. If you stay within the Ubuntu family, Lubuntu with LXDE is the lightest.

When you compare on a distribution basis, Debian or Arch with XFCE are still lighter than, for example, Lubuntu with LXDE. I've setup Debian and Arch with XFCE (32 bit) and such a setup will use around 80 MB of RAM on an empty desktop.
