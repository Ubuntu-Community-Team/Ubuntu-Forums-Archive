### Post by brainkilla on 2005-12-17
You could try the following: go to System > Preferences > Removable drives and media and, under Cameras, replace gthumb entry with the exact path wine asigned to your Picasa installation; you can find it if you right-click on Picasa icon and see what the command entry says. Worked for me with Digikam...
