### Post by sicofante on 2007-01-13
Today I installed Ubuntu in an old computer. It runs fine but it had just a 1024x768 screen and I have realised how much screen real estate is wasted all over the place in Gnome. I know, I know, it's for the sake of readability, clarity, etc. but my point is Gnome in general goes a bit too far with this, while Evolution in particular is simply insane. That switcher takes so much useless space it's crazy. Even if you use just icons they have a huge margin around them for no good reason I can think of. Being almost an Outlook clone, I'm just amazed that I can see almost twice as much information at a glance in Outlook than in Evolution. Why did they do it???

I wish there was a way to reduce that waste of screen space in Gnome. :(
