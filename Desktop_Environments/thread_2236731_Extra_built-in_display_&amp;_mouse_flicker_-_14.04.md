### Post by gsoundsgood on 2014-07-29
I saw this happening on an Asus laptop. Simply turning off the new (effectively non-existant) "built-in display" in the settings page (the one you have opened in your screenshot) solved the mouse flickring issue. Don't know what caused it, but the problem disappeared.
