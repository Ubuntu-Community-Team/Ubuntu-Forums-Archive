### Post by colo on 2006-03-15
The information provided on this thread: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=140852](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=140852) should help you getting your box fixed again. Just prowse through /etc/passwd (by using less) to find out your username, and set the password again by issueing "passwd YOURUSERNAMEHERE" at the root-prompt.
