 No so much that its not surported, but that its frozen.

The packages will stay largly the same for 6 months, then will all resync with the most up to date version. Rinse and repeate. This saves alot of upgrade hassle for those who manage alot of computers.

You can compile packages yourself if you /need/ an upgraded version, or run breezy (in general a bad idea atm)
