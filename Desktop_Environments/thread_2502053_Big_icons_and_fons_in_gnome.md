### Post by grahammechanical on 2024-10-31
In System Settings>Screen Display what resolution is the screen set at? And what scale is being used - 100% or 200%? Compare the result with those in Windows.

Do not expect to reduce the size of the icons in Show Apps pages. This also applies to icons in the Utilities folder. I do not see a Fonts tab in System Settings>Appearance. You might be able to do adjust system fonts size if you install a utility called Gnome Tweaks. It used to be called Gnome Tweaks Tool.

The people who develop Linux into an operating system and a distribution are not seeking to imitate Microsoft. They have their own ideas and are pursuing their own goals. They have absolutely no interest in making Linux an imitation Windows. They often receive no help from hardware manufacturers so that Linux can make better use of the hardware.
