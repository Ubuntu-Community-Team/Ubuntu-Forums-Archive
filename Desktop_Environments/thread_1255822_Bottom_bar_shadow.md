### Post by Giant Speck on 2009-09-01
Are you trying to do this with Compiz or Emerald?

If so, go into the Emerald Theme Manager (System > Preferences > Emerald Theme Manager).  When it comes up, click on the "Edit Theme" tab and then on the "Frame/Shadows" tab.

Under Shadows, make sure that x-offset and y-offset are set to 0 and increase the radius of the shadow.
