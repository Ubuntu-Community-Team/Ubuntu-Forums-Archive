### Post by Mustard on 2006-02-26
You haven't mentioned whether you have tried running...

```
sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg
```

This should allow you to fiddle around with what screen resolutions will be set up on xorg.conf.

If you want to look over a more advanced guide on configuring your xorg.conf then try this link...

[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=83973](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=83973)

What might help to troubleshoot the problem is the output of your Xorg.0.log in your /var/log folder.
