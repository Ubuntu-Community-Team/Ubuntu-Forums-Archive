Hi all, I've noticed something Jaunty does different from Intrepid. On Intrepid, with the "Show Desktop" button in the lower left corner or <ctrl><alt><d>, you can click it, open a folder on your desktop and click it again and all the minimized windows will open again.

On Jaunty however, if you minimize all, open a folder and click it again, it will instead minimize the folder you just opened. Click it again and only that one folder maximizes. I guess Jaunty shifts the focus to the folder you just opened whereas Intrepid maintained focus on all windows.

Does that make sense? Is there anyway to make Jaunty behave like how Intrepid did?
