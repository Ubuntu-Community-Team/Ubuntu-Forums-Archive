### Post by Cordate on 2006-07-26
Are you talking about having a brown login screen where you enter your username and password, or a blank brown screen in between the login and the desktop wallpaper?

Actually, either one can be changed in the options under System/ Administration/ Login Window. One the Local tab, you can pick your login theme by putting a dot in the radio button, and underneath the theme chooser window, there's a color swatch for the background color. Click on it to choose a new one. 

Also, under your desktop wallpaper, there is a background color that is used when the desktop wallpaper image isn't being shown. To set this, right click on your desktop and choose Change Desktop Background. There's a place at the bottom of the Preference box to choose those colors too. 

Have fun!
