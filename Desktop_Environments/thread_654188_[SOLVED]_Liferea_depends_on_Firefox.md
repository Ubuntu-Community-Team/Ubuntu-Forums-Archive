### Post by Linteg on 2007-12-31
Liferea uses (or has an option to use) gecko, which is included with Firefox, why Ubuntu/Fedora and some other distros can't replace it with Xulrunner (basically, a small library with gecko) is beyond my belief, other distros such as (the much smaller) ArchLinux has managed so why can't the big ones... Xulrunner is made for exactly these cases, where you want the gecko rendering engine, but not Firefox.
