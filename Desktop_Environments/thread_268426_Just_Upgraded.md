### Post by Mark76 on 2006-09-30
Yesterday I was informed (on another forum) that it was possible to upgrade from 5.10 to 6.06 simply by downloading it from the Ubuntu site (or a mirror, I guess).  It took over two hours, and I had to add some stuff manually (Automatix was still the BB version), but so far no major issues: though Opera crashed a couple of times, but I've come to expect that and, besides, at least it always gives you the option of returning to the page(s) you were on before it went down.

Incidentally the new build of Automatix allows installation of gaim 2.0.0beta1 which supports file sharing between AIM accounts (at long last :) ) 

I've noticed that Dappper seems to load faster (if "load" is the right word) on start up than Breezy did.  Which is nice. :-D
