### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2013-11-28
No question is stupid.  I of all people should know that.  The LXPanel (unless you have moved it) is the strip at the bottom of your screen that has the main menu button, quick launch icons, the task bar windows, notification icons, and your shut down icon.  If you right click on any open space you can get to panel settings.
