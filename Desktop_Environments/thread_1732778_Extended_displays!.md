### Post by Maheriano on 2011-04-19
Under the Ubuntu menu it'll give you Administration and Preferences. Somewhere in there it'll have an option for Hardware drivers, click that and it'll scan your system for any hardware you have and associated drivers you're not using. If it finds a driver, click it, install it, done. It'll likely find an ATI driver for you.
