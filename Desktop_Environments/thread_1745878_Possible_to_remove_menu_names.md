### Post by Krytarik on 2011-05-02
To remove the names of the submenus:
- right-click at the "Main Menu" icon
- choose "Edit Menus"
- select the respective submenu item
- click at "Properties"
- replace the name by a single Space
- click at "Close"

To enable the icons for the system items follow this guide:
[http://skweez.net/menu-icons-in-ubuntu-10-04-lucid/](http://skweez.net/menu-icons-in-ubuntu-10-04-lucid/)

But afaik you can't remove the names of the system items, which renders the first mentioned one senseless.
