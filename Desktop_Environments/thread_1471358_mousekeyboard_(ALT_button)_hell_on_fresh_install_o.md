Hello. I've just done a fresh install of 10.04. Everything was fine to begin with, but after downloading and using the Sugar Emulator I am having issues with the keyboard/mouse. (Not sure Sugar caused it, but it started immediately after closing the application.)

Now when I click and drag across a window (ie to select text in OO or Firefox) the windows moves. :/

To cut a long story short, Ubuntu is acting as if my ALT key is held down. If I hold the button down it acts almost normally, but things like firefox are still not very happy about it... and nor am I :,(

I have rebooted a couple of times, ensured the button isn't physically stuck, and pulled out a good many hairs; all to no avail.

### Post by ifland on 2010-09-14
I had the same problem and I managed to fix it by going to System -> Preferences -> Windows and selecting "Alt" as the movement key. (To actually click on the window without moving it, I held the Ctrl key and clicked.)
