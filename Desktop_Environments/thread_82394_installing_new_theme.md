### Post by cydizen on 2005-10-26
Worked totally fine for me! Here is what I did:

Saved the file (left it in it's .tar.gz format)

System -> Preferences -> Theme

Then on the right side of the theme manager click Install Theme.....

Then click browse and get to the location you saved it to...

Click on it, then click install.  Let me know if this doesn't work and I can take you through the CL way.
