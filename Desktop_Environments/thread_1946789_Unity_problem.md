### Post by bonzini on 2012-03-26
In 12.04 Unity 2-d, full-screen seems to be up to the edge of the launcher.  You can "push the launcher out of the way" by moving a window in its direction, but the window manager doesn't do that for you.

As for whether the side or the bottom is a better place, count me in with the side.  Screens these days are too short to have horizontal menus, button ribbons etc.

Whatever reasons justified putting task bars / launchers on the bottom of the screen back when OS/2 was being designed don't apply now.  From what I can see of colleagues' computers running Win 7 and using Office 2010 to edit documents, between ribbon and the fat new task bar they get about 10 lines of document visible, and two great gray washes of vertical uselessness to the left and right of the document page.
