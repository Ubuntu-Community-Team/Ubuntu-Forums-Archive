On older versions there was no extra screen, after a mouse movement or keypress the Dektop came back immediately.

### Post by Xian on 2018-06-24

[https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/672/disable-screen-shield/](https://extensions.gnome.org/extension/672/disable-screen-shield/)
