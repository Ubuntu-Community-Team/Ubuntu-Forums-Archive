### Post by benerivo on 2009-10-03
The box they were in was one of many desktop plasmoids available to add to the desktop. If you right click anywhere on the desktop, then you can select 'Add Widget' (i think this is what it says). In the new window that opens you can double click on widgets to add them to the desktop. The one you want is called 'Folder View'.
