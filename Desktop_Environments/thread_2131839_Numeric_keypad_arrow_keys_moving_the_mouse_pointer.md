### Post by bogan on 2013-04-03
Hi!, **joelsf.**

In System Settings>System>Universal Access>Pointing and Clicking, there is a 'Mouse Keys' On/Off switch: "Control the pointer using the keybad", Set it to 'Off'.

But from your Post there is likely something much more than that wrong with your Keypad. Are the other keyboard keys and Hot keys working correctly.??
