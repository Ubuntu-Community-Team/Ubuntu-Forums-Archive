### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-07-02
The nouveau driver is an open source driver and may not preform as well as the proprietary driver . Other than the Nvidia current installed from additional drivers there is a PPA that will provide the latest driver available driver for Ubuntu .

The message that the the driver is installed but not activated is a bug . If you are able to run unity 3D in Ubuntu 11.04 the driver is activated . If you choose to try the PPA see the link . The choppy video is likely due to the driver. 
[http://www.webupd8.org/2010/06/how-to-install-nvidia-25635-display.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2010/06/how-to-install-nvidia-25635-display.html)
