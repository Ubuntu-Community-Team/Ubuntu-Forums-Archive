### Post by ankspo71 on 2011-03-22
Hi,
I am using the Unity desktop in Ubuntu 11.04 (alpha) and I was able to reproduce this double titlebar behavior for maximized windows, but I can correct the problem by doing the following:

Click on the titlebar of your open window and drag it completely to the top of your screen.

Now that I have dragged the window to the very top of the screen I am unable to reproduce this problem. I suppose that's a good thing. :)

Hope this helps your version of Unity too.

this is bug #637827. It is currently being fixed.
as a work around you can use chromium and in the preferences go to "personal stuff/appearance". In there select "Use system title bar and borders" and you will get only one set of buttons and titles.
