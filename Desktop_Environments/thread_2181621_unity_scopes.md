### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-10-18
I think that I have found a way to do this. Open the Dash - type your location (in my case London) and use filter results to filter by weather and you should get the weather for the entered location.

It is a bit more tricky if you live in a city with the name of a city in the US, such as Birmingham. In this case type Birmingham United Kingdom. We most definitely need to filter results by Weather for the location we are looking for.

I can tell you that the weather for Vancouver is looking sunny all week.
