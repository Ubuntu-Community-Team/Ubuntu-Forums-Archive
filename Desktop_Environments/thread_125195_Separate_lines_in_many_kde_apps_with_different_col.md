### Post by CyberAngel on 2006-02-03
OK!
I found the solution :)

It was totaly from good luck that I found it :)

As I was playing with Krusader`s Options I found that it had to configure specifically this one parameter for this program or to use KDE Defaults. So I went to kcontrol and I searched the Colors 'til I find it :)

That color is configured from the "Alternate Background in Lists" in kcontrol -> Appearance & Themes -> Colors

Thanks Anyhow :)
