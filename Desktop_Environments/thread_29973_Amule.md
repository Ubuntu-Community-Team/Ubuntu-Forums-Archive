### Post by SamH on 2005-04-26
You need to set Nautilus to "View hidden files".  The . (dot) in the name of the .amule folder indicates it is a hidden file.  I forgot which menu has the command (View?) but it isn't hard to find.  Once you've done that, you should find several formerly hidden directories.

You can also create a link to the Incoming directory in your Home directory.  Just go to /home/user/.amule and right-click on the Incoming folder, click "Make link", right-click on the link, click "Cut", go back to /home/user and right click in an empty space and click "Paste".
