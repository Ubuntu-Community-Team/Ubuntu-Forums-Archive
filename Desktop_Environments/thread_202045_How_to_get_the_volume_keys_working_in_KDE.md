### Post by boyx on 2006-06-23
After hours of research, I figured out a simple way...posting it for the benefit of others ... Go to KDE menu > system settings > regional&accessibility > keyboard layout. Here check enable keyboard layouts and select your keyboard. There are a lot of layouts like logitech, compaq, dell etc. i chose dell inspiron 8xxx and whoa my volume keys are working :)
