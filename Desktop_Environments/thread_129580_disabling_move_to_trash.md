### Post by tedius on 2006-02-14
Is there a way in Nautilus to disable move to trashcan for given drives.

I would like it so that when I delete something from my USB Pen Drive, or MP3 player that they are not moved to .trash and are inserted deleted permanently.  But I would like it to work as normal on the other drives.

### Post by kaamos on 2006-02-14

### Post by Tiede on 2006-02-14
In Nautilus, go to Edit->Preferences->Behavior (Traduction might not be exact,as I am reading from french-anyway, the second tab) and clik on the last checkbox to enable it.
Then everytime you want to delete something, you can just shift+delete or use right-click as mentioned above!
