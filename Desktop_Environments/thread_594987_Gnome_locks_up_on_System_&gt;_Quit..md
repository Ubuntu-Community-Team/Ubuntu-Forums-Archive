Hey all,

Well, yesterday was a big day for my main Linux box. I installed a lot of stuff (Compiz, etc.) and after much tweaking, everything is up and stable. Except for one thing.

### Post by MicroChris on 2007-10-28
Fixed.

I disabled gnome-power-manager on startup. Don't turn that off kids!
