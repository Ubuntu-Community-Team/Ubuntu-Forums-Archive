### Post by xyzzyman on 2012-01-20
When using input from the PC you need to set the TV to not overscan, that's why parts were cut off at 1920x1080 and most likely on lower resolutions. Since you didn't provide the model # you'll have to look up the manual yourself and see what resolutions are accepted over HDMI and what the procedure for your TV is to have it not overscan.
