### Post by HereInOz on 2010-07-28
If you put that sender in to your contacts and set the Loading Images setting - Edit > Preferences > Mail Preferences > HTML Messages - to Load images in messages from contacts, you will get the images from those who you trust, but still block them from those who you don't.

Cheers,

Alan.
