### Post by localzuk on 2006-03-28
It depends on the card. Some cards have native drivers. Some cards need to use their windows drivers via 'ndiswrapper'.

Take a look at [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WifiDocs/Driver/Ndiswrapper) for information about ndiswrapper. Or [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WifiDocs/](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WifiDocs/) for a more wide ranging overview regarding wifi.
