### Post by dnyal on 2011-12-08
I got a Dell 1464 with a 500 Mb 4330 ATI card; the rest of specs are as yours. Using open-source drives I get smooth effects and video playback, but apparently my video card overheats (my laptop gets very hot with the fan running at max). Then I installed the last proprietary drives (Catalyst 11.11) and the heat problem was solved, but the 3D effects (like maximizing, minimizing, etc.) are choppy. I got rid of the tearing with the "Tear free" option. Still the video playback is kind of laggy and I already tweaked VLC all I could with little improvement.
