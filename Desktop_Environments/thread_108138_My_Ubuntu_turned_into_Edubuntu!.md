### Post by kwaanens on 2005-12-25
You probably replaced ubuntu-desktop with edubuntu-desktop.
Use Synaptic and search for name: "ubuntu" and you should see lots of packages.
Installing ubuntu-desktop may cause you to have to remove some stuff. However, your system is basically the same, you just have edubuntu artwork instead of ubuntu artwork, and, og course, whatever you meant to install.
