Your post doesn't really tell me much bro.  Are you currently in Ubuntu or is it not starting up, because those settings look fine.  What message is it giving if you can't boot into it.  Just like windows, you have to choose the approperiate resolution for a wide screen.  Windows automatically chooses 1024*768 now adays.  But if you were to add a wide screen, you'd have to set up the one that says 1152*768.  That way, your fonts and everything else won't look fuzzy.
