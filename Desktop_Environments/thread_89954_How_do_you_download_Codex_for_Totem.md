### Post by corstar on 2005-11-13
Hi PCB,
there are two ways that I know of to accomplish this.
1/ download "EasyUbuntu" (google this) for your distro version and simply click on the executable file. I think there are only two files in the whole download

This will open up a checkbox dialog and you can check off the apps/upgraded that you like.

2/ download "all the codecs" from the mplayer site. Don't worry, it's in one bunndle. Then mkdir /lib/win32 (under the root directory) and copy all the files to this directory. 

Hope this helps.
