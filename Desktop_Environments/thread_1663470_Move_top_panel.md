### Post by pastalavista on 2011-01-09
Right-click the items on the top panel and select 'move' (you may have to uncheck 'lock to panel' first) and move them to the bottom panel. Then you can right-click the top panel and select 'remove panel'. Or you can remove the bottom panel and select 'properties' in the top panel and change the orientation to bottom. You also might want to replace the 'menu bar' applet with 'main menu' to save space on the new bottom panel.
