That was driving me nuts too.  Turns out it's pretty easy.  Just right click on the items you don't want (the actual item, not the category) and a menu will pop up.  Select the "Hide <whatever>" menu option and it'll disappear.  If you get rid of all the items in a category, the category disappears.
