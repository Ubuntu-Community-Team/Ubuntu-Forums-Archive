### Post by Old *ix Geek on 2009-05-21
Bring up the 'add widgets' function (several ways: right-click on your panel, or select the yellow cashew-shaped thingie on your panel or in the top righthand corner of your screen).  Select 'add widgets' and then select 'folder view' and 'add' it.  Once it's back on your screen go into its properties (with the wrench icon on its side) and set its location to your Desktop.  That should do it!
