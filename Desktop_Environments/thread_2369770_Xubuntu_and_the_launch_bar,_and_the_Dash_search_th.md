### Post by Frogs Hair on 2017-08-27
You can install a dock and move it to the left side if you wish. Some options include docky, cairo-dock  , plank and dockbarx. I've used dockbarx with the xfce desktop, but do a little research before you choose.  Docky and cairodock are in the repository. 

[https://github.com/M7S/dockbarx](https://github.com/M7S/dockbarx)
