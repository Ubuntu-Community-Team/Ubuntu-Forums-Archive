### Post by dbott67 on 2005-10-18
Press CTRL-L to get the location bar (it's under the 'GO' menu).

There's also a Configuration Editor hack.  Open Configuration Editor to the key **/apps/nautilus/preferences/always_use_location_entry** and place a checkmark in the box.
