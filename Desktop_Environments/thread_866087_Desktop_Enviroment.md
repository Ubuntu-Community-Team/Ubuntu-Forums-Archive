### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2008-07-21
DE/WM are largely a matter of personal preference as you might imagine.

Each has advantages / disadvantages.

[http://linuxreviews.org/dictionary/Desktop_environment/](http://linuxreviews.org/dictionary/Desktop_environment/)

[http://xwinman.org/](http://xwinman.org/)

You can run KDE apps in gnome / xfce . most implementations of xfce use primarily gnome apps.

Some users prefer to "roll their own" installing a window manager and adding what they feel are the best applications for each task. This approach takes a little more linux experience in order to know what you want.
