### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-04-04
If you just want the menu in the bottom, then you don't need anything else. Just right click in the panel, select properties, then change the orientation to bottom or simply drag the bar to the bottom. You can also create a new panel in the bottom and drag the menu from the top panel to it or add a new menu. 

Right-click the panel and select  "Add to Panel' so see what other stuff you can put there.

> **Superdude_123 said:**
> If I was going to chose a different look, under what category are the bars under (that bar that will hold my menus, apps, etc)

Is under GTK 2, but is not only the bar, but the entire gnome theme.

If you want to try a new menu, then I recommend Gimmie. 

```
sudo apt-get install gimmie
```
