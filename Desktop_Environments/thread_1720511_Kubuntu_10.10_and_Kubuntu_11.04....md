### Post by SeijiSensei on 2011-04-03
Are you running Kubuntu 10.10?  (Your profile says Ubuntu.)  If you are, I recommend just adding the "backports" repository to Kpackagekit and upgrading.  

In Kpackagekit, use Settings > Edit Origins > Other Software > Add, then enter "ppa:kubuntu-ppa/backports" in the dialog box that follows and hit Apply. You should then be prompted to update.  Now if you choose Software Updates, you'll see the KDE 4.6.1 packages are available.  

If you're not in a hurry to do this, you might wait until later this week when 4.6.2 will appear in backports.
