### Post by mcduck on 2010-03-18
The "Normal" and Extra" settings in the Appearance dialog are just presets of Compiz settings. When you configure the settings yourself you aren't using either one of the presets, so it's supposed to act that way. :)

Can't say anything about wobbly windows, though, at least without some extra information about your settings and what you did when you tried to enable that plugin.

### Post by sebastianabate on 2010-03-18
The normal and extra option in Visual Effects are only a way to turn on a fixed number of compiz effects with one click. When you select or deselect an effect with compizconfig the checkbox of this options gets cleared, but the unchanged effects are still on (you can test this by clicking on extra effects in Visual Effects, and then enabling desktop cube in compizconfig, the checkbox in Visual Effects gets cleared, but all the effects that it enables are still actives, like the minimize animation)
