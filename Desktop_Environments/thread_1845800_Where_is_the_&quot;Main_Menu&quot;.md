### Post by deltaPrime21 on 2011-09-17
The system settings menu should show up under the power button menu in the top right. From there you can access the screensaver and other settings. 

Alternatively, you can open the "Dash" by clicking the home button (top left) or pressing the super key on your keyboard (typically called the "windows" key). Then you can type "system" and the system settings application will appear. 

Another option is to open the "Application" Lens (the icon on the left-hand Launcher bar that has a plus sign in a magnifying glass) and type "screensaver".  In the Application lens you can also filter with the menu on the right side of the search box. If you select "System" from that menu you can see what application you have installed under that category.

(It took me a while to figure it out, but now I really like the new Unity interface).

Edit: I think the "Main menu" you are looking for is either the home button (top left) or the applications lens.
