### Post by skymt on 2006-10-14
Try this:

* Right-click the Gnome menu bar, and choose Remove From Panel.
* Where it was, right-click and choose Add to Panel.
* In the window that pops up, scroll down to Utilities, click Main Menu, click Add, then click Close.

If things are in the wrong places now (quite likely), drag them around with the middle button (usually the scroll wheel), or right-click and choose Move.
