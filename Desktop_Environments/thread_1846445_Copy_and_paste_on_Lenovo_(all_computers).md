### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-09-19
Pressing the two buttons together is middle-click emulation, and the middle click pastes the current selection. I think what you actually have a problem with is the middle-click emulation itself. See [_here_]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/X/Quirks#A2-button_Mice") to re-enable it.
