### Post by Tolaris on 2005-06-29
Hi all, and thanks to many of you for all your help.  I've been finding solutions to my problems with normal searches for months now and I really appreciate it.

My problem is Arabic (Windows-1252) support for Evolution.  I have Arabic fonts and language installed and can view/edit/enter Arabic characters anywhere as Unicode.  When I write Arabic emails, I use UTF-8 and it works fine.

The problem is many Arabic speakers just use the default arabic encoding under windows, which is Windows-1252, not UTF-8.  When these people send me email, I have no way of viewing it correctly.

In Evolution, I can open the individual email in a window and choose View -> Character Encoding.  However, Arabic - Windows-1252 (nor any other Arabic encoding except UTF-8) is available.

Please, how can I add this?
