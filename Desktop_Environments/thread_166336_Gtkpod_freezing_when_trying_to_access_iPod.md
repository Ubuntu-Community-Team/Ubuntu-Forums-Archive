### Post by Edward The Bonobo on 2006-04-26
It's a flakey package.  With me it generally crashes on 'Sync'.  I unplug it (ignoring the 'Do not disconnect') and try again.  I generally try and add tracks one album at a time, syncing between each.

When - less commonly - the whole thing freezes, as in your case, I obviously unplug, shut down gtkpod and start again.  This is an absolute pain. because it takes about half an hour to list my iPod's database after reading it.
