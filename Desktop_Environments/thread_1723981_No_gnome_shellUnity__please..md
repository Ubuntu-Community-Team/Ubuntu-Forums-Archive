### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-04-07
If you upgrade, it won't change anything. The Classic Desktop still ships with Ubuntu 11.04.

If you do find yourself in Unity, just log out, select your name, and before you type your password, select "Classic Desktop" in the Session menu at the lower right. The login screen will remember your preference. You can also change this default by going to Administration -> Login Screen.
