### Post by maxinstuff2 on 2014-09-15
1. Open the K menu, and go to system settings.
2. Click on "Locale" (top row)
3. It will have all the available languages. Select Japanese, and click the blue right arrow in the middle to move it across to your preferred languages.
4. Click "Apply" and it will install the language package.
5. Reboot.
6. You will now have a little keyboard icon in your system tray - bottom right hand side of the screen by default (near network etc.). Right click it, and go to preferences.
7. Go to the input method tab, and in the dropdown click "show all input methods". The dropdown will dissappear when you click that, but that's OK.
8. Open the dropdown again and you will see Japanese in the list, select it, followed by your preferred Japanese input method (SKK?). 
9. You can now toggle between English and Japanese input by pressing Ctrl+Space

Enjoy :)
