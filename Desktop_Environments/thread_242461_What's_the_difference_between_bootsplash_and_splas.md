### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-08-26
The repositories for Splashy are down and have been for a while.  You can still go directly to the site and download the .deb package that way.  But be forewarned, there are many issues/bugs with it, so take caution/note.

The usplash that comes with Ubuntu is the user space splash image that you see when booting up your machine (before the login screen).  Sometimes, after logging in through GDM or KDM, the image that appears that shows certain programs starting up, is called a splash screen.
