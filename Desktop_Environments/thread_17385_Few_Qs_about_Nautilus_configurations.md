### Post by NeoChaosX on 2005-02-28
It's possible. Go to GConf (Applications > System Tools > Configuration Editor), then go to Apps > Nautilus > icon_view and check the box on the label_beside_icons key to get the filenames to the side of the icons. Dunno how to display misc info, though.
