Macbuntu 11.04 is a seperate ubuntu based distribution. It is based on ubuntu 11.04 Natty, but has the look of a Mac in terms of icons, font, docky etc. The .iso file which you burn to cd or dvd and then install on your hard drive can be downloaded from [http://sourceforge.net/projects/macbuntu/](http://sourceforge.net/projects/macbuntu/)

I have installed Macbuntu 11.04 and am liking it very much.

### Post by Nath4n on 2011-09-19
Macbuntu-10.10 will work flawlessly on 11.04.

Macbuntu is not it's own OS, it's a theme + function.

Rename your macbuntu folder Macbuntu-11.04

Open install.sh with gedit  

Change the Ubunu 10.10 to 11.04

Save.

Do the same to uninstall.sh.


When you have done that open a terminal and drag and drop the install.sh file and hit enter. 

 Follow the prompts and you'll have a smooth running beatiful highly customizable desktop.  I am in love with it.  BTW, you can choose not to use the apple logos, I chose the Ubuntu logs.

This little cheat works fine on 11.04, and will hold me over until the official 11.04 comes out.
