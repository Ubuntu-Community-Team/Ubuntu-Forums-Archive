### Post by Erik1984 on 2015-01-25
If you right click on the clock there is an option "Digital Clock Settings". Under timezone there is a drop-down menu called "Clock defaults to" that has two options here "local" and "UTC". Here is it is set to "local", you could try that.

I made a screenshot of the clock. When I hover over the time, two timezones are displayed. Corresponding to the settings it defaults to local time (called Amsterdam here).
