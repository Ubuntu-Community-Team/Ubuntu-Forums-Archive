### Post by mgmiller on 2006-09-16
nautilus will let you add comments and catagories to the meta data of the file.  Right click the image and go to properties and notes.  You can write what you want.  Also g-thumb can put comments on a picture, but I don't think it adds to the metadata.  I know that's not a complete solution, as you can't change the data added by the camera, but at least you can add some info.  What would be nice is a program like easytag, but for pictures.
