### Post by finnbuntu on 2010-08-18
Hello Hellkeepa,

Do you mean a launcher where you press a certain key combination and type in the thing you want to open and press enter? If so, Gnome Do (which is in the repos under gnome-do) can do just that, only without the KDE libraries (Gnome and XFCE are based on the same platform). Check out [http://do.davebsd.com/]("http://do.davebsd.com/") for more info. 

Hope that Gnome Do does what you want,
Finnbuntu ;)
