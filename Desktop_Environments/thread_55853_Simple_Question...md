### Post by debian_n00b on 2005-08-10
Yes, as a matter of fact, there is a simple way to change icons globally.

Under "preferences", open "themes". And under the selcelted theme, select "theme details". There, click the "icons" tab. 

I found these mysterious settings by looking around:-)

### Post by varunus on 2005-08-10
Go to [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) for Icon theme, Window Border themes (metacity), and application appearance themes (GTK2.0).  You can install them all with the theme selector in System->Preferences->Themes.

Linux, unlike windows, does not restrict themeability.  Its all about choice.

Enjoy.
