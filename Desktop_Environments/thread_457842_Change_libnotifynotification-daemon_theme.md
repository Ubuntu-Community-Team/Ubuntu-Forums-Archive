### Post by mcduck on 2007-05-30
You can change the theme into 'normal' with gconf-editor. That at least works, and uses your GTK2-theme properly unlike the ubuntu'-theme..

Transparency is of course possible with Beryl/Compiz, using the Set Window Attributes..-plugin.
