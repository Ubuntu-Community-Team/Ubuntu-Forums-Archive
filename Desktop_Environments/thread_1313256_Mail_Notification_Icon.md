### Post by svr84 on 2010-08-15
You must be signed in to Yahoo! in order to receive new mail alerts.  
You must have the Yahoo Mail icon visible in your toolbar also, to see these alerts.  
To add it to your toolbar in Classic, click the pencil icon.
From the drop down menu, select Personal Options->Add/Edit buttons.   Check the box next to "Yahoo Mail" then go to the bottom and click  "Finished."
