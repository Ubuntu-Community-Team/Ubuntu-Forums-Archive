### Post by santiagoward2000 on 2007-11-10
I KNOW!!! :lolflag:
Just install compizconfig-settings-manager from the repository, then open it, go to "General Options", the "Desktop Size" tab, and increase the "Horizontal Virtual Size" value ( to 4 if you want a cube).
