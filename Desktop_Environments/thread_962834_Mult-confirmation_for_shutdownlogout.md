Using KDE 4 and for the most part, I love it--just waiting for everything else to catch up.

In the mean time, there is a terrible annoyance to sort out.

By default, when shutting down or logging out, one clicks on the K menu, Leave, then Logout, Lock, Switch User, Suspend to RAM, Suspend to Disk, Shutdown or Restart.  At this point, further confirmation is required.  

The way I see it, I've already jumped through hoops to tell my computer to shut down, why do I need to confirm it yet again? :confused:
