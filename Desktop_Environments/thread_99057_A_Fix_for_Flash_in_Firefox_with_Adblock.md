### Post by abou on 2005-12-04
One thing that I noticed was that I was having problems viewing Flash in Firefox; for example there was audio, but now video.  At first, the simple solution was to disable adblock; however, there is a way to keep it enabled.

Go to Tools->Adblock->Preferences->Adblock Options->Uncheck Obj-Tabs

And that is it.  You can also get to the Adblock Preferences menu in Firefox by pressing "CTRL-SHIFT-P".
