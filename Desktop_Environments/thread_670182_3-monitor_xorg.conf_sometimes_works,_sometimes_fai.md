### Post by Mazemaster on 2008-01-17

When I copy over my custom xorg.conf file, then restart X via ctrl+alt+backspace then startx, everything works fine on all 3 monitors for a while. 

However, as soon as I hard reboot, I am presented with the kubuntu splash loading screen, and then all my monitors go blank and remain that way. 

At this point, I can use keyboard shortcuts to login and get a terminal (all while staring at a blank screen), and if I then restore the default xorg.conf and restart x (again, blindly). Then I get functionality for 1 screen, taking me back to square 1.

What is going on!?
