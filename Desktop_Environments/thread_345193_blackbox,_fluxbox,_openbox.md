The computers in my wife's classroom run Fluxbox and I have them set up running fbpanel along the bottom, and I put icons in fbpanel instead of on the desktop.  The nice thing about that is the users (students) can't mess with them, because unless you provide a button for it, fbpanel is not editable from the panel itself.  (I have their entire .fluxbox directory read-only, so they can't mess with a single thing, actually.)
