### Post by ankspo71 on 2011-09-20
Hi,
The web address to the "iGoogle" homepage is [http://www.google.com/ig](http://www.google.com/ig) . Be sure that this is the homepage used in your browser homepage preferences too. 

Also, after joining Google+, Google+ automatically adds a new "Google+" link to that google links menu at the top left of your Gmail page, Google homepage, and Google+ page, and so on. Be sure you using the appropriate link in that Google menu. You might need to click on "more" > even more > iGoogle (see attached image) to find iGoogle in the menu.

I haven't tried this before but to to remove Google+, click on your Google picture in the top right corner of the browser window, then click on Account Settings, then in the Account Overview category, click on "Delete profile and remove associated social features", and then finally "Delete Google+ content".

Hope this helps.

PS. I kind of like Google+ but I'm not that into social networking sites.
