### Post by Erkko on 2006-08-10
One easy way to import radio stations to Rhythbox is to go to [www.shoutcast.com](www.shoutcast.com).
Then don't open the playlist with Rhythbox. Rather do the following:
- Save the playlist to your desktop
- Open the pls-file with text editor.
- Locate the ip-address of the radio-station copy it to clipboard
- Manually add new station to Rhythmbox and paste the url there

Have fun!
