### Post by Efwis on 2005-11-21
[https://addons.mozilla.org/extensions/moreinfo.php?application=firefox&category=Tabbed%20Browsing&numpg=10&id=785](https://addons.mozilla.org/extensions/moreinfo.php?application=firefox&category=Tabbed%20Browsing&numpg=10&id=785)

direct link to what your looking for.

> Quick Description

Adds a close button to each of the browser tabs, and removes the close button at the end of the tab bar.
