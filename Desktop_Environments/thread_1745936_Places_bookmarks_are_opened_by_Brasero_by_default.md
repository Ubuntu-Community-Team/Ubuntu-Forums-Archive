### Post by David D. on 2011-05-01
From the desktop, go up to the panel and select PLACES > COMPUTER.  This should display your disk drive(s).  Right click on a drive and select PROPERTIES.  Go to the OPEN WITH tab and change the selection to FILE BROWSER.
