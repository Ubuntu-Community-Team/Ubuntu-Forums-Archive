Hi people,
How about KDE *AND* Gnome???
I wanted to run both and was amazed to find how simple it was. 
Small thing: I loaded KDE and the std. blue KDE splash screen came up but then defaulted to Gnome as I logged in. 
I MISSED OUT on using the options bottom left side of screen or even mouseover on sign-in.  Didn't use mouse to sign in. Siiimple!
