I couldn't understand the first issue.
But about the second (split Konqueror view)
1. Ctrl+Shift+L splits the screen left and right
2. Ctrl+Shift+T splits the screen top and bottom
3. Ctrl+Shift+R closes the active view (the one with the green circle at the status bar at the bottom)

Of course you can change the keyboard shortcuts if you want.
You can also access these through the View > Split View menu in the menu bar at the top.
