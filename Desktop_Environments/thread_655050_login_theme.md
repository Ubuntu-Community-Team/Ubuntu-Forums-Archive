### Post by mcduck on 2008-01-01
In Gnome desktop: System/Administration/Login Window. Go to Local-tab and click the Install-button to install the themes. Then make sure you actually select the theme you wish to use by clicking on the radio button next to it (instead of just clicking the theme itself).

---

### Post by bonzodog on 2008-01-01
Also, to get more login screens go to [http://gnome-look.org](http://gnome-look.org), and look through the gdm themes section. once you have one, it should be in the .tar.gz format; simply open the gdm login manager, go to the themes tab, then drag and drop the downloaded theme to it.
