### Post by Ratscallion on 2009-02-20
As for your latest question about the themes, what theme engine are you using. For example, are you dragging themes into the "Appearance" Preferences dialogue, or in the emerald themer? If it's the emerald, you can probably go and disable the glowing of the minimize, maximize etc buttons by doing the following. Click the theme you want to edit, then go into "Edit Themes." Once you're in there, you'll probably want to ignore most of the editing things, unless you want to make your own theme. The setting you're looking for is in "Buttons." 
If you want the glow on, check both of the Halo options, uncheck them if you don't want the halo glow.
