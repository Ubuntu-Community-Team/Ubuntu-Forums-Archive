### Post by luisromangz on 2008-04-03
If you mean having the Oxygen style, no, not yet. 

If what you want is consistency, and not just using Oxygen, you could use the QtCurve style, wich is avalable for Gtk, Qt3/KDE3 and Qt4/KDE4 (look in [www.kde-look.org](www.kde-look.org)), and which would provide the almost the same look in all programs.
