### Post by anil_robo on 2005-12-11
Do you mean the new links open in a "new firefox window" or "a different desktop"? If you want all new links to open in the same firefox window, you can do this by enabling tabbed browsing in firefox.  (Edit-->Preferences--> Advanced--> Tabbed Browsing --> Open links from other applications in "the most recent tab/window".

If you meant the firefox links opened in another desktop, then I'm sorry I may not have an answer for this!
