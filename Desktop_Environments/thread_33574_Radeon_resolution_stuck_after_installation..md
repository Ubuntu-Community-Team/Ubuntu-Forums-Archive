### Post by Pixie on 2005-05-11
I had an issue after installing Kubuntu for the first time. My resolution was stuck on 640x480 and I couldn't make it any better. I did a bit of reading around and couldn't seem to find a solution to my problem. I went through the xorg.conf file and saw that the driver being used for my ATI technologies Radeon 7000 VE was "ati". I had a feeling that might be the problem and changed it to "radeon". After restarting X I could change my resolution back to 1280x1024  :-P  Sorry if this a repost, I couldn't find anywhere else and hopefully this'll help anyone else with the same problem.
