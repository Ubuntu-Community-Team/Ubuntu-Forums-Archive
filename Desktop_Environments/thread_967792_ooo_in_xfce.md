### Post by Pro-reason on 2008-11-02
Installing libraries doesn't slow anything down unless those libraries are loaded.  You can see what dependencies OpenOffice.org will bring in by going into Synaptic and marking OpenOffice.org for installation.

OpenOffice.org is a big, unwieldy application suite, however.  It even has an entire Space Invaders game in it, bulking it out even more.  Its developers don't take streamlining seriously.

I tend to avoid it.  AbiWord is a more suitable word processor for Xfce.  Or LaTeX.
