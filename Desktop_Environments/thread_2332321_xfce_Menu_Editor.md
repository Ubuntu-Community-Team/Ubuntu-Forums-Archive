### Post by mook765 on 2016-07-30
The Menu Editor in xfce seems to make problems when you use it to rearrange or hide menu-items.
 Hiding just a single menu item may cause  loss off an undefined number of items in the menu.
Even the desktop-right-click menu is affected.
To solve this, i deleted the XML-file  xfce-applications.menu  in /home/my name/.config/menus .
After that i just had to populate the favorites section, as it was empty.
Enable "show hidden files" in your file manager will make it easy to find the file.
