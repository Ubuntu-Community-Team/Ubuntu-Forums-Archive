### Post by lswb on 2010-01-18
The middle button will paste while the original item is still selected. Also, gnome terminal and other  X terminal programs use Shift-Ctrl-C to copy and Shift-Ctrl-V to paste. (Because Ctrl-C and Ctrl-V have historical use in unix/linux shells that predates their use for copy/paste)
