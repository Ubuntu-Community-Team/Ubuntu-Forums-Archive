I've tried all kinds of Ubuntu derivatives and spin-offs with different desktop environments and I've come to the conclusion that Unity is actually kind of nice once you get used to it, especially if you consider that it's still quite new. Now I'd like to hear what you think about this xfce-ish approach to Unity that came to my mind after switching from Xubuntu back to Ubuntu:

Put the launcher to the bottom of the screen. Don't let the windows extend into the menu bar and use the space to show open windows in a traditional fashion instead of the indications in the launcher. There'd be no additional menu at the top left like in xfce and the dash would be retained.

Whaddaya think?
