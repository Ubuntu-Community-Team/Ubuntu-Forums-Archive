### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-01-28
That blog post is from 2009. It would not be the first time and it most certainly has not been the last time that a blogger has taken a suggestion/discussion of possibilities at a Ubuntu Developers Summit and turned it into a soon to be fact. Back in those days anyone involved in the development of Ubuntu and its flavours could set up a session at a UDS and discuss whatever they liked. It did not mean that their ideas had official support.

> [COLOR=#263034][FONT=Arial]Canonical is building an Android execution environment that will make it possible for Android applications to run on Ubuntu[/FONT][/COLOR]

If any actual work was ever done on this, then it died a death. Of course, there is nothing to stop an Android app developer from porting their app to Ubuntu Touch.

[http://developer.ubuntu.com/community/](http://developer.ubuntu.com/community/)
