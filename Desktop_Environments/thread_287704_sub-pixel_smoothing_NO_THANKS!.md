### Post by ajm2005 on 2006-10-29
I know everyone is supposed to love sub-pixel smoothing, but I find it produces subtle yet annoying color artifacts - the edges of the letters get faint yet noticeable color finges of reg/ green. I'd rather have it turned off, or set to greyscale at most. The characters look a bit more "blocky" but at least they are black and white. I use Tahoma as the system font, as it was designed for the screen and is quite readable even when it is "blocky" or obviously pixelated on the screen.
