### Post by sdennie on 2008-06-23
You shouldn't need to do any of those things under Ubuntu.  I think in rare cases, you can get the filesystem in a state where a defrag might be beneficial but, in general it's completely unnecessary.  In general, I wouldn't try to "fix" or "cleanup" anything in Ubuntu unless you are absolutely certain that you are experiencing a problem.
