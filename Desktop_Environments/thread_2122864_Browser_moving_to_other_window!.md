### Post by r.stiltskin on 2013-03-06
In your Xubuntu Settings Manager go to Window Manger Tweaks and click on the Focus tab.  There, under "When a window raises itself", you'll find 3 choices:

Bring window on current workspace
Switch to window's workspace
Do nothing

Select the second one.

The second poster seems to have an entirely different problem & should probably start a new thread.
