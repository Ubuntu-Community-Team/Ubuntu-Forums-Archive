### Post by Mark76 on 2010-09-26
You need to go into the panel menu, add new panel item, add an applications bar and then add lxterminal to the applications bar.

### Post by Mark76 on 2010-09-26
Yes it can. You just have to use the arrows.

Open Panel Preferences, go to Panel Applets, then highlight the Application Launch Bar and then click on the Up button until it's where you want it to be
