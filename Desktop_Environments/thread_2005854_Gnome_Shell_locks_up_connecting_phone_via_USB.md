When I connect the same phone under Unity, it works perfectly as expected.

I have no idea what the issue could be. I looked through a pile of ask ubuntu posts and couldn't find anything similar. This isn't really the highest priority thing, but I'm just really baffled and thought I'd check to see if anyone here had an idea.
