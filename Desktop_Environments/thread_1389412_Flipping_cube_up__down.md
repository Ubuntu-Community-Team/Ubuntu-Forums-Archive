### Post by mcduck on 2010-01-24
Yes, it is possible. But since the cube top&bottom are not actually the same size and shape as your desktops are you can't use them like the actual dsktops, you can only show images on them (makes a pretty cool way to do a presentation, anyway).

For example if your desktop resolution is 1280x800 the cube tops are 1280x1280. And when rotated to fit your screen they have to be squeezed to fit your screen. Makes it kind of hard to draw any program output there.
