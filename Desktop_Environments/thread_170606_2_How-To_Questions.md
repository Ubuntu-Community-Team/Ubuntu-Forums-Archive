Turboprint has some free drivers for you machine.   They are limited, unless you are willing to pay $30.00 to unlock all the features.  Also you will need to install Alien so you can convert the .rpm to .deb.  

System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager > Search for Alien > highlight Alien > click apply
