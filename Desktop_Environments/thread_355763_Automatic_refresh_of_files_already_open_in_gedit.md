### Post by kebes on 2007-02-08
I'll also add that the KDE text editors "Kate" and "KWrite" both provide prompts when a file has changed on disk. (You can then ignore or reload, as you wish.)

The cross-platform text editor "jEdit" (Linux/Windows/Mac) also automatically reloads files that change on disk.
