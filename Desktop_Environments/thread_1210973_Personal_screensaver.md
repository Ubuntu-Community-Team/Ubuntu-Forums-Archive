### Post by VCoolio on 2009-07-12
Welcome to Ubuntu.

In system > preferences > screensaver you can choose one called "Pictures folder" which will make a slideshow of your ~/Pictures folder (~ is short for /home/username). You can configure that by following the tips at the end of [this thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=488866").

### Post by VCoolio on 2009-07-19
sorry for replying late.

symlin command: ln -s /path/to/fileorfolder/tobelinked /path/to/folder/where/link/appears

[this]("http://gnome-hacks.org/hacks.html?id=656") is also an easy solution: open $HOME/.config/user-dirs.dirs and specify the path behind XDG_PICTURES_DIR to be the folder in which your pictures are.
