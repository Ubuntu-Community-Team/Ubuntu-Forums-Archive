### Post by fossilsking on 2006-07-24
Hi guys, I just want to thank you for the wonderful discussions about the image viewer software. I have installed F-Spot from Aptitude with no problems. I was looking for a viewer which supports Nikon’s NEF-format. I have tried Xnview and some other programs but F-Spot works just perfect (as a viewer). The image import takes some time but then the viewing of the photos is very fast. You can smoothly zoon in with the mouse scroll etc. It works much faster than the Nikon Capture for Windows, IrfanView etc. In Windows I use XnView and it is also quite fast viewer for NEF-format. I use to shoot many photos and later to choose which one is the best and I delete the rest. So I needed viewing software which works fast and allows you to delete immediately the unwanted photos. So, if you are looking for fast image viewer for your Nikon’s NEF’s, grab the F-Spot. Cheers, Emil
