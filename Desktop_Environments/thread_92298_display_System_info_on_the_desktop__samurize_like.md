### Post by taurus on 2005-11-19
I recommand conky (newer and more improve of torsmo) for your linux box...  You can install it with synaptic or apt-get.  Then, head over to [http://conky.sourceforge.net](http://conky.sourceforge.net) and check out the samples of .conkyrc.  Looks for the one that you like the most and save it to your home directory.  Modify it to have it displace whatever else you want...  ;)
