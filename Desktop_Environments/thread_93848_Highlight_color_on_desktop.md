### Post by gingermark on 2005-11-25
OK, this took me a bloody ice age to find, but here we go:

Open up System Settings in the K-Menu.
Click on "Appearence" in the "Personal" section.
Select "Style" in the left hand menu.
Click on the "Effects" tab.
Change "Rubberband effect" from "Make Translucent" to "Disable" and click "Apply".

You should now just a have a thin white border around the area you select, a la Hoary. without any fill colour.

Why on earth someone thought that was a good place to stick that option I'll never know. I do think the option could do with renaming as well!
