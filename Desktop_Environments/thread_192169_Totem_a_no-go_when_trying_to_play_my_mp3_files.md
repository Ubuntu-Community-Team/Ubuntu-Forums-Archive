### Post by Fafnir on 2006-06-08
Easiest way to change file associations is to find an mp3 file somewhere on the system in the file browser, right click on it and choose "Open with Other Application..." Then just select XMMS or Realplayer from the list and hit OK. The same thing works for getting MPlayer to play movies rather than Totem.

Hope that helps!
