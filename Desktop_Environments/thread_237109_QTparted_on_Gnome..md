### Post by kaffelars on 2006-08-15
Yes, you can use QTparted in Gnome, as well as all other KDE-apps :D If you install them through Synaptic, the necessary libs (like QT) will be installed for you.

If it's just for partition editing though, you can also use GParted, which is the Gnome equivalent of QTparted :)
