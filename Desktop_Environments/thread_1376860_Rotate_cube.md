### Post by SecretCode on 2010-01-09
open ccsm > open 'rotate cube' plugin > go to bindings tab > set keyboard bindings for 'rotate left' and 'rotate right'.

Mine are ctrl-alt-button4 - wheel-up rotates left - and ctrl-alt-button5 - wheel-down rotates right.
