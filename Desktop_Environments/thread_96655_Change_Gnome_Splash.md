### Post by mrmcctt on 2005-11-29
If you mean the splash screen displayed after you type in your username and password, you can find some splash screens at [Gnome-Look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org")

When you want to change the splash screen to the one you downloaded, click on MAIN MENU>SYSTEM>PREFERENCES>SPLASH SCREEN and activate your new splash screen from there.

I hope this is what you were looking for.

Edit:  [This Page in Gnome-Look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?page=3") has several Debian splash screens.
