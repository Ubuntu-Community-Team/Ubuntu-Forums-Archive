### Post by tpg on 2007-01-15
I'm running a laptop with an Intel 855GM, and Beryl works fairly well on top of AIGLX. A lot of the fancy plugins (water etc.) that require programmable shaders don't work, but the basics are there (wobbly windows, cube, expose ...). I think XGL and AIGLX are the only main options. AIGLX is part of Xorg in Edgy - maybe you should try Beryl again?

P.S. For an Intel card, I would definitely recommend AIGLX over XGL!:)
