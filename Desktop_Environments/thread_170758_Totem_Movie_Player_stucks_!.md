### Post by glinsvad on 2006-05-05
In breezy the current version of totem (not counting backports) is 1.2.0-0ubuntu3.1 - first of all, you might want to verify this using Synaptics. In the upcoming dapper, totem is version 1.4.0-0ubuntu5 and I'm betting this one's a whole lot more stable. My first advice is: Either use backports for totem, go dapper beta now ([http://www.ubuntu.com/testing](http://www.ubuntu.com/testing)) or hold your breath untill the 1st of June when dapper is scheduled for final release.

Generally breezy isn't really good with multimedia unless you spend time tweaking stuff - you also might have noticed that Rhythmbox is a bit shaky around restricted formats such as mp3. Anyway, bugtracking should be possible from [http://www.gnome.org/projects/totem/](http://www.gnome.org/projects/totem/)
