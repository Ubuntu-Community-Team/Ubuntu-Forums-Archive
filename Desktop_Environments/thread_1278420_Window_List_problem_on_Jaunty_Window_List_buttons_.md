### Post by undecim on 2009-09-29
It seems there is something preventing your window list from expanding. (perhaps a launcher without an icon, or a failed applet?) try right clicking on the blank area just to the right of your window list and see if you get a "Remove from Panel" option. (though make sure you aren't clicking ON your window list.)

If that doesn't work, try moving your window list. Right click on the blank space just to the left of your window list and unchecking the "Lock to Panel" option if it is checked, then use the "Move" option to move your window list to the other side of the empty space on your panel.
