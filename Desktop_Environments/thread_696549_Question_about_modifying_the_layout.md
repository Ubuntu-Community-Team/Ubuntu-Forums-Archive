### Post by erginemr on 2008-02-14
Hello and Welcome to Ubuntu,

I hope you will enjoy your Linux adventure as we do.

The effect in the video you have referenced is called the "burning effect" of Beryl, fork of Compiz, a project developed to provide 3-D effects on linux desktops. But Beryl is discontinued now and has been superceded by Compiz Fusion. I don't remember if Compiz Fusion retains that effect. 

If you have installed Gutsy Gibbon (Ubuntu 7.10) to your computer, then you can easily enable desktop effects by Main Menu -> Preferences -> Apperarance -> Effects tab, provided that you have a powerful-enough graphics card that can handle 3D.

Regarding further customization, reading this site thoroughly may help:
[http://tuxenclave.wordpress.com/2007/11/23/ubuntu-customization-guide-v2/](http://tuxenclave.wordpress.com/2007/11/23/ubuntu-customization-guide-v2/)
