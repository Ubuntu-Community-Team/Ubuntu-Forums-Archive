### Post by andrea000 on 2009-06-09
There is  a few ways to uninstall it.You can go to
system->preference->main menu and uncheck and delete
it from there or you can login in to Nautilus as root
and delete from there.The first would be better i never
like to recommend someone to login to Nautilus as root.
