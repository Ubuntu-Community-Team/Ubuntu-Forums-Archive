### Post by Ramses de Norre on 2006-03-01
It works as good as ide.
I installed ubuntu on a sata drive, just plugged it in, booted from cd and he saw it.
If you are adding it you'll want to format it (gparted can do this) and then add the partitions to your /etc/fstab file.
