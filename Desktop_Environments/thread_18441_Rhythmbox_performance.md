### Post by landotter on 2005-03-07
try zinf. the player UI is skinned like winamp or xmms, but the playlist editor is gtk2 and fits in perfectly with the rest of your desktop. Does shoutcast just fine.

Beep Media Player, the gtk2 port of Xmms isn't bad either.

I still find myself just using xmms or the commandline to player mp3s even with all the advancements in UI. Go figure. :lol:

Do also check out Streamtuner, I think you can get it via synaptic. It's a Shoutcast/xiph stream browser. Super cool. :D
