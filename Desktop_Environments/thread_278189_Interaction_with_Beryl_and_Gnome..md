### Post by Peepsalot on 2006-10-15
You have to temporarily disable beryl to configure gnome themes again.  You can click the beryl icon and use "Select Window Manager" to go back to Metacity.  Then you should be able to configure themes.  When you are done you can switch beryl back on.
