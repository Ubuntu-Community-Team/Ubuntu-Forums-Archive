### Post by petersjm on 2006-07-18
I've figured out a work-around. It's not the nicest way to do it (I'd still have prefered a way to do it in GtkPod), but it does the trick.

What I did was this: Mount the iPod and right-click the icon on your desktop. Select "Browse Folder". Then navigate to "iPod Control" > "Music" and then simply copy all the folders across to your hdd, wherever you want. Now open your favourite app (I've done it in Banshee and it works fine, but not tried it in any other app) and import the folders into your library.

As I said, it works fine in Banshee (for me, anyway), even pulls in the artist, title, etc.

But I much prefer the interface of amaroK so if anyone can tell me how to import these files into amaroK successfully (I just tried; it didn't work) I'd be forever in your debt :D
