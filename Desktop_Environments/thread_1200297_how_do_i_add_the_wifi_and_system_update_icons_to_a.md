### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-06-29
Right click on a blank area on the panel and select "ADD to Panel". I believe your looking for the Notification Area and possibly the Network Monitor. Left click on the applets your wish to include in your panel and select the "ADD" button at the bottom of the window. This will add those applets to your panel.

Hope this helps.
