### Post by blackbelt_jones on 2008-11-03
Fixed.  Sorry

I had some problems with this.  After installing KDE3 and rebooting, my system froze at the the kdm login screen.   I reinstalled intrepid and tried it again, same problem, but I got around it this time by using a knoppix disk to mount the hard drive, and then to disable kdm, 


sudo mv /etc/rc2.d/S13kdm   /etc/rc2.d/K87kdm

and logging onto the console.  I contacted the guy who runs this repository, and he does not know of anyone else having this problem.  You might want to stick with gdm.

[COLOR="Red"][B]NOTE: I'm having a great experience, but I didn't start with Kubuntu; I started with Gnome-based Ubuntu.   How kde3 will play with kde4 on Kubuntu 8.10 is still an open question, as far as I know.  When in doubt, email the administrator.
[/B][/COLOR]
This is my first look at KDE 3.5.10, and while it's described as a maintainance release, I was surprised at some nice changes over KDE 3.5.9.  It's easier to mount remvoable media from the storage media window in Konqueror.
