### Post by JLTB on 2005-07-09
If you have troubles with sound / video most players will give you the option to choose a different engine.  Running Suse 9.3 I couldn't get video in VLC to do anything but display a blue screen until I selected the OpenGL video engine, then everything ran fine.  Similarly some audio engines allow for surround sound and are easier on your CPU/SPU.

It's also important to note that most players out there basically run on Xine anyway (Kaffeine, VLC, etc..) a la xine-lib.  You'll find this out quickly when xine stops playing movies for some reason and all the other players quit working too.
