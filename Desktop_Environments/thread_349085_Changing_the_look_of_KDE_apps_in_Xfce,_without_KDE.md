### Post by psychicdragon on 2007-01-29
qtconfig can only theme Qt apps. KDE apps although written using the Qt toolkit have their own themes.

If you're running KDE applications, you already have most of KDE installed, kdelibs et all are huge. Just install kcontrol and use that to change the KDE theme. You should have most of the dependencies met already, so it won't be a huge download.
