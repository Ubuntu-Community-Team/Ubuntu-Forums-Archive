### Post by compwiz18 on 2006-08-18
Did you try turning IPv6 support off? This often doubles the speed of pages loading under certain circumstances.

To do this, type about:config in the Firefox URL bar, and in the search box on the resulting page, type IPv6.  Double click network.dns.disableIPv6 to change it to true.  Restart Firefox (close *all* the windows, not just the current one).  You should notice a speed increase in loading pages.
