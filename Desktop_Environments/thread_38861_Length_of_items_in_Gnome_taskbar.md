### Post by Alexander Kirillov on 2005-06-03
[QUOTE=basementjack]Well. How do you do that? :-)[/QUOTE]
 Right-click on the taskbar "handle" (thin vertical stripe at the left end of the taskbar) and select Preferences->Size
BTW, the proper name is not taskbar but rather "Windows list applet" - it is an applet in the panel.
