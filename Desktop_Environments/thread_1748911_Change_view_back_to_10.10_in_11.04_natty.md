### Post by wittzi on 2011-05-04
Yeah that's how I did it.

- Press Windows Key (Super key in Linux)
- More Apps
- Click on All Applications in top right and select system
- Select Login Screen
- Click Unlock and enter your password
- Change back to Ubunto Classic

Note that to change back simply do the same in reverse!

I'm desperately trying to give Unity a chance, but even stuff like easily finding WINE wasn't happening for me so I switched to classic.
