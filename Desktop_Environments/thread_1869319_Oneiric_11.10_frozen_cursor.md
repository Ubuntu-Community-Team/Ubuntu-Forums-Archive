### Post by Cushie on 2011-11-03
All fixed, took 5 minutes, advice from here:-

[http://www.insidemylaptop.com/take-apart-acer-aspire-one-netbook/](http://www.insidemylaptop.com/take-apart-acer-aspire-one-netbook/)

Remove the battery!

Not exactly the AOA ZG5 but removing the keyboard and the lock on the Wide ribbon was identical.

I also removed the very short narrow cable protruding from the palmrest/touchpad using the same kind of lock bar.

After reinserting both cables and the battery, 'Voila' the Touchpad and Curser are back
:)
