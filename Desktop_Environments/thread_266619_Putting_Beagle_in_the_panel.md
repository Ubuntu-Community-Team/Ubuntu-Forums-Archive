### Post by croak77 on 2006-09-27
Install deskbar-applet. It's in all-in-one seach bar applet. You can launch program, search with Beagle, web search  and more. I believe the default keybinding is Alt-F3 but I'm not positive.

### Post by Wolki on 2006-09-27
Right-click on the Deskbar applet, preferences, enable Beagle live queries. Deskbar will then search with beagle and show that stuff that was found.
