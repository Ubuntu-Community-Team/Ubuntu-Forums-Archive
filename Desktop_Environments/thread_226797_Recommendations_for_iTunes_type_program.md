### Post by Third Thoughts on 2006-08-01
> **geovino said:**
> what do you mean by "universe" ?

Universe is one of the extra repositories. You have to enable it. Boot up synaptic then go Settings->Repositories.
Look for the entry that says
```

Ubuntu 6.06 LTS
Community maintained (Universe)

```
Check it and then search.
