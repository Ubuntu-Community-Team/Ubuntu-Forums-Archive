gibsonsimswilson, you've tagged your Ubuntu problem onto an existing thread that has been tagged **X**ubuntu. Not a good idea, different desktop, different problems and different solutions.

I  used to have Ubuntu installed on my netbook, until I replaced it with Lubuntu and frequently saw the same as you.

One trick while running Unity, is to move the window or panel down the screen and then move to the workspace below. You should see the bottom part of your window and any buttons or icons that were previously hidden will be visible. Move back to the original workspace when you've finished.

By the way, Lubuntu doesn't quite have the polished look that Ubuntu has but I've found it to be far more usable on a netbook.
