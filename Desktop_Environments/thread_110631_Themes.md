I'm not sure what process you are using to install themes. But, here's what you need to do:

[LIST]
[*]Download your theme to a directory in your **Home** folder. DO NOT unpack it.
[*]Go to **System > Preferences > Theme**
[*]Click on **Install Theme**
[*]Browse to where the downloaded theme is and click on **Open**
[*]The theme will install and you'll get a confirmation that it installed properly
[*]In your theme window, you may not see the theme if it was a Window Border or Icon theme package
[*]To activate the new theme, click on **Theme Details**
[*]Browse through the tabs for **Window Border** or **Icons** and you'll see your new theme. Click on it and you'll see an instant preview of the new theme.
[/LIST]

Hope that's helpful.
