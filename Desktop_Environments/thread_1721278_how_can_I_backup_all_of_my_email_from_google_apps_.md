### Post by chanwoman on 2011-04-26
The best way to set up a failover for Google apps is as follows:

* Set up a DropBox account in [https://www.dropbox.com/]("https://www.dropbox.com/"), you get 2GB free and it is the fastest one to sync, much better than Ubuntu One.  Once you have set it up, there will be a 'Dropbox' folder in your home directory.

*  Download [Backupgoo]("http://en.backupgoo.com/")and follow a few simple steps to set up and schedule your download.  Point your Backupgoo to your 'Dropbox' folder.

It is this simple, now all your precious doc, contacts, calendar and mail in Google App will be backuped up to your computer as well as to a cloud drive.... double failover, you will never lose anything again ever...
