### Post by lyricalpapa on 2006-11-24
what NVIDIA drivers are you running? You need the latest driver to run beryl or compiz.

you might try following this thread from PriceChild, it worked for me:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=263851&highlight=nvidia+drivers](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=263851&highlight=nvidia+drivers).

if you have a widescreen monitor, have you tried setting the resolution to normal screen settings, eg 1024x768, to see if the problem is with your video card or with your monitor setup.

another thing, my monitor doesn't put out fully correct information in the EDID.bin file when it is queried by the system, so I have to manually input the frequency ranges. The EDID problem is common, and the only workaround is to get the info from your manual and input it into your etc/X11/xorg.conf file. Note the difference in our files.
