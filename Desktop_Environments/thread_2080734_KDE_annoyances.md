I tested on  Kubuntu 12.10, but I do not think this is new...

If you right-click on any entry on the Kickoff Application Launcher, you should be able to add it to  (or remove it from) the Favorites, and sort the entries.

You can also click and drag to place your favorites in a custom sequence.
