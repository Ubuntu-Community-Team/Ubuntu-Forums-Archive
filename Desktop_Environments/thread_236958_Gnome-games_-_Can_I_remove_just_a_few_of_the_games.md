### Post by Arisna on 2006-08-15
You can use tha Alacarte menu editor to hide entries for the games you don't use.  Right click on your Application menu, go to Edit Menu, navigate to the Games section, and uncheck the boxes next to the games you wish to hide.  Would this be a good solution for you?

### Post by asplode on 2006-08-15
unfortunately, you cannot remove just one.  The previous suggestion to use Alacarte to remove them from your menu is as close to what you want as you're going to get without removing all of them.

They take up about 6 megabytes between gnome-games and gnome-games-data, so unless you're working with a 3gb hdd, you should be fine in that aspect.
