### Post by Joe_lurker on 2005-11-08
I thought that Evolution sync program sounded pretty cool so I thought I would try it out.  Unfortunatly the build was not going to go.  I had to do a bunch of tweaking but alsa I gave up.  I would wait awhile for this project to be complete.

It looks like you can just drag your calendar to your IPod and it will work.  You need to find your calendar in your home folder.  Open your home folder in Nautalus, then go to edit | show hidden files.  Browse to .evolution/calendar/local/system.  Now connect your IPod.  When the Nautalus window opens you should see a calendar folder.  Drag the evolution calendar to the ipod calendar folder.

It's a hack but it works.

The contacts you should be able to export and copy to the ipod.  However I am having trouble making that happen right now.  I will mess around with it for fun as I would like this feature as well.
