### Post by Mr. Shannon on 2011-07-16
If you are using the Ubuntu Classic desktop (not the Unity desktop) then you can get this by right clicking on the panel and then click Add to Panel.  Choose Sticky Notes and then click Add.  This will place a yellow icon on your panel.  To add a note to the desktop left click on the icon and choose new note.
