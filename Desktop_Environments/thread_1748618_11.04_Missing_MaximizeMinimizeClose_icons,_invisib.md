### Post by wkrekik on 2011-05-04
Compiz is indeed resource demanding. You can start compiz again with this command ```
compiz --replace &
```

If you want to reset your compiz settings, deleting this two hidden folders in your home, ~/.cache/compizconfig and 
~/.compiz, should do the job.
