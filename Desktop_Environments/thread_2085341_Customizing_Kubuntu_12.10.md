### Post by Erik1984 on 2012-11-18
For the global menu (I don't know about the rest of that Mac like setup):

1. Rightclick on the dektop > add widget
2. Look for the widget Window Menubar
3. Drag this widget to the panel where you want the global menu to appear and drop it there.
