### Post by 3Miro on 2010-11-09
Right-click on the menu and select edit. Then go to "System Tools" and select "Configuration Editor" (alternatively you can hit Alt + F2 and type gconf-editor). From the gconf-menu go to Apps -> Metacity -> General and select "compositing_manager". This will give you simple transparency without Visual Effects (compiz) being enabled.
