### Post by oobuntoo on 2006-09-27
I recently built a system using **AMD Athlon 64 X2 3800+ Socket 939 **and **ABIT AN8 32X Socket 939 NVIDIA nForce4 SLI X16**. Cost me more than $480 at the time just for these two parts from newegg. Now these two would only cost around $300.

When you build a new system, keep in mind that you may have to upgrade more than just MB and CPU. You may have to upgrade your graphic card, RAM, as well as power supply due to compatibility and stability reasons, depending on what you already have. It's always end up costing more than you wanted to.

If you only want MB+CPU that cost around $250, I would go with:
**ASUS M2N-SLI Deluxe Socket AM2 NVIDIA nForce 570** and
**AMD Athlon 64 X2 3800+ Windsor 2.0GHz Socket AM2**

Right now this combo would cost you around $290. Yeah, it's a little more than $250, but very decent combination. However, you will need DDR2 memory and PCI Express video card.
