### Post by maruchan on 2005-07-26
Is it possible to show all applications on the taskbar in any given virtual desktop, regardless of whether the applications are active in the current desktop? Ideally, then, you could click on one application and it would switch to the right desktop and show the app.

It's too easy for my wife to get confused when I tell her that "the web browser is open in the fourth desktop, the one on the far right." But she can easily find it if she sees it in the taskbar.

Thanks for any help.

### Post by varunus on 2005-07-26
Isn't that an option in the GNOME taskbar applet?  Just right click on the little tab thing at the left, choose properties, and choose "Show windows from all desktops."  It'll switch desktops automatically too, its nice.
