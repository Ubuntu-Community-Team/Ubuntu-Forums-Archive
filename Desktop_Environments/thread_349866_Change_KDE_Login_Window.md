### Post by Russty of Oz on 2007-01-30
Hmmm, it is there somewhere.  In mine it is under Actions.   If you can't find Settings there then look for Control Center.  If you still cant find it, you can right click on the task bar (or panel as they call it) and  Add Applet to Panel, scroll down to find one called Settings, click it then click Add to Panel.  Now you will have a spanner in your panel, click it and then point to System Administration then KDE Theme Manager.

Good luck.
