Hi guys: i need help on how to keep wallpaper to be saved after shutting down for next reboot,

### Post by egeezer on 2014-06-24
Download the wallpaper to your Pictures folder (you might even want to give it a different name) and in the Settings Manager specify Single Image and list the path to the choice. 

That way you get around the need to reconnect to the web to restore the wallpaper.
