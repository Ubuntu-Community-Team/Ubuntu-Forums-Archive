### Post by aramazan on 2005-04-02
[QUOTE=bored2k]sudo passwd[/QUOTE]
I prefer "sudo su -" for a root session. It both gives me what I want, and doesn't change the designed setup.

Perhaps a menu entry to run konsole in root mode is conceivable. Kanotix (and some others) provide this. Normal konsole is white-on-black, while root-konsole is black-on-yellow.

Pros: Cool.
Cons: Contradicts with, and lessens the effect of no-root strategy.
My resolution: Leave it as it is. Power users can always do "sudo" or "sudo passwd" or "sudo su -". For others let it stay as a ghost.
So: I've refuted my own suggestion. Forget it. :)
