### Post by nanotube on 2006-04-16
well, right click on your existing panel, and select "new panel" and shove it to the bottom.
once you have it, start adding items to it, by rightclicking, and selecting "add to panel"
you probably want the "show desktop" item (obvious), the "window list" item (that will show the open programs in the taskbar), the "trash" item (obvious again), and the "workspace switcher" (to switch between desktops). 

and i think that should do it. :)
