### Post by madjo on 2005-10-08
actually I had this same problem, but I also found the solution (at least it worked for me).

instead of using the submenu "Friendly name" to set your alias, go to Accounts, then click on the account for which you want to change your alias, and click "Modify"... there you can alter the alias. :)

hope this helps.
