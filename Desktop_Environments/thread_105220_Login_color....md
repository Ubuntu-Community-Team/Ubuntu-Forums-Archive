### Post by duminas on 2005-12-18
[QUOTE=yarjar]Whenever I log in, the splash screen appears, and there's a brown color behind it before it loads my wallpaper.  How do I change that brown color?[/QUOTE]I do not know the name for this program in the menus.

[list]Go into Applications > Accessories, and launch **Terminal**.
[*]Type **gnome-background-properties** and hit Enter.
[*]In the window that comes up, you should see a "Desktop Colors" section in the bottom part of the window. You can change if it's a color or a gradient, but hit the little box next to *Solid Color* to change the background color. You can also change your wallpaper from here if you like.[/list]

Hopefully that helped.
