### Post by ron177 on 2010-12-25
If anyone comes to this post I would like to inform you that I have resolved these questions on my own and through discussions with other people:

1. It's pretty easy. As easy as Mac. You go to "Keyboard" and its "Layout" tab. From there, you can add other languages such as Persian, Turkish, French, German, etc.

2. Open Office is pretty good for writing a dissertation although I am also playing with LaTeX. Both of these are very important for academic work. See another thread on academic writing in the humanities:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1650307](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1650307)

3. Yes. But I haven't learned how to work with them.
