### Post by vasa1 on 2012-09-16
Are you on Xfce 4.4? That's what the image title implies. If not, which version are you on?

If you're on a more recent version, you can try a more complete theme like [https://github.com/shimmerproject/Greybird]("https://github.com/shimmerproject/Greybird")Greybird. In any case, it seems you don't like the title bar appearance and that is controlled by the "Window Manager" which is found in Settings, Settings Manager, Window Manager. The dropdown on the left in the Window Manager should indicate the choices available to you.
