### Post by CantankRus on 2015-05-30
As of 14.04, the unity plugin draws the titlebars to display locally integrated menus.
It's not like before where you just replaced gtk-window-decorator with emerald.
You can't use emerald with unity as there isn't a separate application drawing the titlebar.
