### Post by 3Miro on 2011-01-19
Intel 82852/82855 is too old to give you good desktop effects. It is best for you to either upgrade your video card (with a newer and compatible one) or stick to No Effects.

Other than that, you can try installing Compiz-Config-Settings-Manager (CCSM) and disable most if not all effects/animations/transparencies. Hopefully this will lower the demand on the card and it will be able to handle the simple graphics.
