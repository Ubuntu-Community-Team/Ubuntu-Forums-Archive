### Post by AlfyBoy on 2010-07-06
I just had the same problem on Lucid, but the menu wasn't there:

[IMG]http://img202.imageshack.us/img202/8625/preferenciasdelaaparien.png[/IMG]

So, I went to:

[HTML] second click over the menu bar > Edit menus > Apps > System Tools > Checked the box of configuration editor[/HTML]

Then, go to 

[HTML]Apps > System Tools > Configuration Editor > /desktop/gnome/interface/menus_have_icons > and checked the box[/HTML]

[IMG]http://img12.imageshack.us/img12/7753/menushaveicons.png[/IMG]

You probably have to restart your App for the change to take effect or re-start session.

Problem solved.
