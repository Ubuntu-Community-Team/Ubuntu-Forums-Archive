### Post by pyromithrandir on 2006-06-07
Congrats on picking KDE! :) You made the right choice. ;)

XGL/Compiz isn't really very integrated into KDE yet. I am a die-hard KDE fan, and I thought "Man, that eye candy is pretty sweet!" But, when I installed it all on KDE, which I got to work after some difficulty (but that was like 2 months ago, and it's alpha software, so I'm sure it's gotten easier since then) I wasn't happy with the way it worked with KDE. You'll lose the little "Desktop Pager" functionality in the Kicker, and you'll wind up having to have the same desktop background on all your desktops.
I mean, it'll work, and if you're completely new to KDE, you won't notice the bits of lost functionality that I did, so go for it. But, personally, I'm waiting for it to get better integration with KDE before I start using it everyday.
