### Post by buhosabio2012 on 2012-11-28
I had the same problem too. After reinstalling Ubuntu 12.10 I copied back all my saved video files directly from my USB unit, but after that all thumbnails were gone. 

Try this, it worked for me: open **~/.cache** with Nautilus file manager and locate the **thumbnails** folder. Rename it or delete it. After that, open your video folder and that's it, the system will generate again the thumbnails folder and all thumbnail files within. It just works great!
