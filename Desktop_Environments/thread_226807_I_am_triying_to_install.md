### Post by jISh on 2006-07-31
Can you tell us how much RAM you have? 
Mainly, the LiveCD does not work for everyone, personally I don't like installers that require you to boot into the OS first! The LiveCD is great for demo and some functionality, but NOT installing!

I recommend, since this isn't working for you, to get the Alternate Install CD. It has a text based installer that is still quite easy to use, although you may stumble on a few questions. It's much more efficient, and is designed for people who's LiveCD installer fails.

Good luck!
