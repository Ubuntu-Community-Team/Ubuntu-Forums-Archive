### Post by doclivingston on 2005-06-22
I'm fairly sure that icons do appear in the workspace switcher, if it's big enough.

At with two rows in the workspace switcher, on a 24 pixel panel I don't get icons. However if I have 1 row on the same panel, or two rows on a 36 pixel panel, I get icons.
