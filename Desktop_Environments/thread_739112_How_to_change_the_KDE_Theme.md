### Post by urukrama on 2008-03-29
You could use qt-config-qt3 or even kcontrol to set the qt/kde theme. Both are in the repositories. Kcontrol controls much more than the theme, and requires some additional kde parts installed (kicker and perhaps more, I can't remember).

You can find more kde themes on kde-look.org.
