### Post by promet on 2011-04-16
I think that you actually can do that. If you look in the "Main Menu Tool" it's, basically, a "Tree" view. So, you can mess around with apps inside the major categories. If you want to change the visibility of the categories themselves though, scroll up to the "root" entry, which is "Applications" at the top of the left hand navigation screen. Then, in the right, you'll see the major categories themselves and be able to mess around with those. Check it out...
