### Post by mcpish on 2006-08-16
I dislike the new default look of Xubuntu.  But it is easy to switch back to the regular XFCE look.  
 
I may be in the minority, but I really used to love CDE back in the 90s.  I'm really not a fan of this idea that "Linux needs to emulate either Windows or Macintosh".  This was one of the main reasons why I was attracted to XFCE, because it tried to emulate the behavior of CDE.  
 
Icons on the desktop are "minimized applications", A single tool bar on the bottom with launchers, a right-click app menu, a centre-click currently running applications menu.  That's the way I like it.
