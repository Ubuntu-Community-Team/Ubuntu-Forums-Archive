### Post by dbaas41 on 2009-03-17
If you're looking at the ccsm main window, scroll further down the page and under utilities there's an option for Cube Caps.  Make sure that the little box is checked, then click on the actual icon.  From there, you can specify both a top and a bottom image.

Hope this helps.
