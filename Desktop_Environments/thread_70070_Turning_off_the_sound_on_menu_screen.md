### Post by twowheeler on 2005-09-28
Not sure what sounds you are talking about.  I don't get anything like a beep at startup.  However when gdm starts it plays a little drumming sound.  Could that be it?  This is controlled by System --> Administration --> Login Screen Setup.  Go to the Accessibility tab and turn off the 'Make a sound when the login window is ready' option.
