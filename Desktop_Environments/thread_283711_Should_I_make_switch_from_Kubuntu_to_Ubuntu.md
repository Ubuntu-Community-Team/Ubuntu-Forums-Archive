### Post by jaytek13 on 2006-11-03
> **aysiu said:**
> Gnome has more support.

This is kind of misleading. While there are more Gnome/Ubuntu users overall than kde/kubuntu users, you will not be lacking support. Many problems associated with these distros are one in the same, and most kubuntu users are on these forums alongside the ubuntu users (which is why there is less activity on the kubuntu forums). 

It does come down to a choice, though. Some enjoy Gnome more because of it's simplicity, while others despise it for the same reason. Some enjoy KDE because it is full featured and has an option for nearly everything you could want, while others hate it for that same reason.

It's nothing but a personal decision, and you won't be lacking support no matter what you choose.
