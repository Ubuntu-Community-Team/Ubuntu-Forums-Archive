### Post by dmizer on 2006-05-29
this is probably a sound related issue.  how much tweaking have you done with your sound setup?

tried anything you can find here: [http://easylinux.info/wiki/Ubuntu#How_to_configure_sound_to_work_properly_in_GNOME](http://easylinux.info/wiki/Ubuntu#How_to_configure_sound_to_work_properly_in_GNOME)

followed by:
```
sudo nano /etc/libao.conf
```
and change esd to alsa.  save with ctrl+x
reboot and see if that helps any of your problems.
