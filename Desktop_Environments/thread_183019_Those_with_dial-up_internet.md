### Post by Lin-X on 2006-05-27
Trying to download software with a dial-up connection can be a real pain. Those who would like to try another solution might go to Linux-mag.com and look at back issues. There is one that comes with dvd of the Badger and a ton of programs to go with it. I haven't seen a list of the stuff on the dvd, but it is a lot. You'd have to pay about $15 but it might be worth a try for people who have crappy internet service. I live in a rural area where dial-up is the only option and the phone service can be ...
ahem ... less than professional grade. I'm giving some serious thought to the dvd; it would make my life a lot easier.:neutral:
