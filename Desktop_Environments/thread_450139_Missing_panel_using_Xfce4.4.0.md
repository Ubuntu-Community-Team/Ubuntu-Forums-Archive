### Post by Yggdrasill on 2007-05-21
My problem started after adding "quicklauncher" to my xfce-panel.  I added a new program to the panel, but the icon wasn't assigned a program to launch.  I clicked on the icon and my panel disappeared.  So I opened Terminal and typed "xfce4-panel" and my customized panel returned; however, when I end the Terminal, my panel disappears again.  Also, I added some icons to /usr/share/icons and when I applied the icons, all my Desktop icons disappeared.  What could be the problem?

Thanks in advance.

---

### Post by FuturePilot on 2007-05-21
When you launch things from the terminal and then close it, most of the time the program you launched is terminated. Instead press <Alt>F2 and then type in xfce4-panel.

I'm not sure about the icons though.
