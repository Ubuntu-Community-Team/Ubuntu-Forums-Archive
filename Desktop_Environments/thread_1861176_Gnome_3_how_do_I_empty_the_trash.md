### Post by crdlb on 2011-10-15
What are you using? GNOME Shell? Fallback mode? In fallback mode, there is still a trash applet available for the panel. In Shell, you can open the file manager from the dash and click on trash.

You could also turn on the trash desktop icon in gnome-tweak-tool.

### Post by Seb1982 on 2011-10-15
Problem solved! I installed gnome-tweak-tool and aktivated the trash bin.
