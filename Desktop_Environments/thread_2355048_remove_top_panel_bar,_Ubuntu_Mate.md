### Post by howefield on 2017-03-09
Right clicking the panel should give you a "*Delete Panel*" option. After deleting one of the panels the option will ghost out to prevent you from (easily) deleting both panels.

Might be easier to delete the bottom panel and then move the top one to the bottom, depending on the applets that you want running on it.
