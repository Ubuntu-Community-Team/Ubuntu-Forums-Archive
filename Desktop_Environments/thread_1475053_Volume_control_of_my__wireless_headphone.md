### Post by funicorn on 2010-05-06
Hi guys,

I'm using Ubuntu 10.04 upgraded from 9.10 directly. Before upgrade my 2.4G wireless headphone works fine. When I toggle the volume controller (a electronic trigger, not  mechanical wheel), the volume  goes up or down with a beautiful gnome-osd volume bar displayed. But  now the headphone's volume control does not work any more.  There is no osd display, and no change in the system volume. Does anybody know why this happens and how to fix it ? 

Many thanks.

---

### Post by funicorn on 2010-05-06
Well I found the reason. I manually changed the shortcut key of gnome volume rising and lowering.  Changing it back to the default value XF86AudioVolume... (for me I toggle the volume controller on my headphone) solves the problem.
