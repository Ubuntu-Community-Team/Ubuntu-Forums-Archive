### Post by pgibson on 2010-11-13
Addendum:

I appreciate all the eyes that have given this a look.  Clearly it's a Bona Fide Mystery of No Small Import that the Ubuntu monks have to wrestle with. 

I ended up simply installing Xubuntu 10.04.1.  Although enabling Xubuntu's own compositor caused the same sort of display and interaction problems noted in the bug, Xubuntu's window's manager basic windows manager still allows the user to have multiple desktop views that he or she can switch between simply by sliding the mouse off one edge or another.  (I looked, but it appeared that sans Compiz, Ubuntu remains pretty clunky).

My daughter is delighted with Xubuntu.  She expressed disappointment at losing her rotating desk cube, but was thrilled that she didn't have to be, as she put it "stuck in one window" when she "messes around on her computer."

So, moderators please mark this as solved, I suppose. My thanks to everyone who took an interest and a gander at my problem, and my apologies to Compiz whom I inadvertently singled out in the title of my original post.
