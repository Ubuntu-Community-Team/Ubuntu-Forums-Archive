### Post by jem7v on 2007-04-15
NoteEdit might work.  Lilypond has a way to export MIDI, but Lilypond has a steep learning curve on its own... however, it integrates nicely with NoteEdit, so giving the two of them a try together would probably be smart.

[https://canorus.berlios.de/wiki/index.php/Main_Page](https://canorus.berlios.de/wiki/index.php/Main_Page) is NoteEdit's successor, but I haven't tried it yet and it's not in the repositories at all.  However, they DO have an Ubuntu-specific .deb on their site if you want to try it out.
