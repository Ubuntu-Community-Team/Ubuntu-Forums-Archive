### Post by blazemore on 2008-07-17
Yes it can be done. Firstly, you need to find the background of the mac menubar, you can do this by Google searching for a screenshot of Mac, and then cutting out a slice of menu bar. Then right-click the panel and choose properties->background. Selects Background Image, and browse to the image of the menu bar you made.

Alternatively, you could try and find a GTK/Metacity theme (I don't know which, but if you want a mac clook, you'd better get both) on wwww.gnome-look.org
