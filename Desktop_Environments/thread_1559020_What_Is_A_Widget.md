### Post by Ginsu543 on 2010-08-23
1. You use Lock to keep the icon from moving (can't drag with your mouse). Sticky means that the icon will show up on all your Desktops, not just your current one.

2-3. You use Widget to put the particular screenlet to the Widget layer (you must first activate the Widget layer using either Compizconfig Settings Manager or Ubuntu Tweak). It will be invisible to your normal Desktop, but if you activate the Widget layer by pressing F9 (default keyboard shortcut) or moving your mouse to the proper hotspot, the applet will become visible.
