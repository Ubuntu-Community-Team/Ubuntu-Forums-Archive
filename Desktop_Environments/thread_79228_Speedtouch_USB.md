### Post by afonic on 2005-10-19
Its simple why it doesn't work. To get connected via USB with this modem you have to install its drivers in window, no? Well, it seems there are not drivers for this for linux (or you may have to download them).

If I remember well, this modem except the USB port also has an ethernet one. If this is correct, and if your PC has a network adapter (most PCs have them on-board), just connect the modem via ethernet. Internet will then, most likely, be  automatically configured.
