### Post by schnelle on 2010-02-14
KDE 4 is all about widgets. You can add widgets to desktop and to panels. So you just need to unlock them (right mouse click and choose "unlock widgets") and do with them what ever you want (add, remove, move them). So you can remove device notifier and indicator display icons (widgets) from panel. The first icon image you attached is part of system tray and I think it cannot be removed.
