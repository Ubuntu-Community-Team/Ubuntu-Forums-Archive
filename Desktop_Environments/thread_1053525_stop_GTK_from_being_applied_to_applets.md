### Post by JackTheDipper on 2009-01-29
Adapting the background color with GNOME Color chooser is only a workaround in this case, but you cannot gain real transparency this way.

If some applets are not transparent unlike the rest of the panel, it's a bug of your GTK+ theme. Please try an official one (like the original Clearlooks theme) and see if this problem is gone. After this step you can of course use GNOME Color Chooser to change that working theme to look more like your old, buggy theme *G*

Best regards,
JackTheDipper ;-)
