### Post by Rasa1111 on 2011-05-12
Ok..

This is the default "Login Screen" Icon...
[ATTACH]191974[/ATTACH]

So if you can find that icon amongst your applications, Just click it and you should get the login screen. 

I also just checked out ( for the first time) the method mentioned by Krytarik..
and that seems like the easiest way probably. 

These few steps should get you there..
(if the french thing doesnt get in our way again) lol

1. Log out of the desktop. 
2. Once at the Login screen, Click the user-name to login to. 
(but don't give password yet)
3. Look at the very bottom of the screen for a box/bar that says "Ubuntu"
4. Click the little arrow next to where it says "Ubuntu", and a list will pop up. 
5. Find in the list "Ubuntu Classic", and click on it, So that it shows 'Ubuntu Classic" in the box. 
6. Type in password to login,  and press enter.
When the desktop loads up, you should be back to the 'classic style" with no launcher,
and with a panel on the top and bottom.
