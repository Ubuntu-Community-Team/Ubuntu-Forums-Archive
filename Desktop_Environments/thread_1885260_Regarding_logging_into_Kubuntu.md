### Post by LinuxFan999 on 2011-11-22
You should not install kubuntu-desktop in Ubuntu/Xubuntu/Lubuntu, as kubuntu-desktop and ubuntu-desktop/xubuntu-desktop/lubuntu-desktop and their associated applications can conflict with each other. You should have just installed Kubuntu from the Kubuntu iso image, which you can download at the Kubuntu website.
