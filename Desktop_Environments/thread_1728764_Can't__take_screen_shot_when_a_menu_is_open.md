### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-04-14
What do you mean "when you try to take the shot?" 

Did you,

1. Select the application,

2. Set a time limit,

3. Click "Take Screenshot,"

4. Activate your menu?

Give yourself more than 3 seconds - 10 would probably be best. Then activate whatever menu you're trying to get a shot of and don't touch anything until the ten seconds are up. = )
