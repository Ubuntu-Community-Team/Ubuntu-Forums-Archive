### Post by BigBananaMan on 2009-12-22
Although technically you cannot animate the menu titles, you could try to fake the funk.  Have the whole background as an animated gif.  Create false titles out of flaming words or whatever you want in the gif.  Then have the real titles over the fake titles and give them 0 opacity.  Make the before and after click to be both 0 or have the after click with 255 opacity and read "you clicked here" or whatever you want.  You could figure out where the real titles are going to be by making a menu using a test grid pattern or something so you know where on the gif to position the false titles or just trial and error it until it works.  This of course could not be used to make the words explode when clicked or something cool like that but it's better than nothing.

Edit: I just tried using an animated gif myself and it didn't work.  Sorry, you'll have to try something else like mandvd.  I couldn't get mandvd to do it(your video must be between 25-30 frames per second) but it's supposed to and I'm out of time so maybe you'll have more luck.
