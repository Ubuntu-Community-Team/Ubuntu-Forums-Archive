### Post by Bucky Ball on 2011-12-31
Just get a Grateful Dead pic and add that to your screensavers. You can use whatever pic you want. If it doesn't fit as you'd like (or looks crap when stretched or scaled) then resize the pic with Gimp. ;)

In the 'Advanced' tab choose 'Use random image'.
