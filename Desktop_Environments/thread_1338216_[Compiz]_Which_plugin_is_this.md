### Post by jonnjonzzn on 2009-11-26
I believe you're experiencing the "Scale" plugin.  Perhaps the easiest way to modify its behavior would be to install simple-ccsm and then have a look at the "Edges" tab.  Or use compizconfig-settings-manager (ccsm) and adjust the "Scale" properties.
