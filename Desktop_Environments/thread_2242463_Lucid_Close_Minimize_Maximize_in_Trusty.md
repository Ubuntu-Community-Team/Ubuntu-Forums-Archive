### Post by Frogs Hair on 2014-09-02
[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/light-themes](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/light-themes)

Download the light-themes 0.1.66 tar.gz and extract the package in downloads. Rename the theme folders so you can differentiate them from the current light-themes.

Example: Ambiance Old

Move the themes to .themes or usr/share/themes and apply with the Unity Tweak Tool or Gnome Tweak Tool. [U]The 10.04 themes are GTK 2
and might not work at all [/U]
