### Post by Claus7 on 2009-04-22
Hello and welcome as I see that this is your first post!

I just came accross your post searching something else. I'm not able to give you a firm answer to your problem, because I have never faced exactly the same problem and I'm not experienced in Chinese.

I had problems with the hellenic (greek) language and from what I can get it seems that you have an encoding problem. Can you open vi(m) and paste the text from amsn? Does vi(m) recognises the characters you want? Have you installed via synaptic all the packages that concern your language?

If vi does recognise the characters you want, without a doubt (I think) gedit has the problem. In order to be able to see the characters you have to change the encoding of the file after you save it with the ???? characters. What I did in the hellenic language is in this thread:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=748249&highlight=encoding](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=748249&highlight=encoding)

In general I try to avoid gedit, because I have seen that vi opens every text that I cannot read in gedit (up to now!).

Hope you find a solution to this,
