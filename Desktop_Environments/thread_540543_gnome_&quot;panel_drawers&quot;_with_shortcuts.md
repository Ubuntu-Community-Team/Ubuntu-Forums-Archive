### Post by ray bot on 2007-09-04
Right-click the drawer and click on "Add to Drawer".  A window should appear with 2 buttons at the top: "Application Launcher" and "Custom Application Launcher".  Click on "Application Launcher" to add an app that's already in your Applications menu, or "Custom Application Launcher"  to add a custom command.
