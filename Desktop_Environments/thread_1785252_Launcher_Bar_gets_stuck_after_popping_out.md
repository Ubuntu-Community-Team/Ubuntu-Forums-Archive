### Post by cybrsaylr on 2011-06-25
Haven't solved this problem yet but did find a workaround that works well with Opera.

I reset my 'preferences' so that when Opera is launched it continues from where I was last. Then when the launch bar pops out and stays stuck, all I do is close Opera, then relaunch Opera and launch bar auto-hides again till the next time it gets stuck out again. 

This way Opera opens again with all the tabs last used intact.

### Post by parsim on 2011-07-05
[This](https://bugs.launchpad.net/unity/+bug/763275/comments/4) is a good workaround:
> drag an icon from the desktop over the panel and back again.
After that, it's working normal.
I have to hover the icon over the panel for a few seconds, until the bar fully retracts, but after that the Unity bar operates normally.
