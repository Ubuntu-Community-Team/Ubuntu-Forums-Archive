For Firefox, save .mozilla from your home directory to a CD-R, etc.  I've never run Kontact before, but its settings might lie somewhere within .kde.

And if you have /home as its own partition, you can preserve the desired files and folders upon a reinstall and only overwrite / , /boot, etc.
