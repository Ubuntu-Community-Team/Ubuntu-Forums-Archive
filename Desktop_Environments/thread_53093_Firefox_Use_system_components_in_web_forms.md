### Post by z-vet on 2005-07-30
You need to install gtk2-engines-gtk-qt, then you will have a 'GTK Styles and Fonts' entry in your 'Control Center > Appearance & Themes'. Just check 'Use my KDE style...' and 'Use my KDE font..' there and enjoy :)
