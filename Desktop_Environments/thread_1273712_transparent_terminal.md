### Post by wojox on 2009-09-23
You can create a new profile with Edit > Profiles and configure it there.

---

### Post by zeroseven0183 on 2009-09-24
To give you the detail of what Master Yoda answered:

1. Right-click on any space in the Terminal
2. point to **Profiles** then select **Profile Preferences**
3. under **Background** tab, tick the **Transparent background** option then
4. adjust the slider until your desired transparency
