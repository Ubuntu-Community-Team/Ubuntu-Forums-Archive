I'm trying to configure KDE in v12.04.  I want a quiet startup - no sound effects at all during login or startup of the desktop session. ### Post by Grafens on 2013-07-09
System settings > application and system notification > manage notifications > player settings > select 'no audio output'
This will apply to all system sounds though not just startup

---

### Post by DeathByDenim on 2013-07-09
And in case you only wish to disable the login/logoff sounds, you can do so under System settings > application and system notification > manage notifications. In the dropdown menu of programs chose "KDE workspace" and then select "Log off" in the list below, you can then disable it by removing the tick at "Play a sound". Same for "Log in".
I admit it's very hidden. It always takes me a while to find too after a new install.
