### Post by macogw on 2009-08-24
It can show one message-type balloon and one system-type balloon (ex: volume up button pressed) at a time.  This is to prevent the following:
1) wall of text (ie. the balloons cover 1/4 of the screen and you can't read what's behind them)
2) trying to read 5 things at once and failing

So no, no way to change that.
