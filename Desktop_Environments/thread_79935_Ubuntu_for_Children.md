### Post by matthew on 2005-10-24
My kids have my old computer. I installed Edubuntu on it (nicer wallpaper and theme for kids and comes with a lot of the educational stuff and games already set up, otherwise it is Ubuntu Breezy). By default there are a lot of fun things in there like gCompris, KTuberling, TuxPaint and lots of good educational apps that start with k.

Then I went into synaptic and did searches for child, kid, children, kids, and junior and installed just about everything that was related to those terms. I also looked at everything that came up under game and games and chose appropriately.

Anyway, my kids love it and there are lots of great apps/games for them with a fun look.
