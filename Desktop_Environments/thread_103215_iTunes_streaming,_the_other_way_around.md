### Post by chele on 2006-01-03
Just a note to say that the protocol for this is DAAP. I am using both the server and client on Linux, no iTunes anywhere in sight.

On Linux, I have Avahi, mt-daapd, and Rhythmbox 0.9.2 running, allowing music to stream everywhere my wireless LAN will reach. I was even able to listen to the music my neighbour was sharing via his airport express, without any propietary software in sight! Too cool!
