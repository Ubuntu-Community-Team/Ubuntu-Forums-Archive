### Post by ron177 on 2010-12-10
For those who may read this later: you go to 

system > preferences > keyboard

and under "layout" tab "add" a language of your choice. Once you do this, you'll get a tab on the upper right side of your desktop that is quite similar to the language tab in OS X. Whenever you want to switch between languages you simply go to this tab and change it to the appropriate language.
