### Post by jclmusic on 2008-01-30
go to [http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org), [http://www.kde-look.org](http://www.kde-look.org), [http://art.gnome.org](http://art.gnome.org), or copy them from your /usr/share/themes directory.

### Post by x1a4 on 2008-01-30
Ubuntu also has clipart you can install and use.  Install the **openclipart-png** and **openclipart-svg** packages.

EDIT: [OpenClipart.org]("http://openclipart.org/") is also a good source of public domain graphics.
