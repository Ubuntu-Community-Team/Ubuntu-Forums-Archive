### Post by PhantmKllr on 2011-05-11
Gcursor is not required to change the mouse cursor. You can do that in the Appearance Preferences.

Simply right-click on the Desktop, click "Change Desktop Background", and select the "Theme" tab. Then click "Customize", and select the "Pointer" tab.
