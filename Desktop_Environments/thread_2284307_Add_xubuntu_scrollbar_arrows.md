### Post by Dennis N on 2015-06-28
The arrows on the scrollbars come with the theme you use. You can't add them onto an existing theme. Go to All Settings > Appearance, and choose a different 'Style' that provides the arrows. In Xubuntu 14.04, I see Albatross and Bluebird have arrows, and the older style Raleigh as well. Alternate themes with arrows can be also found on gnome-look.org.
