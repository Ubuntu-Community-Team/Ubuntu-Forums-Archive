### Post by prettysum on 2011-10-08
I totally agree on the saying, "Time is worth more than money." It really changed what I think of what I should be doing with my time. That's why we have to track time and do it right. There are lots of tools for time tracking but most of them have flaws. Some real time tracking solution relies on the user entering the details of the task they are working on, as they are working. Unless prompted, most employees will never remember to track their time or even enter the task they are working on. They usually forget tracking their time and what happens is they try to guess what did during the day. And this isn't accurate time tracking. What I suggest is you do some research first and carefully decide on what tool to use.
To start off, you could read [**this article**](http://www.timedoctor.com/blog/2010/11/16/5-reasons-why-most-time-tracking-software-is-flawed) for additional info.
