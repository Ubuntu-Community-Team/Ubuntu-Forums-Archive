### Post by adamkane on 2006-07-31
Realplayer is one of those apps that suffers from lack of Universe support.

gxine plays realmedia. You can use gxine along with the multimediaconnectivity firefox plugin to play your radio streams with ease. You will have to set a few options MMC, so have some patience.

The best solution is to find a MP3 BBC radio station on PublicRadioFan.com and paste the stream into amaroK to play streams on demand.

You really should avoid Realplayer.
