### Post by d3v1ant_0n3 on 2006-12-20
> **nami said:**
> you can use any old picture, just tick the option which says "animated skydome".

Not *any* exactly- the picture needs to be in .png or .svg format, and sized in a power of 2 way (1024*512px, 2048*1024, 1024*1024 etc.). The bigger picture you use, the better.

Whoever asked about taking screenshots, there's a plugin built into beryl for it (which I never got to work. Or, you can rotate the cube manually, take your fingers off of CTRL+ALT, and press the PrtSc key.
