### Post by Blasphemist on 2011-09-22
This really doesn't help me understand what the condition is now for you. But, at the login screen, key in the password and before you press enter, look at the bottom of the screen and chose classic as the session type. This setting is sticky meaning it will be the default after making the choice once. If you are using auto login and therefor don't get the login screen, do describe what is happening now.
