### Post by Motoxrdude on 2007-02-13
Well, typically could be possible, but highly unlikely. In order to get high enough voltages to the monitor to fry it, a short would have been needed to jump circuits. Even in this case however, the traces would have fried. But it could have been a one in a million chance that the traces stayed long enough to minister a lethal dose of amps/volts to your monitor.
