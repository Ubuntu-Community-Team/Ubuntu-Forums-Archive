### Post by Marauder1 on 2004-12-07
You can use Nautilus, the Gnome file browser.

Open your home directory
press ctrl+L and enter burn:/// ( a new Nautilus window should open)
drag and drop all the stuff you need to put.
Press the right button and select burn a cd.
Pick your options and then burn.
It will ask you a cd or dvd (if its a re-writable it will erase right away for you)
And voila !!! all burn like mama use to make !!!
