### Post by undecim on 2009-10-14
You can set an image with Skydome. It's in the appearance tab of the Desktop Cube plugin. If you have a 360 degree panorama, you can also select the Animate option there to have it wrap around the 3d area.

Also look at the "Cube Reflection and Deformation" plugin. Instead of a background, it gives a more 3D feel to your cube when rotating
