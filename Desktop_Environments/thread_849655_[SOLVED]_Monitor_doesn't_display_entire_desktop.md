### Post by Arie123 on 2008-07-04
Hi,

My screen cuts off a part of the desktop on the right side. For instance, if I run Firefox maximized the close button is only partially displayed. On the left side there is a black column of 'unused monitor space'. If I adjust the horizontal position of my screen more to the left, it reveals a part of the missing desktop area, but still not all of it. 

General info:
I'm using Ubuntu 8.04 (2.6.24-19-generic)
My monitor is a Dell E151FP
My graphics card is Nvidia GeForce MX 420 

What can I do?

---

### Post by lukjad on 2008-07-04
Try adjusting the Horizontal and Vertical size and position with your screen buttons. Once that is done, fiddle around with your screen resolution.

---

### Post by Arie123 on 2008-07-04
Wow, fast answer! Thanks!

Actually, I found out it was caused by adjusting the refresh rate of the monitor from 75 Hz to 60 Hz. Now the desktop fits neatly inside the monitor...
