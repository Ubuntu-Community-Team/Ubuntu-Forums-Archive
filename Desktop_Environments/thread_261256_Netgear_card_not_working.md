### Post by karamba_kid on 2006-09-20
I recently purchased a card with an Atheros ar5212 chipset.  I was just looking for a no hassle card that offered all the features of the MadWifi drivers, and so far it's been working just great :-D  Ubuntu just needs the restricted-drivers and then it works. Since I knew I would be using madwifi drivers I looked for my card at the hardware compatibility list found on the madwifi wiki.
