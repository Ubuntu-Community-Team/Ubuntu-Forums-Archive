### Post by dtfinch on 2010-06-28
If you want near-immediate Firefox updates, adding "ppa:ubuntu-mozilla-security/ppa" to your Software Sources should do the trick.

[https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-mozilla-security/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-mozilla-security/+archive/ppa)

3.6.4 came out only a week ago. They skipped over 3.6.5, and 3.6.6 only increases the default unresponsive plugin timeout to satisfy a bunch of Farmville users. I haven't used Farmville, but apparently it freezes many browsers for over 10 seconds on load, and everyone's fine with that, but they're not fine with their browser alerting them to that fact.
