### Post by MikeMLP on 2006-08-12
It is a version of Ubuntu specifically targeted at schools.  It probably shouldn't mess up any of your files and settings but will likely replace the look of your Ubuntu desktop, and add some applications as well (perhaps though an alternate session) just as installing kubuntu-desktop would do.  Check the links below for more information.

look here: [https://wiki.edubuntu.org/EdubuntuWiki]("https://wiki.edubuntu.org/EdubuntuWiki")
and here: [http://www.edubuntu.org/]("http://www.edubuntu.org/")
