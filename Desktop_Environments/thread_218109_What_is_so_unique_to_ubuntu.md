### Post by rattlerviper on 2006-07-18
Best things that stand out about Ubuntu

1.  The support here, it allows noobs to get it working right. a little searching and the answers pop up on what to do.  Worst case scenario post your problem and wait 24 hours.

2.  The software just worked on my computer only problem was my printer, fixed that with the help of #1.  I think more hardware covered in the debian based distros than the others.  This may only be my view, but Suse, Redhat and numerous others never just worked.

3.  Upgrading to new operating system only requires 4 clicks. 1 to tell it to upgrade, one to tell it yes upgrade now, after a beer and barbaque one click to tell it to go ahead and delete the old apps, and a final click to restart.  BANG upgraded! Try that in Windows.

4.  The support here!!!
