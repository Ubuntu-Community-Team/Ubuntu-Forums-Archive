### Post by fabounet on 2007-08-14
Hello everyone !
I've resurected the famous but sadly abandonned gnome-dock.
It's been 1 month since I've started, and from the beginning I've put my work on the [french Ubuntu Forum]("http://forum.ubuntu-fr.org/viewtopic.php?pid=1101922#p1101922"), and written [a doc]("http://doc.ubuntu-fr.org/gnome_dock") too (in french, but the installation part is at the beginning, and is easily guessable).

of course it's not perfect yet (not even finished), but it's already being used daily by many ubuntu users (Ubuntu, Xubuntu for sure, with Beryl or xcompmgr, and Kubuntu should works fine too).

Main features are :
you can place it anywhere on the screen, horizontal/vertical,  it has drawers to not take too much place,  a carousel-like view to take even less place,  is fully themable,  has many animations,  and the cherry on the cake, a set of pre-installed themes to start using it immediately !

Feel free to try it, thanks for giving me your opinion or some new ideas for the future. :)
