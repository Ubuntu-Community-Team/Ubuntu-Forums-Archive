### Post by RaZe42 on 2009-05-31
I take you're using the emerald version of Elegant Brit. Try to open the Emerald Theme Manager(System->Preferences) and edit the theme there.
Go to the Frame/Shadows tab and try to edit the Frame Borders values, specifically the Top Border value.

In case you didn't know the pattern(reflection thingie) on the window decorations is from the Compiz plugin "Reflection"

EDIT: Judging from your post in the screenshot thread you seem to have worked it out :)
