### Post by aysiu on 2006-04-27
The Wiki instructions don't overwrite Ubuntu's Firefox with Mozilla's Firefox.

They install Mozilla's Firefox *alongside* Ubuntu's and then symlink the launcher from Mozilla's to Ubuntu's and redirect Ubuntu's launcher elsewhere.

If you followed the Wiki instructions, you have *two* Firefoxes installed--1.0.7 and 1.5.0.2. By upgrading to 1.0.8, you're not affecting the 1.5.0.2 version.
