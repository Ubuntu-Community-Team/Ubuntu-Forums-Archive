### Post by KdotJ on 2010-08-11
It is easily possible for you to do this...

At the login screen, select your name but do not sign in yet...

... at the bottom of the screen there is a little menu called "Sessions", simply select GNOME from this menu. Then continue to log in as normal with your password (if you have to enter it)
