### Post by Simian Man on 2011-04-18
> **c@ssie said:**
> 1. Fix the button order to be like GNOME
You mean that Applications/Places/System thing?  There's no quick way to do that, but you could create three menu launchers where each has the things you want in them.  I personally hate that split menu and used the combined one even on Gnome.

> **c@ssie said:**
> 2. Make the mouse tracking/acceleration like Mac OSX set to fastest.
I've never used OSX, but you can configure mouse acceleration under System Settings->Input Devices->Mouse.

> **c@ssie said:**
> 3.  Make the wait curser spin instead of bounce
You can't make it spin, but you have some options other than bounce under System Settings->App and Sys Notifications->Launch Feedback.

> **c@ssie said:**
> 4. Get Mac/Gnome like visual effects
If you mean the desktop effects, you can configure them under System Settings->Desktop Effects.  There are at least as many options here as Gnome/OSX.

> **c@ssie said:**
> 5.  Make the plasma panel look/feel like Gnome Panel
How exactly?  If you want two, that can easily be done.  If you mean make it look as ugly as the Gnome panel, you'll have to search for an ugly Plasma theme :).

> **c@ssie said:**
> 6.  replace the ugly bright/glossy/transparent oxygen/air look with something with simple elegance like Aqua/Equinox
I quite like Oxygen, but Bespin and QTCurve are also good.  Unlike Gnome, each KDE theme is quite configurable so you can configure your theme to look better without needing to install a whole new theme.  Look under System Settings->Application Appearance and [KDE Look]("http://kde-look.org/") to get started :).
