### Post by CylnZ on 2010-03-04
Yep, I was searching google for exactly the same thing when I found your thread... No luck yet

Found a good solution. I was thinking of an icon like a widget, which is apparently wrong. Just add the quicklauncher as a widget to your screen, then add your apps as launchers in it. Size the icons as you like and lock the widgets. All done, log in / out as you like and it all stays just the way you set it.
