### Post by benbelly on 2008-03-06
I used to have a little part of the top panel where 'closed' applications would display their icons.  I accidentally closed it.  Things like Deluge and Picasa.  This isn't the window-switcher where minimized apps go.  Can anyone tell me how to get my icons back?

---

### Post by jayde6 on 2008-03-06
If you right click on an empty spot on the panel, and go to add to panel, there should be an applet called notification area. Drag that to where you want on the panel and it should show the icons again.

### Post by santiagoward2000 on 2008-03-06
Hi!
Right-click on the panel, select **Add new item** and look for the tray there. (I'm using Xfce, so the names may be a little different)
Good luck!
