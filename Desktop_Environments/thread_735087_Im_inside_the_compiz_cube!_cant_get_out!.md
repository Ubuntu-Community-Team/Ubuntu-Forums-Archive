### Post by Anubisxian on 2008-03-25
A quick check is to go into "Cube Behavior" under the effects menu and make sure "Inside Cube" is _un_checked. I found that a restart (logoff/logon) was required for that particular change to take effect. I have read, however that a full reboot is sometimes needed.

I've gotten a lot of help from the Compiz configuration forums as well.  You can find them [**_here_**]("http://forum.compiz-fusion.org/forumdisplay.php?f=109").

Cheers!

### Post by Anubisxian on 2008-03-26
Sorry, I'm running Gutsy -- I overlooked your specs.

But anyway, within the CompizConfig Settings Manager I take the following path:

Under "Desktop"
   choose "Desktop Cube"
      choose the "Behaviour" tab
         uncheck the "Inside Cube" tickbox, if it's checked.

For what it's worth, I'm running the Compiz-Core version 1:0.6.2+git20071119ubuntu~gutsy1

I don't know the exact version that Hardy uses - if their internal layouts vary, I apologize for the confusion.
