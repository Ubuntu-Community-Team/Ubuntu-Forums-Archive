### Post by mgmiller on 2008-08-27
I used droplineneu for a long time till I switched to nuovext.  I really like it.  

My current favorite theme uses the following components from gnomelook.org  (I like blue):

Controls.... GTK2-Glossy_P
Cursors..... Blueglass-xcursors-3D
gdm theme...  Relaxing-water
icons........... nuovext
splash theme...  water_splash_2_rounded
window borders... Alphacube-metacity
Firefox theme... firefox 3 linux 1.1  
desktop image is splash...  [http://www.desktopexchange.com/gallery/albums/3D_widescreen_wallpaper/3D_splash.jpg](http://www.desktopexchange.com/gallery/albums/3D_widescreen_wallpaper/3D_splash.jpg)

fonts settings:
Application font...... dejavu sans condensed oblique 11
document font....... dejavu sans condensed   12
Desktop font......... dejavu sans oblique 10
window title font.... dejavu sans bold oblique 12
fixed width font..... monospace bold  12

I also do a few tweaks with compiz to enhance some of my drop shadows and change some transpanencies of menus and dropdowns and turn on a few of the eye candy things.   I also uncheck expand on the top panel to make it shrink to the center of the top of the screen.

I played around a lot to get it to look the way I like.  That's the beauty of linux, make it your own.
