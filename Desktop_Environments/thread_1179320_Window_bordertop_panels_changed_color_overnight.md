### Post by coffeecat on 2009-06-05
Yes, something very weird has happened to the top panel. But your Window border is OK.

Right-click on the top panel and choose Properties. Select the Background tab. Have you got 'Solid Colour' or 'Background image' ticked? If something doesn't look right there, right-click on the bottom panel and set the top panel 'Background' choices to be the same as your bottom panel.
