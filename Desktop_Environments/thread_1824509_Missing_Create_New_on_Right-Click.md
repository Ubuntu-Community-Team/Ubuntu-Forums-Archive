### Post by ankspo71 on 2011-08-14
Hi,
Kde4's "Desktop view" is exactly as you describe, so I think I know where it went :D If you are not using a "folder view" type of desktop, then desktop icons will be hidden from the desktop, therefore there is no need to create new documents on the desktop (so those desktop features are hidden too).

So... Did you change your desktop layout from "Folder View" to "Desktop view"? If  you did, right click on your desktop and choose "Desktop Settings", then when the window opens choose "Folder View" for 'Layout: dropdown menu'

Or...Did you change your activities from a from a folder view to a Desktop view activity? If you did, click on that kde acorn thing at the top right side of your screen then slect "activities" (or right click on your desktop and choose "activities") then select or create a "folder view" activity.

Hope this helps.
