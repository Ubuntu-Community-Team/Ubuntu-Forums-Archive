### Post by RAOF on 2006-02-20
Have you ever bought anything from the iTunes Music Service using that account in iTunes?  If you have, Apple now sets a flag on the account saying that you have a later version of iTunes, and won't allow you to buy anything from an earlier version (like SharpMusique).

A work-around is to create a new account, and never use it in iTunes, only SharpMusique.
