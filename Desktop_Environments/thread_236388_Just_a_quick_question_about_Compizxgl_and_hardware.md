### Post by MikeMLP on 2006-08-14
I don't have an NVidia Card here, but I am running a 1.7 GHZ Pentium M laptop with a 64 meg mobility 9600 from 2004.  XGL/Compiz runs well here.  There are frame drops, but nothing so severe that I cannot use it as my day-to-day desktop (which I do, of course).  I'd say give it a shot.  Generally (depends on how you do it, but generally,) it installs to a separate session, so you can always go right back to regular GNOME whenever you want to.  And if you must, uninstallation shouldn't be that difficult.

Good luck, and enjoy!
