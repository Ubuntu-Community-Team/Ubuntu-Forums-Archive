### Post by ugm6hr on 2007-07-28
Can't help with most of it - but I use Xubuntu (XFCE).  If you lose the panels:
Alt+F2
```
xfce4-panel &
```

This should keep the panels for good.  I always tick the "Save session for future logins" box on the "Quit" option box to ensure they stay.

Some people add *xfce4-panel* to the autostarted applications list, but I haven't had to do that.

As for your installation problems, I can't help...
