### Post by scarpent on 2007-07-10
Someone answered this in the comments for my blog post:

[http://www.movingtofreedom.org/2007/06/28/how-do-you-disable-the-ctrl-t-move-to-trash-shortcut-in-gnome-nautilus/](http://www.movingtofreedom.org/2007/06/28/how-do-you-disable-the-ctrl-t-move-to-trash-shortcut-in-gnome-nautilus/)

Go to System --> Preferences --> Menus & Toolbars and check "Editable menu shortcut keys".

Then you can put your mouse over the "Move to Trash" menu item (in the edit menu in Nautilus) and hit delete to remove the shortcut.  After that you might want to disable the option Menus & Toolbars option again to avoid accidental changes of other shortcuts.  (If you read the help for this feature you'll see it can be a bit dangerous.)
