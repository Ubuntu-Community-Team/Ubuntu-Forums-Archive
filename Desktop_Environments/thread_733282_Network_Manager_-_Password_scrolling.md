### Post by c10 on 2008-03-23
I just tried Ubuntu 8.04 beta via the live CD, and saw one thing I thought could be improved. In the Network Manager, when entering my 26 character WEP password, the characters on screen (they show up as black circles on my screen), seem to stop scrolling after 17 or so characters are entered. The entry field is too small to show all characters at a time. I saw some of the same 'black circle' characters in another place (the password entry for one of these support forums), but didn't pick a long password there so I don't know if it's a 'Gnome' thing or just within the Network settings.

I would suggest either continue the scrollling motion, and/or use a smaller filler character, that would allow more characters to display at a time.
