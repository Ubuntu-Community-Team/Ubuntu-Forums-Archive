### Post by mcduck on 2010-04-13
Those themes aren't for Ubuntu, they just use the colors and some elements from Ubuntu.

The themes are for SuperCard OS, which runs on Nintendo DS.

edit: Gnome Control Center just combines the contents of System/Preferences and System/Administration into one preferences window , much like on OSX & Windows. You can enable it with the menu editor (right-click on the Applicatipns-menu and select "Edit Menus").
