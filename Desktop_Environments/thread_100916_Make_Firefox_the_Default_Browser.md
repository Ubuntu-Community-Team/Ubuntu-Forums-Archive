### Post by mlomker on 2005-12-08
Open up Konqueror (I use the Home Folder pick) and go into Settings, Configure Konqueror.  Click on File Associations and expand the TEXT selection.  Click on HTML and then on the right side you'll see 'Application preference order.'  You can either move firefox to the top or remove the other selections (which is what I did).
