### Post by gemma1 on 2006-09-30
I have had some problems with Firestarter and email (could not send or receive email on standard ports), for a while I played with the options and worked out the fix for my email problem - unclicking "Block traffic on reserved addresses on public interfaces" did the trick. This option is found under edit > preferences > advanced options > traffic validation
