### Post by Bobhuber on 2011-02-22
Cairo - Dock has come a long way. One of my biggest beefs with ubuntu was the awful looking system tray. I was able to shorten and move Ubuntu's top panel (tray area) and with the help of an app called Doncky included with Cairo-Dock make the system tray resemble a small dock on the desktop. You can hide it or leave it visible - your choice.
