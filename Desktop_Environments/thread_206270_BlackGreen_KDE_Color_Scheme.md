### Post by Jucato on 2006-06-29
have you tried searching at [http://www.kde-look.org](http://www.kde-look.org) ?
If you got that theme from gnome-look.org, there's a chance that something similar exists in kde-look.org.

If you couldn't find one, and you're just concerned about the color scheme, it's quite easy to make your own color scheme. Open up System Settings > Appearance > Colors. Make a new one or modify an existing one, and save it as your own color scheme.
