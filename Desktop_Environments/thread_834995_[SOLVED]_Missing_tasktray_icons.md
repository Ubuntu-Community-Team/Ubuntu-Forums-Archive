### Post by bmac on 2008-06-20
Can you locate two vertical, parallel, perforated lines in your panel? Actually you may have multiples. One for updates and hopefully one that is your "Window List". If the Window List is placed all the way to the right then it won't show icons in the panel. Right click on the lines and select "preferences" - this will identify if you've selected the right set of lines. It should say "Window List Preferences". If that exists in your panel move the lines (unlock if necessary) to the left on the panel.


Hope this helps...
