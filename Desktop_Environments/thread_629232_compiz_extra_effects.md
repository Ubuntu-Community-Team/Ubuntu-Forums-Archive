### Post by Cursim on 2007-12-02
This is dependent on your video card and the drivers installed. If you do not have a driver which will support Composite and OpenGL, you're out of luck. I don't know much about Intel on-board cards. Do some googling about your chipset, and see what you can dig up. 

Aside from that, (someone correct me if I'm wrong), there's no compiz-fusion package. Compiz-fusion is the combination the Compiz window manager and the Emerald window decorator. I've not gotten emerald to run stably on my box, so I use compiz and the grk-window-decorator, which works nicely. Can't change theme, but I can live with that. 

Hope this is helpful. I'm just diving into the graphics / compiz stuff myself.
