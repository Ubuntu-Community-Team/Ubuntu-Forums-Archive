### Post by marwal on 2006-07-03
It's easy.
Just open Picasa2 with wine, add folder from your desktop,
make som changes to your pictures, highlight your pictures,
select export image to folder and set the destination folder
same as the current image folder.
And then all desktop content is gone. Not in the trash... just GONE :(
