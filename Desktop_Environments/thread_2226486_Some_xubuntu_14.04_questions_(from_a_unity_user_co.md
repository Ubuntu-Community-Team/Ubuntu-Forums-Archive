### Post by Dennis N on 2014-05-27
#1: I just use a vertical panel on the left side of the screen. Set it to automatically show and hide. On it, add  launchers for programs or other items you want.
#2: Sorry, no idea.
#3: Settings Window > Personal > Window Manager Tweaks > Cycling Tab > Checkbox: Cycle through windows on all workspaces.
