### Post by LesterPalooza on 2009-04-19
System > Preferences > Open **_CompizConfig Settings Manager_** and go to **_Desktop_** option on the **left**. Look for **_Desktop Cube_**. Click on it, and go to the _**Appearance**_ tab. _*Look halfway down*_ and you'll see options for Skydome.

*If Skydome's not in Desktop Cube or Desktop Cube is not even there, you might be missing a plugin.*

When I installed my Compiz, it was complete with all the plugins. I went to synaptic and typed "ccsm" and installed it and everything thats associated with it.
