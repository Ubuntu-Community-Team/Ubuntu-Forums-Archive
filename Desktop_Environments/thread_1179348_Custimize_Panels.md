### Post by mocoloco on 2009-06-05
System -> Preferences -> Appearance.  Choose a pre-made theme, install a new one, or click "Customize" and change what you want about your current theme.  Besides getting themes from sites like [http://gnome-look.org]("http://gnome-look.org") there are some themes in the repositories, just search in synaptic for "theme" (and read the descriptions to be sure they're for gtk/gnome and not something else).
