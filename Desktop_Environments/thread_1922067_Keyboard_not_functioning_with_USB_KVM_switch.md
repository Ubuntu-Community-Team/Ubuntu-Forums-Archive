### Post by Stebra on 2012-02-13
I've used KVM switches for several years, and although I haven't seen your specific issue, with just the keyboard not functioning (typically I've seen issues where no USB functions, not just the keyboard), but I would recommend checking out a connectpro switch.  The UD-14+ is one that I use for DVI, but I used to have the VGA version as well.  The USB is much more stable, it seems, than others.  It has something to do with making computers think your keyboard/mouse are always directly connected, and no re-recognizing every time you switch ports.
