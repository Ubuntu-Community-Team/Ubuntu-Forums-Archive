When I look at Konqueror to view my files, one folder ('music') is shown as a folder icon, but with what looks like a red power plug at the bottom. Does anyone know what this means? KDE's amazing documentation was, as usual, useless.

---

### Post by SGC on 2005-09-19
It means that the folder is a shared folder (can be accessed from the network). To turn sharing off, right click on the folder and choose properties then uncheck "share this folder in the local network" from the share tab.
