### Post by pgmario on 2005-09-13
If you like the minimalist approach of openbox, [aumix]("http://www.jpj.net/%7Etrevor/aumix.html") might be what you're looking for. It offers both ncurses and GTK interfaces. Since it accepts commands on the commandline it can be very convenient in scripts and the like (I used to bind keys to it under fluxbox to control the volume with a simple shortcut).
