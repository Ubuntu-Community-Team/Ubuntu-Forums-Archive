### Post by Nathanael Culver on 2009-04-20
Obviously, I'm a noob to KDE. With the traditional menu enabled, there's a "Remove This Application Launcher Menu Ctrl-R" option. I must've clicked it or typed Ctrl-R unwittingly.

The solution was to left-click the widget control in the upper right corner of your desktop, select Unlock Widgets, then Add Widgets. Add the "Application Launcher Menu (Traditional menu based application launcher)". A duplicate of the blue K-gear icon will appear on your desktop. Now when you right click on the bottom left K-gear, you'll find the "Switch to Classic Menu Style" option is back.

Finally, right-click on the K-gear on the desktop (not the lower left corner one) and select "Remove this Application Launcher".
