### Post by doclivingston on 2005-12-03
The paranoia option is almost always the reason it is slow. You can turn it off, however you may get glitches in the audio. What "paranoia mode" does is deal with:
a) scratched or slightly damaged CDs
b) some drives that don't report errors when reading audio properly
c) some drives that occasionally generate overlaps when reading cd audio
