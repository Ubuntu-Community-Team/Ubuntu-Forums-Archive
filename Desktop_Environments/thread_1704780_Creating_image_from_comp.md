### Post by Rob_H on 2011-03-11
Even if you can create the image, you may encounter problems with hardware drivers. VirtualBox emulates a small set of devices, which are probably not the same ones you've got in your system.

My advice is to just do a fresh install in VirtualBox and copy over the data you need. You'll probably save time in the long run.
