### Post by hizaguchi on 2006-03-01
You can also open up the Gnome main menu and go to System -> Preferences -> Keyboard Shortcuts and set up a convienent key combination to switch windows.  I use Ctrl-Spacebar to hide/show desktop and Ctrl-Alt-Spacebar to switch windows.  This way I can manage what's minimized and what's not without any clicking (laptop touchpad is a pain) plus I don't need a window selector or list to do it.  That frees up alot of panel space for launchers and applets, and cuts down on the ugly factor of those little seperators that won't turn transparent.
