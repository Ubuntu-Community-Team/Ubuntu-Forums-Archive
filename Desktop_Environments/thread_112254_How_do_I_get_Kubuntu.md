### Post by kingsidy on 2006-01-03
open the terminal and type in > sudo apt-get install kubuntu-desktop it is going to download a whole bunch of package, when it is done. reboot, when you get to the login screen, click on "sessions" and click on kde, it is then going to ask you whether you want to make your default session click on yeah. voila. you should login into KDE after that.
