### Post by davidgarcin on 2007-04-22
as far as I know, Hotmail won't let you download messages through POP unless you pay (I used to do this).  Check out [Gmail]("http://gmail.google.com") - it has free POP downloading, message forwarding, etc.  And it's pretty much the best email you will ever have.

If you switch to Gmail, configuring POP access from any email client is pretty straightforward.
