### Post by oldefoxx on 2015-09-04
I found the answer at another forum.  It's awkward, but simple:

(1)  Hold down the Alt key
(2)  With the free hand, put the mouse on the icon to remove and press and hold down the right mouse button.
(3)  Do it wrong, and you will drag a copy onto the desktop.
(3)  Do it right, and you will see a choice to remove it appear.  Drag the mouse to the remove, and you are done.
