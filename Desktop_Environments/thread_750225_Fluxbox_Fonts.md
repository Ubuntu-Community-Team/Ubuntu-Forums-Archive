### Post by RedSquirrel on 2008-04-10
Your screenshot is a bit too small to see what's going on. :p

If the font doesn't look right, it could be that you don't have that font installed. Some styles use artwiz fonts, for example.

In any case, you can use the style overlay to make your font choice apply to every style. See:

[http://fluxbox-wiki.org/index.php/Style_overlay](http://fluxbox-wiki.org/index.php/Style_overlay)

(Note: the line for overlay should already be in your ~/.fluxbox/init)
