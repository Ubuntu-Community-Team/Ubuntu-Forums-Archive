### Post by relic70 on 2012-08-26
You can specify a Keyboard Layout in System Settings. I would assume if you login as user1 and specify a particular layout, then user1 will always have that layout. Then if you logout of user1 and login as user2 and again specify a keyboard layout, different from that of user1, then user2 will have their specific keyboard layout whenver they login in as user2.

Just an assumption on my part.
