Let me introduce you to the best way to copy and paste ever. Highlight some text. Middle click.

WHATTTTTTTT

Alternatively Ctrl+Shift+C/V work in the terminal. Ctrl+C has always stood for "interrupt" (SIGINT) in terminals which is why the combination doesn't mean "copy" in the terminal.

By the way, the xfce-terminal does support copy/paste from the menu bar as well.
