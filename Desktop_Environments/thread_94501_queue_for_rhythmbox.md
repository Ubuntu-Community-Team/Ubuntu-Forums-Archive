### Post by charlieg on 2005-11-24
From what I can tell, proper queuing support is coming in version 0.9.3, and that is probably going to be released over the Christmas period.  I suggest you use amarok until then or check out one of the queue-based players like [Muine](http://muine.gooeylinux.org/), [BMP](http://beep-media-player.org), or maybe [Banshee](http://banshee-project.org).

On a side note, I've found Totem to be adequate as an off-the-cuff basic music player.
