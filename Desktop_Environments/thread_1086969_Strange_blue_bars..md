### Post by C-- on 2009-03-04
From the pic you posted it looks like the bar is part of your window selector. On the gnome panel, the thing that displays all of your open windows in the panel is a panel applet. If you right click on the blue bar it you should have some options appear like "move" and "remove from panel." For example, if you click "move" you can move the window selector applet around on your panel, or to different panels if you have more than one. If you right click on an empty part of the panel and select "Add to panel" and scroll to the bottom of the list you can add multiple copies of the same applet to the panel if you accidentally delete it.

I think the only reason it looks blue is because you have a funky theme. On the default theme it should look grey.
