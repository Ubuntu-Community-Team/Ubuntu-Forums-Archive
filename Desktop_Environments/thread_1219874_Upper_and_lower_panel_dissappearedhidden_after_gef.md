Recently I upgraded my ubuntu box with a geforce 9500GT video card. after installing the nvidia drivers, two strange things started to happen:
1. The screen resolution would always revert back to 1440x900 after a reboot, even after setting it up as 1920x1080 in the "nvidia X server settings". - I finally fixed this by inserting this entry in "startup applications" - "nvidia-settings --load-config-only".
2. The upper and lower panel disappeared. This is the case with any resolution, well, at least with 1440x900 and 1920x1080, I have not tried others. If I disable the nvidia driver, the panels become visible again. I know the panels are hidden since "alt+f1" does bring up the applications menu, and pressing "->" key I can scroll through all menus. I have tried uninstalling/reinstalling the panel, deleting the config files in the home folder, and that did not help.

Running out of ideas here, any help will be greatly appreciated.
