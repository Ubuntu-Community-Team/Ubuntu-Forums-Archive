### Post by meng on 2006-08-31
While you could spend time sorting out all the wrinkles without reinstalling, I think it sounds like a good time to reinstall. To preserve your personal data, I advise creating a separate home partition:
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome.html](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome.html)

Be aware, though, that some glitches may be due to configuration files within hidden directories within your home directory, e.g. in .gconf, .firefox. If you don't mind losing all of these settings, you could just neglect/delete those hidden directories (they'll be reconstituted when you reinstall or run applications at a later date). Otherwise you could selectively delete the ones you know you don't need ... you get the idea I think.
