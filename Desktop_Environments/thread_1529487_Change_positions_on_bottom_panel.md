### Post by SlidingHorn on 2010-07-12
You should just be able to right-click the applets and (if necessary, unlock position, then) move them where you please.  from there, you can right-click again and lock them into position

### Post by mcduck on 2010-07-12
You can also just drag the panel applets around using the middle mouse button.

If something refuses to move, right-click it and make sure "Lock to Panel" is disabled. And also note that dragging and accessing the right-click menu for the Window List-applet (which shows your open windows) is done from  the small handle at the far left end of the Window List..
