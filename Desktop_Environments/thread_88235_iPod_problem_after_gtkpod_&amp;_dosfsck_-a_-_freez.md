### Post by mrzud on 2005-11-09
Hardware: iPod nano
OS: Ubuntu, Windows XP

Problem: iPod works fine when not plugged into computer.  When plugged into computer it freezes.  Unable to mount and unrecognized by fdisk in linux.  In Windows, unable to access drive (causes programs to stop responding).
When plugged into computer in "disk mode" works fine.

Cause: Unknown.

Remedy: Bend over for Microsoft. Boot to [disk mode]("http://www.info.apple.com/kbnum/n93651"). Format iPod drive to FAT32. Reboot iPod (does automatically after "ejecting" or "stopping".  Run [iPod Updater and restore iPod]("http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=60983").

I was having this problem and I couldnt find any support documentation anywhere for this issue (not that I looked to hard) so I decided figure out how to fix it on my own..

Let me know if this helps anyone else. ;)
