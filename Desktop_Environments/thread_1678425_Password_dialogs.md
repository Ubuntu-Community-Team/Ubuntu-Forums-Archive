### Post by madengineer on 2011-01-30
I know you can remove the password prompt on resuming from sleep/exiting screensaver by unchecking "lock screen when screensaver is active" in the Screensaver panel under Preferences. Not sure what the other one with the key ring is though; any further info on it would be helpful.

### Post by asmoore82 on 2011-01-30
You can permanently unlock the keyring:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=10211581&postcount=3](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=10211581&postcount=3)

But keep in mind that this will leave all passwords and
network keys stored under that account unencrypted.
Not a major dealbreaker, just remember to throw out all of those
passwords if the machine is ever lost, stolen, or compromised.
