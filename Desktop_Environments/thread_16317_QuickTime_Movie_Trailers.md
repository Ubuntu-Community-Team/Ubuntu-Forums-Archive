### Post by kassetra on 2005-02-20
oh my. Apple won't license the sorenson codecs (which are quicktime movies on the internet) to linux/unix... *sigh* so it's kind of tricky in linux.
  
  mplayer, however, can handle these special movies, see [codecs]("http://web.njit.edu/all_topics/Prog_Lang_Docs/html/mplayer/codecs.html").
  Installing mplayer can be kind of tricky, and it's interface relies upon gtk1.
 Here is how to install it: [starter guide]("http://ubuntuguide.org/")
  
Also, here is a non-free way to watch movie trailers in the sorenson quicktime format:
  [http://www.codeweavers.com/products/]("http://www.codeweavers.com/products/")
