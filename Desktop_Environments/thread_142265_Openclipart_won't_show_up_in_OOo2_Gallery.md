### Post by arnieboy on 2006-03-10
click on **tools**--> **gallery**
click on **new theme**.
when the new theme wizard opens up, click on files and go to the following location:
> /usr/lib/openoffice/share/gallery
and click on **add all files**.
then click **ok**.
now u will see thumbnails of all clipart under "**new theme**".
Not sure why its not showing up as default though. I distinctly remember it used to do so even in this version of openoffice. possibly some files have been changed in doko's repository..

An additional tip:
If you want to view SVG files in openoffice, here is a quick howto:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=92616](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=92616)
