### Post by Caffeine_Junky on 2007-10-24
Ok try this,   open: K Menu > Run > type: kcontrol   then click Run  this will open the kcontrol window.  Then click "Appearance and Themes" > Launch feedback,  open drop-down menu under "Busy Cursor" and select "No Busy Cursor" from the list,  then go to the bottom of the window and click "Apply"


I hope this is what you where after :)
