### Post by PmDematagoda on 2008-02-05
Unfortunately, one cannot be sure if the upgrade can go perfectly since the outcome mostly depends on your PC and the way your Ubuntu install is currently setup, so the chances of an upgrade succeeding differs a lot from PC to PC.

So any upgrade that is done is done mainly on chance, sometimes your upgrade will succeed, sometimes it may not and in all accounts you have to be prepared for a failure in the upgrade, so keep everything ready such as a SuperGRUB disc and a Ubuntu Live CD and most importantly of all, backups of your important data and files:).
