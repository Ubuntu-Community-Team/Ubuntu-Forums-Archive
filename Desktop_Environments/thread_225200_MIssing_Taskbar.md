### Post by ardchoille on 2006-07-29
Right-click the panel, choose "Add to panel", then choose the Window List applet in the Add to panel window, then click the "+ Add" button and it should appear on your panel. You can right click the new applet and choose Preferences to tweak the settings for the Window List.
