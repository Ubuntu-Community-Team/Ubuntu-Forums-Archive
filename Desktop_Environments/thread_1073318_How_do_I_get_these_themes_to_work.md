### Post by mcduck on 2009-02-18
Depends on what GTK theme engine it needs.

If the error/warning message complains about missing a GTK engine but doesn't say what, you can just ignore the error. In this case it's not *really* missing anything and your theme will work fine without doing anything about it.

When it's actually able to tell what engine it's missing you can simply install the engine with Synaptic (if available in repositories) or by downloading the required engine from gnome-look.org.
