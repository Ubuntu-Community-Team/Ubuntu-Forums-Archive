### Post by benerivo on 2008-02-23
Does this only happen with Firefox, or with all windows that you minimise? 

Also, do you have a windows list in your panel (shows all currently open windows, allowing you to click them when you want to bring a program to the front (exactly as in windows xp)). If not right click on the panel and add 'window list'.

You could try Alt+Tab (like in windows xp) to see if this lets you switch back to Firefox after you have minimised it.
