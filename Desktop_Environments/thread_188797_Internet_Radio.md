### Post by lofty00 on 2006-06-04
for a server you could use icecast ([www.icecast.org)](www.icecast.org)). For a client, it depends on how you want to do it. If you are DJing from decks or external CD player and you just want to broadcast from line in, try ices (also [www.icecast.org)](www.icecast.org)), which streams to an icecast server in ogg vorbis format. Also look at darkice.

If you want to play track from your hard drive, you could either look for a plugin for xmms (I think there is one), or else have a look at MuSE ([http://muse.dyne.org/](http://muse.dyne.org/)) which is a cheap and cheerful graphical front end for streaming live audio and playlists to an icecast server.
