### Post by 3Miro on 2012-04-13
I used to do some experiments with Gnome 2 and compiz vs metacity, I found that unless compiz has too many effects enabled, it makes little difference.

In general, I would say it depends on your video card and CPU. Unless you have heavy effects enabled, both Unity 2D and Unity 3D would do more or less the same amount of work (i.e. render the windows). The question is how is the work distributed between the CPU and GPU. The only way to know for sure is to test it.
