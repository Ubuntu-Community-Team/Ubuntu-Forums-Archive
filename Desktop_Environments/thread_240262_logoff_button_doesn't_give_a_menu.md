### Post by shoofy on 2006-08-20
I don't know what happened, but now when I click the logout button on the toolbar or on the system menu, I don't get the menu with the options to shutdown, reboot, logoff, etc. It just goes straight to logging off. Sometimes when it does this it goes back to the Ubuntu login screen and other times it goes to some blank off-white screen with a white textbox where the username textbox is usually. If it does the latter I have to do a hard reboot, because nothing responds, even ctrl-alt-F1. The only thing that I changed recently that I can think of is installing compiz. Is that likely to have caused the problem? How do I fix it?

### Post by shoofy on 2006-08-23
Well I figured it out. Under System->Preferences->Sessions I had unchecked "Ask on logout." It's back now.
