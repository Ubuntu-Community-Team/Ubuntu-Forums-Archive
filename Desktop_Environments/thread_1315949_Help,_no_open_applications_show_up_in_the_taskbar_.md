### Post by Untitled_No4 on 2009-11-05
Perhaps you've deleted the Task Manager widget from your panel? 

Click on the Cashew on the right end of your panel, then click Add Widgets... and in the Add Widgets window type "task" (without the quotation marks) which will filter out all other widgets.
If there is no red minus sign right of the star icon, then you have deleted the task manager so click once on the widget entry to highlight it and then click the Add Widget button to your panel.

If there is a minus sign next to the star then the Task Manager widget is installed but it will be easier to remove it (by clicking on the minus sign) and install it again than trouble shooting why it's not working as it should.

### Post by smilingfrog on 2009-11-09
I had this happen as well. 
Right click on the task bar.
Choose 'Add to Panel'
Click on 'Window List' at the bottom of the menu, and then click on add.
If the list on the task bar is too small to see, you may have to move or resize it, but the program buttons should reappear on the taskbar.
I don't know how I lost it either.
