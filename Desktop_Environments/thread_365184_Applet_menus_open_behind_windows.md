### Post by el3ktro on 2007-02-19
I'm running Ubuntu 6.10 with the latest Beryl and the NVIDIA drivers. Whenever I have a few windows open and click the menu of an applet, e.g. the calendar, or the network-manager menu, or the Novell Slab menu, it opens *behind* and other open windows. This is very annoying, since I either have to move all windows or switch to an empty desktop.

Has anybody experienced this? Is there a solutionto this?

---

### Post by el3ktro on 2007-02-19
Damn, now while writing this I remembered seeing an option in Beryl that may be related - ad voila! It's solved.

I deactivated the "Focus stealing prevention" in Beryl and it works now. Well I hope this post will at least be helpfull to somebody else ...
