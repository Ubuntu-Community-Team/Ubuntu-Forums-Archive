### Post by spudley on 2005-09-13
Just follow the advice prior to my post... download & examine... it is pretty easy - I setup my own login page from another one I downloaded.

The only part I wasn't sure about was getting a screenshot of the login page once it was done (not necessary, but convenient esp. if you want to distribute it after)... its an easy thing to do:

from the terminal
gdmflexiserver --xnest

(you might have to sudo apt-get install xnest first)
then take your screen shot - (system->take screenshot) and then just crop the login portion in gimp.
