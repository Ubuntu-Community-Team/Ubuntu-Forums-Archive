You can filter on "Sender" "Contains"...... so you can put in "server.com" and then anything with that name in the sender address will be caught.

The options are almost endless, just have a look at them in Evolution and also check the help file.

You can have multiple filters, you can have a filter stop processing a message or you can let the message go through to subsequent filters.
