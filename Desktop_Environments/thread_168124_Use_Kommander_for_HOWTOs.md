### Post by opticyclic on 2006-04-29
I have been using Ubuntu since Jan 2006 and I kept finding myself thinking
"Why isn't there a simple interface to do this"

Most of the HOWTOs that I have looked at have involved 2 things
[LIST=1]
[*]Changing some form of image (splash/background etc)
[*]Lots of copy/paste to the terminal
[/LIST]
Now on the one hand it is good to get comfortable with the terminal.
On the other hand its not the sort of thing my Grandma wants to do.

The HOWTOs are very helpful, but the most popular ones should really be converted into GUIs.

To show how easy this is, starting from no knowledge at all,
 I created a GUI for the usplash HOWTO using Kommander in about 30 mins
[http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=969844&postcount=69](http://www.ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=969844&postcount=69)

Kommander is brilliant!
Why is it not more widely used?
