### Post by gsmanners on 2011-10-16
For people who like the keyboard, its:

alt-ctrl-home/alt-ctrl-end: move window to previous/next
alt-ctrl-num1: move window to workspace 1
alt-ctrl-num2: move window to workspace 2
...
alt-ctrl-num9: move window to workspace 9

You can change these in the "Window Manager" application in Settings.
