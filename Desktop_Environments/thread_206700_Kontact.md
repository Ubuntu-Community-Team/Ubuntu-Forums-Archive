### Post by auricle on 2006-07-01
Actually, there is the KO/PI, KA/PI and OM/PI projects. They stand for KOrganizer/Platform Independant, KAddressbook/Platform Independant and K-OpieMail/Platform Independent respectively. 

Together they are basically Kontact but can be installed in Windows! I use them on my linux based PDA (Zaurus), they are great.

The website is [http://www.pi-sync.net/](http://www.pi-sync.net/)

Binaries for Windows can be downloaded from the site.
