### Post by pt123 on 2007-07-30
Found the solution in another forum:

"Backgrounds and Emblems" dialog, but if you bring up the emblems in the Nautilus sidebar (View -> Side Pane, then choose Emblems in the sidebar dropdown), you can right-click an emblem and choose Delete.

Bugzilla link
[http://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=112292](http://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=112292)
