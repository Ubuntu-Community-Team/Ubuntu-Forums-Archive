### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-11-18
Go to System Settings>Appearance>Look tab and use the slider to Adjust Launcher Icon Size. You will find that the icons can be made much smaller than they ever could in 12.04. If you have been using 14.04 then 14.10 has not changed any of this at all.

The Behaviour tab will let you enable workspaces and put a workspace switcher icon on to the launcher. And also put the Show Desktop icon on the launcher if that is what you are used to.

Perhaps you are not using Ubuntu with Unity but a flavour. You do not say. So, I am making an assumption.
