### Post by Asix on 2007-01-04
Sorry, but there isn't any ATI card which currently supports AIGLX through proprietary ATI driver (fglrx), but any Radeon will work with AIGLX with open source drivers.
When it comes to nVidia cards, basically anything newer than GeFroce 4 will do the job pretty well. And yes, you can install proprietary nVidia drivers as they fully support AIGLX.
Hope it helped.
