### Post by SeijiSensei on 2011-04-25
If you right-click on the desktop, pick Add Widgets, then drag and drop any you want to display onto your desktop.  To get back the Desktop Folder, drag the "Folder View" widget onto the desktop.  To change the target folder, click the little wrench in the pop-out widget menu bar that appears to the right of the widget.  If you choose the Desktop Folder, it will display ~/Desktop.

I can also place links to files and folders on the basic desktop just by dragging and dropping from Dolphin and picking "Icon".

You can also add the widgets to the panel bar and the system tray.

Often the easiest ways to figure out what various things do in KDE are either random experimentation or using the various applications in System Settings.
