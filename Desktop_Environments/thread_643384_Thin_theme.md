### Post by Jeff.J on 2007-12-18
To make the panels ("task bar") smaller, just right click on one and select "Properties".  I don't know if it will go as small as you want?

To make title bars as small as possible, reduce the size of the "Window title font".  The title bar will shrink/grow with the size of the font - within certain limits.

It's probably wise to keep your ppi at 96 if you want your fonts to look their best.

Right click desktop>Change Desktop Background>Fonts tab - That's where you can set the window title font.  As you lower the font, the title bar will get smaller, but it won't let you make the window title bars smaller than needed to display the text.

Same thing goes for the panel sizes.  It won't let you make them smaller than the font.  Reduce your "application font" size and it will allow you to make the panels smaller.

Maybe, like Lostincyberspace said, you can find a theme on gnome-look that emphasizes really small window title bars.  I don't know.
