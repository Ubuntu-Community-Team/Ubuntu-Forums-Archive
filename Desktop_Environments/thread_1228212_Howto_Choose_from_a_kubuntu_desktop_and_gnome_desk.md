### Post by ubudog on 2009-07-31
1.Install the kubuntu-desktop package.
2.Logout
3.Go to the bottom of the login screen and press "Options"
4.Select "Choose Session" and then select KDE.
5.Enter username and password.
6.Enjoy your KDE desktop!:D
