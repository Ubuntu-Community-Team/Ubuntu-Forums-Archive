 The KMenu Sidepanel images are in /usr/share/apps/kicker/pics 

There should be one with just the regular KDE 3.4 artwork already in there. Just rename it to kside-default.png and restart kde.

And the developers made that stuff easily replacable because they want KDE to be branded.
