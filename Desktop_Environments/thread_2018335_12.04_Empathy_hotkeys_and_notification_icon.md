### Post by Ceannfaolaidh on 2012-07-06
Unfortunately, there's no simple way to assign keyboard shortcuts to panel icons. However, you can work around this by locking empathy to the launcher and using Super+# (where # is the position of Empathy on the launcher, not counting the dash button) to focus it.

As to your other question, Empathy should pop a bubble notification whenever you receive a new message. If it's not, open Empathy from the Dash and go to Edit > Preferences > Notifications. Check "Enable bubble notifications" and "Enable notification when the chat is not focused."

### Post by Ceannfaolaidh on 2012-07-06
Unfortunately there isn't much to be done about making waiting Empathy messages more obvious, and bubble notifications can't be dismissed (althouh they go away fairly quickly). Just a couple things to consider: what icon theme are you using? Some themes have terrible notification colors (i.e. grey to slightly darker grey); I'd suggest ubuntu-mono-dark.

For more support, you can join the discussion about this issue at [Launchpad]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/indicator-applet/+bug/450398") or check out the [forum thread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1311812") regarding the same topic.
