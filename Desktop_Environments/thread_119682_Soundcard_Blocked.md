### Post by bernardfrancois on 2006-01-24
It depends on the software mixer you are using. If your sound card doesn't do hardware mixing for you, you need software to mix the sounds together. If you don't have a properly working software mixer, you'll have that problem (of only playing one sound at the same time).

Is only XMMS causing the problem and do you have sound from various sources when you are not running XMMS? If that's the case, you'll probably have to change something in your XMMS settings (you need to change the output plugin). You could just try different ones and see what works...
