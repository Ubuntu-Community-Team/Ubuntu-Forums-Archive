### Post by cespinal on 2010-05-17
Taking the suggestions in this thread [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1405372](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1405372), I am just making a quick translation of them. These are based on how to make facebook work in konqueror, also, it is a nice solution that could be applied to other sites which will not detect konqueror as a valid browser, and therefore will not provide full capabilites at that moment. 

In konqueror, go to settings>configure konqueror>browser identification>Site specific identification: 

Click on add. In this case will be facebook.com 
Then select the type of identification. Google chrome and IE 7 on windows xp will be just fine. 
Apply settings and you are on. 

conclusion: It is not that konqueror  does not have the capabilities of other browsers. Simply websites may or may not identify konqueror as a browser and therefore, you just have to apply these settings in order to ''cheat'' the desired website about the browser that is contacting it.
