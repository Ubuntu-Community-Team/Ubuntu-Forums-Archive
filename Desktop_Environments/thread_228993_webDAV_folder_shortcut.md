### Post by gmcauley on 2006-08-03
In Konqueror (KDE/Kubuntu) you can use 'webdav://' followed by the proper path in the address bar.  A dialog will popup prompting you for your username and password (can be set to remember password).  You can also make a bookmark and/or button on the toolbar for easy one click access.

For me it works great and without a hitch.  I did not even have to do anything to set it up - it worked the first time I tried typing 'webdav:// ...' :D
