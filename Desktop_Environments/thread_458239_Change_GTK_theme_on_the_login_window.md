### Post by hungrysam on 2007-05-29
Thomas,
I think what you want can be found here:[ art.gnome-login themes]("http://art.gnome.org/themes/gdm_greeter/").  Just download the theme (as a .tar.gz file) and then go to the "Administration" control menu and open the "Login Window" program (like you did to change to the Circles theme.  Select the "Add" button and locate your newly downloaded theme archive.  Then, select that theme from the list of available themes and enjoy!  If that is not quite what you were looking for, let me know and I'll try to get back to you in a timely manner.

Hope I helped,
