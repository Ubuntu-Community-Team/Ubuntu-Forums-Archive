### Post by Bukunut on 2006-03-29
wsmoser2004

Sure! No problem, I am sure this is what you may have been refering to. 
Open Kcontrol (alt + F2) 
From 'appearances & themes' then select 'icons'
Goto to the advanced tab. From here you have the animate icons box which you tick and at the bottom you will see three folders with 'set effect' ... this is where you can change to the effect you want for icons.

Bukunut.
