A few weeks ago I wanted to do exactly that: importing my emails from KMail to Thunderbird. I found out there's no way to automatically do this.

There's a manual procedure to follow, but you need a working KMail to do this. It's in the Thunderbird's FAQs if I remember correctly.

Basically from KMail you create a new folder in mbox format, then copy there the messages you want to export to Thunderbird. Then you take the newly created mbox file from the KMail directory, and move it into your Thunderbird profile.

You should read the article on Mozilla's site to learn the details. I never bothered to do all this as to me it looked like too much effort for too little gain, so I gave up.

I hope this helps.
