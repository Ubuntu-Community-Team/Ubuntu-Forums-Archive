### Post by Martin-Herrmann on 2006-10-04
Hi,

you can use Mapivi (GUI; [http://mapivi.de.vu]("http://mapivi.de.vu")),
or Exiftool (command line; [http://www.sno.phy.queensu.ca/~phil/exiftool/]("http://www.sno.phy.queensu.ca/~phil/exiftool/")) to add your copyright into the IPTC copyright tag. Of course you can also add, remove or change the content of any other IPTC tag with these tools.

Regards, Martin
