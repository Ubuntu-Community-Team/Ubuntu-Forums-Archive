### Post by jeremytaylor on 2006-08-16
right well I've found an obvious way around this... just turn off realmedia support in mplayer and let realplayer deal with it. 

To do this (one simple method without having to resort to that command line.. eeek) right click on any file currently playing with mplayer plugin, select configure and untick the box for realmedia support.
