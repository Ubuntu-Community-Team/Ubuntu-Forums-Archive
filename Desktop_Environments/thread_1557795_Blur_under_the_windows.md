### Post by _albatros on 2010-08-21
> I only Have RGBA on Nautilus Elementary , are you using the Blur Windows effect in your Compiz settings ?

Yes, the settings (with effect) are on this screen: [http://iv.pl/images/78426920388729097243.png](http://iv.pl/images/78426920388729097243.png)

> Then go to System > Preferences > CompizConfig Settings Manager and in the filter on the left enter: "Blur". Then, check the box next to "Blur Windows" and then click on it (Blur Windows). Next, under Blur filter select "Gaussian" and for "Gaussian Radius" drag the slider to "5". You can of course play with this value for whatever suits your needs.

I'm doing exactly this instruction, the effect is shown in the screenshot: [http://iv.pl/images/78426920388729097243.png](http://iv.pl/images/78426920388729097243.png)
That blur is applied only on the subtitles :/
