### Post by p.szymkowski on 2014-02-13
Ok I solved it.

Solution was so trivial... 

**I just changed resolution to 1600x900, then back to 1920x1080 in Settings Manager > Display _NOT IN CCC_, then reboot.**

Now everything is OK.... Magic
