### Post by psychicdragon on 2005-12-17
You need to modify these two lines:
```
Section "Monitor"
Identifier "Generic Monitor"
Option "DPMS"
[B]HorizSync 28-51
VertRefresh 43-60[/B]
EndSection
```
The best way to get this info would be from the website of your monitor's maufacturer. Just do a Google search for your monitor.
