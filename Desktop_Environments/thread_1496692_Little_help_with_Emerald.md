### Post by Minsky on 2010-05-29
There's nothing gone wrong, as far as I'm aware the Emerald Manager settings only apply to windows. To change the desktop background, you could go through System>Preferences>Appearance, and you could alter some panel settings using Compiz or by right clicking on a panel and selecting Properties.
