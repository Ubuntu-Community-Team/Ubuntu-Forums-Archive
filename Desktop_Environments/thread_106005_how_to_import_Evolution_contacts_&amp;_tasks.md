### Post by cstudent on 2005-12-19
You can transfer contacts from the old to new by creating a vcard file.

Log into your old account in Evolution and go to contacts. Select all your contacts at once and under the file menu choose "save as vcard". This puts all the contacts in a single vcard file. Then just import that vcard file into your new account. As for task, I'll have to do some tinkering and see what I can come up with.
