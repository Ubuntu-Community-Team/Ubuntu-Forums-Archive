### Post by NilsE on 2008-10-27
There is a utility in the repos called compiz-configuration-manager (or something like that) that will help you control all aspects of the appearances.

You can also set most of the in gconf under apps > compiz.

### Post by Therion on 2008-10-27
It's a subsetting of the Wobbly Windows plugin called **Snap Inverted**. Turn it off and the snapping stops.

### Post by neothorn2301 on 2008-10-28
[IMG]http://i14.photobucket.com/albums/a310/neothorn/Screenshot-CompizConfigSettingsM-2.png[/IMG]

[IMG]http://i14.photobucket.com/albums/a310/neothorn/Screenshot-CompizConfigSettingsM-1.png[/IMG]


Go to "System/Preferences/Advanced Desktop Effects Settings"

the scroll all the way to the bottom, then look at the screen shots I linked. It should be in the bottom right hand corner called "Snapping Windows"   Click on that and on the left side disable it.
