### Post by dmizer on 2006-07-01
that means grub can't see your partitions (this does not mean that your partitions are not there).

what kind of hard drives do you have (sata)?  if they're ide drives, you might be able to go into your bios setting and switch the hdd setting to make sure that it has your hdd listed instead of "auto".
