### Post by 3Miro on 2011-04-01
Canonical provides only a relatively small part of Ubuntu. When it comes to that, all *Ubuntu versions get their updated from Canonical (like kernel drivers, Firefox, Office). Canonical also provides updates for Gnome.

KDE, XFCE and LXDE are provided by the community. You get updated fro those from the community (just like VLC, Chromium and most other apps).
