### Post by tommcd on 2007-05-09
Well, you have sound, so you know your soundcard is working. Try typing "alsamixer" in terminal. You move left to right with the side pointing arrow keys, and you increase or decrease the volume with the up and down arrow keys. Turn all the volumes all the way up and see if that makes a difference.
