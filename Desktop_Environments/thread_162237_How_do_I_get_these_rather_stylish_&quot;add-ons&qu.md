### Post by Mr Egg on 2006-04-19
Open gDesklets (Applications -> Accessories -> gDesklets) and goto the System/CPU catgory and open the widget inside, then goto the System/Disk category and open the SideCandy Disk Info widget, then goto the System/Net category and open the SideCandy Network widget.

Hope that helps.
