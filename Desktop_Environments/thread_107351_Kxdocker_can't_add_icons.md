### Post by Freddy on 2005-12-23
If you add for example 2 diffrent music player (xmms, BMPx) and add them both to the same group, only one will show up (top one). Just place your pointer over the icon and turn the mousewheel and the other one appears.

I've noticed before that some separators can disappear when adding new icons, so it may be needed to readd a separator after adding starters to the dock.   /// Freddan

Edit: You could also make 2 diffrent named groupes for music, music1, music2 and in this case both xmms n' BPMx should appear on the dock.
