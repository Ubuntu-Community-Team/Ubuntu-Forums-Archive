### Post by fubadubrub on 2006-08-29
Gnome windows have rounded corners.  I want square corners, how do I get square.  When you maximize a gnome window the upper left and right window corners are curved slightly.  I want the corners square.  How do I do it.

---

### Post by Cable on 2006-08-29
The style of corners your windows have depends completely on the theme you're using.  You could select a different theme (System>Preferences>Theme) or go [here]("http://www.gnome-look.org/") or [here]("http://art.gnome.org/") and find a new theme and download/install it.
