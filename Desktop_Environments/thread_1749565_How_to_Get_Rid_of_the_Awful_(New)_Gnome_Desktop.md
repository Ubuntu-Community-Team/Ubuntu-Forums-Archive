### Post by Rasa1111 on 2011-05-04
I thought it was awful to, for awhile..
Then i gave it one last chance, 4-5 days ago.. and been using it since! 
I am surprisingly impressed! 

Anyway..
If you are on the new 11.04 Unity desktop now..

Hit the "super/windows" key, a "Dash" will pop up, 
Type in "login screen" [without the quotes]
When the login screen pops up.. give your password to unlock it, 
and simply Select  "Ubuntu Classic" as default session. 

When you log out and back in, you'll be at the regular desktop.
