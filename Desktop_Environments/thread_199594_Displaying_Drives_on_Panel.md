Here's a question - is it possible to display links to all mounted drives on a Gnome panel instead of on the desktop?

---

### Post by amunimanghi on 2006-06-19
Yeah you can. In the panel right click and  press **add to panel**. Scroll down to **System and Hardware** and select Disk Mounter. Hope this helps.
