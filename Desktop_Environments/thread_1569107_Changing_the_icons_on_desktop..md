### Post by e79 on 2010-09-06
Yes you can. If there is no icons in your applications launcher that you can simply right-click and 'Add this launcher to the Desktop', then :

1. right-click your desktop
2. choose 'Create Launcher'
3. fill the information as needed (example in screen shot)
4. click on the icon (top-left, as shown on the screen shot) and select any available icon you want from there. You might also want to download an icon from the web specifically for your apps.
