### Post by jdong on 2004-12-14
xine, totem are in Ubuntu. You have to add a 'legally dubious repository', called **debian-marillat** (search the forums or the wiki -- plenty of references).

An updated gxine is provided by me, through **ubuntu-backports** ([http://ubuntu-bp.sourceforge.net](http://ubuntu-bp.sourceforge.net)). Installing *-mad packages from restricted will get you MP3 support.


All-in-all, you'll find that it's **a LOT simpler** to get MP3 and DVD working in Ubuntu than in Fedora Core!
