### Post by cmost on 2009-05-25
Before updating to Firefox 3.5, you should have installed the 'Nightly Tester Tools' add-on.  Then, you would have a new button in the Add-ons applet to "override all compatibility".  I've found that most of the old plug-ins work fine in the newer Firefox with compatibility overridden.  Just a suggestion for others contemplating trying the newest Mozilla Firefox.
