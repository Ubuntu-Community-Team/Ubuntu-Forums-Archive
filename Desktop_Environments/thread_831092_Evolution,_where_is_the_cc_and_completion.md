### Post by Bultot on 2008-06-16
I'm using Evolution for a while now and it work's like a charm. The one thing I can't find, is an inputfield for the CC (carbon copy) and/or BCC (blind carbon copy).
You can click on the [to:] button for inserting contacts from your addressbook, but sometimes the cc/bcc addresses are not in it and I don't want them in it.

I've used Thunderbird for a long time and a nice feature of it is to complete the addresses you've used before and addresses you've sent to and received from.

Is this possible in Evolution?

---

### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-06-16
In the compose-message window, click view and you can select to make the cc: and bcc: fields visible.

I had this problem once too and it took me longer than it should have to solve it.

As for autocompletion, yes, Evolution will do it for addresses in your address book.  It automatically adds an entry to the address book whenever you send a message to a new address, or you can add entries manually by clicking the "Contacts" button in the lower-lefthand corner.
