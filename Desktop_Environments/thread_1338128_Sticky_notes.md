### Post by meho_r on 2009-11-26
It depends on your definition of "good". If you like simple but powerful note app, [Tomboy](http://projects.gnome.org/tomboy/) (Gnome app) may be sufficient. If you want something really fancy, try [BasKet Note Pads](http://basket.kde.org/download.php) (KDE3 app).
