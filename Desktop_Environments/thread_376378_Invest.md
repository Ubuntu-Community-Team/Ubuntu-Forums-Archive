### Post by Rabidmonkey1 on 2007-06-14
You actually have to double click on the name of the stock to change it - same goes for price and quantity...  

So add a GOOG with the add button, then double click to change.  

You might notice a grey area to the right of the applet too.  This normally changes color depending on how the stocks you're tracking are doing - however, there seems to be a bit of a rendering error with this applet in general in feisty.  (more about the rendering bugs here:[https://bugs.launchpad.net/gnome-applets/+bug/59060]("https://bugs.launchpad.net/gnome-applets/+bug/59060")

Hope that helped.
