### Post by yancek on 2014-07-23
Right click the icon for the text file and select 'Open with' and Kate should be an option.
When you have a text file open in Kate, click the Open tab at the upper left and navigate to the file.
When you have multiple files open in Kate, you have 'Previous Document' and 'Next Document' to go between 2 or more files.
To open a new empty file, click the New tab in the upper left.
