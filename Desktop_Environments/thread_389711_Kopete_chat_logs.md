### Post by HousieMousie2 on 2008-01-29
Just to clear it up a tiny bit... for future forum crawlers.

The bug is that the **History** doesn't work correctly until you disengage it, **Apply**, then reengage it, **Apply**. (Found in _Settings/Configure Plugins..._)

The _Tools_ menu on the chat window will then show up and Kopete should start logging/archiving as expected and be accessible from the _View History_ menu. (Found by Right Clicking on a user's name in the Contact List.)

Cheers!

Edit:

Just so you know, it was a reported bug.
