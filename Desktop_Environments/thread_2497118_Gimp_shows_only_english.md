### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2024-04-23
In GiMP: Edit->Preferences->Interface, Field "Language". You should have two choices there, "English (en_us)" and "Systemsprache". The latter switches to the language your system is set to. GiMP needs to be restarted for the change to become active.

Holger
