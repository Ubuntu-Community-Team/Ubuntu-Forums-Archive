### Post by GoldBuggie on 2005-04-22
Right-click on desktop
goto "Behavior->Device Icons"
enable "Show device icons" and check the devices you want. 

I have a mp3-player with an usb plug that automatically gets detected when i plug it in and an icon is displayed on the desktop so that i can view and modify its contents.

Hope this helps
