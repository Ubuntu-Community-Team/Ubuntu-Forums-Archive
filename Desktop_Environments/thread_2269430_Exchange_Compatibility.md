### Post by SeijiSensei on 2015-03-15
Most any email client will connect to Exchange if your administrator has enabled IMAP access.  Contacts and calendering are more problematic.  Take a look at [SyncEvolution](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SyncEvolution).
