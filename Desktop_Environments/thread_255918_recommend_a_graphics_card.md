### Post by KalasMannen on 2006-09-12
I would go with a cheap Nvidia-card, maybe a 6200? If you won't use your computer for games or graphically demanding things, that would be enough. Seen thoose for both AGP and PCI-e for around 500 kronor here in sweden (~45$). I have no experience with this card in Ubuntu or Linux what so ever, but most nvidia-cards are generally supported by te oficial nvidia driver.

If you, on the other hand, would like to play some games i would go for something more expensive, perhaps a nvidia 6600. They perform quite good, and  isn't too expensive, around 2000 kronor (~150$).

### Post by Bagnaj97 on 2006-09-12
If you dont play games then you can get a cheap nvidia card from ebay. Anything >= geforce 2 would be fine with the standard nvidia drivers. For Nvidia cards older than geforce 2 you have to use the nvidia-legacy drivers.
