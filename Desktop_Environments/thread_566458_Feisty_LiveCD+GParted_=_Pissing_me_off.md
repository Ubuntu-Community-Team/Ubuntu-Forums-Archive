### Post by Rupertronco on 2007-10-03
If you look at this article, apcmag.com/dualboot it'll explain that the fiesty live cd will always mount the partitions, which renders them incapable of being formatted/partitioned.  As kellemes said the gparted live cd (found here [http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php)) will do everything you want it to.
