What is the most correct way to upgrade Jaunty so that it's using Firefox 3.5? Is it possible to do so without losing any of the bookmarks, add-ons, etc?

### Post by XubuRoxMySox on 2009-08-18
It's in the Ubuntu repositories. Open Synaptic Package Manager, find the Firefox that has the **[COLOR="Green"]green[/COLOR]** box next to it. Right-click it and choose "Mark for Removal." Then find Firefox 3.5 (it should have a white square next to it, indicating it's not installed). Mark it for installation. Click on Apply and watch the magic happen.

The new one might go by the name "Shiretoko" but it's Firefox 3.5. All your settings and favorites and even extensions will be preserved.

Enjoy!
