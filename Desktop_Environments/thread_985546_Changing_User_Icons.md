### Post by Ivo Moelans on 2008-11-17
Go to **System&#8594;Preferences&#8594;About Me**. In the window that opens, in the top left is a default picture. When you click on it a directory opens with pictures you can choose from. You can also navigate to a picture you made yourself. I use a picture of 96x96 pixels. You can use a larger picture but, as they say, your mileage may vary.

Hope this helps.
