### Post by professor_chaos on 2005-12-05
I would guess that your download copy you burnt is corrupt. The checksum of your copy must match the checksum of the file your tried to download. If not, then it indicates the overall file size is different and thus corrupted. You may want to try another download. You can also have ubuntu ship-it some cds to you for free, but it can take 6+ weeks to recieve them.
