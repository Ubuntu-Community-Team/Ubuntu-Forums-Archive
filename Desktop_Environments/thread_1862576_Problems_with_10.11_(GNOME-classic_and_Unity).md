### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-10-16
The ability to add and move applet icons was removed from the Unity panel in 11.04 . Many of the the things you have noticed were removed or changed by Gnome not Ubuntu . 

The Gnome developers decided to drop support for Gnome 2 which is no longer maintained . If you choose to use the current Gnome desktop environment (Gnome 3) you have the choice of Unity , the shell , or fall back mode . The last of which will be temporary .The color change option , screen saver and other things were removed by Gnome . 

Remember that there are other desktop environments , such as , XFCE , KDE , LXDE . There are not many Gnome 3 themes yet because it is new . Gnome 2 was released in 2002 so that is why there is so many available .
