### Post by skymt on 2006-09-08
I don't know what's causing the dialogs, I don't get them with non-executable files. However, unless you want to run files from Nautilus frequently, there's a workaround. Go to System > Preferences > File Management Preferences. Open the Behavior tab, and choose View executable files when they are clicked.
