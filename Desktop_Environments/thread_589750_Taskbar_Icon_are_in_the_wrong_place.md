### Post by 505 on 2007-10-24
You can indeed customise you panels. To move the icons again, rightclick on them and choose 'Move' If 'Lock to panel' is on, switch that of first. For some, such as the tray, you click on the little vertical bar before the icons, not on the icons themselves.
