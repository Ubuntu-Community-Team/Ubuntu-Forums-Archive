### Post by royskie on 2006-07-23
I'll try to answer q number 1.....you can export your Firefox bookmarks by:

1.  Launch Firefox, click on the Bookmarks menu.
2.  Click Manage Bookmarks.
3.  On the new window, click on FILE then click on EXPORT.
4.  Choose a name and location for teh HTML file.
5.  Sign on to your email (i.e. yahoo) and attach the file, then send it to yourself.
6.  Use ubuntu and read that mail, download the attachment. 
7.  Open Firefox and click on the Bookmarks menu.
8.  Click Manage Bookmarks.
9.  On the new window, click on FILE then click on IMPORT then..... I think you know the rest. ;)
