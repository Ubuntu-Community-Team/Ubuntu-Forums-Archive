### Post by Arthur D on 2007-05-06
I installed xubuntu yesterday and after I changed my network settings the taskbars disappeared from the top and bottom of the screen. I can't find a setting to turn them back on and rebooting doesn't make any difference. Other then reinstalling xubuntu I'm out of ideas.

---

### Post by john.nicholls on 2007-05-06
Open the run dialog (Alt-F2) and type xfce4-panel to get the panel back. Saving the session should make the change permanent.

### Post by Arthur D on 2007-05-06
That did the trick, thanks.
