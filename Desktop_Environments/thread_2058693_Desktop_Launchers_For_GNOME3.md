### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-09-16
I don't use gnome 3, but try copying the various application .desktop files of the applications you want from /usr/share/applications to the Desktop folder of your user.  I have no idea if it will work or not in gnome 3, but it's worth trying, just to find out.

After copying the files you may need to make sure they are executable, or you could get an "untrusted" error.
