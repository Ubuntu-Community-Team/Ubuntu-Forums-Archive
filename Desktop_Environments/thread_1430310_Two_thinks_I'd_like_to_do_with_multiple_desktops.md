### Post by nilarimogard on 2010-03-15
I only know how to get 1. to work:

in CompizConfig Settings Manager (CCSM), enable the "Fixed window placement" plugin, then in it's settings, under "Windows with fixed viewport", click on "new", then on the "+" sign and then "Grab" and select the window you want to place on a desktop different then the main one. Then, select it's X and Y viewport (desktop).
