### Post by chuckyanutsup on 2011-10-26
My apologies. I'm still pretty new to this. I found a solution, albeit rather drastic. I opened terminal and ran 
```
gconftool-2 --recursive-unset /apps/compiz-1
unity --reset

```then logged in to 2d and re-enabled the unity plugin in compiz. I lost all of my compiz tweaks though, so I'm going about re-adding them.

edit: I should add, I got this fix from [http://www.webupd8.org/2011/04/how-to-reset-unity-launcher-icons-or.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2011/04/how-to-reset-unity-launcher-icons-or.html) 
My os did freeze up a couple of times through the process, but this eventually got unity back.
