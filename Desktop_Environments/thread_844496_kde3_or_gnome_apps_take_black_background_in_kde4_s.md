### Post by ProbablyX on 2008-07-02
Maybe not the best solution, but in Kopete:
Settings -> Behaviour
"Open messages directly"
Then second tab "Actions" tick "Animate system tray"
"Treat all chats on the current desktop like active chats"

That should remove the constant gray popups :) Hopefully :D
