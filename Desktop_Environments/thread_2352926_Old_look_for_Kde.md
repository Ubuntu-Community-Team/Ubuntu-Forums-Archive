### Post by ajgreeny on 2017-02-17
If you are really feeling that you need the old look go to [https://wiki.trinitydesktop.org/UbuntuInstall#For_Xenial_.28Ubuntu_16.04_LTS.29](https://wiki.trinitydesktop.org/UbuntuInstall#For_Xenial_.28Ubuntu_16.04_LTS.29) where you will find a version of Ubuntu with a fork of the KDE 3.5 DE that you can install to your current OS.

I used to love KDE 3.5, but I tried it recently in VirtualBox and realised that I now can't stand it, but it's "horses for courses" as we say in UK, ie, everybody is different.

I used an iso image file from [http://mirror.ppa.trinitydesktop.org/trinity/cdimages/ubuntu](http://mirror.ppa.trinitydesktop.org/trinity/cdimages/ubuntu) to create my VM of this, but have since removed it.
