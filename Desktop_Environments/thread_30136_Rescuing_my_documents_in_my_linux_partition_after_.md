### Post by alastair on 2005-04-27
Do not worry - if it is only a boot/grub problem, your documents/data are safe on your hard disk (assuming your disk is OK!).

I think this sort of thing has been discussed a few times - so you would be best to search the forums for previous GRUB re-install questions.

You can also boot from a CD e.g. LiveCD or Knoppix, and mount your partitions to get your data.

But you don't say what sort of GRUB error.
