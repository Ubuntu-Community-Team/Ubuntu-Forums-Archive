### Post by slooksterpsv on 2010-10-09
Amazingly I fixed the issue, and here's how, I installed kde-full and restarted, now it's working. The previous attempts I've done with Kubuntu, have never worked, now I wonder if they had kde-full installed.

Also I got rid of those annoying notifications and installed colibri, the defaults notifications take up like 30% of your screen space and get in the way and popup over and over - especially if you get notified when someone signs in in messenger.
