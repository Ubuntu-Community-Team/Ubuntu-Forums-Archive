### Post by rufius on 2008-01-17
All you've got to do is install the xfce meta package. Then when GDM loads up (the login screen), you can just tell it to switch your session to XFCe.

If you like it, you can make it default, otherwise you have gnome at your disposal as well. 

Hope that helps :)
