You're going to have to manually restore the bottom panel. The layout goes:

Show Desktop Applet--Window list Applet--Workspace Switcher Applet--Trash Applet

Just add those applets move the Show Desktop applet to the far left and lock it. Then move the Window List Applet as far left as you can and lock it. Move the Trash Applet to the right and lock it and then the same for the Workspace Switcher. Bam! You've got your original bottom panel back.
