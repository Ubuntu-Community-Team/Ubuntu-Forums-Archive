### Post by Buntu Bunny on 2014-01-02
Welcome to the Ubuntu Forums, void092.

You could always add and rearrange your menus with settings > main menu. 

If you highlight an application and select properties, the edit launcher will allow you to change the icon. Just click the icon and "select an icon."

Not what you're asking for, I know, but it's a workaround.
