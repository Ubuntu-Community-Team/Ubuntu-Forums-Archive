### Post by orb9220 on 2006-09-12
For stability always stick with the default apps in synaptic. If you keep trying the latest and greatess you will break something sooner or latter.

They will sooner or latter show up in ubuntu repo's and have usally been tested.

I always amazed at the number of people that seem to have been conditioned to think just cuz it's new it must be better.

When I show up to fix thier computers I always ask them "And what was the reason you decided to update?"

Way too many people are not waiting a bit to see if 

1) The fix or upgrade is stable and does not break more things than they fix.
2) Does the upgrade actually do anything for me. If it ain't broke don't fix it.
