### Post by SuperSonic4 on 2009-12-19
[list=1]
[*]Right Click on the system tray
[*]Select *System Tray Settings*
[*]Select the *Auto Hide* tab on the left
[*]Untick the box which starts with *Automatically... *
[/list]

This will turn off icon hiding in the tray
