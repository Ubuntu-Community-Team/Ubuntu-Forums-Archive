### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-12-28
May I suggest that you open the "Applications" menu and look in there for the system utility that you wish to use? Ubuntu always has more than one link to the system utilities.

You could also try put a right mouse click on to the top panel and selecting Add to Panel. Scroll down the list and see if there is an option to put Administration back into the top panel.

When Administration in back in the top panel it can be moved to where you want it to be but be careful to to delete it.

Regards.
