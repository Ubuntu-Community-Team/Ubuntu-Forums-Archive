### Post by vtel57 on 2007-05-23
If you have the Work Space switcher applet set up in your panel, you can have T-bird running at all times in one of the unused work spaces. This is how I've always used T-bird in all my distros. T-bird is set to access my accounts every 15 minutes w/ a sound alert on new messages.

See [THIS](http://img.photobucket.com/albums/v647/vtel57/Screenshots/slackshot3.png) screenshot... T-bird in open in Work Space 4 on the right side, by the date/clock.
