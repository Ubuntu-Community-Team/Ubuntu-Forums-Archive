### Post by stream303 on 2006-01-16
With my Dell's i810/i815 onboard video, the only solution to poor video was to change the default color depth from 24 down to 16.

Check in your /etc/X11/xorg.conf file:

DefaultDepth 16

Easiest way is to sudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf
and either restart the gdm or just reboot.

This way I was able to keep my 1280x1024 resolution for my lcd screen.  This should help quit a lot.
