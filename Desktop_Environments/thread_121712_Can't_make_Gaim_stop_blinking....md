### Post by slide on 2006-01-27
For gaim 2 beta 1 goto Tools->Plugins (or hit CTRL+U) then find "Message Notification" in the list and hit "Configure Plugin" then check all that you want in the "Notification Removal" section. I had this problem too and it annoyed the hell out of me hehe. Hope this helps :)
