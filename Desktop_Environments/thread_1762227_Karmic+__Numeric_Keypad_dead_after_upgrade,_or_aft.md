### Post by harrismh777 on 2011-05-18
On Karmic, and I presume other(s) the numeric keypad will appear to be non-functional, even though the num-lock light is on. 

This occurs because of a default setting in:

  System &#8594; Preferences &#8594; Keyboard (Mouse Keys tab)

Uncheck the default "pointer may be controlled using keypad"
