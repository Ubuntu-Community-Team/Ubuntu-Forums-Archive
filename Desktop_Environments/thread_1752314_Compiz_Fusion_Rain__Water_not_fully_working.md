### Post by Beowulf.1000 on 2011-05-07
SOLVED: So simple a solution. So frustrating initially (I think Compiz changed from what I was used to). Just had to go into Compiz settings manager, the Water effect, and click the key combo for 'Initiate' and toggle OFF the check box to that is NOT active. The 'initiate' option is for the water/rain effect for mouse movements or whatever, just toggle it off then the Shift+F9 works perfect for constant raid drips!
