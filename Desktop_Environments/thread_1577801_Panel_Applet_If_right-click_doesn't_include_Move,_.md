### Post by kidoruigenso on 2010-11-21
I had the same issue with the NetworkManager applet icon in the panel.

What worked for me was reply #5 by coffeecat in
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1435356&page=1](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1435356&page=1)
"You have to right-click on the notification area to the left of the  Network Manager icon to get the Lock to panel and move choices. It's  virtually invisible in Lucid. You'll need to explore with the mouse."

And coffeecat is right, you have to search...just to the left...of the NetworkManager applet icon.
