### Post by EmilW on 2007-05-05
You can fix this (if you haven't already figured it out) by right clicking on the "kicker" (the bottom panel) and select "Add to Panel".  Then under "Desktops and Windows" choose "[COLOR="Red"]Windows List[/COLOR]".

This is for gnome, which I have loaded (Ubuntu Feisty Fawn 7.04). I think for KDE you have to do something slightly different.

I know this works because I had the same problem all morning, and when I found the answer, I remembered you had the same problem I had.
