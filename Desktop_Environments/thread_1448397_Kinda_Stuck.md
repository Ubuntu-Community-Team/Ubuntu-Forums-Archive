### Post by bigsmitty64 on 2010-04-06
> **blando said:**
>  im looking for when you close the windows it like burns up or whatever
Go to system/administration/synaptic package manager and type **simple-ccsm**. Install it, and then go to system/preferences/simple compiz config settings manager. At the top change "profile" to ultimate.
Then on the "animations" tab, enable animations, and extra animations.This will give you the "extra animations, like "burn"

I'm trying real hard to remember how I got the cube to zoom out or in further, when I remember I'll let ya know :)
**Edit:**Found it! It was one of those "in your face" things that you just can't find!

In your normal Compiz Config Settings Manager, NOT **simple ccsm** Go to "rotate cube" and its right at the bottom on the "general" tab
