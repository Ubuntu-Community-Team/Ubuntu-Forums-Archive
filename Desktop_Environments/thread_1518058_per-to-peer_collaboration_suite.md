### Post by SaintDanBert on 2010-06-26
Can someone recommend linux desktop software that will enable two folks to collaborate in real time?  

I'd like to see their desktop as a window on my desktop and visa versa.
I'd like a shared "white board" or other digital ink space.
I'd like to have VOIP and desktop video sharing so that we can video-chat.

I know that there are several distinct apps for parts of this, but I hope someone has arranged some sort of integration.

My thought began with the 'woof' utility
[http://directory.fsf.org/project/woof/](http://directory.fsf.org/project/woof/)
I launch it at my desk and it tells me what to tell my colleague
so that they can connect back to me. From there we can pass some
files back and forth. If you don't know 'woof' it is wonderful.
