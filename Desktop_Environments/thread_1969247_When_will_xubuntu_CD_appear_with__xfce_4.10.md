### Post by 3Miro on 2012-04-29
xfce 4.10 will not be officially part of Xubuntu until the 12.10 release (6 months from now). 

You can run xfce 4.10 with 12.04 as soon as someone makes an unofficial ppa. Shouldn't be too long, but unofficial ppa are potentially buggy.

Alternatively, you can do this today, but you have to compile it yourself (not recommended unless you know what you are doing).
