### Post by Irony on 2005-12-29
This is what you do;

First make a back up of your xorg.conf file;

*sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf etc/X11/xorg.conf_backup*

Now edit the file;

*sudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf*

[I]Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"AOC Spectrum"
	Option		"DPMS"
	HorizSync	28-49
	VertRefresh	43-72[/I]

Your actual figures you will have to determine from the manual or googling. Alternatively if the Live CD shows the correct resolution you can look at its refresh figures.

Reboot. If rebooting ends up in terminal due to incorrect figures then type;

*sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf_backup etc/X11/xorg.conf*

Then type;

*reboot*
