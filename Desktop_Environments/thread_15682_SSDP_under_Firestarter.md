### Post by chusome on 2005-11-22
SSDP is Simple Service Discovery Protocol aka UPnP, which is a good one to block, even according to MS. Odds are you have a router in front of your computer. If you don't want to recieve these packets any longer disable UPnP on the router. (usually under the security section of the router software).
