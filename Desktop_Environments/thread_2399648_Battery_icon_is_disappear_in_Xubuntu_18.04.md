### Post by ajgreeny on 2018-08-29
I have now just started my laptop with Xubuntu 18.04 and found that to show the battery icon in the panel you need to open power-manager-settings from the settings manager.  In the general tab you can choose either to show or hide the system tray icon, an option that was not available in 16.04, as far as I can remember.
