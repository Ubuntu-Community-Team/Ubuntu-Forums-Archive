### Post by accuser on 2005-04-11
What you need to do is to inform the greeter what session you would like to use. You can make this change permanent (e.g. use XFCE4 for a week, then change to GNOME for a week) or just for the next session. To do this, click the Session 'button' at the bottom of the screen before you have entered you username/password.

Enjoy!
