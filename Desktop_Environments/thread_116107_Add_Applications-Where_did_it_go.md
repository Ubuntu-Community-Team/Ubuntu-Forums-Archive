### Post by jasay on 2006-01-12
Hmm.  I tried it just to test and Applications > sound and video *is* where it came up.  Anyway, if it is installed, you can make a launcher for it.  Right click on the applications menu and select edit menu.  Go to sound and video on the left and hit new entry.  Fill it in like so:```
Name: Sound Converter
Comments: Convert Sounds From One Format To Another
Command: soundconverter
icon: /usr/share/gnome-app-install/icons/_usr_share_soundconverter_logo.png
```
