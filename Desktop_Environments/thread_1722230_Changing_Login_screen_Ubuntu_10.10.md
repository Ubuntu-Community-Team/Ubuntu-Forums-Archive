### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-04-05
Where exactly did you download them? Gnome-Look.org?

It's a bit trickier to change login themes in Ubuntu than in some other distributions. Check out the package [ubuntu-tweak]("http://ubuntu-tweak.com/") to change the wallpaper and the computer's icon logo. You can of course also change the icon image that appears for your user account by going to Preferences -> About Me. You can't really change the skin of the login window and panel, if that's what you're going for (I mean, you can, but it requires installing a slightly risky unsupported package.)
