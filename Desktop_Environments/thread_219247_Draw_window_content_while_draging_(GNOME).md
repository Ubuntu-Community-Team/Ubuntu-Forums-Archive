### Post by tturrisi on 2006-07-19
Applications > System Tools > Configuration Editor > Apps > Metacity > General > reduced_resources
will hide window contents while dragging and show wire frames while dragging IF you also do this: System > Preferences > Assistive Technology Support > uncheck Enable assistive technologies.
