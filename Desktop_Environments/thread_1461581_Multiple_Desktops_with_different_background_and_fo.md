### Post by bigsmitty64 on 2010-04-26
In my experience, you can have the separate wallpapers **BUT** it takes away desktop icons, here's how in Compiz,

In compizconfig settings manager:

click utility then  enable "wallpaper" ,  then click to go into the "wallpaper" editing.

click on "new" to choose your 4 wallpapers from where ever you have them stored.  

LIke I said this works great, **BUT** no desktop icons
