### Post by Dennis N on 2020-12-01
You could:

1) use the Activities overview (super key or hot corner) and drag Firefox window to another workspace in the workspace display at the right; you can also move windows between workspaces within the workspace display of the overview.
 
2) restore the Firefox title bar from Settings  > Customize > check "title bar" in lower left.
