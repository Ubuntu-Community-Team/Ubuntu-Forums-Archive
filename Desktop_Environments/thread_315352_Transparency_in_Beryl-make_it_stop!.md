### Post by _simon_ on 2006-12-09
Right click on your beryl icon.

Select "Beryl Settings Manager"

Scroll down to "Set Window Attribs By Various Criteria" and remove anything that is listed in the top box "Window Opacity" or untick the plugin altogether.

Now scroll down to "Trailfocus" and untick the plugin.

That should remove the transparency for you!
