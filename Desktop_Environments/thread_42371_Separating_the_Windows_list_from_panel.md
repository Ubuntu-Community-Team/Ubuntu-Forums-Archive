### Post by shakin on 2005-06-17
So, you just want the task bar on its own panel?

1. Right-click on the panel in an empty spot, go to Remove From Panel -> Applet -> Taskbar.

2. Create a new panel by right-clicking on the panel again and going to Add To Panel -> Panel -> Panel.

3. Drag the panel to whichever place you want it. It probably defaults to the top of the screen.

4. Right-click on the panel and go to Add To Panel -> Applet -> Taskbar.

Done.
