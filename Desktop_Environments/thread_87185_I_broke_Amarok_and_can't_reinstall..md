### Post by ljamie82 on 2005-11-07
If you go into Synaptic as soon as you open the program it should notice you have a broken package and give you the option to automatically fix it.  It's a great program.  Or at least it does for me.  Alot of times when i download source and don't feel like compiling it (or am unable to compile it for one reason or another) i just doubleclick on the make file in the folder, of course that doesn't really do anything, but it makes the system know its there, then i open synaptic and synaptic sees it as a broken package and auto installs it for me!  it's awesome.
