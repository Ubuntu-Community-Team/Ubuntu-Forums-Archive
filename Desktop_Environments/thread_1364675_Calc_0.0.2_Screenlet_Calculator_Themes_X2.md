### Post by User3k on 2009-12-26
I decided to try and make a couple of themes for "Calc 0.0.2 by Cobra" I really hated the default one and the only other one I could find at Gnome-Look.org did not work well at all with my over all desktop theme (I like a dark desktop.)

So here is my very first two themes I have ever done (not counting the failed attempts, lol) and this is also the first time I ever worked with .svg During my first several attempts the themes where so bogged down that there was a lot of lag using Calc, about a second (or two) from the time I hit the number to when it showed up. I found out what I was doing wrong and tried to keep it simple after that.

Let me know what you think. I am open to tips, tricks and any advice with theme creating and especially with .svg

* Skull-Dark has a skull and crossbones (Which Disappear when the Calculator is in use.)

* Clear-Dark is the same theme as above without the skull and crossbones.


The way to install themes for this is to open the Screenlets manager, Click on Calc and then on the left "Install New Theme"
Restart Calc and then right click the Calc screenlet, go to themes and choose the new theme.

Edit: I just finished uploading this to gnome-look.org as well.
