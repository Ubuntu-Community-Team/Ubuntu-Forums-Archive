### Post by resolv_25 on 2010-06-10
Right click on the menu (like Applications).
Then choose new menu, or new item.
Put the name and command: *wine*
You may change the icon if you want.

However, this part is buggy, at least it was on 8.04 Hardy, and have problems with this panel, disappear from time to time, and have no this option anymore of adding items on menu.
