### Post by vasa1 on 2016-06-01
I'm guessing you can. How exactly, depends on your theme. But most gtk3 themes (the ones that can be easily edited) have something like:```
    -GtkRange-slider-width: 9;

``` in "/path/to/theme/gtk-3.0/gtk-widgets.css"
