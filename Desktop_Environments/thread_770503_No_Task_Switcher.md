### Post by Rigrig on 2008-04-27
Looks like the keyboard shortcut for the application switcher was unset in Hardy, to enable it:

Open System>Preferences>Keyboard Shortcuts
Under 'Window Management' click on 'Move between windows with popup' and press Alt+Tab

Now pressing Alt-Tab should show the window switcher again.
