### Post by keithp on 2006-06-14
I have an Epson Stylus Color670 printer and can't get Dapper to print. 

There were no problems with Breezy, or any other distro I have used.

In Breezy, I just selected system->administration->printing  and selected my printer and the STcolor driver. 

With Dapper, I have tried all the drivers in the list the printer gave me:

STcolor (recommended)
Gutenprint
stc600ih.upp
stc670pl.upp
stc670p.upp

none of them work. They won't even print a test page.

I have downloaded and installed everything which may be of use, but nothing. I'm up-to-date with the Dapper updates.

The "printer icon" on the top panel shows "printing: job printing".

Please, has anyone any ideas how to get Dapper to print? I can't find this problem in either the mailing list or forum archives.

Any advice greatfully received as I don't know hwat else to do.

Many thanks

Keith

### Post by keithp on 2006-06-14
I have sorted the problem, and Dapper now prints.

When setting up the printer, I had to click on "Use another printer by specifying a port". Then select "Parallel Port #1 EPSON"

That fixed it.

Cheers

Keith
