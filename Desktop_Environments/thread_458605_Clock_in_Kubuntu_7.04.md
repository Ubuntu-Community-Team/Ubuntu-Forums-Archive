### Post by teejay17 on 2007-05-29
Can anyone please tell me how to change my clock to 12 hour time in Kubuntu 7.04? I've tried right-clicking every option on the clock, and I can't find it!

---

### Post by scruffyminds on 2007-05-29
Right click the clock and select "Date & Time Format" (or go thru the menu and select System Settings->Country/Region & Language->Date & Time Format) then change the "Time Format" on the "Time & Dates" tab to "pH:MM:SS AMPM".  You will need to log out & back into KDE for the changes to take effect.
