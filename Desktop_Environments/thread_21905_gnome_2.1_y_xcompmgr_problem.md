### Post by Dromio on 2005-03-24
[QUOTE=yoshic]hi, i've just installed xcompmgr, and when  i log out of  gnome, it  freezes,  what is wrong??? does anybody can help me????? please i need help!!!! :sad:[/QUOTE]
 There's a dialog that appears when you logout of gnome.  For some reason, xcompmgr makes that dialog hidden.  Just wait a second, then press the "Enter" key on your keyboard to dismiss the dialog, even though you do not see it.  It should then continue to logout normally.
