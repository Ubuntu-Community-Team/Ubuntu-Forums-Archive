### Post by philidox on 2008-11-22
GOOD NEWS. I got it working!!! Here is what I did. Before I even logged in I hit "alt+f2" to get the cli. I unistalled envyng(sudo apt-get remove envyng-core envyng-gtk envgng-qt) . I also typed in that command that resets your xorg.conf file(sudo dpkg-reconfigure -phigh xserver-xorg). Then I downloaded the lastest Nvidia driver for linux directly from there website on another computer. Transfered it to my computer that I was fixing and installed it thru the cli.
