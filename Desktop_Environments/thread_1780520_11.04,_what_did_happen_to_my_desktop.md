### Post by MARP1961 on 2011-06-12
No need to change your automatic logon. If you need, you can always get to the login screen by going to the shutdown button next to your name.'Logout' is one of the menu options. Click your name on the logon screen and select what desktop/GUI you would like to use. You can also experiment with other desktop styles such as KDE or XFCE (if you install them).
