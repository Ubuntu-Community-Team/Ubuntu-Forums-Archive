### Post by Zorb on 2006-04-19
Hi Zorb, thanks for posting.

Gnome does the automounting. A replacement for this is a program called ivman, which sometimes works, but doesn't seem to be 100%.

While Ubuntu works fine without Gnome, there's very little support or advice telling you how to install your own DE or WM, and likewise few warnings that this type of problem will occur if you do.

You're better off installing Ubuntu-Lite or Xubuntu (which uses the Gnomish, but smaller, XFCE) because these systems have most of the necessary replacements for things like auto-mounting in them, rather than installing IceWM or XFCE off your own bat.

Zorb.
(Yes, I found it out myself! Only took four days.)
