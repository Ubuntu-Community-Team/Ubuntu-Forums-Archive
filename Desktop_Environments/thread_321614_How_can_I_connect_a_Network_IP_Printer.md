### Post by jazzgossen on 2006-12-19
If your printer uses HP JetDirect, then it's really easy. Just choose network printer, HP JetDirect from the "add printer" dialog. Then enter the printer's IP address, and follow the rest of the setup wizard. You can get the printer's IP address by printing the printer's configuration (from the menu on the printer itself).
