### Post by Kognit on 2010-03-31
I have quite a problem adding a launcher to the AWN dock. I read forums and other people have problems too. So, the solution is like that:

-you can add a launcher when the application is running and visible on the dock with the right mouse click on it and select "Add as a laucher"

-the other way is to go to "dock preferences", choose "task manager", "add", and create a launcher like usual in Ubuntu. Then click "+ advanced" and choose "use start-up notification".

That will work!
