### Post by asmoore82 on 2010-09-04
This is one of the changes in 10.04 "Lucid" or just before ...

Right click the panel itself and pick "Add to panel"
Then find and add the **"Indicator Applet"**

*... in earlier releases you have to find and add "Volume Control"*
