### Post by ajgreeny on 2015-04-02
Only by unlocking the panel and placing it vertically on the left but right at the top, then as you add items or open windows  it will grow down towards the bottom, assuming you have the "Window buttons" applet in the panel.

You can also do this the other way round if you wish, ie grow from the bottom up though new items are always added to the bottom of the panel.

So position the top where you want it; simple as that. Unlock it and drag it where you want, then lock it again.
