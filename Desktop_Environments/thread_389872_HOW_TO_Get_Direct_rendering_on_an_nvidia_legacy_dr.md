I usually make things like this beacuse of the fact that I am waiting for someone to help me in my thread. So to let time pass I get these suggestions. Today I will be telling you how to install direct rendering on your Nvidia legacy driver offline. I only do offline suggestion beacuse I don't have internet on my computer witch is an awsome ubuntu edjy eft 6.10.

***_The things you need:_***
1. An old nvidia card. (Not saying go out there and but one if your nvidia card is nice and brand spanking new. This is just for people with old nvidia card.) I will tell you witch ones are probable your nvidia card after the instruction.)

2.Flash drive, cd-r or cd-rw, or anymeans of data storage.



***_The installation_***

Step 1: Go to a computer that has internet. Maybe the libary or your own computer if you do have internet.

Step 2: Go to[http://packages.ubuntu.com/edgy/misc/nvidia-glx-legacy]("http://packages.ubuntu.com/edgy/misc/nvidia-glx-legacy") and download the package for your system. (mine was i386) Then download the packages that have the red dot near it because those are the dependencies for the package. Just letting you know that you would download a new kernel modual along with its dependencies. Just keep downloading until you have all the dependencies for the dependencies and so on.

Step 3: Put all the packages on your means of data storage like a flash drive or something and bring oit to your computer, or if you have internet on your computer you would not need to do anything because all the .deb packages are on your computer already. Click on the package and it will take you to the Gdebi package manager. Click on install, and keep doing this until you have your legacy driver installed.

Step 4: Go to the terminal and type in nvidia-xconfig and that will connect to your xorg.

Step 5: Type in sudo nano -w /etc/X11/xorg.conf and go down to where it says section modules. Load the dri and the glcore and the glx. Line the word Load with the other Loads and like the word dri and glcore and glx with the other words on the right side. (Just letting you know that 1: You have to put a "" in front and back of each word. 2: Each one has its own word Load,just like the others that were there before you typed that in. 3: Before the word Load on "dri" and "glcore" you need the # before that word. I don't know why just do if you want nice screensavers and awsome looking beryl graphics.(No one has helped me with that matter.)

Step 6: Go all the way down to the end and type in 

section "Extenstions"
Composite "Disable"
end section

then type in ctrl+x y return to save the file. Rebot then your done.

Step 7: Celbrate over the victore of direct rendering. (LOL)
