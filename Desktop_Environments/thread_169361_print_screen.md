### Post by tonyr on 2006-05-02
In **GNOME** it's Applications->Accessories->Take Screenshot.  That gets you
the whole screen, which you would have to edit (**gimp** or something else) to
get the area of interest.  In **KDE,** it's **KSnapShot** in the Kmenu
somehwere.  That one is much more flexible, allowing capture of whole screens, 
windows, arbitrary regions.

**gimp** is reported to have a screenshot feature.  The X utility **xv**  (not
installed by default) is reported to have a screenshot feature.   

Images, once captured, can be inserted directly in the post. Use the mountain
looking icon at the top of the editor window.

Files can be **attached** to your post.  Use the paper clip icon at the top 
of the editor window.
