### Post by Krytarik on 2011-03-31
Hey, how about switching to "Classic Desktop" and using AWN as the side panel instead?
See here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1714111](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1714111)

You could remove the bottom panel, move the top panel to the bottom, and add the then missing applets to it. And just remove the items you don't want in it. Or do it the other way around.

And you would have a usual desktop.

### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-03-31
Point of fact, you wouldn't have to follow the entire how-to, because you want to keep one Gnome Panel for the application switcher. You'll need to install and configure AWN-trunk, then remove the lower Panel and move the top panel to the bottom.

That's for Maverick or earlier. In the Classic Desktop in Natty, you'll also need to right-click and remove the Global Menu applet and replace it with the Window List.
