### Post by Jucato on 2007-04-05
The full Oxygen icon set will only be available for KDE 4. One of the biggest reasons is that the naming convention used for KDE 4 will follow the Freedesktop.org Icon naming convention, which is incompatible with the current one used in KDE 3.x. 

There were "snapshots" of the early versions of the icons that were usable on KDE 3.5.x, however, the "license" didn't allow the distribution of the icon theme, so you won't probably find them easily.

### Post by teabeartom on 2007-04-09
Many of the icons have already been completed (kompleted :-)). 

They are available for downloading here:

[http://websvn.kde.org/trunk/KDE/kdelibs/pics/oxygen/](http://websvn.kde.org/trunk/KDE/kdelibs/pics/oxygen/)

Just click on the folder for the icon size you want, then click the category, and when you click on  "icon.png" it will take you to a page, and at the top you have to click on the link that says "Download". 

This method will get you the icons you want one by one.  Good luck.  You may just want to wait for KDE 4 to come out so it will feel nice and new to you as this may be like opening your christmas presents before christmas...
