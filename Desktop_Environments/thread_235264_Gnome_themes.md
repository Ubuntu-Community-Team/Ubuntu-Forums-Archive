### Post by bulldog on 2006-08-12
I don't think so.
A theme is an icon-set and borderstyle with colours and all that.
But I've never seen a font choice in my themes.

I think that it means you can use every font and background you like in your theme of choice,but it's not a part of it.

You could use cgwd and cgwd-themes if you're using xgl/compiz.
It gives you the opportunity to change your border-style, colours AND the font-style to your likings and save them.
Or you could use the theme as it is ofcourse,with no altering.:)
