### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-05-20
The quick way would be to reset your Compiz settings, as explained [_here_]("http://www.webupd8.org/2011/04/how-to-reset-unity-launcher-icons-or.html").

To go back to the Classic desktop, log out and change the Session in the box that appears below the login window after you select your username. However, I don't think you'll see a very big change in performance over Unity. You might consider installing Openbox and running Openbox Gnome (which will appear in the same Session menu) until you get your replacement RAM in.
