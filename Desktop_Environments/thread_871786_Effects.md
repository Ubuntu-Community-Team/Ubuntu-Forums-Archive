### Post by brimlar on 2008-07-27
Go to System > Administration and select Synaptic Package Manager.

Enter your password.

Once Synaptic opens, click the Search button and type in: "compiz" (without the quotes.)

A list of results will return, and among those right-click the "compizconfig-settings-manager" item and choose "Install."

This will add an applet to your System > Preferences menu called Advanced Desktop Effects Settings -- there you can customize every little piece of the desktop effects.
