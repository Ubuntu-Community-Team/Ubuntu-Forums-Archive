### Post by komputes on 2007-12-09
The principal design goals of Edubuntu are centralized management of configuration, users and processes, together with facilities for working collaboratively in a classroom setting.

It is made for thin clients (diskless terminals) and older computers (which can also be used as diskless terminals). This is refered to as LTSP. One central server and many distributed clients (which in most cases are diskless, so quite inexpensive). If you want to turn on Remote desktop protocol go to System>Preferences>Remote Desktop. I'm not sure about a method to control all f them, but with some scripting/programming it could be possible.
