I googled around and found similar problems, but most of them were a couple years old and not on Kubuntu. Most said to try removing ~/.kde or ~/.config and let them be regenerated automatically. That didn't work for me.

[http://mandrivausers.org/index.php?showtopic=29623](http://mandrivausers.org/index.php?showtopic=29623)
