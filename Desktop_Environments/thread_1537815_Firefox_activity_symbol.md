### Post by dv3500ea on 2010-07-24
The dotted circle is just a generic loading symbol. You will find that lots of online videos have something similar. Firefox uses it to mean 'page loading'. The mouse cursor symbol is analogous to the hour glass symbol in windows and means 'program loading'. As far as I know, Firefox was always meant to be cross-platform.
