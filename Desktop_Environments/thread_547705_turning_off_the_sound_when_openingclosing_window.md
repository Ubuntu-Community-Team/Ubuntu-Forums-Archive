### Post by benerivo on 2007-09-10
To turn off the sounds when you close a window by going in the Control Centre, and going to Sound & Multimedia > System Notifications. In this screen, go in to 'Event Source' at the top and choose 'KDE Window Manager'. Choose 'a window closes' and untick 'play a sound'.
