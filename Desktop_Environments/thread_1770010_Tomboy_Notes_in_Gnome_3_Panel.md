### Post by rsoika on 2011-11-12
Yes it is. Start 'gnome-session-properties'. This is the gnome autostarter. There you can add the tomboy application by adding the command
 /usr/bin/tomboy

This will start tomboy after your next login. 
NOTE: tomboy did not integrate into the top panel but into the hidden panel on the bottom of your desktop. Move the mouse to the right-bottom corner!
