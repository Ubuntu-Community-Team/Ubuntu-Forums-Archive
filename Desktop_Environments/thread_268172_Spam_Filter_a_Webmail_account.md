### Post by Old Pink on 2006-09-29
Get a GMail account. 

Set everything at your current email except emails from the GMail address to be forwarded to the GMail address.

Use GMail's amazing spam filtering. 

Use GMail to set all mail that isn't spam to be sent to your current address. :)

### Post by nthdegree on 2006-09-29
If you have trouble signing up drop another GoogleMail utilising Ubuntu user a message and he/she will probably send an invite to your email address if he/she has any spare invites left.

You can use POP3 with GoogleMail and all the mail will be archived if you want it to be so you can check your online GMail archives for old e-mails while getting all the spam that gets past the spam filters (very little) filtered by your mail client.

There's the choice of "Leave E-Mail in the Inbox" till you go online and delete it "Archive Mail" so you can read old e-mail in the archives and "Delete Mail From POP3 Server" so it erases as soon as it gets to your hard disk.
