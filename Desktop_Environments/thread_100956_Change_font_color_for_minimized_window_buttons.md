### Post by CitizenKane on 2006-05-28
In kcontrol
under personal go to appearance
and then select colors

At this point much experimenting will ensue. 
In the widget color box, you can select whatever widget necessary (in this case some kind of text) and then change the color.  I know changing "window text" will change the color of the text for active taskbar item (and a look of other stuff ).  Could always try new color schemes at [KDE Look]("http://www.kde-look.org").

I would suggest trying to play around with those settings to see what you come up with.
