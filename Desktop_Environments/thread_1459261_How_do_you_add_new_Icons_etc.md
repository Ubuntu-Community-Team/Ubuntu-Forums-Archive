### Post by x1a4 on 2010-04-21
Hi,

Icons are in /usr/share/icons/, /usr/share/pixmaps/ and ~/.local/share/icons/
GTK/Mouse/Icon/Window Manager themes: /usr/share/themes/
GDM themes: /usr/share/gdm/themes/
Fonts: /usr/share/fonts/

Visit [xfce-look.org]("http://xfce-look.org/"), [box-look.org]("http://box-look.org/"), [gnome-look.org]("http://gnome-look.org/") [ubuntu-art.org]("http://ubuntu-art.org/") for themes and artwork.
