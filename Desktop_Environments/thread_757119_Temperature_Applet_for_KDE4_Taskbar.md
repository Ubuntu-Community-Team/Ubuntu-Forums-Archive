### Post by shanghaipi on 2008-04-16
As far as plasma widgets or taskbar applets, KDE4 does not have a temperature monitor yet.  There might be one in the svn for KDE 4.1, but not in the current KDE 4.0.3.  You could install "conky" or superkaramba (as you pointed out).  There is probably a simpler superkaramba widget for what your looking for.  If not, you could always use conky, which is very very basic.
