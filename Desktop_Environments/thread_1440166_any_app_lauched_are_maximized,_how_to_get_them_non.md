### Post by spiderbatdad on 2010-03-27
Hi and welcome to the forums.
Go into System>>Preferences>>Startup Applications. Uncheck "Maximus Window Manager."
UNR is definitely a little different. ctrl-alt <left or right arrow> is a nice feature for moving in and out of separate workspaces.
