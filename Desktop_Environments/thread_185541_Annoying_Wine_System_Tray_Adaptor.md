### Post by celem on 2006-05-31
Sorry, I missed it. I DO see a Picassa Icon that lingers. However, it is correct in that it is the Media Detector that continues to run, looking for a connection to camera media, just like it does on Windows. If you click on the icon and select Stop Media Detector, it will go away. Detecting is a normal part of Picassa.
