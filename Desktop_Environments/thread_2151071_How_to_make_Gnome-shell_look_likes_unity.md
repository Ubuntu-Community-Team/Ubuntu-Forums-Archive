### Post by Frogs Hair on 2013-06-03
I believe that ppa is for the the Classic DE and not the shell. In  Classic, use Alt + Right Click or Super +Alt + Right Click on the panels to add items. Modifications to the shell which has no right click add to panel option  are done with extensions.   [https://extensions.gnome.org/](https://extensions.gnome.org/)
