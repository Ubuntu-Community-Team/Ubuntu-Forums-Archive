### Post by XDevHald on 2005-06-19
You could easily get the tar.gz file, save it to your desktop, open System > Preferences > Theme. Drag and drop the tar to the theme window and click "Install". If the file is a tar.bz2 and is not working, untar the file, save the main folder to your desktop, right click on the folder and "Create Archive" and it will rename it to a tar.gz ball and it will recompress the whole set, then do the drag and drop again and it will work.
