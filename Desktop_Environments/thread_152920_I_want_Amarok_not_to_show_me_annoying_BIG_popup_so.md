### Post by amanda on 2008-03-25
Word. I also, was not looking for a setting for "OSD" which is just the kind of acronym that is just a totally made up acronym.

It turns out that you can move the on screen display (aka the really annoying song title popup) to the corner, or you can set it to be a lot smaller, or to stay up not so very long.

Thanks for the tip. 

Just to recap, for people who might have gotten here by searching for "that damn annoying amarok popup thing" and are already tired and irritable: look under 

Settings > Configure Amarok ... > OSD 

and either turn "OSD" off or tweak it to your liking.
