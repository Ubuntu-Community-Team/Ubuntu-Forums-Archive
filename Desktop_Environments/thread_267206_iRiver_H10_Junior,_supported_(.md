### Post by got_nix on 2006-09-28
The device will not work with linux by default. They are MTP devices and need media player 10 on it to work. I have the iriver T30 and was really disappointed with it because of that had me using a win partition as well. but there was a solution convert it to a UMS device (usb mass storage) just get the firmware upgrade application for it and there'll be a button to the bottom left that says ums <-> mtp (and vice versa) click it and it'll do it for you. plug it out it installs new firmware makes changes. reboot. plug it in your linux system and it pops up like a normal drive.
