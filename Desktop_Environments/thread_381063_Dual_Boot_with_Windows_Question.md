### Post by rsambuca on 2007-03-11
Yeah, it works fine.  My only advice would be to make sure that your partitions are set the way you want them prior to installing Vista.  It seems that people are having more problems after re-sizing pre-existing Vista partitions.  Then install ubuntu and grub will take care of the rest.

I have XP, Vista, and 4 linux distros installed and I haven't had any problems yet.
