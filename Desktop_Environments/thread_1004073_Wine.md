### Post by snova on 2008-12-06
"Configure" it? That won't make it any better, if that's what you want. Wine is good for some things, but it is not capable of running everything.

Whether it will work depends on the product. Some work better than others. There is a database of apps known to work/not work at: [http://appdb.winehq.org/](http://appdb.winehq.org/)

If Wine fails, there is a commercial variant called Crossover that may work better. But even Crossover has its limits.
