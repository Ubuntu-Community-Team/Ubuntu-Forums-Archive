### Post by mclode on 2011-10-19
Here is what I just did...

From Evolution go to Edit > Preferences
Edit your account
Go to the Defaults Tab
Choose the "Sent Messages Folder". You can even choose to save in your Gmail "Sent Mail" folder.

My messages are now sent and saved.
