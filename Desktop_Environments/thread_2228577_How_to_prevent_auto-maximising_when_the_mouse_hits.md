### Post by Paddy Landau on 2014-06-08
On a fresh installation of 14.04, when I move a window to the top of the screen and my mouse hits the top of the screen, the window maximises.

I don't want that. I just want the window to move to the top of the screen.

How do I turn off this Windows-like feature? I have looked through CCSM settings carefully, but have not figured out this one.

---

### Post by Paddy Landau on 2014-06-08
I've just found it!

CCSM > Window Management > Grid > Corners/Edges > Top Edge.
