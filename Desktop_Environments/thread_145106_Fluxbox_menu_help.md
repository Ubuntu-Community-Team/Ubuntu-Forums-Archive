### Post by Spano on 2006-03-15
Open the app, move it to were you like.  Right click on the window title bar (the very top part of the window, i think it's called the title bar) you get a drop down menu, select "remember", select "postion" and click.  When you re-open the app it will be in the same positon you closed it in.  You can also "remember" the size and workspace of the app.  
Is that what you're looking for?
