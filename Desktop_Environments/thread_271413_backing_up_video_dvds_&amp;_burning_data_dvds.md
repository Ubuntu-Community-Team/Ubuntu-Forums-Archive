### Post by therunnyman on 2006-10-04
I'm using the now olde Pioneer A05, and it works great under Edgy and Breezy (I can't speak to Dapper).  Bet you could steal one from eBay, had you a mind to do that.

As far as software goes, K3b could be the single finest CD/DVD recording program ever.  Never used dvd::rip, so I can't say anything about that.  Here's something though: I hear tell of these two programs, DVD Decrypter and DVD Shrink, that will back up even copy-protected DVDs.   You then simply burn the ISO image reportedly produced by these two programs using K3b, I hear.  They say you have to install wine to use the abovementioned backup combo, but it's rumored that that's no big deal.
