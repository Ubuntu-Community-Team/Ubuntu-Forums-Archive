### Post by smartboyathome on 2009-01-28
Settings > Settings Panel > Apps > New Application
Name the application what you want, give it the command gnome-settings-daemon
Settings > Settings Panel > Apps > Startup Applications
Select your freshly made gnome-settings-daemon application.
