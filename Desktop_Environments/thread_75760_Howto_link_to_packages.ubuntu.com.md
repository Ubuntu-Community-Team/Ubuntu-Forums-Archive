### Post by manicka on 2005-10-14
Yes, the packages available at packages.ubuntu.com are the same ones as in the repos. just use apt or synaptic to install anything you find there. 

You may need to add the universe amd multiverse repos to apt to get them. You can do this with synaptic by going to 

Settings-->Repositories-->add,  then use the drop down boxes and check boxes to add universe and multiverse.
