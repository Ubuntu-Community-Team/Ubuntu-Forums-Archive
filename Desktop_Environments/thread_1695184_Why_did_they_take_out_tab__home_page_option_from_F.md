### Post by kopiluac on 2011-03-16
Since this post is beyond dated, no doubt you've now found the solution (or this was fixed in a subsequent release) However, for others, who like myself, stumbled in here looking for the answer, I offer this: 

The Options tab wasn't taken out; it was simply renamed and moved to another location. As of FF 3.5.16 the information is located under Edit/Preferences/ in your FF toolbar. Hope that helps someone.
