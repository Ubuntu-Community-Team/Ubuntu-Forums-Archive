### Post by LewisTM on 2012-04-19
You can't. View settings are not remembered in Thunar. This might change in the next version of Xfce (4.10) but don't take my word for it.

What would be easier to implement are shortcut keys for quickly changing the Sort order, the same as View mode (Ctrl-1/2/3) and Zoom (Ctrl-+/-). That would make me happy.
