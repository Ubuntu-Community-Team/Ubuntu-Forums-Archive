### Post by tonyr on 2006-06-07
[This]("http://www.kate-editor.org/article/using_named_sessions") article says, unfortunately, that the **project** functionality has 
been removed from **Kate 2.5**, which I guess is the the Dapper version.  The author 
does express surprise at the negative reaction that it generated, and is looking at the
possibility of adding it back in.  It seems to be superceded by something called
**named sessions**.
