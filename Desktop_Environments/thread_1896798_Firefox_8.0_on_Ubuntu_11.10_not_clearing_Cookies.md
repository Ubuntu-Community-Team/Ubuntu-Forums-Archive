### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-12-18
When you click the button "Show cookies", do you see the cookies you are talking about? The cookies you are talking about are from meebo or the site you are reading? When you restarted Firefox, the site tab was open?

You need to add meebo to the cookies exception list, since you are accepting third-party cookies.

I know you don't want add-ons, but you would get rid of most of this kind of stuff if you use [AdBlock Plus](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1865/) and [Ghostery](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/9609/).
