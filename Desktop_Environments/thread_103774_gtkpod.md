### Post by aysiu on 2005-12-14
You plug your iPod in.
It automounts.
You launch Gtkpod.
Click "Read."
Click "+ Files" and add the files you want to add.
Click "Sync."
Close Gtkpod
Right-click the iPod and select "unmount volume."

That's how it works for me.
