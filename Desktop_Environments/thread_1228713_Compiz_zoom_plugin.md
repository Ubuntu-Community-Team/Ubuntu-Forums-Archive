### Post by mwillams73 on 2009-08-01
Yes it is possible and in my opinion better, press super button and use the mouse scroll button, scroll forward zooms in, scroll backwards zooms out. I hope this helps, Mine was set like that by default, so yours should be too. If yours isnt open up CCSM and look for the zooming plugin and configure it to your liking.
