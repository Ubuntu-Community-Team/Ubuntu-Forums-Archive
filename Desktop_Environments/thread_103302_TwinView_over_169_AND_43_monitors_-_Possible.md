### Post by siorai on 2006-01-09
Hi Andy, 

My Screen Resolution Preferences says 2960x1050 @ 75Hz.

A couple things I've noticed when looking over your xorg.conf though:

1: You have the dri and GLcore modules still active. Those should be commented out as they won't work with the nvidia drivers. You can also comment out the DRI section towards the bottom.

2: For your ConnectedMonitor option, DFP isn't capitalized and I think that it has to be. (Not 100% sure of that since I'm fairly new to all this, but seeing as how in Linux, case is pretty much well always a factor, I would think so.)

3: In your ServerLayout section, the Screen entry doesn't have anything after the option. I've always seen that entry like this: "Default Screen" 0 0

4: In you MetaModes, one of the NULL's is missing an L.

Like I said, I'm pretty new to the whole Linux thing, but I've read so many HowTo's and guides on dual monitors that I hope I've at least steered you in the right direction. I'm thinking that the first three points will have the biggest impact. The fourth would just mean that one mode wouldn't work.

