### Post by Thpn on 2015-02-28
It appears that the proprietary Olympus Master software is required to transfer image files to your system. This software, of course, isn't available for Linux. The instruction manual advises Mac users to close iPhoto and use the Olympus Master application instead. It looks as though you'll need to transfer files from the memory card directly to your system, without using the camera.
