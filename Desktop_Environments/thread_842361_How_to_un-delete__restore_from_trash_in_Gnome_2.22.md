### Post by lobner on 2008-06-29
Oh yes tornado89 and Agent ME, it does!
The new structure of Trash means that it no longer lies in [FONT="Courier New"]~/.Trash[/FONT] but now it is found in [FONT="Courier New"]~/.local/share/Trash/[/FONT] and here the [FONT="Courier New"]files[/FONT]-folder contains the files, and the [FONT="Courier New"]info[/FONT]-folder contains the info regarding point of origin for the file.

I was told in [another tread]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=5274616&postcount=8") that it will appear in Gnome 2.24, supposedly.
