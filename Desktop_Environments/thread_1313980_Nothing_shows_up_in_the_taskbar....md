### Post by Ubunthree on 2009-11-04
The Window List should have a little handle or anchor thing (sorry, don't know the correct term) to show where it is on the panel. Right-click on this, and a menu pops up. Make sure that the "Lock To Panel" option is cleared. Pick "Move" from the menu. The Window List will follow the mouse to wherever you want it on the panel (don't hold the button, just move the mouse, and click again when you want to drop the app). Unlock any other panel apps which are in the way, and they will automatically step aside. That menu also has a "Remove" option, for any panel apps that you don't need.
