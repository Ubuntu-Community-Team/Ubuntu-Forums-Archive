### Post by gandalf2041 on 2006-09-09
I think Amarok is as close as you can come to a total music management software these days.  After fiddling around a little, I discovered that all you have to do is right click on an album name in the collections pane and choose "fetch cover" (you can even adjust the search if you don't get the correct hit).  I haven't been able to determine if it actually updates the tag but, there's an option in Amarok to "update album art" so, once you've set all your covers, you just update and voila! all your art shows up on the ipod. =D>  It would be nice to have something that traverses the entire collection (even at an album at a time, it's kind of a pain) but, as orb9220 points out, you have to manually verify them anyway :rolleyes:

The guys at Amarok are simply amazing! It's really a fantastic program...the best I've seen on any platform...(and I hate KDE :tongue:)
