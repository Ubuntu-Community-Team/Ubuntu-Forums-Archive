### Post by geovino on 2007-11-03
I like the new feature in Gutsy, where by rolling the scroll wheel you can move between the two desktops that are in the lower right hand corner of the taskbar. How can you add more desktops? I know in KDE you can add many more desktops as needed. Can you do that in Gnome?

---

### Post by taurus on 2007-11-03
Place the mouse cursor in one of those two desktops on the bottom right and then click the right button.  Go to Preferences and you will see the Workspaces.  Increase to whatever number you want for the Columns:.
