### Post by IanW on 2008-10-02
To shrink your desktop icons, right click on them & select "stretch icon", then use the handles to adjust your icon's size.

For single-click opening, open Nautilus, and under the Edit menu choose Preferences. Go to the Behaviour tab, and the option is the very first one in that tab.
