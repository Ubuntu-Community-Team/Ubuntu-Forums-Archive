### Post by sideburns on 2010-09-14
I don't know about anybody else, but I happen to have liked SameGnome.  Alas, the Gnome devs have decided to take it out of the Gnome Games.  It's not only gone from Ubuntu, but from Fedora as well.

However, all is not lost!  There's a request at Gnome's bugzilla, [https://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=622322](https://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=622322) to have it returned.  Think of it as a petition.  It's not going to get much attention with three signatures, but if enough people add their voice to it, the devs are much more likely to back down and give us back our beloved SameGnome.  Yes, you will have to create an account there, but it's simple, easy and, if you pick a throw-away email account for it, safe.
