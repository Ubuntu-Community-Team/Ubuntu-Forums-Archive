### Post by guaronet on 2011-05-14
Hi, 

You only have to download the navi-x.zip ([http://navi-x.googlecode.com/files/Navi-X_v36.zip](http://navi-x.googlecode.com/files/Navi-X_v36.zip))  then, in xbmc, you have to go to programs, Get more.., Add-ons... then you have to select "Install from zip file", here you shoud search your zip file...  
Then, for running, you must select programs, and there you'll find navi-x.

Good luck!
