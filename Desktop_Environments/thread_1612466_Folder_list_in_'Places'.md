### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-11-03
All these folders in the left hand pane under "Places" are simply bookmarks you have made.  The folders must still be in the "main area" ie, the right hand pane.

If you want to remove bookmarks open nautilus and in the Bookmarks menu, choose Organise bookmarks.
