### Post by deadflowr on 2013-04-06
Did you close ccsm?

Even if you did, simply try ctrl+alt+T, which should open a terminal and then type ccsm to reopen compizconfig-settings-manager.
Then click on the unity plugin again and click the box to re-enable.

The important thing is to wait for it to reset itself, which can take some time, sometimes.
