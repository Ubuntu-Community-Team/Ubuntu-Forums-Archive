I've had that happen a couple times and reboots always seem to revert it. I've never taken the time to actually figure out why it happened.

But if you want to manually move the indicators or panel applets around, first right click on them to make sure they're not locked (it's the last option in the list). You may want to do the same for the surrounding icons. Once that's unchecked, you can move them around.
