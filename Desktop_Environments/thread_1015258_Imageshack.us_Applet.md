### Post by cyxapeff on 2008-12-18
I created small applet for ease upload images to imagehack.us and other services. Maybe it will be interesting for somebody.

[IMG]http://cyxapeff.org/media/portfolio/thumb/screenshot1_500x1000.png[/IMG]

**Features**

[LIST]
[*]Click and upload.
[*]Drug&Drop.
[*]Support many image hostings (plugins). Available: imageshack.us, tinypic.com, ipicture.ru, radikal.ru
[*]Output templates.
[*]History.
[*]Display screenshort. (shift + click or wheel click)
[*]Internationalization
[*]Display how icon, text or icon and text.
[/LIST]

[Russian interface]("http://cyxapeff.org/portfolio/projects/imageshackus-applet/")

**Requirements**

[LIST]

[*]PIL
[/LIST]
