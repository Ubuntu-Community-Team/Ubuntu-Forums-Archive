Simple way to Connect to Server and add a share or ftp ... also how to order, rename and remove them.


With Ubuntu it's really easy to connect to a windows share, FTP or custom location. It's very easy to permanently mount a custom location to Places Bookmarks and to Nautilus file browser. The part I found harder to find information about was actually how to remove them from Places Bookmarks, or to order and rename them, although it's an extremely easy process, the info was harder to find.


This article will cover:

- How to connect to server available in Places

- How to rename the share or fpt

- How to order those connections and bookmarks

- How to remove a permanent bookmark from Places and Nautilus
