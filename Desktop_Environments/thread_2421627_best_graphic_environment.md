&#8593;+1&#8593;

Frankly, I would not recommend *any* graphical environment for this one. Anything you load will likely give you a very poor experience. The restricted RAM will make a modern browser sluggish even with no other app running.

A better use for this machine would be as some sort of command-line-only server. Without the overhead of a graphical environment, it should get a new life.
