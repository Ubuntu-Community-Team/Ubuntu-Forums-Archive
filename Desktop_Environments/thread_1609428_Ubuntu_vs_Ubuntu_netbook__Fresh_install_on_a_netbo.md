### Post by Jechem on 2010-10-30
Hello,

In my opinion with your 13" screen I would use the desktop version. Netbook launcher for me is like having a cluttered screen. I like my screen tidy. I have an EEEPC 4G and I use the Gnome desktop and removed the netbook interface because with a 7" screen and all those big icons of the netbook launcher I feel my netbook looks like a kid's toy. The netbook launcher with its big icons is more suited for the tablet pc's with their touchscreen interface.

### Post by welshmike on 2010-10-30
I agree with Jechem,

I first installed Ubuntu netbook remix version and the GUI made my  [ ASUS 1001P 10 inch netbook]("http://www.amazon.co.uk/Asus-netbook-1-66GHz-Webcam-Windows/dp/B0039824MC/ref=sr_1_1/280-8281716-2057154?ie=UTF8&s=electronics&qid=1273510461&sr=8-1") screen look like a kids toy and very much different from the previous Gnome desktops I have used on my laptop (or even MS Windows desktops).
