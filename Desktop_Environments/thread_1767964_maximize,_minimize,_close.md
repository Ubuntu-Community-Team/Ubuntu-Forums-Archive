### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-05-26
If you look in the white box in the screen shot in the area where it displays the button names. It is possible to drag and drop those in the order you want to use them . While playing around with that I ended up with two buttons on the right and fixed by changing the order. I don't how that may have happened if you used the configuration editor. If you drag and drop to the right of the title when you have buttons set to the left they will appear on the right.
