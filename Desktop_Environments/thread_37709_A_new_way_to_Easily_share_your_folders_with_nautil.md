### Post by sebest on 2005-05-28
Hi,

have a look at this software [here](http://gentoo.ovibes.net/nautilus-share/) 
Screenshots are also available [here](http://gentoo.ovibes.net/nautilus-share/mediawiki-1.4.4/index.php/NSScreenShots) 

It's totally integrated in nautilus, and you can visually see the folder that you just shared. Not need to be root to use it.

You can share with your friends using windows or macos because it's using samba behind.

Easy instructions on how to install it are [there](http://gentoo.ovibes.net/nautilus-share/mediawiki-1.4.4/index.php/Accueil#Ubuntu_Hoary) 
(from synaptics and from the cli)
