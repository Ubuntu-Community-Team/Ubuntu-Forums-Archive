### Post by kayosiii on 2008-12-23
A KDE4 Specific power management system will be part of kde 4.2 until then do what the other poster said.

The pager currently doesn't thumbnail and while the option to scroll on desktops was in kde 3.5 it doesn't appear to be in 4 yet. What I use and am very happy with is the desktop grid effect with a trigger in the top right hand corner. by pushing the mouse in the top right corner you get an overview of all your desktops and can switch between then or even move non maximised windows between them.

### Post by MarblePanther on 2008-12-26
The upcoming kde 4.2 will include a power manager in settings, though for now you will have to put up with the battery :) (It comes out around the end of January or beginning of February, visit kde.org to see the counter of days left)

As for the beep problem, just go to system settings,accessibility, and then untick use system bell and tick use visible bell (you can configure colors)

as for dolphin preview,open up dolphin then you can hit the button "preview" and go to settings, configure dolphin, view modes, and check use common view properties...

I dont know about the multiple schemes for the battery either though.
