### Post by [S|G] on 2006-05-20
You could install the following packages:

xscreensaver (you probably have this one)
xscreensaver-gl-extra
kscreensaver
kscreensaver-xsavers
ksetisaver (SETI screensaver for kde)

There are other packages, just search for screensavers on adept :)
