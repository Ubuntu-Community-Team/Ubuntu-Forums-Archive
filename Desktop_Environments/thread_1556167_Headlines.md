### Post by utnubuuser on 2010-08-19
right-click the feed icon, scroll to properties and change the feed address to one of your own choosing..

For the feed on a Canadian installation, the feed address is:> [http://en-us.fxfeeds.mozilla.com/en-US/firefox/headlines.xml](http://en-us.fxfeeds.mozilla.com/en-US/firefox/headlines.xml)
and the site location is:> [http://www.bbc.co.uk/go/rss/int/news/-/news/](http://www.bbc.co.uk/go/rss/int/news/-/news/)

PS. the forum software is causing the feed url to be shortened, try copying the link location instead of copying the quoted text.
