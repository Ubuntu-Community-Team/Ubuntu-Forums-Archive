### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-10-19
Perhaps someone spilled coffee in it?  It's probably 20 to 30 years old! (1984-1992?).  Try opening up and cleaning the key switches with contact cleaner.  Also, while you are in there touch up the solder points on the control board--they will be quite corroded by now.
