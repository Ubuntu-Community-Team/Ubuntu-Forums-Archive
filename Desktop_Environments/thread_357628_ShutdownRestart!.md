### Post by xIke on 2007-11-04
You've probably given up or solved this by now...I just spent too much time trying to figure this out and thought I'd share my fix in this thread since it's one of the first I found.

For me, I just went to System: Administration: Login Window: Local and enabled "Show Action Menu".  Yeah...  I don't think settings in "Login Window" should affect in-session behavior, but this does.  Hope this helps somebody...
