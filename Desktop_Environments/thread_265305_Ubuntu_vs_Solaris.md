### Post by colo on 2006-09-25
GNU/Linux is the clear winner for me. It's licensed under terms that minimize corporate influence. You've got the latest and greatest technology for servers (EVMS, netfilter, neat little gadgets like iosched), the desktop (ALSA, Hotplug), and embedded devices, combined with the superior GNU-userland (SunOS' userland just sucks imo. bah.)

Of course, Solaris (10) sports some intersting features as well, like ZFS and dtrace. But still, the drawbacks outweight the vantages, imho.
