Unfortunately, mp3 support cannot be bundled with Ubuntu due to legal issues. Being a free OS, Ubuntu cannot pay for licensing fees like Microsoft and Apple do for their OS's. 
To answer your question, however, there's a cool app called Automatix that auto-installs a host of cool apps and tweaks for Ubuntu, including mp3 support.
