### Post by aberry7670 on 2005-11-14
Thanks, for pointing me in the right direction. I figured out what needed to be changed in the file xorg.conf. Now the screen resolution is just right.  Below the line changed was commented in the file ## and the new line was added.

Identifier "Default Screen"
Device "Intel Corporation 82845G/GL[Brookdale-G]/GE Chipset Integrated Graphics Device"

DefaultDepth 24

EndSubSection
