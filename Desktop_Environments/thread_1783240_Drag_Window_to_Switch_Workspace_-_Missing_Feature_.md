### Post by Krytarik on 2011-06-17
Just make sure that Compiz' "Desktop Wall" plugin is enabled in "System -> Preferences -> CompizConfig Settings Manager", and that its option "Edge Flip Move" under its "Edge Flipping" tab is enabled as well.
