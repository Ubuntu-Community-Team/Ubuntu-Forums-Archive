### Post by angustia on 2006-09-24
are you sure that is the complete xorg.conf?

the mine has this at the bottom:

Section "DRI"
    Mode    0666
EndSection

Section "Extensions"
    Option "Composite" "Enable"
EndSection

(it's a gforce2 mx) Section "Extensions" is not necessary.
also check permissions of the files:

/dev/nvidiactl
/dev/nvidia0

must be read write for owner, group and others (so anybody can use 3d)

i guess you installed the nvidia drivers following the ubuntu's guides...
