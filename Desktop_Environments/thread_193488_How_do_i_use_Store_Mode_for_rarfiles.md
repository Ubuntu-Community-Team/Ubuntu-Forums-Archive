### Post by mm2004 on 2008-03-01
rar a -v15m -m0 -vn name of your.rared.file.rar /your.input.file

-v15m will split your files into 15mb files you can change this to say you want 5mb files change it to -v5m
-m0 is what your after 0 is no compression -m3 is mid compression and -m5 is max compression

and last off -vn allows old naming of rared files default is name.part00.rar name.part01.rar but with -vn it will make them name.rar name.r00 name.r01 and so on. hope this helps you out.
