### Post by kenono on 2008-09-18
You don't need to reinstall, what you can do is right click the panel, and select Add To Panel. Then you can drag and drop the Main Menu applet onto the panel.
Your wife may have just simply removed the applet, which is done by right clicking it and removing it from the context menu.

### Post by jpeddicord on 2008-09-18
Right-click on a blank area on the panel, and select **Add to Panel**.

From that menu, find **Menu Bar**. Click and drag it back on to the panel, or select it and click Add to add it. Middle-click and drag on it to move it around on the panel.
