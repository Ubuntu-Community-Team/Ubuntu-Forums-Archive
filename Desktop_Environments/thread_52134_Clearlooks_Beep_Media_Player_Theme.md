### Post by varunus on 2005-07-26
I've seen color modifications, but never just a straight clearlooks theme...
[http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org) has a some color schemes (graphite, gperfection2, and deepsky come to mind).

If you've got osme time to tinker, you may want to check out [Sonance](http://sonance.aaronbock.net/).  It's a multimedia player that uses GStreamer, and currently is similar to XMMS or BMP (though they're eventually going to be more rhythmbox like).  It looks interesting, though I think you'll have to compile it from source, so you may want to stay away from it...
