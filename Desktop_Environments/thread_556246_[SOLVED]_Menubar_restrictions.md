Check out gnome's unser management and lockdown tools, Sabayon and pessulus. Pessulus iirc has various panel lockdown options, and with sabayon you can easily apply them to some user profiles and enforce them.

[quote]Concerning Firefox, is it possible to "hide" the menubar? If yes, how? (So the Guest isn't't be able to change the preferences.)/QUOTE]

Firefox isn't covered by pessulus (though changing settings with sabayon works iirc), you can disable Epiphany's menubar though.
