### Post by afoglia on 2008-01-20
Try installing different fonts.  I'm a big fan of terminus.  The package is either console-terminus or xfonts-terminus.  (I have both installed, and I don't know which has the ttf fonts required by xfterm4.)  It may be too small for you.  There's also the DejaVu Sans Mono (ttf-dejavu) and Bitstream Vera Sans Mono (ttf-bitstream-vera) which look okay.

If none of those satisfy you, you could try using mrxvt instead of xfterm4.  It's little more than a tabbed xterm/rxvt terminal, but that might be good enough for most uses.
