### Post by hictio on 2008-11-13
> **CholericKoala said:**
> what if you right click on the top and click resize?

That's right, after doing that, you use the keyboard's arrows to actually make the window smaller.
Select 'Resize', then press the arrow down once, and then the arrow up until you see the bottom of the window on screen.
You'll notice that your windows has changed to the "resize me state" because of the overall blueish tint.
