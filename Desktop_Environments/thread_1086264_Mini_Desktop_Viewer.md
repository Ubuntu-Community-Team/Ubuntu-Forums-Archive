### Post by mtbsoft on 2009-03-03
Don't know if this is exactly what you want but, if you use Compiz, you could try the Expo plugin - it lays out all your desktops side-by-side, like one big wall.  The windows are still 'live' but smaller of course.  The more desktops you have, though, the smaller they are so it may not be quite what you need.

Alternatively, if you just want to flick to it and back again, try the Unfold option on the Compiz Desktop Cube (ctrl-alt-down) or if you don't use the Cube, look at Desktop Wall.

Compiz is your friend!
