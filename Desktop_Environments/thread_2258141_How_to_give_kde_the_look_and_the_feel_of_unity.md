> **remmas-sido said:**

You've seen [http://kde-look.org/content/show.php/Unity+Ambiance?content=159937](http://kde-look.org/content/show.php/Unity+Ambiance?content=159937) ? Right-click on the archive file you downloaded, 'Extract to..' and enter */.kde/share/apps/desktoptheme/* 
Logout and log back in and the theme should appear in System Settings.

There's also [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MQPrMiqnXw8](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MQPrMiqnXw8) but I haven't watched it myself.
