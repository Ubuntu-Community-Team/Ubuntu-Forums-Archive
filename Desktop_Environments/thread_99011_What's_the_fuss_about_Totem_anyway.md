### Post by varunus on 2005-12-04
You guys are probably not aware with the patent/legal issues surrounding codecs on linux.  Thanks to the DMCA, Ubuntu could be in serious legal trouble if it included codecs with the distribution...

Here's some information on how to get codecs:
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/RestrictedFormats)
None of it is that hard, just suck it up and do it yourself, or use Automatix.
