After a few days the problem began to appear in other apps too: Nautilus, Evolution, Libre Office. The system was not really usable. Having no way to figure what was causing the error, I re-installed the system from scratch and also, finally, managed to install the AMD proprietary drivers from their web site, to support a Radeon HD 5450 card.

This matches the symptoms reported by a number of other users in this forum and elsewhere.

The rebuilt system had none of the previous sudden app. closing problems and I have run with it, ignoring the offer from Update manager to update the Chromium install.

My other 11.10 system, using an nVidia supplied driver to support a GeForce 5200 card, never had the sudden app. closure problem, but to be on the safe side I have also declined the offer to update Chromium. But Update Manager is becoming quite insistent about it - asking me to update at every logon.

If I do update and suffer the instability problems, is there any way to roll-back the update?
