### Post by tht00 on 2006-09-23
> **sanlaw said:**
> Thank you for the quick reply. I have ordered a CD and have not recieved it yet but wanted to check out the forums for help issues and problems. "Start/install" will not put it on my hard drive and I will be able to still try out the OS? Correct?

Right.  It's what is called a liveCD -- it will load the entire OS into RAM and won't touch the hard drive (unless you explicitly tell it to.. don't worry about this.)... though, it will be a little slower in loading things than the actual install.

When you select "Start/Install", it will load the liveCD.  If/when you want to do the actual install, there will be a launcher on the desktop that will perform the install.
