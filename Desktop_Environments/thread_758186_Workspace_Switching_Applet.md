### Post by GameKing505 on 2008-04-17
Hey I'm new to Ubuntu and love it so far, but I have one gripe. I recently enabled desktop effects by installing XGL, and now my workspace switcher doesn't let me switch the way I used to.

Before I used compiz I could switch workspaces by simply moving my mouse over the workspace switching applet in the GNOME toolbar and roll my clickwheel. This was very fast and I didn't have to aim since I could just direct my mouse towards the corner and roll. Now that I have installed compiz however, I am forced to actually click the little workspace that I want. It's cumbersome to say the least...

How can I get the clickwheel option back?

### Post by russo.mic on 2008-04-18
Do you have CCSM installed?  If not, install via terminal:

sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager

that setting can be found in the rotate cube plugin

Good Luck!

Russo

### Post by GameKing505 on 2008-04-26
Worked like a charm thanks a lot. The only trick was figuring out that button 4 and button 5 were the clickwheel buttons.
