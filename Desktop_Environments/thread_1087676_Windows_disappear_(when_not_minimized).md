### Post by kidux on 2009-03-05
If you accidently use the scroll wheel of your mouse on the desktop it'll switch to a new desktop workspace, in which case alt-tab isn't going to bring it back, but ctrl-alt-tab switches between apps on different workspaces. You can just scroll the mouse wheel in the opposite direction on the desktop, or hit ctrl-alt-(arrow keys) to get it back.
