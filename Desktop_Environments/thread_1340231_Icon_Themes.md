### Post by hhh on 2010-07-18
I've been custom-coloring the Humanity icons via Magicons...
[http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/MagIcons?content=120508](http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/MagIcons?content=120508)
I've been using the eyedropper tool in Magicons and picking colors right from my wallpaper.

To import Humanity/Humanity-Dark into Magicons, you need them in tar.bz2 format...
[http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Humanity-Colors?content=117230](http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php/Humanity-Colors?content=117230)

The screenshot threads in the cafe have tons of suggestions for icons (with screenshots lol!).
