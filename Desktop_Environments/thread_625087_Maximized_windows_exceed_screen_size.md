Hello,

I believe the Live CD incorrectly recognizes your optimum screen resolution as 640x480 or something similar, so that it displays huge and inoperable windows. However, I also believe that this problem will be resolved after the installation either automatically or by installing your graphics driver / editing your xorg.conf file.

Fortunately there is a work-around though: In graphical Linux screen, you can move windows by Alt+ Left Clicking and dragging the mouse button. So, you may move the location selection window with Alt + mouse dragging to see the buttons below.

Good luck with the installation...
