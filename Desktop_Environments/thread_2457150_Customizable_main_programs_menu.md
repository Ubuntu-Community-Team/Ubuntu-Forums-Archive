### Post by GhX6GZMB on 2021-01-27
The second two points are no problem.
Drag 'n drop: just open the application menu and drag the program icon onto the desktop. If the icon has an exclamation point, right click on it and tick "Trust this executable". Then it's active.
Yuo can also drag 'n drop to the taskbar if you like.
