### Post by SoftwareExplorer on 2009-08-22
OK, so you have ubuntu on one section. Are the other sections empty? Each section has its own filesystem. If you don't have a filesystem on the section and you try to open it, it wont work--to see what files are on it you have to have a filesystem. It's possible to have a filesystem that has no files in it, but that is different than having no filesystem on a section.
