### Post by SGC on 2005-06-21
- Right-click on the kmenu and choose **Menu Editor** 
- Left-click on **Internet** and then choose **New item** from the toolbar.
- Name the new shortcut **Opera**
- In the command field from the right panel type **opera** and click on the icon to change it.
- Click on the save button from the toolbar.
