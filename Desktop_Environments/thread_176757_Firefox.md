### Post by pillu on 2006-05-15
I think what you mean to say is that when you click the firefox shortcut on the panel you still get the old version of firefox. If this is the problem then you just need to modify the shortcut. You can do this by right clicking the shortcut, clicking on properties and pointing the command to the new firefox executable file. Or you can also add a new shortcut to the panel and point it to the new firefox executable. Hope this is what you were asking for.
