### Post by TunaCanTommy on 2010-11-17
I just did this on an MSI Wind - 12.1" wide-screen display, and an ATI Radeon HD3200 graphics driver.

Installed netbook edition of Maverick with Windows 7. The netbook version of Maverick w/Mutter doesn't perform very well with the ATI card, BUT the desktop version is great.

After you install the netbook version and you get the basic things working, you can easily get to the desktop version by logging out and logging back in . . . but log back in to the desk-top version.

This will give you some time to fool-around with the netbook edition without getting too frustrated with mutter.
