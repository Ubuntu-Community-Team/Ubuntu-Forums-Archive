### Post by aysiu on 2007-01-29
I recommend not using it for several reasons. 

1. It's not functional. 
2. IceWM is a much better environment for a Windows-looking interface.
3. I don't think the best way to migrate someone is to make the interface look like Windows. The sooner she gets it in her head that this is something different, the sooner she'll be able to adapt to it. "Fooling" her into thinking it's Windows will just confuse her as soon as something doesn't work the way it did back in Windows.

That said, if you really do want to give XPDE a try, here's how:
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/xpde](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/xpde)
