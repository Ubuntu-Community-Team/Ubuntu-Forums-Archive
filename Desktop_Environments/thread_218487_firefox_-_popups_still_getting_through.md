### Post by asplode on 2006-07-18
In microsoft terms they'd call this a "feature"

There is a nuanced balance in the world of popup blocking, and firefox is indeed functioning as intended.

There are such things as good popups, but silly websites looking to maximize ad dollars can show popups on clicks.  But when you think about it, lots of 'good' sites do the exact same thing.  Think about the FAFSA (click here for help) that opens a javascript popup, or the bellsouth website, or any number of websites that use clicks to bring up legitimate popups.

My suggestion?  Find a website that doesn't suck.
