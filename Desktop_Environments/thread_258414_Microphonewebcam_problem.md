### Post by peabody on 2006-09-15
What type of microphone is it?  Is it a usb or a standard sound card sized jack?  Most webcams in linux seem to be supported by the spca5xx module.  If that module doesn't support your web cam and the vendor doesn't provide any support, you may be out of luck on the webcam.  The trick is going to be to try and discover what chipset the cam uses.
