### Post by cka on 2005-02-24
Those themes you downloaded are probably either window borders, control sets, or icons.  Find the 'themes' menu under Preferences (it's under the 2nd menu at the top of the screen, I forgot the name of it - I'm not in Ubuntu right now and I'm also running a Hoary revision) then click on "Modify Theme" - from there you can adjust those 3 values of the current theme into your own custom one.
