### Post by SirKillalot on 2006-09-17
Try CardExport2 [http://www.synclive.com/cardexport2/](http://www.synclive.com/cardexport2/)

It makes your Palm to be seen as a mass storage device on your computer.
Then simply copy the files to /PALM/Launcher and you'll be fine.

But if you want to use pilotd you'll have to change your syncing rules on your palm which make prc files to get synced to /PALM/Launcher. You can do this with many tools, the one I use is RescoExplorer.

But actually I do not think that there is a feature in gpilotd which can sync files to the card.
regards
