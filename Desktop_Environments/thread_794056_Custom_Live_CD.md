### Post by DieB on 2008-05-14
sure it is possible to make your own live-cd
actually there are several possibilities:

uck: [http://uck.sourceforge.net/](http://uck.sourceforge.net/)
reconstructor: [http://reconstructor.aperantis.com/](http://reconstructor.aperantis.com/)
remastersys: [http://wiki.ubuntuusers.de/Remastersys](http://wiki.ubuntuusers.de/Remastersys)

or the howto in the wiki:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/LiveCDCustomization?highlight=(cd)|(live](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/LiveCDCustomization?highlight=(cd)|(live))

But as all needed and changed data gets loaded into ram and stays there it might not be lots of fun running compiz, nexuiz, etc from a live-cd unless you got lots of ram and cpu.
