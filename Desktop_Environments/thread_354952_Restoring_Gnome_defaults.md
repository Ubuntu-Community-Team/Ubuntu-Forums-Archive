### Post by kevinlyfellow on 2007-02-08
in the terminal type
```
cd ~
rm -r .gnome2
```
and yes it will work

another way to do it is to hit ctrl-h while in you home folder
and right click and delete .gnome2 ... this way may be preferable since a backup will remain in the trash
