### Post by Jefferythewind on 2010-02-21
Hi everyone,

  I have had this problem for while.  When I make a call with skype, the microphone volume on the OS automatically adjusts down to almost 0.  It seems to do this either the first call I make or the second.  After I see that it has happened I will just end the call (because the person on the other end can't hear me), turn the volume back up to the appropriate level, and then resume calling.  The mic volume level stays there for the rest of my session, no matter how many calls I make.  But then every time i reboot, i always have to go through the same routine of making a call, watching the mic level go to zero, and then reset it manually.

Does anyone know why this might be happening?

Thanks

### Post by kostkon on 2010-02-21
You could try this:
in Skype's preferences, select *Sound Devices* and then deselect the *Allow Skype to automatically adjust my mixer levels* option.
