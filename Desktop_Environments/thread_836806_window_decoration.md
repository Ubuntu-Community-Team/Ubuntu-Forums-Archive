### Post by lisati on 2008-06-21
I don't know much (if anything) about the settings referred to, but if the PC starting on its own after a power failure is likely to be a problem you might want to check your BIOS settings - my desktop has a BIOS setting that lets me choose what happens after power failure. I normally leave it set to "stay off": if there's a power cut while I'm away from my computer for a while & I've left it doing something, it immediately alerts me to switch into "diagnostic" mode.
