### Post by SteveDee on 2011-07-01
> **tpprynn said:**
> ...For it to be practical for me I need the battery icon and Network Manager on the panel...

Just right click on a blank area of the LXDE panel/task-bar.
Select Add/Remove Panel Items > Panel Applets.
Click Add button, then add the Battery Monitor & the Network Status Monitor.
