### Post by orlox on 2009-12-20
As for the keyboard, you can do that by choosing "always above" from the options given when right-clicking on the window border. You can then alt-tab to another window, and it will have keyboard focus, but the other window will cover the whole screen. However, the mouse will still act on the top window.
