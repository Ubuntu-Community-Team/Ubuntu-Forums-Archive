### Post by Merrattic on 2013-08-31
I may stand to be corrected but I believe the Power Manager one is in relation to the hardware / bios / sleep & hibernate states of the PC, whilst the screensaver options for power management are "software controlled" actions for blanking the screen in X (e.g. xset dpms)

My preference is to have no screen blanking or screen saver and I found I need to disable (change to never or untick) in both places to get this setup.
