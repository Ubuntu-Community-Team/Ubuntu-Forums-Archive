### Post by atoponce on 2005-12-03
Well, yes and no.  The kernel is the same, and the same libraries are installed and available.  However, as I am sure you know, Kubuntu installs KDM and KDE while Ubuntu installs GDM and Gnome.  So with Kubuntu, you have the QT libraries installed for the GUI and KDE software (Konqueror, etc.) while with Ubuntu, you have the GTK libraries installed (Epiphany, etc.).  Other than that, they are virtually identical.
