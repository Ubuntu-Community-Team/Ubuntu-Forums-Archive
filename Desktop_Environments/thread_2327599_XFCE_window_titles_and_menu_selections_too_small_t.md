### Post by kazakore on 2016-06-13
Assuming the entries are the same on Xubuntu as on Ubuntu Studio then Windows Manager is the 10th entry in the first section. Themes are the area it opens into. You can use up/down arrows or the mouse to select.

I have found quite often that changes in both here and in Appearance > Style (also worth trying, second menu item, opens on correct section) often don't get fully applied until you log out and back in again and things can start to look and act a bit weird if you are switching between a few trying to find one you like...
