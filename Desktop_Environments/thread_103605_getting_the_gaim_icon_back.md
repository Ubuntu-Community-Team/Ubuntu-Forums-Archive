### Post by localzuk on 2005-12-14
Right Click on the top 'panel' (which is invisible in your case). Select 'Add To Panel'.

Now select 'Custom Application Launcher' and fill in the details for gaim.
That should place a gaim icon on your panel - you can right click on it, select move, and move it.
