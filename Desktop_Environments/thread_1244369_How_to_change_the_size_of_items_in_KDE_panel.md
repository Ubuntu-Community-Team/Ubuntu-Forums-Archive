### Post by msvolenski on 2009-08-19
I'm not sure if it's what do you want. But try this:

1. Right click on panel and Unlock Widgets;
2. At the right of panel will appear a button, click it;
3. Then click and hold "Height" to choose the panel size, sizing the icons at same time.
4. You could also add your blanck space by clicking the button "Add Spacer", then you could resize it the way you want to;
5. After your changes, you could Lock Widgets again, to avoid any unwanted changes.
