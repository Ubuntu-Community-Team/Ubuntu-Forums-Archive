[QUOTE=senzacionale]My friend use gentoo. Is it possible that he can also use that theme in gnome?[/QUOTE]
He can just download the artwork at
[http://archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/pool/main/u/ubuntu-artwork/](http://archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/pool/main/u/ubuntu-artwork/)

Either the orig.tar.gz or the deb file. Unpack and move the artwork to the correct directories. For example, the GTK theme goes either to /usr/share/themes or ~/.themes.
