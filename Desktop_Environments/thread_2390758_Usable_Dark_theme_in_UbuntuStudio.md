### Post by again? on 2018-05-02
You can use an extension to fix. [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/text-contrast-for-dark-themes/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/text-contrast-for-dark-themes/)
The arc-theme(repos) and [adapta-theme]("https://launchpad.net/~tista/+archive/ubuntu/adapta") work fine in Xubuntu. (still needs extension in firefox)
Chrome doesn't have the issue.
