### Post by ajmoncrief on 2006-03-03
Yes you would need a bluetooth adapter for your pc, assuming that you do not have one.  I just purchase one on ebay for $10.00  (including shipping, USA) that works great with breezy.  Pricewatch should have some cheapies too.

Bluetooth technology basically allows two bluetooth devices (be it pc, pda, phone, etc.) to connect to each other.  Most use pda's bluetooth to wirelessly sync their pda to their computer.  Others use it to wirelessly surf the internet on their pda, check email, if they have a bluetooth cell phone-call people directly from their pda, transfer files, etc.  It really is nice to have considering that the prices are very reasonable.  

Check ubuntus wiki guide to set up your pda once you get an adapter.  Worked for me and my Palm TX. 

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BluetoothSetup?highlight=%28bluetooth%29](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/BluetoothSetup?highlight=%28bluetooth%29)

Hope this helps.
