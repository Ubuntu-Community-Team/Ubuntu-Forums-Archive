### Post by urukrama on 2007-05-30
Have a look at the Random Openbox Chatter thread RedSquirrel linked to. I don't think there is a real need to start a separate thread -- better to keep all information in one place.

What is the difference between Fluxbox and Openbox? Openbox is more minimalistic than Fluxbox (no panel, no transparency, no tabbing, etc.). In terms of speed, I don't see a real difference between the two on my computer(s), though. Both are good, just depends on your tastes. I like the spartan simplicity of Openbox, and never feel quite at home in Fluxbox.

Try it out, and see for yourself. Install Openbox, Obconf and Obmenu and play around. If you need help getting started, have a look at [this]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=192106&highlight=openbox") excellent howto on installing and configuring Openbox. And then read through [Random Openbox Chatter]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=190476&highlight=openbox") -- you'll find a lot of useful info, help and ideas there.
