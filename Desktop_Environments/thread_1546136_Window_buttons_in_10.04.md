### Post by JonasDK on 2010-08-05
You can fill half your workspace with setting up a keyboard-command in Compiz:
```
sudo apt-get install compiz config-settings-manager
```When you start that up go to Windows Management. 

Were you bothered by the window buttons being on the left side? You could've changed that by doing [this]("http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/13535/move-window-buttons-back-to-the-right-in-ubuntu-10.04/").
