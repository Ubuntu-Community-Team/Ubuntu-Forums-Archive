### Post by m_duck on 2009-01-29
Do you mean the system tray that was on the top right of the screen? If so, just right click on some free space in the panel and go to "Add to panel". Look through the list to find "Notification Area" and click add. You can move it by clicking and dragging the left hand edge of it.
