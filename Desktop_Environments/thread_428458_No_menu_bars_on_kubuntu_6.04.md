### Post by Jucato on 2007-04-30
Press Alt+F2 and type in

```
kcontrol
```

to run the KDE Control Center. Go to Desktop -> Panels -> Arrangement tab and Hiding tab and try changing the position and hiding settings of the panel.

Not really sure why your Firefox bookmarks are disappearing, though.
