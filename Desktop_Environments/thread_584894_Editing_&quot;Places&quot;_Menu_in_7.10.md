### Post by NilsE on 2007-10-21
Click on any one of them in the places menu and it will open a nautilus window.  Right click on each one on the left below the marker line and select remove.  Now from the same nautilus session browse to the folder you want and select add bookmark and the will appear below the line and in you places menu.

I know there is another way but this is what I remember and it works.
