### Post by peitschie on 2008-05-20
You could set these up to run in your default Session (System > Preferences > Sessions > Startup Programs).  That may not remember your window layout settings however.  If you have specific requirements for windows settings, "devilspie" ([http://www.burtonini.com/blog/computers/devilspie](http://www.burtonini.com/blog/computers/devilspie)) may be worthwhilte looking at.  But this may be overkill for what you want...
