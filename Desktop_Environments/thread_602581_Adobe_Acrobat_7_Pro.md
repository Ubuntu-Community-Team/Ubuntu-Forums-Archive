### Post by Loan_Refi on 2007-12-29
I have installed Adobe Acrobat Pro 7.0 under Wine, Ubuntu Gutsy 7.10

Most of these features work;
Security, Highliting, Commenting, Notes, Advance Editing (not all fonts), inserting & deleting  (only works on page view) page transitions. Stamps with all commenting tool bar features.

The things don't work;
emailing directly from acrobat, Fonts in advance text editing (Some fonts not available in my system). May be a few others I haven't found yet.

Adobe Acrobat 7.0 Pro works well in my system;
Dell Inspiron 300m Intel PentiumM 1.2, 60g, 1g Memory (Ubuntu Gutsy 7.10 & Vista Enterprise install - Dual Boot)
