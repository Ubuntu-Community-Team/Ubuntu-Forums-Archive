### Post by Jucato on 2006-07-02
Go to System Settings > Regional and Accessibility > Keyboard Shortcuts.
Look for "Walk Through Windows" (it's the first on the list). Click on it (to highlight it), then click on the small box below it (beside None, Default, and Custom). Press Alt+Tab, then OK.

Hope that helps.
