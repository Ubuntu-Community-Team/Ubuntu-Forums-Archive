### Post by pbcartwright on 2007-09-28
I do this all the time. My wife & I are logged in always, we never shut our computer off. The first person logs in, then you go to switch users and log in as another user. You can either use the menus or use CTRL-ALT-F7 to go back to the first user, and CTRL-ALT-F8 to go to the second user. All applications stay in the the state you left them.
