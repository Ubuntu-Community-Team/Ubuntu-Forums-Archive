Does anyone know a way to automatically disable the window bar/border for all maximized windows?

---

### Post by nilarimogard on 2010-02-24
In Compiz, scroll down to the "Effects" section and click "Window Decorations". On  the "Decoration windows" field, replace "**any**" with "**!state=maxvert**"  (without the quotes).
