### Post by gruepig on 2006-02-05
You can share the files on your Mac by turning on what OS X calls "Windows file sharing".  You can share the files on your Ubuntu machine using samba ([http://ubuntuguide.org/#sambaserver](http://ubuntuguide.org/#sambaserver)) or NFS ([http://nfs.sourceforge.net/nfs-howto/)](http://nfs.sourceforge.net/nfs-howto/)).
