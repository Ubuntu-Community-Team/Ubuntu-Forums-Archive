### Post by Xgen on 2008-01-03
If, like me, you find that Beryl is superior to Compiz-Fusion then you can add:

[http://download.tuxfamily.org/3v1deb](http://download.tuxfamily.org/3v1deb) feisty eyecandy

...to your Software Sources list and install through Synaptic. Works just fine in Gutsy and Heron. If you want to compare on the fly, then just right click on the Beryl tray icon and switch between the various window managers. Make sure that C-F is installed and enabled through System-->Preferences-->Appearance-->Visual Effects. Add 'beryl-manager' to your Sessions startup to make it permanent and the appropriate xorg.conf settings.
