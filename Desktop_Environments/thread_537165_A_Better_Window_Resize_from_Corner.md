### Post by WinterWeaver on 2007-08-28
why dont you hold down alt-middle-click & drag? You can do this anywhere inside your window, and it resizes the window, based on the closest corner to your mouse.

I believe you need Feisty to do this, for Dapper and Edgy you will need beryl or compiz Fusion for this functionality.
