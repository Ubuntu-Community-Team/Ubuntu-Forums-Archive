I'm not sure I understand what you're describing, but I think what is happening is that one of your widows goes too close to what I call (with some annoyance!) the Magic Margins, at which time it is automatically a) enlarged to full screen, or b) pushed off to another workspace.  I can't get along with that kind of thing, which is why I use Xfce on 12.04 instead of Unity.  It may not be as visually elaborate, but it is manageable.
