### Post by Prefix100 on 2008-10-10
This isn't the right forum for this question, so I guess you won't get the best active responses.

But depending on the iPod it can be supported by many applications.

I'm not so knowledgeable on the subject, but I believe the newest iPods such as the touch and some nanos DO NOT work at all in Linux.

If you have an older iPod then look at [floolra]("http://www.floola.com/modules/wiwimod/"), it is meant to be very good for iPod management, or [Banshee]("http://banshee-project.org/").

If these don't work try posting in the correct support [forum here]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=334").
