### Post by louis_nichols on 2007-02-27
Hi! No, you won't have any problems. ubuntu-desktop is not a "usual package". Its only purpose is to depend on all packages that make up the Ubuntu desktop as seen by the developers. It's called a metapackage. if you envision your desktop differently, then it's ok to uninstall ubuntu-desktop.
