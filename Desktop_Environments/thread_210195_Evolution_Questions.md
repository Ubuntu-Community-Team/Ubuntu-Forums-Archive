### Post by dpm on 2006-07-06
[quote=BuffaloX][...] a tray option with mail notifier.

Does Evolution have that?[/quote] 
Not built-in, at least as a tray notifyer, but most people who want this functionality tend to use mail-notification:

[http://www.nongnu.org/mailnotify/]("http://www.nongnu.org/mailnotify/")

version 2.0 is available on the universe repositories, although they've disabled Evolution support on that package.

For a ready-made .deb package of mail notification with Evolution support, see this thread:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1215512#post1215512]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=1215512#post1215512")

and this one:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=209361]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=209361")

[quote=BuffaloX]and if yes, can I import my data from Thunderbird?[/quote] Yes, you can easily import your data from Thunderbird to Evolution with the built-in Evolution import assistant.

Cheers.
