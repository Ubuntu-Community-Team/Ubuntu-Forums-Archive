### Post by vincentb on 2006-08-13
All,

I think I have a solution for this.
I have first install the *nvidia-glx* package.
And then I have changed some settings in the Xorg.conf file (especially the depth color from 24 to 16). Now it is working perfectly.
Hope this will also helps other people.

For your info, a copy of my xorg.conf file:
