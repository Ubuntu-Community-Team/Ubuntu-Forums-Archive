### Post by frodon on 2006-06-15
The gnome-bittorent client is part of gnome desktop therefore you can't remove it without removing all the gnome packages because of dependencies, it would be the same if you'd like to remove the totem player.
But don't worry, nothing will start/run without your agreement.
