### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-11-29
You can completely reset the Unity settings as follows. Note: you will lose personalisation such as your choice of background, but you can set it again afterwards.

From a terminal, enter the following command. Warning: It may take a couple of minutes to run.
```
unity --reset
```Let us know whether or not this works.
