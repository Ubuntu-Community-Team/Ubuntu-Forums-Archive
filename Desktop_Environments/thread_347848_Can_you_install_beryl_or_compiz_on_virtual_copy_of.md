### Post by mcduck on 2007-01-28
It won't work, because they both need direct access to your computers graphics card, and virtual machines can't do that.

But if you want to try Beryl without installing anything on your machine you could try Sabayon: [http://www.sabayonlinux.org/]("http://www.sabayonlinux.org/")
