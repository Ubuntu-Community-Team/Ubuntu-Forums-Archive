### Post by Krytarik on 2011-03-09
The color options thing won't work very good with pixmap-based themes, because you may change the background (if they don't have gradient images like some of mine), the text colors, the selection colors and the colors in treeviews, but all the buttons, scrollbars and so on would stay the same.

So, you have to ask yourself what you like of the Mac themes, then try to retain those, and then there might be some kind of a compromise.
