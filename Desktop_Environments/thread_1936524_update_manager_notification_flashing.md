### Post by Bucky Ball on 2012-03-06
Switch off updates and just do it manually whenever you like, then. ;)

Open Update Manager
Hit 'Settings' in the bottom left corner
Go to the 'Updates' tab and untick 'Check for Updates'

Fixed! ;)
