### Post by Sidha on 2006-02-26
When using Gnome

go into "Application" -> "System Toosl" -> "Configuration Editor"
this gives you a windows registry alike screen
click on apps and go down the list to sound juicer

edit the two fields "file_pattern" and "path_pattern"
when you click on it, you see an explanation of the tags you can put in there (or just leave it blank)

This will reflect in sound Juicer as follows...
You will se the two drop down boxes empty, but the example path line will show it's ok!
