### Post by 3Miro on 2011-10-08
The short answer is: NO

The longer answer is: every release comes with its own packages, 11.04 comes with its own xfce, 11.10 comes with a newer xfce.

The longest answer is: there may be a way to circumvent this for some packages, but it is too much trouble; Ubuntu is not designed to work as a rolling release.

Why do you want the newer xfce? It is the same XFCE 4.8 maybe with a few bugs fixed, there are no new features or anything of that sort.
