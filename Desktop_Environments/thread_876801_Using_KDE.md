### Post by prshah on 2008-08-01
> **Jesdisciple said:**
> ```
sudo apt-get install kubuntu-desktop
```I've read something about a login screen, but all I see on startup is a username/password field.  

Enter/select your username from the login screen; before entering your password, press F10, then choose "Session" and choose "KDE". then enter your password and hit enter.

You will be given a choice to make the session change permanent ("Make default"); maybe you shouldn't do this for the first few times, until you're comfortable with KDE.
