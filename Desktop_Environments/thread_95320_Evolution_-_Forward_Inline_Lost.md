Used to be I could *Shft-Ctrl-J* to forward a message inline. Now in Breezy all I can ever do is forward as attachment. I've scoured the menus and Preferences and I can't figure out how to forward inline.

Everything I've read online suggests that either I use the key combination above or go to the "Actions" menu and select Forward inline, problem is I can't find the "Actions" menu.

At the moment I forward the message as an attachment, open the attachment, copy and paste the content into the email and then remove the attachment. Which works, but seems a long way around.

Anybody have any suggestions? Other than use Thunderbird?

### Post by krokodil on 2005-12-07
Got the solution from someone at CLUG:

In Evolution go:
Edit > Preferences
Composer Preferences
General Tab
Change "Forward Style" to Inline.
