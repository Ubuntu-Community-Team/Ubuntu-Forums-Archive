### Post by crdlb on 2011-11-09
One option is to use Caps Lock. In Keyboard Layout -> Layouts -> Options, you can set 'Caps Lock Behavior' to 'Make Caps Lock an additional Super'. I use that even though I have a Super key.

I pulled an old keyboard with two Super keys out of my closet, and I can confirm that Super_R seems to be ignored by GNOME Shell. I guess that's a bug.
