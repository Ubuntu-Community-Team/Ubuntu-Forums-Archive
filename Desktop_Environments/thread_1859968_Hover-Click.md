### Post by Mondair99 on 2011-10-16
The solution!


This makes anyone happy &#8211; there's a non-geek way to solve this. I have seen all kinds of suggestions on this problem, like editing your xorg.conf. No, please don't do that. Here we go:

On the top right of your desktop, click on the gear next to your name...
Click "System settings"...
Click "Universal access"...
Click "Pointing and clicking" tab...
Set "Hover click" to "OFF"!!!!

Your hover-click should now be deactivated. :D
