### Post by DirtDawg on 2005-09-15
Try the Blender Model Repositories:
[http://www.katorlegaz.com/index.php?a=download&c=Blender_3D_Model_Repository](http://www.katorlegaz.com/index.php?a=download&c=Blender_3D_Model_Repository)
People just upload whatever models they're willing to donate, I'm not sure you'll find exactly what you're looking for, but maybe it's a start.
A note: I think Blender is great. Beware the learning curve. Not as nice as some commercial packages (though nicer than others), especially if you've learned said commercial packages already, but also several thousand dollar$ cheaper. I personally (highly) recommend it.
