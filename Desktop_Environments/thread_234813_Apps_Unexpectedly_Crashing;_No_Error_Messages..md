### Post by dday376 on 2006-12-28
Don't know if this will help, but I was also having problems with audacity crashing.  My wife got me a USB Record Player (pretty cool, since we inherited a ton of records from her dad), and when I would try to record, it would crash a few minutes into the record.

What I finally did there was disable the play through (where it plays while recording).  The Audacity wiki has info on using USB mic with Audacity, so I thought you might be trying to use a USB mic.  If that's the case, check the wiki out - it talks about OSS vs. ALSA.  If not, dunno then.

GAIM might suffer the same problem.

For whatever it's worth.
