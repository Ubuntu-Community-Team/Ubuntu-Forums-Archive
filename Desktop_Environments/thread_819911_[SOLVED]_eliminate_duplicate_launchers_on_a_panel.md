### Post by Barriehie on 2008-06-05
Oops!  I've done that before and ended up with like 4 of everything...  What I did was close  any apps that were running and then by right clicking the mouse on the end of the panel, (where your first tab would show up) you'll get the -remove from panel- option.  Might have to move the mouse around a little while you click until you get the pointer in the  right spot.  If you can't find it you can always delete the panel and then add another one.

Barrie
