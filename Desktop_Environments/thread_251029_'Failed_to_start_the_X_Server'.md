### Post by Ziox on 2006-09-04
that warning is nothing, it just means that it might be writing over a customized xorg.conf file...who cares? :p

does x boot after sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg?

also what graphics card are you using?

lspci -v | grep VGA

and show us your xorg.conf file...

nano /etc/X11/xorg.conf

or

cat /etc/X11/xorg.conf
