### Post by #&amp;thj^% on 2019-10-18
I had to disable the experimental views via:
```
gsettings set org.gnome.nautilus.preferences use-experimental-views false
```
BTW: Nemo is what I use now in lieu of Nautilus.

### Post by uRock on 2019-10-18
To remove the trash icon, got to the software center and install gnome tweaks, then launch it, then click Extensions, then click the gear icon to the right of Desktop Icons, then you'll see the option to turn off the icon. 
I made just uploaded a video for it here [https://youtu.be/fcTxCAWz3Ns](https://youtu.be/fcTxCAWz3Ns)
