### Post by JedV on 2006-09-30
I have ADSL in Vancouver, BC and if the torrent is popular, I max out at 160 KB/s.  Not too shabby.  On average though, I pull in around 100 KB/s.

Conversely, upload rates are capped at 40 KB/s.  These'll easily max out in BT, but fortunately are throttleable so you don't cripple your entire connection.

BTW, I use Azureus.
