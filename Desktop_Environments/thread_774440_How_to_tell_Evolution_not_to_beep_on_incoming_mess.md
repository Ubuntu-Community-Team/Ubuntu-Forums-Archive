### Post by lucky_frank on 2008-04-30
It's not a "regular" preference, but a Plugin

Do Edit->Plugins, then look in the list for Mail Notification and select in (don't uncheck the box, just select it).
Then click the Configuration tab on the right and uncheck
Play sound when new messages arrive.

Took me a little while to find it.
