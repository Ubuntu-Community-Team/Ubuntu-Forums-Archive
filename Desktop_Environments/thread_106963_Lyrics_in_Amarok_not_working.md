### Post by kingzasz on 2005-12-22
Found the problem:
> 
Due to changes at [http://lyrc.com.ar](http://lyrc.com.ar) in the way search results are displayed, that unfortunately didn't happen until *after* amaroK 1.3.7 was released, the lyrics lookup will not return results IN the amaroK lyrics tab. If you click the "open in external browser" button, you will see the lyrics for your track.

This has been rectified, and a bugfix version will be released soon.

Note: this affects ALL versions of amaroK (with the exception of svn) that have a lyrics tab.

Thank you for your patience in this matter.
