### Post by LorenzoD on 2005-10-27
Most likely, you don't have the notification area on your panel. You'll need to re-add it. Right click on the panel, select "Add to panel", and in the dialog that appears, find "Notification Area". Add it.

EDIT: In the "Add to panel" dialog, you'll find it under the section called "Utilities".
