### Post by kerry_s on 2006-11-06
A guide? Just right click the top panel> customize panel> click on the "-" next to panel 2, that will remove it. Then just select normal width for panel 1, that's done, now right click the panel 1> add new item and add what you want, then you can right click the icons> move, to get them where you want. Remember linux is very flexible you can have your own setup/look. You have a chose to choose how you want it. I use a mix of xfce4 & fluxbox for my desktop->
