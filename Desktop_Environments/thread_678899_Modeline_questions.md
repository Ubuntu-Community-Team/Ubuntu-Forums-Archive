### Post by dvjunk on 2008-02-08
Just happened across your post.
The length of a VGA cable before losing quality is a function of the quality of the cable. We use VGA cables that are over 30 meters long at our church, but they are about an inch in diameter, and use BNC connections.
So the answer is, buy a high quality cable for the length you need and it should be fine.
