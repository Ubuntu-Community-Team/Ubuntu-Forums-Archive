### Post by 3Miro on 2010-02-10
Try Alt + F2 and then type gconf-editor and press enter.

This will bring up a Gnome editor with many settings. Try Gnome -> Apps -> Component Viewer and/or other settings. To be honest I am not sure exactly where the setting is.

This is for Gnome anyway, for other managers you will have to figure them out one at a time.
