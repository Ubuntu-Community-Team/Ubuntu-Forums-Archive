### Post by Soybean on 2007-07-05
I believe that in the Beryl Settings, under "General Options," you need to adjust the "Level of Focus Stealing Prevention." The option confuses me a bit, so I can't tell you if you need more or less of it, but I do know that it can cause the sort of problems you're having.

### Post by orb9220 on 2007-07-05
You need to set focus stealing to low or none
