### Post by ardchoille on 2006-02-12
The only way I know of to do this is to add the Show Desktop applet to the panel and use that. Right-click the panel, choose "Add to panel", then in the Add to panel ui, go to the "Desktop & Windows" section and click on "Show Desktop" then click the Add button. that applet will do what you are trying to do.
