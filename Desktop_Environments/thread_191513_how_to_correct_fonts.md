### Post by JAW on 2006-06-07
Posted about this earlier, see if it works for you... i dunno why they don't just allow us to select the unaliased "bitmapped" fonts as standard. Just make sure you run fontconfig as mentioned in the linked post below, switch off Hinting and Smoothing in System->Prefs>Fonts settings, and setup the DPI correctly in the Details, on my 15" laptop display @ 1024x768 my dpi is 86 (for reference).
