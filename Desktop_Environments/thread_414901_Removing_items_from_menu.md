### Post by rhay on 2007-04-20
I've installed KDevelop, but then ran short of disk space and decided to uninstall it. I did both with Applications -> Add/Remove... After removing KDevelop the items are still in Applications->Programming. I think I have to remove them manually. How to do it?

Ubuntu 6.12/Gnome 2.16.1

---

### Post by Theimon on 2007-04-21
Applications --> Accessories --> Alacarte or open a terminal and run ```
alacarte
```
Then you can deselect those entries.
