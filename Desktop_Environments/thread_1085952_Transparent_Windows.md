### Post by benerivo on 2009-03-03
You can make window titlebars/window decorations transparent with emerald, but the actual interface elements, such as the back / forward buttons in firefox, remain opaque. You can download emerald themes (also known as beryl or compiz themes) from gnome-look.org.
