### Post by Fancycakes on 2011-01-03
I just recently started using AwesomeWM with Gnome on Ubuntu 10.04 and I have come across an interesting functionality: my number pad, whether num lock is on or off, controls the movement of the mouse.

I was wondering at first if this was something I could edit in my rc.lua, but I haven't found anything to the effect. I have consulted Professor Google to no avail. Is this a normal functionality, or have I just drawn the short straw?

---

### Post by cipherboy_loc on 2011-01-03
Open up gnome-control-center, and in either the mouse or the keyboard properties, it will give you the option of controlling the mouse with with the number keys. Uncheck that box.



Cipherboy
