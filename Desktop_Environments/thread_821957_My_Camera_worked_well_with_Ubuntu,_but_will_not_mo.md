### Post by Margus81 on 2008-06-08
Thank everybody for the quick response!

I can answer now the question myself.

Where f-spot failed, gThumb succeeded.

It started to download drivers and now my computer recognizes the camera.

So everybody who use Xubuntu and also want to watch pictures, install gThumb.

Still, there is no Icon of the Camera on Desktop, it could be useful, but at least now I get the pictures.
