### Post by Mat10 on 2005-05-04
Hey Folks, I'm using Gnome and I was downloading and upgrading the repositories this morning when my screen saver kicked in and froze the system.
Everything is at default and no changes were made since the initial install.  I have a radeon 8500LE video card and using a 800 celeron processor 66mgz sidebus.

I turned off screen saver and after rebooting I restarted the process and things were back to normal no damage done.

You might just as a precaution turn screen saving off before attempting the upgrade and download proceedure.  This could be an isolated event or not, better to be safe than sorry.
