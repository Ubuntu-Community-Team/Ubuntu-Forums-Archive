### Post by vanadium on 2011-08-06
Why they appear? Because it is designed that way.

You can alter some hidden configuration settings to change the behaviour. You can find how to do that here: [http://www.webupd8.org/2011/04/how-to-remove-mounted-drives-from.html](http://www.webupd8.org/2011/04/how-to-remove-mounted-drives-from.html), starting from the second section "How to remove mounted drives from the Ubuntu Unity launcher". Thus, you could decide to have your removable drives apprear on your desktop, in the unity launchbar, or not at all.
