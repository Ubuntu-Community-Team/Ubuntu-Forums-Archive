### Post by Ateo on 2007-05-15
This fix did not work for me. Here is the fix I applied to get rid of the 'always on top' / 'visible window border' issue.

NOTE: KXDocker must be running before you do this!!!

Goto: Beryl Settings Manager -> Window Management

Enable 'Set Window Attribs by various criteria'

Click the down arrow for 'Border'

Add kxdocer to the list. Click the + sign, then select 'window class' from the left most drop down. Click the 'grab' button and then simply click on KXDocker. Save Beryl settings and then quit and restart KXDocker.
