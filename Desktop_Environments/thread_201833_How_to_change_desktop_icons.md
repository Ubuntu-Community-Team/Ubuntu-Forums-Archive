### Post by linuxgeekery on 2006-06-22
Go to gnome-look.org's icons section. Download the theme you want, then open up the Gnome Theme Manager (System > Preferences > Themes) and drag the file you just downloaded onto the theme manager window. The theme will install. Then select your current theme, go to theme details > icons and choose the icons you just installed.
