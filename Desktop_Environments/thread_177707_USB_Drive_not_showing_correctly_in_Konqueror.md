### Post by link141 on 2006-05-16
I believe this is a very common error in breezy.  I've experienced this exact situation with my 250 MB USB Flash Drive, *every* time on the live CD and the first couple of times after booting.  After that, the problem seemed to correct itself.  In some cases I've observed that KDE doesn't like certain mediums, not the type but maybe a certain CD, or in your case, a flash drive.  My advice: make sure this isn't your second time booting or your not on live CD.  If the problem persists after booting a few times, borrow a friend's USB drive and see if that works.  In many ways, Dapper is more stable (Kubuntu version that is) than Breezy, Dapper will be available in June.  We'll have this fixed in no time ;).
