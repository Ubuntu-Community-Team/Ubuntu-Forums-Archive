### Post by anil_robo on 2005-12-23
You can download themes from [http://art.gnome.org](http://art.gnome.org). Search for some Mac themes. Then open the theme manager (System--> Preferences--> Theme) and drag and drop the downloaded theme .gz files in the themes window. They will be installed. Then select them! That's it!
