The options there are *presets*, so if you have done any Compiz configuration of your own you shouldn't switch visual effects settings in the Appearance dialog or you'll load that preset and loose your own configurations.

("None" switches to Metacity, "Normal" and "Extra" are two different pre-configured Compiz setups.)
