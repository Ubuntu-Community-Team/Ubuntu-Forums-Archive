### Post by jordilin on 2006-07-24
Try to put the icons back in the tray and see if that happens again. Icons should not disappear unless you quit them by mistake (you quit some panel) or there's some hardware problem.

### Post by mcduck on 2006-07-24
Are you sure that you didn't remove the notification area from your panel? Right-click on the panel, select 'Add to Panel' and then find 'Notification Area' and drag&drop it to your panel and your icons should be back ;) (This is assuming that you are using Ubntu with Gnome, not KDE or XFCE or something..)

### Post by Uncle Spellbinder on 2006-07-24
This happened to me a week or so ago. For some reason, the notification area just stopped working. A good test is to open Opera or Amarok (both of which have icons placed in the notification area tray). Neither showed for me even though the notification area icon was in the panel. I removed the notification area icon then re-added it. No problems since.
