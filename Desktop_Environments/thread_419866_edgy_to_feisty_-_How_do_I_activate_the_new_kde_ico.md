### Post by hellblade on 2007-04-23
I upgraded from edgy to feisty and while many kde settings changed to the new kubuntu defaults (eg the new konqueror profiles) I can't find how to activate the new icon launch effect.

***Update:** I found it. I had to make VisualActivate=1 in ~/.kde/share/config/kdeglobals*

*Thanks goes to chemist109 from #kubuntu:)*
