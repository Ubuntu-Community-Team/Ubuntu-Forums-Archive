### Post by kingsidy on 2005-12-20
go to preferems > theme, click the latter. just select the icon theme and drag it and drag it onto the theme preference windows. A window will say that the theme installed properly or something like that. To use the theme, go to preferece > theme, then select theme detail, click on the icon tab, and whatever the name of the icon theme you just installed is, it will appear there.
