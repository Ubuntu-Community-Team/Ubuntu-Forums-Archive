### Post by mister_playboy on 2012-02-27
The programs you mention use GTK (Gnome) rather than Qt (KDE), so they don't follow your normal theme settings.

Have you already played around with the settings under System Settings > Application Appearance > GTK Config?  In FF you can adjust colors via Edit > Preferences > Content > Colors.

I assume you are using a dark theme since you report white text on white backgrounds.  I admit I have not found an easy way to run a dark theme without adjusting lots of settings in individual programs. :P
