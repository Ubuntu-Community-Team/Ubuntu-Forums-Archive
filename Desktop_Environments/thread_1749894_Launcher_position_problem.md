### Post by voltaicsca on 2011-05-05
So, I don't want to be a jerk about a *brand new* interface that I'm sure has some kinks that need to be worked out, but the fixed position of the launcher in 11.04 is very frustrating.

I have 3 monitors, and the "main" monitor is in the middle.  So that means that the launcher is always going to be on the seam between 2 screens?  and I have no way to change that?  and that decision was made ON PURPOSE?

The idea to make something as fundamental to the user experience as the means by which they access all of their stuff un-movable is just plain bad practice.  It might work for 90% of users, but it's just going to annoy the other 10%.  I love Linux, and Ubuntu in particular, but it's way too early in the game to start introducing rigid restrictions on how users use the software.

Steve Jobs is in the business of telling users how to use his software.  I didn't think that Mark Shuttleworth was.
