### Post by Happy_Man on 2007-08-12
> **67GTA said:**
> I have several glass or transparent panel backgrounds for Ubuntu, but they won't display properly when I try to use them for Kubuntu kicker. Where can I find some for KDE? I couldn't find any on kdelook.org, unless I overlooked them.
For transparency, right click the panel, Configure Panel, Appearance, and under the Panel Background box, click "enable transparency" and then say apply. For a glass background, just look for one and set it as background in the same area of the Configure dialog I just mentioned.
