### Post by JonasDK on 2010-10-17
Damn, I can't delete my thread. But I found a way! I select my mails and then use 'Copy to...' and then copy them to a folder I made on my gmailaccount in Evolution. It then slowly (but steady) copies my emails via the internet on my gmail account.

Evolution amazes me again!
