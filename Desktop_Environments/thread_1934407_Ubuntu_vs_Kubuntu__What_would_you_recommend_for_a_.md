I wouldn't recommend either of those two, personally, but suggest you look at either Xubuntu or Lubuntu, both of which are much less resource hungry than Ubuntu or Kubuntu.

Of course, if one of those last two runs OK on the machine, stick with the DE you prefer, and don't listen to me or others.  However, I think you owe it to yourself to look at both Xubuntu and Lubuntu, just to see what alternatives there are for you.  You can add any applications/packages in the repos to any of those OSs, so don't imagine that just because you have Xubuntu or Lubuntu you can not still use LibreOffice or anything else;  if it's in the repos you can always add it if you want it.
