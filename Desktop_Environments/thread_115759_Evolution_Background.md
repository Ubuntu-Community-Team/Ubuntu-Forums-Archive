### Post by psYchotic on 2006-01-11
I don't know about the evolution background thing, but I know that I can't stand watching a screen if the refresh rate isn't high enough. I'm sure you know how to change that, but just in case :
Goto System->Preferences->Screen Resolution and set the Refresh rate as high as possible (note that most monitors have lower refresh rates with higher resolutions, so maybe you'll need to make your resolution a little lower to be able to change your refresh rate to something like 75Hz).
I hope this helps, if not, I'm sorry =)
