### Post by mcduck on 2005-11-28
You can customize panels quite freely in Gnome..

To get your bottom panel back click on the top panel, select 'New Panel' and the drag the panel to bottom of screen with middle mouse button (or right-click the new panel, select 'Properties' and set orientation to 'Bottom')

Then you want your window list back. Again, right-click in the panel, select 'Add to Panel' and you'll get a nice window full of widgets you can put in panels. Find one called 'Window List' and drag it to your panel.
