### Post by Krytarik on 2011-04-24
The general font settings are in "System -> Preferences -> Appearance -> Fonts".

Also, if you think that the fonts aren't rendered correctly, click at "Details" at the lower left of those tab, and tinker with those settings a bit.

As for good fonts, personally, I use the "Droid" set, package "ttf-droid", it's in the official repos.
