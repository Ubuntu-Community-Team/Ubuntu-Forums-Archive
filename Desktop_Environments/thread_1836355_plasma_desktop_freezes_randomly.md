### Post by schnelle on 2011-08-31
In KDE 4.6.5 gmail-plasmoid (since version 0.7.18 ) makes my plasma to freeze. If you have gmail plasmoid remove it and test plasma without it.

### Post by schnelle on 2011-09-28
Gmail-plasmoid doesn't freeze my plasma anymore. 

Right click on plasmoid>settings>misc and choose "fetching mechanism" - "KDE". That fixes problem for me :)
