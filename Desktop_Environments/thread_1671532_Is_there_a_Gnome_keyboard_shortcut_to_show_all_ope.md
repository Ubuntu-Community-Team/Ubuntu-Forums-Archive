### Post by john_spiral on 2011-01-20
Not exactly what I was looking for, but more useful in some ways:

Ring Switcher:
This gives you a view kind of like when you flip through CD and DVD covers on iTunes, only the view is of your available windows.
Clockwise rotation: super-tab
Counter-clockwise rotation: super-shift-tab
Let go to land on the window you see in the center of the screen.

above from:

[http://theledgeofknow.blogspot.com/2008/12/compiz-fusion-some-basic-commands.html](http://theledgeofknow.blogspot.com/2008/12/compiz-fusion-some-basic-commands.html)
