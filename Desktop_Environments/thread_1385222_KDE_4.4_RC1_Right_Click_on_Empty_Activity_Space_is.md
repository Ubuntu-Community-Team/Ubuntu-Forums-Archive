### Post by Zorael on 2010-01-19
Click the cashew in the top-right corner, pick Desktop Activity settings and then the Mouse Actions section. Do you have the right mouse button mapped to pop up the context menu, or is it empty? I believe it's called "Standard menu". Else just add it.
