### Post by wieman01 on 2006-09-26
Actually you're very close...

_Change this section (highlighted in 'red'):_
> Section "ServerLayout"
	Identifier	"Default Layout"
        Screen          "IntScr"
        Screen          "ExtScr" LeftOf "IntScr" 
	**[COLOR="Red"]Option  	"Xinerama" "true"[/COLOR]**
	InputDevice	"Generic Keyboard"
	InputDevice	"Configured Mouse"
	InputDevice     "stylus" "SendCoreEvents"
	InputDevice     "cursor" "SendCoreEvents"
	InputDevice     "eraser" "SendCoreEvents"
	InputDevice	"Synaptics Touchpad"
EndSection
_Add these 3 lines at the end of the file:_
> Section "ServerFlags"
       Option		"Xinerama" "true"
EndSection
That should do.
