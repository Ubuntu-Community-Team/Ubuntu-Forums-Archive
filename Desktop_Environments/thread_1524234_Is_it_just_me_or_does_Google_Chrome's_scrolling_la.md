### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2011-01-28
If you're running a recent build of Chromium, you can try to enable the experimental hardware acceleration stuff. Just head to about:flags. You're looking for GPU Accelerated Compositing and GPU Accelerated Canvas 2D.

Not inherently related to scrolling, but it makes a huge difference with [Mozilla's stress test]("http://demos.hacks.mozilla.org/openweb/HWACCEL/") and may have some effect on general rendering performance.
