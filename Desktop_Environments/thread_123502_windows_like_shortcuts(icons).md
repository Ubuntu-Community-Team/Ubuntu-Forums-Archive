### Post by veritas366 on 2006-01-30
Here's one way, if I think I understand your question.  Click on "system" and then on "administration".  Find the entry called "networking".  Right click on then select "add this launcher to panel" or "add this launcher to desktop".  From now on, when you click on that icon in the panel or desktop, you'll get all the network options.  

In general you can add anything to the panel this way, or you can right click on the panel and click "add to panel". When you do that, there will be a bunch of items you can choose from or you can select "custom application launcher" in which you add whatever program you want and select whatever icon you want to represent it.  

Hope that helps.
