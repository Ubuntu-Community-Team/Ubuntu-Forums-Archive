### Post by 23senick on 2012-07-15
&#65279;I came across this thread when trying to solve a notification format issue. My notifications mysteriously changed from the classic Ubuntu white font/black background to black font/grey background.  Small issue, but it didn't match anything else in Ubuntu desktop.  

Got a solution in the end - I have xfce desktop as well as Unity. I started an xfce session, went into applications/settings/settings manager and then notifications. It gives theme options, one of which is "smoke" with white font on black background. Selected this, closed the xfce session, started a Unity 3D session - and pleased to find there were matching notifications (black background/white font).  

So it seems somehow the notification settings in xfce and Unity are carried over. It just seems easier to tweak in xfce...
