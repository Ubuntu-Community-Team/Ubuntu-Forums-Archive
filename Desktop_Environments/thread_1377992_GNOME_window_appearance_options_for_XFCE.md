### Post by gsmanners on 2010-01-11
In Xfce, the "Appearance" app lets you change the GTK theme and the "Window Manager" lets you change the Xfwm4 theme. To customize further, you would have to create (or modify) a theme yourself.

See:
[http://www.xfce.org/xfwm4-theme-howto/](http://www.xfce.org/xfwm4-theme-howto/)
[http://live.gnome.org/GnomeArt/Tutorials/GtkThemes](http://live.gnome.org/GnomeArt/Tutorials/GtkThemes)
