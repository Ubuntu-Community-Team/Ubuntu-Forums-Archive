### Post by henriquemaia on 2006-05-20
Open a nautilus window. Select Edit --> Preferences

Then, on the new window, select the **Preview** tab. You'll see severall options there. Turn off the **Preview of sound files:** choosing the **never** option.

I think this is what you meant.
