### Post by hunteramor on 2005-11-07
i'm pretty sure i've read somewhere on the forums that this is just a limitation of gnome, and there isn't anything you can do about it.  If the only time it bothers you is with notifications, you could remove the notification area from that panel and live without it, or put it on another panel if you have one, that wouldn't be so annoying.  

for example, the workspace switcher never gets transparent either, if you put it next to that it wouldn't appear so incongruous.  i have my notification area on the left side of my bottom panel and the workspace switcher on the right, so the middle is all nice and transparent, rather than being broken up with alternation.

just a matter of matching your aesthetics to the capabilities of what you're working with.
