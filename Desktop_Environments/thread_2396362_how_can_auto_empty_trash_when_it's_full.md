### Post by Rog131 on 2018-07-14
The trash settings with the Dolphin: Configure Dolphin > Trash.

Dolphin Handbook: Dolphin > Help > Dolphin Handbook F1 > 3. Configuring Dolphin > Trash

Online: [https://docs.kde.org/trunk5/en/applications/dolphin/configuring-dolphin.html#preferences-dialog-trash](https://docs.kde.org/trunk5/en/applications/dolphin/configuring-dolphin.html#preferences-dialog-trash)
