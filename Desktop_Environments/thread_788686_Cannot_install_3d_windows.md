### Post by ellalan on 2008-09-11
Hi
You can go to Synaptics package manager( System-> Admin->synaptics),
and click search and type "compiz" in the box.
You can mark the compiz related boxes and click apply. After installing them, you can get in to compiz config settings manager by going system->Preferences. There are lot of helpful posts lying around in this forum and you have to look for them.
If you are stuck, post your problem and there are plenty of people will come forward to help you.
