### Post by Fabled One on 2011-05-20
I got this working:
I initially had "Application Switcher" active which had the problem.
Now, I turned that off and enabled "Static Application Switcher".

Alt-tabbing now works properly with compiz enabled (although I don't know the difference between the two types of switchers.)
