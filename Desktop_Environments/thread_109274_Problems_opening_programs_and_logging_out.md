### Post by Vinze on 2005-12-28
Well, I don't really know what the problem is, but I can help you to shut down your computer correctly next time.

You see, everytime you get a situation like this just press CTRL + ALT + F4. This will get you out of graphical mode. Then you can login, and then you can do sudo reboot if you want to reboot.

However, you can also do startx, it then might ask you to delete something, but you can just do that with sudo rm <file to be deleted>. Then you can do startx again and you will be back in graphical mode.

Hope that helped.
