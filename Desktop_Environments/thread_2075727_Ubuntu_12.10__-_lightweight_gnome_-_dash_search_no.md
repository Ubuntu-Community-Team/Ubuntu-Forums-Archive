### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-10-24
That is because the Dash home search will find recently used applications and documents. To find installed applications you need to search the Dash applications lens. Give it a little time because it needs build and retrieve its index files.

Open the Dash and click the second icon from the left at the bottom of the Dash, or press Super + A.

You may also search for 'help' in the applications lens to read the Ubuntu Desktop Guide as you are clearly unfamiliar with Unity.

Regards.
