### Post by hhh on 2010-07-24
You can't change 1280x800 to 1024x600 without distortion because it's not the same aspect ratio. You can do either 1024x640 or 960x600.

I might be misunderstanding you, but just go to System>Preferences>Display and try out the different available resolutions and refresh rates.

More info...
