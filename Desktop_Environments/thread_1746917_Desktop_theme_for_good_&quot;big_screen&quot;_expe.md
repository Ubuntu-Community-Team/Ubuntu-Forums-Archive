### Post by retchid on 2011-05-02
Best to set screen resokution to 1024 x 768 

That is the best browser size in any event
Everything looks fine other resolutions are either not compatible with tv (may get a message on the tv saying mode not supported) or makes browser pages look to small.

TV 9 feet away no problems reading text etc

doesnt matter whether your using windows, ubuntu or openssuse.

Icons can always be made small or larger but my experience is that it looks fine from the off.
