You're attempting to change your desktop background and getting nothing, yes?

Press the following on your keyboard:
```

A text prompt should show up.
Type in: "gnome-terminal" without quotes into the text box.
Then, in terminal, should it come up on your screen, type:
gnome-background-properties %F
And press return/enter.
```
It should give you a reason why it's not opening.
