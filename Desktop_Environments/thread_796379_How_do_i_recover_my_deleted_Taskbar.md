### Post by psavva on 2008-05-16
Right click on a panel --> "+Add to Panel" --> Menu Bar --> Click Add -->Notification Area --> Click Add

The Main Menu is the applications, the Notifications are is the little applet that allows you to see your currently running programs or otherwise known as i think the taskbar

Play around with the different widgets there, it could discover some cool widgets that you might use :D
