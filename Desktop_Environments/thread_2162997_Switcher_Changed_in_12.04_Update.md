### Post by 9638172 on 2013-07-16
Resolved!  Although I don't believe the graphics driver was on the list of updates, I reinstalled it.  Now everything is back as it should be.  For reference the driver is amd-driver-installer-catalyst-13-4-linux-x86.x86_64.zip and the graphics chip is Radeon 8570 (ie A8 6600k).
