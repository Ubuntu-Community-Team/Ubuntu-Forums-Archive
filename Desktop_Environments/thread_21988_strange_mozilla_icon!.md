### Post by chz on 2005-03-25
download this [http://stuff.xergio.net/firefox.xpm](http://stuff.xergio.net/firefox.xpm) and then save to a location you like. then right click on your icon, select properties, click on the button where the current icon is, and then set it to wherever you saved the xpm file.
