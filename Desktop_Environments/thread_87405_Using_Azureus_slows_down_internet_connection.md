### Post by Cyril on 2005-11-08
When I run Azureus, even tough I am only downloading and uploading at less than 20kB/s, the connection to the internet becomes unbearably slow.  For example it can take several minutes just to load a page on this forum.  

Other computers on the same wireless network will also experience this slow down when azureus is running.

This problem however does not occur if I run azureus on windows.  Not only that  I can acheive much higher download and upload rates in windows. 60kB/s on average.

I am running breezy, have disabled ipv6, am sure that port forwarding is properly configured on my router and have also enabled port forwarding in breezy as reccomended somewhere in this forum.

Any ideas guys?  Thanks in advance.

### Post by Noah0504 on 2005-11-08
Well, I have the same problem when I use the maximum upload speed of my internet connection.  I suggest going into the Azureus settings, and limiting your upload speed to about 5-10 KB/s under your maximum upload speed.
