### Post by goodbye-windows(tm) on 2012-07-15
Solved.

To remove the empty entries, open the settings manager>panel. This shows a fully enabled editor, and can delete the empty entries.

Accessing the menu via the properties (after right clicking on the empty icon) does not allow them to be deleted.
