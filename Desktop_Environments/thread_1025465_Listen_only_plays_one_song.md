### Post by misterbrown on 2008-12-30
Defenestratos,

I thought this was a problem too, but I figured it out.  Let's say you put a CD in.  You'll see the track list in the right column.  If you highlight them all and move them to the left column(playlist) then you won't have to press play again after each song ends.  Just minimize the window, and go about your business!  Hope this helped!
