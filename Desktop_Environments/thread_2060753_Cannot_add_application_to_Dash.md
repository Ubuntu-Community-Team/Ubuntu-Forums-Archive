### Post by cortman on 2012-09-20
There's an app for adding/removing programs from the Dash in Gnome shell- it's usually under "System Tools" and it's called "Main Menu". The actually app name is alacarte. If it's not installed you can install it, and add/remove programs from the dash with it.
