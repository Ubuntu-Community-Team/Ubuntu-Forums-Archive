### Post by Krytarik on 2010-12-11
To change the icon-theme to the one you want:
- "System -> Preferences -> Appearance"
- choose the desktop-theme you want to use, generally
- click on "Customize" below the themes
- then click on the "Icons"-tab
The "Login"-icons are only for the login-screen, like the name suggests.;)
