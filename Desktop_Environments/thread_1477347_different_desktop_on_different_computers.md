### Post by mgmiller on 2010-05-08
You are probably running different themes on the 2 machines.  The new default is to have the window controls on the left side.  In the machine that has them on the right side, you could open System > Preferences > Appearance and just select a different theme.  The 2 new ones are Ambiance and Radiance.  They are both very nice and I use one or the other as a default on all my new installs.  If you don't like the controls on the left, there is a setting in gconf editor to make them what ever you like.  Here is a link that tells you how to do it.

[http://tombott.com/ubuntu_10.04_lucid_lynx_window_control_position](http://tombott.com/ubuntu_10.04_lucid_lynx_window_control_position)
