### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-04-09
```
sudo apt-get remove indicator-messages
``` Then right click indicator applet( mail icon) and remove from panel , next  right click the panel and select > add to panel > add the indicator applet . The sound icon will now return without  the envelope.
