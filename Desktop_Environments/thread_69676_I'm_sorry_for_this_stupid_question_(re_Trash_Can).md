### Post by Zotova on 2005-09-27
Hi Christy,

Do you mean you clicked 'remove from panel'? If so then right click the bottom toolbar and click 'add to panel' - a window will open with various options. Scroll down and select the wastebasket and add it to the panel. Your trash can should now be back, if it is in the wrong place the simply right click on the trash can and click move - you will then be able to move it to the location of your choice.
