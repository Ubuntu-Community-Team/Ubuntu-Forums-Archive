### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-08-16
There really is only one way to find out. But it is best to wait until 13.10 is released or at least until feature freeze has past (29 August). It really is up to the developers of Compiz Fusion to keep their code compatible and there in no point is chasing a moving target. Ubuntu 13.10 will be sitting on Xmir and not the Xserver. That should not make any difference but at some point during 2014 Mir will be the Display Manager/Compositor and how that will affect Compiz and related components is at present unknown at least by ordinary users such as myself.

I am running 13.10 with unity-system-compositor installed. I have not tried using Compiz Configuration Settings Manager to set many effects apart from making the top panel transparent. Compiz Fusion I do not use.
