### Post by pauper on 2008-07-20
Note: This is the way it's done in Gnome; I'm not sure if it's any help in
your case (KDE).

System--Preferences--Keyboard Shortcuts

Look for Desktop:"Show the panel menu" field. "Win" key is called, I believe,
"Super L".
