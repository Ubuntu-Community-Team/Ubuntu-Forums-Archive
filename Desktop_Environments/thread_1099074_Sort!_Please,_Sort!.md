### Post by asmoore82 on 2009-07-29
Gnome sticks to a Best of Both World's approach.
Your Desktop is always exactly as you arrange it.

To see it sorted, simply view the folder itself: "Places -> Desktop"

After working with it however you need in that folder view,
Your actual Desktop is *still* exactly as you arrange it :biggrin:!

If you must clean it up permanently, drag the arrangement out of
"Places -> Desktop" on to your Desktop.

**Edit**: don't know about Xubuntu, though.
