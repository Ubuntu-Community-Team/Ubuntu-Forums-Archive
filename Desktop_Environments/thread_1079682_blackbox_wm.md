### Post by kerry_s on 2009-02-24
i don't think to many still use it, fluxbox is better and more up-to-date, the last release of blackbox was in 2005([http://blackboxwm.sourceforge.net/](http://blackboxwm.sourceforge.net/)), linux moves fast and there have been a lot of changes on the system level, so you might experience more bugs than in other wm's.

trying to remove gnome is like pulling teeth, just use synaptic to
locate the gnome-* packages and remove them, just to let you know it could break your install.

most of us start from the ground and work up.
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/minimal](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/minimal)
