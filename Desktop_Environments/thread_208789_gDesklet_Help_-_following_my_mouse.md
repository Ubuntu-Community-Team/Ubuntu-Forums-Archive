### Post by mhancoc7 on 2006-07-04
I had this same problem. The way I got it to "stick" was to put my cursor over the gdesklets shell title in my taskbar. I then right clicked and selected "Move to another Workspace". Select another Workspace. Then you should be able to place the InfodomesticsBar where you want it.

Let me know if that helps.
