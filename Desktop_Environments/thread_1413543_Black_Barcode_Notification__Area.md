### Post by koleoptero on 2010-02-22
You can replace ubuntu's notification system with xfce's one by installing the xfce4-notifyd package. It's not exactly a solution but it's one of the first things I do when I install xubuntu because it's more configurable (you can then select the corner the notifications appear and even theme from the Settings > Notifications menu).
