### Post by akiro.yamamoto on 2006-02-24
The wine folder is in your home directory.
If your using Gnome, go to your home dir.  Press [CTRL]+[H]. (This will show hidden files and directories)
You should now be able to see the previously hidden .wine directory.
Navigate through to the drive_c/Windows and look for your Font directory.
Hope this helps
