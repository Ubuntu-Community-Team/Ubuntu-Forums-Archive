Do you mean the window thumbnails on your desktop panel? or on the icon-only task manager?

For the windows on your desktop panel- you should have the taskbar thumbnails enabled in desktop effects- which you said you already have.
And if you meant the icon-only task manager (this is a widget by the way)....you need to configure the settings for the widget (rightclick on your desktop- unlock widgets- hover over your icon-only tm- click on settings icon (looks like a wrench); and under appearance you should have the 'tooltips' enabled.
