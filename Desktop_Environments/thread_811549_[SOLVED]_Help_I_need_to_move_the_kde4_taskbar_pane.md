### Post by Naegling23 on 2008-05-29
Im sorry that I cannot help you, but I can lend an explanation...sort of.

KDE 4.0 was put out in january in order for developers to test it out, and begin writing programs for it.  It is usable and stable, however, it is missing features...lots of them.  One of those is the panel, most of the configurations are missing.  Why?  because the programs that control those options have not been ported yet.

The kde team states (and im paraphrasing greatly) that 4.0 is for developers, and recommend for most users, waiting until 4.1 when most of the programs and features will be available.  4.1 should be available by the end of july (or, you can run the 4.1 beta in the meantime).   

So, once again, im sorry that I cannot help solve your problem, but its likely because the programs to control the panel and set up your desktop just have not been ported yet.  You might just have to wait a while, or run 3.5 until then.
