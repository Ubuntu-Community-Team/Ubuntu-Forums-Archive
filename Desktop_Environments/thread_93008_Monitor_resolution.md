### Post by Remmelas on 2005-11-21
I'm with Aysiu on this, those refresh rates don't mathematically support a 1280 resolution with a refresh rate you can look at.... unless u r running an lcd.  Go with ecobuntu's sugestion and try to reconfigure, if that fails, i'm afraid you are in for some manual xserver configuration(I had to do the same to mine to get my refresh rates to be proper for my oddball monitor).  To configure manually, i'd google up x11 modeline calculators, and probably read-edid too(this software will get your hardware's capabilities from the card).  sometimes just plugging in the refresh rates from read-edid into your xorg.conf file will correct your problem.
