### Post by amaroKer on 2008-03-20
As of Gutsy Gibbon, the GUI method is actually easier than with the terminal.  Check this out.

1) Right click on desktop
2) Click on "Change Desktop Background" in context menu.
3) Click on farthest right-hand tab called "Visual Effects".  From her e you can adjust your desktop effects.  For stock Ubuntu "Metacity" manager, click on "None".  For enhanced effects, try "Normal" or "Extra".  

If those run well, install 'compizconfig-settings-manager' from 'universe' repository.  This will enable "Custom" in the "Visual Effects" tab, and show a preferences button from which to launch the compiz configuration tool.

Hope this helps.
