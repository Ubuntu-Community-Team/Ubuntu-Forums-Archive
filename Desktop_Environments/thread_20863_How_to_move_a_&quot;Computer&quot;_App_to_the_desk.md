### Post by Buffalo Soldier on 2005-03-19
At the **top panel** (or taskbar as you call in in MS Windows). Go to **Applications -> System Tools -> Configuration Editor**. When you have that running, browse to **/apps/nautilus/desktop**. Then *check* **computer_icon_visible**.

Not sure whether you have to logout and login again for it to take effect.
