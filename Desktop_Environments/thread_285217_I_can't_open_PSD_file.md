### Post by Shay Stephens on 2006-10-26
Is it a 16bit image or 8bit?  If it is 16bit, then you probably won't be able to open it with gimp, it can't do anything more than 8bit at the moment.  

You can try cinepaint or pixel, they can handle 16bit files and should be able to open the image.
