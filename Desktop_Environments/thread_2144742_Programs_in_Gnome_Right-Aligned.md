Previously, using 10.04 (and Gnome v2 I guess), the icon would display on the left and, as another program was started. its icon would be added to the right; this time it's opposite and is a bit confusing.

### Post by ibjsb4 on 2013-05-13
You have your "Window List" position to the center of the panel.  The new gnome only gives the positions.  Left, right and center.  Just Alt + right click and move to the left :)
