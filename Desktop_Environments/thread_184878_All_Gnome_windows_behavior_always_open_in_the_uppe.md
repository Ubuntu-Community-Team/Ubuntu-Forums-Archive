### Post by bridgidpnh on 2008-05-31
I'm running Hardy and I have the same problem.
This isn't the best solution, but I hit "alt+space" and then "m" which at least lets you move the window down with the mouse or arrow keys so that the controls are accessible at the top of the window. When the window is where you want it, either left click or hit enter to let it go.
Also "alt+F4" closes the window on top. Keyboard shortcuts rock!
