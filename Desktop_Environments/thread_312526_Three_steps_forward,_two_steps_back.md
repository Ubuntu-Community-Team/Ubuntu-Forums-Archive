### Post by TheRingmaster on 2006-12-07
To have them display their names just right click on them, choose preferences, and check the "show workspace names in switcher"

you can change their names as well by going in to the preferences menu again and double-click on the default names (desk 1, desk 2...) usually it takes a few click to get to change the names.
