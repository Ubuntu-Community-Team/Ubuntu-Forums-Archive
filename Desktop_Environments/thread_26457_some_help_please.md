### Post by Buffalo Soldier on 2005-04-13
To install additional codec go to [http://ubuntuguide.org/#codecs](http://ubuntuguide.org/#codecs)

I'm comfortable with Music Player (rhythmbox) but if that's not your cup of tea, you can try install XMMS (kind of a WinAmp for GNU/Linux). For instructions go to [http://ubuntuguide.org/#xmms](http://ubuntuguide.org/#xmms)

There are other instructions for many other usefull things at [UbuntuGuide.org](http://ubuntuguide.org) that you may find interesting.
