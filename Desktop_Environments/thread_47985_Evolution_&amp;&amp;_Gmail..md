### Post by byen on 2005-07-10
ok... lemme see if i can put my 2 cents here...
1st: Yes there is an option under your mail account settings (edit>pref>mail ac>recieve option>leave mssg on server)  to leave a copy on the server. Also you might wanna change your settings in gmail.com>settings so that it may save read messages in the archive folder (this will prevent Evo from downloading the same mails over and over again when deleted from your Email client)
2nd: Which is better? well ..what do you need..an email client which checks mail at regular intervals,is fast and easy (Tbird) or a complete suite which has a good calender integrated into the gnome desktop and reminds you all the preset appointments even when evo is not running? (Evo). If you have mem to spare....evo is good. If you want smart, simple, light and easy..Tbird is the way to go!

Some might say Tbird has a calender extension too..but the gnome integrated calender(in evo) saved my skin a couple of times....

So it comes down to what you need out of it!!! 
Hope it helps. good luck!

EDIT: Yes, they do provide better text editing options
