### Post by Triton on 2005-04-15
I've run into this as well.  If everything isn't defined and properly the resolution and refresh are set funky.  I had to reconfigure mine on an my HP 1702 flat panel.  Here's the section to add.

Section "Monitor"
	Identifier	"Generic Monitor"
	Option		"DPMS"
	HorizSync	30-65
	VertRefresh	50-75
EndSection

obviously with the proper values for your monitor.
