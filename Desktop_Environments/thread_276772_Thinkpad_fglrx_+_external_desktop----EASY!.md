### Post by yatesco on 2006-10-13
Just thought I would share the love :)

I have a thinkpad T43 and I need to perform business presentations.  So, after googling around and becoming more and more dismayed at how hard it seemed to be to run a second monitor, I finally read the documentation for aticonfig and found that "aticonfig --initial=dual-head --screen-layout=above" looked promising.

After executing it (on dapper with flgrx) I rebooted and low and behold there was my external monitor at the right resolution (1024x768) and my main laptop at it's own resolution (1400x1200 or something like that).

I tried changing the resolution and they do indeed change independantly.  They aren't the same desktop though, the external monitor doesn't have any of the customisations that the first desktop does (like desktop icons, icon placement etc.), but for my situation that is actually good....I want the external display to be clear.

So, it works a treat....well happy.
