### Post by retrovertigo on 2009-04-15
This is called a "Plasmoid".  To get it back, click the little icon that looks like a cashew in the upper-right corner, then click "Add Widgets".  Find the widget called "Folder View" and drag it out onto the desktop.

You can actually put any folder on your desktop using the "Folder View" plasmoid, but it will default to the Desktop folder.
