### Post by audax321 on 2006-04-03
I haven't really tried Xinerama, but since you have an nVidia card, why not try out Twinview? That should correct your icon issue as well. I remember it being a lot better than plain old Xinerama. Here's my old Twinview xorg.conf file if you can use it...
