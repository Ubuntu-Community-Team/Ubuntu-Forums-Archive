### Post by RbikeRider on 2006-10-28
1. Right click "Applications". 
2. Click "Edit Menus"
3. Select the menu you want to an an application to. (ex: Internet)
4. Go to "File -> New Entry"
5. Fill in the "New Entry" form and click "OK"

Your new application will show up in menu you specified.  Hope this helps.
