### Post by claracc on 2010-11-25
> I need it became fast and can see videos not in slow-motion.

Perhaps I be wrong but I think it is impossible to see streaming or embeded videos correctly with this video card (64 Mb), taking into account flash software, also the amount of RAM is very short.

I have an old laptop pentium III, 1GHz, 512 Mb RAM but 64 Mb for video card, win xp OS and I see the streaming videos in slow motion with processor 100% working. Surfing the web also is limited in speed by processor and video card memory unless I use a very quick browser as k-meleon.

I tried different releases of xubuntu (8.10, 9.04, 9.10) but they didn't work well with this old hardware.
