### Post by Chame_Wizard on 2010-03-16
ALT+ F2 or System/Settings menu>System Settings>Appearance and/or Desktop or Window behavior>click on Categories and Tabs of choice>Get new schemes/themes.

As for the panel:right-click on the panel itself(unlock the widgets,if it's locked)>panel options>move the arrows to change your panel size/adjust the height and screen edge/add widgets/more settings,add spacers.

PS:You know,you can give each virtual desktop a name!!
