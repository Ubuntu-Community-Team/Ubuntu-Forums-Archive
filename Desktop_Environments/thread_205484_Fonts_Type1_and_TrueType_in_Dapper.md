### Post by barisurum on 2006-06-28
Hi leo. To install truetype fonts: In nautilus (File Manager) press Ctrl + L and
type "fonts:///" to the adress field. Copy any ttf font to that directory. Or in your home directory press Ctrl + H and copy ttf files to .fonts directory.
