### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2009-01-01
I do use KDE so maybe I can help:

1. Adept is pretty quirky these days since ported to KDE 4.  Honestly, in 5 years of KDE and 1.5 years in Kubuntu, I have never found it to be worth running.  I install Synaptic instead.  I know that's not an answer, sorry.

2. There's no panel spacer applet in 4.1.2 that ships with Intrepid, so moving icons is really bizarre.  You can download a panel spacer plasmoid from KDE-look.org: [http://www.kde-look.org/content/show.php/Panel+Spacer?content=89304](http://www.kde-look.org/content/show.php/Panel+Spacer?content=89304)
I have used it and it works well, though it takes some tinkering with to get things just where you want them.

3. You want "Task Manager" 

I love KDE, but honestly 4.1.2 is a desktop "only a mother could love", so to speak.  I expect 4.2 to be a great release, and 4.3 to be dynomite; but the thought of someone's first KDE experience being 4.1.2 kind of makes me cringe.  Just MHO.
