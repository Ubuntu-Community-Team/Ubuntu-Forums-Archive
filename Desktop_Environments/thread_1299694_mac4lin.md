You need to set the background of your top panel by right-clicking on it, and selecting properties, and then selecting the background tab, find the directory where you installed the themes (eg home/username/themes/mac4lin-graphite/ and select the panel-bng.png  file and you panels will look like a mac.

You are supposed to have a top panel and not supposted to remove the top panel.
