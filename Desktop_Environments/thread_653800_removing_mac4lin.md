### Post by benerivo on 2008-01-01
This may be because you are using the emerald window manager, which controls the look of window borders and buttons. I think the Mac4Lin installation steps include the installation of emerald and a 'os-x like' emerald theme. To go back to the human theme, you could try uninstalling emerald and then logging off and logging back in again.
