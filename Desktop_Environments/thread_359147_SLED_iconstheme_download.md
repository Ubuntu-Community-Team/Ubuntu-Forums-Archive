### Post by eurgain on 2007-10-12
If you like the look of SLED, then you could just go and get openSUSE, which, in its Gnome system, looks very much the same as SLED, but is free.

I'm not suggesting you extract all of the stuff from openSUSE (although there is nothing to stop you but the waste of time and effort) - just *run* openSUSE.  You will get something that looks like and works like SLED, and is really not that much different from Ubuntu in other respects.

For historical reasons, I run both distros each on several different systems, and like them (and dislike them) both in equal measure but for different reasons.  If you like the SUSE look, then it seems to me much easier to just *use* SUSE (it is nothing to be ashamed of) than to try to make another distro look like SUSE!

HTH
A
