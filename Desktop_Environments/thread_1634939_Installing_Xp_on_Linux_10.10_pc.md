### Post by C0derBear on 2010-12-01
Most of the Windows cd's I've seen lately have a boot-hook that you have to press a key to get them to boot from the CD, or they just roll back to the HD.

So after you select the CD try slowly tapping the spacebar until the boot starts, somewhere.
