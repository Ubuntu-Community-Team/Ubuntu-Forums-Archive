### Post by elpiel93 on 2014-03-23
Hello,
I just wanted to share my experience for these couple of days.
I bought a new Laptop: Acer Aspire V3-772G with a Clickpad 
I installed Ubuntu 12.04 and encountered one BIG problem! When I try to click with the actual button of the Clickpad my mouse
**GIGGLED on the Y coordinate(up and down)**, even when I didn't click, only press the EDGE of the Bottom of the Clickpad.
[Only in one place I found the answer]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2152733&p=12684269#post12684269"), regarding that I was watching the Wiki of my driver: [**Touchpad Synaptics**]("https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Touchpad_Synaptics") which came with Ubuntu 12.04.04

The thing that wasn't said in the Documentation was this:

Increase the values and your mouse will get better and even better it will be FIXED:
> [COLOR=#000000][FONT=Ubuntu Mono]xinput --set-prop "SynPS/2 Synaptics TouchPad" "Synaptics Noise Cancellation" 20 20[/FONT][/COLOR]

I see it like this: [COLOR=#000000][FONT=Ubuntu Mono]"Synaptics Noise Cancellation" x y[/FONT][/COLOR]
