### Post by jasay on 2005-10-31
To delete without moving to trash:
Applications > System Tools > Configuration Editor
/ > Applications > Nautilus > preferences  and check enable_delete on the right

Then when you right click a file in nautilus you can choose either delete or move to trash.

Not sure about the copying order as I do not have an mp3 player.:rolleyes:
