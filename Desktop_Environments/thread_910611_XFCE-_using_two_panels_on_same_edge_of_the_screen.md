### Post by Sorivenul on 2008-09-05
Got it, I think.  Add your second panel.  Then customize the settings to your size preferences.  Select "Freely Moveable" in the Panel Manager.  Add what you like to the panel, and move it where you want.  I just ran a rough test on my system and it seems to work.  However, maximized applications appear to maximize under the moveable panel.  Good luck!
