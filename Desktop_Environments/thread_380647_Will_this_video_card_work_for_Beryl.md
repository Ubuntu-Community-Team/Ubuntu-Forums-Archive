### Post by murlosad on 2007-03-09
I was using beryl on a fx5200 agp card, don't see why it wouldn't work with pci.

If you aren't going to use the onboard card, check your BIOS and see if it can be disabled.   That way it won't even have a chance to conflict with anything.
