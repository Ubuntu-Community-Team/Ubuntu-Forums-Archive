### Post by Hedgehog1 on 2011-02-02
> **HDave said:**
> Anybody have a good way to set the volume level to about 25% during a Gnome login?  I sometimes have it to 100% watching movies, then I shutdown.  The next day, the login sound is so loud it makes the windows (and my brain) rattle!

Well, I can tell you how to turn off the login sound:

Menu to: System >> Preferences >> Startup Applications

In the startup applications screen, uncheck 'GNOME login sound'.

This is not as fancy as a reduced volume at login, but it will stop your brain from rattling...  :KS
