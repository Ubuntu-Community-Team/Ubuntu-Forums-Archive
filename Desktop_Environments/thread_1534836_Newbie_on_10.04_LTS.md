### Post by pricen2 on 2010-07-20
Welcome.
If you mean the Standby Icon that's the dropdown menu, allowing you to shut down, restart, change user etc then:
 
Right Click the panel
Click Add To Panel
Select 'Indicator Applet Session'
Drag the 'Indicator Applet Session' onto the panel
