### Post by john_c on 2005-10-21
I have done several installations on older hardware (200 to 400 celeron) with 128 to 256 mb. memory.  I much prefer abiword and gnumeric on these as they are decently fast and still read and write word and excel files.  I use a "hoary server" install and then add xfce and other programs that I require.

I doubt that most people need more than is available with abiword - it is very full featured and I quite like it.  I haven't used gnumeric much but when I have it has certainly done anything that I asked of it.

On my faster hardware I use open office - it is better for business purposes perhaps  but pretty slow and big on the older systems.
