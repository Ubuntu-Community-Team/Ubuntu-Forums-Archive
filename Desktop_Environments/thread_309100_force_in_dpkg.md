### Post by bapoumba on 2006-11-29
Hi,
if you run **~ $ dpkg --force-help** in a terminal, you will get all the options. The syntax is **--force-<thing>**. And be aware of :
[QUOTE=dpkg Warning you should read about]WARNING - use of options marked [!] can seriously damage your installation.
Forcing options marked [*] are enabled by default.[/QUOTE]
