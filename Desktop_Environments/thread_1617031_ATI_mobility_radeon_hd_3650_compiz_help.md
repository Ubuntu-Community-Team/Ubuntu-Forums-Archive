### Post by ewz on 2010-11-08
Hi I had a similar problem with my HD3200 ATI mobility Card I ended up installing the drivers from the AMD site
I would suggest reading this first before installing the driver it really helped :-)  
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BinaryDriverHowto/ATI)

I never went for building the Ubuntu packages I just installed the driver 
and it works great, the only thing is you have to watch your updates, when ever you get a kernel update/patch, you will have to uninstall / reinstall the driver after the reboot which I must admit is a pain 

you can download the drivers form here
 [http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/Pages/index.aspx](http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/Pages/index.aspx)
