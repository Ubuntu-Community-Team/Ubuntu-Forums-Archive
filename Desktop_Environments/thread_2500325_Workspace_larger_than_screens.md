Ubuntu 24.04 LTS - Cinnamon 6.0.4 Kernel 6.8.0-40-generic

Three monitors which I've attempted to drag in various configurations after reading all sorts on-line. Two are 1080 and one is 4K. The formal workspace area is much larger than the monitor areas, as pictured.

As a result, the mouse can go way off the right or bottom of the actual screens, into dead workspace area, and get lost.

It's a minor annoyance but something I'd like to get to the bottom of, if possible, but some of the things I was reading on-line didn't inspire confidence so I thought I'd ask here.
