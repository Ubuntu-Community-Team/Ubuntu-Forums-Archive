### Post by nazish on 2006-04-09
there may be two reasons for this

1. the archive format is not recognized properly
2. the theme u downloaded hasnt been downloaded properly

in the 1st case just double click the archive and extract the icons to the location
[B][COLOR="Red"]
/home/(your user name)/.icons
[/COLOR][/B]
in my case i extract it to /home/naz/.icons/

in the 2nd case just dload again

open theme manager again and u will se the new icons
