### Post by stchman on 2007-04-20
Funny story.  I rip MP3s on my Ubuntu machine using the LAME encoder.  I have no problems playing the MP3s on my computers or car stereo that plays MP3s.  A buddy of mine wanted to listen to a couple of MP3 songs I have I said OK.  I brought them over on  flash drive and tried to listen to them on his XP Media Center PC.

The MP3s would not play.  Turns out that XP won't play VBR MP3s.  I was like WTF?!!!!!  I thought XP MCE is a multimedia playing beast.  All I have to say is WOW.  It turns out you need a VBR plugin for MP10.

Chalk up another one for the Linux camp.
