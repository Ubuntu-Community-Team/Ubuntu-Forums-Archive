### Post by kvonb on 2007-02-10
It's called "configuration editor" and it is already installed :).

Right click on the main menu icon and select "Edit Menus", browse to the "system" menu and enable it.

It should then appear on your menu under System.

### Post by kvonb on 2007-02-10
Sorry, I forgot to add the most important part :rolleyes:.

In config editor, browse to:

Apps->Nautilus->Desktop

All the cool stuff is in there.

Another useful one is:

Apps->Panel for when you want to play with themes and changing default icons, ie the main menu icon or rotating panel backgrounds etc'.
