Changing *distro* is not the way to go if the only problem you ahve is with a *desktop environment*... ;)

Same DE will be the same in all the different distros, and on the other hand all the distros provide you with many different desktop environments you can choose from.

Anyway, what comes to changing the fonts in Gnome 3, the easiest way is to install [gnome-tweak-tool]("apt:gnome-tweak-tool") (which by the way is an official tool from Gnome 3 developers themeselves, just not installed by default). One you have installed it, it will appear as "Advanced Settings" in your applicatiosn menus and allows you to twek some settings otherwise hidden in Gnome 3 desktop.
