### Post by jimny85 on 2008-04-25
Go to: Applications->settings->settings manager->window manager tweaks

Transparency options are on the compositor tab. Make sure you've got the binary drivers enabled for your video card (There's plenty of posts on this). I don't know what theme that is for the window borders, perhaps somebody else can recognise it.  I imagine one could come up with something similar playing around in the user interface settings which are also found in the settings manager.  

Have you had a browse around [xfce-look.org]("http://xfce-look.org")?
