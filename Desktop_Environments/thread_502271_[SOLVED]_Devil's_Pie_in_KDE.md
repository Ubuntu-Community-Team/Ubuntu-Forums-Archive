### Post by ldrn on 2007-07-16
Hello;

Unless you've changed this behavior: right click on any window's title bar and select "Configure Window Behavior." Select "Windows Specific Settings" from the area on the left.  You'll see a list of all the window rules you have made so far; a few are included by default.  Hit new to make a new one.

You can fill in those fields manually, like with Devil's Pie, or just click "Detect" then click on a window.  Use the tabs at the top to see different rules you can apply or change, and so on. 

To see your new window rule in action, click "OK" then "Apply". Hope this helps! I find Devil's Pie invaluable when I use gnome.
