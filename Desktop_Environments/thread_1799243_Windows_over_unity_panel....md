### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-07-07
When I press the super key dash opens and when I press super + D it displays the desktop  . The only thing I can think of is maybe  looking at the repeat key settings . If they are set very fast or slow the keys could be a little over sensitive . If you had a conflicting key binding in Compiz it would not work at all , so I would rule that out.
