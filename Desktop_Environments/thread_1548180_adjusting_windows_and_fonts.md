### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-08-08
You can change the size of the text on your display by going to System>Preferences>Appearance and going to the Fonts tab. Once you go to the fonts tab click on Details and lower the DPI (dots per inch). That will make the text appear smaller but I don't know how to make anything else appear smaller.
