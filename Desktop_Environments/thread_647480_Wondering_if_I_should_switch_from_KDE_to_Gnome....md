### Post by p_quarles on 2007-12-22
Other music players you might want to consider are Exaile! (an Amarok clone, more or less), Sonata (a GTK front-end to MPD), and good old Rhythmbox (though it's more iTunes-ish than Amarok, imo). But, I like Amarok best, so I can't offer much in the way of an argument for any of these. 

For video, Totem and VLC are both perfectly decent replacements for Kaffeine. 

Evolution's functionality is pretty similar to that of Kontact, though it was specifically designed to be a drop-in replacement for Outlook in an enterprise setting.
