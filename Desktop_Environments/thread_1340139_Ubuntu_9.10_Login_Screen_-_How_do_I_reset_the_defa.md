### Post by ibuclaw on 2009-11-28
There is no reset button - but you can configure the GDM to make it turn back to what it was to begin with.

See the "Changing GDM Background and Theme" section here: [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1333683](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1333683)

The theme is "HumanLogin" iirc.
