### Post by psb6m on 2007-05-23
You need to have the KDE libraries installed. Just use Synaptic to install the KDE app(s) you want, and the necessary libraries should be installed correctly. The app(s) will show up on your menus; you can place their icons on your taskbar; and they'll run just fine in Gnome.
