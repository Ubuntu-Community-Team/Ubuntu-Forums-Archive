### Post by _simon_ on 2006-08-28
Not sure if you're asking where to download them or where to apply them.

To apply (in Gnome):

Right click on the desktop.
Select "Change Desktop Background"
Any already installed will be displayed.
To add a background you have downloaded, simply choose "Add wallpaper"

Where to download:

Here's a few places to start with, but really it's a matter of googling:

[http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=170x171x172x173x174&PHPSESSID=0afd13071d8e6c6d2b5e533689496320](http://www.gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=170x171x172x173x174&PHPSESSID=0afd13071d8e6c6d2b5e533689496320)
