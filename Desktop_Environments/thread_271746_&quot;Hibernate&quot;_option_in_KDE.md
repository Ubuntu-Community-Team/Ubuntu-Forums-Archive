### Post by TheWizzard on 2006-10-05
KDE goes automatically to hybernate or suspend is you close the lid of your laptop. The settings are in "battery & power". 
You can also go directly to hybernate or suspend by right-clicking on the battery icon (KLaptop) in the system tray.
