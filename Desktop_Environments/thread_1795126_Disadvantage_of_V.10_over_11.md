### Post by coffeecat on 2011-07-02
You can run classic gnome in 11.04. At the login screen, enter your name but before you enter the password, choose the classic option at the bottom of the screen. Now enter your password and you will be taken to a desktop similar to that in 10.04.

Before you make a judgement as to whether you want to revert back to 10.04, try the Unity desktop occasionally (choose Ubuntu at login). There are many people, myself included, who disliked Unity at first but who now much prefer it. The first link in my sig may help you found your way around the interface.
