### Post by Velotix on 2006-11-11
I had a similar problem and just fixed it - KDE doesn't set a channel to represent the master volume so the slider is completely useless until you set it yourself.

Right Click > Select Master Channel > [your choice here]

I chose PCM; probably the best option.
