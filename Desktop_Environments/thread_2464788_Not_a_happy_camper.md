### Post by CatKiller on 2021-07-12
> **sparky3332 said:**
> Is it time to start looking at other platforms?
No, just different desktop environments. The Gnome devs developed a pathological aversion to users being able to customise things from Gnome 3 onwards. 

Cinnamon was created specifically to keep Gnome 2 going as soon as people saw what Gnome 3 was like, and there are several other projects that are based on Gnome but without the insanity. I used Cinnamon for a while, but then switched all my computers to KDE as soon as I'd tried it; if you're particularly after easy customisability then KDE is what I'd recommend. 

There are plenty of [Ubuntu flavours](https://ubuntu.com/download/flavours) available that come with a different desktop environment, or you can add a different one. The latter can get messy (particularly if you add more than one), so the former is preferable.
