### Post by denisesballs on 2006-08-03
Apparently gmailfs in Ubuntu has been broken forever. It seems there is a little known trick I came across a while ago. If you want to store up to 100mb easily for public use, you already have a gmail account and don't want to sign up for advertising heavy upload sites, go to [http://pages.google.com/](http://pages.google.com/) . Skip over the web part and on the right you can upload files with clean URLs. This is perfect for people who post images on high volume forums or want to make something available for download on the incredibly fast Google servers. Hope that helps!
