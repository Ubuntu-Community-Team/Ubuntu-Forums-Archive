### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-08-04
The buttons *are* on the individual windows.

If you mean the application menu, it's configurable to be located in the panel or in the app window:  System Settings->Appearance->Behavior.

---

### Post by mc4man on 2014-08-04
On maximized windows the buttons, (window title bar),  are merged into the top panel, nothing is going to change that. 
If as mentioned you enable the option to show menus in the windows title bar then the buttons, while still in the panel,  will always be visible.
