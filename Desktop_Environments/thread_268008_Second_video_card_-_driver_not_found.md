### Post by CatKiller on 2006-09-29
If you don't mind me asking, why not wait till you've got a second monitor before you put the second card in?

But your setup should work better than mine does - I've got a GeForce 2 and an Ati card in mine. If you're using the proprietary Nvidia Legacy drivers, you should have nvidia-settings that should sort a lot of this out for you. I had to make my xorg.conf by hand, having saved copies of it from dpkg-reconfigure with each of the cards in turn. In case it helps you figure out how to make yours work, this is the xorg.conf that I made. Don't forget that my specific values won't work for you, but the layout might help.
