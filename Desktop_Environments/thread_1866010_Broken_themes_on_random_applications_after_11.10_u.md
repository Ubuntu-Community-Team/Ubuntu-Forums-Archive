### Post by gsmanners on 2011-10-20
Nothing random about it. The "broken" themes are the gtk3 apps (so they fall back to using Raleigh or greybird). You just need a good gtk2 and gtk3 theme at this point.

I'm using a Clearlooks-style theme here on my desktop: [http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php?action=content&content=145210](http://gnome-look.org/content/show.php?action=content&content=145210)

There may be others that will look better, eventually.
