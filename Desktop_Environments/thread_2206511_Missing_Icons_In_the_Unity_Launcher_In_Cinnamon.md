### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-02-19
There's no 'Unity' in Cinnamon, and no 'launcher'.  Cinnamon has a menu, and a panel where icons that launch applications may, optionally, be added.

Are you running Mint's Cinnamon release, or did you install Cinnamon on Ubuntu?

A cause of missing icons can be use of an incomplete icon set. Try switching to different icons in Cinnamon settings.
