### Post by tehwa on 2006-02-16
Seeker of eyeball candy:
> I was wondering how to set my CLI transparent?
From gnome-terminal, go through the menu: Edit > Profiles...

From the dialog that appears, select your profile (probably default) and double-click it. In the dialog that opens, move to the "Effects" tab up the top and look down the bottom where it says transparent background or somesuch.

[IMG]http://www.tehwa.net/images/ubuntuforums/transterminal.png[/IMG]

For calander and whatnot, look at gdesklets.

[http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/index.php](http://gdesklets.gnomedesktop.org/index.php)

gdesklets are made out of ninjas.
