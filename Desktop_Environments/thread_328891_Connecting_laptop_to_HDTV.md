### Post by al108 on 2007-01-03
I wouldn't even bother with S-video cable as the quality of the signal usually very low. Go get that VGA cable or whatever your TV uses and set the resolution to what the manual for the TV suggests.

Your may be able to move picture around by adjusting something in xorg.conf, but like I said I wouldn't spend time with S-video unless you absolutely must.

Do report back to us how it went. What kind of resolution you got and what kind of video adapter/ TV you've got.
