### Post by kemra on 2009-02-22
I'm having a problem with the going to sleep too early in Xubuntu 8.10 on my laptop. It automatically kicks in after 5 minutes of no input rather than the 35 minutes I have it set at. 

I have gone into Applications>Settings>Power Management and the "On AC Power" tab is set to "Put display to sleep when inactive for:" 35 minutes. Now I am definitely using AC Power so it should be 35 minutes and not 5 like it is currently doing. Any suggestions?

---

### Post by kemra on 2009-02-22
Looks like it was the settings in the Applications>Settings>Screensaver part were to blame and I hadn't changed it yet, oops!
