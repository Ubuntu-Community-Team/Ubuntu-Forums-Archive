### Post by kerry_s on 2009-05-28
press **alt+f2**
type: **gksudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf**

in the screen section put:

```
Section "Screen"
	Identifier	"Default Screen"
	Monitor		"Configured Monitor"
	DefaultDepth	24
        SubSection "Display"
                Viewport   0 0
                Depth     24
		Modes     "1024x768"
        EndSubSection
EndSection

```

logout and back in or reboot
