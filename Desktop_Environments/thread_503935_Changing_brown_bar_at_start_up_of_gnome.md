### Post by yabbadabbadont on 2007-07-18
> **Nussbaum said:**
> I changed my background, window theme and GDM theme. Now I still have this brown bar right after logging in that loads a few icons and then disappears.Can anyone tell me how I can replace it or change it's color?

Install gnome-splashscreen-manager and then use it to change out the splash screeen that is used during gnome startup.  You'll probably want to head over to [www.gnome-look.org](www.gnome-look.org) and browse through the gnome splash screens in order to find a nice replacement.
