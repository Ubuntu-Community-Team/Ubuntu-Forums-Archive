### Post by matthew on 2006-05-16
From the Evolution menu select Edit->Preferences

Choose Mail Accounts and highlight the mail account you want to change, click Edit

Select the Receiving Options tab and under Message Storage see if the box labeled "leave messages on server" is checked. If so, deselect it and you should be done.
