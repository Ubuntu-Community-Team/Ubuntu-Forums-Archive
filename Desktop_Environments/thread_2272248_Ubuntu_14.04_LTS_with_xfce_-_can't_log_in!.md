### Post by OllieGab on 2015-04-05
I installed Ubuntu 14.04 with the default desktop environment (Unity) but decided I also wanted to have xfce available. I installed xfce4 (including the 'goodies') from the command line and got the 'feedback' that everything is okay.
However, when i come to log on - either after rebooting my machine or logging out from a Unity session - I don't get the option to chose. The only one available is Unity.
What's going on?
Cheers!

---

### Post by egeezer on 2015-04-05
The log-on bar on the left of the desktop has a dot over it with the Ubuntu icon.  Click on that and the choice of desktops will appear.
