### Post by veloce on 2007-05-18
In your "server layout" section, add the line:

option "Xinerama" "on"

### Post by krismatth3 on 2007-05-18
One thing to note: if you turn Xinerama on, you lose video acceleration (e.g. opengl/"Desktop Effects").

If you have an nVidia card, see "Twinview," but that leads to horrible ugliness with gnome panels stretch across both monitors.

EDIT: Technically you have opengl support on *one* screen - I just don't know how to specify whicih.
