I'm trying to create a bootable USB of Oneiric but I keep getting "Installation failed" error from Startup Disk Creator in Natty. Any ideas what is wrong?

- EDIT -

After viewing the log, I found out that the downloaded ISO had permissions of 640 instead of 644. I corrected that and now it works!
