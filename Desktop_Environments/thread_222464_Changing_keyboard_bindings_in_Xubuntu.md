### Post by harisund on 2006-07-25
Ok I think I found the solution for both of us. 

Head over to your Control Panel of XFCE (settings manager or something) and go to Window Manager and you will see a "keyboard shortcuts" tab. Create a custom configuration and edit it as you please.

I found this one by searching the XFCE forums.
