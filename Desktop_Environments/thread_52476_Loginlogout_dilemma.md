### Post by MrCheese on 2005-07-28
Can you switch to a console (ctrl-alt-f2 for instance) from the blank screen? 
Does alt-f2 give you anything?

If either of these work then your system isn't totally bombed. Log onto the terminal & type startx -- :1 to start a fresh X session.
