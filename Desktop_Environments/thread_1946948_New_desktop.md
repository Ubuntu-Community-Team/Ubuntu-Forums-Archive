### Post by JC Cheloven on 2012-03-25
Mmmm... random answers that come to mind:

- Nvidia graphics cards have good linux support. You can check/choose your particular one at the [nvidia site]("http://www.nvidia.com/object/unix.html").

- In these forums, you have [sticky threads]("http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=332") with compatible and not compatible hardware, that may be very useful for you.

- There are linux people that drop now and then useful videos about building a compatible pc. [Example]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bzBBkywRVbY&feature=digest_sun").

- Ubuntu has an official list of [certified hardware]("http://www.ubuntu.com/certification"). This may help to choose a comercial system instead of caring to build yourself.

Hope this helps
