### Post by bswilson on 2009-06-23
I am running Ubuntu 9.04 with Compiz enabled (with "Normal" setting enabled). I've noticed that when I take a screenshot (either with Ctrl-PrtSc or with the Applications --> Accessories --> Take Screenshot tool) I cannot force my system to also capture the Window border.  Is this a known issue?

---

### Post by enli on 2009-06-23
Hi bswilson, yes this is a known issue when taking screenshots and compiz is enabled. Last time I checked the developers are not going to fix this (I cant link the message but it was somewhere in newsgroup.)

The possible workaround is to disabled the compiz when you want to take screenshots or use program "Shutter". I use the latter one and it has many nice features. Check out [http://shutter-project.org/](http://shutter-project.org/)
