### Post by asmoore82 on 2010-03-21
The next time the panel is not correct,
right-click on one of the hide arrows and pick "Properties"
Temporarily uncheck "Show hide buttons" and hit "OK"

Now the hide buttons should become a small "grip" area for moving a floating panel.
Click on this area and drag the panel to the top edge, it should lock into place.
Then right-click this grip and "Properties" to turn your hide arrows back on.
