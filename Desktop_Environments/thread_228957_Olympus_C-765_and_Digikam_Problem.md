### Post by holmes on 2006-08-14
Fixed mine. 
Found this [http://www.teaser.fr/~hfiguiere/linux/digicam.html](http://www.teaser.fr/~hfiguiere/linux/digicam.html)

Camera comes up as C-350Z.  Seems to work ok.

If your camera mounts automatically when you first connect the device, Which mine does, just doesn't recognize in digikram. You can try this.
Let camera mount then go into digikam click on album ->import folders -> storage media - navigate to your camera folder. hope that does it for ya.:p
