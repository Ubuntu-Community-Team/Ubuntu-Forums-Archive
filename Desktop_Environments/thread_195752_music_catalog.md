### Post by barthel on 2006-06-13
There are a couple of Linux collection managers available, but I haven't found any free ones that really support what I want--at least not yet.

There is a proprietary one I've been testing that seems to do the trick. It's written in Java, so it's cross-platform and it claims to have been around for several years, so it has a bit more longevity. Plus, the data is stored in SQLlite database, for which there are free tools, so there's less chance of your data being lost to a proprietary format.

Check out [http://www.readerware.com/]("http://www.readerware.com/"). It's a suite that handles libraries of books, videos, and music.
