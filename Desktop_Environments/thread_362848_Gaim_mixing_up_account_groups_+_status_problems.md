### Post by louis_nichols on 2007-02-16
You just have to remove your friend's groups. This is the default and actually normal way that gaim runs. It records all friends and contacts that belong to any account ever used by a computer user. The ideal way would have been to give your friend an account on your computer and let him login by starting gaim from there.

As for the other errors, I think those are eligible to be filed as bugs to the developers of gaim. You can do this on the homepage : [http://gaim.sf.net](http://gaim.sf.net)
