### Post by chavo on 2006-08-05
Well it's kind of a vague question. If you are referring to the old gstreamer libs, then no, you do not need these libraries in kubuntu. libxine-extracodecs will cover all your needs as far as that goes. You'll also need the w32codecs to play windows media and some other stuff like quicktime, etc.

Of course this is spelled out plain as day on the restricted formats wiki page.
