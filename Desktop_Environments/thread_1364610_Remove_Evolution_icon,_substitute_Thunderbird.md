### Post by mprice on 2009-12-26
If your right click it will you give you a couple of options, make sure the one that says lock to panel is unchecked, then right click it again and it will give the option to remove from panel. Now just go to Application>Internet and click and hold thunderbird and then move it the panel location that you want. That is all there is to it.
