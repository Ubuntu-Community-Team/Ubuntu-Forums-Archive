### Post by Tibuda on 2010-09-30
An icon theme is just a folder with a bunch of images. You can just replace any image with another one. User themes are installed in /home/USERNAME/.icons (that's a hidden folder) and system themes are installed in /usr/share/icons.

Note: If you going to mess with a system theme, I suggest you to copy the theme from /usr/share/icons to /home/USERNAME/.icons.
