### Post by LastChildren on 2008-11-03
Are you using Gnome or KDE?  If you're using Gnome, it should be the fourth or fifth option down when you right click on the lower panel.  If all else fails, you can right click on the lower panel and choose "Add to Panel" and add the "Main Menu" applet which includes everything that was in your original launcher.
