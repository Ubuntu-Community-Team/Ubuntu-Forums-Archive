### Post by DeShark on 2006-06-12
Nautilus uses a seperate app by totem to produce its thumbnails (known aptly as totem-video-thumbnailer). From its [man page]("http://www.die.net/doc/linux/man/man1/totem-video-thumbnailer.1.html") it doesn't look too promising. However, since nautilus uses an external app, there may yet be hope for you... if you could find a replacement app that does what you want and somehow tell nautilus to use that, I suppose it would work.
