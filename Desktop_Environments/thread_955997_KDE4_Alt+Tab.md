### Post by boreal321 on 2010-11-03
Run /usr/bin/systemsettings (there's also a link under the k-menu app launcher)
Under "Look & Feel" choose "Window Behavior"
On the left, "Navigate Through Windows" should be the default choice otherwise click it
Under the "General" section drop down the "List windows" menu and choose "All Desktops"

The look and feel for switching windows using alt+tab is under
Look & Feel -> Desktop -> Desktop Effects -> General tab
