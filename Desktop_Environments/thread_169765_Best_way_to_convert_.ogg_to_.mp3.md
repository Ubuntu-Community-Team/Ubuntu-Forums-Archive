### Post by pspotts on 2006-05-04
Ditto on re-ripping vs. conversion. Vorbis's (ogg's) web site answers the reverse question (mp3 to ogg) by noting that going from one compressed format to another, where both clip data from the file, is a recipe for a sonic bummer.
