### Post by E-call on 2010-10-29
i had the same issue running ubuntu on my laptop. I have a dual graphics adapter one for 3d gaming and the other for enhanced battery life. i noticed that when ubuntu was running i would get around 45 minutes of battery life.
 
I fixed this problem by forcing the computer to use the weaker video card in the bios screen. after the change over i would get anywhere from 2 to 3 hours of battery life and a lot less heat.
 
it may not be your problem but it sounds like an issue with your video card.
