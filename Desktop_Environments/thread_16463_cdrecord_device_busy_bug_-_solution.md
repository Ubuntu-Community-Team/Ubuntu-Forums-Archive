### Post by MikeBee on 2005-02-21
hi all,

have been frustrated by a bug in cdrecord that reports a 'device busy' error on scanning atapi devices. this causes gnomebaker to fail as it can't pick up the cdr/dvdr drive

i installed the debian cdrecord package from here [http://ftp.de.debian.org/debian/pool/main/c/cdrtools/cdrecord_2.01+01a01-2_i386.deb](http://ftp.de.debian.org/debian/pool/main/c/cdrtools/cdrecord_2.01+01a01-2_i386.deb)

by 'sudo dpkg -i cdrecord....'

now it works fine.

also upgraded my gnomebaker to 0.3. see here - [http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/GnomeBaker](http://www.ubuntulinux.org/wiki/GnomeBaker)
