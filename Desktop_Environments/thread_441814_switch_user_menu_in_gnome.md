### Post by mocoloco on 2007-09-05
You may have already found the answers to these by now, but if not...

User switching in Ubuntu 7.10 is now as easy as in KDE, look at [this page ]("http://www.ubuntu.com/testing/tribe4") and scroll down to "Fast user switching".  So after you upgrade in October you'll have that.

To change to single-click, open any folder, and click "Edit -> Preferences".  On the "Behavio(u)r" tab the first option lets you change between single and double click.
