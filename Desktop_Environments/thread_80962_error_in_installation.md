### Post by LorenzoD on 2005-10-23
If you press ALT+F3 or F4 you will get various log output (I can't remember what output you get where). Could you take a look at what you get there that could indicate what the error is?

This has happened to me several times, and the most common problem has been I/O errors on the CD ROM drive. That often indicates a scratched disk. But there are a number of possible reasons why it could happen so check your log output.
