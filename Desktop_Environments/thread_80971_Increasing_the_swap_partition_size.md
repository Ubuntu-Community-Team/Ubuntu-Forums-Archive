### Post by bvc on 2005-10-23
It's a good general rule but as you increase your ram the less the swap will be used. If you will have 1gb ram you won't even really 'need' your swap, but keep it there just incase of memory leaks or whatever. I have 512mb ram and rarely use my 200mb swap. when it is used it's only 15% to 20%.
