### Post by Toz on 2013-07-04
It depends on which volume plugin you had showing.

Right-click the panel, select Panel->Panel Preferences and go to the Items tab.

If "Indicator Plugin" is not listed there, try adding it. If this doesn't bring back the volume plugin you were familiar with, try adding the "Audio Mixer" plugin.
