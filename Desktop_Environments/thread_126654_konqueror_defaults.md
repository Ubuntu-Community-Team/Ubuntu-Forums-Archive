### Post by MartinG on 2006-02-07
If you press F9 and open the Bookmarks tab in the sidebar, you'll find "Conquer your Desktop", which is the one you want. Press this and do any other setting up you want to get Konqueror the way you want it, then go to Settings and save the profile.
