### Post by aysiu on 2006-09-06
View those files in TagTool, and you'll see more info. Most players (AmaroK, Banshee, Rhythmbox, etc.) won't show you both sets of ID3 tags.

### Post by sam81 on 2007-10-23
found a fix to this problem:
click on the album name on the collection browser and set the option "Do not show under various artists"

see [http://rokymotion.pwsp.net/wiki/Handbook/Using_Amarok](http://rokymotion.pwsp.net/wiki/Handbook/Using_Amarok)
