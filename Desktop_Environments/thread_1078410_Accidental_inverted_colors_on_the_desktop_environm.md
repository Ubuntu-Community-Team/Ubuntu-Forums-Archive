### Post by witoff on 2009-03-02
Winkey+M or Winkey+N invert all, and window only colors respectively.

### Post by hictio on 2009-03-02
You have activated by mistake a [Compiz](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compiz) plugin aimed at accessibility, specifically, the [Negative Plugin](http://wiki.compiz-fusion.org/Plugins/Neg).
