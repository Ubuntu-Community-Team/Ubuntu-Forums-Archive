### Post by aeiah on 2006-02-17
wireless might be a problem with the live cd due to most wireless cards needing drivers to be installed. unfortunatley, wireless cards are a problem in general for ubuntu but i managed to get mine working thanks to some useful how to guides. you'll need to find out the type of wireless card you have, what chipset the card has, and whether linux drivers are available. searching this forum is a good place to start once you've found out what chipset your wireless card has. mine is a usb belkin and works a treat after following a How To from this forum.

the guides may not work for installing them with the live cd, as i dont believe the live cd has write access to the HDD, especially since its probably NTFS if you've still got xp on it.
