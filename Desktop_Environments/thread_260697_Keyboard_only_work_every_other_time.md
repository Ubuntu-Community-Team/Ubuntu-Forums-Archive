### Post by jonasmattsson on 2006-11-03
Ok, solved it. 
What i did was, while the computer was running i unplugged the ps/2 connector for the keyboard, and then reconnected it again (!). 
A few days later a friend of mine had some problem with her mouse "suddenly, the buttons stoped working, but the cursour was following her moves just fine."
The solution was the same as mine, unplug and replug the ps/2 connector.
I dont know why this worked, but when i thougt about it, this was how it stopped working in the first place, thats why i tried it.
