### Post by Krytarik on 2011-05-28
Make sure that the profile "Default" is selected in classic Gnome, "Preferences" at the lower left of CCSM. Also make sure that the Unity specific plugins are not enabled in that profile, particularly "Ubuntu Unity Plugin" and maybe "Grid".

Greetings.
