### Post by bdash on 2005-10-20
It is a very bad idea to use Wine with your Windows drivers. It will never work. What you should do is look for a Linux driver for your hardware - and hope it exists.

A good start is to search in a search engine "<name of your hardware> linux". I found this:
[http://www.linuxquestions.org/questions/history/366927](http://www.linuxquestions.org/questions/history/366927)

Which means that it should work.

If you feel lucky, you can even try to search "<name of your hardware> ubuntu". I found this:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=247434](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=247434)

This should answer to your question.
