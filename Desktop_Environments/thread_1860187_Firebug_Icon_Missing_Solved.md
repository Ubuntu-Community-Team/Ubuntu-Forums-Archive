### Post by ivikas on 2011-10-14
Hello all,

                      Yes, I was angry not being able to see the firebug icon on the bottom right side of Firefox. At this time I am using Firefox 7.0.1 and Firebug 1.8.3.When I installed the Firebug, the icon at bottom right was missing. What a crap! Type of feeling.


The solution is damn simple.

Steps

1.Right click on the top menu bar(not on tabs, anywhere on the empty area) and select customize.

2.’Customize Tool Bar’ window opens, Believe me you WON’T find the Firebug icon in it as you might expect.

3. The Firebug Icon is on the Top Right Panel near to the HOME Icon.

4. Click and hold on that Firebug Icon on the top right side and drag it and place it on the bottom panel where you love to have it on. That’s it. You’re done!!

Remember: In order for this to work, you need to keep open the ’Customize Tool Bar’ window.
