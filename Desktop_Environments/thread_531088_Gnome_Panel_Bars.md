### Post by njknight on 2007-08-21
I recently got asked for some new panel bar designs, so I thought I would share them, so help yourselves.  to use them save them to a folder in your home directory, then simply right-click on the Gnome Panel, select properties, then background, then select background Image, then navigate to where you saved the downloaded files - then click close.  you may have to restart (Ctrl Alt backspace) before the new gnome panel colours take effect.

### Post by WiFi Ed on 2007-10-08
I'm not sure how to restore a panel, but you can create a new one to take its place. Right-click on an existing panel and choose "New Panel". Left-click on the new panel and drag it to top or bottom as needed. Right-click on the new panel and choose "Add to Panel..." and from the Add to Panel menu window choose the stuff you want it to have (menus, apps, shut down button, clock, etc.)
