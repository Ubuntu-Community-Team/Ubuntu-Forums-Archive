### Post by qwerty2009 on 2009-11-04
hi all ):P i usually have my desktop just plain with Cairo dock(Still Great)

but whilst i was browsing package manager i decided in installing "The Ubuntu Netbook Remix system" ( which only took 3 seconds to download using a rubbish connection)

i wasn't expecting much or even expecting it to function properly but to my surprise i restarted my desktop and was presented with a great looking gnome desktop and a new Firefox plug-in which allows me to add web pages to my favorite tab :) 

the first thing to do after testing out your new desktop is 

[LIST]
[*]remove the bottom panel
[*]remove the "Main Menu" or "Menu bar" applets from the top panel and replace it with your new "Go Home" applet
[/LIST]

[LIST]
[*]remove "Windows List" or "Windows Selector" applets from the panel and replace it with your new "Windows Picker" applet
[/LIST]
any way this what my desktop looks like now :D (great improvement to the default ubuntu desktop)
