### Post by ajgreeny on 2006-02-08
If you install KDE you can use the KDE control centre (kcontrol).  This is very easy to use and may be exactly what you are looking for.
You may also be able to simply download this app without KDE but I am not sure about that as I prefer KDE anyway and use it all the time instead of Gnome.  I still have the Gnome Desktop installed just in case something goes wrong with KDE, on an update, perhaps, but I really don't use it very much at all.

### Post by kaamos on 2006-02-08
Open a terminal and type
```
gnome-control-center
```
This could be what you are looking for.
