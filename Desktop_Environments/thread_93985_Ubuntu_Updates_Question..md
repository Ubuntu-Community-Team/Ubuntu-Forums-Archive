### Post by Pablo_Escobar on 2005-11-23
IMHO Universe and Multiverse are very safe to get updates from. (I don't know the status of Ubuntus team support for them, but I believe that they are supported).
Backports on the other hand are not 100% tested, so that is just Your decision to make.
Unofficial repos are a tough call, I personally use a couple of them (like wine, kadu, etc) - it's also Your call if You want to use them. It's good to search and read some info if these repos are harmfull in some way.
