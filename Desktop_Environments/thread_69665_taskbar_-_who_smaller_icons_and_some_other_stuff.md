### Post by mlomker on 2005-09-27
Most of the things that you're describing cannot be changed as KDE was designed to work that way.  There is an add-on program called [superkaramba]("http://www.kde-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=38") that allows you to do a number of fancy things with your desktop, but there isn't a lot that you can do with the main bar and menus.

There are other desktop managers available, such as [XFCE]("http://www.xfce.org/") and [icewm]("http://www.icewm.org/").
