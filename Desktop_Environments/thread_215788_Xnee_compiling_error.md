### Post by jordilin on 2006-07-14
Xnee resides in the universe repository. So it would be better you add the lines:
deb [http://es.archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/](http://es.archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/) dapper universe multiverse
deb-src [http://es.archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/](http://es.archive.ubuntu.com/ubuntu/) dapper universe multiverse
to your /etc/apt/sources.list and then an apt-get update. After this, you will be able to see the package xnee in synaptic.
