### Post by fixgyachi on 2011-07-05
Run gyachi from terminal
Enable packet debugging
Try joining room from gyachi, click ok for the error
Go back to terminal, open the room link show in there in browser and enter captcha.
On successful captcha, gyachi will take to your room!!
