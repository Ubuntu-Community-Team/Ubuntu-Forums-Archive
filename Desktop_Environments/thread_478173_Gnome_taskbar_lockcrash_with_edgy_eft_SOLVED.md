### Post by otchie1 on 2007-06-19
SYMPTOMS

After 'a while' task bar becomes unaccessible. Items minimised can't be recovered. None of the other shortcuts or buttons on the menu bar work either. Whole bar will be seen to pulsate every second or so.

SOLUTION

Delete task bar from shared lower panel.
Make new panel at top of screen and add taskbar to it.
(I make mine auto hide)
