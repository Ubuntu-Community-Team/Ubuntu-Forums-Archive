My first experience with KDE is different from you, i found that font rendering in KDE is horrible and doesn't look nice. I love font rendering in Gnome it's look good in my eyes. But after do some tweaking etc i can got my font rendering in KDE working.

Talking about UNE/WAYLAND/Gnome3/KDE 4.6 is return to your hardware configuration also. If you realize that KDE Effect is much more hardware friendly. It's mean that for unsupported graphic card like SIS, still can enjoy the effect like transparency etc. It can't work if you use Gnome / Compiz. 

For me, i prefer XFCE & KDE. Since they both work well with my Graphic Card. Gnome is too common and it's cool if you look's a bit different from other:)
