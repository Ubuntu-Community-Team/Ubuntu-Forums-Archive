### Post by nilarimogard on 2011-02-20
To get a launcher only on the top dock, add a "Simple launcher" applet, then drag and drop some apps onto the dock.

### Post by Copper Bezel on 2011-02-20
So, essentially, you want AWN to not act like a dock? = ) But yeah, if you use Simple Launcher for your launchers and then remove the launchers from the taskbar applet, that seems to accomplish what you want.
