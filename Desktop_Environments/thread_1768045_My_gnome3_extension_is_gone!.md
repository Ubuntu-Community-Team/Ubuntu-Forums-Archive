### Post by maverick280857 on 2011-05-27
Gnome-shell was updated to v3.0.2 a few hours ago, which requires a modification in metadata.json for each extension.

Specifically, have a look at [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=10865905&postcount=84](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=10865905&postcount=84).

As a general rule, since gnome-shell/gnome-3 are in development, I think it would be a good idea to be careful when updating packages, and make notes.
