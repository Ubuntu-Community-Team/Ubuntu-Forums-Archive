### Post by PurposeOfReason on 2008-01-06
When you have that problem, extract the theme and put it into ~/.themes. Say you download the Nova them (I know it best because I use it), you'll extract it and get the folders:
Nova-blue
Nova-lime
Nova-gold
Nova.emerald

and probably a few others. You would drag the first three to the ~/.themes folder and put them there, leave the emerald alone. Then go to edit themes and choose customize. Go through the options and choose the right components. Then, if you have an emerald theme, open up emerald, click import, and choose just the .emerald theme.
