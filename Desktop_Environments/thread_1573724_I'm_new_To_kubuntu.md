### Post by 3Miro on 2010-09-13
At the bottom right corner of the panel, there is the "KDE Cashew", if you click on it, you will activate settings for the panel. If the "Cashew" doesn't appear, there should be one on the top right corner, click on that one and select "unlock widgets".

Once you enter the panel settings, you can change a lot of things. You can access preferences for different "widgets" like the menu, access folder, taskbar and so on. You will probably have to add new widget "taskbar". Check out the otherones as well, there are even more widgets in the repositories.
