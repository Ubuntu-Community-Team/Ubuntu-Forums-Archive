### Post by matthew on 2005-08-18
Okay...after finally understanding what you wanted and thinking about it, I like the idea. So, I made a small png that you can use and will attach it here. This is a transparent 16x16 pixel png I just made in Gimp that has a 1 pixel black dot in the center. Save it to your hard drive and then, using the method in my previous post, tell the starterbar desklet to use it as the background. Now you have a transparent gDesklets starterbar background!!

EDIT: the image really is there in the box. it is TINY and transparent so you can't see it or find it easily. Hold your right mouse button down and select the whole box and you will find it.
