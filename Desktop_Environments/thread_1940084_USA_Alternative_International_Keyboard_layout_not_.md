### Post by ubuntu27 on 2012-03-13
Choose "US International with Dead keys" OR "US International with [AltGR]("https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AltGr_key") Dead Keys)

and you can have accents or special characters by hitting "right ALT" + letter.

For example for ñ =     [right] Alt+n


For more info: [http://spanish.about.com/od/writtenspanish/a/dia_ubuntu.htm](http://spanish.about.com/od/writtenspanish/a/dia_ubuntu.htm)

Kubuntu (KDE): [http://dry.sailingissues.com/us-international-keyboard-layout.html](http://dry.sailingissues.com/us-international-keyboard-layout.html)
