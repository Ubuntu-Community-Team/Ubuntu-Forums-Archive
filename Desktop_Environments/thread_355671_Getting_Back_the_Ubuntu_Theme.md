### Post by mcduck on 2007-02-07
There's a nice app called Start-up Manager that gives you easy way to configure boot splashes and such. Try searching the forum for it.

And for more workspaces, just right-click the  workspace switcher applet, select 'Preferences' and there you can change the amount of workspaces to your liking.
