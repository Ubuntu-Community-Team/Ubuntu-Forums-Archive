### Post by Xian on 2005-04-07
The only updates that would require a reboot would be major system changes like kernel revisions, Xorg releases and such. Normally, you will not need to reboot on a normal basis as you go about upgrading your system.

---

### Post by jdong on 2005-04-07
With updates, it's generally a good idea to reload the programs involved, so that you actually use the updated verson. This usually means logging out & back in to apply everything but kernel updates.

For kernel updates, you must reboot.
