### Post by wbeck85 on 2005-03-21
Make sure your fonts are antialiased. That helps. Also, get rid of that horrendous helvetica font (shudders). THe easist way to do this is to go to System(right next to the kicker)>Settings>Appearance and Themes>Fonts You can then choose your fonts and check the AA option. THen configure AA for fonts and check [sub-pixel hinting](http://grc.com/ctwhat.htm) if you have a LCD monitor.
You can also open your xorg.conf file and make sure that 100dpi fonts are above the 75dpi fonts and that type1 fonts is at the bottom of your fonts list. Restart X
Now it looks much prettier.
However, there are some fonts that I cannot figure out how to change, and they are driving me crazy. These are the menu/toolbar of Firefox. For some reason, these fonts are really dinky and it is annoying.

IF anyone knows how to fix them, do tell!
