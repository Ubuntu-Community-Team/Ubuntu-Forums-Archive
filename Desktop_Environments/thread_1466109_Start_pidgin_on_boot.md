### Post by claracc on 2010-05-01
Well, I found the answer myself. It is very easy.

All I have to do is to add the running of /usr/bin/pidgin & in system-->Preferences--> Apps at the begining and add the above line to run pidgin on boot.

What I don't understand is why I lost the running of pidgin on boot when I updated to kernel 2.6.31-21.
