### Post by ciscosurfer on 2006-08-03
Those are hex codes that are mapped to reflect the key or key-combo to hit for the action to take place.  

It's easy to change them though.  Simply click on the item you'd like to change, and enter a new key-combo. :grin:

You can just as easily use your special multimedia keys if you've got them...I map my mail to my multimedia-mail-looking-key; search to my multimedia-search-looking-key; web browser to my multimedia-browser/home-looking-key, etc.
