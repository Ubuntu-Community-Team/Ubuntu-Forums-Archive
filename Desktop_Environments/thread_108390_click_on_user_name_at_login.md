### Post by Dr. Nick on 2005-12-25
It depends on your login theme, wether or not it uses face browser. Go to System - Administration - Login Window and look for the theme called Happy Gnome with Browser. May have to install the themes through apt get, look for gdm something I think, not sure the exact name.

Just realize that the face browser is less secure than needing to know the login name and the password, if the security and privacy is needed the user list is not recomended
