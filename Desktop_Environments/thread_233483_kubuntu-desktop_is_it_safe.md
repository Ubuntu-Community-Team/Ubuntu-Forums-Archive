### Post by maniacmusician on 2006-08-10
yes it sounds alright....though I don't use aptitude, i use apt-get instead. but aptitude shouldn't be troublesome or anything. BUT, once you start installing it, you might wanna stick around for a couple of minutes or so; sometimes while it's installing, a message comes up asking you to do something like approve a package, enter a password, choose an option, and it will just stay like that until you answer. I've often started a download like that, gone away for a couple of hours, and then came back to find it stuck at a question. so keep an eye out for that.
