### Post by crdlb on 2011-10-29
In a quick search, the only thing I'm seeing is xfce4-panel + xfce4-wmdock-plugin (it can be used outside of xfce). Make sure you resize the panel to at least 64 pixels before starting any dockapps. Some of the dockapps I tested failed to be swallowed by the applet, but most worked.
