Hi,

there are some threads on UbuntuForums which are related to my problem but are closed and I couldn't replay and this is why I am opening a new thread.

So, after I upgraded to Intrepid I am getting a lot of aborts in Firefox and Thunderbird. Many of you (including me) would thing this is a video driver problem, flash problem, compiz problem... After I made a lot of research (googling) I found out that it is Samba problem in library libnss_wins. You can found out more in this bug report: [https://bugzilla.samba.org/show_bug.cgi?id=5904](https://bugzilla.samba.org/show_bug.cgi?id=5904) and this one [https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/flashplugin-nonfree/+bug/282287](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/flashplugin-nonfree/+bug/282287)

I hope that update for libnss_wins will come soon and my firefox and thunderbird will stop crashing.
