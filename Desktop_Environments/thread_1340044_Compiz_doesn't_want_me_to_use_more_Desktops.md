### Post by etnlIcarus on 2009-11-28
I believe you're referring to this:

[img]http://img137.imageshack.us/img137/5297/screenshotis.png[/img]

Compiz handles *workspaces* in a somewhat different manner than Metacity, Xfwm or Kwin. If you use either of the first two sliders, you can easily change your number and layout of workspaces. Even if the bottom slider is active, it won't have the desired result, as workspaces proper don't play nice with compiz's functionality.
