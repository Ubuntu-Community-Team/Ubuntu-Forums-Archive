### Post by aysiu on 2006-01-05
I may be wrong, but I believe KOffice is a metapackage.
Unlike OpenOffice, which is one application consisting of multiple components, KOffice is just an idea, a collection of separate applications (KWord, KSpread, etc.).

Try Alt-F2 ```
kword
```
