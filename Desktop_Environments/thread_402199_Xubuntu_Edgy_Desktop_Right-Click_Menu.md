### Post by jjalocha on 2007-04-05
One little feature that I really like about Xfce is the 'Xfce Menu' that pops up with a right-click on the desktop. This has been available in Xubuntu until Dapper. For some reason, in Xubuntu Edgy this feature has been somehow crippled. Instead of a full-blown menu with entries for different software categories (accessories, games, graphics, etc.), now we only have the ability to create different items (launcher, link, etc.), and access to the desktop settings, which is quite limited for my daily needs.

I have been trying to get the original functionality back without success. I have even compared an old copy of the .config folder under Edgy with the current one, but I'm clueless. (At least, I managed to extended the Xfce Menu in order to include again top-level links to terminal, thunar and firefox.)

Anyone knows what to do?

### Post by karloz on 2007-04-05
Choose "Desktop Settings" in the right click menu.  Click on the "Behavior" Tab, and check the box "Show Desktop Menu in Right-Click"... Pay me my $5.00 (US), and agree that it was a lot easier than you thought.
