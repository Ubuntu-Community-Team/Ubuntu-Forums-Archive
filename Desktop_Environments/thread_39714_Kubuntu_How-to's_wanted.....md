### Post by az on 2005-06-07
That is one of the problems with the doc team.  There are so many sources of information.  The unofficial guide is great, but the doc team has problems with it.
The forums offer a lot, but it would take a full-time staff person to read every post every day.  That is why the wiki is useful.  Many people can contribute at the same time.
The doc team can also officially support the wiki-delta by correcting inaccuracies and overseeing it.

It is up to the doc team to use the forum-wiki delta information once it is established.  What format they chose is up to them.

As for the ubuntuguide, I would like to think that future versions of the ubuntuguide would include information from the wiki-delta.  As it is, much information is taken from the forums - even the dedicated, sticky ubuntuguide thread.  However, the drawbacks to it being a one-man-show is that it will not be as complete as something anyone can add to.
