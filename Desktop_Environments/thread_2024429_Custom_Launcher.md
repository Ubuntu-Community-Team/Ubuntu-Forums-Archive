### Post by mayagrafix on 2012-07-13
How do I customice the Launcher? 

For instance, can I remove (but not un-install) LibreOffice Calc and LibreOffice Impress icons which are apps I seldom use from the launcher bar, or add an icon to the launcher bar for app I use often, such as Rhytembox?  

To be clear, the Launcher is the pop up bar that appears when you push the mouse to the left of the screen.

---

### Post by drs305 on 2012-07-13
You can right click and "Unlock from Launcher" to remove any of the icons you don't want. In similar fashion, once you open an app you can right click on the icon and select "Lock to Launcher".

---

### Post by diesch on 2012-07-13
In Unity you  can also drag icons from the Dash (the window you get when pressing the Super key) and drop them on the launcher.
