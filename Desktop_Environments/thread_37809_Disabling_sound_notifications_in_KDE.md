### Post by Xian on 2005-05-28
From the System Notifications module goto the 'Event Source' drop-down menu, and choose 'KDE System Notifications' & the 'KDE Window Manager'. Those are the two event sources that you need to disable associated sounds. Or you can just choose to turn off all the sounds in those respective source windows with the option provided.
