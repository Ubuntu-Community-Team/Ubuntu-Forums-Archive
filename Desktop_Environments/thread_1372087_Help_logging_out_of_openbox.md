### Post by mcduck on 2010-01-04
That's Openbox for you (nothing but your programs and a right-click menu). :)


Anyway, the Openbox menu should by default have options to log out/exit Openbox. That should take you back to login screen. If for some strange reason you don't have such option in the menu then I remember the command for exiting Openbox being "openbox --exit".
