### Post by Xian on 2005-07-31
Your kernel and package archs don't need to match, and in most cases with Linux distros this will generally be the case. There are some which optimize the entire system, such as Arch Linux and others, but the specific kernel packages in Ubuntu are for the most part to help make the most of your hardware specs, enabling you to utilize all of the RAM available, and other things like for example if you ran dual processors.
