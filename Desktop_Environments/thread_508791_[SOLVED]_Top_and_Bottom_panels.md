### Post by tgm4883 on 2007-07-24
Why it happens, not sure.

How to fix it, right click on the icon, uncheck lock to panel, then right click the icon and select move.  Once you move it to the right place, you should re lock it to panel

You may have to unlock other icons to get it where you want it to go

### Post by kevinlyfellow on 2007-07-24
Right click on the icons and make sure that the check mark for "lock to panel" is off.  Then choose "move" and drag it around until its in the correct place.  If it can't move past another object on the panel, click on the impenetrable object and make sure that "Lock to panel" is not enabled on it.
