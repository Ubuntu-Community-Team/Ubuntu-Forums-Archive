### Post by heimo on 2005-07-29
Have you tried System->Preferences->Screen Resolution?

If the resolution is not listed there, you probably need to reconfigure X or tweak xorg.conf manually (/etc/X11/xorg.conf).
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=21984]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=21984")


EDIT: And quickest way to change screen resolution when everything is configured properly, is to hit ctrl+alt++ (plus sign or minus sign on numpad).
