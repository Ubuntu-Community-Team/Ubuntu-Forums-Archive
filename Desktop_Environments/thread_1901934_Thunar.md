### Post by 3Miro on 2011-12-29
Take a folder and drag it into the shortcut space to create a new shortcut. Right-click -> "remove shortcut" will remove the existing shortcut. You can also drag the existing shortcuts up and down to sort them.

I have shortcuts plenty of partitions on separate drives, however, if you make a shortcut to an external HDD that isn't always mounted, then the shortcut may not work.
