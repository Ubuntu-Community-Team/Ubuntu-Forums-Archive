### Post by anil_robo on 2005-12-01
I have SBC Yahoo DSL connection, and I faced the same problrm. I called them up, and they said they block port 25 (SMTP port) for all email services except their own! When asked why, they said it's a "security feature". I requested them to remove this blockage, and now I can send emails from evolution like charm! :)

Call SBC and tell them to unblock your port 25, and you'll be done!
