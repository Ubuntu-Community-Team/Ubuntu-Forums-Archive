### Post by blur xc on 2009-09-09
you'd probably find the scale plugin way more functional, and you can program most of these features in compiz to activate by clicking in either the corners or edges of your screen.  and have them do different things depending on where you click.

For instance, my scale plugin- right click the bottom right corner of the screen to show all active widows on the current workspace, right-click the right edge of my screen to show all active windows on all workspaces, and the top right corner to show all windows that are the open documents in the currently active task.
