### Post by Carbonfish on 2006-08-24
I know that this thread is a couple of weeks old, but if you want to remove the games from your machine, just go into Synaptic and remove any (I think this will work with *any*) game and Synaptic should tell you that the app. is associated with other programs, and are you sure you want to remove all of them. If you say yes, all of your games and the link in the "applications" drop-down will be gone.

At least I think that's what I did. It was a couple of weeks ago.
