### Post by Aleksandersen on 2007-01-29
Most probably you are being down prioritised by the network for not sharing enough files yourself. Make sure you share at least fifty files and at lest 250 MB of data. By doing so no default configured client will down prioritise your connection.

And 101 sources sounds to me like a virus or junk result. Make sure to scan the file for virus and malicious software before opening the file.
