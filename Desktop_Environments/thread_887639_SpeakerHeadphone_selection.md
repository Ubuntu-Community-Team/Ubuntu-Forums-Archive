### Post by sayakb on 2008-08-12
Hello and welcome to the forums! :)
Open Totem (Applications->Sound and Video->Movie Player). Goto Edit->Preferences->Audio and change the *Audio output type.* Also, double click on the volume control icon to open Alsamixer. Goto File->Change Device and select PulseAudio.
