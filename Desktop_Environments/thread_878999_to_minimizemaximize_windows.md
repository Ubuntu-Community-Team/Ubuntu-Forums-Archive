### Post by mcduck on 2008-08-04
That sounds like you might have accidentally removed the Window List applet from your bottom panel..

Right-click on any empty space in your panel, select "Add to Panel", find the Window List-applet and drag & drop it into the panel.

You can move it around either by dragging with middle mouse button (the wheel) or by right.clicking and selecting "Move" from the menu.
