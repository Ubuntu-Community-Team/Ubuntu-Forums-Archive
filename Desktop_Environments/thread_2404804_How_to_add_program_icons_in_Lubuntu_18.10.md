### Post by Dennis N on 2018-10-29
Do you mean add program icon to the panel as a launcher? I just dragged the icons from the main menu down to the panel and it jumped into place at the right end of the existing launchers. You can also right-click on icons there to move them left or right.

You can also drag icons from the menu to the desktop to serve as launchers.
