### Post by mr_ed on 2004-12-03
Yes, the current state of Linux printing sucks.  :(

When you say etc, what does that include?  Every app is responsible for its own printing stuff.

Here's what I ended up doing to get my parents' printer (HP PSC 2175) to print:
Turned it off.
Turned it back on.
Went to Computer->System Configuration->Printing
Changed status of printer from Paused to Ready.

It's strange, because it would say 0 jobs until I actually un-paused it.  Then it said "Oh, I'm ready, and I have 3 jobs to do."  :confused:
