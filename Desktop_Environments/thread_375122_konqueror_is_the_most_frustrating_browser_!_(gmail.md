### Post by iamhugeinjapan on 2007-03-03
Being standards compliant doesn't mean sites will send you standards compliant code. *Every* browser on *every* OS has issues displaying certain content correctly. The developers can only do so much, the burden often needs to be on the website creators to use good code.

If you want to talk to your Gmail friend, why not use Kopete or Gaim to do it instead? They support the jabber protocol that GTalk uses.
