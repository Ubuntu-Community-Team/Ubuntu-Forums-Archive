### Post by Dennis N on 2015-10-19
They are provided by the package **gtk3-engines-xfce**.

---

### Post by perknh on 2015-10-20
Thank you, Dennis N. 

Your suggestion pointed me in the right direction.  I guess these themes must be older than the gtk3 engines.  I found them by dropping one version down to **gtk2-engines-xfce**.  Problem solved!

Thank you,

perknh
