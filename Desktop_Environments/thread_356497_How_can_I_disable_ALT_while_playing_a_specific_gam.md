### Post by Dataforce on 2008-03-28
If you are using KDE (3):

[list]
[*] right click on the title bar
[*] go to advanced > special window settings
[*] click OK at the dialog box (if it appears)
[*] In the 1st tab ("window") click on the detect button at the bottom, and click on the wow window
[*] Make sure "Use whole window class" is selected, and tick "Also match window title" then click ok
[*] In the last tab ("workarrounds") tick "block global shortcuts" select "Force" from the drop down, and then tick the other check box
[*] Click ok
[*]  Use alt to your hearts content.
[/list]
