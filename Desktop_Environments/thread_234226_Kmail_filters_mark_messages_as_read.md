### Post by steevc on 2006-08-11
I've got a couple of filters set up for my IMAP accounts. The important ones are those using Bogofilter to move the spam out of the in box. I have another to put mailing list messages in a folder.

The problem I have is that new messages that stay in the in box are marked as read so it's difficult to see when I have something new. If I stop the filters looking at incoming messages then they will stay as unread.

Any ideas?

I have Kmail 1.9.1 on KDE 3.5.2

---

### Post by dutchman25 on 2006-09-12
In Kmail, under "Tools", go to Anti-Spam Wizard and after the first screen in which you choose your filters, there is a box to that asks if you want to keep marked messages as unread or not.  Check that box.
