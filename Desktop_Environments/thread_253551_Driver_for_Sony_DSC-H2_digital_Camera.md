### Post by lakelovers on 2006-09-08
Solved my problem by using "PicBridge Device" on the camera. That loaded the photos into digiKam nicely. I tried the other modes available on the camera and only the "PicBridge" works. I haven't tried the memory stick directly into the computer because I don't have an adapter for the stick.
