### Post by Bölvaður on 2007-12-09
> **carlos1992 said:**
> i recomend you a desktop cuz a kid can easily damage a laptop

If you are going to buy a desktop computer the best thing to do is going to local computer shop and ask for blank (no OS installed) computer for the amount of money you are willing to spend. I would say you don't need anything more than Duo E4400 and 1GigB of RAM.

And about special keyboard for the children. I do not think I ever had much problems with keyboards at their age. A standard one should suit fine.

About the cds. If you have problems running them with wine you can always install and run Windows on a virtual machine. Check [Virtualbox.]("http://www.virtualbox.org/")
