### Post by Liverbones on 2010-12-01
> **Frogs Hair said:**
> You would have to remove your notification applets from the panel.

That's not quite true. You can keep your notification and indicator applets if you like them. The applet that's running on your top panel is the Window List applet, which you will need to remove. You should be able to right-click in the empty space directly to the left of the applet and select Remove From Panel to get rid of it. If you ever want to add it back, simply right-click on any empty space on your panel and click Add To Panel, then select the Window List applet.

Hope that helps. :)
