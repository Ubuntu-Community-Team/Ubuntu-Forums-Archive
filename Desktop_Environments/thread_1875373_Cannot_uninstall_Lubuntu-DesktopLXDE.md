### Post by KBD47 on 2011-11-04
This web site:
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/)
 gives the exact instructions for installing and uninstalling other desktops to Ubuntu.
 I followed the instructions on 11.04 to add, then remove Xubuntu desktop. On 11.10 I followed their instructions to add Gnomes shell/fallback and the instructions are good.
  I added plain LXDE using software center, I think you can do the same in Synaptic Package Manager. Then you just log out of Unity and log in to LXDE.
KBD47
