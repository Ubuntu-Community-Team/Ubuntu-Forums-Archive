It doesn't really depend on your processor or amount of available RAM, only on your graphics card.

And Compiz isn't a desktop environment. It's a window manager, usable in any desktop environment you like. Still any other window manager with similar features would also have similar requirements, meaning a decent graphics card with good drivers.
