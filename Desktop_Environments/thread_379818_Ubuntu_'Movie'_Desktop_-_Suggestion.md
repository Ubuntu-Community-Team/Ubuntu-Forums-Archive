**_Ubuntu Movie Desktop_ - Suggestion**

The idea is to provide the user with capability to customize their desktop background into a typical video playing as if it were wallpaper. In the events of advancing the desktop, optionally acquiring sound from the movie with a "on" and "off" feature. Like wallpaper, the movie background can stretch, center, and tile to fit user's preference.

Those who do not wish to have their computer cycles eaten up, can optionally turn off said movie background.

This is just an effort to "advance" backgrounds to the next level of typical media interaction. If this is ever to catch on, Ubuntu would be the first to implement such a "eye" appealing approach to desktop environments, even before Microsoft. ;)
