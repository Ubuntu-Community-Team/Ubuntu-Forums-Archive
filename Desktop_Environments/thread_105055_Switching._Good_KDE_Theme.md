### Post by f1dave on 2005-12-20
Yeah, that'll happen with kde unfortunately...

Styles- How kde looks. Buttons, status bars, etc.
Color Schemes- How things are coloured. Title bar colour, etc.
Window Decorations- The style used on your window borders.
Background- Desktop background.
Icons- Desktop and system icons.
Themes- KDE themes are combinations of all of the above in a single tarfile.
Karamba- desktop widgets. Weather, system status, etc.

Hope this helps you somewhat.
