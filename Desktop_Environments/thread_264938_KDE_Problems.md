### Post by aysiu on 2006-09-25
```
mv ~/.kde ~/.kde.old
mv ~/.nautilus ~/.nautilus.old
mv ~/.metacity ~/.metacity.old
mv ~/.icons ~/.icons.old
mv ~/.themes ~/.themes.old
mv ~/.gnome ~/.gnome.old
mv ~/.gnome2 ~/.gnome2.old
mv ~/.gconf ~/.gconf.old
mv ~/.gconfd ~/.gconfd.old
``` Then log out and log back in again.
