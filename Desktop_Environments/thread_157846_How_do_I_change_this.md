### Post by jr.gotti on 2006-05-18
iono if you figured this out yet...but to do it...

open the configuration editor

**gconf-editor**

then go to 

**apps>panel>objects>object_0**

tick the box for **use_custom_icon**, and enter the path to the icon in [B]custom_icon

[/B]hope i helped!!
