### Post by N8MAN1068 on 2005-10-14
I think the ACPI functions on 5.10 are much better than 5.04.
I run Breezy on a Dell Latitude C840, and it 'wakes up' so much smoother.
Other than that, I can't so i've noticed a major improvement in speed.

If you already have your video/sound/modem and everything else tweaked the way you want in Hoary, and don't feel like spending a day or two redoing all of that, then I wouldn't suggest an upgrade.

### Post by kernelsandirs on 2005-10-14
I have a Dell C600 and 5.10 works great, and it truly is running much faster than 5.04, but the mouse was really jumpy until I killed powernowd  if you are having jumpy, jittery, irratic mouse movement try killing powernowd, if that fixes the issue remove the S20powernowd file from /etc/rc2.d
