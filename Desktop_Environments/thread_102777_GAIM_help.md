### Post by anil_robo on 2005-12-12
Go to Applications--> Add applications menu.
Click File--> Advanced.
Click the search button, and search for GAIM
When the search finishes, you'll see various software components in the list. The ones with GREEN SQUARES in front of them are installed in your system. GAIM should be on the top of the list, and it'll have a green square. Right click on it, and select "mark for complete removal". Then hit the APPLY button.

This uninstalls GAIM and its configuration files.

Then reinstall GAIM using add applications menu.

Hope it solves the problem. If it does, let me know. If it doesn't even then let me know :)
