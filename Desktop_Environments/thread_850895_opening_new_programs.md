### Post by mcduck on 2008-07-07
Under the General settings in the CCS you'll find an option for "Focus Stealing Prevention" (I can't remember it that's the exact name currently in use, but at leats it used to be called that).

This is most likely the feature that's causing your problem. Set it to "low" or "none".

(it's used to prevent new opening windows and messages from stealing the focus from currently used applicatuin. Some people seem to love it, for others it's just annoying)
