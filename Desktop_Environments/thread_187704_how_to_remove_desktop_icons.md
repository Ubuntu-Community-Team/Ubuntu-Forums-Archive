### Post by tubunu on 2006-06-03
How do I get rid of volume desktop icons without unmounting them?

---

### Post by astoltz on 2006-06-03
Start gconf-editor.  Then go to Apps -> Nautilus -> Desktop and uncheck the volumes-visible box.  That should do it.
