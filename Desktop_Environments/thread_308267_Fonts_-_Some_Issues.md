### Post by mssever on 2006-11-27
I don't know if I have the complete answer, but XMMS is a GTK+ app (GTK2 is the current version). The old GTK+ didn't do a good job of antialiasing. And the problem seems to be magnified in newer systems that apparently tweak settings for newer toolkits. You might want to dump XMMS in favor of Beep Media Player, the newer project, although it's a little hard to dump such a venerable program, I admit.
