### Post by katakaio on 2009-12-08
Things are starting to get hot in this group! If you want to get involved, here's a quick list from [our website]("http://ubuntu-minnesota.org"):

[I]Resources

    * Official Website - [http://ubuntu-minnesota.org/](http://ubuntu-minnesota.org/)
    * Wiki - [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MinnesotaTeam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MinnesotaTeam)
    * Launchpad - [https://edge.launchpad.net/~ubuntu-minnesota](https://edge.launchpad.net/~ubuntu-minnesota)
    * Mailing List - [https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-mn](https://lists.ubuntu.com/mailman/listinfo/ubuntu-us-mn)
    * IRC - irc://irc.freenode.net/#ubuntu-us-mn, or Web Client
    * Forums - [http://minnesota.ubuntuforums.org/](http://minnesota.ubuntuforums.org/)
    * Roadmap - [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Roadmaps/Lucid/MinnesotaTeam](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Roadmaps/Lucid/MinnesotaTeam)
    * Facebook group - Ubuntu Minnesota on Facebook

Meetings & Events

Refer to [http://ubuntu-minnesota.org/events](http://ubuntu-minnesota.org/events) for a full listing of meetings and events.
Participate

We are a pretty new team, so the biggest thing now is attracting new members to get things off the ground. Please start by adding yourself to the Members list below and applying for membership in our team on Launchpad at [http://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-minnesota](http://launchpad.net/~ubuntu-minnesota). Swing by #ubuntu-us-mn on Freenode's IRC servers to introduce yourself and meet some other members, and once the mailing list comes online, subscribe to that to keep current with group events, ask and answer support questions, and interact with your fellow team members![/I]

I would swing by #ubuntu-us-mn to start. We hope to see you around!
