The September PenguinsUnbound.com meeting will be
Saturday September 28th at TIES,
1667 Snelling Ave. N., St. Paul, MN 55108
from 10:00am to 12:00pm
(See the web site [http://www.penguinsunbound.com](http://www.penguinsunbound.com) for directions and more
info.)

At this Months PenguinsUnbound Meeting, John Frisk will talk about:

All about QEMU/KVM
1. Demonstrate QEMU/KVM 1.4 in Ubuntu 13.04
2. Show optimization required for bridged networking of KVM guests
3. Show the different optimization techniques for storage inside of a 
KVM host
4. Show the latest SPICE work for guest graphics capabilities
5. Discuss the different management tools for creating and maintaining 
guests
6. Talk about QEMU/KVM 1.5 upcoming details in Ubuntu 13.10

Sounds Great!
