I would suggest using the alternate text installer version of Ubuntu or Xubuntu rather than a live disc for installation.

Information about them and their meetings can be found here  ( [http://gllug.org/node/12](http://gllug.org/node/12) ).  They do offer an installation night about once every month or two.
