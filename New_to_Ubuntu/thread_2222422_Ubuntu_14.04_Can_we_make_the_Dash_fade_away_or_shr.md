### Post by kostkon on 2014-05-06
You mean the launcher, not the dash. The dash is the overlay that appears when you click the Ubuntu logo in the launcher. You can set it to autohide in the Appearance settings.

### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-05-06
Do you mean the Launcher? The Dash is hidden until we activate it by clicking the Ubuntu icon at the top of the Launcher or by tapping the Super key and the Dash will hide when we tap Esc.

The Launcher, on the other hand, can be set to Autohide and we can reduce the size of the Launcher and its icons. In Ubuntu 14.04 go to System Settings>Appearance and in the Look tab we can change the size of the Launcher and in the Behaviour tab we can set the Launcher to Autohide.

If this is not what you want, then all I can say is that I do not understand what you are talking about.
