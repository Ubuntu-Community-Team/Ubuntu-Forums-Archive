### Post by halitech on 2010-01-17
Home servers have a multitude of uses, just depends on what you need one for. Some people use them for backup locations, music streaming servers, personal websites (check with your ISP first to make sure you aren't going against the Terms of Service agreement), print servers. Possibilities are pretty much endless. Beneficial depends on your needs. If you only have 1 computer, then chances are you don't need a home server.
