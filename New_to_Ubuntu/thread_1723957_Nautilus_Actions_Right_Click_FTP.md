### Post by spikoley on 2011-04-08
I haven't tried this, but it should work.  

1.  Places
2.  Connect to Server
3.  Fill out the details including the Folder and select "Bookmark name"

Once you have the bookmark create a short cut on your desktop.  Then try dragging and dropping files onto the short cut.  They should upload to the server.

Let me know how it goes.
