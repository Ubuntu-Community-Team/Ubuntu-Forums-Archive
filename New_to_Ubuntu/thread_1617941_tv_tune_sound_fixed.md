### Post by fresnofred on 2010-11-10
possible solution to no sound in old tv tuners.  I have an old tv tuner i bought years ago from compusa- a Norwood brand.  finally got the picture to work by googling or the forums.  no sound though.  how i got it to work was opening the pc and running a cd cable from the tv tuner card to a auxiliary sound connection on the mother board.  then i had to open gnome alsa mixer, can get it from synaptic, and enabling the cd rom sound.  have to mute it when done with tv but it works.  other wise the tv sound still stays on even though u close tvtime.  works for me and i want to try it on another ubuntu pc with tv tuner but have to buy another cable- cheap- u may even have one with an old cdrom box u bought in the past.  try this solution if nothing else works,  it worked for me.
