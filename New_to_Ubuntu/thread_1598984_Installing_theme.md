I have a query.
Let's say I have downloaded a theme and extracted it to a folder called themes. Lets call the extracted theme folder as Blue-Jay
How do I install this?

---

### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2010-10-17
If you extracted in ~/.themes folder it's already installed.
Just right click on your desktop>appearance settings and choose it from the appropriate tab.
If your theme isn't there, then delete it from your .themes folder and while you're on the themes tab of the appearance settings window, drag and drop the theme tar archive into the window - it will be automatically installed.
