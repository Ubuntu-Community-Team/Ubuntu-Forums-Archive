### Post by anapob on 2009-03-07
Adding Exaile to the System tray.

1 . Right Click on the Top Panel.

2 . Click on Add to Panel.

3.  Application Launcher > Forward > Sound and Video > Exaile Music Player > Add.

4. The Exaile icon appears on the top panel.

5. Right Click on the separator.

6. Untick the 'Lock to Panel'.

7. Right Click on the Exaile icon > Move > Roll the mouse horizontally to right until you get it in the desired position.

Thats the way i put it in there. You also might wanna lock the Launcher after you do that or else it would get pushed out.


If you want the screenshots [[http://m4v3rix.wordpress.com/2009/03/08/exaile-to-system-tray/](http://m4v3rix.wordpress.com/2009/03/08/exaile-to-system-tray/)]
