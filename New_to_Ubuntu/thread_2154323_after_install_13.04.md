If you meant 
- Adding another user without authenticating with your password, then you can't. Only root can modify the system, and you need to provide your password to do things as root.
- Adding a password-less user, then go into User Accounts, press Unlock and type your password. Then press the plus "+" sign at the bottom left corner to add a new user. Type in the username, press OK, and click the password setting, change "Set a password now" to "Log in without a password".
