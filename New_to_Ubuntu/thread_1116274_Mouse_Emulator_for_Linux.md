### Post by Tim Sharitt on 2009-04-04
You can enable mouse keys to control the cursor with the keypad.

Go to System > Preferences > Keyboard and click the Mouse Keys tab to find where to enable and customize mouse keys.

I am not sure if it is what you are looking for, but there are some clicking options under the accessibility tab of the mouse preferences (System > Preferences > Mouse).

You can also get to these settings by going to System > Preferences > Assistive Technologies.

You can also find more information at [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Accessibility](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Accessibility).
