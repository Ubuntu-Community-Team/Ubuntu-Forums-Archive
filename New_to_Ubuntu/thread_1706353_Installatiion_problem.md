### Post by mörgæs on 2011-03-13
No, don't download again before you know that the first copy is bad. It is a waste of time.

While booting from the CD, you will note a screen with the symbols of a keyboard and a man. It means 'push any key'. After this you will have the option of testing the CD itself. 

I strongly doubt that there is anything wrong with the CD, but it does not hurt to test.
