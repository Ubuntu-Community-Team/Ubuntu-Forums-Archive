### Post by alienexplorers on 2008-09-08
Have you tried right clicking the clock and choosing preferences.  Set the clock to use 12 hour time format.  You can also at this prompt select to add seconds.

I would not worry to much about the AUTO setting in login window screen.  Mine is also set to auto and I just adjusted the clock to 12 hour format upon bootup.
