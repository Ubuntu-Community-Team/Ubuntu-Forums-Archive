### Post by mcduck on 2014-08-01
Since the image is drawn, and black&white, and rather large in size, I'd make a guess that it's just a question of the image scaling in Gnome's image viewer giving you worse result than what the scaling in your browser does. The scaling algorithm is made to work for photographs and black/white hand-drawn picture with some texture from paper could very well result in some visual artefacts when scaled that much...

Try viewing the image at 100% size, can you see any strange visual artefacts or does it look ok then (apart from obviously not fitting to the screen). If that works, try using some image editor and resizing the image to same 10% size you are viewing right now, does that look correct or do you end with similar quality issues?
