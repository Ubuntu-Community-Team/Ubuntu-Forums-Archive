What is Button 1

Thats kind of complicated.  Typically, button1 is the left click, button 2 is middle click, 3 is right click, 4 and 5 are scroll up/down.  But this is not always the case, on some mice the buttons are mapped differently.  Trial and error is the best way to figure it out, however there are some cli programs that can show you what the mouse buttons are mapped as.  But I believe that by the time you install a new app and figure out how to use it, you could play around with the buttons and figure out which does which.
