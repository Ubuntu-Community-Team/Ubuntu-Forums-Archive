### Post by bobnutfield on 2008-09-29
They are stored in your home directory.  To find it, you will have to go to View and tick "Show Hidden Files".  Then it is in /home/XXX/.mozilla/firefox/pepgyb6k.default.

This file is the backups file.  The bookmarks are periodically backed up automatically.
