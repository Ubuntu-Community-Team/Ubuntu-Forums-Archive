### Post by AlucardArg on 2009-02-03
I'm guessing you are trying to paste from something you closed.
Linux works different from Windows in this case. In Windows, what you cut/copy, goes into the Clipboard, and from there, to the "paste target".
Here in Linux, what you cut/copy, stays in the "source" program, and isn't transferred to your temporary memory (RAM, basically). So, for example, if you want to copy from Firefox, into gedit, you have to KEEP FIREFOX OPEN while pasting.

This might seem a little odd, and inconvenient at first, but believe me, it's great. It helps to avoid losing important information, and doesn't fill up your memory, if you copy big chunks of text.
