### Post by SpenceMakesSense on 2008-11-29
for a shortcut right click what you want and click "make link". Kinda the same idea its just a link to what you want. opens just the same. Works with programs also. to add a program to a menu right click the menus click edit menus. On the right-hand side click on where you want it then you can add an item(just make sure you know the command)
Trash can should be on a the gnome panel? If not you can add it. by right clicking where theres no applet and clicking add to panel. Theres a trash (applet?) in there
