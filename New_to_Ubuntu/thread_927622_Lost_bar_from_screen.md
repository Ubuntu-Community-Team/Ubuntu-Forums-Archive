### Post by bumanie on 2008-09-23
If the top bar is still there, right click on it and choose 'new panel'. You can then drag that down to the bottom of the screen. Alternatively, if you don't want a top panel, you should be able to drag it to the bottom of the screen with all its icons still there.

### Post by Orange_and_Green on 2008-09-23
Once you have created the new panel as bumanie explained, you can right-click it, select "Add to panel" and choose any applet you'd like to see on it. I believe you are missing the "Window List" applet.

### Post by Orange_and_Green on 2008-09-23
right-click -> Add to panel -> Trash

You can then right-click the trash icon and "move" it to the far right of the panel, and then "lock to panel" if you like. Also, if you want to restore the panel to the exact original, be sure to add the "Show Desktop" applet on the far left and the "Window List" right next to it.

Have fun:)

EDIT: Oh, and the Workspace switcher on the left of the waste bin...
