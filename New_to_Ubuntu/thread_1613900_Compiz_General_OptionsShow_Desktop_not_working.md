### Post by Unhyper on 2010-11-05
I am new to Linux and Ubuntu. I'm a huge fan of OS X's Exposé feature, and I was thrilled to find a way to enable it through Compiz/General Options (Show Desktop). I assigned a hot corner for it (top right) and it worked until after various software installations and a consequent reboot.

Now I can't get it to work. The hot corner is still assigned in General Options, it just doesn't do anything. I don't know where to begin to troubleshoot what could cause a conflict here.

I am using Ubuntu 10.10 x64. I have all Compiz-related plugins and other stuff installed that's available through Synaptic.

---

### Post by NightwishFan on 2010-11-05
In Compiz Config Settings Manager, at the bottom left corner go to Preferences. Under profile choose "reset to defaults". This will cause you to lose your customized compiz settings, but may fix the problem. If it is more worth it to keep Compiz the way you have it, then please do not follow this advice. I hope it works for you.
