### Post by LowSky on 2011-07-18
I can see a few downsides to this, if you log out users mid session and they didn't save their work it could create problems. I would really suggest just sticking with switch user. Its adds a bit of security because the user needs to know his own name, and it also protects against users who don't like using passwords.

You can check out applications called 'timoutd' and 'autolog' they might fit the need, but I suggest you keep things the way they are for security and data safety.
