### Post by EXCiD3 on 2008-05-22
It sounds like it is a hardware issue if it is putting bars on all sides since a regular monitor would stretch the resolution to fit the screen. Are there any options in bios related to your monitor/graphics card? You should also do a quick google search on your laptop model and see if other users of the same laptop are having the same issues.
