### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-12-27
First, did you check the checksum on the Customization Kit that you downloaded?  Second, did you set your burner to the slowest speed and then check the checksum after burning?  It's possible that you had a bad burn and that would be consistent with freezing.

Any time you have two OS's sharing a hard disk, there is a risk of contamination.  It's more likely that a boot sector virus would take out your boot capability, but that is generally fixable.  I am not aware of any current threats that cross partitions, but I prefer to live in ignorance.

Mark Twain said:  "Put all of your eggs in one basket.  And watch that basket!"
