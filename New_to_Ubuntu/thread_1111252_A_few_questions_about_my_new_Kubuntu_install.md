### Post by raymondh on 2009-03-30
Welcome.  You'll find yourself amazed constantly with what you can do with Linux.  I know I am :)  First a caveat .... am a beginner myself ..... that said, here goes;

1.  I'm playing with Kubuntu 9.04 right now whilst still maintaining my current, production Ubuntu 8.10.  "Suspend-to-Ram" you mean HIBERNATE? 

2.  Try cairo-dock or Avant Window navigator (AWN).  Both have the options you are looking for.  I use AWN on my 8.10 install and have been pleased .... coming from a Mac orientation/preference

3.  Kubuntu 9.04 is still in beta stage.  I think the official release is this april.  The option to "upgrade" will be always be yours.  Update Manager will let you know.  I think it''ll be a simple upgrade as you are coming from 8.10.

4.  Google, the wiki's and the forums are great assets of Linux .... use them at will.

Enjoy
