### Post by ChompTheMan on 2008-08-12
Right-click on kicker(the panel) and choose Add Applet to Panel.  Scroll down until you find KBFX Spinx and then add it to the panel.  You should now have a shiny K Menu button on your panel.

To change themes go to K Menu >> Settings >> KBFX Configurator.  From there you can install themes, change the menu button, etc.
