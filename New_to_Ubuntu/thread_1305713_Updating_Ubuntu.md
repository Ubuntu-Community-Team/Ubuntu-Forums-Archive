### Post by blazemore on 2009-10-30
Of course; it gets security updates, bugfixes and occasional feature enhancements.
These are checked for automatically, but if you want to initiate an update yourself just go to
System -> Administration -> Update Manager

And click on "Check"

A list of possible updates will appear.

Click "Install Updates" to install them. You will be prompted for your password. Occasionally, after updating, you will be required to reboot your computer, or certain applications. You will be informed whether or not this is necessary.

If you want to change the settings for updates, you can click "Settings" in the bottom-left corner of the update windows.

Also, most of the bugs in 9.10 were fixed in the beta stages. Karmic is ready for general use! If you really want long-term stability, use 8.04 Hardy Heron.
