Hello there,

I am a recent convert from Windows 7 to Ubuntu Natty Narwhal. 

When I press Ctrl+ "+", I see that my text has zoomed in, but not the images. How could I zoom in both images and the text at the same time on the Mozilla Firefox browser?

---

### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-07-17
There is a menu setting for that under View->Zoom->Zoom Text Only. Since you want images to zoom as well, make sure it's not checked. Use alt-v to get the View menu if you have the menu bar hidden.
