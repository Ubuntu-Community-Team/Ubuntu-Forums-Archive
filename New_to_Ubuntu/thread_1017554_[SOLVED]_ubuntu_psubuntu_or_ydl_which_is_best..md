### Post by nhasian on 2008-12-23
it looks like everyone is just speculating here so let me chime in with my two cents since i've actually tried YDL on my PS3.  its so sluggish that you'll go bald pulling your hair out.  plus since it runs on a ppc architecture a lot of programs wont work either because they are not prepackaged for ppc chips and you have to compile it yourself (good luck) or not supported altogether.  You wont be able to play bluray movies while running linux, and you also wont be able to play HD mkv files because there is no hardware acceleration.  i tried to play Elephants Dream and it was like 5 frames a second.  

My primary goal was to use the PS3 like an HTPC to watch movies, but i've discovered that streaming videos from my PC via fuppes or ushare is far superior.
