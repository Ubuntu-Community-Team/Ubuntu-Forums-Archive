### Post by Mike_Walsh on 2014-08-20
Hallo,Ray.

I've been using my-weather-indicator for a few months now. If by 'bleeding' you mean that the indicator is showing on top of Firefox's current page, simply click the page in an empty area, and you should find that the indicator disappears. In order to prevent this from happening again (I'm doing this off the top of my head at the moment, since I'm currently using a friend's Vista machine), I believe you need to go into preferences (drop down menu from the top menu bar) and I think there's a setting that will allow you to change it from 'always on top'.

Hope this helps.

Regards,
Mike.
