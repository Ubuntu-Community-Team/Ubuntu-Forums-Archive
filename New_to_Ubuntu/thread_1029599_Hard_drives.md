### Post by Kellemora on 2009-01-03
Hi JDR

Only some very old hard drives that require a software driver to make them work won't work on Linux.  Also the new Maxtor 120 gig external is one of them that requires a software driver only available for the DOZE!  But their 200 gig external (same vintage) works just fine!

I've got old 1, 4 and 10 gig drives that work just fine under Linux, and any new drive works on Linux too.

Why Maxtor made their 120 gig external a DOZE ONLY hard drive has a LOT of folks wondering WHAT ON EARTH WERE THEY THINKING?

But that's the ONLY recent vintage hard drive I've heard of that won't work under Linux.
