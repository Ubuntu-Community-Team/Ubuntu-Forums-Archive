### Post by Zimrie on 2011-10-20
recordmydesktop
for example recordmydesktop -o /home/username/out.ogv -x 400 -y 200 --width 320 --height 240 --follow-mouse  --channels 1 --no-frame  --no-sound --delay 2

also have gtk gui
