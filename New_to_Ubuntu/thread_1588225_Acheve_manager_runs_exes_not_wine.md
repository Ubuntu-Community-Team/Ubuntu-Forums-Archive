### Post by da burger on 2010-10-04
Right click the file, select open with, if wine is there then click it, if not then click other application. This will give you a big list of programs that should hopefully include wine. Select wine. Also ensure the check box that says something along the lines of "remember this application when reading .exe files" is checked.

### Post by pricetech on 2010-10-04
A lot of "exe" files are in fact self extracting archives.  This is why Archive Manager wants to open them in the first place.

Just for future reference.
