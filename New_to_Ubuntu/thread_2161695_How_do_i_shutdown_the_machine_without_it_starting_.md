### Post by BBQdave on 2013-07-11
> **Old-un said:**
> how i can successfully shutdown the machine without it restarting under battery power all the time.

If you are closing the notebook lid while it is running, Ubuntu goes into *Suspend* - it awakes, starts when you open the lid. To do a full shutdown, selected *Shutdown* in the power menu - located top bar, far right :)
