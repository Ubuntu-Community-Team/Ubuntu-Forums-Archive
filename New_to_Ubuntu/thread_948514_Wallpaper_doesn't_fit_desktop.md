### Post by Therion on 2008-10-15
Right-click in an empty space on your desktop and select Change Desktop Background from the pop-up menu.

Try using some different options from the drop down menu, like "Center" or "Stretch".  I think "Zoom" is the default setting and might be messing up your display.  Most likely one of the options listed in that menu will put things in order for you.
