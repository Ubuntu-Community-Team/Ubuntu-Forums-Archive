### Post by unknown user on 2009-01-22
Every email that I send out from my Thunderbirds client, the address is being placed in my contacts. This is a pain as most of the times I do not want it to keep the email addresses of support/sales.
Is there a setting that I can change to prevent ever address being emailed from Thunderbirds being saved in my contacts?

---

### Post by DM was on fire! on 2009-01-22
Go to Edit -> Preferences -> Composition, then click addressing.
You should have the option to unselect "automatically add outgoing e-mail addresses". :)

Hope that works!
