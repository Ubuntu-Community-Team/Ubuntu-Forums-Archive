### Post by S.R on 2010-06-22
I am assuming it is recognizing your cd-rom. Try unmounting your cd-rom  first ... then eject. A handy place to see if the cd-rom is recognized,  mounted or unmounted, and ejecting from all at one place is the "disk  utility" under the "administration tab". For that method to work you  have to have a disk in and it can't be busy.
