### Post by Inxsible on 2008-05-23
If its a regular usb or ps2 mouse or keyboard then you don't need any drivers. 

If you keyboard has some special buttons for launching of applications, they may or may not work in Ubuntu. The same with the mouse, some specialized buttons may or may not work.

Having said that, there are ways to make them work but they are never guaranteed.
