### Post by rockstarrem on 2011-04-26
Hi domoappo,

You can use ffmpeg to do such a conversion, but I must warn you that you are really losing a lot of audio data when converting a lossy format such as WMA to another lossy format such as MP3. If you don't care about the quality of the sound or anything, go ahead and do the conversion with a program like FFMpeg, if you don't want to lose audio data then I would try my best to re-download the files in your desired format.
