### Post by beew on 2011-08-05
I can run Unity(11.04) and before that full Compiz with 3d effects (Ubuntu 10.04 and 10.10) smoothly  on a 6 year old Dell Latitude D410 which has  an ancient Intel card and barely 1 G of ram.

IMO all these talks about Unity requiring super powerful hardware to run are vastly exaggerated. They are most likely from people who are using decades old machines or have some graphic card issues. Anything that you can even put Win7 on would be more than powerful enough to run Unity unless you have some graphic card issues.
