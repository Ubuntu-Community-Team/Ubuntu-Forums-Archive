### Post by Cheesemill on 2013-04-12
This is probably more than you need but their is a great little bash script called screenfetch-dev that you can download from [here]("https://github.com/KittyKatt/screenFetch/blob/master/screenfetch-dev").

It gives you all the information you need about which DE you are running as well as your current theme, it also works on most Linux OS's and autodetects which logo it should use.
