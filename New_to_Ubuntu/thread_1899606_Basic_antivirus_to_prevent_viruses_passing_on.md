### Post by elgordodude on 2011-12-24
Yes, there are several linux antiviral programs capable of scanning Windows programs. My go to is AVG, you can download it by googling AVG linux download and clicking on the .deb package from their repository. Then install the deb by double clicking it on in your downloads folder.

Real-time protection is more of a server/enterprise question, particularly with mailboxes. If you're regularly sneakernetting I would suggest that you scan your info with AVG or clam before transferring.
