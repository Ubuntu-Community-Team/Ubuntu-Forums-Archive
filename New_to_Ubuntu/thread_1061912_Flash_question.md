### Post by Hospadar on 2009-02-06
Not really, While I think adobe technically opened up flash, there are still no particularly good flash authoring programs other than adobe's flash.  There are some utilities for making certain kinds of flash, you can convert regular videos to flash video ".flv", you can make flash tutorials with "wink" but as for true flash authoring you are (to my knowlege) out of luck.

Probably your best bet if you arn't willing to cough up for (or pirate) adobe flash is to make a video that does what your banner wants to do, or maybe an animated gif (this is possible in gimp, among others)
