### Post by bcschmerker on 2012-04-13
In fact, there are users across operating systems that are having problems with Adobe® Flash 11.2.202.*; [Adobe Systems]("http://www.adobe.com/") has received numerous bug reports that concentrate on a few specific areas in terms of audio and video performance.  For example, I use [Userplane® video chat](http://www.userplane.com/) for some of my online activities (Userplane® controls IRC engines via Flash, with the user interface in an HTML IFrame) and found my transmission audio to be quite choppy (which *did not* happen with Flash 11.1); the same problem presents itself on both my hybrid Everex® running 10.04.4-LTS (Kernel 3.0.0.18-generic-pae backported from 11.10) and on an Asus® CM1630-06 (upgraded with video and audio hardware) running Microsoft® Windows® 7.0.8001 (Kernel 6.1.7601).

**Addendum:**  As of 13 April 2012, the Everex® runs Kernel 3.0.0.19 and the problem described this post has not changed.
