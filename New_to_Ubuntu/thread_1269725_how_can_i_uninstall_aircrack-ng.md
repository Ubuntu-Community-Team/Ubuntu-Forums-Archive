### Post by shashwat on 2009-09-19
go to system>administration>synaptic package manager and then type aicrack-ng in it then unmark the apps for removal by clicking on the box besides the name and then click app.It will then show you the statistics (what all are you uninstalling,how much space will be free ect.) and then click apply.It will then uninstall. Tada!\\:D/:) Your app (aircrack-ng) has been uninstalled.Thankyou.):P
