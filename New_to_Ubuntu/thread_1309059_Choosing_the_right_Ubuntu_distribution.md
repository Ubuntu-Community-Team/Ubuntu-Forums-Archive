### Post by Anthenima on 2009-11-01
Hey there! You probably just stumbled into the world of Ubuntu Linux. So which Ubuntu distribution is right for you? Well, you've come to the right thread. In this thread I will be comparing the major distributions of Ubuntu. Shall we get started? Lets.

***Gnome (Ubuntu)***
Gnome is your choice if you like

[LIST]
[*]Visually pleasing effects
[*]Something to do when you're bored
[*]Checking email and surfing the internet
[*]An easy to use interface
[/LIST]
[COLOR=Red]Please note that if you have an older computer, Gnome is very resource heavy.

[/COLOR]The Gnome Desktop
[IMG]http://www.debianadmin.com/wp-content/gallery/karmicbeta/11.png[/IMG]

***KDE (Kubuntu)***
KDE is right for you if:

[LIST]
[*]You like eye candy
[*]You like visually pleasing effects and interface
[*]You like Desktop widgets (Plasmoids)
[*]You like a user friendly interface
[/LIST]
[COLOR=Red]Please note that KDE is also very heavy on resources and can be a pain if you don't know how to work your way around with a terminal.[/COLOR]
