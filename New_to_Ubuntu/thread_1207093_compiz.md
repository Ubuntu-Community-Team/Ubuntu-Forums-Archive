### Post by nhasian on 2009-07-07
if firefox gets maximized try pressing F11 to toggle fullscreen on/off.  you can stop compiz from switching desktops when your mouse reaches the edge of the screen by adjusting the settings in System->Preferences->CompizConfig Settings Manager.
