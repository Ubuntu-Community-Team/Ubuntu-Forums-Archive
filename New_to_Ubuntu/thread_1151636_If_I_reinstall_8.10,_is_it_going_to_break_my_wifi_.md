### Post by scheuri on 2009-05-07
There is NO WAY to say for sure.
That all depends on your wifi hardware (maybe even chipset) and on the implementation of your wifi to your LAN (as in SSID, WEP, WPA, WPA2, etc.).
If you had trouble with your first install of 8.10 then very likely you will have same troubles again.
This time you may prepare yourself first to make sure it is not that much of a hassle, I don't know.

Depending on your hardware you might encounter troubles which maybe solvable or not. And if they are solvable, you might need to work with the CLI.

Beside of that, questions like "why" and "which OS do you use now" arise, however, they might not have anything to do with your actual problem.


good luck
