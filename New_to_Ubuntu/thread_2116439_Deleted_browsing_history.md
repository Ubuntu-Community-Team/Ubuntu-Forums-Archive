### Post by MadmanRB on 2013-02-15
depending on your browser it depends.
Most store items in a hidden folder, or inside a subfolder of one.
For example firefox stores its data in .mozilla
chrome and chromium are a little different, both store in .config in subfolders such as chromium and google-chrome.
All are usually found in your home directory and could be accessed in home/yournamehere/.hiddenfolder

You should be able to unhide these folders its in the menu under "view" and selecting the show hidden folders options.
or you can hit ctrl + h
