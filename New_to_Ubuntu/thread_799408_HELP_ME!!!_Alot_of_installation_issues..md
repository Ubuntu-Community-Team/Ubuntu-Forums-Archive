### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-05-19
You might as well restart the X server by pressing Control-Alt-Backspace, which will restart the whole desktop.

To force-quit just one program, you should add the "Force Quit" applet to one of the bottom panels. Click the applet's icon on your panel and then click the window that you want to close.
