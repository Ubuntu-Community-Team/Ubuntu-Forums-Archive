I don't know if it is a bug, but I do know that from time to time certain programs on my computer have frozen. Thus, I have needed to restart. However, shutting down by pressing the power button is not always the wisest of options.

If it freezes and you do not want to perform a "hard shutdown," I would recommend pressing the following keys in order:

Alt [then] Print Screen [then] R E I S U B

This will safely shutdown and restart your computer :)
