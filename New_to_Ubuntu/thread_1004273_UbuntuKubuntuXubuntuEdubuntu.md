### Post by linuxguymarshall on 2008-12-07
They are all the same. Ubuntu uses what is called a GNOME desktop, Kubuntu uses what is called a KDE desktop Xubuntu uses an Xfce desktop. Google those 3 things and see which one you want to try. You can always flip around once you have it installed by installing GNOME or KDE then logging out and selecting what you want. Edubuntu is Ubuntu with special networking features and educational programs. It is designed for school networks.
