### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2010-01-21
> **jmat said:**
> Hi!

Just a few questions...

When is the next release version of Ubuntu due out? 

April.  Releases are always in April and October.
> 
 Is there currently a "beta" one can test?

It's not in beta yet, still in alpha.  I don't think you can get a disc of it, but you can upgrade a 9.10 system to the alpha.
> 
This may sound stupid, but here goes anyway: When the new version of Ubuntu is released do you have to DL the disk or is there a way to upgrade through something like the update manager???

You can (in theory) upgrade through the update manager.  When a new release comes out, you'll be notified that a new release is available and asked if you want to upgrade to it.  Or you can continue on with the old release and just keep getting security updates.

Upgrading this way has historically been problematic for some, I see a lot of people recommending a fresh install.  I typically go the upgrade route myself.
