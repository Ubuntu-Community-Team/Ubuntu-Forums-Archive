### Post by leunam12 on 2018-05-25
Your laptop's cooler is probably clogged with dust. You must find a tutorial on how to remove the
computer's keyboard and then blow compressed air from the exhaust fins into the computer.
The dust will escape out of the fan hole that is under the keyboard. Do it in a well ventilated area
or put a vacuum on the fan hole.
Overheating may damage your computer.
