### Post by victorbrca on 2009-05-01
If you go to "System=>Preferences=>Display" you should be able to see the projector, enable it and then disable the laptop display.

On 8.10 (also aspire one) I had to quit my X server (either log out or press "Ctrl+Alt+Backspace") and log back in for the changes to take full effect. I haven't tried it on 9.04


Vic.
