### Post by sayakb on 2008-06-06
Open the volume mixer applet, goto File->Change device and select any ***capture*** device. **Unmute** them if necessary. Do this for all ***capture** *devices.

### Post by aktiwers on 2008-06-06
Try go double-click the sound icon in your panel and pick Edit=>Preferences 
Then add "mic select" and a new tab will show up, called "options".

In this Options tab, pick mic 2.

Does it work now?
