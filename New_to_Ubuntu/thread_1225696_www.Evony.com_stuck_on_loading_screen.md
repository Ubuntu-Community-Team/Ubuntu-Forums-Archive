### Post by AgentY on 2009-10-07
EDITED:

Wrong information previously posted (although updating Firefox is never a bad idea...).

To resolve this problem, do the following:

System-> administration -> synaptic 

1. search for swfdec, "mark for removal" to all of them. 
2. search for flash, "mark for installation" to flashplugin-installer

apply.

done, restart firefox, you will be able to play evony.
