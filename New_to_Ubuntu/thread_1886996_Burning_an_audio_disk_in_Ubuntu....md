### Post by insomniaccanuck on 2011-11-26
If your player reads MP3's burn them as MP3's (a data CD) and you get the compression.

If your player doesn't you 'uncompress' them (burnt as an Audio CD) and the results are generally not satisfying.  Hissing or ticking sounds can result.
