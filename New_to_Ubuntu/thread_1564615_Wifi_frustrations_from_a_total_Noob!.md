### Post by Nedlian on 2010-08-30
Hi
I’m a total noob here and found all the information on this site and others to be invaluable in getting my Dell Netbook running Ubuntu.  After a seamless install and set-up, however, I could not get the wifi to work.  I was able to connect when wired in so knew the problem should be solvable.  I read and tried every trick on the ‘net, I swear!  One of the things I did when setting up was to assign a password and every time I rebooted I had to enter the password and it really wasn’t helping my stress level.  So, I decided to disable the password until I could get the wifi going.  I was checking the **Users> User Privileges> Change user settings**, and, lo and behold, part way down the list, “Connect to wireless and ethernet networks” -- tick!  Problem solved.  
Sometimes we outwit ourselves and I thought I’d post this in case some other noob has hit the wifi wall.
Thanks for all the help and support these pages provide. :D
