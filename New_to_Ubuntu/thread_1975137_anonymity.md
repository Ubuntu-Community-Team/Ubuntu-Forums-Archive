### Post by Paqman on 2012-05-07
Depends who you'd like to be anonymous from. True anonymity isn't possible, someone will always be able to figure out at least some information about you. 

Don't use TOR for bit torrent, it hogs the bandwidth. If you want to remain fairly anonymous from the point of view of sites you visit or other members of a swarm then you can use a proxy or VPN service. You'll probably have to pay for this, and obviously you won't be anonymous to the people providing your proxy/VPN. Ubuntu can join VPNs itself, you don't need to install anything. Left click on the network icon to set it up.
