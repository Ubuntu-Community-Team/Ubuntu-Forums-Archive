### Post by Monicker on 2008-05-06
You could try some of the downloadable material from this site.  They are not specific to Ubuntu but still a good reference for linux fundamentals:

[http://www.tldp.org/guides.html]("http://www.tldp.org/guides.html")


And these in particular:

[http://www.tldp.org/LDP/intro-linux/intro-linux.pdf]("http://www.tldp.org/LDP/intro-linux/intro-linux.pdf")
[http://www.tldp.org/LDP/GNU-Linux-Tools-Summary/GNU-Linux-Tools-Summary.pdf]("http://www.tldp.org/LDP/GNU-Linux-Tools-Summary/GNU-Linux-Tools-Summary.pdf")

I looked briefly, and could not find any of the official Ubuntu documentation available as a download.  I suppose you could use wget or another program to mirror the pages of help.ubuntu.com locally.
