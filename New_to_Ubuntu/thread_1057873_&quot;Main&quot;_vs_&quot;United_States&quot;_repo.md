### Post by expatCM on 2009-02-02
It does not matter which server you use for download ....  they all have the same content.  There may be very slight variations during the update times but nothing that you would normally notice.

You may want to try another approach to selecting the server though ....  instead of Main or United States click on "Other" and then click on the button to "Select Best Server".  This will then test the speed of download from all of the available servers and select the fastest one relative to your location.  What is the best server on one day appears to change according to how busy the Internet is ...  Worth exploring :)

### Post by Vadi on 2009-02-02
There shouldn't be, as the main one is the server provided by Canonical, and the 'United States' one was the official mirror in the US. Mirrors sometimes can lag behind the main servers, because they update peridiocally on a timer.

Usually though the mirror closest to you will be the fastest.
