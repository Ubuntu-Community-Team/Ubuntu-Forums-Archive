### Post by Yayestechlab on 2011-10-31
First of all, 8.10 is outdated, download the latest version, 11.10 or 10.04 at [http://ubuntu.com/download](http://ubuntu.com/download). To answer your question, if you use 10.04 or 11.10 there will be an option during install to use the blank partition (I assume it's blank).

Warning: Errors may occur if you don't make a swap partition out of part of the blank partition.
