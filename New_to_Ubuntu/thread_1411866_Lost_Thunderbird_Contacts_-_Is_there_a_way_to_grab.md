### Post by mechro on 2010-02-20
I don't know if there is a Thunderbird add on that you could use but Claws Mail has an address harvesting facility.

You could install Claws Mail, import your Thunderbird mail and then collect the addresses.

[http://www.ghacks.net/2009/07/04/claws-mail-the-unsung-powerhouse-email-client/](http://www.ghacks.net/2009/07/04/claws-mail-the-unsung-powerhouse-email-client/)
