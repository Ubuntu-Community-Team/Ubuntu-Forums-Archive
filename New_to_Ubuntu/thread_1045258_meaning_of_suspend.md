### Post by Locke_99GS on 2009-01-20
The computer shuts power to most systems, except RAM. 

This is what most laptops go to when you close the lid - when it functions properly, it saves a lot of power and can return to its previous state very quickly.

### Post by snova on 2009-01-20
> **chethankrish said:**
> willl the proceses or applications running prior to "suspend" , keep running during and after "suspend"...

It basically saves whatever its doing, powers off any unnecessary devices, and enters a low-power state.

Any applications running before will "pause" when you standby, they will simply become inactive. When you bring it back out of standby, they resume whatever they were doing.
