### Post by scragar on 2009-11-27
When there's one box it's restored, drag to resize it and make it more reasonable. When there's two it's maximised, that means it will try to use the largest possible space it can on the desktop after factoring in certain other objects(margins, panels etc) which it's not allowed to cover.
