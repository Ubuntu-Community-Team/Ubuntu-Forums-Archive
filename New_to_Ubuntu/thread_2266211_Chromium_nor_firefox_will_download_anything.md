### Post by coldraven on 2015-02-21
If you are connected by wifi you could try logging into your router and check the System Status log for errors. If there are lots of errors it could be that the wifi channel is clashing with your neighbours wifi. Changing the channel might help.
Google your router model for instructions on how to do it.
