### Post by sandlidl on 2009-03-30
Hello. To flip your picture do this in OOO.Word.

At the top, click View, Toolbars, Picture. A small toolbar should appear that will give you two buttons to change your picture horizontally or vertically.

Make sure you click on the picture first, the same toolbar may appear when you do.

To rotate it 90 degrees, you should open it in OOODraw, rotate it, then paste it into your document.

Cheers.
