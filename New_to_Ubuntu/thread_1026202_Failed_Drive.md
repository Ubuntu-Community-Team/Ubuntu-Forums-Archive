### Post by Frak on 2008-12-31
> **RealG187 said:**
> Will I need that? I had data recovered from a drive and it cost me only $60.
It depends. Electronic recovery via ghosting or shadow reading is fairly cheap, usually under $100. Laboratory recovery involves removing the platter from the drive, inside a clean room, and extracting it via a very expensive platter spinner with mostly manual labor. That can cost upwards of $10,000 depending on the drive size.
