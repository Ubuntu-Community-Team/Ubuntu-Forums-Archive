### Post by krishnandu.sarkar on 2010-05-29
Click on the Time And Date applet on the panel
Expand Location
Select Edit
On Locations tab click Add
Type in the City and a valid match will pop up
Otherwise select your Time Zone.
Tats all it'll synchronize itself, You'll also get the temparature. :)
