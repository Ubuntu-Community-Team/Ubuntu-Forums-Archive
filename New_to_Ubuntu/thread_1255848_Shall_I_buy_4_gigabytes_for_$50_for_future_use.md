### Post by earthpigg on 2009-09-01
usually you pick the motherboard and CPU first. those are the most expensive components, so most folks tailor the rest of their system to that - rather than tailoring the entire system to the RAM (since RAM is a fairly cheap component anyways).

what kind of RAM is it? <- will limit which motherboards will work with it.
