### Post by jonabyte on 2008-08-19
Click on System - Administration - Users and Groups.

Then click the Unlock button, type in your password to unlock it.

Click on your username and then hit properties.

Click the User Privileges tab, then check all the boxes. 

This will give you system wide access, so be careful since you are new.
