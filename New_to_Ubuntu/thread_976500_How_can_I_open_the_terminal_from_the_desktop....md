Well... You have several options:

close it when you are done...

3 - use tilda

4 - switch to another console CTRL-ALT-F1 and back when you're done CTRL-ALT-F7...

### Post by fooman on 2008-11-09
i like this way,  first open a terminal and type or copy/paste the following:

```
sudo apt-get install nautilus-open-terminal
```

after it installs,  log out and then back in again.

that will give you the ability to right click on the desktop (or in nautlus or just about anywhere) and choose "open in terminal".  :D
