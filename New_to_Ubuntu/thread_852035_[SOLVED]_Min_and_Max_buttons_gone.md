### Post by Bigtime_Scrub on 2008-07-07
Ah HA! I got it!

System-> Preferences-> Appearance-> Visual Effects-> EXTRA

Set from Extra back to normal.

I guess my graphics card sucks. Cool its all back to the way it was and even compiz is working good again. I guess I got too greedy. :)
