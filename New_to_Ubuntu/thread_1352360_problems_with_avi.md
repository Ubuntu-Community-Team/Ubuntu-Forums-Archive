### Post by northern lights on 2009-12-11
Avis are container files. The file extension is in no way related to the codec the video has been encoded with. That can range from H264, mpeg4, mpeg2. mpeg1, etc.pp.

Hence the trouble you're experiencing may not happen to all avi files you might try to watch.

Nonetheless, I've never experienced problems with any avi, so it's highly likely something on your machine's at fault. Do you have Microsoft codecs installed (*w32codecs*)? *ubuntu-restricted-extras* is a meta-package - have all contents been installed? Have you tried reinstalling it? Have you tried another player but vlc?
