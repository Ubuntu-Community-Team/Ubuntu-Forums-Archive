### Post by ispy on 2009-05-19
I would suggest installing XFCE for a window manager. It works better on slower hardware.

If even that is too slow, try Openbox. It's really lightweight.

Also, for the services you should disable, I personally don't use Bluetooth. Honestly, you're not going to save that much speed by disabling services. Use a lighter window manager.
