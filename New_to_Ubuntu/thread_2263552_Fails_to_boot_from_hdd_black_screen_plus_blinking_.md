### Post by oldfred on 2015-02-02
It does seem to make some difference between video modes on live installer and after you install.

And an old BIOS like yours may have issues if just one of the boot files is beyond some point. You can test if that is the issue by using gparted from live installer and shrinking / to less than 100GB. About half of those I suggest that to, find it then works.
