### Post by LeoSolaris on 2008-05-10
Well...  I use 'Remember the Milk" RTM for short. 

Unfortunately I'm not sure it would meet your needs. It's an applet for AWN, I discovered it after installing the bzr version of AWN, which is not in the repos. I is perfect for my needs because it is actually web based. (the applet just signs in and can manipulate the list) 

I can email tasks to it, prioritize them, and sort them into categories. It has a lot of other features, but I have not delved into it yet.

[Here's the web site.]("http://www.rememberthemilk.com/")

Check out the tour for a more complete list of what it does.

Only thing that might be a deal breaker on your part may be the subtasks, possibly the extended notes on a task. I haven't found a max length, but I haven't tried yet either.

Hope it helps,
Leo

P.S. If you use Synaptic, which is in System->Administration->Synaptic, you can do a keyword search of all of the programs in the repos. Mindmapping programs may be able to help out with it, as could Tomboy, which is a note taking program that you can link notes like wikis. It's all in how you use them. Tomboy is install by default if i remember correctly. I use it for my college notes.

P.P.S Evolution has a task manager. Just click on the little arrow right by the new mail button. It will drop down a list and the last on is Tasks.
