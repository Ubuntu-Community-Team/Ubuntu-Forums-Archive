VLC is available in the repositories, so click System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager, and search for "vlc".
MSN Messenger doesn't exist for Linux, and nor does Yahoo messenger. Pidgin is already installed and that is a good alternative. I would also recommend aMSN which is similar to MSN Messenger. (aMSN can also be found in the repositories).
Finally, Frostwire can be downloaded [here]("http://www.frostwire.com/?id=downloads")- click the Ubuntu/Debian icon. When you download installers for Ubuntu, they will be debian packages (file.deb).
