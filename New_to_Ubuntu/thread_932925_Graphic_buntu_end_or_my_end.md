### Post by Locke_99GS on 2008-09-29
Try changing your Screen section to something like this.

```

Section "Screen"
	Identifier	"Default Screen"
	Monitor		"Configured Monitor"
	Device		"Configured Video Device"
	Option		"AddARGBGLXVisuals"	"True"
	Defaultdepth	24
        SubSection "Display"
		Depth		24
		Modes		"1024x768"
	EndSubSection
EndSection

```

Then restart X.
