That doesn't seem like a particularly definitive list to me. It's missing a whole load of official derivatives of Ubuntu anyway. 

To be honest, I don't think it really matters what is and what isn't listed on that page. There are plenty of other more reliable lists of Linux distributions. Go to the official Ubuntu website and you will find other variants listed there. Distrowatch.com also lists a lot of distributions. 

Don't pay any mind to the arguments about certain flavours getting more mention over others. It's easy to get sucked into it; I myself have done so before only to realise that the things you find are pretty groundless.
