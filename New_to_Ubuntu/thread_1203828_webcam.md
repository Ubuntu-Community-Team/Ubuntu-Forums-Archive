### Post by Temposs on 2009-07-03
You shouldn't usually need to download a driver. Your Ubuntu install already has the necessary drivers installed. You just need to worry about getting a compatible webcam.

Just make sure that the webcam you choose works with exactly your version of Ubuntu on exactly the programs that you intend to use. Do a lot of research, because webcams are a bit of a weak point on Ubuntu, as I have experienced. There are some that work, but a lot don't.

Take a look at this page. It's a list of which webcams work with Skype in Ubuntu. Try to pick one that works out of the box with Ubuntu 8.04:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SkypeWebCams](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SkypeWebCams)
