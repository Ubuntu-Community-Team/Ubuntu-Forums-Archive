### Post by Paqman on 2009-08-13
> **mikodo said:**
> 
How will this effect my connection with Ubuntu?


Not at all. Your router takes care of the connection. You may need to use a new router, or else you'll just have to put your new internet username/password into your current router.

If you get a new router and connect wirelessly you'll need to search for and connect to the new router. If it's a wired connection it'll all happen automagically.

> 
Update Manager? PPA's? Any other considerations? Preparations needed before changing? Things to consider/attend to, after changing to new ISP?


Nothing at all as far as Ubuntu is concerned.
