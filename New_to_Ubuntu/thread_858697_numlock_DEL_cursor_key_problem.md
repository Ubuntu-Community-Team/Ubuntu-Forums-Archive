### Post by alh2o on 2008-07-13
I'm running Kubuntu 8.04 and use an ergologic keyboard. My Bios is set to turn numlock on at boot.  This does not happen but no matter; the  system setting for numlock on KDE startup is "Leave unchanged".  When numlock is off everything works as expected.  When numlock is on, the cursor keys (arranged as a T) act to highlight the characters they move over and DEL will delete the highlighted characters.  Otherwise (without having highlighted characters), DEL does not work.  I expect the cursor keys to move the cursor without highlighting and DEL to delete regardless of whether numlock is on or off.  This is how it works in Windows XP on the same machine.  I have observed this behaviour in Firefox, Konqueror and Open Office Writer.  Does anyone have any advice on how I can get the cursor keys and DEL to work independently of numlock?
