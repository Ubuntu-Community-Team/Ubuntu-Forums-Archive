### Post by SweetAurora on 2013-08-10
In a way, yes, in fact every computer does this. It's the reason why you open the start menu and see recent programs and files. Remember, tracking/recording your activity does not equal spying. The data isn't being sent anywhere over the net. It's a convenience feature. Only things typed into Ubuntu's Unity Dash Search is actually goes anywhere over the net, but it's only the text string typed and nothing else. If you don't like that, turn off the online feature in privacy. :) As with other Ubuntu distro's, they lack the Unity search and online integration so nothing is sent over the net, period.
