Well,

for the benfit of others finding themselves in this quandry....

I did find a few new things today.
[SIZE="4"]
In[COLOR="DarkOrange"] FIREFOX PREFERENCES[/COLOR]
[COLOR="Red"] ( Edit --> Preferences --> Content --> Advanced).....[/COLOR]
I checked the box that allows pages to use their own fonts and under
[COLOR="Red"] ( Edit --> Preferences --> Content --> Colors).....[/COLOR]
I checked the box to allow pages to use their own colors

This allowed me to see ALL the page content finally as I was not able to before.  I still had to go back and re-select my specific printer at print time instead of the one it keeps defaulting to but the above steps finally allow me to print pages that were critical for me.[/SIZE]
