### Post by aysiu on 2008-11-04
You have to switch to MSC before you put the files in.

So you can't have it in MTP mode, put files in, switch to MSC, and then see those same files.

What you have to do is go back to MTP mode, delete all the files, switch to MSC, put the files on, and then when you stay with MSC, you'll keep seeing all those files.

Does that make sense?
