### Post by mansonfan78 on 2013-10-20
So, you shut down your computer (power off) and it later turns on by itself?  If that's the case you should check your bios settings, it may have an auto-start setting.  While there check for standby/suspend settings, something there might be causing the random shutdown (or not, but you might as well check).
