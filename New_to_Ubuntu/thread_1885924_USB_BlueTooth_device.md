i recently bought an inexpensive, IOGEAR Bluetooth 2.1 USB micro adapter ($5 after rebate) which seems to work very well.  i only used it to transfer some pics from my phone over to my computer.  ubuntu recognized it as soon as i plugged it in, and it found my phone easily.  ...transfers pics to and from my phone with ease :)
