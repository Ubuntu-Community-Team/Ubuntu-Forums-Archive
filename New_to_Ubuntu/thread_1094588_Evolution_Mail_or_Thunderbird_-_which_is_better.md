### Post by martrn on 2009-03-12
There are plugins to get thunderbird to read emails on hotmail windows etc.... [http://ubuntuswitch.blogspot.com/2007/09/ubuntu-how-to-get-yahoohotmail-emails.html]("http://ubuntuswitch.blogspot.com/2007/09/ubuntu-how-to-get-yahoohotmail-emails.html")

If you are looking to centralise your emails I would recommend, evolution because it is brilliant (and fast) at organising your email folders, and if you receive/send lots of emails through using email lists evolution is a good X11 graphical email program to do that.

If you like wizzy looks I think thunderbird might be better.

There is a way to receive hot mail on evolution but involves hotway or hotswap or simular.
