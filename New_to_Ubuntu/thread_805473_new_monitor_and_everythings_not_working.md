### Post by iaculallad on 2008-05-24
Try doing the steps below to fix your resolution problem:

-Backup your current configuration
sudo cp /etc/X11/xorg.conf /etc/X11/xorg.conf.bak

-We'll try to edit the existing configuration
sudo gedit /etc/X11/xorg.conf

-Try pasting the line of text which does not contain any asterisk to your "Screen" and "Monitor" section.


*Section "Monitor"
*	Identifier	"Configured Monitor"
	HorizSync 30-110
	VertRefresh 50-160
*EndSection

*Section "Screen"
*	Identifier	"Default Screen"
*	Monitor		"Configured Monitor"
*	Device		"Configured Video Device"
	DefaultDepth 24
		SubSection "Display"
		Depth 24
		Modes "1280x1024"
	EndSubSection
*EndSection
