### Post by corrytonapple on 2010-08-21
This might not be easy but here we go.
First open Rythembox and go to **Music>Import Folder. **Go to where your music is stored, which is on your Windows partion of the Hard Drive, then follow this path:
**Users>Your User Name>Music>iTunes>iTunes Media>Music>** and control click the folders of music you want, which is organized like  Artist Name, then Album, and then the Songs in that album.
