### Post by spiderbatdad on 2009-06-21
well open suse uses the linux kernel and many programs individually licensed under gpl, but also many programs that contain the open suse trademark.So my under standing is you could repackage and redistribute any part of open suse except those files specifically bearing open suse's trademark.
