### Post by Mark_in_Hollywood on 2008-11-07
In OO Word Processor did you click the box named:

Tools

Gallery?

I had to click the box and then that opened a box with the clipart in it.

If this does NOT solve your problem, reboot, selecting recover mode (ESC) and when you are offered the choices, select:

dpkg

allow that to run. If you get to a line that reads

username@computer-name:~$

type

shutdown -r now

and press the enter key. All is well and the 'puter will reboot. Then try selecting the "gallery" as I said above.
