Hello and Welcome  

Ubuntu 10.04 was based on Gnome 2 which was discontinued by Gnome developers. The desktop was revived in by the Mate desktop project and and is used with many distributions including Ubuntu-Mate. The future of Edubuntu is uncertain  because there is only two developers/maintainers left. The software _should _be available in the Ubuntu repositories, however if you installed it on mate you would not have the look and feel of Edubuntu.
