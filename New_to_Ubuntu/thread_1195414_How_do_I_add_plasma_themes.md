### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2009-06-23
Well if you are really desperate to add the theme you can unhide the kde/kde4 folder (depending on the version of Ubuntu or linux you use)
unzip your plasma theme
copy its folder to /home/(yournamehere)/.kde4/share/apps/desktoptheme
