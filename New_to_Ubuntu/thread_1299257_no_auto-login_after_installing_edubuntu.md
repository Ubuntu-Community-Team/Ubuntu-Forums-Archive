DId you use an alternate install CD or a LiveCD?  I know Ubuntu 9.10 beta gives the option, although Edubuntu might give a few differences.

On Ubuntu (this may or may not work on Edubuntu, but feel free to try), go to System, Administration, Log-in Screen, click unlock, type password, then check "Log in as [user] automatically, replacing [user] with your username.
