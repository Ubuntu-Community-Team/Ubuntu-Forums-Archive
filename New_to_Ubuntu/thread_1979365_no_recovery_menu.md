### Post by FilterSelect on 2012-05-13
I'm guessing that on the install you put in your windows password, or skipped right past it. Either way, you'll be needing a good password, which you haven't set.

My only advice is (since you installed just yesterday) to re-install Ubuntu and make sure you've picked a good password. When I installed 12.04, I also checked the 'encrypt home folder' option, which I suggest you do too.

It might be a pain to do and take a few minutes, but you might have to chalk it up to learning and go ahead with the reinstall.

Good luck!
