### Post by gjoellee on 2008-10-18
In Add/Remove  enable "All Available Applications" then search for "Art" and find "Art Manager".

Install Art Manager.

Now go to System->Preferences->Art Manager

In Art Manager you will have some tabs in the tabs go to Art->Other Themes->Splash Screen. The download and the preparing of the download may take a while...

When the download is finished select the splash screen you want and click on "Install". A new window should open soon. Select the splash you want and click on "Activate". Next time you log in the splash will be showed (in GNOME it is not as good as in KDE).

NOTE: It may take a while before the splash goes away, but just ignore it when you know your computer is ready. It will go away very soon.... :D
