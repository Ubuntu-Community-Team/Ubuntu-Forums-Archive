### Post by CatKiller on 2011-07-21
> **Andavane said:**
> Now what exactly do I _do_ (type) to get the fraction? :confused:

Press-and-release on the Compose key. Then 7. Then 8. (I checked that the Caps Lock/GEdit combo definitely works, and it does.)

Some of the other methods for getting different symbols need you to hold keys down, which makes for some tricky stretching. The Compose key doesn't.
