### Post by RomeReactor on 2009-02-14
Hi. If you want another multimedia player with an equalizer, try [VLC]("http://www.videolan.org/vlc/") (it's in the repositories). If you want a music player, try [Beep Media Player]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Beep_Media_Player"), [GXmms2]("http://www.xmms.org/"), [Aqualung]("http://aqualung.factorial.hu/"), or [Amarok]("http://amarok.kde.org/"). All these are available through Synaptic. Here are [a few more options]("http://linuxappfinder.com/multimedia/audiocdplayers").

To disable visualizations in Totem, go to its preferences and on the "Display" tab, uncheck the Visual Effects box. Totem doesn't have a 'mini' mode, but if you open it and you're just listening to audio, and the window is too big, press the **1** (one) key above the **Q** key in your keyboard, and it will resize. Not sure about Gxine, though.
