### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2008-12-18
F11 might not be the right key.  Most modern computers have a key you can hit on startup that will create a "boot menu" which will let you select a device to boot to.  On Dell machines it's usually F12.  On a lot of generic motherboards it is F11 or F8.

If you can't find it, you might have to go into your system settings (also called BIOS or CMOS) and set the CD to be the first boot device.

If you're having trouble, post the make and model of your laptop and we'll try to find out for you.
