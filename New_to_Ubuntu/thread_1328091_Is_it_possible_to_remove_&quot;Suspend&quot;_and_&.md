### Post by diablo75 on 2009-11-16
I'm running 9.04 and have the quick-switch menu on my upper panel, which shows my name next to a status icon.  It is what I click on the access a shutdown menu.  I would like to remove "Suspend" and "Hibernate" from the list of options available in this menu, so that Log Out, Restart and Shutdown are the only options available.  The reason for this is because it takes less time for the computer cold-boot than it does to wake up from a suspend/hibernation.  Also, it will ensure I don't click on one of these other options by accident.
