### Post by -kg- on 2010-06-09
OK:

#1 - Do you have another set of headphones you could try to see if you have the same problems?

#2 - What software are you using to generate whatever sound you're trying to play.  Many times the software (like Rhythm Box, VLC, etc.) will have a volume control independent of System Volume.  This includes browser players.  Make sure you have full volume on those controls, as well.

#3 - Also, pull up your Sound Preferences (either "System/Preferences/Sound" or from the Volume Control Applet/"Sound Preferences"), select the "Output" tab, and make sure the volume is at max there, too, especially the headphone volume.
