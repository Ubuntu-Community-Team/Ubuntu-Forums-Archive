### Post by nandemonai on 2009-05-25
Sounds like you're trying to cram a PCI-E card in a PCI slot.

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PCI_Local_Bus](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PCI_Local_Bus)

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PCI_Express](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PCI_Express)

Note the slight differences with the slots themselves.

After looking at the specs for the machine in question, it does not have a PCI-E slot. Only 2 PCI slots.

You'll be hard pressed to find a decent PCI VGA card these days though they do still exist.
