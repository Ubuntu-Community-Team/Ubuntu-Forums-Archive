### Post by frank002 on 2008-09-09
Open your Home folder , click view and mark the View Hidden Files box. There should be a folder named .mozilla. Open it and see if your bookmarks and extensions folders are there. If your bookmarks folder is there, you should be able to import the bookmarks into Firefox. I don't know what to do about the extensions. You might have to download and install them again.  I hope this helps. Good luck.
