As long as it says: USB HDD then that's the one.

On the other hand, your USB might NOT be bootable. What tool you used to create that LiveUSB? Try to re-create it again but first format it. Or you could try it on another machine just to make sure everything is fine.

By the way, I hope you checked [MD5SUM]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM") for your downloaded iso file :)
