### Post by Bucky Ball on 2011-06-08
> **hanzj said:**
> nzjethro,

Thanks for confirming that $360 is a good price. But is this Toshiba laptop the best laptop one can buy for $360 all-inclusive? 8-)

Guess it all comes down to exactly what you want to use it for. Before I buy a machine (or select the parts to build one) I note down what I am intending to do with it. This kinda dictates what machine you are going to need; powerful CPU, a heap of RAM, blue-ray. What will you need?

If you are surfing the net and checking email you could probably get something cheaper. If you are going to want to play around with GIMP and 3D games and the like, you are going to need something with some grunt. 

Make that list. Selecting the 'best' machine for the money is, for me, a rather lengthy process and requires much research. But that Tosh looks fine for the price.  ;)
