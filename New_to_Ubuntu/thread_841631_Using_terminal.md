### Post by Hospadar on 2008-06-26
you can generally copy all the lines in at once, and it will just execute the next line as soon as the previous line is finished.  It's probably a good idea to do them one at a time so you can keep your eyes out for errors and such and to help you better understand what you're doing, but it generally won't hurt to paste in multiple lines at once if they are all accurate (i.e. don't need to be altered to include a specific file path or username)
