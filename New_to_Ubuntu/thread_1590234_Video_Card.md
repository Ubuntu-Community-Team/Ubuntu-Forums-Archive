### Post by Locke_99GS on 2010-10-07
GeForce 9500GT is a very low priced, not too old, midrange card. Name brand versions of this card (Such as from EVGA or XFX) can be had for ~$50 or less. This card (or something similar) would have no problems keeping up with most tasks. As an example of its capabilities: It is powerful enough that you can even do some modern gaming on it, given adequate video settings.
