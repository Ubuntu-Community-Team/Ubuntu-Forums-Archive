### Post by zeroseven0183 on 2010-02-08
I found the site: [http://screenlets.org/index.php/Facebook](http://screenlets.org/index.php/Facebook)

It says there:
> Start the screenlet.

A dialog will prompt you to log in to the application.
Switch to your web browser and log in.
Switch back to the screenlet and press OK.
If you want the screenlet to be able to update your status:

Click on your status.
Fill in your new status.
A dialog will prompt you to give access to the application.
Switch to your web browser and grant access.
Switch back to the screenlet and press OK.
