### Post by tjwoosta on 2008-05-19
hmm..

you could try ctrl-alt-backspace (that quits everything and brings you back to the login screen) but i dont know if it would help in your case where everything completely freezes up


also you could put a force quit icon on your gnome panel to force quit select applications when they are misbehaving (but if you cant move the mouse then this wont help either)

i had this same problem awhile back when using gutsy 32bit on my 64bit laptop but i ended up just removing the 32bit and installing 64bit and had no more problems (i dont know if you have a 64bit machine or not, or if your problem is even related to the problem i had, but just reinstalling ubuntu may solve this problem) 

then again its alot of effort to reinstall your os and start over and it may not even actually solve your problem
