### Post by lukjad on 2009-03-09
When you download files and create folders, it is a good idea to put them in your home folder (*/home/YourUserName/*).

Well, if you wish to change the background, download the image and save it to a folder (I call mine backgrounds). Then right click the desktop  where there are no icons and select *Change Desktop Background*. 

For themes, go to *System -> Preferences -> Appearance* and and click *Install Theme*. Then drill your way to the folder that you downloaded the theme to (I just call mine Themes) and click on it then click *Open*. 
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuEyeCandy](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuEyeCandy)
