### Post by reader4 on 2009-12-26
> **uberlube said:**
> also if someone could tell us how to use the mic thats in it, would be appreciated.

you probably need to setup your input to read from the webcam microphone.  

Applications > Sound and Video > Sound Recorder
File > Open Volume Control
Make sure the hardward is registered on the "Hardware" tab. For me, it says "0807 Analog Mono Input"
Then make sure that device is selected from the "Input" tab.  You should see the volume bar moving in that tab when making noise.

Now use Sound Recorder to verify you can record the sound input.  Should work with Skype after that.
