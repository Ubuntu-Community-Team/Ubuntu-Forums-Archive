### Post by mgranet on 2008-08-24
Right click on the folder you want to open, then select Properties. You will see a little wrench icon across from the "TYPE   folder" text. Click on that. A window will pop up. Make sure Dolphin (or Konqueror) is on top of the Applications Preference order.
