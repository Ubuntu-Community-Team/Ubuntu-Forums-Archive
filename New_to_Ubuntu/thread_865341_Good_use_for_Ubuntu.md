### Post by candtalan on 2008-07-20
The size and weight of the laptop will be relevant when travelling too. If the laptop gets stolen you loose all your stuff. RAW files are very large, but I guess you need that. The large storage needed might keep you using a hard drive (not flash?) in the laptop. What about some backup? The files are too large to upload I guess. 

The Laptop can get damaged and anyway will be an attractive target for a thief. A separate backup with a bus powered usb hard drive, pocket size, might be worth considering. It is 'backup' and can in principle stay in your pocket  not your room or bags.

Good luck
