### Post by MooPi on 2010-06-20
Let it cool down and pop in the Install CD and choose memtest. This will check your memory modules for errors. Also while it runs check your laptop for heat or excessive heat. Clogged vents and exhaust ports for fans can cause shutdowns and lockups.
