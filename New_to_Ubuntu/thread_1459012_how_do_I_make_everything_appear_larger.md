### Post by Seq on 2010-04-20
[LIST=1]
[*]You can change the font DPI in the appearance preferences
System > Preferences > Appearance > Fonts > Details
[*]You can change your screen resolution. This will probably make everything look like crap if you are using an LCD.
[*]In firefox specifically, CTRL+ and CTRL- change the zoom level. Doesn't help for everything else, though (but handy for websites with obscenely small text).
[/LIST]

Myself, I adjust the DPI, though I only change it to what my screen reports in `xdpyinfo | grep dots`. For me, that is 110, which is bigger than the 96 it defaulted to.
