### Post by Paqman on 2008-09-17
Folders and files can be bookmarked from within the file manager, they'll then show up in the places menus. Drag and drop works for this, too. Gnome is quite happy to drag and drop most things to and from pretty much anywhere.

You can also edit the menus to add new launchers, just right click on the menu.

### Post by northern lights on 2008-09-18
In "System > Preferences > Main Menu" you can drag & drop launchers. Also pre-existing ones.

Granted, you can't move a default launcher, it will be copied. Still after copying you can uncheck the default one.
