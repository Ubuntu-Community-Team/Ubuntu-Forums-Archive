### Post by PhilGil on 2012-02-15
There are lots of compatible laptops out there, so if you want suitable suggestions you should really give people some idea of your criteria.

- Large screen, ultra portable or in-between?
- Extra durable (are you a student who will be throwing your laptop in a backpack)?
- Do you need extra-long battery life?
- Will you be using the laptop for gaming or office/web surfing?

You won't be able to get everything in a $600 laptop, but you should be able to find one that meets your most important criteria. Also, [techbargains.com]("http://www.techbargains.com") and [fatwallet.com]("http://www.fatwallet.com") are a couple of good websites for finding laptop deals.
