### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-12-29
What version of Ubuntu are you using? I already have what you are asking for in Ubuntu 12.10.

Open the Dash Applications lens and click Filter Results and we get a menu style grouping of applications. It will even include applications from the Ubuntu Software Centre. By clicking Sources we can limit the search to Local Storage or expand the search to include Software Centre.

The same method of filtering is there in the Music, Photos and Video lenses.
