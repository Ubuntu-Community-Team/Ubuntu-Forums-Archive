### Post by caljohnsmith on 2008-10-01
I agree, I think Google is better than the ubuntuforum.org search engine in most cases. Here are some other ideas that I use:
```
site:ubuntuforums.org -intitle:archive intitle:solved (8.04 OR Hardy) grub error 17
```
I really dislike reading archived threads, so I put the "-intitle:archive" to exclude them. Also, I start with "intitle:solved", because that gives you all the threads that have been marked as solved; if I don't find a solution this way, I remove the "intitle:solved" and look through all related threads. Also, it is helpful to put the Ubuntu version you are using usually, and the "(8.04 OR Hardy)" means it will look for either. If you decide not to include your Hardy version, it is often helpful to put a "2008" in the search terms so that you get the most recent posts, and not old posts from earlier years; you can always take it out if your search results don't return anything useful.
