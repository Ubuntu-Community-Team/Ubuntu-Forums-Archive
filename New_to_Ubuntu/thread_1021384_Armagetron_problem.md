### Post by aotf01 on 2009-01-01
I had the problem with Armagetron Advanced minimizing each time when opening it. The problem started after installing my NVIDIA driver. Turns out the game was set up in 640x480 mode and would not stay full screen with a screen resolution of 1024x768.

My solution:
Change resolution to 800x600. Hit apply but do not hit OK to the 15 second "Confirm ModeSwitch" prompt. 
This will give you 15 seconds to get into Armagetron and change your display settings to turn "Full Screen" off or play with the games resolution settings.

I also read it may have something to do with the screen saver and the solution was to disable the screensaver. Doing this worked for me once but then it did not work anymore for some reason.
