### Post by anpufeng on 2009-12-09
open the terminal
go to edit-> profile preferences
under the color tab, unckeck the "use system colors" option, which is enabled by default
then, you can select one of the schemes  or make your own combination of text and background color.

### Post by MooPi on 2009-12-09
I'm going to assume you're using Gnome-terminal for my description. On the terminal window upper bar are 5 menu's, File Edit, View, Terminal,Help. Click on the Edit tab and scroll down to  Profile Preferences. Under preferences are your color and background selections . Mix and match and get down and dirty with some color my Linux brother.
