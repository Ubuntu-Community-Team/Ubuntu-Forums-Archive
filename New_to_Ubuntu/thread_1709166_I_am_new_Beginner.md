### Post by steveneddy on 2011-03-17
**Upper left**

Applications - self explanatory? click around

Places - folders in your Home folder and a link to the hard drive

System - You can change settings for various systems and administer the system here

**Lower left** - show Desktop button - just like Windows

**Lower right** - all four desktops and a trash can

**Upper right** - log in and out - check mail - volume control - wireless access or ethernet connection info - click around

Remember how you figured out to use Windows? You clicked everything and played with it - same thing here - the learning curve will be steep for a few days - questions - ask in the forums

welcome
