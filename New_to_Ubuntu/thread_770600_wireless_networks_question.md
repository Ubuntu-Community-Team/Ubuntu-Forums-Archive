### Post by mar\/in on 2008-04-27
I'm a total n00b myself, so you might be having the same problem I did a couple of days ago.  In the default Ubuntu panel at the upper right of the screen is a network icon.  Assuming your wifi drivers installed OK, it will probably be in roaming mode and will already have detected your local wireless network.  But you won't know this unless you click it.  And that click has to be a LEFT mouse click, not the right-click for context menu that Windows has trained so many of us to assume is the default.

In my case it took me several hours of breaking my wifi settings by fiddling with the manual setup controls before I discovered the trick.#-o
