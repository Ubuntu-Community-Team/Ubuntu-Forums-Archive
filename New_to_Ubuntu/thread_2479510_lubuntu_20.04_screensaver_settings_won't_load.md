### Post by ne29914 on 2022-09-27
Do you get this:
"The XScreensaver doesn't seem to be running..."?
Then it's probably already disabled.

Otherwise go to Preferencs -> LXQt Settings -> Session Settings, select Autostart and untick XScreensaver.
