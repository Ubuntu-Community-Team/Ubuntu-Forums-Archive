### Post by AtrejuT on 2008-06-26
Do you have wobbly windows enabled? Then you are experiencing Bug#82654
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/compiz/+bug/82654](https://bugs.launchpad.net/compiz/+bug/82654)

A sort of easy work around: you can move a window by pressing alt and clicking the left mouse button anywhere in the window. That should be easier than moving the panel and such...
