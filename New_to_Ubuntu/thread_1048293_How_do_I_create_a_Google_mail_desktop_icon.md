### Post by RyanVanDiemen on 2009-01-23
> **a.v.l said:**
> How do I create a Google mail desktop icon to access email please?

The best thing to do would be to create link to URL address [http://www.googlemail.com](http://www.googlemail.com) 

Than if you double-click on it (or single-click in Kubuntu) it opens-up in your default browser.

You can still set-up it as homepage in one of your browsers and it would open-up when you start the browser. 

One more option comes to my mind. You can set-up Thunderbird or Evolution with your google mail account and even put the notification icon in the taskbar.
