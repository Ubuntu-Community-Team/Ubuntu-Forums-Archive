I recently installed 8.10 on an old Dell Optiplex GLX150; 800mhx PIII, 20gb HD, 512MB RAM. This PC has an Intel82801ba audio card built in, which does work, for the most part. 

Problem is, the volume control in the panel does not work. If I open the mixer control, I can operate volume with the PCM slider or with the headphone slider. The "Main" slider does not work, nor does mute.

Is there a better driver for this card? Or, failing that and probably easier, is there a way for me to put the PCM slider on the panel, instead of a volume control for "Main"? I want my young sons to be able to raise and lower the volume easily, especially when using the headphone jack.

### Post by roccivic on 2009-02-24
Right-click on the picture of the speaker in the panel and select properties.
In the menu that will pop-up choose the channel that you want to control with main slider in the panel.

Enjoy.
