### Post by tjwoosta on 2011-02-12
I dont think UF offers ssl. If you want to be secure on public networks, the best thing to do is just ssh tunnel to your home network.

{your netbook} -->encrypted tunnel through unsecure connection--> {your home network} --> internet


Otherwise its easy for other people on the public connection to sniff your packets and retrieve passwords and login info and such.
