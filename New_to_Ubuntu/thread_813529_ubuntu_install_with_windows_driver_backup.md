### Post by Fuminsho on 2008-05-30
You can go to Dell.com and enter your computer builds service Tag number and it will pull up all the drivers that you will need to reinstall and get your system back up and running. It's a free service and all YOU need is a Win CD or your original with your Key code.

It's rather easy, just time consuming and don't forget the basics, like adding adobe, java, winzip, Flash, and quick time. (I add those to all the builds I do for myself and other people)

OR 

1 You could buy a bigger HD and do one of two things. "clone" your HD and then add Ubuntu(dual boot), saving the original for backup.

2 Just use the new HD to install Ubuntu and use it, saving the old drive just incase you want to revert back to windows.



And Ubuntu will work out of the box, but you will have to search for specific drivers.

The new HD is easy quick and you can pick one up a low price that will not kill your buget or walet.
