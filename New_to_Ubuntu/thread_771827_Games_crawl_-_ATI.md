### Post by ElseIf on 2008-04-28
Woo! :) I got it working! Apparently XGL was no longer required, but I didn't realize that, so that was my problem. 

In case any other newb has the problem, this is what I did:

```
sudo apt-get remove xserver-xgl

```
Then I typed Ctrl+Alt+Backspace, and logged back in again.

Went to the terminal, typed in:
```
fglrxinfo
```

And it listed the ATI driver, rather than the Mesa one. Now I'm getting 120FPS on Sauerbraten. :)

Thank you so much for your time, everyone!!!
