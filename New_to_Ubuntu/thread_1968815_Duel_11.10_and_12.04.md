### Post by Zukaro on 2012-04-29
Yes.  When you're installing the second OS just choose "Install Alongside Ubuntu 11.10".  If you don't have that option you'll have to make the partitions manually (but it's unlikely that it wont work).

As for it being temporary, you'll have to format the partition 12.04 is on when you want to remove it; you may also need to fix the grub bootloader (not sure).  If you have to fix grub there's a tool for that which you can download and run from the LiveCD (I forget the name however).  I think it's called FixBoot or GrubRepair, something like that.
