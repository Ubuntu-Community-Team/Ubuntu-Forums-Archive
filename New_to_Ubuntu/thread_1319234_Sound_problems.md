### Post by jbrown96 on 2009-11-08
When you open the sound mixer (in system tray), are there multiple channels? If not, you need to go into the options and enable some more channels. It's a good idea to have master, pcm, front, headphone, speaker, and microphone enabled. Don't be worried if all of those aren't available; they differ based on your hardware. Speaker doesn't have a slider, it's just a mute checkbox. I'm guessing that it somehow got checked.
