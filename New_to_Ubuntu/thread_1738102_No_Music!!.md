### Post by khelben1979 on 2011-04-24
Use [alsamixer]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alsamixer") to check the volume meters, you may need increase the volume from there.

Other than that, the software haveto be configured so the music can use your sound chip/card to output the sound. So if you're thinking about using regular audio cd's, the first step could be to attach a headphone directly to the DVD drive to know if you hear anything in them.

I don't know if you ever tried [Grip]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grip_%28software%29"), but it works excellent if you want to listen to audio cd's.
