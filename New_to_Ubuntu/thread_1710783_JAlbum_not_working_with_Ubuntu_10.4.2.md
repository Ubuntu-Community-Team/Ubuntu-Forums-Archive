### Post by Don Bowen on 2011-03-20
mystery solved.

The customer had asked for one of the photos in Black and white... I used GIMP to convert it to gray scale and included it in the album in JAlbum....
The black and white photo generates the error!
Remove it, and the error goes away.

I guess the JAlbum folks didn't guess folks would still want anything in black and white.
