### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-03-20
Do you want to talk about software that is "server" or hardware that is "server?" One or the other or it will get confusing and this is already a confusing subject.

I as understand it the meaning of "cloud" is banks of computers used as servers for hire without particular machines being dedicated to particular customers. In this way if a machine breaks down the customer's work load is switched to other machines.

Again, as I understand it, the traditional view of servers was of banks of computers used as servers but owned or hired by a company but dedicated to the use of that customer. 

The meaning of the word "cloud" has become as insubstantial as the literal clouds are. And its use is a foggy as low flying cloud.
