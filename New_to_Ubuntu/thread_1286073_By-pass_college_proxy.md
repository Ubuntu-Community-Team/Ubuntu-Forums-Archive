### Post by scorp123 on 2009-10-08
> **Captain Flowers said:**

If you get caught you will very likely face consequences ... like being kicked out of that school maybe? Is your future really worth that risk? NO. It isn't. You probably have an Internet connection at home as well? If not: get one. At home you can do what you want. But at work or at school you better follow the rules.

If you really really need to access certain web sites even while at school ... You could get one of those 3G/UMTS USB modems? I have one for exactly this reason. I am often at customer sites and they have proxies too. So when I need to download something I simply use my 3G USB stick and voila ... I can download what I need and yet the admins won't get mad at me. Just be aware that there are monthly costs (comparable to mobile phones) ... but if that isn't a problem then maybe such 3G USB stick would be the best idea here.
