### Post by _0R10N on 2012-06-18
If you're refering to autocomplete fields in mail clients, this auto-complete task is almost always performed by some script running on the mail client. In other kind of pages, like registration sites, it uses information collected by your browser itself. For this second case, you could try deleting the autocomplete information  gathered by the application.
