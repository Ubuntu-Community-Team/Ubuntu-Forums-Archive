### Post by Tekagirl on 2009-03-13
Hi.
I had a similar problem of not being able to find the info I thought I had synced w/ my palm via gnome-pilot.  For example, I was trying to get my contacts from evolution onto my palm & nothing seemed to be happening.  Eventually, I went to the conduits tab in gnome-pilot, clicked on e-address.  Then I clicked settings button, and under "one time action" selected "copy to PDA" & ta-da!  When I hotsynced, all of my contacts appeared.  Don't know for sure if this exactly solves the problem you were speaking to, but thought if treesurfer's advice didn't help, this might be another idea for you to try.  
Good luck!
