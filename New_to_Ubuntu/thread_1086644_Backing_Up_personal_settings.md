### Post by jaminux on 2009-03-04
If you have made a /home partition you can simply re-install, using the current home as the new one (don't format it!). This should keep a lot of your settings intact.

Alternatively you can just archive your home folder and restore it after you re-install.

Most programs keep the settings for you in the home folder (lots are hidden, press ctr. h to view them), simply retaining these should keep your settings.
