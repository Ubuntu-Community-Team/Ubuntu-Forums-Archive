### Post by lavinog on 2010-10-13
10.04 uses a new login manager that isn't compatible with the old themes.
It isn't that they reduced the functionality, it is just that themeing hasn't been implemented yet.

You can apparently use ubuntu tweak to change the background:
[http://www.ubuntugeek.com/how-to-install-ubuntu-tweak-in-ubuntu-10-04lucid-lynx.html#more-5496](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/how-to-install-ubuntu-tweak-in-ubuntu-10-04lucid-lynx.html#more-5496)

There is also this workaround, but I haven't tried it:
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=9516807&postcount=14](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=9516807&postcount=14)
