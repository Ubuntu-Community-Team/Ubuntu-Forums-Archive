### Post by TheNad on 2009-06-23
The only thing I can think of off the top of my head is this:

Right click on the sound icon (top right of your screen) > Open Volume Control > Preferences (bottom right of the new window that opened). Check all the tracks in the new window that are listed as playback (should include Master, PCM, etc.) then close that window and in the volume control window make sure all the new tracks are unmuted and turned all the way up. Now play something to test. If that fixes it, awesome! Otherwise, got no clue. Good luck!
