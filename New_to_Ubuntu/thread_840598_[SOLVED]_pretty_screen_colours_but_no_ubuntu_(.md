### Post by phidia on 2008-06-25
Possibly your graphics card is not being detected. You can try the safe graphics mode from the boot menu (which is low rez I believe) and if you get to a desktop there you can try to set up better graphic support. You/we need to know your video card and monitor model/maker though.
