### Post by elliotcroft on 2011-05-02
Do you mean web pages?
Unity is a bit more demanding on resources than Gnome and also Wubi doesn't allow Ubuntu to reach it's full speed potential.
Try changing your desktop interface back to Gnome (the menu on the login screen, bottom right corner I believe) and seeing if that makes things go faster, if they do, then it might mean that your computer isn't completely ready for Unity.

### Post by corncob on 2011-05-02
