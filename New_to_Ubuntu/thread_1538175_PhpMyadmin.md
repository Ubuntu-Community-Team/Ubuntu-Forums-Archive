### Post by Paddy Landau on 2010-07-25
All I can think of is these:

[LIST=1]
[*]Does your IE access other pages on the Internet? (I.e., check your IE connectivity.)
[*]Find the page in Firefox, then cut-and-paste the URL into IE to ensure that you haven't accidentally mistyped something.
[*]Look at the proxy settings in Firefox, and check whether IE has a different proxy setting.
[*]In IE, clear the cache and retry.
[*]If they are on different computers or different operating systems, check the firewall settings on the one with IE. Maybe temporarily turn it off when testing on IE (but remember to turn it on again quickly).
[*]If they are on different routers, check the firewall settings on the router that serves the computer using IE.
[/LIST]
HTH
