### Post by FormatSeize on 2011-07-11
That's about as easy as it is going to get. Its not that it's particularly hard, but rather it's thorough. Once you get going, you will find that it's not as difficult as it seems at first glance.
 
Additionally, you really have to try hard to mess up an install. Windows will take you through step by step, and minimize the possible avenues by which you can screw up. If you installed Ubuntu, you should have no trouble installing Windows next to it. Just give it a try as opposed to looking at the guide without having a reference (your computer in installation mode) to look at.
