### Post by asmoore82 on 2008-11-04
they are likely to be in a "hidden" folder in your home folder named ".BasKet"
Open "Places -> Home" and hit "View -> Show Hidden Files" and see what you find.

P.S. no need to worry about re-hiding files, it will only affect that one nautilus "file browser" window
