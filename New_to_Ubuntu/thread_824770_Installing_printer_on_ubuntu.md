### Post by wpshooter on 2008-06-10
You do not need/use the windows version of the driver.

If you go to System/Administration/Printer, you should be able to walk thru the steps in setting up your HP printer for your Ubuntu O/S.  The Linux driver for your printer is most likely already built into the installed Ubuntu O/S.

Good luck.
