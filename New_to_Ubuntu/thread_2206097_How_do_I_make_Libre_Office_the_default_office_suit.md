### Post by History Teacher on 2014-02-17
Is there a way I can make Libre Office the default office suite in Lubuntu?  I'd like to replace Abi word with Libre Office.  Thanks!

---

### Post by SeijiSensei on 2014-02-17
First, install Libre Office with "sudo apt-get install libreoffice" or pick it from your GUI package manager.

Now right-click on a document you wish to open with, say, LO Writer.  Choose "Open With" from the menu and point it to Writer.  You should also be able to check a box to declare Writer as the default application for this type of file.
