### Post by Krytarik on 2012-01-28
> **AlvitrValkyrie said:**
> 10.04: Killed gnome panel in gconf, want it back.
This should generally revert your system to run Gnome Panel on login:
```
gconftool-2 --unset /desktop/gnome/session/required_components_list
gconftool-2 --unset /desktop/gnome/session/required_components/panel
```... but it won't fix your new issue with the launchers/applets, of course; for that, first relogin, and if you are still getting errors then, reset your panels to their defaults with this method:
```
gconftool-2 --recursive-unset /apps/panel
killall gnome-panel
```Regards.
