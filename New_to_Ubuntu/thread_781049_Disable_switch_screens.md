### Post by Hamchan on 2008-05-04
If you mean that you just want one workspace, simply right click on the workspace switcher.

This will let you choose how many workspaces you want in rows and columns. Just choose one row and one column. That should get rid of all but one workspace.

### Post by Bill magee on 2008-05-04
Meatlegs, you can disable aspects of the touchpad in system-preferences-mouse. Specifically, disable verticle and horizontal scrolling. I think you will find that you can have multiple workspaces again.
