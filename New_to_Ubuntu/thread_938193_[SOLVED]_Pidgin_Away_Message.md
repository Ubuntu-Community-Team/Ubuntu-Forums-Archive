### Post by yhc4113 on 2008-12-09
This may be a little late but did you recently install MusicTracker?  I had this problem and if you went into Tools, MusicTracker, and clicked on Toggle Status Changing then it'll change your away message to the default Away after a few seconds (unless you're listening to music then it'll change it to the name of the song).  So if you just click on that again it should not change your away message anymore.
