### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-02-17
Inactivity = movement of mouse or pressing of keyboard and not activity on screen. Go to System Settings>Brightness and Lock> Turn screen off when inactive for>drop down menu.

You must have used this utility to change to default setting to one minute.
