### Post by Jon Monreal on 2009-11-22
Right click on your "Applications" menu and select "Edit Menus". On the left-side pane, go to "Universal Access". Click the checkbox next to "onBoard". Then, close the window. You can then access onBoard in Applications -> Universal Access -> onBoard.
