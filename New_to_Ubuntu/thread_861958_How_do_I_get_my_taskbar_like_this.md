### Post by MiniMe on 2008-07-17
To get the mac type dock on the bottom, there are a couple of choices but There are a couple of docks available but Avant Window Navigator is one that I like. After installing it, you can remove the bottom panel. 

To make the top panel more transparent, right click on it and select properties. Go to background and select solid colour and then you can use the slider to make the panel more transparent.

You can also install Mac OSX themes of which there are many. You'll probably be able to find a similar desktop background easily.

Cheers.
