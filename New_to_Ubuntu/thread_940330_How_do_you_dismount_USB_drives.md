### Post by seagullplayer77 on 2008-10-06
For any regular volume--a flash drive, a camera card, an external (or internal, for that matter) hard drive--you can just go to Places > Computer and right click the volume you want to dismount and click "Unmount volume." 

As for unmounting an external wireless card, I don't think you would have to--I think dismounting only applies to storage volumes. Don't quote me on that, but I'll say this: My wireless mouse receiver plugs into my USB port and I've unplugged it without any special procedure hundreds of times and never had a problem with it.
