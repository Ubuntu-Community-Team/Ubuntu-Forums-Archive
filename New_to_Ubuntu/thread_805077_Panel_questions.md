### Post by Inxsible on 2008-05-23
In gnome once you place the panel - lets say at the bottom of the screen, it should stay there. What do you mean by "freeze" ?

You can add a launcher to the panel and give it the command ```
nautilus /pathToFolderThatYouWantToOpenAsDefault
``` You can also give it a description and an icon.
