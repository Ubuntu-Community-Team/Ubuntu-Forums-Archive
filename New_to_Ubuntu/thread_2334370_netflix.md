### Post by deadflowr on 2016-08-18
Google-chrome.
Requires 14.04 or newer on a 64-bit system.
Works out of the box.

i guess there are variants also, like Opera and Vivaldi (but I don't use either on a normal enough basis, so cannot tell you how well they run netflix), but chrome most definitely works.

Firefox can run netflix currently by adding a special ppa:
[http://pipelight.net/cms/install/installation-ubuntu.html]("http://pipelight.net/cms/install/installation-ubuntu.html")
but in about a month or two, firefox will be able to play netflix out of the box as well:
[http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2016/08/firefox-49-linux-netflix-google-widevine-cdm]("http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2016/08/firefox-49-linux-netflix-google-widevine-cdm")

So, you have a few options
