### Post by LinuxFox on 2009-09-18
Go to the Adobe Flash download page and choose to download the .deb package.

[http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/?promoid=BUIGP](http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/?promoid=BUIGP)

It works if you're using Ubuntu 8.04, since that's what the site says.
