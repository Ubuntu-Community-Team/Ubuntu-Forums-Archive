### Post by MarkC502 on 2010-01-16
It might be that your sound is working but volume is down, try the following command in that new shiny terminal you found :-)

sudo alsamixer

And that will bring up a window with some sound level bars for all your inputs/outputs and you can use the arrow keys to go left and right and up and down to raise lower the volume levels.

Try that and make sure yours is turned up.
