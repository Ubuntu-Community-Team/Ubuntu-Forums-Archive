### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2014-05-14
Hi, welcome.

A wireless (RF, with dongle, NOT Bluethoot) keyboard or mouse are exactly the same as their wired (USB) counterparts, from the OS (any OS for this matter) "point of view". As such you don't have to worry about that, they all work.
Webcams are almost as easy. Most are generic and natively supported in the same way or better than in Windows. Any plug&play webcam in Windows will work the same way in any modern Linux distro.
