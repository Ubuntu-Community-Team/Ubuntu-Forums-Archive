### Post by cascade9 on 2010-09-16
You could try clearing the CMOS. The manual should be here- 

[http://www.foxconnchannel.com/product/Motherboards/detail_overview.aspx?ID=en-us0000188](http://www.foxconnchannel.com/product/Motherboards/detail_overview.aspx?ID=en-us0000188)

Go to the 'motherboard manual' link and then d/l it to find where the clearCMOS jumper is (or do it the 'dirty' way and just remove the battery). 

I'd agree with CharlesA, somethign is probably dead, but its worth a shot before you go buying new parts.
