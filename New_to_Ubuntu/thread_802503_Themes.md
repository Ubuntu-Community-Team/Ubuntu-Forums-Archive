### Post by jimv on 2008-05-21
Go to gnome-look.org
Look in the GTK 2.x section for themes
Download desired theme (should be a .tar.gz file)
Open Appearance control panel
Drag .tar.gz file into Appearance control panel
Theme is now installed.
