### Post by squakie on 2013-05-09
Even more important:  Does it come with Windows 8 pre-installed? if so , it will UEFI and probably have secure boot turned on  as well.  I cannot stress enough researching this BEFORE you begin.  Do a search on the forum for user oldfred - they are an expert in this and you should read the posts they have made regarding UEFI and Linux.  Be *SURE* to follow the links they post, as they contain some very important information.  Look closely for anything pertaining to HP, and also search the net for your model and installing Linux.  You can save yourself a LOT of trouble and hassle by doing this first - I've been there!
