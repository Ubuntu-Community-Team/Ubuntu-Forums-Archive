Hi,
There's an excellent troubleshooting guide at the start of the Multimedia and Video forum that'll give you a few ideas.  Basically though, right click the applet on the top bar and try changing your selected input in the volume control option; there are further options in preferences.  There's a separate set of options in System/Preferences/Sound that you may need to go to, but start with the simple stuff.
