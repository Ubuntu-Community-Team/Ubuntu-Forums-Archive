### Post by yeats on 2010-03-14
Nearly all the major distros have good support (forums and otherwise)...  If you like Ubuntu, you might try Linux Mint.  If you're looking for something different (but still GUI-oriented/user-friendly), maybe Fedora or OpenSUSE.  If you're looking for "do-it-yourself" distros, ArchLinux and Gentoo are good for learning the inner workings.
