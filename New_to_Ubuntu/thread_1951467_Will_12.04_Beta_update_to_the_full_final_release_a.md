### Post by marin123 on 2012-04-02
I'm a bit late, but I just want to point out that Alpha 1, Alpha 2, Beta 1, Beta 2 and Final Release are just names in a process of making Ubuntu, they are not releases of Ubuntu.

For example, if you have downloaded Ubuntu image few days before Beta 2, you could do an upgrade and get to Beta 2. It's just named to mark the milestones.
