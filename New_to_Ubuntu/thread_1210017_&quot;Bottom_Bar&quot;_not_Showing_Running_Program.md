While using my sister's machine, I noticed that there is a problem in her Ubuntu desktop. The bottom bar is not showing the programs that are currently running. When I talked to her about it, she told me that this has been happening for quite a while now and it has been a hassle because, whenever she was forced to minimize a window, she would have to press the "hide/show all programs" and fish it out instead of simply pressing the programs name at the bottom bar.

How can I fix this for her?

### Post by earthpigg on 2009-07-10
