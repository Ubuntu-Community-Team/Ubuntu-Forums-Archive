### Post by RomeReactor on 2009-01-05
Hi. See i your menu looks [like this]("http://shots.osdir.com/slideshows/791/12.gif"); if so, look in 'Settings->Autostarted Applications' or in 'Settings->Sessions and Startup Settings' to see if there's an entry for Ktorrent. If it's there, disable it.
