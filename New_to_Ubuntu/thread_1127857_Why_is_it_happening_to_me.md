To the OP: Calm down buddy, it'll be ok...

first of all, you shouldn't have to download java from the internet... second, if you are indeed getting errors, its way more helpful to the nice people here if you can give them specific information about what's causing the error, so they can better help you.  For example:

1. the specific application/package/etc (or list of the afore-mentioned items) that you are trying to download (i know java is just one, but what are the other things... if you're not sure, just post links to the sites where you're getting them so others may see for themselves)

2. the EXACT error that you are recieving. Write it down, copy-paste it, or take a screenshot (the 'Print Screen' button, then save and upload with the 'upload' buttons below) so people have a better idea of what exactly is happening.

3. Any other _relevent_ information that may helpful (such as other applications you had running at the time, how you're trying to run the problematic application, etc...) remember... be thurough... often the seemingly most obscure bits of data can be the most important.

I understand that errors can be annoying and stressful, but its just as stressful to people trying to help you when they can't make heads of tails of your problem because of insufficient info. I'm simply trying to be informative so you can be more easily helped in the future if it's required.

Finally, you said you were messing with WINE... why exactly are you messing with WINE? If you're trying to run a particular program, please specify what/why, because there may be an easier solution for you.
