Hotmail/Live mails work just fine with both Evolution and Thunderbird. For evolution, check [here]("http://pricklytech.wordpress.com/2010/05/01/ubuntu-10-4-lucid-configuring-evolution-to-connect-to-hotmail-windows-live-mail/") for the setup. For Thunderbird, just give your full email id, name and password and it will lookup the settings automatically. Personally, I would have chosen Evolution, except that I could not find a way for it to run in the background so that I get notified when a new email arrived. 

I know mail-notification can do that for me, however, I was not interested in another application for doing just that. Moreover, one of the dependencies were acting up.

Hope this help.
