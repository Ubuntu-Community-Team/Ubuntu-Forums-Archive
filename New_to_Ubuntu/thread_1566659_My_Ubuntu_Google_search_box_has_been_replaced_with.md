Are speaking about the home page or the quick search box in the right corner of the browser?

If it is the home page then go to Edit>Preferences under Home Page: type in [http://www.google.com](http://www.google.com)

If it is the quick search box in the right corner of the browser click the down arrow, click Manage Search Engines... and click Restore Defaults.
