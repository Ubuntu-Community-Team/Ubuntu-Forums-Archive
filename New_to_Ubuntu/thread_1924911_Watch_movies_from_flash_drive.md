### Post by HiImTye on 2012-02-13
[http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download)
go to option 2, click on 'USB Stick' and the OS of choice and follow the instructions.
make a folder called movies or storage on the disk to keep them in
viola!

actually you might need to add the required codecs, depending in the file format, but you can always convert them to a playable format.
best bet would be to add medibuntu's VLC or MPlayer (SMPlayer is a nice interface)
[http://www.debuntu.org/how-to-customize-your-ubuntu-live-cd](http://www.debuntu.org/how-to-customize-your-ubuntu-live-cd)
