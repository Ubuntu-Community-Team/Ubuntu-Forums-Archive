If you download a torrent file, right-click it, go to properties and then "open with". Pick azureus. Then it should be the default one.

### Post by victorgreen on 2008-05-09
Hardy doesnt come with Azureus preinstalled, 

sudo apt-get install azureus

After that, when you first download a .torrent, just right click it, hit properties, click the "open with" tab and select Azureus from the list.
