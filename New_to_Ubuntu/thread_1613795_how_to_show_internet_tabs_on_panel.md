### Post by karma101393 on 2010-11-04
I have ubuntu 10.04 and whenever I minimize internet tabs (firefox or google chrome) I can't get them back to maximum size! The little purple square in the bottom left corner doesn't re-maximize any of the windows and they don't show up on the panels. How can I make them show?

---

### Post by uRock on 2010-11-04
If these buttons aren't showing on the bottom panel, then right-click the panel, then click Add to Panel, then scroll to the bottom and double-click Windows List, then click the close button. The buttons should now be showing on the bottom for any open windows.[IMG]http://www.mikesplanet.net/forums/images/smilies/cool.png[/IMG]
