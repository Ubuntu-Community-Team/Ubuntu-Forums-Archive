### Post by Sef on 2010-01-03
For your needs, 9.04 would be fine, but I also would recommend 8.04 which is an LTS release.  (LTS = Long Term Support: 3 years desktop and 5 years server instead of the standard 18 months.)  The next LTS will be 10.04 which is being released in about 3 1/2 months.  You will be able to directly upgrade to it from 8.04.
