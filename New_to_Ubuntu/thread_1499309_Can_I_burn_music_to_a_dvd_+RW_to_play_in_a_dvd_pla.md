### Post by marshmallow1304 on 2010-06-01
Some DVD players have mp3 support.  You might want to check the manual for your DVD player to see what, if any, formats are supported..

To burn files to a DVD, you can just open the DVD drive in Nautilus, drag some files in, and click "Write to Disc".  This is the default option ("Open CD/DVD Creator") upon inserting a blank disc.
