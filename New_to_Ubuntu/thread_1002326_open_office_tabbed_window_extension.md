### Post by Juliehi on 2011-10-25
The [FONT=&quot]Office tabbed window&#8230; Do you mean that open multiple documents in a tabbed window for Microsoft Office? If so, I know an office add-in called [/FONT][Office Tab]("http://www.extendoffice.com/product/office-tab.html"). It allows you to open, edit and manage multiple documents within a single tabbed window. Just like the tabbed interface in web browsers such as Firefox, IE 7 and Chrome, Office Tab brings the tabbed concept to Office by adding a tab bar between the ribbon and workplace. Documents can be switched among tabs and double-clicking on the tab bar will create a new document.


  Amazing idea for office! If you like the tabbed window for your office, visit ExtendOffice.com, and you could find more.
