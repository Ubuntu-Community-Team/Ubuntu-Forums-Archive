### Post by papibe on 2011-01-12
I have a few .m4v files, and they are basically mp4 files. They have AVC video and AAC audio.

VLC, mplayer, smplayer and even Totem (with codecs) can play them. If you are still not able to play them try using mediainfo (from ppa) and post the results here.

### Post by Chronon on 2011-01-12
m4v just denotes an MP4 container containing a video stream.  It can have AVC or h.264 along with various audio formats (usually AAC-LC).
