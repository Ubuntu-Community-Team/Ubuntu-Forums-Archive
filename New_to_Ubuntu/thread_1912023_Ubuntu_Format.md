### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-01-19
Yeah, do a fresh install in his presence. Let him choose his own user name and password. Make sure the partitions are formatted. Then he will get a "raw" ubuntu as you call it and he will know how to do it himself if he messes things up. He will not call you so often to fix things.

Remember, give a man a fish and you feed him once. Teach a man to fish and you feed him for life. Give (sell) a friend a working computer and he will often call you to fix things. Teach him how to fix things and your life is your own.

Show him how to register on the forums and get help.

Regards.
