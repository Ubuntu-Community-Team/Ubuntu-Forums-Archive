### Post by tdrusk on 2009-03-25
Sometimes the audio slider is not up in the sound mixer or it may not be labeled correctly.

Open your Volume Control on the top right of the screen, and click preferences. Select all the possible output selections. Then go through and slide them all up so you can figure out which slider may activate your sound.

Sorry if this doesn't work.
