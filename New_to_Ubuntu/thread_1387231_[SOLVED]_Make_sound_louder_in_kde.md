### Post by Mariane on 2010-01-21
I just discovered something amazing. 
The sound was a whisper even with everything set to maximum. 

In Settings (the one below if you have two main menu options called "settings") choose "Sound & Multimedia" and then "Sound system". 

This opens a window and the first option (top left tick box) is "Enable the sound system". 

Untick this option (all the others become grey), click "Apply" and test your sound again. It's louder! 

This is not a joke, it really does work!
