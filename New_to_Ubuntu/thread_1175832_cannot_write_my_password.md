### Post by Mornedhel on 2009-06-01
Does a password prompt come up ? If nothing shows when you try to type in your password, it's because it's safer that way : harder for an onlooker  to count how many characters your password contains.

Try typing your password in blindly and pressing Enter.

### Post by spiceminesofkessel on 2009-06-01
When you type your password in at a command prompt, the characters will not show up. They won't even be masked. So, it will appear as if you're not typing anything, but you actually are. It's a security measure.
