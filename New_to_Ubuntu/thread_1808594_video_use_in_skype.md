### Post by Anuovis on 2011-07-20
What Marin said, it's one of those blue round buttons that you get in the small window with an avatar of the person you are calling. This window pops up when you call someone. 

I think Windows had an option to make a video call but here you make a general call (audio) and then turn on the camera. Or try the default "on" as Marin suggested.
