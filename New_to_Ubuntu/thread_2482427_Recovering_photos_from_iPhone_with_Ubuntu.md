### Post by MAFoElffen on 2023-01-03
No good news...

If it had been setup before forgetting the passcode, there is an app call KDE Connect, where you can transfer files from your iPhone to a Linux machine... But it would have had to have had the app installed from the Apple Store beforehand, and the Linux app installed... And it still requires interaction on the iPhone side to do it (would need the passcode).

Lots of people take comfort in the security of an iPhone... Until they are locked out of it. What I stress to users is backups. That doesn't help you now, but in the future, maybe backup your photos to iCloud, another machine, or to an external drive. (Lesson learned.)
