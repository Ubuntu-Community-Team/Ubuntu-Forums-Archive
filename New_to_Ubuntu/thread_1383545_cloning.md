### Post by HermanAB on 2010-01-17
Win7 typically occupies about 60GB of disk space.  Making a backup of that much crud is a waste of time.  Your best bet is to defrag the disk in Windows, then partition it down the middle, around the 100GB mark and then make Ubuntu dual boot with Win7.

However - be very careful.  At least try to backup the data to DVDs or that other disk before you start.
