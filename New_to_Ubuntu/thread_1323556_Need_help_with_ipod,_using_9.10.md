Just for the record, if anyone else is interested or has been looking.. I've found a GREAT replacement for iTunes!

"CopyTrans Manager" Works wonderfully! With it you can add CDs, or remove CDs from your ipod without having lose all your current data during syncing. Kinda nice, esp if you're like me and have a smaller hard drive (40gb) you don't have to keep a local copy of all your music and movies on your system to keep them on your ipod.
