Had same problem.  I went into Synaptic and searched for ADOBE and then deleted it.  Then, I went to a youtube video and clicked on it to play.  I gave me a message saying that I needed a plug-in to view it.  It gave me 2 options: GNASH and ADOBE.  I choose GNASH.  Not only did my video now work, but a DVD disc I had been trying to play finally worked, also!  Try it.
