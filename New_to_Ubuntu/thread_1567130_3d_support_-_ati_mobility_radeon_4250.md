### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-09-03
Yes, Ubuntu 10.04 will detect the Radeon and offer you drivers through the Hardware Drivers panel.  It will install the open-source drivers by default, but you'll have to use the Hardware Drivers panel to install the proprietary drivers.

You should also check in the Audio & Video subforum for posts there dealing with your card.
