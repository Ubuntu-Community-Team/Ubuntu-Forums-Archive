### Post by unutbu on 2009-01-31
Another way would be to download a SuperGrubDisk floppy: [http://www.supergrubdisk.org/index.php?pid=6](http://www.supergrubdisk.org/index.php?pid=6)
You can boot off of this floppy, and use the menu-driven system to reinstall GRUB to your hard drive.

And here you can find Herman's guide to using SuperGrubDisk:
[http://users.bigpond.net.au/hermanzone/SuperGrubDiskPage.html#How_To_Use_Super_Grub_Disk](http://users.bigpond.net.au/hermanzone/SuperGrubDiskPage.html#How_To_Use_Super_Grub_Disk)
