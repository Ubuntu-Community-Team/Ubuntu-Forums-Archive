### Post by Autodave on 2020-02-01
Have you tried leaving it to charge with the laptop off?  Do you have the indicator light on when you plug in the charger?  Have you tried another charger?  Your charger could have enough voltage to be recognized but not enough power to actually charge the battery.

This does not sound like an OS problem but rather a hardware problem.

### Post by MoebusNet on 2020-03-08
I had a similar issue with my old (2003) laptop. The battery didn't want to charge from 0% after sitting up for a few weeks unused. I turned the computer off and left it plugged in overnight, even though Power Manager said the battery was not charging. Next morning, *voila!* the battery read 100% charged.
