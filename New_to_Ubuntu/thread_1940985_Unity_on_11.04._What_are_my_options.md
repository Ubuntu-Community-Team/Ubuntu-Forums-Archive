### Post by PhantomTurtle on 2012-03-14
I found this howto on how to make gnome 3 fallback look like the gnome2 on omgubuntu. Link: [http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2011/12/how-to-make-ubuntu-11-10-look-and-feel-like-gnome-2/]("http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2011/12/how-to-make-ubuntu-11-10-look-and-feel-like-gnome-2/")

Also if you get Cinnamon then you don't have to go through the process of tweaking the gnome fallback session. Link: [http://cinnamon.linuxmint.com/]("http://cinnamon.linuxmint.com/")

This is of course if you have 11.10, but if you have 11.04 you can just switch to the gnome classic session.
