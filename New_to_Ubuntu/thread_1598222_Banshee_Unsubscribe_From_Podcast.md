I accidently added the wrong version of a podcast feed to Banshee. I don't see any option or menu to unsubscribe from it. How do I go about doing this (Banshee 1.80)

---

### Post by blazemore on 2010-10-18
Click on "Podcasts" on the left hand pane.

You'll see a list of the podcasts at the bottom-left.
If you see none, make sure you select "All Items", not just "New Items"

Right-click on the podcast and choose "unsubscribe and delete"
