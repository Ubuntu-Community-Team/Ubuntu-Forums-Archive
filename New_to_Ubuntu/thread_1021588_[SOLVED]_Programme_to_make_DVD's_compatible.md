### Post by nhasian on 2008-12-25
yes thats right DeVeDe just makes the ISO.  I think by default it makes the disc images in the home folder, but you can put it anywhere.  i like to make them in /home/username/tmp then i can burn the images with Brasero.  Applications->Sound&Video->Brasero.

if you have trouble reading the dvds you made in a dvd player, try burning them at the minimum speed of the media or try a different brand of media.
