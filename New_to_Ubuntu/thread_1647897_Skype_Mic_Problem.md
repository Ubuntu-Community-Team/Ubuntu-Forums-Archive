### Post by thane1 on 2011-01-02
Have you tried pavucontrol?  Available at least for ubuntu through the synaptic repos.  Its a timing thing once pavucontrol is installed.  Open up Terminal and type in pavucontrol but don't click on enter yet.  Open your Skype and enter Skype Test Call.  Once the test call connects, click on enter in Terminal to open pavucontrol.  Go through the options on the gui, that opens up, to set your mic sound to (probably) the mic on your webcam.  Cheers.
