### Post by bobblehat on 2008-05-18
I think you may be misinterpreting the output. I believe it's telling you your home contains 4.1GB which is 60% of the total of all your usage, not 60% of available space.

For instance, mine says 99% but that's because my home has 388GB of files in it out of 392 total.
