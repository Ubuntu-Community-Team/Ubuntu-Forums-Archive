### Post by perlluver on 2009-12-19
> **Gulfvet91 said:**
> My son's account on my computer is having some problems.  One is that his access menu bar (the one with Applications and Places and all the icons at the right) at the top of the screen is gone.    We've searched and searched and we can't find how to bring it back.  Any suggestions?

Right click on the bottom bar, then select add new panel.  Once you have the new panel, right click on that panel, then select add to panel.  Once inside of there, add menu bar, time, and anything else you need on that panel.
