### Post by Temposs on 2009-05-21
I'm pretty sure you're not going to get WINE to work for a driver. It's just not made to do that. You need to find a way to get your specific wireless card to work with linux, which has a different driver architecture than Windows. Or, you need to get a new wireless card that works with Ubuntu already.

You should find out the exact model of your wireless card and then search for how people have gotten it to work with Linux already.

EDIT: You can also find out your exact wireless card model and post it here so that people can try to help you out.
