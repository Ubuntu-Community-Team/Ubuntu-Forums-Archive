Hi,

I have a first generation iPod touch too.  I have not done anything with gtkpod at all.  It's been my experience that once Ububtu (or another distro) is installed, I would start Rhythmbox (or Banshee or whatever) and it would see my iTouch right away.

If I want to put music on my iTouch I'd select the songs I want, and drag and drop them to the ipod icon on the left side of the program's screen.  Then wait until the transfer is done.   This is how I sync my music.

I don't know if this will help you, I hope it does.

As near as I can tell, it won't work with iTouch apps, like games.  For that you may need iTunes, and iTunes does not like Linux at all
