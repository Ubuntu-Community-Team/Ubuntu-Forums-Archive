### Post by bredman on 2010-09-08
Have you tried to disable security? For example turn off WEP or WPA.

Security problems have a nasty habit of closing connections unexpectedly.

You need to disable security on both the PC and the router.

WARNING - Only disable security if you trust your neighbours.
