### Post by cryptk on 2008-09-15
For some reason, most of the windows that I open now start with their location set to the far top left of my screen.  This puts their title bar under the top panel in Gnome.  then I have to Alt-Click on then to drag them out from under it so I can get to the title bar and it's buttons (min/maximize, close etc.).  I have tried fixing this by killing and restarting the gnome-panels, and resetting the panels to their defaults thinking that there was some "always on top" option that I had unmarked, but this did not fix it.  Any ideas?

---

### Post by cryptk on 2008-09-15
I found a solution.  Under System > Preferences > Advanced Desktop Effects Settings.  Then I enabled the "Place Windows" plugin and made sure the workarounds box was checked.  I finally found this fix right after I made the post, haha.  Just figured I would put the solution in here.
