### Post by DFlame on 2009-08-31
This doesn't put it in the tray, but it does shift it out of the way:

I open Thunderbird, maximise it if needed, then right click it in the window list. Just a matter of "Move to Workspace Right".

When I want to check on it, I just press Ctrl-Alt-Right/Left to switch workspaces

I personally use the addon called FireTray in Thunderbird. It does exactly what I want. It gets a tray icon, notifying if I get a new mail, and closing TB will not end the process, it will just be hidden (minimized to tray).
