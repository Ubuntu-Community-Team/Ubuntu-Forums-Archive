### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-05-12
If a program is installed, I would recommend you update it.

If there are programs you don't need, then uninstall them.  You will then solve the problem of unnecessary updates.

If you really want a slimline installation, then you may have been better off installing a minimal Ubuntu, and picking the apps you want, rather than trying to remove unnecessary ones from a default installation.
