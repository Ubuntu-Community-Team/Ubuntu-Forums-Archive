### Post by mcduck on 2009-02-01
Right-click on some empty spot in the panel, select "Add to panel", find the "Menu bar"-applet and drag&drop it into your panel.

You can move it to correct place by either dragging with middle mouse button, or right-clicking and selecting "Move" from the popup menu. IF it refuses to move past some applet then right-click that applet and deselect "Lock To Panel", after that you should be able to move everything freely.

### Post by krzysz00 on 2009-02-01
If you just lost the MENU, but not the whole bar, right click where the menu used to be and select "Add to Panel". Pick "Menu Bar". and hit "Add".. If the menu is in the wrong place, right-click on it and select move. Then move your mouse left or right, which will move your menu to the right place
