### Post by N.N. on 2008-05-19
If you don't have a window list applet in one of your panels, right click on a panel, click "Add to panel", choose "Window list", and click "Add". This applet shows running programs.

Cheers all in advance.They go to window heaven. :)  Ok... seriously, the Window List is, by default, in your bottom panel in GNOME... if you removed that panel, you also removed the Window List.
