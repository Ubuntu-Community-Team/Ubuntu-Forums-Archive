### Post by walt.smith1960 on 2011-08-07
You could get a little retro and install gnome-panel-fallback.  That should give you a bottom panel while running Ubuntu (Unity).  In Natty that panel should contain a desktop icon, room for several running apps and desktop switcher on the right.  The different desktops are not easy to see on a dark pane but if you hover your cursor over them they'll show up.  I don't know what if any problems are caused by this but it works in 11.04 and 11.10 Alpha 3 so far. This makes the Unity DE usable for me.

Of course on a netbook that bar takes up space you may not want to give up so do what works.
