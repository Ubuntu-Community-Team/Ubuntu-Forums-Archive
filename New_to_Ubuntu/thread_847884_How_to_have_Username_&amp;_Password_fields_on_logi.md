### Post by socceroos on 2008-07-03
Hello Mingle,

By default, the Gnome GDM (Gnome login screen) only shows one field at a time (it shows Username first). So, once you have entered your Username you hit 'Enter' or 'Tab' and it will then display the Password field and once you have entered that then you can login (again, hit 'Enter').

If, though, you still want it to display the login field too then you can download and install a different login screen at [http://www.gnome-look.org/](http://www.gnome-look.org/)  and click on the 'GDM Themes' button in the sidebar on the left. 

Hope this helps.
