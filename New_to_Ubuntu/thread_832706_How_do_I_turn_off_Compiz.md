### Post by Qrikko on 2008-06-18
Easiest (of cause there are more ways) is to use the Ubuntu iconed menu in your Gnome panel.

from there select the System | Preferences | appearance.
And the last tab (visual effects)

There you can turn on/off Compiz-fusion, or customize "how much" to use.
