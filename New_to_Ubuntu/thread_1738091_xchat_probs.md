### Post by cavalier911 on 2011-04-25
**After** you modify colors and text events in Settings from hard coded defaults and shutdown xchat you will see ~/.xchat2/colors.conf and ~/.xchat2/pevents.conf

When installing a theme install colors.conf and pevents.conf with xchat shutdown.Otherwise they will be overwritten when you shutdown xchat.
