### Post by _0R10N on 2010-04-24
From your profile, I assume you're running Karmic. So if what you want is having a login screen when your system starts up, and prompt users to enter a password, this is what you have to do. Under de Administration menu, open the option "Login Screen". Click on "unlock" (it will ask for a password). Then click on "Show the screen for choosing who will login". Click on "close", and you're done!.:popcorn:
