### Post by Volt9000 on 2009-04-26
Is your computer COMPLETELY frozen? As in, your mouse cursor won't move at all? Or you can move it but nothing will respond?

What about your hard drive activity LED on your chassis? Is that blinking or solid? Do you hear the hard drive going crazy?

Are you doing something specific when your computer freezes, or does it happen regardless of what you do? Does the freeze happen after a certain amount of time, and if so, how long?

The most frequent cause of lockups is overheating. Is your system being sufficiently cooled?
