I think I would be inclined to try and narrow down the cause of the problem.  It could be one of the hard drives but it could be some other reason.

Why not disconnect one of the hard drives and then boot the system from the CD and see if the problem appears.  It everything is normal then power off and then disconnect the drive and connect the other and power on and see if the problem then appears.

If the problem appears with neither of the drives ........   does the system work from the CD with neither drive connected?
