### Post by Yvan300 on 2009-05-26
That's a pretty sweet card but most likely the ATI driver may be the problem. Remember that linux is not main stream, so for the ATI team creating drivers is just some unnecessary side job. That is why some drivers are never updated and also the reason why  a lot of cards have been dropped for driver support. They try to provide as much qualtiy as they can within their given time period. Your alternative is to try Jaunty (if you haven't already). The open source drivers are said to be better than the previous ones. I myself was able to enable desktop effects using the freee driver. So just give it a try and let's see what's the outcome.
