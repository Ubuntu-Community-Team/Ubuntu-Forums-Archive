### Post by LowSky on 2011-08-06
I'm guessing that when you built that machine you also used a dedicated graphics card? Are you using the proprietary driver or the default open source one. If the card is of a newer series the open source driver may not be up to date for the card. I suggest using the proprietary one. Even more important may be using the newest driver available from the Nvidia's or AMD's own websites or for ease Ubuntu PPA's.
