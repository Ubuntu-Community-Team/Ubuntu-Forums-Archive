How do I rate songs in rhythmbox, other than from the properties dialogue?  There doesn't seem to be a way to add a ratings column and it's not in any of the right click menus I've seen.

---

### Post by fluteflute on 2008-08-25
Go to **Edit->Preferences**, then in the **General** tab you can select *Rating* from the 'Visible Columns' section.
