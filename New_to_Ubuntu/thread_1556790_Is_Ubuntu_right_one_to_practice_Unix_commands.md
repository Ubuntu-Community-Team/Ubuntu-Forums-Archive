### Post by john newbuntu on 2010-08-20
You can surely use it for practice.  There are subtle differences.  Not all linux commands are available on the unix flavors.  Even on the commands that are common, not all options are supported and vice-versa. Some older unix versions do not even have Bash shell.  For most part, I have worked on Korn and C shells.
But yeah, all said and done, it is a good OS to learn scripting/shell programming in general.
