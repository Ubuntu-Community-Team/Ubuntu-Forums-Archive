### Post by Chase993 on 2013-05-08
When you format a partition or hard drive with the Ubuntu partitioner. You're more than likely not going to see it when you're in Windows, but this is only because Windows doesn't understand that type of partition. If you go back in the Ubuntu installation and get to the part where you're partitioning you hard drive you should see a space (I am not sure how or what it will be labeled as from lack of info) where your partition is.
