### Post by drs305 on 2008-05-31
Even easier, while you are composing a post and are ready to insert the link, click on the little blue world at the top of the compose window. It will ask for the web address, and once you put it in and enter, it will place it in your post. 

You will see the address twice in the post. The first is the actual address. The second reference is what you will see in the post and you can change it to anything you want, like CLICK HERE in the previous post. It will be highlighted in black when you hit enter after completing the link address.
