### Post by t0p on 2009-09-11
There's also the [BBC iPlayer Desktop application]("http://www.bbc.co.uk/iplayer/install"), which allows you to download video and audio content.  It works with intrepid and jaunty.

There's also a command-line app,[ **get_iplayer**]("http://linuxcentre.net/getiplayer/"), that lets you download video and audio content from BBC iPlayer, with no DRM.  My favourite.
