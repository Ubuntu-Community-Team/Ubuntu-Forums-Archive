**ifconfig**

and

**lspci -v**

and

**lsusb** (in case you have a USB wireless adapter)

To do this, open a terminal (Applications -> Accessories) and type the command and press Enter. You can left click mouse and drag across the output then right click and select copy to copy to a post.
