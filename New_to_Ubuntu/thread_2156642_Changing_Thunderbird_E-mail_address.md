### Post by HeroOfCanton on 2013-06-22
Very easy.

1) Open Thunderbird.
2) Click Edit | Account Settings
3) Click the account you want to delete.
4) On the bottom you will see a pull-down menu titled "Account Actions". Select "Remove Account".
