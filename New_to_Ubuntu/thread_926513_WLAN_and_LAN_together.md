### Post by keplerspeed on 2008-09-22
Yes you can, for example if you have ethnet connection to the internet you can share your connection over WLAN using firestarter see:

[http://www.fs-security.com/docs/connection-sharing.php](http://www.fs-security.com/docs/connection-sharing.php)

Im still playing with mine to get it to work, but its possible! firestarter is in the repositories, just search for it in synaptic.

EDIT: just re read your post, In firestarter you select if LAN or WLAN is the internet connection or for networking, so just the same thing as I said, but the other way!
