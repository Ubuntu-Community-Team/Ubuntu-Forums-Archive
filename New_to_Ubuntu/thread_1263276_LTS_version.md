### Post by earthpigg on 2009-09-10
go with 9.04 _**unless**_ 9.04 is having problems with your hardware.

how do you test this?

boot from the 9.04 livecd.

check wireless functionality, if you have a wireless card.

see if you can get system -> preferences -> desktop effects -> extra

then, if you can get both deskop effects and wireless (if you have it working) to work... go ahead and install.

nothing you do in the LiveCD will stick, but its a nice sandbox to test in.

if 9.04 is having problems, say, with enabling desktop effects? that means it is having problems with your video card. try 8.04 and 8.10, and hopefully 9.10 will fix whatever isn't working correctly on your system with 9.04.
