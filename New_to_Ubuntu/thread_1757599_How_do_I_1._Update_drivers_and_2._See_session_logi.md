### Post by JDShu on 2011-05-13
Important bug fixes and other stability updates for drivers will probably be backported and will be done for you on update. However new version of the open source drivers will not be automatically available for you, and in most cases you shouldn't try to get them as they will likely be unstable. If up to date drivers are important to you (some application you know works with new drivers but not old ones), then you should make sure you always have the latest version of Ubuntu.
