### Post by Lisiano on 2011-10-10
Who is hosting your email?
If it's your ISP (Internet service provider) then they might be blocking sending emails from outside networks.
If it's a self hosted email then you need to check that ports are correctly forwarded.
If it's a public host like Gmail then this needs to be looked into since they usually don't block sending messages from non-usual locations.
