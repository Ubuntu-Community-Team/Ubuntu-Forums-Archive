### Post by rosswmcgee on 2012-06-26
OK I figured it out or Ubuntu update may have changed things. 

cntrl alt enter will maximize the dosbox screen. Alt-enter will reduce screen size without disturbing the desktop. So you can go back and forth as you wish. Why the change I have no idea, but it works.
