### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-06-08
Blinking the LED is probably hardcoded into the device's driver. Changing it would require modifying the driver's source code and recompiling it. It's even possible that the blinking of the LED is a hard**wired** thing inside the device, like on webcams.

Cut out a small square of gaffer tape and use it to cover the LED. A lot less hassle.
