### Post by audiomick on 2010-12-29
You generally shouldn't have to get a graphics driver from somewhere else these days.

In System> Administration there is a thing called "Hardware Drivers". Have you tried running that? If there is a proprietary driver for your graphics card, that should get it for you. The computer needs to be connected to the internet, of course.
