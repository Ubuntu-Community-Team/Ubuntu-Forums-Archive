### Post by indytim on 2010-09-02
I'm assuming that you are behind a proxy server and you need to connect.

Go to System->Preferences->Network Proxy

1. Check the Manual Proxy Setting.
2.  Enter the url of your proxy server in the Http:// space
3.  Click details and enter your firewall password etc.
