### Post by mutex023 on 2010-01-03
1. Install 'Deluge' bittorrent client .
 Applications > Ubuntu Software Centre > Search for deluge.
7. Go to a torrent site, [www.*******.com](www.*******.com), *********.org or ********.org
8. Search for a movie(or whatever u want) name, ex:-Day of the jackal.
9. There are several results, but pick the one which has following parameters - 
   Category - Video/movies
   S(Seeders) = atleast 7
   L(Leechers) = atleast 7 (though if S>20, L<7 is OK or vice versa).
   These are actually the no. of people downloading&uploading(leechers) and people only uploading(seeders, who have completed the download).
   So if the no. of people are less, the movie will never start download or take several months to download.
10. Click on the selected link.
11. It will show the list of files. As a rule only download those torrents where the files are .avi or .dat, never download
	.rar or any other kind for movies. Because they are fakes.

13. Its also a good idea to check the comments link at the top, because many .avi's are also fake, and reading the comments 
	gives useful info abt this.
14. Once u know that the torrent is original and has enough S and L, Click on Download Torrent link at the top, the .torrent file will download to your computer. 
15. Now just double click the downloaded .torrent file and deluge will open up and ask u where to save the movie files, select the location u want and click Ok and it will begin downloading.
20. It will take several mins for the download speed to pick up, keep observing the Down SPeed and Up speed columns in deluge.
21. To exit deluge fully, click File->exit.
22. You can pause and resume downloads.
22. Remember that ur both uploading and downloading when using bittorrent.

Hope this helps,
