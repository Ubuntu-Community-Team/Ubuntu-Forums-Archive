### Post by Dynaflow on 2008-06-20
That the problem is with audio playback implies that the difficulty is with sound coming out, not sound going in.  Your mic should be in the clear.

This problem has cropped up before.  Take a look and see if you can find a solution that works for you in this thread:  [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=4526841](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=4526841)
