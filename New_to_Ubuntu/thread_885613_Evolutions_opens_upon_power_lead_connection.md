### Post by alienexplorers on 2008-09-06
First thing is to go into System -> Preferences -> Sessions
Go to the current session tab.
Look for Evolution and Remove it.
Then goto the session options tab
check the box and click on the bar.
Reboot and see if Evolution starts or not.
