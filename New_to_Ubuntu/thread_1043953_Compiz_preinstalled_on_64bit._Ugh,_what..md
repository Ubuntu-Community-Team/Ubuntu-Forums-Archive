### Post by niteshifter on 2009-01-19
Hi,

Try this: System -> Preferences -> Appearance. Should open a dialog titled Appearance Preferences. Click on the Visual Effects tab. In there: None means no compiz. The ones below it bring on more effects. Custom lets you play ;)

After you change from None, you may see - if it's not there already - under System -> Preferences a item called Advanced Desktop Effects Settings. It's the same as Custom in the above.

### Post by mcduck on 2009-01-19
Install the compizconfig-settings-manager, then go to System/Preferences/Advanced Desktop Effects Settings and you'll have full control over every available Compiz feature & plugin.
