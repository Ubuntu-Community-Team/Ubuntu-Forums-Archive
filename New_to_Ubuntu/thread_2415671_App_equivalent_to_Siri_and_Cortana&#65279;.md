### Post by QIII on 2019-03-29
Hello!

[This]("https://alternativeto.net/software/cortana/?platform=linux") has a few alternatives that might work.

Why isn't anything installed by default?  Since it is not possible to install by default everything that anyone might possibly want and still keep the installation medium to the size of a DVD, Canonical makes decisions about what to provide by default.

Apparently such an application is not something they deem to be of particularly broad-based appeal in the community.
