### Post by mgmiller on 2009-08-11
This may be happening because your default media player is still set to rhythmbox, which is the default.

(The previous line is brought to you by the department of redundancy department)

Go to System > Preferences > File Management.

If that entry is not there, just open your home folder and Click on "Edit > Prefereneces"

Open the last tab called " Media"

Next to Media player, click the drop down and select "Songbird"

If it's not there, Click "Open with other Application" and select Songbird from the list.

You may have to log off and back on or, (gasp), reboot for the change to take effect.

Good luck.
