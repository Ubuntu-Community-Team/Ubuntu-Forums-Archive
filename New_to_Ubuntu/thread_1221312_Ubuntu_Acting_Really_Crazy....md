### Post by desperado665 on 2009-07-23
Check your keyboard for a stuck key. Mouse clicks can also be caused by a stuck enter key. Your scroll problem might be stuck arrow keys. Is it possible someone spilled something sticky (coke/orange juice/whisky) on your keyboard? Also check the keyboard cable for crimps, tears in the casing, etc. Failing that, shut down your pc and check the keyboard connector for bent pins.
