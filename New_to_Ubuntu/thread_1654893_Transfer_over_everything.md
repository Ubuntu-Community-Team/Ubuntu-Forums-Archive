### Post by mcduck on 2010-12-29
Simply copying your home directory (including the hidden files inside it) would be enough to copy over all your files and settings for all your programs.

The programs themselves you'll just have to reinstall on the new computer. But that shouldn't take a lot of time if you just check what you have installed, and then mark them all for installation at once in Synaptic Package Manager.
