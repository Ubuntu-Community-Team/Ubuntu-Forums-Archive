### Post by aeiah on 2008-10-28
its unusual that it wouldnt be compatible with linux. i find that the main problem is that windows locks the filesystem of portable hard drives, and when you pull the cable out without doing the 'safely remove hardware' bit in windows, it can keep the drive locked. if you have windows nearby, the best thing to do would be to plug it in, and safely remove it.
