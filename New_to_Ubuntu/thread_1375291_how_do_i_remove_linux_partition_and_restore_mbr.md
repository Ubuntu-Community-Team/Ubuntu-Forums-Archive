### Post by running_rabbit07 on 2010-01-07
Getting rid of the Ubuntu partition is the easy part. Just boot the LiveCD and go to GParted and delete Ubuntu partitions.

The next part is the tricky part. You will need the Windows install disk to repair the MBR.
