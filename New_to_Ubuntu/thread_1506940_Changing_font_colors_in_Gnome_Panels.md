### Post by josephellengar on 2010-06-11
> **TriBlox6432 said:**
> I just did a re install of ubuntu 10.04 and when I changed my background, and made my panels transparent, I can't see any of the writing.  I changed it to white last time so I could see it on the black background, but I can't figure it out now.  Any help?

```

sudo apt-get install gnome-color-chooser

```

Then run alt-f2 and type gnome-color chooser.
It gives you a ridiculous amount of options and you can change the color of literally anything on the system.  Quite fun to play around with.
