### Post by yeats on 2012-02-26
This is another Linux distribution, not something you would install on top of Ubuntu.  If your system has enough hard disk space and memory (and preferably virtualization extensions on your processor), you could run it in VirtualBox.  Or you could look into dual booting.

Hope that helps.
