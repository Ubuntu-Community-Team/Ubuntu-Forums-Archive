### Post by thegod_slayer on 2010-02-24
you will find the document viewer in system>>> preferences>>>main menu
clicking on this you will be directed to the page where you can select the applications
that you want on your main menu......
here click on the graphics tab.
on the right part of the window you will find f spot photo manager, gimp and also document viewer.
click on the check box in font of the document viewer.
now you can find it under application>>>graphics>>>document viewer.:D:D:D:D
