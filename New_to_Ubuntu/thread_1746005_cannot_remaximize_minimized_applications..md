### Post by beew on 2011-05-17
The problem is solved by logging into the Classical Desktop, dragging the launcher for gnome-mplayer from the panel to the desktop and then log back into Unity and drag the launcher from the desktop to the Unity Bar (after removing the old launcher first). Now the launcher behaves as it should.

It is really weird that you need to resort to the desktop to solve a Unity problem.
