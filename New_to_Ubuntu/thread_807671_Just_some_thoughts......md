### Post by dRock1286 on 2008-05-26
A directed attack is unlikely since 85% of all OS's out there are Windows...with only 4% being Linux.  However, if there was an attack, there are several antivirus and firewall programs you can get.  Clam and Panda are decent antiviruses that I have used just for a personal test on them and Firestarter and IPCop are great firewalls you can get as well.
