### Post by Deeday on 2011-12-11
I see now: misleading help text ](*,)

The text in System Settings --> Keyboard --> Shorcuts says: "To edit a shortcut, click the row and hold down the new keys or press Backspace to clear".

In fact you have to click on the key name, not just anywhere in the row. If e.g. you click on *Launch web browser* and then depress the desired key, nothing happens. You have to click on *WWW* - on the same row but to the right of *Launch web browser*.

Maybe is it worth clarifying?
