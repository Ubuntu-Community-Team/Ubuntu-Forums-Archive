### Post by mikewhatever on 2009-05-16
The following links should get you an idea on what each version is.

[http://www.ubuntu.com/products/mobile](http://www.ubuntu.com/products/mobile)
[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download-netbook](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download-netbook)
[http://www.ubuntu.com/products/whatisubuntu/xubuntu](http://www.ubuntu.com/products/whatisubuntu/xubuntu)

To summarise, Ubuntu-mobile is for cell phone like devices, UNR is for netbooks, and Xubuntu is for less powerful PCs. It makes no sense to compare them in terms of performance, since the three are intended for different hardware, sort of like comparing a bicycle, a motorcycle, and a car. Obviously, the car is more powerful then the other two, but what does it matter if it can't go where a bicycle can.
