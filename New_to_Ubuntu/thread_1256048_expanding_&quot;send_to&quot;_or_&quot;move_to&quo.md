### Post by pablopancho on 2009-09-08
Unfortunately I'm not well versed in bash scripts so I must rely on Ubuntu Tweak and that's what I suggest to you.

In UT there is a "Scripts" section. Among various scripts you can find "Move to..." and "Copy to..." as well as "Copy to Home", "Copy to Desktop". You need to drag&drop selected scripts to nautilus-scripts folder and then you'll get scripts in right-click menu.

I hope it helps
