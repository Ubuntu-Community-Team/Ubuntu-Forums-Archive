### Post by PinkFloyd102489 on 2008-04-29
You probably already have Compiz working then, so I sugguest you install Emerald to transparent window titles, etc. Screenlets are also pretty cool, they can range from weather information to system information. Then there's a few that do random, pointless things, such as the HAL9000 screenlet. :-p


Check out [http://gnome-look.org](http://gnome-look.org) to see what Im talking about. Emerald themes are listed under Beryl and Compiz. Screenlets have their own section.


Also, if you install compizconfig-settings-manager and then go to System > Preferences > Appearance > Effects tab and set it to Custom, you can get the cool things you see on other peoples desktops, such as the desktop cube.
