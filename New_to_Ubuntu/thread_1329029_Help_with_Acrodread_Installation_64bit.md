### Post by ohzopants on 2009-11-17
Do you absolutely need the Adobe Acrobat reader?

There are a number of free and superior (in my opinion, at least) options available.

The gnome default pdf viewer is evince, and for KDE it is kpdf.  Both of these are pretty good.

You may want to also give Foxit a try.  It's my personal favourite.  See here for details:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Foxit](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Foxit)
