### Post by TristanMike on 2009-01-04
OK, so I've gotten my surround sound to work. I had to change under "System-Preferences-Sound" "Music and Movies" from Autodetect to the card using ALSA and multichannel playback.

It also seemed that I had to uncheck the box for SB Live Analog/Digital Output Jack.

Once I restarted my applications, it seemed to resolve the issue.

However, now I am experiencing a slight crackle/delay in my audio. Does anyone have any ideas what might be causing this ?
