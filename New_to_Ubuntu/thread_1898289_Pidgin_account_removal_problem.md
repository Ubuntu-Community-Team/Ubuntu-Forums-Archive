Hey guys I'd just got it sorry for the  trouble, took me some time searching over the menus and other places.

Solution:
1. While the Pidgin is open hold ALT and you'll see the options in the menu
2. Go to the  Accounts you'll see the Manage Accounts there and there you can erase an account.

SHORT CUT:
1. Open Pidgin and press CRTL+A.
