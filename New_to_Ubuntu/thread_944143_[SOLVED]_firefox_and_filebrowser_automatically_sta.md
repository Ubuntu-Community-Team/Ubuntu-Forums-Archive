### Post by tjwoosta on 2008-10-11
ok nevermind i figured out how to stop it


i just loged in, then i closed firefox and the file browser

then i went to system-preferences-sessions and i clicked the button that says remember currently running applications

it said at the bottom that my session had been saved

then i loged out and back in, all was back to normal
