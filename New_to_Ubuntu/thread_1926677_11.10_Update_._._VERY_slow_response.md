### Post by acrazyplayer on 2012-02-16
for the spell checker you need to make sure firefox has the dictionary included. download it here, [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/language-tools/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/language-tools/)

as for ubuntu being slow, this could be many things, it could be a hdd going bad, it could be ram going bad, it could also just be ubuntu has messed up somehow and I would recommend a full clean install. this means backing up all your data and formating the partition for ubuntu and then re-install. 

Also I recommend that you use ubuntu 10.10. I find if anyone ever asks me which ubuntu they should try out I always say 10.10 because most things just work, also it doesn't have unity which a lot of people can't get used to. 

good luck
