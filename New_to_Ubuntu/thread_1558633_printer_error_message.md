### Post by jonnywombat on 2010-08-22
No problem..

Is the computer connected to your computer or your network?

Either way mark the printer as to be shared, then install the toolbox to your daughters PC and then when running the device setup part of HPLIP toolbox you will be able to find the printer on your network.

Just remember that if the printer is connected to your main PC then it would have to be turned on and connected to your network for your daughter to be able to print.

Good luck.
