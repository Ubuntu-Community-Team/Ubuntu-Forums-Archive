### Post by HereInOz on 2009-07-14
Have a go at System > Preferences > Keyboard > Layouts > Layout Options and change your Caps Lock Behavior to something else, and see if that makes a difference.

Sometimes setting it at something else, then back to where it was, will sort things out, particularly if you are using VMWare.
