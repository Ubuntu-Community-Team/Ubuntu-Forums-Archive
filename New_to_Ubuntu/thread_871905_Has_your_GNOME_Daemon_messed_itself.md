Whilst trying to solve a dillema with my screens, resolution and lack of dual monitor support I discovered on the forums threads pertaining to the GNOME Daemon not starting correctly problem.

As it happens, I solved my GNOME Daemon problem by going **System** >> **Administration** >> **Hardware Drivers** and checking ATi Accelerated Graphics Driver.

I know that this pertains specifically to my system and perhaps those with ATi Radeon (etc) cards, but it may help others...

Worth trying if the dialog box is driving you nuts...
