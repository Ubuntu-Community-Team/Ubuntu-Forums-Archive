Open Synaptic Package Manager and search for **mfc-240c** and it will show ```
brother-cups-wrapper-bh7
brother-lpr-drivers-bh7
``` select both and install.

Connect and power on your printer and then go to **System > Administration > Printing** and select "new Printer".It should then search and find your printer and the drivers and install it.Easy

Good Luck
