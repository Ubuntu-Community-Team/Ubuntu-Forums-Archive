### Post by kiridude on 2010-08-14
I think you want:
-right click, then "remove from panel" on menu bar.
-right click on panel and add main menu (you'll find it helpful).
-right click on panel and add global menu (if not there already).
-right click on each thing added and select "move" to arrange how you like.
