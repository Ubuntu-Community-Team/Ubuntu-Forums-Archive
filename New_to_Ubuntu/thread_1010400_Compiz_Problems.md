### Post by Kareeser on 2008-12-19
If this is a problem, you could always install "compiz-switch". It's not in the official repository, but installs nicely (.deb) and places a shortcut in the Accessories menu.

It turns on/off compiz at will. Great for gaming, and then switching back for the effects :)
