### Post by kapi on 2009-12-28
After weeks of searching and trying several editors ranging from bluefish to net beans, I've finally settled on quanta plus.

What a machine (well program actually). It's the little things in life that make us smile and quanta plus has me smiling a lot.

I can set my tab size, change the font colour in the editor, have auto code completion and php intellisense  . . .wow!

I may not use all of the really powerful features immediately but what it does at a basic level is really good. 

So if you have just switched over to Ubuntu from windows and are stressing because there's no dream weaver. . don't stress. Most of the developers I know using MS windows don't use dream weaver to it's max. To be honest most use notepadd++. 

Spend a couple of days tweakng the interface to your liking and I think you'll be impressed. Shame there's no youtube vid tutorials on quanta plus. Maybe I'll do a couple.

Just thought I'd share my joy. . . Take care
