### Post by Zzl1xndd on 2010-10-26
RhythmBox doesn't display an Icon in 10.10 as its controls are now integrated with the Sound controls. As this is the case your original configuration may have been working correctly and because the icon wasn't coming up as it had in 10.04 you suspected it was no longer working.
