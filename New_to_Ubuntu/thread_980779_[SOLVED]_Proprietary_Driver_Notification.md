### Post by midgetwithamatch on 2008-11-13
I'm running Ubuntu 8.04 coupled with Gnome+compiz+emerald. I have everything setup fine as far as my drivers and xorg.conf the effects work fine and everything... But I constantly have the notification about "New Drivers Available" in my notification area. When I setup my video card I did so manually and edited my xorg.conf appropriately so I didn't take advantage of the built in proprietary driver support. I was wondering if there was any particular way to get this message to stop notifying me that there is a driver available?

---

### Post by mcduck on 2008-11-13
Remove the restricted driver manager from your Gnome session (in System/Preferences/Session). :)
