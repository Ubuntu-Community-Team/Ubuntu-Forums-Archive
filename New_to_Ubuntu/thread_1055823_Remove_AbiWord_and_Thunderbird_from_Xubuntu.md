### Post by ugm6hr on 2009-01-31
You can remove them - none of those apps are integrated into XFCE.

It will warn you it needs to remove xubuntu-desktop package, which sounds worrying, but it doesn't matter.

If you ever want to upgrade to a newer version, you will need to reinstall it (xubuntu-desktop).
