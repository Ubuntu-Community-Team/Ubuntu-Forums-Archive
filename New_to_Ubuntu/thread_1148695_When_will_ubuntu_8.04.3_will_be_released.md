### Post by snowpine on 2009-05-04
Hi Ravi, if you are already using 8.04, there is no need to worry about 8.04.2 or 8.04.3 or whatever... they are just new "snapshots" of the 8.04 Live CD with current updates applied... as a convenience for new users (so they don't have to upgrade 700 packages after they install). Just keep using the Update Manager regularly, and you will be O.K.
