### Post by sdlynx on 2009-07-04
if you want to stretch the panel to fit the whole top of the window  right click it in an area where there is nothing (no applets there), click Properties, and check off expand

however for your problem right click the panel where there is nothing and click Add to Panel and add the notification area applet and you'll be fine
