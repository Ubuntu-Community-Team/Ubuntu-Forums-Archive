### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-02-17
Undotunately, Wine is NOT necessarily a solution for stuff that works in Windows -- as evidenced by the WineHQ page for Zune:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=10663]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=10663")

I have a Zune, and I can confirm that nothing works -- as you must use the Zone software to access it -- and that will NOT work in Wine.
