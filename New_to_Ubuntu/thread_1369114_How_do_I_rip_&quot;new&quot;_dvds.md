### Post by coatit on 2010-01-03
Handbrake has been great for just getting the movie content in mkv or mp4. Compresses nicely and look good on the computer.

K9Copy is working very well so far on the "rip .iso" to hard drive. I rip them at 4400 mb and they look great when I burn them back to single layer DVD-R.

For getting them back to home viewabilty I use K3b. K9copy actually has a setting for Burn with K3b while make the original ISO. Not sure what it does but I checked it and they seem to work fine.
