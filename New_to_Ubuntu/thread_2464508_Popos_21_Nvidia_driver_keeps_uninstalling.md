### Post by grahammechanical on 2021-07-03
Pop!_OS is based on Ubuntu but it is not one of the flavours of Ubuntu  that this forum is set up to provide support for. Your post should be  put in the Ubuntu/Debian subsection of the forum.

[https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2463009](https://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2463009)

That forum post provides a link to the Pop!_OS forum. 

[https://pop-planet.info/forums/](https://pop-planet.info/forums/)

Personally,  I do not think it is possible for a proprietary video driver (Nvidia)  to uninstall itself. On Ubuntu the command to install and uninstall  proprietary video drivers would require administrator privileges. I do  not know what the Pop!_OS method would be.

I am guessing that the OS is loading without activating the video driver and is then falling back to using a basic video driver. I am also guessing that the OS is unable to find the Nvidia driver files and that is the reason why the Nvidia driver is not loading.
