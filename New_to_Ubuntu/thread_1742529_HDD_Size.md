### Post by Quackers on 2011-04-30
If you are installing Ubuntu with a view to keeping it then the above are good suggestions.
However, if you are installing it just to try it out and see if you like it, intending later to make a fuller installation, then something like a single root partition of 12 to 15GB would be enough. A swap sapce might be required too. A separate home partition would be a waste in these circumstances and further complicates things.
