### Post by Random-penguin on 2009-05-31
Ha ha, this old cookie! There is a way of doing so which circumvents the "security" placed on commercial DVD's. But in a lot of countries doing this is illegal. You could purchase a codec pack from (I think) Fluendo or if you are willing to take the risk/your country doesn't subscribe to the copyright law then you could install libdvdcss (needed to circumvent said security) from:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu)

This will also provide some other extras.
