### Post by deadflowr on 2020-03-08
Gnome desktop requires a graphics card that can handle it's 3d acceleration requirements, if it cannot it automatically reverts to using llvmpipe to do it.

Edit: At last check gnome requires opengl 2.1 at minimum ,
your card is only capable of opengl 1.3.
