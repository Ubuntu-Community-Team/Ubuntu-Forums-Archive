### Post by adult swim on 2008-04-28
if youve already got the ccsm installed and have the cube rotate and desktop cube effects enabled, to rotate the cube, press ctrl+alt+left/right or ctrl+alt+left click and drag your mouse. when you are on your desktop screen (your wallpaper) you can click your middle button (scroll wheel, or on a laptop both mouse buttons and drag on the touchpad.)  to zoom in you can push and hold the super key (usually the one with the windows logo on it) and scroll your mouse/touchpad.  for most effects you can double click from the effect from ccsm and click the bindings tab to see the keyboard combinations you need to press to use the effect.
edit 2: if you havent made your cube 4 sided yet you need to go to system/preferences/advanced desktop effects settings then go to the desktop size tab and set your horizontal virtual size to 4.
