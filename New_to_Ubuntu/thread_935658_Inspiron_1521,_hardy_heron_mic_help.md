What is your problem?  You are not able to record from the microphone?

Open the applet volume that you should see next your your date/time in your top bar (double click on it...)

Select the recording tab to play with volumes, see "options" tab for selecting the internal microphone (Digital 1) or External (Analog Mic).

You have to play around with your volumes to find the proper settings.
