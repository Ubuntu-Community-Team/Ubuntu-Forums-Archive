### Post by londonali1010 on 2009-08-06
As far as I know, you can only have one panel at a time in each location (i.e. top, bottom, left or right) with gnome-panel. You should try and integrate all the panel widgets you need into just one panel, and leave that on the bottom. Other than that, I would recommend searching for another panel program to do what you want.
