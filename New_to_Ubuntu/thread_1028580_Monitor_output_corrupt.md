### Post by oldsoundguy on 2009-01-02
unless you are using a KVM switch, every time you swap the monitor from one computer to another (laptop or desktop) you have to RE-BOOT the computer in order for the drivers to activate.

And if you are on a KVM switch, when you boot a particular computer, the display has to be hooked up and ON in order for the computer to SEE it and install your drivers.

No such thing as hot swapping a monitor (yet).
