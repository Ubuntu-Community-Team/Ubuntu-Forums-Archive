### Post by neo1691 on 2012-04-15
Solved!!

All i had to do is->

System > Preferences > Mouse, under the Touchpad tab uncheck Enable mouse clicks with touchpad


source: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SynapticsTouchpad/PreMaverick]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SynapticsTouchpad/PreMaverick")
