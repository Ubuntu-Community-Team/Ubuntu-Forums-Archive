By the way I had a similar problem where for some reason the order of the applet icons got messed up, with for example the trash icon to the left of the workspaces icon. If it helps you to know, I found that by temporarily turning off "Expand" in the panel properties I could swap the positions of the icons. Then when "Expand" was turned back on they stayed in that order. (Assuming your panel is "expanded" to begin with that is ;-) )
