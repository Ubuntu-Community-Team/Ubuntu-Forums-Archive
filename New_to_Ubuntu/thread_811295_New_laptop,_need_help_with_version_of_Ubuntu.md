I'm running 64bit on my Thinkpad X61s and am getting 5 hours of battery life on my extended battery.  I never even tried to install the 32bit Ubuntu, but the 5 hour battery life is comparable to what I get when running 32bit XP on the same machine.  I realize that this is something of an "apples to oranges" comparison, but they're in the same ballpark, so I wouldn't worry too much about the difference in battery life between 64bit vs. 32bit.
