### Post by Horomancer on 2008-06-01
Not sure if you are still having trouble or not but i used gtkpod and found i never needed to 'sync' anything. I'm not sure what syncing means in gtkpod, but it's not like iTunes where they mean installing music on your iPod.
I've found gtkpod not up to snuff when it comes to my audio book collection (it keeps omiting tracks and claims them to be identical when they are perfectly fine)

You should be able to click the 'Add files' or 'Add fold' button after clicking on your ipod. When finished, just click 'Save changes'

I'm currently playing with Amarok, and having a better time managing my play lists with it, but only time will tell how it all works out.
