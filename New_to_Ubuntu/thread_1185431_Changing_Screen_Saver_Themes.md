### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2009-06-12
This is a round-about solution and may not be what you're looking for.

If you have a screensaver that shows images from your "Pictures" folder, and you want it to show pictures from another location, you can create a shortcut to that location and put the shortcut in your "Pictures" folder.

It does seem like there should be an easy way of changing screen saver settings, but I'm not aware of one.  Maybe this thread will teach me something. ;)
