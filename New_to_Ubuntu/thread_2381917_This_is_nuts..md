### Post by tdurfee on 2018-01-06
I setup Ubuntu 16.04 and I have an annoying problem.

After logging into Ubuntu with my user password everything works fine but if I step away from the computer for more than about 5 minutes I come back to a dark screen and then a login screen. I need to make this stop doing that.  How do I fix this?

---

### Post by deadflowr on 2018-01-06
Open Brightness and Lock and set the time to something different and/or disabl the lock screen option.
Also double check Power or power management settings.

Both are in System Settings, fwiw.
