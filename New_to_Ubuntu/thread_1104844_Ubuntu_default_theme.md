### Post by clive littlewood on 2009-03-24
Hi

All the themes are in system > preferences > appearence >themes

To change just click on the appropriate theme.

Try searching synaptic with the word "themes" for official supported themes and gnome look site for many more gtk themes.

To add downloaded themes just click on the add button on the themes panel and navigate to your downloaded theme.

Hope this helps
