### Post by him610 on 2024-11-02
Ctrl D will save them to last saved-to browser folder. IDK how to save to top of folder list.

Search turns this up...
> To save a bookmark to the top of a folder list in Chrome, open your bookmarks manager, locate the bookmark you want to move, then simply click and drag it to the top of the desired folder where you want it to appear first; you can drag and drop bookmarks within folders to reorder them as needed. 
Key steps:
Open Chrome. 
Click the three dots in the top right corner and select "Bookmarks" then "Bookmark Manager". 
Find the bookmark you want to move to the top. 
Click and hold on the bookmark, then drag it to the top position within the folder. 
This is how I have always done it. 
Sometimes, one needs to purge the bookmark listings because they grow.
