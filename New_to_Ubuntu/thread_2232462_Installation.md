### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-07-02
I would doubt the "any CPU" part of your question. A 64bit version of Ubuntu will not run on a 32bit CPU. And the versions of Ubuntu for Intel and AMD CPUs with X86 compatible architecture will not run on an ARM designed CPUs. I would also expect trouble if the installation used a proprietary video driver and the machine was using a video adapter from another maker.

But all things being equal ... but there are not equal are they? I just do not want you claiming that we said you could when you hit problems.
