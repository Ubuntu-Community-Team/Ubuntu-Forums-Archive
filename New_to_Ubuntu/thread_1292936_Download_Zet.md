### Post by Little Girl on 2009-10-28
It looks like you can get ZET 8 Lite from here: [http://www.zaytsev.com/download8.html](http://www.zaytsev.com/download8.html)

ZET 9 Lite is available here: [http://www.zaytsev.com/downloads.html]("http://www.zaytsev.com/downloads.html")

The Wine Applications page only lists information for ZET 8 Lite, and it looks like it has some issues, but that you can overcome the issues by going full screen. More info here: [http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=7707&iTestingId=13732]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=7707&iTestingId=13732")
