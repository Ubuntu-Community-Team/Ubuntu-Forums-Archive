### Post by tacantara on 2009-10-06
If you're running the standard GNOME DE (taskbars at top & bottom of screen), all it should take is a right-click on the top taskbar, select Add to Panel, and enable the Trash applet & Volume Control applet.

### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-10-06
If you used synaptic to install/uninstall, you can look in synaptic >File >History to see what has happened recently and see if there are any clues there.  Have a look and see if **gnome-volume-control** was removed; it seems unlikely, but worth checking.
