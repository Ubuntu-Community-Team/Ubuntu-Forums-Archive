### Post by kansasnoob on 2008-04-28
I always install the Medibuntu repository, then open synaptic, then click on settings>repositiries and then click the Third-Party Software tab, then check the box next to "packages.medibuntu.org/hardy free-nonfree.

Note: NOT the "source code" box!

Then close the software sources window. Then click reload and wait ......... then click on search and search for acroread. Acroread is adobe. Just install it.

You can get Medibuntu here:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu)
