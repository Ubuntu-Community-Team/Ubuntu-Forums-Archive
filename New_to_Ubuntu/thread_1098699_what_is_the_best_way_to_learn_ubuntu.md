### Post by schizocotic on 2009-03-17
I have been using ubuntu for about a week, and I have to say the [Ubuntu Pocket Guide]("http://www.ubuntupocketguide.com/") is an excellent start! And these forums are awesome!

Yes, trial and error will work, and problems will have to be fixed, and you will learn from that. There are, however, tricks that you can learn from the people that know, for instance, mouse over the volume icon and use the mouse wheel, scroll to change the volume. Thanks to Kellemora [[IMG]http://ubuntuforums.org/images/buttons/viewpost.gif[/IMG]]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?p=6893934#post6893934") for that little tip. You might not accidentally stumble on that in a year of playing with ubuntu.

I haven't even had a real problem yet, I read these forums, just to see what I can see.
