### Post by lyceum on 2010-03-27
> **afc1903 said:**
> Sorry, new to ubuntu, any instructions how to do this after aptitude?

Unfortunately without knowing what exactly is messing things up you are likely going to have to play around with it and figure it out. ? will get you help and g will let you unistall packages. Be careful uninstalling. 

If I were you I'd try to do another update before anything else. If you are using wifi, you may be able to plug in to get the net. Randomly uninstalling stuff can be bad, so back up all you can. You'll need to re-install packages later if you want to do anything. 

Personally, I would go with a clean install. But, that's me. I re-install every 6 months anyway (one of the reasons I love Ubuntu). Don't take this the wrong way, but there is a good change that you could make things worse. But, it is always good to try, you'll be sure to learn something ;)
