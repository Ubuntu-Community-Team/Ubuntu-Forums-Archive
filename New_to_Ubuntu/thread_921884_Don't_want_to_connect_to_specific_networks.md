### Post by PocketDog on 2008-09-17
Sorry, I'm an idiot. Left click the icon > manual configuration > wireless connection > click properties. Untick roaming mode, put in your router's ESSID. Leave password type as none, and connection settings as automatic. Click ok, give the location a name and click the save icon. It should connect to this network automatically from now on.
