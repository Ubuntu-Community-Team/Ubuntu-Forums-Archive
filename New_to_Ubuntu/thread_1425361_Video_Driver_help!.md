### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-03-11
Hey guys, this is Linux, not Windows.  Drivers are installed automatically here -- you don't go roaming around the internet to find them.

If you're seeing a desktop, the default Intel drivers are already installed.

When folks talk about restricted video drivers, they mean ATI or Nvidia, not Intel.
