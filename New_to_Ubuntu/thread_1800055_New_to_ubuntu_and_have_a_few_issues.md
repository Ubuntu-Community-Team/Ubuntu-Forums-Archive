### Post by IWantFroyo on 2011-07-08
Welcome to the forum :)

To fix your brightness problem, try hitting the super (windows) key and typing 'power management.' You might be able to find something there.

To get drivers, open your dash (or hit your super key) and type 'additional drivers.'

As for the display going dark during movies, I once made a Java program to do it for me, but someone else might be able to show you a pre-installed GUI version to this.

Also, Ubuntu is both GUI and text based. You can hit Ctrl-Alt-T for terminal, and do stuff there if you want. Just google "unix commands" to get a bunch of good stuff on this.
