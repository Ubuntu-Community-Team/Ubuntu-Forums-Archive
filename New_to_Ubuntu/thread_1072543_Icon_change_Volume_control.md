### Post by Jeroen Vernooij on 2009-02-17
You need to find where that icon is stored and replace it manually.

Most icons are in [FONT="Fixedsys"]/usr/share/icons[/FONT] and [FONT="Fixedsys"]/usr/share/pixmaps[/FONT]. If it was not in your installed icon pack, ubuntu will most likely fallback into the Human or Gnome icon pack, so you will find it in one of those folders (human or gnome).

If you've got Desktop effects running, the icon may be in [FONT="Fixedsys"]/usr/share/compiz[/FONT]
