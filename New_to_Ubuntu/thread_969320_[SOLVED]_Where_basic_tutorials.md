### Post by elzbal on 2008-11-08
One good resource, especially for command-line commands, is the [man pages]("http://www.manpages.spotlynx.com/"). They are **NOT** a good "tutorial", in that they don't tell you what command to use for a given task. But if you want to know more about a particular command, they are an excellent reference.

From the command line, type "man <command>", as in "[man cp]("http://www.manpages.spotlynx.com/gnu_linux/man/cp.1")" or "[man mv]("http://www.manpages.spotlynx.com/gnu_linux/man/mv.1")".
