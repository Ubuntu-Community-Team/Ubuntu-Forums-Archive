### Post by QIII on 2014-04-18
Since VirtualBox creates a hardware abstraction layer that is not built by Apple and Apple's EULA proscribes the installation of their OSes on hardware other than Apple, this is effectively a violation of the EULA.

Please familiarize yourself with the [Forums CoC]("http://ubuntuforums.org/misc.php?do=showrules") in particular:
