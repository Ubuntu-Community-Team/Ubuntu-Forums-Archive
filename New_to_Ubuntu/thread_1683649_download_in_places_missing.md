### Post by Dude-PWB- on 2011-02-07
Ok, once again, go back to your home directory, open the Downloads folder, once in there, click 'Bookmark' at the top in Nautilus, and then 'add bookmark' and the folder will show up in your Places menu. Remember though, in linux, Downloads, downloads, downloadS, etc are all different folders. Linux is case sensitive.
