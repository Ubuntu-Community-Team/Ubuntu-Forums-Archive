### Post by joshsmith on 2008-05-05
maybe this isnt what you want as you have already asked, but the harsh sound is probably called clipping where you are maxing out your sound card

turn down volumes, go into gnome-volume-control, enable all the tracks on all the devices, and turn down ALL the ones you dont use to mute (ie all but 2 probably)
and turn down the remaining 2 volumes too
