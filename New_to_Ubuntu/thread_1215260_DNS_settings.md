### Post by cariboo on 2009-07-17
Depending on what version you are using, right click on the network-manager icon in the top panel and select Edit Connections, select your device and click the Edit button, enter your password when asked. Click the IPv4 tab, then click the Method dropdown and select Automatic (DHCP) adresses only. Enter the dns adresses in the DNS Servers text box and clcik apply.
