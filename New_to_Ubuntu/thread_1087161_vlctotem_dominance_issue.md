### Post by RomeReactor on 2009-03-10
Hi. This can be a bit tedious if you have many video formats, but right click on a video file--say, an .AVI--and select "Properties"; on the "Open With" tab, make sure "Movie Player" is selected. Do this for all files with different video containers you want to open with Totem (WMV, MPG, MKV, etc).
