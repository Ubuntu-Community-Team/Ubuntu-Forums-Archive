### Post by Bucky Ball on 2014-01-03
Well, the only LTS at the moment is 12.04 LTS. You give no details of what you are currently running. If it is anything after 12.04 you won't be upgrading, you'll be downgrading, but 12.04 is supported for five years.

If you're running 13.10, just wait until the next LTS, 14.04 LTS, is released in April (wait for a month or two after release, as suggested).

If you open Update Manager, click the Settings button at bottom left, then the Updates tab, you will see at the bottom of that page 'Notify me of a new Ubuntu version'. Set that to 'For long-term support versions'. When one is available it will be shown in the update manager when you update.

Which release are you currently using?

(PS: There is no way to downgrade apart from a clean install. For instance, if you are on 12.10 and want the 12.04 LTS, a clean install it is.)
