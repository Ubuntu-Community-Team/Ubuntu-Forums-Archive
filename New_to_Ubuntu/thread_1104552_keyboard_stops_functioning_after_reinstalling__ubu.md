### Post by lavinog on 2009-03-24
Is it a usb keyboard or ps/2?
if usb, does disconnecting and reconnecting it fix the problem?
do you have usb keyboard support enabled in bios?
Maybe try a different port...it could be that your usb header is overloaded so try a front port if you have one.
Is your bios up to date?

I have a couple of microsoft usb keyboards that don't like any of my gigabyte motherboards. What is the make and model of your keyboard and motherboard?
