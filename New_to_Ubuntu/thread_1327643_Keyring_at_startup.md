Everytime I start up my computer keyring asks me to enter my password in order to get my wireless connection to work. I would much prefer it didn't ask me and my wireless connection connected automatically upon start up.

---

### Post by heyho on 2009-11-15
Right click on network icon in panel, select edit connections, wireless > edit, and check the "make available to all users" box at the bottom left.  Close, and all should be good.;)
