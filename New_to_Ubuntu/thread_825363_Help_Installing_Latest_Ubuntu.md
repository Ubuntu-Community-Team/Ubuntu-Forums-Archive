### Post by Paqman on 2008-06-11
Congrats!

Make sure to check that all your hardware is working while you're still running off the LiveCD. If it's all good then you can proceed to installing it on your system.

You can do that in two different ways. Either click on the "install" icon on the LiveCD's desktop and follow the guides (you'll need to do some disk partitioning this way) or you can boot back into Windows and put your LiveCD and use the new simplified "Wubi" installer.

Make sure you back up any important data before you do though, just in case.
