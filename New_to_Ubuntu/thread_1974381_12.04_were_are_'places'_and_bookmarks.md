### Post by Paqman on 2012-05-06
> **vallvi said:**
> My question: where do I find 'places' that used to be on the 10.04 menu bar?

It's actually a still there. Both of these features are actually part of the file manager (Nautilus), which is still part of your system. Right click on the Nautilus icon in the dash and you'll get a list. It's also available when you open a Nautilus window, under "Go" and "Bookmarks" on the top bar.

Since Nautilus is always there you can also use HUD to bring up the list whenever you're in Nautilus or a blank desktop.

> 
 And how do I bookmark specific subdirectories on my computer?


Same as before: either hit Ctrl-D in that directory, or go to Bookmarks > Add bookmark in Nautilus.
