### Post by diablo75 on 2009-03-30
I'll bet money your burner is slowly dying.  I've watched many a drive die over the years.  It's not sudden, more gradual.  The first tracks in a burn might seem fine, but the intensity of the laser during the burn seems to get weaker (I've even seen CDs that came out where the bottom side looked like the rings in a tree trunk after being chopped down because the drive was trying to compensate by slowing down, holding it's speed, and then having to slow down again and again because the laser would continue to get weaker the whole time).

I'd buy a new one if I were you.  Get a cheap Lite-On or Panasonic from Tigerdirect for about $30.
