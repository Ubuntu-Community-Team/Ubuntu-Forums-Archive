Have you tried going into Keyboard Preferences and changing Key Maps to another, then back again?

Only possibly suggestion I have is opening your Home Directory, then press **Ctrl+H** to show hidden files and renaming the **.gconf** directory to **.gconf-bak**

Then logout/login to see if the problem persists.
