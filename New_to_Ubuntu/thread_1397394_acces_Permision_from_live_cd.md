### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-02-03
It's not a bug, but a feature, intended to protect your files from being deleted by accident. To get full access, run <gksudo nautilus>.

### Post by NCLI on 2010-02-03
You can run this command from a terminal, or simply by entering it into the box that appears when you press alt+F2.
