### Post by Yvan300 on 2009-12-16
Yeah you can use a flash drive to boot from instead of the live cd. First you have to be sure that your pc supports usb booting. Then after that you just boot your pc and then go to system> administrator > Create startup disk and it's pretty straight forward from there. Btw, you could always buy an external hd and install ubuntu on it.
