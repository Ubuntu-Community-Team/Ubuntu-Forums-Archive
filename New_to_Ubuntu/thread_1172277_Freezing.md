### Post by philinux on 2009-05-28
If it freezes use this key combo.

Hold down Alt+SysRq and press k.

That will will kill the x session and take you to the login screen.

to fix the freezing first try disabling desktop effects. Sys>prefs> appearance >visual effect = none.

### Post by powel212 on 2009-05-28
To kill individual programs the visual way.

System/Administration/System Monitor

You can also add this little tool to your Taskbar by right clicking your taskbar - add - System Monitor

then you will have a visual tool right at the top of your screen showing CPU activity and if you just click it one time your System Monitor will pop right up
