### Post by el.puerco on 2010-12-06
I've searched for this for quite some time, and am continually frustrated it's not already a base part of the product. It seems like a natural fit.

The best I could find was to use BashPodder ([http://lincgeek.org/bashpodder/](http://lincgeek.org/bashpodder/)) to download ALL of the images from the RSS feed(s) into my Pictures folder. Then, I use the Pictures screensaver.

The problem with this solution is 1) I have to run the BashPodder script, and 2) it eats up hard drive space to store ALL of the images - space that's at a premium on my netbook. I'm sure I could figure out how to schedule the script to run at startup, but since it's not an ideal solution anyway I haven't bothered.

I wish I was a better programmer and could write a screensaver like this. I'm puzzled why no one has.
