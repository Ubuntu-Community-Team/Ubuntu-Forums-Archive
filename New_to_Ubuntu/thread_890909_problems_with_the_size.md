### Post by indecisive on 2008-08-15
If your screen resolution is messed up, try to go into System->Preferences->Screen Resolution, and set the screen resolution to the highest numbers without making the refresh rate too low (or the screen will flicker unbearably and you may get a headache).
--EDIT--
Please reply if this does not solve your problem, for there are other, more advanced ways that may be necessary.
