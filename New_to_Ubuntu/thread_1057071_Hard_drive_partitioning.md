### Post by kestrel1 on 2009-02-01
I wouldn't resize your partitions unless your Linux install requires more space, but I don't expect it does.
With regard to swap I would leave it alone, I have seen post's where people have resized swap & screwed up the system.

### Post by Crafty Kisses on 2009-02-01
Yeah, there's no real need to resize your swap from what you've told us, if you feel your getting low on Linux space, you can always resize the partition.
