### Post by tenmilez on 2009-11-19
If all you want is the click then you should be able to use a combination of xdotool and xbindkeys (I think that's the right combo). I have the buttons on my mouse remapped to alt-tab and such. However, this will click wherever the mouse cursor is at so unless you're clicking in the same spot the entire time you'd still have to move the mouse around.
