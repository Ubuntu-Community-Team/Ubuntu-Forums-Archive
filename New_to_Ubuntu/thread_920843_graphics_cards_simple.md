### Post by sudo_chop on 2008-09-16
The other card you have is probably PCI-express, and it soudns like your mobo takes AGP. We need to know what Motherboard you have though, to know what kind of card it can take. Its probably AGP if those cards fit into it, but it may only take an AGP 4x card and in that case an 8x card wont work... Do you know anything about your mobo? 
 
The cheapest, but still high quality cards on newegg are about 35-45 dollars depending on the spec of your mobo, and would yield you roughly 4 times the performance of the cards you have there. I'm not sure what places like Best Buy and Circuit City carry, but they may have that sort of card there that you can just pick up.
 
I have no idea which of those cards you have is better, I'd say that they are all just sort of at the same level of suck. I can't find a much in the way of specs on those cards on the web. If you really want to know that I would say to just plug each of them in and see how it performs...
 
Anyway, see what you can find out about your mobo...
