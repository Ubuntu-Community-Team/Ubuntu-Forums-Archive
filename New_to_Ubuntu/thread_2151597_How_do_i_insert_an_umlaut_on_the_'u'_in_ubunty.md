### Post by Impavidus on 2013-06-05
compose, u, "
compose, ", u
ctrl-shift-u, fc, <enter>
ctrl-shift-u, fc, <space>

Using the compose key is the most convenient (in my opinion), but you may have to configure a compose key: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ComposeKey](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ComposeKey) . Easy access may be possible on some keyboard layouts.
