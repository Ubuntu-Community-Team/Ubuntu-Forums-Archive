### Post by warbread on 2008-05-06
Have you ruled out heat issues yet?  Often times people forget to clean out their CPU heat sink and then they get something like this happening.  When you play videos or transfer large files, it gets the CPU going, and just a few degrees increase in temperature could trigger the computer to shut itself down.  Try running Gkrellm through Gdesklets and monitor your CPU's heat.
