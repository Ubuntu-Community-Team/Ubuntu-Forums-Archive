### Post by coffeecat on 2010-12-09
Try re-inserting the disc or try another disc. The OK button will only be usable if InfraRecorder can detect a usable disc. When you inserted the blank CD did Windows pop up a small window asking what you wanted to do with it? If it didn't, then the OS or perhaps the optical drive didn't detect the disc.

This could be a problem with either the disc or the optical drive itself. Some drives are fussy with certain brands of discs. If you are out of luck with CDs, there's no reason why you can't burn the image to a DVD+R or DVD+RW. I got so fed up with problems with a bad batch of CD-RWs that I now use DVD+RWs for everything.
