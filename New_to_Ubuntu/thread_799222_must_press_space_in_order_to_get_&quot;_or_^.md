### Post by HotShotDJ on 2008-05-18
> **joethesmith said:**
> after pressing shift+6  or shift+' I have to press space in order to get " or ^This is the behavior of the USA International (With Dead Keys) layout.  I use it all the time, although I don't know how you accidentally ended up with it.  Go to System --> Preferences --> Keyboard --> Layouts and see what you have.  You may need to add a new layout (for example USA with Default variant) and then remove your current layout.
