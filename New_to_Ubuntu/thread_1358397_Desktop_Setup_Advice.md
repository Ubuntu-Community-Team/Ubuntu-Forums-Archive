### Post by tacantara on 2009-12-18
Check out the Community Cafe section of the Forum.  Every month, a thread is maintained for people to post screenshots of their desktop.  I like to look at the variety of screen designs that people come up with, and I occasionally post screenshots of my own.  I have attached pictures of my December screenshots.

The wallpaper was something I discovered on the Forum (don't ask me where, as I've looked at hundreds of threads this month).  The dock is AWN 0.4, and the window dressing is a Vista-based theme (available from gnome-look.org) that requires the Emerald Theme Manager.

I totally removed the upper and lower task bars once I got the dock set up the way I wanted it.
