### Post by avtolle on 2009-03-04
Short answer is yes, that is what you will have to do to switch between the Kubuntu installed under Wubi and Vista. Wubi only installs the (K)ubuntu distro; once that is done, Wubi's job is over. This is not a virtual machine. Perhaps you should consider installing a virtual machine within your Kubuntu to run Vista, allowing you to switch as you wish?

```

```
