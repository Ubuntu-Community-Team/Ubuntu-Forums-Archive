"W:Failed to fetch [http://ppa.launchpad.net/ubuntu-hams/ppa/ubuntu/dists/natty/main/source/Sources](http://ppa.launchpad.net/ubuntu-hams/ppa/ubuntu/dists/natty/main/source/Sources)  404  Not Found

### Post by lechien73 on 2012-01-19
It seems that those repositories don't exist any more - the dists directory is empty. The easiest thing to do would be to remove those repositories.

You can do this by going into **Software Sources** - accessible through the **Edit** menu in the **Software Centre**, click on the **Other Software** tab and then uncheck the problem repositories.

Then try your updates again.
