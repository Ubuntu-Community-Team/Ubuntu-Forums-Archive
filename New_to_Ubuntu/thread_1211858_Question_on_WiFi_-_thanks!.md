> Are you sure you are broadcasting your network?  Try going to the network applet and clicking on "connect to hidden wireless network" - add your network and see if it connects.  Is there a reason for not having your network secure?

what wifi channel is your router set to use? In europe a broader spectrum is used compared to the us, to which ubuntu defaults. Try setting the channel in your router to something < 10. (you could also change the region of your wifi tuner in your ubuntubox, but this is easier) Also to improve the quality, try setting the router to a channel not used by one of your neighbors.
