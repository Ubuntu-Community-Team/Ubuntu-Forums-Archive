### Post by ad_267 on 2008-04-26
Have you selected a filter subscription? Addblock plus is pretty useless without this. I find I hardly get any ads at all using it.

To add a subscription go to Tools -> Add-ons -> Adblock Plus -> Preferences then click on Filters -> Add filter subscription and click on the down arrow to the right of the first box and select a filter.
