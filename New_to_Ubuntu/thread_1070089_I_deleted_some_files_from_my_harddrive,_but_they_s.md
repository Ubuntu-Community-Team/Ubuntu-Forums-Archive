### Post by spcwingo on 2009-02-14
Open up nautilus and navigate to your external drive.  Click view, show hidden, and you should be able to see a folder called ".Trash-1000".  Right click that folder and choose "move to trash".  That should bring up another window.  Within that window select "Delete".  Those files should, hopefully, now be gone.
