### Post by markbuntu on 2008-09-25
You need to right click on the Volume Control and choose preferences. Make sure the device listed at the top is correct and then choose Master or PCM in the box below. You may have to play around with it a little to get it to work but that is usually the way to  select what the multimedia keys control.
