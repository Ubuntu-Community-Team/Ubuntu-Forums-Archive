### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-12-26
I think the best way to recover emails contacts and bookmarks would be to boot Linpus and use the export/backup functions most browsers and email clients have. For example, Firefox can export bookmarks in .html, though the menu for that is hidden in the bookmark organization screen. Once everything is exported, boot into Ubuntu and import.
