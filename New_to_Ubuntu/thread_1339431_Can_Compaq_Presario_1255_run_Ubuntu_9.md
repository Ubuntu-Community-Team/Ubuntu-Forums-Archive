### Post by lisati on 2009-11-27
I concur with the other replies that a more lightweight distribution might be worth investigating.

By the way, when speaaking of version numbers, the numbers after the decimal point can sometimes be relevant, as there are usually differences of some kind. Ubuntu uses a numbering scheme based on the year and month, e.g. 9.04 was released in April 2009, and 9.10 was released in October 2010, so "9" isn't always the same as "9.04", "9.10" or even "9.1"
