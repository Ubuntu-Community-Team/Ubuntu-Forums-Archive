### Post by I2k4 on 2012-02-28
I'll just add that after trying 11.10 versus 10.10 pure Lubuntu, the earlier version is much quicker on my little netbook.  Users probably ought to be made aware that the move from the version 10 to version 11 of Ubuntu, even on the "lightweight" Lubuntu, was something like the move from XP to Vista in Windows - the newer Ubuntu is designed for more powerful hardware and is simply not as snappy on low powered machines.  (New users should also be aware that the full Ubuntu is more user-friendly for newcomers and Lubuntu/LXDE is less helpful about how to do simple things.)
