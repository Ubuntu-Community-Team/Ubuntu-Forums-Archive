### Post by jakev383 on 2009-02-20
There are some awkward ways to sync your BB with Linux in general. You can sync it with Evolution (see here: [http://www.linewbie.com/2007/12/syncing-your-blackberry-on-linux.html](http://www.linewbie.com/2007/12/syncing-your-blackberry-on-linux.html)). You can also look at scheduleworld.com and sync over the air (I'm working on this myself) or Funambol (scheduleworld is an off-shoot of Fundambol).
I can sync my contacts using Funambol, but my calendar craps out. I'm sync'ing my calendar with my Thunderbird/Lightning Linux laptop, my wife's XP laptop (these 2 work fine) but the BB chokes on the 4600 calendar items we have. Once I get that bug worked out it will be sync'ing with all 3 and on my own server here at my office.
