### Post by stefan27 on 2014-04-23
Hey Guys, 
usually you find questions on how to solve problems within forums - which is most probably the main reason why forums exits.

However, I was thinking to post this because I'm sooooo happy to have installed Lubuntu 14.04 on our ASUS EeePC.:P
The ASUS came to us with WIN7 Starter installed and was used only a short period of time. It got slower and slower (typical for
Windows systems I would say) and at the end it was not usable anymore. 
It was laying around for the last two years almost and close to be scrapped.

The nice design and the good keyboard made me thinking about an alternative OS.
I'm Microsoft OS user for almost 25 years and my last experience with Linux dated back 20 years. At that time Linux was not
handy to install and use. So I was not excited to give a trial on the ASUS EeePC.

BUT WHAT A NICE SURPRISE. I have followed the guidance on [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Lubuntu/GetLubuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Lubuntu/GetLubuntu)
and installed Lubuntu from an USB Stick. The installation was absolutely hassle free and easy.
The EeePC runs better than ever. It takes a while for Lubuntu to start up but the applications are running smooth and fast.
The Lubuntu package includes all you need for a netbook. The only additional program I have installed is Skype.

Hope this post will motivate other Linux newbies to give it trial. It is really worth it. 
Big thanks to all people who contributed to this great OS and distribution package.

P.S. last not least I have a question too - we are in a forum :)
Can anyone tell me why the cursor is jumping around while I'm typing this post? Is it Auto Save?
