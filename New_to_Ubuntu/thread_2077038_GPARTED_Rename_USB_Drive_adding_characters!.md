### Post by finechap on 2012-10-27
**If you use GPARTED to rename a USB drive:**

*You will notice that it adds three characters to your new name!*

So **MYUSB_NAME** becomes M**Y_USB_NAME nA**

Or something similar.

This is a fault with the current version of mtools
You will need to visit [here]("http://www.gnu.org/software/mtools/download.html")

And choose your preferred download, I went for:-
**[ftp://ftp.gnu.org/gnu/mtools/mtools_4.0.17_amd64.deb](ftp://ftp.gnu.org/gnu/mtools/mtools_4.0.17_amd64.deb)**

Move the download to your home directory, if it's a .deb type. Click on it and that's about it. Sorted.

That's better
Release 12.04 (precise) 64-bit
Kernel Linux 3.2.0-32-generic
GNOME 3.4.2
