### Post by linuxuser21 on 2009-04-04
In Rhythmbox, go Music> Import Folder/File.  To delete, just open the trash and click on Empty Trash.

### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2009-04-04
Yes you are deleting the files themselves not their shortcuts.
The best way I discovered to reduce desktop clutter is to put the music files in the music folder located in the home directory (found when you click on places> home)
You may want to change the download directory of firefox as this is probably the biggest cause of your desktop clutter.
