### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-08-16
If you need to make characters like that often and you're currently using an English keyboard, you may want to consider switching to an English-International setup, which makes it easier to create accents.  For instance, ä = " + a ; é = ' + e; ß = right-alt + s.  It works well for me, as I'm American but have to write often in French and German.

You can change keyboard layout at System>Preferences>Keyboard.

EDIT: sorry, I just saw that you're Dutch, but there may be a similar Dutch-International option for your keyboard as well.
