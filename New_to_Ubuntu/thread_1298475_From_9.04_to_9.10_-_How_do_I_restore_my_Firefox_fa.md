Xmarks actually sucks. It was good when it was Foxmarks, but now it takes ages to merge the bookmarks due to slow database writing/reading and sometimes even makes Firefox unresponsive. I don't recommend it anymore.

Just backup your Firefox profile with [FEBE](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2109). It will save everything, not only the bookmarks.
