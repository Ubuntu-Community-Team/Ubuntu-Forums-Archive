### Post by TBABill on 2009-12-23
Zoo, Karmic worked for my Atheros card on the first install. However, in Mandriva it was recognized and could see my wireless router, but it would always fail to connect. After some heavy searching online I found, on those systems, that I had to connect directly to my router (wired, not wireless) after installing Mandriva. Once installed I had to walk it through downloading all available updates. Once those were downloaded and installed my wireless came right up.

I have no idea why that works, but apparently something from the first install to the updated files must contain an update for wireless drivers.

I hope this helps with your system. Just run the updates while connected by wire and then try wireless. you may have to reboot, but then you should be good to go if that's a similar problem with Karmic for your hardware.
