### Post by SlappyPappy on 2008-05-24
I've built a bunch of PCs and my best advice is to take your time and double check your work. You will do something goofy and it will have you stumped for a little bit. Don't worry though, stay cool and you'll get it all sorted out.

Also, take notes along the way, stuff like which drives are connected where, etc. Then you can go back to look at your notes to see how you set it up. Save these for later. They can be a great help in troubleshooting or upgrading.

Good luck friend and enjoy. Nothing better than using a computer that you built yourself.  :)
