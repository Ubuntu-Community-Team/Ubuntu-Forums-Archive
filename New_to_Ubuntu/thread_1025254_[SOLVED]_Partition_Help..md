### Post by taurus on 2008-12-29
You cannot resize while you are using Ubuntu and you cannot unmount it since it won't let you.  Therefore, you have to use gparted from either the LiveCD or GParted LiveCD, [http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php), if you want to resize your partition.

### Post by adult swim on 2008-12-29
you can only make changes to drives when they are unmounted.  the best way to do that is to boot the ubuntu live cd and then go to system>administration>partion editor.  your drives will be unmounted and then you can make changes.
