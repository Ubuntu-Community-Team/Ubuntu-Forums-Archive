### Post by aysiu on 2008-06-16
Wine is not GTK- or QT-based, as far as I know, so the desktop environment should not affect the performance of a Wine-using application.

And if you have 1.5 GB of RAM, you shouldn't have to worry about performance anyway. Even the "heavy" desktop environments don't suck up all 1.5 GB of RAM.
