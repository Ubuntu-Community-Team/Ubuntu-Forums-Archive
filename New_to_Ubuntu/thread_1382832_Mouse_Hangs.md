### Post by lile001 on 2010-01-16
When I move my mouse, there is a he........sitation in the response.  This is annoying in normal mousing around the web, but maddening when I am trying to use an art program, since the mouse must be used more precisely.  

I'll move the mouse, and usually it will respond as I move.  However sometimes it will hesitate.  Usually my hand-eye coordination kicks in, and I move the mouse a little farther since it isn't moving on the screen. Then the cursor jumps, and overshoots the mark, often ending up clicking on something I didn't want to hit.  In a graphics program this drives me crazy, and believe me that is a short drive. 

This is a good quality optical mouse - and I've checked it for hairballs.  Same thing happens with an old ball mouse. Happens whether I am playing music or not, so it doesn't seem to be a bandwidth issue.  Happens randomly, often not at all for a minute or two, then it will hang for maybe half a second or one second.  What is going on here?
