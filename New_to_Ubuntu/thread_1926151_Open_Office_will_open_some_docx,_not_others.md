### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-02-16
Sorry ... but there's nothing much you can do to fix this problem in Linux.

MS Word 2007 and 2010 are the ONLY apps that will reliably open and properly format a Word document saved with the .docx extenstion.

The docx extension is a format that Microsoft adopted with their release of Office 2007, and they further complicate that by having that format chosed by default whenever you create or save a Word document in MS Office 2007 or 2010.

Some folks have had better results using abiword to open docx files.  If you haven't tried that, you should.
