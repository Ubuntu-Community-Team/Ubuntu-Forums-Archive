### Post by sallam on 2010-09-24
Many thanks for all the suggestions.
Paper notes are fine, but hard to edit or search or find what you need. And you have to take it wherever you go, and can deteriorate over time. You also need to find it, and find a pencil, and sharpen it. Too much work for a lazy guy like me.

I keep all my notes in one large text file, and I back it up regularly by gmailing it to myself, as a compressed attachment.
I don't see an advantage for wiki-like notes over gedit. Ctrl+F is all I need to get to whatever data I saved earlier.
I think its better to keep one single large note instead of several notes. Easier to handle, search, add or edit.
