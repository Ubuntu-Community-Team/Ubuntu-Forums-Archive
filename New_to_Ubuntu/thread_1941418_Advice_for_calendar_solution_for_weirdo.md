### Post by Zill on 2012-03-15
carbon512:  If you use Firefox then the [ReminderFox]("http://www.reminderfox.org/") add-on *may* do what you want.
> [Can I share events with Google Calendar?]("http://www.reminderfox.org/documentation-user-guide-sharing-reminders/#r9")
In a limited fashion, yes. It is possible to view events from your Google Calendar in ReminderFox, but it is not possible for you to add events in ReminderFox and have them appear in your Google Calendar.  In this scenario, all events must be added using Google Calendar, and ReminderFox  will act as a ‘Viewer’ of these events.
