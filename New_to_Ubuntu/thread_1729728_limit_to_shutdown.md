### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-04-15
I am at this moment using 11.04 Beta Edubuntu. It has some programs that are not installed in standard Ubuntu. One program that might do what you want is called Lockdown Editor. I do not know if it is in the Ubuntu Software Centre of standard Ubuntu but it is in the Edubuntu Software Centre.

Under the Panel tab you get the options to Lock down the panels, Disable force quit, Disable log out.

Here is the website address
