### Post by Marlonsm on 2009-08-11
Have you added your user to the "Vbox users" group? The same thing was happening to me some time ago.
To do so:
-Go to System > Administration > Users and groups
-Unlock and put in your password.
-Click "Manage groups".
-Find "vboxusers" and click Properties.
-Add your user to it and click OK.

Now the USB Dongle (or any other USB device) shouldn't be grayed out.
