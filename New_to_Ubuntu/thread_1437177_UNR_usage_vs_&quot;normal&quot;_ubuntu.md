### Post by snowpine on 2010-03-23
If you are a "tinkerer" looking for maximum performance, you can do a lot better than Ubuntu (or Netbook Remix), IMHO. Ubuntu is designed for ease of use, out-of-the-box one-size-fits-all configuration, and an attractive, full-featured Gnome desktop. These goals come at the expense of performance.

That being said, I have the full Gnome desktop (with Compiz desktop effects) on my Asus eee 900ha, I find that the hardware is more than capable of giving me the full experience, no tinkering necessary.
