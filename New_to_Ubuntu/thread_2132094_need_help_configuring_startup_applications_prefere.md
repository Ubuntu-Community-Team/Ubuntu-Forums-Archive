### Post by Traxster on 2013-04-03
Saw this question posted on this forum on another thread, however it went unanswered. I found [this]("http://askubuntu.com/questions/126268/how-to-find-out-the-terminal-command-of-an-application") intuitive how to on another site, however, it is quite simple to find name and command for your app. On nautilus, navigate to usr/share/applications. Find your app, right click on it, go to properties, and you will see name and command. Enter information in startup applications preferences. See pics below.
