### Post by KeepItFunky on 2009-01-08
So I figured it out...
For the Gnome GUI:
1. I went to System> Preferences> Keyboard> Layouts
2. On mine the selected one was 'USA International (with dead keys)'
3. I clicked on on the '+' to Add a new selected layout and selected USA and United States (in my case).
4. I found I needed to reset to get it all to work.
