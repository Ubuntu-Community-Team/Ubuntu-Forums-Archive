### Post by Linuxisfast on 2012-11-07
I don't know much about Granola but the Ubuntu kernel has been highly tuned and patched for normal power savings with stability. I have used Jupiter and the results are varied depending on the machine used, with my ASUS K53E laptops, there is no difference on the icore5 machine. However it makes good 45 min diff on my ASUS 1015B netbook. Even Colin who tuned the Ubuntu kernel came to the same conclusion with Jupiter, he has posted a test report on using Jupiter and the power savings he got, in his case as well, the results were negligible.
