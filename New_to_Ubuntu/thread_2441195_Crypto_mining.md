### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2020-04-20
I wouldn't bother. The easy to find solutions to the mathematical problem at the heart of bitcoin have been found a long time ago, the remaining solution are very hard to find which means you'll spend Dollars of electricity for cents of bitcoins. Even using GPU-based mining doesn't pay enough to be worth it. The only ones still making money on bitcoin mining are using specialized FPGA based mining rigs.

Holger
