### Post by redsoxreed on 2008-12-27
There is a hidden folder in your home folder called .themes.  You can see it by pressing control + h.  GTK themes get installed there.  In the folder .icons (also a hidden directory in your home folder), all the icon themes you installed on your system will be there.
