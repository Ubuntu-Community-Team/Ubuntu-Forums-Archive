### Post by cnorrisl on 2010-03-04
Hi all,

I've been struggling for weeks trying to get the microphone to work on the newest version of skype, operating with ubuntu 9.10 netbook remix.  My sound recorder worked, but the microphone was useless in skype.  

I downloaded pavucontrol for pulseaudio, and adjusted my mixer for my microphone so that the right side was turned all of the way down with the left side still all of the way up.  This might be a fix only for internal mics that are mono, but the specs on my laptop told me I had a stereo mic and doing this still fixed the problem.  I've seen a ton of other posts about this, so I hope this helps someone.
