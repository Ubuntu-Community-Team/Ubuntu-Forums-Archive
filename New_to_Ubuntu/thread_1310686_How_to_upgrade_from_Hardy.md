### Post by ranch hand on 2009-11-04
I, personally, would wait.  I also would backup and do a clean install.

The reason for this is that 10.04 will be the 3rd version put out to run on ext4 and it is great.  There are also an awful lot of very basic changes taking place in you your base system runs.  I really believe that you are asking for grief upgrading that much.

I do not know a thing about your box but most will support more than one drive and drives are getting bigger all the time.  Drives are also, relatively, inexspensive.  Get another drive and install on it.  Easier than backing up, just transfer the files and then get your apps installed.

9.10 is great but rough around the edges.  10.04 is going to be smooth, well I believe it will.
