### Post by roger_1960 on 2011-11-19
Hi

It could be a permissions problem.

Open nautilus (file browser).  Select View>Sidebar>Tree.  Select the drive (normally listed at the top) and select File>Properties (from the menu in the top bar).  Select the permissions tab.  Does it give you create/delete files access?  Are you logged in as the main user or as a guest?
