### Post by HavocXphere on 2009-02-19
I don't know of a specific fix for the edged borders. In my case they are grey and aren't a real issue.

There is a FF theme called "KDE4 + Firefox3"  that fixed some of my issues. Not sure whether it will help though since you are already running a theme as is.

The other way around this is to change the QT-gtk settings to Raleigh in the system settings. This will fix the problem, but it will make FF look blocky & old school.
