### Post by migs73 on 2010-06-11
If you are using the SSD for boot does that mean you will have a partition on your other drive for your home folder? If so you could copy your home folder from your VM onto removable storage and then repopulate your new one with this once you are ready for it. Don't know if this sorts out your programs but will take care of your settings.
