### Post by QLee on 2010-12-01
Below the field where you entered your username, is there a checkbox for Remember password. If you check it and send an email the system will prompt you for a password and then remember it.

If it's a laptop, I suggest you don't use "remember". So, when you lose it, people can't send mail as you.
