### Post by baligirl on 2010-04-21
Hi Harry,

To follow up on where to find Dolphin once you've installed it:

Go to System->Preferences->Main Menu.  This will allow you to add something to your "Applications" menu in the upper left hand corner of your screen.

In the left column (Menus), select where you want to put it (such as "Accessories").  Then click on the "New Item" button.  Fill out the box so that the "Type" is "Application", the "Name" is "Dolphin", and the "Command" is "dolphin %i -caption "%c" %u".  Then click on "OK".

Now you can go to Applications->Accessories->Dolphin, and hopefully you'll be good to go.

From what I read, it seems like Dolphin is similar to konqueror but more focused on the file manager stuff.  Some links to look at:

[http://dolphin.kde.org/](http://dolphin.kde.org/)
