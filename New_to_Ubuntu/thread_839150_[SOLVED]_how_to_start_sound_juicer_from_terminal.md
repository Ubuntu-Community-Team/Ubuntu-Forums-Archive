1) Right click on **Applications** and select **Edit Menus**.A window will open so navigate to **Sound & Video** and make sure **Sound Juicer** is ticked.

2) Open a terminal and ```
sound-juicer
``` and the GUI should open.

Good Luck

### Post by cpetercarter on 2008-06-24
To add to Sound and Video

Right click on the menu bar.

Select "edit menus", "Sound and Video", "new item"

Enter Sound Juicer as the name, and "soundjuicer" as the command, and whatever you like as the comment.
