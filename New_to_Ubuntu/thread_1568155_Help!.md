### Post by audiomick on 2010-09-04
> **argentineboy said:**
> If I run Ubuntu from my flashdrive, and say I install a program ON UBUNTU (such as flash player, wine), [COLOR=Red]will it download on my flashdrive or my hard drive[/COLOR]. And say I wanted to erase Ubuntu from my flashdrive. (I never said I wanted to) Would that be possible?
If you are running in the live environment, be it a Flash Drive or a CD, it will not install anything on to your hard drive. It will also not save anything onto your hard drive unless you specifically tell it too.

I haven't tried the persistance thing, but I think Ozymandias_117 is right: the purpose of that is to have a live environment that you can tailor to your needs. Otherwise, anything you "install" in the live environment is just installed into RAM, like the system itself, and is gone again when you shut down.
