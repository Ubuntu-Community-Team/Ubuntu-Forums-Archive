### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-05-05
well there is no official way to install this application, as its still under development.
you should be able to just unzip the file you downloaded and place it into your home folder or something.
then on your desktop you can just right click and select "create launcher" with the name "songbird"
and for the command click the "browse" button and go to the directory where you put the songbird folder into, and then just select the executable and click "open"
for the icon same basic thing, click the box with the spring thingie in it and when the icon loader pops up click on the "browse" button and make your way to the songbird folder ang to to the chrome folder and into the icons folder, then into the default folder and click "open" and select the songbird icon and hit "ok"
and again "ok"
that works for me :D
