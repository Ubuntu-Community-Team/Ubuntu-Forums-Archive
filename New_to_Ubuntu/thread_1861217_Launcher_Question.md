In 11.04 - I was afforded the option to access the launcher ONLY when I moused to the left-corner of the screen. Is there a way to establish this in 11.10? I hate the left-side access, as it often results in an unintended accessing of the launcher.

### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-10-15
The answer is yes. You do it the same as in 11.04. Install CompizConfigSettingsManager. Open it and tick the Ubuntu Unity Plugin and open it. Then where you see Hide Launcher click the menu and choose Autohide.

Regards.
