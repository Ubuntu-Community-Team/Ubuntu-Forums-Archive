### Post by amanchesterman on 2014-12-09
There are two 'Google calendar addons' for Thunderbird: the _Google Calendar Provider_ and the _Google Calendar Tab_. If you want us to help you with one of them we need to know which it is: also please tell us your version of Thunderbird and your version of Ubuntu.

For information I have tried both of them and now I use neither. I didn't like the look and feel of the Calendar Tab and I found that the Calendar Provider was unreliable. Instead, I now have Thunderbird set up to use my Google calendar through the CalDAV system. This has been very effective and dependable since I installed it and it does not require any special Thunderbird extension. If you want to use it, post back here and I will provide instructions.
