### Post by silkstone on 2008-08-25
It's all a matter of opinion of course, but to me a resolution of 1680 x 1050 on a 20" screen makes everything too small - a 22" screen is much better, and has approximately the same pixel pitch as a 19" monitor at 1280 x 1024.

I have two 22" monitors and find the combination of real estate and default text/icon size just right.

Having said that, if you're using a 17" screen at 1680 x 1050 your eyes must be better than mine!

By the way, it's not ideal to run an LCD monitor at anything other than its native resolution.
