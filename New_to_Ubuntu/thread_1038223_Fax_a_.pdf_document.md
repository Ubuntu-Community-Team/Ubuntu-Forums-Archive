> **bobnutfield said:**

You cannot fax PDFs - only plain text with TPC, due to the way it works.

Some of the paid fax services give you a few free credits to "try" the service.

The other option is to use fax software and a dialup modem with your own phone line; I have only ever done this in Windows though.

### Post by HotShotDJ on 2009-01-12
This one looks good:

[http://faxzero.com](http://faxzero.com) (can fax *.doc and *.pdf)  -- the free service has page limits (3), allows up to 2 free faxes per day and includes adverts on the first page.  The premium service is $1.99 per fax via paypal for up to 15 pages per fax -- VERY reasonable (thats about what I pay at FedEx) and they don't seem to require a subscription like other services do.

As with a previous poster, I have no personal experience with them.  If you decide to use them, would you be so kind as to post your experience?  I could see myself needing something like this someday.
