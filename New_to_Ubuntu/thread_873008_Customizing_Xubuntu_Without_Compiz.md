### Post by cardinals_fan on 2008-07-28
Xfce is very customizable.  Mess around with your panels (or remove them).  Change the window manager settings.  Add keyboard shortcuts.  Try new themes.

All that can be done from Xfce's settings manager.  Hit Alt-F2 (or open a terminal) and enter "xfce-setting-show" without the quotes.
