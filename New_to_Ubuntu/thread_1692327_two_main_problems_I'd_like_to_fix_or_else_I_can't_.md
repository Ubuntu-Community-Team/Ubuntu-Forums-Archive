### Post by Mikew123 on 2011-02-21
Hi

I'm assuming that you are running Windows in Virtual Box.
I had this issue with USB devices and found that I had to select the relevant devices from the Devices>USB Devices menu at the top of the Virtual Box Window.

You should see a list of all the USB devices that are connected (and installed in the case of a printer).
If any do not have a check next to them, click on the relevant device and it will then be available to Windows.

I'm also assuming that you have created the relevant filters in Virtual Box.
If not I'll go back on the steps I took to get my printer and web cam working.

Hope this helps
