### Post by jimmy the saint on 2010-03-02
If you open the file browser in Ubuntu you will see a few disks listed on the left hand side.  One of them will be your windows installation partition.  If you click on it, it will mount and you will be able to navigate your windows files.  No need to copy then, though if you really want two copies of everything you could just drag and drop them to wherever you wanted the copies.

As far as the wifi settings, just click on the wifi icon, select your schools wireless network and enter the password if it asks for one.  There shouldn't be any need for special configuration.
