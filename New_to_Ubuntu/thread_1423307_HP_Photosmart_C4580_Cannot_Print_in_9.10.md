### Post by GameOverDood on 2010-03-06
I have a C4780 model but I think it'll work the same way...

1. Log onto your router's settings. (If you have Linksys, you may have to type in 192.168.1.1 on your web browser and then log on with the correct credentials.)
2. Go to the Wireless tab.
3. Under Basic Wireless Settings, click on Wi-Fi Protected Setup next to Wireless Configuration.
4. Below should be a field where you can enter a PIN number. There, you can enter the PIN number of your printer. (Read your printer's manual to find out how to get this PIN number.)
5. Click the Register button.

The steps above assume that your router is a Linksys router but it should be somewhat similar regardless of brand.
