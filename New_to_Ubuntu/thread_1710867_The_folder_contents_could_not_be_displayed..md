To use a LiveUSB, you insert the USB, and then reboot your computer.

You want the computer to boot from the USB, and not the hard drive (or SSD) in the computer.

It may do this on it's own, or you may need to change the 'boot order' in the computers 'BIOS'.

If your computer doesn't boot from the LiveUSB on it's own, then reboot it again and look for the text in the BIOS screen for setup or boot.  It can be F10, F11, F12, DEL; every computer manufacturer is a little different.  You can change the boot order to be USB first.

Once you have booted from the LiveUSB, you will be able to install the newer version of Ubuntu from the LiveUSB.
