I would try this first [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1471395&highlight=flash+fullscreen](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1471395&highlight=flash+fullscreen)


My only other suggestion is cleaning the laptop. I think it may be that the video card and/or the processor are over heating due to a build up of dust/junk on the fans exhaust.

This is a common problem on laptops that are 3-4 years old, especially if you have pets.

Some models are easier to clean that others. If you are not comfortable taking it apart I would suggest taking it to someone that know what they are doing.


Also on a side note have you noticed that a full screen flash application will not keep the screensaver from activating in ubuntu?
