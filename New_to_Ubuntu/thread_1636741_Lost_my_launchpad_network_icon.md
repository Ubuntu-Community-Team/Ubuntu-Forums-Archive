Can I ask what you mean by 'launchpad'. If you are referring to Ubuntu's bug management, I am now very confused. However, if it is the WiFi icon that is missing, try right clicking on panel , go to properties and set the pixel size to 25 **do not close the window**. When this is changed, revert back to 24 pixels. Hopefully you should get the icon back. 

[B]Other Fix
[/B]Right-click, click Add to panel. Scroll down to **notification area** (_***NOT ***_indicator applet) . Double click **notification area** to add it to your panel. 

One of these fixes should work. If they don't, or you actually mean launchpad as in [http://launchpad.net/](http://launchpad.net/) then I cannot help you

However, I hope this helps!:KS
