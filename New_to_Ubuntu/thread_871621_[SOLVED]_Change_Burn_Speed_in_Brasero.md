### Post by rhodry on 2008-07-27
> **safetycopy said:**
> Hi,

When you get to the "Burn" commit page there is a 'Properties' box next to the drive that has been detected. It is greyed out unless you have a disk in the drive.

Just click on the 'Properties' box and you can set the burn speed as one of the options in there.
