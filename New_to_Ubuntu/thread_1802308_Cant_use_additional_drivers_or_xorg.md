### Post by Wim Sturkenboom on 2011-07-12
The 8400 in combination with the nouveau driver (that some idiot seems to have added in the kernel if I understand it correctly) is more than likely the cause. Did you apply a 'nomodeset' to the grub bootline? If not, when the grub menu shows, press 'e' for edit, add *nomodeset* at the end of the line that contains quiet splash and press <ctrl>X to start.

This will prevent the piece of crap called nouveau from loading.
