### Post by stalkingwolf on 2009-02-07
a couple things that have worked for me.

1 clean your CD/DVD drive.  sometimes just blowing it out works.

2. make sure you burned your CD at the slowest speed and used quality media.

3. remember that sometimes boot with the live CD takes a bit longer
on a laptop because of drive speeds and, at least with mine, if you dont
have a floppy plugged in you may get I/O errors because it cant find a floppy drive.  Depending on the laptop it will scan 2 - 4 times to find it
then go on loading.  if Im in a hurry I just plug in my USB floppy and off I go.
