### Post by JustinR on 2010-07-11
You have to put your saved game data in the correct place. Like if, for example, you play Sims 3 under Windows - it saves everything to your my documents folder:

"C:\Users\Justin\Documents\Electronic Arts"

When you play Sims 3 in Wine, it also saves this in your my documents folder, usually here:

"/home/justin/.wine/drive_c/users/justin/My Documents/Electronic Arts"

So if your saved game data is in a folder called Electronic Arts in My Documents under Windows, like in the game Sims 3, then put the saved games folder in your Wine My Documents folder.
