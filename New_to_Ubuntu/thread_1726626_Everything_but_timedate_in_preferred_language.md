### Post by rosencrantz on 2011-04-13
Wish I knew Finnish... ;-)
There are two possibilities:
a) Regional formats in Language Settings aren't Finnish (not likely, if I understood your comment about suffixes correctly)
b) Ubuntu's Finnish translation is not complete and patches holes with English.
According to 
[https://translations.launchpad.net/ubuntu/maverick/+translations](https://translations.launchpad.net/ubuntu/maverick/+translations)
it's probably b).
So this is something you can actually fix yourself. Get a translator's account and translate ahead. 
You might want to start on Natty though and just wait for the update - it's going to take some time anyway before translations get accepted.
