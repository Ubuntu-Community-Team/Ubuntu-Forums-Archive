### Post by baracuda68 on 2009-01-04
Goto your FF Preferences>Content>Fonts&Colors section> Advanced button.
Select your preferred Font size & types, then _UNcheck_:"Allow pages to choose their own fonts,instead of my selection above."

Hope this is what you are needing.

:)
