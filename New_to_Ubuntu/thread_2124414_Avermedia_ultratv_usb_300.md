### Post by squakie on 2013-03-17
With USB devices, before we can even try to do anything, we need to know the chipset.  Please plug your device it, open a terminal window, and type:

lsusb <press enter> (that's a lower case "L")

Highlight and copy the results, then post them back here in a reply.

We may or may not be able to make this work, but we need that inforation first.
