> **hopelessone said:**
> Hi,

I have a nvdia video card,

And the TV display is 1/4 the size?

how can i make the TV display to it's full size?

You can't on an s-video port.

You can't count on more than 800x600 on an s-video port although some of them will do 1024x768.  If you want higher resolution you'll need to use either a VGA or DVI port.

I have a Jaunty machine hooked up to a 50" HDTV here - over the VGA port I can only get 1366x768 - in order to get 1920x1080 I had to buy an DVI to HDMI adapter and come in over the HDMI port.

Works fine, though.
