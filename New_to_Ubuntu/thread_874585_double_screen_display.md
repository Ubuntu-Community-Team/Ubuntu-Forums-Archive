Another beginner's question: I seem to have two independent desktop displays, one won't show the open applications in the other. Occasionally I accidentally switch between them when moving the mouse, but haven't figured out how this works. Can anyone tell me what this is and how to use it properly?

### Post by jordanmthomas on 2008-07-30
You've probably accidentally used your mouse's scroll wheel with your mouse on the desktop.

To switch between the two workspaces, press Ctrl + Alt + (Left or Right)

You should also have an applet in your panel (by default at least) that shows which desktop you're currently on.  You can change to the other by clicking on it.
