### Post by Vorian Grey on 2008-07-11
You open up your Home directory and then in view menu of Nautilus click on show hidden files. Find the .gimp folder and then go to brushes folder and you'll be able to drag and drop your brushes there. If you have the Gimp open you'll have to restart it before the brushes show up.
