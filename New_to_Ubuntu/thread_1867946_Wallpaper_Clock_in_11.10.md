Is it possible?  Yes.  Pretty much anything is possible with Linux.

A better question would be how much effort it would take.  I've seen a couple screenshots of a Conky setup with 'wallpaper clocks', which I would take to mean that you mean something like this: [http://milax.org/img/conky172.png](http://milax.org/img/conky172.png)

Conky is very configureable; the default setup looks nothing like that.  There are threads on here for customizing Conky; you can install it with
