### Post by Curtis6767 on 2012-05-26
Ubuntu recommends upgrading from 10.04LTS to 12.04LTS after the first maintenance release.

Scroll down this page to find this: [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PrecisePangolin/ReleaseNotes/UbuntuDesktop](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/PrecisePangolin/ReleaseNotes/UbuntuDesktop)

12.04.1 is scheduled for release in August.  

Unfortunately, upgrading seems to be hit or miss and many people just end up doing a fresh install

As far as your files go, I'd back them up regardless of what you end up doing. Supposedly the upgrade will preserve your files but why take the risk? And if you end up having to do a fresh install due to problems with the upgrade process, then at least you won't be worrying about those important files.

GL
