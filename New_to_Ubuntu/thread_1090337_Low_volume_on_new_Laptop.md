### Post by marshall.robert on 2009-03-08
If you click preferences, and tick all the boxes there, especially stuff like PCM and Front. These are the 2 that affect me. I find that, after a fresh install, both PCM and Front are around the half way point.

It's like PCM is the volume of your sounds, Front is the volume of PCM and Master is the volume of PCM.
