### Post by coldraven on 2013-10-06
Ctrl+Alt+T opens the terminal but you might like to install guake, just press F12 for a drop-down terminal. You will look like a cool hacker to your friends.
After installing, add guake to "Startup Applications".

Ctrl+Shift+C copies from the terminal
Ctrl+Shift+V pastes to the terminal
