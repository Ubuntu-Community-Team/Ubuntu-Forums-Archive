I don't know what you have or haven't tried, so here's some basic stuff - apologies if you've done all this.

Have you checked the audio options to make sure the correct sound card is selected? I sometimes find that Skype forgets its audio settings, especially across a reboot when all the sound card IDs can change.

I also find I have to uncheck the 'Allow Skype to automatically adjust my mixer levels' option, and set the relevant volumes manually - Skype's auto-adjust feature has a habit of being very quiet then suddenly deafening me. It's silenced the audio completely a couple of times as well.
