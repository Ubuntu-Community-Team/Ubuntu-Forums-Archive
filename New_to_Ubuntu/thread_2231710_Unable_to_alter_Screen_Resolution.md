### Post by 3rdalbum on 2014-06-27
There may be a solution for you, but I'll just let you know that SIS graphics chips are very problematic on anything except Windows. Not only are they not very good to start with (not even on Windows), but SIS has not helped the Linux community with making their GPUs work correctly. Hence, you have a problem.

Hopefully somebody here has had the same problem and knows how to sort it out.
