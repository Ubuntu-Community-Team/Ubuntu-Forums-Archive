### Post by ptd2 on 2009-07-12
I would like to be able to create a hanging paragraph in Open's Office's Writer. I don't see an option for doing this. (Hanging paragraphs are those paragraphs where the second and subsequent lines of a paragraph are indented. They are used in bibliographies).

---

### Post by Tom Collier on 2009-07-12
Right-click on the paragraph that you want to format. Left-click on "paragraph."  Then, type a positive number in the "Before Text" box (i.e. 0.50"); then type a negative number in the "First Line" box (i.e.-0.25").  That will give you a quarter-inch hanging indent. Alter the dimensions to suit your needs.
