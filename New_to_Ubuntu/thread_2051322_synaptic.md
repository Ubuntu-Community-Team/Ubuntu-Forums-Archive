### Post by deadflowr on 2012-09-01
When you find it, if you want to place it in the launcher to the left, either drag it over from the dash or when you launch it, an icon will appear at the bottom of the launcher, right click and then lock to launcher.
If you want to rearrange the icons in the launcher, simply hold the left click on the mouse down on the icon, and pull it to the right, then move to where you want it and slide it in.
This method only works on Unity-3d, Unity-2d works differently, as the icon doesn't get pulled out but rather grabbed in the launcher to move.
