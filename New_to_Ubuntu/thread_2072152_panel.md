### Post by Peripheral Visionary on 2012-10-17
Hi! Welcome to this super great community and to Xubuntu, best of the 'buntus! ;)

The Volume Control *is* "Mixer," and the icon depends on what you have select when you add it to your panel.

Assuming you're using Xubu 12.04, then:

*Right*-click on the panel (or you can right-click the desktop, choose **Applications** and then **Settings**, **Settings Manager**, and then **Panel**.  

Three tabs appear: Display, Appearance, and Items. Click on the **Items** tab and then on the **Plus sign** to the right (since you want to *add* something to your panel). You can add all kindsa stuff from here, from launchers to applets. I think "Mixer" is an application, so a**dd a Launcher**, choose **Properties**, select Mixer, and choose an icon.  Hint: You don't have to settle for whatever icons your are first offered. You can select "All Icons" to expand the list and choose any of them!

### Post by Linux_junkie on 2012-10-17
It's the 'indicator plugin' you want to add to the panel to get the adjustable volume control.

Right-click on the panel -> select panel -> Add new items -> select Indicator plugin -> click ADD

That should do it.
