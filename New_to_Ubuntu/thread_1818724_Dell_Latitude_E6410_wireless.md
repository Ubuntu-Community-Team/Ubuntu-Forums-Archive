### Post by digitography on 2011-08-05
Just installed 10.10 onto a Dell Latitude E6410, all working hunky dory except for the wireless, laptop doesn't seem to recognise that there is a wireless card there.

I have installed 10.10 onto numerous workstations and laptops in the past with no real problems.

### Post by mikewhatever on 2011-08-05
Have you tried the Additional Driver utility yet? If not, check it out under System->Administration.

### Post by digitography on 2011-08-05
Well kind of found the answer through bodging.
The Laptop for reasons I cannot explain, had the wireless card turned off in the bios. So the first install of 10.10 didn't find the card. I then discovered that the card was disabled so enabled it, however Ubuntu didn't find it on reboot.

Any to cut a long story short, I did another install (I have 10.10 on a Yumi boot, only takes 10 mins to install), then did all the updates, and voila the additional software widget popped up, so now good to go.

I'll mark it as resolved.
