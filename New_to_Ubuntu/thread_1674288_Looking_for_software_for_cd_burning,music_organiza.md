### Post by StaticIp on 2011-01-23
I think we all need a little more information from you about your phone.. Model, firmware, etc.. As for ringtones, what format do they have to be in? What length do they have to be? Ubuntu should recognize most phones automatically, it does with my phone anyway (iphone 3gs). 

For music organization I would suggest "MusicBrainz Picard". It will take all of your music and tag it properly, sort it into folders for you, and get the album art. Very nice software, and free. I've used it on my 20,000+ collection without problems. 

Cd burning just use the included software, rhythmbox and Brasero.

For making your own music use "LMMS" or "Linux multimedia studio"

Word processing - OpenOffice 

ALL are free and easy to use. 

Hope I could help.
