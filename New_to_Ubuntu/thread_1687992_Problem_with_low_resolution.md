### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-02-15
The screen resolution defines the aspect ratio as well.  Before the advent of wide screens, the resolutions defaulted to 5:4.  Now, with wide screens and super wide screens, there are three different "shapes".

So, when you switch to a different resolution, the "shape" of the screen will change to match that resolution.

1024x760 will yield a different "shape" than 1440x800.
