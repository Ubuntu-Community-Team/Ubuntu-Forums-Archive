### Post by mgmiller on 2009-07-19
If you really want to know how to generate this envelope template, here is how in OO writer 3.0

1) Start writer
2) In upper left corner click the drop down next to the "New" button.
     (This is the button just below the "File" drop down.)
3) Select "Templates and Documents"
4) On the left side, click where it says Templates.
5) On the right side, double click "My Templates"
6) "Envelope" should appear.
7) Double click the word Envelope

8) If you have filled out the other parts of OO where you tell it who you are and give it your address, it should pop up the envelope template, with your return address already inserted.


Have fun....
