### Post by wolfen69 on 2008-08-06
this is how i got apple trailers to work. when you get to the part where you pick the video size, right click the size you want, and select "copy link location". then open vlc and go to file>open network stream>http... and paste it in. click OK. done. if this doesn't work you probably need the quicktime codec from synaptic.(quicktime-utils/quicktime-X11utils)
