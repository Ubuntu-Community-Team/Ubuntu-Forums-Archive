it looks from this thread

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2085094](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2085094)

that the Epson N10 driver works;

get it from here

[http://download.ebz.epson.net/dsc/du/02/DriverDownloadInfo.do?LG2=EN&CN2=&DSCMI=16878&DSCCHK=4be73117a4e330f8f4b08216d6d243176481fae6](http://download.ebz.epson.net/dsc/du/02/DriverDownloadInfo.do?LG2=EN&CN2=&DSCMI=16878&DSCCHK=4be73117a4e330f8f4b08216d6d243176481fae6)

you will need either the [COLOR="#008000"]epson-inkjet-printer-n10-nx127_1.0.1-1lsb3.2_i386.deb[/COLOR] or 	
[COLOR="#008000"]epson-inkjet-printer-n10-nx127_1.0.1-1lsb3.2_amd64.deb[/COLOR] depending on your system (32 or 64?)

if you go to Epson's current site

[http://download.ebz.epson.net/dsc/search/01/search/?OSC=LX](http://download.ebz.epson.net/dsc/search/01/search/?OSC=LX)

and type in L200 it gives you a scanner driver only

if you want a how-to on installing the scanner drivers, please ask
