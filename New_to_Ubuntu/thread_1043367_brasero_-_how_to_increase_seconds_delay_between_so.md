### Post by NewJack on 2009-01-20
In Brasero, you can insert a pause in between tracks that is 2 seconds in length.  You can do this one of the following ways:

1- Right click on the track you want the pause after and choose Insert a Pause

or 

2- Highlight the  track you want the pause after, Go to Edit > Insert a Pause

As far as I know with Brasero, the pause is a fixed length of 2 seconds.

Hope this helped!
