### Post by coffeecat on 2010-09-17
Mint is based on Ubuntu. The default theme is quite different and they've tweaked the gnome desktop and added their own design main menu. One important difference is that Mint comes complete with a lot of restricted stuff so that multimedia and DVDs (mostly) play out of the box. With Ubuntu you have to install this yourself, but people here will help you with that. It's not difficult.
