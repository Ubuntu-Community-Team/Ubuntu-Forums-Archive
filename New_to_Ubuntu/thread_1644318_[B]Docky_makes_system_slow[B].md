### Post by Rasa1111 on 2010-12-13
Sure. 

 First,
 How did you install it? 

Shouldnt matter,
but you can remove it from the same place.

I think for newbees,
the software center is most comfortable. 

So open "Applications" menu,
Go down to "Software Center"
Open it up. 

When it opens, 
You'll see a 'column' on the left hand side. 
Click on "installed software"
Scroll down until you see "Docky", and click the "remove" button. 

 Then you'll probably need your password, 
and if it doesnt start uninstalling..
just click "remove" once more. 

 that should do it.
