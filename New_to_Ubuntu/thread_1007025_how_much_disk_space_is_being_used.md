> I am embarrassed to ask this, but where in the GUI (Gnome) do I go to find out how big my drive is and how much free space there is?

You can use the terminal to view your free spaces:

Command:
```
df -h
```

EDIT: Or the GUI way

Applications->Accessories->Disk Usage Analyzer
