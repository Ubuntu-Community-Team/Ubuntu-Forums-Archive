### Post by 3rdalbum on 2013-07-10
As 2Stoned says, it sounds like you need to install a graphics driver. Click on the Ubuntu logo near the top left corner of the screen and type "software" - one of the first results will be "Software Sources". Click it and when the window appears, go to the tab on the very right which is to do with drivers.

You'll be offered one of several graphics drivers to download, just go with the recommended one. After a reboot you shouldn't have the flickering and lagginess anymore.
