### Post by Bartender on 2008-12-25
Our Acer 5920 has worked quite well in a Vista/Ubuntu 8.04 config.  As mentioned previously, the card reader doesn't work (bug reports exist).  Some of the keyboard buttons, like the little blue-lit touch buttons for playing DVD's, don't work.
Acer gets grief for flimsy construction (ours developed a small crack on the bezel surrounding the screen near the right hinge so perhaps it's justified?) but if you compare internal components Acer gives you more bang for the buck than HP and Dell.  And Acer seems to build more units with Centrino parts, reducing wireless hassles.  Apparently 8.10 works with more wireless chipsets than 8.04 so maybe that's less of an issue?
One hassle with Acers is they come with four primary partitions.  I built, then ran the recovery DVD's.  End result was one Vista partition, clearing the way for Ubuntu!
