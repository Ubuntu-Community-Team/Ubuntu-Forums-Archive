When entering your password in ubuntu, nothing appears on the screen for security reasons. You should just enter it end hit return. 

For installing software in ubuntu, always prefer the software centre (found in menu Applications). Bookmark [this]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installingsoftware") page.

I'd suggest you download and read [this]("http://ubuntu-manual.org/download/10.04e2/en_US/screen") book; it will definitely help you with your first steps with ubuntu.
