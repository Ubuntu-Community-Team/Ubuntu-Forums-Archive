### Post by TKRL2 on 2008-10-12
I always get my alot of my Icons from [http://rocketdock.com/](http://rocketdock.com/).

extract the Icon to pictures (any where..), right click the dock, dock pref., Launchers tab, click add, navigate to the icon, and set the command and give it a name.  Then close and restart the dock, and your done.
