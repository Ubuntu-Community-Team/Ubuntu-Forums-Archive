BTw , dont force restart. Two ways to easily restart with no permanent damages
1 ) hit ctrl+alt+backspace
 this will restart X. I think those are the keys
2 ) press ctrl+alt+sysrq(prntScrn) ( in sequence the following keys ) + R + S + E + I + U + B 
 this will restart your pc unless it has really froze. If this does nothing work then force restart.
