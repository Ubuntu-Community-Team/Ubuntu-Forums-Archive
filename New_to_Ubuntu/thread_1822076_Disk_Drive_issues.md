### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-08-10
To rule out a simple hardware problem, when you first boot the PC next time, start pushing the CD/DVD drive button immediately -- BEFORE you get into Ubuntu.  If it works then, it's a problem in Ubuntu, not your drive.

As to holding the tray open, yes, that will damage the retraction mechanism over time so it won't close anymore.

Have you tried booting from media in the drive?  Give that a go (put the media in there before you boot the PC) and see if that works.  IF it does, then again, it's not a hardware problem with the drive.
