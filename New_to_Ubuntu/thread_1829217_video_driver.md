### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-08-21
Linux is not Windows -- you don't just go charging off installing drivers.

If you're seeing a desktop, you ALREADY have video drivers installed.

If you're having problems with the video, then describe those.  It may not be a driver-related problem.
