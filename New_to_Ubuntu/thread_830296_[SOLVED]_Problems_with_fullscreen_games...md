### Post by Sukarn on 2008-06-18
Guys/Gals,

My suggestion is to use Compiz-Switch. Its what I use. I have put it on my panel, so whenever I want to play a game I just have to click that icon once and I'm out of compiz, and when I exit the game I click it again and I'm using compiz again.

I think I originally read about it here - [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=662926](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=662926)

The webpage for Compiz-Switch is - [http://forlong.blogage.de/article/pages/Compiz-Switch](http://forlong.blogage.de/article/pages/Compiz-Switch)
