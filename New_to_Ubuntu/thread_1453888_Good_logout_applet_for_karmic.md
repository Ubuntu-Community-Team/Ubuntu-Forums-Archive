### Post by Kir_B on 2010-04-13
I'm not sure, but you may be talking about a "drawer".

Just right click the panel, select "add to panel", and then select "drawer". You can then select a shut down applet in the same fashion.

Also, you can right click the icon, select properties, then click on the icon in the properties dialog and change the icon to one that you'd like

Hope that helps. Kirby

### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2010-04-13
Right click on the panel and choose Add to Panel
It's called
Indicator Applet Session
Click Add then Close

Or do you mean to stop the confirmation window from popping up?
[quote="Uffe"]Are you tired of having to confirm when you have to close, restart your computer etc. in Ultimate Edition 2.4 & Ubuntu 9.10 you removed it the following way:
```
gconf-editor
```
Then, select "apps > indicator-session" and put check mark by "suppress_logout_restart_shutdown"  and the annoying little confirmation window is gone  ;)[/quote]
