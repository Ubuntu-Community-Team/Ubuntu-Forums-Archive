### Post by BDNiner on 2008-10-05
AutoCAD requires the .NET framework to run. At least for 2004 to 2006. So I don't think that you will be able to get it running on xubuntu. But other than that xubuntu can fullfill your needs. 

Openoffice will work with most office documents, Some of the more complex Excel, Powerpoint and Word documents will have issues in Openoffice. 

Most printers work with Xubuntu but not all, I would research each printer to be sure that it runs.
