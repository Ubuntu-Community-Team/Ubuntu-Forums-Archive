### Post by oldrocker99 on 2015-01-22
There is lots of information online and in books on how to use Linux. People who go from Android to iOS have a learning curve, people who go from Windows to OSX have a learning curve, and, certainly, people who go from Linux (or, for that matter, Windows 7) to Windows 8 have a very steep learning curve. Learning any new OS requires some education, but, with the vast amount of information on how to use Linux, and the huge amount of helpful people on this very forum, you can get past 99% of problems, and soon become comfortable with what we consider a far superior OS.

The more you know about computers, the more you'll appreciate Linux.
