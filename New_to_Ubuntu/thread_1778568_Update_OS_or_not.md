### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-06-09
The important part is the Long Term Service, the three years support that you mention. This means that you will still get important security fixes until the three years end. Some Ubuntu releases are marked as LTS so that those who do not wish to upgrade every six months do not have to do so. It gives you more control. This could be important when using Ubuntu in a business environment.

Regards.
