### Post by bug67 on 2010-02-17
The PowerPC architecture hasn't been supported by Ubuntu for quite some time.  That doesn't mean you can't get it to work though.  I personally can't help.  I tried to install (if I recall) Hardy on my G4 PowerBook and gave up after I finally got it to boot and did NOT like what I was going to have to do at all.

Having said all that, there are a few things I can suggest:

1.  Try Debian.

[http://www.debian.org/](http://www.debian.org/)

Ubuntu is based on Debian and is fully supported on the PowerPC platform.

2.  Try posting up or browsing the mac.linux site:

[http://mac.linux.be./](http://mac.linux.be./)

Those folks are super knowledgeable about all things Linux on Mac.

Best of luck and have fun!
