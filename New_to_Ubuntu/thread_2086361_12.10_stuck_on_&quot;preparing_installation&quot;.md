After a few attempts  installing 12.10 on a multi-boot box (Win7/several Linux) with each resulting in a perpetual "spinning wheel" I halted install, rebooted to Peppermint3, opened gparted and created a new ext4 partition for the 12.10 to be installed.
[SIZE=2]BINGO!!![/SIZE]
**Rebooted off 12.10 cd and away she sailed !!!**
