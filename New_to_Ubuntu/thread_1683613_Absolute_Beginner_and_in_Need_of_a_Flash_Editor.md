### Post by frotzed on 2011-02-07
The short and long of it is that decent Flash editing / creation is not natively possible on Linux as far as I'm aware.  Your best bet is going to be 1) run Flash editor through WINE or 2) dual boot windows just for that one program.

Edit: Incidentally, this kind of proprietary jail in which Adobe Flash finds itself is part of what turns many people off towards it.
