### Post by jrothwell97 on 2009-11-06
> **paulbyr said:**
> I screwed up something an the system, places and (is it? applications tags are gone.  How do I get them back?

Right click on the panel and select Add Items. Find the Menu Bar applet, and add that. That *should* work.

If there's no panel at the top of the screen at all, you'll need to add a new one by right-clicking on the bottom panel and selecting "New Panel".

Good luck!
