### Post by decoherence on 2011-05-04
Do you have to log in to your computer or do you automatically get to the desktop when you start it up?

If you have to log in, it would be that password.

Keychains are occasionally weird things and are not necessarily your user account password.

If you automatically get to your desktop when you start up, try resetting your user account password by following the directions here [http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/resetpassword](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/resetpassword)
