### Post by JC Cheloven on 2011-02-20
Please be aware:

- Ekiga is great. It's free software and uses an open, well documented, protocol for the communications. Unlike the closed source, secret protocol (which worries me the most, see my sig) Skype. But both users have to use ekiga. Due to the 'secret' protocol, no other program will communicate to skype.

- Surely skype is kinda the fact standard. But it has a number oh ethical issues behind that the the foss community should not forget. Helping governments spying on the users, and having open backports open for who-knows-what-purpose among them. 

Personally, I see the skipe version for linux as undesirable. 
Just my opinion though.
JC
