### Post by TheFu on 2012-12-06
I don't think the 10.10 gnome desktop is available for 12.04, but there are other alternatives that are easily installed, selected for use from the login screen, and switched between at login trivially.  Look for these in synaptic.
* lxde
* xfce

If you add certain PPAs, then you can load other desktops like cinnamon.  Here's how [http://www.ubuntugeek.com/how-to-install-cinnamon-1-4-in-ubuntu-12-0411-10.html](http://www.ubuntugeek.com/how-to-install-cinnamon-1-4-in-ubuntu-12-0411-10.html)
