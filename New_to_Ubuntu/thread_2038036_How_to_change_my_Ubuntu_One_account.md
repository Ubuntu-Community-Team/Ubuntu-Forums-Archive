### Post by blackwolf92 on 2012-08-05
Open the Ubuntu One app on your ubuntu pc, navigate to Devices and Remove  your pc from the list. After doing so, try to reconnect your pc to the new account (hopefully ubuntu one will ask u to enter your info again).
Hope this helps!
