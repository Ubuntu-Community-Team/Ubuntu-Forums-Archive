### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-10-24
Right, add/remove is a very simplistic installer and doesnt have all the applications you can install on your system listed.
Synaptic is a more powerful package installer, its located under system> administration> synaptic package manager.
Fair warning:
You will have to port your bookmarks and cookies on your own, so if you dont have your bookmarks loaded under firefox 3 you will have to manually port your old bookmarks file over.
