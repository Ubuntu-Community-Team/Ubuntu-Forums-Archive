**What is faster - the wired, the wireless, or connecting to both of them at the same time?**

Technically, wired is faster.
Wire runs at 100gb or 1000gb, depending on your setup.

Wireless runs at 11gb, or 51gb, again, depends on your hardware.

So computer-to-computer is faster on wires...
Although how much you will actually NOTICE this increase is highly dependent on what you are moving across the network.

BUT, your INTERNET speed is capped by the pipe entering your house...
1.5meg on DSL, 5meg, maybe up to 10meg on cable.

And of course, us paranoids prefer the security of wires.
Not to mention, I can watch video over my net easily,
with minor impact on transfers.
