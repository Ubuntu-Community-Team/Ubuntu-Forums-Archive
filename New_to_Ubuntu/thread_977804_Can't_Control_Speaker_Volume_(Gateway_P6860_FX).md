### Post by fooman on 2008-11-10
try double-clicking on the speaker icon.  that will bring up the alsa mixer.  play around with the sliders there and see if you can't raise it any higher.

also...on a laptop there is usually an fn key,  you press that along with  1 of 2 other keys (volume up - volume down) on your keyboard to control volume.  they should be clearly marked somewhere on the keyboard.  on my gateway laptop,  the volume up - down are the page up - page down keys.
