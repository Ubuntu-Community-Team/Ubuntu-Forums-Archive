### Post by forrestcupp on 2012-04-06
> **PhantomTurtle said:**
> I have never used a live usb, but maybe it's because of that. Maybe it's because you boot from a USB.

That's what I was wondering. Did you actually install it to the hard drive, or are you just booting to the Live USB environment. The Live CD/USB doesn't go to a login screen. It's just for testing it out until you install it.

If you actually installed it, then you may not have noticed that the autologin checkbox was checked. If you ever install it again, pay close attention, and make sure that checkbox isn't checked. But there's no need to reinstall just for that. Like Frogs Hair said, you can change it in your User Accounts in the System Settings. But that's only if you actually installed to your hard drive, and are not booting to the Live USB environment.

If you installed it, but still have your USB stick plugged in, it may not be booting to your install, but still to the Live USB.
