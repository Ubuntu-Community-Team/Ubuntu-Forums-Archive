### Post by technosysind on 2011-09-20
Ok, then do the following:
Compiz Settings Manager>>Extras>>enable screenshot

Then goto Applications>>Accessories>>Take screenshot

Now select "GRAB THE WHOLE DESKTOP" and at the bottom"GRAB AFTER A DELAY OF '5' SECONDS"
Now when you click on screenshot, it will click a screenshot after 5 seconds and in those 5 seconds you can press whatever keys you want. 

I hope this solves the problem :)
