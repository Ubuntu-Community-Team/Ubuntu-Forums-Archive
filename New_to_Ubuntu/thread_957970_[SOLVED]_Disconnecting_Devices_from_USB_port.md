### Post by Bakon Jarser on 2008-10-24
From the ubuntu community docs:

> Unmounting/Ejecting

Before you disconnect the device, don't forget to unmount it. This can be done by right clicking the desktop icon and selecting "Unmount" (or in some cases, "Eject").
