### Post by rune0077 on 2008-06-18
Sound like your speakers. If headphones work and speakers don't, that would seem the logical conclusion. Maybe there's a loose connection somewhere. If you have some old speakers, or can borrow some, try plugging them in and see if they work. If they do, then you need to buy some new ones. If they don't, then it's probably not a speaker issue.
