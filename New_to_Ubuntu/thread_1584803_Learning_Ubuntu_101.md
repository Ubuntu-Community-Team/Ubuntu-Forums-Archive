### Post by snowpine on 2010-09-29
Welcome to the forums and congratulations on making the switch!

Other than these forums (obviously) the two best resources in my opinion are [http://help.ubuntu.com](http://help.ubuntu.com) and [http://wiki.ubuntu.com](http://wiki.ubuntu.com)

For example:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Printers](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Printers)

One problem you may encounter is that information quickly becomes outdated. I recommend being very cautious of tutorials you find on random websites; you have no way to know if this information is outdated or inaccurate. That is the great thing about these forums: you can ask any question and get an up-to-the-minute answer from real live Ubuntu experts! So I recommend starting a new thread about your printer (be sure to say specifically which model you have) and I'll bet you have the problem fixed within 24 hours. :)
