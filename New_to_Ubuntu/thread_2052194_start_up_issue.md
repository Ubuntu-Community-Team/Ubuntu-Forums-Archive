If she put a hard disk password then the news are not good. Usually the hard disk passwords written in firmware of the HDD . You cannot bypass this password. No. Buy a new HDD , it will cost you less from a technician to unlock the HDD.

If the password setted in BIOS , then you can bypass it , with hardware "surgery"  . Open the laptop and remove the little battery . The BIOS or motherboard battery , for 5 minutes. With this "trick" you will reset the BIOS in default settings.
