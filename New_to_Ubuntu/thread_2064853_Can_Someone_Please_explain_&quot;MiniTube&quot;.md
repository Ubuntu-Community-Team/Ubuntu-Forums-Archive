### Post by Javelin Dan on 2012-10-01
Thanks philinux -

The command for "WebUpd8ppa" seems to be what was missing. I haven't been able to try it on everything yet, but so far it has worked on two of four computers I have Minitube installed on. 

What I did was to uninstall Minitube, execute the "WebUpd8ppa" command, then reinstall Minitube. Don't know if it was necessary to uninstall and reinstall, but that's what I did and it worked - for me anyway. I'll go ahead and mark this thread as "Solved".
