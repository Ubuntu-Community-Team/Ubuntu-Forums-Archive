### Post by restorator on 2010-12-25
hdmi is "capable" of carrying sound, but not all video cards that have an hdmi output do. You should lookup the specs on your particular video card and computer. If you built it yourself such as I do you may need to connect two leads from your motherboard or sound card to the sound inputs on your video card.
