### Post by Zenze on 2008-06-16
> **bkamalnivas said:**
> I ve a 2mbps connection...
am able to get a max download speed of only upto 50kbps...
is there any way to boost this speed...?

It could be that your ISP is purposefully slowing down your download rate for torrents. Or it could be that the torrent that you are trying to download is just slow because there are not a lot of people seeding. Or it could be that you have a firewall up or do not have the proper ports forwarded on your router. And it could also be that your router has a problem with torrents if you have a Linksys router (the WRT54G series specifically).

There are very many reasons a torrent may be slow.
