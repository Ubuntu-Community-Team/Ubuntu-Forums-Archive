### Post by doctorbighands on 2009-05-11
First thing to check is to make sure that all of your volumes are turned up (main and PCM). To do this, go to System > Preferences > Volume Control. This gives you all of your volume options. Low volume through the speakers often means your PCM is turned down.

If you can't find Volume Control in your Preferences menu (which is often the case), open System > Preferences > Main Menu and make sure the box next to Volume Control is checked.

Hope this helps!
