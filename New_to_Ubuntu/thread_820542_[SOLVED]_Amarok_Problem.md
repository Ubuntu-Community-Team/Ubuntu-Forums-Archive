### Post by ruffEdgz on 2008-06-06
If there are songs and albums in the 'Unknown Artist' part of the collection, usually that means you need to edit those song tags to the accourate song artist, album, etc...

I don't know what else to tell you because the information given wasn't really detailed. When looking at my Amarok application, the 'Unknown Artist' section of mine actually hold all the artists that haven't had their tags edited yet. Once I edit them, they should move out of 'Unknown Artists'.

If it doesn't move, go to Tools >> Rescan or Update Collection and see if that helps (after you edit the song tags).
