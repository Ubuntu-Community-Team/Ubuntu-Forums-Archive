### Post by deadflowr on 2022-04-26
To switch to Xorg, logout and in the login screen after you select your username, but before you enter your password,
there will be a small icon in the bottom right corner.
Click on the icon and it should show the login session options.
Xorg should show as *Ubuntu on Xorg.*

Hope that makes sense.
