### Post by hewhowatches on 2008-10-30
See this page:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=953104](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=953104)

Have had the same problem following my upgrade from 8.04 to 8.10, only in my case it was Amarok...

As it says in the other post, go to Places > Computer > Filesystem.
Then right click on Home and choose Properties.
Click the Open With tab and select the Open Folder radio button. 
That should sort it.
