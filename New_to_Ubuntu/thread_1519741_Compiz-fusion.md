### Post by yossell on 2010-06-28
Is this the same as compiz? (I'm not sure if there's a difference these days)

If so, then I think just:

system-preferences-appearance
then clicking on the tab visual effects and clicking extra

will enable compiz. There may be a search for a suitable driver.

From a terminal, typing ccsm, will give you the settings manager (if it's installed) and lots of options to play with.
