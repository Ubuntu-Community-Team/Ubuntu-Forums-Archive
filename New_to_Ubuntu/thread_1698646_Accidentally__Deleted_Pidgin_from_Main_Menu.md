### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-03-02
You can add items to your Applications Menu by right-clicking it, then selecting "Edit Menus", then find where you want to add the item and add it.

You can also create custom launchers by doing the same thing, or right-clicking the panel and selecting "Add to panel", then select your application, or create a custom launcher to your application of choice.
