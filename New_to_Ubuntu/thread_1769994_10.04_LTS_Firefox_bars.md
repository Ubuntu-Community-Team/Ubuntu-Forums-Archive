### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-05-28
Yes. this is my Firefox layout:

[[IMG]http://www4.picturepush.com/photo/a/5749842/640/5749842.png[/IMG]](http://picturepush.com/public/5749842)

Tabs on the right (or left if you prefer), can be achieved with [Tree Style Tab]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/5890/"). 

The toolbar on the right, can be achieved with [Vertical Toolbar.]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/vertical-toolbar/")

The toolbar on the left is provided by [AIOS]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1027/").
