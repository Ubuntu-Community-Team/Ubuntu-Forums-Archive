Even though you may not be touching it, you still may be accidentally tapping the touchpad, which it is very sensitive to, and it shows in Ubuntu.

In **System -> Preferences -> Mouse**, select "**Touchpad**" tab. Then Enable the "**Disable touchpad while typing**" option.

Alternatively, you can also Disable "**Enable mouse clicks with touchpad**" if you don't use it for that.
