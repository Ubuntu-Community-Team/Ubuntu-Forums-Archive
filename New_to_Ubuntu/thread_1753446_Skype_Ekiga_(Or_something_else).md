### Post by BertN45 on 2011-05-09
There is nothing wrong with the computer and it should easily run Skype including the video. The choppiness should be somewhere in the network. You could try to lower the video resolution.

What are your upload and download speeds on both sides? use e.g. [http://www.speedtest.net/](http://www.speedtest.net/) 

Often dsl has a low upload speed and that might cause the video problems. In hotels often people want to call home using Skype all at the same time and that might cause you choppiness too. The speed test can tell you what is the best time to call in that case.
