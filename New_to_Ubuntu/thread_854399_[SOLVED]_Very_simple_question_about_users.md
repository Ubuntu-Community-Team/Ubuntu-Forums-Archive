### Post by DrOlaf on 2008-07-09
Again, it should be fine. All you are doing is changing who is accessing your computer through the monitor and keyboard that's attached to it. If you have multiple accounts, then you could be logged on through your keyboard and monitor, and your wife could be connecting to your machine via ssh over a network, and linux can happily cope with multiple different users being logged on at the same time. When you switch user, you're just really changing who is using the Gnome session to access your PC.
