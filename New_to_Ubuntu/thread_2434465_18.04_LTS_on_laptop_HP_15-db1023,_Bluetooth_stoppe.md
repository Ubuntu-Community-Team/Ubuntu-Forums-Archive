### Post by Autodave on 2020-01-06
He probably recommended 18.04 since that is an LTS release. (LTS = long term service.)  The next LTS will be 20.04.  The 20 stands for the year (2020) and the 04 for the month (April).  All other releases are short term: only supported for 9 months.  The LTS releases are at least 3 years support.  While the LTS releases are *much* more stable, the interim releases have the newest upgrades to them that will support the newest hardware.
