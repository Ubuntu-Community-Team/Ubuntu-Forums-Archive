### Post by Sef on 2009-03-28
Locked for the following reasons:

1) This forum is for asking questions about computers, Linux, and Ubuntu, or getting help for specific problems or both.

2) One cannot be an expert in everything. 

3) If one does not get a response for 24 hours, then one is may bump their thread to the top.  (Bumping sooner can get you an infraction or warning.)

4) Not every problem is fixable.  Some hardware is incompatible with GNU/Linux.

5) More people will see a thread if it is posted separately.

6) One question per thread.   That way your problem will not be lost if you post multiple questions.    It is better to make 10 separate threads with 10 different questions, than one thread with 10 questions.
