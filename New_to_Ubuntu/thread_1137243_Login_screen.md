### Post by stwschool on 2009-04-25
He may have ticked log in automatically when he installed. If so:-

1. System -> Administration -> Login Window
2. Enter your password when asked
3. Click on the security tab
4. Take the tick out of Automatic Login (and enable timed login if that's ticked too)

That should give you a much more secure system.
