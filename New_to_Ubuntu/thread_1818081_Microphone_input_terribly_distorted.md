### Post by rojaasensei on 2011-08-04
Ok, further testing shows, as hinted in some similar threads, that Pulse Audio Volume Control is part of the solution.

It does indeed seem necessary to reduce microphone volume, especially on the right channel. 

This greatly reduces distortion.

There is still noticeable hiss, but the improvement is very good.
This can be marked as solved.

Thank you
