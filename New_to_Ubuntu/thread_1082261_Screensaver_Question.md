### Post by hansdown on 2009-02-27
Hi PuddingKnife.

I'd just like to add to khelben1979's suggestion.

Please note the warning  when you scroll down on xscreensaver.

It is advisable to uninstall gnomescreensaver first, as it may clash with 

xscreensaver. Just search synaptic for gnomescreensaver and if the box is shaded, you need to click the box, click mark for complete removal.

Then install xscreensaver.
