### Post by Autodave on 2020-08-27
I have the exact printer and it took me almost 3 minutes to have it setup and running via wifi.  

You first need to navigate, on the printer, to the"connect wirelessly" or something similar.  Follow the on-screen instructions and make sure that your computer is booted and running.  It will connect to the PC and you will see a notification on the PC screen that it has been found.  After completing that, go to the Software center and instll hplip and hplip-gui.  That's all there is to it!  HP printers are, by far, the easiest to setup in Linux.
