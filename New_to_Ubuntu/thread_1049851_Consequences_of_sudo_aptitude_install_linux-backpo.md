### Post by Shazaam on 2009-01-25
Backports are just that; some VERY nice person(s) reworked/updated the file(s), usually from a newer version unavailable to whatever flavor of Ubuntu you are using. Such as taking an Ibex (8.10) version of an app and "backporting" it to Hardy (8.04). No harm no foul.
The "Proposed section is another animal. This section is basically for testing and not advisable unless you like debugging as things CAN break.
