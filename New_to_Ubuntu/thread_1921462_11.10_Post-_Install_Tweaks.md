### Post by davidr700 on 2012-02-06
I encountered a couple minor issues after installing Ubuntu 11.10 on an older Dell Inspiron 6000 laptop.
The first issue was I loss use of my touch-pad after tying in a browser search. Cursor stopped working!
The 2nd issue was 'adobe flash' was missing so I could not play  online video.
Everything else works like a charm, even my wireless connection!

I fixed the touch-pad issue by 'un-checking' the '_Disable touch-pad while typing_' under 'touch-pad' tab in the 'Mouse & Touch-Pad' folder under 'System Settings'. 

I fixed the missing adobe flash by installing it from the 'Canonical Partners' repository.

I have ubuntu on two of my older computers, a desktop and this laptop. I have ubuntu 10.10 on the desktop and have found it to be very reliable for my needs, i.e surfing the web, etc. So far I like 11.10 on this laptop.

Most issues seem to be minor w/ubuntu so just search the forums for answers and take your time, don't panic, and you will work it out. 

Hope above helps someone!

Cheers!
