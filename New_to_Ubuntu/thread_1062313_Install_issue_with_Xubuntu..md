### Post by yeats on 2009-02-06
You'll need to change the boot order in the BIOS settings on your computer so that your PC sees the CD/ROM before it goes to the hard drive.  Restart your computer and pay attention to any sort of "Hit F12 for Settings" or something. Esc, F2 - something will get you in there, but if the WinXP screen comes up, it's too late - just restart again until you can get it right.  Post back if this isn't clear :-).
