---

### Post by Devi1903 on 2010-07-09
Did you shift delete? Or did you just hit the delete button? If you just hit delete then it will have put the files into a hidden trash folder still on the device.

You can see this by showing hidden files in the view menu of nautilus. You can then select and delete this folder.

---

### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-07-09
Open the player in Nautilus, then click the Trash icon in the left panel. Anything there?
