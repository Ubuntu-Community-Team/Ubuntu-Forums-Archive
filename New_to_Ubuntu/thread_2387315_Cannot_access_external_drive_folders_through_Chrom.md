### Post by yancek on 2018-03-17
I'm not familiar with Chromium, but how are you trying to access your flash drives?  Generally, on a standard Ubuntu you should see a new icon in the left panel when you plug in a drive.  You should also see a new folder created when you plug in a flash drive under the /media/username directory (substitute your actual user name).  When you plug a flash drive in, before removing it do you select to safely remove or eject it?  Have you tried the flash drives which fail in another machine to test?
