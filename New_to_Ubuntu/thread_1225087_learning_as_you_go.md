### Post by Grenage on 2009-07-28
The best thing you can do it to go through the guides your following, set by step, looking up the commands used.  Once you get an understanding of what is happening, you can look at why it's happening.  This again, leads to more googling.

I know that's not a great deal of help, but it's how I (and most of the folks on here) learn things.

The link you provided shows symbolic links being used to direct the browser to where the necessary files for displaying flash content are stored.
