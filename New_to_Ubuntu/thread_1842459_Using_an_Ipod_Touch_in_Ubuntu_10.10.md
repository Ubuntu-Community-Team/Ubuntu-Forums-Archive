### Post by Jagoly on 2011-09-12
You can do most of the stuff you normally would do quite easily. Banshee (on the software centre) and rhythembox (comes with 10.10) can both sync music to the device, and banshee can sync video.

Unfortuanately, for most other things iTunes is a painful necessity, without it, it is very difficult to do software updates (apps are easy if you have wifi, but iOS updates need iTunes). Some jailbreaking tools can restore the firmware though on Linux (I think).

I have a win7 virtual machine with iTunes in it to update my iPod touch 4. But you could give a few other methods a go first.
