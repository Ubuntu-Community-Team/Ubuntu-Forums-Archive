### Post by Off Shore on 2011-09-12
The best way and, I think, the easiest, is to use GTK RecordMyDesktop. 
You can get it from the Software Centre. 
Once installed and on the Launcher you can mimic zooming in and out by left click and drag on the preview window.
Im sure a bright chap like the OP will soon master the knack of producing excellent screen recordings.

Cheers
