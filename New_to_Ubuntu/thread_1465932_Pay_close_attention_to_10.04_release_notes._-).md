This post has been edited:
I previously wrote that I thought that formatting with gparted to avoid the new partition alignment, and formating my partitions into ext3, solved my cd mounting/burning problems, but I ended up being wrong. My cd related problems returned in less than 48 hours, after burning 5 successful test burns or so. I do see some performance gains changing to ext3 (from ext4) in Kubuntu Lucid though. The wallpaper changes smoother, some kde apps launch a little quicker, and dpkg is a bit faster.

Enjoy Ubuntu.
