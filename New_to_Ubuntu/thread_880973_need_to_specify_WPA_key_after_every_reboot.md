[b][color=darkred]
Why are you in the manual network setup? Get out of there. Enable roaming on the network setup and then choose your wireless network on the upper right hand corner. You want to click the icon that looks like two little PC monitors. After you choose your wireless network, it'll ask for your WPA passphrase. Go ahead and put that in. Click OK and it'll remember the passphrase from now on.
[/b][/color]
