### Post by noobuntu7 on 2009-03-05
To answer your question... Liferea doesn't support RSS to e-mail. However, there are several online services which offer this capability for free. My favorite is [FeedBlitz]("http://www.feedblitz.com/"). When you sign up, you can choose how you want to receive your updates. Choose the e-mail option. Hope that helps!

(Another, slightly more popular option is [FeedBurner]("http://www.feedburner.com/"), you might want to check it out too).
