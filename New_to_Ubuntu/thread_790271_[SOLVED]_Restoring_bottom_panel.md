### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-05-11
Easy, go to your top panel and right click and select "new panel"
and in your new panel right click again and select "add to panel"
here are some suggestions on what to add:
Show desktop
window list (taskbar)
workspace switcher
trash

make sure they are locked too, by right clicking on each app and selecting "lock to panel"
