### Post by nothingspecial on 2009-07-02
You might be interested in [[COLOR="Blue"]mp3fs[/COLOR]]("http://mp3fs.sourceforge.net/").

What it does is mirror your flac collection as a directory of mp3s that don`t actually exist untill you use them.

Say you have a 500gig collection of flac files. That means you`re going to need about another 80gigs of space to store mp3s of the same music. This is a neat little way of saving that space.

When you drag them onto your ipod mp3fs will transcode them as it does it. The same if you click them, drag on to a usb stick or whatever.
