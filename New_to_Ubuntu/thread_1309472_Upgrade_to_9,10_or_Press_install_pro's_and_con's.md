### Post by feb3 on 2009-11-01
Don't know the definitive answer to your question, but here is my experience.
This is the first time that I have upgraded as I am fairly new to Ubuntu. It all went pear-shaped. No sound (this is a widespread problem it seems) and bootup became very flaky - sometimes would, sometimes wouldn't. 
Finally got fed up so I did a new install of 9.10 - sound came back and it was working but couldn't download ndiswrapper (the software centre is different now) so I have reinstalled Jaunty and all is well.
In future I will probably go for a new install as I have now created a separate Home partition but I will wait a good while before attempting it in the hope that it is bug-less.
