### Post by khelben1979 on 2010-11-21
It might be the open source ATi driver which causes your cpu to work hard since it's slower than the non-free driver. Have you checked the speed of the fans inside the pc if they are spinning slower than what they do with Windows7?

B.t.w. the non-free driver can get downloaded from [www.ati.com](www.ati.com) or by installing it from the Ubuntu software center application.
