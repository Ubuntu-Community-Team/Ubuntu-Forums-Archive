### Post by Dill on 2009-05-24
I believe the Ultimate Boot CD comes with hard drive diagnostic tests. If you check the UBCD page

[http://www.ultimatebootcd.com/](http://www.ultimatebootcd.com/)

and scroll down to the section on "Hard Disk Diagnostic Tools", you'll find quite a few you can use, most of which are brand-specific. You should use one that's specific to the hard drive in your gf's laptop.

The laptop may also come with internal diagnostic tests, which you may be able to access when the computer first boots up. On the first screen you see -- the "POST" screen -- look for any options like "Press F12 for Diagnostics", for instance.
