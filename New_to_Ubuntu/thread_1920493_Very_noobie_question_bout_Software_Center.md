### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-02-05
Or click on the button above and then click the "More Apps" button to get a listing of all the programs on your system; it's sortable by genre as well.

When you use Software Center you are also given the option to put the program straight into the Launcher (the bar on the left side). This option appears a few seconds after you click the "Install" button.
