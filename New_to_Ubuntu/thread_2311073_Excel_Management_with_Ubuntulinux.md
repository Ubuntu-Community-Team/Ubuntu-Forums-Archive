### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2016-01-24
Libreoffice Spreadsheet - installed by default in Ubuntu and most of its derivative favors - is the FOSS equivalent to Microsoft Excel.
Alternatively you can use the great WPS Office Suite (AKA Kingsoft Office) which is free but proprietary: [http://wps-community.org/](http://wps-community.org/)
