### Post by grumblebum2 on 2014-05-26
You can do this in the dash.
Hit the Super(windows) key+a and click on "filter results" at top right for categories.
[ATTACH=CONFIG]253453[/ATTACH]

or
if you must have an old style menu, install **classicmenu-indicator** from here...
Direct deb download link-----> [**_[COLOR="#B22222"]classicmenu-indicator_0.09_all.deb[/COLOR]_**]("http://www.florian-diesch.de/software/classicmenu-indicator/dist/classicmenu-indicator_0.09_all.deb")

The .deb should be handled by the software center when double clicked or being an older person you may have different method to deal with debs.
Once installed it is added to startup applications and will appear after logout.
