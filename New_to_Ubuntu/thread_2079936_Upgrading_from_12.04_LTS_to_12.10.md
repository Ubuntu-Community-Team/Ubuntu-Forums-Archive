### Post by offgridguy on 2012-11-02
The upgrade to 12.10 is different than an update in12.04.  
12.04  with all the updates becomes 12.04.1  whereas  an upgrade to
12.10 is an upgrade to another OS.

### Post by constellanation on 2012-11-02
in the updates window, click settings then click on the tab that says updates, change the setting "notify me of new ubuntu version" to the appropriate one.
