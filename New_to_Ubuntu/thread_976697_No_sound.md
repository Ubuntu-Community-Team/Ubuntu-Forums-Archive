### Post by eolson on 2008-11-09
I did an update on my lappy and the sound went south.  The update changed one of the volume control preferences.  Try right clicking on the speaker, select preferences and play around with those settings.  Mine wanted to be OSS vice ALSA,  yours may very well be different though.  Before anybody asks,   I do the upgrade and updates just to play around.   I'm running 8.04 on this machine.  Have 8.10 on two others.
