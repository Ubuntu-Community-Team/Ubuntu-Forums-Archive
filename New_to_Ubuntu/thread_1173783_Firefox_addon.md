### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-05-30
Right click on the toolbar, then select customize, then drag the bookmark item to the navigation bar. 

To make them show as icons, without text, right-click on the bookmark, select properties and delete the bookmark name.

It's just an workaround, but it works.
