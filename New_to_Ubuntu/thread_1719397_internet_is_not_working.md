### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-04-01
Can you set up a wired/ethernet connection to the modem router? If so, then get online that way and, go to system>Administration>Additional Drivers. Activate any drivers that are available. You may get one for your wireless adapter.

Here is a link to a trouble shooting guide.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessTroubleShootingGuide](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessTroubleShootingGuide)
