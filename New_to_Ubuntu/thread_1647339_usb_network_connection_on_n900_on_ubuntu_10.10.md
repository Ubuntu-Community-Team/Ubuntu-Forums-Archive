### Post by stooshbunutu on 2010-12-17
The is an app for the n900 called JoikuSpot, this allows you to broadcast the n900's 3G connection over a small-ranged wireless connection so you can connect to it and use it's internet.

This app does cost money on the store, however should you wish to obtain it via other means *cough* bit-torrent *cough* while I wouldn't directly promote the use of illegal downloads a very quick search can and will get you what you want easily.
