### Post by ibuclaw on 2010-05-13
Applications->Accessories->Passwords and Keyrings.

Under the 'Passwords' tab, you should see your '**default**' keyring.
Right-Click it and select 'Change Password'

### Post by ibuclaw on 2010-05-13
Another thing, when you logout / login. You will be prompted to unlock your keyring. To prevent it asking every time, select the drop-down item, and check the "Automatically Unlock at Login" box. Then enter your new password and OK it. :)
