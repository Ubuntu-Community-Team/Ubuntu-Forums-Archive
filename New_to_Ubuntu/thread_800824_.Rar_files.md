As mentioned in one of the above posts just double-clicking on the rar file will fire it into action.

It uses file-roller which is hidden in the menus by default. You can add this to your Menu by going to System > Preferences > Main Menu and then selecting Accessories and placing a tick against Archive Manager. (I think that's what its called in the Menu List, but since I'm not at my Linux box at the moment I'm not absolutely sure ...but you get the idea).
