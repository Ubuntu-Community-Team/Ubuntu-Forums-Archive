### Post by CloudFX on 2008-05-12
You can also go to System > Administration > Partition Editor (alternatively, gksu gparted), and then delete the Ubuntu partition, leaving free space.  When you go to install Ubuntu, a fourth option will appear to 'Install on the largest continuous free space'.
