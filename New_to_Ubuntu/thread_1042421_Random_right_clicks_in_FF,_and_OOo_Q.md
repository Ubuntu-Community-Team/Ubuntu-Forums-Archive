### Post by swoody on 2009-02-17
Well the Firefox right-click issue is one that I've had for a while as well. There's been bugs reported about it for some time now:
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/firefox-3.0/+bug/206295](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/firefox-3.0/+bug/206295)
You can subscribe to that bug, and it will update you on the progress, and whether there's been a fix for it or not.

Also, I'm going to assume you have a laptop? The cursor jumping around has happened to everyone that has typed on my laptop. It's more than likely caused by accidentally tapping your mouse with your hands while you're typing. This tutorial is pretty easy to follow, it simply sets up a daemon to stop your mouse activity within 2 seconds of typing, and it should take care of your problems:
[http://ubuntu-tutorials.com/2007/05/06/temporarily-disable-touchpad-while-typing/](http://ubuntu-tutorials.com/2007/05/06/temporarily-disable-touchpad-while-typing/)

If you're not using a laptop with a touchpad, or if those don't help you at all, please post back here and let me know :)
