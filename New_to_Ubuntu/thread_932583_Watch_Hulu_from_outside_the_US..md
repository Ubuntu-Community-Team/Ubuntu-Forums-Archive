### Post by Jim_in_Germany on 2009-09-26
What you could do is to grab the ip address of an American proxy server from here: [http://www.samair.ru/proxy/type-19.htm]("http://www.samair.ru/proxy/type-19.htm")
Then in Firefox go to edit -> preferences -> advanced -> network -> settings
and then choose manual proxy configuration and enter the proxy ip and the port no in the respective fields (the port in the no. after the colon)
Click on OK and then close and then you are good to go.
I've no idea if Hulu implements any kind of restrictions for proxy servers, however this is the method I use to listen to footy in the UK and it works fine.
Hope that helps
