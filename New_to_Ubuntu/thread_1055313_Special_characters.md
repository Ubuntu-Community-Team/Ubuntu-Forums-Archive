### Post by ajcham on 2009-01-30
> **Gotaro said:**
> How do I use special characters in Linux?  Alt+0176 should be my degrees symbol..  I *need* my degrees symbol!  :P

Where they exist, I much prefer compose sequences to Alt codes.

To set up a compose key: System » Preferences » Keyboard » Layouts » Other Options.  Then select a key to map to the compose key (I use the right super-key (Windows key).

The degrees symbol (°) can be typed with the sequence 'COMPOSE, o, o'
