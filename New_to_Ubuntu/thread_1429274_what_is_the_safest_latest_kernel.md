The general rule of thumb is to use the most current one. 

The main reason that the kernels are updated is because of such security holes and problems. When you update your system, youll be all set.

The only reason that you should hold back on a kernel is because your computer cannot handle it. Some older computers have some stability problems with the newer kernels.
