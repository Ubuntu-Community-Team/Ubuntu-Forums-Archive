### Post by tea for one on 2022-08-19
Use the address/navigation bar to find your desired website
Once the site is loaded, there is a star icon (bookmark this page) to the right of the address bar.
Click to bookmark.

To the right of the star icon, there is another icon (books on a shelf), hover your mouse to see "View and Manage Bookmarks"

To the right of this, there are three vertical dots, click to open more options. 

More info [https://help.gnome.org/users/epiphany/stable/](https://help.gnome.org/users/epiphany/stable/)
