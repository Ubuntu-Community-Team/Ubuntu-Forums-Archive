### Post by JillSwift on 2008-08-16
In KDE 3.5 it's easiest to open the K menu, then right-click on any sub-menu and select "edit menu". In there you can re-arrange add and remove menus and items all you want. Be forewarned: You can delete whole sub-menus without warning. Also, you have to "save" your changes for them to take effect.
