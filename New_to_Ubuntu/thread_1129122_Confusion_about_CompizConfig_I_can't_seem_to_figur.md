### Post by CatKiller on 2009-04-18
Beryl was merged with Compiz to create Compiz-Fusion, which is now included by default.

Emerald is still Compiz' window decorator, and you can still find plenty of Emerald themes on gnome-look and other places.

simple-ccsm is a good mid-point between Ubuntu's default options and the bewildering choice of options that ccsm gives.
