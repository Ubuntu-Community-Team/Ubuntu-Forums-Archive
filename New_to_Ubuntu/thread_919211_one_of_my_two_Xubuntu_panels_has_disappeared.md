### Post by aimpau on 2008-09-13
Just to be clear, when you say, panels, it's the one on top and at the bottom. However, you're saying: "it has two desktop" so I don't know what is really missing.

Anyways:

for the panel:

Press Alt+F2 and type:
xfce4-panel

for the "virtual" desktops or commonly known as workspaces:

Just go to settings, then workspaces.

Now, I'm thinking what you want is the "pager". This is the xfce plugin that allows you to see your workspaces in the panel. Is that what you want? If so, just do the first suggestion I made and just realign or add plugins by right+clicking on the panels themselves.
