It might help to see a screen capture, otherwise I'm not sure what you mean by "strange".

Since you see the problem in BIOS screens and they are presented before an operating system (or any drivers associated with it) are loaded, I would suspect a hardware fault. That could change colours but it should not change icons.

On the other hand, you said this happened after an upgrade so it was presumably working just before the upgrade. Is there any chance that the video cable was disconnected and reconnected at that time? If so, disconnect and inspect the connector to see if there any bent pins. [Edit] CAUTION - power off for this. That might give colour differenced but once again it shouldn't change the icons into something else.
