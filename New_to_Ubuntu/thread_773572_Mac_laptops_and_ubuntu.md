### Post by schauerlich on 2008-04-29
It will work, although you might have better luck using GParted to partition your hard drive and rEFIt to select which OS to boot.

This doc page will help you out loads installing on a MacBook Pro: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBookPro](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/MacBookPro)

Also, there's a forum here for Apple Intel users, which will help you get more specific responses to your question. One of the benefits of the Apple ecosystem. :) Link: [http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=328](http://ubuntuforums.org/forumdisplay.php?f=328)
