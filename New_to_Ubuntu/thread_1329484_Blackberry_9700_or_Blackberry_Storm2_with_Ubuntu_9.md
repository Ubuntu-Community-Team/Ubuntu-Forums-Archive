### Post by dca on 2009-11-17
In 9.xx vers I can charge (it's recognized) the unit by simply plugging in the included USB cable...
 
When you go into the options -> media card on BB and set 'Mass Storage Mode Support' to ON temporarily you can use nautilus & navigate to different directories and add music, pix, etc...
 
On the other end, Banshee sees/identifies the device and allows for music synching/managing...
 
When it comes to the total package BB Desktop Manager from RIM provided by your wireless carrier is only avail for Windows & Mac OSX.  That's the only way to perform non-OTA updates, perform b/u's, synch MS Outlook data, etc, etc... 
 
There's this that may still be in the Ubuntu repos but it was more of a pain because it's CLI and I spent enough time learning vmware from CLI to bother with messing with my Blackberry 8830WE & 8330 from VZW...
 
[http://www.netdirect.ca/software/packages/barry](http://www.netdirect.ca/software/packages/barry)
