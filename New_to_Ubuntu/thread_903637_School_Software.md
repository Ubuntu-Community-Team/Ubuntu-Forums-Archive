### Post by Thelasko on 2008-08-28
> Is there any existing projects preferably open source that has implemented this?
There is existing software, but it isn't open source.  A company called [Blackboard Inc.]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blackboard_Inc") has numerous software patents in the US over what you are trying to do (therefore no open source option).  They used to have a competitor called [Desire2Learn]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Desire2Learn"), but there was a huge lawsuit which Blackboard won.  D2L may go out of business soon.  I would keep your little project under wraps for a while, unless you want to get sued too.

Edit: I notice you are in Nigeria (therefore US patent laws don't concern you) use it to your advantage.
