### Post by Chemical Imbalance on 2009-04-24
If you want the latest and greatest (with the possibility of a few bugs), get Jaunty [9.04].

If you want stable and well-tested (with the possibility of some older drivers or programs that might not be the latest), get Hardy [8.04].

If you end up choosing Hardy but it doesn't work properly, then give Jaunty a try because it may work better with your hardware.

For now, I suggest you just get the 32 bit.
