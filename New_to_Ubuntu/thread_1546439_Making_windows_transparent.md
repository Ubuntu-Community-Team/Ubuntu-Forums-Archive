### Post by Paddy Landau on 2010-08-05
I don't know how to make those specific spots transparent, but I believe it has to do with the Compiz settings.

[LIST]
[*]Install [compizconfig-settings-manager]("apt:compizconfig-settings-manager") (you can do this from System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager).
[*]Have a look at all the settings (System > Preferences > CompizConfig Settings Manager).
[*]You can find extra [plugins]("http://wiki.compiz.org/") on the [Compiz website]("http://www.compiz.org/") that may do what you want.
[/LIST]
