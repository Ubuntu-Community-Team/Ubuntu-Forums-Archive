### Post by aeiah on 2008-04-22
the distortion will most likely be because you're using a flat panel monitor in the wrong resolution. all monitors have a resolution that they're designed for and look bad when they use a different one. you cant really tell with CRT monitors, but with TFT you can. im assuming the only problem is that the resolution is defaulting to the wrong one, which is normal on the livecd. it keeps it pretty low to be no the safe side. you'll be able to change it to the proper one when you install (or you could try doing so on the livecd and see if it lets you. i believe it'll let you if you have intel onboard graphics)
