### Post by adult swim on 2009-01-14
right click the panel
select add to panel
scroll down to notification area and click add
that should put the area back on your panel.  if stuff isnt showing up you may need to restart the panel by hitting alt+f2 and entering in ```
killall gnome-panel
```
