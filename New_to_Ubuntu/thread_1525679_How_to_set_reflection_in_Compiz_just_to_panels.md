### Post by warfacegod on 2010-07-07
Try changing the Match from any to gnome-panel or Panel. You may need to do it as type=panel or something like that.

### Post by metallicamike on 2010-07-07
Thanks for the advice! class=Gnome-panel worked like a charm, as well as adding reflection to the dropdown menus.
