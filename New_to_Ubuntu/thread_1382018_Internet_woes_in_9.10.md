### Post by MarkC502 on 2010-01-16
I am assuming this is a DSL modem. I would say you probably have an issue with the pppoe config which is a login of sorts that DSL modems require. If you find that Ubuntu on your PC does not reliably work with your DSL modem I would suggest investing 50$ in a linksys 54GL or similar model router which you can then do a pppoe bridge to your modem and it will be rock steady and allow you to plug in up to 4 PC's to the 50$ router or use wifi to connect. Having a simple router is also the biggest single addition you can make to your computers security as they come pretty secure with standard settings for home users.
