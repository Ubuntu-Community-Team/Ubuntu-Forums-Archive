### Post by eriktheblu on 2012-02-11
The X button closes the window, but it continues to run. It should leave an icon in your notification area, where you can right click and close (does for me in 10.04 anyway). Banshee is definitely not the only program that does this.

So, yes Banshee actually works, and works well. If you do not like it's behavior, you may certainly replace it with another media player (several available in the software center).
