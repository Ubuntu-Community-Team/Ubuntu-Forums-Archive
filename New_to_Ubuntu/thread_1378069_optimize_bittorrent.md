### Post by talsemgeest on 2010-01-11
> **manny43 said:**
> Hello friends

Is there a way to make bittorrent download faster?Download speed 15.6 kb/s to 18.3 kb/s.
I'd like to increase download speed.My connection is Broadband.Thanks
Due to the nature of bittorrent, your speeds can vary greatly, depending on what torrent you download, and how many people are downloading. However, one thing that can greatly speed up torrent downloads is port forwarding. Take a look in the preferences of your torrent client, and find which port it is using. Then go through [this](http://portforward.com/) site to find instructions for your specific router.
