### Post by nothingspecial on 2010-07-22
```
sudo apt-get install gtkpod-aac
```

Launch it from the multimedia section of your menu

Wait for it to detect your ipod

Click your ipod in the left hand box

Click "add files" up top

Browse to your mp4 file
