### Post by peculiar penguin on 2010-10-30
How thorough was your report? I guess it's more likely to be looked at if you post a detailed description and the exact steps required to cause the problem (if possible). Other people confirming it (either because they've run into it themselves or because they've been able to reproduce it with your instructions) would probably help too.
