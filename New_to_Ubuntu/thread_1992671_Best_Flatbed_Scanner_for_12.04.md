### Post by strask on 2012-05-31
I recently bought a Canon LiDE 110, it's dirt cheap, performs well, and worked out of the box with Ubuntu 12.04.

Unfortunately, I'm not sure it meets all of your requirements as I don't know if it can do negative scanning or not, and it's half the resolution you request.

If you go to the SANE web site at [http://www.sane-project.org/](http://www.sane-project.org/) they have a list of supported devices, so check that before purchasing.
