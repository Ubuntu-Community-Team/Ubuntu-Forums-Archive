### Post by lukjad on 2009-01-20
When you double click the archived file, click *Extract*. Then you will be given a choice to *Extract All* or *Extract Selected*. You most likely would want to choose *Extract All*. Then it will prompt you to choose a file to extract the file(s) to. Just like when you are downloading a file, you can then choose which file you wish to extract it to. Click the extract (or save, I forget just now) button on the bottom right.
