### Post by Miljet on 2012-01-15
I don't know what version you are using. On 10.04, you can click on System -> Administration -> Printing. click on your printer, then select Printer -> Properties and you can set the paper size under Printer Options. 

If you are using 11.10, I'm sure there is an equalivant method, but I am not sure how to access it. Probably click the top of the Unity bar and type in Printers.
