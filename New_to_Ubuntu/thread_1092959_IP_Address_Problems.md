### Post by thtrgremlin on 2009-03-11
1. Right click your network manager applet and "edit connections".
2. Select the device to configure and click "Add".
3. Go to the "IPV4" tab.
4. Select "Method" "Manual".
5. Click add and enter your network information. Your info may be different than the example.
