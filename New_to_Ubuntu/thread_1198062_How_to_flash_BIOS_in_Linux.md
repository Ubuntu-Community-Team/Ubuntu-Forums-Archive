### Post by rustutzman on 2009-06-27
Follow the instructions from ASUS. Put the ROM on a small USB stick or small card. Every card of mine bigger than one gig wouldn't boot.

Put the card or stick in and hold alt and tap F2 on boot then select the drive and go.
