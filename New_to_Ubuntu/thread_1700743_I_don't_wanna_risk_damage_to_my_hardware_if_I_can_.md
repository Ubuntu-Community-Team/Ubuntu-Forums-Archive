### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-03-05
Unless you have serious hard drive problems, running from USB stick is not going to prevent the freezing issue you are experiencing.

As to hardware damage, if your PC is freezing because it is overheating (one of the more common complaints we see with 10.10), once again, running from USB is not going to change that.

I think the more important issue is to resolve the freezing problem -- because if it IS overheating, that is most definitely damaging your hardware.
