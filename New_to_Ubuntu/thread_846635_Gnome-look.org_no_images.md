### Post by sayakb on 2008-07-01
Try clearing the cache. Goto Edit->Preferences->Advanced and click Network tab and click Clear now button
Also, goto Edit->Pref->Content and click on **Exceptions** button adjacent to *Load images automatically* and check whether you have the sites name in it or not.
