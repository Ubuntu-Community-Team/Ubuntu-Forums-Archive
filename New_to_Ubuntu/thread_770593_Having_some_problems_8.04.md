### Post by dokdoom on 2008-04-27
When you say your wireless doesn't work, do you mean you see where it says to connect and it fails? Or you can't even find your network manager? 

For your display information are you using the restricted driver for your card? Also have a look at your /etc/X11/xorg.conf to make sure it is using the correct resolution and refresh rate.

The fact that it's taking long to boot up might meen it's just trying to grab a DHCP address and failing. During the time where you are syaing it just hangs, try hitting CTRL + C to cancel.
