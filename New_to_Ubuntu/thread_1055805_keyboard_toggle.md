### Post by BlueGene1402 on 2010-02-28
[FONT=Verdana][SIZE=2]This worked for my Xubuntu 9.10
I can now quickly & comfortably switch between English & Arabic in all applications through my chosen keyboard shortcut. Hope it helps :)

Right click on the top panel.
Choose "Add new items..."
Choose "Keyboard Layouts" (Keyboard layouts setup & switch plugin) & Add it.
When it appears right click & go to Properties. It's very easy from here on.
You can add Swedish from the "Keyboard Layouts" button then choose your preferred keyboard shortcut from "Change layout option".

Good luck!
