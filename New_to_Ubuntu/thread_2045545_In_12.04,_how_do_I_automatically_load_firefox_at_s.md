### Post by deadflowr on 2012-08-21
If you're looking to have firefox launch at start, go to the top right edge of the top panel and click the cog or whatever it is, and select startup applications, then add. Give it a name, and the command will be firefox(simple). Comment if you'd like, click ok and the next time you login firefox will launch when you session starts. 

However this method is user-specific, and not system wide.
