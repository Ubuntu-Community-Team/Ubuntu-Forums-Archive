### Post by mister_pink on 2008-04-22
Have a look at the rockbox project, [www.rockbox.org](www.rockbox.org). They've made opensource firmware for a variety of mp3 players, giving them a much broader and better range of features. I would recommend one of the sandisk sansa players, you have to make sure you get a version 1 one though. They take SD cards so you can expand it as much as you like. 
[http://www.rockbox.org/twiki/bin/view/Main/BuyersGuide](http://www.rockbox.org/twiki/bin/view/Main/BuyersGuide)
