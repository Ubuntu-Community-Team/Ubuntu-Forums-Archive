### Post by GlazedWicker on 2010-10-23
If you still want a screensaver to come up when you are away from your computer (it decreases the lifespan of your monitor to leave it on all the time) you could use the sleep inhibitor applet (right click on a panel>add to panel) which allows you to disable the screensaver while watching videos by clicking on the icon.
