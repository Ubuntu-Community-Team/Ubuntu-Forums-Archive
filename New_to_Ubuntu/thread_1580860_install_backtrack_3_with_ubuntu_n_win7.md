### Post by arasbm on 2010-09-24
Hi Ashu,
I have not tried backtrack, but in the past I have had 5 or 6 different distributions side by side. I dont think there is any practical limitation on the number of OS that grub can point to.

As far as ideas about backup, you can either zip your important data into a file or even take an image of your whole partition and save it in compressed file somewhere. If you save an image you can restore everything (including mbr and grub) at a later time using any live distro. There are of course many other ways to back up your data and its allways good to have multiple methods. Dont put all your eggs in one basket ;). 

I hope this helps a bit,
