### Post by Skaggz on 2011-04-15
Does the VPN you are trying to connect to support pptp?  If so, it's not difficult to set up.

**Gateway:** check with your VPN provider, this will probably be a hostname in a format such as **host.domain.com**

**User Name:** This will be the user name you set up or received with your VPN provider

**Password:** This will be the password you set up or were given by your VPN provider

**NT Domain:** I just leave this blank.  Unless otherwise instructed by your VPN provider

The IPv4 settings I just leave in their default settings.

Hope this helps.
