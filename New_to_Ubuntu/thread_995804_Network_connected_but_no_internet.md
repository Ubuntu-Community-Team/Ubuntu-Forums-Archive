### Post by doas777 on 2008-12-02
Here is a link to a list of publicly accessible DNS servers: [http://theos.in/windows-xp/free-fast-public-dns-server-list/](http://theos.in/windows-xp/free-fast-public-dns-server-list/)

just configure your network connection in the System -> Preferences -> Network Configuration.
select your BB card's connection ( I guess it would be on the "Mobile Broadband" Tab), and click Edit on the right. select the tab that says IPv4 Settings. in the box that says DNS Servers, put in one or more from the link above, separated by a comma.

I have no idea how secure some of these servers are, but then again, how much do i trust my ISPs? hit OK. 
you may need to reboot before you notice a change, but it ought to pick up teh change without. give it a try.

anyway, have fun,
