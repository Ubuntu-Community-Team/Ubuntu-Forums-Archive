### Post by hodged02 on 2011-02-04
nice and easy in UNR 10.04, just go to "system" in unr mode and then scroll down to and open "login screen", click unlock and enter your login password, at the bottom, change the dropdown box for default session from unr mode to gnome, exit by clicking close then logout and back in, done!
