### Post by jhenager on 2009-04-14
I don't want to disappoint, so . . . 
don't be daft...
Pretty much anything you want to do with Ubuntu can be done. I'd recommend the themes already created. I imagine if you really had to, you could replace any buttons or controls with Mac equivalents if you have a die hard Mac fanatic on your hands (and I pity you if that's the case).
However, just like a lot of people ask if you can make Ubuntu look like Windows XP or Vista, the answer is - yes, but why would you want to unless you are locked into seeing a particular image and only being able to click on that image because it was created by someone who claims to 'think different'?
Just something to think about.
