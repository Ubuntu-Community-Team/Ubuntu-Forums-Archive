### Post by fooman on 2008-11-08
go to places > computer

click on your user name in the left column, then on the right side...right click on any folder and choose "properties" from the drop-down menu.  click the "open with" tab and put a check next to "open folder".

see if that fixes it.

---

### Post by drs305 on 2008-11-08
Another way is to right click any folder, select "Open with other application", select "File Browser" and hit "Open".
