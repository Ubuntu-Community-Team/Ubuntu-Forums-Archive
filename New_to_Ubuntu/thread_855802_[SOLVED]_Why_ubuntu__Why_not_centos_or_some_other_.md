### Post by Foster Grant on 2008-07-10
Because the Ubuntu developer team has put a lot of work into making sure drivers &c. work out of the box, so to speak, thus saving me and many other Ubuntu users the trouble of spending a lot of extra time trying to track down and install drivers for video cards, wireless cards and so on. I also like the Debian family more than the Red Hat family. Finally, the immense financial resources behind Ubuntu mean it will be around for a long, long time and will be kept current for many years.

I realize not everything works the first time for every user in Ubuntu. It has for me so far, with the biggest work being to load up the Restricted Drivers Manager in order to load video (ATI-fglrx) and wireless (Broadcom 4311) drivers.
