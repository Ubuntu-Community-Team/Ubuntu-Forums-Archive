### Post by Impavidus on 2014-05-28
That "splash screen" you're referring to is known as the grub menu. On your system it tries to display at a resolution or refresh rate not supported by your monitor. Try to edit /etc/default/grub (save a backup of the original) and update grub to fix it. I'm not a specialist in this matter, so you may want some further guidance from someone else.
