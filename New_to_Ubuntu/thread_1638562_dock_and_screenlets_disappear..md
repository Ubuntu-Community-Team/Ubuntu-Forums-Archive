### Post by Frogs Hair on 2010-12-05
The Screenlets manager has an option autostart at log in , and you can check preferences > startup applications to make sure Screenlets is added .  There are other threads on this topic so you may want to search the forums for possible solutions. Your dock should also have start automatically option in the settings .
