### Post by meteorrock on 2013-01-21
On my ubuntu 12.10 build I go into the system settings and look for the "sound" option. There are options in there to adjust your volume and other options for volume control.

It should list your sound card in there along with the input and output options you can click on to set up your keyboard for volume control if you want.
