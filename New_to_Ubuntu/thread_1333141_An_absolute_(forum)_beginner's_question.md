### Post by t0p on 2009-11-21
I post my support questions almost exclusively in the General Help forum.  If I was an absolute beginner I would use the Absolute Beginner Talk forum.

The more specialized forums have much less traffic than General Help and ABT.  If you post in one of the more specialized forums then wait long enough, someone might eventually come and answer it.  But, depending on the forum you've used, you might have to wait quite a while.  If you post in ABT, you may get an answer a lot sooner.

As for reposting: if you've posted in one of the specialized forums and haven't had an answer after a few days, I think it would be okay to post the question to ABT or General Help.  But make sure you *do* wait a few days before the repost.  And if the new post in ABT doesn't get answered, please don't repost that question again.  A bump is okay after a day or so.  ("Bumping" is posting a reply to your own question so the thread appears at the top of the forum listing again.)
