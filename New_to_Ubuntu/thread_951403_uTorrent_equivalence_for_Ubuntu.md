### Post by sethvath on 2008-10-18
1. Transmission - light weight and easy to use. not much features
2. Ktorrent - full featured BT client, relatively easy to setup and use
3. Deluge-torrent - Cross platform BT client, closest in looks and use to uTorrent. 

one caveat, I found transmission and ktorrent to be much more stable than the latest deluge torrent.
