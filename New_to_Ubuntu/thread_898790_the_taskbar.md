### Post by stonecoldjha on 2008-08-23
i installed kde 4.1 on my ubuntu hardy,but i want the taskbar to be placed at the top rather than at the bottom.how can i do that?

---

### Post by eightmillion on 2008-08-23
Make sure your panel is unlocked. Right click on it and and select unlock widgets. Now click on "the cashew" (that swirly thing at the edge of the taskbar). A pane should pop up that will all you to drag the panel wherever you want.

### Post by stonecoldjha on 2008-08-23
and how do i increase the number of desktops to 4?

---

### Post by eightmillion on 2008-08-23
System settings should be in your favorites area of your kickoff menu by default. Click on that. Then click Desktop and then Multiple Desktops and you can change it from there.
