### Post by Toz on 2011-05-05
How is your media mounted in gnome? Perhaps its not mounted in XFCE. 

If you select the "File Manager" icon in XFCE Settings, you can configure volume management. On the Advanced tab, make sure "Enable Volume Management" is selected then click on "configure". Ensure that the options "Mount removable drives when hot-plugged" and "Mount removable media when inserted" are selected. Now unplug and replug in the removable drive and try again. Does the drive mount? Do the tunes play?
