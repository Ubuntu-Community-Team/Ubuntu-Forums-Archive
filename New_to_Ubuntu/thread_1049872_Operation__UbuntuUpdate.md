### Post by emarkd on 2009-01-25
Keeping a backup and being prepared for a full install is always a good idea, but don't be afraid to give the upgrade a shot.  I run Ubuntu on a desktop and a laptop.  The upgrades on the desktop tend to be a little rocky and I've had to reinstall twice, I think, but my laptop takes upgrades like a champ.  Until my harddrive gave out about a month ago, I had not reinstalled Ubuntu on it since I put Edgy on it several years ago.  Four upgrade cycles and no real problems to speak of.  and I'm not doing anything special - it just works out.
