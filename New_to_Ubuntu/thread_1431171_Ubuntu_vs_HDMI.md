### Post by aeiah on 2010-03-16
perhaps you should check to see what version of the intel graphics drivers are present in your kernel. according to [this](http://intellinuxgraphics.org/), version 2.6.0 of the intel graphics drivers supports hdmi audio, but this may not be in the kernel.

test it with the newest ubuntu you can, perhaps by downloading [lucid lynx 10.04 alpha 3](http://www.ubuntu.com/testing/lucid/alpha3) and testing it as a live cd, or by doing the same with 9.10 if 10.04 doesn't play nicely (it is alpha, after all)
