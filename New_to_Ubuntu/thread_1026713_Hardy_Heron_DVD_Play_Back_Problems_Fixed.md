### Post by zero244 on 2008-12-31
Ok I installed mplayer, vlc, w32 codecs, Ubuntu restricted extras.
And I was having play back issues with DVD movies.
They would either not play at all, or have choppy play. Much of the time they would crash the player etc.
Fix
Go to this link and follow the instructions and you should have smooth dvd play back in about 10 minutes time.

This how to is for Hardy Heron.

[http://www.ubuntu1501.com/2008/04/medibuntu-for-hardy-heron.html](http://www.ubuntu1501.com/2008/04/medibuntu-for-hardy-heron.html)

The file you are most like missing, which is the one I was missing is:
libdvdread3
Apparently the codec packages dont install this file and its not available in the Ubuntu Repos as far as I could tell.
Good luck.
