### Post by insineratehymn on 2008-08-14
Anything would work good if Linux is your choice. I would go older than newer (not only to help with your price range) but also to help guarantee compliance. Try a dell, solid hardware and easy to upgrade if you want. Go low on memory offered because it is so cheap, laptop HDs are still kind of expensive, but you may be able to find a cheap one online. Remember that when you pay for a new laptop you are paying for the labor to install all of the hardware as well, if you can do it you are saving about $90/hour.
