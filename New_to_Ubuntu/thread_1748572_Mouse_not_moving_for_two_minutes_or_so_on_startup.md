### Post by amiga5k on 2011-05-03
I "upgraded" from 10.10 to 11.04, and discovered that the desktop no longer has a top or bottom panel (so no access to anything...). Tried finding the answer online with no luck (several people had the same issue, but no answers that worked for me - Is 11.04 a beta release?).

Gave up and wiped/re-installed 10.10

Then, my mouse does not react for a full two minutes after the desktop is shown, then works fine. In the boot log, I noticed that the mouse was initialized waaaaaaaaaaay late in the sequence, so tried to figure out how to get it to intialize earlier, with no luck (I'm a linux-Ubuntu noob).

Anyway, I couldn't figure out why anything would be different from the last time (10.10 worked fine). The ONLY thing that was different was I had a USB external hard drive plugged in to the back of the computer, whereas before it was plugged in through my router (so that all the computers in my house could use it). I had moved it from the router (which has a USB port for this purpose) to the back of the computer because Windows 7 couldn't do backups to it for some reason (even though it shows up in "computer" and I can view files on it, etc).

So, long story short: I plugged it back into the router and everything works again (Although Windows 7 still can't "see" it).

Hope this helps someone else in a similar situation.
