### Post by quinnten83 on 2009-08-08
The problems with these books is that they can never keep up with current releases. 8.04 and 8.10 are not the current release, although 8.04 is a long term release.
It would be best if you got a book that explains the basic principles of linux, rather than a book that is distro specific.
I do think though that this might be a good book for learning Ubuntu, since the changes have not been that drastic (yet).
