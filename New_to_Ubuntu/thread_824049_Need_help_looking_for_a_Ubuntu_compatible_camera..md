### Post by lazyart on 2008-06-10
+1 on the card reader suggestion.  Many cameras will only transfer at USB1 speeds (because they want to sell you a docking station that will transfer at USB2).  When you get a $10 card reader you get to stick it to the man. :) Consider the camera dock as proprietary and a card reader as open.  Not a perfect analogy, but you know what I mean-- you can use the card reader with multiple devices.
