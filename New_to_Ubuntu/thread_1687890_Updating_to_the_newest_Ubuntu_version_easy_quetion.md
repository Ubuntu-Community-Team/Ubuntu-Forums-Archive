### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-02-14
Why do you want to upgrade through the terminal? Why not use Update manager? It will tell when there is an upgrade and give you the option to install it.

Do you have Home on a separate partition? If you do then even a re-install does not affect what is on the /home partition if you do not mark the partition for formatting. It is best to create a separate /home partition when you are ready to upgrade from CD.
