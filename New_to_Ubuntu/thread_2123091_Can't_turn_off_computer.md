### Post by skinnymg1 on 2013-03-07
Try hitting ctrl+atl+F1. Log in to the terminal using your normal username and password. then enter without the quotations, "sudo shutdown -h now". It will ask for your password, enter it and hit enter. It should then shut down.

### Post by lwalper on 2013-03-07
Or, try pressing and holding the power button for at least 5 seconds. The BIOS should catch that and power down. Maybe then, the next time you power up, everything will be correct and everything will work as intended.
