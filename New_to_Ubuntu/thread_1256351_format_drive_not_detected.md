### Post by egalvan on 2009-09-02
"It's not recognized" is far too broad.

This can range from
the drive is having mechanical problems..
the cable is bad..
the BIOS is not set up for extra drives..

How is it connected?

Being a laptop drive, the jumpers are probably not the issue.

is it not spinning up? (can you hear or feel it spinning?)

is it not seen by BIOS?

is it not seen by gparted (Partition Editor)


Many questions remain un-answered.
