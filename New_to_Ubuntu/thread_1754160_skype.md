### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-05-09
With audio on Ubuntu, you have to think the opposite way as on Windows.

On Windows, you use Skype to set what microphone and headset you want to use.

On Ubuntu, you use Ubuntu to set what microphone and headset you want to use.

Click on the sound indicator on the top panel (it's the one that gives you your volume control). Go to Sound Preferences.

In the Input and Output tabs, change the Connector popup menu to however your headset/microphone are connected. If your headset and/or microphone use USB, you will also need to use the Hardware tab to select your USB sound devices.
