### Post by speartip on 2013-01-30
Hi.
I had the same problem so I added the GB thesaurus as an extension from here:
[http://www.weeklywhinge.com/?p=69](http://www.weeklywhinge.com/?p=69)
Just click the "here it is" button, download it to your desktop,
then in Libreoffice, go to tools/extension manager/add, & navigate to the file, & install.
Close & open Libreoffice, & you should then have a working thesaurus.
