### Post by bwhite82 on 2010-11-27
You can ask on whichever server you use to get a hostname "cloak". On freenode, look here:

[http://freenode.net/faq.shtml#cloaks](http://freenode.net/faq.shtml#cloaks)

I have one and when I join/leave a channel it says "unnaffiliated/soldierboy" and no IP.
