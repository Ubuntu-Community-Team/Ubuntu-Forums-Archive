### Post by lisati on 2013-04-26
One thing to be aware of is that although ".EXE" files are designed for Windows (and sometimes MS-DOS), and thus won't work on Ubuntu without help, some are what's known as a [self extracting archive]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Self-extracting_archive"). Such files can sometimes be used on Ubuntu with normal archive management.
