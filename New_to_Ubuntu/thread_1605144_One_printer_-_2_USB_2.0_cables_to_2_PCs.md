### Post by cariboo on 2010-10-25
If you have a router, it is pretty simple to share printers, you don't need to install anything extra. On you desktop system, use a browser to access the cups management page. In the url bar type:

[http://localhost:631]("http://localhost:631")

Click the Administration tab and enable ** Share printers connected to this system** and click the change settings button, you will be asked to enter your user name and the password you normally use to login. See the screen shot.

Once you done the above, go to your laptop and setup your printer as a networked printer.
