### Post by cariboo on 2009-04-29
You have to enable the Nvidia restricted drivers before you can use nvidia-settings. Go to System-->Administration-->Hardware Drivers, this will download the the restricted drivers and install them. It may take a while as the repositories are fairly busy at the moment with everybody installing and updating Jaunty.
