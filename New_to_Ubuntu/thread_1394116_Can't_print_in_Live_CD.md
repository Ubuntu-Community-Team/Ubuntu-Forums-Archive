### Post by admiralspark on 2010-01-31
He's indicated that it's a _typing_ problem, *not* a printer problem. 

Please, could you give us more specifics on the keyboard? I'll assume that it's wireless through a USB port on the computer, which indicates that it needs a driver for the computer to recognize the device. The fact that you're only able to type one or two letters seems to show that there's a software problem. 

Until we receive more information about the keyboard, the only thing I can point you towards is going to System --> Preferences --> Keyboard, then look for Microsoft Inc. in the list, then click on your keyboard model (or the one closest to it).
