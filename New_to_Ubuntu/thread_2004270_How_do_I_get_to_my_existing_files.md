### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2012-06-15
If you open the file browser, there should be an entry on the side,  "file system" usually. One of those should be your windows hard disk, it'll probably have the windows-ey directories, C:/Windows and such. Browse to your files, copy to another disk of choice, voila.
