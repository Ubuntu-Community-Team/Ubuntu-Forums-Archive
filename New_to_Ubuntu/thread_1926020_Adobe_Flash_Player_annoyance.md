### Post by winh8r on 2012-02-15
You can right click on the flash player screen when a video is playing and select global settings.
This will take you to an adobe page where you can set what has access to your computer and how much access.

I believe the s.ytimg.com has something to do with webcam detection so you can disable it.

This problem and a good many others can be dealt with by installing adblock plus and ghostery as Firefox add-ons , configure and enable them and you will notice a huge drop in annoying popups and advertising.

Ad  block does what the name suggests while ghostery blocks all known trackers from accessing your browser.
