### Post by Dennis N on 2013-06-19
Recommended for Beginners: [The Ubuntu Manual]("http://ubuntu-manual.org/") 

See p. 21 for information on the launcher. In particular, the numbers appear when you hold down the super (Windows) key. If you depress the super key with the number on the launcher icon, the program will launch. 

The launcher position is fixed on the left.
