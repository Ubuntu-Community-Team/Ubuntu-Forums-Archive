### Post by mgranet on 2009-08-20
You did very good for a first build. That being said, I'd like to offer some insight you may wish to consider.

The 9800 GTX+ has a close cousin. It's the new GTS 250. In fact, the 250 is just a straight up re-brand with an additional 512mb of RAM. You can find a GTS250 on Newegg for 4 dollars more than the card you selected. It's not a lot of money for quite a bit of ram, if your budget can spare a few dollars.

As for your RAM, your system supports up to DDR2 1300. IMHO, fast RAM == happy user, and if you can bow that budget just a few more dollars, you might consider picking up some 1066.

Good luck with your build!
