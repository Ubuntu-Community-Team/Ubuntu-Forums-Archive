[TV tuner]("http://www.google.com/shopping/product/16256910085558619502?q=usb%20to%20tv&hl=en&bav=on.2,or.r_gc.r_pw.r_qf.&bpcl=38897761&biw=1360&bih=681&sa=X&ei=h76yUOKjJoG08ASOkYGoDg&ved=0CHoQ8wIwAA")

### Post by newb85 on 2012-11-26
I suspect that TV tuner does not work for outputting a TV signal to a co-ax cable.  It is designed to go the other way.

Basically, the USB port on your TV is for hooking up a flash drive with media files you want to view on the TV.  For your scheme of using a USB cable to connect to your laptop to work, the TV would have to be designed for that.  I don't think any are--that's the point of HDMI.

There are USB to HDMI adaptors.  I don't know if they will work out-of-the-box with Ubuntu, though.
