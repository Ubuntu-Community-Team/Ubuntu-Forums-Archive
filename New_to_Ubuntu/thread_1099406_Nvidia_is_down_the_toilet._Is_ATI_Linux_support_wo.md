### Post by markbuntu on 2009-03-18
Welll, ati is dropping everything less than the HD3xxx from fglrx after the April 9.4 driver. But, they have given all the info they can legally to the open source driver writers and have provided some financial support as well. This is far more than nvidia is doing.

I expect that by the end of this year many ati cards will have some very good open source drivers. So, if you are in the past, nvidia is the best. At present it is a tossup of proprietary drivers. For the future I will give ati the edge. proprietary drivers will be concentrated on the bleeding edge and open source drivers, which anyone can fool around with and fix will cover the rest. Meanwhile the nvidia open source effort is struggling and the prop drivers are still missing 2D acceleration.
