### Post by pskey28 on 2010-01-06
Hi Jhobdell
 
I'm also new but do have some knowledge of installing and booting from USB's, i have used in the past Unetbootin ([http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/))  This is a little windows app that will either download the required files (ubuntu etc..) or you can point it to an .ISO file, this will then extract the file to the USB key and make it bootable.
 
You will then need to ensure your system boots from the usb drive and not the internal hard disk or cd drive.
 
hope this helps.
