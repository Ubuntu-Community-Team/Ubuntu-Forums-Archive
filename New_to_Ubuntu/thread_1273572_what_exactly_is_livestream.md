### Post by t0p on 2009-09-23
Youtube is a video hosting site.  There are things called "channels", but they are basically just collections of videos posted by a single user.

Livestream is a bit different.  The channels there are more like TV channels - a Livestream channel will stream its content "live", meaning that the content of a channel could be different every day just like a TV channel's programming changes daily.

That's an extremely basic explanation.  But I think it should give you a general idea of what the site does.
