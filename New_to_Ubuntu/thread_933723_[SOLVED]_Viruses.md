### Post by bumanie on 2008-09-29
Basically, NO. There are only around 30 known viruses that can affect linux, they were apparently made in labs. None are in the wild and they are protected at the kernel level in case someone did release one. Even the open source virus detector, clamav, only searches for windows viruses. Unless one is sharing with a windows machine, I don't know anyone using linux who uses antivirus scanners of any kind for their linux box.
