### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-11-04
Reloading, as you call it, is not an option with Linux distributions. We try to fix problems or we re-install. Which of the two problems do you want help with?

a) the crash report that gives no information as to what is wrong.
b) the lost of wireless functionality after you made changes.

It is my experience that the dialog that informs us that Ubuntu has experience an error will also have a label called Details. Click that and we will get some information about which component actually crashed. Does the error affect the ability to use the operating system? Often they do not. Do you wish to send that crash report and want information about the process?

Are the two problems connected?

I do not know how to help with the wireless problem because you do not explain the changes that you made and why you made those changes. Was wireless functional before you made those changes?
