### Post by SEECo on 2010-10-10
I think there is some problem with my ubuntu lucid lynx. When I start my computer it shows that the visual effects are on none. But when I Shut down my computer, it was on extra. What to do to make it on extra whenever I booted my computer.

---

### Post by juil on 2010-10-10
Just go to System --> Preference --> Appearance.
In the Visual Effects tab. Click on extra.
Extra visual effects, when enabled allow compiz to work on your pc.
