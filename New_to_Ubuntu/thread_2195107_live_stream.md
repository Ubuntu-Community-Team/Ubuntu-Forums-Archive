### Post by Impavidus on 2013-12-22
It may.

10.04 is unsupported. I assume your live stream depends on flash or on a feature build into a recent version of your web browser. On 10.04 you have old (and insecure, especially important for flash and web browsers) versions of this software. Installing a supported release of Ubuntu, like 12.04 or 13.10, may solve your problem and many others waiting to happen.
