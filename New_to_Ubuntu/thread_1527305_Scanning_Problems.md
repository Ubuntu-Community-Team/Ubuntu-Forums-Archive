### Post by diablo75 on 2010-07-12
Does the printer/scanner have it's own stand-alone Copy feature that is computer independent?  In other words, could you walk up to it with a piece of paper that you want to copy, press a button that says Copy, and it will scan and print a copy for you?

I'm only asking about this because once my girlfriends father had an All-In-One (Kodak, which is far worse quality that HP in my opinion) that started scanning and printing things in a very funky way.  The copies looked almost like washed out negatives with no color.  And printing just spat out a lot of blank pages with an oddball character here and there.  It had a mind of its own.

Only other simple thing I could suggest is to find a friend who has Windows on their system and take your printer to their computer.  Install it, test it, confirm whether or not it works.
