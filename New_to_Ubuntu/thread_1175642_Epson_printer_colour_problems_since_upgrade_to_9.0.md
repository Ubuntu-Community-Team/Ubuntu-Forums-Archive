### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-06-01
I have an Epson R200 inkjet printer and am printing find under Jaunty with no problems -- but I'm using the Gutenprint driver.  Ran into lots of problems printing with default driver. Installed Gutenprint, changed over to their driver, haven't had any problems since.
