### Post by mgmiller on 2009-06-18
You need to specify the correct horizontal sync and vertical refresh rates for you monitor in xorg.conf here:

```
Section "Monitor"
    # HorizSync source: builtin, VertRefresh source: builtin
    Identifier     "Monitor0"
    VendorName     "Unknown"
    ModelName      "CRT-0"
    HorizSync       28.0 - 55.0
    VertRefresh     43.0 - 72.0
    Option         "DPMS"
EndSection
```You did not say what model monitor you have, but if you google around a bit, you should be able to get this info.  Then just edit your xorg.conf and change the numbers for HorizSync and VertRefresh to match you monitor.  All the supported resolutions should then be available.
