### Post by netipot on 2011-11-22
Hi,  I got a message that someone replied to my old zonbu thread but it directed me to the old one so I looked you up & found you started a new thread. I do not have wireless on my mini so never bothered with it. You might get some help, as I did, from WDL systems who actually make the zonbu mini.
I have moved on to other distros that I think work better. I decided that something more  lightweight might be better suited to the mini. I tried siltaz &  macpup and though they work fine & are fast I was not happy with  them. Elementary I didn't think was light enough so I tried lubuntu. I  think it's very good.....better than the LXDE versions of mint &  opensuse, but wattOS is the best of the bunch and I like their forum. You may find that you will get more help there than here. I  think LXDE is great and it's light......the Zonbu runs pretty fast on it  but then I tried bodhi.... Wow! Enlightenment IS FAST! It's pretty too.....  maybe too much bling,.. but it is hands down fast. I love the simplicity  of LXDE but bodhi is what you need to get the most out of this little  machine. Bodhi & wattOS are both based on ubuntu. The bodhi forums are very helpful & attentive also.
