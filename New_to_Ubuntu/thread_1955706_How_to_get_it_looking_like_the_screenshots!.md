### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-04-10
I am on Ubuntu Studio Beta 2 right now. To change the desktop background/wallpaper image click on the blue icon in the top left corner of the screen and scroll down to Settings and then on the menu that pops out select Settings Manager. In the panel that opens select Desktop.

From there you can select the background image for your desktop to correspond to the image in the web site screenhots page.

In the same Settings Manager panel you can change the theme. It is under Appearance>Style. This will also let you change the icon style as well.

I have the same login background as in the screenshot. The other two images are of programs.
