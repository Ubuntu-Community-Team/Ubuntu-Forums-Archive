### Post by underquark on 2010-04-01
You download the image to your computer, either to the Desktop or or to a particular folder that you have created.  Open your e-mail program, start a New message and then just drag the image from the Desktop (or wherever) into the body of the e-mail; works in Evolution certainly and works in a few others.  As regards posting to a Newsgroup then that depends on their rules but usually you need to first upload your image to a web storage site, whether that is your own website or one of the free image/file hosting sites.
