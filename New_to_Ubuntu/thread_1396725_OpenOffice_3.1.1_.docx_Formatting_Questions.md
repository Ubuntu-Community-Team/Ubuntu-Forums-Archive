### Post by Temposs on 2010-02-02
I believe doc has been developed more fully than docx, so you may be better off saving in that format. Does your work come out better when you do that? 

Also, if possible, you should try to turn in your work as pdf. There's a nice pdf button in OpenOffice. pdf should generally be rendered correctly all the time, and you won't have the issues that occur with the Word formats.
