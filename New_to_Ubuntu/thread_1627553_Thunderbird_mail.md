### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2010-11-21
You can try resizing the pictures.  I always do that when sending pictures, because some of my friends are on dial up and it takes them forever to download large pictures.  Just right click the photo(s) that you want to send and choose resize.  You will have your choice of how you want to resize.  After you make your choices, just click resize and you will have smaller pictures to send.

### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-11-21
As you are using 10.04 you can do this direct from f-spot, if that is what you use to manage your pictures/photos.

Highlight the picture(s) you want to send and go to Photo ->Send by mail.  A dialog will open and in there you get the option to resize the pictures to a more appropriate size for mailing.  A new email message will then open and you just add the recipient's address, subject etc etc.

Maybe shotwell has that ability as well, but I have not used it yet to know.
