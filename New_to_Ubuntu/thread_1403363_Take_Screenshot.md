### Post by mooscape on 2010-02-10
To take a selection of the screen as a screenshot you must click (select) through the following:  

"Applications" --->  "Accessories" ---> "Take Screenshot"  then choose "Grab a Selected Area"   You will use a crossing window = select with mouse by left clicking top left of selected window and move down to bottom right of selection. Then just follow prompts to save.  

I have dragged the screenshot icon onto my user panel for easy access. Select "Take Screenshot" as above, left click and holding down mouse button move over the panel to where you want it.
