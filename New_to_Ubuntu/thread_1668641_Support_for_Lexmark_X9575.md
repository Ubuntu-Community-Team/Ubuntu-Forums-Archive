### Post by davidmohammed on 2011-01-16
I'm afraid you've got a paperweight - no drivers for linux - and therefore you cant even print to it via a shared virtual windows printer share i.e. you still need a driver to print to a printer on a samba share.

I also had a lexmark at one point and I remember spending a weekend trying to get it working using information in [this article]("http://ubuntuforums.org/archive/index.php/t-1099927.html").  I did get it working eventually - dont ask me how - but once I found out the price of lexmark printer cartridges when the black cartridge ran out, I dumped it and bought an epson instead!
