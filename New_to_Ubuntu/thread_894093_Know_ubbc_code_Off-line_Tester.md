### Post by eightmillion on 2008-08-18
This may or may not be over your head, but you can install a [web server]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/ApacheMySQLPHP?action=show&redirect=LAMP") on your machine and install something like [punBB]("http://punbb.informer.com/"). Then you'll be able to post to your own personal forum by typing something like [http://127.0.0.1/myforum](http://127.0.0.1/myforum) in your browser even when you're offline.
