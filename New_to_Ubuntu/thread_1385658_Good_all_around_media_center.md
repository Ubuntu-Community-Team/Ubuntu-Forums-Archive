### Post by scottuss on 2010-01-20
A week ago I would have raved about Boxee, but now some things are starting to annoy me. Many DVDs don't play back correctly, my media is often incorrectly recognised, and the music library is pretty terrible.

XBMC is the software that Boxee is based on, and isn't much better to be honest.

Mythbubuntu / MythTV is clunky and not very pretty, plus it's only any good really for TV recording.

To top all this off, lirc broke and now my remote doesn't work correctly. Just randomly. Nothing changed. Back to default config, removed and re-installed lirc. Nothing.

So what did I do, and what do I recommend for a media center? I bought a mini (very mini!) wireless keypad with touchpad and now navigate my standard Ubuntu desktop with this on the TV.

This way, I get the awesomeness of VLC for DVDs and other media playback (I'm sorry but Boxee is miles behind in this area!) and I get a good web browser for surfing and the "on demand" TV services.

Plus I can play my music through Rhythmbox.

Would I like a product that brings all of this together, properly and in a lovely, pretty interface? Yes.

Does that exist yet? I would argue not.

Your mileage, as they say, may vary.
