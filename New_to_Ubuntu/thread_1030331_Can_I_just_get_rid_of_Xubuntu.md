### Post by taurus on 2009-01-04
Yes.  The installer will ask you which partition to use and where to mount it.  Then, it would give you an option to either format the partition or not format it.  You want to format it to erase all the old stuff on it.  Just make sure you pick the right partition because if you pick the wrong one, all your data will be gone.
