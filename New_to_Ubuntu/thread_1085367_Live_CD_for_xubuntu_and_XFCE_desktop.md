Xubuntu is available as two different CDs, the Live CD (which requires about 256mb of ram) and the Alternate CD (which can install with less ram, but does not have Live CD functionality). I would not personally recommend Xubuntu (or any *Buntu for that matter) with less than 256mb of ram. Puppy or DSL would be excellent choices in your situation.
