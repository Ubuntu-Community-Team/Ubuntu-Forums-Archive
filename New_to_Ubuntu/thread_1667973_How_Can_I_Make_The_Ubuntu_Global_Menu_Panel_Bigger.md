### Post by user333 on 2011-01-21
I make gnome themes for a hobby, and the truth is that using ambiance or radiance will always look like that. The reason is that the panel is a pixmap, so the only ways to fix that is to use a different theme, right-click the panel and go to the background tab and change settings, or make another pixmap.

### Post by BandD on 2011-01-21
To get to the "OK" button hit alt while dragging the Window up.  That will "unconstrain" the Window the to top and bottom of the screen.  

The issue with not being able to right-click on the penel in the 10.10 netbook edition is due to Unity--a custom shell for the Gnome desktop environment developed by Canonical and the Ubuntu community.  The "panel" on the netbook version functions as much more than the panel and that type of customization has not been implemented as of yet.
