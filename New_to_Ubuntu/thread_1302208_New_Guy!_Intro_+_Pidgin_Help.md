### Post by zeroseven0183 on 2009-10-26
Pidgin might still be running even when you click the X button.

You can verify this using **System Monitor** (**System** > **Administration**). Open Pidgin then click the Close button. Check it under the Processes tab and see if Pidgin is visible.

Now, another option in Pidgin is to make the icon visible in the system tray. In the Pidgin window, click **Tools** then select **Preferences**. Under the **Interface** tab, select **Always** from the dropdown box of **Show system tray icon**.

This way, you can still see Pidgin open even after closing the Buddy List.

I hope that helps.

### Post by swoody on 2009-10-27
Hello Ryyy, and welcome to the forum ):P

As zeroseven said, you can enable a system tray icon for Pidgin. When you have the Pidgin window open, simply click on Tools>Preferences, and in the new window that pops up you should see a "Show system tray icon" setting under the 'Interface' tab. Simply change it to 'Always' and you can now minimize/restore your buddy list from the new icon in your System Tray.

Hope this gets you pointed in the right direction :)
