### Post by drs305 on 2008-09-23
For copy/paste *in a terminal window*, ubuntu uses CTRL-SHIFT-C and CTRL-SHIFT-V. You can also highlight to copy and then just middle click to paste. I am not sure if the situation you are having is in terminal or normal operations.

I am not sure about your other issue. You could open a terminal, start an app called "xev" and then hit the CTRL key to see if it is returning the CTRL code, if you are having problems using the CTRL key for *everything*.
