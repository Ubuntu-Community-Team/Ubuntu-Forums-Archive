### Post by groggyboy on 2008-05-12
do you have desktop effects turned on? it's possible you turned negative mode on by accident.

if so, try hitting <super>+n (the super key is also known as the windows key).  this toggles negative mode for individual windows.  if the whole desktop is white text on black (ie, the menubars are white on black as well), then hit <super>+m instead.
