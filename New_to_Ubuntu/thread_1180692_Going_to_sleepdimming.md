### Post by Johan-Steyn on 2009-06-07
Hi,

Go to System/Preferences/Power Management and un-tick the "**Dim display when idle**" options on the On AC Power and On Battery Power tabs. 

Also un-tick the "**Reduce backlight brightness**" option.

This will prevent the computer's display from dimming, but your battery charge wont last as long (assuming you're using a laptop). 

If you're on a desktop - no problem.

Regards,

JS

I am using Xubuntu so i went to Applications -> Settings -> Power Management -> and clicked the On AC Power tab and altered the settings for both:

Put computer to sleep when inactive: "Never"
Put display to sleep when inactive "Never"

Have also altered the screen saver settings to:
Reguard the computer as idle after "2 hours"
