### Post by Locke_99GS on 2008-12-05
Try [http://www.gnome-look.org](http://www.gnome-look.org) and [http://art.gnome.org](http://art.gnome.org) there are many other places to get them from as well.

At shell type: ```
gnome-appearance-properties
```
(or goto System -> Preferences -> Appearance)

Under the Theme tab, click install, and goto the tarball of the theme(s) you downloaded.

### Post by dannytatom on 2008-12-05
Download the theme (there's a download link on the theme's page).
Go to System -> Preferences -> Appearance
Click the **Theme** Tab if it's not already showing

You *should* be able to drag the tar.gz file from your desktop into the themes window and it'll install.  If not, click the "Install" button, browse to your desktop(or wherever you saved it), and open.
