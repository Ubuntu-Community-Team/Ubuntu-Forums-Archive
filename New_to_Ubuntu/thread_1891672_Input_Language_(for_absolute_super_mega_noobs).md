### Post by faul on 2011-12-06
settings > settings manager > keyboard > application shortcuts > add new >
setxkbmap en (then add kb shortcut) #for english
setxkbmap ar (then add kb shortcut) #for arabic

find the 2 letters that represent your language and use it instead of "ar" and don't forget to make the "en" shortcut so you don't get stuck with a language that terminal and most programs don't understand!
