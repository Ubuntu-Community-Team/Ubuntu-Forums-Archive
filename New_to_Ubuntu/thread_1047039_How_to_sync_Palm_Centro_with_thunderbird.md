### Post by rick71 on 2009-07-15
I realize I am late to the thread, however...

You might want to take a look at Scheduleworld (and other syncml-compatible servers). There are Thunderbird and Thunderbird/Lightening connectors.

Scheduleworld is now a paid service, around $25-35/year, and the syncml client costs around $25 (once).

You can use Scheduleworld to sync Calendar, Contacts and Tasks, and jpilot to sync Memos.
