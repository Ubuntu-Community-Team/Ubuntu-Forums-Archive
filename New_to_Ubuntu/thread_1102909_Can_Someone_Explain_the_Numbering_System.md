> **LiamWilson said:**
> It's easy, really, the 1st number is the year it was released (IE; 7=2007) and the second number is the month (.04=April) and combined, they make 7.04, which basically means it was released on April 2007.

So, the next one, 9.04, is the April 2009 release.
