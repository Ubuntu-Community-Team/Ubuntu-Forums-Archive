### Post by jingaling on 2012-10-06
Hi,

The first thing to do would be to prove is the problem is actually Ubuntu or just your internet. Run a speed test ([www.speedtest.net](www.speedtest.net)) within Windows and Ubuntu and see if there is a difference between the two.

I wouldn't rely on the speed of a torrent as their speeds are sporadic by their very nature. If you could come back to us once you've done a speed test in both OS's we can then help further.

Also check that you have the latest Wireless drivers installed. Search for "additional drivers" in the dash to do this. Can you also post your hardware please...ie what computer you have and specs.

Thanks,

Jing
