### Post by friarlawless on 2018-03-02
I've had pretty good luck with Dell's recent laptops. I haven't used their XPS gaming line, but I do have an Inspiron 7000 2-in-1 laptop/tablet that works beautifully for a dual boot Win10/Ubuntu 17.10. Decent onboard graphics, but probably not enough for serious modern games. Base i5 model starts at around $700, my model is closer to $1200 because I wanted the i7 and more RAM.

One BIG caveat, since you said you need it quick - Dell isn't great about realistically giving you a delivery date. They'll say "pay us $45 extra bucks and it will arrive this weekend" and then it will sit in their "processing order" queue for weeks. If you need one quick, consider an off-the-shelf model from a big box store with a good return policy.
