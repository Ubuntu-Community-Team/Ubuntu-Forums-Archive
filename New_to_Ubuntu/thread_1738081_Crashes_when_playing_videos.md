### Post by b0b138 on 2011-04-24
Sounds like it overheating.  I would suggest a good dusting of the inside with canned air, NOT a vacuum. Is it a desktop or laptop?

The fan might have stopped working, the fins might be clogged. The processor heat sink might need new thermal grease.  All things to check when its overheating.
