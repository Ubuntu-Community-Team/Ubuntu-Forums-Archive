### Post by coffeecat on 2015-10-12
And a third option with recent versions of Ubuntu.

Insert the SD card. Open a file browser window (if it doesn't open automatically when you insert the SD card) and you will see the card listed in the left pane. Right-click on that and you will see "format" as one of the options. Take great care that you format the correct device!
