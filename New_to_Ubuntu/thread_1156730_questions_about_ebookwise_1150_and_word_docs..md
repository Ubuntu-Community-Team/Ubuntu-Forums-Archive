### Post by orwellus on 2009-05-23
I'm replying to myself. So to me, I say:

Yes. The ebookwise works extremely well with word documents (or .doc files). I am very impresed. Login and upload the file to the personal content section on the ebookwise website:

[http://www.ebookwise.com/](http://www.ebookwise.com/)

Turn on the reader, plug in the usb cord. Download the converted file into the reader using the online bookstore tab on the ebookwise reader. 

The tricky part, for those who use Ubuntu, is installing the ebookwise usb drive in ubuntu. After much frustration, I did figured out how to install the usb drive in jaunty. I, and everyone else, can read about how I did that in this thread:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1163768&highlight=ebookwise](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1163768&highlight=ebookwise)
