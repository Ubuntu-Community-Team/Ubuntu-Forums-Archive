### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2013-03-30
I just downloaded and installed HPLIP 3.13.3.  Where is the downloaded program located?  If you remember when you started the process, you were cautioned about location.  So, if your downloads are all sent to your Downloads directory, you must first enter in the terminal
```
cd Downloads
```

If your downloads are somewhere else cd to that location.  Then you enter the
```
sh hplip-3.13.3.run
```
I love HP printers.  Have fun.
