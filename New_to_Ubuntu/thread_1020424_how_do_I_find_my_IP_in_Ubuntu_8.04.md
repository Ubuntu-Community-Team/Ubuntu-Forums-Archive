### Post by Badly Overdrawn Boy on 2008-12-24
Whatsmyip.com will tell you what your external IP adress is, and yes, this will be the same for all computers on your local network.

As mick222 says, typing 'ifconfig' in Terminal will give you the details of your local IP addresses.
