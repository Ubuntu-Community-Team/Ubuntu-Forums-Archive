### Post by mcduck on 2011-03-22
It's not even supposed to work for anything else than Ubuntu (and Ubuntu-based distributions).

Unetbootin would be able to handle pretty much any Linux-distribution, and BSD variants as well, but ReactOS doesn't really fit into that either, so you'll really just have to search ReactOS documentation or forums for info about how to do such thing, or if it's even possible. (As far as I know it doesn't have any live environment, so you'd just have to do a normal install into your USB drive instead)
