### Post by dfreer on 2008-05-13
Hmmm... I know my HP dv6000 will take a long time to boot the OS (if it does at all) if my iPod is connected to the machine, or an external hard drive, CD/DVD in the tray, etc. Try removing anything like the above and boot your laptop then.

I think it has to do with the BIOS trying to determine if the media is bootable or not, crashing during the middle of it. If I remember correctly, I went into the BIOS and told it to only boot the local hard drive first and that fixed my problem. 

Dunno about the CD ROM problems though.
