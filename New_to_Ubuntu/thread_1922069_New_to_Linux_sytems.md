### Post by Rafterman414 on 2012-02-08
Best two ways to learn in my opinion are to try and stay away from Windows while you are learning linux, and second try to accomplish the tasks you would normally use a gui for in the terminal. It will give you a better understanding of how things work behind the scenes and get you familiar with the Linux commands. Also check this website [http://linuxmanpages.com/](http://linuxmanpages.com/) it will give you information about the different linux commands, their usage, and syntax. Good luck and have fun.
