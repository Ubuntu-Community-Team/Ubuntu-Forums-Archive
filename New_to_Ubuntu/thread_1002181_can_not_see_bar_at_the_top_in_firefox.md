### Post by Yuki_Nagato on 2008-12-06
Figure out how to get into "Advanced Desktop Settings" For Compiz.

There should be a sub-menu that deals with Window Decoration.  Press the reset button next to all of the values in that menu, and then back out to save the settings.

Make sure you do not have any work open, and then press and hold "Ctrl + Alt + Backspace" (not Delete) to restart X.
