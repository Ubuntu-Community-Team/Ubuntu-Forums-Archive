Sorry I didn't put that clearer in my first post - I looked back at it and while I knew what I was thinking it didn't come out very clear  - 
> Then between setting desktop effects to something other than none or low  If it's not set, you can't run desktop effects.  A lot of newbies (myself was included! ;) ) think they can just use the compiz settings manager and never even know about this.  So, for anyone else reading this - be sure you right-click on a blank space on your desktop, go to the visual effects tab, and set to something other than "none" if you want compiz to work.  I actually set mine to the highest setting there, then mess with the compiz settings manager.

Glad to have helped!

Dave ;)
