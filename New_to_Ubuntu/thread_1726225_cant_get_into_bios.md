### Post by youbuntu on 2011-04-11
> **mushroom08 said:**
> well when i put it back together i got a f1 to continue and a f2 for setup after pressing f10 but it froze and on restart cound not get it again??? anything other than taking the battery out i can try? or would it hurt if i started bios without the button battery in there?

1/ You usually move a jumper from one (normal) position to the other (reset) position (on Desktop PCs) whilst the mains is unplugged, to reset the BIOS - no need to leave the CR2032 battery out for hours. Consult your motherboard manual for jumper location and settings.

2/ No harm will come to the PC if you remove the BIOS battery, and then power it up.
