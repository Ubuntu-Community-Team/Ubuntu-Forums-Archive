### Post by Zarnaik on 2012-02-25
I know this thread is very old, but I thought I'd just post a simple way to do it should anyone be looking for it and not being able to find it.

Go to applications menu->settings->settings manager and select "session and startup" here. Under the tab "Application Autostart" you'll find it's very straightforward to add/remove startup applications.
