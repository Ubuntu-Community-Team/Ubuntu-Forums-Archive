### Post by sassinak on 2008-05-08
I have a T22 and I did a little digging and found a few places that were helpful when I got a wireless card about a month ago.
  For starters I'd check the [Ubuntu WiFiDocs]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported") as this should give you a good idea of what will give you the least hassle.

I finally settled on the [D-Link WNA-2330]("http://www.dlink.com/products/?pid=472") and all I had to do was install the Atheros Restricted drivers and I had no problems on my T22.  I initially installed under a 7.10 install, and haven't had a chance to test the card under 8.04, but I had no problems with it in 7.10 and it works like a charm.  Think the card cost me about $40
