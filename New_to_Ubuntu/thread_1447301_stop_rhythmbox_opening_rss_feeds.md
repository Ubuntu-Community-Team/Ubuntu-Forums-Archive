### Post by pranayama on 2010-04-05
I bet the answer to this will turn out to be really simple but...

Whenever I click on a website's feed icon in Firefox, Rhythmbox opens and tells me that there is an error, there are no downloadable podcasts.

How do I make Rhythmbox shut up and go away, and reinstate whatever default it is that Firefox has for reading feeds?

---

### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-04-05
Go to Firefox menu, "Edit >> Preferences >> Applications", select the "Video Podcast", "Podcast" and "Web Feed" and set them to "Preview in Firefox".
