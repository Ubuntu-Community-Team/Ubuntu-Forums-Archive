### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-08-04
In UNR, the panel is covered by the Windows Picker applet, so that, when you right click, there is no Add to Panel dialog.  The way around it is to temporarily remove the Windows Picker applet, add other applets, and then re-add the Windows Picker applet.
