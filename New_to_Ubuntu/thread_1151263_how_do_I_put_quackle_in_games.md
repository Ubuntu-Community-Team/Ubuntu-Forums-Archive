### Post by doctorbighands on 2009-05-06
You can add, delete, and move main menu entries using alacarte. Either go to System > Preferences > Main Menu, or hit ALT+F2 and type in alacarte. When you get there, navigate on the left to the menu you want to change, and proceed from there. (The "add" button is on the right)
