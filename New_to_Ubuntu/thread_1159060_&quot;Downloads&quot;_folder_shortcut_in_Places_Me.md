### Post by LesterPalooza on 2009-05-14
How can I have a shortcut of my Downloads folder in the Places menu? In Fedora there is one such shortcut. Documents, Music, Downloads.... Is this also possible to achieve in Ubuntu 9.04? Thanks. :)

---

### Post by Celauran on 2009-05-14
Open the file browser, display "Places" on the left pane, drag directory from the right to the "Places" pane. Enjoy.

---

### Post by mprince on 2009-05-14
These are also defined using the Nautilus 'Bookmark' feature. If you ever need to change or delete it, just click on *Bookmarks>Edit Bookmarks*.
