### Post by I&#9829;HTML5 on 2011-06-24
It sounds like your problem is that you have a blank screensaver set, and would prefer your monitor to turn off when idle, rather than your screensaver.

In that case, press Alt and F2 and enter ```
gnome-power-preferences
```In the window that pops up, set "Put display to sleep when inactive for:" to however long you want your computer to be idle for before turning off the screen.

Hope I could help!
