If that's all you lost then it won't take more than couple of minutes to add them back to your panel.

First, right-click the panel and select "Add to Panel" from the popup menu. Then find the "Notification Area"-applet and drag&drop it into your panel. This will bring back all notification icons, including the Network Manager.

Since the default setup only has a couple of program launcher icons in addition to those located inside the Notification Area-applet,  assume the other icons you have lost would be the launchers. They are just as easy to add back to your panel, just right-click any item in the Applications- or System-menu and you'll get option to add it to your panel. Alternatively you can just drag&drop items from the menu into the panel.
