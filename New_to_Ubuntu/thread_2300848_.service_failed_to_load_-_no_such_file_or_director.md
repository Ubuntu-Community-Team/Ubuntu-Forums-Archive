### Post by sandyd on 2015-10-28
Ubuntu 15.04 and upwards uses systemd instead of upstart.

You will want to take a look at the instructions here -> [https://github.com/Sonarr/Sonarr/wiki/Autostart-on-Linux#systemd](https://github.com/Sonarr/Sonarr/wiki/Autostart-on-Linux#systemd)

For the the section named "Create the Service File", use below
```

cat > sonarr.service << EOF
[Unit]
Description=Sonarr Daemon
After=syslog.target network.target

[Service]
User=yourusername
Group=yourgroup

Type=simple
ExecStart=/usr/bin/mono /opt/NzbDrone/NzbDrone.exe -nobrowser
TimeoutStopSec=20
KillMode=process
Restart=on-failure

[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target
EOF
```
Please replace yourusername/yourgroup with your username and group.
