### Post by kulisek on 2008-11-19
Go to System > Administration > Synaptic Package Manager

In the Serach box, type in xvidcap and search for it.

Once the search is over and it is in the list, right click on it and "Mark it for Complete Removal" and hit the "Apply" button. After that just follow the instructions.
