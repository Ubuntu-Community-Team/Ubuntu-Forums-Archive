### Post by raequin on 2009-12-10
Greetings.  I upgraded from 9.04 to 9.10 (Koala) recently.  One thing that failed to transfer was my keyboard command to summon Gnome Do.  That is really beside the point.  What prompted this thread is that the first time I used Do (from the Applications menu), it came up in a "dock" panel at the bottom of the desktop.  Here is a screenshot.

[IMG]http://raequin.uuuq.com/forLinkingTo/Screenshot.png[/IMG]

I removed the bottom panel a long time ago and I want my screen real estate back.  Please tell me what this panel is (why it suddenly appeared) and, most importantly, how to remove it.

Thanks.

* Edit: Wow, I found the answer, quite by accident, after searching for half an hour before posting this thread.  One just has to go to preferences in Do and change appearance from "Docky" to classic.  Phew.
