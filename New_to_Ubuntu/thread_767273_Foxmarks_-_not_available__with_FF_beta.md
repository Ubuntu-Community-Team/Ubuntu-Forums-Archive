### Post by Paqman on 2008-04-25
FF2 and FF3 will also quite happily coexist. I have both on both my Hardy boxes.

Btw, I don't think it works for Foxmarks, but you can force FF2 extensions to work on FF3 by installing the Nightly Tester Tools extension and using it to disable version checking.
