### Post by CatKiller on 2009-08-05
> **BarefootSoul83 said:**
> I do only have 2 desktops... how do you get 4? (like I actually need it...just curious!)

If you have the Workspace Switcher applet on your panel, right-click on it and select Preferences. Increase the number of columns to 4.

If you don't have the Workspace Switcher applet on your panel, right-click on the panel and select Add to Panel... to add the Workspace Switcher applet to the panel. Then do step 1 above.
