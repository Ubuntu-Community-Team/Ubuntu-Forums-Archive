### Post by Dynaflow on 2009-03-16
First question: Do you know if your wireless is working at all in the first place?  Right-click the NetworkManager Applet in the Notification Area in the upper-right of your desktop (assuming you're using the default, vanilla-flavored Ubuntu).  Make sure Enable Wireless is checked.  Then left-click the applet and see if any wireless networks show up as available.
