Far to late to really matter, but here goes anyway. If its a 710MB .iso it should fit on a CD, unless it really means 710MiB. I wish that the whole 1000/1024 had a more logical outcome than the stupid MiB/MB issue, but thats a different story. 

If it is 710MiB, it should fit onto a 700MB CD (OK, 700MiB, but then if you convert 700MiB into MB its more like 735MB.....confusing eh) with overburning. If the overburn fails, or simply doesnt work, then a 800MB CD will do the job just fine.
