### Post by Crandom on 2008-10-26
On the livecd, go to System > Administration > Partition Editor. Let it scan, then right click on the preinstalled xp partition and delete it. Press apply. **BACKUP ANY IMPORTANT FILES FIRST ONTO AN EXTERNAL DRIVE**.
