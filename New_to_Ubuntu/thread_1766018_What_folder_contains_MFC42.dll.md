### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-10-25
> **rhmitul said:**
> They  says if i download this file, it will may damage your system  files and you will get many problem,..is that true..?i am totally confused ..
That's because, on a real MS Windows PC, you would NOT need to download this DLL as it would be installed. Downloading DLLs to a REAL Windows PC presents the possibility of replacing a current, perfectly good file, with an older, and possibly corrupted file.

These DLLs are a common way that malware gets into Windows PCs. That's another reason for the warning.

It's generally a BAD idea to download DLLs.  You would better going to a REAL Windows PC and copying the DLL file from there.
