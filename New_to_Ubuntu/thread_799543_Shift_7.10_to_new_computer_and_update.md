### Post by angry_johnnie on 2008-05-19
[This]("http://www.remastersys.klikit-linux.com/") is good for transfering your entire system.

The **backup** option is the easiest way to go, as it copies /home and all your configuration is carried over.

Just keep in mind that it copies **everything** in your /home folder.  If you have music, pictures or videos, you should move those to your second hard drive.
