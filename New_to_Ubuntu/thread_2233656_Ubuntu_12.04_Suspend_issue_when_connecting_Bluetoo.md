### Post by bediinderjit on 2014-07-09
I have a Dell Vostro 2520 that came with Ubuntu 12.04, I am facing this issue.

When I connect Bluetooth Mouse (Logitech M557) the Suspend feature stops working.

Every time I try to suspend the laptop, it comes back to the login screen, so I  have to shutdown the laptop every time I have to pack.

I tried with fresh installation, as soon as I connect Bluetooth Mouse to the laptop Suspend feature stops working.

Now  I have formatted the laptop again and I am not using the buetooth mouse  as It is very inconvenient to shutdown the laptop every time.

Also every time after unsuccessful suspend when the laptop return to the  login page, the bluetooth gets disabled, so I have to switch off WiFi and  bluetooth by Fn+F2 and then use below command to reactivate Bluetooth.

*sudo /etc/init.d/bluetooth restart*
