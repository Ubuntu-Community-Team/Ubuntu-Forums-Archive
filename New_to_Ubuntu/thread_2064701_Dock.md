### Post by mips on 2012-09-30
Docky does what you need I think (your description is a bit confusing).

Docky will minimise to the dock and place a small dot below the application icon. More dots indicate more than one instance of the app open.
Docky has 3d effects but they are turned off by default.

Try it, it's really cool.
