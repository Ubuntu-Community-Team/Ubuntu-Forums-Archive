### Post by MoebusNet on 2009-12-06
I know this isn't much help, but I'm currently using an RTL-8187b external USB antenna/card combination from my RV. In fact, I'm posting this comment using the card in Ubuntu Hardy Heron 8.04.3. This card should be supported out-of-the-box on all Ubuntu current versions, so if you are having problems I would tend to suspect your wireless router.

Perhaps you could test whether or not the problem is the card or the router by booting from a Live-CD at a known good open wireless router.

I realize that this may be difficult for you if this is a desktop rather than a notebook, but you may be able to borrow a friends wireless router just to test.

Hope these suggestions help...
