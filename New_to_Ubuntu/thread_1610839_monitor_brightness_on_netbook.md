### Post by sky_ceiling on 2010-11-01
the panels that hold the menus et al are editable, and you can put a manual brightness adjustor on them. 
 
To do so, right-click one of the panels, choose Add Widget, and in the popup box, search for "brightness", and select the resulting widget
