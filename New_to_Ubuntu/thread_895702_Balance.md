### Post by perlluver on 2008-08-20
Open up a terminal, and type alsamixer.  You should be able to do it from there, otherwise, your card might not be recognized.

---

### Post by silkstone on 2008-08-20
You can also install alsamixergui from Synaptic, which should then appear in your Sound & Video menu instead of having to use the terminal.
