No troubles after today's updating here. By the way what version and desktop environment are you running ?

I have updated 14.04 (with both xfce and unity installed) and 16.04 (Unity only) today with no problems, though I didn't notice anything likely to cause boot problems included with today's updates; only simple application updates came through here today.
