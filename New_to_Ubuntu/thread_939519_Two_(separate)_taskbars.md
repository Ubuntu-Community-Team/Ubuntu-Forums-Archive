### Post by OutOfReach on 2008-10-06
It is possible to have two 'taskbars' with all your open windows. I do not think that you can have some windows on the top and some windows on the bottom, though.

To add another window list to your other panel, right click on an empty area and click 'Add to Panel', find Window list, select it and press Add.
