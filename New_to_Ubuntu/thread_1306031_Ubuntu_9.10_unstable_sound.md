the problem had been with VLC...

the player had interrupted sound.
the problem had been that, after installing ubuntu 9.10, VLC got -by default- an audio visualization enabled.
thusly, the player worked like quite interrupted.
because i used the video at the bottom of the screen, it took too much time to figure that the visualizations of audio were running down there.

Then, I've disabled the visualizations, and the VLC is running as smoothly as ever...

the problem is solved thus.
