Well, you could probably do something similar to what you want if you searched the forums with the keyword, remaster. For that matter, just restoring your backup would probably be easier.

However, I suggest it would be a good idea to change your mindset. It is only rarely necessary to "reformat and reinstall" over here in the open source world. It also means you would be less likely to break the system in the first place.
