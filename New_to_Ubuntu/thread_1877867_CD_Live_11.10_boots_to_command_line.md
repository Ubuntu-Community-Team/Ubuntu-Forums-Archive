### Post by Rodney9 on 2011-11-08
This is a known bug.

Work around here - [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=11398602&postcount=35](http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=11398602&postcount=35)

before I knew this I burnt my cd at the slowest speed I could and that worked for me, others have had differing results.

startx loads the less attractive LXDE desktop and not the beautiful [COLOR="RoyalBlue"]Lubuntu[/COLOR]

Rodney
