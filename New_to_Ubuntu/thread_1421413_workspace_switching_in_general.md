### Post by underquark on 2010-03-04
This works for me:

Right-click on bottom panel
Select "Add to Panel"
Scroll down to "Window Selector"
Select and click on "Add" button

This places an icon on my bottom panel.  When I click on the icon I am presented with a list of open  windows.  When I select a window I am taken to the desktop that it is currently residing in and the window opens.
