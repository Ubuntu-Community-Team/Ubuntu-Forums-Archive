### Post by Paqman on 2012-01-01
An Eee PC 900 doesn't necessarily need a lightweight distro. Regular Ubuntu runs just fine on my wife's one if you use the Unity-2D desktop.

There are Eee-specific distros available, but there's no specific advantage to using them any more, as all the Eee-specific tweaks have been included in normal distros like Ubuntu and Fedora for a while now. I would recommend installing Jupiter applet, but apart from that you don't need anything special on your Eee PC.
