### Post by maqtanim on 2011-03-05
Go to **Tools > Zoom**
Now if you click the image then it will be zoomed. And to unzoom just click the image by holding Ctrl button.

You can also find the Zoom button from the left Toolbox, it has a icon of a magnifying glass.
