### Post by Kopkins on 2013-02-06
If you want the new version you'll have to install it yourself. It is not available in the official repos yet. It has to trickle down until it gets there though. 

Check here > [http://www.codeblocks.org/downloads](http://www.codeblocks.org/downloads)

and from there you go here > [http://apt.jenslody.de/](http://apt.jenslody.de/)

But note the warning at the bottom of the page for Ubuntu users.

Good Luck!
