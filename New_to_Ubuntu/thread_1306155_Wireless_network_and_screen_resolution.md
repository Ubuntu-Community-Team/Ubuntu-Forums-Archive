### Post by Scunnered on 2009-10-30
When I connect to VirginMedia wireless broadband I have to provide to the network request for a password the one that I agreed with Virgin.  You did not say who was your ISP but I would imagine that it is the password that you gave to the ISP that is required.

If my wireless connection is dropped it is this password I have to provide.  Another thing to watch is that at times it will not immediately connect.  you should SHUT DOWN Ubunto, repeat shut down and re boot when normally the connection will be made.  Restarting the system does not work.  On an odd occassion I have had to SHUT DOWN twice before things took.

I would suggest that you give this a try and see how things go
