### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-06-09
Hi,
Sometimes themes we install don't have a preview. If you know the theme installed successfully, open your appearance preferences window, then click on the button that says customize. Next, look in the 'controls' tab for your theme you installed. This is the 'gtk' part of the theme. If there is an entry for it, select it and click apply? (sorry.. I'm not using the gnome desktop so my instructions might be off a bit)

Next, click on the 'window border' tab, look for the theme that you downloaded. If you find it, select it and click apply? This is the 'metacity' part of your theme.

Hope that helps.
