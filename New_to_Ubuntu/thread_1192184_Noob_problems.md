### Post by alan34 on 2009-06-20
Hi,

Right click on the network icon - top right corner - go edit connection and click the wireless tab - click your wireless connection,click Edit, enter your password - then click Available to all users box (bottom left) should solve your keyring problem.

Go here

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AspireOne](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/AspireOne)

for other information

For example wireless led install linux-backports-modules-jaunty.
But when you start synaptic package manager (system - administration) click on settings then repositories - update tab and tick all the boxes.

Good luck.
