### Post by halitech on 2010-03-02
the ATI catalyst driver for that card only works up to version 8.10 due to changes Xorg made and ATI dropping support for legacy cards.

See here: [http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/linux/Legacy/Pages/radeon_linux.aspx?type=2.4.1&product=2.4.1.3.7&lang=English](http://support.amd.com/us/gpudownload/linux/Legacy/Pages/radeon_linux.aspx?type=2.4.1&product=2.4.1.3.7&lang=English)
