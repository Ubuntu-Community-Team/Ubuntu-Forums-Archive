### Post by colega on 2012-04-15
Hi to all. I just wanted to have some more information about my battery charge and power consumption for Unity, so I wrote a simple Indicator script, it shows your charge and your power consumption in the menu bar, just like
15 W / 52 Wh
or in milliamperes if you want so. There is no new information in comparison with click on battery icon and selection first battery, just because the information is provided by the same upower daemon, so it's just for a quick view of a situation.
I hope it will be useful for someone. It doesn't have any setup script, just place it wherever you want and run it: if you really like it you can add it to startup applications :)
Here is the github link for the latest updated version:
[https://github.com/colega/Advanced-Battery-Indicator](https://github.com/colega/Advanced-Battery-Indicator)
