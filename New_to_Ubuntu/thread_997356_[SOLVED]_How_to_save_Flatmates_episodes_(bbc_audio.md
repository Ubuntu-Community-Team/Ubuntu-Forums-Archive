### Post by beno1990 on 2008-11-29
To download the MP3, on the page there's a link saying "Download mp3". Just right-click that and choose "Save link as".

There are Firefox plugins for downloading YouTube videos, such as this one: [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3590]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3590")
