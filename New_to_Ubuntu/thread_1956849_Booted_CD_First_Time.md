### Post by anewguy on 2012-04-11
I assume you were running off the LiveCD and that you didn't install it as a wubi install or for dual boot, etc..  In that case, your hard disk has not been modified.  In addition, normally the LiveCD won't let you just eject unless you actually shutdown ubuntu.  So, I don't understand "went to sleep".

If you did not install anything in anyway from the ubuntu livecd, just press the reset button on your PC, or lacking one, just physically power it off, wait about 30 seconds, then power it back on - it should boot back into Vista.
