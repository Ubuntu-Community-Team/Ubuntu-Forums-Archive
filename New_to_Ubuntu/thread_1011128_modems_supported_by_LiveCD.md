### Post by ieee488 on 2008-12-14
A topic near and dear to my heart.

You should try the LiveCD and just see if your modem is recognized.
Who knows you might luck out.

If no luck, time to pay a visit to [http://www.xmodem.org](http://www.xmodem.org), and then a visit to eBay. I have a modem which uses [http://xmodem.org/chipsets/ti/ti_700.html](http://xmodem.org/chipsets/ti/ti_700.html) chipset. Look for photos showing those chipsets. You'll have to spend time on eBay looking through the listings, but you should be able to find one.
