### Post by sudodus on 2015-10-29
It may be easier to make an OEM system and clone/install it to your friend's computer. In this case you should avoid proprietary drivers.

[Ubuntu_OEM_Installer_Overview](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Ubuntu_OEM_Installer_Overview)

You can clone it with [Clonezilla](clonezilla.org) or

make a tarball and install from the tarball with the [One Button Installer](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/OBI)
