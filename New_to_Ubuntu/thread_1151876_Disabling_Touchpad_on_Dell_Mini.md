I have just acquired a Dell Mini with Ubuntu.  My hands are fairly big and a little fat, so I've always had trouble with touchpads -- Dell laptops years ago, now MacBookPros, and now the Mini.  

I keep two-button mice everywhere, in all my briefcases and at the office.  Think I have six now.  

But I've discovered with the Dell Mini's very small keyboard and touchpad area that I'm getting random hits on the pad, and that causes all sorts of glitches.  The cursor moves around uncontrollably, positions itself randomly, and text gets moved without asking.  

Is there any way to disable the touchpad completely, other than maybe cutting the wires?  There are some times I'd need to use the pad -- on an airplane -- but not often, so I'd like to be able to turn it back on.  I looked at preferences and there does not seem to be any way to just turn it off, and it does not go off when the mouse is being used.
