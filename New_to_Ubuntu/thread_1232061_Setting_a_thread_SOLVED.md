### Post by mapes12 on 2009-08-05
I do it like this:

Search>Find all your threads

Click on the thread you want to mark SOLVED

Click Edit on your post.

Click Go Advanced

Then just edit the Title field like this [SOLVED!]

Save changes.

Job done :D
