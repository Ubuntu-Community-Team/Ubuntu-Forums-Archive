### Post by TeoBigusGeekus on 2008-04-26
Wireless in Ubuntu can be a real PITA...
A simple search in the forums will show you countless threads about wireless problems. A way to get connected is to install the windows drivers of your adapter with NDISWRAPPER. If network manager cannot detect your wireless network you could try WICD.
But remember, no results are guaranteed...
