### Post by mcduck on 2010-12-30
> **jermza said:**
> Just feeling a little confused.

How do you have your volumes set up?

Well, basically you'd want to keep the source volume level as high as possible, and then adjust the final output level to change the volume. This gives you best audio quality (higher volume in the music player application gives higher dynamic range.)

So my recommendation is keeping volume in all programs as 100%, and then adjusting the master to the listening level you like. Only touch the application volumes if you have several programs playing sound at the same time and wish to adjust the balance between them.

Of course if you use external amplifier or active speakers you'd want to keep even the master volume adjustment on your computer as high as possible and then adjust the sound level from the amp/speakers themselves, once again maintaining highest possible sound quality as long as possible...
