### Post by Bakon Jarser on 2008-05-25
As far as I know SATA has the advantage over IDE.  The only thing to note about SATA is that if you are installing win XP to a SATA drive you will need to either have your SATA drivers on a floppy disk or have them slipstreamed into your win XP disk because winXP can't read SATA by default.
