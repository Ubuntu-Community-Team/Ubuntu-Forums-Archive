### Post by C.S.Cameron on 2008-10-25
The EEEPC will see a Flash drive just like a another hard disk.
You can full install to the flash from a second Live USB drive.
It will be  bit slower than the EEE's internal SSD.
 
Another alternative is to put only your home partition on the new Flash drive.
The space on th internal 4G should last quite a while if it is just used for root.
This should be a faster setup than running off USB drive
