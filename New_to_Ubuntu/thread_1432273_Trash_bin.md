### Post by Revolutionary101 on 2010-03-17
To get the shortcut back on the Desktop press Alt+F2, run gconf-editor and go to Apps>nautilus>desktop. When you go there check the box that says "trash_icon_visible". That should make your trash icon appear on the desktop.
