### Post by LowSky on 2008-10-07
*should* meaning that under normal circumstances with no conflicts with packages or hardware you install will be fine.

The Betas update just like all other version of ubuntu, except they may also include changes to the kernel and updates to software. When these things occur things hae a tendency to break, especially if you use software that is dependent on certain requirement of other packages or the kernekl that update on different schedules. 

Be careful with Betas, I'm glad your only using it to learn or play with becuase I'm seeing too many people lately thinking Beta's are as safe as regular full releases.
