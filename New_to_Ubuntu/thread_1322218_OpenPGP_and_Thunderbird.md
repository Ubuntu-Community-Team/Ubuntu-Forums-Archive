### Post by wojox on 2009-11-12
Thunderbird does not know which to display as plain text (which is a good
idea concerning signed email but also to not display/execute HTML-embedded
spy-/malware) and which as HTML (like adverts you know of and want to read
as HTML, or genuine greeting cards from friends).

You could install this add on: [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/thunderbird/addon/1556](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/thunderbird/addon/1556)
