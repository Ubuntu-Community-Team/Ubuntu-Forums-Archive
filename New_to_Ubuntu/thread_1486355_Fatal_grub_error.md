### Post by drs305 on 2010-05-17
To change the boot option to boot the CD first, it's normally a function key but doesn't have to be. On mine, it's the DEL key. It varies by manufacturer. Whatever the key, you should press it as soon as you hear the first beep - or just continuously from power on.

Usually the BIOS entry key can be found with Google. Just enter the name of your machine and "BIOS". If you still can't find it, provide your machine and perhaps we will be able to help.

Hit it during the time when the manufacturer's splash screen shows up (Dell, E-machine, etc), and hit it several times. The most common keys are F2, F12, DEL, and ESC. Which key you have to hit and when varies from manufacturer to manufacturer, but it's always within the first 5 or 10 seconds of bootup.
