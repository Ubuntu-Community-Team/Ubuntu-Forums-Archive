### Post by Mark_in_Hollywood on 2011-05-11
1. Do you have a CD, DVD or .iso of the Ubuntu OS you want to try?

2. If you have the media or .iso, follow the instructions under step 5. below.,

3. If you cannot install Ubuntu on your laptop, can you download the .iso onto your Windows or Mac desktop? You must have some form of the OS.

4. If you can get the .iso onto your desktop, you can make the thumbdrive (1 gig or larger ONLY for laptop, 2 gig for desktop) run Ubuntu.

5. Please see:

Installation From USB Stick
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick)
