### Post by kahlil88 on 2008-07-21
Flash videos are evil. If you save them using [SaveTube]("http://www.savetube.com/") or [DownloadHelper]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/3006") and then open them with a movie player (I recommend VLC or MPlayer), do you get any sound?

/home/<username>/.mozilla/firefox/<profile_name>/Cache
