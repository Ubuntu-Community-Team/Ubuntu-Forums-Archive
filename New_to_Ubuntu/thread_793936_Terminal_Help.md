### Post by billgoldberg on 2008-05-14
> **mr-Kirch said:**
> How do I make my terminal transparent (i have compiz fusion if that helps)

Open up a terminal, go to "edit", then "current profiles", press the "effects" tab and pick "transparency".

Other transparency/compiz effects :
