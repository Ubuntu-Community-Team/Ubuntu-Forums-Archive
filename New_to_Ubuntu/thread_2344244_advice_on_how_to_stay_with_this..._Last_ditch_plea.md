### Post by Dennis N on 2016-11-23
You got it installed - that's a big first step. I would not start installing software that's not in our repositories, like Open Office, for example. Get familiar with how things work first, and try out the provided applications (except Abiword - I favor using LibreOffice Writer. Just my opinion). Music player - I think Lubuntu has Audacious, which is an excellent choice. Also, Open Office and Libre Office may possibly conflict with each other? 

Having used both, I would have used Xubuntu rather than Lubuntu, but you have already made your choice on that.
