### Post by el_koraco on 2011-02-08
fn + one of the function keys, depending on your laptop model. or go to system-preferences-mouse, touchpad and ckeck the box disable touchpad while typing and uncheck the box with enable mouse clicks with touchpad. 

if you wanna go all the way, here's how, but it's much easier to do it with the fn + function key.
