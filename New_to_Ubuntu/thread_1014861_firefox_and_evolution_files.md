### Post by linux6994 on 2008-12-18
Go to your /home/user directory and show hidden files (crtl+H). The .evolution and .mozilla will contain your data. copy these out and them back in to your other PC.

I use sbackup that backs up everything in /home to an external drive (USB) everything is there. When I play too much or if I want to switch PCs then I can just restore and reload my favorite apps.
