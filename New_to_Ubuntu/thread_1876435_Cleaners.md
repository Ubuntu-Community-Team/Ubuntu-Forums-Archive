### Post by Matt__ on 2011-11-06
my advice would be to avoid computer janitor....I never had any good experience or reports about it, perhaps the reason it was removed? 

Use the clean-up abilities of [ubuntu tweak]("http://ubuntu-tweak.com/") or the terminal;
```
sudo apt-get clean && sudo apt-get autoremove
```I've also seen a lot of people who use bleachbit and report its good (never tried it myself)
