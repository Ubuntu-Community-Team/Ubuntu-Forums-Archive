### Post by avtolle on 2008-07-10
When installing under Wubi, then after the OS is installed, the system needs to be restarted. Then, when the Windows bootloader appears, you should have two options; the first will be Windows (whichever version you have), the second Ubuntu. Unless you choose Ubuntu within the countdown time alloted, you will be booted into Windows.
