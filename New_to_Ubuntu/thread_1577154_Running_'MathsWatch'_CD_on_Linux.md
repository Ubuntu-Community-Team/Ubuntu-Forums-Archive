### Post by Malcolm Waterman on 2010-11-27
If the MathsWatch CD is like the one I've got from my school, then go into Media/Common/Video. In that there should be a series of AVI files, you may need to add AVI support to your computer and you might have problems in totem. Just now those files wouldn't play properly, but they would play in VLC. Try opening in totem, if AVI is not currently supported then you'll be asked to add plugins
