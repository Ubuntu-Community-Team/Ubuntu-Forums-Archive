### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-01-25
You don't actually save videos or photos in digikam, they are still wherever you put them on your hard disk, in whichever folder you chose, so you need not worry about that, but I would suggest you keep the pics and videos on your hard disk anyway, not delete them just because they are on picasa.  It will be much easier to manage them on a local drive.  I can't help with any more info about picasa as I don't use it.
