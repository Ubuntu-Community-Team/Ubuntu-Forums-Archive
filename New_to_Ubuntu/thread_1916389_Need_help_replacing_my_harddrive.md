### Post by Kixtosh on 2012-01-28
The other advantage of Puppy compared to a LiveCD is that it is relatively fast, especially if the hardware isn't ancient, whereas running a full O.S. from a LiveCD can be painfully slow at times.

The main advantage of the LiveCD is that everything is more "familiar". Puppy has it's operational quirks that make it different from other distributions such as Ubuntu variants or Mint ... well, at least for me it does!
