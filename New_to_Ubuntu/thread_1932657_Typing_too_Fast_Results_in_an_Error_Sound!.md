I cannot seem to type slowly enough for this computer. I have never experienced this before. When I type too fast, such as in the case of typing "double e's" or "t's" or hitting "Enter" or the "Backspace" to correct mistakes I get an error sound.

With other applications I have used the sentence would just take a few moments to pop up but with this, it makes a sound and the letter or space seems to be omitted and it won't "take" until I s-l-o-w d-o-w-n!

I have been using Ubuntu 10.04.3 Desktop-i386.150 Lucid Lynx for a few days now. Have no desire to go back to Windows which I used (and offer support for to my customers) for over ten years and am pretty proficient at.

:KS

### Post by pulpfictionfan on 2012-02-28
I fixed it by going into System > Preferences > Keyboard... 

Under "Assessibility" I unchecked "ignore fast duplicate keypresses"!

Hurray!!!
