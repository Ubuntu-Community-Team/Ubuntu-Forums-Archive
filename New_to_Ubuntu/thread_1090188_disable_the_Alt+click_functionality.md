### Post by TheUnabeefer on 2009-03-09
In KDE4, the most I've found you can do is change it to the Meta(Win) key.

In System Settings, under Window Behavior, the Window Actions tab, it has a dropmenu for you to change the "Modifier key"

I had to change this so I could use certain tools in Photoshop in Wine.

Hope that helps.

[edit] Oh! You can also just select "Nothing" on the right set of dropmenus under "Right click" which will keep Alt as the modifier key for left and middle clicking.  [/edit]
