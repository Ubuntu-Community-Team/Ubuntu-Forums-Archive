### Post by AgentZ86 on 2010-03-24
One way is to ctrl + alt and use the arrow keys left and right to move between desktops which also brings up a preview window

Also compiz will allow you to have a sort of 3d cube which allows you preview all desktops on a 3d cube with some levels of transparency so you can see through it to the others sides of the cube as well

Do a google search for compiz cube you'll see some nice vid footage samples.

Anyhow, also you can hold the mouse of the desktop panels and scroll with the wheel but there is no preview just scrolls between desktops.

Compiz has more features also.

I hope this helps
