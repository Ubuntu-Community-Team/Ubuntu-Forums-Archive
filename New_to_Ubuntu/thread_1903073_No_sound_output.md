just a small suggestion
the more detail the better the replies

what is your sound card? make/model on the mobo

and have you made systems before? ( polite way of asking are you sure you have sound connections plugged in correct )

and have you played with the sound settings 
and not knowing what version of ubuntu you have, have you clicked the speaker and made sure not muted, and checked the preferences and turned up the volume?

more info is always better...
would you call a car mechanic and say "my car won't work, how much to fix it? or would you get a better answer asking saying " my starter motor won't turn over?
