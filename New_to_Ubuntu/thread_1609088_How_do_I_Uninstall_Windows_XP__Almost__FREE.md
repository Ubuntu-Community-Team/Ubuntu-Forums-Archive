### Post by Temposs on 2010-10-29
If you want the truly easiest way to go about giving Ubuntu your full computer, here is what I recommend:

1) Copy all of your important data(documents, photos, etc) to an external hard drive or a bunch of CDs or DVDs.

2) Make a Live CD of your favorite Ubuntu version(or use one you already have made).

3) Install Ubuntu again from your Live CD, and choose the "Use your entire hard drive" option when you're installing.

4) Copy all of your important data back to your hard drive with your fresh install of Ubuntu.

This is the most straightforward way to do what you want. Of course, you will have to install all your programs again and make all your favorite settings. But, if you consider yourself a newbie, I think it may be better to do this than to try a more involved option.
