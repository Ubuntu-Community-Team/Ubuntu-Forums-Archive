[LIST=1]
[*]Open a Terminal and go to the directory where you downloaded the file:                 cd /the/path/to/your/directory
[*]Change the script permissions:                 chmod a+x jolicloud-usb-creator-1.1.0.sh
[*]Run the program (at this stage you will be asked to enter your administrator password):                 ./jolicloud-usb-creator-1.1.0.sh
[*]Connect your USB key to your computer.
[*]In the Jolicloud USB Creator window, click *Browse* to find and select the *jolicloud-robby-alpha2c-live.iso* in your system.
[*]Select your target device: The USB key you have inserted should be automatically detected.
[*]Click *Create* to create your Jolicloud USB.
