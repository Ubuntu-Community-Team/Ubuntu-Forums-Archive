### Post by Kellemora on 2009-02-23
Hi Ja

Take a look at Evolution Calendar (called Mail & Calendar), just turn off the mail part.

I use a PalmOne to track a lot of things.  In Ubuntu, the data from the PalmOne is copied into Evolution Calendar.

A nice feature of this is that at the top of my screen where the day, date and time is showing, I can click that and my calendars pop up there.
You can also set alarms to let you know of upcoming events.
And the memo section lets you keep notes as well.

I'm not fond of Evolution for e-mail at all, which is why I have that turned off.  But there are a lot of features in their calendar portion of the program.
