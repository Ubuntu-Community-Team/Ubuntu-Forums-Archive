### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2014-03-29
First, to get your battery icon to show in the LXPanel, launch Menu->Preferences->Power Manager.  Choose the General Tab and then from the drop down menu under System tray icon choose Always show icon.
Second, to get programs to auto-start, launch Menu->Preferences->Default applications for lxsession.  Choose autostart and add your program.
See 
[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2182986](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=2182986)
That is supposed to work but I think there might be a problem.  There is a discussion of a work around as well.
