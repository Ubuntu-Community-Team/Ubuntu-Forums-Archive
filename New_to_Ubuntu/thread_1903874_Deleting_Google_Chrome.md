### Post by rawbertb on 2012-01-03
This is probably a really dumb newbie question, but how do I delete Google Chrome from Kubuntu?   I have checked the software manager, and Chrome is nowhere to be found.  I downloaded and installed it directly from Google instead of just installing Chromium...looking back, I have no idea why!  I never use it and would just like to delete it.  Any suggestions?

---

### Post by cortman on 2012-01-03
Open a terminal and type

```
sudo apt-get purge google-chrome-stable
```

Should do it for you.
