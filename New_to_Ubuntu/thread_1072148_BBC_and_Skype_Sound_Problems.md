### Post by Sirron on 2009-02-17
You may be experiencing an old and annoying problem involving the Flash 9 browser plugin and Pulse Audio.

In my opinion, Pulse Audio is a powerful and useful feature in today's Linux desktop, but it's not suitable for all users as it isn't completely compatible with all software in all situations.

Before resorting to removing Pulse Audio, you should try solving the flash problem.

To do that, I recommend you download [_this_]("http://fpdownload.macromedia.com/get/flashplayer/current/install_flash_player_10_linux.deb"); Flash Player 10.
