### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2010-10-18
Films on DVD use proprietary video codecs and encryption. These are not included with Ubuntu out of the box for legal reasons (depending on locale). Follow these instructions to enable Ubuntu to play films from a DVD.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RestrictedFormats/PlayingDVDs)
