### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-09-22
I have no explanation of why you are seeing that on a released (stable) version of Ubuntu. Some years ago the Restart option was removed from the menu and people complained. So, the Restart option was put back in the menu. I do not see a Restart option in Wily Werewolf (15.10) which I am using.

In my opinion it is not something to care about. Both options, when present, will open the same dialog box asking for confirmation to either Restart or Shut Down. To Cancel we click the x in a circle at the top left corner of the box or click the background wallpaper. With Unity we do not get a count down to action as we do in some user interfaces. So, I do not find it a big deal to click Shut Down and then click Restart. It is a two click option either way without an automatic action from only one click.

Regards.
