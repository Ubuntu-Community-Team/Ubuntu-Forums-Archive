### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2009-01-10
Yes you need compiz running to use emerald.
To make sure you have compiz running in the first place, right click on your desktop and select "change desktop background"
in the last tab see if the effects are enabled.
The next step is to make sure emerald is being loaded, to make sure its your window decorator go to applications and to system tools, then to compiz fusion icon.
This will give you a little icon on your top panel, right click it and go to "select window decorator"
and select emerald.
Hopefully that will do it, but if you need to install emerald its easy to do.
