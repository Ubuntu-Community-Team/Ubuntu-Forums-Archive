### Post by Dutch70 on 2011-03-17
It's not that their minimized. It's that the resolution of the pics are too small to begin with. You need to download pics with the same size resolution as your monitor, or at least close to it.

### Post by mcduck on 2011-03-17
> **sashag said:**
> Hi!

So does this mean that if I have my screen set to 800x600 resolution, I would have to make the pictures the same size? And if I do, how do I go about making that happen?

No, making the picture that size wouldn't help.

The picture you download has to *already* be at least that size, or larger. Trying to increase the size of an image will always make it blurry. Your computer can't create new details into the picture when you increase it's size, it only has the information that already exists in the image.

### Post by mcduck on 2011-03-17
Have you considered increasing the font size instead of adjusting the display resolution? That should make everything easier to see without messing with the image quality. If you have a TFT display, they don't really handle any other than their native resolution well (TFT panels have a certain amount of physical pixels on them, and can only display that resolution. If you set your computer's resolution to anything else, the display has to scale the image to fit the panel's native resolution)

What comes to images, you don't need to have same resolution image as your display is using, same *or larger* resolution should work fine.
