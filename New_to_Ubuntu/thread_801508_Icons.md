### Post by Exsecrabilus on 2008-05-20
You have to download .tar.gz files and install them.

You can do so by **System** >> **Preferences** >> **Appearance**.

Click on the **Theme** tab and click the **Install** button.
Locate to the place where you downloaded the .tar.gz file and double-click on it.

Now go to whatever theme and click on the **Customize** button.
If it is an icon, locate to the **Icon** tab and select it, etc.

You can download cool themes from lots of places, including: [http://gnome-look.org/](http://gnome-look.org/)

### Post by oarion7 on 2008-05-20
As for your second question yes, you have a couple of options. You can right click on the icon of the folder and click properties, and then click the picture of the icon in the properties dialog and then browse from there, click the folder that contains the icons, then click ok, and you'll be able to select from among the icons in the folder. Otherwise you can go to the emblems tab in the properties menu and superimpose items such as a picture of a video camera or something.:)
