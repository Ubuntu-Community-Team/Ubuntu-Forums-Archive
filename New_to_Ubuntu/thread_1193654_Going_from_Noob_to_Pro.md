### Post by .Maleficus. on 2009-06-21
I'd recommend going to both CompTIA's and Red Hat's websites and taking the pretests for Linux+ and RHCE.  It will give you a good handle of what you need to improve on and then you can create your path from there.  I'd recommend just heading to Barnes and Noble or Borders and picking up a beginner's Linux book, even if you don't learn well from reading.  I haven't found a whole lot of interactive "learn Linux" programs or websites.
