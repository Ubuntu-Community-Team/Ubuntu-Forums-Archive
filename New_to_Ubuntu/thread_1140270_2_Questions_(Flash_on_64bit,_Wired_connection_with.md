### Post by buck2825 on 2009-04-27
my aunt had an issue with her DSL this weekend.  if she had it plugged directly into the PC she could get to the world.  but when she plugged into the router nothing.  her router had gotten set back to factory defaults.  

the router factory IP was 192.168.1.1 with a subnet mask of 255.255.255.0.  I changed the routers IP to 192.168.0.1 and everything got happy.  just a thought.
