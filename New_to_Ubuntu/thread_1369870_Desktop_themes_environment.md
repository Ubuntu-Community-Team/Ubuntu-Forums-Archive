Hi and welcome to the forums!


Its quite easy to install themes, granted they were packaged correctly. 

Right click on your desktop and select 'change background'
in the resulting window that appears, select the first tab that says themes.

Now click on install, navigate to and open your theme, and its installed! 
Pick it from the list and all is right!
