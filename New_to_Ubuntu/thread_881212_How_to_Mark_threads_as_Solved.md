Would it be possible to make a change in the forums? The thread is often already solved, but will not be marked as solved until the thread starter reads the proposed solution and then press "thread solved". 

1) the thread owner might never come back to read the solution
2) the thread owner might come back, but it might take a long time.

So basically, everybody on this forum who has knowledge about the subject, will open the thread and read the solution which was already posted but not yet confirmed, which is a waste of time.

What could help, is that somebody who replies to a thread, has the ability of marking the thread "possibly solved". That way other forum users who read "possibly solved" don't have to bother reading the whole story but can leave it there. If the thread owner reads the proposed solution, he can accept or decline the proposed solution.

Drawback is that in some cases the real solution might take a bit longer, but major gain is that a whole lot more forum users will actually help on the forums. The main reason why I am not actively helping is that it's "too much effort" figuring out which thread is needy for help etc.
