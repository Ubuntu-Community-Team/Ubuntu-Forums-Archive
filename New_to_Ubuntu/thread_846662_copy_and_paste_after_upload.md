### Post by bumanie on 2008-07-01
To copy - put cursor at edge of text to copy, hold down left mouse and the drag cursor across text. Text will be highlighted. In linux if you have a wheel mouse, click wheel to paste text. Alternatively do first step then control c to copy and control v to paste. Hope that helps.
