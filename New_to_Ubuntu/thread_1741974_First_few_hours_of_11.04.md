The *ubuntu-bug packagename* command is what you want. Get a launchpad account ([www.launchpad.net](www.launchpad.net)) and report bugs as you come across them. For example, if you find an issue with the Empathy IM program, open a terminal and report it via *ubuntu-bug empathy* and it'll automatically collect info about your system and give you the chance to write a description on the issue.

Good luck! :)
