### Post by nzjethro on 2011-07-04
You can also torrent it, which will be faster than downloading from the Ubuntu website. The .torrent can be found at [http://torrent.ubuntu.com/simple/natty/desktop/](http://torrent.ubuntu.com/simple/natty/desktop/).
You will need to open this file in a bittorrent client (like uTorrent) to download the Ubuntu .iso file, which can then be burned to a CD, or made into a Live USB.
