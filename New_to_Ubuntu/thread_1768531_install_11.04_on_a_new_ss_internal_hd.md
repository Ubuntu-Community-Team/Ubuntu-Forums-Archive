### Post by lightstream on 2011-05-26
Linux needs its own drivers, completely different to Windows ones. There are Open Source drivers available for most hardware, but sometimes you will have to used a closed source (proprietary) one, often this is the case for wireless drivers.

However even if you do need closed source drivers, they are quite likely included with the Ubuntu install.

Before you install, I would make sure everything works fine by running from the USB. In particular check the sound is good, and WiFi works also.
