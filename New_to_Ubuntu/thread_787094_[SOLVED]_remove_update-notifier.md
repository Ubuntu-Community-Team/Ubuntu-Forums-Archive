To make it short and simple:

How to get rid of **update-notifier**(in Hardy)?
I really hate it!

I mean, I like to update manually myself, and that thing takes up 4 Mb of my RAM, that's huge for that little applet. Well, in Gutsy you could disable it in services. Can't do that in Hardy, no.

---

### Post by sdennie on 2008-05-08
Go to System->Preferences->Sessions and uncheck Update Notifier.  Next time you login, it will not bug you (and will never ask you to reboot or anything like that).
