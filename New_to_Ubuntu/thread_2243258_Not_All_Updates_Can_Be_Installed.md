### Post by nerdtron on 2014-09-07
VLC won't update unless you make changes on your repositories. VLC versions are usually locked on the version that shipped on the current version of Ubuntu. Firefox on the other hand updates almost a day after the release from mozilla.
Some programs update, some programs don't. Basically, depends on developers and how the programs are tested for your Ubuntu version.
