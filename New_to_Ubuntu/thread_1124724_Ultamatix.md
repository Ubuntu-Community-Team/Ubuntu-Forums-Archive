### Post by Paqman on 2009-04-13
> **rick astley said:**
> Against forum policy? are we talking about the same thing sir? "Ultamatix" not "Ultimatix."


Ultamatix is based on the older Automatix, which was a badly coded mess, and quite capable of breaking your system.

> 
What i want to use it for is the 100 or so applications it has to offer.


Auto/Ultamatix were designed to try and deal with some limitations that Ubuntu had a few years ago. Those limitations no longer exist. Most of those applications are available in the normal repos, and most of the ones that aren't (eg: Google Earth, Skype) are available from the Medibuntu repository.
