### Post by Trzone on 2008-07-16
I've gotten wine to run itunes, although, i can't seem to get it to detect my ipod.  It can extract cds, i have been able to purchase songs and it does manage a library.  It's slower than what it would be, so don't use it to manage your music.  Banshee is alright to have a music library, however i found that gktpod was the best in terms of actually managing your ipod.  Also, remember that you must use the itunes plus store in order to get non-drm music to play on ubuntu.
