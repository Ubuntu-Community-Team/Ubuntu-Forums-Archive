### Post by cwmoser on 2008-09-26
I have a Maxtor oneTouch Mini an it works.

If you are plugging it into a USB port expander, there are issues.

Also, make sure and plug in both USB connectors -- I found that some systems do not supply enough power to the drive.  Plug in both the red and the black USB cables.
