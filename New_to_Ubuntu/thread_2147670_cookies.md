### Post by KoRnKloWn on 2013-05-22
As PaulyK16 pointed out clear the cookies from your browser, however if you want an app to clean temporary files from multiple programs, then checkout Bleach Bit in the Ubuntu Software Center, it can clean basically all the cache from most programs, including Firefox, Chromium, and Google Chrome, as well as other non-web-browser programs. It even supports deleting empty space (overwriting with 0s) so that it's gone for good, so the CIA will never find the blueprints you deleted ;)
