### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-06-05
If you have a [separate partition for /home](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/separatehome), then you can preserve your settings and personal files, by just mounting the home partition again during the installation, without formatting it. But if you don't have a separate partition, then your personal data will be overwritten.

BTW, if you want to preserve any partition, you must use the manual partition method, not the "entire disk" option.
