### Post by steveneddy on 2008-12-12
You can search Google images for icons.

You can create your own icons.

You can look in

user/share/pixmaps

and maybe use some from there.

You can use an avatar if you find one that you like.

You can even use a small cropped picture you have and make that an icon for a launcher.

Like the previous poster stated, all you are doing is putting up a picture of some kind that launches a **terminal command** (for lack of a better phrase) when you click it.
