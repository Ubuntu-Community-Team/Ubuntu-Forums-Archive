### Post by Thomas Garman on 2010-08-15
Like another poster said...

you go to your tmp folder in your filesystem.  Whenever you watch a video on YOUTUBE it will actually download the entire video into your temporary folder and then play it, which you experience as "streaming"...

So, you cut/paste that .flv file onto your desktop and then rename it as "blueorchid.flv" or whatever you want to name it and then it will persist on your system even after you close your browser.

When you are doing this cut/paste do not close the browser or else the file will be deleted because it is in your tmp folder but once you have save it with a different name it will persist.
