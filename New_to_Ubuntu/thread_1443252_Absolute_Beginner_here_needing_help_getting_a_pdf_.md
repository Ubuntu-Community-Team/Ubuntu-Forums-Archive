### Post by ndefontenay on 2010-03-30
Hi.

pdf are supported natively. When you click on a pdf, a pdf viewer will open it.

If you need a more advanced pdf viewer, go to Applications>Ubuntu Software Centre (or add/rmeove programs depending of the version) and look for acrobat reader. It is in the repository.

If you don't have it there, you can also download acrobat reader online, search for the installer for ubuntu 8.10+ and that's it.


note: preferred way should be from Ubuntu Software Centre, the repository.
