### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-06-03
Since Ultimate Edition is just a jam-packed version of Ubuntu on a DVD, It'll be safe to quote the [Ubuntu System Requirements for Visual Effects]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements#head-2659cd6ecdcc610d675550bceabb5cbfb76541b8").
> 
[LIST]
[*]1.2 GHz x86 processor
[*]384 MB of system memory (RAM)
[*]Supported graphics card¹
[/LIST]

¹: I'd suggest an NVIDIA card, at least 128MB.

The only sort of spec I can find on the site is [here]("http://ultimateedition.info/blog/category/how-to/"):
**It is the spec of a testing machine. So whether or not it's recommended is not known.**
To skip the jargon quoting, in simple terms, 
> 
[LIST]
[*]Compatible Motherboard
[*]2.4GHz Duo CPU
[*]1GB RAM
[*]256MB Graphics
[/LIST]

Although, perhaps the above would be better suited for the [Gamers Edition.]("http://ultimateedition.info/Ubuntu_Ultimate_1.4_Gamers/")

Finally, looking at the download size of the ISO image, I'd say at least 10GB of disk space is needed for the install.

Hope that that has been of some help.
