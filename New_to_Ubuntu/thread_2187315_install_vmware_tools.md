### Post by QIII on 2013-11-11
Perhaps there is a language barrier here.

What sammiev said is correct, but I will try to explain further:

When you are asked for your password, what you are typing will not be visible on the screen.  Nothing will appear to happen.  The terminal does not "echo" what you are typing -- that is, you will not see anything.  However, what you are typing as input is being accepted.  This is normal behavior.

Simply type your password and don't worry that nothing is being shown.  After you have typed your password, press Enter.
