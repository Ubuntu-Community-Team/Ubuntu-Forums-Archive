### Post by androssofer on 2012-09-10
did some hunting! and I found a simple solution..

tested in chrome, Ubuntu 12.04

open the youtube link.. right click on the video and select "pop out". It will open a new window with just the video in it.. 

then press F11, then it will make tht window full screen, and you can do what you like on the other screen..
