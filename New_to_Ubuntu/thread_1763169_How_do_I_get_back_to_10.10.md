### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-05-20
> **TrumpyNZ said:**
> I'm running 10.10 and upgraded to 11.04.
After this upgrade, I can't even get back to 10.10.
Can anyone help?:(

If you upgraded, then your system is now 11.04 - you can't just "go back" to what it was a day ago because that data is no longer on the hard disk.

If there are showstopping problems that cause 11.04 to not work for you, then please do your best to report them on [www.launchpad.net;](www.launchpad.net;) without bug reports, bugs don't get fixed. Give as much information as possible, and if you know the name of the package that is causing the problem use the "ubuntu-bug" command to help you file the bug report. Then reinstall 10.10 from the CD.

If you just don't like the 11.04 Unity interface, and that's why you want to go back, then I'd advise you to give it a couple of weeks; if in that time you still don't like using Unity, then log out, click on your username, and at the bottom of the screen change the session to "Ubuntu Classic". Then you'll have the old Gnome 2 user interface. But be aware that Gnome 2 is abandonware and Ubuntu 11.10 will no longer have it.
