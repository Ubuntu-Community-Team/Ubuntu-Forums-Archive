### Post by Matt__ on 2010-06-11
my DELL mini 10v works great, had some issues with lucid 10.04 but resolved now.
Needed to install STA wireless driver from 'hardware drivers'.
(all previous versions worked out of the box 8.04/8.10/9/04..never tried 9.10)

I also doubled the ram to its max of 2Gb which can be a bit daunting as you have to take the whole netbook to pieces! I also opted for standard desktop rather than netbook remix as I'm not keen on the nbr interface. (autohide panels and drop font size in widow titles to max visual real estate)
