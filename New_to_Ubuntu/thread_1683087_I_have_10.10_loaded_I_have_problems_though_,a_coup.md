### Post by migs73 on 2011-02-07
Hi Jon, welcome to the forums,

Once you have downloaded the image, check it integrity using this guide;

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

The hashes can be found here;

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes)

Once you burn it to CD check it again. You can also use the function to check the disc integrity on the menu it presents.
