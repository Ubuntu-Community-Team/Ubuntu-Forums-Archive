Assuming you have installed the nVidia driver from Additional Drivers you can then edit the power settings by going to:

System > Administration > nVidia X Server Settings

On my card it is under PowerMizer, here you can change a few settings (Set it to always use Max Performance). Otherwise have a look around the settings
