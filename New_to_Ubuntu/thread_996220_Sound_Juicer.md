I use banshee as my music client.
I'm trying to import a CD into my music library and said I needed an encoder for ripping. So I used sound juicer instead. How can I get those music files into my music library? Or is there an alternate way to do this?

Probably a silly question, but thanks for the help!

---

### Post by Sealbhach on 2008-11-28
In Banshee, do Media/Import Media.

Select the folder that Soundjuicer put your CD music in and it will import them.

I would suggest not to duplicate your music files, so in Preferences, make sure that "Copy files to media folders when importing" is unticked.

Hope that answers...
