### Post by Sin@Sin-Sacrifice on 2010-06-06
I've had no trouble with nvidia cards and sony blue ray drives. I agree about using xbmc. It found my mce usb remote without any tweaking and is nice looking and customizable. You can also boot into it as a stand alone gui but there isn't very good tuner card support. Though if you get something that is well supported (Hauppauge PVR-150 with the br chipset for instance) there are plugins and tweaks out there to get them working. Although I've never tried because I use an HVR-1600 and there's nothing out there for it. You can also add youtube, tedtalks, hulu, and others to XBMC if you look hard enough. Remote controlling tedtalks is my favorite xbmc tweak. Make sure, also, your new HTPC has a means to be plugged into the stereo. I get called about that semi-often... (there's always cheater cables).
