### Post by jimmy the saint on 2009-11-03
Follow the instructions to add the medibuntu repository here

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Medibuntu)

Once it is added, go to System>Administration>Synaptic Package Manager

Search for Google Earth.  Check the box next to google earth.  Click on the "Apply" button. 

Enjoy Googel Earth.

Google maps is a webservice you can access via any web browser.

Open Firefox.  

[www.google.com](www.google.com)

Click on "maps"

Enjoy Google Maps
