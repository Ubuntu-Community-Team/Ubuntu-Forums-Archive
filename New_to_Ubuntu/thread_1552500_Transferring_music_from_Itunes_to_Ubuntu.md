### Post by Edserman on 2010-08-13
The most reliable way I've found is to install VirtualBox 3.2.8 (Windows 7) and run Sharepod 3.9.6 create a share (via VirtualBox) and transfer your entire music library and more into ~/music folder.

Other more direct ways seem a little less robust. This sharepod reads the ipod/iphone database really well, and creates a perfect directory structure for you.
