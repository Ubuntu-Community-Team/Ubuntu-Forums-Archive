### Post by k3lt01 on 2008-10-23

> thank for the reply cariboo907.sure, i will upgrade my ubuntu when the next LTS release comes out.:)
my question is just to satisfy my curiosity on what will happen to the repo of obsolete ubuntu releases.
The repos get moved from *archive.ubuntu.com* to *old.releases.ubuntu.com* after the relevant version reaches End-Of-Life status. They can still be downloaded from there if you change your sources.list file to point to that source the only things is after the repo is placed in old.releases.ubuntu.com there are no updates.
