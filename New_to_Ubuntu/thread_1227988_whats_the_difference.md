### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2009-07-31
They are mostly interchangeable these days, but aptitude adds some nice features, especially if you manage a lot of headless CLI-only systems (like a nice ncurses interface -- type "sudo aptitude" by itself to see what I mean).

You might want to stick to one or the other, as I'm not certain they keep track of dependencies (i.e., auto-removeables) quite the same way.
