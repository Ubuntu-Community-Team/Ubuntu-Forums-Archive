### Post by Herman on 2015-07-01
Have you tried right-clicking on the item in your Google Earth sidebar and going to properties?

There's an 'altitude' tab in there, (sometimes you need to click 'share style' to get to it). 

Often after adding a new layer it could have the wrong settings in the altitude tab and that can cause some or all of it to fail to show up.
If it's set on 'absolute' or something, try changing to 'clamped to ground' and see if that helps.
