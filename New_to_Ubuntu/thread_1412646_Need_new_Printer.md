### Post by ScottinSoCal on 2010-02-25
There's a big, long thread about this already, with several recommendations. I'm using a Brother 5250 network printer, and it works beautifully. I picked it up on sale at OfficeMax, or Staples, or one of those places, and was using it with a Mac Powerbook and my laptop configured with Windows. When I switched the Windoze machine to Ubuntu, it was detected and configured without a hitch. Duplex printing (both side of the page), multiple paper trays available, and I've gotten very good life out of the print cartridge. Very happy with it, and wouldn't hesitate to buy it again.
