### Post by sydbat on 2008-06-21
Were you playing with any desktop settings? Sounds like you may have asked the panel (bar) to "hide". Try placing your cursor near the upper right and/or left edges of your screen. Does the bar reappear? If so, right click on it and choose "properties". Then, in the 'General" tab choose you see options to Autohide the panel...if it is checked, uncheck it.

If you do not get anything showing up when you put the mouse cursor near the top of your display, I am not sure as I too am quite new. Someone with more experience in Terminal would be able to help then.
