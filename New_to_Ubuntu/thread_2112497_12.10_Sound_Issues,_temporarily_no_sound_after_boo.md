### Post by Bhaelfur on 2013-02-11
I figured it out, somewhat. I switched to CrunchBang. Then I added "blacklist snd-usb-audio" to /etc/modprobe.d/alsa-base-blacklist.conf

Since I switched distros, I don't know if this fix will work for Ubuntu users, but figured it was worth mentioning.
