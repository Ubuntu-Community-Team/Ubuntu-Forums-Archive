### Post by Bartender on 2012-11-10
Since the version of Thunderbird available from the Ubuntu repos is usually a bit behind the latest version, they will quite often look somewhat different.  I'm happy to use the repository version since it's been vetted for proper function in Ubuntu.  

When Mozilla freshens the interface, Windows users will see it first.  I guess that's less of a concern now that Mozilla has announced that they're not going to spend much time on TB.

Other than that, there are few differences.  I know of one: most of the typical tweaks in Ubuntu TB are under Edit>Preferences.  In Windows, it's Tools>Options.
