### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-10-26
> **EvanRogers said:**
> Ah, sorry, but too complicated. Can you please tell me how to do that? I would really love to have this theme installed.

it should be very easy to do:
Step 1: go to your downloaded theme file
2: unzip it by right clicking and hitting "extract" or "extract here" (I am not using Ubuntu right now but there should be a command like that somewhere in your right click menu)
3: after you extract it you will see a new folder where your files extracted to
4: go inside of the folder
5: right click on the "linista2" folder and select "compress", compress it as a tar.gz
6: open up your theme manager and then load the theme like you usually do.

The process should be a hop, skip and a jump.
