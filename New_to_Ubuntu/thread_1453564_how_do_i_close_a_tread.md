### Post by undecim on 2010-04-13
> **purdurabo said:**
> well last night when i tried to start a new tread a staff member closed it because i already had one open.

If you have two threads about the same problem, the moderators will either merge them or close one to keep the forums tidy.

If you marked it as solved, but then had another question about it, you can try asking the question in the open thread, and often the people who had been helping you earlier will check back on the thread even though it's solved to make sure that everything is fine.
