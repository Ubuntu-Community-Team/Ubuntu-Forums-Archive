### Post by mikewhatever on 2009-05-04
> **ravi_buz said:**
> Hai i have ubuntu 8.04 installed and love it , i have recently downloaded 9.04 alternative cd of  kubuntu . so can i install it in my ubuntu

No, at least not in the sense you chose to word it. Kubuntu is not a program to be installed on top of Ubuntu, but an operating system alike Ubuntu. You also can not install kubuntu-desktop from the 9.04 disk to 8.04, because it requires all the dependencies from 9.04. However, you can install Kubuntu 9.04 on a separate partition, or replace the current 8.04 installation.
