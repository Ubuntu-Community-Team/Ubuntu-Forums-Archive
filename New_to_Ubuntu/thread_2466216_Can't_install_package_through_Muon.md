### Post by guiverc on 2021-08-23
I'll provide the following

To change your mirror (*maybe worth looking just to check it*), refer to the manual page as follows

- [https://manual.lubuntu.me/stable/4/4.3/software_sources.html?highlight=mirror](https://manual.lubuntu.me/stable/4/4.3/software_sources.html?highlight=mirror)

*You didn't mention a release; that link is for the latest stable release; ie. Lubuntu 21.04*.  [I]You can change the word "stable" to say "lts" to see the manual for Lubuntu 20.04 LTS.

[/I]A list of *official* mirrors can be found at 

- [https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+archivemirrors](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+archivemirrors)

I would check your mirror is an official mirror site, and consider changing it to another, OR switching to the 'main' archive.
