### Post by pcdreamer on 2009-05-10
I have installed Ubuntu 9.04 64bit on two of my computers and installed the 32bit version on another computer. All 3 of the computers monitors go into hibernation after about 10 minutes or so. My monitor shut off is set to never. Is there anyway to fix this?

---

### Post by Didius Falco on 2009-05-10
Check the settings in **System/Preferences/Screensaver** . I believe mine was set to ten minutes and defaulted to a blank screen - I think your monitors aren't going to sleep, they are just using the blank screensaver.

To test that, you can just pick a different screensaver and wait a few minutes to see if it displays.

Regards,

Didius
