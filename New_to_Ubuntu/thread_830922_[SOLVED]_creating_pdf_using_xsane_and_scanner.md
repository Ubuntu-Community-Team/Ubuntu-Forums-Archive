### Post by dlmoak on 2008-06-16
Two ways:
1. Scan in your document or image.  Print and choose the pdf printer.  You will wind up with a pdf document of your image.

2. Install scan2pdf,which does exactly what it says.  You can scan multiple documents and create a single pdf of them all or just do singles if you wish.
