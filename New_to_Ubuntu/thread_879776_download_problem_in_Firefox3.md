### Post by kk0sse54 on 2008-08-04
Check where you are saving the files. Go to Edit > Preferences and check where downloaded files go. If they are saved to an unmounted partition or external partition the download won't start until you mount it.

### Post by limasierra on 2008-08-04
Your problem may be related to the place where you're downloads are set to be saved. If you predefined them to be saved on a removable device and it is not connected to your computer at the moment, the download will fail.

In firefox open Edit > Preferences
Select the "Main" tab and locate the "Downloads" items. Choose one item where downloads can be saved. It can be your desktop.
