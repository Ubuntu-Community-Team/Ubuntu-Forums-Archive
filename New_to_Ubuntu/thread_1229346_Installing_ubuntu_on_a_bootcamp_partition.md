### Post by pedro3005 on 2009-08-02
Did you make sure you burned the ISO as a image? Maybe it just burned the ISO raw there. Open up the CD in some OS, check inside. Are there folders and stuff, or just the ISO file? If just the ISO file, you burned it wrong. Then, you can use Google for information on how to burn ISO files on whatever OS you can use.
