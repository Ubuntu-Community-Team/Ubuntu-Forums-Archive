### Post by Scunnered on 2009-08-06
I am considering buying a 22 inch wide screen as a permanent fixture for use at home. The supplier advises that the item that I am looking at will work OK with Jaunty but I will require to change the screen resolution to 1680 x 1050.

When I go to System > Preferences > Display I am given 3 options the largest being 1024 x 768.  Will the new monitor be automatically registered and give the required resolution or what do I have to do to set things up.

I am deferring purchase pending your guidance

Thanking you in anticipation

### Post by squaregoldfish on 2009-08-06
The monitor should* be recognised automatically, and you will get the correct options in the display manager. IT will probably even set itself to the correct resolution automatically.

I say should, because I'm only 99% certain. Even if it isn't, you'll find plenty of advice on here about setting up your system to the correct resolution.

In conclusion, buy the monitor - you won't regret it!

Steve.
