### Post by Lars Noodén on 2012-09-29
PDF isn't really designed for editing.  It's an end format that holds your data before you print it out and throw it into the recycle bin.  Your best bet is to get a hold of it while it is still in an editable format like the OpenDocument Format (ODF).

That said, LibreOffice does offer a limited amount of support for editing PDFs.  So you can open *some* PDFs in LibreOffice.  However, that entirely depends on how the PDF was created and that its internals are in an editable state and not a horrific mess.

If you can, get hold of the original ODF.
