### Post by the_doc on 2008-06-24
Go to XP (presuming you haven't installed over it), open Thunderbird and find the export option, export your contacts (or whatever) to a file, copy to a USB pen drive (or whatever) go to Ubuntu open Thunderbird and find the import option.

Is this what you&#8217;re asking?
