### Post by Bulwinkle on 2011-05-02
I'm not sure what actually happened, but after having jailbroken several iDevices (you get a more in depth view of the device) over the years I've come to the conclusion that the older the iOS version the more prone the devices are to subtle eccentricity when dealing with connections, especially when dealing with Linux.  Over all iOS is very stable but when you get into terminal connections and connection to Linux systems iOS does not always place itself back into a receptive mode for communications; 2.x.x was really bad about this, 3.x.x was much better, and I've yet to experience and issue under 4.x.x.
