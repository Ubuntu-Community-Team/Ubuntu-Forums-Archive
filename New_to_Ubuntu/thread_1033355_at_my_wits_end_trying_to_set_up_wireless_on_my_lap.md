### Post by thegreenblob on 2009-01-07
Have you tried looking in the restricted drivers manager if there is a driver for your wireless card? (System --> Administration --> Hardware Drivers)

Also have you tried to just configure it? (Maybe it's already working but not configured) (System --> Preferences --> Network Configuration)
