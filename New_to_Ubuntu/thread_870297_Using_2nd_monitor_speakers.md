---

### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-07-25
You will need to run a cable from your 1/8" headphone jack to the input of the tv.  This will likely require you to use a splitter than converts a 1/8th audio cable to a Right/Left (red/white) RCA style cable that will plug into the TV's inputs.  These cost just a couple of dollars at your local tech store (like Radio Shack) + a few dollars for the cable(s) if you don't already own the necessary ones.
