### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-05-31
Is that what it defaulted as or was it different before?  That is just the name of the computer, and you can call it whatever you want.
You can go to System->Administration->Network, click the Unlock button, type in your password, then go to the General tab.  Here you can set your host name to whatever you want (no spaces or weird characters).
