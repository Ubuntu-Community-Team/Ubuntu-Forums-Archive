### Post by flanker12 on 2011-09-02
Hi, first post on this forum. I have a Toshiba Satellite with W7 and 11.04 dual boot. I often find that after carrying out updates to 11.04 through update manager that occasionally, but not always after shutting down 11.04 hangs when next trying to boot up. I have tried the repair option that appears in Grub on boot up but this does not always work. What I have found is a method that  works 100% of the time is to boot into W7, shut W7 down then boot 11.04. When using this method it is noticeable that W7 takes longer than normal to shutdown and will sit there for approx 30 seconds with the shutdown/busy circle spinning.
