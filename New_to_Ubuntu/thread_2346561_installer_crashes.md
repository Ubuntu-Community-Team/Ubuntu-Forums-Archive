### Post by poorguy on 2016-12-16
Ubuntu 16.04 requires a lot of system resources especially video graphics and your graphics card may not be able to run Unity Desktop Environment.
Since Xubuntu works you might consider a lighter distro as Ubuntu Mate 16.04 or Lubuntu 16.04 where the requirements are less demanding on system resources.

Also posting what type of graphics adapter you have would give better help.

This link may help.

Removing some dust may help too.
