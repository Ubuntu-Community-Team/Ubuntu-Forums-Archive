### Post by Dill on 2008-08-14
Well, you can't utilize Target disk mode unless both computers are Macs, so you're not doing anything wrong, you just can't do it.

As another poster hinted, you can transfer data wirelessly or otherwise over the network, which may be the easiest way. There are a number of ways to do this. Do you have working network adapters on both of your computers, and are you in range of an available wireless network?

Also, do you have an external hard drive? It may be simpler to transfer data from the Mac to that, and from the external to your Linux machine.
