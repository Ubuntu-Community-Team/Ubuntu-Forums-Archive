### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-09-23
First of all check the MD5SUM of downloaded image. It has nothing to do with the burning problem but it'll be good to check the quality of the image itself so it doesn't cause problems later on during installation.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

Is your CD Writer working properly? When was the last time your burnt a disc? If you can, try to burn some other disc e.g, data or music and see if it gets burnt correctly.

Also try to use some other burning app. I use a KDE app, K3B on Ubuntu to burn my cds. Try with some alternate apps.
