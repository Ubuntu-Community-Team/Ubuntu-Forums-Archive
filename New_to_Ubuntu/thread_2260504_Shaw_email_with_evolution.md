### Post by DuckHook on 2015-01-12
Shaw tech support is usually friendly, obliging and spectacularly unhelpful, because they are trained to deal only with mainstream customers and not with techies trying to push the envelope. AFAIK, Shaw's exchange mapi only works with mobile devices and is not configured to connect or work with desktop apps. I don't know of anyone who has gotten it to work, but you can try the Shaw forums for responses more relevant to Shaw, though you will find that it's almost impossible to find fellow Linux users on them.

Until very recently, they did not even have IMAP, only POP, so I already consider IMAP access a vast improvement over the Dark Ages they were content to wallow in till now. When will they open up exchange to desktop Linux clients? Your best bet is to ask Shaw.
