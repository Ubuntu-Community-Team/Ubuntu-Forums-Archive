### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-09-15
Read this information under the heading Interference

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wi-Fi]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wi-Fi")

Do not forget that you are only wirelessly connecting to the router. After that the telephone cable is being used to make the connection to the ISP. Poor quality telephone cable both inside the apartment building and outside making the connection to the telephone exchange can also affect the quality of the Internet connection. It is one reason why we may not get the speeds that ISPs say we can get when they advertise their services.
