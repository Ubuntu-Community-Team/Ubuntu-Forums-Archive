### Post by jakke1975 on 2014-05-29
+1 on the mouseover. When you click the cog (top right button next to the time on a standard installation), you see "Shutdown...". After clicking that option, a window opens with the Restart and Shutdown option.
The other possibility you have, is pushing the shutdown button of your computer (less than 3 seconds or you will cut the power) and it will give you all the options you have (logout, shutdown, restart, suspend, etc etc)
