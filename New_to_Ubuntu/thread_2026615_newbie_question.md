### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2012-07-15
Yup, reading from a CD is not as fast as from a hard disk. Your PC should run just fine. :)

The simplest and best way to do it is just to boot the CD and click "Install Ubuntu" on the desktop. Then just follow the wizard, choosing "Install over entire disk" at the partitioning menu.

Note: as others have pointed out, the heavier interface of Ubuntu may not run as well as the other buntus with lighter environments. It depends upon a lot of things, including your graphics hardware and the quality of the drivers available for that.
