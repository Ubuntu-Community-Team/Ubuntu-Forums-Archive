### Post by swoody on 2009-02-19
Hi, and Welcome to the forums! ):P

It is always available if you click on 'System' and then 'Help and Support'

To get it back to the bar, just drag it from the 'System' menu, and drop it on the bar that you want.

For background purposes only:
If the shortcut you are missing launches the same app as the System, Help & Support app, it is called "yelp". You can add it to the panel by selecting by rt click, Add to Panel, "Custom Application Launcher" and typing "yelp" in the command line.
