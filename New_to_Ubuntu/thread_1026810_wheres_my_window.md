### Post by CLomax on 2008-12-31
The only reason I can think of that happening is because the window list isn't on the panel. It should work the same way Windows does.

Anywho, if you can't see a list of windows on your panel (as shown in my attachment) you'll need to add it. It isn't difficult... promise! :)

Right click on an empty space on the panel and choose "Add to Panel..."

Find "Window List" near the bottom of the window and add it.

To bring up a minimised window, just click on it.
