How do I make Adobe the default pdf reader for everything, for example, opening an email attachment in Thunderbird, instead of Evince?

---

### Post by WRDN on 2008-08-21
Right click on a PDF file, select Properties and the "Open With" tab. Finally, select Adobe Reader and click OK.
