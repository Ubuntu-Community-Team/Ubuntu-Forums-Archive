### Post by kamitsukai on 2009-06-05
Ok I figure it Out! =]

Set The Video Codec on The Video Tab To:

MPEG-4 (FFMPEG)

And The Bitrate To:

1500 (kbps)

And Make Sure to Set The Scale Dimensions To:

320x240

Also If you want video thumbnails you going to have to create them yourself although easy to do it would of been nice if it did this on the fly...

Just take a screen shot of the video and then crop and resize it to 200x95 and then make sure the JPG thumbnail has the same name as the video =]
