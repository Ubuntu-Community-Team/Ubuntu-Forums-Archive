Don't make promises you want keep ;-)
You can ask as many questions as you like but keep it as one thread per issue for the benefit of others.

There are loads of threads about where to begin which it looks like you've already searched and found but I also recommend the wiki's. There's one in my sig. I particularly recommend "ubnutu (Intrepid: 8.10) Guide".

Since you're new, and before you pick up any bad habits (not saying you're guilty of anything and you may already know some of these):
Intrepid is Ubuntu 8.10 (eight point ten) not 8.1
Hardy is 8.04 (eight point zero four) not 8.4
Linux is pronounced "Lin" as in **Lin**k (without the "g" sound) "ux" not "Line -ux".
Don't be shy asking for help.
No such thing as a daft question.
Be patient.
Remember, Ubuntu is FREE, so is the help. Don't abuse that.
Not everything will work. What does work may not work 100% of the time.
Linux is not Windows (see sig).
Thank people where necessary (not needed for every reply, just when they help you solve something is fine).
Mark a thread as SOLVED when so.
