### Post by sub2007 on 2008-10-09
Whilst it's not a browser plugin, I think Pytube is a great program for downloading Youtube videos. You can either paste in a Youtube URL or do a Youtube search from directly within the program, queue up multiple videos to download at once, encode them into a number of formats and it also does other video sites as well. Well worth checking out in my opinion.

[http://www.getdeb.net/app/PyTube](http://www.getdeb.net/app/PyTube)
