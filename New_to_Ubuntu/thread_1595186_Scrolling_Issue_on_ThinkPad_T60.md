### Post by JHorrocks on 2010-11-18
Problem solved...

After reinstalling x amounts of times, ubuntu 9.xx 10.04, 10.10 i gave up and went back to running windows 7... all was ok as I previously mentioned..... BUT.... after using windows for a few weeks and "wanting" to use ubuntu on my T60 I had to try again...

Cut a very very long story short... it turns out to be the "PgDn" (page down button) on the top right hand side of my keyboard was dirty (please dont ask how i found out!!)

So here is my point to closing out my own original post.... 

"when ubuntu is telling you something is wrong, like your drive is failing, or your keys are stuck, believe it.." I didnt and i blamed ubuntu for being inferior etc... how wrong was I...!!!!
