### Post by staf0048 on 2009-08-22
Lots of stuff, besides the regular desktop uses, web, email, music, movies, you can also edit photos, audio, video - though that needs some development yet.  

You can turn your computer into a server to host websites with Apache, MySQL, and PHP.

You can do as much coding/programming as you please - C, C++, Perl, Python, Java . . you get the idea.  Since it's an open system you can even improve the base OS code to function as you please and redistribute your own changes.  

If you're into gaming there are tons of games in Synaptic - some are much better than others, though that's a matter of opinion.  I personally like NeverBall, Super Maryo Chronicals (for novelty reasons), and Flight Gear.  

If you're into astrophysics or astronomy you may was to check out Celestia or Stellarium.

If you have some Window's programs you liked to try to get working with Ubuntu give Wine as shot - but it's pretty hit and miss.

Install 20 other operating systems on a Virtual Machine with VirtualBox.

I think you get the idea . . . there really isn't much you can't do.
