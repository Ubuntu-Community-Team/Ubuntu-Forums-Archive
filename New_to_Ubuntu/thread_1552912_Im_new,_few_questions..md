### Post by Spice Weasel on 2010-08-14
Any guide for regular Ubuntu will also work with Ubuntu netbook remix.

The "Applications" that is mentioned is referring to a menu that is in the regular desktop version, I am almost sure that clicking on the Ubuntu button in the corner brings up the menu, but I am not entirely sure. The "Applications" menu on the netbook remix is basically that launcher on your desktop. It will display the exact same contents. To move the panel to the bottom, right click on it. There should be an option for adding another panel, after doing this you can just delete the one at the top. I can't give exact instructions at the moment because I am using a different desktop, sorry. 

To install new software, the "Ubuntu Software Center" on Favourites + System will do fine for most common applications, and the "Synaptic Package Manager" in System is better for installing and managing more advanced programs.

Hope this helps. :D
