### Post by Zill on 2011-10-22
Calerid:  The current stable version of CrunchBang is based on Debian 6 “Squeeze”, *not* Ubuntu.  You would therefore have huge problems with dependencies if you tried to combine apps from both repositories.

I recommend you keep your Ubuntu and CrunchBang installations totally separate!

BTW, you may find the [CrunchBang forums]("http://crunchbanglinux.org/forums/") of assistance.
