### Post by Mark Phelps on 2013-05-24
If, as Google indicates, your Dell has the Mobility Radeon 4330, AMD restricted driver support for that ENDED with Ubuntu 12.04.1.  If you install 12.04.2, you will be relegated to the default Radeon open-source drivers as AMD dropped support for their restricted drivers for the HD 2x/3x/4x series last summer.
