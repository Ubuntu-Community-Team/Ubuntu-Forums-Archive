### Post by nrballard on 2014-05-21
I found a couple ways of going about this:

1) When you open the New Tab page (or if it opens by default), you should see your bookmarks and bookmark folders in a little strip just underneath the address bar. From here, you can Ctrl+Left-Click on your bookmarks to open them in new tabs.

2) If you click on Settings > Bookmarks > Bookmarks Manager, you can use Ctrl+Left-Click to select multiple bookmarks, then Right-Click on one of the highlighted bookmarks and select "Open All Bookmarks"

Hopefully this helps.
