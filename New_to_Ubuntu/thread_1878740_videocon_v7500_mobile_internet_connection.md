### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-11-10
I'm presuming that you're connecting your phone to the PC using a USB cable, right? Or, are you trying to access the Internet Connection on the phone through a Wireless connection with your PC?

Either way, this is referred to as "tethering" -- and some ISPs specifically BLOCK that capability on the cell phones they sell.

Others allow it, but you have to pay extra for the tethering capability.

So, the first thing I would do is find out if tethering is available with your cell phone plan with your ISP.
