### Post by bumanie on 2009-11-29
+1 for HP - they have the best and most extensive linux support of any printer manufacturer. Look [here]("http://hplipopensource.com/hplip-web/supported_devices/index.html") to ensure you buy a supported printer that suits your needs. They have a self extracting installer if a printer is added post Ubuntu installation, better still if the printer is plugged in and turned on during the Ubuntu installation, the printer will automatically be installed and configured without any user input.
