### Post by gpilkay on 2008-11-18
Firefox updates are done as part of the normal updates.  To check manually, go to the Update manager, and just click 'check' (you will have to put in your password).  As far as hotmail goes, a lot of issues stem from Java.  If you go to add/remove programs and look for 'restricted extras', you can download a lot of those things all at once.  Usually this fixes the problem.
