### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-05-15
You can use the "Wallpaper" plugin of Compiz, but then you won't have your icons on the Desktop, because those are drawn by Nautilus.

Since you are apparently new here, I would like to give you a tip. This subject is very common and there tons of posts about it in the forums. To search for them you can use Google site search. This type of search, which includes a string for the web site you want to search, gives you only results related to the chosen site. For example you could search for "*wallpaper different workspace site:ubuntuforums.org*" or "*wallpaper different desktop site:ubuntuforums.org*". They will give these results:

[wallpaper different desktop](http://www.google.com/search?q=wallpaper+different+desktop+site:ubuntuforums.org&hl=en&sourceid=mozilla-search&num=20&start=0&start=0)
