### Post by Three11s12 on 2012-05-04
I am in the middle of an Ubuntu install (my first!) myself, so I am afraid I can't help you with really anything to do with that.  But I would recommend [www.udacity.com](www.udacity.com) for learning python.  I took the 101 course, with fantastic results (not my grade per se, but the learning is wonderful).  Also the 200 level courses (with the possible exception of 212) are geared for people with 101 experience.  Anyway, it's a good start for someone like me that has literally NO CS programming experience, and I imagine would be good as well for someone with no PYTHON experience, regardless of other language familiarity.  And the classes have deadlines, but allow for people to go their own pace as well!  Good luck!
