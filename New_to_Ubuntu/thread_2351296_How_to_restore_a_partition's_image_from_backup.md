I used the disk utility to create backup images of the partitions of my drive and now I wonder if I want to restore the image of a certain partition, say that from /dev/sda4 from backup, what should I do? ---

### Post by martemarziano on 2017-02-01
It seems that I was rather quick to post my question. I went back to disks utility and of course after you mark the target partition you should click on the "gears" and choose restore partition from the menu. My mistake was that I chose restore disk image from the menu on top right. Always something new to learn.
