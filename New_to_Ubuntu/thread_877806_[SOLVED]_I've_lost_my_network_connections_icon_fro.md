### Post by ingeva on 2008-08-02
> **t0p said:**
> In the Add to Panel list, there is the option **Network Monitor** - you'll find it quicker if you type "network" in the search field at the top of the list.  Isn't that what you want?

I believe it's this one:

From the System menu, choose Administration. Then right-click on "Network" and choose "Add to panel.

If it doesn't get placed where you want it, right-click on the icon and choose move.

If this is not it, check here, see if you can find something from the description:

[http://www.gnome.org/projects/NetworkManager/](http://www.gnome.org/projects/NetworkManager/)
