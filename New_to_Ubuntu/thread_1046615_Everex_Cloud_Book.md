I was thinking the Cloudbooks came with gOS (maybe Rocket):

[http://www.thinkgos.com/](http://www.thinkgos.com/)

Anyway gOS is based on Ubuntu. I've never really played with a Cloudbook (outside of a store) but I've played with several other Everex products and the one repetitive hangup on Gutsy and Hardy both was having to manually add the "openchrome" display driver to xorg.

Based on the Everex desktops I've installed 8.10 on it seems that Intrepid does a better job of automatically finding the openchrome driver.
