### Post by deadflowr on 2014-08-21
It's the files generated from Ubuntu's backup program.

Maybe open the backup application and look at the settings you have for it.
Looks like you might have it set to automatically backup nightly(or daily).

You can disable it all together or reset it to backup less frequently, if you like.
