### Post by Anxious Nut on 2009-02-24
If you mean you want to let's say to take from 0:00:15 to 0:00:20 and make it a movie,

then play the video that you have, pause it on the scene that you want it to begin with, and press the button in the toolbar below that has a bracket and the letter A "A[=" (when you put your mouse on it says "Selection: start")  and then continue the clip till it is the scene where you want it to end, pause it and click on the button that is next to it (the one with the opposite bracket and the letter B "=]B" and when you put your mouse on it it says "Selection: end")
