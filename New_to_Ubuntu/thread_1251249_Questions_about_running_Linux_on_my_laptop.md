### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-08-27
> **Liolikas said:**
> Is is hard to say from what you provide... we need to know part models... everything 99% will work maybe question is camera and wlan. As I said best way is download liveCd andtry out.

+1

This is your BEST solution, by far!  I've installed Linux to several different laptops, and even among the same models, there are sometimes differences in hardware.

The ONLY way to know if the hardware specific to your laptop will work is to try it -- which is exactly what running from a LiveCD will do.  Anything that does not work in LiveCD mode will range from trivial to impossible to fix after install.

And, before anyone says "nothing is impossible to fix", I have several case histories where I had to reluctantly restore MS Windows to laptops because of problems that could not be fixed.
