### Post by coffeecat on 2010-09-16
You want to save the image you've found from a google image search? I don't know of any scripts, but it's easy enough to do from the GUI - just 2-3 mouse clicks.

Do your google image search and click on the thumbnail of the image you want. The full-size image appears in a window. Right-click on the and "save as".

There's also a Firefox addon called "Google Image Help" but I've not tried it. That may be useful to you.

**Edit:** hmmm, not bad. It saves at least - oh, let's see - one mouse click. :wink: Beware the 'open all google images' option. That's a lot. :-s
