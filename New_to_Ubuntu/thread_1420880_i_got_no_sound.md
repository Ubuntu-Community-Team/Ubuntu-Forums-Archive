### Post by Bradj47 on 2010-03-03
I'm guessing it's either a driver problem or a hardware problem. If it's a hardware problem make sure your sound card is plugged in all the way and it's getting all the power/connections it needs. If you're using the port in the front, make sure it's connected to the sound card properly. 
If it's a driver problem, try Google searching the name of the graphics card and 'drivers for ubuntu'.

### Post by diablo75 on 2010-03-03
Kinda strange for it to suddenly quit working like that.  It could just be muted.  Do you have the little speaker icon at the top to click on?  You might check that, and right-click on it as well to access the sound preferences panel.  There are a couple other little levers and stuff in there you can try adjusting.  It'll also tell you if PulseAudio is recognizing your sound card.
