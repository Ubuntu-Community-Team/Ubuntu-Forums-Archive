### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-01-05
You have 2 options as far as I know:


[LIST=1]
[*]Install Windows on the other hard drive then configure Grub2 to [dual boot]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WindowsDualBoot")
[*]Install VMWare or [VBox]("http://www.virtualbox.org/wiki/End-user_documentation"), then place the hard drive container/image on your external hard drive, then continue to install Windows as a virtual OS
[/LIST]
