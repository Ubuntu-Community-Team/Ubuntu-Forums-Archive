### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-05-31
Linux is not like MS Windows -- you don't go hunting around for drivers for motherboards; instead, when you install Ubuntu, it scans your hardware and loads the proper drivers automatically.

If you're not getting sound, then open a terminal and enter "lspci" -- to get a list of your hardware.  Post that info back here when you're done.
