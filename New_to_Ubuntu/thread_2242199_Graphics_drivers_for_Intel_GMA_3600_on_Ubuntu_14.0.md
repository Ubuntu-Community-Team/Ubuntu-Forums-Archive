### Post by Rob Sayer on 2014-08-31
You should start your own thread.  Hijacking is annoying.

The open source drivers work fine on the Intel GMA 3600, though of course you can't get 3D hardware acceleration, so 'fine' may be pushing it for some.

Are you sure that's the adapter you have?  You may need to set nomodeset when you boot, which isn't actually necessary with cedarview in 14.04.  See this:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1613132](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1613132)

And to see the model of your graphics card:

[http://askubuntu.com/questions/72766/how-do-i-find-out-the-model-of-my-graphics-card](http://askubuntu.com/questions/72766/how-do-i-find-out-the-model-of-my-graphics-card)
