### Post by wmdiem on 2009-01-02
often you can upload to a webserver with ftp. If you have the username and password, use an ftp client (like filezilla or gftp) to connect to [ftp://the.subdomain.edu](ftp://the.subdomain.edu)
If it lets you connect you should be able to move files freely between your computer and your root on the server.
