### Post by loell on 2008-11-12
the webcam needs to be identified.

anyway, by far, there are only two alternatives for yahoo messenger with webcam support, that is kopete (multi client) which is already mentioned and gyachi (yahoo client) which sole purpose is to try copy yahoo messenger features, unfortunately both have no yahoo voice call support. because of these you will need to use a third party service to call yahoo users, **gtalk2voip** service, you can use it through ekiga or gizmo phone ( with less configuration)
