### Post by Giant Speck on 2012-07-22
1.  The version of AWN that comes in the default Ubuntu repositories is AWN 0.4.x.  The themes listed on that page are for 0.3.x.  It says so at the top of the page:
> Warning: These themes are for 0.3.x only. For 0.4.x, go to the [Awn forum]("http://awn.planetblur.org/") (see the "Awn rewrite (0.3.9/0.4) themes" thread at the top, MediaWiki won't render the link properly).

2.  [This blog post]("http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2010/09/how-to-fully-replace-your-gnome-panels-with-awn") may help you with replacing gnome-panel with AWN.  I cannot say for sure whether it will work, as the blog post was written in 2010.
