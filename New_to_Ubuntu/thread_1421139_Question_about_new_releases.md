### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2010-03-03
In theory an upgrade merely changes your old version to the new version.  It downloads the new stuff and installs it on your old system.  No data is supposed to be destroyed or overwritten.  However, you best back up your data because of "Murphy's 1st Law". Even under the best of circumstances, bad things happen.  If your data cannot be replaced, you better have a back up just in case.
