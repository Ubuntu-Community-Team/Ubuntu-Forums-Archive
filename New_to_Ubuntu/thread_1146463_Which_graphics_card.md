### Post by rcayea on 2009-05-04
I can give you my personal experience...

I had an ATI Radeon 3650 installed. I could never use 3d proprietary driver and have good sound at the same time. I was always trying to figure out what the problem was and basically always used no 3D and never had any cool effects.
