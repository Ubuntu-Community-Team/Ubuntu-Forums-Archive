Then in office goto options --> Language settings --> writing aids and check the things you want ie check a while typing etc.

### Post by Icebear on 2008-11-12
I had a similar problem in a earlier release of ubuntu. After searching I found out because I had my language was set to Australian English I had no spell checker by default. You don't need the net because Open office has 2 default English dictionaries US and UK. goto 
open office>Tools>Options>Language settings>Language and goto 
default language for Document and choose English USA (or UK).
