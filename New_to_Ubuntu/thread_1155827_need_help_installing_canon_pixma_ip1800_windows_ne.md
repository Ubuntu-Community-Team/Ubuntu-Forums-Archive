### Post by windfarm on 2009-05-11
I'm a beginner but just installed a Canon printer also connected to a Windows PC. I followed the instructions in the Ubuntu documentation, which for your version are here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/8.10/printing/C/printing.html#network](https://help.ubuntu.com/8.10/printing/C/printing.html#network)
 
The two issues I had were: 1. Firewalls, make sure your firewalls on your two PCs will allow communication between them.
2. Drivers - my printer (MP540) did not have a driver in the Ubuntu distribution, but I was able to download a Linux driver for it from the Canon website. Once that was installed, the printer was listed when I went through the normal printer install process above and worked. 
 
I hope this helps.
