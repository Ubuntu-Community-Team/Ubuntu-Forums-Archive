You can do one of two things:
[LIST=1]
[*]Add a launcher for the file browser to the netbook interface. This will allow you to browse your network. To do this, click on the launcher for the trash can or any removable drives, and when the file manager opoens right click on its icon in the launcher and click "always show in launcher"
[*]OR switch to the default desktop interface that does have "Places" "System" etc at the top. To do this, log out and when you go to log back in select Gnome from the session menu at the bottom of the screen.
[/LIST]
