### Post by Existentialist on 2009-04-16
Sorry I can't give you specific instructions, but in order to get my headphone output working I had to right click the volume icon and select open volume control.  One of the tabs had a headphone box I had to check to turn on the output.  If it's not there, you may have to click preferences from within the volume control and select the headphone option.  All of my computers use Intel or Nvidia chipsets, so I'm not sure if it will work for you.  Just figured I would give you something to try since you hadn't had a response.
