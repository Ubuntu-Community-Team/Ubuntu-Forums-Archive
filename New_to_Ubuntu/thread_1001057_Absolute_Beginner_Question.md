### Post by Het Irv on 2008-12-03
[http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/index.php](http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/index.php)

and a few tips for the forums:
1. I wouldn't use your e-mail address as your user name, its okay to have it publicly available, but having it as a username is just asking for spam.

2. Use Descriptive Titles for your threads.  Alot of times people will skim the titles of threads for one they know the answers to.  With titles like "Absolute Beginner Question" and "Help!!" people may not think that the thread is worth their time.  A better title would be something like... "Need Tutorial for Absolute Beginner"

If you are looking for some other links, always check the sticky threads at the top of the page [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=801404](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=801404)
