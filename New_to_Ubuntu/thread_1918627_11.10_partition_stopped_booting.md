### Post by seawolf167 on 2012-02-01
You should check your hard drive health.

Download a Ubuntu .iso file from their website and either burn it to a cd or write it to a flash drive with [unetbootin]("http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/"), then boot from that media and download/run [smartmontools ]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Smartmontools")on your drive.
