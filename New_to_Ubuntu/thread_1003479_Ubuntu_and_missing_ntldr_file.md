### Post by louieb on 2008-12-06
A jumper is just wire inside a small piece of plastic. Its about 1/4 inch square by about 1/8 thick. I've seen the caps colored black, red, yellow. 
Its used to make an electrical connection between 2 post. I usually use a pair of tweezers or needle nose pliers to pull on one out (pull straight up) or set it back.
