### Post by Kellemora on 2009-01-04
Hi AC

Synaptic Package Manager is located under the word SYSTEM at the top left of your screen, then inside of that drop down box you select ADMINISTRATION, then just look down the list for Synaptic Package Manager.
After you select it, you will be prompted for your Administration Password.  Then the Listing will Load of all the available programs.  There are thousands up on thousands of them there so use your scroll bar to get down to the program you are looking for.

Click the checkbox in front of it, if there are associated files it will automatically highlight them for you.
It will also usually remove conflicting files as well.

Check APPLY in the pop up box and when you are done selecting programs to install, go up to the TOP of the Screen and Select APPLY up there to start the installs.
