### Post by civilmonkey on 2010-03-29
The Win XP recovery disk didn't help (fixmbr,fixboot,bootcfg), so in the end, I reinstalled Ubuntu 9.10 and allowed it to install grub on the root (hd0).  Now grub finds Ubuntu and Windows, and it's the new boot loader.  

I realize that this solution would have worked from the start, but as I am a 'hobby' ubuntu user, I was trying to keep that computer as unmodifed as possible.

Still, everything works, and the new install went just fine once I accecpted grub2 :)
