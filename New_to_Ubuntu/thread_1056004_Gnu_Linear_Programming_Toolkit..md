### Post by carml on 2009-01-31
First of all take a look on /usr/bin,the software should be there;then just go on System->Preferences->Main Menu,click on add a New entry (on the section you want e.g. under the entry Programming and not under Audio & Video),then click on the button Explore,this one is placed near command,and in th windows that pops-up select the path to the software.That's all,if you need more explanations ask us. ;)
