### Post by bobsmith2 on 2008-09-24
Mailbox Alert. I use it. It's excellent. Just be sure to go in and set the global preferences to 99999 seconds (which is plenty long). That will keep the notification up so you don't miss it.

### Post by bobsmith2 on 2008-09-24
Yeah, it's kind of big. There's no missing it, that's for sure. I reduced the problem this way: 

I dedicate a virtual desktop entirely to Thunderbird - just to the right of my main desktop. I leave Thunderbird maximized all the time on that desktop, and I have Mailbox Alert pop up in the bottom right. When I did that, I no longer saw the entire gray box thing - just one end of the gray box (maybe a third of it?) protrudes onto my main desktop down by the tray. It's much less obtrusive that way. 

I don't know if that will work for you, but thought I'd throw it out in case it helps.
