### Post by Spyderkid on 2010-12-04
Wine is a virtul layer that sits between you (ubuntu) and the program.

[COLOR="Red"]___________________________________[/COLOR] [COLOR="Red"]<----program[/COLOR]
[COLOR="Purple"]___________________________________[/COLOR] [COLOR="Purple"]<----wine windows layer[/COLOR]
[COLOR="Orange"]___________________________________[/COLOR] [COLOR="Orange"]<----Ubuntu[/COLOR]


You can download it from the ubuntu software centre.

to use wine on windows programs you just go to properties > Permissions and click the make executable box (if not already ticked)

then just right click "use with Wine" and away you go
