### Post by Temposs on 2009-12-16
I think the system gets confused whether it's on battery or AC power sometimes. It's annoying. Check to see if the "Dim when Idle" checkbox is checked in either the AC power settings or the Battery power settings. Uncheck them. Also uncheck the "Reduce backlight brightness". See if that helps.
