### Post by davmac on 2009-07-11
First off, I wouldn't worry too much about it being 8.04. In your shoes I'd try it out for a while first. Next, all of the upgrade options you mention are viable. You can upgrade straight from 8.04 to 9.04 or via 8.10 if you wish. Personally I prefer to do clean installs, but that is only a preference - nothing more. Do you have an external optical drive available? If not you may need to be a little more creative, but still achievable. 

Not sure about going direct from 8.04 to NBR, but looking at synaptic just now on my 9.04 machine, it looks like it is just available as an installable package.

Even though all of these are possible, what I personally would do would be to try 8.04 for a few days, download a copy of NBR image and create a bootable thumbdrive. Install NRB and then, if I didn't like that, download and install a vanilla 9.04.

Hope this helps,

Dave Mac
