### Post by snowpine on 2009-06-04
Ubuntu Netbook Remix is arguably one of the slowest Linux distros currently in existence. It takes Ubuntu (a slow distro to begin with) and adds another layer of slow (the netbook launcher) on top of it.

I personally am thrilled with Cruncheee on my Asus eee 900ha. It has all the functionality and repositories of Ubuntu, but is much faster. If Cruncheee is still too slow, there are lots of other options like Arch, Puppy, Debian, etc. Finding the right distro for your needs is a rite of passage. ;)
