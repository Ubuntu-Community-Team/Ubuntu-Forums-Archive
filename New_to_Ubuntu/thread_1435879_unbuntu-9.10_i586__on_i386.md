### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-03-22
Hi,
Ubuntu 9.10 is i386 ;) 
when you click on the download link from _[here]("http://www.ubuntu.com/GetUbuntu/download")_ you will see i386 in the filename.

Here is some more info about system requirements.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements)

Also, if you download the desktop version, it is a live cd that lets you try ubuntu without installing anything. Be aware that Live CD could run 10 to 20 times slower than if it were installed to the hard drive though. (because the cd drives is much slower than hard drives)
Hope this helps.
