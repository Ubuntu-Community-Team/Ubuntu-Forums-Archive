### Post by fourthofjuly on 2009-05-08
> **bu2971x said:**
> I just installed 9.04. Looks excellent and install was WAY better.
How do I move the App, Places, System etc bar to bottom..??
**for moving entire top taskbar**
right click on taskbar > Properties > Select Orientation as per requirement > Close

**only for moving menus to bottom taskbar**
right click on bottom panel
select add to panel
select Main Menu or Menu Bar from the list
Close

remove menu from the top panel
right click on ubuntu logo on the left hand side of Applications button
select Remove from Panel

hope that helps...
