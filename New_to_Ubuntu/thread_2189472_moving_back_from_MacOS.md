### Post by grier-devon on 2013-11-22
Hello There,
I do not know if the passwords are stored in plain text but I know on my laptop when I go from my home to my college it auto connects with no problems. Battery life has greatly improved with Ubuntu, still not quiet on par with Windows but no where near as bad as it used to be.  I have had no issues printing to PDF if I am understanding what you mean by this.

I will let you know though the Yoga uses a proprietary driver for it's wireless and from what I could see through a google search there are wireless problems.
Personally if you want to run Ubuntu flawless every time go with system76, they have touch screen too and from what I can see they get there machines from Sager which means they are a better build quality then what most OEM's are going to offer you.

This is one of there touch screen ultrabooks and it looks pretty nice...
