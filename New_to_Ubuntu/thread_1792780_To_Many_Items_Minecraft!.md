### Post by mcduck on 2011-06-28
Same process as with any other Minecraft mod on any OS.

From that mod's website:

> 
1. As usual, copy all .class extension files into minecraft.jar
2. Delete META-INF in minecraft.jar
3. That’s all you need to do.


You'll find the minecraft.jar located in ~/.minecraft/bin/
