### Post by tellapu on 2010-11-25
I was in contact with jf from the recoll team. He was extremely friendly and quick to add a script, so you can open recoll with a hotkey and can toggle (open/hide) with the same key. He will add to the new version the possibility to let it hide in the second desktop, so it won't use any space in the panel.

Here is the link:
[http://bitbucket.org/medoc/recoll/wiki/HotRecoll](http://bitbucket.org/medoc/recoll/wiki/HotRecoll)

He also mentioned that they are working on other improvements ...:popcorn:
