### Post by Mud.Knee.Havoc on 2009-01-26
Swap is something that should be used only to a minimal extent.  I've got a 2GB swap file, which is overkill (I've got a ton of things running, including about 10 torrents, and less than 5MB of swap is used).  If you're hitting swap so much that you want to increase it's size, I'd say that your system doesn't have enough RAM.

A quick and cheap RAM upgrade (a GB costs less than $20 where I live) would be a far better idea than an increase in swap space.
