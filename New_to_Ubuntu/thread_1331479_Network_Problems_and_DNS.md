### Post by Desert Sailor on 2009-11-19
Friends, I just wanted to post my findings to a series of problems I was having in 9.10.  After a clean install I had very very slow respone from Firefox and Thunderbird, Apt-Get wouldn't work at all, Update manager would time out, and several other network related issues plagued me.  

I spent hours trying to set alternative DNS servers, trouble shooting my eth0 connection, all to no avail. 

The solution for me was NOT my Ubuntu install at all, but rather my MODEM and the way it was working with IPv6 (or not working).  Once I was able to swap out the old model modem with a new one, everything started working properly.  

If you're having serious networking issues, I would suggest you might make sure your modem is a newer version, and will correctly process IPv6 (DNS) requests. 

Good luck...But after a month of frustration, I am once more back to the Happy Camper side of things with Ubuntu 9.10.
