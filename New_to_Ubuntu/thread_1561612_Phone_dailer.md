### Post by blazemore on 2010-08-26
If you're in the USA you can use the integrated feature in Gmail's chat.

However, SOMEBODY has to pay for it, and if you're outside the USA I'd recommend using Skype. In the UK it costs me 1.4p a minute to call the USA.

You top up your Skype balance £10 at a time. So one topup gets me over 11 hours of talk-time to the USA!

You can check the rates in your currency here ([http://www.skype.com/intl/en-us/prices/call-rates/cheap-calls-to-united-states](http://www.skype.com/intl/en-us/prices/call-rates/cheap-calls-to-united-states))

The bad news is NO, you can't just call phones in the USA reliably for free. Somebody has to pay for the call to be connected, and that somebody is you.
