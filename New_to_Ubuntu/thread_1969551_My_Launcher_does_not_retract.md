### Post by Aerivan on 2012-04-30
I suppose with retract you're referring to auto-hiding of the launcher? If that's the case, then there should be an option for auto-hiding the launcher in Settings->Appearance->Behavior.

### Post by daslinkard on 2012-04-30
What programs are you running when this is happening?  It seems that in the past a quick work around for this was to grab an icon from the launcher, drag it over above the desktop, but not actually releasing the icon onto the desktop and just bring it back to the launcher.  This should auto hide the launcher.  Another person was able to hit Num Lock on their keyboard when this issue occurred and it would also auto hide the Launcher.

Let me know what you find out with the above info.
