### Post by audiomick on 2012-01-17
Hi. Did you get this solved or not? Sorry I haven't been back for so long.

That all does sound like the computer is having some problems finding or reading your drive. 

You say the disk utility doesn't report any problems. By that, I take it you mean the SMART values that the disk utility reads off the drive? If not, look at them.

The other thing you could look at is if the connector for the drive is plugged in properly. I had problems with this on my desktop. About every three months I would have to re-plug the cable on the drive that the system is on in order to solve problems similar to what you described. I have a different drive for the system now, and have also changed the SATA cable that connects the drive that was having the problem. The install is still on there, and I still use it some of the time. The problem seems to be gone.
