### Post by treesurf on 2009-06-21
There is an applet for this but I can't remember what it's called.

From the terminal you would do:

```
metacity --replace
```

To turn compiz back on is:

```
compiz --replace
```

### Post by clive littlewood on 2009-06-21

Go to System > Preferences > Appearance and click on the last tab , visual effects.

Click on the None button.

Voila !!
