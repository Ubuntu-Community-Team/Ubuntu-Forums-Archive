### Post by bigfootnmd on 2009-02-06
Depending on how you connect to the Internet your IP address may be already hidden.  If you have cable/DSL or Fiber optic you may be connected through a router.
My Fiber Optic Modem is also a router.  The router has one IP address assigned to it.  All computers connecting to the router have a 192.168.1.whatever address.  The only address visible is the address assigned to router by my ISP.
