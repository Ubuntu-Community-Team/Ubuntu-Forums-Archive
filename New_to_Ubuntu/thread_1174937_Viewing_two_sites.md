### Post by Iusedtowearatie on 2009-05-31
You can resize a window by grabbing one of the corners (position - click and hold - move). Open the first application and resize it to your liking, then ope the other and do the same thing. You can have any number of windows visible this way.

### Post by Wheeeze on 2009-05-31
Daniell59, Iusedtowearatie nearly had the answer I think!  

Open your first site and then re-size the window to perhaps a quarter screen size.  Then, make sure that you open the second site using the Firefox symbol at the very top of your screen or File, New Window but not File, New Tab.  

Re-size and position each window to your taste.  I am using this technique at this very moment to watch U-Tube as I type!  Hope that is useful.
