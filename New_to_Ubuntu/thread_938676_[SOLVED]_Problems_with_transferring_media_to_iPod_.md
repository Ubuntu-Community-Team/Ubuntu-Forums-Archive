### Post by BandD on 2008-10-05
This is an older ipod, yes?  What capacity, 30 GB or 60/80GB?

It's hard to say what is happening exactly.  Perhaps your ipod isn't being recognized properly and thus the database hash is off, so the apple firmware can't recongize the songs properly.  

You could try installing [rockbox]("http://www.rockbox.org/download/") on your ipod as an alternative to the apple firmware.  They just released version 3.0!

Give us more info on the process that you are going through to try and add songs to your ipod.  Then maybe we'll have a better idea of how to help.
