### Post by bhold on 2010-02-28
Newer versions of Firefox store bookmarks in a file with .json extension. Firefox makes backup copies and keeps them in your home directory - for example, my most recent bookmark backup is here:
./.mozilla/firefox/a5f7ydnz.default/bookmarkbackups/bookmarks-2010-02-27.json

I have used these backup copies to recover or move bookmarks - it has been a while, I think I just ran Firefox imported bookmarks from the .json file.
