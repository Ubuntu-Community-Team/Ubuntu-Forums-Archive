### Post by csillva on 2013-02-24
I read the 7th edition (I think it was 7th) of this book[1], and thought it was very good. For  someone just starting, there is not only a ton of good information but also tips and suggestions for how to learn more if you are interested.

There are  likely many others available as well. In any case, start with something modern and current that covers a wide area of subjects, then start to focus in and learn more on those areas that especially interest you. 

[1] [http://www.amazon.com/Ubuntu-Unleashed-2013-Edition-Covering/dp/0672336243/ref=sr_1_2?ie=UTF8&qid=1361725377&sr=8-2&keywords=ubuntu](http://www.amazon.com/Ubuntu-Unleashed-2013-Edition-Covering/dp/0672336243/ref=sr_1_2?ie=UTF8&qid=1361725377&sr=8-2&keywords=ubuntu)
