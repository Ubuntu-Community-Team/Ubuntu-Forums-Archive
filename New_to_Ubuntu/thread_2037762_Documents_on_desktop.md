### Post by Gela on 2012-08-05
In Windows it was possible to click the minus icon and put the current document on the status bar at the bottom of the screen so that it remained on the desktop but out of sight, but clicking on it brought it back up again.  

Is it possible to do the same sort of thing in Ubuntu? I find if I click the minus sign in Ubuntu, the document vanishes and I can retrieve it by clicking on the Mozilla Firefox icon - but cannot access another document on the web without closing the existing one. Am I missing something here?

---

### Post by Paqman on 2012-08-05
If you minimise an app or document you can bring it back by clicking it's icon on the launcher. If you want to open a second instance of that app, middle click on the icon.
