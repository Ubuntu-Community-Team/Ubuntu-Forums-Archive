### Post by mcooke1 on 2010-02-25
Solved by using a USB ethernet adapter and being connected (wired) to the internet as I installed 9.10 (today) received updates and the latest kernel as the installation progressed. Then when prompted to remove the installation CD prior to booting I removed the ethernet adapter and the CD then connected the ethernet cable normally to the Laptop, booted and had wired and wireless available.
