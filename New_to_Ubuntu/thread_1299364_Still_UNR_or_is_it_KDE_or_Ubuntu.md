### Post by Paqman on 2009-10-23
The kernel is definitely the same, as is most of the system. KDE and Gnome are just desktop environments, so they're the apps you use on your desktop, plus the dependencies needed to run them. UNR is just an additional set of packages on top of Gnome.

Just tag the thread with whatever desktop you're using at the time.
