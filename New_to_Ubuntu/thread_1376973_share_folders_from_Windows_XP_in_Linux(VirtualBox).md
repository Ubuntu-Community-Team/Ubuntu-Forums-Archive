### Post by etnlIcarus on 2010-01-13
Just a little mandatory disclaimer: the shared folder won't appear in your virtual machine as a drive in My Computer but rather, as a place on the network. You'll need to go to, "Network Places">"Entire Network", and just keep clicking until you find it.

That tripped me up at first, as well.
