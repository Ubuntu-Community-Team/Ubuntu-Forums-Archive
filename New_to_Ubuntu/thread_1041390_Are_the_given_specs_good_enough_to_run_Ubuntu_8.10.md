### Post by Therion on 2009-01-16
Yep, that VIA integrated chipset is going to be a biatch to work with.  

The specs for your PC show an unused AGP 8x slot though, and therein could lie your salvation.

Decent AGP cards can be had on the cheap these days.  You could drop one in for as little as $30 or $40 if it's worth it to you.  
And if you can turn a screw-driver you can install an AGP video card: Tab "A" into Slot "B".  Tighten a screw and you're done.
