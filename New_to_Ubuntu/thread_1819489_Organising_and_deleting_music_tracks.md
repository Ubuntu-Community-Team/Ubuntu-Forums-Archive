### Post by dniMretsaM on 2011-08-06
> **cheekymonky2010 said:**
> Not sure about the first statement, however I was given the music by my brother and as there are over 2000 tracks just listed as tracks, I have no idea which album they belong to or which artist they are other than a few.  I've been using Banshee to play them which is great but can't delete the ones I don't want from there??? there are a few albums that are just taking up space!!!
Any help would be great???

Ok, you have a LOT of work a head of you. First off, delete the ones you don't want through Banshee. Right click on the song you want to delete and click on "Delete From Drive." This will remove the song from your hard drive. After you've deleted all the ones you want to get rid of, use [this](http://musicbrainz.org/doc/MusicBrainz_Picard) to mark all your songs by artist/title. After you've got that, make a folder for each artist in the folder you want to store the music in. After you have that, you can either just move all the songs be each artist into that folder, or you can make a folder for each album inside the artist folder and move the music around accordingly. It'll take you a while, but and organized hard drive is worth almost any amount of work.
Good luck and enjoy your music! :guitar:
