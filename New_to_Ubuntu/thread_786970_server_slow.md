### Post by wootah on 2008-05-08
Have you changed your repositories to download from a location nearest you? In Synaptic under the Settings | Repository menu option you will find an option to choose the closest server.

Here is a good demonstration: [http://www.ubuntu-unleashed.com/2007/09/choose-fastest-apt-repository-with.html]("http://www.ubuntu-unleashed.com/2007/09/choose-fastest-apt-repository-with.html")

I was having a similar problem as I was downloading from the US servers. After I changed to the Canadian servers, everything was good :)

Hope that helps!
