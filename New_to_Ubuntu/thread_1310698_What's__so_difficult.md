### Post by jimmy the saint on 2009-11-02
I keep going back and forth between Arch and Ubuntu.  Ubuntu frustrates me when certain packages get old or if I just want a lighter system.  Arch frusrates me when I just want to do something simple, but realize that I have to spend an hour or so fixing hal rules or figuring out why the brand new cups keeps crashing.  They each have their advantages and disadvantages.  Now I run both.  Ubuntu on the main box and arch on my laptop.  Arch isn't harder (sometimes it is a hell of a lot easier to figure out what's going on) but it is definitely a lot more of a time consumer.
