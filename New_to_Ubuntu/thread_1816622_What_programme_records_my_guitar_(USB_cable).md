### Post by soap1 on 2011-08-09
you can record your guitar on your computer using audacity.  For an electric guitar you just plug the guitar into the line in port (if you have a desktop it is usually blue beside were you plug in speakers which is green - hopefully that makes sense).  If you don't have a desktop most laptops have a line in port generally where a mic would be plugged in.  Once you have your guitar plugged into  the line in port just press the <R> button on your keyboard or <record> on Audacity.
