### Post by Didius Falco on 2009-05-11
Since you are connected to a router, you'll always see the 100/mbs number - that's the speed between your pc and your router.

Google for "internet speed test" and you'll find sites that test the actual speed of your connection.

If the number is low, you might want to have a close look at your ISP's TOS to see if they engage in "traffic shaping", or other ways to control the bandwidth used.

Regards,

Didius
