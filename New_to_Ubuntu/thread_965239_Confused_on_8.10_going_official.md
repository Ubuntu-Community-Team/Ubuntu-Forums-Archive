### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2008-10-31
You do not "need" to update.

8.04 is a LTS , ie Long term support, and is supported for 3 years, 5 years server side.

With that said, most people upgrade.

Upgrades *usually* go smooth, but back up your data first. Worst case scenario you may need to install 8.10 fresh (if there is a problem with the upgrade).
