### Post by tom56 on 2008-05-31
You might as well wait for a bit. A partial upgrade means that some of the updates won't be installed because their dependencies aren't available yet. It shouldn't do any damage to run a partial upgrade, but you may as well wait until all the packages are available which should be in less than 24 hours.
