### Post by Het Irv on 2008-05-23
Go to the add to panel Dialog and add 'notification area' to the panel.  That should get them back.

---

### Post by bwhite82 on 2008-05-23
Right click on panel then click "Add to panel" then scroll the list to "Notification Area" and add it.

---

### Post by gerben1 on 2008-05-23
