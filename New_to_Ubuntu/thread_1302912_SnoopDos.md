### Post by hellblazer on 2009-10-28
Have a look at the System Monitor, from the main menu top left, pick System > Administration > System Monitor
Select the Processes tab to see al the apps running.

Also, to view services running, pick System > Administration > Services
This displays all services currently running, with the option of turning them on or off
