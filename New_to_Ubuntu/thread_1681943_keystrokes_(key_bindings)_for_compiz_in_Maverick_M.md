### Post by Vined Adobo on 2011-02-05
After you finally get compiz working and are able to use the compiz-fusion configuration editor to get cube and 3D windows, you may wonder how to actually use everyting.  Good luck.  I finally stumbled across this link: [http://wiki.compiz.org/CommonKeyboardShortcuts](http://wiki.compiz.org/CommonKeyboardShortcuts) .

My cube came up as a cylinder instead of a cube.  You can change that by going to System>Preferences>appearance>visual effects>Custom>Settings>Profile:>Advanced>Effects>Deformation to change it.  You can choose cube, cylinder or sphere.  (Make sure you installed the compiz-fusion icon.)

To rotate the cube, press ctl+alt+left_click.  After the cube appears, I can release the ctl+alt, but I have to keep the click activated.  Use the mouse to rotate the cube in 3 dimensions.

Compiz is not user-friendly, nor is there any real documentation or instructions.  I hope this helps someone a little.

It took me half a day, but I'm kind of slow.  I hope you fare better!
