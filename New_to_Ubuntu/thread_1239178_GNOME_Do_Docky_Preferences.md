### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2009-08-16
You can add permanent shortcuts to the dock by dragging them onto the dock (from the applications menu or wherever).

If there are shortcuts that you don't want on the dock, just drag them off onto the desktop and they'll disappear with a *poof*.

Have fun.  I love docky.
