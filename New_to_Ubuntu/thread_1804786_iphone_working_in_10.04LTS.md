Hello there,
I do apologize if this have been discussed before, to my defense) i performed a search under "iphone with no results"

i have recently installed Lucid (10.04lts) in my laptop, then from the ubuntu software center downloaded anything to do with iphone (put iphone in the search).
when i connected my 3gs (running 4.3+) magic! it was detected and i was able to use it as a memory stick.

I performed a search and found that i could tether my Aspire 5570Z laptop with my iphone (look at [http://blog.jacobwg.com/104-iphone-usb-tethering-on-ubuntu-10-04-lucid/](http://blog.jacobwg.com/104-iphone-usb-tethering-on-ubuntu-10-04-lucid/)  )(thanks JACOB GILLESPIE!!!!) 

however i could not connect with my iphone because i had no personal hotspot capabilities. I called OPTUS AUSTRALIA and they informed me that i could get hotspot capabilities for free and that my plan was outdated. sooooo for no extra cost my account was updated to 2GB of download.

about an hour later i was able to tether with my iphone.

a couple of days later i got a blue tooth dongle (form deals extreme for 1.8 AU$ yea big expender) and now i am able to tether via blue tooth.  

fist you need to connect the bluetooth on the iphone with the one on your computer.
Switch the personal hotspot in your iphone on.
go to the top right section of Lucid toolbox (the wifi section) and click on the iphone (mine reads something like E6:01:88 xxxxxxxx.)
