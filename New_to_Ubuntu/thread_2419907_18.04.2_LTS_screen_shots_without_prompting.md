### Post by TheFu on 2019-05-27
You've lost me completely.  Is this about grabbing windows/total screens or a keyboard?
The title and words in the first post don't fit together, at least not in my mind.

For screen capture, you can use **import** or **scrot**.  Scrot has options for whether a specific rectangle, specific window or everything are saved.

Both can be tied to a specific key on the keyboard - I like to use ... PrintScreen.  and ALT-PrintScreen keys. How to do this is dependent on your DE and WM.
