### Post by taurus on 2009-01-22
Use gparted from the LiveCD or GParted LiveCD, [http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php](http://gparted.sourceforge.net/livecd.php).  You cannot partition a drive that you are currently using so even if you can install gparted on your machine, it won't let you resize your partition anyway.  Got to do it from a LiveCD.
