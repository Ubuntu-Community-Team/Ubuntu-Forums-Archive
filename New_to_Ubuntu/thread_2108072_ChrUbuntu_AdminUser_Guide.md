### Post by audiomick on 2013-01-23
Firstly, it is considered polite to wait a day or so before you bump a thread. Anyone who is willing to help you here is a volunteer. You have to allow a bit of time for the right person to show up.

Secondly, there are any amount of guides available. If you are looking for general Linux guides, a search engine will show something like this
[https://duckduckgo.com/?q=Linux+user+guide]("https://duckduckgo.com/?q=Linux+user+guide")

Specific Ubuntu guides can be found here
[https://help.ubuntu.com/]("https://help.ubuntu.com/")

The search box at the top right of that page can help you find specific topics in relation to Ubuntu.

Ubuntu is a Linux distribution, so general Linux knowledge applies largely to Ubuntu. Some Ubuntu specific stuff wont apply to other distributions like Fedora, for instance, and vice versa.
