### Post by fiddledd on 2008-09-10
The Freecom DVB-T USB stick worked perfectly for me on Gustsy. I've not tried it on Hardy though. For Hardy I think you need dvb-usb-wt220u-fc03.fw, for Gutsy it was dvb-usb-wt220u-fc02.fw, IIRC.

BTW, I used Kaffiene to watch, pause, and record live tv.
