### Post by Paqman on 2012-01-01
> **PerfectReign said:**
> I want to show the list of running applications along the bottom of my screen.

In Unity all your open apps and files will be shown in the launcher on the left. It'll auto-hide if any app is fullscreen, but just move your mouse to the left and it will pop back out. Multiple windows of the same app will show as a bunch of wee carets on the icon, by clicking on it you'll get a mini view of them all and can pick the one you want.
