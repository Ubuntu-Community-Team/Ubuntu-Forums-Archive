### Post by seamustry on 2008-08-25
How do I make it so that the volume for the headphones is controlled by the master volume so I don't have to open the volume control everytime I use my headphones?

---

### Post by kpkeerthi on 2008-08-25
Type **alsamixer ** in a terminal and make sure both Master and Headphone sliders are at 100%. Then go to System -> Preference -> Sound and then select both Master and Headphone. Now when you adjust the volume, your headphone volume will be kept in sync with the Master's.
