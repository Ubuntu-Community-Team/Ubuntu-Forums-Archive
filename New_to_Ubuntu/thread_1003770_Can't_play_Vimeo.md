### Post by Motorhead Kaze on 2010-12-25
Hi. I just thought I would add my 25cents 2 years after the last post because it is still marked "unsolved" and I came here looking for help for a similar issue. 

Until 30seconds ago, all Vimeo gave me was a still image of the video but no play button. YouTube was fine, and I could watch Vimeo vids via Chrome. I had the proper Flashplayer installed, good/bad/ugly installed, no *Gnash* was messing me up either like in the past. I tried *FlashVideoReplacer* and all sorts of ideas, but finally what worked was to disable *Flashblock*.

Flashblock usually gives me a gray video screen with an "F" button for when I choose to watch videos but for some reason that was not the case with Vimeo. So, that might be helpful to the next guy (like me) who bumps into this unsolved thread.
