### Post by grahammechanical on 2021-11-06
Open system settings>Language & Region. Click the cog icon to the right of Input sources. In the Language Support dialog Click Install/Remove Languages. Scroll down until you come to Hebrew. Tick the box and click apply. You should see an icon on the top panel that lets you change between English and Hebrew.

Regards
