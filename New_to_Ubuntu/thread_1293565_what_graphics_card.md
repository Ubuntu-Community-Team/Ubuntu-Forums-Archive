### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-10-17
> **jrandolph said:**
> so I guess i have another simple question - lots of cards i look at say DDR3 i know my memory is _not_ DDR3 does that matter?

Nope it doesn't matter in this way.

However, the better graphics cards use "GDDR"-based memory which is specifically designed to be graphics memory. If within your budget, choose a card that has (for instance) GDDR3 memory, over one that has just normal DDR3 memory.
