....picking up from where [emoguitarist06]("http://ubuntuforums.org/member.php?u=555068") left off....
After you have installed 'ccsm'(Advanced Desktop Effects Manager); launch it from the main menu: Prefrences>>CompizConfig Settings Manager
On the left hand side, click on 'Window Management' and then click on 'Place Windows'(make sure the checkbox is 'ticked')
Then, under the 'General' tab that you'll see adjust the 'Placement mode' according to your whims and fancies. Thats it!
