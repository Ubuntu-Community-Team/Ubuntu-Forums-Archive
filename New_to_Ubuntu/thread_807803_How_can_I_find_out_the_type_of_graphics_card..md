### Post by shifty_powers on 2008-05-26
well, thats an OLD machine ;)

that graphics looks like an integrated chipset, i.e. it is physically part of the motherboard.

what you can upgrade to is going to be very limited, if not impossible to do.

tbh, it's hard to know unless we know what slot your motherboard as, i.e. does it juts have a pci slot for graphics or does it have an agp 4x/8x slot?
