### Post by aloshbennett on 2008-09-14
For the vcd, you could open them in mplayer like this
> mplayer vcd://<track number>
Usually the track number is 2. If 2 doesnt work, try 1 to 5.

For the Sound problem, from edit-preferences of the Volume Control, select all tracks you think might be relevant and see.
