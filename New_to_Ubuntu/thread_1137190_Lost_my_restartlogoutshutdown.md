### Post by Didius Falco on 2009-04-25
Right-click the panel and choose "Add To Panel". Scroll down until you see the "User Switcher" and click the "Add" button.

That will put it on the panel. Then you can Right-click the icon for User Switcher and move it where you want it, then click the "Lock To Panel" box.

That should do it!!
