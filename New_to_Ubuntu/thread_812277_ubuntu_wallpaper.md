### Post by neurostu on 2008-05-29
Do you know what your screen's native resolution is?  You should download wallpapers that are the same or greater resolution as your screen.  For example my laptop resolution is 1280X800 so whenever I download wallpapers that are 1280X800 or greater.

Smaller wallpapers will get distorted when they are streched to fit your screen, especially if you have a widescreen display and strech a non widescreen image to fit.
