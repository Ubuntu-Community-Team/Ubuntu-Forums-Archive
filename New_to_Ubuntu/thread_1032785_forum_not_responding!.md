### Post by JoshuaRL on 2009-01-06
+1 Buying for Linux compatibility.

Usually you don't have to pay much more to find something that works, and you win in two ways doing so.  First, my time is worth so much more than even a $10 difference.  Second, you support manufacturers that are positive to Linux and FOSS, so you register your dollar votes according to your values.

A good price for an otherwise slightly cheap player.  But it works great with OGG so I'm happy.  Now if I could only get it to mount right.  :$
