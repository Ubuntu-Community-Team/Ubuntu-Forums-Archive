### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-01-26
Hi,
Is this the only cd that you have trouble playing in the Ubuntu 9.10 computer? If so, I'm thinking it might be the quality of the burn, or less likely the quality of the cd itself.

To improve the quality of the burn, try to burn at the slowest speed possible. You might even want to try a different burning application (k3b, Gnomebaker, xfburn, brasero, etc).

Every now and then I will buy a bad batch of cds that don't work well for me (or don't work at all), so if you have any other brands laying around, give that a try and see what happens.

hope this helps
