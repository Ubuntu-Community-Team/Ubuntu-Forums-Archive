### Post by Cheesehead on 2008-08-21
Lots of VOIP software in the repositories and online: Ekiga, Gizmo, Wengo, etc.  

To use your computer to make *analog telephone* calls, you need special hardware - a modem won't work.

The hardware is generally compatible with the Asterisk package, which is a full-featured PBX, with VOIP and analog phone trunks, VOIP/analog/softphone extensions, messaging, transfers, menus, etc. That might be what you want...or it might be overkill.

Asterisk is in Synaptic.
