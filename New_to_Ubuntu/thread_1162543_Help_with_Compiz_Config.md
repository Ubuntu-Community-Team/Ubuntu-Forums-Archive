### Post by Joeb454 on 2009-05-17
It is Super+Scroll Wheel.

### Post by Bölvaður on 2009-05-17
Enhanced Zoom Desktop

[mouse] Zoom In  <Super>Button4 (win+scrollup)
[mouse] Zoom Out  <Super>Button5 (win+scrolldown)

Also try to change it to keyboard..*edit* to Ctrl+At+Super+&#8593;UP&#8593;arrow&#8593; and down arrow &#8595; to zoom in and out. Not all keys will be identified to be used to zoom.*/edit* just as a test to see if it is working at all

Now you are able to zoom in and out no matter what what window has focus :)
