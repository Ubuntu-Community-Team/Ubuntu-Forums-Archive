Ignore the 999999:1 numbers...its just marketing and very susceptible to manipulation.

I'd go to a big IT shop and look at various screens since there is a difference in the clarity between brands.

I'm not familiar with Hanss-G. I'd stay away from Acer though...they do laptops well, but I'm not too convinced about screens.

btw I've had good luck with Samsung screens on the clarity front...but I suppose everyone has their favorite.
