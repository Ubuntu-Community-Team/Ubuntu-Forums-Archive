### Post by davidmohammed on 2010-12-26
press F7 - make sure you've got a dictionary installed e.g. English UK.  Then press Options.  Have a look to see if the option "check while you type" is checked.

---

### Post by gpdas on 2010-12-28
I switched on Dictionary English(UK) from Tools->Language->For Selection->More.
Now its OK.
