Most ISP's offer exactly what you're asking for with no features (PHP support, SQL, etc.). Basically just web space with HTML support. I'd check with your ISP.

I know there are others that give a limited amount of free space by placing ads on the site you make but I havent used or heard of one since I was about 17 yrs old, but to be fair I havent looked.

If ya dont need anyone to look at your page and ya just want to experiment id follow Cl0oud9? You can start an Apache server with all the features you're willing to install. There are countless tutorials about this scattered across the web. A google search should get you set up in an hour or so. This method also saves you a lot of time uploading. Its a lot quicker to move files from one folder to another on your PC than to upload with any connection to a server
