### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2010-04-14
Plug the computer into a wired connection using the ethernet port (just plug it directly into your router or modem).  

Restart the computer

Go to System > Administration > Hardware Drivers

There should be a driver listed there for your wireless - install it.  You should be good to go.
