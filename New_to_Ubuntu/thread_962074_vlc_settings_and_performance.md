### Post by Morpheun on 2008-10-28
VLC isn't really a great audio player, Totem usually works much better for single files and there are plenty of things for managing music libraries. Most of your problems stem from the nature of what VLC is, #1 is a feature, things are supposed to revert to normal between files, #2 is also a feature, VLC is designed to play one file at a time, not multiple files back to back so the reasoning is that if two instances are running then two windows should be open. If you tell me why you're using VLC for sound I can probably recommend a better alternative based on purpose
