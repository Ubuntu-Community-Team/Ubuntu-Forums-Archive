### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-04-04
I know you didn't ask this, but you're not going to be able to fix this through Ubuntu.

You need to ensure the following in MS Windows:
1) Correct motherboard drivers are installed
2) Correct add-on card drivers are installed
3) Settings are correct for both wired and wireless networking connections.

The usual problem we get here is that folks come here with pre-installed Windows laptops, looking for Linux drivers;  you have just the opposite problem.

You might have better luck taking this to a Vista forum -- but they generally do NOT provide drivers, and will most probably tell you to Google for them based on the make and model of your laptop.
