### Post by Zzl1xndd on 2009-06-23
If you wish to change your screen resolution the Option should be under System > Preferences > Screen Resolution. 

If you mean the resolution you require is not listed you might need to install Drivers. 

Check System > Admin > Hardware Drivers.

### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-06-24
The available open source drivers are installed by default.  

If those don't provide the resolution you want, you may have to install restricted drivers -- but those are only available for ATI and Nvidia cards/chipsets, and as for ATI, those are limited in 9.04 to the newer "HD" cards.  

If you have an Intel video chipset, their drivers in 9.04 have problems and won't be solved until new Intel drivers come out.
