### Post by Paqman on 2011-08-16
You can't as fair as I'm aware. That area is for status indicators, launchers go in the bar on the left or your desktop. If you want quick access to Chromium you could just bind it to a hotkey. Go to Keyboard Shortcuts to set that up. There are also keyboard based launchers like Gnome Do and Synapse that you can install to launch apps without using Unity's bar on the left.
