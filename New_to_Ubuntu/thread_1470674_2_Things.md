### Post by Cowboybebop79 on 2010-05-03
1. I assume you are talking about an integrated touchpad in a laptop.
if thats the case then if you go in to your system > preferences > mouse menu there is a tab in the dialogue for your touchpad with an option to disable while using the keyboard just to stop that annoying problem when you accidentally brush the touchpad causing the mouse to jump to somewhere else on the screen. Just deselect this option if you don't need it.

2. There are hundreds of games in Ubuntu just check the games section of the Ubuntu software centre and have a look at [http://www.playdeb.net/updates/Ubuntu/all]("http://www.playdeb.net/updates/Ubuntu/all") as some times they have more up to date versions of the games and some that Ubuntu doesn't list.

Hope this helps
