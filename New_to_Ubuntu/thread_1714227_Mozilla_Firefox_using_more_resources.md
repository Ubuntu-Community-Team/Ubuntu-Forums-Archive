### Post by ShakeyJake on 2011-03-25
Alermist, depending on your cpu, 10% may not be that bad. Obviously, the slower your cpu is the more of it firefox needs to use to do the same work, savvy?

There is also the issue of whether its the page or firefox itself. Flash adds, videos and jave applets use a fair few cpu cycles, try just going to the google homepage with no other tabs open, do you still have high resource use after a few minutes there?

1.3GB is a lot of ram whichever way you look at it. Is it definitely all going to firefox? You can see memory useage in System>Admin>System Monitor.
