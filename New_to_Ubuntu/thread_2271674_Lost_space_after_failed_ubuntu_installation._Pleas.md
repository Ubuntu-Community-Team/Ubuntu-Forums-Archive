### Post by steeldriver on 2015-03-31
Hello and welcome to the forums

I think you may be falling victim to the difference between GIGAbytes and GIBIbytes

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gibibyte](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gibibyte)

In this case, 476.84GiB is exactly 512GB (476.84 x 1.024 x 1.024 x1.024 = 512.03)
