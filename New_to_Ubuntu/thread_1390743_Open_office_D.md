### Post by grief -l on 2010-01-26
Hit F11 to get the Styles and Formatting box, at the bottom select All (or Heirarchical). Right click on Default and choose Modify. A popup will have all the tabs you need to modify that Template.

Warning - modifying "Default" will modyfy **all** templates as they are all based on it.

You can also make a new template based on it or any of the other templates (hit F1 and search for Templates and/or Styles
