### Post by Dkkline on 2010-05-19
I've had the same problem, google'd a little and found something about the fan should be stuffed with bad stuff, and therefor overheats.

I opened the laptop and vacuum cleaned the fan (notice:: don't let the fan rotate while you vacuum clean, hold a finger on it so it wont rotate!)

Ever since it has be silent as a ninja!
