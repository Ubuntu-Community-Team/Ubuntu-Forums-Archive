### Post by oldfred on 2016-01-18
I do not know virtual installs.

But grub2 installed to a MBR has core.img in the sectors just after the MBR. 
If drive is not partitioned, you do not have any sectors after MBR. 

Rarely seen a drive without partitions and in most cases, user error on not creating partitions and then most Linux tools do not work as they first check partition table.
