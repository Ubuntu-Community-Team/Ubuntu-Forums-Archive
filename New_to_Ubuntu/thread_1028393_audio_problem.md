### Post by donkyhotay on 2009-01-02
If it's just a matter of having now sound whatsoever check your volume. Both by the master volume in the upper-righthand corner panel (by default) or by installing alsamixergui from synaptic and verifying all your different sound channels independently. Anytime I have a lack of sound it's the first thing I check (and over half the time is the problem).
