### Post by brianfactors on 2009-07-07
I don't know very much about this issue, but I do know that after 8.04 ubuntu started using a newer version of XOrg (the program in charge of a lot of your hardware, notoriously your video card).

I don't know if NVIDIA caught up with them or not. I do know that the legacy driver (which was for really old cards, such as I had on my old Inspiron) was no longer supported.

I'd recommend you run ```
lspci
```so we can get a little more info on your hardware. Oh, ```
cat /etc/X11/xorg.conf
``` would also be nice.
