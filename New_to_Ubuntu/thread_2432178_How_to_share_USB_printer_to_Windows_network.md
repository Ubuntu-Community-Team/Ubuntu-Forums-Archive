### Post by Mark Phelps on 2019-11-28
@alexkrycek2

On most printers, they can usually be connected to a PC, via USB cable, or to a network -- wired or WiFi -- but NOT both.

You would have to check the specs with your printer manufacturer to see if yours is the exception .
