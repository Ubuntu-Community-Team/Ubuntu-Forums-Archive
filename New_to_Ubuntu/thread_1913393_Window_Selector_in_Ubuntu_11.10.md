### Post by bogan on 2012-01-22
Hi!, **daniell59**.

The easy way is to click on the Launcher for the Window you want to bring to the front. Just as you do to return from 'minimize'.
Though you may need to 'Grab and'Drag' any open Windows that prevent the Launchpad showing.

There will be one or more little arrow heads at the side of the Icon indicating it has active windows open.

If there are more than one Window open from the same Launcher, Thumbnails of them will be shown and you can select the one you want.

There are more sophisticated ways, and no doubt someone else will describe them; this way is OK for my needs.

Chao!, **bogan**.
