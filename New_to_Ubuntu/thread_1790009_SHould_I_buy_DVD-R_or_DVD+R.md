### Post by nomko on 2011-06-24
If you want less write/read errors, go for DVD+. And what does you DVD burner support? Both standards? Then it doesn't matter what you choose. 

DVD- does have a bit larger storage space then the DVD+: 
DVD-: 4.382 GiB <-> DVD+ 4.377 GiB.

The only problem you might run into is the support of your dvd-player. Most DVD players supports DVD- better then DVD+. Although i never had this issue. I'm using DVD+ and my DVD player never made an issue of this.

"Although fans of the DVD+R format hate to hear this, the DVD+R format was originally a rogue format, invented by companies that were unwilling to pay royalties to the DVD Forum in order to use and develop the DVD-R format and/or use the DVD logo.
