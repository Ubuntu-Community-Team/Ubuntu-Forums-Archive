### Post by silkstone on 2008-07-19
It's a bug with Flash 9.

The best solution is to install the Flash 10 beta, following the procedure near the end of this post under the heading 'Troubleshooting, Adobe Flash Player'.

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=766683)

Note that there are now two versions of the Flash 10 beta, and many people find that beta 1 works better than beta 2.

I had exactly the same problem, and installing Flash 10 beta 1 by this method cured it completely.
