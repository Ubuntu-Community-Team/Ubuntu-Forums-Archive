### Post by deadflowr on 2015-03-15
Open the dash and type "Printer"
Or open Ubuntu's system settings, and then open "Printers".
(System setting can be accessed in the dropdown cog icon in the top right corner of the top panel of Ubuntu.

Neither will have anything to do with ccsm.
As printers have nothing to do with compiz.
