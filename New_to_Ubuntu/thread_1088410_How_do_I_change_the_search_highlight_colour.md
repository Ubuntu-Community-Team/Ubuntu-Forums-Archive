### Post by Temposs on 2009-03-06
That should be controllable to some extent from the Appearance Utility under System->Preferences. From there, if you click Customize, and then click on the Colors tab, you should be able to change the colors of various things. I think perhaps you want to change the colors of "Selected Items"
