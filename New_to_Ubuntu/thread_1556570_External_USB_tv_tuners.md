Can't record with Tvheadend?? that is the whole idea of Tvheadend. XBMC it self can not record .. but it is a player .. not a recorder. Tvheadend can record any (FTA) dvb-s/t/c/s2 channel that you can receive and also provide live TV options to XBMC. There is a webgui too

[http://lonelycoder.com/hts/tvheadend_overview.html]("http://lonelycoder.com/hts/tvheadend_overview.html")

and for supported USB tuners .. have a look at this site for all supported USB DVB tuners under linux:

[http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/DVB-S_USB_Devices](http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/DVB-S_USB_Devices)
[http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/DVB-S2_USB_Devices](http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/DVB-S2_USB_Devices)
[http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/DVB-C_USB_Devices](http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/DVB-C_USB_Devices)
[http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/DVB-T_USB_Devices](http://linuxtv.org/wiki/index.php/DVB-T_USB_Devices)
