### Post by efflandt on 2014-01-06
In regular Ubuntu with Unity some settings are Appearance > **Launcher icon size** and Universal Access > **Text size**. Those should help make things readable on a small high resolution screen. Although, text size in 13.10 is only Large On/Off instead of the selection of text sizes in 12.04. I guess Ubuntu no longer has a setting for specific font point sizes or boldness for different things like they did in earlier versions. And I have not found a general DPI setting.
