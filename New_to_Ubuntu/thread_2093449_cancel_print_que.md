### Post by debodas on 2012-12-10
Open dash, type 'printers'. Open up the result, you should see a list of all the printers connected to your computer. Right click on the one you want to cancel the print queue in, and left click on 'view print queue'. From there you just select the print jobs you want to cancel, and hit the cancel button.
