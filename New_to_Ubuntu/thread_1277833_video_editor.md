### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-09-28
Kdenlive is the best compromise between features and ease-of-use currently.

Lots of people mention Pitivi and OpenShot, but they sadly only do the bare basics, although I'm sure OS will progress quickly. Lots of other people mention Cinelerra, and I'm sure it has a lot of features but it's difficult to use.

The only problem with Kdenlive is that it has a bit of a reputation for crashing. But it is quite well-featured so take a look.
