### Post by jimv on 2008-05-05
You can have Firefox skip the compatibility checking:

[http://articles.bluishcoder.co.nz/article/disable-firefox-extension-version-checking/](http://articles.bluishcoder.co.nz/article/disable-firefox-extension-version-checking/)

That way, you'll be able to enable them and Firefox should try to use them regardless of whether or not they were made for that version.  It seems to work ok with most extensions, but themes don't work.
