### Post by jockyburns on 2011-10-28
When you start Skype and have the Skype logo up in the right hand top of your computer, right click it and select options from the drop down menu. In the window that opens select "Video Devices" and make sure you select the correct device. My computer also has a TV card installed and when I first started Skype it was trying to use the TV card as a video source instead of my usb webcam.
