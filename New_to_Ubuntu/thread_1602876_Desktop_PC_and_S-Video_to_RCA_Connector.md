### Post by anewguy on 2010-10-22
I doubt there is a channel setting going out from the PC to worry about - it's just creating the svideo signals and the adapter is giving you composite video connection.

You need to look at the inputs you are connecting to on this TV - match how they are labeled to one of the selections you have.  If they aren't labeled, start at Ld (which I'm sure is really showing it's age as I'd bet this is for laser disk), then video2 and if need be video3.  If you have no way of getting to those, you will probably need the remote control that went with the TV.

A small warning as well:  I know people who have done this and it worked okay for them.  For me, using the svideo output of my ATi 9250 Pro and using an adapter to composite, and mine looked like crap.

Good luck!
