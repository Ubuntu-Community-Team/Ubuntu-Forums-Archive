### Post by 2hot6ft2 on 2008-12-07
First you should use System>Administration>Hardware Drivers to install the drivers for your card.

You neglected to say what card you have.

If you already have desktop effects enabled then the driver is probably already installed and working.

Disabling the desktop effects will make the games graphics better just as it does with watching movies and videos.
