### Post by LewRockwell on 2009-07-15
first, do a back-up if you have stuff you can't afford to lose forever

second, get a LiveCD of 9.04 Jaunty and see how it works with your system

third, if it works ok...and you have room...install 9.04 on a seperate partition for a trial period(you can "upgrade" but I do not recommend that)

IMHO, installing "fresh" is far better than "upgrading" which many have experienced "issues" with...

as another plus, many people do fresh installs as a matter of "maintenance" and "routine flushing and resetting" their whole system

while that isn't for everyone, they seem to experience a higher level of self-satisfaction and are MUCH more likely to NEVER lose data as good data management is a must for those folks
