### Post by Bucky Ball on 2014-06-13

"cog-wheel"? Xubuntu 14.04 doesn't have a cog-wheel by default in the top right corner. Perhaps you've inadvertently installed another settings manager (gnome?). Click on the menu button and on the bottom of the menu is an icon for the Xfce Settings Manager - thats the one you want. Or Alt-F2 and run "xfce4-settings-manager".
