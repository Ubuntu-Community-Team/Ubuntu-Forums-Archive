### Post by northern lights on 2009-04-17
Fair enough.

Personally, I usually give the final release another two to three weeks before upgrading my working machine, but I understand you've got reasons to want to move. Obviously, sometimes immediate upgrades are indeed the most sensible thing to do.

I've been running Jaunty on my laptop since Alpha 4 (Feb '09) and it's been running quite stable since minimum mid-March. The hardware is '05 standard, though - obviously less troublesome in the first place. Still, when you run early releases, the system is altered on a very frequent basis. Some glitches occur and vanish a few days later.

I guess, the conclusion'd be, that your systems bad behaviour is not too unlikely to solve itself on its own, if you give it some time. If, say a few days after the final release nothing has changed whatsoever, you might want to pursue this issue again.
