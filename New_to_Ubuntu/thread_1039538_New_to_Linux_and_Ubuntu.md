### Post by Michael.Godawski on 2009-01-14
hi and welcome,

you can try out Ubuntu without touching your data on your drives by using the Live CD version of Ubuntu. It will load solely into your RAM and will not affect your current system whatsoever. As long as you stay in the Live session and do not install Ubuntu to the hard drive.

What do you mean with standard Microsoft products? There is Open Office of course as an alternative for the office stuff. 

If you are a real hardcore gamer who always wants to play the newest games, try to dual-boot with windows first. Also check the website of wine ( which is a tool to emulate a windows environment, some games work great with this tool, some require additional tweaking): [http://www.winehq.org/](http://www.winehq.org/).
