### Post by PaulW2U on 2010-07-25
> **Vaughn Richie said:**
> [LEFT]Will there come a time that were linux operating system will superseed microsoft windows
[/LEFT]
Just think of all the large companies with 100s, 1000s or even 10,000s of PCs still running Windows XP and Office 2003. A lot of them will also be running their own Windows software which would have to be re-written and tested. Staff would have to be re-trained both in using their new OS/applications and also supporting it on a very large scale. Such companies are often reluctant to upgrade to the latest version of Windows and Office until they have to, let alone switch to something completely different.

At work I often have about a dozen applications open at any one time, a Linux terminal application could replace one or two but the others would have to be completely re-written. 

It's just to big a job for many large companies to ever switch.
