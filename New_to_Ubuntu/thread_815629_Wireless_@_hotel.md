An access point is what the wireless card connects to. You should get both for your personal use at home, not just for hotels, fast food places, ect.

On linux, you may have a few problems due to hardware incompatibility, but NDISWrapper should be a work around. After that, connecting to an unencrypted wireless network is as simple as a mouse click.

On windows, it's pretty much plug and play.
