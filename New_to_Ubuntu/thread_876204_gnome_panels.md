### Post by Bodsda on 2008-07-31
Just to make things a little clearer. 

Ubuntu by default has two gnome-panels which have different widgets/applets that do what you describe. 

To achiueve your one panel dream simply right click on the unwanted panel and press 'delete'. Then rightclick on the other panel, --> 'Add to panel' and choose the applets/widgets that you desire.

Hope this helps
