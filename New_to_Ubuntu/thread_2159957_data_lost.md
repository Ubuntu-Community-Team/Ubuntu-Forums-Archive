### Post by MidnightGrey on 2013-07-05
Ah there is a possibility of recovering **some **of your files. The first thing you have to do is stop using the computer **IMMEDIATELY**.
The data is still sitting there as the ubuntu install doesnt wipe the drive but the more you use your computer the more data will be overwritten.

Do you have a secondary computer you can use to troubleshoot? If possible physically take out the hard drive your lost data is on until you are ready to retrieve your files.
You'll need to look into data recovery software.
