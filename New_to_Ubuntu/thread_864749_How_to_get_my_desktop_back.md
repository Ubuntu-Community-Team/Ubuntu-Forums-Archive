### Post by Randybob on 2008-07-19
OK. Say I do ctrl-alt-F2 to kill a troublesome application. How do I cleanly get back to the desktop? I want to avoid resetting the X session or the computer itself if possible. I'm hoping for something less drastic than ctrl-alt-backspace.

---

### Post by steveneddy on 2008-07-19
ctrl+alt+backspace only restarts X.

ctrl+alt+F2 puts you into the tty2 terminal window.

Try hitting ctrl+alt+F7

That's the Screen with X.

ctrl+alt+F8 is system messages.
