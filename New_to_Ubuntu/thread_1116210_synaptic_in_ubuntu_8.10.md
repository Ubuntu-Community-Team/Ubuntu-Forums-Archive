### Post by CLomax on 2009-04-04
The Synaptic Packet Manager shows you the packages available to you according to your configuration of your software sources (<-- Don't worry about not understanding that right now).

Those with a green square are already installed, the white/clear squares indicate the package has not been installed.

So to answer your question, it does both.
