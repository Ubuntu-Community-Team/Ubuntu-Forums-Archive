### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2010-12-14
To enable the spell check go to "Show contact list"->Edit->Preferences-> Spell Checking.  To enable spell checking in any language you have to have downloaded that particular dictionary.  As to your second question about changing languages, I cannot help you.
