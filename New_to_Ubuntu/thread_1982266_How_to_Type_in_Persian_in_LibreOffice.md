### Post by duracella on 2012-05-18
Hi

If you look on the line on the bottom of writer window, it displays page number and etc, and language set for the specific document, i suppose it says English USA/UK or some other language, click on the language and select More... In the window that pops up you see it says 'Language' Click there and you should be able to find Persian language

You can also go to Tools > Options > Language Settings and specify language you want for new documents.

Hope this helps :)

edit: implying that mustafa and EB0V's explanations is the easiest & fastest way, 
note that their explanations changes the layout of keyboard, 
so then you will type in persian even in the browser until you change back to 
whatever language wanted :)

### Post by EB0V on 2012-05-18
You should go to 'System Settings' click on 'Keyboard Layout', click on the (+) button in the bottom left of the window and choose 'Persian' in the box that pops out.

A small keyboard icon should appear in the upper bar!
