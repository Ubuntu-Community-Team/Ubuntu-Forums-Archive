### Post by lyall on 2010-02-28
for CRT monitors use 5:4 or 4:3 ratios
for wide screen use 9:5 or 16:9 ratios

to see which one works the best for you try 
System > Preferences > Display
answer NO at this time

in the Display Preferences window

go to the Resolution: select one
you will see the radio by the resolution
you will have play around to see which one works the best for you

hope this helps you

good luck and have fun learning
