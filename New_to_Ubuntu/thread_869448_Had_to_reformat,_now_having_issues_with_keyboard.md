### Post by DapperMe17 on 2008-07-24
It's not uncommon to run into these issues, even when upgrading from on version, to another.

Are you reinstalling the same release of Kubuntu, or a new one?

Do you have a wireless keyboard? Every release of Kubuntu can act differently. If so, you just need to re-synch the keyboard with the transponder.

Your screen is off, because you probably do not, yet, have the correct drivers for your video card (which can later be downloaded from the repositories).

You could always try LinuxMint on a LiveCD...it's ubuntu, only fine-tuned a bit.
