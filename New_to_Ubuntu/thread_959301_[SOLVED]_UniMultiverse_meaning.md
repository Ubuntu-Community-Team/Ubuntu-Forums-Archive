### Post by HellNoire on 2008-10-26
Both can apply to your system.

Universe means it's got some non-open source supported licenses/ community support (us) and multiverse means some closed source licenses, if I remember right. 

It's just in case you want OSS (Open Source Software) only. Don't worry too much about it, myself, I use Multiverse so I have the widest amount to install.
