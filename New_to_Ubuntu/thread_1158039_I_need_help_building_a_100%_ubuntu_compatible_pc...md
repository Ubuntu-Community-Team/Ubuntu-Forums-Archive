Personally I have not heard of a motherboard or CPU that is incompatible with GNU/Linux. Most are either 32bit or 64bit and as far as I know there are now issues with the number of cores but then again all my machines have one lonely core. The motherboard and CPU will have to be compatible along with the RAM and the motherboard. In most cases there manufacture will recommend what other to use in these cases.
