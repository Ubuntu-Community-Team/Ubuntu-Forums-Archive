### Post by forger on 2008-08-19
Yeah, does it come after a reboot?

1) Try disable the desktop effects: System > Preferences > Appearance > Visual Effects > None
Log out and log in again

2) Do you have screenlets installed? Go to Applications > Accessories > Screenlets - click on each screenlet icon and be sure to uncheck "auto start on login" for all of them. Log out and log in again to see the changes
