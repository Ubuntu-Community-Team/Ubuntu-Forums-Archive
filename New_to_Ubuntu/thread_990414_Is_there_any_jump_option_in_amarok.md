### Post by Big_astur on 2008-11-23
It works here.

In the collection "tab" if i search it finds all the songs I'm searching for. For example if i search for "holly" it finds at least 5 songs with that filter (hollywood).

So maybe you didn't add that FAT32 folder to your collection and that's why it doesn't search on it. Open amarok, go to Preferences and select Configure Amarok. Then in the Collection tab mark the folder/folders you wanna be part of your collection. (Notice i have it in Spanish and might be not the right translation of the options and path)

PS: I have an external NTFS hard drive where i have the music, just to show you don't need an EXT3 partition for that.
