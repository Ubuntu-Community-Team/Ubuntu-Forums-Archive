### Post by blueridgedog on 2009-01-02
It is possible that when you change the mount method, something in the mount path was also changed, i.e. the path to the songs are now not what the songbird library thinks they are.  You may try and delete one song from your library, then import that one song again.  It is plays, then you should recreate your library.
