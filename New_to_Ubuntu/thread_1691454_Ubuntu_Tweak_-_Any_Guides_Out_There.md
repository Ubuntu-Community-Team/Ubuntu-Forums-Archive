### Post by PaulReaver on 2011-02-19
ubuntu tweak is really easy to use... just don't remove all the kernels and you should be fine...

to save some extra space you might want to also look at "bleachbit".... 
it can remove unused language packs, internet history and cache, flash plugins cache, the application installers cache....

one of the guys on here saved 3Gb using it a couple of days ago... although he did have quite a lot of apps installed.
