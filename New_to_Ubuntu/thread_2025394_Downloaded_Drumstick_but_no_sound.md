### Post by Sealbhach on 2012-07-14
Ok. Try this. Install the timidity package, then open up the drumstick virtual piano, choose Edit/Connections, click the output tab and tick Tmidity:0 or Timidity:1.

Works for me on Kubuntu 12.04.
