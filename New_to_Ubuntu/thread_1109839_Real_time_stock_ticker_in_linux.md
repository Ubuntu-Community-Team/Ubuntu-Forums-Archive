### Post by Penguin Guy on 2009-03-29
Well I really don't know much about stock but I have noticed that there is an applet you can add to one of your panels (the bars at the top and bottom of the screen) which might help:

Right-click on a panel.
Select 'Add to Panel...'.
Type 'Invest'.
Click 'Add'.

Hope it helps!
