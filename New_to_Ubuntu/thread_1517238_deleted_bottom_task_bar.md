### Post by Windows Nerd on 2010-06-24
Right click on the top bar and hit "New Panel". It will automatically make a blank panel on the bottom. Right click the new panel and hit "add to panel" and then add all the applets that were there previously. 

If you are using Lucid, then the bottom panel should have, by default:

-Show Desktop
-Window List
-Workspace switcher
-Trash

You may have to do move the applets around so that they are in thier default positions.

If you have added anything or changed anything, you may or may not have the same applets as the default installation.
