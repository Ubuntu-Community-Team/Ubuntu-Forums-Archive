### Post by Matt__ on 2011-02-01
Have you tried using a wired connection and going to to,

system / administration / hardware drivers

there should be a couple of options there for your broadcom, 
 STA and B43 drivers 
one of those should sort you out. :)


/edit on the off chance you have one of the problem broadcom cards there is a post here to help you,
