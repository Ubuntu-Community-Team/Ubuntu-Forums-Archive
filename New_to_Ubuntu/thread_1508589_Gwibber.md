### Post by Helkaluin on 2010-06-13
> **Gwibber** is an open source microblogging client for Linux.         It brings the most popular social networking web services to  your desktop and         gives you the ability to control how you communicate.

That's taken from [http://gwibber.com](http://gwibber.com)

So function-wise it means you can post status updates and replies to all the supported microblogging services you have without visiting separate websites. Some find that useful.
