### Post by sblunix on 2009-08-13
Well, first off, what are the specs on your Progear? How much space, RAM, etc. do you have? If you've got a considerable amount, then I'd just go ahead and hook the USB DVD Drive up to the webpad, insert your Ubuntu 9.04 LiveCD and install from there... If you don't have much resources, go ahead and download the UNR (Ubuntu Netbook Remix) 9.04 and put that on a 1GB Flash drive and insert that into the webpad... There are more detailed instructions on the Ubuntu Netbook Remix Documentation
