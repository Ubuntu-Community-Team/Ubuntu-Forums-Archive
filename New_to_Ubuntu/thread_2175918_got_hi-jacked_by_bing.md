### Post by tgalati4 on 2013-09-21
You have been *scroogled*.  Click the search bar on the upper right where the search icon is located.  You should see a small, dark grey down arrow.  Press that and go to "Manage Search Engines".  Click on Bing and hit "Remove".  Then click on google if it is still there.  If it is missing, then click "Add more search engines" then add [www.google.com](www.google.com).  Life is too short to use Bing.

### Post by squakie on 2013-09-22
Are you talking for just a search engine, or are you talking about just starting Firefox and the first page you see is Google?  If the latter, go to [www.google.com](www.google.com), then click on "Edit" and then on "Preferences".  Now click on the "General" tab.  Now look at the 2nd from the top section "Startup".  Be sure that "When Firefox starts" is set to "Show my homepage".  The next line down determines what that home page is.  Click on the "Use current page" button, click close, close Firefox and restart Firefox.  It should now open with Google's page.
