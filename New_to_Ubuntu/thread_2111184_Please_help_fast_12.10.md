### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-02-01
The login screen will show you three things.

1) Your user name
2) a panel to enter your password
3) Guest account
4) Remote login.

This is normal. Type your password in the panel and press enter. You will then be taken to the Ubuntu desktop as a standard user and your work will be saved where you save it.

Do not click Remote login or guest account unless you want to do those things.

Guest account is where we allow someone to user our computer but without giving them power to change anything on the operating system, including saving to the hard disk.

Remote login is where we log in to another computer on a network. We will need special information about the network and the other computer to do this, including a password.

I see Remote login on my login screen. My machine is not connected to a network. I ignore Remote login. One day I may get another computer and network the two machines together. Then I shall use the Remote login feature and be thankful that it is there.
