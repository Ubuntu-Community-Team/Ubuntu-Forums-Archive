### Post by damis648 on 2008-08-14
Gmail can only chat with Gmail users, not hotmail. For new buddies, they must first authorize you as a friend to talk to them. The contacts problet you describe is you are adding to the wrong section. You are trying to chat with them, when you must add them to the general contacts list, in the "Contacts" page. Btw, I would recommend using Evolution as a mail client rather than the Web GUI. It is just better that way.
