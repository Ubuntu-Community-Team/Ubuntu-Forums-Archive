### Post by winh8r on 2012-03-01
You can simply create a new playlist in rhythmbox:

Music >Playlist > New Playlist

and name it (it will be in the left hand side window)

then navigate to each of the 20 or so albums that itunes created and when you open an album just click on

Edit > select all > add to playlist > the name of your new playlist

Hope this helps you.
