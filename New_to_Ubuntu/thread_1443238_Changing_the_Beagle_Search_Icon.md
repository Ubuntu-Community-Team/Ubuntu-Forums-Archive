### Post by cboteros on 2010-06-03
I found out how to take down the Beagle search icon from the taskbar and still enjoy from the Beagle search within the "Deskbar" applet.

1) Go to: System -> Preferences -> Startup Applications
2) You might have 2 "Beagle" items in the list, then just keep the "Beagle Search Daemon" and disable the other one.

That's it :-)
