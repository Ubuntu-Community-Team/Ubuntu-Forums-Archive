### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-07-01
Hi and welcome to the forums Kozakie :)

I always recommend backing up all important data **before** such operations _and_ using GParted from the LiveCD/USB so that the partitions are not mounted.

(Working with partitions when they are mounted is a recipe for disaster!)

Also be aware that such an operation could take hours depending on the size of partitions and data to be moved.
