### Post by SRST Technologies on 2010-05-26
On some hardware especially those with the ICH6 sound almost-a-chipset, random freezes are pretty common for just about any version of Linux and especially Ubuntu Lucid.  If you've got an Inspiron 1545 you have ICH6 for sound.  I cannot help any further except to tell you that it is very common for your hardware to do this.  I have a PC with ICH6 on it and the only real method I had to fix it was to update as often as possible and hope.  I had the odd problem of the port on the sound card used to output sound changing ports every reboot.  Strange stuff, really.
