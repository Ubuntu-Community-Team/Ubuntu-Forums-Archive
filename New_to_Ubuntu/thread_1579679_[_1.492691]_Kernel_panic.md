### Post by migs73 on 2010-09-22
Once you have downloaded the image check the MD5 checksum to make sure the copy is good, and do it again when burnt (at the lowest possible speed!) to disc.

Instructions; [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)
