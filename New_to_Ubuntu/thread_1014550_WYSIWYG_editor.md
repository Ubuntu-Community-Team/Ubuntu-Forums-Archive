Truth be told you are looking for vi. XD  Just kidding!  Honestly, Dreamweaver is a very elegant and highly overpriced tool for creating websites.  You would do better to start looking at and learning a simple CMS (Content Management System) like Open Source Wordpress or Joomla.  The days of creating static pages are OVER and people are creating elegant applications that do much more than you could ever imagine.  Just check out the sheer volume of plugins for Wordpress for ex.  

If you really want a WYSIWYG X/HTML editor for Linux I would say you have a few to choose from but none are perfect.  Of all of them I would take a look at Amaya.  It may not be the fastest or the most elegant but the code is clean and is based on the standards.  [http://www.w3.org/Amaya/](http://www.w3.org/Amaya/)  Either way I would encourage you to start looking at the CMS's and learn abit about XHTML/PHP/MYSQL before wasting time with Dreamweaver.  There are plenty of inexpensive hosting accounts that you could set it all up for next to nothing a month.
