### Post by pytheas22 on 2008-06-03
Please try turning off desktop effects by going to the "Visual Effects" tab of the System>Preferences>Appearance utility.  Does the problem still occur with effects turned off?  This will help to narrow down its cause.

### Post by Het Irv on 2008-06-03
Look under the General Tab for "Focus and Raise Behavior"

Focus Level Prevention should be set on "Low"

---

### Post by joethesmith on 2008-06-03
that was it, it was set on very high. now it works as it should. thanks a lot :)
