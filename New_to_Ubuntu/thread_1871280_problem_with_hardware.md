### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-10-29
Hardware drivers, or the lack thereof, is the bane of all Linux distros.  The hardware vendors, for the most part, simply won't take the trouble to write Linux drivers, leaving it to the Linux community to do that.

Your best bet would be to go to the Hardware forum here and do a search based on the model of your Synth.  If you find nothing there, it's likely that you're NOT going to be able to use this with Linux distros.
