### Post by blandman3 on 2009-03-21
This may help, visit this site:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BootOptions](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BootOptions)

in there, you will find something that says vga=xxx

those xxx's are what resolution you need displayed, the lower the number, the bigger the screen, at least that is what happened with mine;)

at the boot menu, you may want just type vga=1400 for example, depending on the necessary screen resolution.

hope this helps!
