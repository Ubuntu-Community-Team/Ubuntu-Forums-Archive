### Post by Paqman on 2008-10-01
> **faron45 said:**

 Why does "suspend" not work on my pc when I have the button for it

Generally this is because the hardware manufacturer hasn't followed the standards for power management correctly. Lazy manufacturers tend to make hardware that isn't quite to spec, then kludge it so it works with Windows and leave it at that. It's a big problem for Linux, and one the kernel guys are constantly battling with.
