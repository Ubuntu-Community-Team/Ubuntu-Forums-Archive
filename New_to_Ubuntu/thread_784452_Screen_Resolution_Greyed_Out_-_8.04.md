### Post by Dazed_75 on 2008-05-06
in a terminal, try sudo dpkg-reconfigure xserver-xorg and after going through it, restart gdm (though the surest is still a reboot, you don't really need to do that).

The idea here is to let xorg try to detect your specific monitor rather than using the generic monitor description it is currently using.
