@lowsky: Off the record is a plugin you can add to pidgin. Once you enable that plugin, anybody else that has OTR or a compatible equivalent will be able to communicate with you securely. The first time you talk to them, you will be prompted to accept them as a verified contact and you'll see "private" in the lower right corner of any subsequent conversations with them.
