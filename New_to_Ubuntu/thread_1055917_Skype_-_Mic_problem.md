### Post by diablo75 on 2009-01-31
The simplest thing to do is go through the devices listed one after another and click the test call button till you find the entry that works.  It's a pain, but it takes less than 5 minutes to do.  I'd also uncheck "Let skype automatically adjust sound levels" or whatever that check box says.

You'll also want to open your volume level controls (double-click on the speaker in the top panel) and click Preferences.  Check everything off, then click OK.  Find your recording tab, and play with those levels and the buttons just below.
