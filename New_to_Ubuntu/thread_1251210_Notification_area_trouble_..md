I don't understand what you mean by "when I click on notify area." There is no Notification Area, because you've removed it. That's what your problem is, isn't it?

It's true that if you click on one of the *other* applets, you won't have the option to add a new applet. Use a blank part of the panel to add in the Notification Area. That's where the icons you no longer have (bluetooth and network) will be displayed.
