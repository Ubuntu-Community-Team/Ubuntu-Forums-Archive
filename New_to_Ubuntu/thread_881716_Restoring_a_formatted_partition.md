### Post by AdamWood on 2008-08-06
Depending on how important those files are to you, anything less than sending it to a data recovery specialist is a waste of time and you're likely to do more damage than good.

I've recovered huge amounts of windows files from disks that have had Ubuntu installed over them so it is possible but it's **VITAL** that you unplug that drive as quickly and safely as possible and not use it until an expert can look at it.
