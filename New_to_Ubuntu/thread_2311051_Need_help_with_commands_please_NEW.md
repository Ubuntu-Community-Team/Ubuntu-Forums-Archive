### Post by QIII on 2016-01-24
Govega --

We are not insensitive to your plight.   However, please understand that we cannot tell you (we will assume you have nothing but the most legitimate of intentions) from someone with nefarious intent who is trying to break into someone else's system. 

If we were to help too much, then we would leave instructions that someone with ill intent could follow in the future.  We do not want to become a resource for criminals and ne'er-do-wells.  That is the reason for our reticence.

The best route here is for you to contact the data center/co-location facility and see if they can help.  Please explore that avenue first.
