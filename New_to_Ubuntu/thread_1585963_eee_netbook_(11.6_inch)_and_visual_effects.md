### Post by JohnHeikkila on 2010-10-01
You can get some eye candy by installing
'compiz' and 'fusion-icon'. Run "fusion-icon" after installing it and select Compiz as the window manager, then install "ccsm" or "CompizConfig Settings Manager".

CCSM lets you open the compiz settings for a big amount of visual effects.
