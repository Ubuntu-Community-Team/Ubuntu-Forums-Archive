### Post by tylerannosaurus on 2010-12-09
I fixed all my album artwork by using gtkpod. When I opened it up I saw that it was processing the album artwork down in the left hand corner of the window, then I added a random track (drag and drop), then clicked “Save Changes” which wrote a new database to my phone. Once I went to my iPod in my phone, it updated the library and everything was hunky dory! I'd read on another site that if you add albums one at a time and wait for the phone to finish syncing that RB will keep the album art in the correct order.
