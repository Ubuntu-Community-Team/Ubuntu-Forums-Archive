### Post by Vaphell on 2012-04-21
my guess is that your laptop has lower display resolution and most websites are created with approx 1000px of width in mind. If you got full hd monitor (1920px horizontal) in your desktop setup then these pages will occupy approx half the width. CTRL+mousescroll is an easy way to regulate the level of zoom on the fly plus firefox should remember the zoom settings on a per-website basis.
