Welcome to the forums :)

Generally, the best way to learn Ubuntu is to test things out yourself :)
Do not be afraid to explore and break the system, it is easily reinstallable as long as you backup your system.

Generally, books are not useful, as Ubuntu changes with each release, and things that apply to Ubuntu in one release may not apply to Ubuntu in the next release. As a result, a nice online site is [http://ubuntuhandbook.org/](http://ubuntuhandbook.org/)
