### Post by cozmicharlie on 2009-03-22
Welcome to the world of Ubuntu/Linux.  The program most people use for accessing Windows shares is called Samba.  You can find a lot of information and tutorials here [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ComprehensiveSambaGuide](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/ComprehensiveSambaGuide).

Their could be lots of reasons your Ubuntu box is not accessing the windows shares. Could be a firewall issue or some other security feature preventing it.  Read through the tutorials and see if something helps.  Since you are new to Ubuntu it may all sound confusing so don't do too much tweaking (you can make it worse) if you are unsure of what you are doing.  Better to post back and ask for help.  Samba is fairly straight foward so you should not have to do too much to make it work.  Their are lots of people on the forums that can help with Samba.    

Enjoy
