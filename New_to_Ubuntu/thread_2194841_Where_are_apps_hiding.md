### Post by deadflowr on 2013-12-20
The dash?
Click on the dash icon, or press the windows-icon key on the keyboard.
If you simply click on the dash normally(left click), at the bottom area of the dash are several icons.
one is for the start(home section, and others are icons for the apps and various other things like folders and music.
The apps are listed alphabetically.

or use the dash search function and launch the apps by typing 3 or 4 letters and hitting enter.
