### Post by scorp123 on 2008-05-07
You may want to check this page:
[http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=HP](http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi?make=HP)

Most HP printers work tip top on Linux (as a matter of fact: no other printer brand has such good Linux support!) and all the drivers are already there, e.g. in the "cups" (Common UNIX printing system) package which already is installed per default on Ubuntu.

If something is not working I'd suggest you describe your problems in far more details so others can help you to get your printer working.

Exception to the above: If your printer is in the "Paperweight" category (see URL above) then unfortunately you're out of luck.
