### Post by mkvnmtr on 2009-02-18
I use Spanish as a second langage. I searched the package manager in the two sections for internationalization and locals. I installed all the language packs and support I could find for my first and second language. Then I put dictionary in the search box and installed everything that I thought would help me. Then in the control panel I went to keyboard and enabled a second keyboard layout. Then I clicked on the upper panel and chose to add to panel. I then looked for the keyboard indicator and added it to the panel and I was done. I would assume it would be about the same for you. Check for the base and the writing support and the fonts you need and get your keyboards like you want them.
