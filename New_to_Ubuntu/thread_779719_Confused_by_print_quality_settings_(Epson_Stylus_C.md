### Post by little_penguin on 2008-05-24
It will differ from driver to driver what exact options are available, but it should be possible to alter printing settings by going to System > Administration > Printing, then selecting your printer and choosing the Printer options tab, there you should be able to change the resolution under Print quality (e.g. economy, draft etc.).
