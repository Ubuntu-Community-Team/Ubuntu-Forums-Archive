### Post by VinnyJ1701 on 2009-10-28
This wont be much of a thread, I suppose.  I just wanted to thank you all very much.  About 2 or 3 weeks ago, I installed Jaunty J on what was a Win XP machine, and I'm impressed and happy.  This computer has not worked this well in a very long time, (it's a few years old).  I'm so happy about not needing to run Anti-virus or anti-spyware programs.  This just makes boot-up time so much faster, when I reboot, which is not often.

The information and help I've gotten from ubuntu forums has made all the difference in my becoming a hard core ubuntu convert.  I've become more comfortable with the terminal (Cut & Paste, mostly.) and will over time grow more accostomed to it.  In some ways it reminds me of my old days typing DOS commands, but Ubuntu is so much more powerful and smart.

I confess I do have a small Win XP Partition on my computer, for my printer (Lexmark X2250) and my Itunes.  I've looked far and wide, I can't find anything that works as well with my 1 Gen iTouch as iTunes does, and it will not work in wine.  My cheap Dynex Webcam does not work in Ubuntu either, but that is a very minor concern.

Anyway, thank you all...  I'll be here quite often, sometimes just to explore....   I'm so excited to upgrade to Karmic Kolola in a day or so.  I already have it on my netbook, and am very happy with it.

Peace,
Vince
