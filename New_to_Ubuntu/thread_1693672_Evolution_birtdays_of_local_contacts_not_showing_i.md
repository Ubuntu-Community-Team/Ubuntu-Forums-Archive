### Post by dagoss on 2011-03-02
I have no problem getting this to work.  Make sure that under Calendar view, you have "Birthdays and Anniversaries" checked.  Also make sure that you actually have birthday for contacts.  I was using GoogleSync between a Blackberry, Google, and Evolution, and I noticed that somewhere along the way, birthdays that were entered into my Blackberry didn't show up in evolution.
