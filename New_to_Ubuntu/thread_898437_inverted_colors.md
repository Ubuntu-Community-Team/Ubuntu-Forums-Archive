### Post by ace007 on 2008-08-23
You can disable that behavior by turning off the negative extension in Compiz (The special effects on your desktop).

To install compiz control panel
```

sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager

```

Then under System>>Preferences>Advanced Desktop Effects, un-check the box next to Negative. Or click on Negative and change what buttons control the toggle on/off.
