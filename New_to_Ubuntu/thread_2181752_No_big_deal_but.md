### Post by Impavidus on 2013-10-19
Just a wild guess: it could have something to do with the locale setting. If the Ubuntu machines are set to a locale where the comma is used as thousands separator and the Mint machine is set to a locale where it's not, it could cause your symptoms. Also, check the version numbers of the calcs. If they're different, maybe something was changed in the program.
