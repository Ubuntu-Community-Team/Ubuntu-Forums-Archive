Is there a way to get a zip program (to zip a folder ) for linux? So I don't have to send individual files?

---

### Post by Rocket2DMn on 2008-06-04
If you right click a folder in nautilus, you can click Create Archive and choose the .zip extension.  Create .tar files is also very common in linux, and they are often compressed to be .tar.gz (using gzip).

However, .zip files work best for sharing with people who use windows.

### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-06-04
sure, just right click on the folder/file you want and select "create archive"
