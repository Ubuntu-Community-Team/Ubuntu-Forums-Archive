### Post by jpmelos on 2010-07-22
Do you share your Internet with other people?

Since your torrents are downloading faster, seems like the Internet itself is OK. What might be the problem is your network (router/switch...), if you are sharing Internet with someone else. Make sure they are not blocking or port-forwarding somewhere else the ports 80 and 8080, for example (those are the default ports used by servers for websites).
