### Post by snowpine on 2011-12-21
Programs are only loaded into RAM if/when you use them. If you log into KDE then the Gnome components (network manager, power manager, etc) will not be loaded and will not affect your system performance. In other words you have my permission to install multiple desktop environments. :)
