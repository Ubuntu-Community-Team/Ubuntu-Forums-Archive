### Post by Ms_Angel_D on 2009-03-01
What your looking for is POP3 and/or IMAP access to your accounts. More than likely your ISP (Cox) provides this access to you for free, however MSN and Yahoo free accounts do not give you POP3/IMAP access. Also there is no way to forward email for free from either hotmail or Yahoo, so basically your locked in to getting your mail from them through the browser. I personally suggest getting an account somewhere like Gmail which provides free POP3/IMAP access, then just letting all your contacts know your email address has changed.
