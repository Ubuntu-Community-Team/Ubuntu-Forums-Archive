### Post by Lars Noodén on 2012-10-02
Any one is welcome to [hack](http://catb.org/jargon/html/H/hack.html) Ubuntu.  Pick a package and start with a PPA for that package:
[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+ppas](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+ppas)

Then make your improvements.

If the results are good enough they can be submitted for consideration for inclusion.
