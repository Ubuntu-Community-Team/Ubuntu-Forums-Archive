### Post by Noblacktie on 2009-01-19
Vista allows you burn in either Live Session or Mastered.

Live Session doesn't finalise the disc, allowing for additional writes, but compatibility is patchy with anything that isn't Windows.

Vista defaults to Live Session burning.

Have your friend click on the drop-down options when burning and choose Mastered for the session.
