### Post by coffeecat on 2016-04-04
> **B._Bishop said:**
> it keeps putting the number in the text 

My guess is you had the cursor in the text when you inserted the footer. 

Simply follow arochester's directions. 

Insert -> Footer.

Make sure the cursor is in the footer before you do the next bit.

Insert -> Fields -> Page number. If you want the page numbers centred, highlight any page number and Format -> Alignment -> Centred.

### Post by Bucky Ball on 2016-04-04
You can do all sorts of tricks like have the page numbers left and right on alternate pages. :)

You can also insert your own text instead of a default field. For instance, if you create a header, stick the pagenumber field in there, then create a footer and type in the name of the document, or author and name or whatever, that will appear on the footer of every page. Neat. (I find it very handy to stick the 'filename' field in the footer when I'm working on drafts so when I print them out to mark them up and proofread I know where to find them when I want to plug the changes in to the digital original copy later). 

I've done seven years of uni with Libreoffice and I love it. Very flexible, but takes a little getting your head around to start, like any new software. :)

Glad you're enjoying the OS, glad you didn't throw the laptop out the window and thanks for marking as solved.
