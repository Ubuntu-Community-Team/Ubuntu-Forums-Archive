### Post by KBD47 on 2014-03-28
Yes, I had the same problem recently with the ugly yellow tint on movies. What helped me was to  go to preferences on Gnome MPlayer and change the video output setting. You have several choices, the first one worked for me.
  This was on Linux Mint 13 which is pretty much the same as Ubuntu 12.04
  Hope that helps.
