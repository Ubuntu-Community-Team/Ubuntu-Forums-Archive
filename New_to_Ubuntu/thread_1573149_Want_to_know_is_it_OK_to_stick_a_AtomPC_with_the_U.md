### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-09-12
I don't think you will see any difference in battery life between UNE and standard gnome Ubuntu, as basically they are the same thing; the UNE just has several optimisations for the screen size.  The kernel and powersavimg options are the same as far as I'm aware, though wait and see what others say.

Certainly there is nothing wrong with a standard Ubuntu on a netbook, and along with others here, I have it on mine.
