### Post by newb85 on 2012-09-24
Found the answer at [http://help.libreoffice.org/Calc/Sheet#Formulas]("http://help.libreoffice.org/Calc/Sheet#Formulas").

Format>Page...>Sheet(tab)

Note: This setting affects how the sheet prints, but not how it displays on your screen.
