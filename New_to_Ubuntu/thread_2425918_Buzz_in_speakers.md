### Post by Adam_GUI on 2019-09-02
Could be your audio-in from the PC.
Can you connect another sound source to the monitor speakers?  
Like an MP3 player, phone, cassette deck, or radio?  Just to see if the buzz is still there with another source.

If the buzz persists with another source, it could be your monitor.  If it doesn't persist with another source, it could be the line-out on your tower.

If it's the line-out on your tower, you'll want to look for a passive or active box ground lift for the mini-cable.

If it's the monitor, you could always just use a cheap set of speakers.
