### Post by murray92 on 2009-06-15
Click the speaker icon on your panel at the top, click volume control and make sure all the speaker values other than master (eg. front) and PCM are set to full. Ubuntu sets them at half by default i think. My speakers were too quiet till i found this, but they're fine now
