### Post by charliehorse55 on 2009-05-22
I've never had a problem with a hard drive and Ubuntu. I've used roughly 20 drives over the year with my desktop, most of which were Western Digital or Hitachi drives. 

I've used seagates, and they work fine. Problems with these arise when thses drives are put into a RAID, as the sleeping drive will make the RAID think it has broken.

IMO, get a 1 TB Western Digital from newegg, here:
