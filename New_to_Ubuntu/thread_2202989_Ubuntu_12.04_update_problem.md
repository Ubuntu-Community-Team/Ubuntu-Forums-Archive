### Post by Impavidus on 2014-02-01
The upgrade to 12.10 should have been offered in October 2012, but maybe you changed some settings recently (to upgrade to any new release, instead of LTS only). If it took 1:30 hours you indeed did a release upgrade. There is no reason to upgrade from 12.04 to 12.10 now, even though 12.10 is still supported. It's successor, 13.04, isn't anymore. I recomment you stay on 12.04 until you can upgrade to 14.04 in a few months.

Those popups can have been anything. Maybe they notified you of some problem, maybe it was nothing important. Impossible to tell now.
