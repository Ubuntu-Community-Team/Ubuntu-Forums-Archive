### Post by dominiquec on 2008-08-27
Whatever you decide to buy, check first if they are supported by SANE drivers.  Verify this through [http://www.sane-project.org/sane-supported-devices.html](http://www.sane-project.org/sane-supported-devices.html).

I've had decent success with my scanner that's over six years old.  The default Xsane works great.  See [http://ubuntuliving.blogspot.com/2007/02/scanners.html](http://ubuntuliving.blogspot.com/2007/02/scanners.html) and [http://ubuntuliving.blogspot.com/2007/02/resurrecting-my-opticslim-1200-scanner.html](http://ubuntuliving.blogspot.com/2007/02/resurrecting-my-opticslim-1200-scanner.html) for my own experience.

As for CBZ and CBR files, these are really just JPG files that are zipped or RAR'ed.  Only the filename extensions are changed.
