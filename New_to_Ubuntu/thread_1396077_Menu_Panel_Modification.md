### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-02-01
Right click in an empty part of the panel and "Add to panel".  Now add the "Main menu".  You can now right click the "Menu bar" and remove it.

You may also like to know that you can add the Linux Mint menu if you prefer that type of single button, slab menu.  How you do that will depend on the version of ubuntu you run, but if you have 9.10, I think you need to try the mint menu from Mint 8.  Do a further search if you are interested, to make sure you get the right version.
