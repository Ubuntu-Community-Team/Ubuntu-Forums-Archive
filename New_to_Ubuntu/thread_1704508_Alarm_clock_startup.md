### Post by Canime on 2011-03-10
Firstly I created an alarm with the accessory alarm clock that comes pre-installed with Ubuntu. Now that I restart my computer every time I get the alarm clock at start-up (not the sound, just the application). There are no properties for the alarm clock (I've looked and can't find them) so I am wondering how to stop that program at startup. I'm a real beginner when it comes to Ubuntu, and I don't want this accessory to appear. Any help would be appreciated.

---

### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-03-10
To remove it from startup applications list go to System > Preferences > Startup Applications and remove it . If it is a Gnome panel application  right click the icon and remove from panel , you can add and remove from the panel as needed .
