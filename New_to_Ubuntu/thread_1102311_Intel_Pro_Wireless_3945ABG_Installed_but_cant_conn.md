### Post by fly-by-night on 2009-03-21
Hi,

Type 3945 in the search box top right and work through the threads there.  Sorry that I can't offer more, but there are many results to that search.  Hope it helps.

Also, I have the same card and it worked out of the box.  Try manually configuring your wireless settings.  Also if you manage to connect to router, but can't open any pages, configure your DNS setting under wireless settings (always type router address in address bar to test).

Right click the network icon in status bar, Edit Connections/Wireless.

Good luck!

PS:  After changing any settings right click network icon and untick Enable Networking, wait few seconds and re-Enable it.  That's also the way you jumpstart your wireless if you switched it off and back on again.

Edit:  Another something, once you did the PS bit, you might have to left click Network icon and either select you connection from the list or in my case - Connect to Hidden Wireless Network and change new to you connection's name and connect.
