### Post by &#1058;&#1086;&#1084;&#1080;&#1094;&#1072; on 2013-04-16
Or you don't have to disable all the Compiz effects. All you need to do is to change the Widgets Layer shortcut in the CCSM: Open CCSM > Widgets Layer > General > Toggle Widget Display > Change to anything else, or even disable it.

Source here: [http://www.linux-archive.org/ubuntu-user/462276-f9-darkens-screen.html](http://www.linux-archive.org/ubuntu-user/462276-f9-darkens-screen.html)
