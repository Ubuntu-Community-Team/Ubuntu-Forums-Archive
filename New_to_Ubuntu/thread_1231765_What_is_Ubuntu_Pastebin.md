### Post by drs305 on 2009-08-04
It is a place to post file contents. It is often used in the Ubuntu IRC channels to display large amounts of text that would be inconvenient to post in its entirety within the channel itself.

An IRC user would post the contents in pastebin and then supply the link to others in the channel so they could view the contents outside of the channel they are currently in.
