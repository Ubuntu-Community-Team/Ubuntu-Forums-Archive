### Post by judedawson on 2011-01-16
When you start your netbook, you are prompted to key in your password at login. Look at the bottom of the screen & you will see a selection for desktop environment. Choose the one that says 'Ubuntu Desktop Edition' or something like that. 

If your machine starts & goes straight to the desktop, you can logout & select the desktop environment. So, the next time you boot up your machine, it should load the desktop version. That's how it was with my netbook.

Jude
