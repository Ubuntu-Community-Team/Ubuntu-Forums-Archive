### Post by wenuswilson on 2008-05-30
If you go into the settings for compiz, click on the actual settings you want to change and click on the tab for bindings. here are where the button combinations activate the various settings. 

In the "group and tabbing" case, the bindings are under "grouping" and "tabbing".



If things aren't working (like they just were for me) Alt + F2 and type "compiz" and it'll refresh compiz.

Ok. To do group and tab, press Super + S to select windows and group them with Super + G or tab them with Super + T. To ungroup or untab press Super + U. To switch tabs press Super + Left/Right or hover over the titlebar.

Also, for transpanency, in CCSM, goto the cube plugin, click the transparnecy tab, and adjust the values.
