### Post by deadflowr on 2014-07-11
You can also right-click select applications from the dash icon, or press super+A from the keyboard.
Both of these will open the applications lens.
The default layout is Recently Used, then Installed, then other stuff, like apps you might also like to try.

In the Installed sections, click on the arrow and it will reveal all apps.
Much like Gnome, they are listed alphabetically.
