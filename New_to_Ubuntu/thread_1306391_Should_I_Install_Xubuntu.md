I ran Xubuntu on a 750Mhz PIII with 512Mb ram and it ran quite well. Most Linux distributions really benefit from a network connection. Is it not possible to use a wired connection. ### Post by cascade9 on 2009-10-30
I've got a Deskpro EN, and it does have USB ports. They will be rear only, right next to the PS2 (mouse and keyboard) ports. 

It will also have PCI slots. Even if they are all full, and thats unlikely, theres probably a PCI modem in one of them. Just flick the modem and get a PCI wireless card. 

BTW, I've never seen a deskpro EN that wasnt Win98 or later.
