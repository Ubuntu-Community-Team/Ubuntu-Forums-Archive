### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-07-15
To play movies, you need to load the codecs.

1. Navigate to [COLOR="Blue"]Applications>Add/Remove[/COLOR]

2. When the window opens, use the drop-down menu in the top right and select [COLOR="Blue"]All Available Applications[/COLOR].

3. In the left hand menu pane, click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Other[/COLOR] menu item.

4. Scroll down the list and check [COLOR="Blue"]Ubuntu Restricted Extras[/COLOR]

5. Click on [COLOR="Blue"]Apply Change[/COLOR], in the bottom right-hand corner.

6. Sit back and relax for a while as you may have a fair wait, depending upon you Internet connection speed.

Enjoy!
