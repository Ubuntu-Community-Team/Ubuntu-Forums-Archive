### Post by AgentZ86 on 2010-01-05
As far as I know YES

It should be no different then adding your MSN,Yahoo,ICQ,IRC,AOL accounts to Pidgin and you will see all your buddies your buddy lists.
But also you can select your option to show next to each name what account they are on so you can see who is online from which account type.

And you can filter those lists to show only online buddies or you can also add a buddy, but you need to remember to pulldown the account in which your adding a buddy to.

If you don't it may get added to the wrong account.

And thus you should also easily be able to add 2)MSN or 2)yahoo accounts as well as far as I know it's no diffent then any other account addition.

Just open your pidgin then select account then manage accounts
Select ADD and you will see all the different account types you can add.

You will need a user and login type for each account because I don't think pidgin allows the actual enrolment part so you have to go to the sites for that.

But anyhow I hope this helps
