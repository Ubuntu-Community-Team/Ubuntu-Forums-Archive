### Post by Temposs on 2010-09-13
If you go to System->Prefs->Appearance and click on the Fonts tab, then you can change the Fonts to be however you'd like.

If you're referring to some other kind of weirdness that cannot be fixed in that way, then please attach a screenshot showing an example of how the fonts look weird.
