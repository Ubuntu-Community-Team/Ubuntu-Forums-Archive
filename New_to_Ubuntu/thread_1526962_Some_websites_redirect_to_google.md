Hi and welcome to the forum,

whilst linux is darn good at protecting you, the browsers by their nature of you asking them to go onto places that are unknown can spring a leak that allows re-diverts. Whilst Firefox is pretty good by installation it can and should be hardened further.

I cover the basics of what should be on your Firefox over at [http://forum.phillw.net/viewtopic.php?f=6&t=15](http://forum.phillw.net/viewtopic.php?f=6&t=15) Addblock Plus is only needed if the others do not solve the problem (although others swear by it - so please feel free to use it as well).

Ensure that you have the latest Firefox installed. If you need any help on doing that, then just ask.
