Ubuntu 12.04 _will not stay_ in Kernel 3.2 . When Kernel 3.5 is ready for 12.04 , will pass through updates.

I assume in 12.04.**2** version . 

Read here : [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Testing/Kernel](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Testing/Kernel)

**MrBledi** you can install Kernel 3.5 with instructions of above site , and participate to Testing team. 
OR

you can install kernel 3.5 from here : [http://kernel.ubuntu.com/~kernel-ppa/mainline/v3.5-quantal/](http://kernel.ubuntu.com/~kernel-ppa/mainline/v3.5-quantal/) , downloading the appropriate packages , but keep in mind that above kernel (mainline) it has not the patches for Ubuntu 12.04
