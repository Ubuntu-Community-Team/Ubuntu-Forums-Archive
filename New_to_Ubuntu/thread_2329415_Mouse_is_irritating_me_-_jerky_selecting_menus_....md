is kinda jerky making it hard to select menu items.

### Post by cwmoser on 2016-07-01
OK I think I figured it out.
I had cleaned the bottom of the Mouse and when I wrote the above post I thought
well maybe I need to blow air into the hole where the optical sensor was.
It looked clean but I blew air in it anyway.
That seemed to fix my problem.  I've been tweaking with the settings for weeks
and it must have been a tiny bit of dust or a cat hair in there.

Thought I would post a solution anyway.
