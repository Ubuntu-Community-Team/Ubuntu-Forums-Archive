### Post by nerdy_kid on 2010-01-25
your cd drive could be dirty.
if you are on a laptop, then just eject the drive (dont look into the laser directly) dip a q-tip in acohol, and <i>gently</i> wash the laser.  then use the dry end to dry it, once again, very carefuly. Then try to boot one of your cds, and if it doesnt work, then burn a new one and try that one.  I had a very similar issue once, and this solved it for me.
