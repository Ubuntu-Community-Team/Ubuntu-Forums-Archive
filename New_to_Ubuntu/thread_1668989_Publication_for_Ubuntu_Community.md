### Post by ubuntu27 on 2011-01-17
You could upload your project to [Launchpad]("https://launchpad.net/") and track bugs, translation, etc.

1) You could make a [PPA]("https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+ppas"), make announcement somewhere and tell people to test our new application. People will blog and the word will spread! :D

See: [How to create a PPA]("https://help.launchpad.net/Packaging/PPA")

2) Ask [MOTU]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MOTU") to [include your application]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/UbuntuDevelopment/NewPackagesMOTO Team") for the next Ubuntu release

3) Join the [MOTO Team]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MOTU")

[How to Get Involved in UBuntu]("http://www.ubuntu.com/community/get-involved")


Thank you Vishrut Shah and Hiral Jhaveri for your contribution to the Ubuntu and Linux community.
We greatly appreciate it.
Good luck!
