### Post by freesitebuilder on 2010-11-08
If you right-click your desktop, and choose "change background", then the Themes tab, choose the theme you are using and there's a button at the bottom that lets you customise the theme. You may be able to do it that way.

Or in OO, try Tools > Options, and click Appearance to change the way it looks.
