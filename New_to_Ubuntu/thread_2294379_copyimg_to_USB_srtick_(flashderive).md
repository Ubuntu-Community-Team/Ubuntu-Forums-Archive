I have several files (documents) written in Libre Office that I would like to copy (backup) to a flash drive for safety.  Can I do this in Ubuntu? If so, how?

---

### Post by Dennis N on 2015-09-10
Plug in the flash drive. A window should open showing its contents. Navigate to the destination folder. Open another window to the folder containing the Libre Office files. Select the files to be copied, and drag them from one window to the other.
