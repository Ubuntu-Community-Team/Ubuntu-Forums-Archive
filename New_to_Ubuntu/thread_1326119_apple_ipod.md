### Post by stormvinyl on 2009-11-14
I can connect my ipod and then look into the ipod to copy the pictures to a directory on my computer.  Just plug the ipod in, Ubuntu should find it and mount it.  There will be a link on your desktop.  Double click on that and voila.  You should be in your ipod.  Now I dont store pics on my ipod and they very well could be in some strange file format that Apple is known to do.  So good luck with that.  A little research on the web and Im sure there is a program to convert them.  Good luck.
