### Post by snobby500 on 2008-06-06
U could change the look if u wanted, im not a fan of the brown that comes standard.

Go to gnome look and download some gtk2 themes, once uve downloaded them, just drag the whole file (usually not unzipped) into the window that opens when you go to system>preferences>appearance.

you could run into one or two problems like the theme not working because of not correct engine installed, so if you dont want to fiddle at all, then dont do it :)
Also you can customise ur theme from system>preferences>appearance if you choose a theme and simply change colours etc.

Enjoy :)
