### Post by pmsumner on 2008-08-20
Most bundled/brand name PCs these days have a rescue partition which you use in conjunction with a rescue CD/DVD to restore everything.

Check with your PC's manufacturer.  If you have wiped any rescue partitions completely, you may be snookered unless you can get hold of the install media for Vista by some other means.
