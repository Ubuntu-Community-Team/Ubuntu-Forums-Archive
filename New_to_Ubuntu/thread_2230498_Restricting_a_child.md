### Post by donkyhotay2 on 2014-06-19
I don't know chrubuntu but with standard ubuntu I would create an account just for them with no sudo privileges. That way the only thing they could delete would be their own personal files from the system and can't affect any of your stuff on your account or systemwide with root.
