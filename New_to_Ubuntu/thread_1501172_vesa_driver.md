### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-06-04
Ubuntu ships with the 'vesa' driver. You can edit your xorg.conf to change the Driver section to 'vesa'.

However, you get no acceleration at all. No 2D, no 3D. Vesa is the most generic driver you can get for your display.
