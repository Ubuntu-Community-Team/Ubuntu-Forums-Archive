[http://www.kashiif.com/firefox-extensions/edit-bookmark-plus/](http://www.kashiif.com/firefox-extensions/edit-bookmark-plus/)

You should also have "Bookmark this tab" as a function, and when "Bookmark this page" balks at something, that usually works.
