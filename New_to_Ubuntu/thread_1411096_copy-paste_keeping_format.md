### Post by SecretCode on 2010-02-19
The clipboard has this capability, but it's up to the program you copy from to place multiple formats on the clipboard (**Firefox does**).

Then you may have to choose the format in the program you're pasting into, usually with Edit > Paste Special... and picking "HTML format" or whatever. **OpenOffice supports this**.

So, **yes!** For those particular programs. No idea about emacs.
