### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-02-10
The password is not visible in the terminal for security reasons . Just complete the password and press enter. For DVDs I suggest movie player. Applications > Sound & Video > Movie Player.

To get your AV plug-ins install Ubuntu Restricted Extras from the software center.
