### Post by sofasurfer on 2009-01-15
Have you entered your encrytion key, or as it is usually called, your password? Do you have system>administration>network-settings? I had tis when I used a Linksys USB adapter under 8.04, but now, using a non-USB Linksys under 8.10 I do not have system>administration>network-settings. But I do have the icon on my taskbar at the bottom of the screen. It has many setting by either right clicking  or left clicking it.

I haven't switched between wireless and wired operation in a long time, but I think that all you need to do is disconnect the cable, open your taskbar icon by right or left click and check "enable wireless".
