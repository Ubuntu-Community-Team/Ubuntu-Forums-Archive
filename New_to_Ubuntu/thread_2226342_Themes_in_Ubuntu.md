### Post by Impavidus on 2014-05-27
The system-wide themes are in /usr/share/themes/theme_name/. You can also have themes in ~/.themes/theme_name/ (~ is your home directory, so these are themes stored in the hidden .themes directory in your home directory). If you want to experiment, copy one of the themes from /usr/share/themes to ~/.themes (first create that directory), rename it, change it and select it as your theme. I did it on xfce, which worked quite nicely, except that I'm not much of an graphical artist.
