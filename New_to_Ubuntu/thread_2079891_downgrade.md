### Post by snowpine on 2012-11-02
The recommended method would be:

1. Back up all files/documents/settings you wish to keep
2. Install 10.04 from CD/DVD/USB
3. Instruct the installer to overwrite your existing system
4. Panic in April when 10.04 reaches its "end of life."

Honestly if you are going to go through all that trouble, I'd recommend that you burn a fresh DVD of 12.04 and take it for a test drive first. A lot of users report problems with upgrading, which they do not have with a fresh install. (Or go with a distro known for its stability such as Debian.)
