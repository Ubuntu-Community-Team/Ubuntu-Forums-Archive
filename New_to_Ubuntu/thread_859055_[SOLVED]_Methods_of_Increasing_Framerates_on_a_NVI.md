### Post by Marshal0505 on 2008-07-14
Glxgears is not a benchmark, You can only use it to show that DRI (direct rendering) works, but its not a good test for DRI.

So if you notice that your card performs worse compared to some older card based on glxgears info ...? Ignore it , cause it's a flawed comparison.
