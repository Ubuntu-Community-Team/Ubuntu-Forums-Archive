### Post by Schendje on 2009-08-13
You can also use the "real" screenshot button on your keyboard for screenshots.

Btw, right-click GNOME's panel, choose "Add to Panel" and add "Main Menu" to the panel. Open that to reach your programs. Right-click on it and choose "Edit Menus" to sort things out.

Hope that helps. :)
