### Post by Irihapeti on 2009-09-06
It will depend on the brand name of your modem. If it's a conexant modem, then you can get drivers from [https://www.linuxant.com/drivers/hcf/downloads-installer.php](https://www.linuxant.com/drivers/hcf/downloads-installer.php)

There is a licence fee if you want to use the driver at full 56k speed, but you can test it for free to see if it is going to work.
