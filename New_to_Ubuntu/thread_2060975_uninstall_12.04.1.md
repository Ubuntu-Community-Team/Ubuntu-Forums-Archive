### Post by Cheesemill on 2012-09-21
Whatever OS you are going to install next should give you the option to use the entire drive, thus wiping Ubuntu in the process.

You could also just boot from a Live CD and use gparted to delete all of your partitions (this will obviously leave your machine without an OS).
