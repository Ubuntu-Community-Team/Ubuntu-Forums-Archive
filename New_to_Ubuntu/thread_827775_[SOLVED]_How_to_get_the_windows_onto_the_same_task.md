### Post by terrorsathan on 2008-06-13
There multiple ways to do that. The easiest is as follows:
Right click on the panel on the bottom. Select "Add to panel...". Now choose "Menu bar", click on add. You can move the menu bar by selecting "Move" when you do a right click on the menu bar. To remove any elements do right click on it and select "remove from panel". If you want to remove the panel on the top, right click on it and select "Delete this panel".

I hope it helps
