Using PriceWatch I came up with this for $41.85 u.s. shipped.
This assumes your new computer has a pci-express slot open (no not the same as regular pci) I have dealt with 3bTech before as well and have found them to be quite reliable, never had any duds from them so can not report on their rma quality.

[http://3btech.net/reboxmsinvge1.html](http://3btech.net/reboxmsinvge1.html)

Thats not a top end card, but for $41.85 its very respectable, I have even played WoW through wine on this card, I run a lot of desktop effects as well as cairo clock, and avant window navigator on this card with no trouble at all indeed with horsepower to spare. IF however you do decide to do some 3d gaming it is advised to turn your destop effects off while you play, while its a great card for the money, there are limits :)
