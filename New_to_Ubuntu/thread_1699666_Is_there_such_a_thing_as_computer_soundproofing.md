### Post by Dutch70 on 2011-03-04
Cleaning it is about all you can do AFAIK.
Also, make sure there are no loose fitting pieces.

 It also depends on how far you want to go. There are ways to soundproof your cabinet using insulation. Just google "computer soundproofing".

---

### Post by mikechant on 2011-03-04
Another way to go is to get a wireless (not infra-red) keyboard and mouse, and an RF transmitter/receiver (or a long cable, cheaper) for the screen, and put the system box 'somewhere else' where you can't hear it.
Obviously this isn't very convienient if you are frequently plugging in USB devices, inserting CDs/DVDs etc., but not everyone does this regularly.
