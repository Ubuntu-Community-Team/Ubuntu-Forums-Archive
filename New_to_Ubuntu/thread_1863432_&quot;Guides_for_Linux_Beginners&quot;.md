### Post by Alwimo on 2011-10-17
There are [http://omgubuntu.co.uk/](http://omgubuntu.co.uk/) and [http://www.webupd8.org/](http://www.webupd8.org/). They are two Ubuntu-focused blogs.
 
Podcasts are great sources as well. Going Linux and Linux For The Rest Of Us are two which should be good for beginners. Linux Outlaws, TuxRadar and the Linux Action Show are more about the news of the week/fortnight, which are also good to know in order to understand Linux better. There's also the Ubuntu UK Podcast, which is a mix of the two styles.
 
[http://goinglinux.com/](http://goinglinux.com/)
[http://podnutz.com/linuxfortherestofus](http://podnutz.com/linuxfortherestofus)
[http://sixgun.org/linuxoutlaws/podcast](http://sixgun.org/linuxoutlaws/podcast)
[http://tuxradar.com/](http://tuxradar.com/)
[http://jupiterbroadcasting.com](http://jupiterbroadcasting.com) (Linux Action Show is one of their shows, there are video and audio versions)
[http://podcast.ubuntu-uk.org/](http://podcast.ubuntu-uk.org/)
