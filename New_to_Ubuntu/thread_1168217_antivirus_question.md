### Post by jfloydb on 2009-05-24
You had better use Avast in your Windows. Just hope that it's up to date. If it does find a virus, move it to the Chest; deleting it out-right may delete an important file. If you move something to the Chest, do some research before deleting it completely. If you are infected by something really bad, you may have some work ahead of you to really get rid of it.

I've been told by many on this forum, that Linux is safe from infection, so you shouldn't have to worry about it.
