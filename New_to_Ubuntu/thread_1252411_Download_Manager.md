### Post by natravis on 2009-08-28
I used a combination of FlashGot and DownThemAll with Firefox to download from Rapidshare with my premium account when I had one. It worked pretty well. I'm unsure of what you could use with other browsers but Firefox is default and easy to use so .... do it! :P

Edit: Follow the 1-3 steps [here](http://www.raymond.cc/blog/archives/2007/03/13/use-download-manager-with-rapidshare-premium/) then tell FlashGot to use DownThemAll as its download manager. I used DTA because it was much more configurable than Firefox's built-in manager. Make sure to login to your Rapidshare account and tell it to keep you logged in, then you can select several links at once and download them all (I don't recommend that, limit it to 4-10 depending on your internet connection) at the same time.
