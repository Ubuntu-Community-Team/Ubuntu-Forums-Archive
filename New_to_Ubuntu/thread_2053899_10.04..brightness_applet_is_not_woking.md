### Post by cortman on 2012-09-06
You can also try

```
xgamma -gamma .75
```

to reduce the brightness by 25%.

If that doesn't work, you may need the proprietary Radeon drivers (as bad as they are). Info on that available [here]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RadeonDriver").
