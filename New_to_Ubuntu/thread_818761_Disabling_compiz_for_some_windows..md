### Post by tj111 on 2008-06-04
I'm not sure of one, since compiz replaces metacity for window drawing.  However there are ways to make it easier to switch back and forth.

The easiest way to switch between compiz and metacity is to install [compiz-switch](http://tombuntu.com/index.php/2008/01/07/toggle-desktop-effects-with-compiz-switch/).  Once its installed just drag it onto your panel and click on it to toggle compiz/metacity.  It's what I use before playing any full screen 3d games.

The easiest to setup would by [Fusion-Icon]("http://tombuntu.com/index.php/2008/03/25/toggle-compiz-with-fusion-icon-in-ubuntu-804/"), which is available in the repositories.  I guess I'm lazy, but right clicking and selecting a window manager is too much work for me :).
