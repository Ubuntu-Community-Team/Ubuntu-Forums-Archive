### Post by jemate18 on 2008-08-31
Alternatively, to see if your modem was detected.

1. Click System -> Administration -> Network

If you see a telephone icon or Point to Point Connection,
1. click the unlock button on the lower right and enter your password
2. click on that telephone icon and click properties
3. Enter your settings there.
