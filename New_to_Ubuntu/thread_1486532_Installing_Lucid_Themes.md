### Post by jrothwell97 on 2010-05-18
Just a warning: I believe Ambiance and Radiance (the 10.04 themes) take advantage of newer features in the theme engine, which mean they might not work just dumped as-is from Lucid into Jaunty.

There are two ways around this:

[list=1][*]Install a more recent version of the theme engine, usually from source or a PPA.
[*]Modify the theme to work on the old engine - this might involve trial and error.[/list]
