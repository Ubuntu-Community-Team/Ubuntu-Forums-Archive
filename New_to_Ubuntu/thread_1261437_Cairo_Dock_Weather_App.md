### Post by k33bz on 2009-09-11
> **nhasian said:**
> how did you do it?  I'd like to change it as well.

right click on the weather applet in your cario-dock. Click on configure this applet. Now go to your configuration tab. Once you have done that, just look for a line that says "Use International System Unit" and untick that box.
