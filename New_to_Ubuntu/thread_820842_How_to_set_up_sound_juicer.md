### Post by plucky on 2008-06-06
> Sound Juicer is on my system. Synaptics sez so, and I've located the file sound-juicer.glade in /usr/share/sound-juicer. I tried adding it with the applications>add/remove feature (after clicking on it in system>preferences>main menu.


You should find **Sound Juicer** in **Applications > Sound and Video > Audio CD Extractor**


> And when one "copies to library" where do the tracks end up? 

In Sound Juicer go to **Edit > Preferences** and you can specify which folder to extract the files to.


Good Luck
