### Post by qamelian on 2009-11-15
You won't find it in a menu because it's a panel applet. Right-click on a panel and click on 'Add to Panel...' in the popup menu that appears. Then scroll through the list of applets that will be displayed until you find the Computer Temp applet that you just installed.

### Post by Dvdre on 2009-11-15
Happens to me all the time. Eventually I do locate it.

In 9.04, you can right click on the Applications header and then choose Edit Menus.

From there you can see what is included in the menus and what isn't. If you are lucky you will find it there and then can click to include it in the menu.
