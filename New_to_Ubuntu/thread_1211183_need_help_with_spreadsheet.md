### Post by GCoffee on 2009-07-12
Go to:

Applications > Office > Openoffice.org Spreadsheet

Now, lets say you type in **2** to the cell **A1** and **3** into the cell **B1**

in cell c1 put: =A1+B1 
this adds the contents of cell a1 and b1

in cell d1 put: =A1*B1
this multiplies the contents of cell a1 with the contents of cell b1.

This is ofcourse, very basic.
