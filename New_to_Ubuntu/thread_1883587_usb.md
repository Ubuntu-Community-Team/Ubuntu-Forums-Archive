In Linux, a flash drive is treated like any other partition. :)

> **Karlof said:**
> 
all I need is a step guide Ie, press this move that etc

1) Click on "Album" menu.
2) Choose the third option "Open in File Manager".
3) Select all your photos.
4) Press 'ctrl+c'.
5) Navigate to your flash drive.
6) Press 'ctrl+v'.
7) :guitar:
