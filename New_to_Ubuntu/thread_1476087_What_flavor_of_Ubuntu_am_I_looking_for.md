The two previous Ubuntu versions are 9.10 and 9.04.  I found that both of those worked pretty well after I got past the problems relating to graphics drivers.  For the time being I am sticking with 9.10 because there are so many bugs being reported on 10.04.

Speaking of graphics, you'll need a graphics driver, a graphics card, an operating system, and an X windows version which are all compatible with each other.  This can be a problem with older graphics hardware because some manufacturers no longer support their older drivers which are needed for the older hardware.  You might wish to post the details of your graphics card(s) and your driver selection.  Then we could warn of any potential conflicts.
