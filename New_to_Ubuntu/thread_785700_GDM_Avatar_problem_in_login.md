### Post by spiderbatdad on 2008-05-07
You can get custom gdm-themes from gnome-look.org and drop the tar packages into System>>Administration>>Login Window>>Local.
Or you could edit the images of the appropriate /usr/share/gdm/themes/'whatever' folder. The latter can be trickier.
