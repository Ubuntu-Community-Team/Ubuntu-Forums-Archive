### Post by Didius Falco on 2009-05-20
Hi,

A better way to go about it might be to make a short list of the ones available in your area and then go check them against the list at the Ubuntu Hardware Compatibility List:

[http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/](http://www.ubuntuhcl.org/)

You could also do a few searches in the forums with the names of the USB wireless adaptors you are looking at. That's a good way to see if anyone is having problems with it, and a good way to find solutions. You might even find a HowTo to help you get it working right away, in case it doesn't work right out of the box.

I hope this helps...
