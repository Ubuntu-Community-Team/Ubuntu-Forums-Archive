### Post by CatKiller on 2019-10-25
The Gnome devs decided that being able to adjust the increments of the volume slider is something that users must never be able to do, so they hard-coded an arbitrary 6% into it and took away the ability to change it.

There are workarounds to bypass Gnome's control of the volume entirely, but my solution was to simply stop using Gnome.
