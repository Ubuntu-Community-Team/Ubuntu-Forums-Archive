### Post by Commisar Jimp on 2009-09-13
Welcome to the forums!

The problem is probably that you don't have the right drivers. What you want to do is go to System > Administration > Hardware and Drivers. This should give you a list of graphics drivers. Download and install the recommended one and, provided you have a 3D graphics card (you probably do) you'll be able to enable desktop effects. 

I'm not entirely clear on Compiz Fusion, but my understanding is that this will also activate it, but to customize it and get effects like the cube you should go to the synaptic package manager and download ccsm, the compizconfig settings manager.
