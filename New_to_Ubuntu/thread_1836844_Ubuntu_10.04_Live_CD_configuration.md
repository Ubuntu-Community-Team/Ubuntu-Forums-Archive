### Post by coffeecat on 2011-08-31
> **gsparx said:**
> I recently updated to 10.04, and before installing 10.04, I booted into the liveUSB just to check it out.  I loved the setup (with the bar on the left side and the dynamic toolbar at the top etc.)  Could someone help me configure my installation of 10.04 to look like the Live CD?

Let's get this straight. You recently updated your hard drive installation to 10.04, the Lucid Lynx. Correct? And you ran a live USB which gave you a bar on the left? That's the Unity desktop and the live USB must have been version 11.04, the Natty Narwhal, not 10.04. You cannot install Unity on 10.04/Lucid. You would need to upgrade your 10.04 to 11.04, and you would have to do this in two steps, 10.04 -> 10.10 and then 10.10 -> 11.04. Or do a fresh install of 11.04.

There is a version of Unity for 10.10/Maverick but it's not very good. If you want the Unity desktop, use 11.04 and look forward to the release of 11.10. :)
