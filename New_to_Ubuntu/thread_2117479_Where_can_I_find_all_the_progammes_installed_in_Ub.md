### Post by deadflowr on 2013-02-18
I know I'm a day late and a dollar short, but 'super + A' for the win.
Press the windows(super) button and the A button to open up applications menu in the dash.
super opens dash
super + f opens files and folder
super + a opens applications
super + m opens music
super + v opens video
Holding down the super key will show a whole bunch of shortcuts.
