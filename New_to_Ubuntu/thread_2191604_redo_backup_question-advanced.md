### Post by Kirboosy on 2013-12-03
You could go the route of making an[ Image Backup]("http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows7/restore-your-computer-from-a-system-image-backup"). 

There are also opensource tools out there that would let you clone the entire hard drive of your computer. (Assuming you have a place to store it)I personally use [Clonezilla]("http://clonezilla.org/downloads.php") for all my cloning but you can use whatever suits your fancy.

Whenever doing a complete reinstall I recommend installing Windows first and making sure its all setup properly and then I go back and install Linux. That works everytime for me.

Hope that helps!
