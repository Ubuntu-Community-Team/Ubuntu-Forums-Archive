### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-06-26
OK, 

1. Navigate to [COLOR="Blue"]Applications>Add/Remove[/COLOR]

2. When the window opens, look in the top right corner and open the drop down menu.

3. Select [COLOR="#0000ff"]All Available Applications[/COLOR]

4. In the left panel, click on [COLOR="#0000ff"]Other[/COLOR]

5. Scroll down the list in the centre panel, until you get to [COLOR="#0000ff"]Ubuntu Restricted Extras[/COLOR] and put a tick in the box.

6.In the bottom right hand corner click on the [COLOR="#0000ff"]Apply Changes[/COLOR] button.

7. This will load several packages and may take some time, depending upon your Internet connection speed.

8. Re-try playing your DVD.

9. There are other video players that are better, such as VLC and SMPlayer.

Regards,
Roger :cool:
