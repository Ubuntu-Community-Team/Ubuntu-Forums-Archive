### Post by namegame on 2009-03-17
I would ignore those recommending to Download 9.04 (Jaunty)

Jaunty is still in Alpha stages, things can and will probably break. If you have never used Linux/Ubuntu before, Alpha software can be difficult to manage and time consuming. Jaunty will be released April 23rd. It's not that long of a wait.

Anyway, I do not use Ubuntu, but as far as a "simple" answer... if you want stability I would go with 8.04, as it is a LTS(Long Term Service) release. You are guaranted security updates until 2011, 8.10 will stop recieving updates in 2010. 

If you want some "newer" and "shinier" I would go with 8.10. 

It's as simple as that.
