### Post by canthus13 on 2010-01-06
The installer will set up dual boot for you, and there really is no reason you shouldn't (Aside from the aforementioned irritation caused by XP).  Just remember to defragment XP *BEFORE* you install.  The installer will give you the option to keep XP, and let you adjust the size of the XP partition.  After install, XP will initially freak out and do a scandisk, but after another reboot it should be fine.  (At least, it always has been on my own installs...)
