### Post by Darkade on 2008-07-25
No but you can put them there just open a root nautilus there
```
gksudo nautilus /usr/share/icons
```
you can also make a folder in $HOME and have your Icons there.
You can change an Icon by right clicking in it and then clicking in the icon, after that just look for your icon somewhere.
If you installed an icon theme then it is probably in ~/.icons you can look for them there
