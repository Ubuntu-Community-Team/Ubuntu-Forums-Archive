### Post by halitech on 2008-05-25
if the drive is clicking it is probably on its way out. Did the drive get dropped any time recently?

you would need to know who made the drive that is being used in the enclosure in order to find the best tool to check it.

I know this will be a long shot but I have had success with putting drives in the freezer (make sure it is in an airtight sealed bag first) for a few hours and then hooking up immediately to a system to retrieve data. YMMV with this trick.
