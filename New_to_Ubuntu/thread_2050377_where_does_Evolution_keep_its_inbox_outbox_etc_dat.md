### Post by gcvisel on 2012-08-30
Don't give up, Old Jimma.  The Evolution files are normally saved in your home folder in folder ".evolution" which, like other folders whose names start with a period, is a hidden folder, cuz it's not normally one people want to mess with.  To show hidden folders, just hit CTRL-H (the CTRL and H keys at the same time.)

   Also, Google is your friend for migrating from T-Bird to Evolution.  Search on "migrating thunderbird to evolution" and you'll find lots of web pages on how to go there.  Here's one:
[https://ruminationsonthedigitalrealm.wordpress.com/2008/04/10/evolution-moving-away-from-thunderbird/](https://ruminationsonthedigitalrealm.wordpress.com/2008/04/10/evolution-moving-away-from-thunderbird/)
