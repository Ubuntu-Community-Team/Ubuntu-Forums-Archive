### Post by gettinoriginal on 2009-03-02
Firstly, you must have Compiz Configuration Settings Manager (CCSM) installed, it can be found in Synaptic. Then System, Preferences, CCSM, Utility, Wallpaper.  But after you enter them, you will have to go to do Alt F2, enter ```
gconf-editor
```, applications, nautilus, preferences, and uncheck "show desktop".  And welcome to Ubuntu :P
