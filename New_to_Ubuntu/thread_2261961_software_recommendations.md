### Post by oldrocker99 on 2015-01-23
> **Derrick_Katula said:**
> [h=3]i suggest cuckoo, nitroshare & Myagenda[/h]

OK, nitroshare and Myagenda are Linux programs, from Launchpad and a PPA respectively. Cuckoo is a malware detector for Windows, and I would not trust something you can just download from the Web. Besides, there is a program in the repositories called clamav, which is designed for servers that handle Windows files, and users who want ro run something with wine. Anything from the repos is trustable. Launchpad programs are pretty safe, and PPAs are untrusted by definition.
