### Post by markbuntu on 2008-10-02
You can buy a panel for your tower that has usb ports. Some have mic and headphone connections and card readers also. They come with the cables to plug into the mobo that are labelled and keyed so it is very difficult to plug them in the wrong place. You don't need to use all the stuff in them, just plugin the stuff you want to use. 

They are cheap too, $15-$40 depending on what its got on it. I have a connectland one that has 2 usb, 1 firewire, mic, headphones, spdif (both types), a multicard reader, a sata connector and a power connector for the sata. I think it cost me $30. You can also find ones that have a 4 or 6 of usb connectors, an internal hub.
