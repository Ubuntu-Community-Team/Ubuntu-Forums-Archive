### Post by howefield on 2008-10-12
The emerald theme would have the buttons on the left automatically, so if they are on the right Id assume the metacity theme is being used.

Go to terminal and type gconf-editor and navigate to apps > metacity > general and double click button_layout.

Now reverse the layout to close,minimize,maximise:menu
