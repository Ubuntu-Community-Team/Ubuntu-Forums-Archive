### Post by avtolle on 2009-02-11
Is network orange a wireless network (either your own or one a neighbor might have)? If so, NM detected it and was trying to connect. 

Click on the "two computers" (icon for Network Manager) and take a look to see what other networks are shown, if network orange isn't yours. I think you might find it is running with security, thus the message. BTW, sounds like your wireless card is detected and operating!

### Post by avtolle on 2009-02-11
On your mentor question, I'd suggest looking around to see if there is a local Linux Users Group, and if so, go to their next meeting. Introduce yourself to those in attendance to make the acquaintance of someone with more experience, and see if s/he would "mentor" you with your Ubuntu experience. I'd think there would be someone, likely somewhat younger, who would be willing to assist you. Good luck.
