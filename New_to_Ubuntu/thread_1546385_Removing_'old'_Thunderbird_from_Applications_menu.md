After upgrading Thunderbird to 3.1.1 I ended up with a "Thunderbird" entry at the top of Applications > Internet

and a Mozilla Thunderbird Mail/News entry at the bottom of the list.

I see how I can edit Applications, but not how to change the Internet sub-menu!

---

### Post by Paddy Landau on 2010-08-05
[LIST=1]
[*]Edit your menu: System > Preferences > Main Menu
[*]You'll get a window with two columns. In the left column, click on Internet (under Applications).
[*]In the right column, you'll see all your Internet entries. You can click on the tick-box to enable or disable it. Or, click on the entry and select Properties (to see what's in it) or Delete (to delete it).
[/LIST]
