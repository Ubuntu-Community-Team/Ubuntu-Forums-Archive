Before you start tinkering with partitions, be sure you understand how you installed Ubuntu. Your comment about choosing the amount of space to allot for Ubuntu leads me to think that you installed using Wubi (inside Windows). If so, be sure to follow instructions for Wubi and not for a regular side-by-side install.
