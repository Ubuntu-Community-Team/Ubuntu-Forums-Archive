### Post by Grenage on 2010-01-20
If you're running a live DVD, they will be stored in the computer's memory (RAM).

### Post by audiomick on 2010-01-20
you mean your are running from the Live CD which is  burned onto a DVD, right?
This is how I understand it, but it may not be absolutely accurate.

When you choose the "try without changing", it loads the system into RAM. If you then load updates, they will also be in RAM. Next time you boot, the will not be there. The live CD doesn't write anything to disk anywhere unless you specifically tell it too.
