### Post by lavinog on 2009-02-15
For downloads:
In firefox click on Tools then Downloads. You should get a list of downloads you have downloaded. If you right click on them and select 'open containing folder' it should open the folder that the file is located.
Normally the downloads are saved where you last manually saved a file. Otherwise it should be your desktop.

For printer:
What is your default printer. I believe the default is PDF printer.
What kind of printer do you have? There are some printers that are not linux friendly. I have come across some canon printers in the past that didn't work so well, but that could have changed. Hp printers have usually been pretty good.
