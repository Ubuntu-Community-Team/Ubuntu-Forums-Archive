### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-07-12
You could just use the deb archive noted in your link, rather than the source archive, but perhaps there's a good reason for you using source.  Add the first of the two lines in the box to your /etc/apt/sources.list file and then add the gpg key by following the instructions in the box that opens when you click on the "What's this?" link beside it.

Then you should be good to go with the .deb binaries, not needing the source tarballs.
