### Post by bmac on 2008-04-30
Right click on the file
Select "open with other application"
left click on triangle next to "Use a custom command"
Left click on browse
Double click on "file system"
search under "user/I believe it's sbin or bin" for the application 
double click on application and close "Open With"

Hope this helps
