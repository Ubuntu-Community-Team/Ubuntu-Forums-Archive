### Post by steveneddy on 2009-07-06
A quick Google search gave me this page:

[http://www.compiz-fusion.org/](http://www.compiz-fusion.org/)

which simply states:

> Compiz Fusion is the result of a merge between the well-known Beryl composite window manager and Compiz Extras, a community set of improvements to the Compiz composite window manager.Beryl is a dead project.

Compiz-Fusion lives in it's place.

Other Compiz reading:

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compiz_Fusion](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compiz_Fusion)

[http://www.opencompositing.org/](http://www.opencompositing.org/)

Hope that answers your question.

Compiz should be installed in Ubuntu since 8.04.
