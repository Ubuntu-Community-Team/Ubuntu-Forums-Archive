### Post by incogweedo on 2011-08-11
When you see '100mbps' on anything, simply disregard this.  True, YOUR internet speed may be 100mbps, but the server you're connecting to may only be able to handle 128kbps due to a large amount of people downloading.  Plus, server admins usually throttle their upload speed because of so many people connecting at once.
