### Post by halitech on 2010-01-17
chances are if its a P3 then it's a best a AGP slot, at worst a PCI only board. You probably won't get a PCI card that will handle the visual effects that you are looking for. If it's AGP, be careful to find out what speed it will handle as if you try and use an 8x card in a 2x slot, it can burn out the card.

The should be a marking on the board somewhere to identify the board.
