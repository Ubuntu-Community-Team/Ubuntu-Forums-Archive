### Post by tarves57 on 2008-12-21
After many disheartening attempts to get any Linux to boot up on my old computer, I discovered that if you use a CD-R instead of a CD-RW or DVD, it works beautifully.  

If I'd known that at the beginning, I could have saved myself a whole load of bother and disappointment, so thought I would share this tip!
