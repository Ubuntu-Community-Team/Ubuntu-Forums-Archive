### Post by asmoore82 on 2009-02-22
I hope someone will correct me if I'm out of date, but...

The last I heard of this situation, Firefox/Flash Player rely on a relatively ancient sound subsystem (OSS).
One of the unpopular quirks of that sound system was the inability to play more than one sound at a time.
So, in my experience, if any sound is already being played when
Firefox/Flash Player first tries to make its own sound,
it will be unable to make sounds for the rest of the session.

Closing Firefox, stopping all other sounds, and re-opening Firefox is what I do and the sound always works after that.


P.S. Back the day I always considered "1 sound at a time" as a Feature instead of a Bug because
I don't like to hear anything else while my music is playing.
