### Post by .nedberg on 2008-09-08
In the Amarok preferences you can set your last.fm username and password.

Add one track to your playlist.

In playlists tab, select dynamic playlist and Suggested music (Not sure about the wording, my Amarok is Norwegian, I need to change that...).

Amarok will now add new songs (from your collection) based on last.fm recommendations.
