I am just posting this in case it happens to anyone else.
Heat Alarm went off on computer. Found cooling fan not running on Nvidia Geo 6600 Gt Card.. Fan actually fell out. Got a new Video Card.. 8400 GS..  Computer would not boot.. kept shutting down..   the.. Fan on the CPU was running. Motherboard Abit.. ### Post by SoftwareExplorer on 2009-09-04
CPUs have sensors to detect how hot they are inside the chip. What probably happened is that when the fan post came loose, it made it so that the cool base of the fan no longer was in contact with the CPU and so it couldn't cool the CPU properly, setting off the alarm.
