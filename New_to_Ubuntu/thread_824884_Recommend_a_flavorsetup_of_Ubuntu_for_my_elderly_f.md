### Post by mikewhatever on 2008-06-10
A PC with these specs should be fine for Ubuntu. To get that screenshot, I've gone to System>Preferences>Appearance and increased all font sizes from 10 to 16. To get bigger icons in Nautilus file browser, adjust the sizes from Edit>Preferences in Nautilus. I've gone to 200%. Firefox can render pretty big fonts, but some sites get distorted by that. Obviously, it needs more tweaking and experimenting though.
