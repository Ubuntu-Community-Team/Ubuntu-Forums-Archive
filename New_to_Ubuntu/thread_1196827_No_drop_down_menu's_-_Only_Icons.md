### Post by dailyacts56 on 2009-06-25
I have always disliked drop down menu's. Windows has always been full of them, but now I have Ubuntu I have figured out how to rid of all of them and to put icons on my top panel. Actually, it is pretty easy to do. Just drag and drop the icons from the main Ubuntu drop down menu onto the panel. You can also add them by right clicking with your mouse on a panel and then clicking on the "+ Add to panel" and selecting which program icons you want.

Another thing I did was add two drop down panels - including a calendar and a local weather panel. You can find this one by clicking on "Add to Panel" and selecting it.
