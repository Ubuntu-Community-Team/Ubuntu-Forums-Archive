### Post by diablo75 on 2009-03-02
Fiddling around with this...

In the customize window, click Colors tab, then change the "Selected Items / Background" color to change window borders.  This will also change the color of your scroll bars though...

### Post by blazemore on 2009-03-02
Yeah that option changes the "fundamental" colour of the theme.
if you go to Gnome-look.org and look at metacity borders, find one you like, download it to your desktop.
Drag and drop the package onto the Theme window in the customisation application.
