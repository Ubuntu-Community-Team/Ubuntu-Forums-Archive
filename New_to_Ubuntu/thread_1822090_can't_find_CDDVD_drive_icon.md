### Post by ~!geek!~ on 2011-08-10
Even if there is no disk in CD/DVD drive, You may see the drives and few other hardware using Nautilus by:
1) Open nautilus
2) Press ctrl+l (address bar will be visible by doing this)
3) Type computer:// on address bar 
4) Done!! You may see harddisk partitions, external usb data drives, cdrom etc.

or 
By clicking on My computer icon on top in nautilus. (Not available in nautilus elementary!!!)
