### Post by colin.p on 2010-01-13
Mpeg4 would be put on a DVD as a data file, same as AVI or MKV.
You open CD/DVD Creator and drag the file over to it and click burn.
However, a stand alone player probably won't play it, you'll have to play it on your computer. I suggest using VLC as the player as I haven't come across anything that it won't play...yet.

Colin

edit, same way you would put an MP3 on a disk, for that matter.
