### Post by gdonwallace on 2010-07-09
Its a pain sometimes.  I have the same problem.  

To fix this, under system go to Preferences then Main Menu.  Scroll to games and then find the game you just installed.  Generally clicking on the check mark in front of it twice (the check will disappear and  re-appear) and this should fix it.
