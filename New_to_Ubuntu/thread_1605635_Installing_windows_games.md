### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-10-25
I was just able to play just 3 games successfully through wine. They are GTA Vice City, GTA San Andreas and Brian Lara 2005.

Most of the enthusiast get there desired apps working by some tweaking/configuration in wine. Not something impossible, neither something that easy.

Winehq is a good place to find out which software/games work for which version of Wine and which distro.

If you are just curious about gaming, dual booting Windows and Ubuntu will be a better option. It stinks that most of the games are only built for Windows. They really need to be ported to Linux. I hope it will become a trend soon.
