### Post by t0p on 2009-09-18
While you have credit, go to [online.vodafone.co.uk]("http://online.vodafone.co.uk") and register a new account.  Then in future, before your credit runs out, go to your local post office or newsagents and buy a vodafone topup voucher for £15.  Then go back to online.vodafone.co.uk and login.  You'll now see various options, including one to activate a topup voucher.  Activate the voucher you bought.  That will add the £15 to your account.

Just make sure you do all this before your credit runs out, unless you have another way to get online.  I've got a vodafone hsdpa dongle and I do all this regularly, so I know it works.
