### Post by IanW on 2008-06-24
Right click on the launcher you don't like the look of & select "Modify this launcher".
Then in the window that opens, browse to an icon you want to use instead.
Click OK to finish.

More tips on the [Cairo-Dock Wiki](http://www.cairo-dock.org/ww_page.php?p=Tip%20and%20Tricks&lang=en)
