### Post by tgalati4 on 2014-03-15
Put the laptop on a solid table.  Tape the electrical cord to the table.  Take the battery out and boot the laptop without the battery.  See if the laptop is stable for several hours.  You could have a faulty power jack (cracked solder connection inside) or a weak battery that will cause momentary losses of power.  It could be a cracked motherboard that will cause freezes when the laptop is lifted by the corner or moved around.  It could be bad capacitors.  How old is the laptop (2006 vintage?)  Many manufacturers made crappy laptops to go with the crappy OS--they just didn't care.
