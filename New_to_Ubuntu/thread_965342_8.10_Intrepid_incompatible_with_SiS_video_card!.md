### Post by Riffer on 2008-11-01
Yes you should to get you at least up and running.  After that you will need to do some research.  I do know that for some SIS chipsets there isn't Linux support, you may have to get a cheap Linux compatible graphics card.  You can get a cheap nVidia for around $50 (CDN) new.

[http://ph.ubuntuforums.com/showthread.php?p=60592](http://ph.ubuntuforums.com/showthread.php?p=60592)
