### Post by k3lt01 on 2010-05-26
If you are willing to disable all operating system sounds, not stop sounds in programs like Movie player and Rythymbox though,you can go System > Preferences > Sound, then when the sound preferences opens you can disable the sounds by selecting "No sounds" in the sound theme box.

### Post by nhasian on 2010-05-26
here is the offending file:

> /usr/share/sounds/ubuntu/stereo/desktop-login.ogg

simply rename it to .backup or delete it.  if you have a different startup sound you'd like to use just name your audio file to the original one.
