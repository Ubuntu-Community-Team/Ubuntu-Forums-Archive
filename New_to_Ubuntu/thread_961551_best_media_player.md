### Post by malcolmci on 2008-10-28
I found Amarok's UI to resemble an extremely cluttered take on iTunes' (which I didn't like that much anyway), and Rhythmbox the same to Winamp.

I think Exaile and Banshee have interfaces most similar to WMP's. However, I recently discovered that you can't manually set the album art in Banshee (albeit it does automatically fetch the art for more popular music).

So, I'm currently using Exaile (they do say it's based on Amarok, but I think it has a much cleaner interface). Even though it's still in preliminary versions, it's showing alot of promise.

As for movies, VLC.
