### Post by el_koraco on 2011-06-23
Yeah, not sure if we were precise about the install process itself. First you go here - [http://lubuntu.net/]("http://lubuntu.net/")

Then you click on the "Get Lubuntu" link on the right side. This downloads the .iso file. Then you follow the procedure outlined in step 2 to burn it to the disk -  [http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download"). 

Next you put the disk in the drive, and reboot. When your computer turns on, you hit F2, or F9 or F12, so you can select to boot the computer from the disk drive. if you're ot presented with such an option, you gotta go into the BIOS, and set the boot order to boot fro the CD/DVD drive. Next step, select the "Try Lubuntu" option, so you can see if your wireless and graphics are picked up. If everything looks OK, you click the install icon on the desktop. Assuming you backed up your files, you just select the "Use entire disk" option. In 10 to 20 minutes, the install will finish (you'll have to enter some details before that), and you reboot. Voila, you have Lubuntu installed. 

Then you come back here, and we go through the stuff you have problems with :D
