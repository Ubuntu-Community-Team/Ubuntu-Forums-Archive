### Post by papibe on 2010-12-18
You can use Clonezilla to clone your current disk into the new one. When everything is working properly you can use gparted to resize (make bigger) the partitions in order to use the whole size of the new disk.

[Clonezilla ]("http://clonezilla.org/")is a "live" tool that works in old school text mode. There's several tutorials in youtube including [this]("http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zz8iUzV0oMk").

Good Luck!
