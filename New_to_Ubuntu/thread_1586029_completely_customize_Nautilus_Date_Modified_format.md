### Post by begtognen on 2010-10-01
I wanted to change the Date Modified format from the default to a very customized format:

10.01.10 11:36 AM

And it's totally possible. Just follow the directions here:

[http://blog.chewearn.com/2008/11/14/change-date-format-globally-in-ubuntu/](http://blog.chewearn.com/2008/11/14/change-date-format-globally-in-ubuntu/)

Two things to be aware of:

1) Restart after you've made the changes to see them take effect.

2) Be sure you have your date format under 

Edit | Preferences  | Display 

set to the FIRST option.
