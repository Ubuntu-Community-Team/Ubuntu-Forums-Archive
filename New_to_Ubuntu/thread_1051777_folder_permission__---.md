### Post by wstay on 2009-01-27
When I right click a folder and open the properties page of a folder and view the permission level for the folder, one of the choices given is ---. 
I wonder what permission level would that be.
Can someone please explain?

---

### Post by bgates on 2009-01-27
It would be all access denied.  rwx is read, write, execute; so everything would be allowed. r-- for example would be read only.  Replacing one of those with a hyphen denies that permission.  Thus --- is no permission to do anything at all.
