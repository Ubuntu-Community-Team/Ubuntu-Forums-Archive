I think it depends *greatly* on what you use your computer for.  If you need stability, you might want to stick with a version you know works with your hardware (8.04 is supported until 2011).  The trade off is that you miss out on the newest of the new.  I tend to take a middle ground position and try to wait at least a month before upgrading to a new release.

Good luck with your decision.
