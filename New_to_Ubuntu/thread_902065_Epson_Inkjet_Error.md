Hi,

The first thing you do before you post a question in any of the forums is to use the SEARCH button.
Typing in "Epson Stylus C67" in the search box and clicking go will lead you to a number of posts in which you may find an answer.  

The next place to look is opeprinting.org.  Here is a link to what is found there about your printer.

[http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Epson-Stylus_C67](http://openprinting.org/show_printer.cgi?recnum=Epson-Stylus_C67).
