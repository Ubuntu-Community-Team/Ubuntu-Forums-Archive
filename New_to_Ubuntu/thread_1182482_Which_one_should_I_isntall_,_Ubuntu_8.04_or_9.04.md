### Post by AndyCooll on 2009-06-09
Unless you have strong reasons for needing a LTS release you are best choosing the latest and greatest, i.e. Jaunty (9.04), if for no other reason than it's the release that the majority of users on these forums currently use. 
And with it you are going to get newer versions of apps and overall better hardware support. Since this is now an official release (and not an alpha or beta) even this version _is_ perfectly stable enough for everyday desktop use.

A strong reason for sticking with an LTS however might be that you run it as a server where stability is the single most important factor, where long-term support is more important, and where the latest and greatest aren't as important. This is typically more of an aspect sought by businesses and enterprises (though not exclusively).
