### Post by Maverick Mungbean on 2010-10-26
> **Oleq said:**
> Share Your solution, please.
oh yeah, well you go to the gconf editor,
then to apps/metacity/general and the on the right side scroll down until you find "mouse_button_modifier" 
Now the default button is set to "Alt" but you have to change it to something else e.g. "Control" (thats how I did it and its working)
):P
