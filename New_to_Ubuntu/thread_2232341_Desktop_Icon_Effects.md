### Post by Rob Sayer on 2014-07-01
I sympathize ... I ran lubuntu on my netbook until recently.  It would be nice to have the panel buttons at least change color.

Actually I never did find a way to solve that in lubuntu.  I installed xubuntu 14.04 instead because lubuntu 14.04 just has too many bugs for my taste.  But I suspect that you could install a theme that'd at least change the icon color.

Try to get supported themes.  There are a lot of 3rd party ones out there but I won't touch stuff like that.  Too many potential problems.

You'll also find lots of stuff on how to add animations/eye candy to lubuntu.  I would definitely not touch that either.  There are too many problems and, besides, the reason lxde is so light and fast is largely because it *has* no eye candy.  Installing lxde and then trying to make it look like a modern highly animated DE is a common noob mistake IMO.
