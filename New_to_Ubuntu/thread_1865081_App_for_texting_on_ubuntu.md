### Post by leunam12 on 2011-10-19
You can send an email to the phone number, it will appear as a text message on the other person's phone as a text message. the address is "phone number @ some domain provided by the carrier". Example: For Virgin Mobile it would be: [email]1234567890@vmobl.com[/email]

Google "email to cellphone" and you will find plenty of websites listing the domains for dif. carriers
