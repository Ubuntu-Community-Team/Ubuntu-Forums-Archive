### Post by Ratscallion on 2009-07-07
So the Applications, Places, System bit has gone? Well, that should be easy enough to sort out.. Just right click on the panel, click add to panel. Then type menu in the search bar. Double click on the custom menu bar and it should add. You can then right click and have many options to move and customize it. Hope that helps :)
