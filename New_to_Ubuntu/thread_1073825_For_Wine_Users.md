### Post by sharathpaps on 2009-02-18
> Do you have to be a member of wine because its only showing me 30 out of the 11708. 


No, you do not have to be a member. Just click on the links [Platinum]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse+Applications&iappVersion-ratingOp0=5&sappVersion-ratingData0=Platinum&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true"), [Gold]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse+Applications&iappVersion-ratingOp0=5&sappVersion-ratingData0=Gold&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true") and [Silver]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&sTitle=Browse+Applications&iappVersion-ratingOp0=5&sappVersion-ratingData0=Silver&sOrderBy=appName&bAscending=true") that are on that page and you will get a complete list of all the applications in that particular category.
