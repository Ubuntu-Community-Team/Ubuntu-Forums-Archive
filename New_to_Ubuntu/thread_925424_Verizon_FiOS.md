### Post by neoAnderson on 2008-09-20
I think a FiOS router has a hex key and not a passphrase. When you try to connect to the Internet does it not ask you whether you are entering a WEP passphrase, a WEP hex key or an ASCII key (it will be a drop down menu)? Choose hex key and then enter the key printed on the router.
