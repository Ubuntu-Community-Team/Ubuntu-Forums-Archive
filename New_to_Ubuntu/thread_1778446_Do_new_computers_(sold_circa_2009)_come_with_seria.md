### Post by colin.p on 2011-06-09
You are asking about two different things. There is a huge difference between "serial ports" and "USB ports". All computers come with USB. However, serial is "legacy" hardware, so few modern (circa 2009) computers would have a serial interface. You may find one with PS2 ports, but good luck finding a PS2 keyboard.
It would be what the others said, it maybe another hardware issue, or even software issue. If you were running windows,as an example, I would say it would be something like an AV that was scanning in the background. Something like that.
