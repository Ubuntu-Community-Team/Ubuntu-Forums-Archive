### Post by Bucky Ball on 2011-07-10
You need to boot from the CD you just made, not try to install from a running Ubuntu install.

Shutdown, restart and go to your BIOS and change the boot order or hit a key to get to the boot menu (on my machine that is F12) and change your boot device to the CD/DVD drive.

Your install will replace 9.10, of course, unless you create another partition for it and have them both installed. If you have valuable data I would back it up and make a solid plan before attempting anything.
