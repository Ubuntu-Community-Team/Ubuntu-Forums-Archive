### Post by dearingj on 2009-11-27
Sounds like your touchpad thinks it's being tapped while you type. Here's the fix:
Open the System menu, point to Preferences, and click on Mouse. Then click on the 'Touchpad' tab, and check "Disable touchpad while typing".
