### Post by sikander3786 on 2010-12-11
Unfortunately, 10.10 netbook-edition is using the new desktop environment Unity that is not much configurable yet. However, there is an option to boot to the regular Gnome session and you can tweak it accordingly.

Logout, click your username at Login Screen. Session menu will appear in the bottom of your screen. Select ubuntu-desktop (or might be Gnome), type your password and login.

Regarding System > Administration, that is a Gnome thing available on ubuntu-desktop or Gnome. In Netbook Edition, you need to click the Ubuntu logo in top left corner or the applications menu (pair of scissors icon) in launchbar and search for whatever you want.
