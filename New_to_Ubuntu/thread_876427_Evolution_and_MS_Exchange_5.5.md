### Post by mailkarthi on 2008-07-31
Hi,

Evolution uses OWA (Outlook Web Access) to connect to Exchange Server and OWA is supported only on Exchange 2000 and 2003.

I believe you cannot use OWA. If you have POP or IMAP enabled you may be able to connect as an POP or IMAP client.
