### Post by migs73 on 2010-10-10
The box at the bottom is called a dock. I don't know which one it is you have but you'll find out if you right click on it, and it should bring up the preferences/settings, there are a few available. I use AWN but there is also Cairo-dock, docky and others.
To change a font, just got to Preferences > Appearance, and then click on the Fonts tab. There you can pick what font you use in different areas.
