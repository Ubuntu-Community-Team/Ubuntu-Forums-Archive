You may have some luck here.[ http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1454702]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1454702")

I am native Cantonese speaker but frankly I think it is a lot easier to read standard Chinese (traditional or simplified) based on Mandarin.

There is no standard way to write Cantonese and what you find in print (Hong Kong magazines and such) is a mess of confusion. There are attempts to standardize it by some academics in HK but IMO their approach choaks  the life out of the  dialect with its compulsive obsessive pedantry.
