### Post by taurus on 2009-03-21
If you want to upgrade OpenOffice to 3, you need to add ppa.launchpad.net to your /etc/apt/sources.list.

[https://launchpad.net/~openoffice-pkgs/+archive/ppa](https://launchpad.net/~openoffice-pkgs/+archive/ppa)

p.s.  Make sure you use the _intrepid_, not jaunty.
