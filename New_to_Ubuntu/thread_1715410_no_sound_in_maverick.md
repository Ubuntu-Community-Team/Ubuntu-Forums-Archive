Could have a couple of reasons....

Here is the one i encountered: 

Pulseaudio (Ubuntu's sound back end) is configured on a per user level. I am using MPD to play my music. But that runs as a different user. So its been a first come first served thing beween the different users so to say. 

Could also be your PulseAudio just crashed due to cosmic radiation interfering with your computers memory. :-D
