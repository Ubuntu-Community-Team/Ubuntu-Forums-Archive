### Post by jenkinbr on 2008-11-14
If it's not under warrenty, you *could* be VERY careful and dissassemble the drive and clean it.  I don't recommend this practice though.

Your best bet may be taking it to a shop and having it looked at.

Another question: have you checked all the connections?  Pressure at that power could also cause connections to loosen.  Completely disconnect the drive at both the motherboard and the drive itself and see if that fixes it.
