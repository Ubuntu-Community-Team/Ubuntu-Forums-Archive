### Post by silverglade00 on 2010-08-25
I have one of those. Try unplugging the Simple Save. Then reboot. Then once you are logged in, plug it in. It will load two things. The first will be a fake CD drive called HPLAUNCHER and the other will be your normal drive called HP SimpleSave. Always ignore the CD drive. That might be what it is trying to launch. It will take a while to come up sometimes because it is trying to load both of those at the same time. Just be patient and it should pop up two windows with contents of each of those drive I mentioned.
