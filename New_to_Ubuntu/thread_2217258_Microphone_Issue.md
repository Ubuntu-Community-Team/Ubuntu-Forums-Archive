Just a FYI for anyone reading this thread and particularly newcomers to the forum.

Mint users are as welcome to join in discussions and support threads on this forum as I would hope Ubuntu users would be in the Mint forums. In fact many members here run both Ubuntu (or one of the official flavours) and Mint systems and are well-placed to advise on their relative merits.
