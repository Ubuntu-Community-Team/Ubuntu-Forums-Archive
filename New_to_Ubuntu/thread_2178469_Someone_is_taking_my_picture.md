### Post by papibe on 2013-10-03
Hi schmitta.

I'd start by checking that you're not pressing inadvertently the PrtScn (print screen) key on your keyboard.

When you press that key (or in combination with Alt or Ctrl) the system takes an screenshot of the whole screen or a window, and make the sound you described.

Hope it helps. Let us know how it goes.

### Post by kurt18947 on 2013-10-03
If you're concerned and have a web cam (it sounds like you do) there's a low tech way to insure you're not being photographed -- tape a bit of paper over the web cam lens if it's integral or unplug it if stand alone.  There was a news story a while ago where someone figured out how to activate a girl's web cam remotely without the in-use  light illuminating.  Sometimes simple measures are the most effective.
