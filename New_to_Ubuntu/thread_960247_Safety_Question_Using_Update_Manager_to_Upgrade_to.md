### Post by roger_1960 on 2008-10-27
Hi

I  upgraded this way (to 8.04.1) a few months ago and it worked without a flaw and with no data loss at all. It took about 30mins as I recall on a 2Mb adsl connection.  Whether you will have anything to do afterwards depends on your hardware. If you had to install specific drivers in 7.10, you may have to in 8.04.1, but I found the new version to be better than 7.10 on this front.

Having said that, BACKUP all your data including hidden email application data files before you do this to be safe.  I have read a few posts which suggest that a clean install is a good idea, but the update method worked for me (on 3 separate laptops)
