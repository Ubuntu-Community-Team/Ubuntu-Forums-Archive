### Post by verymadpip on 2014-03-07
Hi bill.

Personally, I'd go to the Tor website & download the correct bundle for your OS (32 or 64 bit) from there.  There's no need to install anything, it runs from the included binary after extraction.  You can't miss it - it's the file named "start tor browser".
It's not as "neat & tidy" with icons & such as some people would like, but it works & IMO that's a small price to pay for the anonymity.

[https://www.torproject.org/](https://www.torproject.org/)
