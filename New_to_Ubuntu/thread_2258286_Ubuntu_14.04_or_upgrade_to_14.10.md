### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-12-26
Ubuntu 14.04 has 5 years support from its release at the end of April 2014. Ubuntu 14.10 only has 9 months support from its release at the end of October 2014. Unless you are willing to upgrade from 14.10 to 15.04 and on to 16.04, then stick with 14.04.

I do not think that there will be big visual changes changes to Ubuntu over the next 2 years. Ubuntu 14.04 has Unity 7 and so does 14.10 and so will 15.04 and 15.10. The big changes will come with Ubuntu 16.04. It is planned that 16.04 will give us a login option of running Unity 7 on the Xserver and Compiz, as we do now, or to run Unity 8 on the Mir display server/compositor. Even then Unity 8 on the desktop will be very much like Unity 7.

A lot of development work has to be done over the next 12 to 18 months. I cannot see much effort going into making big changes to Unity 7.
