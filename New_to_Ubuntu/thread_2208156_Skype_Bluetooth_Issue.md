Not that I've really tested but I think you can keep the default system wide settings for your audio devices - if that's what you want - and at Skype change the default audio device it uses - the system default - to the specific audio device you want, Bluetooth in this case.

Skype > Option > Audio devices (select the 3 options according to your intended goal)
