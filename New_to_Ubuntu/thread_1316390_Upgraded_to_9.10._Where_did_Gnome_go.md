### Post by ubducted on 2009-11-05
If it is a fresh install, then you may have accidentally downloaded Xubuntu.  Can you check the filename of the ISO you downloaded?

I have both Ubuntu 9.10 and Xubuntu 9.10. On a fresh install, there is no way Ubuntu will have Xfce on it and not Gnome.

You can check to ensure it's the right image file using the MD5:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes)

Hopefully it's not a case of downloading an incorrectly named image file...:cry:
