### Post by walt.smith1960 on 2010-01-04
Hi Deany

Did you see the free beginners guide offer at the beginning of 'absolute beginner talk'?. I bought the paper version from Amazon before I knew it was available here. It does quite a good job, I think. If you or someone you know  is familiar with how to download and burn a CD, Ubuntu can be run from a CD, unlike Windows. You and your daughter can develop some understanding of Ubuntu without making any changes to your new machine. The downside to 'live CDs' is that the machine won't remember any settings once you power the machine down. The upside is that if you REALLY mess something up, the fix is as simple as restarting the machine. Good luck.
