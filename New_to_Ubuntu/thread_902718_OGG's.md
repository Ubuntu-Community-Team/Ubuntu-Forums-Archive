### Post by Crafty Kisses on 2008-08-27
Ogg is a free, open standard container format maintained by the Xiph.Org Foundation. The Ogg format is unrestricted by software patents and is designed to provide for efficient streaming and manipulation of high quality digital multimedia.

The name &#8216;Ogg&#8217; refers to the file format which can multiplex a number of separate independent free and open source codecs for audio, video, text (such as subtitles), and metadata.

VLC and Audacious can play Ogg's fine, both programs are in the repos.

Look here for more information > [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ogg](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ogg)
