### Post by laidback on 2009-11-19
You may also need to look at the Skype Options themselves, Options>Sound devices and now select for Sound In, Sound Out etc. When I switched from a standard mic to an integrated webcam/mic I needed to alter these settings. I found Sound In set to USB device, Sound Out to Pulse and Ringing to Pulse works for me. These is a test button on this screen as well.
