### Post by Dynaflow on 2009-05-12
Ubuntu includes pppd.

Ubuntu can be made quite light.  The LiveCD is a working "installation," and it takes up less than 700MB despite having OpenOffice, GIMP, Firefox, etc., installed.  I'd say 5GB should be plenty to install necessary apps and have plenty of storage space left over.  You can also shift files over to your Windows partition from Linux if the latter's partition starts to run out of room.
