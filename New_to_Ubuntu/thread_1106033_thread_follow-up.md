### Post by kanikilu on 2009-03-25

You can also subscribe to threads using the Thread Tools menu at the top of a thread.

### Post by balaknair on 2009-03-25
Try using the *Search* menu> *Find all your posts* option at the top of the page just below your user ID.

If you want to be notified of new replies to a thread you're interested in, use the 'Thread Tools> Subscribe to this thread' link at the top of the thread, and select 'Instant email notification' on the next page it takes you to.
