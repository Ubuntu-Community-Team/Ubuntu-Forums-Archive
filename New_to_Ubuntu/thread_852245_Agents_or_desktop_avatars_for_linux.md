### Post by karasuman on 2008-07-07
My favorite xpenguins theme is the Sheep, which you can get here:

[http://linuxforblondes.com/applications/esheep/]("http://linuxforblondes.com/applications/esheep/")

I used the applet for a while, but it gets buggy with the number of penguins.  For instance, if I want one sheep, one turtle, and one penguin, I could enable those three and ask for three creatures... but, most of the time, I'd get 2 penguins and 1 of the others.  The applet seems to really like 2 penguins.

To work around that, I wrote a shell script; it's pretty easy to figure out how to do what you want with man xpenguins.

Oneko is a cat that chases your mouse.  I used the position offset to keep it from sitting ON my mouse.  If you decide to try that out, I recommend creating a custom launcher with this command (or some variant):

```
oneko -tora -bg goldenrod -cursor 132 -position +100
```

-tora makes it a tabby cat (there are other variations, like a dog or an anime girl); bg sets a color; the cursor 132 makes it revert to a normal cursor when you're done; the position +100 makes the cat stop further away from what I'm trying to click on.
