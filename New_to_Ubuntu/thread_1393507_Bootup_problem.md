I use 8.04 on my PC and have had a similar problem a few times.  Initially I tried holding the 'on' button for 3 seconds, rather than a quick jab and this seemed to work for a while but only a while.

I then removed the wires that run from the motherboard to the switch and 'jiggled' them a little as I replaced them.  My theory here being that the connection had corroded.  This seemed to resolve my issue.

May I suggest that you note carefully the order of the wires before removing them.
