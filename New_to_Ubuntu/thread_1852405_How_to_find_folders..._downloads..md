### Post by An Sanct on 2011-09-30
It is located in /home/*username*/downloads

But you can press Ctrl + Shift + Y in Firefox and right click on a download and select "Open containing folder" to open it quickly.

PS. My advice, set up FF, so, that it asks every time where to put the download. The download is taking place, when you decide where to put the file, so no time is wasted.
