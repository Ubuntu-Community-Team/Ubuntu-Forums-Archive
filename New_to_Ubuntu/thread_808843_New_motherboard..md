### Post by tamoneya on 2008-05-26
that cpu cooler will work but probably wont be much more effective than the stock cooler that came with your processor.  As for thermal paste you definately need it.  The question you need to ask is "Is the stuff that comes with the cooler good enough".  If you arent going to over clock or anything just use what it comes with.  Otherwise you should probably go with artic silver or some other higher end thermal paste.
