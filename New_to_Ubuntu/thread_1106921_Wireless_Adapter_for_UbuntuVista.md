### Post by karlr42 on 2009-03-26
By amazing coincidence, just today I receieved an MiniPCI (laptop internal) Intel 3945ABG PRO/Wireless as a replacment for the Broadcom BCM4312 in my new Dell Mini 9(was generally fine with the wl proprietary binary blob, but totally failed in connecting to my college's hidden SSID WPA network, which totally eliminated the whole point of having a netbook). Once I installed it into my machine, this card connected just fine to all the networks I have access to(out of the box support with the iwl3945 driver), which made me very happy indeed :D
It's a fine card, have been using it for 2 years in my Inspiron 1520(hence why I chose it as a replacment for this new machine) with no issues at all.
I highly recommend BDNiner's suggestion of using [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported)
