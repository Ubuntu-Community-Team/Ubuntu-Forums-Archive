### Post by hellion0 on 2008-09-19
I'm assuming you mean something like a printer with a built-in scanner?

Here's a decent list for printers: [http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi](http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi)

And the same for scanners: [http://www.sane-project.org/cgi-bin/driver.pl](http://www.sane-project.org/cgi-bin/driver.pl)

In general, HP and Epson printers tend to work well under Linux, and avoid Visioneer at all costs. (As of Gutsy, anyway.)

Hope these help.
