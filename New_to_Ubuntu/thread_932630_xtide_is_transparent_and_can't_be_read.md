### Post by mhjones on 2009-03-31
After posting (within seconds), I had a thought.

Transparent windows.  I made the association with effects.

I turned off all effects, and viola, xtide works perfectly.

Anyway, thanks to anyone who might have thought to give an answer, and I hope this helps out anyone else.

I am running kubuntu, btw.
