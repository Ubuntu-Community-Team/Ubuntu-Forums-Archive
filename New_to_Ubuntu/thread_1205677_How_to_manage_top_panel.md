This may seem pretty stupid but somehow I accidentally moved the order of things on my top panel. Now I can't figure out how to put things back the way they were.   
Applications - Places  - System  :  used to be on the top left in that order & now they're on the top right.

### Post by Augsburg on 2009-07-06
Right click them and unselect "lock from panel". Then select "move" to move them back where you want them.
