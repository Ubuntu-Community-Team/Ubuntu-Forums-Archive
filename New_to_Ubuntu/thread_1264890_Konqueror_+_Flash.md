### Post by swoody on 2009-09-12
Well, it seems that Konqueror doesn't detect the netscape plugins automatically.

In the menu, select "Settings>Configure Konqueror". Select "Plugins" on the left hand side - Then click on the button "Scan for new Plugins".

Konqueror should then scan for plugins and detect Flash and other plugins, which are installed for Firefox. Try this out, and let us know if it works for you or not :)
