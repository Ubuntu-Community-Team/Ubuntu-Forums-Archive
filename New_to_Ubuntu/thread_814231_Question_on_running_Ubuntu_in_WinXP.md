I've been running 8.04 under Vista using the Wubi method (which is what I believe you are doing) since it (8.04) was in Beta, with no problems whatsoever. Moving to a dedicated partition will be doable using LVPM (I believe that's the correct name) once it has been updated to handle 8.04. You might want to take a look at the Wubi Forum, as there is a sticky about this there, for further information and assistance.

[http://lubi.sourceforge.net/lvpm.html](http://lubi.sourceforge.net/lvpm.html) informs that there is, indeed, an updated LVPM for 8.04; offered for your information.
