### Post by Jerry N on 2012-02-02
Linux Mint has a project called "Cinnamon" which is far from complete but its intent is to retain the flavor of gnome 2.x.  Mint 12 also has something called "Mate" which is also trying to look like classic gnome.  Cinnamon and Mate can be added to Ubuntu 11.10.  Mint 13, based on Ubuntu 12.04, will come with upgraded versions of Cinnamon and Mate.
