### Post by theDaveTheRave on 2014-02-07
You need to give us a bit more info regarding what you want to acheive with the pairing of devices.

If you want to synchronise your windows phone to your ubuntu desktop (ie contact from phone to thunderbird sync, and calendar sync), youre going to be out of luck. The old nokia symbian phones didn't play nicely with ubuntu, I doubt you will get a windows phone to!

If you want to connect to your phone to access the internal storage over blue tooth, that shouldn't be a problem. But you will need to tell your phone that is should be in the correct mode (most likely 'data' mode). Otherwise if this is what you are trying to do you should be able to browse the phone memory / sd card using the usb cable (if it has one!).
