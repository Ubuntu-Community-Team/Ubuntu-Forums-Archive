### Post by aysiu on 2008-06-09
Removing it won't remove your settings and bookmarks.

But it's not a bad idea to back them up.

~/.mozilla is just shorthand for /home/*username*/.mozilla

and the .mozilla directory is a hidden directory (that's what the dot at the front of the name does - hide it), so press Control-H to see it (or, if you're using Kubuntu, Alt-V, then H).
