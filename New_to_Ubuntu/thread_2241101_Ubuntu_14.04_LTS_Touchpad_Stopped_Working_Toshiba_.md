Then I attempted to watch a small video stream (4oD UK) in fullscreen mode and the touchpad stopped working... I was able to plug in a mouse and that was working fine. I have tried restarting and searching for similar problems but the touchpad is still not working. 
I looked in Settings -> Mouse and Touchpad and it is showing touchpad on

### Post by Ksiencha on 2014-08-24
Maybe pressing **Fn + F5** will enable touchpad again. If this combination doesn't work, just look at keys F1 - F12 and find the picture where it shows the touchpad, and press Fn+ that button.
I hope it'll help ;)
