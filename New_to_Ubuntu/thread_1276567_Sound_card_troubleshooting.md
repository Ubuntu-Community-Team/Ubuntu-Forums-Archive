### Post by Zeedok on 2009-09-30
Have you checked all the devices listed in 

```
System - Preferences - Sound
```

Try getting a test sound to work.  The other thing you can try is the volume control menu - right click on the volume button on the panel.  Try unmuting all of the volume sliders, and checking a few of the switches.

It's a bit hit and miss, but these simple things have worked for me a few times . . .
