### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-01-25
Did the motherboard come with a manual or user guide? It may be that the 256Mb stick is incompatible.

In some motherboard configurations the sticks have to be in pairs. Or it may be that they have to be in different channels where each channel is a pair a pair of sockets.

So, you may need to put the 256Mb stick in the first socket of channel A and the 512Mb stick in the first socket of channel B. But you must check this out with the motherboard user guide.

It could also be that the 256Mb stick is not seated in the slot correctly.

Regards.
