### Post by dagrump on 2009-07-22
Use alt+F2 type gconf-editor. Then apps>pref>show image thumbnails change that to never. I think you have to restart x to make it change. Just log off & back in might work.
*** never mind restarting x it works as soon as you change it.

### Post by aysiu on 2009-07-22
In Nautilus, go to Edit > Preferences > Preview, and you can change the settings there.
