### Post by Golem XIV on 2008-06-23
I had this happen a couple of weeks ago.  For some reason Evolution decided that every mail I received was spam, so it marked them as read and sent them to the Junk folder, without notifying me that a mail has been received.

Luckily, I don't have the "empty junk at exit" option enabled so when I noticed what was going on I simply marked the mails as "not junk" and recovered them.  This also appeared to reset the junk filter, so now I'm receiving the mails correctly again.
