Thought I would post this here because I couldn't find a similar solution.  Hopefully someone finds this post useful in the future!

I was looking for a way to make the text on the bookmarks toolbar in Firefox smaller.  I came across a better solution that doesn't require any additional Firefox addons or tweaks.

1)  Delete the text associated with the title field for each bookmark in the bookmarks toolbar.  This makes the only visible portion of the bookmark the favicon.  This saves a lot of space.  

2) View --- Toolbars --- Customize in Firefox and drag the bookmarks toolbar items up next to the URL bar (to the left of the google search box).  Also, check the box marked "smaller icons" or something like that.

3)  Uncheck View --- Toolbars -- Bookmarks toolbar

5)  Profit!

Hope you enjoy this!  It saves a lot of browser space.
