### Post by drhpapagino on 2008-07-03
Chris,  You will need to enable your vnc desktop in your Hardy desktop by going to system prefernces, remote desktop and enabling others to see you.  Also, they will need a vnc viewer on their desktop and a password of your chosing to access your Hardy.  If they are outside your network, it is best to do this with tightvnc, which is secure.  vnc is not.  Good luck.
