### Post by Therion on 2008-09-21
Before you go and do a fresh install, have you tried simply removing Gnash from your current install?  
I was having no end of problems with every Flash related until I fully uninstalled Gnash.

Go to System/Administration/Synaptic Package Manager and search on "Gnash".  Right-click on anything Gnash related you have installed and remove it. 

See if that help with your Flash problem.  Beats doing a reinstall if it works.
