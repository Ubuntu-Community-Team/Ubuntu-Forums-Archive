### Post by earthpigg on 2010-12-26
> **pakkatechie said:**
> (screen becomes blank with raining sound inside the laptop) when viewing youtube videos and other flash videos.

this sounds like your laptop is overheating. flash is particularly CPU intensive, so common causes of overheating can be amplified by flash viewing.

have you dusted it out lately?

are you setting the laptop on a flat hard surface, or in your lap?

ie: if you moved from sitting at your desk most of the time to laying in your bed most of the time right around the 10.10 upgrade, combined with not dusting it in a long time, combined with flash being the most CPU-intensive thing many people do on their computers.... that could explain the problem.

### Post by diablo75 on 2010-12-26
It's possible the version of flash you're using is incompatible with something else, or is out of date or what have you.  Fortunately there's an easy extension you can get for Firefox called Flash-Aid.  Install it, and it will automatically check to make sure you're flash is the most compatible and install it for you automatically if necessary:

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/161939/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/161939/)
