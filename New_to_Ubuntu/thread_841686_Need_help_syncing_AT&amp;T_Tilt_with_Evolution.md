### Post by snackbarber on 2008-06-28
mbarvian,  I replied to another thread in which you asked this question.  In case you didn't see the reply check out [http://www.synce.org/]("http://www.synce.org/").  It is a project to allow Linux users to sync their Windows Mobile devices.  They have a wiki and the install instructions seem to be easy to follow.  I have been able to view the files on my Tilt from my Ubuntu(Gutsy) laptop so far.  It appears that syncing to evolution and bluetooth syncing are both possible as well.  Also if you don't already have WMwifirouter installed on your Tilt get it now.  You will thank me.  It turns your tilt into a wireless router that uses the cell connection.  Totally awesome.
