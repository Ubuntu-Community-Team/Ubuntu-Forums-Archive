### Post by drs305 on 2009-01-10
Do your normal saves appear to run normally?

One possible cause: Autosave is working but your normal saves are not. That would explain why you lose the last of the work. 

It could be a permission problem in which your autosaves write to a folder you have permissions to while your normal save does not. You would probably get an error message if that were the case.
