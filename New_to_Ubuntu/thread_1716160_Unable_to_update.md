### Post by aaaaalex on 2011-03-28
In the Update Manager application, click on 'Settings' at the lower left handn corner of the window. 

In the new 'Sofware Sources' you will see towards the bottom 'Installable from CD/DVD'.

Make sure the box is unchecked, then close the window. The Update Manager should now run without the CD.
