### Post by starcannon on 2008-06-04
I have had a few cameras that were not reliable about being mounted by Ubuntu, my solution was to buy a $10 card reader, and to pull the media card out of the camera and plug it into the media card reader, I've not run into any cards that won't comply with this method yet *knock on wood*.

Not sure if that is an acceptable work around for you, but its the method I use.
