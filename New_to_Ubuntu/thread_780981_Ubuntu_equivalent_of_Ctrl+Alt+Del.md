### Post by perlluver on 2008-05-03
That command would bet Ctrl>Alt>Print Screen>REISUB, yes that is r e i s u b.  That will stop the system, and restart it.

### Post by aheckler on 2008-05-03
CTRL+ALT+BACKSPACE will restart X and return you to the login page. It works 99.9% of the time.
