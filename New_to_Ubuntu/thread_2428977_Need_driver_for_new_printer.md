### Post by rbmorse on 2019-10-11
Your equipment is supported by HP's HPLIP utility in version 3.19.6.  You can obtain it from the HPLIP download site at [https://sourceforge.net/projects/hplip/files/hplip/](https://sourceforge.net/projects/hplip/files/hplip/)  That's also the version in the 19.10 repository (as of today).
