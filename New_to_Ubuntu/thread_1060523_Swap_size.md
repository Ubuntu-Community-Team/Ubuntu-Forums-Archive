### Post by m_duck on 2009-02-04
I think for hibernation to work you need a swap partition a bit bigger than the amount of RAM you have. If you do not use this feature, it isn't so necessary. I have 4 gig of RAM in here and a 512 MB swap just in case it needs to be used. In my eeepc I forego a swap partition altogether and haven't encountered any problems relating to it.
