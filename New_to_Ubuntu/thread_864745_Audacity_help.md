### Post by BLTicklemonster on 2008-07-19
Open the file in Audacity.

Look to the left of the actual working space at the part that shows info like title name with an upside down triangle next to it. Click on that. Now go down to Set Sample Rate Format, and choose 16bitpcm. I think that's what you want. 

I think audacity shows 32 bit when you open any file, if I remember right. Like it wants you to save it as 32 bit or something. 

Someone who knows more may come along with a better answer.

*edit: yep, just saved allyourbase.mp3 as a 16 bit wav file, then opened it and it defaulted back to 32 bit.
