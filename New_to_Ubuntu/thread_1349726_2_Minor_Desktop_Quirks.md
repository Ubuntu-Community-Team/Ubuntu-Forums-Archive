### Post by Locke_99GS on 2009-12-08
1) Enable compiz

2) There is an option in Pidgin's setting somewhere. (I'm not at home right now, or I'd direct you right to it) I had the same issue on the laptop, and found it. It's something along the lines of "Show in Tray" or something of that sort.

---

### Post by Locke_99GS on 2009-12-08
Ok:

Pidgin / Tools / Preferences / Interface

Where is says "Show System Tray Icon", set it to "Always" and it should function as you expect.
