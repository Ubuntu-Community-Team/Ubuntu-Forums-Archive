### Post by cavalier911 on 2011-04-22
The topic is x64 **L**ubuntu , _**not **_x64 Kubuntu or x64 Ubuntu.:D
The x64 bit full versions for Kubuntu and Ubuntu iso's are available for free download either from mirrors or torrents. :KS
The x64 **L**ubuntu install requires the minimal install of x64 ubuntu .
[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lubuntu/DocumentationHelp/MinimalInstall](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/Lubuntu/DocumentationHelp/MinimalInstall)
This requires an internet connection on the computer your installing to as it must download most of the packages from the repos.The whole process takes longer than install from 700 MB livecd as your downloading/extracting/installing at the same time.
The advantage is the packages your installing are up-2-date,if you install from an iso depending on how old it is you can end up having to update most of the packages. ):P
