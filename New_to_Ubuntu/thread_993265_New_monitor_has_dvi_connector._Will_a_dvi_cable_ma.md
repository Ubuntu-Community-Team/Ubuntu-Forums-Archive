### Post by Titan8990 on 2008-11-25
You will need to purchase a DVI-D cable as it has less pins than DVI-I and a DVI-I cable will not fit into a female DVI-D connector. Basically, all DVI supported cards will accept DVI-I (Digital + Analog).
