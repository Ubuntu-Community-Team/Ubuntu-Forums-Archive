### Post by dcsoldschool53 on 2011-07-12
When you go into the Application menu to click on a program that you want, instead of left clicking on it, do a right click on it. You will see send Desktop, or gnome-panel.

### Post by k33bz on 2011-07-12
Another solution is to go ahead and create a launcher on your desktop. Then right click on your menu, choose edit, go to where the program(s) that you want to create a short cut of, choose properties. You will see what the command is to launch it, as well as where the image path of the icon is at.
