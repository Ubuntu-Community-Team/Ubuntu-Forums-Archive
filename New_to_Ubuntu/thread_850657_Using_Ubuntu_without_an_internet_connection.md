### Post by t0p on 2008-07-05
You can visit [http://packages.ubuntu.com]("http://packages.ubuntu.com") with an internet-linked computer, and save packages to disk or flash stick for later installation to your netless boxes.  But you may find yourself in **dependency hell** that way, without apt/Synaptic to make sure all dependencies are filled.  You might find it more convenient to get the computers online temporarily by **using a mobile phone as a modem** - see the link in my sig for a tutorial on how to do this.
