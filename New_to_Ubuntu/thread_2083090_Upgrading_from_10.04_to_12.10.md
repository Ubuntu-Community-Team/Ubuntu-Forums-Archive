### Post by ibjsb4 on 2012-11-11
The next LTS after 10.04 is 12.04.  Maybe your not getting any update notification because of "release upgrade" notification.

### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2012-11-11
While you have been updating religiously, it does not upgrade your system unless you tell it to upgrade it to the next version.  It has been a while since I have used plain Ubuntu but if you check Software sources and then navigate to updates you should see an option near the bottom which says notify me of any new Ubuntu release. It can be changed to anything you like.  Now the next LTS is not 12.10 but rather 12.04.  You can upgrade to that one directly from your current 10.04.  However, do not forget to back-up any data that you cannot afford to lose because you never know what might happen.  Once you have upgraded to 12.04 you may then (if you so desire) upgrade to 12.10 (which is not a LTS version but is the most current stable version.)  Alternatively, if so want to you can download the ISO for the most current version, burn it to a CD, and do a fresh install.  Your choice.  In any event, back up your data.  Enjoy.
