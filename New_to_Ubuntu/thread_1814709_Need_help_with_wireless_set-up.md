### Post by Idefix82 on 2011-07-30
I bet the penguin was on your screen ;-)

Right click the network manager icon in the panel, then "edit connections", switch into the wireless tab, choose the WiFi network, click "edit", and go to "IPv4 Settings" or "IPv6 Settings", depending on which you use (if unsure just change both).
