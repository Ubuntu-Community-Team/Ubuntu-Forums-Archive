### Post by Temposs on 2009-03-10
I suspect you're using a machine with a very small screen. Is this the case? Some windows like the one you're trying to use are stupidly too large to use with small screens(less than 800 pixels vertical causes problems). If this is your situation, when it tells you to click Forward, press Alt+F, and this will press the Forward button. Alt+B will click the Back button. This may be enough to get you through the mail wizard.

Let me know how it goes :-)
