### Post by Paqman on 2011-01-23
The netbook interface has/had some serious file management issues, as you've discovered.

Do yourself a favour, click on any removable drives you might have connected, or the trash can. This will open Ubuntu's standard file browser, Nautilus. Right click on the Nautilus icon in the launcher and select "keep in launcher". Now whenever you want to browse and manage files you can just click on that.

The "files and folders" doohickey is rubbish, I wouldn't bother with it. It's been dropped from the next version of the netbook interface.
