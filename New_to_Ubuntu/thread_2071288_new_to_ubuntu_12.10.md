### Post by rasmus91 on 2012-10-15
Hello there, and welcome.

your computer should be just fine for the Standard Ubuntu 12.10, can't imagine you will have any difficulties there.
The software center should provide you with any software available across all of the buntu Distributions.

I recommend installing Ubuntu 12.04 If you do not wish to account for new distribution releases for quite some time. Or 12.10 if you do not mind upgrading or reinstalling once in a while (every 6 months or so)

first thing i do after i install Ubuntu on a new machine is to Update, then download the restricted extras from the Software center.

I've used Ubuntu for 5 years now, and even though it has been a learning experience, i have really enjoyed using it. if it wasn't for all the gaming happening on windows, i would be running Ubuntu on my Desktop as well.

Good Luck, and remember that you can ask this community about anything Ubuntu related (often also computer related) :)
