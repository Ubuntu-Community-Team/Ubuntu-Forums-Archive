how did you install the dual boot. wubi or side by side?

let's start the troubleshooting by first posting some hardware specs [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1422475](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1422475)

and then also running the bootinfo script: [http://bootinfoscript.sourceforge.net/](http://bootinfoscript.sourceforge.net/)


your issues could have various sources. however i suspect the major source is that your hardware in not compatible with linux / or at least is officially unsupported and uses some workarounds and hacks ot mak eit work (e.g. bumblebee).
