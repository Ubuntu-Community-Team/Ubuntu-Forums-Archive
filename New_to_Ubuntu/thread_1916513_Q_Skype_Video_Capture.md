### Post by cryptotheslow on 2012-02-04
Open a Terminal and enter:

```
man ffmpeg
```Or read the same manpage online here:
[http://manpages.ubuntu.com/cgi-bin/search.py?q=ffmpeg](http://manpages.ubuntu.com/cgi-bin/search.py?q=ffmpeg)

Then just choose your Ubuntu version.

Apparently RecordMyDesktop works well too - I haven't tried it myself for Skype but it can be set to record your whole desktop or an individual window. You should be able to find it in Software Centre or Synaptic (if you use that).
