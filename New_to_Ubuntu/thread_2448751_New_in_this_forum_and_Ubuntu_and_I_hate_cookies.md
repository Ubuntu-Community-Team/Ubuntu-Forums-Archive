### Post by sdsurfer on 2020-08-13
If you want to clear browser cookies, right-click on any page, select inspect, and select the storage tab (FireFox and Chrome/Chromium.) You can delete all cookies from there.

Also I have set my browsers to disable all third party cookies, these are the ones that track your movements across multiple web sites. It only "breaks" a small set of sites IME. I have one site it breaks, a mortgage e-signing site, I enable all cookies just for that site.

Note that clearing cookies will sometimes clear the cookies that keep you logged in to some web sites.

### Post by sdsurfer on 2020-08-20
> I don not like to store cookies, what about the tracking cookies? I want them all away from my system!

Cookies in themselves are not evil. You wouldn't stay logged in to this site or any other without them. It's how they are used that can be nefarious. By disabling third party cookies in your browser settings, it will stop most of the tracking cookies from being set on sites that use them.
