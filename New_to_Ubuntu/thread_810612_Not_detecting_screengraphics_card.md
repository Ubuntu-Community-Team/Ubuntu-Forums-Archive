### Post by phr0ze on 2008-05-28
This seems like a bigger system problem than the detection of your graphics card. Perhaps its a heat issue or faulty power supply.  

In the BIOS you can check your temperature and your voltages. I'd look for voltages that are not within 5 or 10% of the stated value.
