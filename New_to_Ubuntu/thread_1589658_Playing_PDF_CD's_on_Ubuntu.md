### Post by llamabr on 2010-10-07
If the autorun is an exe, then it wasn't written for your system.

Nonetheless, if you open the cdrom, which is mounted on your desktop, and poke around in some of the directories, you're likely to find the actual pdfs, which you can open with evince (evince is just the default pdf document viewer).

Don't give up just yet.  Poke around a bit, and if you don't see it, let us know what you do so (a screenshot would help, maybe, too).
