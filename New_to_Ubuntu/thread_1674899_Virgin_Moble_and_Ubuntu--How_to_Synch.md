### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-01-24
Plug it in and wait a few seconds.

Go to the Network Manager applet near the top right corner of the screen.

Right click it and choose Edit Connections. Click on the Mobile Broadband tab. Click the Add button.

Follow the prompts and you're done.
