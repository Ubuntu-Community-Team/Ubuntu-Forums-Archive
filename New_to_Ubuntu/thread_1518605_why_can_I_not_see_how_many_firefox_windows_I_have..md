### Post by nhasian on 2010-06-26
i use [docky]("apt:docky") instead of having a bar on the bottom of the screen, but i added a new panel with the application switcher applet to test it and i do have a separate button for each instance of firefox.

also i wanted to remind you that you can have multiple tabs in firefox.  You can create a new tab with Control-T.  I usually have 6+ tabs open at a time and i've never needed to use a 2nd firefox window.  Although i must admit its pretty cool that you can drag a tab out of firefox and it opens it up in a new window.  or you can take a 2nd firefox window and drag it onto the first and it makes it a tab.  :o
