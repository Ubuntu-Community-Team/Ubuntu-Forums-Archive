Common misconception.  All releases are stable.  LTS versions are supported for longer.  That is all.

It is generally a good idea to wait until a month or two after the release date to upgrade, for the kinks to be worked out.

12.04 will be supported until April 2017, while 12.10 will only be supported until April 2014.  If you think you'll want to upgrade again by 2014, than upgrading to 12.10 isn't a bad idea.
