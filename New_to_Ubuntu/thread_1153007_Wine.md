### Post by NightwishFan on 2009-05-08
Press Alt+f2

Type:
```
winecfg
```

Then push enter.


You can configure wine there, make sure you autodetect drives, and visit the audio tab. Then you are set.

I have gotten Itunes to work, it looks a little odd, but it works. It could not detect my burner drive though.
