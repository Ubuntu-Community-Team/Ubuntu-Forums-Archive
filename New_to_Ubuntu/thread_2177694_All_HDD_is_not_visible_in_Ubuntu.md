### Post by DuckHook on 2013-09-30
Hi Ravi_Roshan and welcome to Ubuntu and the forums.

Please provide far more info. In addition to answering *dshgna's* question, how did you attempt to "see" your HDDs? What apps or methods did you use to query? Did you try the *Disk Utility* app that comes bundled with Ubuntu?

Also, please note that Linux and Ubuntu does not use the old DOS convention to designate HDDs/partitions (C: D: etc), but uses the Unix convention instead (sda1...sda4, sdb1...sdb4, etc). You may wish to read up on Linux HDD and partition basics. [Here]("http://www.linuxplanet.com/linuxplanet/tutorials/4269/1/") is a useful primer.
