### Post by InspiredIndividual on 2011-01-29
Regarding your question about desktop icons:

If you use Xfce 4.4: 
Settings > Desktop Preferences > Behavior > Desktop Icons
If you use an earlier version of Xfce, you need a third party program, for example pcmanfm, rox, nautilus, idesk.

Sources:
[http://wiki.xfce.org/faq](http://wiki.xfce.org/faq)
[http://idesk.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Main_Page](http://idesk.sourceforge.net/wiki/index.php/Main_Page)
