### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-03-17
If you're using Gnome (which I assume you are) you can right-click the disc icon and choose "Copy Disc". You can make a disc image that you can burn to multiple discs, or just copy directly back to the same drive (after you've removed your original source disc and inserted a blank, of course!).

if its a home movie from a camcorder then you might not need to bother with any fancy tools. if it hasn't got copy protection, and if it's smaller in size than your blank dvd media that you wish to copy it on to, you can just right-click on your dvd-drive and select 'copy..' (or whatever its called) and select copy to image file. you can then burn this image file by right-clicking it and selecting 'write to disc' once you've replaced the dvd with a blank one.
