### Post by rejinarudo on 2011-05-02
Thanks for asking.

If you mean opening two instance of applications, let say you want to open two windows of "application-x", all you need to do is to middle-click its icon in the launcher or in the dash.

And if you mean opening two different applications at once, let say you want to open "application-x" and "application-y", you can just simply do the usual clicking just like in windows. 

If opening two different applications cannot be done, I'm pretty sure it's a bug. If that's the case, please report it to [https://launchpad.net]("https://launchpad.net")
