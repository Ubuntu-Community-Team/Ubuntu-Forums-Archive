### Post by askreet on 2008-05-27
This is your BIOS reporting this error, I'm hard pressed to believe it has anything to do with what operating system you were/are running.  Is it 100% consistent that it does not occur when rebooting from Windows?  Have you ever changed the CPU settings on your BIOS from their defaults?

I would attempt a settings reset in your BIOS to eliminate the possibility of bad configuration options.
