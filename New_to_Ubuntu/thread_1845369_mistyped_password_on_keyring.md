When you login to your system, your authenticated password unlocks the keyring which is a master database of your other passwords such as WPA keys and the like.

Autologin systems will get the prompt often, if you login manually it usually gets unlocked automatically for you.

see here to reset [http://ubuntu-tutorials.com/2010/01/16/reset-gnome-keyring-password-on-ubuntu/](http://ubuntu-tutorials.com/2010/01/16/reset-gnome-keyring-password-on-ubuntu/)
