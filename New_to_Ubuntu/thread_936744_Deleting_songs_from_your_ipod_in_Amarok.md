### Post by unknown user on 2008-10-03
I am using Amarok to manage my ipod and it is working good.  I have a few songs that I need to delete of my ipod though.  Can anyone tell me how I can do this without deleting my whole ipod?

---

### Post by jbrown96 on 2008-10-03
If it is already connected in Amarok, just go to the devices menu, connect it, then browse to the artist/album/track that you want to delete and right-click and delete track from ipod. It's very simple.
If haven't connected it before, go to settings-->configure Amarok, then devices, and add a new device, specify that it's an ipod and where it is mounted.

### Post by Lorelei- on 2008-10-03
I run Amarok and the way to do it is to click on 'Settings', then 'Configure Amarok' and then 'Device' and finally 'Add Device'. That should allow you to add your iPod (provided its connected to the machine already!) and from there you can just delete songs exactly as you would in iTunes.
