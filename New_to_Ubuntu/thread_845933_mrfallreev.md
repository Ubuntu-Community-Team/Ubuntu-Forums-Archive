### Post by tsger on 2008-07-01
You might want to try adjusting the dpi setting, if it is the font size that is bothering you.

To do that, 

1. Right click the desktop, then select "change desktop background."
2. Go to the "fonts" tab, then click on "details."
3. Under "resolution", increase the dots per inch to, for example, 100 or higher.  

Hope this helps.
