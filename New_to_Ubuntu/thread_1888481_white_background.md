When you open Writer (or Calc), go to Tools, Options. Then expand the LibreOffice dropdown (near the top left) and click on Appearance. Then in the General section you will see User Interface elements. There, you can specify the color of the page background and font color, etc.

I don't know about one setting for everything but, it's possible individually. I do it. But keep in mind that many pages are designed in such a way that changing the background color of the page will cause a loss of "content". Are you sure you are willing to take that risk?

Then again, browsers have two components: the "chrome" and the "content". You maybe able to find themes that affect the chrome and userstyles that affect the content.

Again, depending on your distro, it's possible to apply themes but you need to be specific about what you want and about what you have: OS, OS version, DE.
