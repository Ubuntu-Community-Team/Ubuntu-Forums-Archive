### Post by BkkBonanza on 2010-09-04
I've done this. I didn't have any issues, just set the Skype option for video and clicked "test", and it worked. You have to choose the correct device in the list in the options. I have an Acer Notebook and it shows up as "CrystalEyes /dev/video0".

I had my webcam working in Cheese before I even tried, so if the webcam isn't tested and working first then I'd do that before before trying Skype.
