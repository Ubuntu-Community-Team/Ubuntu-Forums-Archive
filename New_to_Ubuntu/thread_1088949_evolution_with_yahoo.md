### Post by noobuntu7 on 2009-03-06
Hey,
You can get evolution to work with Yahoo, but not "easily". Depending on which country you're in... if you're in the States you need to subscribe to [Yahoo's Mail Plus!]("http://mailplus.yahoo.com/") service. After that, it's *relatively* easy to [setup Yahoo! Mail with Evolution]("http://help.yahoo.com/l/us/yahoo/mail/original/mailplus/pop/pop-14.html"). There are supposedly workarounds and loopholes for subscribing to Mail Plus! However, you can get your account terminated (It's against Terms of Service). Hope that helps!
