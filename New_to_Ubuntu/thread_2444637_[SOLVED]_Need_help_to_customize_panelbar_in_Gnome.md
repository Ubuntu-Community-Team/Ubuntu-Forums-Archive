### Post by Dennis N on 2020-06-02
> move the button "Show Applications" at the bottom of the left panel to the top bar
You can't move it to the top bar as far as I know, but can move it to the top of the dock.
Install Dash to Dock Extension, then
Launchers Tab  > Move Applicatiions Icon to Beginning of Dock
