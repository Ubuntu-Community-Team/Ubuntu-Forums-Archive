### Post by Chesamo on 2011-01-18
Well, Descent (original) can be played on DOSbox since it's a DOS game, with no problems.

Descent 2 has a Platinum rating in the [AppDB](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=17254), meaning it'll work perfectly.

Descent 3 has a Gold rating in the [AppDB](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=884), meaning it'll work with some fixes (a nocd crack is mentioned in the Platinum rating comments).
