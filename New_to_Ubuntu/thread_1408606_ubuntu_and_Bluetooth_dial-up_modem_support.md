### Post by lkraemer on 2010-02-17
The first thing you need to do is to decide if you want to spend the time
trying to make a Winmodem work, and most likely it won't, or you can
buy an old External Hardware Serial US Robotics Modem and be up and
running in around 30 minutes.....That is assuming you already have
a Dialup ISP with the connect login information.  Netzero and Juno
seem to be more trouble, and might not work with Ubuntu.  I use Copper.net.
