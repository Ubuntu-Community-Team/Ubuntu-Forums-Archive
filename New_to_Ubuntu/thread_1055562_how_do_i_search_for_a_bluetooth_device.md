### Post by unutbu on 2009-01-30
According to [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=227057](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=227057), this command:
```
hcitool scan

```
will tell you MAC address of the bluetooth devices. Hope this helps...
