### Post by Paqman on 2011-05-27
> **millian23 said:**
> should i get a new drive?


Yes, 98 sectors is more than normal wear-and-tear. Something is not right. Back up your data now and get a new drive fairly soon.
 
> Is this down to old drive? 

Possibly. It's quite erratic behaviour, and I certainly wouldn't rule out a hardware problem.

> 
i did not select the proprietary drivers option on install, do i need to download drivers?


That option in the installer just installs things like audio codecs and the like. If you can view videos, listen to music and watch Flash, etc then you're sorted. If you want you could install the package ubuntu-restricted-addons, which is what the installer would have done. If you want to go further there's a package called ubuntu-restricted-extras that has even more stuff like extra fonts.
