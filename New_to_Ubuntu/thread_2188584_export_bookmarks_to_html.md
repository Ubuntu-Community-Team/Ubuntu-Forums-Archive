### Post by heir4c on 2013-11-18
With that you create a .html file with all your bookmarks in it. Click on it to try it out. You will be ask where you want to save that file. Select the folder and click Save.
Than you go to that folder and click on the file. Than it will be open in FF (because it is a .html file) Than you will see how he saved the bookmarks.
Than in the future you can import back the saved bookmarks via the .html file. (That's also a option that you see above "export bookmarks to html".
