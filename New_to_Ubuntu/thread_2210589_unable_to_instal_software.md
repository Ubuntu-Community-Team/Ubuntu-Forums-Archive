### Post by deadflowr on 2014-03-11
Do you see the icon on the left side panel that has the number 4 on it?(Should be called Update Manager)
Click on that to open the update manager, then when it is open, Do Not click install or update, but instead click on the button that says Check.
Let this run and when it comes back to the normal screen, now press update/install.
This will bring the system fully up to date.
And the dependency issues should be fixed.
