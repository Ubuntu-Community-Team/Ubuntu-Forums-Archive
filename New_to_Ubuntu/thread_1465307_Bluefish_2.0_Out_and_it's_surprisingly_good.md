### Post by kapi on 2010-04-29
Just a quick note to let everyone know that bluefish 2.0 is out and its really quite good.

Many web developers including myself have searched these forums for advice on the ideal web IDE when they first migrated from windows.

I've tried a few and still have a soft spot for geany but after seeing a demo of the new bluefish I decide to give it a go. It may not be for everyone but if you are looking for a good IDE then consider this.

The intellisense and code completeion isn't bad at all, it caters for php, css, xhtml and mysql

well its just a few thoughts I thought I'd share as this community has help me in so many ways. It's nice to give something back
