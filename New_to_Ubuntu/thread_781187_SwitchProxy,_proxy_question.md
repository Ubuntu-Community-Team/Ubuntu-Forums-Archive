### Post by Sp|ke on 2008-05-23
There's more than one way to skin a cat (or in this case a proxy). Some (read most good) proxy/IP checkers will use every trick in the book to get your real IP address. Websites are not necessarily that worried to go digging to any depth. Hence whatsmyip showed it and 4chan didn't know it.
