### Post by SirBismuth on 2009-07-17
I just went to System > Administration > Printing

Then New > Printer, and there was a list of network printers displayed under, well, Network Printers, after Ubuntu had scanned for a second or five.  I think you can also find it if you know the IP, can't remember how I did mine.

Got the required PPDs from linuxprinting.org, would recommend this to make your life easier, for either a local or network printer.
