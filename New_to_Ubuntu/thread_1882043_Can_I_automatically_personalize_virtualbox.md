### Post by mister_playboy on 2011-11-16
For the desktop settings, you could simply copy your config folders in ~ and move them into the VM via shared folders.

Copying the programs over and having everything work as expect would require a lot more filesystem knowledge... so I would recommend installing as normal if at all possible.
