### Post by coffeeaddict22 on 2009-05-05
Hi,
You can go the GUI way or the terminal way.  The GUI way, right click on the sound icon, and have a look through the volume panel (you may have a different driver in there that'll work better and the preferences.  You can also go through the System/Preferences/Sound menu and play with the stuff in there. 

There's a fair bit of stuff in the wiki worth looking at- start with [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DebuggingSoundProblems]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/DebuggingSoundProblems") and go from there.  From what's been going lately, I think there's a problem with PulseAudio on some systems not having sufficient user permissions; the details on how to fix are in the wiki, but post back if you want more help.
