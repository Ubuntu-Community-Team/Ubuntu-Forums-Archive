### Post by gdonwallace on 2009-11-07
Yes, you want you boot partition to be on your main hard drive.  If you are wanting to do partitions, you can put /home, /tmp, /opt all on separate partitions.  But /etc/, /var, /boot (plus the other partitions) must all be on the master partition.
