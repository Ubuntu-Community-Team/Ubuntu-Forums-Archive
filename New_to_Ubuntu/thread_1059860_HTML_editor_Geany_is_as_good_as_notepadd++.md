### Post by kapi on 2009-02-04
Just a quick note to anyone who has just switched to the Fabulous Ubuntu and are trying to locate a half decent html text editor similar to that of notepad++. 

its taken me a while and I've tried all the recommended applications like blue fish, elcips, jedit and quanta but no matter how hard I try I still come back to Geany. its simple and easy with no learning curve and you can batter straight in and get some design done.

It may not be everyones cup of tea but i just thought it may save you some time if you are looking for a new editor.
