### Post by quixote on 2010-01-27
I'd suggest putting your first question in its own thread.  (One topic per thread is the usual rule.)  And be a lot more specific.  What's the problem?  Have you tried System > Printing and had a problem?  Which problem?  (But, as I say, in a new thread.)

SDcard or flash drives are not as fast as hard drives, and they have a rated life of, I believe, around 10,000 accesses, which is way less than a hard drive.  So you want to use them for low-access applications.  Document or photo directories, or the like.  Programs and swap files are exactly what they're *not* the best thing for.  That said, they're cheap.  You could try it and it would probably work fine for quite a while.
