### Post by ikt on 2009-11-05
> **frrob said:**
> I have no CD/DVD drive, so have not tried other distros.

Do you have a USB drive?

The best way to install ubuntu or any other distro fresh on a netbook is by using a usb drive.

If you download the ISO image for ubuntu 9.10 from torrents/wherever you can then 'burn' that to your USB drive by going to:

System > Admin > USB Startup disk creator.

Warning that it will completely erase your usb drive.

You can then boot off your usb drive and hopefully check out the LiveCD to see if you get your sound back.
