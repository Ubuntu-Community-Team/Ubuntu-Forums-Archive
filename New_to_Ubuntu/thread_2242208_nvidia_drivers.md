### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-08-31
If you look at this link you will see that the "updates" version is a version of the driver that has been updated in regard to the "tested" version

[https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/nvidia-graphics-drivers-331-updates](https://launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/nvidia-graphics-drivers-331-updates)

driver 331.38.0ubuntu7 is the tested version and driver 331.38-0ubuntu7.1 is the updates version. In the past some of these offered drivers were labelled "experimental."

Regards.
