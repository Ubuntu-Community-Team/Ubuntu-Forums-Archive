### Post by Enternald on 2009-10-04
Yes of course you can. The main concern could be your video card. If it is from diferent manufacturer in worst case your x server may crash and you would be turned back to console. In best case your old driver may work with new video card, or you may fall back in standart video driver (no 3d but works) and request install required driver and uninstall old one. Jaunty is really new distro so yours other hardware may be supported. Althought it is not clear if you give some specification I could say more about effects of migration.
