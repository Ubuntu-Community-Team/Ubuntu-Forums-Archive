### Post by Dill on 2009-05-11
The above suggestion may be better for your purposes, but you can set the Nautilus preferences to not automatically open a window for ANY media (which is why the solution may not be preferable). 

Just open up any Nautilus window and go to Edit->Preferences->"Media" tab->Uncheck the box that says "Browse media when inserted"
