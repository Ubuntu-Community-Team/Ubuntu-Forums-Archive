### Post by neurostu on 2008-08-27
Instead of asking someone to guide you step by step you should try installing and going forward then post a thread when you have problems...

Most people don't have the time (or desire) to write a complete step by step tutorial... especially because the tutorial will vary depending on you skill level and hardware...

From the above links, [here's a direct link to the perfect guided installation]("http://www.psychocats.net/ubuntu/installing"). Further, the "first things to do" highly depend on your usage.
