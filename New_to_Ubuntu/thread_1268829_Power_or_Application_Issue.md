### Post by Bölvaður on 2009-09-17
> **darms said:**
> NightwishFan: I've been to the System --> Preference ---> Power Management and I don't see any where else for me to disable the suspend mode.  Please advice.


System &#8594; Preferences &#8594; Power Management &#8594; General
*When the power button is pressed*: **Shutdown**
*When the suspend button is pressed*: **Hibernate** (this is better in my opinion than suspend)

Now check On Battery and select it so when the lit is closed turn off the monitor. Just go nuts on the settings and check out what every switch does, it will help you a lot.
