### Post by earthpigg on 2009-12-19
may want to find out what language the BIOS interface is in. verify it has an 'english' option which your wife can help you enable.

if it has a DVD drive, may want to verify that the region-coding DRM isn't at the firmware level of the optical drive itself. i have no idea if this is commonly the case, just something you may want to look into.

### Post by Marvin666 on 2009-12-19
Most dvd drives allow up to 5 region switches in the lifetime of the drive, via software settings. Some software will let you play discs from other region without swapping the setting.
