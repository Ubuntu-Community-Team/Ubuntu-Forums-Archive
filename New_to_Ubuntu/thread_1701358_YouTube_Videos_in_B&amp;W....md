### Post by maqtanim on 2011-03-06
Yeah... there is some problems after updating the new flash. 

Go to any sites that has a flash content (may be [Nvidia]("http://www.nvidia.com/content/global/global.php") site). Right click the flash content, go to Settings. Select the first tab and uncheck the "Enable Hardware Acceleration". 

Hope it will work.
