### Post by Kir_B on 2010-07-15
Hey all. I was having problems with some flash videos in firefox and especially with the msnbc website's video player. (For whatever reason, this is not an issue in seamonkey!)

I know that a lot of us use the adblock addon and as it turns out, it is causing a problem in firefox, with certain websites, and I suspect that it's just the one's that run an advertisement either before or between videos.

I found [this fix]("https://support.mozilla.com/en-US/forum/1/44434") (from [feedback10k]("https://support.mozilla.com/en-US/forum/1/44434#threadId112911")) on mozilla's firefox support website.

Just simply select the "disable on msnbc.msn.com" option in the drop down menu for the adblock plus addon.

I hope that helps you, as it did for me.
