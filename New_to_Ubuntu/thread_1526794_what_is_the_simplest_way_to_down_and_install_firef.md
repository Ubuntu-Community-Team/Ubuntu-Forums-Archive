### Post by NFblaze on 2010-07-09
Yes to download the beta from the link earlier.

The thing is that being that it is beta, it will not update your current Firefox install. What you will have to do is extract it out into your home directory. Inside of the extracted files you'll see "firefox" you can create a launcher to that. It will totally be separate from your current install.

Also, to "root an android phone" means to basically break the imposed limitations. So, if you root your android it's like jailbreaking an iphone.

Basically you can install Apps to an SDcard, use pirated/removed apps, etc...

@Ron999

Name it whatever you want

that will create a launcher on the desktop then just double click and your in beta 4!
