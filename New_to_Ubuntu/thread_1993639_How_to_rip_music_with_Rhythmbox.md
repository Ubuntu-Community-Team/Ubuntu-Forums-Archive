### Post by traditionalist on 2012-06-02
You need to configure the plugin first under "Edit" "Plugins";

[[IMG]http://img696.imageshack.us/img696/7852/selection001tn.th.png[/IMG]]("http://img696.imageshack.us/i/selection001tn.png/")

When you have done that put the audio CD in your drive and this will show up under "Devices".  Click on "Audio disc".  You can now rip the tracks you want;

[[IMG]http://img163.imageshack.us/img163/9411/50greatirishrebelsongsa.th.png[/IMG]]("http://img163.imageshack.us/i/50greatirishrebelsongsa.png/")


However, "Asunder CD Ripper"  ( Ubuntu Software Centre;   

[[IMG]http://img843.imageshack.us/img843/5117/ubuntusoftwarecenter001.th.png[/IMG]](http://img843.imageshack.us/i/ubuntusoftwarecenter001.png/)

Is faster and better with more options.
