### Post by Zill on 2011-11-20
Blojic:  You may like to take a look at [DinoPC.com]("http://www.dinopc.com/").  I have bought two desktop PCs from them and have been very satisfied with both quality and price.

The models I bought seem to have been superseded by the Seismo Intel G620, which has a better spec (doh!).  The main difference appears to be that my PCs use Asus motherboards, whereas the Seismo uses a Gigabyte motherboard.

One advantage of DinoPC is that you can customise the PC with the hardware and software you wish, including the option to have no operating system, leaving it totally clean for your shiny new Ubuntu installation. :-)

I would recommend an Intel CPU and a possibly a Nvidia graphics card if the onboard Intel graphics are inadequate for your purposes.
