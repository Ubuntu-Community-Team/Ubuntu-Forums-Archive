Are there any tools available to recover files from an ext3 formated disk after it has been formatted? i.e. files were on disk which was accidentally formatted.

---

### Post by bumanie on 2008-12-27
Your best bet would be look at [cgsecurity]("http://www.cgsecurity.org/wiki/TestDisk") and the programs testdisk or photorec. There are also tools such as [sleuth kit]("http://www.sleuthkit.org/autopsy/"). Despite its name, photorec can look for in excess of 100 file types, not just photo files.
