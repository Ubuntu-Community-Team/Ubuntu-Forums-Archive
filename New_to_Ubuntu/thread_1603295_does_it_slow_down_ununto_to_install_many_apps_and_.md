### Post by SeijiSensei on 2010-10-22
What matters more than the number of applications you've installed is the number that you're running simultaneously and the type of load they place on the system.  Disk I/O and video decoding both stress your system more than word processing.  Games are usually best played with as little running in the background as possible.
