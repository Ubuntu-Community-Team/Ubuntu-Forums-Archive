### Post by oldsoundguy on 2011-07-21
Almost any HP printer will work out of the box on Ubuntu and the drivers are in cups.  NO CODING in most cases. A nice little GUI for installing.

I have an HP All-In-One 6110-xi with duplexer, An HP 1215 Photosmart with duplexer, an HP 1150 3 in one Office Jet, An Epson Photo R1800 (those are ink jet printers) .. and an HP 6MP Postscript laser all hanging off of computers on my network and can and do print to any one of them from any computer.  The drivers for the R1800 allow printing on 8 1/2" x 11" sheets only from Linux .. however from Windows it can print from 17" x 45" down to 3" x 5" and will seal the photos for archiving.

So .. go for it!!  Whatever will do your work.  Just remember a cheap printer is just that, CHEAP.  They die at an early age regardless of the usage!

### Post by lucyi on 2011-07-26
