### Post by Sorivenul on 2008-12-14
Without knowing whether or not it's enabled, my guess is it is Compiz (Desktop Effects) causing the issue, especially if you computer is more than a couple years old.  Try disabling them, then running your game. 

The path to change Desktop Effects settings is:
System > Preferences > Appearance
Then select "Visual Effects" tab, and select the "None" or "Disabled" option.

If this doesn't resolve your issues, you may enable the effect again if you wish, though it may improve your overall performance to run without them.
