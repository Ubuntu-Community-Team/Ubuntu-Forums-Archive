### Post by lucasl on 2008-11-08
Just go System->Administration->Printing, type your password and click new.
The printer should appear on the list on the right.
As for driver choice, I don't see that exact model listed, but you could try a similar one or just a generic postscript printer.
Or if you are really skilled, you could find a PPD file on the Internet or in the windows driver.
