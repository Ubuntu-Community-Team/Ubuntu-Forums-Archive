### Post by stylintile on 2013-05-17
It's called a panel in all of the distros I have used.  Do you still have the top panel?  If so, right click on the top panel and click on new panel.  It should automatically come up on the bottom, then just right click on it and add the items that were on the old one.  If you don't have either panel, then I think the terminal command was either
```
gnome-panel &
```
or
```
gnome-panel &&
```
I can't remember exactly as I am now running Xubuntu and Lubuntu.

Good luck
