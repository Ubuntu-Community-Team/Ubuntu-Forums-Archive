SliTaz has its own tool for making a bootable USB, called tazusb: 

[http://www.slitaz.org/en/doc/manuals/tazusb.en.html](http://www.slitaz.org/en/doc/manuals/tazusb.en.html)

Another option (independent from the SliTaz project) is Unetbootin:

[http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/](http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/)
