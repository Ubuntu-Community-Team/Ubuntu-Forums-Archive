### Post by mrfotheringham on 2009-06-22
I have been trying to install a printer since switching to Ubuntu 9.04.

I have posted threads requesting help for both HP Deskjet F380 and ip1900. Received lots of advice and thanks to all.

I have concluded that my F380 is bust after CUPs okay and HPLIP installed - as it would scan but not print.

Revisited ip1900 installation tonight. Googled PPD files and results included the Polish ubuntu forum. Used Google translation and followed advice:

Installed cnijfilter-common 3.00-1 and cnijfilter-ip1900 series 3.00-1 i386

Printer now works.      :KS to the polish forum.
