### Post by Maheriano on 2010-02-03
Ubuntu doesn't use an APPLY button, you have to click CLOSE. My suggestion would be to either tab down to where you think the CLOSE button might be, then use ENTER to hit it. Or right click the window on the task bar, click MOVE and then use the arrow keys on the desktop to move the window up enough to click CLOSE.
