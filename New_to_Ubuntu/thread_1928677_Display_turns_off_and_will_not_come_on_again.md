### Post by JSinMT on 2012-02-20
I recently installed ubuntu 11.10 on an old desktop computer. When the display turns off, it will not come back on again. I have to press the reboot button on the computer to restart it in order to have the display functioning again. Right now to save the trouble I have set the Screen settings to not turn off the display at all. What do I do?

---

### Post by JSinMT on 2012-02-22
I figured out how to get it to work. I simply uninstalled gnome-screensaver and installed xscreensaver instead. Works great now, and I even have screensaver options.
