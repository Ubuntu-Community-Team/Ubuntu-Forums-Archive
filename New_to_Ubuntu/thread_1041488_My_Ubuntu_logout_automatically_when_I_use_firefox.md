### Post by HotShotDJ on 2009-01-16
I agree with your sources -- this is likely a video card issue that is causing the xserver to crash and restart.  I'd almost be willing to bet that the pages you have open include some flash objects in them.  What video card do you have?  Also, carefully observe what is contained on those pages that you are viewing when the restart happens.  While the crashes may appear random, careful observation may very well reveal a pattern.
