### Post by ManamiVixen on 2013-03-20
512MB of ram is too small for Ubuntu. You have to use Xubuntu or Lubuntu for a smooth system. Cause at the moment, it looks like Ubuntu compensated the low ram with lots of swap and to get the needed memory, most stuff is loaded to swap and that is waht is slowing your PC. Xubuntu and Lubuntu will not have that issue. Also, it's best to use a 32bit OS on less than 4GB of physical ram. 32Bit OS'es use less memory when compared to 64Bit for obvious reasons.
