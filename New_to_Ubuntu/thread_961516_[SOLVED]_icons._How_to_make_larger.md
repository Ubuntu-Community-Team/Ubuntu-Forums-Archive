### Post by Therion on 2008-10-28
> **lentomies said:**
> WHAT? :confused: There is no suck option.
Open a folder.  In the upper right corner there is a drop-down box for viewing options (View as List, View as Icons).  Next to that box there is a (+) sign and a (-) sign.  The (+) enlarges the icons or text size, the second one (-) reduces them.
