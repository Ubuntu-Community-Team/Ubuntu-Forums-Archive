### Post by nathansoz on 2008-11-03
Here is what I would recommend. If the windows partition is useless to you, wipe it out. Let ubuntu do its thing with guided partitioning for the whole disk. Make sure you have no other things interfering with your bios boot process (eg. other hard drives, floppies).
