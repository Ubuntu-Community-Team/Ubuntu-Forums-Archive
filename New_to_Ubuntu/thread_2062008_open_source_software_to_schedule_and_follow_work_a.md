### Post by einonm on 2012-09-24
It sounds like you may need something that works with gantt charts - In which case 'planner' (available from the Ubuntu Software Centre) would meet your needs.

If you're looking for a web frontend to a ticketing-style system, which planner is not, then OTRS may indeed what you need (although I have no experience of this package). Alternatives to this are mantis, bugzilla and roundup.

Without knowing any more details about your needs, it's hard to recommend one. I suggest taking a look at the packages above in more detail.
