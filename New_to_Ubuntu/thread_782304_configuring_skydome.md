### Post by Tatty on 2008-05-04
Go to the compiz config settings manager then browse to "Desktop Cube->Appearance".

Expand the skydome section on the bottom, click the browse icon next to the Skydome Image option, then select your image.

You might also need to Check the option above it (Render skydome)

### Post by marty1011 on 2008-05-04
Hi there. Go to System---> Preferences----> Advanced Desktop Effects Settings. Then click on Desktop Cube (you have to enable it at first though). After that click on the appearance tab and at the end you will see the skydom option. Click on it to get additional options and select your image by browsing. Hope this post helps.
