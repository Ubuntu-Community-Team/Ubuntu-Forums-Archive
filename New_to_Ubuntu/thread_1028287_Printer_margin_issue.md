### Post by balaknair on 2009-01-02
To adjust the margins, open the printing settings(System Menu> Administration> Printing)--> Right click on the printer you want to configure--> select 'Properties'--> 'Job options'--> scroll down to see 'Text options'--> now you can adjust the margins
