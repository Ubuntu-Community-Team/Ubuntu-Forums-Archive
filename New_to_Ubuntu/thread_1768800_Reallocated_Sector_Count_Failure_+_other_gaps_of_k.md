### Post by Rubi1200 on 2011-05-27
Hi and welcome to the forums :-)

First, save as much data as possible, backing up to a medium you know to be good.

That warning means the drive is failing and probably won't last much longer.

However, to get a second opinion go to the website of the disk manufacturer and see if they have a utility available for download so you can check to verify whether the drive is failing.
