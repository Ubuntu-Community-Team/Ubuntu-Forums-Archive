### Post by caravel on 2009-04-29
That card is a relic.  Get at least a 6xxx or 7xxx series card.

Here in the UK you can get a brand new 7300GT or 7300GS for around about £30.  They are low end but ok for what you want.  If you go for anything earlier you will inevitably be stuck having to fiddle about installing earlier drivers.  With a 6xxx or later series card you should only have to enable the proprietary driver in Ubuntu.
