### Post by XCan on 2009-08-31
What you show is probably one of those thermal pads that sometimes come with OEM boxed coolers. They do the job, but of course fancy thermal pastes are better. However, know that the temperature difference between the 'worst' and the best pastes are in the order of 5 degrees C. Your cooler shouldn't be wobbly, it should excert enough pressure downwards that you can't move it without some force.
