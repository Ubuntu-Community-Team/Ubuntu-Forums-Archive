### Post by Eil on 2008-04-25
The reason most people see a performance increase on a fresh install has little to do with the upgrade path. Instead, they're usually seeing the result of all their nifty (but often performance-expensive) customizations disappear like toolbar applets, settings, desktop icons, and so on. Of course on most Linux distros, there's a concerted design effort at keeping things manageable so the difference is far less than a couple of other operating systems I could name.
