What I want to do is install either Windows 7 or some older version of Ubuntu (because my printer doesn't work on Karmic). Dual booting is too complicated for me so I want to have just the new system. However, I don't want to lose my data, and burning everything to DVDs would take away too much of my time (and too many of my DVDs).... ### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2010-01-10
Yes. Boot the live CD and use Gparted to resize your partition and make a new partition of sufficient size.

Then move your data from your current partition to the data partition.

Confirm your data is intact.

Then install whatever OS you want.

Honestly moving your data to a new partition is as complicated as dual booting, LOL.
