### Post by Zill on 2012-05-21
F9 toggles the Side Pane in Nautilus.

If that doesn't make it visible then try dragging the left-hand border to the right with your mouse - it may be just that the pane has shrunk too much to see it ;-)

---

### Post by gpeck157 on 2012-05-21
Thank you. I appreciate your response. I did try your suggestions to no avail. I did solve the problem though. Click System>Preferences>File Management>Behaviour Tab> Then uncheck "Open Each Folder In Own Window." For the life of me, I don't know how it got checked in the first place, but be that as it may.
Again thanks for your assistance and the assistance of Daveth.
