### Post by audiomick on 2010-01-14
First thing to check is that it isn't (in printer properties) set to black and white and has no black ink left. That is not necessarily the problem, but it is always good to exclude simple things first.

Just make sure that the printer settings in windows and in Ubuntu are the same, so you now that you are comparing equals;)
