FFmpeg is probably the big daddy of converters/encoders. But it can be quite complicated. Most of the scripts that people use work thanks to ffmpeg. 

My advice would be to google for scripts for each specific conversion, or get your learning hat on and hit the ffmpeg man page or look for a guide.
