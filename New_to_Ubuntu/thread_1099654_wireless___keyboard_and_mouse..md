### Post by Hospadar on 2009-03-18
I suspect it's just in issue with the keyboard syncing to the reciever.  If it doesn't work even in the bios menu, or if you can try it on a different machine, then the problem is your keyboard.  Take a look at the instructions, often these things require a strange and specific sequence of button pressing.
