### Post by karthick87 on 2010-12-25
Goto System>>Preferences>>Screensaver and untick **lock screen when screensaver is active**

### Post by uRock on 2010-12-25
Hello and welcome to the forums.

To disable the screensaver lockout, go in your menus to **System> Preferences> Screensaver**, then **untick** the box that says, "**Lock screen when screensaver is active**"

Cheers,
uRock
