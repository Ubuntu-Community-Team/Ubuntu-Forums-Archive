Don't know about disabling the keyboard, but if you have a USB port, have you tried plugging in a YSB keyboard and seeing if that works?

General problem using USB keyboards is that they're often not detected until after the OS has completely loaded, making selection of GRUB options (PRIOR to the OS being loaded) impossible.
