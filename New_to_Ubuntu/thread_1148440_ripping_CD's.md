### Post by Niniel on 2009-05-04
Don't convert from wma, rip the CDs again, especially if you want flac. Flac is lossless and wma is lossy (I think there's also a lossless option, but since you used the Zune software, I doubt it was used). Regarding flac though, I compared that to a wav rip the other day, and there was not much of a difference - some tracks were significantly larger in flac vs. wav, and some were smaller, but the entire album was almost the same (flac was a bit smaller overall).
The best ripping & converting software I know is [Easy CD-DA Extractor]("http://www.poikosoft.com/"). It runs on Windows only though (I cannot get it to work with Wine under Ubuntu, been trying since 7.10 :) ) and is not open source or free, although you can test it for a while (maybe even indefinitely). I haven't really seen anything comparable in the Linux world regarding wealth of options, supported formats and ability to fine-tune.
