### Post by egalvan on 2009-02-24
This is stand-by power.

It is related to 'sleep', 'hibernate', 'wake-on-lan' and other
functions of the motherboard.

Some mobo bios can disable this... most cannot.

You need to disconnect the power supply to cut it.

use a power strip, or switch-controlled outlet.

BTW, this is very low power.
But still power being used.
