### Post by tacantara on 2009-11-09
You can make a backup of your Evolution mail files and settings (File > Backup Settings).  The file saves as a .tar file.  I usually place it on my desktop or move it to a USB flash drive until I need to restore (File > Restore Settings).  I've done this a few times, and find that it is a fairly quick and painless process.
