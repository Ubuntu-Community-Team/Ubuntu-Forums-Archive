### Post by hansolo4949 on 2012-04-29
Pick a random project you would like to do with Ubuntu, such as setting it up as a HTPC, and do it! the best way to learn Ubuntu is through experience, and although the tutorials can be useful, the knowledge doesn't really stick in the long run compared to actual experience. What fun is it sifting through manuals when you something goes wrong? It is a far more rewarding experience to actually "get your hands dirty", perhaps with an older machine you have lying around. I have learned 99% of what I know about Ubuntu (and linux in general) by making mistakes, and spending entire afternoons trying to correct them (who knew you couldn't shut off a computer while distro update is running (several years ago)).
