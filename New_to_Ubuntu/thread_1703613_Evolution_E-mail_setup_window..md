### Post by dnairb on 2011-03-09
I'm guessing that this is on a netbook or other small-screen box. This is one of the problems with large windows/small screens.
IIRC, if you hold left <ALT> and left-click anywhere on the box you can push it above the top of the screen until the button you are looking for appears bottom left.
