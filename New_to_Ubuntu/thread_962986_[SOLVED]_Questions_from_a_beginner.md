### Post by randy78 on 2008-10-29
1. If you can hover the mouse over the icon, you can click it once with the scroll wheel (use as a button) to open the file or directory.

You can also set Nautilus to open files or folders with one click:
Open your home folder then: Edit>Preferences>Behavior>Single Click To Open Files

2. Right-click on the desktop shortcut you want to modify>Properties>Click the picture of the icon
A dialog box will open and will allow you to select the icon you want :)
