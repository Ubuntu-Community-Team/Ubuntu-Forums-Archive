My most recent printer purchase was Hewlett Packard. The utility/driver hplip is installed in ubuntu by default as HP support linux. It worked out the box. The gui that is needed for ink levels etc is not but can be easily installed, hplip-gui.

Other manufacturers that are supported well are Epson and Canon.

You may want to refer to the open printing database if you decide to buy a new printer.
