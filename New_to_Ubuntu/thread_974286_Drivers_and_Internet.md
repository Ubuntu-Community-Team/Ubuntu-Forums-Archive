### Post by cariboo on 2008-11-07
If your hardware is supported, the drivers will be loaded automagically. Once you have finished the  installation, go to System-->Administration-->Hardware Drivers. It will tell what restricted drivers are available for your peripherals.

Restricted drivers are non open source software. So they can't be included on the LiveCD.
