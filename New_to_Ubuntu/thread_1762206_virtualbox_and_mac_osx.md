### Post by rosencrantz on 2011-05-18
Virtual systems on a netbook is tough. Just for some perspective: I'm using a virtual Windows on a 64-bit 2.4 GHz i5 with 4GB RAM, and performance-wise it's usable but not glorious.
I'd play a bit with the memory allocation in Virtual Box for both CPU and graphics. 
If you have 3d effects enabled for either your physical or your virtual system, turn them off.
