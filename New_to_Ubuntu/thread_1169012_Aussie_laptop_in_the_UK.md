### Post by Didius Falco on 2009-05-24
Hi,

Yes, it's the same voltage, the difference, as you've already seen, is the plug itself.

If it was going to hurt it, it would have fried it up nicely when you first plugged it in. I did that when I first moved from the US to Oz. 36 hours of traveling made me dumb enough to just plug it in without changing the switch on the power supply. I was wide awake directly after that, though!! <G> 

You might want to get him to invest in a better quality adapter if he's going to be there for long. Otherwise, just keep an eye on it - those adapters can get pretty hot.
