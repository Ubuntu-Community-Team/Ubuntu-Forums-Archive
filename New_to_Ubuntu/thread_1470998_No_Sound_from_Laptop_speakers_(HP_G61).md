### Post by donkyhotay on 2010-05-03
My first thought upon hearing that someone has sound out of headphones but not speakers is that something is wrong with the speakers. Since you are dual-booting do you get sound out of the speakers while in windows? If you do I would check the sound output in preferences and see what they are set for.

//edit: just noticed your post that you get sound on win7, in that case take a look at the sound preferences and see what output is set for then post it here.

### Post by TheTeamFromMars on 2010-05-03
Thanks donkyhotay...

I thought I'd already tried that (about 20 times) but I guess I didn't try hard enough. How embarrassing ;o) 

The 'problem' was on the Output tab in the Sound Preferences - I just changed the "Connector" dropdown from "Analog Output" to "Analog Speakers". So simple... 

With your expert advice I now have sound through my laptop speakers! 
So thanks a lot.

Thanks for your help.
