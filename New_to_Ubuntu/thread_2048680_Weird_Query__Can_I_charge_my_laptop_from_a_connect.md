### Post by Paqman on 2012-08-27
What sort of connection? USB? If so, USB only puts out 5V, and not much power. The charging circuit on your laptop is probably 12V (it'll say on the sticker on the bottom), so even if you did bodge up a cable it's likely it'll refuse to charge from a 5V source, and even if it did it would be *really* slow to charge drawing current from a USB port.
