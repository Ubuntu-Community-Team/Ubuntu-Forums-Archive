### Post by howefield on 2013-02-10
Open the Dash by pressing the Super (Windows) key and start typing Thunderbird, after a few characters the Thunderbird icon will appear.

Once launched you can pin to the launcher by right clicking on the icon in the launcher and selecting lock to launcher.
