### Post by obaid on 2009-03-02
if it is included in ubuntu repos, then simply "sudo apt-get remove amsn" and then "sudo apt-get update" and then "sudo apt-get install amsn" this way you will get updated version of amsn (if availble) through package manager.

if it isn't availble through package manager, then download the package/source from amsn website and install/build.

but why not useing pidgin, since amsn doesnt include voice and video, other functions of pidgin and amsn are similar. pidgin has various plugins to enhance it and support various protocols.
