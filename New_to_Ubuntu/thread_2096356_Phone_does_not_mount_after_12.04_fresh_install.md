### Post by cwsnyder on 2012-12-19
If you can still make your Android phone connect to the outside world as a USB memory device, you can still mount as a USB disk, but Android is turning away from acting as a USB disk to MTPfs, which allows you to still use your phone while it is connected, but doesn't allow your phone to be simply mounted under Ubuntu.  You need to look at [http://forum.xda-developers.com/showthread.php?t=1977812](http://forum.xda-developers.com/showthread.php?t=1977812)
