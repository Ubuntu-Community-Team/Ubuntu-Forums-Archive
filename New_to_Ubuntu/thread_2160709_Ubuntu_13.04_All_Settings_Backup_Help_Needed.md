### Post by Traxster on 2013-07-08
Hi Folks,


Ok, here is what I did, very simple:lolflag:  Just copied the config files from the old hard drive, for each program, e.g (firefox, thunderbird,) and moved them to their respective locations in the new computer. The files were located inside /HOME/USER. You may have to hit ctrl H to show all hidden files. After doing this, all my addons, plugins, and bookmarks appeared instantly.=D>
