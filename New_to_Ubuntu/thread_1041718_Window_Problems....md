### Post by Son of William on 2009-01-16
It looks like your screen resolution is 800x600.  That's pretty low.  System > Preferences > Screen Resolution will tell you what resolution you are at and also let you change it if your monitor/video card supports it.

The CompizConfig Settings Manager on my rig has minimum width it will set to.  It looks to be about 900 pixels wide.  If your resolution is indeed 800x600 there is no way you will get it on one screen.

### Post by JoshuaRL on 2009-01-16
If you have a problem setting your resolution higher, then it might be the driver you're using.  Go to System->Administration->Hardware Drivers and see if there are any proprietary drivers available for your system.  If so, enable and reboot.
