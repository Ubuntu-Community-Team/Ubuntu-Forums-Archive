### Post by bobbob1016 on 2009-07-19
First, don't bump so quick.  Usually 1 time a day, not 30 minutes later.

Second, do you mean moves as in rotates?  Press Alt+F2, then type "metacity --replace &" without quotes (under gnome Ubuntu's default) and press enter.  Once the screen is done flashing open a terminal and type ccsm (install it via the directions it gives you if you don't have it installed).  Look for "Rotate Cube" and change the keybinding to something else, it might be set to rotate if Button 1 is pressed, Button 1 is the left mouse button.

If you have a video camera might be an idea to record the action and put it on youtube or something.
