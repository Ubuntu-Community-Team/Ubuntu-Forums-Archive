### Post by him610 on 2024-11-19
In Xubuntu which I am using, one can increase/decrease the size of the icons without increasing the overall scaling of the screen. 
How to on Xubuntu: 
Mouse click left on screen background from which appears a dropdown list, mouse down to Desktop Settings, mouse click right,  window appears titled Desktop. There are three tabs in upper part with one being Icons, right click on it - page appears where you can adjust icon size.

It could be that you can increase the size of your icons in Ubuntu, you just need to discover how.
