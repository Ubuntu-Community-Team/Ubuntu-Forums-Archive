### Post by Locke_99GS on 2009-12-03
Yes you can, the exact same way you would on any other computer.

The Netbook Remix is basically just Ubuntu with a specially modified environment to fit on the smaller screens of Netbooks, and some optimizations. It is not required over the conventional full blown Ubuntu, but you *may* prefer it.
