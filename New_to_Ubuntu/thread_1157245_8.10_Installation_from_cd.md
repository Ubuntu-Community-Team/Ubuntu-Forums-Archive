### Post by panda1983 on 2009-05-12

---

### Post by Dynaflow on 2009-05-12
Make sure you burned the installer CD as a disk image, not just as data.  If you've made sure of that, go into your BIOS settings to make sure your boot-order is set so that the CD- or DVD-ROM drive comes before your hard disk.  (This is assuming you already have an earlier version of Ubuntu installed on your hard drive that's getting booted instead of the CD).
