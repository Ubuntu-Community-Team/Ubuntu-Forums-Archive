### Post by ghostborg on 2009-03-31
Short answer- None.
Cinelerra-very involved but good, kdenlive(crashes all the time), Kino-very basic.
I have not tried it but some people are using Blender.
All the rest are either dead or not usable.
Kdenlive seemed the best but I had no luck with the constant crashing.
I personally keep a Winblows machine just for video and pictures.
I would not waste your time with using video editors in a Virtual Machine.
I do not know of any that work under Wine either.
