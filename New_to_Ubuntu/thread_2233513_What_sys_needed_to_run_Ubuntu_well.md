As for the P4, I have a minimal install with xfce4 (the desktop environment used by Xubuntu) on my P4 3gHz machine and sweet. That is 12.04. Try Xubuntu 12.04, slightly lighter. Supported until 2017.

You can create your own spin from the minimal of very light window managers, file managers, desktop managers, login managers, apps, desktop environment, whatever. ### Post by grahammechanical on 2014-07-09
You do not mention the video card. You are not alone. Many people fail to see the importance of the graphic adapter in regard to getting a good user experience with a modern OS. My Nvidia GT220 is now outdated but it has 1GB of it own memory. And I get a good user experience with Ubuntu 14.04 on this machine with an Intel Core 2 duo and 1GB RAM. I do not think that the user experience would be so good if the video card had less memory.

Can the video card do hardware acceleration? That is another question to ask. Now that XP is no longer supported many people are thinking of Linux. The CPU and the RAM on their machines might be sufficient but what about the video adapter?

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/SystemRequirements)
