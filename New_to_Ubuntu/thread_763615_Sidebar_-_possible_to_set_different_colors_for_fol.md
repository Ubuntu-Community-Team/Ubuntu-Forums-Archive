### Post by natirips on 2008-04-23
(I'm not 100% sure this what you're looking for but...) Try right-clicking the folder you want to change it's icon, than click properties, than click the icon. You can now chose whatever icon you want.

P.S.: Most icons are stored in /usr/share/icons directory.

### Post by Golem XIV on 2008-04-23
> **sunbird said:**
> Thanks for the tip... I actually know how to do that already, I'm wondering how to (a) change the color of icons and (b) download more icons. Any ideas?

I don't think you can change the colour of the icons without changing the icon files themselves, but you could try Gnome Colour Chooser (gnome-color-chooser, it's in the repos).

To download more icons check Synaptic (search for "icon" or "theme", or visit [Gnome Art]("http://art.gnome.org/"), [Gnome-Look]("http://www.gnome-look.org/") or similar websites.
