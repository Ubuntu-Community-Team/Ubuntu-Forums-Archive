### Post by ankspo71 on 2009-10-12
Hi, 
Open VLC , then click on 'media' then 'services discovery' then enable all of the types of stations you want. Next, open the playlist and you will see the radio listings. You can also copy a url from a radio stream from a website, and paste it into 'Advanced Open File' > 'Network'.
Hope this helps,
James
