### Post by DuckHook on 2019-04-10
There is nothing wrong with those specs. Still perfectly usable. Frogs Hair's suggestion of Lubuntu is excellent. For further insights, see the link in my sig: "Resurrecting Old Hardware".

TBH, sometimes the best use for lovable old clunkers is to use them as CLI servers. It's perfectly usable as a file, backup, print, torrent, media, vpn, etc server. Depends how comfortable with the command line you are, but once the GUI is eliminated, they take on a new life.
