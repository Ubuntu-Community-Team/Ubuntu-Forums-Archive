I set up a laptop with lucid...how do i make the wireless go on automatically on startup? this laptop does not leave the house and always uses the same wireless router.

---

### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-08-27
Right click on the network manager applet in your panel and choose "Edit Connections".

Now click on the wireless tab and choose the connection you are on, and click Edit.  In the next window that appears select the boxes top and bottom left for "Connect automatically" and "Available to all users"

That should do it.
