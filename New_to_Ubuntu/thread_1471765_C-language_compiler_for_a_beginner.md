### Post by switch10 on 2010-05-03
If your son is 12, and is new at programming, I would recommend starting him out with scratch, a program that computer scientists developed at MIT especially for kid's that teaches basic program structure, and many other valuable programming skills.  I recommend downloading some of the examples, and look at how some of the simple programs are created.

I showed this to my 13 year old brother about a year ago, and now he makes his own games!!  Pretty impressive!

here is the download link.  [http://info.scratch.mit.edu/Scratch_1.4_Download](http://info.scratch.mit.edu/Scratch_1.4_Download)
