### Post by su:bhatta on 2013-07-18
If you want a medium weight OS that wont tax your system, I suggest you give Xubuntu 13.04 a try, it runs Xfce DE which is lower in system resources than Ubuntu's stock Unity DE...
You can download Xubuntu ISO from here:   [http://xubuntu.org/getxubuntu/](http://xubuntu.org/getxubuntu/)

Also, I suggest you get both Ubuntu 12.04/13.04 & Xubuntu 12.04/13.04, try both out without installing...
When you boot with the iso, you will be given an option to TRY/Install , you first Try and use a live session to see how your computer responds...
Depending on that you can go for installation of the OS that suits you  !!!
