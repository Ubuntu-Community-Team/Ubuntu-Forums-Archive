### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-02-02
You can't run apps from other OSs (Windows, OSX) directly in Ubuntu, but you can install WINE and run SOME Windows apps inside WINE.  But the success varies by individual app and version.  Suggest you check the app db at codeweavers.org for the apps and versions you want to run.  Link is below:

[http://www.codeweavers.com/compatibility/]("http://www.codeweavers.com/compatibility/")


Unless you see a Platinum or Gold rating, you're wasting your time with WINE.

Can't comment on OSX, don't use it.
