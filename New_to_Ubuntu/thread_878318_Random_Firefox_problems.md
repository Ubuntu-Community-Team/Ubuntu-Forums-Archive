### Post by Mr_Freez3 on 2008-08-02
bmann11,

The dropdown menu problem is with Adobe Flashplayer Plugin 9, not Firefox...I think it might be resolved in version 10, which is in beta testing now.  But I tried the beta, and it's not stable enough for me in everyday use.  Check out this link:  
[http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer10.html](http://labs.adobe.com/downloads/flashplayer10.html)

The problem with the text not rendering correctly is probably due to your text size setting in Firefox Preferences.  I tried that web page in your screenshot and it renders correctly here.  Go into your "Fonts & Colors" Advanced section in Firefox Preferences >>Edit>Preferences>Content>Fonts & Colors>Advanced  and check your values for "Size".  For example, mine are 16...for Proportional, 12...for Monospace, and None...for Minimum font size.  I had the same problem a few months ago, and font size fixed it for me (because I had changed those settings...lol).

And finally, your last problem.  I don't have an answer, but I have a suggestion that will save you some screen space.  Check out Compact Menu 2 add-on here:
[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/search?q=Compact+Menu&cat=all](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/search?q=Compact+Menu&cat=all)

If you add that, and move your throbber at the far right down to the end of your address bar, you can eliminate that whole Menubar via >>View>Toolbars>Customize... just a thought.

Hope this was of some help to you.
