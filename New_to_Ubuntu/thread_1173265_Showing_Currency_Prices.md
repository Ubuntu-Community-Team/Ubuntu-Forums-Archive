### Post by lukeinvancouver on 2009-05-31
OK here's the solution to your problem and I'll post it even though I think you are not even checking. But maybe others will find this useful.

1) Open a gmail acct
2) Click on 'sites'
3) Create one.
4) Create a page.
5) Insert an image (being the currency cross you wish to monitor).
6) Hit F-5 to refresh

Or use any server for your page. Load the page in a "New Window" in Firefox and downsize it to the size of the graphic. Then you'll have it available on your desktop. All you need to do is hit F-5 to refresh.

Hope this helps.
