### Post by Hospadar on 2009-08-13
If you have an icon for it, you can right-click, go to properties, and there should be a field called "command" or "target" (in kde right now so can't say for sure) and you can append the -opengl option to the end of the command (with a space between what's there and -opengl) or just copy/paste what you have in your post here.

If you make a new shortcut, just use the command above as the launch command.
