### Post by I2k4 on 2011-04-03
Good advice above about wireless mouse and USB drive.  

You'll find a netbook fits perfectly into a large plastic freezer bag, which weighs nothing and waterproofs/dustproofs it.  I carry mine in a backpack pocket instead of a case, so I line that pocket with ordinary plastic bubble wrap for protection - again weightless.

Another tip, around the house and even on travels it is often more comfortable to have a lap pad.  I use a 1/2" cheap ($2) three ring binder, which weighs almost nothing and creates a small incline for the netbook keyboard.  I use the three rings and a little alligator cip to guide the power cord and also the cord to a set of speakers near the armchair, so I can listen to good sound instead of tinny netbook speakers.
