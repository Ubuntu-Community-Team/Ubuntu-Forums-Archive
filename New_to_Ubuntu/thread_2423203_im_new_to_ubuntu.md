### Post by deadflowr on 2019-07-19
It's a tit for tat situation.
Nobody can give help or advice without more information.
Such as how did you install the drivers?
What drivers?
What is the specs for the machine?

For what it's worth, Ubuntu comes with a help section built in.
Just click on Activities to access the menu system and type Help.
There is a section for installing drivers there.
It's the exact same as this page:
[https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/index.html]("https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/index.html")
For even more in that link you can click on the community help section and search things beyond the basics that the Ubuntu Desktop Guide gives you.
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CommunityHelpWiki]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CommunityHelpWiki")
