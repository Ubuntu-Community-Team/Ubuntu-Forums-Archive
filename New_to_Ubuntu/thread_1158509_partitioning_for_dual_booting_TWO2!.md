### Post by Yvan300 on 2009-05-13
It's according to your needs. Root requires about 10 gb and the rest is for your media. So what you could do is to format a partiition using your available free space to fat32. fat32 can be written to and read from by linux and windows. Thus you can store your media on that partition to be used by both windows and ubuntu. 

So acooring to what you want you'll know how much to reduce the partition by.
