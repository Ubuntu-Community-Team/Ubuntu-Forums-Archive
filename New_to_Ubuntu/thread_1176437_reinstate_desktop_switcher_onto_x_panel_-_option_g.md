### Post by John M on 2009-06-02
> **Duke Togo said:**
> how do I get the desktop feature back as an option on the panel?

Right-click on a blank part of the panel you have (if you have no blank spot, r-click on a hide button) In the drop down menu select "add to panel", then pull the toggle on the right of the next menu down to the very bottom and the work place switcher should be there.
