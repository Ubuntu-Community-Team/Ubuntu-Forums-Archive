### Post by hanzj on 2009-01-02
If any of you have the opportunity, download the 30 day trial version of Rationale onto a Windows computer. It is quite good. If you want to read about "The Rationale for Rationale", please see the developer's transcript at [http://lpr.oxfordjournals.org/cgi/reprint/mgm032?ijkey=GFHrAQMNkJ09woP&keytype=ref](http://lpr.oxfordjournals.org/cgi/reprint/mgm032?ijkey=GFHrAQMNkJ09woP&keytype=ref) .

I'm quite impressed with the program. The program is really helpful for all thinking people, or for people who want to think better and more clearly and would like to take advantage of external aids (external to our brain).  

I wish we could have a Ubuntu/Linux team working on making a program as good as Rationale. I'm willing to put in my two mites in contribution.
