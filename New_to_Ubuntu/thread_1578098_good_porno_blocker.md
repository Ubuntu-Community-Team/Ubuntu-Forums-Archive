### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2010-09-20
Opendns + dansguardian will do most of what you want. No application will work out of the box without some modification as every parent has somewhat unique thresholds for what is tolerable.

See : [http://blog.bodhizazen.net/linux/web-content-filtering-made-easy/](http://blog.bodhizazen.net/linux/web-content-filtering-made-easy/)

You can use squid as an alternate, but squid will take more time to configure the blacklist.
