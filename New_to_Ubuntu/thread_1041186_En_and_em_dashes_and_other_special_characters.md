### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2009-01-16
When I type two hyphens in OO (as in "--") it automatically replaces them with an em dash (or an en dash.  I don't know the difference).

If that's not working for you, I'd look at setting a compose key.  System > Preferences > Keyboard > Layouts Tab > Click "Layout Options" 

After you set a compose key, I believe a em dash is <compose key> + ---
And an en dash is <compose key> + --.
