### Post by lisati on 2009-05-14
"It depends....."

If your setup is ]your-computer]->LAN->[other computer]->[internet] he'll have to set up internet connection sharing on his machine. 

If the setup is [his computer]->[lan]->[internet] you shouldn't need to access your friend's computer to access the internet.

Either way, it's a good idea to get your friend's blessing before using their connection.
