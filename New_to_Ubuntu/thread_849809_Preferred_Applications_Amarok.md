### Post by whitethorn on 2008-07-04
try typing in a terminal 

whereis amarok

it'll give you a couple different locations of where the program is situated.  
Take the first one.  It should be something like

/usr/bin/amarok

and done.  At least I hope.

### Post by lazyart on 2008-07-04
right click on an mp3 and select properties, then open with.  If Amarok isn't in the list, add it.  Then select it explicitly.  All of your mp3s will now default to Amarok.
