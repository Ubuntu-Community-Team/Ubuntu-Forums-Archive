### Post by forger on 2008-08-26
It's not that hard with pidgin either:
- menu Accounts > Manage Accounts > Add 
- Protocol: IRC
- Username: yournicknamehere
- Server: irc.ubuntu.com
(optional: Advanced > check "Auto-detect incoming UTF-8")
- Press OK and check the new IRC account in the Accounts window
- When it's connected, from the "buddy list" window go to Buddies > Join a chat, choose the IRC account and type a Room (channel), i.e. #ubuntu
