### Post by bumanie on 2009-02-01
Nearly all HP's work well with ubuntu. HP have a dedicated program for supplying linux drivers with their products (at least printers). If you want to try to get your canon going look [here]("http://www.logicgamesonline.com/jdmetz/debian/canon-i250.html") - it may help. Re HP there is a self extracting installer available or if you have the HP printer plugged in during an ubuntu installation, it is automatically installed.
