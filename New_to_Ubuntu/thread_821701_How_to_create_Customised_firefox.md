### Post by st33med on 2008-06-07
First off, you REALLY need to change your nick. You could get spammed because you have your email out in the open.

Second, that is easy: Just go to [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/") and choose plugins and themes there.

EDIT: Also, make sure they work with your version of Firefox. By default, in Hardy Heron, it is FF3b5.
