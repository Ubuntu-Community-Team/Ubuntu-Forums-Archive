### Post by tea for one on 2022-01-03
I bought this one (SKU: ADA1327) years ago (and I'm a bit surprised that it is still available).
[https://thepihut.com/products/bluetooth-4-0-usb-module-v2-1-back-compatible](https://thepihut.com/products/bluetooth-4-0-usb-module-v2-1-back-compatible)
```
Bus 001 Device 008: ID 0a12:0001 Cambridge Silicon Radio, Ltd Bluetooth Dongle (HCI mode)
```
I just tested it with Lubuntu 21.10 and successfully played a music track via a bluetooth speaker.
