### Post by snova on 2008-12-08
There aren't any, at least none that I know of. There are only the applications that come with a KDE install- KMail, Akregator, and so on.

Besides, Kontact has a very different idea of a plugin than Thunderbird. A "plugin" in Kontact is an complete, standalone application. Kontact exists only to wrap them together into one window.
