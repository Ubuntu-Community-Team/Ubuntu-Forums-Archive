### Post by peter d on 2011-11-12
Everything in the drop down menu can be found via the Dash including Shut Down/ Press the Super (Windows) key to open the dash, type shut down and click on the Shut Down icon (or simply press Enter). Log out works in the same way.

Can't think why the Gear wheel icon is missing. Was it there when you booted from the CD/USB into the live session? Yuo could try resetting Unity in the terminal.
