### Post by SeijiSensei on 2010-11-09
If you're running 10.10 (or maybe 10.04 as well), right click in the System Tray (the area with the small applet icons in the panel) and choose "System Tray Settings."  Check the box for "Network Management" and click OK.

You'll now see a new applet appear in the Tray. Clicking it should show you entries for both your wired and wireless connection.  Double-click one of these to set up and establish a connection.
