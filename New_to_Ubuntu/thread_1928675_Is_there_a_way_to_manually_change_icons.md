### Post by Frogs Hair on 2012-02-20
Yes , you can go into the icon folders in home .icons , file system/usr/share/icons , or pixmaps and change icons . If the icons are in usr/share/ icons or  pixmaps you will have to open the file system as root to make changes.  

When making changes I go to the scalable folder . Icons in pixmaps are usually not in folders.  Example: Icons > Faenza > Apps > Scalable.
