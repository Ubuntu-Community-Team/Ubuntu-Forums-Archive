### Post by silverglade00 on 2010-09-07
Unfortunately, many companies do not release drivers with a license that allows Ubuntu to distribute them with the CD. Fortunately, you can install them if you can get a wired connection working. Just plug a network cable into your laptop and into your router. Then run System-Administration-Hardware Drivers. It should give you an entry for Wireless driver. Select it and click activate. You can also activate any video card drivers you may need in here.
