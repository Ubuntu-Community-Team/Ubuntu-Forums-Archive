First, take a look at this:
[http://www.kubuntu.org/getkubuntu/download#desktop-dvd](http://www.kubuntu.org/getkubuntu/download#desktop-dvd)
> Do not be confused, even though DVDs can hold far more data than the typical Kubuntu CD, **the main benefit of the DVD downloads is to get access to all of the available language packs**. Most people will be fine with the standard CD installer.

To verify exactly what language packs are available, look/search through the package list on the DVD. Here is what's on Kubuntu Lucid Lynx (10.04)
[http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/kubuntu/releases/lucid/release/kubuntu-10.04-dvd-i386.list](http://cdimage.ubuntu.com/kubuntu/releases/lucid/release/kubuntu-10.04-dvd-i386.list)
