### Post by theozzlives on 2009-03-06
Go to [http://www.gnome-look.org]("http://www.gnome-look.org") search for and download one you want. Go to System > Administration > Login Window > Local Tab. Click Add and navigate to the *.tar.gz file for your login screen.

### Post by unrealfighter on 2009-03-06
Go on System - Administration - Startup Manager. Under boot options, you can choose to change the resolution of the boot screen. The resolution of the actual login window, in my case, is the same as that of the desktop.
