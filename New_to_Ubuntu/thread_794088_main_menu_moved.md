### Post by RiceMonster on 2008-05-14
If it's locked, unlock from the right click menu, then select move (also from right click), drag it back to the other side and lock it again. 

Or, you can make a new one at the right side and delete the other one. right click > add to panel >> main menu. delete the other one by right clicking and selecting remove from panel.
