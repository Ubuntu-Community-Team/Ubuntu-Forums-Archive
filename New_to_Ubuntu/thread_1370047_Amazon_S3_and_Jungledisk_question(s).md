### Post by Temposs on 2010-01-01
Jungledisk is well-integrated with Amazon S3 and Rackspace Cloud Storage. It is developed by the Rackspace company, and thus will use Rackspace Cloud Storage by default for your storage. If you want to use Amazon S3, you have to register your Amazon S3 account with Jungledisk.

I have tested Jungledisk on Windows and Linux and it works well. It says it works well on MacOS as well, but I haven't personally used it with MacOS.

Jungledisk does charge a flat fee per month, depending on which edition of Jungledisk you purchase. I think the lowest rate is $2-$3 per month.
