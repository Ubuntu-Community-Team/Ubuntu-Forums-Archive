### Post by dvlchd3 on 2010-05-12
It really depends on your preference.  The netbook edition is basically meant to get Internet applications up and running quickly, and a different graphical layout to expedite this process.  I believe the graphical interface also displays better on the smaller netbook screens.

The desktop version is geared more towards personal computing.  The only real difference is the graphical layout.  Either version will run on your laptop.
