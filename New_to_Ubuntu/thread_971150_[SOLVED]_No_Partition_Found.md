### Post by louieb on 2008-11-04
Laptop hard drive are usually in a polished metal case about 2" x 4" x 3/8" thick.  Most laptop hard drives are a FRU (field replaceable unit) and have pretty good instructions on how to remove and install. 

Just Google your laptop model and see if you can find the service manual.  I've been able to find the manual for most of the laptops I've had to open. 

Probably not the drive itself that loose,  Bet you will find a thin flat cable that came loose from its connector (done that myself)
