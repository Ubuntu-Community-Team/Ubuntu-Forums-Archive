### Post by 3rdalbum on 2012-12-21
Vsftpd is if you want to start your own FTP server on your computer. I don't think that's what you want.

Ubuntu has a built-in FTP client. From the desktop, go to the first menu at the top of the screen and come down to "Connect to Server...'.

This will allow you to enter in the details of the FTP site and connect, and drag your HTML files in like it was a local disk.
