### Post by SoFl W on 2011-08-06
Something I notice with a few installs I have done.  You just need to know where it might be in the menus.  Goto 
System-> Preferences-> Main Menu
and start looking through the menus for the program.  If you see it, uncheck it, check it, move up once and back down.  Exit out and see if it appears.  Don't know why but when a program didn't appear in my menu I did that and it was now in the menu.
