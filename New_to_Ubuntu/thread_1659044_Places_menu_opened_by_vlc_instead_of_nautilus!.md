### Post by endotherm on 2011-01-03
1) create a new folder on your desktop
2) rclick teh folder and select "Open with ..."
3) select "open folder" or "nautilus" from the window, and make sure that "remember this setting" is checked. 
4) click ok
5) delete your new folder, and test that everything works.
