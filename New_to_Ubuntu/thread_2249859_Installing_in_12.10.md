### Post by Impavidus on 2014-10-25
Ubuntu 12.10 is 2 years old and passed its use-by-date half a year ago. That means its repositories have been taken offline, so the software centre will no longer be able to download software from the repositories. I recommend new users to try version 14.04, which will be supported until 2019.
