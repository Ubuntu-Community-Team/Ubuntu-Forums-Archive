### Post by halitech on 2009-02-08
on most new installs I do, first thing I do is all updates

1. install build-essential and restricted-extras
2. aMSN (prefer it to pidgin and I only use msn)
3. ffmpeg, manDVD and Devede
4. bittornado for torrents
5. k3b for burning

this works for me for general use and my movie work I do
