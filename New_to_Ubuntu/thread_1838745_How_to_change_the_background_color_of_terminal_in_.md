Go to colors tab and Uncheck the box to ‘Use colors from system theme’, then choose the background color.

### Post by vasa1 on 2011-09-05
It's possible that the confusion is arising because OP is using Unity. In that case, OP has to hover the mouse cursor over the top most bar (at the top of the screen) somewhat to the right of the little Ubuntu logo on the extreme left. At this point, you'll see "File, Edit, View, Search, etc". Then, without moving the mouse cursor too fast or out of the horizontal, click on whatever you want. In this case, it is **Edit**.
