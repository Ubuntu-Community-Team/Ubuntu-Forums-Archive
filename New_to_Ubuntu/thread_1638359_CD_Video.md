This may be a "dumb"question but can CD's playback Video Formats at DVD resolution? Or have playback at all?

---

### Post by qamelian on 2010-12-05
If you means CDs containing video in VCD or SVCD formats, then no, by definition they cannot be the same resolution as DVD video. If you mean DVDs containing video files, such as AVI, MKV, etc, then the answer is yes it is possible, depending on the codecs used for encoding the video, the duration of the video, and the other quality settings used.
