### Post by nickleboyblue on 2011-09-15
Number 1 cause of this: Low screen resolution.

Useless hack:  alt-click the window to grab it from anywhere, even if it isn't a handle.

They make screens wide and short these days, especially for laptops.  Best way to counter your problem is to not get a screen that's too short or to change to a higher resolution, which may require proprietary drivers.
