### Post by ubume2 on 2009-09-21
I was able to enter key shortcuts in System--Preferences--Keyboard Shortcuts, but they did not respond

I entered shortcuts in metacity just like I did successfully in Hardy Heron, but they didnt work in Jaunty.

I tried xbindkeys but got strange responses when entering urls, doing google searches, entering commands from cli.  I entered Alt+f to open firefox,Alt +x for xterm,etc........ Some older tutorials indicated that this would work.
Edit:
I went to metacity keybindings and reentered them.  I went to System--Preferences--Appearance--Visual effects, and changed visual affects to none from normal, and the keyboard shortcuts work.

Update November 21: After a reinstall of Jaunty, I can get keyboard shortcuts to work, if I put them in System>>Preferences>>Keyboard Shortcuts and Visual effects set to normal
