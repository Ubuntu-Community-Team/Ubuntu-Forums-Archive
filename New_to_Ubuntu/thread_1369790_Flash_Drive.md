### Post by candtalan on 2010-01-01
If your flash drive was created in a way to be 'persistent' it will have an ability to write into its own home folder (I think). However, from the apparent size of your drive seen I do not think yours has persistence. In this case when you want to save a document or data you can choose 'save as' then use the navigation to use a drive of your choice, which might be the native Windows area or could be another USB drive used for your data.
