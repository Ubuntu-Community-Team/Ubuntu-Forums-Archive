### Post by Wataru8675 on 2010-01-24
Sounds like your processor or something is overheating.  If you're in a cold area, try opening a window and trying to install it near the window to help cool the computer down.  You might also be able to use a fan and  aim it so that it goes into the computer (there should be slits somewhere where heat can escape).

Those are both makeshift solutions.  If you go to an Officemax, or Bestbuy, or anything of the sort, they also make computer cooling fans.  They look like small bricks with fans on them that you put under your computer and helps keep them cool.  I'd recommend investing in one of them if your computer ever overheats.  They're only about $10~20.
