### Post by Frogs Hair on 2011-10-23
One possibility is to open the main menu and select the section with the application . Right click on the line with the application , and enter properties .

When the properties dialog box opens click the icon picture and travel to a folder where you have placed an icon you want to use .

You may have to remove the ? mark launcher from the the panel first , change the picture and put it back . I hope that works for you .
