could be a zillion things.

reason you where asked to restart the computer is to _duplicate_ the problem - then start changing things up a bit to see what factors, if any, cause you to encounter the problem. is it just that specific picture every time? does it happen randomly? does plugging the drive in after boot affect it? does trying to access it as root affect anything? can you view it in a different image viewer? et cetera. as soon as one of those is answered in a way that breaks the pattern, a solution will be apparent.

common-everyday-happens-all-the-time problems ('i just installed ubuntu, and youtube doesn't work') can usually be fixed in 10 seconds.

this is not one of those problems - more information is needed ;)
