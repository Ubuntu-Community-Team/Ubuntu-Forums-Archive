### Post by wilee-nilee on 2010-08-10
There is a f key prompt for choice of boot, it should tell you on the screen what it is, on starting up, along with the f key for bios. Some computers use different keys; f12 is my choice of boot and f2 is the bios key. As soon as you start you computer look for the screen information or click on f12 to begin with several times until you get to the choice of boot. 

If you trying to install lucid as soon as you choose the usb to boot then hit the shift key until you see the language choice and the the options for using the ISO.
