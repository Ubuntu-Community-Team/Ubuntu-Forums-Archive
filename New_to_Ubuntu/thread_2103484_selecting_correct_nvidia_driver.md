### Post by grahammechanical on 2013-01-10
I have installed on my computer two copies of Ubuntu 12.10, one copy of Ubuntu Gnome Remix, and two copies of Ubuntu 13.04 development branch. In the past I have installed as additional operating systems Kubuntu, Xubuntu, Lubuntu, Edubuntu and Ubuntu studio.

Dual, triple or quadruple booting will not slow down the operating system that we have booted into.

Nor, will installing additional desktops and then booting into them. These things just take up disk space.

If you wish to remove a version of Ubuntu I suggest you examine Ubuntu Secure Remix.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuSecureRemix]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuSecureRemix")

It has a utility called OS-Uninstaller that may be useful.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/OS-Uninstaller]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/OS-Uninstaller")

Regards.
