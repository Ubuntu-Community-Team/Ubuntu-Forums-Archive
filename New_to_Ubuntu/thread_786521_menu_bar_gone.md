### Post by cwgatling on 2008-05-08
If you press Alt+F2 and run the command 'xfce4-panel', you'll get your menu back. Right-click on it and you can add something that your icons will minimise to (taskbar equivalent, I forget what it's called). Otherwise Alt+Tab will get you back you your minimised apps.
