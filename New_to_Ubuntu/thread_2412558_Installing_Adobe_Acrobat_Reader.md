### Post by Impavidus on 2019-02-14
Gimp can handle pdf, but it will convert it to raster format and single page (it's after all an image manipulation programme). I never tried opening pdf with libreoffice or openoffice, I don't know how well they work. There's evince (also known as document viewer) and okular (on Kubuntu, installable on other flavours with a lot of dependencies). Inkscape also handles pdf and of course the bare-bones but fast and reliable gv.

Not all of them support editing pdfs, if that's what you want. After all, pdf was never designed to be editable.
