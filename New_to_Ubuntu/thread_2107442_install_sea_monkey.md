### Post by xycris on 2013-01-22
It depends on your preferences.

Sea Monkey comes with a builtin email program, address book, calendar and more. However, you can do that with separate programs too like a dedicated web browser, email (has address book) and a calendar organiser.

There are tons of other browsers, but the Mozilla ones (Firefox and Sea Monkey) are the more flexible with lots of addons.

You can try others like Opera, Chrome, Chromium, IceWeasel and more.
