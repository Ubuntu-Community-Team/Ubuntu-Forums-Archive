### Post by S.R on 2010-06-23
You don't need a direct hit to get a power surge. Sound's like you are going shopping for a TV, router, and modem. I would also recommend buying a high quality surge protector power strip and making sure the circuits your electronics are on are grounded. When I am at home I still unplug things during a storm though. If it gets to expensive you might want to check to see if your home owner's policy will cover any of your expense.

Best of luck.

### Post by LowSky on 2010-06-23
People dont often think about them but cable and telephone lines do have electric currents, and even a small spike can ruin the electronics they are hooked up to if these lines are not properly grounded. Lke S.R said check your home owners policy to see if they cover lightning damage.
