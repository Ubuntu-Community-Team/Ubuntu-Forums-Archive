### Post by flanagan on 2009-01-22
In Synaptic Package Manager, you can highlight that language pack (openoffice.org-l10n-en-za), then choose Package --> Lock Version. 
I suppose you could also just uninstall that particular language pack and never be bugged again for updates to it.
