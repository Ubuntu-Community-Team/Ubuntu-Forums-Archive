### Post by Hospadar on 2009-07-30
I think really the only way to tell would be

a) plug the monitor into a different computer: if problems persist, then the monitor is at fault

b) plug a different monitor into the box and see if problems persist: if so, then video out is the problem.

If you don't have another machine or another monitor, I'd grab a free CRT off craigslist or salvation army.  They're practically free nowadays as nobody wants them anymore.  I'd be shocked if you couldn't find one for free or >$10.
