### Post by mattismyname on 2008-07-27
I don't know if that is possible (someone else more authoritative will have to answer).

Problem with your proposed method is that you'll lose quality on the songs due to double-compression.  (First being compressed by itunes and second being compressed when you rip them from the cd).

Back when I accidentally purchased a couple albums from itunes what I had to do was find some software that would just remove the DRM from the files.  I would search around on the web for a utility that will do that.

Of course, the legality of doing such a thing is debatable and so probably shouldn't be discussed on this forum.
