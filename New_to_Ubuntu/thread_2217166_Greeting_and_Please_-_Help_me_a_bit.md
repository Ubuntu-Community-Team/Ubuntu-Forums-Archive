### Post by papibe on 2014-04-15
Hi Luke_Sargent. Welcome to the forum ):P

Start installing the basic packages: the [LAMP]("https://help.ubuntu.com/12.04/serverguide/lamp-overview.html") stack (Linux + Apache + MySQL + PHP/Perl/Python). Read about it here.

Then, you will need something to speed up your development like a content management system. I recommend taking a look at [Wordpress]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WordPress") and [Drupal]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Drupal"). You need to choose one. WP is simpler and you can get things running faster. Drupal is a bit more complex, but it allow you tons of functionality though its dozens of extra modules. 

Hope it helps.
