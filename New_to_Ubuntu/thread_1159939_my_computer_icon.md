ALT + F2
gconf-editor
> apps > nautilus > desktop

Mark the icons you want to appear on your desktop.

### Post by hobo14 on 2009-05-15
You mean like a My Computer icon in windows?

If you only want an Ubuntu Computer icon like in the places menu, you can just drag one out of the menu onto the desktop.

If you want a My Computer like windows you'll have to make one (I made one for my girlfriend when she switched from XP)
Make a folder on the desktop called my computer.
Go through your file system from /, and just use the middle mouse button to drag any location you want to access into the MyComputer directory, choose make link.
I put her home, documents, desktop, a copy of Places>Computer, and two mounted disks she uses for storage into her MyComputer.
Then I just changed the icons of MyComputer and the stuff inside to look like what she's used to....
Result: one happy ex-windows user.
