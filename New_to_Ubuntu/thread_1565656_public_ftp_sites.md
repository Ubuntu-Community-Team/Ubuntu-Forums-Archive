### Post by roger_1960 on 2010-09-01
Hi

I suggest you use dropbox (or ubuntuone if he is using ubuntu)

OR

Get some webspace for yourself - most ISPs offer a small amount for nothing.  Almost always you can ftp files to and from it using filezilla or similar (this is how you create and update websites) and your dad will be able to access the files as well if you give him the logon and password.
