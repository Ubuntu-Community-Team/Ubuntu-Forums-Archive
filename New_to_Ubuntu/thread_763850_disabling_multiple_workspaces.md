### Post by sayakb on 2008-04-23
Do you have the workspace switcher applet? Right click on it and select Preferences and reduce the columns to 1.

ALTERNATIVELY

Open CCSM (System->Preferences->Advanced desktop effects settings), goto General Options->Desktop size and set the Horizontal Virtual Size to 1.
