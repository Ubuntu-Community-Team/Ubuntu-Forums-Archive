### Post by xpod on 2009-03-02
When i try and delete photos directly from our cameras/cards the generally end up in some hidden trash can upon the card itself.
Try hitting CTRL+h with the camera folder open to see if you have a hidden trash folder in it.I have to delete the pics from there or delete them through the cameras themselves.
