### Post by avtolle on 2008-06-16
Try the older version and see if it boots; I do not think using the older version of the kernel will necessarily be to your disadvantage at the present.

Since a kernel update was included in the updates you downloaded, you will need to recompile and reinstall your graphics card driver, if you did so manually before.
