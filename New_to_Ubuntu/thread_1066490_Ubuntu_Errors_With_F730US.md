### Post by yeats on 2009-02-10
You'll find with a quick glance at the forums that each of the issues you mention are common and fixable, though some are much more frustrating than others.  What kind of wireless card do you have (which brand and model, that is)?

The white screen issue may just be the live CD - since it runs in RAM it is slower than a hard disk installation will be.

There are several "suspend" related bugs - my wife's computer has this issue too.  I haven't fixed it yet, but it's not a showstopper.

If I were you I would go for it and install Ubuntu.  If you don't like it you can always delete the partition and reinstall the Vista bootloader into the MBR.

Good luck :-)
