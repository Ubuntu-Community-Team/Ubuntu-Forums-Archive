### Post by seawolf167 on 2011-04-01
If the keyboard shortcut is a Ubuntu-shortcut, you can reapply it through System -> Preferences -> Keyboard Shortcuts. If the shortcut is a Compiz-shortcut, you can reapply it by going to the particular entry you wish to reset to default and clicking the little "broom" on the right side of the entry.
