### Post by JKyleOKC on 2010-02-28
At the top of each forum page there's a menu bar that includes "Search" and if you pull down that choice, at the bottom of the list is "Find all your posts." Click that and after a short time you'll get a page that lists all of your posts in all areas, most recent one first. You can scroll through from page to page until you find the thread about your RAM...

I use this from time to time, when I remember about posting something but don't recall the details.

Another useful tool is under "Quick Links" on that same bar: "Subscribed threads." Whenever you post to a thread, you're automatically subscribed to it. This list shows all threads to which you're subscribed. Sometimes that's faster than going through your list of posts...
