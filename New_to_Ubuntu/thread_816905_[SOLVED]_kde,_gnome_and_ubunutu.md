### Post by aeiah on 2008-06-03
gnome and kde are desktop environments that sit on top of ubuntu. by default, ubuntu uses gnome. kubuntu is a variation of ubuntu that uses kde instead of gnome. the other popular desktop environment is xfce which is more lightweight and is represented by the xubuntu version

have a look at gnome-look.org, kde-look.org and xfce-look.org for a quick glance at what you can get them to look like (or go to their official websites for a deeper look). its usually best starting with gnome since its the default, but its not a big deal really
