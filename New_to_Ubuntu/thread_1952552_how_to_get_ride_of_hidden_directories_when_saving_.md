### Post by yetiman64 on 2012-04-04
I am not familiar with python shell, however most "save as" windows on the programs I've used have a context menu (right click menu) in the folder section of the Window. Right click anywhere in the folder section and either select or deselect the "Show hidden files" option.  Edit: in your question you would deselect (detick) it.

Also, you are correct, the period (.) is for hidden folders and files.
