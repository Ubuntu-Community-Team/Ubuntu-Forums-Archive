### Post by superdav42 on 2009-10-14
You want to see the counter strike entry in the [winehq appdb]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=version&iId=3731"). It has many tips in there about getting counter strike to work in Linux. It not 100% supported but many people have gotten it to work well. I think you your case changing the sound driver to ALSA will help. Just run winecfg then click the sound tab and change it to use ALSA. 
Good luck!
