### Post by ibuclaw on 2008-06-15
Yes, that is a perfect question to ask! :)
OK, back on track.

So, you'll want a mail client with direct access file-storage as standard, and has the ability to send links to friends accounts to directly download that uploaded file/access that folder, am I right?

One fresh new email client that you might be interested in is [GMX]("http://www.gmx.com").

They offer a webdav connectible file storage utility (simply open nautilus and type in **davs://storage-file-us.gmx.com** in the title bar to access it, then it's just drag and drop with files into the webdav share).

There's a lot more useful features that I don't want to get bogged down into (someone might think I'm an ad).

But have a small tryout with it, and let us know what you think. (I'm very happy with it).
