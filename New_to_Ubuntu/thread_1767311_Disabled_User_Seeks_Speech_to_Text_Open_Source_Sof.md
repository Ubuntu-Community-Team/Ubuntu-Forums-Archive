### Post by deconstrained on 2011-05-25
A quick and dirty but free solution would be (if you have a Google account) to use Google Voice. Send a voice mail message to yourself and then the transcription shows shows up in your email, copy the text out. In my experience however, Google voice has made some hilarious mistakes when transcribing.

Other than that I'd recommend setting up Windows in a VM and using Dragon NaturallySpeaking, which is the best there is, even though it's not free.
