### Post by Michael.Godawski on 2008-11-28
I rather hesitate when it comes to recommend a linux/ubuntu book. The linux world changes so fast, that often the newest books are outdated the day they are in book-stores.

What commands do you want to learn? The best way is just by doing. There are many internet sites dealing with this topic:


[LIST]
[*][http://linuxcommand.org/](http://linuxcommand.org/)
[*][http://www.pixelbeat.org/cmdline.html](http://www.pixelbeat.org/cmdline.html)
[*][http://www.oreillynet.com/linux/cmd/](http://www.oreillynet.com/linux/cmd/)
[*]many more just search for them ( if I may suggest a new search engine because the big brother is eerie :[Wikia]("http://re.search.wikia.com/index.html") )
[/LIST]
I would use the internet this time although I am a book lover.
