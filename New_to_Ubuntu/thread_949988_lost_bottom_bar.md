### Post by matdombrock on 2008-10-16
Ok, no big deal!

Just right click on your top panel and choose "New Panel".

then grab that panel and drag it to the bottom of your screen (if it isn't already there.)

right click on your new bottom panel and choose "Add to panel".

add "show desktop", "windows list", "trash", and "workspace switcher".

then your good to go!
