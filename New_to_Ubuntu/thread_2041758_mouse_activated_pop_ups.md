### Post by lukeiamyourfather on 2012-08-13
> **ajgreeny said:**
> What do you mean by "mouse activated pop-ups"?  I use 10.04 with firefox, but I am not at all sure what it is that bothers you.

I think the original poster means the inline advertisements on some sites. If you hover over certain words with the mouse they bring up advertisements. For example the word "server" in an article will show a Dell ad if you hover over the word with the mouse. Very annoying for sure. I'd imagine if you prevent them from working there's a lot of legitimate content you'd miss too (they are probably JavaScript). Check for a plugin, somebody has probably created one.

[https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/)
