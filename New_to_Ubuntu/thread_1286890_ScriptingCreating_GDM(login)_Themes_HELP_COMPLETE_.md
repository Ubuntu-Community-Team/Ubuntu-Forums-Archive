### Post by VCoolio on 2009-10-10
See above, copy a theme to your desktop so you won't need root permission to mess around with it and install gdmthemetester so you don't need to logout / -in every time you want to check the results of your changes. Also you may want to know that tweaking gdm is not possible (at least not in this way) in karmic, so if you want to enjoy your work in the long term, don't bother too much with gdm.
