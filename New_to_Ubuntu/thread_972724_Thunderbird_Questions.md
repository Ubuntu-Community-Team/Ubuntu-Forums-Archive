### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-11-06
You may be able to achieve the notification with Mail-Notification.

Install it (v5.4 in Intrepid repo or on getdeb.net for Hardy).
In Prefs - set the message popups to never expire.

It may get annoying to have to click on every pop-up whenever a mail comes in though.
