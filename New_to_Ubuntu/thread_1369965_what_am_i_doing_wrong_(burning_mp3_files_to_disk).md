### Post by fromthehill on 2010-01-01
> **rburkartjo said:**
> cheese yes i used brasero and did that but still didnt work
I'ts possible the cd player is broken. but probably not

an audio cd can only hold 80 minutes of music. it doesn't matter what quality or how big the mp3's(or other music format) are.
if it burns more than 80 minutes without warning you're probably burning a data-cd.

if you use the cd/dvd creator under accesoiries and drop the files onto the cd it is not going to create a audio cd
you should use the brasero burner under 'Sound & video' and click on the first button 'Audio Project'
when dropping files on the cd you should see 'project estimated size: XX:XX min' on the bottom bar
