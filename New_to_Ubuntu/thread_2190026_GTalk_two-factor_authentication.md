### Post by jCjQszKMO5JH on 2013-11-25
What do you mean that you're not given the option to use an application-specific password? If you go to your 2-step authentication settings you should be able to create an application specific password, then use that password to login to the account in Empathy. An application-specific password is just a randomly generated password for your account that bypasses the 2-step authentication.
