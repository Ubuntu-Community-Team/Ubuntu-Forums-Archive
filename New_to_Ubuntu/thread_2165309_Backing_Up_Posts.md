### Post by Bucky Ball on 2013-08-06
Yep. Well, you click on the URL in the right pane to get to the actual webpage. If you double click the topic itself you will get the stored, offline, 'snapshot' of the page. So, as mentioned, no: if the forums go down you're stuff is safe on your hard drive. But in the form of Zotero 'snapshots'. 

Could work well for you and is, in effect, a searchable database. Just put in a word and it will find all corresponding entries (great if you only know the author but not the publication or you want to find all Ubuntu forum pages with 'minimal install' or something). You can also save PDFs with an entry (even adding one on your hard drive or anywhere else) so if you want to build a collection of metadata under a heading, all good (deals with tags, notes, etc. ... I love it, obviously!). ;)
