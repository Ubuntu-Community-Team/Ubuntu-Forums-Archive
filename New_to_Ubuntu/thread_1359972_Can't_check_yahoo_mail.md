### Post by coffeecat on 2009-12-20
@tanics, I use Yahoo Mail. There is no problem. When you get to the bit about it not being tested on your OS, do not choose Classic. Tell it to open your mailbox in the normal way (I can't remember the exact prompt) and it will open just fine.

This is simply Yahoo being cautious. They've been redesigning all their web pages for months now and, because there are so many versions of Linux, they can't test them all. They're just covering themselves if things don't work in your particular combination of browser and OS.

Once you've opted to open mail that way, you won't be prompted again, unless you delete the Yahoo cookies, or until the Yahoo cookies expire.
