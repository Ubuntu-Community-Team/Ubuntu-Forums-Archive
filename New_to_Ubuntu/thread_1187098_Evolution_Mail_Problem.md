### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-06-14
Check to see if evolution is working online.
Bottom left corner you should see a connection icon. If it's disconnected left click on it and it will reconnect to online. Or select file in the menus and the third option from the the bottom should say "Work Offline" - if it says "Work Online" then left click on it and it will go online.

If your online then possibly your account is disabled.

edit/preferences
Make sure there's a check mark next to your account in the box labeled enabled.

Also, you may wish to check and make sure your account information is correct.
edit/preferences/highlight account/select edit from right side.

I'm assuming you have a network connection = internet browser works?  

Hope this helps.
