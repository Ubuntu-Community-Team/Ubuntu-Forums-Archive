This is the one I like to use.  
[http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/My+AER-OS-XK+Pidgin+Theme?content=65364](http://www.gnome-look.org/content/show.php/My+AER-OS-XK+Pidgin+Theme?content=65364)

In order to replace them, you have to sign in under sudo nautilus.  Be careful.  Sudo Nautilus is like a gun.  It can do what you want it to do or shoot you in the foot too.  You have to go into the file where it lists and copy over the icons there.  Its not as easy as changing your normal icons.
