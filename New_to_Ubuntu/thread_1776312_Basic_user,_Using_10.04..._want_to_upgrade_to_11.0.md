### Post by Drate on 2011-06-05
I hate to not give a direct answer to your question, but there is one important thing we need to know:

Are you intending to UPGRADE from 10.04 to 11.04 or are you intending to wipe your hard drive clean and REINSTALL with 11.04?

If the former: then you need to follow an upgrade path, the easiest of which being through the regular update manager utility.

If the latter, then yes, I can see where the disc not booting would be problematic.  Try re-downloading and re-burning the disc.

Elsewise, what is it doing if it's not booting the CD?  Do other CD's boot?  Is there any screen output you can give us to help us understand the nature of the problem?

As regards where to start with Ubuntu, start in this forum, as specific questions: How do I do ______?

This is called the Absolute Beginner forum because we anticipate ABSOLUTE BEGINNER'S to be the ones asking questions, EVERY question.  Not sure how to open Firefox?  Ask us.  What are all those pretty icons for?  Ask us.
