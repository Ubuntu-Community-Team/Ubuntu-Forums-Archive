### Post by no2498 on 2011-11-20
the drivers are built in to the kernels for web cams
if your on a lap top you may need to click the fn+f4 keys if you have the fn key to turn the cam on
open a terminal type  dmesg click enter
after it stops type lsusb click enter
should list your cam and a number
close the terminal
now install guvcview
try the cam in guvcview
