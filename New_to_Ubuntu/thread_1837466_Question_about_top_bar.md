### Post by jfed on 2011-09-02
I have never used Unity before but if you're using 10.04 or 10.10 I can tell you how to do such.

First you'd want to essentially delete your top panel, most of this will be done through right clicking on the panels.

The on the bottom panel, where it already shows your windows, you can right click and select "add to panel" then find the Ubuntu Menu, where it shows all your applications.

Then after that, you're going to want the Notification Area and the Date and Time, so right click and select add to panel, and add those two as well.

You can right click and select "move" to move around certain items on the panel to adjust it how you like it.

Hope this helped
