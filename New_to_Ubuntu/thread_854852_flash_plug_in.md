### Post by drs305 on 2008-07-09
For firefox, Flashblock is probably what you are looking for. To tweak the settings you go to Tools, Addons, and then look at the extension you want. In that section there is usually a Preferences button to modify and tweak that add-ons settings.

There is also the Toolbar Buttons add-on which provides a Flash On-Off button you can put on your menu.
