### Post by addiebk on 2008-04-29
Okay, so a friend of mine helped me with my computer, and here's what he did:

Open the synaptic packet manager
In the settings menu, click "repositories"
There's a tab that lets you add or remove third party repositories
Under that tab, I had two repositories.  I deleted both and the upgrade worked.

Try that, and see if it helps.  :)
