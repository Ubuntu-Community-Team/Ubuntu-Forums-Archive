### Post by blueridgedog on 2009-05-07
When I first plugged in my iPod I was given a choice of programs to launch...I clicked other and entered the path to Floola and it has come up ever since when I plug in my iPod.

### Post by blueridgedog on 2009-05-07
You can also change this, with the iPod plugged in, select places -> computer, then right click on the iPod and select Open With, then Open With other Application, then select Floola or enter the path to Floola in the location provided.
