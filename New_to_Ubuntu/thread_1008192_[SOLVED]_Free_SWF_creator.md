### Post by Sirron on 2008-12-11
Some older versions of Flash work in Wine, check to see if the version you want is supported [_here_]("http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=23"). "Platinum" means the application works flawlessly, gold means it works with a little messing about or has a few bugs, silver means there's more bugs etc.
