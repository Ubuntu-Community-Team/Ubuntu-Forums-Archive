### Post by zkriesse on 2009-12-29
While on your main screen with no apps open hit ALT+F2 and then in the window that pops up type the following, (without quotes of course!). "update-manager -d" That will bring up the Update Manager but it will display the next available distro for you. Hope this helps you out!
