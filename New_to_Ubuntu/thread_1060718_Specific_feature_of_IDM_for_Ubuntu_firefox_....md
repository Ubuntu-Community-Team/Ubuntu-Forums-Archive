### Post by Pollox on 2009-02-05
There's a number of add-ons for firefox that provide specifically this feature (helping you download a video embedded on a site).  For instance, DownloadHelper can add a button to your toolbar for this purpose.  Additionally, since you already have DownloadThemAll, you can open it while viewing a page with an embedded video, go to the pictures and embedded tab, and search for the video file, although that's a bit more work than DownloadHelper and similar add-ons make you go through.
