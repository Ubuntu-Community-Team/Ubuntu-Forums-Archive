### Post by LowSky on 2009-06-04
9.04 64bit will not be an issue, I have a HP laserjet 1018 and it works like a champ. use HPLIP (in the repos) and all will be fine

Just so you know Laserjets are horrible at printing pictures when compaered to a inkjet, and lasjet cannot print on photopaper. the toner will just smear off.

toner is expensive, but keep in mind they last alot longer and dont go bad, like inkjet cartriges do.

usually laser jets are less prone to breaking, my office laserjet prints thousands of copies a month and has only broken down once in 5 years, keep in mind it only a black and white, adding color adds  more parts that can break. I wish our copier was as resistant. LOL

My best suggesiton is to get a black and white laserjet and get a cheap inkjet for color/photos, and buy HP, they seem to be the best out there for linux support.
