### Post by daslinkard on 2012-04-12
When looking to purchase a webcam for use with Ubuntu, you should look for a UVC compatible camera. The [Linux-UVC]("http://www.ideasonboard.org/uvc/") project has a good list of UVC compatible webcams.  The list is pretty good, but I'm not for sure what your definition of cheap is, maybe $30 or it might be $100.
