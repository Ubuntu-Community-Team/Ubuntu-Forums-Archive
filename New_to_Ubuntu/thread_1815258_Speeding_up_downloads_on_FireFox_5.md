### Post by cariboo on 2011-07-31
What broadcom chipset does your laptop use? I have several broadcom devices, and they all work quite well. If you have a BCM4312, you may have to try both the b43 and bcmwl drivers, as there seem to be different versions of that chipset.

### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-07-31
Download speeds also depends on the server you are downloading from. However, you can increase your downloads speed by using [DownThemAll]("https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/downthemall/") extension.
