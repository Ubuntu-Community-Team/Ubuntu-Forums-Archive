### Post by bomanizer on 2009-01-17
Sounds like the music database on the iPod is gone. I've noticed that this happens when you plug the iPod on some other music app that the one you used to upload the music, f.ex. Banshee, Rhythmbox, Amarok...

I have a 6th generation iPod classic and I NEVER plug that on any other app than Rhythmbox on my Ubuntu. (or on whatever the initilal uploading was done)

I would use an iTunes on Windows to reformat the iPod, then plug back to Banishee and upload music. This is my way to go on those 6th gen. models, if you have an earlier model, then I think you don't need this, not sure though...
