### Post by RealPSL on 2008-04-29
Technically your trash is just a hidden directory in your home directory. do the following.

Click Places > Home Folder on the top menu
On the resulting File Browser window click View > Show Hidden files

Double click on .Trash 

Note the leading period. Your stuff should be there provided you did not empty.

As for restoring the trash icon you can right click the panel > Add to Panel > Scroll to Desktop and Windows and select trash which should restore the icon on the panel.
