### Post by AndyM3 on 2010-10-15
Did you try the "standard procedure"?
1. Plug the modem in.
2. Wait a minute or so.
3. Click on the NetworkManager icon in the notification area.
4. Click on "New Mobile Broadband Connection".
5. Pick your country, network and plan.
6. Done.
If it doesn't work, what make/model is it?
