### Post by jimmy the saint on 2009-11-09
You can just install Ubuntu without first "clearing" the drive.  Just tell the installer to use the whole disk and it will reformat it.  If you are paranoid about the NSA inspecting your drives to see what was on them before, you can use a tool like Deriks Boot'n'Nuke first.
