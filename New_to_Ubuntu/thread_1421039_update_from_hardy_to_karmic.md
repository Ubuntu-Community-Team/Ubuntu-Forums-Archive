Easiest way, IMO, is to download a karmic desktop CD and install. You would otherwise need to upgrade 8.04 -> 8.10 -> 9.04 -> 9.10 

Lots of potential for problems and lots of bandwidth.

Another option is to wait for Ubuntu 10.04, just 2 months or so away, which will also be LTS, and at that time you should be able to go direct 8.04 -> 10.04
