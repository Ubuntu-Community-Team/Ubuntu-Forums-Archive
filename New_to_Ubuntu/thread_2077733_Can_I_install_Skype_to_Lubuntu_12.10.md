Hello and Welcome to Ubuntu Forum :)

Thanks for choosing and using Lubuntu, you will never regert that ;)
I'm one of Lubuntu Team members and I can confirm that you will find Skype and you will be able to download and install it after you are done with Lubuntu Installation so no worries.

Menu > System Tools > Synaptic Package Manager

From Synaptic

Settings > Repositories > Other Software - Please check the attached screenshot.

Once you click "Canonical Partners", click "Close" then on the main screen of Synaptic, click "Reload".

After that, just type Skype on the search box of Synaptic and hit Enter.
You will find it right there sitting and waiting for you :D

Right Click on Skype and choose "Mark for installation".

Then, on the main screen of Synaptic, hit Apply.

After that, Skype will be under Menu > Internet > Skype.

Enjoy and if you need anything, please let me know :)
