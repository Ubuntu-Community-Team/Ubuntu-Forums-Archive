### Post by coffeecat on 2008-08-27
I could be wrong, but I think DSL is only available for the x86 architecture which won't work with the G3's PPC CPU.

**clipeuh**, I think you have a double-whammy with the G3 - very small amount of RAM and you need a distro compiled for the PPC. [Yellow Dog Linux](http://www.terrasoftsolutions.com/products/ydl/intro.shtml) is the distro one usually thinks of for the PPC, but looking at that website, I think it only caters for more powerful hardware.

It might be worth searching [Distrowatch](http://distrowatch.com/).
