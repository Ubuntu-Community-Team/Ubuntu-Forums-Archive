### Post by jrlii on 2009-08-19
Ubuntu 9.04 works well on my AcerOne.

The screen is pretty small (`9") and the resolution modest (1024x600.)

It does have a feature missing in lots of the teensy notebooks:A VGA socket, so you can plug it into a real monitor. The resolution still only runs 1024 wide, but can be 4:3 proportions as well as wide screen. Oh. It can use the built-in screen and the external in a dual-head setup. A little weird, but you don't necessarily need a 22" monitor for your tool pallets &c.

No optical drive, but it has two SD card sockets and three USB ports, and came with a little bitty mouse to go with the little bitty netbook.
