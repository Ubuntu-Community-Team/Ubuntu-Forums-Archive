### Post by dark_harmonics on 2008-05-18
Do you know the manufacturer of your wireless card? You can find out in vista by going to the device manager. Wireless support is one of those things that i had a hard time with under ubuntu. I think its mostly due to the hardware venders not opening up support for linux. Check out ndiswrapper. It can allow you to load windows INF files in compatibility with ubuntu. Maybe this can help : [https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SetupNdiswrapperHowto?action=show](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/SetupNdiswrapperHowto?action=show)
