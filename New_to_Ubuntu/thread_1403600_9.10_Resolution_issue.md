### Post by patchwork on 2010-02-10
The Xorg.0.log is found in /var/log , and is updated each time the X server is started.  In it, you may discover some clues as to what is happening.  The first thing that jumped out at me when reviewing your xorg.conf file is that there are no specified Modelines, presumably because your system is relying on the EDID information from the monitor.  

What I mean by the modeline generator is that at 1280x1024 resolution, with the ranges given in your xorg.conf for vertical refresh and horizontal sync, the calculated mode seems to fall outide of the defined ranges.  If this is in fact happening, the X server will select a "reasonable" resolution and timing scheme to maintain the display (instead of shutting off).
This is the nvidia-auto-select mode.

First check your Xorg.0.log for clues (you can post here if you like), and nothing jumps out at you from looking through there we can try something else.
