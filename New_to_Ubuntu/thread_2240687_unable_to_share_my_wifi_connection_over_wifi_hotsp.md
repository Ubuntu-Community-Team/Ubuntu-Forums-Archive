Hi shivametawah. Welcome to the forums!

I am sorry to hear from your disappointment about Ubuntu not fulfilling all your needs. For that reason, I recommend that you dual-boot.

You need to have wired access in Ubuntu to turn it into a hotspot. Sorry :(

Are there any other features in Windows that you would like in Ubuntu? We will try in every possible way to help you adapt to Ubuntu.

Here's a tip: If you don't like the interface, you can change it in a simple manner. Just go to the software center and find Gnome 3, XFCE, KDE, or any other interface you like. Install it. When (and if) prompted to choose lightdm or gdm (or lxdm), choose lightdm, as the login screen appearance will not change if you choose lightdm.
