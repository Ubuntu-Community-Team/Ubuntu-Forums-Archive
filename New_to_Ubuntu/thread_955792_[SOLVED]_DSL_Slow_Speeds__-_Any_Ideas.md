### Post by handydan918 on 2008-10-22
i don't know what you are basing your download speeds on, but very few public servers will just let you flush a d/l at full speed. Try a speed test site like [www.speedtest.net](www.speedtest.net) and see how you fare. 
I would also consider the possibility of putting a router in the network if you don't have one already. It provides an extra layer of insulation from badguys on the Internet.
