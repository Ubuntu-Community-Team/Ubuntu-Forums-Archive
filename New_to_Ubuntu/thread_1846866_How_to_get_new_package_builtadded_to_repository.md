### Post by Paqman on 2011-09-19
Everything in Universe is looked after by the [MOTU]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/MOTU")s (Masters of the Universe). Every package also has an entry in Launchpad, which in [this case]("https://launchpad.net/wyrd") does show a couple of names for people who maintain the package.
