### Post by mc4100 on 2008-08-10
Just like with your computer, you can dual boot Linux on an iPod (I've done it!) but often things an go wrong, so it's a good idea to make sure you have backup copy of you're music and a working copy of iTunes in-case you need to restore.

Edit: The iPodLinux page seems to be down (well, at least for me) but there's a sourceforge page:
[http://ipodlinuxinstl.sourceforge.net/](http://ipodlinuxinstl.sourceforge.net/)
(Also, it seems these instructions are only for a mac :()

Finally, I'd like to add that when I used it (5g iPod -- unsupported), I found it sort-of slow, (but nevertheless useable) and though I had both iPodLinux and Rockbox on it, they were both slower than the default Apple one. So, I guess I recommend sticking with it if it works, that's what I did (Although I do miss playing Doom ;))
