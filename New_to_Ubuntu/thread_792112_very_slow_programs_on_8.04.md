### Post by nullmind on 2008-05-12
It's likely your graphics have been misconfigured :(

What is the output of this command:

glxinfo | grep -i direct

If it says Direct Rendering: Yes, it's likely accelerated and shouldn't be a problem with your graphics configuration (X server). If you could attach your /etc/X11/xorg.conf file we'll have a fighting chance in seeing the error.
