### Post by mc4man on 2010-07-31
Haven't seen this scenario - shutdown while menu editor was open

I'd try this - 
open your home folder -> View -> 'Show Hidden Files', then scroll down and open .config folder -> menus
Inside will be 2 files - applications.menu and settings .menu
Right click on both - Move to trash and see if the menus return.
