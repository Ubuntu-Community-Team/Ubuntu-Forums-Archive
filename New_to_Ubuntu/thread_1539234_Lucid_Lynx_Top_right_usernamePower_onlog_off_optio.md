### Post by Calash on 2010-07-26
Right click on the panel and select "Add to Panel"

Pick "Indicator Applet Session" and select Add.

If that does not look right you may have had the complete one.  Remove that and then add "Indicator Applet Complete".

Adjust location to suit your preferences and enjoy :)
