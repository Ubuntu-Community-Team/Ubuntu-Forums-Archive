### Post by bc.haynes on 2014-02-15
Welcome to the forums,** kinleyradams**  
Drop-down menus in some distros are going to cover part(s) of some windows just by the nature of their location on the desktop. You can use a distro like Ubuntu/Unity, which does not hane drop-down application menus(applications are on the left on the Launcher). Or, you can move the window that is covered by clicking on the top bar (black), holding it and dragging it out from under the drop-down menu.
Hope this helps.
