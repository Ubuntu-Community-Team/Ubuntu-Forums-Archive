### Post by metol on 2008-06-23
> **xmatthillx said:**
> I've heard there were some conflicts with release 7.04, anyone know of any specific issues with the new release, or have any experience installing on this specific computer?

I'm running 8.04 just fine on my Toshiba A135-S4666 laptop.  The only issue that I had specific to the laptop was the headphone jack not functioning.  I found the solution to that issue and posted the fix in [http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=4814666&postcount=2]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showpost.php?p=4814666&postcount=2").  If your laptop is different than the S4666, a slightly different mod may be needed.

I do not have any firewire devices so I have not tested its behavior.  I do not use hibernate.  Suspend has a slight issue with the wireless card not coming back sometimes (running: *sudo /etc/init.d/networking restart* revives it).  Let me know if you have any issues, I'll try and help out.
