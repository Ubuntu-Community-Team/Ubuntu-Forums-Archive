### Post by pme 72 on 2009-12-21
To turn off Compiz temporarily right click anywhere on the desktop. In the menu that opens click on Change Desktop Background. That opens up Appearence Preferences. Click on the last tab, Visual Effects. Then click None. Compiz can be re-enabled from the same menu.

Appearance Preferences is also available from System/Preferences/Appearance. 

You can also add Compiz Fusion Icon to your system tray. It adds an icon that allows you to turn Compiz off and on more quickly. I think it is available from Add/Remove Programs.
