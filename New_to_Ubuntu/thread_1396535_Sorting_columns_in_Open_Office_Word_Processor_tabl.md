### Post by irw on 2010-02-02
click in the first cell on the table that you want to select.

Scroll to the bottom using the scroll bar on the right.

Press Shift key and click in last cell of table.

Or, another thought: click in the first cell, press Shift-Crtl-End - if that just selects the contents of the cell, press the same combination of keys again.

Ian
