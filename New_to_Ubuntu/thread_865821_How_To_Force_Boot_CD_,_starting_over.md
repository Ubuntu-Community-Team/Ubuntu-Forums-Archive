### Post by stoneybroke on 2008-07-21
If you open up your pc there should be a small jumper next to the silver battery with 3 pins two of those will have a jumper on take the jumper off from its position say position 1 and 2 and place it on pins 2 and 3 wait 5-10 seconds then put the jumper back to its origonal position. This will clear the bios. If that don't work take out the silver battery wait about 1 hour then replace the battery.
