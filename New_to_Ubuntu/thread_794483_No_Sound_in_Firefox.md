It seems that many people have had problems with flash (youtube videos) not playing nicely with pulseaudio.  Try going to System--> Preferences--> Sound and chaninging the Music and Movies sound playback device to ALSA in the drop down menu.

That's a good place to start.  Let us know how that works out for you.
