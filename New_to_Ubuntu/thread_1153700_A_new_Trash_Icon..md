### Post by mgmiller on 2009-05-10
Just as a point of information.  If you like the screenlet trash icon because it gives you more control over your trash (?!), you can set it to display its icon using the current themes icon. 

Right click the screenlet once it's running and select Properties.
Go to the Options tab and then click on its Options tab.

You will see a check box for "Use gtk style".  That will toggle between the default wire basket and whatever your current theme uses.
