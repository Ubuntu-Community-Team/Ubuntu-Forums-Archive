### Post by Seti on 2008-10-22
Naturally, Windows won't be able to see your linux partition because Microsoft doesn't provide any support for non Microsoft file-systems. If your google around you may be able to find some third party apps that may be able to mount ext3 and reiserfs. 
good luck!
