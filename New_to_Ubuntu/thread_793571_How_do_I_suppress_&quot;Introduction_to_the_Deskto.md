### Post by Mr A Mouse on 2008-05-13
Hmmm... Try this: the Sessions preference has three tabs, and the last one has a checkbox for "Remember startup programs," or something to that effect. Log in, close the introduction, open Sessions preferences, check that tab, close Sessions, and log out. Log back in and see if it comes up again.

Yeah, it's an aggravating pain in the neck, but if it works, you'll only have to do it once. And if it doesn't ... well, I'm sure someone will come up with something. :)
