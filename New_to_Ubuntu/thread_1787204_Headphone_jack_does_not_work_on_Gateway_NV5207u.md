I'm a brand new user of Ubuntu. Just today I installed to dual boot between 11.04 and windows 7 on a Gateway NV5207u. The sound works perfectly on the external speakers, but it does not switch to output the sound to headphones when plugged in. Google searches on the matter deal with too specific answers for other models. Please help. :confused:

### Post by eleven seven on 2011-06-21
I found what it was. :p And I'm banging my head on the wall for being that simple. I went to sound preferences, went to the output tab, and selected the analog output rather than the HDMI (I have headphones, not HDMI headphones). ](*,)
