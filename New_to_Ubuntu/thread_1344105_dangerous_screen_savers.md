### Post by mprince on 2009-12-02
You could test this yourself. Just open "System Monitor" and click the resource tab. 

Set your screensaver to trigger after a minute and keep the System Monitor running on the desktop. Look at CPU usage before and after the screensaver triggers.

I tried this with the "4D Hypertrois" and my cpu usage went from 6% to 32%

(note: you might want to slow down the graph update interval in System Monitor's prefs.)
