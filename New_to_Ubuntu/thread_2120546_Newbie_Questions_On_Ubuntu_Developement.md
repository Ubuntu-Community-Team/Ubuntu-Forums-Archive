### Post by DuckHook on 2013-02-26
50,000 feet view:

Kernel--absolute minimal core, ergo "kernel". Just brain, heart and lungs.
GNU--essential, fundamental, indispensible tools. Limbs, guts, organs and skeleton.
X--GUI and what makes it practical for real people. Muscle, sinew, some would say...fat.
DE--Gnome/Unity/KDE/XFCE/LXDE. Skin, hair, teeth, symmetry... what makes you attractive to sig other.
Distro--Outside apps, look and feel. Clothes, grooming, career, education, personality.

Silly metaphor, but I'm having fun.
