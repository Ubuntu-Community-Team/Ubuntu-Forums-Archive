### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-01-10
Either will do fine. Just, in my humble opinion, make sure it has 100% solid capacitors as it will be able to deal with more punishment. Solid capacitors are usually squat, coloured grey, and have writing on the top. Liquid caps usually have a metal circle at the top with some crossing lines etched to the top.
