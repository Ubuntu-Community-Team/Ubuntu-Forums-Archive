### Post by ohzopants on 2008-12-19
What filetypes are the movies?  Does your dvd player fully support those formats?

If the answer to the second question is yes, you may want to try burning at a slower speed.

As far as your DVD player is concernend, there is no difference between a disk burned in windows and a disc burned in linux.
