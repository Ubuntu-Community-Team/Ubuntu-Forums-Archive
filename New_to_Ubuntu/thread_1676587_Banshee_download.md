### Post by cariboo on 2011-01-27
You probably have some broken packages, go to System->Administration->Synaptic Package Manager->Edit->Fix broken packages, right-click the packages listed, and mark them to completely remove then, then click apply.

Once the broken packages have been removed, you should be able to re-install banshee.
