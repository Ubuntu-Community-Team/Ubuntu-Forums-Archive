### Post by lisati on 2012-01-30
Most of the machines I've used over the years by default check the floppy drive first if fitted. The "A:" in the prompt suggests to me that there's a disk in it. 

If you're absolutely certain that there's no disk in the A: drive and a CD in the CD/DVD drive, it might be a bad burn, and whatever's on the HDD might be setting up a RAM disk.
