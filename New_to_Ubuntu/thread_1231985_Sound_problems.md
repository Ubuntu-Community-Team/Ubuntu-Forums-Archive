Hi There

People are only volunteering their time here. 2 hours is not necessarily a long wait for a reply on a forum, so please keep that in mind if you expect a response (or many) in that time frame.

Regarding the issue - One thing I have noticed is that the theme songs on this site are stored in .wav files, which are notorious for being quite large files for the length of the audio stored in them, and not often used on the internet for that reason, as they take a long time to download from the internet due to their file size. The media player in Ubuntu tries to play the clip live from the internet, but if your internet connection is not fast enough to keep up with the rate at which the music plays, it will stop, and start, and stop, and start.

I would try right-clicking the links, and chosing "Save Link As..." to save the files to your computer. Once they have downloaded, you can play them from your computer and they should play smoothly.

Please let us know how you travel with this.
