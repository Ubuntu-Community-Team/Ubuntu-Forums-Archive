### Post by Qchan on 2008-08-05
[b][color=darkred]
Well, the error tells you. :P
You gotta go into Synaptic and remove it from there.
To do that, Click System --> Administration --> Synaptic Package Manager

In the search, type totem-gstreamer and press enter.

Once you see totem-gstreamer in the list of results, click on the green box and click on Complete Removal. Click apply on the bottom and you're done!
[/b][/color]
