### Post by CraigPid on 2013-05-21
What kind of sound card do you have? Sometimes you just need to open the mixer and raise the volume slider. You can type alsamixer at a terminal window to open up a mixer.  What device is alsamixer using? Does it give you an option to use another device?If you have more than 1 sound device your player maybe be using the one that your speakers are not plugged into. Take a look in sound settings from the sound icon in your panel.Check that your application is outputting to the correct device.
  Craig
