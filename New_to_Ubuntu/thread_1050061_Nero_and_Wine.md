### Post by Capt. Trouserblast on 2009-02-09
For me I use Brassero, K3b and Nero - I am not keen on Brassero for the reason that it is not possible to change the speed at which the drive reads and writes. Nero (and K3b) have the feature to read at different speeds. I find this useful when reading scratched/damaged discs and Nero will slow down automatically during the burn to sometimes 1x in order to read every scrap of data. This takes hours but if you are struggling to copy a damaged disc this is invaluable and has rescued me on several occasions!
