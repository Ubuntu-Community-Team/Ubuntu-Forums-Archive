### Post by bsell on 2008-05-25
Press ESC or F5 to get out of Presentation mode. You navigate with your mouse or arrow keys in Presentation mode. Under the Edit menu, you'll find the Find feature or you can use Ctrl+F keyboard shortcut to search for text.
