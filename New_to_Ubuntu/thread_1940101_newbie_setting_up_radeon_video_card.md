### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-03-13
When you have multiple monitors, you need to check the Display applet to confirm that mirroring is NOT checked by default.  IF it is, you'll see the same stuff on both screens.

After you uncheck that, you can set individual resolutions on each of the screens.

Also, by default, the Primary monitor is on your right, the secondary on your left.  This makes it confusing when your dragging the cursor from one screen to another.

To change this, go back into the Display applet where you see the icons at the top for the two monitors.  Grab the left icon and drag it to the right of the other one.  Now, your Primary screen will be on your left.
