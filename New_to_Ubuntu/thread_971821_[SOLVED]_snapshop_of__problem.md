Save it to Desktop then attach it to your post under "Additional Options" - Attach Files.

### Post by stevebond001 on 2008-11-05
Johntravis
I assume that you are wanting to take a screenshot of whatever application is causing a problem?
If so, all you need to do is make sure that the application has the focus (i.e. is at the front on your screen), then press ALT+Print Screen.  you will then be prompted to save the screenshot a a graphic file (.jpg, .png, etc) which you can then upload with your post.

Hope this helps
