### Post by applecake on 2009-05-22
Do you only want to write bengali characters, or do you want the whole system in bengali?

As for keyboard layout (what characters and where they are on your keyboard), you can change that on:
System>Preferences>Keyboard
Select the "Layout" tab. Click on the "Add" button, and then choose your country and/or language.

If you want to change the language of the whole system go to:
System>Administration>Language Support. You may be asked to write your password. Then check the box beside the language you want to install.
