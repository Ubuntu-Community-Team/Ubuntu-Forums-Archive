### Post by CatKiller on 2008-12-14
In Hardy there was quite a neat little tool called (I think) displayconfig-gtk. That was what detected the monitor, or let you load your Windows .inf definitions.

Unfortunately, it was Ubuntu-only. Developer resources are limited, and the decision was made that, rather than continually maintain the tool over the many changes to X that are currently happening, they should instead adopt the same Gnome tools as everyone else and pool resources. I think the plan is to put some of that functionality into the standard tools.

At least, as I understand it.
