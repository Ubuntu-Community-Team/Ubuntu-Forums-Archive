### Post by damis648 on 2008-07-15
Try:
Right click the bluetooth applet in the notification area of the panel and click "Preferences" and head to the "Services" tab. Click on "Input", making sure it is checked, checking it if it is not. Not put your mighty mouse in discovery mode and click "add" at the bottom. Let your mouse show up and click on it and OK. Bond it and it should be working.
