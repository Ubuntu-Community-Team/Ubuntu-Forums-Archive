### Post by BobFijiwinkle on 2011-03-18
Apologies for resurrecting an old thread, but it's at the top on Google, so I'm hoping this can help some people.

The blue theme is the default, but if you go to chrome://settings/personal (or click the wrench, then Preferences, then Personal Stuff) you can click "Use system title bar and borders." This should change it.

Hope this helps!
