### Post by mcduck on 2011-12-01
I don't think there's any direct alternative available for Linux. The last project I've seen has disappeared somewere.

However Blender will do the job, it has both non-linear video editing capabilities and a great node-based compositor. You'll definitely have to learn a new way of working, though, it's quite different to use than After Effects is.
