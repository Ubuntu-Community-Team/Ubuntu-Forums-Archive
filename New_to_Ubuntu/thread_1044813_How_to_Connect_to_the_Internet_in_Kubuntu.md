### Post by cmnorton on 2009-01-20
I am running 8.04 Kubuntu, and did not customize the desktop. The Network Manager icon is down on the right hand side of the desktop on a tool bar. The icon reminds me of an automobile muffler. If you pass your mouse over the icon, a tool tip should display telling you what it is. Click on and configure from there, when you find Network Manager

### Post by HavocXphere on 2009-01-20
There is a green globe in the sys tray at the bottom right. Thats the Knetworkmanager. Right-click on that and select edit connections. From there you can add/edit your wired/wireless setup.
