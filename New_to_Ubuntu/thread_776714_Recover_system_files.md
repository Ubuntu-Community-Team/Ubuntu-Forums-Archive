If you have deleted files from there and don't recall the name to get them back. I would suggest you reinstall Xubuntu but just format and reinstall the root section, not your /home directory, leave that alone.

Another thing you can do is look at QuickStart. There is a link in my signature. It will backup and restore your /, /home and config files. It does more than that but you'll find out if you check it out.

Then if you want to play around testing things you can do a "manual" backup of those before you start, or rely on a "scheduled" backup.
