### Post by Inisfad on 2010-10-13
Just in case anyone is interested in this, I finally got a solution from the Scribus 'forum'.  I have read on the internet that there are others who also have problems with margins, etc., in Scribus.  The simple solution that was given to me (and which worked), was to finish your project, then export the project to PDF and save it.  Then find the saved pdf (in Documents, or wherever you saved it) and double click on it.  Ubuntu will open it (in ghostscript?) and you print it from there.  This worked a treat, and it printed exactly the way it was laid out on the monitor.  Of course, this does not solve why Scribus couldn't adhere to my margins, but I finally was able to print my project exactly the way I laid it out.  Maybe this work around will help someone else.
