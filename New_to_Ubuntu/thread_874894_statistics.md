### Post by pi.boy.travis on 2008-07-30
The add/remove system actually has a "popularity contest" built in.  If you look in the right column, you will see a 1-5 star rating on the app's popularity.  While this isn't  necessarily a good indicator of the app's quality, it is a good thing to make note of.

As for OS stats, Distrowatch: [http://distrowatch.com/](http://distrowatch.com/)  has them.  They are based on how many people look at info for each distro.

Hope this helps!
