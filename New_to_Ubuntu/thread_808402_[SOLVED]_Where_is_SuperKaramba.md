### Post by Sarah L on 2008-05-26
SuperKaramba is just a tool to allow widgets to work on your desktop. It doesn't do anything by itself - you have to download the widgets that you want to use.

You'll find a wide selection of Karamba widgets on KDE-Look.org - check here: [http://www.kde-look.org/?xcontentmode=38]("http://www.kde-look.org/?xcontentmode=38").
