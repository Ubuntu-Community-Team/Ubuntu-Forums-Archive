### Post by Alan Rob on 2011-10-13
I use Lucid Lynx on a Dell Latitude D420 and had problems with a Linksys WAG160N wireless modem router dropping off loads of times even though the control panel showed that I had a strong signal. I read heaps of posts and different forums and became totally confused as a person who has used Ubuntu for about 5 years but is just a user who wants to boot up and go. I also had problems with dropping off when I, in desperation, used Windows XP which my computer has as a dual boot system. I've got to say that Ubuntu was by far the less reliable though.
Two things appear to have sorted out my problem and I share them in the hope that it assists others:
* I took the hardware back to the retailer and it was exchanged for a modem router that has worked a treat.
* I spotted a post that said that Network Manager could create problems - so I installed Wicd Network manager.

Result - in the week since I've replaced the modem/router - NO DROP OFFs. Fantastic!:D
