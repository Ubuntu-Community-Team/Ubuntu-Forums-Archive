### Post by drs305 on 2008-08-07
Can you right click on a panel, select Add to Panel, and then Volume Control. Clicking once brings up a single slider volume control but if you double click you should get a full range of sliders. If you don't, click on Edit, Preferences and you can select the controls you want to see.
