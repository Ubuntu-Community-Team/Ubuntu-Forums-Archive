kubuntu-desktop is a meta-package -- "to have kubuntu-desktop installed, all of *this stuff* is required."

.... but uninstalling it just removes that list of *this stuff* but doesn't actually remove the stuff *on* the list.

one of our forum staff has addressed this on his personal ubuntu website:
