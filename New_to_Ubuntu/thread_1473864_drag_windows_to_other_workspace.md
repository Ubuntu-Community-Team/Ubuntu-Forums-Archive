### Post by nhasian on 2010-05-05
to change the number of workspaces, simply right click on the workspace-switcher on the taskbar and choose preferences.  Alternatively you can also do it via System-> Preferences-> CompizConfig Settings Manager, General options, Desktop Size.

to drag windows to other workspaces go to Desktop-> Desktop Wall Plugin-> Edge Flipping and tick the box for "Edge Flip Move"

### Post by nhasian on 2010-05-07
I think no one responded because your thread is marked [solved]

anyway to answer your 2nd question, go to CompizConfig Settings Manager-> Desktop-> Viewport Switcher, then click on the tab "Desktop-based Viewport Switching" and set Move Next, and Move Prev to Button4 and Button5.  that should allow you to use the scroll wheel on your mouse.
