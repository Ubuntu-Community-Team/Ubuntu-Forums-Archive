### Post by fooman on 2008-12-08
press f11 to go fullscreen,  then f11 again to get to a normal size.

it may come back again.  so to fix it do this:

when you get to the normal size screen...unmaximize the screen (not minimize).

then close firefox and reopen it.

maximize firefox and you should be good.
