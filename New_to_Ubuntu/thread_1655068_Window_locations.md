### Post by jermza on 2010-12-29
Is there a way to have the windows open in the middle of the screen?  For example, whenever I create a new email in Evolution, the window opens at the left corner of the screen.  I have to, irritatingly, move it to the middle.

Same applies to other windows.

---

### Post by coffeecat on 2010-12-29
You need compiz enabled. Does your graphics card support this? If so, install compizconfig-settings-manager (if you haven't already done so). Then from ccsm (System > Preferences), Windows Management > Place Windows > Placement Mode. Choose "Centered". Or "Centred" as we like to say over here. :)
