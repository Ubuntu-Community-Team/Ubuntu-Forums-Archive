### Post by Jary316 on 2011-08-13
Hello,

I have a strange problem that I hope someone can please help me with.

I installed Ubuntu 11.04 from the Live CD on my Asus N61J laptop. I have a Logitech optical mouse plugged into the USB port (tried USB 3.0 and 2.0).

The issue that I have is that sometimes the left click gets set even though I am not touching the mouse. Left clicking on my mouse won't unclick it, but the right click works just fine. To get out of this left click I have to left click using my touchpad on the laptop itself. After that I can normally left click again with the mouse, until the problem happens again.

I doubt that this is hardware based: this problem does not occur when I run Windows 7.

As anyone encountered this issue please?

Thank you very much.

### Post by tolf242 on 2011-08-13
De-active your laptop's touch pad when using the mouse.
I found the mouse activated when my other hand hovered over or near the touch pad.
