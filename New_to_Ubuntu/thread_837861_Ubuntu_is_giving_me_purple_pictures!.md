### Post by cariboo on 2008-06-22
Go it to System-->Administration-->Users and Groups,

Click the unlock button and enter your password, Click Manage Groups, Scroll down to scannerusers, highlight it, click properties and make sure there is a tick mark beside you name. If there isn't click the box then click OK, Click Close, Click Close. Logout and then log back in. You should now be able to scan and get the proper colors.
