### Post by flanagan on 2009-03-07
I am guessing that you are referring to the *Workspace Switcher* applet that can be added to any panel. Right-click any panel and add *Workspace Switcher*. When your cursor is over the applet, you can use the scroll wheel to switch between virtual workspaces (screens).

### Post by daplumpkin on 2009-03-07
i'm having somewhat of the same problem. if you have compiz, go to system>preferences>compizconfig settings manager>rotate cube(under desktop) when you get there click on the word rotate cube, don't just check the box. at the top there will be 2 menu tabs. one will say bindings and the other general. click the arrow under bindings to drop the menu for rotate cube. for initiate (mouse pic) if you choose button 2 you can click the scroll button to show the cube and rotate. i have button 4 and 5 set for rotate left and right, but it disables scrolling in a window. no matter where you put the mouse it will rotate the cube left or right. if you tinker with these and find out how to scroll in a window with the scroller and rotate the cube when you're not in the window i would greatly appreciate the info on how to do it.
