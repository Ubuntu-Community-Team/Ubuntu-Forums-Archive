### Post by FrostyFlames on 2008-11-01
The new release of 8.10 has a install to USB flash drive option. Granted it basically just makes your USB a bootable LIVE CD with a preset amount of storage that stays the same. Technically it's not a real install. This option can only be done while using the LIVE CD, not the alternate or server CDs.

As for installing a real version onto a large (I'd recommend at least a 2-4GB drive at the extremely least) USB flash or hard drive, it can be done. It will NOT be portable in the sense that you can unplug it and plug it into another computer and it will work. I'd check your computers boot menu for a USB option if you have not already (read the manual or research how to get into this online). If not, then yes, you are correct in your assumption that you need a custom floppy or CD to tell your computer to boot off of a USB drive.
