### Post by doublewitt on 2010-07-12
Did you check your default settings through the Konqueror interface? I have done it this way and _it works for me_. Open Konqueror and click on the SETTINGS tab and then on CONFIGURE KONQUEROR and finally, on FILE ASSOCIATIONS. While you are there, click on TEXT and then HTML. You can remove "Winebrowser" from the list and move your preferred browser to the top...

hope this helps!

BTW, I have been using Kmail for quite a while now with no issues whatsoever... it has proven to be very stable and I enjoy using it.
