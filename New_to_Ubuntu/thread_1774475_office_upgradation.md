### Post by rosencrantz on 2011-06-03
OpenOffice/LibreOffice apparently can read Office 2007's OOXML, but not save it.
However, it can save anything in Office XP's .doc/.xls/.ppt format, which in turn can be read/saved by Office 2007.
No Microsoft Office version can read OpenOffice's own formats, so you'll have to always export as .doc/.xls/.ppt for compatibility. Don't expect every feature to work perfectly (PowerPoint effects, edit tracking etc.)
