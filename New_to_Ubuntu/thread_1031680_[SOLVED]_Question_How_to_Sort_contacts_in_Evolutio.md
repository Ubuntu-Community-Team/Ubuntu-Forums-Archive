### Post by suomalainen on 2009-01-06
UBUNTEROS,

I finally found how to sort contacts in Evolution.

1. Open Evolution and click on CONTACTS
2. A number of colums will appear containing your contacts details.
3. Choose any header for any column and right click on it.
4. Now choose "Add a Column"
5. Select "Family Name"
6. Drag & Drop this new column to far left and now you can sort contacts via last name/Family name.
