### Post by applehead on 2010-05-09
I love f spot, you you should give it an other chance.

When you import, it gives you a choice whether you want the photos copied to your hd. So if you only want to use fspot to look at your photos then let fspot make a copy of them and delete the originals from the card or where ever. If you want to organize your photos your self then do so and make sure that f spot doesnt make a copy of them. This feature is only annoying if you forget you have the choice.
