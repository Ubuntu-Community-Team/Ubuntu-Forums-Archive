### Post by Tahakki on 2009-12-31
Certainly the internet connection, yes, but on my old computer we had 20MB/s and it still went really slow on fullscreen (512MB RAM).

Any processor above 2gHz should be sufficient, but try and get dual-core if you can - most programs nowadays can use multiple cores.

By the way, on my old computer downloading the video to the iPlayer program meant playback was even slower. It uses Adobe Air, which I think is quite resource-hungry.
