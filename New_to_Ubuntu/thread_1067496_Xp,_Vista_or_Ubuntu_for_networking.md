### Post by eastexsteve on 2009-02-11
I'm new to Ubuntu, but am fairly seasoned with Windows.  In your Windows machine, have a look at your power saving settings in the Control Panel and make sure your Windows machine isn't shutting down your network interface when it goes into sleep mode.  I have one Vista machine that I have to set to not sleep at all because when it does, it drops off the network.
