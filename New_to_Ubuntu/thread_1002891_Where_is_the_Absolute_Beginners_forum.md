We understand this, as does Microsoft, which is why Windows comes pre installed and pre configured.

Take a deep breath, slow down ...

First you need to burn the iso to a cd, not copy the files.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)

Second much of the information you seek is easily available, you simply need to learn how to access it. Either use goggle modify your posting style :twisted:
