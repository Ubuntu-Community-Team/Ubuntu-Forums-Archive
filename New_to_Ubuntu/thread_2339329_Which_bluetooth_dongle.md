### Post by Geoffrey_Arndt on 2016-10-07
I have an older Cambridge based dongle (v2?) that works "sorta" well in Ubuntu (14.04 & 16.04).   Also have a later gen Targus that works "very" well, and a v4 BT dongle via Panda that works "great" (see Amazon - - $9.99).   But, if you already have both, just plug them in and test them.   The initial pairing on the Cambridge might be tricky but with some work, it will pair.
