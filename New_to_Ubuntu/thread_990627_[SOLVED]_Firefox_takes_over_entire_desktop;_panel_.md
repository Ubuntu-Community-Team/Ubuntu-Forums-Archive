### Post by caro on 2009-01-03
Permanent fix for me:

Hit F11 twice to get FF out of full screen.  Then use the maximize/unmaximize button to unmax the screen and then maximize it again.  Close FF and relaunch.  Your settings should be saved.  If not, try again manually resize the window after you unmaximize it.
