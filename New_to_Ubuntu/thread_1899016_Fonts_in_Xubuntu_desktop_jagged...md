### Post by Toz on 2012-02-12
> **sette075 said:**
> not sure if you are asking if he bumped the settings
Not sure what you mean by "bumped the settings", but to configure fonts in xubuntu (xfce), go to the Settings Manager, Appearance, Fonts tab. I would suggest selecting a cleaner-looking font and in the "Rendering" section, enabling "anti-aliasing" and setting "hinting" to slight and "Sub-pixel order" to RGB.

What version of Xubuntu are you running/testing? Was it a clean install or an upgrade from another ubuntu flavour?
