### Post by jimbren on 2008-05-11
Amarok includes access to the Magnatunes music store, which is a service very similar in principle to iTunes, but features independant and lesser known musicians.  You'll find some very cool stuff on there, make no mistake.  

Amazon.com now has a downloader for Linux that you can use to browse for, purchase, and download music.  [http://www.amazon.com/gp/dmusic/help/amd.html](http://www.amazon.com/gp/dmusic/help/amd.html)  Here, of course, you'll find major labor artists as well as some smaller indpendents, and all of your downloads are DRM-free.

Hope that helps.
