### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-07-14
Acrobat reader simply prints, using the settings that you have setup in your printer configuration.

Navigate to [COLOR="Blue"]System>Administration>Printing[/COLOR]

When the window opens, click on the [COLOR="Blue"]Printer Options[/COLOR] button.

Select your desired printout mode from the [COLOR="Blue"]drop-down menu[/COLOR].
