### Post by Alterax on 2009-04-23
Not to the best of my knowledge.  Cyberlink's site might be able to tell you what their product will run under.  From what I understand, they have not ported their code to Linux yet.

On the other hand, you can get the same functionality by installing libdvdcss from the medibuntu packages (more on [www.medibuntu.org](www.medibuntu.org) on adding this repository) and adding the ogle package as a frontend.
