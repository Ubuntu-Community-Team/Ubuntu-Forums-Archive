### Post by Polygon on 2008-07-21
your most likely talking about the 'What U hear' channel on soundcards, i had it on my creative soundblaster when i used it on my win xp machine

the thing is they dont create official drivers for those cards for linux, so most of them are just reverse engineered, so for most cards there is most likely no such channel.

however, there are plenty of utilities that can download youtube videos, and even the mp3 FROM the youtube videos

a program that can do this is pytube, you can download it here (there is a ubuntu deb) and just double click to install it and it goes in the sound/video section of the menu

[http://bashterritory.com/pytube/index.php?option=com_remository&Itemid=26&func=select&id=1](http://bashterritory.com/pytube/index.php?option=com_remository&Itemid=26&func=select&id=1)
