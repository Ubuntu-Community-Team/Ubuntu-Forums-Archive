### Post by newb85 on 2013-07-10
Directories and files beginning with . are hidden. In the terminal ls -a will show them.   In Nautilus, ctrl+H toggles the display of hidden files.

### Post by stinkeye on 2013-07-10
The tilde (~) represents your home folder and a file or folder preceded by a dot makes it hidden.
So open your home folder and ctrl+h to show hidden files and look for the .local folder.
If you don't have the directory ~/.local/share/applications, create it.
