### Post by Uly55es on 2013-07-16
The image you set as your wallpaper is stored in ~/.cache/wallpaper/. If you can't see your wallpaper when you log back in, it could mean this folder being emptied every time... Try to log in and check whether it is or not ( launch the terminal pressing Alt-Ctrl-T and type "ls .cache/wallpaper" without quotes; or open the file manager, press Ctrl-H to reveal hidden files and double click on .cache etc).
