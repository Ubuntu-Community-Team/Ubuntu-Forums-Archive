### Post by Joeb454 on 2011-02-23
All it is doing is mounting your Windows partition, allowing you to read the files.

This means that, for example, if you have some files on Windows you want to use under Ubuntu, that you can just click that, and grab the files you need.

Note, however, you cannot do this the other way around.
