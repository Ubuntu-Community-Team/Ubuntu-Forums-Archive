### Post by yeats on 2009-02-23
I'm not sure what exactly you need to do, but your Gnome settings live in a hidden directory (maybe more than one) in your home directory.  Open Nautilus and look for an option in the menus to "show hidden files/folders" (don't know the exact wording).  You could see what is in there that you could port over when creating a new user account.
