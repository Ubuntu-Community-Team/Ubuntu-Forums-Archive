### Post by heyandy889 on 2010-08-21
The Alt-left click and drag idea is wonderful. I too have experienced this frustration, windows far too large for the screen.

Try hovering your mouse near the window's corner. The cursor should change into an arrow with some lines at a 90 degree angle. Click and hold the left mouse button. Drag the pointer to resize the window.

Note that hovering the mouse over any of the window's four corners allows you to have this resize option.
