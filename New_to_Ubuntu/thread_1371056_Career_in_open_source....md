### Post by jonest1 on 2010-01-03
I've been in IT and working primarily with Linux for about nine of them.  Here's my thoughts on your questions.

1) What is the imnportance of REDHAT CERTIFICATION??

It can be big.  As RH certification is a pretty tough cert to obtain, it shows your skill level better than most certifications.  Even if the site doesn't use RH, it's a good learning process.  LPI certification is also really useful.

2) What is the scope of linux in INDIA??

Don't know, but I think Google can assist.

3) From where i can start improving my linux skills??

There are three places where I would start out.
1.  Learn the bash shell and shell scripting in general.  It's an essential skill for any admin.  Perl would also be good.
2.  This is going to sound weird, but I learnt a lot in a short time.  Check out the gentoo distribution and install from the lowest level.  Gentoo is a sort of build from scratch distribution and I learnt a good deal about how the OS was put together.
3.  Learn about network services.  Specifically, I suggest you become familiar with Apache, Tomcat, and Samba.

4) I have listened about that LINUX DRIVERS have more importance 
than LINUX ADMINISTRATION. Tell me about the linux drivers and which is important from the job point of view??

I'm not sure what you mean by Linux drivers.  You may want to reword your question or explain the term drivers.

5) Suggest me some ebooks/books for my self study..??

I cannot think of any specific that I would recommend, but 'Linux In a Nutshell' from Oreilly is popular.
