### Post by Felix-the-Cat on 2009-03-14
Have you checked all of the levels in in the Volume Manager. In the upper right cornet right click on the volume icon and open the Volume Control and check everything in there. 

If that doesn't work then drop to a terminal (Applications>Accessories>Terminal) and run alsamixer and check everything in there to make sure nothing is off or muted.
