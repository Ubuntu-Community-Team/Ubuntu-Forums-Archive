### Post by BenAshton24 on 2009-11-29
Save the tar.gz that downlownload do some place on your computer.

then open up Appearance Preferences

System->Preferences->Appearance

Then, open up the folder that contains the theme that you just downloaded and drag it into your application preferences window

And that's it, your theme is installed :)

A Better Method is to put the theme in /usr/share/themes so that the theme is globally available across accounts on your pc :)
