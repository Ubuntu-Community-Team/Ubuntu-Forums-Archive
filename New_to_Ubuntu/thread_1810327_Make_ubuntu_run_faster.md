### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2011-07-23
Lag is very non-specific and can be due to anything from a slow cpu to slow ram to , most often, video drivers and wireless drivers.

On older computers with low spec you can try shutting off as many services as possible, or try xubuntu or lubuntu.

If lubuntu is still slow try tinycore, slitaz, or puppy linux.
