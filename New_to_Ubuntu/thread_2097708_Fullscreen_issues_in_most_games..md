No one responded, Wonderful :3...

Anyway I did manage to fix it. I downloaded CompizConfig Settings Manager. you can just get it in the Ubuntu Software Center.

I Went into Composite and checked "Unredirect Fullscreen Windows"

And it suddanly worked <3!

This Problem has been solved :D
