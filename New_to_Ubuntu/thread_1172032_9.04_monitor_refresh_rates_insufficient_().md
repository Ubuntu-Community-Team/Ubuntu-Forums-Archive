### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-05-28
Should get the "IT guy" to explain just what "insufficient" means ...

Most monitors today typically use 60Hz refresh rates, although some use 59, and others can go to 75 and beyond.  The higher the rate, the less likely you'll notice screen flicker.  But generally, 60 is high enough to be unnoticed.

I wouldn't up the rate without checking the monitor specs first.  The rate is tied to the screen resolution.
