### Post by oldsoundguy on 2008-12-28
check eBay and/or New Egg.

A DVI/HDMI KVM is going to cost you!  I have one from IOGEAR that is DVI/ps2 with analog sound .. works off a push button switch rather than doing a double click on the scroll lock key.  It does not come cheap .. mine was 109 US from New Egg on sale, but I run 64 2.8 dual core Windows box and a 8.10 box through it .. pushing wireless keyboard and mouse.  (on mine, I had to make a separate DIGITAL sound out control switch because THAT is what I use.)  Have a 24" wide screen cinema view HP monitor.

A standard KVM with VGA connectors will run a LOT less .. under 30 bucks in most cases (I have a pair of them at other work station locations.) (again, IOGEAR.) You can get them both with and without sound .. and those are KEYBOARD switched.

### Post by mbeach on 2008-12-28
in case this scenario works for you:

my monitor has a dvi and vga input, so I've plugged an older machine in with the vga cable, the newwer computer with a dvi cable, then used synergy to use just one keyboard and mouse.  I had originally used it at work for a multiple display situation where having 3 keyboards on the desk was getting cumbersome.  It may not fit your needs but its worth a mention just in case.  Switch between displays with the 'source' button on the front of the monitor, and move the mouse to the left or right of the screen to get to the other machine.

[http://synergy2.sourceforge.net/](http://synergy2.sourceforge.net/)
