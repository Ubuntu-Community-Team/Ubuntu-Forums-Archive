### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-04-20
While we're talking about scams, watch out for people who telephone you. This really happened to me, by the way, and fortunately I was aware of these scams.

*Him:* "Hello, I'm calling from XYZ about your computer."

*Me:* "My computer? What does your company have to do with my computer?"

*Him:* "We've got notifications over the Internet that your computer is infected with some viruses, so we're phoning to help you get it clean."

*Me:* "**** off!" [Hangs up phone.]

They get you to download a program, and probably charge you for it, and my understanding is that the program contains keylogger malware.

In hindsight, I reckon I should have wasted his time (preventing him from scamming someone else for some minutes) by letting him talk me through it. I simply wouldn't have let him know that I was running Linux, getting him very, very confused! ;)
