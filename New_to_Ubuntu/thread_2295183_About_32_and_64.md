### Post by grahammechanical on 2015-09-16
I am running 64 bit Ubuntu + Unity on a machine with 1GB RAM and I am not complaining about performance. I have sometimes seen 2 GB RAM as a "recommended" amount.

You may find that it is IBM SPSS which needs 4 GB RAM
