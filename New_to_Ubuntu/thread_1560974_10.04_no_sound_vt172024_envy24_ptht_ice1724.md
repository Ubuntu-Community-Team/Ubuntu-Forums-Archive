### Post by brett- on 2012-12-17
I know this is an old post but felt obliged to reply, based on my experience, in order to WARN Linux users to stay away from all sound cards containing the VIA VT1720/24 audio controller.  I have used the Auzen X-Studio 5.1 for two years in a mediacenter application with very poor results.  I have tried everything possible to get this audio controller to work and the best I have been able to achieve is hit or miss (sometimes the controller is recognized, sometimes it is not).

The best solution is to pass your VT1720 on to a Windows pal and get another audio card.  Shame because when working the audio is quite good.
