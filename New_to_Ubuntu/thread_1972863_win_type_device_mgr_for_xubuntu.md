### Post by phillipmaloney on 2012-05-04
google for "ubuntu device manager" and it will give you some choices for what you are looking for. I use the hardinfo package. they are really not specific to xubuntu (which is what I use by the way) but more to what programs your desktop envoirment choice is and what programs they offer by default. I migrate between XFCE and Gnome Classic 2. neither one offered a default device manager so I just went looking for one and found it via google.
