### Post by Ampi on 2010-08-19
Well, if all is good, on the left side in the menu there is a link called "Files". If you click on that your nautilus will open and you should be able to go anywhere in your netbook.

Also, if I remember correctly, you can open the menu 'normally' with right click or something. But Im not sure. 

Anyways, if it doesn't work, you can always change to desktop and back to netbook edition if you want. You don't need to reinstall your distro. It's simply a graphical and some settings that differ between the two. In your terminal (I guess also in Synaptic but I haven't tried) you can install the ubuntu-desktop and then remove the netbook-launcher. Other way around if you wanna go back to the netbook edition.
