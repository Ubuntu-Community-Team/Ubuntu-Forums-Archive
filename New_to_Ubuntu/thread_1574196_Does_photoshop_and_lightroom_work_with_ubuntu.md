### Post by bbqsandwich on 2010-09-14
You can run Photoshop under WINE ([http://www.winehq.org/](http://www.winehq.org/)), although some people report issues with CS3 specifically. See here:

[http://wiki.winehq.org/AdobePhotoshop](http://wiki.winehq.org/AdobePhotoshop)

It appears people have not had as much success with Lightroom:

[http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=5839](http://appdb.winehq.org/objectManager.php?sClass=application&iId=5839)

Really for the best, most reliable performance you should run an application under the operating system it is meant for. :)

You may also want to consider what your needs are; it is possible there is a native Ubuntu application (like The GIMP) that does the job for you.
