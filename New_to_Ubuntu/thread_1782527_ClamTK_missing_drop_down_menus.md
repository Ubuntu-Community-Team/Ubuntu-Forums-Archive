Hello.

You are using the 'Unity' interface. The drop-down menus will appear when you move the mouse-pointer to the panel at the top of the screen. This happens to all applications that I have used.

If you want to use 'Classic' at the log-in screen click on the user-name and type in the password. Before you you click to log-in look at the bar at the bottom of the screen. there you will find a box with the option to change to 'Classic'. 

In 'Classic' the drop-down Menus appear by default.
