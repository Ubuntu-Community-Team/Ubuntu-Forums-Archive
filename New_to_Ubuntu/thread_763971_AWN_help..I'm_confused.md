### Post by SunnyRabbiera on 2008-04-23
> **spacebub said:**
> Whats SVN? where can i find the better version?
And i have AWN downloaded but its wierd.  I want to delete it just start over.  How could i do this?

check my post for installing a better version, you probably installed an older version of AWN
as for un installing AWN go to system>administration>synaptic
synaptic is the main GUI package installer for Ubuntu.
when you click on the synaptic menu entry you will be prompted to enter your password.
after that synaptic will load.
there will probably another window that will come up with it, ignore it and
go to "search" and click it
and then type in "awn"
and click on "search again
it will find awn and just click on the little green box and select "mark for complete removal.
AWN will be uninstalled.


you are welcome for the help
as for your picture, you can just load it on imageshack or something if you are having that much trouble and give us a link.
