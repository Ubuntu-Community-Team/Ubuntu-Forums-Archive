### Post by khedron on 2009-01-28
If you are using Ubuntu (rather than Kubuntu) you can do a screenshot by going to **Applications &#8212;> Accessories &#8212;> Take Screenshot**.

This webpage was the first hit on Google for "ubuntu screenshot" and has other ways of taking screenshots if you need them.
