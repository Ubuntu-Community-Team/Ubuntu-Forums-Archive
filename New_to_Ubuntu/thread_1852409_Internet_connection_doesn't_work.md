### Post by carranty on 2011-09-30
In my experience you don't need to configure anything for a wired connection, its all automatic.  If you left click on the network manager icon (in the system tray, probably near the clock) does a list of available networks come up? Try clicking on the one corresponding to your wired connection, it should be underneath 'Wired Network'.

Also what makes you think it can't connect?  Is is because you can't go to websites in your browser?  This may be a proxy server issue, most universities have them.  If so you'll need to get the details of it from your institution and then update System > Preferences > Network Proxy
