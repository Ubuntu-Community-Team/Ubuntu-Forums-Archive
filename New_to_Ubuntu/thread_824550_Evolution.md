### Post by mdpalow on 2008-06-15
First of all, you have to have a PLUS mail account with Yahoo! if you want to access it via Evolution (pop3). 

If you're paying for it ($20 a year) then you can do this. Otherwise not.

If you would like to see my HOWTO on configuring Evolution with Yahoo!, please go here:

[http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=829046](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=829046)

Good luck and Private Message me with any questions you might have since I'm not following this thread.

P.S. By the way, I love accessing my Yahoo! mail via Evolution. It's much faster and I get a notification in my system tray when mail gets pulled in. Also, with a PLUS account, you get as many DISPOSABLE e-mail addresses as you need to keep your treasured e-mail address free of spam. :)
