### Post by donkyhotay on 2008-12-19
Welcome to linux! Be aware that wiping out windows and diving right in can be a little extreme but you'll definitely learn alot! First of all the download should have been a .iso file. Where did you download it from? Did you get it from the official download site? 
[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/download) 
If necessary redownload the correct file, once thats done your going to need a disc burning utility to burn the disc onto a CD. Something like nero will work if you have it. There are probably some free ones but it's been so long since I've used a windows based CD burner I don't know of any. Once the iso is burned onto the CD it's a simple matter of restarting your computer with the CD in the drive and then starting the installer. You don't have to be too careful when your wiping because your planning on deleting all your data anyways.
