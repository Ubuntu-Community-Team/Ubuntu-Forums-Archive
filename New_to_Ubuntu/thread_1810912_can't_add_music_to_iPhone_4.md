### Post by weberc2 on 2011-07-25
I don't believe this is possible as of right now. There are various libimobiledevice "solutions" on the internet for fourth generation iPods and iPhones; however, none have worked (a few have managed to allow the iPod to be recognized in Nautilus and Rhythmbox; however, none allow syncing--it will try to sync a song as long as the device is plugged in but it will fail everytime). From what I hear, it's an issue with the firmware on the new 4g hardware (iOS4 works on older devices).
 
If anyone knows of a solution, be sure to post, but I don't think any exist as of now.
