### Post by Paqman on 2008-04-23
Ubuntu comes with an email/newsgroup/calendar package called Evolution. Mozilla Thunderbird is also available.

You can check out some of the default software on [this page](http://www.ubuntu.com/products/whatisubuntu/desktop/features). The software repositories also have literally thousands of other software packages that can be installed automatically in a couple of mouse clicks.
