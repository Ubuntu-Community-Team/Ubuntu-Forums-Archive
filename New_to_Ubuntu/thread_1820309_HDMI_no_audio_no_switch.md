### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-08-08
Hi and welcome to the forums. I do not have the answer but here is something for you to consider. When you installed the operating system it was configured for the laptop display. The TV has different settings from the laptop screen. Do you need to have different settings for the TV?

Try switching the laptop to the monitor before loading the operating system, and not after.

The HDMI output from the display adapter may not include audio but only video. You may need a separate audio cable from the computer to the TV.

My DVT/monitor has HDMI sockets but it also has a socket labelled HDMI/PC/DVI-D AUDIO IN. I am using DVI-D for the video from the computer and this AUDIO IN socket for the sound/audio from the computer to play sound through the TV speakers.
