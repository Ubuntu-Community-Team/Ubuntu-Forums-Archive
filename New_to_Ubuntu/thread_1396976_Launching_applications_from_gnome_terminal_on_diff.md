Hey Roly,

Nope- compizconfig isnt related to cairo dock. Its a separate app that you could find in the software center. (go for the full version, rather than simple ccsm)


Once you install that, fire it up and look on the left side for the Window Management section. Click that to filter the effects, and then look for Place Windows.


When you click on the icon for place windows, youll see 2 tabs, click the one that says 'fixed window placement'


Open the app that you want to have on a specific viewport. Then add it like i have illustrated in the screencap.

You click add, then grab, then click the window you want (in my example, its google-chrome), then finally pick what viewport you want the window on. Presto!
