### Post by SlickRick on 2010-03-04
right-click file and select 'extract here' in the drop-down menu. Place extracted files in desired directory by means of dragging and dropping.

Are you installing from source or something? Because you don't have to do that. I had a look at the wiki and there's an easier way of installing XBMC. Go to [http://wiki.xbmc.org/index.php?title=Installing_XBMC_for_Linux](http://wiki.xbmc.org/index.php?title=Installing_XBMC_for_Linux) and go down to the ubuntu section. Then copy and paste each line of code into the terminal and press enter for each one. You will be prompted for your password at least once.
To launch a terminal, go to Applications>Accessories>Terminal on the panel.
