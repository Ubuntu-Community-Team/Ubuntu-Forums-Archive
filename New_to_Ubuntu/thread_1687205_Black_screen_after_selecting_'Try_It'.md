### Post by An Sanct on 2011-02-13
Welcome to the forum.

Maybe the CD is corrupt. If you have a spare 1Gb USB key, try to make a live USB from the ISO you downloaded.

needed tools: [Unetbootin]("http://unetbootin.sourceforge.net/") or the usb-creator, found in the ISO you downloaded.

### Post by Broyd on 2011-02-13
Thanks An Sanct.

This worked ... although I had downloaded the Desktop 10.10 version and the Flash drive installer says it doesn't work with that version.
