### Post by dwasifar on 2009-03-25
You should not need any special software or drivers for that; it should work just like any ordinary keyboard and mouse.

The only issue I have encountered with Ubuntu and Logitech wireless kb/mouse sets is that for some reason the keyboard doesn't work during the 30-second "choose a language" countdown when booting from the Live CD.  Other than that, they work fine out of the box.
