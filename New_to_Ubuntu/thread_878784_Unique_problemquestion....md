### Post by Paqman on 2008-08-04
You could try installing one of the spins of Ubuntu that have multimedia support by default. As long as you can grab the .iso and install you should be able to handle your music and movie files without having to install any extra packages from the repos.

The best known one is [Mint](http://www.linuxmint.com/), but i'd personally recommend [Kiwi](http://kiwilinux.org/). They're both just versions of Ubuntu with extra packages bundled in.
