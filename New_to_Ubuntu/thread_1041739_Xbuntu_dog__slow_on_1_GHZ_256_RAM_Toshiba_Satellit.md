### Post by cardinals_fan on 2009-01-16
Xubuntu has lots of GNOME 'contamination' and is one of the slower Xfce systems.

I would do one of the following:

a) Install a lighter window manager, such as JWM, and then audit your startup services

b) install a lighter distro like SliTaz, Vector Light, or regular Debian
