### Post by pizza-is-good on 2010-02-08
First, make sure that all the drivers you need are enabled.
System>>Administration>>Hardware Drivers

Second, make sure that your system is fully up to date.
System>>Administration>>Update Manager

Then, go to your sound settings
System>>Preferences>>Sound
and see if everything looks fine

Let us know what you find
