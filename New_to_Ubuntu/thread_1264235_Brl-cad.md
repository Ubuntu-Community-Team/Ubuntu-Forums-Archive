Installing from source can be tricky, especially if the ./configure script (which just checks your computer to see if the dependencies are already installed and stops if it hits a required dependency that is not there) indicates that you need dependencies that do not exist in the repositories (Synaptic).  I think you might stick with Synaptic until you have a better grasp on things (just my opinion).
