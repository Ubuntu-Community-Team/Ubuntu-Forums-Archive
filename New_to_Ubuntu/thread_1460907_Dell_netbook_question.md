### Post by snowpine on 2010-04-23
I have used an external VGA monitor with my Dell Mini 9; it works great, no problems at all.

Do yourself a favor and avoid any of the Mini models with Intel GMA500 graphics... this graphics card is not well supported by most Linux distros, so you might have problems if you ever want to switch/upgrade from the custom "Dellbuntu" that comes preinstalled.

### Post by snowpine on 2010-04-23
Dell Mini 10 is available with (at least) three different graphics cards:

10v = Intel GMA950 (same as my Mini 9, well supported by all Linux distros)
1010 = Intel GMA500 (I recommend avoiding this one)
1012 = Intel NM10 (I have no experience with it)

This site is a great resource (especially the forums): [http://mydellmini.com](http://mydellmini.com)
