### Post by sully101 on 2013-03-06
I have noticed something similar on my mini9, when I had "auto login" enabled I would get the gnome keyring asking me for authentification after it started and if I didnt enter my password there then I would get the network connection box popping up. Check that in the connection preferences you have connect automatically and available to all users selected. I ended up disabling auto login. Which version of ubuntu are you using? Sorry I dont have my mini9 with me to try things out.
