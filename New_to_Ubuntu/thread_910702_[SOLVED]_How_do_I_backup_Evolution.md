---

### Post by Alexander Lindskog on 2008-09-04
Evolution:

File -> "Backup Settings..." 

then select where you would like the backup to go

Firefox:

Bookmarks -> Organize Bookmarks

then "Import & Backup"

### Post by NE Key on 2008-09-05
Another way , more "nuts and bolts" is to copy the folders where all your "stuff" is.

In your home folder there is folder called .evolution
(the dot makes it hidden - Ctrl+H makes it visible)

Also one called .mozilla or .mozilla-firefox (or similar depending on version)

I have copied these folders to a disc. Done a fresh install and then copied them back.

The other solution you were given above may be more sophisticated.
