### Post by deadflowr on 2013-12-28
By far the easiest way to is to share a folder.
When you right click on a folder you will see an option for share, when you first try this, the system will tell you that it needs to install some packages, normally samba.
Follow along and the needed packages will be installed.
Then configure to your liking
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Samba/SambaServerGuide](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Samba/SambaServerGuide)
