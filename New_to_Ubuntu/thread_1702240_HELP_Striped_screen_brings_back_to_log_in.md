### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2011-03-07
Perhaps you have a bad burn.  Check the md5sum of the ISO that you downloaded.  [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM) in case you need some information.  If that checks out OK then reburn the ISO to a CD at a lower speed.  That should give you a good burn so you can check it out on your computer.
