### Post by Lisiano on 2011-09-19
Just press the Enter key. There is a weird bug that doesn't show the text like it used to before. The text it is trying to show is "Ejecting CD/DVD, please remove the disk and press Enter" or something along those lines. So since a USB has no CD/DVD disks, just press Enter and unplug the USB.
