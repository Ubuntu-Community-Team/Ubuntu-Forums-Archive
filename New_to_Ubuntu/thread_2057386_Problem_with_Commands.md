### Post by GreatDanton on 2012-09-13
To look for pipe symbol go under: System settings/Keyboard layout. After that click on the little keyboard at left bottom part of the window. It will open up a picture of your keyboard, and check on which key you have pipe symbol. For example I get pipe with alt gr + W  (not Icelandic keyboard). 

Another workaround is to add English layout and you can get pipe with altgr + <. Unfortunately I don't know how icelandic keyboard look like.

Regards.

### Post by Lars Noodén on 2012-09-13
The pipe can be similar to some of the other Scandinavian layouts.  There should be a key to the [left of the Z](http://www.google.fi/search?q=icelandic+keyboard+layout&hl=en&client=ubuntu&hs=Mvs&channel=cs&prmd=imvnsa&tbm=isch&tbo=u&source=univ) key with the greater (>) and less than (<) symbols.  Is that what your keyboard has?

Press alt-gr (the right-hand Alt key) plus that key to get the pipe.
