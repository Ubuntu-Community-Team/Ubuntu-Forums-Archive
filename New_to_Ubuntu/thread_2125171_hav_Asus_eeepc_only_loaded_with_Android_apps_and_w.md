### Post by fantab on 2013-03-13
If these are the specs of your ASUS Eee PC 4G (7-Inch Display, Intel Mobile Processor, 512 MB RAM, 4 GB Hard Drive), then I suggest you try [**Lubuntu**]("http://lubuntu.net/") instead of Ubuntu. Lubuntu needs far less resources then Ubuntu and will suit your needs. When downloading the .iso, I suggest you opt to download it via [torrent]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BitTorrent"), as its more reliable.

After [downloading]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Lubuntu/GetLubuntu") you can follow the instructions to [burn]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto") .iso to a USB drive.
