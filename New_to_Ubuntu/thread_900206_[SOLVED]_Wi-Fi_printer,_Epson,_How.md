### Post by nicedude on 2008-08-25
More than likely you will find you are sunk with a Lexmark as I was. They don't offer hardly any Linux support for their products and what they did offer ( some driver builder stuff ) just confused me into thinking that there was a chance of it working ( until I wasted a few hours figuring out there wasn't ). So I would recommend you send them a Email explaining that you are not pleased with their Linux support, like I have done. At least if we all start buying PRO Linux devices from companies that will at least write a driver for Linux and then maybe the other companies will start to offer Linux drivers as well. Try looking at [http://www.linuxfoundation.org/en/OpenPrinting](http://www.linuxfoundation.org/en/OpenPrinting) to see what options you have under linux with this printer.
