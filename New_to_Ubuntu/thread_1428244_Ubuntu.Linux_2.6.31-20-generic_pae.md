### Post by sisco311 on 2010-03-12
[Physical Address Extension]("http://http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Physical_Address_Extension")(PAE) allows 32 bit operating systems to use up to 64 Gb of memory.

In Karmic, the [server kernel]("http://http://www.ubuntu.com/products/whatisubuntu/serveredition/features/kernel") has been replaced by the PAE kernel.
[uhelp]/community/EnablingPAE[/uhelp]
