### Post by unutbu on 2009-08-07
I'm not sure I understand you situation fully, but...

If you (1) boot the LiveCD to try out Ubuntu, then (2) mounted your hard drive, then (3) run the installer, then when you get to the partitioning step your hard drive will be greyed-out because you are not allowed to edit mounted partitions. 

In this case, simply reboot, do not "try out" Ubuntu, but rather just go straight to the installer.

Edit: If on the other hand, you can not see your hard drive even when just trying-out Ubuntu, then your problem is different and I'm not sure what you should do.
