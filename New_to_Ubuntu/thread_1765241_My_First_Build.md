### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-05-23
I have a Gigabyte motherboard as well, with a six-core AMD CPU -- and it automatically adjusts it's speed based on the workload.

It's actually better that way, as it runs cooler and will last longer.

If you DO find a way to force it to run a max speed all the time, that won't give you any real performance improvement.
