> The places menu is a jumble with various "places" in no logical order - at least to me.  Can I sort or customize this?

Thanks

Places >home folder. Now you're in nautilus Bookmarks>Edit Bookmarks.

Just drag the bookmarks up and down the list to order them how you want.
