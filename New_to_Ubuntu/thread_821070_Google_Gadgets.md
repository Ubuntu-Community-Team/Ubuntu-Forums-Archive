### Post by jimmy the saint on 2008-06-06
my understanding is that google desktop did not work well with linux where the gadgets was concerned.  I think they released a seperate program for that.  I could be wrong though.
Either way, a better app for that type of thing is the screenlets app that you can find in the add/remove programs application.  It supports google gadgets and works great with ubuntu, as it was designed specifically for linux.  I use it and it works great.  
to use google gadgets, you just hit install, select google gadgets and then copy over the html that google kicks out and you have you gadget!!  Also it works well with the widget layer compiz plugin.
