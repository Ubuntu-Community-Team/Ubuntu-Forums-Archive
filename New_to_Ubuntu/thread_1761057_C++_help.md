### Post by compmodder26 on 2011-05-17
Considering you are just starting out, you will be most likely starting out with simple command line programs.  So I think you would be best suited to just fire up a text editor that has syntax highlighting.  Gedit (Gnome), and Kate (KDE), have this. Then just use g++ on the command line to compile them.  Get familiar with that and the IDEs really won't be a necessity.
