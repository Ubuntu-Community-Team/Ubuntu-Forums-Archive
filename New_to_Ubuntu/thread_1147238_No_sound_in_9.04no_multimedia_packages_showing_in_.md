### Post by mgmiller on 2009-05-03
I also seemed to lose sound when I upgraded from Intrepid to Jaunty.  In my case, I opened the sound icon in the top panel and clicked on "Volume control" to get all the sliders for my sound card.  The slider labeled "Front" was set to 50 %.  I needed to turn that up and my sound returned.

To make sure all your slider controls are visible, click the Preferences button in the lower right corner of the window and then put check marks next to the appropriate entries.

Audacity is there, I have it installed.  It's in Universe, make sure all your repositories are enabled.

Ardour is also in universe, I don't have it installed, but it shows up in synaptic.
