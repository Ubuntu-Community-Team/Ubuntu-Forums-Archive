### Post by jonnywombat on 2010-08-27
Hi there...

Have you tried right clicking on the panel, selecting properties and ensuring the "expand" box is checked??

Once you have done that you can right click on each icon/panel item, deselect "lock to panel" and then select move. You can then drag it to where ever you want. Make sure you reselect the lock to panel option once everything it where you want it.

### Post by migs73 on 2010-08-27
Those icons you are trying to move are a bit pesky (the are in the 'notification area', if you ever have to add them to your panel again). Right click, not on the icons, but the three small horizontal lines to their left. This should then allow you to unlock and move them all in one go.:D

EDIT; Unfortunately your battery display is not in that section, this is part of the 'indicator applet' in the 'add to panel' menu. Try adding a new one and moving it to where you want it and then delete the old one.

EDIT; Sorry for all the edits, I have only just booted up my Ubuntu machine, and was doing this from (poor) memory. DO NOT right click on the three horizontal lines, just left click/hold and drag to where you want it.
