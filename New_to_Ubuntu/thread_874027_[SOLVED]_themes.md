### Post by billgoldberg on 2008-07-30
Extract the .tar.gz file.

And put it in /home/username/.themes

Just put the whole folder there. 

Then in "appearances", click customize and pick the newly installed theme.

ps: .themes is a hidden folder, press "ctrl + h" to view it.
