### Post by empty_spaces on 2009-11-06
Go to your Home folder.
Hit "Ctrl H" to show hidden files.
You should then be able to see .mozilla-thunderbird (any file with a . at the beginning is a hidden file)

.mozilla- thunderbird should have everything related to Thunderbird.
.mozilla has all your settings related to Firefox
