I have installed some themes under my login, but I want too share them with another user, is it possible?

---

### Post by CatKiller on 2009-10-09
Move (or copy if you don't want to change your own settings again) the themes you want system-wide from ~/.themes to /usr/share/themes, and the same for ~/.icons and /usr/share/icons for icon themes.
