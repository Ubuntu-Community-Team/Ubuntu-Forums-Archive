### Post by Redache on 2009-01-29
From a quick Google Search it looks like you have an Nvidia Card. Go to System - > Administration - > Hardware Drivers and it should automatically download and install the Nvidia Drivers. it'll reset your Xorg Conf so I wouldn't worry too much about that.

It'll need to Restart after installing and you should have all the resolutions your display is capable of!.
