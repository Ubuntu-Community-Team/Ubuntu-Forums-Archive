### Post by drs305 on 2008-05-07
My beta version of audacity (1.3.4beta) has the pitch control via the Effects, Change Ptich menu, but it didn't include the tempo feature. Tempo allowed you to speed up the playback without raising the pitch so the playback pitch sounds normal. I sorely missed this feature. You can compile audacity with soundtouch yourself and include the tempo feature. Some of the other audacity features were also omitted from the new version of audacity, so following the procedures below may help you restore other features in a similar fashion.

Here are the links that helped me restore tempo.
