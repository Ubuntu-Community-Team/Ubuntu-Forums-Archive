### Post by Iowan on 2010-03-23
Ubuntu provides a number of ways to share files. Besides Samba(for sharing with Windows), there's [SSH]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/SSHFS"), [FTP]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=79588"), and [NFS]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=249889") (more native to Unix/Linux).
