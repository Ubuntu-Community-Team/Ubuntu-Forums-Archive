### Post by kaibob on 2009-02-10
Open nautilus and go to *Edit > Preferences > Behavior* and put a check mark next to "Single Click to Open Items."

```

```

or in terminal
```
alacarte
```

and enable it in Preferences (file management), then it will be in your System -> preferences menu
