### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2011-07-08
Running applications in wine is a combination of 4 factors:

1. How much do you know about Linux ?

2. How much do you know about Windows ?

3. How complex is the program ? Simple applications such a uTorrent vs complex apps such as games.

4. How much you are willing to read and tweak various settings.

In general, IMO, wine's biggest weakness is with sound (input) such as Rosetta stone.

Many people find it much easier to 

1. Use Linux native applications.

2. Use Virtualization (VirtualBox).

3. Dual boot.

Good luck to you.
