### Post by AndThenWhat on 2008-07-11
There's two things that I would try:

- When you are in the themes tab: click the customize button and go to icons and THEN drag your file into that window.

OR

- Move the file to the hidden .icons file in your home directory (You have to click 'Show Hidden Files' in the 'View' menu)


Hope that helps :)

### Post by Nickocosmicfatplanet on 2008-07-12
neither of those worked. i dragged both the tar.gz archive and the compressed folder containing the icons into the .icon directory (which wasn't hidden). but i figured it out. i just had to right-click the icon that i wanted to change, go to properties, and click the icon image and browse for the .ico file i wanted to use.
