### Post by taurus on 2009-01-20
You can always reinstall GRUB to MBR with the LiveCD if you can't boot your Ubuntu anymore.  You would go through the same steps as though you have just installed windows, wiping out GRUB from MBR.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/RecoveringUbuntuAfterInstallingWindows)
