### Post by westie457 on 2011-09-07
Hello. That 'glassy' thing as you call it is the Unity interface. To use your box without it at the login screen click your user name and enter your password. Before you hit Enter, look at the bottom of the screen. You will see a bar with some boxes in it. Here you are looking for the word 'Ubuntu', Click on this and you get more options. Now you are looking for @ubuntu Classic'.
Select this and hit enter.
You now get the Classic Gnome Desktop and it will load by default until you change it at a future Login.
