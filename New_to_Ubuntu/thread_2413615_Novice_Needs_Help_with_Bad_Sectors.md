### Post by oldos2er on 2019-02-27
There's info on running SMART here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Smartmontools](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Smartmontools)

Five bad sectors isn't much, although I don't know if different rules apply to SSDs as opposed to HDDs. You shouldn't need to do anything in Ubuntu other than keep an eye on the SMART data, and be sure you have good backups in place (which should be the case anyway).
