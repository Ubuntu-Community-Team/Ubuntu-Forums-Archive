### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-05-06
The login screen can no longer be themed, so don't bother with those.

For the other themes, once you download them you have to select them in System > Preferences > Appearance. You may need to click the "Customise" button in order to apply them, if the theme only controls window borders or window contents.
