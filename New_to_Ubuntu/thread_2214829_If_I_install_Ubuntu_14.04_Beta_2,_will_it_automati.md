### Post by buzzingrobot on 2014-04-03
You need dist-upgrade to pull in, among other things, the new kernels released for 14.04.  (3.13.0-22-generic here at the moment.) 

I've found 14.04 to be rather smooth, with a few minor hiccups along the way.  I am, though, prepared to do a complete reinstall if necessary. 

If you aren't, then perhaps its better to wait a couple of weeks for the final release.

If you use a proprietary video driver, I'd recommend waiting for the final release and the end of the kernel updates during the beta.  And, perhaps a week or so beyond release day to give you a chance to watch for reports of AMD or Nvidia driver problems with the release kernel.
