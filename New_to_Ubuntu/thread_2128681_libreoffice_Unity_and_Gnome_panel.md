### Post by newb85 on 2013-03-25
The Unity launcher doesn't have an option for stacks of icons that fly out.  I know Cairo-Dock/GLX-Dock does a nice job of this.  If you install the version in the repos for 12.04, it will add a couple different Desktop Environments you can choose from when logging in.  (Be warned, these DE's might not be very stable depending on your graphics setup.  Your Unity DE should still work normally, though.)
