### Post by wardrich on 2008-10-29
Restart your computer.  When it first starts up, it'll ask you to press a button to enter the SETUP, it's usually F2 or Delete.  Hit the button and it'll take you into the BIOS.  From there, the USB Legacy setting will probably be on the first screen.
