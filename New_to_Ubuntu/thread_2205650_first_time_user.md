### Post by Impavidus on 2014-02-15
13.04 only had 9 months of support, which ended last month. This could explain why you can't update things. The latest version is 13.10 (supported until July) and the latest long term support version is 12.04 (supported until April 2017). 12.04.4 *should* support most of the latest hardware, just like 13.10.

Just one setback shouldn't be enough reason to return to Windows. Things don't always work at once on Linux. Keep in mind that hardware manufacturers don't do everything they can to make their hardware Linux compatible, something they always do for Windows compatibility. This may make life a little more difficult at times.
