### Post by flanagan on 2009-03-29
Try this. *Under System --> Preferences --> Assistive Technologies*, click the large button that says *Preferred Applications*. On the Accessibility tab, there should be a checkbox in the *Visual* section that says, "Run at Start". Make sure it is unchecked and Close your way out of the dialog boxes.
