### Post by audiomick on 2012-06-23
I had a first experience with team viewer about 2 weeks ago. A bloke in Poland logged into my laptop that was in the Internet via WLAN in Donetsk in the Ukraine to program a device that was being used to run the sound system there. I was highly sceptical, but  it worked really well.

My Laptop  was booted into Vista. I think the other bloke was on a Mac, but I'm not sure. I assume the Linux team viewer works just as well. There is even a client for the receiving end  that doesn't have to be installed. You just have to click on it and then tell the person who is logging in the ID and the password.
