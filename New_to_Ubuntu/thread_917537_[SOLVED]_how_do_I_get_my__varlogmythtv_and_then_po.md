### Post by Pro-reason on 2008-09-12
There's no finding or saving to be done.  That is the path to the directory in question.  The asterisk on the end means “all files in this directory”.

To get the contents of all those files, open a terminal and type “cat /var/log/mythtv/*”.  You can also open a text editor and then open the file or files you want.
