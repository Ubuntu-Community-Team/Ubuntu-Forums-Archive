### Post by bugardifieno on 2009-05-23
Not sure if your problem is the same as mine, but what I was getting was an electronic hum/crackle whenever I moved the mouse, irrespective of what I was doing. It was driving me nuts! The fix was remarkably simple. I turned down the volume on the CD channel by going into the volume control and muted it when not using it.
