### Post by 4Orbs on 2010-06-19
The Ubuntu-Desktop that is removed is actually just the meta package which is like a list defining the Ubuntu packages. Your currently installed stuff will remain intact (Gnome, apps, etc). I believe you will also have the opportunity to choose whether to keep the Ubuntu startup screen or switch to the Xubuntu splash.
