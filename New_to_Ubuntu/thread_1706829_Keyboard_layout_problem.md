### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-03-16
I am using English and Greek layouts and I use the two win keys to switch between layouts. I press the left win key to get English and I press the right win key to get Greek.

Go to System>Preferences>Keyboard>Layouts and select Options. Go down the list until you see Key(s) to change layout. Expand the list and look for Left win (to first layout) Right win/menu (to last layout). Or you can choose a different key combination if you want to.
