### Post by tom.swartz07 on 2009-09-29
Im not going to lie to you, you dont need any fancy programs if you just want straight video.

The easiest way is to open the video in firefox or whatever your browser is, then navigate to /tmp/ and your video will be there.

Just make sure you let the entire vid load before you copy it. 

By default, youtube videos are loaded on your computer there, and flash plays it in the browser. If you want to keep it, just copy it from there to your hard drive.

Hope this helps!
