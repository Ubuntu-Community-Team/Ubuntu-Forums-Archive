### Post by a.v.l on 2012-02-14
Just solved my problem by installing the HP 1022 printer on an old Ubuntu 8.04 PC.  It worked perfectly on this computer and when I transfer the printer back to my 11.10 PC it recognised that a driver was required and offered to download and install it from the HP website directly. It went through the automatic process and in three minutes this printer was working normally again.
