### Post by Chronon on 2010-04-13
I would suggest a program such as EasyTAG that will allow you to make sure the tags are complete and accurate and then rename them to any path you like (usually depending on the tags).  Once you do this, you will have a file tree that is sorted by whatever method you choose and you can easily drag and drop these onto your phone as you see fit.  

I organize mine in a path like this:
Audio/Music/%artist/[%year] - %album/%discnumber%tracknumber - %title

Alternatively, you could transfer the music you want first (say in Rhythmbox) and organize them in situ with EasyTAG.
