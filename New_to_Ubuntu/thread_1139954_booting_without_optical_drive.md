### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-04-27
Burn a CD as normal. Connect the DVD drive to the laptop and put the disc in. Turn on the laptop and go into the BIOS screen, and the DVD drive will appear as a device that you can boot from. Set it as the first bootable device and off you go. The procedure from there is exactly the same as any other Ubuntu installation.
