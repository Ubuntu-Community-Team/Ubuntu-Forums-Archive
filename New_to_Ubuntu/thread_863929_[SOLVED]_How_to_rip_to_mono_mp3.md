Does anyone know how to rip to mono? My goal is to get 64 bit CBR mono mp3 (for cell phone ringtone). Is [lame -m -b 64 wav-file-name mp3-file-name] the right command? It doesn't work.

### Post by paker on 2008-07-19
I figured out finally. It should be "lame -m m", the first m is for mode, and the second m is for mono.
