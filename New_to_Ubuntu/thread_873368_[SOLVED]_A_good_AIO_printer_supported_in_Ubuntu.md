### Post by deanjm1963 on 2008-07-28
I would recommend a HP Photosmart C5280. Works out of the box with Ubuntu, photo output is stunning - does full bleed Letter/A4 photos/printing and you can print onto CD/DVD's. I couldn't recommend it highly enough.

Depends where you are, but I picked mine up for A$150 - US$145 or so.

Ink is very economical, they also have large ink tanks for it, the 74XL ( A$48 ) (black) is supposed to do around 750 or so pages and the color 75XL ( A$53 ) 530.
