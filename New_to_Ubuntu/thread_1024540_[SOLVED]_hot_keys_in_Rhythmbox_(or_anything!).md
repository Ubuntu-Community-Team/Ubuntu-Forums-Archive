I don't know why you thought it would be Ctrl. Alt is the usual choice in every application I've ever tried. Including this one.

You can <menu key>-o to get to the Pr_o_perties window. Tab and Shift-Tab will take you forwards and backwards through the fields, or you can use Alt-letter combinations to go to a particular field (although Alt-A alternates between _A_rtist and _A_lbum). Alt-F takes you forward to the next song in the list. Alt-B takes you backward to the previous one. Tab (or Shift-Tab) can also highlight the tabs at the top, allowing you to use the arrow keys to select one of the Basic, Details or lyrics tabs.

For menu items, Alt-letter will bring down the relevant menu, and then just the relevant letter will select the appropriate menu entry.

All as you would expect. Does your Alt-key work in other applications, or is it in some way mis-configured? It's worth noting that Alt-Gr is not the same as Alt. Alt-Gr is probably your Compose key, used for putting in things like µ.
