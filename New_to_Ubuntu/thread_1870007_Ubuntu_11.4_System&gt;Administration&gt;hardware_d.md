### Post by cavh on 2011-10-26
As a workaround, you could choose 'Ubuntu classic' as your desktop session type when you log on - click on your user name and before you enter your password, look along the bottom of the login screen and you'll see a row of information as follows:

your language, keyboard type, and then the session type - choose Ubuntu Classic and let us know how you get on
