### Post by DustyMP on 2009-07-14
There are a couple preference/administration applets that go right off the bottom of the screen in the netbook remix. Below are a couple references I found when I had the same issue. The only workaround I know of is to plug an external monitor to your netbook so you can see the entire window on the big screen. It's what I had to do and works okay since I rarely change those settings.

[https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/ubiquity/+bug/325958](https://bugs.launchpad.net/ubuntu/+source/ubiquity/+bug/325958)
[https://bugs.launchpad.net/netbook-remix/+bug/385345](https://bugs.launchpad.net/netbook-remix/+bug/385345)

### Post by Oriolo on 2009-08-26
Alternatively, you can kind of solve this problem by resizing the font resolution:
At preferences > appearence > font tab, press the details button and diminish the font resolution until the window you meant can be fully seen. This of course can make the text on the windows barely visible or worse than that, depends on the quality of your sight, but it is worth trying.
