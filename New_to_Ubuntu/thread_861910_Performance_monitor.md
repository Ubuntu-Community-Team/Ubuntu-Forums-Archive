### Post by Canis familiaris on 2008-07-17
System->Administration->System Monitor.
Choose the Resources Tab.

If you want to monitor always.
RIght click the panel. Click Add to Panel and Select System Monitor.
It will add the monitor to your panel. You could also view the System Monitor as I posted before this by double clicking it.
