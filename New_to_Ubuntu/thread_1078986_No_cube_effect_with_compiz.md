Hello everyone. 

I am running ubuntu 8.10, I saw a video demo of some cool special effects, and wanted to use them. I did some searching and installed Compiz. One of the things I wanted was the cube effect. I enabled it in the CompizConfig Settings. I rebooted. After I rebooted I used the short cut control-alt-left click. It went into a cyllinder instead of the cube effect I wanted.
I rebooted again. As ubuntu booted, when I could see the desktop, but before the panels had loaded I did the short cut and The cube effect was there, including the cube gears which I also enabled. It froze and I took my hands off the keyboard. It unfroze after a few seconds and went back to my desktop. The panels and everything else had loaded. This time when I hit the short cut again, it went back to the cyllinder. 
Whats going on? How do I get the cube effect to stay?

### Post by snowman12 on 2009-02-23
thwhistler, 

There is an plugin under "effects" in compiz config settings manager called "cube deformation and reflection". Disabling this should turn off the sphere.

hope this helps.
