### Post by tacantara on 2009-11-03
Have you checked to make sure that your hardware is compatible with the 9.10 OS?  There's lots of posts on this forum regarding installation issues, and from the portions I've read, it goes back to hardware incompatibility issues.  Of course, many of the problems came about from people trying to upgrade.  The more experienced folks on this forum recommend clean installs, as they tend to go much more smoothly.

It took me about three attempts to get an upgrade to finally work, and it still has some bugs (internet connectivity, mainly).  I couldn't burn the ISO because I had a box of cheap DVD media that refused to work in my combo drive.  

JMO - you may be better off staying with 9.04 until the existing bugs in 9.10 are fixed. Unless, of course, your hardware isn't blacklisted...then it's back to the drawing board ;)
