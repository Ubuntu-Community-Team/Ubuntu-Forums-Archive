### Post by andersvinther on 2010-12-13
Hi Teddy,

You can automatically sync your email, calendars and contacts between Thunderbird on multiple computers if you use Google/Gmail as a central synchronization hub.

Calendar and contact sync is via add ons and email via IMAP on your Gmail account. This means you will be storing your email on your Gmail account, however you do not need to use the Gmail address to send and receive emails from... you can still use whatever email address(es) you use today (with the exception of the free Yahoo Mail). 

Watch the video here: [http://www.easy-email.net/thunderbird/thunderbird-sync/thunderbird-sync]("http://www.easy-email.net/thunderbird/thunderbird-sync/thunderbird-sync")
