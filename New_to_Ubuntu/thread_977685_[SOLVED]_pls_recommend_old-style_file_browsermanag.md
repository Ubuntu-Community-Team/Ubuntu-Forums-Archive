### Post by Bölvaður on 2008-11-10
> **Ascenti0n said:**
> Bright anything is very uncomfortable on my eyes atm. I've already changed background in Nautilus' main window to a dark blue, but can't find option for sidebar.

Im not going to "solve" the second part of the question as it has already been.

It looks like you are looking for themes.. dark theme.

You can try Dust and Shiki-colours and change the white background in nautilus and other programs like this:

1. Right click desktop background and click "change background"
2. move to the Theme tab
3. Press customize
4. Select the Colours tab
5. Click the box next to where it says "inputboxes" below "background"

pick what ever colour you want :) You can change it from white to black, and have the letters White (the other colour box next to that one is for text colour)
But I suggest to you to have it dark gray.
