### Post by DetroitLibertyPenguin on 2008-12-20
Unfortunately, most Verizon Cast phones, like the LG VX-10000 that you have, will not allow you to use regular MP3s as ring tones. They limit their software so that you'll actually pay them for your ring tones. Usually the only way around this is you use a phone that's not running native Verizon software, like an old Motorola, or a SmartPhone like BlackBerry or Windoze Mobile
