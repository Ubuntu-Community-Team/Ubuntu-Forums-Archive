> Hi.  Can I share a whole drive on my local network?Short answer: YES

Long answer: Unlike Windows, when you partition a drive, it is simply mounted as a folder at the given mount path. When you share that mount path, you are sharing that folder - but because there is a whole partition that you have mounted at the same path, in essence, you are sharing the whole drive/partition.

Get it? I know its a little confusing with all the usage of path all around ...but that's the best I could do.
