### Post by mcduck on 2009-03-04
**G**imp **T**ool**k**it.

(Gtk was originally developed for Gimp, and later made it's way into Gnome's default toolkit.)

GTK is what programmers use to make graphical interfaces for their programs, and also what's responsible of drawing those programs on your desktop. Basically everything you see inside a typical program window in Gnome is drawn with GTK.

You can read more here: [http://www.gtk.org/]("http://www.gtk.org/")
