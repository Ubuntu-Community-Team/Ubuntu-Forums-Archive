### Post by okey666 on 2008-10-24
I presume you mean compiz's wiget layer. I can't help with gdesklets but screenlets can provide a similar system and they are much more updated. 

Find the screenlet daemon in add/remove programs (by searching screenlets) and more screenlets on gnome look. To put one on the widget layer first enable it in compiz config, and then right click on the widget and go:
window>>widget
