OK, the problem is that probably the ports are closed, so you are not connectible. For a better speed you should open the ports that your torrent uses. To do that check out this link:
[http://portforward.com/](http://portforward.com/)
Find your router model, follow the instructions and you should be OK.
Cheers!

EDIT: You can forward ports from your router configuration also. Open a browser, in the address bar type your router IP (192.168.1.1), and somewhere in setting you should find the Port Forwarding section. Forward the ports that your client uses and your speed should improve.
