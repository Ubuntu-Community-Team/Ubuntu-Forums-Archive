I know In kde you can just click the mouse once and your folder ect will open how do you do this in Ubuntu?

---

### Post by nick_h on 2008-07-24
Open nautilus - for example by selecting Places->Home

Then select Edit->Preferences and go to the Behaviour tab.

You need the option "Single click to open items".
