### Post by arochester on 2008-07-25
My USB phone has Windows drivers, but works "out-of-the-box" and without drivers in Linux.

Plug in your USB phone. Open Skype. Check out the S button (bottom left)>Options>Sound Devices. Look on Sound Out and Sound In for USB phone options.
