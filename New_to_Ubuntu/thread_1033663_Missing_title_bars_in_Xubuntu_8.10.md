### Post by john.nicholls on 2009-01-08
Once you have the title bar in place, you need to save the session. Go into the Settings Manager, select Sessions and Startup, and tick Automatically save settings on logout. To save your session immediately, you can right-click on a blank part of the panel, and select Restart.
