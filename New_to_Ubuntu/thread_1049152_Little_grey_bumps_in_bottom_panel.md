### Post by jimmy the saint on 2009-01-24
It looks to me like you chose to use an innapropriate picture for your panel background.  right-click on the bottom panel and choose "properties" in the background tab select "none (use system theme)"

That should get rid of that.  If you use a background for your panel, it should be long, short and not contain and designs that you don't want repeated.

If you want to change your DESKTOP background, right click on the Desktop (not the panel) and select "change desktop background"
