### Post by swoody on 2009-09-05
bubbleoffplumb - Hi and welcome to the forums! In this case you probably wouldn't see that much need for a seperate /home partition. There is nothing saying that you cannot run Ubuntu from a single partition, so what you are looking at is perfectly fine. There won't be anything wrong with letting Ubuntu install to your entire disc. That's how it's setup to be. So go ahead with the install, and you'll be good to go :)

If you want to learn a bit more about Ubuntu in general, feel free to grab a fre copy of the Ubuntu Beginner's Guide in the link in my sig. It's a great read, and will probably answer many questions you may have in the future :D
