### Post by Impavidus on 2013-04-04
Use schragge's link to get a new password.

If you wish you can set to login without giving your password, but disabling your password altogether is a huge security risk and asking for problems. For that reason forum policy tells us not to tell people how to disable their password protection. Once you have properly set up your system you'll need your password at most once a week.
