### Post by halitech on 2009-10-06
depends on the program but typically they will be in either /bin, /sbin or /usr/bin with /usr/bin being the most popular. Programs that you install from outside the repos will normally end up in /opt.

here is a good site that explains things a bit better: [http://www.freeos.com/articles/3102/](http://www.freeos.com/articles/3102/)
