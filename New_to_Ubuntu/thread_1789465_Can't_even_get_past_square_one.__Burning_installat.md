### Post by ExileAmongYou on 2011-06-23
Generally when you download a disc image, it's an .iso file that you burn directly to disc without extracting it. Unless you've downloaded the wrong thing, I suspect that you've downloaded an .iso file, and it's being displayed in your Downloads folder with a winrar icon because winrar can open and extract iso files. 
So basically, don't extract it. Open up your disc burning software and burn that unextracted file you've downloaded directly onto the cd.
