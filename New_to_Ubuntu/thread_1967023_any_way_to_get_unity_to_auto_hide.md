### Post by davcav on 2012-04-27
From the Dash:
Type: System Settings
Choose: Appearance
Choose: Behaviour
Select: Autohide "ON"

Best of luck.

### Post by cryptotheslow on 2012-04-27
Additionally, while in there reduce the launcher icon size to 32 - this will reduce the width of the launcher and also allow you to fit more icons on there without having to resort to scrolling down the bar.
