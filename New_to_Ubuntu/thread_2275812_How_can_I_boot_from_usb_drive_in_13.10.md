### Post by RobGoss on 2015-04-28
Like the current posts stated depending on your machines specs and how old the computer is, F12 will bring up your boot options, F2 will get you in to your bios settings so you can change your boot sequence, this applies for most newe pc's. I have a HP Netbook that gives me my boot options if I us F9. Older computers requires a bit more tweaking.
