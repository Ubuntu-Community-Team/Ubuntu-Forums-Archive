### Post by mgranet on 2010-06-09
Unlock the widgets, then click on the cashew (the round quarter circle thing on the top right), then Zoom out. From there, you should be able to remove the activity you added by clicking on the red X. If they red X is not there, select your primary desktop (Green +), then zoom out again to remove the added activity.
