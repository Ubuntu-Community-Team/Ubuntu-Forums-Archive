### Post by r-senior on 2011-04-08
There's a PPA for Faenza and that's the best way to install it because you get updates.

Instructions here: [http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2010/08/faenza-icon-set-gets-a-ppa/](http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2010/08/faenza-icon-set-gets-a-ppa/)

Once you've installed it, go to Appearance settings and hit the Customize button with your current theme selected. Go to the icons tab and select one of the Faenza icon sets. For Ambiance, you'll probably want "Faenza Dark".

Then "Save As" under a suitable name, such as "Faenza Ambiance". And you have an Ambiance theme with Faenza icons.

Any updates through Update Manager, Synaptic or from the command line will pick up new updates from the PPA.
