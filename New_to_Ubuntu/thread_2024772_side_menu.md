---

### Post by mcduck on 2012-07-14
Go to System Settings->Appearance->Behaviour and turn the "Auto-hide the launcher" -option on.

---

### Post by Old-un on 2012-07-14
The "Auto-hide the launcher" option was on. But i restarted the computer and everything is now working. Thanks for the reply
