### Post by IWantFroyo on 2011-03-01
Try Inkscape in the Ubuntu Software Center. It's supposed to have been based off of Corel.
Also, you really don't need an anti-virus for Ubuntu. It's hard to hack Linux systems, so probably the best thing to do is to get one of the firewalls in the Software Center, and configure it (example: don't accept pings or ftp requests or login requests etc. from all IP addresses or all but your own).
