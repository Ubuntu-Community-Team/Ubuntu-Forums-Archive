### Post by diablo75 on 2008-05-25
You may want to consider disabling Compiz if you're having problems like this to see if it makes a difference.  That line of chipsets is rather low on the totem poll as far as video cards go, and is not capable of handling much at all.  There are many a few major effects in particular that I know can't be rendered.

To disable, go to System>Preference>Appearance>Effects Tab

Then select None from the list if it's not already selected and start using your system as usual to see if you notice a difference.
