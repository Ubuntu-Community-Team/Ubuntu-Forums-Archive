### Post by vasa1 on 2017-05-26
Well, in no particular order,

There's "social engineering", quite platform-agnostic, which may trick you into revealing information that you don't really want to part with.

Then there could be self-inflicted wounds by copy-pasting code from dodgy sites into your terminal.

Keep your software updated. 

Don't use exotic browsers that may not be as updated as Firefox or Google Chrome for sensitive sites.
