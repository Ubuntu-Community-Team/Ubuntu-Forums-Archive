### Post by frostschutz on 2010-07-30
TurboPrint offers proprietary Linux printer drivers for many Canon models. I use them with my own Canon printer. While I do not like proprietary software, TurboPrint has offered me great support in the past, and their drivers have never given me any trouble. They support about the same feature set as their Windows equivalents, from high quality prints to ink level / nozzle cleaning / etc., everything's there. If they support your printer model, it's definitely worth considering. At the very least it beats trying to use actual Windows drivers somehow (which most likely won't work).
