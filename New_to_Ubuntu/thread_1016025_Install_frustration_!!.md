### Post by EvilRobotDrew on 2008-12-19
could it be an issue with your CD drive? if the alternate CD doesn't work, you could try burning the iso to a flashdrive, ([here is a tutorial]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation/FromUSBStick")). 

also i would recommend before actually trying to install Ubuntu, you look up your hardware (i.e. the make and model of your wireless card) and do some quick preliminary research for drivers, print the pages to a PDF and keep them handy. Google is your best tool for this, but make sure you check the last date the post or site was updated, no good (most of the time) trying to follow a tutorial that was written in 2002. also for answers found in message boards, look at the last page or 2 to make sure that the tutorial is still valid (i broke an install of Fedora by not doing this).
