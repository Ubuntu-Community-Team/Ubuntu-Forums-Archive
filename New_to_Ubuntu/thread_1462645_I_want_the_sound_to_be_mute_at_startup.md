### Post by bville09 on 2010-04-25
In Kubuntu 9.10 when you log in, you click on the speaker icon in the tray, and hit the "Mute" checkbox, then it will be muted until you unmute it again, even when you logout and log back in again. Should work for pretty much any version of Kubuntu. This is the easiest way to do it.

If there is still sound, you might just have to disable sound in the Settings folder.

EDIT: Or did you mean you want the greeting sound disabled? (the sound that plays when you log in?)
