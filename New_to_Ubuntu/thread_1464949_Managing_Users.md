### Post by marshmallow1304 on 2010-04-28
If you make yourself an administrator (you already are if you're the first user), you will be able to change all users' passwords.  I don't know how to keep users from changing their own passwords, but as an administrator, you can change their passwords and access their accounts anyway.

You can do everything from System->Administration->Users and Groups
