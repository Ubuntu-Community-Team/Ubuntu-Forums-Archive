### Post by Bartender on 2009-07-13
Are you using Thunderbird's settings to downsize photos before attaching?

Another option - it's sort of long-hand but works.

Drop the picture into GIMP.  Don't try to compress it first.  Go to Image>Scale.  You'll get a new window that indicates the scale in pixels.  Pull the resolution down to 700 or 800 pixels on the long side.  Then save the image as .jpg.  The .jpg windows pops up next.  Turn on "Preview File" so you can see how many KB's the picture is using.  If it's still too big, then you can use the slider to compress the image some.  
I've found that scaling first gives much better results than trying to compress the image first.
