### Post by sharks on 2008-06-30
go to synaptic---settings---repositories and third party software and then unselect cd source.

---

### Post by Seisen on 2008-06-30
Just go to System>Administration>Software Sources and uncheck the box that says Cd-rom it should be located on the very first tab.

---

### Post by bumanie on 2008-06-30
You need to go to System --> Administration --> Software Sources and check the top four boxes and uncheck the cd-rom at the bottom. Then look at third party software and check the canonical repositories mentioned there. After that, the system will do a check for updates to the repositories and you should have less problems.
