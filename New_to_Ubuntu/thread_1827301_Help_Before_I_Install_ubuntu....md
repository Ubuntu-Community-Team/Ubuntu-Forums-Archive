### Post by Kixtosh on 2011-08-17
Most wireless cards I've tried do work 'out of the box", but not all, so, yes: try the LiveCD first, and then you'll know. There are some very simple solutions to try if things don't work right away.

Once you get to the Ubuntu desktop on your LiveCD, look for a wireless icon in the top right panel (like a radar wave type icon). Click on that with a left click (normal click, that is), and you should see all available wireless networks displayed.

Dell machines in particular, in my experience, tend to work well with Linux.

If you are installing alongside Windows, make sure you defragment your drive in Windows at least three times first. It should defragment faster each time.
