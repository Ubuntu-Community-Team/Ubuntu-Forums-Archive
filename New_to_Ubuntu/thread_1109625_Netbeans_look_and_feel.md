### Post by niteshifter on 2009-07-19
Hi,

You can change the look and feel from the command line:
```
netbeans --laf com.sun.java.swing.plaf.gtk.GTKLookAndFeel
```
More L&F info [here]("http://java.sun.com/docs/books/tutorial/uiswing/lookandfeel/plaf.html") (sun.com)
