### Post by Eil on 2008-04-25
Your base system specs should be fine for a 3D desktop, but you don't list your video card which is by far the most important aspect. Although a number of video cards support the nifty 3D desktop features, nVidia cards seem to be the most popular and well-supported (although their proprietary drivers can be a pain).

Compiz was the original 3D desktop window manager and Beryl was forked off from it at some point later. Compiz Fusion is the re-merging of the two projects and is considered the "current" codebase.
