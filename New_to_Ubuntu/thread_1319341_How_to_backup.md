### Post by Paqman on 2009-11-08
It all depends on what you want to back up, how often and onto what.

If it's just some files you want to occasionally back up, then just copy and paste them the first time. After that you can use something like Grsync to synchronise the two sets of files once in a while. Rsync is great because it only copies over the changes, so it's really fast.
