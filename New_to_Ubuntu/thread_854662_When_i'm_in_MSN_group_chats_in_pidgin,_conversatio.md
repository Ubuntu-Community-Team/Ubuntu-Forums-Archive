### Post by k3lt01 on 2008-07-09
You need to check your "Buddy Pounces". One person at a time right click on their name and you'll get a drop down menu, click "Add Buddy Pounce" and that will open another window. At the top you have "Pounce on Whom", below that "Pounce when Buddy.." which usually has "Signs On" ticked. Below that there is "Action" now it seems to me the action in your Pidgin is set to "Open an IM Window", if it is Untick it and that should stop the problem. Do that for everyone on your list.
