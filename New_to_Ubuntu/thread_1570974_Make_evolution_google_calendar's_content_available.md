### Post by rabideau on 2011-01-02
I have found that if you link to Google calendar via the evolution traditional method of:

[LIST]
[*]Create New Calendar
[*]Select Google (as the type)
[*]add your username etc.
[*]select the offline availability option
[/LIST]

The links are fully bi-directional although the delay times are something to be watched for... they can get long and frustrating.  Because the calendar information from Google are loaded into evolution they continue to work if I am off-line. (although you must use the setting allowing the Google Calendar to be available for offline operation)

Otherwise this seems pretty straightforward and functional to me.
