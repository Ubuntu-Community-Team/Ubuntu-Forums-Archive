### Post by sanderd17 on 2011-07-02
What interface are you using? Is it unity?

If you want an interface with no effects at all, you can log in to "Ubuntu classic (no effects)". Select it from the drop-down (or drop-up???) list in the bottom of your screen when you have to fill in your password for loggin in.

Just getting rid of that effect in Unity won't make much difference because Unity uses a lot of graphical bling. Even without those effects.
