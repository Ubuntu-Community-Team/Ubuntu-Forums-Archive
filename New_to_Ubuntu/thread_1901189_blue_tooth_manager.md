### Post by owiknowi on 2011-12-30
short and simple:
1. switch on bluetooth on your phone and set it to be 'discoverable'
2. add the computer as a trusted device
3. go to 'bluetooth manager' on your computer and choose 'add device' and add the phone
4. you can now either browse the device with a file manager or send files to your computer

with these two steps i got most bluetooth devices working (mouse, phone, etc.)
