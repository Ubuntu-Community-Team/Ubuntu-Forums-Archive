### Post by ayoung on 2008-12-10
Check out [Damn Small Linux]("http://damnsmalllinux.org/").  It's *really* small :).  The distribution is 50MB total, with the ability to run on as little as 16MB ram.  The entire OS can be run in your 128MB (ooh, fast!)

Of course, your distro choice will probably depend on what sort of functionality you're after.

Cheers.
