### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2012-11-18
[http://ubuntuforums.org]("http://ubuntuforums.org") is "the forum". This is where all the subforums stem from.

All the links you can see lead to sub-forums (and sometimes sub-sub-forums), like "Absolute Beginner Talk" for beginners, or "Hardware" for those with hardware problems, or "Development and Programming" for would-be developers.

Spend some time browsing, and pick one that sounds suitable for your needs. Be polite and sensible, if someone picks you up on your forum choice, apologise and ask a mod to move it. :)

Welcome to the forums!
