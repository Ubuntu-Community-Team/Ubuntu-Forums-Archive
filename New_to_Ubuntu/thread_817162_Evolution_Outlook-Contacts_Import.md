### Post by BgAV on 2008-06-03
Preliminary processing is required, as there is neither mapping nor export (structure study) facilities in Evolution.
Then proceed as follows:
Prepare a 2lines x15 columns table (Word or Writer /";"=easy replacement of vertical lines of the table!): 
'1;'2;'3;'4;'5;6;'7;'8;'9:a;b;c;d;e;f
'1;'2;'3;'4;'5;6;'7;'8;'9:a;b;c;d;e;f

Copy /paste as plain text (note-pad or Gedit) and import this tab-separated document into Evolution contacts;
Open the line (contact Editor) in order to see and note the various positions occupied by the 15 characters (make a snapshot print);
Then arrange your OutLook "exported" contacts accordingly (don't forget to inset empty columns !) and follow copy /paste etc. as above.
Note: Even with a 50 columns exercise, no way to fill “Categories” ! The “f” column will fill postal Adress (3rd tab of the contact Editor window). No other difficulty (I had 150 contacts).
