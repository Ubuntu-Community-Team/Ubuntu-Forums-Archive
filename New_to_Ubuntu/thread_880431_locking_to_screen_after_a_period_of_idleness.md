Is there a way to make the screen automatically lock after a defined period of idleness.  I'm using 7.10 "Gusty Gibbon"

### Post by Jack M. on 2008-08-05
This is handled through the screensaver preferences. Go to System > Preferences > Screensaver and check "Lock screen when screensaver is active", then drag the slider to choose the amount of time before locking the screen.
