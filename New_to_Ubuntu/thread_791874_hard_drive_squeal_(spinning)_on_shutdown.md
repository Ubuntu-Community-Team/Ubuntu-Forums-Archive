### Post by michaelaly on 2008-05-12
Back up all your data now! - Just something I say anytime a hard drive makes unusual noises.  
That being said does the problem occur at any other time? 
You could try changing the hard drive accoustics settings in the BIOS to run the drive quiet, you suffer a small performance hit for it but it may solve your problem.
