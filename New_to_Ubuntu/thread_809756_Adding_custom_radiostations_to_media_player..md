### Post by bmac on 2008-05-27
I use Rhythmbox and gstreamer + plugins and simply go to [SHOUTcast | Free internet radio!]("http://www.shoutcast.com/") Which has a tremendous listing of internet radio stations.

Select "tune in" on any particular station and it loads / saves the station automatically into Rhythmbox.
