### Post by monkeybanana on 2010-03-24
I got it to work with compiz

scroll to the bottom

enable grid

et voila

good key combinations:

for maxising screen or "Put Center": super + m
"Put Left": super + left arrow
"Put Right": super + right arrow
same for bottom and top with down and up arrows

makes copying code from a website/e-mail to terminal a breeze!
