### Post by lisati on 2010-01-02
I don't have any experience with the modems already mentioned, but have noticed a difference between the two I have. The newer ADSL2+ modem/router/wireless-access-point I have (A Thomson speedtouch model) seems to be a lot more sensitive to wiring and connection conditions than the older D-Link ADSL modem I used to regularly use. I haven't figured out a pattern yet when it decides to "go flaky"*, but checking and rewiring the phone line seems to have made some difference. Avoid extension chords on your phone line if you can.
