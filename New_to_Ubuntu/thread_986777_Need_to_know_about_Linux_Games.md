### Post by bettlebrox on 2008-11-18
Quake, and Quake II should play fine on your laptop, and maybe even Quake III. Also, Unreal, Unreal Tournament should work OK too, and maybe even Unreal Tournament 2003 & Unreal Tournament 2004 (if you've a good enough GPU). For most of these you should be able to download the demo versions and test them.

Plus, any of the games ported by Loki should work as they were ported for hardware that now would be much older than your laptop, and you could probably play the original Half Life using wine.
