### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-11-05
In gnome 2 with gtk2 themes you could easily customise a theme to your own colour preferences using the theme changing dialog from a right click of the desktop.

I don't think it's as easy in gnome 3 with the gtk3 themes that are currently available, and as far as I'm aware, a right click on the unity desktop does absolutely nothing, though I admit I don't use unity as I don't have a machine that will run it properly, ie, 3D, and now the 2D version has gone, I can't get 12.10 to show me any unity desktop at all, just a background image, no panel, no launcher.

Why not simply do a search at [GNOME-Look.org]("http://gnome-look.org/") or elsewhere, for another similar theme, more to your liking.
