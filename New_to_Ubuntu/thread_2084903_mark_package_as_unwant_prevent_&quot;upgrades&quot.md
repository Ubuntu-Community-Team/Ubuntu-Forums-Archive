### Post by Paqman on 2012-11-16
You can do this through the package management tool [Synaptic]("apt:synaptic"), which is well worth having on your system IMO. Click on the package > Packages > Lock version.

You can also do it through "pinning" the package in APT: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PinningHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/PinningHowto).
