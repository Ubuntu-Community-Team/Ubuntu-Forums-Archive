### Post by I2k4 on 2011-05-08
Running 4.0 on Lubuntu 10.10 (I think the same version) alt-V just shows the drop-down View menu, and unlike other choices, there are no key-toggles shown for the Menu, Navigation, or Add-On bars - you have to mouse in to close them.

I'll say, I've always used Customize to drag everything into just one Bar and get rid of the others apart from the Tabs. Especially good on my little netbook.  You can drag all the fields, menus and icons into either the Menu or Navigation bars and close the empty one forever.
