It works just fine for most people, and whilst I recommend a clean install where possible, an upgrade should also be fine most of the time. 
Having said that, always keep a backup of files unless they a) mean nothing to you or b) are easily replaced and renew said backup if you plan on doing anything big (like an upgrade).
