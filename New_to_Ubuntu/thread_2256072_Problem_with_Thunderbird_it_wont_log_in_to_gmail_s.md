### Post by Gordonbp531 on 2014-12-10
Log on to GMail on the web.
Click on your oicture or avatar top right of window, and choose Account.
Click on the Security link.
Click on Settings link of App passwords.
Generate an app password for your Thunderbird install and use that in the mail setup.
