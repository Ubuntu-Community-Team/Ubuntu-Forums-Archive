### Post by quelx on 2008-05-30
run two instances of four in a row 

in the first instance change **Player Two** to **Level two** and **Player One** to **Human**

In the second instance change **Player One** to **Level three** and **Player Two** to **Human**

Match the moves of instance one with the computer's moves in instance two.
