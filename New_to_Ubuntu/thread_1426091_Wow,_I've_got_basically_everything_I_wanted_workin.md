### Post by Everynameistaken on 2010-03-09
Including a network printer connected to a Windows XP computer (which was infinitely EASIER to do than it was in Windows 7) and running Guitar Rig under Wine.  I never thought the latter would work because it uses special drivers and all that sort of thing to reduce latency.

I gotta hand it to the Canonical and Wine folks; they've really gotten it together over the last few years.  Ubuntu installed all my hardware perfectly right from the start and Wine's compatibility list makes me wonder if I need Windows for ANYTHING anymore, and even moreso when you take into account the ridiculous software package that's included right on the Ubuntu installation disc.  I keep having to remind myself that I didn't pirate this, that it's legitimately free software.

There are little rough edges that I could definitely see improving, but overall?  What a fantastic package.
