### Post by Vadi on 2008-06-16
Hiya,

You need to make a key to encrypt it with first. Go to Applications - Accessories - Passwords and Encryption keys. Then click on Keys - Create New Key, and choose 'PGP Key'. Fill out the info, click create key.

Now that you got your key, you can encrypt the folder now. After it encrypts, it'll make an encrypted file (so you'll have the original, unencrypted, and an encrypted one). Feel free to delete the original for safety ;)

To un-encrypt something, it seems you just double-click on it, give it the key password, and it decrypts it.
