### Post by cwsnyder on 2009-03-05
Yes, it is possible to create a multi-session disk from Ubuntu.

No, you cannot change a finalized disk to a multi-session disk.

When you create a quick burn from Places, the burner automatically finalizes the session. Period. End of story.

You have to use Brasero, GnomeBaker or one of the other CD/DVD writers which give you the option to NOT finalize the disk.
