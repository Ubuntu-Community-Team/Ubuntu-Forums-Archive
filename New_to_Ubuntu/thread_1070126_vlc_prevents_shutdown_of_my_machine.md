### Post by Kareeser on 2009-02-14
You'll want to uncheck "Inhibit the Power Management Daemon during Playback" checkbox.

You'll notice Transmission Bittorrent Client does the same :)

It's there so the box won't shut down accidentally if you bump the power switch or something.
