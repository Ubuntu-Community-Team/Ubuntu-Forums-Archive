### Post by AndyOpie150 on 2012-10-22
If having troubles with the above try the Boot-Repair.iso. Just burn it to a CD and when in grub.rescue insert the CD then reboot the computer. It will take 5-7 nimutes to fully load and get you to the home screen. Then just follow the prompts. 
It's also a live CD and will allow you to go on line if connected to a wired internet connection to fix other problems.

Here is the link: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Boot-Repair](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Boot-Repair)
