### Post by Butthead on 2008-11-02
Did you happen to "mount" the floppy disk first?

Go to "Storage Media" in the System Menu, right click on the floppy drive icon, and then slide arrow pointer to "Actions" sub-menu.  You should see "mount" as a choice under there.

When finished using floppy drive, be sure to "unmount" it then too.
