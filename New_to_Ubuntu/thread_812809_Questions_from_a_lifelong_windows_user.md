### Post by naggis on 2008-05-30
Well, I too have used Windows all my working life( since 1993). Now I am retired there is time to have a look at Linux. Can you do the same in Windows - can you heck.

Perhaps a point to bear in mind is that this forum is full of helpful folks who will answer any problems you may have very quickly. You should feel part of the community quickly.
Just go for it like the guys have been suggesting - it will perhaps get your grey matter circulating. Come on in, the water is fine!
