### Post by jeremychristopher on 2011-05-11
Go to:
System>Preferences>Power Management 

Then under the tabs, you can change the settings for each power preference like AC Power and Battery Power. Not to mention, there is also a General tab which applies to having specific power preferences at any given condition. 

The previous instruction may help in order to avoid the screen to dim. The next is to avoid the screen to lock the system.

Go to:
System>Preferences>Screensaver
Beside a slider, you can see "Regard the computer as idle after". The slider must be adjusted for your computer to have a longer time before it idles itself.
You may uncheck the following to deactivate the next features: 
Activate screensaver when computer is idle
Lock screen when screensaver is active
