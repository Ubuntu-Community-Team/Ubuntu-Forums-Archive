### Post by snowpine on 2012-03-23
Welcome to the forums!

I have a 3-4 year old Foxconn bare-bones (I forget the model number but it has Atom 330 CPU) and everything works flawlessly except my Intel graphics. Since your graphics are AMD, you might have better luck. Generally speaking, Ubuntu is very good about detecting hardware, and the vast majority of users don't have to do much "tweaking" post-install.

Testdrive a Live CD or Live USB and you will know for sure, good luck! :)
