### Post by Kellemora on 2008-11-09
Hi Majeh

Don't forget your FONTS also!!!!!

We use the SAME fonts in msWord as we do in OOoWriter, else that can mess up the formatting.

Something else to be aware of is that images ported from msWord to OOoWriter become OBJECT files.  So it's best to just use image placeholders in your documents or convert them to PDF files so they retain everything right where you put it.
