### Post by mixmastamyk on 2010-05-22
Usually this is caused by a disconnected/bad pin on a VGA cable.  Sometimes it is the monitor.  If you lose the red or blue signal, everything looks green.

Try reconnecting the cables.  If that doesn't work, swap each piece with different hardware until you find the culprit.
