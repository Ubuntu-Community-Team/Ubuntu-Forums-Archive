### Post by migs73 on 2010-12-13
Ctrl+Alt+T opens a terminal rather than Ctrl+Alt+F1 which opens a tty.

Either that or go to Applications>Accessories>Terminal and drag it to the panel or desktop, now you have a launcher on your panel/desktop.

### Post by utnubuuser on 2010-12-13
You can also create a launcher for your panel by right-clicking on the panel and selecting "add to panel" etc.

I have my Terminal set up to launch trough a F9 keyboard shortcut. No combo.
System>>Preferences>>Keyboard Shortcuts...  the "launch terminal" shortcut is somewhere near the top...
