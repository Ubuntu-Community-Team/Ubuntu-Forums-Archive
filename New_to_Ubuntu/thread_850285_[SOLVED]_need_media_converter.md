### Post by scragar on 2008-07-05
hdvd is a dead format already, blueray won the war... DeVeDe will convert almost all video formats to DVDs though, just note that you can go above it's estimations(if it say's your going over by around 40% the file will still fit on an DVD in my experience.).


the archive manager can convert archive formats, simply double click the archive, choose file>save as and set the archive type at the bottom of the box.
