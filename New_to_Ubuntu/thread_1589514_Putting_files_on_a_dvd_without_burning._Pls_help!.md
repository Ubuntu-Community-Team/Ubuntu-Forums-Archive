### Post by Crazedpsyc on 2010-10-06
Ok open the applications menu at the top of the screen and from there open Accessories. now click on "CD/DVD Creator". it will open up your device in Nautilus, the file browser. Open the places menu again and choose a folder like your Home folder or documents and that folder will open up. In this window navigate to where your files are. select the files you want to copy and press Ctrl+C to copy them. now switch back to the window showing your device and press Ctrl+V. depending on the size of the files they should appear, or a window will appear with a progress bar and when it is finished the files will be there. You should now click the Write to Disk button in the top right corner of this window.

If you fell more like a dragger you can do that too!
