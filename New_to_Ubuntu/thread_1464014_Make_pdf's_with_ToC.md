### Post by Chronon on 2010-04-27
> **slughappy1 said:**
> How would I go about making a PDF document with a table of contents? I have a strategy guide for a game, geeky I know, and it's falling apart. I want to scan the pages in and make one document that is easy to navigate. It doesn't have to be PDF if something else is easier or smaller.

It seems like Open Office Writer would be a good option.  You can import your scanned images and create a TOC before them if you like.  Then you can export to PDF.  If you plan to print this out then you should scan at a high DPI (say 300 to 600).  If it will only be read on screen then you can get away with much lower.
