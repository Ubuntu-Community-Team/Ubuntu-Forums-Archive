### Post by fahadsadah on 2009-11-18
Virgin use unauthenticated DHCP (and, even if it was, that would be handled by the router, not your computer).
Therefore, Computer->Router->Modem and Computer->Modem will work with no configuration.

I'm on Virgin Media 20Mb.
