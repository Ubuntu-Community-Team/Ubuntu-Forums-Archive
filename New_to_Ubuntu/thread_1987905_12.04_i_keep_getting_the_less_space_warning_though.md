### Post by Mark Phelps on 2012-05-26
A wubi installation uses a single file to hold the filesystem -- so it's not a simple matter of enlarging a partition.

The Wubi Guide (linked below) has some information on enlarging the virtual filesystem:

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide]("https://wiki.ubuntu.com/WubiGuide")
