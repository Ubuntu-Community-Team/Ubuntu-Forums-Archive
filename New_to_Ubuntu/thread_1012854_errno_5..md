### Post by bumanie on 2008-12-16
Errno5 seems to be an I/O error or a media error. I remember a few having issues with 8.04 when it was released. Those who managed to intall 8.04 either used an alternate optical drive or burnt the distro onto another brand of media, a few managed with the alternate cd without changing either of the above. Good luck, a bit of trial and error is probably needed to sort thisone out. Easiest thing would be to try the alternate installation cd or plug a friends optical drive into your computer and try that.
