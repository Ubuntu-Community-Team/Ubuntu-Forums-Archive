### Post by BugBuster on 2009-07-11
My bad!!!!!

I lived with this issue for 3 releases of ubuntu, but at last figured it out..

As I was primarily a windows user I had this habit of using weird characters in file/folder names, so I sorted my photos by the month and year they were taken but used a "@" in their names like
Jan@08, [email]Feb@08...etc[/email].

Today I tried launching f-spot from the terminal and noticed that f-spot could not find the location of the files..so changed the name of one such folder without the @ and voila it worked..

Posting here as an experience..hope it helps someone who tries to someone who carries the windows mentality to linux..like I did.
