### Post by themusicalduck on 2012-07-18
There is a setting on your gmail.com inbox to tell it not to delete emails when accessed by POP. I found it by clicking on the cog in the top right, go to Settings and "Forwarding and POP/IMAP" tab. There is a dropdown menu in POP download to select what you want to happen to your messages.

Also, if you contact Google, they might be able to restore your deleted emails for you.
