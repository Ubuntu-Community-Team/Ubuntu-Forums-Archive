### Post by finer recliner on 2008-08-21
Contact your college's CIT Help Desk and ask them if their email system supports POP or IMAP (POP will download the message to your local computer and take it off the server; IMAP will leave the message on the server) Make sure to ask them what the incoming and outgoing (SMTP) servers are.

for hotmail accounts, read here:
[http://www.mozilla.org/support/thunderbird/faq#webmail](http://www.mozilla.org/support/thunderbird/faq#webmail)
