### Post by timcredible on 2008-08-26
most of the music players in linux work with ipods.  see if rhythmbox can see it - since rhythmbox is installed by default.  if not, install amarok.  if neither program can see it, then you're probably out of luck.  you could also try gtkpod or banshee, but if the first 2 don't work.  unfortunately, apple goes out of their way to make each ipod different so that non-itunes programs have a hard time working with their product.  i have to agree with the other poster, for anyone thinking of buying an mp3 player, do yourself a favor and stay away from apple or microsoft products because they aren't friendly to non-apple or non-microsoft software.
