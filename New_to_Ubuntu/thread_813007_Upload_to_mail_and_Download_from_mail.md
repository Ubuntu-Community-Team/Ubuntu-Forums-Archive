### Post by quelx on 2008-05-30
I'll give you an example with Thunderbird (because I like it)

[http://kb.earthlink.net/case.asp?article=127339](http://kb.earthlink.net/case.asp?article=127339)

Your service provider should have the information you need to replace the earthlink information.

If you need thunderbird installed open **System** > **Administration** > **Synaptic Package Manager** and search for **thunderbird**  it will show up in **Applications** > **Internet**
