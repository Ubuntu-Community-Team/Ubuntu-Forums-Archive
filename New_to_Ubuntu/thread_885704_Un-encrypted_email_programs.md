### Post by huxterby on 2008-08-10
The login is encrypted, the mail page isn't.

I actually use Claws mail with gpg encryption for all my mail. You just need to create a key-pair and then share your public key with people you communicate with so that they can use it to encrypt mail they send to you. You will need a copy of their public key to encrypt mail which you send to them.
