I've never used encfs so I can't evaluate that for you, but I have used truecrypt and have never had a problem with it.  Its reliable and covers just about any scenario I've needed.
You can either encrypt whole partitions or 'containers' which are essentially files or groups of files.  Running truecrypt from terminal is plainly put, simple.  If you are looking for something very detailed and rich, use truecrypt.

If you mention what exactly you are looking to accomplish, I'm sure others can give opinions and recommendations.  There are plenty of choices, this is just my choice.
