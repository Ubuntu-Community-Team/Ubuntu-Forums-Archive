### Post by DCGStudios on 2009-07-06
This is known as Vector-Art. There are filters you can apply in photoshop that do a pretty bad job of it but it does work. Furthermore, REAL vector art is done over a long period of time using the pen tool and opacity settings in Illustrator by trying to recreate the image's lighting and features. A good vector artist can make amazing real pictures into extremely detailed "cartoons". 

Example: [IMG]http://fc04.deviantart.com/fs28/f/2008/108/e/0/vector_art__house_m_d__by_mart_art.jpg[/IMG]
