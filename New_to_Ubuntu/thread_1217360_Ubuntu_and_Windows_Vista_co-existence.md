### Post by ssubluesman on 2009-07-19
IIRC after you've put your Ubuntu disc into the cd-rom and rebooted you'll be given the option of trying out Ubuntu without installing it. This way you can test Ubuntu without actually going through the installation process.

There are guides for installing Ubuntu for a dual-boot, and it is recommended you back up your hard drive. [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/Installation)

You can choose your method of installation there. I burned it onto a cd and installed it that way, so I used the GraphicalInstal page and didn't have any problems.
