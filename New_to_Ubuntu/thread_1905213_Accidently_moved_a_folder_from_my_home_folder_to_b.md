### Post by jbru on 2012-01-06
> **Tahinimoon said:**
> Hi, 

I accidentally, while browsing through my pictures folder, (I do not know how exactly) moved my 'pictures' folder from my Home folder and it is now in my bookmarks. How do i get it back into my home folder. I have tried 'moving' it but it does not work.

Go into your bookmarks folder so you can see the Pictures folder.  Right click on the "Pictures" folder icon and then click "Cut" from the menu that appears.

Navigate back to your home folder, then right click on a blank space and click "Paste" from the options in the menu.

This should move your folder back to the correct place.

Good luck!
