### Post by AndThenWhat on 2009-05-15
Doing a fresh install of 9.04 will erase your data. However, Evolution's settings and all of your downloaded mail in Evolution can easily be backed up by copying the hidden ".evolution" folder in your home directory.  Just hit CTRL+H to show/hide hidden files in the file browser.
