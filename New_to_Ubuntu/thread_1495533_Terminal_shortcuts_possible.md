### Post by webtechquery on 2010-05-28
Hi there..

Well, I think i have your answer.

To avoid terminal when you know that you are gonna run sort of software or any other commands individually, then you can use the Launcher.

Simply goto your Desktop, and choose Create Launcher.

A popup form will appear.

In **Type**, choose [B]Application in Terminal
[/B]Give the name of that shortcut, appearing in your desktop..
and give the command in the **Command** field, for example, **sudo gedit**

Click ok, and you're done. 

Hope it works for ya.
