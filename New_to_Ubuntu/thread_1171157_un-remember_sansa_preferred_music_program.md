### Post by flamencoblanco on 2009-05-27
I set my Sansa player to "always open" with Rhythmbox.

How can I change or reset my choice, the next time I plug in my player?

Edit: Nevermind. I just figured out how. I had to launch Nautilus, then go to Edit > Preferences > Media > Music Player. Not terribly intuitive, but whatever. One would assume that right-clicking on the Sansa icon (from the Desktop or Places menu) would allow a way to change its default action.
