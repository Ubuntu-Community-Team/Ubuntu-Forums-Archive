How can I resize a video, so I can only watch the left side? ---

### Post by Holger_Gehrke on 2018-07-20
In the menu in vlc: Tools->Effects and Filters->Tab Video Effects->Tab Crop. Enter the number of pixels to cut off on the right in the field labeled "Right". Enjoy !

Holger
