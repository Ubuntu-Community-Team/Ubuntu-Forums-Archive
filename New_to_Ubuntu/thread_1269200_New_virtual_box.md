### Post by Excedio on 2009-09-17
Very simple to upgrade. Just click the download link and it will download a deb file to your computer. Then just double click the deb file. It auto removes the current version and installs the new version without hurting any of the current Virtual Machine setups.
 
A positive for me was that it's now recognize my Canon printer.
