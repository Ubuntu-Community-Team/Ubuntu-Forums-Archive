### Post by oldfred on 2011-07-12
There was just another poster with similar issues and a 1TB drive. I have seen this several times. I really think it is a BIOS issue on LBA or some related setting. 

But a good work around and better way to configure drive is to have a smaller system partition of 20-25GB and make a large /home or other partitions for data.

Note that some system files are scattered all over drive and that slows down loading system files slightly.
