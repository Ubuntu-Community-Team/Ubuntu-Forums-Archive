### Post by jbrown96 on 2008-07-01
Most likely interference from some of the other channels. I'm not sure about your audio setup, but it's generally a good idea to enable the fewest possible channels. In a terminal, type ```
alsamixer
``` Use the arrow keys and try muting various channels.
