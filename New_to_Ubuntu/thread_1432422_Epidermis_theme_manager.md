After numerous times unsucessfully changing my login screen I came across this program that can change your themes the easy way without the codes,so on and so forth!!Its called Epidermis theme manager,[FONT=arial]The latest v**ersion 0.4 of Epidermis was released 01 november 2009 **, the new version support also changing the Xsplash themes, so changing the look of your boot-up screen is now as simple as changing your wallpaper.[/FONT][FONT=arial]The Epidermis skin handles the following themes,wallpapers[]("http://www.unixmen.com/linux-tutorials/517-change-the-xsplash-themes-easy-way-with-epidermis#"), Metacity window border themes, GTK widget themes ,Icon sets ,GNOME splash images, Mouse cursor themes, Grub bootsplash screen,Usplash bootsplash screen,GDM login screen[/FONT] [FONT=arial]_Changelog of version 0.4 :_[/FONT]
