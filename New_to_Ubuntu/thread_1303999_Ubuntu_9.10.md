### Post by sn3rd on 2009-10-29
> **suyog said:**
> I am also waiting for 9.10.

Can anyone post how to download via Torrent? or any other method to boost speed?

You need to download the .torrent file.

Then, using a torrent client (Transmission is the default on Ubuntu), you download the file.

It's faster because you're pulling smaller pieces of the file from various people around the world, and some of them are pulling small pieces from you. Thus, the load is shared (instead of concentrated on a central server), and everyone gets better speeds.
