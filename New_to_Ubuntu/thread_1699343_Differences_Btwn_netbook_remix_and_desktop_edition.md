### Post by Paqman on 2011-03-03
> **maryalesia said:**
> Other than the unity desktop, are there any major differences between the ubuntu netbook remix 10.10 and the 10.10 desktop edition?

Not really, no.

Technically the package ubuntu-netbook does have slightly different dependencies than the package ubuntu-desktop, which results in a slightly different load of packages getting installed, but the differences aren't really worth bothering with. Underneath the UI the nuts and bolts of the system are the same.

You can actually switch back to the regular interface from the netbook one by logging out and changing your session from the bottom of the screen. So pretty much everything that's on the regular desktop is there on the netbook.
