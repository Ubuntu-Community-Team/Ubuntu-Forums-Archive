### Post by aheckler on 2008-05-01
Right-click on the Desk 1 and Desk 2 thing and click Preferences. Changing the number of columns and rows will give you more "desks".

### Post by johnn1949 on 2008-05-01
You need the   compizconfig-settings-manager.  it's in synaptic.

go to preferences--> advanced desktop settings.

open "general" settings.  Under desktop size change the top number to the number you want.  Going down the screen 4,1,1 would be four desktops.

have fun with all the other settings.
