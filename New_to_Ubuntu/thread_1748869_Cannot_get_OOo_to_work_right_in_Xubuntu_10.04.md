### Post by mikodo on 2011-05-04
Well here's the answer:

Find a .odt file > right click > Properties > Open with OOo Word Processor > Close.

Makes OOo the default Word Processor.

Got this from [https://bbs.archlinux.org/viewtopic.php?id=60168](https://bbs.archlinux.org/viewtopic.php?id=60168)
