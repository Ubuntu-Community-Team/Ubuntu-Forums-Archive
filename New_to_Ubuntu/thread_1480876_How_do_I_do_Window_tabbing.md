### Post by adam22 on 2010-05-12
The application switchers can be found in the Compizconfig Settings Manager.

If you don't have it, do to the Synaptic Package Manager and type in Compiz...grab the CCSM and the extra plugins, and then go to system>Prefs>CCSM.

Scroll down to the bottom to Window Management. You will see various switchers...place a check next to the one you want and then click on it. To the right you will see a key combo, either use that, or click it and you can set your own.
