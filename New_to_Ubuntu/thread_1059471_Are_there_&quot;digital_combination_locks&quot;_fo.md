### Post by Hospadar on 2009-02-03
As for the logins, i would imagine it's possible, but not particularly useful if someone tries to guess your password with brute force, because they will immediately use up your automatic failures, and then proceed to guess your password.  If someone actually knows your password, they'd probably just assume they had a typo and get it right anyways, or know that you have aformentioned protection.

As for the email, there's no way to tell if people have copied your email, but you could encrypt the email so no-one could snoop on it.  You can use Gpg to do this, you should check out the community docs in my sig for more info on that.  search for email encryption.
