### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-02-18
What's your host MS Windows OS? If it's Vista or Win7, the old XP-era "My" folders are not used anymore -- which is why they would show up as empty.  When you click these in Vista/Win7, you're being automatically redirected to the "new" locations of these folder -- which is why you see contents then, but not in Ubuntu.
