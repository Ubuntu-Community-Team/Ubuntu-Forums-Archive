### Post by Cypher on 2008-05-28
As Golem has indicated, the repository is in not a static representation of the packages that are available for a particular Ubuntu release, new version of packages and new packages are added when deemed necessary.

Rather than creating a DVD with the contents as they are today, you could mirror the repository contents onto a local (external) HD so that you can update your machine or update others easily/quickly. You could then keep your local mirror up to date with the greatest of ease..
