### Post by iaculallad on 2008-08-15
We'll, those are either application patches/updates or could be security patches/updates. You could minimize of what could be downloaded for update by re-configuring your "Software Sources" (System->Administration->Software Sources), under the "Update" tab, you could uncheck:

- Important Security Updates
- Recommended Updates
- Pre-released Updates
- Unsupported Updates

(It's your option if you want to uncheck "Important Security Updates" because you'll be needing this)

and, you might also want to change the frequency of your updates to use:

- Daily
- Every two days
- Weekly
- Every two weeks
