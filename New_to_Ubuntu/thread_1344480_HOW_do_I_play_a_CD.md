### Post by Bartender on 2009-12-03
The default player in 9.10 is Rhythmbox.  I don't remember what the default is in Jaunty.  There are about a dozen different players you could download and try.
Audio CD Extractor (also called Sound Juicer) works well as a player or ripper.  Most, if not all, of the "movie players" such as Totem and VLC, etc. will play an audio CD as readily as a DVD movie.
