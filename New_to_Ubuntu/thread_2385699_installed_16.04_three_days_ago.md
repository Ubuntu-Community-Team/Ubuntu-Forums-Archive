### Post by QIII on 2018-02-23
Hello!

You might do well to start a support thread for each of these things individually.  

The issue with the monitor is likely a simple setting.

The problem with viewing your media may be that the sites are optimized for Windows software.  No amount of distro jumping will provide relief if that is the case.  "Crypto error" doesn't help us much.  The exact error would be helpful.

The "quick booting" of Windows is a gimmick -- it doesn't actually start from jump.  When you shut Windows 10 down it does not shut down entirely, but enters a modified hibernation mode.
