### Post by phidia on 2008-07-01
You should be able to get the icon (shortcut/launcher) back by right clicking the panel and choosing "Add to panel.." from the window that comes up choose the Application Launcher tab and navigate to the menu item for your music player.

---

### Post by sayakb on 2008-07-01
Maybe you removed the notification area applet. Right click on the panel and select **Add to panel. **In the list, select **Notification Area** and press Add.
Otherwise, you may kill banshee by typing this in a terminal:
```
killall banshee
```
