### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-07-30
Are you trying to play them directly off the VCD, or from your hard disk?

VLC should be able to play them from your hard disk.

If you're basically trying to play a VCD though, you need to play it as a disc rather than as individual files. Play it the same way as you'd play a DVD, in VLC.
