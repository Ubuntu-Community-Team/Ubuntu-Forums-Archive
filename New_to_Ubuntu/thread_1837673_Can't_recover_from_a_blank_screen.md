### Post by jfed on 2011-09-02
Pulling the power chord out? I assume you're using a desktop, thats not a very good thing to do!

Perhaps you can try other key presses, my mouse wont bring up the login screen, but if I press something like the space bar its fine.

You can also change the fact that it goes to a blank screen in your screen saver options, you can make it not require your password again after it goes idle, see if that changes anything. If not you can always just disable the screen saver.
