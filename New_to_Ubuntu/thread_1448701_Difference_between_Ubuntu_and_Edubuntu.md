### Post by valbaca on 2010-04-06
From the [Edubuntu FAQ ]("http://www.edubuntu.org/FAQ")

> What are the differences between Ubuntu and Edubuntu?
Take the [_tour of Edubuntu._]("http://www.edubuntu.org/screenshots") There are many school-related applications installed by default, including TuxPaint, TuxMath, and TuxTyping, among others. Edubuntu can also refer to the Classroom LTSP server, which is a thin client based system.

It seems to have a simpler install, interface, and lower systems requirements. I couldn't find any specific minimum requirements though.
