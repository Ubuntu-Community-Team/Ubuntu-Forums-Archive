### Post by ciscosurfer on 2008-11-13
Maybe you have an F-Lock key?  Maybe you've hit it by accident?  Also, hover your mouse towards the top of your screen and it should drop down, then hit the maximize icon.  **F11** (F eleven) is the Full Screen F-Key though.  You can also try** Alt-v**, then **f**
