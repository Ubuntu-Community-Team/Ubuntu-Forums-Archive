### Post by megalodon1970 on 2010-10-16
Ok, First off i'm not a nOOb when it comes to trying Linux, but it is a pain in the rear to find a great distro that takes very little
to set up and use. I've had my touchsmart for a year and a half and
I love it. When installing linux....not so much. The wifi drivers &
touchscreen were hard to get working....until now. All you have to
do is install ubuntu, get a lan cable and update the system, get the drivers activated and your ready to go. I'm not a firefox fan
and since I.E. is a no go, I installed Chrome which is really fast
on this particular laptop. The wifi drivers are under system and
administration tab, just click on the additional drivers icon and
a dialog box will pop up and you will be able to download the right
drivers for this rig. I transferred all my music and pics...boom! I
have a great and capable system.
