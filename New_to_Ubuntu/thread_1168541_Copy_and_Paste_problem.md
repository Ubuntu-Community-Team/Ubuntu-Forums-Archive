### Post by mcduck on 2009-05-24
Are you trying to copy&paste from a terminal? In that case simply highlight the text you want to copy and use middle click to paste it.

Or alternatively either use shift-ctrl-c & shift-ctrl-v or copy&paste options from the right-click menu.

ctrl-c has completely different meaning in terminals (close currently running program) so it can't be used for copying.
