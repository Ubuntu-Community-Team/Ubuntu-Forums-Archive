### Post by marcopolo1981 on 2009-11-17
Download the [http://download.macromedia.com/pub/labs/flashplayer10/flashplayer10_1_p1_linux_111709.tar.gz](http://download.macromedia.com/pub/labs/flashplayer10/flashplayer10_1_p1_linux_111709.tar.gz)
Right click on the downloaded file and click "Extract here".
Open a terminal and type:
sudo nautilus (ENTER SUDO PASSWORD)

Navigate to /usr/lib/firefox-addons/plugins

Copy or move the libflashplayer.so into this folder.

Close nautilus.

Close terminal.

Restart firefox

Have fun on youtube!
