### Post by markbuntu on 2008-06-25
You can also use bum, (Boot Up Manager) to stop the loading at boot of all the extraneous stuff you don't need, use, or want like bluetooth and laptop power saving, nvidia, etc. I got rid of about a half-dozen things that bootup that I don't use or need and boot is much faster now on my machine.

You can also speed up boot time by doing the same in System/Adminstration/Services and System/Preferences/Sessions.
