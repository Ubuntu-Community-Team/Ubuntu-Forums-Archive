### Post by philodice on 2010-04-11
I had this problem until I realized I had changed bios to boot from USB first in order to install.  I simply changed the BIOS order back to booting from the hard drive first.
The Acer makes this really very easy, just hit the f2 or f12 during boot and change from there.

I hope that helps.
