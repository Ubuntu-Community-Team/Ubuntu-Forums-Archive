### Post by cariboo on 2009-04-12
I would suggest you start in recovery mode, and when at the menu select drop to root prompt. Once at the prompt type:

```
passwd <username>
```

substitute your userme for <username>. This will allow you to change your password. Once finished type exit, then select resume, this should get you back to your desktop.
