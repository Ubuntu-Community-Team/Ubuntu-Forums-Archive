### Post by GWBouge on 2011-12-22
Goto the 'Main Menu' icon (it was in that list in the picture).  In there you can enable/disable or add/remove what you want.

Alternatively, you can goto /usr/share/applications/ and delete the corresponding .desktop file manually.  Or, just rename it to something such as the-program.desktop.bak so it just won't show up, but you won't lose it in case you change your mind.
