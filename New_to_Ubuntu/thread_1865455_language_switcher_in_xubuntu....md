### Post by ankspo71 on 2011-10-20
Hi,
Try installing 'xfce4-xkb-plugin' and then add "Keyboard Layouts' to the panel. Right click on panel > Panel > Add New Items > Keyboard Layouts. This works good for me in Xubuntu 11.10. Here is what it looks like: [http://goodies.xfce.org/projects/panel-plugins/xfce4-xkb-plugin](http://goodies.xfce.org/projects/panel-plugins/xfce4-xkb-plugin)
