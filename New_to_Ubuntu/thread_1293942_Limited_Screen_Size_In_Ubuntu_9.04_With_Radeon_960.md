### Post by Mark Phelps on 2009-10-17
In (what you called) WinBlowz, you DID have ATI drivers; in Linux, any version newer than 8.10, you do NOT.  

AMD dropped Linux support for all the but newer HD 3X/4X/5X cards a few months back, such that, there is NO ATI restricted driver that works with your card and any Ubuntu version newer than 8.10.
