### Post by CharlesA on 2014-02-19
Well, a kernel panic usually means there is a problem with the hardware or the software running on the machine.

Smoke doesn't sound normal to me. I've heard of episodes of Ubuntu running the CPU at 100% and making it so the cooling fan can't keep up, but it's hard to tell what exactly is happening with your machine, but I will suggest you turn it off and unplug the battery (and get a fire extinguisher if possible).
