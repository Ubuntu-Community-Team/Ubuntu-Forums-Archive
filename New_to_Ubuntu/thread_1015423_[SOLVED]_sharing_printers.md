### Post by donkyhotay on 2008-12-18
goto system > administration > printers and make certain the printer is said to 'shared'. Then make certain port 631 is open on your firewall then point the client computer to print to a network printer @ the IP address of the host computer.
If thats confusing read this blog:
[http://blog.mypapit.net/2008/05/enable-printer-sharing-with-ubuntu-computers.html](http://blog.mypapit.net/2008/05/enable-printer-sharing-with-ubuntu-computers.html)
