### Post by sikander3786 on 2011-02-02
First of all, there is no direct path of upgrade from 8.10 > 10.10. You need to go in steps from 8.10 > 9.04 > 10.04 > 10.10. That means you need to upgrade it 3 times to get to 8.10.

However, I would suggest that you stay with the LTS release i.e, 10.04 as it would be supported for 5 years on the server as compared to 10.10 which will be supported for 18 months only out of which, 4 have already past.

How do you remotely connect to your server? SSH?

And I am not trying to frighten you but upgrades are known to break systems at a few occasions. I hope it won't happen for you but it would have been better that either you or some one else capable of doing the repairs, stays near the system in-case anything goes wrong.

As far as downtime is concerned, if everything goes fine, the down-time shouldn't be more than a reboot.
