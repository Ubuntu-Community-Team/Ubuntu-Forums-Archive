### Post by AmirM on 2010-10-28
is it any model/version of ADSL?
if yes after u connect ur modem w/ ethernet or USB cable it shows a double computers in notification area(up right)
right click on it , choose edit connections. a window will show up with multiple tabs.one of them is DSL that u want to click on.
from right menu u choose ADD and fill it with the information.

after creating a connection,left click on the two computers that i mentioned before and u see the connection name.click and its done.
