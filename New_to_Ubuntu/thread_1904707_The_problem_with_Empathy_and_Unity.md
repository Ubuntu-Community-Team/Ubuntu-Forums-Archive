### Post by jermza on 2012-01-05
I'm beginning to like Unity more and more. But something that doesn't work right (yes, another thing) is the way in which the Unity launcher (doesn't) remind you about an Empathy conversation.

1. Friend sends you a chat message.
2. The envelope, at the top right, lights up.
3. You click on it and see that there is a message waiting in Empathy.
4. You click on it and the chat window opens.
5. You reply and then minimise the window while you continue working.
6. When your friend replies back, the Unity icon wiggles, telling you that he replied.
7. If I was away from my desk, then I would have missed the icon wiggle and there is nothing informing me that he replied. Not even the envelope is lit up.

The only time the envelope seems to light up is when a new conversation is started. And the icon only gets a little number when a new conversation is started by someone else.

This is not particularly intuitive. Do you know if the developers will be evolving this obvious notification flaw?
