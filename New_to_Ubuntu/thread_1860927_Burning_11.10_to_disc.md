First of all CD-R is for single use only. You cannot erase and rewrite new data on it. For this you need CD-RW which is rewritable disk and which you can reuse after erasing the data on it.

**Get yourself a new blank CD-R or CD-RW and burn the .iso to it using Brasero. Burn it at the slowest speed possible**. For more instructions follow [**this link**]("http://www.ubuntu.com/download/ubuntu/download").
