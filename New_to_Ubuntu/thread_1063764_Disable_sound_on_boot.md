### Post by diablo75 on 2009-02-08
Click System>Preferences>Sound  and then inside that, click on the Sounds tab at the top.  You'll find all the sound effects in use there.

If you want to get rid of that sound that plays at the login window, click System>Administration>Login Window>Accessibility (tab).

### Post by drs305 on 2009-02-08
In recent versions, you can disable the login sound via System > Administration > Login Window > Accessibility. 

System > Preferences > Sound > Sounds gives you an option to turn off all system sounds.

**Added**: Also in this section, although to me it is not entirely intuitive, you can customize the sound played by clicking on "Default" and a "Customize" option will appear.
