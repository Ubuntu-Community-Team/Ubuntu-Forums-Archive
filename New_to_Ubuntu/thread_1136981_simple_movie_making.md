### Post by billgoldberg on 2009-04-27
There a quite a few movie editors for linux, but none of them very stable or good (in my opinion).

There is lives, cinnerella (or something like that), kino, open movie editor, kdenlive, ...

If you can live without the adding of titles on the end, it's very simple to add audio to a video without needing a video editor.

Install this, it's the easiest way I have found to add audio to videos:

[http://www.getdeb.net/search.php?search_distro_id=9&keywords=pytube](http://www.getdeb.net/search.php?search_distro_id=9&keywords=pytube)
