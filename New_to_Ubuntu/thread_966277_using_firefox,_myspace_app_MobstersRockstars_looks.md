### Post by PierreDeKat on 2008-11-01
Just an example of poor website design, if you ask me. 

Website proprietors are supposed to be thinking about different people with different computer setups, but sometimes you get one who thinks the whole world should be using Internet Explorer at 800x600 resolution.

Anywho, try View->Zoom->Zoom Out (for starters). If that fails, go to Edit->Preferences->Content, then click the "Advanced" button under "Fonts & Colors" and adjust your fonts to something a little smaller.
