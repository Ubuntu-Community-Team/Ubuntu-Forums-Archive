### Post by cpetercarter on 2008-06-12
I may be able to help on one of your questions.

I think that you mean that the "maximised" and "unmaximised" size of the windows is almost the same. ("Minimised" is when the window disappears into the panel at the bottom of the screen). When the widow is "unmaximised", position the cursor at one edge - you will see that it changes shape -and then use the left mouse button to drag the side of the window to the desired position. Generally, your computer will then remember the new window size/shape for that application.
