I have used this successfully on Hardy for a Shuttle iPod.

### Post by madu on 2008-09-23
There is a workaround you can do with gtkpod, but even then I think atmost you can put music files in there.. not movies..

The easiest way would be to jailbreak your iTouch and install Open SSH.. then just SSH connect into your iTouch and transfer any file you want.. mp3.. movies..pdfs.. i have installed 'aviplayer' and 'FileBrowser'... just browse to wherever you put the files to and click.. it will automatically run from aviplayer... you can also have youtubedownloaders.. heaps there.. but that is only if you want to jailbreak.. until then I am not sure how you can get anything into iTouch except for mp3s...
