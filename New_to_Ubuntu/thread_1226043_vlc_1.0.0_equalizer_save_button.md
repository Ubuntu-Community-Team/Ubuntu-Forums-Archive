### Post by DarinB on 2009-07-29
Ok i have finally figured this monstrosity out.
talk about making it difficult when so many ubuntu apps are easy as far as setting go >>>>>
1 open vlc from menu bar
2 tools 
3 Preferences 
4 at bottom left choose show settings all
5 upper left below advanced setting expand audio
6 click filters check equilizer with 10 bands
7 go back to left column and expand filters 
8 click equalizer under that 
then you can pick the preset and hit save then any vlc from any app will have those presets. 
It would be easier to just put the save button on the player under show extended settings.
