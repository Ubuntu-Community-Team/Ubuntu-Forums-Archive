### Post by Juffo-Wup on 2008-04-24
You can check in System->Software sources, or in Synaptic under the Configuration->Repositories menu entry (which opens the same dialog).

Also you can check that directly in the /etc/apt/sources.list config file.
(everything that starts with a '#' is a comment and thus ignored by the package manager).
