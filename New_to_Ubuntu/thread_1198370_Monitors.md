### Post by QIII on 2009-06-27
I doubt you are pierced by a fastening device characterized by a thin conical body wrapped by a sharp, raised helical ridge.  Which is to say you are not screwed.

I'm sure your card will give you that resolution as an option.  What that means is that physically your monitor will be a little more squarish (still wide-screen, but not as thin a rectangle.)

If you play a wide-screen movie on it, you may have half an inch of black across the top and bottom to keep the same aspect ratio.

Not even an issue to worry about!
