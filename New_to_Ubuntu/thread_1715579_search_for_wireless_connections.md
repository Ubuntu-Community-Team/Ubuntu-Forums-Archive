### Post by jerryatrick on 2011-03-27
I installed Ubuntu 10.10 and could not do a search for wireless connections.  Finally out of frustration I decided to add a connection (my own).  Right click the wireless icon at the top of the page, select edit (I think) and when given a chance, click add.  On the SSID line enter the name of the connection and the password on the appropriate line.  Bingo!  For me at least, when I clicked the wireless icon I got a drop down list of all wireless networks within range.  Keep in mind that I am a rank amateur with this OS so your results might vary.
