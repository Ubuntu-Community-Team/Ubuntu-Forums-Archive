### Post by spydeyrch on 2009-12-14
I believe tha tyou have to actually click on the time/date and a dropdown calendar will appear. Down towards the bottom of that, you should find somewhere that allows you to click on edit current location or something similar. In the new window that shows up, you should be able to see some tabs. One is location, other time, other weather (I believe). You can add your location by typing in the city. Sometimes you will have to chose a major city near your area because it won't have the smaller ones. I have to put in portland even though I live in a smaller city nearby. Once you do that, you should see the weather, time, and date in your notification area. Hope it helps!! ;)
 
-Spydey

### Post by ST3ALTHPSYCH0 on 2009-12-14
Right click on panel> add to panel> weather applet.
Right click on applet>move.
Move where you like.
