### Post by 3rdalbum on 2008-12-07
I'd suggest not buying an iPod, and instead buying an MP3 player that works through drag and drop, like the Sony Walkmans. You might not have heard that Apple tried to put DRM into their iPods a few months ago to stop Linux users from being able to work with new iPods. It failed because the hackers managed to reverse-engineer it and get them working, but there's no guarantees that Apple won't change the iPods again overnight to stop 3rd party management programs from working.

The other good thing about buying a drag and drop MP3 player is that you're not tied to an operating system that has a loader program available for it, and if you need to make copies of the music on your MP3 player you can just copy it off easily (unlike the iPod which scrambles your music).
