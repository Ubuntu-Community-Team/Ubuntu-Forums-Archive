### Post by Therion on 2008-09-16
Click on the email messages you do NOT want going to your junk folder, and mark them, individually, as "Not Spam" or "Not Junk".  Can't recall what Evolution mail calls them...  There should be a menu button for that under the right-click menu, or buttons at the top that indicate Junk/Not Junk.

You can either select "Mark as Junk" under the edit or context menu &#8212; or simply click the junk button on the toolbar. 

Ah yes... The option is in the [context menu](http://www.gnome.org/projects/evolution/images/screenshots/2.0/evo-junkmail.png).
