### Post by Bucky Ball on 2012-03-20
Seems TVersity might be of some help but you'd need to use Wine (which can be problematic).

[http://forums.xbox-scene.com/index.php?showtopic=632730](http://forums.xbox-scene.com/index.php?showtopic=632730)

[http://mediatomb.cc/pages/download#debian_ubuntu](http://mediatomb.cc/pages/download#debian_ubuntu)

> * allows to watch YouTube(tm) videos on your UPnP player device
