### Post by rsgangr on 2013-06-22
After inserting the image look at it carefully. On the corners and in the middle of each margin of the image you will see small green squares. These are the "handles" referred to. To re-size the image, press and hold down Shift and move the cursor over one of the small green squares. As it covers the square you will see that a double-headed arrow appear. Click and hold the Left mouse button and move the cursor to change the size of the image. As it changes, you will see that the aspect ratio remains constant, i.e. as the sides change in length, the top and bottom margins change in proportion.
