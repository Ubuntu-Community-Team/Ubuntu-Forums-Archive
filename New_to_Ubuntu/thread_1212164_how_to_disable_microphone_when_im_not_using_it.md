### Post by soumo on 2009-07-18
Sounds obvious, but here's something you may have missed.

Unless enabled in the preferences/options of your volume control manager, there may be a 'front mic' in addition to the regular mic.  Check that option so it shows up in the applet.

Maybe posting your System>Preferences>sound (preferences) to see what you are putting your audio through might help others.

I have no other suggestions.
