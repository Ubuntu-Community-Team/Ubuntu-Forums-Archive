### Post by cascade9 on 2011-01-31
If you've got a nice video card, KDE (kubuntu) should work pretty well. Its not happy with old or very slow video cards though. 

I know that I'll get told of for this......but I'd avoid standard (gnome) ubuntu now. Gnome 2.xx, which all versions of ubuntu use, it just about dead. The next version will have a choice between unity and 'classic' gnome (gnome 2.xx) but its only a question of time untill that option is gone. 

Not much point getting used to a desktop that is already dying, and soon will be dead. 

Depending on what hardware you have, kubuntu, xubuntu or lubuntu are all options.
