### Post by GrouchyGaijin on 2015-05-01
I too use DejaDup - I back up my home to an external drive and then in the case I need to reinstall Ubuntu I just reinstall any programs and restore home from the DejaDup backup - all of my settings and everything are restored.  I've had to do this a couple of times and it made life a **lot** easier.
