### Post by CrystalMoth on 2008-12-05
I just got Ubuntu working via Unetbootin and whenever I open up a window the taskbar is covered by the application window and the title bar for the app does not show. I cannot move the app window. Please help.

---

### Post by ohzopants on 2008-12-05
If you hold down the Alt key you can click and drag anywhere in a window to move it around.

Move it so that you can see the edges of the window and then resize it so that it fits.

---

### Post by Locke_99GS on 2008-12-05
I assume you have compiz running? If so, goto ccsm and ensure that the "Place Windows" plug-in is enabled.

In the meantime, holding down alt and clicking anywhere on an application window will allow you to move it with the mouse.
