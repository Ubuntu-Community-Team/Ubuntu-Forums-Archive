### Post by fantab on 2013-03-04
Yes, they appear to be Grey. However if too much light is flashed on the item during photo shoot colors do appear different.

You can use [**this**]("http://www.lagom.nl/lcd-test/") to test your monitor if its LCD. I am sure you can find similar utilities for LED or CRT.
