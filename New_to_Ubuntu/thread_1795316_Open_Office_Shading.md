### Post by amanchesterman on 2011-07-02
Hi

I just tried it out on my machine, using OpenOffice 3.2.1 and Ubuntu 10.10.

The text selection function shades the text, as you say, but on my system the text is still perfectly visible. That's because the highlighting colour is a pale lilac -- not to everyone's taste maybe, but I like it. The text shows through the shading with no problem.

Since the shading is so dark on your machine I suspect that you have chosen a different colour scheme to me. The one I picked is called 'clearlooks' -- I chose it because it is fairly transparent.

You can change it by using the System > Preferences > Appearance menu. There are many options to choose from, but they affect all the applications on your system, so you may want to try out several.

Good wishes

John
