### Post by doas777 on 2010-03-02
do you mean you want to be able to click in the whitespace on a nautilus window with vertical scroll bars, and then use the mouse wheel to scroll up and down, it should do that by defaut. to test, go to Places -> Home folder, and hit Ctrl+h to show hidden files. click in whitespace between files, and try your scrollwheel. 
if you don't click in the file window it won't work, but that is to be expected. you have to be in a control that supports scrolling before scrolling will work, and the nautilus window itself doesn't (but the pane that displays files does).

my guess is that there is no way to disable auto-refresh, as they have given us no mechanism for manual refresh, except to go somewhere else and come back. I have to do that when i add a file to a share on the server, but the client already had the share window open.
