### Post by MatsSoderhall on 2012-06-14
I found out what was the issue...

When i was running 10.04 i had selected some "Theme" other than the default one. The same theme does not exist in 12.04. The result seems to be that no theme at all is selected which seems to lead to many strange issues such as the "Gear"-icon missing.

To solve the issue i right-clicked the background, and selected "Change Desktop Background". In the bottom of the Appearance window you can select Theme. In my case, no theme at all was selected.

After selecting a theme the gear turned up in the upper right corner.

This seems like a bug to me...
