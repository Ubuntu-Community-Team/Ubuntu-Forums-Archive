### Post by llamabr on 2009-07-25
Hi.  wine is very rarely the best solution.  Instead, there's often a native application that will run better.

To exit a man page, just press q.

to change your password, from the terminal, run:

```
passwd
```

### Post by ibutho on 2009-07-25
You can exit from man pages by pressing "q" on your keyboard. The same applies when you use less, more or most to read text files. You can change your password by running the command "passwd" in a terminal.
