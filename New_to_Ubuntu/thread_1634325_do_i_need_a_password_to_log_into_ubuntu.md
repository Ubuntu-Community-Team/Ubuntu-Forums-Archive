### Post by migs73 on 2010-11-30
No you don't.

If you go to System>Administration>Users and Groups, select your account, and there where it says 
Password: Asked on Login  
Click on Change and check the box for 'don't ask for password on login'. (It's near the bottom).

Restart and you should go straight to your desktop, no password required.
