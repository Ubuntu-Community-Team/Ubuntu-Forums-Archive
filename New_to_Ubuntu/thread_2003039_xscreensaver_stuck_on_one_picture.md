### Post by mystmaiden on 2012-06-14
To fix this problem I went to /home and checked show hidden files and looked for the xscreensaver cache and just manually removed the names of the folders I had removed, leaving just the names of the images themselves. Rebooted and started screensaver again and that portion of my screensaver issue was solved. I'm sure there is a better or easier way but this way does work just in case anyone else runs into the same thing.
