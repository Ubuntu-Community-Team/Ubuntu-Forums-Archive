### Post by seagullplayer77 on 2008-07-16
I use Pidgin, and although I only use it for AIM (only IM account that I have), it works for just about everything else (the package description in Synaptic says it'll do the following: AIM/ICQ, Yahoo!, MSN, IRC, Jabber, Napster, Zephyr, Gadu-Gadu, Bonjour, Groupwise, Sametime, SILC, and SIMPLE). There's a bunch of other lesser-known clients out there, but Kopete is the only one that comes to mind at the moment. I know that there's also an official version of AIM for Linux, although I wasn't ever able to get it working.
