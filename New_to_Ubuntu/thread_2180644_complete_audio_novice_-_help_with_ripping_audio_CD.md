### Post by squakie on 2013-10-14
I found something called Sound Juicer in the repositories so I tried it.  I don't really know what I should be expecting, but it ran and then I added the folder containing a couple of albums I ran through Sound Juicer to XBMC and it updated it's library.  I don't know what all I'm supposed to see, but they are organized - just no album descriptions or pictures - maybe there's not supposed to be.

At any rate, since this appears to have worked so that at least the titles and things like sorted albums are in XBMC, I guess it's all I needed.  Time will tell I guess.

EDIT:  Yep - this is working just great!  Even XBMC adds them to its music library and shows pictures, etc.
