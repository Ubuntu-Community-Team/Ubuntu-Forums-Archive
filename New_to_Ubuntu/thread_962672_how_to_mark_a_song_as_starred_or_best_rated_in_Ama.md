### Post by snova on 2008-10-29
Yes. First check that you have the appropriate columns enabled. There are actually two forms of rating: the star rating that you pick and an automatic one based on the frequency that you listen to it.

As for playing all the high-rated ones, there's probably a way ("dynamic" or "smart" playlists), but I don't know what it is. At any rate you could just add your entire library to the list, sort by rating, and remove all the lower rated ones...
