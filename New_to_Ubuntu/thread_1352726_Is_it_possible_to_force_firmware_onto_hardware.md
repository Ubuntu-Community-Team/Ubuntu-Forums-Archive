### Post by jacobs444 on 2009-12-12
The answer would invariably be No. You would not want to force firmware on a device, because if it isn't taking the firmware then there may be a problem, or you could have the wrong firmware for the wrong hardware, in which case it may brick (make useless) your dvd burner.
