### Post by Paddy Landau on 2010-07-11
Unless Verizon has special custom drivers, it should work out the box.

There is an easy way to test.

Download the Live CD ([check the MD5 sum]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM") after downloading), burn to CD, and boot from the CD. Can you connect to the Internet?

If so, you know it will work. If not, post back to the forum.
