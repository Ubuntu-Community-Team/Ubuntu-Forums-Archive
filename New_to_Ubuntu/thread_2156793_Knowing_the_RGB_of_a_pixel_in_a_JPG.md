### Post by ktat on 2013-06-23
ok, so I'm going to assume that when you "double click in tools" you mean that you are double clicking on either the foreground or background colors.  Doing ths does open up a dialogue box, if you look closely, on the right hand side there are 6 color sliders.  Each slider has a radio button and letter before it.  The letters are H, S, V, R, G, B.
H is for hue
S is for saturation
V is for value
and the remainders.... yep, RGB.  I think this is what you're looking for.
