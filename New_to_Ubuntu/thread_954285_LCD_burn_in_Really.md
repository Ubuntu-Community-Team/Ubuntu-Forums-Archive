### Post by DFlame on 2008-10-21
It's quite possible, though I think the "method" works differently as opposed to regular CRT monitors. My current 4 year old laptop has some burn in issues of this kind. I can clearly see parts of the bottom panel in XFCE when I switch to full screen apps or the Windows install.

This link should provide you with more information: [http://compreviews.about.com/od/monitors/a/LCDBurnIn.htm](http://compreviews.about.com/od/monitors/a/LCDBurnIn.htm)
