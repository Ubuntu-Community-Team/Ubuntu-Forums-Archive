Watever you do don't do anything yet. 11.04 is still in alpha testing and very buggy. Some features just don't work, crashes are frequent and if you're happy with 10.10 stay with it until 11.04 is released or may be a little while after.

When 11.04 is finally released in April, your machine will give you the option to upgrade to it if you want. All you settings and data will carry over to the upgraded system. Alternatively you could back up your data, download the new disk, and install from scratch. Up to you really.
