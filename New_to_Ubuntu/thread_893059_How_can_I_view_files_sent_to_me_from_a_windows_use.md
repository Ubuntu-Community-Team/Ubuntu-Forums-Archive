### Post by nicedude on 2008-08-17
You should add the medibuntu repositories to your software sources ( easy to do just google medibuntu and go there for instructions )

There are lots of cool things that adds, also you can watch stuff without the win32codecs package with VLC player as it has its own codecs installed but it will play everything (including wmv windows media ) but maybe flash and realmedia player files for those you need to get flash and realmedia if you must have that playback ability but I would say no one does as realmedia is junk format. Stick for the most part to trying to get AVI ( which will be  divx or xvid formats ) or mpeg files to watch. AVI is the preferred pirate format and most movies or TV you download will be in this format.


Hope that helps and welcome to Ubuntu
