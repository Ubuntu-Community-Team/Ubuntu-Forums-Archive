### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2011-04-24
I am having WIFI problems, but here goes.  Left click the date time display in your top panel.  Then click edit and add.  Type in Portland OR and select which airport is closest to you and then click OK.  That will put Portland on your top panel.  I hope that was what you are looking for.
