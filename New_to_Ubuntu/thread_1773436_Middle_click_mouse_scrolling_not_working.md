### Post by iclestu on 2011-06-02
ooooo - I know I know....

1. open new tab, type

```
about:config
```

2. scroll down till you find general.autoscroll

3. rightclick on it and select toggle to set it to true

4. close tab, use your scroll wheel as before and bask in your own glory :)
