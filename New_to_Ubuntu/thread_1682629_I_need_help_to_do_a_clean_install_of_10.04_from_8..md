### Post by mcduck on 2011-02-06
Take a portable hard drive, bunch of CD's or DVD's or whatever tool capable of storing stuff you might have available. Then copy your personal files there. Now you have a backup.

(for extra points also copy the hidden files in your home directory. Press Ctrl-h in the file manager to make them visible. They contain all your program settings, Firefox bookmarks & extensions and that kind of stuff)
