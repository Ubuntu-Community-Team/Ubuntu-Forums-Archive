### Post by I2k4 on 2010-12-13
I think Ubuntu's "show me how" instructions if read carefully for USB drive installation

[http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop/get-ubuntu/windows-installer](http://www.ubuntu.com/desktop/get-ubuntu/windows-installer)

using the PenDrive are great and almost foolproof, _**BUT**_ they don't include the necessary step which is to set "Persistence" to the maximum your USB drive will support.  I'm getting good results with a 4GB drive and 3GB of "Persistence".   

I suppose Ubuntu wants people to use the USB drive as a bridge to full hard drive installation, but like me lots of people want to test drive it for a while before that.
