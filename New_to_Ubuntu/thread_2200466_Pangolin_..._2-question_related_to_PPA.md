### Post by Dave_L on 2014-01-19
As far as I know, the existence of PPA's and other repositories in your software sources only affects the installation and upgrade of packages. It has no effect on currently installed software.

So the answers to your questions are "yes" (you can add the PPA) and "no" (deleting the PPA will not cause a problem).
