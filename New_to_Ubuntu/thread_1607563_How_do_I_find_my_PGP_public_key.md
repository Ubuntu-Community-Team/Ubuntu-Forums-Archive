### Post by lombaardcj on 2010-11-08
Ok, here goes:

**Step 1:** Click on arrow next to "**Thread Tools**" at the top of the thread (Always visible to everyone)
**Step 2:** Click on "**Mark this thread as solved**" from drop-down list that appears (**ONLY** visible to creator of thread and admin users)

Once this is done, the thread will have [SOLVED] appearing next to the thread title.

Makes sense that only creator and forum admin can change it to SOLVED status.
