Hey Buddy! Welcome to the forums!

Not sure why the wireless fix worked.. will need someone with more know how than me to explain.

for yahoo messenger, there is a free messenger in ubuntu that can connect to it called pidgin. its in the software center.. see if it does the job for you.

To use evernote you will need wine. Fortunately wine is not an emulator.. its called a 'comparability layer'.. and its name is actually a recursive name that stands for "Wine Is Not an Emulator". So there isnt much of a decline in performance, and you will only notice it in the app itself (if even), not the rest of your system.. 

get 'myUnity' from the software center, it should allow more customisation of you launcher.. or in the appearance settings you could enable auto-hide, then you wont need to worry much about its size.. :-)

no idea about the mouse.. perhaps someone else will know..
