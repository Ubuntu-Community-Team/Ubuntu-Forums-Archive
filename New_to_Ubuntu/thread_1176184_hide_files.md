### Post by nhasian on 2009-06-02
yeah if you just want to hide your files to remove them out of view just put a period in front of the filename.  then you can toggle hidden files viewable on/off with Control-H.

If on the other hand, you have files, folders, or drives you need to secure I recommend truecrypt:

[http://www.truecrypt.org/](http://www.truecrypt.org/)

You can hide an entire volume inside of a file.
