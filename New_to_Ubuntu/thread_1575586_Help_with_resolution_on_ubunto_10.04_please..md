### Post by alanwalterthomas on 2010-09-17
What's your GPU? (Graphics Processing Unit)? Is it ATI Radeon? ATI Radeon HD? nVidia? Intel? Via? SiS? Matrox? ...?
If you don't know, go to Applications>Accessories>Terminal & type:

lspci | grep VGA

That should tell you who made your GPU. Post the result here.
Also, how big is your monitor? If it's a 7" netbook, maybe the screen just can't handle better resolution.
