### Post by SlappyPappy on 2008-05-11
Working in Gutsy I was trying to use the 0.6.1 version of Brasero but it couldn't recognize blank disks.

So I removed Brasero, I opened up the backports and installed Brasero 0.7.1 and man, it works great! It recognized the blank disk right off the bat. The disk plays on everything so I'm happy.

Oh well, hope this helps anyone else out there having difficulty in Gutsy with the Brasero that comes with Synaptic. Good luck!  :guitar:
