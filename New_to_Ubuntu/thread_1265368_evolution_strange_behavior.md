### Post by Tamlynmac on 2009-09-13
One thought.

Have you checked "evolution/edit/preferences/mail accounts (select your mail account if more than one)/edit button"

On both the "Receiving Email" and "Sending Email" tabs make sure "Remember password" is checked. If not, check the box (both if necessary). You may need to put in your password (send & receive) one more time, for it to store your password.

Hope this helps.
