### Post by starcannon on 2009-04-16
Does Alt+Tab give a list of open windows? If so perhaps some how your bottom panel got mugged up a bit, and it could be something as easy as:

1. R+click on the bottom panel
2. Click on "Add to Panel"
3. Select "Window List"
4. Click on "+ Add"
5. Click on "Close"
