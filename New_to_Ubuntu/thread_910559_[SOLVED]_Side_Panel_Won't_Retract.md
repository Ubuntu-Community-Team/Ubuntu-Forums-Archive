### Post by knattlhuber on 2008-09-04
There's three programs that create a MacOSX-like dock that positioned as a sidebar. They're called

[LIST]
[*][Kiba]("http://www.kiba-dock.org/")
[*][Cairo-Dock]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/CairoDock")
[*][Avant-Window-Navigator (AWN)]("http://wiki.awn-project.org/index.php?title=Main_Page")
[/LIST]
