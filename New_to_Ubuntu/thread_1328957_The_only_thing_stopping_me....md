For the moment, just dual boot. Find out how Ubuntu runs on your hardware... see what things you can do in Linux. If anything goes wrong, you will have your Windows partition to fall back on.

According to WineHQ, the latest version of WoW, 3.2 runs Gold or Platinum in Wine, for some 64-bit users so it's worth having a go to see if you can get it working.
