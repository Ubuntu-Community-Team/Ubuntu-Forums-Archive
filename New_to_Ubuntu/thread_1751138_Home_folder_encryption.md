### Post by Fraoch on 2011-05-06
> **fractalman said:**
> i'm just wondering if this is going to cause any security weaknesses

I believe the intention with encrypting your /home folder is to prevent access to your data from anyone who has *physical* access to your machine i.e. a family member/roommate/ninja tries to snoop around your computer when you aren't around, or your computer is stolen.

Even if you throw out your hard drive after you upgrade it, the information will be inaccessible.  Actually if your hard drive doesn't work and you dispose of it, the data is still there if someone has the resources to examine it, but if it's encrypted, it's unavailable.

It does not prevent Internet-based security exploits, but Linux generally has this covered.
