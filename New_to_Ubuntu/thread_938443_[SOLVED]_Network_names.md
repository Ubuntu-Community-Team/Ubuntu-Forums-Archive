### Post by RealPSL on 2008-10-04
eth0 - first ethernet adapter
lo - loopback network adapter

The others are most likely wireless devices. In most cases these are set to dynamically obtain IP addresses so you should not have to do too much for them work. Are you having a problem connecting to your physical or wireless network?
