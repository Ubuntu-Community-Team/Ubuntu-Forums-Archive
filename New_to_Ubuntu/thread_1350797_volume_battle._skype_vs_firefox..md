### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2009-12-09
This worked for me:

Open synaptic and find and install the following:

alsa-firmware
alsa-firmware-loaders
alsa-mixer-gui

Then, open up your sound preferences and set everything to alsa.

You may have to restart, I can't remember.  At the very least, you'll need to restart firefox and skype.
