### Post by migs73 on 2010-09-30
I'm not at my Ubuntu machine just now, but on Firefox at this windows machine, if you select Tools->Options and click on the Applications Icon you can choose what App to run when certain things are opened. the E-mail one is called MailTo and (on this machine) starts outlook.
