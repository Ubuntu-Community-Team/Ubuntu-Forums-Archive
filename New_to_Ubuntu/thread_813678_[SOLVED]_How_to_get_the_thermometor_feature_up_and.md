### Post by cariboo on 2008-05-31
Right click on the clock and choose preferences. Under Panel Display select Show Weather and Show Temperature. Then click the locations tab and add your location. Next click the Weather Tab and select your Temperature unit and Wind Speed unit, click close and your done.
