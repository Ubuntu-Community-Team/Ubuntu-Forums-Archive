I was wondering why my wireless does not connect each time I start up my computer? It connects fine when I select it from the list but it wont do it automatically.  Any ideas?

---

### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2011-03-13
Right click the wireless indicator and choose Edit Connections.  Choose the Wireless heading and then high light the network and select edit.  Check the connect automatically box.  Apply the setting and back out.  That should connect you automatically to your network.  Enjoy.
