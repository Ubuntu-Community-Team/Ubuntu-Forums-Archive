### Post by SteveDee on 2009-01-24
> **pramitghsh said:**
> 

And, is there any way to **run Ubuntu at a resolution of 1280 * 1024** ?? In the System > Preferences > Screen Resolution dialog box the max. resolution option is 1152 * 864. I have the required hardware to support the resolution.

The resolutions presented under the "Monitor Screen Resolutions" dialog you are referencing are those that your system believes your monitor supports.

Assuming your monitor can do 1280 x 1024, try the "Detect Displays" button.

Not sure about your first question, but I would use the live CD to make a USB startup disk on your USB stick (assuming its big enough). Then you can run 'buntu from your memory stick and save stuff.
