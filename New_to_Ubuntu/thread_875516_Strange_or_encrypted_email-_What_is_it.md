### Post by dmizer on 2008-07-30
Looks to me like you don't have the proper character encoding to view whatever text that is.  Something like Japanese or Chinese often looks like this if you don't have the correct font or language support.

I wouldn't worry about it.  Most likely just spam.

If you're really curious, the character encoding will probably be listed in the email header if you have it.
