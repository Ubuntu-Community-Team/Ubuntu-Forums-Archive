### Post by cwsnyder on 2009-02-16
There are several programs for what you specify.  What you are looking for is a calendar program, such as is included in Evolution Calendar portion, or the Sunbird stand alone calendar program.

If they are not on your Applications menu, you can add them with either Add/Remove Programs from the Applications menu, or Synaptic Package Manager from the System >> Administration menu.
