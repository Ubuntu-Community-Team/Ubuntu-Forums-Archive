### Post by Peter Maunder on 2011-11-16
> **merlin666 said:**
> I am specifically interested in using a slideshow of vacation pictures as screensaver - is this possible with xscreensaver?

Yes it is possible with 11.10. You need xscreensaver and xscreensaver-gl (the latter contains a program glslideshow an add-in for xscreensaver) Select glslideshow from the xscreensaver setup screen, choose your folder and away you go. 

If you use the Ubuntu Software Centre, you will not find the above under "screensaver", at least on my system, you must look first for xscreensaver and then for xscreensaver-gl.

You will need to add xscreensaver to your startup programs.
