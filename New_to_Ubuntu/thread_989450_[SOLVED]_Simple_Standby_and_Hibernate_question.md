### Post by kerry_s on 2008-11-21
standby: uses power, but offers faster resume
hibernate: is a complete shutdown, you can do anything as if you had shut it down the normal way, offers faster startup than a complete boot.

---

### Post by marshall.robert on 2008-11-21
both standby and hibernate result in the hard drive being turned off, so yes, it would park the head and be safe to move around.
