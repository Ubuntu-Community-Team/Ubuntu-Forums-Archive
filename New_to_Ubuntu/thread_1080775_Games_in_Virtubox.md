### Post by yther on 2009-02-25
I have had a little bit of personal experience with running games in VMWare (not VirtualBox, as I require DirectX support) now, and I can say that my sound and USB work fine, but there are sometimes performance issues.  If you don't mind experimenting, tweaking settings, trying again until you get it, you should be OK.  Mainly it's down to memory and processor power; if you have lots of both, you should have a good experience.

Edit:  I see I cross-posted with you.  Spore has been reported to work well for some people using WINE.  You can check [their database](http://appdb.winehq.org/index.php) for other games you may be interested in.  Your decision about hardware is a good one, though; research before you buy and you should have a great machine for Linux!  :)
