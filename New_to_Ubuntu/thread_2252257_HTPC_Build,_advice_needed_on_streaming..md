### Post by papibe on 2014-11-10
Hi Alexander_Dixon. Welcome to the forum ):P

There are very inexpensive solutions for streaming content from the Internet. No HTPC can't compete with that, regardless of the OS.

You have Chromecast, Roku, Fire TV stick, and even the slightly more expensive Fire TV, or Nexus player.

On the other hand, if you have a significant amount of local content, then an HTPC is the way to go.

Personally, I have both. I have an HTPC for my local content, and a Chromecast for anything else.

Just my thoughts.
