.....I cant seem to change the launcher from opening when you hit the left side to when you hit the top left corner, which I must prefer, I typer about:config but I cannot open the unity icon, is there anyway of opening it with the terminal?

run ccsm (type that into Dash Home or use a terminal)
In the Desktop section, click on Ubuntu Unity Plugin
Under the Behaviour tab, Revel Mode, choose TopLeft

......note that the top left activation of the Launcher bar is slightly different to earlier behaviour. Put your mouse pointer top left and stroke the left margin for a short distance.
