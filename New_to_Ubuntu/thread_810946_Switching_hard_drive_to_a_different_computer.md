### Post by phread59 on 2008-05-28
I'm not a huge expert but I believe you have a hardware incompatability issue (if that's the right phrase).  Basicly when you install an OP system in a machine on a hard drive the basic system's hardware is configured and stored in the drive.  What I'm trying to say is the operating system on the hard drive still thinks it's in the old machine and working accordingly.  it does not know it has a new motherboard and processor,different CD/DVD drives ect.  I am not sure how to change the hardware settings to the current machine.  check in Hardware Testing in the system/Admin file.  You may get what you need from a hardware check.  If not I do not know any other ways other than saving your important data and reinstalling fresh.  That would set up your new hardware and store it on the drive and you should be good to go.
