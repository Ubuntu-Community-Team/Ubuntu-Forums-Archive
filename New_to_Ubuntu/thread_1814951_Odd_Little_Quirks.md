### Post by Paqman on 2011-07-30
Odd little quirks are par for the course with an Alpha, you should expect things to change and/or break on a daily basis. I would highly not recommend running Oneiric as your everyday system, especially if you're new to Linux.

To report bugs, hit Alt-F2 and type:
```

ubuntu-bug packagename

```
You'll need a Launchpad account to file the bug.
