Hi palmgrower.

Note that TV's don't usually display the signal as a regular monitor would do.  Be aware of [overscan]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Overscan") on HDMI, and extra settings on the VGA input related to shifting horizontal and vertical postitions, and scaling.

Before yo gave up, try playing with your TV remote to see if you can find those advance settings.

Just a thought.
