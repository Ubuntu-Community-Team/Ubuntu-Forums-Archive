### Post by Icebear on 2008-12-18
I had a look at the Portuguese layout and I think the default layout has a ? as a third key on the the W key.

So goto System>Preferences>Keyboard

then click the **layouts** tab

then click **Other Option** 

then click **Third lever choosers**

and choose your best option.
Then to get the ? press the key you have chosen and the **w** 

to choose a different varient of the Portuguese keyboard  in the lay out tab hit the **+** then choose Portuguese and look at the varients which would best suit you.
