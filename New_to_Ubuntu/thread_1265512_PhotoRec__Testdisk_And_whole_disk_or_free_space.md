### Post by bumanie on 2009-09-14
For what you want to do, photorec is probably the best bet. Testdisk is more useful for recovering partitons, partition tables etc. As far as I know, photorec copies the files it finds and leaves the underlying filesystem as it is. Use the whole disk option, to answer your second question.
