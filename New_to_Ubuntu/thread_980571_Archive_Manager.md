### Post by bscbrit on 2008-11-13
I'll have to go and look this one up - it is an unusual question!  My first guess would be that it is unlikely that there is a command - you have an incomplete archive.  But I'll go and do some digging to see what I can find.

However, I have helped users who have made an archive and then forgotten the password. I have had success with right clicking on the directory while it is being unpacked (but **before** you are asked for a password) and selecting 'copy'.  With a few minutes practice I managed to obtain a copy of 99% of the archive contents.  It depends, of course, on the size of the archive and how quick (or slow!) your computer is.  Slower is better in this instance.
