### Post by NightwishFan on 2010-02-02
[LIST=1]
[*]First off, please check that the wifi is indeed enabled. If you are on a laptop there might be a switch.
[*]If the wifi is enabled, do you see a network icon near the clock? Does your Wifi network appear there?
[*]If not, then you likely may need a restricted driver. In the menu, search "hardware" and open the Hardware Drivers program. Enable any restricted drivers relevant to wireless there, then log out and back in.
[/LIST]

If none of the above applies, I will try to help you out then.
