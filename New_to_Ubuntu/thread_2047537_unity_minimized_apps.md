### Post by evilsoup on 2012-08-25
It goes to the launcher, on the left side of the screen. For example, If I minimised a Firefox window, I would then have to click on the firefox icon to get it up again.

If you have multiple windows of a program open at the same time, you may have to click the launcher icon twice, which will bring up a exposé-style 'spread' mode for that program.

### Post by evilsoup on 2012-08-25
When you minimise a window it visibly 'shrinks', using a fancy compiz effect, into the icon on your launcher. Also, there are little 'pips' next to the icon, one for every window, including the minimised ones.
