### Post by meho_r on 2009-10-22
And if you want to go extreme, try [gmusicbrowser.](http://squentin.free.fr/gmusicbrowser/gmusicbrowser.html) After setting layout of your preference (I like "Playlist, Library & Context" layout) and a little bit of experimenting and getting used to it, I doubt you'll ever search for another music player ;)

And of course you can use multiple genre tags. You can organize your files by labels too (beside tags and playlists).

P.S. Since many users run away at first look, you should know that icons, stars and layouts CAN be changed :D
