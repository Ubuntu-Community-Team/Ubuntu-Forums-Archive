How long did you wait for this black screen. depending on the hardware, it can take up to 30 minutes on a slow disk drive to load the installer into memory. Faster disk drives take less time, and USB sticks are very zippy. Many laptops support booting from USB stick - consult your user manual or SONY support for more information.
