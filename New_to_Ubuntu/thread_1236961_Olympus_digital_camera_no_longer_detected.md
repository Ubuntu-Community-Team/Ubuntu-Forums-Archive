### Post by colin.p on 2009-08-20
I have several (older) digital cameras, two of which are Olympus. One, the oldest, works fine when I plug it in but funnily enough the newer one doesn't. However, I gave up on plugging in any of them while in win or linux and just take the memory card out and use a USB card reader. That way any photo program should see them.
