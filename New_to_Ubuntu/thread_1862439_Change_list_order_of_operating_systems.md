### Post by drs305 on 2011-10-16
If you are using Grub 2 (which is used by the currently-supported versions of the Ubuntu Family) I recommend installing Grub Customizer. It was designed for Grub 2 and can do a variety of tasks such as reordering, renaming, hiding, etc.

Click on the "Customizer" link in my signature line to take you to a thread about it.

The "Tweaks" thread can show you how to tinker with the system files manually. It was necessary when Grub 2 first came out but I consider it a pretty barbaric way to do it nowadays (and I'm the one that wrote the guide).  ;-)
