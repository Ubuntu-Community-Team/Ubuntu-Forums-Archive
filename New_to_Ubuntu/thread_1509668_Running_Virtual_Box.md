### Post by QIII on 2010-06-14
You will need to assign at least one processor, which means you will need at least a dual-core.  Better if you have a quad and can assign two.

1GB at least.  2GB is better.  You can probably get away with less RAM than that, but you might not be happy with the performance.

VirtualBox will not let you assign more than 80% (I believe) of your RAM to the virtual machine.

Other hardware issues will likely not be a problem, since the vm will use its own virtual hardware.
