### Post by snowpine on 2010-01-25
Ubuntu Netbook Remix is known to work on the 4gb Asus eee (except for some apps being too large for the tiny 7" screen): 

[https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/Machines/Netbooks#Asus%20Eee%20701-SD%20/%20702](https://wiki.ubuntu.com/HardwareSupport/Machines/Netbooks#Asus%20Eee%20701-SD%20/%20702)

You will certainly want an SD card for additional storage.

### Post by Temposs on 2010-01-25
+1 to Ubuntu Netbook Remix. It is great for netbooks.

For the windows that are too large for the screen, what you do is you hold down Alt and Click the window and drag it to show the portion of the window you need to see. Most programs will work just fine, though.
