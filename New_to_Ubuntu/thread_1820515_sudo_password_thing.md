### Post by Rex Bouwense on 2011-08-07
The password that you are asked to provide for sudo is your log in password.  You will not see anything on your screen.  Just type the whole password and hit enter.  Do remember that Ubuntu is case sensitive so make sure that your Caps Lock is off.
