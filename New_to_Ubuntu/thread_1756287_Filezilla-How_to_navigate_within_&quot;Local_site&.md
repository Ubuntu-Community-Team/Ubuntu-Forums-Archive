### Post by frankbooth on 2011-05-12
Kind of depends on how you've configured Filezilla, but I assume it looks something like this?:

[img]http://www.liberiangeek.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/filezilla_ubuntu_lucid_3_thumb.png[/img]

You can browse to where your drive is mounted in the left pane, it should be mounted at (by default):

```
/media/NAMEOFDRIVE/
```
