### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2008-11-25
It depends, obviously, on what services you want.  The items listed by binbash are not crucial, but it's good to know what your turning off just in case it's something you actually use.

I like having the Update Notifier on there so that updates come through.  Tracker is good if you use the "Search for Files" feature a lot (it still works without tracker, but it's slower).

In most cases, a quick google search will tell you what the item is, and you can decide if it's something you use or not.
