Hi and as I understand it just because you have a linksys router you do not have a linksys wireless card in your system. 
For a compatible wireless card you may look here 
[http://hardware4linux.info/search/](http://hardware4linux.info/search/)
and here
[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/WifiDocs/WirelessCardsSupported)
