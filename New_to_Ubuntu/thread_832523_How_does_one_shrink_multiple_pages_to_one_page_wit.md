### Post by konungursvia on 2008-06-17
I guess you're thinking of a PowerPoint feature, in which nine or twelve pages print on one sheet... Mostly shrinking regular document pages to a page would result in very small type, hard to read. But if you want to do it, I suggest this: create a new document, create a table in it with the number of desired "pages" on it, select the pages you want to insert one by one, shrink the font to very small, such as 5 or 6 point, and paste the pages in the cells of the table.
