### Post by SpenceMakesSense on 2009-03-10
You can do ```
sudo apt-get install kubuntu-desktop
```
Then at the login screen you can go to sessions and change it to KDE.

This will though give you all the applications that comes with kubuntu. And theyll be listed with your gnome applications while in gnome.
