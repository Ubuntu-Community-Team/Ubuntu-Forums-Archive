### Post by lidex on 2010-01-17
If you're talking about the metal pins on the back of the cd-rom drive, they don't come out. The power supply connector, which is plastic, slides onto them. If you're referring to the power supply connector, that should come off -- might need to press on some tabs on the sides depending on design.
