### Post by nosehat on 2011-07-23
I assume there's a simple solution here, but I can't find it.

When I mount a drive in /media (USB drive, truecrypt volume, whatever) a Nautilus file explorer window opens up on my workspace and steals focus from whatever application I'm using.  How can I turn this behavior off?  Ideally I want just an icon on the desktop to appear, but no other behavior.

Using Ubuntu 10.04 LTS, and Gnome 2.30.2 and Nautilus 2.30.1.

Thanks!

---

### Post by ExileAmongYou on 2011-07-23
Open Nautilus, go to Edit->Preferences, then the Media tab, and untick "Browse media when inserted".
