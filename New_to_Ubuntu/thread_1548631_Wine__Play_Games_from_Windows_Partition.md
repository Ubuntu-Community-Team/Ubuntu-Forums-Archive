### Post by EnigmaticCoder on 2010-08-08
Can you use Wine to play games from your Windows partition without installing them to your "Wine directory?"

---

### Post by cariboo on 2010-08-08
Yes you can, but it isn't a very good idea, linking to your C:\ drive in the wine directory, allows anyone that uses or has access to your computer to have access to your Windows partition.

---

### Post by Eddie Wilson on 2010-08-08
Some games you can. I have found that if your game installs everything in one directory you may be able to. But if certain files have to be installed into different Windows system directories then you won't be able to, also some games uses certain window calls you wouldn't have in your Wine directory. Wine uses an emulation of a Windows install in your Wine directory. There are some games that I've been able to do that on but most of them I couldn't.
