### Post by Cheesemill on 2009-12-31
The speed of your CPU is the speed at which both cores run. In your case you have 2 cores both running at 1.75 GHz (you meant GHz not GB).

All processors can be overclocked, whether or not it's a good idea depends mostly on your cooling system and your knowledge/experience of computers.

Also I seriously doubt Packard Bell underclocked your system, it's probably just got CPU throttling enabled which automatically slows down your CPU when it's not under load.
