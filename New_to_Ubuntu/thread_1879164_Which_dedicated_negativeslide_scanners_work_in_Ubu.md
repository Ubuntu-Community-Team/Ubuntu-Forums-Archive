### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-11-11
A lot of dedicated slide scanners can scan directly to SD card, as they are basically just a digital camera that takes a photograph of the slide.

I'd advise you to buy one of those. There are plenty around, and most computer stores (online and offline) should have them. Even if the device doesn't work with Linux, you can still just scan straight to an SD card and then read the SD card in the computer.
