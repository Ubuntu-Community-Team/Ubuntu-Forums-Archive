### Post by Paqman on 2011-12-17
> **jermza said:**
> How would you tweak Ubuntu to maximise graphical performance?

Depends on what hardware you have. The type of graphics chipset you've got is going to affect what drivers are available to you. For example, if you have an Nvidia card then you have the choice of the open source nouveau driver (the default), or several proprietary drivers from the Additional Drivers tool. Performance will vary between those. With Intel graphics your drivers are built in to the kernel, so you have less choice.
