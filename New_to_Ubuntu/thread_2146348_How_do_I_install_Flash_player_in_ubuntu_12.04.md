### Post by Rob Sayer on 2013-05-18
One of the first things I do in a new install is go into synaptic and install ubuntu-restricted-extras (actually in my case kubuntu-restricted-extras since I vastly prefer kde to unity).  That'll take care of your flash plugin and add lots of other useful things.  For me, especially, LAME.  I will not use any other mp3 encoder.
