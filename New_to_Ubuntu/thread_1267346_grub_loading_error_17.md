### Post by K7522 on 2009-09-15
The last time I had this error the /boot directory was too far "back" on the partition for my MoBo to go read. I'd suggest creating a partition (only need about 100mb if you have multiple kernels) at the beginning of the drive and have it mounted to /boot. This usually happens when you're dual-booting and the /boot drive is on the back end of the drive.
