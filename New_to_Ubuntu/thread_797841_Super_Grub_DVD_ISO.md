### Post by Benbow on 2008-05-17
This is a very useful tool which I haven't seen mentioned lately which is easy to use and can get you out of an "I've lost my grub menu" situation.
It probably doesn't belong here but I guess that most seasoned users have it.
I have used it many times and have just used it to recover my grub menu which I had lost after downloading Foresight Linux (which is ok but still needs a lot of work on it).
Super Grub can be downloaded here:
[http://supergrub.forjamari.linex.org/?section=download](http://supergrub.forjamari.linex.org/?section=download)

or just put "super grub" into Google.

Hope this helps someone.
