### Post by WunderSlug on 2008-07-07
I don't know if anyone's covered this already, but I just got my local sports radio station WEEI 103.7 FM to play in Rythymbox.  I've been trying for a week now, and I'm kinda proud of myself.

It's a stream that only plays once you've logged in.

While I was still using Windows, I tried to do this as well, but to no avail and I forgot about it.  Now that I have switched to Ubuntu, I tried again.  

All you have to do is click on the arrow next to the volume control and select copy.  Then paste it into Rythymbox.  So simple.  

I understand this is probably common knowledge, but maybe someone else in Boston wants to listen to WEEI.
