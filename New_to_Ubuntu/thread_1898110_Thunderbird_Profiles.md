### Post by Lars Noodén on 2011-12-20
The Thunderbird configurations and data go in [font=Courier New].thunderbird[/font]

~ is a shortcut for your home directory.  So, [font=Courier New]~/Downloads[/font] would be the download directory in your home directory.

### Post by shaneo1 on 2011-12-20
You will find your Thunderbird profile in your Home folder.  Just press [ctrl] + [h] to show hidden files and all the .files will appear look for .Thunderbird and you are there.
