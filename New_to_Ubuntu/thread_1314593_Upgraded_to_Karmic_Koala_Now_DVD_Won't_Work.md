### Post by quofan on 2009-11-05
I upgraded to 9.10 from a stable 9.04 at the weekend then changed the boot priority in bios to make HDD first option thinking I'd save a few seconds boot up.  At this point I lost all access to the CD/DVD drive.   Changed boot priority back to DVD drive first, HDD second and the CD/DVD drive was accessible again.

Only a newbie and nowhere near as knowledgeable as some of the chaps on here but this simple fix worked for me!
