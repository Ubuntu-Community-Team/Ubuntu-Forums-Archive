### Post by fooman on 2008-11-27
i don't use limewire,  but i have used frostwire and the files are automatically downloaded to the /home/<username>/shared folder by default.  i believe limewire is the same.

not sure 'bout limewire....but in *frostwire* you can change the default player.  look in the toolbar > tools > options > player 

you will see a selection for "use frostwire media player"...if you *uncheck* that,  frostwire will use whatever player you have set as your default audio player in ubuntu (amarok, vlc, totem, etc..).  

hope that helps.
