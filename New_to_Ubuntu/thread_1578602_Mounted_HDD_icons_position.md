### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-09-20
If you mount every internal drive that is likely to ever be mounted and move the icons to the position you want them, they will normally remain in that position even after unmounting and then remounting.

The problem is with external USB flash thumb drives which always seem to mount on my machine on the far left below the icons I already have on the desktop, which could be the position where you have put another internal drive icon, but do not at that time have the internal drive mounted..
