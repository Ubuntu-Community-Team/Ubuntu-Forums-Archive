### Post by Impavidus on 2013-02-15
In the default interface the menus are hidden in the taskbar at the top of your screen. Move your mouse there, I think on the left side, and the menu bar should appear. Else, you may be able to right-click on whatever bar you've got and then enable the menu bar. I use a different interface (xfce, looks more traditional), so I can't tell you exactly.

Otherwise, you always save your torrent files in your Downsloads directory and then start transmission.
