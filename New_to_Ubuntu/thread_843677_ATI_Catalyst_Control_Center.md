### Post by markbuntu on 2008-06-28
Usually CCC is in Applications/Other which may be turned off in your menus, or CCC may be turned off. You can use System/Preferences/Main Menu to look for it and turn it on in the menus. I have also found it in Accessories though I may have moved it there by accident when I was fooling around with the menus.
