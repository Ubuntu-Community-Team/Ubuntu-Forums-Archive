### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-04-09
Of course, you're welcome to try it, but being new to Linux, you would do better holding off on 10.04 until it's released, and even then, given that new releases are often buggy for a few weeks, even a few weeks more.

You could burn a LiveCD and see how that looks and works, but given the instability of beta versions, I still suggest that you wait until the stable version is released.  You'll encounter a lot less problems that way.
