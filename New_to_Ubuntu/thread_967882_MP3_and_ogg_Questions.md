### Post by logos34 on 2008-11-02
iRiver players too (i.e. Clix, etc)

Rockbox gives ogg support to iPods (not out of the box, but easily added)

ogg is superior to mp3, but you'll only notice the diff if you go below a certain bitrate threshold--like maybe V4 vbr mp3 or -q6 ogg, where the ogg really shines (especially the aotuvb5 codec)

I prefer ogg and to my ears anything at or above q6 setting (~192kbps) is transparent and indistinguishable from lossless
