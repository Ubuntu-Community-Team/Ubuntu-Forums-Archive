### Post by earruda on 2015-07-20
If you are using apache, check the files /var/log/apache/access.log and /var/log/apache2/error.log as soon as you try to access the webpage. They might tell you the error, or at least an idea of whats the problem.

The access.log file stores the requests made to your webserver and the error.log stores the errors generated by the apache or it's mods.
