### Post by cmnorton on 2008-08-07
Network both computers, so you can transfer files.

One area of difficulty you may have in getting an executable to run on a new system is library compatibilities. So, I would not sell your old computer right away, and transfer over what you want to run on the new computer one at a time. 

You could bring over everything and put it in a special directory.

I would not worry about transferring links.
