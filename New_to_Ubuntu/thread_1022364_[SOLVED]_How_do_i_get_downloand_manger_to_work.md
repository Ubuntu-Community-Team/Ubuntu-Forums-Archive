### Post by swoody on 2009-01-02
> **desy83 said:**
> **Could you please tell me what programme tools is in as I can't find it to disable down them all as i have been checking the web and aria has an awful lot of problems with it. As the ubuntu system is new to me. **

I was referring to the 'Tools' at the top of your Firefox browser window. Just open Firefox as you normally do, and it's at the top (near File, Edit, View, etc.). Click on "Add-Ons' and a new smaller window will open. In that new window click on the 'Extensions' tab at the top, and look in the list below for 'Down-Them-All' Click on that, and you will two buttons next to it to either disable or uninstall down-them-all. Pick which one you want, and after a few seconds it will prompt you to restart Firefox. After restarting it will be disabled (or removed if you decided to go with 'Uninstall').  

If you're having more difficulties, please post back, and let us know :)
