### Post by Twitch04 on 2010-05-02
Oh, got it figured out. 

All of the 0 byte files are mirrors. The update manager just checks various mirrors for the file. If it's not found, it slaps "0 B" on it and moves on until it finds one that works. 

^_^

So it's normal. Thanks anyway, haha.
