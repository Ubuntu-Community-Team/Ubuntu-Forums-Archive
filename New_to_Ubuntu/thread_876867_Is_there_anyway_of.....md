### Post by mcduck on 2008-08-01
Right-click on the applet and select "remove from panel" to get rifd of the menu list applet (the one with applications/places & system menus).

Then right-click on the panel, select "Add to Panel", find the Main Menu-applet and drag & drop it into the panel. The Main Menu applet combines all three menus into one, accessed through a single icon in the panel.

### Post by t0p on 2008-08-01
If you right-click on the top panel, and select **Add to Panel**, there is an option called **Main Menu** - this puts the little circular Ubuntu icon onto the panel, then when you click on the icon a drop-down menu gives you the choice of Applications, Places, System and more - so, instead of having all those menus displayed on the panel all the time, you have just the circular icon.  Is this what you had in mind?
