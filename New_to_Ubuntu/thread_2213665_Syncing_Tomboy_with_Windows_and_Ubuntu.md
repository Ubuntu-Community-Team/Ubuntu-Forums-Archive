### Post by gifford on 2014-03-28
I do not use Tomboy anymore and as I remember the syncing issue was always problematic.
However, I am now an avid fan of Google Keep. If you use chrome on both the windows and Ubuntu machines, or if you dual boot, keep will sync all your notes.
Actually, I have keep as an app in the launcher on 12.04, it syncs everything automatically with my Ubuntu 14.04 laptop and my android moto x phone. It has been truly a pleasant experience to find an app that works so well and requires such little attention to work.
I know that this was not the answer to the question you posed, but I wanted to let you know what I have found that works without problems to address the issue you raised.
Here is a link describing how to put keep in the launcher:[http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2013/05/add-google-keep-to-unity](http://www.omgubuntu.co.uk/2013/05/add-google-keep-to-unity)
