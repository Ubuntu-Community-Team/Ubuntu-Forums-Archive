### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-10-28
As far as I know -- NO.  The primary use for sfc /scannow in Windows is to replace system files that get damaged or overwritten by malware.  You don't have anywhere near the exposure in Linux that you did in Windows, so basically, there is no need for this.
