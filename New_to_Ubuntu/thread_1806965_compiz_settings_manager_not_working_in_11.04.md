### Post by MG&amp;TL on 2011-07-18
Natty narwhal uses the Unity desktop, which means that it is only-semi-compatible with compiz effects. It IS possible to use both, but it is difficult.

You have two easy options:

-Put 10.04 back.

-Select 'Ubuntu classic' from the login menu.

Hard option:

Google 'use compiz and unity desktop' or similiar and follow instructions. This may cause problems, break unity, require terminal work, depending on what you want to enable (some effects are easier than others)

Have fun :D
