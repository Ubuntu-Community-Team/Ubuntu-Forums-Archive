### Post by snowpine on 2010-07-13
Not a lot of details to work from :) but I will try to help.

A good first step is the "check CD for errors" option that appears on the first screen when you boot the Live CD.

The next step is to figure out which graphics card you have, so you can begin looking for answers. If you are not sure, use the terminal command 'lspci' (without the quotes) or check your laptop manual.
