### Post by poodoopealeoaph on 2011-04-11
You may have issues with your video card drivers. go to applications>system>administration>additional drivers and see if there are any video card drivers that need to be installed. Also, for your battery... did you install ubuntu, kubuntu, or xubuntu? For some computers, especially ones with older batteries, you should probably shoot for xubuntu because it uses much less resources and would probably extend your battery life.
