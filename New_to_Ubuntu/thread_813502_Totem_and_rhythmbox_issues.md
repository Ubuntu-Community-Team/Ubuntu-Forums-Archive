### Post by Exsecrabilus on 2008-05-30
For the first question:

Do you mean you're trying to use the **Totem YouTube plugin**?

If so, I can help. If not, then further explain your problem please.

If you *are* talking about the Totem YouTube plugin, just go **Edit** >> **Plugins** and check *YouTube browser*.

On the sidebar, and where it says *Playlist*/*Properties*, click it.
From the drop-down, click *YouTube*, and now you'll be able to search and watch YouTube videos.
