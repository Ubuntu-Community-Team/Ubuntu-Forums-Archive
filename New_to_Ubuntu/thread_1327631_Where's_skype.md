### Post by Flimm on 2009-11-15
The packages you've installed (python-skype and skysentials) are not Skype, they're extensions to Skype. To get Skype itself, go to [Skype's website](http://www.skype.com/intl/en-gb/download/skype/linux/choose/) and download the .deb file for Ubuntu 8.10+. Or you can use the medibuntu repositories if you're familiar with third party repositories and the legal issues surrounding medibuntu.
