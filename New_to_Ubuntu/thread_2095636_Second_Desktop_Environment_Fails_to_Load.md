### Post by audiomick on 2012-12-17
> **JHawk56 said:**
> 
So, uh, where is it? When I log out and come back in, there is no indication that Lubuntu is there at all.

On the log on page, after you select your user name to log on, look for an icon in the bar at the bottom on the right. I can't remember exactly what it looks like, but you should find one that open a menu where you can choose the desktop environment.

No, wait on, that is on 10.10. On the newer ones, I think it is the cog wheel icon to the right of the box for your password.

Either way, you have to click on an icon somewhere on the log on page to get the list to choose from. The system will boot at each new start into whichever DE you actively chose last.
