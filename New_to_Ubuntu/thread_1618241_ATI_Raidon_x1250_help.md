### Post by Mark Phelps on 2010-11-10
I can't specifically solve your resolution problem, but you need to know that there are no new drivers you can install that will work with your card and current versions of Ubuntu.  During initial setup, Ubuntu queries the hardware, and in your case, installed the default open-source drivers -- which are the only ones that will work.

Just mentioning this in case folks come along telling you to look for hardware drivers (you won't find any) or to download and install drivers from the AMD/ATI website -- bad idea, will just trash your display even more.
