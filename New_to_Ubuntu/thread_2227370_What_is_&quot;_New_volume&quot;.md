### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2014-06-01
"New volume" is a new volume, ie, an existent drive/partition that don't belongs to the Linux file system. It probably is your Windows drive if you have a dual-boot configuration.

---

### Post by Impavidus on 2014-06-01
The "New volume" is a partition on your hard drive (or a removable drive, if you have one plugged in). "New volume" is its label. It must have been the default label when the partition was created.
