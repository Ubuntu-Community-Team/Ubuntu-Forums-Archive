Hey all, I just wanted to share my fix for Firefox & Flash on Ubuntu 8.04 in the 64 bit architecture.  Flash has been unreliable sometimes working, others giving only grey boxes - last night I found GNASH [http://www.gnashdev.org/]("http://www.gnashdev.org/")

I downloaded the xpi file, chose firefox under /usr/bin as the opening program, restarted Firefox and SHAZAM - I have YouTube, Google Analytics and more!  

I just wanted to share my find, not sure if anyone else is having the same problem - but this worked for me....   :)
