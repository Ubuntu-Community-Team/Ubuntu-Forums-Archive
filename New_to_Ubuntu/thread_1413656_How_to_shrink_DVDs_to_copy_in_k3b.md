### Post by ron999 on 2010-02-22
Hi
Your dad's correct.
You need a program to shrink those DVDs down to fit on single-layer discs before you use K3b.
Try **K9Copy**, it's in Ubuntu's repository.

It shrinks them in two ways:-
1. It slightly reduces the quality.
2. You have the option to remove unwanted stuff such as interviews, adverts, out-takes etc. Also you can remove foreign language soundtracks and subtitles.

There's more information here:- [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K9Copy]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/K9Copy")

And here:-[http://k9copy.sourceforge.net/]("http://k9copy.sourceforge.net/")
