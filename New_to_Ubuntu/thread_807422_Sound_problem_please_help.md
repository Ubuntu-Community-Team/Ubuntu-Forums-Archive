### Post by ghantoos on 2008-05-25
The sound outputs are dealt with independently, so it is normal that when you plug in your headphone, the speakers are not switched off.

Double click on the speaker on the top left of your desktop, the go to Edit-> Preferences, and click on all devices available.

There is a chance that your headphone output is muted.

You can also check it out via a terminal ```
alsamixer
```Cheers,
