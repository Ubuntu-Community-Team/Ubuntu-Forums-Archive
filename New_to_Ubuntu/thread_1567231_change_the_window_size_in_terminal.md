### Post by nothingspecial on 2010-09-03
Well, in that situation (assuming you are using Gnome) there are 2 things you can do.

Either hold down Alt, then  left click on the window and move it (keeping the left click held down).

Or

You can press Alt-F8 and either resize it with the arrow keys or your mouse.
