### Post by coffeecat on 2009-07-12
A newly installed theme will be called whatever it's called. If you're getting a theme called 'custom', it means you've made some changes yourself using the 'customise' button - window border, icons, whatever.

Once you're happy with your customisation, simply click on the 'Save As' button and assign it a name of your choice.
