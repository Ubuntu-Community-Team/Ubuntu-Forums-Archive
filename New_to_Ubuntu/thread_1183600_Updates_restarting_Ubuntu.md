### Post by egalvan on 2009-06-10
OK folks, correct me if I'm wrong,

but i think the OP was referring to **AUTOMATIC RE-BOOTS**

and not **REQUIRED RE-BOOTS.**

MS Windows will happily and merrily re-boot your computer after up-dating...with your permission, or without.
(and if you ignore the warnings, it will keep popping them up every ten minutes until you give in)
"Resistance is Futile"

I have never seen this behavior in a *nix OS.

As far as I know, there is no **AUTOMATIC RE-BOOT** in Ubuntu, or other Linux.
Just a notification that a re-boot is needed to complete the up-date process.

And as others have stated, **REQUIRED RE-BOOTS** are fairly rare in Linux.
