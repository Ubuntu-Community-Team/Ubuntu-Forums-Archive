### Post by NickJones on 2009-11-02
In 9.10:
Applications, Ubuntu Software Centre, Type "DS" into the search box, click on DeSmuME, then the arrow to the right, then install. Bingo its installed, then go Applications, Games, DeSmuME.
Hope this helped.
