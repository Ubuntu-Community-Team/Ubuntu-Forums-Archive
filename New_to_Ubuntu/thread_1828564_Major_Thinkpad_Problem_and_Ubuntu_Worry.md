### Post by coffeecat on 2011-08-19
A loud clicking noise could possibly be a failing hard drive. The freeze and what followed could be explained by that. If you want to investigate, boot a live Ubuntu CD or live USB. Choose "try Ubuntu" to get to the desktop and open Disk Utility. See what it says about SMART data on the hard drive.

As far as having a dual-boot, this would not be the cause of a hardware fault. However, if your supplier wanted to be difficult and cite the presence of Ubuntu as a reason for invalidating any warranty, they might try to do so. They would be wrong to do so, imo. I don't know what consumer law has to say about such things in India (are you in England or India at the moment?) but in the UK a well-known computer retailer did try this one on, but had to backtrack in the end, if I remember correctly.
