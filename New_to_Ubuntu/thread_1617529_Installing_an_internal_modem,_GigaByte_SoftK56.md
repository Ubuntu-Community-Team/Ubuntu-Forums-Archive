### Post by oldsoundguy on 2010-11-09
Most internal modems are WinModems and will not work in ANY form of Linux.  Best to convert to an external if you are stuck on dial up and USRobotics has the best track record for compatibility.  NOT that expensive as you can pick one up on eBay.(I used a Sportster for years on Windows and early Linux experiments.

### Post by lkraemer on 2010-11-10
You can try [www.linmodems.org](www.linmodems.org) for help/support of the winmodem.  Marvin
and the folks there will assist, but the best thing to do is buy an external Serial
Modem from ebay.com and go from there.  You will savedays of work, and tons of
emails in your attempt.  It's just not worth the time......when you can be up and
running in 15-20 minutes.
