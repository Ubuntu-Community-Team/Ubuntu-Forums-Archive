### Post by rouge568 on 2008-04-25
A note for everyone who is upgrading from a previous version of Ubuntu:

Go to the Synaptic Package Manager.
Go to Settings > Repositories. 
Click on the pulldown menu where it says "Download from:"
Click "Other..."
Press the "Select Best Server" button.
Press "Choose Server"

This will streamline your upgrade and ease the load on servers. The difference for me was at least 100x faster (no exaggeration).
