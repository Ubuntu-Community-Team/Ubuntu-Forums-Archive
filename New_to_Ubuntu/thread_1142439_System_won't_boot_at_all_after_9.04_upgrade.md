### Post by Pollox on 2009-04-29
I don't have a solution for it not booting, but there's certainly no reason to lose everything.  You can always boot from a live cd, then backup your home folder for all your data (and perhaps your /etc folder for settings) to a device such as an external hdd.  Make sure to backup the folders on your hard drive, not the ones on the user account of the live cd.
