### Post by Ms. Daisy on 2011-12-15
I would look at the sound card, see if there is any visible damage. Check any wires or connections coming off of it.  Then make sure it's seated firmly and completely. If it's still being naughty, then I would swap it for a known good one (if you happen to have extra sound cards randomly around your house). 

Sound cards aren't all that expensive, you can probably get a used one for a song.
