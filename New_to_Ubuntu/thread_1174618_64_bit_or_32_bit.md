### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-05-31
Linux, and most Linux programs, are open-source. Because they're open-source, they can be recompiled for 64-bit.

And that's what Ubuntu does. All open-source packages in the Ubuntu repositories on 64-bit systems are 64-bit. All closed-source packages are wrapped or simply programmed correctly to work on 64-bit.

I've been on 64-bit for a little over 6 months now and it's posing no problems for me. I'd say, you might as well try it.
