### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2010-12-03
What kind of microphone do you have?

When you go to the sound preferences (click on the volume icon on your panel and select "Preferences"), and you go to the "input" tab, do you see your mic listed there?  Is it the mic that is selected as default? Is the volume turned up? (I know you said it wasn't muted, but it doesn't hurt to double-check)

One other thing to try would be to open a terminal, and type:
```
alsamixer
```

See if you can find your mic listed there.  You can use the up and down arrow keys to adjust volume, and the 'm' key to toggle the mute. 

Also, you probably don't need to bump this thread quite so often. :)  Once or twice a day should be plenty.
