### Post by coldReactive on 2009-11-14
How can I make it so that cairo dock doesn't show me notifications for empathy both via dialogs and via icon animation? It's very annoying to see a dialog come up in the middle of doing something when my friend messages me.

---

### Post by coldReactive on 2009-11-14
Once again, I found it myself.

Configuration > Behaviour > Taskbar > Interaction

Deselect "Signal the applications demanding your attention with a dialog bubble?"

and

Blank "Signal the applications demanding your attention with an animation?"
