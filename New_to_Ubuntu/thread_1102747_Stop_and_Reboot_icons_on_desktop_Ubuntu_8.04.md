### Post by diablo75 on 2009-03-22
Well... I know that you can restart a PC from the terminal using "sudo init 6"... maybe there's a way to make a custom launcher that will do that.  There's probably a better way, but I don't know.

Something you might do for the shut down shortcut is just use the power button on your PC for that.  By default if you hit the power button, a shutdown menu comes up, but you can change that behavior in your power usage settings (it's in your System>Administration menu I think) so it will just shut down without asking any questions and do so safely.
