### Post by Paresh on 2010-05-18
A panel is like a shelf on your wall, it's a place to put things.

If you right click on the panel, you should get some options, including **Delete This Panel**, which will remove it.

But before you do that, it's probably worth playing with some of the other options to see what the panels can do.

**Properties** will allow you to change the panel background, width autohide etc.

**Add To Panel ...**  This is the biggie.  you can put applets on the panel, where they will be available to click and do things with.  Just select one from the dialog that appears.  Try a simple one at first (eg Fish, which is just for fun).

The application launcher options will allow you to choose an application that is not in the list, a little more involved than the predefined apps.

If you then click on any item in the panel it will do something (Fish will tell your fortune :) )

if you want to remove the app, right-click should provide the options.
