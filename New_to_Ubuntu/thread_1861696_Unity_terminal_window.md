### Post by Prince Valiant on 2011-10-15
Hello!

If you press the windows key, or hit the unity button to open the dash home, you'll open the Unity Dash.  Then if you type 'terminal' into the search bar you'll bring up what you want; or if you go the second icon from the left on the bottom of the dash you'll find a listing of all your applications, with a filter option at the top right. 

Have fun!
