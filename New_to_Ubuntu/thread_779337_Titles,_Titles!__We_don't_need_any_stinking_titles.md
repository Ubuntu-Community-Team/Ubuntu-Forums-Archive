### Post by ibutho on 2008-05-02
In the enterprise sector, Red Hat is dominant and their RHCT/RHCE certification is probably the most highly regarded Linux certification, so its something thats worth considering. The [LPI]("http://www.lpi.org") certs are also highly regarded becuase their vendor neutral. Ubuntu has its own cert which is like a top of of the LPI Level 1 certs.
