### Post by EXCiD3 on 2008-05-16
What video card do you have? You probably havent installed the video drivers for your card yet. You can install them simply by going to System->Administration->Hardware and Drivers. Check the box for your video card and it should go ahead and download and install the drivers for you. After a restart you should have a better screen resolution if everything went correctly.
