My Swiftfox just recently developed a bad habit of immediately jumping to the end of a webpage when the down-arrow is pressed instead of scrolling, in essence it is acting as an End key.

I've fiddled with turning on & off both 'Auto-scrolling' and 'Smooth scrolling,' underneath Preference/Advanced... but it still misbehaves.

### Post by 416inversed on 2010-02-20
Just an FYI for future reference if anyone else faces this same issue:

Press F7.

Apparently F7 toggles 'Caret mode' in Firefox/Swiftfox, which apparently ruins the scrolling function of the arrow keys, among other things.
