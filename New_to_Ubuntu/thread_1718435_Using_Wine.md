### Post by Mark Phelps on 2011-03-31
Before you start installing stuff, you need to visit the WineHQ site to see how well the specific version of each app will work.  They have an application compatibility database that allows you to search for apps and it gives a performance rating (from Garbage to Platinum) for each app and version.

As you can guess, Garbage means the app won't work, no matter what you do.

If there is no rating at that site, the app probably won't work.

Also, in future, you should post Wine questions to the Wine subforum -- where folks can provide you more detailed responses.
