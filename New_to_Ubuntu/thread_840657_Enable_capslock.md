### Post by kennethvenken on 2008-06-25
Hi, my sister and me have the same laptop.
She can't use her capslock. She can't enable it for some reason. Mine is working.

Is there a way to enable her capslock. (preferable gui)

---

### Post by Eisenwinter on 2008-06-25
Go to System -> Preferences -> Keyboard.

In the dialog box, go to Layouts, then Layout options.
Collapse the CapsLock Key Behaviour tab.

Check the option which says "Default", or change it to your liking.
