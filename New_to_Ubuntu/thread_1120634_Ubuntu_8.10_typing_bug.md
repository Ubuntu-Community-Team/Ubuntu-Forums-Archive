### Post by HereInOz on 2009-04-09
This is going to sound a bit odd, but I reckon you are tapping the mouse pad with your thumb while you are typing.  This has the effect of repositioning the cursor to where the mouse pointer currently is, and then the text is entered at that point.

If you are able to turn off the mouse pad (not familiar with the machine, so I don't know if you can or not) do so, and use a USB mouse, and see if the problem goes away.

Apart from this, I can't offer any other solution.
