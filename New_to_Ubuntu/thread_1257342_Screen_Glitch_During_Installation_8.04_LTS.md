### Post by nandemonai on 2009-09-03
Looks to me like 1 of 2 things. Video over heating / on it's way out or a memory issue.

Run memtest86+ off the LiveCD for an hour or two and see if it throws any errors.

Also, I'd open up the case and make sure there isn't too much dust in there, especially around the CPU / Video card / Chipset.
