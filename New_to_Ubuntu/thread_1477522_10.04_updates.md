### Post by sixthwheel on 2010-05-08
You have to be online to download updates from the ubuntu servers.(that includes security updates)
Click on System/Administration/Software Sources.
Then look at what servers are checked to download updates from.

You can install programs and such, that are already downloaded. This would be in your Software Center, in your Applications menu.
