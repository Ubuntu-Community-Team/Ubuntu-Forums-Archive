### Post by HolyDonut on 2011-09-21
I got this to work in 11.04 very easily.

I opened up VLC on the iPhone, then connected my iPhone to my computer. 

In the Ubuntu "Documents on [name of iPhone]" folder I double-clicked the VLC icon, **there was a folder called "Documents" in there**. I copied my two .flv videos into the "Documents" folder.

My two videos showed up on my iPhone after I closed out of VLC (on the iPhone) and opened it back up (on the iPhone).
