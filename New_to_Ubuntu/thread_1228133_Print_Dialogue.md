### Post by Temposs on 2009-07-31
Some dialog boxes are too tall for mini notebooks(vertical resolution less than 800), but there is a way to work with them.

Just put your mouse over the dialog window, press and hold the Alt button, click and hold the left mouse button, and you should be able to move the window around. This is the best way to deal with the dialog, I think.

### Post by Temposs on 2009-07-31
If you click on the Print Queue icon when you print something, it should bring up a window with the list of print jobs in the queue. if you right click a print job, there should be an option there to cancel the job.
