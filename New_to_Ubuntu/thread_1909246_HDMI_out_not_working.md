### Post by freebird54 on 2012-01-15
Without being an expert, or particularly experienced with HDMI, I still have a suspicion as to the cause.  First question to answer is which screen resolutions does the TV support? If the resolution your sending is NOT supported by the TV, you will get exactly those results - only the resolutions sent at bootup and shutdown (lower VESA resolutions usually) will be visible.

If you can set your laptop to use a resolution that your TV recognizes - or at least differing ones to test - your difficulty may be resolved.

Good luck!
