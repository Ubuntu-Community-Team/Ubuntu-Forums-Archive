### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-04-09
The netbook remix ties directly into Gnome - if you add a bookmark in Nautilus or an open/save dialog, it will appear in the right-hand panel of the netbook interface. Same with applications - use Gnome's application menu editor and your new items will appear in the netbook interface.
