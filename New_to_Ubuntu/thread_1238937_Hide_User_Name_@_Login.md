### Post by starcraft.man on 2009-08-13
I am guessing your thinking about security from a local pov. Someone coming up to screen seeing username and then just trying to guess your pass. Let me just say, don't be. If someone's got physical access to your computer, there's an endless number of ways to get at your stuff, and no way to stop em all. Easiest being taking your HD out of comp (thus having all data) and then just working on it at home. Trivial. By same token, having a pass that isn't something everyone knows about you never hurts either.

Kubuntu a good choice btw. 

If you really insist, see System  Settings in Kmenu > Advanced Tab > Login Manager > Convenience Tab and tinker with settings. There's an option there says preselected user or none, choose none. Might be more settings too ya might like, look around.
