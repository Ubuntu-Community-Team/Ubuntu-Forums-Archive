### Post by jtarin on 2011-07-06
Most YouTube URLs take the form of:

    [http://www.youtube.com/**watch?v=VIDEOID**](http://www.youtube.com/**watch?v=VIDEOID**)

Note the 11-character code at the end,of the video you want to watch and place it like so:

    [http://www.youtube.com/v/VIDEOID](http://www.youtube.com/v/VIDEOID)

This move brings up the widget normally used to play embedded videos—a bit over-sized, sometimes, but nicely distraction-free as well.
