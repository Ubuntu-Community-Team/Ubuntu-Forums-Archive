### Post by ajgreeny on 2009-01-29
You can easily add an icon to the taskbar for Numbering On/Off and also Bullets On/Off.  Click on the small vertical bar at the right of the taskbar and choose Customise.  You can then add various other icons as you wish, and also get rid of any default ones you don't ever use by selection of the visible buttons you want.
