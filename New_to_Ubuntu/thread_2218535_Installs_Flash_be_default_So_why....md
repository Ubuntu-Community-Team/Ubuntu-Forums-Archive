### Post by ajgreeny on 2014-04-21
I do not install those things when installing the OS as I prefer to control and faster installation speed of doing it later, but I do not believe you have anything to worry about regarding any reinstallation.

One possible disadvantage of doing what you did can be that a poorer working graphic driver might be installed rather than one that you may prefer and know to work best with your graphic card, but if you have only an Intel card, for example, or an older nvidia or ATI card with no proprietary driver available, this will not have happened.

I can't help with your flash problem.
