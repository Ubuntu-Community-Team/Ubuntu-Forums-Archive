### Post by Buzzdee on 2008-10-13
Last.fm works differently in ubuntu than in Windows. In Windows, you install last.fm and the programme itself scrobbels your data to the web. In ubuntu, every programme has its own scrobbling plugin (amarok, exaile, rhythmbox, banshee). You just need to enter your user data in the player of your choice and can uninstall the last.fm package you installed earlier, if you don't want to use the last.fm-player - there's no use for it otherwise.
