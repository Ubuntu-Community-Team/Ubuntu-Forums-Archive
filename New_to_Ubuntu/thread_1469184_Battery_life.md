### Post by 3rdalbum on 2010-05-02
I'd recommend getting a bigger battery (like a 9 cell) as there's only so much life you can squeeze out of your battery!

Other than that, you could run ([sudo powertop]("apt:powertop") to identify what options can be applied to help with battery life.
