### Post by Dill on 2008-08-13
On the screen that shows up when you first boot your machine (which may have something that indicates the manufacturer of the motherboard-- likely Toshiba), you may see a set of key commands that say, "Press F2 for Setup" or "Press F10 for Boot menu". Look for the key that corresponds to your Boot Menu and press that (you may have to restart your computer, as you'll have to press it fairly soon after the computer starts). You should then see an option to boot from the CD. Some common keys are F9, F10, and F12, but it's different for different manufacturers. 

If you don't see instructions telling you which key corresponds to your Boot Menu, please let us know.
