### Post by mikechant on 2009-03-03
> Compiz is the visual effects. AKA eye candy. Uses a lot of system resources.

But it's not just a resource issue. Even if you have tons of RAM and CPU, Compiz is just not compatible with certain combinations of video card/driver/video file type, and you can get various problems including choppy video, wrong size video (postage stamp) and missing window frames.
