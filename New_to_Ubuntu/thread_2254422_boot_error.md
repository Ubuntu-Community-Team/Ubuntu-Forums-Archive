### Post by Impavidus on 2014-11-27
It seems there is only a tiny 250MB ext2 partition on your hard drive. It looks like a /boot partition. The rest of the drive is empty. It seems you lost a partition. As you have a /boot partition (I assume), this may well have been an encrypted install. I think the only way to solve this is by reinstalling. I hope you have backups. It may be possible to recover files, but on encrypted systems chances of success are low.

Do you have any idea how this could have happened? Were you changing partitions? Else this could be the result of a failing hard drive.
