### Post by click4851 on 2010-01-03
So okay along the top bar of your desktop do you see the three words "applications", "places", and "system"?

If so, click on "places", do you get a drop down menu?

If so, select "home" ( technically you could select anything here, but lets just stay with home), do you see your home folder?

If so, select "edit" from the bar in the window your home folder is displayed in. do you see the drop down menu under edit?

If so, select "preferences", do you see the preferences dialog box open up?

If so, select the "behavior" tab, do you see the selections for click behavior?

If so, make your choice and have at that clickin...:)
