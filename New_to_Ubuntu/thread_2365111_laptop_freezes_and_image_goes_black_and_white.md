### Post by CatKiller on 2017-07-02
The black and white window isn't a symptom, it's an indicator. It's provided by the Fading Windows plugin of Compiz, which is used by Ubuntu to provide its effects.

It means that the window is no longer responding to requests from the window manager. It might mean that the program that created the window is busy, or it might be an actual problem. I suspect that it's just that your computer is struggling to keep up, and that it all goes back to normal after it's finished doing whatever it's doing, but we won't know without more information from you.

If you just don't like the effect and you're otherwise happy, you can just turn off the plugin.
