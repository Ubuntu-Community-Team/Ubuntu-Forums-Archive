### Post by Hospadar on 2008-07-01
I believe you can generate an iso of the disk and that will be enough.  I think you can right-click the disk on the desktop, go to Copy Disk, and choose the "image writer" as your burner.  This will save an image of the disk.

As above, I'm sure you'd need a modchip to actually play said disk, but I think that's how you'd go about backing it up.  It's also possible that there are other (non-free) tools like alcohol 120% that are designed to make copies of hard-to-copy media that would do a better job.  For that though you'd be better off asking around on a gaming forum somewhere.
