### Post by Ms_Angel_D on 2009-02-22
You want to create a launcher.

1)Right click on panel & click add to panel
2)Select Application Launcher
3)Select the application you wish to add (You'll notice the structure here is the same as your menu structure).

Hope this helps.

Angel
