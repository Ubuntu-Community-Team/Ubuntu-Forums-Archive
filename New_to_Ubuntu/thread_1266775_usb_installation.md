### Post by j7%&lt;RmUg on 2009-09-15
Easy:

Get into your BIOS(usually the F2 or F10 key)Just turn your computer on and press the right key until it pops up.

You will need to change your primary boot device from your hard drive to your USB drive, then save and exit the bios.

Reboot your machine and it (should) boot from your USB drive.
