### Post by oldrocker99 on 2019-05-23
And, it should be mentioned that in Linux, 64-bit packages are suffixed AMD64, in honor of the first 64-bit commercial CPU. It doesn't matter which brand of CPU you use.

Also, 32-bit programs are suffixed i386, in honor of Intel's feat in releasing the first commercial 32-bit CPU.
