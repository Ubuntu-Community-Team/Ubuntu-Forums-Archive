### Post by ramjet_1953 on 2008-08-05
Unfortunately, Lexmark are the most Linux Unfriendly printers out there.

They do not release Linux drivers, or information so that programmers can write Linux drivers.

I had a quick look on the open printing database and the 6150 is not listed.

However if you visit the site and try a driver that is close to the model number of your printer, you may get it to work. The x6170 is listed and apparently partially works.

Here's the link: [http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi](http://openprinting.org/printer_list.cgi)

For future reference, HP seem to make the most Linux Friendly printers, as they release Linux drivers.
