### Post by avtolle on 2009-02-19
Nope; that refers to those users who have the 64-bit version of Ubuntu installed on their computers, those with a 64-bit processor(s). Has nothing to do with graphics drivers generically.

### Post by Therion on 2009-02-19
Simply put it refers to a specific type of processor.  Some processors are 32-bit and some are 64-bit.  64-bit processors are capable of running a 64-bit operating system.  Ubuntu, and many other Linux distros, offer 64-bit versions of their operating system that only 64-bit processors can use.
