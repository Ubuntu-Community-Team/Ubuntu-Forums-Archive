### Post by JD8182 on 2011-09-13
Most CD drives have two rings for CDs/DVDs the large ring for regular size and a smaller ring in the center of the larger ring.. so if it does not have that small ring in the middle, I would not recommend placing a mini CD in your drive, they do make adapters for the mini CDs though, however I have not seen them for a few years but they might still be around..Hope this helps.
