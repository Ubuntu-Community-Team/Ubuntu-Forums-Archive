### Post by dark_harmonics on 2008-12-08
It might be that your desktop resolution is set larger than mine, but my text doesnt intertwine. Try this out: open a nautilus window ( places -> home for example) and go to edit-->preferences. Under the view tab check "use compact layout". You could also try shrinking the icons here. 

If it is possible on your system, you could alternately increase your desktop resolution and things on your desk will shrink. 

I think the first solution may have been what you were searching for.
