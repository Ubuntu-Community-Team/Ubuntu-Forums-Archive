### Post by Vordark on 2008-06-04
Nevermind.  I had the question answered elsewhere.  The settings for this are under "Viewport Switcher" -> "Actions" -> "Desktop-based Viewport Switching".

The entries in the list for "Move Next" and "Move Prev" are bound to Mouse5 and Mouse4, respectively.  You can either assign them to something else, or delete the assignments so that it reads "None".  Doing the latter retains all other behavior and you won't flip workspaces if you happen to mouse over the desktop while scrolling through a file.
