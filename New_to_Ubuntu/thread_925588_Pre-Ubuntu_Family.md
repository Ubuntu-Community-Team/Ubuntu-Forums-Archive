Seems to me that "Ubuntu Family" is simply an umbrella term for the various flavours of whatever-buntu that grew out of Ubuntu proper. As for the Pre, say "Pre-Ubuntu Family 8.04 Hardy Heron"... I suppose it means pre-8.04, not pre-Family. So "Pre-Ubuntu Family 8.04 Hardy Heron" would be any U-, Ku-, Edu- or Xu-buntu prior to version 8.04.
