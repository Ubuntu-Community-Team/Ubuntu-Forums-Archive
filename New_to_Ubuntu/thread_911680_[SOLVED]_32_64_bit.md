### Post by iaculallad on 2008-09-05
> **hyperhopper said:**
> what is the difference between 32 and 64 and how do I tell what version I am using---8.04 from live cd

For the difference, read [this]("https://help.ubuntu.com/community/32bit_and_64bit").

On how you can tell of what version you're currently using, do the command below on your terminal:

```
uname -m
```

i686 = 32-Bit
x86_64 = 64-Bit
