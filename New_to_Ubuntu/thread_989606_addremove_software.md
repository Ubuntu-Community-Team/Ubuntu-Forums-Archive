### Post by Paqman on 2008-11-21
> **Baldemar said:**
> I know I can use synaptic package, or are they separate functions?

They both use the same system behind the scenes. They're just two different GUIs with different levels of complexity.

To edit your menus and restore Add/Remove, right click on the menu and "edit menus". If you need to make an entry from scratch you'll need to specify gnome-app-install as the way to launch Add/Remove.

### Post by drs305 on 2008-11-21
If you are talking about the Add/Remove icon, right click on the panel, Add to Panel, Application Launcher, and drag the Add/Remove icon to the panel and release.
