### Post by quadra on 2009-11-04
Maybe not exactly the way you want it, but similar:

You may use the functionality that lets you add a sub-menu of your main menu to the panel. The sub-menus contain both icons and text.

Example: Applications - Accessories - [Right-click one of the items in there] - Entire menu - ADD THIS AS MENU TO PANEL.
Now, you may adapt the contents of the menu.

Better, leave the menus as they are but add your own dedicated "My drawer" menu (Right-click on "Apllications" - Edit menus - New menu) and add this one to the panel as described above.
