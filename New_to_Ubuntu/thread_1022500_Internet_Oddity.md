### Post by crf on 2008-12-27
It happens because your computer isn't able to translate the names like "ubuntuforums.org" into their numerical internet addresses ("91.189.94.12"). That is what a DNS server does. 

Your computer either queries the DNS server itself (a computer on the internet, usually run by your internet company), or if you have a router, your router may do that.

The literature that came with your internet connection may help you. And also these help pages [https://help.ubuntu.com/8.10/internet/C/index.html](https://help.ubuntu.com/8.10/internet/C/index.html) ,
see the "check DNS" section.
