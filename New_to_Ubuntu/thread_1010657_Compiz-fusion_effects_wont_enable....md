### Post by sandy8925 on 2008-12-14
Did you enable the effects? 
Right click on the desktop and click on 'Change Desktop Background'. Then select the 'Visual Effects' tab.
There should be 3 options:

None
Normal
Extra

Select Normal or Extra whichever you want.The effects eill then be enabled.
