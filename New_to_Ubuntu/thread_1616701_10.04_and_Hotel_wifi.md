### Post by blazemore on 2010-11-08
It's probably safe in terms of viruses etc, as very few malware authors bother with Linux.

However, it could be the case that someone is eavesdropping on every single packet sent. Avoid doing anything involving online banking, credit cards etc.
Even checking your emails: if someone got your email password could they use it to get into your Paypal for example?

If possible, use your 3G modem and not a completely unencrypted WiFi connection.
