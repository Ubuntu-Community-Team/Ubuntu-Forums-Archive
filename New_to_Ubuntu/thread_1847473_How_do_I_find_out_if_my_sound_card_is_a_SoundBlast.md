### Post by 3rdalbum on 2011-09-20
There's programs you can install that give you a GUI to see what kind of sound card you have... or you can go to the terminal and paste in the word:

```
lspci
```

which will tell you much quicker.

(note: that's LSPCI, but lowercase; just copy and paste that into the terminal and it will tell you.)
