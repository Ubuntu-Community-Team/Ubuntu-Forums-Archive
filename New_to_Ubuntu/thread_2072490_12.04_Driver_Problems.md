I have tried looking for drivers under system settings. I have also tried searching google and these forums for answers to similar problems to no avail. I am not sure which chip controls the HDMI port so I have listed them both below. Sorry if this is a stupid question or posted in the wrong spot. I will greatly appreciate any help you guys could give me.

Motherboard: Gigabyte GA-EP45-UD3R
North bridge: Intel P45 Express Chipset
South bridge: Intel ICH10R
Other motherboard specs: [http://www.gigabyte.us/products/product-page.aspx?pid=3013&dl=1#sp](http://www.gigabyte.us/products/product-page.aspx?pid=3013&dl=1#sp)
