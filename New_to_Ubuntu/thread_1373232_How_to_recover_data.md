### Post by audiomick on 2010-01-05
> **iindikaa said:**
> I'm using ubuntu 9.10
After updating, the screen became blank. As I could do nothing, I re installed the system again. Is there any way I can recover the previous data? (something like recovery tools in windows)

It is shutting the gate after the horse has bolted, I know, but if something like that happens, you can usually boot into the live CD and save stuff onto an external drive before re-installing.

Another safety net is to have your /home on a separate partition. This means that you can simply re-mount /home during a fresh install, and retain your data. After you have tried everything you can to recover your stuff, you might consider re-doing your new install this way.

Good luck with the recovery attempt.
