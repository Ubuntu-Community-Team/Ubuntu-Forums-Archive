### Post by cariboo on 2011-09-01
From what I can see the iPad is dlna capable, so you can use something like [mediatomb]("http://mediatomb.cc/"), it's available in the repositories, or minidlna, available [here]("http://sourceforge.net/projects/minidlna/") to stream media of almost all types.
