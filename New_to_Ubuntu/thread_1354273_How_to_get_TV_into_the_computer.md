### Post by lovinglinux on 2009-12-13
I did some googling and it seems to be video in indeed, but it doesn't have a TV tuner. So, you can't plug an antenna RF cable. You need a setup box or a TV with S-Video out.

You can use TVTime. It has an option to capture from S-Video input, but you will probably need to configure some stuff to make it detect your card.
