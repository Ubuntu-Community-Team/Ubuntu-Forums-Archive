### Post by tompickles on 2008-05-11
it could be they are using a technology called LSPT - think thats the right way round!) basically you have a load of machines with like a rubbish processer, some RAM and a graphics card and a network card. you then connect it to your network and set the BIOS to boot from network. your powerfull linux machine then runs all the different users sessions on each of these "thin clients". Edubuntu is very good at setting this up for you as part of its mission is easy and cheap networking in education.
