### Post by Paqman on 2008-08-24
All your desktop settings (and all those of all your apps) are stored in files in your /home directory. That's why a lot of people like to put /home on a seperate partition. When they reinstall the system files, all their settings are retained, so as soon as the system is installed, it's got their custom setup.
