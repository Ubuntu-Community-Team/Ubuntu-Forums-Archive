### Post by RomeReactor on 2008-06-11
Hi. As sam and Inxsible said, during installation you were prompted to enter a login name and password for your system; do you remember what those were? Also, Ubuntu is case-sensitive, so if your login name/password were composed with some capital letters, you should input them as you entered them the first time (although I think you can't use caps for the login name; don't remember if the password allows it). Make sure you don't have caps lock activated.
