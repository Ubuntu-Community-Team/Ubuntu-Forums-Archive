Section 1: Retry.

Defragment windows, disable Pagefile. Afterwards, retry the download (do not use the windows installer). Burn this to a DVD. Run wubi.exe from the DVD, and select "Try ubuntu without installing" Select Reboot now. Then it should reboot to an Ubuntu Live Session (if not, go to Section 2).  Select "Try ubuntu". Then familiarize yourself with it! Once done, select "Install ubuntu 13.04" Follow everything until step 4. There, choose "Install alongside windows". Re-size to your liking (remember to give each OS enough space). Everything else, adjust to your liking. Wait, watch the slideshow, or make a cup of coffee/tea. Reboot, then choose windows 7. Enable Pagefile. Reboot. Choose ubuntu. Enjoy!

[HR][/HR]Section 2: Ubuntu won't start in Live-mode!

Use 64-bit ubuntu, as you may have UEFI. Set BIOS/UEFI to boot from the DVD drive first. If you reach a Man=Keyboard logo at the bottom, and nothing appears afterwards hit enter. Choose language, then hit F6, add nomodeset and/or xforcevesa. If you reach a Grub Menu, and nothing else appears after hitting enter, hit e, and add nomodeset and/or xforcevesa.


[HR][/HR]Section 3: The splash screen looks old.

First of all, use Xubuntu, as this may mean unity will not run. 

Hit F6 or e, and replace:
> quiet splash
with:
> nosplash

[HR][/HR]Section 4: Do not use wubi.

Do not use the windows installer due to many unassigned bugs and the possibility of making your machine unbootable!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!! Instead, use dual-boot.

[HR][/HR]Hope this helps!
