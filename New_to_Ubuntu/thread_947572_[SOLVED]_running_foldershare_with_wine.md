### Post by Nxion on 2008-10-17
I tried to use it and I was pretty unhappy with it. How much data are you wanting to share? A couple of gigabytes or a lot of gigabytes. I would recommend [Dropbox]("https://www.getdropbox.com/"). The other cool part about Dropbox is that they offer a .deb to install it. Makes things very easy. I love it, it is very reliable. Also I found this of someone that sat down and compared the two [(FolderShare and Dropbox)]("http://gigaom.com/2008/03/17/foldershare-vs-dropbox/").
