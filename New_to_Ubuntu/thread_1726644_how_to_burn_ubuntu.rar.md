### Post by coffeecat on 2011-04-11
> **naved ..... said:**
> **** man i had deleted that rar files after extracting it so now what to do ? 

As has been explained, that was not a rar file, it was an ISO. If you have deleted it, you will have to download it again. Once you have downloaded it, get Windows to show filename extensions. Then you will see that it is called either ubuntu-10.10-desktop-amd64.iso or ubuntu-10.10-desktop-i386.iso depending whether you downloaded the 64 or 32 bit version. 

Then you'll need this guide for burning it to CD:

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BurningIsoHowto)
