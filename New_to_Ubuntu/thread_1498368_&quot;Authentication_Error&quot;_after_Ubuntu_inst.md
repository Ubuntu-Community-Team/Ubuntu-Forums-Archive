### Post by jwbrase on 2010-05-31
Well, a few possible reasons I can think of: 

Is caps-lock on?

Do you generally use a US keyboard on Windows? If, for example, you're used to typing on a German keyboard, but Ubuntu is defaulting to a US keyboard, you're going to get your z's and y's switched when you type your password, ä, ö, ü are going to be replaced by ', ;, and [, etc. Similar problems will occur for French, Polish, or Elbonian keyboards.
