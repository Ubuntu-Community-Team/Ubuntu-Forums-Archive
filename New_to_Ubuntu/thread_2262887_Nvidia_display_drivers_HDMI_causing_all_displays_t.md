### Post by efflandt on 2015-01-28
Define what you mean by "revert". Does it revert to single display instead of 3 or does resolution change? Is the HDTV one of or all of your monitors, or do you sometimes connect to single HDTV instead of the other 3 displays?

I know that when some people turn off an HDMI HDTV connected to a Raspberry Pi or switch to a different input on the TV, it shuts down their Pi unless CEC is disabled on the Pi (most likely related to CEC [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HDMI#CEC](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HDMI#CEC) ). And while my LG HDTV remote can do some things when running XBMC on a Pi, I have never had that issue. Ubuntu (12.04 or 14.04) never even seems to notice if my HDTV is on or off (actually a form if its own standby). But I am only using a single 1080p HDTV.
