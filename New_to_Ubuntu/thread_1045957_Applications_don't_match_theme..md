### Post by jerome1232 on 2009-01-21
[http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=100](http://gnome-look.org/index.php?xcontentmode=100)

Download a gtk-2.x theme, all gtk aware apps will look the same. Now if your using qt apps (K apps) then they won't look the same as your gnome (gtk) apps.

--edit-- let me make myself a bit more detailed I think I was too brief

Gnome uses GTK to theme, metacity to draw window decorations. Most of your apps will be gtk aware and should be appearing fairly similar as defined by your gtk theme.

As an example I'm useing Icewm with the ThinBlack theme, I'm using the "Glossy" gtk theme and the "Elementary" Icon set. Notice how all of my apps buttons and general look is the same. If you want a uniform look you will need to restrict yourself to gtk aware applications.
