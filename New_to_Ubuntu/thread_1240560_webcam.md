### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2009-08-16
Look around for preferences settings on those sites and see if there is a place to specify the location of your webcam.  If you can find it, there will probably be several options.  Just try them one after another until you find one that works.

Also, assuming these sites use flash, you will have to give it permission to use your webcam.  Usually there's a popup asking for permission.
