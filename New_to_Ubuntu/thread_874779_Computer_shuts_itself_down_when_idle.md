### Post by coffeecat on 2008-07-30
Does the computer turn itself off without warning while you are working at it, or only after a period of time left alone? Is it a laptop, and are you running off battery? When the battery gets too low it will shut itself down, depending on power management settings - on my Sony laptop with an alarmingly loud beep.

If you are using a laptop, check the settings in System > Preferences > Power Management. If a desktop, power management won't be relevant.
