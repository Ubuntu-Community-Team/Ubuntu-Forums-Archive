### Post by EXCiD3 on 2008-05-18
Theres always the "Search" box. ;) But yes it is still there: When you go to create a new thread, right after the title box, there is a box that says "Check if already posted", after you type in your title, just click the button (its actually a button, it sure doesnt look like it!) and a popup window will give you the results instead of updating the page.
