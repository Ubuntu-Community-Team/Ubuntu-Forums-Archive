### Post by tgalati4 on 2012-12-17
Open in a browser:

[http://localhost:631/printers/](http://localhost:631/printers/)

Cut and paste your printer's information.

It will look like:

Description: Hewlett-Packard hp color LaserJet 1500
Location: tpad-Gloria7
Printer Driver: HP Color LaserJet 1500 Foomatic/foo2oak (recommended)
Printer State: idle, accepting jobs, published. 
Device URI: usb://HP/color%20LaserJet%201500
