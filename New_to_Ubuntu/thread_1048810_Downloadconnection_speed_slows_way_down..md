### Post by dnRoyston on 2009-01-23
In gnome, go to System>Administration>System Monitor. Click "Resources", and tell us what the "Recieving" section reads normally when not loading webpages or downloading anything. It should stay at 0, with the occasional (in my case, every 3 seconds or so) tip a bit higher.
