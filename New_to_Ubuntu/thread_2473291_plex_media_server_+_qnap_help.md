It's tough to advise you on this one. Following the instructions on the site ActionParsnip links to will work, but, given your self-described unfamiliarity with Linux, you must realize that what you are trying to do is equivalent to jumping into the deep end. You need to set your expectations accordingly.

Linux is a geek's OS. It is ultra powerful/flexible/versatile, but it's also got a significant learning curve. No one, including the members on this forum, can give you a magical crash course on Linux. Before you start on this project, my recommendation is to first get more comfortable with the OS. The links in my sig: Linux is Not Windows, A great CLI Guide & Resources For Newcomers will help. The following is also very helpful: [https://linuxjourney.com](https://linuxjourney.com)

Of course, you can just dive in and figure out how to swim with everything at stake from the get go. It all depends on your tolerance for stress and frustration.
