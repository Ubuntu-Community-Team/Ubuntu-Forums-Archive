### Post by ugm6hr on 2008-05-11
You can run a system upgrade from the AlternateCD, essentially the same way that you can online (the same files are on the AlternateCD).  Just boot up, and then pop the AlternateCD in, and it should auto-run and offer to upgrade.

Just a point of warning though - if you had major problems getting dial-up to work before, it may be no easier the second time around.  I have no idea what the dial-up support on Hardy is like.
