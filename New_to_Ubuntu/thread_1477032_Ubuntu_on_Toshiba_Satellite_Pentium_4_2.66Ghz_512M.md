### Post by flyfishingphil on 2010-05-08
Not the same Toshiba,I have a Toshiba L305 32 bit, w/Pentium Dual @ 2.16GHz, 3 GB RAM, but I am using 10.04 with no problems whatsoever. 

Use Firefox for browser, no problems there. 

Have used OpenOffice for quite a while, even on Windough$, no problems. 

Still adjusting some of the factors like the SE Walkman cell phone, but everything seems to work fine. 

Was using 9.10 and did a full upgrade online to 10.04. No problems there but did need to do a little fine tuning in Firefox and a couple of other areas. Still pretty much a "noob" with Ubuntu but have not seen 2% of the problems listed here. (Maybe that's because I just "use" and don't try to make things "work better"?)

Only problems I came across were getting the Canon MP560 to print/scan to work right but that's a problem with Canon and mating with Ubuntu. Also can't use older Lexmark printer I carried with me to outdoor expos because of same problem. No Linux drivers for it from Lexmark.

Rely on update manager for updates and have had no problems there with updates or results from.

Been using Ubuntu for about a month now and don't even know if my Vista is still accessible, and don't really care. Next step I will probably do before long is to do a backup of all of my files, dump the Windough$ space taking stuff and go Lucid exclusively. 

Much respect, and many thanks, for those that developed, and continue to improve the Ubuntu system.
