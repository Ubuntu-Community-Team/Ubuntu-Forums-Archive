### Post by KdotJ on 2010-08-13
Hey, 

try going to System > Administration > Login Screen, and then unclick "play login sound"

Also, I have that netbook too, if you have the sound muted when you turn it off, it remembers, and will be muted the next time you turn it on, and therefore muting the login sounds

[edit]
And another thing to try... go to System > Preferences > Startup Applications, and look for "GNOME Login Sound", and deselected it
[/edit]
