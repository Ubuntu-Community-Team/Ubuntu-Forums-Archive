### Post by ajgreeny on 2012-11-21
Why do you want firestarter to run at bootup, and why firestarter, which I believe is now deprecated in ubuntu and replaced by ufw or gufw.

Firestarter is not a firewall in itself, but merely a way to configure iptables, the real firewall of ubuntu.  There is unlikely to be a good reason to run firestarter or ufw/gufw every time you boot the machine.
