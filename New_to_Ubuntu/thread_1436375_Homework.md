### Post by Calash on 2010-03-22
Are we talking storage as in Computer storage?

Assuming yes, then you are talking about the collection of digital information on a medium.  The most common forms are magnetic, as seen in Floppy and Hard drives, and optical, as seen in CD and DVDs. 

In basic terms both mediums do the same thing.  They store sequences of 1 and 0's that the computer can then translate for you into usable information.
