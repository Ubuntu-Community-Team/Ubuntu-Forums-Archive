### Post by SeijiSensei on 2011-03-23
My daughter's 1201N runs both Win7 and Kubuntu just fine in a dual-boot arrangement.  If you've got a newer Asus with the Optimus graphics card like the 1015 or 1215, switching between the Intel and NVIDIA chip from within Linux isn't an easy thing to do.  I hear it's not too hard to boot up in one or the other, but switching is more a problem.  Some innovative fellow members have come up with [methods]("http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=1677780") to work around this, but they're a bit complex for beginners.
