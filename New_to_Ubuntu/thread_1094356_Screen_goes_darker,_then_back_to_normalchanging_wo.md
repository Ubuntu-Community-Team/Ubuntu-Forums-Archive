### Post by The Judderman on 2009-03-12
The changing to another desktop thing when you scroll is to do with the settings in your compiz manager. In one of the settings of the cube rotate (or I think the desktop wall), you have the option to scroll to other desktops with the mouse wheel. This only happens if your mouse is over the desktop background, not an open window.

i find this very annoying, so I disable wheel scrolling from the behaviour menu.

Hope this makes sense!

The Judderman

### Post by The Judderman on 2009-03-12
What have you set the appearances too in terms? ie, if you go to System>Preferences>Appearance, and click on the Visual Effects tab, what have you selected? I presume that you have normal (which I think means that Compiz is enabled, but not using all the exciting features).

if you install the compiz manager (compizconfig-settings-manager in synaptic or sudo apt-get install compizconfig-settings-manager), then you can look at the viewport switcher plugin. You can disable the mouse scrolling in the bindings, or just disable the whole plugin if you don't need it! 

That also lets you play around with compiz and get the most out of it!! 

Have fun!
