### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2013-11-06
I think somehow you have accidentally changed the number of virtual desktops. Open ccsm and go to General Options > Desktop Size and reset it to 4 horizontal, 1 vertical.

---

### Post by deadflowr on 2013-11-06
Adding to post #2, if when you click on the workspace icon in the dash,  it shows four workspaces all in a row, then the workspaces are set correctly for the cube.
If, however, they are set with two high and two low(the default workspace layout) then the behavior you see is what will happen.
