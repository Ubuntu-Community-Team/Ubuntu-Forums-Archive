### Post by Mark Phelps on 2014-12-09
I checked the WineHQ site for the two things you mentioned:  

Corona SDK is rated Bronze, meaning, it does little more than install.  You're not going to able to use it effectively due to the lack of functionality; otherwise, it would have earned a much higher rating.

Visual Studio recent versions are rated Garbage, meaning, they are not going to work no matter what you do.

If your plan is to use Ubuntu to do Windows application development, then you're wasting your time as you really need a Windows environment for that.
