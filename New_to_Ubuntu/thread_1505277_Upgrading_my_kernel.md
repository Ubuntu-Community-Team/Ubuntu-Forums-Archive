### Post by Paqman on 2010-06-09
There's a slightly newer one available. Go to System > Admin > Update Manager and click on "check". It'll offer you any new packages that are available, including kernels. You'll need to reboot after installing a new kernel.

Kernel updates are generally for security, so you should definitely do them.
