### Post by natex on 2009-12-06
Just about any point and shoot you buy will be recognized.  Ubuntu uses libgphoto2 and can recognize any camera on this list (maybe more).

[http://gphoto.sourceforge.net/proj/libgphoto2/support.php](http://gphoto.sourceforge.net/proj/libgphoto2/support.php)

The image files themselves are jpegs and are universally compatible. If your new camera saves image in a raw format (i.e. cr2, nef, pef, dng, etc...), then you are still most likely covered since Ubuntu utilizes dcraw. [http://www.cybercom.net/~dcoffin/dcraw/](http://www.cybercom.net/~dcoffin/dcraw/)

natex
