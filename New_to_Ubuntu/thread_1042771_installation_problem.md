### Post by NewJack on 2009-01-18
First, did you check the MD5sum for the version of Ubuntu you downloaded?

Check here: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/UbuntuHashes)

If you don't know how to check the MD5: [https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/HowToMD5SUM)

This will verify the files integrity.

Second, if the MD5 checks out ok, I (as well as others) will recommend that you burn the image file at x4 speed.  It is believed that burning at a faster rate gives you a greater chance at making a coaster out of your CD.

Hopefully that helped
