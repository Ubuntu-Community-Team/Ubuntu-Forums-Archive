### Post by grahammechanical on 2011-11-23
Have you read the Help documentation that was installed with Ubuntu?

I will assume that you are using Ubuntu 11.10 Oneiric Ocelot. So, I suggest that you open the Dash by clicking on the Ubuntu icon on the top of the panel that is resting on the left side of the screen. This panel is called the Launcher.

When you have opened the Dash type Help in the search bar and you will see a blue circle with a white question mark on it. Click on that and read about Ubuntu. There is a section on Networking.

Regards and welcome.
