### Post by Mr. Picklesworth on 2009-03-30
I think what you are looking to do is organize your panels (the two bars on the top and bottom of the screen). Panels hold applets, which you can usually play with by right clicking on them. (Or right click on an empty part of the panel and go to Add to Panel to add more applets!).
The menu bar, the clock, the volume control, that window list on the bottom... those are all applets.

A theme will change the *appearance* of those, but not the layout :)

Oh, also you don't need to manually add themes to .themes. In System -> Preferences -> Appearance there's an Install button to the bottom right of the Theme section, or you can drag and drop the downloaded theme into there. You don't even need to extract the archive; it handles that for you.
