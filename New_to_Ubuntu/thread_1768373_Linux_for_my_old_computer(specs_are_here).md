Puppy Linux will work also. Another thought would be if you could scrounge up another 256 Mb of ram, You could then run 10.04 or 11.04. I am running both of these on a dual boot setup. I would dump all your Windows 98 stuff and do a fresh install. You don't say how big your hard drive is but even Natty only takes up about 5 Mb.
