### Post by ptn107 on 2009-02-07
1. No, to you it won't matter.  I can't even tell the difference in my machines, which use both.  Intel *had* some past issues with Ubuntu that have since been resolved.  Price wise though AMD tends to be more cost effective.

2. Either card will work under Ubuntu, but will most likely require the proprietary drivers from their respective websites in order to reach their full potential.  Remember gaming is hit or miss in Ubuntu even with wine.  Most people choose to dual boot windows.

3. Personally speaking I've never ever had problems with 64-bit Ubuntu since 7.04.  Currently 64-bit flash is available (not sure about java).  In either case their 32-bit counterparts will run fine in 64-bit Ubuntu for the time being.
