### Post by daimaru on 2010-01-09
dont have any answer to your problem, since I think hibernation and suspend never worked well. I dont use them. But maybe you should enable the ctrl+alt+backspace command to avoid hard reboot. 

not sure if the comp is still responsive when its in that state but if it works i guess its better to restart the gdm then to do a hard reboot.

you can enable it under system->pref->keyboard  | on layouts tab click "layout options" and tick the checkbox under "key sequence to kill the x server" to enable ctrl+alt+backspace.
