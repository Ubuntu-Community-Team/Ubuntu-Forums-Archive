Please wait 24 hours before bumping a post.  We are all here voluntarily, on our own time and we live all around the world.  It may simply be that the person with the best answer is having breakfast or dinner or asleep or at work or spending time with family and children.

Please see the ATI driver wiki in my signature for how to install it through the repo in the section about alternate command line method with hardware acceleration.
