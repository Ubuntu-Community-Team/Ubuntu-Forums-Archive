### Post by mlentink on 2009-09-04
Postfix is a MTA (Mail Transfer Agent). You might say it's a SMTP mailserver with which you can receive and send mail for one or several domains. To actually deliver mail to local mailboxes you would need to complement Postfix with a MDA (Mail Delivery Agent) such as Courier

You might want to google 'smtp'
