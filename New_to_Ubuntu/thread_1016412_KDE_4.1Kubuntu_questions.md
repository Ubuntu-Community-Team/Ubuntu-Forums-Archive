### Post by Chew N Tobacca on 2008-12-20
I, too, am new to Kubuntu and KDE. After doing some research, it seems that Firefox doesn't give much for customization with QT. Opera is much more configurable. I found some good information and other links here via Wikipedia:

[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_(toolkit)]("http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Qt_(toolkit)")

The widgets being locked, I am unsure. In my trials, everything moved freely by simply grabbing the toolbar beside the window. Sorry not much help there. You may consider looking at SuperKaramba here:

[http://netdragon.sourceforge.net/]("http://netdragon.sourceforge.net/")

You should be able to configure your own custom keyboard shortcuts by going to K-Menu->System Settings->Regional & Accessibility. There should be a submenu titled Keyboard shortcuts.

I hope you find this information helpful.
