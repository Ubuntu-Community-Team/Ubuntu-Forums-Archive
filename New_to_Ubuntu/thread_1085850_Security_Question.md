### Post by cwsnyder on 2009-03-03
If you are going to share files with a Windows computer or partition, you should probably scan for viruses with ClamAV.  You won't get bit by the viruses or trojans, but you could pass them on if you do not scan.

I also use Firestarter if I am not behind a router firewall.
