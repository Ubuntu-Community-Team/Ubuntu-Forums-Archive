### Post by Ghost|BTFH on 2011-04-27
Generally speaking, that's usually a sign of video driver issues.  I'm going to take a stab in the dark and say you have an nVidia card in the computer, and it's a slightly older one.

If this is the case, you may wish to attempt the following:

At grub menu, press **e** (This tells grub you'd like to **e**dit the grub startup)

Using arrow keys, move the cursor to **quiet splash**, delete and type in **nomodeset**.  Then use CTRL+X to boot with those options.

You should see a normal boot up screen.  After that, just install the nVidia drivers that area available and you should be good to go.

Cheers,
Ghost|BTFH
