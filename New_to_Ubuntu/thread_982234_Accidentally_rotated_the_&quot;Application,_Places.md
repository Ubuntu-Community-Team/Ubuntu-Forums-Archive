### Post by blackened on 2008-11-14
Right-click on the menu, select "Remove from Panel". Then right-click in an open spot on the panel, select "Add to Panel" from the menu, then in the dialog that appears, scroll down and double-click "Menu Bar". 

That should bring it back onto the panel, then you just have to move it where you want it, then right-click and "Lock To Panel" if you feel so inclined.
