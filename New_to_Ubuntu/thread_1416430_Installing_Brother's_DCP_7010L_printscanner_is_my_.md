### Post by ajgreeny on 2010-02-26
Try using synaptic.  Even if you didn't install it that way it may still be shown there and removable.  Search for the word "brother" in synaptic and you will find packages that are installed, I think, and should allow you to try again.

When installing a brother mfc5860 here, I used the printer driver from the ubuntu repos, but had to get the scanner driver direct from brother.  You may need to do the same.
