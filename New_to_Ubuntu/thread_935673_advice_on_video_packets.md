### Post by doctorbighands on 2008-10-01
With this ([http://www.zamzar.com/conversionTypes.php](http://www.zamzar.com/conversionTypes.php)) you can convert from .ppt (PowerPoint) or .odp (OO Impress)to .swf, which is a flash video file. You could use that instead of .mpeg, or search for something to convert from .swf to .mpeg - I'm sure there are plenty of apps available to do that job!
