### Post by spiderbatdad on 2008-12-08
the quality is best by setting video and audio to 50%. There are much better ways to get videos from youtube. Like:
```
sudo apt-get install youtube-dl
youtube-dl -o /home/username/filename.flv http://www.youtube.url
```where username/filename are set by you to your user name and what you wish to call the file...[http://www....etc](http://www....etc) can be pasted into the command line.
Firefox may also have some extensions for this purpose...any of the above options would provide much better audio/video quality than you can get from recordmydesktop.
