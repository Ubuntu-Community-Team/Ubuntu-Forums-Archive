### Post by nnadeau on 2008-12-19
I don't know if this is related to your issue, but I've noticed that Ubuntu detects native screen resolution during its boot process.  If my monitor isn't on during boot, the screen resolution will default to a 1280x1024 resolution.  The native resolution of my monitor is 1680x1050.

Again, don't know if this is related to your problem, but it's worth a try.
