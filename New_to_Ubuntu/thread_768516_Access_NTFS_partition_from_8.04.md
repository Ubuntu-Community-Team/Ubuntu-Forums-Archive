Go to "places" and then open the home folder.

On the left there should be a drive that just says "xxxgb media".

Double click it, enter your password and you'll have access to the windows partition and everything on it.

(it could be that the drive is accessible from the desktop icons, but I turn those off because I don't like anything on my desktop, so I'm not 100% sure about that)
