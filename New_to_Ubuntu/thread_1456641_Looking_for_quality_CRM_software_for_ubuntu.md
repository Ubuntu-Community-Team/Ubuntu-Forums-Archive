### Post by HermanAB on 2010-04-17
Give Evolution a try.  It has Calendars, Contacts, Tasks, Memos and so on.  Tasks can raise reminders for example and you can sort/classify them according to sales leads, birthdays, yadda yadda - a very long laundry list is available.  It is what I use at the moment - it works.

There is also KDE Kontact, which does the same thing, but Kontact was always buggy and crashed on me when I tried it in the past.
