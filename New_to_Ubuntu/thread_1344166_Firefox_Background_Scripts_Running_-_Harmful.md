### Post by Locke_99GS on 2009-12-02
It's letting you know that an advertiser's script on that site isn't functioning correctly. It won't affect you.

Installing "Adblock Plus" Firefox addon will prevent ads, and that particular ad-bound script, from bothering you. [https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1865](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1865).
