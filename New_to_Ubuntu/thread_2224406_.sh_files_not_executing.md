### Post by monkeybrain20122 on 2014-05-16
To enable click to execute, open Nautilus, go to Preferences > Behaviour and for executable text files choose "ask what to do", then when you click an executable text file a popup will appear asking you whether to run or to display it. The default 'display' is nonsensical, it seems to be a typically gnome bone headed thing. On the other hand click to run is kind of dangerous, I think "always ask" is a good default.
