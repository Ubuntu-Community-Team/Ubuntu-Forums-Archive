### Post by compiz addict on 2010-04-27
If you want you can hide all the desktop icons. Not sure if this works in Xubuntu, but press Alt+F2 and type gconf-editor, press enter, and goto apps > nautilus > prefrences. now look for "show_desktop" and set it to false. This will disable cube transparency and deformation, and it will prevent changing of the background.

### Post by 4Orbs on 2010-04-27
