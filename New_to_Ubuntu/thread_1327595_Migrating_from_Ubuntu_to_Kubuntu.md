### Post by Schendje on 2009-11-15
Almost every setting is in your Home folder. Got here, press Ctrl+H and it'll show the hidden folders (the ones with dots in front of the names).

I think that if you copy the ".mozilla" folder, all your Firefox and Thunderbird settings will be saved. Not sure about Thunderbird though. I'd try it out first before deleting the old Wubi installation. :)
