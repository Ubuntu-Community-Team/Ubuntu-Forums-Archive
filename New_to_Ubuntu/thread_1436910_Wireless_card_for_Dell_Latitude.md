### Post by waynefoutz on 2010-03-23
If your Latitude has the built in model...

plug it in with a hardwire to your router. Open up synaptic and hit the reload button. Reboot. Then click on System>Administration>Hardware Drivers and you'll see one or two Wireless drivers available. Broadcom STA is the one you want. Activate it. Sometimes this turns the wi-fi on immediately, but most of the time you'll see it come to life after you reboot again.
