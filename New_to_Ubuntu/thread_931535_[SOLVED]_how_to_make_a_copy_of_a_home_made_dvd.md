### Post by thane1 on 2008-09-27
In my experience if you can put the dvd in the tray and get it to start playing, you can stop it, right click on the dvd drive icon on your desktop and choose the copy disc option.  Try to see if this works for you --- hopefully in your case.  Cheers.
