### Post by tamoneya on 2008-04-25
It seems to me like nothing is wrong?  You are able to boot all of your OSes.  UUID is a reference to your harddrives.  It specifies where the root of the OS is.  It is similar to saying (hd0,1). Just leave it the way it is unless you run into problems.
