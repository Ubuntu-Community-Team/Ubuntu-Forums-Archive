### Post by bybirius on 2010-10-10
I had the same problem. Nothing but a flashing cursor for 10 minutes. I have a Toshiba netbook and I updated to 10.04 and that's when the problem started. Try this, it worked for me. Enter the bios (f2) go to Advance tab and change the Sata dirve to compatibility, then reboot. My boots went from 10-15 minutes to 30 seconds. Good luck!
