### Post by donkyhotay on 2014-06-20
First, there are no stupid questions. Everyone starts off as a beginner, even the most "l33t h4x0r" were all noobs once. That being said, welcome! To help maybe you can clarify some things, are you able to get the video from your atari onto your monitor yet and just need to record? if so I would recommend recordmydesktop as suggested by kansasnoob. Or are you at the point where you can't see anything your atari is displaying? If it's the 2nd one I would start with:[http://www.hauppauge.com/site/support/linux.html](http://www.hauppauge.com/site/support/linux.html) which has official information from your TV tuners manufacturer on their devices on linux. As always google is your friend doing a search for "using hauppauge on ubuntu" brought up a number of sites that looked promising. I've never used a TV tuner on linux before so I don't have much more advice then that.
