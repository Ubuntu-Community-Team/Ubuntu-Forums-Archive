### Post by zipperback on 2009-12-31
1) Launch Gimp.

2) Open the image you want to edit.

3) Select "Colors" from the menu bar.

4) Select "Brightness-Contrast" from the available options.

5) Now slide the brightness and/or contrast slider control to the lighten or darken your image as needed.

6) Click "OK".

7) Now save the image.

You're all done.

I hope this helps you.
