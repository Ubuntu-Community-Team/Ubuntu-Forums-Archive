### Post by pqwoerituytrueiwoq on 2012-02-12
reset zoom:
[ctrl]+[0] (Zero)
Zoom in:
[ctrl]+[+] (or mouse wheel up)
Zoom out:
[ctrl]+[-] (or mouse wheel down)

there is a setting i forget where but is affects the way zoom works with text
it is called [FONT=Courier New]browser.zoom.full[/FONT] in about:config
