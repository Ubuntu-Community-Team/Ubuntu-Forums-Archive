### Post by Sand Lee on 2008-11-01
You don't really "use" Pidgin with FUSA, it just allows you to manage your status. As it should, the status changer becomes available once you have Pidgin running. In a standard installation of Hardy, Pidgin never started automatically. If you want it to however, you'll have to add "pidgin" to the gnome Session[s] manager.
