### Post by etnlIcarus on 2008-08-24
VLC Player is my rock. It's solid, whether you're running it on *nix, OSX or Windows. Far better than the default included movie player (totem gstreamer), which tends to be pretty picky.

If you're specifically having trouble with WMVs, while there's no sure-fire fix, I have found that MPlayer handles them slightly better (though you'll have to accept that some WMVs are going to be unplayable).
