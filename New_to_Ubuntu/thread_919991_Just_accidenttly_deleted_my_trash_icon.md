### Post by Parksy on 2008-09-14
Are you referring to the trash can in your panel?  Here's how to add it back:
[LIST=1]
[*]Right-click an empty part of the panel.
[*]Click "Add to Panel."
[*]Scroll down to "Trash."
[*]Click "Add."
[/LIST]

It will be added to the panel in the location where you clicked.
