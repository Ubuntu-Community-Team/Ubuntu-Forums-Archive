you didn't say what graphics card you have. but since computer seems new i will assume  your GPU supports more than one monitor. If so then all you need to watch video on TV is a propper cable. Not sure what kind of TV you have here. Most TV these days have HDMI input (these are ideal as sound and video goes through them), some older one might have DVI or S-Video. Even older ones will have SCART or whatever (depends on the region standards). 
 
Then you just play the movie on your computer and the picture is then sent to the TV. I do it like that on my WinXp Home computer. Not need to buy extra gear for that.
