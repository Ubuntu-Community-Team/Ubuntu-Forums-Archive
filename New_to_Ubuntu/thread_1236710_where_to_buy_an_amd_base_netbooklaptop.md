### Post by walt.smith1960 on 2009-08-10
I was running 2 Think Pads with Jaunty. R51 until the power supply or system board died. Running an R61i right now WiFi & everything works great. There are a few different R61 subvariants--I got one that had W2K/XP drivers available. My reasoning is that if the hardware has W2K support available, the hardware has been around for a while and will likely have good Ubuntu support. That thinking worked in 2 cases at least.
