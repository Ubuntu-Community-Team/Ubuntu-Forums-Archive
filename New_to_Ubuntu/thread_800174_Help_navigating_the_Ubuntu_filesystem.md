### Post by greyfox1 on 2008-05-19
Hello,

Typically, programs will be installed in your home folder (/home/username/). The related folders are normally hidden, so you will have to show hidden files & folders (ctrl+h will do this, I forget where in the Nautilus menu this can be found and I'm stuck at work ATM).  Anyway just open your home folder, show hidden folders, and look for the appropriate folders-- they will typically be titled ".ProgramName".  

I'm not sure about the temp folder though.  Hope this helps!
