### Post by bobthebaritone on 2014-12-12
Hi Biggie_Bamaataa,

[https://helpp.ubuntu.com/community/BluetoothSetup](https://helpp.ubuntu.com/community/BluetoothSetup) provides an excellent starting point to troubleshooting.

I would not recommend starting to troubleshoot at the BUS level ( software messaging layer). Start at the level of ensuring your Bluetooth can function from the command line.

note that you will get much better performance than bluetooth via ad-hoc networking to transfer files. See [http://howtoubuntu.org/how-to-create-a-wireless-ad-hoc-network-in-ubuntu](http://howtoubuntu.org/how-to-create-a-wireless-ad-hoc-network-in-ubuntu)
