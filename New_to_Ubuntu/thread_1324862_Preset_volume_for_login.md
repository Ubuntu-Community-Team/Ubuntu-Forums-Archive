### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2009-11-13
I don't know how to mute the sound on startup, but you could always just disable the login sound.  In Jaunty, it's in System > Preferences > Startup Applications.  Look for 'Gnome Login Sound'.

You may also be able to disable it by right-clicking on your volume control, clicking "Open Volume Control", and going to the "Sound Theme" tab.
