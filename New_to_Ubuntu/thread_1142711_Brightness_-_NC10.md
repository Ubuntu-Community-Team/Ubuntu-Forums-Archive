### Post by gumblina on 2009-04-29
I've got dual boot on my netbook, so when I got to the menu to choose whether to go on xp or ubuntu, I used the fn keys to put the brightness up as high as it would go. When it started up, it stayed brighter :)

hope that helps, its a totally non-technical answer!
