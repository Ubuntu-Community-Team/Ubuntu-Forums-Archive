### Post by Locke_99GS on 2008-10-01
I can't think of any big buttoned players - not sure if any exist - but I can offer a couple of other possible solutions.

If you have Bluetooth on your PC, you can install a Java app on your phone called Remuco that lets you control Rhythmbox (amongst others) from your phone, like a Bluetooth remote, but instead of only play-stop-etc.. you get a playlist.

If you have a laptop, you could setup MPD on your desktop, and a remote MPD client on a laptop to have nearer to you.
