### Post by avtolle on 2009-01-10
If any of your boxes are running 7.04, support for it ended in October, 2008 and the repositories have been taken down. Support for 7.10 will end in April, 2009, so it would behoove you to consider upgrading to at least 8.04, as it is an LTS.
