Welcome to the Ubuntu forums!

Are you getting any error messages or is the install just quitting? Is it quitting before the partitioner is loaded, or after it loads?

Have a read through this guide on different boot options you can use. Pay particular attention to the F4 and F6 options.

[https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BootOptions](https://help.ubuntu.com/community/BootOptions)

It may be that you need to use one of these to get the installation to complete. If it turns out to be one or more of the F6 options that works, make a note of the ones you needed, they could come in handy later.

If none of those work, you could also download and burn a copy of the Alternate installer, which can be found here:

[http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors#alternate](http://www.ubuntu.com/getubuntu/downloadmirrors#alternate)

The Alternate installer is a text-based install, that often works well on computers that are having problems with the regular Live CD.

I hope this helps...

If it doesn't, come on back and someone will try and help you through it.
