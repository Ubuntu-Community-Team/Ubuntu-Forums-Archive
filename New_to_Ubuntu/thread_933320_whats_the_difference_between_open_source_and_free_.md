### Post by hyper_ch on 2008-09-29
Open Source --> You are provided with the source code or you can ask for it... however you may have restrictions on how you can use that source.

Free --> You have (almost) no restrictions on what you can do with the software/source... you can manipulate it, you can give it to others, ....
