### Post by tinker312 on 2008-08-11
Hi Strat, 

Seems the DI-624 has issues with linux.  I've found [http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=641466](http://ubuntuforums.org/showthread.php?t=641466) posted back in 2007 about this issue and a more technical webpage explaining the problem in depth.  

[http://paulsiu.wordpress.com/2006/12/28/unable-to-connect-linux-computer-to-dlink-di-624-router-due-to-ipv6/](http://paulsiu.wordpress.com/2006/12/28/unable-to-connect-linux-computer-to-dlink-di-624-router-due-to-ipv6/)

The gist of it is to disable ipv6 in the browser your using. 

Good Luck, 
Tinker
