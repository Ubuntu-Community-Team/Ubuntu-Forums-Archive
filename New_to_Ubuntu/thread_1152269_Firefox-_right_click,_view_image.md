### Post by presence1960 on 2009-05-08
> **suitedaces said:**
> It's also the same now if I click a direct link to an image, either in firefox or within emesene even.

In Firefox go to Edit>Preferences>Applications. Check your image file types and make sure **save **is not selected under action. I believe you may have save selected for image file types such as jpeg etc. Hope this works for you.
