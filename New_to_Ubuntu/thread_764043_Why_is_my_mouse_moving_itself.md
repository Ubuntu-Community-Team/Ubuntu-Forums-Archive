### Post by Sylverius on 2010-02-02
I have the same problem on both my laptop and desktop.  The mouse will center on the active window whenever I alt-tab or use ctrl-w or -q to quit the current active window.  

This only seemed to happen after installing compiz config settings manager.  After reading the above replies I disabled the "Enhanced Zoom Desktop" feature in Compiz, which fixed the problem.  

It's unfortunate because I really liked super-mousewheel to zoom into stuff on my desktop.  I thought it was a great feature, but I'd rather live without zoom than have my mouse try and out-think me.  I personally despise any kind of auto mouse movement.

I hope this helps someone!

Jim
