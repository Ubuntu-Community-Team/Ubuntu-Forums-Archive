### Post by jvcastle on 2014-09-04
Had the same problem - this was mentioned in another thread.  And it seems to be a DISPLAY problem rather than a MOUSE problem...  The experts suggested installing the proprietary NVIDIA drivers to fix it.  HOWEVER, on my machine it also has INTEL 4000 graphics which is used for both my displays - the laptop display and and external one connected to the minidisplay port. - the NVIDIA card is connected to the HDMI port with nothing connected there.  In any case, going to settings - Display, there seems to be a phantom THIRD display active - clicking on that and turning it off/disabling it and rebooting fixed the problem.
