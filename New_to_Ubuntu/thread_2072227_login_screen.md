### Post by grahammechanical on 2012-10-17
Edubuntu is based on Ubuntu. So, you do not need the full Edubuntu download.

If you open the Ubuntu Software Centre and search for Edubuntu or look in the Education section you will find the Edubuntu Desktop. It may also be call Educational Desktop for Ubuntu. That is 776MB download.

There are many components to this package that you can deselect and that will reduce the download size. Many of those components are already installed. You may not want the games or the educational software. So, you can reduce the download size a lot.

After the packages have been installed log out and before you log back in click the Ubuntu icon on the password panel and you can select the Edubuntu desktop from a menu.
