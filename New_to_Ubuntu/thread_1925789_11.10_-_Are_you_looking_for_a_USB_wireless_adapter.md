### Post by anewguy on 2012-02-15
If you're looking for a USB wireless adapter that does n/g/b and works out of the box (no hassles, no driver loading, etc. - just plug it in) you may want to try the Tenda model W322UA.  My old USB adapter (also worked out of the box - Trendnet TEW-424UB) got, well, more than a little bent by my cat rubbing on it today.  So I went to our local MicroCenter and found this one - it's 300mbps as well instead of 150mbps of their cheaper USB model.  It uses RTL2800 and just "works".  It was $39.99 at our local MicroCenter.

Thought I'd pass this along if there are people out there needing to buy a USB wireless adapter but not sure which one to get for Ubuntu 11.10.
