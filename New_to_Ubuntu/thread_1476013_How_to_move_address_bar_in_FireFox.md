### Post by lovinglinux on 2010-05-07
What I believe you want to do is to move the address bar from the main bar to the status bar. You can't. 

Items of the status bar are separate entities and you can't move them to/from other bars. Perhaps it could be possible to write an extension to overlay the address bar on a bottom position. I have been thinking about this myself. Nevertheless, I would need to research a little. Since I'm currently very busy with other extensions development, this won't happen soon. Nevertheless, I will post here if I find a solution or develop an extension to do that.

You might find the extensions below useful. I'm not the developer, but I use some of them:

[TotalToolbar](http://totaltoolbar.mozdev.org/)
[Organize Status Bar](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1759)
[TinyMenu](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/1455)
[Hide GUI bars](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/57805)
