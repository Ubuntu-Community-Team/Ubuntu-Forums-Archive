next click the unmaximize button.

close firefox.

reopen firefox.

maximize firefox.

done...should be fixed for good.

### Post by Therion on 2008-12-31
Just another fix I thought I'd pass along.  This is what worked for me and fixed that incredibly annoying problem permanently as well:

Open the Compiz Config Settings Manager (CCSM) under System/Preferences.
Go to the "Utility" section of CCSM and open the "Workarounds" plugin.
UN-check the box for **Legacy Fullscreen Support**.

Problem solved.
