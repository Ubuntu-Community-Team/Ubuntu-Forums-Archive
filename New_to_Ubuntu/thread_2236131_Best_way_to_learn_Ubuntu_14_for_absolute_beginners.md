### Post by ian-weisser on 2014-07-24
Unity in Ubuntu 14.04 is designed to be discovered more than taught.
Play with it. You will figure most of it out.

It is _not_ designed to behave similarly to a Mac or Windows. It is different. Be prepared to unlearn a few habits as you explore.

### Post by Impavidus on 2014-07-25
The best way (in my opinion): Find somebody who already knows Linux. Sit next to him/her and ask any questions you have. Once you know the basics you can explore further on your own. And be inquisitive, but not reckless, if you don't want to be a newbie any more a few years from now.

### Post by bigmonmulgrew on 2014-07-25
Little tip I did which lessens the learning curve. Any programs you use regularly in windows check to see if they have a version that will run on ubuntu and install them. You won't have to learn new programs AND new OS.

I'd also avoid WINE since you sound like your here to learn new things and WINE is cheating. :P
