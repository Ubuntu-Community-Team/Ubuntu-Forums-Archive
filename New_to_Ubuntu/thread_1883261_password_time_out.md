### Post by Paddy Landau on 2011-11-18
What do you mean? Do you mean after the screensaver has come on, how long after you move the mouse or press the keyboard before the password prompt appears?

If that's what you mean, it depends on a few things.
[LIST]
[*]How fast is your hardware?
[*]Did your hard drive power down?
[*]What else is running in the background?
[*]Did your screen power down?
[*]Did the computer go to sleep?
[/LIST]

You don't have to wait for the prompt before you start typing the password. Just start typing the password and press Enter. If you're quick enough, you won't even see the prompt before you get back in.
