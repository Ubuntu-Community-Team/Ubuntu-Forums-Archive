### Post by Paddy Landau on 2010-07-04
HP has a good reputation for supporting Linux.

AFAIK, any modern HP printer will work.

You can contact HP's support to ask, should you have any concerns.
[http://www.hp.com/](http://www.hp.com/)

I use the Officejet Pro L7780 (a network printer), which has been fantastic. It's inkjet, not laser; but when set to high quality it's still quick and quiet, sufficient quality that it's hard to spot that it's not a laser, and of course cheaper than a laser.
