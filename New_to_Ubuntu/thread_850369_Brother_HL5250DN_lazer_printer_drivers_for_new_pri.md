### Post by avtolle on 2008-07-05
Good luck to you. From a "historical note", the additions to Synaptic of the Brother drivers occurred shortly after 8.04 was released, and not knowing the same were there, I installed a MFC-240C using the drivers, etc., from the Brother site. I have since installed the same printer on the same computer under LinuxMint5, using the correct driver from the repositories, and prints just fine. For anyone reading, if you have a MFC, you will still need to d/l the scanner and fax drivers from the Brother site.
