### Post by mkvnmtr on 2010-08-10
Be aware that everything you need in the way of software can be downloaded from the Ubuntu repositories through software center, Synaptic Package Manager or the terminal. It is not to your benefit to search the web for software. The restricted extras mentioned above is a good first step. That should give you a system that will do most of what you need. Then for specific apps you can ask for recomendations in the forum and find them in the repositories.
