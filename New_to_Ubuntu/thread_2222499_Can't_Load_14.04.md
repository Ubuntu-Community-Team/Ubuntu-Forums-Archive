### Post by jbaerboc on 2014-05-06
So you burned the image to a DvD and then rebooted with the DvD in there and it just ignored it and booted your normal system? You might now have your computer set up to boot from the DvD drive before the hardrive maybe? I know when I got a new motherboard and had to reinstall everything the Bios in the mboard was not setup to check the DvD drive before booting to the OS on the hard drive.

If everything is fine on that end it's possible the DvD didn't burn correctly and you might have to try burning another one.
