In Mac OS X, when you activate the switcher (by keyboard shortcut command-tab), you can cycle through the applications by continuing to press tab; or you can continue to hold down command and use the left and right arrow keys to choose a different app; or you can continue to hold down command and then hover the mouse over one of the switcher's app icons to directly go to that app.

Concept of workspaces: if you're the kind of person that likes to have one fullscreen app per workspace-do that, then press Super+s and select the right workspace.
