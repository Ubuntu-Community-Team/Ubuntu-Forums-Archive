### Post by coffeecat on 2010-12-03
There is no proprietary Intel driver so the Hardware Drivers utility won't be of help.

What is it about the colour display that is wrong? When you go to System > Preferences > Monitors, does it give you the correct resolution? Are there any settings on the monitor itself that will help? Also - check the VGA/DVI cable. I once had a faulty cable - in fact it was one of the VGA connectors - that caused one of the three primary colours to fail.
