### Post by chrisod on 2009-02-06
To answer my own question...it turns out that the .dat files on a VCD are really just mpeg files. If you rip them and save them as .mpg they work just fine in any video player. I did notice that the image resolution was sharper in VLC than it is in Totem. You do lose the menu this way, but that was not a big deal for me.
