### Post by BIGtrouble77 on 2009-05-10
Gnome panel does have issues with shifting content.  The problem is mostly a result of some gnome panel applets that dynamically take up extra space.  When you have everything locked the panel does it's best to move everything around.  Sometimes things go awry.

Simply put, gnome panel is terrible; it's had very little improvement in the past 10 years.  It desperately needs a rewrite, but the developers seem more interested in other projects.  I love gnome (nautilus is the best file manager ever), but the panel is simply terrible.
