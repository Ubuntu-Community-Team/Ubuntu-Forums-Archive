As **Google Is My Friend**, I thought I'd do a search for ["AWLH4130 chipset"]("http://www.google.co.uk/search?hl=en&source=hp&q=awlh4130+chipset&btnG=Google+Search&meta=").

Most of the results said "Chipset: Atheros" (like [this one]("http://www.jaykang.com/aiawpcisu10w.html"), and [this one]("http://www.airlink101.com/products/awlh4130.php"))... but then I clicked on the result at [madwifi-project.org]("http://madwifi-project.org/wiki/Compatibility/AirLink?action=edit") and it said:
