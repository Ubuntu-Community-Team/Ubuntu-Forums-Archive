### Post by mcduck on 2009-10-11
Have you restarted after that? Ubuntu detects available displays & resolutions at boot time, so the resolutions you have available will change depending on what displays you have connected at the time.

(Of course it should also be mentioned that apart from HDMI, display connectors are not made to be plugged/unplugged when the system is running. In worst cases doing so might actually cause damage to the graphics card and/or the display..)
