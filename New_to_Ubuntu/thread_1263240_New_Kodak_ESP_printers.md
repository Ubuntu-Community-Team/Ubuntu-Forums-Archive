### Post by cthulhucookie on 2009-09-10
I don't know much about Ubuntu's support for Kodak printers, but after owning this Kodak ESP-3 for a while I could care less. I do not recommend this printer to anyone. The ink is of poor quality and it drains quickly. Also, I just replaced the black ink cartridge today and it's not printing any black ink! I'm just getting blank pages!! The scanner wont work at all half of the time. And if the paper jams, its hard to open up the printer to get the paper out.
