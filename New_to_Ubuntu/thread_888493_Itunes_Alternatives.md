> **Lorelei- said:**

Files obtained through the iTunes Music Store will not play outside iTunes, due to the DRM encryption. If you don't like this situation (I don't), then e-mail Apple and complain.

The only exception is files downloaded as "iTunes Plus", as these don't have DRM.

There are ways to unencrypt iTMS audio files, but I can't assist you in finding the programs for this - forum rules.
