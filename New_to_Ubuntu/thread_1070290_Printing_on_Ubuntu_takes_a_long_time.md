### Post by 4Bhere on 2009-05-26
Try the Xerox DocuPrint P1202 Foomatic/hpijs (recommended) driver from the Xerox printer drivers list.

Administration
Printing
Discover Printers

It wont find the driver but you can then select from available drivers "Xerox" and use the setting above.  It works fine on my 204A;);)
