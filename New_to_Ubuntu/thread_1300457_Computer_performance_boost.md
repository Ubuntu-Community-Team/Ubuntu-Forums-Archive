### Post by Dark_Stang on 2009-10-25
You'll have a decent boost from the memory upgrade. Unfortunately the video card isn't going to do too much in a PCI slot. But yes, those two parts will work in that machine. My only concern would be the power supply. 250 watts isn't much so that may have to be upgraded with the video card.

It isn't going to be a gaming machine. If that's what you're looking for I'd be looking at building or buying a new PC rather than wasting the money right now.
