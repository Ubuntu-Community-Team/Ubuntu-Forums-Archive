### Post by Teegoat on 2009-01-08
Hello everybody,
A few days ago I installed Avant Window Navigator, didn't like it much and uninstalled but now every time I minimize a window it disappears instead of going to the bottom panel.
What's wrong and how do I fix it?
I assume my Ubuntu is still thinking it's got AVN and just sending windows somewhere...

---

### Post by Sand &amp; Mercury on 2009-01-08
You can make sure AWN is really not there anymore by pressing ALT-F2 and typing: 

killall avant-window-navigator

Also, you might not have window list on your panel anymore. Right click the panel, go to 'Add To Panel' and drag 'Window List' onto it somewhere; now try again and see if they're minimizing properly.
