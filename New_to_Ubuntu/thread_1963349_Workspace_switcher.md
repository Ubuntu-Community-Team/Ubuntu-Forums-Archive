Several options to make more or less boxes etc. do a search and study up, you might like it.

### Post by na5h on 2012-04-24
It's like having multiple monitors, only...not! Seriously though, if you are running several applications at the same time, it's a lot easier to have them spread out over multiple workspaces, rather than having to maximize/minimize them on the same workspace all the time!

It's also great when your girlfriend wants to "just quickly look at something". Just move across to a new workspace, let the other person do what the want, and move back. No minimising, no moving stuff around. You can pick up exactly where you left off.
