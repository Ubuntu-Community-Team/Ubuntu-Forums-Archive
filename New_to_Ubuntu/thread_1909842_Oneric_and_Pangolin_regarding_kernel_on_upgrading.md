### Post by Paqman on 2012-01-16
If you upgrade from Oneiric Ocelot to Precise Pangolin you'll receive a new kernel, and new packages, but they will be for the same architecture (so 32-bit Oneiric will upgrade t 32-bit Precise)

If you want to switch from 32-bit to 64-bit you'll have to do a reinstall instead of an upgrade. That's not necessarily a hassle. You can back up all your files and settings and the system can reinstall all your programs with one command.
