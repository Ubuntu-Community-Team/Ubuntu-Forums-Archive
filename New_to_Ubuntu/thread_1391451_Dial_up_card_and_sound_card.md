### Post by lkraemer on 2010-01-27
If you were to insert an Audio CDR  (Music) in the CD Drive, and wanted
to play the music, your system would need a way to get the sound routed
to the Sound Card.  Therefore the need for the two-wire audio cable that
connects the CD Drive to the Sound Card.  

I haven't a clue as to what the White Cable you are trying to describe could be.
How many conductors are in the cable.  What type plugs on the
ends of the cable.  Take a photo, convert to a png and post it!

Sounds as if you have a Multi-Function card, that could have a Modem,
Sound Card, and maybe other features all onboard.

If you don't need sound, Modem, and whatever else is on the card you can
remove it and continue.  But, I'd think you would lose a lot of functionality.
Maybe you need to seek more help before continuing!
