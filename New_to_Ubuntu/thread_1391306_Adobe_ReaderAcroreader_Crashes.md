### Post by Greenwidth on 2010-01-26
I don't have much experience using adobe reader, you could try starting it (or Firefox) from the terminal and seeing what / if any errors are reported when it crashes.

Or, if you're not already using it, there's a new version of reader (9.3 - Jan/2010) which might be worth installing as:
"Critical  vulnerabilities have been identified in Adobe Reader 9.2 and Acrobat 9.2 for Windows, Macintosh and UNIX, and Adobe Reader 8.1.7 and Acrobat 8.1.7 for Windows and Macintosh.
