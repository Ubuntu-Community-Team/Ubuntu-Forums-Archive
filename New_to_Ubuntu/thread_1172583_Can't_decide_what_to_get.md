### Post by freeman2000 on 2009-05-28
SEMPER FI  !!!

Here's my 2 cents worth:

Stay away from Dell... unless it's comes pre-loaded with a Linux distro and has no MS crapware.  My top-of-the-line Dell laptop lasted 13 months before it fried the motherboard - $2500 down the drain!  Dell's stance:  too bad the warranty's only 12 months!
