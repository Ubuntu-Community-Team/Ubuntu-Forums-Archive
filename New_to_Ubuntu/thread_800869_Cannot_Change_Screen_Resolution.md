### Post by avtolle on 2008-05-20
One other thought (why not, I appear to be on a *negative* roll here): right click Applications in the top tool bar, select Edit Menu when it opens. Scroll down to Other, then in the list that should open to the right select "Screens and Graphics", close. Then. click on Applications, scroll to Other, Screens and Graphics will appear to the right. Click on it to open; there you have two tabs, one for your screen, one for your graphics card.
