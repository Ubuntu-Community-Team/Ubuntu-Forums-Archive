### Post by mikewhatever on 2010-07-25
Netbooks these days are mostly Intel based Atom driven machines that should more or less work with Ubuntu. However, some of them have bad Linux support, namely the ones with Atom Z530/Z520 + gma500. Do yourself a favor and avoid these models.
