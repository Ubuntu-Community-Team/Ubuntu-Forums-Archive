### Post by nakama85 on 2008-11-21
After Rereading. I think what you are trying to ask is simply how to get it to install.

First you need to put the cd in the drive and then restart the computer.
Let the computer boot from disk. you will come across a prompt that gives you options like. Run without making changes to computer (live cd) and install.

Choose install or live cd it does not make a difference. However if you choose live cd you will have to click on the "install" icon that will appear on your desktop.
