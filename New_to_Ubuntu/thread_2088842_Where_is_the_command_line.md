### Post by Lars Noodén on 2012-11-27
You can press ctrl-alt-T to get a terminal in Ubuntu.  Also, in the dash, you can enter 'terminal'

If you want a console, then press ctrl-alt-f1.  To get back from the console to return to the graphics, press ctrl-alt-f7
