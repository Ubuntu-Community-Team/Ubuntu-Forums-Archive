### Post by Double.J on 2012-01-28
hi there!

Sounds like the compiz water effect to me... on mine the keys are CTRL+SUPER (super=windows key). The ripple is the first drop of "rain" that falls where your mouse pointer is. I think it's SHIFT+F9 to start the rain, and SHIFT+F8 to "wipe the screen" - again I may have changed my keys, but I don't think so :)

To make changes, open CCSM and go
effects>water(click on, not just tick) To change the water effects :)

Hope it helps!
