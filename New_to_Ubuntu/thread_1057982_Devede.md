### Post by stchman on 2009-02-02
> **Jynxx said:**
> Needing a little help please. I'm using devede to convert avi to dvd then burn it to disk but I keep getting this same error. Any ideas?

GstPlayBin: A subtitle stream was detected, but no video stream.

DeVeDe will convert your .avi to a DVD format with the AUDIO_TS and VIDEO_TS folders.

TO create the DVD use K3b to create a video DVD and drag 'n' drop those into the K3b manager.
