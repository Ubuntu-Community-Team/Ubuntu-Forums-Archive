### Post by boof1988 on 2008-10-31
I found out how to do it on [codeweavers.com](http://www.codeweavers.com/).

Use [Manage Bottles Tab](http://www.codeweavers.com/support/docs/crossover-pro/officesetup#managebottles) to change settings and "Publish" the bottles to other users on the system. {located about 2/3 way down the page}

Edit#1[COLOR="Blue"]Also... I realized I have Crossover Pro and I still need to register the serial number.  Because of that I don't have the "share" options in the "Manage Bottles" tab.  The version I downloaded must be a 'demo' version or something.  Hopefully the "share" option will appear after I register.[/COLOR]

Edit#2[COLOR="Blue"]I figured out that each license is for a single user.  So two people need two licenses.  Glad I didn't pay for the Crossover package.  Fortunately I registered for the free Crossover deal as well as the person I live with.  So we each have a copy to use.  Anyway...  I think I want to stop using MS stuff totally...  In time it'll happen.[/COLOR]

Maybe this'll help someone else.
