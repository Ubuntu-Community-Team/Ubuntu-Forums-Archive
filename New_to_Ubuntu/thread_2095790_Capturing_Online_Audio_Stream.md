### Post by Terry of Astoria on 2012-12-17
Some of those can be captured pretty easily using VLC. 
In VLC, click on "Media"-"Convert-Save"
Then you choose your source, which can be a stream URL.
... Good luck. Sometimes the popup player ones are hard to capture for me. Straight-up streams are what I've done w/VLC.
