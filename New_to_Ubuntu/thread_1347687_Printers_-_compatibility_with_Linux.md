### Post by ofb on 2009-12-06
It's worth emphasizing that a printer marked as compatible may not do everything. It'll print fine, but you don't always get the ink-level warning and head-cleaning function that Windows and Mac users get thanks to the factory driver.

So just make sure your compatible printer has those functions available on the printer's own display panel. Some of the cheaper ones omit it.
