### Post by Peripheral Visionary on 2012-07-29
I have the very same computer, same RAM, and **[COLOR=RoyalBlue]_[SIZE=3]X[/SIZE]_ubuntu[/COLOR]** runs wonderfully on it! Lubuntu is technically more "lightweight," but I experience no discernible difference in performance on my Dell Dimension between one and the other.

[COLOR=DarkOrange]**Ubuntu**[/COLOR] and [COLOR=Cyan]**Kubuntu**[/COLOR] are definitely too much for our low-spec machines, but [COLOR=Blue]**Xubu**[/COLOR] and [COLOR=DarkOrchid]**Lubu**[/COLOR] will run fine on them. You might also try the *ultralight* [Bodhi Linux]("http://www.bodhilinux.com/") (based on Ubuntu) for it's really fun, very pretty Enlightenment desktop.
