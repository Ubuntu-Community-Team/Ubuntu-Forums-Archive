### Post by grahammechanical on 2016-01-02
Skype is proprietary software. The icon we get is the icon we get. Unless you want to create your own icon and replace the Skype icon with it. It would need to have the same name.

The icon that is a rectangle with a red circle is what we get when the normal icon is missing or cannot be loaded for some reason. There are various system icons of different sizes in /usr/share/icons. The Skype icons may be in a Skype folder that may itself be a hidden folder.
