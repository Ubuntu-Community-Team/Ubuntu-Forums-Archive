### Post by Jive Turkey on 2010-05-07
I think you can make gedit do that, bluefish does it too.  Ya, I just checked.  In gedit under preferences > plugins, make sure file browser pane is checked, and then you can press f9 to make the side pane appear or disappear.  Note the two tabs at the bottom of the side pane.

### Post by kestal on 2010-05-07
Unless I am mistaken in what exactly you are looking for...

gedit would be able to do that. I use it for php/javascript, the syntax highlighting feels nice. Also, for folders, you can click on view on the top menu, click on side bar. Then at the bottom of the sidebar is a folder icon which lets you play around through that.

Gedit comes with Ubuntu. Look for it under Applications->Accessories->Gedit Text Editor

To set the language you want to highlight, once its opened... its under View -> Highlight Mode
