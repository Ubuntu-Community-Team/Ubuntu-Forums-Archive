### Post by Jon Monreal on 2010-04-17
There are two options really:

If you haven't clicked in the link yet, you can simply right-click on the link and select "Save Link As..." to download the file.

If you are already watching or listening, in Firefox go the the menu Tools>Page Info, then the "Media" tab (it's to the right of "General" near the top and has a small image associated with it).

Below, you will see a button that says "Save As...". Click that and you will be able to save the file.
