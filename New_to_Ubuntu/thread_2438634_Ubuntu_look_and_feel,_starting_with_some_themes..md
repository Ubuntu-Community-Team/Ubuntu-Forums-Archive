### Post by Impavidus on 2020-03-15
Have you read the [wiki article]("https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wayland_(display_server_protocol)") about Wayland? It's the software that makes it possible to have a GUI and is meant to be a replacement for X, which is the over 30 year old software for that task and severely outdated in several respects. But creating Wayland is a slow process.

Why run Ubuntu 14.something? It's no longer supported. GUI stuff changes quickly, so whatever you can do to tweak the appearance of 14.04 may not be relevant to 18.04 or later.
