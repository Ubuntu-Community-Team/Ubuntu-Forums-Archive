### Post by XCan on 2009-05-19
If you want to preserve the permissions/dates etc of your files, as well as ensure your hidden files are included, I think that the easiest thing for you to do is to create an archive of your home folder (tar or tar.gz) and just burn the tar onto your CD-R. To restore it you would then simply unpack the contents of the tar later on.
