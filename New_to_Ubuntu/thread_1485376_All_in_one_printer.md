### Post by dearingj on 2010-05-16
I have a Brother MFC-9840CDW. It works just fine with Ubuntu except that its scanning function only works when it's connected by USB (Windows computers can scan over a network). You can download the printer and scanner drivers from these pages on Brother's site: [printer]("http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/download_prn.html#MFC-9840CDW") and [scanner]("http://welcome.solutions.brother.com/bsc/public_s/id/linux/en/download_scn.html#brscan2").
