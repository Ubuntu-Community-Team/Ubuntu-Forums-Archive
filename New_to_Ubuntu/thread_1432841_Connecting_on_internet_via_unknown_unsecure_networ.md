### Post by lykwydchykyn on 2010-03-18
I suppose in theory a person could log all the packets going through the router, from which they could theoretically reconstruct things like GET or POST requests to a website.

Most people wouldn't know how to do this, or bother to do it if they did; and probably nobody could do it without an expensive or custom-built router.

You do realize, though, that it's illegal in some places to connect to a network without the owner's permission?
