### Post by techvish81 on 2012-07-23
most of the samsung galaxy series phones which are on__ android 2.3 __ have mass storage mode in the usb connections,  also sony erricson arc/arc s on 2.3  have the same feature in usb, so they work.
most tablets are 3.2/4.0.x and many of them have only MTP mode in usb connections, so thry won't work.
MTPFS is under development in linux and when it becomes a finished product,  we could expect a better support for all the android phones/tablets. 

i have used 3 galaxy phones and a sony phone, they all have connected readily,  but tablets i have used were not supported due to the MTP mode.
