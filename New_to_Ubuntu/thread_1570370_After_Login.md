### Post by Peter09 on 2010-09-08
This is most probably a video Driver problem, what is the model of your machine and do you know the type of graphics card you are using?
 
Try holding down the shift key during boot. This should get you to a grub menu where you can select the recovery mode (one option down). Boot to that and when you get to the menu try the reconfigure graphics option.
