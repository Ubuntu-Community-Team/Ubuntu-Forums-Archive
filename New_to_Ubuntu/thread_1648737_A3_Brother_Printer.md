### Post by walt.smith1960 on 2010-12-20
Oftentimes with Brother printers, you can change the paper size by right clicking the printer icon and selecting "properties", or when you get to the print page there are tabbed options.  There should be drop down lists of supported paper sizes, including "user" where you can enter sizes and margins. To change the default paper size and other settings, try (in Ubuntu/gnome) system->administration->printing->right click the brother printer and select "properties". I hope this makes sense to you.
