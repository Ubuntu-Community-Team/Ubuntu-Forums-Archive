You got absolute control over where sound goes and from where it comes.
For an instance if you plug in your headphones and have a film playing you can have the film playing with sound coming from your speaker and at same time you can play a game with sound coming form your headphones.

So you can direct sound from some program to some output you choose (if you use a program named PulseAudio Volume Control), but you can also direct every output sound from any program to only a single output source (speakers/headphones/line out). The second method is described below, but you can install that PulseAudio Volume Control if you want to try that and have more control.
[B]
With that said turn on some song and lets try this out.
Click on that sound icon on top panel and choose *Sound Preferences* and go to *Output*. There click some of the output courses under *Choose a device for sound output*.[/B]
