### Post by munkyeetr on 2008-05-01
If they're in your Trash, juts go into the trash and copy or move them back out to where you want. I have not seen a *Restore* command that will automatically put them back where they were deleted from.

If you've *emptied* the Trash, I think your out of luck.

### Post by aeiah on 2008-05-01
you can probably use a recovery livecd to try and find your files, but you should stop using your computer right now of that is the case. the files are never deleted, they're just flagged as 'its ok to overwrite this data as and when its needed'. if you havent wrote much new data to the disk, a recovery tool may be able to retrieve your lost data. it may be more trouble than its worth though. it depends how important your data is.
