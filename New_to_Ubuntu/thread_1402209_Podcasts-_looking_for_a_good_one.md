### Post by ankspo71 on 2010-02-09
Hi,
If you install 'miro', that podcast subscription manager has several audio and video linux podcast channels. Even if you decide not to use miro as your podcast viewer or downloader, it still provides a good way to locate podcasts on the internet. It's available in the ubuntu repos by searching synaptic package manager or software center, but here is a homepage link so you can see what it is [http://www.getmiro.com/](http://www.getmiro.com/)

My favourite is [http://www.category5.tv/](http://www.category5.tv/) . Robbie uses Ubuntu as his main OS :D On that page you can watch live, or [http://www.category5.tv/rss_feeds.php](http://www.category5.tv/rss_feeds.php) for past episodes. My other favorites are [http://meetthegimp.org/](http://meetthegimp.org/) and "sneekylinux" found in miro.
Hope these ideas help.
