### Post by lovinglinux on 2011-10-13
Flash performance is not good. Most likely your CPU is struggling to deal with it.

Try [Flash-Aid](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flash-aid/). If that doesn't help, use [FlashVideoReplacer](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/flashvideoreplacer/). It will allow to watch YouTube HD at least.

Also check [http://www.webgapps.org/tutorials/firefox/optimization/flash-optimization](http://www.webgapps.org/tutorials/firefox/optimization/flash-optimization)
