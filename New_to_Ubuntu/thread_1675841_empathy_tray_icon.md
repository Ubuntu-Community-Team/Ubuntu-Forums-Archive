### Post by stoogiebuncho on 2011-01-26
A lot of apps are moving their tray icons from the Notification Area Applet to the Indicator Applet.

Do you have the indicator applet displayed on one of your panels?

I don't use empathy much, but I seem to remember in recent versions that in order to access it from the tray, you have to click on the icon that looks like an envelope.  So assuming you have the indicator applet on your panel, try left-clicking on the envelope and see what happens.

And if you think this seems counter-intuitive, I'm with you 100%.
