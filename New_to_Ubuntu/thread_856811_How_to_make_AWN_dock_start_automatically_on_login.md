### Post by OutOfReach on 2008-07-11
Goto System>Preferences>Sessions, then click the Add button, fill in the Name as AWN, and in the Command box, type "avant-window-navigator" (without the ""). Then click OK, reboot and it awn should pop-up after the login. (BTW I didn't test this out, im not at my ubuntu computer at the moment, but im sure this is the right way.)
