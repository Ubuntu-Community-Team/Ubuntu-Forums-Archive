### Post by amanchesterman on 2011-05-05
Like Oldfred, I have version 4.5.0 which installed easily with the 'Ubuntu software center' a couple of months ago. (I have Maverick.)

No problems with the font size, and my eyes aren't good: when I installed it it just seemed to pick up the font sizes I had already set for the Gnome desktop.

I too have noticed a couple of small hiccups when I downloaded transactions from my bank and they were already entered as scheduled transactions in MyMoney, but those were easily sorted out.

On the plus side, it updates share prices with no problem, and the range of inbuilt reports is more than adequate for my needs. I used Microsoft Money before I switched from Windows, and MyMoney does the job just as well for me.

(I tried out three or four Linux programs for managing personal finances and it seems to have the most features.)
