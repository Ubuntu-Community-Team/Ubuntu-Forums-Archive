### Post by cascade9 on 2010-04-30
> **den160593 said:**

'AMD64' is just one naming convention for 64bit x86 (the other common version is x86_64). AMD got the 1st x86 comptible 64bit CPU out, so they called it 'AMD64', as is their right. 

AMD64 will work fine on an i5 (or i3, i7, Core2Duo, Core2Qaud, and some of the Intel Atom, P4, P4D and celeron CPUs as well)
