### Post by sarimura on 2009-12-30
Right click on the other panel and select "New Panel"

Then right click on the newly created panel and select its properties.

You will again right click on this newly created panel and select "Add to Panel" option. This will bring up a new screen. Search for "Workspace Switcher" and add it to the panel. 

You are done with the first question.. :)

For the second one, the easiest way is to drag and drop the shortcuts from the "Applications" menu to the desktop. 

Have a nice day..
