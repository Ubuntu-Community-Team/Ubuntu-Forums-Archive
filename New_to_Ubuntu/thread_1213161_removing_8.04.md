### Post by bodhi.zazen on 2009-07-14
You can either upgrade 8.04 -> 8.10 -> 9.04

Or do a fresh install of 9.04. You can simply install over the top of 8.04 (back up your data first or it will be erased when you re-install).

If you are dual booting, 8.04 does not need to be removed, simply reformat the 8.04 partition.
