### Post by t0p on 2008-09-01
If you want Firefox to use the font that you choose:

1. Click through to **Edit > Preferences > Content**;

2. Click the **Advanced** button next to **Fonts & Colours**;

3. There's an option: "Allow pages to choose their own fonts, instead of my selections above" - make sure this box is *unchecked*.

### Post by philinux on 2008-09-01
If you are using an lcd monitor do this.

System>Prefs>appearance then Fonts tab.

Click LCD smoothing, and then click details. Click slight hinting.

Play with these settings to get the affect you need.
