### Post by Vladlenin5000 on 2015-03-29
Hello and welcome.

A quick Google search for the notebook's model - the search I shouldn't be doing because you should have provided at least the hardware specs from the start - revealed this:
> Sony's VGN-FS710/W Vaio FS series computer, also known as a PCG-7D2L, was an entry level notebook computer **released in 2005**. [my bold].

So...
Although it's possible you've been experiencing some OS-related (software) issue, the possibility of hardware failure in a **10 years old** notebook is HUGE! And even that same hardware in good condition DOESN'T support the current standard Ubuntu with Unity and 3D rendering. Only current Ubuntu spins with lightweight desktop have the chance of running acceptably in such old hardware.
