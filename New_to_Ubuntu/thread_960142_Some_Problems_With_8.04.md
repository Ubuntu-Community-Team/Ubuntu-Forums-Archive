### Post by eternalnewbee on 2008-10-27
You won't be able to use AWN, unless compiz is installed and activated, through: Main Menu > System > Administration > Hardware Drivers, and see if your card is enabled. If it isn't, enable it and reboot and if it is, don't reboot, but go to: Main Menu > System > Preferences > Appearances > Visual Effects and choose "Normal" or "Extra".
Good luck.
