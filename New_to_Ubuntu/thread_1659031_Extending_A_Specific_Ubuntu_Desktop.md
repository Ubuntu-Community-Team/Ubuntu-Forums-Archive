### Post by audiomick on 2011-01-03
If, with "Virtual Desktops" you mean the ones you can switch to with the desktop switcher on the right in the bottom task bar, no, I don't believe it is possible to have them appear on separate monitors.

As I understand it, the part of the OS that sends the picture to the monitor is "below" the part that shows you the desktop. The bit that drives the monitor doesn't know what it is sending out, just where it has to go to, and the bit that shows you the desktop doesn't know how many monitors it is going to, I think.
