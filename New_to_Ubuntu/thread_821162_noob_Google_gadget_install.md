### Post by fjf on 2008-06-07
Here are the debs: [http://ppa.launchpad.net/googlegadgets/ubuntu/pool/main/g/google-gadgets/](http://ppa.launchpad.net/googlegadgets/ubuntu/pool/main/g/google-gadgets/)

Get google-gadgets_0.9.1-0ubuntu0~ppa2_all.deb and google-gadgets_0.9.1-0ubuntu0~ppa5_i386.deb for 32 bits.

Once installed, type in a terminal: /usr/bin/ggl-gtk -s -bg and you'll get them in sidebar mode.
