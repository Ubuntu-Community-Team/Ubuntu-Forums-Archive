### Post by ajgreeny on 2020-07-21
Open **pulseaudio volume control** either from the terminal with command **pavucontrol** or from the volume icon in the panel and check the volume of your headphones there in the Output devices tab.
Headphones and speakers volume are set separately there so your headphones may be muted.
