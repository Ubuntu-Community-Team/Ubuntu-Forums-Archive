### Post by sirebral on 2008-12-28
Newb answer #1 is to re install it

DON'T Just reinstall it.

It's simple.

Hit F11, then Hit F11 again.  This will bring the Title bar back.  Then, grab the Title Bar and drag it so it breaks away from the corners.

This happens to me after I visit a web site with funky code or get slammed with crappy coded Pop Under.

### Post by fooman on 2008-12-28
press f11 to get go to fullscreen mode...then f11 once more to get to a normal screen.

if it comes back again next time you open firefox....


press f11 to go fullscreen and f11 again to get to a normal size screen.

next click the unmaximize button, then use the cursor to make that window slightly smaller.

close firefox.

reopen firefox.

maximize firefox.

done...should be fixed for good.
