### Post by WanderingAlbatross on 2011-10-23
If you are coming from windows the workspaces may mystify you.  Most work I like to do in a fully maximized window.  It's great to have four fully maximized windows.  Using ctrl-alt-arrow key will allow switching between the different workspaces.  It ends up being much faster than windows way of having to move things out of your way to get to other things.

Some also have pretty fancy transitions, or different wallpapers on each screen.
