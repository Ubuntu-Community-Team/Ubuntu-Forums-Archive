### Post by _0R10N on 2010-05-01
I don't think that a correct upgrade from an ISO is possible. Why? Well, because upgrading your system is a process that, among other things updates the packages of the applications you have installed. That's why you should upgrade your system using the online repositories and not a DVD/CD.

If you want to upgrade the way I suggested, I recommend you to update your installed packages, first.

```

sudo aptitude update
sudo aptitude upgrade
sudo aptitude dist-upgrade

```

Kind regards!
