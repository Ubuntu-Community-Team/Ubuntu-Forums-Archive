### Post by owiknowi on 2010-12-07
Part of your problem could be the newest hardware you have.
Drivers are often first (only) available for ms wx.

After you've installed Ubuntu connect to the Internet using a ethernet cable. This way Ubuntu can look for available drivers.

Look in the thread 'Hardware (in)compatibility' to see if your computer is already listed. If not you can add it there.

You also can submit a hardware report using 'System testing'.

Good luck!
