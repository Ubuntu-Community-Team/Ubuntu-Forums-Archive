### Post by Tyler27 on 2011-02-10
No worries. You actually have a pretty easy issue. First thing your going to do is when it boots up into the login screen press CTRL+ALT+F1 which will bring you to text mode. That answers your question. Then restart your computer and go into the bios after inserting your OS of choice that your going to change too. When that is done go into the BIOS and change the boot up so that the OS disk will boot your new OS first and not Ubuntu 10.10; keep me updated and let me know how everything is going!
