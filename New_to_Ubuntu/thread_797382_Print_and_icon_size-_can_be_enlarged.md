[FONT="Comic Sans MS"]Your screen resolution should be in your manual or you can search on the net for your monitor type.
Screen resolution is how many pixels it has, for instance mine is 1280 x 1024 on a 19" screen. Choosing a lower resolution will increase the size of everything on yor screen, maybe too much.
If everything fits well onto the screen but is hard to see then try the second alternative I suggested previously.
And yes changes made in ubuntu only affect ubuntu, ditto for what you do in xp.[/FONT]
