### Post by BenAshton24 on 2008-09-06
It should mount so long as it is plugged in and turned on everytime you boot, make sure it is on before your computer is. if this fails you could allways put the mount command in the startup. if it isn't mounting when you boot then it could be that you don't have detect usb devices enabled in your BIOS, a quick google should sort it.

Hope this helps :D
