### Post by sayakb on 2008-05-26
Thunderbird should automatically set the outgoing and incoming server adresses. Select POP3. Also, have you enabled the use of POP/IMAP with gmail. Open gmail.com and goto Settings (top right). Click "Forwarding and POP/IMAP" tab in settings and at the POP download section, enable POP.
