### Post by 3rdalbum on 2009-12-12
Futuresplash was what Flash was called, a long time ago. It's not a seperate plugin, it's just the Flash Player plugin. If Firefox encounters a really old Flash movie that identifies itself as "Futuresplash", it knows to use the Flash plugin.

If you're having Firefox crashing problems, then try removing some of your addons/extensions or try a different browser.
