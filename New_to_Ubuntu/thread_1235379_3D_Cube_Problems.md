### Post by CaptainMark on 2009-08-09
right click on the two squares in the bottom right of your screen, go to preferences and set columns=4 rows=1.
You have the right setup but by default you only 2 faces to your cube, hence a two sides sheet/not cube. With the controls for desktop cube thats configured via the rotate cube plugin on compiz under >rotate cube>bindings you can customise the controls, i recommend using the windows/super key as a master key for controlling the cube, i hold it and hit space to unfold the cube left/right to spin it 1,2,3,4 to jump to a face and the mouse to free rotate it,
desktop cube is the easiest coolest plugin you can get for ubuntu
