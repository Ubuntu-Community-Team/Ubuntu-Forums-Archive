### Post by Bucky Ball on 2017-02-05
Welcome. Might be able to help with more detail. What happens when you plug the USB stick in for instance and what are you expecting but not seeing? When you plug it in, is is not showing in the right hand pane of the file manager under whatever label you've given it? When you open a terminal and use this command:

```
lsusb
```

... do you see it listed there? 

Have you got Gparted installed? Or 'Disks' (I think that comes default in Ubuntu)? Does it show up in either of them? Was the USB ejected from a Windows or other machine correctly, not just yanked out before unmounting? What are you expecting to find on the USB or is it blank?
