### Post by MartyBuntu on 2012-03-01
Which Hauppauge card?

Capturing an analogue signal from an analogue card like the PVR series is as easy as entering **cat /dev/video0 > out.mpg** into your terminal.
Press **Ctrl+C** when you're done to terminate the recording.

To set input through the composite in on your Hauppauge card, use v4l2 control settings. Type in **v4l2-ctl** into your terminal for a list of commands.
