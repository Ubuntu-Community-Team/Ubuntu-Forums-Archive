### Post by legatek on 2010-04-30
First tip on not being an idiot: write a descriptive subject line.

To restore the bar at the bottom of the screen, right click on the bar on the top of the screen and select New panel. There should now be a panel at the bottom of the screen. Right click on this panel, Add to panel... and add Show Desktop, Window List, Workplace Switcher and Trash. To restore the panel to how it was, move the Show Desktop to the far left (right click, Move, and left click when finished) and the Trash to the far right. Workplace Switcher goes beside the trash and Window List goes beside the Show Desktop. Done!

### Post by Jon Monreal on 2010-04-30
After you add a new one, you will need to right-click on it and select "Add to Panel..." to get what was there back.

By default, this means Show Desktop, Window List, Workspace Switcher and Trash. You can middle-click on them to move them around.
