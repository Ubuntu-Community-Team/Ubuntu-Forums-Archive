### Post by iaculallad on 2008-09-02
Installing the GNOME theme would be a simple process: After you had finished downloading the packaged theme (usually in your Desktop), right click on your Desktop and select "Change Desktop Background", on the "Theme" tab, click on the "Install" button and browse the packaged theme (downloaded on your Desktop).
