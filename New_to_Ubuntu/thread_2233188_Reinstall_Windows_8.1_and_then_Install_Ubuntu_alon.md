### Post by yancek on 2014-07-08
A pre-installed windows system should have a Recovery partition.  Users are prompted to create a Recovery partition on first boot.  If you did not do that the suggestion above of downloading from the microsoft site with your key for activiation is a possibility.  You can also contact the manufacturer and give them this same information and they will provide you with an installation DVD, in most cases.   There is almost always a charge for this, last I checked $15-$25USD.
