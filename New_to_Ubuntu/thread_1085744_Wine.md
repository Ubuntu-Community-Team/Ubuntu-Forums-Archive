### Post by Kellemora on 2009-03-03
Hi drs

You might try blurred text or scratched out text to find similar problems.

I would use google for this and add the word Ubuntu in your search criteria so it lands you back to the forums here for the answers.  

ON the bright side, they are in here somewhere!

I would suggest that you install msttcorefonts and AND the Ubuntu Restricted Extras.  If you install the Ubuntu Restricted Extras from Synaptic I believe it installs the msttcorefonts automatically.

To do this, you would go to the top of your screen under System, select from the drop down box Administration, then in the next box select Synaptic Package Manager.  You will have to put in your password and then select Ubuntu-Restricted-Extras and hit apply on that screen and also apply at the top of the screen when you are done selecting packages.

This might fix your problem in Wine also!
