### Post by wrgb2 on 2009-11-18
First, in Outlook, export your contacts and choose .csv for the format. Save that to a USB flash drive or other device that can be read by Ubuntu.  Then in Thunderbird, open your address book, select Tools > Import from the menu, choose Choose Address Books, then .csv format, then point it to the file you exported from Outlook.  This should bring in the basic data from your contact list.
